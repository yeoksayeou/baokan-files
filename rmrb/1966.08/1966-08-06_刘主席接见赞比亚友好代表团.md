Chairman Liu Receives Zambian Friendly Delegation

New China News Agency, August 5th: This afternoon, Chairman Liu Shaoqi met with Zambian Minister of Commerce and Industry Chiba and the entire delegation led by him, engaging in a warm and friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were relevant officials including Lin Haiyun, Nan Hanchen, Lei Renmin, and Hou Yefeng.

August 5th, Chairman Liu Shaoqi met with Zambian Minister of Commerce and Industry Chiba and the entire delegation led by him. The photo shows a group picture taken during the meeting.

### 刘主席接见赞比亚友好代表团

1966-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见赞比亚友好代表团
    新华社五日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见赞比亚工商部长钦巴和由他率领的赞比亚友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有林海云、南汉宸、雷任民、侯野烽等有关方面负责人。（附图片）
    八月五日，刘少奇主席接见赞比亚工商部长钦巴和由他率领的赞比亚友好代表团全体成员。图为接见时合影。
