Title: Reform of the Uniforms of the Chinese People's Police
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 1

The uniforms of the Chinese People's Police have been approved for reform by the State Council and will be implemented nationwide from September 1 to 10. The new police uniforms feature a grass-green jacket, navy blue trousers, a liberation cap, a national flag insignia, and a fully red collar tab. Starting from the date of implementation, the old police uniforms will be abolished.

### 中国人民警察服装实行改革

1966-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民警察服装实行改革
    新华社二十七日讯　中国人民警察的服装，经国务院批准改革，于九月一日至十日在全国开始实行。新的民警服装样式，上衣为草绿色，裤子为藏蓝色，戴解放帽、国旗帽徽、全红领章。自实行之日起，旧的民警服装予以废除。
