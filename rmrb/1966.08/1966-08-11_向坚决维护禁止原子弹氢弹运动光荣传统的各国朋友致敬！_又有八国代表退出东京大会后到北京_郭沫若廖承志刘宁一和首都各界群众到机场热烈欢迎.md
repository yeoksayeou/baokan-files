Title: Salute to Friends from Various Countries Who Resolutely Defend the Glorious Tradition of the Movement to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs!
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 6

		Salute to friends from various countries who resolutely defend the glorious tradition of the movement to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs!

		Another eight countries' representatives, after withdrawing from the Tokyo conference, arrive in Beijing

		Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, and people from all walks of life in the capital warmly welcome them at the airport

		New China News Agency, August 10th

		Another eight countries' representatives, who withdrew from the 12th World Conference to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to defend its glorious tradition, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this evening. They were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in the capital.

		The friends from various countries are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, Liu Ningyi, Head of the Chinese delegation to the 12th World Conference to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, and other members of the delegation welcomed the friends from various countries at the airport.

		The eight countries' representatives who arrived in Beijing this evening are: Mopefiya Nane from Basutoland, Jonni Sechele from Bechuanaland, A. M. Gallianu from Brazil, Ricardo Samper from Colombia, Menendez from Panama, Kazonga from South West Africa, Herbert Nkosi from Swaziland, Teruma Toshiko Stassen, John Hawkins, and George Salan from the United States. Jaime Barros, a Chilean senator, also arrived in Beijing at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament.

		As the guests stepped off the plane, the welcoming crowd from all walks of life in the capital beat drums, waved flower bouquets, and applauded warmly to salute the friends from various countries who are resolutely fighting to defend the glorious tradition of the movement to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs. Young Pioneers from the capital presented flowers to the friends from various countries.

		Those who welcomed the guests at the airport also included leaders from various people's organizations, including Nan Hanchen, Gu Dazhun, Yong Longgui, Shen Ziji, Liu Qingyang, Peng Yan, Xu Kui, and Wu Shaozu.

		The six countries' representatives who withdrew from the 12th World Conference to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and arrived in Beijing on the 6th, Japanese peace activist Nishi Sonjiro, Sudanese peace activist Kair, and other foreign friends in Beijing also went to the airport to welcome them.

		The photograph shows the eight countries' representatives who withdrew from the 12th World Conference to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, and others who welcomed them at the airport.

### 向坚决维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动光荣传统的各国朋友致敬！　又有八国代表退出东京大会后到北京　郭沫若廖承志刘宁一和首都各界群众到机场热烈欢迎

1966-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　向坚决维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动光荣传统的各国朋友致敬！
　　又有八国代表退出东京大会后到北京
　　郭沫若廖承志刘宁一和首都各界群众到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社十日讯　又有为维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动光荣传统而退出第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的八个国家的代表，今晚由广州乘飞机到达北京，受到首都各界群众的热烈欢迎。
    各国朋友是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请到中国访问的。中国和委会主席郭沫若，中国亚非团结委员会主席、中国和委会副主席廖承志，准备出席第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长、中华全国总工会主席、中国和委会副主席刘宁一和代表团其他成员，到机场迎接各国朋友。
    今晚到北京的八个国家代表是：巴苏陀兰马佩菲亚内，贝专纳琼尼·塞切尔，巴西阿·米·加利亚努，哥伦比亚里卡多·桑佩尔，巴拿马梅嫩德斯，西南非卡宗加，斯威士兰赫伯特·恩科西，美国特尔马·托希科·斯太曾，约翰·霍金斯，乔治·萨兰。应中国和委会邀请来中国访问的智利参议员海梅·巴洛斯，也同机到北京。
    当客人们走下飞机时，前来欢迎的首都各界群众敲锣打鼓，挥舞花束，热烈鼓掌，向为维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动光荣传统而坚决斗争的各国朋友致敬。首都少先队员向各国朋友献花。
    到机场欢迎的，还有各人民团体的负责人南汉宸、顾大椿、勇龙桂、沈兹九、刘清扬、彭炎、徐葵、伍绍祖等。
    六日到北京的退出第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的六个国家代表，日本和平人士西园寺公一，苏丹和平人士凯尔，以及在北京的其他一些外国朋友，也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
    图为退出第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的八国代表们和前来机场欢迎的郭沫若、廖承志和刘宁一等在机场合影。　　　　　　　
　　新华社记者摄
