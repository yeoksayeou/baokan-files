Title: Comrade Kang Sheng Hosts a Banquet for the Delegation of the French "New Humanity Daily"
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, today received and hosted a banquet for the delegation of the French "New Humanity Daily," led by Thibault, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist). The hosts and guests engaged in warm and friendly conversations.

The delegation of the "New Humanity Daily" includes Casas, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist), Bergeon, a member of the Political Bureau and the editor-in-chief of the "New Humanity Daily," as well as Campestre, Martin, and others.

Present at the reception were Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Tang Pingzhu, a responsible person from the "People's Daily."

The delegation of the "New Humanity Daily" arrived in Beijing on August 6. During their stay in China, they visited Yanan, Shanghai, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, and other cities, toured factories and people's communes, and were warmly welcomed by workers and commune members.

### 康生同志宴请法国《新人道报》代表团

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    康生同志宴请法国《新人道报》代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记康生今天接见并宴请由法国共产主义运动（马列主义）政治局委员兼书记处书记蒂贝拉率领的法国《新人道报》代表团。宾主进行了热烈友好的谈话。
    《新人道报》代表团的成员，还有法国共产主义运动（马列主义）政治局委员兼书记处书记卡萨斯，法国共产主义运动（马列主义）政治局委员兼《新人道报》总编辑贝热龙，以及坎佩斯特雷、马丁等。
    接见时在座的，有中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，和《人民日报》负责人唐平铸等。
    《新人道报》代表团是在八月六日到北京的。他们在中国期间，先后访问了延安、上海、武汉、郑州、洛阳等城市，参观了工厂和人民公社，受到工人和社员们的热烈欢迎。
