Title: Some Foreign Table Tennis Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 5

	Some foreign table tennis teams have arrived in Beijing.

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 21st, a batch of outstanding athletes from various countries and regions participating in the highly anticipated 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational have been gathering in Beijing in recent days.

	The guests were warmly welcomed by the chairman of the organizing committee of this invitational tournament, Chairman Chen Xian of the Chinese Table Tennis Association, and vice-chairmen Zhao Xiwu and Dou Haoran, as well as Chinese table tennis players upon their arrival.

	By the 21st, the table tennis teams from the following countries and regions had arrived in Beijing: the men's and women's teams from Cambodia, the men's and women's teams from Ceylon, the men's and women's teams from North Korea, the men's team from Lebanon, the men's and women's teams from Nepal, the men's team from Pakistan, the men's and women's teams from Palestine, the men's and women's teams from Syria, the men's and women's teams from Vietnam, and the men's team from Yemen.

### 一些外国乒乓球队到京

1966-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    一些外国乒乓球队到京
    据新华社二十一日讯  参加举世瞩目的一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的一批国家和地区的优秀选手，连日来纷纷汇集北京。
    客人们到达时，分别受到了这次邀请赛组织委员会主席、中国乒乓球协会主席陈先、组织委员会副主席赵希武和都浩然，以及我国乒乓球运动员的热烈欢迎。
    截止二十一日，到达北京的国家和地区的乒乓球队有：柬埔寨男女队、锡兰男女队、朝鲜男女队、黎巴嫩男子队、尼泊尔男女队、巴基斯坦男子队、巴勒斯坦男女队、叙利亚男女队、越南男女队和也门男子队。
