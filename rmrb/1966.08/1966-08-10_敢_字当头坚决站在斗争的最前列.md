Title: Stand Firmly at the Forefront of the Struggle with "Dare" as the Motto
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 1

After hearing the news about the Party Central Committee's decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution from the radio, we are all filled with excitement and high spirits. The Party Central Committee's wise decision has pointed out the direction for China's cultural revolution. It expresses the aspirations of the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers in China and is the fundamental guarantee for the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. All workers of our mine firmly support the Party Central Committee's decision. We will resolutely follow the Party's lead, hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and with "dare" as our motto, stand at the forefront of the struggle. We will fight to overthrow, smash, and expose all the bourgeois ghosts and monsters. We vow to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end, and we will not stand down until we achieve complete victory.

River South Province Kaifeng District Xinzhong Coal Mine All Workers

### “敢”字当头坚决站在斗争的最前列

1966-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    “敢”字当头坚决站在斗争的最前列最敬爱的党中央最敬爱的毛主席：
    当我们从广播里听到党中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定的消息以后，个个心情振奋，斗志昂扬。党中央的这一英明决定，给我国的文化大革命指明了方向，它表达了我国广大工农兵群众的愿望，是无产阶级文化大革命取得胜利的根本保证。我矿全体职工，最坚决地拥护党中央的决定。我们坚决听党的话，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，“敢”字当头，站在斗争的最前列，要把一切资产阶级牛鬼蛇神斗倒、斗垮、斗臭。誓把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，不获全胜，决不收兵。　　　　　　河南省开封专区新中煤矿全体职工
