Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Vietnamese Guests
Vietnamese Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Matches in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-19
Edition: 4

	Vice Premier He Long received the Vietnamese guests.
	The Vietnamese men's and women's volleyball teams held friendly matches in Beijing.

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 18th, a grand welcoming ceremony was held at the Beijing Gymnasium this evening to warmly welcome the men's and women's volleyball teams from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for friendly matches in Beijing.

	Vice Premier He Long and the acting charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Leh Chungshi, attended the welcoming ceremony. Vice Premier He Long met with the team leader, Nguyen Yu Yan.

	Two matches were held this evening, with the home teams being the Hubei men's volleyball team and the Chinese women's volleyball team, facing the visiting men's and women's volleyball teams respectively.

	The results of the matches were as follows: the visiting men's team won with a score of 3-2, while the women's team lost with a score of 0-3.

### 贺龙副总理接见越南客人　越南男女排球队在京进行友谊赛

1966-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　贺龙副总理接见越南客人
　　越南男女排球队在京进行友谊赛
    据新华社十八日讯  北京体育馆今晚举行隆重欢迎仪式，热烈欢迎越南民主共和国男、女排球队在京进行友谊赛。
    贺龙副总理，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始出席了欢迎仪式。贺龙副总理接见客队领队阮玉寅。
    今晚双方共进行两场比赛，主队分别由湖北省男子排球队和中国女子排球队迎战客队男、女排球队。
    比赛结果，客队男子队以三比二获胜，女子队以零比三失利。
