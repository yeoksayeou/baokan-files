Title: The Egyptian Men's Gymnastics Team Performs in Beijing with the Chinese Men's Gymnastics Team
Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 4

The Egyptian men's gymnastics team performed with the Chinese men's gymnastics team at the Beijing Gymnasium this evening.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and the Chargé d'Affaires of the Egyptian Embassy in China, Muhammad Atif Nawawi, watched the performance. Before the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the leader of the visiting team, the Chairman of the Egyptian Gymnastics Association, Tuni, and walked down from the platform to shake hands with the athletes from both sides and took a group photo with them.

### 中国阿联男子体操队在京进行共同表演  陈毅副总理接见客队领队

1966-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中国阿联男子体操队在京进行共同表演
　　陈毅副总理接见客队领队
    据新华社十五日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国国家男子体操队今晚在北京体育馆同中国男子体操队进行了共同表演。
    陈毅副总理，阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办穆罕默德·阿特夫·那瓦威观看了今晚的表演。表演前，陈毅副总理接见客队领队、阿拉伯联合共和国体操协会主席图尼，并且走下主席台同双方运动员一一握手并在一起照了相。
