Title: Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits Outside the Capital
Author:
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 6

The Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits Outside the Capital
Chairman Ju De and His Wife See Off the Guests at the Airport

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, August 14th - The Speaker of the Pakistani National Assembly, Abdul Jabbar Khan, and his wife, along with the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation led by him, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, and his wife Yu Liqun, departed Beijing by special plane this morning to visit places outside the capital.

Chairman Ju De and his wife Kang Keqing came to the airport to warmly see off the guests. The airport was adorned with the national flags of China and Pakistan. Young Pioneers presented flowers to Speaker Jabbar Khan and his wife, as well as other Pakistani guests. Before boarding the plane, Speaker Jabbar Khan shouted, "Long live Sino-Pakistani friendship!"

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Kang Sheng, and his wife Cao Yiou, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Ningyi, and his wife Li Shuying, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Gao Chongmin, Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong, and China's Ambassador to Pakistan, Zhang Wenjin, also came to the airport to see off the guests.

Those who came to the airport to see off the guests also included members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, leaders of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and relevant departments, as well as leaders from Beijing. Among them were: Xu Guangping, Mao Yisheng, Luo Shuzhang, Zhao Yimin, Nan Hanchen, Xie Fumin, Han Guang, Chen Cisheng, Zhao Boping, Zhang Zhirang, Huang Huoxing, Mei Gongbin, Hu Ziyang, Wang Shaohuo, Ji Fang, Xu Deheng, Chen Qiyou, Shi Huaipei, Shen Ziji, Sha Mengbi, Wang Chuanbin, Hu Jiewen, and Le Songsheng.

Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan, and his wife Lin Lang also accompanied the Pakistani guests on their visit outside the capital.

Accompanying the visit were also the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan.

August 14th, the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation leaves Beijing to visit places outside the capital, posing for a group photo with those who came to see them off. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 巴基斯坦议会访华代表团离京赴外地访问  朱德委员长和夫人等到机场欢送贵宾

1966-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦议会访华代表团离京赴外地访问
    朱德委员长和夫人等到机场欢送贵宾
    新华社十四日讯　巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团，由人大常委会副委员长郭沫若和夫人于立群陪同，今天上午乘专机离开北京前往外地访问。
    朱德委员长和夫人康克清，到机场热烈欢送贵宾。今天，机场上飘扬着中国和巴基斯坦两国国旗。少先队员向贾巴尔·汗议长和夫人，以及其他巴基斯坦贵宾献花。登上飞机前，贾巴尔·汗议长高呼：“巴中友好万岁！”
    人大常委会副委员长康生和夫人曹轶欧，人大常委会副委员长、中巴友好协会会长赛福鼎，人大常委会副委员长刘宁一和夫人李淑英，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民，外交部副部长韩念龙，我国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋，也到机场欢送贵宾。
    到机场欢送的还有，人大常委会委员、各民主党派、人民团体和有关部门负责人以及北京市负责人，他们当中有：许广平、茅以升、罗叔章、赵毅敏、南汉宸、谢扶民、韩光、陈此生、赵伯平、张志让、黄火星、梅龚彬、胡子昂、王绍鏊、季方、许德珩、陈其尤、史怀璧、沈兹九、沙梦弼、王传斌、胡厥文、乐松生等。
    人大常委会副秘书长连贯和夫人林琅也陪同巴基斯坦贵宾去外地访问。
    陪同访问的，还有巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗。（附图片）
　　八月十四日，巴基斯坦议会访华代表团离京前往外地访问，在机场上同前往欢送的人合影。新华社记者摄
