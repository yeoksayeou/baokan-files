Title: Chinese Government Delegation to Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-08-08
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Chinese government, led by Minister of the Interior Zeng Shan, departed Beijing by train this afternoon to attend the third anniversary celebrations of the August Revolution in the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville). The delegation was invited by the Congolese government.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and Vice Minister of the Interior Wang Yifu. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Congolese Embassy in China, Bakula, was also present at the station to see them off.

### 我政府代表团去刚果（布）

1966-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团去刚果（布）
    新华社七日讯　由内务部部长曾山率领的中国政府代表团，应刚果共和国（布拉柴维尔）政府邀请，于今天下午乘火车离开北京，前往布拉柴维尔，参加刚果（布）八月革命三周年庆典。
    前往车站送行的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，内务部副部长王一夫等。刚果（布）驻华大使馆临时代办巴库拉也到车站送行。
