Title: Important Announcement from the Trade Union and Mutual Aid Society of the Liuhegou Coal Mine

Author:
Date: 1947-07-03
Page: 1

　　Important Announcement from the Trade Union and Mutual Aid Society of the Liuhegou Coal Mine:
    Our society is ordered to close down. Accounts will be settled at the end of June this year. Shareholders are requested to bring their shares to withdraw dividends. Those with relevant procedures are requested to come for settlement within July. We will not be responsible for any overdue matters. This is hereby announced.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Address: Caoshi Street, Pengcheng Town, Cixian County.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  　　　　　Manager: Wang Yushi

### 六河沟煤矿职工总会　职工互助社重要启事：

1947-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　六河沟煤矿职工总会　职工互助社重要启事：
    本社奉命结束，在本年六月底结帐，希各股员持股票前来抽股分红，并希有关手续者，在七月内前来清理，过期概不负责，特此声明。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　地址　磁县彭城镇草市街，
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  　　　　　经理　王雨时
