Title: In the Battle to Recover Lingyuan, We Annihilated 4,000 Chiang Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-07-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe, 5th. (Delayed dispatch) In the battle to recover Lingyuan on June 21st, the Rehe Eastern Liberation Army annihilated over 3,800 enemy troops. Detailed battle results are as follows: 1,400 enemy soldiers of the Northeast Security Cavalry Third Detachment were captured, over 2,400 were killed or wounded, and the following items were seized: 789 rifles, 18 light machine guns, 2 82mm mortars, 4 60mm mortars, 532 warhorses, 195,000 rounds of ammunition, and 2 locomotives.

### 收复凌源之役我歼蒋军四千

1947-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　收复凌源之役我歼蒋军四千
    【新华社冀察热辽五日电】（迟到）热东解放军六月二十一日收复凌源之役，歼敌三千八百余人。详细战果如下：俘敌东北保安骑兵三队一千四百名，毙伤二千四百余名，缴获步枪七百八十九支，轻机枪十八挺，八二迫击炮二门，六○炮四门，战马五百三十二匹，子弹十九万五千余发，火车头二个。
