Title: Sound Film Produced Successfully in Jin-Cha-Ji Region
Author:
Date: 1947-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 15th -- The Jin-Cha-Ji Military Region Film Team has successfully produced the sound film "Self-Defense War News No. 1," after four months of work. The entire film is 1,500 feet long.

### 有声影片  晋察冀摄制成功

1947-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    有声影片
    晋察冀摄制成功
    【新华社晋察冀十五日电】晋察冀军区电影队摄制之有声影片“自卫战争新闻第一号”，费时四月之久，已告成功，全片长达千五百英尺。
