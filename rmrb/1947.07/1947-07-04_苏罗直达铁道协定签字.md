Title: Soviet-Romanian Direct Railway Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1947-07-04
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: Moscow Dispatch: The railway departments of the Soviet Union and Romania signed an agreement in the middle of this month on direct railway lines between the two countries.

### 苏罗直达铁道协定签字

1947-07-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏罗直达铁道协定签字
    【新华社陕北三十日电】莫斯科讯：苏联与罗马尼亚两国铁道部已于本月中旬签订两国间直达铁道的协定。
