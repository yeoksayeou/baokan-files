Title: Serving the Field Armies on All Fronts: Handan Xinhua Radio Station Adds Broadcast Programs
Author:
Date: 1947-07-29
Page: 1
　　
　　Serving the Field Armies on All Fronts
  　Handan Xinhua Radio Station Adds Broadcast Programs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jinji Luyu, 27th] To meet the needs of the front, the Handan Xinhua Radio Station will add new programs starting August 1st, specifically to serve our field armies on all fronts. The broadcast time will be from 7:00 AM to 8:30 AM every morning. The programs will include important news, commentaries, dispatches, etc., all broadcast as recorded news. Afternoon broadcasts will remain the same, from 17:00 to 21:00. The station's call sign is XGHT, wavelength 49.2 meters, frequency 6096 kHz.

### 为各线野战军服务　邯郸新华电台增设广播节目

1947-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　为各线野战军服务
  　邯郸新华电台增设广播节目
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫二十七日电】邯郸新华广播电台为适应前线需要，八月一日起增设新节目，专为我各线野战军服务，广播时间为每晨七点至八点半。节目包括各项重要新闻、评论、通讯等，全部以记录新闻播送。下午十七点至二十一点广播仍旧，该台呼号ＸＧＨＴ，波长四十九点二公尺，周率六○九六千周。
