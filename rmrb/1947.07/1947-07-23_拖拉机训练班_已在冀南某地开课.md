Title: Tractor Training Class Opens in a Certain Place in Southern Hebei
Author: Feng Zhou
Date: 1947-07-23
Page: 2

　　Tractor Training Class
  　Has Opened in a Certain Place in Southern Hebei
    [Reported by this newspaper] The tractor training class organized by the Shanxi-Hebei-Shandong-Henan Border Region officially opened in early July in ×× County, Southern Hebei. Each administrative office sent ten trainees, totaling forty people. The training period is set for six months, and there are forty tractors available for practical training. Everyone is highly enthusiastic about learning to use this new type of agricultural machinery. In order to closely integrate learning and practice, a tractor station has been established in the ×× area, and more than 40,000 mu of wasteland have been designated for reclamation using tractors. (Feng Zhou)

### 拖拉机训练班　已在冀南某地开课
凤洲
1947-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　拖拉机训练班
  　已在冀南某地开课
    【本报讯】晋冀鲁豫边区，所举办的拖拉机训练班已于七月初在冀南××县正式开课，每一行署区保送受训学员十人，共有四十人，学习期限定为半年，供实习的拖拉机有四十架。大家对此种新式农具的运用，学习情绪都很高。为了学与做密切结合，并在××地区成立拖拉机站，划出荒地四万余亩，用拖拉机进行开垦。（凤洲）
