Title: Chiang Government's Sale of Air Rights Sparks Discontent in Aviation Industry
Author:
Date: 1947-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th: Nanjing dispatch: At the "National Aviation Administration Conference" convened here by the Chiang government at the beginning of this month, the aviation industry expressed much dissatisfaction with the Chiang government's selling of air rights, undermining China's aviation industry, and monopolizing Japanese reparations of ships. Important resolutions of the conference regarding navigation affairs included "protecting the domestic shipping industry and strengthening the management of foreign ships," "stopping the Executive Yuan Relief and Rehabilitation Administration (CRRRA) water transport corps from soliciting passengers and cargo." Regarding ships, they included "negotiating with Japan for compensation of 530,000 tons of our ships, and opposing the Bowles plan to retain 1.5 million tons of Japanese merchant ships" and "returning Chinese ships occupied by Japan," etc.

### 蒋政府出卖航权  航业界均表不满

1947-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋政府出卖航权
    航业界均表不满
    【新华社陕北十一日电】南京讯：本月初蒋政府在此间召开之“全国航政会议”上，航业界对蒋政府出卖航权、摧残中国航业及垄断日本赔偿之船等，多表示不满。该会重要议决案，关于航务者有“保护本国航业，加强管理外籍船舶”。“制止行总水运大队揽载客货。”关于船舶有“向日本交涉赔偿我国船舶五十三万吨，并反对鲍莱保持日本商船一百五十万吨之计划”及“发还日本占用我国船只”等。
