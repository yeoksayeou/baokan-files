Title: Níngyáng Captures a Chiang Kai-shek Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1947-07-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 15th. A U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek aircraft flew from Xuzhou over Ningyang County on July 1st for reconnaissance. Due to mechanical failure, it was forced to land in a certain area of the county. The pilot, Wu Dingchen, a major special commissioner of the Flight Section of the Fourth Military Region of the Chiang Kai-shek army in Wuhan, was captured. All equipment inside the aircraft was also seized by us. Wu has arrived at the military region headquarters and yesterday telegraphed his wife, Dai Yunqing, at 118 Liu Yang Road, Wuhan, stating: "I was forced to land in the liberated area and have been treated favorably by the local military and civilians. Everything is fine. I hope the civil war ends soon and the whole family can be reunited."

### 宁阳缴获蒋机一架

1947-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    宁阳缴获蒋机一架
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫十五日电】美制蒋机一架于七月一日由徐州飞至我宁阳县上空侦察，因机器发生故障，被迫降落于该县某地。该机驾驶员武昌蒋军第四军区司令部飞行科少校特派员吴鼎臣被俘，机内一切装备亦全部被我缴获。吴已抵军区司令部，于昨日致电武昌刘杨路一一八号其妻戴云清，略称：“余被迫降落解放区，获得此间军民优待，一切均好，愿内战早日结束，全家重获团圆。”
