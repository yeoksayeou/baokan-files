Title: Iranian People Oppose US-Iran Loan Agreement
Author:
Date: 1947-07-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. General news: The U.S. State Department announced on June 20th that it would grant the reactionary Iranian government a credit loan of 25 million U.S. dollars to be used for purchasing surplus materials and improving Iranian military equipment. The Iranian Mado newspaper commented on the signing of the U.S.-Iran loan agreement on the 27th, stating: The Iranian people oppose the Iranian rulers borrowing foreign currency and weapons to maintain their position; they are extremely angry at the signing of this slave treaty that violates national interests. The Ruz newspaper also protested against the Iranian government accepting foreign loans during the parliamentary recess.

### 伊朗人民反对美伊贷款协定

1947-07-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　伊朗人民反对美伊贷款协定
    【新华社陕北二日电】综合消息：美国务院于六月二十日宣布美国予伊朗反动派以二千五百万元之信用贷款，作为购买剩余物资及改进伊朗军备之用。伊朗马多报二十七日评论美伊贷款协定签字时称：伊朗人民反对伊朗统治者借用外国贷币及武器来保持他们的地位；对于此种违犯国家利益奴役性条约之签订，极为愤怒。鲁兹报亦抗议伊政府乘国会之休会接受外国贷款。
