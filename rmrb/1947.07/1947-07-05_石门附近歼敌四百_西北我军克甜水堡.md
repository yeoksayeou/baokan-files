Title: Annihilating Four Hundred Enemies Near Shimen; Our Northwest Army Captures Tianshuibao
Author:
Date: 1947-07-05
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀, 3rd: All of the 95th Regiment of the 32nd Division of the Chiang Army in Shijiazhuang, sneaked into the Dongyan and Fama Town area (about 40 li southeast of Shimen) on the 24th of last month to grab wheat. They were intercepted by a part of our Jizhong Local Corps, and more than 400 people were annihilated. The remaining enemies fled back to the eastern strongholds of Shimen in a panic.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 3rd: (Delayed) After the Northwest People's Liberation Army recovered Huanxian, it recovered Tianshuibao, 140 li northwest of the county, on the 26th. On the 21st, our army also recovered Sancha Town between Qingyang and Guyuan.

### 石门附近歼敌四百　西北我军克甜水堡

1947-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　石门附近歼敌四百
  　西北我军克甜水堡
    【新华社晋察冀三日电】石家庄蒋军三十二师九十五团全部，上月二十四日窜往东宴、乏马镇（石门东南约四十里）一带抢麦，遭我冀中地方兵团一部阻击，歼灭其四百余人，残敌狼狈退回石门东部各据点。
    【新华社西北前线三日电】（迟到）西北人民解放军一部于收复环县后，二十六日收复该县西北百四十华里之甜水堡，二十一日我军并收复庆阳与固原间之三岔镇。
