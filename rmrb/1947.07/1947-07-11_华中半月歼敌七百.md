Title: Annihilating 700 Enemies in Central China in Half a Month
Author:
Date: 1947-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Central China, 9th: In the latter half of June, the military and civilians of the Central Jiangsu and Northern Jiangsu Liberated Areas continued to strike at the Chiang Kai-shek puppet regime's conspiracy to seize grain. They fought more than 460 battles (excluding the achievements of the militia in Central Jiangsu), killing, wounding, and capturing 772 Chiang Kai-shek puppet troops, seizing 23 light machine guns and 274 rifles and pistols, and capturing several Chiang Kai-shek military strongholds, including Luji and Yanghua in Northern Jiangsu, and Hongqiao in Central Jiangsu.

### 华中半月歼敌七百

1947-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    华中半月歼敌七百
    【新华社华中九日电】六月下半月，苏中、苏北蒋后军民继续打击蒋伪抢粮阴谋，共作战四百六十余次（苏中民兵战绩未计入），毙伤俘蒋伪军七百七十二名，缴获轻机枪二十三挺，长短枪二百七十四支，攻克蒋军据点计苏北之陆集、仰化、苏中之虹桥等数处。
