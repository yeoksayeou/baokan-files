Title: New Democratic Nations Strengthen Unity
Author:
Date: 1947-07-22
Page: 3
　　New Democratic Nations Strengthen Unity
    ★Helsinki Dispatch: Finland and Bulgaria recently signed a one-year trade agreement. It stipulates that the total trade volume between the two countries will be 135 million Finnish marks. Bulgaria will export tobacco, dried fruits and other commodities to Finland, while Finland will export paper, timber and finished wood products to Bulgaria.
    ★The governments of Yugoslavia and Albania signed a cultural cooperation treaty in Tirana on July 11.
    ★Belgrade held enthusiastic celebrations for the first anniversary of the Yugoslav-Albanian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance. The Albanian capital's "Voice of the People" newspaper published an article commemorating the anniversary, stating: "The alliance between the two countries has formed a strong bulwark for peace and democracy in the Balkans."
    ★The Polish Socialist Party held a summit meeting earlier this month and decided to strengthen the united front of the working class.
    ★The Executive Committee of the Finnish People's Democratic League recently sent a letter to the Social Democratic Party, inviting the party to promptly begin cooperation negotiations in the upcoming municipal elections. The People's League demands the establishment of a united front of the working people to crush the reactionary forces' conspiracy to obstruct the victory of the working people. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新民主国家加强团结

1947-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　新民主国家加强团结
    ★赫尔辛基讯：芬兰与保加利亚两国近签订一年贸易协定。规定两国贸易总额为一亿三千五百万芬兰马克，保将输出烟草干果与若干其他商品予芬，芬则将以造纸木材及木材工业成品输保。
    ★南斯拉夫与阿尔巴尼亚两国政府于七月十一日在地拉那签订文化合作条约。
    ★南阿（阿尔巴尼亚）友好互助条约一周年，贝尔格拉德举行热烈庆祝。阿京“海斯德”报撰文纪念称：“两国联盟已构成巴尔干和平与民主坚强堡垒。”
    ★波兰社会党月初举行首脑会议，决定加强工人阶级之统一阵线。
    ★芬兰人民民主联盟执委会顷函社会民主党，邀请该党迅即开始在即将到来之市选中合作谈判，人民联盟要求建立劳动人民统一阵线，在市选中粉碎反动力量阻扰劳动人民胜利之阴谋。（新华社）
