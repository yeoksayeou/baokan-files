Title: Yuwansu Continues Offensive Development; Our Army Recaptures Taikang; Dingtao Campaign Captures 3,000 Chiang Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-07-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Jiluyu, 13th: After the Yuwansu People's Liberation Army recovered Luyi and Bozhou, the main force turned to Taikang. The defending security team fled in panic on the night of the 10th, and Taikang was thus recovered by us for the fifth time.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Jiluyu, 13th: The Jinjiluyu Field Army Headquarters announced the results of the Dingtao operation, stating: A unit of our army captured Dingtao on the night of the 10th, annihilating the entire 153rd Brigade of the 63rd Division of the Chiang army occupying the city, killing and wounding more than 200 enemies, capturing more than 3,130 officers and soldiers, and seizing three infantry guns, twelve mortars, one hundred and twenty-three light and heavy machine guns, more than one thousand and one hundred rifles, three radio stations, and other materials. The brigade belongs to the Yu Hanmou's unit of Guangdong, which was transferred to North China by Chiang Kai-shek to fight, and suffered great losses along the way. There are now less than 3,500 people remaining.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Jiluyu, 13th: When the People's Liberation Army broke through Yuncheng, the Chiang troops scattered like birds and beasts. The masses in various villages along the way and the rear organs of the People's Liberation Army went out to capture them. From dawn to late at night on the 8th, people searched in the sorghum fields with various weapons. A certain organization captured 125 people, including Feng Junhua, director of the News Office of the 55th Division of the Chiang Army.

### 豫皖苏继续发展攻势  我军再克太康  定陶战役擒蒋军三千

1947-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    豫皖苏继续发展攻势
    我军再克太康
    定陶战役擒蒋军三千
    【新华社冀鲁豫前线十三日电】豫皖苏人民解放军继收复鹿邑、亳州之后，主力即转指太康，守敌保安团队于十日夜闻风逃窜，太康遂为我第五次收复。
    【新华社冀鲁豫前线十三日电】晋冀鲁豫野战司令部公布定陶作战战果称：我军一部于十日夜攻克定陶，歼灭侵占该城之蒋军六十三师之一五三旅全部，计毙伤敌二百余，俘官兵三千一百三十余，缴获步兵炮三门，迫击炮十二门，轻重机枪一百二十三挺，步枪一千一百余支，电台三部，其他物资甚夥。按该旅系广东余汉谋部，被蒋介石调至华北作战，沿途减员极大，现存不足三千五百人。
    【新华社冀鲁豫前线十三日电】人民解放军突破郓城时，蒋军皆作鸟兽散，沿途各村群众及解放军后方机关人员，纷纷出动捕捉。八日拂晓至深夜，人们手执各种武器，到高粱地里搜寻，某机关即捕获蒋军五十五师新闻室主任冯俊华等以下一百二十五人。
