Title: Our Army Recovers Xia County Town
Author: Zhang Keren Yang Langqiao
Date: 1947-07-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 18th: A unit of the Jinan People's Liberation Army recovered Xia County town on the 14th, killing and capturing more than 400 "Love the Homeland Corps" deputy团长 and below.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 18th: Frontline reporters Zhang Keren and Yang Langqiao of the Taiyue Branch of this agency were commended for their meritorious reporting while accompanying the army. Comrade Zhang Keren went deep into the companies to conduct interviews during the Jinan Campaign. In the Quxiang battle, he was bold and calm, observing the battle at the front lines with machine gunners and artillerymen. His reports greatly encouraged the troops. The soldiers said, "Reporter Zhang has been a great help to us. In the future, we must achieve more merits to be worthy of him." Comrade Yang Langqiao charged into the city with the army during the Jiexian battle, deeply experiencing the lives of the soldiers. As a result, he wrote many wonderful works, such as "Eight Tigers Create Havoc at Plum Blossom Fort," which were very popular with the troops. The Taiyue District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has issued a circular of commendation.

### 我军收复夏县城
张克仁  杨朗樵
1947-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我军收复夏县城
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫十八日电】晋南人民解放军一部十四日收复夏县城，毙俘“爱乡团”副团长以下四百余人。
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫十八日电】本社太岳分社前线记者张克仁、杨朗樵二同志随军报导立功。张克仁同志在晋南战役中，深入连队采访。在曲襄战斗中，大胆沉着，与机枪手、炮手在一起，观察火线战斗，他的报导给部队以极大的鼓舞，战士们说：“张记者对我们帮助很大，今后我们要更多立功才对得起他。”杨朗樵同志在解县战斗中，随军冲入城内，深入体验战士生活，因之写出了不少精彩作品，如“八虎大闹梅花垛”等作品，甚受部队欢迎。中共太岳区党委已通报表扬。
