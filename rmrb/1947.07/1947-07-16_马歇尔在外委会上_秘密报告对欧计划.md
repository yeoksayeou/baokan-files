Title: Marshall Secretly Reports on European Plan to Foreign Affairs Committee
Author:
Date: 1947-07-16
Page: 3

    Marshall Secretly Reports on European Plan to Foreign Affairs Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: Agence France-Presse, Washington, 10th: U.S. Secretary of State Marshall will deliver a secret report to the Foreign Affairs Committee on the 11th. According to several members of the committee, Marshall will discuss the U.S. economic aid plan for Europe.

### 马歇尔在外委会上  秘密报告对欧计划

1947-07-16
第3版()
专栏：

    马歇尔在外委会上
    秘密报告对欧计划
    【新华社陕北十二日电】法新闻社华盛顿十日讯：美国务卿马歇尔将于十一日对外交委员会作秘密报告。据该委员会若干委员称：马歇尔将论及美国对欧洲之经济援助计划。
