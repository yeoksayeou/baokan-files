　　The Battle of Goose and Duck Factory
    On June 23rd, at Goose and Duck Factory near Liangshan (northeast of Yuncheng), a part of the local armed forces of the Jilu-Yu (冀鲁豫) region attacked the enemy, annihilating over a thousand men of the First and Third Battalions of the 87th Regiment of the 29th Brigade of the 55th Division of the Chiang Kai-shek army, coordinating with the main force in crossing the river southward. These are two close-ups of that battle.
　　　　　　　　　—Editor

### 鹅鸭厂之战

1947-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　鹅鸭厂之战
    六月二十三日，在梁山（郓城东北）附近的鹅鸭厂，冀鲁豫地方武装一部向敌进击，歼灭了蒋军五五师二九旅八七团一、三两个营全部千余人配合大军南渡。这是那个战役的两个特写。　　　　　　　　　——编者
