Title: Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, Issues Statement Opposing the Kuomintang's "General Mobilization Order"
Author:
Date: 1947-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th. According to a United Press report from Shanghai on the 10th: "Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, issued a statement expressing his deep sorrow regarding the government's (Chiang Kai-shek's government) 'Communist Suppression' General Mobilization Order issued at the beginning of the month." Zhang stated: "As long as we have a breath left, we will continue to fight for China's democracy and peace."

### 民盟主席张澜声明  反对蒋党“总动员令”

1947-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    民盟主席张澜声明
    反对蒋党“总动员令”
    【新华社陕北十二日电】据合众社十日沪讯称：“民盟主席张澜发表声明，对政府（蒋政府）月初发表的‘剿共’总动员令甚表痛心。”张氏称：“只要我们一息尚存，将为中国的民主、和平而继续奋斗。”
