Title: Three Thousand Workers in Jinan Angrily Beat the Chiang Kai-shek Railway Bureau

Author:

Date: 1947-07-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 12th. The Chiang police in Jinan massacred workers, triggering unrest. On July 4th, more than three thousand workers in the city went to the Chiang Kai-shek's Jiaoji Railway Bureau to petition against mass layoffs and wage cuts. The Chiang police opened fire and killed one worker and wounded two others. Enraged, the workers immediately surrounded the bureau's office, broke down the doors, drove out the Chiang police, and beat up the bureau chief.

### 济南三千工人  怒打蒋记路局

1947-07-16
第3版()
专栏：

    济南三千工人
    怒打蒋记路局
    【新华社山东十二日电】济南蒋警屠杀工人，激起骚动。七月四日，该市三千余工人因蒋记胶济铁路局大批裁员减薪，齐赴路局请愿，蒋警竟开枪击毙工人一人，伤二人。工人激愤之下，立将该局办公室包围、破门而入，驱逐蒋警，并将该局局长痛打一顿。
