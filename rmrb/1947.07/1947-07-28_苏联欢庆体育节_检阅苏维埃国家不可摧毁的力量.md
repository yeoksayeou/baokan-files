Title: The Soviet Union Celebrates Sports Festival, Inspecting the Indestructible Strength of the Soviet State
Author:
Date: 1947-07-28
Page: 3

　　Soviet Union Celebrates Sports Festival
  　Inspecting the Indestructible Strength of the Soviet State
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th] Moscow News: On the 20th, the Soviet people celebrated the Sports Festival with revelry throughout the Soviet Union. At the Dynamo Stadium in Moscow, 40,000 athletes representing sixteen republics held a sports parade, followed by various physical education performances and sports competitions. Young people from various European countries sent representatives to visit as invited guests, creating an unprecedented grand occasion. After the meeting, the Soviet government hosted a banquet for the participants of the Sports Festival in the Kremlin on the 22nd. As Stalin, Molotov, Zhdanov, Voroshilov, Andreev, Malenkov, Beria, Mikoyan, and Bulganin entered the venue, the 1,500 attendees greeted them with enthusiastic cheers. Molotov delivered the opening speech, first congratulating the new achievements of the Sports Festival on behalf of the Soviet government and the Central Committee of the Communist Party. He said: "During this festival, all the people of the Soviet Union see our youth getting stronger, becoming the indestructible force of our Soviet state. Stalin's concern and leadership have encouraged our youth, opening up a smooth road for the physical and mental development of the youth. Let us drink to Stalin's youth, to our glorious athletes, and to their creation of new records!" Amid thunderous applause, he continued: "Let us drink to the health of the great and beloved Comrade Stalin, who endows our country, our people, and our youth with the best qualities!" Molotov's speech ended in a storm of prolonged cheers from the audience. Subsequently, representatives of athletes from all over the Soviet Union spoke, guaranteeing that they would bring new success to the motherland in future world competitions.

### 苏联欢庆体育节　检阅苏维埃国家不可摧毁的力量

1947-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联欢庆体育节
  　检阅苏维埃国家不可摧毁的力量
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】莫斯科讯：二十日苏联人民在全苏各地狂欢庆祝体育节。莫斯科狄那摩运动场有代表十六个共和国的四万运动员举行体育游行，继之以各种优育表演与运动比赛。欧洲各国青年多派代表应邀参观，盛况空前。会后苏联政府于二十二日在克里姆林宫欢宴体育节的参加者。正当斯大林、莫洛托夫、日丹诺夫、伏罗希洛夫、安德列夫、马伦科夫、贝利亚、米高扬、布尔加宁等步入会场时，一千五百个与会者报以热烈的欢呼。莫洛托夫致开会词，首先代表苏联政府与联共党中央庆贺体育节的新成就。他说：“在这节日中，全苏联人民都看见我们的青年锻炼得更强壮，成为我们苏维埃国家不可摧毁的力量。斯大林的关切与领导鼓舞着我们青年，为青年的身心发展开辟了一条平坦的大道。我们为斯大林的青年，为我们光荣的运动员们，为他们创造新纪录而干杯！”在如雷的鼓掌声中，又接着说：“为那赋予我们国家、我国人民与青年最好品质的伟大的亲爱的斯大林同志的健康而干杯！”莫洛托夫的讲话在全场暴风雨一样的持久欢呼中结束。接着由全苏运动员代表发言，保证今后的世界竞赛中，能为祖国带来新的成功。
