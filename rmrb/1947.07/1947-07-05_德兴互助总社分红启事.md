Author:
Date: 1947-07-05
Page: 1

　　Dexing Mutual Aid Cooperative Dividend Announcement

    Our cooperative's eleventh dividend period will expire at the end of June. We are scheduled to begin distributing dividends on July 9th. Please bring your share certificates. If you entrust someone to draw dividends, the person or unit must attach a share certificate introduction from yourself or your unit. If you do not wish to draw the dividend but still wish to add the dividend to your shares, and cannot come due to work, you can contact Jin Yulong on Yangdao Street, Xingtai City, Dexing Store on Hanshan Road, Handan City, Dexing Mutual Aid Cooperative in Rencun, Dexing Mutual Aid Cooperative on Hero Street, Changzhi City, or Dexing Ironware Store in Yincheng to arrange for them to forward it to the head office, or write to us directly.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Council　He Baisha
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Manager　　　Yue Zengli
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Address: Henandian, She County

### 德兴互助总社分红启事

1947-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　德兴互助总社分红启事
    本社第十一分红期，六月底即届期满，兹定于七月九日开始分红，请各地社员携带股票前来。如托人抽股分红必须本人或本单位附带股票介绍抽取，如不分取，仍愿将红加股，因工作不能来时，可到就近邢台市羊道街晋豫隆，邯郸市邯山路德兴商店，任村德兴互助社，长治市英雄街德兴互助社，荫城德兴铁货庄接洽转交总社办理或直接来信为荷。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　理事主席　何白沙
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　经理　　　岳增理
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　住址：涉县河南店
