Title: Communist Party and Chairman Mao in Northern Jiangsu Folk Songs
Author:
Date: 1947-07-22
Page: 4

(I)

    Big glutinous rice, yellow and yellow,
    This year it becomes my grain.
    Thanks to the benefactor, the Communist Party,
    For helping us poor people with accounting and land distribution.

　　(II)

    The east-west land is long and long,
    Young San marries a tall girl,
    At home she can spin yarn,
    In the field she can help,
    Everyone sees it, happy and cheerful,
    They all say Young San has a strong fate.
    Young San shakes his head:
    "It's not about having a strong fate or not,
    It depends on the life-saving benefactor, the Communist Party,
    If it weren't for the land distribution,
    How could Young San marry such a tall girl?"

　　(III)

    Communist Party, live ten thousand years!
    Only with the Communist Party do the poor see the light of day.

　　(IV)

    As soon as the sun rises, it's red and bright,
    The sun is like Mao Zedong,
    Without the sun, grains won't grow,
    Without Chairman Mao, the poor will be poor for ten thousand years.

　　(V) Nursery Rhyme:

    Mao Zedong, warm and cozy,
    Everyone come, bow deeply.

　　(VI) An old woman's daily blessings offered to Chairman Mao's portrait:

    White rice cake, fragrant and delicious,
    Chairman Mao, please have a taste!
    If it weren't for your help,
    How could my family have fragrant rice cakes?
    　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency, Central China Dispatch)

### 苏北民谣中的　共产党和毛主席

1947-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏北民谣中的
  　共产党和毛主席
    （一）
　
    大釉稻，黄又黄，
    今年变成我的粮。
    感谢恩人共产党，
    算帐分田帮我们穷人忙。
　
　　（二）
　
    东西地长又长，
    小三娶个大姑娘，
    在家能纺纱，
    下地能帮忙，
    人人见，喜洋洋，
    都说小三命运强。
    小三摇摇头：
    “不是命运强不强，
    靠的救命恩人共产党，
    要不是分田和分地，
    那有我小三娶的大姑娘？
　
　　（三）
　
    共产党，万万年！
    有了共产党穷人才见天。
　
　　（四）
　
    太阳一出红通通，
    太阳好比毛泽东，
    五谷没太阳不生长，
    穷人没毛主席万年穷。
　
　　（五）童谣：
　
    毛泽东，暖烘烘，
    大家来，一鞠躬。
　
　　（六）老太婆供奉毛主席像每天祝词
　
    白米饼、喷喷香，
    毛主席，请你尝尝！
    不是你帮忙，
    我家那有米饼香？
    　　　　　　　　　（新华社华中电）
