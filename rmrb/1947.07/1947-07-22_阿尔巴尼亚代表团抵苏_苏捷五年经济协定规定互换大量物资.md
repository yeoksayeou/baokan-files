Title: Albanian Delegation Arrives in Soviet Union; Soviet-Czech Five-Year Economic Agreement Stipulates Exchange of Large Quantities of Goods

Author:

Date: 1947-07-22
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th: Moscow News: The Albanian delegation led by Hoxa, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Foreign Minister, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 14th, and Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov personally welcomed them. After arriving, Hoxa delivered a radio address stating that the Albanian people's ability to obtain a free, independent, and bright life today is entirely due to the heroic struggle and just support of the Soviet people. The Soviet Union is the protector of weak nations and a reliable guarantee of freedom and peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th: The Soviet-Czech five-year economic agreement stipulates the types and approximate quantities of goods to be exchanged between the two countries in 1948 as follows: The goods delivered by the Soviet Union to Czechoslovakia are: 200,000 tons of wheat, 300,000 tons of feed grains and corn, 65,000 tons of fertilizer, 20,000 tons of cotton, and other agricultural products. The goods delivered by Czechoslovakia to the Soviet Union are: rails, locomotives and other railway equipment, oil pipelines, equipment for the sugar industry, machine tools, generators, excavators, small power stations, bicycles, purebred livestock, shoes, socks, and textiles.

### 阿尔巴尼亚代表团抵苏　苏捷五年经济协定规定互换大量物资

1947-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚代表团抵苏
  　苏捷五年经济协定规定互换大量物资
    【新华社陕北十七日电】莫斯科讯：阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席兼外长霍查所率领之代表团，十四日飞抵莫斯科，苏外长莫洛托夫亲往欢迎。霍查到达后，即发表广播演说称：阿国人民今天所以能获得自由独立光明的生活全赖苏联人民英勇斗争与正义的支援。苏联是弱小民族的卫护者，是自由与和平的可靠保障。
    【新华社陕北十七日电】苏联与捷克的五年经济协定规定一九四八年两国互换物资之种类与大概数量如下：苏联同捷克交付之货物为：小麦二十万吨，饲料谷物及玉蜀黍三十万吨，肥料六万五千吨，棉花两万吨及其他农产品。捷克交付苏联之货物为：铁轨、机车及其他铁路装备、输油管、食糖工业之装备、机器工具、发电机、挖掘器、小电力站、自行车、纯种牲畜、鞋袜及纺织品等。
