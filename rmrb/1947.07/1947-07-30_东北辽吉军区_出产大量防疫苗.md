Title: Northeast Liaoji Military Region Produces Large Quantities of Vaccines
Author:
Date: 1947-07-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 26th: A certain pharmaceutical factory in the Liaoji Military Region of Northeast China has produced enough vaccines to prevent various infectious diseases for over 2,730,000 people since the beginning of summer. These include vaccines for cholera, typhoid, diphtheria, pertussis, anthrax, and gas gangrene, greatly benefiting both military personnel and civilians. In addition, vitamins, glucose, ephedrine injections, and emergency treatment drugs such as emergency fluid have also been produced in large quantities recently. Several tens of thousands of plaster bandages and first-aid kits specially made by the factory's technicians to support the front lines have been sent to the front.

### 东北辽吉军区　出产大量防疫苗

1947-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北辽吉军区
  　出产大量防疫苗
    【新华社东北二十六日电】辽吉军区某制药厂，入夏以来，已制成足可预防二百七十三万余人的各种传染病预防注射液，其中包括预防霍乱、伤寒、白喉、百日咳以及炭疽瓦斯坏疽等疾病，造福军民非浅。至于维他命、葡萄糖、麻黄素注射液，及紧急治疗的特效药急救水等药，近来亦有大量出品。该厂技师为支援前线所特制石膏绷带，救急包等若干万个，已陆续送往前方。
