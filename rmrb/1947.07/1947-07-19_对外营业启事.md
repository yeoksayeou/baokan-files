Business Announcement

Since its establishment, our factory has been dedicated to repairing and manufacturing machinery for the Fengfeng Coal Mine. Recently, to meet the needs of the liberated areas, we have decided to open to the public, with the aim of creating social wealth and contributing to the nascent industry of the liberated areas. Besides collecting fixed costs for labor and materials, we will not charge any other fees. Those who wish to repair, assemble, or manufacture all kinds of machine parts, as well as rivet small and large boilers, fans, and other components, are welcome to contact our factory in Fengfeng, Cixian.

Fengfeng Mining Area Machinery Repair Factory

### 对外营业启事

1947-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    对外营业启事
    本厂自成立以来，即专为峰峰煤矿修制机器。近为适应解放区之需要，特决定对外营业，以本创造社会财富，有助于解放区新生工业之宗旨。除收定额之工料费外，不收任何费用，希愿修装配制一切机器零件及铆大小锅炉风扇等零件者，请到磁县峰峰本厂接洽可也。
    峰峰矿区机器修理厂
