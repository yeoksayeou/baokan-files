Title: Chiang Kai-shek Clique Continues to Suppress Student Movement; Unjustly Dismisses Henan University Professors; Over Sixty Students Expelled

Author:

Date: 1947-07-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th. TASS Shanghai, 26th dispatch citing Ta Kung Pao: The Chiang family's Ministry of Education continues to adopt high-handed measures against the student anti-hunger and anti-civil war movement. Following the large-scale arrest of students at Henan University in Kaifeng the day before "June 2nd," four professors at Henan University were recently unjustly dismissed, and more than sixty students were also "dismissed" without cause.

### 蒋党继续箝制学生运动　无理解聘河大教授　学生六十余被开除

1947-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋党继续箝制学生运动
  　无理解聘河大教授
  　学生六十余被开除
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】塔斯社上海廿六日电引大公报讯：蒋家教育部续对学生反饥饿反内战运动采取高压。继“六二”前一日大举逮捕开封河南大学学生后，最近河大四位教授被无理解聘，学生六十余人亦无故“斥退”。
