Title: More than 1,400 Liberated Soldiers from Menglianggu Join Our Army
Author:
Date: 1947-07-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 28th: More than 1,400 soldiers of the Chiang Kai-shek's 74th Division, who laid down their weapons in the Battle of Menglianggu, have joined the People's Liberation Army under the influence of the PLA's education and transformation. In addition, of the more than 500 wounded officers of the said division who were captured, after treatment, one-third of those with minor injuries have had their wounds healed, and their health is gradually recovering.

### 孟良崮被解放士兵　千四百余参加我军

1947-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　孟良崮被解放士兵
  　千四百余参加我军
    【新华社华东前线二十八日电】孟良崮战役放下武器之蒋军七十四师士兵，在人民解放军感化教育下，续有一千四百余名参加解放军。又该师被俘之中下级负伤军官五百余名，经治疗，现轻伤者已有三分之一创口愈合，健康渐复。
