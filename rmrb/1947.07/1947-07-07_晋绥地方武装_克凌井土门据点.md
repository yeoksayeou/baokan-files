Title: Jin-Sui Local Armed Forces Capture Lingjing and Tumen Strongholds
Author:
Date: 1947-07-07
Page: 1

　　Jin-Sui Local Armed Forces
  　Capture Lingjing and Tumen Strongholds
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Sui, 4th] A unit of our local armed forces in Yangqu, on the night of the 21st of last month, captured the Lingjing stronghold of the Yan army, located seventy li northwest of Taiyuan City (the area is the gateway between the mountains of northwestern Shanxi and the plains of Taiyuan), wiping out a platoon of the 68th Division of the enemy, and capturing 18 enemy soldiers including the platoon leader. On the 22nd, another unit of our local armed forces attacked the Tumen stronghold of the Chiang army, located thirty li north of Linfen. The defending enemy of over a hundred fled into Linfen, and the area was liberated.

### 晋绥地方武装　克凌井土门据点

1947-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋绥地方武装
  　克凌井土门据点
    【新华社晋绥四日电】阳曲我地方武装一部，于上月二十一日夜，一举攻克太原市西北七十里之阎军据点凌井（该地系晋西北山地与太原平川间之孔道），歼敌六十八师一个排，俘排长以下十八名。二十二日我另一部地方武装向临汾以北三十里之蒋军据点土门进攻，守敌百余逃入临汾，该地遂告解放。
