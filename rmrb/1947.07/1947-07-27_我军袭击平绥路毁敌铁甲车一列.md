Title: Our Army Attacks the Ping-Sui Railway, Destroying an Enemy Armored Train

Author:
Date: 1947-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe, 24th—It's late: In the latter half of last month, part of the local armed forces of the Chahar East Liberation Army attacked the Ping-Sui Railway, destroying an enemy armored train.

### 我军袭击平绥路毁敌铁甲车一列

1947-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军袭击平绥路毁敌铁甲车一列
    【新华社冀察热辽二十四日电】迟到：上月下旬察东解放军地方武装一部，袭击平绥路，毁敌铁甲车一列。
