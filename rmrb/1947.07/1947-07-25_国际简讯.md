　　International Briefs
    ◇ U.S. Navy Commander at Yokosuka, Captain Dyke, recently told visiting American newspaper editors that the United States should, after the signing of a peace treaty with Japan, indefinitely maintain Yokosuka, Japan, as a U.S. naval base in Asia.
    ◇ A certain U.S. oil company has invested 150 million U.S. dollars in Turkey, stepping up the plunder of Turkish oil to compensate for its domestic oil shortage. It is reported that U.S. domestic oil resources will be exhausted in thirty-five years.

### 国际简讯

1947-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际简讯
    ◇美驻横须贺舰队司令戴克上校顷告访日之美报编辑称：美国应于对日和约签订后，将日本横须贺无限期作为美在亚洲之海军基地。
    ◇美某石油公司已在土耳其投资一亿五千万美元，加紧掠夺土国石油，以弥补其国内之油荒。据悉，美国内油源将在三十五年后枯竭。
