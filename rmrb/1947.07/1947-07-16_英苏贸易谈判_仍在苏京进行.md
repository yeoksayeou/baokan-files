Title: Anglo-Soviet Trade Negotiations Still Underway in the Soviet Capital
Author:
Date: 1947-07-16
Page: 3

    Anglo-Soviet Trade Negotiations
    Still Underway in the Soviet Capital
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th: According to the Associated Press, London, 11th: Anglo-Soviet trade negotiations are still underway in Moscow, and the British Minister for Overseas Trade, Wilson, remains in the Soviet capital. British government officials claim that the British side sincerely hopes that the Soviet side will supply as much as one million tons of wheat annually.

### 英苏贸易谈判  仍在苏京进行

1947-07-16
第3版()
专栏：

    英苏贸易谈判
    仍在苏京进行
    【新华社陕北十三日电】据美联社伦敦十一日讯：英苏贸易谈判仍在莫斯科进行中，英海外贸易部大臣威尔逊仍逗留苏京。英政府方面人士宣称：英方深愿苏方供给小麦至每年一百万吨之多。
