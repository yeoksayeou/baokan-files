Title: Local Armed Forces in Luzhong Annihilate Over 1,000 Enemy Troops in One Month
Author: 
Date: 1947-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 9th. In June, the local armed forces of Luzhong, while protecting the review of land reform and the summer harvest and planting, engaged in 410 battles against Chiang's puppet troops, killing or wounding 965 enemy troops below the rank of battalion commander, capturing 488 enemy troops, and seizing 5 light machine guns, 5 submachine guns, 184 long and short guns, and a quantity of other military supplies.

### 鲁中地方武装  一月歼敌千余

1947-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    鲁中地方武装
    一月歼敌千余
    【新华社山东九日电】鲁中地方武装六月份于保卫七地改革复查与夏收夏种中，对蒋伪作战四百一十余次，毙伤敌营长以下九百六十五名，俘敌四百八十八名，缴获轻机枪、冲锋枪各五挺，长短枪一百八十四支，其他军用品一部。
