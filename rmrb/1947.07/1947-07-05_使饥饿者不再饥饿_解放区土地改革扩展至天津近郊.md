Title: Ending Hunger for the Hungry: Land Reform in Liberated Areas Expands to Tianjin's Suburbs
Author:
Date: 1947-07-05
Page: 1

　　Ending Hunger for the Hungry
　　Land Reform in Liberated Areas Expands to Tianjin's Suburbs
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 1st】The land reform movement in the new district of Wuqing County has reached the suburbs of Tianjin. In the eighth district of the county, more than ten li from Tianjin, by the end of April, more than 4,000 landless and land-poor peasants in more than ten villages had obtained 21,000 mu of land, all rising to the level of middle peasants, and the rule of fifteen large feudal landlords in the area has now been completely smashed. The landlords in this area mostly own more than ten qing to eighty qing of land. This vigorous movement of Tianjin's suburban peasants has greatly inspired the broad masses of working people in Tianjin.

### 使饥饿者不再饥饿　解放区土地改革扩展至天津近郊

1947-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　使饥饿者不再饥饿
  　解放区土地改革扩展至天津近郊
    【新华社晋察冀一日电】武清县新区土地改革运动已达及天津市近郊。距津市十余里之该县八区二十余村，截止四月底，其中十余村四千多无地少地农民已获得土地二万一千亩，全部上升为中农，现已彻底打垮了当地十五户大封建地主的统治。该区地主多为拥地十余顷至八十余顷之大地主。津郊农民这一轰轰烈烈的翻身运动，大大激动了天津市内的广大劳苦人民。
