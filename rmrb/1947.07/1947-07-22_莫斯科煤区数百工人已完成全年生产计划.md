Title: Hundreds of Workers in the Moscow Coal Region Have Completed Their Annual Production Plans

Author:

Date: 1947-07-22
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th: Moscow dispatch: The socialist competition being carried out to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the October Revolution is flourishing throughout the Soviet Union and has led to new achievements in industrial production. In the first ten days of July, many factories in Leningrad completed their July plans and started work on their August plans. Some factories have even completed their August and even September plans. Factories in Moscow have similarly completed their July plans. Hundreds of workers in the Moscow coal region have miraculously completed their annual plans. Various parts of Latvia have completed their plans ahead of schedule, and factories in Riga have also begun their August plans. Fourteen coal mines in the Donets Basin have exceeded pre-war output. Coal trains from the Tula coal region, carrying coal exceeding the plan to Moscow, are arriving daily.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: According to a U.S. Information Service dispatch from Washington on the 10th, the Soviet government has paid the U.S. government $4.1 million, which is part of the interest due on July 10, 1947, under the U.S.-Soviet Lend-Lease Agreement of October 4th and 5th years. Negotiations have also begun regarding the liquidation of Lend-Lease between the two governments as stipulated in the Basic Lend-Lease Agreement of June 1942.

### 莫斯科煤区数百工人已完成全年生产计划

1947-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科煤区数百工人已完成全年生产计划
    【新华社陕北十九日电】莫斯科讯：为庆祝十月革命三十周年而举行的社会主义竞赛，正在苏联全国蓬勃展开中，且在工业生产上已得到新的成就。在七月的头十日内，列宁格勒许多工厂即已完成其七月份计划，并已开始八月份计划之工作。有若干工厂且已完成八月份乃至九月份之计划，莫斯科各工厂也已同样完成其七月份计划。莫斯科煤区数百工人更如奇迹似的完成了全年计划。拉胶维亚各地均提前完成计划，里加各工厂亦已开始八月份的计划。顿尼兹各盆地十四个煤矿已超过战前产量，自图拉煤区载运超过计划之煤斤至莫斯科之煤列车，日以“打”计。
    【新华社陕北十二日电】据美新闻处华盛顿十日电，苏联政府已以四百一十万美元付与美国政府，为根据四、五年十月美、苏租借协定于四七年七月十日到期应付利息之一部。又关于根据四二年六月基本租借协定中所规定的两国政府之清算租借谈判，业已开始。
