Title: Russian and Ukrainian Budgets for This Year Mostly Used for Social and Cultural Undertakings
Author:
Date: 1947-07-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd dispatch: TASS reports: The annual budgets for 1947 passed by the Supreme Soviets of Russia and Ukraine both allocate the largest portion to the development of social and cultural undertakings. In the Russian budget, expenditures amount to 46,100,500,002 rubles, of which 31.7 billion are used for this purpose. In the Ukrainian budget, expenditures amount to 14,839,990,000 rubles, of which two-thirds will also be used to meet the social and cultural needs of the people.

### 俄罗斯乌克兰今年预算　大部用于社会文化事业

1947-07-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　俄罗斯乌克兰今年预算
  　大部用于社会文化事业
    【新华社陕北三日电】塔斯社讯：俄罗斯及乌克兰两最高苏维埃会议所通过的一九四七年度预算，最大部分均用于发展社会与文化事业。俄罗斯预算中岁出为四六一亿零五百万零二千卢布，其中三一七亿用于这一方面。乌克兰岁出为一四八亿三九九万九千卢布，其中三分之二亦将用于满足人民的社会与文化的需要。
