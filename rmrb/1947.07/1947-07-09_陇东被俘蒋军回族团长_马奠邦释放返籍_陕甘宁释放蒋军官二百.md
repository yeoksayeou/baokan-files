Title: Captured Kuomintang Hui Regiment Commander Ma Dianbang from Longdong Released and Returned Home; Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Releases 200 Kuomintang Officers
Author:
Date: 1947-07-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, July 7th. A unit of the Northwest People's Liberation Army, on June 26th in a certain area of the Longdong sub-district in Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia, held a special Hui-style farewell banquet for Ma Dianbang, the commander of the 197th Regiment of the 60th Brigade under Ma Hongbin, sending him back to his hometown with travel expenses and a fine horse. Mr. Ma expressed his admiration for the People's Liberation Army's policy of respecting and supporting domestic ethnic minorities, as well as its firm commitment to striving for national independence, peace, and democracy. Mr. Ma is 40 years old, from Linxia County, Gansu Province, and started his career as a soldier. He laid down his arms during the Jiangtai battle in Huachi County. He lived in the People's Liberation Army for more than 20 days and received generous treatment according to Hui customs.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, July 2nd. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region's Officer Reception Office sent another 193 Kuomintang officers, liberated in the Panlong Campaign, back to their hometowns on June 18th. On the afternoon of the 17th, the Reception Office held a special farewell meeting. A deputy battalion commander said: "I have two gifts to give to everyone upon your return: a match and a grain of millet. The match represents the brightness of the border region; may you ignite the fire of brightness everywhere you go. The millet represents the common people; do not forget to serve the people after eating their grain."

### 陇东被俘蒋军回族团长　马奠邦释放返籍　陕甘宁释放蒋军官二百

1947-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　陇东被俘蒋军回族团长
  　马奠邦释放返籍
  　陕甘宁释放蒋军官二百
    【新华社西北前线七日电】西北人民解放军某部于上月二十六日，在陕甘宁陇东分区某地，设回教专席饯送马鸿宾部六十旅一九七围团长马奠邦返籍，并赠送给路费及骏马一匹。马氏对人民解放军尊重与扶助国内少数民族之政策及争取全国独立和平民主之坚定方针，极为赞佩。马氏现年四十岁，甘肃临夏县人，行伍出身，于华池县蒋台战斗中放下武器，在人民解放军中生活二十余日，备受按回教俗尚之优渥招待。
    【新华社西北二日电】陕甘宁边区解放军官，招待处于六月十八日，又遣送蟠龙战役解放之蒋军军官一百九十三名，返回原籍。十七日下午招待处特开欢送会，有一位副营长说：“大家回去我有礼物两件赠送，即是：一支火柴，一颗小米，火柴代表边区光明；你们回去到处燃起光明之火，小米代表老百姓，吃了老百姓的粮勿忘为人民服务。”
