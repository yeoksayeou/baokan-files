Title: Soviet Light Industry Increases Production, People's Lives Further Improved
Author:
Date: 1947-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th: Moscow dispatch: Soviet light industry has completed its half-year plan, exceeding it by 4 percent. The output in the second quarter of this year increased by 29 percent compared to the same period last year. Cotton fabric production increased by 42 percent, wool fabric by 30 percent, leather shoes by 42 percent, rubber shoes by 62 percent, and socks by 73 percent. As a result of the increased production, goods worth 32.5 billion rubles will be sold to residents by state-owned and cooperative retail stores. The stock of major commodities on the market this winter will increase by 36 percent compared to 1940, and the total retail sales of state-owned and cooperative stores will increase by 38 percent. In order to continue to improve the people's lives, the Soviet Ministry of Light Industry is currently rebuilding 200 factories that were destroyed by the war. Many factories have been equipped with new equipment and are adopting special methods to accelerate production.

### 苏联轻工业增产  人民生活更提高

1947-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联轻工业增产
    人民生活更提高
    【新华社陕北十三日电】莫斯科讯：苏联轻工业已完成半年计划，并超过百分之四。本年第二季产量比去年同一时期增加百分之二十九，棉织品生产增加百分之四十二，毛织品增加百分之三十，皮鞋增加百分之四十二，胶鞋增加百分之六十二，袜子增加百分之七十三。由于增产的结果，将有价值三千二百五十万万卢布之货物，由国营的与合作社的零售商店卖给居民。市场上主要货品的存贮，今冬将比一九四○年增加百分之三十六，国营和合作社商店的零售总额将提高百分之三十八。苏联政府为继续提高人民的生活，苏联轻工业部现正重新建筑二百所被战争破坏的工厂，许多工厂已装设新装备，并采用特殊方法以加速生产。
