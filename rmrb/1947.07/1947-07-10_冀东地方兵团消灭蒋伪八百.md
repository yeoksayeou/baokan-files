Title: Jidong Local Troops Eliminate 800 Chiang Kai-shek and Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 8th -- The Jidong People's Liberation Army local troops, in defense of the wheat harvest, recovered eight strongholds on the 28th of last month between Jixian and Sanhe counties, namely Dazhaozhuang, Baijian, Gaozhuangzi, and Babaihu, annihilating most of the stubborn and puppet armed forces such as the Chiang Kai-shek county government of Jixian, the Home Returning Corps, and the conscript team entrenched along the line. A total of 504 stubborn and puppet troops were killed or wounded, 271 were captured, and 26 light and heavy machine guns and more than 750 long and short guns were seized. On the same day, another unit of local armed forces crossed the Beining Railway and recovered the two Chiang Kai-shek and puppet strongholds of Daxiaoshentang on the coast of the Bohai Sea. In addition, the local troops of the Jizhong People's Liberation Army also swept the two lines from Longwan to Dongwu and Longwan to Sanjiadian on the west bank of Daqing River on the 29th of last month, destroying 23 Chiang Kai-shek and puppet bunkers. Two battalions of puppet troops fled upon hearing the news.

### 冀东地方兵团消灭蒋伪八百

1947-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀东地方兵团消灭蒋伪八百
    【新华社晋察冀八日电】保卫麦收之冀东解放军地方兵团，于上月二十八日收复蓟县、三河两县间之大赵庄、白涧、高庄子、八百户等八据点，将盘踞该线之蓟县蒋记县府、还乡团、壮丁队等顽伪武装大部歼灭。计毙伤顽伪五百零四人，俘二百七十一人，缴获轻重机枪二十六挺，长短枪七百五十余支，同日另部地方武装越过北宁路，收复渤海沿岸大小神堂两蒋伪据点。又：冀中解放军地方兵团亦于上月二十九日，扫荡大清西岸新城之龙湾至东务及龙湾至三家店两线，平毁蒋伪碉堡二十三座，伪军两个大队闻风逃窜。
