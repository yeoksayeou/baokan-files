Title: Jinsui Our Army Wins Counterattack, Captures Over 1,000 Yan Troops Alive
Author:
Date: 1947-07-09
Page: 1

　　Jinsui Our Army Wins Counterattack
  　Captures Over 1,000 Yan Troops Alive
    【Xinhua News Agency, Jinsui, 7th】On the night of the 3rd, a certain unit of the Jinsui People's Liberation Army launched a self-defense counterattack against the Yan troops' 72nd Division, 216th Regiment, in Xiaoyi Town, more than ten miles south of Wenshui City. By the next morning, the enemy was completely annihilated, and more than one regiment of Yan troops coming to the aid from Beijicheng Town in Fenyang was repelled. In this battle, a total of more than 1,300 people, including Qiu Ru＠, the commander of the 216th Regiment, and Zhang Su, the deputy commander, were captured. Five mountain guns and mortars, and fourteen heavy machine guns were seized. The regiment was newly replenished in southern Shanxi this spring, and was completely annihilated in less than half a year.

### 晋绥我军反击获胜　活捉阎军千余

1947-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋绥我军反击获胜
  　活捉阎军千余
    【新华社晋绥七日电】晋绥人民解放军某部三日夜，向文水城南十余里孝义镇等地之阎军七二师二一六团发动自卫反击，至翌晨即将该敌全部歼灭，并击退由汾阳北冀城镇来援之阎军一个多团。是役计俘二一六团团长邱如＠、副团长张苏以下一千三百余人，缴获山炮及迫击炮五门，重机枪十四挺。该团系今春在晋南新补充者，不及半载即被全歼。
