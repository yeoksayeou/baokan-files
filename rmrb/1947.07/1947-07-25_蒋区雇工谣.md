Title: Hired Laborer Rhyme in the Chiang Area
Author: Kong Zhuangpan, Zuo Minquan
Date: 1947-07-25
Page: 4

　　Hired Laborer Rhyme in the Chiang Area

　　Toil, toil, vow to not toil for the landlords.
　　Today’s landlords are much more stingy than those of the past.
　　Short stubble of dead wheat, short-handled ladle, trying to eat thick congee but can’t reach it.
　　Want a pinch of water tobacco, want a pinch of incense,
　　When you ask for money, they evade and dodge,
　　If you push hard, they give you two coppers to spend.
　　　　（Kong Zhuangpan, Zuo Minquan）

### 蒋区雇工谣
孔庄潘　佐民权
1947-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒋区雇工谣
    熬活，熬活，誓死不给地主熬活。
    现在的地主比过去更涩的多。
    死气麦茬短把杓，想吃稠的＠不着。
    要水烟一摄摄，要火香一撮撮，
    要钱去胡推胡躲，
    要的紧了，给两个抽。
    　　　　（孔庄潘、佐民权）
