Title: Hebei-Shanxi Rejoice in Rain, Sowing Completed
Author:
Date: 1947-07-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, July 1st: Most areas of western Hebei and northeastern Shanxi received rain in the latter part of last month, and the sowing of all major autumn crops has been completed.

### 冀晋喜雨播种完成

1947-07-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀晋喜雨播种完成
    【新华社晋察冀一日电】河北西部与山西东北部之大部地区上月下旬均已获雨，大秋作物已全部播种。
