Title: Shandong Our Army Recaptures Huandunbu
Author:
Date: 1947-07-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 7th. (Delayed) A unit of the East China People's Liberation Army recovered Huandunbu, an important town 48 miles southwest of Ganyu, north of the Longhai Railway, and its surrounding areas on the 26th of last month. The 156th Regiment of the 52nd Brigade of the 28th Division of the Chiang Kai-shek army, which was entrenched in the area, fled upon hearing the news, and the Chiang-backed puppet "Homecoming Corps" and others also disintegrated.

### 山东我军克欢墩埠

1947-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　山东我军克欢墩埠
    【新华社华东前线七日电】（迟到）华东人民解放军一部，上月二十六日收复陇海路北赣榆西南四十八里之重镇欢墩埠及其周围广大地区。盘踞该地之蒋军二十八师五十二旅一五六团闻风逃窜，蒋伪“还乡团”等亦纷纷瓦解。
