Title: Hungarian New President Árpád's Speech: Strive to Strengthen Domestic Unity and Establish Friendly Relations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1947-07-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, July 8th: Budapest - The Hungarian National Assembly unanimously elected Árpád as the new president on the 5th. Árpád was nominated by the Smallholders Party and supported by non-partisans. In his pre-inauguration speech, he stated that he would try to eliminate friction between parties, and said: "I am willing to do everything in my power to establish mutual understanding between Hungary and the Soviet Union."

### 匈牙利新总统阿帕演说  尽力加强国内团结与苏建立友好关系

1947-07-13
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利新总统阿帕演说
    尽力加强国内团结与苏建立友好关系
    【新华社陕北八日电】布达佩斯讯：匈国民议会于五日一致选举阿帕为新总统；阿氏是小农党提出并为无党派人士所赞同。他在就职前发表演说，表示将尽量消除党派间的摩擦，并称：“我愿尽一切可能的力量，建立匈牙利与苏联两国间之互相谅解。”
