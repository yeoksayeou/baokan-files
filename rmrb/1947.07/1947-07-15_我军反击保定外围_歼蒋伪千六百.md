Title: Our Army Counterattacks Outside Baoding, Annihilating 1,600 Chiang and Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-07-15
Page: 1

    Our Army Counterattacks Outside Baoding
    Annihilating 1,600 Chiang and Puppet Troops
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 13th] (Delayed dispatch) The People's Liberation Army local corps active around Baoding launched a counterattack against Chiang and puppet strongholds on the outskirts of Baoding during the ten-day period from June 27th to July 7th. They successively captured Suncun and Liushouying, about 15 *li* northeast of Baoding, and Nanbeiqi Village and Wujiazhuang, about 20 *li* to the west. A total of more than 1,600 enemy troops from Chiang's local armed forces and "Home Return Corps" in Qingyuan and Mancheng were annihilated, including over 700 captured alive, including Sheng Huanan, the puppet Mancheng police captain, and over 900 killed or wounded.

### 我军反击保定外围  歼蒋伪千六百

1947-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我军反击保定外围
    歼蒋伪千六百
    【新华社晋察冀十三日电】（迟到）活跃保定四周之解放军地方兵团，于上月二十七日至本月七日一旬间向保定外围蒋伪据点反击，先后攻克保定东北约十五华里之孙村、留守营及以西约二十华里之南北奇村、五家庄等处，歼敌清苑、满城之蒋记地方武装及“还乡团”等共千六百余人，计生俘伪满城保警大队长盛化南以下七百余，毙伤九百余。
