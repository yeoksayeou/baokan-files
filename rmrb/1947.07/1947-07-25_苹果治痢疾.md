　　Apple Cures Dysentery
    Zhu Maoji
    Recently, many people have been suffering from dysentery and diarrhea. Readers have written to inquire about treatment methods. I am now introducing a simple treatment method, please try it.
　　　　　　　　　　　　——Editor
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    Why can apples cure dysentery? This is because:
    A. Apples contain acids such as malic acid, malt acid, lipid acid, lactic acid, and tannic acid. Malt acid has antiseptic properties and can prevent the development of bacteria, while tannic acid has astringent properties and can stop diarrhea.
    B. The sugar in apples can generate heat and also increase colloidal properties, which can prevent vomiting and diarrhea.
    C. The fiber in apples can make the stool spongy, and it has a detoxification effect when passing through the intestines.
    D. Apples contain vitamins A, B, and C. Vitamin A protects the intestinal mucous membrane and helps it return to normal. Vitamin B can increase appetite and maintain the tension of the stomach. Vitamin C has a detoxifying effect.
    E. The more important reason is that it contains plant colloids. Plant colloids can absorb bacteria and toxins, absorb water, sweep away toxins in the intestines, form a kind of colloidal protective film, and have a buffering effect, which can maintain the normal alkaline reaction in the intestines.
    The method of using apples to treat dysentery is very simple. Just wash fresh apples with cold boiled water, wipe them dry, peel them, remove the cores, place them in a clean bowl, and grind them into a reddish-brown paste. Give the patient one to four small spoonfuls every hour, continuously for two days and two nights. During this period, abstain from other medicines and foods. Children can only drink warm boiled water, and adults can drink a little rice soup.

### 苹果治痢疾
朱茂辑
1947-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　苹果治痢疾
    朱茂辑
    近日患痢疾及泻肚子的很多，读者来函询问治法。兹介绍一个简易的治法，请大家试一试。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　——编者
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    为什么苹果可以治痢疾呢？这因为：
    甲、苹果里含有果酸、麦牙酸、脂酸、乳酸及单宁酸等酸类。麦牙酸有防腐作用，能防止细菌的发育，单宁酸能具有收敛性，能止泻。
    乙、苹果中的糖质能产生热力，还能增加胶质性，可以防止上吐下泻。
    丙、苹果中的纤维质，功用能使粪便变成海绵质，经过肠道时，便有解毒作用。
    丁、苹果含有维他命甲乙丙三种，甲种维他命有保护肠粘液膜使回复正常的作用。乙种维他命能增进食欲及维持肠胃之紧张力。丙种维他命有解毒作用。
    戊、更重大的原因，是因其含有植物胶质。植物胶质可吸收细菌及毒素，吸收水分，扫净肠中之毒物，形成一种胶性保护膜，并有缓冲作用，可以保持肠中碱性反应之正常。
    用苹果治痢的方法非常简单，只须把新鲜苹果用冷开水洗净，拭干水，剥皮去了核，放在清洁的钵子内，磨成红棕色的浆状物，每小时给病人服一至四小调羹，连服二日二晚。在此期间，禁食其他药品及食物。孩童只可饮温开水，成人略喝米汤。
