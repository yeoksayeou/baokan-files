Title: Inner Mongolia Establishes Party Committee
Author:
Date: 1947-07-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 21st: The Inner Mongolia Communist Party Working Committee has been established in Wangyemiao. The local people are extremely excited about this, unanimously believing that it will usher in a new situation for the Inner Mongolia autonomous movement. In recent days, people from all walks of life have been going to the Inner Mongolia Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to offer their congratulations, and congratulatory letters and telegrams from all over the region have been pouring in.

### 内蒙成立党委会

1947-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　内蒙成立党委会
    【新华社东北二十一日电】内蒙古共产党工作委员会已在王爷庙成立。当地人民对此兴奋异常，一致认为将使内蒙自治运动开创新的局面，连日来各界人士纷往中共内蒙党委致贺，各地祝贺函电如雪片飞来。
