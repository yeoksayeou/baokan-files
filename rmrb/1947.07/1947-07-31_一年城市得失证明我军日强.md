Title: One Year of City Gains and Losses Proves Our Army is Growing Stronger
Author:
Date: 1947-07-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th: The General Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army has released the fourth communiqué summarizing the war achievements of the past year, as follows: Phased statistics on city gains and losses throughout the year: (1) From July to October of last year, the enemy occupied 153 cities, and we recovered and liberated 48 cities. In comparison of gains and losses, we lost 105 cities. The lost cities included Chengde, the capital of Rehe Province, which was lost in August, Huaiyin, the capital of the Suwan Border Region, and Heze, the capital of the Jiluyu Region, which were lost in September, and Zhangjiakou, the capital of Chahar Province, which was lost in October. In October, the enemy occupied 63 of our cities (we recovered and liberated 9 cities), which was the highest number for the whole year. (2) From November of last year to February of this year, the enemy occupied 87 cities, and we recovered and liberated 87 cities, offsetting the gains and losses. The lost cities included Linyi, the capital of the Shandong Liberated Area. (3) From March to June of this year, the enemy occupied 95 cities, and we recovered and liberated 153 cities. In comparison of gains and losses, we gained 58 cities. The lost cities included Yan'an, the capital of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region, and the recovered cities included Anxian, the capital of Andong Province.

### 一年城市得失证明我军日强

1947-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　一年城市得失证明我军日强
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】人民解放军总部发表一年战绩总结第四号公报如下：全年城市得失分期统计：（一）去年七月至十月，敌侵占城市一百五十三座，我收复和解放四十八座；得失相较，我失一百零五座；失去城市中包括八月所失的热河省会承德，九月所失的苏皖边区首府淮阴，冀鲁豫区首府荷泽，十月所失的察哈尔省会张家口。十月敌占我城市六十三座（我收复和解放九座），为全年最高数字。（二）去年十一月至今年二月，敌侵占城市八十七座，我收复和解放八十七座，得失相抵；失去城市中包括山东解放区首府临沂。（三）今年三月至六月，敌侵占城市九十五座，我收复和解放一百五十三座；得失相较，我得五十八座；失去城市中包括陕甘宁边区首府延安，收复城市中包括安东省会安县。
