Title: Northwest Bureau Convenes Border Region-Level Party Congress, Calls for Doing a Good Job in Supporting the Front

Author:
Date: 1947-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 3rd. The Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee convened a Party Congress of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region to celebrate "July 1st." At the meeting, Deputy Secretary Ma Mingfang called on every party member to conscientiously support the front lines; Border Region Government Chairman Lin Boqu called on every party member to strictly abide by the party's program, policies, and resolutions, and to carry forward the traditional style of hard struggle; Deputy Commander of the Joint Defense Army Wang Weizhou called on every party member to become a model of observing mass discipline.

### 西北局召集边区级党员大会　号召做好支前工作

1947-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北局召集边区级党员大会　号召做好支前工作
    【新华社西北三日电】中共中央西北局召集陕甘宁边区一级党员大会，庆祝“七一”。会上马明芳副书记号召每个党员必须切实做好支援前线工作；边府林伯渠主席号召每个党员必须严格遵守党的纲领、政策、决议和发扬艰苦奋斗的传统作风；联防军副司令员王维舟则号召每个党员成为遵守群众纪律的模范。
