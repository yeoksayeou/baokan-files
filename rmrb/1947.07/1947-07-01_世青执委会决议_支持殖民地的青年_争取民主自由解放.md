Title: World Youth Executive Committee Resolution: Support Youth in Colonial Territories in Their Struggle for Democratic Freedom and Liberation
Author:
Date: 1947-07-01
Page: 3

　　World Youth Executive Committee Resolution
  　Supporting Youth in Colonial Territories
  　Striving for Democratic Freedom and Liberation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th] According to TASS, Moscow: At the recent meetings of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, reports were heard on the situation and needs of youth in colonial territories, as well as reports on the struggles of young men and women in China, Spain, Vietnam, and Indonesia for democracy, and a report from the Federation Secretariat on activities to strengthen youth cooperation in the struggle for peace. The committee unanimously agreed that the Secretariat had made significant contributions to expanding the Federation's connections with democratic youth organizations in various countries and resolved to support the struggles of working youth in colonial and dependent countries for national liberation, independence, democracy, and freedom. The committee also made decisions regarding the program for the International Youth Festival, which will begin in Prague, Czechoslovakia, in late July. It is reported that youth groups from forty-three countries have requested to participate in this Youth Festival. Preliminary estimates suggest that over 10,000 young men and women will participate in this grand event. The Executive Committee meeting adjourned on the 22nd.

### 世青执委会决议　支持殖民地的青年　争取民主自由解放

1947-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　世青执委会决议
  　支持殖民地的青年
  　争取民主自由解放
    【新华社陕北廿六日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界青年联盟执行委员会近几天的会议上，已听取了关于殖民地青年的情况与需要，及中国、西班牙、越南、印尼男女青年为争取民主的斗争之报告，以及联盟秘书处关于在为争取和平的斗争中巩固青年合作的活动之报告。该会一致认为，秘书处对于扩展本联盟与各国民主青年团体联系的工作上有很大的贡献，并决议支持殖民地与附属国家的劳动青年争取民族解放、独立、民主、自由的斗争。该会对其将于七月下旬开始在捷京布拉格举行之国际青年节的节目亦有所决定，据悉：已有四十三个国家的青年团体请求参加此届青年节。据初步估计，参加此一盛会的男女青年将有一万余人。执行委员会会议已于二十二日休会。
