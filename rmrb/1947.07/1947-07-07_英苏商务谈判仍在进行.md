Title: Anglo-Soviet Business Negotiations Still Underway
Author:
Date: 1947-07-07
Page: 3

　　Anglo-Soviet Business Negotiations Still Underway
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: London dispatch: The British Minister of Food announced on the 1st that Anglo-Soviet business negotiations are still underway in Moscow. It is extremely hopeful that an agreement can be reached between Britain and the Soviet Union as soon as possible; Britain will thus be able to exchange machinery for a large amount of grain from the Soviet Union.
    Another dispatch: The British House of Commons passed the Electricity Nationalization Bill on the 30th of last month with 321 votes against 173.

### 英苏商务谈判仍在进行

1947-07-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　英苏商务谈判仍在进行
    【新华社陕北四日电】伦敦讯：英粮食部大臣一日宣称：英苏商务谈判现仍在莫斯科进行，英苏及早成立协定，实极有希望；英国将因此可以机械向苏联换取大量粮食。
    又讯：英下院于上月三十日以三百二十一票对一百七十三票通过电力国营法案。
