Title: Literary News
Author:
Date: 1947-07-28
Page: 4

　　Literary News
　　◇ Writers Ouyang Shan and his wife recently arrived in this area from the Northwest. They have decided to join the comrades and work of the Border Region Federation of Literary and Art Circles. The Federation's literary and art workers are extremely excited upon hearing the news and are preparing a warm welcome. Comrade Ouyang Shan, while participating in the South District Cooperative work in Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia, spent more than a year, based on in-depth experience and collected materials, creating a lengthy novel of more than 100,000 words. The writing is accessible, and the content is rich. It has been submitted to the Xinhua Bookstore in this area for printing and should be published soon.
　　◇ Writer Hei Ding has recently gone to Hebei-Shandong-Henan to participate in the land reform movement.

### 文艺消息

1947-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　文艺消息
    ◇作家欧阳山夫妇，近由西北到达本区，已决定参加边区文联同志及工作，文联文艺工作者闻讯极为兴奋，准备热烈欢迎。欧阳山同志在陕甘宁参加南区合作社工作中，由深入体验及搜集之材料，费时年余，创作十余万字之长篇小说，文字通俗，内容丰富；已交本区新华书店付印，不日当可出版。
    ◇作家黑丁近已前赴冀鲁豫，参加土地改革运动。
