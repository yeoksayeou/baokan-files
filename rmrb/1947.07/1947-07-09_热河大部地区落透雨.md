Title: Heavy Rain Throughout Most of Rehe Area
Author:
Date: 1947-07-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Jicha-Rehe-Liaoning, 6th. Since the last ten days of last month, most areas of Rehe Province have had heavy rain, and the seedlings in both southern and northern Rehe are recovering well. According to experienced farmers, an 80% harvest is expected this year.

### 热河大部地区落透雨

1947-07-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　热河大部地区落透雨
    【新华社冀察热辽六日电】热河省大部地区上月下旬以来均落透雨，热南热北禾苗均庆复苏。据老农说：今年可望八成收获。
