Title: Additional War Results from Baobei Campaign: Over 7,000 Enemies Annihilated
Author:
Date: 1947-07-08
Page: 1

　　Additional War Results from Baobei Campaign
  　Over 7,000 Enemies Annihilated
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 6th] The Jin-Cha-Ji People's Liberation Army Field Headquarters recently issued a supplementary communique on the Baoding North (Baobei) Campaign from June 26th to 28th, stating: During the three days of combat, our army completely annihilated the entire 362nd Regiment of the 121st Division of the 94th Army of the Chiang Kai-shek forces, and the entire 325th Regiment of the 109th Division of the 16th Army, both American-equipped regiments; the majority of two companies and their artillery company of the 363rd Regiment of the 121st Division; and one battalion of Liu Hua's troops of the puppet Security Third Division. 2,817 Chiang Kai-shek soldiers, including battalion commanders and below, were killed or wounded, and 4,468 were captured, including Zhang Jianqiu, the commander of the 362nd Regiment, for a total of 7,285 Chiang Kai-shek and puppet troops killed, wounded, or captured. 67 various American-style artillery pieces, 230 light and heavy machine guns, more than 2,800 rifles and carbines, more than 475,000 rounds of ammunition, three trucks, one jeep, and some other military supplies were seized.

### 保北战役补充战果　共歼敌七千余

1947-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　保北战役补充战果
  　共歼敌七千余
    【新华社晋察冀六日电】晋察冀人民解放军野战司令部顷发表六月二十六日至二十八日保（定）北战役补充公报称：三天作战中，我军共歼灭蒋军九十四军一二一师之三六二团与十六军一○九师之三二五团两个美械团全部，一二一师三六三团两个连及其机炮连大部，另伪保安三师刘化南部一个营。毙伤蒋军营长以下二千八百一十七人，生俘三六二团团长张剑秋以下四千四百六十八人，总计毙伤俘蒋伪军七千二百八十五人。缴各种美式炮六十七门，轻重机枪二百三十挺，步马枪二千八百余支，子弹四十七万五千余发，卡车三辆，吉普车一辆，其他军用品一部。
