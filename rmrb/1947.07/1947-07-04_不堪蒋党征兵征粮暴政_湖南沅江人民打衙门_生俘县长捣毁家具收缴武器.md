Title: Unable to Endure the Kuomintang's Tyrannical Conscription of Soldiers and Grain, Yuanjiang People of Hunan Attack the Yamen, Capture the County Magistrate Alive, Smash Furniture, and Seize Weapons
Author:
Date: 1947-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th: Shanghai newspapers report: The people of Yuanjiang County, Hunan, unable to endure the tyrannical conscription of soldiers and grain by the Chiang government, gathered hundreds of people on May 17th, all wielding farm tools and wooden sticks, stormed into the Chiang-controlled county government, captured County Magistrate Wang Yifan alive, smashed all the furniture, and seized all the guns and weapons before leaving.

### 不堪蒋党征兵征粮暴政　湖南沅江人民打衙门  生俘县长捣毁家具收缴武器

1947-07-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　不堪蒋党征兵征粮暴政
  　湖南沅江人民打衙门
    生俘县长捣毁家具收缴武器
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】沪报讯：湖南沅江县人民不堪蒋政府征兵征粮暴政，五月十七日聚众数百人，齐执农具木棍，冲入蒋记县政府，生俘县长王一凡，捣毁全部家具，收缴全部枪支武器而去。
