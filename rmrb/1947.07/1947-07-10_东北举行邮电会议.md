Title: Northeast Holds Postal and Telecommunications Conference
Author:
Date: 1947-07-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 7th. The first Northeast Postal and Telecommunications Conference, lasting nine days, concluded at the end of last month. According to statistics from the conference, the Northeast already has more than 130 postal and telecommunications agency offices, 140 postal and telecommunications bureaus, postal routes reaching 4,324 kilometers, and telecommunications reaching 6,695 kilometers, with a line length of 2,677 kilometers. At the conference, representatives from various regions, in addition to summarizing the achievements of the past year, made detailed discussions on developing postal and telecommunications work in the newly recovered areas and other issues under the new situation.

### 东北举行邮电会议

1947-07-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北举行邮电会议
    【新华社东北七日电】东北第一届邮电会议，历时九天，已于上月底闭幕。据会议统计东北已有邮电代办所一百三十余，邮电局一百四十，通邮路长四千三百二十四公里，电讯通达六千六百九十五公里，线长二千六百七十七公里。会议上各地代表除总结一年来工作成绩之外，对新形势下开展新收复区邮电工作及其他问题，曾作详尽之讨论。
