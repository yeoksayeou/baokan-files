Title: Northeast Democratic Allied Army Transfers to Enemy Rear, Annihilating Over a Thousand Chiang Kai-shek Puppet Bandit Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-07-28
Page: 1

　　Northeast Democratic Allied Army Transfers to Enemy Rear
  　Annihilating Over a Thousand Chiang Kai-shek Puppet Bandit Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 26th (Delayed Dispatch) The democratic allied forces in the enemy's rear in West Manchuria, South Manchuria, and Rehe have annihilated over a thousand Chiang Kai-shek troops and puppet bandits in the first and middle ten days of this month. On the 8th, the West Manchuria Democratic Allied Army annihilated parts of the 3rd and 12th Divisions of Mongol bandits in the area south of Faku, capturing over 350 enemy officers and soldiers, including the Deputy Director of the Political Department and the commander and deputy commander of the enemy, killing and wounding over 190 enemy soldiers including the commander of the regiment and below, and seizing six light machine guns and over 200 rifles. On the 15th, a unit of the South Manchuria Democratic Allied Army attacked Jiguan Mountain, northeast of Fushun, annihilating a company of the 3rd Regiment of the New 6th Army's 207th Division, capturing over 120 enemy soldiers alive, and seizing one mortar, three 60mm mortars, eight light machine guns, and over 120 rifles. On the 4th, the Rehe Democratic Allied Army suppressed Chiang Kai-shek bandits in Shuangmiao and Longwangmiao areas west of Chaoyang, capturing over 370 enemy soldiers, killing or wounding over 50 enemy soldiers, and seizing over 270 long and short guns.

### 东北民主联军转战敌后　歼灭蒋伪匪军千余

1947-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北民主联军转战敌后
  　歼灭蒋伪匪军千余
    【新华社东北二十六日电】（迟到）西满、南满及热河敌后民主联军，本月上、中旬共歼灭蒋军及伪匪一千余名。西满民主联军八日于法库以南地区，歼灭蒙匪第三师及十二师各一部，俘敌政治部副主任、正副团长以下官兵三百五十余名，毙伤敌团长以下一百九十余名，缴获轻机枪六挺，步枪二百余支。十五日，南满民主联军一部，向抚顺东北之鸡冠山进击，歼敌新六军二○七师第三团一个连，生俘一百二十余名，缴获迫击炮一门，六○炮三门，轻机枪八挺，步枪一百二十余支。热河民主联军四日在朝阳西之双庙、龙王庙一带进剿蒋匪，俘敌三百七十余名，死伤敌五十余名，缴获长短枪二百七十余支。
