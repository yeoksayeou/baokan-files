Title: East China Liberated Area Captures More Than 500 Chiang Kai-shek Puppet Troops and Special Agents at Dakou

Author:

Date: 1947-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, July 17th: The East China People's Liberation Army, after capturing Tai'an City, continued to clear out Chiang Kai-shek's puppet troops on both sides of the Jinpu Railway. On the dawn of the 10th, they recovered Dakou, a strategic location on the Jinpu Railway (50 li south of Tai'an). The First Garrison Brigade under the jurisdiction of Chiang Kai-shek's Fourth Pacification Headquarters in Shandong fled upon hearing the news. The People's Liberation Army captured a total of 560 Chiang Kai-shek puppet troops and special agents from the "Extermination Team," "Homecoming Corps," and other organizations.

### 华东克大汶口  生擒蒋伪蒋特五百余

1947-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    华东克大汶口
    生擒蒋伪蒋特五百余
    【新华社华东前线十七日电】攻克泰安城之华东人民解放军继续扫清津浦路两侧蒋伪军，十日拂晓收复津浦路上战略要地大汶口（在泰安以南五十里）。蒋记山东第四绥署所辖之警备第一旅闻风逃窜。解放军俘获蒋伪蒋特“除奸队”、“还乡团”等共五百六十名。
