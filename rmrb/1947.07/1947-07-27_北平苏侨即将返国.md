Title: Soviet Nationals in Beiping to Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1947-07-27
Page: 1

　　Soviet Nationals in Beiping to Return Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd] According to a Central News Agency dispatch from Beiping on the 21st: A group of 195 Soviet nationals are scheduled to leave Beiping for Tianjin and then return home before the end of the month. The list of Soviet nationals returning home has been handed over to the Kuomintang authorities. The Soviet Consul General in Beiping, Ledovsky, will also accompany them back to his country.

### 北平苏侨即将返国

1947-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平苏侨即将返国
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】据中央社北平二十一日电：苏联侨民一部一百九十五名，定月底前撤离北平转津返国。返国苏侨名单已交国民党当局，届时苏驻平总领事列多夫斯基亦将偕同返国。
