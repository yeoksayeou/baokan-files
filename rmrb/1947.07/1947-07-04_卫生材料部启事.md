　　Health Materials Department Announcement

　　For the convenience of our work and to supply military and civilian needs, our department has a retail department, offering both retail and wholesale, all are welcome. To facilitate medical care for merchants and civilians, an adjacent clinic is available, providing daily consultations with simple procedures and affordable medicine prices. Customers are most welcome.
　　　Address: Moved from Xingtai to No. 118, South Side of Heping West Avenue, Handan Railway Station Area

### 卫生材料部启事

1947-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　卫生材料部启事
    敝部为了工作便利，供应军需民用，特设有门市部，零整批发，无不欢迎。便利商民医疗，隔壁附有诊疗所，每日施诊，手续简便，药价低廉。主顾驾临欢迎之至。
　　　地址——由邢台移到邯郸车站区和平西大街路南门牌一一八号
