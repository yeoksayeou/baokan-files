Title: News from the Chiang Kai-shek Controlled Areas
Author:
Date: 1947-07-24
Page: 1

　　News from the Chiang Kai-shek Controlled Areas
    ◇Due to the grain shortage in Changchun, prices have been soaring daily, causing extreme social unrest and frequent robberies. According to statistics from the Chiang government's security authorities, eleven robbery cases occurred in nine days during the month of May.
    ◇Zang, the wife of Qingdao policeman Li Guiqing, was unable to survive due to rising prices. She sold her own son, who was less than five months old, in exchange for a bag of coarse flour and 50,000 legal tender (Chinese currency at the time) to prolong her life.

### 蒋区零讯

1947-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋区零讯
    ◇长春粮荒，物价连日狂涨，社会极端不安，抢案迭出。据蒋政府治安当局统计，在五月份内九日中发生了抢案十一起。
    ◇青岛警察李贵清之妻藏氏，因物价上涨无法生活，将不满五月之亲生子出卖，换来粗面一袋，法币五万元，以延残生。
