Title: Nine-Month Battle Achievements in Hebei-Rehe-Chahar: Over 11,000 Enemies Eliminated
Author:
Date: 1947-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 2nd: The Hebei-Rehe-Chahar Military Region Command recently announced the following battle results from September of last year to May of this year: A total of 221 battles were fought, resulting in 6,833 enemy soldiers killed or wounded and 4,231 captured. The total number of enemies killed, wounded, or captured amounts to 11,064. The following were seized: 49 cannons of various types, 133 light and heavy machine guns, 3,809 rifles and carbines, over 300,300 rounds of various types of ammunition, 1,190 artillery shells, over 1,200 war horses, 11 radio stations, and 60 vehicles. 110 bunkers were destroyed, and one aircraft was shot down.

### 冀热察九个月战绩  共歼敌一万一千余

1947-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀热察九个月战绩
    共歼敌一万一千余
    【新华社晋察冀二日电】冀热察军区司令部顷公布自去年九月至今年五月战果如下：共作战二百二十一次，毙伤敌六千八百三十三名、俘敌四千二百三十一名。统计毙伤俘一万一千零六十四名。缴获各类型炮四十九门，轻重机枪一百三十三挺，长短枪三千八百零九支，各种子弹三十万三千余发，炮弹一千一百九十发，战马一千二百余匹，电台十一部，汽车六十辆，摧毁碉堡一百一十座，击落飞机一架。
