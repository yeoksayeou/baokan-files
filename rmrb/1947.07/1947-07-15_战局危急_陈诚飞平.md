Title: The War Situation is Critical; Chen Cheng Flies to Beiping
Author:
Date: 1947-07-15
Page: 1

    The War Situation is Critical
    Chen Cheng Flies to Beiping
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th—News from Beiping on the 11th: Chen Cheng, one of the main culprits of the civil war and Chief of Staff of the Kuomintang, is currently holding a meeting in Beiping with commanders and political officials from North China. He will leave Beiping for Shenyang in the Northeast on the morning of the 12th. It is widely believed that Chen's hurried travels are related to the critical situation of the Kuomintang army in North China and the Northeast.

### 战局危急  陈诚飞平

1947-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    战局危急
    陈诚飞平
    【新华社陕北十二日电】北平十一日消息：内战祸首之一、国民党参谋总长陈诚正在北平召开一华北指挥官与政治官员的会议，彼将于十二日晨离平赴东北沈阳。众信陈氏之匆忙奔波，与国民党军在华北、东北所处之危急情势有关。
