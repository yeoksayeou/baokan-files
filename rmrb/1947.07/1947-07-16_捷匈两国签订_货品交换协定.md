Title: Czechoslovakia and Hungary Sign Goods Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1947-07-16
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: Prague dispatch: Czechoslovakia and Hungary have signed a goods exchange agreement. Czechoslovakia will supply Hungary with coking coal, pig iron, steel, and other raw materials in exchange for fruits, poultry, and other agricultural products.

### 捷匈两国签订  货品交换协定

1947-07-16
第3版()
专栏：

    捷匈两国签订
    货品交换协定
    【新华社陕北十二日电】布拉格讯：捷克和匈牙利已签订货品交换协定、捷克将以焦煤、生铁、钢及其他原料供给匈牙利，以换水果家禽及其他农产物。
