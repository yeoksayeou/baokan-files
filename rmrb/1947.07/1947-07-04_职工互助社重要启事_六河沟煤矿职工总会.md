Title: Important Announcement from the Employee Mutual Aid Society, Six River Gulch Coal Mine Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1947-07-04
Page: 1

　　Important Announcement from the Employee Mutual Aid Society, Six River Gulch Coal Mine Trade Union

　　Our society is ordered to close. Accounts were settled at the end of June this year. We hope all shareholders will bring their stock certificates to withdraw shares and receive dividends. We also hope that those with relevant procedures will come for clearance within July. We will not be responsible for any matters after the deadline. This is a special announcement.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Manager Wang Yushi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Address: Caoshi Street, Pengcheng Town, Cixian County

### 职工互助社重要启事　六河沟煤矿职工总会

1947-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　职工互助社重要启事　六河沟煤矿职工总会
    本社奉命结束，在本年六月底结帐，希各股员持股票前来抽股分红，并希有关手续者，在七月内前来清理，过期概不负责，特此声明。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　经理　王雨时
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　地址：磁县彭城镇草市街
