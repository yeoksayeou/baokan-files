Title: Romanian Red Cross Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th. A five-member women's delegation from the Central Committee of the Romanian Red Cross Society, led by Ekaterina Stoica, First Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Red Cross and wife of Romanian Council of Ministers Chairman Stoica, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

The delegation was passing through Beijing on its way to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for a friendly visit.

### 罗马尼亚红十字会妇女代表团到京

1957-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚红十字会妇女代表团到京
    新华社6日讯　罗马尼亚红十字会总会中央委员会妇女代表团一行五人，在罗马尼亚红十字会第一副主席、罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡的夫人埃卡特丽娜·斯托伊卡率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团是在前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国作友好访问途中路过北京的。
