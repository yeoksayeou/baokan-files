Title: British Troops Frequently Invade the Border, Yemeni People Stand Ready

Author:

Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 6

British Troops Frequently Invade the Border
Yemeni People Stand Ready
Since the failure of the aggression against Egypt, British troops have been launching armed attacks on the Yemeni border from the "Aden Protectorate" for months, attempting to seize new strategic positions and oil deposits in Yemen. However, the Yemeni people, like the Egyptian people, are not to be bullied. They bravely defend their homeland and deal a head-on blow to the invading British troops.

### 英军经常侵犯边境  也门人民严阵以待（图片）

1957-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    英军经常侵犯边境
    也门人民严阵以待
    自侵略埃及失败后，英国军队连月以来又不断从“亚丁保护地”对也门边境发动武装进攻，企图在也门夺取新的战略阵地和石油矿藏。但是也门人民也和埃及人民一样是不可欺侮的，他们英勇地捍卫着自己的国土，给予进犯的英军以迎头痛击。
