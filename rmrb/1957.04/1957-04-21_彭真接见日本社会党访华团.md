Title: Peng Zhen Receives Japanese Socialist Party Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1957-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th, Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing, received the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission headed by Inejiro Asanuma this afternoon.

Vice Mayor Wang Kunlun was also present at the meeting.

### 彭真接见日本社会党访华团

1957-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真接见日本社会党访华团
    新华社20日讯　北京市市长彭真今天下午接见以浅沼稻次郎为首的日本社会党访华亲善使节团。
    接见的时候，副市长王昆仑也在座。
