Title: Ehrenburg Visits Japan, Will Give Lectures in Tokyo and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1957-04-08
Page: 3

    Ehrenburg Visits Japan
    Will Give Lectures in Tokyo and Other Places
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th - Soviet famous writer Ehrenburg arrived in Tokyo by plane today.
    Ehrenburg was invited by the Japan-Soviet Friendship Association for a three-week visit to Japan. Since many of his works have been translated and published in Japan, his name is very familiar to the Japanese people. During his visit to Japan, Ehrenburg will give literary lectures in Tokyo, Osaka, Nagoya and other places.

### 爱伦堡访问日本  将在东京等地演讲

1957-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

    爱伦堡访问日本
    将在东京等地演讲
    新华社7日讯　东京消息：苏联著名作家爱伦堡今天乘飞机到达东京。
    爱伦堡应日苏亲善协会的邀请，到日本作为时三周的访问。由于他的很多著作已经在日本翻译出版，他的名字是日本人民十分熟悉的。在访问日本期间，爱伦堡将在东京、大阪、名古屋等地发表文学演讲。
