Title: Helicopter (Photos)
Author: Ling Yun, Hu Baoyu
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 4

Helicopter

    Helicopters have diverse capabilities. They can take off and land in any terrain without relying on airports. Even if landing is impossible in extremely specific terrain conditions, they can hover in the air and use rope ladders for loading and unloading.
    Right picture: During a combat exercise, PLA infantry soldiers descend from a helicopter using rope ladders. Photo by Ling Yun (Manuscript from the PLA Pictorial)
    Bottom picture: Artillery has also arrived. Photo by Hu Baoyu (Manuscript from the PLA Pictorial)

### 直升飞机（图片）
凌云  胡宝玉
1957-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    直升飞机
    直升飞机具有多样的性能，它可以不依赖机场在任何地形条件下起飞或降落；即使在极特殊的地形条件下不能降落，也可以停悬在空中，利用软梯进行装卸。
    右图：在战斗演习中，解放军步兵战士从直升飞机上利用软梯着陆。　凌　云摄（解放军画报社稿）
    下图：火炮也运来了。　胡宝玉摄（解放军画报社稿）
