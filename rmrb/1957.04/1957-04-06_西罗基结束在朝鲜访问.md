Title: Siroky Ends Visit to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 5th. Premier Viliam Široký and the Czechoslovak government delegation he led concluded their visit to North Korea and left Pyongyang by plane this morning. They will travel through China to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Kim Tu-bong and other government and party leaders, responsible persons from various democratic parties and social organizations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to North Korea saw them off at the airport. The roadsides from downtown Pyongyang to the airport were lined with crowds of people seeing them off.

Premier Široký delivered a farewell speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude to the Korean people for their warm hospitality to the delegation. He said that during the delegation's visit to North Korea, they witnessed the achievements of the Korean people in restoring construction and were deeply impressed. The joint statement signed by the representatives of the two governments yesterday is of great significance for the friendship between the two countries and the cause of peace. He said that we will work to further strengthen the friendship between the two countries.

In addition to staying in Pyongyang, the delegation also visited Hamhung City, the Hungnam Fertilizer Factory, and the Heechon Machine Manufacturing Factory.

### 西罗基结束在朝鲜访问

1957-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    西罗基结束在朝鲜访问
    新华社平壤5日电　西罗基总理和他率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团结束了在朝鲜的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开平壤。他们将经过中国前往蒙古人民共和国访问。
    金日成首相、金枓奉委员长等政府和党的领导人，各民主政党和社会团体负责人，各国驻朝使节到飞机场欢送。从平壤市区到飞机场的道路两旁，排满了欢送的人群。
    西罗基总理在飞机场上临别时讲了话，他对朝鲜人民对代表团的热情款待表示感谢。他说，代表团访问朝鲜期间看到了朝鲜人民恢复建设的成就，留下了深刻的印象。两国政府的代表在昨天签署的联合声明，对两国的友谊和和平事业具有重大的意义。他说，我们将为进一步加强两国的友谊而努力。
    代表团访问朝鲜期间除了在平壤逗留以外，还曾经到咸兴市、兴南肥料工厂和熙川机器制造工厂等地进行了参观访问。
