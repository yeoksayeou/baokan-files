Title: Egypt Allocates 100,000 Egyptian Pounds to Aid Suffering Residents of Gaza
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th: The chairman of the Gaza Municipal Committee, Munir al-Rais, told reporters on the 14th that President Nasser had informed him that the Egyptian government had decided to allocate 100,000 Egyptian pounds to aid residents of the Gaza Strip who had been ravaged by the Israeli aggressors.

President Nasser met with al-Rais on the 14th, and al-Rais reported to the President on the situation in Gaza.

### 埃及决拨十万埃镑  救济加沙受难居民

1957-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及决拨十万埃镑
    救济加沙受难居民
    新华社16日讯　开罗消息：加沙市政委员会主席穆尼尔·拉伊斯14日对记者说，纳赛尔总统通知他，埃及政府决定拨十万埃镑来救济加沙地区遭受以色列侵略者蹂躏的居民。
    纳赛尔总统曾经在14日接见了拉伊斯，拉伊斯向总统报告了加沙情况。
