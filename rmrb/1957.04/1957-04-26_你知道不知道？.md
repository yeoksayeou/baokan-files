Title: Did you know?
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 3

Column: Small Statistics

    Did you know?
    △ By the end of 1956, there were 100,432 handicraft cooperatives (groups) nationwide.
    △ Currently, there are more than 4.7 million members in handicraft cooperatives (groups) nationwide, accounting for 59.4% of the total number of workers in the country.
    △ The total value of handicraft production in 1956 was 7.6 billion yuan, exceeding the 1957 target of the First Five-Year Plan by more than 60%.
    △ In rural markets, handicrafts account for 80% of the goods supplied.
    △ The planned output value of handicrafts nationwide in 1957 will increase by 13.7% compared to 1956.

### 你知道不知道？

1957-04-26
第3版()
专栏：小统计

    你知道不知道？
    △截至1956年底，全国共有手工业合作社（组）十万零四千三百二十二个。
    △现在全国共有手工业合作社
  （组）员四百七十多万人，占全国工人总数的59.4％。
    △1956年手工业生产总值共七十六亿元，超过第一个五年计划1957年指标的60％以上。
    △在农村市场上，手工业供应的商品占80％。
    △1957年全国手工业的计划产值比1956年增加13.7％。
