    Photography Art
    Today, we have selected several beautiful photographic art works with different styles to publish. These are some of the exhibits from the "Guangzhou-Hong Kong-Macau Photographers' Works Exhibition" which will soon be held in Beijing.
    The works exhibited this time are selected masterpieces from the "1957 Guangzhou Photography Art Exhibition," totaling 224 works by 98 new and veteran photographers. These works either sing praises of the country's construction or reproduce the country's beautiful scenery, with lifelike portraits, and fascinating flowers, birds, and still lifes. Many works have reached a fairly high level in terms of subject matter, lighting, color, expressive techniques, and production. This exhibition will help to promote photographic art creation and encourage photographers and photography enthusiasts to work hard to improve their artistic level. (Attached pictures)

### 摄影艺术

1957-04-03
第8版()
专栏：

    摄影艺术
    今天，我们选刊了几幅各具风格的优美的摄影艺术作品。这是即将在京展出的“穗港澳摄影家作品展览”的部分展品。
    这次展出的作品，是从“1957年广州摄影艺术展览会”上精选出来的佳作，共有九十八位新老摄影家的作品二百二十四幅。这些作品或者歌颂祖国的建设，或者再现祖国的绮丽风光，有栩栩如生的人像，也有引人入胜的花鸟、静物。许多作品，在选题、用光、色调、表现手法和制作上，达到相当高的水平。这次展出，将有助于繁荣摄影艺术创作、鼓励摄影工作者和摄影爱好者努力提高艺术水平。（附图片）
