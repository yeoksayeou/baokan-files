Title: People Throughout the Soviet Union Solemnly Commemorate Lenin's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 5

    People Throughout the Soviet Union
    Solemnly Commemorate Lenin's Birthday
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 23rd: More than two thousand representatives from the Moscow City Party, Soviet, social organizations, and people from all walks of life gathered at the Bolshoi Theatre of the Soviet Union on the evening of April 22nd to solemnly commemorate the 87th anniversary of Lenin's birth.
    Soviet Party and government leaders Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Zhukov, Furtseva, Shepilov, as well as some old Bolsheviks, attended the celebration.
    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, presided over the meeting.
    The meeting began with the playing of the Soviet national anthem. Then, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Furtseva, delivered a report on celebrating the 87th anniversary of Lenin's birth.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: TASS reported that people throughout the Soviet Union solemnly commemorated the 87th anniversary of Lenin's birth on April 22nd.
    On this day, Moscow was sunny and bright. Early in the morning, Muscovites and visitors to the capital came to Red Square and to the Mausoleum to see, once again, the face of this great man who fought his whole life for the happiness of millions of ordinary people. At 2:45 PM, a delegation from Lenin's hometown, Ulyanovsk, laid a wreath at the Mausoleum. Following closely behind was a procession of veteran Bolsheviks. Some of them had participated in the revolutions of 1905 and 1917, and some had worked with Lenin and carried out the tasks entrusted to them. The crowd of people coming to pay their respects was endless. In Moscow's enterprises, institutions, and universities, grand meetings were held to commemorate this significant day.
    In Leningrad, on April 22nd, tens of thousands of workers and collective farm members from the Leningrad region visited places related to Lenin's life. The Leningrad City Party, Soviet, and social organizations held a commemorative meeting at the Kirov Opera and Ballet Theatre that evening.
    A commemorative meeting, presided over by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, Kirichenko, was also held in Kiev, the capital of Ukraine.

### 苏联各地人民  隆重纪念列宁诞辰

1957-04-24
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联各地人民
    隆重纪念列宁诞辰
    据新华社莫斯科23日电　莫斯科市党、苏维埃、社会团体和各界人民的代表共两千余人4月22日晚间在苏联大剧院集会，隆重纪念列宁诞生八十七周年。
    苏联党和政府的领导人卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫、福尔采娃、谢皮洛夫以及一些老布尔什维克们都出席了庆祝会。
    苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫担任大会主席。
    大会开始时演奏了苏联国歌。接着，苏联共产党中央委员会书记福尔采娃作了关于庆祝列宁诞辰八十七周年的报告。
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联各地人民在4月22日隆重地纪念列宁诞辰八十七周年。
    这一天，莫斯科阳光明媚。一大清早，莫斯科人和首都的来宾就来到红场，来到陵墓，以便一次又一次地看这个毕生为千千万万普通人的幸福而斗争的伟人的容貌。下午两点四十五分，列宁故乡乌里扬诺夫斯克的代表团到陵墓前献花圈。紧跟着的是老一代布尔什维克的队伍。他们中间有的人曾经参加过1905年和1917年的革命，有的人曾同列宁一道工作和执行过他所付托的任务。前来谒墓的人群川流不息。在莫斯科的企业、机关和高等学校里，纷纷举行大会，纪念这个意义重大的日子。
    列宁格勒4月22日这一天有数万职工和列宁格勒州的集体农庄庄员参观了那些和列宁生平事迹有关的地方。列宁格勒市党、苏维埃和社会团体这天晚上在基洛夫歌舞剧院举行了纪念会。
    乌克兰首都基辅，也举行了由乌克兰共产党中央委员会第一书记基里钦科主持的纪念会。
