Title: Japanese Fukuoka Prefecture Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Japanese Fukuoka Prefecture Friendship Delegation, a group of ten people led by the head of the delegation, Takashi Oka, Chairman of the Fukuoka Prefecture Federation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries.
    Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, and others welcomed the delegation at the station.

### 日本福冈县访华团到京

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    日本福冈县访华团到京
    新华社27日讯　日本福冈县友好访华代表团一行十人在团长、日中友好协会福冈县联合会会长高丘稳率领下，今天晚上坐火车到达北京。代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国参观访问的。
    中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙等到车站欢迎代表团。
