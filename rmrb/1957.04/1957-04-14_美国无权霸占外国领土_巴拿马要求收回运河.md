Title: The United States has no right to occupy foreign territory. Panama demands the return of the Canal.
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 13th: According to TASS, the "Daily Express" published a statement by the President of Panama, Ernesto de la Guardia Jr., regarding the US control of the Panama Canal, saying that Panama wants to "recover full sovereignty over the Panama Canal and the Canal Zone." He said: "We firmly demand to enjoy sovereignty over the canal, and we will never compromise on this issue."

The "Daily Express" said that "anti-American sentiment has developed to a dangerous degree" in Panama. Even "the most stable" Panamanians have raised the question: "Why should foreigners control a vast area of Panama?"

### 美国无权霸占外国领土  巴拿马要求收回运河

1957-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    美国无权霸占外国领土
    巴拿马要求收回运河
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社伦敦13日讯：“每日快报”登载了巴拿马总统拉古阿迪亚就美国控制巴拿马运河一事发表的谈话说，巴拿马要“收回对巴拿马运河和对运河区的全部主权”。他说：“我们坚决要求享有对运河的主权，在这个问题上我们绝不让步。”
    “每日快报”说，在巴拿马“反美情绪已经发展到危险程度”。甚至“最稳重的”巴拿马人也提出了这样的问题：“凭什么外国人要控制巴拿马一块广阔的区域？”
