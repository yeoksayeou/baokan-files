Title: Soviet Petroleum Industry Ministry and River Transport Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Soviet Petroleum Industry Ministry delegation, headed by Soviet Deputy Minister of Petroleum Industry S.A. Orudzhev, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Petroleum Industry.
    Members of the delegation include S.A. Vekilov, Minister of the Petroleum Industry of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic, and seven people including prominent Soviet petroleum geologists M.F. Mirchink, Corresponding Academician, and A.A. Traphimuk, Corresponding Academician.
    During their stay in China, the delegation will visit Yumen, Xinjiang, Qaidam, Sichuan, Lanzhou, Fushun and other places. They will also participate in the petroleum exploration conference held by the Ministry of Petroleum Industry and assist China in making suggestions for petroleum exploration work in the Second Five-Year Plan.
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Soviet River Transport Delegation headed by Shashkov, Minister of River Transport of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications.
    Those who greeted them at the airport included Zhang Guojian, Deputy Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council, Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications, Li Yunchang, Pan Qi, Vice Ministers, and Sun Daguang and Yu Mei, Assistant Ministers.

### 苏联石油工业部和河运两代表团到京

1957-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联石油工业部和河运两代表团到京
    据新华社6日讯　以苏联石油工业部副部长斯·阿·奥鲁杰夫为首的苏联石油工业部代表团，应我国石油工业部邀请在6日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团成员中有苏联阿塞拜疆共和国石油工业部部长斯·阿·维基洛夫及苏联著名石油地质家米·弗·米尔钦柯通讯院士，阿·阿·特拉费姆克通讯院士等七人。
    代表团在中国期间将去玉门、新疆、柴达木、四川、兰州、抚顺等地参观访问，并将参加石油工业部召开的石油勘探会议和帮助我国对第二个五年计划内石油勘探工作提出建议。
    新华社6日讯　以俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国河运部部长夏什科夫为首的苏联河运代表团一行六人，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应交通部部长章伯钧的邀请来我国访问的。
    到机场迎接的有国务院第六办公室副主任张国坚，交通部部长章伯钧、副部长李运昌、潘琪，部长助理孙大光、于眉等。
