Title: New Bone Meal Factory in Inner Mongolia Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 5th — An Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region bone meal factory has been built in the Front Banner of Horqin Right Wing and went into production in mid-March. This bone meal factory will be able to produce one million jin of bone meal this year. This bone meal can be supplied to more than 50,000 mu of cultivated land for top dressing.
    The grasslands and rural areas of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have a large number of miscellaneous bones, which provides good conditions for the development of the fertilizer industry.

### 内蒙古新建骨粉厂投入生产

1957-04-07
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古新建骨粉厂投入生产
    新华社呼和浩特5日电　内蒙古自治区的一座骨粉工厂已经在科尔沁右翼前旗建成，在3月中旬投入生产。这座骨粉工厂今年能生产一百万斤骨粉。这些骨粉可以供给五万多亩耕地作追肥用。
    内蒙古自治区的草原和农村有着大量的杂骨，这对发展肥料工业提供了良好条件。
