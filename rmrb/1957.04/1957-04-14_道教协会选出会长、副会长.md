Title: Taoist Association Elects President, Vice Presidents
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Council of the Chinese Taoist Association held its first meeting today.

The meeting elected Yue Chongdai as President of the Chinese Taoist Association, Chen Yingning, Wang Yueqing, Meng Minghui, Qiao Qingxin, and Yi Xinying as Vice Presidents of the Chinese Taoist Association, Chen Yingning as Secretary-General, and Li Yuhang as Deputy Secretary-General.

### 道教协会选出会长、副会长

1957-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    道教协会选出会长、副会长
    新华社13日讯　中国道教协会理事会今天举行第一次会议。
    会议选出岳崇岱为中国道教协会会长，陈樱宁、汪月清、孟明慧、乔清心、易心莹为中国道教协会副会长，陈樱宁为秘书长，黎迂航为副秘书长。
