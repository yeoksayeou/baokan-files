Title: Philippine Political Circles Warn the United States Not to Interfere in Presidential Elections
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-01
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Manila, March 31st: According to an "influential Philippine leader," leaders of the ruling Nacionalista Party and the main opposition Liberal Party in the Philippines have asked the United States "not to meddle" in this year's Philippine presidential election.

    In the 1953 presidential election, the United States blatantly interfered, enabling Magsaysay to defeat the incumbent Liberal Party President Quirino.

### 菲政界警告美国  不要干预总统选举

1957-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    菲政界警告美国
    不要干预总统选举
    据新华社31日讯　马尼拉消息：据一位“有影响的菲律宾领袖”说，菲律宾执政的国民党和主要的反对党自由党的领袖已经要求美国在今年的菲律宾总统选举中“不要管闲事”。
    在1953年的总统选举中，美国曾经露骨地进行了干预活动，使麦格赛赛击败了当时在职的自由党总统季里诺。
