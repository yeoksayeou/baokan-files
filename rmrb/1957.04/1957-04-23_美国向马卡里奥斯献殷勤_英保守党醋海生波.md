Title: US Courts Makarios, British Conservatives Get Jealous

Author: 
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 22nd: It is reported that New York Governor Averell Harriman has invited Cypriot self-determination movement leader Makarios to visit the United States. This incident has once again stirred up anti-American sentiment among British Conservatives.

Conservative right-wing group leader Paul Williams angrily said: "Cyprus is not yet an American colony. The sooner the United States stops its divisive behavior in the Mediterranean, the better." He described the release of Makarios as a "humiliation" forced upon Britain by the United States.

Member of Parliament Maitland accused the United States of "interfering" in Cyprus.

Influential Lord Hinchingbrooke and Lord Beaverbrook also issued accusations against Harriman. Beaverbrook said in the "Daily Express" that Harriman's "motives and actions are inexcusably interfering with British policy."

### 美国向马卡里奥斯献殷勤  英保守党醋海生波

1957-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    美国向马卡里奥斯献殷勤
    英保守党醋海生波
    据新华社22日讯　伦敦消息：据报道，美国纽约州长哈里曼已经邀请塞浦路斯民族自决运动领袖马卡里奥斯访问美国。这件事在英保守党中又一次激起了反美情绪。
    保守党右翼集团领袖保罗·威廉斯气愤地说：“塞浦路斯现在还不是美国的殖民地。美国在地中海挑拨离间的行为越早停止越好。”他把释放马卡里奥斯说成是美国强迫英国接受的“屈辱”。
    议员梅特兰责备美国对塞浦路斯进行“干涉”。
    有势力的欣琴布鲁克勋爵和比维布鲁克勋爵也对哈里曼发出指责。比维布鲁克在“每日快报”上说，哈里曼的“动机和行动都不可原谅地干涉了英国的政策。”
