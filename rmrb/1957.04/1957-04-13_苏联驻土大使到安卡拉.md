Title: Soviet Ambassador to Turkey Arrives in Ankara
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, April 12th -- TASS reports from Ankara: The new Soviet Ambassador to Turkey, Mr. Ryzhov, arrived in Ankara on the 10th aboard a "Tu-104" special plane.

Upon arrival, Ryzhov told reporters that the Soviet Union and Turkey are neighboring countries, and the relationship between the two countries should be harmonious and friendly. This is not only beneficial to the people of the Soviet Union and Turkey but also conducive to peace.

The "Tu-104" aircraft remained at the airport for two hours, and a large number of Turkish officials, at the invitation of the Soviet Embassy, ​​visited the aircraft.

### 苏联驻土大使到安卡拉

1957-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联驻土大使到安卡拉
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社安卡拉讯：苏联新任驻土耳其大使雷若夫在10日乘“图—104”专机抵达安卡拉。
    雷若夫在到达之后向记者发表谈话说，苏联和土耳其是毗邻国家，两国的关系应该是和睦和友好的。这不仅有利于苏联和土耳其两国人民，也有利于和平。
    “图—104”飞机在机场上停留了两小时，土耳其的大批官员，应苏联大使馆的邀请参观了这架飞机。
