Title: Abundant Iron Ore Deposits Discovered in Lianping, Guangdong

Author:

Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 2

According to the Southern Daily, the iron ore discovered in Lianping County, Guangdong Province, in April of last year, has been confirmed by geologists to be a large-scale deposit after detailed exploration.

This iron ore has an iron content of 55% to 65%, very low sulfur and phosphorus content, and abundant reserves. It has the conditions for building a considerable-scale iron and steel industrial base.

### 广东连平铁矿蕴藏丰富

1957-04-03
第2版()
专栏：

    广东连平铁矿蕴藏丰富
    据南方日报消息　去年4月间在广东省连平县发现的铁矿，经过地质人员详细的勘探证实，它是一个规模很大的矿床。
    这个铁矿含铁量达55％到65％，硫、磷的含量很低，蕴藏量很丰富，具备了建设一个相当规模的钢铁工业基地的条件。
