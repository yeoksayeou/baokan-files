Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces First List of Athletes of National Level in Basketball

Author:

Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 4

Sports News

    State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces First List of Athletes of National Level in Basketball
    (Our Newspaper dispatch) Yesterday, the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China approved and announced the first list of athletes of national level in basketball in our country, totaling eighteen men and twelve women.
    Men: Wang Lifa, Zhang Xishan, Bai Jinshen, Liu Erzhu, Yang Boyong, Qian Chenghai, Liu Guiyi, He Shisun, Wang Yicheng, Zhang Fukui (all from Beijing), Yu Bangji, Wang Zhaoyu (both from Bayi Team), Cai Jijie, Zheng Li, Zhang Dexin (all from Public Security Army), Ma Qingsheng (Shenyang Military Region), Mi Baorong (Tianjin), Jia Qinshan (Shanghai).
    Women: Zhou Yixian, Li Shaofen, Zheng Yulian, Yang Jie, Ma Shenmei, Wang Xiafei, Hu Yingxin (all from Beijing), Xu Min, Ge Li, Chen Changfeng, Qian Youping, Zhou Zhengyun (all from Bayi Team).

### 国家体委公布第一批篮球运动健将名单

1957-04-09
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    国家体委公布第一批篮球运动健将名单
    本报讯　昨日，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会批准和公布了我国第一批篮球运动健将名单，共计男子十八名，女子十二名。
    男子：王利发、张锡山、白金申、刘二柱、杨伯镛、钱澄海、刘贵乙、何诗荪、王忆诚、张福奎（以上北京市）、余邦基、王兆钰（以上八一队）、蔡集杰、郑礼、张德鑫（以上公安军队）、马清盛（沈阳部队）、米宝荣（天津市）、贾钦升（上海市）。
    女子：周懿娴、李少芬、郑于莲、杨洁、马申妹、王霞飞、胡英信（以上北京市）、许敏、葛莉、陈常凤、钱幽屏、周正云（以上八一队）。
