Title: Chiba Prefecture Peace Delegation from Japan Arrives in Beijing, Japanese Film Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st. The Japanese Chiba Prefecture Peace Delegation, consisting of Katsuta Tomosaburo, Yamaguchi Tsugio, and Kawana Isamu, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Beijing on the evening of March 30th. Xu Baoqu, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others welcomed them at the station.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the nine-member delegation of the Japanese film industry, which visited China, left Guangzhou for home on March 27th. The other two members of the delegation had already returned home on March 17th.

### 日千叶县和平代表团到京  日电影界代表团回国

1957-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    日千叶县和平代表团到京
    日电影界代表团回国
    新华社31日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的日本千叶县和平代表团胜田友三郎、山口次男、川名勇等三人，已于3月30日晚抵京，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员许宝驹等人曾去车站欢迎。
    据新华社讯　日本电影界访华代表团一行九人，于3月27日离广州回国。代表团的其他二人已在3月17日先行回国。
