    A Blank Space
    The French weekly "L'Express" recently published an article criticizing the French government's policy toward Algeria. However, in the edition sold in Algeria, this article was removed, leaving a large blank space where the article would have been. The editorial department called this method voluntary censorship.

### 一片空白

1957-04-21
第6版()
专栏：国际点滴

    一片空白
    法国“快报”周刊不久前发表了一篇批评法国政府对阿尔及利亚政策的文章。但是在销往阿尔及利亚的版上却撤销了这篇文章，而在登载这篇文章的地方留下一大片空白。编辑部把这种办法叫做自愿检查制度。
