Title: He Xiangning Holds Reception to Welcome Overseas Chinese and Compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao Returning for Sightseeing

Author:

Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, held a reception this afternoon to welcome overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao returning to Beijing to attend the "May Day" celebrations and sightseeing.

Nie Rongzhen, Vice Premier of the State Council, attended the reception.

At the beginning of the reception, Fang Fang, Vice Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, delivered a welcome speech. Then, Vice Chairman Zhuang Mingli read a speech on behalf of He Xiangning to the overseas Chinese returning for sightseeing. In her speech, He Xiangning first welcomed the overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao returning for sightseeing. She hoped that everyone would promptly offer criticisms of the problems and shortcomings they see during their sightseeing in various places, in order to help the government improve its work. She also hoped that everyone would carry forward the patriotic tradition of overseas Chinese and assist and support the country's construction.

### 何香凝举行酒会欢迎回国观光的侨胞和港澳同胞

1957-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    何香凝举行酒会欢迎回国观光的侨胞和港澳同胞
    据新华社29日讯　华侨事务委员会主任何香凝今天下午举行酒会，欢迎来北京参加“五一”节观礼的回国观光侨胞和港澳同胞。
    国务院副总理聂荣臻出席了酒会。
    酒会开始时，华侨事务委员会副主任方方致欢迎词。接着，由副主任庄明理代何香凝宣读向回国观光侨胞的讲话。何香凝的讲话中首先对回国观光的侨胞和港澳同胞表示欢迎。她希望大家在各地观光时，对所看到的问题、缺点及时地提出批评，以帮助政府改进工作。她并希望大家发扬华侨爱国爱乡的传统，协助和支持国家的建设。
