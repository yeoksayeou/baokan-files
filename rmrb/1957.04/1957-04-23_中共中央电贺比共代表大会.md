Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Belgian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 5

    CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Belgian Communist Party Congress
    To the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party,
    To all comrades of the Twelfth Congress of the Belgian Communist Party:
    As the working people of the world greet the great "May Day" International Labor Day with new struggles, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China, extends warm, fraternal greetings to the Twelfth Congress of the Belgian Communist Party.
    The Communist Party of China and the Chinese people are watching with close concern and sympathy the struggle of your party and your people to uphold and fight for peace, democracy, and socialism. We wish your party's Twelfth Congress complete success in holding high the banner of Marxism-Leninism, wish your party new progress in uniting the entire working class, uniting all democratic and progressive forces and the entire Belgian people, and wish your party and your people greater achievements in safeguarding the vital interests of the working masses and in the struggle for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism.
                                     The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共中央电贺比共代表大会

1957-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺比共代表大会比利时共产党中央委员会转比利时共产党第十二次代表大会全体同志们：
    正当世界劳动人民以新的战斗迎接伟大的“五一”国际劳动节的时候，中国共产党中央委员会谨代表中国共产党全体党员，向比利时共产党第十二次代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    中国共产党和中国人民抱着密切的关怀和同情注视着你们的党和你们的人民维护和争取和平民主和社会主义的斗争。谨祝你们党的第十二次代表大会在高举马克思列宁主义的旗帜下获得圆满的成功，祝你们的党在团结整个工人阶级、团结一切民主进步力量和比利时全体人民的事业上获得新的进展，祝你们的党和你们的人民在维护劳动群众切身利益、在为和平、民主和社会主义事业的斗争中获得更大的成就。
                                     中国共产党中央委员会
