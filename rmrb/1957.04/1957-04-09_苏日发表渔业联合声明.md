Title: Soviet-Japanese Joint Communiqué on Fisheries

Author:

Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 8th dispatch: The Soviet fisheries delegation, headed by Kutirev, left Tokyo for home today after participating in the first meeting of the Soviet-Japanese Fisheries Commission and signing the Soviet-Japanese Fisheries Protocol.

The Soviet-Japanese Fisheries Commission issued a joint communiqué on April 6. The communiqué stated that the first meeting of the commission decided that the Japanese catch in the Northwest Pacific Ocean in 1957 would be 120,000 metric tons, and the catch in the Sea of Okhotsk would be 13,000 metric tons. In order to protect fish resources, salmon fishing will be prohibited in the area east of the Kamchatka Peninsula north of 52 degrees north latitude and west of 170 degrees 25 minutes east longitude after July 20. The meeting also stipulated the number of walleye pollock and female crabs that each fishing vessel can catch each time.

The joint communiqué stated that the second meeting of the Soviet-Japanese Fisheries Commission will be held in Moscow in January 1958 to discuss the issue of protecting fish resources in the area south of 48 degrees north latitude centered on the Kuril Islands and Sakhalin.

### 苏日发表渔业联合声明

1957-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏日发表渔业联合声明
    新华社8日讯　东京消息：以库蒂雷夫为首的苏联渔业代表团在参加了日苏渔业委员会第一次会议和签署了苏日渔业议定书后，今天离开东京回国。
    日苏渔业委员会曾经在4月6日发表一项联合声明。声明说，委员会第一次会议决定，1957年度日本在太平洋西北部海面的捕鱼量为十二万公吨，在鄂霍次克海的捕鱼量为一万三千公吨；为了保护鱼源，在北纬五十二度以北、东经一百七十度二十五分以西的堪察加半岛以东区域，自7月20日以后停止捕鲑鱼。会议还规定了每艘渔船每次捕捉小青鱼和雌蟹的数量。
    联合声明说，1958年1月将在莫斯科举行日苏渔业委员会第二次会议，讨论保护以千岛群岛和库页岛为中心的北纬四十八度以南地区的鱼源问题。
