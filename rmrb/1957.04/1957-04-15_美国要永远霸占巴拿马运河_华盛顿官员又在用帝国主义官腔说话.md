Title: The United States Wants to Occupy the Panama Canal Forever - Washington Officials Speak Again in Imperialist Tones
Author:
Date: 1957-04-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 14th: United States officials stated on the 13th that the United States wants to occupy the Panama Canal forever. These officials revealed the intentions of the United States when commenting on the recent remarks made by Panamanian President Guadiato to a reporter from the British "Daily Express." In his remarks, Guadiato said that Panama wants to "recover full sovereignty over the Canal and the Canal Zone."

American officials, in opposing this statement, argued that, according to the 1903 Panama Canal Treaty, the United States has effectively obtained permanent sovereignty over the Panama Canal and the Canal Zone, while Panama itself only has "nominal sovereignty."

Latin American legal experts, who recently held a meeting in Panama City, unanimously pointed out that sovereignty over the Panama Canal belongs to Panama, and that the words implying the "permanence" of the treaty in the Panama Treaty are also inconsistent with international law.

### 美国要永远霸占巴拿马运河  华盛顿官员又在用帝国主义官腔说话

1957-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    美国要永远霸占巴拿马运河
    华盛顿官员又在用帝国主义官腔说话
    新华社14日讯　华盛顿消息：美国官员13日表示美国要永远霸占巴拿马运河。这些官员在评论巴拿马总统古阿迪亚最近对英国“每日快报”记者谈话的时候表露了美国的企图。古阿迪亚在谈话中说，巴拿马要“收回运河和运河区的全部主权”。
    美国官员在反对这个谈话时辩解说，根据1903年的巴拿马运河条约，美国已经永远实际上获得了巴拿马运河和运河区的主权，而巴拿马自己只有“名义上的主权”。
    不久以前在巴拿马城举行会议的拉丁美洲法律专家们曾经一致指出，巴拿马运河的主权属于巴拿马，而且巴拿马条约中的意味着条约“永久性”的字眼也是不合国际法的。
