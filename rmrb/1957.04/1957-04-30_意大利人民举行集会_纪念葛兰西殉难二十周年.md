Title: Italian People Hold Rallies to Commemorate the 20th Anniversary of Gramsci's Martyrdom
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 6
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 29th, Rome: According to TASS, Italian communists commemorated the 20th anniversary of the death of Antonio Gramsci, founder and prominent activist of the Italian Communist Party, who died in a fascist prison on April 27, 1937. Mass memorial rallies were held in many Italian cities.

　　A delegation from the Central Committee and the Central Control Commission of the Italian Communist Party, consisting of Longo, Vittorio, Terracini, and Scoccimarro, laid a wreath on Gramsci's tomb on April 27th. Worshippers also included delegations from the Italian Communist Youth Federation, the National Association of Victims of Political Persecution for Anti-Fascism, the Marxist Academy named after Antonio Gramsci, and other democratic groups.

### 意大利人民举行集会  纪念葛兰西殉难二十周年

1957-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利人民举行集会
    纪念葛兰西殉难二十周年
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利共产党员4月28日纪念意大利共产党的奠基人和杰出活动家安东尼奥·葛兰西1937年4月27日在法西斯监牢中遇害二十周年。意大利许多城市都举行了群众纪念大会。
    意大利共产党中央委员会和中央监察委员会的代表团由隆哥、维多里奥、特拉齐尼和斯科奇马罗等人组成。4月27日在葛兰西墓上献了花圈。扫墓的还有意大利共产主义青年团、因反法西斯而受政治迫害的牺牲者的全国协会、以安东尼奥·葛兰西命名的马克思主义学院和其他民主团体的代表团。
