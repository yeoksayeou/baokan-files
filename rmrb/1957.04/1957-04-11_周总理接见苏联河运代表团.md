Title: Premier Zhou Receives Soviet River Transport Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 9th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Soviet River Transport Department delegation at 2:30 pm today in Xihua Hall, Zhongnanhai. The head of the delegation was Z.A. Shashkov, and the members were D.D. Pokrovsky, M.L. Nikulin, G.I. Akhonikov, B.A. Romanov, A.B. Subbotin, and V.N. Maslyakov.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications, and others.

### 周总理接见苏联河运代表团

1957-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏联河运代表团
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理今天下午二时半在中南海西花厅接见了苏联河运部访华代表团团长札·阿·夏什可夫和团员季·季·波科罗夫斯基、米·洛·尼库林、格·依·阿霍尼科夫、巴·阿·罗曼诺夫、安·波·苏鲍钦、瓦·尼·马斯良科夫。
    接见时在座的，有交通部部长章伯钧等人。
