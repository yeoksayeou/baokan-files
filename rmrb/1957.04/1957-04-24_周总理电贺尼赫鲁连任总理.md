Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to Nehru on Re-election as Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai cabled Jawaharlal Nehru on April 20th to congratulate him on his re-election as Prime Minister of the Republic of India. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:

To His Excellency Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India:

I am delighted to learn of Your Excellency's re-election as Prime Minister of the Republic of India, and I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

During Your Excellency's tenure as Prime Minister in the previous term, the friendly relations between China and India have made remarkable progress on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The Republic of India is playing an increasingly important role in safeguarding peace. I am convinced that Your Excellency's renewed appointment as Prime Minister will make even greater contributions to promoting friendship between China and India and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, April 20, 1957, Beijing

### 周总理电贺尼赫鲁连任总理

1957-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺尼赫鲁连任总理
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理4月20日电贺贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁连任印度共和国总理。贺电全文如下：印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁阁下：
    欣悉阁下连任印度共和国总理，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下致以热烈的祝贺。
    在阁下担任上届总理期间，中印两国的友好关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上已经取得了显著的发展。印度共和国在维护和平事业中起着日益重要的作用。我深信，阁下再度荣任总理，定将对促进中印两国友谊和维护亚洲和世界和平作出更大的贡献。
       中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来　1957年4月20日于北京
