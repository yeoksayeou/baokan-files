Title: Supply and Marketing Departments Serve Fishermen at Sea
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 3

Supply and Marketing Departments Serve Fishermen at Sea

　　(Reported by this newspaper) Zhejiang Province's spring fishing season at sea has begun. Subsequently, the transport and sales boats of the Zhoushan supply and marketing departments will also follow the fishing boats to places such as Nanjiushan, Dachen, Shibu, and Wushamen to collect fish. The Shenjiamen Fishing Port Supply and Marketing Cooperative has also set up supply points in various fishing grounds, transporting large quantities of prop roots, pig blood, cigarettes, liquor, and other necessities to supply the fishermen day and night, ensuring their needs are met. (End)

### 供销部门为出海渔民服务

1957-04-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　供销部门为出海渔民服务
    本报讯　浙江省春汛海洋渔业生产已开始，以后，舟山供销部门的运销船也随渔船开往南韭山、大陈、石浦、乌沙门等地收鱼。沈家门渔港供销社还在各渔场设立了供应点，他们把大量的撑风、猪血、香烟、酒等运去，日夜供应，保证渔民需要。　（正）
