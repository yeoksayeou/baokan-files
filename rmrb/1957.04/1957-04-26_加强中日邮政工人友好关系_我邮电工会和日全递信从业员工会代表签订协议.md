Title: Strengthening Friendly Relations Between Chinese and Japanese Postal Workers: China's Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union and Representatives of the All-Japan Postal Industry Workers' Union Sign Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union and the delegation of the All-Japan Postal Industry Workers' Union visiting China, signed an agreement this afternoon at Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai, Beijing, to strengthen friendly cooperation between postal workers of the two countries. The agreement stipulates that both parties will work together to achieve the normalization of diplomatic relations and postal communications between the two countries, oppose the manufacture, testing, and use of atomic and hydrogen bombs, and take various measures to strengthen the ties and friendly relations between postal workers of the two countries. Liu Dongcen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, signed the agreement on behalf of Li Jinghan, Chairman of the Committee. On the Japanese side, Eijiro Harigaya, head of the delegation of the All-Japan Postal Industry Workers' Union visiting China, signed the agreement.

### 加强中日邮政工人友好关系  我邮电工会和日全递信从业员工会代表签订协议

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    加强中日邮政工人友好关系
    我邮电工会和日全递信从业员工会代表签订协议
    据新华社24日讯　中国邮电工会全国委员会和日本全递信从业员工会访华使节团，今天下午在北京中南海紫光阁签订了加强两国邮政工人友好合作关系的协议。协议规定双方将共同为实现两国邦交正常化和通邮正常化而努力，反对制造、试验和使用原子弹、氢弹，采取各种措施加强两国邮政工人的联系和友好关系。中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席刘东岑代表这个委员会主席李景韩在协议上签字，日本方面由日本全递信从业员工会访华使节团团长针谷荣次郎在协议上签字。
