Title: In Fraternal Lands
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 5

　　The Soviet "Tajik Literature and Art Decade" opened in Moscow on April 9th. Before the revolution, the Tajik people had no professional theaters. This time, two drama theaters and one opera house came to Moscow to participate in the "Literature and Art Decade." Soviet Party and government leaders attended the first performance by Tajik artists.

　　Chinese writer Zhao Shuli's "Sanliwan" and Du Pengcheng's "Defend Yan'an" will be published in Bulgaria. The translation and publication of these two works have been included in Bulgaria's 1957 publishing plan. In addition, Bulgaria will publish for the first time the Egyptian writer Abd al-Rahman al-Sharqawi's "Land" as well as works by some Algerian and Icelandic writers.

　　Recently, the National Committee of the National Front of Democratic Germany for Aiding Korea and Vietnam presented 201 boxes of clothing, toys, and various recreational items to the children of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The first batch of these gifts has recently been shipped from Berlin to Pyongyang.

　　A new chocolate factory in Tirana recently went into production. In recent years, Albania's sugar production has increased significantly, increasing fivefold in 1955 compared to 1950, and will double again by 1960 (the last year of the Second Five-Year Plan).

　　In Anshan County, Xuan Quang Province, North Vietnam, a forest logging team has several capable transportation experts—elephants. The logging team entrusts them with transporting the felled trees. Each elephant can transport 4,000 catties of wood each time, making six round trips a day, covering a total distance of 24 kilometers. (Picture attached)

　　More than 2,000 gifts prepared by Moscow youth for the delegates of the 6th World Youth and Student Festival began to be exhibited at the Soviet State Lenin Library. This is a "Footballer" made by the youth of the "March 8th" Puppet Factory.

### 在兄弟之邦

1957-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    在兄弟之邦
    苏联“塔吉克文学艺术旬”4月9日在莫斯科开幕了。塔吉克人民在革命前根本没有职业剧院。这次来莫斯科参加“文学艺术旬”的就有两个话剧院和一个歌剧院。苏联共产党和政府领导人，都出席观看了塔吉克艺术家们的第一次演出。
    中国作家赵树理的“三里湾”和杜鹏程的“保卫延安”将在保加利亚出版。这两部作品的翻译、出版工作，已经列入了保加利亚1957年的出版计划。此外，保加利亚将第一次出版埃及作家阿卜德·拉赫曼·夏尔加维的“土地”以及阿尔及利亚和冰岛一些作家的作品。
    最近，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会援助朝鲜和越南委员会赠送给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国儿童二百零一箱衣物、玩具和各种娱乐品等礼物。这些礼物的第一批已在最近从柏林运到了平壤。
    地拉那一所新的巧克力糖厂最近投入了生产。近几年来阿尔巴尼亚糖的产量有了显著的提高，1955年已经增加为1950年的五倍，到1960年（第二个五年计划的最后一年）将再增加一倍。
    越南北方宣光省安山县的一个森林木材采伐小组，有几只得力运输能手——大象，伐木小组把伐下来的大树交给它们搬运，一只大象每次能搬运四千斤木材，每天往返六次，行程共二十四公里。（附图片）
    莫斯科青年为第六届世界青年与学生联欢节的代表们准备的二千多件礼物，在苏联国立列宁图书馆开始展出。这是“三·八”木偶工厂的青年制造的“足球家”。
