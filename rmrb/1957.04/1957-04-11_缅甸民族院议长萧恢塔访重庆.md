Title: Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio Visits Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, April 10th: Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities and former President, accompanied by Liu GuanYi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, arrived in Chongqing by plane from Kunming today with his wife and others. In the evening, Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio and his party toured Chongqing and attended a welcome banquet hosted by Mayor Ren Baige.

While in Kunming, Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio was entertained by Liu Geping, Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, and Xie Fuzhi, Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. He also visited some factories, schools, and scenic spots in Kunming.

### 缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔访重庆

1957-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔访重庆
    据新华社重庆10日电　缅甸联邦民族院议长、前总统萧恢塔和他的夫人等由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一陪同，今天由昆明乘飞机到达重庆。傍晚，萧恢塔议长等游览了重庆市容，并出席了任白戈市长的欢迎宴会。
    萧恢塔议长在昆明期间，曾先后受到了全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平和云南省代省长刘明辉、政协云南省委员会主席谢富治的宴请。并先后到昆明市的一些工厂、学校和风景区进行了参观游览。
