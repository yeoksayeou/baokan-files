Title: World Population Reaches 2.7 Billion; Population Growth Rate at 30 Million Annually
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th - According to statistics published by the East German "Petermann's Geographical Review" on April 25th, the world population at the beginning of this year had reached 2.7 billion. Among them, China has a population of over 600 million, India 392 million, the Soviet Union 205 million, the United States 171 million, and Japan 91 million. The population growth rate is 30 million annually.

### 世界人口达二十七亿  人口增长率每年三千万

1957-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    世界人口达二十七亿
    人口增长率每年三千万
    新华社柏林26日电　根据民主德国“彼得曼地理评论”4月25日所发表的统计数字，今年年初全世界人口已经达到二十七亿。其中中国人口是六亿多，印度三亿九千二百万，苏联两亿零五百万，美国一亿七千一百万，日本九千一百万。人口增长率是每年三千万。
