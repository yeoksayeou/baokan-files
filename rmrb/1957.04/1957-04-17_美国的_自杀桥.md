Title: America's "Suicide Bridge"
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 6

Gleanings

    The Washington Bridge, a huge and spectacular suspension bridge over the Hudson River near New York City in the United States, has eight traffic lanes. As many as 90,000 vehicles of various kinds pass over the bridge every day, with toll revenue reaching $42,000. However, people call this bridge the "Suicide Bridge" because suicides often occur on this bridge.

### 美国的“自杀桥”

1957-04-17
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    美国的“自杀桥”
    美国纽约市附近哈得逊河上的华盛顿桥，是一座巨大而壮观的吊桥。桥上有八条交通路，每天在桥上驶过的各种车辆，多至九万辆，收入通行税达四万二千美元。但人们管这条桥叫“自杀桥”，因为，在这条桥上常常发生自杀事件。
