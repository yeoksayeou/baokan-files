Title: Commemorating the Second Anniversary of the Asian-African Conference: "Bandung Week" Begins in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 13th: "Bandung Week," held in New Delhi to commemorate the second anniversary of the Asian-African Conference, began on April 12th.

Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairman of the Indian Association for Asian Solidarity, said at the meeting that the purpose of organizing "Bandung Week" activities is to "reaffirm the principles adopted at the Bandung Conference and promote Asian-African solidarity."

Today's celebration program included a meeting presided over by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to India. At the meeting, H.D. Malaviya gave a report "On Land Reform and Cooperation in China," and the Chinese film "The Fairy Couple" was also shown.

"Bandung Week" is organized by the Indian Association for Asian Solidarity. During this week, the Burmese Ambassador and Indian writer Dr. Gyan Chand, among others, will deliver speeches at various gatherings. Films from various countries will also be shown daily.

### 纪念亚非会议两周年  新德里“万隆周”开始

1957-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念亚非会议两周年
    新德里“万隆周”开始
    据新华社新德里13日电　新德里的为纪念亚非会议两周年而举行的“万隆周”已经在4月12日开始。
    印度争取亚洲团结委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人在会上说，组织“万隆周”的活动是为了“重申万隆会议上所采纳的原则，并且促进亚非团结”。
    今天的庆祝项目包括举行一个由中国驻印度大使潘自力主持的会议。会上由赫·德·马拉维亚作“关于中国的土地改革和合作化”的报告，还放映中国电影“天仙配”。
    “万隆周”是由印度争取亚洲团结委员会组织的。在这一周内，缅甸大使和印度作家吉安·昌德博士等将在一些集会上发表演说。每天还将放映各国的电影。
