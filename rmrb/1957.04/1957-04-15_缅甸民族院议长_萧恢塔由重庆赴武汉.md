Title: Burmese Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio Traveled from Chongqing to Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-04-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, April 13th: Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities, and his wife, accompanied by Liu Guan-yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, left Chongqing this morning aboard the "Minzhong" steamer for a visit to Wuhan. Ren Baige, the Mayor of Chongqing, and others were at the dock to see them off.

### 缅甸民族院议长  萧恢塔由重庆赴武汉

1957-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族院议长
    萧恢塔由重庆赴武汉
    据新华社重庆13日电　缅甸联邦民族院议长萧恢塔和夫人等，由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一陪同，在今天早晨由重庆乘“民众”号轮船前往武汉访问。到码头送行的有重庆市长任白戈等。
