Title: Repelling Chiang Kai-shek's Warships and Protecting Fishermen's Production: A Certain Artillery Company 2nd Platoon Receives Commendation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, April 13th: The command organ of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front issued a circular to its subordinate units yesterday, commending the 2nd Platoon of a certain artillery unit on the Liu'ao Peninsula in southern Fujian for repelling Chiang Kai-shek's warships and protecting fishermen's production.
    At about 5:00 PM on April 4th, the officers and soldiers of this artillery platoon discovered three Chiang Kai-shek warships in the sea south of Hongyu Island near the peninsula, intending to intercept three fishing boats that were fishing. They quickly prepared for battle. When the Chiang Kai-shek warships were about to approach the fishing boats, they immediately opened fire. The battle lasted for twenty minutes, and the Chiang Kai-shek warships escaped under the cover of smoke. All three fishing boats escaped safely.

### 击退蒋军军舰　保护渔民生产  某部炮二连受到表扬

1957-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    击退蒋军军舰　保护渔民生产
    某部炮二连受到表扬
    新华社福建前线13日电　中国人民解放军福建前线部队指挥机关，昨天向所属部队发出通报，表扬福建南部六鳌半岛上的某部炮兵第二连击退蒋军军舰保护渔民生产。
      4日下午五时左右，这个炮兵连的官兵发现在半岛附近红屿岛以南海面上有三艘蒋舰，打算拦劫三条正在捕鱼的渔船，便迅速作好战斗准备。等蒋舰快要靠近渔船的时候，他们便立刻开炮轰击。战斗进行了二十分钟，蒋舰在烟幕掩护下逃去，三条渔船都安全脱险。
