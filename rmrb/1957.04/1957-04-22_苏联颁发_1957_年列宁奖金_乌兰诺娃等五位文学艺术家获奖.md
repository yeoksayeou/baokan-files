Title: USSR Awards 1957 Lenin Prizes: Ulanova and Five Other Literary and Artistic Figures Win Awards
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4

    Soviet Union Awards 1957 Lenin Prizes
    Ulanova and Five Other Literary and Artistic Figures Win Awards
    (Dispatch from our Moscow correspondent, April 21) Tomorrow, on the anniversary of Lenin's birth, the Soviet Union will announce the winners of the 1957 Lenin Prizes. The Lenin Prize Committee for Literature and Art under the Council of Ministers of the USSR has decided to award the 1957 Lenin Prizes to the following outstanding works of literature, music, and visual arts, and the most outstanding achievements of dramatic artists: First, "Self-Portrait," a sculpture by the oldest Soviet sculptor, S.T. Konenkov. Second, "Russian Forest," a novel by the famous veteran writer L.M. Leonov. Third, "Moabit Notebook," a collection of poems by Musa Jalil, a poet from the Tatar Autonomous Republic who was murdered by fascist executioners in Moabit Prison, Berlin, in 1944. Fourth, "Symphony No. 7" by the famous musician S.S. Prokofiev. Prokofiev was one of the most famous Soviet musicians and died in 1953. Fifth, G.S. Ulanova for her outstanding achievements in ballet art. (Attached pictures)

### 苏联颁发1957年列宁奖金  乌兰诺娃等五位文学艺术家获奖

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联颁发1957年列宁奖金
    乌兰诺娃等五位文学艺术家获奖
    本报莫斯科21日专电　明天，在列宁的诞辰纪念日，苏联将颁布1957年列宁奖金。苏联部长会议的文学和艺术方面的列宁奖金委员会决定把1957年列宁奖金授予如下文学、音乐、造型艺术的最出色的作品和戏剧艺术家的最出色的成就：一，苏联最老的雕刻家谢·特·柯年科夫的雕刻“自塑像”。二，著名老作家里·马·列昂诺夫的长篇小说“俄罗斯森林”。三，穆萨·嘉里尔的“莫阿比特狱中诗钞”，嘉里尔是鞑靼自治共和国的诗人，1944年被法西斯刽子手杀害于柏林莫阿比特监狱。四，著名音乐家谢·谢·普罗科菲耶夫的“第七交响乐”，普罗科菲耶夫是苏联最著名的音乐家之一，于1953年逝世。五，珈·谢·乌兰诺娃在巴蕾舞艺术方面的出色成就。（附图片）
