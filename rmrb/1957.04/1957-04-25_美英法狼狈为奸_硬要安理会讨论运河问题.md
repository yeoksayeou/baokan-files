Title: US, Britain and France Collude, Insisting on Security Council Discussion of the Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to Xinhua News Agency reports: U.S. Representative to the United Nations Lodge announced on the 23rd that the United States will "definitely" request the Security Council to discuss the Suez Canal issue. Sources say the U.S. hopes the meeting will be held on the 25th or 26th. Lodge already held talks with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the same day regarding this issue.

That evening, authoritative British sources said that Britain supports the U.S. request. Simultaneously, French officials also expressed their welcome to the U.S. action.

### 美英法狼狈为奸  硬要安理会讨论运河问题

1957-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    美英法狼狈为奸
    硬要安理会讨论运河问题
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：美国驻联合国代表洛奇23日宣布，美国“肯定地”将要求安全理事会讨论苏伊士运河问题。有消息说，美国希望会议在25日或26日举行。洛奇已经在同一天就这个问题同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德进行会谈。
    当天晚上权威的英国人士说：英国支持美国的这个要求。同时，法国官方人士，也对美国这个行动表示欢迎。
