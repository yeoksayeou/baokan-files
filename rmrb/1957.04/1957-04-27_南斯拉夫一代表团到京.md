Title: Yugoslav Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 1

    A Yugoslav delegation arrived in Beijing
    (Dispatch from this newspaper) A delegation from the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, comprised of social welfare, health, and trade union workers, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th. The delegation was invited by our Ministry of Health and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and to visit.

### 南斯拉夫一代表团到京

1957-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫一代表团到京
    本报讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国社会福利、卫生工作者和工会工作者联合组成的代表团，在24日下午乘飞机抵达北京。代表团是应我卫生部和中华全国总工会的邀请来参加“五一”节观礼和访问的。
