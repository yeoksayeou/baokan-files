Title: French Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, April 16th. A delegation from the French Communist Party arrived in Bucharest by plane on the afternoon of the 15th.

The head of the delegation is French Communist Party Central Political Bureau member, Roger. Members of the delegation include French Communist Party Political Bureau members Frachon and Servin, and French Communist Party Central Committee members M. Vincent and R. Leroy.

The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Romanian Workers' Party Central Political Bureau members Apostol, Ceaușescu, and Pătrășcanu, among others.

A meeting will be held in Bucharest between the Romanian Workers' Party delegation and the French Communist Party delegation.

### 法共代表团抵罗马尼亚

1957-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    法共代表团抵罗马尼亚
    新华社布加勒斯特16日电　法国共产党代表团15日下午乘飞机到达布加勒斯特。
    代表团团长是法国共产党中央政治局委员罗歇，团员有法共政治局委员弗里希曼、塞尔文和法共中央委员马·樊尚和罗·勒罗瓦。
    代表团在机场受到罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员阿波斯托尔、齐奥塞斯库、帕伏列斯库等的欢迎。
    罗马尼亚工人党代表团和法共代表团将在布加勒斯特举行会谈。
