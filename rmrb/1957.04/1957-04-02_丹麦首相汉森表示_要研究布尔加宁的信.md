Title: Danish Prime Minister Hansen Says He Will Study Bulganin's Letter
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, April 1st: Danish Prime Minister Hansen said on March 30 that the Danish government will study in detail the letter from Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin to him on the issue of Soviet-Danish relations, and then will make a reply within two to four weeks.

### 丹麦首相汉森表示  要研究布尔加宁的信

1957-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    丹麦首相汉森表示
    要研究布尔加宁的信
    据新华社1日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦首相汉森3月30日说，丹麦政府将详细研究苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁关于苏联—丹麦关系问题给他的信，然后要在两个到四个星期之内作出答复。
