Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Calls on Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Anton ? Galkov, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today. Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were present at the airport to welcome him. Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, Eteropolski, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries in China, also came to greet him.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Anton ? Galkov, called on Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present at the meeting were Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 保加利亚大使拜会章汉夫副外长

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚大使拜会章汉夫副外长
    新华社25日讯　保加利亚人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使安东·?加尔科夫在今天中午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有外交部苏联东欧司副司长李汇川，礼宾司副司长张世杰。保加利亚驻华大使馆临时代办埃特罗波尔斯基及使馆人员，各国驻华使节及外交官也前往迎接。
    新华社25日讯　保加利亚人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使安东·?加尔科夫今天下午拜会我外交部副部长章汉夫商谈递交国书事宜。拜会时在座的有外交部苏联东欧司副司长李汇川，礼宾司副司长张世杰。
