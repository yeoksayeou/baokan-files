Title: Exchange of Notes Ceremony for Joint Statement by Zhang Xiruo and Asanuma Inejirō
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The exchange of notes ceremony for the joint statement by Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Asanuma Inejirō, head of the Japan Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to China, was held this morning in Beijing.
    Zhang Xiruo and Asanuma Inejirō exchanged texts.
    On the Japanese side, the participants in the ceremony included Kiyoshi Katsumada, Tadataka Sata, Masu Soene, Shichiro Hozumi, Hideo Yamahana, Tomoki Narita, and Ryosaku Sasaki, all members of the Japan Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to China.
    On the Chinese side, the participants in the ceremony included Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Liao Chengzhi, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Xie Nanguang and Zhao Anbo, Council Members; and Yang Yu, Director of the China Fisheries Association.

### 张奚若和浅沼稻次郎共同声明换文

1957-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    张奚若和浅沼稻次郎共同声明换文
    新华社23日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和日本社会党访华亲善使节团团长浅沼稻次郎共同声明换文仪式今天上午在北京举行。
    张奚若和浅沼稻次郎互换了文本。
    日本方面参加仪式的，有日本社会党访华亲善使节团团员胜间田清一、佐多忠隆、曾祢益、穗积七郎、山花秀雄、成田知己、佐佐木良作。
    中国方面参加仪式的，有中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华，中国红十字会总会会长李德全，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员廖承志，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，理事谢南光、赵安博，中国渔业协会主任杨煜。
