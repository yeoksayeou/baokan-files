Title: Another Batch of Ball Sports Athletes List Announced by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission

Author:

Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 4

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission recently approved and announced the list of thirteen sports masters in table tennis, badminton, and tennis.

Table Tennis: Wang Chuanyao, Jiang Yongning, Zhuang Jiafu, Fu Qifang, Sun Meiying (female), Ye Peiqiong (female), Qiu Zhonghui (female).

Badminton: Wang Wenjiao, Chen Fushou, Huang Shiming.

Tennis: Wu Shengkang, Mei Fuji, Zhu Zhenhua.

Additionally, in the news "The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces the First Batch of Basketball Sports Masters List" published in this newspaper on the 9th, Zheng Li of the Public Security Army and Jia Qin Sheng of Shanghai Municipality should be removed.

### 又一批球类运动健将名单  国家体委公布

1957-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    又一批球类运动健将名单
    ·国家体委公布·
    本报讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会最近批准和公布了十三名乒乓球、羽毛球和网球的运动健将名单。
    乒乓球：王传耀、姜永宁、庄家富、傅其芳、孙梅英（女）、叶佩琼（女）、丘钟惠（女）。
    羽毛球：王文教、陈福寿、黄世明。
    网球：吴生康、梅福基、朱振华。
    又9日本报刊登的“国家体委公布第一批篮球运动健将名单”的新闻中，应除去公安军队的郑礼、上海市的贾钦升。
