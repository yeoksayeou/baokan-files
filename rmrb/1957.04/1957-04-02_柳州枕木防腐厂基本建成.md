Title: Liuzhou Sleeper Anti-Corrosion Factory Basically Completed
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 2

    The Liuzhou Sleeper Anti-Corrosion Factory Basically Completed
    (From Our Newspaper) The Liuzhou Sleeper Anti-Corrosion Factory of the Ministry of Railways has been basically completed. It is expected to be officially put into production in the second quarter.
    This factory is designed according to the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and at a modern level. It can produce about 1.1 million anti-corrosion sleepers annually. This kind of sleeper is more durable than untreated sleepers and generally lasts for fifteen years, and can save the country a lot of money.

### 柳州枕木防腐厂基本建成

1957-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

    柳州枕木防腐厂基本建成
    本报讯　铁道部柳州枕木防腐厂基本建成。预计在第二季度正式投入生产。
    这个工厂是根据苏联先进经验、按现代化的水平设计的，每年能生产防腐枕木一百一十万根左右。这种枕木要比未经过防腐的枕木经久耐用，一般可用十五年。并能为国家节约大量资金。
