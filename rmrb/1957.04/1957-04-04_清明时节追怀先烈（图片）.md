Title: Remembering the Martyrs During Qingming Festival (Pictures)
Author: Shen Bingjun, Hu Baoyu
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 4

    Remembering the Martyrs During Qingming Festival
    Before Qingming Festival, people from all walks of life in Nanjing City, with solemn hearts, came to the Martyrs' Cemetery on Yuhuatai Hill, which is covered with pines and cypresses and blooming plum blossoms, to pay their respects to the martyrs and mourn them.
    Photo by Shen Bingjun
    In Handan City, Hebei Province, at the "Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Martyrs' Cemetery," every day many workers, farmers, students, units from the army, and individuals from all over come to visit the mausoleum and remember the martyrs. The picture shows Young Pioneers paying their respects to the martyrs. Photo by Hu Baoyu (Contribution from the PLA Pictorial)

### 清明时节追怀先烈（图片）
沈秉钧  胡宝玉
1957-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    清明时节追怀先烈
    清明节前，南京市各界人民怀着肃穆的心情，来到长满松柏，盛开梅花的雨花台烈士墓前，向烈士致敬和悼念。
    沈秉钧摄
    河北省邯郸市“晋冀鲁豫烈士陵园”，每天都有许多来自各地的工人、农民、学生、部队的单位和个人，来谒陵追怀先烈。图为少先队员向烈士们致敬。　　　　　　　　胡宝玉摄（解放军画报社稿）
