Title: Richards' Trip in Vain; Eisenhower Doctrine Hits Wall in Sudan
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, 23rd: A spokesman for U.S. President Eisenhower's special envoy Richards said on April 22nd that Richards and the Sudanese government had not reached a final understanding on the Eisenhower Doctrine.
    Richards held talks with the Sudanese government in Khartoum from April 20th to 22nd regarding the Eisenhower Doctrine. A communique issued after the talks stated that the two sides agreed to "further study" the issue.

### 理查兹白走一趟  艾森豪威尔主义在苏丹碰壁

1957-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    理查兹白走一趟
    艾森豪威尔主义在苏丹碰壁
    据新华社23日讯　喀土穆消息：美国总统特使理查兹的发言人4月22日说，理查兹和苏丹政府对艾森豪威尔主义没有达成最后的谅解。
    理查兹从4月20日到22日在喀土穆同苏丹政府举行了关于艾森豪威尔主义的会谈。会谈结束后发表的公报说，双方商定对这个问题“将进一步加以研究”。
