	International Briefs

	Bulgarian Military Delegation Departs to Visit Our Country
		At the invitation of Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, the Bulgarian military delegation left Sofia by plane on the morning of the 24th for a friendly visit to our country.

	Pyongyang Soviet Red Cross Hospital Transferred to Korea
		The Pyongyang Soviet Red Cross Hospital has, in accordance with the decision of the Soviet government, transferred all medical equipment and medicines to the Central Hospital of the Korean Red Cross free of charge. The relevant parties of Korea and the Soviet Union signed the transfer protocol in Pyongyang on the 24th.

	Belgium Establishes Belgium-China Friendship Association
		The Belgium-China Friendship Association, composed of people from all walks of life in Belgium, was officially established in Brussels recently. The Speaker of the Belgian House of Representatives, Mr. Huysmans, was elected as the honorary chairman of the association, and the Deputy Speaker, Mr. Brunfaut, was also elected as one of the four honorary members. Mr. Smetz, Honorary Dean of the Belgian Social Academy, was elected as the Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Friendship Association.
						(Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-04-26
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    保军事代表团启程来我国
    应中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅邀请到中国作友好访问的保加利亚军事代表团，24日早晨乘飞机从索非亚启程来我国。
    平壤苏联红十字医院移交朝鲜
    平壤苏联红十字医院已经根据苏联政府的决定把所有的医疗设备和药品无偿地转让给朝鲜红十字会中央医院。朝鲜和苏联的有关方面24日在平壤签订了移交的议定书。
    比利时成立比中友协
    由比利时各界人士组成的比中友好协会最近在布鲁塞尔正式成立。比利时众议院议长胡斯曼被选为协会的名誉主席，副议长布兰福也被选为四位名誉委员之一。被选为友协执行局主席的是比利时社会学院名誉院长斯梅兹。
                      （新华社）
