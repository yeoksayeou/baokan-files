Title: Mongolian Government Delegation Arrives in Erenhot
Author:
Date: 1957-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, April 28th: The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Tserendondovyn Sürenjav, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, arrived in Erenhot at 4:15 PM today after entering China at the invitation of the Chinese government to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

Welcoming the delegation in Erenhot were Kui Bi, Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Ga Rubusengge, Deputy Secretary-General of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Wang Qin, League Leader of the Xilin Gol League, among others. Also present to greet the delegation was Tserenpil. Dash, Consul General of the Mongolian People's Republic in Hohhot and member of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic.

The art and sports troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic also arrived on the same train.

After a short rest in Erenhot, the distinguished guests proceeded to Hohhot by special train.

Xinhua News Agency, Hailar, April 28th: The delegation from Choibalsan Province of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Batosukhe, Governor of Choibalsan Province, arrived in Hailar at approximately 10:00 AM today. They are visiting at the invitation of the People's Committee of the Hulunbuir League to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 蒙古政府代表团到达二连

1957-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府代表团到达二连
    新华社呼和浩特28日电　应我国政府邀请前来参加内蒙古自治区成立十周年庆祝大会的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，在团长（蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员、中央委员会第二书记）齐米德道尔吉·苏伦扎布率领下，于今天下午四时十五分进入我国国境到达二连。
    到二连欢迎的有中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记、内蒙古自治区副主席奎璧、内蒙古自治区人民委员会副秘书长嘎如布僧格、锡林郭勒盟盟长旺钦等。前往迎接的还有蒙古人民共和国驻呼和浩特总领事、蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团员策伯尔玛·达什。
    同车前来的还有蒙古人民共和国的艺术体育团。
    贵宾们在二连稍事休息后，即乘专车前往呼和浩特。
    据新华社海拉尔28日电　以蒙古人民共和国乔巴山省省长巴图苏和为首的乔巴山省代表，在今日上午十时许到达海拉尔。他们是应呼伦贝尔盟人民委员会邀请前来参加庆祝内蒙古自治区成立十周年的。
