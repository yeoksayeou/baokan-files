Title: Rhododendron
Author: Cai Qingsheng
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 8

    Rhododendron
    Cai Qingsheng
    This mountain, we've met before,
    Eight years ago, we fought here,
    I carried the red flag torn by shrapnel,
    And planted it on the highest point of this mountain peak.
    Now, I've become a surveyor,
    And I'm here again, to "capture" this mountain peak,
    The rhododendrons blooming all over the mountain!
    Are you fragments of the red flag from back then?

### 映山红
蔡庆生
1957-04-26
第8版()
专栏：

    映山红
    蔡庆生
    这座山，我们曾经见过面，
    八年前，曾在这里作过战，
    我扛着被弹片撕碎的红旗，
    插上了这座山峰的制高点。
    现在，我成了个测量队员，
    又来了，要把这座山峰“攻占”，
    满山盛开着的映山红啊！
    你们是不是当年红旗的碎片？
