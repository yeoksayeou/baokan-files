Title: Nosaka Sanzō Points Out That Japan Faces a Choice of Two Paths: Not Shaking Off American Shackles Means Self-Destruction

Author:

Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th Dispatch from TASS: “Akahata” published on the 16th the speech of Nosaka Sanzō, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, at a mass rally in Osaka. When talking about the issue of banning the testing of thermonuclear weapons, Nosaka Sanzō emphasized that it is extremely important to resolve this issue in the shortest possible time for the future of all mankind.

He criticized the Japanese government's attitude towards this issue. He pointed out that, in particular, the Japanese government's proposal for prior registration of thermonuclear weapons tests in fact cannot promote the resolution of this issue.

When talking about the domestic situation, Nosaka Sanzō emphasized that Japan is militarily and economically dependent on the United States. He said that in the current situation, Japan has only two paths before it. The first path is to follow the American policy, which is a path to the destruction of Japan; the second path is to get rid of the American shackles, which is a path to national independence.

### 野坂参三指出  日本面临两条道路的抉择  不摆脱美国束缚就是自寻毁灭

1957-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    野坂参三指出
    日本面临两条道路的抉择
    不摆脱美国束缚就是自寻毁灭
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社东京讯：“赤旗报”16日发表了日本共产党中央委员会第一书记野坂参三在大阪群众大会上的演说。野坂参三谈到禁止试验热核武器问题时强调指出，为了全人类的未来，在最短时期内解决这个问题是极重要的。
    他批评日本政府对这个问题的态度。他指出，特别是日本政府提出的预先登记热核武器试验的建议事实上不能促进这个问题的解决。
    野坂参三在谈到国内形势时强调指出日本在军事和经济上都依赖美国。他说，在目前的情况下，日本的面前只有两条路。第一条路是追随美国政策的路，这是毁灭日本的路；第二条路是摆脱美国束缚的路，这是一条民族独立的路。
