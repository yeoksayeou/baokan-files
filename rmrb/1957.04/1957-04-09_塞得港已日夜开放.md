Title: Port Said Open Day and Night

Author: 
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 8th: According to a report in "People's News" on April 7th, Port Said began to open day and night on the 6th.

    The port has permitted all ships waiting their turn to pass through the canal to dock. As many as four thousand port vendors have also been permitted to go aboard ships to sell famous Egyptian souvenirs.

### 塞得港已日夜开放

1957-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    塞得港已日夜开放
    新华社开罗8日电　据“人民报”4月7日报道，塞得港已经在6日开始昼夜开放。
    港口已经许可一切等着轮到它们通过运河的船只停泊。为数达四千人的港口小贩又得到许可到船上去售卖埃及有名的纪念品。
