Title: Czechoslovakian Delegation Arrives in Tianjin En Route to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 5th: The Czechoslovakian government delegation headed by Premier Viliam Široký, after concluding its visit to Korea, arrived in Tianjin by special plane from Pyongyang at noon today en route to a visit to the Mongolian People's Republic, accompanied by Park Sung-chul, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

    When Premier Široký and others stepped off the plane, Huang Huoqing, the Mayor of Tianjin, and his wife stepped forward to express a warm welcome to the distinguished guests. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and his wife.

    Avelzhid, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Dugar Surun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Tsedendishi, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, and his wife, who had specially arrived here yesterday to welcome the Czechoslovakian government delegation, also welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

    Also present at the airport today to welcome the distinguished guests were Grigel, the Czechoslovakian Ambassador to China, and his wife, and Kim Gui-nam, Chargé d'Affaires of the Korean Embassy in China.

### 捷代表团赴蒙古途中到天津

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    捷代表团赴蒙古途中到天津
    据新华社天津5日电　以威廉·西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团，在结束了对朝鲜的访问以后前往蒙古人民共和国访问的途中，由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相朴成哲陪同，在今天中午从平壤乘专机到达天津。
    西罗基总理等走下飞机后，天津市市长黄火青和夫人上前向贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有：我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、礼宾司副司长张世杰和我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛及夫人。
    昨天下午特地到这里来迎接捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团的蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席阿维尔齐德、外交部副部长杜格尔苏伦和蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使馆代办策登依什及夫人也到机场欢迎贵宾。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使格里哥尔和夫人、朝鲜驻华大使馆代办金贵南。
