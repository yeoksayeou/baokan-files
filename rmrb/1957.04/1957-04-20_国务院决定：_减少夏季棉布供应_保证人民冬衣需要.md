Title: State Council Decides: Reduce Summer Cotton Fabric Supply to Guarantee People's Winter Clothing Needs
Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — State Council Decision on Cotton Fabric Supply for 1957
(Approved at the 46th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on April 12, 1957)

The State Council reviewed the report from the Ministry of Commerce on the issue of cotton fabric supply for 1957 (see page 3 of today's newspaper). Due to reduced cotton field acreage in some areas following the 1956 floods, the state's cotton purchase plan was not fulfilled. Furthermore, relaxed supply policies last year led to increased fabric sales. Therefore, the market supply of yarn and fabric in 1957 will be less than the estimated supply when this year's fabric purchase coupons were issued in July 1956. To adjust the discrepancy between issued fabric purchase coupons and the possible supply of cotton fabric, and to ensure the people's need for winter clothing, it is necessary to appropriately reduce the supply of cotton fabric for the summer. The following decisions are now made:

	I.	The first phase of fabric purchase coupons, valid from September 1, 1956, to April 30, 1957, will remain fully valid until the end of August 1957.

	II.	The second phase of fabric purchase coupons, valid from May 1, 1957, to August 31, 1957, will be used at half the face value.

	III.	Individual provinces that issued year-round fabric purchase coupons without phased coupons, starting from April 20th, all fabric purchase coupons will be used at half the face value.

	IV.	All types of cotton fabric stores in national state-owned, supply and marketing cooperatives, and joint public-private stores, selling cotton fabric based on the aforementioned three provisions, will guarantee full supply based on the fabric purchase coupons.

	V.	Knitted goods, silk, and wool fabrics can still be purchased freely without coupons.

### 国务院决定：  减少夏季棉布供应  保证人民冬衣需要

1957-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院决定：
    减少夏季棉布供应
    保证人民冬衣需要
    新华社19日讯　国务院关于1957年棉布供应的决定
    （1957年4月12日国务院全体会议第四十六次会议通过）
    国务院审查了商业部关于1957年棉布供应问题的报告（见今天本报第三版）。由于1956年水灾以后，部分地区棉田减产，国家收购棉花的计划未能完成，再加上去年放宽了供应，多销了布匹，因此1957年市场纱布的供应量将比1956年7月发出本年度购布证的时候所估计的供应量为少。为了调整已发购布证和可能供应的棉布数量之间的差额，为了保证人民的冬衣需要，必须适当减少夏季的棉布供应，现在决定：
    一、由1956年9月1日至1957年4月30日通用的第一期购布证继续十足通用至1957年8月底。
    二、由1957年5月1日至8月31日通用的第二期购布证一律按票面数额对折使用。
    三、凡是发了全年通用购布证而没有分期发购布证的个别省份，自4月20日起，全部购布证一律按票面数额对折使用。
    四、凡是按照上述三项规定向全国国营、供销合作社、公私合营出售棉布的商店凭购布证买布的，各类棉布商店保证全部供应。
    五、针织品、绸缎、呢绒，照旧自由购买，不需凭证。
