Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for the Sino-Ceylonese Friendship Association Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 11th — Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a welcome banquet at noon today for the Cultural Delegation of the Sino-Ceylonese Friendship Association, which is currently visiting Beijing.

At the banquet, Chu Tunan said: The long history, the common destiny suffered in modern times, and current aspirations link the Chinese and Ceylonese people together, and therefore we feel particularly cordial in welcoming our Ceylonese friends here. He said that he believes that through this visit by the delegation, cultural exchanges and understanding and friendship between the two peoples of China and Ceylon will be further promoted.

Ananda K. Coomaraswamy, the head of the delegation, said at the banquet that today conditions are such that the long-standing cultural and friendly ties between Ceylon and China can be renewed and developed. He said that although Ceylon is not a wealthy country in terms of resources, the friendship of the Ceylonese people towards the Chinese people is very rich. He also presented gifts on behalf of the Sino-Ceylonese Friendship Association to the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

The Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Pereira, was invited to attend the banquet.

On the Chinese side, those who attended the banquet also included Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Vice Secretary-General Jusan Venerable Master, as well as representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and educational, artistic, women's, sports, and Christian circles in the capital.

### 楚图南欢宴锡中友协文化代表团

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    楚图南欢宴锡中友协文化代表团
    新华社11日讯　正在北京访问的锡中友好协会文化代表团，今天中午出席了中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南举办的欢迎宴会。
    楚图南在宴会上说：悠久的历史、在近代所遭受的共同命运和当前的愿望，把中国人民和锡兰人民联系起来，因而在这里欢迎锡兰朋友使我们感到格外亲切。他说，相信通过代表团的这次访问，将会进一步促进中、锡两国的文化交流和人民之间的了解和友谊。
    代表团团长阿南达·库马拉在宴会上说，今天已经有条件使锡中两国有着悠久历史的文化和友好联系得到更新和发展。他说，锡兰虽然是物产并不富裕的国家，但是锡兰人民对中国人民的友情却是很富有的。他还代表锡中友好协会向中国人民对外文化协会赠送了礼品。
    锡兰驻华大使佩雷拉应邀出席了宴会。
    中国方面出席宴会的还有中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、副秘书长巨赞法师，以及外交部和首都教育、艺术、妇女、体育、基督教界的代表。
