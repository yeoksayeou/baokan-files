Title: Vietnam Purchases a Civilian Aircraft from China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: Vietnam National Corporation for the Import of Machinery, Fats and Oils recently contacted the China National Technical Import Corporation to purchase a civilian aircraft for re-export. Both parties signed the trade contract on the 11th.

### 越南向我购买民用飞机一架

1957-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    越南向我购买民用飞机一架
    新华社13日讯　越南机器油脂进口总公司最近向中国技术进口公司接洽购买转口的民用飞机一架，双方已经在11日签订了这项贸易合同。
