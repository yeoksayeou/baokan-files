Title: Cyrankiewicz Ends Visit to North Korea; Polish Delegation Passes Through Beijing En Route to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 17th: Chairman Cyrankiewicz and the Polish People's Republic government delegation he led concluded their visit to North Korea and left Pyongyang by plane at 9:00 AM today. The delegation will pass through Beijing on its way to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Kim Tu-bong, and other Korean state leaders went to the airport to see them off. Many people lined the streets from Pyongyang to the airport to bid farewell to the delegation.

Chairman Cyrankiewicz delivered a farewell speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude for the warm reception given to the delegation by the Korean people. He said that the Polish government delegation was moved by the brilliant achievements of the Korean people in peaceful construction after suffering serious damage in the war. Chairman Cyrankiewicz expressed his satisfaction with the joint statement he signed with Premier Kim Il-sung on the same day and believed that the friendly relations between the two countries would be further developed.

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: After concluding their visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Polish Council of Ministers Chairman Cyrankiewicz and the Polish government delegation he led flew through Beijing this morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, Ministers Ye Jizhuang and Zhang Bojun, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu and his wife, and Ambassador Wang Bingnan and his wife were at the airport to greet them.

Polish Ambassador to China Kirylok and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, Kim Kwi-nam, also greeted them at the airport.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, Park Sung-chul, accompanied the Polish government delegation to Beijing.

After disembarking, Chairman Cyrankiewicz and Premier Zhou Enlai and others had lunch together at the airport.

At 1:10 PM, the Polish government delegation shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai and others to say goodbye before boarding the plane. Mongolian Ambassador to China Ochirbat also went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 17th: At the invitation of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Polish government delegation led by Cyrankiewicz arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane today. Thousands of residents of the Mongolian capital lined up to welcome the Polish guests.

At the airport, Cyrankiewicz said to the people welcoming the delegation that the Mongolian people are fighting for the construction of socialism, and the Polish people are also building socialism like you. Our goals are the same, and our friendship is unbreakable. Our visit will further strengthen the economic and cultural development of the two countries. He also said in Mongolian: "Long live Polish-Mongolian friendship."

Chairman Tsedenbal of the Mongolian Council of Ministers warmly welcomed the Polish government delegation on behalf of the Mongolian people. He said that the Polish people have bravely defeated the provocations of imperialism, and we are very happy. Poland has made great achievements in economic construction, and we wish the Polish people even greater achievements in future construction.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them today included Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsend, and other party and government leaders. Envoys from various countries to Mongolia also went to the airport to welcome them. (Attached Photo)

### 西伦凯维兹结束在朝鲜的访问  波兰代表团过北京到蒙古

1957-04-18
第5版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹结束在朝鲜的访问
    波兰代表团过北京到蒙古
    新华社平壤17日电  西伦凯维兹主席和他率领的波兰人民共和国政府代表团结束了在朝鲜的访问，今天上午九时乘飞机离开平壤。代表团将途经北京去蒙古人民共和国访问。
    金日成首相、金枓奉委员长等朝鲜国家领导人都到机场欢送。从平壤市到机场的路上有许多群众夹道欢送代表团。
    西伦凯维兹主席在机场上致告别词，对朝鲜人民对代表团的热情接待表示感谢。他说，波兰政府代表团看到朝鲜人民在遭受战争的严重破坏的情况下在和平建设中取得的光辉成就，并且受到了感动。西伦凯维兹主席对他同金日成首相在当天签署的联合声明表示满意，并且认为两国之间的友好关系将得到进一步的发展。
    新华社17日讯  波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和他所率领的波兰政府代表团，结束了在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问后，于今天上午乘飞机途经北京，前往蒙古人民共和国访问。
    到机场迎接的，有周恩来总理，贺龙副总理，彭真副委员长，叶季壮、章伯钧部长，章汉夫副部长和夫人，王炳南大使和夫人。
    波兰驻中国大使基里洛克，朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南也到机场迎接。
    朝鲜外务省副相朴成哲陪同波兰政府代表团到北京。
    西伦凯维兹主席等下机后，和周恩来总理等在机场共进午餐。
    下午一时十分，波兰政府代表团在登上飞机前和周恩来总理等一一握手告别。蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特也到机场欢送了贵宾们。
    据新华社乌兰巴托17日电  应蒙古人民共和国的邀请，由西伦凯维兹率领的波兰政府代表团今天坐飞机到达乌兰巴托。成千上万的蒙古首都人民列队欢迎波兰贵宾。
    西伦凯维兹在机场上向欢迎代表团的人们说，蒙古人民正为建设社会主义而奋斗着，波兰人民也和你们一样建设着社会主义，我们的目标一致，我们的友谊是牢不可破的。我们的访问将更加加强两国经济文化的发展。他还用蒙古话说：波蒙友谊万岁。
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔代表蒙古人民热烈欢迎波兰政府代表团。他说，波兰人民英勇地击败了帝国主义的挑衅，我们感到很高兴。波兰在经济建设中已经取得很大成就，祝波兰人民在今后建设中取得更大成就。
    今天到机场欢迎的有蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴、部长会议副主席曾德和其他党政领导人。各国驻蒙古使节也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
