Title: Commemorating the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution: The Soviet Union Will Publish Many Important Documents
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 5

    To Commemorate the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution
    The Soviet Union Will Publish Many Important Documents

    (Our Moscow Dispatch, April 2nd) The Soviet Union will publish many important collections of documents to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. These collections are being edited by the Institute of Marxism-Leninism directly under the Central Committee of the CPSU, mainly compilations of documents from the Central Committee of the Bolshevik Party during the preparation and execution of the October Revolution. Among them is the record of the Central Committee from 1917 to 1918, which will add many new documents compared to the 1929 version. A compilation of "Resolutions of the Central Committee of the CPSU on Economic Issues" will be published, collecting important resolutions of the Central Committee of the CPSU on the organization and management of socialist economic issues over the past forty years. Active preparations are underway for a new edition of the Complete Works of Lenin, with the first volume expected to be published by the end of the year. The Institute of Marxism-Leninism will involve the Marxism-Leninism branches of the Union Republics and the Party History Research Institutes of the People's Democratic countries in the editing of the collection of essays commemorating the October Revolution. These essays will explain the role of the central and local organizations of the Communist Party in preparing and carrying out the October Revolution, and the influence of the October Revolution on the people of Europe and Asia. Memoirs of active participants in the October Revolution will be published. The Institute of Marxism-Leninism and the Institute of History of the Soviet Academy of Sciences are jointly compiling a collection of "Decrees of the Soviet Government." It is also jointly compiling a multi-volume collection of "The Great October Socialist Revolution" with other historical research institutions.

### 纪念十月革命四十周年  苏联将出版许多重要文件

1957-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念十月革命四十周年
    苏联将出版许多重要文件
    本报莫斯科2日电　苏联将出版许多纪念十月革命四十周年的重要文集，这些文集正由苏共中央直属马列主义学院编辑中，主要是布尔什维克党中央在准备和进行十月革命时期的文件的汇编。其中有1917到1918年中央的纪录，它比1929年版本将增添许多新文件。将出版“苏共中央关于经济问题的决议”汇编，它收集四十年来苏共中央关于组织和管理社会主义经济问题的重要决议。积极进行新版列宁全集的准备工作，年底可出第一卷。马列学院将吸收加盟共和国的马列分院和人民民主国家党史研究所参加十月革命纪念论文集的编辑工作，这些论文将说明共产党中央和地方组织在准备和进行十月革命的作用，和十月革命对于欧洲和亚洲人民的影响。将出版十月革命积极参加者的回忆录。马列学院和苏联科学院历史研究所正合编着“苏维埃政权法令”集。它还和其他研究历史的机关合编“伟大的十月社会主义革命”多卷集。
