Title: Night at Sea
Author: Jing Yindun
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 8

    Night at Sea
    Jing Yindun
    The clouds and sky are a blur,
    The tide surges and swells,
    Looking at the lights on the shore from afar,
    Each one shines like crystal.
    Ah, here is the vast sea outside Yulin Port,
    Patrol boats lurk in the waves,
    The mountains and waters are guarded everywhere,
    To ensure the tranquility of the motherland.
    Please, dear people, think of them,
    Trust in the loyalty of the naval defense fighters,
    Sleep quietly, sweetly, and peacefully,
    Regardless of rain,
    Or wind…
    Late night on the Spring Festival, January 30, 1957.

### 海上之夜
井岩盾
1957-04-09
第8版()
专栏：

    海上之夜
    井岩盾
    云天一片模糊，
    潮流起伏汹涌，
    远看岸上灯火，
    颗颗亮似水晶。
    啊，这里是榆林港外的汪洋大海，
    波涛中潜藏着巡逻快艇，
    山头水上处处戒备，
    为了确保祖国的安宁。
    请亲爱的人们想着他们，
    信赖海防战士的忠诚，
    静静地甜蜜地安稳入睡，
    不管是下雨，
    也不管是刮风……
    1957、1、30春节深夜。
