Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 5

	The six-member delegation of the National Consultative Assembly of Maghreb (i.e., Morocco) arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 29th. The delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. The delegation is headed by Haji Muhammad Bu Amrani, Member of the Independence Party and First Vice-Chairman of the Parliamentary Budget Committee. The members include: Hussein Hajibi, Member of Parliament, First Vice-Chairman of the Parliamentary Committee on Economic Affairs, and Chairman of the Agricultural Workers' Union; Omer Dweiri, Member of Parliament and Secretary of the Parliamentary Committee on Economic Affairs; Abendu Le-Kerim, Member of Parliament, Secretary of the Parliamentary Committee on Social Affairs, and Secretary-General of the Independence Party Youth League; Muhammad Saldani, Secretary of Parliament; and Idris Merkwar, owner of a film studio.
	The seven-member delegation of the Arab International Confederation of Trade Unions, led by Muhammad Helmi Ibrahim, Advisor to the Arab International Confederation of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 29th. The delegation, composed of representatives from six trade union organizations from four countries—Egypt, Sudan, Syria, and Lebanon—came to Beijing at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations. Also arriving in Beijing on the 29th to attend the "May Day" celebrations were the Bulgarian Trade Union Delegation headed by Boris Blagoev, the Moroccan Trade Union Delegation headed by Dibari Mosaddan, the Chilean Railway Workers' Union Delegation, and two trade union representatives from New Zealand.
	The Finnish Peace Delegation, consisting of six members led by Amas Torssa, Chairman of the delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Finnish Peace Defenders Organization, arrived in Beijing on the 29th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
	At the invitation of the Architectural Society of China, the first group of seven members of the All-German Architects Delegation to China, jointly formed by authoritative figures in the architectural field from the German Democratic Republic and the Federal Republic of Germany, arrived in Beijing on the 29th. The remaining members will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.
	The Japanese Archaeological Delegation, consisting of ten members headed by Harada Sukito, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 29th. The delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 友好往来

1957-04-30
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    马格里勃（即摩洛哥）国民协商议会访华代表团一行六人，在29日上午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国访问的。代表团由独立党议员、议会预算委员会第一副主席哈吉·穆罕默德·布阿姆拉尼任团长，团员有：议员、议会经济事务委员会第一副主席、农业工人联合会主席侯赛因·哈吉比，议员、议会经济事务委员会秘书欧默尔·杜威里，议员、议会社会事务委员会秘书、独立党青年团总书记阿本杜勒·克里姆，议会秘书穆罕默德·萨尔达尼和电影制片厂主伊德里斯·默克瓦尔。
    由阿拉伯国际工会联合会顾问穆罕默德·海尔米·伊布拉罕率领的阿拉伯国际工会联合会代表团一行七人，在29日上午乘飞机到达北京。这个由埃及、苏丹、叙利亚、黎巴嫩四个国家的六个工会组织的代表组成的代表团，是应中华全国总工会的邀请前来北京参加“五一”观礼的。29日到达北京参加“五一”观礼的，还有以包利斯·布拉哥耶夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团，以狄巴里·摩萨旦为首的摩洛哥工会代表团，智利铁路工会代表团和新西兰的工会代表两人。
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国参观访问的芬兰和平代表团，一行六人，在团长、芬兰和平保卫者组织副主席阿玛斯·托尔萨率领下在29日到达北京。
    应中国建筑学会的邀请，由德意志民主共和国和德意志联邦共和国的建筑界权威人士联合组成的全德建筑师访华代表团，首批七人于29日到达北京，其余人员将在日内到京。
    以原田淑人为首的日本考古代表团一行十人29日晚上坐火车到达北京。代表团是应中国科学院邀请来我国访问的。
