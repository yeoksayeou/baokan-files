Title: Chairman of the Swedish Dance Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Mr. Haeger, Chairman of the Swedish Dance Promotion Association, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 5th for a visit to China.

During their stay in China, Mr. Haeger and his wife will visit and learn about the development of drama art and dance in our country.

### 瑞典舞蹈协会主席到京

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典舞蹈协会主席到京
    新华社5日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，来我国访问的瑞典舞蹈促进协会主席海格和他的夫人在5日下午乘机抵京。
    海格和他的夫人在中国期间，将对我国戏剧艺术和舞蹈发展情况进行访问。
