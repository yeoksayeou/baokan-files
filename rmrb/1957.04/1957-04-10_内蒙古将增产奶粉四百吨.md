Title: Inner Mongolia to Increase Milk Powder Production by 400 Tons
Author:
Date: 1957-04-10
Page: 2

Inner Mongolia to Increase Milk Powder Production by 400 Tons

(Our report) The Inner Mongolia Dairy Industry Company has formulated a plan to increase production and practice economy in accordance with market needs. On the basis of the state's controlled targets, it has decided to increase milk powder production by 300 to 400 tons, and the annual output will exceed three times the output of last year.

### 内蒙古将增产奶粉四百吨

1957-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古将增产奶粉四百吨
    本报讯　内蒙古乳品工业公司根据市场的需要，制定了增产节约计划。在国家控制指标的基础上，决定增产乳粉三百吨到四百吨，年产量将超过去年产量的三倍。
