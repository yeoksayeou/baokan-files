Title: Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik Visits Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, April 18th: Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma and former President Sao Shwe Thaik and his wife arrived in Hangzhou by special plane from Wuhan at noon today. In the afternoon, Sao Shwe Thaik and his wife, accompanied by Hangzhou Deputy Mayor Zhang Shixiang, toured West Lake. In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a welcoming banquet hosted by Zhejiang Provincial Governor Sha Wenhan.

### 萧恢塔议长游杭州

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    萧恢塔议长游杭州
    据新华社杭州18日电　缅甸联邦民族院议长、前总统萧恢塔和他的夫人等今天中午由武汉乘专机到达杭州。下午，萧恢塔和他的夫人等由杭州市副市长张世祥陪同游览了西湖。傍晚，贵宾们出席了浙江省省长沙文汉的欢迎宴会。
