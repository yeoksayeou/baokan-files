Title: Demanding that British Authorities Abandon Hydrogen Bomb Tests; British Communist Party Calls on People to Launch Struggle; Manchester Intellectual Circles Establish Organization Against Hydrogen Bomb Tests
Author:
Date: 1957-04-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th, TASS London, April 13th dispatch: The Political Committee of the British Communist Party issued a statement on the issue of hydrogen bomb testing. The statement said: "At the Bermuda Conference, the British and American governments made it clear that they had decided to continue testing. They rejected the Soviet Union's proposal on the eve of the Bermuda Conference, which advocated the immediate conclusion of an agreement to stop testing as a step towards a complete ban on testing.

The Political Committee calls on the British people to demand that the British government agree to the Soviet proposal and cease preparing for tests on Christmas Island. It also calls on the British working people to launch a large-scale struggle to ban the testing of hydrogen weapons, organizing mass rallies and demonstrations to protest the government's attitude.

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th dispatch: TASS London reports: The "Manchester Guardian" published on the 12th a call from the extraordinary committee fighting to cancel the planned hydrogen bomb tests, calling for the collection of funds for this struggle. Among those who signed the call were: the famous British scientist Russell, actor Milligan, writer Housman, poet Comfort, etc.

This committee was established by a group of well-known British science, culture, and art workers in Manchester. Committee members plan to contact the Japanese government to work together to strive for the cancellation of British thermonuclear weapons tests to be conducted in the Pacific region.

### 要求英国当局放弃氢弹试验  英共号召人民展开斗争  曼彻斯特知识界成立反对试验氢弹组织

1957-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    要求英国当局放弃氢弹试验
    英共号召人民展开斗争
    曼彻斯特知识界成立反对试验氢弹组织
    据新华社13日讯　塔斯社伦敦13日消息：英国共产党政治委员会就试验氢弹问题发表声明。声明说：“在百慕大会议上，英国和美国政府明确表示，他们决定继续试验。他们拒绝了苏联在百慕大会议前夕提出的建议，这个建议主张立即缔结停止试验的协定，作为完全禁止试验的一个步骤。
    政治委员会号召英国人民要求英国政府同意苏联的建议，不再继续准备在圣诞岛进行试验。它还号召英国劳动人民展开一个禁止试验氢武器的大规模斗争，组织群众集会和示威游行，抗议政府的态度。
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：“曼彻斯特卫报”12日刊登了争取取消预定的氢弹试验的非常委员会的号召书，呼吁为这一斗争募集基金。在号召书上签名的有：英国著名的科学家罗素、演员米利甘、作家豪斯曼、诗人康福特等。
    这个委员会是由在曼彻斯特的一批有名的英国科学、文化和艺术工作者成立的。委员会委员计划同日本政府进行接触，以便共同努力，争取取消英国要在太平洋区域进行的热核武器试验。
