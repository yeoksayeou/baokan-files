Title: U.S. Response to Egyptian Memorandum: Insists on Depriving Egypt of Sovereignty over Canal
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 1st, Cairo Dispatch: U.S. Ambassador to Egypt, Hare, notified Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi on March 31st of the U.S. opinion regarding Egypt's memorandum on the Suez Canal management issue.

According to Western news agency reports, the U.S. demands that the six principles adopted by the Security Council last year be the basis for resolving the problem. It emphasizes that the so-called operation of the canal should be insulated from the politics of any one country, which in the past has been the excuse used by Western countries to deprive Egypt of its sovereignty over the Suez Canal.

These reports also say that the U.S. still insists on giving half of the canal tolls to the World Bank for safekeeping and only giving half to Egypt as canal management fees.

According to Washington reports: U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Near Eastern Affairs Rountree and State Department Coordinator for Suez Affairs Dillon convened a meeting of the executive committee of the Suez Canal Users Association on March 31st, attended by the ambassadors of Britain, France, Italy, Norway, and Iran. According to U.S. officials, the meeting discussed how Western countries should proceed in light of Egypt's submission of a memorandum on the use of the Suez Canal.

### 美国答复埃及备忘录  坚持剥夺埃及对运河主权

1957-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    美国答复埃及备忘录
    坚持剥夺埃及对运河主权
    新华社1日讯　开罗消息：美国驻埃及大使赫尔已经在3月31日把美国对埃及关于苏伊士运河管理问题的备忘录的意见通知埃及外交部长法齐。
    据西方通讯社的消息透露，美国要求以去年安全理事会通过的六项原则为解决问题的基础。它强调所谓运河的经营应该同任何一个国家的政治绝缘，而这一点过去一直是西方国家要剥夺埃及对苏伊士运河的主权的借口。
    这些消息还说，美国仍然坚持要把运河通行费的一半交给世界银行保存，而只把一半交给埃及作为运河管理费。
    另据华盛顿消息：美国负责中东事务的助理国务卿朗特里和国务院统筹苏伊士问题的狄龙3月31日召集了英、法、意大利、挪威和伊朗五国大使，举行苏伊士运河使用国协会执行委员会会议。据美国官员说，会议讨论了在埃及提出关于使用苏伊士运河的备忘录的情况下西方国家怎样办的问题。
