Title: Overseas Chinese Service Centers Established Along the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway Line
Author:
Date: 1957-04-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 9th: Overseas Chinese service centers have been established in major towns along the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway, including Yingtan, Nanping, Zhangzhou, and Xiamen, to serve returning overseas Chinese and their families.

These service centers are funded and built by the central and Fujian provincial overseas Chinese affairs departments. The locations of the centers are all in scenic areas. Each service center has service counters, information desks, etc., to introduce places of interest and guide sightseeing tours for overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots; to book train, ship, and bus tickets; to handle the collection, storage, consignment, and mailing of luggage and packages; and to assist returned overseas Chinese and their families in handling entry and exit visas and Hong Kong transit visa procedures.

At present, the Overseas Chinese Service Centers in Yingtan, Nanping, and Zhangzhou have already started operations. The new three-story building of the Xiamen Overseas Chinese Service Center is also nearing completion and is expected to officially open on May 1st.

### 鹰厦线设立华侨服务社

1957-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

    鹰厦线设立华侨服务社
    新华社福州9日电　鹰厦铁路沿线主要城镇鹰潭、南平、漳州、厦门等地都设立了为归国华侨、侨眷等服务的华侨服务社。
    这些服务社是由中央和福建省的华侨事务部门投资兴建的。社址都设在风景优美的区域。各个服务社都设有服务台、询问处等，以便为华侨和港澳同胞介绍名胜古迹，引导游览参观，代订火车、轮船、汽车客票，代办提取、保管、托运行李包裹和邮寄包裹，以及协助归侨、侨眷办理出入国和过港签证手续等。
    目前，鹰潭、南平和漳州的华侨服务社已经开始营业。厦门华侨服务社新建的三层大楼也即将完工，5月1日可以正式营业。
