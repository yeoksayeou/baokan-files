Title: Korean Gymnastics Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 8th. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea men's and women's gymnastics team, which was invited to visit our country, returned home by train today from Tianjin after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, and Tianjin. This gymnastics team, composed of Korean People's Army soldiers, university students, and sports teachers, exchanged experiences with Chinese gymnasts during their stay in China.

### 朝鲜体操队回国

1957-04-09
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    朝鲜体操队回国
    新华社天津8日电　应邀来我国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国男女体操队在访问了北京、上海、天津以后，今天由天津乘火车回国。这个由朝鲜人民军战士、大学生和体育教师组成的体操队，在中国期间曾经同中国的体操运动员们在一起交流了经验。
