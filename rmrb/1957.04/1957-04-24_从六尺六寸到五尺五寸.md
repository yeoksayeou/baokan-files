Title: From Six Chi Six Cun to Five Chi Five Cun
Author: Chen Yinhan
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 4

Column: Saving Cotton Cloth

    From Six Chi Six Cun to Five Chi Five Cun
    Chen Yinhan
    Liang Futang, Ye Lin, Lu Xiangquan and other tailoring workers from the Second and First Production Cooperatives of Jinhua Garment Factory in Zhejiang Province improved the cutting method, reducing the amount of cloth used for each medium-sized young woman's garment from the original six chi six cun to five chi five cun, saving one chi one cun of cloth per garment. This is a very outstanding creative labor! Here, we publish the cutting pattern of the medium-sized young woman's garment created by comrades Liang Futang and others below:
    Instructions: Material needed: 5 chi 5 cun, Sleeve allowance length: 1 chi 7 cun 6 fen, Cuff: 5 cun
          Cloth width: 2 chi 4 cun, Sleeve finished length: 1 chi 6 cun 5 fen, Collar length: 1 chi 1 cun 4 fen
          Garment allowance length: 2 chi 2 cun 3 fen, Chest: 1 chi 1 cun
          Garment finished length: 2 chi 1 cun, Middle back: 1 chi 1 cun 4 fen
          Waist: 2 chi 9 cun 5 fen, Shoulder width: 4 cun 2 fen        (Collar uses horizontal material)
    Scraps: 1. 2. Pocket flap face 7. 8. Collar stand  14. 15. Lower facing
    Instructions: 3. 4. Pocket flap lining 9. Small pocket opening 16. 17. Pocket welt
          5. Collar face  10. 11. Upper facing 18-22 Large and small pocket reinforcement pieces
          6. Collar lining  12. 13. Middle facing
                              (Originally published in Zhejiang Daily, slightly edited by this newspaper)

### 从六尺六寸到五尺五寸
陈因汗
1957-04-24
第4版()
专栏：节约棉布

    从六尺六寸到五尺五寸
    陈因汗
    浙江省金华被服第二、第一生产社裁缝工人梁福堂、叶林、卢香全等改进裁剪方法，使每件中号女青年装由原用布六尺六寸缩减到五尺五寸，使每件衣服节约用布一尺一寸。这是一件十分出色的创造性劳动！这里，我们把梁福堂等同志创造的中号女青年装的裁剪图样刊登在下面：
    说明  用料5尺5寸　出袖毛长1尺7寸6分　袖口5寸
          布阔2尺4寸　出袖净长1尺6寸5分　领长1尺1寸4分
          衣毛长2尺2寸3分　胸1尺1寸
          衣净长2尺1寸  中背1尺1寸4分
          腰身2尺9寸5分　肩阔4寸2分　　　　（领用横料）
    碎料  1.2．袋盖面　7.8．领脚  14.15．下接挂面
    说明  3.4．袋盖里　9．小袋口　16.17．袋嵌线
          5．领面　10.11．上接挂面  18—22大小袋垫头
          6．领里　12.13．中接挂面
