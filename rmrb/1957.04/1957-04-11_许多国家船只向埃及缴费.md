Title: Many Countries' Ships Pay Fees to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 10th: Following the clearing of the Suez Canal, yesterday twelve ships flying the flags of Italy, Belgium, the Federal Republic of Germany, Panama, Sweden, Costa Rica, Greece, Ethiopia, and other countries passed through the canal. All ships paid transit fees to the Egyptian authorities as required.

### 许多国家船只向埃及缴费

1957-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    许多国家船只向埃及缴费
    新华社开罗10日电　在苏伊士运河清理完毕后，昨天有悬挂意大利、比利时、德意志联邦共和国、巴拿马、瑞典、哥斯达黎加、希腊、埃塞俄比亚和其他国家旗帜的船只十二艘通过运河。所有船只都按照规定向埃及当局缴纳了通行费。
