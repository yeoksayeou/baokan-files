Title: Defying World Opinion, the United States Will Conduct Nuclear Weapons Tests Again
Author:
Date: 1957-04-05
Page: 6

    Defying World Opinion
    The United States Will Conduct Nuclear Weapons Tests Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, April 4th: The United States Atomic Energy Commission announced on the 3rd that it will begin a series of new nuclear weapons test explosions in Nevada around May 15th, and that these explosions will be carried out intermittently throughout the summer.

### 蔑视世界舆论的要求  美国又将试验核武器

1957-04-05
第6版()
专栏：

    蔑视世界舆论的要求
    美国又将试验核武器
    新华社4日讯　华盛顿消息：美国原子能委员会3日宣布，大约将从5月15日起在美国内华达州开始作一系列的新核子武器试验性爆炸，而且在整个夏季都将时断时续地举行这类爆炸。
