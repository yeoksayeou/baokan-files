    Xiaogiao Town, Yingshan County, Sichuan Province, is a distribution center for draught cattle. The county supply and marketing cooperative held a draught cattle exchange meeting here. Farmers are leading their surplus draught cattle to the exchange meeting to sell. Photo by Liu Quanyu (Sichuan Peasant Newspaper)
