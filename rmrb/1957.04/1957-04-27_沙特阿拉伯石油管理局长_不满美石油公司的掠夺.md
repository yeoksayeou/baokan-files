Title: Saudi Arabian Oil Administration Director Dissatisfied with Exploitation by US Oil Companies
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, April 24th: According to the "Commercial Daily" report, the Director of the Saudi Arabian Oil Administration, Turayki, said that Saudi Arabia is dissatisfied with the policies of the American oil monopoly capital, ARAMCO, on various issues, mainly tax issues.

The newspaper quoted Turayki as saying that ARAMCO is making a fortune at the expense of Saudi Arabia's interests. For example, it sells oil extracted from Saudi Arabia at $2.69 per barrel, but only pays taxes at $1.97 per barrel. In this way, ARAMCO can obtain a profit of 72 cents from each barrel of oil.

### 沙特阿拉伯石油管理局长  不满美石油公司的掠夺

1957-04-27
第6版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯石油管理局长
    不满美石油公司的掠夺
    据新华社25日讯　塔斯社纽约24日讯：据“商务日报”报道，沙特阿拉伯石油管理局局长图拉伊基说，沙特阿拉伯对美国石油垄断资本的阿美石油公司在各种问题、主要是纳税问题上的政策感到不满。
    报纸援引图拉伊基的话说，阿美石油公司靠损害沙特阿拉伯的利益发财，比如，它按每桶两美元六十九分的价格出售从沙特阿拉伯开采的石油，但是却按每桶一美元九十七分的价格纳税。这样一来，阿美石油公司就可以从每桶的石油获得七十二分的利润。
