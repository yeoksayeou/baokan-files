Title: Chairman Mao Receives and Hosts Banquet for Polish Government Delegation; Premier Zhou Begins Talks with Chairman Cyrankiewicz
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Polish People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, at Qin Zheng Dian in Zhongnanhai this afternoon.

Chairman Mao Zedong held a ninety-minute conversation with Chairman Cyrankiewicz and others in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, He Long, Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan.

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong hosted a banquet at Qin Zheng Dian in Zhongnanhai this evening to welcome Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland Józef Cyrankiewicz and his wife, as well as all members of the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic.

Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a speech at the banquet, expressing a warm welcome to the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic headed by Chairman Cyrankiewicz.

Chairman Mao said that Poland is a country with a deep friendship with China, and Poland is a member of the socialist camp. The friendship between China and Poland is growing day by day. He wished Poland all the best, wished the socialist countries ever greater unity, and wished the friendship between China and Poland ever greater development.

Chairman Mao raised his glass to wish all the comrades of the delegation good health, to wish First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party Gomulka good health, and to wish Chairman of the Polish Council of State Zawadzki good health.

Chairman Cyrankiewicz then delivered a speech. He said that he would convey Chairman Mao Zedong's congratulations to Poland and his wishes for the increasing unity of the socialist countries to all the Polish people.

Cyrankiewicz believed that the best response of the Polish people to Chairman Mao Zedong's congratulations would be to build socialism better in Poland and to strengthen Poland's unity with socialist countries. He added that Chairman Mao Zedong's congratulations were helping Poland to build socialism and helping socialist countries to unite.

Chairman Cyrankiewicz proposed a toast to the good health of Chairman Mao Zedong, to the Communist Party of China, to the cooperation between the two parties, and to the friendship between China and Poland.

Also present at tonight's banquet were Vice Chairman Zhu De and his wife, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, He Long, Nie Rongzhen, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan.

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic Józef Cyrankiewicz held talks at 3:00 PM on the 8th.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Chairmen of the State Planning Commission Yang Yingjie, Chai Shufan, Vice Minister of Light Industry Song Naide, Vice Minister of Higher Education Zeng Zhaolun, and Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Polish People's Republic Wang Bingnan.

The Polish side participating in the talks included Minister of Culture and Art Karol Kuryluk, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Marian Naszkowski, Vice Chairmen of the Planning Commission Mieczysław Lesz, Bolesław Strużek, Vice Minister of Higher Education Eugenia Krassowska, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Franciszek Modrzewski, Ambassador-Ranked Member of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Juliusz Katz-Suchy, and Polish Ambassador to China Kirylok.

The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere, and the talks will continue. (Attached pictures)

### 毛主席接见和欢宴波兰政府代表团  周总理同西伦凯维兹主席开始会谈

1957-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见和欢宴波兰政府代表团
    周总理同西伦凯维兹主席开始会谈
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午在中南海勤政殿接见了以波兰部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团。
    毛泽东主席同西伦凯维兹主席等在亲切和友好的气氛中进行了九十分钟的谈话。
    接见的时候在座的，有朱德副主席，周恩来总理，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，副总理陈云、贺龙、聂荣臻，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义、龙云，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和中国驻波兰大使王炳南等人。
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚间在中南海勤政殿设宴，欢迎波兰部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹和夫人，以及波兰人民共和国政府代表团全体人员。
    毛泽东主席在宴会上致词，对以西伦凯维兹主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团表示热烈的欢迎。
    毛主席说，波兰是同中国有着深厚友谊的国家，波兰是社会主义阵营的一员。中波两国之间的友谊一天天在增进。祝波兰一切都好，祝社会主义国家日益团结，祝中波两国的友谊日益发展。
    毛主席举杯祝代表团的全体同志身体健康，祝波兰统一工人党第一书记哥穆尔卡同志身体健康，祝波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基身体健康。
    西伦凯维兹主席接着致词。他表示将把毛泽东主席对波兰的祝贺、对社会主义各国日益团结的祝贺转达给全体波兰人民。
    西伦凯维兹认为，波兰人民对毛泽东主席的祝贺的最好回答，就是在波兰更好地建设社会主义和加强波兰同社会主义国家的团结。他又说，毛泽东主席的祝贺是帮助波兰建设社会主义，是帮助社会主义国家团结一致。
    西伦凯维兹主席提议为毛泽东主席的身体健康，为中国共产党，为两国党的合作，为波中两国友谊而干杯。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的有朱德副主席和夫人，周恩来总理，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，副总理陈云、贺龙、聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长董必武，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义、龙云，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞和中国驻波兰大使王炳南等人。
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹在8日下午三时举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理贺龙，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰、柴树藩，轻工业部副部长宋乃德，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使王炳南。
    波兰方面参加会谈的，有文化艺术部长卡罗尔·库里卢克，外交部副部长马里安·纳希科夫斯基，计划委员会副主席米奇斯瓦夫·莱什、布莱斯瓦夫·斯特鲁泽克，高等教育部副部长欧根尼亚·克拉索夫斯卡，对外贸易部副部长弗朗齐歇克·莫德日夫斯基，外交部部务委员会大使衔委员尤利乌什·卡茨—苏希，波兰驻华大使基里洛克。
    会谈是在亲切友好的空气中进行的，会谈还将继续进行。（附图片）
