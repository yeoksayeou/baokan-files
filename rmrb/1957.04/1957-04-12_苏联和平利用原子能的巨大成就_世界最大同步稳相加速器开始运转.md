Title: Great Achievements of the Soviet Union in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy: World's Largest Synchrophasotron Begins Operation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th. The world's largest synchrophasotron has been fully constructed in the Soviet Union and has already begun operation. Currently, it can accelerate the energy of protons to 8.3 billion electron volts. After further adjustments, it will be able to accelerate the energy of protons to 10 billion electron volts, reaching 1.6 times that of the largest similar accelerator in the United States.

　　The ring track of this unparalleled accelerator has a diameter of 60 meters. When the accelerator is in operation, it can make protons revolve 4.5 million times in the ring track in 3.3 seconds, almost equal to the speed of light. These accelerated protons can be used to break the atomic nuclei of many elements, transforming one element into another.

　　The construction of this accelerator used 36,000 tons of electromagnets and 1,000 kilometers of cable. The accelerator is equipped with 6,000 various relays, 2,000 types of control and measurement instruments, and 2,000 types of management and operating instruments. This behemoth is housed in a circular building of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research. Scientists from twelve countries, including China, are engaged in research at this joint institute.

　　Now, the Soviet Union has already begun designing another larger synchrophasotron. After this accelerator is completed, it will be able to accelerate the energy of protons to 50 billion electron volts.

### 苏联和平利用原子能的巨大成就  世界最大同步稳相加速器开始运转

1957-04-12
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联和平利用原子能的巨大成就
    世界最大同步稳相加速器开始运转
    新华社莫斯科11日电　世界最大的同步稳相加速器已经在苏联全部建成，并且已经开始运转。目前它能够把质子的能量加速到八十三亿电子伏特。经过进一步的调整后，它将能够把质子的能量加速到一百亿电子伏特，达到美国最大的同类加速器的一点六倍。
    这部举世无匹的加速器的环行道，直径有六十公尺。加速器运转时，能在三·三秒钟的时间内使质子在环形道中回转四百五十万次，几乎等于光的速度。这种加速后的质子可以用来击破许多种元素的原子核，使一种元素变成另一种元素。
    建造这座加速器共用了三万六千吨电磁铁和一千公里长的电缆。加速器内装有六千个各式继电器，二千种各式控制测量仪器和二千种管理操纵仪器。这个庞然大物建立在联合原子核研究所的一座圆形大楼里。有十二个国家的科学家在这个联合研究所里从事研究工作，其中也有中国的科学家。
    现在，苏联又已经开始在设计另外一台更大的同步稳相加速器了。这台加速器建成以后，能够把质子的能量加速到五百亿电子伏特。
