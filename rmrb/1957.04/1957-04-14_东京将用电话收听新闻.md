Title: Tokyo to Use Telephone to Listen to News
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency
    Tokyo to Use Telephone to Listen to News
    The Japanese Telegraph and Telephone Public Corporation announced on the 12th that it will start a business of reporting news by telephone in Tokyo at the beginning of next month. Citizens can pick up the phone to listen to the latest news. In the initial stage, the service will be limited to between 8:00 PM and 7:00 AM the next morning, so as not to disrupt normal telephone communication.
    The Japanese Telegraph and Telephone Public Corporation has already launched a telephone weather forecast service in Tokyo.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 东京将用电话收听新闻

1957-04-14
第5版()
专栏：国际文化消息

    东京将用电话收听新闻
    日本电信电话公社12日宣布，下月初将要在东京开办用电话报告新闻的业务。市民拿起电话机就可以收听最新的新闻。开办初期，这项新业务的时间只限于每天下午八时到第二天早晨七时，以免扰乱一般的电话联系。
    日本电信电话公社已经在东京举办了用电话预告天气的业务。
                      （新华社）
