Title: Zhejiang Governor Hosts Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, April 25th: Zhejiang Province Governor Sha Wenhan hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Chairman Voroshilov. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao, First Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Jiang Hua, and First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Hangzhou Wu Xian, and others attended the banquet.

### 浙江省长设欢迎宴会

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    浙江省长设欢迎宴会
    新华社杭州25日电　浙江省省长沙文汉今晚举行宴会欢迎伏罗希洛夫主席。出席宴会作陪的有周恩来总理，贺龙副总理，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国驻苏联大使刘晓和中共浙江省委员会第一书记江华，中共杭州市委员会第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪等。
