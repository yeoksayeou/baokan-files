Title: Poland Prepares to Establish Polish-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 10th: The preparatory committee for the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association held a meeting in Warsaw on the 9th. The meeting decided to call for the establishment of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and will hold a general membership meeting soon.

The meeting elected the secretariat of the preparatory committee. The members of the secretariat are Banach, a member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the United People's Party; Borowski, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "People's Tribune"; Burkin, former Polish Ambassador to China; Jabłoński, Professor of Sinology at the University of Warsaw; and writer Pszimanowski. Many Polish social and political activists, as well as figures from the scientific and cultural circles, attended this meeting of the preparatory committee. The meeting was presided over by Jendrihovsky, Chairman of the Polish Planning Commission and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the United Workers' Party.

### 波兰筹备成立波中友好协会

1957-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰筹备成立波中友好协会
    新华社华沙10日电　波中友好协会筹备委员会9日在华沙举行会议，会上决定号召成立波中友好协会，并且将在最近举行全体会员会议。
    会上选出了筹备委员会的书记处。书记处的组成人员有统一农民党中央委员会主席团委员巴纳赫、“人民论坛报”副总编辑鲍罗夫斯基、波兰前任驻中国大使布尔金、华沙大学汉学教授雅布翁斯基和作家普希曼诺夫斯基。出席筹备委员会这次会议的有波兰许多社会政治活动家、科学和文化界人士。会议是由波兰计划委员会主席、统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基主持的。
