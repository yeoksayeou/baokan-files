Title: Guangzhou 100,000 People Rally to Welcome Voroshilov; Soviet Guests Returned to Beijing from Guangzhou via Wuhan Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 29th. Approximately 100,000 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou gathered at the Yuexiushan People's Stadium this morning to welcome Chairman Voroshilov, who is visiting here. Also attending today's welcoming rally were Soviet guests such as Rashidov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

Accompanying Chairman Voroshilov at the meeting were Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, Governor of Guangdong Province Tao Zhu, and Mayor of Guangzhou Zhu Guang.

Chairman Voroshilov spoke at the meeting (full text on page 5). His speech was warmly welcomed by the masses present.

Mayor of Guangzhou Zhu Guang delivered a welcome speech before Chairman Voroshilov's speech. He led the 100,000 people in shouting: "Long live the respected Chairman Voroshilov!" At this time, the whole场 was filled with tsunami-like cheers and applause.

Chairman Voroshilov accepted gifts from all walks of life in Guangzhou at the meeting. The meeting also received many letters and gifts from the masses for Chairman Voroshilov, including gifts to be passed on to Soviet friends.

After accepting the gifts, Chairman Voroshilov walked to the front of the rostrum and waved to the masses to express his thanks.

While in Guangzhou, Chairman Voroshilov visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute. Chairman Voroshilov asked the staff of the Training Institute for an inscription book, and in the guesthouse, he signed his name along with Rashidov, Yelyutin, Yudin, and Fedin, and wrote the inscription: "Today we visited the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which Comrade Mao Zedong led in 1926. This training institute played a significant role in spreading Marxism-Leninism in China and in the development of the Chinese people's revolutionary movement for liberation. Under the leadership of the heroic Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, the Chinese working people are victoriously building a new socialist life. Preserving this memorial building is of great significance for educating the working people in the glorious revolutionary tradition."

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 29th. Chairman Voroshilov and the Soviet guests who came with him arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Guangzhou at 4 p.m. today.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport today were Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, General Chen Zaidao, Commander of the Wuhan Garrison, Wang Kewen, Vice Mayor of Wuhan, and more than 15,000 people from all walks of life.

Also at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests were Soviet experts who are helping with construction in Wuhan.

After disembarking from the plane, Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by his hosts, went by car to the guesthouse. Along the ten-kilometer route from the airport to the guesthouse, crowds of people were everywhere, and cheers, applause, and the sound of gongs and drums lasted for a long time.

After resting briefly at the guesthouse, the distinguished guests drove to visit the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, which is about to be completed. Chairman Voroshilov was very interested in this bridge. On the steel beams, he carefully inquired about the project from Silin, a Soviet expert who is helping with the work on the construction site. He said to Peng Min, Director of the Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau, and the workers on the beams: "Congratulations to you, your bridge is rare in the world, it is very great."

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Chairman Voroshilov and the Soviet guests who accompanied him on their visit to our country, accompanied by Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, Vice Premier He Long, and others, returned to Beijing from Wuhan by special plane at 10:55 tonight.

Greeting them at the airport were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and officials from the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 广州十万人集会欢迎伏罗希洛夫  苏联贵宾们昨天从广州过武汉回到北京

1957-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    广州十万人集会欢迎伏罗希洛夫
    苏联贵宾们昨天从广州过武汉回到北京
    据新华社广州29日电　广州各界人民约十万人今天上午在越秀山人民体育场集会欢迎来这里访问的伏罗希洛夫主席。参加今天欢迎大会的还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席拉希多夫等苏联贵宾。
    陪同伏罗希洛夫主席参加大会的有彭真副委员长以及广东省省长陶铸和广州市市长朱光。
    伏罗希洛夫主席在大会上讲了话（全文见第五版）。他的讲话受到了到会群众的热烈欢迎。
    广州市市长朱光在伏罗希洛夫主席讲话前致欢迎词。他领导十万群众高呼：“敬爱的伏罗希洛夫主席万岁！”这时，全场响起了海啸般的欢呼声和掌声。
    伏罗希洛夫主席在大会上接受了广州市各界的献礼。大会还收到许多群众给伏罗希洛夫主席的信和礼物，其中还有请伏罗希洛夫主席转带给苏联朋友的礼物。
    伏罗希洛夫主席接受了礼物以后，走到主席台前，向到会群众挥手表示谢意。
    伏罗希洛夫主席在广州时，曾参观了农民运动讲习所旧址。伏罗希洛夫主席向讲习所的工作人员要了题词册，在迎宾馆里署上了他和拉希多夫、叶留金、尤金和费德林的名字，并写下题词：“今天我们访问了1926年毛泽东同志曾经领导过的农民运动讲习所。这个讲习所在中国传播马克思列宁主义及中国人民为争取解放的革命运动的发展上起了重大作用。中国劳动人民在以毛泽东同志为首的英勇的中国共产党领导下，正在胜利地建设社会主义的新生活。保存这一座值得纪念的建筑物，对劳动人民进行光荣的革命传统教育有着巨大的意义。”
    据新华社武汉29日电　伏罗希洛夫主席和同来的苏联贵宾今天下午四时由广州乘专机到达武汉。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的，有湖北省省长张体学，中共湖北省委第一书记王任重，中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫、武汉市驻军首长陈再道上将，武汉市副市长王克文，以及各界人士共一万五千多人。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有在武汉帮助建设的苏联专家。
    伏罗希洛夫主席下机后，在主人陪同下登车前往宾馆。在从机场到宾馆的长达十公里的途中，到处人山人海，欢呼声、鼓掌声、锣鼓声经久不息。
    贵宾们在宾馆休息片刻后，就驱车前往参观即将合拢的武汉长江大桥。伏罗希洛夫主席对这座大桥很感兴趣。在钢梁上，他很仔细地向在工地上帮助工作的苏联专家西林询问了有关工程的情况。他向长江大桥工程局局长彭敏和在梁上的工人说：“祝贺你们，你们的桥在世界上都是少有的，是很伟大的。”
    新华社29日讯　伏罗希洛夫主席和随同他访问我国的苏联贵宾们由彭真副委员长、贺龙副总理等人陪同，今晚十时五十五分从武汉乘专机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有周恩来总理、彭德怀副总理、邓小平副总理、李济深副委员长，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫和苏联驻中国大使馆官员。
