Title: Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 11th. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Józef Cyrankiewicz, and the Polish government delegation he led, arrived in Shanghai this afternoon from Beijing on a special plane. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan, and others accompanied them.
    At 3:15 PM, the special plane carrying the distinguished Polish guests landed at Longhua Airport amidst welcoming music and the sound of gongs and drums. The distinguished guests were met here by Ke Qingshi, Secretary of the Shanghai Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, as well as Vice Mayors of Shanghai Cao Diqiu, Liu Jiping, Song Jiwen, Jin Zhonghua, Sheng Pihua, Rong Yiren, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Shanghai.
    Also present at the airport today to welcome the distinguished guests were the Polish Consul General in Shanghai, Bernacki, and consuls from various countries stationed in Shanghai.
    Accompanied by Ke Qingshi and Cao Diqiu, Cyrankiewicz walked through the ranks of more than 5,000 welcoming people who were waiting at the airport, cheering and paying their respects to them. At this time, young students in the welcoming crowd sang the Polish folk song "May You Live a Hundred Years." Cyrankiewicz and his wife were holding bouquets of flowers given to them by the welcomers.
    In the evening, Cyrankiewicz and his wife, along with other distinguished Polish guests, attended a grand welcoming banquet held by the Vice Mayors of Shanghai at the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee Hall.

### 波兰政府代表团到达上海

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团到达上海
    新华社上海11日电　波兰部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹和他所率领的波兰政府代表团，今天下午从北京乘专机到达上海。外交部副部长章汉夫、中国驻波兰大使王炳南等陪同前来。
    三时一刻，载送波兰贵宾的专机在欢迎曲和锣鼓声中降落在龙华机场。贵宾们在这里会见了前来欢迎的中共中央上海局书记、中共上海市委员会书记处第一书记柯庆施，上海市副市长曹荻秋、刘季平、宋季文、金仲华、盛丕华、荣毅仁，以及上海各界知名人士。
    今天在机场上欢迎贵宾的还有波兰驻上海总领事贝尔纳茨基和各国驻上海的领事们。
    西伦凯维兹由柯庆施、曹荻秋陪同，穿过守候在机场上向他们欢呼致敬的五千多名欢迎群众的行列。这时候，欢迎队伍中的青年学生们唱起了波兰民歌“长命百岁”。西伦凯维兹和夫人的手里，抱满了欢迎者送给他们的鲜花。
    晚上，西伦凯维兹和夫人和其他波兰贵宾们出席了上海市副市长在市人民委员会大厅举行的盛大欢迎宴会。
