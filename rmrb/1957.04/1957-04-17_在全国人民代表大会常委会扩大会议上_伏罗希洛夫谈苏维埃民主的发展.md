Title: At the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Voroshilov Discusses the Development of Soviet Democracy
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Chairman Voroshilov delivered a speech today at the enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its invitation, on the development of Soviet democracy (full text on page 2).

In his speech, Chairman Voroshilov introduced the development of Soviet democracy. He said that due to the unwavering implementation of socialist democracy, a great friendship has been forged among all nationalities in the Soviet Union. The Union of Soviet Socialist Republics has become a model of a united and therefore powerful multinational state.

He said that while developing Soviet democracy, the Soviet people pay close attention to and are very excited to see the vigorous development of democracy with special forms in the People's Republic of China.

He said that in the two great countries of People's China and the Soviet Union, socialism has taken a dominant position. This fact alone eloquently proves that the great cause of socialist transformation of the lives of people of all countries has become an unshakable historical fact, and this cause is invincible.

He said that the Soviet people will do everything possible to enable China and the Soviet Union to advance together in close brotherly unity towards new victories.

Voroshilov's speech was constantly interrupted by stormy applause.

The enlarged meeting, attended by more than 1,500 people, was held in the Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai.

At 3 p.m., Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, walked onto the rostrum amidst prolonged and enthusiastic applause. Also on the rostrum were Rashidov, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic; Yelyutin, Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union; Fedrin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; and Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China; Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of National People's Congress, Vice-Premiers of the State Council, President of the Supreme People's Court, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice-Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs, and the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

At the beginning of the enlarged meeting, Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered a speech (full text on page 2) expressing a warm welcome to the distinguished guests. Then, the audience stood up and warmly applauded Chairman Voroshilov's speech for a long time.

After Chairman Voroshilov finished his speech, Chairman Liu Shaoqi thanked him again. Chairman Liu Shaoqi said that Comrade Voroshilov just gave us a brilliant speech. He vividly introduced the great achievements and rich experiences of the great Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government in building socialism and developing Soviet democracy, and gave a high evaluation of our work. This is a great encouragement to the Chinese people.

At the end of the meeting, the audience stood up again and applauded Chairman Voroshilov warmly. Chairman Voroshilov waved to the audience frequently. Chairman Voroshilov and Chairman Mao Zedong shook hands tightly with smiles in a very cordial manner.

Attending today's meeting were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, deputies to the National People's Congress in Beijing, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of offices, ministries, commissions, and directly affiliated agencies of the State Council, Vice-Presidents of the Supreme People's Court, Deputy Procurators-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, members of the National Defense Council in Beijing, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of democratic parties and people's organizations, heads of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

Soviet friends in Beijing were also invited to attend. (Attached photo)

### 在全国人民代表大会常委会扩大会议上  伏罗希洛夫谈苏维埃民主的发展

1957-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    在全国人民代表大会常委会扩大会议上
    伏罗希洛夫谈苏维埃民主的发展
    新华社16日讯　伏罗希洛夫主席今天应邀在全国人民代表大会常务委员会扩大会议上发表演说（全文见第二版）。
    伏罗希洛夫主席在演说中介绍了苏维埃民主的发展情况。他说，由于始终不渝地实行社会主义民主，苏联各族人民之间结成了伟大的友谊。苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟已经成为团结一致因而强大有力的、多民族国家的典范。
    他说，苏联人民在发展苏维埃民主的同时，非常注意并且十分兴奋地看到中华人民共和国的具有特殊形式的民主的蓬勃发展。
    他说，在人民中国和苏联这两个伟大的国家里，社会主义已占统治地位，仅仅这一件事实就雄辩地证明，对各国人民生活进行社会主义改造的伟大事业已经成为历史上不可动摇的事实，这一事业是不可战胜的。
    他说，苏联人民将尽一切努力来使中苏两国在紧密的兄弟般的团结中共同前进，走向新的胜利。
    伏罗希洛夫的演说，不断地被暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
    这次有一千五百多人参加的扩大会议是在中南海怀仁堂举行的。
    下午三时，伏罗希洛夫主席由毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理陪同，在经久不息的热烈的掌声中走上主席台。同时走上主席台的，还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、苏联乌兹别克加盟共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫，苏联高等教育部部长叶留金，苏联外交部副部长费德林，苏联驻中国大使尤金；全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长，国务院副总理，最高人民法院院长，最高人民检察院检察长，国防委员会副主席，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席，中苏友好协会总会副会长，外交部副部长和中国驻苏联大使。
    扩大会议开始时，刘少奇委员长讲话（全文见第二版），向贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。接着，全场起立，长时间的热烈鼓掌欢迎伏罗希洛夫主席演说。
    伏罗希洛夫主席演说结束后，刘少奇委员长再次向他表示感谢。刘少奇委员长说，刚才伏罗希洛夫同志给我们做了精采的讲演，他生动地介绍了伟大的苏联共产党、苏联政府在建设社会主义和发展苏维埃民主方面所获得的伟大的成就和丰富的经验，并且对于我们的工作给予了很高的评价。这对于我们中国人民是一种很大的鼓舞。
    会议结束时，全场再次起立向伏罗希洛夫主席热烈鼓掌。伏罗希洛夫主席向全场频频挥手致意。伏罗希洛夫主席和毛泽东主席欢笑着非常亲热地紧紧握手。
    出席今天会议的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，在京的全国人民代表大会代表，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员，国务院各办公室、各部、各委员会和国务院各直属机构负责人，最高人民法院副院长，最高人民检察院副检察长，在京的国防委员会委员，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，中苏友好协会总会负责人，北京市各界人士。
    在京的苏联友人也应邀出席。（附图片）
