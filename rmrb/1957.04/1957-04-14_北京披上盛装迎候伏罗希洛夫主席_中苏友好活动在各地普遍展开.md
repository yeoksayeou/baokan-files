Title: Beijing is Decked Out to Greet Chairman Voroshilov; Sino-Soviet Friendship Activities are Universally Unfolded in Various Places
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Chairman Voroshilov of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, at the invitation of Chairman Mao Zedong, will visit China and arrive in Beijing on the 15th.

    To welcome the arrival of the honored guest, Beijing has been decked out in festive attire. Along the road from Nanyuan Airport to Xinhua Gate, colorful archways and tall slogan towers are being built. On the ancient Qianmen Arrow Tower, huge oil portraits of Chairman Voroshilov and Chairman Mao Zedong are hung. Outside Yongdingmen, a colorful archway nine meters high and seventeen meters wide reads: "Long Live the Unity of the Socialist Camp Headed by the Soviet Union!"

    The broad masses of workers, peasants, students, and cadres of government organizations in Beijing are rushing to make the national flags of China and the Soviet Union, as well as other colorful flags, to welcome the honored guest.

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th: On the eve of Chairman Voroshilov's visit to our country, people in various places are universally launching activities concerning Sino-Soviet friendship.

    In Beijing, a report meeting with the participation of more than 1,300 people was held this morning. Comrade Wang Li of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China was invited to give a report on strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship. In his report, Wang Li introduced the achievements of the Soviet Union's communist construction and emphasized the importance of strengthening the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. This report meeting is the first in a series of report meetings sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Starting next week, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will also invite Soviet experts and people who have returned from visiting the Soviet Union to give reports on the achievements of Soviet construction and the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

    In major cities such as Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Shenyang, and Harbin, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations are also actively preparing to hold report meetings on Sino-Soviet friendship. Zhou Shutao, Vice Mayor of Tianjin, who has visited the Soviet Union, and other people from Tianjin who have visited the Soviet Union, will report on their experiences in the Soviet Union at report meetings held by the Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The lecture groups of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions and the amateur lecturers of various factories will also report to the broad masses of workers on the achievements of Soviet construction, the Soviet Union's assistance to our country, and the great friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. The Shenyang Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is preparing to join with various people's organizations to organize five to seven special report meetings and film evenings. In addition to introducing the achievements of the Soviet people in economic construction, culture, and science, they will also introduce the noble communist moral qualities of the Soviet people. In Anshan, the steel capital, technicians and workers who have studied in the Soviet Union will report to the broad masses of workers on how the Soviet people sincerely and selflessly train construction personnel for China. The Anshan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China is also preparing to organize the city's reporters and propagandists to conduct a large-scale propaganda campaign on strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship among the city's workers and people around the 15th.

    Various activities concerning Sino-Soviet friendship will also be carried out in agricultural cooperatives, schools, government organizations, and military units throughout the country.

    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations have printed a large number of pamphlets introducing Chairman Voroshilov's life and the achievements of Soviet construction and distributed them to people from all walks of life.

    Sino-Soviet friendship photo exhibitions will be held in cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Harbin.

### 北京披上盛装迎候伏罗希洛夫主席  中苏友好活动在各地普遍展开

1957-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    北京披上盛装迎候伏罗希洛夫主席
    中苏友好活动在各地普遍展开
    新华社13日讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团伏罗希洛夫主席应毛泽东主席邀请访问中国，将于15日到达北京。
    为了欢迎贵宾的来临，北京已经披上节日盛装。从南苑机场到新华门的路上，正在建造一座座彩牌楼和高大的标语塔。在古老的前门箭楼上，悬挂着伏罗希洛夫主席和毛泽东主席的巨幅油画像。在永定门外，一座高达九公尺、宽十七公尺的彩牌楼上写着：“以苏联为首的社会主义阵营团结万岁！”
    北京广大的工人、农民、学生和机关干部正在赶制中苏两国国旗及其他各色旗帜，准备欢迎贵宾。
    新华社13日讯　在伏罗希洛夫主席来我国访问前夕，各地人民普遍地展开关于中苏友好的活动。
    在北京市，今天上午举行了有一千三百多人参加的报告会，中共中央王力同志应邀在会上作了关于加强中苏友好的报告。王力在报告中介绍了苏联共产主义建设的成就，并强调指出加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结的重要性。这个报告会是北京市中苏友好协会主办的一系列报告会中的第一个。从下周开始，北京市中苏友好协会还将邀请苏联专家和访苏回来的人士作有关苏联建设成就和中苏人民友谊的报告。
    在上海、天津、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、沈阳和哈尔滨等大城市，中苏友好协会也都在积极筹备举行关于中苏友好的报告会。曾经访问过苏联的天津市副市长周叔韬和天津市其他访问过苏联的人士，将在天津市中苏友好协会举行的报告会上报告访苏见闻。天津市工会系统的讲演团和各工厂的业余讲演员，也将向广大职工报告苏联的建设成就、苏联对我国的援助和中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。沈阳市中苏友好协会准备联合各人民团体，组织五次到七次的专题报告会和电影晚会，除介绍苏联人民在经济建设和文化科学等方面所取得的成就外，并介绍苏联人民高尚的共产主义道德品质。在钢都鞍山，曾经到苏联学习的技术人员和工人，将向广大职工报告苏联人民真诚无私地为中国培养建设人材的情况。中共鞍山市委员会还准备组织全市的报告员和宣传员，在15日前后向全市职工和人民进行一次大规模的关于加强中苏友好的宣传活动。
    在全国各地农业合作社、学校、机关和部队中，也将进行各种关于中苏友好的活动。
    中苏友好协会总会和各地中苏友好协会已经印了大量介绍伏罗希洛夫主席生平和苏联建设成就的小册子，发给各界人民。
    北京、上海、天津、武汉、重庆、哈尔滨等城市将举办中苏友好图片展览会。
