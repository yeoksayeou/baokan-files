Title: Liu Quanyu
Author: Liu Quanyu
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 3

    In order to solve the water source problem for the expanded 210 mu of double-cropping rice fields this year, the Xinsheng Agricultural Cooperative in Yingshan County, Sichuan Province, mobilized 130 waterwheels and more than 200 cooperative members every day to continuously draw water. Now, the water needed for all 4,000 mu of fields in the cooperative has been resolved. Photo by Liu Quanyu (Manuscript from Sichuan Peasant Newspaper)

专栏：

    四川省营山县新生农业社，为了解决今年扩大的二百一十亩双季稻田的水源，每天出动水车一百三十多架，两百多名社员，不停地车水。现在全社四千亩田所需要的水，都已解决了。刘全玉摄（四川农民报社稿）
