Title: Chairman Mao Receives Representatives from Four Units
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. Chairman Mao Zedong received this afternoon the representatives attending the Chinese People's Liberation Army Signal Corps Competition Conference, the Shaanxi Old Revolutionary Base Area Visiting Group, the Henan Province Religious and Ethnic Minority Youth Visiting Group, and the ethnic minority students from the Political Department of the Central Institute for Nationalities.

    When receiving the representatives, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Bojun, were also present.

### 毛主席接见四个单位的代表

1957-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见四个单位的代表
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军通信兵竞赛大会代表，陕北老区参观团，河南省宗教民族青年参观团和中央民族学院政治系少数民族学员等。
    接见的时候，李济深、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通副委员长，陈云、彭德怀、邓小平副总理和政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧等也在座。
