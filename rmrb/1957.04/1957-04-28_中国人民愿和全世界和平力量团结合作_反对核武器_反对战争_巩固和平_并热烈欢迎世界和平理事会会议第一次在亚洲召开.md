Title: The Chinese People are Willing to Unite and Cooperatively Work with All Peaceful Forces in the World to Oppose Nuclear Weapons, Oppose War, and Consolidate Peace, and Warmly Welcome the First Meeting of the World Peace Council in Asia
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace held an enlarged meeting last night, supporting the appeal and statement regarding the demand for the cessation of nuclear weapons testing and the resolution regarding the international situation passed at the meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council held in Berlin from March 30th to April 2nd this year. The meeting also supported the decision of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council to hold a plenary meeting of the World Peace Council in Colombo, the capital of Ceylon, in June this year.

  Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, presided over the enlarged meeting. In his speech, he first talked about the achievements of the world peace movement. He said that the world peace movement has greatly developed, and the call of the world peace movement has had a significant impact. Our friends engaged in the peace movement should work harder to promote further easing of the world situation, so as to facilitate our country's development of peaceful construction. Then he talked about the situation of the Berlin meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. He expressed his support for the resolutions passed by the Berlin meeting, and said that the Berlin meeting decided to hold a plenary meeting of the World Peace Council in Colombo in June this year, which is the first World Peace Council meeting held in Asia, and is good news.

  Liao Chengzhi, Chinese representative to the Berlin meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, reported on the situation of the Berlin meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

  The meeting finally passed a resolution on supporting the resolutions of the Berlin meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

  The meeting was attended by standing committee members of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Chinese council members of the World Peace Council, and well-known figures from all walks of life.

### 中国人民愿和全世界和平力量团结合作  反对核武器　反对战争　巩固和平  并热烈欢迎世界和平理事会会议第一次在亚洲召开

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民愿和全世界和平力量团结合作
    反对核武器　反对战争　巩固和平
    并热烈欢迎世界和平理事会会议第一次在亚洲召开
    新华社27日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会昨晚举行扩大会议，拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会今年3月30日到4月2日在柏林召开的会议上所通过的关于要求停止核武器试验的呼吁书和声明及关于国际局势的决议。会议并支持世界和平理事会常务委员会决定今年6月在锡兰首都科伦坡召开世界和平理事会全体会议。
  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若主持这次扩大会议。他在讲话中首先谈到了世界和平运动的成绩。他说，世界和平运动有了很大的发展，世界和平运动的号召已发生了重大影响。我们从事和平运动的朋友们，应该更加努力工作，促使世界局势进一步和缓，便于我国发展和平建设事业。接着他谈到了世界和平理事会常务委员会柏林会议的情况。他对柏林会议所通过的决议表示拥护，并说，柏林会议决定今年6月在科伦坡举行世界和平理事会全体会议，这是第一次在亚洲召开的世界和平理事会会议，是一个好消息。
    出席世界和平理事会常务委员会柏林会议的中国代表、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志在会上报告了世界和平理事会常务委员会柏林会议的情况。
    会议最后通过了关于支持世界和平理事会常务委员会柏林会议决议的决议。
    出席今天会议有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、世界和平理事会中国理事和各界著名人士等。
