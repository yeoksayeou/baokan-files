Title: Yemenese People Take Up Arms to Defend Their Motherland; Elderly Also Stand in the Ranks of the Anti-Aggression War

Author: 
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 6

The people of Yemen are taking up arms one after another to defend their motherland, and the elderly are also standing in the ranks of the anti-aggression war.
