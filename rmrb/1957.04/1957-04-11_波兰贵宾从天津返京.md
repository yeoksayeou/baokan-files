Title: Polish Guests Return to Beijing from Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 10th. The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Chairman Cyrankiewicz, returned to Beijing by special train from Tianjin at 11:00 PM tonight. Premier Zhou Enlai, Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and our Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan, who accompanied the delegation on their visit, also returned to Beijing on the same train.

### 波兰贵宾从天津返京

1957-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰贵宾从天津返京
    新华社天津10日电　以西伦凯维兹主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团今晚十一时由天津乘专车回北京。陪同代表团前来访问的周恩来总理和交通部部长章伯钧、外交部副部长章汉夫和我国驻波兰大使王炳南等也同车回北京。
