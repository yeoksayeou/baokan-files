Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Supports the Struggle of the Algerian People
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to the General Union of Algerian Trade Unions, supporting the Algerian people's struggle for liberation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions, to the General Union of Algerian Trade Unions, and to all Algerian worker brothers:

The Chinese workers deeply sympathize with and support the just struggle of the Algerian workers and all the people for national independence and freedom, and express our solemn protest with infinite indignation against the brutal and criminal acts committed by the French imperialist colonial authorities in Algeria. We firmly demand that the French government immediately stop this genocidal war of aggression and negotiate with the representatives of the Algerian people on the basis of recognizing Algeria's independence. The Chinese workers believe that the heroic struggle of the Algerian people will surely achieve final victory.

All-China Federation of Trade Unions, April 6, 1957

### 中华全国总工会  支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争

1957-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会
    支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争
    新华社6日讯　中华全国总工会今天致电阿尔及利亚工会总联合会，支援阿尔及利亚人民的解放斗争。电报全文如下：世界工联书记处转阿尔及利亚工会总联合会转阿尔及利亚全体工人兄弟们：
    中国工人深刻同情并支持阿尔及利亚工人及全体人民为争取民族独立与自由而进行的正义斗争，并以无限愤慨的心情，对法国帝国主义殖民当局在阿尔及利亚进行的残暴罪恶行为表示严正的抗议，坚决要求法国政府立即停止这种灭绝人性的侵略战争，在承认阿尔及利亚独立的基础上和阿尔及利亚人民的代表进行谈判。中国工人相信阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争必将获得最后胜利。
     中华全国总工会　1957年4月6日
