Title: The Largest Soviet-Assisted Equipment for China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 3

    The largest piece of Soviet-assisted equipment for China—a four-roll plate bending machine—is being installed at the Harbin Boiler Factory. This machine weighs over 400 tons and can bend over 1,000 meters of 80mm thick steel plate per hour. With this machine, the factory will be able to produce complete sets of steam boilers for generators.
                       Photo by Zhang Xuegang (New China News Agency draft)

### 苏联支援我国的最大设备

1957-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联支援我国的最大设备
    苏联帮助我国制造的最大型设备之一——四滚筒卷板机正在哈尔滨锅炉厂安装。这台机器重四百多吨，每小时能弯卷八十公厘厚的钢板一千多公尺。这个厂有了这台机器，将能生产全套发电机用的蒸气锅炉。                       张雪刚摄（新华社稿）
