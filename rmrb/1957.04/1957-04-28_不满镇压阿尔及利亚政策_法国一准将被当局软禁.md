Title: Dissatisfied with the Algerian Policy of Suppression, French Brigadier General Placed Under House Arrest by Authorities
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th, Paris Dispatch: Brigadier General Paris de Bollardière, commander of the East Atlas region of the French army in Algeria, has been placed under a sixty-day military disciplinary punishment of house arrest by the French Ministry of Defense since April 15th, due to his disagreement with the French government's policy of blindly suppressing the Algerian people.

De Bollardière had requested the French government to relieve him of his duties in Algeria in March, and had also published his dissatisfaction with the authorities' policies in the form of an open letter in the "Express" weekly magazine.

### 不满镇压阿尔及利亚政策  法国一准将被当局软禁

1957-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    不满镇压阿尔及利亚政策
    法国一准将被当局软禁
    新华社27日讯　巴黎消息：阿尔及利亚法军东阿特拉斯地区司令帕里·德博拉迪埃准将由于不同意法国政府在阿尔及利亚盲目镇压的政策，已经在4月15日被法国国防部给予六十天软禁的军纪处分。
    德博拉迪埃曾经在3月间要求法国政府解除他在阿尔及利亚的职务，并且还曾经把他对当局政策的不满用公开信的形式刊登在“快报”周刊上。
