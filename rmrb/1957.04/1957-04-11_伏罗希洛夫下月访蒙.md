Title: Voroshilov to Visit Mongolia Next Month
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 10th: At the invitation of Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will visit the Mongolian People's Republic in May of this year.

### 伏罗希洛夫下月访蒙

1957-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫下月访蒙
    新华社乌兰巴托10日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫应蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布的邀请，将在今年5月访问蒙古人民共和国。
