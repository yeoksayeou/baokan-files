Title: Three Female Athletes from Fujian Province Break National 1500-Meter Cycling Record
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 6th. Today, at the 1957 Fujian Provincial Cycling Championship held in Putian County, Fujian Province, Li Yufeng, a sixteen-year-old athlete from the Jinjiang area, broke the national record of two minutes and forty-one point eight seconds set by Wang Shuzhi in Yantai City in 1956, with a time of two minutes and thirty-nine point eight seconds in the women's 1,500-meter race. Hong Shubin and Li Jingying, athletes from Xiamen City, also broke the record, with times of two minutes and forty-one point two seconds and two minutes and forty-one point six seconds respectively.

### 福建省三个女运动员打破自行车1500公尺全国纪录

1957-04-07
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    福建省三个女运动员
    打破自行车1500公尺全国纪录
    新华社福州6日电　今天在福建省莆田县举行的1957年福建省自行车锦标赛的运动会上，在女子一千五百公尺比赛中，晋江专区十六岁运动员李玉凤以二分三十九秒八的成绩，打破了1956年王淑芝在烟台市创造的二分四十一秒八的全国纪录。打破这项纪录的还有厦门市运动员洪淑彬和李景莹，她们的成绩是二分四十一秒二和二分四十一秒六。
