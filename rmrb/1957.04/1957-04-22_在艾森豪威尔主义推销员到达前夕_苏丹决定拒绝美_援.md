Title: Sudan Decides to Reject U.S. "Aid" on the Eve of Eisenhower Doctrine Salesman's Arrival
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 21st: According to a Middle East News Agency dispatch from Khartoum, the Sudanese government convened a special meeting on the eve of the arrival of U.S. President's special envoy Richards in Khartoum. The meeting adopted a memorandum to be given to Richards.

The memorandum emphasized that Sudan refuses to accept U.S. military aid or any economic aid that may harm Sudan's neutrality or contain conditions that restrict Sudan's independence.

### 在艾森豪威尔主义推销员到达前夕  苏丹决定拒绝美“援”

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    在艾森豪威尔主义推销员到达前夕
    苏丹决定拒绝美“援”
    新华社开罗21日电　据中东通讯社喀土穆消息，苏丹政府在美国总统特使理查兹来到喀土穆的前夕召开了特别会议。会上通过了将交给理查兹的备忘录。
    备忘录中强调指出，苏丹拒绝接受美国的军事援助或是任何可能损及苏丹的中立或是带有限制苏丹的独立的条件的经济援助。
