Title: Mongolia and Poland Agree on Concrete Measures to Strengthen Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, April 18th: The governments of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Polish People's Republic issued a joint statement this afternoon, saying that the friendly relations between Mongolia and Poland will continue to develop in full compliance with the desire of the two peoples to build a better life, consolidate the unity of the socialist camp, and consolidate world peace.

    The joint statement, signed by Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, and Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, stated that both sides believe that the visit of the Polish government delegation to Mongolia is of great significance for consolidating the fraternal friendship and common cooperation between the people of Mongolia and Poland.

    The statement said that both sides noted with satisfaction that the friendly cooperative relations between Mongolia and Poland in the political, economic, and cultural fields are developing and strengthening year by year. In order to continue to develop and expand trade and economic cooperation in the future, both sides have agreed to take concrete measures within this year.

    The statement said that after exchanging views on international issues, both sides agreed that although certain imperialist groups are carrying out conspiracies to undermine peace and international security, the powerful forces of the peace, democracy, and socialism camp are capable of easing international tensions.

    Both sides pointed out that under the current circumstances, it is extremely important to consolidate the unity of the socialist family, based on the principles of proletarian internationalism and the equality of large and small countries, in all aspects. The unity of socialist countries is the basic factor in ensuring peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems and maintaining lasting world peace. The statement said that the Mongolian government believes that the Polish people have made valuable contributions to easing international tensions and safeguarding peace in Europe and the world.

    The Polish government stated that it will continue to support the request of the Mongolian People's Republic to enjoy a legitimate seat in the United Nations.

### 蒙波商定具体措施加强合作

1957-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙波商定具体措施加强合作
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电　蒙古人民共和国政府和波兰人民共和国政府今天下午发表联合声明说，蒙、波两国间的友好关系将在完全符合两国人民建立美好生活的愿望、巩固社会主义阵营的团结和巩固世界和平的情况下继续发展。
    由蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔和波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹共同签署的联合声明说，双方相信，波兰政府代表团的访问蒙古，对巩固蒙、波两国人民之间的兄弟友谊和共同合作具有重要意义。
    声明说，双方满意地指出，蒙波两国在政治、经济和文化方面的友好合作关系正在一年年地发展和巩固。为了在今后继续发展和扩大贸易及经济合作，双方商定将在今年内采取具体措施。
    声明说，双方就国际问题交换意见后一致认为，某些帝国主义集团虽然在进行破坏和平和国际安全的阴谋活动，但是和平民主社会主义阵营的强大力量是能够缓和国际紧张局势的。
    双方指出，在目前情况下，从各方面来巩固以无产阶级国际主义原则和大小国家平等原则为基础的社会主义大家庭的团结是极其重要的。社会主义国家的团结是保证不同社会制度的国家和平共处和维护世界持久和平的基本因素。声明说，蒙古政府认为，波兰人民在缓和国际紧张局势、维护欧洲及世界和平的事业中作出了宝贵的贡献。
    波兰政府表示，它将继续支持蒙古人民共和国在联合国享有合法席位的要求。
