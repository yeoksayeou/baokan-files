    Cherry Blossoms Fade, May Day Arrives
    In Japan, cherry blossoms are in full bloom around April 10th every year. The cherry blossom is the national flower of Japan, and the Japanese people love cherry blossoms. On this day, the Japanese people sing and dance to their hearts' content, celebrating this annual flower viewing season. This is a photo of nurses at the Kiyose Sanatorium in Tokyo, singing songs to celebrate May Day under the blossoming cherry trees. A saying circulates among the Japanese working people: "Cherry blossoms fade, May Day arrives."

### 樱花开过“五一”来（图片）

1957-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    樱花开过“五一”来
    在日本，每年4月10日前后，樱花盛开。樱花是日本的国花，日本人都喜爱樱花。在这天中，日本人民尽情歌舞，欢度这一年一度的赏花时节。这是东京清濑疗养院的女护士们，在盛开的樱花树下，练唱庆祝“五一”节的歌曲。日本劳动人民流传着这样一句话：“樱花开过‘五一’来。”
