    The Simplest Editorial Title
    The above picture is a clipping taken from the April 1st issue of the London "Times." That diagonal line is not a misprinted horizontal line, but the title of an editorial. This editorial discusses the problem of the declining purchasing power of the British pound. This is probably the simplest editorial title in a newspaper.

### 最简单的社论题目（图片）

1957-04-20
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    最简单的社论题目
    上图是采自4月1日伦敦“泰晤士报”的一块剪报。那一斜线并不是印歪了的横线，而是一篇社论的题目。这篇社论是论述英镑购买力日益下降问题的。这恐怕可以说是报纸社论中最简单的题目了。
