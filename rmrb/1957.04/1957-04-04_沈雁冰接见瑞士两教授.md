Title: Shen Yanbing Receives Two Swiss Professors
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd — Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, received Anne Babel and Henri Ziegelaar, professors from the University of Geneva, Switzerland, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, on the afternoon of April 2nd.

### 沈雁冰接见瑞士两教授

1957-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰接见瑞士两教授
    新华社2日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的瑞士日内瓦大学教授安·巴贝尔和亨·齐格莱，于4月2日下午由文化部部长沈雁冰接见。
