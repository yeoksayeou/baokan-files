Title: Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region: Hohhot to Hold Grand Celebration; Mongolian Delegation Arrives in Hohhot and Receives Enthusiastic Welcome
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 2

Hohhot, April 29th. To mark the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region on May 1st, people of all nationalities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will hold a grand celebration. Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, and representatives from sixteen provinces and autonomous regions, who are here to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the autonomous region, arrived here today.

Hohhot is filled with a joyous atmosphere. The streets are adorned for the festival, and the streets have been swept clean. People of all nationalities, dressed in beautiful costumes, are busy preparing to welcome their own festival.

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, April 29th. The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Art and Sports Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic arrived in Hohhot by special train at 4:00 p.m. today. Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, as well as leaders of the Party and government of the autonomous region, and representatives from all walks of life in Hohhot, greeted them at the station. Mongolian experts working in the autonomous region were also present to welcome them.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and others, who arrived in Hohhot on the same day, also remained at the station to welcome the distinguished Mongolian guests.

After Chimiddorjyn Sürenjav, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, alighted from the train, he was accompanied by Ulanhu to inspect the guard of honor of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the station platform.

When the distinguished Mongolian guests appeared at the entrance of the station amidst the sound of military music, the welcoming crowd gathered in front of the station shouted, "Long live Sino-Mongolian friendship!" People held aloft the national flags of China and Mongolia, waved colorful bouquets, and cheered thunderously.

At the welcoming ceremony held in front of the station, Ulanhu delivered a welcoming speech in Mongolian. He said that the arrival of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic indicates the further development of the brotherly friendship between the people of China and Mongolia, which is an honor for the people of all nationalities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

Chimiddorjyn Sürenjav, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, also delivered a speech in Mongolian. He said that the Mongolian people are extremely concerned and admire the achievements made by the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. He said that the people of Mongolia and China have always encouraged each other and fought together in the struggle for freedom and independence and against common enemies, and that the friendship and brotherly cooperative relationship between the people of Mongolia and China is being consolidated and is moving forward.

Their speeches were constantly interrupted by applause and cheers.

Finally, Ulanhu and Li Xiannian accompanied Sürenjav, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, in an open-top car through the welcoming crowds to the guesthouse for rest.

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, April 29th. Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and some representatives from various provinces and autonomous regions, who are here to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, also attended the banquet as guests. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to strengthening Sino-Mongolian friendship during the banquet.

### 内蒙古自治区成立十周年  呼和浩特将举行盛大庆祝会  蒙古代表团到达呼和浩特受到热烈欢迎

1957-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区成立十周年
    呼和浩特将举行盛大庆祝会
    蒙古代表团到达呼和浩特受到热烈欢迎
    本报呼和浩特29日电　5月1日，是内蒙古自治区成立十周年纪念日，内蒙古自治区各民族人民将在这一天举行盛大的庆祝大会。前来祝贺自治区成立十周年的国务院副总理李先念和十六个省、自治区的代表，今天已经到达此间。
    呼和浩特充满一片欢乐气氛，街头披上了节日的盛装，街道打扫得干干净净。穿着美丽的服装的各民族人民，正在为迎接他们自己的节日而忙碌。
    新华社呼和浩特29日电　蒙古人民共和国政府代表团和蒙古人民共和国艺术体育团，今天下午四时乘专车到达呼和浩特。内蒙古自治区主席乌兰夫以及自治区党政领导人、呼和浩特市各族各界代表都在车站迎接。在车站迎接的还有在自治区工作的蒙古专家等。
    同天到达呼和浩特的国务院副总理李先念等，也留在车站迎接了蒙古贵宾。
    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长齐米德道尔吉·苏伦扎布下车后，即由乌兰夫陪同，在车站站台上检阅了中国人民解放军内蒙古军区仪仗队。
    当蒙古贵宾们在军乐声中出现在车站大门口时，聚集站前的欢迎队伍高呼：“中蒙友好万岁！”人们高举中、蒙两国国旗，挥舞着鲜艳的花束，欢声雷动。
    在车站前举行的欢迎仪式上，乌兰夫用蒙古话致欢迎词。他说，蒙古人民共和国政府代表团的到来，表明中蒙两国人民兄弟般友谊的进一步发展，这是内蒙古自治区各族人民的光荣。
    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长齐米德道尔吉·苏伦扎布也用蒙古话致词。他说，蒙古人民异常关切和钦佩内蒙古自治区所取得的成就。他说蒙、中两国人民在争取自由独立、反对共同敌人的斗争中，一向是互相鼓励、共同斗争的，而且蒙、中两国人民的友谊和兄弟般的合作关系正在巩固和向前发展着。
    他们的讲话不断被掌声、欢呼声打断。
    最后，乌兰夫、李先念陪同蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长苏伦扎布，乘坐敞篷汽车穿过夹道欢迎的人群，到迎宾馆休息。
    据新华社呼和浩特29日电　内蒙古自治区主席乌兰夫今晚设宴欢迎蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。
    前来祝贺内蒙古自治区成立十周年的国务院副总理李先念和各省、自治区的部分代表，也出席作陪。宾主们在席间频频为加强中蒙友谊而干杯。
