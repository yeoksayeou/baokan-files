Title: One of China's Largest Sugar Refineries – Guangdong Zini Sugar Refinery (Picture)
Author: Zhang Luo
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 2

    One of China's Largest Sugar Refineries – Guangdong Zini Sugar Refinery
    The state-owned Guangdong Zini Sugar Refinery has been expanded and completed. This factory can now crush 2,100 tons of sugarcane per day, producing over 48,000 tons of refined white sugar in one sugarcane crushing season. (Photograph by Zhang Luo, submitted by Southern Daily)
    Above: A panoramic view of the crushing workshop after the expansion.
    Right: The refining workshop originally had only one vacuum filter; now, two more have been added.

### 我国最大的糖厂之一——广东紫坭糖厂（图片）
张洛
1957-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    我国最大的糖厂之一——广东紫坭糖厂
    国营广东紫坭糖厂已经扩建完成。这个厂每日可榨蔗二千一百吨，一个榨蔗季节可以生产白砂糖四万八千多吨。　张洛摄　（南方日报社稿）
    上图：扩建后的榨蔗车间全景。
    右图：制炼车间原来只有一台真空过滤机，现在又增加了两台。
