Title: Sino-Polish Talks Continue; Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Polish Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-04-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 9th

　　Sino-Polish Talks Continue
　　Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Polish Guests
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 9th – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, continued talks with Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Józef Cyrankiewicz, on the morning of April 9th.
　　At the same time, the heads of relevant departments of the Government of the People's Republic of China held talks in groups with members of the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic.
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 9th – Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a luncheon at Quanjude today for Chairman Józef Cyrankiewicz and his wife, as well as members of the Polish Government Delegation. Vice Premier He Long, Mayor Peng Zhen and his wife, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan and his wife were present as hosts. ### 中波会谈继续举行  周总理宴请波兰贵宾

1957-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中波会谈继续举行
    周总理宴请波兰贵宾
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹在9日上午继续举行了会谈。
    同时，中华人民共和国政府有关部门的负责人同波兰人民共和国政府代表团团员分组举行了会谈。
    新华社9日讯周恩来总理今天中午在全聚德宴请约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹主席和夫人，波兰政府代表团的团员。出席作陪的有贺龙副总理，彭真市长和夫人，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国驻波兰大使王炳南和夫人等。（附图片）
