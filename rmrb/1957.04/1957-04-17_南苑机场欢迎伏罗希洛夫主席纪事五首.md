Title: Five Poems Commemorating Chairman Voroshilov's Visit to Nanyuan Airport
Author: Ye Gongchuo
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 8

　　Five Poems Commemorating Chairman Voroshilov's Visit to Nanyuan Airport
　　Ye Gongchuo

　　Today's grand event is recorded in Yanjing,
　　Seas of people and flowers line the streets to welcome him,
　　Six hundred thousand people sing and dance,
　　Joy descends from the heavens like a bright star.

　　Two heroes join hands in joyous celebration,
　　Captivating the world's attention,
　　World peace relies on concerted efforts,
　　To use these twin pillars to block the raging tides.

　　A forty-mile rainbow spans the square,
　　The city and suburbs are adorned with new splendor,
　　To lightly depict this grand scene,
　　Ten thousand acres of red clouds embrace the sun.

　　The atmosphere of the five continents transforms into auspicious harmony,
　　Let the mythical creatures and dragons dance,
　　May weapons of war be cleansed forever,
　　In peaceful conversations, we can save the Milky Way.

　　Thousands of red and purple flowers are planted in time,
　　Waiting to clear away thorns and open up wastelands,
　　Arm in arm, we open up a new century,
　　Living together in a beautiful building in all directions.
                                      April 15th

### 南苑机场欢迎伏罗希洛夫主席纪事五首
叶恭绰
1957-04-17
第8版()
专栏：

    南苑机场欢迎伏罗希洛夫主席纪事五首
    叶恭绰今朝盛事纪燕京，人海花潮夹道迎，六十万人歌且舞，喜从天上降明星。两雄携手共联欢，惹得全球注目看，世界和平凭协力，好将双柱障狂澜。卌里长虹贯广场，城郊是处换新妆，若将盛况轻摹拟，万顷红云拥太阳。五洲氛雾化祥和，任彼鱼龙曼衍过，愿得甲兵长净洗，敦槃谈笑挽天河。万千红紫及时栽，待扫荆棘辟草莱，联臂拓开新世纪，八方同住好楼台。
                                      4月15日
