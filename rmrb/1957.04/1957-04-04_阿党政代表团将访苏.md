Title: Albanian Party and Government Delegation to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 3rd. An Albanian government and Albanian Labor Party delegation will visit Moscow soon at the invitation of the Soviet government and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for a friendly visit.
    The delegation will be led by the Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, Shehu. Members of the delegation include the member of the Presidium of the Albanian People’s Assembly, the First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha, and the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Nushi, and others.

### 阿党政代表团将访苏

1957-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    阿党政代表团将访苏
    据新华社地拉那3日电　应苏联政府和苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请，阿尔巴尼亚政府和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的代表团将在最近去莫斯科进行友好访问。
    代表团将由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡率领。代表团团员有阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团委员、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会书记处第一书记霍查，部长会议第一副主席努什等人。
