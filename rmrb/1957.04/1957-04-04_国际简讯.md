International Briefs

    Czechoslovakia Presents Books to Indonesia
    The Director of the Foreign Relations Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs said on April 2nd that the Czechoslovak government, through its Cultural Counselor in Jakarta on April 2nd, presented the Indonesian government with gifts including educational and other scientific books, as well as records of famous Czechoslovak musical works.

    Yugoslavia and Britain Sign Trade Agreement
    Yugoslavia and Britain signed a one-year trade agreement in Belgrade on April 2nd, which will be effective until the end of March next year. It is expected that the trade volume between the two countries will increase compared to the past, totaling over 15.75 million pounds this year. Last year, Britain accounted for 6% of Yugoslavia's exports and 9.8% of its imports.
                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    捷赠书给印度尼西亚
    印度尼西亚外交部对外关系司司长2日说，捷克斯洛伐克政府2日通过驻雅加达的文化参赞赠送给印度尼西亚政府一些礼物，其中包括教育学和其他科学书籍以及捷克斯洛伐克著名音乐作品的唱片等。
    南英两国签订贸易协定
    南斯拉夫和英国4月2日在贝尔格莱德签订了一个为期一年的贸易协定，这个协定的有效期限到明年3月底为止。预料，两国的贸易额将比过去增加，总计这一年将超过一千五百七十五万英镑。去年，英国在南斯拉夫的出口额中占6％，在进口额中占9.8％。
                        （新华社）
