Title: Western Countries Have Different Plans Regarding the Laying of a New Oil Pipeline in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 8th dispatch: The Paris "International Forum" weekly published a communication on March 29, revealing that Western powers have different plans for laying new oil pipelines in the Middle East.

The communication, entitled "Middle East Oil Diplomacy Tricks," said that French financial groups are planning to build an oil pipeline on Israeli territory, and it is said that Israel and France will greatly benefit from this plan. However, Britain and the United States have already expressed their disapproval of this plan.

### 在敷设中东新油管问题上  西方国家各怀鬼胎

1957-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    在敷设中东新油管问题上
    西方国家各怀鬼胎
    据新华社8日讯　巴黎消息：巴黎“国际论坛”周刊3月29日登载一篇通讯，揭露西方大国在中东敷设新油管问题上各怀打算。
    这篇题目是“中东的石油外交把戏”的通讯说，法国金融集团正筹划在以色列领土上建立一条油管，据说以色列和法国将从这个计划中得到很大的利益。但是，英国和美国已经表示它们不赞成这个计划。
