Title: Yemen's King Gives Richards the Cold Shoulder, Who Departs Crestfallen
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 20th: According to the Middle East News Agency on the 19th, U.S. President's special envoy Richards, who went to Yemen to promote the "Eisenhower Doctrine," left Yemen for Sudan without achieving any results.
    The Middle East News Agency said that during Richards' stay in Yemen, he was unable to meet with the King of Yemen, Ahmed, and no agreement was reached. No communique was issued by either side at the end of the visit.

### 也门国王赏给闭门羹  理查兹灰溜溜地走了

1957-04-21
第6版()
专栏：

    也门国王赏给闭门羹
    理查兹灰溜溜地走了
    新华社开罗20日电　据中东通讯社19日报道：到也门去推销“艾森豪威尔主义”的美国总统特使理查兹毫无结果地离开了也门前往苏丹。
    中东通讯社说，理查兹在也门停留期间，没有能会见也门国王艾哈迈德，也没有达成协议。访问结束时双方没有发表公报。
