Title: Greek Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: The Greek Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st.

The delegation, led by Simos Hadjidimitriou, General Secretary of the Greek Trade Union Democratic Front, consists of twelve members and is the first Greek trade union delegation to visit our country from within Greece. The delegation includes representatives from industrial unions such as the electrical, railway, and stevedoring industries.

### 希腊工会代表团到京

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    希腊工会代表团到京
    据新华社21日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”节观礼的希腊工会代表团21日乘飞机抵达北京。
    这个由希腊工会民主阵线总书记西莫斯·哈吉狄米特里欧率领的由十二人组成的代表团，是第一个从希腊国内来我国访问的希腊工会代表团。代表团中包括电气、铁路、搬运业等产业工会的代表。
