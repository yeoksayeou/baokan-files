Title: Egypt Revokes Seizure of Enemy Property
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 22nd. Egyptian Minister of Finance, Kaissouni, announced on April 21st the revocation of the decree seizing enemy property, which was issued during the Anglo-French invasion of Egypt.

    The order will take effect from May 4th.

    The new order does not apply to the British and French property that has already been nationalized by Egyptian citizens.

### 埃及撤销查封敌产

1957-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及撤销查封敌产
    新华社开罗22日电　埃及财政部长凯舒尼4月21日宣布撤销在英法侵埃战争期间公布的查封敌人财产的法令。
    这个命令从5月4日起生效。
    新命令并不适用于已经收归埃及公民所有的英、法两国的财产。
