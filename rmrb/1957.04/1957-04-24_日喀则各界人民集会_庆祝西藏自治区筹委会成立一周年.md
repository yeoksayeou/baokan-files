Title: Rikaze People from All Walks of Life Gather to Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region

Author:

Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rikaze, 23rd - People from all walks of life in Rikaze gathered on the 22nd to celebrate the first anniversary of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

　　Jamdong Jigme, Director of the Rikaze Base Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, spoke at the meeting, encouraging monks, nuns, and laypeople in the Rikaze area to continue following Chairman Mao's instructions of "unity, progress, and greater development" and strive for the early establishment of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region. He said, "Since the establishment of the Rikaze Base Office on September 15 last year, six Dzong-level offices have now been established in the Rikaze area. The construction of the Preparatory Committee's political power is gradually developing. Since the establishment of the Base Office in the Rikaze area, national unity has been further enhanced due to the full promotion of the spirit of consultation."

　　Lamin Yexi Chuchen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, said in his speech at the meeting: "Since the establishment of the Preparatory Committee a year ago, all work has been developing forward. In the future, we must hold even higher the banner of unity and patriotism and build a prosperous and happy Tibet."

　　Liang Xuanxian, Secretary of the Rikaze Sub-Work Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, also spoke at the meeting to offer congratulations.

### 日喀则各界人民集会  庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立一周年

1957-04-24
第2版()
专栏：

    日喀则各界人民集会
    庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立一周年
    新华社日喀则23日电　日喀则各界人民在22日集会庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立一周年。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会日喀则基巧办事处主任詹东·计晋美在会上讲话勉励日喀则地区僧俗人民，继续遵循毛主席“团结、进步、更加发展”的指示，为早日成立统一的西藏自治区而努力。他说：“在去年9月15日成立了日喀则基巧级办事处以后，现在已经在日喀则地区成立了六个宗级办事处，筹委会的政权建设工作在逐步发展中，日喀则地区自从成立了基巧办事处以后，由于充分发扬了协商办事的精神，在民族团结方面得到了进一步的增加。”
    西藏自治区筹委会副秘书长拉敏·益喜楚臣在会上的讲话中说：“筹委会成立一年来，各项工作都是向前发展的，今后我们要更高地举起团结爱国的旗帜，建设繁荣幸福的西藏。”
    在会上讲话祝贺的，还有中共日喀则分工委书记梁选贤。
