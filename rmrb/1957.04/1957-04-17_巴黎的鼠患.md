Title: Rat Infestation in Paris
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 6

Happenings

    Rat Infestation in Paris
    According to foreign press reports, there are now approximately five million rats in Paris, and many are large rats weighing over half a pound. Parisian authorities estimate that the losses caused by the rat infestation amount to about 100 billion to 200 billion francs annually.

### 巴黎的鼠患

1957-04-17
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    巴黎的鼠患
    据外国报刊报道，巴黎现在约有五百万只老鼠，而且很多是重达半磅以上的大老鼠。据巴黎当局估计，由于鼠患造成的损失，每年约达一千亿法郎到二千亿法郎。
