Title: Indian Ambassador Holds Reception for Lakshman Sisters' Visit to China
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd. Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to welcome the visit of the famous Indian dancers, the Kamala Lakshman sisters, to China. At the reception, the Lakshman sisters had cordial conversations with Chinese friends and performed Bharatanatyam dance to add to the enjoyment.
    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Foreign Minister, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles were invited to attend the reception. Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 为拉克希曼姊妹访华  印度大使举行酒会

1957-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    为拉克希曼姊妹访华
    印度大使举行酒会
    新华社3日讯　印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人为欢迎印度著名舞蹈家卡玛拉·拉克希曼姊妹访问中国，今天下午在北京举行酒会。拉克希曼姊妹在酒会上同中国朋友们亲切交谈，并表演了婆罗多舞助兴。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长郑振铎，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中印友好协会会长丁西林以及首都文艺界著名人士应邀出席了酒会。应邀出席酒会的，还有各国驻华使节。
