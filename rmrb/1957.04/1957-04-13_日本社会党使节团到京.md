Title: Japanese Socialist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Delegation, led by Asanuma Inejirō, Secretary-General of the Central Executive Committee of the Japanese Socialist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon.

　　This delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

　　After disembarking, Asanuma Inejirō issued a statement and held a press conference at the airport.

　　Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport today included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Qiao Guanhua, Vice President; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Liao Chengzhi, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and people from all walks of life, including Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, and Sun Pinghua.

### 日本社会党使节团到京

1957-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    日本社会党使节团到京
    据新华社12日讯　以日本社会党中央执行委员会书记长浅沼稻次郎为首的日本社会党访华亲善使节团由广州乘飞机在今天下午到达北京。
    这个使节团是应中国人民外交学会的邀请访问中国的。
    下机后，浅沼稻次郎发表了声明，并在机场举行了记者招待会。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员廖承志，中国红十字会总会会长李德全，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，以及社会各界人士谢南光、赵安博、孙平化等。
