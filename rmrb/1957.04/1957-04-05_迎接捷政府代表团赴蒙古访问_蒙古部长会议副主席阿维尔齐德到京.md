Title: Welcoming the Czechoslovak Government Delegation's Visit to Mongolia; Mongolian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Avirmed Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Avirmed, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Deputy Foreign Minister Dugersuren arrived in Beijing this afternoon. They are here to welcome the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Prime Minister Široký on their friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.

Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Ji Pengfei, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, were at the airport to greet them, as well as Tseden-Ishiin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China.

### 迎接捷政府代表团赴蒙古访问  蒙古部长会议副主席阿维尔齐德到京

1957-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接捷政府代表团赴蒙古访问
    蒙古部长会议副主席阿维尔齐德到京
    新华社4日讯　蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席阿维尔齐德和外交部副部长杜格尔苏伦在今天下午到达北京。他们是前来迎接以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团赴蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问的。
    到机场迎接的有中华人民共和国国务院秘书长习仲勋、外交部副部长姬鹏飞等；还有蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办策登依什。
