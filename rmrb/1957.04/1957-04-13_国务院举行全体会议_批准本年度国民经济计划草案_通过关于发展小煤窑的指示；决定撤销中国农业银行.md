Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session; Approves Draft of This Year's National Economic Plan; Passes Instructions on Developing Small Coal Mines; Decides to Abolish the Agricultural Bank of China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th – The 44th, 45th, and 46th plenary sessions of the State Council were held on the 6th, 8th, and 12th, respectively.

    The meetings successively heard explanations from Vice Premier and Director of the State Economic Commission, Bo Yibo, regarding the draft of the 1957 National Economic Plan, and from Vice Premier and Minister of Finance, Li Xiannian, regarding the 1956 financial revenue and expenditure situation and the 1957 budget arrangements, and conducted discussions. The meetings approved the State Council's decision regarding the approval of the 1957 National Economic Plan draft. The 1957 National Economic Plan draft, as well as the 1956 financial revenue and expenditure situation and the 1957 budget arrangements, will all be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the National People's Congress for deliberation.

    After hearing the explanation by Vice Premier and Director of the State Economic Commission, Bo Yibo, the meetings passed the State Council's instructions on developing small coal mines.

    The meetings also discussed the issue of cotton cloth supply in 1957.

    The meetings approved the State Council's notice on abolishing the Agricultural Bank of China. The notice stated that the State Council has decided to merge the Agricultural Bank's organizations at all levels with the People's Bank, and the name of the Agricultural Bank will be abolished. In the future, rural credit work will be uniformly handled by the People's Bank.

    The meetings also approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  批准本年度国民经济计划草案  通过关于发展小煤窑的指示；决定撤销中国农业银行

1957-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    批准本年度国民经济计划草案
    通过关于发展小煤窑的指示；决定撤销中国农业银行
    新华社12日讯　国务院全体会议第四十四次会议、第四十五次会议和第四十六次会议，在6日、8日和12日分别举行。
    会议先后听取了副总理兼国家经济委员会主任薄一波关于1957年国民经济计划草案的说明，副总理兼财政部长李先念关于1956年财政收支情况和1957年预算安排的说明，并进行了讨论。会议通过了国务院关于批准1957年度国民经济计划草案的决定。1957年度国民经济计划草案和1956年财政收支情况和1957年预算安排问题，都将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会和全国人民代表大会审议。
    会议在听了副总理兼国家经济委员会主任薄一波的说明后，通过了国务院关于发展小煤窑的指示。
    会议还讨论了1957年棉布供应的问题。
    会议通过了国务院关于撤销中国农业银行的通知。通知中说，国务院决定，将农业银行的各级机构同人民银行合并，农业银行的名义即予撤销。今后农村信贷工作由人民银行统一负责办理。
    会议还通过了任免名单。
