Title: Brazilian Congressman Castro Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Brazilian Congressman and former Chairman of the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations, José de Castro, and his wife arrived in Beijing today for a visit.

### 巴西议员卡斯特罗到京

1957-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    巴西议员卡斯特罗到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国参观访问的巴西众议员、前联合国粮食和农业组织主席若泽·德·卡斯特罗夫妇两人在今天到达北京。
