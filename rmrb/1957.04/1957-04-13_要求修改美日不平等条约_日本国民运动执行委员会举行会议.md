Title: Demanding Revision of the Unequal US-Japan Treaty: Japanese National Movement Executive Committee Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 6
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 12th dispatch: According to the "Asahi Shimbun", the Japanese National Movement Executive Committee for demanding the revision of unequal treaties with the United States held its first meeting on the 10th in the House of Councillors of the Japanese Diet. Representatives from the Socialist Party, labor unions, farmers' groups, student groups, and cultural figures attended the meeting. The Executive Committee discussed the issue of launching a struggle to revise the unequal treaties with the United States, especially the US-Japan "Security Treaty".

    On the same day, Nozoe Masaru, chairman of the Socialist Party's Special Committee Against Military Bases, visited Chief Cabinet Secretary Hirohide Ishida and proposed that the government demand that the United States revise the unequal treaties.

### 要求修改美日不平等条约  日本国民运动执行委员会举行会议

1957-04-13
第6版()
专栏：

    要求修改美日不平等条约
    日本国民运动执行委员会举行会议
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社东京讯：据“朝日新闻”报道，日本要求修改对美不平等条约的国民运动执行委员会，10日在日本国会参议院举行了第一次会议。出席会议的有社会党、工会、农民团体、学生团体的代表以及文化界人士代表。执行委员会讨论了为修改同美国的不平等条约、特别是日美“安全条约”而展开斗争的问题。
    社会党反对军事基地特别委员会委员长野沟胜在同一天拜会了日本内阁官房长官石田博英，向他提出了建议，希望政府要求美国修改不平等条约。
