Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Japanese Socialist Party Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission headed by Inejiro Asanuma this evening.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Zhang Xiruo, Ye Jizhuang, Xu Deheng, Liao Chengzhi, Lei Renmin, and Qiao Guanhua were also present at the meeting. (Attached picture)

Chairman Mao Zedong received the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission on April 21st. The picture shows the scene during the meeting. On the left is Inejiro Asanuma. (Photo by Hou Bo, Xinhua News Agency)

### 毛主席接见日本社会党访华团

1957-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见日本社会党访华团
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席在今天晚间接见了以浅沼稻次郎为首的日本社会党访华亲善使节团。
    接见的时候，周恩来总理和张奚若、叶季壮、许德珩、廖承志、雷任民、乔冠华也在座。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席4月21日接见了日本社会党访华亲善使节团。图为接见时情形。左端是浅沼稻次郎。　新华社记者　侯波摄
