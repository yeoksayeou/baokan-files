Title: Premier Zhou Receives Mr. Guda, Editor-in-Chief of Al-Gomhouria of Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Mr. Guda, Editor-in-Chief of Al-Gomhouria of Egypt and Director of the Middle East News Agency, this afternoon. Mr. Guda attended the recent meeting of the Council of the International Organization of Journalists in Beijing as an observer representing the Egyptian press.

Present at the reception was Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 周总理接见埃及共和国报总编辑古达

1957-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见埃及共和国报总编辑古达
    新华社13日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了埃及共和国报总编辑兼中东通讯社董事古达。古达是代表埃及新闻界以观察员身份列席日前在北京召开的国际新闻工作者协会理事会会议的。
    接见时在座的有中国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西。
