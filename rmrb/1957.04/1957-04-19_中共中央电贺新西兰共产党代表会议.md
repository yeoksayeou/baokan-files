Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to New Zealand Communist Party Congress

Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: The CPC Central Committee sent a telegram congratulating the New Zealand Communist Party Congress, which will convene on April 19th. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows: To all delegates of the New Zealand Communist Party Congress:

　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand.

　　We wish the Congress success in upholding the principles of Marxism-Leninism and in developing the internationalist solidarity of the New Zealand working class with the working classes of all countries. We wish the Congress new achievements in expanding and consolidating the strength of the Party, realizing the united action of the New Zealand working class, uniting all democratic forces in New Zealand, and striving for the immediate interests of the working people. We wish the Congress new progress in strengthening friendly cooperation among the people of all countries and in the struggle to defend world peace.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　April 15, 1957

### 中共中央电贺新西兰共产党代表会议

1957-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺新西兰共产党代表会议
    新华社18日讯　中共中央致电祝贺将在4月19日召开的新西兰共产党代表会议。贺电全文如下：新西兰共产党代表会议全体代表：
    中国共产党中央委员会向新西兰共产党全国代表会议致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    祝代表会议在维护马克思列宁主义的原则、发展新西兰工人阶级同各国工人阶级的国际主义的团结方面获得成功。祝代表会议在扩大和巩固党的力量、实现新西兰工人阶级的统一行动、团结新西兰的一切民主力量、争取劳动人民的切身利益的斗争中获得新的成就。祝代表会议在加强各国人民之间的友好合作和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的进展。
                      中国共产党中央委员会　1957年4月15日
