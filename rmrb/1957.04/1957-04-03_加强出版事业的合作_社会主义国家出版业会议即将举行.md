Title: Strengthening Cooperation in the Publishing Industry: Socialist Countries Publishing Conference to be Held Soon
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 2nd. A publishing conference of socialist countries will be held in Leipzig from April 7th to 16th. This conference is being convened to strengthen close cooperation in the publishing industry among socialist countries and to exchange experiences. Representatives from twelve countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Hungary, Albania, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia, totaling more than 160 people, will participate in the conference. Yugoslavia will send observers to attend the conference.
During the conference, a book exhibition of socialist countries, an exhibition of book design and printing samples from around the world, and an exhibition of book distribution advertisements will be held in Leipzig.

### 加强出版事业的合作  社会主义国家出版业会议即将举行

1957-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    加强出版事业的合作
    社会主义国家出版业会议即将举行
    新华社柏林2日电　社会主义国家出版业会议将在4月7日到16日在来比锡举行。这次会议是为了加强社会主义国家出版事业的紧密合作和交流经验而召开的。参加会议的将有苏联、中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、民主德国、保加利亚、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜、越南、蒙古等十二个国家的代表共一百六十多人。南斯拉夫将派遣观察员出席会议。
    会议举行期间将在来比锡举办社会主义国家图书展览会，世界各国书籍装璜、印刷样品展览会以及书籍发行广告展览会。
