Title: Romanian Guests Pay a Courtesy Call on Chairman Liu

Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Chairman of the Romanian Grand National Assembly, Constantin Pîrvulescu, and the delegation of the Romanian Grand National Assembly and the Bucharest City People's Council he led, paid a courtesy call on Chairman Liu Shaoqi this afternoon.

    Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Zhang Bojun, Wang Weizhou, Zhang Su, Qu Wu, Yu Xinqing, Hu Zi'ang, Wu Xinyu, Zhang Youyu, and Yue Songsheng.

    The Romanian Ambassador to China, Teodor Georgescu, and his wife were also present at the meeting.

### 罗马尼亚贵宾拜会刘委员长

1957-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚贵宾拜会刘委员长
    新华社29日讯　罗马尼亚大国民议会主席康·帕伏列斯库和他率领的罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团，今天下午拜会了刘少奇委员长。
    拜会时，在座的有林伯渠、李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培副委员长和章伯钧、王维舟、张苏、屈武、余心清、胡子昂、武新宇、张友渔、乐松生。
    拜会时，罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤和夫人也在座。
