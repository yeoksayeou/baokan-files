Title: American Fascist Thugs Expand Territory: Ku Klux Klan Takes Root in Britain
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Labour MP Brockway today charged that the American racial terror organization, the Ku Klux Klan, is establishing organizations in major British cities such as London, Birmingham, and Liverpool. These organizations are intended to counter the black people who have immigrated to the British mainland from Jamaica and Trinidad.

It is reported that many Britons have received forms from a man named Miller in Texas, USA, inviting them to join the Ku Klux Klan. It is said that Miller has already established agents of the Ku Klux Klan in the aforementioned British cities to carry out activities.

### 美国法西斯暴徒扩展地盘  “三K党”在英国扎根

1957-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美国法西斯暴徒扩展地盘
    “三K党”在英国扎根
    据新华社29日讯　工党议员布罗克威今天指责说，美国的种族恐怖组织“三K党”正在伦敦、伯明翰、利物浦等英国大城市里建立组织。这些组织是为了对付从牙买加和特立尼特移入英国本土的黑人的。
    据报道，有许多英国人接到美国得克萨斯州一个名叫米勒的人寄出的加入“三K党”的表格。据说，米勒已经在上述英国城市里建立了“三K党”的代理人从事活动。
