Title: Swiss First Ambassador Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to China, Mr. Benoît, called on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on the afternoon of the 19th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

Present were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 瑞士首任大使拜会周外长

1957-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士首任大使拜会周外长
    新华社19日讯　瑞士联邦驻我国首任特命全权大使贝努义在19日下午拜会我外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。
    在座的有外交部西欧司司长黄华和礼宾司副司长张世杰。
