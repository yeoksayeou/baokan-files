Title: Voroshilov Visits Agricultural Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Chairman Voroshilov visited the National Agricultural Exhibition this morning.

Chairman Voroshilov stated that this exhibition is like a mirror, reflecting the labor achievements of Chinese farmers.

Chairman Voroshilov and other Soviet distinguished guests, accompanied by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Premier Deng Zihui, and others, visited the exhibition for more than two hours. He toured every exhibition hall with great interest.

After the visit, Chairman Voroshilov, along with Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Premier Deng Zihui, and others, had lunch at the Moscow Restaurant.

### 伏罗希洛夫参观农业展览会

1957-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫参观农业展览会
    新华社17日讯　伏罗希洛夫主席今天上午参观了全国农业展览会。
    伏罗希洛夫主席认为，这个展览会好像一面镜子，反映了中国农民的劳动成果。
    伏罗希洛夫主席和其他的苏联贵宾由刘少奇委员长、邓子恢副总理等陪同在展览会内参观了两个多小时，他兴致勃勃地走遍每一个展览馆。
    在参观以后，伏罗希洛夫主席同刘少奇委员长、邓子恢副总理等一起在莫斯科餐厅进午餐。
