	International Briefs
	Japanese "Kabuki-za" to Visit Soviet Union
	The Japanese "Kabuki-za," which performed in our country in 1955, has decided to visit Moscow, Leningrad, and Kiev in the Soviet Union around September this year for performance tours. On April 6th, the head of the "Kabuki-za" troupe, Ennosuke Ichikawa, visited the Soviet Embassy in Japan and formally accepted the Soviet invitation after discussing the matter with Stepanov, the director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, who was visiting Japan.
	Korean Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
	At the invitation of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, a 22-member sports delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Liu Byung-sik, Vice Chairman of the Physical Education Guidance Committee of the Korean Cabinet, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 19th.
	Yugoslavia Appoints Ambassador to Mongolia
	Yugoslav President Tito has appointed the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Micanovic, to concurrently serve as the Yugoslav Ambassador to the People's Republic of Mongolia.
	Bulgaria and Hungary Sign Trade Protocol
	Bulgaria and Hungary signed a trade protocol for this year and an agreement on Bulgaria providing Hungary with commodity loans on April 17 in Sofia.
	Chinese Health Work Delegation Returns Home
	The Chinese Health Work Delegation, which participated in the Second Conference on Healthcare Work of Socialist Countries, left Bucharest on the 18th to return home. In addition to participating in the conference, the delegation also visited various places in Romania.

### 国际简讯

1957-04-20
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    日本“歌舞伎座”将去苏联
    前年曾来我国演出的日本“歌舞伎座”，已决定在今年9月前后前往苏联莫斯科、列宁格勒、基辅作访问演出。本月6日，“歌舞伎座”团长市川猿之助访问了苏联驻日大使馆，和正在日本访问的苏联文化部外事局局长斯捷潘诺夫当面洽谈，正式接受了苏联的邀请。
    朝鲜体育考察团抵京
    应中华人民共和国体育运动委员会的邀请来中国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国体育考察团一行二十二人，由朝鲜内阁体育指导委员会副委员长刘秉锡率领，于19日下午乘火车到达北京。
    南斯拉夫任命驻蒙古大使
    南斯拉夫总统铁托任命南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇兼任南斯拉夫驻蒙古人民共和国大使。
    保匈签订换货议定书
    保加利亚和匈牙利4月17日在索非亚签订了今年的换货议定书和保加利亚给予匈牙利以货物贷款的协定。
    中国卫生工作代表团返国
    参加第二届社会主义国家保健工作会议的中国卫生工作代表团18日离开布加勒斯特回国。代表团除参加会议外，还到罗马尼亚各地进行了访问。
