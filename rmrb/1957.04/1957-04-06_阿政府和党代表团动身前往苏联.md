Title: Albanian Government and Party Delegation Depart for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 5th. The Albanian government and Labor Party delegation, at the invitation of the Soviet government and the Communist Party, departed from Durrës port on the "Crimea" ship on the afternoon of the 4th for a visit to the Soviet Union.

### 阿政府和党代表团动身前往苏联

1957-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    阿政府和党代表团动身前往苏联
    新华社地拉那5日电　阿尔巴尼亚政府和劳动党代表团应苏联政府和共产党的邀请4日下午在都拉斯港乘“克里米亚号”轮船前往苏联访问。
