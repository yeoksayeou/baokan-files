Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Tea Party to Welcome Madame Cyrankiewicz
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th April: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party this afternoon to welcome the wife of Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz.
    Madame Cyrankiewicz was warmly welcomed by her hosts. Shi Liang, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, said that we hit it off at first sight, just like sisters. The deep feeling of Chinese women for Polish women cannot be expressed in words. She asked Madame Cyrankiewicz to convey the Chinese women's cordial, warm, and sisterly respect to the Polish women.
    Madame Cyrankiewicz thanked her hosts for their warm reception. She said: Although Poland and China are geographically far apart, the people of China and Poland have common ground in history. In the past, everyone fought for their own independence and liberation, and today they are fighting to build a better life. She said that she would definitely tell Polish women what she saw and felt in China after returning home. Madame Cyrankiewicz was very happy with today's gathering, and she repeatedly said to Shi Liang, we are really like sisters when we are together.
    Also attending today's tea party were the wife of Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryluk, the wife of Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, and the wives of diplomatic officials from the Polish Embassy in China.

### 全国妇联举行茶会欢迎西伦凯维兹夫人

1957-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联举行茶会欢迎西伦凯维兹夫人
    新华社8日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天下午举行茶会，欢迎波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹的夫人。
    西伦凯维兹夫人受到了主人们的热情欢迎。全国妇联副主席史良说，我们一见如故，就像亲姊妹一样。中国妇女对波兰妇女的深厚感情是不能以言语来表达的。她请西伦凯维兹夫人向波兰妇女转达中国妇女对她们的亲切、温暖、姊妹般的敬意。
    西伦凯维兹夫人感谢主人们的热情接待。她说：虽然波兰和中国在地理上相隔很远，但是中波两国人民在历史上有着共同的地方，大家过去都是为争取自己的独立和解放而斗争，今天都在为建设美好的生活而斗争。她说她回国后一定把她在中国所见所感转告波兰妇女。西伦凯维兹夫人很高兴今天的聚会，她一再向史良说，我们处在一起真像亲姊妹。
    今天参加茶会的有波兰驻华大使基里洛克的夫人，中国驻波兰大使王炳南的夫人和波兰驻华大使馆外交官员的夫人等。
