Title: Swiss Legation in China Upgraded to Embassy
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 1

    Swiss Legation in China Upgraded to Embassy
    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th -- The Swiss Confederation has decided to upgrade the Swiss Legation in the People's Republic of China to an embassy and to appoint Mr. B. de Tscharner, the current Envoy to China, as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to the People's Republic of China. The Government of the People's Republic of China has agreed to this.

### 瑞士驻华公使馆升格为大使馆

1957-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士驻华公使馆升格为大使馆
    新华社12日讯　瑞士联邦已决定将瑞士驻中华人民共和国公使馆升格为大使馆，并任命现任驻华公使贝努义先生为瑞士联邦驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权大使。中华人民共和国政府已予同意。
