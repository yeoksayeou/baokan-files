Title: British Defence Minister Arrives in Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: British Defence Minister Sandys arrived in Cyprus from London on the 22nd. He will stay on the island for three days, inspecting the British colonial authorities' military installations here and holding talks with the British Governor of Cyprus, Harding.

Sandys revealed in the British Defence White Paper published earlier this month that squadrons of bombers capable of dropping nuclear weapons would soon be stationed in Cyprus.

United Press International pointed out that Sandys is making this trip at a time when Jordan is experiencing a government crisis, and it is expected that he will "study the status of the British troops still remaining in Jordan."

### 英国防大臣到塞浦路斯

1957-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    英国防大臣到塞浦路斯
    据新华社23日讯　英国国防大臣桑迪斯22日从伦敦到达塞浦路斯。他将在这个岛上逗留三天，视察英国殖民当局在这里的军事设备，并且同英国驻塞浦路斯总督哈定会谈。
    桑迪斯在本月初发表的英国国防白皮书中曾经透露说，能够投掷核武器的轰炸机空军中队不久将驻在塞浦路斯。
    合众社指出，桑迪斯是在约旦发生政府危机的时候作这次旅行的，预料他将“研究仍然留在约旦的英国军队的地位”。
