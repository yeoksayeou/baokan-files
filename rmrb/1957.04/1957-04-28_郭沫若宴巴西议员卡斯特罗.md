Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for Brazilian Congressman Castro
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Brazilian Congressman and former Chairman of the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization, José de Castro, who is visiting China.

### 郭沫若宴巴西议员卡斯特罗

1957-04-28

    郭沫若宴巴西议员卡斯特罗
