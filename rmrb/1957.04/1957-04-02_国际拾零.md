	International Gleanings

	Recto Condemns Philippine Government's Submission to the United States
		Philippine Senator Recto delivered a speech on March 31, condemning the Philippine government for "submitting to the diplomatic control of the United States" and criticizing the Philippines' participation in the Manila Treaty Organization.
		Prior to this, on March 28, the senator, who had announced his intention to run in the Philippine presidential election in November of this year, delivered a speech demanding the implementation of an industrial development plan in the Philippines that does not involve direct foreign investment.

	Malaya Had Over Two Hundred Strikes Last Year
		The Malayan Federation of Labor Department announced on March 31 that a total of 213 strikes occurred in Malaya in 1956, the highest number of strikes in the past decade.

	Chilean Copper Miners Strike
		Seven thousand workers at the El Teniente mine of the Chilean Braden Copper Company decided to go on strike on March 31 because the management refused the workers' demand for wage increases. The Chilean Braden Copper Company is a subsidiary of the American Anaconda Copper Company.

	Argentine University Students Unwelcome U.S. Ambassador
		About one hundred and fifty Argentine university students held a demonstration in San Miguel on March 28 to express their unwelcome of the U.S. Ambassador to Argentina, Mr. Polak.

### 国际拾零

1957-04-02
第6版()
专栏：国际拾零

    国际拾零
    雷克托谴责菲政府屈从美国
　　菲律宾参议员雷克托在3月31日发表演说，谴责菲律宾政府“屈从于美国的外交统制”，并且抨击菲律宾参加马尼拉条约组织。
    在此以前，这位曾经宣布要在今年11月菲律宾总统选举中参加竞选的参议员，在3月28日还曾经发表演说，要求在菲律宾执行一个不要外国直接投资的工业发展计划。
    马来亚去年罢工二百余次
    马来亚联合邦劳工司3月31日宣布，马来亚在1956年共发生二百一十三次罢工，这是过去十年里罢工次数最多的一年。
    智利铜矿工人罢工
    智利布拉登铜矿公司埃尔特尼恩特矿场的七千工人，由于资方拒绝工人的增加工资要求，在3月31日决定开始罢工。智利的布拉登铜矿公司是美国安那康达铜公司的一个子公司。
    阿根廷大学生不欢迎美大使
    阿根廷的一百五十名左右大学生3月28日在圣米格尔举行示威，表示不欢迎美国的驻阿根廷大使波拉克。
