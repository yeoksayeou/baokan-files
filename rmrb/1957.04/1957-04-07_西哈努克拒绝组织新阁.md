Title: Sihanouk Refuses to Organize New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 6th: The Cambodian News Agency reported on the 4th that Prince Sihanouk wrote to King Suramarit expressing his refusal to organize a new cabinet, but he proposed a plan for the future cabinet.

Prince Sihanouk said that he made this decision because he felt it would be difficult for him to serve as the prime minister of the new government after consulting with some people who should be won over to participate in the new cabinet.

In the part of the plan proposed by Sihanouk for the new cabinet regarding foreign policy, he said, "To maintain a neutral policy that enables Cambodia to obtain the friendship of all countries; to strive to safeguard the tradition of the motherland based on national unity."

### 西哈努克拒绝组织新阁

1957-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克拒绝组织新阁
    据新华社金边6日电　柬埔寨新闻社4日报道，西哈努克亲王写信给国王苏拉玛里特表示拒绝组织新内阁，但是他为未来的内阁提出了一个方案。
    西哈努克亲王说，他是在同一些应当争取他们参加新内阁的人商谈以后，感到由他担任新政府的首相很困难而作出这个决定的。
    西哈努克在为新内阁提出的方案中关于外交政策的一部分里说，“要维持使柬埔寨获得一切国家的友谊的中立政策；要努力维护以全国团结为依据的祖国的传统”。
