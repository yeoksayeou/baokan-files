Title: Paul's Bi-Party Delegation Holds Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, April 1st. A delegation of the Bulgarian Communist Party and a delegation of the Romanian Workers' Party held talks here on March 30.
    The communique issued by both parties pointed out that the two party delegations exchanged information on the work and tasks of the two parties, discussed important issues in the international workers' movement and communist movement, and the current tasks of the two parties in further strengthening mutual ties and strengthening the unity of socialist countries.
    The Bulgarian Communist Party was represented in the talks by Zhivkov, Yugov, Chervenkov, and others.
    The Romanian Workers' Party was represented in the talks by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Stoica, Borila, and others.

### 保罗两党代表团会谈

1957-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    保罗两党代表团会谈
    新华社索非亚1日电　保加利亚共产党代表团和罗马尼亚工人党代表团3月30日在这里举行会谈。
    双方发表的公报指出，两党代表团交换了关于两党工作和任务的情报，讨论了国际工人运动和共产主义运动中的重要问题以及两党在进一步加强相互联系和加强社会主义国家团结方面的当前任务。
    保加利亚共产党方面参加会谈的有日夫科夫、于哥夫、契尔文科夫等。
    罗马尼亚工人党方面参加会谈的有乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡、波里勒等。
