Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Japanese Women's Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 12th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese Women's Delegation to China.
　　Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to all members of the delegation. She said, "The sacred task of seeking peace and happiness for our future generations unites us and makes us feel particularly close to each other." On behalf of the All-China Women's Federation and women from all walks of life in Beijing, she expressed cordial friendship and respect to all Japanese sisters.
　　The head of the delegation, Kufu Shiranui, thanked the hosts for their hospitality. She believed that through this visit, she would further understand China's construction achievements and the welfare of women and children. She wished Chinese and Japanese women success in their joint efforts to strive for world peace.
　　Attending tonight's banquet were the heads of organizations such as the Chinese Women's Federation, the National Association of Chinese Christian Young Women, and the National Federation of Chinese Women's Temperance Society, as well as representatives of women from all walks of life in Beijing.

### 全国妇联欢宴日本妇女访华团

1957-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联欢宴日本妇女访华团
    新华社12日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今晚设宴欢迎日本妇女访华代表团。
    全国妇联副主席许广平向代表团全体人员表示了热情的欢迎。她说：“为我们的后代谋求安宁和幸福的神圣任务，把我们联系在一起，使我们相处在一起感到特别亲切。”她代表全国妇联和北京各界妇女向全体日本姊妹表示亲切的友谊和敬意。
    代表团团长久布白落实感谢主人们的招待。她相信通过这次访问，一定会进一步了解中国的建设成就和妇女儿童的福利情况。她祝中日两国妇女在争取世界和平的共同努力中获得成功。
    参加今晚宴会的有中国妇女联谊会、中华基督教女青年会全国协会、中华妇女节制会全国总会等团体的负责人以及北京市各界妇女代表。
