Title: Welcome Soviet Head of State Chairman Voroshilov, Tune: A Thousand Autumns
Author: Huang Shaohong
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 4

Welcome Soviet head of state.
China’s dear friend.
You are like, the spring breeze arriving.
Beijing has a new atmosphere,
All admiring your demeanor.
Looking across the fields,
Hundreds of flowers bloom after Qingming.
Brotherly embrace.
Lip and teeth-like reliance.
Words cannot express,
The goodness of China and the Soviet Union.
Laughing and talking in the tent,
Hands of world peace.
A thousand autumns,
Your achievements and fame will be immortal in the universe.

### 欢迎苏联元首伏罗希洛夫主席　调寄千秋岁
黄绍竑
1957-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎苏联元首伏罗希洛夫主席　调寄千秋岁
    黄绍竑苏联元首。中国亲朋友。您好比，春风到。北京新气象，万众瞻仪表。看遍野，百花招展清明后。兄弟般拥抱。唇齿般依靠。写不尽，中苏好。笑谈帏幄里，世界和平手。千秋岁，勋名宇宙同不朽。
