Title: Polish Government Delegation Departs Mongolia for Home, Concluding Friendly Visit to Seven Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 18th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Józef Cyrankiewicz, and the Polish government delegation he led, left Ulaanbaatar by plane this afternoon to return home. With this, the Polish government delegation concluded its friendly visit to seven Asian countries.

### 结束对亚洲七国的友好访问  波兰政府代表团离蒙返国

1957-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    结束对亚洲七国的友好访问
    波兰政府代表团离蒙返国
    据新华社乌兰巴托18日电　波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和他率领的波兰政府代表团今天下午离开乌兰巴托乘飞机回国。至此，波兰政府代表团结束了对亚洲七个国家的友好访问。
