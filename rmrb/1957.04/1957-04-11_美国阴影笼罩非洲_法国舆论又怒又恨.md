Title: America's Shadow Looms Over Africa, French Public Opinion Expresses Anger and Resentment
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th dispatch from Paris: U.S. Vice President Nixon's report proposing a broad expansion of American influence in Africa has caused serious unease in France. "L'Humanité" published an article on the 8th, pointing out that this report shows Nixon has drafted "a broad scope of activities in Africa" for the United States. The article believes that this fact should serve as a "warning" to Europe, because the United States "is about to replace other colonial countries in Africa."

Paris's "Perspectives" weekly recently commented on the U.S. signing of an economic "aid" agreement with Tunisia, emphasizing that France's position in Tunisia is threatened by its partners across the ocean. "Perspectives" angrily asked: "How long will the French government tolerate its so-called allies selling it out?"

### 美国阴影笼罩非洲  法国舆论又怒又恨

1957-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国阴影笼罩非洲
    法国舆论又怒又恨
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社巴黎8日讯：美国副总统尼克松提出的建议美国势力向非洲广泛扩张的报告，在法国引起了严重不安。“义勇军报”8日发表文章指出，从这个报告可以看出，尼克松为美国拟定了“在非洲活动的广泛范围”。文章认为，这一事实应当成为对欧洲的“警告”，因为美国“正要在非洲取其他殖民国家而代之”。
    巴黎的“展望”周报最近在评论美国同突尼斯签订经济“援助”协定时强调指出，法国在突尼斯的地位受到大洋彼岸的伙伴的威胁。“展望”周报气愤地问道：“法国政府对它的所谓盟国出卖它的行动将要忍耐到什么时候？”
