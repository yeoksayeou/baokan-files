Title: Chairman Voroshilov at the Banquet Hosted by Premier Zhou: Wishing All People on Earth Live in Peace. Chairman Mao Said: We are Still Prepared to Conduct a Third Kuomintang-Communist Cooperation.
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Premier Zhou Enlai held a grand banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to warmly welcome Chairman Voroshilov and all the distinguished Soviet guests accompanying him on his visit to our country.

Chairman Voroshilov and all the Soviet guests accompanying him on his visit to our country arrived at the Beijing Hotel accompanied by Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Premier Zhou Enlai and the Vice Premiers were all waiting at the entrance to greet them. When Chairman Voroshilov and Chairman Mao Zedong entered the banquet hall, all the Chinese and foreign guests gathered in the hall stood up and applauded warmly for a long time.

Also attending the banquet were Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Lin Boqu, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Long Yun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Zhang Bojun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao, as well as heads of various departments of state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, generals, well-known figures from all walks of life in the capital, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

More than 1,400 people attended the banquet today.

At the beginning of the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass and said, "We are very honored to be able to hold such a banquet today to entertain Comrade Voroshilov and the Soviet comrades who have come with him. To express our joy, I invite everyone to fill their glasses and drink to the great Soviet people, to the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the great Chinese and Soviet peoples, to the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, to world peace, and to the health of the head of our great ally, the Soviet Union - Comrade Voroshilov!"

During the banquet, Chairman Voroshilov, together with Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, went around the hall to meet with Chinese and foreign guests. At this time, the atmosphere in the hall reached a climax of joy. Wherever they went, there was applause and laughter. Many people rushed to shake hands and talk with Chairman Voroshilov, and also rushed to shake hands and talk with Chairman Mao Zedong.

When Chairman Voroshilov saw Wei Lihuang, Premier Zhou Enlai introduced them. Voroshilov smiled and said to Wei Lihuang, "As long as we are united, we are invincible." Premier Zhou Enlai said that the Kuomintang and the Communist Party have cooperated twice in the past. Chairman Mao then said: "We are still prepared to conduct a third cooperation." Chairman Voroshilov waved his hand and said, "Cooperate forever."

When Chairman Voroshilov saw the generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, he smiled and said to the generals, "I love generals, but I am against war."

Chairman Voroshilov shook hands and talked with Mei Lanfang, a famous Chinese Peking Opera performer. He said, "I hope to have the opportunity to appreciate your performing arts."

Many Soviet experts present today were all in high spirits. When Chairman Voroshilov and Chairman Mao Zedong and others walked up to them, they applauded warmly. Chairman Voroshilov encouraged them to work hard. He said that helping the great Chinese people in their construction is the most glorious thing. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai all shook hands with the Soviet experts cordially to express their gratitude.

Chairman Voroshilov also spoke with the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to China. He said that Japan, the Soviet Union, and China are close neighbors and should become brothers and friends. He also said that we should have cooperated in a friendly manner long ago, and if we are willing to do so, we can achieve our goals.

After meeting with Chinese and foreign guests, Chairman Voroshilov walked to the microphone. He excitedly raised his glass to the health of his hosts headed by Comrade Mao Zedong; to the health of the national activists of the People's Republic of China.

He said: "I raise my glass to wish that all people on Earth live in peace, will not think of war, and strive for world peace."

When Chairman Voroshilov delivered his speech, the whole audience was jubilant, and bursts of stormy applause followed one after another, lasting continuously.

Finally, the guests and hosts together enjoyed wonderful singing and dancing, acrobatics, and other programs. (Attached picture)

### 伏罗希洛夫主席在周总理举行的酒会上  祝地球上所有的人生活在和平之中  毛主席说：我们还准备进行第三次国共合作

1957-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫主席在周总理举行的酒会上
    祝地球上所有的人生活在和平之中
    毛主席说：我们还准备进行第三次国共合作
    新华社16日讯　周恩来总理今晚在北京饭店举行盛大的酒会，热烈欢迎伏罗希洛夫主席和随同他来我国访问的苏联全体贵宾。
    伏罗希洛夫主席和随他同来我国访问的苏联全体客人，由毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长陪同来到北京饭店。周恩来总理和副总理们都在大门口迎候。当伏罗希洛夫主席和毛泽东主席走进酒会的大厅的时候，聚集在大厅里的中外人士全体起立，长久地热烈鼓掌。
    出席酒会的，还有副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻、薄一波，副委员长李济深、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会副会长林伯渠，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席张治中、龙云，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国驻苏联大使刘晓，以及国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，将军们，首都社会各界著名人士和各国驻中国的外交官员。
    今天有一千四百多人参加了酒会。
    酒会开始的时候，周恩来总理举杯说，我们今天非常荣幸能够举行这样的酒会，招待伏罗希洛夫同志和同来的苏联同志们。为了表示我们大家的高兴，我请大家斟满了酒，为伟大的苏联人民，为伟大的中苏两国人民兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国的团结，为世界和平，为我们伟大盟邦苏联元首——伏罗希洛夫同志的健康，干杯！
    酒会进行中，伏罗希洛夫主席同毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理一起绕着会场和中外人士相见，这时，会场上达到了欢乐的高潮，他们走到那里，那里就响起掌声和欢笑。许多人争着同伏罗希洛夫主席握手交谈，也争着同毛泽东主席握手交谈。
    伏罗希洛夫主席见到了卫立煌，周恩来总理作了介绍。伏罗希洛夫笑着对卫立煌说，只要我们团结一致，我们就是无敌的。周恩来总理说，国共两党过去已经合作了两次。毛主席紧接着说：我们还准备进行第三次合作。伏罗希洛夫主席挥着手说：“永远合作。”
    伏罗希洛夫主席见到中国人民解放军的将军们的时候，他笑着对将军们说，我爱将军，但是，我反对战争。
    伏罗希洛夫主席和中国著名的京剧表演艺术家梅兰芳握手交谈，他说，我希望能够有机会欣赏你的表演艺术。
    今天在座的许多苏联专家个个兴高采烈，当伏罗希洛夫主席和毛泽东主席等走到他们面前时，他们热烈鼓掌。伏罗希洛夫主席勉励他们好好工作。他说，帮助伟大的中国人民建设是一件最光荣的事情。毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理都同苏联专家们亲切握手致谢。
    伏罗希洛夫主席还同日本社会党访华亲善使节团谈了话。他说，日本、苏联、中国是近邻，应该成为兄弟朋友。他还说，我们很早就应该友好合作，如果我们愿意这样做，我们是可以达到我们的目的的。
    同中外人士会见以后，伏罗希洛夫主席走到扩音器前。他兴奋地举杯为他的以毛泽东同志为首的主人们的健康干杯；为中华人民共和国的国家活动家们的健康干杯。
    他说：我举杯祝地球上所有的人生活在和平之中，不会想到战争，争取全世界的和平。
    在伏罗希洛夫主席致词的时候，全场欢腾，暴风雨般的掌声一阵接着一阵，持久不息。
    最后，宾主一起欣赏了歌舞、杂技等精采节目。（附图片）
