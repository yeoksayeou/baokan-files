Title: Indonesia Establishes Special Agency to Receive Foreign Heads of State
Author:
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 16th: The "Foreign Heads of State Reception Committee," headed by Minister of Manpower Mobilization and Construction, Hanafi, was announced at the State Palace on the 15th. This committee will be responsible for receiving Chairman Voroshilov of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and other heads of state visiting Indonesia.

Voroshilov has accepted President Sukarno's invitation and will visit Indonesia in early May.

### 印度尼西亚设专门机构  负责接待外国元首

1957-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚设专门机构
    负责接待外国元首
    据新华社雅加达16日电　以人力动员建设部长哈纳菲为首的“外国元首接待委员会”15日在国家宫宣布成立。这个委员会将负责接待苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和访问印度尼西亚的其他国家的元首。
    伏罗希洛夫已经接受苏加诺总统的邀请，将在5月初到印度尼西亚进行访问。
