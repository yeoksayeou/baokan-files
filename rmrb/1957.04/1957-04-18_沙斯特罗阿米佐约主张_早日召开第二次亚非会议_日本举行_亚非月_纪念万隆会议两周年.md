Title: Sastroamidjojo Advocates for Early Convening of Second Asian-African Conference; Japan Holds "Asia-Africa Month" to Commemorate Second Anniversary of Bandung Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 17th: Former Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo said that the time has come to convene the Second Asian-African Conference. He believes that, in addition to discussing the issue of strengthening the unity of Asian and African countries and examining the implementation of the resolutions passed two years ago, the Second Asian-African Conference should find ways to strengthen the Bandung Spirit and make it more vibrant.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th: According to reports from Tokyo, the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee began holding "Asia-Africa Month" on the 15th of this month to commemorate the second anniversary of the Bandung Conference. On the evening of the 16th, a Japan-India Friendship Evening Party was held in Tokyo to commemorate the second anniversary of the Bandung Conference. Kunisuke Nagano, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee, Tokutaro Kitamura, Representative Committee Member, and the Chargé d'affaires of the Indian Embassy in Japan spoke at the meeting respectively. After the meeting, the Indian Sarabha Dance Troupe, which is currently visiting Japan, gave a farewell performance.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约主张  早日召开第二次亚非会议  日本举行“亚非月”纪念万隆会议两周年

1957-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约主张
    早日召开第二次亚非会议
    日本举行“亚非月”纪念万隆会议两周年
    据新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚前总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约说，现在已经到了召开第二次亚非会议的时候了。他认为，除了讨论加强亚非国家团结的问题和检查两年前通过的决议执行情况以外，第二次亚非会议应当设法加强万隆精神并且使它更有生气。
    据新华社17日讯　东京消息：日本亚洲团结委员会从本月15日起开始举行“亚非月”，纪念万隆会议两周年。16日晚间，在东京举行了万隆会议两周年纪念日印友好晚会。日本亚洲团结委员会理事长长野国助、代表委员北村德太郎和印度驻日本代办分别在会上讲了话。会后还由正在日本进行访问的印度撒拉杯舞蹈团举行了告别演出。
