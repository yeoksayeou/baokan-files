Title: Algerian Fighting Resumes; French Colonial Army Takes Hits Everywhere
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: Fighting between the Algerian Liberation Army and the French army has intensified again in the past week.

In the mountains near Marnia, French troops engaged in fierce fighting with a 200-strong Algerian Liberation Army unit. Near Inkermann, a French patrol was ambushed, resulting in nine deaths and five injuries. In Guelma, Constantine Province, Algerian insurgents launched a series of fierce attacks on French strongholds, forcing the French army to rush reinforcements to the area. In two battles near Orléansville and Affreville, the French suffered 22 dead and wounded.

### 阿尔及利亚激战又起  法国殖民军到处挨打

1957-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚激战又起
    法国殖民军到处挨打
    据新华社23日讯　阿尔及利亚解放军同法国军队之间的战斗一周来又趋激烈。
    在马尔尼亚附近的山区里，法军同一支二百人的阿尔及利亚解放军部队激战。在英克尔曼附近，法军巡逻队中埋伏，结果九人阵亡，五人受伤。在君士坦丁省的格尔马，阿尔及利亚起义者对法军据点发动了一系列的猛烈攻击，迫使法军慌忙向这个地区增援。在奥尔良斯维尔和阿弗来维耳附近的两次战斗中，法军死伤二十二人。
