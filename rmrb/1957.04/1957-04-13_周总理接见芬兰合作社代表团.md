Title: Premier Zhou Receives Finnish Cooperative Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th—Premier Zhou Enlai received the Finnish Cooperative Delegation, headed by Rakso and Ratvala, and all its members yesterday afternoon. The delegation is visiting China.
	Those accompanying the reception were Cheng Zihua, Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives; Zhang Qilong and Deng Chenxi, Deputy Directors; and Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade.
	The Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sun Shishi, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见芬兰合作社代表团

1957-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见芬兰合作社代表团
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理昨天下午接见了来我国访问的芬兰合作社代表团团长拉克索和拉特瓦拉以及全体团员。
    陪同接见的有，中华全国供销合作总社主任程子华，副主任张启龙、邓辰西和对外贸易部副部长雷任民等。
    接见时，芬兰驻华大使孙士敦也在座。
