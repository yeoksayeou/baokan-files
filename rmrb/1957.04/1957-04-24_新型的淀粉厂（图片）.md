    A New Type of Starch Factory
    The new type of starch factory built in Shijiazhuang with the help of the Soviet Union is about to begin trial production. The factory will produce 15,000 tons of starch and more than 3,000 tons of glucose annually for the pharmaceutical industry. Its by-product, corn steep liquor, is an important raw material for the production of antibiotics. This is a schematic diagram of the factory building designed by the Soviet Union for our country.　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency稿

### 新型的淀粉厂（图片）

1957-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

    新型的淀粉厂
    苏联帮助我国在石家庄建设的新型淀粉厂，即将开始试生产。这个厂每年生产一万五千吨淀粉和三千多吨葡萄糖，供制药工业用。它的副产品——玉米浆是制作抗生素的重要原料。这是苏联为我国设计的厂房示意图。　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社稿
