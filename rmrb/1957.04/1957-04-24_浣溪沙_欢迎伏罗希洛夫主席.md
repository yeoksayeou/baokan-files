Title: W溪沙 - Welcoming Chairman Voroshilov
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 2

    W溪沙
　　Welcoming Chairman Voroshilov
　　Li Jishen
    The whole world admires the vigorous elder,
    Soaring ten thousand miles, riding the long wind,
    Purple aura from the east, a dragon is hoped for.
    Brotherly alliance, lasting friendship,
    Protecting peace, establishing great merit,
    Joyfully watching the flags reflect the red sky!
