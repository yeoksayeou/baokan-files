Title: The Tibet Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee and other units jointly host a banquet to welcome the Dalai Lama.
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 5th. The Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly hosted a banquet yesterday afternoon to welcome the Dalai Lama. The banquet was held in the Gesang Deji Hall of the Dalai Lama's summer palace, Norbulingka. The Dalai Lama and his deputy scripture teacher, the Chi Jiang Living Buddha, both attended the banquet.
    Those present at the banquet included: Zhang Guohua, Second Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region and Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China; Tan Guansan and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretaries of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China; Major General Chen Mingyi, Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region; Major General Jin Shaoshan, Deputy Political Commissar; and Pingcuo Wangjie, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region.

### 西藏自治区筹委会等单位  联合设宴为达赖喇嘛洗尘

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会等单位
    联合设宴为达赖喇嘛洗尘
    新华社拉萨5日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会、中共西藏工委、中国人民解放军西藏军区，昨天下午联合设宴为达赖喇嘛洗尘。筵席设在达赖喇嘛的夏宫罗布林卡的格桑德基殿里。达赖喇嘛和他的副经师赤江活佛都参加了宴会。
    出席宴会的有：自治区筹委会第二副主任委员、中共西藏工委副书记张国华，中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三、周仁山，西藏军区副司令员陈明义少将、副政治委员金绍山少将，自治区筹委会副秘书长平措旺阶。
