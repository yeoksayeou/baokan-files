Title: Soviet Museum Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: Chen Sheng
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 7

    Soviet Museum Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing

    (Our newspaper dispatch) A Soviet museum workers delegation arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow on April 14th.  Wang Shuzhuang, Deputy Director of the State Bureau of Cultural Relics of the Ministry of Culture, and heads of various central museums in Beijing were present at the airport to welcome them.  This delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the 1957 Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The members of the delegation are Pavlov, a researcher at the Pushkin Museum of Fine Arts in Moscow, Dr. Jiake Nuova, Deputy Director of the Oriental Department of the Hermitage Museum in Leningrad, and Glukharyova, a researcher at the Museum of Oriental Culture in Moscow. They will conduct professional visits to museums in Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Jinan and other places.
                       (Chen Sheng)

### 苏博物馆工作者代表团抵京
辰生
1957-04-18
第7版()
专栏：

    苏博物馆工作者代表团抵京
    本报讯　苏联博物馆工作者代表团4月14日从莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有文化部文物事业管理局副局长王书庄及北京中央各博物馆的负责人。这个代表团是根据1957年中苏文化合作协定应邀来我国访问的。代表团的成员是莫斯科普希金造型艺术博物馆研究员巴夫洛夫、列宁格勒爱尔米塔什博物馆东方部嘉柯诺娃副博士、莫斯科东方文化博物馆研究员格鲁哈略娃。他们将在北京、南京、上海、杭州、济南等地博物馆进行专业性的参观访问。
                        （辰生）
