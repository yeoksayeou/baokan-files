Title: U.S. Spares No Effort in Preparing for War: To Use Artificial Satellites for Military Reconnaissance
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: TASS, London, March 31st: The Washington correspondent for "The Daily Telegraph and Morning Post" reports that the U.S. Navy Department has signed a contract to manufacture "artificial satellites for military reconnaissance purposes." Photographs of any region on Earth can be taken from this satellite. According to the reporter's confirmation, this type of "satellite" will be manufactured by the missile division of the California-based "Lockheed Aircraft Corporation" in about two years.

According to news from the American "Martin Company," a group of scientists will go to Baltimore to study plans for manufacturing another artificial satellite. This is a 150-pound scientific research device that can be placed at an altitude of 2,000 miles, or even into deeper interstellar space.

### 美国备战无所不为  要用人造卫星搞军事侦察

1957-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美国备战无所不为
    要用人造卫星搞军事侦察
    据新华社2日讯　塔斯社伦敦3月31日讯：“每日电讯与晨邮报”驻华盛顿记者报道，据悉，美国海军部签订了一项制造“用于军事侦察目的的人造卫星”的合同。从这个卫星上可以摄取地球上任何地区的照片。据记者证实，这类“卫星”将在两年左右的时间内由加利福尼亚“洛克希德飞机公司”生产导弹的分公司制造出来。
    据美国“马丁航空公司”消息，有一批科学家将去巴尔的摩研究制造另一个人造卫星的计划。这是一个重一百五十磅的科学研究器械，可以放到二千英里的高空，甚至能放到更远的星际空间去。
