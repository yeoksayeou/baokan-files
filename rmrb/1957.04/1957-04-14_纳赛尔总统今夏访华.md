Title: President Nasser to Visit China This Summer
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th – Egyptian Ambassador to China, Hassan Ragab, announced at a press conference held here this morning that President Gamal Abdel Nasser of the Republic of Egypt will visit China this summer.

Ambassador Hassan Ragab told reporters: "We are now working hard to set up the new embassy to prepare for the President's upcoming visit." (Attached picture)

### 纳赛尔总统今夏访华

1957-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统今夏访华
    新华社13日讯　埃及驻华大使哈桑·拉加卜今天上午在这里举行的记者招待会上宣布，埃及共和国纳赛尔总统将在今年夏天访问中国。
    哈桑·拉加卜大使向记者们说：“我们现在正在紧张地布置新的大使馆，准备迎接总统即将到来的访问。”（附图片）
