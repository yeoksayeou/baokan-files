Author: Hui Xuan
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 7

Which Foreign Languages are Taught in Universities?

Currently, there are eight foreign language institutes in the country: Beijing Russian Language Institute, Beijing Foreign Language Institute, Harbin Foreign Language Institute, Shanghai Foreign Language Institute, Southwest Russian Language Specialized School, Northwest Russian Language Specialized School, Shenyang Russian Language Specialized School, and Xinjiang Language Institute. Foreign language departments are also established in eight comprehensive universities (Peking University, Nankai University, Fudan University, Nanjing University, Xiamen University, Wuhan University, Sun Yat-sen University, Shandong University).

The foreign languages taught in foreign language institutes and foreign language departments of comprehensive universities across the country total seventeen: Russian, English, German, French, Czech, Romanian, Polish, Spanish, Mongolian, Korean, Japanese, Vietnamese, Siamese, Burmese, Hindi, Indonesian, and Arabic.

Currently, there are approximately 12,000 students studying these foreign languages in schools.

(Hui Xuan)

### 高等学校教那些外国语？
惠萱
1957-04-13
第7版()
专栏：小统计

    高等学校教那些外国语？
    目前全国已设有外国语学院八所：北京俄语学院、北京外国语学院、哈尔滨外国语学院、上海外国语学院、西南俄语专科学校、西北俄语专科学校、沈阳俄语专科学校、新疆语文学院。在八所综合大学（北京大学、南开大学、复旦大学、南京大学、厦门大学、武汉大学、中山大学、山东大学）中，还设有外语专业。
    全国各外语学院和综合大学外语专业所教学的外国文字共有十七种：俄语、英语、德语、法语、捷克斯洛伐克语、罗马尼亚语、波兰语、西班牙语、蒙古语、朝鲜语、日本语、越南语、暹罗语、缅甸语、印地语、印度尼西亚语、阿拉伯语。
    目前学习这些外国语的在校学生约有一万二千多人。
                        （惠　萱）
