    Sun Chapter
    Yi Ge
    The greatness of the sun lies in its eternal consumption of itself, providing all things with light and heat without compensation.
    Spring bestows vitality upon all things, and the new green repays spring with beauty.
    Although winter is severe, it nurtures life.
    Bees diligently collect honey, never asking whether it is for themselves or for others.
    The sea never cares whether a drop comes from the river or a stream from the mountains.

### 太阳篇
易鸽
1957-04-25
第8版()
专栏：

    太阳篇
    易鸽
    太阳的伟大，在于它永远消耗自己，无偿地供给万物以光热。
    春天赐万物以生机，新绿偿春天以美。
    冬天虽然严峻，可是它孕育着生命。
    蜜蜂辛勤地采蜜，从不过问为自己还是为别人。
    大海，绝不计较那一滴来自大江，那一滴来自山涧。
