    Mei Lanfang and Others to Perform Benefit Performances in Beijing

    (Our newspaper) Beijing News – Mei Lanfang, chairman of the Beijing Opera Workers' Union, will hold benefit performances at the Beijing Music Hall on the 24th and 25th of this month to raise welfare funds for members. Tan Fuying, Qiu Shengrong, Li Wanchun, Li Hezeng, Jiang Miaoxiang, Liu Lianrong, Huang Yuanqing, and Li Xiaochun will also participate in the performances. The repertoire includes "Drunken Concubine," "Imperial Stele Pavilion," "The Chain Trap," "Double Investiture of Tang," "Picking the Chariot with Niu Gao's Letter," and "At the Crossroads." (Fei Yu)

### 梅兰芳等将在京义演
非余
1957-04-19
第7版()
专栏：

    梅兰芳等将在京义演
    本报讯　北京市京剧工作者联合会主任委员梅兰芳，将于本月24、25两日在北京市音乐堂，为会员筹集福利基金举行义演。参加演出的还有谭富英、裘盛戎、李万春、李和曾、姜妙香、刘连荣、黄元庆和李小春等，演出的剧目是“贵妃醉酒”“御碑亭”“连环套”“双投唐”“挑滑车带牛皋下书”和“三岔口”等。　（非余）
