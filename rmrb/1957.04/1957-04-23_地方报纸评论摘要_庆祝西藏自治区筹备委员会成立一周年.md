Title: Summary of Local Newspaper Commentary: Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 22nd. *Tibetan Daily* published a commentary today entitled "Continue to Strengthen Anti-Imperialist Patriotic and National Unity Work, and Strive to Gradually Realize National Regional Autonomy in Tibet," celebrating the first anniversary of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

The commentary pointed out that since the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region a year ago, certain achievements have been made in various tasks, the relationship between the central government and the local government of Tibet has become closer, and national unity has been strengthened. Facts have proved that the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region is suitable for the current specific situation of the Tibetan people and is needed by the Tibetan people.

Regarding the main tasks of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region in the future, the commentary pointed out: First, continue to prepare for the establishment of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region. Second, vigorously carry out anti-imperialist patriotic education. Third, protect the lives and property of the Tibetan people in accordance with the law, implement freedom of religious belief, and protect lama temples and their income. To this end, it is necessary to further strengthen national unity, strengthen the unity between the Han and Tibetan nationalities and within the Tibetan nationality, continue to expand the anti-imperialist patriotic united front, and oppose the subversive activities of imperialism and the criminal activities of splitters betraying the motherland.

### 地方报纸评论摘要  庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立一周年

1957-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    地方报纸评论摘要
    庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立一周年
    新华社拉萨22日电　西藏日报今日以“继续加强反帝爱国和民族团结的工作，为逐步实现西藏地区的民族区域自治而努力”为题发表评论，庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立一周年。
    评论指出：西藏自治区筹备委员会成立一年来，各项工作都取得了一定成绩，更加密切了中央和西藏地方的关系，民族团结加强了。事实证明自治区筹备委员会是适合于西藏民族目前具体情况的，是西藏人民所需要的。
    关于自治区筹备委员会今后的主要工作，评论指出：第一、继续筹备成立统一的西藏自治区的各项具体工作。第二、大力开展反帝爱国主义教育。第三、依据法律保护西藏人民生命财产，实行宗教信仰自由，保护喇嘛寺庙及其收入。为此，必须进一步加强民族团结，加强藏汉民族与西藏民族内部的团结，继续扩大反帝爱国统一战线，反对帝国主义的颠覆活动和分裂分子背叛祖国的罪恶活动。
