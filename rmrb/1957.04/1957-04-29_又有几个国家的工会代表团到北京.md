Title: More Union Delegations from Several Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations in our country, the Pakistani trade union delegation headed by Rahman, Chairman of the All Pakistan Postal and Telegraph Workers Union, the Nepalese trade union delegation headed by Kamahaliya, Joint Secretary of the All Nepal Trade Union Congress, the Indonesian trade union delegation headed by Sutoyo, Member of Parliament of Indonesia and Member of the National Council of the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, and the Burmese trade union delegation headed by Tekin Miao Niu, Member of Parliament of Burma and Central Executive Committee Member of the Trade Union Congress (Burma), arrived in Beijing by plane today. Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 又有几个国家的工会代表团到北京

1957-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    又有几个国家的工会代表团到北京
    据新华社28日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的以全巴基斯坦邮电工会主席拉曼为首的巴基斯坦工会代表团，以全尼泊尔工会大会联合书记卡马哈利亚为首的尼泊尔工会代表团，以印度尼西亚国会议员、全印度尼西亚中央工会全国理事会理事苏托约为首的印度尼西亚工会代表团和以缅甸国会议员、工会大会（缅甸）中央执行委员德钦苗纽为首的缅甸工会代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范等人。
