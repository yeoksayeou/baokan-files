Title: Fertilizer Replaces Explosives

Author:

Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 2

Fertilizer Replaces Explosives
　　The use of chemical fertilizer - fertilizer powder (ammonium nitrate) - instead of ammonium nitrate explosives for blasting mountains has been basically successfully tested in the third engineering section of the Sichuan-Guizhou Railway. This test used a total of 0.36 kg of fertilizer powder, chaff, charcoal skin and T.N.T. for detonation, and blasted 1.97 cubic meters more stone than the equivalent amount of ammonium nitrate explosives. The cost of the above four materials is only a little over six cents, while ammonium nitrate explosives would cost more than thirty-eight cents.

### 肥田粉代替炸药

1957-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

    肥田粉代替炸药
    用化学肥料——肥田粉（硝酸铵）代替硝铵炸药开山放炮，已在川黔铁路第三工程段基本上试验成功。这次试验共用肥田粉、糠壳、炭皮和T．N．T共零点三六公斤作引爆之用，比同等分量的硝铵炸药的爆破效能多炸掉石头一点九七方。上述四项材料的成本只有六分多，要用硝铵炸药就要三角八分多。
