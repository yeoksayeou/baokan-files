Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 58th expanded meeting on the afternoon of the 8th. They heard a report on water conservancy work by Minister of Water Conservancy Fu Zuoyi.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议

1957-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    新华社8日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会8日下午举行第五十八次会议扩大会议。听取了水利部部长傅作义关于水利工作的报告。
