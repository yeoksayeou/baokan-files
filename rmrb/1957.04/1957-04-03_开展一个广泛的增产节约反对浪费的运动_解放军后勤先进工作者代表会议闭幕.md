Title: Launch a Broad Campaign to Increase Production, Practice Economy, and Combat Waste: Closing of the Representative Meeting of Advanced Logistics Workers of the People's Liberation Army

Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. General Hong Xuezhi, Director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, announced at the closing ceremony of the All-Army Advanced Logistics Workers Representative Meeting today: The logistics departments of the entire army must launch a broad campaign to increase production, practice economy, and combat waste. He called on every logistics worker to pay attention to economy in everything and prevent waste in everything, so as to spend less money and accomplish more and better things.

In his speech, General Hong Xuezhi pointed out that this year's logistics budget for the entire army has been reduced by nearly one-third compared to last year. In order to use these funds most effectively and economically to fully guarantee the supply of the troops, the main tasks of the logistics departments at all levels are currently: to streamline institutions and carry out the movement of increasing production and practicing economy and opposing waste; to improve operations and improve the quality of work; to strengthen professional training and improve the quality of training. Only in this way can we accelerate the modernization and regularization of the PLA's logistics construction.

Since the opening of the Representative Meeting of Advanced Logistics Workers of the People's Liberation Army on the 22nd of last month, a total of thirty-five representatives of advanced workers and advanced units have introduced their achievements at the meeting. All representatives also divided into thirty-one professional groups to extensively exchange experiences.

During the meeting, CCP Central Committee leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De and others received all the representatives. Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Commission Marshal Ye Jianying, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Su Yu, Director of the General Political Department General Xiao Hua, and Deputy Director of the General Political Department General Gan Siqi, attended the meeting and gave instructions.

### 开展一个广泛的增产节约反对浪费的运动  解放军后勤先进工作者代表会议闭幕

1957-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    开展一个广泛的增产节约反对浪费的运动
    解放军后勤先进工作者代表会议闭幕
    新华社2日讯　中国人民解放军总后勤部部长洪学智上将今天在全军后勤先进工作者代表会议闭幕式上宣布：全军后勤部门要开展一个广泛的增产节约、反对浪费的运动。他号召每一个后勤工作人员要处处注意节约、事事防止浪费，做到钱要花得少，事要办得多、办得好。
    洪学智上将在讲话中指出，今年全军的后勤事业费比去年减少了将近三分之一。为了最有效、最节约地使用这些资金，来充分保证部队的供应，各级后勤部门当前的主要任务是：精减机构，开展增产节约、反对浪费运动；改进业务，提高工作质量；加强业务训练，提高训练质量。只有这样，才能加速解放军现代化、正规化的后勤建设。
    解放军后勤先进工作者代表会议从上月22日开幕以来，共有三十五名先进工作者和先进单位的代表在大会上介绍了事迹。全体代表还划分三十一个专业小组，广泛地交流了经验。
    会议期间，中共中央领导人周恩来、朱德等接见了全体代表。国务院副总理薄一波，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将、总干部部部长萧华上将、总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将，分别到会作了指示。
