Title: Ulaanbaatar Rally Welcomes Czechoslovak Delegation; Premier Široký Leads Delegation Home

Author:

Date: 1957-04-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 9th: Approximately 30,000 citizens of Ulaanbaatar gathered at Sükhbaatar Square on the afternoon of April 8th to welcome Premier Široký and the Czechoslovak government delegation he led.

The Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Ulaanbaatar Municipal People's Representatives' Hural, Bat-Ochiryn Jargalsaikhan, first delivered a speech on behalf of the citizens to welcome the distinguished guests. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, spoke about the achievements of the Czechoslovak people in socialist construction. Subsequently, Premier Široký delivered a speech. In his speech, he extended greetings from the Czechoslovak people to the Mongolian people and praised the spirit of the Mongolian people in overcoming difficulties and advancing victoriously in construction.

That evening, Premier Široký and his wife held a banquet to entertain the leaders of the Mongolian Party and government, as well as foreign envoys to Mongolia.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 9th: The Czechoslovak government delegation, headed by Premier Široký, left Ulaanbaatar by plane today to return home after concluding its visit to four fraternal countries in Asia. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Dashiin Damba, other Mongolian Party and government leaders, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Mongolia saw them off at the airport.

### 乌兰巴托集会欢迎捷代表团  西罗基总理率领代表团回国

1957-04-10
第5版()
专栏：

    乌兰巴托集会欢迎捷代表团
    西罗基总理率领代表团回国
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　乌兰巴托约有三万市民8日下午在苏赫巴托尔广场集会，欢迎西罗基总理和他率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团。
    乌兰巴托市劳动人民代表呼拉尔执行局主席巴特热尔加尔首先代表市民致词欢迎贵宾。蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔讲述了捷克斯洛伐克人民在社会主义建设中所获得的成就。接着，西罗基总理发表了演说。他在讲话中代表捷克斯洛伐克人民向蒙古人民问候，并且赞扬了蒙古人民在建设中克服困难、胜利前进的精神。
    当天晚上，西罗基总理和夫人举行宴会，招待蒙古党和政府的领导人以及各国驻蒙古使节。
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团在结束了对亚洲四个兄弟国家的访问以后，今天乘飞机离乌兰巴托回国。蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴和蒙古其他党政负责人以及各国驻蒙古外交使节等到机场欢送。
