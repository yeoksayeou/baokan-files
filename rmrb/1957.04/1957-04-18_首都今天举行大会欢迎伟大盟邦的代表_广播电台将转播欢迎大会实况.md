Title: The Capital Will Hold a Grand Meeting Today to Welcome Representatives of the Great Allied Nation; Radio Station to Broadcast the Meeting Live
Author:
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the afternoon of the 18th to welcome Comrade Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the welcome rally on its first program starting at 2:55 PM on the 18th.

### 首都今天举行大会欢迎伟大盟邦的代表  广播电台将转播欢迎大会实况

1957-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今天举行大会欢迎伟大盟邦的代表
    广播电台将转播欢迎大会实况
    新华社17日讯　首都各界人民将在18日下午举行盛大集会，欢迎苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫同志。中央人民广播电台将从18日下午十四点五十五分开始，在第一种节目里，转播欢迎大会的实况。
