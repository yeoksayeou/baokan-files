Title: Soviet Kazakhstan National Kurmangazy Folk Orchestra Performs in Beijing (Photos)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 2

    Soviet Kazakhstan National Kurmangazy Folk Orchestra Performs in Beijing
    The orchestra performs "Senjide Ma" and "Evening Party" composed by Chinese composer He Luting.
    Chinese literary and art workers visit the orchestra's "Dombra" instrument.
    Kazakh Republic Honored Artist and winner of the 4th World Youth and Student Festival in Bucharest, Serkebayev sings Figaro's cavatina.
    Soviet Kazakhstan National Conservatory soprano soloist Tulekinova sings "The Nightingale".
                  Our reporter Gao Liang photographed

### 苏联哈萨克斯坦国立库尔曼喀则民间乐队在北京演出（图片）
高粮
1957-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联哈萨克斯坦国立库尔曼喀则民间乐队在北京演出
    乐队演奏中国作曲家贺绿汀作的“森吉德马”和“晚会”。
    我国文艺工作者参观乐队的“东不拉”琴。
    哈萨克共和国功勋演员、布加勒斯特第四届世界青年与学生联欢节比赛获奖者谢尔开巴也夫演唱费加罗的卡伐蒂那。
    苏联哈萨克斯坦国立音乐院女高音独唱家图列基诺娃演唱的“夜鹰”。
                  本报记者　高粮摄
