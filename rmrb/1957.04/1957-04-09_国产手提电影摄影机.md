Title: Domestic Handheld Movie Camera
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 2
	
    Domestic Handheld Movie Camera
    The first domestic handheld 35mm movie camera has been successfully trial-produced at the Nanjing Film Machinery Factory. This camera can be used to shoot newsreels, science education films, and outdoor scenes for artistic films.
    This camera will be used for the first time to shoot a documentary film at the inaugural meeting of the Chinese Film Workers Association in mid-April.

### 国产手提电影摄影机

1957-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

    国产手提电影摄影机
    第一台国产手提三十五毫米电影摄影机在南京电影机械厂试制成功。这台摄影机可以拍摄新闻纪录片，科学教育片和艺术片的外景。
    这台摄影机将在4月中旬举行的中国电影工作者联谊会成立大会上首次拍摄纪录影片。
