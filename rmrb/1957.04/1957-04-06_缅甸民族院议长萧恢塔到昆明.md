Title: Burmese Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio Arrives in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, April 5th. Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma and former President, arrived in Kunming by plane today at the invitation of Yunnan Province, China, accompanied by Ambassador Yao Zhongming. Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio was accompanied by his wife and several sons and daughters.

Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio was warmly welcomed at Kunming Airport by Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, and those in charge of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Liu Geping, Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, also came from Beijing to welcome Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio.

Also present at the airport to welcome him was Wu Mao Mao Duo, Burmese Consul General in Kunming, and others.

Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio also met his relative, Dao Jingban, Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province, at the airport. Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio's wife and Governor Dao Jingban's wife are sisters. They expressed particular joy at this first meeting in fifteen years.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 5th. Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities, Sao Hkun Hkio, and his wife, Member of the Chamber of Representatives Maha Devi, left Rangoon by special plane this morning for a visit to Yunnan Province, China.

Sao Hkun Hkio told reporters that he and his family were going to Yunnan Province, China to visit relatives there. He said that in his childhood he often went to Yunnan because his family had many relatives there.

Sao Hkun Hkio was the first president of the Union of Burma, and he is now one of the leaders of the Shan State, which borders Yunnan Province, China.

### 缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔到昆明

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔到昆明
    新华社昆明5日电　应邀来我国云南省昆明市访问的缅甸联邦民族院议长、前总统萧恢塔由姚仲明大使陪同，今天乘飞机到达昆明。和萧恢塔议长一同到达昆明的，有他的夫人和几位男女公子。
    萧恢塔议长在昆明机场上受到了云南省代省长刘明辉、昆明市市长潘朔端及政协云南省委员会负责人的热烈欢迎。
    我全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一也特地由北京赶来欢迎萧恢塔议长。
    前往机场欢迎的还有缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎等。
    萧恢塔议长在机场上还会见了他的亲戚——云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州州长刀京版。萧恢塔议长的夫人和刀京版州长的夫人是同胞姊妹。他们对于分别十五年以来这第一次相会，表示特别高兴。
    据新华社仰光5日电　缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔和他的夫人、代表院议员马哈德维在今天上午乘专机离仰光前往中国云南省访问。
    萧恢塔对记者说，他和他的家庭是去中国云南省探望在那里的亲戚的。他说，在他的童年时期，他经常去云南，因为他的家庭有许多亲戚在那里。
    萧恢塔是缅甸联邦的第一任总统，他现在是掸邦的领导人之一，掸邦同中国云南省交界。
