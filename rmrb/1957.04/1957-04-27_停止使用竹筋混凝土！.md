Title: Stop Using Bamboo-Reinforced Concrete!
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 3

    Stop Using Bamboo-Reinforced Concrete!
    (Reported by this newspaper) Since 1956, due to the shortage of steel, many construction units, in order to urgently solve housing problems, and construction teams, in order to avoid work stoppages, have used bamboo-reinforced concrete to replace steel-reinforced concrete. Now there is a growing trend of popularizing this method. However, based on usage in various areas, the method of using bamboo-reinforced concrete to replace steel-reinforced concrete still has many technical and economic issues that deserve further research. To avoid potential losses to state property, the State Construction Commission recently notified relevant departments to temporarily stop using bamboo-reinforced concrete in buildings.

### 停止使用竹筋混凝土！

1957-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    停止使用竹筋混凝土！
    本报讯　从1956年以来，由于钢材供应不足，不少建设单位为了急于解决住房问题，施工单位为了避免窝工，曾采用了竹筋混凝土来代替钢筋混凝土，现在更有逐渐普遍推广的趋势。但是，从各方面使用情况来看，以竹筋混凝土代替钢筋混凝土的办法，不论在技术上、经济上都还存在着很多值得研究的问题。为了避免可能造成国家财产的损失，国家建设委员会最近通知有关部门，在建筑物中暂时停止使用竹筋混凝土。
