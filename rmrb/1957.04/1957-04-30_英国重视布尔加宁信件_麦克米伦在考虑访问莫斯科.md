Title: Britain Values Bulganin's Letter; Macmillan Considering Visit to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th, London Dispatch: British Prime Minister Macmillan hastily convened a cabinet meeting after ending his Easter holiday. He also held talks with former Prime Minister Churchill regarding British foreign policy. Sources say that the British government is increasingly valuing Bulganin's recent letter to Macmillan.

According to Western news agencies and newspapers, Macmillan is considering a visit to Moscow. British Conservative MP Maud, speaking on the issue in the "Sunday Express" on April 28th, said he approved of Macmillan visiting Moscow. He said: "Macmillan's visit to Moscow should not be conditional on the permission of the United States." He also said: "I hope we have not completely become a satellite state of the United States."

### 英国重视布尔加宁信件  麦克米伦在考虑访问莫斯科

1957-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    英国重视布尔加宁信件
    麦克米伦在考虑访问莫斯科
    据新华社29日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相麦克米伦在结束复活节假期后，仓促地召开了内阁会议。他还同前首相丘吉尔就英国外交政策进行了会谈。有消息说，英国政府越来越重视布尔加宁最近给麦克米伦的信。
    据西方通讯社和报刊报道，麦克米伦正在考虑访问莫斯科的问题。英国保守党议员莫德4月28日在“星期日快报”谈到这个问题的时候说，他赞成麦克米伦访问莫斯科。他说：“麦克米伦的访问莫斯科，不应该以美国的许可为条件。”他还说：“我希望我们还没有完全成为美国的卫星国。”
