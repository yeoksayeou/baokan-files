Title: Disarmament Subcommittee Discusses Missiles and Rockets
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 6

    Disarmament Subcommittee
    Discusses Missiles and Rockets

    Xinhua News Agency, London, April 26th: The United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee reconvened on April 25th after the Easter recess. According to Western news agencies, the meeting began to discuss the fifth item on the agenda - the issue of missiles and rockets. It is said that the Soviet Union suggested at the meeting to ban rocket launchers, missiles and all types of weapons, including atomic cannons, as part of a general ban on atomic weapons. Representatives of Western countries emphasized the need to first establish a monitoring system on this issue.
    The next meeting of the Subcommittee is scheduled for April 29th.

### 裁军小组委员会  讨论导弹和火箭问题

1957-04-27
第6版()
专栏：

    裁军小组委员会
    讨论导弹和火箭问题
    新华社26日讯　伦敦消息：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会在复活节休会后，于4月25日复会。据西方通讯社透露，会议开始讨论议程上的第五个项目——导弹和火箭问题。据说，苏联在会上建议禁止火箭炮、导弹和包括原子大炮在内的各种类型的武器，作为总的禁止原子武器的禁令的一部分。西方国家代表在这个问题上强调必须首先建立监督体系。
    小组委员会下次会议定在4月29日举行。
