Title: Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik Visits Nanjing, then to Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, April 24th: Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma and former President Sao Shwe Thaik, and others, left Nanjing by train for Tianjin tonight after visiting and touring Nanjing.

Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik and his wife, accompanied by Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, arrived in Nanjing by train from Shanghai yesterday evening.管文蔚, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, held a welcome banquet for Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik and others at noon today.

Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik paid his respects at Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and laid a wreath. He, his family members, and entourage also visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Linggu Park, Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum, and Xuanwu Lake.

### 萧恢塔议长访南京后去天津

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    萧恢塔议长访南京后去天津
    新华社南京24日电　缅甸联邦民族院议长、前总统萧恢塔等在南京参观游览后，今晚乘火车离开南京去天津。
    萧恢塔议长和他的夫人等在昨天晚上由我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一陪同，从上海乘火车来到南京。江苏省副省长管文蔚和南京市市长彭冲今天中午为萧恢塔议长等举行了欢迎宴会。
    萧恢塔议长曾晋谒中山陵并献花圈。他和他的家属、随员们还游览了灵谷公园、明孝陵、玄武湖等名胜古迹。
