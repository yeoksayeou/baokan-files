Title: Israel Clamors to Re-Invade Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th, Tel Aviv dispatch: Israeli Foreign Minister Golda Meir publicly issued a war threat to Egypt at a Rotary Club banquet in Haifa on the evening of the 4th. She said that if necessary, Israeli troops would return to the Sinai Peninsula and Gaza in Egypt.

She said, "We must make it unnecessary for us to return to the areas we have just withdrawn from. But if circumstances force us to do so, we will go back no matter how unwilling we are."

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th, Jerusalem Israeli area dispatch: Israeli Prime Minister David Ben-Gurion said in parliament on the 2nd that he had obtained assurances regarding "free and innocent passage" in the Gulf of Aqaba. He explained that Israel's concept of "free and innocent passage" is that all ships have freedom, regardless of what cargo they carry. He said that the only condition is that they do not fire on other ships.

According to him, the Israeli port of Eilat in the Gulf of Aqaba will soon become the second Haifa. Haifa is Israel's main seaport on the Mediterranean.

### 以色列叫嚣重新侵略埃及

1957-04-06
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列叫嚣重新侵略埃及
    据新华社5日讯　特拉维夫消息：以色列外交部长梅厄夫人4日晚在海法的一次扶轮社的宴会上公然对埃及发出战争威胁。她说，如果必要，以色列军队会回到埃及的西奈半岛和加沙去。
    她说，“必须使我们没有必要再回到我们刚刚撤出的那些地区去。但是，如果环境逼迫我们这样做的话，我们不管多末不愿意也会回去的。”
    据新华社4日讯　耶路撒冷以色列区消息：以色列总理本—古里安2日在议会说，他已经得到了关于在亚喀巴湾“自由和无害通航”的保证。他解释说，以色列对于“自由和无害通航”的概念是，一切船只都有自由，不管它们载的是什么货。他说，唯一的条件是它们不向其他船只开火。
    据他说，亚喀巴湾的以色列港埃拉特将很快成为第二个海法。海法是以色列在地中海的主要海港。
