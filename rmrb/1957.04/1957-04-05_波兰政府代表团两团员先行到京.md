Title: Two Members of the Polish Government Delegation Arrive in Beijing Ahead of Main Group
Author:
Date: 1957-04-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: Mieczysław Lesz, Vice-Chairman of the Polish People's Republic Planning Commission, and Bolesław Strużek arrived in Beijing this afternoon. They are members of the Polish People's Republic Government Delegation that will soon be visiting China. Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of the People's Republic of China, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 波兰政府代表团两团员先行到京

1957-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团两团员先行到京
    据新华社4日电　波兰人民共和国计划委员会副主席米奇斯瓦夫·莱什和布莱斯瓦夫·斯特鲁泽克在今天下午到达北京。他们是即将来华进行友好访问的波兰人民共和国政府代表团团员。到机场迎接的有中华人民共和国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰等。
