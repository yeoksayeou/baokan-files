Title: National Congress of Advanced Workers in Film Distribution and Screening Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 3
 
    National Congress of Advanced Workers in Film Distribution and Screening Opens
    (Our newspaper reports) The National Congress of Advanced Workers in Film Distribution and Screening opened in Beijing on April 10. The congress is attended by 357 representatives of advanced workers from film distribution, film machine repair and supply, screening training, screening management institutions, film projection teams, and cinemas, including twelve ethnic minorities such as Uyghur, Hui, Miao, Tibetan, Mongolian, and Zhuang. The representatives come from factories, mines, rural areas, troops, and remote areas of the motherland. During the congress, they will extensively exchange advanced experiences in film distribution and screening work to further improve the quality of work, better serve the audience, and serve socialist construction.
    At the meeting, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, delivered the opening speech. He affirmed the great achievements made in film distribution and screening work in the past seven years; and pointed out that vigorously increasing production and practicing economy, actively improving the quality of work, continuing to carry forward the hardworking and simple work style, and maintaining close ties with the people are important tasks for film distribution and screening workers at present and in the future. ### 全国电影发行、放映先进工作者代表大会开幕

1957-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    全国电影发行、放映先进工作者代表大会开幕
    本报讯：　全国电影发行、放映先进工作者代表大会于4月10日在北京开幕。出席这次大会的有电影发行、电影机器修理供应、放映训练、放映管理等机构和电影放映队、电影院的先进工作者代表共三百五十七人，其中包括维吾尔、回、苗、藏、蒙古、僮等十二个少数民族。代表们来自祖国的工矿、农村、部队以及边远地区，他们将在大会期间广泛地交流电影发行、放映工作的先进经验，以便进一步提高工作质量，更好地为观众服务，为社会主义建设服务。
    会上，文化部副部长夏衍致开幕词。他肯定了七年来电影发行、放映工作所取得的巨大成就；同时指出，厉行增产节约，积极提高工作质量，继续发扬艰苦朴素的工作作风，与人民群众保持密切的联系，是电影发行和放映工作者当前和今后的重要任务。（附图片）
