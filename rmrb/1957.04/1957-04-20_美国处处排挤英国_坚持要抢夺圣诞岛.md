Title: The United States Excludes Britain Everywhere, Insisting on Seizing Christmas Island
Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: An official of the U.S. State Department said on April 17th that the United States will maintain its claim to sovereignty over Christmas Island, although it is not currently pressing that claim.

The question of sovereignty over Christmas Island has been a point of contention between the United States and Britain. Britain's hydrogen bomb test is scheduled to take place on this island soon.

### 美国处处排挤英国  坚持要抢夺圣诞岛

1957-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    美国处处排挤英国
    坚持要抢夺圣诞岛
    据新华社19日讯　美国国务院的一个官员4月17日说，美国将保持它对圣诞岛的主权要求，虽然目前不坚持这种要求。
    关于圣诞岛的主权问题，美国和英国曾经有所争执。英国的氢弹试验不久就要在这个岛上举行。
