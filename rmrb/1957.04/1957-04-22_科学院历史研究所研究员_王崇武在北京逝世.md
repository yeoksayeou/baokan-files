Title: Researcher Wang Chongwu of the Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Dies in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 2

    Researcher Wang Chongwu of the Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
    Wang Chongwu Dies in Beijing
    (Our newspaper reports) Comrade Wang Chongwu, researcher of the Third Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, died of illness in Beijing at 8:00 p.m. on April 21st. A funeral committee has been formed by Tao Menghe, Fan Wenlan, and 24 others.

### 科学院历史研究所研究员  王崇武在北京逝世

1957-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

    科学院历史研究所研究员
    王崇武在北京逝世
    本报讯　中国科学院历史研究所第三所研究员王崇武同志因病于4月21日下午八时在北京逝世，已由陶孟和、范文澜等二十六人组成治丧委员会。
