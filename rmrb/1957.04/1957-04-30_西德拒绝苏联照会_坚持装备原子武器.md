Title: West Germany Rejects Soviet Note, Insists on Equipping with Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, April 29th: The West German government issued a statement on April 28th, flatly rejecting the Soviet government's note warning West Germany not to equip its army with atomic weapons.

The statement described the Soviet note as "insulting" and "impolite," and said that the Soviet Union was attempting to "interfere in internal affairs."

### 西德拒绝苏联照会  坚持装备原子武器

1957-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    西德拒绝苏联照会  坚持装备原子武器
    据新华社29日讯　波恩消息：西德政府4月28日发表声明，断然拒绝苏联政府警告西德不要用原子武器装备它的军队的照会。
    声明把苏联的照会说成是“侮辱性的”和“不客气的”，并且说苏联企图“干涉内政”。
