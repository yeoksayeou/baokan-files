Title: Further Develop the Fraternal Relations Between the Two Countries: Soviet-Albanian Party and Government Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 5

    Further Develop the Fraternal Relations Between the Two Countries
    Soviet-Albanian Party and Government Delegations Begin Talks
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th – According to TASS, the Soviet government and Communist Party delegation began talks with the Albanian government and Labor Party delegation on the 11th in the Kremlin.
    The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Bulganin, Khrushchev, Mikoyan, Saburov, and Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, and others.
    The Albanian side participating in the talks included: Shehu, Hoxha, Nushi, Koleka, Marko, and Foreign Minister Shtulla, and others.
    At the beginning of the talks, Bulganin warmly welcomed the Albanian delegation and, through it, extended greetings to the fraternal Albanian people. Shehu then gave a reply.
    During the talks, the two sides friendly exchanged views on political and economic issues of significant importance for further developing the fraternal relations between the Soviet Union and Albania. The participants in the talks also exchanged views on issues related to the development of the current international situation. On all issues covered in the talks, both sides demonstrated complete unanimity of opinion and full mutual understanding. The talks decided to entrust experts from the delegations of the two countries to draft relevant proposals and submit them to the delegation meeting for discussion.
    The talks will continue.

### 进一步发展两国间的兄弟关系  苏阿党政代表团开始会谈

1957-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步发展两国间的兄弟关系
    苏阿党政代表团开始会谈
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府和共产党代表团同阿尔巴尼亚政府和劳动党代表团11日开始在克里姆林宫举行会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、米高扬、萨布罗夫和苏联外交部长葛罗米柯等。
  阿尔巴尼亚方面参加会谈的有：谢胡、霍查、努什、柯列加、马尔科和外交部长什图拉等。
    会谈开始，布尔加宁热烈地欢迎阿尔巴尼亚代表团，并且通过它向兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民致意。谢胡接着致了答词。
    会谈时，双方就对于进一步发展苏阿两国间的兄弟关系具有重要意义的政治问题和经济问题，友好地交换了意见。会谈参加者还就同目前国际局势的发展有关的问题交换了意见。在会谈中所涉及到的一切问题，双方都表现了充分的意见一致和充分的相互了解。会谈通过决定，委托两国代表团的专家拟定有关的建议，提交代表团会议讨论。
    会谈将继续举行。
