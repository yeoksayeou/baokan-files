Title: Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Zhu, Premier Zhou Receive Film Industry Personnel and Two Ethnic Minority Visiting Delegations
Author:
Date: 1957-04-15
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai received the representatives attending the founding conference of the China Film Workers Association, representatives of winners of outstanding films from 1949 to 1955, and representatives attending the National Film Distribution and Projection Advanced Workers Conference this afternoon.

Those present at the reception included Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and Bao Erhan.

Chairman Mao Zedong cordially shook hands and talked with many famous film actors, directors, and projection workers.

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the Yunnan Provincial Ethnic Minority Visiting Delegation and the Hunan Provincial Ethnic Minority Visiting Delegation this afternoon.

Those present at the reception included Deng Xiaoping, Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Bao Erhan and others. (Picture attached)
Right picture: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai receiving the representatives attending the founding conference of the China Film Workers Association, representatives of winners of outstanding films from 1949 to 1955, and representatives attending the National Film Distribution and Projection Advanced Workers Conference. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Hou Bo.

### 毛主席、朱副主席、周总理  接见电影界人士和两个民族参观团

1957-04-15
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席、朱副主席、周总理
    接见电影界人士和两个民族参观团
    新华社14日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理，今天下午接见了出席中国电影工作者联谊会成立大会的代表、1949年到1955年优秀影片得奖者代表和出席全国电影发行放映先进工作者代表大会的代表。
    接见的时候在座的，有国务院副总理邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、彭真、陈叔通，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧、包尔汉。
    毛泽东主席同许多著名的电影演员、导演和放映工作者亲切握手和谈了话。
    新华社14日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理，今天下午接见了云南省各民族参观团和湖南省各民族参观团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有邓小平、李济深、彭真、陈叔通、章伯钧、包尔汉等人。（附图片）
