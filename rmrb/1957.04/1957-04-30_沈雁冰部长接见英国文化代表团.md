Title: Minister Shen Yanbing Receives British Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th — Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing received the entire British Cultural Delegation in Beijing this afternoon.

Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also held a reception at noon today to welcome the British Cultural Delegation's visit to China.

The first group of the British Cultural Delegation arrived in Beijing on April 11th. The second group arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They are: Lord Huntingdon, Lady Huntingdon, Mr. Basil Gray, Mr. Grieve, and Miss de Zoete.

### 沈雁冰部长接见英国文化代表团

1957-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰部长接见英国文化代表团
    新华社29日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天下午在北京接见了英国文化代表团全体人员。
    中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南也在今天午间举行酒会，欢迎英国文化代表团来中国访问。
    英国文化代表团的第一批人员已经在4月11日来到北京。第二批人员是今天上午坐飞机到达北京的，他们是：杭廷顿勋爵，杭廷顿勋爵夫人，巴西耳·格雷先生，格里夫先生，德祖特小姐。
