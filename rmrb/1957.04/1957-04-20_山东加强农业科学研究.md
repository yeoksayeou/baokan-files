Shandong Strengthens Agricultural Scientific Research

(Our newspaper dispatch) The Shandong Provincial Agricultural Science Work Committee held its inaugural meeting in Jinan from April 2nd to 10th. The meeting reviewed the Shandong Province's 1957 agricultural scientific research projects, adopted a draft of the committee's provisional organizational rules, and elected the chairman, vice-chairmen, and members of the standing committee.

This committee is composed of forty-four agricultural technicians and professors from units affiliated with the Shandong Provincial Department of Agriculture, the Qingdao Municipal Bureau of Agriculture and Forestry, Shandong Agricultural College, and Shandong University. Its main tasks are to review and promote agricultural scientific research in the province, exchange experiences to improve academic standards, promote technological reforms, and develop agricultural production. According to work needs, the committee has twelve specialized groups, including grain crops, cash crops, fruit trees, vegetables, plant diseases, insects, animal husbandry, and veterinary medicine.

At the meeting, Zhang Zhusheng, Deputy Governor of Shandong Province, delivered a speech. He analyzed the many favorable conditions for carrying out agricultural scientific research and encouraged everyone.

After the meeting, the professional groups immediately assisted relevant personnel in carrying out the various agricultural scientific research projects for 1957 according to the established plan. (Sun Zhihao)

### 山东加强农业科学研究
孙志皓
1957-04-20
第7版()
专栏：

    山东加强农业科学研究
    本报讯　山东省农业科学工作委员会已于4月2日至10日在济南举行了成立大会。会议审查了山东省1957年度农业科学研究项目，并通过了委员会的试行组织简则草案，选出了正、副主任委员和常务委员会委员。
    这个委员会由山东省农业厅所属单位、青岛市农林处、山东农学院和山东大学等单位的农业技师和教授四十四人组成。它的任务主要是负责审议、推动该省的农业科学研究工作，交流经验，以提高学术水平，推进技术改革，发展农业生产。根据工作需要，委员会下设粮食作物、经济作物、果树、蔬菜、植物病害、昆虫、畜牧和兽医等十二个专业小组。
    会上，山东省副省长张竹生曾讲了话。他分析了开展农业科学研究工作的许多有利条件，并对大家作了勉励。
    会后，各专业小组即按既定计划帮助有关人员进行1957年度各项农业科学研究工作。　（孙志皓）
