Title: Lenin Monument Unveiled Near Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 21st (TASS dispatch): A grand mass rally was held today in Kashno village near Moscow to celebrate the unveiling of a monument to Lenin, the founder of the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet state. In November 1920, Lenin participated in the opening ceremony of a rural power station in this village.

The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, Khrushchev, participated in today's mass rally.

### 莫斯科近郊  列宁纪念碑揭幕

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科近郊
    列宁纪念碑揭幕
    新华社21日讯　塔斯社莫斯科21日电：莫斯科近郊卡什诺村今天举行隆重的群众大会，庆祝苏联共产党和苏维埃国家的奠基人列宁纪念碑揭幕。1920年11月，列宁在这个村庄参加了一个农村发电站的开幕式。
    苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫参加了今天的群众大会。
