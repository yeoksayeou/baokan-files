Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Postponed
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 9th—It is announced here that due to administrative reasons, the Sino-US ambassadorial talks originally scheduled for April 11th have been postponed to May 15th.

### 中美大使级会谈延期举行

1957-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈延期举行
    新华社日内瓦9日电　据此间宣布，由于行政性的原因，原定在4月11日举行的中美两国大使级会谈延期到5月15日举行。
