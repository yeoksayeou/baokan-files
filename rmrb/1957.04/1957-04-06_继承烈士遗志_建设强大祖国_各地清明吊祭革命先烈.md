Title: Inheriting the Will of the Martyrs, Building a Strong Motherland: Various Regions Commemorate Revolutionary Martyrs on Qingming Festival
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: Today is Qingming Festival. Throughout the country, activities to sweep and pay respects at the tombs of revolutionary martyrs have been held on the festival and in the days leading up to it.

The General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee sent representatives to pay respects at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery today. Representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life also attended. Those who came to pay respects laid many flowers and wreaths at the tombs of revolutionary martyrs such as Qu Qiubai, Ren Bishi, and Zhang Lan, and stood in silent tribute before their tombs. This year marks the 30th anniversary of the martyrdom of Li Dazhao, one of the founders of the Chinese Communist Party, and many people also went to pay respects at the tomb of Li Dazhao in the Wanan Public Cemetery in Yuquan Mountain. More than seventy Young Pioneers stood before Li Dazhao's tomb and expressed their determination to inherit the martyr's legacy.

In Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, more than 50,000 people today went to the Yuhuatai Martyrs' Cemetery, where pines and cypresses are green and plum blossoms are in full bloom, to pay respects and mourn. At 9:00 AM, Leng Yu and Ji Fang, vice governors of Jiangsu Province, Peng Chong, mayor of Nanjing, representatives of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee and Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, as well as representatives of various democratic parties, mass organizations, People's Liberation Army units stationed in Nanjing, and families of martyrs, totaling more than 800 people, held a memorial ceremony at Yuhuatai, paying their respects to the martyrs' portraits and relics.

Representatives from all sectors of society in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, as well as representatives from the Shenyang Garrison, held a tomb-sweeping ceremony at the tombs of martyrs Huang Jiguang, Yang Gensi, and others in the Beiling Martyrs' Cemetery in Shenyang at 9:00 AM today. Huang Oudong, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, Lieutenant General Du Ping, head of the People's Liberation Army garrison in Shenyang, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations presented wreaths to the martyrs' tombs. Those who participated in the tomb-sweeping ceremony circled the tombs. Some family members of the martyrs even traveled from Shanghai to sweep the tombs of their relatives. Troops stationed in Haicheng County also sent a team of officers and soldiers to sweep the martyrs' tombs.

Nearly 5,000 people from all walks of life in Hebei Province and youth representatives from across the province held a public memorial ceremony today at the Martyrs' Cemetery in Longhua City, the place where the famous blasting hero Dong Cunrui heroically sacrificed his life. Dong Quanzhong, the martyr's father, and Zhi Shunyi, a comrade-in-arms of the martyr, traveled from afar to participate in the public memorial, and they reported the heroic deeds of Martyr Dong Cunrui to the youth of Longhua. In the days leading up to the ceremony, people flocked to the martyrs' cemetery along the road leading to it, and the area around the martyr's tomb was filled with wreaths and elegiac couplets presented by people from all walks of life.

Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Tang Xing, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, and Kalbinsky, representative of Soviet experts in Wuhan, led more than 300 people from all walks of life and Soviet experts in a tomb-sweeping ceremony this morning at the tombs of the Soviet Air Force Volunteer Team martyrs who sacrificed their lives helping China resist Japanese invaders. Those who came to pay respects laid wreaths in front of the Martyrs' Monument and circled the tombs. After the ceremony, Kalbinsky expressed his gratitude for the Chinese friends' concern for the Soviet martyrs, as if they were their own relatives. Today, people from all districts and sectors of Wuhan also separately swept the tombs of martyrs from the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom era and later.

Nearly 1,000 people from Taiyuan and Pingyao in Shanxi Province today went to Yunzhou West Village in Wenshui County to pay respects at the tomb of the revolutionary heroine Liu Hulan. The person in charge of the Wenshui County Committee of the Communist Party of China and other people who came to pay respects presented sacrifices and wreaths at the martyr's tomb. Teachers and students from the Taiyuan Forestry School also specially planted seedlings of cypress and lilac trees brought from afar around the martyr's tomb. Many people listened to the introduction of Martyr Liu Hulan's life deeds at the martyr's tomb, and people also visited the Martyr's Deeds Exhibition Hall and the "Liu Hulan's Home."

Before the Qingming Festival, the Fujian Provincial Youth Delegation to visit the old revolutionary base areas went to the top of Huling Mountain on the outskirts of Longyan County to pay homage to the Minxi Revolutionary Martyrs Monument. The delegation presented wreaths to the Minxi Revolutionary Martyrs Monument and the nearby tomb of Guo Diren, one of the creators of the old Soviet area in western Fujian.

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, April 5th: This morning, more than 300 people, including leaders from Party, government, and military organs in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and Urumqi City, leaders from various people's organizations, and youths of all ethnic groups, went to the Yan'erwo Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in the southern suburbs of Urumqi City to sweep the tombs and mourn revolutionary martyrs such as Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the Chinese Communist Party, and outstanding Communist Party members Mao Zemin, Qiao Guozhen, Lin Jilu, and Wu Maolin. Saifudin, Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Liu He, Secretary of the Urumqi Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Asaid, Mayor of Urumqi, and others who came to mourn, planted many tree seedlings in the cemetery together with the people.

### 继承烈士遗志　建设强大祖国  各地清明吊祭革命先烈

1957-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    继承烈士遗志　建设强大祖国
    各地清明吊祭革命先烈
    据新华社5日讯　今天是清明节。全国各地在节日和节日以前都举行了祭扫革命先烈陵墓的活动。
    中共中央办公厅、全国人民代表大会常委会和北京市人民委员会，今天派代表前往八宝山革命烈士公墓祭扫。前往祭扫的还有各民主党派、人民团体的代表及各界人民。祭扫的人们在瞿秋白、任弼时、张澜等革命先烈墓前献了许多鲜花和花圈，并在他们的墓前默哀致敬。今年是中国共产党创始人之一李大钊烈士殉难三十周年纪念，前往玉泉山万安公墓祭扫李大钊烈士陵墓的人也很多。七十多个少先队员在李大钊烈士墓前表示要继承烈士的遗志。
    江苏省和南京市有五万多人今天到松柏吐绿、梅花盛开的雨花台烈士陵园祭扫凭吊。上午九时，江苏省副省长冷遹、季方，南京市市长彭冲，中共江苏省委和南京市委的代表，以及江苏省和南京市各民主党派、各群众团体、人民解放军驻南京部队代表、烈士家属等八百多人，到雨花台举行祭扫仪式，瞻仰烈士遗像、遗物。
    辽宁省、沈阳市各界人民和沈阳驻军代表今天上午九时在沈阳北陵烈士陵园内黄继光、杨根思诸烈士墓前，举行了扫墓仪式。中共辽宁省委员会第一书记黄欧东、辽宁省长杜者蘅、中共沈阳市委员会第一书记焦若愚、沈阳市长刘宝田、中国人民解放军沈阳驻军首长杜平中将和各民主党派、人民团体的负责人等，向烈士墓敬献花圈后，参加扫墓仪式的人绕墓一周。有些烈士家属还远从上海赶来给她们的亲人扫墓。驻在海城县的部队，也派来一队官兵赶来给烈士扫墓。
    河北省各界人民和全省青年代表近五千人，今天在著名爆破英雄董存瑞壮烈牺牲的地方——隆化城烈士墓前举行了公祭。烈士的父亲董全忠和烈士生前战友郅顺义，都远道赶来参加公祭，他们向隆化广大青年报告了董存瑞烈士的英雄事迹。在通往烈士陵园的路上，几天来前往凭吊的人们络绎不绝，烈士墓的周围布满了各界人民敬献的花圈和挽联。
    中共武汉市委员会第一书记宋侃夫、武汉市副市长唐星和在武汉的苏联专家的代表卡尔宾斯基率领各界人民和苏联专家共三百多人，今天上午在帮助我国抗击日寇牺牲的苏联空军志愿队烈士的墓前，举行了扫墓仪式。前往祭扫的人们把花圈献在烈士墓纪念碑前，并绕墓一周，仪式完毕后，卡尔宾斯基对中国朋友像关怀自己亲人一样怀念苏联先烈，表示感激。武汉市各区各界人民今天还分别到自太平天国以来的历代烈士墓前进行了祭扫。
    山西省太原、平遥等地今天有近一千人前往文水县云周西村祭扫了革命女英雄刘胡兰烈士的陵墓。中共文水县委员会的负责人和其他祭扫的人们，在烈士的墓前献了祭品和花圈。太原林业学校的师生们还特意把从远道带来的柏树、丁香等树苗栽植在烈士陵墓的周围。许多人在烈士墓前听了有关刘胡兰烈士生平事迹的介绍，人们还参观了烈士事迹陈列馆和“刘胡兰之家”。
    福建省各界青年赴革命老根据地访问团在清明节前夕到龙岩县城郊虎岭山顶谒闽西革命烈士纪念碑。访问团向闽西革命烈士纪念碑和近傍的闽西老苏区创造人之一郭滴人烈士墓分别献了花圈。
    据新华社乌鲁木齐5日电　今天上午，新疆维吾尔自治区和乌鲁木齐市的党政军机关、各人民团体负责人和各民族青年三百多人，前往乌鲁木齐市南郊燕儿窝革命烈士陵园扫墓，吊念中国共产党创始人之一陈潭秋和中共优秀党员毛泽民、乔国桢、林基路、吴茂林等革命烈士。中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会书记、新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎，中共乌鲁木齐市委员会书记刘鹤，乌鲁木齐市市长阿赛德等，和同来吊念的人们一起，在陵园栽植了许多树苗。
