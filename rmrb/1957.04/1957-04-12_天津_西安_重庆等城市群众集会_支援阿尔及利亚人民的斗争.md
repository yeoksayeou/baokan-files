Title: Mass Rallies in Tianjin, Xi'an, Chongqing and Other Cities Support the Struggle of the Algerian People
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 11th. Activities to support the Algerian people's struggle for national independence have been successively launched among the workers and masses in many cities in our country.

    In Tianjin, one of China's largest industrial cities, more than 1,300 workers held a rally today to support the Algerian people. Pan Changyou, vice chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, said at the meeting: More than 500,000 workers in Tianjin are determined to stand with the Algerian people until they achieve final victory. Shan Shu, a worker and advanced producer from the jointly owned Tianjin No. 1 Steel Rolling Plant, represented the plant's more than 1,000 steel rolling workers in paying tribute to the fighting Algerian worker brothers. Xiang Yulan, a female worker from the Tianjin No. 4 Cotton Textile Factory, represented young female workers and mothers in expressing particular sympathy and support for the Algerian sisters. The workers at the meeting repeatedly shouted slogans such as "Oppose colonial rule!" and "Resolutely support the struggle of the Algerian people!" Finally, they unanimously passed a resolution to support the Algerian people.
    More than 1,000 workers from the construction, machinery, railway, textile, and medical trade union systems in Xi'an also held a rally today. Representatives from various trade unions unanimously condemned the brutal crimes of French imperialism in Algeria at the meeting. Lü Zanxiang, a representative of the education sector in Xi'an, said: The French imperialists have been driven out by the people of Vietnam and Egypt, and they will certainly be driven out by the people of Algeria. An exhibition of pictures about the heroic struggle of the Algerian people and the aggressive crimes of the French colonialists was also displayed in front of the meeting venue.
    Zhang Xianyi, chairman of the Chongqing Federation of Trade Unions, represented 400,000 workers in the city at a support rally attended by more than 400 workers and trade union cadres today, expressing respect for the heroic struggle of the Algerian people. Yang Ruikun, a deputy to the National People's Congress and a model worker in Chongqing, and Yang Jiahua, a national model textile worker and a female worker from the Chongqing Shashi Yarn Factory, expressed their firm support for the Algerian people's struggle at the meeting.
    More than 300 worker representatives from 15 industrial systems including the No. 1 Machinery, Railway, Construction, and Light Industry systems in Harbin held a rally on the afternoon of the 6th to support the Algerian people. Wang Jun, vice chairman of the Harbin Federation of Trade Unions, called on grassroots trade union organizations throughout the city to widely carry out publicity activities to support the Algerian people among the workers. Recently, factories and mines in Beijing have also generally used forms such as broadcasting and blackboards to introduce the situation of the Algerian people's struggle to the workers. The Beijing Laboring People's Cultural Palace, as well as factories and mines such as the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant and the Mentougou Coal Mine, are also preparing to hold picture exhibitions introducing the Algerian people's struggle.

### 天津　西安　重庆等城市群众集会  支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    天津　西安　重庆等城市群众集会
    支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争
    新华社11日讯　支援阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立斗争的活动，已经在我国许多城市的职工群众中陆续展开。
    中国最大工业城市之一的天津市，今天有一千三百多名工人为支援阿尔及利亚人民举行了集会。天津市工会联合会副主席潘长有在会上说：天津市的五十多万名职工决心和阿尔及利亚人民站在一起，直到他们取得最后胜利。公私合营轧钢一厂工人、先进生产者单书代表这个厂的一千多名轧钢工人向斗争中的阿尔及利亚工人兄弟们致敬。天津第四棉纺织厂女工相玉兰代表年青女工和母亲们特别表示对阿尔及利亚的姊妹们的同情和支持。工人们在会上不断高呼“反对殖民统治！”、“坚决支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争！”等口号，最后还一致通过了一项支援阿尔及利亚人民的决议。
    西安市建筑、机械、铁路、纺织、医务等工会系统的一千多名职工也在今天举行了集会。各个工会的代表们在会上都一致谴责法国帝国主义在阿尔及利亚的残暴罪行。西安市教育界职工代表吕赞襄说：法国帝国主义者已经被越南、埃及人民赶出去了，他们也一定会被阿尔及利亚人民赶出去。在大会会场前面还展出了关于阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争和法国殖民者侵略罪行的图片。
    重庆市工会联合会主席张显仪今天在有四百多名职工和工会干部参加的支援集会上代表全市四十万职工对阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争表示敬意。全国人民代表大会代表、重庆市劳动模范杨如坤、全国纺织劳动模范、重庆沙市纱厂女工杨嘉华等都在会上表示坚决支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争。
    哈尔滨市第一机械、铁路、建筑、轻工业等十五个产业系统的三百多名职工代表在6日下午为支援阿尔及利亚人民举行了集会。哈尔滨市工会联合会副主席王军在会上号召全市基层工会组织在职工群众中广泛开展支援阿尔及利亚人民的宣传活动。北京各厂矿最近也普遍通过广播、黑板报等形式，向工人们介绍了阿尔及利亚人民斗争的情况，北京市劳动人民文化宫以及石景山钢铁厂、门头沟煤矿等厂矿还准备举行介绍阿尔及利亚人民斗争情况的图片展览。
