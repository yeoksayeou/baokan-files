Title: West German Writer Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — West German writer Paul Disterbart and his son arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 西德一作家到京

1957-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    西德一作家到京
    新华社6日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的西德作家保罗·迪斯特尔巴特和他的儿子在今天到达北京。
