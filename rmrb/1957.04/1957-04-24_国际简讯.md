　　International Briefs

　　Czechoslovakia Established 400 Agricultural Cooperatives This Year
　　Prague dispatch: Agricultural cooperatives in Czechoslovakia are developing rapidly. Since the beginning of this year, small and medium farmers have established nearly four hundred unified agricultural cooperatives, of which about one hundred are in the Slovak region.

　　Polish and French Student Groups Signed Cooperation Agreement
　　According to the Polish Press Agency, after a two-week visit to Poland, a delegation from the French National Student Union signed an agreement with Polish student groups for cooperation between the two countries' student groups.

### 国际简讯

1957-04-24
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    捷今年来建立了四百个农业社
    布拉格讯　捷克斯洛伐克的农业合作社正在迅速发展。从今年年初以来，中小农民又建立了差不多四百个统一农业合作社，其中约有一百个是在斯洛伐克地区。
    波法两国学生团体订合作协定
    据波兰通讯社报道，法国全国学生联合会代表团最近在波兰进行两周的访问后，同波兰的学生团体签订了两国学生团体进行合作的协定。
