Title: Sunken Revetment
Author: Qu Chaohong
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 3

    Sunken Revetment
    A large-scale water conservancy project is underway on the opposite bank of Nanjing City - constructing a five-li long revetment slope, building a flood control embankment, and constructing thirteen sluice gates. After the completion of this project, the harm of floods to factories and residents in this area during the big flood season can be eliminated. Photo by Qu Chaohong (Nanjing Daily)

### 沉排护岸（图片）
曲超宏
1957-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

    沉排护岸
    南京市对岸正在进行一项大型的水利工程——修筑五华里长的护岸坡、筑一道防洪堤、建十三座闸门。这项工程完成后，可消除大汛期间洪水对这一带工厂、居民的危害。　曲超宏摄（南京日报社稿）
