Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Reception to Bid Farewell to Indian Dancers Kamala Sisters Give Final Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception in Beijing this evening to bid farewell to the famous Indian dancers, the Kamala Lakshman sisters, who are about to leave Beijing.

Those attending the reception included Vice Premiers He Long and Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Peng Zhen, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and many prominent figures from the capital's literary and art circles, such as Mei Lanfang and Cheng Yanqiu.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife were invited to the reception.

Before the reception this evening, Zhou Enlai, He Long, Nie Rongzhen, Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and others watched the Lakshman sisters' final performance in Beijing. After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai went on stage to present flowers to the Indian artists. Premier Zhou Enlai and others also shook hands with the Indian artists and took a group photo.

The Lakshman sisters gave a total of six performances in Beijing, with an audience of over 9,000 people. They will leave Beijing on the 6th for a visit and performance in Guangzhou. Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, hosted a banquet at noon today to bid them farewell. (Photo attached)

Premier Zhou raised his glass with Miss Kamala at the reception, wishing her greater achievements in art.

### 周总理设酒会欢送印度舞蹈家  卡玛拉姊妹在京举行最后一场演出

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设酒会欢送印度舞蹈家
    卡玛拉姊妹在京举行最后一场演出
    据新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上在北京举行酒会，欢送即将离开北京的印度著名舞蹈家卡玛拉·拉克希曼姊妹。
    出席酒会的有副总理贺龙、聂荣臻，副委员长李济深、彭真，国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长郑振铎、夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及首都文艺界著名人士梅兰芳、程砚秋等多人。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人应邀出席了酒会。
    周恩来、贺龙、聂荣臻、李济深、彭真等今晚在酒会举行以前，观看了拉克希曼姊妹在北京的最后一场演出。在演出结束以后，周恩来总理登台向印度艺术家献了花。周恩来总理等还同印度艺术家们握手，并合影留念。
    拉克希曼姊妹在北京共演出了六场，观众有九千多人。她们将在6日离开北京去广州访问演出。中印友好协会会长丁西林曾在今天中午设宴欢送她们。（附图片）
    周总理在酒会上同卡玛拉小姐举杯，预祝她在艺术上获得更大的成就。
          本报记者　高粮摄
