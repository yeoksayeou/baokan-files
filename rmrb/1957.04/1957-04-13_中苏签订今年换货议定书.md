Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Protocol
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th -- After negotiations, the Chinese and Soviet trade delegations signed the China-Soviet Union 1957 Goods Exchange Protocol in Moscow on the 11th.
    The protocol stipulates that in 1957, the Soviet Union will supply China with machine tools, forging and pressing equipment, oil drilling equipment, mining equipment, geological survey instruments, electrical equipment, excavators, various instruments, petroleum, petroleum products, various steels, non-ferrous metals, various chemical products, medical equipment, medicines, and other goods.
    In 1957, China will supply the Soviet Union with tungsten, molybdenum, tin, mercury, non-ferrous metal concentrates, cement, sulfur, chemical products, tung oil, wool, raw silk, woolen fabrics, silk fabrics, knitwear, bristles, soybeans, tea, flue-cured tobacco, citrus fruits, handicrafts, and other goods.
    The protocol was signed by Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Kabanov and Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren.

### 中苏签订今年换货议定书

1957-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏签订今年换货议定书
    新华社莫斯科12日电　中国和苏联贸易代表团经过会谈后，11日在莫斯科签订了中苏1957年货物交换议定书。
    议定书规定：苏联方面1957年将向中国供应工作母机、锻压设备、石油钻探设备、矿山设备、地质勘察仪器、电气设备、挖土机、各种仪器、石油、石油产品、各种钢材、有色金属、各种化学产品、医疗器械、药品等货物。
    中国方面1957年将向苏联供应钨、钼、锡、水银、有色金属精矿、水泥、硫黄、化学产品、桐油、羊毛、生丝、毛织品、丝织品、针织品、猪鬃、大豆、茶叶、烤烟、柑桔、手工艺品等货物。
    在议定书上签字的苏联方面是对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫；中国方面是对外贸易部副部长李哲人。
