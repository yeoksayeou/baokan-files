Title: New World Records in a Batch of Sports

Author:

Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: A batch of new world records in sports:

    Middleweight Press
    Soviet athlete Bogdanovsky, the middleweight weightlifting champion of the 16th Olympic Games, broke his own world record in the press on March 26th in Leningrad. He pressed 135 kilograms with both hands, one kilogram more than the world record he set in Moscow last August.
    Men's 400m Individual Medley
    Soviet athlete Struyanov set a new world record in the men's 400m individual medley (long course) of 5 minutes 8.3 seconds on March 18th in Moscow. The current official world record is 5 minutes 15.4 seconds, set by Struyanov himself in 1954, which has been broken twice recently. The first time was on March 10th by Soviet athlete Androsov, with a time of 5 minutes 9.4 seconds. Eight days later, Struyanov surpassed him again. Struyanov's times for the four strokes are: 100m butterfly 1 minute 6.8 seconds, 100m breaststroke 1 minute 30.2 seconds, 100m freestyle 1 minute 11.8 seconds, and 100m backstroke 1 minute 19.5 seconds.
    Women's 200m Butterfly
    Dutch athlete Lagerberg set a world record in the women's 200m butterfly (50-meter pool) on the evening of March 23rd in West Germany, with a time of 2 minutes 38.8 seconds. This event does not yet have an official world record. The American She Mann once achieved a time of 2 minutes 44.4 seconds (set in a 55-yard pool), but it has not yet been recognized.

### 一批体育运动的世界新成绩

1957-04-07
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    一批体育运动的世界新成绩
    新华社6日讯　一批体育运动的世界新成绩：
    中量级推举
    第十六届奥林匹克运动会中量级举重冠军、苏联运动员鲍格达诺夫斯基3月26日在列宁格勒打破了他自己所保持的推举世界纪录。他双手推举了一三五公斤，比他自己去年8月在莫斯科创造的世界纪录多了一公斤。
    男子四○○公尺个人混合泳
    苏联运动员斯特鲁雅诺夫3月18日在莫斯科创造了男子四○○公尺混合泳长池内的世界新成绩——五分八点三秒。目前的正式世界纪录是斯特鲁雅诺夫本人在1954年所创造的五分一五点四秒，这一纪录最近被刷新了两次。第一次是在今年3月10日苏联运动员安德罗索夫打破的，成绩是五分九点四秒。八天之后斯特鲁雅诺夫又超过了他。斯特鲁雅诺夫的四种姿势的成绩是：一○○公尺蝶泳一分六点八秒，一○○公尺蛙泳一分三○点二秒，一○○公尺自由泳一分一一点八秒，一○○公尺仰泳一分一九点五秒。
    女子二○○公尺蝶泳
    荷兰女运动员拉格贝格3月23日晚上在西德创造了女子二○○公尺蝶泳五十公尺池内的世界成绩，她的成绩是二分三八点八秒。这个项目还没有正式世界纪录，美国的谢·曼恩曾经达到过二分四四点四秒（在五十五码池内创造的）的成绩，可是还没有得到承认。
