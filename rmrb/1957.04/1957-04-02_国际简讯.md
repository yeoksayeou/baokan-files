International Briefs

Chinese Cultural Delegation Leaves Hanoi for Home
The Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Xia Yan, left Hanoi on April 1st to return home. Vietnamese Deputy Minister of Culture Cu Huy Can, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo, and others saw them off at the station.

Mongolia and Poland Sign Barter Agreement
Mongolia and Poland signed a barter and payment agreement for 1957 in Ulaanbaatar on March 30.

According to the agreement, Mongolia will supply Poland with wool, livestock, leather, horsehair, hides, and other animal products. Poland will supply Mongolia with woolen fabrics, cotton fabrics, machinery, medical equipment, and other goods.

Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Arrives in Syria
Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović arrived in Damascus, the capital of Syria, on March 31 for a three-day state visit. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-04-02
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    我文化代表团离河内返国
    中国文化代表团在团长夏衍率领下1日离开河内回国。越南文化部副部长瞿辉近和中国驻越南大使罗贵波等到车站送行。
    蒙波签订换货协定
    蒙古和波兰两国1957年换货和支付协定3月30日在乌兰巴托签字。
    根据协定，蒙古将供给波兰羊毛、牲畜、皮革、马鬃、兽皮和其他畜产品。波兰将供给蒙古毛织品、棉织品、机器设备、医药器材和其他货物。
    南联邦执委会副主席到叙利亚
    南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇在3月31日到达叙利亚首都大马士革作为时三天的国事访问。　（据新华社讯）
