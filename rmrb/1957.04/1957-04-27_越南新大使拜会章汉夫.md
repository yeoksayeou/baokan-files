Title: New Vietnamese Ambassador Calls on Zhang Hanfu
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Nguyen Khang, called on Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 越南新大使拜会章汉夫

1957-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    越南新大使拜会章汉夫
    新华社26日讯　越南民主共和国新任驻华特命全权大使阮康今天下午拜会我外交部副部长章汉夫，商谈递交国书事宜。拜会时在座的有外交部亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司副司长张世杰。
