Title: Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Personnel Huang Gangcun Uprising; Ministry of National Defense Awards Rank of Major
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th—The General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force held a rank awarding ceremony for Chiang Kai-shek's uprising personnel Huang Gangcun this afternoon. Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Commander of the Air Force, Lieutenant General, on behalf of Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense, awarded Huang Gangcun the rank of Major and appointed him Deputy Head of the Training Regiment of a certain Air Force School.

After the rank awarding ceremony, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang extended warm congratulations to Huang Gangcun and had a cordial conversation with him. Wang Bingzhang said, we welcome Chiang Kai-shek's officers and soldiers to return to the mainland to visit relatives and friends and participate in the motherland's socialist construction. The government will reward and employ those returning from Taiwan based on their merits and abilities. In the conversation, Huang Gangcun deeply thanked the government and the leading organs of the People's Liberation Army for the care and attention they had given him since his return from the uprising, and expressed that he would work hard to learn the fine traditions of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and resolutely serve the interests of the people of the whole country.

Huang Gangcun was originally a Major officer in the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Military Academy in Taiwan. On August 15 last year, he flew back to the mainland from Gangshan Airport in Taiwan in an AT-6 advanced trainer aircraft.

### 蒋军空军人员黄纲存起义  国防部授予少校军衔

1957-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋军空军人员黄纲存起义
    国防部授予少校军衔
    新华社10日讯　中国人民解放军空军总部今天下午为蒋军起义人员黄纲存举行授衔仪式。空军副司令员王秉璋中将代表国防部部长彭德怀元帅，授予黄纲存以少校军衔，并任命他为某空军学校训练团副团长。
    授衔仪式以后，王秉璋中将向黄纲存表示热烈祝贺，并和他亲切交谈。王秉璋说，我们欢迎蒋军官兵回大陆省亲会友和参加祖国社会主义建设事业。对台湾归来人员，政府将予以论功行赏并量材录用。黄纲存在谈话中，深切地感谢政府和解放军领导机关在他起义归来以后所给予的关怀和照顾，并表示将努力学习中国人民解放军的优良传统，坚决为全国人民的利益服务。
    黄纲存原来是台湾蒋军空军军官学校的少校军官，去年8月15日由台湾冈山机场架驶AT——6型高级教练机起义飞回大陆。
