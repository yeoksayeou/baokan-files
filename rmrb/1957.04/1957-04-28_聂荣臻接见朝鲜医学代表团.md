Title: Nie Rongzhen Receives Korean Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th - Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen received this afternoon in Beijing the entire delegation of the Korean Democratic People's Republic medical delegation headed by Vice Minister of Health of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Liu Jichun, as well as Minister of Health of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Li Bingnan, President of Pyongyang Medical University, Kim Bae-jun, and Secretary-General of the Academic Committee of the Ministry of Health, Kim Dong-bin, who were returning from Romania and passing through Beijing.

### 聂荣臻接见朝鲜医学代表团

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻接见朝鲜医学代表团
    据新华社27日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今日下午在北京接见以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国保健省副相柳基春为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国医学代表团全体人员以及从罗马尼亚归国途经北京的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国保健相李炳南，平壤医科大学校长金培浚，保健省学术委员会秘书长金东斌。
