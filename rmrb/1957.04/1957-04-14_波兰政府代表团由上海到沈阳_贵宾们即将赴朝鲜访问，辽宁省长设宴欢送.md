Title: Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Shenyang from Shanghai; Guests to Visit North Korea, Liaoning Governor Hosts Banquet in Farewell

Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 13th: The Polish Government Delegation, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, arrived in Shenyang by plane from Shanghai at 4:10 PM today. Over three thousand people braved the rain and snow at the airport to welcome them.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Sung-chul of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who came to Shenyang to welcome the Polish Government Delegation on their visit to North Korea, also welcomed the Polish guests at the airport.

After a short rest, Chairman Cyrankiewicz and others visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 13th: Liaoning Province Governor Du Zheheng held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Polish Government Delegation led by Chairman Cyrankiewicz, who are about to leave China.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and our country's ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan, who accompanied the delegation to Shenyang, as well as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Sung-chul of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who came to Shenyang to welcome the delegation on their visit to North Korea, also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Du Zheheng raised his glass to wish Chairman Cyrankiewicz and the Polish guests a safe journey, and to wish them success in their upcoming visits to other countries. Chairman Cyrankiewicz raised his glass to thank the Chinese people for their warm hospitality and sincere friendship towards the Polish people. He wished the Chinese people success in their industrialization endeavors.

### 波兰政府代表团由上海到沈阳  贵宾们即将赴朝鲜访问，辽宁省长设宴欢送

1957-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团由上海到沈阳
    贵宾们即将赴朝鲜访问，辽宁省长设宴欢送
    据新华社沈阳13日电　以波兰部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹为首的波兰政府代表团今天下午四时十分由上海乘飞机到达沈阳。三千多人冒着雨雪在机场欢迎。
    到沈阳来迎接波兰政府代表团去朝鲜访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相朴成哲也到机场欢迎波兰贵宾。
    西伦凯维兹主席等稍事休息后，参观了沈阳第一机床厂。
    新华社沈阳13日电　辽宁省省长杜者蘅今晚举行宴会，欢送即将离开中国的以西伦凯维兹主席为首的波兰政府代表团。
    陪同代表团来沈阳的外交部副部长章汉夫、我国驻波兰大使王炳南，以及来沈阳迎接代表团去朝鲜访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相朴成哲也参加了宴会。
    杜者蘅在宴会上举杯祝西伦凯维兹主席和波兰贵宾们一路平安，并祝他们将要到其他国家进行的访问获得成功。西伦凯维兹主席举杯感谢中国人民对他们的热情招待和对波兰人民的诚挚友谊。他祝中国人民在工业化的事业中获得成功。
