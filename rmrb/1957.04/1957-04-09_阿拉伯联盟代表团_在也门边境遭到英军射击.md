Title: Arab League Delegation Shot at by British Forces on Yemeni Border
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 8th. The Middle East News Agency reported that the Arab League delegation investigating the British aggression against Yemen was shot at by British soldiers on the southern Yemeni border on April 6th.

The delegation, led by Arab League Deputy Secretary-General Shukairy, was traveling from Taiz to Bayda on this day when British forces suddenly launched an attack on a frontline village in the city and the Yemeni army stationed there. When delegation members went to the scene to investigate the attack, they came under fire, but no one was injured.

After arriving in Yemen, the delegation visited the Ibb region in southern Yemen on April 5th and listened to the testimony of tribal leaders in the region. The tribal leaders requested that the delegation members advise their respective governments to bring their suffering at the hands of the British to the attention of the United Nations.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 8th. Yemeni Envoy to Egypt, Taleb, condemned Britain for launching armed attacks on some Yemeni villages during the Muslim month of Ramadan. He said: This is an act that offends the religious feelings of Muslims.

### 阿拉伯联盟代表团  在也门边境遭到英军射击

1957-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟代表团
    在也门边境遭到英军射击
    新华社开罗8日电　中东通讯社报道，调查英国侵略也门问题的阿拉伯联盟代表团4月6日在也门南部边境遭到英国士兵的射击。
    由阿拉伯联盟副秘书长舒凯里率领的代表团在这一天从泰兹到达贝达后，英国军队突然向这个城市的一个前线村庄和那里的也门军队发动攻击。代表团人员在到现场去调查这次攻击时遭到射击，但是没有人受伤。
    代表团到达也门后，在4月5日访问了也门南部的伊卜地区，听取了这个地区的部族领袖的证词。部族领袖们要求代表团团员建议本国政府把他们在英国人手中所遭受的苦难提请联合国注意。
    据新华社开罗8日电　也门驻埃及公使塔列布谴责英国在穆斯林的“莱玛丹月”期间对也门的一些村庄发动武装进攻。他说：这是触犯穆斯林的宗教感情的行为。
