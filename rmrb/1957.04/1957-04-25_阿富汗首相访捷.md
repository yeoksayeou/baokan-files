Title: Afghan Prime Minister Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 24th: Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan and his entourage arrived in Prague by plane on the 23rd for a state visit at the invitation of the Czechoslovak government.

Czechoslovak Prime Minister Široký and National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger, among others, welcomed the guests at the airport.

That evening, the Afghan Prime Minister was received by Czechoslovak President Zápotocký and Prime Minister Široký.

In the past three years, trade relations between Czechoslovakia and Afghanistan have expanded significantly. Currently, Czechoslovakia is the socialist country with the largest trade volume with Afghanistan, after the Soviet Union.

### 阿富汗首相访捷

1957-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相访捷
    新华社布拉格24日电　阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗和随行人员应捷克斯洛伐克政府的邀请，在23日乘飞机抵达布拉格进行国事访问。
    捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和国民议会主席费林格等都到机场迎接客人。
    当天晚上，阿富汗首相受到了捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基和西罗基总理的接见。
    在过去三年中，捷阿两国之间的贸易关系有了很大的扩展。目前，捷克斯洛伐克是社会主义国家中除了苏联以外同阿富汗贸易量最多的国家。
