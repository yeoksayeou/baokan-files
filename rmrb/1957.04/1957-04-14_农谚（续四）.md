Agricultural Proverbs (Continued 4)
Su Keqin Collected

Density of Seedlings: "Ten ears are better than one, many dropped, nine stalks of straw" (referring to the benefits of dense planting). "To harvest more, plant them one next to the other." "To love the seedlings, hold a gourd" (referring to the disadvantage of leaving seedlings too dense; holding a gourd means being a beggar). "Small clumps densely planted" (referring to the method of leaving millet seedlings). "Three incense sticks, incense burner legs, grains scattered like sheep droppings" (referring to the number of millet seedlings). "One-foot sorghum, half-foot millet" (referring to the spacing of sorghum and millet seedlings). "To get a good harvest of cotton and sorghum, three seedlings per step." "A reclining ox for sesame, a lying ox for millet, two-foot sorghum yields 150 catties." "A chicken lying in the soybean field still complains the soybeans are sparse." "A dog lying in the cornfield will yield several more dou per mu." "To pick more cotton, plant them one next to the other." "Cotton plants turn around, each plant yields one jin." "Cotton plants stretch out a fist, each plant yields a basket."

### 农谚（续四）
苏克勤
1957-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

    农谚（续四）
    苏克勤  搜集
    留苗的密度：“十穗顶一穗，多落九根秸”（指密植的好处）“要打多，一棵挨一棵”。“要爱苗，抱了瓢”（指留苗过密的坏处，抱了瓢是当乞丐的意思）。“小撮密留”（指谷子留苗的方法）。“三柱香儿香炉腿儿，粒粒拉拉羊拉屎儿”（指谷子留苗的棵数）。“一尺高粱半尺谷”（指高粱、谷留苗的距离）。“棉花、高粱要收好，一步三棵苗”。“立牛芝麻卧牛黍，二尺高粱打石五”。“小豆地里卧下鸡，还嫌小豆稀”。“棒子（即玉米）地里卧下狗，一亩多打好几斗”。“棉花要摘多，一棵挨一棵”。“棉花转开身，一棵摘一斤”。“棉花伸开拳，一棵摘一篮”。
