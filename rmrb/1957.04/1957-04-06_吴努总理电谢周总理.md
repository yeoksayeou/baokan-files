Title: Premier U Nu Cables Thanks to Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Premier U Nu of the Union of Burma sent the following cable of thanks to Premier Zhou Enlai:
    To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, Beijing.
    Our party has returned to Burma, and I hereby send a cable to express our heartfelt thanks to you and other friends for the most gracious hospitality extended to us. We felt very happy during our stay in Kunming. Now we are looking forward to welcoming our Chinese friends in the coming days.
                        U Nu, Premier of the Union of Burma
                      　April 4, 1957, Rangoon

### 吴努总理电谢周总理

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理电谢周总理
    新华社5日讯　缅甸联邦吴努总理给周恩来总理的感谢电如下：北京中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生阁下
    我们一行已经回到缅甸，谨去电对你和其他朋友们所给予我们的最殷勤的款待表示衷心的感谢。我们在昆明期间感到十分愉快。现在我们正盼望着日内就能欢迎中国朋友们的光临。
                        缅甸联邦总理吴努
                      　1957年4月4日于仰光
