Title: Disarmament Subcommittee Discusses Conventional Armaments Reduction
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 5th: The United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee continued its secret meeting on the 4th. According to Western news agencies, the meeting that day began to discuss the second item on the agenda - the reduction of conventional armaments. Representatives from the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, France, and Canada all spoke at the meeting.

### 裁军小组委员会  讨论削减常规军备问题

1957-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    裁军小组委员会
    讨论削减常规军备问题
    据新华社5日讯　伦敦消息：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会4日继续举行秘密会议。据西方通讯社透露，这一天会议开始讨论议程上的第二项——削减常规军备的问题。苏联、美、英、法和加拿大代表都在会上发了言。
