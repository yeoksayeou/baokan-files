Title: Beijing People From All Walks of Life Will Hold a Meeting Today to Welcome the Polish Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The people of all circles in Beijing will hold a meeting on the afternoon of the 9th to welcome the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Poland headed by Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland.
    The first program of the Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting the event live at 15:50 on the 9th.

### 北京各界今天将举行大会欢迎波兰政府代表团

1957-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京各界今天将举行大会欢迎波兰政府代表团
    新华社8日讯　北京市各界人民将在9日下午举行大会，欢迎以波兰部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团。
    中央人民广播电台第一种节目将在9日十五时五十分开始转播这次大会的实况。
