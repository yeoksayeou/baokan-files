Title: Xi Zhongxun Hosts Journalists from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 5th. Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun held a reception this evening to host delegates from various countries who are in Beijing to attend the Executive Committee meeting of the International Organization of Journalists.

	At the reception, Xi Zhongxun, on behalf of the State Council and Premier Zhou Enlai, welcomed journalists from various countries. He raised his glass to congratulate the success and smooth progress of the Executive Committee meeting of the International Organization of Journalists, and wished the journalists a pleasant trip in China and greater success in their future work.

	Among those invited to attend tonight were IJO President Ermann, Vice Presidents Mrs. Rydberg and Kraminov, Secretary-General Knobloch, Deputy Secretary-General Pronin, and the delegates from the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Hungary, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, Vietnam, and Mexico who are participating in the Beijing meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists, as well as guests from Pakistan, and journalists from various countries stationed in China.

	Also present were Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, heads of various newspapers, periodicals, news agencies, and radio stations in Beijing, as well as many people from the press.

### 习仲勋招待各国新闻工作者

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    习仲勋招待各国新闻工作者
    新华社5日讯　国务院秘书长习仲勋今晚举行酒会，招待来北京参加国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会会议的各国代表。
    习仲勋在酒会上代表国务院和周恩来总理欢迎各国新闻工作者，他举杯祝贺国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会会议的成功和工作的顺利，祝贺各国新闻工作者在中国旅行愉快和在今后工作中获得更大的成功。
    今晚应邀出席的，有国际新闻工作者协会主席埃尔曼，副主席里德堡夫人、克拉米诺夫，秘书长克诺布洛赫，副秘书长普罗宁和德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、匈牙利、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、越南、墨西哥等国参加国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会北京会议的代表和巴基斯坦的来宾，以及各国驻中国的记者。
    出席作陪的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓，北京各报刊和通讯社、广播电台的负责人以及新闻界人士等多人。
