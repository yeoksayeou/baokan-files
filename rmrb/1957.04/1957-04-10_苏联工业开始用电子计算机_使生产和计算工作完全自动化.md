Title: The Soviet Union Begins to Use Electronic Computers to Fully Automate Production and Calculation Work
Author:
Date: 1957-04-10
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 9th: The Soviet Union has already established calculation centers equipped with various types of electronic computers in some areas, specifically to serve production and scientific work. Electronic computers are also gradually being used in industrial production departments to fully automate production and calculation work.
　　The industrial enterprises under the Ministry of Ferrous Metallurgy have established 188 machine calculation bureaus and machine calculation stations, equipped with highly efficient electronic computing machines. Using these machines, many industrial enterprises have completely mechanized the calculation work for finished products, raw materials, wages, etc. The Moscow "Electrosteel" factory uses electronic computing devices to calculate and regulate the heat of electric furnaces. The computing device can very accurately calculate the power required in the electric furnace. Due to the use of this computing device, the factory has greatly shortened the smelting time and improved product quality.
　　The Soviet Union has successively manufactured various types of electronic computing machines. The newly designed electronic computer "Ural" will be mass-produced. This machine, specially designed for engineering and industrial production calculations, can perform one hundred operations per second. Engineers from the Soviet Ministry of Instrument Making and Automation Tools have designed the smallest electronic computer in the Soviet Union to date. This machine is only 0.3 square meters in size and weighs 45 kilograms. It can be used for automatic management and calculation in production. (Attached picture)

### 苏联工业开始用电子计算机  使生产和计算工作完全自动化

1957-04-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联工业开始用电子计算机
    使生产和计算工作完全自动化
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　苏联已经在一些地方建立了配备有各种型式的电子计算机的计算中心，专门为生产和科学工作服务。在工业生产部门也开始逐步使用电子计算机，使生产和计算工作完全自动化。
    钢铁工业部所属各工业企业已经建立了一百八十八个机器计算局和机器计算站，配备着有高度效率的电子计算机器。利用这些机器，许多工业企业已经使工厂对成品、原料、工资等的计算工作完全机械化。莫斯科“电钢”工厂用电子计算装置来计算和调节电炉的热量。计算装置能够非常精确地计算出电炉中需要电力的情况。由于运用这种计算装置，这个工厂大大缩短了冶炼时间，并且提高了产品质量。
    苏联已陆续制造出各种型式的电子计算机器，新设计的电子计算机“乌拉尔牌”将大量生产。这种专门供工程和工业生产上计算的机器每秒钟可作一百次运算。苏联仪器制造和自动化工具部的工程师已经设计出一种在苏联迄今为止最小的电子计算机，这种机器仅有零点三平方米，重四十五公斤，这种机器可供生产上自动管理和计算应用。（附图片）
