Title: East German Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 4

    East German Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 18th) A delegation of fifteen from the German Democratic Republic's weightlifting team arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    The team is led by Ernst Lassahn. Among the team's ten athletes (covering seven weight classes), five are Master Athletes of the German Democratic Republic, and five are first-class athletes.

### 民主德国举重队到北京

1957-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国举重队到北京
    据新华社18日讯　德意志民主共和国举重队一行十五人，今天下午坐飞机到达北京。
    举重队由恩斯特·拉萨恩任领队。队里的十名运动员（包括七个举重等级），五名是德意志民主共和国的运动健将，五名是一级运动员。
