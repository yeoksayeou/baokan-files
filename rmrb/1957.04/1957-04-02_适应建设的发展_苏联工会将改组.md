Title: Adapting to the Development of Construction: Soviet Trade Unions to be Reorganized
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: Grishin, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, said at a meeting of All-Union trade union activists in Moscow on March 30th that due to changes in the management structure of industry and construction, it is necessary for Soviet trade unions to carry out reorganization work.

Grishin said that the upcoming plenary session of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions will discuss this issue in detail.

### 适应建设的发展  苏联工会将改组

1957-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    适应建设的发展
    苏联工会将改组
    据新华社31日讯　全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申3月30日在莫斯科举行的全苏工会积极分子会议上说，由于工业和建设的管理结构的改变，苏联工会有必要进行改组工作。
    格里申说，即将举行的全苏工会中央理事会全体会议将详细讨论这一问题。
