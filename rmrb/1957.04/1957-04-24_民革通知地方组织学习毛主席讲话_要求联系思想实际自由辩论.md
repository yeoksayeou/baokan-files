Title: R.O.C. Revolutionary Committee Notifies Local Organizations to Study Chairman Mao's Speech, Requiring Connection to Ideological Reality and Free Debate
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (R.O.C.) recently issued a notice to its various local organizations, requiring them to promptly organize members and related social figures to earnestly study Chairman Mao Zedong's speech on the correct handling of contradictions among the people, Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the CPPCC meeting, and other documents, as well as the work report made by Li Jishen, Chairman of the R.O.C. Revolutionary Committee, at the recent plenary session of the R.O.C. Central Committee.

The notice stated that these reports and documents have extremely important guiding significance for promoting the future work of the R.O.C. Revolutionary Committee as a whole.

The notice requested members to clarify the trend of the international situation during their studies, recognize the important significance of strengthening and consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union; recognize the difference between contradictions between the people and contradictions between ourselves and the enemy and the possibility of their mutual transformation, recognize the reasons for the prominent contradictions among the people and the correct ways to handle them; and recognize the great significance of the peaceful liberation of Taiwan. At the same time, members are required to clearly recognize the great significance of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party's proposal of the policy of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision," and recognize the current tasks of the R.O.C. Revolutionary Committee and various guidelines and requirements in its work.

The notice also put forward specific learning requirements for the social figures connected with the R.O.C. Revolutionary Committee. The notice also requires members of the R.O.C. Revolutionary Committee and related social figures to be able to connect with the reality of their work and thoughts in learning and discussion, and to implement the spirit of "complete openness, free debate; adhering to the truth, patiently persuading; distinguishing right from wrong, correcting mistakes," encouraging them to delve into documents, think independently, and, after fully and freely expressing different opinions, help each other and gradually reach a unity of understanding.

### 民革通知地方组织学习毛主席讲话  要求联系思想实际自由辩论

1957-04-24
第2版()
专栏：

    民革通知地方组织学习毛主席讲话
    要求联系思想实际自由辩论
    新华社22日讯　中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会最近向各级地方组织发出通知，要求他们及时组织成员和所联系的社会人士认真学习毛泽东主席关于正确处理人民内部矛盾问题的讲话，周恩来总理在政协会议上的报告和其他文件，以及民革主席李济深在民革中央委员会最近举行的全体会议上所作的工作报告等。
    通知说，这些报告和文件对推进民革全党今后的工作，具有极其重要的指导作用。
    通知中要求成员在学习中明确国际局势的趋向，认识加强和巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营团结的重要意义；认识人民内部矛盾和敌我矛盾的区别及其相互转化的可能性，认识当前人民内部矛盾突出的原因及正确处理的办法；认识和平解放台湾的重大意义。同时要求成员明确认识中共中央提出“长期共存、互相监督”方针的重大意义，认识民革当前的任务和工作中的各项方针和要求。
    通知中对民革所联系的社会人士也提出了具体学习要求。通知中并要求民革成员和所联系的社会人士，在学习和讨论中能够联系工作和思想实际，并能贯彻“彻底敞开，自由辩论；坚持真理，耐心说服；分清是非，修正错误”的精神，鼓励钻研文件，独立思考，经过充分自由发表不同意见，互相帮助，逐渐达到认识上的统一。
