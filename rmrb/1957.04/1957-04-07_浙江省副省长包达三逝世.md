Title: Bao Dasan, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, April 6th. Bao Dasan, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, delegate to the National People's Congress, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Standing Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, passed away at his residence in Hangzhou this morning due to hypertension and hypertensive heart disease, at the age of seventy-three.

The Bao Dasan Funeral Committee has been established, consisting of forty-four members, with Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, as the Chairman. Bao Dasan's remains will be buried at the Nanshan Cemetery in Hangzhou on the 9th.

### 浙江省副省长包达三逝世

1957-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    浙江省副省长包达三逝世
    新华社杭州6日电　浙江省副省长、全国人民代表大会代表、中国民主建国会中央常务委员、中华全国工商业联合会常务委员包达三，因高血压症及高血压性心脏病，今天上午在杭州寓所逝世，享年七十三岁。
    包达三治丧委员会已经成立，共四十四人，由浙江省省长沙文汉任主任委员。包达三的遗体9日将在杭州南山公墓安葬。
