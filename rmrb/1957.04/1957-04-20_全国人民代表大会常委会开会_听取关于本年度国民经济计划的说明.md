Title: NPC Standing Committee Meets, Hearing Explanation on This Year's National Economic Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 66th meeting this morning in Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai, to hear Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Economic Commission Bo Yibo's explanation of the 1957 national economic plan.

Premier Zhou Enlai made supplementary explanations at the meeting.

During the meeting, Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De, visited the venue. All the committee members warmly applauded to express their welcome.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会开会  听取关于本年度国民经济计划的说明

1957-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会开会
    听取关于本年度国民经济计划的说明
    新华社18日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天上午在中南海紫光阁举行第六十六次会议，听取国务院副总理兼国家经济委员会主任薄一波关于1957年度国民经济计划的说明。
    周恩来总理在会上作了补充说明。
    会议进行中，伏罗希洛夫主席由朱德副主席陪同到会场参观，全体委员们热烈鼓掌，表示欢迎。
