Title: Panamanian President Reaffirms Determination to Defend Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1957-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Panama City, April 16th: Panamanian President Ernesto de la Guardia Jr. issued a statement on the evening of April 14th, reaffirming that Panama will defend its sovereignty over the Canal Zone.

### 巴拿马总统  重申要保卫主权

1957-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴拿马总统  重申要保卫主权
    据新华社16日讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马总统古阿迪亚14日晚上发表声明，重申巴拿马将保卫它对运河区的主权。
