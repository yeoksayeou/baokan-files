Title: Voroshilov to Visit Indonesia in May
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 5

    Voroshilov
    To Visit Indonesia in May
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 4th: TASS Moscow, April 4th: During the visit of President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia to the Soviet Union in 1956, he invited Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to visit Indonesia. Voroshilov gratefully accepted this invitation. He plans to visit the Republic of Indonesia in May this year after his visit to the People's Republic of China.

### 伏罗希洛夫将在5月访问印度尼西亚

1957-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫
    将在5月访问印度尼西亚
    据新华社4日讯　塔斯社莫斯科4日讯：印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺在1956年访问苏联的时候，曾经邀请苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫访问印度尼西亚。伏罗希洛夫感谢地接受了这个邀请。他准备在访问中华人民共和国以后在今年5月访问印度尼西亚共和国。
