Title: International Organization of Journalists Presidium Discusses Organizational Work; Executive Committee Sympathizes with Egyptian Guest's Speech on Maintaining Journalistic Ethics
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Presidium of the International Organization of Journalists held a meeting on the 5th, after the conclusion of the Executive Committee's Beijing meeting, to discuss the organizational work of the association.

At the meeting on the 4th, the Executive Committee of the Beijing meeting listened with great attention and sympathy to the speech of the Egyptian guest, Mr. Ahmed Kassem Gouda, editor-in-chief of the Egyptian "Republic" newspaper and former chairman of the Egyptian Journalists Association, who was invited to attend the meeting. Gouda said that journalists all over the world should safeguard the principles and ethics of their noble profession and use all means to avoid such attempts by some governments, groups, or individuals to withhold, distort, misinterpret, or fabricate news, thereby deceiving public opinion and being exploited by imperialists and monopolists. This is a real threat to peace, stability, and the establishment of better understanding between the people of all countries.

### 国际新闻工作者协会主席团讨论组织工作  执委会同情埃及来宾关于维护记者职业道德的发言

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    国际新闻工作者协会主席团讨论组织工作
    执委会同情埃及来宾关于维护记者职业道德的发言
    新华社5日讯　国际新闻工作者协会主席团，在协会的执行委员会北京会议闭幕后，在5日举行了会议，讨论了协会的组织工作问题。
    执行委员会北京会议在4日会议上，以巨大的注意和同情听取了应邀参加会议的埃及来宾、埃及“共和国报”总编辑、埃及新闻工作者协会前任主席艾哈迈德·卡塞姆·古达先生的发言。古达说，全世界新闻工作者应该维护他们崇高职业的原则和道德，用一切办法避免一些政府、集团或个人的这样的企图：扣压、歪曲、曲解或捏造新闻，从而迷惑舆论和为帝国主义者及垄断资本家的势力所利用，这对和平、稳定和建立各国人民之间更好的谅解是一个真正的威胁。
