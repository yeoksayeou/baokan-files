Title: Mongolia Establishes Consulate General in Hohhot
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 2

    Mongolia Establishes Consulate General in Hohhot

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. With the consent of our government, the Mongolian People's Republic has decided to establish a Consulate General in Hohhot and has appointed Tsevelmaa Dash as Consul General.

### 蒙古在呼和浩特设总领事馆

1957-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    蒙古在呼和浩特设总领事馆
    新华社29日讯　蒙古人民共和国征得我国政府同意后，决定在呼和浩特设立总领事馆，并已委任策维尔玛·达什为总领事。
