Title: US Lays a Spiderweb to Plunder Arab Nations; International Chamber of Commerce Advocates "Developing" the Western Mediterranean Region
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 22nd: The Chairman of the International Chamber of Commerce, Pearson, proposed a plan on the 17th to develop the Western Mediterranean region, engaging in economic development in areas such as Spain, France, Italy, Morocco, Algeria, and Tunisia. He said that this plan must rely on the support of capital exporting countries like the United States.

Pearson said that if this plan is properly initiated and implemented, it is likely to arouse the "interest" of many underdeveloped countries in the world. Once Arab countries believe in such a plan, U.S. relations with the Middle East "may have a new beginning." He hopes that Egypt and Libya will participate in this plan in the future.

### 美国布设掠夺阿拉伯国家的蜘蛛网  国际商会主张“开发”西地中海地区

1957-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    美国布设掠夺阿拉伯国家的蜘蛛网
    国际商会主张“开发”西地中海地区
    据新华社22日讯　纽约消息：国际商会主席皮尔逊17日提出一个开发西地中海地区的计划，以从事西班牙、法国、意大利、摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚和突尼斯等地的经济开发。他说，这个计划必须依靠像美国这样的资本输出国的支持。
    皮尔逊说，如果适当地开始和执行这个计划，就很可能引起世界上许多不发达的国家的“兴趣”。一旦阿拉伯国家相信这样的计划，美国同中东的关系就“可能有一个新的开端”。他希望，埃及和利比亚将来都会参加这个计划。
