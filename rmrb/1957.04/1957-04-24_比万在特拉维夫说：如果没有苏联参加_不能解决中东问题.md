Title: Bevan Says in Tel Aviv: The Middle East Problem Cannot Be Solved Without Soviet Participation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 5

    Bevan Says in Tel Aviv:
    The Middle East Problem Cannot Be Solved Without Soviet Participation

    (Dispatch from this newspaper) British Labour Party foreign policy spokesman Bevan said in Tel Aviv on the 21st that no Middle East problem could be solved without Soviet participation. He said he hoped developments would lead to a broad agreement with Soviet participation. When asked his opinion of the Eisenhower Doctrine, Bevan said he did not think American Cadillac cars would be more popular in the Middle East than British Rolls-Royce cars.

    Bevan recently visited India, Pakistan, Iran, Iraq and Turkey. He arrived in Tel Aviv, Israel on the 21st.

### 比万在特拉维夫说：如果没有苏联参加  不能解决中东问题

1957-04-24
第5版()
专栏：

    比万在特拉维夫说：
    如果没有苏联参加  不能解决中东问题
    本报讯　英国工党外交政策发言人比万21日在特拉维夫说，如果没有苏联参加，任何中东问题都不能解决。他说，他希望局势的发展能够达成有苏联人参加的广泛的协议。有人问他对艾森豪威尔主义有何看法时，比万说，他认为美国的卡迪拉克牌汽车在中东并不会比英国的罗尔斯·劳埃斯牌汽车更受人欢迎。
    比万最近曾访问了印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗、伊拉克和土耳其。他是21日到达以色列的特拉维夫的。
