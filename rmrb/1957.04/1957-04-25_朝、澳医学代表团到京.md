Title: Korean, Australian Medical Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 19th—The medical delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, consisting of five people led by Liu Jichun, Vice Minister of Public Health of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of April 19th at the invitation of the Ministry of Public Health of the People's Republic of China to visit our country and participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

He Biao, Vice Minister of Public Health of the People's Republic of China, and others welcomed them at the station. Kim Gui-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also welcomed them at the station.

According to this newspaper, the Australian medical delegation, led by Professor Cox, arrived in Beijing on April 23rd for its first visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association. The delegation members are mostly experts and professors in various disciplines.

### 朝、澳医学代表团到京

1957-04-25
第7版()
专栏：

    朝、澳医学代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中华人民共和国卫生部邀请来我国参观访问并参加“五一”节观礼的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国医学代表团五人，在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国保健省副相柳基春的率领下已于4月19日下午到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中华人民共和国卫生部副部长贺彪等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办金贵南也到车站欢迎。
    本报讯　应中华医学会邀请首次来我国访问的澳大利亚医学代表团于4月23日抵达北京。澳大利亚医学代表团系由考克斯教授率领，代表团成员多为各科专家教授。
