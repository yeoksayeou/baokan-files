Title: Chinese Buddhist Association Holds Representative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-04-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The Second National Representative Meeting of the Chinese Buddhist Association was held in Beijing from March 26th to 31st.

More than 200 Living Buddhas, Lamas, Dharma Masters, monks, nuns and lay Buddhists from eleven ethnic groups including Han, Tibetan, Mongolian and Dai from all over the country participated in the meeting.

The honorary president of the Chinese Buddhist Association, Chagang Gegen, attended the meeting. The meeting opened with a speech by the President of the Chinese Buddhist Association, Xirao Jiacuo, and the Vice President, layman Zhao Puchu, gave a "Work Report of the First Council of the Chinese Buddhist Association". The meeting invited Sakongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, to give a report on the current situation. At the closing meeting, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, made a speech on religious policies.

The meeting discussed and revised the charter of the Chinese Buddhist Association. The meeting elected Xirao Jiacuo as President, and Gaden Seit, Yingci, Jingquan, Songliu Agamu Niy (Jinghong), Neng Hai, Zhao Puchu, Gala Zang, Awang Jiacuo, Juzan, Zhou Shujia, and Wugula and ten others as Vice Presidents. The meeting also elected the Second Council of the Chinese Buddhist Association, consisting of 220 directors.

The meeting received congratulatory telegrams from the honorary president of the Chinese Buddhist Association, the Dalai Lama, and the Panchen Erdeni.

### 中国佛教协会举行代表会议

1957-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国佛教协会举行代表会议
    新华社31日讯　中国佛教协会第二届全国代表会议，于3月26日到31日在北京举行。
    参加这次会议的有来自全国各地的汉、藏、蒙古、傣等十一个民族的二百多位活佛、喇嘛、法师、僧、尼和居士。
    中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根出席了会议。会议由中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错致开幕词，副会长赵朴初居士作了“中国佛教协会第一届理事会工作报告”。会议邀请民族事务委员会副主任委员萨空了作了时事报告。在闭幕会议上，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘作了有关宗教政策的讲话。
    这次会议讨论和修改了中国佛教协会的章程。会议选举喜饶嘉错为会长，噶登赛特、应慈、静权、松榴·阿戛木尼亚（景洪）、能海、赵朴初、噶喇藏、阿旺嘉错、巨赞、周叔迦、伍古腊等十一人为副会长。会议并选出由二百二十名理事组成的中国佛教协会第二届理事会。
    会议收到了中国佛教协会名誉会长达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的贺电。
