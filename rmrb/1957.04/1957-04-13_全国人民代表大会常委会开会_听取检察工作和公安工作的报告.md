Title: NPC Standing Committee Meets to Hear Reports on Procuratorial and Public Security Work
Author:
Date: 1957-04-13
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its sixty-first expanded meeting this afternoon. The meeting heard a report by Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, on the work of the procuratorates in 1956 and opinions on the work for 1957.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its sixty-second expanded meeting this afternoon. The meeting heard a report by Zhou Xing, Vice Minister of Public Security, on public security work.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会开会  听取检察工作和公安工作的报告

1957-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会开会
    听取检察工作和公安工作的报告
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今日下午举行第六十一次会议扩大会议。会议听取了最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞作的关于1956年检察工作情况和1957年工作意见的报告。新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今日下午举行第六十二次会议扩大会议。会议听取了公安部副部长周兴关于公安工作的报告。
