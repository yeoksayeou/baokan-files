Title: Over Ten Thousand Rangoon Citizens Rally, Demanding Restoration of Domestic Peace
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 8th. Over ten thousand Rangoon citizens rallied on April 7th, organized by the domestic peace organization led by the famous Burmese peace activist, Tekin Ko Tu Mai, to demand the restoration of domestic peace in Burma. Over ten thousand Rangoon citizens participated in the rally, including more than two thousand monks.

    U Narda, chairman of the Rangoon Abbot Organization, read out the organization's statement, demanding an end to the civil war and the realization of peace through fair and reasonable means.

    Tekin Ko Tu Mai said at the meeting that he would be very happy if the government and the underground army could achieve peace through negotiations and work together.

    The meeting passed a resolution expressing its belief that the domestic peace organization would strive to achieve domestic peace and demanding that it implement the statement issued by the Rangoon Abbot Organization.

### 要求恢复国内和平  仰光万余市民集会

1957-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    要求恢复国内和平
    仰光万余市民集会
    新华社仰光8日电　缅甸著名和平人士德钦哥都迈领导的国内和平组织4月7日在仰光举行群众大会，要求在缅甸恢复国内和平。参加大会的仰光市民有一万多人，其中有僧侣二千余人。
    仰光方丈组织主席吴纳拉达宣读了这个组织的声明，要求停止内战，通过公平合理的办法实现和平。
    德钦哥都迈在大会上说，如果政府和地下军能够通过谈判实现和平，并且携手工作，他将感到非常高兴。
    大会通过决议，表示相信国内和平组织会争取实现国内和平，并且要求它实现仰光方丈组织发表的声明。
