Title: Soviet "Literary Gazette" Publishes Chairman Mao's Poems
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th -- The Soviet "Literary Gazette" today published Chairman Mao Zedong's four poems and Chairman Mao Zedong's letter to the "Poetry Journal" editorial department on the first page, as published in the Chinese "Poetry Journal."

These poems were translated into Russian by the Soviet poets Aseyev, Marshak, and Surkov, and annotated by Fedin. They have translated all eighteen poems published in the "Poetry Journal" into Russian and will publish them in the May issue of "Foreign Literature."

### 苏联“文学报”刊登毛主席的诗词

1957-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联“文学报”刊登毛主席的诗词
    新华社莫斯科27日电　苏联“文学报”今天在第一版发表了中国“诗刊”上登载的毛泽东主席的四首词和毛泽东主席给“诗刊”编辑部的信。
    这些词是由苏联诗人阿谢耶夫、马尔夏克和苏尔科夫三人译成俄文的，并由费德林加以注释。他们已经把“诗刊”上登载的毛主席的全部十八首诗词译成俄文，将在5月份的“外国文学”上发表。
