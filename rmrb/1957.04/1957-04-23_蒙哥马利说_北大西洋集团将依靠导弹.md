Title: Montgomery Says North Atlantic Group Will Rely on Missiles
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd dispatch from Paris: Montgomery, Deputy Supreme Commander of the North Atlantic Group's forces, said on the 18th that the military plans of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization will be based on possessing missiles with a range of 2,000 miles within ten years.

Montgomery stated that the group intends to equip its army with a weapon with "unprecedented destructive power" by 1966. He said: "The new key to our strategy is this unprecedented degree of destructive power."

### 蒙哥马利说  北大西洋集团将依靠导弹

1957-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙哥马利说
    北大西洋集团将依靠导弹
    据新华社22日讯　巴黎消息：北大西洋集团军队副总司令蒙哥马利18日说，北大西洋公约组织的军事计划将以在十年内拥有射程为两千英里的导弹为基础。
    蒙哥马利表示，这个集团打算使它的军队在1966年装备一种“毁灭力达到空前程度”的武器。他说：“我们的战略的新关键，就是这种空前程度的毁灭力。”
