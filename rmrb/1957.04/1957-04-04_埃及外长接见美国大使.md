Title: Egyptian Foreign Minister Receives U.S. Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 3rd: Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi received the U.S. Ambassador to Egypt, Hare, on April 2nd. According to press reports, they held talks on the Suez Canal issue.

The "Al-Ahram" expressed surprise at the United States' insistence on using the six principles adopted by the Security Council last year to resolve the Suez Canal issue. The newspaper said in an editorial published on April 2nd that the U.S. approach "is to impose obligations that should be fulfilled by both parties on only one party."

### 埃及外长接见美国大使

1957-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及外长接见美国大使
    新华社开罗3日电　埃及外交部长法齐2日接见了美国驻埃及大使赫尔。据报界消息，他们就苏伊士运河问题作了会谈。
    “金字塔报”对于美国坚持要用安全理事会去年通过的六项原则来解决苏伊士运河问题的态度表示惊讶。这家报纸在2日发表的一篇社论中说，美国这种做法“就是把应当由双方履行的义务强加在一方身上”。
