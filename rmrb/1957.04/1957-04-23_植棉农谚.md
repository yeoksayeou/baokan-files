Planting Cotton Farming Proverbs

When the jujube sprouts, plant cotton. (Zhejiang and other places)
Early-blooming peach, late-blooming seedlings. (Guizhou)
Plant early when there is moisture, plant by spot when there is no moisture. (Northern cotton-growing areas)
Seeing seedlings is half the harvest. (Hebei and other places)
Thin out dense seedlings, keep dense seedlings sparse, keep the strong ones if not sparse or dense. (Hebei)
Sparse seedlings look at the bolls, dense seedlings pick cotton. (Jieyu, Shanxi)
Hoe the seedlings seven or eight times, and the bolls will be as big as garlic cloves. (Northern cotton-growing areas)
Hoe cotton seven times, as white as frost. (Hunan and other places)

### 植棉农谚

1957-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    植棉农谚
    枣发芽，种棉花。（浙江等地）
    早花桃，迟花苗。（贵州）
    有墒抢种，没墒点种。（北方棉区）
    见苗一半收。（河北等地）
    稀留密，密留稀，不稀不密留壮的。（河北）
    稀苗看疙瘩，稠苗摘棉花。（山西解虞）
    苗锄七八遍，结桃赛蒜瓣。（北方棉区）
    棉薅七道白如霜。（湖南等地）
