Title: Mikoyan Visits Vienna
Author:
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 5

    Mikoyan Visits Vienna
    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 23rd (via TASS): At the invitation of the Austrian government, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, arrived in Vienna on the 23rd for a visit.
    Austrian Federal Chancellor Raab delivered a welcoming speech to Mikoyan at the airport. He expressed his gratitude to the Soviet Union for its great contribution to the conclusion of the Austrian State Treaty, and pointed out that the conclusion of this treaty was a major step in international relations contributing to the consolidation of peace, and that this step had also greatly promoted the easing of tensions between countries.
    Mikoyan responded, expressing his hope that this visit would expand and strengthen contacts with Austrian national leaders, and further understand the lives of the Austrian people, so as to promote the development of friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Austria, and benefit peace and security in Europe.

### 米高扬到维也纳访问

1957-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬到维也纳访问
    据新华社24日讯　据塔斯社维也纳23日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬应奥地利政府的邀请，23日到维也纳访问。
    奥地利联邦总理拉布在机场上向米高扬致欢迎词。他对苏联在缔结奥地利国家条约方面所作出的巨大的贡献表示感谢，并且指出：这个条约的缔结在国际关系方面是一项对巩固和平事业有巨大贡献的步骤，这个步骤还在很大程度上促进了各国之间矛盾的缓和。
    米高扬致答词，表示希望通过这次访问能够扩大和加强同奥地利国家领导人的接触，并且进一步了解奥地利人民的生活，以便促进苏奥两国友好关系的发展，而利于欧洲的和平和安全。
