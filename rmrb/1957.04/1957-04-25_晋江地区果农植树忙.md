Title: Fruit Farmers in Jinjiang Area Busy Planting Trees
Author: Su Zongqi
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 4

    Fruit Farmers in Jinjiang Area Busy Planting Trees
    (Our dispatch) Fruit farmers in the Jinjiang Special District of Fujian Province are vigorously planting fruit trees. As of April 4, the district has newly planted more than 750,000 trees of various types, including tangerines, longans, lychees, loquats, bananas, and pineapples.
    The Jinjiang Special District is one of China's major fruit-producing areas. The "Xinghua Guiyuan" (dried longan), "Putian Jiefangzhong Loquat" and lychees from Xianyou, Nan'an and other places produced here enjoy a great reputation at home and abroad, and are of high economic value. The district's longan fruit trees alone can produce 200,000 dan of dried longan in a normal bumper year. (Su Zongqi)

### 晋江地区果农植树忙
苏宗琦
1957-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    晋江地区果农植树忙
    本报讯　福建省晋江专区的果农，正在大力栽植果树。截至4月4日为止，全区已新栽植柑桔、龙眼、荔枝、枇杷、香蕉、凤梨等各种果树七十五万多株。
    晋江专区，是我国主要果产区之一。这里出产的“兴化桂圆”、“莆田解放钟枇杷”与仙游、南安等地的荔枝，在国内外享有很大的声誉，经济价值也很高。全区单龙眼果树每年（大年）就可产桂圆干二十万担。　（苏宗琦）
