Title: Danish Prime Minister Hansen Says Seize the Opportunity to Ease the Situation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, March 3rd: Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hansen said on the evening of the 1st, when talking about the letter he received from Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin, "We must seize every opportunity to ease international tensions." He emphasized that if we want to avoid disaster, the East and the West "must negotiate together."

### 丹麦首相汉森表示  要抓紧机会缓和局势

1957-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    丹麦首相汉森表示
    要抓紧机会缓和局势
    新华社3日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森1日晚间谈到苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁给他的来信时说：“我们必须抓紧一切机会缓和国际紧张局势。”他强调，如果要避免灾祸，东方和西方就“必须共同商谈”。
