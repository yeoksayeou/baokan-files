Title: "New York Times" Slanders Ceylon Buddhism, Bandaranaike Protests to US Government, Ceylon Ministry of Posts, Telegraphs and Broadcasting Demands Full Inspection of "Voice of America"
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 1st -- Colombo dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike has lodged a protest with the US government through the US Ambassador to Ceylon against an article in the American "New York Times" attacking Ceylon Buddhism.

The "New York Times" published an article on March 21 by Cyrus L. Sulzberger entitled "Storm Clouds Gathering in Ceylon Before the Storm." The article said: "A strange anti-Western Buddhist revival movement is invading political life and sowing seeds of stubbornness (in Ceylon)." The article said that this situation "incites unrest and chaos that hinders democracy."

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 1st -- Colombo dispatch: Ceylon's Minister of Posts, Telegraphs and Broadcasting, Marikar, expressed concern that the work of inspecting "Voice of America" broadcasts from Ceylon was not thorough enough.

Marikar said that many Americans have consistently favored "reactionaries" like Chiang Kai-shek over "progressive figures" like Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, or Bandaranaike. He said these progressive figures are "beloved by the reawakened people of Asia."

Marikar also said: "There is a dangerous fact that Americans are unable to respect the religious sentiments of other countries' people."

Marikar has written to Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike, warning him in the letter that the consequences of this situation are serious for relations with the United States and for Ceylon, because the "Voice of America" broadcasts from Ceylon contain religiously insulting remarks.

### “纽约时报”诽谤锡兰佛教  班达拉奈克向美国政府抗议  锡邮电和广播部要求充分检查“美国之音”

1957-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    “纽约时报”诽谤锡兰佛教
    班达拉奈克向美国政府抗议
    锡邮电和广播部要求充分检查“美国之音”
    新华社新德里1日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克通过美国驻锡兰大使向美国政府提出一项抗议，抗议美国“纽约时报”上的一篇文章对锡兰佛教所进行的攻击。
    “纽约时报”在3月21日刊载了塞鲁斯·勒·苏兹贝格写的一篇文章，题目是“暴风雨前乌云涌集锡兰”。文章中说：“一种奇怪的反西方的佛教复兴运动正在侵入政治生活，并且（在锡兰）散布顽固不化的种子。”这篇文章说，这种情况“煽起妨害民主的不安和混乱”。
    新华社新德里1日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰邮电和广播部长马里卡尔对于检查“美国之音”从锡兰发出的广播的工作做得不充分，表示不安。
    马里卡尔说，许多美国人一贯喜欢像蒋介石那样的“反动分子”，而不喜欢像毛泽东、周恩来或班达拉奈克这样的“进步人物”。他说，这些进步人物是“重新觉醒的亚洲人民所爱戴的”。
    马里卡尔还说：“有这样一个危险的事实：美国人不能够尊重其他国家人民的宗教感情。”
    马里卡尔已经写信给锡兰总理班达拉奈克，他在信中警告说，这种情况所引起的后果对美国的关系和对锡兰都是严重的，因为从锡兰发出的“美国之音”广播稿中包含了侮辱宗教的言论。
