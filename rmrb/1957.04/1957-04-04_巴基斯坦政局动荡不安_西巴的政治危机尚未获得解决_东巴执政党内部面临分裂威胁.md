Title: Pakistan's Political Situation is Unstable; The Political Crisis in West Pakistan Has Not Been Resolved; The Ruling Party in East Pakistan Faces the Threat of Internal Division
Author:
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, April 3rd: The political crisis in West Pakistan has not yet been resolved, while the ruling Awami League in East Pakistan is facing the threat of division.

After Prime Minister Suhrawardy went to Lahore to discuss with the leaders of the Republican Party and the Muslim League on how to resolve the political crisis in West Pakistan, he returned here on the 2nd.

It is reported that during the talks, the Muslim League leaders reiterated that they hold the majority in the provincial assembly and once again demanded that the Muslim League organize a new West Pakistan provincial government.

In East Pakistan, the Awami League Standing Committee, on March 30th, on the recommendation of Rahman, the General Secretary of the Awami League and Minister of Commerce of East Pakistan who supports Suhrawardy's policies, suspended the membership of Ahad, the Secretary of the Awami League, on the grounds of "indiscipline." Nine standing committee members of the Awami League Standing Committee have protested the suspension of Ahad's membership by withdrawing from the committee.

The "Pakistan Times" pointed out that the current situation in West and East Pakistan is due to the increasing contradictions within the ruling party and the deteriorating domestic economic situation.

### 巴基斯坦政局动荡不安  西巴的政治危机尚未获得解决  东巴执政党内部面临分裂威胁

1957-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政局动荡不安
    西巴的政治危机尚未获得解决
    东巴执政党内部面临分裂威胁
    据新华社卡拉奇3日电　西巴基斯坦的政治危机尚未解决，而在东巴基斯坦执政的人民联盟内部又受到了分裂的威胁。
    苏拉瓦底总理去拉合尔同共和党和穆斯林联盟领袖商讨解决西巴基斯坦政治危机的办法以后，已经在2日回到这里。
    据报道，在会谈期间，穆斯林联盟领袖重申他们在省议会中占多数，并且再次要求让穆斯林联盟组织新的西巴基斯坦省政府。
    在东巴基斯坦，人民联盟常务委员会3月30日在支持苏拉瓦底政策的人民联盟总书记、东巴基斯坦省商务部长拉赫曼的建议下，停止了人民联盟的书记阿哈德的盟籍，理由是他“不守纪律”。人民联盟常务委员会的九个常务委员已经以退出委员会的行动来对停止阿哈德盟籍的措施表示抗议。
    “巴基斯坦时报”指出，西巴基斯坦和东巴基斯坦目前的局势是由于执政党内部的矛盾日益增长和国内经济情况日益恶化而产生的。
