Title: Chairman Mao Receives Cambodian Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th -- Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Yang An, head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, and Nai Walangdan, the deputy head, this evening.

Also present at the reception were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席接见柬埔寨经济代表

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见柬埔寨经济代表
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东今天晚上接见了柬埔寨王国驻我国经济代表团团长扬安和副团长乃瓦朗丹。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫、对外贸易部副部长雷任民和外交部亚洲司司长章文晋。
