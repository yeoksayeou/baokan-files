Title: Overseas Chinese Tourist Groups Arrive in Beijing Successively
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

Overseas Chinese Tourist Groups Arrive in Beijing Successively

　　This newspaper reports that overseas Chinese tour groups and returned overseas Chinese compatriots who are coming to Beijing to attend the “May 1st” celebrations are arriving in Beijing one after another.

　　The Indonesian Overseas Chinese Tourist Group arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 27th. This group has a total of seventy-nine people.

　　Prior to this, a total of one hundred and seventy-five overseas Chinese from the Soviet Union, Mongolia, Indonesia, India, Burma, the Netherlands, France, Africa, Switzerland and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots have arrived in Beijing.

### 华侨观光团陆续到京

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨观光团陆续到京
    本报讯　来北京参加“五一”节观礼的华侨回国观光团和回国观光侨胞正陆续到达北京。
    27日晚上到达北京的是印度尼西亚华侨回国观光团。这个团共七十九人。
    在这以前，先后到达北京的，有苏联、蒙古、印度尼西亚、印度、缅甸、荷兰、法国、非洲、瑞士等地侨胞以及港澳同胞共一百七十五人。
