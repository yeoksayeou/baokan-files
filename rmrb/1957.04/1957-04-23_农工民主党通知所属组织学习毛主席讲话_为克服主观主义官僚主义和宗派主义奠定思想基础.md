Title: The Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Notifies Subordinate Organizations to Study Chairman Mao's Speeches to Lay the Ideological Foundation for Overcoming Subjectivism, Bureaucratism, and Sectarianism
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Central Executive Bureau of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party recently notified all subordinate organizations, requiring them to organize all members to earnestly study Chairman Mao Zedong's speech on the correct handling of contradictions among the people and Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the National Propaganda Work Conference.

The notice said that these two speeches have significant theoretical and practical significance for the work of democratic parties.

The notice pointed out that this study is aimed at obtaining the correct viewpoints and methods for understanding and handling contradictions among the people, thereby achieving the goal of enhancing unity and improving work. The notice requires members of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party to further clarify the social roots of contradictions among the people through this study, thereby laying the ideological foundation for overcoming subjectivism, bureaucratism, and sectarianism. The notice also requires organizations at all levels to consciously encourage party members to respond first to the call of the Chinese Communist Party and the state on the issue of enhancing internal party unity and on the issue of their children's enrollment in schools. The notice also stipulates that the study time will generally be two months.

### 农工民主党通知所属组织学习毛主席讲话  为克服主观主义官僚主义和宗派主义奠定思想基础

1957-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

    农工民主党通知所属组织学习毛主席讲话
    为克服主观主义官僚主义和宗派主义奠定思想基础
    新华社22日讯　中国农工民主党中央执行局最近通知所属各级组织，要求组织全体成员认真学习毛泽东主席关于正确处理人民内部矛盾问题的讲话以及毛泽东主席在全国宣传工作会议上的讲话。
    通知说，这两个讲话对民主党派的工作具有重大的理论意义和实践意义。
    通知指出，这次学习是为了获得认识和处理人民内部矛盾的正确观点和方法，从而达到增强团结改进工作的目的。通知要求农工民主党的成员通过这次学习，要进一步明确认识人民内部矛盾的社会根源，从而为克服主观主义、官僚主义和宗派主义奠定思想基础。通知还要求各级组织有意识地去鼓励党的成员在增强党内团结问题上，在对待自己子女的升学问题上，首先响应中国共产党和国家的号召。通知还规定学习时间一般为两个月。
