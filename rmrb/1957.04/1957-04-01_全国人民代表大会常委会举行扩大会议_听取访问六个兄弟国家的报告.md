Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting to Hear Reports on Visits to Six Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-04-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) held its 54th enlarged meeting on the afternoon of March 31st to hear the "Report on the Visit of the Delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and Yugoslavia" by Peng Zhen, head of the NPC delegation. (The full text of the report is on page two).
    At the meeting, Li Jishen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Bojun, and Hu Ziang, deputy heads of the NPC delegation, successively made supplementary reports, expressing their feelings about this foreign visit.
    Deputy Head Li Jishen focused on introducing the situations in these fraternal countries regarding transportation, forestry, construction, and the improvement of people's lives.
    Deputy Head Cheng Qian said that in every moment of the friendly visits to these fraternal countries, he felt the deep friendship of the people of the fraternal countries towards the Chinese people. He said he saw that each fraternal country had a strong economic power and a powerful national defense force. Therefore, he believed that world peace was indeed guaranteed.
    Deputy Head Zhang Bojun introduced the work of the people's democratic united front in our country and the high regard and welcome given to the NPC delegation composed of various parties by the fraternal countries.
    Deputy Head Hu Ziang especially mentioned that the socialist transformation of private capitalist industry and commerce in our country received great attention from the fraternal countries.
    The meeting expressed satisfaction with the NPC delegation's foreign visit and report, and believed that through this visit, the mutual understanding between fraternal countries was further deepened, the deep friendship between fraternal countries was enhanced, the close unity between fraternal countries was strengthened, and many advanced experiences of fraternal countries were learned. This has significant implications for the smooth progress of our country's socialist construction, for maintaining lasting world peace, and for promoting the cause of human progress.
    Those attending the meeting included leaders of the State Council and heads of government departments, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations, NPC deputies in Beijing, members of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and members of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee's delegation to the Soviet Union and other countries.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行扩大会议  听取访问六个兄弟国家的报告

1957-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行扩大会议
    听取访问六个兄弟国家的报告
    新华社31日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在3月31日下午举行第五十四次会议扩大会议，听取了全国人民代表大会代表团彭真团长作的“中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团访问苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫的报告”（报告全文见第二版）。
    会上，全国人民代表大会代表团副团长李济深、程潜、章伯钧、胡子昂先后作了补充报告，畅述了这次出国访问的观感。
    李济深副团长着重介绍了这些兄弟国家有关交通、林业、建筑等方面的情况和人民生活改善的情况。
    程潜副团长说，在这些兄弟国家进行友好访问的每时每刻，都感受到兄弟国家人民对中国人民的深厚友谊。他说，他看到各兄弟国家都有着雄厚的经济力量和强大的国防力量。因此，他相信世界和平确实是有保证的。
    章伯钧副团长介绍了我国人民民主统一战线工作和由各党派组成的全国人民代表大会代表团受到各兄弟国家重视和欢迎的情况。
    胡子昂副团长特别谈到了我国对私人资本主义工商业进行社会主义改造工作受到各兄弟国家重视的情况。
    会议对全国人民代表大会代表团这次出国访问工作和报告表示满意，并且认为通过这次访问，进一步加深了兄弟国家之间的相互了解，增进了兄弟国家之间的深厚友谊，加强了兄弟国家之间的亲密团结，并且学习了兄弟国家的许多先进经验，对于顺利地进行我国的社会主义建设，对于维护世界持久和平和促进人类进步事业，都有重大的意义。
    列席这次会议的，有国务院领导人和政府各部门负责人，政协全国委员会常务委员，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，全国人民代表大会在京代表，以及北京市人民委员会委员和北京市人民委员会访问苏联等国代表团团员。
