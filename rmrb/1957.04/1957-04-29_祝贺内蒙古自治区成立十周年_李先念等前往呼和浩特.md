Title: Congratulating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Li Xiannian and Others Go to Hohhot
Author:
Date: 1957-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: To attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian left Beijing for Hohhot by special train this afternoon.

Accompanying him were Yang Jingren (Hui nationality), Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Zhou Jianren, Deputy Minister of Higher Education, and Tian Fuda (Gaoshan nationality), member of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress.

Also going to offer congratulations were Iminov (Uyghur nationality), Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, Sokang Wangqing Gelei (Tibetan nationality), member of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, Xi Raojiacuo (Tibetan nationality) and Ma Fuchen (Hui nationality), Vice Governors of Qinghai Province, Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan nationality), Vice Governor of Gansu Province, Zhu Dehai (Korean nationality), Vice Governor of Jilin Province, as well as representatives from autonomous prefectures and autonomous counties in various provinces, totaling fifty-three people, including nineteen ethnic groups.

More than forty people, including youth representatives from various ethnic groups, representatives from local trade unions, and representatives from cultural and artistic departments, also went to offer congratulations.

Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, and others were at the station to see them off.

### 祝贺内蒙古自治区成立十周年  李先念等前往呼和浩特

1957-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺内蒙古自治区成立十周年
    李先念等前往呼和浩特
    据新华社28日讯  前往祝贺内蒙古自治区成立十周年庆祝大会的国务院副总理李先念，今天下午乘专车离北京去呼和浩特。
    随同前往的有民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁（回族）、高等教育部副部长周建人、全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员田富达（高山族）。
    前往祝贺的还有新疆维吾尔自治区副主席伊敏诺夫（维吾尔族），西藏自治区筹备委员会委员索康·旺清格来（藏族），青海省副省长喜饶嘉错（藏族）、马辅臣（回族），甘肃省副省长黄正清（藏族），吉林省副省长朱德海（朝鲜族），以及各省的自治州、自治县的代表共五十三人，包括十九个民族成分。
    前往祝贺的还有各民族青年代表、各地工会代表和文化艺术部门的代表四十多人。
    到车站欢送的有国务院秘书长习仲勋等。
