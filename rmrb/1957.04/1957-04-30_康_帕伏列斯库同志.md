Title: Comrade Constantin Pîrvulescu
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 5

Comrade Constantin Pîrvulescu, Chairman of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Romanian Workers' Party, was born in Olănești-Vâlcea in 1895.

　　From the age of fourteen, he began working in public utility workshops, railways, and military arsenals in Bucharest and Iași.

　　In 1915, he began participating in the workers' movement. He participated in various activities, including the strike held at the Iași railway repair shop in April 1918.

　　Comrade Pîrvulescu joined the Romanian Communist Party in 1919 and was elected as a member of the Central Committee in 1929. He participated in the Central Committee's work to prepare for the August 23, 1944 event, and in 1945 he was elected as Chairman of the Control Commission of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party. In the 1946 parliamentary elections, he was elected as a representative, and in 1948, 1952, and 1957, he was re-elected as a representative.

　　For his underground work in overthrowing the fascist regime, his contribution to strengthening the Party and creating and consolidating the people's democratic system, and his achievements in the socialist construction of Romania, he was awarded the First Class Order for the Defense of the Fatherland, the First Class Order of the Star of the Republic, and was awarded the title of Hero of Socialist Labor.

### 康·帕伏列斯库同志

1957-04-30
第5版()
专栏：人物介绍

    康·帕伏列斯库同志
    罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席、罗马尼亚工人党政治局委员康·帕伏列斯库同志于1895年生于奥兰耐什提·渥尔卡。
    他从十四岁起，开始在布加勒斯特和雅西的公用事业附设工场、铁路、部队军火库工作。
    1915年他开始参加工人运动。他曾参加过各种活动，其中包括1918年4月雅西铁路修配厂举行的罢工。
    帕伏列斯库同志1919年参加罗马尼亚共产党，1929年当选为中央委员。他曾参加党中央准备1944年8月23日事件的工作，1945年当选为罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会监察委员会主席。1946年议会选举时，当选为代表，1948年，1952年，1957年均当选为代表。
    由于他在推翻法西斯制度中进行的地下工作，在加强党和缔造、巩固人民民主制度方面所作的贡献，在罗马尼亚社会主义建设中的功绩，曾荣获一级保卫祖国勋章，一级共和国星勋章，并获得社会主义劳动英雄称号。
