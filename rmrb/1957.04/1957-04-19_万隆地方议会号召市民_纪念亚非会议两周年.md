Title: Bandung Local Council Calls on Citizens to Commemorate Second Anniversary of Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 17th: Antara News Agency reported that the Bandung Transitional Local Council passed a resolution on the 16th, calling on Bandung citizens to hold commemorative activities for the second anniversary of the Asian-African Conference from April 18th to 24th.

### 万隆地方议会号召市民  纪念亚非会议两周年

1957-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    万隆地方议会号召市民
    纪念亚非会议两周年
    据新华社雅加达17日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，万隆过渡性地方议会16日通过决议，号召万隆市民从4月18日到24日举行亚非会议召开两周年的纪念活动。
