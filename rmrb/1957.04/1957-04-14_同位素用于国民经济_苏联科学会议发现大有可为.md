Title: Isotopes Used in the National Economy: Soviet Scientific Conference Finds Great Potential
Author:
Date: 1957-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: According to TASS reports, the Soviet scientific and technological conference on the application of radioactive isotopes, stable isotopes, and radiation in the national economy and science, which began on April 4th, closed on the 12th. The conference summarized the application of isotopes and radiation in various sectors of industrial and agricultural production, in medicine, and in science. The conference also discovered some new aspects and methods of utilizing isotopes and radiation in the national economy that have not yet been fully understood.

### 同位素用于国民经济  苏联科学会议发现大有可为

1957-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    同位素用于国民经济
    苏联科学会议发现大有可为
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社报道：4月4日开始的苏联关于在国民经济和科学中应用放射性同位素、稳定性同位素和射线的科学技术会议于12日闭幕。会议作出了在工业和农业生产各部门、在医疗和科学中应用同位素和射线的总结。会议也发现了一些至今还未充分了解的在国民经济中利用同位素和射线的新的方面和方法。
