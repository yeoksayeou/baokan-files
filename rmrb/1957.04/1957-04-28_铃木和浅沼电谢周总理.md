Title: Suzuki and Asanuma Send Telegrams Thanking Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. Suzuki Mosaburo, Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Japan Socialist Party, and Asanuma Inejirō, head of the Japan Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to China, sent a telegram from Tokyo today to Premier Zhou Enlai, thanking the Chinese side for the warm reception given to the Japan Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to China.
    The telegram also stated: "We firmly believe that this successful meeting will promote mutual understanding and will encourage our nationwide movement to restore relations between our two countries."

### 铃木和浅沼电谢周总理

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    铃木和浅沼电谢周总理
    新华社27日讯　日本社会党中央执行委员会委员长铃木茂三郎和日本社会党访华亲善使节团团长浅沼稻次郎，今天从东京打电报给周恩来总理，感谢中国方面对日本社会党访华亲善使节团的盛情接待。
    电报还说：“我们坚信，这次成功的会谈将促进相互的了解，并且将鼓舞我们在全国展开的争取恢复我们两国关系的运动。”
