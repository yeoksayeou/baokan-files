Title: Ceylon Education Minister Orders University of Ceylon to Investigate U.S. Professor's Espionage Activities
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, April 8th: Ceylon's Minister of Education, Dahanayake, has requested the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Ceylon to submit a report on the issue of certain American professors at the University of Ceylon collecting military intelligence. The investigation into the American professors' intelligence activities was initiated following a revelation made by a member of the Ceylon Parliament on April 2nd.

It is reported that an American professor took aerial photographs of Colombo, Gal Oya (a river in southeastern Ceylon where there are important water conservancy projects), and the Trincomalee naval base. It is said that this professor once worked in the "Map Section" of the U.S. Army.

It is also reported that another American attempted to obtain photographs of maps from the government survey bureau.

### 锡教育部长命令锡兰大学  调查美教授的间谍活动

1957-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    锡教育部长命令锡兰大学
    调查美教授的间谍活动
    新华社8日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰教育部长达哈纳亚克已经要求锡兰大学副校长就锡兰大学的某些美国教授收集军事情报问题提出报告。调查美国教授的情报活动这件事情是根据4月2日锡兰议会上一位议员的揭发而进行的。
    据报道，一位美国教授从空中拍摄了科伦坡、加耳沃亚（锡兰东南部的一条河流，在那里有重要的水利工程）以及亭可马里海军基地的照片。据说，这位教授曾经一度在美国陆军的“地图科”中任职。
    据报道，还有一个美国人企图得到政府测量局的地图的照片。
