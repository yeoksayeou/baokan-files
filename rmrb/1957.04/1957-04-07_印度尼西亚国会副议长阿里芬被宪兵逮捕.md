Title: Indonesian Deputy Speaker of Parliament Arifin Arrested by Military Police
Author:
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 6th: According to a report by the Indonesian News Agency today, Zainul Arifin, First Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament and an important figure in the Nahdatul Ulama (Islamic Teachers Association), has been arrested by the military police.

Arifin was arrested at his residence in Jakarta last night and later taken to Bandung. The reason for his arrest is still unknown.

A week ago, the military police authorities summoned Arifin for questioning regarding the activities of the Waras Company. Arifin is a director of this company.

### 印度尼西亚国会副议长阿里芬被宪兵逮捕

1957-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会副议长阿里芬被宪兵逮捕
    新华社雅加达6日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社今天报道，印度尼西亚国会第一副议长、伊斯兰教师联合会的重要人物蔡努耳·阿里芬已经被宪兵所逮捕。
    阿里芬是昨天晚上在雅加达他的寓所中被捕的，后来被带到万隆去。到现在还不知道他被捕的原因。
    在一个星期以前，宪兵当局曾经就瓦拉斯公司的活动问题传讯了阿里芬。阿里芬是这家公司的董事。
