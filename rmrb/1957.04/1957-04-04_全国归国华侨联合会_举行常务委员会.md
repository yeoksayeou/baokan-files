Title: All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Holds Standing Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese recently held its third Standing Committee meeting.

The meeting expressed its support for the resolutions passed by the Third Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). It was decided to notify the Overseas Chinese Federations at all levels to promptly organize members, returned overseas Chinese, and relatives of overseas Chinese to study the "Political Resolution" and the "Resolution on Increasing Production and Practicing Economy" passed by this CPPCC meeting, and to conscientiously implement them.

At the meeting, in-depth discussions were held on the work of the Overseas Chinese Federations in various regions, and the 1957 work outline for the Overseas Chinese Federations at all levels was unanimously approved. The Federations were called upon to vigorously assist the Party and the government in doing a good job in all aspects of overseas Chinese affairs, and to further serve the great construction cause of the socialist motherland and the broad masses of overseas Chinese, returned overseas Chinese, and relatives of overseas Chinese.

### 全国归国华侨联合会  举行常务委员会

1957-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全国归国华侨联合会
    举行常务委员会
    新华社3日讯　中华全国归国华侨联合会在最近举行了第三次常务委员会。
    会议对中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第三次会议所通过的决议表示拥护。并且作出决定，通知各级侨联及时组织会员及归侨、侨眷学习这次政协大会所通过的“政治决议”和“关于增产节约问题的决议”，认真贯彻。
    会上，对各地侨联的工作问题曾经进行了深入的讨论，并一致通过了1957年各级侨联的工作纲要，号召各级侨联大力协助党和政府做好各项侨务工作，进一步为社会主义祖国的伟大建设事业与广大华侨、归侨和侨眷服务。
