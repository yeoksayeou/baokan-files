Title: Arab Trade Union International Confederation Condemns French Atrocities in Algeria; Chairman of Moroccan National Consultative Assembly Advocates Replacing Lacoste
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 8th -- The Arab Trade Union International Confederation issued a statement here during Algeria Week, condemning the French suppression of the Algerian national movement. The statement said that using NATO weapons to suppress the Algerian liberation movement is a violation of the freedom and human rights of the people and a breach of the United Nations Charter.

The Confederation called on all Arab countries and all trade unions and labor organizations around the world to demand that their governments swiftly adopt a united attitude towards France's barbaric actions in Algeria. The Confederation called on world public opinion to support the Algerian people.

The statement also condemned the unreasonable arrest of the secretary of the Algerian Workers' Union.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- New York News: Medi Ben Barka, Chairman of the Moroccan National Consultative Assembly, who is visiting the United States, said in New York on the evening of the 5th that replacing the French Resident Minister in Algeria, Lacoste, should be the first step in France's expression of at least minimal trust in the UN's demand for a peaceful resolution of the Algerian issue. Ben Barka condemned Lacoste for turning Algeria into a veritable "wild west." He said that French military police are indiscriminately killing ordinary people.

### 阿拉伯工会国际联合会  谴责法国在阿尔及利亚暴行  摩国民谘询议会主席主张撤换拉戈斯特

1957-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯工会国际联合会
    谴责法国在阿尔及利亚暴行
    摩国民谘询议会主席主张撤换拉戈斯特
    新华社开罗8日电　阿拉伯工会国际联合会在举行阿尔及利亚周的时候在这里发表声明，谴责法国对阿尔及利亚民族运动的镇压。声明说，使用北大西洋公约组织的武器镇压阿尔及利亚的解放运动是对人民的自由和人权的侵犯，并且是对联合国宪章的破坏。
    联合会呼吁各阿拉伯国家和全世界所有工会和劳工组织要求它们的政府对法国在阿尔及利亚的野蛮行为迅速采取一致态度。联合会要求世界舆论支持阿尔及利亚人民。
    声明还谴责了对阿尔及利亚工人联合会书记的无理逮捕。
    据新华社8日讯　纽约消息：正在美国访问的摩洛哥国民谘询议会主席麦迪·本·巴尔卡5日晚间在纽约发表谈话，主张撤换法国在阿尔及利亚的驻节部长拉戈斯特，作为法国对联合国要求的和平解决阿尔及利亚问题表示起码信任的第一步。本·巴尔卡谴责拉戈斯特正在把阿尔及利亚变作一个真正的“蛮荒地带”。他说，法国军警正在乱杀一般的老百姓。
