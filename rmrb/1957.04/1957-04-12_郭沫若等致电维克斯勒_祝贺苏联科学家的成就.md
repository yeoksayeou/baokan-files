Title: Guo Moruo and Others Send a Telegram to Veksler Congratulating Soviet Scientists on Their Achievements
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhao Zhongyao and Peng Huanwu, Deputy Directors, today sent telegrams to the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in Moscow and Academician Veksler, the head of the synchrophasotron, congratulating them on the great achievement of the commencement of operation of the world's largest synchrophasotron built by the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research.

### 郭沫若等致电维克斯勒  祝贺苏联科学家的成就

1957-04-12
第7版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等致电维克斯勒
    祝贺苏联科学家的成就
    新华社11日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若，中国科学院物理研究所所长钱三强、副所长赵忠尧和彭桓武，今天分别打电报给莫斯科联合原子核研究所和同步稳相加速器的主持人维克斯勒通讯院士，祝贺联合原子核研究所建立的世界最大的同步稳相加速器开始运转这一巨大成就。
