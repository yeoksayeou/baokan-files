Title: Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists to Hold Beijing Meeting Today; Deng Tuo Hosts Banquet for Representatives and Guests from Fourteen Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. The Beijing meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists will be held in Beijing on the 3rd.

This meeting will discuss issues such as the work of the Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists and strengthening international ties between journalists.

The Vice President of the International Organization of Journalists, Daniel Kraminov (Soviet Union), and representatives from various countries attending the meeting: Boraghi György, Editor-in-Chief of "Magyar Nemzet" (Hungary), Csátár György, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Népszabadság," Vojtěch Dolejší, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Journalists' Association and Editor-in-Chief of the Czechoslovak "Tvorba" magazine, Štefan Sabo, Editor-in-Chief of the "Brouk" comic magazine, and Nikolai Potapov, Chairman of the Central House of Journalists of the Soviet Union, have also arrived in Beijing successively.

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. Prominent figures from the press in fourteen countries gathered in Beijing tonight with Chinese journalists. Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, on behalf of Chinese journalists, extended a warm welcome to the representatives and guests from various countries who came to Beijing to attend the Beijing meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists.

At the welcoming banquet, Deng Tuo said in his speech that when you come to Beijing, spring also comes to Beijing, and spring is warmed by your arrival. He raised his glass to congratulate the growing development of friendly cooperation among journalists from all countries.

Jean-Maurice Hermann, Chairman of the International Organization of Journalists, expressed his gratitude in his speech. He said that in the past, a European scientist hoped to have a small house to entertain his best friends, but your (China's) house is very large, including rivers, plains, and mountains, but it is also impossible to accommodate all your friends in the world. He said, "I am one of your best friends."

Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, on behalf of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee and the people of Beijing, extended a warm welcome at the banquet to the holding of the meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists in Beijing and to the arrival of journalists from various countries in Beijing.

Among those invited to the banquet tonight were Hermann (France), Chairman of the International Organization of Journalists, Vice Presidents Mrs. Rydberg (Finland), Kraminov (Soviet Union), Secretary-General Knobloch (Czechoslovakia), Deputy Secretary-General Pronin (Soviet Union), and representatives from the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Hungary, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, Vietnam, Mexico, and guests from Egypt.

Present as hosts were the Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, Chinese representatives attending the Beijing meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Organization of Journalists, the heads of Beijing newspapers, periodicals, news agencies, radio stations, and many other journalists.

### 国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会  北京会议定于今日举行  邓拓欢宴十四个国家的代表和来宾

1957-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会
    北京会议定于今日举行
    邓拓欢宴十四个国家的代表和来宾
    新华社2日讯　国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会北京会议将于3日在北京举行。
    这次会议将讨论关于国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会的工作和加强新闻工作者的国际联系等问题。
    国际新闻工作者协会副主席达尼埃尔·克拉米诺夫（苏联）和参加会议的各国代表：“匈牙利民族报”总编辑波腊吉·捷尔吉、“人民自由报”副总编辑洽塔尔·捷尔吉，捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者协会主席、捷克斯洛伐克“创造”杂志总编辑伏依特赫·多列依希、“甲虫”漫画杂志总编辑斯特凡·萨博，苏联中央新闻工作者之家主席尼克拉·波塔波夫，也都已陆续到达北京。
    新华社2日讯　十四个国家的新闻界知名人士，今晚在北京同中国新闻界人士欢聚一堂。中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓代表中国新闻工作者，对前来北京参加国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会北京会议的各国代表和来宾表示热烈欢迎。
    在这个欢迎宴会上，邓拓致词说，当你们来到北京的时候，春天也来到了北京，春天因你们的来到而增加了温暖。他举杯祝贺各国新闻界友好合作事业的日益发展。
    国际新闻工作者协会主席让·莫里斯·埃尔曼致词表示感谢。他说，从前欧洲有一个科学家希望有一幢小房子来招待他最好的朋友，而你们（中国）的房子却非常大，它包括了大江、平原和高山，可是它也不可能把你们在世界上所有的朋友容纳下。他说：“我就是你们最好朋友中的一个。”
    北京市副市长张友渔，在宴会上代表北京市人民委员会和北京人民，对国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会会议在北京举行和各国新闻界朋友来到北京表示热烈欢迎。
    今晚应邀出席宴会的，有国际新闻工作者协会主席埃尔曼（法国）、副主席里德堡夫人（芬兰）、克拉米诺夫（苏联）、秘书长克诺布洛赫（捷克斯洛伐克）、副秘书长普罗宁（苏联）和德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、匈牙利、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、越南、墨西哥等国的代表，以及埃及的来宾。
    出席作陪的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长，出席国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会北京会议的中国代表，北京各报刊和通讯社、广播电台的负责人以及新闻界人士多人。
