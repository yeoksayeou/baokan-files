Title: Polish Government Delegation Leaves Guangzhou, Passes Through Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 7th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, left Guangzhou by plane at 8:00 this morning for Beijing.

Governor Tao Zhu of Guangdong Province, Mayor Zhu Guang of Guangzhou, Commander Liang Xingchu of the Guangzhou Garrison, as well as responsible persons from Party and government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, all went to the airport today to see off the distinguished Polish guests.

After arriving in Guangzhou yesterday, the distinguished Polish guests visited the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs' Cemetery to pay respects to the martyrs' tombs. Cyrankiewicz and delegation member, Polish Minister of Culture and Art Kuryluk, laid wreaths at the martyrs' tombs. Subsequently, the distinguished guests visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute and also toured the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, Yuexiu Mountain, and other places.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 7th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, made a short stop in Wuhan today while en route.

The Wuhan airport was decorated with the national flags of China and Poland and many colorful flags. At 10:45 a.m., Chairman Cyrankiewicz and his wife, as well as members of the Polish government delegation, stepped off the plane. They were warmly welcomed by Governor Zhang Tixue of Hubei Province and his wife, First Secretary Wang Renzhong of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Vice Mayor Wang Kewen of Wuhan, First Secretary Song Kanfu of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Commander Kong Qingde of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan, as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations.

After having lunch and taking a short rest in the airport restaurant, Chairman Cyrankiewicz and the delegation he led continued their flight to Beijing at 12:00 noon.

### 波兰政府代表团离广州过武汉情况

1957-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团离广州过武汉情况
    据新华社广州7日电　以波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，在今天早晨八时乘飞机离开广州前往北京。
    广东省省长陶铸，广州市市长朱光，广州驻军首长梁兴初中将以及广东省、广州市党政机关、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，今天都到机场欢送波兰贵宾。
    波兰贵宾们昨天到达广州后，曾到广州公社烈士陵园参谒烈士陵墓，西伦凯维兹和代表团团员、波兰文化艺术部部长库里卢克在烈士墓前献了花圈。随后，贵宾们参观了农民运动讲习所旧址，还参观游览了中山纪念堂、越秀山等处。
    据新华社武汉7日电　以波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团今天途经武汉时，曾作了短时间的停留。
    武汉机场上飘扬着中波两国国旗和许多彩旗。上午十时四十五分，西伦凯维兹主席和夫人以及波兰政府代表团人员走下飞机，他们受到了湖北省省长张体学和夫人，中共湖北省委员会第一书记王任重，武汉市副市长王克文，中共武汉市委员会第一书记宋侃夫，中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长孔庆德中将以及各民主党派、人民团体负责人的热烈欢迎。
    在机场餐厅用了午餐和稍事休息以后，西伦凯维兹主席和他率领的代表团在中午十二时继续乘飞机赴北京。
