Title: Premier Zhou Pays a Return Visit to Inejiro Asanuma
Author:
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai paid a return visit to the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission headed by Inejiro Asanuma at the Beijing Hotel tonight, engaging in a cordial conversation for an hour and a half. Also present at the talks were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Qiao Guanhua, Vice President; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; and Liao Chengzhi, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 周总理回拜浅沼稻次郎

1957-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理回拜浅沼稻次郎
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理在今晚到北京饭店回拜了以浅沼稻次郎为首的日本社会党访华亲善使节团，畅谈了一小时半。谈话时在座的还有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员廖承志等人。
