Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Canadian Labour Progressive Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party sent a telegram congratulating the Sixth National Congress of the Labour Progressive Party of Canada, which will be held on April 19th. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: To all comrades of the Sixth National Congress of the Labour Progressive Party of Canada:
	The Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the Sixth National Congress of the Labour Progressive Party of Canada.
	The Communist Party of China, with great sympathy, is concerned with the achievements made by the Labour Progressive Party of Canada in its long and difficult struggle, and wishes you continuous new and greater achievements on the road of advancement indicated by Marxism-Leninism, in safeguarding the daily interests of the working people, and in the struggle for peace, democracy and socialism. Congratulations on the success of your Congress!
			The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, April 16, 1957

### 中共中央电贺加拿大劳工进步党代表大会

1957-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺加拿大劳工进步党代表大会
    新华社18日讯　中共中央致电祝贺将在4月19日召开的加拿大劳工进步党第六次全国代表大会。贺电全文如下：加拿大劳工进步党第六次全国代表大会全体同志们：
    中国共产党向加拿大劳工进步党第六次全国代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    中国共产党抱着极大的同情，关怀着加拿大劳工进步党在长期的困难的斗争中所取得的成就，并且祝你们在沿着马克思列宁主义所指示的前进道路上，在维护劳动人民的日常利益中，在为和平、民主和社会主义事业的斗争中，不断获得新的更大的成就。祝贺你们大会的成功！
                        中国共产党中央委员会　1957年4月16日
