Title: Soviet Newspapers Prominently Publish News of Voroshilov's Activities in China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 5

    Soviet Newspapers Prominently Publish News of Voroshilov's Activities in China
    (Our Moscow Dispatch, April 19th) The Soviet people heard on the radio on the 18th the report of the mass rally in Beijing welcoming Chairman Voroshilov, and on the 19th they read this exciting report in the newspapers. "Pravda" and "Izvestia" devoted most of the front and second pages to the enthusiastic situation of the mass rally, while "Red Star", "Soviet Russia", "Trud" and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" described the enthusiastic situation of the mass rally in a prominent position on the front page.

### 苏联各报以显著地位  刊登伏老在我国活动新闻

1957-04-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联各报以显著地位
    刊登伏老在我国活动新闻
    本报莫斯科19日电　苏联人民18日在广播中听到北京欢迎伏罗希洛夫主席的群众大会的报道，19日在报纸上又读到这个令人兴奋的报道。“真理报”和“消息报”以头版和二版的大部分篇幅，“红星报”、“苏维埃俄罗斯报”、“劳动报”、
  “共青团真理报”以头版的显著地位叙述了群众大会的热烈情形。
