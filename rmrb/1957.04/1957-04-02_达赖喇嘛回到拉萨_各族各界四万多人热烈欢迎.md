Title: The Dalai Lama Returns to Lhasa, Over 40,000 People from All Ethnic Groups and All Walks of Life Give a Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 1st. Today, more than 40,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lhasa grandly welcomed the Dalai Lama's return to Lhasa. The welcoming procession stretched for several kilometers, from in front of the Drepung Monastery in the western suburbs all the way to the Norbulingka.

When the Dalai Lama arrived at the reception hall, Lieutenant General Tan Guansan, member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Work Committee, and Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and Major General Chen Mingyi, Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region, presented hadas to the Dalai Lama and accompanied the Dalai Lama in reviewing the honor guard. Tibetan and Han cadres of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and officials of the Tibetan local government were all here to greet the Dalai Lama.

After reviewing the honor guard, the Dalai Lama entered the reception hall to rest.

The welcoming ceremony was held in a newly built circular tent in the reception hall. Lieutenant General Tan Guansan delivered a speech, in which he, on behalf of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the CPC Tibet Work Committee, and all the staff and officers and soldiers of the Tibet Military Region, warmly welcomed the Dalai Lama and all accompanying officials, and extended cordial greetings to him. The Dalai Lama spoke here to all Tibetan and Han cadres and monks and laypeople. In his speech, he recounted his and the Panchen Erdeni's invitation to attend the 2,500th anniversary of the Nirvana of Sakyamuni in India and his visit to India. The Dalai Lama finally instructed everyone to study diligently, strengthen unity, and work together to build a prosperous and happy Tibet.

Before the speech, the Dalai Lama had accepted the worship ceremony of Tibetan officials and lamas in another large tent surrounded by yellow cloth walls.

After the welcoming ceremony, the Dalai Lama rode in a palanquin through the welcoming procession of Tibetan and Han cadres, troops, and monks and laypeople, and arrived at his summer palace, Norbulingka, to rest.

Also present at the reception hall to welcome the Dalai Lama were: Mr. Cai Boer, Acting Consul General of the Consulate General of the Republic of India in Lhasa, and Mr. Yu Dagong Bixing and Mr. Naride Badu of the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 达赖喇嘛回到拉萨  各族各界四万多人热烈欢迎

1957-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛回到拉萨
    各族各界四万多人热烈欢迎
    新华社拉萨1日电　今天拉萨各族各界四万多人隆重欢迎达赖喇嘛返抵拉萨。欢迎行列长达几公里，从西郊哲蚌寺前一直排到罗布林卡。
    当达赖喇嘛到达接官厅时，西藏自治区筹备委员会委员、中共西藏工委副书记、人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三中将、西藏军区副司令员陈明义少将，向达赖喇嘛献哈达，并陪同达赖喇嘛检阅了仪仗队。西藏自治区筹备委员会的藏、汉族干部和西藏地方政府的官员，都在这里迎接达赖喇嘛。
    检阅仪仗队后，达赖喇嘛便进入接官厅休息。
    欢迎仪式在接官厅里新搭的圆形帐篷里举行。谭冠三中将致词，他代表西藏自治区筹备委员会、中共西藏工委和西藏军区的全体工作人员和全体军官、士兵，热烈欢迎达赖喇嘛和全体随行官员，并向他致亲切的慰问。达赖喇嘛在这里向全体藏、汉干部和僧俗人民讲话。他在讲话中叙述了他和班禅额尔德尼应邀去印度参加释迦牟尼涅槃二千五百周年纪念大会和在印度访问的情况。达赖喇嘛最后指示大家必须努力学习，加强团结，为建设繁荣幸福的西藏而共同努力。
    达赖喇嘛在讲话前，曾经在另一个绕有黄布围墙的大帐篷里接受了藏族官员和喇嘛们的朝拜的仪式。
    在欢迎仪式举行后，达赖喇嘛乘轿通过藏、汉干部、部队和僧俗人民的欢迎行列，到达他的夏宫罗布林卡休息。
    到接官厅欢迎达赖喇嘛的还有：印度共和国驻拉萨总领事馆代理总领事蔡伯尔，尼泊尔王国的宇大公必兴先生和纳里德巴都先生。
