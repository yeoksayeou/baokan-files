Title: Japanese Communist Party Decides to Launch Movement to Abolish the U.S.-Japan Treaty
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th dispatch: Japanese Communist Party First Secretary Sanzo Nosaka and Central Committee member Yoshio Shiga announced yesterday at a press conference held in Osaka that the Japanese Communist Party will launch a nationwide movement demanding the abolition of the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and the "Administrative Agreement."
    Nosaka and Shiga told reporters that a signature campaign demanding the abolition of these two unequal treaties has already begun among scholars and intellectuals. They said that in this supra-party national movement, they will also oppose the British hydrogen bomb test to be held on Christmas Island, and oppose the Japanese government's punishment of workers participating in the spring struggle to demand improved living standards.

### 日本共产党决定  展开废除日美条约运动

1957-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    日本共产党决定
    展开废除日美条约运动
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本共产党第一书记野坂参三和中央委员会委员志贺义雄昨天在大阪举行的记者招待会上宣布，日本共产党将要展开一个全国性的要求废除日美“安全条约”和“行政协定”的运动。
    野坂和志贺告诉记者们说，现在已经在学者和知识分子中间展开了要求废除这两个不平等条约的签名运动。他们说，在这个超党派的全国性运动中，还要反对英国将在圣诞岛举行的氢弹试验，并且反对日本政府处分参加要求改善生活待遇的春季斗争的工人。
