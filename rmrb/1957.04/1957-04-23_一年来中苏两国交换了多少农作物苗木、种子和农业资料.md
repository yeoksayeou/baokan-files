Title: How many crop seedlings, seeds, and agricultural materials did China and the Soviet Union exchange in the past year?
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-23
Page: 4

Column: Small Statistics

How many crop seedlings, seeds, and agricultural materials did China and the Soviet Union exchange in the past year?
(April 1956 – April 1957)
China supplied to the Soviet Union:
        Seeds: 2,318 kinds, 173,072 grams
        Seedlings (grafts): 117 kinds, 3,200 plants (branches)
        Materials: 11 kinds
The Soviet Union supplied to China:
        Seeds: 95 kinds, 18,204 grams
        Seedlings: 8 kinds, 64,000 branches
        Materials: 275 kinds
