Title: American Criminal Grewse Departs China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th -- American criminal Grewse, who was released by us on March 28th, departed China today from Shanghai on the British ship "Glenorchy."

### 美国犯人格娄斯出境

1957-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美国犯人格娄斯出境
    新华社上海5日电　3月28日被我释放的美国犯人格娄斯，今天已在上海乘英国轮船“格林诺采”号出境。
