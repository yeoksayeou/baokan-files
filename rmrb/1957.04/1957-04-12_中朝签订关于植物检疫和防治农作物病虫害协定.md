Title: China and North Korea Sign Agreement on Plant Quarantine and Prevention of Crop Pests and Diseases
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 10th. The agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on plant quarantine and prevention and control of crop pests and diseases was signed in Pyongyang today.
    Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, signed on behalf of China, and Li Yongshuo, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of North Korea, signed on behalf of North Korea.

### 中朝签订关于植物检疫和防治农作物病虫害协定

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    中朝签订关于植物检疫和防治农作物病虫害协定
    新华社平壤10日电　中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府关于植物检疫和防治农作物病虫害的协定今天在平壤签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，代表朝鲜方面签字的是朝鲜农业省副相李勇硕。
