Title: Pyongyang Hosts State Banquet, Korean and Polish Government Leaders Jointly Toast Eternal Friendship

Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th April: The Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, hosted a grand banquet on the evening of the 15th to receive the government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Cyrankiewicz.

Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, heads of various departments of the Korean Party and Government, and representatives of various democratic parties and social organizations attended the banquet.

Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, China, and other countries stationed in Korea were also invited to attend the banquet.

Premier Kim Il-sung spoke first at the banquet, saying that the Korean people cherish the friendship between the Korean and Polish people and regard strengthening friendly solidarity with all countries of the socialist camp as their important duty.

Kim Il-sung said that the visit of the Polish government delegation to Korea will help the people of the two countries further develop fraternal friendship and economic and cultural cooperation.

Next, Chairman Cyrankiewicz spoke at the banquet. He said that the Polish people have always supported the Korean people's struggle against aggression and for freedom. This struggle of the Korean people is not only a struggle for the freedom of their motherland, but also a struggle to safeguard the common interests of the socialist camp. Cyrankiewicz congratulated the Korean people on their greater achievements in peaceful construction.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th April: The head of the Polish government delegation and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, hosted a reception tonight for the delegation's visit to Korea.

Invited to attend the banquet were Premier of the Korean Cabinet Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Kim Tu-bong, Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Hong Myung-hee, Kim Il, Park Ui-wan, Jung Joon-taek, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, heads of various democratic political parties and social organizations, and senior generals of the People's Army. Envoys from various countries stationed in Korea also attended the banquet.

Chairman Cyrankiewicz and Premier Kim Il-sung spoke separately at the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of harmony and friendship.

### 平壤举行国宴  朝波政府领导人同祝永好

1957-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    平壤举行国宴
    朝波政府领导人同祝永好
    新华社平壤16日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成15日晚间举行盛大宴会，招待以西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁副首相崔庸健、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱和朝鲜党政各部门负责人以及各民主党派、社会团体的代表出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有苏联、中国和其他国家驻朝鲜的外交使节。
    金日成首相首先在宴会上讲话说，朝鲜人民珍视朝波两国人民之间的友谊，并且把加强与社会主义阵营各国的友好团结作为自己的重要义务。
    金日成说，波兰政府代表团这次到朝鲜访问，将有助于两国人民进一步发展兄弟般友谊和经济、文化的合作关系。
    接着，西伦凯维兹主席在宴会上讲了话。他说，波兰人民一贯支持朝鲜人民反对侵略、争取自由的斗争。朝鲜人民的这个斗争不仅是争取自己祖国自由的斗争，也是维护社会主义阵营共同利益的斗争。西伦凯维兹祝贺朝鲜人民在和平建设中取得更大的成就。
    新华社平壤16日电　波兰政府代表团团长、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹今天晚上为代表团访问朝鲜举行招待宴会。
    应邀参加宴会的有朝鲜内阁首相金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相崔庸健、洪命熹、金一、朴义琓、郑准泽，劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满，外务相南日，各民主政党和社会团体负责人，人民军高级将领等。参加宴会的还有各国驻朝使节。
    西伦凯维兹主席和金日成首相在宴会上分别讲了话。
    宴会在融洽友好的气氛中进行。
