Title: Asian Student Technology Conference Concludes

Author:

Date: 1957-04-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st. The Asian Student Technology Conference closed in Tokyo yesterday.

　　The conference opened on March 14th. During the conference, student representatives from China, Cambodia, India, Indonesia, Pakistan, Thailand, and Japan formed a tour group that visited factories in Osaka, Kobe, Hiroshima, Yahata, Kurashiki, Kyoto, and other places, and held meetings to discuss industrial technology issues.

### 亚洲学生技术会议闭幕

1957-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    亚洲学生技术会议闭幕
    据新华社1日讯　东京消息：亚洲学生技术会议昨天在东京举行了闭幕式。
    会议是在3月14日开幕的。在会议期间，中国、柬埔寨、印度、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、泰国和日本等国家的学生代表组成参观旅行团，参观了大阪、神户、广岛、八幡、仓敷、京都等地的工厂，并且举行会议，讨论了工业技术问题。
