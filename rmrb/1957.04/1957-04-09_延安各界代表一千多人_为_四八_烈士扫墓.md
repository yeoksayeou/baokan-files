Title: More Than 1,000 Representatives From All Circles in Yan'an Sweep Tombs for "April 8th" Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 8th - More than 1,000 representatives from all circles in Yan'an today swept tombs for the "April 8th" martyrs, commemorating the eleventh anniversary of the martyrdom of Comrades Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Deng Fa, Ye Ting, and Huang Qisheng.

　　At 11:00 a.m., Yang Cunfu, Commissioner of the Yan'an Commissioner's Office and chief mourner, held a memorial ceremony in front of the martyrs' spirits and presented wreaths. Afterwards, all the representatives lined up and circled the tombs, and added soil to the martyrs' graves.

　　Around the Qingming Festival, cadres and students from various Yan'an agencies also separately paid respects to the tombs of the deceased CPC Central Committee member Zhang Hao, former editor-in-chief of the *Liberation Daily* Yang Song, and the "Heroic Little Eighth Route Army Soldier" Xu Zhiyuan, and planted trees in front of the tombs. Representatives from all circles in Zhidan County also went to the Liu Zhidan Comrade Mausoleum to sweep the tomb and planted flowers and plants in the park.

### 延安各界代表一千多人  为“四八”烈士扫墓

1957-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    延安各界代表一千多人
    为“四八”烈士扫墓
    新华社延安8日电　延安各界代表一千多人今天为“四八”烈士扫墓，纪念王若飞、秦邦宪、邓发、叶挺、黄齐生诸烈士殉难十一周年。
    上午十一时，主祭人、延安专员公署专员杨存福在烈士灵前举行了祭奠仪式，并献了花圈。随后，全体代表列队绕墓一周，并为烈士坟墓添土。
    清明节前后，延安各机关的干部和学生们还分别祭扫了已故中共中央委员张浩、前解放日报总编辑杨松及“英雄小八路”许智远等先烈的墓，并在墓前植了树。志丹县各界代表也前往刘志丹同志陵园扫墓，并在园内种植花草。
