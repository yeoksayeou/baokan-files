Title: Yunnan Provincial Military and Political Leaders Visit Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, April 11th: At the invitation of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu to pay a friendly visit to Burma, Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, and his wife, and General Xie Fuzhi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, left Kunming by special plane this morning for Rangoon. They will celebrate the Water Splashing Festival with the Burmese people.

The Yunnan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe also traveled with them and will perform in Burma. Some members of the troupe arrived in Rangoon on April 9th and 10th.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 11th: Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, and Xie Fuzhi, head of the Yunnan garrison, and their entourage arrived in Rangoon by plane from Kunming at noon today. They are visiting the Union of Burma at the invitation of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu.

Among those welcoming the Chinese guests at the airport were Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, Daw Aung San, Minister of Culture U Tun Tin, Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming.

Prime Minister U Nu made a brief speech at the airport, saying that he would treat the Chinese guests like his relatives and that he hoped they would enjoy participating in the Water Splashing Festival. Prime Minister U Nu believes that their visit to the Union of Burma will further strengthen Sino-Burmese friendship.

Acting Governor Liu Minghui said in reply that because of the development and consolidation of traditional Sino-Burmese friendship on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence announced by the two prime ministers, the relationship between the people of Yunnan, China, and the people of Burma is becoming closer and closer like that between relatives. Liu Minghui said that our ability to celebrate the Burmese Water Splashing Festival with the Burmese people in the Union of Burma will be an unforgettable event in the history of Sino-Burmese friendship.

Acting Governor Liu Minghui and General Xie Fuzhi are staying at the Presidential Palace during their stay in Rangoon.

The Yunnan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe, led by Peng Hua, arrived in Rangoon on April 10. The song and dance troupe was also invited by Prime Minister U Nu to visit the Union of Burma and participate in the Burmese Water Splashing Festival.

### 云南省军政领导人赴缅甸访问

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    云南省军政领导人赴缅甸访问
    新华社昆明11日电　应缅甸联邦吴努总理邀请到缅甸作友好访问的云南省代省长刘明辉夫妇和中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长谢富治上将，今天上午由昆明乘专机前往仰光。他们将和缅甸人民一起欢度泼水节。
    云南省歌舞团也随同前往，他们将在缅甸作访问演出，部分团员已经在4月9、10两日分别到达仰光。
    新华社仰光11日电　中国云南省代省长刘明辉和云南驻军首长谢富治以及他们的随行人员已经在今天中午乘飞机从昆明到达仰光。他们应缅甸总理吴努的邀请前来缅甸联邦访问。
    在机场欢迎中国客人的有吴努总理和他的夫人、昂山夫人、文化部长吴吞丁、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
    吴努总理在机场发表简短讲话说，他将像款待他的亲戚那样款待中国客人，并且使他们在泼水节期间很愉快地参加泼水。吴努总理认为，他们对缅甸联邦的访问将进一步加强中缅友谊。
    刘明辉代省长致答词说，由于中缅传统友谊在两国总理宣布的和平共处五项原则的基础上得到发展和巩固，中国云南人民和缅甸人民之间的关系像亲戚一样愈来愈密切。刘明辉说，我们能够在缅甸联邦同缅甸人民一起欢度缅甸泼水节，这将是中缅友谊史上难忘的一件事。
    刘明辉代省长和谢富治将军在仰光逗留期间住在总统府。
    由彭华率领的云南省歌舞团在4月10日到达仰光。歌舞团也是应吴努总理邀请访问缅甸联邦和参加缅甸泼水节的。
