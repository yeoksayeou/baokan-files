Title: Representatives of Germany and Romania Reach Consensus on Current International Issues and Issues Related to Both Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 26th - According to a report by the German News Agency: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic and the government delegation of Romania began talks on the afternoon of the 24th at the Prime Minister's residence in Berlin.

The two sides discussed the current international issues and issues related to the two countries, and reached a complete consensus. The two sides instructed their respective foreign ministers to draft documents based on the agreements already reached for approval by both sides.

On the German side, those participating in the talks included Prime Minister Grotewohl, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz, and others. On the Romanian side, those participating included Chairman of the Council of Ministers Stoica, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Foreign Minister Preoteasa, and others.

### 就目前国际问题和两国有关问题  德罗两国代表会谈意见一致

1957-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    就目前国际问题和两国有关问题
    德罗两国代表会谈意见一致
    新华社柏林26日电　据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国政府代表团和罗马尼亚政府代表团24日下午在柏林总理官邸开始会谈。
    双方讨论了目前的国际问题和同两国有关的问题，并且取得了完全一致的意见。双方责成两国的外交部长根据已经取得的协议草拟文件以待双方批准。
    参加会谈的德国方面有总理格罗提渥、统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希、副总理兼外交部长博尔茨等，罗马尼亚方面有部长会议主席斯托伊卡、工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治、外交部长普利奥蒂亚萨等。
