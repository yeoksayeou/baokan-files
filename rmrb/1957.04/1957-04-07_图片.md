Hubei Province, Mianyang County's cotton farmers are actively selling their surplus cotton to the state to support yarn mill production. This is Yuanjiakou Wharf in Mianyang County, where a large amount of cotton is being loaded onto ships, ready to be transported to Wuhan and other places.
