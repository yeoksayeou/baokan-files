Title: Indonesia to Send Military Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 5

    Indonesia
    To Send Military Delegation to Visit China
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 15th: Indonesian Army Deputy Chief of Staff Subroto revealed that Indonesia will send a military delegation to visit China.
    The military delegation, led by Subroto, will depart from Jakarta for China in early May.

### 印度尼西亚  将派军事代表团访问我国

1957-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚
    将派军事代表团访问我国
    新华社雅加达15日电　印度尼西亚陆军副参谋长苏布罗图透露，印度尼西亚将派军事代表团到中国进行访问。
    由苏布罗图率领的这个军事代表团将在5月初从雅加达启程前往中国。
