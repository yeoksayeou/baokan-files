Title: U.S. Decides to Dump Cotton to Exert Pressure on Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 5th. TASS reported from New York on the 4th: The "New York Herald Tribune" reported on the 3rd that the United States has decided to sell 300,000 bales of American cotton at dumping prices as a form of pressure on Egypt.

The "New York Herald Tribune" pointed out that U.S. officials said that widespread promotion of U.S. cotton "could undermine Egypt's economy."

### 美国决定抛售棉花  借以对埃及施压力

1957-04-06
第6版()
专栏：

    美国决定抛售棉花  借以对埃及施压力
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社纽约4日讯：“纽约先驱论坛报”3日报道，美国决定以抛售价格出售美国存棉三十万包，作为对埃及的一种压力。
    “纽约先驱论坛报”指出，美国官方人士说，广泛推销美国棉花“可以破坏埃及的经济”。
