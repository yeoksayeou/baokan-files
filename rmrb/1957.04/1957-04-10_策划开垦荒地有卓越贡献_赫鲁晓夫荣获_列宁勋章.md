Title: Khrushchev Awarded the "Order of Lenin" for Outstanding Contributions to Planning and Developing Wasteland
Author:
Date: 1957-04-10
Page: 5

    Khrushchev Awarded the "Order of Lenin" for Outstanding Contributions to Planning and Developing Wasteland
    
    Moscow Dispatch, April 9th: The First Secretary of the Soviet Communist Party Central Committee, Khrushchev, was awarded the "Order of Lenin" and a second "Hammer and Sickle" Gold Medal yesterday by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR for his outstanding achievements in planning and implementing the reclamation of wasteland. Local newspapers today published photos of Khrushchev and the decree.
    According to Soviet regulations, receiving the "Hammer and Sickle" Gold Medal also entails receiving the title of Hero of Socialist Labor. Khrushchev was first awarded the title of Hero of Socialist Labor in April 1954, on the occasion of his sixtieth birthday.

### 策划开垦荒地有卓越贡献  赫鲁晓夫荣获“列宁勋章”

1957-04-10
第5版()
专栏：

    策划开垦荒地有卓越贡献
    赫鲁晓夫荣获“列宁勋章”
    本报莫斯科9日电　苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫因为在计划和实现开垦荒地上有卓越的功绩，苏联最高苏维埃主席团昨天颁布奖给他“列宁勋章”和第二枚“镰刀与铁锤”金星。此间各报今天都刊载了赫鲁晓夫的照片和这项指令。
    根据苏联规定，获得“镰刀与铁锤”金星同时也就获得社会主义劳动英雄称号。在1954年4月赫鲁晓夫六十岁生日的时候，曾经第一次荣获社会主义劳动英雄的称号。
