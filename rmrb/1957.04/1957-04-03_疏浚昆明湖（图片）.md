Title: Dredging Kunming Lake (Picture)
Author: Sun Huiqing
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 4

    Dredging Kunming Lake
    Kunming Lake in the Summer Palace in Beijing is being dredged. The mud discharge pipeline of the "Beijing" dredge looks like a long dragon, discharging the silt from the bottom of the lake to a distant place. Photo by Sun Huiqing (People's Shipping Newspaper)

### 疏浚昆明湖（图片）
孙惠卿
1957-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    疏浚昆明湖
    北京颐和园内的昆明湖，正在进行疏浚。北京号挖泥船的排泥管线好像一条长龙，把湖底淤泥排送到很远的地方去。　孙惠卿摄（人民航运报社稿）
