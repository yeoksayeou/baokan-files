Title: Chairman Voroshilov Visits Hangzhou, Premier Zhou and Nearly 200,000 People of Hangzhou Welcome the Distinguished Guest
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, April 25th. Chairman Voroshilov and the accompanying Soviet guests arrived in Hangzhou by special plane this morning from Shanghai. Deputy Premier He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao and his wife accompanied them.

　　Today in Hangzhou, the weather cleared after the rain, and the spring sunshine was bright and beautiful. At ten o'clock in the morning, Chairman Voroshilov's special plane slowly landed amidst the cheers of over 12,000 welcoming people. Premier Zhou Enlai, who was waiting for the distinguished guest in Hangzhou, was the first to step forward to welcome him. Premier Zhou Enlai warmly shook Chairman Voroshilov's hand and said, "Springtime Hangzhou welcomes you!" Then, he introduced Governor of Zhejiang Province Sha Wenhan, First Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee Jiang Hua, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Party Committee and Mayor of Hangzhou Wu Xian, Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region Lieutenant General Lin Weixian, and others to the distinguished guests.

　　As Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Governor Sha Wenhan, walked past the welcoming crowds, countless flower petals showered down on the distinguished guests like a rain. The band played the tune "Moscow-Beijing," and the whole crowd shouted, "Long live Chairman Voroshilov!"

　　Chairman Voroshilov, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Governor Sha Wenhan boarded an open-top car to the guesthouse. Along the way, there were crowds of people; nearly 200,000 people who had been waiting on both sides of the road since early morning waved Chinese and Soviet national flags, beat waist drums, and danced dragon and lion dances, continuously cheering for Chairman Voroshilov. Chairman Voroshilov was covered in flower petals and colored confetti thrown by the welcoming crowds.

　　When Chairman Voroshilov arrived at the guesthouse and got out of the car, over 18,000 welcoming people nearby continuously cheered and paid their respects to the distinguished guest. Chairman Voroshilov stood on the balcony of the guesthouse and waved to the welcoming crowds.

　　According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 25th, Chairman Voroshilov and the accompanying Soviet guests flew to Hangzhou this morning from Shanghai by special plane for a visit.

　　At nine o'clock, Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Deputy Premier He Long, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Cao Diqiu, and others, arrived at Longhua Airport. Over 20,000 people were at the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

　　After Chairman Voroshilov got out of the car, he shook hands with Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Deputy Premier Marshal Lin Biao, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Shanghai Bureau and First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee Ke Qingshi, as well as Shanghai's vice mayors and prominent figures from all walks of life, to say goodbye.

　　Before leaving, Chairman Voroshilov walked to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling again, warmly grasped her hand, and expressed his gratitude for her warm hospitality.

　　Chairman Voroshilov said to Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu, I, on behalf of our country, extend my heartfelt thanks and respect to you and, through you, to the people of Shanghai. I hope you will achieve more in your work and better benefit the people of this great city.

### 伏罗希洛夫主席访问杭州  周总理和杭州近二十万群众欢迎贵宾

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫主席访问杭州
    周总理和杭州近二十万群众欢迎贵宾
    新华社杭州25日电　伏罗希洛夫主席和随来的苏联客人们，今天上午从上海乘专机到达杭州。国务院副总理贺龙元帅、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、中国驻苏联大使刘晓和夫人陪同前来。
    今天杭州雨过天晴，春光明媚。上午十时，伏罗希洛夫主席乘坐的专机在一万二千多欢迎群众的欢呼声中徐徐降落，在杭州迎候贵宾的周恩来总理第一个上前欢迎。周恩来总理紧紧地握着伏罗希洛夫主席的手说：“春天的杭州在欢迎你！”接着，他介绍浙江省省长沙文汉，中共浙江省委第一书记江华，中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪和浙江军区司令员林维先中将等人和贵宾相见。
    当伏罗希洛夫主席由周恩来总理和沙文汉省长陪同，从欢迎群众的行列前走过时，无数花瓣像阵雨一样地撒到贵宾身上，乐队奏起“莫斯科—北京”的曲子，全场高呼：“伏罗希洛夫主席万岁！”
    伏罗希洛夫主席和周恩来总理、沙文汉省长登上敞篷汽车前往宾馆。沿途人山人海，从清早便守候在马路两旁的近二十万群众挥动着中苏两国国旗，打着腰鼓，舞着龙灯和狮灯向伏罗希洛夫主席不断地欢呼。伏罗希洛夫主席的身上沾满了欢迎群众投的花瓣和彩色纸屑。
    伏罗希洛夫主席到达宾馆下车时，附近一万八千多欢迎群众不断地向贵宾欢呼致敬。伏罗希洛夫主席站在宾馆阳台上向欢迎群众挥手致意。
    据新华社上海25日电　伏罗希洛夫主席和随来的苏联客人，今天上午由上海乘专机飞往杭州访问。
    九时，伏罗希洛夫主席由刘少奇委员长、贺龙副总理、上海市副市长曹荻秋等陪同来到龙华机场。两万多群众在机场上欢送贵宾。
    伏罗希洛夫主席走下汽车后，和在机场上欢送他的宋庆龄副委员长，副总理林彪元帅，中共中央上海局书记、中共上海市委员会第一书记柯庆施以及上海市的副市长们、各界知名人士一一握手告别。
    临行前，伏罗希洛夫主席又走到宋庆龄副委员长面前，紧握着她的手，对她的热情招待表示感谢。
    伏罗希洛夫主席对曹荻秋副市长说，我代表我们的国家向你，并通过你向上海市人民致衷心的谢意和敬礼，希望你们在工作中取得更多的成就，更好地造福于这个大城市的人民。
