Title: Japanese Socialist Party Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission, headed by Inejiro Asanuma, left Beijing by plane this morning and will return home via Guangzhou.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and people from all walks of life in China, including Li Dequan, Wang Zhen, Liao Chengzhi, Lei Renmin, Zhang Su, Yang Yu, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, and Zhao Pinghua.

### 日社会党访华团离京

1957-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    日社会党访华团离京
    新华社24日讯　以浅沼稻次郎为首的日本社会党访华亲善使节团今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，将经广州回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，北京市副市长王昆仑，以及中国各界人士李德全、王震、廖承志、雷任民、张苏、杨煜、谢南光、赵安博、赵平化等。
