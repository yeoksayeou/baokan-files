Title: Japanese Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Japanese Women's Delegation arrived in Beijing this evening. They were warmly welcomed at the station by Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and others.
    The delegation consists of thirty members, composed of representatives of women from all walks of life in Japan. The head of the delegation is Kufu Shirokurasumi, Vice-President of the Japan Christian Women's Reform Society. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation for a friendly visit.

### 日本妇女访华代表团抵北京

1957-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本妇女访华代表团抵北京
    新华社11日讯　日本妇女访华代表团今天晚上到达北京。她们在车站上受到全国民主妇联副主席许广平等人的热烈欢迎。
    代表团共三十人，是由日本各阶层妇女代表组成。团长是日本基督教妇女矫风会副会长久布白落实。代表团是应中华全国民主妇女联合会的邀请来中国进行友好访问。
