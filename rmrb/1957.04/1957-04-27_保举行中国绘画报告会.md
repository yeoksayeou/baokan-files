Title: Bulgaria Holds Chinese Painting Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-04-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, April 26th: The Bulgarian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Artists Association held a report meeting on Chinese painting art and Qi Baishi's works here at the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences Conference Hall on the evening of April 24th. At the meeting, Bulgarian painter Zahariyev, who had visited China, introduced the history of Chinese painting art and Qi Baishi's works.

### 保举行中国绘画报告会

1957-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    保举行中国绘画报告会
    据新华社索非亚26日电　保加利亚对外文化联络委员会和画家协会，4月24日晚上在这里的保加利亚科学院会议厅举行了关于中国绘画艺术和齐白石作品的报告会。会上，曾经访问过中国的保加利亚画家扎哈里耶夫介绍了中国绘画艺术的历史和齐白石的作品。
