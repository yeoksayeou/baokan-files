Title: Cold Air Has Moved South and Weakened
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a forecast at 7:00 PM today: This cold air has moved south and weakened.

On the morning of the 12th, the temperature in the Yellow River basin, Huai River basin, and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will generally rise by 2 to 4 degrees Celsius compared to this morning. Tomorrow morning, there will still be slight frost in some areas of central and western Shandong, central and northern Hebei, and central and northern Shanxi.

On the 13th, the cold wave warning will be lifted in the above areas. In the next twenty-four hours, there will still be northerly winds of force six or seven in the Bohai Sea and the Taiwan Strait.

### 冷空气已南下减弱

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    冷空气已南下减弱
    据新华社11日讯　中央气象科学研究所今天下午七时发布预报：这次冷空气已经南下减弱。
    12日晨，黄河流域、淮河流域、长江中下游的气温，将比今天早晨普遍回升摄氏二到四度。明天早晨，山东中部西部、河北中部北部、山西中部北部的部分地区还会有轻微的霜冻。
    13日，以上地区寒潮警报解除。未来二十四小时内，渤海、台湾海峡仍有六、七级偏北风。
