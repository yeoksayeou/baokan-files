Title: Pressuring Panama: US Holds Exercises in Canal Zone
Author:
Date: 1957-04-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington: The United States will conduct a three-day large-scale exercise in the Panama Canal Zone from April 24th, involving ten thousand personnel from the Navy, Army, and Air Force. According to reports, the purpose of this large-scale exercise is to exert pressure on Panama in response to Panama's declaration of its sovereignty over the Canal Zone.

### 对巴拿马施加压力  美国在运河区举行演习

1957-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    对巴拿马施加压力
    美国在运河区举行演习
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国将从4月24日起在巴拿马运河区由一万名海、陆、空军举行三天的大演习。消息说，这次大规模演习的目的是对巴拿马施加压力，作为对巴拿马声明它享有运河区主权的答复。
