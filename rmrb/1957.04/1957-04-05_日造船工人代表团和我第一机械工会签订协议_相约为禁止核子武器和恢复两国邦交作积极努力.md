Title: Japanese Shipyard Workers Delegation and First Machine Building Workers Union of China Sign Agreement: Pledge to Actively Work for the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons and the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The Japanese Shipyard Workers Delegation to China left Beijing by train this morning to return home. Yesterday evening, the delegation and the National Committee of the First Machine Building Workers Union of China signed an agreement in Beijing, stipulating that both parties should make further active efforts for the following matters: 1. To demand the prohibition of the testing, manufacture, and use of atomic and hydrogen bombs; to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations and normal trade between the two countries; to oppose wars of aggression and defend peace in Asia and the world. 2. To regularly exchange various materials; to further strengthen the exchange of shipyard industrial worker representatives (including the exchange of technical experience); in 1957, the Japanese Shipbuilding Workers Union will invite representatives from the First Machine Building Workers Union of China to visit Japan.

### 日造船工人代表团和我第一机械工会签订协议  相约为禁止核子武器和恢复两国邦交作积极努力

1957-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    日造船工人代表团和我第一机械工会签订协议
    相约为禁止核子武器和恢复两国邦交作积极努力
    据新华社4日讯　日本造船工人访华代表团今天上午乘火车离开北京回国。昨天晚上，代表团和中国第一机械工会全国委员会在北京签订了一项协议，约定双方要为下列事项作进一步积极的努力：一、要求禁止试验、制造与使用原子弹、氢弹；促进恢复两国邦交与正常贸易；反对侵略战争，保卫亚洲与世界和平。二、经常交换各种资料；进一步加强交换造船产业工人代表（包括交换技术经验）；1957年内日本造船工会邀请中国第一机械工会代表访问日本。
