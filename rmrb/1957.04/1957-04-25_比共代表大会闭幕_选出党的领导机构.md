Title: Belgian Communist Party Congress Concludes, Elects Party Leadership

Author:

Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ghent, April 23rd: According to TASS, the 12th National Congress of the Belgian Communist Party concluded on the morning of April 22nd after electing the party's leadership.

    The Secretary of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, Burnelle, delivered a closing speech, summarizing the work of the congress over the past four days. He pointed out that this congress demonstrated the party's true unity. The party is steadfastly loyal to the principles of Marxism-Leninism and the principles of proletarian internationalism. The party recognizes its role as the vanguard of the workers' movement and will do its utmost to unite the ranks of the working class.

    The congress elected the Central Committee, the Political Supervisory Committee, and the Financial Supervisory Committee. A total of seventy-three people were elected to the new Central Committee.

    After the congress concluded, the newly elected Central Committee held its first plenary session and elected the Politburo and the Secretariat. The following were elected to the Politburo: Reynders Beelen, Jean Blume, E. Burnelle, F. Vanden Brande, G. Van Moerkerke, Grillina, Albert De Coninck, Gaston Moulin, Jean Terfve, B. Walravens.

    Burnelle, Beelen, Blume, De Coninck, and Van Moerkerke were elected as Secretaries of the Central Committee.

### 比共代表大会闭幕  选出党的领导机构

1957-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    比共代表大会闭幕
    选出党的领导机构
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社根特讯：比利时共产党第十二次全国代表大会在4月22日上午选出党的领导机构后闭幕。
    比利时共产党中央委员会书记伯内尔致闭幕词，总结了这次代表大会四天来的工作情况。他指出这次代表大会表现出党的真正团结。党坚定不移地忠于马克思列宁主义的原则，忠于无产阶级国际主义的原则。党认识到自己作为工人运动先锋队的作用，并且将竭尽全力把工人阶级的队伍团结起来。
    大会选出了中央委员会、政治监察委员会和财政监察委员会。被选入新的中央委员会的共有七十三人。
    代表大会结束以后，新选出的中央委员会举行了第一次全体会议，选出了政治局和书记处。被选入政治局的有：雷·贝伦、让·布鲁姆、埃·伯内尔、弗·范登布兰登、格·范穆克尔克、格利纳、阿尔贝·德贡宁克、加斯东·穆兰、让·特尔夫、博·华耳斯坦。
    当选为中央委员会书记的是：伯内尔、贝伦、布鲁姆、德贡宁克和范穆克尔克。
