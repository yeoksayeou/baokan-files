Title: Changzhi Agricultural Experiment Station Cultivates New Corn Varieties
Author: Deng Lezhai, Li Changyou, Zhao Pingcheng
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 3

    Changzhi Agricultural Experiment Station
    Cultivates New Corn Varieties

    (Our dispatch) The Changzhi Agricultural Experiment Station in Shanxi Province, after six years of experimental research, has selected four inter-varietal hybrids (Changza No. 1, 2, 4, and 5) with early maturity, high yield, and good quality from twelve corn hybrid combinations. These hybrid varieties are bred by hybridizing Jin Huanghou (Golden Queen) with local corn, Erhuangcao, Huanong No. 2, Qin Zhouhuang and other local varieties as parents.

    The most important characteristics of these varieties are their short growth period (118-129 days), neat plants, moderate ear position (sixth and seventh nodes), strong drought and disease resistance, and excellent quality. The average yield per mu is between 621 and 872 jin, with the highest reaching 939 jin. This is an increase of more than 10%-17% compared to "Golden Queen" and an increase of about 34%-54.8% compared to local varieties. This year, the first generation of hybrid seeds will be propagated in counties such as Luan, Changzi, Xiangyuan, and Jincheng, preparing for large-scale promotion next year.
      (Deng Lezhai, Li Changyou, Zhao Pingcheng)

### 长治农业试验站  培育出玉米新品种
邓乐斋  李长有  赵聘丞
1957-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

    长治农业试验站
    培育出玉米新品种
    本报讯　山西省长治农业试验站经过六年的试验研究，在十二个玉米杂交组合中，选出了成熟早、产量高、品质好的四个品种间杂交种（长杂1、2、4、5号）。这些杂交种是用金皇后和当地土玉米、二黄糙、华农二号、沁州黄等当地品种作亲本杂交选育而成的。
    这些品种最主要的特点，是生育期较短（118—129天），植株整齐、穗位适中（第六、七节），抗旱、抗病性强，品质优良。平均亩产在六百二十一至八百七十二斤，最高的达九百三十九市斤。比“金皇后”增产10％—17％以上，而比当地品种增产34％—54.8％左右。今年在潞安、长子、襄垣、晋城等县繁育第一代杂交种子，准备明年作大量推广。
      （邓乐斋、李长有、赵聘丞）
