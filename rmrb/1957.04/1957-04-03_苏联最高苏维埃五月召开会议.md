Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet to Convene Meeting in May
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd: TASS reported from Moscow on the 2nd that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR decided to convene the Seventh Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR in Moscow on May 7th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃五月召开会议

1957-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃
    五月召开会议
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定于5月7日在莫斯科召开第四届苏联最高苏维埃第七次会议。
