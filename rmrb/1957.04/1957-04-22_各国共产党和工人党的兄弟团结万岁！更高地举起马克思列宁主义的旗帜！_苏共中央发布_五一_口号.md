Title: Long Live the Fraternal Unity of the Communist and Workers' Parties of All Countries! Hold High the Banner of Marxism-Leninism! The CPSU Central Committee Issues "May Day" Slogans
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 21st: The Moscow newspapers published the slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for the celebration of the "May Day" International Labor Day this year.

The slogans, totaling seventy-seven, first hail Long Live the International Day of Solidarity of Working People, the Holiday of Fraternal Friendship of Workers of All Countries, and "May Day"!

The CPSU Central Committee calls on proletarians of all countries to unite! Hold high the banner of proletarian internationalism!

The CPSU Central Committee then extends fraternal greetings to all people who are fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism, and calls on working people all over the world to fight for the reduction of armaments and armed forces, and for the easing of international tensions! Expose the instigators and warmongers of war!

Demand the cessation of testing and the complete prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons! No atomic war!

The slogans then say, Long Live the unbreakable ideological unity and fraternal solidarity of the Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries! Hold higher the banner of Marxism-Leninism!

Strengthen cooperation between Communists, Social Democrats, and all progressive forces in the struggle for peace, democracy, and independence of all nations!

Long live the great family of the people of the countries of the socialist camp! Our strength lies in fraternal friendship and mutual assistance!

The slogans then extend fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people - builders of socialism, and hail Long Live the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China - a strong fortress of world peace! The slogans also extend fraternal greetings to the working people of all the People's Democratic countries who are building socialism!

The CPSU Central Committee then, in its slogans, pays tribute to the great Indian people who are striving for the progress of their motherland and for peace in Asia and the world, to the people of the Arab East who are fighting against the neo-colonialist plans of the imperialists and for the consolidation of national independence and state sovereignty, to the people of Indonesia and Burma who are fighting for peace and for the consolidation of national independence, and to the people of colonies and dependent countries who are fighting against imperialist oppression and for freedom and national independence.

The slogans propose that the friendship and cooperation between the people of Britain, the United States, France, and the Soviet Union is a guarantee of lasting peace in the world.

The CPSU Central Committee also extends greetings to the Japanese people who are fighting against militarism and for the peaceful, democratic, and independent development of their country.

Nearly half of the slogans of the CPSU Central Committee are on international aspects. In concluding this part, the CPSU Central Committee said: Long live the Soviet Leninist foreign policy - the policy of peaceful coexistence of countries with different social systems, the policy of defending and consolidating peace, and the policy of developing economic and cultural ties with all countries!

The CPSU Central Committee then calls on the working people of the Soviet Union to mobilize all forces to implement the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, to strive for a new and great upsurge in socialist economy and culture, and to strive to complete the tasks of the Sixth Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule; calling on them to launch a nationwide socialist competition to celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the Great October Revolution and strive to complete the 1957 national economic plan ahead of schedule; and to further improve the organization of management of industry and construction.

### 各国共产党和工人党的兄弟团结万岁！更高地举起马克思列宁主义的旗帜！  苏共中央发布“五一”口号

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    各国共产党和工人党的兄弟团结万岁！
    更高地举起马克思列宁主义的旗帜！
    苏共中央发布“五一”口号
    新华社莫斯科21日电　莫斯科各报发表了苏联共产党中央委员会庆祝今年“五一”国际劳动节的口号。
    总数达七十七条的口号首先欢呼劳动人民国际大团结日、全世界各国工人友爱的节日、“五一”节万岁！
    苏共中央号召全世界无产者，联合起来！更高地举起无产阶级国际主义的旗帜！
    苏共中央接着向一切为和平、民主、社会主义而奋斗的人民致兄弟的敬礼，并且号召全世界劳动人民，为裁减军备和武装力量、为缓和国际紧张局势而奋斗！揭露战争煽动者和战争贩子！
    要求停止试验和完全禁止原子武器和氢武器！不许发动原子战争！
    口号接着说，各国共产党和工人党牢不可破的思想一致和兄弟团结万岁！更高地举起马克思列宁主义的旗帜！
    共产党人、社会党人和一切进步力量在争取各国人民的和平、民主和独立斗争中加强合作！
    社会主义阵营各国人民的大家庭万岁！我们的力量就在于兄弟般的友谊和互助中！
    口号接着向伟大的中国人民——社会主义建设者致兄弟的敬礼，并且欢呼世界和平的坚强堡垒——苏中两国人民的牢不可破的友谊和合作万岁！口号也一一向正在建设社会主义的各人民民主国家的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    苏共中央接着在口号中向为争取祖国的进步，争取亚洲及世界和平的伟大印度人民，向反对帝国主义者新殖民主义计划、为巩固民族独立和国家主权而斗争的阿拉伯东方各国人民，向为和平、为巩固国家的民族独立而斗争的印度尼西亚和缅甸人民，以及反对帝国主义的压迫、争取自由和民族独立的殖民地和附属国的人民致敬礼。
    口号提出，英国、美国、法国和苏联人民的友谊和合作是全世界持久和平的保证。
    苏共中央还向反对军国主义、为国家的和平、民主和独立的发展而斗争的日本人民致敬。
    苏共中央的口号有将近半数是关于国际方面的。在结束这一部分的时候，苏共中央说：苏联列宁主义外交政策——不同社会制度的各国和平共处的政策、保卫和巩固和平、同所有国家发展经济和文化联系的政策——万岁！
    苏共中央接着号召苏联的劳动人民，动员一切力量来实现苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议，为争取社会主义经济和文化的新的巨大高涨，为争取提前完成第六个五年计划的任务而斗争；号召他们展开庆祝伟大十月革命四十周年的全民社会主义竞赛，争取提前完成1957年的国民经济计划；进一步改善管理工业和建设的组织工作。
