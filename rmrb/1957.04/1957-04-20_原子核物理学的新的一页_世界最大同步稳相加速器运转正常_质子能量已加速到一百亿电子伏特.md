Title: A New Page in Nuclear Physics: World's Largest Synchrophasotron Operating Normally, Proton Energy Accelerated to 10 Billion Electron Volts
Author:
Date: 1957-04-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th -- TASS reports: Joint Institute for Nuclear Research Director Blokhintsev announced on the 17th that the energy of protons in the synchrophasotron, which began operating at the institute a few days ago, was accelerated to the design standard of 10 billion electron volts on April 16th.

A reporter from the Soviet "Literary Gazette" recently visited Soviet Academy of Sciences Corresponding Member Veksler, the director of the design and construction work of this, the world's largest synchrophasotron. Veksler was the first scientist to study the so-called automatic phase stability principle, which is the basis for the operation of synchrophasotrons.

Veksler told the reporter that the appearance of this accelerator opens a new page in nuclear physics. In the past three years, our understanding of elementary particles and the entire nucleus has undergone tremendous changes. The problem we now face is to determine the interconnections in the microcosm of the nucleus and to understand the nature of the forces acting within it, so that it will be possible to make this enormous force serve mankind more broadly. Using this accelerator, it is possible to delve into some previously unstudied aspects of nuclear structure. It is now anticipated that new intranuclear phenomena, and even new elementary particles, will be discovered.

Veksler said that Soviet scientists, designers, and engineers encountered many complex problems in manufacturing this accelerator. However, the new problems arising from the start of the synchrophasotron will still be numerous. It is necessary to hold a conference of authoritative physicists to discuss the priority work plan. He said that the Academic Committee of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research is scheduled to hold a meeting in May to discuss this issue.

### 原子核物理学的新的一页  世界最大同步稳相加速器运转正常  质子能量已加速到一百亿电子伏特

1957-04-20
第5版()
专栏：

    原子核物理学的新的一页
    世界最大同步稳相加速器运转正常
    质子能量已加速到一百亿电子伏特
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社报道：联合原子核研究所所长勃洛欣采夫17日宣布，研究所中几天以前开始运转的同步稳相加速器的质子的能量在4月16日已经加速到设计标准——一百亿电子伏特。
    苏联“文学报”的记者不久前访问了这座世界上最大的同步稳相加速器的设计和建造工作的主持人苏联科学院通讯院士维克斯勒。维克斯勒是第一个研究出成为同步稳相加速器工作基础的所谓自动稳相原理的科学家。
    维克斯勒对记者发表谈话说，这座加速器的出现在原子核物理学上展开了新的一页。最近三年，在我们对基本粒子和整个原子核的概念上产生了巨大变化。现在我们面临的问题是确定原子核的微观世界中的相互联系，理解其中作用的力的本质，这样才有可能使这种巨大的力量更广泛地为人类服务。利用这座加速器就可以深入到原子核结构中一些以前无法研究的方面。现在可以预期，将会发现新的核内现象，甚至新的基本粒子。
    维克斯勒说，苏联科学家、设计师、工程师在制造这座加速器时遇到了许许多多复杂的问题。但是，由于这座同步稳相加速器开始使用而产生的新问题仍将是很多的。必须举行权威物理学家会议，以讨论首要工作计划。他说，联合原子核研究所学术委员会预定在5月举行会议，讨论这项问题。
