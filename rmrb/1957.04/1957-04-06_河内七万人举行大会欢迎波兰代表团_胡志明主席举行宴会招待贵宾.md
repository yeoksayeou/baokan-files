Title: Hanoi Holds Rally of 70,000 to Welcome Polish Delegation; Chairman Ho Chi Minh Holds Banquet to Host Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th April: Seventy thousand people in the Vietnamese capital braved the rain this afternoon at Ba Dinh Square to hold a rally welcoming the Polish government delegation.

	Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Administrative Committee, spoke first, extending a warm welcome on behalf of the people of the Vietnamese capital to the distinguished Polish guests and paying respects to the heroic people of Warsaw.

	Prime Minister Pham Van Dong spoke at the rally. He thanked the Polish people for their assistance to the Vietnamese people during the war of resistance and after the peaceful restoration, and for their great efforts in consolidating peace in Vietnam.

	Speaking about the current international situation, Pham Van Dong strongly condemned the imperialist bloc headed by the United States for creating international tensions, undermining national independence movements in the Middle East, and plotting to threaten peace in Southeast Asia.

	Pham Van Dong concluded by wishing the Polish people even greater victories in the cause of building socialism under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party.

	Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz then spoke, praising the achievements of the Vietnamese people in their struggle for national independence and freedom.

	Cyrankiewicz said that Poland, working with India and Canada in the International Commission in Vietnam, will strive to implement the Geneva Accords so that Vietnam can achieve unification. Poland guarantees to continue to assist the struggle and construction of the Vietnamese people in all aspects. In the evening, Cyrankiewicz held a banquet to host leaders of the Vietnamese state and the Vietnam Workers' Party, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th April: At noon today, Chairman Ho Chi Minh hosted a banquet to entertain the Polish government delegation headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz. At the banquet, Ho Chi Minh, on behalf of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the government, and the people, expressed his respect to the Polish people who are building socialism. He mentioned the Polish people's assistance to the Vietnamese people and the efforts made by the Polish delegation in the International Commission to implement the Geneva Accords.

	In his reply, Cyrankiewicz said that the talks held between the Polish government delegation and the Vietnam Workers' Party and the government during this visit to Vietnam fully demonstrated the spirit of mutual understanding and solidarity between the two fraternal countries. He said he hoped this spirit would be manifested through more practical actions.

	Those present at today's banquet included Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Ministers Phan Ke Toai and Vo Nguyen Giap, Chairman Ton Duc Thang, ministers of various ministries, representatives of democratic parties and mass organizations, diplomatic envoys from various countries, as well as the chairman and members of the International Commission.

### 河内七万人举行大会欢迎波兰代表团  胡志明主席举行宴会招待贵宾

1957-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    河内七万人举行大会欢迎波兰代表团
    胡志明主席举行宴会招待贵宾
    据新华社河内5日电　越南首都七万人民今天下午冒雨在巴亭广场举行大会欢迎波兰政府代表团。
    河内行政委员会主席陈维兴首先致词，他代表越南首都人民热烈欢迎波兰贵宾，并向英雄的华沙人民致意。
    范文同总理在大会上讲了话。他感谢波兰人民在抗战中和和平恢复以后给予越南人民的援助和在巩固越南的和平中所作出的巨大努力。
    范文同在说到目前国际形势的时候，强烈地指责以美国为首的帝国主义集团制造国际紧张局势、破坏中近东民族独立运动以及威胁东南亚地区和平的阴谋活动。
    范文同最后祝波兰人民在波兰统一工人党的领导下在建设社会主义的事业中取得更大的胜利。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹接着讲话。他赞扬越南人民在争取民族独立自由的斗争所取得的成就。
    西伦凯维兹说，波兰同印度、加拿大在越南国际委员会中工作，将努力实现日内瓦协议，使越南获得统一。波兰保证继续从各方面来援助越南人民的斗争和建设。晚间，西伦凯维兹举行宴会招待越南国家和越南劳动党的领导人以及各国驻越南的外交使节。
    据新华社河内5日电　今天中午，胡志明主席设宴招待以西伦凯维兹部长会议主席为首的波兰政府代表团。宴会上，胡志明代表越南劳动党、政府和人民向正在建设社会主义的波兰人民表示敬意。他提到波兰人民对越南人民的援助和波兰代表团在国际委员会中为实现日内瓦协议所作的努力。
    西伦凯维兹致答词说，波兰政府代表团这次访问越南时同越南劳动党和政府举行的会谈，充分表现了两个兄弟国家之间的互相了解和团结的精神。他说，希望这种精神将通过更多的实际行动来表现出来。
    出席今天宴会的有范文同总理，潘继遂和武元甲副总理，孙德胜委员长，各部部长，民主党派和群众团体代表，各国使节以及国际委员会主席和委员们。
