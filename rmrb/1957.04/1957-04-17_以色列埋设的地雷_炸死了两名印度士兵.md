Title: Israeli Mines Kill Two Indian Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 6

    Israeli Mines
    Kill Two Indian Soldiers
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 16th. According to the Middle East News Agency's report from Gaza on the 14th, two Indian soldiers belonging to the United Nations Emergency Force were killed by landmines near the ceasefire line in the Gaza area on April 10th. An investigation was conducted on the 13th regarding this incident. The results of the investigation confirmed that the Israelis had planted landmines in a section of the ceasefire demarcation line in the Gaza area, where the Indian troops were carrying out surveillance tasks.

### 以色列埋设的地雷  炸死了两名印度士兵

1957-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列埋设的地雷
    炸死了两名印度士兵
    新华社开罗16日电　据中东通讯社14日从加沙报道，属于联合国紧急部队的两名印度士兵4月10日在加沙地区停战线附近触地雷被炸死，13日就这一事件进行了调查，调查结果证实以色列人在加沙地区由印度部队执行监察任务的停战分界线沿线一段地区里埋设了地雷。
