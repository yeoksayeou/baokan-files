Title: Welcome - Dedicated to Chairman Voroshilov
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1957-04-16
Page: 8

    Welcome
    ——Dedicated to Chairman Voroshilov
    Guo Moruo
  The sunshine is so clear,
  The spring breeze is so gentle! On the road to Nanyuan,
  Cars flow into a long river. A hundred times the emptying of the streets,
  Is beating drums and gongs. Welcome arches reach into the clouds,
  Red flags fill the street like fire. The Marshal flies from beyond the sky,
  His benevolent beard is already white. Cheers shake the earth,
  Beijing is churning with people. This friendship is unbreakable,
  This joy comes from the heart. Eight hundred million people share joys and sorrows,
  Let peace overwhelm the war demons. May flower rain spread throughout the world,
  May jade and silk transform into weapons of war. May the Marshal live a hundred years,
  Forever like a towering mountain.
                    April 15, 1957

### 欢迎——献给伏罗希洛夫主席
郭沫若
1957-04-16
第8版()
专栏：

    欢迎
    ——献给伏罗希洛夫主席
    郭沫若
  阳光这样地晴明，
  春风这样地温和！开往南苑的路上，
  汽车流成了长河。百倍的万人空巷，
  在打着鼓，敲着锣。欢迎的华表入云，
  满街的红旗如火。元帅自天外飞来，
  慈祥的须发已皤。欢呼声震动大地，
  北京城翻滚人波。这友谊牢不可破，
  这欢乐出自心窝。八亿人同甘共苦，
  使和平压倒战魔。愿花雨洒遍人间，
  愿玉帛化尽干戈。愿元帅长命百岁，
  永远像山岳峨峨。
                    1957年4月15日
