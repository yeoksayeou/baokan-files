Title: Chairman Cyrankiewicz Visits the Summer Palace and the Forbidden City; Ministry of Culture Holds Peking Opera Gala to Entertain Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-04-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Chairman Józef Cyrankiewicz and members of the Polish government delegation, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long, visited the Summer Palace this morning.

After visiting the Hall of Benevolence and Longevity (Renshou Dian) and the Garden of Virtue and Harmony (De He Yuan), Chairman Cyrankiewicz and his wife walked through the Long Corridor to the Gate of Dispelling Clouds (Pai Yun Men) and ascended the Tower of Buddhist Incense (Fo Xiang Ge) on Longevity Hill, overlooking the rainy scenery of Kunming Lake from the corridor beside the tower. During their visit to the park, the distinguished guests were greeted with applause from Young Pioneers who were on spring break excursions. Two Young Pioneers from Beijing's Bamiantao Elementary School also presented Chairman Cyrankiewicz and his wife with red scarves.

This morning, the distinguished guests also visited the Forbidden City, accompanied by Vice Minister of Culture Zheng Zhenduo. The guests visited the display of ancient art in the Hall of Supreme Harmony (Tai He Dian), which showcased various precious cultural relics from 2000 BC to the early 20th century. They then visited the Hall of Mental Cultivation (Yang Xin Dian) and other places, and toured the Imperial Garden.

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Chairman Józef Cyrankiewicz and his wife, along with Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai, attended a Peking Opera gala held by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China this evening.

Members of the government delegation of the Polish People's Republic, officials from the Polish Embassy in China, and Polish experts in Beijing were invited to attend the gala.

Also accompanying the viewing were Vice Premier He Long, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan, among others.

The three Peking Operas performed at the gala, "The Reconciliation of the General and the Minister," "Autumn River," and "Havoc in Heaven," were warmly received.

After the performance, Chairman Cyrankiewicz presented the actors with flower baskets and went on stage to meet them.

### 西伦凯维兹主席游览颐和园和故宫  文化部举行京剧晚会招待贵宾

1957-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹主席游览颐和园和故宫
    文化部举行京剧晚会招待贵宾
    新华社8日讯　约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹主席和波兰政府代表团人员由贺龙副总理陪同，今天上午游览了颐和园。
    西伦凯维兹主席和夫人等在参观了仁寿殿、德和园以后，步过长廊入排云门，登上万寿山上的佛香阁，在阁旁的回廊里眺望了雨中的昆明湖景色。贵宾们在园内游览时，到处受到了正在这里作春假旅行的少先队员们的鼓掌欢迎，北京八面槽小学的两个少先队员还向西伦凯维兹主席和夫人赠送了红领巾。
    贵宾们在今天上午还由文化部副部长郑振铎陪同游览了故宫。贵宾们参观了太和殿内古代艺术品陈列中的从公元前两千年到二十世纪初的各种珍贵文物，然后又参观了养心殿等处，并且游览了御花园。
    新华社8日讯　约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹主席和夫人今晚同朱德副主席、周恩来总理一起出席了中华人民共和国文化部举行的京剧晚会。
    波兰人民共和国政府代表团团员们、波兰驻华大使馆官员和在北京的波兰专家等应邀出席了晚会。
    陪同观剧的有贺龙副总理，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国驻波兰大使王炳南等人。
    晚会上演出的“将相和”、“秋江”和“闹天宫”三出京剧，受到了热烈的欢迎。
    在演出结束后，西伦凯维兹主席向演员们赠送了花篮，并且上台同他们见面。
