Title: Korean-Czech Talks Begin; Kim Il-sung Hosts Banquet for Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd - According to the Korean Central News Agency, government leaders of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by Premier Široký, held talks in Pyongyang today.

    The Korean side participating in the talks included Premier Kim Il-sung, Vice Premiers Choi Yong-kun and Hong Myung-hee, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jeong-ae, Foreign Minister Nam Il, Minister of Internal and External Commerce Chen Ban-soo, and Korean Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Yang Young-seop.

    The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of mutual cooperation, understanding, and sincerity. The two sides broadly exchanged views on international issues of concern to both countries, consolidating and developing the friendly solidarity between socialist countries, and further developing the friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and Czechoslovakia.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd - Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet hosted a banquet this evening in honor of the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by Premier Široký.

    Kim Doo-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premier Choi Yong-kun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jeong-ae, and other national leaders, as well as heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, attended the banquet. Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Korea also attended the banquet.

### 朝捷双方开始会谈  金日成设宴招待嘉宾

1957-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    朝捷双方开始会谈
    金日成设宴招待嘉宾
    新华社平壤2日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府领导人和以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团今天在平壤举行了会谈。
    朝鲜方面参加会谈的有内阁首相金日成，副首相崔庸健、洪命熹，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，外务相南日，对内外商业相陈班秀，朝鲜驻捷克斯洛伐克大使杨永笋。
    会谈在互相合作、理解和诚恳的气氛中进行。双方就两国关心的国际问题、巩固和发展社会主义阵营国家间友好团结以及进一步发展朝捷两国间的友好合作关系等问题广泛地交换了意见。
    新华社平壤2日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天晚间设宴招待以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁副首相崔庸健、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱等国家领导人和朝鲜内阁各相以及各民主政党、社会团体的负责人等出席作陪。各国驻朝鲜的使节也出席了宴会。
