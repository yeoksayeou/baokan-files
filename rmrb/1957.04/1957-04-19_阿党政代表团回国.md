Title: Albanian Party and Government Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th. TASS reported: The Albanian government and Labor Party delegation, after concluding their talks with the Soviet government and Communist Party delegation, left Moscow by train on the evening of the 17th to visit Kiev and Odessa, and then from there took a route back to their country.

### 阿党政代表团回国

1957-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿党政代表团回国
    据新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿尔巴尼亚政府和劳动党代表团，在结束了和苏联政府和共产党代表团的会谈以后，在17日晚间乘火车离莫斯科前往基辅和敖得萨访问，然后从那里取道回国。
