Title: Congratulations on "May Day" International Labor Day. Lai Ruoyu writes to Grishin, Chairman of the All-Soviet Trade Union. ACFTU sends letters to trade unions of North Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, etc.
Author:
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, wrote a letter today to V.V. Grishin, Chairman of the Central Council of All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, congratulating him on "May Day" International Labor Day.

The congratulatory letter stated that on the occasion of May 1st, 1957, the day of international solidarity of working people around the world, on behalf of the Chinese working class and its trade unions, I would like to extend warm and heartfelt congratulations to the brotherly Soviet working people and Soviet trade unions.

In the past year, the heroic Soviet working class and the Soviet people have achieved brilliant achievements in implementing the tasks of the magnificent Sixth Five-Year Plan formulated by the 20th National Congress of the CPSU, especially in vigorously developing agriculture and improving the people's living standards. Your achievements have inspired the confidence of the Chinese working class and people in building socialism.

At present, the Soviet working class and its trade unions, together with the Soviet people, are working hard to find new forms of improving industrial and construction management, and we sincerely wish you success.

The congratulatory letter also stated that in the past year, the ties and friendship between the Chinese and Soviet trade unions have also been further developed and strengthened. The visit of delegations of advanced producers from various Soviet industries and other trade union delegations to China has greatly helped China's socialist construction and played a major role in strengthening the friendship between Chinese and Soviet workers. We deeply appreciate the friendship and assistance of the Soviet workers, and we are convinced that this great friendship will continue to develop in the future.

The congratulatory letter concluded by wishing a long life to the unbreakable and eternal brotherly friendship between the working classes of China and the Soviet Union! Long live the great unity of the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union! Long live the great unity of the working people of the world!

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions wrote letters on the 20th and 23rd respectively to the Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, the National Federation of Free Trade Unions of Hungary, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, the Federation of Free German Trade Unions, and the Central Council of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, congratulating them on "May Day" International Labor Day.

In its congratulatory letters, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions congratulated the working classes of these countries on the achievements they have made in socialist construction, consolidating the socialist system, and safeguarding world peace. It emphasized the importance of strengthening the unity of the working classes of all countries and strengthening the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and wished the working classes of all countries new achievements in the cause of socialist construction and greater contributions to the cause of safeguarding world peace.

### 祝贺“五一”国际劳动节  赖若愚写信给全苏工会主席格里申  全总致函给朝、越、蒙、捷等国工会

1957-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    祝贺“五一”国际劳动节
    赖若愚写信给全苏工会主席格里申
    全总致函给朝、越、蒙、捷等国工会
    新华社23日讯　中华全国总工会主席赖若愚今天写信给全苏工会中央理事会主席符·符·格里申，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。
    贺信说，值此全世界劳动人民国际大团结的日子——1957年五一节之际，谨代表中国工人阶级及其工会向兄弟的苏联劳动人民和苏联工会致以热烈的衷心的祝贺。
    在过去一年中，英勇的苏联工人阶级和苏联人民在执行苏共第二十次代表大会所制定的宏伟的第六个五年计划的任务中，特别是在大力发展农业和提高人民的生活水平的斗争中获得了辉煌的成就。你们的成就鼓舞着中国工人阶级和人民建设社会主义的信心。
    目前，苏联的工人阶级及其工会与苏联人民一道，正在努力寻求改善工业和建筑管理的新形式，我们衷心地祝你们获得成功。
    贺信又说，过去一年来，中苏工会间的联系和友谊也获得了进一步的发展与加强。苏联各产业的先进生产者代表团和其他工会代表团的访问中国，对中国的社会主义建设给了很大帮助，并在加强中苏工人间的友谊方面起了重大的作用。我们深切地感谢苏联工人的友谊和帮助，并且深信这种伟大友谊今后还会得到不断的发展。
    贺信最后祝中苏两国工人阶级间的牢不可破的、永恒的兄弟友谊万岁！以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的大团结万岁！全世界劳动人民的大团结万岁！
    新华社23日讯　中华全国总工会在20日和23日分别写信给朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会、越南总工会、蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会、保加利亚工会中央理事会、匈牙利自由工会全国联合会、罗马尼亚工会中央理事会、阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会、自由德国工会联合会、南斯拉夫工会中央理事会，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。
    中华全国总工会在贺信中祝贺这些国家的工人阶级在社会主义建设、巩固社会主义制度和保卫世界和平事业中所取得的成就，强调加强各国工人阶级的团结、加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结的重要意义，并祝各国工人阶级在社会主义建设事业中取得新的成就，在保卫世界和平事业中作出更大的贡献。
