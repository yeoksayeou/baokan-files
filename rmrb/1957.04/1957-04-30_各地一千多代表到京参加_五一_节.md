Title: More than a Thousand Representatives from Various Places Arrive in Beijing to Participate in the "May Day" Holiday

Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: More than a thousand representatives from various places have arrived in Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

Among those coming to the capital for the celebrations are representatives of the People's Liberation Army stationed in various locations, as well as representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning from Korea, and representatives of ethnic minorities from various border regions.

Among the worker representatives from various regions are also representatives from the Tibetan Trade Union activists delegation.

These days, staff at the Beijing Overseas Chinese Travel Service are busy receiving sightseeing groups of returned overseas Chinese from India, Indonesia, Thailand, Singapore, France, and other places.

### 各地一千多代表到京参加“五一”节

1957-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    各地一千多代表到京参加“五一”节
    据新华社29日讯　前来北京参加“五一”节观礼的各地代表，已有一千多人到达北京。
    来首都观礼的，有驻各地的人民解放军的代表，还有从朝鲜回来的中国人民志愿军的代表和来自各边疆地区的少数民族的代表。
    在各地来的工人观礼代表中，还有西藏工会积极分子代表团。
    这些天来，北京华侨旅行服务社的工作人员正忙着接待来自印度、印度尼西亚、泰国、新加坡、法国等地的归国华侨观光团。
