Title: New Vietnamese Ambassador to China Nguyen Khang and his wife arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Nguyen Khang, and his wife arrived in Beijing tonight. Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Xie Shuangqiu, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, were at the station to welcome them.

Also at the station to welcome them were Pham Binh, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 越南新任驻华大使阮康及夫人到京

1957-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    越南新任驻华大使阮康及夫人到京
    新华社25日讯　越南民主共和国新任驻华特命全权大使阮康及夫人在今天晚上到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长王雨田和亚洲司副司长谢爽秋。
    到车站欢迎的还有越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办范平和使馆人员，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。
