Title: Nineteen Asian and African Nations Write to Hammarskjöld, Requesting Attention to the Situation in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1957-04-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th. Representatives of nineteen Asian and African nations at the United Nations requested Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the 15th to pay “urgent attention” to the situation in Algeria.

In a letter to Hammarskjöld, the nineteen Asian and African nations accused the French authorities of not only failing to make a peaceful, just, and democratic resolution to the Algerian problem in accordance with the United Nations Charter, but also of systematically using violence and large-scale suppression. As a result, the intent and spirit of the resolution unanimously adopted by the United Nations General Assembly on this issue has not only been hindered, but also nullified.

The letter stated that the nineteen Asian and African nations believe that all possible efforts must be made to ensure that the intent of the resolution unanimously adopted by the United Nations General Assembly is implemented rather than undermined. Due to these considerations, the nineteen Asian and African nations wish to draw the Secretary-General's urgent attention to the truth of the current situation.

The countries that signed the letter were: Afghanistan, Burma, Ceylon, Egypt, Ethiopia, India, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Libya, Morocco, Nepal, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Tunisia, Syria, and Yemen.

### 亚非十九国致函哈马舍尔德  要求注意阿尔及利亚局势

1957-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非十九国致函哈马舍尔德
    要求注意阿尔及利亚局势
    新华社16日讯　纽约消息：联合国十九个亚非国家的代表15日要求秘书长哈马舍尔德“迫切注意”阿尔及利亚的局势。
    这十九个亚非国家在写给哈马舍尔德的一封信中指责法国当局不但不根据联合国宪章对阿尔及利亚问题作出和平、公正和民主的解决，而且还有系统地运用暴力和大规模镇压。因而，联合国大会就这个问题一致通过的决议的意图和精神不仅受到阻挠，而且被取消了。
    这封信说，十九个亚非国家认为，必须运用一切可能的努力以保证联合国大会一致通过的决议的意图被执行而不是被破坏。由于这些考虑，十九个亚非国家希望提请秘书长迫切注意目前局势的真相。
    在信上签名的国家是：阿富汗、缅甸、锡兰、埃及、埃塞俄比亚、印度、印度尼西亚、伊朗、伊拉克、约旦、利比亚、摩洛哥、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、沙特阿拉伯、苏丹、突尼斯、叙利亚和也门。
