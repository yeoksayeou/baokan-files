Title: Soviet Union Prints Lenin's Works: 300 Million Copies in Forty Years
Author:
Date: 1957-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: According to statistics from the All-Soviet Book Chamber, from 1918 to April 1, 1957, the Soviet Union published more than 7,000 editions of Lenin's works. The total circulation reached approximately 300 million copies. These works were published in 84 languages.

During the Soviet era, "Lenin's Collected Works" has already been published four times. The most recent one, the fourth edition, was published in seventeen national languages of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联印行列宁著作  四十年来达三亿册

1957-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联印行列宁著作
    四十年来达三亿册
    据新华社21日讯　据全苏书库的统计，从1918年到1957年4月1日为止，苏联共出版了列宁的著作七千多版。总发行额约达三亿册。这些著作用八十四种文字出版。
    在苏维埃政权时代，“列宁全集”已经出版了四次。最近一次，即第四版是用苏联十七种民族文字出版的。
