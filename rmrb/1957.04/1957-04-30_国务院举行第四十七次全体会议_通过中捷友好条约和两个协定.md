Title: State Council Holds 47th Plenary Meeting; Approves Sino-Czech Friendship Treaty and Two Agreements
Author:
Date: 1957-04-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th - The State Council held its 47th plenary meeting on April 29th.

    After hearing the explanation from Yan Baohang, Director of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the meeting approved the Sino-Czech Friendship and Cooperation Treaty and decided to submit the Sino-Czech Friendship and Cooperation Treaty to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation. The meeting approved the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the Sino-Czech Health Cooperation Agreement, and decided to submit these two agreements to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

    After hearing the explanation from Li Yu'ang, Director of the Taxation Bureau of the Ministry of Finance, the meeting, in view of the fact that the country has entered a period of planned economic construction and that capitalist industry and commerce have implemented full-industry public-private joint ownership with fixed interest, approved the State Council's resolution to abolish Article 10 of the Interim Regulations on Industrial and Commercial Taxes. This article stipulated a 40% reduction in income tax for certain industries based on the needs of national economic construction.

    The meeting approved the Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province and the Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Durbert Mongol Autonomous County of Heilongjiang Province.

    The meeting approved the second batch of personnel lists submitted by the Ministry of National Defense for the awarding of medals and decided to submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation. The meeting also approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第四十七次全体会议  通过中捷友好条约和两个协定

1957-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第四十七次全体会议
    通过中捷友好条约和两个协定
    新华社29日讯　国务院全体会议第四十七次会议在4月29日举行。
    会议在听了外交部条约委员会主任委员阎宝航的说明后，通过了中捷友好合作条约，并且决定将中捷友好合作条约提交全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。会议通过了中捷文化合作协定和中捷保健合作协定，并且决定将这两个协定提交全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议听了财政部税务总局局长李予昂的说明后，根据国家已进入有计划的经济建设和资本主义工商业已实行全行业公私合营定股定息的情况，通过了国务院关于取消工商业税暂行条例第十条的决议。这一条规定根据国家经济建设需要分别对某些行业减征所得税40％。
    会议通过了云南省大理白族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）和黑龙江省杜尔伯特蒙古族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）。
    会议通过了国防部提出的第二批申请授予勋章的人员名单，并且决定提交全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。会议还通过了任免事项。
