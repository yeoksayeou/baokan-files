Title: Cyrankiewicz Says at Press Conference: Sino-Polish Talks Deepened Friendship and Unity
Author:
Date: 1957-04-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th. Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, said at a press conference held here this morning that the Polish government delegation's visit to China deepened the friendship and mutual understanding between China and Poland.

Chairman Cyrankiewicz expressed complete satisfaction with his talks in China. He said that the talks held with Chinese leaders actually "concerned all issues related to the two countries, the path of development of the two countries, the international situation, international cooperation, and peace," and that these talks had achieved "complete mutual understanding."

Chairman Cyrankiewicz expressed his firm belief that these talks would "help strengthen the friendship and unity of the socialist family and help strengthen international peace and cooperation."

Chairman Cyrankiewicz said that the Polish government delegation was warmly welcomed everywhere during its visit to Asian countries, and he was very satisfied with his contacts with the leaders of all these Asian countries. He said that the Polish representatives working on the International Supervisory Commissions in some Asian countries are using their work "to help the people there realize their desire to maintain peace and neutrality in this region of the world." He believes that these aspirations are correct and will be supported by people who advocate maintaining peace. Chairman Cyrankiewicz also praised India's role in the struggle for neutrality and the development of international cooperation.

Speaking about his impressions of his visit to China, Chairman Cyrankiewicz said that they saw the construction enthusiasm of the Chinese people and Chinese youth, the huge construction sites in Beijing, the new construction areas in Tianjin, and the factories and residences being built in China, and that the speed of China's development surprised them. China's construction will help strengthen the socialist family. (Picture attached)

### 西伦凯维兹在记者招待会上说  中波会谈加深了友谊和团结

1957-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹在记者招待会上说
    中波会谈加深了友谊和团结
    新华社11日讯　波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹今天上午在这里举行的记者招待会上说，波兰政府代表团的访问中国加深了中波两国的友谊和相互了解。
    西伦凯维兹主席对他在中国的会谈表示完全满意。他说，同中国领导人举行的会谈，实际上“涉及了有关两国的一切问题，两国发展的道路问题，国际局势问题，国际合作和和平问题”，这些会谈已取得了“完全的相互了解”。
    西伦凯维兹主席深信这些会谈将“有助于加强社会主义国家大家庭的友谊和团结，并且有助于加强国际和平与合作”。
    西伦凯维兹主席说，波兰政府代表团在亚洲各国访问时到处受到了热烈欢迎，他对他同所有这些亚洲国家的领导人的接触感到非常满意。他说，在亚洲的一些国家的国际监察委员会工作的波兰代表，正以自己的工作“来帮助那里的人民实现在世界上这个地区维护和平和中立的愿望”。他认为这些愿望是正确的，并且将得到主张维护和平的人民的支持。西伦凯维兹主席并且赞扬印度在主张中立和发展国际合作的斗争中所起的作用。
    在谈到访问中国的印象时，西伦凯维兹主席说，他们在中国看到中国人民和中国青年的建设热情，看到北京的庞大的建筑工地，看到天津的新建筑区，看到中国正在兴建工厂和住宅，中国的发展速度使他们惊奇。中国的建设将有助于加强社会主义国家的大家庭。（附图片）
