Title: One of the World's Largest Zoos: Berlin Zoo is Fascinating

Author:

Date: 1957-04-19
Page: 5

One of the World's Largest Zoos
    Berlin Zoo is Fascinating

    (Reported by our newspaper) The Berlin Zoo is one of the most attractive places in Berlin. Every Sunday, Berlin residents enjoy spending their holidays in this beautiful environment. Last year alone, one million residents visited this new zoo.

    The site of the Berlin Zoo was originally the garden of a feudal nobleman. When the Berlin Municipal Government decided to rebuild it in March 1955, the enthusiastic and confident Berliners immediately responded to the call. They said to the government: "If you want to build a zoo, we'll do it ourselves, we don't want the state to spend a penny!"

    The Berlin Zoo covers an area of 400 acres and is currently one of the largest zoos in the world. By the end of last year, it had 360 species of animals, totaling 1,320. The zoo's staff make every effort to arrange the animals' surrounding environment to be exactly the same as where they were originally born, so that visitors not only see the animals, but also understand the environment in which these animals live.

### 世界上最大的动物园之一  柏林动物园引人入胜

1957-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    世界上最大的动物园之一
    柏林动物园引人入胜
    本报讯　柏林动物园是柏林最吸引人的场所之一。每逢星期天，柏林居民总是喜欢在这个幽美的环境里愉快地度过他们的假日。去年一年之内，就有一百万居民访问了这座新的动物园。
    柏林动物园的原址，是一个封建贵族的花园。1955年3月，柏林市政府决定改建时，充满了热情和信心的柏林人，马上就热烈响应了这个号召。他们向政府说：“要搞动物园的话，我们自己来，我们不要国家花一文钱！”
    柏林动物园共占地四百英亩，它是目前世界最大的动物园之一。到去年年底为止，它已拥有三百六十种动物，共计一千三百二十只。动物园的工作人员尽力设法把动物的周围环境布置得和它们原来出生的地方一模一样，使参观的人不但看到了动物，而且也了解了这些动物所生活的环境。
