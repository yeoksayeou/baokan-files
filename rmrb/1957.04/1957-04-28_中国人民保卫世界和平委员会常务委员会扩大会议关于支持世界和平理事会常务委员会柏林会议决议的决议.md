Title: Resolution of the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace on Supporting the Resolution of the Berlin Meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

    The Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, after discussing the appeal for the cessation of nuclear weapon tests, the statement, and the resolution on the international situation adopted by the recent Berlin meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, unanimously expressed its support for these resolutions, believing that they will help to promote and expand the great movement of people of all countries to strive for peace and oppose war.
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace warmly supports the decision of the Berlin meeting to hold a meeting of the World Peace Council in Colombo, the capital of Ceylon, in June this year, and believes that this first meeting of the World Peace Council held in Asia will not only make new contributions to the cause of world peace but will also further strengthen the unity of the people of Asia and other regions in the cause of peace and will encourage the people of Asia and Africa to fight more resolutely for the development of the Bandung spirit, the maintenance of national independence, and the defense of peace.
    The Chinese people fully support the just struggle of the people of all countries, especially the Japanese people who have suffered atomic weapons disasters several times, against nuclear weapons, and resolutely oppose the stubborn refusal of the United States and British governments to the Soviet Union's proposal to conclude an international agreement prohibiting the use and cessation of nuclear weapon tests, and oppose the United States' expansion of atomic bases and atomic forces around the world. The Chinese people are willing to strengthen unity and cooperation with all peace forces in the world and work together to oppose nuclear weapons, oppose the threat of war, further ease the international situation, and consolidate lasting world peace.
                April 26, 1957

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会扩大会议关于支持世界和平理事会常务委员会柏林会议决议的决议

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会扩大会议关于支持世界和平理事会常务委员会柏林会议决议的决议
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会扩大会议，讨论了最近在柏林召开的世界和平理事会常务委员会会议所通过的关于要求停止核武器试验的呼吁书和声明及关于国际局势的决议，一致表示支持这些决议，并认为这些决议有助于推动和扩大各国人民为争取和平和反对战争的伟大运动。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会热烈支持柏林会议关于今年6月间在锡兰首都科伦坡举行世界和平理事会会议的决定，并且相信，这个第一次在亚洲召开的世界和平理事会会议不但将对世界和平事业作出新的贡献，并将进一步加强亚洲人民和其他地区人民在和平事业上的团结，将鼓舞亚、非各国人民更坚强地为发展万隆精神，维护民族独立、保卫和平而斗争。
    中国人民完全支持各国人民，特别是几次受原子武器灾害的日本人民所进行的反对核子武器的正义斗争，并坚决反对美国和英国政府顽固地拒绝苏联提出的缔结禁止使用和停止试验核武器的国际协定，反对美国在世界各地扩建原子基地和原子部队。中国人民愿和全世界一切和平力量加强团结和合作，为反对核武器、反对战争威胁、进一步缓和国际局势和巩固世界持久和平而共同努力。
                1957年4月26日
