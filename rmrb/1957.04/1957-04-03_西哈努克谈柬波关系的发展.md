Title: Sihanouk Discusses the Development of Cambodian-Polish Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-04-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 2nd: Cambodian Prince Norodom Sihanouk recently told a Polish Press Agency reporter that the Polish government delegation's visit to Cambodia confirmed the deep, sincere friendship and mutual understanding between the peoples of the two countries. When discussing the prospects for the development of Cambodian-Polish relations with Polish Press Agency reporter Babad, Sihanouk said that Cambodia was very satisfied and pleased with Poland's proposals to send experts in various fields to help Cambodia's economic construction, and to grant scholarships to Cambodian students and economic and social activists to study in Poland.

### 西哈努克谈柬波关系的发展

1957-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克谈柬波关系的发展
    据新华社华沙2日电　柬埔寨西哈努克亲王最近对波兰通讯社记者说，波兰政府代表团对柬埔寨的访问证实了两国人民之间的深厚、真诚的友谊和互相的了解。西哈努克在同波兰通讯社记者巴巴德谈到柬波两国关系发展的前景时说，柬埔寨对于波兰提出的关于派遣各方面的专家帮助柬埔寨进行经济建设，以及给予柬埔寨学生和经济、社会方面的活动分子奖学金使他们到波兰进行学习的建议，感到非常满意和高兴。
