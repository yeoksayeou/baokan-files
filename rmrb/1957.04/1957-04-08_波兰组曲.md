Title: Polish Suite
Author: Zou Difan
Date: 1957-04-08
Page: 4

    Polish Suite
    Zou Difan
    In the broadcasting room of the shipyard
    In the Gdańsk shipyard,
    In the broadcasting room of the shipyard,
    Let us greet the workers,
    Chinese is broadcast in the shipyard.
    Here steel is singing,
    White clouds flow through the overhead power lines,
    Cranes are like skyscraper buildings,
    New ships enter the water, waves surging.
    At the moment of Polish labor and creation,
    Our voice is broadcast in the shipyard.
    "Danzig" is no longer an imperialist
    "corridor,"
    We regard the ocean as a long corridor,
    Friendship ships travel between China and Poland.

    To the Mazowsze Folk Song and Dance Ensemble
    Flowers sway in the Polish fields,
    The colors dye your dance skirts and robes.
    Sing, dance,
    This is the laughter of young men and women.
    Your melody:
    A cuckoo in the cranberry forest.
    Your dance:
    A maiden searching for a spring in the forest.
    Harvesting seeds from their own soil,
    And sowing them back into their own soil,
    More and more people are laughing,
    Poland, you nourish your own children with milk.

    Visiting Auschwitz Concentration Camp
    Trenches and barbed wire plus guard towers,
    Cremation ovens, gas chambers plus gallows.
    Blood once flowed here,
    Freedom blooms here.
    How big Hitler's barracks were,
    The concentration camp is even more enormous than the barracks.
    Oh, hard labor, torture, and shooting,
    How afraid the dictator is of the people!
    Auschwitz Concentration Camp,
    You are by no means the tomb of the victims.
    You are a blazing underground fire,
    Burning the hearts of Polish patriots.
    Oh, Auschwitz Concentration Camp,
    Witness to the history of suffering.
    Shattering shackles, breaking through cages, Poland marches forward,
    Shackles and cages have become exhibits of revolutionary history.

### 波兰组曲
邹荻帆
1957-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰组曲
    邹荻帆
    在造船厂的播音房
    在革但斯克的造船厂，
    在造船厂的播香房，
    让我们向工人们问好，
    中国话在造船厂播响。
    这里是钢铁在歌唱，
    白云穿流在高架的电线上，
    起重机像摩天的建筑，
    新船入水，排波涌浪。
    在波兰人劳动创造的时刻，
    我们的声音在造船厂播响。
    “但泽”再不是帝国主义的
    “走廊”，
    我们把海洋当长廊，
    友谊的船只在中国和波兰来往。
    给玛佐夫舍民间歌舞团
    波兰的田野上花朵摇摆，
    花色染上你们的舞裙和舞袍。
    歌唱吧，舞蹈吧，
    这是少男少女在欢笑。
    你们的歌调：
    红莓林中的杜鹃鸟。
    你们的舞蹈：
    少女在森林把泉水找。
    从自己的土壤收获种子，
    又播撒到自己的土壤，
    欢笑的是更多更多的人，
    波兰啊，你用奶水把自己的孩子哺养。
    访奥斯威辛集中营壕沟和电网再加警卫塔，
    烧人炉、煤汽室加上绞刑架。
    血，曾经在这里流，
    自由就在这里开花。
    希特勒军营多么大，
    集中营比军营更庞大。
    哦，苦役、酷刑和枪杀，
    独裁者对人民多么害怕！
    奥斯威辛集中营，
    你决不是受难者的坟。
    你是一团熊熊地下火，
    燃烧着波兰爱国者的心。
    哦，奥斯威辛集中营，
    苦难历史的见证人。
    粉碎枷锁，突破牢笼，波兰向前进，
    枷锁和牢笼成了革命历史的展览品。
