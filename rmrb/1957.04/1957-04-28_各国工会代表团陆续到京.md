Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1957-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The delegation from sixteen trade union organizations in Thailand, headed by Nai Prasert Khongphlun Chit, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

This first Thai trade union delegation to visit China was invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in Beijing.

At the airport, delegation member Nai Supachaiya Srisathit told reporters that the purpose of the Thai trade union representatives' visit to China was to promote mutual understanding and cooperation between the people and workers of China and Thailand. He believed that no force could separate the people and workers of China and Thailand.

Also arriving in Beijing by plane this afternoon were the All-German Trade Union delegation, headed by Rudi Speckin, the Mongolian Trade Union delegation, headed by Sambu, and twelve Indonesian trade union leaders. These twelve Indonesian trade union leaders represent the Central Organization of Indonesian Workers, the Customs Union, the Indonesian Village Officials Association, the Health Union, the Pawnshop Union, the Indonesian Islamic Union, the All-Indonesia Bank Union, the Ministry of Education and Culture Union, the Indonesian Civil Rights Unity Union, the Immigration Office Union, the Indonesian Teachers' Association, and the Indonesian Workers' Federation, who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

The Singapore Trade Union delegation, headed by Lee Soo Lim, Vice Chairman of the Singapore Trades Union Congress, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

### 各国工会代表团陆续到京

1957-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表团陆续到京
    新华社27日讯　泰国十六个工会组织的代表团团长乃普腊塞·孔普伦·契特等一行在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这个第一个来中国进行访问的泰国工会代表团，是应中华全国总工会的邀请来北京参加“五一”观礼的。
    代表团团员乃苏帕恰亚·斯里萨提在机场向记者发表谈话说，泰国工会代表来中国进行访问的目的，是为了促进中泰两国人民和工人之间的相互谅解和合作。他相信在中泰两国人民和工人之间是没有什么力量可以使他们分离的。
    今天下午乘飞机到达北京的，还有以鲁迪·斯彼金为首的全德国工会代表团、以桑布为首的蒙古工会代表团和印度尼西亚工会代表十二人。这十二位印度尼西亚工会领袖是代表印度尼西亚中央职工会、关税工会、印度尼西亚村公所人员联合会、卫生工会、典当工会、印度尼西亚伊斯兰工会、全印度尼西亚银行工会、文教部工会、印度尼西亚民权统一工会、移民所工会、印度尼西亚教师联合会和印度尼西亚职工联合会前来北京参加“五一”观礼的。
    在今晚乘火车到达北京的，有以新加坡职工总会副主席李树林为首的新加坡工会代表团。
