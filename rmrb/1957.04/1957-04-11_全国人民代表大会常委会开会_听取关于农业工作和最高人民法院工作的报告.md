Title: NPC Standing Committee Meets; Listens to Reports on Agricultural Work and the Work of the Supreme People's Court
Author:
Date: 1957-04-11
Page: 1

    NPC Standing Committee Meets
    Listens to Reports on Agricultural Work and the Work of the Supreme People's Court
    Xinhua News Agency, April 9th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 59th enlarged meeting this afternoon. The meeting heard a report on agricultural work by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 60th enlarged meeting this afternoon. The meeting heard a work report from Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, on the work of the Supreme People's Court.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会开会  听取关于农业工作和最高人民法院工作的报告

1957-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会开会
    听取关于农业工作和最高人民法院工作的报告
    新华社9日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今日下午举行第五十九次会议扩大会议。会议听取了农业部副部长刘瑞龙作的关于农业工作的报告。
    新华社10日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今日下午举行第六十次会议扩大会议。会议听取了最高人民法院副院长张志让作的最高人民法院的工作报告。
