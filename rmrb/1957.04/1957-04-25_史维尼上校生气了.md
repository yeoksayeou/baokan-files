Title: Colonel Sweeney is Angry
Author: Lu Wei
Date: 1957-04-25
Page: 6

    Colonel Sweeney is Angry
    Lu Wei
    In the American Embassy in Jordan,
    Colonel Sweeney is getting angry:
    "Nowar says I interfered in Jordan's internal affairs,
    "What's so strange about that?
    "Nowar is too inexperienced,
    "That's what we Americans do!
    "He can go ahead and announce the truth,
    "I won't deny it, and I won't blush.
    "What's annoying is that he didn't spell my name right,
    "This greatly hinders my future;
    "If the State Department rewards merits in the future,
    "Tell me: who will get this reward for subversion?"
    (Note) Former Jordanian Army Chief of Staff Nowar accused Colonel Sweeney, the U.S. military attaché in Jordan, of interfering in Jordan's internal affairs. The U.S. Embassy has declined to comment. Sweeney shamelessly said, "I hope they spell my name right."

### 史维尼上校生气了
卢韦
1957-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    史维尼上校生气了
    卢韦
    在约旦的美国大使馆里，
    史维尼上校正在生气：
    “诺瓦尔说我干涉约旦内政，
    “这事情又有什么希奇？
    “诺瓦尔这小子见识太少，
    “我们美国人干的就是这一套！
    “他尽管去宣布事情真相，
    “我是既不否认，脸上也不发烧。
    “可恼的是他没有把我的名字拼对，
    “这对我的前程大有妨碍；
    “如果将来国务院论功行赏，
    “请问：这一份颠覆有功的奖赏给谁？”
    （注）约旦前陆军参谋长诺瓦尔指责美国驻约旦大使馆武官史维尼上校干涉约旦内政。美国大使馆对此拒绝评论。史维尼则只是无耻地说：“我希望他们把我的名字拼对。”
