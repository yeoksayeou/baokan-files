Title: National People's Congress Standing Committee Meets to Hear Report on Education Work
Author: 
Date: 1957-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 57th enlarged meeting on the afternoon of the 6th, and listened to a report on education work by Dong Chun-cai, Vice Minister of Education.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会开会  听取关于教育工作的报告

1957-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会开会
    听取关于教育工作的报告
    新华社6日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在6日下午举行第五十七次会议扩大会议，听取了教育部副部长董纯才关于教育工作的报告。
