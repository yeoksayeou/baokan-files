Title: British Trade Minister Arrives in Moscow, Sino-British Continue Business Negotiations
Author: 
Date: 1947-06-27
Page: 1

　　British Trade Minister Arrives in Moscow
    Sino-British Continue Business Negotiations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd Dispatch] TASS, Moscow: British Overseas Trade Minister Wilson, accompanied by his entourage, arrived in Moscow on the 20th to continue business negotiations. Those who welcomed him at the airport included Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Menshikov, Director of the Currency Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade Pistorov, Deputy Director of the Treaty Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade Kukinsky, and officials from the British Embassy also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 英贸易大臣抵莫斯科  苏英继续商务谈判

1947-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　英贸易大臣抵莫斯科
    苏英继续商务谈判
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：英国海外贸易大臣威尔逊偕随员于二十日抵莫斯科，继续商务谈判。到机场欢迎者，有苏联对外贸易部副部长门雪科夫、苏联对外贸易部货币司司长彼斯托罗夫、苏联对外贸易部条约司副司长库金斯基等，英大使馆官员亦到机场欢迎。
