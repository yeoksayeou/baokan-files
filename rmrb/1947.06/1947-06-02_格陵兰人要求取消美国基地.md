Title: Greenlanders Demand the Abolition of US Base
Author:
Date: 1947-06-02
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st. Comprehensive news: Marshall declared to the American press on the 29th that the United States had notified Denmark that it was preparing to begin discussions on a new agreement regarding the US air base in Greenland. At the same time, the Copenhagen, Denmark newspaper *Land og Folk* published an article titled "Greenlanders Oppose Base," stating: "When discussing the US military base in Greenland, no one thought to consult the Greenlanders' opinion on the matter. Undoubtedly, the Greenlanders hope to abolish these bases as soon as possible."
    
    Further news: The United States has received a request from the Danish government requesting a meeting to discuss abolishing the 1941 agreement on the US military occupation of Greenland.

### 格陵兰人要求取消美国基地

1947-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　格陵兰人要求取消美国基地
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】综合消息：马歇尔于二十九日对美国报界宣称：美国已通知丹麦准备开始讨论美国在格陵兰的空军基地的新协定。与此同时，丹麦哥本哈根《兰得·奥格·福尔克》报则以“格陵兰人反对基地”为题发表论文称：“当讨论关于美国在格陵兰之军事基地时，没有一个人想到征询格陵兰人对此事之意见，毫无疑义的格陵兰人希望尽早取消这些基地。”
    又讯：美国已接获丹麦政府请求书，请求召开会议以讨论废止美军占领格陵兰的一九四一年协定。
