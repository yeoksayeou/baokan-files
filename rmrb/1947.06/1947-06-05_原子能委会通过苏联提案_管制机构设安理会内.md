Title: Atomic Energy Commission Passes Soviet Proposal; Control Agency to be Established Within the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1947-06-05
Page: 3

　　Atomic Energy Commission Passes Soviet Proposal
    Control Agency to be Established Within the Security Council
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st] Chenggong Lake News: The Executive Committee of the United Nations Atomic Energy Commission unanimously passed the Soviet proposal on the 29th of last month to establish the International Atomic Energy Control Agency within the scope of the Security Council. The motion stated: In accordance with the resolution of the General Assembly on December 14th of last year, the conference will establish the Atomic Energy Control Agency within the scope of the Security Council.

### 原子能委会通过苏联提案  管制机构设安理会内

1947-06-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　原子能委会通过苏联提案
    管制机构设安理会内
    【新华社陕北一日电】成功湖讯：联合国原子能委员会执行委员会于上月二十九日一致通过苏联之提案，将国际原子能管制机构设立在安理会范围以内。该议案称：该会为遵循去年十二月十四日大会之决议，将原子能管制机构设立在安理会范围以内。
