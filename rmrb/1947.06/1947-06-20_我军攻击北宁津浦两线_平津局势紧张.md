Title: Our Army Attacks the Beining and Jinpu Lines; Situation Tense in Tianjin and Beijing

Author:

Date: 1947-06-20
Page: 1

　　Our Army Attacks the Beining and Jinpu Lines
    Situation Tense in Tianjin and Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th] Shanghai's Ta Kung Pao reported: On the night of June 11th, the Tianjin Chiang Kai-shek military police conducted a large-scale search of residences, arresting a total of 762 people. Furthermore, according to Agence France-Presse: In recent days, due to the Communist army's attacks on railways in North China, the situation in Tianjin has become increasingly tense. Armored vehicles are patrolling the streets, the cost of living is rising, and traffic between Beijing and Tianjin has been cut off. In addition, according to the Peiping Daily News: The Chiang government has allocated 600,000 billion Chiang currency to build bunkers in the suburbs of Peiping, and the people in Beijing and Tianjin are in extreme panic.

### 我军攻击北宁津浦两线  平津局势紧张

1947-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军攻击北宁津浦两线
    平津局势紧张
    【新华社陕北十八日电】沪大公报载：六月十一日天津蒋家宪警于夜晚大规模搜查居户，共逮捕七百六十二人。又据法国新闻处讯：连日来，由于中共军攻击华北铁路，天津局势日益紧张，装甲车在街上巡逻，生活费用递增，平津交通已断绝。另据平报载：蒋政府已拨蒋币六十万万元，于北平近郊修筑碉堡，平津两地人心惶恐万分。
