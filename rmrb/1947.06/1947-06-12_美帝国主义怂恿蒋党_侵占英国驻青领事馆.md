Title: American Imperialism Instigates Chiang Kai-shek Clique to Seize British Consulate in Qingdao
Author:
Date: 1947-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 10th—According to the Qingdao "Junmin Daily," after Wang Yaowu confiscated the British Consulate building in Jinan last month, the British Consulate in Qingdao lodged a serious protest with the Chiang Kai-shek's Shandong Provincial Government and the Second Pacification Zone Commander Wang Yaowu, citing "seizure of diplomatic consular property." Wang stated that it was being guarded on their behalf during wartime. According to insiders, the Chiang government's tough stance this time was instigated by American imperialism.

### 美帝国主义怂恿蒋党  侵占英国驻青领事馆

1947-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　美帝国主义怂恿蒋党
    侵占英国驻青领事馆
    【新华社山东十日电】据青岛《军民日报》透露：王耀武于上月将济南英领事馆房屋没收后，英驻青领事馆即以“侵占外交领事房屋”向蒋记山东省政府及第二绥靖区司令官王跃武提出严重抗议。王表示军事时期，代为看守。据熟悉内幕者谈：蒋政府此次强硬态度，乃美帝国主义所促使。
