Date: 1947-06-18

Regulations of the Jinan Bank Head Office on the Exchange of Damaged Currency

    The regulations of this bank on the exchange of damaged currency have now been revised and are announced as follows:

    I. Any banknote with three-quarters of the surface area remaining on any side, confirmed to be genuine, will be exchanged at its original value (same for vertical banknotes).

    II. Any banknote with one-half of the surface area remaining will be exchanged at eighty percent of half its value.

    III. If the character head and serial number of the left and right halves of a banknote are not the same, it will still be exchanged at its original value.

    IV. Wet or oil-soaked banknotes that are complete and confirmed to be genuine will be exchanged at their original value.

    V. Banknotes with less than one-half of the surface area remaining will not be exchanged.

    VI. Banknotes pieced together from multiple fragments, whether a whole or half note, will not be exchanged.

    VII. The handling fee for exchanging damaged banknotes through entrusted exchange offices is set at ten to fifteen yuan per hundred banknotes.

### 冀南银行总行残缺本币兑换办法规定

1947-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南银行总行残缺本币兑换办法规定
    本行残缺本币兑换办法，兹经重新修正，公布如下：一、票面靠任何一边存在四分之三确系真票者按原值兑换（立式票同）。二、票面存在二分之一者，以半数计八折兑换。三、票面左右两半截字头号码不相同者，亦按原值兑换。四、水湿油浸票面完整确系真票者，原值兑换。五、各种票面不足二分之一者不予兑换。六、票面多块凑成者，不论整张半张一律不予兑换。七、委托兑换所代兑破票其手续费规定每百张十元至十五元计。
