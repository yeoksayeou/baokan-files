Title: Liaodong Draws Four Hundred Cadres to New Areas to Mobilize the Masses
Author:
Date: 1947-06-17
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Liaodong, 13th: Under the slogan of the Liaoning Provincial Party Committee, "Mobilize the broad masses in newly recovered areas, thoroughly destroy the feudal exploitation of labor by landlords, and build strong and consolidated base areas," the Liaodong Sub-district recently drew more than four hundred cadres at the district level and above to form five work teams to work in the newly recovered areas.

### 辽东抽调四百干部分赴新区发动群众

1947-06-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　辽东抽调四百干部分赴新区发动群众
    【新华社辽东十三日电】辽东分区在辽宁省委“发动新收复区广大人民，彻底摧毁地主封建劳力，建设强大巩固根据地”的口号下，近抽调区以上干部四百余人组成五个工作团，分赴新收复区工作。
