Title: Three Thousand Shandong Chiang Kai-shek Puppets Repent and Re-educate Themselves to Us
Author:
Date: 1947-06-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 1st. Under the military prestige of the East China People's Liberation Army's several large-scale annihilations of the enemy in Laiwu, Tai'an, and Menglianggu, Chiang Kai-shek's puppet soldiers and "Returning Home Legions" in Jinan, Sixian, Changle, and Anqiu strongholds fled in droves. According to incomplete statistics, 1,925 people from Zichuan, Boshan, Zhangqiu, and Yidu in the 96th Army and the newly編73rd Army of Chiang Kai-shek stationed in Jinan have fled back to their hometowns. More than 3,000 members of the "Returning Home Legions" have repented and re-educated themselves to the local government. Wang Xianliang, the Chiang Kai-shek Security Captain of Anqiu, led more than 20 men, carrying more than 20 long and short guns, and revolted to the people's armed forces.

### 山东三千蒋伪向我悔过自新

1947-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　山东三千蒋伪向我悔过自新
    【新华社山东一日电】在华东人民解放军莱芜、泰安、孟良崮几次大规模歼敌的军威声势下，济南、丝县、昌乐、安邱据点蒋伪士兵与“还乡团”纷纷逃亡。据不完全统计：驻济南之蒋九十六军与新编七十三军中之淄川、博山、章邱、益都人已有一千九百二十五人逃返家乡。“还乡团”三千余人向当地政府悔过自新。安邱蒋保安队长王宪良率部二十余人，携长短枪二十余支，向人民武装反正。
