Title: Military Negotiations Between U.S. and Turkish Reactionaries
Author:
Date: 1947-06-02
Page: 3
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 31st: TASS, Ankara, May 28th: Negotiations are underway between the U.S. military delegation and representatives of the Turkish General Staff. The "Cumhuriyet" newspaper reported that prominent issues in the negotiations include the motorization of the Turkish army and the construction of strategic transportation lines and naval ports. The U.S. Air Force delegation joined the negotiations on May 26th. The newspaper pointed out that the two delegations (referring to the military and air force delegations) will inspect several major strategic areas within Turkey in June.

### 美土反动派间进行军事谈判

1947-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　美土反动派间进行军事谈判
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】塔斯社安哥拉五月二十八日电：美国军事代表团和土耳其总参谋部代表之间的谈判正在进行中。堪舒里格特报顷报导：在谈判中显著的问题就是土耳其军队的摩托化及战略交通线和军港的建筑。美国空军代表团五月二十六日参加了谈判。该报指出：该两个代表团（按：指军事和空军代表团）将于六月间往土国内几个首要的战略地区视察。
