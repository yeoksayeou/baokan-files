Title: Five-Nation Social Democratic Party Conference Unanimously Declares Cooperation with Communists, Opposes American Imperialist Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1947-06-11
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th—According to TASS: The conference of Social Democratic Parties of Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, and Romania, held in Budapest, Hungary, has successfully concluded. The conference unanimously declared: Cooperation with the Communist Party is important. The conference also shouted: Oppose the conspiracy and attempts of American imperialism to drag our countries into its orbit! The Social Democratic Parties of the five countries all announced that they are prepared to cooperate with all peace-loving and freedom-loving countries headed by the Soviet Union, and rapidly cut off foreign interference that devours their national independence. The Hungarian delegation declared: The strength of the Hungarian Social Democratic and Communist Parties and their united front is a guarantee for defeating the reactionaries and achieving Hungary's rebirth.

### 五国社会民主党会议  一致宣称与共产党合作  反对美帝国主义的阴谋

1947-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　五国社会民主党会议
    一致宣称与共产党合作
    反对美帝国主义的阴谋
    【新华社陕北八日电】据塔斯社讯：保、捷、波、匈、罗等国社会民主党在匈京布达佩斯举行的会议顷已胜利闭幕。会议一致宣称：与共产党合作是重要的。会议并高呼：反对美帝国主义将我们国家拖到它的轨道上去的阴谋与企图！五国社会民主党均宣布他们准备与以苏联为首的一切爱好和平及自由的国家合作，并急速斩断吞食他们民族独立的外国干涉。匈牙利代表团宣称：匈牙利社共两党的力量及其联合阵线，是击败反动派并获得匈牙利新生的保证。
