Title: Regulations of the Jinan Bank Headquarters on the Exchange of Damaged Local Currency

Author: 
Date: 1947-06-15
Page: 1

　　Regulations of the Jinan Bank Headquarters on the Exchange of Damaged Local Currency
　　The regulations of our bank on the exchange of damaged local currency, having been revised and amended, are now promulgated as follows:
　　I. Any banknote of which three-quarters of the surface remains on any side and is confirmed to be genuine shall be exchanged at its original value (applies to vertical banknotes as well).
　　II. Any banknote of which one-half of the surface remains shall be exchanged at eighty percent of half its value.
　　III. Any banknote of which the serial numbers on the left and right halves do not match shall also be exchanged at its original value.
　　IV. Any banknote that is wet, oil-soaked, and intact, and is confirmed to be genuine, shall be exchanged at its original value.
　　V. Any banknote of which less than one-half of the surface remains shall not be exchanged.
　　VI. Any banknote pieced together from multiple fragments, regardless of whether it forms a whole or half, shall not be exchanged.
　　VII. The handling fee for entrusted exchange offices to exchange damaged banknotes is set at ten to fifty yuan per hundred banknotes.

### 冀南银行总行残缺本币兑换办法规定

1947-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南银行总行残缺本币兑换办法规定
    本行残缺本币兑换办法，兹经重新修正，公布如下：一、票面靠任何一边存在四分之三确系真票者按原值兑换（立式票同）。二、票面存在二分之一者，以半数计八折兑换。三、票面左右两半截字头号码不相同者，亦按原值兑换。四、水湿油浸票面完整确系真票者，原值兑换。五、各种票面不足二分之一者不予兑换。六、票面多块凑成者，不论整张半张一律不予兑换。七、委托兑换所代兑破票其手续费规定每百张十元至五十元计。
