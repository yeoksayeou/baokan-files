　　Brief News
    [Our newspaper, Shahe dispatch] This spring, the masses in Shahe engaged in afforestation and achieved great results. According to the current summary, the entire county has planted more than 740,000 trees. The most successful plantings were the willow trees planted along the Shahe River. For example, Gao Miao Village originally planted 31,000 trees, of which 30,946 have survived, representing a survival rate of 99.8%. The Taihang Administrative Office has now issued a large amount of bonuses and plans to allocate a large portion to reward Shahe. The Taihang Xingtai Agricultural and Forestry Bureau is discussing with the Shahe County Government to select afforestation models. (Xingtai Agricultural and Forestry Bureau Communications Group)

### 简讯

1947-06-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　简讯
    【本报沙河讯】沙河今春群众植树造林，获得巨大成绩，根据目前总结，全县共植树七十四万余棵，成活数目最多的是沙河沿岸所栽植的杨柳树。如高庙村原栽三万一千棵，已成活三万零九百四十六棵，成活率占百分之九十九点八。现太行行署已发下巨额奖金，准备抽出大部奖励沙河。太行邢台农林局正同沙河县府讨论评选造林模范。（邢台农林局通讯小组）
