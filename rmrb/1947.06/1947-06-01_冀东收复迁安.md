Title: Jidong Recaptures Qian'an
Author:
Date: 1947-06-01
Page: 1

　　Jidong Recaptures Qian'an
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, Dispatch of the 30th] The Jidong People's Liberation Army liberated Qian'an County, east of the Luan River, on the night of the 25th, as well as Shimenzhai, about 40 Chinese miles northwest of Shanhaiguan. The results of the battle are currently being tallied.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Ji-Cha-Re-Liao, Dispatch of the 30th] General Han Meicun, the Major General Commander of the Northeast Security Third Detachment, who revolted in the Lingyuan battle in southern Rehe, and his troops have officially formed the Independent First Brigade of the Democratic National Salvation Army. General Han Meicun serves as the brigade commander. The brigade was announced on May 9th. It issued a nationwide circular denouncing Chiang Kai-shek's dictatorial, traitorous, and civil war policies. It vehemently condemned his political corruption, treason for personal gain, and corruption. It also announced that the purpose of the army's uprising and future battles is to achieve an independent, peaceful, and democratic New China.

### 冀东收复迁安

1947-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀东收复迁安
    【新华社晋察冀三十日急电】冀东人民解放军于二十五日夜解放滦河东岸之迁安县城，及山海关西北约四十华里之石门寨。战果正清查中。
    【新华社冀察热辽三十日电】在热南凌源战役起义之东北保安第三支队少将司令韩梅村将军及其所率部队，已正式组成民主救国军独立第一旅。由韩梅村将军任旅长。该旅由五月九日宣布成立。通电全国声讨蒋介石之内战独裁卖国。痛斥其政治腐败卖国求荣与贪污腐化。并宣布该军此次起义及今后为实现独立和平、民主新中国而战的宗旨。
