Title: Qingdao Jimo Outskirts: Our Army Captures Aoshanwei
Author:
Date: 1947-06-07
Page: 1

　　Qingdao Jimo Outskirts
    Our Army Captures Aoshanwei
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 4th] A certain unit of the Jiaodong People's Liberation Army, on the night of the 29th of last month, captured the Qingdao-Jimo outer defense stronghold of Aoshanwei in one fell swoop, wiping out most of the Qingbao First Brigade's Sun Ke's detachment. It is estimated that more than 60 enemies were killed or wounded, more than 140 were captured, seven light machine guns, twenty American-style rifles, a jeep, and some other military supplies were seized.

### 青岛即墨外围  我军克鳌山卫

1947-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　青岛即墨外围
    我军克鳌山卫
    【新华社山东四日电】胶东人民解放军某部，于上月二十九日夜一举攻克青（岛）即（墨）外卫据点鳌山卫，将守敌青保一大队孙克分部大部歼灭，计毙伤敌六十余，俘一百四十余名，缴获轻机枪七挺，美式长短枪二十支，吉普车一辆，其他军用品一部。
