Title: French Gas and Electricity Workers Strike Against Ramadier
Author:
Date: 1947-06-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th. Paris dispatch: Following the general strike of French national railway workers, many gas and electricity factories have joined the strike movement against the wage policy of the Ramadier government. Workers at five gas and electricity factories near Paris went on strike, and the Gas and Electricity Workers' Union rejected the Ramadier government's proposals for resolving the union's demands.

### 法煤气电力工人罢工反对拉马第

1947-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　法煤气电力工人罢工反对拉马第
    【新华社陕北十四日电】巴黎讯：继法全国铁路工人总罢工之后，许多煤气及电力工厂纷纷参加反对拉马第政府工资政策之罢工运动。巴黎附近五处煤气及电力工厂工人举行罢工，煤气及电力职工联合会并拒绝拉马第政府关于解决该工会要求之建议。
