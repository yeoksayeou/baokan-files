Title: Japan Forms New Cabinet, Tetsu Katayama Appointed Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1947-06-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd: Tokyo dispatch: The tripartite coalition cabinet (Socialist Party, Democratic Party, People's Cooperative Party) of Japan, with Socialist Tetsu Katayama as Prime Minister, has been formed. The list of cabinet members was announced on the 31st of last month: Foreign Minister Hitoshi Ashida (President of the Democratic Party), Interior Minister Kozaemon Kimura (Democratic Party), Finance Minister Shotaro Yano (Democratic Party), Justice Minister Yoshio Suzuki (Socialist Party), Education Minister Tatsuo Morito (Socialist Party), Minister of Commerce and Industry Chozaburo Mizutani (Socialist Party), Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Rikizo Hirano (Socialist Party), Minister of Communications Takeo Miki (People's Cooperative Party), Minister of Health and Welfare Teikichi Matsuoka (Democratic Party), Minister of Transportation Yoshizo Tsuchiya (Democratic Party), Chief Cabinet Secretary Suehiro Nishio (Socialist Party), Minister of State Suehiro Nishio (Socialist Party), Rikizo Hirano (Socialist Party), Junzo ＠mori (People's Cooperative Party), Heima Hayashi (People's Cooperative Party), Mitsuaki Yonekubo (Socialist Party), Takao Saito (Democratic Party), Hiroo Wada (Independent). According to official statements: Junzo ＠mori may be appointed as President of the Demobilization Agency, Mitsuaki Yonekubo may be appointed as the Minister of Labor when it is established, and Hiroo Wada may be appointed as the Director-General of the Economic Stabilization Board. The Director-General of the Cabinet Legislation Bureau and the Deputy Chief Cabinet Secretary will be appointed later.

### 日本组成新阁片山哲任首相

1947-06-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本组成新阁片山哲任首相
    【新华社陕北二日电】东京讯：以社会党片山哲为首相的日本三党（社会党、民主党、国民协同党）联合内阁，顷告组成。阁员名单已于上月三十一日宣布：外相芦田均（民主党总裁）、内相木材小左卫门（民主党）、藏相矢野庄太郎（民主党）、法相铃木义雄（社会党），文相森户辰男（社会党）、商工相水谷长三郎（社会党）、农林相平野力三（社会党）、递信相三木武夫（国民协同党）、厚生相松定吉一（民主党）、运输相地义三（民主党）、内阁书记官长西尾末广（社会党）、国务大臣西尾末广（社会党）、平野力三（社会党）、＠森顺造（国民协同党）、林平马（国民协同党）、米洼满亮（社会党）、斋藤隆夫（民主党）、和田博雄（无党派）。据官方声明表示：＠森顺造可能被任命为复员局总裁，米洼满亮可能任行将成立之劳工相，和田博雄可能任经济安定本部长官，内阁法制局长及副书记官长以后再行任命。
