Title: London Worker's Daily Exposes US-UK Plot to Create New Crisis in Hungary
Author:
Date: 1947-06-23
Page: 3

　　London Worker's Daily Exposes
    US-UK Plot to Create New Crisis in Hungary
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, June 20th: London - The British Communist Party's newspaper, the Worker's Daily, recently published a front-page article exposing the British political mission in Hungary for maintaining close ties with personnel from the former fascist regime in Hungary. It also revealed that the British and American governments are currently attempting to create a new crisis in Hungary. The article concluded by denouncing the British demand that the Soviet government provide information on the situation in Hungary as laughable.

### 伦敦工人日报揭露  英美图在匈制造新危机

1947-06-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　伦敦工人日报揭露
    英美图在匈制造新危机
    【新华社陕北二十日电】伦敦讯：英共机关报工人日报日前以首页地位刊载专文揭露英驻匈牙利政治代表团与匈国前法西斯政权人员保持密切联系，并揭露英美政府现正企图在匈牙利制造新危机。该文最后指斥：英国要求苏政府就匈国情势提供情报，实属可笑。
