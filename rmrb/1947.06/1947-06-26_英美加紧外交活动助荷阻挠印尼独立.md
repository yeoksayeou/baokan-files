Title: Britain and the US Intensify Diplomatic Efforts to Help the Netherlands Obstruct Indonesian Independence
Author:
Date: 1947-06-26
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th: Tass, The Hague, reported: Handelsblad reports that regarding the crisis in Dutch-Indonesian relations, Britain and the United States are intensifying their diplomatic activities. Representatives of the United States and Britain in The Hague, Batavia, and Yogyakarta have become active. Recently, there have been widespread rumors here that the US military has obtained permission from the Dutch government to take aerial photographs of Indonesian islands to create military maps. These rumors have been confirmed by qualified individuals.

### 英美加紧外交活动助荷阻挠印尼独立

1947-06-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　英美加紧外交活动助荷阻挠印尼独立
    【新华社陕北十八日电】塔斯社海牙讯：汉德尔斯伯拉德报称：关于荷兰与印尼关系之危机，英美正加强他们的外交活动，美国和英国在海牙以及巴达维亚和日惹的代表都已活跃起来。近来此间盛传美军得到荷兰政府之许可，从空中摄取印尼各岛之照片，以绘制军事地图，此种传闻，已为有资格人士所证实。
