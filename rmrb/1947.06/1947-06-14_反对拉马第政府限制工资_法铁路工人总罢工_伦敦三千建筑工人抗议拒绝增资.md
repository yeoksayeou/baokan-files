Title: Against Ramadier Government's Wage Restrictions: French Railway Workers' General Strike; London Three Thousand Construction Workers Protest Refusal to Increase Wages
Author:
Date: 1947-06-14
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th: Paris dispatch: In order to demand improved living conditions and oppose the Ramadier government's wage restriction policy, French national railway workers began a general strike on the 7th of this month, and railway transportation throughout France has been halted. According to a United Press dispatch: At present, the Ramadier government's Ministry of Transportation is using requisitioned military vehicles to maintain transportation between major cities, and it is said that the military is allocating aircraft to strengthen transportation between various ports and the interior.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: According to a TASS dispatch from London: A London assembly of three thousand construction workers strongly protested the landowners' and arbitration tribunal's refusal to their wage increase demands. Speakers criticized the government's lack of sympathy for the workers' demands and criticized the executive committee for delaying the assembly of workers for active action. The assembly also raised serious protests against the introduction of Poles and displaced persons into industry.

### 反对拉马第政府限制工资  法铁路工人总罢工  伦敦三千建筑工人抗议拒绝增资

1947-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　反对拉马第政府限制工资
    法铁路工人总罢工
    伦敦三千建筑工人抗议拒绝增资
    【新华社陕北十一日电】巴黎讯：为要求改善生活待遇，反对拉马第政府之限制工资政策，法国全国铁路工人已于本月七日开始实行总罢工，全法铁路交通已告停顿。据合众社电称：目前拉马第政府运输部以征用之军车维持各大城市间的交通，并谓军方拨用飞机增强各港埠与内地间之运输。
    【新华社陕北十二日电】据塔斯社伦敦讯：伦敦三千建筑工人大会，严重抗议地主及仲裁法庭拒绝他们的增资要求。发言人抨击政府对工人要求缺乏同情，并抨击执行委员会拖延集合工人作积极的行动。大会并对引入波兰人及流离失所者至工业界，提出严重抗议。
