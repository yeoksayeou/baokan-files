Title: Cadres Sent by Xiuwu County Organize Wheat Harvest in Border Areas
Author: Ziqin, Xianmou
Date: 1947-06-07
Page: 2

　　Xiuwu Sends Cadres to
    Organize Wheat Harvest in Border Areas
    [Report from Xiuwu] In order to vigorously protect the wheat harvest and thwart the enemy's conspiracy to seize grain, the Xiuwu County government specially selected eight cadres, formed into two groups, and sent them to the border areas of the third and fourth districts to organize the masses to harvest wheat. Before departing, each cadre formulated a plan to perform meritorious service, determined to complete the task of protecting the harvest.
    (Ziqin, Xianmou)

### 修武派出干部  组织边地麦收
子琴　宪谋
1947-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　修武派出干部
    组织边地麦收
    【本报修武讯】为了大力保卫麦收，打破敌人抢粮阴谋，修武县府特抽干部八人，编成二个小组，分赴三、四区边地，组织群众收麦，在未行前，每个干部均订出立功计划，决心完成保麦任务。    （子琴、宪谋）
