Title: Demanding Wage Increases, Shanghai West Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1947-06-10
Page: 1

　　Demanding Wage Increases
    Shanghai West Workers Strike
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th] According to Shanghai's Continental Daily, workers at two machinery factories in Shanghai West have gone on strike due to the factory owners' refusal to increase wages.

### 要求增加工资  沪西工人罢工

1947-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　要求增加工资
    沪西工人罢工
    【新华社陕北八日电】据沪大陆报载，沪西两家机械工厂工人已因厂方拒绝增加工资而举行罢工。
