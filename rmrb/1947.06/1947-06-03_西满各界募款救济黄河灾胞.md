Title: All Circles in Western Manchuria Donate to Relieve Yellow River Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1947-06-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Western Manchuria, 29th: Since the Western Manchuria Daily initiated a donation campaign to aid the compatriots in the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu and Shandong liberated areas who were affected by the Yellow River disaster, all circles in the region have enthusiastically contributed. In just one month, 14,832,578 Northeast currency yuan has been raised. The Financial and Economic Office of the Northeast Government Committee is now remitting the funds to the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region Government to be distributed to the disaster-stricken compatriots along the river.

### 西满各界募款救济黄河灾胞

1947-06-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　西满各界募款救济黄河灾胞
    【新华社西满二十九日】此间西满日报发起援救晋冀鲁豫、山东两解放区沿河蒋灾被难同胞募捐运动后，全区各界，无不踊跃输将，甫经一月，已募集东北币一千四百八十三万二千五百七十八元，现正由东北政委会财经办事处汇寄晋冀鲁豫边区政府，请为转发沿河被灾同胞。
