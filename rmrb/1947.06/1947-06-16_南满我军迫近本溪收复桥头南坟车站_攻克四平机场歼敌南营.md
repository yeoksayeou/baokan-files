Title: Our Southern Manchurian Forces Approach Benxi, Recover Qiaotou and Nanfen Stations, Capture Siping Airport, and Annihilate Enemy Southern Camp
Author:
Date: 1947-06-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Liaodong, 14th. The Democratic Allied Forces, having captured the Tongshanguan Station on the central section of the An(dong)-Shen(yang) line, continued to expand their battle northward, recovering the Qiaotou and Nanfen Stations south of Benxihu on the afternoon of the 11th, annihilating a portion of the enemy and capturing one locomotive and five vehicles. Note: Qiaotou is only ten kilometers away from Benxi, with only Gongyuan station in between.
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 14th. A unit of the Northeast Democratic Allied Forces captured the Siping City West Airport on the morning of the 11th. The battle began at 11:30 PM on the 10th and ended at 8:00 AM the next morning, completely annihilating the defending enemy 71st Army Transportation Team and one battalion of the Security Regiment. A total of 122 enemy soldiers were killed, 27 wounded, and 262 officers and soldiers of the Security Regiment and below were captured. Three 60mm mortars, 28 light machine guns, 21 submachine guns, 258 rifles, and 42,000 rounds of ammunition were seized.
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 14th. During the pursuit of the enemy along the Gongzhuling road, the Third Battalion of a certain unit of the Democratic Allied Forces created a new method of political work. At that time, the situation was urgent, and they departed immediately after receiving orders. Before the battle, they adopted a "flying object" method three times. Political workers were dispersed in all parts of the troops, studying and explaining the task, and taking measures while marching. Nine times during the march, they said to everyone: "The enemy is about to collapse! We must pursue the enemy, mainly the ××× enemy, but how to do it!" Then he held eight meetings of deputy platoon leaders and above while marching, deploying how to ensure the safety of the task and organizing dialogue groups. As soon as they came into contact with the enemy, the Third Battalion immediately and highly pointed out among the comrades: "Comrades! Attack fiercely! Work harder, victory is ours."

### 南满我军迫近本溪收复桥头南坟车站  攻克四平机场歼敌南营

1947-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　南满我军迫近本溪收复桥头南坟车站
    攻克四平机场歼敌南营
    【新华社辽东十四日电】攻克安（东）沈（阳）线中段同山关车站之民主联军，继续向北扩张战斗，于十一日午后收复本溪湖以南之桥头及南坟车站，歼敌一部，火车头一个，汽车五辆。按：桥头距本溪仅十公里，中间只隔宫原一站。
    【新华社东北前线十四日电】东北民主联军一部，于十一日晨攻占四平城西飞机场，战斗于十日晚十一时三十分开始，至翌晨八时结束，全部歼灭守敌七十一军运输队及保安团一个营。计毙其一百二十二名，伤二十七名，俘保安团正以下官兵二百六十二名。缴六○炮三门，轻机枪二十八挺，冲锋式二十一支，步枪二百五十八支，子弹四十二千发。
    【新华社东北前线十四日电】公（主岭）路追歼之中，民主联军某队三营创政治工作的方式。当时情况紧迫，接到命令马上出发，战前，三次采取“飞行异物”，员工干部分散在部队各部分，边走边研究，说明任务，采取措施。九次行进中对大家说：“要在敌人溃败啦！我们要追击敌人，主要是×××一个敌人，可不怎么做！”接着他又向行进中的八次召开副排长以上干部会，布置如何保证安全任务，和组织对话组。一与敌接触，三营并指即在同志中间，往返高度：“同志们！猛攻啊！加劲努力，就是胜利。”
