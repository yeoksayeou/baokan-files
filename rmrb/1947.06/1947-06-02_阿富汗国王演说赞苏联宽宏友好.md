Title: Afghan King's Speech Praises Soviet Generosity and Friendship
Author:
Date: 1947-06-02
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: TASS, Kabul: The King of Afghanistan delivered a speech at the opening ceremony of the National Assembly, emphasizing the achievements of the new government. Regarding foreign policy, the King pointed out that the measures taken by the government have greatly strengthened Afghanistan's position. The King cited the border issue that has been resolved between Afghanistan and the Soviet Union as an example to illustrate the Soviet Union's generous and friendly attitude.

### 阿富汗国王演说赞苏联宽宏友好

1947-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿富汗国王演说赞苏联宽宏友好
    【新华社陕北三十日电】塔斯社喀布尔讯：阿富汗国王在国民会议开幕典礼中发表演说，着重说明新政府的成就。关于外交政策，阿王指出：政府所着手的措施曾大大的加强的阿富汗的地位。阿王援引在阿国与苏联间业经解决的边疆问题为例，说明苏联的宽宏友好态度。
