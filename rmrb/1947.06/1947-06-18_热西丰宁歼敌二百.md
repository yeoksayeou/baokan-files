Title: Annihilating Two Hundred Enemies in Fengning, Western Rehe
Author:
Date: 1947-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe, 16th. The Rehe People's Liberation Army annihilated most of the more than 400 troops of Fu Zuoyi's 14th Column (now reorganized into the 2nd Regiment of the 4th Division) in the area of Guojiatun, northwest of Fengning, western Rehe, on the 2nd. One hundred and forty men, including Regiment Commander Wang Wanzhong, were killed, and fifty-six men, including the Major Quartermaster Director, were captured. Five machine guns and sixty-two rifles and carbines were seized. We suffered only six casualties.

### 热西丰宁歼敌二百

1947-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　热西丰宁歼敌二百
    【新华社冀察热辽十六日电】热河人民解放军二日在热西丰宁西北郭家屯地方，将傅作义部十四纵队（现改编为四师第二团）四百余名大部歼灭。击毙团长王万仲以下一百四十名，俘少校军需主任以下五十六名。缴获机枪五挺，长短枪六十二支，我仅伤亡六人。
