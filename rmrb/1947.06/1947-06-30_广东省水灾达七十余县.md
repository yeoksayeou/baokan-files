Title: Guangdong Province Flooding Affects More Than Seventy Counties
Author:
Date: 1947-06-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th: According to a Central News Agency report on the 26th, more than seventy counties in Guangdong Province have reported flooding, accounting for about seven-tenths of the province's 103 counties and cities. The number of disaster victims and the severity of the losses are unprecedented in a century.

### 广东省水灾达七十余县

1947-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　广东省水灾达七十余县
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】据中央社二十六日讯：广东省报水灾县份已逾七十，约占全省一○三县市十分之七。灾民之多，损失之重，百年仅见。
