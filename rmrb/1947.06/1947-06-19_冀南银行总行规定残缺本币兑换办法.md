Title: Regulations of the General Office of Jinnan Bank on the Exchange of Damaged Currency

Author:

Date: 1947-06-19
Page: 1

　　The regulations of our bank on the exchange of damaged currency have been revised and are hereby announced as follows:
　　I. Any banknote with three-quarters of its surface intact on any side, which is confirmed to be genuine, will be exchanged at its original value (vertical banknotes are treated the same).
　　II. Any banknote with one-half of its surface intact will be exchanged at 80% of half its original value.
　　III. Any banknote with different serial numbers on the left and right halves will also be exchanged at its original value.
　　IV. Any banknote that is wet or oil-soaked, but with its surface intact and confirmed to be genuine, will be exchanged at its original value.
　　V. Any banknote with less than one-half of its surface intact will not be exchanged.
　　VI. Any banknote pieced together from multiple fragments, regardless of whether it forms a whole or half note, will not be exchanged.
　　VII. The commission fee for exchange offices handling broken banknotes on behalf of the bank is set at 10 to 15 yuan per hundred banknotes.

### 冀南银行总行规定残缺本币兑换办法

1947-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南银行总行规定残缺本币兑换办法
    本行残缺本币兑换办法，兹经重新修正，公布如下：一、票面靠任何一边存在四分之三确系真票者按原值兑换（立式票同）。二、票面存在二分之一者，以半数计八折兑换。三、票面左右两半截字头号码不相同者，亦按原值兑换。四、水湿油浸票面完整确系真票者，原值兑换。五、各种票面不足二分之一者不予兑换。六、票面多块凑成者，不论整张半张一律不予兑换。七、委托兑换所代兑破票其手续费规定每百张十元至十五元计。
