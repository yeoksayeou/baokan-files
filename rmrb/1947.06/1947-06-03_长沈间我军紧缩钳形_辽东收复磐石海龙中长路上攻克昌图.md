Title: Our Army Tightens the Pincers Between Changchun and Shenyang; Liaodong Recovers Panshi and Hailong; On the Changchun-Shenyang Railway, Changtu is Captured

Author:
Date: 1947-06-03
Page: 1

　　Our Army Tightens the Pincers Between Changchun and Shenyang
    Liaodong Recovers Panshi and Hailong; On the Changchun-Shenyang Railway, Changtu is Captured
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, Urgent Telegram, 31st] A unit of the Northeast Democratic Allied Army recovered Panshi County, a town on the Jilin-Meihekou line southeast of Changchun, this morning (31st). The defending Chiang Kai-shek troops abandoned the city last night (30th) and fled. The Democratic Allied Army is in pursuit. Note: Panshi County was occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's New Sixth Army on May 26th last year.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, Urgent Telegram, 31st] Following the recovery of Panshi County this morning (31st), a unit of the Northeast Democratic Allied Army recovered Hailong County and Chaoyang Town to its northeast on the Jilin-Shenyang line. The Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched in the town fled in disarray. Note: Hailong was occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's New Sixth Army and Fifty-Second Army on May 23rd last year.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 1st] On the Eastern Manchuria front, the Northeast Democratic Allied Army shot down a U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek aircraft during combat on the 30th. After being hit, the plane burned and crashed in Huadian City. This is the third Chiang Kai-shek aircraft shot down during the Northeast Summer Offensive.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 1st] In the battle in which the Northeast Democratic Allied Army recovered Dongfeng and annihilated the Sixth Regiment of the Second Division of Chiang Kai-shek's Youth Army, it has now been verified that Chen Chengxiang, the deputy division commander and concurrently commander of the Sixth Regiment, was killed, and Chen Quan, the deputy commander of the Sixth Regiment (erroneously reported as the commander in the previous telegram), was captured alive.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 31st] The ammunition abandoned by the defending Chiang Kai-shek troops when the Northeast Democratic Allied Army recovered Tonghua has now been verified to be over 350,000 rounds.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Western Manchuria, Urgent Telegram, 31st] On the 29th, the Northeast Democratic Allied Army cut off the Changchun-Shenyang Railway between Shenyang and Siping and recovered Changtu County. Note: Changtu County was occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's troops on April 4th last year.

### 长沈间我军紧缩钳形  辽东收复磐石海龙中长路上攻克昌图

1947-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　长沈间我军紧缩钳形
    辽东收复磐石海龙中长路上攻克昌图
    【新华社东北前线三十一日急电】东北民主联军一部，今（三十一日）晨收复长春东南吉（林）梅（河口）线上之磐石县城，守城蒋军昨（三十日）晚弃城逃窜，民主联军正跟踪追击中。按：磐石县城系于去年五月二十六日为蒋军新六军侵占。
    【新华社东北前线三十一日急电】继今（三十一）晨收复磐石县城后，东北民主联军一部收复吉（林）沈（阳）线上之海龙县城及其东北之朝阳镇。盘踞该城镇之蒋军，抱头鼠窜。按：海龙系于去年五月二十三日为蒋军新六军及五十二军所侵占。
    【新华社东北前线一日电】东满前线东北民主联军，三十日于作战中，击毁美制蒋机一架。该机被击中后，即燃烧堕毁于桦甸城。此为东北夏季攻势中击落之第三架蒋机。
    【新华社东北前线一日电】东北民主联军收复东丰歼灭蒋杜青年军第二师第六团之役，顷已查明该师副师长兼六团团长陈呈祥被击毙，六团副团长（前电误为团长）陈铨被生俘。
    【新华社东北前线三十一日电】东北民主联军收复通化时，守城蒋军遗弃之弹药，现已查清者达三十五万余发。
    【新华社西满三十一日急电】二十九日，东北民主联军斩断沈阳至四平之中长铁路，收复昌图县城。按：昌图县城系去年四月四日被蒋军所侵占。
