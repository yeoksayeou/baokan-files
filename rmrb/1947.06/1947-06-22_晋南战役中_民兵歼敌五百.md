Title: People's Militia Annihilates 500 Enemies in the Jinan Campaign
Author:
Date: 1947-06-22
Page: 1

　　During the Jinan Campaign
    People's Militia Annihilates 500 Enemies
    [This newspaper, Taiyue, 19th dispatch] The Taiyue Military Commission General Association announced the glorious achievements of more than 10,000 militiamen from 20 counties in the region who participated in the Jinan Campaign in April and May. A total of 56 enemies were killed or wounded, and 451 enemies were captured; one mortar, three small cannons, eleven grenade launchers, twenty light machine guns, four hundred and eighty-three rifles, ten submachine guns, sixty various short guns, and more than one hundred thousand rounds of various bullets were seized.

### 晋南战役中  民兵歼敌五百

1947-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋南战役中
    民兵歼敌五百
    【本报太岳十九日电】太岳武委总会公布该区二十个县万余民兵参加四、五两月晋南战役的光辉战绩。计毙伤敌五十六名，俘敌四百五十一名；缴获迫击炮一门，小炮三门，掷弹筒十一个，轻机枪二十挺，步枪四百八十三支，冲锋枪十支，各种短枪六十支，各种子弹十余万发。
