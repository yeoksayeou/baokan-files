Title: Bohai Local Armed Forces Capture Sangzidian, Active on Both Banks of the Yellow River Near Jinan
Author:
Date: 1947-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 25th—A unit of the Bohai Second Military Sub-district's local armed forces, after four hours of fierce fighting on the night of the 15th, recovered the Sangzidian railway station north of the Yellow River in Jinan, annihilating more than 40 enemies and destroying one enemy armored locomotive. The remaining enemies fled to Qihe city. A unit of the Jiyuan local armed forces, in coordination with the action, entered Xingjiadu, more than 20 li northeast of Luokou on the north bank of the Yellow River on the 16th, annihilating more than 20 enemies, seizing more than ten long and short guns, two radio stations, and other grain, food, and materials. Now, the enemy strongholds around Qihe, north of the Yellow River, have all become isolated islands.

### 渤海地方武装克桑梓店  活跃济南附近黄河南北两岸

1947-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　渤海地方武装克桑梓店
    活跃济南附近黄河南北两岸
    【新华社山东二十五日电】渤海二分区地方武装一部，于十五日夜经四小时激战收复济南黄河以北桑梓店车站，歼敌四十余，击毁敌铁甲车头一个，残敌窜逃齐河城内。济源地方武装一部配合行动，于十六日打入黄河南洛口东北二十余里之邢家渡，歼敌二十余，缴长短枪十余支，电台两部，及其他粮食物资等。现黄河以北齐河外围敌据点已全成孤岛。
