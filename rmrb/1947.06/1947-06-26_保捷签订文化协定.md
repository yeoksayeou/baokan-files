Title: Czechoslovakia and Bulgaria Sign Cultural Agreement
Author:
Date: 1947-06-26
Page: 3
　
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 24th: Sofia dispatch: Following the signing of a trade agreement, Bulgaria and Czechoslovakia signed a cultural cooperation agreement here on the 20th. Participants in the signing included Bulgarian Minister of Propaganda and Art, Kasasov, and Czechoslovak Foreign Minister, Clementis, among others. In his speech, Clementis emphasized the importance of this agreement and expressed his firm belief that the signing of a political cooperation agreement between the two countries would follow.

### 保捷签订文化协定

1947-06-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　保捷签订文化协定
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】索非亚讯：保、捷两国继签定贸易协定之后，又于二十日在此间签订文化合作协定。参加签字者有保国宣传与艺术部长卡萨索夫、捷外长克里孟蒂斯等。克里孟蒂斯在演说时强调指出这一协定的重要性，并表示坚信：接踵而来的将是两国政治合作协定的签订。
