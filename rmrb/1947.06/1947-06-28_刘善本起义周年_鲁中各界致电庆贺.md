Title: Anniversary of Liu Shanben's Uprising: People from All Walks of Life in Central Shandong Send Telegrams of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1947-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 26th. On the anniversary of the uprising of Air Force Captain Liu Shanben (June 22nd), the Central Shandong People's Congress and the Administrative Office sent congratulatory telegrams. Liu's elder brother, Shanxin, also sent a letter from his hometown of Anqiu, stating: "My brother generously flew his plane to Yan'an, pioneering the anti-civil war uprising of the Chiang family's air force. All the fathers and brothers in my hometown are proud of him." Yu Zhensheng, a second lieutenant from the former Air Force 28th Squadron of the Shandong People's Liberation Army Officer Training Corps, also sent a congratulatory telegram, saying: "Fortunately, I have been liberated and can follow in my brother's footsteps, and I am willing to work together for independence, peace, and democracy."

### 刘善本起义周年  鲁中各界致电庆贺

1947-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘善本起义周年
    鲁中各界致电庆贺
    【新华社山东二十六日电】刘善本空军上尉起义周年纪念日（二十二日），鲁中参议会及行政专署特致贺电。刘之胞兄善新并从安邱家乡致函称：弟慷慨驾机飞延，首创蒋家空军反内战起义，凡我家乡父兄，均引以为荣。山东解放军官教导纵队前空军二八中队少尉于振声也贺电称：“幸获解放，得随兄后，愿为独立和平民主共同奋斗。”
