Title: Hebei-Shanxi Local Corps Annihilates 500 Yan's Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-06-22
Page: 1

　　Hebei-Shanxi Local Corps
    Annihilates 500 Yan's Troops
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Chahar-Hebei, 20th] (Delayed dispatch) A unit of the Hebei-Shanxi Military Region's local corps, on the 26th, 27th, and 28th of last month, continuously repelled the harassment by the 49th Division, 1st Regiment, and the puppet Shanxi Security 8th Column, 2nd Regiment, of Yan's troops from Yuci in areas about 80 li northeast of Yuci, such as Tai'an. In the three days of combat, more than 480 Yan's troops were killed, wounded, or captured, and one mortar, nine light and heavy machine guns, eleven small cannons, and more than one hundred rifles and carbines were seized.

### 冀晋地方兵团  歼灭阎军五百

1947-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀晋地方兵团
    歼灭阎军五百
    【新华社晋察冀二十日电】（迟到）冀晋军区地方兵团一部，于上月二十六、二十七、二十八三日中，连续于榆次东北约八十里之太安等地，击退由榆次出扰之阎军四十九师第一团及伪山西保安八纵队二团等部。三日作战中，计毙伤俘阎军四百八十余名，缴臼炮一门，轻重机枪九挺，小炮十一门，步马枪百余支。
