Title: East China Rapidly Launches Armed Wheat Protection Struggle
Author:
Date: 1947-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 9th: The wheat protection war is rapidly unfolding in Haimen, Qidong, Zishi and other places in central Jiangsu. From May 19th to June 1st, 269 KMT soldiers were killed, wounded, or captured, 6 machine guns and 62 rifles were seized, along with over 2,000 rounds of ammunition, and 3 trucks and 1 motor/wooden boat used for looting wheat were destroyed. In addition, farmers in Nanpi County in the Bohai area are carrying out a bombing campaign to defend the wheat harvest. Two-thirds of the villages in the Dianzi district have started female militia bombing training classes. In one village, out of 67 households, more than 50 have learned landmine and mine-laying techniques. The bombing squad laid mines around Fenghua Store, a KMT stronghold on the periphery of Cang County, on three occasions, killing or wounding nearly 20 KMT and puppet soldiers who came to the countryside to loot wheat.

### 华东急剧开展武装保麦斗争

1947-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东急剧开展武装保麦斗争
    【新华社华东九日电】苏中海门、启东、紫石等地保麦战急剧开展。自五月十九日至六月一日，毙伤俘蒋军二百六十九名，缴机枪六挺，步枪六十二支，子弹二千余发，击毁抢麦卡车三辆，汽船木船各一艘。又渤海区南皮县农民开展爆炸运动，保卫麦收。店子区三分之二村庄开办了女民兵爆炸训练班。某庄六十七户，有五十余户学会了地雷和布雷技术。爆炸队于沧县外围据点风化店布雷三次，毙伤下乡抢麦蒋伪近二十名。
