Title: Xingtai County District Government Agencies Save Two Liang of Millet Per Person

Author:

Date: 1947-06-09
Page: 2

　　Xingtai County District Government Agencies Save Two Liang of Millet Per Person
    [This Newspaper's News] From June 1st, the Xingtai County district government agencies are saving two liang of millet per person per day. This has already begun. County leaders are also calling on the masses to practice diligence and thrift in this spirit, save bit by bit, and get through the famine year. (Xingtai Small Newspaper)

### 邢台县区机关每人节米二两

1947-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　邢台县区机关每人节米二两
    【本报讯】邢台县区机关从六月一日起每人每日节约小米二两、现已开始实行，县领导上并号召群众本此精神厉行节约，细水长流，渡过荒年。（邢台小报）
