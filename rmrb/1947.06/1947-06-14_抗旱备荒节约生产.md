Title: Combat Drought, Prepare for Famine, Practice Economy and Production
Author: 
Date: 1947-06-14
Page: 4

　　Combat Drought, Prepare for Famine, Practice Economy and Production
　　Folks, listen to your hearts, let's talk about preventing drought and preparing for famine.
　　Plant the land, don't wait for rain, want to eat, don't rely on heaven.
　　Want to eat common meals, want to wear cloth clothes.
　　Live frugally over time, mix cornmeal with bran.
　　Dig more vegetables for extra grain, eat less white flour dumplings.
　　Don't buy fried dough twists and sesame candy, don't wear foreign cloth new clothes.
　　Oppose the lazy good-for-nothings and lazy women. (Double sentences)
　
　　Folks, listen to my words, let's all recall.
　　The two famine years of '42 and '43, eating bran and swallowing vegetables was hard.
　　Bellies rumbled with hunger, faces were thin and yellow-blue.
　　If it weren't for the government's relief, our lives would be hard to save.
　　Everyone should prevent disasters and droughts,
　　Oppose those who forget the past pain. (Double sentences)
　
　　Folks, listen to your hearts, implement production and economy.
　　Production is like a money tree, economy is like a treasure bowl.
　　Saving rice at the top of the jar is still possible, saving at the bottom of the jar results in nothing.
　　Mix bran and eat vegetables to be thrifty, to fight against drought.
　　Men, women, old and young, gather wild vegetables, every household stores grain,
　　Saving an ounce of rice per meal (per person) adds up to a lot.
　　Oppose the big eaters and drinkers, the wasteful insects. (Double sentences)
　
　　Folks, listen to your hearts, be vigilant and make decisions immediately.
　　Each village should accumulate public granary grain, produce, economize, and prepare for famine.
　　Everyone works together, labor can create a bright future.
　　Support the front lines to win victories, eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek troops with joy. (Double sentences)

### 抗旱备荒节约生产

1947-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　抗旱备荒节约生产
    老乡们听心间，防旱备荒谈一谈。
    该种地，不等雨，要吃饭，不靠天。
    要吃还是家常饭，要穿还是布衣衫。
    细水长流过时光，玉茭面里掺上糠。
    多挖菜多余粮，少吃白面圪瘩汤。
    不买油角和麻糖，不穿洋布新衣裳、
    反对好吃懒做懒汉懒婆娘。（双句）
　
    老乡们听我言，大家回忆想一番。
    四二四三两荒年，吃糠咽菜受艰难。
    饿的肚子＠噜响，瘦的脸皮黄蓝蓝。
    要不是政府救济咱，咱们的性命难保全。
    大家应该防灾旱，
    反对那过去痛苦忘了干。（双句）
　
    老乡们听心中，生产节约要实行。
    生产好比摇钱树，节约好比聚宝盆。
    缸口省米还可以，缸底节省落个空。
    掺糠吃菜要俭省，要跟旱灾作斗争。
    男女老少采野菜，家家户户把粮存，
    每顿节约一两米（每人）计算起来了不成。
    反对那大吃二喝浪费虫。（双句。）
　
    老乡们听心肠，马上警惕定主张。
    各村要积义仓粮，生产节备灾荒。
    大家动手一齐忙，劳动就能发时光。
    支援前线打胜仗，消灭蒋军喜洋洋。（双句）
          武安八高通讯小组编
