Title: Soviet Ambassador Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1947-06-27
Page: 1

　　Soviet Ambassador Returns Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th] Central News Agency reports: Soviet Ambassador Petrov and his wife, along with their two daughters and Secretary Karabukha, left Beijing on a special plane on the 19th to return to their country. Before returning, the affairs of the embassy will be managed by Counselor Fedlin. Before his departure, the Soviet Ambassador held a cocktail party to bid farewell to all parties.

### 苏大使返国

1947-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏大使返国
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】中央社讯：苏大使彼得罗夫夫妇偕两女公子及秘书喀拉布哈夫一行，十九日乘专机离京返国。返任前馆主由参事费德林代理。苏大使行前并曾举行鸡尾酒会，与各方话别。
