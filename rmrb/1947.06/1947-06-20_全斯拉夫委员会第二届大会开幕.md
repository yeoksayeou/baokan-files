Title: Second Congress of the All-Slavic Committee Opens
Author:
Date: 1947-06-20
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th. Comprehensive report: The Second Plenary Congress of the All-Slavic Committee opened in Warsaw on the 16th. Delegations from the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and other countries participated. After adopting the agenda and electing the drafting committee, the Chairman of the All-Slavic Committee, Masaryk, delivered a work report on the committee's activities. The congress will discuss the committee's work and its tasks for 1947. One of the most important tasks of the committee this year is to hold a conference of Slavic scientists and to commemorate the 800th anniversary of Moscow, the 6th anniversary of Prague University, and the 1000th anniversary of Slavic alphabet, among other anniversaries.
　　[Another report] The First Slavic Congress was held in Belgrade at the end of last year, and representatives of American Slavic descendants participated. They stayed in Yugoslavia for half a year, traveled throughout the country, and recently returned to the United States. Before leaving, Martin Zoric, representing the two thousand Yugoslavs in California, told reporters: "American reactionaries are directing a fierce anti-Yugoslav movement, spreading lies that the South has no democracy or freedom. But our travels throughout the South have convinced us that there is no country among those we have visited that has more freedom than the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia."

### 全斯拉夫委员会第二届大会开幕

1947-06-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　全斯拉夫委员会第二届大会开幕
    【新华社陕北十八日电】综合报导：全斯拉夫委员会第二届全体大会于十六日在华沙开幕，参加者有苏、南、保、捷、波等国代表团，大会在通过议事日程并选举起草委员会后，即有全斯拉夫委员会主席玛斯拉瑞克作委员会的工作报告。大会将讨论委员会的工作及其一九四七年的任务等问题。委员会今年最重要的工作之一为举行斯拉夫科学家会议，并将纪念莫斯科八百周年、布拉格大学六周年、斯拉夫字母一千周年等纪念日。
    【又讯】第一届斯拉夫大会于去年年底在贝尔格莱德举行，曾有美国斯拉夫族后裔的代表参加，彼等在南斯拉夫留居半年，曾游历全国，最近始行返美。临行时，马丁佐瑞克代表美国加里福尼亚洲的两千南斯拉夫人对记者宣称：“美国反动分子正导演猛烈的反南斯拉夫运动，散布南国毫无民主自由的谎言，但我们遍游全南国，使我们相信，在我们所访问过的国家中，没有任何一国比南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国有更多的自由。”
