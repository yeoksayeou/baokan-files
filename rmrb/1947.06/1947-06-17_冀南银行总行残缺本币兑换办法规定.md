Title: Regulations of the Head Office of Jinnan Bank on the Exchange of Damaged Local Currency

Author:

Date: 1947-06-17
Page: 1

The regulations of this bank on the exchange of damaged local currency have been revised and are now announced as follows:
　　I. Any bill with three-quarters of its surface remaining on any side and confirmed as genuine will be exchanged at its original value (including vertical bills).
　　II. Any bill with one-half of its surface remaining will be exchanged at 80% of half its value.
　　III. If the serial numbers on the left and right halves of a bill are different, it will still be exchanged at its original value.
　　IV. Any bill that is wet or oil-soaked but complete and confirmed as genuine will be exchanged at its original value.
　　V. Bills with less than one-half of their surface remaining will not be exchanged.
　　VI. Bills pieced together from multiple fragments, whether whole or half, will not be exchanged under any circumstances.
　　VII. The handling fee for entrusted exchange offices to exchange damaged bills is set at 10 to 15 yuan per hundred bills.

### 冀南银行总行残缺本币兑换办法规定

1947-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南银行总行残缺本币兑换办法规定
    本行残缺本币兑换办法，兹经重新修正，公布如下：一、票面靠任何一边存在四分之三确系真票者按原值兑换（立式票同）。二、票面存在二分之一者，以半数计八折兑换。三、票面左右两半截字头号码不相同者，亦按原值兑换。四、水湿油浸票面完整确系真票者，原值兑换。五、各种票面不足二分之一者不予兑换。六、票面多块凑成者，不论整张半张一律不予兑换。七、委托兑换所代兑破票其手续费规定每百张十元至十五元计。
