Title: A Section of the Suizhong Chiang Kai-shek Troops' Traffic Police Surrender with Weapons
Author:
Date: 1947-06-15
Page: 1

　　Suizhong Chiang Kai-shek Troops' Traffic Police Surrender with Weapons

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 13th—According to reports, a section of the Eighth Regiment of the Chiang Kai-shek troops' traffic police in Suizhong on the Beining Railway, totaling twenty-five men, unwilling to be tools of Chiang Kai-shek's civil war, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army with all their weapons a few days ago.

### 绥中蒋军交警一部  携械向我投诚

1947-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　绥中蒋军交警一部
    携械向我投诚
    【新华社东北前线十三日电】据北宁路绥中之蒋军交通警察第八总队一部二十五人，因不甘作蒋介石内战工具，于日前携带全部武器向人民解放军投诚。
