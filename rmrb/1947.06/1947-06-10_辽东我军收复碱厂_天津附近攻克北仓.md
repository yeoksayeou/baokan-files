Title: Liaodong Our Army Recovers Jianchang, Tianjin Vicinity Captures Beicang
Author:
Date: 1947-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Liaodong, 8th. Local armed forces of the Andong Military Region continue to report good news, capturing the Jiang-puppet stronghold of Jianchang, east of Benxi, at 5:00 AM on the 4th, capturing a part of the puppet Security Fourth Regiment, 25 men. The rest fled to Tianshifu. Note: Jianchang was once liberated in mid-March this year after the Jiang-Du army's second failed invasion of Linjiang.

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 8th. (Delayed) A part of the Jidong Local Corps, on the midnight of the 18th of last month, stormed Beicang Station on the Beining Railway, 15 miles north of Tianjin. More than ten members of Jiang's Tianjin Security Team and the Tianjin urban sweeping team were annihilated. Jiang's puppet Tianjin was shocked by this.

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 8th. A guerrilla team in southern Rehe on the 2nd attacked the Jiguanshan (10 kilometers south of Chengde City) stronghold, wiping out 20 enemies and seizing 7 rifles, 1 submachine gun, and 1,200 rounds of ammunition.

Xinhua News Agency, West Manchuria, 8th. The warriors of the West Manchuria Liberation Regiment (composed of captured Jiang's military officers and soldiers) of Qiqihar City, encouraged by the victories, have been requesting to go to the front line to kill enemies and make meritorious contributions. On the 31st of last month, the first batch of more than 300 people has left for the front line and joined the ranks of the People's Liberation Army.

### 辽东我军收复碱厂  天津附近攻克北仓

1947-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　辽东我军收复碱厂
    天津附近攻克北仓
    【新华社辽东八日电】安东军区地方武装续报捷音，于四日晨五时攻克本溪以东之碱厂蒋伪据点，俘伪保安四团一部二十五名，余众向田什夫逃窜。按：碱厂曾于今年三月中旬蒋杜军第二次进犯临江失败后，一度获得解放。
    【新华社晋察冀八日电】（迟到）冀东地方兵团一部，于上月十八日午夜，一举袭入天津北十五里北宁路上之北仓车站。蒋记天津保安队及天津市区扫荡队被歼十余名。津市蒋伪为之震惊。
    【新华社晋察冀八日电】热南某游击队于二日奔龚鸡冠山（承德市南十公里）据点，歼敌二十名，缴步枪七支，冲锋机枪一挺，子弹一千二百发。
    【新华社西满八日电】齐齐哈尔市西满解放团（系历次被俘蒋军官兵所组成）战士，闻捷鼓舞，纷纷请求上前线杀敌立功。上月三十一日，第一批三百余人已开赴前线，投入人民解放军之行列。
