Title: Our Army Counterattacks in the Northern Section of the Tianjin-Pukou Railway; Qing County Liberated, Cangzhou Becomes an Isolated Island; More Than 1,000 Enemies Annihilated in Yongqing, Between Tianjin and Beiping

Author: 
Date: 1947-06-17
Page: 1

　　Our Army Counterattacks in the Northern Section of the Tianjin-Pukou Railway
　　Liberation of Qing County, Cangzhou Becomes an Isolated Island
　　More Than 1,000 Enemies Annihilated in Yongqing, Between Tianjin and Beiping

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin-Pukou Front, urgent telegram, 20:00 on the 14th] A unit of the Jin-Cha-Ji People's Liberation Army launched a powerful offensive on the northern section of the Tianjin-Pukou Railway, from the suburbs of Jinghai in the north to south of Cang County, starting on the 12th. After two days and nights of fierce fighting, it has liberated Qing County, 160 li south of Tianjin, as well as Chenguantun, Tangguantun, Machang, Qing County, Xingji, Yaoguantun, and Jiedi stations. Cang County station has also been captured. Approximately 200 li of the railway is now under our control. Important Chiang Kai-shek and puppet strongholds on both sides of the railway, such as Xuguantun, Gaoguantun, Caishikou, as well as Dawatou, more than 20 li west of Jinghai city, and Dalibakou, 20 li south of Tianjin, have also been liberated. A total of more than 20 towns and villages have been captured, and all the defending enemies have been annihilated. The results of the battle are being tallied.

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beining Front, urgent telegram, 20:00 on the 14th] The Jin-Cha-Ji People's Liberation Army, advancing on the Beining Railway, liberated Yongqing County, south of the Beining Railway, at 20:00 on the 13th. More than 1,500 men of the puppet Security Third Regiment stationed in the city were completely annihilated. The city was occupied by Chiang's army on April 4th of this year, and returned to the hands of the people after only 73 days.

　　[Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, dispatch of the 15th] Another group of more than 2,000 Chiang Kai-shek army officers from the 74th Division who laid down their weapons in the Battle of Menglianggu have safely arrived at the East China Liberation Army Officer Training Center. There are now more than 4,000 Liberation Army officers from Chiang Kai-shek's 74th Division at the center, and the opening ceremony has been officially held. At the dinner party, Xin Ming, deputy regimental commander of the 172nd Regiment of the 58th Brigade, took the lead, and more than a dozen officers raised their glasses and shouted: "Wishing Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Commander Chen good health!"

### 我军反攻津浦北段  解放青县沧州已成孤岛  平津间克永清歼敌千余

1947-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军反攻津浦北段
    解放青县沧州已成孤岛
    平津间克永清歼敌千余
    【新华社津浦前线十四日夜二十时急电】晋察冀人民解放军一部，于十二日起，向津浦北段，北起静海城郊，南至沧县以南，展开强大攻势。经两昼夜激战，已解放天津以南一百六十华里之青县县城，及陈官屯、唐官屯、马厂、青县、兴济、姚官屯、捷地等车站，沧县车站亦已攻克。控制铁路约二百华里。铁路两侧之蒋伪重要据点如徐官屯、高官屯、菜市口及静海城西二十余里之大瓦子头，天津市南二十里之大里八口等，亦均解放。计前后攻克大小城镇廿余处、守敌悉数被歼。战果正清查中。
    【新华社北宁前线十四日夜廿时急电】向北宁线进击之晋察冀人民解放军，于十三日夜二十时解放北宁路南侧之永清县城。全歼驻守该城之伪保安第三总队一千五百余名。按该城系于今年四月四日为蒋军侵占，为时仅七十三天，即重归人民手中。
    【新华社华东前线十五日电】孟良崮之战放下武器的蒋军七十四师军官，又一批二千余名安抵华东解放军官训练处。现该处连前已有蒋军七十四师解放军官四千余名，已举行入校典礼正式开学。聚餐席上，五十八旅一七二团中校副团长辛明自动带头，十余校官举杯欢呼：“敬祝毛主席、朱总司令、陈军长健康！”
