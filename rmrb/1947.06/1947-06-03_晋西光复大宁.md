Title: Jinxi Liberates Daning
Author:
Date: 1947-06-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 1st.—Hu’s army's 67th Brigade, 199th Regiment of the 30th Division, entrenched in Daning (this brigade was reorganized after being wiped out in Puxian last winter) feared being annihilated by the People's Liberation Army and was forced to abandon the city on the 27th and flee to Jixian, and Daning City was liberated.

### 晋西光复大宁

1947-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋西光复大宁
    【新华社晋绥一日电】盘踞大宁之胡军三十师六十七旅一九九团（该旅系于去冬在蒲县被歼后又重新拚凑编成者），惧遭人民解放军歼灭，被迫二十七日弃城向吉县境逃窜，大宁城遂告光复。
