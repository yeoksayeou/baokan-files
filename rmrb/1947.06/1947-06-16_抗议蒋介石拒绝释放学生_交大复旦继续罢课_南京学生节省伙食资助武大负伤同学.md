Title: Protesting Chiang Kai-shek's Refusal to Release Students: Jiaotong University and Fudan University Continue Strikes; Nanjing Students Save on Food Expenses to Support Wounded Wuhan University Students
Author:
Date: 1947-06-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th. According to an Associated Press dispatch on the 7th: Jinling University in Nanjing has decided to join the week-long strike initiated by Central University to protest the Chiang government's arrest of students. Students from these two universities have also launched a campaign to save on food expenses to support the wounded students of Wuhan University. Strikes at Shanghai Jiaotong University and Fudan University are continuing, protesting the Chiang government's refusal to release students. Nanchang Zhongzheng University is also continuing its strike.

### 抗议蒋介石拒绝释放学生  交大复旦继续罢课  南京学生节省伙食资助武大负伤同学

1947-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　抗议蒋介石拒绝释放学生
    交大复旦继续罢课
    南京学生节省伙食资助武大负伤同学
    【新华社陕北十四日电】据美联社七日电：南京金大决定参加中大发起之一周罢课，以抗议蒋政府逮捕学生。该两校学生并发动节省伙食，资助负伤的武汉大学学生。上海交大及复旦罢课仍在继续，抗议蒋政府拒绝释放学生。南昌中正大学仍继续罢课。
