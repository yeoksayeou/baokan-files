Title: World Youth Executive Committee Fourth Meeting Focuses on Colonial Youth Issues
Author:
Date: 1947-06-26
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, June 23rd: TASS, Moscow - The World Federation of Democratic Youth Executive Committee is holding its fourth meeting in Moscow. According to the Federation's Chairman, Guy de Boisson, speaking to TASS reporters, this meeting will focus on the issues of colonial youth, and the Federation delegation that visited India, Burma, Indonesia, and Malaya will report on the situation and needs of youth in those regions. At the meeting on the 18th, a resolution on the procedures for the International Youth Day, to be held in Prague from July 20th to August 17th, was passed. The slogan of the Youth Day is: Democratic youth unite, and struggle for democracy in all countries, consolidated and genuine peace between all nations, and the unity and happy future of youth!

### 世青执委第四次会议注意殖民地青年问题

1947-06-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　世青执委第四次会议注意殖民地青年问题
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界民主青年联盟执行委员会正在莫斯科举行第四次会议，据联盟主席盖德·鲍埃逊告塔斯社记者称：此次会议将集中注意于殖民地青年问题，将访问印、缅、印尼及马来亚等地归来之联盟代表团报告各该地青年之情形及其需要。十八日之会议上通过了关于七月二十日至八月十七日在布拉格举行国际青年节之程序的决议案。青年节的口号为：民主青年团结起来，为所有国家实行民主、各国间巩固而真诚的和平及青年的团结与幸福前途而奋斗！
