Title: Czech Military Delegation Arrives in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1947-06-20
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 16th: Belgrade dispatch: A Czech military delegation led by Czech Republic Minister of National Defense General Svoboda and Army Chief of Staff General Bosik arrived in Belgrade on the 11th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included representatives of the Yugoslav government, personnel from the Czech Embassy in Belgrade, and foreign military attachés.

### 捷军事代表团抵达南斯拉夫

1947-06-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷军事代表团抵达南斯拉夫
    【新华社陕北十六日电】贝尔格莱德讯：由捷克斯拉夫共和国国防部长斯沃波达将军及陆军参谋总长波西克将军率领的捷克军事代表团，于十一日抵达贝尔格莱德。赴飞机场欢迎者有南斯拉夫政府代表、捷驻贝尔格莱德大使馆人员及外国武官等。
