Title: Czechoslovak National Front Meeting Resolution: Suggest Political Purge, Strengthen Unity Against Domestic and Foreign Reactionaries
Author:
Date: 1947-06-20
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th: Prague dispatch: The National Front of various political parties in Czechoslovakia and Slovakia recently held a meeting here and passed an important resolution. The resolution decided to strengthen unity and construction, oppose the conspiracies of domestic and foreign reactionaries, and will recommend that the government conduct a thorough political purge. The main contents of the resolution are as follows: (1) Continue to implement the policy of sincere unity and democratic cooperation between the political parties of the National Front, and resolve the relationship between the Czech and Slovak nations on the basis of national unity and equality. (2) Resolutely struggle against the remnants of fascism and escaped traitors who attempt to hinder the construction of Slovakia and the entire republic, consolidate the people's unity in economic, political, cultural, and social life, so as to forever maintain the democratic system of Slovakia and benefit the Slovak and Czech nations, as well as the international status of the republic. (3) The political parties sternly refuted the slander and destructive rumor offensive of certain foreign news agencies against Czechoslovakia. An independent Czechoslovakia will never be allowed to become a springboard for any international conspiracy or foreign reactionary groups to interfere. (4) The committee composed of representatives of the political parties will recommend that the government take certain measures to conduct a thorough political purge to strengthen political unity and ensure the happy development of fraternal relations between the Czech and Slovak nations.

### 捷国各党民族阵线开会决议  建议进行政治清洗  加强团结反对国内外反动派

1947-06-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷国各党民族阵线开会决议
    建议进行政治清洗
    加强团结反对国内外反动派
    【新华社陕北十七日电】布拉格讯：捷克与斯洛伐克各政党的民族阵线顷在此间开会，通过重要决议，决定加强团结与建设，反对国内外反动派的阴谋，并将向政府建议进行彻底的政治清洗。决议主要内容如下：（一）继续执行民族阵线各政党间精诚团结与民主合作的政策，在国家统一与民族平等团结的基础上解决捷克与斯洛伐克两民族间的关系问题。（二）对企图阻碍斯洛伐克及全共和国建设的法西斯残余及漏网的谋叛者进行坚决的斗争，巩固人民在经济、政治、文化与社会生活上之团结，以永远保持斯洛伐克的民主政体，而裨益于斯洛伐克及捷克两民族以及共和国之国际地位。（三）各政党严厉驳斥某些外国新闻机关对捷克的诽谤与破坏性的谣言攻势，独立的捷克斯拉夫绝不允许成为任何国际阴谋或外国反动集团干涉的跳板。（四）各政党代表所组成的委员会，将向政府建议采取某些措施，进行彻底政治清洗，以加强政治团结，并保证捷克与斯洛伐克两民族间兄弟般关系之幸福发展。
