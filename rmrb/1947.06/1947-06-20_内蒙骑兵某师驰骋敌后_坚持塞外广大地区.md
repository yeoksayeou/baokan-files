Title: Inner Mongolia Cavalry Division Gallops Behind Enemy Lines, Persists in Vast Areas Outside the Great Wall
Author:
Date: 1947-06-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe Liaoning, 17th -- An Inner Mongolia cavalry division galloping in Bayannur League and Ulanqab League has stood firm behind enemy lines for half a year, persisting in the vast areas of Shangdu, Taolin, Wuchuan, Huade, and Kangbao, and restoring an area of 500 li from east to west and 300 li from north to south, with a population of hundreds of thousands. In the past six months, they have fought 48 battles, killing and capturing more than 400 enemies, seizing 388 long and short guns, more than 560 war horses, and 53 camels; and returning 3,000 shi of grain to the masses that had been seized by the Fu troops. They have pinned down the city defense forces of seven counties of the Jirecha Military Region and destroyed 42 puppet regime establishments. The Jirecha Military Region, in view of the division's remarkable military achievements, has issued a circular of commendation.

### 内蒙骑兵某师驰骋敌后  坚持塞外广大地区

1947-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　内蒙骑兵某师驰骋敌后
    坚持塞外广大地区
    【新华社冀察热辽十七日电】驰骋于巴彦格拉盟、乌兰察布盟之内蒙骑兵某师，半年来屹立敌后，坚持商都、陶林、武川、化德、康保广大地区，恢复了东西五百里、南北三百里拥有数十万人口的地区。半年来，共作战四十八次，毙俘敌四百余名，缴获长短枪三百八十八支，战马五百六十余匹，骆驼五十三头；发还被傅军抢夺之群众粮食三千石。牵制傅军七个县的城防军，摧毁伪政权四十二处。冀热察军区鉴于该师战功卓著，特予通令嘉奖。
