Title: Our Army Behind Enemy Lines in An Dong Captures Fenchuanling Stronghold
Author:
Date: 1947-06-08
Page: 1

　　Our Army Behind Enemy Lines in An Dong
    Captures Fenchuanling Stronghold
    [Xinhua News Agency, Liaodong, 5th] A certain unit of the Democratic Allied Army, active behind enemy lines in An Dong, captured the Chiang-puppet stronghold of Fenchuanling, north of Huadian, on the 1st. They also routed the entire 11th Detachment of Chiang-puppet security forces that came to reinforce them, killing or wounding more than 40 and capturing 35 alive. They seized a grenade launcher, more than 30 rifles, and some ammunition. The remaining enemy fled towards Niumaowu.

### 安东敌后我军  克分川岭据点

1947-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　安东敌后我军
    克分川岭据点
    【新华社辽东五日电】活跃于安东蒋后之民主联军某部，于一日攻克桦甸北之分川岭蒋伪据点，并击溃增援之蒋伪保安十一支队全部，计毙伤四十余，生俘三十五名。缴获掷弹筒一个，步枪三十余支，弹药一部，残敌向牛毛坞方向逃窜。
