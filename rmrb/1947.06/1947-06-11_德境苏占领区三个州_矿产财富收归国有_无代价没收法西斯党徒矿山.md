Title: Three States in the Soviet Zone of Occupation in Germany Nationalize Mineral Wealth; Confiscate Mines of Fascist Party Members Without Compensation
Author:
Date: 1947-06-11
Page: 3

　　German Soviet Occupation Zone Three States
    Mineral Wealth Nationalized
    Confiscate Mines of Fascist Party Members Without Compensation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th] TASS Berlin Dispatch: The New Germany newspaper reported that the State Assembly of the United States of Saxony-Anhalt in the Soviet occupation zone has passed a resolution to nationalize mineral wealth. Representatives of the Miners' Trade Union Federation from the Soviet, American, and British occupation zones attended the State Assembly. According to the resolution of the State Assembly, nationalization will be applied to all coal, bituminous coal, mineral seedlings, potassium and other mining enterprises produced in Saxony-Anhalt. Mines and mineral deposits belonging to war criminals and active Hitler party members will be nationalized without compensation. This resolution has been unanimously passed by the State Assembly. Furthermore, the State Assembly of Thuringia in the German Soviet occupation zone has also passed a resolution to nationalize mineral wealth.

### 德境苏占领区三个州  矿产财富收归国有  无代价没收法西斯党徒矿山

1947-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　德境苏占领区三个州
    矿产财富收归国有
    无代价没收法西斯党徒矿山
    【新华社陕北七日电】塔斯社柏林讯：新德意志报记者报导：苏占领区萨克森、安哈特联合州州议会顷通过矿产财富国有化之决议。出席州议会者有苏、美、英三占领区矿工职工联合会之代表。根据州议会之决议，国有化施用于萨克森、安哈特之一切出产无烟煤、烟煤、矿苗、钾等采矿企业。属于战争罪犯和积极的希特勒党徒之矿山与矿床将无代价收归国有。此项决议已由州议会一致通过。又讯：德国苏占领区绍伦吉亚州议会亦通过矿产财富国有化之决议。
