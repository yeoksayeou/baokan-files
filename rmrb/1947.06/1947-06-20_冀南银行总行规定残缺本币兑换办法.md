Title: Regulations of the General Office of Jinnan Bank Regarding the Exchange of Damaged Banknotes

Author: 
Date: 1947-06-20
Page: 1

　　The regulations of this bank regarding the exchange of damaged banknotes have now been revised and are announced as follows:
　　I. Banknotes with three-quarters of the surface remaining on any side, and confirmed to be genuine, will be exchanged at face value (applies to vertical banknotes).
　　II. Banknotes with one-half of the surface remaining will be exchanged at 80% of half the face value.
　　III. Banknotes where the serial numbers on the left and right halves do not match will also be exchanged at face value.
　　IV. Intact banknotes that are water-damaged or oil-soaked, and confirmed to be genuine, will be exchanged at face value.
　　V. Banknotes with less than one-half of the surface remaining will not be exchanged.
　　VI. Banknotes pieced together from multiple fragments, regardless of whether they constitute a whole or half, will not be exchanged.
　　VII. The handling fee for entrusted exchange offices to exchange damaged banknotes is set at ten to fifteen yuan per hundred banknotes.

### 冀南银行总行规定残缺本币兑换办法

1947-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南银行总行规定残缺本币兑换办法
    本行残缺本币兑换办法，兹经重新修正，公布如下：一、票面靠任何一边存在四分之三确系真票者按原值兑换（立式票同）。二、票面存在二分之一者，以半数计八折兑换。三、票面左右两半截字头号不相同者，亦按原值兑换。四、水湿油浸票面完整确系真票者，原值兑换。五、各种票面不足二分之一者不予兑换。六、票面多块残成者，不论整张半张一律不予兑换。七、委托兑换所代兑破票其手续费规定每百张十元至十五元计。
