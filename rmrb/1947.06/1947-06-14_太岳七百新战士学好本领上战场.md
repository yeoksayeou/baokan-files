Title: Taiyue Seven Hundred New Soldiers Learn Skills to Go to the Battlefield
Author:
Date: 1947-06-14
Page: 1

　　Taiyue Seven Hundred New Soldiers
　　Learn Skills to Go to the Battlefield
　　[This newspaper's Taiyue dispatch, 13th] To welcome the great counter-offensive, seven hundred new soldiers and one hundred and fifty convalescing personnel from Taiyue set off for the front line on the 5th of this month. They are all strong peasants who turned themselves over in the land reform. After two months of martial arts training after joining the army, everyone has learned a set of skills. Everyone can throw grenades 31 to 40 meters, and very accurately.

### 太岳七百新战士学好本领上战场

1947-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　太岳七百新战士
  　学好本领上战场
    【本报太岳十三日电】为迎接大反攻，太岳七百名新战士与百五十名休养员，于本月五日同时开赴前线。他们都是在土地改革中翻了身的健壮农民，参军后经过两个月的练武，每人都学会了一套本领，每人投弹三十一米到四十米，并且很准确。
