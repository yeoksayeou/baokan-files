Title: The Four Big Families Open Their Ravenous Maws to Swallow Up Local Private Banks
Author:
Date: 1947-06-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th. According to a Central News Agency dispatch on the 6th: Amidst a serious economic crisis and the Four Big Families' policy of annexation and plunder, banks and money shops in the Chiang-controlled areas have been forced to close down. Shanghai's Jianghai Bank, Jianguo Bank and their branches in Nanjing, Hangzhou, Chongqing, Chongqing's Chengdu Money Shop, and Tianjin's Yiyuan Bank, etc., have all been closed down by the Chiang authorities due to huge losses. The Central Bank is investigating their accounts, and all deposits will be merged into that bank.

### 四大家族大张血口  吞并各地民营银行

1947-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　四大家族大张血口
    吞并各地民营银行
    【新华社陕北九日电】据中央社六日讯：在严重经济危机和四大家族并吞掠夺政策下，蒋区银行、银号纷纷被迫倒闭。上海江海银行、建国银行及其在京、杭、渝各地分行，重庆之成大银号，及天津之义垣银行等各行已因亏累甚巨，遭蒋当局封闭交由中央银行清查账目，并将所有存款归并该行。
