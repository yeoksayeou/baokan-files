Title: Greek Guerrillas Capture Daporte City; US Actively Aids Greek Reactionaries in Attacking the People
Author:
Date: 1947-06-23
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th: TASS, Athens: On the morning of June 12th, the Greek government forces began an offensive against the guerrillas operating in the areas of Mount Olympus and Mount Piaria. According to official newspapers, the 2nd and 3rd Army Corps, totaling twenty battalions and the gendarmerie, are carrying out the offensive with naval and air support. It is also claimed that 3,000 guerrillas have been concentrated in the above two areas. Fighting is ongoing in the Kalamata area of the Moria Peninsula. Guerrillas have appeared in the Ayia area of northern Moria Peninsula and have occupied Daporte City, burning down the city's gendarmerie headquarters.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th: TASS, Paris: According to a report in *l'Humanité*, a large quantity of American military supplies has been handed over to Tsaldaris so that he can continue to attack the Greek guerrillas. The report continues: In a few days, many American officials will arrive in Athens. Monarchist fascists are intensifying their terror tactics against the people. Recently, seventeen patriots were executed. According to reports from Athens, on the 12th of this month, another fifty people were sentenced to death by military tribunals in various Greek cities. In Slana, five farmers were sentenced to be shot for providing useful information to the guerrillas.

### 希腊游击队克达埠城  美积极援希反动派进攻人民

1947-06-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　希腊游击队克达埠城
    美积极援希反动派进攻人民
    【新华社陕北十九日电】塔斯社雅典讯：希腊政府军在六月十二日晨，开始向活动于奥林匹斯山及派阿里亚山一带的游击发动攻势。据官方报纸报导：陆军第二和第三军团共有二十个营及宪兵队在海空配合下从事进攻。又称：有三千游击队在上述两地区已经集中起来。摩利亚半岛卡拉马塔地区的战斗正在进行中，游击队已在摩利亚半岛北部之亚希亚地区出现，并且已占领了达埠城，将该城的宪兵司令部烧毁。
    【新华社陕北十九日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：据人道报讯：大批美国军火已交与曹达利斯，以使其能继续进攻希腊游击队。该报继称：几天内很多美国官员将到达雅典，君主主义法西斯分子正加强恐怖手段进攻人民，最近就有十七个爱国者被枪决。又据雅典讯：希腊各城于本月十二日又有五十人被军事法庭判处死刑。在斯拉那有五个农民被判枪决。罪名是供给游击队以有用情报。
