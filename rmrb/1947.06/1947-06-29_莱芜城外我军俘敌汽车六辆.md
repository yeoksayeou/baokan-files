Title: Our Army Captures Six Enemy Automobiles Outside Laiwu City
Author:
Date: 1947-06-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 27th. (Delayed report) A team from a certain unit of the East China People's Liberation Army, on the 17th at dawn, penetrated deep into Nanjiang Village, seven li east of Laiwu City, capturing six Chiang Kai-shek army automobiles, killing a Chiang Kai-shek squad leader and platoon leader each, capturing a deputy battalion commander of a new recruit battalion, and burning two automobiles on the spot. Diao Zhongren, the special-class combat model who led the team, has been commended by the unit's order.

### 莱芜城外我军俘敌汽车六辆

1947-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　莱芜城外我军俘敌汽车六辆
    【新华社华东前线二十七日电】（迟到）华东人民解放军某部一队，十七日拂晓深入莱芜城东七里之南姜家庄，俘获蒋军汽车六辆，毙蒋军班排长各一，俘新兵营营副一，并当场焚毁汽车二辆。率领该队之特等战斗模范刁忠仁，已获该部传令嘉奖。
