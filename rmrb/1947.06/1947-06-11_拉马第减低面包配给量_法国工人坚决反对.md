Title: Ramadier Reduces Bread Ration, French Workers Firmly Oppose

Author:

Date: 1947-06-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th. According to Agence France-Presse, Paris: The food situation in France is extremely serious. The bread ration in the Paris area for Monday and Tuesday has been reduced to 150 grams (approximately 4.8 taels) per person per day. French workers firmly oppose the reduction of the bread ration, and bakers have declared that they will go on strike to protest this measure. The Ramadier government was forced to decide on the 1st that manual laborers would receive an additional 75 grams per day.

### 拉马第减低面包配给量  法国工人坚决反对

1947-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　拉马第减低面包配给量
    法国工人坚决反对
    【新华社陕北七日电】据法国新闻社巴黎讯：法国粮食情况异常严重，巴黎区之星期一、星期二面包配给量已减为每人每日一百五十克（约四·八两）。法国工人坚决反对减低面包配给量，面包工人宣称将举行罢工抗议此项措施。拉马第政府被迫于一日决定粗工工人每日将额外获得七十五克。
