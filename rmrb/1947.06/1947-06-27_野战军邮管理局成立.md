Title: Field Army Postal Administration Established
Author: Rong Hua
Date: 1947-06-27
Page: 2

　　Field Army Postal Administration Established
    [Our Newspaper] In order to strengthen the unified leadership of the field army's accompanying postal bureaus, improve work efficiency, and seek close contact between the front and rear, the Border Region Postal Administration and the Political Department of the Field Army have jointly decided to establish the Field Army Postal Administration. Comrade Chen Moran, the former director of the 15th Army Postal Bureau, will continue to serve as the director. Other cadres are being selected and transferred by the General Administration.
        (Rong Hua)

### 野战军邮管理局成立
荣华
1947-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　野战军邮管理局成立
    【本报讯】为了加强野战随军邮局的统一领导，提高工作效能，求得前后方密切联系，特由边区邮政总局协商野战军政治部共同决定：设立野战军邮政管理局，局长由原十五军邮局长陈墨然同志继任，其他干部均由总局筹调中。
        （荣华）
