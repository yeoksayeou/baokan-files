Author: Chen Guojun
Date: 1947-06-14
Page: 4

Folk Rhyme
    Chen Guojun
    Good child,
    Has a good mother;
    Good seeds,
    Need good fertilizer;
    If seeds are thin,
    Seedlings are yellow;
    If seeds are plump,
    Seedlings are strong;
    Stalks grow tall,
    Ears grow long;
    In spring, plant good seeds,
    In autumn, the granary will be full of grain.

### 农谣
陈国均
1947-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　农谣
    陈国均
    好孩子，
    有好娘；
    好种籽，
    打好肥；
    种籽瘦，
    苗儿黄；
    种籽肥，
    苗儿壮；
    秸秆长的高，
    穗子长的长；
    春天种下好种村，
    秋来粮食打满仓。
