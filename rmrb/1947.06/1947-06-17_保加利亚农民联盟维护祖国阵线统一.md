Title: Bulgarian Agrarian Union Upholds Fatherland Front Unity
Author:
Date: 1947-06-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th. TASS, Sofia: The Supreme Committee of the Bulgarian Agrarian Union recently passed a resolution endorsing the Supreme Committee of the Bulgarian Agrarian Union's full maintenance of the Fatherland Front's unity, and the faithful and brotherly friendship between the Communist Workers' Party and the Agrarian Union based on mutual trust. Regardless of the enemy's wishes, our union has been maintained by the strength of the entire peasant organization and will be strengthened. The resolution finally urged union members to do their best to consolidate the Fatherland Front and implement its pro-national plans.

### 保加利亚农民联盟维护祖国阵线统一

1947-06-17
第3版()
专栏：欧洲民主力量不断战胜反对分子

　　保加利亚农民联盟维护祖国阵线统一
    【新华社陕北十五日电】塔斯社索菲亚讯：保加利亚农民联盟高级委员会会议顷通过决议，赞同保国农民联盟高级委员会充分的维护祖国阵线之统一，及共产主义工人政党与农民联盟间基于相互信任的忠实与兄弟般之友谊。不管敌人的愿望如何，我们的联盟已经为整个农民组织之力量所维护，而且将得以加强。决议最后敦促联盟会员尽力巩固祖国阵线，并执行其有利国民的计划。
