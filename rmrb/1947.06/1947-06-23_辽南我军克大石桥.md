Title: Our Army in Southern Liaoning Captures Dashiqiao
Author:
Date: 1947-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 20th. On the Southern Liaoning front, a unit of the Democratic Allied Army recovered the important railway junction of Dashiqiao on the evening of the 16th, and is now pursuing the enemy towards Yingkou and Haicheng.

### 辽南我军克大石桥

1947-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　辽南我军克大石桥
    【新华社东北前线二十日电】辽南方面，民主联军一部十六日晚收复重要铁路联络点大石桥，现正分向营口、海城追击中。
