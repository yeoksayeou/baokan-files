Title: North China Foreign Trade Administration Beijing Office Established; Handles Applications for Import and Export Goods
Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 3

　　North China Foreign Trade Administration
    Beijing Office Established
    Handles Applications for Import and Export Goods

    [This Newspaper's Report] The North China Foreign Trade Administration, in light of actual needs and in response to requests from various parties, specially requested and received approval from the Central Trade Ministry to establish an office in Beijing. The office is now fully prepared and will begin operations on the 9th of this month at 29 Nanchizi, this city. All applications for import and export goods and other related specific matters will be accepted and processed. Hereafter, import and export merchants in Beijing encountering application and business matters can directly handle them at this office, which will reduce the inconvenience of traveling between Beijing and Tianjin.
    (Foreign Trade Beijing Office Communications Group)

### 华北对外贸易管理局  北京办事处成立  办理出进口货物申请等事项

1950-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　华北对外贸易管理局
    北京办事处成立
    办理出进口货物申请等事项
    【本报讯】华北对外贸易管理局鉴于实际需要，并应各方要求，特呈准中央贸易部在京成立办事处。现该处业已筹备就绪，定于本月九日在本市南池子二十九号开始办公。凡出进口货物之申请及其他有关具体事项均行接受办理。嗣后在京之出进口商遇有申请及洽办事项可径向该处办理，将减少京津往来之不便。     
    （外贸京处通讯组）
