Title: US Imperialism Plans to Establish "Cold War" Center in Bijing (Brussels)
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 4

　　US Imperialism Plans to Establish "Cold War" Center in Bijing (Brussels)
    [Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 9th] TASS Rome, 8th: "The National Newspaper" reported: "It is learned that Acheson proposed at the recent London Three Foreign Ministers Conference the establishment of a new center for ideological warfare against the Soviet Union and Eastern European countries. Brussels will be the location of this new center. This center will be headed by Donovan, the former head of the US Strategic Services (US intelligence agency).
    The main task of the Brussels center is to carry out "Cold War" propaganda. In addition, it will also assist in implementing measures to suppress the activities of communist parties and left-wing organizations in the North Atlantic Treaty countries. To this end, the Brussels center will establish permanent contact with the intelligence agencies of all North Atlantic Treaty countries."

### 美帝拟在比京  设“冷战”中心

1950-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝拟在比京
    设“冷战”中心
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社罗马八日讯：《国家报》报道：“据悉，艾奇逊在最近举行的伦敦三国外长会议上，曾提议建立一个新的中心，以便对苏联和东欧国家进行思想战。比京布鲁塞尔将是这个新的中心的地点。这个中心将由前美国战略服务处（美国谍报机关）处长杜诺万主持。
    布鲁塞尔中心的主要任务是进行“冷战”宣传。除此之外，它也要协助执行镇压北大西洋公约各国共产党及左翼组织的活动的措施。为此目的，布鲁塞尔中心将与所有北大西洋公约国家的特务机关建立永久的联系。”
