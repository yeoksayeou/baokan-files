Title: The 37th Administrative Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passes List of Appointments and Submissions for Appointment and Removal

Author:

Date: 1950-06-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 26th—The 37th Administrative Council meeting of the Government Administration Council approved the following list of appointments:
  I. Supplementary list of staff of the Central Financial and Economic Planning Bureau
     Basic Construction Planning Division
     Director: Sun Yueqi (concurrent)
     Deputy Director: Guo Kequan, formerly Deputy Director of the Planning Division of the Industrial Department of the East China Military and Political Committee.
     Transportation Planning Division
     Deputy Director: Zhang Jiucheng, formerly Deputy Director of the Fourth District Highway Engineering Administration Bureau of the Highway General Bureau of the Kuomintang Ministry of Communications.
   II. List of Deputy Directors of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government
     Soviet and Eastern European Department
     Deputy Director: Xu Yixin, formerly Minister of the Garrison Work Department of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
   III. List of Director of the General Office of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee
     General Office
     Director: Wei Jinfei (concurrent)
    Xinhua News Agency, 26th—The 37th Administrative Council meeting of the Government Administration Council approved the following list of submissions to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals:
   I. Supplementary list of staff of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee
     Deputy Secretary-General: Wei Jinfei, currently Deputy Minister of the Finance Department of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee.
     (Remove Wei Jinfei from his current position as Deputy Minister of the Finance Department of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee.)
   II. Supplementary list of members of the Northwest Military and Political Committee
     Six members (in order of number of strokes in surname)
     Li Zhimin, currently Political Commissar of the Army Corps of the People's Liberation Army.
     Gao Guizi, military personnel.
     Huang Zhengqing, currently member of the Gansu Provincial People's Government.
     Dali Zaya, currently member of the Ningxia Provincial People's Government.
     Liao Hansheng, currently Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government.
     Pan Zili, currently Chairman of the Ningxia Provincial People's Government.
