Title: Soviet Scholars' Lectures Today: "Local Organs of Soviet State Power" and "Marxist-Leninist View on the Party Spirit of Philosophy"
Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 3

　　Soviet Scholars' Lectures Today:
　　"Local Organs of Soviet State Power" and "Marxist-Leninist View on the Party Spirit of Philosophy"
　　[Our Newspaper] Professor Askerov, a renowned Soviet legal scholar invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will lecture on "Local Organs of Soviet State Power" at the Fu Jen Catholic University auditorium at 3:00 PM on June 9th. He will elaborate on the people's nature of Soviet state power, democratic centralism (the relationship between local and central state power organs), the formation, organization, and powers of local state power organs (Soviets at the township, district, city, and provincial levels). He will also cite numerous practical materials to illustrate the actual work of Soviet local state power organs, the activities of representatives of Soviets at all levels, and how to absorb the broad masses of people to actively participate in the work of the regime. At 7:00 PM, he will lecture at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on "The Stalin Constitution is a Constitution of the Victory of Socialism and Worker-Peasant Democracy."
　　Professor Chesnokov, a Soviet authoritative philosopher, will lecture on "Marxist-Leninist View on the Party Spirit of Philosophy" at the Marxist-Leninist Institute at 9:00 AM on the same day. (Propaganda Department of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 苏联两学者今日讲题：《苏联国家政权的地方机关》及《马列主义论哲学的党性》

1950-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联两学者今日讲题：
    《苏联国家政权的地方机关》及《马列主义论哲学的党性》
    【本报讯】应中苏友协总会之邀来我国讲学的苏联著名法学家阿斯凯洛夫教授，于六月九日下午三时，在辅仁大学礼堂讲《苏联国家政权的地方机关》一题，阐述苏联国家政权的人民性、民主集中制（地方政权机关与中央政权机关之相互关系）、地方政权机关（乡、区、市、省各级苏维埃）之产生、组织及权限，并引举许多实际材料，说明苏联地方政权机关之实际工作、各级苏维埃代表之活动以及吸收广大人民群众积极参加政权工作之情形。下午七时在外交部讲《斯大林宪法是社会主义及工农民主胜利的宪法》。
    苏联权威哲学家车斯洛柯夫教授于同日上午九时在马列学院讲《马列主义论哲学的党性》。    （北京中苏友好协会宣传部）
