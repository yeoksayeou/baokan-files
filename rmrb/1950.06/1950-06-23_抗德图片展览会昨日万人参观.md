Title: Anti-German Photo Exhibition Attracts Ten Thousand Visitors Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 3

　　Anti-German Photo Exhibition Attracts Ten Thousand Visitors Yesterday
    [Report from this newspaper] The "Ninth Anniversary of the Soviet Anti-German War Photo Exhibition" sponsored by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at the Laboring People's Cultural Palace officially opened yesterday. Yesterday, more than 10,000 people visited. In response to audience requests, the association has decided to change the exhibition hours to 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM daily. On June 25th (Sunday), the Trade Union Council will hold a workers' garden party at the Cultural Palace. To avoid overcrowding, visitors to the Cultural Palace on the 25th must have garden party tickets.

### 抗德图片展览会昨日万人参观

1950-06-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗德图片展览会昨日万人参观
    【本报讯】中苏友协在劳动人民文化宫主办之“苏联抗德战争九周年图片展览会”，昨日正式开幕。昨天有一万余人前往参观。该会为应观众要求，决定将展览时间改为每日上午十时起至下午七时止，本月廿五日（星期日）总工会在文化宫举办工人游园联欢大会，为避免过于拥挤起见，在二十五这一天，进入文化宫参观者，须有游园大会入场券。
