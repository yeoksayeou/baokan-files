Date: 1950-06-17

　　Brief News
    Xinjiang Education Director Visits Normal University
    Anniwaer, Director of the Xinjiang Provincial Department of Education, and Yibulapu, Deputy Director of the Xinjiang Provincial Cultural and Education Committee, visited the Normal University and a part of the Normal University High School at 10:00 AM on the 14th.
                                                      (Zhu Liang)
    Guangzhou Women's Normal School Students Visit Renmin University
    All 82 students of the Guangdong Provincial Guangzhou Women's Normal School Northern Education Delegation arrived in Beijing on the 1st of this month. They visited Beijing Normal University, Yucai School, and other schools, and participated in seminars on land reform, the establishment of Young Pioneers, and education issues in Beijing. The delegation also visited the Renmin University of China at 8:00 AM on the 14th.
                                      (China Renmin University Communications Group)

专栏：

　　简讯
    新疆教育厅长参观师范大学
    新疆省教育厅长安尼瓦尔、新疆省文教委员会副主任伊不拉普，于十四日上午十时到师大及师大附中一部参观。
                                                      （诸良）
    广州女师同学参观人民大学
    广东省立广州女师北上教育考察团全体同学八十二人，于本月一日抵京，曾参观北师、育才等校。并参加有关土地改革、少年儿童建队问题及北京教育问题等座谈会。该团并于十四日晨八时前往中国人民大学参观。
                                      （中国人民大学通讯组）
