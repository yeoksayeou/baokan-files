Title: People in Various Parts of Britain Continue to Sign Up Enthusiastically

Author:

Date: 1950-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The peace movement in Britain is surging. Since the British Peace Committee launched a nationwide peace signature campaign on April 24th, by the end of May, 145,000 people had signed the declaration of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. Local peace committees are being widely established throughout Britain. By the beginning of May, more than sixty peace committees had already been established.

### 英国各地人民继续踊跃签名

1950-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国各地人民继续踊跃签名
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：英国的和平运动在高涨中。自英国和平委员会于四月二十四日发动全国性和平签名运动以来，到五月底为止，已有十四万五千人在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的宣言上签了名。英国各地正在普遍建立地方和平委员会。在五月初，已经建立的和平委员会，就有六十来个。
