Title: Philippine Government's Illegal "New Order" Forces Overseas Chinese to Re-register
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 22nd: Following Philippine President Quirino's signing on the 12th of an "Immigration Act" aimed at restricting the entry of Overseas Chinese, Philippine Undersecretary of Justice and Acting Immigration Commissioner Benson announced on the 20th a new measure to oppress Overseas Chinese, declaring that foreign nationals in the Philippines (the vast majority of whom are Overseas Chinese) must re-register in accordance with the "New Order". According to the new order, each foreign national must pay a registration fee of 50.05 Philippine pesos.

### 菲政府非法“新令”  迫使华侨重新登记

1950-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲政府非法“新令”
    迫使华侨重新登记
    【新华社二十二日讯】马尼拉讯：在菲律宾总统季里诺于十二日签署一项旨在限制华侨入境的《移民法案》之后，菲司法部次长兼代移民局长本生又于二十日发布了一项压迫华侨的新措施，宣布在菲外侨（绝大多数为华侨）需遵照《新法令》重行登记。根据新法令，每一外侨须缴纳登记费菲币五十元零五角。
