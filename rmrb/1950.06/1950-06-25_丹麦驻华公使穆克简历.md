Biography of Danish Envoy to China, Mr. Munk

　　Mr. Munk was born on March 19, 1896. He graduated from the Faculty of Law of the University of Copenhagen in 1922. In the same year, he joined the Danish diplomatic service and held various positions in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and abroad. From 1929 to 1934, he served as Vice Consul at the Danish Consulate General in Shanghai. In 1941, he became Section Chief of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In 1945, he served as Acting Director of the General Affairs Department and the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In 1946, he was appointed Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to China. In 1947, he concurrently served as Minister to Thailand. In 1948, he also concurrently served as Minister to the Republic of the Philippines.

### 丹麦驻华公使穆克简历

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　丹麦驻华公使穆克简历
    穆克生于一八九六年三月十九日。一九二二年在哥本哈根大学法律系毕业。是年即进入丹麦外交界服务，在外交部及国外历任各职。一九二九年至一九三四年曾任丹麦驻上海总领事馆副领事。一九四一年任外交部科长。一九四五年任外交部总务司及交际司代司长。一九四六年任驻华特命全权公使。一九四七年兼任驻泰国公使。一九四八年再兼任驻菲律宾共和国公使。
                【新华社】
