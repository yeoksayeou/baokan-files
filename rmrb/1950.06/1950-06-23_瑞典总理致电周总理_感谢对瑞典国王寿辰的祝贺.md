Title: Swedish Prime Minister Cables Premier Zhou to Thank Him for Congratulations on the Swedish King's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Swedish Prime Minister Tage Erlander cabled Premier Zhou Enlai on the 17th, expressing his gratitude for Premier Zhou's congratulatory message on the Swedish King's birthday. The cable is as follows:
His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Please accept my sincere thanks, on behalf of the Swedish people, for your kind congratulations on the occasion of His Majesty the King's birthday.

Tage Erlander, Prime Minister
Stockholm, June 17

### 瑞典总理致电周总理  感谢对瑞典国王寿辰的祝贺

1950-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞典总理致电周总理
    感谢对瑞典国王寿辰的祝贺
    【新华社二十二日讯】瑞典总理特基·艾兰德于十七日致电周恩来总理，对周总理电贺瑞典国王寿辰表示谢忱。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国政府总理周恩来阁下：
    于敝国国王陛下寿辰之日，承蒙赐贺，谨代表瑞典人民向阁下表示真诚的谢忱。
      总理特基·艾兰德
            六月十七日于斯德哥尔摩
