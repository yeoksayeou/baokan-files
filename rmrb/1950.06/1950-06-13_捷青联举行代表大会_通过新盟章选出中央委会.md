Title: Czechoslovak Youth League Holds Congress, Adopts New Constitution, and Elects Central Committee

Author:

Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague: The Czechoslovak Youth League, with 1.1 million members, held its first congress from June 7th to 10th. At the final session, the congress adopted the new constitution, stipulating that the eligible age for joining the league is 15 to 26 years old. The congress elected the Central Committee of the League, and elected Herzlal as Chairman of the League and Krutina as Secretary-General. The congress approved the political line and program of activities of the League's Central Committee. Delegates passed a salute telegram to Czechoslovak President Gottwald with great enthusiasm.

### 捷青联举行代表大会  通过新盟章选出中央委会

1950-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷青联举行代表大会
    通过新盟章选出中央委会
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：拥有会员一百一十万人的捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟，于六月七日至十日举行了第一次代表大会。大会在最后一次会议中通过新盟章，规定参加联盟的合格年龄为十五岁到二十六岁。大会选举了联盟中央委员会。并选出赫兹拉尔为联盟主席，克鲁蒂纳为总书记。大会批准了联盟中央委员会的政治路线和活动纲领。与会代表以极大的热情，通过了向捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德致敬电。
