Title: This Newspaper Receives Peace Signatures: Total Exceeds 510,000

Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 4

　　This Newspaper Receives Peace Signatures: Total Exceeds 510,000

　　[This Newspaper News] From the 4th to the 7th of this month, for four days, this newspaper received 70,496 signatures from participants in the movement to defend world peace. Among them were 774 signatures from children and mothers forwarded by the Beijing Children and Youth Society, 2,446 signatures from readers forwarded by the Beijing Sanlian Bookstore, and signatures from over 200 units, including Fourth Field Army soldiers stationed in You County, Hunan, workers from the Yantai Xingtai Construction Factory, farmers from Ji'an County in Liaodong, members of the Chengdu October Reading Club, and employees of the Huiyang County branch of the People's Bank of China in Guangdong. New language workers in Linhai City, Zhejiang, signed using the new script. This newspaper has now received a total of 510,769 signatures from peace supporters.

### 本报收到和平签名  共达五十一万余人

1950-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　本报收到和平签名  共达五十一万余人
    【本报讯】本报自本月四日至七日的四天里收到七万零四百九十六名参加保卫世界和平签名运动者的签名。其中有北京少年儿童社转来七百七十四名小朋友及母亲的签名，北京三联书店转来二千四百四十六名读者的签名，以及驻在湖南攸县的四野战士、烟台兴台营造厂工人、辽东辑安县农民、成都十月读书会会员、广东惠阳县中国人民银行分行职工等二百余单位的签名。浙江临海市新文字工作者是以新文字签名的。本报连前已共收到五十一万零七百六十九名和平拥护者的签名。
