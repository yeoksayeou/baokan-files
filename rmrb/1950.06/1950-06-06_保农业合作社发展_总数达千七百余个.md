Title: Developing Agricultural Cooperatives: Total Reaches Over 1,700

Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 4th: According to TASS, the total number of agricultural cooperatives in Bulgaria has grown to 1,727, with over 206,000 participating farm households. Between January and May 15th of this year alone, 124 new agricultural cooperatives were established, uniting 8,540 farm households with 30,500 hectares of cultivated land.

### 保农业合作社发展  总数达千七百余个

1950-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　保农业合作社发展  总数达千七百余个
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚的农业合作社，总数已发展到一千七百二十七个，参加的农户已超过二十万六千户。仅在今年一月到五月十五日之间，就成立了一百二十四个新的农业合作社，团结了拥有耕地三万零五百公顷的八千五百四十家农户。
