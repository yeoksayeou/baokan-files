Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Opens Dongdan Reading Room in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-06-07
Page: 3

　　Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Opens Dongdan Reading Room in Beijing
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Dongdan Reading Room of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association opened on June 3rd. The address is 185 Chongnei Street. It has a variety of Russian newspapers and magazines, as well as Chinese books, magazines and newspapers. It is also equipped with an exhibition room, which is currently exhibiting Soviet photos including: Soviet Trade Unions, the May Day celebration in Moscow this year, and Soviet children. The reading room is open from 12:00 noon to 8:00 pm daily.
    In addition, the exhibition room of the association at 19 Nanheyan is currently exhibiting photos of Soviet labor heroes and Soviet care for mothers and children. It is open daily from 11:00 am to 7:00 pm (closed on Saturdays).

### 苏联对外文协在北京  增设东单阅览室

1950-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联对外文协在北京
    增设东单阅览室
    【本报讯】苏联对外文化协会之东单阅览室已于六月三日开幕。地址在崇内大街一八五号，备有俄文报纸杂志及中文书籍杂志和报纸多种，并附设有展览室，现正在该室展览的苏联照片计有：苏联职工会，本年五一节在莫斯科的庆祝，苏联儿童等。该处阅览室每日中午十二时至下午八时开放。
    又南河沿十九号该会之展览室现正展览苏联劳动英雄、苏联对母亲和儿童的关怀等照片。每日上午十一时起至下午七时（星期六休息）开放。
