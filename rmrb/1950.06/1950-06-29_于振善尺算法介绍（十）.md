Introduction to Yu Zhenshan's Slide Rule Algorithm (10)

    Example】6 people should pay 300 yuan. How much should 8 people pay?
    Solution: As shown in Figure 17
    (1) Align 6 on the upper scale (representing 6 people) with 3 on the lower scale (representing 300 yuan);
    (2) The number on the lower scale aligned with 8 on the upper scale (representing 8 people) is 4, which is the total amount sought;
    (3) Determine that the total number should be a three-digit number according to the positioning method.
    Therefore, we find that: 8 people should pay 400 yuan.
   【Note】The numbers 10, 9, 7, 4... on the upper scale correspond to 5, 4.5, 3.5, 2... on the lower scale, which represent 300÷6×10, 300÷6×9, 300÷6×7, 300÷6×4... respectively.
    X. Continuous Multiplication and Division Algorithm
    Number A × Number B ÷ Number C = Total
    Slide Rule: Square slide rule calculator (including one reciprocal upper scale and one lower scale).
    The 0 point of the reciprocal upper scale is fixed on the 0 line of the lower scale.
    Solution:
    (1) Align Number A (or Number B) on the reciprocal upper scale with Number B (or Number A) on the lower scale;
    (2) The number on the lower scale aligned with Number C on the upper scale is the total number sought;
    (3) Determine the number of digits of the total number according to the positioning method.
   【Example】Each box contains 800 tacks. Now there are 6 boxes. If they are distributed to 12 people, how many tacks can each person get?
    Solution: As shown in Figure 18
    (1) Align 8 on the reciprocal upper scale (representing 800 tacks) with 6 on the lower scale (representing 6 boxes);
    (2) The number on the lower scale aligned with 12 on the upper scale (representing 12 people) is 4, which is the total number sought;
    (3) Determine that the total number is a three-digit number according to the positioning method.
    【Note 1】Therefore, we find that: Each person can get 400 tacks. The numbers 8, 16... on the upper scale correspond to 6, 3... on the lower scale, which represent 800×6÷8, 800×6÷16... and the respective totals are 600, 300...
    【Note 2】This situation can also be calculated once using a square slide rule calculator, a circular slide rule calculator, or a rectangular slide rule calculator that only includes positive number upper and lower scales, following the method of dividing first and then multiplying. ### 于振善尺算法介绍（十）

1950-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    于振善尺算法介绍（十）
    例题】6个人应交300元，问8个人应交多少元？
    求法：如第十七图所示
    （1）把天尺上的6（代表6个人）对准地尺上的3（代表300元）；
    （2）天尺上的8（代表8个人）所对着的地尺上的4即所求的总数；
    （3）按照定位法求出总数应为三位数。
    所以求得：8个人应交400元。
   【注】天尺上的10，9，7，4，……所对着的地尺上的5，4，5，3，5，2，……就是分别代表300÷6×10，300÷6×9，300÷6×7，300÷6×4，……的总数。
    十、乘法连算法
    甲数×乙数÷丙数=总数
    用尺：方形尺算器（包括倒数天尺及地尺各一个）。
    倒数天尺的0点固定在地尺的0线上。
    求法：
    （1）把倒数天尺上的甲数（或乙数）对准地尺上的乙数（或甲数）；
    （2）天尺上的丙数所对着的地尺上的数即是所求的总数；
    （3）按照定位法求出总数的位数。
   【例题】每盒800个按钉，今有6盒，分给12个人，问每人可分得按钉多少个？
    求法：如第十八图所示
    （1）把倒数天尺上的8（代表800个）对准地尺上的6（代表6盒）；
    （2）天尺上的12（代表12个人）所对着的地尺上的4即是所求的总数；
    （3）按照定位法求出总数为三位数。
    【注1】所以求得：每人可分得按钉400个。天尺上的8，16……所对着的地尺上的6，3……就是代表800×6÷8，800×6÷16……的总数各为600，300，……。
    【注2】这种情形亦可用仅包括正数天尺及地尺的方形尺算器或圆形尺算器或长形尺算器按照先除后乘的方法一次求出。（附图片）
