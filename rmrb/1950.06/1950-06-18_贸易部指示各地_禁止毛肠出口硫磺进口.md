Title: Ministry of Trade Directs All Regions to Prohibit Export of Casings, Import of Sulfur
Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: To safeguard domestic production, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government issued a directive on June 1st to all regions, stipulating that from now on, no approval shall be granted for the export of hog casings. At the same time, in order to protect production and save foreign exchange, a decision was made on June 2nd that the import of foreign sulfur in lump or powder form will be prohibited from now on.

### 贸易部指示各地  禁止毛肠出口硫磺进口

1950-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　贸易部指示各地
    禁止毛肠出口硫磺进口
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府贸易部为维护国内生产，曾于六月一日指示各地今后一律不得批准毛肠出口。同时为了保护生产与节省外汇，在六月二日决定今后外国硫磺块粉一律禁止进口。
