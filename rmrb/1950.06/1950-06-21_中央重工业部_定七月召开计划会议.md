Title: Central Ministry of Heavy Industry to Convene Planning Conference in July
Author:
Date: 1950-06-21
Page: 1

　　Central Ministry of Heavy Industry
    To Convene Planning Conference in July
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] The Central People's Government Ministry of Heavy Industry has decided to convene a planning conference in Beijing in early July, bringing together responsible persons from its subordinate units and the industrial departments of major administrative regions, to discuss the organization and methods for compiling production plans and basic construction plans for the national heavy industry sector. The ministry has notified the industrial departments of major administrative regions and its subordinate specialized bureaus to attach importance to this conference and to make serious preparations in advance: to collect and compile detailed information on their subordinate heavy industrial plants and mines; to study the current situation of their subordinate key plants and mines and to plan for next year's production; and to estimate the future development of their subordinate heavy industrial plants and mines based on existing conditions.

### 中央重工业部  定七月召开计划会议

1950-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央重工业部
    定七月召开计划会议
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府重工业部决定七月上旬召集所属各单位、各大行政区工业部负责人在京举行计划会议，讨论编制全国重工业部门的生产计划及基本建设计划的组织与方法问题。该部已通报各大行政区工业部及所属各专业局重视这一会议，并认真地预作准备：搜集并编写所属各重工业厂矿详细资料；研究所属各重要厂矿现在之情况并计划明年之生产；根据现有条件估计所属各重工业厂矿今后之发展。
