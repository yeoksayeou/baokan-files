Circular of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government

The Ministry, acting on behalf of the central government agencies, has received the advance payments for disaster relief materials saved in May and June, as well as disaster relief donations from Beijing. In addition to the timely distribution of these materials to various disaster areas, the amount contributed by each unit is hereby announced. If there are any errors, please notify the Ministry immediately for correction.

June 12, 1950
