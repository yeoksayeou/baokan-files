Title: Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government Issues Order: Trade Union Law to be Promulgated and Implemented from Today
Author:
Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government issued an order on the 29th, promulgating the Trade Union Law of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the order is as follows: Order of the Central People's Government:

The Trade Union Law of the People's Republic of China, passed by the Eighth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, shall be promulgated and implemented from June 29, 1950. This order.

                Chairman Mao Zedong
              June 29, 1950

### 中央人民政府毛主席发布命令  工会法自即日起公布施行

1950-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府毛主席发布命令
    工会法自即日起公布施行
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于二十九日发布命令，公布中华人民共和国工会法。命令全文如下：中央人民政府命令：
    中央人民政府委员会第八次会议通过的中华人民共和国工会法，应自一九五○年六月二十九日起公布施行。此令
                主席  毛泽东
              一九五○年六月二十九日
