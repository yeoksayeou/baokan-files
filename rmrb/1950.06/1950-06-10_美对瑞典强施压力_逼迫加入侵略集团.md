Title: US Applies Strong Pressure on Sweden, Forcing it to Join the Aggression Bloc
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 4
　
　　US Applies Strong Pressure on Sweden
    Forcing it to Join the Aggression Bloc
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch] According to a broadcast from Moscow on the 5th: The Hague's "Truth" newspaper, citing sources in the Swedish capital's press circles, revealed that the United States is exerting pressure to force Sweden to join the North Atlantic Aggression Bloc. The US State Department recently summoned the Swedish Ambassador to the United States, Boheman, demanding that Sweden participate in the North Atlantic Treaty, and hinted to Boheman that if Sweden refused to participate, it would be subjected to suppression. The United States intends to freeze Swedish orders in the United States and restrict its trade with West Germany. According to the same source, the former US Ambassador to Sweden once declared that the United States would take all measures to shake Sweden's confidence that it could maintain neutrality in the event of an anti-Soviet war.

### 美对瑞典强施压力  逼迫加入侵略集团

1950-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　美对瑞典强施压力
    逼迫加入侵略集团
    【新华社九日讯】据莫斯科五日广播：海牙《真理报》引瑞典首都报界人士所透露的消息称：美国正在行使压力，逼迫瑞典加入北大西洋侵略集团。美国国务院最近曾召见瑞典驻美大使波曼，要求瑞典参加北大西洋公约，并向波曼暗示，如果瑞典拒绝参加，就要受到镇压。美国拟冻结瑞典在美国的定货，并限制其与西德的贸易。据同一消息透露，美国前任驻瑞典大使曾宣称：美国将采取一切措施，以动摇瑞典打算在反苏战争发生时能保持中立的信心。
