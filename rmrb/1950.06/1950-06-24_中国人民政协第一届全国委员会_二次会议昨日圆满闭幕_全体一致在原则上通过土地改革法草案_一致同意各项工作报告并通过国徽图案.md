Title: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Successfully Concluded Yesterday; Unanimously Approved the Draft Land Reform Law in Principle; Unanimously Agreed to Various Work Reports and Approved the National Emblem Design
Author:
Date: 1950-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its final meeting today. After passing various resolutions, Chairman Mao delivered the closing remarks, and the meeting was adjourned.

The meeting unanimously approved the draft of the Land Reform Law of the People's Republic of China in principle, and suggested that the Central People's Government Committee review and approve it for implementation.

The meeting also unanimously approved the reports of Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yun, Deputy Director Bo Yibo, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, Acting Chief of General Staff Nie Rongzhen, and President Shen Junru on land reform, political affairs, economic finance, taxation, culture and education, military affairs, and court work.

The meeting accepted the proposal of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace and adopted measures to call on the people of the whole country to launch a signature campaign to defend world peace, and decided to designate the week from July 1st to July 7th as the Peace Signature Campaign Week.

The meeting passed the resolution on the local committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

The meeting agreed to the report of Ma Xulun, representative of the National Emblem Review Group, on the review opinions of the national emblem design, and approved the national emblem design, and suggested that the Central People's Government Committee adopt it.

The meeting received a total of seventy-four proposals and suggestions. The meeting agreed with the review opinions of each group on these proposals and suggestions, and decided to hand them over to the Standing Committee for separate handling.

The meeting passed a message of condolence to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army. (Attached pictures)

### 中国人民政协第一届全国委员会  二次会议昨日圆满闭幕  全体一致在原则上通过土地改革法草案  一致同意各项工作报告并通过国徽图案

1950-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协第一届全国委员会
    二次会议昨日圆满闭幕
    全体一致在原则上通过土地改革法草案
    一致同意各项工作报告并通过国徽图案
    【新华社二十三日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议今天举行最后一次大会。会议在通过各项决议后，由毛主席致闭会辞，随即宣布散会。
    会议全体一致在原则上通过中华人民共和国土地改革法草案，建议中央人民政府委员会把它审核通过后颁布施行。
    会议并一致同意刘少奇副主席、周恩来总理、陈云副总理、薄一波副主任、郭沫若副总理、聂荣臻代总参谋长、沈钧儒院长关于土地改革、政治、经济财政、税收、文化教育、军事、法院工作的各项报告。
    会议接受中国保卫世界和平大会委员会的建议，通过了号召全国人民展开保卫世界和平签名运动的办法，并决定以七月一日到七月七日的一星期为和平签名运动周。
    会议通过了关于中国人民政协地方委员会的决定。
    会议同意国徽审查组代表马叙伦关于国徽图案审查意见的报告，并通过了国徽图案，建议中央人民政府委员会采用。
    会议曾收到各项提案和建议案一共七十四件。会议同意各小组对于这些提案和建议案的审查意见，决定交常务委员会分别处理。
    会议通过了致人民解放军全体指战员的慰问电。（附图片）
