Title: Defend Peace, German-Soviet Friendship; All-German Youth Sends Respects to Stalin; Stalin's Reply Sends Blessings to German Youth
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd Dispatch: The more than 500,000 participants of the All-German Youth Congress in Berlin, passed a telegram of respect to Stalin at the congress on May 28, assuring him that German youth will do their utmost to stop war, defend peace and German-Soviet friendship. TASS reported on the 2nd that Moscow newspapers published Stalin's reply to the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League as follows:

    "I express my gratitude for the telegram from the German youth peace fighters - the participants of the All-German Youth Congress. I wish the German youth - active builders of a unified, democratic and peace-loving Germany - new achievements in this great cause.
     Joseph Stalin (signed)"

### 保卫和平、德苏友好  全德青年电斯大林致敬  斯大林复电祝福德青年
约瑟夫·斯大林
1950-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    保卫和平、德苏友好
    全德青年电斯大林致敬
    斯大林复电祝福德青年
    【新华社三日讯】柏林全德青年大会五十万以上的参与者，曾在五月二十八日大会上通过向斯大林致敬的电文，向他保证：德国青年决尽最大力量制止战争，保卫和平及德苏友好。塔斯社二日电报道，莫斯科各报刊载斯大林致自由德国青年联盟中央委员会的复电如下：
    “我对于德国青年和平战士——全德青年大会的参加者——的来电表示感谢。我祝福德国青年——统一、民主与爱好和平的德国的积极建设者——在这伟大的事业上取得新的成就。
     约瑟夫·斯大林（签字）”
