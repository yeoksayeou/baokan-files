　　Brief News
    Children's Life Pictures to be Sent to Nanjing for Exhibition
    The exhibition jointly organized by the National Peking Library, the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Capital's Preparatory Committee for Celebrating the June 1st International Children's Day, closed on the 15th. This part of the materials will be sent to the Nanjing Library for exhibition within days. In addition, pictures of Soviet children's life will be moved to the Municipal First Cultural Center (Gulou) for exhibition.
                                             (Chen Zhongchi)
    Lectures Held in the Cultural Palace
    The Municipal Federation of Trade Unions originally scheduled a film performance to entertain workers at the Labor People's Cultural Palace on Sunday (the 18th) evening, but it has been temporarily canceled and changed to a lecture evening.
                                                (Cultural Palace Correspondence Group)

### 简讯

专栏：

　　简讯
    儿童生活图片将寄南京展览
    国立北京图书馆与苏联对外文化协会，首都庆祝六一国际儿童节筹委会联合举办的展览会，已于十五日闭幕。这部分资料将于日内寄往南京图书馆展览。又苏联儿童生活图片，将移到市立第一文化馆（鼓楼）展览。
                                             （陈仲篪）
    文化宫中开讲演会
    市总工会原定于星期日（十八日）晚在劳动人民文化宫举行招待工人的电影演出，临时停止，改为讲演晚会。
                                                （文化宫通讯组）
