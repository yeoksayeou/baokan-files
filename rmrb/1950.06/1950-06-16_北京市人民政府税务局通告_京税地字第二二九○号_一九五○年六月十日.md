Beijing Municipal People's Government Taxation Bureau Notice
　　Jing Shui Di Zi No. 2290 June 10, 1950

　　1. This year's summer property tax collection date has arrived. It is now decided to start collection from June 16th. It is hoped that all taxpayers will prepare early or purchase pre-paid tax rice coupons in order to pay on schedule.

　　2. On the payment notice, in addition to the listed tax amount and the additional 30% for sanitation and education, the amount converted according to the treasury's rice price on June 11th will also be listed.

　　3. Payment notices will be delivered by the post office. Whether it is the owner, landlord, current user, or manager, none may refuse to accept them or return them without reason.

　　4. Payment locations are the branches of the People's Bank of this city and private banks and money shops entrusted by the People's Bank.

　　5. If any taxpayer has any questions, they may inquire at the district tax bureau nearby.

　　6. It is hoped that all taxpayers will comply.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director: Zhao Zishang
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Director: Ma Yingzhong

### 北京市人民政府税务局通告  京税地字第二二九○号  一九五○年六月十日

1950-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府税务局通告
    京税地字第二二九○号  一九五○年六月十日
    一、本年夏季房产税已屈征收日期，兹决定自六月十六日起开征。希各纳税义务人及早准备，或购预缴税款折米券，以便如期缴纳。
    二、缴款通知书上，除照列应纳税额及附加卫生教育费百分之三十米数外，并按六月十一日金库米介折合款数一并开列。
    三、缴款通知书一律交邮局投递，无论业主、房主、现时使用人及管理人，均不得借词拒收，无故退回。
    四、缴款处所为本市人民银行各办事处及人民银行委托之私营银行钱庄。
    五、各纳税义务人如有疑问，可向住在区税务分局。就近查询。
    六、希各纳税义务人遵照为要。
                                                局长  赵子尚
                                                副局长  马应中
