Title: Relief for Explosion Victims: Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association Donates One Million Yuan
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 3

　　Relief for Explosion Victims
    Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association Donates One Million Yuan
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association's Council of Directors, yesterday (the 17th), specially dispatched Comrade Zeng Ping and two others as representatives of the Friendship Association to express their condolences to the citizens injured in the Fuhua Firecracker Factory explosion, and donated 1,000,000 yuan in Renminbi.
    The Municipal Friendship Association also made the following two decisions: (1) Members affected by the disaster will be exempt from paying membership fees for the summer, autumn, and winter seasons. (2) The amount of summer membership fees submitted by the 13th District will be retained by the Friendship Association's General Branch in that district, to be used to assist members injured in the Fuhua incident.
                                              (Chen Yingnan)

### 救济爆炸受伤者  北京中苏友协捐款一百万元
陈应楠
1950-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　救济爆炸受伤者
    北京中苏友协捐款一百万元
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会干事会，昨日（十七日）特派曾平同志等三人代表友协前往慰问因辅华火药厂爆炸而受伤的市民。并捐助人民币一百万元。
    市友协为此并作了下列二项决定：（一）受灾会员一律免缴夏、秋、冬三季会费。（二）十三区夏季会费上交之数，存留该区友协总支会，作为帮助此次因辅华事件而受伤的会员之用。
                                              （陈应楠）
