Introduction to Yu Zhenshan's Slide Rule Algorithm (4)
  II. Subtraction
    Minuend - Subtrahend = Difference
    Using the rule: Use two rules with the same scale; one is called the "Heaven Rule" and the other is called the "Earth Rule."
    Method:
    (1) Align the subtrahend on the Heaven Rule with the minuend on the Earth Rule.
    (2) The number on the Earth Rule opposite the 0 on the Heaven Rule is the difference.
    [Example] A comrade has 70 yuan in RMB and spends 30 yuan. How much is left?
    Method: As shown in Figure 7.
    (Attached Figure [Figure])
    (1) Align 3 (representing 30 yuan) on the Heaven Rule with 7 (representing 70 yuan) on the Earth Rule.
    (2) The 0 on the Heaven Rule is aligned with 4 on the Earth Rule, which is the difference.
    The answer is: 40 yuan are left.
    (Note) 1, 2, 4, 5, ... on the Heaven Rule are aligned with 5, 6, 8, 9, ... on the Earth Rule, representing 5-1, 6-2, 8-4, 9-5..., and their difference is always 4.
  III. Combined Subtraction and Addition Algorithm
    Number A - Number B + Number C = Total
    Using the rule: Use two rules with the same scale; one is called the "Heaven Rule" and the other is called the "Earth Rule."
    Method:
    (1) Align Number B on the Heaven Rule with Number A on the Earth Rule.
    (2) The number on the Earth Rule opposite Number C on the Heaven Rule is the total.
    [Example]: A comrade has 150 yuan, spends 90 yuan on shoes, and then receives 70 yuan. How much does he have in total?
    Method: As shown in Figure 8.
    (Attached Figure [Figure])
    ① Align 9 (representing 90 yuan) on the Heaven Rule with 15 (representing 150 yuan) on the Earth Rule.
    ② The 7 (representing 70 yuan) on the Heaven Rule is aligned with 13 on the Earth Rule, which is the total.
    The answer is: 130 yuan in total.
    (Note) 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, ... on the Heaven Rule are aligned with 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, ... on the Earth Rule, representing 15-9+1, 15-9+2, 15-9+3, 15-9+4, 15-9+5, ..., the resulting totals. No matter what Number C is, it can be calculated in one step.

### 于振善尺算法介绍（四）

1950-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　于振善尺算法介绍（四）
  二、减法
    被减数—减数＝差
    用尺：用两根刻度相同的尺子；一叫天尺，一叫地尺。
    求法：
    （１）把天尺上的减数对准地尺上的被减数。
    （２）天尺上的０所对着的地尺上的数即是所求的差。
    【例题】某同志有人民币７０元，用去３０元，问还剩多少元？
    求法：如第七图所示。
    （附图  [图]）
    （１）把天尺上的３（代表３０元）对准地尺上的７（代表７０元）。
    （２）天尺上的０对准地尺上的４即是所求的差。
    答案是：还剩４０元。
    （注）天尺上的１，２，４，５，……分别对着地尺上的５，６，８，９，……，就是代表５—１，６—２，８—４，９—５……，它们的差数都是４。
  三、减加连算法
    甲数—乙数＋丙数＝总数
    用尺：用两根刻度相同的尺子，一叫天尺，一叫地尺。
    求法：
    （１）把天尺上的乙数对准地尺上的甲数。
    （２）天尺上的丙数所对着的地尺上的数，即是所求的总数。
    〔例题〕：某同志有１５０元，买鞋用去９０元，又收入７０元，问共有多少元？
    求法：如第八图所示。
    （附图  [图]）
    ①把天尺上的９（代表９０元）对准地尺上的１５（代表１５０元）。
    ②天尺上的７（代表７０元）所对着的地尺上的１３，即是所求的总数。
    答案是共有１３０元。
    （注）天尺上的１，２，３，４，５，……，所对着的地尺上的７，８，９，１０，１１，……，就是代表１５—９＋１，１５—９＋２，１５—９＋３，１５—９＋４，１５—９＋５，……所得的总数。无论丙数是什么数目均可由此一次求出。
