Title: Singapore British Authorities Arrest Overseas Chinese Students
Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st—According to Reuters Singapore: The British colonial authorities in Singapore dispatched hundreds of police on the morning of the 30th to conduct a large-scale search of the Singapore Overseas Chinese High School, and "detained" nineteen students and one teacher. The search lasted four hours.

### 新加坡英当局搜捕华侨学生

1950-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　新加坡英当局搜捕华侨学生
    【新华社一日讯】据路透社新加坡讯：新加坡英国殖民当局于三十日晨出动数百名警察，对新加坡华侨高级学校大肆搜查，并“扣留”十九名学生和一名教师。搜查历四小时方止。
