Introduction to Yu Zhenshan's Slide Rule Algorithm (8)
    Example: Eighty yuan is divided among four people. How much does each person get?
    Solution: As shown in Figures 13, 14, and 15.
    (1) Align the 8 on the upper scale (representing 80 yuan) with the 4 on the lower scale (representing 4 people).
    (2) The 1 on the lower scale (representing one person) corresponds to the 2 on the upper scale, which is the quotient we are looking for.
    (3) Determine the place value: Since 2 is less than 8, the number of digits in the quotient = 2-1+1=2. Therefore, we find: Each person should get 20 yuan.
    [Note] The 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, ... on the upper scale correspond to 5, 4.5, 4, 3.5, 3, 2.5, ... on the lower scale respectively. And the 1 on the lower scale corresponds to the 2 on the upper scale, which represents 10÷5, 9÷4.5, 8÷4, 7÷3.5, 6÷3, 5÷2.5, ... Their quotients are all 2. (Attached images)

### 于振善尺算法介绍（八）

1950-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    于振善尺算法介绍（八）
    例题：四人分钱八十元，每人可得多少？
    求法：如第十三图，第十四图，第十五图所示。
    （1）把天尺的8（代表80元）和地尺的4（代表4人）对准。
    （2）地尺上的1（代表一个人）所对着的天尺上的2即是所求的商数。
    （3）定们：因2小于8所以商数的位数=2-1+1=2所以求得：每人应分得20元
    【注】天尺上的10，9，8，7，6，5，……分别对着地尺上的5，4，5，4，3，5，3，2，5……而地尺上的1对着天尺上的2，就是代表10÷5，9÷4.5，8÷4，7÷3.5，6÷3，5÷2.5，……它们的商数全是2。（附图片）
