Title: Biography of Mr. Amason
Author:
Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: Biography of the Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Amason:
　　
Mr. Amason was born on June 20, 1896. After graduating from university, he served as an attaché in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in 1921. In 1922, he served as an attaché at the Swedish Embassy in Germany. In 1923, he served as the Third Vice Consul at the Consulate General in Chicago. In 1927, he served as an attaché at the Swedish Embassy in Finland. From 1929 to 1931, he served as Second Secretary at the Swedish Embassies in Belgium and the Netherlands. In 1930, he served as the secretary of the Swedish delegation to the Hague Conference on the Codification of International Law. From 1931 to 1932, he served as First Secretary in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In 1935, he served as First Secretary at the Swedish Embassy in Germany. In 1936, he served as Chief of the Legal and Political Affairs Division of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In 1939, he served as Chief of the European Section of the Trade Department. In 1939, he served as a representative to the Stockholm Nordic Business Conference. In the same year, he served as a specialist member of the Swedish-German Business Conference. In 1940, he served as Counselor at the Swedish Embassy in Italy. From 1940 to 1945, he served as a representative to the Swedish Business Conference in Rome. In 1945, he served as the Swedish Minister to Czechoslovakia. In 1947, he served as the Swedish Ambassador to China. In 1948, he concurrently served as the Swedish Minister to the Philippines and Thailand.

### 阿马斯顿传略

1950-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿马斯顿传略
    【新华社十二日讯】瑞典驻我国大使阿马斯顿传略
    阿马斯顿生于一八九六年六月二十日。大学毕业后于一九二一年任外交部随员。一九二二年任驻德使馆随员。一九二三年任驻芝加哥领事馆第三副领事。一九二七年任驻芬使馆随员。一九二九年至一九三一年任驻比及驻荷使馆二等秘书。一九三○年任海牙国际公法编纂会议瑞典代表团秘书。一九三一年至一九三二年任外交部一等秘书。一九三五年任驻德使馆一等秘书。一九三六年任外交部法政司科长。一九三九年任商业司欧洲科长。一九三九年任斯德哥尔摩北欧商务会议代表。同年任瑞、德商务会议专门委员。一九四○年任驻义使馆参事。一九四○年至一九四五年任罗马瑞典商务会议代表。一九四五年任驻捷克公使。一九四七年任驻华大使、一九四八年兼任驻菲及驻泰公使。
                                            【新华社】
