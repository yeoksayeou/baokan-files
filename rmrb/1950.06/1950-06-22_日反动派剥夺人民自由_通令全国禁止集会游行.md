Title: Japanese Reactionaries Deprive People of Freedom, Issue Order Banning Assemblies and Demonstrations Nationwide
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th (via TASS): According to the Asahi Shimbun, Japanese police authorities have issued an order nationwide banning assemblies and demonstrations. This decision was taken by the Japanese government following instructions from MacArthur Headquarters.

　　Recently, police searched the Osaka and Kyoto branches of the Electrical Workers' Union and the Japanese Communist Party branch at Osaka University.

### 日反动派剥夺人民自由  通令全国禁止集会游行

1950-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　日反动派剥夺人民自由  通令全国禁止集会游行
    【新华社讯】塔斯社上海二十日电：据朝日新闻报道：日本警察当局通令全国禁止集会游行。此项决定系日本政府奉麦克阿瑟总部的训令而采取的。
    最近警察曾经搜查大阪与京都两地的电气工人工会的分会和大阪大学的日本共产党支部。
