Title: Commemorating the Ninth Anniversary of the Soviet Union's War of Resistance Against Germany
Author: Weinokurov
Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 6

    Weinokurov Gives a Lecture on Commemorating the Ninth Anniversary of the Soviet Union's War of Resistance Against Germany
    To commemorate the great festival of the ninth anniversary of the Soviet Union's War of Resistance Against Germany on June 22, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Soviet Foreign Culture Association are scheduled to jointly hold a commemorative meeting at 8:00 PM today (June 22) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Weinokurov will lecture on "The Great Patriotic War." Those with admission tickets or long-term "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture passes can attend. (Chen Yingnan)

### 纪念苏联抗德战争九周年
维诺库罗夫
1950-06-22
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念苏联抗德战争九周年
    维诺库罗夫讲演
    六月二十二日为苏联抗德战争九周年纪念日。北京市中苏友好协会与北京苏联对外文化协会为纪念这个伟大的节日，定于今日（二十二日）下午八时在南河沿十九号联合举行纪念会，由维诺库罗夫讲“伟大的卫国战争”。持入场券或“苏联介绍”长期听讲证，可往参加。（陈应楠）
