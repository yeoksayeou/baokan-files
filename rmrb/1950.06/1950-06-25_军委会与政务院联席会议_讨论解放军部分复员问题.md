Title: Joint Meeting of the Military Commission and the Government Administration Council Discusses the Partial Demobilization of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission and the Government Administration Council held a joint meeting at 3:00 PM on the 24th to discuss the partial demobilization of the People's Liberation Army in 1950, and passed a resolution on this work.

### 军委会与政务院联席会议  讨论解放军部分复员问题

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　军委会与政务院联席会议
    讨论解放军部分复员问题
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会与政务院，于二十四日下午三时举行联席会议，讨论了人民解放军于一九五零年进行一部分复员工作的问题，通过了关于此一工作的决定。
