Title: After Visiting the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe, Our Trade Union Delegation Returned Home and Arrived in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1

　　After Visiting the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe
　　Our Trade Union Delegation Returned Home and Arrived in Shenyang
　　[This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang: The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, after visiting the Soviet Union and various new democratic countries in Eastern Europe, has returned home and arrived in Shenyang. The delegation will report on their overseas visit to workers in Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, and other places.

### 访问苏联东欧后  我工会代表团返国抵达沈阳

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　访问苏联东欧后
    我工会代表团返国抵达沈阳
    【本报讯】据新华社沈阳电：访问苏联及东欧各新民主国家的中国工会代表团，已返国抵沈。代表团将分别在沈阳、鞍山、抚顺等地向工人报告出国访问情况。
