Title: Nearly Ten Million Sign for Peace in Romania; Over Half of the Mongolian People Participate in the Signing; Peace Signature Campaign in France Proceeding Enthusiastically
Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 5th dispatch from Bucharest via TASS: Nearly ten million people in Romania have signed the World Peace Congress Standing Committee declaration, accounting for nearly two-thirds of Romania's total population of sixteen million. The signature campaign is still ongoing.
　　Of the 1.2 million people in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, over 760,000 have signed.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 5th dispatch from Ulan Bator via TASS: As of the 2nd, 550,000 people in the Mongolian People's Republic have signed the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's peace declaration (Note: The population of the Mongolian People's Republic is approximately one million). The total number of local peace committees in the Mongolian People's Republic has now reached 267.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 5th dispatch consolidating TASS news: The peace signature campaign among the French people is proceeding enthusiastically, with over seven million people nationwide having participated in the signing thus far. The signature collection campaign has achieved great success in the Seine province; the number of signatures in several towns in the province has exceeded two-thirds of the total population. For example, out of 42,000 residents in Ivry, 37,000 have signed; out of 25,000 residents in Gennevilliers, 20,000 have signed; and out of 45,000 residents in Saint-Ouen, 30,000 have signed.
　　The Seine province has collected the signatures of 2,000 physicians and medical students. Many members of the Socialist Party in the Seine province, as well as the secretary of the party in the Aveyron province, have also signed the peace declaration.
　　Students from various universities in Paris have not only signed the peace declaration themselves but have also actively collected signatures from residents in the Latin Quarter. They have visited homes and sought signatures from those strolling in the parks. According to incomplete statistics, they have collected over 43,000 signatures in the Latin Quarter.

### 罗和平签名者近千万  蒙古过半数人民参加签名  法国和平签名运动热烈进行

1950-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗和平签名者近千万
    蒙古过半数人民参加签名
    法国和平签名运动热烈进行
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特三日讯：罗马尼亚人民在世界和大常委会宣言上签名的，迄今已近一千万人，几占罗马尼亚总人口一千六百万人的三分之二。签名运动现仍在继续中。
    在罗京布加勒斯特一百二十万人中，签名者已达七十六万以上。
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔讯：截至二日为止，蒙古人民共和国已有五十五万人在世界拥护和平大会常委会和平宣言上签名（按：蒙古人民共和国人口约一百万）。蒙古人民共和国各地方和平委员会的总数，现在已达二百六十七个。
    【新华社五日讯】综合塔斯社消息：法国人民的和平签名运动正在热烈进行中，全国迄今已有七百万以上的人民参加了签名。征集签名运动在塞纳省获得了很大成就；该省若干市镇的签名人数，已超过居民总数的三分之二，例如伊维的四万二千居民中，已有三万七千人签名，根尼维利埃的二万五千居民中，已有二万人签名，圣图昂的四万五千居民中，已有三万人签名。
    塞纳省已征集到两千名医师和医科学生的签名。塞纳省的许多社会党员以及该党阿维隆省的书记，也在和平宣言上签了名。
    巴黎各高等学校的学生，不仅自己在和平宣言上签了名，而且积极到拉丁区居民中征集签名。他们挨户访问，并向那些在公园中散步的人们征求签名。根据不完全统计，他们已在拉丁区征得了四万三千余人的签名。
