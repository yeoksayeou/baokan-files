Title: Cultivating Youth to Become Assistants of the Workers' Party: Hungarian Labor Youth League Established; Membership Now Exceeds 500,000

Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 4

　　Cultivating Youth to Become Assistants of the Workers' Party
　　Hungarian Labor Youth League Established; Membership Now Exceeds 500,000
　　[Xinhua News Agency, June 26th dispatch from Budapest] A comprehensive report from TASS, Budapest: The Hungarian Labor Youth League held its founding congress in Budapest, the Hungarian capital, from the 17th to the 19th. 559 delegates attended, representing 2,550 youth organizations across the country. Youth delegations from the Soviet Union, China, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, and other foreign countries came to participate.
　　Suz, Chairman of the Hungarian People's Youth League, reported on the tasks of the Labor Youth League at the meeting, stating: The Labor Youth League is a non-party mass organization that unites the broad masses of youth. The League should be built on the basis of Marxism-Leninism, cultivating Hungarian youth to become loyal assistants of the Hungarian Workers' Party and vanguards of socialist construction.
　　Suz announced: After absorbing 150,000 new members, the Labor Youth League's membership has exceeded 500,000.
　　Based on Suz's report, the congress unanimously passed a resolution, defining the tasks of the Labor Youth League, and elected 75 members and 20 alternate members of the Central Committee.
　　On the afternoon of the 18th, over 100,000 young workers, farmers, students, soldiers, and intellectuals in Budapest held a grand rally in Kossuth Square to warmly celebrate the establishment of the Labor Youth League.

### 培养青年成为工人党的助手  匈成立劳动青年联盟  盟员现已逾五十万人

1950-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　培养青年成为工人党的助手
    匈成立劳动青年联盟  盟员现已逾五十万人
    【新华社二十六日讯】综合塔斯社布达佩斯消息：匈牙利劳动青年联盟于十七日至十九日在匈京布达佩斯举行成立大会。出席代表五五九名，代表全国各地的二五五○个青年组织。苏联、中国、德意志民主共和国、捷克和其他外国的青年代表团均前来参加。
    匈牙利青年人民联盟主席苏兹在会上报告劳动青年联盟的任务称：劳动青年联盟是一个团结广大青年的非党的群众组织。联盟应建立在马列主义的基础上，培养匈牙利青年成为匈牙利工人党的忠实助手，成为社会主义建设的先锋队。
    苏兹宣布：在吸收了十五万新盟员之后，劳动青年联盟的盟员已超过五十万人。
    大会根据苏兹的报告，一致通过决议，确定了劳动青年联盟的任务，并选出了中央委员会的七十五名委员和二十名候补委员。
    布达佩斯的十余万青年工人、农民、学生，士兵和知识分子，于十八日下午在科苏特广场举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝劳动青年联盟的成立。
