Title: "U.S. News & World Report" Admits Eurasian People Hate War, Anti-American Sentiment is Rising

Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 6th (TASS): The American magazine "U.S. News & World Report" recently published an article admitting that anti-American sentiment is growing among the people of Europe and Asia. They believe that American policy is preparing for a new war. The article admits: "American ideas are encountering increasing opposition. American power and actions are causing rising anger."

The article further admits that the American policy of "total cold war" is unpopular because people fear that this policy will lead to a real war. The article also admits: "In Europe or Asia, ordinary people hate war. They are unwilling to participate in war."

The article concludes: "It is time to end the American occupation; the Japanese want a peace treaty and not American bases." - This is the opinion expressed by the Japanese people.

### “美国新闻与世界报道”承认  欧亚人民痛恨战争  反美情绪日益高涨

1950-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　“美国新闻与世界报道”承认
    欧亚人民痛恨战争  反美情绪日益高涨
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社纽约六日讯：美国杂志《美国新闻与世界报道》最近刊载一文，承认欧洲及亚洲人民中的反美情绪正在增长中。他们相信：美国的政策是准备新战争。该文承认：“美国的思想正遭遇日益增长的反对。美国的势力及行为引起日益高涨的愤怒”。
    该文进一步承认：美国的“总体冷战”政策不受欢迎，因为人民害怕这一政策将导致真的战争。该文又承认：“在欧洲或在亚洲，老百姓皆痛恨战争。他们不愿参加战争”。
    该文最后说：“是结束美国占领的时候了，日本人希望有和约，而不要美国基地。”——这就是日本人所表示的意见。
