Title: This Newspaper Has Received Peace Signatures from Over 440,000 People
Author:
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 4

　　This Newspaper Has Received Peace Signatures from Over 440,000 People

[Report from this newspaper] The peace signature campaign launched by this newspaper has received a total of 147,303 peace signatures from various regions in the past three days (from the 1st to the 3rd of this month). This includes over 300 organizations such as the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the People's Public Security Central Column, directly subordinate units of the North China Military Region, ethnic minorities in the capital (including mosques, Lama temples, Hui schools, and various industries, totaling 14,558 people), and the Beijing Private Qiming School for the Blind (all signed with needle pricks). There were also letters from Guangzhou, Guilin, Hankou, Shashi, and other cities, as well as from various groups and individuals in Hunan, Guangxi, and other provinces. For example, over 5,000 farmers, local cadres, and school teachers and students in Anxiang County, Hunan Province, jointly signed. In Mancheng County, Hebei Province, 1,462 people also signed. The Baoding Hebei Daily also forwarded 60,778 signatures from various parts of Hebei Province. As of yesterday, the total number of signatures received by this newspaper has reached 440,273.

### 本报收到和平签名已达四十四万余人

1950-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　本报收到和平签名已达四十四万余人
    【本报讯】本报发动的和平签名运动，最近（本月一日至三日）三日内收到各地和平签名共十四万七千三百零三人，内有人民革命军事委员会总政治部、人民公安中央纵队、华北军区直属各单位、首都少数民族（各清真寺、喇嘛庙、回民学校及各行业共一四、五五八人）、北京市私立启明瞽目院（均以针刺签名）等三百多个团体，并有广州、桂林、汉口、沙市等城市及湖南、广西等省各地团体及个人远地来书。如湖南安乡县农民、地方干部及学校师生五千余人联合签名。河北省满城县亦有一千四百六十二人签名。保定河北日报并转来河北省各地六万零七千七百八十人签名。截至昨日止，本报收到签名总数共达四十四万零二百七十三人。
