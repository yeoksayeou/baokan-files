Title: Summer Harvest Begins in Southern Regions of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st dispatch from TASS: Harvesting has begun in most of the southern regions of the Soviet Union. Following Turkmenistan, collective farms in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, and Azerbaijan have begun harvesting barley. Azerbaijan will begin harvesting wheat in a few days. In the Kuban, Stavropol Territory, Crimea, and southern Ukraine, the grains have ripened.

　　This year's barley harvest is abundant, and collective farmers and workers at machine tractor stations have launched a large-scale socialist competition for harvesting.

### 苏联南部地区开始进行夏收

1950-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联南部地区开始进行夏收
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联南部大部分地区现已开始进行收获。继土库曼之后，乌兹贝克、塔吉克和阿塞拜疆的集体农场都已开始收割大麦。阿塞拜疆将在数日内开始收割小麦。在库班、斯塔夫罗波尔边区、克里米亚和乌克兰南部，谷物已经成熟了。
    今年大麦收成丰富，集体农民和机器拖拉机站的工人们，已展开大规模的社会主义竞赛，进行收割。
