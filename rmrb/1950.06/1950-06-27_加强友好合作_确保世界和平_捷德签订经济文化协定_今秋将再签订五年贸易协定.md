Title: Strengthen Friendly Cooperation · Ensure World Peace - Czechoslovakia and Germany Sign Economic and Cultural Agreement - Five-Year Trade Agreement to be Signed Again This Autumn
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th dispatch from Prague: The delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and representatives of the Government of Czechoslovakia held negotiations in Prague from June 21st to 23rd regarding ensuring peace and strengthening friendly cooperation between the two countries. The negotiations were conducted in an extremely friendly atmosphere.

The two delegations issued a joint statement, declaring that there were no disputes or outstanding issues between the two countries. They also believed that the executed transfer of German citizens from Czechoslovakia was a fair and final decision, and did not need to be changed.

The negotiating parties have signed the following agreements: (1) Agreement on granting short-term credit loans to the German Democratic Republic. The loan will be used by Germany to pay for the special goods supplied by Czechoslovakia in addition to the existing trade agreement. (2) Agreement on scientific and technological cooperation. (3) Protocol on cultural cooperation. (4) Protocol on financial settlement related to non-trade business. Both parties also reached an agreement stipulating that the issue of transshipment of goods via the Czechoslovak-German border would be resolved within two months.

After exchanging information on the two countries' five-year plans, the negotiating parties have agreed to sign a five-year trade agreement this autumn.

### 加强友好合作·确保世界和平  捷德签订经济文化协定  今秋将再签订五年贸易协定

1950-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　加强友好合作·确保世界和平
    捷德签订经济文化协定
    今秋将再签订五年贸易协定
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：德意志民主共和国政府代表团和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表，于二十一日至二十三日在布拉格举行关于确保和平和加强两国友好合作的谈判。谈判系在极为友好的空气中进行的。
    两国代表团发表了联合声明，宣称两国之间并无任何争议或悬而未决的问题。并且认为已执行的自捷克斯洛伐克移送德国公民的工作，是公正的和最后的决定，无须予以改变。
    谈判双方已签订了下列各项协定：（１）关于给予德意志民主共和国短期信用贷款的协定。贷款将供德国偿付捷克斯洛伐克在现有的贸易协定之外所供给的特种货物的货价。（２）关于科学和技术的合作协定。（３）关于文化合作的议定书。（４）关于与非贸易性业务有关的财务清算的议定书。双方同时达成协议，规定经由捷德边界转运货物的问题将在两个月内予以解决。
    谈判双方于交换了两国五年计划的情报之后，已同意在今年秋季签订一项为期五年的贸易协定。
