Title: South Korean Newly Liberated Areas to Implement Land Reform; Various Restoration Works Begin
Author:
Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 28th - According to the Korean Central News Agency, Pyongyang, June 28th: The newly liberated areas of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea south of the 38th parallel, including Ongjin, YeonAn and YeonBaek, under the leadership of their own people's power organs—the Provisional People's Committee—have begun various restoration and reform works. The broad masses of peasants are now investigating the land of pro-Japanese traitors and national betrayers, preparing to implement land reform of uncompensated confiscation and uncompensated distribution. To comfort and encourage the people in the newly liberated areas, the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has organized a 600-person cultural propaganda work team, carrying a large number of progressive publications and films, which departed on the 27th to Ongjin, YeonAn, Baekcheon, Gaeseong and other places to carry out comfort and propaganda work.

### 南朝鲜新解放区  准备实行土地改革  各种恢复工作开始进行

1950-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜新解放区
    准备实行土地改革
    各种恢复工作开始进行
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社平壤二十八日报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国三八线以南的瓮津、延安、延白等新解放区，在人民自己的政权机关——临时人民委员会的领导下，已开始进行各种恢复和改革工作。广大的农民，现正着手调查亲日派卖国贼与民族叛徒的土地，准备实施无偿没收、无偿分配的土地改革。为慰问并鼓励新解放区的人民，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府已组织了六百人的文化宣传工作队，携带大批进步出版物与电影片，于二十七日出发赴瓮津、延安、白川、开城等地进行慰问与宣传工作。
