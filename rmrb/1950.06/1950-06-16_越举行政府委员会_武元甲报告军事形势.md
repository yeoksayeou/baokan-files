Title: Vietnam Holds Government Committee Meeting; Vo Nguyen Giap Reports on Military Situation
Author:
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 13th: According to the Electric News Agency, in mid-May, the Government Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held an important meeting chaired by Ho Chi Minh. During the meeting, Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Liberation Army, reported on the military situation, emphasizing that American imperialists are now openly interfering in Vietnam's war of liberation, and the Vietnamese people are contributing all manpower, materials, and financial resources for an early general counter-offensive against the French army. He continued: In the area from Lao Cai and Ha Giang in northern Vietnam to Quang Binh, Quang Tri, and Thua Thien provinces in central Vietnam, the Liberation Army is launching offensives against the French army. The Cambodian Liberation Army is also constantly attacking the French army, liberating large areas of land equivalent to those in southern Vietnam.
    During this meeting, the Government Committee also passed a number of democratic reform bills aimed at coordinating with the general counter-offensive mission.

### 越举行政府委员会  武元甲报告军事形势

1950-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　越举行政府委员会
    武元甲报告军事形势
    【新华社讯】据电通社仰光十三日讯：五月中旬，越南民主共和国政府委员会在胡志明主持下曾举行重要会议。会中，越南民主共和国国防部长兼越南人民解放军总司令武元甲在报告军事形势时强调指出：美国帝国主义者目前公开干涉越南的解放战争，越南人民正为早日对法军发动总反攻而贡献出一切人力、物力与财力。他继称：在北起北越的老街、河江，南至中越的广平、广治、承天府的地区，解放军正向法军采取攻势。柬埔寨解放军也在不断地向法军进攻，解放了相当于南越的大片土地。
    政府委员会在此次会议中，并通过旨在配合总反攻任务的民主改革法案多项。
