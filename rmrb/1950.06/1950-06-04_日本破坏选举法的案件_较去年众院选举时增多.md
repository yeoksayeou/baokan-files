Title: Cases of Election Law Violations in Japan Increase Compared to Last Year's House of Representatives Election
Author:
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd dispatch from TASS on June 2nd: According to a report from the Central Election Commission for the Japanese Parliamentary Upper House Election in Tokyo, as of May 25th, 578 cases of election law violations have occurred, and the number of people violating election laws exceeds five and a half times that of last year's parliamentary lower house election. Eighty-three people were arrested for bribing voters, and two hundred and eighty-three were arrested for visiting voters' homes.

### 日本破坏选举法的案件  较去年众院选举时增多

1950-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本破坏选举法的案件  较去年众院选举时增多
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社二日讯：据东京日本议会参院选举中央选举委员会的报告称：截至五月二十五日为止，已有五百七十八件破坏选举法的案件发生，破坏选举法的人数超过去年议会众院选举时的五倍半。八十三人因贿赂选民而被捕，二百八十三人因至选民家庭访问而被捕。
