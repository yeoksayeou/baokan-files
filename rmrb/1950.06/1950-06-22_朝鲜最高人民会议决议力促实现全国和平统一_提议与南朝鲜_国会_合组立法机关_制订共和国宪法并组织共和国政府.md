Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Resolution Strives to Achieve Peaceful National Reunification, Proposes Forming a Legislative Body with South Korean "National Assembly," Drafting a Republic Constitution and Organizing a Republic Government
Author:
Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 21st. The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a meeting on the 19th to discuss the issue of promoting peaceful national reunification and unanimously adopted an important decision.

The decision points out: The Syngman Rhee clique strictly prohibits representatives of various democratic parties and social organizations in South Korea from participating in the North-South Consultative Conference, attempting to once again undermine the realization of peaceful reunification. This once again demonstrates the Syngman Rhee clique's betrayal of the interests of the Korean people and is being carried out under the direction of American imperialists.

The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, representing the will of the Korean people to unite, gain independence, and achieve democratic development in their homeland, and based on the wishes of various democratic parties and social organizations, has decided to propose the following to the South Korean "National Assembly":

　　I. The Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the South Korean "National Assembly" jointly establish a single, all-Korean legislative body to achieve the peaceful reunification of the homeland.

　　II. The all-Korean legislative body established in the aforementioned manner shall draft a Republic Constitution and organize a Republic government.

　　III. On the basis of the Republic Constitution, hold general elections for the all-Korean legislative body.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议决议力促实现全国和平统一  提议与南朝鲜“国会”合组立法机关  制订共和国宪法并组织共和国政府

1950-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜最高人民会议决议力促实现全国和平统一
    提议与南朝鲜“国会”合组立法机关
    制订共和国宪法并组织共和国政府
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会于十九日举行会议，讨论促进国家和平统一问题，并一致通过重要决定。
    决定指出：李承晚匪帮严禁南朝鲜各民主政党与社会团体的代表参加南北协商会议，妄图再度破坏和平统一的实现，这又一次表明了李承晚匪帮背叛朝鲜人民利益的行为，并且是在美帝国主义者的指使之下进行的。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，代表朝鲜人民要求祖国统一独立与民主发展的意志，并根据各民主政党、社会团体的愿望，决定向南朝鲜“国会”提议如下：
    一、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议与南朝鲜“国会”联合成立单一的全朝鲜立法机关，以实现祖国的和平统一。
    二、以上述方法成立的全朝鲜立法机关，应制订共和国宪法并组织共和国政府。
    三、在共和国宪法的基础上，举行全朝鲜立法机关的总选举。
