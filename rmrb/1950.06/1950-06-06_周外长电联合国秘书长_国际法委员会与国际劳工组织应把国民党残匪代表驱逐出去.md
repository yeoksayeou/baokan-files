Title: Zhou Enlai Cables UN Secretary-General: The International Law Commission and the International Labour Organization Should Expel the Representatives of the Kuomintang Remnant Bandits

Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai cabled UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie on June 5th, and requested that he forward it to Mr. Hudson, Chairman of the International Law Commission, Mr. Koletski, First Vice-Chairman, and Sir Benegal Narsing Rau, Second Vice-Chairman, notifying them that the so-called "commissioners" of the remnant group of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries are completely unqualified to participate in the United Nations International Law Commission and must be expelled from the commission and its various meetings (including the second session to be held in Geneva on June 5th this year). In the cable, Foreign Minister Zhou requested them to reply and to inform all relevant parties.

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent cables of the same content to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie and Mr. Morse, Director-General of the International Labour Organization, on June 5th, notifying them that the so-called "representatives" of the remnant group of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries are now completely unqualified to participate in the International Labour Organization and must be expelled from the various institutions and meetings of the International Labour Organization (including the 33rd International Labour Conference to be held in Geneva on June 7th this year). In the cable, Foreign Minister Zhou requested them to reply and to inform all relevant parties.

### 周外长电联合国秘书长  国际法委员会与国际劳工组织应把国民党残匪代表驱逐出去

1950-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长电联合国秘书长
    国际法委员会与国际劳工组织应把国民党残匪代表驱逐出去
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于六月五日致电联合国秘书长赖伊，并请其转达国际法委员会主席赫德逊先生，第一副主席柯莱茨基先生及第二副主席饶爵士，通知他们：中国国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“委员”完全没有参加联合国国际法委员会的资格，必须将其从该委员会及其各项会议（包括今年六月五日在日内瓦召开的第二届会议）中驱逐出去。周外长在电文中要求他们答复，并转知有关各方。
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于六月五日以同样内容的电文分别致电联合国秘书长赖伊先生及国际劳工组织总干事摩斯先生，通知他们说：中国国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“代表”现已完全没有参加国际劳工组织的资格，必须将其从国际劳工组织的各项机构和会议（包括今年六月七日在日内瓦召开的第三十三届国际劳工大会）中驱逐出去。周外长在电文中要求他们答复，并转知有关各方。
