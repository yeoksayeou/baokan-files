Title: Locomotive Flag "Mao Zedong" Completes Demonstration Task Victoriously, Returns to Beijing from Zhengzhou Laden with Honors
Author: Liu Hongchen
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 2

　　Locomotive Flag "Mao Zedong"
    Victory in Completing Demonstration Task, Returns to Beijing from Zhengzhou Laden with Honors

    [This Dispatch from Zhengzhou] The national railway locomotive banner – the “Mao Zedong” locomotive – went to the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau in November of last year to carry out demonstration work. It has now victoriously completed its mission and returned to Beijing on May 31st, laden with honors. To thank the "Mao Zedong" locomotive for its help in Zhengzhou's locomotive work, the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau held a grand and enthusiastic farewell party on June 30th. The "Mao Zedong" locomotive crew and various units of the Zhengzhou Locomotive Depot exchanged flags at the meeting. Wu Shien, Director of the Zhengzhou Bureau, and others attended and delivered speeches. Those present unanimously expressed that they will continue to humbly learn from the "Mao Zedong" locomotive, promote the responsibility system with a high degree of enthusiasm, launch a model locomotive team movement, and implement the spirit of the "Mao Zedong" locomotive into every crew member so that the locomotives of the Zhengzhou Bureau, under the leadership of the "Mao Zedong" locomotive, will continue to move forward.
    (Liu Hongchen)

### 机车旗帜“毛泽东号”  胜利完成示范任务  满载荣誉自郑返京
刘洪晨
1950-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　机车旗帜“毛泽东号”
    胜利完成示范任务  满载荣誉自郑返京
    【本报郑州讯】全国铁路机车的旗帜——“毛泽东号”，自去年十一月赴郑州铁路局进行示范工作，现已胜利完成任务，于五月三十一日，满载荣誉，返回北京。为感谢“毛泽东号”对郑州机务工作的帮助，郑州路局特于六月三十日，举行隆重而热烈的欢送晚会。“毛泽东号”机车乘务员与郑州机务段各单位，在会上互相献旗。郑州局局长吴士恩等均亲临讲话。到会者一致表示：今后仍然要虚心的向“毛泽东号”学习，以高度的积极性来推行负责制，开展模范机车队运动，把“毛泽东号”的精神，贯彻到每一个乘务员中，使郑州局的机车，在“毛泽东号”的领导下，继续前进。
    （刘洪晨）
