Title: Korean Republic Army Switches to Counter-Offensive; Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Issues Emergency Mobilization Order; Kim Il-sung, Park Hon-yong, et al., Appointed to Organize Military Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 1

　　The Korean Republic Army Switches to Counter-Offensive
    Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Issues Emergency Mobilization Order
    Kim Il-sung, Park Hon-yong, et al., Appointed to Organize Military Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th] The Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a communique at 6:00 PM on the 25th stating: The guard units of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, after being unexpectedly attacked by the South Korean puppet national defense forces at dawn on the 25th, launched a tenacious defensive battle. In coordinated operations with the Korean People's Army, the guard units quickly defeated the enemy's offensive and switched to a counter-offensive. They have now advanced five to ten kilometers south of the 38th parallel in many areas. The war is currently continuing.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th] The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 26th stating: Due to the puppet "National Defense Army" of the Syngman Rhee puppet government launching a full-scale offensive north of the 38th parallel, a very tense situation has been created in the country. In order to urgently mobilize the strength of the entire nation to eliminate the Syngman Rhee bandit gang that launched the civil war, the following decisions are made: (1) Kim Il-sung, Park Hon-yong, Hong Myung-hee, Kim Chaek, Choi Yong-kun, Park Il-u, and Jung Joon-taek are appointed to organize a military committee, with Kim Il-sung as the chairman of the military committee. (2) All domestic power is concentrated in the military committee. (3) All citizens, all levels of state power organs, political parties, social organizations, and military organs must absolutely obey the orders and instructions of the military committee.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th] The Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea convened an emergency meeting on the 25th to discuss issues related to the tense situation caused by the South Korean puppet government's puppet national defense forces launching an offensive north of the 38th parallel at dawn on the 25th, and passed important decisions.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th] According to the Korean Central News Agency: In the two days before the South Korean puppet army launched an offensive north of the 38th parallel on the morning of the 25th, they continuously shelled the Pyoksong County area of Hwanghae Province in North Korea. From 22:00 on the 23rd to 4:00 on the 24th, they fired over two hundred rounds of 105mm howitzer and 81mm mortar shells at the Taedong district area of Pyoksong County. From 12:25 to 18:00 on the 23rd, they fired over three hundred rounds of howitzer and mortar shells at the Kajwa district area of Pyoksong County, and again fired two hundred rounds at the Kajwa district area from 6:00 to 10:00 on the 24th. As a result of the puppet army's frenzied shelling, dozens of farmhouses within the territory of Pyoksong County were destroyed, thirteen farmers were injured, one guard member of the republic was killed, and six were injured.

### 朝鲜共和国军队转入反攻  最高人民会议常委会发布紧急动员令  委任金日成朴宪永等组织军事委员会

1950-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜共和国军队转入反攻
    最高人民会议常委会发布紧急动员令
    委任金日成朴宪永等组织军事委员会
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内务部二十五日下午六时发表公报称：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国警备队于二十五日拂晓遭到南朝鲜伪国防军的意外进攻后，展开了顽强的防御战。警备队在与朝鲜人民军的协同作战下，迅即击溃敌人的进攻，转入了反攻。刻已在许多地区前进至三八线以南五公里至十公里。目前战争正在继续进行中。
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会二十六日发表命令称：由于李承晚傀儡政府的伪“国防军”向三八线以北发动全面进攻，造成了国内非常紧张的形势。为紧急动员全国人民的力量，消灭发动内战的李承晚匪帮，特作如下决定：（一）委任金日成、朴宪永、洪命熹、金策、崔庸健、朴一禹、郑准泽七人组织军事委员会，金日成为军事委员会委员长。（二）国内一切权力集中于军事委员会。（三）全体公民各级政权机关、政党、社会团体及军事机关，必须绝对服从军事委员会的命令和指示。
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议于二十五日召开非常会议，就有关二十五日拂晓南朝鲜傀儡政府的伪国防军向三八线以北地区发动进攻因而造成紧张事件的各项问题，进行讨论，并通过了重要决定。
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：南朝鲜伪军于二十五日晨向三八线以北发动进攻以前的两天中，曾连续炮轰北朝鲜的黄海道碧城县一带地区。自二十三日二十二时到二十四日四时，向碧城县代东区一带共发射了一百零五毫米的榴弹炮和八十一毫米的迫击炮二百余发。二十三日十二时二十五分到十八时，向碧城县茄佐区一带发射了榴弹炮和迫击炮三百余发，二十四日六时到十时，复向茄佐区发炮二百余发。伪军疯狂发炮的结果，使碧城县境的数十户农家被炸毁，农民十三人被炸伤，共和国的警备队员被炸死一人，伤六人。
