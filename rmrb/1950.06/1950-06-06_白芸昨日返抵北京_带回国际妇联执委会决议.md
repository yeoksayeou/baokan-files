Title: Bai Yun Returns to Beijing Yesterday, Bringing Back Resolution of the International Federation of Women's Executive Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Bai Yun, the Chinese executive committee representative who went to Finland in mid-March to attend the Ninth Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation, returned to Beijing on the 5th. In addition to attending the above-mentioned meeting, Bai Yun participated in the Chinese delegation's visit to Hungary and Czechoslovakia to celebrate the national days of the two countries. She brought back the resolutions, reports, and other materials of the Women's International Democratic Federation Executive Committee; and brought back gifts from democratic women's organizations in Finland, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Mongolia, and other countries to the All-China Women's Federation.

### 白芸昨日返抵北京  带回国际妇联执委会决议

1950-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　白芸昨日返抵北京
    带回国际妇联执委会决议
    【新华社五日讯】三月中赴芬兰出席国际民主妇联第九次执行委员会的中国执委代表白芸，已于五日返抵北京。白芸除出席上述会议外，曾参加我国代表团赴匈牙利及捷克，庆贺两国国庆。她带回了国际民主妇联执委会的决议、报告及其他材料；并带回芬兰、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、蒙古等国民主妇女团体送给我全国妇联的礼物。
