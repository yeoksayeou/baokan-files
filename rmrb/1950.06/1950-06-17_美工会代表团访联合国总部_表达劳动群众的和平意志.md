Title: Delegation of American Trade Unionists Visits UN Headquarters, Expressing the Peace Will of the Working Masses
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York: A delegation of active members from various American trade unions, sponsored by the American National Labor Conference for Peace, representing hundreds of thousands of union members, visited the United Nations Headquarters on June 14 to express the American people's fervent desire for peace and the prohibition of atomic weapons, and to commend UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie's recent efforts for peace. They were immediately received by UN Assistant Secretary-General Cohen.

After handing Cohen a declaration expressing the American working masses' will for peace, the representatives also handed Cohen a register of approximately 150,000 signatures gathered by American labor peace advocates for the peace declaration.

### 美工会代表团访联合国总部  表达劳动群众的和平意志

1950-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美工会代表团访联合国总部
    表达劳动群众的和平意志
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社纽约讯：在美国全国劳工和平会议赞助下的美国各工会积极分子代表团，代表着几十万工会会员，于六月十四日访问联合国总部，表明美国人民热望和平与禁止原子武器，并且赞扬联合国秘书长赖伊最近为和平而作的努力。他们当即为联合国助理秘书长科亨所接见。
    代表们在把表达美国劳动群众和平意志的宣言递给科亨后，更把美国劳工保卫和平的战士们已为和平宣言征集到的约十五万人的签名册递给科亨。
