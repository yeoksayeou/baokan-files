Title: East China Makes Second-Grade Flour to Ship to Disaster Areas
Author: Zhang Yunrui
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 3

　　East China Makes Second-Grade Flour to Ship to Disaster Areas
    [Shanghai News] In order to cooperate with disaster relief, the East China Grain Bureau, in addition to manufacturing 600,000 jin of mixed grain flour in April and shipping it to the disaster areas in northern Jiangsu, specially manufactured 2 million jin of red car soybean cake with extremely low water content and no mud and sand, 2 million jin of No. 3 and No. 4 flour, and a large number of ordinary soybean cake and shipped them to the disaster areas in northern Jiangsu in early May. (Zhang Yunrui)

### 华东制次等面粉运往灾区
张允瑞
1950-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　华东制次等面粉运往灾区
    【本报上海讯】华东区粮食局为配合救灾，除四月间曾制造杂粮粉六十万斤运往苏北灾区外，五月初又特制含水份极微、无泥沙之红车豆饼二百万斤，三、四号面粉二百万斤及大量普通豆饼运往苏北灾区。（张允瑞）
