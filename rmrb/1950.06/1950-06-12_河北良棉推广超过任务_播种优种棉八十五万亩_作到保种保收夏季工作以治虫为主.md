Title: Hebei Exceeds Cotton Promotion Target: 850,000 Mu Sown with Superior Cotton Varieties; Focus on Pest Control for Summer Work to Ensure Seed and Harvest
Author:
Date: 1950-06-12
Page: 2

(Baoding News) Hebei Province has overfulfilled its task of promoting superior cotton varieties. According to statistics from the Cotton Production Improvement Guidance Division of the Provincial People's Government's Department of Agriculture: the province's superior cotton areas include 30 counties (towns), 83 districts, 1,606 villages, and 175,273 households, with a total of 853,434 mu sown with superior cotton, exceeding the original plan of 800,000 mu by 53,434 mu. Specifically: the Shijiazhuang Cotton Production Improvement Guidance Area completed 191,876 mu, Tianjin Area 152,247 mu, Linqing Area 90,381 mu; Baoding Area 86,163 mu, Tongxian Area 69,798 mu, Xingtai Area 48,057 mu, Cangxian Area 10,970 mu, Handan Area 175,810 mu; Tangshan Area 28,132 mu. Only the Tangshan Area did not complete its original planned acreage.

In order to ensure seed and harvest, and to lay a good foundation for future promotion of superior varieties, the Cotton Production Improvement Division of the Hebei Provincial Government's Department of Agriculture convened a meeting of the promotion section chiefs of all cotton production improvement guidance areas from May 31st to June 1st. The meeting summarized the work of sowing superior cotton and clearly stipulated that the summer work should focus on pest control, supplemented by technical guidance on cotton management. Each guidance area will actively cooperate with governments at all levels to thoroughly organize and mobilize the masses to take swift action, achieving early treatment and universal treatment, ensuring timely detection and timely eradication, so as not to allow pests to spread and become a disaster. In terms of management technology, leaders should guide the masses to hoe more, apply early and more topdressing fertilizer, and provide cotton farmers with necessary technical guidance on seedling establishment, plant spacing, and pruning.
(Hebei Provincial People's Government Department of Agriculture)

### 河北良棉推广超过任务  播种优种棉八十五万亩  作到保种保收夏季工作以治虫为主

1950-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　河北良棉推广超过任务
    播种优种棉八十五万亩     
    作到保种保收夏季工作以治虫为主
    【保定讯】河北省已超额完成了优良棉种的推广工作。据省人民政府农业厅棉产改进指导处统计：全省良棉区包括三十县（镇），八十三个区，一千六百零六个村，十七万五千二百七十三户，共播种良棉八十五万三千四百三十四亩，与原计划八十万亩相较，超过五万三千四百三十四亩。计：石家庄棉产改进指导区完成了十九万一千八百七十六亩，天津区十五万二千二百四十七亩，临清区九万零三百八十一亩；保定区八万六千一百六十三亩，通县区六万九千七百九十八亩，邢台区四万八千零五十七亩，沧县区一万零九百七十亩，邯郸区十七万五千八百一十亩；唐山区二万八千一百三十二亩。其中仅唐山区未完成原计划亩数。
    为了保种保收，给今后优种推广打好基础，河北省府农业厅棉产改进处，特于五月三十一日至六月一日召开了全省各棉产改进指导区推广股股长会议，总结了良棉播种工作，明确规定夏季工作以防治害虫为主，辅以棉花管理技术指导。各指导区将主动配合各级政权，深入组织发动群众，迅速行动，作到早治普治，务使及时发现、及时扑灭，不使蔓延成灾。在管理技术上，领导群众多锄，早施及多施追肥，对定苗、株行距离及打枝打尖等，都应给棉农以必要的技术指导。
                                        （河北省人民政府农业厅）
