Title: Democratic League of Chinese Industry and Commerce Holds a Party Congress, Calling for Support of the Land Reform Law and Preparing to Participate in Land Reform Work
Author:
Date: 1950-06-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: The Democratic League of Chinese Industry and Commerce in Beijing held a party congress on the 25th, organized by the Beijing Organizing Committee of the party, in order to communicate the resolution on land reform from the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Central Committee members of the party in Beijing and all comrades from Beijing attended. Zhang Bojun, Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the party, called on party comrades to: 1. Support the Land Reform Law and prepare to participate in land reform work. 2. Learn from the Chinese Communist Party's rectification movement to correct all backward ideas and practices of the old society and armchair revolutionaries. He requested that all party comrades be transformed and improved through participation in the land reform movement. Peng Zemin, Chairman of the Supervisory Committee, reported on the situation of Comrade Deng Zesheng (Yanda)'s research on land reform at the beginning of the party's founding, and called on comrades to carry forward this traditional spirit and complete the historical task of land reform under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. Huang Qixiang, Secretary-General of the Executive and Supervisory Committee, reported on the content of the draft Land Reform Law, elaborated in detail on the spirit of the draft Land Reform Law, and put forward two tasks: 1. Should learn the firm and hardworking spirit of the CCP cadres, and also learn the CCP's rectification movement; 2. Must be fully prepared to participate in land reform and successfully pass the test of land reform. Finally, Guo Guanjie, a member of the Central Committee, explained the relationship between land reform and new democratic construction.

### 农工民主党召开党员大会  号召拥护土地改革法  准备参加土地改革工作

1950-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　农工民主党召开党员大会
    号召拥护土地改革法
    准备参加土地改革工作
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国农工民主党为传达人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议关于土地改革的决议，由该党北京市整理委员会于二十五日召开党员大会。出席者有该党在京中委及北京市全体同志。该党中央执委会主席章伯钧，号召该党同志：一、拥护土地改革法，准备参加土地改革工作。二、学习中共整风运动，纠正旧社会的及口头革命派一切落伍的思想和作风。他要求在参加土地改革运动中改造和提高全党同志。监察委员会主席彭泽民报告了该党在创党之初邓择生（演达）同志研究土地改革的情形，号召同志应发扬这种传统精神，在中国共产党领导下完成土地改革的历史任务。执监委员会秘书长黄琪翔报告土地改革法草案的内容，对土地改革法草案的精神有详尽的阐发，并提出两项任务：一、应学习中共干部的坚定刻苦的精神，同时也应该学习中共的整风运动；二、要有充分准备，来参加土地改革，胜利地渡过土地改革这一关。最后由中委郭冠杰说明土地改革与新民主主义建设的关系。
