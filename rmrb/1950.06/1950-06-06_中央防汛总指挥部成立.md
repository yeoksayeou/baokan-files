Title: Central Flood Control Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 1

　　Central Flood Control Headquarters Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] With the flood season approaching, the central flood control headquarters, the unified leading body for national flood control work, was established in the Ministry of Water Resources at 9:00 AM on June 3rd, according to a decision by the Government Administration Council, and began its operations on the same day. Vice Premier Dong Biwu serves concurrently as the Director of the headquarters, and Minister of Water Resources Fu Zuoyi and Minister of the Operations Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission Li Tao serve concurrently as Deputy Directors.

### 中央防汛总指挥部成立

1950-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央防汛总指挥部成立
    【新华社五日讯】汛期即届，全国防汛工作的统一领导机关——中央防汛总指挥部已根据政务院决定于六月三日上午九时在水利部成立，即日起开始办公。该指挥部由董必武副总理兼任主任，水利部傅作义部长和中国人民革命军事委员会作战部李涛部长兼任副主任。
