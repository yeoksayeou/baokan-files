Title: Italian Socialist Party Central Committee Plenary Session Concludes, Calls on the Entire Party to Struggle to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, June 25th -- According to TASS, the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Italian Socialist Party has concluded in Rome. The leader of the Socialist Party, Nenni, delivered a report at the meeting on the international situation, the Italian situation, the struggle for peace, and the urgent tasks of the Socialist Party. Central Committee members Morandi and Gaccaiattore presented reports at the meeting on the Socialist Party's participation in the struggle of workers and peasants, and the attitude of the Italian Socialist Party towards the draft land reform.

The Central Committee passed a resolution, calling on all Socialist Party members to pay attention to the extreme importance of the worldwide vote against the manufacture and use of atomic weapons and to strive for the establishment of control over atomic energy, and urging them to do their utmost to ensure the complete success of this movement in Italy.

The Central Committee called on all party organizations and all party members to dedicate all their strength to the struggle of the working class to defend labor, freedom and peace.

### 意社会党中央全会闭幕  号召全党为保卫和平而斗争

1950-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　意社会党中央全会闭幕
    号召全党为保卫和平而斗争
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社罗马二十五日讯：意大利社会党中央委员会全体会议已在罗马闭幕。社会党领袖南尼曾在会上发表关于国际形势、意大利局势、和平斗争以及社会党紧急任务的报告。中委摩兰地和加克夏托莱在会上提出了关于社会党参加工农的斗争以及意大利社会党对于土地改革草案态度的报告。
    中央委员会通过一项决议，唤起所有社会党党员注意全世界为反对制造和使用原子武器，以及争取建立原子能的管制而举行的投票所具有的非常重要性，并且敦促他们竭尽全力使这个运动在意大利获得完全的成功。
    中央委员会号召党的一切组织和全体党员，把全部力量献给劳动阶级为保卫劳动、自由与和平而进行的斗争。
