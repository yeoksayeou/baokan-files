Title: All Employees of Beijing 70th Ordnance Factory Protest Against US Imperialist Persecution of the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 4

　　Beijing 70th Ordnance Factory All Employees
    Protest Against US Imperialist Persecution of the Japanese Communist Party
    [From our newspaper] The entire staff of the Beijing 70th Ordnance Factory issued a statement protesting the heinous crimes of American imperialism and Japanese reactionaries in persecuting the Japanese Communist Party. The full text of the statement is as follows: "We, the entire staff of the Beijing 70th Ordnance Factory, express our extreme anger and strongly protest against the heinous crimes of US imperialism and Japanese reactionaries in persecuting the Japanese Communist Party. We will resolutely stand with the Japanese working class and the Japanese people, and fight for the world peace and democracy movement. Whoever undermines peace will be eliminated. We will forever stand closely united in the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and fully support the heroic struggle of the Japanese people!"

### 北京七十兵工厂全体职工  抗议美帝迫害日共

1950-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京七十兵工厂全体职工
    抗议美帝迫害日共
    【本报讯】北京七十兵工厂全体职工发表声明，抗议美国帝国主义及日本反动派迫害日本共产党的暴行。声明全文如下：“我们北京市七十兵工厂全体职工，以极大愤怒严重抗议美帝及日本反动派迫害日共的恶劣暴行。我们将坚决与日本工人阶级和日本人民站在一起，为争取世界和平民主运动而斗争。谁破坏和平，谁将被消灭。我们永远紧紧地团结在以苏联为首的和平民主阵营里，全力支持日本人民的英勇斗争！”
