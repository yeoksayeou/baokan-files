Title: GDR Diplomatic Mission to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, Ambassador Koenig, and his party of twelve arrived in Beijing by plane at 5:30 this afternoon. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Department of Soviet and European Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director, representing Ma Wenbo, Director of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; Luo Shen, Soviet Ambassador to China, Fedlin, Counselor, Polish Ambassador to China, Burkin, Commercial Commissioner, Gushiduo, Hungarian Ambassador to China, Xia Falangke, Czech Embassy in China, Counselor Hasek, Romanian Embassy in China, Counselor Silad, and others.

### 德驻华外交使团抵京

1950-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　德驻华外交使团抵京
    【新华社十六日讯】德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希等一行十二人，于今日下午五时半乘机抵京，赴机场欢迎者有我外交部副部长章汉夫、办公厅主任王炳南、苏欧司司长伍修权、交际处处长王倬如、副处长沈平，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部参谋长唐延杰代表马文波处长；苏联驻华大使罗申，参赞费德林，波兰驻华大使布尔金、商务专员顾士多，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，捷克驻华大使馆参赞哈谢克，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆参赞席拉德等。
