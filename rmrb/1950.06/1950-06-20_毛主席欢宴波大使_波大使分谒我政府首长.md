Title: Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for the Polish Ambassador; Polish Ambassador Pays Visits to Our Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-06-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on the 19th for the Polish Ambassador to China, Mr. Burkin, and all members of the Polish Embassy. Invited to accompany him were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Li Jishen; Secretary General Lin Boqu; Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council Zhou Enlai; our Ambassador to Poland Peng Mingzhi; Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan; Deputy Director Yan Baohang; Director of the Central People's Government Bureau of Ceremonies Yu Xinqing; Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan; Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru; and Deputy Director Shen Ping.

After presenting his credentials, the Ambassador of the Republic of Poland to China, Mr. Burkin, paid separate visits to Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei, Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and also visited our Ambassador to Poland, Peng Mingzhi.

### 毛主席欢宴波大使  波大使分谒我政府首长

1950-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席欢宴波大使
    波大使分谒我政府首长
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于十九日下午七时宴波兰驻我国大使布尔金及波兰大使馆全体馆员，应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席，刘少奇副主席，李济深副主席，林伯渠秘书长，政务院周恩来总理兼外交部长，我驻波兰大使彭明治，外交部办公厅王炳南主任，阎宝航副主任，中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长，及外交部苏联东欧司伍修权司长，交际处王倬如处长，沈平副处长。
    波兰共和国驻我国大使布尔金于呈递国书后，曾分谒朱德副主席，李济深副主席，张澜副主席，黄炎培副总理，贸易部叶季壮部长，文化部沈雁冰部长，并访问我驻波兰大使彭明治等。
