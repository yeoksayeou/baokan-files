Title: Holding Various Beneficial Summer Activities for Youth: Youth Summer Life Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Beijing Youth Summer Life Committee was established on the 10th. The committee includes responsible persons from the Beijing Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Student Federation, the Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, and the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Education Workers' Union, and other relevant organs and organizations. The committee has decided to hold various meaningful cultural, recreational, and sports activities suitable for the needs of young people during this summer vacation, which will be beneficial to their physical and mental health, so that young students can get proper rest and exercise in these activities, and can make progress in their thinking and culture. The main contents of the activities are: to hold youth lectures focusing on current affairs and policies in different districts; to organize city-wide choirs, hold large-scale choruses of more than a thousand people, and singing and drama competitions; to guide the literary research and drama and dance activities of various schools; to organize youth swimming teams and sports competitions such as ball games; to help various schools establish current affairs, history, radio and other research associations and specimen collection, hold glider model performances and other learning activities; to hold youth and children's camping in batches; to hold children's paradises in different districts; to assist in organizing travel and visits and to hold preferential film and theater performances.

### 举办各种有益青年的暑期活动  青年暑期生活委员会成立

1950-06-14
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　举办各种有益青年的暑期活动
    青年暑期生活委员会成立
    【新华社十二日讯】北京市青年暑期生活委员会已于十日成立。该委员会包括青年团北京市工作委员会和北京市学联以及市文教局、市教育工作者工会筹委会等有关机关团体的负责人。该委员会已决定今年暑期举办各种适合青年需要的、有意义的、有益于身心健康的文化、娱乐和体育活动，使青年学生在这些活动中可以得到适当的休息与锻炼，并能在思想上与文化上得到进步。活动的主要内容为：分区举办以时事政策为主的青年讲座；组织全市性的合唱团，举办千人以上的大合唱及歌咏、戏剧比赛；辅导各校的文学研究及戏剧、舞蹈活动；组织青年游泳队及球类等运动比赛；帮助各校建立时事、历史、无线电等各种研究会及采集标本、举行滑翔机模型表演等学习活动；分批举办青年及少年儿童露营；分区举办少年儿童乐园；协助举办旅行参观以及举办影剧优待演出等。
