Title: Tianjin Special District League Members Take the Lead in Overcoming Disaster and Famine
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 3

(By this newspaper's dispatch from Tianjin) The Communist Youth League committees at all levels and all League members in Tianjin Special District, Hebei Province, played an active leading role in production and disaster relief. They mobilized 9,200 spinning wheels in three districts of Baodi to spin thread; launched production competitions, boosting the production enthusiasm of the masses; taught 109 people in Dacheng how to boil saltpeter; and organized mutual aid with the masses during wheat planting to solve the difficulties of manpower and animal power. Therefore, they joined the entire people of the district in overcoming the disaster and famine. During this disaster relief campaign, the number of League members in the district increased from 3,000 to 22,085, and another 1,016 League members joined the Communist Party.

### 天津专区团员带头战胜灾荒

1950-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　天津专区团员带头战胜灾荒
    【本报天津讯】河北天津专区青年团各级团委和全体团员，在生产救灾中，起了积极的带头作用。他们带动了宝坻三个区九千二百辆纺车进行纺线；开展了生产竞赛，鼓舞了群众生产情绪；教会大城一百零九人学会熬硝；种麦时与群众组织互助，解决人力畜力困难，因此与该区全体人民共同战胜了灾荒。在此次救灾运动中，全区团员从三千人发展到了二万二千零八十五人，又有一千零一十六个团员加入了共产党。
