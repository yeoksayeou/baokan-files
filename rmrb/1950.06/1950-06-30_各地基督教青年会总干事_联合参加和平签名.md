Title: YMCA General Secretaries from Various Regions Jointly Participate in Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-06-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 26th -- The General Secretaries of the YMCA in various regions of China have been holding a meeting in Shanghai since June 21st to discuss how to improve future work based on New Democracy and the Common Program. The meeting is scheduled to end on the 28th. In the previous days of the meeting, participants also discussed issues such as defending world lasting peace and opposing imperialist plots of aggressive war. In addition to deciding to promote the participation of YMCA's in various regions in the signature campaign to defend world peace, the General Secretaries from various regions, together with the staff of the YMCA National Association, signed the peace declaration, and announced: The General Secretaries of the YMCA in China enthusiastically support the call of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, demanding that all governments unconditionally ban atomic weapons, and declare the first user of atomic weapons to be a war criminal. The signatories included Wu Yaozong, Liu Liangmo, Jiang Wenhan, Lu Qichen, Li Shoupao, Situ Tong, Dong Chengxian, Ma Minggang, Huang Peiyong, Xu Youen, Tu Yuqing and other eleven staff members of the National Association. The General Secretaries from various regions who signed include Lu Ganchen from Shanghai, Gao Shangren from Beijing, Song Ruhai from Hankou, Wang Yidun from Guangzhou, Huang Cixian from Chongqing, Cai Zhichuan from Nanchang, Wang Qixing from Yunnan, Yang Xiaopeng from Tianjin, Zhu Peien from Nanjing, as well as the General Secretaries from Chengdu, Suzhou, Qingdao, Kaifeng, Taiyuan, Ningbo, Xi'an, Baoji, Lanzhou, Guiyang, Wuchang, Changsha, Hangzhou, Jinan, Xiamen, Shandong and other places, totaling thirty people.

### 各地基督教青年会总干事  联合参加和平签名

1950-06-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　各地基督教青年会总干事
    联合参加和平签名
    【新华社上海二十六日电】中国各地基督教青年会总干事于六月二十一日起在沪举行会议，讨论如何根据新民主主义与共同纲领，改进今后工作。会议预定二十八日结束。在前几天的会议上，与会者还讨论了保卫世界持久和平、反对帝国主义侵略战争阴谋等问题。除决定推动各地青年会参加保卫世界和平签名运动外，各地总干事并与青年会全国协会各干事联合在和平宣言上签名，并宣布：全中国基督教青年会的总干事们热烈地拥护世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的号召，要求各国政府无条件地禁用原子武器，并宣布第一个使用原子武器者为战争罪犯。签名的有全国协会干事吴耀宗、刘良模、江文汉、陆琪臣、李寿葆、司徒桐、董承先、马铭刚、黄培永、许由恩、涂羽卿等十一人。各地总干事签名的有上海陆干臣、北京高尚仁、汉口宋如海、广州王以敦、重庆黄次咸、南昌蔡智传、云南王齐兴、天津杨肖彭、南京诸培恩，以及成都、苏州、青岛、开封、太原、宁波、西安、宝鸡、兰州、贵阳、武昌、长沙、杭州、济南、厦门、山东等地总干事三十人。
