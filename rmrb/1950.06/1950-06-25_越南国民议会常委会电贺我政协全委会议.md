Title: The Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to the All-China Committee Meeting of the CPPCC
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, dated the 24th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 23rd: Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, recently sent a telegram to the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, expressing congratulations. The telegram stated: The National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam extends warm congratulations to the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. We sincerely wish your conference great success in the construction of Chinese People's Democracy under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. The new China will, together with other people's democratic countries in the world, actively engage in the struggle against imperialism in order to establish democracy and lasting peace.

### 越南国民议会常委会电贺我政协全委会议

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南国民议会常委会电贺我政协全委会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】越南通讯社北越二十三日讯：越南国民议会常务委员会代理主席孙德胜，顷致电中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第二次会议，表示庆贺。电文称：越南民主共和国国民议会向中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第二次会议致以热烈的庆贺。我们谨祝你们的会议在毛泽东主席的领导下，在中国人民民主主义的建设上获得伟大成功。新中国将和世界上其他人民民主国家一道积极地进行反对帝国主义的斗争，以建立民主及持久和平。
