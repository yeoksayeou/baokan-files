Title: China Establishes Diplomatic Relations with Indonesia; Appoints Wang Renshu as Ambassador to Indonesia
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: On January 11th of this year, Hatta, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of the United States of Indonesia, sent a letter to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, notifying the establishment of the Republic of the United States of Indonesia. In response, Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government of China, replied to Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hatta on March 29th, stating that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is willing to establish normal diplomatic relations with the Republic of the United States of Indonesia on the basis of equality, mutual benefit, and mutual respect for territorial sovereignty.

Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hatta sent another letter to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on April 13th, expressing deep satisfaction with our willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the government of the Republic of the United States of Indonesia, and proposed exchanging diplomatic envoys to promote understanding and friendly relations between the two countries. Our Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai then sent a note to Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hatta on May 31st, expressing acceptance of the proposal to exchange envoys and proposing that the two countries exchange ambassadors to enhance the friendship between the two countries. Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hatta replied to our Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on June 9th, agreeing to our proposal to exchange ambassadors.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Central People's Government has appointed Wang Renshu as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Republic of the United States of Indonesia.

### 我与印尼建立外交关系  任命王任叔为驻印尼大使

1950-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我与印尼建立外交关系
    任命王任叔为驻印尼大使
    【新华社十二日讯】今年一月十一日印度尼西亚联邦共和国总理兼外交部部长哈达致函我中央人民政府外交部部长，通知印度尼西亚联邦共和国之成立。我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来为此于三月二十九日复函哈达总理兼外长，表示中华人民共和国中央人民政府愿在平等、互利及互相尊重领土主权的基础上，与印度尼西亚联邦共和国建立正常外交关系。
    哈达总理兼外长于四月十三日再致函周恩来总理兼外长，对我愿与印度尼西亚联邦共和国政府建立外交关系一事深为满意，并提议与我互换外交使节，以促进两国之间的了解和友好关系。我周总理兼外长当于五月三十一日照会哈达总理兼外长，表示接受互换使节之建议，并提议两国互换大使，以增进两国的友谊。哈达总理兼外长于六月九日电复我周总理兼外长，同意我关于互换大使的提议。
    【新华社十二日讯】我中央人民政府已任命王任叔为我国驻印度尼西亚联邦共和国首任特命全权大使。
