Title: Firmly Defend World Peace: Japanese Million-Person Signature Campaign; Mongolian Peace Signature Campaign Successfully Concluded
Author:
Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 4
　　Firmly Defend World Peace
    Japanese Million-Person Signature Campaign
    Mongolian Peace Signature Campaign Successfully Concluded
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st Dispatch] TASS, 20th Dispatch, citing the Tokyo Akahata Newspaper: According to incomplete materials, from April to June 15th, 1,010,000 people in Japan signed the declaration of the Permanent Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. The Kanagawa Prefecture Trade Union Committee collected the most signatures, totaling 480,000; the League for the Defense of Democracy collected 240,500 signatures in Osaka; and the Patriotic United Front collected 150,000 signatures in Kyoto.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th Dispatch] According to TASS, Ulaanbaatar: The peace signature campaign in the Mongolian People's Republic has been successfully concluded. 686,782 people across the country participated in the signing, accounting for nearly 70% of the total population. To this end, the National Peace Committee held a grand meeting at the National Mongolian University in the capital of the Republic on the 17th to discuss the results of the peace signature campaign. Shirendev, Chairman of the National Peace Committee and President of Mongolian University, pointed out in his summary report to the meeting that 397 local peace committees have been established across the country during the signature campaign.

### 坚决保卫世界和平  日本百万人民签名  蒙古和平签名运动已胜利结束

1950-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　坚决保卫世界和平
    日本百万人民签名
    蒙古和平签名运动已胜利结束
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社二十日电引东京赤旗报消息：根据不完全的材料，从四月到六月十五日，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的宣言，在日本已有一百零一万人签名。神奈川县的工会委员会收集的签名人数最多，共达四十八万人；保卫民主同盟在大阪征集了二十四万零五百人的签名；爱国统一阵线在京都收集了十五万人的签名。
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托尔讯：蒙古人民共和国的和平签名运动，业已胜利结束，全国参加签名者达六十八万六千七百八十二人，几占人口总数的百分之七十。全国和平委员会为此于十七日在共和国首都国立蒙古大学召开了盛大的会议，讨论和平签名运动的结果。全国和平委员会主席、蒙古大学校长希伦迪在向会议作的总结报告中指出，在签名运动中，全国已建立了三百九十七个地方和平委员会。
