Title: Huang Zhen Appointed Ambassador to Hungary; Hungarian Envoy to China Promoted to Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-14
Page: 1
　　
　　Huang Zhen Appointed Ambassador to Hungary
　　Hungarian Envoy to China Promoted to Ambassador
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 13th] The Central People's Government of our country has appointed General Huang Zhen as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Hungarian People's Republic.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 13th] The government of the Hungarian People's Republic has decided to upgrade the Hungarian Legation in China to an Embassy. Mr. Xiafalan Ke, the current Hungarian Envoy to China, has been promoted to the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China.

### 黄镇任驻匈大使  匈驻我国公使升任大使

1950-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　黄镇任驻匈大使
    匈驻我国公使升任大使
    【新华社十三日讯】我中央人民政府已任命黄镇将军为我国驻匈牙利人民共和国首任特命全权大使。
    【新华社十三日讯】匈牙利人民共和国政府已决定由匈牙利驻华公使馆升格为大使馆，匈牙利现任驻华公使夏法朗柯先生升任匈牙利人民共和国驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权大使。
