Title: Soviet Expert Sulike Teaches Coking Workers at Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant
Author: Li Hong
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 2

Local Briefs:

　　Soviet Expert Sulike Teaches Coking Workers at Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant
    In order to improve the operational skills of the workers in the coking department of the Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant, Soviet expert Comrade Sulike voluntarily suggested that he give a three-hour lecture every week to explain basic coking knowledge. The first lecture has already begun on June 7th. This spirit of lofty internationalist friendship has been warmly welcomed by all the workers.
       (Li Hong)

### 苏联专家苏力克给太原钢铁厂炼焦职工上课
李泓
1950-06-27
第2版()
专栏：各地简讯

　　苏联专家苏力克给太原钢铁厂炼焦职工上课
    为提高太原钢铁厂炼焦部职工的操作技术，苏联专家苏力克同志自动提议每星期由他上大课三小时，讲解炼焦基本常识，六月七日已开始第一讲。此种国际主义的高度友爱精神，受到全体职工的热烈欢迎。 
       （李泓）
