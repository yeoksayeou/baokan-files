Title: Cambodian National United Front Established; Vietnamese National United Front Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:

Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: According to Vietnam News Agency North Vietnam, the Vietnamese National United Front sent a telegram on the 12th to congratulate the establishment of the Cambodian National United Front, stating: "The Vietnamese National United Front is pleased to learn that the Cambodian National United Front was proclaimed to be established in April 1950. We hope that the establishment of the Cambodian National United Front will have a beneficial impact on the establishment of the Lao National United Front, and we hope that due to the close unity of the three peoples of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, the American and French imperialists who are engaged in the war of aggression in Indochina will be defeated more quickly, thereby restoring freedom to Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and becoming three prosperous countries."

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: According to Vietnam News Agency North Vietnam, June 20th, General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army, summarized the achievements of the Vietnamese local army and militia at the National Conference of Local Army and Militia Cadres held in North Vietnam in early June, stating: "The local army cooperates with the main force of the Liberation Army in fighting the enemy, thus expanding the victory. They have successfully turned the enemy's area into the front line and their own area into the rear, thereby fulfilling their glorious mission." Vo Nguyen Giap continued, "In the upcoming next stage of battle, the local army will also be responsible for defending local territories, a task previously undertaken by the Liberation Army."

### 柬埔寨民族统一战线成立  越民族统一战线致电祝贺

1950-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　柬埔寨民族统一战线成立
    越民族统一战线致电祝贺
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社北越讯：越南民族统一战线于十二日电贺柬埔寨民族统一战线的成立称：越南民族统一战线欣闻柬埔寨民族统一战线已于一九五○年四月间宣告成立。我们希望柬埔寨民族统一战线的成立，将对老挝民族统一战线的成立发生有益的影响，并望由于越南、老挝、柬埔寨三民族的紧密团结，将加速击败在印度支那从事侵略战争的美、法帝国主义者，从而使越南、老挝、柬埔寨恢复自由，并成为三个繁荣的国家。
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十日讯：越南人民解放军总司令武元甲将军，曾于六月初在北越举行的全国地方军、民兵干部大会上总结越南地方军与民兵的成就称：地方军与解放军主力配合对敌人作战，因而扩大了胜利，他们已经顺利地把敌人的区域变成前线，把自己的区域变成后方，从而尽了他们辉煌的使命。武元甲继称，在即将到来的下一阶段战斗中，地方军还要负起以前是由解放军担任的保卫地方领土的任务。
