Title: Suggest Planting Trees Along Embankments
Author: Ma Yupu, Huang Jianhua
Date: 1950-06-05
Page: 4

People's Forum

To the Editor:

　　The embankment construction task we are undertaking is now mostly completed. We suggest planting two rows of trees along the roots of the embankment. In this way, on the one hand, the embankment can be consolidated, and on the other hand, it will be a source of income for the country in the future.

　　Literacy and Education, 1st Platoon, 1st Battalion, 613th Regiment, 205th Division Ma Yupou
　　Clerk Huang Jianhua

### 建议堤旁植树
马玉璞　黄建华
1950-06-05
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　建议堤旁植树编辑同志：
    我们所担任的筑堤任务，现在大部分都将完成了。我们建议，在堤根两旁栽上两行树。这样，一方面可以使堤巩固，另一方面将来也是国家的一笔收入。
    二○五师六一三团一营一连文教  马玉璞
                            文书  黄建华
