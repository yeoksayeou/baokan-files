Title: China National Native Produce & Animal By-products Import & Export Corporation Established in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1950-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, June 29th: The China National Native Produce & Animal By-products Import & Export Corporation, acting on the order of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, was established in Tianjin on June 27th. In addition to setting up two regional corporations in Shanghai and Xi'an for East China and Northwest China respectively, the company has also established three directly affiliated branches in Beijing, Baotou, and Shijiazhuang. Furthermore, four processing plants for wool, hides, casings, and feathers are being established in Tianjin City under the direct leadership of the company. These plants will be responsible for the acquisition of wool, hides, casings, and feathers in Tianjin.

### 中国皮毛公司在津成立

1950-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国皮毛公司在津成立
    【新华社天津二十九日电】中国皮毛公司奉中央人民政府贸易部命令，已于六月二十七日在天津成立。该公司除在上海、西安暂设华东、西北两个区公司外，并在北京、包头、石家庄等地设立三个直属分公司。此外，在天津市设立绒毛、皮张、肠衣、羽毛等四个加工厂，在该公司直接领导下，负责在津市进行绒毛、皮张、肠衣、羽毛的收购业务。
