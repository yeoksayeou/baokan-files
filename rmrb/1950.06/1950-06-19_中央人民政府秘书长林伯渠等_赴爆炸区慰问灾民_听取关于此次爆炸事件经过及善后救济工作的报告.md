Title: Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu and Others Visit Disaster Victims in Explosion Area, Listen to Report on the Explosion Incident and Relief Work
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu and Others
    Visit Disaster Victims in Explosion Area
    Listen to Report on the Explosion Incident and Relief Work
    [Beijing News Agency] On the morning of the 18th, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Deputy Director of the Supervisory Committee of the Government Administration Council Liu Jingfan, Members of the Central People's Government Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Minister of Health Li Dequan, and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, went to the explosion area of the Fuhua Firecracker Factory and hospitals to inspect the relief work and comfort the disaster victims. While comforting the disaster victims, Secretary-General Lin and his party listened to a report from Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, on the explosion incident and relief work.

### 中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠等  赴爆炸区慰问灾民  听取关于此次爆炸事件经过及善后救济工作的报告

1950-06-19
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠等
    赴爆炸区慰问灾民   
    听取关于此次爆炸事件经过及善后救济工作的报告
    【京市新闻处讯】中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，政务院副总理郭沫若，人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，政务院监察委员会副主任刘景范，中央人民政府委员蔡廷锴、彭泽民、卫生部部长李德全及各民主党派，各人民团体负责人员，于十八日上午前往辅华火药厂爆炸地区及医院，视察善后救济工作，并慰问受灾灾民。林秘书长等一行在慰问灾民时，曾听取北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正关于此次爆炸事件经过及善后救济工作的报告。
