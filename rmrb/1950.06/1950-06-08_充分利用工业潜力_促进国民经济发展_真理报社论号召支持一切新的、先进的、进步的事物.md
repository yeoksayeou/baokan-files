Title: Fully Utilize Industrial Potential to Promote National Economic Development; "Pravda" Editorial Calls for Support of All New, Advanced, and Progressive Things
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 5th: Pravda recently published an editorial urging the full utilization of the potential of socialist industry. The editorial emphasized that the creative initiative of workers unearths new potentials and helps to better utilize the brilliant technology of our industry. The editorial called for vigorous support for the creative initiative of the masses in the socialist competition to strive for early completion of production plans, and called for support of all new, advanced, and progressive things. The editorial also emphasized that the capable and most effective utilization of the technology of all industrial sectors will promote the further development of the national economy and accelerate the pace of communist construction.

### 充分利用工业潜力  促进国民经济发展  真理报社论号召支持一切新的、先进的、进步的事物

1950-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　充分利用工业潜力  促进国民经济发展
    真理报社论号召支持一切新的、先进的、进步的事物
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：真理报顷发表社论，敦促充分利用社会主义工业的潜力。社论着重指出：工人们创造性的倡议，发掘出新的潜力，并且有助于更好地利用我们工业光辉的技术。社论号召竭力支持在争取提前完成生产计划的社会主义竞赛中群众创造性的倡议，号召支持一切新的、先进的、进步的事物。社论并强调指出：能干地、最有效地利用一切工业部门的技术，将促进国民经济进一步的发展，并加速共产主义建设的速度。
