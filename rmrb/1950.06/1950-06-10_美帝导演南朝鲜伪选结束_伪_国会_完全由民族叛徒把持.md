Title: US Imperialists Direct South Korean Puppet Election to Conclusion; Puppet "National Assembly" Entirely Controlled by National Traitors
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th: The South Korean puppet "National Assembly" election, directed by US imperialism and its tool of aggression, the United Nations Commission on Korea, has been completed under police terror. In the new puppet "National Assembly," the majority are so-called "non-partisan" groups of national traitors and pro-Japanese elements, occupying a total of 128 seats. Syngman Rhee's Democratic Nationalist Party of Korea occupies 22 seats, Syngman Rhee's opposition party, the Democratic National Party headed by the pro-Japanese element Kim Seong-soo, occupies 22 seats, the Great Korean Youth Corps, one of Syngman Rhee's intelligence organizations, occupies 12 seats, Syngman Rhee's National Association occupies 11 seats, and other minority parties occupy 15 seats.
    The Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea, in a letter to all democratic political parties, social organizations, and the people of the whole country issued on the 7th, pointed out: The South Korean puppet election was carried out under the special agent rule of threat and deception by the US and Korean reactionaries. The new puppet "National Assembly" is entirely composed of anti-people elements, and there is not a single representative of the working people.

### 美帝导演南朝鲜伪选结束  伪“国会”完全由民族叛徒把持

1950-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝导演南朝鲜伪选结束
    伪“国会”完全由民族叛徒把持
    【新华社平壤九日电】为美帝国主义及其侵略工具——联合国朝鲜委员会所导演的南朝鲜伪“国会”选举，已在警察恐怖下举行完毕。在新的伪“国会”中，大多数是民族叛徒和亲日分子的所谓“无党派”集团，共占一百二十八席，李承晚的大韩国民党占二十二席，李承晚的反对党、以亲日分子金性洙为首的民主国民党占二十二席，李承晚的特务组织之一的大韩青年团占十二席，李承晚的国民会占十一席，其他少数党派占十五席。
    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会，在七日发表的告朝鲜各民主政党、社会团体与全国人民书中指出：南朝鲜的此次伪选，是在美、朝反动派威胁欺瞒的特务统治之下进行的。新的伪“国会”完全由反人民的分子所组成，没有一个劳动人民的代表。
