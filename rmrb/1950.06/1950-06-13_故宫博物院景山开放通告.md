Title: Notice Regarding the Opening of Jingshan Park, Palace Museum

Author:

Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 5

Notice Regarding the Opening of Jingshan Park, Palace Museum

    Jingshan Park of this museum will be reopened from the twelfth of this month.

    Hours: Doors open daily at 6:00 AM and close at 7:00 PM.

    Ticket Price: 400 yuan per person, half price for children.

    Preferential Treatment: Subject to the museum's preferential treatment measures.

    Attached is a library reading room located in Qiwang Pavilion.
