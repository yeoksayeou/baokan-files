Title: China and Burma Establish Diplomatic Relations; Yao Zhongming and U Min Thein Appointed as Ambassadors; Swedish Ambassador Amashton Visits Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:

Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 1

　　China and Burma Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Yao Zhongming and U Min Thein Appointed as Ambassadors
    Swedish Ambassador Amashton Visits Foreign Minister Zhou
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The Burmese Union Government announced on December 16th of last year its willingness to enter into diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs replied to Burmese Foreign Minister U E Maung via telegram on December 21st of last year, stating that after severance of relations between the Burmese government and the remnant forces of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries, the Central Government of the People's Republic of China is willing to establish diplomatic relations with Burma on the basis of equality, mutual benefit, and mutual respect for territorial sovereignty, and requested that the Burmese Union Government send a representative to Beijing to negotiate the preliminary and procedural issues of establishing diplomatic relations between China and Burma. On April 26th, U Pe Khin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, the negotiating representative dispatched by the Burmese Union Government, arrived in Beijing and immediately began these negotiations. The negotiations have now been successfully concluded. The Chinese and Burmese governments have also agreed that the Burmese Union Government will appoint Mr. U Min Thein as Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China will appoint Mr. Yao Zhongming as Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Burmese Union.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The first Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to our country, Mr. Amashton, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 2:00 PM today to discuss matters concerning the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs, Wen Pengjiu, Deputy Director, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 中缅建立外交关系  互派姚仲明吴敏登为大使  瑞典大使阿马斯顿拜会周外长

1950-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中缅建立外交关系
    互派姚仲明吴敏登为大使
    瑞典大使阿马斯顿拜会周外长
    【新华社八日讯】缅甸联邦政府于去年十二月十六日宣布愿与中华人民共和国进入外交关系。我外交部周恩来部长当于去年十二月二十一日电复缅甸外交部长吴伊蒙，表示在缅甸政府与中国国民党反动派残余断绝关系之后，中华人民共和国中央人民政府愿在平等互利及互相尊重领土主权的基础上，建立两国外交关系，并请缅甸联邦政府派遣代表前来北京，就中缅两国建立外交关系的初步与程序问题进行谈判。四月二十六日缅甸联邦政府派遣的谈判代表吴辟临时代办到达北京，随即开始进行此项谈判。现谈判已顺利结束。中缅两国政府并已同意：缅甸联邦政府派遣吴敏登先生为驻中华人民共和国大使，中华人民共和国中央人民政府派遣姚仲明先生为中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦大使。
    【新华社八日讯】瑞典驻我国首任特命全权大使阿马斯顿于今日下午二时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，就呈递国书问题有所商谈。在座者尚有欧非司司长宦乡、副司长温朋久、交际处处长王倬如。
