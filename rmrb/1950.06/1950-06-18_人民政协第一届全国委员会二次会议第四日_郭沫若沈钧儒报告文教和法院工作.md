Title: The Fourth Day of the Second Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC: Guo Moruo and Shen Junru Report on Cultural, Educational, and Court Work

Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 1

　　The Second Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, Day Four
　　Guo Moruo and Shen Junru Report on Cultural, Educational, and Court Work
　　The conference enters the discussion phase. Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Huang Yanpei, and Xi Zhongxun speak, expressing unanimous satisfaction with the reports.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 17th] The Second Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC continued group discussions on land reform this morning. This afternoon, a plenary session was held to hear the report on cultural and educational work by Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, and the report on court work by Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee and President of the Supreme People's Court. The conference then entered the discussion phase. Today, those who spoke at the conference included Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League; Huang Yanpei, Convener of the Standing Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association; and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee. The speakers unanimously expressed satisfaction with all the reports.
　　Tomorrow (the 18th) is Sunday, and the meeting will be adjourned for one day.

### 人民政协第一届全国委员会二次会议第四日  郭沫若沈钧儒报告文教和法院工作

1950-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协第一届全国委员会二次会议第四日
    郭沫若沈钧儒报告文教和法院工作
    大会进入讨论阶段，李济深张澜黄炎培习仲勋发言，对各项报告一致表示满意。
    【新华社十七日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议今日上午继续分组讨论土地改革问题。下午举行大会，听取全国委员会副主席、政务院文化教育委员会主任郭沫若的文教工作报告，全国委员会副主席、最高人民法院院长沈钧儒的法院工作报告。大会随即进入讨论阶段。今日在大会上发言的有中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、中国民主同盟主席张澜、中国民主建国会常务委员会召集人黄炎培、西北军政委员会副主席习仲勋。发言的人对各项报告一致表示满意。
    明日（十八日）为星期日，休会一天。
