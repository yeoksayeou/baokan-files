Title: The Government Administration Council's Thirty-Seventh Meeting Listens to Work Reports from Xinjiang and Other Provinces
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th—The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its thirty-seventh meeting at 3:00 PM on the 26th. The meeting, after hearing work reports from Burhan Shahidi, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, Zhao Shoushan, Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, and Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Government, discussed and approved the aforementioned work reports. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第三十七次会议听取新疆等省工作报告

1950-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第三十七次会议听取新疆等省工作报告
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第三十七次政务会议于二十六日下午三时举行。会议在听取了新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉，青海省人民政府主席赵寿山，甘肃省人民政府主席邓宝珊关于各该省的工作报告之后，讨论并同意了以上各项的工作报告。此外，会议通过任命及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
