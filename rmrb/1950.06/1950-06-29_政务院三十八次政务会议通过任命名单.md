Title: List of Appointments Approved by the 38th Government Affairs Council of the Government Administration Council
Author:
Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The following is the list of appointments approved by the 38th Government Affairs Council of the Government Administration Council:

　　List of Staff of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government
　　
　　Secretary-General: Zhou Lin, formerly Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government.
　　
　　Civil Affairs Bureau
　　Director: Cao Manzhi, currently Deputy Director of the Political Affairs Takeover Committee of the Shanghai Military Control Commission.
　　Deputy Director: Huang Xuzhou, formerly Chief of Staff of the Jinan Garrison Command in Shandong Province.
　　
　　Public Security Bureau
　　Director: Yang Fan, currently Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau.
　　Deputy Director: Yang Guangchi, currently Political Commissar of the Shanghai Municipal People's Police Force.
　　
　　District Government Guidance Office
　　Director: Cao Manzhi, currently Deputy Director of the Political Affairs Takeover Committee of the Shanghai Military Control Commission.
　　
　　Finance Bureau
　　Director: Gu Zhun, currently a member of the Financial and Economic Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　Deputy Director: Zhu Ruyan, formerly Deputy Director of the Finance Bureau of the Jinan Municipal People's Government in Shandong Province.
　　
　　Industry and Commerce Bureau
　　Director: Xu Dixin, currently Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　Deputy Director: Cai Beihua, economic worker.
　　　　　　　　　　Yang Yanxiu, formerly Deputy Director of the Industry and Commerce Department of the Shanghai Military Control Commission.
　　
　　Public Works Bureau
　　Director: Zhao Zukang, former Director of the Shanghai Municipal Public Works Bureau of the Kuomintang government.
　　Deputy Director: Wang Jiqi, formerly an engineer at China United Engineering Corporation.
　　　　　　　　　　Xu Yifang, formerly Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Public Works Bureau.
　　
　　Labor Bureau
　　Director: Ma Chungu, formerly Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.
　　Deputy Director: Xu Zhouliang, formerly Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau.
　　
　　Land Administration Bureau
　　Director: Wang Weiheng, formerly Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Direct Tax Bureau.
　　Deputy Director: Xie Zhuke, formerly a receiving specialist at the Land Administration Bureau of the Shanghai Military Control Commission.
　　
　　Tax Bureau
　　Director: Gu Zhun, currently a member of the Financial and Economic Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　Deputy Director: Zhu Ruyan, formerly Deputy Director of the Finance Bureau of the Jinan Municipal People's Government in Shandong Province.
　　　　　　　　　　Wang Jihua, formerly Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Tax Bureau.
　　　　　　　　　　Wang Liang, formerly Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Goods Tax Bureau.
　　
　　Public Utilities Bureau
　　Director: Ye Jinming, formerly Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Public Utilities Bureau.
　　
　　Public Housing Management Office
　　Director: Chen Naichang, formerly Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Public Housing Management Office.
　　
　　Culture Bureau
　　Director: Xia Yan, currently a member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　Deputy Director: Yu Ling, currently a member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　　　　　　　　　Liu Simu, currently Editor-in-Chief of the Shanghai News.
　　
　　Education Bureau
　　Director: Dai Baitao, currently a member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　
　　Health Bureau
　　Director: Cui Yitian, currently a member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　Deputy Director: Li Musheng, formerly Director of the Health Department of the Shanghai Military Control Commission.
　　
　　News and Publication Office
　　Director: Yao Qin, currently Director of the Propaganda Department of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.
