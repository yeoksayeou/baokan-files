Title: Peace Preservation Propaganda Troupe Departs Guangzhou; China Peace Committee Hubei Provincial Branch Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 17th. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Propaganda Troupe, including Xiao San, Ai Qing, Zhao Zhongchi, Huang Zhensheng, and Bai Yun, departed Guangzhou on the morning of the 17th. After arriving in Guangzhou on the 10th, the propaganda troupe held a forum with representatives from all walks of life to understand the progress of the peace signature campaign in Guangzhou, and separately conducted propaganda among workers, women, cultural and artistic circles, as well as Southern University, Zhongshan University, and Lingnan University in the city. Bai Yun, the Chinese executive representative who came from Beijing to participate in the work of the propaganda troupe and attended the Ninth Executive Committee meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation in Finland, conveyed the situation of the Women's International Democratic Federation's implementation of the World Peace Congress Third Standing Committee resolution and the decisions of the Women's International Democratic Federation at the welcome meeting of the Guangzhou Municipal Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee. The propaganda troupe's seven-day work in Guangzhou has further promoted the peace signature campaign.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 13th. The Hubei Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace has been officially established on the 6th of this month. Xiong Jinhuai, Vice Chairman of the Hubei Provincial People's Government, was elected as the Chairman of the branch.

### 保卫和平宣传团离穗  和大湖北省分会正式成立

1950-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平宣传团离穗  和大湖北省分会正式成立
    【新华社广州十七日电】中国保卫世界和平宣传团萧三、艾青、赵仲池、黄振声、白芸等已于十七日晨离穗。宣传团于十日抵穗后，即与各界代表举行座谈会，了解广州市和平签名运动进展情况，并分头对该市工人、妇女、文化艺术界及南方大学、中山大学、岭南大学进行宣传。由京赶来参加宣传团工作的、曾出席芬兰国际民主妇联第九次执委会会议的中国执委代表白芸，在广州市民主妇联筹委会的欢迎会上传达了国际民主妇联执行世界保卫和平大会第三次常设委员会决议的情况与国际民主妇联的决定。宣传团在穗工作七日，已进一步推动了和平签名运动。
    【新华社汉口十三日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会湖北省分会，已于本月六日正式成立。湖北省人民政府副主席熊晋槐被推选为该会主任委员。
