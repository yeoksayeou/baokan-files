Title: Danish Minister to China, Mr. Munk, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: The first Danish Minister Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Munk, accompanied by his secretary, Mr. Mejstrup, arrived in Beijing by car at 7:55 this morning. Those welcoming him at the station included Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Section Chief Han Xu, Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Armashton, Burmese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Mr. Wu Bi, Hungarian Embassy Counselor, Mr. Fekete, First Secretary of the Indian Embassy, Mr. Yan Ye Sen, Second Secretary of the Soviet Embassy, Mr. Jabhou, Second Secretary of the Polish Embassy, Mr. Dai Peilo Xinski, Third Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy, Mr. Luo Dai, and Norwegian government representative, Mr. Gaolan.

### 丹麦驻华公使穆克抵京

1950-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　丹麦驻华公使穆克抵京
    【新华社十六日讯】丹麦驻我国首任特命全权公使穆克，偕秘书迈思妥于今晨七时五十五分乘车抵京，到站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如、科长韩叙，北京市人民政府外侨事务处副处长马振武，瑞典驻华大使阿马斯顿，缅甸大使馆临时代办吴辟，匈牙利大使馆参赞费凯尔特，印度大使馆一等秘书雁谒森，苏联大使馆二等秘书贾布候，波兰大使馆二等秘书戴裴罗辛斯基，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆三等秘书罗戴，挪威政府代表高兰等。
