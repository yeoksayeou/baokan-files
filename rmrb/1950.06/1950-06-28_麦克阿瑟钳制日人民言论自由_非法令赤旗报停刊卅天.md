Title: MacArthur Restricts Freedom of Speech of the Japanese People; Illegally Orders 30-Day Suspension of Publication for *Akahata*

Author:
Date: 1950-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: MacArthur, again violating the provisions in the Potsdam Declaration and the Far Eastern Commission's basic policy toward Japan regarding guaranteeing freedom of speech for the Japanese people, on the 26th, using the pretext of the Japanese Communist Party's organ *Akahata*'s so-called "one-sided reporting" of the current situation in Korea, instructed the Yoshida government to order *Akahata* to suspend publication for thirty days.

### 麦克阿瑟钳制日人民言论自由  非法令赤旗报停刊卅天

1950-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟钳制日人民言论自由
    非法令赤旗报停刊卅天
    【新华社二十七日讯】麦克阿瑟再次违犯波茨坦宣言及远东委员会决议的对日基本政策中关于保障日本人民言论自由的规定，竟于二十六日以日本共产党机关报赤旗报所谓“片面报道”目前朝鲜形势为借口，指令吉田政府勒令赤旗报停刊三十天。
