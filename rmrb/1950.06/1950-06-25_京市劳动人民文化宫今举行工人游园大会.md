Title: Beijing Municipal Labor People's Cultural Palace to Hold Workers' Garden Party Today
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1

Beijing Municipal Labor People's Cultural Palace to Hold Workers' Garden Party Today

[Report from this newspaper] The Beijing Municipal Labor People's Cultural Palace will hold a workers' garden party today (the 25th). The general public will not be admitted for sightseeing for one day. The event will begin at 8:30 AM. The east, west, and south gates of the Cultural Palace will open at 7:00 AM. All those participating in the garden party must enter with a ticket. The movie will be screened starting at 8:00 PM. Those holding movie tickets will only be allowed to enter after 6:00 PM.

### 京市劳动人民文化宫今举行工人游园大会

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市劳动人民文化宫今举行工人游园大会
    【本报讯】京市劳动人民文化宫今（二十五）日举行工人游园联欢大会，一般游人停止游览一日。大会活动自早八点半开始，文化宫之东、西、南三门自早七时开放，参加游园者一律凭票入内。电影自晚八时放映，持有电影票者自下午六时方准入内。
