Title: Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Amasden, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th:
　　The first Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Amasden, accompanied by three members of his staff, Ma Muming, Section Chief of the Foreign Affairs Section of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government Foreign Affairs Office, and Mr. Goroos, the Swedish Chargé d'affaires ad interim, arrived in Beijing at 6:25 PM today. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Ma Wenbo, Chief of Section representing Tang Yanjie, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, Fedlin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, Birochi, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy, Simoni, Counselor of the Hungarian Legation, Mentianu, First Secretary of the Romanian Embassy, Yan Yejiesen, First Secretary of the Indian Embassy, Rodai, Third Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy, Hou Weimin, Representative of the Dutch Government, Golan, Representative of the Norwegian Government, and Mr. Golay Kahn, staff member of the Representative of the British Government.

### 瑞典驻我国大使  阿马斯顿昨抵京

1950-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞典驻我国大使  阿马斯顿昨抵京
    【新华社六日讯】瑞典驻我国首任特命全权大使阿马斯顿及随员等一行三人，由天津市人民政府外侨事务处交际科马牧鸣科长及瑞典临时代办高罗士陪同，于今日下午六时二十五分抵京。赴站欢迎者有我外交部办公厅主任王炳南、欧非司司长宦乡、交际处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部参谋长唐延杰代表马文波处长，苏联大使馆参赞费德林，波兰大使馆代办毕罗奇，匈牙利公使馆参赞希蒙尼，罗马尼亚大使馆一等秘书孟天努，印度大使馆一等秘书雁谒森，捷克大使馆三等秘书罗戴，荷兰政府代表侯卫民，挪威政府代表高兰及英国政府代表胡阶森之随员高来含等。
