Title: Philippines Issues New Immigration Law Restricting Entry of Our Overseas Chinese
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: According to an Associated Press dispatch from Manila: Philippine President Quirino signed the "Philippine Immigration Act of 1950" on the 12th, stipulating that, with the exception of the United States, which still retains an annual quota of 1,200 immigrants, the annual immigration limit for any other country to the Philippines is reduced from 500 to 50. Philippine authorities make no secret of the fact that the main purpose of this bill is to restrict the entry of overseas Chinese.

### 菲又颁新移民法限制我侨胞入境

1950-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲又颁新移民法  限制我侨胞入境
    【新华社十七日讯】据美联社马尼拉讯：菲律宾总统季里诺于十二日签署一项“一九五○年菲律宾移民法案”，规定除美国仍保留每年一千二百名人口名额外，任何其它国家到菲律宾的每年移民限额，由五百名减至五十名。菲律宾当局毫不隐讳该法案主要目的，在于限制华侨的入境。
