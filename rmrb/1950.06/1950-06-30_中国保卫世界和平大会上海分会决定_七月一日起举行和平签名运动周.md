Title: Shanghai Branch of the China Association for Defending World Peace Decides to Launch Peace Signature Campaign Week Starting July 1st
Author:
Date: 1950-06-30
Page: 3

　　China Association for Defending World Peace Shanghai Branch Decides
    To Launch Peace Signature Campaign Week Starting July 1st
    And on behalf of Shanghai's five million people, supports the international protest on July 4th, demanding the U.S. government release anti-fascist leaders.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 28th—The Shanghai Branch of the China Association for Defending World Peace Committee, in response to the call of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to launch a nationwide peace signature campaign, has decided to hold a Peace Signature Campaign Week starting July 1st. In order to deepen and broaden the peace signature campaign, the association has decided to extend the signature period until July 14th. During this period, factories, government offices, organizations, and democratic parties throughout the city will combine the commemorative activities of "July 1st" and "July 7th" to hold mass rallies on a selected day, extensively launching the peace signature campaign. They will also vigorously carry out publicity and education work through newspapers, magazines, radio, movies, dramas, literature and art, and lectures. The association also requires each member of various groups and organizations to conduct in-depth publicity to at least three to five unorganized people, so that they sign the Peace Declaration, with the aim of collecting two million signatures throughout the city.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th—The Shanghai Branch of the China Association for Defending World Peace Committee, on the 26th, on behalf of the five million peace-loving citizens of Shanghai, sent a telegram to Helen Brayan, Executive Secretary of the "United Committee of Anti-Fascist Refugees" in New York, supporting the committee's call to hold an international protest on July 4th, demanding that the U.S. government release the committee's chairman, Baresky, and eleven other anti-fascists; at the same time, it protested the U.S. government's crimes of oppressing peace fighters. The telegram stated: We warmly support your committee's call to hold an international protest on July 4th demanding the release of your committee chairman Baresky and eleven other anti-fascists. On behalf of the five million peace-loving people of Shanghai, we demand the immediate restoration of the freedom of the eleven anti-fascist leaders, and protest the U.S. government's crimes of oppressing peace fighters.

### 中国保卫世界和平大会上海分会决定  七月一日起举行和平签名运动周

1950-06-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国保卫世界和平大会上海分会决定
    七月一日起举行和平签名运动周
    并代表上海五百万人民，支持七月四日国际抗议，要求美政府释放反法西斯领袖。
    【新华社上海二十八日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会上海分会为响应中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会发动全国人民展开和平签名运动的号召，已决定于七月一日起举行和平签名运动周。该会为求和平签名运动的深入和广泛展开，并决定签名日期延长至七月十四日为止。在此期间，全市各工厂、机关、团体、民主党派均将结合“七一”和“七七”的纪念活动，择定一日举行群众大会，广泛地发动和平签名运动，并将通过报纸、杂志、广播、电影、戏剧、文艺以及讲演等方式，大力展开宣传教育工作。该会并要求各团体、组织的每个人员至少向三个至五个无组织的人民，进行深入的宣传，使他们在和平宣言上签名，以期达到全市征集二百万人签名的目标。
    【新华社上海二十七日电】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会上海分会，二十六日代表上海市五百万和平市民，致电纽约“反法西斯流亡者联合委员会”执行秘书海兰·布里安，支持该委员会发起的于七月四日举行国际抗议，要求美国政府释放该委员会主席巴尔斯基等十一位反法西斯人士的号召；同时，并对美国政府压迫和平战士的罪行表示抗议。电文称：贵委员会发起于七月四日举行国际抗议，要求释放贵委员会主席巴尔斯基等十一位反法西斯人士，我们表示热烈拥护。兹代表上海五百万爱好和平人民，要求立即恢复十一位反法西斯领袖的自由，并抗议美国政府压迫和平战士的罪行。
