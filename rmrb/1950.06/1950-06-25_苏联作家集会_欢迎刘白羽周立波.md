Title: Soviet Writers Gather to Welcome Liu Baiyu and Zhou Libo
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd: Soviet writers gathered on the 21st to welcome the Chinese writers Liu Baiyu and Zhou Libo who recently visited the Soviet Union. Soviet writers introduced them to the organization of the Soviet Writers' Union and the work of its various departments and committees; and talked about the Soviet Union's publishing plans for translating Chinese literary classics this year. This year, in addition to publishing two volumes of Lu Xun's selected works, the Soviet Union will also publish Cao Ming's novel "The Motivator", Zhou Libo's novel "Hurricane", and a selection of Chinese poetry. These works enjoy high reputation among Soviet readers.

### 苏联作家集会  欢迎刘白羽周立波

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联作家集会
    欢迎刘白羽周立波
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联作家于二十一日集会欢迎最近访苏的中国作家刘白羽和周立波。苏联作家向他们介绍了关于苏联作家协会的组织，和它的各部各委员会工作的概况；并谈到苏联今年翻译中国文学名著的出版计划。今年，苏联除了出版鲁迅的选集两卷以外，并将出版草明的长篇小说《原动力》，周立波的小说《暴风骤雨》，和中国的诗歌选集。这些作品在苏联读者中都享有很高的声誉。
