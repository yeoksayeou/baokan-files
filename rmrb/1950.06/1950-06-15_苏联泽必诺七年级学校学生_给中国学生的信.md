Title: Letter from Students of the Soviet Zebino 7-Year School to Chinese Students
Author:
Date: 1950-06-15
Page: 4

People's Forum

　　Letter from Students of the Soviet Zebino 7-Year School
　　to Chinese Students
　　Dear friends:
　　We love your country—China. The Chinese people are our best friends. The Chinese people and the Soviet people are closely connected. Because the Chinese people have waged a resolute struggle for their freedom and independence, and have built a new life and happiness for all working people. We would like to tell you how we live and study. In our country, universal seven-year education is implemented. Our education is free, and textbooks and stationery are also provided by the state. Our school is clean and bright. There are ninety students in our school, including boys and girls. Most of us are members of the Communist Youth League and Young Pioneers. We study hard and strictly observe discipline, actively participating in social life. In summer, we help our collective farm work; cultivating crops and harvesting grains. Currently, we are fighting to eliminate drought. This plan was formulated by our leader, Comrade Stalin. Here, due to the dry wind blowing from the southeast, there are sometimes poor harvests. Our people have decided to block this wind path and plant forest shelterbelts throughout the southeastern part of the Soviet Union. Last year, in order to plant trees, we collected 12,500 wild fruit trees, 3.5 quintals of oak seeds, and 4.5 quintals of pine cones.
　　In order to become builders of communism in the near future, we are studying various sciences in school. In addition to general science, we also study biology. Our Soviet biology is based on the theory of the great nature transformer—Michurin. Biology teaches us to cultivate new plants that have never existed in nature, and teaches us knowledge to obtain abundant agricultural harvests. In our school, we have our own learning experimental area. Here, we plant all kinds of crops, and transplant nurseries and new varieties of beneficial plants.
　　In school, we not only study science, but we also engage in strengthening our physical fitness: we ski, practice on horizontal bars, and do obstacle course races. We really like sports. In our spare time, we play chess, Chinese chess, and cards. We read all kinds of interesting books, newspapers, and magazines. We also participate in the recreational activities of the collective farm club.
　　On March 12th this year, our district held elections for the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. We did not participate in the election this time because we are not yet eighteen years old.
　　What we like is that the Chinese people are fighting alongside us to build a happy, joyful, and free life. What we like even more is that your government and our country have signed the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, and have established friendly relations.
　　We will help each other. We will fight for world peace. We would be even happier if you could write to us about your life, work, and studies. We extend our warm greetings to you, to your people, and to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China headed by Chairman Mao Zedong!
　　Entrusted by the school meeting, we sign this letter:
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Principal Treinin
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Communist Youth League Secretary Bochuzhin
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Young Pioneer Instructor Azhiva
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the School Affairs Committee Zhivoglazov
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Excellent Young Pioneer Kuznetsova

　　Editor's note: This is a letter from students of the Zebino 7-Year School in the Ratov Oblast of the Soviet Union to Chinese students, forwarded by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. This letter is full of warm friendship, hoping that our students will correspond with them and report on some of their study and life situations. Because they are about to have their summer vacation, it is best to send the replies written by our students to the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at Yuheqiao No. 2 before June 17th for collection and forwarding.

### 苏联泽必诺七年级学校学生  给中国学生的信

1950-06-15
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　苏联泽必诺七年级学校学生
    给中国学生的信亲爱的朋友们：
    我们爱你们的国家——中国。中国人民是我们最好的朋友。中国人民和苏联人民是亲密地联系着的。因为中国人民为自己的自由和独立曾进行了坚决的斗争，为全体劳动人民建设了新的生活和幸福。我们愿意告诉你们，我们是如何地生活着和学习着。在我国实行着普遍的七年教育。我们学习是免费的，教科书和文具也是由国家发给我们的。我们的学校清洁又明亮。我们学校里有九十个学生，其中有男孩子和女孩子。我们中的大多数是共青团员和少先队员。我们努力地学习着。并严格遵守纪律，积极参加社会生活。夏天，我们帮助自己的集体农场工作；耕种作物，收割庄稼。目前我们正在为消灭旱灾而斗争。这个计划是由我们的领袖斯大林同志所制定的。在我们这里，由于从东南方吹来的干风，有时发生歉收。我们人民已决定了要把这股风道给遮断，在苏联东南部的全部国土上种植森林防护带。在去年，为了栽种林木，我们曾搜集了一万二千五百棵野生果树、三·五公担樫树种子、四·五公担松球。
    为了在不久的将来成为共产主义的建设者，我们在学校里正在研究着各种科学。除掉一般科学外，我们还学习生物学。我们苏联的生物学是以伟大的自然改造者——米邱林的学说为基础的。生物学教给我们培养出来在自然界里从来所没有过的新的植物，教给我们获得丰富农产收获的知识。在我们的学校里有自己的学习实验区。在这里，我们种植着各式各样的作物，又移植苗圃和新的有益植物的品种。
    在学校里，我们不仅研究科学，我们更从事锻炼我们的体力：我们滑雪、练习铁杠、作障碍物竞走。我们非常喜欢体育。在课余时间我们玩西洋棋、象棋、纸牌。我们阅读各种有趣味的书籍、报纸、杂志。集体农庄俱乐部的文娱活动，我们也去参加。
    今年三月十二日，我们区里举行了苏联最高苏维埃的选举。这次我们没有参加选举，因为我们的年龄还不到十八岁。
    我们喜欢的是，中国人民和我们一道为建设幸福、快乐、自由的生活而斗争着。我们更喜欢的是，你们的政府和我们的国家已签订了中苏友好同盟互助条约，并且建立了友谊的关系。
    我们将要互相帮助。我们将为世界和平而进行斗争。如果你们能将自己的生活、工作和学习情形写给我们，我们将更加高兴。我们向您们，向您们的人民，向以毛泽东主席为首的中华人民共和国中央人民政府致以热烈的敬礼！
    我们受全校会议的委托，在这封信上签名：
                                        校长  特雷宁
                                        共青团书记  波楚金
                                        少先队指导员  阿吉瓦
                              校务委员会主席  日沃格拉作夫
                                  优秀的少先队员  库兹涅错娃
    编者按：这是中苏友协总会转来的苏联拉托夫州泽必诺七年级学校学生给中国学生的一封信。这封信里充满着热情的友爱，希望我国学生和他们通信，报告一些学习与生活的情况。因为他们快要放暑假了，所以我国学生所写的回信，最好在六月十七日以前寄交御河桥二号北京市中苏友协宣传部，以便汇集转发。
