Title: Great Sino-Soviet Youth Meeting Strengthens Friendship Between the Two Peoples; Soviet Youth Delegation Members Report on Their Visit to China

Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 22nd: Members of the Soviet Youth Delegation who visited the new China last month are now meeting with Soviet youth across the country, reporting on their experiences in the new China and the life and work of Chinese youth. They are also reporting on how the Sino-Soviet youth meeting has become a moving demonstration of the invincible friendship between the two great peoples.

In Kiev, Xie Weili, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Youth League, recounted the heroic struggle of the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader Mao Zedong, to win liberation, and the labor enthusiasm of Chinese youth in participating in the construction of a new life. Enthusiastic gatherings were also held in Minsk and Tbilisi. Many Stakhanovite workers, labor heroes, farmers, and students unanimously expressed their determination to strive for the permanent friendship of the Chinese people.

### 中苏青年伟大会晤加强两国人民友谊  苏青年代表团团员报告访华见闻

1950-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　中苏青年伟大会晤加强两国人民友谊
    苏青年代表团团员报告访华见闻
    【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】月前访问新中国的苏联青年代表团团员，现正在国内和苏联青年开会，向他们报告在新中国的见闻及中国青年的生活和工作，报告中苏青年的会晤，如何成为两国伟大人民的不可战胜的友谊的动人示威。
    在基辅，乌克兰共产主义青年团中委会书记谢维里，讲述了中国人民在中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东的领导下争取解放的英勇斗争，和中国青年参加建设新生活的劳动热情。在明斯克，在第比利斯，青年们也热烈集会。许多斯塔哈诺夫式工人、劳动英雄、农民和学生，一致表示要争取对中国人民的永久友谊。
