Title: Beijing Municipal Literary and Art Workers Representative Assembly Sends a Message to the World Congress of Defenders of Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 2

　　Beijing Municipal Literary and Art Workers Representative Assembly
    Sends a Message to the World Congress of Defenders of Peace
    [Beijing Municipal News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal Literary and Art Workers Representative Assembly sent a message to the World Congress of Defenders of Peace. The full text is as follows:
    We, all the literary and art workers of Beijing, demand the unconditional prohibition of the use of atomic weapons to engage in destructive warfare. We respond to the just call of the World Peace Congress and are willing to use all our strength to shatter the conspiracies of these international warmongers! We declare: Whoever dares to use atomic weapons first against any country is an enemy of all mankind and a war criminal!
    This is our oath. We call on all peace-loving people in the city to respond to this call to defend world peace, led by the Soviet Union!
    Long live world peace!
    Long live the victory of peace!

### 京市文艺工作者代表大会  致电世界拥护和平大会

1950-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　京市文艺工作者代表大会
    致电世界拥护和平大会
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市文艺工作者代表大会致世界拥护和平大会电，全文如下：
    我们全北京市文艺工作者要求无条件地禁止使用原子武器从事毁灭性的战争。我们响应世界和平大会的正义号召，愿用一切力量，粉碎这些国际战争贩子的阴谋！我们宣布：谁敢首先使用原子武器去反对任何国家，谁就是全人类的敌人，就是战争罪犯！
    这就是我们的誓词，我们号召全市爱好和平的人们，响应这以苏联为首的保卫世界和平的号召！
    世界和平万岁！
    和平胜利万岁！
