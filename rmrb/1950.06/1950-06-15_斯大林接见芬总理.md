Title: Stalin Receives Finnish Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1950-06-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 14th: TASS, Moscow: Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Stalin, received Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen on the 13th. Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Gromyko, was also present at the meeting.

　　Kekkonen arrived in Moscow on June 9th. According to Kekkonen's statement upon disembarking, his arrival in Moscow is related to the signing of a Soviet-Finnish trade agreement. Kekkonen visited Lenin's Mausoleum on the 12th and attended the opening ceremony of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 斯大林接见芬总理

1950-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　斯大林接见芬总理
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席斯大林于十三日接见芬兰总理吉科宁。接见时，苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯亦在座。
    吉科宁系于六月九日抵达莫斯科。据吉科宁在下车时发表的声明，他这次到达莫斯科是与签订苏芬贸易协定有关。吉科宁曾于十二日晋谒列宁墓，并出席了苏联最高苏维埃大会的开幕式。
