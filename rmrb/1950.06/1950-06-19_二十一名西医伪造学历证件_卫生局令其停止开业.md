Title: Twenty-one Western Medicine Practitioners Falsified Academic Credentials; Health Bureau Orders Closure
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　Twenty-one Western Medicine Practitioners Falsified Academic Credentials
　　Health Bureau Orders Closure

　　[Beijing News Agency] The Municipal Public Health Bureau discovered that twenty-one individuals (all Western medicine practitioners) had falsified academic credentials to fraudulently obtain medical personnel practice licenses. They have been dealt with according to the circumstances. Li Zixiang (No. 6, Qian Waitongzi Hutong) has been suspended from practice. The remaining twenty have confessed their mistakes to the Bureau and have been suspended from practice until they pass the upcoming medical personnel examination to obtain qualifications. The list is as follows: Hou Litian (No. 37, Fuxue Hutong), Zhao Shaoliang (No. 6, Daxihutong, Jinshifang Street), Zhao Fengqi (Henan Street, Mentougou), Tang Gangti (Beixin Hospital, Beixinqiao), Zhai Yisheng (No. 3, East Chunshu Hutong, He Wai), Zhang Zhenyong (Songyan Clinic, No. 69, Dapaifang Hutong, Chaonei), Wang Mingjin (No. 39, West Zhuan Hutong, Guangnei), Bai Yuncheng (No. 50, Di’anmen Wai Avenue), Qi Zhenjiang (No. 105, Bowuyuan Road, Xizhimen Wai), An Xilan (No. 101, North Avenue, Xisi), Jin Shouyi (Liren Clinic, Bowuyuan Road, Xizhimen Wai), Luo Xianting (No. 97, Nanluoguxiang, Nei Wu), Yang Zhaoxin (No. 37, Zhongjie, Sanjiadian, West Suburb), Lü Zeng (No. 8, Songlin Village, West Suburb), Wang Dingwu (No. 18, Chuilu Hutong, Nei Qi), Qiu Wenxian (No. 9, Tan'er Hutong, Qian Wai), Zhao Qingyu (Tongji Pharmacy, West Avenue, Haidian), Wu Rangrong (No. 167, Funei Avenue), Yu Yueren (No. 104, Jia, Yingtao Xiejie, Qian Wai), Zhang Zhentao (No. 98, Civilian Market, Henan Street, 20th District).

### 二十一名西医伪造学历证件  卫生局令其停止开业

1950-06-19
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　二十一名西医伪造学历证件
    卫生局令其停止开业
    【京市新闻处讯】市公共卫生局发现有二十一人（均为西医）伪造学历证件蒙混领取医事人员开业执照，业经分别情况予以处理。其中李子祥（前外桐梓胡同六号）受停止开业处分，其余二十人均已到该局坦白认错，在参加即将举行的医事人员考试取得资格前，停止开业，名单如下：侯立田（府学胡同三十七号），赵少良（锦什坊街大喜胡同六号），赵凤岐（门头沟河南街），唐纲提（北新桥北新医院），翟谊生（和外东椿树胡同三号），张振永（朝内大牌坊胡同六十九号松岩诊所），王铭金（广内西砖胡同三十九号），白云程（地安门外大街五十号），齐振江（西直门外博物院路一○五号），安锡兰（西四北大街一○一号），金守义（西直门外博物院路立仁诊所），罗显庭（内五南锣鼓巷九十七号），杨昭欣（西郊三家店中街三十七号），吕曾（西郊松林村八号），王定武（内七垂露胡同十八号），秋文贤（前外炭儿胡同九号），赵庆余（海甸西大街同济药房），吴让荣（阜内大街一六七号），于悦仁（前外樱桃斜街甲一○四号），张振涛（二十区河南街平民市场九十八号）。
