Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Holds Joint Session of Two Chambers; Shvernik Unanimously Elected Chairman of the Presidium; Approves Current Council of Ministers to Continue Performing Duties
Author:
Date: 1950-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th – TASS reports: The Soviet Supreme Soviet's Council of the Union and Council of Nationalities held a joint session on the 19th in the Kremlin. The session was presided over by Yasnov, Chairman of the Council of the Union.

The session first organized the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, unanimously electing Shvernik as Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, sixteen Vice-Chairmen of the Presidium, and fifteen members, and elected Gorkin as Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet.

The session then proceeded to organize the Soviet government. Following a suggestion by Deputy Khrushchev, the session unanimously approved the activities of the current Soviet Council of Ministers and passed a motion ordering the current Council of Ministers to continue performing its duties of managing the state.

After the two motions were passed, all the delegates and guests celebrated with thunderous applause.

### 苏联最高苏维埃举行两院联席会议  一致选出什维尔尼克为主席团主席  并批准现任部长会议继续执行职责

1950-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃举行两院联席会议
    一致选出什维尔尼克为主席团主席
    并批准现任部长会议继续执行职责
    【新华社二十日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科消息：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院与民族院十九日在克里姆林宫举行联席会议。会议由联盟院主席雅斯诺夫主持。
    会议首先组织了最高苏维埃主席团，一致选出什维尔尼克为最高苏维埃主席团主席，选出主席团副主席十六人及委员十五人，并选出高尔金为最高苏维埃主席团秘书。
    会议继而进行组织苏联政府。按照代表赫鲁舍切夫的建议，会议一致批准苏联现任部长会议的活动，并命令现任部长会议继续执行其管理国家的职责的提案。
    两项议案通过后，全体代表和来宾以暴风雨般的掌声表示庆祝。
