Title: National Signature Count for Peace Exceeds Ten Million

Author: 
Date: 1950-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: According to incomplete statistics, the number of people nationwide who have signed in support of the World Peace Congress declaration has exceeded ten million. The number of signatures in Beijing and Tianjin has exceeded one million in each city, accounting for more than half of the total population of the cities. In Shanghai, as of the end of May, more than 500,000 people had signed. In Chongqing, 930,000 people out of a total population of 1.2 million had signed. In Wuhan, as of the end of May, more than 410,000 people had signed. In Guangzhou, 400,000 people had signed. In Hangzhou, as of the beginning of this month, more than 210,000 people had signed, and in Wuhu, more than 100,000 people had signed. In Xi'an, as of the end of May, more than 250,000 people had signed, and in Fuzhou, more than 150,000 people had signed. Qingdao and Kaifeng each had 100,000 signatures. In Hunan Province, the number of signatories has exceeded 730,000. The total number of signatories in the northeastern provinces and cities has reached more than 3.4 million.

### 和平签名人数全国已逾千万

1950-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　和平签名人数全国已逾千万
    【新华社十九日讯】全国在拥护世界和平大会宣言上签名的人数，据不完全统计，已超过一千万人。北京与天津两市签名人数都已超过一百万人，已占全市人口一半以上。上海据五月底统计已有五十多万人签名。重庆市全市一百二十万人口中已有九十三万人签名。武汉据五月底统计已有四十一万余人签名。广州市已有四十万人签名。杭州市据本月上旬统计，已有二十一万余人签名，芜湖已有十万余人签名。西安于五月底统计已有二十五万人签名，福州已有十五万余人签名，青岛、开封都各有十万人签名。湖南全省签名者已超过七十三万人。东北各省、市签名者合计已达三百四十余万人。
