Title: Dihua City Federation of Trade Unions Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 2

Local Briefs

    Dihua City Federation of Trade Unions Established
    The first Workers' Representative Assembly of Dihua City, lasting four days, concluded on May 20th. The assembly elected Gao Yipin, Maitoudula, and twenty-seven others as members of the executive committee of the City Federation of Trade Unions. Gao Yipin and Maitoudula were elected as chairman and vice-chairman respectively. The Dihua City Federation of Trade Unions was immediately declared established. During the meeting, Jia Tuofu, director of the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, Burhan, chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and Rao Zhengxi, secretary of the CPC Dihua Municipal Committee, attended and delivered speeches. The city has now established twenty-three formal trade unions and thirty-five trade union preparatory committees, with members accounting for approximately 82 percent of the city's more than 12,700 employees.

### 迪化市总工会成立

1950-06-04
第2版()
专栏：各地简讯

    迪化市总工会成立
    迪化市第一次工人代表大会历时四天，已于五月二十日闭幕，大会选出高一品、买他都拉等二十九人为市总工会执行委员会委员，高一品、买他都拉分任正副主任委员。迪化市总工会当即宣告成立。会议期中西北总工会筹委会主任贾拓夫、新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉和中共迪化市委书记饶正锡等均曾出席讲话。该市现已成立了二十三个正式工会和三十五个工会筹备委员会，会员约占全市职工一万二千七百余人的百分之八十二。
