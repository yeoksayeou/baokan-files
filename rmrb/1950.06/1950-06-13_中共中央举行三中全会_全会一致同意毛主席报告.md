Title: CPC Central Committee Holds Third Plenary Session; The Plenary Session Unanimously Approved Chairman Mao's Report
Author:
Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 1

　　CPC Central Committee Holds Third Plenary Session
    The Plenary Session Unanimously Approved Chairman Mao's Report
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China held its Seventh Central Committee's Third Plenary Session in Beijing from June 6th to 9th. Thirty-five members of the CPC Central Committee and twenty-seven alternate members attended the meeting, along with forty-three secretaries of provincial and municipal party committees and other staff. At this meeting, Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a report entitled "Striving for a Fundamental Turn for the Better in the Nation's Financial and Economic Situation." In addition, Liu Shaoqi delivered a report on the land reform issue, Zhou Enlai delivered a report on foreign affairs and united front work, Chen Yun delivered a report on financial and economic work, and Nie Rongzhen delivered a report on military work. The plenary session discussed and unanimously approved Chairman Mao's report and other reports. The plenary session successfully concluded on June 9th.

### 中共中央举行三中全会  全会一致同意毛主席报告

1950-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央举行三中全会
    全会一致同意毛主席报告
    【新华社十二日讯】中国共产党中央委员会六月六日到九日在北京举行了第七届第三次中央全体会议。出席这次会议的有中共中央委员三十五人，候补中央委员二十七人，并有各省市党委书记及其他工作人员四十三人参加会议。在这个会议上，毛泽东主席作了“为争取国家财政经济状况的基本好转而斗争”的报告。此外，刘少奇作了关于土地改革问题的报告，周恩来作了关于外交工作和统一战线工作的报告，陈云作了关于财政经济工作的报告，聂荣臻作了关于军事工作的报告。全会讨论了并一致同意毛主席的报告及其他报告。全会于六月九日完满闭幕。
