Title: People's Bank of China Tianjin Branch Granted 30 Billion Loans Last Month to Support Private Industry and Commerce
Author:
Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 2

　　People's Bank of China Tianjin Branch
    Granted 30 Billion Loans Last Month
    To Support Private Industry and Commerce
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 11th] In order to help private industrial and commercial enterprises that are beneficial to the national economy and people's livelihood to solve the difficulties in capital turnover caused by slow sales of products, the Tianjin Branch of the People's Bank of China focused on issuing loans of 37.5 billion yuan in May, an increase of more than 10 billion yuan compared with April. Among them, industrial loans were 15.2 billion yuan, trade loans were 3.6 billion yuan, inter-port exchange was 12.3 billion yuan, and other loans were 6.4 billion yuan. The targets of industrial loans are mainly industries such as textiles, chemicals, dyes, rubber, steel, hardware, and machinery.

### 人民银行天津分行  上月贷款三百亿  扶助私营工商业

1950-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行天津分行
    上月贷款三百亿
    扶助私营工商业
    【新华社天津十一日电】中国人民银行天津分行为扶助有利于国计民生的私营工商业解决因产品滞销而引起的资金周转等困难，五月份重点发放贷款三百七十五亿元，较四月份增加一百余亿元。其中工业贷款一百五十二亿元，贸易贷款三十六亿元，埠际押汇一百二十三亿元，其他六十四亿元。工业贷款对象主要为纺织、化学、染料、橡胶、钢铁、五金、机器等行业。
