Title: US Imperialist Military Aircraft Constantly Fly Over the Arctic

Author: 
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 4

　　US Imperialist Military Aircraft Constantly Fly Over the Arctic

　　[Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th dispatch] According to TASS, US military aircraft have recently been constantly flying over the Arctic region. The Christian Science Monitor reports that US Air Force aircraft based at Eielson Air Force Base near Fairbanks, Alaska, have flown over the Arctic more than 300 times.

### 美帝军用飞机不断飞临北极

1950-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝军用飞机不断飞临北极
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯：美国军用飞机最近不断飞临北极区上空。基督教科学箴言报载称，阿拉斯加腓尔班克斯城附近爱尔森基地的美国空军飞机，已在北极上空飞行达三百余次。
