Title: Government of Assam Province, India Intensifies Persecution of Progressive Forces, Actually Declares Indian Communist Party Illegal
Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 4

　　New China News Agency, Delhi, June 17th: According to the Indian News Service in Delhi on the 15th, the government of Assam Province, India, has declared the Communist Party of India illegal in the province. Prior to this, under the policy of frantic persecution of progressive forces by the Indian federal government, the Communist Party of India had already been declared illegal in West Bengal Province, Hyderabad State, and the two southern Indian states of Travancore and Cochin.

### 印度阿萨密省政府  加紧迫害进步势力  竟宣布印共为非法

1950-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度阿萨密省政府
    加紧迫害进步势力  竟宣布印共为非法
    【新华社十七日讯】据印度新闻处德里十五日讯：印度阿萨密省政府已宣布印度共产党在该省为非法。在此以前，在印度联邦政府对进步势力实行疯狂迫害的政策下，印度共产党已先后在西孟加拉省、海德拉巴邦和印度南部的特拉凡哥尔和可城两土邦，被宣布为非法。
