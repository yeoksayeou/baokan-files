Title: At the Moscow Youth Activists Congress, Mikhailov Introduces New China, Pointing Out That the Meeting of Chinese and Soviet Youth is a Huge Demonstration of Love and Friendship Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th—At the Moscow City and Moscow Oblast Youth Activists' gathering on the evening of the 7th, held in the Trade Union Building, attendees listened to the report from the Soviet youth delegation that had returned from visiting New China.

The head of the Soviet youth delegation, Mikhailov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League, first described the heroic struggle and great construction undertaken by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader, Mao Zedong. He then went on to introduce in detail the New Democratic Youth League and the production and learning situation of Chinese youth, saying: This great common goal of fighting for the cause of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin unites the Lenin Communist Youth League and the New Democratic Youth League like brothers, and Soviet and Chinese youth are fighting shoulder to shoulder to defend peace.

Mikhailov spoke of the grand occasion of the warm welcome the Soviet youth delegation received in various parts of China, saying: The meeting of Soviet and Chinese youth is a huge demonstration of love and friendship between the two countries. Amidst prolonged and thunderous applause, Mikhailov congratulated Stalin, the Soviet motherland, and the friendship between Chinese and Soviet youth.

The meeting passed a letter of greetings to the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China.

### 莫斯科青年积极分子大会上  米哈依洛夫介绍新中国  指出中苏青年的会见是两国青年亲爱与友好的巨大示威

1950-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科青年积极分子大会上
    米哈依洛夫介绍新中国           
    指出中苏青年的会见是两国青年亲爱与友好的巨大示威
    【新华社莫斯科十一日电】莫斯科市及莫斯科州的青年积极分子于七日晚在工会大厦举行集会，听取访问新中国归来的苏联青年代表团的报告。
    苏联青年代表团团长、列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记米哈依洛夫，首先叙述了中国人民在中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东领导下所作的英雄斗争和巨大建设。他接着在详细地介绍新民主主义青年团和中国青年的生产和学习情况时说：为马恩列斯的事业而斗争的这一伟大的共同目标，把列宁共产主义青年团和新民主主义青年团像兄弟般地团结起来了，苏联青年和中国青年正肩并肩地进行保卫和平的斗争。
    米哈依洛夫谈到苏联青年代表团在中国各地受到热烈欢迎的盛况，他说：苏联和中国两国青年的会见，是两国青年之亲爱和友好的巨大示威。在历久不息的雷动的掌声中，米哈依洛夫为斯大林、为苏维埃祖国、为中苏青年的友谊祝贺。
    会议通过致函向中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会致意。
