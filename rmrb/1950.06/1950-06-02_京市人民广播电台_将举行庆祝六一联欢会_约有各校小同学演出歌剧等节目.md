Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to Hold "June 1st" Celebration Gala, Featuring Opera and Other Performances by Students from Various Schools

Author: 
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 2

　　Beijing People's Broadcasting Station
　　To Hold "June 1st" Celebration Gala
　　Featuring Opera and Other Performances by Students from Various Schools
　　[This newspaper reports] To celebrate the "June 1st" International Children's Day, the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will hold a celebration gathering at 9:00 AM on Sunday, June 4th. At the gathering, elementary school students from various schools will perform wonderful programs such as opera, clapper talk, songs, and dances. The address of the celebration gathering is the auditorium of the Beijing Management College of China Jiaotong University, Fuyou Street. Students from various elementary schools, children from literacy classes, and out-of-school children are welcome to participate. Those who wish to participate should go to the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at No. 3 West Chang'an Street to collect admission tickets.

### 京市人民广播电台  将举行庆祝六一联欢会  约有各校小同学演出歌剧等节目

1950-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　京市人民广播电台
    将举行庆祝六一联欢会    
    约有各校小同学演出歌剧等节目
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台为庆祝“六一”国际儿童节，将于六月四日星期日上午九时，举行联欢大会。会上将由联欢各小学校小朋友们演出歌剧，快板歌舞等精彩节目，联欢会地址在府右街中国交通大学北京管理学院礼堂。欢迎各小学校同学，各识字班儿童及失学儿童参加。欲参加者请至西长安街三号北京市人民广播电台领取入场券。
