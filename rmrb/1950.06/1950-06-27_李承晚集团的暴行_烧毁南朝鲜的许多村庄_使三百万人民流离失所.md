Title: Syngman Rhee Clique's Atrocities: Burning Many Villages in South Korea, Displacing Three Million People
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 1

　　Syngman Rhee Clique's Atrocities
    Burning Many Villages in South Korea
    Displacing Three Million People
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 26th] According to a TASS report from Pyongyang on the 23rd, the Syngman Rhee clique, under the pretext of fighting guerrillas, has been burning many villages in South Korea, leaving residents facing hunger and poverty. According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency, the widespread destruction of villages by the Syngman Rhee followers has resulted in approximately three million people losing their homes and being unable to find work.

### 李承晚集团的暴行  烧毁南朝鲜的许多村庄  使三百万人民流离失所

1950-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　李承晚集团的暴行
    烧毁南朝鲜的许多村庄
    使三百万人民流离失所
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社平壤二十三日讯：李承晚集团以对游击队作战为口实，大肆烧毁了南朝鲜的许多村庄，使居民面临饥饿与贫穷。据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：由于李承晚党羽大肆破坏乡村的结果，已使三百万左右的人民丧失了他们的安身之所，找不到工作。
