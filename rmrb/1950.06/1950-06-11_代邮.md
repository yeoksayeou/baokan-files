Title: Mail for…
Author: Editor
Date: 1950-06-11
Page: 4

To Liu Ruihua, Liu Zhenya, Jin Sha, Reader of Newspapers, and Zeng Dingzhi:

　　Your letters have been forwarded to relevant departments for reference.

    To Feng Jingyuan, Men Shitu, A Group of Shandong Readers in Beijing, Bao Ying, and Zheng Xuehong: Please provide detailed mailing addresses so we can respond to your questions.

    To Liang Xing, Si Tong, Niu Zaixing, Wang Lian, Cai Jie, Chang Ming of the Suburban District of Beijing, Zhang Yinxin of this city, and Li Hongze of the Eighth Middle School:

　　Please come to the People's Garden Group of this newspaper for a discussion so that we can answer your questions.

    To Mr. Zhao Jinzhan:

　　Regarding the matter of private fundraising to operate a farm, the Agricultural Policy Department of the Ministry of Agriculture invites you to come for a discussion.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Editor
