Title: *Pravda* Editorial Calls for Expanding the Peace Movement: Increase People's Signatures by Hundreds of Millions Before the Second World Peace Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: According to TASS, *Pravda* published an editorial on the 8th entitled "The Growing Activities of Peace Fighters," calling on peace fighters to strive for more signatures and further expose the enemies of peace and the helpers of war-mongers.

After pointing out the heavy blow to the war-mongers dealt by the peace signature campaign in which 100 million people have participated, *Pravda* demanded that peace fighters not be complacent with the initial results and must continue to work hard to attract the broadest working people, social leaders, and political leaders in all countries of the world into the struggle to defend peace; to increase the peace signatures of hundreds of millions of people before the opening of the Second World Peace Congress (to be held in October this year).

Parliaments of all countries and parties in parliaments, academies of sciences of all countries and their branches, upper-level trade union central organizations, writers associations of all countries, groups of cultural workers, and other mass organizations—all sincere members of these leading institutions who sincerely seek to consolidate peace must sign the declaration of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee calling for the declaration of the atomic bomb as illegal. Peace advocates demand that those who refuse to sign the peace declaration publicly explain their reasons. Because the people of all countries want to know: which side do scientists and cultural workers, social leaders and political leaders stand on; they want to know: whether they are fighting for peace or helping the makers of war.

Exposing the helpers of war-mongers and subjecting them to the sanction of public opinion is a major task for peace fighters in their current movement. The efforts of some social leaders, political leaders, and intellectuals to adopt a "neutral" attitude on today's fundamental issues can only be regarded as helping the enemies of peace.

*Pravda* called for making the movement for peace and against war instigators more grand in scale, better organized, and more active. Because the struggle for peace and against war-mongers concerns all the people of all countries in the world.

### 真理报社论号召  扩大和平运动规模  二届和大以前要增加亿万人民签名

1950-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　真理报社论号召
    扩大和平运动规模
    二届和大以前要增加亿万人民签名
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报于八日以“和平战士的活动日益增长”为题发表社论，号召和平战士争取更多的签名，进一步揭露和平的敌人，战争贩子的助手。
    真理报在指出已有一万万人参加签名的和平签名运动所给予战争贩子的沉重打击之后，要求和平战士不要自满于初步成果，必须继续努力，把全世界一切国家中最广大的劳动人民、社会领袖和政治领袖都吸引到保卫和平的斗争中来；要在第二次世界拥护和平大会（今年十月举行）开幕以前，增加亿万人民的和平签名。
    各国议会以及在议会中的各党派，各国科学院及其分支，各上层的工会中心组织，各国作家协会，文化工作者的团体以及其他群众团体——这些具有领导作用的机构的一切真诚企求巩固和平的成员，必须在世界和大常委会要求宣布原子弹为非法的宣言上签名。和平拥护者要求那些拒绝在和平宣言上签名的人公开解释他们的理由。因为各国人民要知道：科学家和文化工作者，社会领袖和政治领袖，究竟跟谁站在一起；要知道：他们究竟是在为和平而斗争，还是在帮助战争制造者。
    揭露战争贩子的助手并使他们受到舆论的制裁，这就是和平战士在其当前运动中的重大任务。某些社会领袖、政治领袖和知识分子，企图对今日的根本问题采取“中立”态度的种种努力，不能看作是别的，只能看作是对和平敌人的帮助。
    真理报号召使争取和平、反对战争挑拨者的运动的规模更加宏大，组织更加优良，并加紧活动。因为争取和平，反对战争贩子的斗争，是关系着全世界各国一切人民的。
