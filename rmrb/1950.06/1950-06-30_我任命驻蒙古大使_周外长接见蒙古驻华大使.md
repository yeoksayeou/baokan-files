Title: I Appoint Ambassador to Mongolia, Foreign Minister Zhou Receives Mongolian Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1950-06-30
Page: 4

　　I Appoint Ambassador to Mongolia
    Foreign Minister Zhou Receives Mongolian Ambassador to China
    【Xinhua News Agency, 29th】The Central People's Government of our country has appointed Mr. Ji Yatai as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Mongolian People's Republic.
    【Xinhua News Agency, 29th】Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, received Mr. Jalkhasaikhan (formerly translated as Dyalhkasakhn), the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 6:15 PM on the 29th. Also present were Qiao Guanhua, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 我任命驻蒙古大使  周外长接见蒙古驻华大使

1950-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　我任命驻蒙古大使
    周外长接见蒙古驻华大使
    【新华社二十九日讯】我中央人民政府已任命吉雅泰先生为我国驻蒙古人民共和国首任特命全权大使。
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于二十九日下午六时十五分在外交部接见蒙古人民共和国驻华首任特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗（前译德亚尔卡赛汗）。在座者尚有外交部亚洲司乔冠华代司长，交际处王倬如处长。
