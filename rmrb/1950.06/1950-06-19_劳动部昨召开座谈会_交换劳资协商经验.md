Title: Ministry of Labor Holds Symposium Yesterday to Exchange Labor-Management Negotiation Experiences
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government invited representatives of industrialists and businessmen, representatives of trade unions, and model workers attending the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to a symposium on the 18th. More than 40 representatives attended. Everyone exchanged information and experiences on labor-management consultation meetings in various places. They unanimously agreed that labor-management consultation meetings have played a significant role in overcoming current difficulties and maintaining and developing production, and have greatly improved labor-management relations. The symposium also exchanged views on the issue of dismissals and other issues in current labor-management relations.

### 劳动部昨召开座谈会  交换劳资协商经验

1950-06-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　劳动部昨召开座谈会
    交换劳资协商经验
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府劳动部十八日邀请此次出席中国人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议的工商业者代表和工会代表、劳动模范举行座谈会。到会代表四十余人。大家交换了各地劳资协商会议的情况与经验，一致认为劳资协商会议在克服目前困难、维持生产与发展生产上起了很大作用，使劳资关系获得了很大改善。座谈会对解雇问题及目前劳资关系上的其他问题也交换了意见。
