Title: Important Document for Studying Stalin's Cause and Thought: "Collection of Articles Celebrating Stalin's Seventieth Birthday" Published
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. "Collection of Articles Celebrating Stalin's Seventieth Birthday," edited by the Shishi Congkan (Current Affairs Series) Publishing House of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, was published by Xinhua Bookstore on the 3rd of this month. The content of the collection is divided into three parts. The first part consists of essays written by Stalin's close comrades-in-arms, leaders of the Soviet government and the Communist Party, such as Malenkov, Molotov, Beria, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, and Shvernik. The second part consists of essays written by Chairman Mao, Professor Chen Boda, as well as leaders of the Italian Communist Party Togliatti, leaders of the French Communist Party Thorez, leaders of the British Communist Party Pollitt, leaders of the Hungarian Workers' Party Rakosi, leaders of the Polish United Workers' Party Beirut, and others. The third part consists of essays written by Soviet Marshals Vasilevsky, Budyonny, Malinovsky, Sokolovsky, and others. This collection is one of the important documents for studying Stalin's cause and thought, totaling 250,000 words.

### 学习斯大林的事业和思想的重要文件  《庆祝斯大林七十寿辰文集》出版

1950-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　学习斯大林的事业和思想的重要文件
    《庆祝斯大林七十寿辰文集》出版
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府出版总署时事丛刊社编辑的《庆祝斯大林七十寿辰文集》已于本月三日由新华书店出版。该文集内容共分为三个部分。第一部分是斯大林的亲密战友、苏联政府和联共党的首长马林科夫、莫洛托夫、贝利亚、伏罗希洛夫、米高扬、什维尔尼克等撰写的论文。第二部分是毛主席、陈伯达教授以及意大利共产党领袖托格里亚蒂、法国共产党领袖多列士、英国共产党领袖波立特和匈牙利工人党领袖拉科西、波兰统一工人党领袖贝鲁特等撰写的论文。第三部分是苏联元帅华西列夫斯基、布琼尼、马林诺夫斯基、索科洛夫斯基等撰写的论文。该文集是学习斯大林的事业和思想的重要文件之一，全书达二十五万言。
