Title: The Young Generation in Democratic Germany is Developing to Defend Peace - German Youth Congress Successfully Concludes - Telegram to the World Peace Congress Expressing Firm Support for the Peace Declaration
Author:
Date: 1950-06-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 31st: A comprehensive report from TASS: On May 29th, a grand sports meet was held at the Ulbricht Stadium in Berlin to celebrate the closing of the All-German Youth Congress. Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, Deputy Prime Minister Walter Ulbricht, World Federation of Democratic Youth President Boi Björnsson, International Union of Students President Grohman, Chairman of the Communist Party of West Germany Reimann, and representatives of various foreign delegations attended the stadium.

　　On the same day, the Soviet folk dance troupe led by Moiseyev performed Soviet folk dances in Park Square. In the evening, hundreds of thousands of participants in the All-German Youth Congress held their final meetings in various squares in Berlin, holding high flags and banners. After the meetings, the youth enthusiastically held torchlight parades in the streets of the city, and the citizens of Berlin happily and warmly welcomed these young people.

　　The All-German Youth Peace Fighters Congress held in Berlin on May 27th sent a telegram to the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, declaring unanimous support for the declaration of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee on the prohibition of atomic weapons.

　　The All-German Youth Congress demonstrated to the world that German youth are unwilling to be cannon fodder for the American and British imperialist warmongers. They are willing to work and fight for the interests of the German people and all mankind. At the same time, it also proves that a young generation willing to engage in peaceful and constructive labor and willing to defend the cause of peace is growing and developing in the German Democratic Republic.

### 民主德国保卫和平的青年一代在发展  德青年大会胜利闭幕  致电和大坚决拥护和平宣言

1950-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国保卫和平的青年一代在发展
    德青年大会胜利闭幕
    致电和大坚决拥护和平宣言
    【新华社三十一日讯】综合塔斯社消息：五月二十九日，柏林乌布利希运动场举行了盛大的运动会，庆祝全德青年大会的闭幕。德意志民主共和国总统皮克、副总理乌布利希、世界民主青年联盟主席鲍埃逊、国际学联主席格罗曼、西德共产党主席雷曼以及各外国代表团的代表，均莅临运动场。
    同日，由莫伊塞伊夫率领的苏联民间舞蹈演出队，在公园广场演出苏联民间舞蹈。当天晚间，全德青年大会的数十万参与者，高擎旗帜和标语牌，在柏林各个广场上，举行最后的会议。会后，青年们在市内各街道热烈举行火炬游行，柏林市民均愉快而热情地欢迎这些青年人。
    五月二十七日在柏林举行的全德青年和平战士大会曾致电世界拥护和平大会常设委员会，声明一致拥护世界和大常委会关于禁止原子武器的宣言。
    全德青年大会向全世界表明：德国青年不愿做美英帝国主义战争挑拨者的炮灰，他们愿意为德国人民以及全人类的利益而工作、而斗争。同时，它也证明了愿作和平建设性劳动并愿保卫和平事业的青年一代，正在德意志民主共和国成长着、发展着。
