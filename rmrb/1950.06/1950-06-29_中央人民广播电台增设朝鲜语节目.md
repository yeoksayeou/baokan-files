Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Adds Korean Language Program
Author:
Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: The International Department of the Central People's Broadcasting Station will add a Korean language broadcast program starting July 2nd. The daily broadcast time: 17:30 to 18:00 (Beijing time). Call sign: Beijing Broadcasting Station. Wavelength: 468.7 meters, 640 kilocycles; 416.6 meters, 720 kilocycles; 49.1 meters, 6100 kilocycles; 42.2 meters, 7100 kilocycles; 40 meters, 7500 kilocycles; 33.1 meters, 9040 kilocycles; 30.8 meters, 9730 kilocycles.

### 中央人民广播电台增设朝鲜语节目

1950-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台增设朝鲜语节目
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民广播电台国际台从七月二日起增设朝鲜语广播节目。每天播音时间：十七点三十分到十八点（北京时间）。呼号：北京广播电台。波长：四六八·七公尺，六四○千周；四一六·六公尺，七二○千周；四九·一公尺，六一○○千周；四二·二公尺，七一○○千周；四○公尺，七五○○千周；三三·一公尺，九○四○千周；三○·八公尺，九七三○千周。
