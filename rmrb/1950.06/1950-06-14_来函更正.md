　　Letter of Correction
    In the "Beijing News" article published on June 12 regarding the situation of private industry, it stated that "the China Cotton Yarn Company's Beijing branch supplied a total of 80,831 pieces of yarn." The correct figure should be 80,831 bundles.
    China Cotton Yarn Company Beijing Branch
