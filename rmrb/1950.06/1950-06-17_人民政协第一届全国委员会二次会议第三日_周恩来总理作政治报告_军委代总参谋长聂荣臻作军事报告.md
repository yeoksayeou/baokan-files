Title: The Third Day of the Second Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC: Premier Zhou Enlai Delivers Political Report, Acting Chief of General Staff Nie Rongzhen Delivers Military Report
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a general meeting this afternoon to hear the political report by Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the National Committee and Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government, and the military report by Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission. This morning, group discussions on the land reform issue continued.

### 人民政协第一届全国委员会二次会议第三日  周恩来总理作政治报告  军委代总参谋长聂荣臻作军事报告

1950-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协第一届全国委员会二次会议第三日
    周恩来总理作政治报告
    军委代总参谋长聂荣臻作军事报告
    【新华社十六日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议今日下午举行大会，听取全国委员会副主席、中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来的政治报告，和人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻的军事报告。今天上午继续分组讨论土地改革问题。
