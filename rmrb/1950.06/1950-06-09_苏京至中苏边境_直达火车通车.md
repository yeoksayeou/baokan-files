Title: Direct Train Service Inaugurated Between Soviet Capital and Sino-Soviet Border
Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 3rd: According to a report from the Electric Communications Agency in Moscow on June 1st, a direct train service from Moscow to the Otpor Station on the Sino-Soviet border has been inaugurated. The line is 6,700 kilometers long, and the journey takes approximately six and a half days. The train is equipped with facilities for passenger entertainment.

### 苏京至中苏边境　直达火车通车

1950-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏京至中苏边境
  　直达火车通车
    【新华社三日讯】据电通社莫斯科一日讯：由莫斯科到中苏边境的奥特波尔车站的直达火车，业已通车。该线全长六千七百公里，旅程需时约六天半，车上有供旅客娱乐的设备。
