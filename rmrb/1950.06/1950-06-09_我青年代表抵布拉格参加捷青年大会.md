Title: Our Youth Representatives Arrive in Prague to Attend Czechoslovak Youth Congress
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 4

　　Our Youth Representatives Arrive in Prague to Attend Czechoslovak Youth Congress
    【Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th】Chinese youth representatives Xu Liqun, Wu Xueqian, and Li Guoying arrived in Prague on the 6th from Berlin to attend the Czechoslovak Youth Congress, which opened on the 7th. The congress will last for five days. After the congress, Czech youth will host youth representatives from various countries to visit and tour different places.

### 我青年代表抵布拉格参加捷青年大会

1950-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　我青年代表抵布拉格参加捷青年大会
    【新华社布拉格八日电】中国青年代表许立群、吴学谦和李国英，已于六日自柏林抵达布拉格，参加七日开幕的捷克斯洛伐克青年代表大会。大会会期共五日。会后，捷克青年将招待各国青年代表赴各地参观游览。
