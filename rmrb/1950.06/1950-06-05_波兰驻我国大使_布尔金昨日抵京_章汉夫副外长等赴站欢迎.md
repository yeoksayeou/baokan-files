Title: Polish Ambassador to China, Mr. Burgin, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday; Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Others Welcomed Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1950-06-05
Page: 1

　　Polish Ambassador to China
    Mr. Burgin Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
    Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Others Welcomed Him at the Station

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Mr. Burgin, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Poland to the People's Republic of China, and his party of fifteen people, accompanied by Mr. Zeng Yuanhui, Director of the Liaison Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Manchuria, arrived in Beijing by train at 6:30 this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han; Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Counselor Fedlin, Deputy Military Attache Colonel Duleven, Hungarian Minister Szafranko, Polish Embassy Charge d'Affaires Birowcki, Korean Embassy Counselor Choi Il, Romanian Embassy Counselor Sirad, Indian Embassy First Secretary Yeye Sen, Czechoslovakian Embassy Third Secretary Rodai, and others.

### 波兰驻我国大使  布尔金昨日抵京  章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎

1950-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　波兰驻我国大使
    布尔金昨日抵京
    章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎
    【新华社四日讯】波兰共和国驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权大使布尔金等一行十五人，由外交部驻满洲里联络处曾远辉处长陪同，于今日上午六时三十分乘车抵京。赴站欢迎者有外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅主任王炳南，苏联东欧司司长伍修权，交际处处长王倬如，北京市副市长吴晗；苏联驻华大使罗申，参赞费德林、次席武官杜乐文上校，匈牙利公使夏法朗柯，波兰大使馆代办毕罗奇，朝鲜大使馆参赞崔一，罗马尼亚大使馆参赞席拉德，印度大使馆一等秘书雁谒森，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆三等秘书罗戴等。
