Title: Our Army Continues to Advance in the Wanshan Archipelago, Liberating Islands such as Baili, Big Wanshan, and Small Wanshan

Author:

Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 15th -- Delayed news: The People's Liberation Army, having landed in the Wanshan Archipelago, continued to liberate Baili Island, Big Wanshan Island, Small Wanshan Island, and Zhuzhou Island on the 5th of this month. Remnants of the bandits on each island fled in panic towards Macau after the People's Liberation Army landed. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army occupied Aizhou Island on the 4th.

### 万山群岛我军继续前进  解放白沥大小万山等岛

1950-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　万山群岛我军继续前进
    解放白沥大小万山等岛
    【新华社汉口十五日电】迟到消息：登陆万山群岛的人民解放军，于本月五日继续解放白沥岛、大万山岛、小万山岛、竹州岛。各岛残匪于解放军登陆后仓惶向澳门方向逃窜。另部解放军于四日占领隘州岛。
