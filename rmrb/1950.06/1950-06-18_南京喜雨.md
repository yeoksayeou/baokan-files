Title: Welcome Rain in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 16th. Nanjing and the surrounding areas experienced rainfall around 3:00 PM on the 14th and around 8:00 AM on the 15th, with a total rainfall of 9 millimeters. The Nanjing area had not seen rain for forty days since the beginning of summer. The timely rain has brought immense joy to the farmers in the suburbs, who are rushing to plant soybeans, corn, and other crops that could not be sown due to the lack of rain.

### 南京喜雨

1950-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京喜雨
    【新华社南京十六日电】南京及附近地区在十四日下午三时许和十五日上午八时许降雨，两次共降雨九公厘。南京地区入夏以来已四十日不雨，得此时雨，郊区农民莫不欢欣异常，纷纷抢种因缺雨尚未播种的黄豆和玉米等作物。
