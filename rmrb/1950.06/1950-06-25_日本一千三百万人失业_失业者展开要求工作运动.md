Title: Thirteen Million Unemployed in Japan; Unemployed Launch Movement Demanding Work
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 4

　　Thirteen Million Unemployed in Japan
    Unemployed Launch Movement Demanding Work
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 24th dispatch from Tokyo] TASS reported on June 22nd, citing Tokyo sources: The Japanese Ministry of Labor has released statistics on the number of unemployed nationwide. According to official, greatly reduced estimates, a total of 1.21 million unemployed people requested assistance from labor agencies in finding work in April of this year. However, according to statistics from democratic groups, there are a total of 13 million unemployed people in Japan. The Ministry of Labor's statement specifically emphasized that a mass movement with the slogan "Give us work" is unfolding among the unemployed in Japan.

### 日本一千三百万人失业  失业者展开要求工作运动

1950-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本一千三百万人失业
    失业者展开要求工作运动
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社二十二日电引东京消息：日本劳工省已公布全国失业人数的统计。根据官方大打折扣的估计，今年四月间共有一百二十一万失业者请求各劳工机构协助找寻工作，但根据民主团体的统计，日本共有一千三百万人失业。劳工省的声明中特别着重指出，日本失业者当中正在展开一个以“给我们工作”为口号的群众运动。
