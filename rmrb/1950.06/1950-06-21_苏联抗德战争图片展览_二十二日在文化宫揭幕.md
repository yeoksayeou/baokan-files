Title: Soviet Anti-German War Photo Exhibition Opens at the Cultural Palace on the 22nd
Author:
Date: 1950-06-21
Page: 6

Soviet Anti-German War Photo Exhibition

    Opening at the Cultural Palace on the 22nd. The "Ninth Anniversary of the Soviet Anti-German War" photo exhibition, jointly sponsored by the All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and with the assistance of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in Beijing, is scheduled to officially open on the 22nd. The address is the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. The exhibition content is divided into three parts: ① Socialist industrial construction. ② Soviet rural economy. ③ A brief history of Soviet military construction. The main purpose of this exhibition is to introduce the brilliant achievements of the Soviet Union's post-war recovery and construction, in order to learn from the Soviet Union and enhance the confidence of the broad masses of our people in building the motherland.
  Exhibition time: From June 22nd to July 10th, from 7:00 to 10:00 in the morning, and from 3:00 to 8:00 in the afternoon every day. It is hoped that the broad membership of the Friendship Association and the general public will visit on time.

### 苏联抗德战争图片展览  二十二日在文化宫揭幕

1950-06-21
第6版()
专栏：图片展览

    苏联抗德战争图片展览
    二十二日在文化宫揭幕中苏友好协会总会，北京市中苏友好协会，在北京苏联对外文化协会协助下，联合主办的“苏联抗德战争九周年纪念”图片展览，订于二十二日正式揭幕。地址借北京市劳动人民文化宫。展览内容计分：①社会主义工业建设。②苏联农村经济。③苏联建军简史等三个部分。这个展览会主要是介绍苏联战后复兴与建设的辉煌成就，借以学习苏联，增强我国广大人民建设祖国的信心。
  展览时间：从六月二十二日起至七月十日止，每日上午七时至十时，下午三时至八时。希望友协的广大会员及市民群众准时前往参观。
