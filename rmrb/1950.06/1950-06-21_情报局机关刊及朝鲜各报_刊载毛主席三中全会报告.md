Title: Intelligence Bureau Organ and North Korean Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Report from the Third Plenary Session

Author:

Date: 1950-06-21
Page: 1

　　Intelligence Bureau Organ and North Korean Newspapers
    Publish Chairman Mao's Report from the Third Plenary Session
    [This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: The 84th issue of "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy," published on the 16th, contained the full text of Chairman Mao's report at the Third Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China: "Strive for a Fundamental Turn for the Better in the Nation's Financial and Economic Situation."
    Pyongyang's "Rodong Sinmun," "Democratic Korea," and other newspapers in North Korea all published Chairman Mao Zedong's report at the Third Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in prominent positions on the 17th. The organ of the North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation, "Democratic Overseas Chinese" newspaper, published a special supplement containing the full text of Chairman Mao's report and news of the Third Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee, as well as the statement of the CPC Central Committee on the Japanese situation.

### 情报局机关刊及朝鲜各报  刊载毛主席三中全会报告

1950-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　情报局机关刊及朝鲜各报
    刊载毛主席三中全会报告
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：十六日出版的《争取持久和平，争取人民民主》第八十四期，全文刊载了毛主席在中国共产党第七届第三次中央全体会议上的报告：“为争取国家财政经济状况的基本好转而斗争”。
    朝鲜平壤《劳动新闻》、《民主朝鲜》及其他各报，均于十七日以显著地位全文刊载毛泽东主席在中国共产党第七届中央委员会第三次全体会议上的报告。北朝鲜华侨联合总会机关报《民主华侨》报特出增刊，登载毛主席报告全文和中共中央举行三中全会的消息以及中共中央关于日本情势的声明。
