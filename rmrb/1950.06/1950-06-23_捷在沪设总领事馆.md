Title: Czechoslovakia Establishes Consulate General in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: With the consent of our Central People's Government, the Czechoslovak Embassy in China has established a Consulate General in Shanghai. It officially opened on the 21st of June. Mr. Srimak, the former Czechoslovak Consul General in Budapest, Hungary, has been appointed as Consul General. On the 21st, Srimak, accompanied by his secretary, Ms. Gurkova, paid a formal visit to Mayor Chen Yi of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government.

### 捷在沪设总领事馆

1950-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷在沪设总领事馆
    【新华社二十二日讯】捷克驻我国大使馆征得我中央人民政府同意后，已在上海设立总领事馆，并于二十一日正式开馆办公，捷克前驻匈牙利布达佩斯总领事斯立马克已被任命为总领事。斯立马克于二十一日偕秘书顾尔柯娃赴上海市人民政府向陈毅市长作正式拜会。
