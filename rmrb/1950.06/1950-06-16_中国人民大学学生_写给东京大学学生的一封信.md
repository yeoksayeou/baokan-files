Title: A Letter from Students of Renmin University of China to Students of Tokyo University
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 4

　　People's Garden

　　A Letter from Students of Renmin University of China
    to Students of Tokyo University

    To all students of Tokyo University:

    Seven days after the heroic Japanese people held the "May 30th People's Rally" to oppose American imperialism's colonization and militarization of Japan, MacArthur, the executor of the American imperialist policy of monopolizing Japan, actually instructed the Yoshida puppet government to dismiss 24 members of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party from public office. This vicious attack on the Japanese Communist Party, the loyal defender of Japanese independence, democracy, and peace, thoroughly exposed the hideous face of American imperialism. You bravely stood at the forefront of the Japanese people, calling on students across Japan to strike and protest the atrocities of American imperialism and its running dogs in Japan. Peace-loving people all over the world will fully support your just actions.

    Today, we, the Chinese people, are watching your anti-imperialist struggle for people's democracy with great concern. We are confident that the Japanese people will overcome any difficulties and achieve final victory. All the students of Renmin University of China will always be your loyal comrades-in-arms and resolutely support you.

    Finally, let us shout:

    Long live the solidarity between the Chinese and Japanese people!

    Long live the Japanese people and their leader, the Japanese Communist Party, who are fighting for independence and democracy!
                                 All students of Renmin University of China
                                 June 10, 1950

### 中国人民大学学生  写给东京大学学生的一封信

1950-06-16
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　中国人民大学学生
    写给东京大学学生的一封信东京大学全体同学们：
    在日本人民英勇地举行了“五·三○人民大会”反对美帝国主义把日本殖民地化与军事基地化后的第七天，美国帝国主义独占日本政策的执行者麦克阿瑟，居然指令吉田傀儡政府撤消日本共产党中央委员会二十四位委员的公职。这种对日本独立、民主与和平的忠贞保卫者——日本共产党的恶毒进攻，彻底暴露了美帝国主义的狰狞面目。你们英勇地站在日本人民的前列，号召全日各地同学举行罢课，抗议美国帝国主义及其在日本的走狗的暴行。全世界爱好和平的人民，将全力支援你们这种正义的行动。
    今天，我们中国人民正以无限关切的心情注视着你们争取人民民主的反帝斗争。我们确信：日本人民将会克服任何困难，获得最后的胜利。我们中国人民大学全体同学永远是你们的忠实战友，坚决做你们的后盾。
    最后，让我们高呼：
    中日两国人民团结万岁！
    为争取独立、民主而奋斗的日本人民及其领导者——日本共产党万岁！
                                 中国人民大学全体同学
                                 一九五○年六月十日
