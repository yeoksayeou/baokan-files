Title: Soviet-Romanian Treaty on Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of Border Agreement
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 4

　　Soviet-Romanian Treaty on Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of Border Agreement
    (Xinhua News Agency, June 26th) TASS, Moscow, June 23rd: The Soviet Ambassador to Romania, Kavtaradze, and the Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, exchanged instruments of ratification in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, on the 20th, regarding the treaty on the state of the Soviet-Romanian border. This treaty was signed by the Soviet Government and the Romanian Government in Moscow on November 25th of last year and has been ratified by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on June 3rd of this year and by the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania on February 13th of this year, respectively.
    The Soviet-Romanian border treaty was signed in accordance with Article 1 of the Treaty of Peace with Romania concluded in Paris on September 15, 1947, stipulating the return to the Soviet Union of Bessarabia, which Romania had seized from the Soviet Union after World War I.

### 苏罗互换国界条约批准书

1950-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏罗互换国界条约批准书
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：苏联驻罗马尼亚大使卡夫塔拉兹与罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议副总理乔治斯库，于二十日在罗国首都布加勒斯特交换了关于苏罗国界状况条约的批准证书。是项条约系由苏联政府与罗马尼亚政府于去年十一月二十五日在莫斯科签订，并已先后由苏联最高苏维埃主席团于本年六月三日及由罗马尼亚国民议会主席团于本年二月十三日批准。
    按苏罗国界条约系根据一九四七年九月十五日在巴黎缔结的对罗和约第一条签订，规定将罗马尼亚在第一次世界大战后夺自苏联的比萨拉比亚归还苏联。
