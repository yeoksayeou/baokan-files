Title: Danish Envoy to China, Mr. Moerk, Presents Credentials to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The first Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, Mr. Moerk, accompanied by his secretary, Mr. Mejstrup, presented his credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China at 7:30 PM today. Present at the occasion were Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Protocol of the Central People's Government; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Department of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. During the presentation of his credentials, Envoy Moerk delivered a speech to Chairman Mao Zedong, and Chairman Mao Zedong gave a reply.

Envoy Moerk's speech was as follows:
	Your Excellency, Chairman:
	I have the honor to present to Your Excellency the letter of credence from my monarch, His Majesty the King of Denmark, who is pleased to appoint me as Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to your esteemed country. I am instructed to take this solemn occasion to express to you the high respect of His Majesty the King of Denmark, and the sincere wish of His Majesty the King to wish the Chinese people prosperity and happiness.
	His Majesty the King of Denmark, as well as the Danish Government and people, attach great importance to maintaining the friendly relations that happily exist between Denmark and China, and will welcome with the utmost joy and satisfaction the further strengthening of cultural and commercial relations between our two countries.
	I have been instructed to serve as the Envoy of the King of Denmark to the People's Republic of China, representing His Majesty the King to maintain and further develop the friendly relations between Denmark and China. I accept this important task with deep gratitude and a sense of heavy responsibility. I eagerly hope that my efforts to achieve this goal will be successful, and I hope that in carrying out this task, I will receive the strong and indispensable assistance from Your Excellency and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China in completing my mission.

Chairman Mao's reply was as follows:
	Mr. Envoy:
	I am very pleased to accept the credentials presented by Your Excellency, the Envoy of His Majesty the King of Denmark, and I thank Your Excellency for your congratulations.
	Friendship exists between the peoples of China and Denmark. I believe that our two peoples also share a common desire in dedicating ourselves to the development of economic relations and the safeguarding of world peace. The establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Denmark will undoubtedly further consolidate the friendship that already exists between the peoples of our two countries, and I hope that it will contribute to lasting world peace.
	I warmly welcome Your Excellency as the first Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to the People's Republic of China, and I am willing to assist Your Excellency in strengthening the friendly relations between our two countries.
	I wish your country prosperity and your people prosperity, and I wish your head of state good health.

### 丹麦驻我国公使穆克向毛主席呈递国书

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　丹麦驻我国公使穆克向毛主席呈递国书
    【新华社二十四日讯】丹麦王国驻我国首任特命全权公使穆克于今日下午七时半偕秘书迈思妥，向我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席呈递国书。在场的有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，中国人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部交际处处长王倬如。穆克公使呈递国书时曾向毛泽东主席致颂词，毛泽东主席致答词。
  穆克公使的颂词如下：主席阁下：
    我荣幸地将我的君主，丹麦国王陛下，乐于任命我为驻贵国特命全权公使的国书递交阁下，我受命借此隆重的场合向您表达我国国王陛下崇高的敬意，以及我国国王陛下敬祝中国人民繁荣幸福的忠诚愿望。
    丹麦国王陛下以及丹麦政府和人民对于保持丹中两国间愉快存在着的友好关系十分重视，并将以极其欣喜而满意地心情迎接贵我两国的文化及商业关系进一步之加强。
    我受命为丹麦国王派驻中华人民共和国的公使，代表我国国王陛下来保持和进一步发展丹中两国间的友好关系，我怀着深切感激和责任沉重的心情荣耀地接受了这一重大任务。我热望我为达到此目的而作之努力能获得成功并希望我在执行此项任务时能得到阁下及中华人民共和国中央人民政府在完成我的使命时所不可缺少的强有力的协助。
  毛主席的答词如下：公使先生：
    我很高兴地接受贵公使呈递的丹麦国国王陛下的国书，并感谢贵公使的祝贺。
    中丹两国人民之间是存在着友谊的，在致力于经济关系的发展和世界和平的维护上，我相信贵我两国人民又有着共同的愿望。现中丹两国外交关系的建立，无疑将使已存于两国人民间的友谊更加巩固，我并且希望其将有助于世界的持久和平。
    我热烈地欢迎贵公使出任丹麦王国驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权公使，并愿对贵公使加强两国友好关系的工作，予以协助。
    谨祝贵国国家繁荣，人民兴旺，并祝贵国元首健康。
