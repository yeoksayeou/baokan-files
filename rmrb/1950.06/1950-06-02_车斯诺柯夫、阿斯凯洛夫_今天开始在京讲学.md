Title: Chesnokov and Askelov to Begin Lecturing in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 1

　　Chesnokov and Askelov
	Begin Lecturing in Beijing Today

[This Newspaper's Report] Chesnokov, Vice President of the Institute of Philosophy of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet magazine "Problems of Philosophy," and Askelov, Deputy Director of the Department of Law and Director of the Law Lecture at the State Moscow University of the Soviet Union, who were invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to come to China to lecture, will lecture in Beijing from June 2nd to 10th. After completing their lectures in Beijing, they will go to other major cities to lecture.

All units in the capital who wish to attend the lectures may contact the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Branch. The topics and schedules for the lectures by the Soviet scholars on the 2nd and 3rd are as follows:

Chesnokov: (1) Stalin on the Bolshevik Party as the Leading Force of the Soviet State (June 2nd, 6:00 PM, Zhongshan Park Concert Hall); (2) Marxist-Leninist Theory on the Party Spirit of Philosophy (June 3rd, 3:00 PM, Peking University).

Askelov: (1) Stalin's Development of the Marxist-Leninist Theory of the State (June 2nd, 6:30 PM, Tsinghua University); (2) The Stalin Constitution is a Constitution of the Victory of Socialism and Worker-Peasant Democracy: (June 3rd, 8:30 AM, Sino-French University Auditorium).

### 车斯诺柯夫、阿斯凯洛夫  今天开始在京讲学

1950-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　车斯诺柯夫、阿斯凯洛夫
    今天开始在京讲学
    【本报讯】应中苏友协总会之邀而来我国讲学的苏联科学院哲学院副院长及苏联《哲学问题》杂志总编辑车斯诺柯夫，苏联国立莫斯科大学法学系副主任及法学讲座主任阿斯凯洛夫，将于六月二日至十日在京讲学；在京讲学完毕后，将赴其他各大城市讲学。
    首都凡愿听讲各单位，可向中苏友协总会及北京市分会接洽。苏联学者二、三两日的讲题及日程如后：
    车斯诺柯夫：（一）斯大林论布尔什维克党是苏维埃国家的领导力量（六月二日下午六时，中山公园音乐堂）；（二）马列主义论哲学的党性（六月三日下午三时，北京大学）。
    阿斯凯洛夫：（一）斯大林对马列主义国家学说的发展（六月二日下午六时三十分，清华大学）；（二）斯大林宪法是社会主义及工农民主胜利的宪法：（六月三日上午八时三十分，中法大学礼堂）。
