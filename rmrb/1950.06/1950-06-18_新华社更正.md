Title: Xinhua News Agency Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency Correction
    In the "Current Situation of Education in Inner Mongolia" released by this agency on May 28th (see page 3 of this newspaper on May 30th), the paragraph "The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region includes eleven leagues and seventy-nine banners of the Mongolian ethnic group originally within the provinces of Heilongjiang, Liaoxi, Rehe, Chahar, Suiyuan, and Ningxia, covering an area of approximately 1.5 million square li, with a population of approximately 12 million" should be corrected to "The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government currently directly leads six leagues, namely Xing'an, Hulun, Chahar, Xilingol, Jerim, and Jirem, covering an area of approximately 600,000 square kilometers, with a population of over 2.3 million. This includes a total of thirty-two banners, four counties, and three cities."

### 新华社更正

1950-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华社更正
    本社五月二十八日所发“内蒙教育现况”（见五月三十日本报第三版）中“内蒙自治区包括原在黑龙江、辽西、热河、察哈尔、绥远、宁夏诸省内之蒙古民族十一个盟七十九个旗，面积约一百五十万平方里，人口约一千二百万左右”一段应更正为“内蒙自治区人民政府现在直接领导的计有兴安、呼纳、察哈尔、锡林郭勒、哲里木、昭乌达等六个盟，面积约为六十余万平方公里，人口二百三十余万。共包括三十二个旗，四个县、三个市。”
