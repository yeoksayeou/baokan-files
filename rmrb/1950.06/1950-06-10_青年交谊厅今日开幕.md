Title: Youth Friendship Hall Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 3

Youth Friendship Hall Opens Today
    [This newspaper] To help promote cultural and recreational activities in government offices and popularize youth art activities, the Youth Palace and the Youth Service Department have jointly opened a Friendship Hall on the third floor of the Youth Palace, where weekend parties, artistic soirees, and cultural tea parties will be regularly held. The opening ceremony will be held on June 10, and it will be officially open to the public from the 11th.
    (China Youth Press)

### 青年交谊厅今日开幕

1950-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年交谊厅今日开幕
    【本报讯】为了协助推进机关文娱工作，普及青年的文艺活动，青年宫及青年服务部联合在青年宫三楼开辟交谊厅，经常举办周末晚会、文艺晚会及文化茶座等活动。六月十日举行开幕典礼，十一日起正式对外开放。
    （中国青年社）
