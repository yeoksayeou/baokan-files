Title: Henan Conducts Industrial and Commercial Survey to Guide Public and Private Enterprises in Overcoming Difficulties
Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 3rd. To assist and guide the industrial and commercial sectors that benefit the national economy and the people's livelihood to overcome temporary difficulties during the transition period in a planned way, Henan Province has started an industrial and commercial survey. The Henan Provincial People's Government recently organized three groups to conduct surveys of the status of public and private industrial and commercial enterprises in Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, and Zhuji cities. The Kaifeng Municipal People's Government also plans to begin a city-wide industrial and commercial survey soon. The Kaifeng Municipal Government and the Kaifeng Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party have recently held several meetings, convening relevant departments to study issues such as production management and business operations of the city's state-owned enterprises, in order to provide specific guidance to overcome current difficulties.

### 河南进行工商业调查  指导公私企业克服困难

1950-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　河南进行工商业调查
    指导公私企业克服困难
    【新华社开封三日电】河南省开始进行工商业调查工作，以便有计划地帮助和指导有利于国计民生的工商业克服过渡时期的暂时困难。河南省人民政府最近已组织三个小组，分赴郑州、开封、朱集三市调查公私营工商业状况。开封市人民政府也拟于最近开始全市工商业调查。开封市政府及中共开封市委并于最近数次举行会议，召集有关部门研究该市公营企业的生产管理和企业经营等问题，以具体指导其克服目前困难。
