Title: US Religious Conference Resolution Calls for Ending the Cold War Against the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-06-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 9th (TASS): According to the Washington correspondent of the "Worker Daily," the Northern Religious Conference of the American Evangelical and Reformed Church, with 700,000 members, recently passed a resolution in Fairmont, Minnesota, urging Truman and the US State Department to negotiate with the Soviet Union to "eliminate East-West contradictions and end the Cold War." The conference declared its "firm opposition to the US government's policy of huge military expenditures" and condemned the "unilateral decision to manufacture the hydrogen bomb" and the increased military control over civilian life.

### 美国宗教会议决议要求终止对苏冷战

1950-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国宗教会议决议  要求终止对苏冷战
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约九日讯：据“工人日报”华盛顿记者报道：拥有七十万信徒的美国福音教会和改正教会的北方宗教会议，最近在明尼苏达州的费尔蒙通过决议，敦促杜鲁门和美国国务院与苏联进行谈判，以求“消除东西间的矛盾，并终止冷战”。会议声言“坚决反对美国政府庞大军费支出的政策”，并谴责“制造氢弹的片面决定”以及对平民生活增强了的军事控制。
