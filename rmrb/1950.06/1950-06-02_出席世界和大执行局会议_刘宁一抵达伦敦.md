Title: Liu Ningyi Arrives in London to Attend World Peace Council Executive Bureau Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 1st -- According to the London Daily Worker, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Peace Committee, arrived in London on May 29th to attend the World Peace Council Executive Bureau meeting.

　　Liu Ningyi said, "General MacArthur is rebuilding fascism in Japan. Not only China, but all the peoples of Southeast Asia are concerned about this matter, and the Japanese people themselves have suffered greatly under MacArthur's rule."

　　He also declared that the recent meeting of foreign ministers of the Atlantic Pact was "a step forward in the war plan." But he is convinced that the peoples of Asia are determined to unite against war. The world's peace forces are growing rapidly. The establishment of New China is an example.

### 出席世界和大执行局会议  刘宁一抵达伦敦

1950-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席世界和大执行局会议
    刘宁一抵达伦敦
    【新华社布拉格一日电】据伦敦工人日报报道：中国保卫世界和平大会全国委员会副主席刘宁一于五月二十九日抵达伦敦，出席世界和平大会执行局会议。
    刘宁一说：“麦克阿瑟将军正在重建日本的法西斯主义。不仅中国，而且所有东南亚的各族人民，都对此事感到关心，日本人民本身已因麦克阿瑟的统治而受苦良深。”
    他又宣称，不久前大西洋公约的外长会议是向“战争计划前进了一步”。但是他确信，亚洲各族人民决心团结起来反对战争。世界和平力量正迅速增长。新中国的建立便是一个例子。
