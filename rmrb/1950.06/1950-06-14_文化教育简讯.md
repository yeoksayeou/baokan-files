　　Cultural and Educational Briefs
    North China Coal Miners' Union Prepares to Convene Cultural and Educational Conference
    The Preparatory Committee of the North China Region of the China Coal Miners' Union, in order to understand the cultural and educational situation of workers in various mines in the region and to implement the decision of the National Coal Miners' Union Representative Conference on "strengthening the amateur cultural and technical education of mine workers," will hold a cultural and educational conference starting on the 28th of this month, hosted by the union's Cultural and Educational Department. Participants will include the heads of cultural and educational departments of various mines under the region and the Zhangjiakou Electromechanical Repair Factory, as well as the cultural and educational leaders of major mining area trade unions. In addition, learning role models from various mines have been specially invited to participate. (Guang Jun)
    Jiaotong University Beijing Institute Students Prepare for Summer Internship
    In order to combine theory with practice, the Beijing Management Institute of China Jiaotong University has decided that all students in the first, second, and third grades will go to various railway bureaus and machine factories in Northeast and North China for internships during the summer vacation this year, and has announced a preliminary internship plan. The student union has called on students at all department levels to discuss this internship plan and has invited professors, lecturers, and teaching assistants to attend and provide guidance. After heated discussions in each class and grade, ideological problems among the students, such as regarding the internship as a "vacation trip" or "summer pastime," have been resolved.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Jiaotong University Beijing Institute Communication Group)
    Chairman Burhan Gives Lecture at Hui People's College
    The eighth "Nationalities Question Lecture" held by the Beijing National Hui People's College was held in the auditorium of the college on the afternoon of the 10th. Chairman Burhan of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, who was in Beijing on official business, was specially invited to be the speaker. In addition to the faculty and students of the college, cadres from the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Eighth District Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the District Office, the Nationalities Affairs Section of the Civil Affairs Bureau of the Municipal Government, and teachers and staff from various Hui primary schools in the city, totaling more than 600 people, attended the lecture. In his speech, Chairman Burhan recounted the oppression and disasters suffered by the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang from the Qing Dynasty to the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang. At the same time, he explained that the people of Xinjiang have been engaged in unyielding struggles for freedom and liberation, and finally achieved liberation under the great victory of the Chinese people's revolution. (National Hui People's College Communication Group)

### 文化教育简讯
光军
1950-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　文化教育简讯
    华北煤矿工会筹开文教会议
    中国煤矿工会华北区筹委会，为了解该区各矿工人文教情况及贯彻执行全国煤矿工会代表会议关于“加强矿山工人业余文化、技术教育工作”的决定，定于本月二十八日起由该会文教科主持召开文教会议。参加的有该区所属各矿和张家口机电修配厂的文教部长以及各大矿区工会的文教负责人。此外并特别邀请各矿的学习模范参加。（光军）
    交大京院学生准备暑假实习
    中国交通大学北京管理学院为了使理论与实践相结合，决定今年暑期一、二、三年级全体同学赴东北、华北各路局、机厂实习，并公布了初步实习计划。学生会已号召各系级同学对此项实习计划展开讨论，并请教授、讲师、助教出席指导。各班各级经过热烈的讨论后，解决了同学们中间的将实习看成“假期旅行”、“暑假中的消遣”等思想问题。
                                          （交大京院通讯组）
    鲍尔汉主席回民学院讲演
    北京国立回民学院所举办的第八次“民族问题讲座”，于十日下午在该院礼堂举行。特请因公来京之新疆省人民政府鲍尔汉主席主讲。听讲者除该院师生外，并有民族事务委员会，中共北京市第八区区委会，区公所，市府民政局民族事务科等单位干部及本市各回民小学校教职员等共计六百余人。讲演中鲍尔汉主席历述从满清到国民党反动统治时期，新疆各族人民所受的压迫与灾难。同时说明新疆人民为求自由与解放，一直进行着不屈不挠的斗争，终于在中国人民革命伟大胜利的形势下，获得了解放。（国立回民学院通讯组）
