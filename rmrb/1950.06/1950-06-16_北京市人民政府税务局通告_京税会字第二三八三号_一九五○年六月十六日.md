　　Notice from the Beijing Municipal People's Government Taxation Bureau
    Jing Shui Hui Zi No. 2383 June 16, 1950

    1.  In order to facilitate taxpayers in paying the spring business tax and summer property tax, we have consulted with the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China and have specially added eleven temporary collection locations in the urban area, which will begin collecting payments from the date of this announcement.

    2.  The spring business tax is limited to being paid at the competent taxation sub-bureaus in each district and the collection locations within their jurisdiction. (See attached table)

    3.  The summer property tax is generally not limited by region or bureau; all collection locations will accept payments.

    4.  The procedures for recovering pre-paid tax coupons for offsetting tax payments will be handled by the competent taxation sub-bureaus in each jurisdiction.

    5.  This is hereby announced.
                                         Director: Zhao Zishang
                                         Deputy Director: Ma Yingzhong
