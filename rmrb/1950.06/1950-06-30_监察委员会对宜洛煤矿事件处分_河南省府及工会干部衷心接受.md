Title: Supervisory Committee's Disciplinary Actions Regarding the Yiluo Coal Mine Incident: Henan Provincial Government and Trade Union Cadres Sincerely Accept Them
Author:
Date: 1950-06-30
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 28th: After the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council's People's Supervisory Committee's circular regarding the handling of the methane explosion disaster at the Yiluo Coal Mine in Henan on February 27th reached here, responsible cadres of the Henan Provincial People's Government and the Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions unanimously agreed that the analysis in the circular was correct and the disciplinary actions were appropriate. Wu Zhipu, Chairman of the Henan Provincial People's Government, and Niu Peicong, Vice Chairman, stated in their telegram to the Government Administration Council that they sincerely accepted the disciplinary actions taken against them, and guaranteed to implement the instructions on industrial and mining safety work in the future. The Henan Provincial People's Government has dispatched Ge Jiwu, Deputy Director of the Industrial Guidance Committee, to the Xin Yu Coal Mining Company and the Yiluo Coal Mine to handle the derelict cadres who should be disciplined in accordance with the circular. Wang Zhihao, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Liu Jie, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, Song Chuan, Deputy Director, Li Huimin, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xin Yu Coal Mine Trade Union, and Sun Jixian, Director of the Yiluo Coal Mine Trade Union, have all made public self-criticisms to the workers in the newspapers.

### 监察委员会对宜洛煤矿事件处分  河南省府及工会干部衷心接受

1950-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　监察委员会对宜洛煤矿事件处分
    河南省府及工会干部衷心接受
    【新华社开封二十八日电】中央人民政府政务院人民监察委员会关于处理河南宜洛煤矿二月二十七日沼气爆炸灾变事件的通报到达此间后，河南省人民政府及河南省总工会负责干部一致认为通报分析正确和处分得当，河南省人民政府主席吴芝圃、副主席牛佩琮在呈政务院电文中，表示衷心接受所受处分；并保证今后贯彻执行工矿保安工作的指示。河南省人民政府已派工业指导委员会副主任葛季武前往新豫煤矿公司及宜洛煤矿，遵照通报所示，分别处理各应受处分的失职干部。河南省总工会筹备委员会副主任王志浩及河南省总工会筹备委员会主任刘杰、副主任宋川、新豫煤矿工会筹委会主任李惠民、宜洛煤矿工会主任孙即显等，均在报纸上向工人群众作了公开自我检讨。
