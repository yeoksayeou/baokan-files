Title: Chairman Mao's Report at the Third Plenary Session Published in Full by Three Soviet and Beijing Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th. Pravda, Izvestia, and Labor Daily published in full Chairman Mao Zedong's report at the Third Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the 15th.
