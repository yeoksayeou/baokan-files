Title: Mongolian Ambassador to China, D. Delyar Kharsaikhan, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China, D. Delyar Kharsaikhan, and his party of five arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:30 PM today. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the General Office Yan Baohang, Acting Director of the Asian Department Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Shen Ping, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Wang Changjiang, Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador to China Li Zhouyuan, Counselor Cui Yi, Hungarian Ambassador to China Saffronko, Counselor Fekete, Counselor of the Romanian Embassy Sirad, Second Secretary of the Polish Embassy Dai Peilasinski, Third Secretary of the Indian Embassy Kumar, and Third Secretary of the Burmese Embassy Wu Zaowen.

### 蒙古驻华大使  德亚尔卡赛汉抵京

1950-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古驻华大使
    德亚尔卡赛汉抵京
    【新华社二十四日讯】蒙古人民共和国驻华大使德亚尔卡赛汉等一行五人，于今日下午四时半乘机抵京，赴机场欢迎者有我外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅副主任阎宝航，亚洲司代司长乔冠华，交际处副处长沈平，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长王长江，苏联驻华大使罗申，捷克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜驻华大使李周渊，参赞崔一，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，参赞费凯尔特，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆参赞席拉德，波兰驻华大使馆二等秘书戴裴拉辛斯基，印度驻华大使馆三等秘书古玛尔，缅甸驻华大使馆三等秘书吴藻文等。
