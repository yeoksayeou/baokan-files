Title: U.S. Expands Bases in the Philippines, Concludes New Agreement with the Quirino Regime
Author:
Date: 1950-06-26
Page: 4

　　U.S. Expands Bases in the Philippines
    Concludes New Agreement with the Quirino Regime
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] According to a report from the Central News Agency in Manila: U.S. authorities stationed in the Philippines and their Philippine puppets reached a new agreement on June 15 regarding the expansion of U.S. naval bases in the Philippines. Negotiations for this new agreement began on May 17 between Philippine President Quirino, U.S. Ambassador Cowen, and U.S. Naval Commander in the Philippines, Olde. The agreement stipulates the expansion of the U.S. naval base in Cavite, Manila Bay, and the lengthening of airstrips to facilitate the landing of U.S. jet aircraft.

### 美帝在菲扩充基地  与季里诺政权订新协定

1950-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝在菲扩充基地
    与季里诺政权订新协定
    【新华社二十一日讯】据匪中央社马尼拉讯：驻菲美国当局十五日与其菲律宾傀儡成立关于扩大美国在菲海军基地的新协定。这次订立新协定的谈判，是由菲总统季里诺和美大使柯温、菲岛美海军司令欧尔德于五月十七日开始进行的。协定规定扩充马尼拉湾甲美地的美国海军基地，并加长飞行地带，以便利美国喷气飞机的着陆。
