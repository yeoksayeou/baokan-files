Title: Soviet Philosopher Professor Chesnokov Lectures on "On Soviet Patriotism"
Author:
Date: 1950-06-07
Page: 3

　　Soviet Philosopher Professor Chesnokov
    To Lecture on "On Soviet Patriotism" Today
    [Our Newspaper] At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Professor Chesnokov, an authoritative Soviet philosopher who has come to China to lecture, will speak on the topic of "On Soviet Patriotism" at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall at 6:30 pm on June 7 (postponed if it rains). The lecture will expound on the characteristics of old patriotism, the relationship between patriotism and nationalism, the relationship between patriotism and internationalism, the emergence of Soviet patriotism, its essence and characteristics (national character, comprehensiveness and consciousness, action, the spirit of internationalism and friendship among all ethnic groups, respect for the characteristics and traditions of other ethnic groups), the formation of socialist nations and their characteristics, and other issues. Tickets can be obtained from the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at No. 2 Yuhe Bridge.
    Professor Askellov, a well-known Soviet jurist, will lecture on "Stalin's Development of Marxist-Leninist State Theory" at Renmin University at 7:00 pm on the same day.

### 苏联哲学家车斯诺柯夫教授  今讲“论苏维埃爱国主义”

1950-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联哲学家车斯诺柯夫教授
    今讲“论苏维埃爱国主义”
    【本报讯】应中苏友协总会之邀来我国讲学的苏联权威哲学家车斯诺柯夫教授于六月七日下午六时半（遇雨延期）在中山公园音乐堂讲“论苏维埃爱国主义”一题，阐述旧爱国主义的特点、爱国主义与民族主义、爱国主义与国际主义、苏维埃爱国主义之发生、其本质与特点（全民性、全面性与自觉性、行动性、国际主义精神与各民族友好、尊重其他民族的特点与传统）、社会主义民族之形成及其特点诸问题。入场券向御河桥二号北京市中苏友协洽领。
    苏联著名法学家阿斯凯洛夫教授于同日下午七时在人民大学讲“斯大林对于马列主义国家学说的发展”一题。
