Title: All-China Youth Federation Representative in England Protests Arrest of West German Youth
Author: Liao Chengzhi
Date: 1950-06-07
Page: 4

　　
My All-China Youth Federation Representative in England stationed in Germany
    Protests Arrest of West German Youth
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 6th] The All-China Democratic Youth Federation sent a telegram to Robertson, the British High Commissioner in Germany in Berlin, on June 5th, protesting the British authorities' atrocities of arresting a large number of youths returning to West Germany after attending the All-German Youth Congress. The telegram reads as follows: Berlin

    British High Commissioner in Germany, Robertson:

    We solemnly protest against the British authorities' atrocities of arresting a large number of youths returning to West Germany after attending the All-German Youth Congress. We demand immediate release.
     Chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation Liao Chengzhi
      June 5th

### 我青联电英驻德专员  抗议逮捕西德青年
廖承志
1950-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　我青联电英驻德专员
    抗议逮捕西德青年
    【新华社六日讯】中华全国民主青年联合会于六月五日致电柏林英国驻德高级专员罗伯逊，抗议英国当局大批逮捕参加全德青年大会后返回西德的青年的暴行，电文如下：柏林
    英国驻德高级专员罗伯逊：
    我们严正地抗议英国当局大批逮捕参加全德青年大会后返回西德的青年的暴行。要求立即释放。
     中华全国民主青年联合总会主席  廖承志
      六月五日
