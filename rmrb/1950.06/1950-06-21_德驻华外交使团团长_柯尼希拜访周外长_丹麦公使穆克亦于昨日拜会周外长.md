Title: Head of the German Diplomatic Mission in China, Koenig, Visits Foreign Minister Zhou / Danish Minister Munk Also Visited Foreign Minister Zhou Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-06-21
Page: 1

　　Head of the German Diplomatic Mission in China
    Koenig Visits Foreign Minister Zhou
    Danish Minister Munk Also Visited Foreign Minister Zhou Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Koenig, the head of the German Democratic Republic's diplomatic mission in China, with the rank of ambassador, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at 10:00 AM today to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Munk, the first Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at 6:15 PM today to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs, Wen Pengjiu, Deputy Director, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 德驻华外交使团团长  柯尼希拜访周外长  丹麦公使穆克亦于昨日拜会周外长

1950-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　德驻华外交使团团长
    柯尼希拜访周外长
    丹麦公使穆克亦于昨日拜会周外长
    【新华社二十日讯】德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希于今日下午十时拜访我外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有苏欧司司长伍修权及交际处处长王倬如。
    【新华社二十日讯】丹麦王国驻华首任特命全权公使穆克于今日下午六时一刻拜会我外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有欧非司司长宦乡、副司长温朋久及交际处处长王倬如。
