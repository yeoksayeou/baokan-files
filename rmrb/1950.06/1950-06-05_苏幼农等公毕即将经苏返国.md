Title: Su Younong and Others to Return Home Via Soviet Union After Completing Official Duties
Author:
Date: 1950-06-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th. Su Younong, the plenipotentiary representative of our country, advisor Ge Baoquan, and attendant Xu Chuanxian, who participated in the Executive and Liaison Committee meeting of the Universal Postal Union held in Montreux, Switzerland, arrived in Prague by plane on the afternoon of the 2nd after the meeting concluded. They plan to stay here for a few days before returning home via the Soviet Union. Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Posts Lejimosai, Director of the General Post Office Raibert, and Karl Bishek of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who is responsible for Chinese affairs, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 苏幼农等公毕即将经苏返国

1950-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏幼农等公毕即将经苏返国
    【新华社布拉格四日电】参加在瑞士蒙特罗举行的万国邮政联盟执行及联络委员会会议的我国全权代表苏幼农、顾问戈宝权及随员徐传贤等一行三人，在会议闭幕后，已于二日下午飞抵布拉格，并拟在此稍留数日即经由苏联返国。捷克斯洛伐克邮政部副部长莱伊莫赛、邮政总局局长赖伯特、外交部负责中国事务的卡尔比谢克等多人，均曾亲往机场欢迎。
