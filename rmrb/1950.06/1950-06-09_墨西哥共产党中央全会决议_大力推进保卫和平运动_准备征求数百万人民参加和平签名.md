Title: Resolution of the Central Plenary Session of the Communist Party of Mexico: Vigorously Promote the Movement to Defend Peace; Prepare to Solicit Millions of People to Participate in Peace Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, 8th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Mexico held a plenary session here from May 27th to 30th. The plenary session passed a resolution: Determining that the central task of Mexican communists is to fight for peace, vigorously carry out daily work, and collect millions of signatures from the Mexican people in support of the Stockholm Peace Appeal. Prior to passing this resolution, the plenary session listened to and discussed the report by General Secretary Encina on the struggle for peace and the tasks of the Communist Party. In his report, Encina pointed out various facts illustrating the serious existence of the danger of war, and asked the Central Committee to discuss methods of mobilizing the entire country to collect peace signatures.
    The plenary session also discussed the organizational problems of the party, and listened to the report of the organizational secretary of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee on this issue. His report addressed the issue of building a mass party closely linked to the working class, peasants, and various problems of the Mexican people, and the issue of fundamentally directing the party's struggle towards striving for peace and national independence.

### 墨西哥共产党中央全会决议  大力推进保卫和平运动  准备征求数百万人民参加和平签名

1950-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　墨西哥共产党中央全会决议
    大力推进保卫和平运动
    准备征求数百万人民参加和平签名
    【新华社八日讯】电通社墨西哥城七日讯：墨西哥共产党中央委员会全体会议于五月二十七日至三十日在此间举行。全会通过决议：确定墨西哥共产党人的中心任务是为和平而斗争，大力进行日常工作，在墨西哥人民中征集数百万个拥护斯德哥尔摩和平宣言的签名。在通过这项决议以前，全会曾听取了并讨论了总书记安西纳关于为和平而斗争与共产党的任务的报告。安西纳在报告中指出各种说明战争危险严重存在的事实，并要求中央委员会讨论全国动员起来征集和平签名的方法。
    全会又讨论了党的组织问题，听取了中央委员会政治局组织书记关于这个问题的报告。他的报告论及建设一个与工人阶级、农民及墨西哥人民的各种问题有密切连系的群众性大党的问题，以及使党的斗争基本上朝向争取和平与民族独立这一方向的问题。
