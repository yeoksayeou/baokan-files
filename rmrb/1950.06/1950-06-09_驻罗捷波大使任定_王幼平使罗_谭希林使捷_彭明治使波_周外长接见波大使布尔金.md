Title: Appointment of Ambassadors to Romania, Czechoslovakia and Poland: Wang Youping to Romania, Tan Xilin to Czechoslovakia, Peng Mingzhi to Poland. Foreign Minister Zhou Receives Polish Ambassador Burkin

Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Central People's Government of our country has appointed General Wang Youping as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Romanian People's Republic, General Tan Xilin as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Czechoslovak Republic, and General Peng Mingzhi as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Polish Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, received Burkin, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Poland to China, at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 2 pm on the 7th. Those present included Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 驻罗捷波大使任定  王幼平使罗·谭希林使捷·彭明治使波  周外长接见波大使布尔金

1950-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　驻罗捷波大使任定
    王幼平使罗·谭希林使捷·彭明治使波
    周外长接见波大使布尔金
    【新华社八日讯】我中央人民政府已任命王幼平将军为我国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国首任特命全权大使，谭希林将军为我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国首任特命全权大使，彭明治将军为我国驻波兰共和国首任特命全权大使。
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于七日下午二时在外交部接见波兰驻华首任特命全权大使布尔金。在座者尚有外交部苏联东欧司伍修权司长，交际处王倬如处长。
