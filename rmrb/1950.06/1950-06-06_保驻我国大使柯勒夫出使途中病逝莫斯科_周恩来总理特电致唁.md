Title: Bulgarian Ambassador to Our Country, Kolev, Dies of Illness En Route to Post in Moscow; Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Condolences
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 1

　　Bulgarian Ambassador to Our Country, Kolev, Dies of Illness En Route to Post in Moscow
    Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Condolences via Telegram
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] Svetoslav Kolev, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to our country, unfortunately passed away in Moscow on May 30th while en route to his post in our country. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a telegram of condolences to Pop Tomov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bulgaria. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Pop Tomov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria:
    I was shocked to learn that Mr. Svetoslav Kolev passed away in Moscow while en route to the People's Republic of China. I extend my condolences to Your Excellency and to the family of Mr. Kolev.
     Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                    June 3, 1950, in Beijing

### 保驻我国大使柯勒夫出使途中病逝莫斯科  周恩来总理特电致唁
周恩来
1950-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　保驻我国大使柯勒夫出使途中病逝莫斯科
    周恩来总理特电致唁
    【新华社五日讯】保加利亚人民共和国派驻我国大使斯费托斯拉夫·柯勒夫于出使我国途中，不幸于五月三十日病逝莫斯科，周恩来总理兼外长特电保部长会议副主席兼外交部长波普托莫夫致唁，电文如下：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部部长波普托莫夫阁下：
    惊悉斯费托斯拉夫·柯勒夫先生于出使中华人民共和国途中，病逝莫斯科，谨向阁下及柯勒夫先生之家属致悼唁。
     中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长  周恩来
                    一九五○年六月三日于北京
