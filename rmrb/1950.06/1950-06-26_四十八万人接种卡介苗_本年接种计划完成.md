Title: 480,000 People Vaccinated with BCG; This Year's Vaccination Plan Completed
Author:
Date: 1950-06-26
Page: 3

　　480,000 People Vaccinated with BCG
    This Year's Vaccination Plan Completed
    [This newspaper's report] The Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government has completed its plan to vaccinate 480,000 people with BCG vaccine this year. The second BCG vaccination personnel training class run by the Ministry also concluded on the 11th of this month. Thirty-two medical personnel from more than ten cities, including Shenyang, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Wuhan, Changsha, Hangzhou, Jinan, and Gui Sui, received training this time.
              (China Health News Group)

### 四十八万人接种卡介苗  本年接种计划完成

1950-06-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　四十八万人接种卡介苗
    本年接种计划完成
    【本报讯】中央人民政府卫生部本年接种卡介菌苗四十八万人的计划已全部完成。该部所办的第二期卡介菌苗人员实习班亦已于本月十一日结业。此次受训的有沈阳、西安、兰州、武汉、长沙、杭州、济南、归绥等十余城市医务人员三十二人。
              （中卫通讯组）
