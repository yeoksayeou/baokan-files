Title: Czechoslovak Peace Signature Drive Ends in Victory: Over Nine Million Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 31st (TASS): The signature campaign in Czechoslovakia for the Great National Assembly's peace declaration has concluded victoriously. The signature drive was carried out in a spirit of national unity and high morale. A total of 9,482,422 people signed, approximately equivalent to the number of all adults in the country's population of over 12 million.

The Czechoslovak peace signature drive began on May 14th. Czechoslovak Premier Zápotocký, members of the Czechoslovak government, and all members of the National Assembly signed the peace declaration.

### 捷和平签名胜利结束  签名人数九百余万

1950-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷和平签名胜利结束
    签名人数九百余万
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社布拉格五月三十一日讯：捷克斯洛伐克在和大常委会和平宣言上的签名运动，业已结束。签名运动是在全国一致与情绪高涨的气氛中进行的。签名人数共有九百四十八万二千四百二十二人，约略等于全国一千二百余万人口中全体成年人的数目。
    按捷和平签名运动系自五月十四日开始。捷总理萨波托斯基、捷政府委员及国民议会全体议员，都在和平宣言上签了名。
