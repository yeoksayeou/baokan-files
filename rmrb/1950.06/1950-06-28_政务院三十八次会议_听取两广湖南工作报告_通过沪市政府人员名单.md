Title: The 38th Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Heard Work Reports from Guangdong, Guangxi, and Hunan; Approved the List of Personnel for the Shanghai Municipal People's Government
Author:
Date: 1950-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The 38th Plenary Meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 3 p.m. today. At the meeting, the Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Ye Jianying, the Chairman of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, Zhang Yunyi, and the Chairman of the Hunan Provincial People's Government, Wang Shoudao, delivered work reports. After hearing these work reports, the meeting held discussions and approved them. In addition, the meeting approved the list of personnel for the Shanghai Municipal People's Government.

### 政务院三十八次会议  听取两广湖南工作报告  通过沪市政府人员名单

1950-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院三十八次会议
    听取两广湖南工作报告
    通过沪市政府人员名单
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第三十八次政务会议于今日下午三时举行。会上广东省人民政府主席叶剑英、广西省人民政府主席张云逸、湖南省人民政府主席王首道作了工作报告。会议在听取了这些工作报告后，曾进行讨论，并予同意。此外，会议通过了上海市人民政府的工作人员名单。
