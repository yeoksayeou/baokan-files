Title: Yesterday was Swedish National Day; Premier Zhou sent a telegram to congratulate; Swedish Ambassador to China held a reception
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Yesterday was the National Day of the Kingdom of Sweden. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Prime Minister Erlander of the Swedish government to congratulate him. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Prime Minister Erlander of the Government of the Kingdom of Sweden:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day and the 93rd birthday of His Majesty the King, I would like to extend my congratulations and wish the Swedish people happiness and progress.
     Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                           June 15, 1950, Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: The Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Amason, held a reception at 6 p.m. today to celebrate the Swedish National Day and the 93rd birthday of His Majesty the King. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's State Council was invited to attend. Other leaders invited to attend included Vice Premier Huang Yanpei of the Central People's Government's State Council, Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xielin. Diplomatic envoys invited to attend included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burgin, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Danish Minister Munk, Chargé d'Affaires Wu Bi of the Burmese Embassy, and negotiating representatives from Britain, the Netherlands, Norway, and Switzerland.

### 昨为瑞典国庆日  周总理致电庆贺  瑞典驻华大使举行招待会

1950-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　昨为瑞典国庆日
    周总理致电庆贺
    瑞典驻华大使举行招待会
    【新华社十六日讯】六月十六日为瑞典王国国庆日，周恩来总理兼外长特电瑞典政府艾德兰总理致贺，电文如下：瑞典王国政府艾德兰总理阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆及贵国国王九十三岁寿辰，特电庆贺，并祝瑞典人民幸福和进步。
     中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来
                           一九五○年六月十五日于北京
    【新华社十六日讯】瑞典驻我国大使阿马斯顿为庆祝瑞典国庆暨其国王九十三岁寿辰，特于今日下午六时举行招待会。中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来应邀出席。其他应邀与会的尚有中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培，贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长丁燮林等首长。外交使节应邀出席的有苏联大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，印度大使潘尼迦，波兰大使布尔金，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，丹麦公使穆克，缅甸大使馆临时代办吴辟及英、荷、挪、瑞（士）等国谈判代表。
