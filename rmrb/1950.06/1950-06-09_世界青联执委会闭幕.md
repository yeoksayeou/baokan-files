Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Executive Committee Concludes

Author:
Date: 1950-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th.—The Second Session of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, which opened in Berlin on May 31st, concluded on the 4th. The meeting began its third agenda item on the 3rd, hearing a report by World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Hokam on "Progress in the Preparation for the Children's Health and Democratic Education Conference," and held a discussion on this report on the 4th.

### 世界青联执委会闭幕

1950-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青联执委会闭幕
    【新华社布拉格八日电】五月三十一日在柏林开幕的世界民主青年联盟第二届执行委员会第二次会议，已于四日闭幕。会议曾于三日开始进行第三项议程，听取了世界民主青联书记霍卡姆关于《儿童保健及民主教育会议筹备工作之进展》的报告，并于四日进行了关于这个报告的讨论。
