Title: Korean Cabinet Issues Decision to Formulate Three-Year Economic Plan, to be Implemented in the Northern Half Starting Next Year
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd—The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decision on the 20th stating: In view of the imminent completion of the Two-Year People's Economic Plan from 1949 to 1950, it has been decided to implement the Three-Year People's Economic Plan from 1951 to 1953 in the northern half of the Republic. Chairman Jung Joon-taek of the State Planning Commission under the Cabinet is instructed to submit to the Cabinet by July 15, 1950, a written opinion on the formulation of the Three-Year People's Economic Plan for the northern half of the Republic from 1951 to 1953. The decision also instructed each minister of the Cabinet, each director of bureaus directly under the Cabinet, and each chairman of the People's Committee of each province (including Pyongyang Special City) to closely assist the State Planning Commission in carrying out the preparatory work for formulating the plan.

### 朝鲜部长会议发布决定  制订三年经济计划  将自明年起在北半部实施

1950-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜部长会议发布决定
    制订三年经济计划
    将自明年起在北半部实施
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议二十日发布决定称：鉴于一九四九年到一九五○年的两年人民经济计划行将完成，决定在共和国北半部实施一九五一年到一九五三年的三年人民经济计划，责成部长会议所属国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽于一九五○年七月十五日，向部长会议提出关于制订共和国北半部一九五一年到一九五三年的三年人民经济计划的意见书。决定并指示部长会议各部长、部长会议直属各局长及各道（包括平壤特别市）人民委员会委员长，应紧密协助国家计划委员会进行制订计划的准备工作。
