Title: Letter from Students of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Chinese Students (Abstract)
Author:
Date: 1950-06-11
Page: 4

To All Students of the Korean Language Department of the Oriental Languages ​​Department of National Peking University of the People's Republic of China:

We are students of the Korean Language Department and Korean Literature Department of the Korean Language and Literature Department of Kim Il-sung University of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

First of all, we extend our heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese people who, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, broke the shackles of feudalism and imperialism and established the People's Republic of China!

We have received your friendly letter. The students of our Kim Il-sung University express our brotherly gratitude and respect to you!

The victory of the Chinese people has strengthened the world peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union; and dealt a decisive blow to the anti-democratic imperialist camp! The victory of the Chinese revolution is the greatest event since World War II. This is a great encouragement and inspiration to all Eastern nations fighting for national liberation, especially to us Koreans fighting for the unification of our motherland.

The Democratic People's Republic of Korea, thanks to the heroic struggle of the Soviet army, has been liberated from imperialist enslavement for five years. In these five years, the Korean people have been strongly united around Comrade Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people. In all democratic construction, and in the effort to consolidate and develop the fruits of various democratic constructions, we devote all our strength and continuously strive. Korean students, firmly armed with Marxism-Leninism, are working hard to devote themselves to various scientific research undertakings to build a democratic national culture in Korea.

We have received tremendous help from the science and culture of the great socialist country, the Soviet Union. In our university, there are famous scholars and professors from the Soviet Union. They have given us rich experience on our way to socialism. They unreservedly teach us excellent and advanced science, which has greatly developed our scientific research.

The friendship between Korea and the People's Republic of China will greatly help the development of science and culture in both countries; and at the same time strengthen the power of the world peace and democracy camp. Let us unite closely with you and further strengthen our ties!

Long live Marshal Stalin!
Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!
Long live Premier Kim Il-sung!
Long live the strong unity of international democratic youth!

              All students of the Korean Language Department and Korean Literature Department of the Korean Language and Literature Department of Kim Il-sung University of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea

### 朝鲜民主主义人民共和国学生给中国同学的信（摘要）

1950-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国学生给中国同学的信（摘要）中华人民共和国国立北京大学东方语文学系朝鲜语科全体同学们：
    我们是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成综合大学朝鲜语文学部朝鲜语学科、朝鲜文学科的学生。
    首先，我们向在中国共产党领导下打破了封建主义与帝国主义枷锁，建立了中华人民共和国的中国人民致以衷心的祝贺！
    你们友谊的来信收到了。我们金日成综合大学的同学们，对你们表示兄弟般的感激与敬意！
    中国人民的胜利，使以伟大的苏联为首的世界和平、民主阵营更加坚强；并给反民主的帝国主义阵营一个决定性的打击！中国革命的胜利，是第二次大战以来的一件最伟大的事。这对为民族解放而斗争的东方各个民族，特别是对于我们为祖国统一而斗争的朝鲜，是一个极大的鼓舞和激励。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国，由于苏联军队英勇的斗争，从帝国主义的奴役下解放出来，已经有五年了。在这五年里，朝鲜人民坚强地团结在朝鲜人民领袖金日成同志的周围。在一切民主建设中，在为了巩固并发展各种民主建设成果的努力中，我们倾注所有的力量，不断地奋斗。朝鲜的学生们，以马列主义坚强地武装了自己的头脑，努力投身于各种科学研究事业，建设朝鲜民主的民族的文化。
    我们从伟大的社会主义国家苏联的科学与文化中，得到了莫大的帮助。在我们的大学里，有从苏联来的著名学者、教授。在我们走向社会主义的途中，他们给了我们丰富的经验。他们把优秀的、先进的科学毫不吝惜地教给我们，使我们的科学研究，有很大的发展。
    朝鲜和中华人民共和国的友谊将对两国科学和文化的发展有极大的帮助；同时更加强了世界和平、民主阵营的力量。让我们和你们紧密地团结，更进一步地联系吧！
    斯大林大元帅万岁！
    毛泽东主席万岁！
    金日成总理万岁！
    国际民主青年们的坚强团结万岁！
              朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成综合大学朝鲜语文学部朝鲜语学科、朝鲜文学科全体学生
