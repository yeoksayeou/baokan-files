    Introduction to Yu Zhenshan's Slide Rule Algorithm (9)
    [Example 11] 6 bolts of cloth cost 48,000 yuan. What is the price per bolt? Method:
    (1) Align the 6 (representing 6 bolts) on the Heaven Ruler with 4.8 (representing 48,000 yuan) on the Earth Ruler;
    (2) The 8 on the Earth Ruler aligned with the 10 on the Heaven Ruler is the quotient.
    (3) Determine the position: Since 8 is greater than 4, then
         The number of digits in the quotient = 6 - 1 = 5
         Therefore, the price per bolt is 8,000 yuan.
    (Note) 2, 3, 4, 5... on the Heaven Ruler correspond to 1.6, 2.4, 3.2, 4... on the Earth Ruler, representing 2 bolts, 3 bolts, 4 bolts, 5 bolts... costing 16,000 yuan, 24,000 yuan, 32,000 yuan, 40,000 yuan... respectively.
    9. Multiplication and Division Chain Algorithm
    Number A ÷ Number B × Number C = Total
    Ruler: Square slide rule calculator (including a Heaven Ruler for positive numbers, an Earth Ruler, and one other), or a circular slide rule calculator, or a rectangular slide rule calculator.
    When using a square slide rule calculator, fix the 0 point of the Heaven Ruler on the 0 line of the Earth Ruler.
    Method:
    (1) Align Number B on the Heaven Ruler with Number A (or Number C) on the Earth Ruler;
    (2) The number on the Earth Ruler that aligns with Number C (or Number A) on the Heaven Ruler is the total sought.
    (3) Determine the number of digits in the total according to the positioning method. (Attached picture)

### 于振善尺算法介绍（九）

1950-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    于振善尺算法介绍（九）
    【例题11】布6疋共介48000元问每疋价多少？求法：
    （1）把天尺的6（代表6疋）对准地尺上的4.8（代表48000元）；
    （2）天尺上的10所对着的地尺上的8即是所求的商数。
    （3）定位：因8大於4所以
         商数的位数=6-1=5
         所以求得：每疋价8000元。
    （注）天尺上的2，，3，4，5，……分别对着地尺上的1.6，2.4，3.2，4……就是代表2疋，3疋，4疋，5疋……各价16000元，24000元，32000元，40000元，……
    九、除乘连算法
    甲数÷乙数×丙数=总数
    用尺：方形尺算器（包括正数天尺，及地尺和一个）或圆形尺算器或长形尺算器。
    用方形尺算器时将天尺的0点固定在地尺的0线上。
    求法：
    （1）把天尺上的乙数以对准地尺上的甲数（或丙数）；
    （2）天尺上的丙数（或甲数）所对着的地尺上的数即是所求的总数，
    （3）按照定位法求总数的位数。（附图片）
