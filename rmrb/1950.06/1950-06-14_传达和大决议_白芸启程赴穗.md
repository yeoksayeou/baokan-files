Title: Conveying Messages and the Grand Resolution - Bai Yun Sets Off for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1950-06-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: Bai Yun, the Chinese executive committee representative who attended the Ninth Executive Committee Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation in Finland, has returned to Beijing. In order to convey the situation regarding the International Democratic Women's Federation's Executive Committee resolution on the Third Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress and the decisions of the International Democratic Women's Federation, she is confirmed to participate in the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace propaganda group led by Xiao San to conduct propaganda work in various places. Bai Yun departed for Guangzhou on the 11th. Bai Yun is currently the Director of the Tangshan City Women's Federation and the Deputy Director of the Tangshan City Cooperative General Office. She formerly served as the County Magistrate of Zunhua County, Hebei Province, and was one of the famous female county magistrates in the Liberated Areas.

### 传达和大决议  白芸启程赴穗

1950-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　传达和大决议  白芸启程赴穗
    【新华社十一日讯】出席芬兰国际民主妇联第九次执委会会议的中国执委代表白芸返京后，为传达国际民主妇联执行世界保卫和平大会第三次常设委员会决议的情况，与国际民主妇联的决定，确定参加萧三率领的中国保卫世界和平大会宣传团，往各地进行宣传工作。白芸已于十一日启程赴广州。白芸现任唐山市妇联主任及唐山市合作总社副主任。曾任冀东遵化县县长，为解放区著名的女县长之一。
