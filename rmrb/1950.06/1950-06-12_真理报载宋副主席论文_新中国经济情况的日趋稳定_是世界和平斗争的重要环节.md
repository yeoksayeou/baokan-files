Title: Pravda Publishes Vice Chairman Song's Article: The Increasing Stability of the New China's Economy is an Important Link in the Struggle for World Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-06-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th: According to TASS, Pravda published an article by Vice Chairman Song Qingling on the 8th, discussing the upsurge of the Chinese people's movement to defend world peace. The article stated: The Chinese people are voting for peace. A strong, liberated China, allied with other peace-loving nations, is a huge obstacle blocking the path of American and British warmongers.

　　The article pointed out: The current stability of China's economy is an important link in the struggle for world peace. The Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and the trade and economic agreements we have signed with our great neighbor and ally, the Soviet Union, have further strengthened our growing power. With the fraternal help of the Soviet Union, the Chinese people will do their utmost to build their country's industry and revive agriculture.

　　The article also stated: Currently, the Chinese people are mobilizing all forces for the world peace movement. All progressive men and women around the world can rest assured that the Chinese people will never give up halfway on the road to their destination. The millions of peace declarations signed by the Chinese people demonstrate how firm our determination is to fight for peace.

　　The article concluded: We demand peace. The Chinese people demand a free, happy, and peaceful life. At the same time, they are willing to contribute their share to the good life of all mankind. We can achieve this goal with the various facilities of our People's Republic of China. We can achieve this goal under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party. We can achieve this goal by unhesitatingly supporting the indestructible fortress of peace—the Soviet Union, headed by Stalin.

### 真理报载宋副主席论文  新中国经济情况的日趋稳定  是世界和平斗争的重要环节

1950-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　真理报载宋副主席论文
    新中国经济情况的日趋稳定  是世界和平斗争的重要环节
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报于八日刊载宋庆龄副主席所著一文，论述中国人民保卫世界和平运动的高涨。该文说：中国人民正为和平而投票。强大的解放了的中国与其他爱好和平的国家结成联盟，是阻塞美英战争贩子去路的巨大障碍。
    该文指出：目前中国经济的稳定，是争取世界和平斗争的重要环节。我们与我们伟大的邻国及盟国苏联订立的友好同盟互助条约以及贸易协定与经济方面的协定，更加强了我们不断增长的力量。中国人民，在苏联兄弟般的帮助之下，将竭尽他们的全力来缔造自己国家的工业并复兴农业。
    该文又说：目前，中国人民正为世界和平运动动员一切力量。全世界所有进步男女们可以放心：中国人民决不会在走向他们目的地的道路上中途而废。中国成百万的和平宣言签名，说明我们为和平而斗争的决心是多么坚定。
    该文结语说：我们要求和平。中国人民要求过自由、愉快、和平的生活。同时，他们愿意为整个人类的美好生活贡献他们的一份力量。我们能够以我们中华人民共和国的各项设施来达到这个目的。我们能够在毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下达到这个目的。我们能够以毫不犹豫地支持坚不可摧的和平堡垒——斯大林为首的苏联来达到这个目的。
