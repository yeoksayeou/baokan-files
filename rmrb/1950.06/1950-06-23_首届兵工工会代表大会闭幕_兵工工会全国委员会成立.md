Title: First National Congress of Armaments Industry Trade Union Concludes; National Committee of the Armaments Industry Trade Union Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, June 19th—The first National Congress of the Chinese Armaments Industry Trade Union concluded on the 17th. The meeting discussed 357 proposals put forward by representatives from various regions, determined the content and methods of the work of the Armaments Industry Trade Union, and adopted the organizational charter of the Chinese Armaments Industry Trade Union, as well as the "Resolution on Learning Technology and Improving Quality" and the "Suggestion to the Central People's Government's Ministry of Heavy Industry on Implementing the Eight-Hour Work System." The meeting elected 41 people including Li Guochao, Wang Jie, and Le Shaohua as members of the National Committee of the Chinese Armaments Industry Trade Union and established the National Committee of the Chinese Armaments Industry Trade Union. During the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government attended, answered questions from representatives from various regions, and encouraged everyone to study hard, build a modern national defense industry, and defend world peace. In addition, the meeting also heard reports from Vice Minister Liu Ding of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Heavy Industry, Minister Liu Zijiu of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions' Department of Culture and Education, and others on issues such as the production tasks for 1950, the Trade Union Law, democratization of factory management, wages, and trade union organizations.
    Vice Chairman Li Lisan of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions repeatedly emphasized the importance of armaments industry workers learning technology and improving their cultural level in his report to the congress, pointing out that the central task of the armaments industry trade union work at present is to do a good job in the technical and cultural learning of employees in their spare time.
    Zhao Guoqiang, Director of the Armaments Industry Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, requested all representatives to deeply convey the spirit of the congress resolutions in his closing remarks, calling on armaments industry workers across the country to work hard to learn technology, improve quality, launch a conservation movement, promote mass mutual assistance, consolidate grassroots trade union organizations, promote the experience of the Ma Hengchang Group, and complete the production tasks given by the state.

### 首届兵工工会代表大会闭幕  兵工工会全国委员会成立

1950-06-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　首届兵工工会代表大会闭幕
    兵工工会全国委员会成立
    【新华社十九日讯】中国兵工工会第一次代表大会已于十七日闭幕。这次会议讨论了各地代表所提出的三百五十七条提案，确定了兵工工会工作的内容、方法，通过了中国兵工工会组织章程及“关于学习技术、提高质量的决议”和“为实行八小时工作制向中央人民政府重工业部的建议”。会议选出了李国超、王杰、乐少华等四十一人为中国兵工工会全国委员，成立了中国兵工工会全国委员会。会议期间，中央人民政府朱德副主席曾出席为各地代表解答问题，并勉励大家努力学习，建设现代化国防工业，保卫世界和平。此外，会议还听取了中央人民政府重工业部刘鼎副部长、全国总工会文教部刘子久部长等关于一九五○年的生产任务、工会法、工厂管理民主化、工资、工会组织等问题的报告。
    全国总工会李立三副主席在向大会的报告中再三强调了兵工职工学习技术、提高文化的重要性，指出目前兵工工会工作的中心任务是作好职工业余的技术、文化学习工作。
    全国总工会兵工工作委员会主任赵国强在闭幕词中要求各代表深入传达大会决议的精神，号召全国兵工职工努力学习技术，提高质量，开展节约运动，推动群众互助，巩固工会基层组织，推广马恒昌小组经验，完成国家给予的生产任务。
