Title: To Promote Vietnamese-Soviet Friendship: Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 4

　　To Promote Vietnamese-Soviet Friendship
　　Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] According to a Vietnam News Agency report on the 2nd, many prominent figures, representatives of people's organizations, intellectuals, and journalists of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam established a preparatory committee for the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association on May 19th. Vietnam News Agency also reported on the 3rd: Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the National Assembly of Vietnam, on behalf of the working committee of the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, stating: "To promote closer ties between Vietnam and the Soviet Union in political, economic, social, and cultural aspects, the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association has been established in Vietnam. We earnestly request you to convey our admiration and solidarity to Stalin, the great defender of human peace and freedom, and to the Soviet people, the builders of a new society."

### 促进越苏人民友谊  越苏友协成立

1950-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　促进越苏人民友谊
    越苏友协成立
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社二日报道：越南民主共和国的许多名人、人民团体的代表、知识分子与新闻工作者，已于五月十九日成立越苏友好协会筹备委员会。越南通讯社三日又报道：越南国民议会代理主席孙德胜已代表越苏友好协会工作委员会致电苏联对外文化协会称：“为了促进越苏两国间在政治、经济、社会及文化方面更密切的联系，越苏友好协会已在越南成立。我们恳求你们向人类和平与自由的伟大的保卫者斯大林与新社会的建设者苏联人民转达我们的仰慕与团结之意。”
