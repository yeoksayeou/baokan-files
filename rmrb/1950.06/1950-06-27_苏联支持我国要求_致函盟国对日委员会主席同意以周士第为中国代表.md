Title: The Soviet Union Supports China's Demand: Letter to the Chairman of the Allied Council for Japan Agreeing to Zhou Shidi as China's Representative
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 1

　　The Soviet Union Supports China's Demand
    Letter to the Chairman of the Allied Council for Japan Agreeing to Zhou Shidi as China's Representative
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 26th] TASS Tokyo, June 26th dispatch: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, telegraphed Sebald, Chairman of the Allied Council for Japan, on June 19th, demanding that the Kuomintang representative be expelled from the Allied Council for Japan and notifying him that China had appointed Zhou Shidi as the representative to attend the Allied Council for Japan.
    On June 23rd, Poliansky, Acting Soviet Member of the Allied Council for Japan, sent a letter to Sebald supporting the demand of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to expel the Kuomintang representative from the Allied Council and to recognize General Zhou Shidi as the legitimate representative of China to the Allied Council for Japan.

### 苏联支持我国要求  致函盟国对日委员会主席同意以周士第为中国代表

1950-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联支持我国要求
    致函盟国对日委员会主席同意以周士第为中国代表
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社东京二十六日电：中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部长周恩来于六月十九日致电盟国对日委员会主席赛鲍尔，要求将国民党代表自盟国对日委员会中驱逐出去，并通知他中国已任命周士第为出席盟国对日委员会的代表。
    六月二十三日，盟国对日委员会苏联代理委员波莱耶兴哥致函赛鲍尔，支持中华人民共和国中央人民政府所提自盟国委员会驱逐国民党代表，并以周士第将军为中国出席盟国对日委员会合法代表的要求。
