Title: Opening of the Joint Session of the Two Chambers of the Third Supreme Soviet; Nationalities Chamber Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-06-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 14th, Moscow Dispatch: The joint session of the Union Chamber and the Nationalities Chamber of the Third Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened at 7:00 PM on the 13th. When Stalin and his closest comrades entered the government box, they were met with prolonged, stormy applause from all the deputies and guests. Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev delivered a report at the joint session on the Soviet Union's national budget for 1950 and the execution of the national budget for 1948-1949.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 14th, Moscow Dispatch: The first meeting of the Nationalities Chamber of the Third Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union was held at the Kremlin at 8:00 PM on the 12th. The deputies present symbolized the inviolable friendship of all the nationalities of the Soviet Union. Guests attending the meeting included diplomatic missions from various countries, representatives of the working people of Moscow, and many others.
　　When Soviet leaders Bulganin, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Shvernik, Kosygin, Suslov, Ponomarenko, and Shkiryatov appeared on the presidium seats, they were warmly welcomed by the deputies and guests.
　　One of the oldest deputies, Otto Kuusinen, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Karelo-Finnish Republic, presided over the opening. In his opening remarks, he pointed out that the Soviet Union steadfastly strives for peaceful coexistence with all countries while redoubling its efforts to fight for peace. After the speech, all the deputies stood up and cheered, "Glory to the great Stalin!"
　　The assembly unanimously elected Shaya Metov, a representative of the Kazakh people, as Chairman of the Nationalities Chamber; Azerbaijani representative Yakubov, Belarusian representative Zimyanin, Latvian Republic Council of Ministers Chairman Latsis, and Georgian middle school principal Matiashvili as Vice-Chairmen of the Nationalities Chamber.
　　The assembly then adopted the following agenda: 1. Election of the Credentials Committee of the Nationalities Chamber; 2. Election of the Standing Committees of the Nationalities Chamber; 3. Approval of the decrees promulgated by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; 4. Election of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; 5. Organization of the Soviet Government—the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; 6. Approval of the report on the execution of the Soviet Union's national budget for 1950 and the budget for 1948-1949.
　　The meeting decided to discuss the election of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the organization of the Soviet government at a joint session of the Union Chamber and the Nationalities Chamber. The joint session of the two chambers will also hear a report on the Soviet national budget, but the discussion of the report and the adoption of the budget bill will be conducted separately in the two chambers. The joint session of the two chambers is scheduled to be held on the 13th.
　　The meeting then proceeded with the first and second items on the agenda, electing a seventeen-member Credentials Committee and establishing the standing committees of the Nationalities Chamber: the Legislative Proposals Committee, the Budget Committee, and the Foreign Affairs Committee.

### 苏三届最高苏维埃两院联席会议开幕  民族院举行第一次会议

1950-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏三届最高苏维埃两院联席会议开幕
    民族院举行第一次会议
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第三届最高苏维埃联盟院与民族院联席会议于十三日下午七时开幕。当斯大林与他最亲密的战友们走进政府席时，受到全场代表和来宾们长时间的暴风雨般的欢呼。苏联财政部长兹维列夫在联席会议上作了关于苏联一九五○年的国家预算，及一九四八——一九四九年国家预算执行情况的报告。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第三届最高苏维埃民族院第一次会议于十二日下午八时在克里姆林宫举行。出席的代表象征了苏联各族人民不可侵犯的友谊。参加会议的来宾有各国外交使团，莫斯科劳动人民的代表等多人。
    当苏联领袖们布尔加宁，伏罗希洛夫，米高扬，什维尔尼克，柯西金，苏斯洛夫，波诺马林科和施基利亚托夫诸人出现在主席团席次上时，代表们和来宾们予以热烈的欢迎。
    年事最高的代表之一、卡累利阿——芬兰共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席奥图·库西宁主持开幕。他在开幕词中指出苏联不屈不挠地力求与一切国家和平共处，同时再接再厉地为和平而斗争。致词完毕后，代表全体起立，欢呼“光荣归于伟大的斯大林！”
    大会一致选举哈萨克人民代表沙亚米托夫为民族院主席；阿塞拜疆代表亚库伯夫，白俄罗斯代表齐姆亚宁，拉脱维亚共和国部长会议主席拉希斯和乔治亚中学校长马提阿施维利等人为民族院副主席。
    大会继通过会议议程如下：一、选举民族院资格审查委员会；二、选举民族院各常设委员会；三、批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布的法令；四、选举苏联最高苏维埃主席团；五、组织苏联政府——苏联部长会议；六、批准苏联一九五○年国家预算及一九四八——一九四九年度预算之执行情况的报告。
    会议决定在联盟院与民族院联席会议上讨论选举最高苏维埃主席团与组织苏联政府的问题。两院联席会议并将听取关于苏联国家预算的报告，但是讨论报告与通过预算法案将分别在两院进行。两院联席会议定于十三日举行。
    会议继进行了议程的第一、第二两项，选出了十七人的资格审查委员会，并成立了民族院的各常设委员会：法案委员会、预算委员会与外交事务委员会。
