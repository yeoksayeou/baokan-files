Title: Second Cultural Center Holds Women's Marriage Lecture

Author: Luo Zhenqing
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 3

　　The Second Cultural Center Holds Women's Marriage Lecture
    [Reported by this newspaper] In order to ensure that the broad masses of sisters have a general understanding of the Marriage Law, the Second Cultural Center and the Women's Work Group of the Sixth District Committee will hold the second women's marriage problem lecture at the center at 7 pm on the 10th. Comrades from the Women's Work Group will be the main speakers. Discussions will be held after the lecture. After the meeting, the amateur theater troupe of the Cultural Center will perform the three-act play "Freedom of Marriage."
    (Luo Zhenqing)

### 第二文化馆举行妇女婚姻讲座会
罗振青
1950-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　第二文化馆举行妇女婚姻讲座会
    【本报讯】第二文化馆与六区区委会妇女工作组，为了使广大姐妹们对婚姻法有普遍的认识，订于十日下午七时在该馆举办第二次妇女婚姻问题讲座会，由妇女工作组同志主讲，讲后进行讨论，会后由文化馆业余剧团演出《婚姻自由》三幕话剧。
    （罗振青）
