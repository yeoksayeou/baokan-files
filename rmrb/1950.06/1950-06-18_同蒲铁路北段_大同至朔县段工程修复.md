Title: Restoration of the Datong-Shuoxian Section of the Northern Segment of the Tongpu Railway
Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Datong-Shuoxian section of the Tongpu Railway was restored on May 31st. This section is 128 kilometers long. With the restoration of this section, only the Shuoxian-Yuanping section, which is 104 kilometers long, remains to be restored on the entire Tongpu Railway line.

### 同蒲铁路北段  大同至朔县段工程修复

1950-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　同蒲铁路北段
    大同至朔县段工程修复
    【新华社十七日讯】同蒲铁路大同至朔县段已于五月三十一日修复。该段长一百二十八公里。该段修复后，同蒲路全线仅余朔县至原平一段一百零四公里尚待修复。
