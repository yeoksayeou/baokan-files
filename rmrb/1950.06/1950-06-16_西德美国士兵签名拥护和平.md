Title: West German American Soldiers Sign in Support of Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 15th. TASS reported from Berlin on the 13th: According to the "Deutsche Nachrichten" newspaper, a large number of American black soldiers in Mainz, West Germany, have signed the World Peace Council's Peace Declaration. Many soldiers of the American occupation forces have also signed in Bavaria and Hesse.

### 西德美国士兵签名拥护和平

1950-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　西德美国士兵签名拥护和平
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社柏林十三日讯：据《德意志新闻报》消息：在西德的美因兹，有一大批美国黑人兵士已在世界和大常委会的和平宣言上签了名。在巴伐利亚和黑森州，也有很多美国占领军的兵士签了名。
