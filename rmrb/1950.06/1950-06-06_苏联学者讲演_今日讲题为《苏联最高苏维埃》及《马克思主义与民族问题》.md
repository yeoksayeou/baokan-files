Title: Soviet Scholars Lecture: Today's Topics are "The Supreme Soviet of the USSR" and "Marxism and the National Question"
Author:
Date: 1950-06-06
Page: 3

　　Soviet Scholars Lecture
    Today's Topics are "The Supreme Soviet of the USSR" and "Marxism and the National Question"
    [This newspaper report] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association are sponsoring a series of academic lectures by Soviet scholars. The fifth lecture on law is scheduled for 3:00 PM on June 6th at Fu Jen Catholic University. Professor Askelov, a renowned Soviet legal expert, will lecture on "The Supreme Soviet of the USSR," elaborating on the social system and state system of the Soviet Union.
    The fifth lecture on philosophy will also be held at 7:00 PM on June 6th at Fu Jen Catholic University. Professor Chesnokov, an authoritative Soviet philosopher lecturing in China, will present "Marxism and the National Question," clarifying the definition of a nation, the three stages of development of the national question, the October Socialist Revolution and the national question, the Second World War and the national question, and the future of nations and national languages. Those holding admission tickets and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's "Introduction to the Soviet Union" long-term lecture passes are welcome to attend.

### 苏联学者讲演  今日讲题为《苏联最高苏维埃》及《马克思主义与民族问题》

1950-06-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联学者讲演
    今日讲题为《苏联最高苏维埃》及《马克思主义与民族问题》
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会总会与北京市中苏友好协会主办的苏联学者学术讲演会，法学第五讲定于六月六日下午三时在辅仁大学举行。由苏联著名法律学专家阿斯凯洛夫教授主讲《苏联最高苏维埃》，阐述苏联的社会制度与国家制度。
    哲学第五讲亦于六日下午七时在辅仁大学举行。由此次来华讲学的苏联权威哲学家车斯诺柯夫教授主讲《马克思主义与民族问题》，阐明民族的定义，民族问题发展的三个时期，十月社会主义革命与民族问题，第二次世界大战与民族问题，民族和民族语言的未来等问题。凡持有入场券和北京市中苏友协“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
