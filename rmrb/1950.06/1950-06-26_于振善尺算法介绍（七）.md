Introduction to Yu Zhensan's Slide Rule Algorithm (7)

　　[Example] A person has 500 yuan. How much money should 40 people have?
　　Calculation: As shown in Figure 12:
　　(1) Align 1 or 10 on the sky scale (representing people) with 5 on the ground scale (representing yuan) (representing 500 yuan).
　　(2) The number on the ground scale that aligns with 4 on the sky scale (representing 40 people) is the product.
　　(3) Locate the decimal: Since 2 is less than 5 or 4, the number of digits in the product = 3 + 2 = 5. Therefore, we get: 40 people should have 20,000 yuan.
　　(Note) The numbers 2, 3, 4, ..., on the sky scale correspond to 1, 1.5, 2, ... on the ground scale, which are the products of 5 × 2, 5 × 3, 5 × 4, ... respectively.
　　8. Division
　　Dividend ÷ Divisor = Quotient
　　Use a slide rule: Square slide rule calculator (including one positive sky scale and one ground scale), circular slide rule calculator, or rectangular slide rule calculator.
　　When using a square slide rule calculator, fix the 0 point of the sky scale on the 0 line of the ground scale.
　　Calculation:
　　(1) Align the divisor on the sky scale (or ground scale) with the dividend on the ground scale (or sky scale).
　　(2) The number on the ground scale (or sky scale) that aligns with 1 or 10 on the sky scale (or ground scale) is the quotient.
　　Decimal Placement: There are two scenarios:
　　(1) If the first digit of the quotient is less than or equal to the first digit of the dividend, then:
　　Number of digits in the quotient = Number of digits in the dividend - Number of digits in the divisor + 1.
　　(2) If the first digit of the quotient is greater than the first digit of the dividend, then:
　　Number of digits in the quotient = Number of digits in the dividend - Number of digits in the divisor.
　　(Note) When using a square slide rule calculator, in order to facilitate reading, the inclination of the sky scale should not be too small. Sometimes, the sky scale and the ground scale need to be interchanged.

### 于振善尺算法介绍（七）

1950-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　于振善尺算法介绍（七）
    〔例题〕一个人有５００元，问４０人应有多少元？
    求法：如第十二图所示：
    （１）把天尺（代表人）的１或１０对准地尺（代表元）上的５（代表５００元）。
    （２）天尺上的４（代表４０人）对着的地尺上的２即是所求的乘积。
    （３）定位：因２小于５或４，所以乘积的位数＝３＋２＝５。所以求得：４０人应有２００００元。
    （注）天尺上的２，３，４，……，分别对着地尺上的１，１．５，２……，即是５×２，５×３，５×４，……的乘积。
    八、除法
    被除数÷除数＝商数
    用尺：方形尺算器（包括正数天尺及地尺各一个）或圆形尺算器或长形尺算器。
    用方形尺算器时，将天尺的０点固定在地尺的０线上。
    求法：
    （１）把天尺（或地尺）的除数对准地尺（或天尺）的被除数。
    （２）天尺（或地尺）的１或１０对着的地尺（或天尺）上的数即是所求的商数。
    定位法：分两种情形：
    （１）如商数的第一位数小于或等于被除数的第一位时，则：
    商数的位数＝被除数的位数—除数的位数＋１。
    （２）如商数的第一数位大于被除数的第一位数时，则：
    商数的位数＝被除数的位数—除数的位数。
    （注）用方形尺算器时为了读数方便起见，天尺的倾斜度不能太小，有时需要将天尺和地尺互相调换来用。
