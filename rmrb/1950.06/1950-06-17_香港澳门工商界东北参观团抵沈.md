Title: Hong Kong and Macau Business Community Delegation Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 16th – The Hong Kong and Macau business community delegation arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 15th.栗又文, Secretary-General of the Northeast People's Government, Zhou Qiuye, Secretary-General of the United Front Work Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Han Yongzan, Director of the Shenyang Municipal People's Government Industry and Commerce Bureau, and Lu Guangji, Chairman of the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, went to the station to welcome them. The delegation consists of forty-two people, led by Huang Changshui (Manager of Hong Kong Quanchang Co., Ltd.), Chen Junleng (Standing Director of the Hong Kong Chinese Manufacturers' Association), Mo Yinggui (Representative of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce), and Ma Wanqi (Representative of the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce). Peng Guanghan, Deputy Director of the Research Office of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, accompanied them to the Northeast.

### 香港澳门工商界东北参观团抵沈

1950-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　香港澳门工商界东北参观团抵沈
    【新华社沈阳十六日电】港澳工商界东北参观团十五日上午抵达沈阳。东北人民政府秘书长栗又文、中共中央东北局统一战线工作部秘书长周秋野、沈阳市人民政府工商局长韩永赞及沈市工商联合会理事长卢广绩等均赴车站欢迎。参观团由四十二人组成，由团长黄长水（香港泉昌有限公司经理）、副团长陈君冷（香港中华厂商联合会常务理事）、莫应桂（香港华商总会代表）、马万祺（澳门华商总会代表）率领。中央人民政府华侨事务委员会特派研究室副主任彭光涵陪同前来东北。
