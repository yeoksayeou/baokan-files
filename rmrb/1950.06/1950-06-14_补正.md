　　Corrigendum
    In the news item "Special Agent Li An and Others Sneak into Schools, Resist Registration; Municipal Public Security Bureau Decides to Arrest and Punish" on page of the May 29th edition, the following should be added after "Special agent Wu Guohua infiltrated Jiaotong University’s Pingyuan campus before liberation": "(currently a fourth-year 'student' in the Transportation Department of the said campus)". This is hereby corrected.
