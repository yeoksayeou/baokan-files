Title: Oppose the US Imperialist Crimes of Destroying the Greek People: Athens Establishes Underground Peace Committee, Calls on People Nationwide to Participate in Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 15th, TASS: Free Greece Radio broadcast on June 13th that a Peace Committee has been established in Athens. Due to rampant domestic terror, the committee has to work underground.

The Athens Peace Committee issued a proclamation to the Greek people, pointing out that the United States, on the one hand, is preparing for a new war, and on the other hand, is conducting new war exercises in Greece, causing countless Greeks to lose their lives. The proclamation emphasized, "The Americans are preparing to completely destroy Greece and its people." The proclamation continued, "The struggle for peace will save Greece from the catastrophe prepared by the imperialists. The Peace Committee urges Greeks to sign the Stockholm Appeal."

Free Greece Radio declared that the Peace Committee's peace declaration is "a militant call to all Greek people, urging them to establish national-scale organizations to defend peace."

### 反对美帝毁灭希腊人民的罪行  雅典成立地下和委会  向全国人民呼吁参加和平签名

1950-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对美帝毁灭希腊人民的罪行
    雅典成立地下和委会 
    向全国人民呼吁参加和平签名
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：自由希腊电台于六月十三日广播：雅典已成立和平委员会。因为国内恐怖盛行，该会不得不在地下工作。
    雅典和平委员会发表告希腊人民书指出：美国一方面在准备新战争，一方面在希腊演习新战争，使无数希腊人丧失性命。文告中强调指出：“美国人正在准备整个地毁灭希腊及其人民。”文告继称：“为和平而进行的斗争，将把希腊从帝国主义者准备着的浩劫中拯救出来。和平委员会敦促希腊人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。”
    自由希腊电台宣称：和平委员会的这个和平宣言，是“向一切希腊人民发出的战斗性的号召，要求他们建立全国规模的保卫和平的组织”。
