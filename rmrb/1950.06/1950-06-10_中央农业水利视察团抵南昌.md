Title: Central Agricultural Water Conservancy Inspection Group Arrives in Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 7th. The agricultural water conservancy inspection group of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, led by Minister Li Shucheng, arrived in Nanchang on the morning of the 5th. The group departed from Beijing in early May, arrived in Kaifeng on the 11th of last month, and after inspecting the Yellow River flooded area in Henan, proceeded to Hankou, passing through Changsha to Guangzhou and returning to Nanchang. The inspection group inspected the agricultural water conservancy situation in various regions along the way, and provided timely guidance to local agricultural production and water conservancy construction. On the day of its arrival in Nanchang, the group held a symposium at the Jiangxi Provincial Government to learn about the province's agricultural water conservancy construction, as well as the situation of animal diseases, pests, soil, and special agricultural products.

### 中央农业水利视察团抵南昌

1950-06-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央农业水利视察团抵南昌
    【新华社南昌七日电】中央人民政府农业部李书城部长率领的农业水利视察团，已于五日晨到达南昌。该团于五月上旬自北京出发，上月十一日到达开封，在河南视察黄泛区后到汉口，经长沙至广州折返南昌。该视察团沿途视察各地区农业水利情况，并予各地的农业生产和水利建设以及时指导。该团到南昌当日即在江西省府举行座谈会，了解该省农业水利建设概况及兽疫、害虫、土壤及农业特产等情形。
