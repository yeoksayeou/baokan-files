Title: Shanghai English Newspaper "Shanghai News" Published
Author:
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th. The English newspaper "Shanghai News" here was launched on the 10th. The newspaper is published six days a week, with a two-sided sheet each day and no newspaper on Mondays. Jin Zhonghua serves as the president and editor-in-chief of the newspaper. The newspaper's address is 150 East Yan'an Road, Shanghai.

### 上海英文报纸《上海新闻》出版

1950-06-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海英文报纸《上海新闻》出版
    【新华社上海十三日电】此间英文报纸《上海新闻》已于十日创刊。该报每周出版六天，每天出对开两面一张，星期一无报。金仲华任该报社长兼总编辑。该报地址为上海市延安东路一五○号。
