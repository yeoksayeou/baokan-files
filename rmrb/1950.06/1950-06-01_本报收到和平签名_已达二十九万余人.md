Title: This Newspaper Receives Peace Signatures: Over 290,000 People
Author:
Date: 1950-06-01
Page: 4

This Newspaper Receives Peace Signatures
　　Already Reached Over 290,000 People
　　[This Newspaper] This newspaper yesterday continued to receive letters containing peace signatures from various places, totaling 31,578 people. This included the entire body of commanders and fighters of the First Division of the Central Column of the People's Public Security Forces, all commanders and fighters of the artillery units of the North China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and the entire village of Shaxinzhuang in the Third District of Xiongzhou, Hebei, as well as signatures from groups and individuals such as all teachers and students of Dali Teachers College in Shaanxi. Together with the previous count, this totals 292,970 people.

### 本报收到和平签名  已达二十九万余人

1950-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　本报收到和平签名
    已达二十九万余人
    【本报讯】本报昨日继续收到各地和平签名来信，总计三万一千五百七十八人。其中包括人民公安部队中央纵队第一师全师指战员、人民解放军华北军区炮兵部队全体指战员和河北雄县三区沙辛庄全村农民、陕西大荔师范全体师生等团体与个人的签名。连前合计二十九万二千九百七十人。
