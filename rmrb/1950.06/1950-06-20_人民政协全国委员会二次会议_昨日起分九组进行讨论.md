Title: The Second Session of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Commenced Discussions in Nine Groups Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference began discussions in nine groups today, namely the General Affairs Group, Land Reform Group, Finance and Economics Group, Political Group, Military Group, Culture and Education Group, Court Work Group, National Emblem Group, and Proposal Review Group. The list of conveners for each group is as follows:

　　General Affairs Group: Wang Kunlun, Zhou Xinmin;
　　Land Reform Group: Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun;
　　Finance and Economics Group: Zhang Naiqi, Chen Jiageng, Pan Hannian;
　　Political Group: Xu Deheng, Luo Longji, Ulanhu;
　　Military Group: Peng Dehuai, Zhang Zhizhong;
　　Culture and Education Group: Zheng Zhenduo, Zhou Yang, Xu Guangping;
　　Court Work Group: Dong Biwu, Shi Liang;
　　National Emblem Group: Shen Yanbing, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Chong;
　　Proposal Review Group: Lin Feng, Chen Qiyuan, Zhang Youyu.

### 人民政协全国委员会二次会议  昨日起分九组进行讨论

1950-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协全国委员会二次会议
    昨日起分九组进行讨论
    【新华社十九日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议今日起分为九组进行讨论，即会务组、土改组、财经组、政治组、军事组、文教组、法院工作组、国徽组、提案审查组，各组召集人名单如下：
    会务组：王昆仑、周新民；
    土改组：彭真、陈叔通、章伯钧；
    财经组：章乃器、陈嘉庚、潘汉年；
    政治组：许德珩、罗隆基、乌兰夫；
    军事组：彭德怀、张治中；
    文教组：郑振铎、周扬、许广平；
    法院工作组：董必武、史良；
    国徽组：沈雁冰、张奚若、张冲；
    提案审查组：林枫、陈其瑗、张友渔。
