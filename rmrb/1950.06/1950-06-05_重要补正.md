　　Important Correction
    In the fifth line of the second paragraph of the *People's Daily* editorial on page one, "Run Factory Management Committees Well and Conscientiously Implement Democratic Management," the words "trade union," should be added after "Communist Party organization in the factory, ". This is hereby corrected.
