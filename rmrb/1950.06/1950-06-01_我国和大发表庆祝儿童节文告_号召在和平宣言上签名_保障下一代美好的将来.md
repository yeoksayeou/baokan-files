Title: Our Country and Da Fa Issue a Message Celebrating Children's Day, Calling for Signatures on the Peace Declaration to Guarantee a Bright Future for the Next Generation
Author:
Date: 1950-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 31st: The China Peace Committee today issued a message celebrating the June 1st International Children's Day, calling on all Chinese mothers and fathers to sign the World Peace Council's Declaration for Peace on the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons. The message emphasizes: "Only by thoroughly crushing the imperialists' sinister plot to create a new war can we save our own children from the most unfortunate disasters and truly guarantee a bright future for our next generation."

### 我国和大发表庆祝儿童节文告  号召在和平宣言上签名  保障下一代美好的将来

1950-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国和大发表庆祝儿童节文告
    号召在和平宣言上签名  保障下一代美好的将来
    【新华社三十一日讯】中国保卫世界和平委员会本日发表庆祝六、一国际儿童节文告，号召全中国的母亲和父亲们，在世界和大常委会关于禁止原子武器的和平宣言上签名。文告着重指出：“只有彻底粉碎帝国主义者制造新战争的罪恶图谋，才能使我们自己的孩子避免最不幸的灾害，才能真正保障我们下一代美好的将来。”
