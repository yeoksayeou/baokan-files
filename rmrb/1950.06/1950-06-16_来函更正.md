Title: Correction

Author:

Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 1

　　Correction

    In the news item "Beijing Public Security Bureau Arrests Three Batches of Special Agents" issued by the Propaganda Section of this bureau and published on the front page of the People's Daily on the 10th of this month, in the last paragraph, line seventeen, "贾伯侯" should be "贾伯侠", and in line twenty-six, "雷宝山" should be "雷宝正". We hereby make this correction.
                                      Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau

### 来函更正

1950-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　来函更正
    本月十日人民日报第一版刊登本局宣传科所发的“北京市公安局逮捕三批特务”消息中最后一段，第十七行之“贾伯侯”应为“贾伯侠”，第二十六行之“雷宝山”应为“雷宝正”，特此更正。
                                      北京市人民政府公安局
