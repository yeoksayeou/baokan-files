Title: Widespread Rain in East China, Shandong Rainfall Reaches Tilling Depth, Farmers Across the Province Are Rushing to Summer Planting
Author:
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 25th. During the summer planting season, East China has experienced widespread rainfall in recent days. Shandong Province generally experienced heavy rain on the 22nd and 23rd, with rainfall reaching tilling depth in most areas. Northern Jiangsu, Northern Anhui, and Southern Jiangsu also experienced heavy rain, with accumulated water in Northern Jiangsu reaching about two to three inches. Southern Anhui, Zhejiang, and Fujian also experienced light rain. After the rain in Shandong Province, farmers are rushing to summer planting. However, Ningyang, Dongping, Wenshang, Taining (newly established county, parts of Taian and Ningyang), Boxing and other counties in the province, as well as some areas in Northern Anhui, experienced hail during the rainfall, causing damage to some crops. In Ningyang County, more than 24,700 mu of cotton fields were destroyed.

### 华东各地普雨  山东雨量达透犁程度  全省农民正突击夏种

1950-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　华东各地普雨
    山东雨量达透犁程度  全省农民正突击夏种
    【新华社上海二十五日电】时值夏种节令，近日华东各地普遍降雨。山东全省在二十二、二十三日普降大雨，大部地区雨量已达透犁程度。苏北、皖北、苏南也普下大雨，苏北积雨约达二三寸。皖南、浙江、福建各地也下了小雨。山东全省下雨后，农民正突击夏种。但该省宁阳、东平、汶上、泰宁（新设县，泰安、宁阳各一部）、博兴等县以及皖北部分地区在下雨时曾同时降冰雹，某些农作物遭受损害，其中宁阳县有棉田二万四千七百余亩被打毁。
