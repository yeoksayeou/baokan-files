Title: Ministry of Heavy Industry Issues Circular to Subordinate Units: Summarize Work of First Two Quarters Within a Deadline; Stipulates that Quarterly Work Summaries Should Become a Regular System
Author:
Date: 1950-06-26
Page: 2

　　Circular from the Ministry of Heavy Industry to Subordinate Units
    Summarize Work of First Two Quarters Within a Deadline
    Stipulates that Quarterly Work Summaries Should Become a Regular System

    [Xinhua News Agency, 23rd] To comprehensively understand the recovery and construction of heavy industry production in various regions, and to facilitate the accelerated formulation of future national plans, the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government issued a circular on June 23rd to the industrial departments of major administrative regions and various specialized bureaus, setting a deadline for summarizing work from the second quarter and the first quarter of this year. It also stipulates that summarizing work on a quarterly basis should become a regular system.

### 重工业部通令所属单位  限期总结今年首两季度工作  规定按季总结工作应成为经常制度

1950-06-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　重工业部通令所属单位
    限期总结今年首两季度工作
    规定按季总结工作应成为经常制度
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央人民政府重工业部为集中了解各地区重工业生产的恢复、建设情况，便于加速制订今后全国计划，于六月二十三日通令各大行政区工业部及各专业局限期总结今年第二季度及第一季度工作；并规定按季度总结工作应成为经常制度。
