Title: Important Programs of the Central People's Broadcasting Station Next Week
Author:
Date: 1950-06-25
Page: 6

[This News Agency Report] The important broadcast programs from June 25th to July 1st are as follows:

Social Science Lectures: (Broadcast three times daily, at 6:00-7:00, 7:30-8:30, and 19:00-20:00).

　　26th and 27th: The lecture section of the station will speak on: Several questions regarding social science lectures. 28th: Yu Guangyuan will lecture on: Average Profit and Production Price (Political Economics Lecture 15). 29th: Same as above, replayed by the station's announcer. 30th, July 1st (changed to arts and entertainment programs).

Natural Science Lectures: (Broadcast from 18:45 to 19:00)

　　25th: (Changed to arts and entertainment programs). 26th and 27th: Huang Shuze will lecture on: Water and Hygiene. 28th: The lecture section of the station will speak on: Scabies. 29th: Zhang Yan will lecture on: Wind and Sandstorms. 30th: The lecture section of the station will speak on: Michurin's Method for Selecting Hybrid Seedlings. July 1st: The lecture section of the station will speak on: On the treatment of anemia and others (answering questions from Mr. Wang Li of Tianjin, etc.).

People's Forum: (Broadcast from 20:00 to 20:30)

　　25th: Program sponsored by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: Xu Yihang, Deputy Director of the Production Department of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, will speak on: How the trade union leads the masses in production competition from the economic perspective of the Northeast in the past year (recording). 26th: Program sponsored by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: Liu Keming, Director of the Production Department of the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, will speak on: Several issues in the production competition in Shenyang (recording). 27th: Yan Peilin, Director of the Education Department of the Industrial Department of the Northeast People's Government, will speak on: Higher industrial education in the Northeast that develops with industrial needs (recording). 28th: Program sponsored by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government: Liu Shi, Director of the Supervision Department of the Ministry of Education, will speak on: On the educational principle of integrating theory with practice. 29th: Listener contact time. 30th: Shen Jurui, Dean of Academic Affairs of Beijing Agricultural University, will speak on: The implementation of the new educational policy of Beijing Agricultural University. July 1st: Program sponsored by the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

Arts and Entertainment Programs (Broadcast from 20:30 to 21:00)

　　25th: Opera: The Central Academy of Drama Opera Troupe will perform The White-Haired Girl (He Jingzhi, Ding Yi, Ma Ke, Liu Chi, Chen Zi compose the music). 26th: Film introduction recording: Soviet film: Returning with Victory (translated by Northeast Film Studio). 27th: Soviet Songs: Lenin's Mountains, Volga River, The Green Open Country of the Village, Sparks, I Know a Beautiful Village, Lantern, Along the Small Garden. 28th: Literary Works Recitation: Qi Yue recites: The Story About a Child (by Soviet Lev Kassil, translated by Zi Zhu and Fang Fei). 29th: Drama: The Broadcasting Group of the Central Academy of Drama Troupe will perform Heading Down a Path (written by Zhao Xun and Lan Xian). 30th: Western Music Appreciation: Introducing the works of Mendelssohn. - Commemorating the "July 1st" Musical Songs Program

Sunday Arts and Entertainment Program (Broadcast from 18:45 to 20:00)

Music Evening: (1) Chorus: Tianjin Municipal Workers' Music Troupe sings: Red May Medley (lyrics by Zhang Xuexin, etc., music by Wang Xin). (2) Instrumental Ensemble: Jiang Fengzhi, etc. of the Music Department of Hebei Provincial Normal College play: Folk Song Medley, Mei Hu Medley. (3) Solo: Yu Yixuan sings: Liu Hulan (theme song of the opera "Liu Hulan"), Yan Yan Goes to the River to Wash Clothes (excerpt from the opera "Chi Ye River"). (4) Orchestra: The Central Conservatory of Music's Junior Class plays: "Baghdad" Overture (conducted by Huang Yuanli). (5) Recording of the music program broadcast to China by Moscow Radio on June 2nd.

### 中央人民广播电台下周重要节目

1950-06-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台下周重要节目
    【本报讯】六月二十五日至七月一日重要广播节目如下：
    社会科学讲座：（每天重复播讲三次，时间在六点到七点，七点三十分到八点三十分，十九点到二十点）。
    二十六、七日  该台讲座科讲：关于社会科学讲座的几个问题。二十八日于光远讲：平均利润和生产价格（政治经济学第十五讲）。二十九日  同上，由该台播音员重播三十日、七月一日（改播文艺节目）。
    自然科学讲座：（十八点四十五分到十九点播送）
    二十五日  （改播文艺节目）。二十六、七日  黄树则讲：水和卫生。二十八日  该台讲座科讲：疥疮。二十九日  章淹讲：风和风沙。三十日  该台讲座科讲：米邱林对于混种苗木的选择方法。七月一日  该台讲座科讲：关于贫血病的治疗及其他（答天津王利先生等问题）。
