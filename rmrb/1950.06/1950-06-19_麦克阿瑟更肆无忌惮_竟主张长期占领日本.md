Title: MacArthur More Unscrupulous, Actually Advocating Long-Term Occupation of Japan
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 15th (TASS): The New York Times' Tokyo correspondent, citing reliable sources, claims that MacArthur will suggest to US Secretary of Defense Johnson, Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Bradley, and Secretary of State Acheson's senior advisor Dulles, who are about to arrive in Japan, that "US armed forces be maintained in Japan, regardless of the form of the Japanese peace treaty." American military personnel are turning Japan into an American military base and demanding that the American occupation of Japan continue as long as possible.

### 麦克阿瑟更肆无忌惮  竟主张长期占领日本

1950-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟更肆无忌惮  竟主张长期占领日本
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社纽约十五日讯：纽约时报驻东京记者引可靠消息称：麦克阿瑟将向即将抵达日本的美国国防部长詹逊、参谋长联席会议主席布莱德雷及国务卿艾奇逊的高级顾问杜勒斯建议“在日本保持美国武装部队，不管对日和约的形式如何”。美国军人正将日本变成美国的军事基地，并要求尽可能长久地继续美国对日本的占领。
