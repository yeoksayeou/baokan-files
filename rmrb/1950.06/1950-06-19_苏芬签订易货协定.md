Title: Soviet-Finnish Barter Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1950-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th—It is reported by TASS that the Soviet Union and Finland signed a barter agreement regarding the years 1951 to 1955, and a barter protocol regarding 1950, on June 13th. The agreement was signed by Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, and Minister of Foreign Trade, Menshikov, and by Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen and Minister of Trade and Industry, Torniainen, respectively. After the agreement was signed, a communique was issued in Moscow. The communique stated that the negotiations were conducted in an atmosphere of complete mutual understanding.

### 苏芬签订易货协定

1950-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏芬签订易货协定
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏芬两国于六月十三日签订了关于一九五一年至一九五五年的易货协定及关于一九五○年的易货议定书。协定由苏联部长会议副主席米高扬与对外贸易部长孟希科夫及芬兰总理吉科宁与工商部部长杜米奥雅分别签字。协定签订后，在莫斯科发表了公报。公报称，谈判是在充满着互相充分谅解的空气中进行的。
