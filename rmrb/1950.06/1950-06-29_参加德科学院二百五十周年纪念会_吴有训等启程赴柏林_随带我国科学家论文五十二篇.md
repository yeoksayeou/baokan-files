Title: Wu Youxun and Others Depart for Berlin to Attend the 250th Anniversary Celebration of the German Academy of Sciences; Carrying Fifty-Two Papers by Chinese Scientists

Author:

Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th. The Chinese Academy of Sciences has sent Wu Youxun, Director of the Institute of Modern Physics of the Academy, Hua Luogeng, Professor of Tsinghua University, Wang Ganchang, Professor of Zhejiang University, and Yun Ziqiang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Academy, as representatives of China's scientific community to Berlin to participate in the 250th anniversary celebration of the German Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic, which will be held from July 11th to 14th. Wu Youxun and others departed on the 24th, bringing with them fifty-two papers by Chinese scientists, twenty-nine kinds of books and periodicals, a set of the science film "Tussah Silk," and other gifts. After the meeting, Wu and others plan to stay in the Soviet Union for a period to investigate the Soviet scientific research work.

### 参加德科学院二百五十周年纪念会  吴有训等启程赴柏林  随带我国科学家论文五十二篇

1950-06-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　参加德科学院二百五十周年纪念会
    吴有训等启程赴柏林
    随带我国科学家论文五十二篇
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国科学院派该院近代物理研究所所长吴有训、清华大学教授华罗庚、浙江大学教授王淦昌及该院办公厅副主任恽子强等四人，代表我国科学界赴柏林，参加将于七月十一日至十四日举行的德意志民主共和国德意志科学院二百五十周年纪念庆祝会。吴有训等已于二十四日启程出国，并随带有中国科学家论文五十二篇、书刊二十九种、科学电影片《柞蚕》一套和其他礼物多件。吴氏等于会后并拟在苏联逗留一个时期，考察苏联科学研究工作情况。
