Title: First Representative Conference of the Ordnance Industry Trade Union Opens: Conference to Determine the Content and Methods of Trade Union Work and Elect the National Committee of the China Ordnance Industry Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The first representative conference of the China Ordnance Industry Trade Union has opened in Beijing on the 8th. There are 131 representatives attending from all over the country, including ordnance workers, engineers, technicians, administrative personnel, and trade union workers. This conference will determine the content and methods of the ordnance industry trade union's work and will elect the leading body of the China Ordnance Industry Trade Union - the National Committee of the China Ordnance Industry Trade Union. After Zhao Guoqiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Ordnance Industry Trade Union, delivered the opening speech, Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke. He pointed out that the ordnance industry workers have a glorious record in the history of the Chinese People's Revolution and called on the ordnance industry workers to study culture and technology diligently in the future, improve product quality, and strive to defend world peace and build a modern national defense industry. Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also pointed out in his speech that before imperialism is eliminated, in order to ensure our country's peaceful construction, we must strengthen national defense and build a modern national defense industry.
　　Liu Ding, Deputy Minister of the Central Heavy Industry Department, reported to the conference on the production situation and production tasks of the ordnance industry this year, pointed out the development prospects of the ordnance industry, and called on all workers, staff, and technicians to unite, learn science and technology, and complete the task of national defense industrial construction in a planned and step-by-step manner.
　　He Changgong, Deputy Minister of the Central Heavy Industry Department, Mao Qihua, Deputy Minister of the Central Labor Department, and representatives of the China Railway Trade Union also spoke at the conference, congratulating the conference on its successful opening.

### 兵工工会首次代表会揭幕  会议将确定工会工作内容和方法  并选举中国兵工工会全国委员会

1950-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　兵工工会首次代表会揭幕
    会议将确定工会工作内容和方法
    并选举中国兵工工会全国委员会
    【新华社八日讯】中国兵工工会第一次代表大会已于八日在京揭幕，出席代表有全国各地的兵工工人、工程师、技术人员、行政人员及工会工作者一百三十一人。这次会议将确定兵工工会工作的内容、方法并将选举中国兵工工会的领导机关——中国兵工工会全国委员会。会议在中国兵工工会筹委会主任赵国强致开幕词后，由中华全国总工会李立三副主席讲话。他指出兵工业职工在中国人民革命史上有着光荣的成绩，号召兵工业职工今后努力学习文化、技术，提高产品质量，为保卫世界和平、建设现代化国防工业而奋斗。全国总工会朱学范副主席在发言中也指出：在帝国主义没有被消灭之前，为了保证我们国家的和平建设，必须加强国防，建设近代化的国防工业。
    中央重工业部刘鼎副部长向大会报告了本年度兵工工业的生产情况与生产任务，指出了兵工工业的发展前途，并号召全体工人、职员、技术人员团结起来，学习科学技术，有计划、有步骤地完成国防工业建设的任务。
    中央重工业部何长工副部长、中央劳动部毛齐华副部长及中国铁路工会的代表都相继在大会上讲话，祝贺大会的胜利成功。
