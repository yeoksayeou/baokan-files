Title: North China Liquor Monopoly Work Significantly Improved in April
Author:
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 2

　　North China Liquor Monopoly Work Significantly Improved in April
　　Since the Second Liquor Industry Management Conference in March, the North China liquor monopoly work has improved due to the attention of leadership at all levels. According to preliminary statistics, the actual sales volume of liquor in major cities in North China in April mostly exceeded the original plan, except for Shijiazhuang. Among them, Tangshan achieved the best results, with actual sales exceeding the original plan by 73.34%.

### 华北酒专卖工作四月有显著改进

1950-06-10
第2版()
专栏：各地简讯

　　华北酒专卖工作四月有显著改进
    华北酒业专卖工作，自三月间二届酒业经营会议后，由于各级领导机关的重视，已有改进。据初步统计：华北各主要城市四月份实销酒数除石家庄外，大部均已超过原计划。其中以唐山市成绩最好，实销超过原计划百分之七三·三四。
