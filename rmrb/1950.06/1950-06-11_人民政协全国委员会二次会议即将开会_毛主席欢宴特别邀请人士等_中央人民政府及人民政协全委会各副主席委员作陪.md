Title: The Second Session of the National Committee of the CPPCC is About to Open, Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Specially Invited Guests, Accompanied by Vice Chairmen and Members of the Central People's Government and the National Committee of the CPPCC

Author:
Date: 1950-06-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is about to open in the coming days. Chairman Mao Zedong specially hosted a banquet on the 10th for the specially invited guests of this meeting: Burhan, Liu Wenhui, Deng Xihou, Lu Han, Xiong Kewu, Li Genyuan, Zhang Fenghui, Liang Shuming, Dan Maoxin, Tang Shengzhi, Tao Zhiyue, Pei Changhui, Chen Tie, Chen Quzhen, Liang Juwu, Wu Yun Chu, Rong Yiren, Guo Dihuo, Xiang Shuxiang, Zai Tao, and 35 others, 46 representatives dispatched by local consultative committees, and Li Siguang, Hua Luogeng, Zhou Taixuan from the cultural circles, and labor heroes from various places Zhao Guoyou, Zhao Guilan, Du Xianyang, Pan Changyou, Yuan Daohua, Gong Quanmei, etc. Vice Chairmen and members of the Central People's Government and the National Committee of the CPPCC: Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Gao Gang, Zhou Enlai, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Huang Yanpei, Chen Yi, He Long, Peng Dehuai, Liu Bocheng, Nie Rongzhen, Rao Shushi, Ma Yinchu, Lin Feng, Gao Chongmin, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Zhizhong, Cheng Qian, Zhang Nanxian, Long Yun and others were invited to accompany them.

　　人民政协全国委员会二次会议即将开会
    毛主席欢宴特别邀请人士等
    中央人民政府及人民政协全委会各副主席委员作陪
