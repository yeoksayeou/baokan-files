Title: Chekanov to Give a Lecture Today Commemorating Sverdlov; Ostrovsky's 64th Death Anniversary Memorial Meeting to be Held Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-06-13
Page: 3

　　Chekanov to Give a Lecture Today
    Commemorating Sverdlov
    Ostrovsky's 64th Death Anniversary Memorial Meeting to be Held Tomorrow
    [Reported by this newspaper] To commemorate the 65th anniversary of the birth of Sverdlov, one of the main organizers of the Soviet Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the Soviet Republic, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association will hold a memorial meeting at 7:30 pm today (June 13th) at 19 Nanheyan. Chekanov will speak on "Sverdlov."
    In addition: June 14th is the 64th anniversary of the death of the great Russian playwright Ostrovsky. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association have scheduled a memorial meeting on this day at 19 Nanheyan. Saviyalova will speak on "Ostrovsky." Anyone holding a ticket and a long-term listening pass to the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's "Introduction to the Soviet Union" series can attend.
                                (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 切卡诺夫今讲演  纪念斯维尔德洛夫  明天举行奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世六十四周年纪念会

1950-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　切卡诺夫今讲演
    纪念斯维尔德洛夫
    明天举行奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世六十四周年纪念会
    【本报讯】北京苏联对外文化协会与北京市中苏友好协会为纪念苏联共产党（布）和苏维埃共和国的主要组织者之一斯维尔德洛夫诞生六十五岁周年，定于今日（六月十三日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行纪念会，请切卡诺夫讲“斯维尔德洛夫”。
    又讯：六月十四日是俄国大剧作家奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世六十四周年纪念日。北京苏联对外文化协会与北京市中苏友好协会定是日在南河沿十九号举行纪念会，请萨维亚洛娃讲《奥斯特洛夫斯基》。凡持有入场券和北京市中苏友协“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，均可前往参加。
                                （北京市中苏友好协会）
