Title: People's Pharmacy to Open on July 1st
Author: 
Date: 1950-06-29
Page: 3

　　People's Pharmacy to Open on July 1st

　　[Our Dispatch] The People's Pharmacy, under the leadership of the Beijing Public Health Bureau, is now fully prepared and scheduled to officially open on July 1st. On the morning of the 30th, at 8:00 AM, a forum will be held at the pharmacy (Donghuamen Street), inviting responsible persons from various pharmacies and pharmaceutical factories in Beijing to participate, reporting on the preparation process and the significance of the opening. The establishment of this pharmacy aims to unite public and private pharmacies and pharmaceutical factories in the city, and to establish connections with pharmacies and pharmaceutical factories in various provinces and cities, jointly seeking the improvement and development of the Western medicine industry.

### 人民药房七一开业

1950-06-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　人民药房七一开业
    【本报讯】京市公共卫生局领导的人民药房业已筹备就绪，订于七月一日正式开幕，并于三十日上午八时在该药房（东华门大街）召开座谈会，邀集京市各药房，药厂负责人参加，报告筹备经过及开设意义。该药房的成立，旨在团结本市公私立药房、药厂，并与各省市药房、药厂取得联系，共谋西药业的改进与发展。
