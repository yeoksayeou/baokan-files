Title: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC Continued to Hold a General Meeting Yesterday. Vice Chairman Liu Made Conclusions on the Discussion of Land Reform. Fourteen People, Including Li Wujiu, Spoke During Yesterday's Discussion.
Author:
Date: 1950-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a general meeting today.

　　After the meeting began, the discussions continued, followed by a speech by Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Standing Committee Member of the National Committee, regarding the conclusions on the discussion of land reform issues at this meeting.

　　Those who spoke during the discussion today included industrialist Li Wujiu, Wen Shaohe, representative of the Chongqing Local Consultative Committee, Wang Baozhen, member of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council, Li Dequan, member of the National Committee and Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Fu Zuoyi, member of the National Committee and Chairman of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee, Cheng Qian, member of the National Committee and Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, Lin Feng, member of the National Committee and Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Chen Jiageng, member of the National Committee, Zhang Naiqi, member of the National Committee, Zhang Bojun, Chairman of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party, gentleman Ye Gongchuo, Pan Hannian, member of the National Committee and Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, Liu Bocheng, Chairman of the Southwest Military and Political Committee, and Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee and Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government.

### 人民政协首届全国委员会二次会议昨继续举行大会  刘副主席作讨论土地改革问题的结论  昨日讨论时有厉无咎等十四人发言

1950-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协首届全国委员会二次会议昨继续举行大会
    刘副主席作讨论土地改革问题的结论
    昨日讨论时有厉无咎等十四人发言
    【新华社二十二日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议今日举行大会。
    大会开始后继续进行讨论，接着听取全国委员会常务委员中央人民政府副主席刘少奇关于这次会议讨论土地改革问题的结论。
    今日在讨论时发言的，有工业家厉无咎，重庆地方协商委员会代表温少鹤，政务院政治法律委员会委员王葆真，全国委员会委员、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全，全国委员会委员、绥远军政委员会主席傅作义，全国委员会委员、中南军政委员会副主席程潜，全国委员会委员、东北人民政府副主席林枫，全国委员会委员陈嘉庚，全国委员会委员章乃器，中国农工民主党主席章伯钧，绅耆叶恭绰，全国委员会委员、上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年，西南军政委员会主席刘伯承，中南军政委员会副主席、广东省人民政府主席叶剑英。
