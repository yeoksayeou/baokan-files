Title: Syngman Rhee Puppet Army Launches Full-Scale Offensive Against North Korea; Korean Republic Guard Units Initiate Defensive Battles; Korean Central News Agency Condemns War-Monger Dulles' Activities in South Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-06-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th: The Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a communique at 10:00 AM on the 25th, stating: The South Korean puppet national defense forces unexpectedly launched a full-scale offensive against North Korea along the 38th parallel (note: the 38th parallel north is the demarcation line between North and South Korea) at dawn on the 25th. The enemy forces invaded North Korea in three directions, one to two kilometers deep, in the western Haeju area and the Kimchon area of Hwanghae Province, and the Chorwon area of Kangwon Province. The Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has ordered the Republic's guard units to repel the enemy forces invading north of the 38th parallel. Currently, the Republic's guard units are resisting the enemy and engaging in fierce defensive battles.

　　The communique stated: The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has entrusted the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Republic to warn the South Korean puppet government authorities: If the South Korean puppet government authorities do not immediately cease their adventurous offensive north of the 38th parallel, the Republic will take decisive countermeasures to strike the enemy. At the same time, the South Korean puppet government will bear full responsibility for all serious consequences arising from this adventurous offensive.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 25th: TASS, Pyongyang, 22nd: Korean newspapers published a commentary by the Korean Central News Agency on the arrival of the notorious war-monger Dulles in Seoul on the 17th. After describing Dulles' activities in South Korea, the Korean Central News Agency pointed out: Dulles came to Korea to encourage the Syngman Rhee traitorous clique and the "United Nations Commission on Korea." American imperialists have ordered Syngman Rhee to disregard everything, obstruct the peaceful reunification of the country, and create a civil war in order to further strengthen American rule in South Korea.

### 李承晚伪军向北朝鲜发动全线进攻  朝鲜共和国警备队展开防御战  朝鲜中央通讯社斥责战贩杜勒斯在南朝鲜的活动

1950-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　李承晚伪军向北朝鲜发动全线进攻
    朝鲜共和国警备队展开防御战
    朝鲜中央通讯社斥责战贩杜勒斯在南朝鲜的活动
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内务部二十五日上午十时发表公报称：南朝鲜的伪国防军突于二十五日拂晓在三八线（按：北纬三十八度为南北朝鲜的交界线）全线向北朝鲜发动了意外的进攻。敌军在黄海道的海州西部地区与金川地区以及江原道的铁原地区，三路侵入北朝鲜一公里至二公里。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内务部已命令共和国警备队将侵入三八线以北地区的敌人予以击退。目前，共和国警备队正抵抗着敌人，展开了激烈的防御战。
    公报称：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府已委托共和国内务部向南朝鲜伪政府当局提出警告：假若南朝鲜伪政府当局不立即停止对三八线以北地区的冒险进攻，共和国将采取决定性的对策，打击敌人。同时，由于这一冒险的进攻而引起的一切严重后果，应由南朝鲜伪政府负完全责任。
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社平壤二十二日讯：朝鲜各报发表了朝鲜中央通讯社对臭名昭彰的战争贩子杜勒斯于十七日抵达汉城一事的评论。朝鲜中央通讯社在叙述了杜勒斯在南朝鲜的活动后指出：杜勒斯是为了鼓励李承晚卖国集团和“联合国朝鲜委员会”而来朝鲜的。美国帝国主义者已命令李承晚不顾一切，阻挠全国的和平统一，并制造内战，以便更加强美国在南朝鲜的统治。
