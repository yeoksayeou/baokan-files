Title: South Korean Patriotic Students Support Fatherland Front Call: Daegu Middle School Girls Hold Anti-American Strike
Author:
Date: 1950-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th. According to the Korean Central News Agency: The appeal to the people of the whole country issued by the Korean Fatherland Front on the 7th to promote the peaceful reunification of the motherland has been warmly supported by patriotic students in South Korea. In response to the call of the Fatherland Front, students of the Daegu Girls' Middle School in Gyeongsangnam-do started a strike on the 10th to oppose the conspiracy of the American and Syngman Rhee reactionaries to split Korea and enslave the Korean people. Dozens of female students were arrested and interrogated by the Syngman Rhee police as a result, but the strike is still ongoing.

### 朝鲜南部爱国学生拥护祖国战线号召  大邱市女中举行反美罢课

1950-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜南部爱国学生拥护祖国战线号召
    大邱市女中举行反美罢课
    【新华社平壤十五日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜祖国战线七日为促进祖国和平统一而发表的告全国人民书，已获得南朝鲜爱国学生的热烈拥护。庆尚南道的大邱市女子中学学生为响应祖国战线的号召，反对美、李反动派分裂朝鲜与奴役朝鲜人民的阴谋，于十日开始举行罢课。数十名女学生因此被李承晚警察逮捕拷问，但罢课仍在坚持中。
