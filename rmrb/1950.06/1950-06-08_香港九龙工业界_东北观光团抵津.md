Title: Hong Kong and Kowloon Industrial Circles' Northeast Sightseeing Group Arrives in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th. The Hong Kong and Kowloon Industrial Circles' Northeast Sightseeing Group, led by Huang Changshui and comprising 41 people, arrived in Tianjin at 3 PM on the 7th from Hankou. The group will stay in Tianjin for several days to visit Tianjin's industrial production and construction.

### 香港九龙工业界  东北观光团抵津

1950-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　香港九龙工业界  东北观光团抵津
    【新华社天津七日电】港九工业界东北观光团黄长水等一行四十一人，七日下午三时由汉口抵津。该团将在天津逗留数日，参观津市工业生产建设。
