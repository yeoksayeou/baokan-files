Title: Central South Grain Outward Transfer Completed, Shipping Seven Hundred Million Jin to North China and Other Regions, Seventy Percent of Intra-Regional Allocation Completed
Author:
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 29th: The arduous task of transferring 1.48 billion jin of grain in the Central South region, of which the outward transfer has been overfulfilled, and the internal transfer task is also nearing completion. As of the 25th of this month, the rice and coarse grains transferred from the four provinces of Henan, Hunan, Jiangxi, and Hubei to the three friendly neighboring regions of North China, Northwest China, and East China have reached 702.16 million jin, exceeding the original outward transfer task of 678 million jin by more than 3.54 percent. The allocation of grain to major cities and grain-deficient areas in the region, originally tasked at 810 million jin, has also been 72 percent completed, and the remainder is being rushed for transport and will soon be fully completed.

### 中南粮食外调完成  调运华北等区达七亿斤  区内调剂完成十分之七

1950-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　中南粮食外调完成
    调运华北等区达七亿斤    
     区内调剂完成十分之七
    【新华社汉口二十九日电】中南区调运粮食十四亿八千万斤的艰巨任务，其中外调者已超额完成；内调任务亦已接近完成。截至本月二十五日止，由河南、湖南、江西、湖北四省调往华北、西北及华东三大友邻区的大米和杂粮已达七亿零二百一十六万斤，超过原定外调任务六亿七千八百万斤的百分之三点五四以上。调剂本区各大城市及各省缺粮地区的粮食，原定总任务为八亿一千万斤，亦已完成了百分之七十二，其余正在继续赶运，不久即可全部完成。
