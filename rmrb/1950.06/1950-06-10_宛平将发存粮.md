Title: Wanping to Distribute Stored Grain
Author: Song Lianjun, Shi Fuhui
Date: 1950-06-10
Page: 2

Local Briefs

    Wanping to Distribute Stored Grain
    Wanping County has decided to completely distribute 280,000 jin of stored grain by early June to support supply and marketing cooperatives in purchasing local products, as well as to support mass production and summer hoeing.
   (Song Lianjun, Shi Fuhui)

### 宛平将发存粮
宋连君　史甫辉
1950-06-10
第2版()
专栏：各地简讯

　　宛平将发存粮
    宛平县决定将存粮二十八万斤在六月初旬完全发放出去，扶植供销社收购山货及扶植群众生产和夏锄。
   （宋连君、史甫辉）
