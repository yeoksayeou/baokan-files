Title: Kyrgyz Mountain Named Mao Zedong Peak in Tribute to the Leader of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1950-06-18
Page: 1

　　Expressing Respect for the Leader of the Chinese People
    Kyrgyz Mountain Named Mao Zedong Peak
    [Xinhua News Agency, Frounze, 17th] According to a TASS report from Frounze on the 15th, a group of mountaineers from the "Bolshevik" Sports Club of Frounze, the capital of the Kyrgyz Republic of the Soviet Union, climbed the highest peak of the Saryk Mountains in the Kyrgyz Ala-Too range in early June. The peak reaches an altitude of 4,780 meters. In order to express their respect for the warriors of peace, freedom and democracy, they have named the peak they conquered after the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong. Two other peaks have been named after Togliatti and Dimitrov, respectively.

### 表示对中国人民领袖的崇敬  吉尔吉斯一座高山命名为毛泽东山峰

1950-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　表示对中国人民领袖的崇敬
    吉尔吉斯一座高山命名为毛泽东山峰
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社伏龙芝十五日讯：苏联吉尔吉斯共和国首都伏龙芝“布尔什维克”体育俱乐部的一群爬山家，六月初攀登吉尔吉斯阿拉塔乌山脉萨里克山的最高峰。该峰海拔达四千七百八十公尺。他们为了表示对和平、自由与民主的战士们的崇敬，已用中国人民领袖毛泽东的名字为他们所征服的山峰的名字。另外有两个山峰，已分别以托里亚蒂和季米特洛夫的名字命名。
