Title: Zhengzhou Railway Bureau Recovers Locomotive Buried at Bottom of River
Author: Liu Hongchen
Date: 1950-06-27
Page: 2

Zhengzhou Railway Bureau Recovers Locomotive Buried at Bottom of River

　　The No. 706 locomotive, which had been buried in the Yellow River滩 (tān, sandbar, shoal) of Zhongmu County, Henan Province for more than nine years, was finally salvaged after more than three months of active work by some workers of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau and farmers of the county. The bureau held a celebration meeting on June 14 to commend the successful salvage of the locomotive.
(Liu Hongchen)

### 郑州铁路局捞出埋沉河底的机车
刘洪晨
1950-06-27
第2版()
专栏：各地简讯

　　郑州铁路局捞出埋沉河底的机车
    埋沉在河南中牟县黄河滩里已有九年多的七○六号机车，在郑州铁路局部分工人和该县农民的积极工作下，经过三个多月的时间，终于打捞出来。该局特于六月十四日举行打捞机车庆功大会。        （刘洪晨）
