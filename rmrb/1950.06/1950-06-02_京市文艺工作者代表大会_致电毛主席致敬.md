Title: Beijing Literary and Art Workers' Representative Conference Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao to Pay Respects
Author:
Date: 1950-06-02
Page: 2

Beijing Municipal News Agency Report: The full text of the telegram from the Beijing Literary and Art Workers' Representative Conference to Chairman Mao is as follows: Respected Chairman Mao:

　　In order to strengthen unity and expand the popularization of literature and art in Beijing, we held the Beijing Literary and Art Workers' Representative Conference. At this conference, we extensively exchanged work experiences and determined the direction and tasks for our joint efforts in the future. We are willing to serve the production and construction of the capital under the guidance of your great thought. We extend our highest respects to you and wish you: eternal health!

### 京市文艺工作者代表大会  致电毛主席致敬

1950-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　京市文艺工作者代表大会
    致电毛主席致敬
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市文艺工作者代表大会向毛主席致敬电全文如下：敬爱的毛主席：
    为加强团结，展开北京市的文艺普及工作，我们举行了北京市文学艺术工作者代表大会。在这次大会上，我们广泛的交换了工作经验，确定了今后共同努力的方向和任务。我们愿意在你伟大思想的照耀下，为配合首都的生产建设而服务，我们向你致最敬礼，并祝你：永远健康！
