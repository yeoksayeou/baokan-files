Title: CCP Central South Military Region Party Committee, etc., Call on Party Members and Cadres to Study Chairman Mao's Report
Author:
Date: 1950-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 16th. After the publication of Chairman Mao's political report at the Third Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the CCP Central South Military Region Party Committee and Political Department and the CCP Wuhan Municipal Committee respectively issued notices calling on all officers and soldiers in the military region and all Party members and cadres in the city to conscientiously study Chairman Mao's report and ensure the completion of the eight major tasks proposed by Chairman Mao.

### 中共中南军区党委会等  号召党员和干部学习毛主席报告

1950-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中南军区党委会等
    号召党员和干部学习毛主席报告
    【新华社汉口十六日电】毛主席在中共中央七届三中全会的政治报告发表后，中共中南军区党委会暨政治部和中共武汉市委会，分别发出通知，号召全军及全市的党员和干部，认真学习毛主席的报告，保证完成毛主席提出的八大工作。
