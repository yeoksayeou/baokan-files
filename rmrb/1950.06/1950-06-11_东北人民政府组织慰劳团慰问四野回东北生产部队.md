Title: Northeast People's Government Organizes慰劳团 to Visit Fourth Field Army Troops Returning to Northeast for Production

Author: 
Date: 1950-06-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 6th: The Northeast People's Government has organized a 慰劳团 (comfort delegation) to visit the Fourth Field Army troops that have been transferred back to Northeast China to engage in large-scale agricultural production. The delegation is led by Vice Chairman Gao Chongmin and is carrying nearly 100,000 items for慰劳 (comfort), including many agricultural production books and a large number of慰问 (condolence) letters. The慰问 (condolence) letters, signed by Gao Gang, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and Vice Chairmen Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, and Gao Chongmin, express warm welcome and respect to the Fourth Field Army troops for gloriously completing the military tasks of liberating the Northeast, Beijing-Tianjin, and Central South China, and then returning to the Northeast to carry out production tasks. The letters also earnestly hope that they will complete their production tasks, combine production and learning well, improve their cultural and political levels, learn scientific military technology, and develop into a stronger, modern national defense force.

### 东北人民政府组织慰劳团慰问四野回东北生产部队

1950-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

    东北人民政府组织慰劳团慰问四野回东北生产部队
    【新华社沈阳六日电】东北人民政府组织慰劳团，前往某地慰问第四野战军调回东北进行农业大生产的部队。该慰劳团由高崇民副主席率领，携带有近十万件的慰劳品，包括许多农业生产书籍和大批的慰问信。在东北人民政府高岗主席，李富春、林枫、高崇民副主席签署的慰问信中对第四野战军部队光荣地完成了解放东北、京津和华中南的军事任务后，又回到东北执行生产任务，表示热烈的欢迎与敬意。信中并殷切地期望他们完成生产任务，并把生产和学习很好地结合起来，提高文化和政治水平，学会科学的军事技术，锻炼成更坚强的近代化的国防军。
