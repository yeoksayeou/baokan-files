Title: MacArthur Again Announces Release of Four Japanese War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: MacArthur continues to illegally "parole" Japanese war criminals. According to Associated Press and Kyodo News Agency Tokyo reports: MacArthur Headquarters recently announced that, in accordance with the war criminal "parole" method, four Japanese war criminals will be released from prison on the 15th. This is MacArthur's fifth illegal "parole" of Japanese war criminals. Including these four war criminals, the total number of war criminals illegally released by MacArthur through so-called "parole" has reached twenty-seven.

### 麦克阿瑟又宣布  释放日战犯四名

1950-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟又宣布
    释放日战犯四名
    【新华社七日讯】麦克阿瑟继续非法“假释”日本战犯。据美联社、共同社东京消息：麦克阿瑟总部最近宣布：根据战犯“假释”办法，日本战犯四名将于十五日被释出狱。这是麦克阿瑟第五次非法“假释”日本战犯。连这次四名战犯计算在内，经麦克阿瑟以所谓“假释”的方式非法释放的战犯，共达二十七名。
