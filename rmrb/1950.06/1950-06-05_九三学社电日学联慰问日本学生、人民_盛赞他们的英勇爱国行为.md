Title: The Jiu San Society Sends a Telegram to the Japan Student Federation to Express Sympathy to Japanese Students and People, Praising Their Heroic Patriotic Actions
Author:
Date: 1950-06-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Jiu San Society sent a telegram on the 3rd to the All-Japan Student Autonomy Association to express solidarity with the Japanese students and people from all walks of life who were persecuted for participating in the Tokyo "May 30th People's Assembly." The telegram praised the heroic struggle waged by the Japanese people under the leadership of the Japan Democratic National United Front in recent months and strongly condemned the atrocities of the American occupiers. The telegram said: We express our infinite admiration and sympathy for your heroic and patriotic actions today.

### 九三学社电日学联慰问日本学生、人民  盛赞他们的英勇爱国行为

1950-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　九三学社电日学联慰问日本学生、人民
    盛赞他们的英勇爱国行为
    【新华社三日讯】九三学社三日致电全日本学生自治会联合会，声援参加东京“五·三○人民大会”被迫害的日本学生和日本各界人民。该电盛赞日本人民近数月来在日本民主民族统一战线领导下所进行的英勇斗争，并猛烈抨击美国占领者的暴行。该电说：对于你们今天这样英勇爱国的行为，我们表示无限的敬佩和同情。
