Title: Supreme Soviet Session Concludes; Passes Declaration Supporting World Peace and Great Suggestions; Soviet Government Will Adhere to a Policy of Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st - TASS dispatch: After electing the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and forming the government, the Soviet Supreme Soviet's Council of the Union and Council of Nationalities, in joint session on the 19th, completed its work.

　　The session concluded by passing a declaration supporting the World Peace Council's suggestions, announcing that the Soviet Supreme Soviet is willing to cooperate with the legislative bodies of other countries to jointly formulate and implement the measures necessary to realize the World Peace Council's suggestions. The Soviet Supreme Soviet expressed its firm belief that the Soviet government, which has consistently safeguarded peace and cooperation with all countries, will unswervingly implement a policy of maintaining peace, continue to take necessary measures through the United Nations, and follow all other avenues to ensure peace and international security.

　　At this point, Representative Yasnov, serving as chairman, announced that the agenda of the first session of the Supreme Soviet had been completed and declared the session closed.

### 苏最高苏维埃会议闭会  通过拥护和大建议声明  苏政府将坚持实行和平政策

1950-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏最高苏维埃会议闭会
    通过拥护和大建议声明
    苏政府将坚持实行和平政策
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院与民族院在十九日举行联席会议，选出最高苏维埃主席团并组成政府之后，已完成其工作。　  会议最后通过拥护世界和大常委会建议的声明，宣布苏联最高苏维埃愿与其他国家的立法机关合作，共同拟订与实施为实现世界和大常委会的建议所必需的措施。苏联最高苏维埃表示深信：一贯维护与各国和平与合作的苏联政府，将坚持不渝地实行维持和平的政策，继续经由联合国采取必要的措施，并将为保证和平与国际安全而遵行一切其他的途径。
    至此，担任主席的代表雅斯诺夫宣布最高苏维埃第一次会议议程已进行完毕，并宣布闭会。
