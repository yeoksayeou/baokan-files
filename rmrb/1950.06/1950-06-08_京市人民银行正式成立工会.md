Title: Beijing Municipal People's Bank Officially Establishes Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 3
Brief News

    The trade union of the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China was officially established on May 28th. The trade union of the bank began preparations in February this year. During the preparation period, a total of 1,785 members were developed, accounting for 75 percent of the bank's total number of employees. In mid-May, each branch elected member representatives, and a member representative assembly was held on May 27th and closed on May 28th. The assembly passed six resolutions, including fixed quota, fixed quality, fixed quantity, promoting democracy, ensuring the completion of the administrative tasks entrusted by the state in June, July and August, and trade union organizational rules; it elected the members of the first trade union committee and the supervisory committee, and officially established the trade union. (Communications Group of the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China)

### 京市人民银行正式成立工会

1950-06-08
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    京市人民银行正式成立工会
    中国人民银行北京分行工会已于五月二十八日正式成立。该行工会自今年二月开始筹备，筹备期间，共发展会员一千七百八十五人，占全行人数百分之七十五。五月中旬各支会分别选出会员代表，于五月二十七日举行了会员代表大会，二十八日闭幕。大会通过了六、七、八月份定额、定质、定量、发扬民主、保证完成国家委托的行政任务及工会组织章则等六项决议；选出首届工会委员会委员与监察委员会委员，正式成立了工会。（中国人民银行北京分行通讯组）
