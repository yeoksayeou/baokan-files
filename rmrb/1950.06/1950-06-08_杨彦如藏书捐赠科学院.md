Title: Yang Yanru Donates His Book Collection to the Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1950-06-08
Page: 3

　　Yang Yanru Donates His Book Collection to the Academy of Sciences
    [This newspaper dispatch] Mr. Yang Yanru, an elderly gentleman from Beijing, recently donated 29 kinds of books, totaling 1,181 volumes, from his treasured collection to the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Among them are the Song Dynasty Qisha Buddhist Canon in photographic reprint and many early Ming and Qing Dynasty printed editions, which are currently being sorted and cataloged by the Academy's Department of Historical Research.

### 杨彦如藏书捐赠科学院

1950-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　杨彦如藏书捐赠科学院
    【本报讯】北京市杨彦如老先生，日前把他所珍藏的书籍二十九种共一千一百八十一册送给中国科学院。其中有影印宋碛砂藏经和其他明清初印刊本很多，现正由该院史学研究部门整理编目。
