Title: Preparatory Committee of Guangxi Federation of Trade Unions Established
Author:
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 2

Local Briefs

    Preparatory Committee of Guangxi Federation of Trade Unions Established
    The first Guangxi Provincial Workers' Congress was held from May 18th to 26th. According to the instructions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the meeting focused on discussing the issues of overcoming difficulties and stepping up production; and elected 23 members including Li Chuli and Liu Yun as members of the preparatory committee of the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions. The preparatory committee of the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions was immediately announced. Of the more than 100,000 workers in the province, more than 30,000 have now joined the trade union.

### 广西总工会筹委会成立

1950-06-04
第2版()
专栏：各地简讯

    广西总工会筹委会成立
    广西省首届工人代表大会已于五月十八日至二十六日召开。会议根据全国总工会的指示，集中讨论了克服困难和加紧生产的问题；并选出李楚离、刘云等二十三人为省总工会筹备委员会委员。省总工会筹委会当即宣告成立。全省十万余工人中，现已有三万余人加入工会。
