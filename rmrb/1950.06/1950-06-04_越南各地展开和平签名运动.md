Title: Peace Signature Campaign Unfolds Across Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1950-06-04
Page: 4

　　Vietnam-wide Peace Signature Campaign Unfolds
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 1st dispatch] According to the Vietnam News Agency: Vietnamese democratic organizations have jointly launched a nationwide peace signature campaign in response to the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's peace declaration, demanding the prohibition of atomic bombs and condemning the warmongers who attempt to use them. The democratic organizations have instructed their branches to expose the imperialists' war schemes and point out that the warmongers' schemes will surely fail in the face of the strong will of 800 million men and women defending peace.

### 越南各地展开和平签名运动

1950-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南各地展开和平签名运动
    【新华社三日讯】据越南通讯社北越一日讯：越南各民主团体已联合发动全国性的和平签名运动，以响应世界和大常委会的和平宣言，要求禁用原子弹，并谴责那些企图使用原子弹的战争贩子们。各民主团体已指示其分支机构，揭露帝国主义的战争计谋，并指出在八万万男女保卫和平的坚强意志面前，战争贩子们的计谋必将破产。
