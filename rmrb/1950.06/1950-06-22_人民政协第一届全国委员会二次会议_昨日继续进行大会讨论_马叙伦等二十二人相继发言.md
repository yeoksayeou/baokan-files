Title: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC Continued its Plenary Discussion Yesterday: Ma Xulun and 22 Others Spoke in Succession

Author: 
Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st: The Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) continued its plenary discussion today. Those who spoke at the meeting today included Ma Xulun, Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Burhan (Uyghur), Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government; Xiong Kewu, a veteran member of the Tongmenghui; Tang Shengzhi, a former KMT general and Vice Chairman of the Hunan Provincial People's Government; Liu Hongsheng, representative of the Shanghai Local Consultative Committee; Zhao Guilan, a model female worker who protected factories; Li Siguang, member of the National Committee and Vice President of the Academy of Sciences; Liu Wenhui, a former KMT general; Li Genyuan, a respected elder; Xirao Jiacuo (Tibetan), Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government; Liu Changsheng, member of the National Committee; Shu Sheyu, member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council and writer; Tao Zhiyue, a former KMT general and member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee; Pei Changhui, a former KMT general; Qian Duansheng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; Lu Han, a former KMT general and Director of the Yunnan Military and Political Committee; Zhou Shutao, member of the National Committee and Vice Mayor of Tianjin; Deng Xihou, a former KMT general; Hu Ming, member of the National Committee; Li Zhangda, member of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government; Zhang Fenghui, a respected elder and Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government; and Liang Juwu (Miao), a figure from the judicial circles.

### 人民政协第一届全国委员会二次会议  昨日继续进行大会讨论  马叙伦等二十二人相继发言

1950-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协第一届全国委员会二次会议
    昨日继续进行大会讨论
    马叙伦等二十二人相继发言
    【新华社二十一日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第二次会议今日继续进行大会讨论。今日在大会上发言的，有中国民主促进会主席马叙伦，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉（维族），老同盟会员熊克武，起义将领、湖南省人民政府副主席唐生智，上海地方协商委员会代表刘鸿生，保护工厂的模范女工赵桂兰，全国委员会委员、科学院副院长李四光，起义将领刘文辉，绅耆李根源，青海省人民政府副主席喜饶嘉措（藏），全国委员会委员刘长胜，政务院文化教育委员会委员、作家舒舍予，起义将领、西北军政委员会委员陶峙岳，起义将领裴昌会，北京市人民代表会议协商委员会副主席钱端升，起义将领、云南军政委员会主任卢汉，全国委员会委员、天津市副市长周叔韬，起义将领邓锡侯，全国委员会委员胡明，中央人民政府委员、广东省人民政府副主席李章达，绅耆、陕西省人民政府副主席张凤翙，司法界人士梁聚五（苗族）。
