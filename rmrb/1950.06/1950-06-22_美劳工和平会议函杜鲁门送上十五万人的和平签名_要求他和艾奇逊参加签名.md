Title: US Labor Peace Conference Sends Truman Peace Signatures of 150,000 People; Demands He and Acheson Sign

Author:

Date: 1950-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th Dispatch from TASS: The National Labor Peace Conference of the United States announced that it has sent a letter to Truman, delivering a peace declaration calling for peace and the banning of atomic weapons, signed by more than 150,000 Americans, and requesting Truman and Acheson to sign the declaration.

The letter declares that these signatures are merely an expression of the very widespread and continuously rising sentiment of the American people yearning for peace. These more than 150,000 signatures are part of a campaign to collect at least five million American signatures for the World Peace Appeal.

### 美劳工和平会议函杜鲁门送上十五万人的和平签名  要求他和艾奇逊参加签名

1950-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　美劳工和平会议函杜鲁门送上十五万人的和平签名
    要求他和艾奇逊参加签名
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十日讯：美国全国劳工和平会议宣布：该会议已致函杜鲁门，送上有十五万以上美国人签名的要求和平及禁止原子武器的和平宣言，并要求杜鲁门和艾奇逊在宣言上签名。
    该函宣称：这些签名只不过是非常普遍的、继续高涨的美国人民热望和平的情绪的一种表示。这十五万人以上的签名，是为世界和大常委会宣言征集至少五百万美国人签名运动中的一部分。
