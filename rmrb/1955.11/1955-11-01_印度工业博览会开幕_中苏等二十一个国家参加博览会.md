Title: Indian Industrial Exposition Opens, Twenty-One Countries Including China and the Soviet Union Participate
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 30th. The Indian Industrial Exposition was inaugurated by Indian Prime Minister Nehru at 4:00 PM on October 29th. Approximately 10,000 people attended the opening ceremony, including Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, senior Indian government officials, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in India, prominent figures from Delhi society, and citizens from all walks of life.

In addition to India itself, the following twenty-one countries are participating in the exposition; Austria, Belgium, Burma, China, Czechoslovakia, France, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Iran, Iraq, Italy, Japan, the Netherlands, Pakistan, Poland, Romania, the United Kingdom, the Soviet Union, the United States, West Germany, and Yugoslavia.

Prime Minister Nehru, on behalf of the Indian government, welcomed the representatives of the participating countries. He also said that the exposition is a modest effort by India to strive for coexistence and cooperation. He expressed his belief that the exposition would promote a deeper understanding between the peoples of all countries.

After the opening ceremony, the huge crowd dispersed to visit the pavilions of various countries. Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and Prime Minister Nehru visited the Chinese pavilion on the same day. They were accompanied by Indian Minister of Commerce and Industry Krishnamachari and other Indian government ministers. Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, and Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese delegation to the Indian Industrial Exposition, accompanied them on the tour. Prime Minister Nehru visited every part of the Chinese pavilion, and he was particularly interested in the models of water conservancy projects such as the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project, the Yellow River Diversion to Jiwei Project, and the Huai River Harnessing Project.

Many members of the diplomatic corps stationed in India also visited the Chinese pavilion that day.

Also visiting the Chinese pavilion that day were Prime Minister Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and the chairman of the Indian Industrial Exposition, B.M. Birla, and about 3,000 other people.

The Chinese pavilion covers an area of more than 5,000 square meters and is one of the largest exhibition halls in the exposition. There are more than 4,000 exhibits, including new-type machine tools, heavy machinery, agricultural products, mineral products, light industrial products, textiles, and exquisite handicrafts.

The exposition is scheduled to be open until December 15th.

### 印度工业博览会开幕  中苏等二十一个国家参加博览会

1955-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工业博览会开幕
    中苏等二十一个国家参加博览会
    新华社新德里三十日电　印度工业博览会已经在十月二十九日下午四时由印度尼赫鲁总理揭幕。参加揭幕典礼的有印度副总统拉德哈克里希南、印度政府高级官员、各国驻印度使节和外交官员、德里社会知名人士和各界市民共约一万人。
    参加这个博览会的，除了印度本国以外，有奥地利、比利时、缅甸、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、法国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、伊朗、伊拉克、意大利、日本、荷兰、巴基斯坦、波兰、罗马尼亚、英国、苏联、美国、西德和南斯拉夫等二十一个国家。
    尼赫鲁总理代表印度政府对各参加国的代表们表示欢迎，他还说，这个博览会是印度在争取共处与合作方面的一个微薄的努力。他表示相信这个博览会能够促进各国人民之间的更深的了解。
    在揭幕典礼结束后，巨大的人群分散到各国的展览馆去参观。印度副总统拉德哈克里希南和总理尼赫鲁当天参观了中国馆。同他们一起参观的有印度工商部长克里希纳马查里和印度政府其他部长。中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤和中国参加印度工业博览会展览团团长马一民陪同他们参观。尼赫鲁总理参观了中国馆的每一个部分，他对于荆江分洪、引黄济卫和治淮等水利工程的模型特别感到兴趣。
    驻印度的外交使团的许多人员也在当天参观了中国馆。
    当天到中国馆参观的还有尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人和印度工业博览会的主席布·麦·比拉等共约三千人。
    中国馆面积达五千多平方公尺，是博览会中最大的展览馆之一。展览品共有四千多种，包括新型工作母机、重型机械、农产品、矿产品、轻工业产品、纺织品以及精美的手工艺品等。
    博览会预定开放到十二月十五日为止。
