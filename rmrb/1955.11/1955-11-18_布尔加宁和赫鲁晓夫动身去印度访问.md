Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Leave for Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 17th: According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, left Moscow by plane on November 17th for Delhi at the invitation of the Government of the Republic of India.

Accompanying Bulganin and Khrushchev were: Minister of Culture of the USSR, Mikhailov; General Serov; First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Gromyko; Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the USSR, Rasulov; Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Kumykin; and Deputy Minister of Culture of the Uzbek Republic, Rasim Basaev.

Those seeing them off at the airport included Kirichenko, Mikoyan, Saburov, Aristov, Belyaev, Pospelov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Kosygin, Kucherenko, Lobanov, Malyshev, Tevosyan, Khrunichev, ministers of various ministries of the USSR, Marshals of the Soviet Union, senior generals of the Soviet Army, and journalists.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union also came to the airport to see Bulganin and Khrushchev off.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫动身去印度访问

1955-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫动身去印度访问
    新华社十七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫，应印度共和国政府的邀请在十一月十七日乘飞机离莫斯科前往德里。
    随布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫前往的有：苏联文化部部长米哈伊洛夫，大将谢罗夫，苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯，苏联农业部副部长拉苏洛夫，对外贸易部副部长库米金，乌兹别克共和国文化部副部长拉锡姆巴萨耶夫。
    到机场送行的有基里钦科、米高扬、萨布罗夫、阿里斯托夫、别利雅耶夫、波斯伯洛夫，苏联部长会议副主席柯西金、库切连科、洛巴诺夫、马利歇夫、捷沃西安、赫鲁尼切夫，苏联各部部长，苏联元帅，苏联军队的高级将领和新闻记者。
    还有各国驻苏联的外交使节也到机场为布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫送行。
