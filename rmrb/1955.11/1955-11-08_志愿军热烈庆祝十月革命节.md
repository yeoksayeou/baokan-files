Title: Volunteer Army Warmly Celebrates the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. The leadership organs and many units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army warmly celebrated the 38th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

　　On the evening of the 6th, the Volunteer Army leadership organs held a celebration meeting. A responsible person of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delivered a report at the meeting. After describing the great significance of the victory of the October Socialist Revolution, he said that as we celebrate the 38th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, we sincerely thank the Communist Party, government, and people of the Soviet Union for their generous and selfless assistance in all aspects of our country's revolution. We must strive to develop the fraternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, continue to work hard to learn from the Soviet Union's experience in building the army and advanced military science and technology, be constantly vigilant against the enemy's aggressive conspiracies, and work together with the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries, as well as peace-loving people all over the world, for the peaceful resolution of the Korean issue and for lasting peace in the Far East and the world.

　　The leadership organs of the Volunteer Army units stationed on the west coast of Korea and the leadership organs of a certain coastal defense unit held celebration meetings on the 7th. The clubs of many companies also introduced the Soviet Union's economic and cultural construction achievements through blackboards, wall newspapers, and oral broadcasts. The directly subordinate units of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed in the central Korean front also held a picture exhibition introducing the October Socialist Revolution and the construction achievements of the Soviet Union.

### 志愿军热烈庆祝十月革命节

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军热烈庆祝十月革命节
    新华社平壤七日电　中国人民志愿军领导机关和许多部队热烈庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年。
    六日晚，志愿军领导机关举行了庆祝大会。中国人民志愿军某部负责人在会上作了报告。他在叙述了十月社会主义革命胜利的伟大意义后说，当我们庆祝十月社会主义革命三十八周年的时候，我们衷心地感谢苏联共产党、政府和人民在我国革命的各个时期给予我们的慷慨无私的各方面的援助。我们要努力发展中、苏两国人民间的兄弟的、牢不可破的友谊，要继续努力学习苏联的建军经验和先进的军事科学技术，时刻警惕敌人的侵略阴谋，同苏联等兄弟国家以及世界各国爱好和平的人民一道，为了朝鲜问题的和平解决，为了远东及世界的持久和平而努力奋斗。
    驻在朝鲜西海岸的志愿军部队的领导机关和某海防部队的领导机关都在七日举行了庆祝会。许多连队的俱乐部还通过黑板报、墙报和口头广播等形式介绍了苏联的经济及文化建设成就。驻在朝鲜中线的志愿军某部直属部队还举办了介绍十月社会主义革命和苏联建设成就的图片展览会。
