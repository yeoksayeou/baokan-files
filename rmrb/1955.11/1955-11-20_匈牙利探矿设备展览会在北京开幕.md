Title: Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 1
Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Opens in Beijing

　　(Our report) The Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition opened on the afternoon of the 18th in Beijing. Those attending the opening ceremony included people from Chinese scientific circles, geological exploration workers, and representatives from relevant departments.

　　At the opening ceremony, after the representative of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Tsillag, delivered the opening speech, Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, delivered a congratulatory speech. He sincerely congratulated the successful exhibition on behalf of the Chinese people, and expressed a warm welcome and gratitude to the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation, headed by Vilmos Bessi, for coming to China. He said that through this exhibition, we will learn from Hungary's advanced scientific and technological experience. Especially during the expansion of geological exploration work in our country's first five-year plan, holding such an exhibition has even greater significance.

　　Vilmos Bessi, head of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation, introduced the purpose of this exhibition and Hungary's exploration work at the meeting. He said, "I believe that the technical exchanges between Chinese and Hungarian experts will help accelerate the development of exploration work in both our countries."

　　After He Changgong, Vice Minister of Geology, cut the ribbon, the tour began. People showed great interest in each exhibit and praised the great achievements made in Hungary's geological exploration work.

### 匈牙利探矿设备展览会在北京开幕

1955-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利探矿设备展览会在北京开幕
    本报讯　匈牙利探矿设备展览会在十八日下午开幕。参加开幕式的有我国科学界人士、地质勘探工作者和各有关部门的代表等。
    在开幕典礼上，匈牙利驻华大使馆代表齐拉格致开幕词后，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸致祝词，他代表中国人民衷心地祝贺展览会的顺利展出，并对以维尔摩什·贝西为首的匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团来中国表示热烈欢迎和谢意。他说，通过这次展出，我们将学习到匈牙利先进的科学技术经验。特别是在我国第一个五年计划中扩大地质勘探工作的时候，举办这种展览会就具有更重大的意义。
    匈牙利探矿设备展览会代表团团长维尔摩什·贝西在会上介绍了这次展出的目的以及匈牙利的勘探工作。他说，我相信中匈两国专家之间进行的技术交流活动将有助于加速我们两国勘探工作的发展。
    在地质部副部长何长工剪彩后，开始参观。人们对每件陈列品表示出很大的兴趣，并赞扬匈牙利地质勘探工作所取得的巨大成就。
