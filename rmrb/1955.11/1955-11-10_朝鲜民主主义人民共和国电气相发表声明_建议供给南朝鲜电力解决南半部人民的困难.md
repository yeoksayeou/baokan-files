Title: Statement by the Minister of Electricity of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea: Suggesting the Supply of Electricity to South Korea to Solve the Difficulties of the People in the Southern Half
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th. According to the Korean Central News Agency: Kim Doo-sam, Minister of Electricity of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, issued a statement on the 8th regarding the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's readiness to supply electricity to South Korea.

The statement said, "Currently, due to insufficient electricity in South Korea, the lives of the South Korean people, especially the production of various factories and enterprises in South Korea, have encountered enormous difficulties." The statement continued: The northern half of the Republic now has abundant electricity, which can not only meet the needs of all the Korean people, but also has a surplus. The entire people of the northern half of the Republic believe that the serious situation of lack of electricity faced by the people of the southern half cannot be ignored, and that it is their national duty to share the electricity of the northern half with their compatriots in the southern half.

The statement then pointed out: "If the South Korean authorities show sincerity, it is possible to supply South Korea with electricity from the northern half now, just as it has been done in the past."

The statement then put forward a specific proposal: "In order to quickly realize the above proposal, the Ministry of Electricity of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea proposes that representatives from relevant government agencies or power companies in South Korea, as well as other industrial or private organizations, hold talks in Kaesong on November 25, 1955."

### 朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电气相发表声明  建议供给南朝鲜电力解决南半部人民的困难

1955-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电气相发表声明
    建议供给南朝鲜电力解决南半部人民的困难
    据新华社平壤九日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电气相金斗三在八日就朝鲜民主主义人民共和国准备供给南朝鲜以电力一事发表声明。
    声明说：“目前，南朝鲜由于电力不足，致使南朝鲜人民的生活，特别是南朝鲜各工厂、企业的生产遭到巨大困难。”声明接着说：共和国北半部现在有着丰富的电力，它不仅能满足全体朝鲜人民的需要，而且还有剩余。共和国北半部全体人民对于南半部人民所面临的缺乏电力的严重情况，认为不能置之不顾，并且认为和南半部同胞分用北半部的电力是自己的民族义务。
    声明接着指出：“如果南朝鲜当局表示诚意，北半部供给南朝鲜以电力在现在也是可能的，正像过去所做的一样。”
    声明接着提出具体建议说：“为了迅速实现上述建议，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电气省建议在一九五五年十一月二十五日在开城和南朝鲜有关政府机关或电力公司以及其他实业团体或民间代表举行会谈。”
