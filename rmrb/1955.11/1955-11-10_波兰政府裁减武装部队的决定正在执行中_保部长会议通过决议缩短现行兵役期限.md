Title: The Polish Government's Decision to Reduce Armed Forces is Being Implemented; Bulgarian Council of Ministers Passes Resolution to Shorten Current Military Service Term
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 9th November: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the Polish People's Republic government's decision to reduce the armed forces by 47,000 people before December 20th this year is being implemented. Currently, thousands of officers and soldiers of the Polish armed forces are being demobilized from the army and have begun working in factories, mines, state-owned farms and other work positions.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 9th November: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reports: The Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria has passed a resolution shortening the current term of military service.

The resolution stipulates: The current term of service for compulsory military service in Bulgaria is two years. Sergeants and soldiers in the army, sergeants and soldiers in the air force, sergeants and soldiers in the naval coastal defense artillery and engineering corps, and sergeants and soldiers in the Ministry of Internal Affairs will have their military service shortened by two years; sergeants and soldiers in the National Air Defense Forces, chief petty officers and soldiers in the navy, and air force technicians stipulated by the Minister of National Defense will have their military service shortened by three years.

### 波兰政府裁减武装部队的决定正在执行中  保部长会议通过决议缩短现行兵役期限

1955-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰政府裁减武装部队的决定正在执行中
    保部长会议通过决议缩短现行兵役期限
    新华社华沙九日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰人民共和国政府关于在今年十二月二十日以前裁减武装部队四万七千人的决定正在执行中。目前，数以千计的波兰武装部队的军官和士兵从部队上复员，并且开始在工厂、矿山、国营农场等工作岗位上工作。
    新华社九日讯　保加利亚通讯社索非亚九日讯：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议通过了一项缩短现行兵役期限的决议。
    决议规定：保加利亚现行义务兵役制的服役期限为两年。陆军士官和战士、空军士官和战士、海军海防炮队和工兵部队士官和战士、内政部的士官和战士一律缩短兵役两年；国家空防部队士官和战士、海军水手长和战士以及国防部长命令所规定的空军技术人员一律缩短兵役三年。
