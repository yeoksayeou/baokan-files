Title: Head of the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation Delivers Radio Address
Author:
Date: 1955-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 20th: The Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation left Kunming on the 20th to return to their country.

Prior to departure, U Than Swe, the head of the delegation, delivered a radio address on the Yunnan People's Broadcasting Station on the 20th.

U Than Swe said: The Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation came to China with the sincere friendship and peaceful wishes of the Burmese people towards the Chinese people. We felt extremely excited to receive such a warm welcome and gracious hospitality in China.

He said, "Our visit to China will certainly further promote the friendship between China and Burma, which will not only contribute to peace in Asia, but also to world peace."

U Than Swe also said: "We brought the deep friendship of the Burmese people to the Chinese people and the art of the Burmese people as a gift to the Chinese people. We are very happy to see that the Chinese people have very treasuredly accepted these gifts of friendship."

U Than Swe said: The Burmese Cultural Delegation's "friendly visit and performance in China was so successful because it received the care and attention of the dear Chinese people, who are like brothers and sisters."

U Than Swe said: "We are about to return to Burma, and we bid farewell to you with extremely heavy hearts. May we work together even harder for cultural exchange and friendship between China and Burma, and for world peace."

### 缅甸联邦文化代表团团长发表广播演说

1955-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　缅甸联邦文化代表团团长发表广播演说
    据新华社昆明二十日电　缅甸联邦文化代表团在二十日离昆明回国。
    行前，代表团团长吴丹盛二十日在云南人民广播电台发表广播演说。
    吴丹盛说：缅甸联邦文化代表团带着缅甸人民对中国人民的真挚友情和和平的愿望来到中国。在中国，受到了热烈欢迎和盛情招待，感到极大的兴奋。
    他说：“我们到中国进行访问，一定更能促进中缅两国的友谊，这种友谊不仅有助于亚洲和平，而且也会有助于世界和平。”
    吴丹盛又说：“我们带来了缅甸人民对中国人民的深厚友情和缅甸人民的艺术，作为对中国人民的献礼。我们很高兴地看到了中国人民非常珍贵地接受了这些友谊的礼品。”
    吴丹盛说：缅甸文化代表团“在中国进行的友好访问演出所以能够获得成功，那是因为得到了同胞般的亲爱的中国人民的关怀和照顾。”
    吴丹盛说：“我们就要回缅甸了，我们以极其难过的心情和你们告别，但愿我们共同为中缅两国的文化交流和友谊，为世界的和平而更加努力。”
