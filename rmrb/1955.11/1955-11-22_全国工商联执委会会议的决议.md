Title: Resolution of the Executive Committee Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
Author:
Date: 1955-11-22
Page: 1

　　The First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held its Second Meeting from November 1 to November 21, 1955. The meeting warmly discussed the opening speech of Chairman Chen Shutong and unanimously agreed with it. The organizations at all levels of the Federation should, under the leadership of the local Party and government, actively convey it to the broad industrial and commercial circles, and organize them to conduct in-depth study and discussion, and strive to promote the further acceptance of socialist transformation by private industrialists and businessmen.
   The meeting reviewed and approved the Federation's two-year work report. This report summarizes the work experience of the past two years and points out the main tasks for the future. The Federation and organizations at all levels should draw on past experience, and constantly summarize new experiences and lessons, introduce exchanges, correct shortcomings in a timely manner, and continuously improve our work.
   The meeting reviewed and approved the Federation's two-year financial report, the 1954 final accounts, the 1955 and 1956 budget estimates, and the methods for members to pay membership fees. The meeting believes that the Federation's financial work in the past two years has basically implemented the principle of thrift and reduced the burden on members accordingly. In the future, the Federation and organizations at all levels should continue to work hard to implement the principle of practicing economy and opposing waste.

### 全国工商联执委会会议的决议

1955-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国工商联执委会会议的决议
　　中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会于一九五五年十一月一日至十一月二十一日举行了第二次会议。会议热烈地讨论了陈叔通主任委员的开幕词，一致表示同意。本会各级组织应该在当地党政领导下，积极向广大工商界传达，并组织工商界进行深入的学习和讨论，为推动私营工商业者进一步接受社会主义改造而努力。
   会议对于本会两年工作报告进行了审查，并予以同意。这一报告总结两年来的工作经验，指出了今后的主要工作。本会和各级组织应该吸取过去的经验，并随时总结新的经验和教训，介绍交流，及时改正缺点，不断地改进我们的工作。
   会议对于本会两年财务报告、一九五四年度决算、一九五五年度和一九五六年度经费预算及会员交纳会费办法，进行了审查，并予以同意。会议认为本会两年来财务工作基本上贯彻了节约的原则，并相应地减轻了会员的负担。今后，本会和各级组织要继续努力贯彻厉行节约、反对浪费的原则。
