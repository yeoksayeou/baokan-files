Title: Preparatory Committee for International Women Workers' Conference Established in China
Author:
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. In order to welcome the International Women Workers' Conference to be held in June 1956, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the National Committees of various industrial trade unions in China have jointly formed the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the International Women Workers' Conference.

　　At its first meeting held in Beijing in early November, the Chinese Preparatory Committee decided to immediately begin widely publicizing the great significance of the International Women Workers' Conference among the Chinese women workers, according to the spirit of the appeal to women workers worldwide by the Preparatory Committee of the International Women Workers' Conference. Through trade union newspapers at all levels, various gatherings, and other forms of publicity, it will systematically introduce the labor, life, and struggles of women workers in various countries around the world to Chinese women workers to strengthen internationalist education.

　　The first meeting of the Chinese Preparatory Committee also decided to elect representatives to attend the International Women Workers' Conference in May 1956.

### 国际女工会议中国筹备会成立

1955-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　  国际女工会议中国筹备会成立
    新华社二十二日讯　为了迎接将在一九五六年六月召开的国际女工会议，中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中国各产业工会全国委员会已经联合组成了国际女工会议中国筹备会。
    中国筹备会十一月初在北京举行的第一次会议上，决定立即开始根据国际女工会议筹备委员会告全世界女工书的精神，在中国女工群众中广泛宣传国际女工会议的重大意义；通过各级工会报纸、各种集会以及其他各种宣传形式，有系统地向中国女工群众介绍世界各国女工的劳动、生活和斗争情况，以加强国际主义教育。
    中国筹备会第一次会议还决定在一九五六年五月选出出席国际女工会议的代表。
