Title: Chinese Art Troupe Holds First Performance in Belgrade; Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia Hosts Reception for Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 17th: Belgrade Dispatch: The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to Yugoslavia for a performance tour (they arrived in Belgrade from London on November 14th), gave its first performance at the Belgrade National Theater on the evening of November 16th.

	President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia attended the performance. Also in attendance were President of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, Pijade; Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, Ranković, Vukmanović-Tempo, and Ćolaković; Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Popović; Chief of the General Staff Vukčević; members of the Federal Executive Council; and many other senior Yugoslav officials and figures from the cultural sphere.

	Many foreign diplomatic envoys to Yugoslavia also attended the performance.

	Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 16th: Belgrade Dispatch: Vice President Ćolaković of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia hosted a reception at the Federal Executive Council on the evening of November 15th to welcome the Chinese Art Troupe, which was invited to Yugoslavia for a performance tour.

	Approximately six hundred people attended the reception, including President of the Yugoslav National Assembly, Pijade; Vice President Simić; Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, Ranković and Vukmanović-Tempo; Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Popović; Chief of the General Staff General Vukčević; people from all walks of life in Yugoslavia; and foreign envoys to Yugoslavia.

	Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 17th: Belgrade Dispatch: Vice President Ćolaković of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia received Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and deputy head Zhao Feng, as well as actor representatives Zhang Yunxi and Du Jinfang, on the morning of November 15th.

	Wu Xiuquan, Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, was present at the meeting.

	On the same day, Zhang Zhixiang and others, accompanied by Ambassador Wu Xiuquan, visited Ristić, President of the Yugoslav Association for International Cultural Cooperation.

### 我国艺术团在贝尔格莱德举行首次演出　南斯拉夫联邦执委会副主席举行招待会招待我国艺术团

1955-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国艺术团在贝尔格莱德举行首次演出
　　南斯拉夫联邦执委会副主席举行招待会招待我国艺术团
    新华社布拉格十七日电　贝尔格莱德消息：应邀到南斯拉夫作访问演出的中国艺术团（按他们是十一月十四日自伦敦到达贝尔格莱德的）十一月十六日晚在贝尔格莱德国家剧院举行首次演出。
    南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托出席观看了这次演出。出席观看演出的还有南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰，联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇、伏克曼诺维奇—泰波和乔拉柯维奇，外交秘书长波波维奇，参谋总长伏奇科维奇，联邦执行委员会委员以及许多其他的南斯拉夫高级官员和文化界人士。
    许多国家驻南斯拉夫的外交使节也出席观看了演出。
    新华社布拉格十六日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席乔拉柯维奇十一月十五日晚间在联邦执行委员会举行招待会，招待应邀到南斯拉夫作访问演出的中国艺术团。
    出席招待会的共约六百人，其中有南斯拉夫国民议会主席皮雅杰、副主席西米奇、联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇和伏克曼诺维奇—泰波、外交秘书长波波维奇、参谋总长伏奇科维奇将军等南斯拉夫各界人士和各国驻南斯拉夫的使节。
    新华社布拉格十七日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席乔拉柯维奇在十一月十五日上午接见了中国艺术团团长、中华人民共和国文化部副部长张致祥和副团长赵沨，以及演员代表张云溪和杜近芳。
    接见时在座的有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。
    同日，张致祥等在伍修权大使的陪同下，拜访了南斯拉夫对外文化协会主席里斯蒂奇。
