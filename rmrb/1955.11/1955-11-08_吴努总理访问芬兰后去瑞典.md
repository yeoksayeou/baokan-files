Title: Prime Minister U Nu Visits Sweden After Visiting Finland
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, November 7th: According to TASS, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, arrived in Stockholm on the 6th for an official visit. Those who welcomed U Nu at the airport included the Swedish Foreign Minister Undén, the Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ståhlberg, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.
Xinhua News Agency: Finnish President Paasikivi received U Nu, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, who was visiting Finland, on the 5th. U Nu also met with Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen and attended a banquet held by the Finnish Social Democratic Party.
At a press conference held on the 4th after arriving in Helsinki from Moscow, U Nu said that the talks he had with Soviet leaders in Moscow were sincere. He said that the Burmese people have an ardent desire for peace and international cooperation. U Nu emphasized that the goal of Burmese foreign policy is neutrality. He said: "The Geneva spirit" has had an impact all over the world, and as I understand it, this is a good start on the road to international peace.

### 吴努总理访问芬兰后去瑞典

1955-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理访问芬兰后去瑞典
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：缅甸联邦总理吴努六日到达斯德哥尔摩作正式访问。到机场欢迎吴努的有瑞典外交大臣恩顿、外交部礼宾司司长斯塔克尔伯格和各国外交使节。
    据新华社讯　芬兰总统巴锡基维五日接见了访问芬兰的缅甸联邦总理吴努。吴努还会见了芬兰总理吉科宁，参加了芬兰社会民主党的宴会。
    吴努在四日从莫斯科抵达赫尔辛基后举行的记者招待会上说：他在莫斯科同苏联领导人进行的会谈是诚恳的。他说，缅甸人对和平和国际合作抱有热切的愿望。吴努着重指出，缅甸外交政策的目标是中立。他说：“日内瓦精神”在全世界发生了影响，据我理解，这是走上国际和平的道路的良好的开端。
