Title: Ambassador Wu Xiuquan Holds Reception for Chinese Art Troupe's Visit to Yugoslavia
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 22nd: Belgrade News: Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia Wu Xiuquan held a reception on the evening of the 20th for the Chinese Art Troupe's visit and performance in Yugoslavia.

Those attending the reception included: President of the Yugoslav National Assembly Pijade, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council Ranković and Ćolaković, Vice President of the National Assembly Simić, Secretary-General of Foreign Affairs Popović, Chief of General Staff Vukčević, and other senior leaders and figures from the cultural and artistic circles.

Also present were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Yugoslavia.

Yan Jici, head of the Chinese Engineering and Technical Delegation, and all members of the delegation, who were invited to attend the Fourth Congress of the Federation of Yugoslav Engineers and Technicians, also attended the reception.

At the meeting, members of the Chinese Art Troupe and famous Yugoslav artists performed programs. A dance party was held afterward.

### 伍修权大使为我国艺术团访南举行招待会

1955-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　伍修权大使为我国艺术团访南举行招待会
    新华社布拉格二十二日电　贝尔格莱德消息：中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权为中国艺术团到南斯拉夫作访问演出，在二十日晚举行了招待会。
    出席招待会的有：南斯拉夫国民议会主席皮雅杰、联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇和乔拉柯维奇、国民议会副主席西米奇、外交秘书长波波维奇、总参谋长伏奇科维奇以及其他高级领导人员和文化艺术界人士。
    出席的还有各国驻南斯拉夫的外交使节。
    应邀参加南斯拉夫工程师技师联合会第四次代表大会的中国工程技术代表团团长严济慈和全体团员也出席了招待会。
    会上，中国艺术团团员们和南斯拉夫的著名艺术家们表演了节目。随后还举行了舞会。
