Author:
Date: 1955-11-06

    Our Great Motherland
    The Benxi Gongyuan Cement Plant, which had been shut down for ten years and was dilapidated, has completed more than three-quarters of its total project since it began reconstruction and expansion in April this year. After the restoration and expansion of this plant is completed, it will not only increase the production of cement, but also increase the variety of cement. This is the scene of the finished product workshop after restoration.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    停产十年、破旧不堪的本溪工源水泥厂，从今年四月开始动工恢复改建以来，已经完成了总工程量的四分之三以上。这个厂恢复改建完成后，不但能增加水泥的产量，还将增加水泥品种。这是恢复后的制成车间的场面。
