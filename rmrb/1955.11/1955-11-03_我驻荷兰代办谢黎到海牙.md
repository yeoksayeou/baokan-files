Title: Xie Li, China's Chargé d'Affaires to the Netherlands, Arrives in The Hague
Author:
Date: 1955-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 2nd: The Hague Dispatch: Xie Li, Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of the Netherlands, arrived in The Hague on November 1st. He was greeted at the airport by Mr. Rookmaaker, Head of the Eastern Affairs Department of the Dutch Foreign Ministry, the Czechoslovak Minister to the Netherlands, and representatives from the Soviet Union and other countries' embassies in the Netherlands.

### 我驻荷兰代办谢黎到海牙

1955-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻荷兰代办谢黎到海牙
    新华社布拉格二日电　海牙消息：中华人民共和国驻荷兰王国代办谢黎在十一月一日抵达海牙。在机场迎接的有荷兰外交部东方司长罗克迈克尔，捷克斯洛伐克驻荷兰公使和苏联等国驻荷兰使馆的代表等。
