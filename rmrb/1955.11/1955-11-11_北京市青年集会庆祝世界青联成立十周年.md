Title: Beijing Youth Rally Celebrates the Tenth Anniversary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth

Author: 
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 3

    Beijing Youth Gather to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth
    Over a thousand young people from all walks of life in Beijing held a grand rally on the evening of the 10th to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
    The conference was jointly held by the Beijing Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation.
    Luo Yi, Wang Zonghuai, and Zhang Ze, secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Wu Han, vice chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, attended the celebration.
    International students from fourteen countries studying in universities and technical secondary schools in Beijing were invited to attend the meeting.
    The entire delegation of the All-German Youth was also invited to attend the meeting.
    Wang Zhaohua, Second Secretary of the Beijing Committee of the Youth League, spoke at the meeting. After introducing the history of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, he said that the World Federation of Democratic Youth, on its tenth anniversary, can proudly look back on the glorious road it has traveled for the benefit of youth. Now it has grown to have 85 million members in 97 countries and is the largest international youth organization in the world. There is no doubt that the World Federation of Democratic Youth, which is constantly growing, will play a more significant role in uniting the youth of the world in the struggle for peace, democracy, national independence and a better future for youth. Wang Zhaohua then said that the youth of Beijing, together with the youth of the entire China, actively responded to the call of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, participated in the peace movement, and supported the struggles of the youth of various countries to defend their own rights. Wang Zhaohua called on the youth of Beijing to continue to dedicate all their strength and enthusiasm to the cause of promoting international youth unity, to the cause of defending world peace, and to the cause of striving for a better future for the whole world.
    Max Stoll, the head of the All-German Youth Delegation, also spoke at the meeting and extended warm congratulations to the assembly.
    Zhang Jinlin, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Committee of the Youth League, reported on the grand occasion of the Fifth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival held in Warsaw this year and the activities of the Chinese Youth Delegation at the festival and its achievements in art and sports.
    Zhang Baifa, captain of the steel bar worker youth shock brigade, Ren Jinghua, a young worker in the coal washing workshop of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, and Wang Tailai, a student from Peking University, also spoke successively. They unanimously expressed that they would take practical actions, produce more steel, improve production efficiency, and study hard, in order to accelerate the construction of the country's industrialization and strive for world peace.
    The conference passed a congratulatory telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市青年集会庆祝世界青联成立十周年

1955-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

    北京市青年集会庆祝世界青联成立十周年
    北京市各界青年一千多人十日晚举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝世界民主青年联盟成立十周年。
    这次大会是由中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会和北京市民主青年联合会共同举行的。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅、王宗槐、章泽，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席吴晗都出席了庆祝会。
    在北京市各高等学校和中等技术学校学习的十四个国家的留学生应邀出席了大会。
    全德青年代表团全体团员也应邀出席了大会。
    青年团北京市委员会第二书记王照华在会上讲话。他介绍了世界民主青年联盟的历史以后说，世界民主青年联盟在它成立十周年的时候，可以骄傲地回顾它为青年的利益而走过的光荣道路。现在它已壮大到拥有九十七个国家的八千五百万会员，是世界上最大的国际青年组织。毫无疑问，不断壮大着的世界民主青年联盟在团结全世界青年为争取和平、民主、民族独立和青年更美好将来的斗争中将起着更加重大的作用。王照华接着说，北京市青年同全中国青年一道积极响应世界青联的号召，参加和平运动，支援各国青年保卫切身权利的斗争。王照华号召北京青年继续把全部力量和满腔热情献给增进国际青年团结的事业，献给保卫世界和平的事业，献给为争取全世界的美好未来而斗争的事业。
    全德青年代表团团长马克斯·斯托尔也在会上讲了话，他向大会致热烈的祝贺。
    青年团北京市委员会副书记张进霖报告了今年在华沙举行的第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的盛况及中国青年代表团在联欢节的活动和在艺术与体育方面所取得的成就。
    钢筋工青年突击队队长张百发、石景山钢铁厂洗煤车间青年工人任景华、北京大学学生王泰来都相继讲了话。他们一致表示，要以实际行动，多生产钢铁，提高生产效率，努力学习，为加速祖国工业化的建设，为争取世界和平而努力。
    大会通过了致世界民主青年联盟的贺电。
                                  （新华社）
