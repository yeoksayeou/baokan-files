    Our Great Motherland
    In the comprehensive planning for agricultural cooperation in Xinglong Township, District 6, Licheng County, Shandong Province, it is planned to build two small reservoirs that can irrigate 1,500 mu of land. This is the scene of Tian Yuchuan (middle), Secretary of the Xinglong Township Branch of the Chinese Communist Party, inspecting the terrain with comrades from the work group.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山东省历城县六区兴隆乡在进行农业合作化的全面规划中，计划修建两个小型水库，可灌溉一千五百亩土地。这是中共兴隆乡支部书记田玉川（中）正同工作组的同志察看地形的情形。
