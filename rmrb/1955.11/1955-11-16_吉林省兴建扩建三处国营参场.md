Title: Jilin Province to Build and Expand Three State-Owned Ginseng Farms
Author: Wang Lingyun Wang Duyu
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 2

Jilin Province is building two state-owned ginseng farms this year in Ji'an and Jingyu counties at the foot of Changbai Mountain, and expanding the original Fusong State-owned Ginseng Farm. The construction and expansion of these three state-owned ginseng farms will be completed successively from 1957 to 1959. After completion, the ginseng farm area will reach 255,000 square meters, an increase of about ten times compared with the current operating area, and the annual output of red ginseng will be 13,230 jin, worth 500,000 yuan.

### 吉林省兴建扩建三处国营参场
王凌云　王度裕
1955-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　吉林省兴建扩建三处国营参场
    吉林省今年在长白山山下的辑安、靖宇两县，兴建两处国营参场，并对原抚松国营参场进行了扩建工作。这三处国营参场的新建和扩建工作将在一九五七年到一九五九年相继完成，完成后参场面积可达二十五万五千平方公尺，比目前经营面积增加十倍左右，每年可产红参一万三千二百三十市斤，价值五十万元。　　（王凌云　王度裕）
