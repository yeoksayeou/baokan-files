Author:
Date: 1955-11-08

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to develop the agricultural cooperation movement, the number of tractors at the Wu'an County Agricultural Machinery and Tractor Station in Hebei Province has been expanded to thirteen. The cultivated land area has increased from 3,600 mu to 20,564 mu, and they are actively training agricultural tool operators. This is a driver explaining the operation method of the five-furrow plow to the agricultural tool operators.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了开展农业合作化运动，河北省武安县农业机器拖拉机站的拖拉机已扩大到十三部，耕地面积由三千六百亩扩大到二万零五百六十四亩，并积极训练农具手。这是驾驶员给农具手们在讲解五铧犁的操作方法。
