Title: The Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House Publishes Comrade Mao Zedong's "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation"
Author:
Date: 1955-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th. The Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House has published Comrade Mao Zedong's report "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation," delivered on July 31st of this year at the conference of secretaries of provincial, municipal, and district Party committees.

### 苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版毛泽东同志“关于农业合作化问题”

1955-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版毛泽东同志“关于农业合作化问题”
    新华社莫斯科五日电  苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版了毛泽东同志今年七月三十一日在省委、市委和区党委书记会议上的报告：“关于农业合作化问题”。
