Title: Voroshilov, Bulganin, and Molotov's Reply Telegrams Thanking Chinese Leaders for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 1

Reply Telegrams from Voroshilov, Bulganin, and Molotov Thanking Our National Leaders for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    We extend our deep gratitude to you, dear comrades, and to the entire Chinese people, for your heartfelt congratulations and sincere, friendly wishes on the thirty-eighth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The Soviet people accept your congratulations and wishes with immense satisfaction.

    We firmly believe that the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China will continue to be consolidated in the future, and will play a significant role in promoting the comprehensive progress of our two countries, in strengthening the power of the socialist camp, and in consolidating world peace and international security. The Chinese people can rest assured that they will always receive the full support of the Soviet Union in their efforts to build socialism.

    We wish the fraternal Chinese people new achievements in the cause of developing the great People's Republic of China.

            K. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
            N. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
            V. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics

               Moscow, Kremlin, November 17, 1955

### 答谢我国家领导者对十月革命节的祝贺　伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、莫洛托夫的复电

1955-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　答谢我国家领导者对十月革命节的祝贺
　　伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、莫洛托夫的复电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志：中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年时，承致衷心的祝贺和诚挚友好的愿望，我们谨向您们，亲爱的同志们，并向全中国人民表示深切的谢意，苏联人民以极为满意的心情接受您们的祝贺和愿望。
    我们坚信，苏联同中华人民共和国两国人民底牢不可破的友谊今后仍将继续巩固，并在促进我们两国全面进步、在加强社会主义阵营的力量以及巩固世界和平和国际安全的事业中起重大的作用。中国人民可以相信，在其为建成社会主义而作的努力中，始终会获得苏联的全力支持。
    我们祝兄弟般的中国人民在发展伟大的中华人民共和国的事业中获得更新的成就。
            苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团主席　克·伏罗希洛夫
            苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席　尼·布尔加宁
            苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长　维·莫洛托夫
               一九五五年十一月十七日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
