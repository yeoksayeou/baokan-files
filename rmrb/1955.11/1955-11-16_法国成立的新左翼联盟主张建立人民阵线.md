Title: New Left-Wing Alliance Formed in France Advocates Establishing a People's Front
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The new left-wing alliance formed by the French Republican Union of Progress, the Young Republican Party, and other political organizations and groups held its first national congress in Paris. The congress listened to and discussed Manuel Bridier's report on the political situation and Jean Duler's report on economic policy.
    Bridier emphasized that the new left-wing alliance should include left-wing parties and groups. He pointed out that the new left-wing alliance advocates the establishment of a People's Front.
    Jean Duler, a member of the French Economic Council, advocated France's economic independence. He said that striving for economic independence from the United States is necessary to unite left-wing forces.
    The congress adopted a program. The foreign policy portion of it calls for the organization of a European collective security system before Germany is reunified and demands recognition of the People's Republic of China.

### 法国成立的新左翼联盟主张建立人民阵线

1955-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国成立的新左翼联盟主张建立人民阵线
    据新华社讯　由法国进步共和联盟、青年共和党和其他一些政治组织和集团组成的新左翼联盟在巴黎举行第一次全国代表大会。大会听取并且讨论了曼努埃尔·布里迪埃关于政治形势的报告和让·杜勒关于经济政策的报告。
    布里迪埃强调指出，新左翼联盟应当包括左翼政党和集团。他指出，新左翼联盟主张建立人民阵线。
    让·杜勒是法国经济委员会的一个委员，他主张法国在经济上独立。他说，争取对美国经济独立是团结左翼力量所必要的。
    代表大会通过了一个纲领。其中外交政策的部分要求在德国重新统一以前组织欧洲集体安全体系，并且要求承认中华人民共和国。
