Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Japanese Trade Union Delegation Reach Agreement on Various Issues Related to the Workers' Movement
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Japanese Trade Union Delegation visiting China held a signing ceremony today for the agreement reached on various issues related to the workers' movement.

　　The signing ceremony was held at 9:30 PM in Ziguang閣, Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

　　Signing the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side were Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman; and Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat.

　　Signing the agreement on behalf of the Japanese side were Tōtarō Fujita, Head of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation; Masahiro Asai, Secretary-General; and Mitsuo Imai, member of the delegation.

　　Participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Liu Zijiu, Dong Xin, and Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Yu, member of the Executive Committee; Ma Chungu, Director of the International Liaison Department; Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the China Educational Trade Union; Kang Yonghe, Chairman of the First Machine Trade Union; He Yingcai, Head of the Preparatory Committee of the Agricultural and Water Conservancy Trade Union; Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the Coal Mine Trade Union; Zhao Guoqiang, Chairman of the Heavy Industry Trade Union; Li Jinghan, Chairman of the Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union; An Lifu, Chairman of the Highway Transportation Trade Union; Zhang Tianmin, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Construction Trade Union; Zhang Zhaomei, Director of the Working Committee of the Petroleum Trade Union; Zhang Zifu, Vice-Chairman of the Seamen's Trade Union; Huang Minwei, Vice-Chairman of the Electric Power Trade Union; He Ruilin, Vice-Chairman of the Second Machine Trade Union; Liu Jiannong, Vice-Chairman of the Forestry Trade Union; Shao Jingwa, Vice-Chairman of the Textile Trade Union; Zheng Ren, Director of the Working Committee of the Salt Industry Trade Union; Xia Fei, Vice-Director of the Working Committee of the Light Industry Trade Union; Fan Hui, Secretary-General of the Railway Trade Union; Zhao Zhengzhi, Director of the Office of the Store Employees Trade Union; Chen Yongwen, President of the Workers' Daily; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Chen Hansheng, Qian Duansheng, and Luo Longji, Vice-Presidents; Zhao Yimin and Yang Hansheng, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Chen Zhongjing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

　　Participating in the signing ceremony on the Japanese side were Masatake Hashida, Kin'ichi Kubota, Saburō Igarashi, Tamimin Kura, Kazue Taniyanagi, Hisao Maruyama, Akiki Inamura, Masao Yokote, Shigematsu Matsui, and Masayoshi Sakurai, members of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation.

### 中华全国总工会和日本工会代表团　就有关工人运动各项问题达成协议

1955-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会和日本工会代表团
  　就有关工人运动各项问题达成协议
    新华社二十七日讯　中华全国总工会和访问中国的日本工会代表团今日就有关工人运动的各项问题达成的协议举行了签字仪式。
    签字仪式在下午九时半在北京中南海紫光阁举行。
    中国方面在协议上签字的，有中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席刘宁一，书记处书记李颉伯。
    日本方面在协议上签字的，有日本工会代表团团长藤田藤太郎、秘书长浅井正弘、团员今井三男。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记刘子久、董昕、张修竹，执行委员会委员陈宇，国际联络部部长马纯古，中国教育工会主席吴玉章、第一机械工会主席康永和、农业水利工会筹备委员会主任何英才、煤矿工会主席金直夫、重工业工会主席赵国强、邮电工会主席李景韩、公路运输工会主席安力夫、建筑工会筹备委员会主任张天民、石油工会工作委员会主任张兆美、海员工会副主席张孑夫、电业工会副主席黄民伟、第二机械工会副主席贺瑞林、林业工会副主席刘健农、纺织工会副主席邵井蛙、盐业工会工作委员会主任郑仁、轻工业工会工作委员会副主任夏菲、铁路工会秘书长范辉、店员工会办公室主任赵正之，工人日报社社长陈用文，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长陈翰笙、钱端升、罗隆基，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏、阳翰笙，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长陈忠经。
    日本方面参加签字仪式的，有日本工会代表团团员桥田正武、洼田钧一、五十岚三郎、田村民藏、谷沿嘉瑞、丸山久雄、稻村明喜、横手真夫、松井繁松、樱井政由。
