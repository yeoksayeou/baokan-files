Title: Prime Minister U Nu Returns to Moscow After Visiting Various Parts of the Soviet Union; Diplomats from Bandung Conference Nations in the Soviet Union Host a Banquet for U Nu
Author:
Date: 1955-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November. TASS reports: After visiting various parts of the Soviet Union for a week, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his entourage returned to Moscow on the afternoon of November 1st from Alma-Ata via Stalingrad. Since leaving Moscow on October 25th, U Nu has visited Leningrad, Crimea, Baku, Samarkand, and Alma-Ata.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd November. TASS reports: Diplomats from Bandung Conference nations stationed in the Soviet Union—Ambassadors Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary: Menon of India, U Maung of Burma, Tazi of Afghanistan, Subandrio of the Republic of Indonesia, Esin of Turkey, Houni of Egypt; Ministers Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary: Zaraka of Ethiopia, Sreshthaputra of Thailand, Najjar of Lebanon; Chargés d'affaires ad interim: Etesami of Iran, Ahmed of Pakistan, Su Quang Dau of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Wen Ning of China—hosted a banquet on November 1st in honor of U Nu and his wife Daw Mya Yi.

Prime Minister U Nu and his wife Daw Mya Yi, along with members of the Prime Minister's entourage: U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Khin, the Burmese Ambassador to the United Kingdom, and his wife Daw Khin, U Thant, the Prime Minister's Secretary, Colonel Aung Gyi, U Hla, and U Lei, attended the banquet.

The Soviet side was represented by Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zorin, Semyonov, and Federin, the Soviet Ambassador to Burma, Siborin, the Director of the Southeast Asia Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Volkov, the Director of the Near East and Middle East Department, Zaitsev, the Director of the Far East Department, Gudev, and the Director of the Protocol Department of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Tarabrin.

Ambassador of the Republic of India Menon, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma U Nu, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Bulganin all spoke at the banquet. (Attached photo)

Bulganin and U Nu held talks. Kaganovich and Molotov were present at the talks.

U Nu and his entourage visited the "Agricultural Mechanization and Electrification" pavilion of the All-Union Agricultural Exhibition. Photo by Yegorov.

### 吴努总理在苏联各地访问后回到莫斯科  万隆会议国家驻苏外交使节设宴招待吴努

1955-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理在苏联各地访问后回到莫斯科
    万隆会议国家驻苏外交使节设宴招待吴努
    据新华社二日讯　据塔斯社报道：缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的随行人员在苏联各地访问一周以后，已经在十一月一日下午从阿拉木图取道斯大林格勒回到莫斯科。吴努从十月二十五日离开莫斯科以后曾访问了列宁格勒、克里米亚、巴库、撒马尔罕、阿拉木图等地方。
    新华社二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：万隆会议国家驻苏联的外交使节——特命全权大使：印度的梅农、缅甸的吴旺、阿富汗的塔齐、印度尼西亚共和国的苏班德里奥、土耳其的艾辛、埃及的扈尼，特命全权公使：埃塞俄比亚的扎拉卡、泰国的斯勒什塔普特拉、黎巴嫩的纳贾尔；临时代办：伊朗的艾特萨米、巴基斯坦的艾哈迈德、越南民主共和国的苏光斗、中国的温宁，在十一月一日设宴招待吴努及其夫人都妙意。
    吴努总理和他的夫人都妙意，总理的随行人员：缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、缅甸驻英国大使吴钦和他的夫人钦、总理秘书吴丹、上校昂季、吴拉和吴累都出席了宴会。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，苏联外交部副部长佐林、谢苗诺夫和费德林，苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林，苏联外交部东南亚司司长沃尔科夫、近东和中东司司长扎伊采夫、远东司司长顾德夫，苏联部长会议主席典礼局局长塔拉布林。
    印度共和国大使梅农、缅甸联邦总理吴努和苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁都在宴会上讲了话。（附图片）
