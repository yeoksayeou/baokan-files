Title: Portuguese Colonialists Prepare to Celebrate 450th Anniversary of the Occupation of Goa; People of All Strata in India Express Indignation

Author:

Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 24th: The decision by Portuguese colonialists to celebrate the 450th anniversary of their occupation of Goa on November 25th has aroused indignation among people of all strata in India.

Peter Alvares, President of the Goa Congress Party, called on the people to hold an "Anti-Colonialism Day" on November 25th to protest against this action of the Portuguese rulers, which seriously insults the national sentiments of the Indian people.

The Bengali newspaper "Ananda Bazar Patrika" said in an editorial on the 22nd: The Portuguese rulers must be made to "abandon this inexcusable and unreasonable action."

### 葡萄牙殖民者准备庆祝侵占果阿四百五十周年　印度各阶层人民一致表示愤慨

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　葡萄牙殖民者准备庆祝侵占果阿四百五十周年
　　印度各阶层人民一致表示愤慨
    新华社新德里二十四日电　葡萄牙殖民者决定在十一月二十五日庆祝他们占领果阿四百五十周年，这引起了印度各阶层人民的愤慨。
    果阿国大党主席彼得·阿尔瓦雷斯号召人民在十一月二十五日举行“反殖民主义日”，抗议葡萄牙统治者这种严重侮辱印度人民民族情绪的行动。
    孟加拉文日报“欢喜市场报”在二十二日发表社论说：必须使葡萄牙统治者“放弃这种不能宽恕的无理行动”。
