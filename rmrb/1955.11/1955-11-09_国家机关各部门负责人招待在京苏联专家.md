Title: Heads of State Organs and Departments Host Soviet Experts in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th—From the 5th to the 8th, heads of state organs and various departments held various activities to host Soviet experts in Beijing and celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution with them.

	On the 5th, Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, respectively held banquets to entertain Arkhipov, Chief Economic Advisor to the State Council, and Malyshev, Chief Cultural and Educational Advisor to the State Council. On this day, the heads of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China, the First Ministry of Machine Building, the Second Ministry of Machine Building, the Ministry of Coal Industry, the Ministry of Electric Power Industry, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, the Ministry of Geology, the Ministry of Railways, and other departments held banquets to entertain Soviet experts. Some departments held celebrations before the banquets and held joint evening parties after the banquets.

	On the 6th, Soviet experts from various departments invited the heads of various departments and Chinese friends who had contact with them in their daily work to their homes for gatherings and get-togethers.

	On the 8th, heads of state organs, departments, and various universities invited Soviet experts from their departments to their homes as guests, while others invited Soviet experts to visit the capital's scenic spots and have picnics.

### 国家机关各部门负责人招待在京苏联专家

1955-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    国家机关各部门负责人招待在京苏联专家
    新华社八日讯　从五日到八日，国家机关和各部门负责人举行各种活动，招待在京的苏联专家，并同他们进行联欢，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年。
    五日，中华人民共和国国务院副总理李富春、中国科学院院长郭沫若，分别举行宴会招待国务院经济总顾问阿尔希波夫和国务院文教总顾问马里采夫。在这一天，中华人民共和国重工业部、第一机械工业部、第二机械工业部、煤炭工业部、电力工业部、石油工业部、地质部、铁道部以及其他各部门的负责人都举行了宴会招待苏联专家，有的部门在宴会前举行了庆祝会，宴会后并举行了联欢晚会。
    六日，各部门的苏联专家曾邀请各部门的负责人和在日常工作中有联系的中国朋友到自己家里作客联欢。
    八日，国家机关各部门和各高等学校负责人，有的邀请本部门苏联专家到自己家里作客，有的邀请苏联专家游览首都名胜并进行野餐。
