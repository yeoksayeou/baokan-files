Title: India Celebrates Nehru's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 17th. All over India, the sixty-sixth birthday of Indian Prime Minister Nehru was celebrated on November 14th. Many Delhi citizens went to the Prime Minister's residence that morning to offer their congratulations to Nehru.

Prime Minister Nehru also received congratulatory telegrams from political leaders of many countries, including telegrams from Bulganin, Khrushchev, Ho Chi Minh, Pham Van Dong, Eisenhower, and Dulles.

Bulganin's congratulatory telegram stated, "I most happily wish you great success in your noble efforts for world peace and friendship between the Soviet Union and India."

### 印度庆祝尼赫鲁的寿辰

1955-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度庆祝尼赫鲁的寿辰
    据新华社新德里电　印度全国各地在十一月十四日庆祝了印度总理尼赫鲁的六十六岁寿辰。许多德里市民当天上午到总理官邸向尼赫鲁致贺。
    尼赫鲁总理并收到了许多国家的政治领袖的贺电，其中有布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、胡志明、范文同、艾森豪威尔和杜勒斯的贺电。
    布尔加宁的贺电说，我最愉快地祝贺在为世界和平和苏印两国之间的友好所作的崇高努力中取得远大的成功。
