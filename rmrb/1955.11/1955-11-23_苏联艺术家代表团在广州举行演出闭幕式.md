Title: Soviet Artists Delegation Holds Closing Ceremony in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 22nd: The Soviet Artists Delegation, after nearly two months of performance visits in China, held its closing ceremony and final performance in Guangzhou on the evening of the 21st.

In attendance at the closing ceremony and watching the performance were Zhu Guang, the mayor of Guangzhou, compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau who had returned to Guangzhou to visit the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, and more than 5,000 workers, students, cadres, and literary and art workers from Guangzhou.

Song Zhidi, the representative of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, also attended the closing ceremony.

After the closing ceremony began, Zhu Guang spoke on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, congratulating the Soviet artists on the success of their performances in China and expressing heartfelt thanks to them.

The head of the Soviet Artists Delegation, S.F. Nikishkin, spoke to warm applause, repeatedly expressing his gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their warm welcome and hospitality.

Following this, the Soviet artists performed wonderful music, dance, and acrobatics programs.

### 苏联艺术家代表团在广州举行演出闭幕式

1955-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联艺术家代表团在广州举行演出闭幕式
    据新华社广州二十二日电　在中国访问演出将近两个月的苏联艺术家代表团，二十一日晚在广州举行了闭幕式和最后一场演出。
    出席闭幕式和观看演出的，有广州市市长朱光等以及回到广州参观苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会的港澳同胞和广州市的工人、学生、干部、文艺工作者共五千多人。
    中华人民共和国文化部代表宋之的也出席了闭幕式。
    闭幕式开始后，朱光代表我国文化部和中苏友好协会讲话，祝贺苏联艺术家在中国演出的成功，并且向他们表示衷心的感谢。
    苏联艺术家代表团团长斯·弗·尼基什金在热烈的掌声中讲话，他一再感谢中国政府和人民的热烈欢迎和招待。
    接着，苏联艺术家们演出了精采的音乐、舞蹈和杂技节目。
