Our Great Motherland
Eight agricultural machinery stations in Anhui Province are actively helping agricultural production cooperatives with their autumn plowing and sowing work. This year, the land cultivated on their behalf will exceed 86,000 mu, an increase of more than three times over last year. The Cao Lao Ji Agricultural Machinery Station is plowing on behalf of an agricultural production cooperative on the Huaibei Plain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    安徽省八个农业机器站积极地帮助农业生产合作社进行秋耕秋种工作。今年代耕的土地将有八万六千多亩，比去年代耕面积增加三倍以上。曹老集农业机器站正在为淮北平原上的一个农业生产合作社代耕。
