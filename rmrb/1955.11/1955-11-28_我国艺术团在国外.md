　　Chinese Art Troupes Abroad
　　Our Art Troupe Continues its Visit to Yugoslavia
    The Chinese Art Troupe, currently visiting Yugoslavia, performed at the National Opera House in Sarajevo, the capital of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Republic, on the 23rd and 24th, and was warmly welcomed by the audience. Due to popular demand, the theater sold more than half the total number of seats in excess of the theater's capacity. The corridors and lounges were packed with spectators. At the end of the performance, during the curtain call, the actors and the audience enthusiastically threw flowers at each other.
    Responsible personnel from the Bosnia-Herzegovina Republic and the city of Sarajevo attended the performance on the 23rd. After the performance on the 24th, Ubavić, the director of the National Opera House, also delivered a speech on behalf of the public of Sarajevo, expressing his gratitude to the art troupe. Zhang Zhixiang, the head of the art troupe, gave a reply and presented gifts such as ethnic musical instruments.
    Prior to this, the art troupe had performed at the miners' club in the coal city of Tuzla on the 21st. On the 22nd, the art troupe arrived in Sarajevo from Tuzla. When passing through the city of Doboj, thousands of residents and students stood on both sides of the road in the rain, cheering to welcome the art troupe.
    At 8 p.m. on the 22nd, Pucar, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Republic, hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the art troupe. More than 300 high-ranking officials from the republic and the city, as well as people from the literary and artistic circles, attended the reception. Artists from China and Yugoslavia performed artistic programs and held a dance party.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)
　　Our Classical Song and Dance Troupe Concludes its Visit to Denmark
    The Chinese Classical Song and Dance Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Denmark and then went to Iceland for a visit.
    On the evening of the 20th, the troupe held a farewell performance at the New Theater in Copenhagen. On the 19th, the troupe, at the invitation of the Danish National Association for the Welfare of Children with Rheumatic Paralysis, gave a charity performance to raise welfare funds for Danish children with rheumatic paralysis.
    Chu Tunan, the head of the Chinese Classical Song and Dance Troupe, held a reception on the afternoon of the 21st to mark the end of the troupe's visit to Denmark. More than 100 people from all walks of life in Denmark were invited to attend. Chai Chengwen, the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Denmark, also attended the reception.
    In his speech at the meeting, Chu Tunan expressed his gratitude to people from all walks of life in Denmark for their assistance to the troupe. He pointed out that the troupe's visit to Denmark had promoted mutual understanding and friendship between the people of the two countries. He also wished for further development of cultural exchanges between China and Denmark.
    Hansen, chairman of the Danish Association for Cultural Relations with China, praised New China for preserving China's cultural heritage very well in his speech at the meeting.
    Hansen said, "You have gained many new friends in Denmark. You have established new and solid ties between our two peoples. You have captured us like conquering Yandang Mountain. We will never forget you." (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我国艺术团在国外

1955-11-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　我国艺术团在国外
　  我国艺术团继续在南斯拉夫访问
    现在正在南斯拉夫访问的中国艺术团二十三日、二十四日在波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那共和国首都萨拉热窝国家歌剧院举行演出，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。在群众的坚持要求下，剧院出售了超过剧院座位总数一半以上的票。走廊和休息通道上都挤满了观众。当演出结束谢幕时，演员们和观众热烈地互相抛送鲜花。
    波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那共和国和萨拉热窝市的负责人员出席观看了二十三日的演出。在二十四日的演出结束后，国家歌剧院院长乌巴维奇还代表萨拉热窝公众致词，向艺术团表示谢意，艺术团团长张致祥致了答词，并且赠送了民族乐器等礼品。
    在这以前，艺术团二十一日曾在煤都图斯拉市的矿工俱乐部作了演出。二十二日，艺术团从图斯拉市到达萨拉热窝。途中经多波伊城时，成千的居民和学生冒雨站在公路两旁向艺术团欢呼表示欢迎。
    二十二日晚八时，波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那共和国国民议会主席普察尔设宴欢迎艺术团全体人员。出席这次招待会的有这个共和国和市的高级负责人和文艺界人士三百余人。中南两国的艺术家在会上表演了艺术节目并且举行了舞会。
                                    （新华社）
　  我古典歌舞剧团结束在丹麦访问
    中国古典歌舞剧团结束了在丹麦的访问演出，接着去冰岛访问。
    二十日晚上，剧团在哥本哈根新剧院举行了告别演出。十九日，剧团曾经应丹麦风湿瘫痪儿童福利全国协会的邀请，为丹麦风湿瘫痪儿童筹募福利基金义务演出了一场。
    中国古典歌舞剧团团长楚图南二十一日下午为剧团结束在丹麦的访问举行招待会。应邀出席的有丹麦各界人士一百多人。中国驻丹麦公使柴成文也出席了招待会。
    楚图南在会上讲话时对丹麦各界人士给予剧团的协助表示感谢。他指出，剧团在丹麦的访问促进了两国人民的相互了解和友谊。他并且祝中丹两国文化交流进一步发展。
    丹麦对华文化关系协会主席汉生在会上的讲话里赞扬新中国很好地保存了中国的文化遗产。
    汉生说，“你们在丹麦得到了很多新的朋友。你们在我们两国人民间建立了新的牢固的联系。你们像攻占雁荡山一样抓住了我们。我们永远不会忘记你们。”　（新华社）
