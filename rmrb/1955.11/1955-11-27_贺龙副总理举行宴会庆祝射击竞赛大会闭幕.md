Title: Vice Premier He Long Hosts Banquet Celebrating the Closing of the Shooting Competition

Author:
Date: 1955-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 26th: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 26th to celebrate the successful closing of the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition.

Invited to attend the banquet were the Soviet Union's Voluntary Society for Assisting the Army, Navy, and Air Force shooting delegation led by Kustov, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Fatherland Defense Support Association shooting delegation led by Kim Byung-yeon, the Polish People's Republic's Soldiers' Friend League shooting delegation led by Zaidler, the Czechoslovak Republic's Voluntary Association for Assisting the Army shooting delegation led by Gruska, the Romanian People's Republic's Voluntary Association for Supporting Defense shooting delegation led by Niagu, the Bulgarian People's Republic's Voluntary Association for Supporting Defense shooting delegation led by Kamanov, the Mongolian People's Republic's shooting delegation led by Sianiaabu, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's shooting observation delegation led by Wu Lun, and the People's Republic of China's Central National Defense Sports Club shooting delegation led by Ji He.

Also present at the banquet was Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of National Defense General Li Da, Vice Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission Lu Han, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition Cai Shufan, Vice Chairmen General Peng Shaohui, Rong Gaotang, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department General Xiao Hua, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Xue Zizheng, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, and sports figures from the capital.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries to China were also invited to attend the banquet.

Vice Premier of the State Council He Long spoke at the banquet. He extended his heartfelt and warm congratulations on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people to all comrades of the delegations from the fraternal countries participating in the competition and observation.

After discussing the brilliant achievements made by this competition, He Long said: I firmly believe that after this competition, the mass shooting movement in our fraternal countries will be pushed to a higher stage, and the close cooperation between athletes of various countries will be further developed.

At the banquet, the Central National Defense Sports Club of the People's Republic of China presented gifts to the delegations and all members of the delegations from various countries.

A dance party was held after the banquet.

### 贺龙副总理举行宴会庆祝射击竞赛大会闭幕

1955-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　贺龙副总理举行宴会庆祝射击竞赛大会闭幕
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙在二十六日晚举行宴会，庆祝一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会胜利闭幕。
    应邀出席宴会的，有以库斯托夫为首的苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会射击代表团，以金秉渊为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国祖国保卫后援会射击代表团，以扎伊德尔为首的波兰人民共和国战士之友同盟射击代表团，以格鲁斯卡为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国支援军队志愿协会射击代表团，以尼亚古为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会射击代表团，以卡曼诺夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会射击代表团，以西亚尼亚布为首的蒙古人民共和国射击代表团，以吴轮为首的越南民主共和国射击观察代表团和以吉合为首的中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部射击代表团的全体人员。
    出席宴会的，有国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院秘书长习仲勋，国防部副部长李达上将，体育运动委员会副主任卢汉，一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会组织委员会主席蔡树藩、副主席彭绍辉上将、荣高棠，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将，总政治部副主任萧华上将，外交部部长助理何伟，北京市副市长薛子正，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，以及首都体育界人士等。
    苏联和人民民主国家驻中国使节和外交官员也应邀出席了宴会。
    国务院副总理贺龙在宴会上讲话。他代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，向参加竞赛和观察的各兄弟国家代表团全体同志，致以衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    贺龙在谈到这次竞赛大会所取得的光辉成就以后说：我深信，在这次竞赛以后，我们各兄弟国家的群众性射击运动，必将推向更高的阶段，各国运动员之间的紧密合作，必将获得进一步的发展。
    宴会上，中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部分别向各国代表团和代表团全体人员赠送礼品。
    宴会以后举行了舞会。
