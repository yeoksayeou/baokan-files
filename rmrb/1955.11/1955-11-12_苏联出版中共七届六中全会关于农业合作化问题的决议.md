Title: Soviet Union Publishes Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the CPC on the Question of Agricultural Cooperativization
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th: The Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House recently published the Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Question of Agricultural Cooperativization, and will be widely distributed.

### 苏联出版中共七届六中全会关于农业合作化问题的决议

1955-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联出版中共七届六中全会关于农业合作化问题的决议
    新华社莫斯科十一日电　苏联国家政治书籍出版局最近出版了中国共产党第七届中央委员会第六次全体会议（扩大）关于农业合作化问题的决议，并且将普遍发行。
