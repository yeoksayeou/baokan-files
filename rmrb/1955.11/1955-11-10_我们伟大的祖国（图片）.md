Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Gongyuan blast furnace system of Benxi Iron and Steel Company is undergoing restoration and reconstruction. This blast furnace has been out of production for ten years. After restoration and reconstruction, it will have the capacity to produce a large amount of iron, just like the automated blast furnace renovated by Anshan Iron and Steel Company. This is the belt corridor of the Gongyuan blast furnace system. Workers are installing belt conveyors inside.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    本溪钢铁公司工源高炉系统正在进行恢复改建。这个高炉已停产十年，经过恢复和改建后，它将和鞍山钢铁公司改建的自动化高炉一样，具有大量炼铁的能力。这是工源高炉系统的皮带走廊。工人们正在里面安装皮带运输机。
