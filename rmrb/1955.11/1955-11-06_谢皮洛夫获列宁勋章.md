Title: Shepilov Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1955-11-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 5th: TASS reports from Moscow on the 4th that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to celebrate the 50th birthday of Comrade D.T. Shepilov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and to recognize his contributions to the Communist Party and the Soviet state, has awarded him the Order of Lenin.

### 谢皮洛夫获列宁勋章

1955-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫获列宁勋章
    新华社五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科四日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团为祝贺苏联共产党中央委员会书记德·特·谢皮洛夫五十寿辰和表彰他对共产党和苏维埃国家的功绩，特授给他以列宁勋章。
