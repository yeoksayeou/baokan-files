Title: Panmunjom Holds Memorial Service to Commemorate Deceased Polish Personnel
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th - According to the Korean Central News Agency: On the afternoon of November 11th, a memorial service was held in the hall of the Polish delegation of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission (NNSC) in Panmunjom for Major Ciesielski, Captain Runik, and Captain Zielinski of the Polish delegation. The three individuals tragically died in a plane crash while performing their duties aboard a plane provided by the American side.

Those who attended the memorial service included the members of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, the members and other personnel of the Polish, Czechoslovakian, Swiss, and Swedish delegations of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, representatives of the Supreme Headquarters of the Korean People's Army and the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers, representatives of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, representatives of the Polish Embassy in Korea and the Chinese Embassy in Korea, as well as local figures from Kaesong.

Minister Morski, the Polish member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, in his eulogy, demanded that necessary conclusions be drawn in terms of morality, material support, and administration concerning this tragic incident.

### 板门店举行追悼会　纪念遇难的波兰人员

1955-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　板门店举行追悼会
　　纪念遇难的波兰人员
    据新华社平壤十三日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：十一月十一日下午，在板门店中立国监察委员会波兰代表团所在地的大厅内为波兰代表团的齐杰尔斯基少校、鲁尼克大尉、泽林斯基上尉举行追悼会，他们三人是乘坐美方提供的飞机在执行任务时因飞机失事而牺牲的。
    参加追悼会的有军事停战委员会朝中方面的委员，中立国监察委员会波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞士和瑞典的委员和其他人员，朝鲜人民军最高司令部和中国人民志愿军总部的代表，朝鲜外务省代表，波兰驻朝鲜大使馆和中国驻朝鲜大使馆的代表，以及开城当地人士。
    中立国监察委员会波兰委员莫尔斯基公使在致悼词时要求对这次悲惨事件在道义、物质和行政方面作出必要的结论。
