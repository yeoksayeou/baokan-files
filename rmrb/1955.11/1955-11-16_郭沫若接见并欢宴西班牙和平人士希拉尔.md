Title: Guo Moruo Receives and Fetes Spanish Peace Activist Hilar
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received Dr. José Hilar, Chairman of the Spanish Peace Committee and member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, on the evening of the 15th, and held a banquet. Hilar was invited to China to attend the centennial commemoration of the first edition of Walt Whitman's "Leaves of Grass" and the 350th anniversary of the publication of Miguel de Cervantes' "Don Quixote."

Present at the reception were Luo Longji and Chen Hansheng, members of the World Peace Council; Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Chinese scientists Zeng Zhaolun, Chen Kangbai, Wu Xuezhou, Feng Depei, and Xue Yu.

### 郭沫若接见并欢宴西班牙和平人士希拉尔

1955-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若接见并欢宴西班牙和平人士希拉尔
    新华社十五日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在十五日晚接见了西班牙和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会常务委员何塞·希拉尔博士，并且举行了宴会。希拉尔是应邀来我国参加美国惠特曼所著“草叶集”初版一百周年和西班牙塞万提斯所著“堂·吉诃德”出版三百五十周年纪念大会的。
    接见时在座的，有世界和平理事会理事罗隆基、陈翰笙，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎和中国科学界人士曾昭抡、陈康白、吴学周、冯德培、薛愚等人。
