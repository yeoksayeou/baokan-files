Title: China and East Germany Sign Exchange of Goods and Payment Agreement for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1955-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 21st. After sincere and friendly negotiations, the trade delegations of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the German Democratic Republic successfully reached an agreement and signed the Sino-German Exchange of Goods and Payment Agreement for 1956 in Berlin on the 20th.

The agreement stipulates that China will supply Germany with various mineral products, animal products, food, tea, silk, and light industrial products. Germany will supply China with complete sets of equipment and power stations, various machinery, precision instruments, transportation equipment, and chemical fertilizers.

Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, Zeng Yongquan, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of China. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, signed the agreement on behalf of the German Democratic Republic.

### 我国和民主德国签订明年换货和付款协定

1955-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国和民主德国签订明年换货和付款协定
    新华社柏林二十一日电　中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府的贸易代表团经过诚挚友好的谈判后，圆满地达成协议，于二十日在柏林签订了中德一九五六年交换货物和付款协定。
    协定规定：中国方面将供给德国各种矿产品、畜产品、食品、茶叶、丝绸和轻工业产品等。德国方面将供给中国成套设备及发电站、各种机械、精密仪器、运输工具和化学肥料等。
    代表中华人民共和国方面在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉，代表德意志民主共和国方面在协定上签字的是德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与国内贸易部部长海因里希·劳。
