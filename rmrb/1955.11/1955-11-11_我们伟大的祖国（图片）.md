    Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Baotou Sugar Factory, about to go into production, is a highly mechanized and semi-automated factory built with the help of the German Democratic Republic. It can process 1,000 tons of sugar beets daily, producing 260,000 jin of sugar. This is one of the main pieces of equipment in the sugar factory – a molasses crystallizer undergoing trial operation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    即将投入生产的国营包头糖厂是德意志民主共和国帮助我国建设的一座高度机械化、半自动化的工厂，每天能处理甜菜一千吨，产糖二十六万斤。这是糖厂的主要设备之一——糖蜜结晶罐在进行试运转的情形。
