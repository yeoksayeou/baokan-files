Title: Celebrating the 38th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution: Soviet Ambassador Yudin Holds Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th. Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, held a grand reception on the evening of the 7th to celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, and Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, attended the reception tonight.

At the reception, Ambassador Yudin and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches successively (full texts will be published separately).

A total of more than 800 people attended the reception. Among them were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Deng Zihui, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong; Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei; Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and Bao Erhan; Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Ma Yinchu, Shao Lizi, and Li Zhuchen; as well as Directors and Deputy Directors of the State Council's offices, Ministers and Vice Ministers of various ministries, Chairmen and Vice Chairmen of various commissions, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and figures from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, and journalistic circles.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials of various countries in China, Soviet experts and members of Soviet scientific and artistic groups visiting China, and the head of the Yugoslav "Kolo" folk dance troupe also attended the reception.

Before the reception, the Soviet Artists Delegation and the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe performed wonderful programs.

After the reception, a dance was held.

### 庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命三十八周年  苏联大使尤金举行盛大招待会

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命三十八周年
    苏联大使尤金举行盛大招待会
    新华社七日讯　苏联驻中国大使尤金在七日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命三十八周年。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来出席了今晚招待会。
    在招待会上，尤金大使和周恩来总理先后讲了话（全文另发）。
    出席招待会的共有八百多人。他们中有国务院副总理陈云、邓子恢、陈毅、李富春，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、李维汉、陈叔通，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、鲍尔汉，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、刘宁一、廖承志、马寅初、邵力子、李烛尘，以及国务院各办公室主任、副主任，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，中国人民解放军的将军们，北京市副市长，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员，苏联专家和正在中国访问的苏联科学艺术团体人员，南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团负责人等也出席了招待会。
    在招待会举行前，苏联艺术家代表团和苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团演出了精采节目。
    招待会结束后，举行了舞会。
