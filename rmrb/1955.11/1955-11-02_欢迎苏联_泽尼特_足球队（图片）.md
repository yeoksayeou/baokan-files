Title: Welcome the Soviet "Zenit" Football Team (Photos)
Author: Wang Zhiyuan
Date: 1955-11-02
Page: 3

    Welcome the Soviet "Zenit" Football Team
    The captains of the Soviet "Zenit" football team exchange team flags with the Central Institute of Physical Education and the First Machine Football United Team of our country.
    Female athletes from our country present flowers to all members of the "Zenit" football team.
    The opening ceremony of the visiting competition of the Soviet Leningrad "Zenit" Volunteer Sports Association football team was held at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing on the afternoon of October 30. This is the Soviet "Zenit" football team with the Central Institute of Physical Education and the First Machine Football United Team of our country at the opening ceremony of the competition.
    On the afternoon of November 1, the Soviet "Zenit" football team held a friendly match with the Chinese Youth Football Team. This is a shot of the Youth Team players attacking the "Zenit" goal, and "Zenit" goalkeeper Le. Ivanov saving a dangerous ball.
              (All photos in this set were taken by Wang Zhiyuan of this newspaper)

### 欢迎苏联“泽尼特”足球队（图片）
王志渊
1955-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

    欢迎苏联“泽尼特”足球队
    苏联“泽尼特”足球队同我国中央体育学院和第一机械足球联合队的队长交换队旗。
    我国女运动员向“泽尼特”足球队全体队员献花。
    苏联列宁格勒“泽尼特”志愿体育协会足球队十月三十日下午在北京先农坛体育场举行来我国访问比赛的开幕式。这是在比赛开幕式上的苏联“泽尼特”足球队同我国中央体育学院和第一机械足球联合队。
    十一月一日下午，苏联“泽尼特”足球队同我国青年足球队举行友谊比赛。这是青年队队员向“泽尼特”队球门进攻、“泽尼特”队守门员列·伊凡诺夫抢救险球。
              （本组照片均为本报王志渊摄）
