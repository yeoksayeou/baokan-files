Title: Japanese Socialist Party and Hokkaido Assembly Oppose US Military Atomic Rocket Artillery Exercises in Hokkaido
Author:
Date: 1955-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th dispatch from Tokyo: The Japanese Socialist Party sent representatives to visit Minister of Labor Tadao Kuraishi and Director of the American and European Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Akira Chiba, on the 25th, requesting the Japanese government to prevent the US military authorities from carrying out atomic rocket artillery exercises in Hokkaido.

On the 24th, the Agricultural Land Committee of the Hokkaido Assembly passed a resolution firmly opposing the US military's atomic rocket artillery exercises in Chigun County, Hokkaido. The committee believes that such exercises by the US military will threaten the safety of local residents.

### 日本社会党和北海道议会　反对美军在北海道进行原子火箭炮演习

1955-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本社会党和北海道议会
　　反对美军在北海道进行原子火箭炮演习
    新华社二十六日讯　东京消息：日本社会党二十五日派代表访问劳动相仓石忠雄和外务省欧美局长千叶皓，要求日本政府阻止美国军事当局在北海道演习原子火箭炮的计划。
    二十四日，北海道议会的农地委员会通过决议，坚决反对美军在北海道千岩郡进行原子火箭炮演习。委员会认为，美军的这种演习将威胁当地居民的安全。
