Title: South "Kolo" Dance Troupe Presents National Costumes to Chairman Mao, Accepted on His Behalf by Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe presented a set of Serbian national costumes to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 9th, which was accepted on his behalf by Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

	At the presentation ceremony, Skovran, the head of the dance troupe, said in his speech that the dance troupe's two-month visit to China had left us with unforgettable impressions of the Chinese people. Chairman Mao Zedong received the leaders of the dance troupe on National Day, and Chairman Mao Zedong and other Chinese leaders watched the dance troupe's performance, which was a great concern for us and we are honored by it. Skovran concluded by thanking the Chinese people for the friendship and care shown to the dance troupe.

	Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popovic then said in his speech that we feel honored and excited at this moment because our thoughts and feelings are directed towards the great son of the Chinese people, Comrade Mao Zedong. Popovic said, I firmly believe that the brotherly, equal, and sincere friendship between the people of our two countries, China and Yugoslavia, will be developed in the future.

	Qian Junrui also spoke at the ceremony. He said that this gift represents the high degree of friendship of the Yugoslav people towards the Chinese people. The Chinese people will certainly make the greatest efforts to strengthen and consolidate the friendship between the people of the two countries. Qian Junrui finally asked the comrades of the dance troupe to convey the friendship of the Chinese people to the Yugoslav people.

	Also present at the presentation ceremony were Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, some members of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe, and literary and art figures such as Ouyang Yuqian, President of the Central Academy of Drama.

### 南“科罗”歌舞团向毛主席献赠民族服装  由文化部代理部长钱俊瑞代为受礼

1955-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    南“科罗”歌舞团向毛主席献赠民族服装
    由文化部代理部长钱俊瑞代为受礼
    新华社九日讯　南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团在九日以塞尔维亚民族服装一套献赠给毛泽东主席，由中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞代为受礼。
    歌舞团团长斯科夫兰在献礼仪式上致词说，歌舞团在中国进行了两个月的访问，中国人民给我们留下了难忘的印象。毛泽东主席在国庆节接见了歌舞团领导人，毛泽东主席和中国其他领导同志观看歌舞团的演出，这是对我们极大的关怀，我们引以为光荣。斯科夫兰最后并感谢中国人民对歌舞团所表示的友情和关怀。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇接着致词说，此刻我们感到光荣和兴奋，因为我们的思想和感情是向着中国人民的伟大儿子毛泽东同志。波波维奇说，我深信我们中南两国人民兄弟般的平等诚恳的友谊，在将来是会得到发展的。
    钱俊瑞也在仪式上致词。他说，这次送礼表示着南斯拉夫人民对中国人民的高度的友谊。中国人民一定用最大的努力来加强和巩固两国人民之间的友谊。钱俊瑞最后请歌舞团同志向南斯拉夫人民转达中国人民的友谊。
    参加献礼仪式的，还有中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙，南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团部分团员，以及中央戏剧学院院长欧阳予倩等文艺界人士。
