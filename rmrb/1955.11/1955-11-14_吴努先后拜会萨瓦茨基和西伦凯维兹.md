Title: U Nu Visits Zawadzki and Cyrankiewicz
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 13th: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, who is visiting Poland at the invitation of the Polish government, paid visits to the Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, Aleksander Zawadzki, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, on November 11th.
    On the evening of the same day, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Józef Cyrankiewicz, held a reception to welcome Prime Minister U Nu.
    The Polish side attending the reception included: leaders of the Polish United Workers' Party and the state, Bolesław Bierut, Aleksander Zawadzki, Konstanty Rokossowski, and Edward Ochab, among others.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland were also invited to attend the reception.
    At the reception, Cyrankiewicz and U Nu delivered speeches successively. In his speech, U Nu announced an invitation to Zawadzki and Cyrankiewicz to visit the Union of Burma.
    On the 12th, Prime Minister U Nu also attended a welcoming banquet held for him by the Chairman of the Council of State of Poland, Aleksander Zawadzki.
    In the past two days, Prime Minister U Nu and his entourage also visited the urban area of Warsaw and visited some factories and cultural institutions.

### 吴努先后拜会萨瓦茨基和西伦凯维兹

1955-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　吴努先后拜会萨瓦茨基和西伦凯维兹
    新华社华沙十三日电　应波兰政府的邀请到波兰访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努，十一月十一日先后拜会了波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基和部长会议主席西伦凯维兹。
    同日晚间，波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹举行招待会，欢迎吴努总理。
    波兰方面出席招待会的有：波兰统一工人党和国家的领导人贝鲁特、萨瓦茨基、罗科索夫斯基和诺瓦克等。
    各国驻波兰的外交使节也应邀出席招待会。
    招待会上，西伦凯维兹和吴努相继致词。吴努在讲话中宣布邀请萨瓦茨基和西伦凯维兹访问缅甸联邦。
    十二日，吴努总理又出席了波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基为他举行的招待宴会。
    这两天来，吴努总理和他的随行人员还参观了华沙市区，访问了一些工厂和文化机关。
