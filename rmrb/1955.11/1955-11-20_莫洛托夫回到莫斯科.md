Title: Molotov Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: According to TASS, Moscow: Molotov, head of the Soviet delegation to the Four Foreign Ministers Conference, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and Soviet Foreign Minister, returned to Moscow on November 18th. Returning to Moscow with Molotov were members of the Soviet delegation, Marshal of the Soviet Union Sokolovsky, and others.

### 莫洛托夫回到莫斯科

1955-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　  莫洛托夫回到莫斯科
    新华社十九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席四国外交部长会议的苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，十一月十八日回到莫斯科。同莫洛托夫一起回到莫斯科的还有：苏联代表团团员、苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基等人。
