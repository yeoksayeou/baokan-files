　　Agreement Between the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Japanese Trade Union Delegation

　　In November 1955, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Japanese Trade Union Delegation visiting the People's Republic of China held talks in a friendly atmosphere on various issues related to the labor movement. During these talks, both sides reached a basically unanimous opinion not only on the friendship and solidarity between the trade unions of China and Japan, but also more broadly on the friendship and solidarity between all trade unions in Asia and Africa.

　　In order to specifically promote the above matters, the following efforts will be made through mutual consultation:

　　1. Call on all trade unions in Asia and Africa to further strengthen friendship and solidarity for their common tasks, namely national independence, peace, and improved working conditions.

　　2. In order to further deepen the friendship between the trade unions of China and Japan, efforts should be made to promote exchanges between the trade unions of the two countries. In this regard, special attention should be paid to exchanges between industrial unions.

　　3. The trade unions of China and Japan will, from the perspective of the trade unions, work to restore normal diplomatic relations and economic and cultural exchanges between the two countries.

　　4. Exchange information, etc., to deepen mutual understanding.

　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Lai Ruoyu
　　Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi
　　Secretary Li Jiebo

　　　　Head of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation Visiting China, Fujita Totaro
　　Secretary General Asai Masahiro
　　Delegate Imai Mitsuo

　　November 27, 1955

### 中华全国总工会和日本工会代表团的协议

1955-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会和日本工会代表团的协议
    一九五五年十一月，中华全国总工会和访问中华人民共和国的日本工会代表团，在友好的气氛中，对有关工人运动的各项问题进行了座谈。在这次座谈中，双方不但在中国同日本两国工会之间的友好、团结问题上，而且更广泛地在有关亚洲和非洲所有工会之间的友好、团结问题上，取得了基本上一致的意见。
    为了具体地促进以上事项，互相协商决定作以下的努力：
    一、号召亚洲和非洲的一切工会，为了它们的共同任务，即民族独立、和平和提高劳动条件，更进一步地加强友好和团结。
    二、为了进一步加深中日两国工会之间的友好，要为两国工会之间的来往而努力。在这方面特别要重视产业工会之间的来往。
    三、中日两国工会，将从工会的角度上，为恢复两国正常的邦交和经济、文化的交流而努力。
    四、相互交换资料等，以加深互相的理解。
