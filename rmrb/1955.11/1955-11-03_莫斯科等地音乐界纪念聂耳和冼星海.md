Title: Musical Circles in Moscow and Other Places Commemorate Nie Er and Xian Xinghai
Author:
Date: 1955-11-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: According to TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Composers Association, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Moscow State Conservatory held an evening party on October 31st to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the death of Chinese composer Nie Er and the 10th anniversary of the death of Xian Xinghai. The evening party was attended by famous Soviet composers and musicians. Representatives from the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union were also present.

　　The evening party was opened by Khrennikov, General Secretary of the Soviet Composers Association. He pointed out that Nie Er and Xian Xinghai, two outstanding sons of the great Chinese people, dedicated all their strength, creative energy, and talent to their motherland.

　　Khrennikov said that the creative legacy of Nie Er and Xian Xinghai is not only loved by the Chinese working people, but also frequently performed in the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries. The Soviet people, together with the Chinese people, hold these two outstanding composers in high esteem.

　　Shneurson, the author of a famous work on Chinese music, reported on the life and work of Nie Er and Xian Xinghai at the meeting.

　　Works by the two composers were also performed at the evening party.

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: According to TASS, Alma-Ata: Musical circles in Alma-Ata, the capital of the Kazakh Republic, commemorated the 10th anniversary of the death of the famous Chinese composer Xian Xinghai.

　　Kazakh composer Bakadanov told a TASS reporter that Xian Xinghai spent the last five years of his life in the Soviet Union, most of which were spent in Kazakhstan. He met many Kazakh composers in Alma-Ata. Here, he created the "National Liberation Symphony" praising the Great Patriotic War, the "Rear Area" suite, a violin solo, and two piano pieces praising the heroes of the Kazakh people, "Amangeldy," as well as many chamber and instrumental works.

　　Yerzakovitch, Director of the Art Department of the Kazakh Republic Academy of Sciences and a composer who was familiar with Xian Xinghai, said that Xian Xinghai was a humble and approachable person who came to Alma-Ata to further understand the musical art of the Kazakh people, which he loved very much. Yerzakovitch pointed out that Xian Xinghai had always lived a passionate and creative life. The Kazakh composers, in commemorating Xian Xinghai, are very pleased to see the rapid development of Chinese music, to which this glorious son of the Chinese people made valuable contributions.

### 莫斯科等地音乐界纪念聂耳和冼星海

1955-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科等地音乐界纪念聂耳和冼星海
    新华社二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联作曲家协会、苏联对外文化协会和莫斯科国立音乐馆在十月三十一日举行了一个晚会，纪念中国作曲家聂耳逝世二十周年和冼星海逝世十周年。参加晚会的有著名的苏联作曲家和音乐家。到会的还有中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆的代表。
    晚会由苏联作曲家协会总书记赫连尼科夫宣布开幕。他指出，聂耳和冼星海这两个伟大中国人民的优秀儿子曾把全部力量、创作精力和全部天才贡献给祖国。
    赫连尼科夫说，聂耳和冼星海的创作遗产不仅为中国劳动人民所喜爱，而在苏联和在人民民主国家也常常演奏。苏联人民和中国人民一道对这两位卓越的作曲家怀着崇高的敬意。
    一本论中国音乐的著名作品的作者希涅尔逊，在会上报告了聂耳和冼星海的生平和事业。
    在晚会上还演奏了这两位作曲家的作品。
    新华社二日讯　据塔斯社阿拉木图讯：哈萨克共和国首都阿拉木图音乐界纪念中国著名作曲家冼星海逝世十周年。
    哈萨克作曲家巴卡达诺夫对塔斯社记者说，冼星海生命中的最后五年是在苏联度过的，其中大部分时间又是在哈萨克度过的。他在阿拉木图结识了许多哈萨克的作曲家。他在这里创作了歌颂伟大卫国战争的“民族解放交响乐”、“后方”组曲、一首小提琴独奏曲和两首歌颂哈萨克人民英雄的钢琴曲“阿曼盖尔德”以及许多室乐和器乐作品。
    熟悉冼星海的哈萨克共和国科学院艺术学部主任、作曲家叶尔扎科维奇说，冼星海是个谦虚、令人亲近的人，他为了更深地了解他很喜爱的哈萨克人民的音乐艺术而到了阿拉木图。叶尔扎科维奇指出，冼星海一直过着充满热情的、创造性的生活。哈萨克的作曲家在纪念冼星海的时候，对于这位中国人民光荣儿子曾对之作过宝贵贡献的中国音乐得到急速的发展感到非常高兴。
