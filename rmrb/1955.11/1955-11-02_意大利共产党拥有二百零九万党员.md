Title: The Italian Communist Party Has 2,090,000 Members
Author:
Date: 1955-11-02
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 1st (TASS dispatch): The leading body of the Italian Communist Party has released a public announcement regarding the results of the 1955 campaign to renew party membership cards for Italian Communist Party members. As of September 30th, there were a total of 2,091,000 members, including 160,000 new members who joined the party in 1955.

### 意大利共产党拥有二百零九万党员

1955-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利共产党拥有二百零九万党员
    新华社一日讯　塔斯社罗马一日讯：意大利共产党的领导机关发表了关于一九五五年更换意大利共产党员党证运动的结果的公报。截至九月三十日，共有党员二百零九万一千人，其中包括一九五五年入党的十六万新党员。
