Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive French Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China received the French Parliamentary Delegation led by Daniel Mayer, along with members Maurice Faure, Jean-Raymond Fruchié, and René Kuhn, on the afternoon of the first.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President, Huang Hua, Executive Director, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General. (Photo attached)

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi pictured with the French Parliamentary Delegation after the meeting. From left to right: Zhou Gengsheng, Jean-Raymond Fruchié, Daniel Mayer, Zhou Enlai, Maurice Faure, René Kuhn, Chen Yi, Zhang Xiruo.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wu Huaxue.

### 周总理和陈毅副总理接见法国议员代表团

1955-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和陈毅副总理接见法国议员代表团
    新华社一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅，在一日下午接见了法国议员代表团团长丹尼埃·麦耶和团员莫里斯·富尔、让—雷蒙·弗吕希埃、雷纳·库恩。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长周鲠生，常务理事黄华，秘书长吴茂荪。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理接见法国议员代表团后合影。自左至右：周鲠生、让—雷蒙·弗吕希埃、丹尼埃·麦耶、周恩来、莫里斯·富尔、雷纳·库恩、陈毅、张奚若。
