Title: JCP Central Committee Issues Slogans for Striving to Restore Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and the Soviet Union and Between Japan and China
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th. The Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party issued slogans on October 28 regarding striving to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union and between Japan and China. The slogans are as follows:

    Restoring diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union and between Japan and China is the path to peace, independence, and prosperity.
    Restore diplomatic relations first, then resolve outstanding issues.
    The government must fulfill its promise to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union.
    The delay in Japan-Soviet diplomatic negotiations is at the instigation of the United States.
    Use the power of the people to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union and between Japan and China.

### 日共中央发布争取恢复日苏日中邦交口号

1955-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    日共中央发布争取恢复日苏日中邦交口号
    新华社七日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央委员会常任干部会于十月二十八日发布了关于争取恢复日苏日中邦交的口号。口号如下：
    恢复日苏、日中邦交，才是和平、独立和繁荣的道路。
    先恢复邦交，后解决悬案。
    政府要履行恢复日苏邦交的诺言。
    日苏邦交谈判的拖延，是美国指使的。
    以人民的力量使日苏、日中邦交恢复。
