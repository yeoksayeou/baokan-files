Our Great Motherland
The Deli Temple Fruit Tree Production Cooperative in Fuxian County, Liaoning Province, has achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest of apples this year. The average yield per tree has increased by nine catties, with high-quality apples accounting for more than two-thirds. Female cooperative members are selecting high-quality apples to sell to the state.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    辽宁省复县得利寺果树生产合作社今年苹果获得空前丰收。每棵果树平均增产九斤，其中优等苹果占三分之二以上。妇女社员们正挑选优等苹果卖给国家。
