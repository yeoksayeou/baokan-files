Title: Japanese Navy Holds Combat Exercises with Participation of the US Navy
Author:
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 28th: The Japanese Navy began a large-scale sea and air combined combat exercise on the 27th, lasting eleven days, in the eastern coastal area. Simultaneously, exercises such as enemy beach landings will be conducted in the Seto Inland Sea area.

A total of more than 5,000 Japanese naval personnel, 80 ships, and 20 aircraft are participating in this exercise.

US Navy submarines and aircraft are also participating in this exercise.

Not long ago, in coordination with the US Air Force, the Japanese army conducted large-scale combat exercises in Hokkaido.

### 在美国海军参加下  日本海军举行作战演习

1955-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    在美国海军参加下
    日本海军举行作战演习
    新华社二十八日讯　东京消息：日本海军从二十七日开始在东部沿海一带举行为期十一天的大规模海空立体作战演习。同时，在濑户内海方面还将举行敌前登陆等演习。
    参加这次演习的共有日本海军五千多人，舰艇八十艘和飞机二十架。
    美国海军的潜水艇和飞机也在参加这次演习。
    不久以前，在美国空军配合下，日本军队曾在北海道举行了大规模的作战演习。
