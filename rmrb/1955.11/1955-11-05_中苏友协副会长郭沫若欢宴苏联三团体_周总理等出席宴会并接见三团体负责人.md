Title: Guo Moruo, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Hosts a Banquet for Three Soviet Delegations; Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Attend the Banquet and Receive the Heads of the Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th – Guo Moruo, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 4th to welcome the Soviet Artists Delegation headed by Nikishkin, the Soviet Medical Science Delegation headed by Krotkov, and the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe headed by Nadezhdina. Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the banquet and, before the banquet, received the heads and some members of the Soviet Artists Delegation, the Soviet Medical Science Delegation, and the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe.

Also attending the banquet were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Li Jishen, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Liao Chengzhi, Ma Yinchu, Shao Lizi, and Li Zhuchen, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; as well as directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, heads of various people's organizations, and people from the capital's scientific, artistic, and journalistic circles, totaling more than two hundred people.

Guo Moruo spoke at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the outstanding Soviet scientists and artists. He said that since the victory of the October Revolution, over the past thirty-eight years, Soviet science and art have achieved brilliant accomplishments, making great contributions to the great socialist and communist construction cause of the Soviet Union. Guo Moruo said: We must study diligently, work hard, and, under the brilliant example of the outstanding Soviet scientists and artists, complete our socialist construction cause ahead of schedule.

Nikishkin, head of the Soviet Artists Delegation, delivered a speech at the banquet, thanking the Chinese government for their care and the Chinese people for their deep friendship, and raising a glass to congratulate the development of Chinese culture and art.

Krotkov, head of the Soviet Medical Science Delegation, then spoke, extending warm greetings to Chinese scientists and medical experts, wishing them greater achievements in their work, and wishing that the friendly relations between Chinese and Soviet scientists would become increasingly close.

Nadezhdina, the artistic director of the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe, thanked the Chinese people for their kind care, saying that no matter where the dance troupe went in China, they were always greeted with smiling faces and friendly handshakes.

At the banquet, the guests repeatedly toasted the great and unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, the development of scientific and artistic undertakings of the two countries, the good health of the leaders of the two parties and governments, and the cause of world peace.

After the banquet, artists from China and the Soviet Union performed brilliant artistic programs. (Attached picture)

### 中苏友协副会长郭沫若欢宴苏联三团体  周总理等出席宴会并接见三团体负责人

1955-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协副会长郭沫若欢宴苏联三团体
    周总理等出席宴会并接见三团体负责人
    新华社四日讯　中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若，在四日晚举行宴会，欢迎以尼基什金为首的苏联艺术家代表团、以克罗特科夫为首的苏联医学科学代表团和以纳杰日金娜为首的苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团。苏联驻华大使尤金和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅出席了宴会，并在宴会举行前，接见了苏联艺术家代表团、苏联医学科学代表团、苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团的负责人和部分团员。
    出席宴会的还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、吴玉章、沈钧儒、廖承志、马寅初、邵力子、李烛尘，卫生部部长李德全，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，外交部副部长张闻天，北京市副市长王昆仑，以及中苏友好协会总会理事，各人民团体负责人，首都科学界、文艺界、新闻界人士等二百多人。
    郭沫若在宴会上讲话，对优秀的苏联科学家和艺术家表示热烈的欢迎。他说：自从十月革命胜利以来，三十八年间，苏联的科学和艺术有了辉煌的成就，对苏联的伟大的社会主义和共产主义建设事业，有了很大的贡献。郭沫若说：我们要努力学习，努力工作，在优秀的苏联科学家和艺术家们的光辉的示范之下，把我们的社会主义建设事业提前完成。
    苏联艺术家代表团团长尼基什金在宴会上致词，感谢中国政府对他们的关怀，感谢中国人民的深厚的友谊，并举杯祝贺中国文化艺术的发展。
      苏联医学科学代表团团长克罗特科夫接着讲话，向中国科学家和医学家致热烈的敬礼，祝他们在工作中获得更大的成就，祝中苏两国科学家之间的友好联系日益密切。
      苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团艺术领导人纳杰日金娜在讲话中感谢中国人民的亲切关怀，她说：舞蹈团不论是在中国的那一个地方，迎接她们的总是微笑的脸和友谊的握手。
    宴会上，宾主们一再为中苏两国人民伟大的牢不可破的友谊，为两国科学、艺术事业的发展，为两国党和政府的领袖的身体健康，为世界和平事业而干杯。
    宴会以后，中苏两国的艺术家们表演了精采的文艺节目。（附图片）
