Title: Nehru Speaks on China's Representation in the United Nations and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 14th. According to a report by the Indian Press Trust: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said on the 12th that the only way to resolve the issue of China's representation in the United Nations is to restore the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China.
    Nehru made these remarks while speaking at a mass meeting in Aligarh. He also said: "The idea of Taiwan claiming to represent China is like the Laccadive Islands saying they represent India."

### 尼赫鲁谈中国在联合国的代表权等问题

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁谈中国在联合国的代表权等问题
    新华社新德里十四日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道：印度总理尼赫鲁十二日说，解决中国在联合国的代表权问题的唯一办法，就是恢复中华人民共和国的合法权利。
    尼赫鲁是在阿里加一个群众大会上讲话时说这些话的。他还说：“台湾声称自己代表中国这种事情就像辣喀代夫岛说它代表印度一样。”
