Title: While Conferring with Representatives of the Malayan Communist Party on Peace Talks, Malayan Authorities Announce Large-Scale Offensive
Author:
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - Singapore Dispatch: Malayan Federation Chief Minister Rahman and British Operational Commander in Malaya, Bourne, issued a joint statement in Kuala Lumpur on the 21st, announcing that they would "resume large-scale offensive operations" against the Malayan National Liberation Army. The joint statement also stated that "bombing, shelling, and mortar fire will be resumed wherever necessary."

According to foreign news agencies, the day before this joint statement was issued (the 20th), representatives of the Chief Minister of the Malayan Federation were still conferring with representatives of the Malayan Communist Party in Klian Intan to arrange peace negotiations.

### 在同马共代表会商和谈问题的同时　马来亚当局宣布要大规模进攻

1955-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　在同马共代表会商和谈问题的同时
　　马来亚当局宣布要大规模进攻
    新华社二十二日讯　新加坡消息：马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼和英国驻马来亚作战司令鲍恩二十一日在吉隆坡发表联合声明，宣布将对马来亚民族解放军“恢复采取大规模的进攻行动”。联合声明还说，“将在需要的任何地方恢复进行轰炸、炮轰和以迫击炮进行轰击”。
    据外国通讯社的消息，在这项联合声明发表的前一天（二十日），马来亚联合邦首席部长的代表还同马来亚共产党的代表在克连英丹会商安排和平谈判的问题。
