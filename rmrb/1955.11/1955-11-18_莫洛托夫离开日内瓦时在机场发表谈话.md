Title: Molotov Speaks at Airport Upon Leaving Geneva

Author:

Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Geneva Dispatch from TASS: V.M. Molotov, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, left Geneva on November 17.

　　At Cointrin Airport to see Molotov off were: representatives of the Swiss authorities, the Soviet Envoy to Switzerland, Mr. Yershov, heads of diplomatic missions of various countries to Switzerland, Wang Bingnan, the representative of the People's Republic of China to the Sino-American talks in Geneva, members of the Soviet delegation, experts, and technical personnel, among others.

　　Molotov delivered the following remarks at the airport:

　　At the closing meeting of the Foreign Ministers' Conference, the Soviet delegation explained its opinions and evaluations of the results of this conference. The Soviet delegation pointed out the significance of this conference, as well as what the conference failed to achieve.

　　On behalf of the Soviet delegation, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the Federal Government of Switzerland and the authorities of the Canton and City of Geneva for their traditional hospitality, and to convey our respects and best wishes to the residents of Geneva and the entire Swiss people.

### 莫洛托夫离开日内瓦时在机场发表谈话

1955-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫离开日内瓦时在机场发表谈话
    新华社十七日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫在十一月十七日离开日内瓦。
    到康特兰机场欢送莫洛托夫的有：瑞士当局的代表，苏联驻瑞士公使叶尔绍夫，各国驻瑞士外交使团首长，中华人民共和国出席日内瓦中美会谈的代表王炳南，苏联代表团的团员、专家和技术人员等人。
    莫洛托夫在机场发表了下列谈话：
    苏联代表团在外交部长会议的闭幕会议上说明了它对这次会议的结果的意见和评价。苏联代表团指出了这次会议的意义，也指出了会议所未能实现的东西。
    我愿意代表苏联代表团对瑞士联邦政府、日内瓦州和日内瓦市当局的传统的好客精神表示深切的感谢，并且向日内瓦的居民和全体瑞士人民转达我们的敬意和最好的祝愿。
