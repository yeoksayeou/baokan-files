Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the West German Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received the West German Youth Delegation, who were invited to visit our country, on the afternoon of the 7th.

During the meeting, the head of the West German Youth Delegation, Max Stoll Geboren, spoke about his impressions of visiting China and expressed his intention to convey the concern of the Chinese people and youth for the German people's struggle for national reunification, as well as their friendship, to the youth of all of Germany. Chen Yi answered the questions raised by the delegates.

Also present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Wu Han, Vice Chairman; Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; and Zhao Fusan, Deputy General Secretary of the Beijing YMCA.

### 陈毅副总理接见全德青年代表团

1955-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见全德青年代表团
    新华社七日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在七日下午接见了应邀来我国访问的全德青年代表团。
    在接见时，全德青年代表团团长马克斯·斯托尔·格博伦谈了他们访问中国的观感，并且表示要将中国人民和青年对德国人民争取祖国统一的斗争的关怀和对他们的友情转达给全德国的青年。陈毅回答了代表们提出的问题。
    接见时在座的有中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志、副主席吴晗，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅，北京中华基督教青年会副总干事赵复三等人。
