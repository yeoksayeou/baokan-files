　　Friendly Exchanges

　　Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Pakistan
    At the invitation of the All Pakistan Women's Association, a Chinese women's delegation has recently departed from Beijing for a friendly visit to Pakistan. The delegation is led by Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Members include Gong Pusheng, Liu Qingyang, Han Youtong, Zhang Qian, Rashida, Huang Ganying, and Qian Xing. Two staff members are accompanying the delegation.
　　
　　Chinese Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Albania
    The Chinese cultural delegation, which is visiting Albania according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing for the People's Republic of Albania by plane on the 22nd. The head of the delegation is Liu Shi, Deputy Minister of Education, and the members include Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Sun Jinglu, Vice-President of the Beijing Agricultural Mechanization College. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-11-24
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
　　我国妇女代表团访问巴基斯坦
    中国妇女代表团应巴基斯坦全巴妇女协会的邀请，已在日前由北京启程前往巴基斯坦作友好访问。代表团由中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全率领，团员有龚普生、刘清扬、韩幽桐、张茜、拉希达、黄甘英、钱行。另有两名工作人员随行。
　　我国文化代表团离京赴阿尔巴尼亚
    根据中阿文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划前往阿尔巴尼亚访问的中国文化代表团，在二十二日乘飞机离开北京去阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国。代表团团长是教育部副部长柳湜，团员有民族事务委员会副主任萨空了、北京农业机械化学院副院长孙景鲁。（据新华社讯）
