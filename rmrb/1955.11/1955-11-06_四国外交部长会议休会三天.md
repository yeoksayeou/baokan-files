Title: Four Foreign Ministers Conference Adjourns for Three Days
Author:
Date: 1955-11-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 5th. The Four Foreign Ministers Conference will adjourn for three days from November 5th to 7th. The next meeting is scheduled for November 8th.
November 5th and 6th are Saturday and Sunday, and the meeting will be adjourned as usual. November 7th is adjourned because of the Soviet National Day—the 38th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
During the adjournment, the foreign ministers will leave Geneva.

### 四国外交部长会议休会三天

1955-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    四国外交部长会议休会三天
    新华社日内瓦五日电　四国外交部长会议从十一月五日到七日休会三天。下次会议定在十一月八日举行。
    十一月五、六两日是星期六和星期日，会议照例休会。十一月七日是因为苏联国庆节——十月社会主义革命三十八周年纪念日而休会。
    在休会期间，外交部长们要离开日内瓦。
