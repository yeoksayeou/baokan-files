Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Visits Belgium
Author:
Date: 1955-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels: The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by head of delegation Wu Xueqian, arrived in Brussels on November 21st for a friendly visit to Belgium at the invitation of the Belgian Committee for the Promotion of East-West Exchange.

　　Since the Chinese Youth Delegation arrived in Belgium a week ago, it has received a warm reception everywhere. Belgian youth organizations have taken turns being responsible for reception matters, and many cities have established reception committees. Brussels radio and television stations regularly broadcast information about the delegation's activities.

　　The Mayor of Antwerp, Craeybeckx, and the Mayor of Grammont, Reyns, have successively received the delegation. They all expressed their welcome for cultural and economic exchanges and mutual visits between the youth of Belgium and China, believing that this would strengthen the understanding and friendship between the people of the two countries.

### 中国青年代表团访问比利时

1955-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国青年代表团访问比利时
    据新华社讯　布鲁塞尔消息：应比利时东西方交流促进委员会邀请到比利时作友好访问的中国青年代表团，在团长吴学谦率领下已经在十一月二十一日到达了布鲁塞尔。
      中国青年代表团到达比利时一星期以来，到处受到热情的接待。比利时各青年组织轮流负责接待事宜，许多城市都成立了接待委员会。布鲁塞尔各电台和电视台经常播送代表团的活动情况。
    安特卫普市长克拉埃贝克斯、格拉蒙市长雷恩先后接见了代表团，他们都表示欢迎比、中两国的文化、经济交流和青年的相互访问，认为这会加强两国人民的了解和友谊。
