Title: Indian President Holds State Banquet to Welcome Saudi Arabian King; Delhi Citizens Hold Grand Meeting to Welcome Saudi Arabian King
Author:
Date: 1955-11-30
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 28th: Indian President Prasad held a state banquet at the Presidential Palace on the evening of November 27th to welcome Saudi Arabian King Abdulaziz Saud.

　　Prasad delivered a welcome speech at the banquet. He said that the policy of neutrality and peaceful coexistence implemented by the Saudi King has had a welcome impact on the international situation, especially on the situation in the East. He said that India also pursues the same policy, so it is naturally very happy about the Saudi Arabian King's visit to India. He expressed his belief that the Saudi King's visit to India will further strengthen the long-standing friendly ties between the peoples of the two countries.

　　In his reply, the Saudi King said that our two countries' geographical locations, the similarity of our general positions, and our sincere desire to improve the living standards of our people make it necessary for us to cooperate so that we can contribute our part to the cause of building peace and friendship.

　　He said: "The Bandung Conference was held to establish peace and equality among all peoples, and the historic resolutions unanimously adopted at this conference opened a new era in the East. We hope and pray that this new era will bring to the people of these countries the freedom and progress they all yearn for."

　　Earlier that day, Indian Prime Minister Nehru paid a visit to the Saudi King. The President of the Indian Council for Cultural Relations, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad, held a reception to welcome the Saudi King.

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 29th: Delhi citizens held a grand meeting on November 28th in a large hall in the famous Red Fort to welcome Saudi Arabian King Abdulaziz Saud.

　　Indian Prime Minister Nehru spoke at the meeting, welcoming the Saudi King. He said, we are very pleased that Saudi Arabia and India share the same views on the most important issue facing the world today – the issue of peace. Nehru said that the city of Delhi has seen and heard the speeches of some of the world's greatest leaders of our time, and has heard their voices and their friendly and peaceful greetings. Delhi also reciprocates with friendly and peaceful greetings.

　　In his reply, the Saudi King said that Saudi Arabia and India both advocate promoting world peace, and both countries reaffirmed their faith in the Five Principles at the Bandung Conference. He said, I believe that the governments and peoples of Saudi Arabia and India can cooperate with each other to promote peace as a common goal.

　　Earlier that day, the Saudi King had held talks with Nehru.

### 印度总统举行国宴欢迎沙特阿拉伯国王　德里市民举行大会欢迎沙特阿拉伯国王

1955-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度总统举行国宴欢迎沙特阿拉伯国王
　　德里市民举行大会欢迎沙特阿拉伯国王
    新华社新德里二十八日电　印度总统普拉沙德十一月二十七日晚间在总统府举行国宴欢迎沙特阿拉伯国王阿齐兹·沙特。
    普拉沙德在宴会上致欢迎词。他说，沙特国王实行的中立和和平共处的政策，对于国际局势，特别是对东方的局势已经产生了令人欢迎的影响。他说，印度也奉行同样的政策，因此对于沙特阿拉伯国王到印度访问当然感到非常愉快。他表示相信，沙特国王的访问印度将进一步加强两国人民之间长久存在的友好联系。
    沙特国王在致答词时说，我们两国的地理位置，我们的一般地位的近似，以及我们提高我们人民的水平的真诚愿望都使我们有必要来进行合作，以便我们在建立和平和友好的事业中贡献我们的一部分力量。
    他说：“举行万隆会议是要建立和平和各国人民的平等，我们在这个会议中一致通过的历史性决议在东方开辟了一个新的时代，我们希望并且祈祷，这个新的时代将给这些国家的人民带来他们都切望得到的自由和进步。”
    当天较早的时候，印度总理尼赫鲁曾拜会沙特国王。印度对外文化协会主席毛拉纳·艾卜尔·卡拉姆·阿扎德曾举行招待会欢迎沙特国王。
    新华社新德里二十九日电　德里市民十一月二十八日在著名的红堡中的一个大殿举行大会，欢迎沙特阿拉伯国王阿齐兹·沙特。
    印度总理尼赫鲁在会上讲话表示欢迎沙特国王。他说，我们很高兴，沙特阿拉伯和印度对今天世界所面对的最重要问题——和平问题的看法是一致的。尼赫鲁说，德里市已经看到过和听到过我们时代几位世界最伟大的领袖的讲话，并且听过他们的声音和他们的友好和和平的问候。德里方面也报之以友好和和平的问候。
    沙特国王在致答词时说，沙特阿拉伯和印度同样主张促进世界和平，两国都在万隆会议上重申对于五项原则的信心。他说，我相信沙特阿拉伯和印度两国的政府和人民能够互相合作，促进作为共同的目标的和平。
    当天早些时候，沙特国王曾经同尼赫鲁举行了会谈。
