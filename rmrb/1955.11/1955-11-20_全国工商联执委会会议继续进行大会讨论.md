Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Executive Committee Meeting Continues Plenary Discussion
Author:
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The second day of the plenary discussion of the Second Meeting of the First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, was held on the 19th. Gong Tianmin, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and twenty-one executive committee members spoke at the meeting.
    The twenty-one members are: Sun Fuling, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, Jing Shuping, Liu Jingji, and Wei Ru, Standing Committee Members of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce, Zhang Aiguo, Member of the Culture and Education Committee of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce, Jian Rilin, Member of the Industrial Committee, Zhu Jisheng, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce, Chen Tiaofu, Executive Committee Member, Yu Zuochen, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Zhejiang Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Maimaitiniyazi Hare, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Federation of Industry and Commerce, Song Zongyu, Executive Committee Member of the Liaoning Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Wang Shaoyan, Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Han Wangchen, Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Xiang De, Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Miao Hainan, Chairman of the Shandong Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Pan E'zhang, Chairman of the Anhui Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Xiao Zek, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Sichuan Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Chen Shujing, Standing Committee Member of the Chongqing Federation of Industry and Commerce, Pan Shiyan, Chairman of the Jiangxi Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, Tong Shaosheng, Standing Committee Member of the Hubei Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Liang Huanwen, Executive Committee Member of the Guangxi Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce. Most of them are private shareholder representatives of joint state-private enterprises, as well as managers and private merchants of state-owned company consignment stores.
    Those who spoke today all mentioned that this meeting gave them a very profound education and strengthened their determination to accept socialist transformation. They cited many facts to vividly illustrate the transformation of their thinking in the six years since liberation, and expressed that they will continue to work hard to transform themselves in the future.
    Many committee members introduced the situation of socialist transformation of private industry and commerce and methods of overcoming difficulties in their regions to the meeting. Some reported on the achievements and experiences of their enterprises in implementing joint state-private ownership, and put forward many positive suggestions as well as the opinions and requirements of the industrial and commercial sectors.

### 全国工商联执委会会议继续进行大会讨论

1955-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国工商联执委会会议继续进行大会讨论
    新华社十九日讯　十九日，是中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会第二次会议进行大会讨论的第二天，在会上发言的有全国工商联副主任委员巩天民和二十一位执行委员。
    这二十一位委员是：北京市工商联副主任委员孙孚凌，上海市工商联常务委员经叔平、刘靖基、魏如，上海市工商联文教委员会委员张爱国、工业委员会委员简日林，天津市工商联副主任委员朱继圣、执行委员陈调甫，浙江省工商联筹委会副主任委员俞佐宸，新疆维吾尔自治区工商联主任委员买买提尼牙孜哈热，辽宁省工商联执行委员宋宗禹，云南省工商联副主任委员王少岩，陕西省工商联主任委员韩望尘，湖南省工商联主任委员向德，山东省工商联主任委员苗海南，安徽省工商联主任委员潘锷鏱，四川省工商联筹委会副主任委员萧则可，重庆市工商联常务委员陈叔敬，江西省工商联主任委员潘式言，湖北省工商联常务委员童少生，广西省工商联执行委员梁焕文。他们中绝大部分是公私合营企业的私股代表，也有国营公司代销店的经理和私商。
    今天发言的人都谈到这次会议给了他们以极深刻的教育，增强了他们接受社会主义改造的决心。他们列举了许多事实，生动地说明解放六年来自己思想的转变，表示今后要继续努力改造自己。
    不少委员向大会介绍了本地区私营工商业社会主义改造的情况和克服困难的办法。有的报告了本企业实行公私合营的成就和经验，并且提出了不少积极建议以及工商界的意见和要求。
