Title: National Volleyball League Begins in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 2

　　The National Volleyball League Begins in Beijing
    The 1955 National Volleyball League began on the 27th in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium.

    Participating in this league are men's and women's volleyball teams from four sports academies (Central-South, Northeast, Southwest, and East China), the China Railway Sports Association, and Chongqing City, as well as the men's volleyball team of the Guangzhou Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the men's volleyball team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Public Security Forces, the White team of the Central-South Sports Academy men's volleyball team, and the Jiangxi men's volleyball team.

    This league adopts a round-robin competition system, where each men's team will compete against the other nine men's teams, and each women's team will compete against the other five women's teams. The final rankings of each team will be determined based on the number of points scored.

    Seven matches have already been held on the morning, afternoon, and evening of the 27th.

    The volleyball league is scheduled to end on December 7th.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国排球联赛开始在京举行

1955-11-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国排球联赛开始在京举行
    一九五五年全国排球联赛二十七日开始在北京体育馆的比赛馆中举行。
    参加这次联赛的，有中南、东北、西南、华东四个体育学院、中国火车头体育协会和重庆市的男、女排球队，以及中国人民解放军驻广州部队男子排球队、中国人民解放军公安军男子排球队、中南体育学院男子排球队白队和江西男子排球队。
    这次联赛采取循环制的竞赛办法进行比赛，每个男子队都要同其他九个男子队进行比赛；每个女子队都要同其他五个女子队进行比赛。最后根据得分的多少来排定各队的名次。
    二十七日上午、下午和晚上已经举行了七场比赛。
    这次排球联赛预定在十二月七日结束。
                                  （新华社）
