Title: Nehru Thanks the Chinese Red Cross for Donating Funds to Help Indian Disaster Victims; Pham Van Dong Cables Thanks to the Chinese and Czech People for Relief to Vietnamese Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 9th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru wrote a letter to Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, on November 7th, expressing his gratitude to the Chinese Red Cross for donating funds to help the people in the flood-stricken areas of India.
	In his letter, Nehru wrote: "Thank you for your letter of November 7th, and the attached check for 95,785 rupees, which you sent on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross to relieve the flood victims in India. I would like you to convey my thanks to the Chinese Red Cross. I am grateful for this generous gift."
	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 9th: According to Vietnam News Agency, Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the 7th, on behalf of the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram thanking the government and people of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese Red Cross for donating cloths and medicines worth 200,000 yuan in RMB to help the Vietnamese people who suffered from the typhoon disaster.
	On the same day, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong sent a telegram thanking the government, people, and Red Cross of the Czechoslovak Republic for donating cloths, medicines, and milk worth 100,000 crowns to relieve the Vietnamese people affected by the typhoon.

### 尼赫鲁感谢我红十字会捐款援助印度灾民  范文同电谢中捷人民救济越南受灾人民

1955-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁感谢我红十字会捐款援助印度灾民
    范文同电谢中捷人民救济越南受灾人民
    新华社新德里九日电　印度总理尼赫鲁十一月七日写信给中国驻印度大使袁仲贤，对中国红十字会捐款援助印度水灾地区人民表示感谢。
    尼赫鲁在信中写道：“感谢你十一月七日的来信以及信内所附的你代表中国红十字会寄来的救济印度水灾灾民的九万五千七百八十五卢比的支票。我希望你向中国红十字会转致我的谢意，我感谢这一慷慨的赠与。”
    新华社河内九日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国总理范文同在七日代表越南民主共和国政府和人民，打电报感谢中华人民共和国政府、人民和中国红十字会捐赠价值人民币二十万元的布匹和药品援助遭受台风灾害的越南人民。
    范文同总理在同一天打电报感谢捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府、人民和红十字会捐赠价值十万克朗的布匹、药品和牛奶救济遭受风灾的越南人民。
