Title: Bulganin Holds Reception to Celebrate October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1955-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 8th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, held a reception in the Grand Kremlin Palace on November 7th to celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    From the Soviet side, the attendees included: Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Kirichenko, Malenkov, Molotov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Aristov, Pospelov, Shepilov, Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Ministers and Deputy Ministers of various ministries of the Soviet Union, Marshals and Generals of the Soviet Union, representatives of various social organizations, advanced producers, scientists, writers, actors, artists, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists.

    Also attending the reception were the ambassadors of Sweden, Burma, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Argentina, Mongolia, Albania, Yugoslavia, the United Kingdom, Greece, Mexico, Afghanistan, Canada, Bulgaria, Norway, Indonesia, Romania, Hungary, Turkey, the People's Republic of China, Israel, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Egypt, the German Democratic Republic, Belgium, the Netherlands, Denmark, and the envoys of Luxembourg, Ethiopia, Thailand, Iceland, and Lebanon, as well as the charge d'affaires ad interim of Iran, Pakistan, the United States, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Italy, France, Finland, Austria, India, and Switzerland, and the military attachés and staff of the land, sea, and air forces of various embassies.

    Guests attending the reception also included the heads of trade delegations from the German Democratic Republic, Burma, Norway, Finland, the Mongolian People's Republic, Romania, the People's Republic of China, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, France, Czechoslovakia, and other countries who are currently in the Soviet Union, as well as representatives of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

    A concert was held at the reception, with the participation of some famous Soviet artists.

### 布尔加宁为庆祝十月革命节举行招待会

1955-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁为庆祝十月革命节举行招待会
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁十一月七日在大克里姆林宫举行招待会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、基里钦科、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、阿里斯托夫、波斯伯洛夫、谢皮洛夫，苏联部长会议各位副主席，苏联各部部长和副部长，苏联元帅和将领，各社会团体的代表，先进生产者，科学家，作家，演员，艺术家以及苏联和外国的新闻记者。
    出席招待会的还有：瑞典、缅甸、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、阿根廷、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫、英国、希腊、墨西哥、阿富汗、加拿大、保加利亚、挪威、印度尼西亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、土耳其、中华人民共和国、以色列、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、埃及、德意志民主共和国、比利时、荷兰、丹麦等国的大使，卢森堡、埃塞俄比亚、泰国、冰岛、黎巴嫩等国的公使，伊朗、巴基斯坦、美国、越南民主共和国、意大利、法国、芬兰、奥地利、印度、瑞士等国的临时代办，各国使馆的陆海空军武官和工作人员。
    出席招待会的客人还有现在苏联的德意志民主共和国、缅甸、挪威、芬兰、蒙古人民共和国、罗马尼亚、中华人民共和国、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰、法国、捷克斯洛伐克等国家的贸易代表团团长以及国际民主妇女联合会代表等。
    招待会上举行了音乐会，苏联的一些著名艺术家参加演出。
