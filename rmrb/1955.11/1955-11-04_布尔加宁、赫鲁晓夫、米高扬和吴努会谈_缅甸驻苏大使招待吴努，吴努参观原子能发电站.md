Title: Bulganin, Khrushchev, Mikoyan and U Nu Hold Talks; Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Hosts U Nu; U Nu Visits Atomic Power Station
Author:
Date: 1955-11-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd, Moscow Dispatch: According to TASS, on November 2nd, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Bulganin, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Khrushchev, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Mikoyan, held talks with Prime Minister of the Union of Burma U Nu in the Kremlin.

The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Participating in the talks from the Burmese side were: Minister of Supply of the Union of Burma Bo Min Gaung, Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union U Want, Burmese Ambassador to the People's Republic of China U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to the United Kingdom U Kin, etc. Participating in the talks from the Soviet side were: Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Zorin and Fedrin, Soviet Ambassador to Burma Siborin.

On the same day, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Saburov received U Nu.

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd, Moscow Dispatch: TASS reports from Moscow: On November 2nd, the Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, U Want, held a reception in honor of the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, Daw Mya Yee, who are currently visiting the Soviet Union.

Those present at the reception included Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, Daw Mya Yee, members of the Prime Minister's entourage: Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to the United Kingdom U Kin and his wife, Daw Khin, Prime Minister’s Secretary U Thant, Colonel Aung Gyi, U Hla, and U Lei.

Those present from the Soviet side included Bulganin, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Aristov, Shepilov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Kosygin, Tevosyan, Khrunichev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Tarasov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, and ministers of various Soviet ministries.

Also present at the reception were members of the Burmese Federal Government Purchasing Delegation, diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, etc.

The reception took place in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd, Moscow Dispatch: TASS reports from Moscow: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his entourage visited the Atomic Power Station of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the 2nd.

The guests first watched a documentary film about the Atomic Power Station of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, entitled "The First in the World." Subsequently, they went to the hall where the atomic reactor was installed, and visited the central control panel, steam generator, X-ray intensity laboratory, and pumping equipment. During their visit, Soviet scientist Professor Blokhintsev explained the operating conditions of these machines and answered many questions about the power station raised by Prime Minister U Nu.

### 布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、米高扬和吴努会谈  缅甸驻苏大使招待吴努，吴努参观原子能发电站

1955-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、米高扬和吴努会谈
    缅甸驻苏大使招待吴努，吴努参观原子能发电站
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十一月二日，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，在克里姆林宫同缅甸联邦总理吴努进行了会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    缅甸方面参加会谈的有：缅甸联邦供应部部长波敏贡，缅甸驻苏联大使吴旺，缅甸驻中华人民共和国大使吴拉茂，缅甸驻英国大使吴钦等。苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联外交部副部长佐林和费德林，苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林。
    同一天，苏联部长会议第一副主席萨布罗夫接见了吴努。
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：十一月二日，缅甸联邦驻苏联大使吴旺举行招待会，招待现在苏联访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人都妙意。
    出席招待会的有吴努总理和他的夫人都妙意，总理的随行人员：缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、缅甸驻英国大使吴钦和他的夫人钦、总理秘书吴丹、上校昂季、吴拉和吴累。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有布尔加宁、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、阿里斯托夫、谢皮洛夫，苏联部长会议副主席科西金、捷沃西安、赫鲁尼切夫，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫、俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫和苏联各部部长等。
    出席招待会的还有缅甸联邦政府采购代表团人员，各国驻苏联的外交使节等。
    招待会在友好融洽的气氛中进行。
    据新华社三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的随行人员在二日参观了苏联科学院的原子能发电站。
    客人们首先看了关于苏联科学院原子能发电站的纪录影片——“世界上第一个”。随后，他们到安置原子反应堆的大厅，参观了中央操纵台、蒸汽发电机、爱克斯光强度实验室和抽水设备。在他们参观时，苏联科学家布洛欣泽夫教授从旁讲解了这些机器开动时的情况，并且回答了吴努总理提出的许多有关这个发电站的问题。
