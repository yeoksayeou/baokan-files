Title: Japanese Socialist Party Decides to Expand the Struggle Against Military Bases; Demands Fundamental Revision of Unequal Treaties such as the US-Japan "Administrative Agreement"
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: The Military Base Committee of the Japanese Socialist Party held a meeting in Tokyo on the 9th and decided to develop the current struggle of people in various parts of Japan against US military bases into a national movement for fundamental revision of unequal treaties such as the US-Japan "Administrative Agreement." The meeting pointed out that the struggles of the people in Sunagawa Town and other areas against the bases are major issues related to Japan's independence.
　　The meeting decided to resolutely support the residents of Sunagawa Town in opposing the expansion of the US military base and to pursue the government's suppression policies implemented to expand the US military base in the Diet session.

### 日本社会党决定扩大反对军事基地的斗争　要求根本修改美日“行政协定”等不平等条约

1955-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本社会党决定扩大反对军事基地的斗争
　　要求根本修改美日“行政协定”等不平等条约
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本社会党军事基地委员会九日在东京举行会议，决定要把目前日本各地人民反对美国军事基地的斗争，发展成为根本修改美日“行政协定”等不平等条约的国民运动。会议指出，目前砂川町及其他地区人民反对基地的斗争，都是关系到日本独立的大问题。
    会议决定坚决支持砂川町居民反对扩大美军基地，并且要在国会会议中追究政府为了扩大美军基地而实行的镇压政策。
