This year's cotton quality is better than last year's. This is the staff of the Nantong Inspection Station of the Jiangsu Provincial Textile Fiber Inspection Bureau inspecting the moisture and impurities of cotton. The inspection proves that it is generally high-quality fine-staple cotton. Photo by Xu Bihua (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)
