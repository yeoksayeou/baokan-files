Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Saburo Endo
Author:
Date: 1955-11-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Saburo Endo, a member of the Japanese "National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution" delegation and former Lieutenant General of the Army, on the morning of the 16th.

### 陈毅副总理接见远藤三郎

1955-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见远藤三郎
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在十六日上午接见了日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团团员、前陆军中将远藤三郎。
