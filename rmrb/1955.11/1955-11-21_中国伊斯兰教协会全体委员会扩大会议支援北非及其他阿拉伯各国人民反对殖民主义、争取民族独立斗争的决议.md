Title: Resolution of the Enlarged Plenary Session of the China Islamic Association Supporting the People of North Africa and Other Arab Countries in Their Struggle Against Colonialism and for National Independence
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-21
Page: 1

　　Resolution of the Enlarged Plenary Session of the China Islamic Association Supporting the People of North Africa and Other Arab Countries in Their Struggle Against Colonialism and for National Independence

　　As the China Islamic Association convenes its Second Enlarged Plenary Session of the First Committee, we, the Ahongs, Mullahs, and village elders from ten Islamic ethnic groups across the country, noting the situation in North Africa and the Near and Middle East, and in accordance with the Islamic doctrines of seeking human equality, peace, and freedom, unanimously pass a resolution condemning the bloody suppression and barbaric rule of the colonialists over the Arab people of North Africa. The ten million Muslims in China express sympathy for and support the just struggle of the people of North Africa and other Arab countries against colonialism and for the achievement of national independence.

### 中国伊斯兰教协会全体委员会扩大会议支援北非及其他阿拉伯各国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立斗争的决议

1955-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国伊斯兰教协会全体委员会扩大会议支援北非及其他阿拉伯各国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立斗争的决议
    值此中国伊斯兰教协会召开第一届全体委员会第二次扩大会议之际，我们来自全国各地的属于十个信仰伊斯兰教民族的阿訇、毛拉和乡老注意到北非和中近东地区的局势，本着伊斯兰教谋求人类平等、和平和自由的教义，一致通过决议，谴责殖民主义者对北非阿拉伯人民的血腥镇压和野蛮统治。中国一千万伊斯兰教徒对北非和其他阿拉伯各国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立的正义斗争，表示同情和支持。
