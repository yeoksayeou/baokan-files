Title: Danish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 4

　  Danish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency: An eighteen-member Danish trade delegation, led by head of delegation Axel Grunn, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th.  Those welcoming them at the airport included Lu Xuzhang, a member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Deputy Secretary-General Xiao Fangzhou, Tian Suixiang, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and responsible persons from China's specialized import and export corporations.
    The secretary of the Danish Embassy in China also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 丹麦贸易代表团到京

1955-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  丹麦贸易代表团到京
    新华社讯　丹麦贸易代表团一行十八人，由团长阿克塞尔·格鲁恩率领在二十五日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会委员卢绪章、副秘书长萧方洲，对外贸易部交际处副处长田绥祥和我国进出口各专业公司的负责人。
    丹麦驻华公使馆的秘书也前往机场欢迎。
