Title: Czechoslovakia to Help India Build a Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 11th: The head of the Czechoslovak trade delegation currently visiting India, Minister of Foreign Trade Richard Dvořák, met with Indian Minister of Steel, Krishna Machari, on the 9th. According to Indian newspapers, Czechoslovakia has expressed its willingness to help India build a steel plant with an annual production capacity of one million tons of steel and is prepared to provide long-term credit of 1 billion rupees.
　　President of the Czechoslovak Chamber of Commerce, Adamec, said at a press conference in New Delhi that when exporting machinery and industrial equipment, Czechoslovak trade organizations are prepared to provide technical assistance to India by sending experts to India, training experts for India in Czechoslovakia, and sharing patent rights.
　　Meanwhile, a Hungarian trade delegation headed by Ferenc Biro, First Deputy Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Manufacturing Industry, is negotiating with representatives of various Indian government departments in Delhi. According to the "Indian Express," Biro said at a press conference on the 9th that they discussed expanding economic, technical, and scientific ties between the two countries and providing technical assistance to India.

### 捷克斯洛伐克将帮助印度兴建一个钢铁厂

1955-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克斯洛伐克将帮助印度兴建一个钢铁厂
    新华社新德里十一日电　正在印度访问的捷克斯洛伐克贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部部长理·德沃夏克在九日会见了印度钢铁部部长克里希纳马查里。据印度报纸报道，捷克斯洛伐克已经表示愿意帮助印度兴建一个每年能生产一百万吨钢的钢铁工厂，并且准备提供十亿卢比的长期信用贷款。
    捷克斯洛伐克商会主席阿达麦克在新德里的一次记者招待会上说，捷克斯洛伐克贸易机构在输出机器和工业装备时，准备通过派遣专家前往印度、在捷克斯洛伐克为印度训练专家、共享专利权等办法来给印度提供技术援助。
    同时，以冶金和机器制造工业部第一副部长费伦茨·比罗为首的匈牙利贸易代表团正在德里和印度政府各部的代表进行谈判。据“印度快报”说，比罗九日在一次记者招待会上说，他们讨论了两国扩大经济、技术和科学联系以及给予印度以技术援助的问题。
