Title: Bought a Batch of Unnecessary Precision Instruments
Author: Xiang Qun
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 6

To the Editor:

　　During the comprehensive production reform in February this year, the Fuxin Mining Bureau's Geological Survey Department used over 7,000 yuan of funds allocated for the comprehensive production reform to blindly purchase a batch of precision instruments. Since these instruments were bought, they have not been used until now. The reason is that geological work has not yet been carried out, and these instruments are simply not needed; at the same time, the existing staff do not know how to use them. For example, one "polarizing microscope" is worth more than 4,500 yuan, but even the engineer, Tong Jingzong, who advocated buying this instrument, does not know how to use it.

　　The behavior of the Geological Survey Department's leadership in recklessly spending national funds should be criticized.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xiang Qun

### 买了一批用不着的精密仪器
向群
1955-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

　  买了一批用不着的精密仪器编辑同志：
    今年二月间，阜新矿务局地质测量处趁着全面生产改革的机会，用了七千多元的全面生产改革的经费盲目地买了一批精密仪器。这批仪器买来以后，一直到现在还没有使用过。原因是地质工作还没有开展，这批仪器根本用不上；同时现有的工作人员也不会使用。例如其中一架“偏光显微镜”价值是四千五百多元，但是连主张买这架仪器的佟敬宗工程师都不会使用。
    地质测量处领导工作人员这种乱花国家资金的行为，应该受到批评。
                                        向群
