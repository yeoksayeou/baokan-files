Author:
Date: 1955-11-06
Page: 1
Advancing on the Socialist Road

    Jiuquan Basin Completes Exploration Task This Year
    The field exploration personnel of the Jiuquan Basin Petroleum Geological Exploration Team have overfulfilled this year's geological exploration task.
    Dozens of various types of petroleum geological exploration teams in the Jiuquan Basin went to the Gobi Desert and sparsely populated areas in April this year to conduct general surveys, detailed surveys, and detailed measurement work, discovering new potential oil-bearing structures, some of which show great promise for oil storage. Through this year's exploration, the geological exploration personnel have accumulated valuable experience for finding more potential structures in the Jiuquan Basin in the future. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 酒泉盆地今年勘探任务完成

1955-11-06
第1版()
专栏：在社会主义的道路上前进

    酒泉盆地今年勘探任务完成
    酒泉盆地石油地质勘探队的野外勘探人员已超额完成今年的地质勘探任务。
    酒泉盆地的几十个各种类型的石油地质勘探队，在今年四月间就到戈壁滩上和人烟很少的地区进行普查、详查和细测工作，发现了新的石油潜伏构造，有的构造储油希望很大。经过今年的勘探，地质勘探人员为今后在酒泉盆地寻找更多的潜伏构造积累了宝贵的经验。　（据新华社讯）
