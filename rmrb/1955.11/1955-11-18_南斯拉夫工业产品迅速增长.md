Title: Rapid Growth of Yugoslav Industrial Products
Author:
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th -- According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Belgrade: At present, Yugoslavia's steel production, calculated per capita, has increased from an average of 21 kilograms per person before the war to 76 kilograms. Electricity has increased from 71 degrees to 205 degrees, coal from 220 kilograms to 390 kilograms, petroleum products from 13 kilograms to 58 kilograms, and cement from 30 kilograms to 90 kilograms. This was mentioned in a report read at the Fourth Congress of the Federation of Engineers and Technicians of Yugoslavia on November 13.
　　The report also pointed out that Yugoslavia has built more than 2,000 kilometers of railways after the war, completed more than 30 large metal processing enterprises, and more than 240,000 residential units. The production of chemical fertilizers has greatly increased, and the average amount of chemical fertilizer used per hectare of land has increased from 4.5 kilograms before the war to 40 kilograms.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th -- According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: The Boris Kidrič Institute of Nuclear Energy near Belgrade, the capital of Yugoslavia, has completed preparations for manufacturing its first experimental reactor.
　　This reactor will be used for experiments and for manufacturing industrial isotopes.

### 南斯拉夫工业产品迅速增长

1955-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫工业产品迅速增长
    新华社布拉格十四日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德讯：目前，南斯拉夫钢的生产量，按人口平均计算，已经由战前每人平均二十一公斤增加到七十六公斤，电力由七十一度增加到二百零五度，煤由二百二十公斤增加到三百九十公斤，石油产品由十三公斤增加到五十八公斤，水泥由三十公斤增加到九十公斤，这是十一月十三日在南斯拉夫工程师技师联合会第四次代表大会上宣读的报告中讲到的。
    报告还指出，南斯拉夫在战后修建了二千多公里长的铁路、建成了三十多个大型的金属加工企业和二十四万多套住宅。化学肥料的生产大大增加了，平均每公顷土地使用的化学肥料已经由战前的四公斤半增加到四十公斤。
    新华社布拉格十六日电据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：南斯拉夫首都贝尔格莱德附近的鲍里斯·基德里奇核子能研究所，已经完成了制造本国第一个实验性反应堆的准备工作。
    这个反应堆将作实验用和制造工业用的同位素。
