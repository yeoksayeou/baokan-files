Title: Memorial Ceremony for the Renowned Traditional Chinese Medicine Doctor Kong Bóhuá Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 27th. Mr. Kong Bóhuá, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and renowned traditional Chinese medicine doctor, passed away in Beijing on the morning of the 23rd due to illness, at the age of 71.

	On the morning of the 27th, the funeral committee for Mr. Kong Bóhuá held a public memorial ceremony at Jiaxing Temple in Beijing. Among those attending the memorial ceremony were Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xing Xiping, Secretary-General; Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin, Wu Han, Lu Han, Chen Qiyou, and some members of the committee in Beijing; Fu Lianzhang, Vice Minister of Health; Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; medical professionals Shi Jinmo, Yuan Hechai, and over two hundred friends of Mr. Kong Bóhuá.

	In the afternoon of the same day, Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Premier of the State Council, personally visited Mr. Kong Bóhuá's residence to express condolences.

### 著名中医孔伯华先生公祭在京举行

1955-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　著名中医孔伯华先生公祭在京举行
    新华社二十七日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、著名中医孔伯华先生因病于二十三日上午在北京逝世，享年七十一岁。
    二十七日上午，孔伯华先生治丧委员会在北京嘉兴寺举行公祭。参加公祭的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席彭真、秘书长邢西萍、常务委员马叙伦、彭泽民、吴晗、卢汉、陈其尤和部分在京委员，卫生部副部长傅连暲，北京市副市长王昆仑，医务界人士施今墨、袁鹤侪以及孔伯华先生生前好友二百余人。
    同日下午，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席、国务院总理周恩来亲往孔伯华先生寓所吊唁。
