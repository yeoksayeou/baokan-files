Date: 1955-11-16

　　Communiqué of the First Session of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee
　　The first session of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee recently concluded in Beijing. In a spirit of friendly cooperation, both sides adopted the Statute of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, discussed the projects for scientific and technological cooperation and exchange between the two sides, and signed the Protocol of the First Session of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee on November 8.
　　According to the signed protocol, the People's Republic of China will provide the People's Republic of Bulgaria with crop seeds and cultivation methods, fruit tree cuttings, medicinal plant seeds and research data, ramie, silk, tobacco processing methods, manufacturing methods of refined porcelain and penicillin, etc. The People's Republic of China will receive Bulgarian experts to investigate issues such as cotton, rice, and medicinal plants.
　　The People's Republic of Bulgaria will provide the People's Republic of China with seeds of crops, medicinal plants, etc., as well as information on crop seed stimulants, cotton cultivation and processing, sugar beet and hemp cultivation, prevention and control of the most contagious diseases, crop quarantine measures, and quarantine measures for seaports and international aviation. The People's Republic of Bulgaria will receive Chinese experts to investigate the processing of tobacco, fruits, and vegetables.
　　During the first session, the Bulgarian delegation visited agricultural production cooperatives, state farms, heavy industry, light industry, and food industry enterprises in Northeast and East China, as well as agricultural, health, and other research institutes and scientific research institutions.

### 中保科学技术合作委员会第一届会议公报

1955-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中保科学技术合作委员会第一届会议公报
    中保科学技术合作委员会第一届会议最近已在北京闭幕。双方在友好合作的精神下，通过了科学技术合作委员会章程，讨论了双方科学技术合作交换的项目，并于十一月八日签订了中保科学技术合作委员会第一届会议议定书。
    根据所签订的议定书，中华人民共和国将供给保加利亚人民共和国农作物种籽和栽培方法，果树的插条，药用植物种子和研究资料，苎麻、蚕丝、烟草加工方法，精制瓷器和青霉素的制造方法等。中华人民共和国将接受保加利亚专家考察棉花、稻和药用植物等问题。
    保加利亚人民共和国将供给中华人民共和国以农作物、药用植物等种籽，以及关于农作物种籽刺激剂，棉花栽培和加工，甜菜和大麻栽培，防治最易传染病，农作物检疫措施和海港、国际航空检疫措施等资料。保加利亚人民共和国将接受中国专家考察烟草、水果和蔬菜的加工。
    保加利亚代表团在第一届会议期间，曾在东北、华东等地参观了农业生产合作社，国营农场，重工业、轻工业和食品工业等企业，以及农业、卫生等研究院和科学研究所。
