Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received the entire Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Education Delegation on the afternoon of December 12th, headed by the delegation leader and Chairman of the Japanese Teachers and Staff Union, Takeshi Kobayashi.

Present at the meeting was Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China.

### 郭沫若接见日本教职员工会教育考察团

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若接见日本教职员工会教育考察团
    新华社十二日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若十二日下午接见了以团长、日本教职员工会主席小林武为首的日本教职员工会教育考察团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明。
