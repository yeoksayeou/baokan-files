Title: Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory Produces New Drilling Machine
Author: Li Wenlu
Date: 1955-11-17
Page: 2

Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory Produces New Drilling Machine

The YKC drilling machine, successfully trial-produced by Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory, has begun formal production.

The YKC drilling machine is a new type of Soviet drilling equipment. This type of drilling machine can drill holes up to 500 mm in diameter and 300 meters deep. This machine has a wide range of uses and can be used to explore strata and water volume, and is widely applicable to projects such as the mining of open-pit coal mines and the excavation of tap water channels.

(Li Wenlu)

### 太原矿山机器厂生产新打眼机
李文禄
1955-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　太原矿山机器厂生产新打眼机
    太原矿山机器厂试制成功的YKC打眼机已经开始正式生产。
    YKC打眼机是苏联新式打眼机器设备。这种打眼机打眼口径可达五百公厘，深三百公尺。这种机器的用途很广，可用来勘察地层和水量，并普遍适用于开采露天煤矿和开凿自来水道等工程。
                                    （李文禄）
