Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting; Approves Draft Model Regulations for Agricultural Cooperatives and Decides that the Draft Regulations May Be Used on a Trial Basis by Agricultural Cooperatives in Various Regions
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its twenty-fourth meeting on November 9th.

The meeting heard the explanation by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, discussed and approved the "Draft Model Regulations for Agricultural Production Cooperatives" proposed by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the State Council, and after being passed by the plenary meeting of the State Council, submitted to the Standing Committee for deliberation. The meeting resolved to have this draft issued by the State Council to people's committees at and above the county level for discussion and solicitation of public opinion. The meeting also resolved that agricultural production cooperatives in various regions may use this draft as their own regulations on a trial basis.

Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the Draft Model Regulations for Agricultural Production Cooperatives

(Adopted at the Twenty-Fourth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 9, 1955)

On November 9, 1955, the Twenty-Fourth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress discussed the draft model regulations for agricultural production cooperatives proposed by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, and resolved to have this draft issued by the State Council to people's committees at and above the county level for discussion and solicitation of public opinion, in order to further revise it and submit it to the next meeting of the National People's Congress for discussion and approval. During the period of soliciting opinions, agricultural production cooperatives in various regions may use this draft as their own regulations on a trial basis, and according to their own needs and circumstances, make supplementary provisions for matters not stipulated or not specifically stipulated in these regulations.

Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Whether Members of Local People's Committees at All Levels Are Limited to Deputies of the People's Congress at the Same Level

(Adopted at the Twenty-Third Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 8, 1955)

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its Twenty-Third Meeting on November 8, 1955, discussed the issue raised by the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee and the former Xinjiang Provincial People's Government regarding whether the members of local people's committees at all levels are limited to deputies of the people's congress at the same level, and decided that the governors, chairmen of autonomous regions, mayors, heads of autonomous prefectures, county heads, and district heads of local people's committees at all levels should be elected from among the deputies of the people's congress at the same level; vice-governors, vice-chairmen of autonomous regions, vice-mayors, vice-heads of autonomous prefectures, vice-county heads, vice-district heads, and members should also generally be elected from among the deputies of the people's congress at the same level, but in order to meet the needs of work, a few may not be deputies of the people's congress at the same level; members of people's committees of townships and towns should all be elected from among the deputies of the people's congress at the same level.

Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the Issue of Filling Vacancies of Governors, Chairmen of Autonomous Regions, Mayors, Heads of Autonomous Prefectures, County Heads, District Heads, Township Heads, Town Heads, and Presidents of Local People's Courts at All Levels During the Recess of Local People's Congresses at All Levels

(Adopted at the Twenty-Third Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 8, 1955)

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its Twenty-Third Meeting on November 8, 1955, discussed the issue raised by the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee regarding how to fill vacancies when governors, mayors, county heads, district heads, township heads, town heads, and presidents of local people's courts at all levels are unable to perform their duties due to unforeseen circumstances during the recess of local people's congresses at all levels, and whether they can be appointed by higher authorities, and decided that:

During the recess of local people's congresses at all levels, when governors, chairmen of autonomous regions, mayors, heads of autonomous prefectures, county heads, district heads, township heads, and town heads are unable to perform their duties due to unforeseen circumstances, one person should be nominated by the people's committee at the same level from among the vice-governors, vice-chairmen of autonomous regions, vice-mayors, vice-heads of autonomous prefectures, vice-county heads, vice-district heads, vice-township heads, and vice-town heads and reported to the higher-level state administrative organ for approval to temporarily act in their capacity, and a supplementary election should be held when the people's congress at the same level holds its next meeting.

Local people's committees at all levels simultaneously exercise the functions of the standing organs of the people's congresses at the same level. Therefore, during the recess of local people's congresses at all levels, when presidents of higher people's courts and basic people's courts are unable to perform their duties due to unforeseen circumstances, one person should be designated by the people's committee at the same level from among the vice-presidents of the people's court at the same level to temporarily act as president, and a supplementary election should be held when the people's congress at the same level holds its next meeting; when presidents of intermediate people's courts are unable to perform their duties due to unforeseen circumstances, one person should be designated by the people's committees of the provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, larger cities, and autonomous prefectures respectively from among the vice-presidents of the intermediate people's court to temporarily act as president, and a supplementary election should be held when the people's congress of the provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, larger cities, and autonomous prefectures holds its next meeting.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  通过农业合作社示范章程草案  并决定章程草案可由各地农业合作社试用

1955-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    通过农业合作社示范章程草案
    并决定章程草案可由各地农业合作社试用
    新华社九日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十一月九日举行第二十四次会议。
    会议听了农业部部长廖鲁言的说明，讨论和通过了中国共产党中央委员会向国务院提出的并且经过国务院全体会议通过然后提请常务委员会审议的“农业生产合作社示范章程草案”。会议决议将这一草案交由国务院发给县以上各级人民委员会讨论和征求人民意见。会议还决议：各地农业生产合作社可以把这一草案作为自己的社章试用。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于农业生产合作社示范章程草案的决议
    （一九五五年十一月九日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十四次会议通过）
    一九五五年十一月九日，全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十四次会议讨论了国务院周恩来总理提出的农业生产合作社示范章程草案，决议将这一草案交由国务院发给县以上各级人民委员会讨论和征求人民意见，以便再加修改，提请全国人民代表大会下一次会议讨论通过。在征求意见期间，各地农业生产合作社可以把这一草案作为自己的社章试用，并且按照自己的需要和情况，对于本章程没有规定和没有具体规定的事情，作出补充的规定。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于地方各级人民委员会的组成人员是否限于本级人民代表大会代表问题的决定
    （一九五五年十一月八日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十三次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五五年十一月八日第二十三次会议讨论了广东省人民委员会、前新疆省人民政府提出的关于地方各级人民委员会的组成人员是否限于本级人民代表大会代表的问题，决定：地方各级人民委员会的省长、自治区主席、市长、州长、县长、区长应该从本级人民代表大会的代表中选出；副省长、自治区副主席、副市长、副州长、副县长、副区长和委员一般地也应该从本级人民代表大会的代表中选出，为了适应工作的需要，个别的也可以不是本级人民代表大会的代表；乡、镇人民委员会的成员应该全部从本级人民代表大会代表中选出。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于在地方各级人民代表大会闭会期间省长自治区主席市长州长县长区长乡长镇长和地方各级人民法院院长缺额补充问题的决定
    （一九五五年十一月八日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十三次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五五年十一月八日第二十三次会议讨论了黑龙江省人民委员会提出的关于在地方各级人民代表大会闭会期间，省长、市长、县长、区长、乡长、镇长和地方各级人民法院院长因故不能担任职务的时候，其缺额如何补充，是否可由上级委派的问题，决定：
    在地方各级人民代表大会闭会期间，省长、自治区主席、市长、州长、县长、区长、乡长、镇长因故不能担任职务的时候，应该由本级人民委员会在副省长、自治区副主席、副市长、副州长、副县长、副区长、副乡长、副镇长中推举一人报请上一级国家行政机关批准暂时代理其职务，等到本级人民代表大会举行下一次会议的时候补选。
    地方各级人民委员会是同时行使本级人民代表大会常务机关的职权的，因此，在地方各级人民代表大会闭会期间，高级人民法院和基层人民法院的院长因故不能担任职务的时候，应该由本级人民委员会在本级人民法院副院长中指定一人暂时代理院长的职务，等到本级人民代表大会举行下一次会议的时候补选；中级人民法院院长因故不能担任职务的时候，应该分别由省、自治区、直辖市、较大的市、自治州的人民委员会在中级人民法院副院长中指定一人暂时代理院长的职务，等到省、自治区、直辖市、较大的市、自治州的人民代表大会举行下一次会议的时候补选。
