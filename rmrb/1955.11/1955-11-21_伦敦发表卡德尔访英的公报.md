Title: London Publishes Communiqué on Kardelj's Visit to Britain
Author:
Date: 1955-11-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, November 19th: TASS, London, November 18th, reported that a communiqué regarding the visit of Kardelj, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, to Britain was published in London on the 18th. After arriving in Britain on the 14th, Kardelj held talks with the British Prime Minister, Foreign Secretary, and Chancellor of the Exchequer.
    The communiqué stated that during the talks, "a large degree of agreement was reached" on many common issues, and both sides "made the same assessment on the issues of maintaining the independence of sovereign states, easing international tensions, ensuring lasting peace, and genuine cooperation between countries."

### 伦敦发表卡德尔访英的公报

1955-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　伦敦发表卡德尔访英的公报
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社伦敦十九日讯：十八日在伦敦发表了关于南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔访问英国的公报。卡德尔在十四日到达英国后，同英国首相、外交大臣和财政大臣举行了谈判。
    公报说，在谈判期间在许多共同的问题上
  “取得了很大程度的一致意见”，双方“对维护主权国家的独立、缓和国际紧张局势、保障持久和平和各国之间的真正合作这些问题作了同样的估价”。
