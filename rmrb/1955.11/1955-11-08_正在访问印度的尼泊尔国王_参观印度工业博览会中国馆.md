Title: Nepalese King Visiting India Visits the Chinese Pavilion at the Indian Industries Fair
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 7th. The King and Queen of Nepal, who are currently visiting India, visited the Chinese Pavilion at the Indian Industries Fair on the 7th. Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India and concurrently Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, along with Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation to the Indian Industries Fair, received and guided the King and Queen of Nepal on their visit. Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, the Nepalese Ambassador to India, also accompanied the King of Nepal on his visit.

The King and Queen of Nepal visited every section of the Chinese Pavilion and expressed great interest in the exhibited industrial products, textile machinery, and hydraulic models.

Exhibition Delegation Head Ma Yimin presented gifts to the King and Queen of Nepal.

### 正在访问印度的尼泊尔国王  参观印度工业博览会中国馆

1955-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    正在访问印度的尼泊尔国王
    参观印度工业博览会中国馆
    新华社新德里七日电　现在正在访问印度的尼泊尔国王和王后七日参观印度工业博览会中国馆。中华人民共和国驻印度大使兼驻尼泊尔大使袁仲贤和夫人以及中国参加印度工业博览会展览团团长马一民接待和引导尼泊尔国王和王后参观。陪同尼泊尔国王参观的还有尼泊尔驻印度大使达曼·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳。
    尼泊尔国王和王后参观了中国馆的每一部分，对展出的工业产品、纺织机器和水利模型感到很大的兴趣。
    展览团团长马一民向尼泊尔国王和王后赠送了礼品。
