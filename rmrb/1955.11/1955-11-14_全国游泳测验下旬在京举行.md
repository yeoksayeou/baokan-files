Title: National Swimming Test to be Held in Beijing in Late November
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 2

　　National Swimming Test to be Held in Beijing in Late November

　　The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China has decided to hold the "1955 National Swimming Test" in Beijing in late November. This test will be held in the swimming pool of the newly built Beijing Gymnasium.

　　Participating in this test will be outstanding male and female swimmers from units such as the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Central Institute of Physical Education, the Central South Institute of Physical Education, the East China Institute of Physical Education, the Southwest Institute of Physical Education, and provinces and cities such as Fujian, Sichuan, Guangxi, Beijing, Tianjin, and Guangzhou.

　　The events in this test include freestyle, breaststroke, butterfly, backstroke, and relay races of various distances. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国游泳测验下旬在京举行

1955-11-14
第2版()
专栏：

　  全国游泳测验下旬在京举行
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会决定十一月下旬在北京举行“一九五五年全国游泳测验”。这次测验将在新建成的北京体育馆的游泳馆中举行。
    参加这次测验的将有中国人民解放军、中央体育学院、中南体育学院、华东体育学院、西南体育学院等单位和福建、四川、广西、北京、天津、广州等省市的优秀的男女游泳运动员。
    这次测验的项目包括各种距离的自由泳、蛙泳、蝶泳、仰泳和接力赛。　（新华社）
