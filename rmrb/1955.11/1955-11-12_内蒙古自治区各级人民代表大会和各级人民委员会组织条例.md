Organization Regulations of People's Congresses and People's Committees at All Levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
(Approved at the 27th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 11, 1955)

Chapter I General Principles

Article 1 These Organization Regulations of People's Congresses and People's Committees at All Levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are formulated in accordance with Section 5 of Chapter Two of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.
Article 2 The following levels of People's Congresses and People's Committees are established in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:
	(1) The People's Congress and People's Committee of the Autonomous Region;
	(2) The People's Congresses and People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region;
	(3) The People's Congresses and People's Committees of Banners, Counties, Cities, and Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region;
	(4) The People's Congresses and People's Committees of Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns.
Article 3 The People's Congresses and People's Committees of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are the organs of self-government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and are local state organs.
Article 4 The People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region uniformly implement democratic centralism.
Article 5 The organization and work of the organs of self-government in other ethnic autonomous areas within the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall be governed by the provisions of Section 5 of Chapter Two of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

Chapter II People's Congresses at All Levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region

Article 6 The People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are all local state power organs.
Article 7 Representatives to the People's Congresses of the Autonomous Region, Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, and Counties are elected by the People's Congresses at the next lower level; representatives to the People's Congresses of Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns are directly elected by the voters.
	The number of representatives to the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the methods for their election shall be in accordance with the provisions of the Election Law.
	In the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, each ethnic group shall have an appropriate number of representatives.
Article 8 The term of office of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Region is four years per term. The term of office of the People's Congresses of Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns is two years per term.
Article 9 The People's Congress of the Autonomous Region shall exercise the following functions and powers within the Autonomous Region:
	(1) To ensure the observance and enforcement of laws, decrees, and resolutions of the National People's Congress;
	(2) To formulate autonomous regulations and specific regulations in accordance with the powers prescribed by the Constitution and in accordance with the characteristics of the Autonomous Region, and submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval;
	(3) To adopt and issue resolutions within its scope of authority;
	(4) To plan economic construction, cultural construction, public services, preferential treatment work, and relief work;
	(5) To review and approve the budget and final accounts in accordance with the Autonomous Region's financial authority prescribed by law;
	(6) To decide on the organization of the Autonomous Region's public security forces in accordance with the state's military system;
	(7) To elect the members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region;
	(8) To elect the President of the Higher People's Court of the Autonomous Region;
	(9) To elect representatives to the National People's Congress;
	(10) To hear and review the work reports of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region and the Higher People's Court of the Autonomous Region;
	(11) To change or revoke inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region;
	(12) To change or revoke inappropriate resolutions of the People's Congresses of Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, and inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region;
	(13) To protect public property, maintain public order, and guarantee citizens' rights;
	(14) To guarantee the equal rights of all ethnic groups.
Article 10 The People's Congresses at and above the Banner and County levels shall exercise the following functions and powers within their respective administrative regions:
	(1) To ensure the observance and enforcement of laws, decrees, and resolutions of the People's Congresses at higher levels;
	(2) To adopt and issue resolutions within their scope of authority;
	(3) To plan economic construction, cultural construction, public services, preferential treatment work, and relief work;
	(4) To review and approve the budget and final accounts;
	(5) To elect the members of the People's Committee at the same level;
	(6) To elect the President of the People's Court at the same level;
	(7) To elect representatives to the People's Congress at the next higher level;
	(8) To hear and review the work reports of the People's Committee and the People's Court at the same level;
	(9) To change or revoke inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committee at the same level;
	(10) To change or revoke inappropriate resolutions of the People's Congress at the next lower level and inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committee at the next lower level;
	(11) To protect public property, maintain public order, and guarantee citizens' rights;
	(12) To guarantee the equal rights of all ethnic groups.
Article 11 The People's Congresses of Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns shall exercise the following functions and powers within their respective administrative regions:
	(1) To ensure the observance and enforcement of laws, decrees, and resolutions of the People's Congresses at higher levels;
	(2) To adopt and issue resolutions within their scope of authority;
	(3) To approve production plans for agriculture, animal husbandry, and handicrafts, and to decide on specific plans for mutual aid and cooperation undertakings and other economic work;
	(4) To plan public services;
	(5) To decide on implementation plans for cultural, educational, health, preferential treatment, and relief work;
	(6) To review financial revenues and expenditures;
	(7) To elect the members of the People's Committee at the same level;
	(8) To elect representatives to the People's Congress at the next higher level;
	(9) To hear and review the work reports of the People's Committee at the same level;
	(10) To change or revoke inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committee at the same level;
	(11) To protect public property, maintain public order, and guarantee citizens' rights;
	(12) To guarantee the equal rights of all ethnic groups.
	When exercising its functions and powers, the People's Congress of an Ethnic Township may take specific measures suitable to its ethnic characteristics.
Article 12 The People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have the power to remove from office the members of the People's Committee at the same level and the Presidents of the People's Courts elected by them.
Article 13 Meetings of the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall be convened by the People's Committee at the same level.
Article 14 The People's Congress of the Autonomous Region shall hold one meeting each year; the People's Congresses of Leagues and Administrative Regions shall hold one to two meetings each year; the People's Congresses of Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, and Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region shall hold two meetings each year; Banners in pastoral areas may hold one meeting each year; the People's Congresses of Sumu shall hold two to three meetings each year; the People's Congresses of Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns shall hold a meeting every three or four months.
	If the People's Committee at any level in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region deems it necessary or if one-fifth of the representatives propose it, a temporary meeting of the People's Congress at the same level may be convened.
Article 15 When the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, they shall elect a presidium to preside over the meeting.
	People's Congress meetings at and above the Banner and County levels shall have a Secretary-General and several Deputy Secretaries-General. The candidate for Secretary-General shall be nominated by the presidium and approved by the People's Congress meeting; the candidates for Deputy Secretaries-General shall be decided by the presidium.
Article 16 When the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, they may establish a credentials committee, a proposals review committee, and other committees that need to be established to carry out their work under the leadership of the presidium.
Article 17 When the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, the representatives, the presidium, and the People's Committee at the same level may submit proposals.
	Proposals submitted to the People's Congress meetings at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall be submitted by the presidium to the People's Congress meeting for discussion, or submitted to the proposals review committee for review before being submitted to the People's Congress meeting for discussion.
Article 18 Resolutions of the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall be adopted by a majority of all representatives.
Article 19 The candidates for members of the People's Committee and Presidents of the People's Courts at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall be jointly nominated or individually nominated by the representatives to the People's Congress at the same level.
	The People's Congresses at and above the Banner and County levels shall elect the members of the People's Committee and the Presidents of the People's Courts at the same level by means of secret ballot; the People's Congresses of Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns may elect the members of the People's Committee at the same level by a show of hands.
Article 20 When the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, the commonly used Mongolian and Han languages and scripts shall be used, and necessary translation shall be prepared for representatives of other ethnic groups.
	When the People's Congress of an Ethnic Township holds a meeting, the commonly used language and script of the local area shall be used.
Article 21 When the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, responsible persons from the working departments under the People's Committee at the same level, the President of the People's Court, the Chief Procurators of the People's Procuratorate, and other personnel agreed to by the presidium may attend the meeting as observers.
Article 22 When the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, questions raised by representatives to the People's Committee or the working departments under the People's Committee at the same level shall be submitted to the questioned organs by the presidium. The questioned organs must be responsible for answering in the meeting.
Article 23 During the period when representatives to the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region attend People's Congress meetings, they shall not be arrested or tried without the consent of the presidium. If they are detained because they are caught in the act, the organ executing the detention must immediately report to the presidium for approval.
Article 24 During the period when representatives to the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region attend People's Congress meetings, the state shall provide travel expenses and necessary material conveniences to and from the meeting as needed.
Article 25 Representatives to the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall maintain close contact with the original electoral units or voters, publicize laws, decrees, and policies, assist the People's Committee at the same level in promoting its work, and reflect the opinions and requirements of the masses to the People's Congress and the People's Committee.
	Representatives to the People's Congresses of the Autonomous Region, Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, and Counties may attend meetings of the People's Congress of the original electoral unit as observers.
	Representatives to the People's Congresses of Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns shall be assigned to contact voters. Residential areas or production units with three or more representatives may organize representative groups to assist the People's Committee at the same level in promoting its work.
Article 26 Representatives to the People's Congresses of the Autonomous Region, Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, and Counties are subject to the supervision of the original electoral units; representatives to the People's Congresses of Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns are subject to the supervision of the voters.
	The electoral units and voters of representatives to the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have the right to remove the representatives they elected at any time. The removal of a representative must be approved by a majority of all representatives of the original electoral unit or by a majority of the voters present at the original constituency's voters' meeting.
Article 27 When a representative to the People's Congress at any level in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is unable to serve as a representative due to unforeseen circumstances, a supplementary election shall be held by the original electoral unit or by the voters in the original constituency.

Chapter III People's Committees at All Levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region

Article 28 The People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, namely, the People's Governments at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, are the executive organs of the People's Congresses at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and are local state administrative organs at all levels.
Article 29 The People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are responsible to and report their work to the People's Congress at the same level and the state administrative organs at the next higher level.
	The People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are all state administrative organs under the unified leadership of the State Council and are subordinate to the State Council.
Article 30 The People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are respectively elected by the People's Congress at the same level to consist of one Chairman, League Head, Administrative Region Director, Mayor, Banner Head, County Head, District Head, Sumu Head, Gacha Head, Township Head, and Town Head, several Vice Chairmen, Vice League Heads, Deputy Administrative Region Directors, Deputy Mayors, Deputy Banner Heads, Deputy County Heads, Deputy District Heads, Deputy Sumu Heads, Deputy Gacha Heads, Deputy Township Heads, and Deputy Town Heads, and several members.
	The number of members of the People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall be:
	(1) Forty-three to forty-nine in the Autonomous Region;
	(2) Fifteen to thirty-one in Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region;
	(3) Nine to twenty-one in Banners, Counties, and Cities;
	(4) Nine to seventeen in Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region;
	(5) Five to thirteen in Sumu;
	(6) Three to thirteen in Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns.
Article 31 The term of office of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region is four years per term. The term of office of the People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns is two years per term.
	When a member of the People's Committee at any level in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is unable to perform his or her duties due to unforeseen circumstances, a supplementary election shall be held by the People's Congress at the same level.
Article 32 The People's Committee of the Autonomous Region shall exercise the following functions and powers within the Autonomous Region:
	(1) To prescribe administrative measures, issue resolutions and orders in accordance with the laws, decrees, resolutions and orders of the State Council, and the resolutions of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Region, and to review the implementation of these resolutions and orders;
	(2) To preside over the election of representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Region;
	(3) To convene meetings of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Region and submit proposals to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Region;
	(4) To lead the work of its subordinate working departments and the People's Committees at lower levels;
	(5) To suspend the implementation of inappropriate resolutions of the People's Congresses of Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region;
	(6) To change or revoke inappropriate orders and instructions of its subordinate working departments and inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committees at lower levels;
	(7) To handle matters related to administrative divisions in accordance with the provisions of the law;
	(8) To appoint and remove state organ personnel in accordance with the provisions of the law;
	(9) To implement economic plans;
	(10) To manage finances and execute budgets in accordance with the powers prescribed by law;
	(11) To manage markets, manage local state-owned industrial and mining enterprises and commerce, and lead the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce;
	(12) To lead agricultural, animal husbandry, and handicraft production and mutual aid and cooperation undertakings;
	(13) To lead the construction work in pastoral areas, especially the construction work of economic centers in pastoral areas;
	(14) To lead forest management, forest protection, and afforestation work;
	(15) To manage water conservancy undertakings;
	(16) To manage transportation and public services;
	(17) To manage tax collection work;
	(18) To manage cultural, educational, and health work;
	(19) To manage preferential treatment, relief, and social welfare work;
	(20) To manage public security forces in accordance with the state's military system;
	(21) To manage conscription work;
	(22) To protect public property, maintain public order, and guarantee citizens' rights;
	(23) To guarantee the equal rights of all ethnic groups, to assist areas inhabited by various minority ethnic groups within the Autonomous Region in implementing regional autonomy or establishing ethnic townships, and to assist various minority ethnic groups in developing political, economic, and cultural construction undertakings;
	(24) To handle other matters assigned by the State Council.
Article 33 The People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, and Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region shall exercise the following functions and powers within their respective administrative regions:
	(1) To prescribe administrative measures, issue resolutions and orders in accordance with the laws, decrees, resolutions and orders of the state administrative organs at higher levels, and the resolutions of the People's Congresses at the same level, and to review the implementation of these resolutions and orders;
	(2) To preside over the election of representatives to the People's Congresses at the same level;
	(3) To convene meetings of the People's Congresses at the same level and submit proposals to the People's Congresses at the same level;
	(4) To lead the work of its subordinate working departments and the People's Committees at lower levels;
	(5) To suspend the implementation of inappropriate resolutions of the People's Congresses at the next lower level;
	(6) To change or revoke inappropriate orders and instructions of its subordinate working departments and inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committees at lower levels;
	(7) To appoint and remove state organ personnel in accordance with the provisions of the law;
	(8) To implement economic plans;
	(9) To execute budgets;
	(10) To manage markets, manage local state-owned industrial and mining enterprises and commerce, and lead the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce;
	(11) To lead agricultural, animal husbandry, and handicraft production and mutual aid and cooperation undertakings;
	(12) To lead the construction work in pastoral areas, especially the construction work of economic centers in pastoral areas;
	(13) To lead forest management, forest protection, and afforestation work;
	(14) To manage water conservancy work;
	(15) To manage transportation and public services;
	(16) To manage tax collection work;
	(17) To manage cultural, educational, and health work;
	(18) To manage preferential treatment, relief, and social welfare work;
	(19) To manage conscription work;
	(20) To protect public property, maintain public order, and guarantee citizens' rights;
	(21) To guarantee the equal rights of all ethnic groups and to assist various minority ethnic groups in developing political, economic, and cultural construction undertakings;
	(22) To handle other matters assigned by the state administrative organs at higher levels.
Article 34 The People's Committees of Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns shall exercise the following functions and powers within their respective administrative regions:
	(1) To issue resolutions and orders in accordance with the laws, decrees, resolutions and orders of the state administrative organs at higher levels, and the resolutions of the People's Congresses at the same level;
	(2) To preside over the election of representatives to the People's Congresses at the same level;
	(3) To convene meetings of the People's Congresses at the same level and submit proposals to the People's Congresses at the same level;
	(4) To manage finances;
	(5) To lead agricultural, animal husbandry, and handicraft production, and to lead mutual aid and cooperation undertakings and other economic work;
	(6) To manage forest management, forest protection, and afforestation work;
	(7) To manage public services;
	(8) To manage cultural, educational, health, preferential treatment, and relief work;
	(9) To manage conscription work;
	(10) To protect public property, maintain public order, and guarantee citizens' rights;
	(11) To guarantee the equal rights of all ethnic groups;
	(12) To handle other matters assigned by the People's Committees at higher levels.
Article 35 The People's Committee of the Autonomous Region shall hold a meeting once a month; the People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, and Sumu shall hold a meeting once every one or two months; the People's Committees of Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns shall hold a meeting once every half month or month, and temporary meetings may be held when necessary.
	When the People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region hold meetings, relevant personnel may be invited to attend as observers.
	When the People's Committees at and above the Banner and County levels hold meetings, the President of the People's Court and the Chief Procurator of the People's Procuratorate at the same level may attend as observers.
Article 36 The Chairman, League Head, Administrative Region Director, Mayor, Banner Head, County Head, District Head, Sumu Head, Gacha Head, Township Head, and Town Head shall respectively preside over the meetings of the People's Committee at each level and the work of the People's Committee.
	The Vice Chairman, Vice League Head, Deputy Administrative Region Director, Deputy Mayor, Deputy Banner Head, Deputy County Head, Deputy District Head, Deputy Sumu Head, Deputy Gacha Head, Deputy Township Head, and Deputy Town Head shall respectively assist the Chairman, League Head, Administrative Region Director, Mayor, Banner Head, County Head, District Head, Sumu Head, Gacha Head, Township Head, and Town Head in their work.
	The Chairman, League Head, Administrative Region Director, Mayor, Banner Head, County Head, and District Head may convene administrative meetings to handle routine work.
Article 37 The People's Committee of the Autonomous Region shall establish the Department of Civil Affairs, the Department of Public Security, the Department of Justice, the Department of Supervision, the Planning Commission, the Department of Finance, the Department of Grain, the Department of Industry, the Department of Commerce, the Department of Transportation, the Department of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry, the Department of Forestry, the Department of Water Conservancy, the Department of Education, the Department of Health, the Sports Commission, the Ethnic Affairs Commission, the Urban Construction Bureau, the Handicraft Industry Administration Bureau, the Foreign Trade Bureau, the Labor Bureau, the Cultural Bureau, the Statistics Bureau, the Materials Supply Bureau, the Personnel Bureau, the Meteorological Bureau, the Confidential Communications Bureau, the Religious Affairs Office, the Foreign Affairs Office, and shall establish a General Office, a Counselor's Office, and a Mongolian Language Reform Committee.
Article 38 The People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region may establish bureaus, offices, sections, or commissions such as civil affairs, public security, justice, supervision, planning, finance, grain, taxation, industry, commerce, transportation, agriculture, animal husbandry, forestry, water conservancy, labor, culture, education, health, sports, municipal construction, and public utilities as needed, and may establish offices. Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region with a large number of ethnic affairs may establish institutions to manage ethnic affairs as needed. The public security, taxation, and other bureaus of the People's Committees of Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region may establish subordinate organs within the districts under the jurisdiction of the city.
Article 39 The People's Committees of Banners, Counties, Cities, and Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region may establish sections, units, or bureaus such as civil affairs, public security, finance, grain, taxation, industry and commerce, agriculture and animal husbandry, forestry, water conservancy, production cooperation, transportation, construction, labor, culture and education, health, and planning statistics as needed, and may establish offices.
Article 40 The People's Committee of Sumu shall divide its work to lead civil affairs, public security, production cooperation, finance and economics, culture and education, etc., and may establish several secretaries.
	The People's Committees of Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns may establish working committees such as civil affairs, public security, armed forces, production cooperation, finance and grain, culture and education, and mediation as needed, and absorb representatives to the People's Congress at the same level and other appropriate personnel to participate.
	The People's Committees of Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns may establish a clerk.
	People's Committees of towns with large populations and industry and commerce may, with the approval of the People's Committee of the Banner or County, establish working departments with reference to the provisions of Article 39 of these Regulations.
Article 41 The establishment, increase, reduction, or merger of the working departments of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region shall be reported to the State Council for approval by the People's Committee.
	The establishment, increase, reduction, or merger of the working departments of the People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region, Sumu, Gacha, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns shall be reported to the People's Committee at the next higher level for approval by the People's Committee.
Article 42 Each department, bureau, office, commission, section, and unit shall have a director, bureau chief, office director, commission chairman, section chief, and unit chief, and may have deputies when necessary.
	The General Office and Office shall have a director, and may have a deputy director when necessary.
	The People's Committees of the Autonomous Region, Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region shall each have a Secretary-General and several Deputy Secretaries-General.
Article 43 The People's Committee of the Autonomous Region shall establish several working institutions to assist the Chairman in managing the work of the working departments under the People's Committee.
	Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region may establish necessary working institutions to assist the Mayor in managing the work of the working departments under the People's Committee.
Article 44 The working departments of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region are under the unified leadership of the People's Committee and under the leadership of the competent departments of the State Council.
	The working departments of the People's Committees of Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, and Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region are under the unified leadership of the People's Committee at the same level and under the leadership of the competent departments of the People's Committee at the next higher level.
Article 45 Within the scope of their respective functions, the working departments of the People's Committees of the Autonomous Region, Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region may issue orders and instructions to the competent departments of the People's Committees at lower levels in accordance with laws and decrees, the resolutions and orders of the People's Committee, and the orders and instructions of the competent departments of the state administrative organs at higher levels.
Article 46 The People's Committees of the Autonomous Region, Leagues, Administrative Regions, Municipalities under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Region, Banners, Counties, Cities, and Districts under the jurisdiction of Municipalities in the Autonomous Region shall assist state organs, state-owned enterprises, and joint state-private enterprises that are not under their management in carrying out their work within their respective administrative regions, and shall supervise them in observing and implementing laws, decrees, and policies, but shall have no right to interfere in their operations.
Article 47 When necessary, the People's Committee of a Banner or County may, with the approval of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region, establish several Nutoek Public Offices or District Public Offices as its subordinate organs.
	When necessary, the People's Committee of a City or a District under the jurisdiction of a Municipality may, with the approval of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region, establish several street offices as its subordinate organs.
Article 48 The People's Committees at all levels and their working departments in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region shall use the commonly used Mongolian and Han languages and scripts when performing their duties.
	The People's Committee of an Ethnic Township shall use the commonly used language and script of the local area when performing its duties.

Chapter IV Supplementary Provisions

Article 49 These Regulations shall be implemented after being approved by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress after being adopted by the People's Congress of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例

1955-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例
    （一九五五年十一月十一日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十七次会议批准）
    第一章总则
    第一条内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例，根据中华人民共和国宪法第二章第五节制定。
    第二条内蒙古自治区设立下列各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会：
    （一）自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会；
    （二）盟、行政区、自治区辖市人民代表大会和人民委员会；
    （三）旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区人民代表大会和人民委员会；
    （四）苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇人民代表大会和人民委员会。
    第三条内蒙古自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会是内蒙古自治区的自治机关，是地方国家机关。
    第四条内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会，一律实行民主集中制。
    第五条内蒙古自治区内其他民族自治地方的自治机关的组织和工作，依照中华人民共和国宪法第二章第五节的规定。
    第二章内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会
    第六条内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会都是地方国家权力机关。
    第七条自治区、盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县的人民代表大会代表由下一级人民代表大会选举；市、自治区辖市的区、苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民代表大会代表由选民直接选举。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会代表名额和代表产生办法依照选举法规定。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会中，各民族都应当有适当名额的代表。
    第八条自治区人民代表大会每届任期四年。盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区、苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民代表大会每届任期两年。
    第九条自治区人民代表大会在自治区内行使下列职权：
    （一）保证法律、法令和全国人民代表大会决议的遵守和执行；
    （二）根据宪法规定的权限，依照自治区的特点，制定自治条例和单行条例，报请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准；
    （三）在职权范围内通过和发布决议；
    （四）规划经济建设、文化建设、公共事业、优抚工作和救济工作；
    （五）依照法律规定的自治区财政权限，审查和批准预算和决算；
    （六）依照国家的军事制度，决定组织自治区的公安部队；
    （七）选举自治区人民委员会的组成人员；
    （八）选举自治区高级人民法院院长；
    （九）选举全国人民代表大会代表；
    （十）听取和审查自治区人民委员会和自治区高级人民法院的工作报告；
    （十一）改变或者撤销自治区人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （十二）改变或者撤销盟、行政区、自治区辖市人民代表大会的不适当的决议和盟、行政区、自治区辖市人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （十三）保护公共财产，维护公共秩序，保障公民权利；
    （十四）保障各民族的平等权利。
    第十条旗、县级以上的各级人民代表大会在本行政区域内行使下列职权：
    （一）保证法律、法令和上级人民代表大会决议的遵守和执行；
    （二）在职权范围内通过和发布决议；
    （三）规划经济建设、文化建设、公共事业、优抚工作和救济工作；
    （四）审查和批准预算和决算；
    （五）选举本级人民委员会的组成人员；
    （六）选举本级人民法院院长；
    （七）选举上一级人民代表大会代表；
    （八）听取和审查本级人民委员会和人民法院的工作报告；
    （九）改变或者撤销本级人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （十）改变或者撤销下一级人民代表大会的不适当的决议和下一级人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （十一）保护公共财产，维护公共秩序，保障公民权利；
    （十二）保障各民族的平等权利。
    第十一条　苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民代表大会在本行政区域内行使下列职权：
    （一）保证法律、法令和上级人民代表大会决议的遵守和执行；
    （二）在职权范围内通过和发布决议；
    （三）批准农业、畜牧业、手工业的生产计划，决定互助合作事业和其他经济工作的具体计划；
    （四）规划公共事业；
    （五）决定文化、教育、卫生、优抚和救济工作的实施计划；
    （六）审查财政收支；
    （七）选举本级人民委员会的组成人员；
    （八）选举上一级人民代表大会代表；
    （九）听取和审查本级人民委员会的工作报告；
    （十）改变或者撤销本级人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （十一）保护公共财产，维护公共秩序，保障公民权利；
    （十二）保障各民族的平等权利。
  民族乡的人民代表大会在行使职权的时候，可以采取适合民族特点的具体措施。
    第十二条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会有权罢免本级人民委员会的组成人员和由它选出的人民法院院长。
    第十三条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会会议由本级人民委员会召集。
    第十四条　自治区人民代表大会会议每年举行一次；盟、行政区人民代表大会会议每年举行一次至两次；自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区的人民代表大会会议每年举行两次；牧业区的旗可以每年举行一次；苏木人民代表大会会议每年举行两次至三次；嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民代表大会会议每三个月或者四个月举行一次。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会如果认为必要或者有五分之一的代表提议，可以临时召集本级人民代表大会会议。
    第十五条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会举行会议的时候，选举主席团主持会议。
  旗、县级以上的各级人民代表大会会议设秘书长一人，副秘书长若干人。秘书长的人选由主席团提名，由人民代表大会会议通过；副秘书长的人选由主席团决定。
    第十六条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会举行会议的时候，可以设立代表资格审查委员会、议案审查委员会和其他需要设立的委员会，在主席团领导下进行工作。
    第十七条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会举行会议的时候，代表和主席团，本级人民委员会，都可以提出议案。
  向内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会会议提出的议案，由主席团提请人民代表大会会议讨论，或者交付议案审查委员会审查后提请人民代表大会会议讨论。
    第十八条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会的决议，以全体代表的过半数通过。
    第十九条　内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会组成人员和人民法院院长的人选，由本级人民代表大会代表联合提名或者单独提名。
  旗、县级以上的各级人民代表大会选举本级人民委员会组成人员和人民法院院长，采用无记名投票方式；苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇人民代表大会选举本级人民委员会组成人员，可以采用举手方式。
    第二十条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会举行会议的时候，使用通用的蒙、汉语言文字，并且为其他民族代表准备必要的翻译。
  民族乡的人民代表大会举行会议的时候，使用当地通用的语言文字。
    第二十一条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会举行会议的时候，本级人民委员会所属各工作部门负责人员和人民法院院长、人民检察院检察长及主席团同意的其他人员可以列席。
    第二十二条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会举行会议的时候，代表向本级人民委员会或者本级人民委员会所属各工作部门提出的质问，经过主席团提交受质问的机关。受质问的机关必须在会议中负责答复。
    第二十三条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会代表在出席人民代表大会会议的期间，非经主席团同意不受逮捕或者审判，如果因为是现行犯被拘留，执行拘留的机关必须立即报请主席团批准。
    第二十四条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会代表在出席人民代表大会会议的期间，国家根据需要给以往返的旅费和必要的物质上的便利。
    第二十五条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会代表应当和原选举单位或者选民保持密切联系，宣传法律、法令和政策，协助本级人民委员会推行工作，并且向人民代表大会和人民委员会反映群众的意见和要
  求。
  自治区、盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县的人民代表大会代表可以列席原选举单位的人民代表大会会议。
  市、自治区辖市的区、苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民代表大会代表分工联系选民，有代表三人以上的居民地区或者生产单位可以组织代表小组，协助本级人民委员会推行工作。
    第二十六条　自治区、盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县的人民代表大会代表受原选举单位的监督；市、自治区辖市的区、苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民代表大会代表受选民的监督。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会代表的选举单位和选民有权随时撤换自己选出的代表。代表的撤换必须由原选举单位以全体代表的过半数通过，或者由原选区选民大会以出席选民的过半数通过。
    第二十七条　内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会代表因故不能担任代表职务的时候，由原选举单位或者由原选区选民补选。
    第三章　内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会
    第二十八条　内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会，即内蒙古自治区各级人民政府，是内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会的执行机关，是地方各级国家行政机关。
    第二十九条　内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会都对本级人民代表大会和上一级国家行政机关负责并报告工作。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会都是国务院统一领导下的国家行政机关，都服从国务院。
    第三十条　内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会分别由本级人民代表大会选举主席、盟长、行政区主任、市长、旗长、县长、区长、苏木长、嘎查长、乡长、镇长各一人，副主席、副盟长、行政区副主任、副市长、副旗长、副县长、副区长、副苏木长、副嘎查长、副乡长、副镇长各若干人和委员各若干人组成。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会组成人员的名额：
    （一）自治区四十三人至四十九人；
    （二）盟、行政区、自治区辖市十五人至三十一人；
    （三）旗、县、市九人至二十一人；
    （四）自治区辖市的区九人至十七人；
    （五）苏木五人至十三人；
    （六）嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇三人至十三人。
    第三十一条　自治区人民委员会每届任期四年。盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区、苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民委员会每届任期两年。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会的组成人员因故不能担任职务的时候，由本级人民代表大会补选。
    第三十二条自治区人民委员会在自治区内行使下列职权：
    （一）根据法律、法令、国务院的决议和命令和自治区人民代表大会的决议，规定行政措施，发布决议和命令，并且审查这些决议和命令的实施情况；
    （二）主持自治区人民代表大会代表的选举；
    （三）召集自治区人民代表大会会议，向自治区人民代表大会提出议案；
    （四）领导所属各工作部门和下级人民委员会的工作；
    （五）停止盟、行政区和自治区辖市的人民代表大会的不适当的决议的执行；
    （六）改变或者撤销所属各工作部门的不适当的命令和指示和下级人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （七）依照法律的规定办理有关行政区划事项；
    （八）依照法律的规定任免国家机关工作人员；
    （九）执行经济计划；
    （十）依照法律规定的权限管理财政，执行预算；
    （十一）管理市场，管理地方国营工矿企业和商业，领导资本主义工商业的社会主义改造；
    （十二）领导农业、畜牧业、手工业生产和互助合作事业；
    （十三）领导牧业区的建设工作，特别是牧业区经济中心地点的建设工作；
    （十四）领导森林经营、森林保护和植树造林等工作；
    （十五）管理水利事业；
    （十六）管理交通和公共事业；
    （十七）管理税收工作；
    （十八）管理文化、教育和卫生工作；
    （十九）管理优抚、救济和社会福利工作；
    （二十）依照国家的军事制度，管理公安部队；
    （二十一）管理兵役工作；
    （二十二）保护公共财产，维护公共秩序，保障公民权利；
    （二十三）保障各民族的平等权利，帮助自治区内各少数民族聚居的地方实行区域自治或者建立民族乡，帮助各少数民族发展政治、经济和文化的建设事业；
    （二十四）办理国务院交办的其他事项。
    第三十三条　盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区的人民委员会在本行政区域内行使下列职权：
    （一）根据法律、法令、上级国家行政机关的决议和命令和本级人民代表大会的决议，规定行政措施，发布决议和命令，并且审查这些决议和命令的实施情况；
    （二）主持本级人民代表大会代表的选举；
    （三）召集本级人民代表大会会议，向本级人民代表大会提出议案；
    （四）领导所属各工作部门和下级人民委员会的工作；
    （五）停止下一级人民代表大会的不适当的决议的执行；
    （六）改变或者撤销所属各工作部门的不适当的命令和指示和下级人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
    （七）依照法律的规定任免国家机关工作人员；
    （八）执行经济计划；
    （九）执行预算；
    （十）管理市场，管理地方国营工矿企业和商业，领导资本主义工商业的社会主义改造；
    （十一）领导农业、畜牧业、手工业生产和互助合作事业；
    （十二）领导牧业区的建设工作，特别是牧业区经济中心地点的建设工作；
    （十三）领导森林经营、森林保护和植树造林等工作；
    （十四）管理水利工作；
    （十五）管理交通和公共事业；
    （十六）管理税收工作；
    （十七）管理文化、教育和卫生工作；
    （十八）管理优抚、救济和社会福利工作；
    （十九）管理兵役工作；
    （二十）保护公共财产、维护公共秩序、保障公民权利；
    （二十一）保障各民族的平等权利，帮助各少数民族发展政治、经济和文化的建设事业；
    （二十二）办理上级国家行政机关交办的其他事项。
    第三十四条　苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇的人民委员会在本行政区域内行使下列职权：
    （一）根据法律、法令、上级国家行政机关的决议和命令和本级人民代表大会的决议，发布决议和命令；
    （二）主持本级人民代表大会代表的选举；
    （三）召集本级人民代表大会会议，向本级人民代表大会提出议案；
    （四）管理财政；
    （五）领导农业、畜牧业、手工业生产，领导互助合作事业和其他经济工作；
    （六）管理森林经营、森林保护和植树造林等工作；
    （七）管理公共事业；
    （八）管理文化、教育、卫生、优抚和救济工作；
    （九）管理兵役工作；
    （十）保护公共财产，维护公共秩序，保障公民权利；
    （十一）保障各民族的平等权利；
    （十二）办理上级人民委员会交办的其他事项。
    第三十五条　自治区人民委员会会议每一个月举行一次，盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区、苏木的人民委员会会议每一个月或者两个月举行一次，嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇人民委员会会议每半个月或者一个月举行一次，在必要的时候都可以临时举行。
  内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会举行会议的时候，可以邀请有关人员列席。
  旗、县级以上的人民委员会举行会议的时候，本级人民法院院长、人民检察院检察长可以列席。
    第三十六条　主席、盟长、行政区主任、市长、旗长、县长、区长、苏木长、嘎查长、乡长、镇长分别主持各级人民委员会会议和人民委员会的工作。
  副主席、副盟长、行政区副主任、副市长、副旗长、副县长、副区长、副苏木长、副嘎查长、副乡长、副镇长分别协助主席、盟长、行政区主任、市长、旗长、县长、区长、苏木长、嘎查长、乡长、镇长工作。
  主席、盟长、行政区主任、市长、旗长、县长、区长为处理日常工作，可以召开行政会议。
    第三十七条　自治区人民委员会设立民政厅、公安厅、司法厅、监察厅、计划委员会、财政厅、粮食厅、工业厅、商业厅、交通厅、农牧厅、林业厅、水利厅、教育厅、卫生厅、体育运动委员会、民族事务委员会、城市建设局、手工业管理局、对外贸易局、劳动局、文化局、统计局、物资供应局、人事局、气象局、机要交通局、宗教事务处、外事处，并且设立办公厅、参事室、蒙古文字改革委员会。
    第三十八条　盟、行政区、自治区辖市的人民委员会按照需要可以设立民政、公安、司法、监察、计划、财政、粮食、税务、工业、商业、交通、农业、牧业、林业、水利、劳动、文化、教育、卫生、体育运动、市政建设和公共事业等局、处、科或者委员会，并且可以设立办公室。民族事务较多的盟、行政区、自治区辖市按照需要可以设立管理民族事务的机构。自治区辖市人民委员会的公安、税务等局可以在市辖区内设立派出机关。
    第三十九条　旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区的人民委员会按照需要可以设立民政、公安、财政、粮食、税务、工商、农牧、林业、水利、生产合作、交通、建设、劳动、文化教育、卫生、计划统计等科、股或者局，并且可以设立办公室。
    第四十条　苏木人民委员会分工领导民政、治安、生产合作、财经、文化教育等工作，并且可以设秘书若干人。
  嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇人民委员会按照需要可以设立民政、治安、武装、生产合作、财粮、文化教育、调解等工作委员会，吸收本级人民代表大会代表和其他适当的人员参加。
  嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇人民委员会可以设文书一人。
  人口和工商业较多的镇人民委员会，经旗、县人民委员会批准，可以参照本条例第三十九条的规定设立工作部门。
    第四十一条　自治区人民委员会的工作部门的设立、增加、减少或者合并，由人民委员会报请国务院批准。
  盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区、苏木、嘎查、乡、民族乡、镇人民委员会的工作部门的设立、增加、减少或者合并，由人民委员会报请上一级人民委员会批准。
    第四十二条　各厅、局、处、委员会、科、股分别设厅长、局长、处长、委员会主任、科长、股长，在必要的时候可以设副职。
  办公厅、办公室设主任，在必要的时候可以设副主任。
  自治区、盟、行政区、自治区辖市的人民委员会各设秘书长一人，副秘书长若干人。
    第四十三条　自治区人民委员会设立若干办公机构，协助主席分别掌管人民委员会所属各工作部门的工作。
  自治区辖市可以设立必要的办公机构，协助市长分别掌管人民委员会所属各工作部门的工作。
    第四十四条　自治区人民委员会的各工作部门受人民委员会的统一领导，并且受国务院主管部门的领导。
  盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区的人民委员会的各工作部门受本级人民委员会的统一领导，并且受上级人民委员会主管部门的领导。
    第四十五条　自治区、盟、行政区、自治区辖市的人民委员会的各工作部门在本部门的业务范围内，根据法律和法令，人民委员会的决议和命令，上级国家行政机关主管部门的命令和指示，可以向下级人民委员会主管部门发布命令和指示。
    第四十六条　自治区、盟、行政区、自治区辖市、旗、县、市、自治区辖市的区的人民委员会应当协助设立在本行政区域内不属于自己管理的国家机关、国营企业和公私合营企业进行工作，并且监督它们遵守和执行法律、法令和政策，但是无权干涉它们的业务。
    第四十七条　旗、县人民委员会在必要的时候，经自治区人民委员会批准，可以设立若干努图克公所或者区公所，作为它的派出机关。
  市、自治区辖市的区的人民委员会在必要的时候，经自治区人民委员会批准，可以设立若干街道办事处，作为它的派出机关。
    第四十八条　内蒙古自治区各级人民委员会和各工作部门在执行职务的时候，使用通用的蒙、汉语言文字。
  民族乡的人民委员会在执行职务的时候，使用当地通用的语言文字。
    第四章附则
    第四十九条　本条例经内蒙古自治区人民代表大会通过，报请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准后实行。
