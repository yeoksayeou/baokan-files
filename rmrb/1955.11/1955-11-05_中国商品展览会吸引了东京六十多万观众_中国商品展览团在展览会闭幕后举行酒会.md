Title: Chinese Goods Exhibition Attracts Over 600,000 Visitors in Tokyo; Chinese Goods Exhibition Delegation Holds Reception After Exhibition Closing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 4th. The Chinese Goods Exhibition held in Tokyo closed on November 3rd. During the seventeen-day exhibition period, the Chinese Goods Exhibition attracted more than 660,000 visitors, of whom 150,000 watched New China's films.

    On the evening of the closing day, the Chinese Goods Exhibition Delegation held a reception at the Chinese Goods Exhibition Hall.

    More than 1,100 people from all walks of life in Japan were invited to attend the reception. They were: Murata Shozo, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association; Ikeda Masanosuke, representative of the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade; Kuhara Fusanosuke, Chairman of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations; Katayama Tetsu, representative of the National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution of Japan; Matsumoto Jiichiro, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Democratic Party Diet members Kitamura Tokutaro and Matsuura Shutaro; Asanuma Inejirō, Secretary-General of the Socialist Party; Sugiyama Motojiro, Socialist Party Diet member and Vice-Speaker of the House of Representatives; Nagashima Ginzo and Miyamoto Kunihiko, Liberal Party Diet members; Hori Makoto, Labor-Farmer Party Diet member; Nosaka Sanzo, First Secretary of the Communist Party, and Miyamoto Kenji, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee; Fujita Tohtaro, Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan; Yoshida Sukesaburo, Chairman of the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Organizations; Suzuki Kinu, Secretary-General of the All-Japan Women's Organizations Federation; Tan Tokusaburo, International Director of the Japan Peace Committee; Tajima Masao, head of the Japanese business community delegation to China; Suzuki Kazuo, executive director of the Sino-Japanese Trade Association; Hasegawa Tetsuzo, Dean of the Faculty of Letters of Hosei University; playwright Koreya Senda; actress Kishida Kyoko; and commentator Matsuoka Yoko, among others.

    Also invited to the reception were Domnitsky, head of the Soviet delegation in Tokyo, Han Dok-su, chairman of the Central Council of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, and representatives of overseas Chinese in Japan.

    Cao Zhongshu, head of the Chinese Goods Exhibition Delegation, delivered a speech at the reception. He first expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the more than 660,000 Japanese friends who visited the Chinese Goods Exhibition. He said that thanks to the assistance of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade, and the sincere and friendly support of friends from all walks of life in Japan and Tokyo, the Chinese Goods Exhibition in Tokyo has achieved great success. This success shows the results of our mutual cooperation efforts over the past few years.

    Cao Zhongshu emphasized that the Japanese people, like the Chinese people, have a sincere desire to establish friendly relations between China and Japan and develop Sino-Japanese trade. He believed that this exhibition further enhanced the understanding and friendship between the two peoples, which would contribute to promoting normal trade relations.

    Murata Shozo said in his speech that not only have more than 660,000 visitors seen a microcosm of New China, but also through news reports, almost all the people of Japan know about the situation in New China. He believed that this Chinese Goods Exhibition is a prelude to the restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Japan.

    Ikeda Masanosuke said in his speech that this Chinese Goods Exhibition allowed the Japanese people to see New China with their own eyes. This will have a good impact on the friendly relations between China and Japan.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The guests and hosts frequently toasted the great success of the Chinese Goods Exhibition. Many of the guests and members of the Chinese Goods Exhibition Delegation linked arms and sang the song "Tokyo-Beijing," which praises Sino-Japanese friendship, and repeatedly chanted "Long live Sino-Japanese friendship."

    The Chinese Goods Exhibition will also be exhibited in Osaka.

### 中国商品展览会吸引了东京六十多万观众  中国商品展览团在展览会闭幕后举行酒会

1955-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国商品展览会吸引了东京六十多万观众
    中国商品展览团在展览会闭幕后举行酒会
    新华社东京四日电　在东京举办的中国商品展览会已经在十一月三日闭幕。这次中国商品展览会在十七天的展览期中，吸引了六十六万多名观众，其中有十五万人看了新中国的电影。
    在闭幕这一天的晚上，中国商品展览团在中国商品展览馆内举行了酒会。
    应邀参加酒会的有日本各界人士一千一百多人。他们是：日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏，日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表池田正之辅，日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议会长久原房之助，日本拥护宪法国民联合会代表片山哲，日中友好协会会长松本治一郎，民主党国会议员北村德太郎、松浦周太郎，社会党总书记浅沼稻次郎，社会党国会议员、众议院副议长杉山元治郎，自由党国会议员长岛银藏、宫本邦彦，劳农党国会议员堀真琴，共产党第一书记野坂参三、中央委员会常任干部会委员宫本显治，日本工会总评议会主席藤田藤太郎，全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治，全日本妇女团体联合会事务局长铃木绢，日本拥护和平委员会国际部长淡德三郎，日本工商界访华代表团团长田岛正雄，中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄，法政大学文学院长谷川彻三，戏剧家千田是也，演员岸辉子，评论家松冈洋子等人。
    应邀参加酒会的还有苏联驻东京代表团团长多穆尼茨基，在日朝鲜人总联合会中央议长韩德珠和旅日华侨代表等。
    中国商品展览团团长曹中枢在酒会上致词。他首先对六十六万多位参观中国商品展览会的日本朋友们表示衷心的感谢。他说：由于日本国际贸易促进协会和日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟的协助，由于日本和东京各界朋友们真诚友好的支持，中国商品展览会在东京的展出得到了很大的成功。这个成功显示出我们几年来相互合作努力的结果。
    曹中枢着重指出：日本人民对建立中日两国友好关系和发展中日贸易，同中国人民一样地具有真诚愿望。他认为，这次展览会进一步增进了两国人民之间的了解和友谊，从而将对促进正常贸易关系有所贡献。
    村田省藏致词说：不仅有六十六万多个参观的人们看到了新中国的缩影，并且通过新闻报道使几乎日本全国人民都知道了新中国的情况。他认为，这次中国商品展览会是中日两国恢复邦交的先声。
    池田正之辅致词说：这次中国商品展览会使日本人民亲眼看到新中国。这将对中日两国的友好关系发生很好的影响。
    酒会在热烈友好的空气中进行。宾主频频为中国商品展览会获得的巨大成功而干杯。许多与会的客人和中国商品展览团人员手臂挽着手臂，合唱歌颂中日友好的歌曲“东京—北京”，并且一再高呼中日两国友好万岁。
    中国商品展览会还将在大阪展出。
