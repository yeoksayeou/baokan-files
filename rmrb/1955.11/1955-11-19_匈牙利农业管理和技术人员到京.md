Title: Hungarian Agricultural Management and Technical Personnel Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 4
　
    Hungarian Agricultural Management and Technical Personnel Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Five agricultural management and technical personnel from the Hungarian People's Republic, who are assisting our country in building an agricultural machinery station, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th.

### 匈牙利农业管理和技术人员到京

1955-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

　  匈牙利农业管理和技术人员到京
    新华社十八日讯　匈牙利人民共和国帮助我国建设一个农业机器站的五位农业管理和技术人员，已在十八日乘机到达北京。
