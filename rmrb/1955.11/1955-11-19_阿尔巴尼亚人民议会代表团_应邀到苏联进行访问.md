Title: Albanian People's Assembly Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The delegation of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania arrived in Moscow on the 16th at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The delegation is led by the Chairman of the People's Assembly, Gogo Nushi.

　　Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Komarov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, received the delegation of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, which was invited to visit the Soviet Union, on the 17th. The two sides held cordial talks during the meeting. Albanian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Piro Prijfti, also participated in the talks.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团　应邀到苏联进行访问

1955-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团
　　应邀到苏联进行访问
    据新华社讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请，在十六日到达莫斯科。代表团由人民议会主席果·努什率领。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院副主席科马罗夫，在十七日接见了应邀访问苏联的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团。接见时宾主双方进行了亲切的谈话。参加谈话的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻苏联大使普里夫蒂。
