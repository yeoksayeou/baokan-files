Title: Pakistan's Governor-General Receives Chinese Women's Delegation; Pakistan's Prime Minister's Wife Welcomes Chinese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 26th: Pakistan's Governor-General Iskander Mirza received the Chinese Women's Delegation headed by Li Dequan on the 23rd. The Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Han Nianlong, accompanied the delegation during the meeting with the Governor-General.

During the reception, Mirza stated that mutual visits by delegations from both countries can promote friendship between Pakistan and China.

That noon, Governor-General Mirza's wife hosted a luncheon in honor of the Chinese Women's Delegation.

The delegation also visited Pakistan's Prime Minister Ali's wife, Jinnah's sister Fatima Jinnah, and the President of the All Pakistan Women's Association, Liaquat Ali Khan's wife. The Pakistan Business and Professional Women's Club held a tea party to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 29th: Pakistan's Prime Minister Mohammed Ali's wife held a reception on November 24th to welcome the Chinese Women's Delegation headed by Li Dequan, which is visiting Pakistan at the invitation of the All Pakistan Women's Association.

On the same day, the delegation visited the Dow Medical College and St. Joseph's Convent School, as well as the handicraft workshop sponsored by the All Pakistan Women's Association. The delegation also attended the opening ceremony of the Pakistan Social Workers Conference and sent a congratulatory letter.

On the evening of November 25th, the Vice-President of the All Pakistan Women's Association, Mrs. Iqbal Hussaini Malik, held a banquet to welcome the Chinese Women's Delegation.

### 巴基斯坦总督接见中国妇女代表团　巴基斯坦总理夫人欢迎中国妇女代表团

1955-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦总督接见中国妇女代表团
　　巴基斯坦总理夫人欢迎中国妇女代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦总督伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎在二十三日接见了以李德全为首的中国妇女代表团。中国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙陪同代表团会见总督。
    米尔扎在接见时说，两国代表团互相访问可以促进巴中两国的友谊。
    当天中午，米尔扎总督夫人设午餐招待中国妇女代表团。
    代表团还访问了巴基斯坦总理阿里夫人，真纳的妹妹法蒂玛·真纳，和全巴基斯坦妇女协会主席利雅卡特·阿里汗夫人。巴基斯坦职业妇女俱乐部曾举行茶会招待代表团。
    据新华社二十九日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里的夫人在十一月二十四日举行招待会，欢迎应全巴基斯坦妇女协会的邀请到巴基斯坦访问的以李德全为首的中国妇女代表团。
    代表团在当天参观了杜尔弗学院和圣约瑟夫女子学院，以及全巴基斯坦妇女协会主办的手工艺品制造社。代表团还参观了巴基斯坦社会工作者会议的开幕仪式，并且写信祝贺。
    十一月二十五日晚上，全巴基斯坦妇女协会副主席伊格巴尔·侯赛尼·马利克夫人举行宴会欢迎中国妇女代表团。
