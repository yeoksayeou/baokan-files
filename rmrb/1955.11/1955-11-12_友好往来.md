　　Friendly Exchanges
　  Chinese Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for North Korea
    The Chinese cultural delegation, which is visiting North Korea according to the 1955 China-North Korea cultural exchange executive plan, left Beijing for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea by train on the 10th. The head of the delegation is Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, and the members include Yang Gang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Chang Sumin, President of the Southwest Conservatory of Music, and Zheng Zhidong, Secretary of the Publicity and Promotion Department of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee.
　  Indian National Volleyball Team Returns Home
    The Indian National Volleyball Team, a group of sixteen people led by team leader Fa. Qian. Alora, who were invited to China by the All-China Sports Federation, left Guangzhou on the morning of the 10th to return home. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-11-12
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　友好往来
　  我国文化代表团离京赴朝
    根据中朝一九五五年文化交流执行计划赴朝访问的中国文化代表团，已在十日乘火车离北京去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。代表团团长是教育部副部长韦悫，团员有人民日报副总编辑杨刚、西南音乐专科学校校长常苏民、中国文字改革委员会宣传推广部秘书郑之东。
　  印度国家排球队回国
    应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的印度国家排球队一行十六人，由领队法·钱·阿罗拉率领在十日上午离开广州回国。（据新华社讯）
