Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-11-21
Page: 1

Our great motherland:
The Jiamusi Sugar Factory, a fully mechanized and automated facility built with the help of the People's Republic of Poland, has been completed and has begun trial production and will soon be officially put into operation. After it goes into production, it will produce tens of thousands of tons of high-quality granulated sugar every year to meet the needs of our people. This is a scene from the sugar production workshop.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    波兰人民共和国帮助我国建设的全部机械化、自动化的佳木斯糖厂已经建成，并已开始试生产，不久将正式投入生产。投入生产后，每年将出产数万吨优质砂糖，供应我国人民需要。这是制糖车间的场面。
