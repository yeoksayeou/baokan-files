Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Arrives in Pakistan for Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 24th: Karachi dispatch: The Chinese Women's Delegation, invited by the All Pakistan Women's Association for a friendly visit to Pakistan, arrived in Karachi on the night of the 22nd. The delegation is led by Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
　　Welcoming them at the airport were Mrs. Hussain Malik, Vice-Chairman of the All Pakistan Women's Association, Nawaz Khan, Director of Protocol of the Pakistan Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Han Nianlong, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and diplomatic and commercial representatives from other countries.
　　Upon arriving in Karachi, Head of the Delegation Li Dequan issued a written statement at the airport. She pointed out that the delegation is visiting Pakistan with the friendship of hundreds of millions of Chinese women and with the good wishes of the Chinese people for the happiness and prosperity of the Pakistani people. She expressed her firm belief that through mutual visits and contacts between Chinese and Pakistani women's delegations, the understanding between the women and people of the two countries will certainly be further enhanced. This will undoubtedly contribute to the development of friendly relations between China and Pakistan.
　　The Chinese Women's Delegation will pay a three-week friendly visit to Pakistan.

### 我国妇女代表团到达巴基斯坦作友好访问

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国妇女代表团到达巴基斯坦作友好访问
    新华社新德里二十四日电　喀喇蚩消息：应全巴基斯坦妇女协会邀请到巴基斯坦作友好访问的中国妇女代表团在二十二日夜间到达喀喇蚩。代表团由中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全率领。
    在机场欢迎的有全巴基斯坦妇女协会副主席侯赛尼·马利克夫人，巴基斯坦外交部礼宾司司长纳瓦兹汗，中国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙以及其他国家的外交代表和商务代表。
    代表团团长李德全抵达喀喇蚩时，曾在飞机场发表书面谈话。她指出，代表团带着亿万中国妇女的友情，并且带着中国人民对巴基斯坦人民的幸福和繁荣的良好愿望来访问巴基斯坦。她表示深信，通过中、巴妇女代表团的相互访问和接触，两国妇女和两国人民之间的了解一定能够进一步地增加。这无疑会有助于中、巴两国友好关系的发展。
    中国妇女代表团将在巴基斯坦作三个星期的友好访问。
