Title: The Soviet Ministry of Culture Presents Two Soviet Films to China's Ministry of Culture
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 7th. The Soviet Artists Delegation and the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe, on the afternoon of the 7th in Beijing, on behalf of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, presented two Soviet color films to the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China. One of them is the Soviet color film "The Liu Family Brothers," based on a Chinese story, and the other is the color feature film "Unusual Competition."

    Nikishkin, head of the Soviet Artists Delegation, and Nadezhdina, artistic director of the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe, spoke successively during the presentation, thanking the Chinese people for their kind care and attention, and wishing close cooperation between Chinese and Soviet artists.

    Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, accepted the gifts on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and delivered a speech of thanks. He pointed out that the performances of the Soviet artists in China have given us new strength and spurred us on to strive to improve the ideological and artistic level of Chinese art work. The two films presented by the Soviet Ministry of Culture will enable us to further study the excellent Soviet film art.

    Attending the presentation ceremony were Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, some members of the Soviet Artists Delegation and the Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe, Cheng Haofei, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau, Ge Baoquan and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and others.

### 苏联文化部赠给我国文化部两部苏联影片

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化部赠给我国文化部两部苏联影片
    新华社七日讯　苏联艺术家代表团和苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团，七日下午在北京代表苏联文化部向中华人民共和国文化部赠送了两部苏联彩色影片。其中一部是苏联根据中国故事摄制的彩色影片“刘家兄弟”，另一部是彩色故事片“不平凡的竞赛”。
    苏联艺术家代表团团长尼基什金、苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团艺术领导人纳杰日金娜在赠礼时相继致词，感谢中国人民对他们的亲切关怀和照顾，并祝中苏两国艺术工作者的亲密合作。
    中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞代表文化部接受礼品并致词答谢，他指出苏联艺术家们在中国的演出使我们得到新的力量，鞭策我们来努力提高中国艺术工作的思想水平和艺术水平，苏联文化部赠送的这两部影片，将使我们能进一步学习苏联卓越的电影艺术。
    参加赠礼仪式的，有苏联驻华大使馆参赞李海秋，苏联艺术家代表团和苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团的部分团员，中华人民共和国文化部办公厅副主任程浩飞，电影事业管理局局长王阑西，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权、李沾吾，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙等人。
