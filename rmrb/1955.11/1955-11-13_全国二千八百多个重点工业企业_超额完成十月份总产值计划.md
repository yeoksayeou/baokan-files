Title: Over 2,800 Key Industrial Enterprises Nationwide Exceed October's Total Output Value Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th. According to statistics from the State Statistical Bureau, the nation's 2,830 key industrial enterprises fulfilled 103% of their total output value plan for October. Among these, state-owned enterprises fulfilled 102.9% of their plan, locally-run state-owned enterprises fulfilled 104.8%, centrally-administered joint state-private enterprises fulfilled 101.1%, and locally-administered joint state-private enterprises fulfilled 101.1%. All central industrial ministries also fulfilled their total output value plans for October. Among them, the Ministry of Electric Power Industry fulfilled 100.9% of its plan, the Ministry of Coal Industry fulfilled 104.1%, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry fulfilled 105.3%, the Ministry of Heavy Industry fulfilled 103.3%, the First Ministry of Machine Building fulfilled 100.9%, the Ministry of Textile Industry fulfilled 100.6%, and the Ministry of Light Industry fulfilled 105.6%. The majority of key local state-owned and joint state-private enterprises under the jurisdiction of provinces and cities across the country fulfilled their total output value plans for October relatively well. However, the fulfillment of the plan by key local state-owned and joint state-private enterprises in Shanghai, Shaanxi, Guangxi, and Inner Mongolia was comparatively poor.

Accumulated from January to October, the total output value of the aforementioned key enterprises increased by 9.4% compared to the same period last year. Among these, state-owned enterprises increased by 11.2%, local state-owned enterprises increased by 14%, centrally-administered joint state-private enterprises increased by 2.7%, and locally-administered joint state-private enterprises decreased by 2.7%. Among the central industrial ministries, the Ministry of Electric Power Industry increased by 16.4%, the Ministry of Coal Industry increased by 15.2%, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry increased by 43.4%, the Ministry of Heavy Industry increased by 25.2%, the First Ministry of Machine Building increased by 13.4%, the Ministry of Light Industry increased by 4.4%, and the Ministry of Textile Industry decreased by 15.2%.

Among the thirty important industrial products of the aforementioned key enterprises, twenty-three products, including electricity, raw coal, crude oil, pig iron, steel, rolled steel products, copper, sulfuric acid, soda ash, ammonium sulfate, cement, generators, electric motors, metal cutting machine tools, double-shared plows, cotton yarn, cotton cloth,麻袋 (ma dai, sacks), automobile tires, paper, sugar, flour, and cigarettes, completed their plans. Rubber shoes and edible vegetable oil nearly completed their plans, and the completion rate of other products was also above 90%.

The aforementioned key enterprises completed 106.4% of their October labor productivity plan. The October labor productivity of the central industrial ministries generally increased compared to the same month last year. Among them, the Ministry of Electric Power Industry increased by 11.6%, the Ministry of Coal Industry increased by 14.6%, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry increased by 35%, the Ministry of Heavy Industry increased by 21.3%, the First Ministry of Machine Building increased by 28.4%, the Ministry of Light Industry increased by 24%, and the Ministry of Textile Industry decreased by 2.2%.

Enterprises under the various industrial ministries that performed well in completing their production plans for October include the Beijing Electric Power Bureau, Tangshan Electric Power Bureau, Chengdu Power Plant, Fushun Mining Bureau, Jixi Mining Bureau, Huainan Mining Bureau, Yumen Mining Bureau, Yanchang Oil Mine, Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, Shanghai Machine Tool Plant, Tianjin Bicycle Factory, Harbin Ball Bearing Factory, Shanghai State-owned Cotton Mill No. 1, Shanghai State-owned Cotton Mill No. 2, Shanghai State-owned Cotton Mill No. 15, Qingdao State-owned Cotton Mill No. 6, Shixianshan Paper Mill, Shandong Paper Mill, and many other enterprises. However, some enterprises completed their plans relatively poorly, such as the Zhabei Water and Electricity Company, Xinjiang Petroleum Company, Xia Hua Coal Mine, Tongguanshan Mining Bureau, Pangu Mountain Tungsten Mine, Dalian Alkali Plant, Shenyang General Machinery Factory, Acheng Instrument Factory, Jiamusi Textile Factory, Xinjiang July 1st Cotton Textile Factory, and Liaoxi Salt Affairs Administration.

### 全国二千八百多个重点工业企业　超额完成十月份总产值计划

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国二千八百多个重点工业企业
　　超额完成十月份总产值计划
    新华社十二日讯　根据国家统计局的统计，全国二千八百三十个重点工业企业完成十月份总产值计划百分之一百零三。其中国营企业完成计划百分之一百零二点九，地方国营企业完成计划百分之一百零四点八，中央公私合营企业完成计划百分之一百零一点一，地方公私合营企业完成计划百分之一百零一点一。中央各工业部也都完成了十月份总产值计划。其中电力工业部完成计划百分之一百点九，煤炭工业部完成计划百分之一百零四点一，石油工业部完成计划百分之一百零五点三，重工业部完成计划百分之一百零三点三，第一机械工业部完成计划百分之一百点九，纺织工业部完成计划百分之一百点六，轻工业部完成计划百分之一百零五点六。全国绝大部分省、市所属地方国营和地方公私合营重点企业十月份总产值计划完成的都比较好，但上海市、陕西省、广西省和内蒙古的地方国营和地方公私合营重点企业完成计划则较差。
    上述重点企业一至十月份累计，总产值比去年同期增长了百分之九点四。其中国营企业增长了百分之十一点二，地方国营企业增长了百分之十四，中央公私合营企业增长了百分之二点七，地方公私合营企业降低了百分之二点七。在中央各工业部中，电力工业部增长了百分之十六点四，煤炭工业部增长了百分之十五点二，石油工业部增长了百分之四十三点四，重工业部增长了百分之二十五点二，第一机械工业部增长了百分之十三点四，轻工业部增长了百分之四点四，纺织工业部则降低了百分之十五点二。
    上述重点企业三十种重要工业产品中，有电力、原煤、原油、生铁、钢、钢压延品、铜、硫酸、纯碱、硫铵、水泥、发电机、电动机、金属切削机床、双铧犁、棉纱、棉布、麻袋、汽车外胎、纸、糖、面粉、卷烟等二十三种产品完成了计划，胶鞋、食用植物油接近完成计划，其他产品完成计划也都在百分之九十以上。
    上述重点企业完成十月份劳动生产率计划百分之一百零六点四。中央各工业部十月份劳动生产率一般比去年同月都有了增长。其中电力工业部增长了百分之十一点六，煤炭工业部增长了百分之十四点六，石油工业部增长了百分之三十五，重工业部增长了百分之二十一点三，第一机械工业部增长了百分之二十八点四，轻工业部增长了百分之二十四，纺织工业部下降了百分之二点二。
    各工业部所属企业十月份完成生产计划比较好的，有北京电业局、唐山电业局、成都电厂、抚顺矿务局、鸡西矿务局、淮南矿务局、玉门矿务局、延长油矿、石景山钢铁厂、上海机床厂、天津自行车厂、哈尔滨滚珠轴承厂、上海国棉一厂、上海国棉二厂、上海国棉十五厂、青岛国棉六厂、石岘山造纸厂、山东造纸厂等许多企业。但也有一些企业完成计划是比较差的，如闸北水电公司、新疆石油公司、夏花煤矿、铜官山矿务局、盘古山钨矿、大连碱厂、沈阳通用机器厂、阿城仪表厂、佳木斯纺织厂、新疆七一棉纺织厂、辽西盐务管理处等企业。
