Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet to Welcome Danish Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 26th: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 26th to welcome the Danish Trade Delegation headed by Aksel Grunn.

Among those present were Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and committee members Xu Dixin, Meng Yongqian, Chen Hansheng, Lu Xuzhang, and the heads of various import and export corporations in China.

The Danish Envoy to China, Gregerse, was also invited to attend.

Nan Hanchen and Aksel Grunn both spoke at the banquet and raised their glasses to wish for the increasing development of trade exchanges, cultural exchanges, and mutual understanding and friendship between the two peoples of China and Denmark.

### 南汉宸设宴欢迎丹麦贸易代表团

1955-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　南汉宸设宴欢迎丹麦贸易代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸在二十六日晚设宴欢迎以阿克塞尔·格鲁恩为首的丹麦贸易代表团。
    出席作陪的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，委员许涤新、孟用潜、陈翰笙、卢绪章和中国进出口各专业公司的负责人。
    丹麦驻华公使格瑞杰生也应邀出席。
    南汉宸和阿克塞尔·格鲁恩都在会上讲了话，并且举杯互祝中丹两国的贸易往来、文化交流以及两国人民间的相互了解和友谊日益发展。
