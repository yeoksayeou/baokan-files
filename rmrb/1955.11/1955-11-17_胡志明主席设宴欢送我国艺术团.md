Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-11-17
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a banquet on the evening of November 15th to bid farewell to the Chinese Art Troupe, which is about to return home (the Ministry of Culture of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and the Vietnam Arts Association held a grand closing ceremony for the Chinese Art Troupe in Hanoi on the evening of the 14th. The Chinese Art Troupe gave a farewell performance).

Before the banquet, Chairman Ho Chi Minh awarded medals and gifts to the Chinese Art Troupe. Amidst warm applause, he said: The Chinese Art Troupe has performed for 750,000 people in the past period, which is a great achievement. But the even greater achievement is that you have used art to deepen the ever-growing friendship between our two peoples. Chairman Ho Chi Minh concluded by saying, please convey our heartfelt thanks and friendly greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese Government, and the Chinese brothers and sisters.

Then, Chairman Ho Chi Minh awarded the First Class Labor Medal to the Chinese Art Troupe, which was accepted by Ding Xilin, the head of the Chinese Art Troupe. Chairman Ho Chi Minh also handed over to Ding Xilin the gifts from the Vietnam Lao Dong Party, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the people to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Art Troupe. These gifts included a brocade banner embroidered with "Vietnam-China Solidarity," exquisite handicrafts, works of art, and woven items.

Ding Xilin spoke after receiving the medal and gifts. He said that during the performance in Vietnam, we received warm welcome and deep friendship everywhere, and we were asked to bring this friendship to the Chinese people. We guarantee to accomplish this glorious task. Ding Xilin also expressed his gratitude to Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Lao Dong Party, the Vietnamese government, various Vietnamese people's organizations, and literary and art workers for their concern and assistance to the work of the Chinese Art Troupe.

Attending the banquet and award ceremony were Truong Chinh, Pham Van Dong, Ton Duc Thang, and ministers of various government departments, as well as leaders of various democratic parties and mass organizations.

### 胡志明主席设宴欢送我国艺术团

1955-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席设宴欢送我国艺术团
    据新华社河内十六日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在十一月十五日晚间举行宴会，为即将回国的中国艺术团送行（按越南民主共和国政府文化部、越南祖国战线中央委员会、越中友好协会和越南文艺会曾于十四日晚间在河内为中国艺术团举行了盛大的闭幕式。中国艺术团作了告别演出）。
    在宴会前，胡志明主席向中国艺术团授勋和赠礼。他在热烈的掌声中说：中国艺术团在过去的时间里，为七十五万人作了演出，这是一个很大的成绩。但是更伟大的成绩是你们用艺术来加深了我们两国人民日益发展中的友谊。胡志明主席最后说，请把我们衷心的感谢和友谊的敬礼，转告毛泽东主席、中国共产党、中国政府和中国的兄弟姐妹们。
    接着，胡志明主席把一级劳动勋章授给中国艺术团，由中国艺术团团长丁西林接受。胡志明主席同时把越南劳动党、越南民主共和国政府和人民送给毛泽东主席和中国艺术团的礼品交给丁西林。在这些礼品中有绣着“越中团结”的锦旗、精美的工艺品、美术品和编织品等。
    丁西林在接受勋章和礼品后讲话。他说，在越南演出期间，我们到处受到热烈的欢迎和深厚的友情，并且要我们把这种友情带给中国人民。我们保证完成这个光荣的任务。丁西林并且向关怀和帮助中国艺术团工作的胡志明主席、越南劳动党中央、越南政府、越南各人民团体和文艺工作者表示感谢。
    出席宴会和授勋仪式的有长征，范文同，孙德胜和政府各部部长，各民主党派、各群众团体的负责人。
