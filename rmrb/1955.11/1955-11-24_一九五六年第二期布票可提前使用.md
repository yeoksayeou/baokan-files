Title: The Second Issue of 1956 Cloth Coupons Can Be Used in Advance
Author:
Date: 1955-11-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Ministry of Commerce has decided that, in order to meet the needs of the people of the whole country, especially the broad masses of peasants, for cotton cloth, from November 25th, the second issue of cloth coupons for the second planned supply year (i.e., cloth coupons originally scheduled for use from May 1st to August 31st, 1956) can be used in advance and simultaneously with the first issue of cloth coupons.

### 一九五六年第二期布票可提前使用

1955-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　一九五六年第二期布票可提前使用
    新华社二十三日讯　商业部为了适应全国人民特别是广大农民对棉布的需要，决定从十一月二十五日起，第二个计划供应年度的第二期布票（即原规定自一九五六年五月一日到八月三十一日使用的布票），可以提前和第一期布票同时使用。
