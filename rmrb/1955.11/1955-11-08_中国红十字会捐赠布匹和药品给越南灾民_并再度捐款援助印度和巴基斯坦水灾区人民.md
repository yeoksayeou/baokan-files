Title: The Red Cross Society of China Donates Cloth and Medicine to Vietnamese Disaster Victims; Resumes Donations to Aid Flood Victims in India and Pakistan

Author:

Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. To assist the victims of the typhoon disaster in the Haiphong area of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Red Cross Society of China donated cloth and medicine worth 200,000 yuan to the Red Cross Society of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The above materials are being actively prepared for shipment to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Some areas of India and Pakistan have recently experienced further flooding. On November 3, the Red Cross Society of China again donated 50,000 yuan each to the Indian Prime Minister's National Relief Fund and the Pakistan Red Cross Society as aid to the people in the flood-stricken areas of the two countries. Both donations have been telegraphed to Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, and Han Nianlong, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, for their respective transfers.

### 中国红十字会捐赠布匹和药品给越南灾民  并再度捐款援助印度和巴基斯坦水灾区人民

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会捐赠布匹和药品给越南灾民
    并再度捐款援助印度和巴基斯坦水灾区人民
    新华社七日讯　为了援助越南民主共和国海防地区遭受台风灾害的灾民，中国红十字会捐赠给越南民主共和国红十字会价值人民币二十万元的布匹和药品。上项物资正在积极准备运往越南民主共和国。
    新华社七日讯　印度和巴基斯坦的一些地区最近又发生水灾。中国红十字会在十一月三日再度捐赠印度总理全国救济基金会和巴基斯坦红十字会人民币各五万元，作为对上述两国水灾区人民的援助。两项捐款已经分别电请中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤和中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙代为转交。
