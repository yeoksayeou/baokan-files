Title: Goan Portuguese Authorities Sentence Goa National Congress Chairman to Ten Years of Hard Labor
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 4

　　Goan Portuguese Authorities
　　Sentence Goa National Congress Chairman to Ten Years of Hard Labor
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 10th -- According to a press release from the Indian News Service on the 9th, the Goan Portuguese military tribunal has sentenced Mrs. Joshi, chairman of the Goa National Congress, to ten years of hard labor and imposed a fine.
    Mrs. Joshi entered Goa in April of this year to carry out the struggle to liberate Goa.

### 果阿葡萄牙当局　判果阿国大党主席十年苦役

1955-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　果阿葡萄牙当局
　　判果阿国大党主席十年苦役
    新华社新德里十日讯　据印度新闻处九日消息，果阿葡萄牙军事法庭已经判处果阿国大党主席乔希夫人十年苦役，并且判处了罚款。
    乔希夫人是在今年四月间进入果阿进行解放果阿的斗争的。
