Title: The First Congress of Activists of the PLA Engineer Corps Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st. The First Congress of Activists of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Engineer Corps opened in Beijing on the 21st.

Over the past six years, the Engineer Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has produced a large number of people's heroes and advanced individuals in the execution of combat, national defense construction, and national economic construction tasks. Furthermore, eight hundred and twenty-one units have been awarded collective merit citations. A total of two hundred and forty-nine activists and representatives of advanced units attended the meeting. Representatives of the Engineer Corps of the Chinese People's Volunteers were also invited to attend the conference.

The opening ceremony of the meeting began at 9:00 a.m. Lieutenant General Hu Qicai, Deputy Commander of the Engineer Corps, delivered the opening speech. He pointed out that the task of this meeting is to promote the fine traditions of the Engineer Corps in performing various tasks and to exchange various advanced experiences so as to make more contributions to the glorious task of liberating Taiwan and defending the socialist construction of the motherland.

General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, both attended the meeting and gave instructions.

General Chen Shiju, Commander of the Engineer Corps, spoke at the opening ceremony. In his speech, he encouraged the representatives to be modest and prudent, work diligently, and make constant progress.

Representatives from various departments of the General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army attended the opening ceremony. Guests from various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and various agencies in Beijing also attended the opening ceremony.

### 解放军工程兵首届积极分子会议在京开幕

1955-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放军工程兵首届积极分子会议在京开幕
    据新华社二十一日讯　中国人民解放军工程兵首届积极分子会议二十一日在北京开幕。
    中国人民解放军工程兵各部队六年来在执行战斗、国防建设以及国家经济建设的各项任务中，曾经涌现出大批人民功臣和先进人物，还有八百二十一个单位立了集体功。出席会议的积极分子和先进单位的代表共有二百四十九人。中国人民志愿军工程兵代表也应邀出席了大会。
    会议开幕式在上午九时开始举行。工程兵副司令员胡奇才中将致开幕词。他指出，这次会议的任务就是要发扬工程兵在执行各项任务中的优良作风，交流各种先进经验，以便在解放台湾、保卫祖国社会主义建设的光荣任务中作出更多的贡献。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将和总政治部副主任萧华上将都到会作了指示。
    工程兵司令员陈士榘上将在开幕式中讲了话，他在讲话中勉励到会的代表们戒骄戒躁，勤勤恳恳，不断前进。
    中国人民解放军总部各部派代表出席了开幕式。参加开幕式的还有中国人民解放军各军种、各兵种和中华全国总工会、北京市各机关的来宾。
