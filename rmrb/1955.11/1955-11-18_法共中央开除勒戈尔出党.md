Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Expels Lecoeur from the Party
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 4

　　French Communist Party Central Committee Expels Lecoeur from the Party
    Xinhua News Agency, November 17th, TASS, Paris, November 16th: It was previously reported that in January 1955, the Central Committee of the French Communist Party suspended Auguste Lecoeur's party membership for one year.

    According to "L'Humanité," the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, in view of Lecoeur's subsequent behavior as a complete traitor and enemy of the Communist Party and the working class, has decided to finally expel him from the party.

### 法共中央开除勒戈尔出党

1955-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　法共中央开除勒戈尔出党
    新华社十七日讯　塔斯社巴黎十六日讯：前已报道，一九五五年一月法国共产党中央委员会曾经停止奥古斯特·勒戈尔党籍一年。
    据“人道报”报道，法国共产党中央委员会鉴于勒戈尔在那以后的表现完全成了共产党和工人阶级的叛徒和敌人，因此决定把他最后开除出党。
