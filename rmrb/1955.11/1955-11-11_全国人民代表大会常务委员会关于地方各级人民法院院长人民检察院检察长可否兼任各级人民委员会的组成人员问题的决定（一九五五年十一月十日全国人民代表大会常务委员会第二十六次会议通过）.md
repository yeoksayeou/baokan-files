Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Regarding Whether Presidents of Local People's Courts and Procurators-General of People's Procuratorates May Concurrently Serve as Members of People's Committees at Various Levels (Adopted at the 26th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 10, 1955)
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 1

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its 26th meeting on November 10, 1955, discussed the questions raised by the Fujian Provincial People's Committee and the Hubei Provincial People's Committee regarding whether the presidents of local people's courts and the procurators-general of people's procuratorates could concurrently serve as members of people's committees at various levels, and decided: Presidents of local people's courts and procurators-general of people's procuratorates shall not hold any concurrent positions on people's committees at any level.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于地方各级人民法院院长人民检察院检察长可否兼任各级人民委员会的组成人员问题的决定（一九五五年十一月十日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十六次会议通过）

1955-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于地方各级人民法院院长人民检察院检察长可否兼任各级人民委员会的组成人员问题的决定
        （一九五五年十一月十日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十六次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五五年十一月十日第二十六次会议讨论了福建省人民委员会、湖北省人民委员会提出的关于地方各级人民法院院长、人民检察院检察长是否可以兼任各级人民委员会的委员的问题，决定：地方各级人民法院院长和人民检察院检察长一概不能在各级人民委员会兼任职务。
