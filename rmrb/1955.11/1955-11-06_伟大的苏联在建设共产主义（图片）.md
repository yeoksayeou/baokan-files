The Great Soviet Union is Building Communism

    Looking out over the scenery of Moscow from the Ivan the Great Bell Tower in the Kremlin.
    Photo by A. Sergeyev (TASS dispatch)
    The immense scale of the construction of the world's largest hydroelectric power station – the Soviet Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Power Station project. Photo by A. Batanov (TASS dispatch)
    The Soviet Union is actively putting atomic energy to work for peaceful progress. The synchrocyclotron installed at the Institute of Nuclear Problems of the Soviet Academy of Sciences is a device for accelerating charged particles to near the speed of light. The highest energy of protons obtained on this accelerator is 700 million electron volts. The equipment of the synchrocyclotron marks a significant achievement of the Soviet Union in atomic energy research.
  　Photo by Helongshi, originally published in "Soviet Union" Pictorial
    At the Institute of Nuclear Problems of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, a specialized oscilloscope is installed to monitor the work of the synchrocyclotron. Photo by Helongshi, originally published in "Soviet Union" Pictorial. The Soviet Union is using electric cultivation more and more in agricultural production. This is the Belyarsk District of Sverdlovsk Oblast using electric tractors to cultivate land. Photo by A. Grakhov (TASS dispatch)
    The astronomical observation field of the Moscow Planetarium.
    Photos by V. Masyukov and E. Rakhmanov (TASS dispatch)
