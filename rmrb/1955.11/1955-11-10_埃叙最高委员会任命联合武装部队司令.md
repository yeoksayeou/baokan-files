Title: Egyptian-Syrian Supreme Council Appoints Commander of Joint Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 9th: It was reported from Cairo that Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser and Syrian Prime Minister Ghazi exchanged the instruments of ratification for the recently signed defense treaty between the two countries in Cairo on the 8th. They also discussed issues related to the implementation of this treaty.
    On the same day, the Supreme Council established under this treaty held its first meeting. It was announced after the meeting that the Commander-in-Chief of the Egyptian Armed Forces, Abdel Hakim Amer, had been appointed as the Supreme Commander of the joint armed forces of Egypt and Syria.

### 埃叙最高委员会任命联合武装部队司令

1955-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    埃叙最高委员会任命联合武装部队司令
    新华社九日讯　开罗消息：埃及总理纳赛尔和叙利亚总理加齐八日在开罗交换了两国政府最近签订的防御条约的批准书。他们并且就履行这个条约的问题进行讨论。
    同一天，根据这个条约而设立的最高委员会举行第一次会议。会后宣布，埃及武装部队总司令阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔已被任命为埃及和叙利亚联合武装部队的最高司令。
