Title: President Pieck Receives Delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th (TASS): President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic received the delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the USSR at the Niederschönhausen Palace on the 26th.

　　Present at the reception were Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Grotewohl, and other leaders of the German Democratic Republic.

　　Patolichev, head of the delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, said in his speech that he first, on behalf of the delegation and all the Soviet people, congratulated President Pieck on the 50th anniversary of his election as a member of the Bremen City Council, and expressed his respect for Pieck's lifelong active struggle for the interests of the German working class and working people, and his selfless service to the cause of the German people.

　　Pieck said in his reply: Firm and friendly relations have already been established between the German Democratic Republic and the Soviet Union. The delegation's visit will undoubtedly promote the development of comprehensive cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and Germany. Pieck said that we are jointly struggling against the dangerous policy of restoring German militarism. The delegation's visit will strengthen our common will and determination: to do everything possible to establish a collective security system, thereby guaranteeing a stable peace for the people of all countries. In this regard, the German Democratic Republic is sincerely willing to make its contribution to safeguarding European peace, which is a necessary prerequisite for the restoration of German unity.

### 皮克总统接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团

1955-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　皮克总统接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团
    据新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社柏林二十六日讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克二十六日在尼德申豪森宫接见了苏联最高苏维埃代表团。
    接见时在座的有德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、总理格罗提渥和德意志民主共和国其他领导人员。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长帕托利切夫在致词中首先代表代表团和全体苏联人民祝贺皮克总统当选不来梅市议会议员五十周年，并且对皮克终身为德意志工人阶级和劳动人民的利益积极斗争，为德国人民的事业忘我服务表示敬意。
    皮克在答词中说：牢固和友好的关系已经在德意志民主共和国和苏联之间建立起来了。代表团的访问无疑将会促进苏德两国人民之间的全面合作的发展。皮克说，我们正在对恢复德国军国主义的危险政策进行共同的斗争。代表团的访问将加强我们的这种共同意志和决心：竭尽全力来建立一个集体安全体系，从而为各国人民保证一个稳定的和平。在这方面，德意志民主共和国真诚地愿意为保障欧洲和平作出它的贡献，这是恢复德国统一的必要的先决条件。
