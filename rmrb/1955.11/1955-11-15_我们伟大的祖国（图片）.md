    Our Great Motherland
    The Nanwan Reservoir in the upper reaches of the Huai River was successfully completed on November 5th, providing a reliable guarantee for 300,000 mu of farmland on both sides of the Shi River. This is the scene of the water diversion tunnel gate opening to discharge water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮河上游的南湾水库已在十一月五日胜利建成，师河两岸的三十万亩农田有了可靠的保障。这是输水隧道闸门开放泄水的情形。
