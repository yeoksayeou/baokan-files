Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Soviet-style OKC sprayer-duster, recently successfully trial-produced by the Shanghai Joint State-Private Xin Yi Machinery Factory, can exterminate insects in different environments such as forest protection zones, orchards, and farmland. It can spray twenty hectares of farmland in eight hours. The newly made sprayer-duster is being tested in the vegetable fields of the state-owned Huacao Farm.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海公私合营信义机器厂最近试制成功的仿苏式OKC喷雾喷粉两用器，能在护林区、果园和农田等不同环境下灭虫，八小时可喷二十公顷农田。新制成的喷雾喷粉两用器正在国营华漕农场菜田里进行试验。
