Title: East German Observer Delegation to the Four Foreign Ministers Conference Hands Over Statement on the Question of German Reunification to the Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-11-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 3rd: The observer delegation of the German Democratic Republic to the Four Foreign Ministers Conference in Geneva handed over the statement of the government of the German Democratic Republic on the question of German reunification (full text in today's issue, pages 3 and 4) to the Four Foreign Ministers Conference on November 1st.

### 民主德国派到四外长会议观察员代表团  把关于重新统一德国问题的声明交给会议

1955-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国派到四外长会议观察员代表团
    把关于重新统一德国问题的声明交给会议
    据新华社日内瓦三日电　德意志民主共和国派赴日内瓦的四国外交部长会议观察员代表团在十一月一日把德意志民主共和国政府关于重新统一德国问题的声明（全文见今日本报三、四版）交给四国外交部长会议。
