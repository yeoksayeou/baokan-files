Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This year, most cotton-producing areas across the country have had good harvests. The broad masses of farmers are actively selling cotton to the state to support socialist construction. The Agricultural Production Cooperative of Dachenzhuang Village, Xingtai County, Hebei Province, plans to sell 300,000 jin of seed cotton to the state.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年全国大多数产棉区收成良好，广大农民积极把棉花卖给国家，支援社会主义建设。河北省邢台县大陈庄村农业生产合作社计划卖给国家三十万斤籽棉。
