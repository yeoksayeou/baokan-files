Title: Indonesian Parties Launch Constituent Assembly Election Campaigns
Author:
Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 23rd - As the date of the Constituent Assembly election, scheduled for December 15th, draws nearer, Indonesian parties are intensifying their campaign activities.
　　The Indonesian Constituent Assembly election will elect 520 members. The task of the Constituent Assembly is to formulate the first constitution of the Republic of Indonesia and to elect the President and Vice-President of the Republic.
　　The Indonesian National Party recently published a booklet for the Constituent Assembly election explaining its plans for the future system and foundation of the Indonesian state. The booklet states that the Indonesian National Party's Popular Front will strive to realize and defend a unified republic based on the Five Principles (belief in God, humanitarianism, nationalism, democracy, social justice).
　　The "People's Daily" in Jakarta published an article on the 18th by the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party entitled: "The Indonesian Communist Party Conducts Campaign for the Constituent Assembly Election." This article explains the basic position of the Indonesian Communist Party on the Constituent Assembly election, as well as the proposals for which it will fight in the Constituent Assembly. The article states that the Indonesian Communist Party calls on all people to fight for the victory of the Indonesian Communist Party and other democratic parties.
　　Indonesian President Sukarno recently delivered a speech at a mass rally convened by the All-Indonesia People's Congress in Bandung, pointing out the importance of national unity in the Constituent Assembly election and calling on voters to elect parties that are fighting for the complete independence of the country.
　　Meanwhile, the Indonesian Muslim Scholars Association and other parties have also launched campaign activities.
　　Indonesian Minister of Information, Makmur, recently announced that measures have been taken to ensure that the Constituent Assembly election proceeds as smoothly as possible.

### 印度尼西亚各党派　展开制宪议会选举竞选活动

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚各党派
　　展开制宪议会选举竞选活动
    新华社雅加达二十三日电　随着预定在十二月十五日举行的制宪议会选举的日期越来越近，印度尼西亚各党派正加紧展开竞选活动。
    印度尼西亚制宪议会选举将选出五百二十名议员。制宪议会的任务是制定印度尼西亚共和国的第一部宪法，并且选举共和国的总统和副总统。
    印度尼西亚国民党最近为制宪议会选举发表了一本说明它对印度尼西亚国家将来的制度和基础的计划的小册子。这本小册子说，印度尼西亚国民党的平民阵线将为实现和保卫以五基（神道、人道主义、民族主义、民主、社会公道）为基础的统一的共和国而奋斗。
    雅加达“人民日报”十八日发表了印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会鼓动和宣传部的一篇文章，题目是：“印度尼西亚共产党为制宪议会选举进行竞选运动”。这篇文章说明了印度尼西亚共产党对制宪议会选举的基本立场，以及它在制宪议会中将要为之进行斗争的各项主张。文章说，印度尼西亚共产党号召全体人民为印度尼西亚共产党和其他民主党派的胜利而进行斗争。
    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺最近在全印度尼西亚人民代表大会在万隆召集的群众大会上发表演说，指出了在制宪议会选举中民族团结的重要性，并且号召选民选举为国家的完全独立而斗争的政党。
    同时，印度尼西亚回教师联合会和其他党派也展开了竞选活动。
    印度尼西亚情报部长马克慕尔最近宣布，已经采取措施，以便尽可能顺利地进行制宪议会选举。
