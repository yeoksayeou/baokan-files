Title: Bulgaria and Burma Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 18th: Newspapers report that after negotiations, the governments of Bulgaria and Burma have reached an agreement to establish diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Union of Burma, and to exchange diplomatic representatives at the level of ministers.

### 保加利亚和缅甸建立外交关系

1955-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚和缅甸建立外交关系
    新华社十九日讯　塔斯社索非亚十八日讯：报纸报道，保加利亚和缅甸两国政府经过谈判以后达成协议，决定在保加利亚人民共和国和缅甸联邦之间建立外交关系，并且互派公使级外交代表。
