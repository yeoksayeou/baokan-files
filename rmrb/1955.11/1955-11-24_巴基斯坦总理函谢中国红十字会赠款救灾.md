Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Thanks Chinese Red Cross for Donation for Disaster Relief
Author:
Date: 1955-11-24
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 22nd. Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali wrote a letter on the 11th to Han Nianlong, the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, thanking the Chinese Red Cross for donating funds to relieve Pakistani flood victims. The letter stated that he "expresses deep gratitude for the generous donation and heartfelt sympathy of the Chinese Red Cross."

The donation from the Chinese Red Cross amounted to 50,000 yuan, equivalent to 95,785 rupees. Previously, in September of this year, the Chinese Red Cross had already donated 50,000 yuan to the Pakistan Red Cross to relieve Pakistani flood victims.

### 巴基斯坦总理函谢中国红十字会赠款救灾

1955-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦总理函谢中国红十字会赠款救灾
    新华社二十二日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里在十一日写信给中国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙，感谢中国红十字会捐款救济巴基斯坦水灾灾民。信中说，他“对中国红十字会的慷慨捐赠和衷心同情表示深切的感谢”。
    中国红十字会捐赠的款项为五万元人民币，折合九万五千七百八十五卢比。在这以前，中国红十字会在今年九月间已经捐赠人民币五万元给巴基斯坦红十字会，救济巴基斯坦水灾灾民。
