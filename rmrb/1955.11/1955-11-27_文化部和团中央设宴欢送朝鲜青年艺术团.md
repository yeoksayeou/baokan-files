Title: Ministry of Culture and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Host Banquet to See Off Korean Youth Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a banquet on the evening of the 26th to see off the Korean Youth Art Troupe led by Cui Wenqi. Li Zhongyi, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, said in his speech at the banquet: The Korean Youth Art Troupe has been visiting and performing in various parts of China for nearly two months and is about to return home. On the eve of departure, our hearts are filled with the warmth of friendship and reluctance. We warmly congratulate the art troupe on the success of this visit and performance, and express our heartfelt thanks to the art troupe for its contribution to strengthening the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Korea and further promoting cultural exchanges between the two countries.

    Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, then said: The visit and performance of the Korean Youth Art Troupe in our country after participating in the Warsaw Youth Festival has further strengthened the close friendship between the people and youth of China and Korea.

    Cui Wenqi, head of the art troupe, gave a speech in reply, saying: The Korean Youth Art Troupe has successfully concluded its friendly visit and performance in China for more than 50 days with the warm welcome and love of the fraternal Chinese people. We deeply know that all our achievements are inseparable from the kind help of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and inseparable from the friendship forged by the blood of the people of Korea and China.

    Among those present at tonight's banquet were Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, Xia Yan and Chen Kehan, Vice Ministers, Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, heads of various departments of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, as well as literary and art figures, young literary and art workers, and representatives of youth and students, totaling more than 200 people.

    After the banquet, the Korean Youth Art Troupe presented gifts to Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, the Ministry of Culture, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Department of Oriental Languages of Peking University. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, accepted them on their behalf.

### 文化部和团中央设宴欢送朝鲜青年艺术团

1955-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　文化部和团中央设宴欢送朝鲜青年艺术团
    据新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会二十六日晚举行宴会，欢送以崔文岐为首的朝鲜青年艺术团。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞李忠翼也应邀出席了宴会。
    文化部代理部长钱俊瑞在宴会上致词说：朝鲜青年艺术团在中国各地访问演出了将近两个月，现在快要回国了。在临别的前夕，我们的内心是充满着友谊的温暖和依恋的心情。我们热诚地祝贺艺术团这次访问演出的成功，并且对艺术团为加强中朝两国人民的兄弟友谊和进一步促进中朝两国的文化交流所作的贡献表示衷心的感谢。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅接着致词说：朝鲜青年艺术团在参加了华沙青年联欢节之后在我国所进行的访问演出，更进一步地密切了中朝两国人民和青年之间的亲密友谊。
    艺术团团长崔文岐致词答谢，他说：朝鲜青年艺术团在兄弟般的中国人民的热烈欢迎和爱戴下，圆满地结束了在中国的历时五十多天的友好访问演出。我们深知，我们的一切成就是同中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的亲切帮助分不开的，是同朝中两国人民用鲜血结成的友谊分不开的。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理彭德怀，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长夏衍、陈克寒，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，北京市副市长吴晗，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会各部门和中华全国民主青年联合会的负责人，以及文艺界人士、青年文艺工作者和青年、学生代表共二百多人。
    宴会以后，朝鲜青年艺术团向国务院总理周恩来、副总理彭德怀以及文化部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和北京大学东方语系献礼品。由文化部代理部长钱俊瑞代为接受。
