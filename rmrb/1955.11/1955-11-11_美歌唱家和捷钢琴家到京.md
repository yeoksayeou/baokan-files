Title: American Singer and Czech Pianist Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 3

　　American Singer and Czech Pianist Arrive in Beijing

　　American famous black singer Aubrey Pankey and his wife, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit and perform in China, arrived in Beijing on the 8th. Czech female pianist Marie Knopková also arrived at the same time. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 美歌唱家和捷钢琴家到京

1955-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

　  美歌唱家和捷钢琴家到京
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问演出的美国著名黑人歌唱家奥布雷·潘基同他的夫人在八日下午到达北京。同时到达的还有捷克斯洛伐克女钢琴家玛丽·克诺普科娃。　（新华社）
