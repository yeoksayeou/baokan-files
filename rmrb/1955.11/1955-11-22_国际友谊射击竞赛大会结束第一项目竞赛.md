Title: International Friendship Shooting Competition Concludes First Event

Author:

Date: 1955-11-22
Page: 3

　　International Friendship Shooting Competition Concludes First Event
Xinhua News Agency

　　The 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition held the Men's Free Rifle Small-Bore 50-meter Standing Position shooting event on the 21st. With this, the first event competition has come to a complete end.

　　According to the preliminary statistics of the competition's judging committee, after three days of competition in the first event, the Soviet shooting team won with 5,770 rings. The scores of the other national shooting teams are: Romania 5,646 rings, Bulgaria 5,596 rings, Czechoslovakia 5,532 rings, China 5,503 rings, Poland 5,496 rings, North Korea 5,011 rings, and Mongolia 4,860 rings.

　　According to the regulations of the competition's judging committee, in each shooting event of the first event, each national shooting team can have reserve athletes to replace the official team members, but the total score of the first event of each national shooting team is still calculated according to the total score of the five athletes determined in advance.

　　The top three individual total score winners in the first event are all Soviet athletes. Their scores are: Shamburkin with 1,160 rings, Perebelin with 1,157 rings, and Gubernatorov with 1,157 rings.

　　In today's standing position shooting, the scores of the national shooting teams are: Soviet Union 1,828 rings, Romania 1,759 rings, Bulgaria 1,747 rings, Czechoslovakia 1,731 rings, China 1,695 rings, Poland 1,657 rings, North Korea 1,430 rings, and Mongolia 1,376 rings.

　　The top three individual total scores in standing position shooting are also Soviet athletes Shamburkin, Gubernatorov, and Perebelin. Their scores are 371 rings, 371 rings, and 366 rings.

　　The preliminary scores of the kneeling position shooting competition held on the 20th have been officially assessed by the competition's judging committee. The winners received prizes from Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the Organizing Committee, on behalf of the Organizing Committee at noon today.

　　On the mornings and afternoons of the 22nd and 23rd, the third event, the Men's Free Pistol Small-Bore 25-meter Silhouette Target Rapid Fire Competition, will be held respectively.

### 国际友谊射击竞赛大会结束第一项目竞赛

1955-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际友谊射击竞赛大会结束第一项目竞赛
    一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会在二十一日进行了男子自选小口径步枪五十公尺立姿射击。至此，第一项目竞赛已全部结束。
    据大会裁判委员会初步统计，第一项目经过三天竞赛，苏联射击队以五千七百七十环获胜。其他各国射击队的成绩是：罗马尼亚五千六百四十六环，保加利亚五千五百九十六环，捷克斯洛伐克五千五百三十二环，中国五千五百零三环，波兰五千四百九十六环，朝鲜五千零一十一环，蒙古四千八百六十环。
    根据大会裁判委员会的规定，在第一项目每种姿势的射击竞赛中，各国射击队可以有预备运动员替换正式队员，但是各国射击队第一项目的总成绩仍按事先确定的五名运动员的总成绩计算。
    在第一项目中个人总分前三名的获得者都是苏联运动员。他们的成绩是：尚布尔金得一千一百六十环，佩列别林得一千一百五十七环，古别尔纳托罗夫得一千一百五十七环。
    今天的立姿射击中，各国射击代表队的成绩是：苏联一千八百二十八环，罗马尼亚一千七百五十九环，保加利亚一千七百四十七环，捷克斯洛伐克一千七百三十一环，中国一千六百九十五环，波兰一千六百五十七环，朝鲜一千四百三十环，蒙古一千三百七十六环。
    立姿射击的个人总分前三名也是苏联运动员尚布尔金、古别尔纳托罗夫和佩列别林。他们的成绩是三百七十一环、三百七十一环和三百六十六环。
    二十日进行的跪姿射击竞赛的初步成绩已经大会裁判委员会评定为正式成绩。优胜者在今天中午接受了大会组织委员会副主席荣高棠代表组织委员会发给的奖品。
    二十二日、二十三日两天的上午和下午，将分别进行第三项目男子自选小口径手枪人像靶二十五公尺立姿速射竞赛。　（新华社）
