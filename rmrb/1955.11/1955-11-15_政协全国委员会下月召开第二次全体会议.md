Title: CPPCC National Committee to Convene Second Plenary Session Next Month
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its tenth meeting on the 14th. The meeting decided to convene the Second Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in late December 1955, and discussed relevant issues concerning the convening of this meeting.

### 政协全国委员会下月召开第二次全体会议

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　政协全国委员会下月召开第二次全体会议
　　新华社十四日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在十四日举行第十次会议。会议决定在一九五五年十二月下旬召开中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议，并且对召开这次会议的有关问题进行了讨论。
