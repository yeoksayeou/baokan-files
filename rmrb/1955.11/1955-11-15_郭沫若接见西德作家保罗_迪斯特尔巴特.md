Title: Guo Moruo Receives West German Writer Paul Distelbarth
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th – Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, received the famous West German writer Paul Distelbarth and his son, Frank Distelbarth, on the evening of the 14th. They had been invited by the China Peace Committee and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries to visit China, and a banquet was held.

Present at the reception were prominent Chinese writers, professors, and journalists Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Hou Wailu, Feng Zhi, Chen Baichen, Yang Shuo, Wu Wentao, Hu Zhongchi, as well as Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Wu Huazhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, and others.

Paul Distelbarth arrived in Beijing with his son on October 14th. Over the past month, they have visited Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places. On November 15th, they will leave Beijing for the Northeast for sightseeing, and then return to their country from Shenyang.

### 郭沫若接见西德作家保罗·迪斯特尔巴特

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若接见西德作家保罗·迪斯特尔巴特
    新华社十四日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在十四日晚接见了应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来中国访问的西德名作家保罗·迪斯特尔巴特和他的儿子弗兰克·迪斯特尔巴特，并且举行了宴会。
    接见时在座的，有中国著名作家、教授和新闻界人士郑振铎、夏衍、侯外庐、冯至、陈白尘、杨朔、吴文焘、胡仲持及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长吴化之等人。
    保罗·迪斯特尔巴特十月十四日同他的儿子到北京。一个月来他们曾在北京、郑州、武汉、南京、上海、杭州等地参观访问。十一月十五日他们将离京赴东北参观，然后自沈阳回国。
