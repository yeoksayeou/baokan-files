Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Visit Bangalore
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 4

　　Bulganin and Khrushchev Visit Bangalore
    Xinhua News Agency, Bangalore dispatch from TASS, November 27th: After visiting Pune, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, arrived in Bangalore, the capital of Mysore State in South India, on the morning of the 26th by plane from Pune.
    The Chief Minister of Mysore State, Hanumanthayya, the Speakers of the State Legislative Council and the Legislative Assembly, and state government officials welcomed Bulganin, Khrushchev, and their entourage at the airport and escorted them to their residence. Citizens lined the streets to warmly welcome the Soviet guests as they passed through the city on their way to their residence.
    In the afternoon of the same day, Khrushchev and Bulganin visited the Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore and toured the various departments of the institute. Professor K. Srinivasan, the director of the institute, and Khrushchev each delivered brief speeches to the many guests present. Afterwards, Bulganin and Khrushchev visited the Machine Tool and Tool Factory in Bangalore.
    Later in the day, the citizens of Bangalore held a grand rally to welcome the Soviet guests. Participating in the rally were the Governor of Mysore State and other representatives of the local government, as well as nearly 200,000 citizens of Bangalore.
    In response to the welcome speech by the Mayor of Bangalore, Khrushchev delivered a lengthy and vivid speech on international relations. This speech aroused great interest among those present.
    In the evening, Bangalore was brightly lit. Citizens held a enthusiastic city-wide parade to celebrate the friendship between India and the Soviet Union. According to local residents, such a grand occasion was unprecedented in Bangalore.
    Finally, the Soviet guests attended a South Indian folk dance performance hosted by the Chief Minister of Mysore State. At the end of the performance, the guests received gifts, and Chief Minister K. Hanumanthayya and Bulganin exchanged speeches.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问班加罗尔

1955-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问班加罗尔
    据新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社班加罗尔讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫在访问浦那以后，已于二十六日上午从浦那乘飞机到达南印度卖索尔邦的首府班加罗尔。
    卖索尔邦首席部长哈努曼塔亚和邦立法会议和立法院的议长们和邦政府官员等人到机场欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫及他们的随行人员，并且陪送他们到住所。苏联贵宾通过城市走向住所的时候，市民们夹道热烈欢迎。
    当天下午，赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁访问了班加罗尔市的印度科学研究所，参观了这个研究所的各部门。研究所所长克·斯里尼瓦桑教授和赫鲁晓夫对在场的很多客人先后作了简短的讲话。然后，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问了班加罗尔市的机床和工具工厂。
    较晚的时候班加罗尔市民举行了欢迎苏联贵宾的大会。参加大会的有卖索尔邦长和地方政府的其他代表以及班加罗尔的市民将近二十万人。
    赫鲁晓夫在回答班加罗尔市长的欢迎词的时候就国际关系问题作了长篇的生动的演说。这篇演说引起了与会人士很大的兴趣。
    晚间，班加罗尔市到处灯火辉煌。市民们为庆祝印度和苏联的友谊举行了热烈的全市人民大游行。据当地居民说，这种盛况是班加罗尔市从来不曾有过的。
    苏联贵宾最后出席观看了卖索尔邦首席部长举办的南印度民间舞蹈演出会。在演出结束的时候，贵宾们接受了献礼，首席部长克·哈努曼塔亚和布尔加宁曾相互致词。
