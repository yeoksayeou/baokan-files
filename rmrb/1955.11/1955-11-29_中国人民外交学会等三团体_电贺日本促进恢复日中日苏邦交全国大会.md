Title: Three Organizations, Including the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Send Congratulatory Telegrams to the National Conference for Promoting the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries sent telegrams on November 27th to the National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, congratulating them on the National Conference for Promoting the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, which is scheduled to be held in Tokyo on November 28th and 29th.

　　The telegram from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs stated: "We are delighted to learn that your council is holding a national conference. The Chinese people warmly support your movement to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union. The normalization of relations between China and Japan is not only the common aspiration of the two peoples, but also an important factor in ensuring peace in the Far East and Asia. We firmly believe that your nationwide movement will create favorable conditions for the normalization of Sino-Japanese relations, and will draw greater attention in Japan to the urgency of the rapid restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Japan."

　　The telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace stated: "The friendly relations between the Chinese and Japanese people are growing day by day. We firmly believe that your conference will make an important contribution to promoting the normalization of relations between China and Japan and the early restoration of diplomatic relations, so as to powerfully defend peace in the East and Asia."

　　The telegram from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries stated: "Your meeting reflects the firm will of the broad masses of the Japanese people demanding that the Japanese government quickly restore normal relations with China and the Soviet Union, which will receive the deep sympathy and support of the Chinese people."

### 中国人民外交学会等三团体　电贺日本促进恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民外交学会等三团体
　　电贺日本促进恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会
    新华社二十八日讯　中国人民外交学会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会十一月二十七日分别打电报给日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议，祝贺订于十一月二十八日和二十九日在东京召开的促进恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会。
    中国人民外交学会的电报中说：“欣悉贵会举行全国性大会，中国人民热烈地支持你们为促进日中、日苏恢复邦交的运动。中日两国关系的正常化，不仅是两国人民的共同愿望，而且是保障远东和亚洲和平的一个重要因素。我们深信，你们正在开展的全国性运动将为中日关系正常化创造良好的条件，并且对中日两国迅速恢复邦交的迫切性将在日本引起更大的注意。”
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的电报中说：“中日两国人民的友好关系正在日益增进，我们深信贵大会必能对促进中日两国关系的正常化和邦交的早日恢复作出重要的贡献，来有力地保卫东方和亚洲的和平。”
    中国人民对外文化协会的电报中说：“你们的会议反映着广大日本人民要求日本政府迅速恢复与中国、苏联的关系正常化的坚决意志，它将获得中国人民深切的同情和支持。”
