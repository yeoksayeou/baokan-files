Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Congratulate Yugoslavia on the 10th Anniversary of its Founding
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 28th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, congratulating the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia on the tenth anniversary of its founding. The telegram is as follows:

To the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia:

On the occasion of the great festival celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, we extend warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese working class to the fraternal Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia and the entire working class! We wish you more achievements in socialist construction and in building a happy life for the people!

All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 中华全国总工会　电贺南斯拉夫建国十周年

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会
　　电贺南斯拉夫建国十周年
    新华社二十八日讯　中华全国总工会致电南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会，祝贺南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年。电文如下：南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会：
    欣逢南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年纪念的伟大节日之际，谨代表中国工人阶级向兄弟的南斯拉夫工会联合会和全体工人阶级致热烈的祝贺！祝您们在社会主义建设中，和在为人民建设幸福的生活中取得更多的成就！
                      中华全国总工会
