Title: National People's Congress Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Approves Regulations on National Construction Bonds for Next Year, etc.
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 26th meeting on the afternoon of November 10th.

    The meeting discussed and approved the "Regulations on National Economic Construction Bonds for 1956" proposed by the State Council.

    The meeting also passed a resolution approving the agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on crop quarantine and the prevention and control of plant diseases and insect pests.

    The meeting also passed the "Decision on Whether the Presidents of Local People's Courts at All Levels and the Chief Procurators of People's Procuratorates May Concurrently Serve as Members of People's Committees at All Levels."

    Finally, the meeting approved a number of appointments and decisions regarding appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  通过明年国家建设公债条例等项

1955-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    通过明年国家建设公债条例等项
    新华社十日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十一月十日下午举行第二十六次会议。
    会议讨论并通过了国务院提出的“一九五六年国家经济建设公债条例”。
    会议还通过了批准中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府关于农作物检疫和防治病虫害的协定的决议。
    会议还通过了“关于地方各级人民法院院长人民检察院检察长可否兼任各级人民委员会的组成人员问题的决定”。
    会议最后通过了任命和决定任免的事项多起。
