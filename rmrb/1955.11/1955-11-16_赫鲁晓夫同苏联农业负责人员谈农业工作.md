Title: Khrushchev Talks with Soviet Agricultural Officials on Agricultural Work
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 15th: TASS reported from Moscow on November 15th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, held a conversation with senior leading cadres of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture and the Ministers of Agriculture of the various Union Republics on agricultural work on November 14th at the office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

The Soviet Minister of Agriculture, Matskevich, gave a brief report on the expanded meeting of the Collegium of the Ministry of Agriculture, which was held on November 11th and 12th with the participation of the Ministers of Agriculture of the various Union Republics. The meeting discussed the following issues: 1. Preliminary achievements of the 1955 agricultural year and the issue of striving for higher yields per unit area and higher livestock productivity in the coming year; 2. Measures for further improving the work of agricultural organs, the organization of purchase work, and the activities of scientific research institutions; 3. The issue of extra remuneration system for collective farm work.

In the conversation, Khrushchev drew the attention of agricultural leading staff to the need to eliminate as soon as possible the serious shortcomings in leading machine tractor stations, further strengthening collective farms, and scientific institutions in the work of increasing agricultural production.

Khrushchev emphasized that the following work is very important: to make a broad summary of the results of this agricultural year, summarize and introduce the experience of the best farms, train collective farm staff, improve breeding work, especially the cultivation of hybrid maize seeds, take exemplary care of livestock in winter, and organize preparations for next year's spring sowing.

### 赫鲁晓夫同苏联农业负责人员谈农业工作

1955-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　赫鲁晓夫同苏联农业负责人员谈农业工作
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫十一月十四日在苏联共产党中央委员会办公厅同苏联农业部高级领导干部和各加盟共和国农业部长举行了一次谈话。
    苏联农业部长马茨凯维奇就十一月十一日和十二日举行的、有各加盟共和国农业部长参加的农业部部务委员会扩大会议作了简短的报告。这次会议讨论了下列问题：一、一九五五农业年度的初步成就以及在明年争取更高的单位面积产量和更高的畜产品生产率的问题；二、进一步改进农业机关的工作、征购的组织工作以及科学研究机关的活动的措施；三、集体农庄工作的额外报酬的制度问题。
    在谈话中，赫鲁晓夫提请农业领导工作人员注意，必须尽快消灭在领导机器拖拉机站、进一步加强集体农庄和科学机关在增加农业生产的工作中的严重缺点。
    赫鲁晓夫着重指出，下列工作是非常重要的：对本农业年度的结果作广泛的总结，总结和介绍最优秀的农庄的经验，训练集体农庄工作人员，改进育种工作、特别是杂交玉蜀黍种子的培育，在冬季模范地照顾牲畜和组织明年春季播种的准备工作。
