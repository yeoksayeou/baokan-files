Title: Burmese Military Friendship Delegation Holds Farewell Banquet in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 14th: The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, led by Acting Head of Delegation Brigadier General Thakin Tin, left Kunming on the 14th to return to their country. Colonel Zhang Ting, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, came to see the delegation off.

　　At 6:00 PM on the 13th, the delegation held a farewell banquet.

　　At the banquet, Brigadier General Thakin Tin said in his speech: “During our visit, wherever we went, we saw the Chinese people filled with a deep sense of national honor. Your leaders are busy educating the broad masses to create a new era.”

　　Thakin Tin said: "I believe that since the mutual visits of our two countries' premiers and the publication of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence declaration, the relationship between China and Burma has been greatly improved. Our two countries are not only working together for mutual benefit in economic, cultural and social activities, but are also working to promote and maintain peace."

　　Thakin Tin also said: "We have been treated with great hospitality everywhere in your country. We have made friends here and visited beautiful places. We want to take these pleasant memories back with us. I sincerely hope that through this delegation, the foundation of friendship between the armed forces of our two countries has been consolidated.”

　　Colonel Zhang Ting, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Jin Rubai, head of the Kunming Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also spoke at the banquet.

### 缅甸军事友好代表团在昆明举行告别宴会

1955-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸军事友好代表团在昆明举行告别宴会
    据新华社昆明十四日电　缅甸军事友好代表团在代理团长特·克利夫特率领下，已在十四日离开昆明回国。国防部代表张庭大校前来为代表团送行。
    十三日下午六时，代表团举行了告别宴会。
    在宴会上，特·克利夫特准将致词说：“在我们的参观中，不论到那里，都看到中国的人民充满着深厚的民族荣誉感。你们的领袖们正在忙于教育广大的群众去创立一个新的时代。”
    特·克利夫特说：“我认为自从我们两国总理相互访问并且发表了和平共处的五项原则的声明以后，中缅两国的关系已得到了大大改善。我们两个国家不仅是为了相互的利益而正在一致地从事经济、文化和社会的活动，并且正在从事促进和维护和平的工作。”
    特·克利夫特还说：“在你们国家里，我们到处受到殷勤招待。我们在这里交了朋友，我们到过美丽的地方。我们要把这些愉快的回忆带回去。我衷心希望，通过这个代表团，我们两国武装力量之间的友谊的基础，已经得到巩固。”
    国防部代表张庭大校和中国人民解放军昆明驻军首长金如柏也分别在宴会上讲了话。
