　　Soviet Union's European Part to Establish Unified High-Voltage Power Grid
    The Soviet Academy of Sciences is studying the establishment of a unified high-voltage power grid in the European part of the Soviet Union. The Power Engineering Institute has completed the design plan for this power grid. The entire power grid will be controlled by a central automatic management center, allowing managers to transmit electricity from any location in this region to another at any time, according to the needs of the national economy.
    The European part of the Soviet Union accounts for about half of all of Europe. Establishing a unified high-voltage power grid in this vast area must first solve the problem of long-distance power transmission. The Long-Distance Power Transmission Committee, led by Academician Venter, has been established at the Power Engineering Institute to specifically discuss the opening of the 400,000-volt high-voltage transmission line between Kuibyshev and Moscow.
    The DC Laboratory of the Power Engineering Institute is designing specialized equipment to increase the load capacity of rectifiers, thereby improving the safety of long-distance DC power transmission. In addition, the institute is also working with the Moscow Transformer Factory to design powerful transformers for long-distance DC power transmission.
　　Poland Trains Experts in Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
    The Institute of Nuclear Research of the Polish Academy of Sciences has opened various workshops to train experts in the peaceful use of atomic energy.
    A few days ago, about thirty engineers from various specialties graduated from the first workshop in Krakow. They learned to use radioactive isotopes in industry. Currently, specialized workshops for the peaceful use of atomic energy have been established here for biologists, biochemists, physiologists, and agricultural scientists.
    The Institute of Nuclear Research is now preparing to train more atomic energy experts for various sectors of the national economy.
　　Czech Prochazka Team Wins Chinese Miners' Banner
    After evaluating the results of the socialist emulation of miners' teams in the third quarter of this year, the Presidium of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Fuel and the Central Committee of the Mining Workers' Union recently decided to award the Chinese Miners' Banner to the Prochazka team of the "Sverma Coal Mine." The miners of this team achieved remarkable results in the competition, utilizing the efficiency of machinery and equipment to 100% and overfulfilling the third-quarter production plan by 48.81%.
    The Chinese Miners' Banner was presented to the Czechoslovak miners by the Chinese miners during the Czechoslovak Miners' Festival last year. In order to win this banner symbolizing Sino-Czech friendship, more and more Czechoslovak miners' teams have participated in the socialist emulation.
　　Yugoslavia's Giant Blast Furnace Put into Production
    The second blast furnace of the largest Zenica Iron and Steel Plant in Yugoslavia was put into production on the 23rd. This blast furnace can produce 600 tons of iron per day. With the commissioning of this blast furnace, the annual pig iron output of the Zenica Iron and Steel Plant will reach 400,000 tons, and the national pig iron output of Yugoslavia will reach 700,000 tons, equivalent to seven times the national pig iron output before the war.
                            (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联和人民民主国家的建设

1955-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和人民民主国家的建设
　　苏联欧洲部分将建立统一高压输电网
    苏联科学院正在研究建立欧洲部分统一高压输电网的工作。能学研究所已经完成了这个输电网的设计方案。整个输电网将由一个总的自动管理中心来控制，使管理人员可以根据国民经济的需要，在任何时候把这一区域内任何一个地方的电力输往另一个地方。
    苏联欧洲部分约占整个欧洲的一半。在这一大片土地上建立统一高压输电网，必须首先解决远程输电问题。在能学研究所里，已经设立了由文捷尔院士领导的远程输电委员会，专门讨论了有关古比雪夫和莫斯科之间，电压达四十万伏特的高压输电线的开放问题。
    能学研究所直流实验室正在设计专门的装置，以便增加整流机的负载能力，从而提高长距离输送直流电的安全。此外，研究所还正在和莫斯科变压器工厂共同设计长距离输送直流电的强大变压器。
　  波训练和平利用原子能专家
    波兰科学院原子核研究所已经开办了各种讲习班来训练和平利用原子能方面的专家。
    几天前，三十名左右各个专业的工程师在克拉科夫的第一届讲习班毕业。他们学会了在工业上使用放射性同位素。现在，这里为生物学家、生物化学家、生理学家和农业科学家设立了和平利用原子能的专门讲习班。
    原子核研究所现在准备为国民经济各部门培养更多的原子能专家。
  　捷普罗哈兹卡小组荣获中国矿工锦旗
    捷克斯洛伐克燃料部和矿业工人工会中央委员会主席团最近在评定了今年第三季度矿工小组的社会主义竞赛结果后，决定把中国矿工锦旗奖给“斯维尔马煤矿”的普罗哈兹卡小组。这个小组的矿工在竞赛中的成绩十分显著，他们把机器设备的效能发挥到百分之百的程度，并且超额百分之四十八点八一完成了第三季生产计划。
    中国矿工锦旗是中国矿工去年在捷克斯洛伐克矿工节时赠给捷克斯洛伐克矿工的。为了争取获得这面象征中捷友谊的锦旗，越来越多的捷克斯洛伐克矿工小组参加了社会主义竞赛。
　  南斯拉夫一座巨型高炉投入生产
    南斯拉夫全国最大的泽尼查钢铁厂的第二座高炉在二十三日投入生产。这座高炉每天能够炼铁六百吨。随着这个高炉的投入生产，泽尼查钢铁厂的生铁年产量将达到四十万吨，南斯拉夫全国的生铁年产量将达到七十万吨，相当于战前全国生铁产量的七倍。
                            （据新华社讯）
