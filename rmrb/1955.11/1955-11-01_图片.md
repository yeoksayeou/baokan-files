Title: Picture
Author:
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Manuscript

    The Mayor of Rome, Rebecchini, held a reception on the morning of October 15th to entertain all members of the Chinese Art Troupe. The picture shows Mayor Rebecchini (front row, fifth from the left) and the Art Troupe members in a group photo. Shaking hands with the mayor is the head of the troupe, Zhang Zhixiang.
