Title: Trade Delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The trade delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a party of seven, led by Chen Ban-soo, the Minister of Trade of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing by train at 9:00 p.m. on the 11th. Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Lin Haiyun, Assistant Minister, and responsible persons from relevant bureaus of our Ministry of Foreign Trade were at the station to greet them.

Also at the station to welcome them were Kim Gui-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and Kim Kuk-po, Commercial Counselor.

### 朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团到京

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团到京
    新华社十二日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团一行七人，由团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易相陈班秀率领，在十一日晚九时乘火车到达北京。到车站迎接的，有我对外贸易部副部长雷任民、部长助理林海云及有关各局负责人。
    到车站欢迎的，还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办金贵南和商务参赞金国辅。
