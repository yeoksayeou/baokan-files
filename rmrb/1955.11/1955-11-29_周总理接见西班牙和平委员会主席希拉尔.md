Title: Premier Zhou Receives José Giral, Chairman of the Spanish Peace Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received Dr. José Giral, Chairman of the Spanish Peace Committee and Standing Committee Member of the World Peace Council, in the afternoon of November 28th. Dr. Giral was invited to China to attend the centennial commemoration of the publication of the world-famous work "Leaves of Grass" and the 350th anniversary of the publication of "Don Quixote."

Present at the meeting were Liu Ningyi, Standing Committee Member of the World Peace Council and Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Wu Zhengyi, Deputy Director of the Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 周总理接见西班牙和平委员会主席希拉尔

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见西班牙和平委员会主席希拉尔
    新华社二十八日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十八日下午接见了应邀来我国参加世界名著“草叶集”出版百周年、“堂·吉诃德”出版三百五十周年纪念大会的西班牙和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会常务委员何塞·希拉尔博士。
    接见的时候在座的，有世界和平理事会常务委员、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员刘宁一、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙和中国科学院植物研究所副所长吴征镒等人。
