Title: Sukarno at the "Anti-Colonialism Mass Rally" in Yogyakarta Calls on the Indonesian People to be Vigilant Against Imperialist Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 24th: Indonesian President Sukarno on the 23rd once again called on the entire nation to be wary of the grave danger of imperialists plotting to destroy the Republic of Indonesia. Sukarno said at the "Anti-Colonialism Mass Rally" in Yogyakarta, attended by approximately one million people, that the most powerful weapon of the imperialists is to divide the Indonesian nation. One of the contents of their long-term subversion plan is to create discord within the Indonesian nation so that they can divide and rule.

Sukarno said that the opposition and discord between domestic political parties will only benefit the imperialists and weaken the strength of the country. The masses present responded with enthusiastic applause to these words he spoke.

Sukarno called on the nation to strengthen revolutionary unity, heighten vigilance, and crush the imperialists' conspiracy to overthrow the Republic of Indonesia.

In his speech, Sukarno once again spoke of the five basic tasks for the nation that he put forward in August of this year, pointing out that revolutionary unity is the primary task.

Sukarno said that the second task is to mobilize the entire people to eliminate armed bandits who disrupt social order and undermine national unity. He was referring to the "Islamic State Movement" and other terrorist groups that plague the people.

Regarding the third task, Sukarno called on the people to intensify their efforts to build Indonesia into a just and prosperous society.

When Sukarno spoke of the fourth task, regarding the election of the Constitutional Assembly, he asked the people to elect honest parties that would uphold the republic stipulated in the Declaration of Independence.

Regarding the last task—recovering West Irian—Sukarno once again pointed out that national unity is needed to accomplish this task.

### 苏加诺在日惹的“反对殖民主义群众大会”上　号召印度尼西亚人民警惕帝国主义的阴谋

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏加诺在日惹的“反对殖民主义群众大会”上
　　号召印度尼西亚人民警惕帝国主义的阴谋
    据新华社雅加达二十四日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺二十三日再次号召全国提防帝国主义者阴谋摧毁印度尼西亚共和国的严重危险。苏加诺在日惹举行的大约有一百万人参加的“反对殖民主义群众大会”上说，帝国主义者最厉害的武器就是分裂印度尼西亚民族。他们的长期的颠覆计划的内容之一就是制造印度尼西亚民族内部的不和，以便他们可以分而治之。
    苏加诺说，国内各政党之间的对立和不和只会使帝国主义者得到好处，并且会削弱国家的力量。到会群众对他所说的这些话报以热烈的鼓掌。
    苏加诺号召全国加强革命的团结，提高警惕，粉碎帝国主义者推翻印度尼西亚共和国的阴谋。
    苏加诺在演说中再一次谈到他在今年八月间提出的全国的五项基本任务，他指出，革命的团结是首要的任务。
    苏加诺说，第二项任务是动员全体人民肃清扰乱社会治安和破坏全国团结的武装匪徒。他指的是为害人民的“回教国运动”和其他恐怖集团。
    关于第三项任务，苏加诺号召人民加紧努力把印度尼西亚建设成为一个正义和繁荣的社会。
    苏加诺在谈到第四项任务关于制宪议会选举问题，他要求人民选那些将维护独立宣言所规定的共和国的正直党派。
    关于最后一项任务——收回西伊里安，苏加诺再一次指出，要全国团结来完成这一项任务。
