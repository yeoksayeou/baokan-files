　　Reactions to Criticism from This Newspaper
    In this newspaper's August 20th editorial, "Vigorously Conserve Raw Materials in Industrial Production," the serious waste of steel at the Jiangnan Shipyard was criticized. Now, the Jiangnan Shipyard's Director Zheng Zhong has sent a letter of self-criticism. The letter states that they have taken some measures, such as compiling material consumption quotas, implementing material requisition limits, strictly implementing the systems for the storage, requisition, and recycling of raw materials, and improving process operations, and that initial results have already been achieved.

### 对本报批评的反应

1955-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　对本报批评的反应
    八月二十日本报社论“在工业生产中大力节约原材料”中，批评了江南造船厂严重浪费钢材的现象。现在，江南造船厂厂长郑重已来信检讨。来信中说，他们已采取了一些措施，如编制材料消耗定额，实行限额领料，严格贯彻执行原材料的保管、领用、回收等制度，改善工艺操作等，现在已经初步收到了效果。
