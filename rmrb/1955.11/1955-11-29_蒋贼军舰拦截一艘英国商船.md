Title: Chiang Bandit Warships Intercept a British Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 27th. According to a Reuters report, a spokesman for the British government in Hong Kong said on the 27th that Chiang bandit warships had intercepted a British merchant ship, the "Dolingseia," near Fuzhou. This British merchant ship has been forced to sail to Matsu Island under the surveillance of Chiang bandit warships.

### 蒋贼军舰拦截一艘英国商船

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋贼军舰拦截一艘英国商船
    新华社二十七日讯　据路透社报道，香港英国政府发言人二十七日说，蒋贼军舰在福州附近拦截了一艘英国商船“多林赛亚号”。这艘英国商船在蒋贼军舰的监视下，已经被迫开往马祖岛。
