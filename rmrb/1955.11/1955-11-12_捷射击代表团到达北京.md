Title: Czechoslovakian Shooting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Czechoslovakian People's Republic's Army Support Volunteer Association shooting delegation, comprising twenty-five members, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 11th at the invitation of China to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition. The head of the delegation, Chenik Gruska, had already arrived by plane on the afternoon of the 10th.

### 捷射击代表团到达北京

1955-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　  捷射击代表团到达北京
    据新华社十一日讯　应中国邀请前来参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的捷克斯洛伐克人民共和国支援军队志愿协会射击代表团一行二十五人，在十一日晚乘车到达北京。团长契尼克·格鲁斯卡已先在十日下午乘机到达。
