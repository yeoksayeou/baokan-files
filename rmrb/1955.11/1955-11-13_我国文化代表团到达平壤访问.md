Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang for Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 12th: The People's Republic of China Cultural Delegation, consisting of six members and led by Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Pyongyang on the evening of the 11th via the Beijing-Pyongyang direct train, according to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Xu Zhengyu, Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Dongjian, Vice Minister of Education Zhang Yihuan, Li Leyan, and others, totaling more than 1,000 people. Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, also went to the station to welcome them.

A welcome ceremony was held at Pyongyang Station. Zheng Lu, Vice Minister of Culture and Propaganda of Korea, and Wei Que, Head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, delivered speeches successively.

The delegation will conduct a one-month visit to Korea. During this period, the delegation will have extensive contact with people from the educational, journalistic, and literary and art circles of Korea.

### 我国文化代表团到达平壤访问

1955-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国文化代表团到达平壤访问
    新华社平壤十二日电　根据中朝文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来朝鲜访问的中华人民共和国文化代表团一行六人，由团长、中华人民共和国教育部副部长韦悫率领，在十一日晚乘北京—平壤直达列车到达平壤。
    前往车站欢迎的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国文化宣传相许贞琡，外务省副相李东建，教育省副相张益焕、李乐彦等共一千多人。我国驻朝鲜大使潘自力也到车站欢迎。
    在平壤车站举行了欢迎仪式。朝鲜文化宣传省副相郑律和中国文化代表团团长韦悫相继致词。
    代表团在朝鲜将进行一个月的访问。在此期间，代表团将和朝鲜教育、新闻、文艺界人士广泛接触。新华社更正
