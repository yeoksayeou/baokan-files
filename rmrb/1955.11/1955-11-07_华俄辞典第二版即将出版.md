Title: Second Edition of Chinese-Russian Dictionary to be Published Soon
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-07
Page: 2
    The second edition of the Chinese-Russian Dictionary will soon be published by the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The first edition of this dictionary, published in 1952, quickly sold out. The second edition adds more than two hundred characters and also includes a new radical index. Its print run will be double that of the first edition.
  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 华俄辞典第二版即将出版

1955-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    华俄辞典第二版即将出版
    苏联科学院东方学研究所即将出版华俄辞典第二版。这部辞典在一九五二年初版发行以后，很快就销售完了。第二版中增加了两百多个字，还附有新的部首检查表。它的发行量将比第一版增加一倍。
  （新华社）
