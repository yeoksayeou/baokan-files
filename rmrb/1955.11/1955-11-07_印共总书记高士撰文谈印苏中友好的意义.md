Title: Indian Communist Party General Secretary Ajoy Ghosh Writes About the Significance of Indo-Soviet-Chinese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1955-11-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 6th: Indian Communist Party General Secretary Ajoy Ghosh, in an article published in the latest issue of the "New Age" weekly, hailed the growing friendly relations between India, the Soviet Union, and China (whose populations together account for more than half of the world's population). The article stated that the Indian Communist Party "will strive for closer cooperation between them, which can become the most powerful factor in maintaining world peace and also help India strengthen its own independence and economic position."

The article pointed out that the friendship and cooperation between India and China have already played a significant role in uniting Asian countries against the war plans of imperialism and in supporting national liberation.

The article stated that the Indian Communist Party "advocates the conclusion of a solid Asian security pact that will guarantee Asian independence and Asian peace."

### 印共总书记高士撰文谈印苏中友好的意义

1955-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    印共总书记高士撰文谈印苏中友好的意义
    新华社新德里六日电  印度共产党总书记阿约艾·高士在最近一期的“新世纪”周报发表的文章中，欢呼印度同苏联和中国（它们的人口共占世界人口的一半以上）之间已经增长的友好关系。文章说，印度共产党“将为它们之间的更密切的合作而努力，这种合作可以成为维护世界和平的最有力的因素，同时也能帮助印度加强它自己的独立和经济地位”。
    文章指出，印度同中国之间的友谊和合作已经在团结亚洲国家来反对帝国主义的战争计划和支持民族自由方面起了巨大的作用。
    文章说，印度共产党“主张缔结一个坚固的亚洲安全公约，这个公约将保障亚洲的独立和亚洲的和平”。
