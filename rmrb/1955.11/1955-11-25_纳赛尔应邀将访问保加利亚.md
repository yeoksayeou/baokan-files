Title: Nasser to Visit Bulgaria at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 23rd (TASS) - Newspapers report that Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser has accepted an invitation from the People's Republic of Bulgaria and will pay an official visit to Bulgaria.
　　The date of the visit will be determined later.

### 纳赛尔应邀将访问保加利亚

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  纳赛尔应邀将访问保加利亚
    新华社二十四日讯　塔斯社开罗二十三日讯：据报纸报道，埃及总理纳赛尔接受了保加利亚人民共和国的邀请，将正式访问保加利亚。
      访问日期将在以后确定。
