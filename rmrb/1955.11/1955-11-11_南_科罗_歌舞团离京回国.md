Title: South "Kolo" Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 3

　The Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Troupe, headed by O. Skovran, left Beijing by train on the 10th to return home. Those who went to the station to see them off included Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and more than one hundred people from literary and art circles in the capital.
    Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popović and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.
    On the evening of the 9th, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the song and dance troupe. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 南“科罗”歌舞团离京回国

1955-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

　  南“科罗”歌舞团离京回国
    以奥·斯科夫兰为首的南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团在十日乘火车离北京回国。到车站欢送的，有中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙和首都文艺界人士一百多人。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    九日晚间，中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞曾设宴欢送歌舞团。　（新华社）
