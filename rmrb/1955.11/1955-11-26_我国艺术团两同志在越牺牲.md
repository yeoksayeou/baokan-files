Title: Two Comrades of Our Country's Art Troupe Sacrificed in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 25th: Wu Baihua, Deputy Head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Zhang Yiming, Art Instructor, unfortunately sacrificed on the 21st while returning to Hanoi from Hong Gai.

To commemorate the merits of Comrades Wu Baihua and Zhang Yiming, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam posthumously awarded them the First Class Labor Medal on the 23rd.

### 我国艺术团两同志在越牺牲

1955-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  我国艺术团两同志在越牺牲
    新华社河内二十五日电　中国艺术团副团长吴白桦、艺术指导张一鸣二十一日在从鸿基返回河内途中，不幸牺牲。
    越南民主共和国政府为纪念吴白桦、张一鸣两同志的功绩，在二十三日追授给他们以一级劳动勋章。
