Title: Bulgarian Musician Zlatev Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 3

　　Bulgarian Musician Zlatev Arrives in Beijing

　　Professor Chirkin Georgiev Zlatev, a Bulgarian vocalist who will be lecturing in China according to the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing on the 7th.

　　Professor Zlatev is the Director of the Teaching and Research Office of the Bulgarian National Academy of Music and an outstanding music educator in Bulgaria. He is also one of the composers of the national anthem of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 保加利亚音乐家兹拉捷夫到京

1955-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚音乐家兹拉捷夫到京
    根据中保文化合作协定来中国讲学的保加利亚声乐家契尔金·格奥尔基·兹拉捷夫教授已在七日到达北京。
    兹拉捷夫教授是保加利亚国家音乐学院教研室主任，保加利亚的优秀的音乐教育家。他还是保加利亚人民共和国国歌作曲者之一。　（新华社）
