Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 10th. The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their twenty-fifth meeting at the Palace of Nations at 10:00 AM today. The meeting adjourned at 12:10 PM. The next meeting will be held at 10:00 AM on November 17th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

  　中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦十日电　中美两国大使级会谈十日上午十时在国联大厦举行第二十五次会议。会议在十二时十分休会。下次会议将在十一月十七日上午十时举行。
