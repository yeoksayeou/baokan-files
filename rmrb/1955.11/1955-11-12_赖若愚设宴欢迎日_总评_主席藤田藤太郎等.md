Title: Lai Ruoyu Hosts Banquet to Welcome Mr. Fujita Totaro, Chairman of the Japanese "Sohyo," and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency:

　　Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, held a banquet on the evening of the 11th to welcome Fujita Totaro, Vice-Chairman of the Japanese "National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution," Head of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation, and Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), as well as all members of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation. Ohno Koichi, Secretary-General of the Japanese "National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution" Visiting Delegation, Permanent Executive Committee Member of the "National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution," and Director of the Secretariat, was also invited to attend the banquet.
    The Japanese Trade Union Delegation, headed by Fujita Totaro, consists of thirteen trade union workers from the Japanese "National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution."
    Attending the banquet this evening were Liu Ningyi and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Jiebo, Zhang Weizhen, Li Zaiwen, Dong Xin, and Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretaries of the Secretariat; members of the Presidium and heads of various departments; chairmen and vice-chairmen of various industrial trade unions in China; and Japanese trade union workers in Beijing, such as Kaneko Kenta.
    At the banquet, Lai Ruoyu delivered a speech welcoming the Japanese guests to visit China. In his speech, Lai Ruoyu pointed out that the friendship between the trade unions of China and Japan plays a positive role in promoting and strengthening the friendship between the people of China and Japan, and also has a significant impact on promoting and strengthening the friendly solidarity between workers in Asia and the world, and thus has a positive impact on maintaining peace in the Far East and the world. He wished this friendly relationship would grow stronger day by day.
    Then, Fujita Totaro delivered a speech, expressing his gratitude for the host's hospitality. When he talked about the growing friendly exchanges between the trade unions of Japan and China in recent years, he said: This mutual visit "is of great help to deepen mutual understanding." He also stated that the Japanese people and workers all hope that Japan and China will carry out economic exchanges and restore diplomatic relations, because "the development of this friendly and amicable relationship plays a great role and influence in the peace of Asia and the world."

### 赖若愚设宴欢迎日“总评”主席藤田藤太郎等

1955-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　赖若愚设宴欢迎日“总评”主席藤田藤太郎等
    据新华社讯　中华全国总工会主席赖若愚在十一日晚举行宴会，欢迎日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团副团长、日本工会代表团团长、日本工会总评议会主席藤田藤太郎和日本工会代表团全体人员。日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团秘书长、“拥护宪法国民联合会”常任执行委员兼事务局长大野幸一也应邀出席了宴会。
    以藤田藤太郎为首的日本工会代表团是由日本
  “拥护宪法国民联合会”中十三位工会工作者所组成。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、朱学范，书记处书记李颉伯、张维桢、栗再温、董昕、张修竹、主席团委员和各部门负责人，中国各产业工会主席、副主席以及在北京的日本工会工作者金子健太等。
    在宴会上，赖若愚致词欢迎日本客人来中国访问。赖若愚在讲话中指出，中日两国工会间的友好对促进和加强中日两国人民间的友好起着积极作用，对促进和加强亚洲工人及世界工人间的友好团结也有着重大影响，因而对维护远东和世界和平也有积极的影响。他祝这种友好关系日益发展。
    接着，藤田藤太郎致词，他对主人的盛情招待表示感谢。他谈到近年来日中两国工会间友好往来的日益发展情况的时候说：这种相互访问“对加深相互间的了解有很大帮助。”他并表示，日本人民和工人都希望日本和中国进行经济交流和恢复邦交，因为“这种友好亲善关系的发展，对亚洲和世界和平都起有很大作用和影响。”
