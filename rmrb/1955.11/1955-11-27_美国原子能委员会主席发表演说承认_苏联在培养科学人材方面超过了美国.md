Title: U.S. Atomic Energy Commission Chairman Admits: Soviet Union Surpasses the United States in Cultivating Scientific Talent
Author:
Date: 1955-11-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: While delivering a speech at the Edison Institute in West Orange, U.S. Atomic Energy Commission Chairman Strauss said that one of the direct results of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy held in Geneva was: “We have learned more about the nature of Soviet science… What we have learned is enough to shatter any complacency we may have felt in terms of imagination and ability.”

　　Strauss pointed out that the United States "will soon be behind Soviet Russia in the training of experts," although the United States and the Soviet Union currently have roughly the same number of engineers and scientific personnel - about one million each. Strauss said: "Russia has about 520,000 degreed engineers, and we can believe that almost all of them are working in the fields they studied. We have more such experts now, but because only 65 percent of our engineers are actually working in their professional fields, having a few more is equivalent to not having them. One-third of them are doing administrative work or working in advertising, finance, and other fields."

　　Strauss said, "We need 45,000 to 50,000 new engineers each year. We are only getting half that number." "In June, about 23,000 people graduated. In Russia, 53,000 new engineers graduated. It is expected that this number will be much larger next year, and will increase greatly every year thereafter. This year they have trained 120,000 specialists in various fields, while we have trained 70,000."

### 美国原子能委员会主席发表演说承认  苏联在培养科学人材方面超过了美国

1955-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国原子能委员会主席发表演说承认
　  苏联在培养科学人材方面超过了美国
    据新华社讯美国原子能委员会主席斯特劳斯在西奥兰基的爱迪生学院发表演说时说，在日内瓦举行的和平利用原子能国际会议的直接结果之一是：“我们更多地了解了苏联科学的特质……我们所了解到的东西足可以使我们在想像力和能力方面所感到的一切自负碰得粉碎。”
    斯特劳斯指出，美国“在培养专家方面很快地就要落后于苏俄了”，虽然现在美国和苏联有着数目大约相等的工程师和科学工作人员——各约一百万人。斯特劳斯说：“俄国约有五十二万名领有文凭的工程师，而且我们可以相信，他们差不多都是在做着他们所学的那一行工作。现在我国这样的专家还要多一些，但是，由于我国的工程师只有百分之六十五是实际在做专业工作的，所以多这一些也等于是没有。其中有三分之一是在做行政工作或者是广告工作、金融工作以及其他一些工作。”
    斯特劳斯说，“我们每年需要四万五千至五万名新的工程师。我们得到的只有这个数字的一半。”“六月间，约有二万三千人毕业。而在俄国毕业的有五万三千个新的工程师。可以预料，明年这个数字还要大得多，而且以后每年都会大大增加。今年他们培养出来的各种专业的专家有十二万人，我们是七万人。”
