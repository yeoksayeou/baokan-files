Title: Meeting of Heads of Shooting Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The organizing committee of the International Friendship Shooting Competition convened a meeting of the heads of shooting delegations from various countries on the afternoon of the 15th.

　　The meeting was presided over by Cai Shufan, Chairman of the Organizing Committee.

　　The meeting discussed and approved the program for the opening ceremony of the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition. Subsequently, Rong Gaotang, Director of the Credentials Committee, reported on the process and opinions of reviewing the qualifications of shooting athletes of shooting delegations from various countries. After discussion, the meeting adopted the report of the Credentials Committee and fully approved the shooting athletes of the shooting delegations of various countries to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition.

### 各国射击代表团团长开会

1955-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国射击代表团团长开会
    新华社十五日讯　国际友谊射击竞赛组织委员会在十五日下午召开了各国射击代表团团长会议。
    会议由组织委员会主席蔡树藩主持。
    会议讨论并通过了一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛开幕式的程序。接着，资格审查委员会主任委员荣高棠报告了审查各国射击代表团射击运动员资格的经过和意见。会议经过讨论后，通过了资格审查委员会的报告，并全部批准了各国射击代表团的射击运动员参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛。
