Title: Lebanese Government Agrees to Sign Defense Agreement with Syria
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, October 31st: TASS, Beirut, 30th dispatch: Syrian Prime Minister al-Ghazzi arrived in Beirut on October 29th and met with Lebanese Prime Minister Karami and President Chamoun to discuss the draft bilateral defense agreement proposed by Syria.

According to Lebanese newspapers, Lebanese leaders have informed Ghazzi of the Lebanese government's view that the Lebanese government agrees in principle to sign the aforementioned agreement with Syria. It is believed that in the coming days, military personnel from both countries will jointly study the draft agreement in order to submit the final plan for signature by the two governments.

### 黎巴嫩政府同意和叙利亚签订防御协定

1955-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩政府同意和叙利亚签订防御协定
    新华社三十一日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特三十日讯：叙利亚总理加齐在十月二十九日到达贝鲁特，并且会晤了黎巴嫩总理卡拉米和总统夏蒙，同他们讨论了叙利亚所提出的双边防御协定草案。
    据黎巴嫩报纸报道，黎巴嫩领导者们已经把黎巴嫩政府的看法通知加齐说，黎巴嫩政府在原则上同意和叙利亚签订上述协定。人们认为，在最近几天内，两国军方人士将共同研究这个协定草案，以便提出最后的方案交由两国政府签署。
