Title: 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition (Photos)
Author: Yuan Ling, Liu Dong'ao
Date: 1955-11-21
Page: 2

　　1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition

　　The top three shooters in the men's free rifle 50-meter prone position individual total score competition: First place, Soviet shooter Tochilov; second place, Soviet shooter Perebelin; third place, Bulgarian shooter Hristov.
                            Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling photographed

　　The top three athletes in the women's free rifle 50-meter and 100-meter prone position individual total score competition: First place, Soviet master of sports Kormushkina; second place, Bulgarian female athlete Vasilyeva; third place, Soviet female athlete Novozhilova.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed

　　The Soviet shooting team that won the women's free rifle 50-meter and 100-meter prone position competition championship.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed

　　The Bulgarian shooting team that won second place in the women's free rifle 50-meter and 100-meter prone position competition.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling photographed

　　Soviet athlete Tochilov won the individual championship in the men's free rifle 50-meter prone position competition with a score of 399 rings.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed

　　The Chinese shooting team that won third place in the women's free rifle 50-meter and 100-meter prone position competition.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed

### 一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会（图片）
袁苓  刘东鳌
1955-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会
    男子自选小口径步枪五十公尺卧姿射击竞赛个人总分前三名的射击运动员：第一名、苏联射击运动员托奇洛夫，第二名、苏联射击运动员佩列别林，第三名、保加利亚射击运动员赫里斯托夫。
                            新华社记者　袁　苓摄
    女子自选小口径步枪五十公尺和一百公尺卧姿射击竞赛个人总分前三名运动员：第一名是苏联运动健将科尔穆什金娜、第二名是保加利亚女运动员瓦西利娃、第三名是苏联女运动员诺沃杰罗娃。
                                  新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    获得女子自选小口径步枪五十公尺和一百公尺卧姿射击竞赛冠军的苏联射击代表团射击队。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    获得女子自选小口径步枪五十公尺和一百公尺卧姿射击竞赛第二名的保加利亚射击代表团射击队。　新华社记者　袁　苓摄
    苏联运动员托奇洛夫在男子自选小口径步枪五十公尺卧姿射击竞赛中，以三百九十九环的成绩获得个人冠军。
                                    新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    获得女子自选小口径步枪五十公尺和一百公尺卧姿射击竞赛第三名的中国射击代表团射击队。
                                    新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
