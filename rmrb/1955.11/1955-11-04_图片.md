    The director of the Lenin State Farm in the Kharkov Oblast of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, Molebny, discovered a special corn plant on a corn plantation in 1950. This corn plant had two small tillers next to the central axis. After agricultural scientists studied and tested the genetic characteristics, it is now possible to cultivate corn with eight to twelve tillers, with each clump of spikes weighing five hundred to six hundred grams.

    苏联乌克兰共和国哈尔科夫州的列宁国营农场场长莫列勃奈一九五○年在玉米种植场上发现了有一株特殊的玉米。这种玉米的中轴旁有两个不大的分蘖。经过农学家的研究试验遗传的特性，现在已能培育出有八个到十二个分蘖的玉米，每一丛轴穗重达五百到六百公分。
