Title: National Swimming Tests Break Three More National Records

Author:

Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The 1955 National Swimming Tests held their final six events on the 28th, and four more athletes broke three national records.

　　In the afternoon, Lin Jinzhu of the Central Institute of Physical Education achieved an excellent result of 58.9 seconds in the men's 100-meter freestyle final. This result not only broke the national record of 1 minute and 0.4 seconds, but also surpassed the fourth-place result of 59.2 seconds in the same event at the Second International Youth Games of the Fifth World Youth Festival. Lin Jinzhu also won first place in the men's 100-meter backstroke this afternoon.

　　In the men's 200-meter butterfly final, Zhang Tianhui of the Central Institute of Physical Education broke the national record of 2 minutes and 46.8 seconds with a time of 2 minutes and 46.1 seconds. Zhang Tianhui had already broken the national record in the preliminary round on the 27th with a time of 2 minutes and 46.6 seconds.

　　In the women's 100-meter backstroke final, Fu Cuimei and Huang Guo Hui of the Central Institute of Physical Education broke Huang Guo Hui's national record of 1 minute and 30.2 seconds with times of 1 minute and 25 seconds and 1 minute and 27.9 seconds respectively.

　　Huang Lianhua of the East China Institute of Physical Education won first place in the women's 100-meter butterfly final. Zheng Sufei of the Central Institute of Physical Education won first place in the women's 200-meter breaststroke final.

　　After the end of the finals, representatives of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China awarded medals for breaking national records to the ten athletes and four relay teams who broke eight national records during the three days of testing.

　　Divers and water polo players from all over the country gave a brilliant performance of springboard diving, platform diving, comedic diving, and water polo for the audience today.

### 全国游泳测验又打破三项全国最高纪录

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国游泳测验又打破三项全国最高纪录
    新华社二十八日讯　一九五五年全国游泳测验二十八日进行了最后的六项决赛，又有四名运动员打破了三项全国最高纪录。
    下午，中央体育学院的林锦珠在男子一百公尺自由泳决赛中，创造了五十八点九秒的优异成绩。这个成绩不仅打破了一分零点四秒的全国最高纪录，并且超过了第五届世界青年联欢节第二届国际青年运动会这项比赛第四名的五十九点二秒的成绩。林锦珠今天下午还获得了男子一百公尺仰泳第一名。
    在男子二百公尺蝶泳的决赛中，中央体育学院的张天辉以二分四十六点一秒的成绩，打破了二分四十六点八秒的全国最高纪录。张天辉在二十七日的预赛中，就曾经以二分四十六点六秒的成绩打破全国最高纪录。
    在女子一百公尺仰泳决赛中，中央体育学院的傅翠美和黄帼会分别以一分二十五秒和一分二十七点九秒的成绩，打破了黄帼会保持的一分三十点二秒的全国最高纪录。
    在女子一百公尺蝶泳决赛中获得第一名的是华东体育学院的黄莲华。获得女子二百公尺蛙泳决赛第一名的是中央体育学院的郑素绯。
    在各项决赛结束以后，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会的代表把打破全国最高纪录奖章发给在这三天测验中打破了八项全国最高纪录的十名运动员和四个接力队。
    来自各地的跳水和水球运动员，今天为观众做了精采的跳板跳水、跳台跳水、滑稽跳水和水球表演。
