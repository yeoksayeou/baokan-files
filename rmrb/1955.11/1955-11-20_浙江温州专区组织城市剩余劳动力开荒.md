Title: Zhejiang Wenzhou Prefecture Organizes Urban Surplus Labor to Reclaim Wasteland
Author: Zhu Zukun, Ding Shanliang
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 2

　　Zhejiang Wenzhou Prefecture Organizes Urban Surplus Labor to Reclaim Wasteland
　　Recently, the Wenzhou Prefecture in Zhejiang Province organized more than 1,200 demobilized soldiers and urban surplus laborers to establish a large-scale advanced agricultural production cooperative on the coastal mudflats of Huangyan County to reclaim wasteland and engage in agricultural production.
　　The coastal mudflats of Huangyan County are formed by long-term alluvial silt from seawater, covering an area of more than 14,700 mu. The soil is fertile, and the terrain is flat. Beans and wheat can be harvested three times a year, allowing for the large-scale development of fisheries and animal husbandry. Currently, this agricultural cooperative has formulated a production plan, accumulated more than 6,000 dan of fertilizer, and sown more than 1,700 mu of broad beans. It is estimated that in 1956, the income from agricultural and livestock products will reach 510,000 yuan.
　　　　　　　　（Zhu Zukun, Ding Shanliang）

### 浙江温州专区组织城市剩余劳动力开荒
朱祖坤　丁善良
1955-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　浙江温州专区组织城市剩余劳动力开荒
    浙江省温州专区最近组织了一千二百多名复员军人和城市剩余劳动力，在黄岩县沿海的大海涂上成立了一个大型高级农业生产合作社，开垦荒地和进行农业生产。
    黄岩县沿海的大海涂是由于海水长期冲积泥沙淤成的，面积有一万四千七百多亩，土壤肥沃，地势平坦，豆麦一年能三熟，能大量发展渔业和畜牧。目前这个农业社已订出了生产计划，并积肥六千多担，播种了一千七百多亩蚕豆。预计在一九五六年，农、畜品的收入可达五十一万元。
                      （朱祖坤　丁善良）
