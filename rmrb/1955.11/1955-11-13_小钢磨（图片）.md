　　Small Steel Mill
    The above picture shows the small steel mill manufactured by the Yangliuqing Iron Factory in Tianjin Special District, Hebei Province. It can be driven by a locomobile boiler, diesel engine, or gas engine. The grinding disc is only nine inches in diameter, and it can grind 200 jin of corn flour per hour (an ordinary stone mill can grind up to 100 jin per day). The sieve is also automatic, with two layers of screens. The top layer produces bran, the bottom layer produces flour, and the bottommost layer produces coarse flour, all automatically flowing into winnowing baskets. One small steel mill costs 110 yuan. Compared with ordinary stone mills, a small steel mill only requires 7.5 yuan in labor and fuel costs to grind 3,000 jin of flour, while a stone mill requires 84 yuan. Moreover, both sides of the steel mill's grinding disc can be used, and it can grind 30,000 jin of corn before becoming dull. A stone mill, on the other hand, becomes dull in a week, and it costs 2 yuan for manual resharpening.

### 小钢磨（图片）

1955-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　小钢磨
    上图是河北省天津专区杨柳青铁工厂制造的小钢磨。用锅驼机、柴油机、煤气机都可以带动。磨扇直径只有九英寸，每小时可以磨二百斤玉米面（普通石磨一天最多能磨一百斤）。筛子也是自动的，两层罗，上层出皮，下层出面，最底下出罗底，都自动流入簸箩里。一台小钢磨价值一百一十元。和普通石磨比较，小钢磨只要七元半钱的人工、燃料费，就可以磨三千斤面；而用石磨就需要八十四元。而且，钢磨的磨扇两面都能用，可以磨三万斤玉米才磨钝；而用石磨一星期就会磨钝，重新打一次要花两元钱手工。
