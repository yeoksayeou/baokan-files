Title: Molotov Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-11-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th. TASS, Moscow, November 5th: Due to the three-day adjournment of the Four Foreign Ministers Conference, the head of the Soviet delegation, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, returned to Moscow from Geneva on the 5th.

### 莫洛托夫回到莫斯科

1955-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫回到莫斯科
    新华社六日讯  塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：由于四国外长会议休会三天，苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长莫洛托夫五日从日内瓦回到莫斯科。
