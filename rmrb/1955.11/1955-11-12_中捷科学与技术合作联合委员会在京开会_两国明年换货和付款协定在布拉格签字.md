Title: Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meets in Beijing; Sino-Czech Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement for Next Year Signed in Prague
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 10th

　　The third meeting of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation opened in Beijing on the 10th. The Chinese side attending the meeting included Liu Lanbo, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Minister of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry of the People's Republic of China, as well as committee members and experts. The Czechoslovak side attending the meeting included S. Frner, Vice Chairman of the Czechoslovak Group of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Czechoslovak Republic, as well as committee members and experts.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 11th. After sincere and friendly negotiations, the representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic have reached a satisfactory agreement and signed the Sino-Czech Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement for 1956 in Prague on the 11th.
    According to the agreement, China will supply Czechoslovakia with various minerals, livestock products, food, tea, silk, and native products and other materials needed by the national economy. Czechoslovakia will supply China with power generation equipment, various machinery, steel, instruments, chemical raw materials, and petroleum products and other materials needed by the national economy.
    Ye Jizhuang, head of the Chinese trade delegation and Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of China. David, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic, signed the agreement on behalf of the Czechoslovak Republic.

### 中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会在京开会　两国明年换货和付款协定在布拉格签字

1955-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会在京开会
　　两国明年换货和付款协定在布拉格签字
    新华社十日讯　中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第三届会议十日在北京开幕。中国方面出席会议的有中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国电力工业部部长刘澜波和委员、专家等多人；捷克斯洛伐克方面出席会议的有中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组副主席、捷克斯洛伐克共和国国家计划委员会副主席史·弗尔纳及委员、专家等。
    新华社布拉格十一日电　中华人民共和国政府和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府的代表在经过诚挚友好的谈判后，圆满地达成了协议，并且在十一日在布拉格签订了中捷一九五六年交换货物和付款协定。
    根据协定规定，中国方面将供给捷克斯洛伐克各种矿产、畜产、食品、茶叶、丝绸和土产品等国民经济所需要的物资。捷克斯洛伐克方面将供给中国发电设备、各种机械、钢材、仪器、化工原料和石油产品等国民经济所需要的物资。
    代表中华人民共和国方面在协定上签字的是中国贸易代表团团长、中华人民共和国对外贸易部长叶季壮。代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国方面在协定上签字的是捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长戴维。
