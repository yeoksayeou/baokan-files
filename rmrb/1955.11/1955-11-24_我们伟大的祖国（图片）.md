    Our Great Motherland
    Shuajing Temple, a new city in northwestern Sichuan, was a sparsely populated grassland before liberation. Now it has been gradually built into a beautiful mountain city. With the opening of the Chengdu-Aba Highway, this mountain city will become more prosperous. On this newly built street, the state-owned National Trading Company has built tall buildings.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四川西北部的新城——刷经寺，解放前是人烟稀少的草坝，现在已经逐步建设成一座美丽的山城了。随着成阿公路的通车，这座山城将更加繁荣。在这一条新建的大街上，国营民族贸易公司建成了高大的楼房。
