Title: More National Records Broken at Shanghai Autumn Games
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 3

At the Shanghai Autumn Games, several more national records were broken.

On the 13th, the track and field team of the Central Institute of Physical Education broke two national records, created a new national record, and equaled two national records at the 1955 Shanghai Autumn Games.

The men's 4x100 meter relay team of Zhu Ming, Lin Tao, Wu Zaiyu, and Liu Jingren achieved a time of 44 seconds, 0.2 seconds faster than the national record. Fu Xueyan, the national record holder in women's high jump, broke the national record of 1.48 meters with a jump of 1.51 meters. Xian Shaomei created a new national record in the women's shot put (4 kg). Her score was 11.67 meters, one centimeter farther than the score Luo Yunqi set in April during the "1955 National Track and Field Test."

The two events that equaled the national record were: Zheng Fengrong's women's high jump and Xu Shizhang's women's 400 meters. Zheng Fengrong's score was 1.48 meters. Xu Shizhang's score was 1 minute 1.7 seconds.

Light industry workers Cai Changhua, Xu Guoqing, and Li Zhengqing created new national records in weightlifting performances at the games. In light heavyweight weightlifting, Cai Changhua's total score for press, snatch, and clean and jerk was 300 kg, equaling the national record, with a clean and jerk score of 115 kg, breaking the national record of 112.5 kg. Xu Guoqing's clean and jerk score in light heavyweight weightlifting was 7.5 kg more than the national record. In middleweight weightlifting, Li Zhengqing's clean and jerk score was also 2.5 kg more than the national record of 122.5 kg. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 在上海秋季运动会上又有多项成绩打破全国纪录

1955-11-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　在上海秋季运动会上又有多项成绩打破全国纪录
    十三日，中央体育学院田径队在一九五五年上海市秋季运动会上又打破了两项全国最高纪录，创造了一项全国新成绩，还有两项成绩同全国最高纪录相等。
    男运动员朱明、林涛、吴在瑜、刘敬仁四人四百公尺接力赛的成绩达到了四十四秒，比全国最高纪录快了零点二秒。女子跳高全国最高纪录保持者傅雪雁，以一公尺五十一公分的成绩打破了一公尺四十八公分的全国最高纪录。冼少梅创造了女子掷铅球（四公斤）的全国新成绩。她的成绩是十一公尺六十七公分，比罗运琪今年四月在“一九五五年全国田径测验”的时候创造的成绩远了一公分。
    成绩同全国最高纪录相等的两个项目是：郑凤荣的女子跳高和徐世璋的女子四百公尺。郑凤荣的成绩是一公尺四十八公分。徐世璋的成绩是一分一秒七。
    轻工业工人蔡昌华、徐国庆、李正清在运动会的举重表演中创造了全国新成绩。在次重量级举重中，蔡昌华推举、抓举和挺举的总成绩是三百公斤，同全国最高纪录相等，其中挺举的成绩达到一百一十五公斤，打破了一百一十二点五公斤的全国最高纪录。徐国庆在次重量级举重中的挺举成绩比全国最高纪录多了七公斤半。在中量级举重中，李正清挺举的成绩也比一百二十二点五公斤的全国最高纪录多了二公斤半。　　（据新华社讯）
