Title: 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition to Be Held in Beijing; Shooting Delegations from Romania, Bulgaria, Poland, Mongolia, and North Korea Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: The 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition will be held in Beijing from November 17th to 26th.

This international friendship shooting competition was suggested by the Soviet Union's Voluntary Association for Supporting the Army, Navy, and Air Force to the China Central National Defense Sports Club in November 1954 for organization by China. In February of this year, relevant departments in China established a preparatory committee and began preparatory work. In February, the preparatory committee organized the selection of shooting athletes from major cities in China, and in April, it held the selection of shooters from eight cities in Beijing.

The countries that have registered to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition at the invitation of the China Central National Defense Sports Club include the Soviet Union, North Korea, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, and Mongolia. The delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea participating in the competition will only participate in the men's competition events. The Democratic Republic of Vietnam is sending an observation delegation of three people to participate. Delegations from various countries have begun to arrive in Beijing successively.

The Organizing Committee and the Referee Committee of the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition have been formally established. The chairman of the organizing committee is Cai Shufan, the vice-chairmen are General Peng Shaohui and Rong Gaotang, and the chief referee is General Peng Shaohui.

The shooting range, which began construction in June of this year, has now been fully completed. This shooting range is named "Beijing Shooting Range" and is located at the foot of the Badachu Mountains in the western suburbs of Beijing, covering a total area of 265.5 mu. The shooting range has one 300-meter military rifle range with 70 target positions; one 50-meter and 100-meter small-caliber rifle range with 68 target positions; and one 25-meter small-caliber pistol shooting human target range with 10 target positions. The shooting range also has a two-story building attached, with dressing rooms for athletes from various countries, repair rooms, medical rooms, equipment rooms, referee rooms, conference rooms, weapons rooms, ammunition rooms, and a reviewing stand. In front of the reviewing stand is the parade ground.

According to Xinhua News Agency, the shooting delegation of the Romanian People's Republic Voluntary Association for Supporting National Defense, invited to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th, led by Andrei Niattu, First Vice Chairman of the Romanian People's Republic Voluntary Association for Supporting National Defense.

According to Xinhua News Agency, the shooting delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic Voluntary Association for Supporting National Defense, invited to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 8th, led by Zolo Kristev Kamanov, Chairman of the Bulgarian People's Republic Voluntary Association for Supporting National Defense.

According to Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: The shooting delegation of the Polish People's Republic League of Friends of Soldiers, the shooting delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the shooting delegation of the Korean Democratic People's Republic Fatherland Defense Support Association, invited by our country to participate in the upcoming 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition in Beijing, arrived in Beijing by plane and train respectively on the afternoon of the 9th.

The Polish League of Friends of Soldiers shooting delegation has 23 people, and the leader is Jan Zajdel; the Mongolian shooting delegation has 26 people, and the leader is Manjevn. Siayanabu; the North Korean shooting delegation has 15 people, and the leader is Kim Byung-yeon.

### 一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛将在京举行  罗、保、波、蒙、朝射击代表团先后到京

1955-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛将在京举行
    罗、保、波、蒙、朝射击代表团先后到京
    新华社九日讯　一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛将在十一月十七日到二十六日在北京举行。
    这项国际友谊射击竞赛，是苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会在一九五四年十一月向中国中央国防体育俱乐部建议由中国组织的。今年二月，中国各有关部门成立了筹备委员会，开始进行筹备工作。在二月间，筹备委员会组织了中国各大城市射击运动员的选拔工作，四月间在北京举行了八个城市射手的选拔。
    应中国中央国防体育俱乐部的邀请报名参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的，有苏联、朝鲜、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古等国。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国参加竞赛的代表团只参加男子竞赛项目。越南民主共和国派三人组成的观察代表团参加。各国代表团已开始陆续到达北京。
    一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛组织委员会和裁判委员会已正式成立。组织委员会主席为蔡树藩、副主席为彭绍辉上将和荣高棠，总裁判为彭绍辉上将。
    在今年六月间开始修建的靶场现已全部完工。这个靶场定名为“北京射击场”，它设在北京西郊八大处山麓，共占地二百六十五点五亩。射击场计有三百公尺军用步枪靶场一个，内有七十个靶位；五十公尺和一百公尺小口径步枪靶场一个，内有六十八个靶位；二十五公尺小口径手枪射人像靶场一个，内有十个靶位。射击场还附属二层楼房一幢，设有各国运动员更衣室、修械室、医疗室、器材室、裁判室、会议室、武器室、弹药室和检阅台。检阅台前为检阅场。
    据新华社讯　应邀参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的罗马尼亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会射击代表团，在罗马尼亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会第一副主席安德烈·尼亚图率领下，在八日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    据新华社讯　应邀参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的保加利亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会射击代表团一行二十人，在保加利亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会主席佐洛·克里斯捷夫·卡曼诺夫率领下，在八日晚乘火车到达北京。
    据新华社九日讯　应我国邀请参加即将在北京举行的一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的波兰人民共和国战士之友同盟射击代表团、蒙古人民共和国射击代表团和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国祖国保卫后援会射击代表团，在九日下午分别乘飞机和火车到达北京。
    波兰战士之友同盟射击代表团有二十三人，团长是扬·扎伊德尔；蒙古射击代表团有二十六人，团长是曼热文·西亚尼亚布；朝鲜射击代表团有十五人，团长是金秉渊。
