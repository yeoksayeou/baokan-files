Title: A Batch of Soviet Experts Arrive in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-11-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 21st. According to a report in the "Hindustan Standard," a group of Soviet experts arrived in Delhi on the 18th. They will provide the Indian government with advice on the development of India's oil resources.

### 一批苏联专家到达德里

1955-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　一批苏联专家到达德里
    新华社新德里二十一日电　据“印度斯坦旗报”报道，一批苏联专家已经在十八日到达德里。他们将向印度政府提供开发印度石油资源问题的意见。
