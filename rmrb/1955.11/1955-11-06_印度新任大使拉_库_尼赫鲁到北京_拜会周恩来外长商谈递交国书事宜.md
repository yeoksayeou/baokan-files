Title: Indian New Ambassador R. K. Nehru Arrives in Beijing to Meet Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1955-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th – The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to China, R. K. Nehru, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 5th. Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, were at the station to welcome them.

Personnel from the Embassy of the Republic of India in China, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, also welcomed them at the station.

On the afternoon of the 5th, Ambassador R. K. Nehru, accompanied by Counselor Bahadur Singh, paid a visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to discuss the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Asian Department, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 印度新任大使拉·库·尼赫鲁到北京  拜会周恩来外长商谈递交国书事宜

1955-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度新任大使拉·库·尼赫鲁到北京
    拜会周恩来外长商谈递交国书事宜
    新华社五日讯　印度共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人在五日上午乘火车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有我外交部礼宾司司长柯华、中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙和中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君等人。
    印度共和国驻华大使馆人员以及各国驻华使节和外交官员也到车站欢迎。
    五日下午，拉·库·尼赫鲁大使偕参赞巴哈杜尔·辛格到外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有：外交部部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康和礼宾司司长柯华。
