Title: East German Deputy Prime Minister Visits Egypt
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 6th (TASS): At the invitation of the Egyptian government, Heinrich Rau, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Cairo on the 6th for a visit.

At Cairo Airport, Heinrich Rau was greeted by Egyptian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Transportation Gamal Salem, Minister of Industry and Commerce Muhammad Abu Nusseir, Minister of Finance Keshudi, and officials from various Egyptian ministries, as well as the Soviet Ambassador to Egypt, Dan. Xie. Solod, and diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries to Egypt.

Egyptian newspapers believe that Heinrich Rau's arrival in Cairo is related to the successful completion of negotiations between the East German economic delegation currently in Cairo and the relevant Egyptian departments and economic groups.

### 民主德国副总理到埃及访问

1955-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国副总理到埃及访问
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社开罗六日讯：德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与国内贸易部长亨利希·劳应埃及政府的邀请，六日到达开罗进行访问。
    在开罗机场迎接亨利希·劳的有埃及副总理兼交通部长加麦尔·萨勒姆、工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔、财政部长凯舒迪和埃及各部的官员以及苏联驻埃及大使丹·谢·索洛德和各人民民主国家驻埃及的外交使节。
    埃及报纸认为，亨利希·劳的到达开罗是同目前在开罗的德意志民主共和国经济代表团和埃及的有关部门和经济团体之间的谈判顺利完成有关的。
