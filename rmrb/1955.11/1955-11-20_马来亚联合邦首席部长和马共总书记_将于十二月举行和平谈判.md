Title: Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya and General Secretary of the Malayan Communist Party to Hold Peace Talks in December
Author:
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 19th. According to a Reuters Singapore dispatch on the 18th, negotiations between Chen Ping, General Secretary of the Malayan Communist Party, and Rahman, Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, regarding the realization of peace in Malaya, are expected to take place around the middle of December.

　　The Reuters dispatch said that officials of the Federation of Malaya government met with Chen Tian, a representative of the Malayan Communist Party, on the 18th in the village of Kroh, northern Malaya, to discuss arrangements for the aforementioned negotiations. Prior to this, the two sides had held a consultation on October 17th at the same location.

### 马来亚联合邦首席部长和马共总书记  将于十二月举行和平谈判

1955-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　马来亚联合邦首席部长和马共总书记
　  将于十二月举行和平谈判
    新华社十九日讯　据路透社新加坡十八日消息，马来亚共产党总书记陈平和马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼之间的关于实现马来亚和平的谈判大约在十二月月中举行。
    路透社的消息说，马来亚联合邦政府的官员十八日在马来亚北部的克连英丹村同马来亚共产党代表陈田会商了关于安排上述谈判的问题。在这以前，双方曾经在十月十七日在同一地点举行过一次会商。
