Title: Part of Xuanzang's Cranial Bone Snatched to Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: According to Kyodo News Agency, Tokyo, part of the cranial bone of the Chinese Buddhist sage, Xuanzang, was snatched to Taiwan by Chiang Kai-shek's agents from Tokyo on the 25th.

Zhao Puchu, Secretary-General of the Chinese Buddhist Association, telegraphed the All-Japan Buddhist Association twice, on the 5th and 22nd of this month, requesting them to prevent this action that damages the friendly relations between Buddhist believers of China and Japan. However, the All-Japan Buddhist Association, under the coercion of a small number of individuals with ulterior motives in the Japanese Buddhist community, failed to take action in accordance with the wishes of the Chinese Buddhist community.

### 玄奘部分顶骨被劫往台湾

1955-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    玄奘部分顶骨被劫往台湾
    新华社二十五日讯　据共同社东京讯，中国佛教先哲玄奘法师顶骨的一部分，在二十五日被蒋贼分子由东京劫往台湾。
    中国佛教协会秘书长赵朴初曾在本月五日、二十二日两次致电全日本佛教会，要求阻止这种破坏中日两国佛教徒友好关系的行动，但是，全日本佛教会在日本佛教界的少数别有用心的分子的挟持下，未能作出符合中国佛教界愿望的行动。
