Title: China People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Japan's National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution Reach Agreement on Various Issues Concerning Cultural Exchange
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The China People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Japan's National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution held a signing ceremony today for the agreement reached on various issues concerning cultural exchange.

　　The signing ceremony was held at 10:30 PM on the 27th in the Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

　　Signing the agreement on behalf of the China People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries were Zhao Yimin, Yang Hansheng, Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, Ma Sicong, Liu Kaiqu, Cai Chusheng, Dai Ailian, and Chen Zhongjing.

　　Signing the agreement on behalf of Japan's National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution were Tohtaro Fujita, Akira Nakamura, Koreya Senda, Akiki Inamura, Satoshi Nakazaki, Kenji Ishihara, Kozo Inomata, Masahiro Asai, and Koichi Ohno.

　　Chinese participants in the signing ceremony included Xia Yan, Wu Huazhi, Wu Maosun, and Hua Junwu, all members of the Council of the China People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Vice Presidents Hu Yuzhi, Chen Hansheng, Qian Duansheng, and Luo Longji; Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi; Li Jiebo and Dong Xin, Secretaries of the Secretariat; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee; prominent figures from the Chinese literary and art circles Chen Baichen, Yu Fei'an, Meng Bo, Sun Weishi, and Huang Yuhua; Zhao Anbo and Liu Jinzhong, members of the Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Chen Yu, member of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Ma Chungu, Director of the International Liaison Department.

　　Japanese participants in the signing ceremony included Tetsu Katayama, Chairman of Japan's National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution and head of the Japanese "National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution" delegation to China; members of the Japanese "National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution" delegation to China Ayako Ishigaki, Chikako Yoshii, Mitsuo Imai, Masatake Hashida, Kinichi Kubota, Saburo Igarashi, Tamimin Kura, Kazuyoshi Tani, Hisao Maruyama, Masao Yokote, Shigematsu Matsui, Masayoshi Sakurai, and Yasuo Ogura; Secretary Kyuhei Muraoka, and reporter Shinichi Ogawa.

### 中国人民对外文化协会和日本拥护宪法国民联合会　就有关文化交流各项问题达成协议

1955-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民对外文化协会和日本拥护宪法国民联合会
  　就有关文化交流各项问题达成协议
    新华社二十七日讯　中国人民对外文化协会和日本拥护宪法国民联合会今日就有关文化交流各项问题达成的协议举行签字仪式。
    签字仪式在二十七日下午十时半于北京中南海紫光阁举行。
    中国人民对外文化协会方面在协议上签字的，有赵毅敏、阳翰笙、老舍、欧阳予倩、马思聪、刘开渠、蔡楚生、戴爱莲、陈忠经。
    日本拥护宪法国民联合会方面在协议上签字的，有藤田藤太郎、中村哲、千田是也、稻村明喜、中崎敏、石原宪治、猪俣浩三、浅井正弘、大野幸一。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中国人民对外文化协会理事夏衍、吴化之、吴茂荪、华君武，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长胡愈之、陈翰笙、钱端升、罗隆基，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席刘宁一，书记处书记李颉伯、董昕，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国文艺界著名人士陈白尘、于非闇、孟波、孙维世、黄玉华，中国人民外交学会理事赵安博、刘进中，中华全国总工会执行委员会委员陈宇，国际联络部部长马纯古。
    日本方面参加签字仪式的，有日本拥护宪法国民联合会主席、日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团团长片山哲；日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团团员石垣绫子、吉井千贺子、今井三男、桥田正武、洼田钧一、五十岚三郎、田村民藏、谷沿嘉瑞、丸山久雄、横手真夫、松井繁松、樱井政由、小仓康男。秘书村冈久平、记者小川信一。
