Title: Comrade Chen Yun Receives Soviet Youth Delegation; Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds Banquet to See Off Soviet and Vietnamese Youth Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st November: Comrade Chen Yun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received all members of the Soviet Youth Delegation on the evening of October 31st. The delegation was invited to China to participate in the National Congress of Active Builders of Socialism. During the reception, Zarubin, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation and Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, spoke about the delegation's impressions of its visit to China. Chen Yun expressed his gratitude for the assistance provided by the delegation to the Chinese Communist Youth League during their visit to China, and introduced the situation of China's socialist transformation of agriculture and the transformation of capitalist industry and commerce. The conversation lasted for three hours.

Present at the reception were Liu Lantao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Sun Zhiyuan, Deputy Director of the Third Office of the State Council, and Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st November: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 1st to bid farewell to the Soviet and Vietnamese Youth Delegations, who were invited to China to participate in the National Congress of Active Builders of Socialism and to visit various parts of China.

All members of the Soviet Youth Delegation, headed by Zarubin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and all members of the Vietnamese Youth Delegation, headed by Hoang Van Dinh, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam National Salvation Youth League, attended the banquet.

The hosts of tonight's banquet were Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Rong Gaotang, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League. Heads of various departments of the Central Committee of the League and heads of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League also attended the banquet.

After the banquet, there was an evening party of Peking Opera, singing, and dancing, and a ball. (Attached picture)

### 陈云同志接见苏联青年代表团  青年团中央设宴欢送苏、越青年代表团

1955-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云同志接见苏联青年代表团
    青年团中央设宴欢送苏、越青年代表团
    新华社一日讯　中共中央书记处书记陈云十月三十一日晚接见了应邀来中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的苏联青年代表团全体团员。在接见时，苏联青年代表团团长、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记扎鲁日尼谈了代表团在中国访问的观感。陈云对代表团在中国访问期间对我国青年团所作的帮助表示感谢，并向代表团介绍了我国实行农业社会主义改造和资本主义工商业改造的情况。谈话进行了三小时。
    接见时在座的有中共中央副秘书长刘澜涛，国务院第三办公室副主任孙志远，青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅、章泽、胡克实等。
    新华社一日讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会一日晚在北京饭店举行宴会，欢送应邀来中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会并到中国各地访问的苏联青年代表团和越南青年代表团。
    以苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记扎鲁日尼为首的苏联青年代表团全体团员和以越南救国青年团中央常务委员黄文鼎为首的越南青年代表团全体团员出席了宴会。
    今天宴会的主人是中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅、荣高棠、章泽、胡克实。团中央各部负责人和青年团北京市委员会负责人也出席了宴会。
    宴会后，举行了京剧、歌唱、舞蹈晚会和舞会。（附图片）
