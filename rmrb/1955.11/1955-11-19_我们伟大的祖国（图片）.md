Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region had a bumper cotton harvest this year. The first batch of 42,000 jin of new cotton was transported from Urumqi to Shaanxi to supply the needs of the textile industry in this area. This is a picture of trucks full of new cotton about to depart from Urumqi.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆维吾尔自治区今年棉花丰收，第一批新棉四万二千斤从乌鲁木齐市运往陕西，供应这个地区的纺织工业生产需要。这是满载新棉的汽车正要从乌鲁木齐出发。
