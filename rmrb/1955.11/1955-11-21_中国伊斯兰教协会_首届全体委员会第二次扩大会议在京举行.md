Title: The Second Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the First National Committee of the China Islamic Association Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The Second Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the First National Committee of the China Islamic Association was held in Beijing from the 8th to the 19th of November. Attending the meeting were 184 individuals, including the Director and Deputy Directors of the China Islamic Association, committee members, and representatives from Islamic circles across the country, Ahongs, and Mullahs, representing ten ethnic groups practicing Islam, including Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Dongxiang.

    Burhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, delivered the opening address at the meeting. Deputy Directors Yusuf Ma Yu Huai, Nur Muhammad Dapusheng, and Imin Ma Hesumu successively presented reports on "The Work of the China Islamic Association Over the Past Two Years and Opinions on Future Work," "Report on the Pilgrimage and Visit of the China Islamic Pilgrimage Delegation," and "Report on Preparations for the Establishment of the China Islamic Theological Seminary." Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, also delivered a report on current affairs and policies at the meeting.

    In Yusuf Ma Yu Huai's report on the work of the Islamic Association over the past two years and opinions on future work, he stated that in the two years since its establishment, the Islamic Association has conducted patriotic education and education on the policy of religious freedom among Islamic circles, received Islamic figures from Asian and African countries who came to China to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day and National Day celebrations and to visit China, and introduced them to the situation of Chinese Muslims after liberation. Responsible persons of the Islamic Association have accompanied Chinese delegations to the Asian-African Conference held in Bandung and the World Peace Conference held in Helsinki. At these conferences, friendly contacts were made with Islamic figures from various countries. With the assistance of the government in many aspects, the Islamic Association also organized a pilgrimage delegation to Mecca, Saudi Arabia, fulfilling the pilgrimage wish, one of the five major commandments of Muslims.

    Yusuf Ma Yu Huai said that the Islamic Association translated the "Constitution of the People's Republic of China" into Arabic and printed the "Quran," and filmed the life of Muslims in Beijing with Chinese, Arabic, and English narration, and also edited a picture album of "The Life of Chinese Muslims."

    The committee members and Ahongs and Mullahs attending the meeting discussed the above reports and introduced the rapid development of the people of various ethnic groups who believe in Islam in their localities in terms of politics, economy, and culture during the national socialist construction and socialist transformation. They unanimously agreed that the destiny of the people of all ethnic groups who believe in Islam is closely linked to the destiny of the motherland. In the future, they must continue to participate in and support the country's socialist construction and socialist transformation, especially to support and assist the enthusiasm of farmers of various ethnic groups who believe in Islam to take the road of cooperation, and help them solve the difficulties encountered after joining cooperatives and the specific problems arising from differences in religious beliefs or customs in jointly forming cooperatives with other ethnic groups.

    The meeting determined that the future tasks of the China Islamic Association are: to continue to conduct in-depth patriotic education among Islamic circles, actively support and assist the country’s socialist construction and the socialist transformation of agriculture, handicrafts, and capitalist industry and commerce, actively carry out international peace and friendship activities, and organize pilgrimage delegations.

    Burhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, emphasized the relationship between the country's socialist construction and socialist transformation and Islamic circles in his concluding speech at the conference, and answered the religious questions and requests raised by the committee members and representatives at the meeting.

    Yang Peizhi, Ding Qishan, Ma Mengda, Zhao Mingzhou, Shang Xixian, Huo Liu, Ma Renbin, Ma Quanqin, and Ma Huiting were elected as members of the China Islamic Association in a by-election. Resolutions were passed endorsing the report on the work of the China Islamic Association over the past two years and opinions on future work, the report on the pilgrimage and visit of the China Islamic Pilgrimage Delegation, and the report on preparations for the establishment of the China Islamic Theological Seminary.

    The meeting also passed a resolution supporting the struggle of the people of North Africa and other Arab countries against colonialism and for national independence.

### 中国伊斯兰教协会　首届全体委员会第二次扩大会议在京举行

1955-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国伊斯兰教协会
　　首届全体委员会第二次扩大会议在京举行
    据新华社讯　中国伊斯兰教协会第一届全体委员会第二次扩大会议，八日到十九日在北京举行。出席这次会议的有中国伊斯兰教协会主任、副主任和委员以及全国各地伊斯兰教界代表、阿訇、毛拉等共一百八十四人，包括回、维吾尔、哈萨克和东乡等十个信仰伊斯兰教的民族成分。
    中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉在会上致开幕词，副主任优素福·马玉槐、努尔·穆罕默德·达浦生、伊明·马合苏木在会上先后作了“关于中国伊斯兰教协会两年来的工作情况和今后工作意见的报告”、“关于中国伊斯兰教朝觐团朝觐及访问经过的报告”和“关于筹办中国伊斯兰教经学院工作的报告”。中共中央统一战线工作部副部长汪锋也在会上作了时事和政策的报告。
    优素福·马玉槐在伊斯兰教协会两年来的工作情况和今后工作意见的报告中说，伊斯兰教协会成立两年来，对伊斯兰教界进行了爱国主义的教育和宗教信仰自由政策的教育，接待了来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节、国庆节观礼和前来我国访问的亚非国家的伊斯兰教界的人士，向他们介绍了解放以后的中国穆斯林的情况。伊斯兰教协会的负责人曾随同中国代表团参加了在万隆举行的亚非会议和在赫尔辛基举行的世界和平大会。在这些会议上同各国的伊斯兰教人士进行了友谊的联系。在政府多方面的帮助下，伊斯兰教协会还组织了朝觐团到沙特阿拉伯的麦加朝觐，实现了穆斯林的五大大命之一的朝觐的愿望。
    优素福·马玉槐说，伊斯兰教协会用阿拉伯文翻译了“中华人民共和国宪法”和印制了“古兰经”，并且把北京穆斯林的生活拍成了用中文、阿拉伯文和英文解说的影片，还编辑了“中国穆斯林生活”的画册。
    出席会议的委员和阿訇、毛拉讨论了以上几个报告，并且介绍了在国家社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业中，本地信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民在政治、经济和文化方面迅速发展的情况。他们一致认为，信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民的命运是同祖国的命运紧密地连系在一起的，今后要继续参加和支持国家的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业，特别是要支持和赞助信仰伊斯兰教的各族农民走合作化道路的热情，帮助他们解决参加合作社以后遇到的困难和在同其他民族联合组成合作社中因宗教信仰或风俗习惯不同而产生的具体问题。
    会议确定中国伊斯兰教协会的今后工作任务是：继续在伊斯兰教界中深入地进行爱国主义教育，积极支持和赞助国家的社会主义建设和对农业、手工业、资本主义工商业的社会主义改造，积极进行国际和平友好活动，并且组织朝觐团。
    中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉在大会总结中，着重地说明了国家的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业同伊斯兰教界的关系，并且对各委员和代表在会上提出的有关宗教的问题和要求作了答复。
    会议补选了杨培之、丁歧山、马梦达、赵铭周、尚希贤、霍流、马人斌、马全钦、马惠亭九人为中国伊斯兰教协会委员。通过了拥护关于中国伊斯兰教协会两年来的工作情况和今后工作意见的报告的决议、关于中国伊斯兰教朝觐团朝觐及访问经过的报告的决议和关于筹办中国伊斯兰教经学院工作的报告的决议。
    会议还通过了支援北非及其他阿拉伯各国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立斗争的决议。
