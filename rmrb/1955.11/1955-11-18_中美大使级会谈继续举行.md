Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 17th: The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their 26th meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on November 17th. The meeting adjourned at 11:55 a.m. The next meeting will be held on November 23rd.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦十七日电　中美两国大使级会谈十一月十七日上午十时在国联大厦举行二十六次会议。会议在十一时五十五分休会。下次会议将在十一月二十三日举行。
