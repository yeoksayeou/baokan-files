Title: People from All Circles in the Capital Hold a Grand Meeting Yesterday to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th Dispatch:

　　People from All Circles in the Capital Hold a Grand Meeting Yesterday to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia

    The grand meeting of people from all circles in the capital to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia was solemnly held at the auditorium of the State Council in Beijing on the evening of the 28th. The celebration meeting was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

    In the venue, a huge red banner with golden characters was hung high, reading "Long Live the Friendly Cooperation Between the Chinese and Yugoslav Peoples!" in both Chinese and Yugoslav languages. On the curtain in the center of the rostrum, there were portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and President Tito, along with the national flags of China and Yugoslavia. In the hall of the venue, workers, government officials, scientists, artists, and people from all walks of life, heads of various departments of the Chinese government and people's organizations, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries in China, were seated.

    The meeting began at 7:30 PM. On the rostrum were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Zhang Wentian, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; and Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade. The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, was also on the rostrum. When they entered the venue and ascended the rostrum, the hall resounded with prolonged applause.

    Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, presided over the meeting and delivered a brief opening speech. After he announced the opening of the meeting, the orchestra played the national anthems of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the People's Republic of China.

    Zhang Wentian, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, first spoke at the meeting (full text on page 3). On behalf of the Chinese people and government, he extended sincere congratulations and respect to the Yugoslav people and the government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, then spoke at the meeting (full text on page 3), and was warmly welcomed by those present.

    At the celebration meeting, Chinese literary and artistic workers presented wonderful song and dance performances. Chinese young art workers who were studying with the Yugoslav "Kolo" folk dance troupe performed Yugoslav folk dances and songs.

### 庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年　首都各界人民昨日举行大会

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年
　　首都各界人民昨日举行大会
    据新华社二十八日讯　首都各界人民庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年大会，二十八日晚在北京国务院礼堂隆重举行。庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。
    会场中高悬着巨幅的红底金字标语，上面用中南两国文字写着：“中南两国人民友好合作万岁！”大会主席台正中的帷幕上，悬挂着毛泽东主席、铁托总统的画像和中南两国国旗。在会场的大厅中，坐满了工人、机关干部、科学家、艺术家和各界人士，中国政府各部门和各人民团体负责人，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    大会在下午七时三十分举行。在主席台上的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理李先念，秘书长习仲勋，中共中央政治局委员、中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，对外贸易部部长叶季壮。南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也在主席台上。当他们走进会场登上主席台的时候，场内响起了经久不息的掌声。
    文化部代理部长钱俊瑞主持大会并致简短的开会词。他宣布大会开始后，乐队高奏南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国和中华人民共和国国歌。
    中共中央政治局委员、中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天首先在会上讲话（全文见第三版），他代表中国人民和政府，向南斯拉夫人民和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府表示衷心的祝贺和敬意。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇接着在会上讲话（全文见第三版），受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
    在庆祝大会上，中国文艺工作者作了精彩的歌舞表演，随同南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团学习的中国青年艺术工作者表演了南斯拉夫民间舞蹈和歌曲。
