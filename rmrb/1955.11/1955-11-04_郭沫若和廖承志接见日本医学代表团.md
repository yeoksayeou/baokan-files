Title: Guo Moruo and Liao Chengzhi Receive Japanese Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received the Japanese medical delegation headed by Honorary Head Abe Katsuma and Head Domori Yoshio on the afternoon of the 3rd.

Present at the meeting were Fang Shishan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association, Zhong Huilan, an expert in internal medicine, Xue Yu, a pharmacology expert, and Meng Jimou, a surgical expert.

### 郭沫若和廖承志接见日本医学代表团

1955-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若和廖承志接见日本医学代表团
    新华社三日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，在三日下午接见了以名誉团长阿部胜马、团长堂森芳夫为首的日本医学代表团。
    接见时在座的有，中华医学会副理事长方石珊、内科专家钟惠澜、药学专家薛愚、外科专家孟继懋。
