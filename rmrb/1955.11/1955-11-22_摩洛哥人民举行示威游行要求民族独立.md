Title: Moroccan People Hold Demonstrations Demanding National Independence
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-22
Page: 4
　
　　Moroccan People Hold Demonstrations Demanding National Independence
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 20th: According to TASS, Moroccan people held mass demonstrations in some cities of Morocco to support the demand for national independence for Morocco. For example, the Independence Party and the Democratic Independence Party organized demonstrations in Casablanca. According to Agence France-Presse, tens of thousands of people participated in the demonstrations. In Meknes, demonstrators clashed with the police, resulting in some casualties.

### 摩洛哥人民举行示威游行要求民族独立

1955-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥人民举行示威游行要求民族独立
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社巴黎二十日讯：摩洛哥人民在摩洛哥一些城市举行了群众示威游行，支持关于给摩洛哥以民族独立的要求。例如，独立党和独立民主党在卡萨布兰卡组织了示威游行，据法新社报道，参加这次游行的有几万人。在麦克尼斯，游行者同警察发生冲突，结果有一些人伤亡。
