Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Hungarian Mining Equipment Exhibition Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, held a banquet on the 17th to host the Hungarian Mining Equipment Exhibition Delegation, headed by Vilmos Becsy.

Personnel from the Hungarian Embassy in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

Those present at the banquet included He Changgong and Xu Jie, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Geology of the People's Republic of China; Li Fan Yi, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry; Zhong Ziyun, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry; Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and responsible persons from relevant departments and institutions of higher learning.

Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, delivered a speech at the banquet. He said that Hungary has high technology and rich experience in geological work, and the exhibition of Hungarian mining equipment will be of great help to China's industrial construction.

Vilmos Becsy, head of the Hungarian Mining Equipment Exhibition Delegation, said in his speech that through this exhibition, the friendship between the people of China and Hungary and the cooperation of geological workers will be further enhanced.

### 南汉宸招待匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团

1955-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　南汉宸招待匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团
    新华社十七日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸十七日举行宴会，招待以维尔摩什·贝西为首的匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国地质部副部长何长工、许杰，石油工业部副部长李范一，煤炭工业部部长助理钟子云，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，以及有关部门和高等院校负责人。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸在宴会上致词。他说，匈牙利在地质工作方面有很高的技术和很丰富的经验，这次匈牙利探矿设备展览会的展出，将对我国的工业建设有很大的帮助。
    匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团团长维尔摩什·贝西在讲话中说，通过这次展出，将会进一步增进中匈两国人民的友谊和地质工作者的合作。
