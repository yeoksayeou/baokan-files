Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting, Approves Decision on Handling Illegal Books and Magazines

Author: 
Date: 1955-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its twenty-third meeting on November 8th.

    The meeting approved the "Decision on Handling Illegal Books and Magazines."

    The meeting also approved the "Decision on Whether Members of Local People's Committees at All Levels Are Limited to Deputies of the People's Congresses at the Corresponding Level" and the "Decision on Supplementing Vacancies for Governors, Chairmen of Autonomous Regions, Mayors, Heads of Autonomous Prefectures, County Heads, District Heads, Township Heads, Town Heads, and Presidents of Local People's Courts at All Levels During the Recess of Local People's Congresses at All Levels."

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  通过关于处理违法的图书杂志等决定

1955-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    通过关于处理违法的图书杂志等决定
    新华社八日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十一月八日举行第二十三次会议。
    会议通过了“关于处理违法的图书杂志的决定”。
    会议还通过了“关于地方各级人民委员会的组成人员是否限于本级人民代表大会代表问题的决定”和“关于在地方各级人民代表大会闭会期间省长自治区主席市长州长县长区长乡长镇长和地方各级人民法院院长缺额补充问题的决定”。
