Title: Yao Zhongming Holds Reception to Celebrate Signing of Sino-Burmese Shipping Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 13th. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, held a reception on the evening of November 10th to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Burmese Air Transport Agreement.

　　Those invited to attend the reception included U Thein Maung, Chief Justice of the Burmese Supreme Court, Judge U Min Thein, U Shwe Mra, Secretary of the Ministry of Transport and Communications, and other officials.

　　Also invited to the reception were the consuls from India, the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, Pakistan, Indonesia, Czechoslovakia in Rangoon, and the representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Burma.

　　General Wu Faxian, head of the Chinese Civil Aviation Delegation, and all members of the delegation also attended the reception.

　　A Chinese film was shown at the reception. The reception ended in a friendly atmosphere.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 13th. "Burma New Light of Rangoon" published an editorial on November 10th, welcoming the signing of the Sino-Burmese Air Transport Agreement.

　　The editorial stated: Although China and Burma are neighbors, transportation in the past was very inconvenient. The signing of this Sino-Burmese Air Transport Agreement will not only strengthen economic and cultural exchanges between the two countries, but will also help promote understanding between the two countries and make friendly relations even closer in the future.

　　The editorial also stated: The opening of the Sino-Burmese air route also provides great convenience to the people of Southeast Asian countries who want to travel to China.

　　The editorial believes that the air route between the two countries stipulated in the agreement is "very suitable" and beneficial to both governments.

### 姚仲明举行招待会庆祝中缅签订航运协定

1955-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　姚仲明举行招待会庆祝中缅签订航运协定
    新华社十三日讯　仰光消息：中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明在十一月十日晚举行招待会，庆祝中缅两国航空运输协定的签订。
    应邀出席招待会的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴登貌、法官吴敏登、运输和交通部秘书吴瑞马拉和其他官员。
    应邀参加招待会的还有印度、苏联、南斯拉夫、巴基斯坦、印度尼西亚、捷克斯洛伐克驻仰光的领事和越南民主共和国驻缅甸的代表等。
    中国民用航空代表团团长吴法宪将军和代表团全体人员也出席了招待会。
    会上放映了中国电影。招待会在友好空气中结束。
    新华社十三日讯　仰光消息：“缅甸新光报”十一月十日发表社论，欢迎中缅两国航空运输协定的签订。
    社论说：中缅虽然是邻国，但是过去的交通很不方便。这次中缅两国航空运输协定的签订不仅可以使两国经济、文化交流有所加强，而且必将有助于两国之间的了解，使友好关系今后更加密切。
    社论又说：中缅航线的开辟也给予想去中国旅行的东南亚各国人民以很大的方便。
    社论认为协定规定的两国间的航线是“非常适宜的”，而且对两国政府都是有利的。
