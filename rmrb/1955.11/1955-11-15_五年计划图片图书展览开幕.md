Title: Five-Year Plan Photo and Book Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 3

　  Five-Year Plan Photo and Book Exhibition Opens
    The "First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy Photo and Book Exhibition" held by the Beijing Library opened on the 12th.
    The exhibition displays more than 200 pictures, introducing the backward economic situation of old China and the achievements of new China in ending the period of national economic recovery, explaining the basis for formulating my country's first five-year plan and its grand scale. The "Schematic Diagram of Key Construction Projects of the First Five-Year Plan" reflects the new look of the motherland after the completion of the first five-year plan. From the pictures on display, we can also see the will of the people across the country to strive to complete and over fulfill the first five-year plan, and we can also see the enthusiastic assistance of Soviet experts and experts from various people's democratic countries in our country's construction.
    Nearly two hundred reference books and periodicals related to the Five-Year Plan were also exhibited. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 五年计划图片图书展览开幕

1955-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

　  五年计划图片图书展览开幕
    北京图书馆举办的“发展国民经济的第一个五年计划图片图书展览”在十二日开幕。
    展览会展出二百多幅图片，介绍了旧中国落后的经济状况和新中国在结束国民经济恢复阶段的成就，说明了制定我国第一个五年计划的基础和它的宏伟规模。“第一个五年计划主要建设示意略图”反映出第一个五年计划完成以后的祖国的新面貌。从展出的图片中，还可以看到全国人民为完成和超额完成第一个五年计划而奋斗的意志，也可以看到苏联专家及各人民民主国家专家热心帮助我国建设的情况。
    同时展出的还有近二百册有关五年计划的参考书刊。　（新华社）
