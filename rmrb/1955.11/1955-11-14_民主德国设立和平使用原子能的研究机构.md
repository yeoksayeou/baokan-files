Title: East Germany Establishes Research Institution for Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 12th: TASS reported from Berlin on November 11th: The Press Office of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic announced that the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a joint meeting with prominent scientists and technical experts on the 10th, and the meeting adopted a decision on the peaceful use of atomic energy.

　　According to this decision, a Scientific Committee for the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy will be established under the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic. Professor Gustav Hertz will serve as the chairman of this committee. At the same time, it was also decided to establish the Nuclear Research and Nuclear Technology Department and the National Institute of Nuclear Physics.

### 民主德国设立和平使用原子能的研究机构

1955-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国设立和平使用原子能的研究机构
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社柏林十一日讯：德意志民主共和国总理新闻处宣布，德意志民主共和国部长会议在十日同著名的科学家和技术专家举行了联席会议，会议通过了一项关于和平使用原子能的决定。
    根据这个决定，在德意志民主共和国部长会议之下设立一个和平使用原子能的科学委员会。这个委员会将由古斯塔夫·赫茨教授担任主席。同时还决定设立核子研究与核子技术处和国立核子物理学研究所。
