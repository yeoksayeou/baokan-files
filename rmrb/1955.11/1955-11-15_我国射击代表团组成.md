Title: Composition of China's Shooting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The shooting delegation of the Central National Defense Sports Club of the People's Republic of China, which will participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition, has been officially formed. The delegation has a total of twenty-five people, the head of the delegation is Ji He, and the coaches are Qian Jinfu and Dai Haichang. Among the twenty marksmen, six are female marksmen.
　　China's shooting delegation will participate in all competition events.

### 我国射击代表团组成

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　  我国射击代表团组成
    新华社十四日讯　参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部射击代表团已经正式组成。代表团共有二十五人，团长是吉合，教练员是钱锦福和戴海常。在二十名射击运动员中，有六名是女子射击运动员。
    中国的射击代表团将参加全部竞赛项目。
