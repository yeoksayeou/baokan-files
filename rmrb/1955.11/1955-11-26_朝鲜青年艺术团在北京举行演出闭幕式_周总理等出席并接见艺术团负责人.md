Title: Korean Youth Art Troupe Holds Closing Ceremony in Beijing; Premier Zhou and Others Attend and Receive Leaders of the Art Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th -- The Korean Youth Art Troupe held its closing performance ceremony of its visit to China at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of the 25th. After the closing ceremony, the five-act opera "Kong Guk and Pan Guk" was performed.

Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Peng Dehuai attended the closing ceremony, watched the art troupe's performance, and received Choi Mun-gi, head of the Korean Youth Art Troupe, Kim Ki-deok, conductor of the orchestra, Ju Yong-seop, director, and some members of the troupe.

Also present at the closing ceremony and performance were Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Liu Zhiming, Vice Minister, Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, and more than 1,500 people from literary and art circles and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Liu Zhiming delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League at the closing ceremony, congratulating the Korean Youth Art Troupe on the great success of its visit and performances in China. Liu Zhiming said: During the art troupe's visit and performances in China, it made outstanding contributions to continuously enhancing the friendship between the people of China and Korea, which was forged in battle with blood.

Choi Mun-gi, head of the art troupe, then delivered a speech, saying: We have conducted a friendly visit and artistic performances in China for more than fifty days. In our lives, this period is full of unforgettable and moving events. Everywhere we have been, we have felt the love and friendship of the Chinese people for the Korean people, seen the achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction, and learned from the rich experience of the Chinese people in building socialism. Choi Mun-gi thanked the Chinese people for their friendly assistance to the Korean people, and thanked the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League for their kind reception and care. He concluded by shouting long live the unbreakable friendship between the people of Korea and China!

The performance of the Korean young artists was a great success tonight. After each act of the opera, thunderous applause rang out in the theater. The performers repeatedly bowed in front of the red curtain. At the end of the entire play, the audience in the hall stood up and applauded warmly for several minutes. Young literary and art workers in the capital presented flowers to all members of the art troupe. At this time, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, Qian Junrui, Liu Zhiming, Luo Yi, and others walked onto the stage, shook hands with the Korean young artists, expressing their regards and congratulations. ### 朝鲜青年艺术团在北京举行演出闭幕式  周总理等出席并接见艺术团负责人

1955-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜青年艺术团在北京举行演出闭幕式
　  周总理等出席并接见艺术团负责人
    据新华社二十五日讯　朝鲜青年艺术团二十五日晚在北京天桥剧场举行访华演出闭幕式。在闭幕式以后，演出了五幕歌剧“孔菊与潘菊”。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理彭德怀出席了闭幕式，观看了艺术团的表演，并且接见了朝鲜青年艺术团团长崔文岐、乐队指挥金基德、导演朱永涉和部分团员。
    出席闭幕式和观看表演的，还有文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长刘芝明，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫、部长助理陈家康，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅，中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，北京市副市长吴晗，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南和各国驻华外交官员，以及首都文艺界和各界人士等一千五百多人。
    刘芝明代表中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会在闭幕式上致词，祝贺朝鲜青年艺术团在中国访问演出的极大成功。刘芝明说：艺术团这次在中国访问演出期间，对于不断增进中朝两国人民在战斗中用鲜血凝成的友谊，作了卓越的贡献。
    艺术团团长崔文岐接着致词说：我们在中国进行了历时五十多天的友好访问和艺术表演。在我们的生活中，这一时期充满令人难忘的和令人感动的事件。在我们走过的每一个地方，我们感到中国人民对朝鲜人民的热爱和友好感情，看到了中国人民在社会主义建设中的成就，学习到了中国人民建设社会主义的丰富经验。崔文岐感谢中国人民对朝鲜人民的友谊的援助，感谢中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会对他们的亲切招待和关怀，他最后高呼朝中两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    朝鲜青年艺术家们的表演今晚得到了很大的成功。歌剧的每一幕演完以后，剧场中就响起了经久不息的掌声。表演者到红色帷幕前再三谢幕。全剧结束的时候，场内观众起立热烈鼓掌，持续达几分钟之久。首都青年文艺工作者向艺术团全体团员献花。这时，周恩来总理、彭德怀副总理和钱俊瑞、刘芝明、罗毅等走上舞台，同朝鲜青年艺术家们握手，表示慰问和祝贺。（附图片）
    周恩来总理、彭德怀副总理接见了朝鲜青年艺术团团长崔文岐等领导人员和部分团员。
                      新华社记者　吴化学摄
