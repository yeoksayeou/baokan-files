Title: King of Nepal Arrives in India for Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-11-07
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 6th. King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva of Nepal and the Queen arrived in New Delhi by plane on the afternoon of the 6th.
    President Prasad of India and Prime Minister Nehru were among those who welcomed them at Palam Airport.
    The King of Nepal will pay a month-long friendly visit to India.

### 尼泊尔国王到达印度进行友好访问

1955-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王到达印度进行友好访问
    新华社新德里六日电  尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦和王后在六日下午乘飞机到达新德里。
    到巴兰机场欢迎的有印度总统普拉沙德和总理尼赫鲁等人。
    尼泊尔国王将在印度作为期一个月的友好访问。
