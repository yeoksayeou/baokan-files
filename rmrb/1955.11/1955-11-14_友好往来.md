Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 4

　  José Giral Arrives in Beijing
    Dr. José Giral, Chairman of the Spanish Peace Committee and Permanent Member of the World Peace Council, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 12th at the invitation of our country to participate in the centennial commemoration of the first edition of Walt Whitman's "Leaves of Grass" and the 350th anniversary of the publication of Miguel de Cervantes' "Don Quixote".
　  All-German Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    The All-German Youth Delegation, headed by Max Stoll, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 13th to return to their country.
  　Chinese Architectural Engineering Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Home
    The Chinese Architectural Engineering Delegation, which visited the Soviet Union at the invitation of the State Construction Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, returned home by train from Moscow on the 11th. The Chinese Architectural Engineering Delegation, led by Liu Xiufeng, Minister of Architectural Engineering of the People's Republic of China, and Wang Shitai, Deputy Director of the State Construction Committee, studied in detail the experience of Soviet industrial plant construction and residential building construction, as well as the working conditions of Soviet architectural institutions.
                            (Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-11-14
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　  何塞·希拉尔到达北京
    应邀来我国参加世界名著美国惠特曼“草叶集”初版一百周年和西班牙塞万提斯“堂·吉诃德”出版三百五十周年纪念大会的西班牙和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会常务委员何塞·希拉尔博士，在十二日下午乘飞机到达北京。
　  全德青年代表团离京返国
    以马克斯·斯托尔为首的全德青年代表团在十三日上午乘飞机离开北京返国。
  　我国建筑工程考察团离苏返国
    应苏联部长会议国家建设委员会的邀请到苏联访问的中国建筑工程考察团，在十一日从莫斯科搭火车回国。由中华人民共和国建筑工程部长刘秀峰和国家建设委员会副主任王世泰率领的中国建筑工程考察团在苏联详细地研究了苏联工业厂房建筑和住宅建筑的经验，以及苏联建筑机构的工作情况。
                            （据新华社讯）
