Title: To Strengthen the Peaceful Friendship Between Japan and Asian Countries, the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee is Established in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1955-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 1st: The Japan Asian Solidarity Committee was officially established at its inaugural meeting held in Tokyo on the afternoon of October 31st. This organization dedicated to strengthening the peaceful friendship between Japan and Asian countries was established based on the suggestion of the Asian Countries Conference.

About 120 prominent figures from various circles participated in the inaugural meeting, including Democratic Party member of the Diet, Wataru Narahashi; Liberal Party member of the Diet, Yoshimi Otani; Socialist Party members of the Diet, Akira Kazami, Shichiro Hozumi, and Kimiko Abe; Chairman of the Labor-Farmer Party, Hisao Kuroda; Communist Party representative, Karoku Hosokawa; Director-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Takeo Ito; Vice-Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Kumaichi Yamamoto; Vice-Chairman of the Prime Minister's Office Resources Investigation Committee and Professor at the University of Tokyo, Kyoichi Aki; representative of the Japan Cultural People's Conference, Yoichi Fukushima; former Chairman of the Japan Federation of Bar Associations, Kunisuke Nagano; Secretary-General of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, Tsuyan Majima; International Director of the Japan Peace Committee, Tokusaburo Awa; and Chairman of the Japan National Railway Workers' Union, Isamu Koyanagi.

Foreign guests invited to attend the meeting included the Egyptian Ambassador to Japan, Osman Abaza; the Indonesian Consul-General in Japan, Binol; the Indian representative to the World Religious Conference, Dharam Singh; Overseas Chinese representative, Gan Wenfang; and the Central Chairman of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, Shin Hong-sik.

After hearing Tokusaburo Awa's report on the preparations for the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee, the meeting adopted the declaration of establishment, the charter, and the current work guidelines.

The declaration of establishment called on the Japanese people to closely join hands with their Asian and African brothers to break free from the chains of colonialism and establish world peace, and to actively contribute all technology to the industrialization of Asia and Africa.

Finally, the meeting elected Tokutaro Kitamura, Wataru Narahashi, Jiichiro Matsumoto, Akira Kazami, Kikujiro Yamaguchi, Yoshimi Otani, Kyoichi Aki, Shigeto Tsuru, Kenkichi Nakabe, Kumaichi Yamamoto, Kunisuke Nagano, Yoshitaro Hirano, and Kaoru Kawasaki as representative members of the committee, and elected Kunisuke Nagano as chairman of the committee.

### 为了加强日本同亚洲各国的和平友谊  日本亚洲国结委员会在东京成立

1955-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    为了加强日本同亚洲各国的和平友谊
    日本亚洲国结委员会在东京成立
    新华社一日讯　东京消息：日本亚洲团结委员会于十月三十一日下午在东京举行的成立大会上正式成立。这个加强日本同亚洲各国和平友谊的组织是根据亚洲国家会议的建议而成立的。
    参加成立大会的有民主党国会议员楢桥渡，自由党国会议员大谷莹润，社会党国会议员风见章、穗积七郎、安部君子，劳农党主席黑田寿男，共产党代表细川嘉六，日中友好协会理事长伊藤武雄，日本团际贸易促进协会副会长山本熊一，总理府资源调查会副会长、东京大学教授安艺皎一，日本文化人会议代表福岛要一，前日本律师联合会会长长野国助，日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议事务总长马岛僴，日本拥护和平委员会国际部长淡德三郎，日本国营铁道工会主席小柳勇等各界知名人士约一百二十人。
    应邀参加大会的外国来宾有埃及驻日本大使奥斯曼·艾贝德，印度尼西亚驻日本总领事比诺尔，世界宗教会议印度代表达拉姆·辛格，华侨代表甘文芳，在日朝鲜人总联合会中央主席申鸿湜等。
    大会在听取淡德三郎关于日本亚洲团结委员会筹备经过的报告后，通过了成立宣言、章程和当前的工作方针。
    成立宣言号召日本人民与亚洲和非洲的兄弟们紧密地携起手来，摆脱殖民主义锁链和建立世界和平，并且以一切技术为亚洲和非洲的工业化而作积极的贡献。
    大会最后选出北村德太郎、楢桥渡、松本治一郎、风见章、山口喜久一郎、大谷莹润、安艺皎一、都留重人、中部谦吉、山本熊一、长野国助、平野义太郎、河崎夏等为委员会代表委员，并且选出长野国助为委员会理事长。
