Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Investigates Polish Personnel Deaths; Poland Demands U.S. Conduct Prompt and Thorough Investigation into Polish Personnel Deaths
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 9th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong, a viewing team consisting of personnel from the four countries of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission was dispatched on November 8th to the site of the incident to investigate the sudden deaths on November 7th of three members of the Polish delegation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, who were traveling on official business from Gunsan to the Panmunjom headquarters on a U.S.-supplied aircraft.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 9th: According to the Polish Press Agency on November 9th, the Government of the Polish People's Republic has requested the United States Government to conduct a swift, active, and thorough investigation into the unfortunate incident in which three members of the Polish delegation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea died in a plane crash while conducting an inspection flight in South Korea on a U.S. military aircraft, and to inform the Polish Government of the results of the investigation.

### 中立国监察委员会调查波兰人员遇难事件　波兰要求美国对波兰人员遇难事件迅速进行充分调查

1955-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　中立国监察委员会调查波兰人员遇难事件
　　波兰要求美国对波兰人员遇难事件迅速进行充分调查
    新华社平壤九日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：由中立国监察委员会四国人员组成的视察小组，已经在十一月八日被派往出事地点调查驻中立国监察委员会波兰代表团的三位人员在十一月七日因公乘美方供给的飞机从群山去板门店总部的途中突然遇难的事件。
    新华社华沙九日电　据波兰通讯社九日讯：波兰人民共和国政府已经要求美国政府，对驻朝鲜中立国监察委员会波兰代表团的三位人员在乘一架美国军用飞机在南朝鲜进行视察飞行的时候坠毁遇难的不幸事件，进行迅速的、积极的和充分的调查，并且把调查的结果通知波兰政府。
