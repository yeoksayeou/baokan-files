Author:
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 2

　　National Major and Medium-Sized City Table Tennis League
  　Competitions Begin in Tianjin, Shanghai, and Wuhan Competition Zones
      The opening ceremonies for the first phase of the "1955 National Major and Medium-Sized City Table Tennis League" competition in the Tianjin, Shanghai, and Wuhan competition zones were held successively on the 22nd and 23rd. After the first phase of the competition, outstanding male and female table tennis players will be selected to participate in the second phase of the competition held in Beijing.
    Participating in the Tianjin competition zone are fifty-two athletes from nine cities—Beijing, Tianjin, Taiyuan, Hohhot, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Qingdao, and Urumqi—and two provinces, Heilongjiang and Hebei. These athletes include workers, students, teachers, and office staff. Most of them are newly emerging outstanding table tennis players.
    Participating in the Shanghai competition zone are forty male and female athletes from eleven cities—Shanghai, Jinan, Hangzhou, Fuzhou, Xiamen, Nanjing, Suzhou, Wuxi, Changzhou, Xuzhou, and Xin Hailian. Participating in the Wuhan competition zone are forty-six male and female athletes from twelve cities—Wuhan, Chongqing, Chengdu, Xi'an, Guangzhou, Changsha, Nanchang, Zhengzhou, Nanning, Liuzhou, and Shiqi and Foshan. These athletes were all selected from local table tennis competitions. Among them are workers, students, and Young Pioneers. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国大中城市乒乓球联赛　天津上海武汉三个竞赛区的比赛开始

1955-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国大中城市乒乓球联赛
  　天津上海武汉三个竞赛区的比赛开始
      “一九五五年全国大、中城市乒乓球联赛”第一阶段比赛天津、上海、武汉三个竞赛区的比赛大会，在二十二日和二十三日先后开幕。在第一阶段比赛结束后，将选出优秀男女乒乓球运动员，参加在北京举行的第二阶段比赛。
    参加天津竞赛区比赛的有北京、天津、太原、呼和浩特、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、青岛、乌鲁木齐九个城市和黑龙江、河北两省的五十二名运动员。这些运动员中有工人、学生、教师和机关工作人员等。他们大部分是新成长起来的优秀的乒乓球运动员。
    参加上海竞赛区比赛的有上海、济南、杭州、福州、厦门、南京、苏州、无锡、常州、徐州、新海连十一个城市的男女运动员四十人。参加武汉竞赛区比赛的有武汉、重庆、成都、西安、广州、长沙、南昌、郑州、南宁、柳州和石岐、佛山十二个城市的四十六名男女运动员。这些运动员都是从当地的乒乓球比赛中选拔出来的。他们当中有工人、学生和少年先锋队队员。　　（据新华社讯）
