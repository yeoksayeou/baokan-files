Title: Romanian Deputy Minister of Petroleum Industry Says Ready to Assist India in Oil Exploration
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 4
　
　　Romanian Deputy Minister of Petroleum Industry Says
　  Ready to Assist India in Oil Exploration
    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, November 11th: "The Times of India" reported that the Romanian Deputy Minister of Petroleum Industry, Horatiu, said in Calcutta on the 10th that Romania is ready to provide India with various technical assistance in oil exploration. He said that he had already contacted the Indian government on this issue. He said that Indian students and experts could be trained in Romania, and Romanian experts and oil equipment could also be sent and transported to India.

### 罗马尼亚石油工业部副部长说　准备协助印度进行石油勘探

1955-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚石油工业部副部长说
　  准备协助印度进行石油勘探
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社德里十一日讯：“印度时报”报道，罗马尼亚石油工业部副部长霍拉丘十日在加尔各答说，罗马尼亚准备在石油勘探方面给予印度各种技术援助。他说，已经就这个问题同印度政府进行接触。他说，印度的学生和专家可以在罗马尼亚受训练，罗马尼亚的专家和石油装备也可以派遣和运送到印度去。
