Author:
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the southern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway, thirty-three long-span stone arch bridges have been built. This large-scale construction of long-span stone arch bridges is the first time in the history of railway construction in our country. This is the scene of workers applying mortar to the joints on the already-built arch rings.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    宝成铁路南段建造了三十三座长跨度石拱桥。这样大量地修筑长跨度石拱桥，在我国铁路修建史上还是第一次。这是工人们在已经砌好的拱圈上抹灰缝的情形。
