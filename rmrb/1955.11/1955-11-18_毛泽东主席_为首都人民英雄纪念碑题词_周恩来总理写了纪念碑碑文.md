Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Wrote the Inscription for the Monument to the People's Heroes in the Capital; Premier Zhou Enlai Wrote the Monument Inscription

Author:

Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong wrote the inscription for the Monument to the People's Heroes in the capital. The original text of the inscription is "The People's Heroes are Immortal." Premier Zhou Enlai also wrote a 150-character inscription for the monument. Workers at the monument site are meticulously tracing the characters they wrote, enlarging them, and making plaster models in preparation for the official engraving. The engraving project will begin in December.

More than 590 core and body stones for the Monument to the People's Heroes in the capital have been installed, and the installation of the monument top is also nearing completion.

### 毛泽东主席　为首都人民英雄纪念碑题词  周恩来总理写了纪念碑碑文

1955-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东主席
　　为首都人民英雄纪念碑题词
    周恩来总理写了纪念碑碑文
    新华社十七日讯　毛泽东主席为首都人民英雄纪念碑题了词，题词的原文是“人民英雄永垂不朽”。周恩来总理也写了一百五十个字的纪念碑碑文。纪念碑工地的工人正在细心地把他们写的字描下来，进行放大，并且制作石膏模型，为正式镌刻作好准备。刻字工程将在十二月开始。
    首都人民英雄纪念碑的五百九十多块碑心、碑身石料都已安装完毕，碑顶安装工程也快要完成。
