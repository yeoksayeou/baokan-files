Title: Cai Shufan Hosts Banquet to Welcome Shooting Delegations from Various Countries; Vice Premier He Long and Others Receive Heads of Delegations

Author:

Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th. Cai Shufan, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition and Director of the Central National Defense Sports Club of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 15th to welcome the delegations from various countries participating in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition.

Invited to the banquet were the Soviet Union Army, Navy and Air Force Voluntary Assistance Association Shooting Delegation headed by Kustov, the Polish People's Republic Soldier's Friend League Shooting Delegation headed by Zaidel, the Czechoslovak Republic Army Assistance Voluntary Association Shooting Delegation headed by Gruska, the Romanian People's Republic National Defense Assistance Voluntary Association Shooting Delegation headed by Niagu, the Bulgarian People's Republic National Defense Assistance Voluntary Association Shooting Delegation headed by Kamanov, the Mongolian People's Republic Shooting Delegation headed by Sianiaabu, the Korean Democratic People's Republic Fatherland Defense Support Association Shooting Delegation headed by Kim Byung-yeon, the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Shooting Observation Delegation headed by Wu Lun, and all members of the Central National Defense Sports Club Shooting Delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Ji He.

He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, attended the banquet and received the heads of the delegations from various countries before the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Cai Tingkai, Lu Han, Huang Qixiang, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Directors of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, General Peng Shaohui, Chief Referee of the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition, General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xue Zizheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and figures from the capital's sports circles.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries stationed in China were also invited to the banquet.

Cai Shufan delivered a speech at the banquet. On behalf of the Central National Defense Sports Club of the People's Republic of China and the Organizing Committee of the conference, he expressed a warm welcome to all members of the shooting delegations from the Soviet Union and various countries who had come to Beijing from afar. He said: Chinese shooting athletes and shooting workers are very happy to be able to gather together today with shooting workers and outstanding athletes from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries. We must work hard to do the work of the host, and earnestly learn from you in this gathering. I firmly believe that with the joint efforts of our countries, this competition will achieve the goals of exchanging experience in shooting sports, strengthening ties in sports, and consolidating and developing the friendship between us. Cai Shufan finally congratulated the upcoming 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition for achieving brilliant results, and congratulated all athletes for promoting the advancement of international shooting sports with new achievements and new records.

At the banquet, the heads of the shooting delegations from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Poland, North Korea, Mongolia, Romania and the Vietnamese shooting observation delegation delivered speeches successively. They expressed the deep friendship of the people of all countries towards the Chinese people, and unanimously pointed out that the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition to be held in Beijing will greatly contribute to the development and consolidation of friendship between fraternal countries. Delegations from various countries also presented gifts to the Central National Defense Sports Club.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the development and consolidation of the cause of world peace, and to the further development of shooting sports in various countries.

After the banquet, a dance party was held.

### 蔡树藩设宴欢迎各国射击代表团  贺龙副总理等接见了各国代表团团长

1955-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　蔡树藩设宴欢迎各国射击代表团
　  贺龙副总理等接见了各国代表团团长
    新华社十五日讯　一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛组织委员会主席、中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部主任蔡树藩，在十五日晚设宴欢迎前来参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的各国代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的，有以库斯托夫为首的苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会射击代表团，以扎伊德尔为首的波兰人民共和国战士之友同盟射击代表团，以格鲁斯卡为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国支援军队志愿协会射击代表团，以尼亚古为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会射击代表团，以卡曼诺夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国支援国防志愿协会射击代表团，以西亚尼亚布为首的蒙古人民共和国射击代表团，以金秉渊为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国祖国保卫后援会射击代表团，以吴轮为首的越南民主共和国射击观察代表团和以吉合为首的中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部射击代表团全体人员。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，北京市市长彭真出席了宴会，并在宴会前接见了各国代表团团长。
    出席宴会的，还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、卢汉、黄琪翔、荣高棠，一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛总裁判彭绍辉上将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将、总政治部副主任萧华上将，外交部部长助理何伟，北京市副市长薛子正以及首都体育界人士等。
    苏联和人民民主国家驻中国使节和外交官员也应邀出席了宴会。
    蔡树藩在宴会上致词。他代表中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部和大会组织委员会向远道来北京的苏联和各国射击代表团全体人员表示热烈欢迎。他说：中国射击运动员和射击工作者今天能和苏联及各人民民主国家的射击工作者和优秀运动员欢聚在一堂感到很高兴。我们要努力做好主人的工作，并在这一次会聚中认真地向你们学习。我深信，在我们各国共同努力下，这次竞赛大会能达到交流射击运动方面的经验、加强体育运动方面的联系、巩固和发展我们之间的友谊的目的。蔡树藩最后祝贺即将开幕的一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛获得光辉的成就，祝贺全体运动员以新成绩、新纪录推动国际射击运动向前进步。
    宴会上，苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、波兰、朝鲜、蒙古、罗马尼亚等国射击代表团和越南射击观察代表团的负责人相继致词。他们表达了各国人民对中国人民的深厚友谊，一致指出即将在北京举行的一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛，对发展和巩固各兄弟国家之间的友谊将会有很大的贡献。各国代表团并分别向中央国防体育俱乐部赠送礼品。
    席间，宾主一再为世界和平事业的发展和巩固，为各国射击运动的进一步开展而干杯。
    宴会结束后，举行了舞会。
