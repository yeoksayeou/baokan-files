Title: Vice Premier Deng Zihui Receives Hungarian Agricultural Management and Technical Personnel
Author:
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Deng Zihui, received Hungarian agricultural management and technical personnel József Váczérna, Gyula Páncél, Albert Véress, Árnos Busia, and László Péter on the afternoon of the 22nd. They were helping China build an agricultural machinery station.

When receiving them, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan and Second Secretary of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Gaonder, were also present.

On the 19th, Liao Luyan hosted a banquet in honor of these five Hungarian agricultural management and technical personnel.

### 邓子恢副总理接见匈农业管理和技术人员

1955-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　邓子恢副总理接见匈农业管理和技术人员
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓子恢在二十二日下午接见了帮助中国建设一个农业机器站的匈牙利农业管理和技术人员约瑟夫·瓦采尔纳、纠拉·潘哈茨、阿尔巴特·维莱斯、阿诺斯·布西亚和拉斯洛·皮特。
    接见的时候，农业部部长廖鲁言和匈牙利驻华大使馆二等秘书高恩德也在座。
    在十九日，廖鲁言曾设宴招待这五位匈牙利农业管理和技术人员。
