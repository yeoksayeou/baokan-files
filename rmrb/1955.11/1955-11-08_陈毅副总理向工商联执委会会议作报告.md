Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Delivers Report to Executive Committee Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th November. The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held a reporting session for its first Executive Committee’s second meeting on the morning of the 7th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council was invited to give a report on "Several Ideological Questions Regarding the Socialist Transformation of Capitalist Industry and Commerce."

More than 1,300 people attended the reporting session, including the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce attending the meeting, representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and cadres from relevant government departments.

The reporting session lasted five hours.

### 陈毅副总理向工商联执委会会议作报告

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理向工商联执委会会议作报告
    新华社七日讯　七日上午，中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会第二次会议举行报告会，请国务院副总理陈毅作“关于资本主义工商业社会主义改造的若干思想问题”的报告。
    参加报告会的有出席这次会议的全国工商联执行委员会主任委员、副主任委员、委员和列席人员、各民主党派和人民团体的代表以及政府各有关部门的干部共一千三百多人。
    报告会进行了五个小时。
