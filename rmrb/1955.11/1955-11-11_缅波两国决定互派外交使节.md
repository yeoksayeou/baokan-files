Title: Myanmar and Poland Decide to Exchange Diplomatic Envoys
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, November 9th, TASS: The Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement on November 9th, announcing that the Government of the Union of Burma and the Government of the Polish People's Republic, desiring to strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries and to promote the further normalization of international relations, have decided to exchange diplomatic envoys.

### 缅波两国决定互派外交使节

1955-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅波两国决定互派外交使节
    新华社十日讯　塔斯社仰光九日讯：缅甸外交部十一月九日发表声明，宣布缅甸联邦政府和波兰人民共和国政府由于希望加强两国之间的友好关系，并且促进国际关系的进一步正常化，已经决定互派外交使节。
