Title: Moscow Holds Celebration Meeting for the Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th. According to TASS Moscow: On the evening of November 6th, the Moscow Soviet of Working People's Deputies, together with representatives from the Communist Party organizations, social groups, and the Soviet Army, held a celebration meeting at the Soviet Grand Theater to celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    The Soviet Grand Theater was adorned in festive attire. The front of the theater building was decorated with sixteen red flags and the national emblems of each of the republics, embodying the unbreakable friendship of the various nationalities of the Soviet Union. In the center of the stage hung portraits of V.I. Lenin, the founder of the world's first socialist country, and J.V. Stalin, the successor to his cause, surrounded by flags.

    Attending the celebration meeting were advanced workers from Moscow's enterprises and construction sites, intellectuals, representatives from Communist Party organizations, Soviet organs, and social groups, ministers from various departments of the Soviet government, representatives from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, Soviet military personnel, foreign guests, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union.

    Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Aristov, Pospelov, and Shepilov ascended to the rostrum, at which point the attendees applauded enthusiastically and for a long time.

    Yasnnov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, announced the opening of the meeting.

    Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Kaganovich, ascended to the podium amidst enthusiastic applause and delivered a report on the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. His report was repeatedly interrupted by thunderous applause.

    After the conclusion of the meeting, a grand performance was held, featuring artists from Moscow's theaters and other cities in the Soviet Union.

### 莫斯科举行十月社会主义革命节庆祝大会

1955-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行十月社会主义革命节庆祝大会
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十一月六日晚间，莫斯科劳动人民代表苏维埃会同共产党组织、社会团体和苏联军队的代表，在苏联大剧院举行庆祝大会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年。
    苏联大剧院披上了节日的盛装。剧院建筑物的正面装饰着体现苏联各民族牢不可破友谊的十六面红旗和各个加盟共和国的国徽。剧院舞台的正中间挂着世界上第一个社会主义国家的奠基人弗·伊·列宁和他的事业的继承者约·维·斯大林的画像，画像的周围装饰着旗帜。
    参加庆祝大会的有莫斯科各企业和建筑工地的先进工人，知识分子，共产党组织、苏维埃机关和社会团体的代表，苏联政府各部部长，苏联最高苏维埃和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃的代表，苏军军人，外国来宾，以及各国驻苏联的外交使节。
    布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、阿里斯托夫、波斯伯洛夫和谢皮洛夫登上主席台，这时，到会者热烈鼓掌，经久不息。
    莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫宣布大会开始。
    苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席卡冈诺维奇在热烈的掌声中登上讲台，作了关于伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年的报告。他的报告屡次被雷动的掌声所打断。
    大会结束以后，举行了盛大的演出会，参加演出的有莫斯科各剧院和苏联其他城市的艺术家们。
