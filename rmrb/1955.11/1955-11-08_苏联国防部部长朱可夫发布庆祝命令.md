Title: Zhukov, Soviet Minister of Defense, Issues Commemorative Order
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: Marshal of the Soviet Union Zhukov, the Soviet Minister of Defense, issued an order on the 7th commemorating the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The order stated: Today, the Soviet people and their heroic armed forces celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Our working class, engineers, and technicians have completed the Fifth Five-Year Plan's task for total industrial output ahead of schedule. Collective farmers are victoriously implementing the Party and government's resolutions on further developing agriculture. The material well-being of the Soviet people is constantly improving.

Due to the Soviet government and the Communist Party's unwavering adherence to Lenin's foreign policy, our country's international position has recently been further strengthened, and international tensions have eased to some extent.

The order then emphasized that, while safeguarding the cause of peace, the Soviet people have not forgotten that aggressive forces still exist in capitalist countries. To ensure the security of the Soviet motherland, the Soviet armed forces must continue to maintain a high degree of vigilance, strive for new achievements in combat and political training, and continuously improve the army's combat readiness.

### 苏联国防部部长朱可夫发布庆祝命令

1955-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部部长朱可夫发布庆祝命令
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅朱可夫，在七日发布了庆祝十月社会主义革命三十八周年的命令。
    命令说：今天，苏联人民和他们英勇的武装部队一起庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年。我国的工人阶级、工程师和技师提前完成了第五个五年计划在工业生产总量方面的任务。集体农民正在胜利地实现党和政府关于进一步发展农业的决议。苏联人民的物质福利正在不断地提高。
    由于苏联政府和共产党始终不渝地奉行列宁的外交政策，最近，我国的国际地位更加加强了，国际紧张局势有了一定程度的缓和。
    命令接着强调指出，在保卫和平事业的同时，苏联人民并没有忘记在资本主义国家中还存在着侵略势力。为了保障苏维埃祖国的安全，苏联武装部队今后仍然必须保持高度的警惕性，在战斗和政治训练方面争取获得新的成就，不断地提高军队的战斗准备。
