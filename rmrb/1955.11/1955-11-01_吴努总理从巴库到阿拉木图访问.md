Title: Prime Minister U Nu Visits Almaty from Baku
Author:
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: According to TASS, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his entourage arrived in Almaty, the capital of the Kazakh Republic, on October 30th from Baku, the capital of the Azerbaijan Republic. While passing through the Uzbek Republic, U Nu and others made a brief stop in Samarkand, one of the oldest cities in Central Asia, and visited some of its famous ancient buildings.

Prime Minister U Nu and his entourage visited Baku from Crimea on October 28th. There, U Nu met with Abdullayev, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Azerbaijan Republic, and held talks with him. Subsequently, he and his entourage visited the Azerbaijan Republic Academy of Sciences, the Nizami Museum, and the "Taza Pir" Mosque to learn about the scientific work and religious activities in Azerbaijan. In addition, the Burmese guests also visited the Azerbaijan Industrial Institute, the Baku Oil Refinery, and offshore oil fields.

After arriving in Almaty and taking a short rest, Prime Minister U Nu visited Kunayev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Kazakh Republic, and held talks with him.

### 吴努总理从巴库到阿拉木图访问

1955-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理从巴库到阿拉木图访问
    据新华社三十一日讯　据塔斯社报道：缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的随行人员，在十月三十日从阿塞拜疆共和国首都巴库到达哈萨克共和国首都阿拉木图。在路过乌兹别克共和国时，吴努等曾在中亚细亚最古老的城市之一撒马尔罕稍作停留，参观了那里的一些著名的古代建筑物。
    吴努总理和他的随行人员是在十月二十八日从克里米亚到巴库访问的。吴努在那里拜会了阿塞拜疆共和国部长会议第一副主席阿布杜拉耶夫，并且和他进行了谈话。随后，他和他的随行人员访问了阿塞拜疆共和国科学院、尼萨米博物馆和“塔扎—皮尔”清真寺，了解了阿塞拜疆的科学工作和宗教活动的情况。此外，缅甸贵宾们还参观了阿塞拜疆工业学院、巴库炼油厂和海上油田。
    在离开巴库到达阿拉木图稍作休息后，吴努总理就拜访了哈萨克共和国部长会议主席库纳耶夫，和他进行了谈话。
