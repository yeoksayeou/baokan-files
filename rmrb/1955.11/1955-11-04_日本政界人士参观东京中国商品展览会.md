Title: Japanese Political Figures Visit Tokyo Chinese Commodity Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-11-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 3rd: Japanese Minister of International Trade and Industry Tanzan Ishibashi and Standing Director of the Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade, Masanosuke Ikeda, accompanied by Cao Zhongshu, head of the Chinese Commodity Exhibition Delegation, visited the Chinese Commodity Exhibition in Tokyo on the morning of November 2nd.

    After the visit, Ishibashi Tanzan said: This kind of commodity exhibition not only contributes to the promotion of Sino-Japanese trade, but will also give great encouragement to Japanese industry. He believes that China's industry has laid a solid foundation and will develop rapidly in the future. Ishibashi Tanzan also wrote in the opinion book: "Happy for the success of the first Chinese Commodity Exhibition."

    In the afternoon of the same day, Minister of Education Kenzo Matsumura, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party and Diet member Jotaro Kawakami, and Chairman of the Japanese Peace Committee Ikuo Oyama also visited the Chinese Commodity Exhibition separately.

    After the visit, Kenzo Matsumura expressed surprise at China's tremendous development. He also said that he originally loved Chinese culture. He welcomed the invitation of Chinese scholars headed by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, to visit Japan by the Japanese Academic Conference.

    After the visit, Jotaro Kawakami excitedly said: Excellent!

    After the visit, Ikuo Oyama said: The Chinese Commodity Exhibition has achieved a success that no one expected. He believes that the Chinese Commodity Exhibition has made the Japanese people feel more and more that Japan and China must not only promote economic exchanges, but also join hands and restore diplomatic relations as soon as possible.

### 日本政界人士参观东京中国商品展览会

1955-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本政界人士参观东京中国商品展览会
    据新华社东京三日电　日本通商产业大臣石桥湛山和日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟常任理事池田正之辅，十一月二日上午在中国商品展览团团长曹中枢陪同下一同参观中国商品展览会。
    石桥湛山在参观后说：这种商品展览会不但对于促进中日贸易有所贡献，而且将给予日本产业以很大的鼓励。他认为，中国的工业已经打定了基础，今后将迅速地发展下去。石桥湛山还在意见簿上写道：“为第一次中国商品展览会获得成功而高兴”。
    这一天下午，文部大臣松村谦三，日本社会党顾问、国会议员河上丈太郎、日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫也分别前来参观了中国商品展览会。
    松村谦三在参观后，对于中国的巨大发展表示惊讶。他并且说，他原来是爱好中国文化的。他对于日本学术会议邀请以中国科学院院长郭沫若为首的中国学者到日本来访问，表示欢迎。
    河上丈太郎在参观后兴奋地说：好极了！
    大山郁夫在参观后说：中国商品展览会已经获得了谁都预想不到的成功。他认为，中国商品展览会，已经使日本人民越发感到日中两国不但必须促进经济交流，而且必须携起手来，早日恢复邦交。
