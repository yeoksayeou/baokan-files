Title: Syrian Prime Minister Reaffirms Syria's Unwillingness to Join Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, November 24th: Syrian Prime Minister Said al-Ghazzi reiterated on the 22nd that Syria has no intention of joining the Baghdad Pact.

Ghazzi said that Syria had decided long ago not to join the pact. He said that nothing has happened—nor is it likely to happen—that would make us change our minds.

### 叙利亚总理再一次表示　叙利亚无意参加巴格达条约

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚总理再一次表示
　　叙利亚无意参加巴格达条约
    新华社二十四日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理加齐二十二日再一次表示，叙利亚无意参加巴格达条约。
    加齐说，叙利亚很早以前就决定不参加这个条约。他说，并没有发生——也没有可能发生——任何事情使我们改变主意。
