Title: International Friendship Shooting Tournament Holds Third Event

Author:

Date: 1955-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd Dispatch: The 1955 International Friendship Shooting Tournament held its third event, the men's free pistol rapid fire at a 25-meter silhouette target in a standing position, on the 22nd and 23rd.

In the third event, the Romanian shooting team won with 240 hits and 2305 rings. The hit and ring scores of other national shooting teams were: Czechoslovakia 240 hits, 2196 rings; Soviet Union 239 hits, 2311 rings; China 238 hits, 2253 rings; Bulgaria 238 hits, 2201 rings; Poland 232 hits, 2014 rings; Mongolia 224 hits, 1955 rings. The above teams' hit and ring scores have been officially assessed by the tournament's referee committee. The Korean shooting team did not participate in this event.

The top three individual scores in this event were: Zhidkov of the Soviet Union 60 hits, 582 rings; Nikolayev 60 hits, 580 rings; Dokilicha of Romania 60 hits, 579 rings.

The preliminary total scores of the first event, the men's free rifle 50-meter prone, kneeling, and standing three-position shooting competition, which took place from the 19th to the 21st, and the preliminary scores of the standing position shooting competition on the 21st, have been officially assessed by the tournament's referee committee. In the standing position shooting competition, only the score of the Romanian shooting team was changed from 1759 rings to 1768 rings, and the score of the Bulgarian shooting team was changed from 1747 rings to 1765 rings. The winners of the first event's overall score and the winners in the standing position shooting competition received prizes from the tournament organizing committee at noon on the 22nd.

### 国际友谊射击竞赛大会进行第三项目竞赛

1955-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际友谊射击竞赛大会进行第三项目竞赛
    据新华社二十三日讯　一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会在二十二日和二十三日进行了第三项目男子自选小口径手枪人像靶二十五公尺立姿速射竞赛。
    在第三项目竞赛中，罗马尼亚射击队以命中二百四十靶和二千三百零五环获优胜。其他各国射击队命中靶数和环数的成绩是：捷克斯洛伐克二百四十靶、二千一百九十六环，苏联二百三十九靶、二千三百一十一环，中国二百三十八靶、二千二百五十三环，保加利亚二百三十八靶、二千二百零一环，波兰二百三十二靶、二千零一十四环，蒙古二百二十四靶、一千九百五十五环。以上的各队命中靶数和环数已经被大会裁判委员会评定为正式成绩。朝鲜射击队没有参加这个项目的竞赛。
    在这个项目中个人成绩的前三名是：苏联的日古托夫六十靶、五百八十二环，尼古拉耶夫六十靶、五百八十环；罗马尼亚的多基利查六十靶、五百七十九环。
    十九日到二十一日进行的第一项目男子自选小口径步枪五十公尺卧、跪、立三种姿势射击竞赛初步统计的总成绩和二十一日进行的立姿射击竞赛的初步成绩，已经大会裁判委员会评定为正式成绩。立姿射击竞赛中只有罗马尼亚射击队的成绩由一千七百五十九环改为一千七百六十八环，保加利亚射击队的成绩由一千七百四十七环改为一千七百六十五环。第一项目竞赛总成绩的优胜者和立姿射击竞赛中的优胜者在二十二日中午接受了大会组织委员会发给的奖品。
