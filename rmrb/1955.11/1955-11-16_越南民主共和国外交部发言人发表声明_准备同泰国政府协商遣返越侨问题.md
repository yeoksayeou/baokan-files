Title: Spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Issues Statement: Ready to Consult with the Thai Government on the Repatriation of Overseas Vietnamese
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 15th: According to Vietnam News Agency, the spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, when meeting with a Vietnam News Agency reporter on November 14th, issued a statement saying: "Regarding the issue of Vietnamese nationals seeking refuge in Thailand, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is prepared to consult with the Thai government to reasonably resolve the repatriation issue for those nationals who wish to return home."

The spokesman's statement pointed out that there are approximately 50,000 Vietnamese nationals in Thailand who originally resided in Laos and Cambodia and went to Thailand in 1946 to avoid the war. They work diligently and contribute to the economic activities of their place of residence in Thailand. They do not interfere in Thailand's internal affairs and consistently respect Thai laws and customs.

The statement said that after the restoration of peace in Indochina, most of the Vietnamese nationals in Thailand who originated from North Vietnam hoped to have the opportunity to return home and participate in construction work. The Thai government also agreed to repatriate Vietnamese nationals.

The statement also said that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam hopes that the resolution of this matter will help enhance relations between the two countries.

### 越南民主共和国外交部发言人发表声明　准备同泰国政府协商遣返越侨问题

1955-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国外交部发言人发表声明
　　准备同泰国政府协商遣返越侨问题
    新华社河内十五日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国外交部发言人在十一月十四日接见越南通讯社记者时，发表声明说：“关于在泰国避难的越南国民问题，越南民主共和国政府准备同泰国政府协商，以便合情合理地解决这些国民中愿意回家的人的遣返问题。”
    发言人的声明中指出，在泰国的越南国民约有五万人，他们原来居住在老挝和柬埔寨，在一九四六年为避开战争而前往泰国。他们勤勉工作，对于居住地的泰国经济活动有所贡献。他们不干涉泰国内部事务，并且一贯尊重泰国的法律和风俗习惯。
    声明说，在印度支那和平恢复后，在泰国的大部分原籍在北越的越南国民希望能有机会回家参加建设工作。泰国政府也同意遣返越南国民。
    声明中还说，越南民主共和国政府希望，这件事情的解决将有助于增进两国的关系。
