Title: Four Foreign Ministers' Conference Adjourns for One Day
Author:
Date: 1955-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 2nd. The Four Foreign Ministers' Conference of France, Britain, the Soviet Union, and the United States adjourned on the 1st due to the Catholic holiday of All Saints' Day.

　　On the 1st, US Secretary of State Dulles left Geneva for Spain to hold talks with Franco; French Foreign Minister Pinay returned to Paris to hold talks with former Moroccan Sultan Yusuf, who had just arrived in France from Madagascar.

　　The Four Foreign Ministers' Conference is scheduled to continue on the 2nd.

### 四国外长会议休会一日

1955-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    四国外长会议休会一日
    新华社二日讯　日内瓦消息：法、英、苏、美四国外长会议一日因为天主教的万圣节而休会一天。
    一日，美国国务卿杜勒斯离开日内瓦到西班牙同佛朗哥举行了会谈；法国外交部长比内回到巴黎同刚从马达加斯加到达法国的摩洛哥前苏丹优素福会谈。
    四国外长会议定在二日继续开会。
