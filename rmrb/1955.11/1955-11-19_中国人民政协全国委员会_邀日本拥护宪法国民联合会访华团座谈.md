Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee Holds Symposium with Japanese Delegation from the National Federation for Defending the Constitution

Author: 
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) invited the Japanese delegation from the "National Federation for Defending the Constitution" to a symposium on the morning of the 17th.

The Japanese delegation from the "National Federation for Defending the Constitution," including delegation leader Tetsu Katayama, members Saburo Endo, Satoshi Nakamura, Kenji Ishihara, Kozo Inomata, Satoshi Nakazaki, Ayako Ishigaki, Chikako Yoshii, Yasuo Ogura, and Secretary-General Koichi Ono, attended the symposium.

On the Chinese side, those attending the symposium included Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Baurhan, Secretary-General Xing Xiping, members of the National Committee of the CPPCC, heads of various democratic parties, and social figures Wang Yunsheng, Shen Bochun, Zhu Jisheng, Wu Maosun, Li Zhuchen, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Sun Qimeng, Rong Yiren, Weng Wenhao, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Mei Gongbin, Chen Qiyou, Fu Zuoyi, Huang Shaohong, Huang Qixiang, Ye Gongchuo, Cai Tingkai, Deng Chumin, Wei Lihuang, and Luo Longji.

The symposium was presided over by Dong Biwu, who delivered a speech on behalf of the National Committee of the CPPCC, welcoming the Japanese delegation from the "National Federation for Defending the Constitution," and introducing the Chinese representatives from various fields to the Japanese friends.

Tetsu Katayama then delivered a speech, expressing his gratitude for the opportunity to meet with the heads of various democratic parties and social figures in China. Katayama said that he was deeply pleased that Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Koichi Ono, Secretary-General of the Japanese National Federation for Defending the Constitution, had signed a joint communiqué yesterday. He added that the delegation's opportunity today to hold a symposium with representatives from various fields in China and listen to their opinions would enable them to better strive to achieve the goals outlined in the joint communiqué.

At the symposium, the guests and hosts engaged in cordial talks on the functions and powers of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, the election of representatives, the organization and work of the CPPCC, the situation of the various democratic parties in China, the rights of ethnic minorities in China, and religious issues.

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Accompanied by Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the Japanese delegation from the "National Federation for Defending the Constitution," led by Tetsu Katayama, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 17th to visit Northeast China. Delegation member Sumio Takakuwa left Beijing by plane on the morning of the same day to return to his country via Guangzhou.

Xinhua News Agency, 16th Dispatch: Accompanied by Chen Yu, member of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Japanese trade union delegation led by Totaro Fujita left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 16th to visit various places in Northeast China.

### 中国人民政协全国委员会　邀日本拥护宪法国民联合会访华团座谈

1955-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协全国委员会
　　邀日本拥护宪法国民联合会访华团座谈
    新华社十七日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会邀请日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团在十七日上午举行了座谈会。
    日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团团长片山哲，团员远藤三郎、中村哲、石原宪治、猪俣浩三、中崎敏、石垣绫子、吉井千贺子、小仓康男，秘书长大野幸一等应邀出席了座谈会。
    中国方面出席座谈会的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席董必武、李济深、沈钧儒、陈叔通、章伯钧、鲍尔汉，秘书长邢西萍，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、各民主党派负责人和社会人士王芸生、申伯纯、朱继圣、吴茂荪、李烛尘、邵力子、马叙伦、孙起孟、荣毅仁、翁文灏、胡愈之、许广平、许德珩、梅龚彬、陈其尤、傅作义、黄绍竑、黄琪翔、叶恭绰、蔡廷锴、邓初民、卫立煌、罗隆基等人。
    座谈会由董必武主持，他代表中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会致词欢迎日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团，并介绍到会的中国各方面人士同日本友人见面。
    片山哲接着致词，他对有机会同中国各民主党派负责人和社会人士见面表示感谢。片山哲说，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪同日本拥护宪法国民联合会秘书长大野幸一在昨天签订了联合公报，使他们深深感到高兴。今天访华团有机会同中国各方面人士举行座谈，倾听各位的意见，将使他们能更好地努力实现联合公报中的各项目标。
    在座谈会上，宾主双方就中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会的职权，代表的选举，中国人民政治协商会议的组织和工作情况，中国各民主党派的情况，中国少数民族的权利和宗教问题等进行了亲切的谈话。
    据新华社讯　以片山哲为首的日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团由中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升陪同，在十七日晚乘火车离开北京去东北参观访问。访华团团员高桑纯夫在同日上午乘飞机离开北京经广州返国。
    新华社十六日讯　以藤田藤太郎为首的日本工会代表团由中华全国总工会执行委员会委员陈宇陪同，在十六日上午乘飞机离开北京去东北各地参观访问。
