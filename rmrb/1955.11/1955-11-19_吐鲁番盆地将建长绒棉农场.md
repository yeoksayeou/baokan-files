Title: Long-staple Cotton Farm to be Built in Turpan Basin
Author:
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 1

A relatively large state-owned farm for growing long-staple cotton will be established 25 kilometers northwest of Ayding Lake in the Turpan Basin. The overall plan and design of the farm will begin in December.

As early as the summer and autumn of this year, the former Xinjiang Provincial Water Conservancy Bureau, the Wasteland Survey and Design Bureau, and the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps successively sent a group of surveyors to survey the site and find water sources. In order to solve the difficulty of irrigating the cotton fields, the former Xinjiang Provincial Water Conservancy Bureau and others plan to pave a canal more than 40 kilometers long with cement before the construction of the farm to extend the Tianshan black water, which seeps and gathers underground in the Gobi Desert, to the farm. The construction of this canal project will commence in the spring of 1956.

(Our correspondent in Urumqi)

### 吐鲁番盆地将建长绒棉农场

1955-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　  吐鲁番盆地将建长绒棉农场
    吐鲁番盆地艾丁湖西北二十五公里的地方，将开辟一个规模较大的种植长绒棉的国营农场。农场的全面规划和设计将在十二月开始进行。
    早在今年夏秋两季，原新疆省水利局、荒地勘测设计局和新疆生产建设部队先后派出了一批勘测人员来到这里勘测场址，寻找水源。为了解决棉田灌溉水的困难，原新疆省水利局等在建场前将计划用水泥铺砌一条长四十多公里的渠道，将渗漏和汇集在戈壁地下的天山的黑水引伸到农场。这个渠道工程将在一九五六年春季动工兴建。
                    （本报驻乌鲁木齐记者）
