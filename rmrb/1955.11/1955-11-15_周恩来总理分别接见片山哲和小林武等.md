Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Tetsuji Katayama, Takeshi Kobayashi, and Others Separately
Author:
Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th November – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received Tetsuji Katayama, head of the Japanese "National Federation for the Protection of the Constitution," on the morning of the 14th. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi was present. After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai and others had lunch with Tetsuji Katayama.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th November – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received all members of the Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Education Inspection Group, headed by Takeshi Kobayashi, on the afternoon of the 14th.

Wu Yuzhang, Liu Ningyi, Fang Ming, and Wang Yunsheng were present at the reception. (Attached pictures)

### 周恩来总理分别接见片山哲和小林武等

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理分别接见片山哲和小林武等
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在十四日上午接见了日本“拥护宪法国民联合会”访华团团长片山哲。在座的有国务院副总理陈毅。接见后周恩来总理等并同片山哲共进午餐。
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在十四日下午接见了以小林武为首的日本教职员工会教育考察团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有吴玉章、刘宁一、方明、王芸生。（附图片）
