Title: Vietnam Workers' Party Central Committee Convenes Agricultural Production Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-11-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 23rd: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the agricultural production conference, which opened in Hanoi on November 13th, closed on the 21st.

　　This conference was convened by the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party. Representatives from the Vietnam Workers' Party's inter-zones, provinces, and county committees, as well as representatives from rural branches and agricultural production cadres, attended the meeting.

　　Ho Viet Thang, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, delivered a report at the meeting on making agricultural recovery a major task in the government's economic recovery plan. After discussing this report, the meeting proceeded to discuss the agricultural recovery plan for next year. This plan covers many aspects, including grain production, planting economic crops, animal husbandry, fisheries, and developing forest products.

　　The representatives attending the meeting unanimously stated that they recognized the great potential in Vietnam's agricultural production and expressed their determination to implement the decisions of the meeting.

　　During the conference, President Ho Chi Minh attended the meeting. Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, both spoke at the meeting.

### 越南劳动党中央委员会召开农业生产会议

1955-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南劳动党中央委员会召开农业生产会议
　　新华社河内二十三日电　据越南通讯社讯：十一月十三日在河内开幕的农业生产会议，已经在二十一日闭幕。
　　这个会议是由越南劳动党中央委员会召开的。出席会议的有越南劳动党各联区、省、县委员会的代表和乡村支部的代表，以及农业生产干部等。
　　越南劳动党中央委员会委员、越南民主共和国政府农林部副部长胡曰胜在会上作了关于把恢复农业作为政府经济恢复计划中的一项主要任务的报告。会议在讨论这个报告以后，接着讨论了明年的农业恢复计划。这个计划涉及许多方面，包括粮食生产、种植经济作物、畜牧、渔业和开发森林产品。
　　出席会议的代表一致表示他们认识到越南农业生产中的巨大潜力，并且表示决心实现会议的决定。
　　会议期间，胡志明主席曾参加会议。越南劳动党总书记长征和越南民主共和国总理范文同都曾在会上讲了话。
