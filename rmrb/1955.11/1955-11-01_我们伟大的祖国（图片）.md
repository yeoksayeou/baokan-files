    Our Great Motherland
    Lhasa recently held an agricultural, livestock, cultural, educational, and health exhibition. This exhibition demonstrated the new achievements in agricultural experiments in the Tibetan plateau region and inspired the Tibetan people's confidence in building a new Tibet. The Tibetan people are watching the threshing performance of combine harvesters here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    拉萨市最近举行了一个农牧、文教、卫生展览会。这个展览会说明了西藏高原地区农业试验的新成就，鼓舞了藏族人民建设新西藏的信心。藏族人民正在这里看联合收割机的脱粒表演。
