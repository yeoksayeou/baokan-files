Title: Japanese Trade Union Delegation Visits the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Twice; Fujita Totaro Gives a Lecture on the Japanese Workers' Movement at the Trade Union Cadre School
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 1

　　Japanese Trade Union Delegation Visits the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Twice
　  Fujita Totaro Gives a Lecture on the Japanese Workers' Movement at the Trade Union Cadre School
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: On the afternoon of the 25th at 7:00 PM, Fujita Totaro, the head of the Japanese Trade Union Delegation, gave a lecture entitled "On the Japanese Workers' Movement" at the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Secretariat Secretary Dong Xin, Director of the International Liaison Department Ma Chungu, Vice-Principal of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School Zhang Yunying, all heads of departments and industrial unions of the ACFTU, cadres, and more than 1,000 students from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School attended the lecture.
    At 9:00 AM on the 26th, the Japanese Trade Union Delegation, headed by Fujita Totaro, visited the All-China Federation of Trade Unions again and held discussions with the heads of various departments of the ACFTU on issues such as wages, production, propaganda, labor employment, labor insurance, and financial work. After listening to the explanations from the heads of each department, the guests raised many questions on related business, and the hosts provided detailed answers.

### 日本工会代表团两度访问中华全国总工会  藤田藤太郎向工会干部学校作关于日本工人运动的讲演

1955-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　日本工会代表团两度访问中华全国总工会
　  藤田藤太郎向工会干部学校作关于日本工人运动的讲演
    据新华社讯　二十五日下午七时，日本工会代表团团长藤田藤太郎向中华全国总工会干部学校作了题为“关于日本工人运动”的讲演。参加讲演会的有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、书记处书记董昕、国际联络部部长马纯古，中华全国总工会干部学校副校长张云莹，全总各部门及各产业工会全体负责人和干部，以及中华全国总工会干部学校学员一千多人。
    二十六日上午九时，以藤田藤太郎为首的日本工会代表团再度访问了中华全国总工会，并同全总各部门负责人就工资、生产、宣传、劳动就业、劳动保险和财务工作等问题进行了座谈。客人们听了各部门负责人的说明后，就有关业务提出了许多问题，主人们作了详细解答。
