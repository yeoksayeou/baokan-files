Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Launches Activities to Celebrate the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 1
    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Launches Activities to Celebrate the October Revolution Day

    (Our dispatch) To celebrate the 38th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to hold a series of commemorative activities around the holiday.

    On October 29th, in the lobby of the Beijing Hotel, workers, students, and staff from organizations in Beijing, along with Soviet friends in Beijing, held a grand friendship celebration.

    Before November 7th, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will organize two report meetings. The first will feature Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, speaking on "The Great Assistance of the Soviet Union to China's First Five-Year Plan," and the second will feature Gu Dachun, Director of the Production Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, speaking on "Learning from the Soviet Workers' Spirit of Practicing Economy and Working Hard." Members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's delegation to the Soviet Union will deliver reports on their visit to some units in Beijing. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center, the Working People's Cultural Palace, and showcases in Wangfujing, Xidan and other places will exhibit pictures such as "The Great Achievements of the Soviet People in 1955." In addition, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has prepared 300 sets of propaganda posters, such as "Learning from the Creative Labor Spirit of the Soviet People to Strive to Complete China's First Five-Year Plan" and "Development of a Soviet Collective Farm," to be distributed to factories and rural areas.

    From November 4th, seven film projection teams will go to the countryside to screen Soviet films such as "Dawn on the Neman River" and "Marina's Destiny" for farmers in 48 screenings. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center and the Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles will also jointly organize a Soviet poetry recital. From November 4th, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center will also hold five Soviet music film events.

    Many factories, mines, organizations, and schools in Beijing's Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations will also hold various commemorative activities around the holiday.

### 北京市中苏友协展开庆祝十月革命节活动

1955-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协展开庆祝十月革命节活动
    本报讯　为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十八周年，北京市中苏友好协会决定在节日前后举办一系列的纪念活动。
    十月二十九日在北京饭店大厅里，北京的工人、学生、机关工作人员和在京的苏联友人举行了盛大的友谊联欢晚会。
    十一月七日前，北京市中苏友好协会将组织两次报告会，由国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰讲“苏联对我国第一个五年计划的伟大援助”，中华全国总工会生产部部长顾大椿讲“学习苏联工人厉行节约艰苦奋斗的精神”。中苏友好协会访苏代表团在京团员将为一些单位作访苏报告。北京市中苏友好协会文化馆、劳动人民文化宫和王府井、西单等处的橱窗中将展出“苏联人民在一九五五年中的伟大成就”等图片。此外，北京市中苏友好协会并准备了“学习苏联人民创造性的劳动精神为完成我国第一个五年计划而奋斗”、“苏联一个集体农庄发展情况”等宣传挂图三百套，发给工厂和农村。
    十一月四日起将有七个电影队下乡为农民放映“尼门河上的黎明”、“玛利娜的命运”等苏联影片四十八场。北京市中苏友好协会文化馆和市文联还将合办苏联诗歌朗诵会。从十一月四日起在北京市中苏友好协会文化馆并将举办苏联音乐电影会五场。
    北京市许多厂矿、机关、学校的中苏友好协会也将于节日前后举办各种纪念性的活动。
