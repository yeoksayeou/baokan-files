Title: Guizhou Distributes Over 60,000 Pieces of Free Farm Equipment
Author:
Date: 1955-11-17
Page: 2

Guizhou Province has distributed over 60,000 pieces of free farm equipment to minority farmers in 47 counties, including Lushan, Danzhai, and Weining. This equipment includes corn threshers, rice threshers, hillside plows, sprayers, and other iron farm tools manufactured by local farm equipment factories in the province, as well as 300 double-wheeled, double-furrow plows ordered from the Changsha Agricultural Machinery Factory in Hunan Province. From mid-October to November 10th, over 4,000 pieces of equipment have been transported to various locations, and the rest will be shipped successively. (Our correspondent in Guiyang)

### 贵州发放六万多件无偿农具

1955-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

　  贵州发放六万多件无偿农具
    贵州省发放六万多件无偿农具给炉山、丹寨、威宁等四十七个县的少数民族农民。这部分农具除了包括本省农具厂制造的玉米脱粒机、打谷机、山地犁、喷雾器和其他铁质农具外，还有向湖南省长沙农业机械厂订购的双轮双铧犁三百台。从十月中旬到十一月十日止，已有四千多件农具运往各地，其余也将陆续运去。　（本报驻贵阳记者）
