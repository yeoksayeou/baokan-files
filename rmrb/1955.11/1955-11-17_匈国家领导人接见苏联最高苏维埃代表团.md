Title: Hungarian State Leaders Receive Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Hungarian Party and state leaders receive the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Rakosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and Hegedus, Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, received the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation headed by Begov on November 15th (they arrived in Budapest on the 14th), and held cordial talks with the delegation members.
    The Hungarian side participating in the talks also included Gero, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Ronai, Speaker of the National Assembly. Soviet Ambassador to Hungary Andropov was also present at the talks.

### 匈国家领导人接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团

1955-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈国家领导人接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团
    据新华社讯　匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西和匈牙利部长会议主席赫格居斯，十一月十五日接见了以别哥夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团（他们是十四日到达布达佩斯的），并且和代表团人员进行了亲切的交谈。
    匈牙利方面参加谈话的还有：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会政治局委员、部长会议第一副主席格罗和国民议会议长罗奈伊。谈话时在座的还有苏联驻匈牙利大使安德罗波夫。
