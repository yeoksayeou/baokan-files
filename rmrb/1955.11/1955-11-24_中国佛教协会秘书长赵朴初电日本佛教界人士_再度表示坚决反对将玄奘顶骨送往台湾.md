Title: Zhao Puchu, Secretary-General of the Chinese Buddhist Association, Cables Japanese Buddhist Figures, Reiterating Firm Opposition to Sending Xuanzang's Skull to Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: Zhao Puchu, Secretary-General of the Chinese Buddhist Association, sent telegrams on the 22nd to Otani Kōshō of Nishi Hongwanji Temple in Kyoto, Japan, Nakayama Riri of Tsukiji Hongwanji Temple in Tokyo, Japan, Shiio Benkyō and Masuda Nichien of Taisho University in Tokyo, Japan, and Sugawara Keikei of the Japan-China Buddhist Exchange Association in Tokyo, Japan. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To Reverend Otani Kōshō of Nishi Hongwanji Temple in Kyoto, Japan, Mr. Nakayama Riri of Tsukiji Hongwanji Temple in Tokyo, Japan, Reverend Shiio Benkyō of Taisho University in Tokyo, Japan, and forwarded to Reverend Masuda Nichien, Reverend Sugawara Keikei and fellow reverends of the Japan-China Buddhist Exchange Association at 23 Matsukiyo-cho, Asakusa, Taito-ku, Tokyo, Japan:
    We have previously telegraphed Otani Kōshō, Otani Eijun, Mr. Nakayama Riri, etc., requesting that they prevent the sending of Venerable Xuanzang's skull, currently in Japan, to Taiwan, which we trust you are aware of. Now, according to a telegram from Mr. Nakayama Riri at 10:45 on November 22nd, there are still those who, disregarding the firm opposition of the vast number of Chinese Buddhists, intend to transport Venerable Xuanzang's skull to Taiwan. Chinese Buddhists would like to reiterate to the All-Japan Buddhist Association's board meeting, which is to be held tomorrow, that Chinese Buddhists resolutely oppose the transportation of Venerable Xuanzang's remains to Taiwan. We specifically request that each reverend kindly protect and care for Venerable Xuanzang's remains, striving to prevent this action, which is detrimental to the friendly relations between Chinese and Japanese Buddhists.

                                        Zhao Puchu, Secretary-General of the Chinese Buddhist Association
                                        November 22, 1955

### 中国佛教协会秘书长赵朴初电日本佛教界人士　再度表示坚决反对将玄奘顶骨送往台湾

1955-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国佛教协会秘书长赵朴初电日本佛教界人士
　　再度表示坚决反对将玄奘顶骨送往台湾
    新华社二十三日讯　中国佛教协会秘书长赵朴初二十二日分别打电报给日本京都西本愿寺大谷光照、日本东京筑地本愿寺中山理理、日本东京大正大学椎尾辨匡和增田日远以及日本东京日中佛教交流恳谈会菅原惠庆等人。电报全文如下：日本京都西本愿寺大谷光照法师，日本东京筑地本愿寺中山理理先生，日本东京大正大学椎尾辨匡法师并转增田日远法师，日本东京台东区浅草松清町二十三番地日中佛教交流恳谈会菅原惠庆法师及诸位法师：
    我们曾先后电大谷光畅、大谷莹润、中山理理先生等，要求制止将现在日本的玄奘法师顶骨送往台湾，谅承察悉。现据十一月二十二日十时四十五分中山理理先生来电，仍有人不顾中国广大佛教徒的坚决反对，想将玄奘法师顶骨运往台湾。中国佛教徒谨向明日即将召开的全日本佛教会理事会再一度表示，中国佛教徒坚决反对把玄奘法师遗骨运往台湾。现特电请各法师对玄奘法师灵骨善为护持，务期制止此种不利于中日两国佛教徒友好关系的行动。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国佛教协会秘书长　赵朴初
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九五五年十一月二十二日
