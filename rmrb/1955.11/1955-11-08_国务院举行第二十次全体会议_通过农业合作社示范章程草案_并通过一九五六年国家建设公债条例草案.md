Title: State Council Holds 20th Plenary Meeting, Passes Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Cooperatives, and Passes Draft Regulations on 1956 National Construction Bonds
Author:
Date: 1955-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th

    The State Council of the People's Republic of China held its 20th plenary meeting on the 7th. The meeting heard explanations from Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, discussed and approved the "Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives" proposed by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and decided to submit the draft to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

    The "Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives" had been discussed and basically approved by the Sixth Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and had also been submitted to the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for discussion. The draft charter submitted by the CPC Central Committee to the State Council for discussion had been appropriately revised based on the opinions raised in these two discussions.

    The meeting also approved the "Regulations on National Economic Construction Bonds for 1956 (Draft)," and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

    The meeting also approved regulations on the standards for urban and rural division, instructions on the issue of fully utilizing the original local industrial production capacity when state-owned enterprises newly build or expand affiliated factories (workshops), and the decision on the abolition of the administrative establishment of Luoyang County, Henan Province.

    Finally, the meeting approved appointments and removals.

    Resolution of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on the Adoption of the Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives

    (Adopted at the 20th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on November 7, 1955)

    The 20th Plenary Meeting of the State Council discussed the Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives submitted by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the State Council, unanimously resolved to adopt this draft, and submitted this draft to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

### 国务院举行第二十次全体会议  通过农业合作社示范章程草案  并通过一九五六年国家建设公债条例草案

1955-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第二十次全体会议
    通过农业合作社示范章程草案
    并通过一九五六年国家建设公债条例草案
    新华社七日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第二十次会议在七日举行。会议听了农业部部长廖鲁言的说明，讨论并通过了中国共产党中央委员会所提出的“农业生产合作社示范章程草案”，决定将这个草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    “农业生产合作社示范章程草案”曾经由中国共产党第七届中央委员会第六次全体会议（扩大）讨论和基本通过，并且曾经提交中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会讨论。由中共中央提交国务院讨论的章程草案曾根据这两次讨论中提出的意见作了适当的修改。
    会议还通过了“一九五六年国家经济建设公债条例（草案）”，决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议还通过了关于城、乡划分标准的规定，关于国营企业新建或扩建附属工厂（车间）的时候应充分利用原有地方工业生产能力问题的指示以及关于撤销河南省洛阳县建制的决定。
    会议最后通过了任免事项。
    中华人民共和国国务院关于通过农业生产合作社示范章程草案的决议
    （一九五五年十一月七日国务院全体会议第二十次会议通过）
    国务院全体会议第二十次会议讨论了中国共产党中央委员会提交国务院的农业生产合作社示范章程草案，一致决议通过这个草案，并且将这个草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
