Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 27th meeting on November 11th. After discussion and revision, the meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations for the People's Congresses and People's Committees at All Levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region" (see page 3 of today's newspaper). The draft of these organizational regulations was formulated by the Second Session of the First People's Congress of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, passed by the 18th Plenary Session of the State Council, and then submitted to the Standing Committee for approval.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议

1955-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
　　批准内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例
　　新华社十一日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十一月十一日举行第二十七次会议。会议经过讨论和修正，批准了“内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例”（见今天本报第三版）。这个组织条例的草案是由内蒙古自治区第一届人民代表大会第二次会议制定，并且经过国务院全体会议第十八次会议通过，然后提请常务委员会批准的。
