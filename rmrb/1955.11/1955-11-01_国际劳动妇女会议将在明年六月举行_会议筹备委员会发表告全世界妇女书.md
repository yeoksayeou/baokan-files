Title: International Labor Women's Conference to be Held Next June; Preparatory Committee Issues Appeal to Women of the World
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, October 31st: According to the proposal of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the International Labor Women's Conference will be held in June 1956.

The conference preparatory committee issued the following appeal to the women of the world:

Women workers, female staff members, female teachers, housewives, and rural and urban working women!

You must strive to win a better life for yourselves, for your children, and for all your loved ones.

You should rise up to defend your economic and social interests, strive to realize your hopes, and exchange experiences in this struggle.

You earnestly hope for peace, because peace is a necessary condition for improving your lives. You understand that much remains to be done to take advantage of the recent relaxation of international relations to establish a solid and lasting peace.

You number in the hundreds of millions, therefore you are a tremendous force.

Whether the living and working conditions of you and your families can be improved depends on whether all of you can unite regardless of nationality, worldview, religious belief, or profession.

In order to achieve these tasks, the World Federation of Trade Unions will convene the first International Labor Women's Conference from June 14 to June 17, 1956.

The committee calls on working women all over the world to actively participate in the preparation for this conference.

### 国际劳动妇女会议将在明年六月举行  会议筹备委员会发表告全世界妇女书

1955-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国际劳动妇女会议将在明年六月举行
    会议筹备委员会发表告全世界妇女书
    新华社三十一日讯　塔斯社维也纳三十一日讯：根据世界工会联合会的倡议，国际劳动妇女会议将在一九五六年六月举行。
    会议筹备委员会为此发表告全世界妇女书如下：
    女工们、女职员们、女教师们、家庭主妇们、城乡劳动妇女们！
    你们要努力为你们、为你们的孩子、为你们一切亲近的人争取美好的生活。
    你们应当起来保卫经济的和社会的利益，努力实现你们的希望，交流这个斗争中的经验。
    你们热切希望和平，因为和平是改善你们生活的必要条件。你们明白，为了利用不久前国际关系中出现的缓和的成果来建立巩固的持久和平，还必须做很多事情。
    你们的人数可以亿计，因此你们是一支巨大的力量。
    你们以及你们的家庭的生活条件和劳动条件能不能得到改善，就要看你们大家能不能不分国籍、世界观、宗教信仰、职业的不同一致团结起来。
    为了实现这些任务，世界工会联合会将在一九五六年六月十四日到十七日召开第一次国际劳动妇女会议。
    委员会号召全世界的劳动妇女积极参加准备这个会议。
