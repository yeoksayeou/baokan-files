Title: Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble Visits Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 28th: The Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble, invited to perform in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived at the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers on November 28th, accompanied by personnel from the External Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and representatives from the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army.

　　On that day, the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers held a grand gathering to welcome the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble. At the meeting, Luo Libin, Director of the Cultural Department of the Political Department of the Volunteers, delivered a welcome speech. On behalf of the leadership of the Volunteers, he expressed a warm welcome and heartfelt thanks to all members of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble for their visit from afar.

　　General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, presented gifts to all members of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble on behalf of the leadership of the Volunteers. The gifts included the "Long Live Peace" commemorative badge presented to the People's Volunteers by the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, some commemorative war trophies captured by the Chinese People's Volunteers during the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, and some daily necessities created by the Volunteer soldiers during the difficult years, demonstrating a high degree of spirit in overcoming difficulties.

　　Lieutenant Colonel Z. Volva, representative of the General Political Department of the Polish Army and Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Polish Army, addressed the meeting. He said that the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble felt very honored to have the opportunity to visit the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers. He expressed his gratitude to the personnel of the Volunteers' leadership for their warm welcome and for presenting them with meaningful gifts.

　　On the morning and afternoon of that day, the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble held two wonderful performances for the personnel of the Volunteers' leadership, and their performances were warmly welcomed.

### 波兰军队歌舞团在朝鲜访问中国人民志愿军

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　波兰军队歌舞团在朝鲜访问中国人民志愿军
    新华社平壤二十八日电　应邀到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行访问演出的波兰军队歌舞团，在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国文化宣传省对外文化联络局负责人员和朝鲜人民军总政治局代表的陪同下，二十八日到达中国人民志愿军领导机关。
    当日，中国人民志愿军领导机关举行了盛大集会，欢迎波兰军队歌舞团。会上，志愿军政治部文化部部长罗立斌致欢迎辞。他代表志愿军领导机关，对波兰军队歌舞团全体人员远道前来访问的盛情表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的感谢。
    中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇将军代表志愿军领导机关，向波兰军队歌舞团全体人员赠送了礼物。礼物中，有中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团赠给人民志愿军的“和平万岁”纪念章、中国人民志愿军在抗美援朝战争中缴获的一部分富有纪念意义的战利品和志愿军战士们在艰苦的岁月中，发挥高度克服困难的精神所创造的一部分生活用具。
    波兰军队总政治部代表、波兰军队总政治部文化部部长兹·沃尔瓦中校在会上致辞。他说，波兰军队歌舞团能够有机会访问英雄的中国人民志愿军，觉得非常荣幸。他对志愿军领导机关人员对他们的热情欢迎，并且赠送给他们富有意义的礼物，表示感谢。
    当日上午和下午，波兰军队歌舞团为志愿军领导机关人员举行了两场精采的演出，他们的演出受到热烈的欢迎。
