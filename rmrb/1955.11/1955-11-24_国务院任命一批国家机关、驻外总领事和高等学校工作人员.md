Title: The State Council Appoints a Batch of Staff for State Organs, Consuls General Abroad, and Institutions of Higher Education
Author: 
Date: 1955-11-24
Page: 2

The State Council of the People's Republic of China appointed a batch of state personnel, consuls general stationed abroad, and staff of institutions of higher education on November 7. The list is as follows:

　　Liao Luyan and Chen Zhengren are appointed as Deputy Directors of the Seventh Office of the State Council;

　　Xie Bangzhi is appointed as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Supervision;

　　Liu Bin is appointed as Vice Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building;

　　He Bingzhang and Li Jianping are appointed as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Coal Industry;

　　Li Rui and Li Daigeng are appointed as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry;

　　Zhao Lide is appointed as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Communications;

　　Qiao Peixin is appointed as Vice President of the People's Bank of China, and Ding Dongfang is appointed as Assistant President;

　　Shen Ping is appointed as Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Geneva, Swiss Confederation;

　　Song Minzhi is appointed as Vice President of Harbin Institute of Technology;

　　Su Zhuang is appointed as Vice President of Jiaotong University;

　　Luo Ruiqing is appointed as President of the Central People's Public Security Academy, and Zhou Zhongying, Zhang Dehan, and He Xia are appointed as Vice Presidents;

　　Zhong Fuxiang is appointed as President of Beijing Institute of Posts and Telecommunications;

　　Hu Chuan Kui is appointed as President of Beijing Medical College, and Qu Zheng, Xue Gongchuo, Yan Yi, and Ma Xu are appointed as Vice Presidents;

　　Guo Yiqing is appointed as President of Inner Mongolia Normal College;

　　Ma Zhemin is appointed as President of Zhongnan University of Economics.

                    ×　×　×

　　The State Council of the People's Republic of China appointed Gao Zhiguo as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry on August 5.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国务院任命一批国家机关、驻外总领事和高等学校工作人员

1955-11-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　国务院任命一批国家机关、驻外总领事和高等学校工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院在十一月七日任命一批国家工作人员、驻外总领事和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    廖鲁言、陈正人为国务院第七办公室副主任；
    谢邦治为监察部部长助理；
    刘彬为第三机械工业部副部长；
    贺秉章、李健平为煤炭工业部部长助理；
    李锐、李代耕为电力工业部部长助理；
    赵立德为交通部部长助理；
    乔培新为中国人民银行副行长，丁冬放为行长助理；
    沈平为中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦共和国日内瓦总领事；
    宋敏之为哈尔滨工业大学副校长；
    苏庄为交通大学副校长；
    罗瑞卿为中央人民公安学院院长，周仲英、张德含、何侠为副院长；
    钟夫翔为北京邮电学院院长；
    胡传揆为北京医学院院长，曲正、薛公绰、阎毅、马旭为副院长；
    郭以青为内蒙古师范学院院长；
    马哲民为中南财经学院院长。

    中华人民共和国国务院在八月五日任命了高治国为电力工业部部长助理。
