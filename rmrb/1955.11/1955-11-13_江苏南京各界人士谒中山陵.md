Title: People from All Walks of Life in Nanjing, Jiangsu Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th November. Today is the 89th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Representatives from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City paid respects and laid wreaths at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum at 9 a.m.

　　Those who participated in the ceremony included Ji Fang, Deputy Governor of Jiangsu Province; Leng Yu, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing City; and Gao Juefu, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

　　Also participating in the ceremony were the heads of various people's organizations, local organizations of various democratic parties, and religious figures.

### 江苏南京各界人士谒中山陵

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　  江苏南京各界人士谒中山陵
    新华社南京十二日电 今天是孙中山先生诞生八十九周年的纪念日。江苏省和南京市各界代表在上午九时到中山陵谒陵献花圈。
    参加谒陵的有江苏省副省长季方，中国人民政治协商会议江苏省委员会副主席冷遹，南京市市长彭冲、中国人民政治协商会议南京市委员会副主席高觉敷。
    参加谒陵的还有各人民团体、各民主党派地方组织的负责人，以及宗教界人士。
