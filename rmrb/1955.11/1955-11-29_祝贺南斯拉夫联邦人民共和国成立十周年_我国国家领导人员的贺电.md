Title: Congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia - Congratulatory Messages from Chinese National Leaders
Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

Congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia
Congratulatory Messages from Chinese National Leaders

To Comrade Josip Broz Tito, President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and Chairman, Federal Executive Council:
To Comrade Moša Pijade, President of the Federal National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia:
To Comrade Koča Popović, Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia:

　　On the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, we, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the heroic people of Yugoslavia.

　　In the past, the people of China and Yugoslavia shared a deep friendship in the struggle for the independence and liberation of their respective countries. Since the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Yugoslavia, friendly cooperation between the two countries has made great progress. We believe that this friendly cooperation will continue to be strengthened and developed. We wish the Yugoslav people further achievements in building socialism and safeguarding world peace.

　　Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
　　Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
　　Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing, November 26, 1955

### 祝贺南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年　我国国家领导人员的贺电

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年
　　我国国家领导人员的贺电南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统兼联邦执行委员会主席约·布·铁托同志：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦国民议会主席莫萨·皮雅杰同志：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国外交秘书长科查·波波维奇同志：
    值此南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年之际，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你们，并且通过你们向英雄的南斯拉夫人民致热烈的祝贺。
    中、南两国人民过去在争取自己祖国独立和解放的斗争中就存在着深厚的友谊。中、南两国的友好合作自从两国外交关系建立以来，已经有了很大的进展。我们相信，这种友好合作还会不断地得到加强和发展。祝南斯拉夫人民在建设社会主义和维护世界和平的事业中获得进一步的成就。
　　中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
　　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
　　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来
　　　　　　　　　　　　一九五五年十一月二十六日于北京
