Our Great Motherland
Farmers in the mountainous area of Shaxian County, Fujian Province, are actively responding to the call for increased production. The county's autumn crop planting area has expanded by more than 3,000 mu compared to last year. Members of the Fusheng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shaxian County are weeding in the soybean fields.

    我们伟大的祖国
