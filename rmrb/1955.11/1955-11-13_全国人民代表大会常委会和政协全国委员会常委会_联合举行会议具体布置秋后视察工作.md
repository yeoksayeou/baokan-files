Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC Jointly Hold Meeting to Specifically Arrange Post-Autumn Inspection Work
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th - The 28th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the 9th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese Political Consultative Conference were jointly held on November 12th.
    The meeting made specific arrangements for the inspection work after the autumn harvest in 1955. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, made a report on the issue of inspection work at the meeting.
    Deputies to the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing attended the expanded meeting.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会　联合举行会议具体布置秋后视察工作

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会
　　联合举行会议具体布置秋后视察工作
　　新华社十二日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十八次会议和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第九次会议在十一月十二日联合举行扩大会议。
    会议对一九五五年秋收后视察工作作了具体布置。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席彭真在会上作了关于视察工作问题的报告。
    在北京的全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员列席了这次扩大会议。
