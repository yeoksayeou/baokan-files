Author:
Date: 1955-11-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Dachen Village, Ninghe County, Hebei Province, which has realized agricultural cooperation, has reaped bumper harvests of crops year after year. This year, the 1,400 mu of paddy fields in the Dachen Village Agricultural Cooperative produced an average of about 827 jin per mu. The cooperative's grain carts are transporting the surplus grain that the members sold to the state to the purchasing station.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    实现了合作化的河北省宁河县大陈庄，农作物连年获得丰收。今年大陈庄农业合作社的一千四百多亩水稻，平均每亩约产八百二十七斤。合作社的运粮大车，正把社员们卖给国家的余粮运到收购站去。
