Title: National People's Congress Deputies and Others Begin Inspection Tours in Various Regions

Author:
Date: 1955-11-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 17th. Deputies to the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) in Beijing have begun or are about to begin inspection tours in various regions. As of November 17th, more than 360 deputies and members in Beijing have decided to participate in the inspection tours, and more than 100 of them have already left Beijing, one after another. The remaining deputies and members will also depart for their inspection tours in the coming days.

　　More than 140 of the deputies and members participating in this inspection will conduct their work in Beijing. The inspection areas for the remaining deputies and members will include 20 provinces, including Hebei, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Guangdong, and Shandong, as well as the two municipalities directly under the central government, Shanghai and Tianjin, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

　　To ensure that deputies and members could understand the general situation of current work in urban and rural areas before departing for their inspection tours, the General Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Secretariat of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference jointly held two report meetings on the 12th and 14th. Minister of Food Zhang Naiqi introduced the situation of the unified purchase and sale of grain, Deputy Director of the Eighth Office of the State Council Xu Dixing introduced the situation of the transformation of capitalist industry and commerce, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan introduced the situation of agricultural production and the agricultural cooperation movement, and Deputy Director of the Central Handicraft Administration Bureau Deng Jie introduced the situation of handicraft cooperatives. Deputies and members also held discussions in groups according to their inspection areas on relevant issues in the inspection work.

### 全国人民代表大会代表等  开始到各地去视察工作

1955-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会代表等
　  开始到各地去视察工作
    新华社十七日讯　在京的全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员已经开始或者即将开始到各地视察工作。到十七日止，决定参加视察工作的在京代表和委员已有三百六十多人，其中已有一百多人先后离京，其余的代表和委员们也将在日内陆续出发视察。
    参加这次视察工作的代表和委员中将有一百四十多人在北京市视察工作。其余的代表和委员们的视察地区将包括河北、江苏、浙江、广东、山东等二十个省，上海、天津两个直辖市以及内蒙古自治区和新疆维吾尔自治区。
    为了使代表们和委员们在出发视察工作前能了解目前城乡工作概况，全国人民代表大会常务委员会办公厅和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会秘书处曾在十二日和十四日联合举行了两次报告会，请粮食部部长章乃器介绍了粮食统购统销的情况，国务院第八办公室副主任许涤新介绍了资本主义工商业改造的情况，农业部部长廖鲁言介绍了农业生产和农业合作化运动的情况，中央手工业管理局副局长邓洁介绍了手工业合作社的情况。代表和委员们并且曾按视察地区分组对视察工作中的有关问题进行了座谈。
