Title: Swimming Test Sees Six National Records Broken
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

　　The 1955 National Swimming Test, which began on the 26th, has seen seven athletes and four relay teams break six national records in the past two days; one athlete equaled the national record.

　　Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long and Li Fuchun, and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu watched the swimming test on the afternoon of the 27th.

　　On the 26th, Li Xiqing, a student from the Central Institute of Physical Education, completed the 400-meter distance in 5 minutes and 1.8 seconds, 0.6 seconds faster than the national record.

　　In the men's 100-meter breaststroke final held on the morning of the 27th, Mu Xiangxiong of the Central Institute of Physical Education reached the finish line first with a time of 1 minute and 13.65 seconds; Qi Lieyun of the Central South Institute of Physical Education and Xu Zhixiang of the Central Institute of Physical Education almost reached the finish line at the same time, with times of 1 minute 14.2 seconds and 1 minute 14.3 seconds respectively. All three athletes broke the national record of 1 minute 14.9 seconds. Twelve athletes from three relay teams participated in the women's 400-meter freestyle relay final. The relay team from the Central Institute of Physical Education won the championship. The four athletes in this team broke the national record of 5 minutes and 36.8 seconds with a total time of 5 minutes and 13.7 seconds; the relay team from the Central South Institute of Physical Education and the relay team from the East China Institute of Physical Education won second and third place respectively. The total times of these two teams were 5 minutes 20.6 seconds and 5 minutes 22.6 seconds respectively, also breaking the national record.

　　On the afternoon of the 27th, in the men's 200-meter butterfly preliminary, Zhang Tianhui of the Central Institute of Physical Education broke his own national record of 2 minutes 46.8 seconds with a time of 2 minutes 46.6 seconds. In the women's 100-meter breaststroke final, Zheng Sufei and Zhao Jinqing of the Central Institute of Physical Education broke Zheng Sufei's national record of 1 minute 31.9 seconds with times of 1 minute 31.2 seconds and 1 minute 31.8 seconds respectively. The relay team from the Central Institute of Physical Education won first place in the men's 800-meter freestyle relay final. The total time of the four athletes in this team reached 9 minutes 11.8 seconds, 22 seconds faster than the national record. In the men's 100-meter freestyle preliminary, Lin Jinzhu of the Central Institute of Physical Education achieved a time of 1 minute 0.4 seconds, equaling the national record.

　　On the 27th, the women's 100-meter and 400-meter freestyle finals, the men's 100-meter butterfly, 200-meter breaststroke, and 1500-meter freestyle finals, the women's 200-meter breaststroke preliminary, as well as 3-meter springboard diving, 7.5-meter platform diving, comical diving, and water polo performances were also held.

　　Participating in this swimming test were 62 male athletes and 31 female athletes from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Central Institute of Physical Education, the Central South Institute of Physical Education, the East China Institute of Physical Education, the Southwest Institute of Physical Education, and nine units including Guangxi, Fujian, Beijing, and Tianjin.

### 游泳测验已有六项成绩突破全国最高纪录

1955-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　游泳测验已有六项成绩突破全国最高纪录
    据新华社讯　从二十六日开始举行的一九五五年全国游泳测验，两天来有七个运动员和四个接力队打破了六项全国最高纪录；有一个运动员的成绩和全国最高纪录相等。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理贺龙、李富春，最高人民法院院长董必武曾观看了二十七日下午的游泳测验。
    二十六日，中央体育学院的学生李喜庆在五分一点八秒的时间里游完四百公尺的距离，比全国最高纪录快了零点六秒。
    在二十七日上午举行的男子一百公尺蛙泳决赛中，中央体育学院的穆祥雄首先到达终点，他的成绩是一分十三点六五秒；中南体育学院的戚烈云和中央体育学院的徐致祥几乎是同时到达终点的，他们的成绩分别是一分十四点二秒和一分十四点三秒。这三名运动员都打破了一分十四点九秒的全国最高纪录。女子四百公尺自由泳接力决赛有三个接力队的十二名运动员参加。中央体育学院的接力队获得了冠军。这个队的四个运动员以五分十三点七秒的总成绩打破了五分三十六点八秒的全国最高纪录；中南体育学院的接力队和华东体育学院的接力队分别获得第二和第三名。这两个队的总成绩分别是五分二十点六秒和五分二十二点六秒，也打破了全国最高纪录。
    二十七日下午，在男子二百公尺蝶泳预赛中，中央体育学院的张天辉以二分四十六点六秒的成绩，打破了他自己保持的二分四十六点八秒的全国最高纪录。女子一百公尺蛙泳决赛，中央体育学院的郑素绯和赵锦清分别以一分三十一点二秒和一分三十一点八秒的成绩，打破了郑素绯保持的一分三十一点九秒的全国最高纪录。中央体育学院的接力队在男子八百公尺自由泳接力决赛中获得了第一名。这个队的四个运动员的总成绩达到了九分十一点八秒，比全国最高纪录快了二十二秒。在男子一百公尺自由泳预赛中，中央体育学院的林锦珠的成绩是一分零点四秒，和全国最高纪录相等。
    二十七日还举行了女子一百公尺和四百公尺自由泳决赛、男子一百公尺蝶泳、二百公尺蛙泳和一千五百公尺自由泳决赛、女子二百公尺蛙泳预赛以及三公尺跳板跳水、七公尺半跳台跳水、滑稽跳水和水球表演。
    参加这次游泳测验的有中国人民解放军和中央体育学院、中南体育学院、华东体育学院、西南体育学院和广西、福建、北京、天津等九个单位，共有六十二名男运动员和三十一名女运动员。
