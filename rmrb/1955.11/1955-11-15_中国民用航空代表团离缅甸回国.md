Title: Chinese Civil Aviation Delegation Leaves Burma for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 14th: The head of the Chinese Civil Aviation Delegation, General Wu Faxian, and all members left Rangoon by plane on the morning of the 12th to return home.

### 中国民用航空代表团离缅甸回国

1955-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　  中国民用航空代表团离缅甸回国
    据新华社十四日讯　仰光消息：中国民用航空代表团团长吴法宪将军和全体人员已在十二日上午乘飞机离开仰光回国。
