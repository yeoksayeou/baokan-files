Title: Yugoslav People's Struggle and Construction Photo Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - An exhibition of "The Yugoslav People's Struggle and Construction" photographs opened at the Water Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing, organized by the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia. The opening ceremony was held this morning, the 29th.

Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Jingfan, Vice Minister of Geology, Liu Chengdong, Vice Minister of Forestry, Ding Hua, Assistant Minister of Higher Education, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhao Yiming, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, other heads of people's organizations, people from all walks of life in the capital, Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries to China attended the opening ceremony.

Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, and Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, delivered speeches at the opening ceremony.

After Qian Junrui cut the ribbon for the photo exhibition, the guests visited the more than 400 photos provided by the Yugoslav Embassy in China, displayed in four exhibition rooms. The first exhibition room displayed some historical photos of the Yugoslav people's struggle for liberation from 1941 to 1945, including photos of Marshal Tito inspecting the front lines and troops, and valuable photos of the Yugoslav National Liberation Army in combat. The photos exhibited in the other exhibition rooms systematically introduced the construction achievements in various fields of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia over the past ten years and the friendly relations between China and Yugoslavia.

### 南斯拉夫人民斗争和建设图片展览开幕

1955-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫人民斗争和建设图片展览开幕
    据新华社二十九日讯　对外文化联络局为庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年，在北京中山公园水榭举办了“南斯拉夫人民斗争和建设图片展览”。这次展览在二十九日上午举行了开幕式。
    中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长夏衍，教育部部长张奚若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、部长助理何伟，地质部副部长刘景范，林业部副部长刘成栋，高等教育部部长助理丁华，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，北京市副市长张友渔，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏和其他各人民团体负责人，首都各界人士，南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇，各国驻华使节和外交官员等参加了开幕式。
    文化部代理部长钱俊瑞和南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇先后在开幕式上致词。
    钱俊瑞为图片展览剪彩后，来宾们参观了陈列在四个展览室、由南斯拉夫驻华大使馆供给的四百多幅图片。第一展览室展出了一九四一年到一九四五年期间南斯拉夫人民争取解放斗争的一些历史图片，其中有铁托元帅视察前线和部队的图片，有南斯拉夫民族解放军作战的珍贵图片。在其他的展览室中展出的图片，系统地介绍了南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国建国十年来各方面的建设成就和中南两国的友好关系。
