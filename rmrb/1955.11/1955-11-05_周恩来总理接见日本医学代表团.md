Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the Japanese Medical Delegation at 2:00 PM on the 4th. Those received included Honorary Head of the Delegation, Katsuma Abe; Head of the Delegation, Yoshio Tomori; Deputy Head of the Delegation, Seiichi Ota; and delegation members Sadataka Tasaka, Senji Sakakibara, Satoru Sasa, Tatsuyo Fuwa, Minoru Nakahara, Tomoyuki Kinoshita, Atsuo Fujii, Sanei Suzuki, Yoshitada Minami, and Masako Fukuda; as well as attendants Eiji Mizushiro and Kenji Maeda.

Also present at the reception were Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the People's Republic of China; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Fu Lianzhang, Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association; Fang Shishan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association; and Chinese medical experts Lin Qiaozhi, Zhong Huilan, and Wu Yingkai. ### 周恩来总理接见日本医学代表团

1955-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本医学代表团
    新华社四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在四日下午二时接见了日本医学代表团名誉团长阿部胜马，团长堂森芳夫，副团长太田清一，团员田坂定孝、榊原仟、佐佐学、不破龙登代、中原实、木下友敬、藤井厚男、铃木佐内、南义忠、福田昌子，随员王水英治、前田健士。
    接见时在座的有，中华人民共和国卫生部部长李德全，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中华医学会理事长傅连暲、副理事长方石珊和我国医学专家林巧稚、钟惠澜、吴英恺。（附图片）
