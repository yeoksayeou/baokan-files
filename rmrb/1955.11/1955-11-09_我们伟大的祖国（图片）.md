Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-11-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to promote the development of the agricultural cooperation movement, the Xinjing, Dachang, and Gaoqiao districts in the suburbs of Shanghai are preparing to build three agricultural machinery stations, with an initial plan to equip them with forty-five tractors. This is a picture of workers inspecting the first batch of six Czechoslovakian medium-sized tractors that have just arrived.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了促进农业合作化运动的发展，上海市郊区新泾区、大场区和高桥区正在筹建三个农业机器站，初步计划配备四十五台拖拉机。这是工人们正在检查刚运到的第一批六台捷克斯洛伐克中型拖拉机。
