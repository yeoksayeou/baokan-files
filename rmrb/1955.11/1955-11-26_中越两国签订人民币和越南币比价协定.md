Title: China and Vietnam Sign Agreement on Exchange Rate Between Renminbi and Vietnamese Currency
Author:
Date: 1955-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: An agreement on the exchange rate between the Renminbi and the Vietnamese currency, of great significance for the development of economic cooperation and cultural exchange between China and Vietnam, was signed in Beijing on the 25th by the government of the People's Republic of China and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The People's Bank of China and the National Bank of Vietnam also signed the protocol on border currency exchange, the protocol on trade settlement, the protocol on aid settlement, and the protocol on non-trade remittance.

    Representing China, the signatory was Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, and representing Vietnam, the signatory was Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Yang Shaoqiao, Assistant Minister of Finance; Lin Haiyun, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade and concurrently Director-General of the General Administration of Customs; Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the People's Bank of China and concurrently Director-General of the Foreign Operations Bureau; Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and others. On the Vietnamese side were: Pham Binh, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China; Dang Khanh Van, member of the trade delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and others.

    After the signing, Cao Juru hosted a banquet at the Peace Hotel.

### 中越两国签订人民币和越南币比价协定

1955-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中越两国签订人民币和越南币比价协定
　  新华社二十五日讯　对发展中越两国经济合作和文化交流有重要意义的中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府关于人民币和越南币比价的协定，二十五日在北京签字。中国人民银行和越南国家银行关于边境货币兑换议定书、贸易结算议定书、援助结算议定书和非贸易汇兑议定书也在同时签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国人民银行行长曹菊如，代表越南方面签字的是越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：财政部部长助理杨少桥、对外贸易部部长助理兼海关总署署长林海云、中国人民银行副行长兼国外业务管理局局长乔培新、中华人民共和国外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮等。越南方面有：越南民主共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书范平、越南民主共和国贸易代表团团员邓庆云等。
    签字后，曹菊如假和平宾馆举行宴会。
