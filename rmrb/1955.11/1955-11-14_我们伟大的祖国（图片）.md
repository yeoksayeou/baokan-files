Our Great Motherland
The No. 9 blast furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Company has begun reconstruction and will be rebuilt into Anshan Iron and Steel's fifth automated blast furnace. After the renovation, the output of this blast furnace will be approximately 7% higher than the original design output, and the coke consumption per ton of iron produced can be reduced by about 100 kilograms. This is the new furnace body transported to the construction site, waiting to be installed after the foundation is poured.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山钢铁公司九号炼铁炉已开始改建，它将被改建成鞍钢的第五座自动化炼铁炉。这座高炉改建后的产量将比原设计产量约提高百分之七，每炼一吨铁所消耗的焦炭可减少一百公斤左右。这是运到工地的新炉体，它等待基础浇灌完毕后就可安装。
