Title: Communiqué on Sino-Burmese Air Transport Negotiations Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 1

    The People’s Republic of China and the Union of Burma, in order to establish air transport links between the two countries, each sent a civil aviation delegation to Rangoon for negotiations. The negotiations were conducted in a friendly and cooperative atmosphere and have been successfully concluded. As a result of the negotiations, both sides signed an Air Transport Agreement and a Protocol based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit. The signatories to the agreement and protocol were Wu Faxian, head of the civil aviation delegation on the Chinese side, and U Win Maung, Minister of Transport and Communications on behalf of the Government of the Union of Burma.

    According to the provisions of the agreement: the Civil Aviation Administration of China will operate the Kunming-Mandalay-Rangoon round-trip route; Burma Airways will operate the Rangoon-Mandalay-Kunming-Guangzhou round-trip route.

    The establishment of civil air transport between China and Burma will further develop the economic and cultural ties between the two countries, and promote friendship and cooperation between the Chinese and Burmese peoples.

### 中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府  关于中缅航空运输谈判公报

1955-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府
    关于中缅航空运输谈判公报
    中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦为建立两国间的航空运输交通，各派民用航空代表团在仰光举行谈判。谈判在友好和合作的气氛中进行，并且已经圆满达成协议。谈判结果，双方根据平等互利原则签订了航空运输协定和议定书。在协定和议定书上签字的中华人民共和国方面为民用航空代表团团长吴法宪；缅甸联邦政府方面为运输和交通部部长吴温貌。
    根据协定规定：中国民用航空局将经营昆明—曼德勒—仰光往返航线；缅甸联邦航空公司将经营仰光—曼德勒—昆明—广州往返航线。
    中缅两国间民用航空运输的建立，将进一步发展中缅两国间的经济上和文化上的连系，并且促进中国人民和缅甸人民之间的友好和合作。
