Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Visit Madras
Author:
Date: 1955-11-30
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Madras, TASS dispatch: Bulganin, Khrushchev, and their entourage arrived in Madras, the largest industrial and cultural center in southern India, at noon on the 28th.

Welcoming them at Madras airport were the Governor of Madras Province, Prakasa, Chief Minister Nadar, and other officials.

On the afternoon of the 28th, Bulganin and Khrushchev, accompanied by Governor Prakasa, left their residence and drove through the city center to visit the State-owned Vehicle Manufacturing Factory, one of India's newly built factories, in the Perambur district. As the Soviet guests drove through the city, they were warmly welcomed by people standing on the streets holding flags of India and the Soviet Union and flowers.

After visiting the factory, the Soviet guests arrived at the city stadium, where about 50,000 children had gathered. Madras children performed gymnastics and sports, danced folk dances from southern India, and sang folk songs on the stadium, which was brightly lit by colored lights and spotlights. Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov thanked the children of Madras on behalf of Bulganin, Khrushchev, and their entourage.

The Soviet guests then left the city stadium and drove through the city to the coast of the Bay of Bengal to attend a city-wide civic welcoming ceremony. They were warmly welcomed along the way.

The Mayor of Madras, M.A. Chidambaram, read the welcoming address to Bulganin and Khrushchev at the civic welcoming ceremony. He particularly emphasized the great contributions of the Soviet Union in developing international relations and cooperation with other countries. He said that your efforts in this regard have played a significant role in the history of this century.

He went on to say: Our leaders believe they have an obligation to strive for the progress of countries that are now oppressed. They are making every effort to prevent war and establish peace throughout the world. We are convinced that your lofty ideal is to build a world in which mankind will be able to live in an atmosphere of peace and friendship, regardless of race, religion or social distinctions.

The Chief Minister of Madras Province and the Mayor presented handicrafts to Bulganin and Khrushchev on behalf of the citizens of Madras Province and Madras City.

Bulganin and Khrushchev delivered speeches at the gathering.

After the meeting, the Governor of Madras Province, Prakasa, hosted a banquet for Bulganin, Khrushchev, and their entourage.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到马德拉斯访问

1955-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到马德拉斯访问
    新华社据塔斯社马德拉斯讯　布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫以及他们的随行人员在二十八日中午到达印度南部最大的工业和文化中心马德拉斯。
    在马德拉斯机场欢迎他们的有马德拉斯省省长普拉卡萨、首席部长纳达尔和其他官员。
    二十八日下午，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在普拉卡萨省长陪同下离开了他们的住所，乘车经过市中心前往皮拉姆波尔区参观印度新建工厂之一国营车辆制造厂。当苏联客人乘车经过市区的时候，他们受到站在街上手持印苏两国国旗和鲜花的人们的热烈欢迎。
    参观了这个工厂以后，苏联客人们来到了聚集着约有五万名儿童的市运动场。马德拉斯的儿童们在五彩灯光和强光灯照得通亮的运动场上表演了体操和运动，跳了印度南部的民间舞蹈，并且唱了民歌。苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫代表布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫以及他们的随行人员向马德拉斯的儿童致谢。
    苏联客人们随即离开市运动场乘车经过市区去孟加拉湾海岸出席全市的市民欢迎会，他们沿途受到了人们不断的热烈欢迎。
    马德拉斯市市长麦·阿·奇·巴拉姆在市民欢迎会上宣读了对布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的欢迎词。他特别强调苏联在发展国际关系和同其他国家的合作方面的伟大功绩。他说，你们在这方面的努力在本世纪的历史中起了显著的作用。
    他接着说：我们的领袖们认为他们有义务努力争取现在被压迫的国家的进步。他们正在尽一切努力来防止战争和在全世界建立和平。我们确信，你们的崇高理想是要建立这样一个世界，在这个世界中，人类将能够在和平和友好的气氛中生活，而不管种族、宗教或社会的区别如何。
    马德拉斯省首席部长和市长代表马德拉斯省和马德拉斯市的公民向布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫赠送了手工艺品。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在集会上发表了演说。
    会后，马德拉斯省省长普拉卡萨设宴招待布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和他们的随行人员。
