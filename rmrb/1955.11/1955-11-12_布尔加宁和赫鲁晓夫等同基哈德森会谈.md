Title: Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Others Hold Talks with Gerhardsen
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Norwegian Prime Minister E. Gerhardsen and his entourage, who were invited by the Soviet government to visit the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow on November 10.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 11th Dispatch from Moscow via TASS: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, held talks with Norwegian Prime Minister Gerhardsen in the Moscow Kremlin on the 11th.
    Participating in the talks on the Norwegian side were: Norwegian Minister of Commerce Skaug, Norwegian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Brodland, and S. Sommerfelt, head of the Norwegian trade delegation currently in the Soviet Union, among others.
    Participating in the talks on the Soviet side were: Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Kabanov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Zorin and Semyonov, and Soviet Ambassador to Norway Arkadyev.
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, received Norwegian Prime Minister Gerhardsen, who was invited to visit the Soviet Union, on the 10th.
    Accompanying Gerhardsen were Norwegian Minister of Commerce Skaug and Norwegian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Brodland.
    During the meeting, Bulganin and Gerhardsen held talks, attended by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Zorin and Soviet Ambassador to Norway Arkadyev.
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, received Norwegian Prime Minister Gerhardsen on the 11th.
    During the meeting, Voroshilov and Gerhardsen held talks.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫等同基哈德森会谈

1955-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫等同基哈德森会谈
    据新华社讯　应苏联政府的邀请到苏联访问的挪威首相埃·基哈德森和他的随行人员，十一月十日到达了莫斯科。
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，十一日在莫斯科克里姆林宫同挪威首相基哈德森举行会谈。
    挪威方面参加会谈的有：挪威商务大臣斯科格，挪威驻苏联大使布罗德兰德和目前正在苏联的挪威贸易代表团团长斯·索穆菲尔特等人。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联对外贸易部部长卡巴诺夫，苏联外交部副部长佐林和谢苗诺夫，苏联驻挪威大使阿尔卡杰耶夫。
    据新华社讯　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，在十日接见了应邀到苏联访问的挪威首相基哈德森。
    陪同基哈德森的有挪威商务大臣斯科格和挪威驻苏联大使布罗德兰德。
    在接见时，布尔加宁和基哈德森进行了谈话，当时在座的有苏联外交部副部长佐林和苏联驻挪威大使阿尔卡杰耶夫。
    据新华社讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，在十一日接见了挪威首相基哈德森。
    在接见时，伏罗希洛夫和基哈德森进行了谈话。
