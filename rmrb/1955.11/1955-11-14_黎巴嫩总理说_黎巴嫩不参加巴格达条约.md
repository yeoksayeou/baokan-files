Title: Lebanese Prime Minister Says Lebanon Will Not Participate in Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-11-14
Page: 4
　　
　　Lebanese Prime Minister Says
　　Lebanon Will Not Participate in Baghdad Pact
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, December 12th: Lebanese Prime Minister Karami said on the 11th that Lebanon has no intention of participating in the Baghdad Military Treaty, which was instigated by the United States. Karami's statement was published in the Lebanese newspaper "Al Anwar."
    Karami said that Lebanon is "impossible" to join the Baghdad Treaty, "even in the distant future." He said that he and his ministers had reached a "complete understanding" on this issue.

### 黎巴嫩总理说　黎巴嫩不参加巴格达条约

1955-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　黎巴嫩总理说
　　黎巴嫩不参加巴格达条约
    新华社十二日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩总理卡拉米十一日说，黎巴嫩无意参加美国策动缔结的巴格达军事条约。卡拉米的声明刊载在黎巴嫩“新闻报”上面。
    卡拉米说，黎巴嫩“不可能”加入巴格达条约，“即使在遥远的将来”。他说，他和他的部长们在这个问题上取得了“完全的谅解”。
