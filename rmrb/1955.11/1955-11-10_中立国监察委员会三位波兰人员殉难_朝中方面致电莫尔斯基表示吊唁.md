Title: Three Polish Personnel of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Died in the Line of Duty; Korean and Chinese Sides Send Condolences to Ambassador Morski
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th. Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong, 9th: Three members of the Polish delegation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission tragically died on the morning of November 7th when the plane provided by the American side, on which they were traveling on official business from Gunsan to the headquarters in Panmunjom, suddenly crashed.

Major General Zheng Guolu, Chief Commissioner of the Korean and Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, sent a letter of condolence to Ambassador Morski, the Polish Commissioner of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, on November 8th, expressing deep sympathy to the Ambassador and all members of the Polish delegation and the families of the victims.

### 中立国监察委员会三位波兰人员殉难  朝中方面致电莫尔斯基表示吊唁

1955-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会三位波兰人员殉难
    朝中方面致电莫尔斯基表示吊唁
    新华社平壤九日电　朝鲜中央通讯社开城九日讯：驻中立国监察委员会波兰代表团的三位人员在十一月七日上午在他们因公乘美方供给的一架飞机从群山去板门店总部途中突然殉难。
    军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员郑国禄少将为此在十一月八日给中立国监察委员会波兰委员莫尔斯基公使送去了一封吊唁信，对这位公使以及波兰代表团全体人员和遇难者的家属表示深切的同情。
