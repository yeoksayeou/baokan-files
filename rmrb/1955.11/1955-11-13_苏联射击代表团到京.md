Title: Soviet Shooting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Soviet shooting delegation of the Volunteer Association for Assisting the Army, Navy and Air Force, invited to China to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 12th, a group of nineteen people.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the International Friendship Shooting Competition, Wang Ke, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Lianhua, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Sports Commission, and many representatives of Chinese athletes. Andrei Niagu, head of the Romanian shooting delegation, also went to the airport to welcome them.

The head of the Soviet shooting delegation, A.V. Kustov, had already arrived in Beijing on the 6th, and some more members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 苏联射击代表团到京

1955-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联射击代表团到京
    新华社十二日讯　应邀来中国参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛的苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会射击代表团一行十九人于十二日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有国际友谊射击竞赛组织委员会副主席荣高棠、副秘书长王克、中央体育运动委员会国际联络司副司长张联华和中国运动员代表多人。罗马尼亚射击代表团团长安德烈·尼亚古也到机场欢迎。
    苏联射击代表团团长阿·弗·库斯托夫已于六日先期抵达北京，还有部分团员将于日内抵京。
