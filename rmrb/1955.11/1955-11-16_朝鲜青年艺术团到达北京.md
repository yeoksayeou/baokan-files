Title: Korean Youth Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Korean Youth Art Troupe, a group of 102 people led by Choi Moon-gi, Director of the Propaganda Bureau of the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing by special train from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 15th.

Those who welcomed the Korean Youth Art Troupe at the Beijing Railway Station today included Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Luo Yi and Zhang Ze, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and more than 300 others.

Kim Gui-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and others also welcomed the troupe at the station.

Then, a welcoming ceremony was held at the station. Zheng Zhenduo delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. Choi Moon-gi delivered a reply.

The Korean Youth Art Troupe came to China for a performance tour at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League after participating in the Warsaw Youth Festival. From the beginning of October to now, the art troupe has visited seven cities including Harbin, Changchun, Shenyang, Tianjin, Jinan, Nanjing and Shanghai.

### 朝鲜青年艺术团到达北京

1955-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜青年艺术团到达北京
    据新华社十五日讯　朝鲜青年艺术团一行一百零二人在团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国文化宣传省宣传局局长崔文岐率领下，在十五日下午从上海乘专车到达北京。
    今天到北京车站欢迎朝鲜青年艺术团的，有中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅、章泽等三百多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南等人也到车站欢迎。
    接着，在车站上举行了欢迎仪式。郑振铎代表文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会致欢迎词。崔文岐致答词。
    朝鲜青年艺术团是在参加华沙青年联欢节以后应中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的邀请，前来中国进行访问演出的。从十月初到现在为止，艺术团已先后访问了哈尔滨、长春、沈阳、天津、济南、南京、上海等七个城市。
