Title: U Nu Arrives in Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1955-11-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki Dispatch from TASS: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his entourage arrived in Helsinki from Moscow on November 4th. Welcoming U Nu at the airport were Finnish Speaker of Parliament Fagerholm, Prime Minister Kekkonen, Foreign Minister Virolainen, Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Waris-Kangas, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

　　Prime Minister U Nu will stay in Finland until November 6th, after which he will go to Stockholm.

### 吴努到达赫尔辛基

1955-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努到达赫尔辛基
    新华社五日讯　塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：缅甸联邦总理吴努和随行人员在十一月四日从莫斯科到达赫尔辛基。到机场欢迎吴努的有芬兰议会议长法格霍姆、总理吉科宁、外交部长维洛莱宁、外交部礼宾司司长瓦里康加斯和各国外交使节。
    吴努总理将在芬兰逗留到十一月六日，然后去斯德哥尔摩。
