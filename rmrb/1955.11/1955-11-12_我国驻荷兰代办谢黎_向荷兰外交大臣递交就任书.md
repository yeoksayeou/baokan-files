Title: Xie Li, China's Chargé d'Affaires to the Netherlands, Presents Letter of Credence to the Dutch Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 10th: The Hague dispatch: Xie Li, Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China to the Netherlands, called on Dutch Foreign Minister Beyen and Minister Without Portfolio Luns on the 9th and presented them with his letter of credence. Xie Li also paid visits to Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Serosekerken and Director of the Political Department Eschauzère.

### 我国驻荷兰代办谢黎　向荷兰外交大臣递交就任书

1955-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国驻荷兰代办谢黎
　　向荷兰外交大臣递交就任书
    新华社柏林十日电　海牙消息：中华人民共和国驻荷兰代办谢黎于九日拜会了荷兰王国外交大臣贝恩和不管部大臣伦斯，并向他们递交了就任书。谢黎同时拜会了外交部秘书长塞罗斯凯肯和政治司司长埃邵兹尔。
