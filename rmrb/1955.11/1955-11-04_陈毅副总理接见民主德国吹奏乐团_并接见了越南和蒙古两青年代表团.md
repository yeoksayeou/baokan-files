Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Wind Music Orchestra from the German Democratic Republic, and Also Receives Youth Delegations from Vietnam and Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1955-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received the head of the wind music orchestra from the German Democratic Republic, Paul Kurtzbach, and all the members of the orchestra at 9:30 AM on the 3rd.

Present at the reception were Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China; He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Ma Sicong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, and others.

Also present at the reception was Grutner, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received all the members of the Vietnamese Youth Delegation and the Mongolian Youth Delegation who were invited to China to participate in the National Conference of Active Youth in Socialist Construction, at 5 PM on the 3rd.

During the reception, the head of the Vietnamese Youth Delegation, Hoang Van Dinh, and the head of the Mongolian Youth Delegation, Zhamu Surong, spoke about their impressions of visiting China, and expressed their intention to tell the youth of Vietnam and Mongolia about the construction achievements of Chinese youth and the deep friendship towards them. Chen Yi answered the questions raised by the delegates and introduced the situation of China's construction to the guests.

After the reception, the heads of the Vietnamese and Mongolian Youth Delegations presented their own youth league badges to Chen Yi, and asked him to convey the lofty respect of the youth of Vietnam and Mongolia to Chairman Mao Zedong.

Present at the reception were Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and others. (Attached photos)

### 陈毅副总理接见民主德国吹奏乐团  并接见了越南和蒙古两青年代表团

1955-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见民主德国吹奏乐团
    并接见了越南和蒙古两青年代表团
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在三日上午九时三十分接见了德意志民主共和国吹奏乐团团长保尔·库茨巴赫和全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳，中国音乐家协会副主席马思聪等人。
    接见时，德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳也在座。
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在三日下午五时，接见了应邀来中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的越南青年代表团和蒙古青年代表团的全体团员。
    在接见时，越南青年代表团团长黄文鼎和蒙古青年代表团团长扎木苏荣，谈了他们访问中国的观感，并表示要把中国青年的建设成就和对他们的深厚的友谊告诉越、蒙两国青年。陈毅回答了代表们所提出的问题，并向客人们介绍了中国建设的情况。
    接见后，越南青年代表团团长和蒙古青年代表团团长都把他们自己青年团的徽章赠给陈毅，并且请他转达越南和蒙古两国青年对毛泽东主席的崇高敬意。
    接见时在座的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅和胡克实等人。（附图片）
