Title: Theoretical Conference Commemorating Engels' Birthday Held in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 27th: A theoretical conference was held in Berlin starting on November 24th to commemorate the 135th anniversary of the birth of Friedrich Engels on November 28th. The conference was jointly organized by the Science and Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Academy of Social Sciences of the Party Central Committee, the Marx-Engels-Lenin-Stalin Institute, and the Karl Marx Higher Party School.
    The conference discussed the question of Engels' struggle to establish a revolutionary Marxist workers' party in Germany. Bartel, a staff member of the Academy of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, gave a report on this issue at the meeting on the 25th.

### 柏林举行纪念恩格斯诞辰的理论会议

1955-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　柏林举行纪念恩格斯诞辰的理论会议 
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：为了纪念十一月二十八日弗里德里希·恩格斯诞生一百三十五周年，十一月二十四日起在柏林举行了一个理论会议。这个会议是由德国统一社会党中央委员会科学和宣传部同党中央委员会社会科学学院、马克思—恩格斯—列宁—斯大林学院及卡尔·马克思高级党校联合举办的。
    会议讨论有关恩格斯为争取在德国创立革命的马克思主义的工人政党而进行的斗争的问题。德国统一社会党中央委员会社会科学学院工作人员巴尔特尔已在二十五日的会议上就这个问题作了报告。
