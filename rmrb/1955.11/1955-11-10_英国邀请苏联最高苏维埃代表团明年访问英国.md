Title: Britain Invites Delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to Visit Britain Next Year
Author:
Date: 1955-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, November 9th: TASS reported from London on November 9th: Prime Minister Eden stated in the House of Commons that a Soviet parliamentary delegation would visit Britain in July next year, and an invitation has been sent to the chairmen of the two chambers of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 英国邀请苏联最高苏维埃代表团明年访问英国

1955-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    英国邀请苏联最高苏维埃代表团明年访问英国
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社伦敦九日讯：英国广播公司报道，艾登首相在下院说，一个苏联议会代表团将在明年七月访问英国，邀请书已经送交苏联最高苏维埃两院主席。
