Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegations Depart Separately for the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Li Zheren, head of the People's Republic of China trade delegation to the Soviet Union and Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, left for Moscow by plane on the 10th. Other members of the trade delegation, led by Deputy Head Bai Xiangyin, have already arrived in Moscow.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. In order to develop friendly trade between the People's Republic of China and the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, the Government Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Kong Yuan, head and Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Li Zhuozhi, deputy head, left Beijing for Belgrade on the 8th.

### 我国政府贸易代表团　分别启程赴苏联和南斯拉夫

1955-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国政府贸易代表团
　　分别启程赴苏联和南斯拉夫
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国赴苏贸易代表团团长、中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李哲人在十日乘飞机去莫斯科。贸易代表团其他人员在副团长白向银率领下，已先期到达莫斯科。
    新华社十日讯　为发展中华人民共和国同南斯拉夫联邦共和国之间的友好贸易，中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团在团长、中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长孔原、副团长李琢之率领下，已在八日离北京前往贝尔格莱德。
