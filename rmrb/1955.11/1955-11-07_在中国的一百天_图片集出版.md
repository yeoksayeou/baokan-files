Title: "One Hundred Days in China" Photo Album Published
Author:
Date: 1955-11-07
Page: 2

The Publishing Bureau of the Soviet newspaper Pravda published a photo album "One Hundred Days in China" in late October, taken by the Soviet photographer Baltermants. Baltermants stayed in China for one hundred days last year, visiting places like Beijing, Shanghai, Xinjiang and Tibet. His photo album contains more than two hundred pictures, vividly introducing the significant achievements made by the Chinese people. (Xinhua News Agency)

### “在中国的一百天”图片集出版

1955-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    “在中国的一百天”图片集出版
    苏联真理报出版局在十月下旬出版了苏联摄影师巴尔捷尔曼茨拍摄的一部图片集“在中国的一百天”，巴尔捷尔曼茨去年曾经在中国逗留过一百天，访问过北京、上海、新疆和西藏等地方。他的图片集共选印了两百多张图片，形象地介绍了中国人民所取得的重大成就。（新华社）
