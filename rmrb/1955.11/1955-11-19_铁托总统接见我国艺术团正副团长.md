Title: President Tito Receives the Head and Deputy Head of the Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th: Belgrade Dispatch: On November 16th, during the intermission of the Chinese Art Troupe's premiere performance at the Belgrade National Theatre, President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia received Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Zhao Feng, deputy head, in the theatre's hall. Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, accompanied them during the reception. During the meeting, the Chinese Art Troupe presented gifts to President Tito.

At the end of the performance, President Tito, accompanied by Ranković and Đurašković, Vice-Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, went backstage to congratulate all the members of the Chinese Art Troupe. All the performers responded to President Tito's congratulations with prolonged applause and cheers.

### 铁托总统接见我国艺术团正副团长

1955-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托总统接见我国艺术团正副团长
    新华社布拉格十八日电　贝尔格莱德消息：十一月十六日，当中国艺术团在贝尔格莱德国家剧院举行首次演出中间休息的时候，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托在剧院的大厅里接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥、副团长赵沨。陪同受接见的有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。在接见当中，中国艺术团向铁托总统赠送了礼品。
    在演出结束时，铁托总统偕同联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇和乔拉柯维奇，到后台向中国艺术团全体演员祝贺。全体演员用长时间的鼓掌和欢呼来回答铁托总统的祝贺。
