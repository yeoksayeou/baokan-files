Title: Saudi Arabian King Visits India
Author:
Date: 1955-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 27th: King Saud of Saudi Arabia arrived in New Delhi at noon on November 27th for a state visit to India at the invitation of the Indian government. Saud is the first head of state of an Arab country to visit the Republic of India. He was accompanied by princes and ministers.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Indian President Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, senior Indian government officials, foreign envoys to India, and tens of thousands of Delhi citizens.

President Prasad delivered a speech expressing the hope that King Saud's visit would further strengthen the friendly ties between India and Saudi Arabia.

King Saud expressed the same hope in his speech.

### 沙特阿拉伯国王到印度访问

1955-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

　  沙特阿拉伯国王到印度访问
    新华社新德里二十七日电　应邀到印度作国事访问的沙特阿拉伯国王沙特，十一月二十七日中午到达新德里。沙特是访问印度共和国的第一个阿拉伯国家的元首。和他一同到达的还有王子和大臣等人。
    到机场欢迎的有印度总统普拉沙德，副总统拉德哈克里希南，总理尼赫鲁，印度政府高级官员，各外国驻印度使节和德里市民上万人。
    普拉沙德总统致词，表示希望国王沙特的访问将进一步加强印度和沙特阿拉伯的友谊联系。
    国王沙特致词时表示了同样的愿望。
