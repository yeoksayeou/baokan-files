Title: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Invites Executive Committee Members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce to a Symposium; Vice Premier Chen Yun Delivers a Report on the Transformation of Private Industry and Commerce
Author:
Date: 1955-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch: The United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China invited the members of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce to a symposium on the afternoon of the 1st, where Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun delivered a report on the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce.

Chen Shutong, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Vice Chairmen Li Zhuchen, Nan Hanchen, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Dixin, Meng Yongqian, Sheng Pihua, Rong Yiren, Chen Jingshu, Huang Changshui, Hu Ziang, Gong Tianmin, and the members attending the Second Meeting of the First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce were all invited to attend.

The symposium was presided over by Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Also participating in the symposium were members of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, representatives of the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, as well as cadres from relevant departments of the Central Committee.

More than a thousand people participated in the symposium.

### 中共中央统一战线工作部邀工商联执委举行座谈会  陈云副总理作私营工商业改造问题的报告

1955-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央统一战线工作部邀工商联执委举行座谈会
    陈云副总理作私营工商业改造问题的报告
    新华社一日讯　一日下午，中国共产党中央统一战线工作部邀请中华全国工商业联合会执行委员会的委员们举行座谈会，由国务院副总理陈云作关于资本主义工商业社会主义改造问题的报告。
    中华全国工商业联合会执行委员会主任委员陈叔通，副主任委员李烛尘、南汉宸、章乃器、许涤新、孟用潜、盛丕华、荣毅仁、陈经畬、黄长水、胡子昂、巩天民以及出席全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会第二次会议的委员们都应邀出席。
    座谈会由中国共产党中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉主持。
    参加座谈会的还有中国民主建国会中央委员会委员、中国民主建国会北京市分会和北京市工商业联合会的代表以及中央各有关部门的干部。
    参加座谈会的共有一千多人。
