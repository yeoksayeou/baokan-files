    Our Great Motherland
　　The Cheng (du)-A (ba) Highway, extending from the western Sichuan plain to the snowy mountains and grasslands, was fully opened to traffic on November 10th. The completion of this highway has brought prosperity and happiness to the people of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Sichuan Province. Automobiles are passing through the Zhegushan Mountain, where snow never melts and the altitude is over 4,100 meters above sea level.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　从川西平原伸向雪山草地的成（都）阿（坝）公路，已在十一月十日全线通车。这条公路的建成，给四川省藏族自治区人民带来了繁荣和幸福。汽车正在经过积雪不化、拔海四千一百多公尺的鹧鸪山。
