Title: Tianjin and Shenyang Youth Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth

Author: 
Date: 1955-11-12
Page: 3

Tianjin and Shenyang Youth Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth

　　More than 2,000 young people from all walks of life in Tianjin held an evening party on the 10th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. Students from North Korea, Vietnam, and Indonesia who are studying in Tianjin were also invited to attend the evening party.

　　Pang Maoxiang, Standing Committee Member of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, called on Tianjin youth at the meeting to support the struggle of the World Federation of Democratic Youth for peace with practical actions of hard work in production and active learning. Sun Xiuhua, a youth representative from the state-owned Tianjin No. 6 Cotton Textile Factory who participated in the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, reported on her experience of participating in the festival.

　　After the meeting, Tianjin youth and young friends from foreign countries watched the film "Striving for Peace and Friendship" together. (Xinhua News Agency)

　　On the evening of the 9th, more than 900 young people from all walks of life in Shenyang gathered to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. Young people from Vietnam and North Korea who are studying in our country also participated in the celebration.

　　Zhang Hongjun, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Youth League, spoke at the meeting. He described the situation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth uniting young people from various countries over the past ten years to fight for lasting peace, democracy, national independence, and a bright future for young people. He called on young people in all work posts throughout the city to contribute their strength to fully and overfulfill the glorious tasks given by the motherland.

　　At the meeting, Han Kedong and Li Hua, who had been to the Soviet Union for internships and visits, respectively introduced the Soviet youth's love of peace and heroic construction of a communist society. Student representatives Wang Qihui and Wang Huizhen, who participated in the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, vividly described the grand occasion of the festival and the will of young people from various countries to love peace. Li Xing, the leader of the First Carpentry Youth Shock Team of the Northeast Second Engineering Company, and Jia Bangning, a student of the Northeast Institute of Technology, also expressed their determination to work and study hard at the meeting.

　　After the meeting, a documentary of the Fourth World Youth Festival was shown. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 天津、沈阳青年庆祝世界青联成立十周年

1955-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

    天津、沈阳青年庆祝世界青联成立十周年
　　天津市各界青年二千多人，十日举行了庆祝世界民主青年联盟成立十周年的晚会。在天津的朝鲜、越南、印度尼西亚三个国家的留学生也应邀出席了晚会。
    中国新民主主义青年团天津市委员会常务委员庞茂祥在会上号召天津市青年以努力生产、积极学习的实际行动，来支持世界民主青年联盟为争取和平而进行的斗争。参加第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表、国营天津第六棉纺织厂的青年孙秀华在会上报告了参加联欢节的经过。
    会后，天津市青年和外国的青年朋友们共同观看了电影“争取和平与友谊”。（新华社）
    九日晚，沈阳市各界青年九百多人集会庆祝世界民主青年联盟成立十周年。在我国学习的越南和朝鲜的青年也参加了庆祝会。
    青年团沈阳市委员会书记张鸿钧在会上讲话。他叙述了世界民主青年联盟十年来团结各国青年，为争取持久和平、民主、民族独立和青年的美好将来而斗争的情况。他号召全市各个工作岗位上的青年，要贡献出自己的力量，全面超额完成祖国所给予的光荣任务。
    会上，曾到苏联实习和访问过的韩克栋、李华，分别介绍了苏联青年爱好和平、英勇建设共产主义社会的情况。参加过第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的学生代表王其慧和王慧贞，都生动地叙述了联欢节的盛况及各国青年爱好和平的意志。东北第二工程公司第一木工青年突击队长李兴、东北工学院学生贾邦宁，也在会上表示了他们努力工作和学习的决心。
    会后，放映了第四届世界青年联欢节的纪录片。（新华社）
