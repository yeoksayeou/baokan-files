Title: Indian President Receives Bulganin and Khrushchev. Bulganin and Khrushchev Call on Nehru.
Author:
Date: 1955-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 19th: Indian President Prasad received the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, on November 19th.

After the reception, President Prasad hosted a luncheon for the Soviet leaders.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 19th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, accompanied by the Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov, called on the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, on November 18th.

On the Indian side, those present at the meeting were Mrs. Indira Gandhi, the Ambassador of the Republic of India to the Soviet Union, K. P. S. Menon, and the Chief of Protocol of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, R. A. Baig.

### 印度总统接见布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫拜会尼赫鲁

1955-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度总统接见布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫
　　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫拜会尼赫鲁
    新华社新德里十九日电　印度总统普拉沙德十一月十九日接见了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫。
    在接见后，普拉沙德总统设午宴招待苏联领袖们。
    新华社新德里十九日电　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫十一月十八日由苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫陪同拜会了印度共和国总理尼赫鲁。
    会见时，印度方面在座的有英迪拉·甘地夫人、印度共和国驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农和印度外交部礼宾司司长拉·阿·巴伊格。
