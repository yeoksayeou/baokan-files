Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author:
Date: 1955-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, November 3rd: The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their twenty-fourth meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM this morning. The meeting adjourned at 12:45 PM. The next meeting will be held at 10:00 AM on November 10th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦三日电　中美两国大使级会谈三日上午十时在国联大厦举行第二十四次会议。会议在十二时四十五分休会。下次会议将在十一月十日上午十时举行。
