Title: 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition (Photos)
Author: Wang Zhiyuan Yuan Ling
Date: 1955-11-23
Page: 2

　　1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition
　　The Soviet shooting team, winner of the first event competition.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Our Newspaper Photographer Wang Zhiyuan)
    The top three individual overall scores in the first event competition (men's free small-bore rifle 50-meter prone, kneeling, and standing three-position shooting) were all won by Soviet shooting athletes. The first place was Shamburkin, with 1,160 rings; the second place was Perebelin, with 1,157 rings; and the third place was Gubernatorov, with 1,157 rings.
                                (Our Newspaper Photographer Wang Zhiyuan)
    Chinese female athletes congratulate Soviet sports star Kormushkina on winning the individual championship in the women's free small-bore rifle 50 and 100-meter prone shooting competition.
                                 (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yuan Ling)

### 一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会（图片）
王志渊  袁苓
1955-11-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会
　　获得第一项目竞赛冠军的苏联射击代表团射击队。
　　　　　　　　　　　　本报　王志渊摄
    第一项目竞赛（男子自选小口径步枪五十公尺卧、跪、立三种姿势射击）个人总分前三名的获得者都是苏联射击运动员。第一名是尚布尔金，得一千一百六十环，第二名是佩列别林，得一千一百五十七环，第三名是古别尔纳托罗夫，得一千一百五十七环。
                                本报　王志渊摄
    中国女运动员祝贺苏联运动健将科尔穆什金娜获得女子自选小口径步枪五十和一百公尺卧姿射击竞赛个人冠军。
                                 新华社记者　袁苓摄
