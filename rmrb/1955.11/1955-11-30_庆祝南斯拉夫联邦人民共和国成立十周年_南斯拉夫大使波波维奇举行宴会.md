Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Yugoslav Ambassador Popovic Holds Banquet
Author:
Date: 1955-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 29th. Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, held a banquet this noon to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

Those invited to the banquet included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Peng Dehuai and He Long, Vice Premiers; Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, and Li Weihan, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; and Zhang Zhizhong and Luo Longji, members of the Standing Committee; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.

At the banquet, Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, delivered speeches successively.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the prosperity and strength of China and Yugoslavia, to the friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and Yugoslavia, and to the good health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

From 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM, Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, also held a grand reception to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia. Those attending the reception included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, and all the personnel who attended the noon banquet, as well as heads of various departments of state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, people from all walks of life in Beijing, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries in China, totaling more than 500 people. ### 庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年　南斯拉夫大使波波维奇举行宴会

1955-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年
　　南斯拉夫大使波波维奇举行宴会
    新华社二十九日讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻中国大使波波维奇今天中午举行宴会，庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年。
    应邀出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来、副总理彭德怀、贺龙，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、彭真、李维汉和委员张治中、罗隆基，国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，外交部副部长张闻天等。
    宴会上，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇和中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先后讲了话。
    席间，宾主不断为中南两国的繁荣和富强，为中南两国人民的友好合作，为中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托同志的身体健康，干杯。
    下午五时到七时，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇又举行盛大酒会，庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年。出席酒会的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德、国务院总理周恩来和出席午间宴会的全体人员，以及国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，北京社会各界人士，各国驻华使节和外交官员等共五百多人。（附图片）
