Page: 1

Hunan Builds First Agricultural Machine Station

Changde County, Hunan Province, is building the province's first agricultural machine station, with plans to complete all construction work this year. At present, the Hunan Provincial Department of Agriculture and other relevant departments are stepping up the transfer of machinery and equipment to the agricultural machine station and assigning cadres, striving to carry out trial cultivation in November.

This agricultural machine station will be equipped with two Soviet-made DT-54 medium tractors, two Ford tractors, and eighteen new-style agricultural machines, including 24-row grain seeders, 4-row cotton seeders, four-wheeled trailers, and DT-5 furrow plows. The tentatively planned cultivation area for this agricultural machine station is 5,000 mu, and in the future, it will cultivate more than 50,000 mu of land for nearby agricultural production cooperatives and mutual aid teams.
         (Our collective correspondent, Xin Hunan Bao Editorial Department)

### 湖南兴建第一个农业机器站

1955-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　  湖南兴建第一个农业机器站
    湖南省常德县正在兴建全省第一个农业机器站，计划在今年内全部完成建站工作。目前，湖南省农业厅等有关部门正在加紧给农业机器站调运机器设备和配备干部，争取在十一月份进行试耕。
    这个农业机器站将配备有苏联中型德特五四号拖拉机两台、福特拖拉机两台和二十四行谷物播种机、四行棉花播种机、四轮拖车、德特五铧犁等新式农业机器十八台。这个农业机器站目前暂定的耕作面积为五千亩，将来要为附近的农业生产合作社和互助组的五万多亩土地耕种。
          （本报集体通讯员新湖南报编辑部）
