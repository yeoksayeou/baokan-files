Title: Soviet Moscow newspapers prominently publish statement by our Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1951-11-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th—The newspapers Pravda, Izvestia, Trud, Krasnaya Zvezda, and Red Fleet all prominently published on the 20th Foreign Minister Zhou's statement regarding the Soviet government's four peace proposals to the Sixth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 苏联莫斯科各报显著刊登我周外长声明

1951-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联莫斯科各报
    显著刊登我周外长声明
    【新华社莫斯科二十日电】真理报、消息报、劳动报、红星报和红海军报二十日都以显著地位刊登周外长关于苏联政府向联合国第六届大会提出四项和平建议的声明。
