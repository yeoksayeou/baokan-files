Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Representatives' Meeting Determines One-Year Work Plan; Comrade Peng Zhen Elected President
Author:
Date: 1951-11-09
Page: 1

    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Representatives' Meeting
    Determines One-Year Work Plan
    Comrade Peng Zhen Elected President
    [Report from this newspaper] The first session of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Representatives' Meeting concluded on the afternoon of the 8th, lasting one and a half days. On the morning of the 8th, the representatives held group discussions on the reports "Summary of Work in the Past Two Years" and "Work Plan for the Coming Year." In the afternoon, general speeches were given at the meeting, resolutions and the charter of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association were adopted, and Peng Zhen was elected as president, with Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Liu Ren, Wu Han, Yu Xinqing, Xiao Ming, Zhang Xiaomei, Shu Sheyu, Sun Xiaocun, Cao Jinghua, Liao Mosha, and Wang Zhizhi elected as vice presidents.
    In their speeches, the representatives unanimously agreed with the reports on the summary of work in the past two years and the work plan for the coming year. The representatives pointed out that due to the development of Sino-Soviet friendship work in the past two years, the general public's understanding of the Soviet Union has been greatly enhanced, and the Friendship Association has also seen great development. The friendship between China and the Soviet Union has been greatly consolidated and developed. Everyone decided to continue strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship work after returning, widely carrying out patriotic and internationalist propaganda among the masses, and vigorously developing membership.
    In his concluding report, President Peng Zhen gave specific instructions on the future work of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. He pointed out that the central task of members is to learn from and promote the advanced experiences of the Soviet Union. The content and form of education and publicity must be suitable for the specific circumstances of people from all walks of life and strive to be popular. He also called on everyone to continue developing Sino-Soviet friendship work based on the resolutions of the meeting and around the city's current major tasks such as resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, increasing production, and practicing economy. Finally, the conference successfully concluded after a closing speech by Executive Chairman Yu Xinqing.

### 北京市中苏友协代表会  确定一年工作计划  彭真同志当选为会长

1951-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协代表会
    确定一年工作计划
    彭真同志当选为会长
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会第一届会员代表大会在八日下午闭幕，会议历时一天半。八日上午，代表们分组讨论了“两年来工作总结”和“今后一年工作计划”的报告。下午进行大会发言，通过决议和北京市中苏友好协会会章，并选举彭真为会长，张奚若、许德珩、刘仁、吴晗、余心清、萧明、张晓梅、舒舍予、孙晓村、曹靖华、廖沫沙、王之相等十二人为副会长。
    在大会发言中，代表们一致同意关于两年工作总结和今后一年工作计划的报告。代表们指出过去两年由于中苏友好工作的开展，广大群众对苏联的认识已普遍提高，友协组织也有了广大的发展，中苏两国的友好，已获得极大的巩固和发展。大家决定回去后，继续加强中苏友好工作，在群众中广泛进行爱国主义和国际主义的宣传，并大力发展会员。
    彭真会长在总结报告中，对今后北京市中苏友好协会的工作作了具体的指示。他指出会员的中心工作是学习苏联的先进经验和宣传苏联的先进经验。教育宣传的内容和形式必须适合各阶层人民的具体情况并力求通俗化。他并号召大家根据大会决议围绕着抗美援朝、增产节约等本市当前几项主要工作，继续发展中苏友好工作。最后由执行主席余心清致闭会词后大会胜利闭幕。
