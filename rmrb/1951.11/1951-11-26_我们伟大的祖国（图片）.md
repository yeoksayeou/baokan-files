Improving the health work of minority peoples is valued by the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government and the People's Governments of various minority areas. In July of this year, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government sent an epidemic prevention team to the Xilin Gol League to treat the herdsmen. This is the scene of the epidemic prevention team being warmly welcomed upon arrival.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    改进少数民族人民的卫生工作，是中央人民政府卫生部和各少数民族地区人民政府所重视的。今年七月间内蒙古自治区人民政府派出防疫队，到锡林郭勒盟去给牧民治病。这是防疫队到达时受到热烈欢迎的情形。
