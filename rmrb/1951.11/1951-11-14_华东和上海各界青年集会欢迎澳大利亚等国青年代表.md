Title: East China and Shanghai Youth Gather to Welcome Youth Representatives from Australia and Other Countries

Author: 
Date: 1951-11-14
Page: 4

　　East China and Shanghai Youth Gather to Welcome Youth Representatives from Australia and Other Countries

    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th] More than 1,800 youth from all walks of life in East China and Shanghai gathered on the evening of the 11th to celebrate "World Youth Day" and "International Students' Week," and to welcome youth representatives from six countries, including Australia, Ceylon, Cuba, Argentina, Chile, and South Africa, who are visiting East China.

### 华东和上海各界青年集会欢迎澳大利亚等国青年代表

1951-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　华东和上海各界青年集会
    欢迎澳大利亚等国青年代表
    【新华社上海十三日电】华东和上海各界青年一千八百多人十一日晚集会庆祝“世界青年日”和“国际学生周”，并欢迎前来华东访问的澳大利亚、锡兰、古巴、阿根廷、智利和南非等六国青年代表。
