Title: Nanjing and Xi'an Railway Workers and Automobile Drivers Continue to Organize Transportation Teams to Go to Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-11-17
Page: 4

　　Nanjing and Xi'an Railway Workers and Automobile Drivers
    Continue to Organize Transportation Teams to Go to Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Railway workers from Nanjing and Xi'an and automobile drivers from Nanjing continue to go to Korea to support the Chinese People's Volunteers. Railway workers from Nanjing and Xi'an organized volunteer aid Korea transportation teams to go to Korea during the anniversary of the Chinese Volunteer Army's participation in the war. On the 10th of this month, another group of volunteer automobile drivers from Nanjing set off north to support the front lines in Korea. People from all walks of life in the two cities held farewell meetings respectively.

### 南京西安铁路职工与汽车司机  继续组织运输队前往朝鲜

1951-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　南京西安铁路职工与汽车司机
    继续组织运输队前往朝鲜
    【新华社十六日讯】南京、西安两市的铁路职工和南京汽车司机继续前赴朝鲜支援中国人民志愿军。南京、西安两市铁路职工曾在纪念中国志愿军出国作战一周年时，分别组织了志愿援朝运输队前赴朝鲜。本月十日，南京市又有一批志愿支援朝鲜前线的汽车司机启程北上。两市各界人民曾分别举行欢送会。
