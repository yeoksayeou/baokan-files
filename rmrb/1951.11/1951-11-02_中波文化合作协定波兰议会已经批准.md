Title: Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement Approved by Polish Parliament
Author:
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 1st—TASS reports from Warsaw: The autumn session of the Polish Republic's Parliament opened in Warsaw on October 30th. The Parliament approved the cultural cooperation agreement concluded between Poland and the People's Republic of China in Warsaw on April 3rd. The Parliament also passed a bill on the establishment of the Polish Academy of Sciences.

### 中波文化合作协定波兰议会已经批准

1951-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　中波文化合作协定波兰议会已经批准
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰共和国议会的秋季会议已于十月三十日在华沙开幕。议会批准了波兰与中华人民共和国于四月三日在华沙缔结的文化合作协定。议会并通过了关于成立波兰科学院的法案。
