Title: Shanghai Municipal Democratic Parties Cadre Training Class Graduates
Author:
Date: 1951-11-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 22nd. The Shanghai Municipal Democratic Parties Cadre Training Class, organized by the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties in August this year, held its graduation ceremony on the 20th of this month. Sheng Pihua, representative of the Shanghai Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, Pan Hannian, representative of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, responsible persons from the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties, and Shanghai municipal people's organizations attended the meeting to offer congratulations. Sheng Pihua and Pan Hannian encouraged all students in their speeches to continue ideological remolding in practice in the future, to do a good job in consolidating and expanding the people's democratic united front and the organizations of various democratic parties, and to strive to launch the campaign to increase production and practice economy.
    The class had a total of 224 students, including working cadres from the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties and some people's organizations in Shanghai. During the study period, the students listened to reports by Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Chen Yi, Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, on the Chinese revolution and the experience of the Chinese Communist Party in building the Party, as well as the report by Chen Cisheng, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, on the transmission of the resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. After the study, all students generally improved their political understanding; they also gained a lot in transforming their thinking and changing their work style.

### 上海市各民主党派干部训练班已结业

1951-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    上海市各民主党派干部训练班已结业
    【新华社上海二十二日电】各民主党派上海地方组织在今年八月间举办的上海市各民主党派干部训练班，已在本月二十日举行结业典礼。上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会代表盛丕华、中国共产党上海市委员会代表潘汉年、各民主党派上海地方组织和上海市各人民团体的负责人都到会致贺。盛丕华和潘汉年在讲话中勉励全体学员今后在实践中继续进行思想改造，做好巩固和扩大人民民主统一战线和各民主党派组织的工作，并为展开增产节约运动而努力。
    该班共有学员二百二十四人，包括各民主党派上海地方组织和上海市部分人民团体的工作干部。学员们在学习期间，曾听取了华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、上海市人民政府市长陈毅等有关中国革命问题和中国共产党建党经验的报告，以及中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员陈巳生关于中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议各项决议的传达报告。全体学员经过学习后，普遍提高了政治认识；在改造思想和改变作风方面也有很大收获。
