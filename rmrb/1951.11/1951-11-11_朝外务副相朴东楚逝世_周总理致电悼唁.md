Title: DPRK Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Dong-chu Passes Away, Premier Zhou Sends Condolences
Author:
Date: 1951-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th: Park Dong-chu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, unfortunately passed away on November 4th while performing important national duties, at the age of thirty-eight. The Republic's Cabinet has organized a funeral committee headed by Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Heon-yeong, and a grand funeral was held on the 6th. Those attending the funeral included: Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Heon-yeong, Vice Premiers Hong Myung-hee, Heo Ga-i, Central Committee Secretaries of the Workers' Party Lee Seung-yeop, Park Jeong-ae, Park Chang-ok, etc. Representatives from the embassies of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and various People's Democratic countries in Korea also attended the funeral.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, sent a telegram to Park Heon-yeong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the 9th to express condolences for the death of Park Dong-chu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea. The original telegram is as follows: Comrade Park Heon-yeong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

I was shocked and saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Vice Minister Park Dong-chu of your Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Please accept my deepest condolences.

						Zhou Enlai
					November 9, 1951, Beijing

### 朝外务副相朴东楚逝世  周总理致电悼唁

1951-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝外务副相朴东楚逝世
    周总理致电悼唁
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务副相、朝鲜劳动党中央委员朴东楚于十一月四日在执行国家重要任务时不幸逝世，享年三十八岁。共和国内阁特组织以副首相兼外务相朴宪永为委员长的治丧委员会，并在六日举行隆重葬仪。参加葬仪的有：内阁副首相兼外务相朴宪永，副首相洪命熹、许嘉谊，劳动党中央书记李承烨、朴正爱、朴昌玉等。苏联、中华人民共和国及各人民民主国家驻朝使馆代表也参加了葬仪。
    【新华社十日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在九日致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永，吊唁朝鲜外务省副相朴东楚的逝世，原电如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永同志：
    惊悉贵国外务省朴东楚副相不幸逝世，谨致沉痛的哀悼。
                                周恩来
                    一九五一年十一月九日北京
