Title: International Union of Land and Air Transport Workers Calls on Transport Unions Worldwide to Support the "Matsukawa Incident" Defendants
Author:
Date: 1951-11-16
Page: 4

　　International Union of Land and Air Transport Workers
    Calls on Transport Unions Worldwide to Support the "Matsukawa Incident" Defendants
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated the 14th] Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, wrote to Comrade Moraru, General Secretary of the International Union of Land and Air Transport Workers (an industry department of the World Federation of Trade Unions), on October 15, suggesting that the International Union of Land and Air Transport Workers mobilize all its member organizations and friendly groups to support the just struggle of the innocent defendants in the Japanese "Matsukawa Incident". According to a TASS report from Bucharest on the 13th: The Secretariat of the International Union of Land and Air Transport Workers has issued an appeal to all transport unions worldwide, requesting them to unanimously support all defendants in the "Matsukawa Incident" - twenty Japanese patriotic workers. The Secretariat of the International Union of Land and Air Transport Workers urged transport workers worldwide to expand the movement demanding the annulment of the Japanese court's first-instance judgment against the defendants in the "Matsukawa Incident" and the release of these twenty Japanese patriots.
    Romania, Bulgaria, India, Sweden, and other countries have sent numerous telegrams protesting the framing of Japanese patriotic workers.

### 陆空运输工人工会国际  呼吁全世界运输工会声援“松川事件”被告

1951-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　陆空运输工人工会国际
    呼吁全世界运输工会声援“松川事件”被告
    【新华社十四日讯】中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯曾在十月十五日写信给陆空运输工人工会国际（世界工会联合会产业部之一）总书记莫拉鲁，建议陆空运输工人工会国际发动一切会员组织和友好团体，支援日本“松川事件”无辜被告的正义斗争。据塔斯社布加勒斯特十三日讯：陆空运输工人工会国际书记处已向全世界的一切运输工会发出呼吁，要求它们一致声援“松川事件”的全体被告——二十名日本爱国工人。陆空运输工人工会国际书记处敦促全世界的运输工人扩大要求取消日本法院在第一审中对“松川事件”被告的判决和释放这二十名日本爱国者的运动。
    罗马尼亚、保加利亚、印度、瑞典和其他国家已经发了无数封电报，抗议对日本爱国工人的陷害。
