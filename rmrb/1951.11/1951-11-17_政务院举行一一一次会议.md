Title: Government Administration Council Holds 111th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-11-17
Page: 1

　　Government Administration Council Holds 111th Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The 111th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 16th. The meeting heard and approved the summary report by Li Dequan, head of the Central People's Government Central and Southern Nationalities Visiting Group, on the group's visit to the various nationalities in Central and Southern China. The meeting passed several appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行一一一次会议

1951-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　 政务院举行一一一次会议
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十一次政务会议于十六日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府中南各民族访问团团长李德全所作的关于该团访问中南各民族的总结报告。会议通过了任免案多起。
