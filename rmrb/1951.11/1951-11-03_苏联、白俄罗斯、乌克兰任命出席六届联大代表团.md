Title: USSR, Byelorussia, Ukraine Appoint Delegations to Sixth UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1951-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 31st: TASS reports that the governments of the USSR, Byelorussia, and Ukraine have separately appointed delegations to the Sixth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, scheduled to open in Paris on November 6th.

    The head of the Soviet delegation is Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky. The representatives are Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister and Soviet Representative to the United Nations Security Council Malik, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Zarubin, Soviet Ambassador to Poland Sobolev, and Soviet Ambassador to France Pavlov. The deputy representatives are: Soviet Foreign Ministry Secretary-General Podtserochkov, Soviet Foreign Ministry Special Committee Member Yarujian, Foreign Minister of the Turkmen Soviet Socialist Republic Betevev, and Soviet Deputy Representative to the Security Council Tsarapkin. The delegation advisors are: Rosin, Morozov, Saksin, and Sudatov.
    The head of the Byelorussian delegation is the Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, Kiselev. The representatives are: Astapenko, Kustov, Kutannikov, Kusov.
    The head of the Ukrainian delegation is the Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, Baranov. The representatives are: Balamarzhuk, Demchenko, Stadnik, and Shcherbatyuk.

### 苏联、白俄罗斯、乌克兰任命出席六届联大代表团

1951-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联、白俄罗斯、乌克兰任命出席六届联大代表团
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十月三十一日讯：苏联、白俄罗斯、乌克兰政府已分别任命出席定于十一月六日在巴黎开幕的联合国大会第六届会议的代表团。
    苏联代表团团长为苏联外交部长维辛斯基，代表为苏联外交部副部长、苏联驻联合国安全理事会代表马立克，苏联驻英国大使查鲁宾，苏联驻波兰大使索波列夫，苏联驻法国大使巴甫洛夫。副代表为：苏联外交部秘书长波得切罗夫，苏联外交部专门委员会委员亚鲁疆，土库曼苏维埃社会主义共和国外交部长贝捷夫，苏联驻安全理事会副代表查拉普金。代表团顾问有：罗辛，莫罗佐夫，萨克辛和苏达托夫。
    白俄罗斯代表团团长为白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长基塞列夫，代表为：阿斯塔平科，库斯托夫，库坦尼柯夫，库索夫。
    乌克兰代表团团长为乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席巴拉诺夫斯基，代表为：巴拉马尔朱克，杰姆琴科，斯塔德尼克和施契尔巴秋克。
