Title: Guo Moruo and Others Arrive in Vienna
Author:
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 1st. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Second Session of the World Peace Council, and his party arrived in Vienna on October 30th. Representatives of the Austrian Peace Council and others were at the airport to welcome them. In response to a request from a reporter from the "Austrian People's Voice," Guo Moruo delivered a brief speech at the airport, saying: "Although China and Austria are far apart, the Chinese and Austrian people share a common desire for peace. Let us further strengthen our joint efforts for the cause of peace."

### 郭沫若等抵达维也纳

1951-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若等抵达维也纳
    【新华社维也纳一日电】出席世界和平理事会第二次会议的中国代表团团长郭沫若等一行已在十月三十日抵达维也纳。到机场欢迎的有奥地利和平理事会的代表多人。郭沫若应“奥地利人民之声报》记者之请在机场发表了简短谈话，他说：“中国和奥地利相距虽远，但是中国人民和奥地利人民都有着要求和平的共同愿望。让我们进一步加强我们为和平事业而进行的共同努力。”
