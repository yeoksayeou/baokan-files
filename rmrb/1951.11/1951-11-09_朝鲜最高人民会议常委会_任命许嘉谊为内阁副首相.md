Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee Appoints Ho Ka-i as Vice Premier of the Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1951-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th. The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 2nd, appointing Ho Ka-i as Vice Premier of the Republic's Cabinet.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常委会  任命许嘉谊为内阁副首相

1951-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常委会
    任命许嘉谊为内阁副首相
    【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在二日发布命令，任命许嘉谊为共和国内阁副首相。
