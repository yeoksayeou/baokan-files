Title: Soviet Volga-Don Canal Project: Excavation Work Enters Final Stage
Author:
Date: 1951-11-06
Page: 4

　　Soviet Volga-Don Canal Project
　　Excavation Work Enters Final Stage
    [News from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The excavation work of the Soviet Volga-Don Canal has entered the final stage. According to TASS news: In the area of the canal, which stretches over 500 kilometers, construction work is proceeding day and night. The speed of construction is astonishing; construction workers are excavating nearly 500,000 cubic meters of soil and completing 9,000 cubic meters of concrete work in a single day. Hundreds of highly efficient machines are being used extensively in the construction zone. The 101-kilometer navigable canal connecting the Volga and Don rivers must traverse the watershed between these two rivers, and the watershed is nearly 100 meters higher than the Volga's water surface. Ships traveling along this canal from the Volga to the Don must ascend 88 meters through nine locks and then descend through four locks. Currently, construction workers have successfully overcome many difficulties related to building this cascade. Several hydraulic engineering projects are nearing completion. In the main construction area of the canal—the Tsimlyansk Hydroelectric Project area—construction workers are working hard to complete the 13-kilometer-long earth dam before winter arrives. By next spring, this great communist construction project will be fully completed.＊＊＊＊＊＊

### 苏联伏尔加河—顿河运河工程  开凿工作已进入最后阶段

1951-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联伏尔加河—顿河运河工程
　  开凿工作已进入最后阶段
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：苏联伏尔加河—顿河运河的开凿工作已经进入最后阶段。据塔斯社消息：在运河地区五百多公里长的地段上，建筑工作正在昼夜进行着。建筑工程的速度是很惊人的，建筑工人一天之中差不多要挖掘五十万公方的泥土，完成九千公方的混凝土工程。工程地带广泛使用着几百部高效率的机器。连接伏尔加河和顿河的一○一公里长的通航运河，要横贯这两条河流之间的分水岭，而分水岭差不多比伏尔加河的河面高一百公尺。船只沿着这条运河从伏尔加河驶往顿河，必须经过九道水闸上行八十八公尺，然后再经过四道水闸往下驶行。现在，建筑工人们已经顺利地克服了和建筑这个阶梯有关的许多困难。有几处水利工程即将完成。在运河主要工程地区——齐姆良水利工程地带，建筑工人们正在全力争取冬天来临以前完成十三公里长的土堤。到明年春天，这个伟大的共产主义建设工程就可以全部完成。＊＊＊＊＊＊
