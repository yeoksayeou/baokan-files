Title: PLA Soldiers and Officers Entering Tibet Join Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1951-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 14th -- The People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Lhasa, at a grand meeting on the 7th of this month to celebrate the anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution, all commanders and fighters swore to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. General Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the PLA troops entering Tibet, said at the meeting: "We are now celebrating the October Revolution and becoming glorious members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. We should especially learn from the great spirit of the Soviet Union in building Siberia and assist the Tibetan people in building Tibet." He pointed out that the Siberia of the Soviet Union today is the Tibet of our tomorrow. He finally called on all commanders and fighters to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Red Army and build a strong and modern national defense force to consolidate the national defense of the motherland.

At the meeting, all commanders and fighters held a ceremony to swear to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 解放军进藏部队指战员加入中苏友协

1951-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放军进藏部队指战员加入中苏友协
    【新华社拉萨十四日电】进驻拉萨的人民解放军进藏部队，在本月七日热烈庆祝苏联十月革命节的纪念会上，全体指挥员战斗员宣誓加入中苏友好协会。进藏部队政治委员谭冠三将军在大会上讲话说：“我们现在庆祝十月革命节，和加入中苏友好协会做个光荣的会员，就应该特别学习苏联建设西伯利亚的伟大精神，来协助西藏人民建设西藏。”他指出苏联西伯利亚的今天，就是我们西藏的明天。他最后号召全体指战员学习苏联红军的先进经验，建设一支强大的现代化的国防军，巩固祖国国防。
    会上，全体指战员们举行了宣誓加入中苏友好协会的仪式。
