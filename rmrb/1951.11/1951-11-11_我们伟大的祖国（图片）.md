Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-11-11
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The patriotic education has continuously improved the working enthusiasm of the working class. Workers of the No. 3 rotary kiln in the Xicun Cement Factory in Guangdong have safely operated the machine for 238 days since December 25th of last year, increasing the wealth of the country by approximately 1.853 billion yuan in Renminbi, creating the highest record in the country. This is the situation where the workers voluntarily stopped the kiln for a major overhaul after breaking the record.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    爱国主义的教育使工人阶级的劳动热忱不断提高。广东西村士敏土工厂第三号回转窑的工人，从去年十二月二十五日起使机器安全运转了二百三十八天，为国家增加了财富折合人民币约十八亿五千三百多万元，创造了全国最高的纪录。这是工人们在突破纪录后主动停窑，进行大检修的情形。
