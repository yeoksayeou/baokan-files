Title: Both Sides' Staff Have Corrected the Specific Location of the Existing Contact Line; Plenary Session of Both Delegations Scheduled for Today
Author:
Date: 1951-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 26th—On the 26th, the staff officers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers side and the United Nations Command side corrected the specific location of the existing contact line between the two sides, and it was decided to submit it to the Korean Armistice Negotiation Subcommittee for approval at 10:00 am (Korean time) on the 27th. The Subcommittee will determine this existing contact line as the military demarcation line and the median line of the demilitarized zone. The plenary session of both delegations is scheduled to be held at 11:00 am on the 27th to approve the agreement of the Subcommittee on the second agenda item and the specific location of the military demarcation line and the demilitarized zone determined by the Subcommittee. After the delegations formally reach an agreement on the second agenda item, they can enter the discussion of the third agenda item, "Specific arrangements for the realization of a ceasefire and armistice in Korea, including the composition, powers and responsibilities of the agency supervising the implementation of the ceasefire and armistice terms."

### 双方参谋人员已校正双方现有接触线具体位置  双方代表团大会定今日举行

1951-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    双方参谋人员已校正双方现有接触线具体位置
    双方代表团大会定今日举行
    【新华社开城二十六日电】二十六日朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军方面及联合国军方面的参谋人员校正了现有双方接触线的具体位置，并定于二十七日上午十时（朝鲜时间）呈交朝鲜停战谈判小组委员会批准，由小组委员会确定这一现有接触线为军事分界线与非军事区的中线。双方代表团大会定于二十七日上午十一时举行，以便批准小组委员会对于第二项议程的协议及小组委员会所确定的军事分界线与非军事区的具体位置。代表团大会对于第二项议程正式达成协议以后，即可进入第三项议程“在朝鲜境内实现停火与休战的具体安排，包括监督停火休战条款实施机构的组成、权力与职司”的讨论。
