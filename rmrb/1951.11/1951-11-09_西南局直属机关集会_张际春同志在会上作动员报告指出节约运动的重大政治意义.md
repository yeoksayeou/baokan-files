Title: Southwest Bureau Directly Subordinate Organs Hold Meeting; Comrade Zhang Jichun Delivers Mobilization Report, Pointing Out the Great Political Significance of the Economy Drive

Author: 

Date: 1951-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:
    Southwest Bureau Directly Subordinate Organs Hold Meeting
    Comrade Zhang Jichun Delivers Mobilization Report, Pointing Out the Great Political Significance of the Economy Drive

[本报讯]据新华社讯：为响应毛主席关于增加生产，厉行节约以支持中国人民志愿军的号召，中共中央西南局在一日举行全体工作人员大会，由西南局委员张际春同志作关于节约运动的动员报告。

[This Dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: To respond to Chairman Mao's call to increase production and practice strict economy in order to support the Chinese People's Volunteers, the Southwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China held a general meeting of all staff members on the 1st, at which Comrade Zhang Jichun, a member of the Southwest Bureau, delivered a mobilization report on the economy drive.

    张际春同志指出：我们应该从毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的开会词中领会到：节约不是普通小事情，而是全中国人民今天的中心任务。节约是为了支援中国人民志愿军争取抗美援朝斗争的胜利；为了加强我们祖国的国防力量和经济力量。节约运动有重大的政治意义。

    Comrade Zhang Jichun pointed out: We should understand from Chairman Mao's opening remarks at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference that economy is not an ordinary trivial matter, but the central task of all the Chinese people today. Economy is to support the Chinese People's Volunteers in winning the victory in the struggle to resist US aggression and aid Korea; to strengthen our motherland's defense and economic power. The economy drive has great political significance.

    张际春同志说：目前在许多单位中，浪费现象普遍存在。我们必须遵照毛主席的指示厉行节约。首先是尽量节约开支。据各单位今年一月到十月的统计，器具设备、汽车修理、公杂费、会议费等六大项开支无一项不超支。一月到六月的半年中，七个部门在公杂、水电、学习、汽油四项开支中，只有一个部门没有超支。在日常生活方面，各单位一般都不大注意节约，随便用电用水；对房屋、家具和办公用具也不够爱惜。修建工作缺乏计划性和检查，房屋塌了又重修，浪费极大。所有这些现象都必须坚决予以消除。其次，必须合理调整机构，精简可以精简的人员，以便作其他的事情。

    Comrade Zhang Jichun said: At present, waste is widespread in many units. We must follow Chairman Mao's instructions and practice strict economy. The first is to save expenses as much as possible. According to the statistics of various units from January to October this year, none of the six major items of expenditure, such as equipment and apparatus, automobile repair, miscellaneous public expenses, and conference fees, have not exceeded the budget. In the half year from January to June, among the four items of public affairs, water and electricity, study, and gasoline expenses of seven departments, only one department did not exceed the budget. In daily life, units generally do not pay much attention to economy, using electricity and water casually; they also do not cherish houses, furniture, and office supplies enough. Construction work lacks planning and inspection, and houses are rebuilt after they collapse, resulting in great waste. All these phenomena must be resolutely eliminated. Secondly, we must reasonably adjust the structure of organizations and streamline personnel that can be streamlined in order to do other things.

    张际春同志最后指出：要开展节约运动，必须普遍深入动员，全面进行；必须有领导地坚持下去，使节约成为经常的习惯。这样，我们就会节约出大量的经费，用到国家和人民事业最重要的方面去，同时又能够保证我们一定的生活水平。各单位应即订出自己的节约计划，在发动群众的基础上展开节约运动。
    Comrade Zhang Jichun finally pointed out: To carry out the economy drive, we must conduct widespread and in-depth mobilization and implement it comprehensively; we must persevere under leadership so that economy becomes a regular habit. In this way, we will save a lot of funds and use them in the most important aspects of the country and the people's cause, while at the same time ensuring our certain standard of living. Each unit should immediately formulate its own economy plan and launch an economy drive on the basis of mobilizing the masses.
