Title: China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Beijing Branch Widely Publicize with Propaganda Vehicles; October Revolution Anniversary Celebration Evening to be Held Tonight
Author:
Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 3

China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Beijing Branch
　　Widely Publicize with Propaganda Vehicles
　　October Revolution Anniversary Celebration Evening to be Held Tonight

To celebrate the 34th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association jointly dispatched five propaganda vehicles newly donated by the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to various urban and suburban areas of Beijing from the 3rd to the 6th of this month for extensive mass propaganda activities.

In addition, the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association, together with the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and other units, jointly held a lecture meeting at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace to celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary. This evening (the 7th), it will jointly hold a celebration evening with the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries at No. 19 Nanheyan Street. At the meeting, Zhukov, the representative of the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, will speak on "Celebrating the 34th Anniversary of the October Revolution," and the film "Lenin in October" will be shown.

All levels of branches of the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association will also hold various activities today to expand the celebration.

### 中苏友协总会和北京市分会  出动宣传车广泛进行宣传  今晚将举行十月革命节庆祝晚会

1951-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会和北京市分会
    出动宣传车广泛进行宣传
    今晚将举行十月革命节庆祝晚会
    为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年，中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会联合在本月三日至六日出动苏联对外文化协会新赠宣传车五辆赴京市城郊各地进行了广泛群众性宣传活动。
    此外，北京市中苏友好协会并与北京市总工会等单位在北京市劳动人民文化宫联合举办了庆祝十月革命节的讲演会。今（七日）晚将与苏联对外文化协会在南河沿十九号举行庆祝晚会，会上将有苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表茹克福讲“庆祝十月革命节三十四周年”，并放映电影“列宁在十月”。
    京市中苏友好协会各级支会也于今日分别举行各种活动扩大庆祝。
