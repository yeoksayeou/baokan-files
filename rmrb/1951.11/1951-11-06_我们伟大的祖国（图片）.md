    Our Great Motherland
    Inspired by patriotism, the working class of Northeast China is using various methods to unleash the potential of industry and accumulate huge wealth for the country. Here, Zhu Shusong, a technician from the Second Machine Tool Plant in Northeast China, is guiding workers to increase production using high-speed cutting methods.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北工人阶级在爱国主义的鼓舞下，正用种种办法发挥工业的潜在能力，为国家积累巨大的财富。在这里，东北第二机器厂的技术员朱澍松正指导着工人以高速度切削法来增加生产。
