Title: Pakistan's First Ambassador to China, Mr. Raza, and Entourage Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st - Pakistan's first ambassador to China, Mr. Raza, and his entourage arrived in Beijing by special train from Guangzhou at 8:20 a.m. on the 1st. Those who went to the station to greet them included: Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. Swedish Ambassador Torsten Hammarström, Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Swiss Minister René Zoelly, Pakistani Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Ahmed Ali, Polish Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Daoding, Indian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Koirala, Bulgarian Embassy Counselor Tsarlvoranov, Czechoslovak Embassy First Secretary Reigr, Romanian Embassy First Secretary Mentianu, Indonesian Embassy Second Secretary Tan Taowm, as well as the British, Dutch, and Norwegian government negotiation representatives also went to the station to greet them.

### 巴基斯坦驻我国首任大使罗查等一行昨日抵京

1951-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦驻我国首任大使罗查等一行昨日抵京
    【新华社一日讯】巴基斯坦政府驻我国首任大使罗查等一行在一日晨八时二十分乘专车由广州抵京。赴车站迎接的有：我外交部办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司代司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗和京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美。瑞典大使赛德勃隆、缅甸大使吴拉茂、瑞士公使任佐立、巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办阿默德·艾礼、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、印度大使馆临时代办高尔、保加利亚大使馆参赞察尔伏拉诺夫、捷克斯洛伐克大使馆一等秘书赖格勒、罗马尼亚大使馆一等秘书孟天努、印度尼西亚大使馆二等秘书谭陶姆以及英国、荷兰、娜威政府谈判代表等也到车站迎接。
