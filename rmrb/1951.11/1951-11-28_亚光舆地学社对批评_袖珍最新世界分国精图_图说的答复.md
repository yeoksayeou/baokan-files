Title: Ya Guang Cartography Society's Response to Criticism of the "Pocket Latest World National Division Precise Map" Explanation

Author:
Date: 1951-11-28
Page: 2

Response to People's Daily Readers' Criticism and Suggestions:

    Ya Guang Cartography Society's Response to Criticism of the "Pocket Latest World National Division Precise Map" Explanation
    To the Editor: We are very grateful for Mr. Wang Chang's criticism of the explanation of the "Pocket Latest World National Division Precise Map" published by our society in the "Readers' Letters" column of your newspaper on November 10th, pointing out several errors. We have revised the explanation and attached an errata sheet to the remaining maps. We extend our sincere thanks to your newspaper. Ya Guang Cartography Society

### 亚光舆地学社对批评“袖珍最新世界分国精图”图说的答复

1951-11-28
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    亚光舆地学社对批评“袖珍最新世界分国精图”图说的答复
    编辑同志：十一月十日你报“读者来信”栏刊登王昌先生对我社出版的“袖珍最新世界分国精图”图说的批评，指出几点错误，我们对此非常感谢。我们已将该项说明加以修正，并在存图中附印勘误表。谨向你报致深切的谢忱。　亚光舆地学社
