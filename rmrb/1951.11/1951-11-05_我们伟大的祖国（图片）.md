Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-11-05

    Our Great Motherland
    This person, holding harvested grain and beaming with a smile, is Cui De, a labor model from Songjiang Province in Northeast China. The mutual aid team he led completed the autumn harvest on 65 hectares of land in thirteen days, starting from September 18th.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这一位抱着收割的谷子、满面笑容的人就是东北松江省劳动模范崔德。他所领导的互助组从九月十八日起的十三天内，在六十五垧土地上完成了秋收。
