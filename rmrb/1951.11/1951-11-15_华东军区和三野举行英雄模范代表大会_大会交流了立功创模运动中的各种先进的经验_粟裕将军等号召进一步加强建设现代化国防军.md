Title: East China Military Region and Third Field Army Hold Hero and Model Representatives Conference; Conference Exchanges Various Advanced Experiences in Merit and Model Creation Movement; General Su Yu and Others Call for Further Strengthening the Construction of a Modernized National Defense Army
Author:
Date: 1951-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The First Hero and Model Representatives Conference of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army began on November 1st in Nanjing and closed on the 8th. Three hundred and twenty-five hero and model representatives from the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army's army, air force, navy, military regions, special forces, logistics departments, and people's armed forces in East China attended the conference.

The conference was presided over by Su Yu, Deputy Commander of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, Chief of Staff Zhang Zhen, Deputy Chief of Staff Zhou Junming, Director of the Political Department Tang Liang, and Deputy Director Zhang Kai. Wang Zonghuai, Deputy Director of the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, was specially sent to the conference to offer congratulations. Guests attending the conference included Zhou Wenjiang and Xu Wenbin, combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who were invited to give reports in various parts of East China, as well as representatives from all walks of life in Shanghai, Nanjing, and other places.

The conference reported on the achievements of the merit and model creation movement in the East China armed forces in the year since the National Combat Heroes Representatives Conference last year. From the early days of the Liberation War to December of last year, more than 11,000 heroes and models and hundreds of thousands of meritorious personnel emerged in the East China armed forces. Since January of this year, more than 11,000 heroes and models and hundreds of thousands of meritorious personnel have emerged again. These heroes, models, and meritorious personnel have emerged in the past year in the execution of tasks such as combat, coastal defense, suppression of bandits, garrisoning cities, and building a regularized and modernized national defense army. The merit and model creation movement in all units of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army has been launched deeply and universally. In the past, various units only paid attention to launching the merit and model creation movement in companies and among soldiers, but now this movement has also been launched in leadership organs and among cadres at all levels; in the past, they only paid attention to carrying out the merit and model creation movement in combat, but now this movement has also been carried out in various tasks. The merit and model creation movement has been closely integrated with the military, political, and logistical work of the troops, becoming an important driving force for the troops to complete various tasks. This is a new development in the merit and model creation movement in the East China armed forces in the past year.

The conference exchanged various advanced experiences in the merit and model creation movement. Advanced figures emerging in combat, bandit suppression, military training, and special forces construction made typical reports to the conference. Bao Qichen, Minister of the Organization Department of the Political Department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, gave a summary report to the conference on the merit and model creation movement in the past year. The representatives discussed these reports, visited each other, and extensively exchanged experiences.

Su Yu, Deputy Commander of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, and Tang Liang, Director of the Political Department, both made reports to the conference, calling on the heroes and models to guard against arrogance and impetuosity, learn humbly, maintain close ties with the masses, and play the three major roles of leading, backbone, and bridge, to strive for the construction of a strong and modernized national defense army, for the defense of the motherland, for the defense of the East, and for world peace. The representatives discussed Commander-in-Chief Zhu's order issued on National Day this year and the above-mentioned reports, and unanimously stated: resolutely implement Commander-in-Chief Zhu's order, accelerate the construction of a regularized and modernized national defense army, prepare for the liberation of Taiwan, Penghu, and Jinmen, and strive to defend the motherland, defend the East, and defend world peace.

### 华东军区和三野举行英雄模范代表大会  大会交流了立功创模运动中的各种先进的经验  粟裕将军等号召进一步加强建设现代化国防军

1951-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    华东军区和三野举行英雄模范代表大会
    大会交流了立功创模运动中的各种先进的经验
    粟裕将军等号召进一步加强建设现代化国防军 【本报讯】据新华社讯：中国人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军第一届英雄模范代表大会，十一月一日在南京开始举行，已在八日闭幕。出席大会的有华东军区和第三野战军所属陆军、空军、海军部队、各军区、各特种兵部队、后方勤务部门和华东各地人民武装中的英雄模范代表三百二十五名。
    大会由华东军区和第三野战军副司令员粟裕、参谋长张震、副参谋长周骏鸣、政治部主任唐亮、副主任张凯主持。中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会特派总政治部青年部副部长王宗槐到会致贺。参加大会的来宾有应邀至华东各地作报告的中国人民志愿军战斗英雄周文江、徐文斌等，以及上海、南京等地各界代表。
    大会上报告了自去年全国战斗英雄代表会议以后一年间华东武装部队中立功创模运动的成绩。华东武装部队自解放战争初期至去年十二月止共涌现出英雄、模范一万一千多名，功臣数十万名。自今年一月份以来，又涌现出英雄、模范一万一千多名，功臣数十万名。这些英雄、模范和功臣是一年来在执行作战、保卫海防、清剿土匪、警备城市和建设正规化现代化国防军的任务中涌现出来的。华东军区和第三野战军各部的立功创模运动，已深入普遍地展开。各部过去只注意在连队里和战士中展开立功创模运动，现在在各级领导机关和干部中也展开了这一运动；过去只注意在战斗中开展立功创模运动，现在在各种工作中也开展了这一运动。立功创模运动已和部队的军事、政治、后勤等工作密切结合，成为部队完成各种工作任务的重要的推动力量。这是一年来华东武装部队中立功创模运动的新发展。
    大会上交流了立功创模运动中的各种先进的经验。在作战、剿匪、军事练兵和特种兵建设中涌现的先进人物向大会作了典型报告。华东军区和第三野战军政治部组织部部长鲍奇辰向大会作了一年来立功创模运动的总结报告。代表们根据这些报告作了讨论，并相互进行了访问，广泛地交流了经验。
    华东军区和第三野战军副司令员粟裕、政治部主任唐亮都向大会作了报告，号召英雄模范们戒骄戒躁，虚心学习，密切联系群众，发挥带头、骨干和桥梁的三大作用，为建设强大的现代化国防军、为保卫祖国保卫东方和世界和平而奋斗。代表们讨论了朱总司令在今年国庆节发布的命令和上述报告，一致表示：坚决执行朱总司令的命令，为加速建设正规化和现代化国防军，为准备解放台湾、澎湖、金门，为保卫祖国、保卫东方与世界和平而努力奋斗。
