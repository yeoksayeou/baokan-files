Title: Dalai Lama Sends Representatives to慰問(Wèiwèn - Express Regards to) Officers and Soldiers of the Entering Tibet Troops
Author:
Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 7th. The Dalai Lama dispatched the Khenpo Ngawang Lobsang on the morning of the 5th, carrying a large number of gifts, to express regards to all commanders and fighters of the entering Tibet troops. When delivering the gifts, Khenpo Ngawang Lobsang delivered a message of regards on behalf of the Dalai Lama. He said: "The Han and Tibetan nationalities have now achieved good peaceful relations. According to the seventeen-article agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, Chairman Mao's representative, General Zhang Jingwu, the commander of the advance detachment entering Tibet, Wang Qimei, and all commanders and fighters, as well as the commander of the entering Tibet troops, Zhang Guohua, and all commanders and fighters have arrived in Lhasa safely, which is a great undertaking to consolidate national defense and seek happiness and prosperity for the monks and lay people of Tibet, which is truly gratifying. I, the Dalai Lama, respectfully send 100 bags of rice and 10,000 taels of Tibetan silver for food expenses to all troops, hoping they will be distributed to all units." Chief of Staff Chen of the advance detachment accepted the gifts on behalf of General Zhang Guohua and expressed gratitude. On the same day, Zhang Guohua, commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army entering Tibet, and Tan Guansan, political commissar, on behalf of all commanders and fighters, wrote to the Dalai Lama to thank him for his慰劳(wèiláo -consolation). The letter said: "Since the signing of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet have successively arrived in Lhasa. We have overcome many difficulties with your help and support and the support of all the monks and lay people of Tibet. We believe that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government and under the leadership of the Dalai Lama, the Tibetan people will surely move towards increasingly happy and joyful days. On behalf of all the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet, we respectfully wish the Dalai Lama good health."

### 达赖喇嘛派员慰问入藏部队指战员

1951-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛派员慰问入藏部队指战员
    【新华社拉萨七日电】达赖喇嘛于五日上午派遣机巧堪布昂旺郎吉携带大批礼品，慰问入藏部队全体指挥员战斗员，机巧堪布昂旺郎吉于送礼时代表达赖喇嘛致慰问词。他说：“汉藏两大民族现已得到和平的良好关系，根据和平解放西藏办法的协议十七条，毛主席的代表——张经武将军、进藏先遣支队王其梅司令员暨全体指挥员战斗员以及进藏部队张国华司令员暨全体指挥员战斗员先后平安到达拉萨，是为巩固国防、为西藏僧俗人民谋幸福繁荣的伟大事业而来，实为欣喜。我达赖喇嘛谨向所有部队赍送大米一百包，菜金藏银一万两，希转发各部。”当由先遣支队陈参谋长代表张国华将军受礼，并表示谢意。当日，中国人民解放军入藏部队司令员张国华、政治委员谭冠三两将军代表全体指挥员战斗员，致书达赖喇嘛，感谢他的慰劳。信中说：“自从和平解放西藏办法的协议签订以后，人民解放军入藏部队陆续到达拉萨，承你及西藏全体僧俗人民的帮助与支援，克服不少困难。我们相信：西藏人民在毛主席和中央人民政府的英明领导下及达赖喇嘛的率领下，一定可以走向日益幸福与快乐的日子。我们代表人民解放军进藏部队全体指战员，敬祝达赖喇嘛身体健康。”
