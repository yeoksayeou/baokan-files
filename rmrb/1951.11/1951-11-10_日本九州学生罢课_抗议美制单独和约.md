Title: Japanese Kyushu Students Strike Against US-Imposed Separate Peace Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 3rd: News from Tokyo: Students on Kyushu Island in southern Japan have gone on strike to protest the separate peace treaty with Japan. On the 1st, more than 1,000 students from Kurume University went on strike. On October 31st, they held a meeting and resolved to oppose the rearmament of Japan and the granting of military bases to the United States.

Students at Kyushu University in Fukuoka also held a strike to protest the House of Representatives' approval of the separate peace treaty. When they held a mass rally on October 31st, they were brutally dispersed by a large number of armed police. Eighteen students, led by Osaka (transliteration), chairman of the student self-government association, were arrested. Several people were injured in the clashes with the police.

### 日本九州学生罢课  抗议美制单独和约

1951-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本九州学生罢课
    抗议美制单独和约
    【新华社三日讯】东京消息：日本南部九州岛的学生举行罢课，抗议单独对日和约。一日，久留米大学的学生一千多名，举行罢课。他们在十月三十一日曾举行大会，决议反对日本重新武装和把军事基地给予美国。
    福冈的九州大学学生也举行了罢课，抗议众院批准单独和约。他们在十月三十一日举行群众大会时，被大批武装警察横暴地驱散。以学生自治会主席大坂（译音）为首的十八个学生被捕。在和警察的冲突中，并有多人受伤。
