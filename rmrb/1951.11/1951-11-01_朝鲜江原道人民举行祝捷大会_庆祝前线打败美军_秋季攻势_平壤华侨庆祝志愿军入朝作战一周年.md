Title: People in Jiangyuan Province, Korea, Hold Victory Celebration Rally to Celebrate the Front Line's Defeat of the US Military's "Autumn Offensive"; Overseas Chinese in Pyongyang Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers Entering Korea to Fight
Author:
Date: 1951-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 31st -- The people of all circles in Jiangyuan Province, Korea, warmly celebrated the brilliant victory of the Korean-Chinese People's Army in defeating Van Fleet's "Autumn Offensive" and extended warm greetings to the Korean-Chinese People's forces fighting side by side on the front line in the province. At the provincial victory celebration rally, the heads of the provincial party and government and representatives from all walks of life unanimously praised a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers for their brilliant record of annihilating more than 17,000 enemies in just three days, and expressed deep gratitude to the commanders and fighters of the unit for their heroic struggle to defend Korean peace. Many counties (districts), townships (areas), and villages (towns) in the province also held mass rallies and symposiums to celebrate the victory. People from all walks of life presented gifts and condolences to the Volunteers and the People's Army. The people of Huaiyang County presented four oxen for consolation, as well as daily necessities such as socks, towels, and soap. The people of a certain village in the county also separately presented a fat pig as a gift. Model farmer Park Dal-sam presented two bags of patriotic rice (rice). Five elderly people from a certain village voluntarily organized themselves to cut reeds and rush to weave straw mats overnight to send to the Volunteers. The people of all circles in the province expressed their determination to more actively support the front and ensure that all autumn public grain would be paid in full by the end of October.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th -- The Pyongyang Overseas Chinese Federation held a celebration gathering here on the 26th to commemorate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. More than 800 overseas Chinese from the Pyongyang area attended the meeting. Representatives from the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, representatives from a certain unit of the Volunteers, as well as Hong Ji-hwang, Vice Chairman of the Pyongyang People's Committee, Xu Guangri, Vice Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang were also invited to attend.
    Ma Yusheng, Vice Chairman of the North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation, spoke at the meeting. He said: We, all overseas Chinese residing in Korea, celebrate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' expedition to Korea with ten thousand points of excitement and gratitude. We would like to pay the highest respect to the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong and the Communist Party of China, who led the people of the motherland to achieve the glorious victory in the just struggle to resist US aggression and aid Korea. At the same time, we would like to extend our sincere thanks and condolences to all the commanders and fighters of the ever-victorious and meritorious Chinese People's Volunteers. He called on overseas Chinese to strengthen unity and do their best to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army to defeat the American aggressors. Representatives of a certain unit of the Volunteers who attended the meeting, representatives of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and Hong Ji-hwang, Xu Guangri, and others also spoke successively.
    Then, the Pyongyang Overseas Chinese Federation, on behalf of all overseas Chinese in North Korea, presented flags and gifts to a certain unit of the Volunteers. The banner was embroidered with the words: "You are the iron and steel troops who eliminate the American bandits! You are the heroic fighters who defend peace!" At the end of the meeting, a congratulatory telegram was unanimously adopted to Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Kim Il-sung, and Commander Peng Dehuai and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 朝鲜江原道人民举行祝捷大会  庆祝前线打败美军“秋季攻势”  平壤华侨庆祝志愿军入朝作战一周年

1951-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜江原道人民举行祝捷大会
    庆祝前线打败美军“秋季攻势”
    平壤华侨庆祝志愿军入朝作战一周年
    【新华社朝鲜前线三十一日电】朝鲜江原道各界人民热烈庆祝朝中人民军队打败范佛里特“秋季攻势”的辉煌胜利，并向在该道前线并肩作战的朝中人民部队进行热烈的慰问。在道的祝捷大会上，该道党、政首长和各界代表一致赞扬志愿军某部仅在三天内歼敌一万七千余人的辉煌战绩，并对该部指战员为捍卫朝鲜和平而作的英勇斗争表示深切的感谢。该道许多郡（县）面（区）里（乡）也都分别举行了庆祝胜利的群众大会和座谈会。各界人民并纷纷向志愿军和人民军献礼与慰问。淮阳郡人民慰劳食牛四头，与袜子、毛巾、肥皂等日用品。该郡某里人民并单独慰劳肥猪一口。模范农民朴达三慰劳爱国米（大米）两草袋。某里的五个老人自动组织起来割苇子，漏夜赶编成草席送给志愿军。该道各界人民纷纷表示要更积极地支援前线，保证在十月底前全部缴清秋季公粮。
    【新华社平壤三十日电】平壤市华侨联合会为纪念中国人民志愿军入朝作战一周年，于二十六日在此间举行庆祝集会。参加大会的有平壤地区的华侨八百多人。我国驻朝鲜大使馆代表、志愿军某部代表、以及平壤市人民委员会副委员长洪基璜、朝鲜劳动党平壤市党部副委员长许光日、平壤市各界代表也应邀到会。
    北朝鲜华侨联合总会副委员长马玉声在大会上讲话。他说：我们住居在朝鲜的全体华侨，以万分兴奋和感激的心情来庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年。我们要向领导祖国人民取得抗美援朝正义斗争的光辉胜利的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和中国共产党致以最崇高的敬礼。同时，我们要向百战百胜劳苦功高的中国人民志愿军全体指挥员和战斗员致以亲切的感谢和慰问。他号召华侨加紧团结，尽力支援中国人民志愿军与朝鲜人民军，打败美国侵略者。出席大会的志愿军某部代表、我国驻朝鲜大使馆代表和洪基璜、许光日等人，均相继讲了话。
    接着，平壤市华侨联合会代表北朝鲜全体华侨向志愿军某部献旗献礼。锦旗上绣着：“你们是消灭美寇的钢铁队伍！你们是保卫和平的英勇斗士！”的字样。大会最后一致通过了给毛泽东主席、金日成首相以及中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员暨全体指战员的致敬电。
