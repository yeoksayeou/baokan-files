Title: Tianjin People from All Walks of Life Complete Plan to Donate 100 Airplanes

Author:
Date: 1951-11-19
Page: 1

　　Tianjin People from All Walks of Life
　　Complete Plan to Donate 100 Airplanes
　　Suiyuan and Hunan also Complete Donation Plans
    【Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 18th】 The people of Tianjin from all walks of life have completed the plan to donate 100 fighter planes ahead of schedule on November 17th, with a total donation of over 152.6 billion yuan.
    【Xinhua News Agency, GuiSui, 16th】The Mongolian, Han, and Hui people of Suiyuan Province have overfulfilled the original plan of increasing production and donating 12 fighter planes in half a year ahead of schedule. As of November 12th, the people of all ethnic groups in the province had paid over 19.7 billion yuan in donations, exceeding the original plan by more than 9.4 percent.
    Suiyuan Province is the fourteenth province in the country to complete the half-year donation plan ahead of schedule and exceeding the target.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 17th】As of November 15th, the people of Hunan Province had paid over 163.95 billion yuan in donations, exceeding the original half-year plan to donate 100 fighter planes by more than 9 percent.

### 天津市各界人民  完成献机百架计划

1951-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    天津市各界人民
    完成献机百架计划
    绥远湖南捐献计划相继完成
    【新华社天津十八日电】天津市各界人民已在十一月十七日提前完成捐献一百架战斗机的计划，共缴纳献款一千五百二十六亿多元。
    【新华社归绥十六日电】绥远省蒙、汉、回各族人民，已提前超额完成半年增产捐献战斗机十二架的原订计划。截至十一月十二日止，全省各族人民已缴纳献款一百九十七亿余元，超过原订计划百分之九点四强。
    按绥远省是全国提前超额完成半年捐献计划的第十四个省份。
    【新华社长沙十七日电】截至十一月十五日止，湖南省人民已缴纳献款一千六百三十九亿五千余万元，超过原订半年捐献战斗机一百架计划百分之九强。
