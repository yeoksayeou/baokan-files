Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Reply: Warmly Welcome All Officers and Soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers to Join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1951-11-12
Page: 1

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Reply
    Warmly Welcome All Officers and Soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers to Join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, on November 10th, sent a reply telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteers, warmly welcoming all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as group members. The telegram reads as follows:
    To all Commanders and Fighters of the glorious Chinese People's Volunteers:
    Your telegram of November 7th has been received. We regard your collective joining of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as the greatest honor. This is because the Chinese People's Volunteers are a team composed of the most outstanding sons and daughters of our motherland, standing at the forefront of defending peace in the Far East and the world. Your joining the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will greatly enhance the Chinese people's friendly cooperation with our great ally, the Soviet Union, and promote the confidence of the Chinese and Korean people in achieving final victory in their just struggle.
    We wish you even greater success in the struggle against American imperialist aggression and in aiding the Korean people to defend their country!
                                            Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters
                                        November 10, 1951

### 中苏友好协会总会复电  热烈欢迎志愿军全体指战员加入中苏友协

1951-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会总会复电
    热烈欢迎志愿军全体指战员加入中苏友协
    【新华社十一日讯】中苏友好协会总会，十一月十日复电中国人民志愿军，热烈欢迎中国人民志愿军全体指挥员战斗员加入中苏友好协会为团体会员，电文如下：光荣的中国人民志愿军全体指挥员战斗员同志：
    你们十一月七日的来电已经收到。我们以你们的集体加入中苏友好协会作为最大的光荣。因为中国人民志愿军是由我们祖国最优秀的儿女所组成的队伍，是站在保卫远东与世界和平的最前线。你们的加入中苏友好协会，将极大地增强中国人民对伟大盟邦苏联的友好合作，并促进中朝人民的正义斗争到达最后胜利的信心。
    祝你们在反抗美帝国主义侵略与援助朝鲜人民保卫祖国的斗争中获得更大的成功！
                                            中苏友好协会总会　
                                        一九五一年十一月十日
