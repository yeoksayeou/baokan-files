　　Economic Briefs
    ▲The Nanjing Machinery Factory has adopted high-speed cutting methods, increasing production efficiency by six to thirteen times, which has played a significant role in completing the factory's production tasks.
    ▲In the East China region, 270 small enterprise units have signed collective labor insurance contracts. Approximately 40,000 employees have begun to enjoy the labor insurance benefits stipulated in the contracts. Among them, Shanghai accounts for 150 units and 10,000 employees.
    ▲In Kaifeng and Zhengzhou, Henan Province, many transport workers have signed up to participate in the Huai River harnessing project, with over 4,000 people being approved. The transport workers from Kaifeng departed for the Baisha Reservoir construction site in the upper reaches of the Huai River on October 18th.

### 经济简讯

1951-11-12
第2版()
专栏：经济简讯

　　经济简讯
    ▲南京机器厂推行了高速切削法后，使生产效率提高六倍到十三倍，对完成全厂生产任务起了很大的作用。
    ▲华东区已有二百七十家小型企业单位签订了劳动保险集体合同。约有四万多职工开始享受合同中规定的劳保待遇。其中上海占一百五十家，职工一万人。
    ▲河南开封、郑州两市搬运工人纷纷报名参加治淮工作，被批准的有四千多人。开封的搬运工人已于十月十八日出发到淮河上流白沙水库工地。
