Title: Heavy Snow in Guanzhong Area, Shaanxi Province
Author:
Date: 1951-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 23rd. The Guanzhong area of Shaanxi Province experienced its first heavy snow of the winter from the morning of the 21st to the morning of the 22nd. The snowfall measured 12.2 millimeters, with snow accumulation generally around three to four inches thick. Farmers say this heavy snow is very beneficial to the crops, allowing wheat to take root and potentially freezing some of the dormant pests.

### 陕西省关中地区大雪

1951-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    陕西省关中地区大雪
    【新华社西安二十三日电】陕西省关中地区从二十一日上午到二十二日上午下了今冬的第一场大雪。降雪量十二点二公厘，积雪一般约三、四寸厚。农民说：这场大雪对庄稼很好，麦子可以扎根，潜伏的害虫可以冻死一些。
