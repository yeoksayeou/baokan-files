Author:
Date: 1951-11-22

    Our Great Motherland
    In certain areas of Yili, Xinjiang, wild rubber grass grows everywhere. It is an important industrial raw material. The Yili Farm Rubber Grass Cultivation Station is collecting seeds in large quantities and studying planting methods, preparing to promote it to various regions. This is a scene of the farm's staff working in the fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆伊犁某些地区遍地都是野生的橡胶草。它是重要的工业原料。伊犁农场橡胶草培植站正在大量收集种籽和研究种植方法，准备向各地推广。这是该农场工作人员在田野工作的情形。
