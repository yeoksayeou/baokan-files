Title: Vietnamese People's Delegation to China Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1951-11-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th. The fifteen-member Vietnamese People's Delegation to China, headed by Ton Duc Thang, arrived in Guangzhou by train this morning from Hankou. Xiao Guichang, Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Rao Zhangfeng, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Branch, representatives of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Guangzhou City, Guangdong People's Organizations, and people from all walks of life went to the station to welcome them.

### 越南人民访华代表团抵广州

1951-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南人民访华代表团抵广州
    【新华社广州十六日电】越南人民访华代表团团长孙德胜等一行十五人于今晨由汉口乘车抵达广州。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会广州市分会主席萧桂昌、广东省分会秘书长饶彰风、广东省人民政府和广州市、广东省各人民团体、各界人士都派代表前往车站欢迎。
