Congratulations on the 34th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union

    Telegram from the China New Democratic Youth League
    To Dear Comrade Mikhailov and the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union

    On the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, on behalf of the Chinese youth, I extend warm greetings to you and the heroic Soviet youth and their vanguard—the Leninist Young Communist League—and sincerely thank you for your kind care and help to the Chinese youth. The Soviet youth of the Stalin era is the most glorious example for the Chinese youth. The great achievements you have made in carrying out the great communist construction projects and defending world peace have given unlimited encouragement to the Chinese youth who stand at the forefront of the construction of the motherland and the movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea. I wish the Soviet Union, the bastion of world peace, will become stronger and more prosperous under the wise leadership of Stalin, and I wish the unbreakable friendship between the youth of China and the Soviet Union will last forever!

    Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    November 6, 1951

    Telegram from the China Young Pioneers

    Dear Soviet Young Pioneer members:
    On the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, let us warmly congratulate you. The October Revolution brought happiness to you and hope and a path to victory to the people of the world. Under the shining light of the victory of the October Revolution and under the leadership of our dear Chairman Mao, the Chinese people have achieved great victories.
    We warmly congratulate your country on achieving more brilliant achievements in communist construction, and congratulate you on achieving greater success in your studies.

    Long live Comrade Stalin, the leader of progressive mankind in the world!
    Long live the great October Socialist Revolution!

    China Young Pioneers

    Telegram from the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization
    First National Work Conference
    To Chairman Obalin of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge:

    In the Soviet Union, which is moving from socialism to communism, your association's work in popularizing scientific knowledge has played a significant role; our Chinese scientific workers are learning from you. Today is the 34th anniversary of the great October Revolution Day. As our All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization holds a work conference in Beijing, we extend our heartfelt congratulations and highest respect to you.

    All representatives of the First National Work Conference of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization

### 祝贺苏联十月社会主义革命三十四周年

1951-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    祝贺苏联十月社会主义革命三十四周年
    中国新民主主义
    青年团贺电亲爱的米哈依洛夫同志并苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会
    值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年纪念日，谨代表中国青年向您和英勇的苏联青年及其先锋队——列宁共产主义青年团致以热烈的敬礼，并衷心地感谢你们对中国青年亲切的关怀和帮助。斯大林时代的苏联青年是中国青年最光辉的榜样，你们在进行伟大的共产主义建设工程与保卫世界和平的斗争中所获得的巨大成就，给予站在建设祖国与抗美援朝运动前列的中国青年以无限的鼓舞。谨祝世界和平堡垒苏联在斯大林英明领导下日益强大繁荣，并祝中苏两国青年之间牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬
    一九五一年十一月六日
    中国少年儿童队贺电亲爱的苏联少年先锋队队员们：
    在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年的时候，让我们热烈地祝贺你们。十月革命为你们带来了幸福，也给全世界人民带来了希望和指向胜利的道路。在十月革命胜利光辉的照耀下，在我们亲爱的毛主席领导下，中国人民取得了伟大的胜利。
    我们热烈地祝贺你们祖国在共产主义建设上获得更辉煌的成就，祝贺你们在学习上获得更大的成绩。
    世界进步人类的领袖——敬爱的斯大林同志万岁！
    伟大的十月社会主义革命万岁！
    中国少年儿童队
    中华全国科学技术普及协会
    第一次全国工作会议贺电全苏政治与科学知识普及协会奥巴林主席：
    在由社会主义向共产主义迈进的苏联，贵会的科学知识普及工作起了很大的作用；我们中国的科学工作者正向你们学习。今天是伟大的十月革命节三十四周年的纪念日，适值我们中华全国科学技术普及协会在北京举行工作会议，谨向你们致衷心的祝贺和崇高的敬礼。
    中华全国科学技术普及协会
    第一次全国工作会议全体代表
