Title: Egyptian Chambers of Commerce Hold Meeting, Resolve to Boycott British Goods and Sever Egyptian-British Trade
Author:
Date: 1951-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 26th (TASS): According to newspaper reports: A meeting of the heads of Egyptian chambers of commerce discussed the issue of boycotting British goods. The Deputy Minister of Industry and Commerce, Abdullah Abbas, also attended the meeting. The meeting passed a resolution advising members of the chambers of commerce not to import British goods, indicating a desire to sever trade between Egypt and Britain. The meeting also suggested establishing a special department in every chamber of commerce in Egypt to be responsible for inspecting the implementation of the boycott of British goods.

### 埃及各商会会长举行会议  决议抵制英货断绝埃英贸易

1951-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及各商会会长举行会议
    决议抵制英货断绝埃英贸易
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社开罗二十六日讯：据报纸消息：埃及各商会会长所举行的会议讨论了抵制英国货的问题。副工商大臣阿布杜拉·阿巴斯也曾出席会议。会议通过决议，劝告各商会的会员们不要输入英国商品，这表示要断绝埃英之间的贸易。会议并建议在埃及的每一个商会中设立一个特别部门，负责检查抵制英货的执行情况。
