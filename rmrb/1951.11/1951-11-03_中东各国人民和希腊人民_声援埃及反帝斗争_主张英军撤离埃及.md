Title: People of Middle Eastern Countries and Greek People Support Egyptian Anti-Imperialist Struggle and Advocate the Withdrawal of British Troops from Egypt
Author:
Date: 1951-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November: People from countries in the Middle East such as Iraq, Iran, Pakistan, and Greece are firmly supporting the Egyptian people's struggle against imperialism for independence. On October 25th, the Iraqi people held demonstrations in Basra to oppose British imperialists and demand that Iraq support the Egyptian people's struggle against British occupation. Police attempted to forcibly disperse the demonstrators. Some demonstrators were injured, and some were arrested.

In Tehran, the capital of Iran, thousands of students and workers held demonstrations on October 30th. Demonstrators shouted, "Down with British and American imperialists!" "The Iranian people sympathize with and support the Egyptian people!" "Support the national liberation movements of all oppressed people in the world who are fighting for freedom, independence, and the consolidation of world peace!" Tehran police interfered with the students' demonstrations, stabbing and injuring twenty students.

The Karachi Peace Committee in Pakistan passed a resolution on the 24th, denouncing "British armed interference in Egypt." The committee demanded that all British troops withdraw from Egypt. The resolution emphasized that "the presence of British troops on Egyptian territory against the will of the Egyptian people is a threat to the cause of peace."

Greek expatriates living in Egypt also expressed their support for the Egyptian people's anti-imperialist struggle on the 24th. The Greek expatriate associations in Cairo and Alexandria jointly sent a telegram to Egyptian Prime Minister Nahas, expressing "deep sympathy for Egypt's just actions and interests." The Greek Freelancers Association and other groups have also sent telegrams to the Egyptian Prime Minister and the Speaker of the Egyptian Parliament, respectively, in support of the Egyptian people's anti-imperialist struggle.

### 中东各国人民和希腊人民  声援埃及反帝斗争  主张英军撤离埃及

1951-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中东各国人民和希腊人民
    声援埃及反帝斗争
    主张英军撤离埃及
    【新华社二日讯】伊拉克、伊朗、巴基斯坦和希腊等国人民坚决支持埃及人民反对帝国主义争取独立的斗争。伊拉克人民十月二十五日在巴士拉举行示威游行，反对英帝国主义者，并要求伊拉克支持埃及人民反对英国占领的斗争。警察企图用武力驱散示威群众。有些示威者受伤，有些被捕。
    在伊朗首都德黑兰，数千学生和工人在十月三十日举行示威游行。示威者高呼“打倒英、美帝国主义者！”“伊朗人民同情和支持埃及人民！”“支持世界上为争取自由独立与巩固世界和平而斗争的一切被压迫人民的民族解放运动！”德黑兰的警察干涉学生们的示威游行，刺伤与打伤学生二十人。
    巴基斯坦喀喇蚩和平委员会二十四日通过决议，痛斥“英国武装干涉埃及”。委员会要求所有的英国军队撤离埃及。决议强调说：“英军违反埃及人民的意志驻留在埃及领土上，是对和平事业的威胁。”
    住在埃及的希腊侨民也于二十四日对埃及人民的反帝斗争表示声援。开罗和亚历山大港的希腊侨民联谊会联合致电埃及首相纳哈斯，“对埃及的正义行动和利益表示深切的同情”。希腊自由职业者协会和其他团体也曾分别致电埃及首相和埃及议会议长，支持埃及人民的反帝斗争。
