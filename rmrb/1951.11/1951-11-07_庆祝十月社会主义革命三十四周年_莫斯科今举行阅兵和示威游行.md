Title: Celebrating the 34th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution: Moscow to Hold Military Parade and Demonstration Today
Author:
Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 4

    Celebrating the 34th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
    Moscow to Hold Military Parade and Demonstration Today
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th] According to TASS, Moscow: The garrison of the Soviet capital, Moscow, will hold a military parade in Red Square at 10:00 AM on November 7th, the 34th anniversary of the October Revolution. The parade will be commanded by Colonel General Artyemyev, commander of the Moscow Garrison and commander of the Moscow Military District, and reviewed by Marshal of the Soviet Union Malinovsky. After the parade, the people of Moscow will begin a festive demonstration at 11:00 AM to celebrate the holiday.

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十四周年  莫斯科今举行阅兵和示威游行

1951-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十四周年
    莫斯科今举行阅兵和示威游行
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联首都莫斯科的卫戍部队，将在十月革命三十四周年纪念日——十一月七日上午十时，在红场举行阅兵，由莫斯科卫戍司令兼莫斯科军区部队司令官阿尔杰米也夫上将任指挥官，由苏联元帅马林诺夫斯基检阅部队。阅兵结束以后，莫斯科人民将在上午十一时开始举行庆祝节日的示威游行。
