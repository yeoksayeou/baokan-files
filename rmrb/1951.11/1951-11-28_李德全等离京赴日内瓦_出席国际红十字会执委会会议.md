Title: Li Dequan and Others Leave Beijing for Geneva to Attend the Executive Committee Meeting of the International Red Cross

Author:

Date: 1951-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th—The third Executive Committee of the 21st Board of Governors of the League of Red Cross Societies is scheduled to be held in Geneva, Switzerland, from December 6th to 8th. Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, Deputy Secretary-General Lin Shixiao, and Ji Feng, Director of the Liaison Group, and their party, left Beijing by plane on November 27th to attend the meeting.

### 李德全等离京赴日内瓦  出席国际红十字会执委会会议

1951-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全等离京赴日内瓦
    出席国际红十字会执委会会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】国际红十字会协会第二十一届理事会第三次执行委员会，定在十二月六日到八日在瑞士日内瓦开会。中国红十字会总会会长李德全、副秘书长林士笑和联络组主任纪锋等一行，已在本月二十七日乘飞机离北京赴会。
