Title: Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Sends a Telegram to Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 1

    To Comrade Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Moscow:

	On the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, I would like to extend my warmest and most sublime congratulations to you and, through you, to the Soviet people, and wish the Soviet Union, under the leadership of the great Comrade Stalin, ever-increasing development towards communist construction, the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples ever-strengthening, and the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union growing ever stronger.

	Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
	November 5, 1951

### 中苏友协总会会长刘少奇致苏对外文协主席吉尼索夫电

1951-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会会长刘少奇致苏对外文协主席吉尼索夫电莫斯科苏联对外文化协会吉尼索夫主席：
    兹值伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年纪念日，谨向您并经过您向苏联人民致最热烈和崇高的祝贺，并祝苏联在伟大的斯大林同志的领导之下迈向共产主义的建设日益发展，中苏两国人民的伟大友谊日益巩固，以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营愈臻强大。
    中苏友好协会总会会长刘少奇
    一九五一年十一月五日
