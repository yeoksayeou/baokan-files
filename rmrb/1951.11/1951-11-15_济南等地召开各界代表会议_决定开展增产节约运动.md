Title: Jinan and other places convened representative conferences of all sectors, deciding to launch a production increase and economy campaign
Author:
Date: 1951-11-15
Page: 1

　　Jinan and other places convened representative conferences of all sectors
    Deciding to launch a production increase and economy campaign
    Jinan People's Representatives Conference of All Circles calls for
    Rapidly launch a patriotic production increase and economy campaign
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Jinan Municipal People's Representatives Conference of All Circles, acting on behalf of the People's Congress, held its Third First Session from October 25th to 30th. This conference re-elected the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and members of the Municipal People's Government Committee, and elected the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Consultative Committee of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles for this term. The conference responded to Chairman Mao's great call at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and unanimously resolved to continue strengthening the Resist America, Aid Korea work, increase production, practice strict economy, and vigorously support the Chinese People's Volunteers, as the central task of the city's people at present. The conference called on the city's people to rapidly launch a large-scale patriotic production increase and economy campaign, and strive to complete the payment plan for donating weapons ahead of schedule and in excess of the quota.
    Changsha City held a People's Representatives Conference of All Circles
    Resolved to mobilize manpower and resources to support the Volunteers
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Second Second Session of the Changsha Municipal People's Representatives Conference of All Circles was held from October 28th to 30th. One of the central topics of the conference was how to implement the resolution of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on the work of resisting America and aiding Korea. The conference decided to continue to launch a movement to increase production and practice strict economy among people from all walks of life in the city, widely promote patriotic pacts, and mobilize the city's manpower, resources, and financial resources to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army. Regarding the work of giving preferential treatment to the families of revolutionary martyrs and the families of revolutionary servicemen, the conference decided to raise 3 billion yuan in production funds for the families of revolutionary martyrs and the families of revolutionary servicemen before the end of the year to help the families of martyrs and servicemen engage in production. Regarding the donation of weapons, the conference called on the city's people to complete the plan to donate 21 fighter planes within half a year by mid-November. In addition, the conference passed a resolution on implementing the Marriage Law, reviewed the implementation of the resolutions of the previous conference, and conducted criticism and self-criticism on municipal construction work.
    Qingdao People's Representatives of All Circles unanimously proposed to guarantee
    Unite the city's people to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Fourth First Session of the Qingdao Municipal People's Representatives Conference of All Circles was held from the 28th of last month to the 4th of this month. The conference unanimously resolved to support and implement the "Resolution on the Work of Resisting America and Aiding Korea" adopted by the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. All representatives unanimously guaranteed to call on the city's people to unite and strive to do a good job in the seven tasks proposed in the resolution and strive for a greater victory in the Resist America, Aid Korea movement. This conference re-elected the Municipal People's Government Committee and the Consultative Committee of the Municipal People's Representatives Conference of All Circles (the Qingdao Municipal People's Representatives Conference of All Circles has been acting on behalf of the People's Congress since the Third Session).
    After group discussions, the participating representatives unanimously stated that they would unite the city's people to continue to work hard to strengthen the strength of resisting America and aiding Korea with practical actions and strive for greater victories. Worker representatives said: The current work of clearing assets and verifying funds is an important work for implementing production economy. We must mobilize workers throughout the city to assist the administration in completing this task well, so that national assets are not stockpiled and wasted. We ourselves must also be careful and calculating in production to create more wealth for the country. Representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors also stated that they will continue to strengthen patriotic education, supervise and encourage each other, and widely launch a production increase and economy campaign in the future.

### 济南等地召开各界代表会议  决定开展增产节约运动

1951-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　济南等地召开各界代表会议
    决定开展增产节约运动
    济南各界人民代表会议号召
    迅速开展爱国增产节约运动 【本报讯】据新华社讯：代行人民代表大会职权的济南市各界人民代表会议，在十月二十五日到三十日举行了第三届第一次会议。这次会议改选了市长、副市长和市人民政府委员会委员，选出了本届各界人民代表会议协商委员会的主席、副主席和委员。会议响应了毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议上的伟大号召，一致决议以继续加强抗美援朝工作，增加生产，厉行节约，大力支持中国人民志愿军，作为全市人民当前的中心任务。会议号召全市人民迅速开展大规模的爱国增产节约运动，争取提早超额完成捐献武器的缴款计划。
    长沙市举行各界人民代表会议
    决议动员人力物力支援志愿军
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：长沙市第二届第二次各界人民代表会议在十月二十八日至三十日举行。会议以如何贯彻执行中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议关于抗美援朝工作的决议为中心议题之一。会议决定在全市各界人民中继续开展增加生产和厉行节约的运动，广泛推行爱国公约，动员全市人力、物力和财力，支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。关于优待革命烈士家属和革命军人家属的工作，会议决定在年底以前募集革命烈士家属、革命军人家属的生产基金三十亿元，以扶助烈属、军属进行生产。关于捐献武器方面，会议号召全市人民在十一月中旬完成半年内捐献战斗机二十一架的计划。此外，会议通过了贯彻执行婚姻法的决议，检查了上次会议决议案的执行情况，并对市政建设工作进行了批评和自我批评。
    青岛各界人民代表一致提出保证
    团结全市人民加强抗美援朝工作
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：青岛市第四届第一次各界人民代表会议在上月二十八日到本月四日举行。会议一致决议拥护和执行中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议通过的“关于抗美援朝工作的决议”。全体代表一致保证要号召全市人民团结一致为做好该决议中所提出的七项工作、争取抗美援朝运动的更大胜利而奋斗。这次会议改选了市人民政府委员会和市各界人民代表会议协商委员会（青岛市各界人民代表会议已从第三届会议开始代行人民代表大会职权）。
    与会代表在进行小组讨论后，一致表示要团结全市人民继续努力以实际行动加强抗美援朝力量，争取更大的胜利。工人代表说：目前正在进行的清理资财、核定资金工作是实行生产节约的重要工作，我们要发动全市工人协助行政很好地完成这个任务，使国家资财不致积压和浪费。我们自己在生产中也要做到精打细算为国家创造更多的财富。工商界代表也表示今后要继续加强爱国主义教育，互相监督策励，广泛开展增产节约运动。
