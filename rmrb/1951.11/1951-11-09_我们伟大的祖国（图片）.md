    Our Great Motherland
    The apple production in Northeast China is constantly increasing. Last year's production was 156 percent of that in 1949. This is a scene of Northeast labor model Liu Changzeng leading the cooperative staff in selecting a large batch of apples purchased.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北的苹果生产量，正在不断地提高。去年的生产量为一九四九年的百分之一百五十六。这是东北劳动模范刘长增领导着合作社的工作人员，在挑选大批收购来的苹果的情形。
