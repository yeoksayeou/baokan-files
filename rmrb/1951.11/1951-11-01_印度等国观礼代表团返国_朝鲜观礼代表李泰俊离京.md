Title: Indian and Other Countries' Observation Delegations Return Home; Korean Observation Delegate Li Taijun Departs Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 31st: The Indian Goodwill Delegation and the Burmese and Indonesian People's Observation Delegations, who were invited to attend our National Day celebrations, left Guangzhou by train on October 30th to return to their countries via Hong Kong.  Those who went to the station to see them off included Rao Zhangfeng, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Zhang Luchun, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch, and representatives from workers', women's, youth, and student organizations.

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: Li Taijun, a member of the Korean People's Observation Delegation who was invited to attend our National Day celebrations, left Beijing this evening to return to his country. Those who went to the station to see him off included: Ding Cong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Sha Kefu, Secretary of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Yan Baohang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and artist Wang Chaowen. Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan also went to the station to see him off.

### 印度等国观礼代表团返国  朝鲜观礼代表李泰俊离京

1951-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度等国观礼代表团返国
    朝鲜观礼代表李泰俊离京
    【新华社广州三十一日电】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的印度亲善访华团和缅甸、印尼两国人民观礼代表团于十月三十日乘车离开广州，经香港返国。赴车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会广东分会秘书长饶彰风、广州市分会副主席张醁村以及工人、妇女、青年、学生团体的代表。
    【新华社三十一日讯】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的朝鲜人民观礼代表团团员李泰俊在今晚离北京返国。到车站欢送的有：中华全国民主青年联合总会副秘书长丁聪，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会秘书长沙可夫，中苏友好协会总会副总干事阎宝航和美术家王朝闻等人。朝鲜大使李周渊也到车站欢送。
