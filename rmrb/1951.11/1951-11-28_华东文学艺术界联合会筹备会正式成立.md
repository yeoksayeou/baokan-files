Title: Preparatory Committee of the East China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1951-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    The preparatory committee of the East China Federation of Literary and Art Circles was officially established on November 25th. At the inaugural meeting, seventy-two members of the preparatory committee were elected. From these, Yu Ling, Ba Jin, Fang Guangtao, Wang Tongzhao, Wu Qiang, Li Junmin, Zhou Xinfang, Hu Feng, Xia Yan, Yuan Xuefen, Chen Baichen, Chen Xinren, Chen Wangdao, He Luting, Jin Yi, and Liu Kaiqu, among others, twenty-five people in total, were elected as standing committee members. The standing committee then mutually elected Xia Yan as director, Ba Jin and Huang Yuan as deputy directors, and Yu Jiyu as secretary-general. Shu Tong, director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the East China Military and Administrative Committee, delivered a report at the meeting. He said: East China's literary and art workers should actively respond to Chairman Mao's call to carry out a self-ideological remolding study movement, systematically study Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, especially Chairman Mao's literary and art thought; develop literary and art criticism, and overcome the serious liberalism that exists in East China's literary and art circles.

### 华东文学艺术界联合会筹备会正式成立

1951-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    华东文学艺术界联合会筹备会正式成立
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：华东文学艺术界联合会筹备会在十一月二十五日正式成立。在成立大会上，选出了筹备委员会委员七十二人，并从中推选于伶、巴金、方光焘、王统照、吴强、李俊民、周信芳、胡风、夏衍、袁雪芬、陈白尘、陈辛人、陈望道、贺绿汀、靳以、刘开渠等二十五人为常务委员，常务委员会又互推夏衍为主任，巴金、黄源为副主任，于寄愚为秘书长。华东军政委员会文化教育委员会主任舒同在会上作了报告。他说：华东文艺工作者应当积极响应毛主席号召，开展自我思想改造的学习运动，有系统地学习马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想，特别是学习毛主席的文艺思想；发展文艺批评，克服华东文艺界中存在着的严重的自由主义习气。
