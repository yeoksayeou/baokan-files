Title: Soviet Peace Committee and All-Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Host Representatives of Chinese and Vietnamese Peace Organizations
Author:
Date: 1951-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st: TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Peace Committee and the Soviet All-Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a reception on the 20th to host the representatives of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression and the representatives of the Vietnam Committee for World Peace, who are currently visiting Moscow.

    The attendees at the reception included the representatives of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, headed by Guo Moruo, and the representatives of the Vietnam Committee for World Peace, headed by Le Dinh Tham.

    On the Soviet side, the attendees included prominent scientists, artists, writers, actors, artists, directors, Stakhanovite workers from Moscow industrial enterprises, as well as officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and personnel from the Soviet All-Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

    Zhang Wentian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also attended the reception.

    Music was played for the guests at the reception.

    The reception ended in a warm atmosphere.

### 苏联拥护和平委员会和对外文协  招待中越两国和平团体代表

1951-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联拥护和平委员会和对外文协
    招待中越两国和平团体代表 【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联拥护和平委员会和苏联对外文化协会在二十日举行招待会，招待现在莫斯科访问的中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会的代表和越南保卫世界和平委员会的代表。
    出席招待会的有以郭沫若为首的中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会代表和以黎廷探为首的越南保卫世界和平委员会代表。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有著名的科学家和艺术工作者、作家、演员、艺术家、导演和莫斯科工业企业的斯塔哈诺夫工作者，以及苏联外交部的官员和苏联对外文化协会的人员。
    中华人民共和国驻苏大使张闻天和大使馆人员也出席了这次招待会。
    招待会上曾为来宾们演奏了音乐。
    招待会在热烈的气氛中结束。
