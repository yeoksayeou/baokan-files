Title: National Hygiene Work Team Departs for Work in the Xikang Tibetan Area
Author:
Date: 1951-11-30
Page: 1

    National Hygiene Work Team
    Departs for Work in the Xikang Tibetan Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] The National Hygiene Work Team, organized by the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, departed from Beijing on the 29th for the Xikang Tibetan area. The team is based on the Seventh Detachment of the Central Anti-Epidemic General Team, which had worked in the Tibetan areas of Qinghai and Gansu and the Inner Mongolia region. It is augmented by seven members of the Central Maternal and Child Health Team, as well as doctors and nurses from Peking University Medical College and the Peking Union Medical College, totaling 57 people. The team also plans to add 26 ethnic minority cadres locally, who will study and work with the team, so that they can continue working locally after the team leaves. The team is carrying medical equipment worth over 830 million yuan. The team's tasks are: 1. To train hygiene cadres from fraternal ethnic groups on the spot; 2. To prevent and treat venereal diseases; 3. To carry out maternal and child health work; 4. To investigate the health status of the Xikang Tibetans and conduct health education. Before departure, the team underwent a short period of study and was briefed on the situation in Xikang by relevant personnel. When the team departed, Vice Minister of Health He Cheng and others went to the station to see them off.

### 民族卫生工作大队  出发西康藏族区工作

1951-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    民族卫生工作大队
    出发西康藏族区工作
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府卫生部组织的民族卫生工作大队，在二十九日由京出发前往西康藏族地区。大队以曾在青海、甘肃藏族地区和内蒙地区工作的中央防疫总队第七大队为基础，增加中央妇幼卫生大队队员七人和北京大学医学院、中国协和医学院的医师护士等共五十七人组成。该队还拟在当地增加民族干部二十六人，他们一面随队学习，一面工作，以便大队离开后能够继续在当地进行工作。该队携带了约值八亿三千多万元的医药装备。大队工作任务是：一、就地训练兄弟民族卫生干部；二、防治性病；三、开展妇幼卫生工作；四、调查西康藏族卫生状况和进行卫生宣传教育。该队在出发前曾进行了短时期的学习，并请有关人员介绍了西康情况。该队出发时，卫生部副部长贺诚等曾到车站欢送。
