Title: Leading Organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Convenes Meeting; Du Ping Conveys Various Reports and Resolutions of the Third Session of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-11-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, November 24th: The leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army (CPVA), in order to implement the spirit of the resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) throughout the entire army, convened a meeting on the 22nd. Du Ping, representative of the CPVA attending the CPPCC and Deputy Director of the Political Department of the CPVA, conveyed the various reports and resolutions of the Third Session of the National Committee of the CPPCC. Representatives from various units attended the meeting.
    In his report, Du Ping pointed out that all commanders and fighters must earnestly study Chairman Mao's opening speech, Premier Zhou's political report, and other reports. They must resolutely implement the resolutions of this meeting. He said: We, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, are standing on the front lines of the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. Therefore, in the face of the central task of the entire Chinese people, as pointed out by Chairman Mao, we should work even harder to complete our combat missions and other tasks closely related to these missions.
    Du Ping also put forward practical and feasible methods for carrying out the movement for streamlining and economizing within the CPVA, suitable for the combat situation at the front. He called on all commanders, fighters, and staff to further improve their tactical and technical skills, increase work efficiency, and ensure that every battle is fought even better. He urged them to cherish the resources of the motherland, eliminate all waste, and avoid all avoidable losses, so that all the manpower, materials, and financial resources that the people of the motherland support us with are used most rationally and achieve maximum effectiveness. He emphasized: In completing the central task of the people of the whole country today, we must maintain the lofty honor of the Volunteer Army, fight more bravely, work actively, and work together with the people of the whole country to strive for the final victory of the war to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea.
    After Du Ping's report, the representatives attending the meeting unanimously supported the various reports and resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as the measures for streamlining and economizing in the CPVA proposed by Du Ping. The representatives attending the meeting are all prepared to seriously convey the message after returning to their respective units, and to mobilize all commanders, fighters, and staff to strive for the complete fulfillment of the central task of the people of the whole country today, and to strive for the final victory of the war against aggression.
    Simultaneous with this meeting, the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army issued instructions regarding the entire army implementing the spirit of the Third Session of the National Committee of the CPPCC and carrying out the task of vigorously streamlining and economizing and cherishing state property.

### 人民志愿军领导机关召开会议  杜平传达人民政协全国委员会第三次会议的各项报告和决议

1951-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    人民志愿军领导机关召开会议
    杜平传达人民政协全国委员会第三次会议的各项报告和决议
        【新华社朝鲜前线二十四日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关为在全军中贯彻中国人民政治协商会议第
    一届全国委员会第三次会议的决议的精神，特于二十二日召开会议，由中国人民志愿军列席人民政协会议的代表、志愿军政治部副主任杜平传达人民政协全国委员会第三次会议的各项报告和决议。参加会议的有各部队代表。
    杜平在传达报告中指出，全军指挥员、战斗员必须认真学习毛主席的开会词、周总理的政治报告和其他各项报告，必须坚决地执行这次会议的决议。他说：我们是站在抗美援朝斗争最前线的中国人民志愿军，因此在毛主席所指的全中国人民的中心任务前面，应该更加努力地来完成我们的战斗任务，以及与此任务密切相关的其他任务。
    杜平并对在志愿军中如何开展精简节约运动，提出了适合前方作战情况的切实可行的办法。他号召全军指挥员战斗员和工作人员，要进一步地提高战术技术，提高工作效率，使每一仗都能打得更好；要万分爱护祖国的资财，消除一切浪费现象，避免
    一切可以避免的损失，使祖国人民所支援我们的一切人力、物力、财力做到最合理的使用，发挥最大的效能。他并强调地指出：在完成全国人民今天的中心任务中，要保持志愿军的崇高的荣誉，要更加英勇战斗，积极工作，与全国人民协力同心地争取抗美援朝战争的最后胜利。
    与会的代表们在杜平传达报告后，一致拥护中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的各项报告和决议以及杜平所提出的在志愿军中精简节约的办法。与会代表都准备回本部后认真地进行传达，并动员全体指挥员战斗员和工作人员为彻底完成全国人民今天的中心任务，争取反侵略战争的最后胜利而奋斗。
    与此会议同时，中国人民志愿军政治部并发出关于全军贯彻人民政协全国委员会第三次会议精神，执行厉行精简节约、爱护国家资财的任务的指示。
