Title: World Peace Council Holds Press Conference to Introduce Various Resolutions of the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, November 11th: The World Peace Council held a press conference in Vienna on the 7th. The press conference was presided over by Mr. Farge, a member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council. He announced that a total of 239 council members and guests attended the Second Session of the World Peace Council. This included 108 people from Western European countries, 31 people from the People's Republic of China and other Asian countries, 27 people from the Soviet Union and people's democracies, 21 people from Near Eastern and North African countries, 20 people from Latin American countries, and 8 people from other countries. In total, 58 countries sent representatives to the meeting. Mr. Farge also introduced in detail the various resolutions passed by the World Peace Council meeting.

Members of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council answered questions raised by reporters at the meeting. They especially explained the relationship between the World Peace Council and various peace-advocating organizations and religious circles in various countries. Executive Bureau member Ehrenburg also condemned some reactionary newspapers in Austria and France for disregarding the work of the World Peace Council and distorting news of the meeting.

### 世界和平理事会举行记者招待会  介绍会议各项决议

1951-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会举行记者招待会
    介绍会议各项决议
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社维也纳讯：世界和平理事会七日在维也纳举行记者招待会。招待会由世界和平理事会执行局委员法奇主持。他宣布：参加世界和平理事会第二届会议的，计有世界和平理事会理事和来宾二百三十九人。其中包括西欧国家一百零八人，中华人民共和国和亚洲其他国家三十一人，苏联和人民民主国家二十七人，近东和北非国家二十一人，拉丁美洲国家二十人，其他国家八人等。总计有五十八个国家派有代表出席会议。法奇并详尽地介绍了世界和平理事会会议所通过的各项决议。
    世界和平理事会执行局委员们在会上答复了记者们所提出的问题。他们特别解释了世界和平理事会和各国各种主张和平的团体及宗教界的关系。执行局委员爱伦堡并谴责了奥地利和法国一些反动报纸漠视世界和平理事会的工作和歪曲会议的消息。
