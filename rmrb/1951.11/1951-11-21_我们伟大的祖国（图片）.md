    Our Great Motherland
    Wolfberry is a medicinal material produced in our country, with Ningxia's production being the most famous. Thanks to the government's loans and the trade company's large-scale purchases, Ningxia's farmers are planting wolfberry on a large scale. Last year, the planting area expanded to 3,500 mu, with a yield of approximately 2,500 dan. This is a rural woman from Zhongning County, Ningxia Province, picking wolfberries.
