Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-11-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Under the ideological education of patriotism and the worker-peasant alliance, cotton farmers in various regions have revised their patriotic covenants and responded to the people's government's call to purchase and store cotton. Cotton farmers in the Fourth Township of Yunyang District, Jingyang County, Xianyang Special District, Shaanxi Province, are bringing their ginned cotton to supply and marketing cooperatives for sale.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在爱国主义和工农联盟的思想教育之下，各地棉农纷纷修订爱国公约，响应人民政府购棉储棉的号召。陕西关中咸阳专区泾阳县云阳区第四乡的棉农们正把轧好的棉花送到供销合作社来出售。
