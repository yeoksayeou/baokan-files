Title: World Peace Council Meeting Concludes; Nenni Delivers Speech Wishing Further Achievements in the Cause of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 10th: According to TASS, the Second Session of the World Peace Council concluded victoriously on the 7th, with the adoption of a supplementary list of members of the World Peace Council and its Executive Bureau, eleven important resolutions, and an open letter to the United Nations and the people of the world.

At the meeting, the Belgian representative, Blum, first rose to read the list of candidates for new council members of the World Peace Council and new members and secretaries of the Executive Bureau. The list was unanimously adopted by all participants amid warm applause.

The meeting then discussed the resolutions prepared by the various committees of the World Peace Council during the session.

Feierhe read out the full text of the draft resolutions on cultural relations, on using the 1952 holiday for the cause of peace, and on the great cultural anniversary. Lafajete proposed the draft resolution on the movement to strive for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. Bosali put forward three draft resolutions on the Japanese Peace Treaty, on Korea, and on Southeast Asia and Vietnam. Faji proposed the draft resolution on disarmament. The Italian representative, Donini, proposed the draft resolutions on the Middle East and on the Near East. Blum read out the draft resolution on the German question. All resolutions were unanimously adopted by all participants.

Lafajete, Secretary-General of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, proposed that the Council members review the draft of the World Peace Council's letter to the United Nations and the people of the world. This letter, addressed to the President of the Sixth General Assembly of the United Nations, was to be signed by Joliot-Curie and Nenni. The Council members warmly welcomed and unanimously approved the proposal.

Nenni delivered a short closing speech. He once again thanked the peace fighters of Austria for their hospitality and efforts in arranging the Vienna meeting of the World Peace Council. Nenni also expressed his gratitude to the people of Vienna, the capital of Austria, for their support for the international movement for peace, which was demonstrated in the mass demonstration on the 3rd of this month. Nenni finally wished all members of the World Peace Council and peace fighters around the world further achievements in the sacred cause of maintaining world peace and the struggle for the happiness of mankind.

The Second Session of the World Peace Council was then declared closed.

### 世界和平理事会会议闭幕  南尼致词祝世界和平事业获得进一步成就

1951-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会会议闭幕
    南尼致词祝世界和平事业获得进一步成就
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社维也纳讯：世界和平理事会第二届会议七日举行最后一次会议，在通过了世界和平理事会及理事会执行局成员的补充名单、十一项重要决议与告联合国和全世界人民书后胜利闭幕。
    会上，比利时代表布伦姆首先起立宣读了世界和平理事会新理事及理事会执行局新委员和秘书候选人的名单。这一名单在热烈掌声中经全体与会者一致通过。
    会议接着讨论世界和平理事会各委员会在会议期间准备的各项决议。
    费雅贺宣读了关于文化关系的决议、关于利用一九五二年假期从事和平事业的决议和关于伟大的文化周年纪念的决议的草案全文，拉斐德提出了关于争取缔结五大国和平公约运动的决议草案，波萨里提出了关于对日和约、关于朝鲜、关于东南亚和越南的三项决议的草案，法奇提出了关于裁减军备的决议草案，意大利代表多尼尼提出了关于中东的决议草案和关于近东的决议草案，布伦姆宣读了关于德国问题的决议草案。各项决议均分别经全体与会者一致通过。
    世界和平理事会执行局秘书长拉斐德提议世界和平理事会理事审议世界和平理事会告联合国和世界人民书的草案。这封信是写给联合国第六届大会主席的，由约里奥——居里和南尼署名。理事会理事热烈欢迎并一致通过了这个提案。
    南尼发表了简短的闭幕词。他再一次向奥地利的和平战士们致谢，感谢他们的殷勤招待和为布置世界和平理事会维也纳会议而作的努力。南尼并向奥国首都维也纳的人民表示感谢，感谢他们对于国际争取和平运动的支持，他们这种支持，已经在本月三日的群众示威中表示出来了。南尼最后祝世界和平理事会全体理事和全世界和平战士在维护世界和平的神圣事业和争取人类幸福的斗争中，获得进一步的成就。
    世界和平理事会第二届会议至此宣告闭幕。
