Title: Celebrating the 34th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution: Moscow Holds Grand Rally and Military Parade
Author:
Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: According to TASS Moscow, representatives of the Moscow Labor People's Deputies Soviet, the Communist Party, people's organizations, and the Soviet Army held a grand rally at the Moscow Grand Theatre on the 6th to celebrate the 34th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The Grand Theatre was decorated in a festive atmosphere. Portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and the flags of the sixteen union republics were hung high above the main entrance of the theatre. The halls of the theatre were also magnificently decorated. Portraits of Lenin and Stalin were hung on the front of the stage. A huge banner read: "Long Live the Leader of the Soviet People – the Great Stalin!" and "Celebrating the 34th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!"

The leaders of the Bolshevik Party and the Soviet Government: Malenkov, Beria, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Andreyev, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Suslov, Ponomarenko, and Shkiryatov took their seats on the presidium, greeted by thunderous applause from workers of Moscow enterprises, scientists, famous artists, and Soviet soldiers attending the celebration.

The conference opened with the solemn strains of the Soviet national anthem. The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Stalin, the leader of the Soviet people and all progressive humanity, was unanimously elected as the honorary presidium amidst thunderous applause from the entire audience.

Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Beria, delivered a report on the 34th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The conference concluded with enthusiastic adoption of a telegram of salute to the great Marshal Stalin.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: TASS Moscow reports: On November 7th, in celebration of the 34th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Moscow garrison held its annual military parade in Red Square.

Red Square was adorned in festive attire. Huge portraits of Lenin and Stalin, the founders of the Soviet state, the Soviet national emblem, the emblems of the various union republics, various red flags, and the slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party (Bolshevik) of the Soviet Union celebrating the October Revolution were hung on the buildings opposite the Kremlin. One banner read: "Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance towards the victory of Communism!"

The troops participating in the review were lined up in Red Square early in the morning. The granite stands along the Kremlin wall were filled with guests – workers, collective farmers, office staff, scientists, and artists. Delegations from various countries and foreign diplomatic envoys were also invited to attend the parade.

At 10:00 AM, the leaders of the Bolshevik Party and the Soviet government stepped onto the reviewing stand at Lenin's Mausoleum. The troops were reviewed by Marshal of the Soviet Union Malinovsky, who rode out of the Spassky Tower gate of the Kremlin. Together with the parade commander, Lieutenant General Artemyev, he began to review the troops and congratulated them on the 34th anniversary of the October Revolution.

After the review, Malinovsky delivered a speech on Lenin's Mausoleum to the combat personnel of the Soviet armed forces, the entire Soviet people, and the foreign friends who came to the Soviet Union to observe the ceremony. As the speech ended, the solemn Soviet national anthem resounded throughout Red Square, and simultaneously, a salute was fired.

The march began. According to tradition, the vanguard of the reviewing formation was the students of the military academies.

First to pass the Mausoleum was the combined regiment of students from the Frunze Military Academy – the oldest officer training school in the country. They were followed by students from the Lenin Military Political Academy, the Artillery Academy, the Military Academy of Mechanization and Motorization, and the Air Force Engineering Academy, who marched past in neat formations.

As the troops passed through Red Square, planes flew overhead. Among them were bombers and fighters, as well as high-speed jet aircraft. The air force review was commanded by Guards Lieutenant General Vasily Stalin.

A naval regiment, border guards, and students from the Moscow Infantry School, established thirty-four years ago, marched in step across Red Square.

Students from the Suvorov and Nakhimov schools were the youngest participants in the parade. The Taman Guards Rifle Division entered Red Square. All units of this division are equipped with highly sophisticated equipment. This was followed by motorized vehicles carrying paratroopers. Cannons, tanks, and automatic guns of various calibers participated in the parade.

Countless guests on the reviewing stand enthusiastically cheered the Soviet soldiers who vigilantly defend the peace and security of the Soviet state.

After the military parade ended, jubilant columns of Moscow's working people continued to pass through Red Square.

### 庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年  莫斯科举行盛大集会和阅兵典礼

1951-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年
    莫斯科举行盛大集会和阅兵典礼
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：莫斯科劳动人民代表苏维埃、共产党、人民团体和苏军的代表们，六日在莫斯科大戏院举行了伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年庆祝大会。
    大戏院装饰得一片节日气象。戏院的正门高挂着马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的肖像，和十六个加盟共和国的国旗。戏院的大厅也布置得富丽堂皇。舞台的正面悬挂着列宁、斯大林的肖像。巨幅的横联上面写着：“苏联人民的领袖——伟大的斯大林万岁”，“庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年”。
    布尔什维克党和苏联政府的领袖们：马林科夫、贝利亚、伏罗希洛夫、米高扬、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、安德列耶夫、赫鲁舍切夫、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、波诺马林科和施基利亚托夫在主席团席位上就座，参加庆祝大会的莫斯科各企业的工人、科学家、著名的艺术家和苏联军人报以暴风雨般的掌声。
    大会在庄严的苏联国歌声中开幕。以苏联人民和全体进步人类的领袖斯大林为首的苏联共产党中央委员会政治局，在全场暴风雨般的掌声中一致当选为名誉主席团。
    苏联部长会议副主席贝利亚在会上作了关于伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年的报告。
    大会最后以高涨的热情通过了向伟大的斯大林大元帅致敬电。
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：十一月七日，为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年，莫斯科卫戍部队在红场举行了一年一度的阅兵。
    红场穿上了节日的盛装。克里姆林宫对面的建筑物上悬挂着苏维埃国家的创立者列宁和斯大林的巨幅画像、苏联国徽、各加盟共和国的国徽、各种红旗和苏联共产党（布）中央委员会庆祝十月革命节的口号。有一幅横旗上写着下列字句：“在列宁的旗帜下，在斯大林的领导下，朝着共产主义的胜利前进！”
    参加检阅的军队一清早就在红场上排好了队伍。克里姆林宫墙边的花岗石看台上站满了客人——工人、集体农民、机关职员、科学家和艺术家。各国代表团和各国外交使节亦应邀参加阅兵典礼。
    上午十时，布尔什维克党和苏联政府的领袖们步上列宁墓的检阅台。检阅部队的是苏联元帅马林诺夫斯基，他骑马从克里姆林宫斯巴斯基塔的门中出来检阅。他和阅兵指挥官阿尔杰米也夫中将一道开始检阅部队，并向他们祝贺十月革命节三十四周年纪念。
    检阅后，马林诺夫斯基在列宁墓上向苏联武装部队战斗人员、全体苏联人民和到苏联来观礼的外国朋友们发表演说。演说完了时，苏联庄严的国歌声响彻了红场，同时礼炮齐鸣。
    游行开始。依照传统，检阅队伍的前导是各军事学院的学生。
    最先经过陵墓的是伏龙芝军事学院——全国最老的军官训练学校——的学生混成团。接着列宁军事政治学院、炮兵学院、机械化与摩托化军事学院、空军工程学院的学生排成整齐的队伍迈步走过。
    当军队经过红场时，飞机在上空掠飞而过。其中有轰炸机和战斗机及速度极高的喷气式飞机。空军检阅由近卫军中将华西里·斯大林指挥。
    水兵团、边防军、三十四年前成立的莫斯科步兵学校的学生齐步经过红场。
    苏沃罗夫和纳希莫夫学校的学生是阅兵队伍中最年轻的参加者。塔曼警卫军来福枪师进入红场了。这个师全体部队都有非常精良的装备。接着是载着伞兵部队的摩托车辆。大炮、坦克和各种口径的自动炮都参加了阅兵行列。
    检阅台上无数的来宾都热烈地向警觉地保卫苏维埃国家的和平与安全的苏维埃战士欢呼。
    军队检阅终了后，莫斯科劳动人民的狂欢队伍接着经过红场。
