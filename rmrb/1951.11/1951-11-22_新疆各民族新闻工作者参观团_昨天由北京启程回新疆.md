Title: Xinjiang Ethnic Minority Journalists Delegation Returns to Xinjiang from Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: The Xinjiang Ethnic Minority Journalists Delegation, after three months of visits and tours in the Northwest, East China, Northeast, and Tianjin, Beijing, departed from Beijing today to return to Xinjiang. Peng Sike, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government's State Council, Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the General Administration of News of the Central People's Government, and representatives from various news organizations in the capital went to the station to see them off.

After arriving in Beijing, the delegation presented a flag to Chairman Mao to express their respect and listened to a report on ethnic policies by Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government's State Council. During their ten days in the capital, all members of the delegation visited and toured the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Central People's Broadcasting Station, Central Institute for Nationalities, and Tsinghua University; they also visited various scenic spots in the capital and the Palace Museum.

The head of the delegation, A·Zununi, said before returning to Xinjiang: "During our visit to various places, the local people's governments and the masses showed great concern for us ethnic minorities, which made us personally feel the spirit of unity and friendship among all ethnic groups. During this visit, we saw that the construction of our motherland in all aspects is progressing rapidly. We must bring everything we have seen back to Xinjiang and tell the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang." The deputy head of the delegation, Hali, said: "After this visit, I have a deeper love for the motherland and am full of confidence in the future of the motherland. The Communist Party and the People's Government are always concerned about the news work of ethnic minorities in Xinjiang. We must, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, and with the help of our elder brother Han, build Xinjiang well and contribute our share to the motherland."

### 新疆各民族新闻工作者参观团  昨天由北京启程回新疆

1951-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    新疆各民族新闻工作者参观团
    昨天由北京启程回新疆
    【新华社二十一日讯】新疆各民族新闻工作者参观团在西北、华东、东北和天津、北京各地经三个月的参观和访问后，今日由京启程回新疆。中央人民政府政务院民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克、中央人民政府新闻总署副署长萨空了和首都各新闻机关代表曾去车站欢送。
    参观团到达北京后，曾向毛主席献旗致敬，并听了中央人民政府政务院民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平关于民族政策的报告。全体团员在首都的十天当中，参观和访问了人民日报社、新华通讯社、中央人民广播电台、中央民族学院和清华大学等处；游览了首都各名胜区域和故宫博物院。
    参观团团长阿·祖努尼在返回新疆前发表谈话说：“我们这次到各地参观，各地人民政府和群众对我们少数民族非常关心，使我们亲身感受到各民族团结友爱的精神。这次参观中，我们看到祖国各方面建设都在突飞猛进，我们要把所看到的一切带回新疆去，告诉新疆各民族人民。”副团长哈利说：“我在参观以后，对祖国更加深了浓厚的热爱，对祖国的前途充满了信心。共产党和人民政府时时关心着新疆少数民族的新闻工作，我们一定要在毛主席和共产党的领导下以及在汉族老大哥的帮助下，好好建设新疆，为祖国贡献一分力量。”
