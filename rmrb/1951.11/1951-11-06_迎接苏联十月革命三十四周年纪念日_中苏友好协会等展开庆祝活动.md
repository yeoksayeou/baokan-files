Title: Commemorating the Thirty-Fourth Anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Others Launch Celebration Activities
Author:
Date: 1951-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: To welcome the thirty-fourth anniversary of the Great Soviet October Socialist Revolution on November 7th, the National Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to hold a celebration rally on the evening of the 7th. On the same day, starting from 6:15 PM, a broadcast will be held on the Central People's Broadcasting Station, featuring speeches by Lin Boqu, a council member of the association, on "The Soviet Union Defending World Peace and Advancing Towards Communism," and by Qian Junrui, the Secretary-General, on "Celebrating the October Revolution and Strengthening Sino-Soviet Friendship Work." The association has also decided to jointly host the "Soviet Film and Picture Exhibition" with the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in eighteen major cities across the country, and at the same time assist the China Film Distribution and Exhibition Corporation in holding Soviet film exhibitions in twenty major cities nationwide.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 2nd: The people of Shanghai are enthusiastically launching various propaganda activities about Sino-Soviet friendship and internationalism to welcome the great thirty-fourth anniversary of the Soviet October Socialist Revolution. The broadcast lectures jointly organized by the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Shanghai Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association will begin on the 2nd at the Shanghai People's Broadcasting Station, featuring special lectures introducing the achievements of the Soviet Union in various constructions and the happy lives of the Soviet people. Eight cinemas throughout the city will jointly hold a Soviet film exhibition, scheduled to continuously screen famous Soviet films starting from the 7th. The "Soviet Communist Construction Picture Exhibition" held by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Museum is also scheduled to open on the 7th. The Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will invite representatives from all sectors of the people of East China and Shanghai to hold a celebration meeting on the evening of the 6th. People from all walks of life will also hold various forums, chats, and celebrations around the 7th.

### 迎接苏联十月革命三十四周年纪念日  中苏友好协会等展开庆祝活动

1951-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接苏联十月革命三十四周年纪念日
    中苏友好协会等展开庆祝活动
    【新华社二日讯】十一月七日为苏联十月社会主义革命三十四周年纪念日，中苏友好协会总会决定在七日晚举行庆祝大会。同日晚六时十五分起在中央人民广播电台举行广播，由该会理事林伯渠讲“保卫世界和平向共产主义迈进的苏联”、总干事钱俊瑞讲“庆祝十月革命节和加强中苏友好工作”。该会又决定联合苏联对外文化协会，在全国十八个大城市共同举办“苏联电影图片展览会”，同时协助中国影片经理公司在全国二十个大城市举办苏联影片展览。
    【新华社上海二日电】上海市人民为迎接伟大的苏联十月社会主义革命三十四周年，正热烈地展开关于中苏友好与国际主义的各项宣传活动。中苏友好协会上海分会与上海市抗美援朝分会联合举办的广播讲座，自二日起假上海市人民广播电台连续举行专题演讲，向全市人民介绍苏联各项建设成就和苏联人民的幸福生活。全市八家电影院联合举行苏联影片展览，定七日起连续放映苏联名片。中苏友谊馆举办的“苏联共产主义建设图片展览”，亦定于七日揭幕。中苏友好协会上海分会将于六日晚邀请华东暨上海各界人民代表举行庆祝大会。各阶层人民亦将于七日前后分别举行各种座谈、漫谈和庆祝会。
