Title: Volunteer Army Combat Heroes' Observation Delegation Arrives in Chongqing and Lanzhou to Give Reports
Author:
Date: 1951-11-13
Page: 1

  　Volunteer Army Combat Heroes' Observation Delegation
    Arrives in Chongqing and Lanzhou to Give Reports
    [Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 12th] The Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat heroes' National Day observation delegation, including Zheng Enxi and eight others, arrived in Chongqing on the 9th by boat at the invitation of the Southwest China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. Accompanying the Volunteer Army combat heroes were Liu Zilin, a combat hero of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who attended the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival, and heroic model representatives from the Southwest Military Region who went to Beijing to participate in the National Day observation.
    More than 5,000 representatives from all walks of life in Southwest China and Chongqing held a welcoming ceremony on the riverside. When the heroes passed through the streets of Chongqing in floats, they were frequently stopped by enthusiastic crowds. The masses scrambled to grab the heroes' hands and showered them with paper flowers.
    Nine hundred representatives from all walks of life in Southwest China and Chongqing held a welcoming party that day. At the meeting, Zheng Enxi, a combat hero of the Volunteer Army, reported on the heroic combat deeds on the Korean front.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 12th] The Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat heroes' National Day observation delegation, including Song Changfu and eight others, arrived in Lanzhou on the 6th at the invitation of the Northwest China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. Those who went to the station to welcome them included responsible persons from various agencies in Gansu Province and Lanzhou City, as well as more than 500 representatives of twelve ethnic groups. When the representatives' cars passed through the city center, more than 100,000 citizens lined the streets to welcome them.

### 志愿军战斗英雄观礼代表  应邀抵重庆兰州作报告

1951-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

  　志愿军战斗英雄观礼代表
    应邀抵重庆兰州作报告
    【新华社重庆十二日电】中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表郑恩喜等九人，应西南区中国人民抗美援朝总分会的邀请，于九日乘轮船抵重庆。和志愿军战斗英雄同行的有曾出席第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节大会的中国人民解放军战斗英雄刘子林及西南军区赴京参加国庆节观礼的英雄模范代表。
    西南区和重庆市各界人民代表五千多人在江滨举行了欢迎会。英雄们乘花车通过重庆市街时，不时被热情的群众拦住。群众争着拉住英雄们的手，把纸花撒在英雄们的身上。
    西南及重庆各界人民代表九百人当日举行了欢迎晚会。会上，志愿军战斗英雄郑恩喜报告了朝鲜前线的英勇战斗事迹。
    【新华社兰州十二日电】中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表宋长福等九人，应西北区中国人民抗美援朝总分会的邀请，于六日抵兰州。前往车站欢迎的有甘肃省和兰州市各机关负责人，及十二个民族的代表五百余人。代表们坐的汽车经过市中心区时，夹道欢迎的市民有十万余人。
