Title: Observation Delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, and Other Countries Return to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The people's observation delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic, who were invited to participate in our National Day celebrations, have returned to Beijing on October 30 after visiting and touring East China and Tianjin. They will rest in the capital for a short period and then set off to return to their countries.

### 保、罗等国观礼代表团返京

1951-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　保、罗等国观礼代表团返京
    【新华社一日讯】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的保加利亚、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国人民观礼代表团，在到华东和天津等地参观访问后已于十月三十日回到北京。他们将在首都稍事休息，然后启程返国。
