Title: Denisov's Reply to Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

Author:

Date: 1951-11-18
Page: 1

　　Denisov's Reply to Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association:

The Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries sincerely thanks you for your congratulatory telegram celebrating the 34th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and expressing your friendly wishes.

We wish you and the Chinese friends of the Soviet Union further success in the construction of the new democratic country and the consolidation of the cause of friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples—a strong guarantee of peace and security in the Far East.

Long live the great and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!

               Andrey Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
           November 14, 1951

### 吉尼索夫复中苏友协刘少奇会长电

1951-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　吉尼索夫复中苏友协刘少奇会长电
    中苏友好协会刘少奇会长：
    苏联对外文化协会理事会衷心地感谢您庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年和表示友好愿望的贺电。
    祝你和苏联的中国朋友们，在新民主主义国家的建设和巩固中苏两国人民的友好事业——远东和平和安全的坚强保障——中，获得更进一步的成功。
    中苏两国人民伟大的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
               苏联对外文化协会理事会主席安德列·吉尼索夫
           一九五一年十一月十四日
