Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee and other organizations send letters to the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students to congratulate World Youth Day and International Students' Week
Author:
Date: 1951-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth to warmly congratulate the World Federation of Democratic Youth on its great achievements over the past six years. The telegram stated: "Chinese youth are determined to unite with all peace-loving youth around the world, and under the banner of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, will carry out unremitting struggles against the new war plots of imperialism and for lasting world peace and people's democracy."

    This newspaper reports: November 10th to 17th is "International Students' Week." The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram to the International Union of Students, congratulating the International Union of Students on its significant achievements in uniting students around the world in the struggle for peace, national independence, and democratic education over the past five years. The telegram stated: "Chinese students, under the banner of the International Union of Students, will unite closely with students around the world and resolutely fight against imperialist aggression, for peace, and for better living and learning conditions for students around the world."

### 青年团中央等致函世界青联和国际学联  祝贺世界青年日和国际学生周

1951-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    青年团中央等致函世界青联和国际学联
    祝贺世界青年日和国际学生周
    【新华社九日讯】十一月十日是“世界青年日”，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会暨中华全国民主青年联合总会，特致电世界民主青年联盟，热烈祝贺世界民主青年联盟六年来的巨大成就。电文中说：“中国青年坚决与全世界一切爱好和平的青年团结一起，在世界民主青年联盟旗帜下，为反对帝国主义新战争阴谋，争取世界持久和平与人民民主而进行不懈的斗争。”
    【本报讯】十一月十日至十七日为“国际学生周”，中华全国学生联合会特致电国际学生联合会，祝贺五年来国际学联团结全世界学生进行争取和平、民族独立和民主教育的斗争中的重大成就。电文中说：“中国学生，在国际学联旗帜下，将与世界学生紧密团结，坚决为反对帝国主义侵略、争取和平以及世界学生更好的生活与学习条件而斗争。”
