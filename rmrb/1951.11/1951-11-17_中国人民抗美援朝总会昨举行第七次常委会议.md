Title: China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea Holds Seventh Standing Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-11-17
Page: 4

　　China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea Holds Seventh Standing Committee Meeting

    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea held its seventh Standing Committee meeting today to discuss future work in resisting America and aiding Korea, and to welcome Comrades Ma Yinchu and Zhang Bojun, two council members who attended the Second World Peace Council Meeting.

    Those present at the meeting included Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, vice-chairmen of the China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea; Standing Committee members Situ Meitang, Li Siguang, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Jiang Nanxiang, Deng Yingchao, Liu Ningyi, Luo Longji; World Peace Council members Zhang Bojun, Li Dequan; and heads of various departments of the China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, totaling twenty-four people.

    After hearing Secretary-General Liu Ningyi's report on the work of the China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea over the past four months, and discussing the progress of the weapon donation campaign in various regions, the meeting unanimously adopted a notice on striving to complete the weapon donation payment work on schedule and continuing to strengthen the movement to resist America and aid Korea.

    At the meeting, Ma Yinchu and Zhang Bojun conveyed the various decisions of the Second World Peace Council Meeting. The meeting unanimously adopted a statement endorsing all resolutions of the Second World Peace Council Meeting.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会昨举行第七次常委会议

1951-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国人民抗美援朝总会昨举行第七次常委会议
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会今日举行第七次常务委员会议，讨论今后抗美援朝工作，并欢迎出席世界和平理事会第二次会议的马寅初、章伯钧两理事。
    出席会议的有抗美援朝总会副主席彭真、陈叔通，常务委员司徒美堂、李四光、邵力子、马叙伦、马寅初、张奚若、章乃器、许德珩、许宝驹、蒋南翔、邓颖超、刘宁一、罗隆基，世界和平理事会理事章伯钧、李德全及抗美援朝总会各部门负责人共二十四人。
    会议在听取了秘书长刘宁一关于抗美援朝总会四个月工作情况的报告，并对各地开展捐献武器运动的情况进行了讨论之后，一致通过关于争取按期完成武器捐献缴款工作并继续加强抗美援朝运动的通知。
    会上，马寅初、章伯钧传达了世界和平理事会第二次会议的各项决定。会议一致通过了关于拥护世界和平理事会第二次会议各项决议的声明。
