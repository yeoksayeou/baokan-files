Title: Deng Yingchao Arrives in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 29th. Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, arrived in Moscow today to attend the 11th meeting of the Executive Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation. Those who welcomed her at the station included Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, Vice-Chairman Troitskaya, Secretary-General Petrova, Avogenyikova, Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet Women's Magazine, Tong Jin, Director of the First Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kulazhenkov, Director of the Protocol Department. Liu Ying, wife of our Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian, and Counselor Ge Baoquan also greeted her at the station.

### 邓颖超抵达莫斯科

1951-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    邓颖超抵达莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】出席国际民主妇女联合会执行委员会第十一次会议的中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超今日抵莫斯科。到站欢迎者有苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃、副主席特罗意斯卡雅，秘书长彼得罗娃，苏联妇女杂志主编阿沃疆尼科娃，苏联外交部第一远东司司长童金，交际司司长库拉任柯夫。我国驻苏大使张闻天夫人刘英和参赞戈宝权等人亦到站迎接。
