Reply to the Tibetan Local Government

To the Honorable Gentlemen of the Tibetan Local Government:

Your telegram of October 19, 1951, has been received. Thank you for your congratulations on the Third Session of our Committee. All members of this committee also extend heartfelt congratulations on the peaceful liberation of Tibet and the great unity of all nationalities under the banner of Chairman Mao Zedong.

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
November 1, 1951

### 复西藏地方政府电

1951-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    复西藏地方政府电西藏地方政府诸位先生：
    一九五一年十月十九日的来电已经收到。感谢你们对本会第三次会议的祝贺。本会同人并衷心地庆贺西藏和平解放及全国各民族在毛泽东主席旗帜之下的伟大团结。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    一九五一年十一月一日
