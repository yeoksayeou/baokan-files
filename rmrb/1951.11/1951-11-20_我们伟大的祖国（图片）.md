Our Great Motherland
    Excellent timber is essential for national construction. Many large mountains in Hunan Province produce a large amount of fir wood. Many of the ethnic minorities here rely on planting fir trees and rafting timber for their livelihoods. This is a scene of ethnic minority people in Tongdao County, Hunan Province, bundling cut fir trees into rafts and transporting them to various places via waterways.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    优良的木材，是国家建设所必需的。湖南境内的许多大山，大量出产杉木。这里的少数民族人民，很多是依靠种杉木和放木排来维持生活的。这是湖南通道县的少数民族人民，正在把砍下来的杉木，编成木排，经渠道运到各地去。
