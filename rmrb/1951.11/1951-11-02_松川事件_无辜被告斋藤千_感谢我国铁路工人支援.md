Title: "Matsukawa Incident" Innocent Defendant Saito Sen Thanks Chinese Railway Workers for Support
Author:
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 4

　　"Matsukawa Incident" Innocent Defendant Saito Sen
　　Thanks Chinese Railway Workers for Support
　　[Reported by this newspaper] The Japan-China Friendship Association of Tokyo recently forwarded a letter written on October 1st by Saito Sen, an innocent defendant in the Japanese "Matsukawa Incident," from Miyagi Prison in Sendai City, Japan, to the Chinese people. In this letter, Saito Sen sincerely congratulates the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and thanks Chinese railway workers for their support. The original letter is translated as follows:
　　I heartily congratulate the second anniversary of the birth of the People's Republic of China and extend warm greetings to the Chinese people.
　　From the iron bars of a three-mat prison cell, gazing at the southern sky, I cannot help but clench my thin fist tightly, wanting to shout to the Chinese people:
　　"The victory of the Chinese people is the victory of the Japanese people!"
　　From my bed, I respectfully salute the portrait of Chairman Mao pasted on the wall.
　　October, the days of the people's victory, have begun. On the 23rd of this month, the public trial of us twenty innocent defendants of the "Matsukawa Incident" will begin, and we are determined to fight to the end for peace and truth.
　　I have been lying in bed for nine months and unable to get up for a long time. My three requests for bail have been denied.
　　However, we cannot stop fighting because of illness; to stop fighting is to die.
　　We not only express our gratitude to our Chinese railway worker brothers who specially sent us 650,000 yen and 20,000 signatures, but also thank all the people of Asia and peace-loving friends around the world.
　　We will grit our teeth and continue to fight a desperate battle.
　　We will surely win!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Saito Sen
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 1, 1951, at Miyagi Prison, Sendai City, Japan

### “松川事件”无辜被告斋藤千  感谢我国铁路工人支援

1951-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　“松川事件”无辜被告斋藤千
　  感谢我国铁路工人支援
    【本报讯】日本东京市日中友好协会顷转来日本“松川事件”无辜被告斋藤千十月一日自日本仙台市宫城监狱中写给中国人民的一封信。斋藤千在这封信里衷心祝贺中华人民共和国成立二周年，并感谢我国铁路工人给他们的支援。原信译文如下：
    我衷心祝贺中华人民共和国诞生二周年，向中国人民热烈地握手。
    从三席牢房的铁栏中，遥望着南方的天空，我不由得使劲地捏紧着瘦拳，要向中国人民诸君高呼：
    “中国人民的胜利就是日本人民的胜利！”
    从床上，我恭谨地向贴在墙上的毛主席的肖像致敬。
    十月，人民胜利的日子开始了。这个月的二十三日，我们二十名“松川事件”无辜被告的公审将要开始，我们决心为和平与真理斗争到底。
    我已经躺在病床上九个月了，长时间不能起床。我三次请求保释都被拒绝了。
    但是，不能因为病痛而停止斗争；停止斗争只有死亡。
    我们不仅向特意给我们送来了六十五万日元和两万个签名的中国铁路工人弟兄们表示感谢，同时要感谢全亚洲人民和全世界爱好和平的朋友们。
    我们将咬紧牙关继续进行决死的斗争。
    我们一定胜利！
            斋藤千
      一九五一年十月一日于日本仙台市宫城监狱
