Our Great Motherland

Wang Jibo, a worker at the foundry of the Qingdao Sifang Railway Factory, worked hard to improve his work methods and created the "×-" type freight car brake shoe support sand mold, which increased production efficiency by eight times compared to the past, setting a new national record. This is a scene of Wang Jibo explaining the work method to his fellow workers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    青岛四方铁路工厂翻砂场的工人王继伯，努力改进工作方法，创造了“×－”型货车闸瓦托砂模，使生产效率较前提高了八倍，创全国的新纪录。这是王继伯向工友们讲解工作法的情形。
