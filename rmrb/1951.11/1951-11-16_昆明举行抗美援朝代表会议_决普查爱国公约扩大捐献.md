Title: Kunming Holds Representative Meeting on Resisting America and Aiding Korea; Decides to Conduct Universal Review of Patriotic Pledges and Expand Donations
Author:
Date: 1951-11-16
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency report:] To implement Chairman Mao's instruction at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on continuing to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, the Third Kunming Municipal Representative Meeting on Resisting America and Aiding Korea was held on October 26th and 27th. More than 1,500 representatives from all ethnic groups and all sectors of society attended the meeting. The meeting decided to conduct a universal review of the patriotic pledges of people from all walks of life in the city in November, mobilize people from all walks of life in the city to complete the half-year donation plan before the end of November, and strive to continue to expand the donation plan. During the meeting, the representatives conscientiously studied Chairman Mao's opening remarks at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, summarized the work since the Second Kunming Municipal Representative Meeting on Resisting America and Aiding Korea, and exchanged experiences in the work of resisting America and aiding Korea. The meeting also commended various model workers and model units that have worked actively in the movement to resist America and aid Korea.

### 昆明举行抗美援朝代表会议  决普查爱国公约扩大捐献

1951-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　昆明举行抗美援朝代表会议
    决普查爱国公约扩大捐献
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：为贯彻执行毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议中关于继续加强抗美援朝工作的指示，昆明市第三次抗美援朝代表会议于十月二十六、二十七两日举行。出席会议的有各族各界代表一千五百多人。会议决定在十一月中，普遍检查全市各界人民的爱国公约，动员全市各界人民在十一月底以前完成半年捐献计划，并为继续扩大捐献计划而奋斗。会议期间，代表们认真学习了毛主席在政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议上的开会词，总结了该市召开第二次抗美援朝代表会议以来的工作，交流了抗美援朝工作的经验。会议并表扬了在抗美援朝运动中积极努力的各种模范工作者和模范单位。
