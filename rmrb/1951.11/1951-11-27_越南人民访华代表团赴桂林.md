Title: Vietnamese People's Delegation to China Heads to Guilin
Author:
Date: 1951-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 24th: The Vietnamese People's Delegation to China departed from Guangzhou by train on the evening of the 23rd, en route to Guilin. Chen Xiangnan, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, along with 300 representatives from all sectors of the people in the province and city, went to the station to see them off.

During their eight-day stay in Guangzhou, the delegation attended two grand welcoming rallies held by representatives of the people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, as well as cadres from various government departments and units. Delegation leader Ton Duc Thang and others reported on the heroic deeds of the Vietnamese people in resisting French imperialism. The delegation visited the South China Native Products Exhibition and Exchange Conference, and toured revolutionary historical sites and scenic spots such as Huanghuagang, Sanyuanli, and the Peasant Movement Training Institute presided over by Chairman Mao during the Great Revolution. The delegation also visited Zhongshan County to observe the county's land reform movement.

### 越南人民访华代表团赴桂林

1951-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民访华代表团赴桂林
    【新华社广州二十四日电】越南人民访华代表团已于二十三日晚乘车离广州转赴桂林。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会广州分会秘书长陈翔南和省市各界人民代表三百人前往车站送行。
    代表团在广州八天中，曾出席广东省、广州市各界人民代表和各机关单位干部的两次盛大欢迎会。代表团团长孙德胜等在会上报告了越南人民抗击法帝国主义者的英勇事迹。代表团参观了华南土特产展览交流大会，游览黄花岗、三元里、大革命时代毛主席主持的农民讲习所等革命史迹名胜。代表团还到中山县参观了该县的土地改革运动。
