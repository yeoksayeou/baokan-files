Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 109th Meeting: Discussing Various Issues in Government Work and Premier Zhou Giving a Comprehensive Solution and Explanation
Author:
Date: 1951-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: The 109th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 3rd to discuss various issues in government work raised by the heads of the major administrative regions, provinces, and municipalities attending the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). Those attending the meeting included responsible persons in Beijing from the Military and Political Committees of the major administrative regions, the Northeast People's Government, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, the people's governments of various provinces and municipalities, the people's administrative offices of various regions, and the responsible persons from the commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks under the Government Administration Council, as well as Vice Chairman Li Jishen and Secretary-General Lin Boqu of the Central People's Government, and Vice Chairman Chen Shutong of the CPPCC National Committee. At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai gave instructions on future work tasks, resolved and answered various important questions and opinions raised by the heads of local people's governments, and assigned certain specific issues to the relevant competent departments of the Central People's Government for research and handling. During the discussion, the heads of the local people's governments present unanimously agreed with Premier Zhou's instructions and answers, and expressed that they would resolutely implement Premier Zhou's various work instructions after returning to their respective regions. Note: During the Third Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, the Central People's Government Administration Council twice invited the heads of local people's governments in Beijing to discuss government work, and referred the issues raised at the symposiums to the relevant competent departments of the Central People's Government for proposing solutions. This meeting of the Government Administration Council was a comprehensive resolution and explanation given by Premier Zhou.

### 政务院举行一百零九次会议  讨论关于政府工作中的各项问题并由周总理作综括的解决和解答

1951-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一百零九次会议
    讨论关于政府工作中的各项问题并由周总理作综括的解决和解答
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百零九次政务会议于三日下午举行，讨论此次出席和列席人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议的各大行政区、省、市人民政府负责人所提出的关于政府工作中的各项问题。列席这次会议的，有各大行政区军政委员会，东北人民政府，内蒙古自治区人民政府，各省、市人民政府，各区人民行政公署在京的负责人和中央人民政府最高人民法院，最高人民检察署，政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人，此外，并有中央人民政府李济深副主席、林伯渠秘书长，人民政协全国委员会陈叔通副主席参加。会上，周恩来总理指示了今后工作任务，解决和解答了各地人民政府负责人提出的各项重要问题与意见，并将某些具体问题，交由中央人民政府各有关主管部门负责研究处理。在讨论中，到会的各地人民政府负责人对周总理的指示与解答，均一致同意，并表示对于周总理的各项工作指示，将于回到各地后坚决执行。按：中央人民政府政务院于人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议开会期间，曾两次邀集来京的各地人民政府负责人座谈政府工作，并将座谈会上提出的各项问题，分别交由中央人民政府各有关主管部门提出处理意见。此次政务会议即由周总理作综括的解决和解答。
