Title: Taiyuan Private Dafang Construction Company Severely Exploits Workers; Workers Demand Swift Action
Author:
Date: 1951-11-06
Page: 2

    To the Editor:
    We are construction workers who recently came from Nanjing to work at the Wanbailin construction site of the private Dafang Construction Company in Taiyuan. This company still exhibits serious feudal "ba tou" (foreman) exploitation of workers, and the capitalist side turns a blind eye. For example, the brick wall project that Dafang Construction Company contracted to the feudal "ba tou" Zhuang Dehua has a unit price of 300,000 yuan for a twenty-brick wall, 160,000 yuan for a fifteen-brick wall, and 120,000 yuan for a ten-brick wall. But Zhuang Dehua subcontracts to us at a unit price of 150,000 yuan for a twenty-brick wall, 80,000 yuan for a fifteen-brick wall, and 60,000 yuan for a ten-brick wall, reaping illegal profits of up to one time the original price. Furthermore, regarding transportation, labor and management initially agreed that workers would handle distances within 50 paces, and Zhuang Dehua would handle distances beyond 50 paces. But now we are transporting materials as far as 150 to 200 paces. When we came, the contract stipulated 100 days of work, but now, less than three months in, there is no more work. We hope the relevant departments will handle this matter swiftly.

    Construction Workers Zhang Shengwang, Huo Peikuan, et al.
    (Written on behalf of Cheng Ming, Chang An)

### 太原私营大方工程公司  把头严重地剥削工人工人要求迅速处理

1951-11-06
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    太原私营大方工程公司
    把头严重地剥削工人工人要求迅速处理
    编辑同志：我们都是建筑工人，最近由南京来到太原私营大方工程公司万柏林工地做工。该公司还存在着严重的封建把头剥削工人的现象，而资方却置之不闻。比如大方工程公司包给封建把头庄德华的砖墙工程，单价是二十砖墙为三十万元，十五砖墙为十六万元，十砖墙为十二万元；但庄德华包给我们，单价就成了二十砖墙十五万元，十五砖墙八万元，十砖墙六万元，获非法利润竟达一倍。又如在来往搬运中，劳资双方曾说明五十步以内归工人，五十步以外归庄德华，但现在我们搬运已远至一百五十步至二百步了。我们来的时候，订的合同是做一百天的工作，可是，现在还不到三个月，就没有工作了。我们希望有关部门迅速处理此事。
    建筑工人  张盛旺、霍培宽等
    （代笔人  成    名、长    安）
