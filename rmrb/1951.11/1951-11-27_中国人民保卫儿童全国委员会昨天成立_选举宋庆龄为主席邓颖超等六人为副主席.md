Title: China National Committee for the Defense of Children Established Yesterday; Song Qingling Elected Chairman, Deng Yingchao and Six Others Elected Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1951-11-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th: In response to the call of the Berlin meeting of the Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation to strengthen and expand the movement to defend children, and to promote the cause of children's welfare in our country, the China National Committee for the Defense of Children, headed by Song Qingling, was established today. More than 70 representatives from all walks of life, including women, youth, trade unions, medical and health, education, science, literature and art, justice, relief and welfare, religious circles, and various ethnic groups across the country, attended the founding meeting. At the meeting, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, reported on the significance and tasks of the establishment of the China National Committee for the Defense of Children. Kang Keqing, Minister of the Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, reported on the preparatory process.

    The conference adopted the charter of the China National Committee for the Defense of Children, and elected Song Qingling as the Chairman of the China National Committee for the Defense of Children, Deng Yingchao, Li Siguang, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, Fu Lianzhang, and Wei Que as Vice-Chairmen, and Kang Keqing as Secretary-General. Amidst warm applause from the audience, Chairman Song Qingling read the declaration of the establishment of the China National Committee for the Defense of Children. The conference concluded with the passage of a telegram to the Women's International Democratic Federation, and the children of the Beihai Experimental Nursery presented flowers to the conference.

### 中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会昨天成立  选举宋庆龄为主席邓颖超等六人为副主席

1951-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会昨天成立
    选举宋庆龄为主席邓颖超等六人为副主席 【新华社二十六日讯】为响应国际民主妇女联合会理事会柏林会议关于加强与扩大保卫儿童运动的号召，并促进我国儿童福利事业，以宋庆龄为首的中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会今天宣告成立。参加成立大会的有全国妇女、青年、工会、医药卫生、教育、科学、文艺、司法、救济福利、宗教界和各兄弟民族等各方面的代表七十多人。会上，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全报告中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会成立的意义和任务，全国民主妇联儿童福利部部长康克清报告了筹备经过。
    大会通过了中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会的章程，并选举宋庆龄为中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会的主席，邓颖超、李四光、李德全、廖承志、傅连暲、韦悫为副主席，康克清为秘书长。在全场热烈掌声中，宋庆龄主席宣读了中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会成立宣言。大会最后通过了给国际民主妇女联合会的电报，并由北海实验托儿所的小朋友向大会献花。
