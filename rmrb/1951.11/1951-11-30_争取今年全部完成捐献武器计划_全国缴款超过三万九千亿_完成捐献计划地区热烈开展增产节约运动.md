Title: Strive to Complete the Weapon Donation Plan This Year / National Payments Exceed 3.9 Trillion / Regions Completing Donation Plans Enthusiastically Launch Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th: As of the 29th, the total amount of money donated by the people nationwide for weapons has reached 3,911.19 billion yuan, enough to purchase 2,607 fighter jets, accounting for more than 95% of the total pledged amount of 4,113.9 billion yuan.

Currently, the regions that have completed the donation plan ahead of schedule or exceeded the target include Qinghai, North Sichuan, West Sichuan, Zhejiang, North Jiangsu, Hubei, Hebei, Gansu, East Sichuan, Fujian, Henan, South Sichuan, North Anhui, Suiyuan, Hunan, Chahar, Shanxi, South Anhui, Shandong, etc., nineteen provinces and regions, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the municipalities directly under the Central Government and the Greater Administrative Regions, such as Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Xi'an, and Benxi. Among them, the people of Shanghai exceeded their donation target by 83 fighter jets, the people of Zhejiang Province exceeded their target by 50 fighter jets, and the people of Hebei Province exceeded their target by 23 fighter jets. Other provinces, regions, and cities also exceeded their donation target by one to ten fighter jets. The people in the above-mentioned regions who have completed the six-month donation plan ahead of schedule or exceeded the target are enthusiastically responding to Chairman Mao's call to "Increase production, practice economy, to support the Chinese People's Volunteers," and are continuing to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement with the vigorous development of the patriotic production increase and economy movement as the center. The people of Shanghai are preparing to comprehensively revise the patriotic pacts around the end of this year and launch a mass New Year and Spring Festival preferential treatment movement; farmers in Haining, Pinghu, Haiyan and other counties in Zhejiang Province are also working intensely on winter rice borer control and digging up rice roots to prepare conditions for increased production next year; many farmers in the Tengxian Special Zone of Shandong Province are also rapidly carrying out winter plowing, and some farmers have also drawn up plans for increasing production and practicing economy.

The people of the seventeen provinces and regions of Plain, Liaodong, Liaoxi, Jilin, Songjiang, Heilongjiang, Rehe, Shaanxi, Ningxia, Xinjiang, Southern Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Yunnan, and Xikang, as well as the municipalities directly under the Central Government and Greater Administrative Regions such as Beijing, Chongqing, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Lüda, Anshan, and Fushun, who are close to completing the donation plan, are actively increasing production and enthusiastically making payments, striving to complete the original donation plan in December.

### 争取今年全部完成捐献武器计划  全国缴款超过三万九千亿  完成捐献计划地区热烈开展增产节约运动

1951-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    争取今年全部完成捐献武器计划
    全国缴款超过三万九千亿
    完成捐献计划地区热烈开展增产节约运动
    【新华社二十九日讯】截至二十九日的统计，全国人民实缴捐献武器的款项已达三万九千一百一十九亿元，可购买战斗机二千六百零七架，占认捐总数四万一千一百三十九亿元的百分之九十五强。
    现在全国提前和超额完成捐献计划的地区，已有青海、川北、川西、浙江、苏北、湖北、河北、甘肃、川东、福建、河南、川南、皖北、绥远、湖南、察哈尔、山西、皖南、山东等十九个省区、内蒙古自治区和天津、上海、南京、武汉、西安、本溪等中央和大行政区直辖市。其中上海市人民超额捐献战斗机八十三架，浙江省人民超额捐献战斗机五十架，河北省人民超额捐献战斗机二十三架，河南省人民超额捐献战斗机二十二架，其他各省、区、市，也超额捐献了战斗机一架至十架不等。上述提前和超额完成半年捐献计划地区的人民，正热烈响应毛主席“增加生产，厉行节约，以支持中国人民志愿军”的号召，以积极开展爱国增产节约运动为中心，继续加强抗美援朝运动。上海市人民准备在今年年底前后全面修订爱国公约，开展群众性的新年和春节的优抚运动；浙江省海宁、平湖、海盐等县农民，也紧张地进行冬季治螟和掘毁稻根的工作，为明年的增产准备条件；山东省滕县专区很多农民，也正迅速地进行冬耕，有些农民并订出了增产节约计划。
    全国接近完成捐献计划的平原、辽东、辽西、吉林、松江、黑龙江、热河、陕西、宁夏、新疆、苏南、江西、广东、广西、贵州、云南、西康等十七个省区以及北京、重庆、广州、沈阳、旅大、鞍山、抚顺等中央和大行政区直辖市的人民，正积极增产，踊跃缴款，争取在十二月份内全部完成原订的捐献计划。
