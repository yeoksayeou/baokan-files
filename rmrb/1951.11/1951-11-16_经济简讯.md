　　Economic Briefs
　　▲State-owned trading departments in North China overfulfilled the October task of purchasing and transporting new cotton. The total amount of ginned cotton purchased by state-owned trading companies and cooperatives in the region in October exceeded the purchase plan by 5.34 percent, and the transportation of raw cotton also exceeded the plan by 3.93 percent.
　　▲The "No. 1124" locomotive crew of the Suihua Locomotive Depot under the Qiqihar Railway Administration in Northeast China has safely traveled 300,000 kilometers without a single accident in nearly four years from January 1948 to the present, creating the highest record of safe locomotive operation in the entire Northeast. Now, they have proposed to strive for the goal of safe operation of 350,000 kilometers.
　　▲Factories and mines under the Northwest Industrial Department have formulated plans for increasing production and practicing economy in the fourth quarter of this year. The value of the increased production and savings planned by the Xi'an Power Plant in the fourth quarter of this year is equivalent to 116 tons of millet. The Northwest Machinery Factory plans to increase production and save the equivalent of 90 tons of millet. All employees of the No. 1 and No. 2 cotton mills under the Northwest Textile Administration Bureau guarantee that the cotton consumption per piece of 20-count yarn will not exceed 410 jin, the power consumption will not exceed 200 degrees, and the 20-hour output will reach 1.1 pounds.
　　▲The amount of cotton collected in Shandong Province in October more than doubled that in September. Many cotton farmers store cotton in cooperatives, and the amount of stored cotton accounts for 24 percent of the total amount of cotton sold and stored.

### 经济简讯

1951-11-16
第2版()
专栏：经济简讯

　　经济简讯
    ▲华北区国营贸易部门超额完成十月份新棉收购和调运任务。全区国营贸易公司和合作社十月份收购皮棉总数超过收购计划百分之五点三四，原棉调运也超过计划百分之三点九三。
    ▲东北齐齐哈尔铁路管理局绥化机务段“一一二四号”机车包乘组从一九四八年一月到现在将近四年中，共安全运行三十万公里，没有发生过一件事故，创造了全东北机车安全运行的最高纪录。现在，他们又提出向安全运行三十五万公里的目标努力。
    ▲西北工业部所属各厂矿已订出今年第四季度的增产节约计划。西安电厂今年第四季度计划增产节约的价值等于一百一十六吨小米。西北机器厂计划增产节约等于九十吨小米的价值。西北纺织管理局所属第一、第二棉纺织厂的全体职工，保证使每件二十支纱用棉量不超过四百一十斤，用电不超过二百度，二十小时产量达到一点一磅。
    ▲山东省十月份收棉量比九月份增加一倍以上。棉农向合作社储存棉花的很多，储棉数量占全部售、储棉花总数的百分之二十四。
