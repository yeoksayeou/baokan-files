Date: 1951-11-08

    Order of the Central People's Government
    The "Provisional Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of Personnel of State Organs by the Central People's Government" have been approved by the 13th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee and are hereby promulgated for implementation.
                                                                                                Chairman Mao Zedong
                                                                                                November 5, 1951

### 中央人民政府命令

1951-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府命令
    中央人民政府任免国家机关工作人员暂行条例，业经中央人民政府委员会第十三次会议批准，特令公布施行。主席  毛泽东
    一九五一年十一月五日
