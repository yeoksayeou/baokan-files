Title: China National Cotton Yarn and Cloth Corporation Decides to Reduce National Yarn and Cloth Prices Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1951-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Since the new cotton came on the market, the China National Cotton Yarn and Cloth Corporation has exceeded its original plan for monthly purchases, and there is no longer a problem with the supply of raw cotton. At the same time, national yarn mills have increased production since November, and the source of yarn and cloth will be more abundant. In order to narrow the scissors difference between industrial and agricultural products and reduce the burden on consumers, the China National Cotton Yarn and Cloth Corporation has decided to reduce the national yarn price by an average of 2.8 percent and the cloth price by 1.8 percent, starting from the 30th.

### 花纱布公司决定从今天起  降低全国纱布价格

1951-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    花纱布公司决定从今天起
    降低全国纱布价格 【新华社二十九日讯】新棉上市以来，中国花纱布公司每月收购数量都超过原定计划，原棉供应已无问题；同时全国纱厂自十一月份起增加生产，纱布来源将更充裕。中国花纱布公司为缩小工农业品的剪刀差和减轻消费者的负担，决定自三十日起将全国纱价平均降低百分之二点八，布价降低百分之一点八。
