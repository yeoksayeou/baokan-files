Title: PLA Air Force, Navy, Public Security Forces, and Other Directly Subordinate Organs Hold Collective Ceremony to Join Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 2

    Units directly under the People's Liberation Army Air Force, Navy, Public Security Forces, and the North China Military Region, etc., have held grand collective ceremonies to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in recent days.
    Units directly under the Air Force held a collective ceremony to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on the evening of the 7th. At the collective membership ceremony held by units directly under the Navy on the 5th, Deputy Commander Wang Hongkun said: The Soviet Navy is the model for the Chinese People's Navy and the direction for the construction of the Chinese People's Navy. We must learn from the great Red Navy to accelerate the construction of a strong people's navy. At the meeting, all attendees unanimously raised their hands to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
    Units of the People's Public Security Forces stationed in the Beijing-Tianjin area also held a membership ceremony on the 7th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 解放军空军海军公安部队等的直属机关部队  举行集体加入中苏友好协会的仪式

1951-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    解放军空军海军公安部队等的直属机关部队
    举行集体加入中苏友好协会的仪式
    中国人民解放军空军、海军、公安部队与华北军区等的直属机关部队，连日分别隆重地举行集体加入中苏友好协会仪式。
    空军直属机关部队于七日晚举行集体加入中苏友好协会仪式。在海军直属机关部队于五日举行集体入会仪式会上，王宏坤副司令员讲话说：苏联海军是中国人民海军的榜样，是中国人民海军建设的方向。我们要向伟大的红海军学习，以加速建设一支强大的人民海军。会上，到会人员一致举手加入中苏友好协会。
    人民公安部队驻京、津区机关部队、亦已于七日举行入会仪式。（新华社）
