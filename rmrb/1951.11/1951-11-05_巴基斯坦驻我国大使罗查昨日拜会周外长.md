Title: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Roza, Visited Foreign Minister Zhou Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Pakistan to China, Roza, visited Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:30 pm on the 4th to discuss matters related to presenting his credentials. Also present were Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Shuliang, Commissioner of the Asian Department.

### 巴基斯坦驻我国大使罗查昨日拜会周外长

1951-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦驻我国大使罗查昨日拜会周外长
    【新华社四日讯】巴基斯坦驻我国首任特命全权大使罗查，在四日下午四时三十分赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者还有外交部交际处处长王倬如和亚洲司专员陈叔亮等。
