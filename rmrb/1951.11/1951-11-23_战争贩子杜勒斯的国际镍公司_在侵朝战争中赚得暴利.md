Title: War Profiteer Dulles' International Nickel Company Profiteers in the Korean War

Author:

Date: 1951-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 20th: The "Wall Street Journal" published reports on the profits of some American companies for the first nine months of this year. These reports show that since the American aggressors launched the war in Korea, the profits of American monopoly capital have increased significantly. Among these monopoly capitals, the most famous is the International Nickel Company, one of whose leaders is the notorious war profiteer Dulles. In the first nine months of 1949, this company headed by Dulles obtained a net profit of 26 million US dollars. After the outbreak of the Korean War, its profits began to rise rapidly, reaching a record number this year: a total net profit of approximately 46 million US dollars in the first nine months, an increase of almost 80 percent compared to the same period in 1949.

The New York "Daily Worker" published an article on November 19, explaining how one of the American monopolists—the war profiteer Dulles—made a fortune from the war of aggression against Korea. The "Daily Worker" article said: The bloody war in Korea has made Dulles, the head of the nickel company who advised President Truman to launch the war, a huge profit. The company's huge profits began to increase rapidly, precisely when the Department of the Army ordered huge quantities of military supplies for the Korean War from the company.

The "Daily Worker" article pointed out that British chemical magnate Sir Melchett and American tycoon Rockefeller jointly preside over the International Nickel Company with Dulles. This company, presided over by the three giants, controls the entire capitalist world's nickel mining and refining operations. The "Daily Worker" pointed out that continuing the Korean War is of great interest to this company and other American monopolists, because it will enable them to make huge profits.

### 战争贩子杜勒斯的国际镍公司  在侵朝战争中赚得暴利

1951-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    战争贩子杜勒斯的国际镍公司
    在侵朝战争中赚得暴利
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社纽约二十日讯：“华尔街日报”发表若干美国公司本年前九个月利润的报告。这些报告表明：自从美国侵略者在朝鲜发动战争以来，美国垄断资本的利润已经大量增加。在这些垄断资本中，最著名的是国际镍公司；该公司的领袖之一，便是臭名昭著的战争贩子杜勒斯。在一九四九年头九个月，以杜勒斯为首的这个公司获得了二千六百万美元的纯利。在侵朝战争爆发以后，它的利润开始迅速上升，在本年内到达了创纪录的数字：头九个月共获纯利约四千六百万美元，比一九四九年同期几乎增加了百分之八十。
    纽约“工人日报”在十一月十九日发表文章，说明美国垄断资本家之一——战争贩子杜勒斯是怎样靠着对朝鲜的侵略战争发财的。“工人日报”的文章说：在朝鲜的血腥的战争，使建议杜鲁门总统发动这次战争的镍公司的首脑杜勒斯赚得巨额的利润。这个公司的巨额利润开始迅速增加，正是在陆军部向这个公司订购供应朝鲜战争的巨量军火的时候。
        “工人日报”的文章指出：英国化学大王迈尔采特爵士和美国巨头洛克菲勒与杜勒斯一同主持国际镍公司。由三巨头主持的这个公司，控制着整个资本主义世界的采镍和炼镍的工作。“工人日报”指出：把朝鲜战争继续下去对于这个公司和美国其它的垄断资本家们都有重大的利益，因为这将使他们赚得巨额的利润。
