Title: PLA Delegation Returns to Beijing from Czechoslovakia After Attending Czechoslovak Army Day Sports Celebration
Author:
Date: 1951-11-26
Page: 4

    Invited to Attend Czechoslovak Army Day Sports Celebration
    PLA Delegation Returns to Beijing from Czechoslovakia
   [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, invited to participate in the Czechoslovak Republic Army Day sports celebration, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th. Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and representatives from various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, totaling more than 20 people, went to the station to welcome them. Czechoslovak Republic Ambassador to China, Weiskopf, and Military Attaché of the Embassy, Prochazka, also went to the station to welcome them.

### 应邀出席捷建军节体育庆祝会  我解放军代表团自捷返京

1951-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀出席捷建军节体育庆祝会
    我解放军代表团自捷返京
   【新华社二十五日讯】应邀参加捷克斯洛伐克共和国建军节体育庆祝会的中国人民解放军代表团，已在二十四日下午返抵北京。赴车站欢迎的有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观、人民革命军事委员会各部代表等共二十多人。捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使魏斯柯普夫和大使馆武官普罗查也到车站欢迎。
