Title: "China Young Pioneers Daily" Launched

Author:

Date: 1951-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:
    "China Young Pioneers Daily" Launched
    [Xinhua News Agency News] The "China Young Pioneers Daily", founded by the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, was launched in Beijing on the 5th. The publication of this newspaper is to cooperate with school education, to educate children and young people with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, so that they will become builders of the new China in the future. The newspaper contains current affairs, cultural and scientific knowledge, activities of the Young Pioneers, fairy tales, novels, pictures and photographs. The newspaper is aimed at young people with a cultural level above the fourth grade of primary school, to help them study and guide the activities of the Young Pioneers.
    "China Young Pioneers Daily" is the team newspaper of the China Young Pioneers. After its launch, the magazine "China Young Pioneers" will cease publication.

### “中国少年报”创刊

1951-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    “中国少年报”创刊
    【新华社讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会创办的“中国少年报”五日在北京创刊。出版这个报纸是为了配合学校教育，以马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想教育少年儿童，使他们将来成为新中国的建设者。该报内容有时事、文化科学知识、少年儿童队的活动、童话、小说和图画、照片。该报以具有小学四年级文化水平以上的少年儿童为对象，帮助他们进行学习，并指导少年儿童队的活动。
    “中国少年报”是中国少年儿童队队报。它创刊后，“中国少年儿童”杂志便停止刊行。
