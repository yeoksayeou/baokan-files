Author:
Date: 1951-11-28
Page: 1

The Hao Jianxiu advanced work method is continuing to be promoted in Shanghai. Currently, 200 female fine yarn workers in 30 public and private yarn factories in Shanghai have basically learned the Hao Jianxiu work method. This is a scene of the Xu Fuzhen group in the fine yarn workshop of the state-owned Shanghai No. 11 Cotton Textile Factory learning the Hao Jianxiu work method.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    郝建秀先进工作法在上海正继续推广中。目前上海已有三十家公私营纱厂的二百名细纱女工基本上学会了郝建秀的工作法。这是国营上海第十一棉纺织厂细纱间的许福珍小组学习郝建秀工作法的情形。
