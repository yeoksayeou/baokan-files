Title: Volunteer Army Combat Heroes Delegation: Du Ping and Others Return to the Korean Front; Cui Jianguo and Others to Report to Various Units of the North China Military Region

Author:

Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Du Ping, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat heroes' National Day observation delegation, and delegates Jia Chenghong, Jiang Shuhua, Shen Shugen, Zhang Honglin, Chen San, Su Zhaodan, and Wang Guiwen, having attended the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, left Beijing on the afternoon of November 6th to return to the Korean front. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and others went to the station to see them off.

Cui Jianguo, Liu Xiuzhen, Wang Wanxia, Guan Chonggui, Qian Xijun, Li Yunhan, Yang Liandi, Guo Chuancai, Chen Chao, Zheng Qi, Huang Shutian, and Xu Henglu, twelve representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat heroes' National Day observation delegation, who attended the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, are now invited by the Headquarters and Political Department of the North China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army to report to various units of the North China Military Region on their combat experience and heroic deeds in Korea.

### 志愿军战斗英雄代表团  杜平等返朝鲜前线  崔建国等赴华北军区各部队作报告

1951-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军战斗英雄代表团
    杜平等返朝鲜前线
    崔建国等赴华北军区各部队作报告
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团团长杜平、代表贾成红、姜淑华、沈树根、张洪林、陈三、苏兆丹、王贵文八人，于列席中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议后，已在十一月六日下午离京重返朝鲜前线。中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志等曾往车站欢送。
    曾列席中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表崔建国、刘秀珍、王万夏、关崇贵、钱锡钧、李云汉、杨连第、郭传才、陈超、郑起、黄树田、徐恒禄十二人，今应人民解放军华北军区司令部、政治部邀请，向华北军区各部队报告他们在朝鲜的作战经验及英勇事迹。
