Title: National Football Tournament Rescheduled for Next Month
Author:
Date: 1951-11-21
Page: 3
　
Xinhua News Agency:

    The 1951 National Football Tournament, originally scheduled to be held in Tianjin on November 25, has been postponed to December 1-9 due to unforeseen circumstances. The tournament organizing committee has been formally established, with Tianjin Mayor Huang Jing as the director, and Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation Preparatory Committee, Huang Zhong, and Director of the Tianjin Sports Branch Association, He Qijun, as deputy directors. The committee is currently actively carrying out various preparatory tasks.

### 全国足球比赛大会改在下月举行

1951-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    全国足球比赛大会改在下月举行
    原定十一月二十五日在天津举行的一九五一年全国足球比赛大会，因故改在十二月一日至九日举行。大会筹备委员会已经正式成立，由天津市市长黄敬任主任，中华全国体育总会筹备委员会副秘书长黄中、天津市体育分会主任何启君任副主任。现该会正积极进行各项筹备工作。　　（新华社）
