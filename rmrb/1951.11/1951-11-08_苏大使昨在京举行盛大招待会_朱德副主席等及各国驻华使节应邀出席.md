Title: Soviet Ambassador Holds Grand Reception in Beijing Yesterday; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Other Dignitaries and Ambassadors to China Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Comrade Roschin, Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, held a grand reception at the Soviet Embassy in Beijing at 5:00 p.m. yesterday to celebrate the 34th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Those invited to attend included: Zhu De, Song Qingling, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Wu Yuzhang, Bo Yibo, Ma Xulun, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Peng Zemin, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Xiruo, Liu Yazi, Luo Ruiqing, Shao Lizi, Li Fuchun, Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Li Lisan, Deng Yingchao, Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, Zhou Yang and other heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 600 people.

Foreign diplomatic envoys to China invited to attend included: Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic, König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Swedish Ambassador Söderblom, Burmese Ambassador Ula Maung, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Minister Renzo L., Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistan Embassy Ahmed Ali, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy Gaul, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Second Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy Tan Taomo, British Government Negotiation Representative Lan Lainuo, Dutch Negotiation Representative Hou Weimin, Norwegian Government Negotiation Representative Golan, and embassy staff from various countries.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏大使昨在京举行盛大招待会  朱德副主席等及各国驻华使节应邀出席

1951-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏大使昨在京举行盛大招待会
    朱德副主席等及各国驻华使节应邀出席
    【新华社七日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻我国大使罗申，为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年纪念，特于七日下午五时在北京苏联大使馆举行盛大招待会。
    应邀与会的有：中华人民共和国中央人民政府副主席朱德、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理董必武、黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及吴玉章、薄一波、马叙伦、陈叔通、司徒美堂、彭泽民、傅作义、张奚若、柳亚子、罗瑞卿、邵力子、李富春、王稼祥、章汉夫、伍修权、李立三、邓颖超、冯文彬、廖承志、周扬等中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派和各人民团体负责人等六百余人。
    各国驻华外交使节应邀出席的有：捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，瑞典大使赛德勃隆，缅甸大使吴拉茂，丹麦公使穆克，瑞士公使任佐立，巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办阿默德·艾礼，波兰大使馆临时代办道定，印度大使馆临时代办高尔、越南代表团临时代办周亮、印尼大使馆二等秘书谭陶墨、英国政府谈判代表蓝来讷、荷兰谈判代表侯卫民、挪威政府谈判代表高兰和各国使馆人员。
    招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。
