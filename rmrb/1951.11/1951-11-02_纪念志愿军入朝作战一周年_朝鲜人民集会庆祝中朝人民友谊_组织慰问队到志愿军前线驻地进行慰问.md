　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 31st — People in various parts of Korea are enthusiastically commemorating the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea to fight. On October 25th, people in various parts of Korea held commemorative meetings, speeches, and get-togethers to celebrate the great friendship between the Chinese and Korean people. People in various parts of Korea sent the Chinese People's Volunteer Army large numbers of banners,慰劳items, and letters of sympathy. Art troupes from the Korean People's Army, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, and factories and villages in various places performed artistic programs at the驻地of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. People in the front-line areas of江原道and黄海道organized慰问teams to comfort Volunteer Army units stationed there. The淮阳郡People's慰问team in江原道alone sent the Volunteer Army more than 30,000慰问items and more than 8,000 letters of sympathy.
　　At the commemorative meetings in various places, the Korean people unanimously praised the glorious victories won by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army over the past year and the great internationalist spirit of the Chinese people. At the commemorative meeting held in江界市,慈江道,李孝淳, chairman of the慈江道People's Committee, called on the Korean people to support the front line with all their strength to ensure that the朝中people's army continue to achieve new victories. A representative of a unit of the Volunteer Army emphasized in his speech that with the strong support of peace-loving people all over the world, the朝中people will surely achieve final victory.
　　In their letters of sympathy to the Volunteer Army, the Korean people expressed their heartfelt gratitude to the Volunteer Army.朴顺姬, a female worker at a factory in定州郡,平安北道, wrote in a letter of sympathy to the Volunteer Army: "Chinese brothers, my mother, sister, and many companions were all massacred by the American invading bandits, and you have avenged me. Our factory was destroyed by the enemy, and we were able to resume production only with your enthusiastic help. Dear Chinese brothers, on the first anniversary of your arrival in our country, I extend to you my highest respect and infinite gratitude."

### 纪念志愿军入朝作战一周年  朝鲜人民集会庆祝中朝人民友谊  组织慰问队到志愿军前线驻地进行慰问

1951-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念志愿军入朝作战一周年
　  朝鲜人民集会庆祝中朝人民友谊
　  组织慰问队到志愿军前线驻地进行慰问
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜各地人民热烈纪念中国人民志愿军到朝鲜作战一周年。在十月二十五日，朝鲜各地人民都举行了纪念大会、演讲会和联欢会，以庆祝中朝人民的伟大友谊。朝鲜各地人民送给中国人民志愿军大批锦旗、慰劳品和慰问信。朝鲜人民军、内务省和各地工厂及农村的文艺工作团都到中国人民志愿军驻地表演了文艺节目。在江原道和黄海道的接近前线地区的人民纷纷组织慰问队到志愿军部队驻地进行慰问。仅江原道淮阳郡人民慰问队就送给志愿军慰问品三万余件和慰问信八千余封。
    在各地的纪念大会上，朝鲜人民一致盛赞中国人民志愿军一年来和朝鲜人民军并肩作战中所获得的辉煌胜利和中国人民的伟大的国际主义精神。在慈江道江界市举行的纪念大会上，慈江道人民委员会委员长李孝淳号召朝鲜人民以全部力量支援前线，以保证朝中人民军队不断取得新的胜利。志愿军某部代表大会发言中强调：朝中人民在全世界爱好和平的人民的有力支援下，一定能取得最后胜利。
    朝鲜人民在给志愿军的慰问信里，对志愿军表示了衷心的感谢。平安北道定州郡某工厂女工朴顺姬在给志愿军的慰问信中写道：“中国的兄弟们，我的母亲、妹妹和许多同伴都给美国侵略强盗屠杀了，你们给我报了仇。我们的工厂给敌人破坏了，在你们的热情帮助下，我们才恢复了生产。亲爱的中国兄弟们，在你们来到我国一周年的时候，我向你们致崇高的敬礼和无限的感谢。”
