Title: National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Continues to Hold General Assembly Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st -- The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its general assembly this afternoon. Those who made special reports and speeches at today's general assembly included Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council; Liang Xi, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Railways; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Trade; Hu Zi'ang, Vice Chairman of the Chongqing Municipal Consultative Committee; Lian Huizhu (Tibetan), Deputy Director of the Panchen's Beijing Office; Wang Zhen, Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region; Chen Yuan, President of Fu Jen Catholic University; Rong Yiren, representative of the industrial and commercial circles; Ai Wu, representative of literary and art workers; Liang Shuming, social figure; Ma Jian, member of the National Committee; and Chen Heqin, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Consultative Committee.
　　The meeting adjourned at 6:30 PM and will continue tomorrow.

### 人民政协全国委员会  昨日继续举行大会

1951-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会
    昨日继续举行大会
    【新华社三十一日讯】中国人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议于今日下午继续举行大会。在今日大会上作专题报告和发言的，有中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，中央人民政府林垦部部长梁希，中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远，中央人民政府贸易部部长叶季壮，重庆市协商委员会副主席胡子昂，班禅驻京办事处副处长联慧珠（藏族），新疆军区司令员王震，辅仁大学校长陈垣，工商界代表荣毅仁，文艺工作者代表艾芜，社会人士梁漱溟，全国委员会委员马坚，南京市协商委员会副主席陈鹤琴。
    会议于下午六时半休会，明日将继续进行。
