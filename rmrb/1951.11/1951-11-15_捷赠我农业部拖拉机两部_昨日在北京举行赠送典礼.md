Title: Czechoslovakia Presents Two Tractors to the Ministry of Agriculture; Presentation Ceremony Held in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-11-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic and state-owned metallurgical industrial institutions presented two tractors and other agricultural machinery to the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government of China. The presentation ceremony was held in Beijing on the afternoon of the 14th. The ceremony was presided over by Mr. Weisskopf, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia to China. Ambassador Weisskopf presented the gifts on behalf of the Czechoslovak government; Mr. Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government of China, accepted the gifts on behalf of our side.

### 捷赠我农业部拖拉机两部  昨日在北京举行赠送典礼

1951-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷赠我农业部拖拉机两部
    昨日在北京举行赠送典礼
     【新华社十四日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国国外贸易部及国有冶金工业机构赠给我国中央人民政府农业部拖拉机两部及其他农业机械。十四日下午在北京举行赠送典礼。典礼由捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫主持。魏斯柯普夫大使代表捷克斯洛伐克政府授礼；我方由我中央人民政府农业部李书城部长代表接受。
