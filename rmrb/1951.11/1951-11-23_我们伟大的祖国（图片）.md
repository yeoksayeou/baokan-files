    Our Great Motherland
    Zhou Afeng, a female worker at the State-owned Shanghai No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, created the anti-knotting method, which resulted in good knots and less waste, and played a significant role in improving production and reducing waste. Recently, after democratic evaluation, Zhou Afeng was awarded first place among warping machine operators at the State-owned Shanghai Cotton Textile Factory. This is a picture of her explaining the anti-knotting method in the workshop.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海第一棉纺织厂挡车女工周阿凤创造了反结头工作法，打结好，回丝少，对于提高生产和减少浪费起了很大作用。最近经过民主测定的结果，周阿凤荣获上海国营棉纺织厂竖锭式络经机挡车工的第一名。这是她正在车间内讲解反结头工作法的情形。
