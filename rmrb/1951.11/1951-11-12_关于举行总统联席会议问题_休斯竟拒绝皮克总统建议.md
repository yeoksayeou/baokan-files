Title: On the Question of Holding a Joint Presidential Meeting, Heuss Actually Rejected President Pieck's Proposal
Author:
Date: 1951-11-12
Page: 4

　　On the Question of Holding a Joint Presidential Meeting
    Heuss Actually Rejected President Pieck's Proposal
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 11th] According to a TASS dispatch from Berlin: The Office of the President of the German Democratic Republic announced that President Pieck's proposal to Federal Republic of Germany President Heuss on November 2nd regarding holding a joint presidential meeting in Berlin to discuss convening an all-German conference to discuss the peaceful reunification of Germany and accelerating the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany, was rejected by Heuss in a letter on November 7th.

### 关于举行总统联席会议问题  休斯竟拒绝皮克总统建议

1951-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　关于举行总统联席会议问题
    休斯竟拒绝皮克总统建议
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：据德意志民主共和国总统办公厅宣布：皮克总统在十一月二日向西德联邦共和国总统休斯所提出的关于在柏林举行总统联席会议商量召开全德会议以讨论和平统一德国及加速缔结对德和约的问题的建议，已于十一月七日为休斯复函拒绝。
