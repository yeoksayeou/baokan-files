Title: The Great Soviet Communist Construction Projects Are Proceeding Victoriously and Rapidly (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-11-06
Page: 4

The Soviet people are successfully constructing the great communist construction projects such as the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station, the Stalingrad Hydroelectric Station, the Volga-Don Canal, and the Turkmen Main Canal. Thousands of enterprises and millions of workers are preparing materials for these great projects. The above picture shows a workshop of the Slavyansk Construction Machinery Factory. On the giant cement mixer, the workers wrote: "A gift from the Slavyansk Construction Machinery Factory to the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station."＊

### 伟大的苏联共产主义建设工程正在胜利迅速进行（图片）

1951-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　伟大的苏联共产主义建设工程正在胜利迅速进行
    苏联人民正在成功地建设着古比雪夫水电站、斯大林格勒水电站、伏尔加河——顿河运河、土库曼大运河等伟大的共产主义建设工程。数以千计的企业，千百万的工人为这些伟大工程准备材料。上图为斯拉乌扬斯克建筑机器工厂的一个车间。在巨大的水泥搅拌机上，工人们写着：“斯拉乌扬斯克建筑机器工厂给古比雪夫水电站的礼物。”＊
