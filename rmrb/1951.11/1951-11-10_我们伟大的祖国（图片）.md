    Our Great Motherland
    After visiting many factories in Beijing and Tianjin, agricultural labor models from various regions have gained a deeper understanding of the importance of the worker-peasant alliance and the greatness of industrial productivity. This is a scene of them visiting Beijing and watching a crane transporting a tractor.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农业劳动模范在北京和天津参观了许多工厂以后，对于工农联盟的重要和工业生产力的伟大有了进一步的认识。这是他们在北京参观搬运机搬运拖拉机的情形。
