Title: 1951 Weaving Work Method Training Class Opens in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 23rd: The National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China has opened the 1951 Weaving Work Method Training Class in Tianjin, according to the decision of the National Weaving Work Method Conference. Trainees are selected from various regions, totaling more than 70 people. Representatives from various regions who participated in the National Weaving Work Method Conference are the teachers of the training class. The opening ceremony of the training class was combined with the summary meeting of the National Weaving Work Method Conference on the 21st of this month. The training class began teaching on the 22nd and will last for one week.

### 一九五一织布工作法训练班已在天津开学

1951-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    一九五一织布工作法训练班已在天津开学
    【新华社天津二十三日电】中国纺织工会全国委员会根据全国织布工作法会议的决定，已在天津开办一九五一织布工作法训练班。学员由各地选派，共约七十多人。参加全国织布工作法会议的各地代表就是训练班的教员。训练班的开学典礼已在本月二十一日和全国织布工作法会议的总结大会合并举行。自二十二日起训练班开始教学，期限为一个星期。
