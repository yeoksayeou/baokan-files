Title: The People of East China Increase Donations to 157.67 Billion Yuan; The People of Shanghai Enthusiastically Focus on the Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement After Completing the Donation Plan; Shandong Completes the Plan to Donate 130 Aircraft; Southern Anhui Region Completes the Plan Ahead of Schedule

Author:

Date: 1951-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 28th. The actual donations of funds for weapons by the people of East China have so far reached 157,678,434,000+ yuan. This amount can buy 1,051 fighter planes, exceeding the original donation plan by 11 percent.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th. The people of all circles in Shanghai have basically and victoriously completed the plan to donate weapons, and are now implementing their surging patriotic enthusiasm into the Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement. As of the 26th of this month, the donations for weapons by the people of the city have reached more than 626.5 billion yuan, which can purchase 417 fighter planes. At present, people from all walks of life in the city are responding to the call of Chairman Mao and the China Association to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. In various tasks centered on "increasing production and practicing economy," they are further promoting patriotic enthusiasm and are preparing to comprehensively review and revise the Patriotic Pacts around the end of the year and the Spring Festival, and to carry out mass New Year and Spring Festival support activities, implementing the seven tasks proposed by the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 28th. Inspired by patriotic enthusiasm, the people of Shandong Province have, as of the 27th of this month, paid 196.1 billion yuan for airplane and artillery donations, completing the plan to donate 130 fighter planes six months ahead of schedule. Now the people of the province are gradually implementing their vigorous patriotic enthusiasm into the Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement. The Teng County Special District Branch of the China Association to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea has issued a notice requiring the people of the special district not to be satisfied with the achievements already made, to prevent slackness, and to combine the current Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement, to generally review and revise the Patriotic Pacts in December, and to promote the further deepening of the Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea Movement. After paying donations, the farmers of Fengcun Township in the Tenth District of Teng County heard Chairman Mao's call for increasing production and practicing economy, immediately formulated a plan to increase production and practice economy, and quickly carried out winter plowing. Now they have plowed more than 92 percent of the township's arable land.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhu, 24th. As of November 22, the people of Southern Anhui Region have paid more than 21.3 billion yuan in donations, completing the original plan to donate 13 fighter planes and two artillery pieces six months ahead of schedule.

### 华东人民缴纳献款增至万五千七百余亿元  上海市人民完成捐献计划后热情贯注增产节约运动  山东献机百三十架计划完成、皖南区提前完成计划

1951-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    华东人民缴纳献款增至万五千七百余亿元
    上海市人民完成捐献计划后热情贯注增产节约运动
    山东献机百三十架计划完成、皖南区提前完成计划
    【新华社上海二十八日电】华东人民实缴捐献武器的献款到目前为止，已有一万五千七百六十七亿八千四百三十四万多元，这个数目可买战斗机一千零五十一架，超过原订捐献计划百分之十一。
    【新华社上海二十七日电】上海各界人民已基本上胜利完成捐献武器的计划，现正将高涨的爱国热情贯彻到增产节约运动中去。截至本月二十六日止，全市人民缴纳捐献武器的献款已达六千二百六十五亿余元，可购买战斗机四百一十七架。现全市各界人民正在响应毛主席和抗美援朝总会的号召，在以“增产节约”为中心的各项工作中，进一步发扬爱国主义的积极性并正准备在年底前后全面检查修订爱国公约、开展群众性的新年春节优抚运动，贯彻中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议所提出的七项任务。
    【新华社济南二十八日电】山东省人民在爱国热情鼓舞下，截至本月二十七日止，已缴纳飞机大炮献款一千九百六十一亿元，提前完成了半年捐献一百三十架战斗机的计划。现该省人民正逐步地把蓬勃的爱国热情贯彻到增产节约运动中去。滕县专区抗美援朝分会已发出通知，要该专区人民不要满足于既得成就，防止松懈，要结合目前正在开展的增产节约运动，在十二月份普遍检查修订爱国公约，促使抗美援朝运动深入一步。滕县第十区冯村乡农民缴纳献款后，听到毛主席关于增产节约的号召，立即订出了增产节约计划，迅速进行冬耕，现已耕完全乡耕地面积的百分之九十二以上。
    【新华社芜湖二十四日电】截至十一月二十二日止，皖南区人民已缴纳献款二百一十三亿多元，提前完成了半年捐献十三架战斗机和两门大炮的原订计划。
