Title: Establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association East China Branch

Author: 
Date: 1951-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 26th: The inaugural meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association East China Branch was held in Shanghai on the 25th. The meeting elected 180 people, including Rao Shushi, Chen Yi, Zeng Shan, Su Yu, Ma Yinchu, Pan Hannian, Liu Changsheng, Xia Yan, Ba Jin, Lu Yudao, Zhang Yun, Sheng Pihua, Chen Jianchen, Xie Xuehong, and Wu Yaozong, as directors, with Rao Shushi as the president and Shu Tong and Chen Wangdao as the vice presidents.

More than 500 representatives from Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations and various circles in East China attended the inaugural meeting. Wei Lixiajin, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Shanghai, was also invited to participate. Chen Wangdao reported on the preparations for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association East China Branch and the work of Sino-Soviet friendship in various parts of East China. He said: Over the past year, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in various parts of East China have made great achievements in publicizing Sino-Soviet friendship and introducing the Soviet Union. To date, there are 251 branches and 6,733 sub-branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in East China, with more than 3.6 million members. The various publicity and education activities carried out by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in various places have generally enhanced the understanding of the Soviet Union among the broad masses of the people in East China. Rao Shushi then spoke, saying: Like the people all over the country, the people of East China have deeply realized from the liberation struggle that the Soviet Union is our best example and most faithful friend. In order to implement Chairman Mao's call, continue to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, increase production, and practice strict economy to support the Chinese People's Volunteers, we must intensify our study of the Soviet people's selfless fighting spirit and learn from the Soviet Union's revolutionary and national construction experience to promote the motherland's national defense and economic construction. Wei Lixiajin, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Shanghai, was invited to speak. On behalf of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, he congratulated the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association East China Branch on its success in the noble work of consolidating Sino-Soviet friendship.

### 中苏友好协会华东总分会成立

1951-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会华东总分会成立
    【新华社上海二十六日电】中苏友好协会华东总分会成立大会二十五日在上海举行。大会选出饶漱石、陈毅、曾山、粟裕、马寅初、潘汉年、刘长胜、夏衍、巴金、卢于道、章蕴、盛丕华、陈建晨、谢雪红、吴耀宗等一百八十人为理事，饶漱石为会长，舒同、陈望道为副会长。
    参加成立大会的有华东各地中苏友好协会代表和华东各界代表五百多人。苏联对外文化协会驻上海代表魏立夏金等也应邀参加。陈望道在会上报告了中苏友好协会华东总分会的筹备经过和华东各地中苏友好工作的情况。他说：一年来，华东各地中苏友好协会在宣传中苏友好和介绍苏联的工作上已有很大成绩。到目前为止，全华东已有中苏友好协会分会二百五十一个，支会六千七百三十三个，会员达三百六十多万人。各地中苏友好协会进行的各项宣传教育工作，使华东广大的人民普遍提高了对苏联的认识。接着由饶漱石讲话。他说：华东人民和全国各地人民一样，从解放斗争中，深深体会到苏联是我们最好的榜样和最忠实的朋友。为了贯彻毛主席的号召，继续加强抗美援朝工作，增加生产，厉行节约，以支持中国人民志愿军，我们就必须加紧学习苏联人民忘我的战斗精神，学习苏联的革命和建国经验，以推动祖国的国防和经济建设。苏联对外文化协会驻上海代表魏立夏金应邀讲话。他代表苏联对外文化协会祝贺中苏友好协会华东总分会在巩固中苏友谊的崇高的工作中获得成功。
