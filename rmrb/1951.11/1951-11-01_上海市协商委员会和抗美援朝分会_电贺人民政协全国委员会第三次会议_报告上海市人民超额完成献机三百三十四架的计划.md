Title: The Shanghai Municipal Consultative Committee and the Resist America, Aid Korea Branch Congratulate the National Committee of the CPPCC on its Third Session, Reporting that the People of Shanghai Have Exceeded the Planned Donation of 334 Airplanes
Author:
Date: 1951-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference received a congratulatory telegram today from the Shanghai Municipal Consultative Committee and the Shanghai Municipal Resist America, Aid Korea Branch. The telegram stated:

    In order to celebrate the great victory of the Resist America, Aid Korea struggle over the past year, accelerate support for the front lines, and celebrate the opening of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, the people of Shanghai have today exceeded the planned donation of 334 airplanes, remitting over 510 billion yuan. In the future, we will certainly resolutely implement the resolutions on the Resist America, Aid Korea work passed by the Third Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, unite closely with the people of the whole country, expand the patriotic production increase and economy campaign, promote patriotic pacts, and transport more airplanes, artillery, and huge manpower and materials to the front lines, and struggle to thoroughly defeat the aggression of American imperialism and defend people's victory and lasting peace!

### 上海市协商委员会和抗美援朝分会  电贺人民政协全国委员会第三次会议  报告上海市人民超额完成献机三百三十四架的计划

1951-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    上海市协商委员会和抗美援朝分会
    电贺人民政协全国委员会第三次会议
    报告上海市人民超额完成献机三百三十四架的计划
    【新华社三十日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议，今日收到上海市协商委员会和上海市抗美援朝分会的贺电。电文称：
    上海各界人民为了庆祝一年来抗美援朝斗争的伟大胜利，加紧支援前线，庆祝人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议的开幕，在今天超额完成了预定捐献飞机三百三十四架的缴款——五千一百亿余元。今后，我们一定坚决贯彻人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议所通过的关于抗美援朝工作的决议，与全国人民紧密地团结在一起，扩大爱国增产节约运动，推行爱国公约，输送更多的飞机、大炮和巨大的人力、物力到前线去，为彻底打败美帝国主义的侵略和保卫人民胜利与持久和平而斗争！
