Title: Beijing Industrial and Commercial Circles Hold Mobilization Meeting, Decide to Broadly Launch Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement and Strengthen the Study of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1951-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch:

    In response to Chairman Mao's call at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) to increase production, practice economy, and support the Chinese People's Volunteers, and to implement the various resolutions of the CPPCC session, Beijing's industrial and commercial circles held a mobilization meeting on the 8th for the chairmen and business committee members of various industries, markets, districts, and suburban areas. The meeting decided to focus on "continuing to strengthen the Resist-America, Aid-Korea movement," "broadly launching the patriotic increase production and practice economy movement," and "strengthening the study of Mao Zedong Thought and promoting ideological remolding movement" as the central tasks for the future.
    Regarding "continuing to strengthen the Resist-America, Aid-Korea movement," the meeting decided that in addition to vigorously conducting patriotic and internationalist education, conscientiously reviewing and revising patriotic pacts, and strengthening the work of giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and military personnel, efforts should be made to complete the original plan of donating 40 fighter planes in six months by the end of November.
    Regarding "broadly launching the patriotic increase production and practice economy movement," the meeting decided on six measures: First, clarify the idea of relying on the working class, resolutely rely on the workers, unite the workers, give full play to the enthusiasm and creativity of workers, technicians, and staff, commend and reward production models at any time, and actively improve the workers' welfare undertakings with the development of production. Second, tap the potential capacity in production, improve business management, increase production, and improve quality. Third, establish and strengthen the quota system, strive to save raw materials, reduce consumption and waste, and reduce costs. Fourth, strengthen capital management, accelerate capital turnover, do a good job in revaluing assets, strive to clean up stagnant materials, promote the sale of backlog products, utilize semi-finished products, and check and eliminate the phenomenon of capital accumulation, and gradually move towards economic accounting system. Fifth, strengthen the democratization of enterprise management, establish and strengthen the labor-capital consultation meeting system, and establish a rationalization proposal system. Sixth, strengthen labor-capital unity, develop urban-rural trade under the leadership of the state-owned economy, and serve the production of workers, peasants, cities, and villages. In order to ensure the implementation of the above measures, the meeting also decided that various industries and markets should organize patriotic increase production and practice economy committees to study, lead, and promote this movement. All factories and stores should formulate detailed increase production and practice economy plans based on specific circumstances and establish a regular inspection system.
    Regarding "strengthening the study of Mao Zedong Thought and promoting the ideological remolding movement," the meeting decided to take the various reports and resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference as the direction and important guidelines for future efforts, and various industries, markets, factories, and stores should launch study and discussion. At the same time, the study of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong should be carried out in an organized and led manner.

### 北京市工商界举行动员大会  决定广泛开展增产节约运动并加强对于毛泽东思想的学习

1951-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市工商界举行动员大会
    决定广泛开展增产节约运动并加强对于毛泽东思想的学习
    【新华社八日讯】北京市工商界为响应中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议上毛主席关于增加生产、厉行节约、支援中国人民志愿军的号召，并贯彻实现人民政协这次会议的各项决议，于八日举行了全市各行业、各市场、各区、各郊区主任委员和业务委员的动员大会。会上决定以“继续加强抗美援朝运动”、“广泛开展爱国增产节约运动”及“加强毛泽东思想的学习、推动思想改造运动”为今后中心工作。
    在“继续加强抗美援朝运动”方面，会议决定除大力进行爱国主义与国际主义教育，认真检查并修订爱国公约，加强优待烈士家属、军人家属的工作外，争取在十一月底，全部完成半年捐献战斗机四十架的原订计划。
    在“广泛开展爱国增产节约运动”方面，会议决定六项办法：第一、明确依靠工人阶级的思想，坚决地依靠工人，团结工人，充分发挥工人、技师与职员的积极性和创造性，随时表扬和奖励生产模范，并随着生产的发展，积极地改善工人的福利事业。第二、发挥生产中的潜在能力，改善经营管理，增加生产，提高质量。第三、建立与加强定额制度，努力节省原料，减少消耗浪费，减低成本。第四、加强资金管理，加速资金周转，做好重估财产工作，努力清理呆滞材料，推销积压的产品，利用半成品，并检查与消灭资金积压现象，逐步走向经济核算制。第五、加强企业管理的民主化，建立与加强劳资协商会议制度，树立合理化建议制度。第六、加强劳资团结，在国营经济领导下，开展城乡贸易，为工人、农民，城市、乡村的生产服务。为保证上述办法贯彻实现，会议并决定各行业、各市场都要组织爱国增产节约委员会研究领导与推动这一运动。各工厂、各商店都要依据具体情况、订立详细的增产节约计划并建立经常的检查制度。
    关于“加强毛泽东思想的学习、推动思想改造运动”方面，会议决定以中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的各项报告和决议，做为今后的努力方向和重要指针，各行业、各市场、各工厂、各商店都要展开学习讨论。同时有组织、有领导地进行毛泽东选集的学习。
