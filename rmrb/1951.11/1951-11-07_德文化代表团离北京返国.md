Title: German Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency) The entire members of the Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Rentmeister, Harfmann and Meckisch, left Beijing by plane for home on the 1st after a visit and tour of more than a month in New China. Those who went to the airport to see them off were: Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director, Wu Qing, Specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Gruttner, Counselor of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China.

    During their stay in Beijing, in addition to signing the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the delegation also participated in our National Day celebrations, visited cultural and artistic institutions and schools, watched famous song and dance dramas and movies, visited factories in Beijing and visited the new rural areas in the suburbs of Beijing after the land reform. The delegation visited and toured Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing and Tianjin, and returned to Beijing on October 30. The delegation was warmly welcomed by our government agencies and people from all walks of life in Beijing and other places.

### 德文化代表团离北京返国

1951-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    德文化代表团离北京返国
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：德意志民主共和国文化代表团全体团员伦特迈斯特、哈福曼及麦契希在新中国作了一个多月的访问与参观后，一日乘机离北京返国。前往机场欢送者有：政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、副局长陈忠经、外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青及德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团参赞葛鲁特纳等。
    代表团在北京时，除了签订了中德文化合作协定外，还参加了我国的国庆典礼，访问了文化艺术机关和学校，观看了著名的歌舞剧和电影，参观了北京的工厂和访问了京郊实行土地改革后的新农村。代表团曾到上海、杭州、南京和天津等地访问与参观，于十月三十日返北京。代表团在北京及各地都受到我政府机关及各界人民的热烈欢迎。
