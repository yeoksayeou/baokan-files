Title: Supplementary List of Members of the World Peace Council and Executive Bureau
Author:
Date: 1951-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 10th: According to TASS, the Vienna meeting of the World Peace Council passed the following supplementary list of members of the World Peace Council and the Executive Bureau of the Council on the 7th:

Australia: Reverend Dick, Chairman of the Australian National Peace Committee.

Burma: Tekin Lun, Member of Parliament; Wu La, Writer; Saya Kang, Writer.

Bolivia: Mancilla, Dean of the College of Engineering, University.

Brazil: Silva, Textile Industrialist; General Cardoso, Editor of "Liberation" Magazine.

Chile: Poblete, Professor and Leader of the Chilean Women's Movement.

China: Liu Qingshan, Farmer.

Cyprus: Lyssaridis, Chairman of the Cyprus Peace Committee.

Egypt: Mrs. Nabarawi, Chairman of the Egyptian Women's Federation.

United Kingdom: Watkins, Vice-Chairman of the British Peace Committee; Reverend Nicol; Chaffiel, Chairman of the British Peace Committee; Gore, Teacher.

Guatemala: Luis-Cardoza, Writer and Former Diplomat.

Netherlands: Dahlen, Pastor of the Dutch Reformed Church.

India: Anand, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council; Saifuddin, Leader of the Indian National Congress and Chairman of the All-India Peace Council; Chanda, Writer and Secretary-General of the All-India Peace Council; Kapoor, Film Actor and Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council.

Iran: Lankarani, Publisher of the Iranian Peace Association's newspaper, "Maslahat."

Italy: Longobardi, Former Minister, Member of Parliament, and Economist; Suklat, Writer; Parello, Dockworker from Genoa; Mrs. Novello, Leader of the Farmers' Group in the Montescalioso district.

Martinique: Sisale, Member of Parliament.

Pakistan: Iftikharuddin, Member of the Pakistan Constituent Assembly, Publisher of the Pakistan Times and Today.

Poland: Ignar, Professor, Deputy Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the United Peasants' Party; Wasilkovska, Deputy Chairman of the Polish Women's League.

Turkey: Hikmet, Novelist, Poet.

Vietnam: Le Dinh Tham, Doctor, Former Chairman of the Central Buddhist Association, Current Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front; Xie Guangbao (transliteration), Professor of Physics at the National University of Vietnam; Hong Xuan Tui (transliteration), Writer.

Members of the Executive Bureau: Gott (France), Casanova (France), Sereni (Italy), Longobardi (Italy), Korneichuk (USSR), Dr. Saifuddin (India), Billinger (Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth).

Secretary of the Secretariat: Gore (UK).

### 世界和平理事会和执行局委员补充名单

1951-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会和执行局委员补充名单
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社维也纳讯：世界和平理事会维也纳会议在七日通过世界和平理事会理事和理事会执行局委员的补充名单如下：
    澳大利亚：狄克牧师，澳大利亚全国和平委员会主席。
    缅甸：德钦伦，议员；吴拉，作家；萨雅冈，作家。
    玻利维亚：曼西拉，大学工学院院长。
    巴西：席尔瓦，纺织工业家；卡多索将军，“解放”杂志编辑。
    智利：波布列特，教授和智利妇女运动的领袖。
    中国：刘青山，农民。
    塞浦路斯：李沙里迪斯，塞浦路斯和平委员会主席。
    埃及：娜巴拉薇夫人，埃及妇女联合会主席。
    英国：瓦金士，英国和平委员会副主席；尼柯尔牧师；夏菲尔，英国和平委员会主席；戈尔，教师。
    危地马拉：路易斯—卡多萨，作家和以前的外交官。
    荷兰：达伦，荷兰归正教会牧师。
    印度：安纳德，全印和平理事会副主席；赛福丁，印度国大党领袖兼全印和平理事会主席；钱达，作家兼全印和平理事会秘书长；卡普尔，电影演员兼全印和平理事会副主席。
    伊朗：兰卡拉尼，伊朗和平协会机关报“马斯拉哈特报”的发行人。
    意大利：龙巴迪，前部长，议员及经济学家；苏克拉特，作家；派莱托，热那亚码头工人；诺维罗夫人，蒙特斯卡利奥索区农民团体领袖。
    马提尼克：西沙莱，议员。
    巴基斯坦：伊夫提卡鲁丁，巴基斯坦制宪议会议员，巴基斯坦时报和今日报发行人。
    波兰：伊格纳尔，教授，统一农民党中央执行委员会副主席；华西尔考夫斯卡，波兰妇女联盟副主席。
    土耳其：希克梅特，小说家，诗人。
    越南：黎廷探，医生，前任中央佛教协会主席，现任越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席；谢光宝
    （译音），国立越南大学物理学教授；洪宣退（译音），作家。
    执行局委员：戈特（法国）、卡桑诺伐（法国）、塞伦尼（意大利）、龙巴迪（意大利）、考涅楚克（苏联）、赛福丁博士（印度）、比林格尔（世界民主青年联盟主席）。
    秘书处秘书：戈尔（英国）。
