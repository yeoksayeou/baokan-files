Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League School Holds Fourth Graduation Ceremony; Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Attends the Ceremony and Accepts Graduates' Dedication to Chairman Mao and the CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-11-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: The Central Committee School of the China New Democratic Youth League held its fourth graduation ceremony on the 13th of this month. Commander-in-Chief Zhu De attended the ceremony. He accepted the dedication from all graduating students to Chairman Mao and the CCP Central Committee, and encouraged the students to actively participate in the Resist America, Aid Korea, Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement, and to conscientiously carry out patriotic propaganda and education. At the meeting, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and Principal of the Central Committee School, pointed out in his speech that the students should do a good job after returning, continue to study hard, and combine the theory they have learned with practical work.
    Note: The fourth class of graduates from the Central Committee School of the Communist Youth League totaled 723 people, most of whom were propaganda ministers of county-level Youth League committees or equivalent propaganda cadres. The study period lasted nearly six months. They successively studied courses such as the Communist Manifesto, Our Great Motherland, the Eight Standards of Communist Party Members, as well as the basic theories and propaganda work of the League. After graduation, most of the students returned to their original places to take up the propaganda and education work of the League.

### 青年团中央团校举行第四期学员毕业典礼  朱总司令参加典礼接受学员给毛主席和中共中央的献词

1951-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央团校举行第四期学员毕业典礼
    朱总司令参加典礼接受学员给毛主席和中共中央的献词
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央团校在本月十三日举行第四期学员毕业典礼。朱总司令参加了典礼。他接受了全体毕业学员给毛主席和中共中央的献词，勉励学员积极参加抗美援朝、增产节约运动，认真地进行爱国主义宣传教育。会上青年团中央委员会书记、中央团校校长冯文彬在讲话中指出，学员回去后要做好工作，继续努力学习，要把所学到的理论和实际工作相结合起来。
    按：青年团中央团校第四期毕业学员共七百二十三人，大部分是团县委宣传部长或相当团县委宣传部长的宣传工作干部。学习期间将近半年。他们先后学习了共产党宣言、我们伟大的祖国、共产党员的八项标准以及团的基本理论和宣传业务等课程。毕业后，大部分学员都回原地担任团的宣传教育工作。
