Title: Resolutions of the Fujian People's Representative Conference: Continue to Strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 16th. The Second Session of the Second Fuzhou People's Representative Conference was held from the 8th to the 12th. The meeting focused on discussing three central tasks: continuing to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea work, promoting the production increase and economy drive, and extensively carrying out the ideological remolding movement. All representatives unanimously agreed that these three central tasks fully conformed to the specific situation of Fuzhou and the urgent demands of the city's people, and resolved to strive for their thorough realization.

The meeting formulated specific implementation plans for each central task and decided to distribute the important documents of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to people from all walks of life in order to promote a broad and in-depth study among the city's people, so that the principles and methods of continuing to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement could be known to every household. The meeting decided to vigorously advocate and promote the patriotic production increase and economy drive among the city's people, calling on suburban farmers to carry out winter plowing in a timely manner and make all preparations for large-scale production next year. The meeting also decided to establish a General Study Committee within the Fuzhou People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee to organize intellectuals to study Mao Zedong Thought and extensively carry out the ideological remolding movement.

### 福州人民代表会议决议  继续加强抗美援朝运动

1951-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    福州人民代表会议决议
    继续加强抗美援朝运动
    【新华社福州十六日电】福州市第二届第二次各界人民代表会议在八日到十二日举行。会议着重讨论了继续加强抗美援朝工作，推动增产节约运动和广泛开展思想改造运动等三大中心工作。全体代表一致认为这三项中心工作完全符合福州市具体情况和全市人民的迫切要求，决定为其彻底实现而奋斗。
    会议对各项中心工作都作出了具体的实施计划，并决定将中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的重要文件印发给各界人民，推动全市人民进行一次广泛而深入的学习，使继续加强抗美援朝运动的道理和办法能够家喻户晓。会议决定在全市人民中大力提倡和推动爱国增产节约运动，号召郊区农民及时冬耕，做好明年进行大生产的一切准备工作。会议还决定在市各界人民代表会议协商委员会内设立总学习委员会，组织知识分子学习毛泽东思想，广泛开展思想改造的运动。
