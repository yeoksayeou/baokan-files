Title: Harbin China Standard Pencil Company: Tinplate Scrap Used to Replace Copper for Copper Ferrules
Author:
Date: 1951-11-29
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    Harbin China Standard Pencil Company
    Tinplate Scrap Used to Replace Copper for Copper Ferrules
    Editor: Copper is a necessary material for national defense and economic construction, but there are still undesirable wasteful phenomena in some places. For example, the copper ferrules on erasers of pencils are a relatively concrete example. According to the current production volume of eraser-tipped pencils in the pencil industry nationwide, 20 tons of copper skin are consumed every month, which amounts to 240 tons per year (enough to load eight cargo trucks carrying 30 tons each). If this huge number is saved and invested in national defense or economic construction, it will play a significant role.
    The employees of Harbin China Standard Pencil Company, in order to respond to Chairman Mao's call for increased production and economy, have adopted waste tinplate (i.e., scrap iron corners cut from enamel factories) to replace copper skin after much research. Trial production results show that in terms of practicality and aesthetics, it is no less than that of copper skin. It is hoped that manufacturers using copper ferrules and copper buckles to make pencils will try to produce them, and put the saved copper skin into more useful places.
                Harbin China Standard Pencil Company

### 哈尔滨中国标准铅笔公司  制铜箍已用废马口料代替铜皮

1951-11-29
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    哈尔滨中国标准铅笔公司
    制铜箍已用废马口料代替铜皮
    编辑同志：铜皮是国防建设和经济建设事业的必要物资，但是有的地方还存在着不应有的浪费现象。如橡皮头铅笔上面镶的铜箍就是一个比较具体的例子。按目前全国各铅笔业的橡皮头铅笔制造量来计算，每月须耗用铜皮二十吨，全年即须二百四十吨（可装载重三十吨的货车八辆）。如把这个巨大的数字节省下来投到国防建设或经济建设上面去，将会起很大的作用。
    哈尔滨中国标准铅笔公司的职工，为了响应毛主席增产节约的号召，经过多方的钻研，已经采用了废马口料（即搪磁工厂裁下的废铁角）代替铜皮，试制结果，在实用及美观方面，均不次于用铜皮制造的。希望使用铜箍以及铜扣制造铅笔的厂商试制，把节省下来的铜皮投到更有用的地方去。
                哈尔滨中国标准铅笔公司
