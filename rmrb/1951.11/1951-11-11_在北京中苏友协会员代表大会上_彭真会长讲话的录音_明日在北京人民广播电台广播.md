Title: Recording of Comrade Peng Zhen's Speech at Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Member Representative Assembly to be Broadcast Tomorrow on Beijing People's Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1951-11-11
Page: 1

　　Recording of Comrade Peng Zhen's Speech at Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Member Representative Assembly to be Broadcast Tomorrow on Beijing People's Broadcasting Station

　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has scheduled to broadcast the recording of Comrade Peng Zhen, Chairman of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's summary speech at the First Member Representative Assembly, the work plan for the coming year, and the resolutions of the assembly from 6:30 PM to 7:30 PM on Monday, November 12th, on the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station (850 kHz, 1080 kHz). All levels of branches are requested to organize members to listen at the designated time, and representatives of each branch are expected to provide supplementary communication to the members.

### 在北京中苏友协会员代表大会上  彭真会长讲话的录音  明日在北京人民广播电台广播

1951-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　在北京中苏友协会员代表大会上
    彭真会长讲话的录音
    明日在北京人民广播电台广播
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会定于十一月十二日（星期一）晚六时半到七时半假北京人民广播电台（八五○千周、一○八○千周）广播北京市中苏友好协会第一届会员代表大会彭真会长总结讲话的录音、今后一年工作计划及大会决议。希各级支会一律届时组织会员收听，并望各支会代表向会员作补充传达。
