Title: Telegram from the CPC Central Committee to the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 1

    Telegram from the CPC Central Committee to the CPSU Central Committee
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks):
    On the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, we extend our warm congratulations to Comrade Stalin and all comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks). We wish you even more brilliant victories in the struggle for lasting world peace and in the great cause of communist construction. We wish the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union to be increasingly consolidated and developed.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    Beijing, November 5, 1951

### 中共中央致联共中央电

1951-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致联共中央电苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会：
    值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年之际，谨向斯大林同志和苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会全体同志致以热烈的祝贺。祝你们在争取世界持久和平的斗争中与伟大的共产主义建设事业中获得更辉煌的胜利。祝中苏两国伟大的友谊日益巩固与发展。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五一年十一月五日北京
