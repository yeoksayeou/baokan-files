Author:

Our Great Motherland
    The Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement first unfolded in the Northeast and many factories have already overfulfilled their targets. This is a scene of the workers of the Sixth Workshop of the Northeast Second Machine Factory, who are summarizing the achievements of the third quarter of this year. On average, each worker increased production and practiced economy equivalent to thirty tons of grain, equal to the entire year's production of twelve farmers. Now they are signing collective contracts, preparing to create more wealth for the country in the fourth quarter.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    增产节约运动在东北首先开展起来，并且已经有许多工厂超额完成了任务。这是东北第二机器厂第六车间的职工，他们总结本年第三季度的成绩，平均每个职工增产节约三十吨粮食，等于十二个农民的全年生产量。现在他们正在签订集体合同，准备在第四季度中为国家创造更多的财富。
