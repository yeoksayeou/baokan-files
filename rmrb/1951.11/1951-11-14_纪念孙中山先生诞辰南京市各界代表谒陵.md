Title: Nanjing Representatives From All Circles Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum to Commemorate His Birth Anniversary
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-14
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 13th: Representatives from all circles of Nanjing paid respects at Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum on the 12th to commemorate the 85th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. The participants in the mausoleum visit included Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing, Liu Shuzhou, member of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Chen Heqin, Vice Chairman of the Consultative Committee of the Nanjing Municipal People's Representative Conference, and more than 40 representatives from various democratic parties and all walks of life.

### 纪念孙中山先生诞辰南京市各界代表谒陵

1951-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　纪念孙中山先生诞辰南京市各界代表谒陵
    【新华社南京十三日电】南京市各界人民代表在十二日晋谒中山陵，纪念孙中山先生八十五周年诞辰。参加谒陵仪式的有南京市人民政府副市长李乐平，中共南京市委员会委员刘述周，市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席陈鹤琴，各民主党派和各界人民代表共四十多人。
