Our Great Motherland
　　The safety equipment in the mines and factories of New China is being strengthened and improved day by day, and the workers' production enthusiasm and political zeal are increasing. This is a scene of workers at the Fengfeng Coal Mine going to work. They are taking a security vehicle to go down the mine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的矿山和工厂的安全设备正在一天天加强和改进着，工人们的生产积极性和政治热情日益高涨。这是峰峰煤矿的工人上班的情形，他们正坐着保安车准备下矿工作。
