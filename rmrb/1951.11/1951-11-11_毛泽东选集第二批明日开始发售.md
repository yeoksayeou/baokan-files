Title: Mao Zedong Selected Works Second Batch to be Sold Tomorrow
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-11
Page: 1

    Mao Zedong Selected Works
    Second Batch to be Sold Tomorrow
    [This newspaper dispatch] "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" (Volume I), second batch, will be available for sale starting on the 12th. In addition to the four retail departments of Xinhua Bookstore and the three retail departments of China Book Distribution Company, the Fifties Publishing House, Dazhong Bookstore, etc., will also be selling them on the same day.
                        (Yan Hua)

### 毛泽东选集第二批明日开始发售

1951-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东选集
    第二批明日开始发售
    【本报讯】“毛泽东选集”（第一卷）第二批十二日开始发售。这次除新华书店四个门市部和中国图书发行公司三个门市部发售外，五十年代出版社、大众书店等也在同一天发售。
                        （延华）
