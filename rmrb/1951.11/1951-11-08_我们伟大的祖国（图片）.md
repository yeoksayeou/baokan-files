Our Great Motherland
	On the vast plains of the Huai River, land that used to suffer from floods and "nine years of harvest failure out of ten" has seen a general bumper harvest this year due to the completion of the first phase of the People's Government's project to harness the Huai River. This is a scene of peasants in Jiangjia Village, Lingbi County, northern Anhui Province, busy sifting mung beans.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在辽阔的淮河平原上，过去时常遭受水灾以致“十年九不收”的土地，今年因为人民政府根治淮河第一期工程完成，已普遍丰收了。这是皖北灵璧县姜家庄的农民正在忙着筛绿豆。
