Title: Cold Wave Reaches Xinjiang Area of ​​China; Temperatures in North China Will Drop Significantly Tonight
Author:
Date: 1951-11-21
Page: 1

    Cold Wave Reaches Xinjiang Area of ​​China
    Temperatures in North China Will Drop Significantly Tonight

    [This newspaper's dispatch] At 10:00 AM on the 20th, the Central Meteorological Observatory issued a cold wave warning stating: The first relatively strong cold wave of this winter has reached the Xinjiang area of China and is expected to reach North China and Northwest China on the 21st, and the Yangtze River basin on the 22nd. On the evening of the 21st, temperatures in various parts of North China will begin to drop significantly, with minimum temperatures possibly reaching below minus ten degrees Celsius.

### 寒潮到达我国新疆一带  今晚华北各地温度将显著下降

1951-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    寒潮到达我国新疆一带
    今晚华北各地温度将显著下降
    【本报讯】二十日上午十时中央气象台发布寒潮消息说：本年冬季第一次比较强大的寒潮已经到达我国新疆一带，预计二十一日将到华北和华西，二十二日可到长江流域。二十一日晚，华北各地温度将开始显著下降，最低温度可到摄氏零下十度以下。
