Title: Tianjin State-Owned Jianhua United Transport Company and Other Units: Negligence Led to Fire of Dangerous Goods, Over 500 People Poisoned, Losses Exceeding 800 Million Yuan
Author:
Date: 1951-11-21
Page: 6

Tianjin State-Owned Jianhua United Transport Company and other units were negligent in handling dangerous goods, resulting in a poisoning incident involving over 500 people and losses exceeding 800 million yuan. After the Tianjin State-Owned Jianhua United Transport Company transported a certain dangerous drug to Tianjin, it failed to clarify the nature of the goods. It was stored in a warehouse as a general chemical, along with flammable materials such as ostrich feathers and the poisonous substance abiatic acid. After the Tianjin Import and Export Company took over the item, it noticed the words "Strictly Prohibit Fire" on the wooden box, but it did not raise any alarm. It did not handle it according to the "Regulations for the Handling of Dangerous Goods" but was eager to allocate it to the Industrial Construction Materials Company. The Industrial Construction Materials Company found that the item was deteriorating but did not immediately accept it; however, they also did not pay attention to its specifications, performance, or dangers. Later, under the urging of higher authorities, the Industrial Construction Materials Company hired a truck to receive the materials on May 11th of this year. Due to the poor original packaging, powder had already leaked out; furthermore, the truck was overloaded. During the workers' bundling, friction from the rope caused a fire. According to preliminary statistics, the total loss amounted to 857,927,360 yuan. In addition, during the firefighting efforts, the abiatic acid stored in the warehouse decomposed due to water, releasing toxic gas, which poisoned 91 firefighters and 456 nearby residents.

The occurrence of this incident shows that the degree of negligence on the part of the leadership of the three companies is very serious, and they seriously lack a spirit of high responsibility for state property. The Tianjin Municipal People's Government People's Supervision Committee has given warnings or demerits to the relevant personnel of the three companies, according to the severity of their responsibilities.

### 天津国营建华联运公司等单位  麻痹大意致使危险物品失火  五百余人中毒损失八亿余元

1951-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    天津国营建华联运公司等单位
    麻痹大意致使危险物品失火
    五百余人中毒损失八亿余元
    天津市国营建华联运公司等单位对待危险物品麻痹大意，造成五百余人中毒事件，并损失八亿余元。天津国营建华联运公司将某种危险药品运至天津后，没有弄清货物的性质，当作一般化学药品存入仓库，并与易燃物鸵毛等及毒品亚比酸放在一起。天津市进出口公司接管此项物品后，发现木箱上标有“严禁火气”字样，但也没有引起警惕，没有按照“危险品处理办法”加以处理，只急于拨给工业建筑器材公司。工业建筑器材公司发现该项物品正在变质，没有即时接受；但对它的规格、性能及危险性如何，也没有加以注意。后经上级催促，工业建筑器材公司于今年五月十一日雇车来接收是项物资。由于原包装不良，已经漏出粉末；又加车上装货太多，在工人捆扎时因拉绳摩擦，引起火灾。据初步统计，共损失人民币八亿五千七百九十二万七千三百六十元。又因在救火时，仓库所存亚比酸被水分解，放出毒气，使九十一名防火人员及附近居民四百五十六人中毒。
    这一事件的发生，说明了该三公司领导方面的麻痹大意的程度是很严重的，他们十分缺乏对国家财产高度负责的精神。天津市人民政府人民监察委员会对该三公司有关人员，已分别责任轻重，予以警告或记过等处分。
                            政务院人民监察委员会通讯组
