Title: Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej's Fiftieth Birthday - Telegram of Congratulations from the CPC Central Committee and Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. November -- November 8th is the fiftieth birthday of Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej on November 7th to express his congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej:

On the occasion of your fiftieth birthday, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you on behalf of the Communist Party of China and myself. I wish you good health.

Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mao Zedong
November 7, 1951

### 乔治乌—德治同志五十寿辰  中共中央毛主席致电祝贺

1951-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治同志五十寿辰
    中共中央毛主席致电祝贺
    【新华社七日讯】十一月八日是罗马尼亚工人党总书记乔治乌—德治同志的五十寿辰。中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席特在十一月七日致电乔治乌—德治同志，表示祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的乔治乌—德治同志：
    当您五十寿辰之际，我谨代表中国共产党和我个人向您致热烈的祝贺。并祝您健康。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席    毛泽东
    一九五一年十一月七日
