Our Great Motherland
Li Shunda, the nationally renowned model agricultural laborer, and agricultural labor models from all over the country who participated in the Second National Day celebration, visited the state-owned farm in the eastern suburbs of Beijing. Here, veterinarian Li Qifu is holding a fat piglet and explaining breeding methods to the agricultural labor models.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国著名的农业劳动模范李顺达和参加第二届国庆节的全国各地农业劳动模范，在北京东郊参观国营农场。这是兽医李其富同志抱着肥胖的小猪，正在向农业劳动模范们讲解饲养法。
