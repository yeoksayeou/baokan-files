    Our Great Motherland
    The Wild Goose Dance is one of the dances of the Mongolian people. There is a Mongolian proverb: "The wild goose symbolizes peace." Therefore, this dance also expresses the Mongolian people's love of peace and love of the brightness of the motherland. This is a pose of Jia Zuoguang, a member of the Inner Mongolia Art Troupe, performing the Wild Goose Dance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    雁舞是蒙古族人民的舞蹈之一。内蒙古民间有一句谚语：“鸿雁象征和平”。因此这个舞蹈又表示蒙古族人民热爱和平热爱祖国光明的意思。这是内蒙文艺工作团团员贾作光表演雁舞的姿势。
