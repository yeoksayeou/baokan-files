Our Great Motherland
　　Using grafting to cultivate fruit trees can not only improve varieties, but also rapidly expand the planting area of fruit trees. This year, the farmers of Xiyulin Village in Huailai County, Chahar Province, harvested more than 400,000 catties of fruit and made a large profit. This is Liu Zhensheng, a farmer in the village, grafting fragrant fruit trees in preparation for expanding production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    用接木法来培育果树，不仅可以改良品种，而且可以迅速扩大果树的种植面积。察哈尔省怀来县西榆林村的全村农民，今年收获水果达四十多万斤，获利很大。这是该村农民刘振生正在接香果树，准备扩大生产。
