Title: People of All Nationalities in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Exceed Half-Year Donation Plan

Author:

Date: 1951-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, 15th. The people of all nationalities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have overfulfilled the half-year plan to donate 14 fighter planes and 3 artillery pieces ahead of schedule. As of November 10, the actual donations received in various regions of the area have reached 24.65 billion yuan, exceeding the original plan by more than 4 percent.

### 内蒙古自治区各族人民超额完成半年捐献计划

1951-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　内蒙古自治区各族人民超额完成半年捐献计划
    【新华社张家口十五日电】内蒙古自治区各族人民提前超额完成半年捐献战斗机十四架、大炮三门的计划，截至十一月十日止，该区各地实缴献款已达二百四十六亿五千余万元，超过原计划百分之四强。
