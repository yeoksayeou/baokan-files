Title: Western European Progressive Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Opening Address
Author:
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 30th. Progressive newspapers in Western European countries, such as the British "Daily Worker," the French "L'Humanité," the Belgian "Red Flag," the Austrian "People's Voice," and the Swiss "Vorwärts," all prominently published Chairman Mao Zedong's opening address at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on the 25th.

Major newspapers in Eastern European People's Democratic countries, such as the Czechoslovak "Rudé Právo," the Romanian "Scînteia," the Bulgarian "Rabotnichesko Delo," and the Hungarian "Szabad Nép," have all published the full text of Chairman Mao's opening address.

### 西欧国家进步报纸  刊载毛主席开会词

1951-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    西欧国家进步报纸
    刊载毛主席开会词
    【新华社布拉格三十日电】英国“工人日报”、法国“人道报”、比利时“红旗报”、奥地利“人民之声报”、瑞士“前进报”等西欧国家的进步报纸，都在二十五日以显著地位刊载了毛泽东主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议上的开会词。
    东欧人民民主国家各大报，如捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”、罗马尼亚“火花报”、保加利亚“工人事业报”、匈牙利“自由人民报”等，都全文刊载了毛主席的开会词。
