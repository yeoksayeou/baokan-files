Title: Government Administration Council Holds 112th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd—The 112th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 23rd. The meeting heard and approved the report by Minister Ye Jizhuang of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government on the National Ethnic Trade Conference, the report by Minister Ma Xulun of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government on the National Ethnic Education Conference, and the report by Vice Minister He Cheng of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government on the National Ethnic Health Conference. The meeting approved several cases of appointments and removals, and appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行一一二次会议

1951-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一一二次会议
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十二次政务会议于二十三日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府贸易部叶季壮部长所作的关于全国民族贸易会议的报告、中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长所作的关于全国民族教育会议的报告、中央人民政府卫生部贺诚副部长所作的关于全国民族卫生会议的报告。会议通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
