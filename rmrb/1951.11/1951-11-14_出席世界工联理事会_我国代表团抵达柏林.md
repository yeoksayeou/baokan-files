Title: Attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Council Meeting: Our Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1951-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th -- The Chinese delegation of ten people, led by Zhu Xuefan, to attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Council meeting, arrived in Berlin by train on the afternoon of November 11th. Representatives from the Free German Trade Union Federation, Diette, and Wang Yutian, Counselor of our Diplomatic Mission to the German Democratic Republic, welcomed them at the station.

### 出席世界工联理事会  我国代表团抵达柏林

1951-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　出席世界工联理事会
    我国代表团抵达柏林
    【新华社柏林十二日电】由朱学范率领的出席世界工会联合会理事会的中国代表团一行十人，已于十一日下午乘火车抵柏林。自由德国工会联合会代表狄特和我驻德意志民主共和国外交使团参赞王雨田等人均往车站欢迎。
