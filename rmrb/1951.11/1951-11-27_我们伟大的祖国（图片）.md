    Our Great Motherland
    Shanhaiguan, the thoroughfare of transportation between Northeast and North China, was liberated on this day three years ago. Shanhaiguan, also known as "The First Pass Under Heaven", is the first pass on the eastern end of the Great Wall.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国东北和华北交通的孔道——山海关是在三年前的今天解放的。山海关又名“天下第一关”，是万里长城东部的第一关口。
