Title: Caring for Ethnic Minorities' Festivals: Ministry of Trade Stipulates Preferential Measures
Author:
Date: 1951-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. In order to take into account the different customs of ethnic minorities regarding festivals, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government has specifically stipulated preferential measures for ethnic minority festivals. On the 7th of this month, the ministry issued an instruction stating that during ethnic minority festivals, state-owned department store retail departments and comprehensive subsidiaries in ethnic minority areas should grant a 5% discount on retail sales of general merchandise, grain, cloth, salt, oil, and coal to the respective ethnic groups for three days. This regulation also applies to areas where ethnic minorities live in concentrated communities and areas with a larger population of ethnic minorities.

### 照顾少数民族年节  贸易部规定优待办法

1951-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    照顾少数民族年节
    贸易部规定优待办法
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府贸易部为了照顾少数民族年节习惯时间的不同，特规定对少数民族年节优待办法。本月七日，该部曾发出指示，指示少数民族地区的国营百货零售部门和一揽子公司在少数民族年节时，对各该民族零售的百货、粮食、布匹、盐、油、煤等，一律按九五扣优待三天；这个规定并适用于少数民族聚居地区和少数民族较多的地区。
