Title: Beijing and Tianjin Higher Education Teachers Hold Second Study Report Meeting, Comrade Peng Zhen Attends and Delivers Report
Author:
Date: 1951-11-19
Page: 1

    Beijing and Tianjin Higher Education Teachers Hold Second Study Report Meeting
    Comrade Peng Zhen Attends and Delivers Report
    [Xinhua News Agency, 18th] The Beijing-Tianjin Higher Education Teachers Study Committee of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government held its second study report meeting on the 18th, inviting Comrade Peng Zhen to give a report on "Some Ideological Issues Related to the Three Major Movements." Participating in this report meeting were teachers from various universities in Beijing, representatives of teachers from various universities in Tianjin, representatives of the Ministry of Education from major administrative regions, and scientific research personnel from units such as the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, and the Central Institute of Health Sciences, totaling approximately four thousand people.

### 北京天津高等学校教师举行第二次学习报告会  彭真同志出席作报告

1951-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    北京天津高等学校教师举行第二次学习报告会
    彭真同志出席作报告
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府教育部京津高等学校教师学习委员会在十八日举行第二次学习报告会，请彭真同志作“有关三大运动的一些思想问题”的报告。参加这次报告会的有北京各高等学校教师、天津各高等学校教师代表、各大行政区教育部代表和中国科学院、华北农业科学研究所、中央卫生科学研究所等单位科学研究工作人员共约四千余人。
