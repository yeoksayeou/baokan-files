Title: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko Receives Foreign Guests to Celebrate the 34th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1951-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th -- According to TASS, Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko held a reception on the 7th to entertain foreign guests in celebration of the 34th anniversary of the great October Revolution. Invited to attend were: diplomatic envoys and embassy personnel from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Ze Dengbaal and Meidaer, Hungarian Minister of Foreign Trade Sobek, Polish Minister of Foreign Trade Kidd, World Peace Council members Anna Seghers and Toledano, heads of visiting delegations to the Soviet Union from foreign organizations such as the German-Soviet Friendship Association, the Czech-Soviet Friendship Association, the French-Soviet Association, the Italian-Soviet Association, and the Icelandic-Soviet Association, heads of cultural workers delegations from Bulgaria and Uruguay, as well as personnel from other foreign cultural delegations and trade union delegations visiting the Soviet Union.

The Soviet side attending the reception included: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Tarasov, President of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union Volin, Procurator General of the Soviet Union Safonov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Zorin, Gusev, and Bogomolov, Soviet Marshals Budyonny, Govorov, Malinovsky, ministers of various ministries of the Soviet government and generals of the Soviet army, as well as scientists, writers, artists, and representatives from the Soviet press and foreign press.

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十四周年  苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯招待外宾

1951-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十四周年
    苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯招待外宾
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，为庆祝伟大的十月革命三十四周年，特在七日举行招待会招待外宾。应邀出席的有：各国驻苏外交使节和使馆人员，蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席泽登巴尔、梅达尔，匈牙利对外贸易部长索贝克，波兰对外贸易部长基德，世界和平理事会理事安娜·西格斯、托列达诺，及德苏友好协会、捷苏友好协会、法苏协会、意苏协会和冰苏协会等外国团体的访苏代表团团长，保加利亚和乌拉圭两国的文化工作者代表团团长，以及访问苏联的其他各国文化代表团和工会代表团人员。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫、苏联最高法院院长伏林、苏联最高检察长萨弗洛夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金、苏联外交部副部长佐林、古雪夫和鲍格莫洛夫，苏联元帅布琼尼、戈沃罗夫、马林诺夫斯基和苏联政府各部部长与苏军将领，以及科学家、作家、艺术家、苏联报界和外国报界的代表多人。
