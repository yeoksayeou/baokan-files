Title: Study "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" conscientiously and grasp the ideological weapon for building a new China!
Author:
Date: 1951-11-14
Page: 5

　　The "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" collects the important works of Comrade Mao Zedong from the five historical periods that the Chinese Communist Party has experienced since 1926. The first volume now published collects the works of Comrade Mao Zedong during the First Domestic Revolutionary War and the Second Domestic Revolutionary War. The publication of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" is an important event in the cause of Marxism-Leninism. Mao Zedong Thought has not only been fully proven by practice to be the only correct guide for leading the Chinese people to defeat enemies at home and abroad and win revolutionary victory, but also the only correct guide for consolidating and developing our people's revolutionary cause and building a great motherland in the future.
　　After the publication of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong", a large-scale study movement is being actively prepared and launched throughout the country.
　　Countless readers crowded into the sales department of the Tianjin branch of the China Book Distribution Company, carefully browsing through the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."
　　The youths in Shenyang City bought the first volume of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" and happily walked out of the gate of the Shenyang Xinhua Bookstore.
　　On the morning of October 12th, the people of Harbin lined up in a long queue in front of the bookstore, waiting to buy the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."
　　In Shanghai on the morning of October 12th, the doors of the bookstore had not yet opened, but the square in front of the bookstore was already filled with people eager to purchase the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."
　　The people of the capital welcomed the publication of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" with warm and excited feelings. People flocked to the Xinhua Bookstore to buy the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong". Some happily browsed through the newly purchased "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in front of the bookstore.
　　Combat heroes and model workers who bought the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" at the Xinhua Bookstore in Nanjing are impatiently browsing through the great works of the people's leader.
　　Not only in big cities, but also in some small and medium-sized cities, people are scrambling to buy the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong". On the morning of October 12th, the people of Ruian County, Zhejiang Province, gathered in front of the bookstore to compete to buy the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."
　　The workers of the letterpress printing department of the Beijing Xinhua Printing Factory, which printed the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong", unanimously proposed to guarantee that the printing quality of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" would reach 100%, expressing their love for their leader.
　　On October 12th, the Wuhan Xinhua Bookstore held an evening party to celebrate the publication of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong". Zhao Xingyuan, a combat hero of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, said at the party that he must study Chairman Mao's great works well so that he can participate in the construction of our great motherland! Many people are buying the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" at the Xinhua Bookstore in Xi'an.
　　Workers of the letterpress printing group of the Changchun Xinhua Printing Factory excitedly accepted the glorious task of printing the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong", ensuring that the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" would be well printed, and launched a heated production competition.
　　After the people of Dihua bought the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong", they all happily walked out of the bookstore gate. In order to facilitate the reading of people of all ethnic minorities, the Xinjiang People's Publishing House decided to translate the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" into Uyghur, Kazakh, and Mongolian. The Uyghur translation team has translated eight articles from the first volume of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong", which will be published separately as single editions.
　　People from all walks of life in Chongqing crowded into bookstores to register for pre-ordering the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong".

### 认真学习毛泽东选集，掌握建设新中国的思想武器！（图片）

1951-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　“毛泽东选集”收集了毛泽东同志从一九二六年以来在中国共产党所经历的五个历史时期的重要著作。现在出版的第一卷收集了毛泽东同志在第一次国内革命战争时期和第二次国内革命战争时期的著作。“毛泽东选集”的出版是马克思列宁主义事业中的重要事件。毛泽东思想不仅为实践所完全证明是领导中国人民击败内外敌人取得革命胜利的唯一正确的指针，而且是今后巩固和发展我国人民革命事业和建设伟大祖国的唯一正确的指针。
  　“毛泽东选集”出版后，全国各地都正在积极准备展开一个大规模的学习运动。
    无数读者拥挤在中国图书发行公司天津市分公司门市部里，他们正在细心地翻阅着“毛泽东选集”。
    沈阳市的青年们买到了“毛泽东选集”第一卷，高兴地走出沈阳市新华书店的大门。
    十月十二日清晨，哈尔滨市人民就在书店门前排成了很长的队伍，等着购买“毛泽东选集”。
    上海市在十月十二日的早晨，书店的大门都还没有开，书店门前却已经站满了购买“毛泽东选集”的人民群众。
    首都人民以热烈兴奋的心情来迎接“毛泽东选集”的出版。人们络绎不绝地拥到新华书店来购买“毛泽东选集”。有的就在书店门前愉快地翻阅着刚买到的“毛泽东选集”。
    在南京市新华书店门市部购买“毛泽东选集”的战斗英雄和劳动模范们，正在迫不及待地翻阅着人民领袖的伟大著作。
    不但在大城市，就是在一些中小城市中，人们也都在争先购买“毛泽东选集”。浙江省瑞安县的人民群众十月十二日早晨，就聚集在书店门前竞购“毛泽东选集”。
    承印“毛泽东选集”的北京新华印刷厂铅印部工人，一致提出保证“毛泽东选集”印刷质量达到百分之百，表示他们对自己领袖的热爱。
    十月十二日，武汉市新华书店为庆祝“毛泽东选集”的出版举行了一个晚会。中国人民解放军战斗英雄赵兴元在晚会上表示，一定要好好学习毛主席的伟大著作，以便参加建设我们伟大的祖国！许多人正在西安市新华书店里购买“毛泽东选集”。
    长春市新华印刷厂活版印刷组工人，兴奋地接受了印刷“毛泽东选集”的光荣任务，保证印好“毛泽东选集”，并展开了热烈的生产竞赛。
    迪化市人民买到了“毛泽东选集”后，都很高兴地走出书店大门。新疆人民出版社为了便利各少数民族人民阅读，决定将“毛泽东选集”翻译成维吾尔文、哈萨克文、蒙古文三种民族文字。现维文翻译组已译出“毛泽东选集”第一卷中八篇文章，即将分别出版单行本。
    重庆市各界人民拥挤在书店里办理预购“毛泽东选集”的登记手续。
