Title: Regarding Ambiguous Points in the U.S. Side's 17th Proposal, Our Side Demands Clarification from the Other Side
Author:
Date: 1951-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 18th. The Korean armistice negotiation subcommittee meeting on the 18th began at 11:00 AM. Our side's representatives demanded that the other side clarify the ambiguous points in their 17th proposal. The meeting ended at 12:25 PM and was scheduled to continue at 11:00 AM on the 19th.

### 对美方十七日提案中含糊各点  我方要求对方予以澄清

1951-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    对美方十七日提案中含糊各点
    我方要求对方予以澄清
    【新华社开城十八日电】十八日的朝鲜停战谈判小组委员会在上午十一时开始，我方代表要求对方对其十七日提案中意义含糊各点予以澄清。会议于十二时二十五分结束，并定于十九日上午十一时继续举行。
