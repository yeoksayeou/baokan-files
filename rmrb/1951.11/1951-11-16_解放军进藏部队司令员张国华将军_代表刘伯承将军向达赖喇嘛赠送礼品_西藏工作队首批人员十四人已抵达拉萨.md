Title: General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the PLA Entering Tibet, Presents Gifts to the Dalai Lama on Behalf of General Liu Bocheng; First Group of Fourteen Members of the Tibet Work Team Arrives in Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1951-11-16
Page: 1

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 15th】 General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the People's Liberation Army entering Tibet, presented gifts to the Dalai Lama on October 30th on behalf of Liu Bocheng, Chairman of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee. The gifts included gold lamps, jade bowls, ivory carvings, and valuable items such as silks and sable furs. When the gifts arrived at Norbulingka (Lingka means park), the Dalai Lama's guards and band came out to greet them. The Dalai Lama happily accepted the gifts at Norbulingka. General Zhang Guohua, on behalf of Liu Bocheng, Chairman of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, extended sincere greetings to the Dalai Lama and expressed heartfelt gratitude for the welcome and support received from officials at all levels in Tibet and the broad masses of Tibetan monks and laypeople during the advance. The Dalai Lama expressed his deep gratitude for Chairman Liu Bocheng's kindness. He said: Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, the Tibetan people will surely unite more closely and friendly with all nationalities across the country.
　　
    【Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 15th】 The first group of fourteen members of the Tibet Work Team organized by the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council arrived in Lhasa on the 9th of this month. They traversed towering snow mountains and conducted preliminary investigations into Tibetan history, geography, people's economic life, and medical and health conditions along the way. After their arrival in Lhasa, General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the PLA entering Tibet, and General Tan Guansan, Political Commissar, held a banquet to welcome them. Another group of the work team is currently working in the Qamdo area and will detour through the Bomi area to Lhasa.

### 解放军进藏部队司令员张国华将军  代表刘伯承将军向达赖喇嘛赠送礼品  西藏工作队首批人员十四人已抵达拉萨

1951-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放军进藏部队司令员张国华将军
    代表刘伯承将军向达赖喇嘛赠送礼品
    西藏工作队首批人员十四人已抵达拉萨
    【新华社拉萨十五日电】人民解放军进藏部队司令员张国华将军在十月三十日代表西南军政委员会主席刘伯承向达赖喇嘛赠送礼品。礼品中有金灯、玉碗和象牙等雕刻器皿，以及绸缎、貂皮等贵重物品。当礼品送抵罗布林卡（林卡即公园）时，达赖喇嘛的卫队和乐队都出门迎接。达赖喇嘛在罗布林卡愉快地接受了礼物。张国华将军代表西南军政委员会主席刘伯承向达赖喇嘛致以亲切慰问，并对此次进军途中受到西藏地方各级官员及广大藏族僧俗人民的欢迎和支援表示衷心感谢。达赖喇嘛表示深为感谢刘伯承主席的盛意。他说：西藏人民在毛主席和中央人民政府的领导下，一定会和全国各民族更加亲密友好地团结起来。
    【新华社拉萨十五日电】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会组织的西藏工作队首批十四人，已在本月九日抵达拉萨。他们越过了重重的雪山，沿途对西藏历史、地理、人民经济生活和医药卫生情况作了初步调查。他们到达拉萨后，人民解放军入藏部队司令员张国华将军、政治委员谭冠三将军曾举行宴会欢迎。工作队另批人员现留昌都地区工作，将绕道波密地区到拉萨。
