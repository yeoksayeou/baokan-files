Title: Generals Zhang Guohua and Tan Guansan Host Banquet for Important Monastic and Secular Officials of the Tibetan Local Government
Author:
Date: 1951-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 26th: Generals Zhang Guohua, Commander of the People's Liberation Army in Tibet, and Tan Guansan, Political Commissar, held banquets at the Seitelingka (park) in Lhasa from the 12th to the 14th of this month to entertain officials of the Tibetan local government. Important monastic and secular officials of the Tibetan local government, such as the Kalons, Kachus, and Jikyo Khenpos, as well as the Tibetan Buddhist Bishop Gaden Tripa, and more than 80 living Buddhas and Khenpos from various monasteries, were invited to attend the banquets. At the banquet, General Zhang Guohua said in his speech: The peaceful liberation of Tibet is a congratulatory event in Chinese history, and the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet is the common goal of the troops entering Tibet and the Tibetan monks and laypeople in the future. He said: "This banquet marks the unity of the troops entering Tibet and the Tibetan monks and laypeople in the future, and this unity is built on the foundation of the agreement." Kalon Lhalu of the Tibetan local government thanked the troops entering Tibet at the banquet for helping the Tibetan people build a new Tibet and consolidate the national defense of the motherland. The Tibetan Buddhist Bishop Gaden Tripa thanked General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, for the gifts he gave to the monasteries. He said: "We are very grateful to the Central People's Government for its care and love for Tibetan religious temples." The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of unity and harmony throughout. Guests and hosts toasted to the increasing unity of all ethnic groups in China, to the good health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all ethnic groups, and to the good health of the Dalai Lama. During the banquet, the cultural troupe of the troops entering Tibet performed dances such as "Great Unity of All Ethnic Groups in China" and sang various songs, and showed films such as "Great Unity of All Ethnic Groups in China."

### 张国华、谭冠三两将军  欢宴西藏地方政府重要僧俗官员

1951-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    张国华、谭冠三两将军
    欢宴西藏地方政府重要僧俗官员
   【新华社二十六日讯】拉萨消息：中国人民解放军进藏部队司令员张国华、政治委员谭冠三两将军，于本月十二日至十四日，在拉萨侧德林卡（公园）分别举行了宴会，招待西藏地方政府官员。应邀参加宴会的有西藏地方政府噶伦、噶楚、机巧堪布等重要僧俗官员，和西藏佛教主教噶登慈巴，以及各寺院活佛、堪布共八十余人。宴会上，张国华将军致词时指出：和平解放西藏是中国历史上一件值得庆贺的事情，和平解放西藏办法的协议是今后进藏部队和西藏僧俗人民共同奋斗的目标。他说：“这次宴会标志着今后进藏部队和西藏僧俗人民的团结，这团结是建筑在协议的基础之上的。”西藏地方政府噶伦拉鲁在宴会上感谢进藏部队帮助西藏人民建设新西藏和巩固祖国国防。西藏佛教主教噶登慈巴感谢中央人民政府代表张经武将军赠送各寺院的礼物。他说：“我们非常感谢中央人民政府对西藏宗教寺庙的关心和爱护。”宴会中始终充满了团结和谐的气氛，宾主间相互为中国各民族的日益团结而干杯，为各民族的伟大领袖毛主席身体健康而干杯，为达赖喇嘛的身体健康而干杯。宴会期间，进藏部队的文艺工作团表演了“中国各民族大团结”等舞蹈和演唱了各种歌曲，并放映了“中国民族大团结”等影片。
