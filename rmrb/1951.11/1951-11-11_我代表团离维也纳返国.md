Title: Our Delegation Leaves Vienna for Return to China
Author:
Date: 1951-11-11
Page: 1
　　
　　 Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 10th. The Chinese delegation led by Guo Moruo, which attended the Second Session of the World Peace Council, left Vienna on the 9th via the Soviet Union to return to China.

### 我代表团离维也纳返国

1951-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　 我代表团离维也纳返国
    【新华社维也纳十日电】由郭沫若率领的中国出席世界和平理事会第二届会议的代表团，已在九日离维也纳取道苏联返国。
