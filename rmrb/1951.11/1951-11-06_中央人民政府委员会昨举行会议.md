Title: Central People's Government Committee Holds Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: The 13th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee was held yesterday afternoon. The meeting heard Personnel Minister An Ziwen's explanation of the "Draft Provisional Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of Staff of State Organs of the Central People's Government," and approved the Provisional Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of Staff of State Organs of the Central People's Government. The meeting heard Vice Minister of Culture Zhou Yang's report on the cultural agreements signed between China and Poland, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic, and approved the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in Warsaw on April 3, 1951, the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in Beijing on July 12, 1951, and the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in Beijing on October 9, 1951. The meeting passed the "Resolution to Rename the Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government as the Ministry of Forestry." In addition, the meeting approved several appointment cases and ratified requests from the Government Administration Council for appointments and removals.

Present at the meeting were Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan of the Central People's Government, and members of the Central People's Government Committee Chen Yi, Li Lisan, Lin Boqu, Ye Jianying, He Xiangning, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, Wu Yuzhang, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Tan Kah Kee, Ulanhu, Xu Teli, Liu Geping, Lin Feng, Ma Xulun, Gao Chongmin, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zemin, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Li Zhangda, Li Zhuchen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Mingshu, Tan Pingshan, Zhang Nanxian, Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun, totaling forty-two people.

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: The Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government has been renamed the Ministry of Forestry by resolution of the 13th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee. The land reclamation work under the jurisdiction of the ministry will be transferred to the Ministry of Agriculture.

### 中央人民政府委员会昨举行会议

1951-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府委员会昨举行会议
    批准中央人民政府任免国家机关工作人员暂行条例和中波、中匈、中德文化合作协定。决议将林垦部改名为林业部。
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府委员会第十三次会议于五日下午举行。会议听取中央人民政府人事部安子文部长所作的关于“中央人民政府任免国家机关工作人员暂行条例草案”的说明后，批准了中央人民政府任免国家机关工作人员暂行条例。会议听取中央人民政府文化部周扬副部长所作的关于我国和波兰、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国签订文化协定的报告后，批准了一九五一年四月三日在华沙签订的中波文化合作协定，一九五一年七月十二日在北京签订的中匈文化合作协定，一九五一年十月九日在北京签订的中德文化合作协定。会议通过了“中央人民政府林垦部改名林业部决议”。此外，会议通过任命案及批准政务院提请任免案多起。
    出席这次会议的，有中央人民政府主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府委员会委员陈毅、李立三、林伯渠、叶剑英、何香凝、林彪、刘伯承、吴玉章、彭真、薄一波、聂荣臻、周恩来、董必武、陈嘉庚、乌兰夫、徐特立、刘格平、林枫、马叙伦、高崇民、沈钧儒、陈叔通、司徒美堂、黄炎培、彭泽民、张治中、傅作义、李章达、李烛尘、程潜、张奚若、陈铭枢、谭平山、张难先、柳亚子、张东荪等共四十二人。
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府林垦部业经中央人民政府委员会第十三次会议决议改名为林业部。该部所管垦务工作移交农业部主管。
