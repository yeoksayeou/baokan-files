Our Great Motherland
With high patriotic enthusiasm, railway workers across the country are launching a patriotic red banner competition centered on increasing production and practicing thrift. Workers in the operation departments of the Guye Station of the Tianjin Railway Administration have set shortening the stopping time as the goal of the competition, greatly improving efficiency. In September, it was rated as a model station for the entire administration. This is a picture of the workers checking their results in front of the scoreboard.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    全国铁路工人正以高度的爱国热忱，开展以增产节约为中心的爱国主义红旗竞赛。天津铁路管理局古冶车站运转部门的工人，以缩短停站时间为竞赛的目标，效率大为提高，九月份被评为全局的模范站。这是工人们在揭示牌前查看成绩的情形。
