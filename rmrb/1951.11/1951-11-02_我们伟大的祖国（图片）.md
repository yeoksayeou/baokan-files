Author:
Date: 1951-11-02

    Our Great Motherland
    The youths of New China all feel honored and happy to be able to contribute their strength to the construction of the motherland. This is Ji Yue'e, a young member of the fine hemp washing mutual aid group in Twelve Village, Bolu Township, Hang County, Zhejiang. She is busy collecting the dried hemp, ready to sell it to the state.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的青年们都以能够为祖国的建设事业贡献出自己的力量而感到光荣和愉快。这是浙江杭县博陆乡十二村精洗麻互助组的青年组员季月娥。她正忙着把晒干了的麻收下来，准备卖给国家。
