Title: Comrade Deng Yingchao Departs for Berlin to Attend Executive Committee Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1951-11-20
Page: 1

    Comrade Deng Yingchao Departs for Berlin
    To Attend Executive Committee Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation
    [Our Newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Women's International Democratic Federation will hold an Executive Committee meeting in Berlin from December 7th to 9th. The main agenda of the meeting includes discussing the convening of the Third International Women's Congress and the International Conference for the Defense of Children. Deng Yingchao, Executive Committee member of the Women's International Democratic Federation and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, departed on the 19th to attend the meeting, accompanied by a secretary, translator, and others.

### 邓颖超同志启程赴柏林  参加国际民主妇联执行委员会

1951-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    邓颖超同志启程赴柏林
    参加国际民主妇联执行委员会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：国际民主妇女联合会将在十二月七日至九日在柏林召开执行委员会，会议主要议程是讨论召开第三次国际妇女代表大会和国际保卫儿童代表大会等问题。国际民主妇联执行委员、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超，在十九日启程前往出席会议，随行的有秘书、翻译等人。
