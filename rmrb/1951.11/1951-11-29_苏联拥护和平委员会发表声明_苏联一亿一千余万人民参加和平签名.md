Title: Statement by the Soviet Peace Committee; Over 117 Million Soviet People Participate in Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1951-11-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 28th (TASS dispatch): The Soviet Peace Committee issued the following statement:
    The Soviet Union's campaign to collect signatures for the World Peace Council's declaration calling for a peace pact between the five major powers—the United States, the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, Great Britain, and France—has concluded, and the Soviet Peace Committee has summarized the achievements of this campaign.
    The results of the campaign demonstrate that the entire Soviet people unanimously support the World Peace Council's declaration, and Soviet men and women are determined to avoid war and safeguard the cause of peace.
    From the start of the campaign until November 15th, a total of 117,766,920 Soviet citizens signed the World Peace Council's declaration.
    The signature collection campaign for the peace pact declaration was conducted in an atmosphere of high political and labor enthusiasm. At numerous gatherings and meetings, the Soviet people expressed their complete agreement with and firm support for the Soviet government's peaceful foreign policy, and expressed their solidarity with peace advocates in all countries. Soviet men and women expressed their anger at the aggressive actions of the imperialist ruling cliques who are trying to launch a new world war, and demanded an end to American intervention in Korea, the prohibition of atomic weapons, disarmament, and the conclusion of a peace pact between the five major powers. In the campaign to collect signatures for the World Peace Council's declaration, the Soviet people also strived for new achievements in peaceful labor in order to further strengthen the power of the Soviet state, the fortress of world peace.
    The success of the Soviet Union's campaign to collect signatures for the peace pact declaration proves the Soviet people's love for peace and their unwavering willingness to cooperate with all countries to maintain and strengthen peace. Peace advocates in all countries can rely on the Soviet people as their most reliable ally in the great struggle for peace and the security of all nations.
    The signature collection campaign for the peace pact declaration has concluded victoriously, with the participation of all citizens aged sixteen and above. This is a striking demonstration of the Soviet people's wholehearted unity around the government, the Bolshevik Party, and Comrade Stalin, the great standard-bearer of peace.

### 苏联拥护和平委员会发表声明  苏联一亿一千余万人民参加和平签名

1951-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联拥护和平委员会发表声明
    苏联一亿一千余万人民参加和平签名
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日电：苏联拥护和平委员会发表声明如下：
    苏联为世界和平理事会要求五大国——美国、苏联、中华人民共和国、英国和法国——缔结和平公约的宣言征集签名的运动已经结束，苏联拥护和平委员会已总结了这次运动的成就。
    运动的结果证明，苏联全体人民一致支持世界和平理事会的宣言，苏联男女们决心避免战争，并维护和平事业。
    从运动开始到十一月十五日为止，共有一亿一千七百六十六万九千三百二十名苏联公民在世界和平理事会的宣言上签了名。
    为和平公约宣言征集签名的运动，是在政治和劳动热潮高涨的气氛中进行的。在无数集会和会议上，苏联人民都表示完全同意并坚决支持苏联政府的和平外交政策，表示与各国和平拥护者团结一致。苏联男女们对于力图发动新的世界战争的帝国主义国家统治集团的侵略行动表示愤怒，并要求结束美国对朝鲜的干涉、禁止原子武器、裁减军备及五大国缔结和平公约。苏联人民在为世界和平理事会宣言征集签名的运动中，并努力在和平劳动方面争取新的成就，以便更进一步增强世界和平堡垒——苏维埃国家的威力。
    苏联为和平公约宣言征集签名的运动的成就，证明了苏联人民热爱和平，证明了他们始终不变地愿与一切国家合作，维持并加强和平。一切国家的和平拥护者在争取和平及各国安全的伟大斗争中都能够以苏联人民为他们最可靠的盟友。
    为和平公约宣言征集签名的运动已经胜利结束，凡是年满十六岁及十六岁以上的公民都参加了这个运动。这件事显著地证明了，苏联人民万众一心地团结在政府、团结在布尔什维克党、团结在伟大的和平旗手斯大林同志的周围。
