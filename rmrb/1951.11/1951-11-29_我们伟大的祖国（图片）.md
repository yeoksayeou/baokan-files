Our Great Motherland
Qu Yao-li, a cotton farmer in Jie County, Shanxi Province, has created a new national record for cotton field yield per unit area, and his farming methods are worthy of widespread promotion. This is a picture of Qu Yao-li studying cotton varieties in a farm near the Temple of Heaven when he came to Beijing for National Day this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山西省解县棉农曲耀离创造了全国棉田单位面积产量的新纪录，他的耕作方法值得普遍推广。这是今年国庆节曲耀离来北京时，在天坛附近农场中研究棉花品种的情形。
