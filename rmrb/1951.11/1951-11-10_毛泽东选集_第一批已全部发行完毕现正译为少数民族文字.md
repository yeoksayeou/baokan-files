Title: Selected Works of Mao Zedong: The First Batch Has Been Completely Distributed and is Now Being Translated into Minority Languages
Author:
Date: 1951-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The first batch of 650,000 copies of Volume I of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has been completely sold out. The "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" began to be sold simultaneously in most Xinhua Bookstore branches and sub-branches across the country on October 12th. Other stores also sold them as soon as they received the books. Most bookstores that received Volume I of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" sold them all within one day.

The "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" is distributed uniformly by Xinhua Bookstore. By the end of October, it had been wholesale distributed from Beijing, Shanghai, and Changchun, with 200,000 copies in Beijing, 300,000 copies in Shanghai, and 150,000 copies in Changchun. During the distribution of the first batch of Volume I, the number of readers who registered for organized orders reached 1.5 million.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Xinjiang People's Publishing House is actively carrying out the translation and publication of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in minority languages. Under the guidance of the Propaganda Department of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the publishing house plans to first translate the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" into Uyghur, Kazakh, and Mongolian. The Uyghur translation team has already translated eight articles from Volume I of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong", which will soon be published as separate editions.

### 毛泽东选集  第一批已全部发行完毕现正译为少数民族文字

1951-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东选集
    第一批已全部发行完毕现正译为少数民族文字
    【新华社九日讯】“毛泽东选集”第一卷第一批六十五万册已全部发售完毕。“毛泽东选集”是在十月十二日全国大部分的新华书店分、支店同时开始发售的，其他各店也在收书后即行发售。凡收到“毛泽东选集”第一卷的书店，大部分在一天之内就全部售完。
    “毛泽东选集”是由新华书店统一发行的，在十月底即已分别从北京、上海、长春三处批发完毕，计北京二十万册，上海三十万册，长春十五万册。第一卷第一批发行过程中，有组织地登记定购的读者已达一百五十万人。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：新疆人民出版社正积极进行“毛泽东选集”的少数民族文字的翻译和出版工作。该社在中共中央新疆分局宣传部的指导下，准备首先把“毛泽东选集”翻译成维吾尔文、哈萨克文、蒙古文三种民族文字。现维文翻译组已将“毛泽东选集”第一卷译出八篇，即将分别出版单行本。
