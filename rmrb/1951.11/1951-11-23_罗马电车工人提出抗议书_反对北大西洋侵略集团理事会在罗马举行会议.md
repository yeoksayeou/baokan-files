Title: Roman Tram Workers Present Protest Letter Opposing North Atlantic Aggression Group Council Meeting in Rome
Author:
Date: 1951-11-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 22nd: TASS, Rome, November 21st: The working people of Rome are protesting the North Atlantic Aggression Group Council meeting held in Rome. The Roman tram workers passed a letter of protest, demanding the cancellation of this meeting, and suggesting that efforts be made to hold a world disarmament conference here as soon as possible to eliminate the threat of a new war.
    This letter of protest has been sent to the United Nations Secretariat, distributed to the diplomatic envoys of various countries in Rome, and has received a wide response among the working people of Rome. Professor Donini, a member of the World Peace Council, spoke at a press conference about the widespread participation of the working people and all progressive people in the "Peace Week" activities, which are being held in Rome and are scheduled to end on November 24th and 25th. Italy will soon hold a peace and disarmament conference, and representatives will be sent from all regions of the country to attend. During this week, many famous artists, writers, scientists, and parliamentarians will collect signatures in various districts of Rome for a declaration demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace pact.

### 罗马电车工人提出抗议书  反对北大西洋侵略集团理事会在罗马举行会议

1951-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马电车工人提出抗议书
    反对北大西洋侵略集团理事会在罗马举行会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社罗马二十一日讯：罗马劳动人民抗议北大西洋侵略集团理事会在罗马召开会议。罗马电车工人通过了一个抗议书，要求取消这个会议，并建议设法尽早在这里举行世界裁减军备会议，以消除新战争的威胁。
    这个抗议书已经寄给联合国秘书处，分发给各国驻罗马使节，并在罗马劳动人民中间得到了广泛的反应。世界和平理事会理事多尼尼教授在记者招待会上谈到劳动人民和全体进步人士广泛地参加“和平周”活动的情况，这个“和平周”是在罗马举行的，预定在十一月二十四、五日结束。意大利不久将要举行和平与裁减军备大会，全国各区都将派代表出席。在这个星期内，许多著名的艺术家、作家、科学家和议员将在罗马各区为要求五大国缔结和平公约的宣言征集签名。
