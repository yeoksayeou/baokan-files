Title: Stalin's Reply to Chairman Mao's Telegram of Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1951-11-18
Page: 1

　　Reply to Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
    Stalin's Reply to Chairman Mao
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Comrade Chairman, I sincerely thank you for your friendly congratulations on the thirty-fourth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. I share your belief that the unbreakable friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics is a reliable guarantee for the victory of peace and progress throughout the world.
                                     Stalin, Moscow, November 17

### 答谢对于十月革命节的祝贺  斯大林同志复毛主席电

1951-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　答谢对于十月革命节的祝贺
    斯大林同志复毛主席电
    北京  中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    主席同志，衷心地感谢您在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年时所表示的友谊祝贺。我和您同样地相信，中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间牢不可破的友谊是全世界和平及进步事业胜利的可靠的保证。
                                     斯大林十一月十七日莫斯科
