　　Our Great Motherland
    "Selected Works of Mao Tse-tung" is a collection of Comrade Mao Tse-tung's achievements in applying the theories of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin to solve the problems of the Chinese revolution. Its publication has been warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life across the country. This is a scene of people from all walks of life in Beijing rushing to the Xinhua Bookstore to buy "Selected Works of Mao Tse-tung."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    “毛泽东选集”是毛泽东同志应用马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的学说解决中国革命问题的成果的汇集，它的出版受到全国各界人民热烈的欢迎。这是北京各界人民拥到新华书店门前竞购“毛泽东选集”的情形。
