Title: Pakistani Students Hold "Egypt Day" in Support of Egypt's Anti-Imperialist Struggle
Author:
Date: 1951-11-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Karachi dispatch, TASS: Student organizations in Karachi held "Egypt Day" on the 13th.

　　Egyptian Ambassador to Pakistan, Wahab Azzam, spoke at the conference. He said: The Egyptian people are determined to drive the enemy out of their land and will continue the struggle until victory is won.

　　When students from various universities spoke at the meeting, they pointed out that Pakistani students will support Egypt's struggle against foreign aggression and exploitation.

　　The resolution passed at the meeting emphasized that Britain's occupation of Sudan and the Suez Canal area violated the United Nations Charter.

　　After the meeting, students held a demonstration march in front of the British High Commission. Demonstrators shouted the following slogans: "Get out of the Suez Canal and Sudan!" "Get out of Asia!" "Down with British imperialism!" "Long live the Egyptian people!"

### 巴基斯坦学生举行“埃及日”  声援埃及反帝斗争

1951-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦学生举行“埃及日”
    声援埃及反帝斗争
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩讯：喀喇蚩的学生组织在十三日举行“埃及日”。
    埃及驻巴基斯坦大使瓦哈布·阿扎姆在大会上讲话。他说：埃及人民已决定把敌人逐出国土，并将继续进行斗争，直到赢得胜利为止。
    各大学的学生在会上发言时指出，巴基斯坦学生将支持埃及反对外国的侵略和剥削的斗争。
    会上通过的决议着重指出：英国占领苏丹和苏彝士运河区的行动违背了联合国宪章。
    在集会之后，学生们在英国高级专员公署门前举行示威游行。示威者高呼下列口号：“滚出苏彝士运河和苏丹！”“滚出亚洲！”“打倒英帝国主义！”“埃及人民万岁！”
