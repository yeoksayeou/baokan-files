Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 110th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The 110th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 9th. Liu Lantao, Minister of the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government, gave a report on the first county head conference in North China. The meeting approved Minister Liu Lantao's report and the documents adopted by the first county head conference in North China: Minister Liu Lantao of the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government's report on "Further Strengthening the Work of County People's Representative Conferences," Minister Xie Juezai of the Ministry of Internal Affairs' report on "Earnestly Strengthening Preferential Treatment Work," and the "Resolution on the Current Ten Tasks of County People's Governments." In addition, the meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一一零次会议

1951-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一一零次会议
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十次政务会议于九日下午举行。中央人民政府华北事务部刘澜涛部长作了关于华北第一次县长会议的报告。会议批准了刘澜涛部长的报告和华北第一次县长会议通过的文件：中央人民政府华北事务部刘澜涛部长所作的“进一步加强县人民代表会议的工作”的报告，内务部谢觉哉部长所作的“认真加强优抚工作”的报告，“关于县人民政府当前十项工作的决议”。此外，会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
