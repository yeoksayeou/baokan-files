Title: Recording of Premier Zhou's Political Report to be Broadcast at 6:15 PM Today
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the recording of Premier Zhou Enlai's political report at the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference from 6:15 PM to 7:30 PM today. Tomorrow (3rd), the recording of Vice Premier Chen Yun's report on economic and financial work at the same meeting will be broadcast. The day after tomorrow (4th), the recording of Vice Premier Guo Moruo's report on cultural and educational work will be broadcast. The time for each broadcast will be from 6:15 PM to 7:30 PM. All local people's broadcasting stations across the country will simulcast the above broadcasts.

### 周总理政治报告录音  今日下午六时十五分广播

1951-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理政治报告录音
    今日下午六时十五分广播
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民广播电台定于今日下午六时十五分至七时三十分广播周恩来总理在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议上作的政治报告的录音，明日（三日）广播陈云副总理在同一会议上作的关于经济工作和财政工作的报告的录音，后日（四日）广播郭沫若副总理关于文化教育工作的报告的录音，时间均为下午六时十五分至七时三十分。上述广播全国各地人民广播电台同时联播。
