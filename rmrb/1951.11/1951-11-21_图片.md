Title: Pictures
Author: Kong Xiangzhu, Wang Naiqing
Date: 1951-11-21
Page: 4

    Top Right: American and British prisoners of war in a POW camp in northern Korea are treated kindly by our army and live happily. In their spare time, they plant flowers and form a pattern symbolizing peace on the flower bed, expressing their desire for peace.
           (Photo by Kong Xiangzhu)
    Top Left: To celebrate the establishment of their peace committee, American and British prisoners of war happily performed some programs. The English on the curtain reads "Facing the Light, Fighting for Justice, Demanding Peace."
           (Photo by Kong Xiangzhu)
    Right: In the summer, American and British prisoners of war fish and swim by the pond, and enjoy the cool under the big trees.
           (Photo by Wang Naiqing)

### 图片
孔祥竺  王奈庆
1951-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    上右：在朝鲜北部俘虏营内的美英俘虏们受到我军宽待，在愉快地生活着。他们在空隙时间种植花草，并在花池上砌成象征和平的图案，表示他们要求和平的愿望。
           （孔祥竺摄）
    上左：美英俘虏为了庆祝他们的和平委员会的建立，愉快地演出了一些节目。在幕布上的英文是“面向光明，为正义而战，要求和平”。
           （孔祥竺摄）
    右：夏天，美英俘虏们在池边钓鱼、游泳，在大树下乘凉。
           （王奈庆摄）
