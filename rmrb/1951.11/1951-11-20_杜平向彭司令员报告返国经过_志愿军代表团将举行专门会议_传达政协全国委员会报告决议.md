Title: Du Ping Reports to Commander Peng on His Return to China; Volunteer Army Delegation to Hold Special Meeting; To Convey Reports and Resolutions of the CPPCC National Committee

Author:

Date: 1951-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 19th -- Du Ping, head of the delegation of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who attended the National Day celebrations, and his party, upon returning to the Korean front, the head of the delegation, Du Ping, reported to Commander Peng Dehuai on his experiences participating in the Second National Day celebrations of the People's Republic of China and attending the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The Volunteer Army delegation will also hold a special meeting to convey the various reports and resolutions of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. More than 40 combat heroes of the Volunteer Army who observed the National Day celebrations, who left Beijing on October 28th to return to Korea, have also arrived at the front one after another and are reporting their impressions of participating in the National Day celebrations to their respective units.

### 杜平向彭司令员报告返国经过  志愿军代表团将举行专门会议  传达政协全国委员会报告决议

1951-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    杜平向彭司令员报告返国经过
    志愿军代表团将举行专门会议
    传达政协全国委员会报告决议
    【新华社朝鲜前线十九日电】中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团团长杜平等一行回到朝鲜前线后，代表团团长杜平即向彭德怀司令员报告参加中华人民共和国第二届国庆节典礼和列席中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的经过情形。志愿军代表团并将举行专门的会议，传达中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议的各项报告和决议。十月二十八日离京返朝的志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表四十多人，也已先后到达前线，正分别在各部队中报告他们这次回国参加国庆典礼的观感。
