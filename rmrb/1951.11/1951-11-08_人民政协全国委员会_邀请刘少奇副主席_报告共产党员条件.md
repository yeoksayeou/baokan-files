Title: CPPCC National Committee Invites Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi to Report on the Conditions of a Communist Party Member
Author:
Date: 1951-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th -- To promote the ideological remolding study movement, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) invited Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi to report on the conditions of a Communist Party member after the conclusion of its Third Session. This report meeting was held on the afternoon of the 4th. Those attending the report meeting included all attendees and observers of the Third Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, staff members of various democratic parties, and senior cadres from various departments of the Central People's Government, totaling more than 1,000 people.

### 人民政协全国委员会  邀请刘少奇副主席  报告共产党员条件

1951-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会
    邀请刘少奇副主席
    报告共产党员条件
    【新华社六日讯】为推动思想改造学习运动，中国人民政协全国委员会特于第三次会议闭幕后，邀请刘少奇副主席报告关于共产党员的条件。这一报告会在四日下午举行。参加报告会的有人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议的全体出席、列席人员，各民主党派的工作人员和中央人民政府各部门的高级干部一千余人。
