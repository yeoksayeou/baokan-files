Title: Death of Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Yefremov; Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to Comrade Stalin Expressing Condolences
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 25th: According to TASS, Moscow, November 24th, Central Committee member of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Yefremov, passed away on the 23rd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR issued an obituary stating: "The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the USSR deeply announce that outstanding statesman, Central Committee member of the CPSU (B), Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Alexander Illarionovich Yefremov, passed away on November 23, 1951, after a long and serious illness."

Note: Yefremov was one of the outstanding leaders of Soviet industry. He was born in 1904 in a worker's family in Moscow. At the age of twelve, he began working as a blacksmith's assistant in the machine factory of the Moscow-Belarus-Baltic Railway. From January 1924, Yefremov was a member of the Central Committee of the CPSU. After leaving the Moscow Mother Machine Institute in 1935, he held many leading positions in the machine tool manufacturing industry. As an outstanding expert in machine manufacturing, Yefremov became Deputy People's Commissar of the People's Commissariat of Heavy Industry of the USSR in 1939, and was promoted to Commissar the following year. During the Second World War and the post-war period, Yefremov served successively as the People's Commissar and Minister of the Soviet Machine Tool Manufacturing People's Commissariat and the Ministry of Machine Tool Manufacturing of the USSR. During the Great Patriotic War, the People's Commissariat of Machine Tool Manufacturing did much work in ensuring the supply of military equipment and ammunition to the front lines. In March 1949, Yefremov was elected Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR. In 1946 and 1950, the Soviet people elected him as a Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government, today sent a telegram to Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, to express condolences on the death of Alexander Illarionovich Yefremov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

I was shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Alexander Illarionovich Yefremov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. I express my deepest condolences.

                                                    Zhou Enlai
                                           November 25, 1951
                                           In Beijing

### 苏联部长会议副主席叶非列莫夫逝世  周恩来总理致电斯大林同志表示哀悼

1951-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议副主席叶非列莫夫逝世
    周恩来总理致电斯大林同志表示哀悼
        【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联共产党（布）中央委员、苏联部长会议副主席叶非列莫夫在二十三日逝世。苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议发出讣告说：“苏联共产党
    （布）中央委员会和苏联部长会议沉痛地通告：杰出的政治家、联共（布）中央委员、苏联部长会议副主席、苏联最高苏维埃代表阿历山大·伊拉里奥诺维奇·叶非列莫夫，于长期重病以后，在一九五一年十一月二十三日逝世。”
    按：叶非列莫夫是苏联工业的卓越领导人之一。他在一九○四年出生在莫斯科一个工人的家庭。十二岁开始在莫斯科—白俄罗斯—波罗的海铁路的机器工厂中当铁匠助手。从一九二四年一月起叶非列莫夫就是联共中央委员。他在一九三五年离开莫斯科母机器械研究所以后，曾在母机制造工业方面担任许多领导职务。叶非列莫夫由于是机器制造方面的杰出专家，一九三九年就任苏联重工业人民委员部副委员长，次年升任为委员长。在第二次世界大战和战后时期，叶非列莫夫曾先后担任苏联母机制造人民委员部委员长和苏联母机制造部部长。而在卫国战争时期，母机制造人民委员部曾在保证前线军事装备和弹药的供应上作了许多工作。一九四九年三月，叶非列莫夫当选为苏联部长会议副主席。一九四六年和一九五○年，苏联人民选举他为苏联最高苏维埃代表。 【新华社二十五日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理本日致电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林，吊唁苏联部长会议副主席叶非列莫夫的逝世。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林同志：
    惊悉苏联部长会议副主席、苏联最高苏维埃代表阿历山大·伊拉里奥诺维奇·叶非列莫夫不幸逝世，谨致沉痛的哀悼。
                                                    周恩来
                                           一九五一年十一月
                                           二十五日于北京
