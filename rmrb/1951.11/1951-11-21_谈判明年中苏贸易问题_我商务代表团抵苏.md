Title: Delegation Arrives in Soviet Union for Negotiations on Sino-Soviet Trade for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1951-11-21
Page: 1

    Negotiations on Sino-Soviet Trade for Next Year
    Our Trade Delegation Arrives in Soviet Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 20th] TASS reported on the 18th: The Chinese trade delegation headed by Sha Qianli, Deputy Minister of Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Moscow on the 18th to hold negotiations on trade issues between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China in 1952. Welcoming the delegation were Yelemin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Sladkovsky, Director of the Oriental Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Kurdyukov, Deputy Director of the First Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Sherbina, Chief of the Chinese Section of the Oriental Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Danissenko, Deputy Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union in the People's Republic of China, and Nikolaev, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and many embassy staff members also went to welcome them.

### 谈判明年中苏贸易问题  我商务代表团抵苏

1951-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    谈判明年中苏贸易问题
    我商务代表团抵苏
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社十八日电：中华人民共和国中央人民政府贸易部副部长沙千里率领的中国商务代表团已在十八日到达莫斯科，就一九五二年苏联和中华人民共和国之间的贸易问题举行谈判。欢迎代表团的有苏联对外贸易部副部长叶列明、对外贸易部东方司司长斯拉德考夫斯基、外交部第一远东司副司长库尔久科夫、对外贸易部东方司中国处处长舍尔比纳、苏联驻中华人民共和国商务副代表丹尼森柯和苏联对外贸易部交际司司长尼可拉耶夫。我国驻苏联大使张闻天暨大使馆人员多人亦前往欢迎。
