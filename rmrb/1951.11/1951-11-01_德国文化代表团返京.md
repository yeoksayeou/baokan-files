Title: German Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The Cultural Delegation from the German Democratic Republic returned to Beijing on October 30th after visiting East China. During their visit to Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou and other cities, the delegation visited factories and schools, toured various scenic spots, and received a warm welcome from the people of each city. On their way back north, the delegation also visited Tianjin, where they visited the North China Region's Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition, visited several schools, factories, workers' sanatoriums, and farms, and held a forum with people from Tianjin's cultural and educational circles to exchange experiences and opinions on cultural work.

### 德国文化代表团返京

1951-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    德国文化代表团返京
    【新华社三十一日讯】德意志民主共和国文化代表团在访问华东以后，已于十月三十日返抵北京。代表团在访问上海、南京、杭州等城市时，曾参观了工厂、学校，并游览了各名胜地区，受到各该市人民的热烈欢迎。代表团在北返途中又访问了天津，在天津参观了华北区城乡物资交流展览会，访问了若干学校、工厂、工人疗养院和农场，并曾与天津市的文化教育界人士举行座谈会，交换文化工作的心得和意见。
