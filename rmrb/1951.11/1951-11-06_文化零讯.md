　　Cultural News Briefs
    The "Soviet Film Photo Exhibition," jointly sponsored by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and New Movie Magazine, is scheduled to open at 10:00 AM on November 6th at the Shuixie in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. The exhibition consists of ten parts, featuring over 800 photos. These photos introduce more than 50 excellent Soviet films (including over ten new films not yet shown in China), and introduce more than 30 film workers and Chinese and Soviet film workers who have won Stalin Prizes. It also explains the work of dubbing and copying films in Chinese. The exhibition is scheduled to close on November 18th.
    The "Resist America, Aid Korea, and Defend Peace Exhibition" organized by the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, which opened on October 2nd at the Wenhua Hall of the Forbidden City in Beijing, has already attracted more than 30,000 visitors. The content of the exhibition is divided into four main parts: the first part is "The Chinese People's Volunteers Have Become an Invincible Force Defending the Motherland and World Peace"; the second part is "Exposing the Aggressive Conspiracy and Aggressive Nature of US Imperialism"; the third part is "The US Imperialist Plan to Enslave Asia Can Be Defeated." The above three parts mainly consist of photos, supplemented by cartoons and charts. The fourth part is "War Trophies of the Chinese People's Volunteers and Gifts from the Korean People to the Chinese People Brought Back by the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea."
    In October, a conference of representatives of reading groups from all ethnic groups in Qinghai was held in Xining. The representatives attending represented more than 46,000 members from over 2,600 reading groups across the province. The conference studied how to organize and develop reading groups, how to use reading groups to enhance the patriotism of all ethnic groups, strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, and promote the rent reduction and land reform movement this winter and next spring. The conference also discussed how to contact newspapers in the future and how to help newspapers become more mass-oriented and improve newspaper distribution.
    On October 24th, all the representatives attending the conference sent a telegram to Hu Qiaomu, Director of the News Administration of the Central People's Government, and Deputy Directors Fan Changjiang and Sa Kongliao, reporting on the proceedings of the conference and pledging to implement the spirit of the conference and complete the tasks assigned to them by the conference.

### 文化零讯

1951-11-06
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

　　文化零讯
    中苏友好协会、苏联对外文化协会及新电影杂志社联合主办的“苏联电影图片展览”，定于十一月六日上午十时在北京中山公园水榭开幕。展览内容共有十部分。展出的图片共八百余帧。这些图片介绍了苏联的优秀影片五十余部（其中包括未在我国放映的新片十余部），并介绍了三十多位电影工作者及荣获斯大林奖金的中、苏电影工作者，还说明华语配音复制影片的工作情况。展览会预定于十一月十八日闭幕。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会举办的“抗美援朝保卫和平展览会”，自十月二日在北京故宫文华殿开幕以来，观众已超过三万人。展览会的内容分四大部分：第一部分为“中国人民志愿军已经成为保卫祖国、保卫世界和平的不可战胜的力量”；第二部分为“揭露美帝国主义的侵略阴谋和侵略本质”；第三部分为“美帝国主义奴役亚洲的计划是可以打败的”，以上三部分是以照片为主，并有漫画、图表等相配合；第四部分是“中国人民赴朝慰问团从朝鲜前线带回来的中国人民志愿军的战利品和朝鲜人民送给中国人民的礼物”。
    十月间，青海各族各界读报组代表会议在西宁举行。出席的代表，代表着全省两千六百余读报组的四万六千多个读报组员。这次会议研究了怎样组织和发展读报组，怎样利用读报组去提高各族人民爱国主义思想，加强抗美援朝运动及推动今冬明春的减租和土地改革运动。会议又讨论了今后如何与报社联系问题及如何帮助报纸办得更群众化和报纸发行的问题。
    参加会议的全体代表在十月二十四日致电中共中央人民政府新闻总署署长胡乔木及副署长范长江、萨空了，报告这次会议的经过，并保证贯彻这次会议的精神，完成会议所交付他们的任务。
