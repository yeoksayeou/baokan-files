Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 1

    All-China Democratic Women's Federation Congratulatory Telegram to Madam Popova, Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, Moscow:
    On behalf of all the women of China, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation extends warm congratulations to you and all our Soviet sisters on the 34th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. Chinese women are willing to redouble their efforts to learn from the noble example of our Soviet sisters, to consolidate the unbreakable friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union, to build our motherland, and to fight against American aggression and defend peace in Asia and the world.
    Cai Chang

### 全国民主妇联贺电

1951-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国民主妇联贺电莫斯科苏联妇女反法西斯委员会波波娃夫人：
    中华全国民主妇女联合会谨代表全中国妇女，向您及全体苏联姊妹热烈祝贺伟大十月社会主义革命三十四周年。中国妇女愿加倍努力学习苏联姊妹的崇高榜样，为巩固中苏两大民族的牢不可破的友谊，为建设我们的祖国，为反对美国侵略、捍卫亚洲与世界和平而奋斗。
    蔡畅
