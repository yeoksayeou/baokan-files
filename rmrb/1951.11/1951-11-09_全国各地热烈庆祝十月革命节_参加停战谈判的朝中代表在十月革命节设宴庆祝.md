Title: Nationwide Celebrations of the October Revolution Anniversary; Korean and Chinese Representatives to the Armistice Negotiations Host Banquet to Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1951-11-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Report: People throughout the country warmly celebrate the 34th anniversary of the Soviet October Socialist Revolution.

    The Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association invited more than 2,000 representatives from government organizations in East China and Shanghai, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, various people's organizations, and local organizations of various democratic parties in East China and Shanghai to a celebration meeting on the evening of the 6th. Soviet Deputy Consul in Shanghai, V. Likhachev, Czechoslovak Consul General in Shanghai, Srimak, and Sun Duc Thang, head of the Vietnamese People's Delegation to China, and all members of the delegation, a total of more than 70 foreign guests, were also invited to attend. At the meeting, Pan Hannian, Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a report. The meeting also passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin.
    At the Tianjin Municipal People's Representative Celebration Meeting, Huang Jing, President of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life attended. Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Matveev, and Polish Acting Consul in Tianjin, Drelevianka, were also invited to participate. At the meeting, Huang Songling, Vice President of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, called on people from all walks of life in the city to continue to strengthen the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship work, carry out the patriotic production increase and economy campaign, and conscientiously study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought. Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Matveev, introduced the great achievements of the Soviet people in construction.
    At the Shenyang Municipal People's Representative Celebration Meeting, more than 1,300 people, including leaders of first-level organizations in Northeast China and representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations, attended. Soviet Consul General in Shenyang, Ledav, and 180 Soviet experts were also invited to participate. Li Zhuoran, Vice President of the Northeast General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Soviet Consul General in Shenyang, Ledav, both spoke at the meeting.
    People from all walks of life in Wuhan have been carrying out various activities to publicize Soviet construction in recent days. Troops, factories, schools, organizations, suburban rural areas, and residential groups have successively held various forums and lectures. The Central and Southern General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also held a celebration meeting.
    People's representatives from all walks of life in Chongqing held a celebration evening, and Soviet friends were also invited to participate. He Long, President of the Southwest General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, called in his speech to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and build the motherland.
    At the Xi'an Municipal People's Representative Celebration Meeting, representatives of workers, students, and women unanimously called on the people of the city to learn from the Soviet Union's experience in nation-building and its spirit of patriotism and internationalism, carry out the production increase and economy campaign, and strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.
    In addition, the people of Nanjing, Dalian, Guangzhou, Dihua, Taiyuan, Wuhu, Wuxi, Hangzhou, Fuzhou, Qingdao and other cities also enthusiastically carried out activities to celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 8th: On the 34th anniversary of the great Soviet October Revolution, the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations hosted a banquet for the representatives of the Korean People's Army delegation to the negotiations. On the evening of the 6th, the Korean People's Army representatives to the armistice negotiations hosted a banquet for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army representatives to the armistice negotiations. Citizens of Kaesong gathered in the city square on the afternoon of the 7th to celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary. The participants were all dressed in festive and brightly colored clothes, warmly celebrating this great festival. Citizens who spoke at the meeting unanimously thanked the Soviet Red Army for its great contribution in liberating the Korean people from the enslavement of the Japanese aggressors, paid tribute to Marshal Stalin, the savior of the Korean people, and the Soviet Red Army under his leadership, and paid tribute to the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 全国各地热烈庆祝十月革命节  参加停战谈判的朝中代表在十月革命节设宴庆祝

1951-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地热烈庆祝十月革命节
    参加停战谈判的朝中代表在十月革命节设宴庆祝
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：全国各地人民热烈庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十四周年。
    中苏友好协会上海分会于六日晚邀请华东暨上海的政府机关、人民解放军驻上海部队、各人民团体及各民主党派华东暨上海地方组织的代表共二千余人，举行庆祝会。苏联驻上海副领事魏立夏金、捷克斯洛伐克驻上海总领事斯立马克及在上海的越南人民访华代表团团长孙德胜与全体团员等外宾七十余人也应邀赴会。大会上，中苏友好协会上海分会副会长潘汉年作了报告。大会并通过打电报向斯大林大元帅致敬。
    天津市各界人民代表庆祝大会上，到会的有中苏友好协会天津分会会长黄敬及各界代表二千多人，苏联驻天津总领事马特维耶夫、波兰驻天津代理领事德列夫梁卡也应邀参加。会上中苏友好协会天津分会副会长黄松龄号召全市各界人民继续加强抗美援朝运动，加强中苏友好工作，开展爱国增产节约运动，认真学习马克思列宁主义毛泽东思想。苏联驻天津总领事马特维耶夫介绍了苏联人民建设的伟大成就。
    沈阳市各界人民代表庆祝大会上，到会的有东北一级机关首长及各民主党派、各人民团体代表等一千三百余人。苏联驻沈阳总领事列达夫和一百八十位苏联专家亦应邀参加。中苏友好协会东北总分会副会长李卓然和苏联驻沈阳总领事列达夫都在大会上讲了话。
    武汉市各界人民连日来开展了宣传苏联建设的各种活动。部队、工厂、学校、机关团体、郊区农村及居民小组，先后举行了各种座谈会、讲演会。中苏友好协会中南总分会，也举行了庆祝大会。
    重庆市各界人民代表举行了庆祝晚会，苏联友人亦应邀参加。中苏友好协会西南总分会会长贺龙在讲话中号召学习苏联先进经验，建设祖国。
    在西安市各界人民代表庆祝大会上，工人、学生、妇女代表在讲话中一致号召全市人民学习苏联的建国经验和爱国主义、国际主义的精神，开展增产节约运动，加强抗美援朝工作。
    此外南京、旅大、广州、迪化、太原、芜湖、无锡、杭州、福州、青岛等城市的人民都热烈地进行了庆祝十月革命节的活动。
    【新华社开城八日电】伟大的苏联十月革命节三十四周年纪念日，参加朝鲜停战谈判代表团的中国人民志愿军谈判代表，设宴招待朝鲜人民军谈判代表。六日晚，朝鲜人民军停战谈判代表曾宴请了中国人民志愿军停战谈判代表。开城市民七日下午在市区广场集会欢庆十月革命节。与会者都穿着节日的艳丽服装，热烈庆祝这个伟大的节日。在会上讲演的市民，一致感谢苏联红军从日本侵略者奴役下解放了朝鲜人民的伟大功勋，向朝鲜人民的救星斯大林大元帅及其领导下的苏联红军致敬，并向英勇的中国人民志愿军致敬。
