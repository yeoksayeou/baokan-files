Title: China Democratic National Construction Association Congratulatory Telegram

Author:

Date: 1951-11-07
Page: 1

    China Democratic National Construction Association Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Stalin:
    On the occasion of the thirty-fourth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, we extend our warm congratulations to you and the great Soviet people! And we wish the Soviet Union's socialist construction cause to develop increasingly, the friendship between China and the Soviet Union to be increasingly consolidated, and the strength of the world peace and democracy camp to be increasingly strengthened and consolidated!
    China Democratic National Construction Association

### 民主建国会贺电

1951-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    民主建国会贺电斯大林大元帅：
    欣逢伟大的十月社会主义革命三十四周年纪念日，谨向阁下及伟大的苏联人民致热烈的祝贺！并祝苏联社会主义建设事业的日益发展，中苏两国友谊的日益巩固，世界和平民主阵营力量的日益壮大和巩固！
    民主建国会
