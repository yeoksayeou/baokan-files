Title: Volunteer Army Combat Hero Observation Delegation Du Ping and Seven Others Return to Korean Front

Author:

Date: 1951-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 16th: Du Ping, the head of the National Day observation delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat heroes, and his party of eight, have recently returned to the Korean front, where they were warmly welcomed by the frontline commanders and fighters.

### 志愿军战斗英雄观礼代表  杜平等八人返抵朝鲜前线

1951-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　志愿军战斗英雄观礼代表
    杜平等八人返抵朝鲜前线
    【新华社朝鲜前线十六日电】中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团团长杜平等一行八人，已于日前回到朝鲜前线，受到前线指挥员和战斗员们的热烈欢迎。
