Title: U.S. Representative's Statements and U.S. Proposals Still Contradictory; I Reiterate Demand for Clarification
Author: 
Date: 1951-11-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 19th: Because the U.S. representative's statements at the November 18th subcommittee meeting of the Korean armistice negotiations and the U.S. proposal of November 17th still contained ambiguities and contradictions, our representative reiterated the demand for clarification at the subcommittee meeting on the 19th. The U.S. representative provided explanations for each point. In order to further study the other party's proposal, our side suggested adjourning the meeting until 11:00 AM on the 21st.

### 美方代表发言和美方提案仍有矛盾  我再度要求对方予以澄清

1951-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美方代表发言和美方提案仍有矛盾
    我再度要求对方予以澄清
    【新华社开城十九日电】由于美方代表在十八日朝鲜停战谈判小组委员会上的发言及美方十七日的提案，仍有不够明确及互相矛盾之处，我方代表在十九日的小组会上再度要求对方予以澄清，美方代表逐一予以解释。为进一步研究对方提案，我方建议休会至二十一日上午十一时。
