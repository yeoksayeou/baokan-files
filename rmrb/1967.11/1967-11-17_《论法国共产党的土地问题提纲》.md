Title: On the Land Question of the French Communist Party
Author: Liening
Date: 1967-11-17
Edition: 4

It is absolutely necessary to distinguish between imperialist wars and revolutionary wars. The former are wars of capitalist plunder, wars to strangle small nations. The latter are wars against counter-revolutionary capitalists and to break free from the shackles of capitalism.
