Title: The Sino-North Korean Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Signs a Protocol
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 6

The tenth session of the Sino-North Korean Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee was held in Pyongyang from November 18 to 23 and signed a protocol on the 23rd.

The protocol was signed by Li Su, Chairman of the Chinese side of the Committee and Deputy Minister of the Chemical Industry, and Won Dong-gu, Chairman of the North Korean side of the Committee and Vice Premier of the Ministry of Chemical Industry.

### 中朝科技合作委员会签订议定书

1967-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    中朝科技合作委员会签订议定书
    新华社平壤二十四日电　中朝科学技术合作委员会第十届会议，于十一月十八日至二十三日在平壤举行，并于二十三日签订了议定书。
    在议定书上签字的：中国方面是委员会中国组主席、化学工业部副部长李苏；朝鲜方面是委员会朝鲜组主席、化学工业省副相元东球。
