Title: Changes in Yemen's Political Situation
Author:
Date: 1967-11-08
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency in Sana'a, the Sana'a radio station announced in the early morning of the 5th that the Yemeni Armed Forces Headquarters issued a statement declaring the removal of Saleh from his positions as President and Prime Minister of the Arab Republic of Yemen.

The next afternoon, the Sana'a radio station announced that Yemen had established a new Republican Council and a new cabinet. The Republican Council consists of Abdurrahman al-Iryani, Ahmed Muhammad al-Numan, and Muhammad Ali al-Osman, with the chairmanship rotating among them. The Yemeni Armed Forces Headquarters announced the removal of Saleh from all his positions while he was out of the country on a visit.

### 也门政局发生变动

1967-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    也门政局发生变动
    据新华社萨那六日电　萨那电台五日清晨发表也门武装部队司令部的一项声明宣布，解除萨拉勒的阿拉伯也门共和国总统、总理等职务。
    第二天下午，萨那电台宣布，也门已经成立了一个新的共和国委员会和一个新的内阁。共和国委员会由阿卜杜·拉赫曼·埃里亚尼、艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·努曼和穆罕默德·阿里·奥斯曼三人组成，委员会主席由他们轮流担任。也门武装部队司令部是在萨拉勒出国访问期间宣布解除他的一切职务的。
