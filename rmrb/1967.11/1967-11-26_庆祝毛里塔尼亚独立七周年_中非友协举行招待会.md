Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Mauritania's Independence
Author:
Date: 1967-11-26
Edition: 5

The China-Africa Friendship Association held a reception today to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the independence of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania. Vice Chairman Ding Xilin of the China-Africa Friendship Association and Ambassador Harchi of Mauritania both spoke at the reception. They wished that the friendship between the peoples of China and Mauritania would continue to develop and strengthen in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism.

Ambassador Harchi of Mauritania said at the reception held by the China-Africa Friendship Association that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao Zedong, has achieved great victories. The people of Mauritania are delighted and inspired by this.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Pengfei and representatives of the revolutionary rebels of the proletariat from relevant departments attended the reception.

### 庆祝毛里塔尼亚独立七周年  中非友协举行招待会

1967-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝毛里塔尼亚独立七周年
    中非友协举行招待会
    据新华社二十五日讯  毛里塔尼亚驻中国大使哈尔希今天在中非友协举行的招待会上说，由毛泽东主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命已经取得了伟大的胜利，毛里塔尼亚人民对此感到欢欣鼓舞。
    中国非洲人民友好协会今天举行招待会，庆祝毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国独立七周年。中非友协副会长丁西林和哈尔希大使，在招待会上先后讲了话，他们祝中国和毛里塔尼亚两国人民的友谊，在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中，继续不断地得到发展和加强。
    外交部副部长姬鹏飞和有关方面的无产阶级革命派代表，出席了招待会。
