Title: Acting President of Pakistan Receives Chinese Friendly Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-11-02
Edition: 6

Acting President of Pakistan Jabar Khan received the Chinese friendly trade delegation led by Jia Shi, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, on October 30 in Rawalpindi. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

	Chinese Chargé d'affaires to Pakistan Xu Ying was also present at the reception.

	Pakistani Minister of Central Commerce Abdul Ghaffar Khan held a banquet to entertain the Chinese delegation on October 29.

### 巴基斯坦代总统接见我友好贸易代表团

1967-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦代总统接见我友好贸易代表团
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第一日电　巴基斯坦代总统贾巴尔·汗十月三十日在这里接见了由中国国际贸易促进委员会副主任贾石率领的中国友好贸易代表团，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英也在座。
    巴基斯坦中央商业部长阿卜杜勒·加福尔·汗十月二十九日曾举行宴会招待中国代表团。
