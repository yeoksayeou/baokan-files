Title: Syrian President Atassi Speaks: Imperialism Cannot Intimidate Heroic Arab People
Author:
Date: 1967-11-23
Edition: 6

Syrian President Atassi delivered a speech at a meeting in Hasakah on the 20th, stating that Arab history has proven that imperialism can never intimidate our heroic Arab people.

Atassi said, "Although the challenges we face are growing, we know how to resist and eliminate them." He continued, "In the face of the immense challenges posed by imperialism and Zionism, every citizen will not hesitate to make sacrifices to defend our homeland. Our fighting people are resisting them with courage and determination."

Atassi mentioned the Vietnamese people's brave struggle against American aggression, saying, "The United States has sent nearly 500,000 modern-equipped American troops to South Vietnam, but every day we hear news of American defeats." Atassi emphasized that the Arab people fighting in our resistance need to learn from the example of the Vietnamese people.

According to reports from Syrian newspapers, the Syrian Cabinet discussed and firmly rejected the proposals put forward by the United States, Britain, and other countries at the United Nations Security Council regarding the Middle East issue. The cabinet announced its rejection of any proposals that aim to recognize the results of Israeli Zionist aggression or erase the Palestinian issue.

Recently, U.S. and British imperialists have successively proposed resolutions at the Security Council, attempting to use the withdrawal of Israeli occupation forces from territories seized during the Middle East war as a "bargaining chip" to force Arab countries to recognize the U.S. imperialist tool, Israel, and guarantee "freedom of navigation" in the Gulf of Aqaba and the Suez Canal. These resolutions blatantly threaten Arab countries not to "use force," attempting to tie the hands of the Arab people in their anti-aggression struggle and strangle the Palestinian people's struggle to reclaim their homeland.

According to reports, the Syrian Cabinet also discussed measures to mobilize all forces to resist imperialist-Zionist aggression.

### 叙利亚国家元首阿塔西发表谈话  帝国主义吓不倒英雄的阿拉伯人民  叙政府拒绝美英在联合国提出的关于中东问题的提案

1967-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚国家元首阿塔西发表谈话
    帝国主义吓不倒英雄的阿拉伯人民
    叙政府拒绝美英在联合国提出的关于中东问题的提案
    新华社大马士革二十一日电　据这里的报纸报道，叙利亚国家元首阿塔西二十日在哈塞克举行的一次会上发表讲话说，阿拉伯的历史证实，帝国主义决不可能吓倒我们英雄的阿拉伯人民。
    阿塔西说，虽然我们面临的挑战大起来了，但我们知道如何反对和消灭它。他接着说，在反击帝国主义和犹太复国主义的巨大挑战的时候，每一个公民会毫不犹豫地作出牺牲来保卫祖国。我们战斗的人民以英勇和坚定的气概来抵抗他们。
    阿塔西谈到越南人民抗美救国的英勇斗争时说，美国向南越派出了近五十万装备现代化的美国兵，但是我们每天听到的是美国兵打败仗的消息。阿塔西强调指出，我们战斗中的阿拉伯人民在我们的反抗斗争中需要学习越南人民的榜样。
    新华社大马士革二十一日电　据叙利亚《复兴报》报道，叙利亚内阁十九日讨论了美英等国在联合国安全理事会上提出的关于中东问题的一些提案后，表示坚决拒绝这些提案，并宣布拒绝任何企图导致确认以色列犹太复国主义者的侵略结果或勾销巴勒斯坦问题的提案。
    最近，美、英帝国主义在安理会上先后提出提案，企图用以色列侵略军撤出在中东战争中占领的领土作为“交换条件”，迫使阿拉伯国家承认美帝侵略工具以色列，并保证亚喀巴湾和苏伊士运河的“航运自由”。这些提案还明目张胆地威胁阿拉伯国家不得“使用武力”，妄图捆住阿拉伯人民反侵略的手脚和阴谋扼杀巴勒斯坦人民收复家园的斗争。
    据报道，叙利亚内阁还讨论了为抵抗帝国主义—犹太复国主义的侵略而采取的动员一切力量的措施。
