Title: Delegation of the Japan Committee for Asia-Africa Solidarity Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-11-11
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Asia-Africa Solidarity, a delegation of six members from the Japan Committee for Asia-Africa Solidarity, led by Chairman and Standing Committee Member Hayashi Takeo, arrived in Beijing by airplane today.

### 亚非人民团结日本委员会代表团到京

1967-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结日本委员会代表团到京
    新华社十日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请，前来我国访问的亚非人民团结日本委员会代表团一行六人，在团长、常任理事林毅率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
