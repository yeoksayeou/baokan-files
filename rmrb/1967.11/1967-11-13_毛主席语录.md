Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-11-13
Edition: 1

The great situation of the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat throughout the country is excellent, not just good. The overall situation is better than at any other time in the past. The important sign of this excellent situation is that the masses of the people have been fully mobilized. Never before has a mass movement been mobilized so extensively and deeply as this one.

### 毛主席语录

1967-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    全国的无产阶级文化大革命形势大好，不是小好。整个形势比以往任何时候都好。形势大好的重要标志，是人民群众充分发动起来了。从来的群众运动都没有象这次发动得这么广泛，这么深入。
