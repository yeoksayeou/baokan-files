Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Watches the Revolutionary Modern Ballet "The Partisan"
Author:
Date: 1967-11-01
Edition: 5

Comrade Enver Hoxha watched the revolutionary modern ballet "The Partisan."

The first performance of "The Partisan" in Albania is another significant achievement in the revolutionary transformation of cultural workers.

"The Partisan" vividly reflects the glorious achievements of the Albanian people and women in the national liberation war under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Communist Party (the predecessor of the Labor Party).

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 29th: The first revolutionary modern ballet in Albania, "The Partisan," premiered at the Tirana Cultural Palace on the 27th and was warmly received by the audience.

Albania's beloved leader, Comrade Enver Hoxha, along with other party and government leaders such as Shehu and List, and representatives attending the Sixth Congress of the Albanian Women's Union, watched the performance together.

Invited to watch the performance were also women's delegations from various countries and regions attending the Sixth Congress of the Albanian Women's Union.

The revolutionary modern ballet "The Partisan," performed by the Albanian National Opera and Ballet Theatre, vividly depicts the heroic armed struggle of the Albanian people and women under the wise leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Communist Party (the predecessor of the Labor Party) during the national liberation war. The staging of Albania's first revolutionary modern ballet "The Partisan" is another fruitful achievement in the continuous revolutionary transformation of Albanian cultural workers.

After the performance, the entire audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically with the performers, shouting, "Enver—Labor Party!"

### 霍查同志观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《游击队员》  阿尔巴尼亚首次演出《游击队员》是文艺工作者革命化的又一硕果

1967-11-01
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《游击队员》
    阿尔巴尼亚首次演出《游击队员》是文艺工作者革命化的又一硕果
    《游击队员》生动地反映了阿人民和妇女在以霍查同志为首的共产党（劳动党前身）的领导下进行民族解放战争的光辉业绩
    新华社地拉那二十九日电　阿尔巴尼亚第一个现代题材的革命化的大型芭蕾舞剧《游击队员》，二十七日晚在地拉那文化宫首次演出，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民敬爱的领袖霍查以及谢胡、列希等其他党政领导人和出席阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会第六次代表大会的代表一起，观看了演出。
    应邀观看演出的还有应邀出席阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会第六次代表大会的各个国家和地区的妇女代表团。
    由阿尔巴尼亚国家歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《游击队员》，生动地反映了阿尔巴尼亚人民和妇女在以霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚共产党（劳动党的前身）的英明领导下，在民族解放战争时期所进行的英勇的武装斗争。阿尔巴尼亚第一个革命现代芭蕾舞剧《游击队员》搬上舞台，是阿尔巴尼亚文艺工作者不断实现革命化的又一丰硕成果。
    演出结束后，全体观众起立，同演员一起热烈鼓掌，欢呼：“恩维尔—劳动党！”
