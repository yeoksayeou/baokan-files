Title: Lao Patriotic Soldiers and Civilians Achieve Great Victories in Continuous Brave Battles and Proactive Attacks on the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1967-11-07
Edition: 5

Lao patriotic soldiers and civilians have been continuously fighting bravely and proactively attacking the enemy over the past two months, achieving new and great victories. They have smashed multiple "sweeping" operations and attacks by the American aggressors and their lackeys, and have continuously and proactively attacked the enemy, annihilating a large number of enemy forces and achieving new and great victories.

According to broadcasts by the Pathet Lao radio station, in September, Lao patriotic soldiers and civilians engaged in 116 battles with the enemy, killing 945 enemy personnel, shooting down or destroying 12 American aircraft, sinking 8 enemy boats, destroying 3 military vehicles, blowing up two ammunition depots and one bridge, and seizing a large amount of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

Since October, Lao patriotic soldiers and civilians have continued to bravely fight the enemy, and victories have been continuously reported.

In mid-October, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Xieng Ngeun and Bassac counties of Luang Prabang Province smashed the attacks by American aggressors and their lackeys, who had gathered a large number of right-wing troops and bandits. According to preliminary information, the soldiers and civilians of this province killed 92 enemy personnel from October 16 to 25. Additionally, the soldiers and civilians of this province simultaneously attacked the command post of the enemy's 1st Paratrooper Battalion and the 39th Infantry Battalion in the Vientiane area on October 11, and also shelled the enemy stronghold in the Phou Voi area, killing a total of 121 enemy personnel.

In early October, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Xieng Khouang Province launched a fierce attack on the enemy stationed in the Phou Khao and Muan Khoun areas, killing 20 enemy personnel on the spot. The patriotic armed guerrilla forces and local troops in the Muan Khoun area attacked the enemy's supply lines on October 18 and 19, killing 19 enemy personnel. Additionally, the patriotic soldiers and civilians of this province repelled enemy attacks on the Phou Hong and Phou Voi areas from October 1 to 18, killing 88 enemy personnel.

The patriotic armed local forces and guerrilla units in the Ban Kang village of Sam Neua Province, with the assistance of local people, launched a fierce attack on a company of bandits stationed in this village on October 14, liberating Ban Kang village.

In addition, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Vientiane, Xam Neua, and Xaisomboun provinces have also inflicted heavy blows on the enemy in multiple battles, achieving great victories.

According to another report, from early October to October 19, Lao patriotic soldiers and civilians also shot down 11 American aggressor aircraft and damaged several more.

### 连续英勇作战　主动袭击敌人  老挝爱国军民近两月来取得巨大战果

1967-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    连续英勇作战　主动袭击敌人
    老挝爱国军民近两月来取得巨大战果
    本报综合报道　老挝爱国军民近两个月以来，连续英勇作战，粉碎了美国强盗及其走狗的多次“扫荡”和进攻，并不断主动袭击敌人，歼灭了敌人大批有生力量，取得了新的巨大战果。
    据巴特寮电台广播，九月份老挝爱国军民同敌人作战一百一十六次，消灭敌人九百四十五名，击落击毁美国飞机十二架，击沉敌艇八艘，击毁军车三辆，炸毁弹药库两座、桥梁一座，并缴获大批武器弹药和军用物资。
    十月份以来，老挝爱国军民继续英勇杀敌，不断传来捷报。
    十月中旬琅勃拉邦省川银和巴森县的爱国军民，粉碎了美国强盗及其走狗纠集大批右派军队和土匪的进攻。据初步消息，这个省的军民从十月十六日到二十五日的十天中歼敌九十二人。另外，这个省军民于十月十一日同时袭击了会娘地区敌人第一伞兵营指挥所和敌人第三十九步兵营，并且炮轰了富威地区敌军据点，共消灭一百二十一名敌人。
    川圹省爱国军民十月初猛烈袭击驻扎在富好和芒铳地区的敌人，当场打死二十名敌人。芒铳地区爱国武装游击队和地方部队十月十八日和十九日袭击敌人交通线，消灭敌人十九名。另外，这个省爱国军民在十月一日到十八日期间，接连打退敌人向富洪和富威地区的进攻，消灭敌人八十八名。
    桑怒省山康乡爱国武装地方部队和游击队，在当地人民协助下，十月十四日痛击盘踞在这个乡的一连土匪，解放了山康乡。
    此外，万象、甘蒙和阿速坡等省的爱国军民，在和敌人的多次作战中，也给予敌人很大打击，取得了很大战果。
    另据报道，老挝爱国军民从十月初到十月十九日，还击落美国强盗飞机十一架，击伤多架。
                                         （据新华社讯）
