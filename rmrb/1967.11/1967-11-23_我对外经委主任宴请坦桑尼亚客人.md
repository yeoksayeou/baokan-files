Title: The Minister of the State Commission for Foreign Economic Relations Hosts a Banquet for Tanzanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-11-23
Edition: 6

The Minister of the State Commission for Foreign Economic Relations, Fang Yi, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Tanzanian health delegation led by the Tanzanian Minister of Health and Housing, Sabu.

Fang Yi and Sabu both spoke at the banquet. They pointed out that the anti-imperialist solidarity and friendship between the Chinese and Tanzanian peoples are becoming increasingly solid and cannot be destroyed by imperialism.

Relevant officials such as Ding Xilin, Xie Huai, and representatives of the proletarian revolutionary faction of the Ministry of Health attended the banquet.

Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, and diplomatic staff from the Tanzanian embassy were also invited to attend the banquet.

The Tanzanian health delegation led by the Tanzanian Minister of Health and Housing, Sabu, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 21st.

### 我对外经委主任宴请坦桑尼亚客人

1967-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我对外经委主任宴请坦桑尼亚客人
    新华社二十二日讯  对外经委主任方毅今晚举行宴会，欢迎由坦桑尼亚卫生和住房部长沙巴率领的坦桑尼亚卫生代表团。
    方毅和沙巴在宴会上讲话。他们指出中国和坦桑尼亚人民之间的反帝团结和友谊日益巩固，是帝国主义破坏不了的。
    有关方面负责人丁西林、谢怀德等以及卫生部无产阶级革命派代表，出席了宴会。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了宴会。
    由坦桑尼亚卫生和住房部长沙巴率领的坦桑尼亚卫生代表团，是二十一日晚上乘飞机到达北京的。
