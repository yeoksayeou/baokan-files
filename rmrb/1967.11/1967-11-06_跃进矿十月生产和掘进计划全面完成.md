Title: Yujin Mine Completes October Production and Excavation Plans
Author:
Date: 1967-11-06
Edition: 4

The revolutionary cadres and workers of the Yujin Mine of the Henan Hengbei Mining Bureau, with the slogan "Criticize and Repudiate Revisionism, Struggle Against Selfishness," have been vigorously promoting revolution and vigorously pushing production. In October, the mine fully completed the national plan for coal production and excavation progress.

### 跃进矿十月生产和掘进计划全面完成

1967-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    跃进矿十月生产和掘进计划全面完成
    河南鹤壁矿务局跃进矿的无产阶级革命派和革命职工，以“斗私，批修”为纲，狠抓革命，猛促生产，十月份煤炭生产和掘进进尺全面完成国家计划。
