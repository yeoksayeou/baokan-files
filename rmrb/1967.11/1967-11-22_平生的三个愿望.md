Title: Lifelong Wishes
Author:
Date: 1967-11-22
Edition: 5

A student from Guatemala said: “I have three wishes in my life: First, to obtain a Chairman Mao badge; second, to get a copy of ‘Selected Works of Mao Zedong’; third, one day I want to go to China, to Beijing, to see with my own eyes our great leader Chairman Mao!”

### 平生的三个愿望

1967-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    平生的三个愿望
    危地马拉一位学生说：“我的一生有三个愿望：第一，得到一枚毛主席像章；第二，得到一本《毛泽东选集》；第三，有朝一日我要到中国去，到北京去，去亲眼见见我们伟大的领袖毛主席！”
