Title: The Chinese Red Cross Donates to Aid Victims of Flooding in Mali
Author:
Date: 1967-11-11
Edition: 6

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Bamako, on the morning of the 3rd, Liu He Lin, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Mali, met with Dolo Somine, the chairman of the Malian Red Cross. On behalf of the Chinese Red Cross, he presented a message of condolence and a cash donation of 100,000 yuan (20 million Malian francs) to aid the people of Mali affected by the flooding of the Niger River.

### 我红十字会捐款救济马里灾民

1967-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    我红十字会捐款救济马里灾民
    据新华社巴马科电　中国驻马里大使馆临时代办刘和林，三日上午会见了马里红十字会主席多洛·索米内，代表中国红十字会，将一份慰问电和救济尼日尔河泛滥受灾的马里人民的十万元人民币（二千万马里法郎）现金捐款交给他。
