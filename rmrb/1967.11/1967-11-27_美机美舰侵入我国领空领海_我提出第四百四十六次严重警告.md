Title: U.S. Aircraft and Vessels Intrude into Our Country's Airspace and Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 6

On November 24, from 8:23 to 10:52, a U.S. military vessel intruded into the territorial waters of China's Xisha Islands, specifically the area around the Xian De Islands and Dong Island. On the same day, from 10:22 to 10:32, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the area east of Bei Island, Zhong Island, and Shi Island in the Xisha Islands. On November 25, from 10:05 to 10:19 and from 14:20 to 14:32, two U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the territorial waters west of Guangdong Province's Leizhou Peninsula and northwest of Hainan Island.

In response to these military provocations by U.S. aircraft and vessels, the spokesperson for China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 446th serious warning.

### 美机美舰侵入我国领空领海  我提出第四百四十六次严重警告

1967-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美机美舰侵入我国领空领海
    我提出第四百四十六次严重警告
    新华社二十五日讯　十一月二十四日八时二十三分至十时五十二分美国军舰一艘，侵入我西沙群岛的宣德群岛及东岛地区领海；同日十时二十二分至十时三十二分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我西沙群岛的北岛、中岛及石岛以东地区领空。十一月二十五日十时零五分至十时十九分及十四时二十分至十四时三十二分美国军用飞机各一架，侵入我广东省雷州半岛以西、海南岛西北领海上空。
    对于美机、美舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百四十六次严重警告。
