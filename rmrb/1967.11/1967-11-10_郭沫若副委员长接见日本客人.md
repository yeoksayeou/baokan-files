Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-11-10
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo today met with the delegation from the Kobe branch of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, led by the president of the Friendship Trade Company, Murakami Masajiro, and the secretary-general, Watanabe Takao, along with the entire delegation. He held friendly discussions with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社九日讯　郭沫若副委员长今天接见了日本国际贸易促进协会神户支局友好商社访华代表团团长村上正二郎、秘书长渡贵雄和全团人员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
