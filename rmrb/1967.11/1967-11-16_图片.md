Title: Forever Read Chairman Mao's Works
Author: Huang Zhenyong
Date: 1967-11-16
Edition: 2

We will forever read Chairman Mao's books, listen to Chairman Mao's words, and act according to Chairman Mao's instructions. We will be good soldiers of Chairman Mao!

	Huang Zhenyong
