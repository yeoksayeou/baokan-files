Afghan King Issues Order to Appoint New Cabinet

Afghan King Muhammad Zahir Shah issued a royal decree on the 16th to appoint Nur Ahmad Etemadi as Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and appointed new members of the cabinet.

The new cabinet includes First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Education Ali Ahmad Popal; Second Deputy Prime Minister Abdulla Yaftali; Minister of Defense General Khan Muhammad; Minister of the Interior Muhammad Omar Wardak, and others.

The Afghan People's Assembly, after three days of discussion starting on the 13th, voted to approve the new cabinet in the evening session on the 15th.

The former Prime Minister Muhammad Hashim Maywandwal and his cabinet resigned on October 11th.

Sudanese Students Protest in Support of the Palestinian People

Thousands of Sudanese students held a demonstration in the capital Khartoum on the 2nd to support the Palestinian people's just struggle against the U.S.-Israeli aggression.

The demonstrators chanted slogans such as "Down with American imperialism!" and "Down with Israel!" along the way.

In the pamphlets distributed, the students pointed out: "The only way to liberate Palestine is to take up arms, not through diplomatic negotiations or compromise."

    阿富汗国王发布命令任命新内阁
    阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿十六日发布王室命令，任命努尔·艾哈迈德·埃特马迪为首相兼外交大臣，并且任命了新内阁的阁员。
    新内阁包括第一副首相兼教育大臣阿里·艾哈迈德·波帕尔；第二副首相阿卜杜拉·雅夫塔利；国防大臣汗·穆罕默德上将；内政大臣穆罕默德·奥马尔·瓦尔达克等。
    阿富汗人民院从十三日起经过三天的讨论，在十五日晚上的会议上对新内阁投了信任票。
    前首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔和他的内阁是在十月十一日向国王提出辞职的。
    苏丹学生示威支持巴勒斯坦人民
    数千名苏丹学生二日在首都喀土穆举行了示威游行，支持巴勒斯坦人民反对美以侵略的正义斗争。
    示威游行的群众一路上不断高呼“打倒美帝国主义！”“打倒以色列！”等口号。
    学生们在散发的小册子中指出：“解放巴勒斯坦的唯一道路是拿起武器，而不是靠外交谈判或者妥协。”　（据新华社讯）
