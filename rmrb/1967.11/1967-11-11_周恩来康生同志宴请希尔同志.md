Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng Host a Banquet for Comrade Hill
Author:
Date: 1967-11-11
Edition: 1

	Comrade Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, members of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, hosted a banquet today to welcome Comrade Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and Comrade N. Gallagher. Also present at the banquet was Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee. Before the banquet, Comrade Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng had a very cordial conversation with Comrade Hill and others.

### 周恩来康生同志宴请希尔同志

1967-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来康生同志宴请希尔同志
    新华社十日讯　中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志和康生同志，今天设宴欢迎澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席希尔同志以及诺·加拉格尔同志。参加宴会的有中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志。宴会前，周恩来同志和康生同志同希尔等同志进行了极为亲切的谈话。
