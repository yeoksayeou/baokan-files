Title: Comrade Hill Watches the Modern Revolutionary Peking Opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy"
Author:
Date: 1967-11-25
Edition: 1

Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Chairman E.F. Hill and Comrade N. Gallagher attended a cultural evening held in their honor today, watching the modern revolutionary Peking opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy."

Accompanying Comrades Hill and others to watch the performance were CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee members Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, Cultural Revolution Group First Deputy Director Jiang Qing, and CPC Central Committee Secretariat member Liu Ningyi. As they entered the evening's venue, the entire audience erupted in prolonged, enthusiastic applause. After the performance, Comrades Hill and Gallagher, accompanied by Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others, walked onto the stage amidst enthusiastic applause, shaking hands warmly with the performers and congratulating them on their successful performance. (See attached photograph)

After watching the modern revolutionary Peking opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy" with Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and Liu Ningyi, Comrades Hill and Gallagher posed for a group photo with the performers on stage.

### 希尔同志看现代革命京剧《智取威虎山》  周恩来、康生、江青、刘宁一等同志陪同观看演出

1967-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    希尔同志看现代革命京剧《智取威虎山》
    周恩来、康生、江青、刘宁一等同志陪同观看演出
    新华社二十四日讯　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席爱·弗·希尔同志和诺·加拉格尔同志，今天出席了为欢迎他们而举行的文艺晚会，观看了现代革命京剧《智取威虎山》。
    陪同希尔等同志观看演出的，有中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志、康生同志，中央文革小组第一副组长江青同志，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志等。当他们走进晚会会场的时候，全场发出长时间的热烈掌声。演出结束以后，希尔同志和加拉格尔同志，由周恩来、康生、江青等同志陪同，在热烈的掌声中走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，祝贺演出成功。（附图片）
    希尔同志和加拉格尔同志，由周恩来、康生、江青、刘宁一同志陪同观看现代革命京剧《智取威虎山》以后，上台和演员合影。
     新华社记者　王刚法摄
