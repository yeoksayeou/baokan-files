Title: Asia-Africa Journalists Association and All-China Journalists Association Hold a Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-11-13
Edition: 6

The Asia-Africa Journalists Association and the All-China Journalists Association held a reception tonight to warmly welcome the delegation of young journalists from the Japan Journalists Union, led by team leader Matsuoka Nobuo. They are visiting China for friendly exchanges and to participate in the Asia-Africa Journalists Training Class.

Secretary-General Charan of the Asia-Africa Journalists Association, Vice Chairman Tang Pingzhuo of the All-China Journalists Association, and team leader Matsuoka Nobuo each made a toast at the reception, wishing for the great unity of the Asian and African peoples and the long life of the great leader of the world's people, Chairman Mao!

Tang Pingzhuo, on behalf of the All-China Journalists Association, presented each member of the delegation with a set of revolutionary classics, "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."

Members of the Secretariat of the Asia-Africa Journalists Association, Secretary-General of the Asia-Africa Writers' Permanent Bureau Senanayake, and others attended the reception.

### 亚非记协和中国记协举行酒会  欢迎日本青年记者团

1967-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非记协和中国记协举行酒会
    欢迎日本青年记者团
    新华社十一日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会和中华全国新闻工作者协会，今晚举行酒会，热烈欢迎由团长松冈信夫率领的日本记者同盟青年记者团。他们是来我国进行友好访问并参加亚非记者学习班学习的。
    亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主任唐平铸和松冈信夫团长先后在酒会上祝酒，祝愿亚非人民大团结，祝愿全世界人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！
    唐平铸代表中华全国新闻工作者协会，向代表团成员每人赠送了一套革命宝书《毛泽东选集》。
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处成员，亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克等出席了酒会。
