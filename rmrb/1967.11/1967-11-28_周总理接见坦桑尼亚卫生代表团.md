Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Tanzanian Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-11-28
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Enlai today received Tanzanian Minister of Health and Housing Austin Shaba and the members of the Tanzanian health delegation he led, engaging in a warm and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Minister of Foreign Economic Relations Fang Yi, Deputy Minister Xie Huai De, and others. Tanzanian Ambassador to China Mwaluko was also present. (Photo attached)

Premier Zhou Enlai poses for a group photo with Tanzanian Minister of Health and Housing Austin Shaba (front left fourth) and the members of the Tanzanian health delegation he led.

### 周总理接见坦桑尼亚卫生代表团

1967-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见坦桑尼亚卫生代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天接见坦桑尼亚卫生和住房部长奥斯丁·沙巴以及由他率领的坦桑尼亚卫生代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外经委主任方毅、副主任谢怀德等。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也在座。（附图片）
    周恩来总理接见坦桑尼亚卫生和住房部长奥斯丁·沙巴（前左四）以及由他率领的坦桑尼亚卫生代表团的成员时合影。　　　　　　
　　新华社记者　王敬德摄
