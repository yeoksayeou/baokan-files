Title: Somali Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Somali Republic Cultural Delegation, led by its head Muhammad Jama Urdoh, arrived in Beijing by plane today to make a friendly visit to China.

### 索马里文化代表团到京

1967-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    索马里文化代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应对外文委邀请前来我国进行友好访问的索马里共和国文化代表团，在团长穆罕默德·贾马·乌尔多率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
