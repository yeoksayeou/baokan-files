Title: A Brilliant Ambush Battle in the Mekong Delta
Author:
Date: 1967-11-22
Edition: 6

In the Mekong Delta, a brilliant ambush battle was fought. The military and civilian people of Mỹ Tho Province in South Vietnam completely annihilated an American camp.

In the provinces of Tân An and Dĩ An, the military and civilian people annihilated two enemy main force camps in three days.

HANOI, Nov. 21 (Xinhua) — According to a report from the Liberation Communication Society of South Vietnam, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Mỹ Tho Province in South Vietnam launched a brilliant ambush battle on the Fourth Highway on November 17, completely annihilating a U.S. invasion infantry camp. More than 300 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, six 105mm cannons were destroyed, and a large amount of weapons and military supplies were seized.

The report said that on November 17, the U.S. aggressors dispatched an American infantry camp and part of the forces of the puppet Seventh Division to "sweep" through some villages along the Fourth Highway in Mỹ Tho Province's Gò Công District in an attempt to "clear the road." The People's Liberation Armed Forces immediately launched a fierce attack and launched an offensive against the enemy's stronghold in the village of Tháp Mười, Gò Công District. At the same time, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also heavily shelled the enemy's artillery positions in Thiên Hậu Town, turning the cannons there into dummies, and quickly annihilating this American infantry camp. The report said that this was the first time the People's Liberation Armed Forces had completely annihilated an American infantry camp in the Mekong Delta region.

According to a report from the Liberation Communication Society of South Vietnam, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Tân An and Dĩ An provinces in South Vietnam launched fierce attacks on the enemy from November 10 to 12, completely annihilating two puppet main force camps, one security company, and severely damaging one main force camp. A large number of enemy weapons and military supplies were destroyed and seized.

On the night of November 11 and the early morning of November 12, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Tân An Province simultaneously launched fierce attacks on the enemy in Kiến Đức and Tân Trụ districts, killing and wounding more than 500 enemy soldiers. Among them, a puppet main force camp and a security company were completely annihilated, four enemy platoons were severely damaged, 12 cannons and mortars were destroyed, two aircraft were shot down, and many bunkers, barracks, and artillery positions were destroyed.

The report said that in Kiến Đức District, the People's Liberation Armed Forces attacked the enemy's military sub-district and other U.S.-puppet military strongholds. After only 30 minutes of battle, a puppet camp and a security company were completely annihilated, three "Civil Defense" platoons and one artillery platoon were severely damaged.

In Tân Trụ District, the People's Liberation Armed Forces attacked the U.S. invaders and the enemy's artillery positions in the military sub-district of Tân Trụ, killing and wounding 85 U.S. invaders, and destroying eight 105mm cannons.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces of Dĩ An Province launched fierce attacks on the enemy in Châu Thành District over two days from November 10 to 11, completely annihilating a puppet main force camp and a "pacification" force, severely damaging one main force camp, and killing, wounding, and capturing more than 500 enemy soldiers.

During this period, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also launched fierce attacks on the military training camp in Minh Long and the enemy stronghold in Đức Hóa, capturing three enemy bunkers, destroying and damaging two 155mm cannons, burning down 13 barracks, and killing and wounding dozens of enemy soldiers.

### 在湄公河三角洲又打一场漂亮的袭击战  南越美萩省军民全歼美军一个营  新安、迪石两省军民三天歼灭敌人两个主力营

1967-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

    在湄公河三角洲又打一场漂亮的袭击战
    南越美萩省军民全歼美军一个营
    新安、迪石两省军民三天歼灭敌人两个主力营
    新华社河内二十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方美萩省人民解放武装力量十七日在第四号公路上打了一场漂亮的袭击战，全歼美国侵略军一个步兵营，打死打伤敌人三百多名，击毁一○五毫米大炮六门，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    报道说，美国强盗十七日出动美军一个步兵营和伪军第七师的一部分兵力，向美萩省丐?县第四号公路沿线的一些村庄进行“扫荡”，妄图“打通公路”。美萩省人民解放武装力量立即猛烈地进行袭击，并且向丐?县厚美乡的敌军驻地发起进攻，与此同时，人民解放武装力量还猛烈炮轰天瑚镇的敌军炮兵阵地，使这里的大炮变成了哑巴，从而迅速地消灭了这个美军步兵营。报道说，这是人民解放武装力量在湄公河三角洲地区全歼的第一个美军步兵营。
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方新安、迪石两省人民解放武装力量，在十日到十二日三天中，连续猛烈袭击敌人，全歼伪军两个主力营，一个保安连，重创一个主力营，击毁和缴获敌人大量武器和军用物资。
    新安省人民解放武装力量在十一日夜间和十二日凌晨同时猛烈袭击芹得县和新柱县的敌军，打死打伤敌人五百多名，其中全歼伪军一个主力营和一个保安连，重创敌军四个排，击毁大炮和迫击炮十二门，击落飞机二架，摧毁许多碉堡、营房和炮兵阵地。
    报道说，在芹得县，人民解放武装力量袭击敌人军事支区和其他美伪军据点时，仅经过三十分钟的战斗，就全歼伪军一个营和一个保安连，重创三个“民卫”排和一个炮兵排。
    在新柱县，人民解放武装力量袭击盘踞在平静乡的美国侵略军和新柱军事支区的敌军炮兵阵地，打死打伤美国侵略军八十五名，击毁一○五毫米口径大炮八门。
    迪石省人民解放武装力量在十一月十日和十一日两天，猛烈袭击周城县地区的敌人，全歼伪军一个主力营和一支“绥靖队”，重创一个主力营，打死打伤和俘虏敌人五百多名。
    在此期间，人民解放武装力量还猛烈袭击明良地区的军事训练营和德构敌军据点，攻克敌人三个碉堡，击毁击伤一五五毫米口径大炮两门，烧毁营房十三座，打死打伤敌人数十名。
