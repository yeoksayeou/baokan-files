Chairman Mao Meets Comrade Hill

Chairman Mao had an extremely sincere and cordial conversation with Comrade E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), and Comrade N. Gallagher.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Liu Ningyi of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China participated in the meeting.

	November 27, Xinhua News Agency reported: The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, our most beloved and respected great leader Comrade Mao Zedong, met this afternoon with Comrade E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), and Comrade N. Gallagher. Chairman Mao had an extremely sincere and cordial conversation with Comrades Hill and Gallagher. Attending the meeting were Politburo Standing Committee members Comrades Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, and Central Secretariat member Comrade Liu Ningyi.

	Our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao poses for a group photo with Comrades Hill and Gallagher. The third from the right is Comrade Hill. Photo by our reporter Lü Xiangyou.

### 毛主席会见希尔同志  毛主席同希尔同志、加拉格尔同志进行了极为真挚亲切的谈话  周恩来同志、康生同志、刘宁一同志参加会见

1967-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见希尔同志
    毛主席同希尔同志、加拉格尔同志进行了极为真挚亲切的谈话
    周恩来同志、康生同志、刘宁一同志参加会见
    新华社二十七日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席、我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东同志，今天下午会见了澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席爱·弗·希尔同志，以及诺·加拉格尔同志。毛主席同希尔同志、加拉格尔同志，进行了极为真挚亲切的谈话。参加会见的，有中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志、康生同志，中央书记处书记刘宁一同志。（附图片）
    我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席同希尔同志、加拉格尔同志合影。右三是希尔同志。　本报记者　吕相友摄
