Title: Qingdao Pesticide Factory Completes Annual Production Value Plan Ahead of Schedule and in Excess
Author:
Date: 1967-11-23
Edition: 4

The revolutionary masses and revolutionary factions of the Qingdao Pesticide Factory have resolutely implemented Chairman Mao's great line of "grasp revolution, promote production." They have completed the annual production value plan and the plans for the quantity, quality, and cost of major products ahead of schedule by fifty days, with the total production value increasing by 39.9 percent compared to last year. The quality of the products has been steadily improved, and the cost has been reduced month by month.

### 青岛农药厂提前超额完成全年总产值计划

1967-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    青岛农药厂提前超额完成全年总产值计划
    青岛农药厂广大革命派和革命群众，坚决贯彻执行毛主席
  “抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，提前五十天超额完成了全年总产值计划和主要产品的产量、质量、成本计划，总产值比去年全年增长百分之三十九点九，产品质量稳定提高，成本逐月下降。
