Title: Hail to Chairman Mao
Author: Gushla
Date: 1967-11-05
Edition: 5

Tiananmen Square is bathed in sunlight, and the Red Guards shout "Long live Chairman Mao, a thousand, ten thousand times!" His mighty hand is raised high like the North Star in the sky, pointing the way forward. Hail to the great October 1st! Millions of people surge like a vast, tumultuous ocean. Ah! The brilliance and power of Mao Zedong Thought will annihilate all enemies in the world. A little red book in our hands is a torch emitting rays of light; it is like a red flag fluttering in the wind, and like a gleaming bayonet piercing the enemy's heart. I believe with all my heart: the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought, like the sun over the Himalayas, will illuminate the entire world in a brilliant red light.

### 向毛主席致敬
古什拉
1967-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    向毛主席致敬
    阿尔巴尼亚沃德杰水电站职工  古什拉天安门广场充满了阳光，红卫兵千万遍高呼祝毛主席万寿无疆。他的巨手高高举起象高空中的北斗星，指引着前进的方向。向伟大的十月一日致敬！亿万人流象汹涌的波涛翻腾的海洋。啊！毛泽东思想的光辉和力量将把世界上一切敌人消灭光。一本红书我们手中的火炬放射出万丈光芒；象是迎风飘扬的红旗，又象是雪亮的刺刀直刺敌人的心脏。我一千万个相信：毛泽东思想的光辉，象喜马拉雅山上空的太阳，把全世界照得通红明亮。
