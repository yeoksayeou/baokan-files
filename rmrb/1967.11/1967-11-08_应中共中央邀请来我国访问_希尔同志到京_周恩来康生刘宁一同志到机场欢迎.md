Title: Hilary Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1967-11-08
Edition: 1

	At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), arrived in Beijing today by plane for a friendly visit to China. Comrade N. Gallagher also came to China for a visit with Comrade Hill.

	Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrade Kang Sheng, and Member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Ningyi, went to the airport to give a warm welcome.

	Comrade Frank Johnson of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist) and his wife, who are in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome the guests.

### 应中共中央邀请来我国访问  希尔同志到京  周恩来康生刘宁一同志到机场欢迎

1967-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国访问
    希尔同志到京
    周恩来康生刘宁一同志到机场欢迎
    新华社七日讯　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会主席爱·弗·希尔同志，应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请来我国进行友好访问，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。和希尔同志一同来我国访问的，还有诺·加拉格尔同志。
    中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志、康生同志，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志等，前往机场热烈欢迎。
    在北京的澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）的弗兰克·约翰逊同志和夫人，也到机场迎接。
    （附图片）
    希尔同志（右四）应中共中央邀请，来我国进行友好访问，于七日乘飞机到达北京。周恩来、康生、刘宁一同志等到机场热烈欢迎。
                                      新华社记者   王刚法摄
