Title: Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-11-21
Edition: 1

	We must launch a movement to study and apply Chairman Mao's works extensively among the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers, as well as among the broad ranks of cadres and intellectuals. We must take Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle for the cultural revolution.
