Title: Shukairy Denounces Imperialists, Revisionists, and Reactionaries Using the United Nations to Intervene in Palestinian Affairs
Author:
Date: 1967-11-11
Edition: 6

	Shukairy Denounces Imperialists, Revisionists, and Reactionaries Using the United Nations to Intervene in Palestinian Affairs
	The Liberation of Palestine Must Be Achieved Through Armed Struggle

	New China News Agency, Algiers, November 8

	On November 8, Shukairy, Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization, stated in Algiers that the liberation of Palestine can only be achieved through armed struggle and not through speeches at the United Nations.

	Shukairy pointed out, "The United Nations cannot find a political solution to the Palestinian problem because the United Nations is a place for conspiracies." He said, "Our path and our goal are clear: armed struggle is our path, liberation is our goal."

	He emphasized, "Our responsibility is to mobilize the Palestinian people, train the Palestinian people, and arm the Palestinian people, so that the struggle becomes a comprehensive people's war." He said, only the Palestinian people can determine their own destiny. The liberation of Palestine can only be achieved on Palestinian soil.

	Shukairy reaffirmed his opposition to imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries interfering in the affairs of the Palestinian people. He also said, "The Palestinian people have never appointed a king or a president to accept a political solution to the Palestinian problem."

	Regarding the refugee problem, he said the only solution is for them to return to their homes.

	On November 8, Shukairy concluded his visit to Algeria and left Algiers. He held talks with Houari Boumedienne, Chairman of the Algerian Revolutionary Council, and Cherif Belkacem, Coordinator of the Executive Bureau of the Algerian National Liberation Front.

### 舒凯里谴责帝修反利用联合国干预  巴勒斯坦事务巴勒斯坦的解放必须通过武装斗争

1967-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    舒凯里谴责帝修反利用联合国干预
    巴勒斯坦事务巴勒斯坦的解放必须通过武装斗争
    新华社阿尔及尔八日电　巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里八日在阿尔及尔说，巴勒斯坦的解放只能通过武装斗争的道路来实现，而不能通过在联合国发表演说来实现。
    舒凯里指出：“联合国不可能对巴勒斯坦问题求得政治解决，因为联合国是进行阴谋活动的场所。”他说：“我们的道路和我们的目标是明确的：武装斗争是我们的道路，解放是我们的目标。”
    他强调说：“我们的责任是，动员巴勒斯坦人民、训练巴勒斯坦人民、武装巴勒斯坦人民，使战斗成为全面的人民战争。”他说，只有巴勒斯坦人民能够掌握自己的命运。巴勒斯坦的解放只能在巴勒斯坦土地上实现。
    舒凯里重申反对帝国主义、修正主义和反动派干预巴勒斯坦人民的事务。他还说：“巴勒斯坦人民从来没有委派一个国王或一个总统去接受巴勒斯坦问题的政治解决。”
    谈到难民问题时，他说，唯一的解决办法是，他们返回自己的家园。
    舒凯里八日结束了对阿尔及利亚的访问离开阿尔及尔。他和阿尔及利亚革命委员会主席布迈丁和阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线执行书记处协调书记谢里夫·贝勒卡塞姆进行了会谈。
