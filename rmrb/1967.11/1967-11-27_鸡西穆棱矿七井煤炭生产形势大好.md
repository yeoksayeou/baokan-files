Title: The Coal Production Situation at MuLing Mine's No. 7 Shaft is Excellent
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 2

    Over the past five months, the No. 7 Shaft of the MuLing Mine under the Jixi Mining Bureau in Heilongjiang Province has consistently exceeded the state's original coal production plan. Since entering November, the production situation has become even better.

### 鸡西穆棱矿七井煤炭生产形势大好

1967-11-27
第2版()
专栏：

    鸡西穆棱矿七井煤炭生产形势大好
    黑龙江省鸡西矿务局穆棱矿七井，近五个月来月月超额完成国家原煤生产计划。进入十一月份以后，生产形势更好。
