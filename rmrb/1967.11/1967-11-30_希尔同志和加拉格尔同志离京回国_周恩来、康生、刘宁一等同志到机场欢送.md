Title: Comrades Hill and Gallagher Depart Beijing for Their Home Country
Author:
Date: 1967-11-30
Edition: 1

Comrades E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and N. Gallagher, have concluded their visit to our country and departed Beijing by plane today to return home.

During their stay in China, Comrades E.F. Hill and N. Gallagher visited factories, schools, and people's communes in Beijing and Shanghai, where they were warmly welcomed by workers, students, and commune members.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Liu Ningyi, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, were present at the airport to see them off.

Comrade Frank Johnson of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) and his wife, who are in Beijing, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 希尔同志和加拉格尔同志离京回国  周恩来、康生、刘宁一等同志到机场欢送

1967-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    希尔同志和加拉格尔同志离京回国
    周恩来、康生、刘宁一等同志到机场欢送
    新华社二十九日讯　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席爱·弗·希尔同志，以及诺·加拉格尔同志，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    爱·弗·希尔同志和诺·加拉格尔同志在中国期间，在北京和上海参观了工厂、学校和人民公社，受到了工人、学生和社员们的热烈欢迎。
    今天到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志、康生同志，中央书记处书记刘宁一同志。
    在北京的澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）的弗兰克·约翰逊同志和夫人，也到机场送行。
