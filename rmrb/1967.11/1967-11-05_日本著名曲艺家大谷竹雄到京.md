Title: Renowned Japanese Variety Artist Ota Takeo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-11-05
Edition: 4

Renowned Japanese variety artist Ota Takeo, invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 3rd.

### 日本著名曲艺家大谷竹雄到京

1967-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    日本著名曲艺家大谷竹雄到京
    新华社四日讯　应对外友协邀请来我国访问的日本著名曲艺家大谷竹雄，已于三日乘飞机到达北京。
