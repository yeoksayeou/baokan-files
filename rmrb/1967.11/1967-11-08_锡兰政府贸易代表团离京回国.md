Title: The Sri Lankan Government Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1967-11-08
Edition: 5

The Sri Lankan government trade delegation, led by the Minister of Commerce and Trade of Sri Lanka, P. de S. K. Peris, left Beijing by plane this morning, heading back to their country via Guangzhou. The deputy leader of the delegation, the Sri Lankan Ambassador to China, Gunawardena, also boarded the plane to Guangzhou. Those who went to the airport to see them off included the Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Lin Haiyun, and others.

### 锡兰政府贸易代表团离京回国

1967-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府贸易代表团离京回国
    新华社七日讯　由锡兰商业和贸易部长佩里斯率领的锡兰政府贸易代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京经广州回国。代表团副团长、锡兰驻中国大使古纳瓦德纳也同机去广州。前往机场送行的，有对外贸易部代理部长林海云等。
