Title: Tanzanian Health Delegation Head Holds Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1967-11-28
Edition: 5

Tanzanian Health Delegation Head Holds Farewell Banquet

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 27th, Tanzanian Health Delegation Head and Tanzanian Minister of Health and Housing Austin Shaba said at his farewell banquet today, "We have seen in China that the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is excellent, and the masses have been fully mobilized. The Tanzanian people firmly believe that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution will make China more prosperous and stronger."

Shaba said, "The Tanzanian people have deep respect for the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao. We sincerely wish China greater achievements."

Shaba said, "Imperialism and colonialism do not like the friendship between the Tanzanian people and the Chinese people, but no force can destroy this friendship. He thanked China for its aid to Tanzania."

Director of the State Planning Commission, Fang Yi, said in his speech, "The friendship between the peoples of China and Tanzania is built on the foundation of the struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and it is indestructible by imperialism. Our aid to each other is mutual. The Chinese people, after the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, will better support the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of all countries and fulfill our internationalist duties."

Relevant officials and representatives from China were invited to attend the banquet. Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, also attended the banquet.

### 坦桑尼亚卫生代表团团长举行告别宴会

1967-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚卫生代表团团长举行告别宴会
    据新华社二十七日讯  坦桑尼亚卫生代表团团长、坦桑尼亚卫生和住房部长奥斯丁·沙巴今天在他举行的告别宴会上说，我们在中国看到中国无产阶级文化大革命的形势大好，人民群众已经充分发动起来了。坦桑尼亚人民深信，无产阶级文化大革命将使中国变得更繁荣、更强大。
    沙巴说，坦桑尼亚人民对中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席怀有深切的敬意。我们衷心祝愿中国取得更加伟大的成就。
    沙巴说，帝国主义、殖民主义不喜欢坦桑尼亚人民和中国人民之间的友谊，但是任何力量也破坏不了这种友谊。他感谢中国对坦桑尼亚的援助。
    对外经委主任方毅在讲话中说，中国和坦桑尼亚两国人民的友谊，是建立在反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义斗争的基础上的，是帝国主义无法破坏的。我们之间的援助也是相互的。经过无产阶级文化大革命的中国人民，将更好地支援世界各国人民的革命斗争，完成我们应尽的国际主义义务。
    我有关方面负责人和代表应邀出席了宴会。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也出席了宴会。
