Title: Soldiers of the Railway Engineering Corps Remember Chairman Mao's Teachings and Save Coal
Author: Tie Zheng Xuan
Date: 1967-11-25
Edition: 4

    Soldiers of the Eleventh Company's kitchen brigade from a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps have firmly remembered Chairman Mao's teaching to "be thrifty and make revolution." They have carried forward the spirit of saving every penny's worth of coal, thus saving the country a large amount of coal.
