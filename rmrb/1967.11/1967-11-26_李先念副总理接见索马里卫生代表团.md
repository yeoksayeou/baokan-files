Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Meets with Somali Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-11-26
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council met today with a Somali health delegation led by Elmi Ahmed Du'ale, Director of the Office of the Somali Ministry of Health, and held friendly talks with them.

### 李先念副总理接见索马里卫生代表团

1967-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见索马里卫生代表团
    新华社二十五日讯　国务院副总理李先念，今天接见了由索马里卫生部办公厅主任埃勒米·艾哈迈德·杜阿莱率领的索马里卫生代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
