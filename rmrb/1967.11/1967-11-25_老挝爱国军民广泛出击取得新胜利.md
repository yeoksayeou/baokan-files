Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Achieve New Victories in Wide-Ranging Attacks
Author:
Date: 1967-11-25
Edition: 5

Lao patriotic military and civilians have achieved new victories in wide-ranging attacks since early November.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the patriotic military and civilians of various provinces in Laos have continuously launched attacks and achieved many new victories since early November.

In Khammouane Province, the patriotic military and civilians inflicted heavy casualties on the enemy from November 10 to 12, killing more than 50 enemy soldiers.

The patriotic military and civilians defending the heroic Phou Kout Mountain region killed 35 enemy soldiers and wounded many more from October 30 to November 16.

According to another broadcast, the patriotic military and civilians of Xieng Nguen Province killed 51 enemy soldiers and seized a large number of weapons in anti-bandit operations on November 12, 19, and 20. Additionally, the patriotic military and civilians of Luang Prabang, Vientiane, Vang Vieng, and Xam Neua provinces also launched wide-ranging attacks in early November, killing 52 enemy soldiers who were conducting "sweeping" operations and seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies.

### 老挝爱国军民广泛出击取得新胜利

1967-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民广泛出击取得新胜利
    新华社康开二十四日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝各省爱国军民十一月初以来连续出击，取得许多新战果。
    川圹省爱国军民，在十一月十日到十二日期间，痛击来犯的敌人，歼灭敌人五十多名。
    守卫在英雄的富科特山地区的爱国军民，从十月三十日到十一月十六日期间，打死敌人三十五名，打伤多名。
    另据广播，在十一月十二日、十九日和二十日三天内，桑怒省爱国军民在剿匪战斗中，打死敌人五十一名，缴获许多武器。此外，琅勃拉邦、会晒、万象和甘蒙等省爱国军民在十一月上旬也广泛地出击，消灭前往“扫荡”的敌人五十二名，缴获武器和军用物资一批。
