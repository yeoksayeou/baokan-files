Title: Sri Lankan Government Trade Delegation Head Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-11-02
Edition: 6

The head of the Sri Lankan government trade delegation, Sri Lankan Minister of Commerce and Trade C. P. de Silva, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun and others. The deputy head of the Sri Lankan government trade delegation, Sri Lankan Ambassador to China Gunawadana, as well as delegation members who had arrived in Beijing earlier, including Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Agriculture and Food Mahadeva, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 锡兰政府贸易代表团长到京

1967-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府贸易代表团长到京
    新华社一日讯　锡兰政府贸易代表团团长、锡兰商业和贸易部长佩里斯，今晚乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部代理部长林海云等。锡兰政府贸易代表团副团长、锡兰驻中国大使古纳瓦德纳，以及先期到北京的代表团团员、农粮部常任秘书马哈德瓦等也到机场迎接。
