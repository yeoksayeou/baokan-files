Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four More U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-11-25
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians have once again achieved new victories in their punishment of American air pirates. According to reports from here, the civilian militia of Thanh Hoa Province shot down two invading U.S. aircraft on the 20th and 21st. The civilian militia of Hai Phong shot down one U.S. aircraft on the 22nd.

Additionally, it has been reported that the military and civilians of Thua Thien Province shot down one U.S. aircraft on the 17th.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机四架

1967-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民又击落美机四架
    新华社河内二十四日电　越南北方民兵在严惩美国空中强盗的战斗中又立新功。据这里公布，清化省民兵在二十日、二十一日共打下两架入侵的美国飞机，海防民兵二十二日打下一架美国飞机。
    另据补充报道，太平省军民十七日击落美机一架。
