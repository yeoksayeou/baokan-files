Title: The 4602 Unit and Coal Mine Workers in Jingxing Coal Mine Complete the State Production Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author: Bì Bǎo
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 2

The 4602 unit and revolutionary workers of the Jingxing Coal Mine, with the motto "Criticize and Repudiate Revisionism, Oppose Privileges," have used revolutionary spirit to drive production. They have completed this year's state production plan ahead of schedule and exceeded the target.
