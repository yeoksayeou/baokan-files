Title: Chinese Friendship Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1967-11-08
Edition: 5

A delegation led by Jia Shi, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, has returned to Beijing after a friendly visit to Pakistan.

### 我友好贸易代表团访巴基斯坦后回京

1967-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我友好贸易代表团访巴基斯坦后回京
    新华社七日讯　由中国国际贸易促进委员会副主任贾石率领的中国友好贸易代表团，在巴基斯坦进行了友好访问后，已在最近回到北京。
