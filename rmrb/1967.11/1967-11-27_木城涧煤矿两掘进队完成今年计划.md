Title: Two Tunneling Teams at Muchengjian Coal Mine Complete This Year's Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 2

The rock tunneling teams six and two at the Muchengjian Coal Mine in the western suburbs of Beijing, together with revolutionary cadres, workers, and revolutionary masses, under the assistance of the People's Liberation Army in supporting the left and supporting work, have seized the revolution and vigorously promoted production. They have completed the annual national tunneling plan ahead of schedule by 80 days and 87 days, respectively. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 木城涧煤矿两掘进队完成今年计划

1967-11-27
第2版()
专栏：

    木城涧煤矿两掘进队完成今年计划
    京西木城涧煤矿岩石掘进六队和二队的无产阶级革命派和革命职工，在支左、支工的人民解放军帮助下，狠抓革命，猛促生产，分别提前八十一天和八十七天完成了全年国家进尺计划。　（据新华社讯）
