Title: Guangdong's Sugarcane Harvests in Large Areas, Nearly 100 Sugar Mills Start Crushing New Sugar
Author:
Date: 1967-11-23
Edition: 4

Guangdong Province this year has achieved a large-scale bumper harvest of sugarcane. More than ten large-scale sugar mills in the province have started crushing sugar since mid-November. Nearly 100 medium and small sugar mills distributed throughout the province have also completed preparations for crushing and will soon start production.

### 广东甘蔗大面积丰收近百座糖厂开榨新糖

1967-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    广东甘蔗大面积丰收近百座糖厂开榨新糖
    广东省今年种植的甘蔗获得了大面积丰收。全省十多座大型糖厂，从本月中旬起已经陆续开始榨糖。分布在省内各地的近百座中小型糖厂，也已做好开榨前的准备工作，即将陆续投入生产。
