Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Visits Albania
Author:
Date: 1967-11-24
Edition: 5

A trade delegation led by the acting minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Lin Haiyun, departed Beijing by airplane this afternoon to visit Albania. The delegation's purpose is to negotiate and sign the 1968 trade agreement.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Li Qiang. The Albanian ambassador to China, Nataneli, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 我政府贸易代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚访问

1967-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚访问
    新华社二十三日讯　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团，为商谈并签订一九六八年度贸易议定书，今天下午乘飞机离开北京前往阿尔巴尼亚。
    到机场送行的，有对外贸易部副部长李强。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利也到机场送行。
