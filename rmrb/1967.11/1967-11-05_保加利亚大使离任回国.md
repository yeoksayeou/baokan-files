Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Leaves Post to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1967-11-05
Edition: 4

Bulgarian Ambassador Leaves Post to Return Home

New China News Agency, November 4th: Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Stoichev, has been recalled and will leave today to return home.
