Title: Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou Commemorate the 101st Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1967-11-13
Edition: 3

Today marks the 101st anniversary of the birth of Dr. Sun Yat-sen. Leaders of the China National Democratic Construction Association (Minzhu Jianxie Dang) held a commemorative ceremony at the Zhongshan Memorial Hall in Biyun Temple, Xingshan.

Those who attended the ceremony included Vice Chairman Cai Tingkai of the Minzhu Jianxie Dang Central Committee, and members of the Minzhu Jianxie Dang Central Committee in Beijing such as Wang Kunlun, Lu Han, Lu Yuwen, Zhu Lufan, Zhu Yushan, Liu Fei, Shao Lizi, Chen Qisheng, and Chen Qiyuan. Mr. Cheng Siyuan also participated in the ceremony.

Representatives of the proletarian revolutionary faction of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party's United Front Work Department also attended the ceremony.

At the Zhongshan Memorial Hall, Cai Tingkai, on behalf of the Minzhu Jianxie Dang Central Committee, presented a floral basket to the portrait of Dr. Sun Yat-sen.

Today, in Shanghai at the Sun Yat-sen Former Residence and in Guangzhou at the Zhongshan Memorial Hall, representatives from relevant departments and mass organizations also held commemorative activities.

### 北京、上海、广州等地纪念孙中山诞生一百零一周年

1967-11-13
第3版()
专栏：

    北京、上海、广州等地
    纪念孙中山诞生一百零一周年
    新华社十二日讯　今天是孙中山先生诞生一百零一周年，中国国民党革命委员会中央负责人前往香山碧云寺中山纪念堂举行纪念仪式。
    参加纪念仪式的有民革中央副主席蔡廷锴，民革中央部分在京常委王昆仑、卢汉、卢郁文、朱学范、朱蕴山、刘斐、邵力子、陈此生、陈其瑗。参加纪念仪式的还有程思远先生。
    中共中央统一战线工作部无产阶级革命派的代表也参加了纪念仪式。
    在中山纪念堂，蔡廷锴代表民革中央向孙中山先生遗像献了花篮。
    今天，在上海的中山故居和广州中山纪念堂，有关方面人士和群众组织的代表，也前往举行了纪念活动。
