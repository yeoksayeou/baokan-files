Title: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs Has Informed the Indonesian Embassy in China
Author:
Date: 1967-11-06
Edition: 6

	The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China issued a note to the Indonesian Embassy in China on October 28.

	The note stated: Due to the Indonesian government's abrupt severance of diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China, the Chinese government has had to decide to temporarily close the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, the Consulate General in Jakarta, and the Consulates in Medan, Surabaya, and Makassar. The Chinese government has obtained the consent of the Government of the Socialist Republic of Romania, and the Romanian Embassy in Indonesia will fully manage the interests of the People's Republic of China and Chinese citizens in Indonesia.

### 我外交部曾照会印尼驻华使馆  罗驻印尼使馆全面代管中国在印尼的权益

1967-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我外交部曾照会印尼驻华使馆  
    罗驻印尼使馆全面代管中国在印尼的权益
    新华社四日讯　中华人民共和国外交部十月二十八日曾照会印尼驻华大使馆。
    照会说：由于印尼政府悍然中断同中华人民共和国的外交关系，中国政府不得不决定暂时关闭驻印尼大使馆、驻雅加达总领事馆以及驻棉兰、锡江和马辰等地领事馆。中国政府已经征得罗马尼亚社会主义共和国政府同意，由罗马尼亚社会主义共和国驻印尼大使馆全面代管中华人民共和国和中国公民在印尼的权益。
