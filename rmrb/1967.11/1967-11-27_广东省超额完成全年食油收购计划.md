Title: Guangdong Province Exceeds Annual Edible Oil Procurement Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 2

The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants in Guangdong Province, with a revolutionary spirit of "seizing the day," have enthusiastically delivered edible oil and oilseeds to the state. By the end of October, the province had already exceeded the annual edible oil procurement plan by 13.26 percent.

### 广东省超额完成全年食油收购计划

1967-11-27
第2版()
专栏：

    广东省超额完成全年食油收购计划
    广东省广大贫下中农以“只争朝夕”的革命精神，踊跃地把食油、油料交售给国家。到十月底止，全省已经超额百分之十三点二六完成了年度的食油收购计划。
