Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Italian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-11-26
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today received Maria Regis, President of the Italian Oriental Publishing House, and Giuseppe Regis, President of the Italian "Guideline" News Agency. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见意大利客人

1967-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见意大利客人
    新华社二十四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天接见意大利东方出版社社长玛丽娅·雷吉斯夫人和意大利《方针报》社长朱塞佩·雷吉斯，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
