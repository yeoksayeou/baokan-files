Title: Revolutionary Teachers and Students of Beijing Shijingshan Middle School Respond to the Call of Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee to Resume Classes and Make Revolution
Author: zhèng shū fú
Date: 1967-11-05
Edition: 3

Revolutionary teachers and students of Beijing Shijingshan Middle School are resolutely responding to the call of Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee to resume classes and make revolution. This is a "Criticize Selfishness, Oppose Revisionism" sharing meeting held by the students of Class 6, Grade 8.
