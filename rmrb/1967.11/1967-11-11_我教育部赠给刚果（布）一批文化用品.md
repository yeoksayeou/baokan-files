Title: China's Ministry of Education Donates a Batch of Cultural Supplies to Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1967-11-11
Edition: 6

	China's Ministry of Education Donates a Batch of Cultural Supplies to Congo (Brazzaville)

	According to Xinhua News Agency from Brazzaville, on the afternoon of the 4th, Li Chen Guang, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), on behalf of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, handed over a batch of cultural supplies donated to the Government of Congo (Brazzaville) to Levi Makani, the Minister of National Education of Congo (Brazzaville).

	This batch of cultural supplies includes more than 900,000 pencils and more than 2,300,000 exercise books.

### 我教育部赠给刚果（布）一批文化用品

1967-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    我教育部赠给刚果（布）一批文化用品
    据新华社布拉柴维尔电　中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光四日下午在布拉柴维尔代表中华人民共和国教育部，把赠送给刚果（布）政府的一批文化用品交给刚果（布）国民教育部长列维·马卡尼。
    这批文化用品包括九十余万枝铅笔和二百三十余万册练习本。
