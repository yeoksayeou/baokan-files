Title: The Book "Advancing Along the Path Blazed by the October Socialist Revolution" Published
Author:
Date: 1967-11-12
Edition: 3

To commemorate the 50th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the People's Publishing House has published the book "Advancing Along the Path Blazed by the October Socialist Revolution." The book has been on sale in Beijing since November 10 and will soon be available nationwide.

The book includes the important speech made by Comrade Lin Biao, the great leader Mao Zedong's close comrade-in-arms, at the capital's rally commemorating the 50th anniversary of the October Revolution. It also features the important article "Advancing Along the Path Blazed by the October Socialist Revolution—Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution" published by the editorial departments of the People's Daily, Red Flag magazine, and the Liberation Army Daily.

### 《沿着十月社会主义革命开辟的道路前进》一书出版

1967-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

    《沿着十月社会主义革命开辟的道路前进》一书出版
    新华社十一日讯　为纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命五十周年，由人民出版社出版的《沿着十月社会主义革命开辟的道路前进》一书，已从十日起在北京发行，并即将在全国各地陆续发行。
    这本书的内容有，伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪同志在首都人民纪念十月革命五十周年大会上作的极为重要的讲话，以及《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》编辑部发表的重要文章《沿着十月社会主义革命开辟的道路前进——纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命五十周年》。
