Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Warmly Praises the Shpandi Youth Volunteer Labor Brigade
Author:
Date: 1967-11-29
Edition: 5

Comrade Enver Hoxha Warmly Praises the Shpandi Youth Volunteer Labor Brigade

	Comrade Enver Hoxha, the beloved leader of the Albanian people and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, received on the morning of the 27th at the Party Central Committee building the entire brigade of the Shpandi Youth Volunteer Labor Brigade, who had worked voluntarily with the heroine Shkurtë Vata on the Logjçinë-Fieri railway construction site, as well as the father of the martyr Shkurtë Vata, Parë Vata, and had a very cordial conversation with them.

	Present at the reception were: Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Kadri Hazbiu, Member of the Central Committee, Manush Myftiu, and Candidate Member of the Central Committee and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Youth Union, Mehmet Shehu.

	The more than 140 members of the Shpandi Youth Volunteer Labor Brigade, wearing shining shock-worker badges on their chests, greeted their great leader, Comrade Enver Hoxha, with enthusiastic applause and shouts of slogans, expressing their boundless love for the glorious Party of Labor and Comrade Enver Hoxha.

	In a joyous atmosphere, the leader of the Shpandi Youth Volunteer Labor Brigade, Ymer Ishku, reported to Comrade Enver Hoxha about the great enthusiasm of the youth volunteers in their labor on the railway construction site, what they had learned in collective labor, the life, work, and sacrifice of the heroine Shkurtë Vata, and their efforts to learn from her.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha warmly praised the work of the youth volunteers of the Shpandi brigade and the father of Shkurtë Vata, Parë Vata, on the railway construction site. He said, Shkurtë Vata is the daughter of the people, with an iron will and a pure mind. She is the banner of heroism for Albanian youth, especially the youth of the Dukagjin highlands.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha highly praised the young women of the Dukagjin highlands for faithfully carrying out the Party's line and teachings, bravely joining the struggle for the complete liberation of women in the deep mountain regions.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha also highly praised the patriotic deeds of Shkurtë's father, Parë.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha then spoke with these young men and women from the Dukagjin highlands about the great prospects for the development of the mountains and the immense care that the Party and government have given for the continuous development and progress of the mountains.

### 霍查同志热烈赞扬什潘迪青年义务劳动旅  表彰女英雄什库塔是人民的女儿是青年的英雄主义旗帜

1967-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志热烈赞扬什潘迪青年义务劳动旅
    表彰女英雄什库塔是人民的女儿是青年的英雄主义旗帜
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民敬爱的领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记霍查同志，二十七日上午在党中央大厦，接见了同女英雄什库塔·瓦塔生前一起在洛格齐恩—费里铁路工地义务劳动的穆罕默德·什潘迪青年义务劳动旅的全体同志，以及什库塔·瓦塔烈士的父亲帕尔·瓦塔，并同他们进行了十分亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党政治局委员、中央书记处书记卡博，中央委员马马基，中央候补委员、劳动青年联盟中央第一书记梅罗等。
    穆罕默德·什潘迪义务劳动旅的一百四十多名队员，胸前佩着闪闪发光的突击手奖章，怀着十分兴奋和激动的心情，以热烈的掌声和口号声欢迎自己伟大的领袖霍查同志，表达了对光荣的劳动党和霍查同志无比热爱的激情。
    在一片欢腾的气氛中，穆罕默德·什潘迪义务劳动旅旅长伊夫齐库向霍查同志汇报了青年义务劳动者在铁路工地上劳动的巨大干劲，他们在集体劳动中学到的东西，女英雄什库塔·瓦塔生前的生活、劳动和牺牲经过以及他们向她学习的情况。
    霍查同志热烈赞扬这个旅的青年义务劳动者和什库塔·瓦塔的父亲帕尔·瓦塔在铁路工地上所做的工作。他说，什库塔·瓦塔是人民的女儿，有着钢铁般的意志和纯洁的思想。她是阿尔巴尼亚青年，特别是斯库台杜卡吉尼山区青年的英雄主义旗帜。
    霍查同志高度赞扬杜卡吉尼山区的女青年忠实执行党的路线及其教导，勇敢地投入彻底解放深山地区的妇女的斗争。
    霍查同志还高度赞扬了什库塔的父亲帕尔的豪迈的爱国主义的行为。
    霍查同志接着同这些来自杜卡吉尼山区的男女青年谈到了山区发展的伟大远景，以及党和政府为了山区的不断发展和进步所给予的巨大关怀。
