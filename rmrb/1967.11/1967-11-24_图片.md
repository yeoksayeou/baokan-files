Title: Workers of Huainan Coal Mine, Zhong Hanchao and Zhang Lijang, Fight Together
Author: Liu Xijian
Date: 1967-11-24
Edition: 4

Workers of Huainan Coal Mine, Zhong Hanchao and Zhang Lijang, are fighting together. Their team has created a new record by exceeding the original plan by 70% in one shift (eight hours) of tunneling.
