Title: Workers of Beijing Chemical Factory Achieve Revolutionary Great Alliance
Author: Yang Changzhong
Date: 1967-11-17
Edition: 2

    The revolutionary masses of Beijing Chemical Factory No. 4, who have already achieved revolutionary great alliance, together with revolutionary cadres and the People's Liberation Army, are seriously studying and discussing Chairman Mao's latest instructions. They are determined to further strengthen the unity between old and new cadres and jointly shoulder the dual burdens of revolution and production.
