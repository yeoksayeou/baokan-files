Title: Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-11-07
Edition: 2

For the commemoration of the 50th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the film "Lenin in October" will be shown in various parts of the country. This film recreates the historic moment when the Soviet people, under the leadership of Lenin and Stalin, with revolutionary pioneering spirit, smashed the old state machine in the dark world of capitalist rule through a violent revolution, and established the first proletarian dictatorship in human history. It marked the beginning of a new era in human history. On the screen, we can see the glorious images of Lenin, the great founder of the October Revolution, and his successor Stalin, who are deeply cherished by the Chinese people and revolutionary people of all countries.

### 纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命五十周年  影片《列宁在十月》将在全国各地陆续上映

1967-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命五十周年
    影片《列宁在十月》将在全国各地陆续上映
    新华社六日讯　为纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命五十周年，全国各地将陆续上映苏联革命影片《列宁在十月》。这部影片重现了苏联人民在列宁和斯大林的领导下，以革命的首创精神，在资本主义统治的黑暗世界里，经过暴力革命，打碎了旧的国家机器，建立了人类历史上第一个无产阶级专政的国家，开创了人类历史新纪元的伟大史实。从银幕上，可以看到我国人民和世界各国革命人民所深深怀念的十月革命的伟大缔造者列宁和他的继承者斯大林的光辉形象。
