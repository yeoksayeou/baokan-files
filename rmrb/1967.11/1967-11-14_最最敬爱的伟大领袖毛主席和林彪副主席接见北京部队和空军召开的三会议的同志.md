Title: The Most Beloved and Respected Great Leader Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Biao Receive Comrades from the Beijing Military Region and the Air Force's Three Conferences
Author:
Date: 1967-11-14
Edition: 1

The most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, and other comrades, arrived at the reception hall to a burst of enthusiasm. Revolutionaries who are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao and infinitely loyal to Mao Zedong Thought joyfully greeted Chairman Mao, waving the radiant revolutionary treasure, the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and continuously shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long, long live Chairman Mao!" Chairman Mao beamed with smiles, repeatedly waving and clapping to the crowd.

Xinhua News Agency, November 13th: Our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, and other comrades, received the representatives attending the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Beijing Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, the representatives attending the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Air Force of the Beijing Military Region, the comrades attending the plenary meeting of the Central Military Commission of the Communist Party of China, and the comrades who participated in receiving revolutionary masses during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Also present were Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Wu Faxian, Yu Lijin, Zheng Weishan, Fu Chongbi, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, Li Xuefeng, Liu Ningyi, and Yu Qili.

At 6 PM, our great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao, full of vitality, along with Vice Chairman Lin Biao and other leaders, arrived at the reception hall to meet with everyone warmly.

Instantly, the hall was filled with excitement. Revolutionaries who are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao and infinitely loyal to Mao Zedong Thought joyfully greeted Chairman Mao, waving the radiant revolutionary treasure, the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and continuously shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long, long live Chairman Mao!"

The great leader Chairman Mao beamed with smiles, repeatedly waving and clapping to the crowd. Vice Chairman Lin Biao and Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and other comrades waved the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and warmly greeted everyone.

As the reception ended, everyone, still filled with boundless excitement, continuously shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long, long live Chairman Mao!" and enthusiastically sang "The Sailboat Needs a Helmsman" and "Father and Mother Are Dearer Than Chairman Mao," wishing our dearest red sun Chairman Mao eternal life! ### 最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和林彪副主席接见北京部队和空军召开的三会议的同志

1967-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和林彪副主席接见北京部队和空军召开的三会议的同志
    同时接见在文化大革命中参加接待革命群众工作的同志
    毛主席和林彪副主席以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、张春桥、戚本禹、姚文元等同志来到接见大厅时，顿时一片欢腾。无限忠于毛主席、无限忠于毛泽东思想的革命战士，幸福地迎着毛主席，挥动红光闪耀的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，连续不断地高呼：“毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！”毛主席满面笑容，频频向大家招手、鼓掌。
    新华社十三日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、张春桥、戚本禹、姚文元等同志，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军北京部队学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表、出席中国人民解放军北京部队空军学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表、出席中国共产党空军委员会全体会议的同志，以及在无产阶级文化大革命中参加接待革命群众工作的同志。
    参加接见的，还有陈毅、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢富治、杨成武、粟裕、吴法宪、余立金、郑维山、傅崇碧、汪东兴、叶群、李雪峰、刘宁一、余秋里等同志。
    下午六时，我们伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席，神采奕奕，和林彪副主席以及其他领导同志，来到接见大厅，同大家亲切见面。
    顿时大厅里一片欢腾。无限忠于毛主席、无限忠于毛泽东思想的革命战士，幸福地迎着毛主席，挥动红光闪耀的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，连续不断地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”
    伟大领袖毛主席满面笑容，频频向大家招手、鼓掌。林彪副主席和周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青等同志，挥动《毛主席语录》，向大家亲切致意。
    接见结束了，大家仍然以无限激动的心情，一遍又一遍地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”并且激动地唱起了《大海航行靠舵手》和《爹亲娘亲不如毛主席亲》，祝我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！（附图片）
