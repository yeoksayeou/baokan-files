Title: Resolved to Defend Mao Zedong Thought with One's Life
Author:
Date: 1967-11-22
Edition: 5

A teacher from the Agricultural Academy of Albania was overjoyed when he finally received the long-awaited, red and shiny "Quotations from Chairman Mao." He kissed and embraced it repeatedly, saying excitedly, "To defend Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought, I am willing to sacrifice my life!"

### 决心用生命保卫毛泽东思想

1967-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    决心用生命保卫毛泽东思想
    阿尔巴尼亚农学院的一位教师当得到盼望已久的红光闪闪的《毛主席语录》的时候，十分高兴，亲了又亲，吻了又吻，他激动地说：“为了保卫毛主席和毛泽东思想，我可以献出自己的生命！”
