Title: The Jingxing Mining Bureau Completes the Plan Ahead of Schedule for Ten Consecutive Months
Author:
Date: 1967-11-06
Edition: 4

The revolutionary rebels of the proletariat and the broad revolutionary workers of the Jingxing Mining Bureau in Hebei Province, after completing the state's raw coal production plan ahead of schedule every month from January to September, resolutely responded to the battle call of the great leader Chairman Mao, launched a high tide of struggling against selfishness and criticizing revisionism, and increased their production enthusiasm. In October, they again completed the state's raw coal production plan 2.8% ahead of schedule. The total advance of this mining bureau also completed the state's plan for October 16% ahead of schedule.

### 井陉矿务局连续十个月超额完成计划

1967-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    井陉矿务局连续十个月超额完成计划
    河北井陉矿务局无产阶级革命派和广大革命职工，在一至九月份月月超额完成国家原煤生产计划之后，坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席的战斗号召，掀起斗私批修高潮，生产干劲倍增，十月份又超额百分之二点八完成了国家的原煤生产计划。这个矿务局的掘进总进尺，也超额百分之十六完成了十月份的国家计划。
