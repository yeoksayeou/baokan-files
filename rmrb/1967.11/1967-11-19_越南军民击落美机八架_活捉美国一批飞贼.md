Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Eight U.S. Aircraft and Capture a Group of American Pilots
Author:
Date: 1967-11-19
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down eight U.S. aircraft and capture a group of American pilots.

According to preliminary information released here, on the morning of the 18th, the military and civilians of North Vietnam jointly shot down eight U.S. aggressor aircraft and captured some American pilots.

The military and civilians around Hanoi and the surrounding provinces, with a high fighting spirit to defend the capital, fiercely struck back at the invading U.S. aircraft. The People's Army Air Force shot down two enemy aircraft over Phu Tho Province, and the military and civilians of Ninh Binh Province shot down two enemy aircraft. The military and civilians of Hai Hung Province and Hai Phong Province each shot down one enemy aircraft.

The other two invading enemy aircraft were shot down by the military and civilians of Hai Phong City.

In front of the brave Vietnamese female militia, the American pilots surrendered and became prisoners.

	New China News Agency (Telephoto)

### 越南军民击落美机八架  活捉美国一批飞贼

1967-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机八架
    活捉美国一批飞贼
    新华社河内十八日电　据这里公布的初步消息，十八日上午，越南北方军民共击落美国强盗飞机八架，并活捉了一些美国飞贼。
    河内市周围各省军民和越南人民军空军怀着保卫首都的高昂的战斗意志，痛击入侵的美机。人民军空军在富寿省上空击落敌机两架，永福省军民击落敌机两架，河北省和河西省军民各击落敌机一架。
    另外两架入侵的敌机是由海防市军民击落的。（附图片）
    在英勇的越南女民兵面前，美国飞贼乖乖地当了俘虏。
          新华社发（传真照片）
