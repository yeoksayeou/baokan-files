Title: Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade Vice Chairman Lin Receive Comrades Attending Mao Zedong Thought Study Classes and Meetings
Author:
Date: 1967-11-15
Edition: 1

Our great leader, teacher, commander, and helmsman Chairman Mao, and his close comrade Vice Chairman Lin, along with Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Chen Boda, and Yao Wenyuan, received comrades attending the Mao Zedong Thought study classes and meetings in Beijing, representatives of revolutionary mass organizations from certain provinces and cities, PLA cadres and local cadres, as well as red diplomats recently returned from Indonesia and red experts and technicians who had successfully completed their mission in Burma.

The comrades gathered in the Great Hall of the People, holding the red book of Chairman Mao's quotations, repeatedly studying Chairman Mao's teachings, eagerly awaiting the most joyous moment of their lives.

At 7:30 PM, Comrade Zhou Enlai announced, "Our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade Vice Chairman Lin are coming to see you!"

Our dearest and most beloved great leader Chairman Mao, radiating with health and vigor, ascended the platform with Vice Chairman Lin and other leading comrades, warmly greeting everyone. At that moment, the Great Hall of the People resonated with cheers, and every face beamed with excitement, like sunflowers turning towards the sun. A wave of happiness surged through the hearts of every revolutionary. People waved the Little Red Book and shouted enthusiastically, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live Chairman Mao's revolutionary line!" "Long live the great Mao Zedong Thought!" "Wish Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao strode vigorously from one end of the platform to the other, waving and clapping to the revolutionary comrades. Amidst the endless cheers, Vice Chairman Lin stepped forward, leading everyone to shout, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Chairman Mao ten thousand times!" "Wish Chairman Mao eternal life!"

After the reception, the revolutionary comrades were still immersed in boundless happiness, spontaneously singing, "The Party's kindness is greater than the heavens and the earth, parents are not as dear as Chairman Mao!" and "The ship sails on the sea relying on the helmsman, all things grow relying on the sun, dew and rain make the seedlings grow strong, revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." Many comrades opened their Little Red Books and wrote on the first page, "On the evening of November 14, 1967, at 7:30 PM, I saw our dearest and most beloved red sun, Chairman Mao."

Those who participated in tonight's reception also included Comrades Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Wu Faxian, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, Liu Ningyi, Yu Quli, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Qiu Huizuo, Li Zuopeng, Wang Hongkun, Zhang Xiuquan, Yu Lijin, Liu Jinping, Zheng Weishan, Li Xuefeng, Fu Chongbi, Huang Zuojian, Chen Xilian, Song Renqiong, Pan Fugeng, Du Ping, Han Xianchu, Huang Yongsheng, Liu Xingyuan, Wei Guoqing, and others.

Long live the great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao! Ten thousand times long live!

Our dearest and most beloved red sun Chairman Mao and his close comrade Vice Chairman Lin received comrades studying and attending meetings in Beijing, red diplomats, and Burmese aid experts and technicians.

Photographed by People's Daily reporter Lü Xiangyou.

### 伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席接见参加毛泽东思想学习班和开会的同志

1967-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席接见参加毛泽东思想学习班和开会的同志
    同时接见最近从印尼光荣归来的红色外交战士和从缅甸胜利归来的红色援缅专家
    周恩来陈伯达康生李富春江青张春桥戚本禹姚文元等同志参加接见
    毛主席同林彪副主席等领导同志走上主席台，十分高兴地同大家亲切见面。这时，人民大会堂里欢声雷动，一张张激动的笑脸，象葵花朝阳，迎着毛主席。大家挥动《毛主席语录》，纵情欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”毛主席迈着刚健的步伐，从主席台的一端走到另一端，向全场革命战士招手、鼓掌。在经久不息的欢呼声中，林彪副主席走向台前，率领大家高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！”
    新华社十四日讯　在无产阶级文化大革命空前大好的形势下，我们伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、张春桥、戚本禹、姚文元等同志，今天晚上接见了正在北京参加毛泽东思想学习班学习的中国人民解放军干部和革命群众的代表，来京开会的某些省市的革命群众组织的代表、解放军干部和地方干部，以及最近从印度尼西亚光荣归来的红色外交战士，从缅甸胜利归来的我国红色援缅专家和技术人员。
      “毛主席啊，毛主席！我们日夜想念您，我们时刻盼望见到您！”在接见之前，聚集在人民大会堂的各方面的革命战士，手捧红彤彤的革命宝书，一遍又一遍地学习毛主席的教导，等待着最最幸福的时刻的到来。
    七时三十分，周恩来同志向大家宣布：“我们的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席看你们来了！”
    我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席，容光焕发，同林彪副主席等领导同志，走上主席台，十分高兴地同大家亲切见面。这时候，人民大会堂里欢声雷动，一张张激动的笑脸，象葵花朝阳，迎着毛主席；一股股幸福的暖流涌上每个革命战士的心头。人们挥动《毛主席语录》，连续不断地纵情欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！”“毛主席的革命路线胜利万岁！”“伟大的毛泽东思想万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”
    我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，迈着刚健的步伐，从主席台的一端走到另一端，向全场革命战士招手、鼓掌。在经久不息的欢呼声中，林彪副主席走向台前，率领大家高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！”
    接见结束，革命战士们仍然沉浸在无限幸福之中，情不自禁地唱起了：“天大地大不如党的恩情大，爹亲娘亲不如毛主席亲！”唱起了：“大海航行靠舵手，万物生长靠太阳，雨露滋润禾苗壮，干革命靠的是毛泽东思想。”许多同志打开《毛主席语录》，在扉页上写下这样的语句：“一九六七年十一月十四日晚七时三十分，我见到了我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席。”
    参加今晚接见的，还有陈毅、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢富治、杨成武、粟裕、吴法宪、汪东兴、叶群、刘宁一、余秋里、李天佑、王新亭、邱会作、李作鹏、王宏坤、张秀川、余立金、刘锦平、郑维山、李雪峰、傅崇碧、黄作珍、陈锡联、宋任穷、潘复生、杜平、韩先楚、黄永胜、刘兴元、韦国清等同志。（附图片）
    伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！万万岁！
    我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见了在北京学习和开会的同志、红色外交战士和援缅专家及技术人员。
