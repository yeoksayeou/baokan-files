Title: Cambodian Government Issues Severe Warning to South Vietnamese Puppets
Author:
Date: 1967-11-27
Edition: 6

	Cambodian Government Issues Severe Warning to South Vietnamese Puppets
	Any Aggressor Against Cambodia Will Face Resolute Retaliation
	The United States Must Bear Full Responsibility for the Aggressive Actions of the South Vietnamese Puppet Army

	PHNOM PENH, Nov. 23 (Xinhua) — The Cambodian News Agency today released a statement from the Royal Government of Cambodia. The statement condemns the mad rantings of the Saigon puppet regime leader Nguyen Van Thieu to expand the war into Cambodia and points out that any attack on Cambodia by the aggressors will meet with deserved retaliation.

	The statement notes that Nguyen Van Thieu recently declared that his army (referring to the South Vietnamese puppet army) “reserves the right to pursue the enemy across the Cambodian border.”

	The statement points out that Nguyen Van Thieu’s so-called “right to pursue” is merely a pretext to expand the war into Cambodian territory.

	The statement says: “The Royal Government once again issues a severe warning to any would-be aggressors: any attack will meet with deserved retaliation and may bring serious consequences to the aggressors.” The statement emphasizes: “In any case, the U.S. government must bear full responsibility for the hostile actions taken by the South Vietnamese army, which is completely under its control, against Cambodia.”

### 柬埔寨政府严重警告南越傀儡  侵略者对柬埔寨任何侵犯都将遭到坚决回击  美国必须对南越伪军的侵略行动负全部责任

1967-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨政府严重警告南越傀儡
    侵略者对柬埔寨任何侵犯都将遭到坚决回击
    美国必须对南越伪军的侵略行动负全部责任
    新华社金边二十三日电　柬埔寨新闻社二十三日发表了柬埔寨王国政府的一项声明。声明谴责西贡傀儡集团头目阮文绍要把战争扩大到柬埔寨的疯狂叫嚣，并且指出，侵略者对柬埔寨的任何进攻都将遭到应得的回击。
    声明指出，阮文绍最近发表声明叫嚷，他的军队（指南越伪军）“保留越过柬埔寨边境追踪敌人的权利”。
    声明指出，阮文绍提出的所谓“追踪权利”，就是要借口把战争扩大到柬埔寨的领土。
    声明说：“王国政府要再一次严重警告敢于来犯的侵略者，任何进攻都将遭到应得的回击，并可能给侵略者招致严重的后果。”声明强调说：“不管怎样，美国政府必须对完全听命于它的南越军队（对柬埔寨）所采取的敌对行动负全部责任。”
