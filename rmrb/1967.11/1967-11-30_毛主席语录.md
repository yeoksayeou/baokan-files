Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-11-30
Edition: 1

	Let the Chinese and Algerian parties and peoples unite, let all Marxist-Leninists in the world unite, let all revolutionary peoples in the world unite. Down with imperialism, down with modern revisionism, down with all reactionaries! A new world without imperialism, without capitalism, without systems of exploitation, will certainly be established.

### 毛主席语录

1967-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    中阿两党、两国人民团结起来，全世界马克思列宁主义者团结起来，全世界革命人民团结起来，打倒帝国主义，打倒现代修正主义，打倒各国反动派。一个没有帝国主义、没有资本主义、没有剥削制度的新世界，一定要建立起来。
