Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-11-23
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Li Xiannian today received the vice chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox faction) and a standing director of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, Kawase Ikkan, along with members of the Japanese trade delegation stationed in Beijing. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 李先念副总理接见日本客人

1967-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见日本客人
    新华社二十二日讯　国务院副总理李先念今天接见了日中友协（正统）本部副会长、日本国际贸易促进协会常任理事川濑一贯和常驻北京日本贸易团体的人员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
