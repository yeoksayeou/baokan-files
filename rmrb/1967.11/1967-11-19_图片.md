Title: Representatives Share Experiences in Studying Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1967-11-19
Edition: 4

	Representatives attending the conference of active participants in studying Chairman Mao's works from the Beijing Military Region, including He Xiangui (third from the right), Kong Xiangxiu (first on the right), and militia members active in studying Chairman Mao's works, Wang Yiner (second from the right), Guo Fenglian (first on the left), and Rong Gaigai (second from the left), are engaged in exchanging experiences in applying Chairman Mao's teachings in their work.
