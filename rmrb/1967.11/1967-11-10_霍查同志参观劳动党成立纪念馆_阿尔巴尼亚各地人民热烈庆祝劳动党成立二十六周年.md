Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Visits the Memorial Hall of the Founding of the Party
Author:
Date: 1967-11-10
Edition: 5

Comrade Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, visited the Memorial Hall of the Founding of the Party in Tirana on the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Labor Party.

Other leaders of the Albanian Party and state who visited the memorial hall included Qemal Stafa, Mehmet Shehu, Hysni Kapo, Beqir Balluku, Kadri Hazbiu, Spiro Koleka, Ramiz Alia, and others. When Comrade Hoxha and other Party and state leaders arrived at the memorial hall, they were warmly welcomed by the members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, the First Secretary of the Tirana Municipal Committee, Muftiu, and representatives of mass organizations, workers, students, and local residents.

Comrade Hoxha and other leaders visited the room where the founding meeting of the Party was held and other exhibition halls.

Before leaving the memorial hall, Comrade Hoxha and other leaders had cordial conversations with many residents who had lived and worked here on the day the Party was founded.

The Albanian people in various parts of the country organized various activities to celebrate the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Labor Party.

In the capital, Tirana, many units held reporting meetings and symposiums.

The speakers at the meetings spoke about the glorious path and great contributions made by the Albanian Labor Party under the leadership of the Party Central Committee headed by Comrade Enver Hoxha in the national liberation war, socialist construction, and the resolute struggle to defend Marxism-Leninism and oppose modern revisionism centered on the leadership of the CPSU.

Many workers, peasants, soldiers, and cadres of government agencies also visited the Memorial Hall of the Founding of the Party and the Museum of the National Liberation Struggle. Young Pioneers asked old guerrillas to tell stories about the national liberation war.

In Comrade Enver Hoxha's hometown of Gjirokastra, people visited places where guerrillas had fought, lit bonfires in the wilderness, and asked revolutionary cadres to talk about the glorious history of the Labor Party.

Four hundred young workers, agricultural cooperative members, and students in the district of Fieri organized a march along the paths taken by the guerrillas to celebrate the Party's birthday.

The district of Korça also held a lecture titled "The Albanian Labor Party - A Revolutionary Party, An Invincible Party."

Young people laid flowers at the statue of the martyrs.

Many young people in the district of Peshkopi went to mountain agricultural cooperatives to work with the members and performed cultural programs there to celebrate the Party's birthday.

The agricultural cooperative members in the district of Saranda held an amateur drama performance to celebrate the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Labor Party. Four hundred and fifty amateur performers performed dozens of programs, vividly reflecting their work in completing the tasks set by the Fifth Congress of the Labor Party.

The people of the district of Mirdita visited the families of many martyrs, war heroes, and labor models on the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Labor Party.

### 霍查同志参观劳动党成立纪念馆  阿尔巴尼亚各地人民热烈庆祝劳动党成立二十六周年

1967-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志参观劳动党成立纪念馆
    阿尔巴尼亚各地人民热烈庆祝劳动党成立二十六周年
    新华社地拉那九日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志，在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十六周年的时候，八日在地拉那参观了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立纪念馆。
    参观这个纪念馆的还有阿尔巴尼亚党和国家的其他领导人：查尔查尼、巴卢库、努什、托斯卡、卡博、谢胡、科列加、凯莱齐、哈兹比乌、蒂奥多西、皮里斯特里和斯巴修等同志。当霍查同志以及其他党和国家领导人来到纪念馆时，受到了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、地拉那市委第一书记穆夫蒂乌以及群众组织的代表、工人、学生和附近居民的十分热烈的欢迎。
    霍查同志以及其他党和国家领导人，参观了曾经在这里举行过党的成立会议的房间和其它陈列室。
    霍查同志和其他领导同志在离开纪念馆的时候，还同在党成立的日子里曾经在这里生活和工作过的许多居民，进行了亲切的交谈。
    新华社地拉那九日电　阿尔巴尼亚全国各地的工厂企业、农业合作社、人民军部队、机关和学校八日组织各种活动，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十六周年。
    在首都地拉那，许多单位举办了报告会和座谈会。
    报告人在会上谈到了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党在以阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志为首的党中央的领导下，二十六年来，在民族解放战争、社会主义建设以及为捍卫马克思列宁主义、反对以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的坚决斗争中，所经历的光荣道路以及作出的巨大贡献。
    许多工人、农民、战士和机关干部还参观了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立纪念馆和民族解放斗争博物馆。少先队队员们请老游击队员讲民族解放战争的故事。
    在霍查同志的故乡吉诺卡斯特，人们参观了游击队员战斗过的地方，在野外升起了篝火，请革命干部讲劳动党的光荣历史。
    费里区四百名青年工人、农业合作社社员和学生为了庆祝党的生日，沿着游击队员经过的地方组织了一次行军。
    科尔察区还举办了题为“阿尔巴尼亚劳动党——革命的党、战无不胜的党”的讲座。
    青年们在烈士塑像前献了鲜花。
    佩什科比区的许多青年为了庆祝党的生日，到山区农业合作社去同社员们一起劳动，并在那里演出了文娱节目。
    萨兰达区的农业合作社社员为了庆祝劳动党成立二十六周年，举行了杂剧业余会演。四百五十名业余演员演出了几十个节目，生动地反映了他们为完成劳动党第五次代表大会所制订的任务所进行的工作。
    米尔迪塔区人民在劳动党成立二十六周年的日子里，访问了许多烈士、战斗英雄和劳动模范的家庭。
