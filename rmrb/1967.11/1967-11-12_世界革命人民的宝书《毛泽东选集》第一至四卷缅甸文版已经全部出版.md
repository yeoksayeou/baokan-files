Title: The Revolutionary People of the World's Treasure: The First to Fourth Volumes of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in Burmese Have All Been Published
Author:
Date: 1967-11-12
Edition: 1

The first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," a treasure for the revolutionary people of the world, was published in Burmese in mid-October. Recently, the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House has successively translated and published the Burmese versions of the second to fourth volumes. Thus, the Burmese versions of the first to fourth volumes of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" have all been completed. This set of treasures is in a 32-opening format, with red plastic covers. Each volume is divided into upper and lower parts, making a total of eight volumes.

### 世界革命人民的宝书《毛泽东选集》第一至四卷缅甸文版已经全部出版

1967-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    世界革命人民的宝书《毛泽东选集》第一至四卷缅甸文版已经全部出版
    新华社十一日讯  世界革命人民的宝书《毛泽东选集》第一卷的缅甸文版，于十月中旬出版后，北京外文出版社最近又陆续翻译出版了第二卷至第四卷的缅甸文版。至此，《毛泽东选集》第一卷至第四卷的缅甸文版全部出齐。这套宝书为三十二开本，红塑料封面，每卷分上下册，全套共八册。
