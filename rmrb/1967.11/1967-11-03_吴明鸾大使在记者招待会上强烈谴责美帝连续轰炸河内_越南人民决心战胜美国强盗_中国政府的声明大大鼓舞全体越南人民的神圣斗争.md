Title: Ambassador Vu Minh Loan Strongly Condemns U.S. Bombing of Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1967-11-03
Edition: 7

	Ambassador Vu Minh Loan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a press conference this afternoon to strongly condemn the recent barbaric acts of U.S. imperialism in continuously bombing Hanoi.

	He said, "The Vietnamese people have only one response to every escalation of war by U.S. imperialism: resolutely fight back, fight fiercely, and fight accurately!"

	He vehemently denounced U.S. imperialism's attempt to use bombs and escalate the war to threaten the Vietnamese people, hoping to escape its desperate situation. At the same time, it shamelessly plays the "peace talks" trick.

	Ambassador Vu Minh Loan stated that on the fiery land of Vietnam, U.S. imperialism will find no place to hide. The Vietnamese people are determined to advance victoriously, smashing all new war plots of U.S. imperialism, and decisively defeating the U.S. aggressors until there is not a single trace of U.S. invaders on their soil, until they achieve final victory.

	On behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people, Ambassador Vu Minh Loan expressed gratitude to the Chinese government for its statement on November 1, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's bombing of Hanoi. He pointed out that the Chinese government's statement has greatly encouraged the sacred struggle of the 31 million Vietnamese people.

### 吴明鸾大使在记者招待会上强烈谴责美帝连续轰炸河内  越南人民决心战胜美国强盗  中国政府的声明大大鼓舞全体越南人民的神圣斗争

1967-11-03
第7版()
专栏：

    吴明鸾大使在记者招待会上强烈谴责美帝连续轰炸河内
    越南人民决心战胜美国强盗
    中国政府的声明大大鼓舞全体越南人民的神圣斗争
    新华社二日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾今天下午举行记者招待会，强烈谴责美帝国主义最近连续轰炸河内的野蛮罪行。
    他说：“越南人民对于美帝国主义的每一次战争升级，只有一个回答：坚决打，打得狠，打得准！”
    他痛斥美帝国主义妄想用炸弹，用战争升级，威胁越南人民，以摆脱它走投无路的困境，并且在进行战争升级的同时，无耻地玩弄“和谈”阴谋。
    吴明鸾大使说，在怒火熊熊的越南国土上，美帝国主义休想找到容身之地。越南人民决心乘胜前进，粉碎美帝国主义一切新的战争阴谋，决战决胜美国侵略者，直到在自己的国土上没有一个美国侵略者的影子，直到取得最后的胜利。
    吴明鸾大使代表越南民主共和国政府和越南人民，对中国政府在十一月一日发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义轰炸河内，表示感谢。他指出，中国政府的声明大大地鼓舞了三千一百万越南人民的神圣斗争。
