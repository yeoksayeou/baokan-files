Title: Singing Praises of the Motherland
Author: Lazi
Date: 1967-11-30
Edition: 5

With our most beautiful songs, we wholeheartedly sing praises of our motherland. We sing of the mountains and the vast plains, we sing of the places where we live in happiness. Beautiful motherland, you stand tall and strong like a fortress. You are a giant standing tall against the sky, and no storm or rain can stop you. Ah! Great motherland, you are a giant standing tall against the sky, and no storm or rain can stop you.

### 歌唱祖国
拉齐
1967-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    歌唱祖国
    阿尔巴尼亚　拉齐用我们最美妙的歌声，衷心地把祖国歌唱。歌唱群山和那广阔的原野，歌唱我们幸福生活的地方。美丽的祖国耸入云霄，象堡垒一样无比坚强。你是顶天立地的巨人，狂风暴雨也不能把你阻挡。啊！伟大的祖国，你是顶天立地的巨人，狂风暴雨也不能把你阻挡。
