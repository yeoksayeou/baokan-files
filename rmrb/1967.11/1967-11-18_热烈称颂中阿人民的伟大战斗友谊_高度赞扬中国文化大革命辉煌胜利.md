Title: Warmly Praise the Great Fighting Friendship Between Chinese and Albanian Peoples and Highly Commend the Brilliant Victory of China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-11-18
Edition: 6

    Comrade Shehu Reports on Albanian Delegation's Visit to China
    Warmly Praise the Great Fighting Friendship Between Chinese and Albanian Peoples and Highly Commend the Brilliant Victory of China's Cultural Revolution

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 17th – According to Albanian newspapers, Comrade Shehu, the head of the Albanian Party and Government delegation that recently visited China, and a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, recently reported to party, government, and agricultural cooperative leaders and workers in the districts of Mat and Mirdita. He spoke enthusiastically about the Albanian Party and Government delegation's visit to China, highly praised the brilliant victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and warmly praised the great fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Albania, which was forged and nurtured by the great leaders of the two countries, Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha.

    Comrade Shehu highly evaluated the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Comrade Mao Zedong. He emphasized the brilliant achievements and great prospects of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Comrade Shehu also spoke about the great world-historical significance of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the Albanian Party of Labour's great support for it. He said that this revolution ensures that China, as an unshakable bastion and a powerful base of the world revolution, will forever remain red.

    When speaking about the extremely warm and fraternal reception the Albanian Party and Government delegation received in China, Comrade Shehu said that this reception vividly reflected the deep affection that the 700 million Chinese people, the glorious Communist Party of China, and Comrade Mao Zedong have for the Albanian people, the Albanian Party of Labour, and the leader of the party and people, Comrade Enver Hoxha. He pointed out that this great revolutionary friendship is unbreakable and will grow stronger and develop further because it was established by the great leaders of the two glorious parties and peoples, Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Hoxha, and is built on the immortal principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism.

    Comrade Shehu's report was frequently interrupted by enthusiastic applause and cheers. Those who listened to the report warmly wished the Albanian Party of Labour, the Communist Party of China, and the great 700 million Chinese people, as well as the great leaders of the two peoples, Comrade Enver Hoxha and Comrade Mao Zedong, every success. Comrade Shehu gave the above report to the leaders and workers of the two districts during his inspection of Mat and Mirdita.

### 热烈称颂中阿人民的伟大战斗友谊  高度赞扬中国文化大革命辉煌胜利

1967-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志向阿尔巴尼亚干部和职工作访华报告
    热烈称颂中阿人民的伟大战斗友谊  高度赞扬中国文化大革命辉煌胜利
    新华社地拉那十七日电　据阿尔巴尼亚报纸报道，不久前访问过中国的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席谢胡，最近向马蒂区和米尔迪塔区的党、政和农业合作社负责干部、职工作报告，畅谈阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访华观感，高度赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命的辉煌胜利，热烈称颂由中阿两国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志和霍查同志缔造和培育起来的中阿两国人民之间伟大的战斗友谊。
    谢胡同志高度评价由毛泽东同志亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命。他着重谈到了中国无产阶级文化大革命的辉煌成就及其伟大的远景。谢胡同志还谈到了中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大的世界历史意义和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党对中国无产阶级文化大革命的巨大支持。他说，这个革命使作为坚不可摧的堡垒和世界革命强大基地的中国永远保持红色。
    谢胡同志在谈到阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团在中国所受到的极其热烈和兄弟般的接待时说，这一接待生动地反映了七亿中国人民、光荣的中国共产党和毛泽东同志对阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党以及党和人民的领袖恩维尔·霍查同志怀有的伟大的深情厚谊。他指出，这一伟大的革命友谊是牢不可破的，它将与日俱增，日益发展，因为这一友谊是由中阿两个光荣的党和两国人民的领袖毛泽东同志和霍查同志建立起来的，是建筑在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义不朽原则的基础上的。
    谢胡同志的报告不断被热烈的掌声和欢呼声所打断。听报告的人热烈祝福阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、中国共产党和伟大的七亿中国人民，热烈祝福两国人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志和毛泽东同志。谢胡同志是在马蒂和米尔迪塔区视察时，向这两个区的干部和职工作上述报告的。
