Title: China and Mauritania Sign Agreement on Dispatching Chinese Medical Team
Author:
Date: 1967-11-13
Edition: 6

	China and Mauritania signed an agreement on the dispatch of a Chinese medical team.

	Nouakchott, Nov. 10 (Xinhua) — Ahmad Kilali, Minister of Public Affairs, Labor, and Health of Mauritania, and Wei Yongqing, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Mauritania, signed an agreement on behalf of the two governments on Nov. 8 regarding the dispatch of a Chinese medical team to Mauritania. This agreement is based on the cultural cooperation agreement signed between China and Mauritania in Beijing in February this year.

### 中国和毛里塔尼亚签订关于中国派出医疗队的议定书

1967-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    中国和毛里塔尼亚签订关于中国派出医疗队的议定书
    新华社努瓦克肖特十日电　毛里塔尼亚公共事务、劳工和卫生部长艾哈迈德·基利和中国驻毛里塔尼亚大使馆临时代办卫永清八日在这里代表两国政府签订了一项关于中国向毛里塔尼亚派出医疗队的议定书。这项议定书是根据今年二月在北京签订的中国和毛里塔尼亚文化合作协定签订的。
