Title: Jinan Successfully Produces Sea Water Transparency Meter
Author:
Date: 1967-11-09
Edition: 2

Jinan's Eighth of August Optical Instrument Factory's revolutionary faction of the proletariat, under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, overcame the blockade imposed by imperialism and revisionism, as well as the sabotage by the capitalism-roaders within the factory's party, to conquer numerous difficulties. After conducting over five hundred continuous experiments, they recently successfully developed China's first sea water transparency meter. Through various forms of testing and appraisal, it has been proven that this new product fully meets the specified quality requirements.

### 济南制成海水透明度仪

1967-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    济南制成海水透明度仪
    济南八一光学仪器厂的无产阶级革命派在毛泽东思想指引下，战胜了帝国主义、修正主义的封锁和本厂党内走资本主义道路当权派的破坏，克服重重困难，进行了连续五百多次试验，最近试制成功了我国第一台海水透明度仪。经过多种方式的试验和鉴定证明，这种新产品完全达到了规定的质量要求。　　（据新华社讯）
