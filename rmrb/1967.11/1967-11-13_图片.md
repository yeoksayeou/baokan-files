Title: Workers of the Dongfeng Mine's May 1st Tunneling Team Respond to Chairman Mao's Call
Author: Sū Jìn
Date: 1967-11-13
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Dongfeng Mine's May 1st Tunneling Team of the Héběi Coal Mining Bureau have responded to Chairman Mao's great call, focusing on the revolution and vigorously promoting production. This is them working diligently.
