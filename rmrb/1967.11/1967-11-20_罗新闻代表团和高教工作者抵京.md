Title: Romanian Journalists and Educators Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-11-20
Edition: 5

A delegation of five Romanian journalists led by Deputy Director of the Romanian News Agency, Ion Galeata, arrived in Beijing on the 18th. They are visiting China as part of the 1966-1967 execution plan under the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Relevant officials from the Chinese side and the Charge d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Istrati, welcomed them at the airport.

A delegation of four educators from the Socialist Republic of Romania arrived in Beijing today. They are visiting China as part of the 1966-1967 execution plan under the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Relevant officials from the Chinese side and the Charge d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Istrati, welcomed them at the airport.

### 罗新闻代表团和高教工作者抵京

1967-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    罗新闻代表团和高教工作者抵京
    新华社十八日讯　以罗马尼亚通讯社副社长扬·加列特亚努为首的罗马尼亚新闻代表团一行五人，于十八日抵达北京。他们是根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六——一九六七年执行计划前来我国进行友好访问的。我有关方面负责人和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代到机场迎接。
    新华社十八日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六——一九六七年执行计划前来我国访问的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国高教工作者一行四人，今天到达北京。我有关方面负责人和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代到机场欢迎。
