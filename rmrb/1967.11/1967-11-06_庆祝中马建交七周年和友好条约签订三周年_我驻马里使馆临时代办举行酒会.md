Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of China-Mali Diplomatic Relations and the Third Anniversary of the Signing of the Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1967-11-06
Edition: 6

	China's Chargé d'Affaires in Mali, Liu He Lin, hosted a reception on the evening of November 3rd in the Malian capital of Bamako to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali and the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Malian Friendship Treaty.

	Four hundred Malian guests attended the reception, including members of the National Committee for the Defense of the Revolution, such as Sedou Badian Kouyate, Chairman of the Economic and Financial Commission of the Sudanese Union Party, Jean-Marie Koné, Minister of Planning and Economic Coordination, Ataer Megu, Minister of Trade, Mamadou Gologou, Minister of Information and Tourism, Segou Traore, Chief of Staff of the Malian Army, Bilama Traore, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, leaders of various mass organizations, and representatives of various sectors of Bamako and the Malian working people.

	At the reception, Malian friends warmly congratulated the development of Sino-Malian friendship and wished Chairman Mao a long life, and wished the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China great success.

### 庆祝中马建交七周年和友好条约签订三周年  我驻马里使馆临时代办举行酒会

1967-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中马建交七周年和友好条约签订三周年
    我驻马里使馆临时代办举行酒会
    据新华社巴马科三日电　中国驻马里大使馆临时代办刘和林三日晚上在马里首都巴马科举行酒会，庆祝中马建立外交关系七周年和中马友好条约签订三周年。
    四百名马里客人出席了酒会，其中有保卫革命全国委员会成员、苏丹联盟党财经委员会主席塞杜·巴迪昂·库亚特，负责计划和经济协调国务部长让—马里·科奈，贸易部长阿塔埃尔·梅加，新闻、游览部长马马杜·戈洛戈，马里陆军参谋长塞古·特拉奥雷，外交部秘书长比拉马·特拉奥雷，各群众组织的负责人，以及巴马科各界人士和马里劳动人民的代表。
    在酒会上，马里朋友热烈祝贺中马友谊的发展，并祝毛主席万寿无疆，祝中国的无产阶级文化大革命取得巨大成就。
