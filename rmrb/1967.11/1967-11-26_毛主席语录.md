Title: Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1967-11-26
Edition: 1

To carry out the proletarian educational revolution, we must rely on the broad masses of revolutionary students, revolutionary teachers, and revolutionary workers in the schools. We must rely on the activists among them, that is, the proletarian revolutionaries who are determined to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end.
