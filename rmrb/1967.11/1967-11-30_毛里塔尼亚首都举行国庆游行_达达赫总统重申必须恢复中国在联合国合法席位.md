Title: Mauritania's Capital Holds National Day Parade; President Daddah Reaffirms Need to Restore China's Legal Seat in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1967-11-30
Edition: 6

Residents of Nouakchott, the capital of Mauritania, held a parade on November 28 to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania.

The parade featured participants holding banners with slogans such as "Oppose Imperialism!" "Firmly support the peoples of Arab countries!" and calls to oppose U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam.

After the parade, President Daddah of Mauritania delivered a state-of-the-nation address to the National Assembly. In his speech, he reaffirmed Mauritania's full support for the African liberation movement in its struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism.

Speaking about the friendly relations between Mauritania and China, President Daddah said, "Since we established an embassy in Beijing this year, we have been engaged in fruitful and friendly cooperation."

He reaffirmed the need to restore the People's Republic of China's legal seat in the United Nations and expel the "representatives" of the Chiang Kai-shek clique. President Daddah also condemned the United States' criminal war against the heroic people of Vietnam.

### 毛里塔尼亚首都举行国庆游行  达达赫总统重申必须恢复中国在联合国合法席位

1967-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚首都举行国庆游行
    达达赫总统重申必须恢复中国在联合国合法席位
    新华社努瓦克肖特二十九日电　毛里塔尼亚首都努瓦克肖特居民二十八日举行游行，热烈庆祝毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国成立七周年。
    参加游行的队伍，高举着标语牌，上面写着：“反对帝国主义！”“坚决支持阿拉伯各国人民！”和反对美帝国主义侵略越南等口号。
    游行结束后，毛里塔尼亚总统达达赫在国民议会上作了国情报告。他在报告中重申毛里塔尼亚全力支持为摆脱帝国主义、新老殖民主义压迫而英勇斗争的非洲解放运动。
    在谈到毛里塔尼亚和中国之间的友好关系时，达达赫总统说：“自从今年我们在北京建立大使馆以来，我们一直进行着有成果的和友好的合作。”
    他重申必须恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法席位，并把蒋匪帮“代表”驱逐出去。达达赫总统还谴责了美国对英雄的越南人民进行的罪恶战争。
