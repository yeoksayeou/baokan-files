Title: The Party—Our Mother
Author:
Date: 1967-11-08
Edition: 4

The Party—Our Mother

Albania, you are so fortunate!
Who leads you on the path to victory?
It is Enver and our mother—the invincible Party,
In the dense fog and stormy weather, she leads us on the march.
Like a red lantern, she illuminates the way in the vast sea.

The Albanian people hold deep affection for Mao Zedong,
We have the great friend, China.
We cherish the words of our friend:
"Whoever invades Albania is invading China,
They will surely be crushed, they will surely be defeated!"

Traitors and enemies have failed,
Blockades and threats cannot make Albania yield.
No, we are not afraid,
Because the Party guides us.
She leads the people, working day and night for victory…

Party, you are our mother,
May you live forever!
You lead and educate us,
Saving us from countless dangers.
You lead the people to carry the revolution through to the end,
We have not fallen into the hands of opportunists.

Party, for your glorious cause,
We are willing to shed our blood, sacrifice our lives,
We are willing to offer our hearts and lives to you,
Even if it means sacrificing our sons, we will not hesitate.

The great China with its seven hundred million people,
Is our best friend.
May the friendship between China and Albania,
Last forever, for all eternity!
Ah, Enver, you are a brave eagle,
A true Marxist-Leninist,
You share the fate of strong Albania,
You breathe with the great Chinese friend,
May you and the great helmsman Mao Zedong live forever!

### 党——我们的母亲

1967-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    党——我们的母亲
    阿尔巴尼亚民歌阿尔巴尼亚，你是多么幸福！是谁领导你走向胜利的道路？是恩维尔和我们的母亲——无比坚强的党，在密云浓雾、狂风暴雨中率领我们，踏上征途。就如同一盏红灯，在大海中指明航路。阿尔巴尼亚人对毛泽东无比敬爱，我们有伟大的中国这个朋友。我们把朋友的话牢记心头：“谁侵犯阿尔巴尼亚，就等于侵犯中国，他一定要粉身碎骨，他一定要头破血流！”叛徒、敌人破了产，封锁、恫吓都不能使阿尔巴尼亚屈服。不，我们绝不害怕，因为有党给我们引路。她领导着人民，为夺取胜利日夜忙碌……党啊，你是我们的母亲，愿你永生长寿！你领导着、教育着我们，把我们从千难万险中拯救。你率领人民把革命进行到底，我们没有落入机会主义者之手。党啊，为了你那光荣的事业，我们愿意洒热血，抛头颅，愿意把我们的心和生命献给你，就是再献出儿子，也毫不踟蹰。伟大的七亿人民的中国，是我们最好的朋友。愿中国和阿尔巴尼亚的友谊，流芳百代，万古千秋！啊，恩维尔，你是勇敢的山鹰，是一个真正的马克思主义者，你与坚强的阿尔巴尼亚同命运，你与伟大的中国朋友共呼吸，祝你和伟大舵手毛主席万寿无疆！
