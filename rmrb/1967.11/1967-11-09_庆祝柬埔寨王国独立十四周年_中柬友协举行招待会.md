Title: Celebrating the 14th Anniversary of the Independence of the Kingdom of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1967-11-09
Edition: 6

China-Cambodia Friendship Association Holds Reception

According to Xinhua News Agency, the China-Cambodia Friendship Association held a reception tonight to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the independence of the Kingdom of Cambodia.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant officials Han Nianlong, Zhou Yiping, Ding Xilin, and others attended the reception. The chargé d'affaires of the Cambodian Embassy in China, Du Kewu, and Cambodian friends in Beijing were invited to attend the reception.

Vice President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association Zhou Yiping and Du Kewu spoke at the reception. Zhou Yiping said that the peoples of China and Cambodia have a traditional deep friendship. In the common struggle against American imperialist aggression, the friendship between the two peoples has continued to develop. The Chinese people firmly support the Cambodian people in their just struggle against American imperialism to maintain national sovereignty, independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity. We will continue to make efforts to strengthen friendly cooperation between the two countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the principles of mutual respect and non-interference in each other's internal affairs.

Du Kewu said that the friendship between the peoples of Cambodia and China has been further strengthened in the struggle against American imperialism. This friendship has contributed to the consolidation of peace in Asia.

### 庆祝柬埔寨王国独立十四周年  中柬友协举行招待会

1967-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝柬埔寨王国独立十四周年
    中柬友协举行招待会
    据新华社八日讯　中国柬埔寨友好协会今晚举行招待会，庆祝柬埔寨王国独立十四周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅和有关方面负责人韩念龙、周一萍、丁西林等出席了招待会。柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办杜克武提和在京柬埔寨朋友应邀出席招待会。
    中柬友协副会长周一萍和杜克武提先后在招待会上讲话。周一萍说，中柬两国人民有传统的深厚友谊。在反对美帝侵略的共同斗争中，两国人民的友谊，不断得到发展。中国人民坚决支持柬埔寨人民反对美帝国主义，维护国家主权、独立、中立和领土完整的正义斗争，并且在和平共处五项原则的基础上，在互相尊重、互不干涉内政的原则下，继续为加强两国友好合作关系作出自己的努力。
    杜克武提说，柬中两国人民的友谊，在反对美帝国主义的斗争中得到了进一步的加强，这种友谊对巩固亚洲和平作出了贡献。
