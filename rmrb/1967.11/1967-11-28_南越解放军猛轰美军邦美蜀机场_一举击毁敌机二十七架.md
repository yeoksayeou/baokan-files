Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bombards U.S. Military Base at Ban Me Thuot
Author:
Date: 1967-11-28
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army heavily bombarded the Ban Me Thuot enemy military airfield on November 17, inflicting massive losses on the U.S. puppet forces. According to reports, a total of 27 enemy aircraft, 3 cannons, and 2 armored vehicles were destroyed. An enemy fuel depot and ammunition depot were also burned down, and more than 60 enemy personnel were killed or wounded, including nearly 50 U.S. invaders.

Additionally, it was reported that on the night of November 3, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army in Dac Lac Province bravely attacked an enemy artillery position in the De Lieng area, annihilating and severely damaging a U.S. infantry company and an artillery company. More than 150 enemy personnel were killed, many were wounded, and 2 enemy helicopters, 2 cannons, 2 armored vehicles, an ammunition depot, and an underground fuel storage tank were destroyed.

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Hanoi on November 26, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army heavily bombarded a special forces camp of the U.S. puppet forces at the Nha Trang U.S. Air Force Base in the early hours of November 26. Preliminary information indicates that three U.S. transport planes were damaged.

### 南越解放军猛轰美军邦美蜀机场  一举击毁敌机二十七架

1967-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军猛轰美军邦美蜀机场
    一举击毁敌机二十七架
    新华社河内二十五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方多乐省人民解放武装力量，十七日猛烈炮击邦美蜀敌军军用机场，使美伪军遭到巨大损失。据报道，共击毁敌人的飞机二十七架、大炮三门、装甲车两辆，烧毁敌人的汽油库和弹药库各一座，打死打伤敌人六十多名，其中包括美国侵略军近五十名。另据报道，多乐省人民解放武装力量，三日夜间英勇袭击德浪地区的敌人炮兵阵地，歼灭和重创美军一个步兵连和一个炮兵连，打死敌人一百五十多名，打伤多名，击毁敌人的直升飞机两架、大炮两门、装甲车两辆、弹药库一座和地下蓄油池一个。
    据新华社河内二十六日电　越南南方人民解放武装力量二十六日凌晨猛烈炮击了芽庄美国空军基地的一个美伪军特种部队的营地。据初步消息，击伤美军运输机三架。
