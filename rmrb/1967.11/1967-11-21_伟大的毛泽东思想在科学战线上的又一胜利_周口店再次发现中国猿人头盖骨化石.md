Title: A Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought on the Scientific Front: The Discovery of a Peking Man Skull Fossil at Zhoukoudian
Author:
Date: 1967-11-21
Edition: 3

A great victory of Mao Zedong Thought on the scientific front: the discovery of a Peking Man skull fossil at Zhoukoudian

	Xinhua News Agency, November 15th - In the famous Zhoukoudian site in Beijing, our workers and young scientific workers have once again discovered extremely precious materials for the study of human origins - a Peking Man skull fossil. This major discovery is the result of the participants in this work holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasizing politics, and actively applying Chairman Mao's works. It is another victory of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought on the scientific front.

	Peking Man is an important link in the early development of humans. The fossils of Peking Man are indispensable precious materials for the study and exploration of human origins and development. In 1929, China discovered the first skull of Peking Man in Zhoukoudian, and later discovered many more materials of Peking Man. However, in old China, the excavation, preservation, and research rights of these precious materials were all in the hands of American imperialists. During the Anti-Japanese War, all the fossils of Peking Man were plundered by American imperialists.

	After liberation, under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, Chinese workers and young scientific workers broke away from superstition, relied on their own efforts, and continued to discover teeth, mandibles, limb bones, stone tools, and coexisting animal fossils of Peking Man in Zhoukoudian, achieving remarkable results.

	This excavation work in Zhoukoudian was carried out during the great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The comrades from the Institute of Vertebrate Paleontology and Paleoanthropology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the guidance of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, pooled their strengths, with high political enthusiasm and revolutionary vigor, and achieved this major result.

	This time, the discovered Peking Man skull includes the frontal bone, occipital bone, and other parts. Also discovered were stone tools made of quartz and flint, as well as fossils of deer, rhinoceros, and hyenas. These fossils are of great value in further understanding the physical form and living conditions of Peking Man.

### 伟大的毛泽东思想在科学战线上的又一胜利  周口店再次发现中国猿人头盖骨化石

1967-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    伟大的毛泽东思想在科学战线上的又一胜利
    周口店再次发现中国猿人头盖骨化石
    新华社十五日讯　我国工人和青年科学工作者在著名的北京周口店，又一次发现了研究人类起源的极为珍贵的材料——中国猿人头盖骨化石。这一重大发现是参加这项工作的有关人员高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出政治，活学活用毛主席著作的结果，是战无不胜的毛泽东思想在科学战线上的又一胜利。
    中国猿人是早期人类发展的一个重要环节。中国猿人的化石，是研究、探讨人类起源和发展的不可缺少的珍贵材料。一九二九年，我国在周口店发现了中国猿人的第一个头盖骨，以后又陆续发现了中国猿人的很多材料。但是在旧中国，这些珍贵材料的发掘、保管和研究权都掌握在美帝国主义者手中。抗日战争期间，所有中国猿人的化石，都已被美帝国主义者掠夺去。
    解放以后，在党和毛主席的领导下，我国工人和青年科学工作者破除迷信，自力更生，在周口店又陆续发现了中国猿人的牙齿、下颌骨、肢骨、石器以及共生的动物化石，取得了显著成果。
    这次周口店的发掘工作是在伟大的无产阶级文化大革命中进行的。从事这项工作的中国科学院古脊椎动物与古人类研究所的同志，在毛主席无产阶级革命路线的指引下，群策群力，以饱满的政治热情和冲天的革命干劲，取得了这个重大成果。
    这次发现的中国猿人头盖骨，包括额骨、枕骨等，一起发现的还有石英和燧石制作的石器以及肿骨鹿、犀牛、鬣狗等哺乳动物化石。这些化石对于进一步了解中国猿人的体质形态及其生活状况，有很大的价值。（附图片）
