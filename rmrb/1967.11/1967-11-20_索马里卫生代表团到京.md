Title: Somali Health Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-11-20
Edition: 5

According to the 1967 implementation plan of the China-Somalia Cultural Cooperation Agreement, a five-member Somali health delegation, led by Dr. Elmi Ahmed Du'ale, Director of the Office of the Somali Ministry of Health, arrived in Beijing on November 18 to visit China.

### 索马里卫生代表团到京

1967-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    索马里卫生代表团到京
    新华社十八日讯　根据中国索马里文化合作协定一九六七年执行计划来我国访问的索马里卫生代表团一行五人，在团长、索马里卫生部办公厅主任埃勒米·艾哈迈德·杜阿莱大夫的率领下，于十一月十八日到达北京。
