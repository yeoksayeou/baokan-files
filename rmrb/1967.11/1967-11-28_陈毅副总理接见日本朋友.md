Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-11-28
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with the full delegation of the Japan Committee for Asia-Africa Solidarity, led by Hayashi Yasuaki. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本朋友

1967-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本朋友
    新华社二十七日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了由林毅率领的亚非人民团结日本委员会访华代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
