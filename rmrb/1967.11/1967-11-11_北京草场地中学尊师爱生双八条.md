Title: Beijing Caochangdi Middle School's "Eight Points for Respecting Teachers and Loving Students"
Author:
Date: 1967-11-11
Edition: 3

	Beijing Caochangdi Middle School's "Eight Points for Respecting Teachers and Loving Students" is an initial draft by the school's revolutionary teachers and students. It will be further improved through practice and is provided here for reference by other regions.—Editors

	Eight Points for Respecting Teachers:
	(1) Study Chairman Mao's books, listen to Chairman Mao's words, and act according to Chairman Mao's instructions to be a good student of Chairman Mao.
	(2) Trust and respect teachers, learn humbly from teachers, and actively support teachers in their teaching work.
	(3) Voluntarily observe revolutionary discipline and follow commands in all actions.
	(4) Strictly demand of oneself, voluntarily eliminate personal interests for public good, and humbly accept criticism and correct mistakes.
	(5) Politically care for teachers' progress, actively help teachers improve their work by building on their strengths and correcting their mistakes.
	(6) Foster a spirit of unity and mutual love, proactively care for teachers, and ensure that teachers and students "care for, love, and help each other."
	(7) Respond to Chairman Mao's call to "criticize and repudiate revisionism" and promote the revolutionization of thought.
	(8) Respond to Chairman Mao's call to "seize the revolution and promote production," and work with teachers to carry out the educational revolution, turning the school into a red school of Mao Zedong Thought.

	Eight Points for Loving Students:
	(1) Lead by example in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, "criticizing and repudiating revisionism," eliminating personal interests for public good, and carrying out self-revolution.
	(2) Emphasize proletarian politics and Mao Zedong Thought, using Mao Zedong Thought to guide teaching work.
	(3) Practice the "five together" principle: study, fight, exercise, work, and entertain together with students. Unite closely and fight shoulder to shoulder.
	(4) Implement the "four firsts," insist on positive education, and be both strict and patient in persuasion.
	(5) Respect the masses, trust the masses, and humbly learn from students, willingly being students' little students.
	(6) Value investigation and research, continuously summarize experiences, and reform teaching methods through practice.
	(7) Strictly demand of oneself, actively listen to criticism, and promptly check and correct mistakes.
	(8) Politically care for students' progress and actively help students overcome difficulties.

### 北京草场地中学尊师爱生双八条

1967-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

    北京草场地中学尊师爱生双八条
    草场地中学的尊师爱生双八条，是该校革命师生初步拟订的，还要在实践中进一步完善，仅供各地参考。——编者
    尊师八条：（1）读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好学生。（2）相信老师，尊重老师，虚心向老师学习，积极支持老师搞好教学工作。（3）自觉遵守革命纪律，一切行动听指挥。（4）严格要求自己，自觉破私立公，有了缺点错误，虚心接受批评并坚决改正。（5）从政治上关心老师的进步，积极帮助老师发扬成绩，纠正缺点错误，改进工作。（6）发扬团结友爱精神，主动照顾老师，师生“互相关心，互相爱护，互相帮助”。（7）响应毛主席的号召，和老师一起“斗私，批修”，促进思想革命化。（8）响应毛主席的号召，抓革命，促教学，和老师一起，搞好教育革命，把学校办成红彤彤的毛泽东思想大学校。
    爱生八条：（1）带头活学活用毛主席著作，“斗私，批修”，破私立公，自我革命。（2）突出无产阶级政治，突出毛泽东思想。用毛泽东思想统帅教学工作。（3）坚持五同：和同学同学习、同战斗、同出操、同劳动、同娱乐。紧密团结，并肩战斗。（4）落实四个第一，坚持正面教育，既严格要求，又耐心说服。（5）尊重群众，相信群众，虚心向同学们学习，甘当同学们的小学生。（6）重视调查研究，不断总结经验，在实践中改革教学方法。（7）严格要求自己，主动听取批评，及时检查和纠正缺点错误。（8）从政治上关心同学的进步，积极帮助同学们克服困难。
