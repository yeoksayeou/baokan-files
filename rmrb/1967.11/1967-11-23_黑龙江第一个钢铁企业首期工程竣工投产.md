Title: The First Steel Enterprise in Heilongjiang Province Completes the First Phase of Construction and Begins Production

Author:

Date: 1967-11-23

Edition: 4

The first phase of construction of the first steel enterprise built by Heilongjiang Province has been completed and put into production amidst the triumphant songs of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. This is a fruitful result of the proletarian revolutionary cadres and revolutionary masses on the industrial front in Heilongjiang Province earnestly carrying out Chairman Mao's great policy of grasping revolution, promoting production, promoting work, and promoting preparedness for war. It is a victory of the invincible thought of Mao Zedong.

### 黑龙江第一个钢铁企业首期工程竣工投产

1967-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    黑龙江第一个钢铁企业首期工程竣工投产
    在无产阶级文化大革命节节胜利的凯歌声中，黑龙江省地方建设的第一个钢铁企业的首期工程竣工投入生产。这是黑龙江省工业战线的无产阶级革命派和革命群众，认真贯彻执行毛主席抓革命、促生产、促工作、促战备的伟大方针的丰硕成果，是战无不胜的毛泽东思想的胜利。
