Title: Deep into Israeli-occupied areas to launch guerrilla warfare in the enemy's rear
Author:
Date: 1967-11-15
Edition: 6

The "Storm" guerrilla units, active in the Israeli-occupied areas, have recently intensified their military activities in the Judaea region, striking at the Israeli aggressors and achieving new victories.

On the evening of October 9, a reconnaissance team from the "Storm" guerrillas engaged in a battle with Israeli aggressors in the Malar region, killing three enemy soldiers and wounding several others. On the same evening, another "Storm" guerrilla unit fought with Israeli aggressors in the same region, killing and wounding thirteen enemy soldiers and destroying one enemy vehicle.

On the evening of October 13, another "Storm" guerrilla unit laid mines in the Kafr Lubin settlement, killing four Israeli guards and destroying one Israeli jeep.

On the evening of October 14, a "Storm" guerrilla unit engaged in a fierce battle with Israeli aggressors, which lasted for forty-five minutes. The guerrillas killed thirty-three Israeli aggressors, wounded many others, and destroyed one Israeli vehicle.

On the same evening, "Storm" guerrillas in the Sheikh Hussein Bridge region fought with enemy forces across the river, killing many enemy soldiers, including one high-ranking Israeli officer, and destroying three Israeli tanks and two cannons.

In addition, the "Storm" guerrillas attacked the Bisan Airport, the Shariah region, and the Erosaba region on October 23, 26, and 29, respectively, killing and wounding many enemy soldiers and seizing a batch of enemy weapons and ammunition.

### 深入以色列占领区　展开敌后游击战  巴勒斯坦“暴风”突击队狠揍侵略军

1967-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    深入以色列占领区　展开敌后游击战
    巴勒斯坦“暴风”突击队狠揍侵略军
    新华社大马士革电  据巴勒斯坦地下民族主义组织“暴风”总部的一项战报说，活跃在以色列占领地区的“暴风”突击队，最近在齐地加紧军事活动，打击以色列侵略者，不断取得新的胜利。
    十月九日晚上，在马勒地区，“暴风”突击队的一支侦察部队同以色列侵略者展开了一场战斗，打死敌人三人，打伤若干人。同一天晚上，“暴风”突击队同以色列侵略者在这个地区进行的另一次战斗中，打死打伤敌人十三人，击毁敌人汽车一辆。
    十月十三日晚上，“暴风”突击队的另一支部队在卡法罗宾居留地埋设地雷，炸死了守卫居留地的四个以色列卫兵，炸毁以色列一辆吉普车。
    十月十四日晚上，“暴风”突击队的一支部队同以色列侵略军展开激战，战斗持续达四十五分钟。突击队打死以色列侵略者三十三人，打伤许多人，并捣毁一辆以色列车辆。
    同一天晚上，谢赫·侯赛因桥地区的“暴风”突击队员在与对岸的敌人的一次战斗中，打死许多敌人，其中包括一名以色列高级军官，同时炸毁以色列三辆坦克和两门大炮。
    另外，“暴风”突击队在十月二十三日、二十六日和二十九日分别袭击了比桑机场、沙里亚特地区和埃罗萨巴地区，杀伤大批敌人，缴获敌人一批武器弹药。
