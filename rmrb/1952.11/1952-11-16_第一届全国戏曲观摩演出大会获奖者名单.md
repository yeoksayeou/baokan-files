List of Winners of the First National Opera Performance and Observation Conference

I. Honorary Awards: Mei Lanfang, Zhou Xinfang, Cheng Yanqiu, Yuan Xuefen, Chang Xiangyu, Wang Yaoqing, Gai Jiaotian.

II. Script Awards (Nine):
	* Yue Opera "The Butterfly Lovers," collectively adapted by the Yue Opera Creation Office of the East China Academy of Opera, written by Xu Jin, Chen Yu, Song Zhiyou, Cheng Rong, and Hong Ying, original adapter Nan Wei;
	* Ping Opera "Little Son-in-Law," playwright Cao Keying;
	* Shanghai Opera "Lohan Money," collectively adapted by the Creation and Research Office of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture and Art Administration, written by Zong Hua, Wen Mu, and Xing Zhi;
	* Sichuan Opera "The Story of Willow Shade," collectively adapted by the Southwest Performance Delegation;
	* Peking Opera "Generals and Ministers United," adapted by Wang Xiezhu and Weng Ouhong;
	* Huai Opera "Wang Gui and Li Xiangxiang," collectively adapted by the Creation Office of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture and Art Administration, written by Wang Jianmin and Tang Jishi;
	* Yue Opera "Romance of the West Chamber," adapted by the Yue Opera Troupe of the Cultural Work Troupe of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission;
	* Chu Opera "Ge Ma," adapted by the Wuhan Chu Opera Troupe;
	* Qin Opera "Touring Turtle Mountain," adapted by Ma Jianling.
