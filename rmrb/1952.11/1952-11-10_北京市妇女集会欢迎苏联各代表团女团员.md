Title: Beijing Women's Gathering Welcomes Female Members of Soviet Delegations
Author:
Date: 1952-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: Beijing women from all walks of life held a grand meeting at 1 PM this afternoon to welcome the female members of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, the Soviet Art Troupe, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation.

The female members of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, including Beloshapko, Head of the Agricultural Department of the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy, and twelve female members of the Soviet Art Troupe including Ulanova, were invited to attend the welcoming event.

The leader of the Soviet Art Troupe, Churaki, was also invited to attend the meeting.

Also invited to the meeting were the wife of the Chargé d'affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China, Mrs. Gudev, and the wife of the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Mrs. Zhukova.

Among those attending the meeting were Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Tian Xiujuan, Director of the North China Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wang Ziru, Deputy Director; Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation; Yang Yunru, Vice-Chairman; Liu Qingyang; as well as standing committee members and executive committee members of the aforementioned three organizations in Beijing, and over 1,200 female literary and art workers, female workers, female medical workers, female educators, female students, and female cadres from the capital. The meeting was presided over by Zhang Xiaomei.

Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a speech first. On behalf of the women of the capital and all of China, she warmly welcomed the Soviet female members who came to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities. She explained that the friendship between China and the Soviet Union is unbreakable and eternal. She said: "The present of Soviet women is our tomorrow, and the path of Soviet women is our path. Through you, we more clearly feel the happier tomorrow and brighter future for ourselves and our children; it inspires us even more to enthusiastically participate in national construction, defend world peace, defend women's rights, and defend the sacred cause of children."

Beloshapko then delivered a speech. She said that the Soviet people love the Chinese people and are infinitely delighted with the achievements of New China's construction. She introduced the great achievements of the Soviet Union in communist construction and the active participation of Soviet women in peaceful construction. She said that the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union is a guarantee for consolidating world peace.

Gaidai, Halima Nasyrova, and Tamara Hanon, members of the Soviet Art Troupe, delivered speeches one after another, unanimously congratulating the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

After the speeches, the Soviet female members received flowers from Beijing children and gifts from the Beijing women's community. The welcoming meeting ended in warm applause.

After the meeting, Soviet singers, Soviet People's Artists, Stalin Prize winners Gaidai and Halima Nasyrova, Soviet pianist and Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic Yemelyanova, Soviet violinist and international competition winner Kaverzneva, and Soviet singer, People's Artist of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, performer of songs and dances of all Soviet nationalities, and Stalin Prize winner Tamara Hanon performed wonderful musical and dance programs. Chinese Yue Opera famous actor Yuan Xuefen and others performed "Broken Bridge." These programs were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 北京市妇女集会欢迎苏联各代表团女团员

1952-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市妇女集会欢迎苏联各代表团女团员
    【新华社九日讯】北京各界妇女九日下午一时举行大会，欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团、苏军红旗歌舞团和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团的女团员。
    苏联文化工作者代表团女团员、蒂米利亚泽夫农业学院农业系主任别洛沙普柯、苏联艺术工作团团员乌兰诺娃等十二位女团员、苏军红旗歌舞团团长亚历山大罗夫夫人应邀出席了欢迎会。
    苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基也应邀出席了大会。
    应邀出席大会的，还有苏联驻华大使馆代办顾德夫的夫人和苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福的夫人。
    出席大会的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超，中华全国民主妇女联合会华北区工作委员会主任田秀涓、副主任王子如，北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅、副主席杨蕴如、刘清扬及上述三团体的在京常务委员和执行委员以及首都女文艺工作者、女工、女医务工作者、女教育工作者、女学生、女干部共一千二百余人。大会由张晓梅主持。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超首先致词。她代表首都的和全中国的妇女热烈欢迎这次来参加“中苏友好月”活动的苏联女团员。她说明中苏两国的友谊是牢不可破的和永恒的。她说：“苏联妇女的今天就是我们的明天，苏联妇女的道路就是我们的道路。通过你们，我们更清楚地感到自己的和我们孩子们的更幸福的明天、更美好的将来；更加鼓舞我们热烈参加祖国建设和保卫世界和平、保卫妇女权利、保卫儿童的神圣事业。”
    别洛沙普柯接着致词。她说：苏联人民热爱着中国人民，为新中国建设的成就感到无限的欢欣。她介绍了苏联共产主义建设方面的伟大成就和苏联妇女积极参加和平建设的情形。她说中苏两国人民的伟大友谊是巩固世界和平的保证。
    苏联艺术工作团团员盖达伊、哈利马·纳赛罗娃、塔马拉·哈侬相继致词，她们一致祝贺中苏两国人民的友谊。
    致词毕，苏联女团员接受了北京市儿童的献花和北京市妇女界的献礼。欢迎会在热烈的掌声中结束。
    会后由苏联歌唱家、苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者盖达伊和哈利马·纳赛罗娃，苏联钢琴家、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员叶美里扬诺娃，苏联提琴家、国际比赛获奖者卡威尔兹涅娃和苏联歌唱家、乌兹别克加盟共和国人民演员、苏联各民族歌曲及舞蹈表演者、斯大林奖金获得者塔马拉·哈侬表演了精彩的音乐舞蹈节目。中国越剧名演员袁雪芬等表演了“断桥”。这些节目受到观众的热烈欢迎。
