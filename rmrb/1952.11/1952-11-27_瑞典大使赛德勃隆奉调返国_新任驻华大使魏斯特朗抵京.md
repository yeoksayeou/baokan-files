Title: Swedish Ambassador Soderblom Recalled to His Country; New Ambassador to China, Wistrand, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Former Swedish Ambassador to our country, Soderblom, has been recalled to his country. The Swedish government has now appointed Wistrand as the Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to our country.

Ambassador Wistrand, accompanied by Mr. Bevan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy in China, arrived in Beijing by car on the afternoon of the 26th. Those who went to the station to greet him included Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of Western Europe and Africa, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. Many diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China were also present to welcome him.

### 瑞典大使赛德勃隆奉调返国  新任驻华大使魏斯特朗抵京

1952-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典大使赛德勃隆奉调返国
    新任驻华大使魏斯特朗抵京
    【新华社二十六日讯】瑞典前任驻我国特命全权大使赛德勃隆已奉调返国，瑞典王国政府现任命魏斯特朗为瑞典驻我国特命全权大使。
    魏斯特朗大使由瑞典驻我国大使馆临时代办贝凡陪同，在二十六日下午乘车抵京。赴车站迎接的有我中央人民政府外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、西欧非洲司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗和京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美等。到站迎接的还有各国驻我国使节和外交官员多人。
