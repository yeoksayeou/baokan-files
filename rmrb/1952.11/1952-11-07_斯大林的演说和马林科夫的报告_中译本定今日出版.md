Title: Stalin's Speech and Malenkov's Report to be Published in Chinese Today
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Chinese translation of Stalin's speech at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Malenkov's "Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) to the 19th Party Congress" will be published by the People's Publishing House on November 7th. Both books will be printed in 32mo format, with an initial print run of 800,000 copies each.

### 斯大林的演说和马林科夫的报告  中译本定今日出版

1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林的演说和马林科夫的报告
    中译本定今日出版
    【新华社六日讯】斯大林在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会上的演说和马林科夫所作的“苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会在第十九次党代表大会上的报告”的中译本，将在十一月七日由人民出版社出版。两书均以三十二开形式印制，初版将各发行八十万册。
