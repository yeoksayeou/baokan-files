Title: The Central Ministry of Culture Holds Banquet to Welcome Performers and Others from the Opera Performance and Observation Event

Author:

Date: 1952-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Central Ministry of Culture held a grand banquet on the 8th to welcome over 1,200 actors and other opera workers from across the country who came to Beijing to participate in the First National Opera Performance and Observation Event. Ma Xulun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, Qi Yanming, Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government, Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles, including Lao She, Yang Hansheng, Ouyang Yuqian, Ai Qing, Hong Shen, Zhang Geng, and Lü Ji, also attended the banquet. During the banquet, Ma Xulun, Qi Yanming, Yu Xinqing, and Wu Han all gave speeches, celebrating the success of the opera performance and observation event and encouraging actors and opera workers from all over the country, on the basis of the success of this event, to further implement Chairman Mao's opera work policy of "Let a hundred flowers bloom, weed through the old to bring forth the new." Representatives of delegations and veteran artists and famous actors Mei Lanfang, Cheng Yanqiu, Zhou Xinfang, Yuan Xuefen, Chang Xiangyu, and Gai Jiaotian, among others, also exchanged greetings and encouraged each other.

After the conclusion of the First National Opera Performance and Observation Event, delegations from East China, Northwest China, Central South China, and Shanxi Province continued to stay in Beijing to hold public performances. The performances included Yue Opera, Shanghai Opera, Jianghuai Opera, Qinqiang Opera, Mei Hu Opera, Han Opera, Chu Opera, Gui Opera, Xiang Opera, Hunan Flower Drum Opera, and Jin Opera, a total of eleven types. At the same time, the Northeast China delegation will go to Wuhan and Shanghai for public performances; the Xiangyu Opera Troupe from the Northwest China region will go to Tianjin for public performances; and the Southwest China delegation will travel to the Northeast for a short tour before returning to Beijing for public performances.

### 中央文化部举行宴会  欢宴戏曲观摩会演的演员等

1952-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部举行宴会
    欢宴戏曲观摩会演的演员等
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府文化部八日举行盛大宴会，欢宴各地来京参加第一届全国戏曲观摩演出大会的一千二百多位演员及其他戏曲工作者。中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副主任马叙伦，中央人民政府委员会办公厅主任齐燕铭，中央人民政府委员会办公厅副主任余心清，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，以及首都文化艺术界人士老舍、阳翰笙、欧阳予倩、艾青、洪深、张庚、吕骥等也出席了宴会。席间马叙伦、齐燕铭、余心清及吴晗都讲了话，庆祝戏曲观摩会演的胜利，并勉励各地来京演员及戏曲工作者在这次会演胜利的基础上，进一步贯彻毛主席“百花齐放，推陈出新”的戏曲工作方针。代表团及到会的老艺人和著名演员梅兰芳、程砚秋、周信芳、袁雪芬、常香玉、盖叫天等也互致祝词，相互勉励。
    第一届全国戏曲观摩演出大会演出结束后，华东、西北、中南等区及山西省的代表团继续留京举行公演。公演的有越剧、沪剧、江淮戏、秦腔、郿鄠戏、汉剧、楚剧、桂剧、湘剧、湖南花鼓戏、晋剧等十一种。同时，东北区代表团将赴武汉和上海公演；西北区香玉剧社将赴天津公演；西南区代表团将赴东北作短期旅行演出后再返京公演。
