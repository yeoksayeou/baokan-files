Title: Our Delegation Leaves Beijing for Berlin to Attend the 5th International Plant Protection Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Our delegation, invited to attend the 5th International Plant Protection Conference to be held in Berlin on December 2nd, departed from Beijing on the 24th. The Chinese delegation consists of Xi Fengzhou (head of the delegation), Deputy Director of the Plant Protection Division of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, Shen Qiyi, Provost and Professor of the Department of Plant Pathology of Beijing Agricultural University, Cao Ji, Entomologist of the North China Agricultural Science Institute, and Zhang Ruoshi, Engineer of the General Administration of Commodity Inspection of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government.

### 参加第五届国际植物保护会议  我国代表团离北京赴柏林

1952-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    参加第五届国际植物保护会议
    我国代表团离北京赴柏林
    【新华社二十四日讯】应邀参加将于十二月二日在柏林召开的第五届国际植物保护会议的我国代表团，已在二十四日自北京启程。我国代表团由中央人民政府农业部病虫害防治司副司长席凤洲（团长）、北京农业大学教务长兼植物病理系教授沈其益、华北农业科学研究所昆虫系工程师曹骥、中央人民政府对外贸易部商品检验总局工程师张若蓍等四人组成。
