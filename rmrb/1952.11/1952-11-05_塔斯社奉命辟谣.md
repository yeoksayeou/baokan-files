Title: TASS is Ordered to Refute Rumors
Author:
Date: 1952-11-05
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: TASS, Moscow, November 3rd: TASS refutes rumors. Recently, some American news agencies and Agence France-Presse have spread news saying that American and Soviet representatives have held negotiations on the Korean issue.
    TASS is authorized to declare that such news is untrue and is intended to mislead world public opinion.

### 塔斯社奉命辟谣

1952-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    塔斯社奉命辟谣
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：塔斯社辟谣。近来某些美国通讯社与法国新闻社散布消息说，美国与苏联代表就朝鲜问题举行了谈判。
    塔斯社奉命声明，这种消息都是不真实的，其目的在于迷惑世界舆论。
