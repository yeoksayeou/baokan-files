Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: The Polish People's Republic Cultural Delegation, consisting of seven members, which came to visit our country under the 1952 cooperation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, with the exception of delegate Peterkiewicz who remained in our country for continued visits, the other six members visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Tianjin and other cities, and then took a plane back home on November 5.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Sha Kofu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Culture and Education, advisor Zhao Feng, and officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirylok, and his wife, as well as embassy staff, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their visit, the delegation met with people from our country's literary, artistic, and educational circles, held symposiums, visited factories, rural areas, schools, scenic spots, and historical sites, and held piano and vocal concerts in the major cities they visited, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 波兰文化代表团离京返国

1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰文化代表团离京返国
    【新华社五日讯】根据中波文化合作协定一九五二年合作计划来访问我国的波兰人民共和国文化代表团一行七人，除代表彼得克维兹还留在我国继续参观外，其他六人在访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉、天津等城市后，于十一月五日乘机返国。
    到机场欢送的有中央人民政府文化部办公厅主任沙可夫，政务院文教委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、参事赵渢及外交部官员等。
    波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克夫妇以及使馆人员亦赴机场欢送。
    代表团在访问期间曾先后会见了我国文艺、教育界等人士，举行了座谈会，参观了工厂、农村、学校、名胜古迹等，并曾在所访问的各大城市中举行钢琴及歌唱演奏会，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
