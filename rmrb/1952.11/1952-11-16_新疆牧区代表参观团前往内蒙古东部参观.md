Title: Xinjiang Pastoral Area Delegation Visits Eastern Inner Mongolia

Author: 
Date: 1952-11-16
Page: 2

Economic Briefs

    Xinjiang Pastoral Area Delegation Visits Eastern Inner Mongolia
    After staying in Gui Sui City, the location of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, for thirteen days, a Xinjiang Province pastoral area delegation of sixty-three people set off on November 6 to visit the eastern part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
    During their stay in Gui Sui, the Xinjiang Province pastoral area delegation listened to reports on the construction of various aspects of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, given by responsible cadres of the CCP Central Committee's Sui branch and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, and held several symposiums. The delegation received a warm reception from people of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. The Hui Autonomous Region prepared Xinjiang cuisine to treat all members of the delegation. The deep friendship between the fraternal nationalities was like that of blood relatives.

### 新疆牧区代表参观团前往内蒙古东部参观

1952-11-16
第2版()
专栏：经济简讯

    新疆牧区代表参观团前往内蒙古东部参观
    新疆省牧区代表参观团一行六十三人，在内蒙古自治区人民政府所在地——归绥市逗留十三天后，于本月六日启程前往内蒙古自治区东部参观。
    新疆省牧区代表参观团在归绥逗留期间，听取了中共中央蒙绥分局及内蒙古自治区人民政府负责干部所作的有关内蒙古自治区各方面建设情况的报告，并举行了多次座谈会。参观团在归绥受到了内蒙古自治区各族各界人民的热情招待，回民自治区并作了新疆饭菜招待全体团员。兄弟民族间的深厚友谊，如同一家骨肉。
