Title: China's Second Delegation to North Korea Returns to Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 17th. The Second Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, both the main delegation and its sub-delegations, have successively returned to Shenyang from the Korean front.
    The Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Northeast People's Government, the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea jointly held a banquet to welcome representatives of the main delegation. At the meeting, Lin Feng, Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and He Jinnian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhang Xiushan, Deputy Political Commissar, Mo Wenhua, Director of the Political Department, and Lu Bo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, all raised their glasses to congratulate the delegation on fulfilling the entrustment of the people of the motherland; and wished the delegation to widely convey the heroic fighting deeds and victorious confidence of the Chinese and Korean people's troops to the people of the whole country, further strengthen the confidence of the people of the whole country in defending peace, and inspire the people of the whole country to devote themselves to the large-scale economic construction and cultural construction that is about to begin. Liu Jingfan, head of the main delegation, assured on behalf of the entire delegation: The many heroic struggles and great victories of the Chinese and Korean people's troops witnessed by the delegates will be conveyed to the people of the whole country in various ways, and the glorious mission entrusted by the people of the whole country will be fulfilled satisfactorily.

### 我国人民第二届赴朝慰问团回到沈阳

1952-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民第二届赴朝慰问团回到沈阳
    【新华社沈阳十七日电】从朝鲜前线回国的中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团和各分团已先后回到沈阳。
    中共中央东北局、东北人民政府、中国人民解放军东北军区、东北抗美援朝总分会曾联合举行宴会，欢迎慰问团总团的代表。会上，中共中央东北局副书记、东北人民政府副主席林枫和中国人民解放军东北军区副司令员贺晋年、副政治委员张秀山、政治部主任莫文骅、东北抗美援朝总分会副秘书长鲁伯，都举杯祝贺慰问团完成了祖国人民的嘱托；并预祝慰问团把中朝人民部队的英勇战斗事迹和胜利信心广泛地向全国人民进行传达，进一步加强全国人民保卫和平的信心和鼓舞全国人民投入即将开始的大规模的经济建设和文化建设。慰问团总团长刘景范代表全团保证：将慰问团代表们亲眼见到的中朝人民部队的许多英勇斗争事迹和伟大胜利用各种方法传达给全国人民，圆满地完成全国人民嘱托的光荣的使命。
