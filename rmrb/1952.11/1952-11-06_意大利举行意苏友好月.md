Title: Italy Holds Italian-Soviet Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1952-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome: According to TASS, the Italian-Soviet Association held a conference at the Atriano Theatre on the 2nd to celebrate the beginning of the annual Italian-Soviet Friendship Month. Five thousand people attended the conference, including cultural workers, social celebrities, political leaders, and foreign diplomats stationed in Italy.

Professor Monti, a history professor at the University of Turin, spoke at the conference about the cultural relations between Italy and the Soviet Union and the significance of expanding cultural exchanges. Vittorio, the General Secretary of the Italian General Confederation of Labour, spoke about the necessity of expanding Italian-Soviet trade and economic relations. Nenni, the General Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party and Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, delivered a speech before the conference concluded. Professor Borti, the chairman of the conference, read out many congratulatory telegrams from all walks of life in Italy celebrating the beginning of Italian-Soviet Friendship Month.

### 意大利举行意苏友好月

1952-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利举行意苏友好月
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：意苏协会二日在阿特里亚诺剧院举行大会，庆祝一年一度的意苏友好月的开始。参加大会的有五千人，其中有文化工作者、社会名流、政界领袖和各国驻意大利的外交人员。
    都灵大学历史学教授孟蒂在大会上讲述了意苏两国的文化关系和扩展文化交流的意义。意大利总工会总书记维多里奥讲述了扩大意苏贸易和经济关系的必要。意大利社会党总书记、意大利全国和平理事会主席南尼在大会结束前发表了演说。大会主席勃尔蒂教授在会上宣读了意大利各界人士庆祝意苏友好月开始的许多贺电。
