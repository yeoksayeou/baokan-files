Title: Profound Friendship Between the Chinese and Soviet People (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 6

The head of the Soviet Film Artists Delegation, Fedorov (third from the right), and others held a discussion with workers during a visit to the Shijingshan Power Plant. Second from the right is Liu Yingyuan, director of the Shijingshan Power Plant. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)

On November 10th, Chu Zhilaki, Vice Chairman of the Arts Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Soviet violinist Kogan, Soviet magician Chitoshiwili, and pianist accompanist Metnik visited the Changxindian Railway Factory and were warmly welcomed by the workers. This is Comrade Chu Zhilaki giving a speech to the workers. (Photo by Liu Shen)

Members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble visited the Summer Palace and were warmly welcomed by soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in front of the Hall of Dispelling Clouds. They sang "Moscow-Beijing" together in Chinese and Russian. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Yang Ziyi)

The General Political Department of the Central People's Government and People's Revolutionary Military Commission held a report meeting, inviting the head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, B.A. Alexandrov, to report on the experiences of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble. (Photo by Bai Shizao, PLA Pictorial)

Women from all walks of life in Beijing welcomed the female members of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, the Soviet Art Troupe, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Soviet Film Artists Delegation. Comrade Chu Zhilaki was also invited to attend. At the welcome meeting, the honored guests received flowers from Beijing children. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)

On November 10th, the Soviet Art Troupe visited Huangtugang, a rural village on the outskirts of Beijing. Workers at the Huangtugang Village flower farm were busy arranging flowers to give to their Soviet friends. (Photo by Zhang Li of this newspaper)

At the welcome rally for the Soviet Film Artists Delegation in the capital, film workers presented flowers to (from left) Fedorov (head of the delegation), Petrov, Cherkasov, Smirnova, Ladynina, and Pavlov. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Jia Huamin)

At the reception held by Premier Zhou Enlai to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, Mei Lanfang had a cordial conversation with Tamara Khanum (left) and Halima Nasyrova (right), members of the Soviet Art Troupe. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Qi Guanshan)

Members of the Yin Weichen Agricultural Production Cooperative in Huangtugang Village specially selected the best corn, peanuts, and sweet potatoes they had grown themselves to present to their Soviet friends as souvenirs to welcome the guests from afar. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Yuan Hao)

Farmers from nearby villages in Huangtugang also came to participate in the grand welcome meeting. This is a scene of farmers from Wanquansi Village performing the "Lion Dance" at the welcome meeting. (Photo by Zhang Li of this newspaper)

The famous Soviet ballet dancer Ulanova, art director of the Puppet Theater Obraztsov, and four others visited Beijing No. 12 Girls' Middle School and were warmly welcomed by the students. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Yang Ziyi)

Beloshapko, a member of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation and head of the Agronomy Department of the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy, gave a report on agricultural issues to the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Beijing Agricultural University. The audience applauded enthusiastically as he left the stage after the report. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Yuan Hao)

Academic Secretary of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Doctor of Biology, and two-time Stalin Prize winner Grusenko participated in a scientific symposium at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Yang Ziyi)

Yefimov, a member of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, professor at the Soviet State Leningrad University, and author of "A History of the Development of Sino-Soviet Friendship," gave a report to teachers and cadres of Beijing universities and government offices. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Li Jiuling)

When the Soviet Art Troupe left Huangtugang Village, every farmer rushed to shake hands with the representatives to say goodbye. (Photo by Zhang Li of this newspaper)

### 中苏人民深厚的友情（图片）

1952-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏人民深厚的友情
    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫（右起第三人）等访问石景山发电厂时，与工人们举行了座谈会。右起第二人为石景山发电厂厂长刘英源。    （新华社稿·方弘摄）
    十一月十日，苏联部长会议艺术委员会副主席楚拉基、苏联提琴家柯岗、苏联魔术家契托希维利和钢琴伴奏家梅特尼克等访问长辛店铁路工厂时，受到工人们热烈的欢迎。这是楚拉基同志向工人演讲时的情形。    （刘申摄）
    苏军红旗歌舞团团员游览颐和园时，在排云殿前受到中国人民解放军战士们的热烈欢迎，他们用中苏两国语言共同歌唱“莫斯科——北京”。（新华社稿·杨子颐摄）
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部举行报告会，邀请苏军红旗歌舞团团长鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫报告苏军红旗歌舞团的经历。    （解放军画报社稿·白世藻摄）
    北京市各界妇女欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团、苏军红旗歌舞团和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团的女团员，楚拉基同志也应邀出席。在欢迎会上，贵宾们接受了北京市儿童的献花。 
    （新华社稿·方弘摄）
    十一月十日苏联艺术工作团访问了京郊农村黄土岗。黄土岗村的花场工人忙着扎好鲜花准备送给苏联朋友。    （本报张力摄）
    首都电影工作者在欢迎苏联电影艺术工作者代表团大会上，向（左起）费道罗夫（团长）、彼得罗夫、契尔柯夫、斯米尔诺娃、拉迪尼娜和巴甫洛夫等献花。（新华社稿·贾化民摄）
    在周恩来总理举行庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年酒会上，梅兰芳与苏联艺术工作团团员塔马拉·哈侬（左）、哈利马·纳赛罗娃（右）欢谈。    （新华社稿·齐观山摄）
    黄土岗村殷维臣农业生产合作社的社员们为了迎接远来的宾客，特地选取了自己亲手栽培起来的最好的玉米、花生和白薯等献给苏联朋友们留为纪念。（新华社稿·袁浩摄）
    黄土岗附近各村的农民也都赶来参加盛大的欢迎会。这是万泉寺村的农民们在欢迎会上表演“狮子舞”的情形。    （本报张力摄）
    苏联著名巴蕾舞演员乌兰诺娃、木偶戏剧院艺术指导奥布拉兹卓夫等六人访问北京市第十二女子中学时，受到同学们热烈的欢迎。
    （新华社稿·杨子颐摄）
    苏联文化工作者代表团团员、蒂米利亚泽夫农业学院农学系主任别洛沙普柯向中国科学院和北京农业大学等单位作关于农业问题的报告，报告完毕离场时，听众热烈鼓掌。    （新华社稿·袁浩摄）
    苏联科学院学术秘书、生物学博士、斯大林奖金两次获得者格鲁森科访问中国科学院时应邀参加了科学座谈。（新华社稿·杨子颐摄）
    苏联文化工作者代表团团员、苏联国立列宁格勒大学教授、中国近代史作者叶菲莫夫向北京各大学教师和机关干部作“中苏友谊发展史”的报告。    （新华社稿·李九龄摄）
    当苏联艺术工作团离开黄土岗村的时候，每一个农民都争着和代表们握手告别。（本报张力摄）
