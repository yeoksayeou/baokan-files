Author:
Date: 1952-11-03

Our Great Motherland
    People across the country are warmly welcoming "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." These are female students from the Liulitun People's School in the suburbs of Beijing. During "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," they are preparing to open a photo exhibition to introduce the communist construction of the Soviet Union and the lives of the Soviet people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地人民正在热烈迎接“中苏友好月”。这是北京郊区六里屯民校的女学生，她们在“中苏友好月”，正准备开一个图片展览会，介绍苏联的共产主义建设和苏联人民的生活。
