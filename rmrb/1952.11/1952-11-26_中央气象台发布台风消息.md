Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Announcement
Author:
Date: 1952-11-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 25th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon announcement stating: At 2:00 PM on the 25th, a typhoon appeared over the sea east of the Philippine Islands. Its center was located at 17.2 degrees north latitude and 123.2 degrees east longitude. It is currently moving in a northeast by north direction, and there will be large waves in the East China Sea.

### 中央气象台发布台风消息

1952-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布台风消息
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央气象台发布台风消息称：二十五日下午二时，菲律宾群岛东部的海面出现台风，它的中心位置为北纬十七点二度，东经一百二十三点二度。目前正向东北偏北方向进行，东海将有大浪。
