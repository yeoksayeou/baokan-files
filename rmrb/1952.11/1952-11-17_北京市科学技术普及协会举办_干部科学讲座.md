Title: Beijing Association for Science and Technology Popularization Holds "Cadre Science Lectures"
Author:
Date: 1952-11-17
Page: 3

Culture Briefs

    To equip cadres with general scientific knowledge to meet the needs of the country's large-scale economic construction, the Beijing Association for Science and Technology Popularization began holding weekly "Cadre Science Lectures" in October this year. The audience consists of cadres from central and Beijing-level government organizations. The lecturers are members of the Beijing Association for Science and Technology Popularization and domestic experts in various scientific fields. Since the "Cadre Science Lectures" began on October 18th, four lectures have been held: the first lecture was given by water conservancy expert Zhang Hanying, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government, on "New China's Water Conservancy Construction"; the second lecture was given by Chen Fengtong, Director of the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, on "The Great Stalin Plan for the Transformation of Nature"; the third lecture was given by nuclear physicist Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Modern Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, on "Atomic Energy and World Peace"; and the fourth lecture was given by Fu Lianzhang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, on "Learning from the Advanced Medicine of the Soviet Union." Each lecture has been warmly received by the audience. During the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," the "Cadre Science Lectures" will introduce the achievements of advanced Soviet science and technology, and will also cover topics such as "The Great Victory of Michurin's Agricultural Biology," "The Brilliant Achievements of Pavlov's Theory," and "The Geography of the Soviet Union."

### 北京市科学技术普及协会举办“干部科学讲座”

1952-11-17
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    北京市科学技术普及协会举办“干部科学讲座”
    为了使机关干部获得一般科学知识以适应国家大规模经济建设的需要，北京市科学技术普及协会从今年十月份起开始举办每周一次的“干部科学讲座”。听讲的是中央一级及北京市一级的机关干部。讲员由北京科学技术普及协会的会员和国内各门科学专家担任。干部科学讲座自十月十八日起举办以来，已进行了四讲：第一讲由水利专家、中央人民政府水利部副部长张含英讲“新中国的水利建设”，第二讲由华北农业科学研究所所长陈凤桐讲“伟大的斯大林改造自然计划”，第三讲由原子核物理学家、中国科学院近代物理研究所所长钱三强讲“原子能与世界和平”，第四讲由中央人民政府卫生部副部长傅连暲讲“学习苏联的先进医学”。每次讲演都受到听众的热烈欢迎。干部科学讲座在“中苏友好月”内将介绍苏联先进的科学技术的成就，还要讲“米丘林农业生物学的伟大胜利”、“巴甫洛夫学说的辉煌成就”及“苏联的地理”等。
