Title: At the Chinese Art Exhibition in Moscow, Soviet People Eagerly Visit Our Country's Works
Author:
Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th: According to TASS, Soviet people are deeply interested in the national culture of their great eastern neighbor, China, and this has been especially evident in the first few days since the start of "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." In recent days, large numbers of viewers have gone to the Moscow State Museum of Oriental Culture to visit the large-scale permanent Chinese art exhibition. They are viewing the many works of ancient Chinese artists, as well as the works of the finest artists, sculptors, bone carvers, and stone carvers of the People's Republic of China. The Soviet people have a deep friendship for the people of free China. They know that the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is a strong bastion of peace and security in the Far East and the world.

### 在莫斯科中国艺术展览会上  苏联人民踊跃参观我国作品

1952-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    在莫斯科中国艺术展览会上
    苏联人民踊跃参观我国作品
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民对于他们伟大的东方邻国——中国的民族文化深感兴趣，在“中苏友好月”开始后头几天内，这一点表现得特别明显。数日来，大批观众前往莫斯科国立东方文化博物馆参观大规模的常设的中国艺术展览会。他们参观陈列着的许多古代中国艺术家的作品，以及中华人民共和国的最优秀的艺术家、雕刻家、骨刻家及石刻家的作品。苏联人民对自由中国的人民怀着一种深厚的友情。他们知道，中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊是远东和全世界和平及安全的坚强堡垒。
