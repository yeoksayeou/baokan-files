Title:  Calling for Participation in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities - China Zhi Gong Party Issues Notice
Author: 
Date: 1952-11-03
Page: 1

    Calling for Participation in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities
    China Zhi Gong Party Issues Notice
    [From Our Newspaper] In order to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union and mobilize party members to participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities, the China Zhi Gong Party has issued a notice to all local organizations. The notice requires local branches to hold celebrations during "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," organize special reports on studying advanced Soviet experiences, and promote efforts by party members and workers in culture, education, and science to learn from advanced Soviet experiences. It also calls for holding discussion meetings at the branch or group level, discussing the importance of the Sino-Soviet friendship alliance for safeguarding world peace and promoting China's construction. The notice also requires all branches and sub-branches to actively participate in various "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities organized by local and affiliated organizations and groups.

### 号召参加“中苏友好月”活动  九三学社发出通知

1952-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    号召参加“中苏友好月”活动
    九三学社发出通知
    【本报讯】九三学社为了庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年和动员社员参加“中苏友好月”活动，特向各地方组织发出通知。通知要求各地分社在“中苏友好月”中举办庆祝会，组织有关学习苏联先进经验的专题报告，推动社员和文教科学工作者努力学习苏联先进经验；以支社或小组为单位举行座谈会，座谈中苏友好同盟对于保卫世界和平和促进我国建设的重要性。通知并要求各分社、支社积极参加当地和所在机关团体所举办的“中苏友好月”各项活动。
