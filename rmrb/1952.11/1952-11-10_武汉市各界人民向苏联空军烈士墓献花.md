Title: People from All Walks of Life in Wuhan Lay Wreaths at the Tomb of Soviet Air Force Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1952-11-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 9th. People from all walks of life in Wuhan went to the International Cemetery on the 7th to lay wreaths at the Tomb of Soviet Air Force Martyrs. The Soviet Air Force Volunteer Team martyrs gloriously sacrificed themselves during the air battles defending Wuhan in 1938 during the War of Resistance Against Japan. Their names are: Giselev, Balamonov, Gijieluoheden, Kozlov, Bishotsky, Vasin, Kashellov, and eight other unknown heroes.

On the 7th, in front of the solemn and solemn martyrs' tomb, Zhang Nanxian, Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, and Li Xiannian, Mayor of Wuhan, laid wreaths and paid their respects to the martyrs amid mournful music. Accompanying them were Li Xuefeng, Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations in Central South and Wuhan. In addition, workers, government staff, and citizens also went to lay wreaths.

### 武汉市各界人民向苏联空军烈士墓献花

1952-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉市各界人民向苏联空军烈士墓献花
    【新华社汉口九日电】武汉市各界人民七日前往万国公墓，向苏联空军烈士墓献花。苏联空军志愿队烈士们是一九三八年抗日战争时期在保卫武汉空战中与敌机激战时光荣牺牲的。他们的名字是：吉谢辽夫、巴拉莫诺夫、吉皆洛赫登、考兹洛夫、比捎特斯基、瓦新、卡谢洛夫，另外还有八位无名英雄。
    七日，在庄严而肃穆的烈士墓前，中南军政委员会副主席张难先、武汉市市长李先念在哀乐声中向烈士献花、敬礼。陪同献花的有中南军政委员会副主席李雪峰，及中南、武汉各民主党派、各人民团体负责人。此外，前往献花的还有工人、机关工作人员和市民等。
