Title: Soviet Cultural and Art Workers Arrive in Hangzhou, Chengdu, and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1952-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd:

    Soviet Art Work Delegation leader Churaki and delegation members Mikhailov, Halima Nasyrova, Nechayev, Ulanova, and others, a group of ten, flew from Guangzhou to Shanghai by special plane and arrived in Hangzhou by car at 12:00 midnight on the 22nd. They were warmly welcomed at the station by the heads of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City, as well as representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 5,000 people. The people of Hangzhou City, eager to celebrate with their Soviet friends, decorated the streets with lanterns and banners and lined the streets with torches to welcome them.

    Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation members Zakharov, Beloshapko, and Soviet Art Work Delegation members Gaidai and others, a group of twelve, flew from Xi'an to Chengdu on the afternoon of the 21st. Li Dazhang, President of the Sichuan Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, heads of various people's organizations, students from 49 ethnic groups at the Southwest Institute for Nationalities, and representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 500 people, went to the station to welcome them. Chengdu presented a festive atmosphere throughout the day, with citizens wearing festive attire, holding flowers, and lining the streets for more than five miles to welcome the Soviet friends.

    Soviet Art Work Delegation members Tamara Hanon, Rakhimov, and others, a group of seven, arrived in Kaifeng by train from Xi'an on the 22nd. Among those who went to the station to greet them were the heads of the Henan Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and various people's organizations, as well as representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 1,000 people. When the honored Soviet guests entered the city by car, more than 50,000 citizens applauded and cheered along the way, warmly welcoming them. On this day, the entire city was decorated with lanterns and streamers, and red flags fluttered everywhere.

    Soviet Art Work Delegation members Puchitsen and Karacheva returned to Beijing by plane from Xi'an on the 21st.

    In addition: The Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation, headed by Fedorov, a group of seven, arrived in Tianjin by car from Changchun on the morning of the 23rd. Also accompanying them was Mr. Sford, the Chief Representative of the Soviet Film Export Company in China.

    Head of the delegation Fedorov and the members received a warm welcome at the station. Many workers and students enthusiastically presented them with flowers and embraced them.

    After arriving in Tianjin, the delegation visited the New Port and the state-owned Tianjin No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory and Tianjin Normal College, where they were warmly welcomed by the masses everywhere they went.

    During their stay in Shenyang and Changchun, the delegation conducted visits and tours. While in Changchun, delegation leader Fedorov and member, the famous screenwriter Smirnova, were invited to give reports at the Northeast Institute of Science and the Northeast Normal University, respectively. The members also held four separate seminars with directors, actors, and photographers from the Northeast Film Studio, specifically introducing many advanced experiences in film work. The famous Soviet actors Cherkasov and Radinina vividly answered questions about acting techniques at the seminars. They emphasized the importance of actors going deep into reality, deep into life, and studying theory, and not being satisfied with the status quo, but constantly seeking new creative paths. They also offered valuable suggestions on the films "Gate No. 6," "Harvest," and "When the Grapes are Ripe," which the studio was currently producing. The famous Soviet director Petrov, using his direction of the film "The Battle of Stalingrad" as an example, gave a comprehensive and specific introduction from reading the script to film editing. He said: Film art is performing the task of educating the younger generation, and this sacred responsibility requires us to have the determination to dedicate ourselves to this work and infuse all our energy and talent into it.

### 苏联文化艺术工作者分别抵杭州成都等地

1952-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化艺术工作者分别抵杭州成都等地
    【新华社二十三日讯】苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基和团员米哈伊洛夫、哈利马·纳赛罗娃、聂恰耶夫、乌兰诺娃等一行十人由广州乘专机飞抵上海，在二十二日夜十二时乘车到达杭州。他们在车站上受到浙江省和杭州市的中苏友好协会负责人及各界代表五千多人的热情欢迎。渴望着和苏联友人联欢的杭州市人民，到处张灯结彩，并高举火炬夹道欢迎。
    苏联文化工作者代表团团员查哈罗夫、别洛沙普柯，苏联艺术工作团团员盖达伊等十二人在二十一日下午由西安飞抵成都。四川省中苏友好协会会长李大章、各人民团体负责人和西南民族学院四十九个民族的学员及各界代表五百多人前往车站欢迎。成都市整日呈现一片节日气象，市民们穿着盛装，举着鲜花，排了五里多长的行列夹道欢迎苏联友人。
    苏联艺术工作团团员塔马拉·哈侬、拉希莫夫等一行七人二十二日自西安乘火车抵达开封。前往车站迎接的有河南省中苏友好协会和各人民团体的负责人及各界代表一千多人。当苏联贵宾们乘车进入市区时，沿途有五万多市民鼓掌欢呼，热烈欢迎。这一天全市悬灯挂彩，红旗飘扬。
    苏联艺术工作团团员普奇岑、卡拉巧娃在二十一日自西安乘机返抵北京。
    又讯：以费道罗夫为首的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团一行七人，于二十三日上午自长春乘车抵达天津。同来的还有苏联影片输出公司驻华总代表史福德。
    费道罗夫团长和团员们在车站受到热烈欢迎。许多工人、学生热情地向他们献花、和他们拥抱。
    代表团到天津后，参观了新港，访问了国营天津第一棉纺织厂、天津师范学院，到处受到群众热烈的欢迎。
    代表团在沈阳、长春期间，曾先后进行了参观和访问。在长春期间，团长费道罗夫、团员著名电影剧作家斯米尔诺娃曾应邀分别在东北科学研究所和东北师范大学作了报告。团员们并与东北电影制片厂的导演、演员及摄影人员等分别举行了四个座谈会，具体地介绍了电影工作中的许多先进经验。苏联名演员契尔卡索夫、拉迪尼娜在座谈会上生动地回答了大家提出的关于表演艺术方面的问题。他们强调演员深入实际、深入生活和学习理论的重要，不要满足现状，要一刻不停地寻找新的剧作道路。他们对该厂正在拍制的“六号门”、“丰收”、
    “葡萄熟了的时候”等影片，也提出了一些宝贵的意见。苏联名导演彼得罗夫以自己导演“斯大林格勒大会战”影片为例，从读文字剧本到电影剪接，作了全面而具体的介绍。他说：电影艺术是在执行教育年青一代的任务，这个神圣职责要求我们必须具有献身于这一工作的决心，并把所有的精力和才能贯输到工作中去。
