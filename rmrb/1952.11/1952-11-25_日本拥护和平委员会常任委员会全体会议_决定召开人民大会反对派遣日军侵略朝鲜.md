Title: Japan Peace Preservation Committee Standing Committee Plenary Session Decides to Convene People's Congress Against Dispatch of Japanese Troops to Invade Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-11-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 24th: "Peace News" reports that the Standing Committee of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee held a plenary session in Tokyo. The meeting decided to convene a People's Congress in December to oppose the dispatch of Japanese armed forces to Korea. The meeting also decided to hold a national conference in January 1953 to protest the establishment of military bases. The meeting adopted an action program drafted by the Japan Peace Preservation Committee. This program encourages the Japanese people to rise up against war, strive for peace and independence, and fight against the revival of militarism, against the stationing of foreign troops in the country, against turning Japan into a military base, and against rearmament.
    The meeting of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee called on the nation to unite and cooperate with the peace forces of the world.
    "Peace News" also reported that a students' peace conference will be held at the end of November.
    "Industrial Economic News" reported that approximately 1,500 representatives from labor unions and other organizations in Kyushu participated in the peace conference held in Kumamoto. At the meeting, Tan Tokusaburo, a representative of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee, reported on the achievements and resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held in Beijing. The conference decided to send representatives from Kyushu to the World People's Peace Congress and elected Matsumoto Jiichiro as the head of the delegation. The conference passed resolutions against rearmament and the establishment of military bases. The participants in the conference also unanimously demanded an immediate end to the Korean War.

### 日本拥护和平委员会常任委员会全体会议  决定召开人民大会反对派遣日军侵略朝鲜

1952-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会常任委员会全体会议
    决定召开人民大会反对派遣日军侵略朝鲜
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社东京二十四日讯：“和平新闻”报道，日本拥护和平委员会常任委员会在东京举行了全体会议。会议决定在十二月召开一次人民大会，反对派遣日本武装部队到朝鲜去。会议并决定在一九五三年一月召开一个全国会议，抗议建立军事基地。会议通过了日本拥护和平委员会所拟定的行动纲领。这个纲领鼓励日本人民起来反对战争，争取和平与独立，为反对军国主义的复活、反对在国内驻扎外国军队、反对把日本变为军事基地以及反对重新武装而斗争。
    日本拥护和平委员会的会议号召全国团结起来，并与全世界的和平力量合作。
    “和平新闻”又报道，十一月底将举行一次学生的和平会议。
    “产业经济新闻”报道，九州的工会和其他团体的代表约一千五百人，参加了在熊本举行的和平大会。会上，日本拥护和平委员会的代表淡德三郎报告了在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的成就和各项决议。大会决定派九州的代表参加世界人民和平大会，并选出松本治一郎为代表团团长。大会通过了反对重新武装和建立军事基地的决议。参加大会的人并一致要求立即结束朝鲜战争。
