Title: Tibetan Delegation and Others Arrive in Hailar City, Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1952-11-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The Tibetan Delegation, the Changdu Area Tibetan People's National Day Observation Delegation, Ma Yinu'er, head of the National Day Observation Delegation from various ethnic groups in the Northwest, and Xi Raojiacuo, Vice Chairman of the People's Government of Qinghai Province, who are visiting the Northeast and Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 10th. The Tibetan Delegation and the Changdu Area Tibetan People's National Day Observation Delegation took the same train to the eastern part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region for a visit and arrived in Hailar City, Hulunbuir League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, on the 11th.
    Correction: The word "yesterday" in the title of the article "National Opera Festival Concludes Yesterday" on the first page of this newspaper on the 16th should be deleted.

### 西藏致敬团等抵内蒙古海拉尔市

1952-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏致敬团等抵内蒙古海拉尔市
    据新华社讯：到东北、内蒙古自治区等地参观的西藏致敬团、昌都地区藏族人民国庆节观礼代表团，西北各民族国庆节观礼代表团团长马依努尔和青海省人民政府副主席喜饶嘉措等一行，于十日上午到达沈阳。西藏致敬团和昌都地区藏族人民国庆节观礼代表团人员，当日乘原车赴内蒙古自治区东部地区参观，并于十一日到达内蒙古自治区呼纳盟海拉尔市。更    正：十六日本报第一版“全国戏曲观摩演出大会昨闭幕”一稿标题中之“昨”字，应删去。
