Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Today's Beijing Municipal Celebration of the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 3

Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Today's Beijing Municipal Celebration of the October Revolution Anniversary

    People from all walks of life in Beijing will hold a celebration meeting this afternoon to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union. Beijing People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast live the celebration meeting from 1:15 PM today. The broadcasting frequencies are 850 kilocycles, 1080 kilocycles, 750 kilocycles, and 1320 kilocycles. People from all walks of life are welcome to tune in on time.

### 北京人民广播电台今日转播北京市庆祝十月革命节大会实况

1952-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

    北京人民广播电台今日转播北京市庆祝十月革命节大会实况
    北京市各界人民为庆祝苏联伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年，今天下午举行庆祝大会。北京人民广播电台决定于今日下午一时十五分起转播庆祝大会的实况，转播的周率是八五○千周，一○八○千周，七五○千周，一三二○千周，欢迎各界人民准时收听。
