Title: Tikhonov, Chulaki, and Others Arrive in Wuhan to a Warm Welcome; Yesterday, They Laid Wreaths at the Cemetery of Soviet Air Force Volunteer Martyrs and Toured Wuhan City

Author:

Date: 1952-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The head of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, Tikhonov, and delegation members Grusenko and Yefimov, the leader of the Soviet art troupe, Chulaki, and troupe members Mikhailov, Halima Nasyrova, Nechayev, Ulanova, Kondratov, Kogan, Chitoshiwili, Metnik, and the troupe's administrative staff member Bubenin arrived in Wuhan on the afternoon of the 15th. Accompanying the Soviet guests were Rong Gaotang, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, He Qifang, member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literature and Art Circles and director of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Chen Huangmei, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, and musician Ma Ke, among others. Those who went to the airport and the No. 4 Wharf in Hankou to welcome the Soviet guests included Zhao Yimin and Chen Mingshu, Vice Presidents of the Central and Southern Branch and the Wuhan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as representatives from all walks of life in the Central and Southern District, Wuhan City, and Hubei Province, totaling three thousand people. As the Soviet guests entered the city from the airport, they were warmly cheered by tens of thousands of citizens and staff members of various organizations who had gathered along the way. At 7 p.m. that evening, the Central and Southern Branch and the Wuhan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association hosted a banquet for the Soviet guests. Li Xiannian, Vice President of the Central and Southern Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Tikhonov, head of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, delivered speeches at the meeting. A welcome evening party was held after the banquet.

At 12:00 noon on November 16, Tikhonov, Chulaki, and others went to the Wuhan International Cemetery to pay their respects to the fifteen Soviet Air Force volunteer martyrs who heroically sacrificed their lives in 1938 while fighting against the Japanese invaders to help China resist the war. Amidst mournful music, Tikhonov, Chulaki, and Mikhailov laid wreaths at the graves of the Soviet martyrs. Grusenko and Yefimov, members of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, and Halima Nasyrova, Nechayev, Ulanova, Kondratov, Kogan, Chitoshiwili, Metnik, and Bubenin, members of the Soviet art troupe, also laid flowers at the graves of the Soviet martyrs. Accompanying them were Chen Huangmei, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, He Qifang, member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literature and Art Circles and director of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Rong Gaotang, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Zeng Ping, Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Central and Southern and Wuhan Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations.

Tikhonov, Grusenko, Yefimov, and others visited the Central and Southern District Land Reform Exhibition in Wuhan on the 16th. During the visit, Tikhonov and others inquired in detail about the changes in various aspects of the peasants in the Central and Southern District before and after the land reform, and Yefimov took notes.

The Soviet art troupe toured the main urban areas of Hankou. After overlooking the Yangtze River at the No. 4 Wharf in Hankou, they also visited the cadre cultural club and the northwest suburbs of Hankou. They were warmly welcomed by the masses when they visited the cadre cultural club.

### 吉洪诺夫和楚拉基等抵武汉受到热烈欢迎  昨向苏联空军志愿队烈士墓献花并在武汉市区进行参观

1952-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    吉洪诺夫和楚拉基等抵武汉受到热烈欢迎
    昨向苏联空军志愿队烈士墓献花并在武汉市区进行参观
    据新华社讯：苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫、团员格鲁森科和叶菲莫夫，苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基、团员米哈伊洛夫、哈利马·纳赛罗娃、聂恰耶夫、乌兰诺娃、康德拉托夫、柯岗、契托希维利、梅特尼克和该团行政工作人员布别宁等于十五日下午到达武汉市。陪同苏联嘉宾前往的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记荣高棠，中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、中苏友好协会理事何其芳，中南军政委员会文化部副部长陈荒煤，音乐家马可等人。前往机场和汉口第四码头欢迎苏联嘉宾的有：中苏友好协会中南总分会、武汉市分会副会长赵毅敏和陈铭枢以及中南区、武汉市和湖北省各界代表三千余人。苏联嘉宾由机场进入市内时，受到沿途聚集着的数万市民和机关工作人员的热烈欢呼。当晚七时，中苏友好协会中南总分会和武汉市分会欢宴苏联嘉宾。中苏友好协会中南总分会副会长李先念和苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫先后在会上致词。宴会后并举行了欢迎晚会。
    吉洪诺夫和楚拉基等于十一月十六日中午十二时到武汉市万国公墓凭吊在一九三八年为帮助中国抗战而与日本侵略者作战中英勇牺牲的十五位苏联空军志愿队烈士。在哀乐声中，吉洪诺夫、楚拉基、米哈伊洛夫三人向苏军烈士墓献花圈，苏联文化工作者代表团团员格鲁森科和叶菲莫夫，苏联艺术工作团团员哈利马·纳赛罗娃、聂恰耶夫、乌兰诺娃、康德拉托夫、柯岗、契托希维利、梅特尼克和该团行政工作人员布别宁也分别向苏军烈士墓献鲜花。陪同献花的有中南军政委员会文化部副部长陈荒煤，中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、中苏友好协会总会理事何其芳，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记荣高棠，北京市中苏友好协会总干事曾平，和中南、武汉中苏友好协会副会长赵毅敏等。
    吉洪诺夫、格鲁森科、叶菲莫夫等三人十六日在武汉市参观了中南区土地改革展览会。吉洪诺夫等在参观中很详尽地询问了中南区农民土地改革前后各方面的变化，叶菲莫夫并作了笔记。
    苏联艺术工作团游历了汉口的主要市区；他们在汉口第四码头眺望长江之后，还参观了干部文化俱乐部和汉口的西北郊区。他们在参观干部文化俱乐部时，受到群众的热烈欢迎。
