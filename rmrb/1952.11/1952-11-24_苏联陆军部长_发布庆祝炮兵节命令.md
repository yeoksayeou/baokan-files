Title: Soviet Army Minister Issues Order Celebrating Artillery Day
Author:
Date: 1952-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 23rd: TASS, Moscow, November 23rd: The Minister of the Soviet Army issued the following order on November 23, 1952:

Comrades artillerymen and workers of the artillery industry!
Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers! Comrades lieutenants, colonels, and generals of the Army!

Today, our Army and the entire Soviet people celebrate Artillery Day together.

On this Artillery Day, I extend my greetings and congratulations to you.

I wish all artillerymen and artillery industry workers new achievements in mastering modern technology and armaments, and in further developing and perfecting our glorious artillery.

To celebrate Artillery Day, I order:

Today, November 23, 1952, a twenty-gun salute will be fired in our homeland's capital, Moscow, and in the capitals of each of the republics.

Long live the Soviet artillery and artillery industry workers!
Long live the heroic Soviet people and their valiant Army!
Long live the Soviet government!
Long live the organizer and inspirer of all our victories—the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!
Glory to the wise leader and mentor, the brilliant commander—the great Stalin!

Minister of the Soviet Army, Marshal of the Soviet Union A. Vasilevsky (signed)

### 苏联陆军部长  发布庆祝炮兵节命令

1952-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联陆军部长
    发布庆祝炮兵节命令
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日电：苏联陆军部部长一九五二年十一月二十三日发布命令如下：
    炮兵和炮兵工业的工作人员同志们！
    陆军战士和士官同志们！陆军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天我们的陆军和全体苏联人民一起庆祝炮兵节。
    值此炮兵节，我向你们致敬和祝贺。
    祝全体炮兵和炮兵工业工作人员在掌握现代技术和军备上、在使我们光荣的炮兵进一步发展和完美上获得新的成就。
    为了庆祝炮兵节，我命令：
    今天，一九五二年十一月二十三日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科和各加盟共和国首都各鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏联炮兵和炮兵工业工作人员万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们英勇的陆军万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们一切胜利的组织者和鼓舞者——苏联共产党万岁！
    英明的领袖和导师、天才的统帅——伟大的斯大林光荣！
    苏联陆军部长、苏联元帅  阿·华西列夫斯基（签字）
