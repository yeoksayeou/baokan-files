Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Hosts Evening Party to Entertain Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, etc. Soviet Film Artists Delegation Departs for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-11-05
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters held an evening party at 8:00 PM on the 4th to entertain the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation headed by Tikhonov, the Soviet Art Workers Troupe led by Churaki, and all members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble headed by Alexandrov.

    Invited to attend the evening party were personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China and many Soviet experts assisting our country in its construction work.

    Attending the evening party were Wu Yuzhang and Guo Moruo, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, directors and staff in Beijing, and representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations of major administrative regions. Also attending the evening party were heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, and over a thousand people from cultural circles in the capital.

    The reception was jointly performed by the Central Song and Dance Troupe, the Affiliated Song and Dance Theater of the Central Academy of Drama, and the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera. The evening's program included the chorus "Moscow-Beijing," "Sino-Soviet Friendship Song," "People of the World Are of One Heart," "Ode to Mao Zedong," "In Praise of Stalin," folk drama music "Joyful Dance," the dance "Great Unity Dance of the Chinese Nation," and Peking Opera excerpts from "Killing the Fisherman" and "Havoc in Heaven."

    The evening party proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: The entire Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation and Soviet Art Workers Troupe, accompanied by literary and art figures from the capital, including Ai Qing, Hong Shen, Cao Yu, Dai Ailian, Wu Xue, Yuan Shuipai, and Qu Xixian, visited the Summer Palace, a scenic spot in Beijing, on the afternoon of the 4th. The Soviet friends were warmly applauded by the visiting crowds wherever they went. The Soviet friends highly praised the ancient architectural art of the Hall of Dispelling Clouds in the Summer Palace and the beautiful scenery of Longevity Hill and Kunming Lake.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 4th: The Soviet Film Artists Delegation, consisting of seven members led by Fedorov, which has been invited to China to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," departed from Moscow by plane on the 3rd for Beijing.

    Also traveling with them were Soviet People's Artist Ulanova, Honored Artist of Russia Kondratov, and pianist Zaitseva. The three of them are members of the Soviet Art Workers Troupe who were invited to China to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" and have already arrived in Beijing. Ge Baoquan, Charge d'Affaires of our embassy in the Soviet Union, and people from the Soviet film and art circles saw them off at the airport.

### 中苏友好协会总会举行晚会  招待苏联文化工作者代表团等  苏联电影艺术工作者代表团启程来北京

1952-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会举行晚会
    招待苏联文化工作者代表团等
    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团启程来北京
    【新华社四日讯】中苏友好协会总会四日下午八时举行晚会，招待以吉洪诺夫为首的苏联文化工作者代表团、由楚拉基领导的苏联艺术工作团和以亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团全体团员。
    应邀出席晚会的，有苏联驻华大使馆人员，帮助我国进行建设工作的苏联专家多人。
    出席晚会的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、郭沫若，总干事钱俊瑞，在京理事、干事以及各大行政区中苏友好协会来京代表。出席晚会的，还有中央人民政府各部门首长、各人民团体负责人和首都文化界人士一千多人。
    招待晚会由中央歌舞团、中央戏剧学院附属歌舞剧院和中国戏曲研究院联合演出。晚会节目有大合唱“莫斯科——北京”、“中苏友好歌”、“全世界人民心一条”、“歌唱毛泽东”、“斯大林颂”，民间戏剧音乐“欢喜舞曲”，舞蹈“中华民族大团结舞”，京剧“打渔杀家”、“闹天宫”。
    晚会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    【新华社四日讯】苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团全体人员，四日下午由首都文学艺术界人士艾青、洪深、曹禺、戴爱莲、吴雪、袁水拍、瞿希贤等陪同，游览了北京名胜颐和园。苏联友人每到一个地方，都受到游览群众的热烈鼓掌欢迎。苏联友人对颐和园中排云殿的古代建筑艺术和万寿山、昆明湖的美丽风光，赞扬备至。
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】应邀前来我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团一行七人，在团长费道罗夫率领下，于三日乘飞机离莫斯科飞往北京。
    同行者还有苏联人民演员乌兰诺娃，俄罗斯功勋演员康德拉托夫和钢琴家查伊采娃。他们三人是应邀前来我国参加“中苏友好月”并已抵北京的苏联艺术工作团的团员。到机场送行的有我国驻苏联大使馆代办戈宝权和苏联电影界和艺术界人士。
