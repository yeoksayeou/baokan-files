Title: Alexandrov and Members of the Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble Arrive in Tianjin. Tianjin Invites Soviet Guests to Attend the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Garden Party.
Author:
Date: 1952-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The entire membership of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by B.A. Alexandrov, arrived in Tianjin at 4:20 PM on the 15th. Those who greeted them at the station included Huang Songling, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Tianjin Branch, and more than 3,000 representatives from various agencies, organizations, military units, schools, and other institutions. Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Matveev, and others were also present at the station to welcome them.

People from all walks of life in Tianjin held a grand welcoming ceremony at the station for the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble. That evening, Huang Songling, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Tianjin Branch, hosted a grand reception to entertain all members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble.

The head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, B.A. Alexandrov, and all members of the ensemble were invited to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" garden party held in Ningyuan by the Tianjin Municipal Government at noon on the 16th. Those attending the gathering included Huang Songling, Vice President of the Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as representatives from Tianjin municipal agencies, military units, people's organizations, and writers Wang Lin, singer Yu Yixuan, industrial labor models Zhang Shuyun and Liu Yinfu, combat hero Niu Jian, and more than 18,000 others.

Soviet Consul General in Tianjin Matveev, and Guo Like, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Tianjin, were also invited to attend.

When the entire song and dance ensemble traveled by car to Ningyuan, they were cheered by citizens along the way. The ensemble leader and members entered Ningyuan in two groups, participating in the mass celebration and constantly surrounded and cheered by the crowds. All members of the song and dance ensemble watched Chinese folk art performances at the garden party.

### 亚历山大罗夫及红旗歌舞团团员抵天津  天津市邀请苏联嘉宾参加“中苏友好月”游园联欢大会

1952-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    亚历山大罗夫及红旗歌舞团团员抵天津
    天津市邀请苏联嘉宾参加“中苏友好月”游园联欢大会
    据新华社讯：以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团全体团员，在十五日下午四时二十分到达天津。到车站欢迎的有：中苏友好协会天津市分会副会长黄松龄等，以及各机关、团体、部队、学校等单位的代表三千多人。到车站欢迎的还有苏联驻天津总领事马铁夫等。
    天津市各界人民在车站举行了欢迎苏军红旗歌舞团的盛大欢迎会。当晚，中苏友好协会天津市分会副会长黄松龄举行盛大酒会，招待苏军红旗歌舞团全体团员。
    苏军红旗歌舞团团长鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫和全体团员，在十六日中午应邀参加了天津市在宁园举行的“中苏友好月”游园联欢大会。出席大会的有：天津市中苏友好协会副会长黄松龄，以及天津市机关、部队人民团体的代表和作家王林，歌唱家喻宜萱，工业劳动模范张淑云、刘荫福，战斗英雄牛健等一万八千多人。
    苏联驻津总领事马铁夫、苏联对外文化协会驻天津代表郭力克等也应邀参加。
    歌舞团全体人员乘汽车前往宁园时，受到沿途市民的欢呼。歌舞团团长和团员们分作两路进入宁园，参加了群众的联欢大会，始终为人群簇拥着、欢呼着。歌舞团全体人员在游园大会中观摩了中国民族艺术表演。
