Title: Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation and Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble Arrive in Manchouli
Author:
Date: 1952-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Manchouli, October 31st: The Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation headed by Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee and famous poet, and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, a total of 290 people (including famous actress Galina Ulanova and three others who will travel to Beijing by plane from Moscow), arrived in Manchouli, our border city, at 11:00 a.m. on the 31st, and were warmly welcomed by the local people. They were invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities in our country. The Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation includes famous Soviet scientists, musicians, singers, and famous ballet dancers. The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble consists of 260 people.

The welcoming delegation, consisting of Yan Baohang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Song Zhidi, Director of the Literature and Art Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, Zou Difan, Director of the Liaison Department of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and Cao Yu, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, led by Liu Guanyi, arrived in Manchouli from Beijing on the 29th to welcome the delegation.

In addition to the above-mentioned welcoming delegation, those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Cao Yuanhui, Director of the Liaison Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government in Manchouli, Su Lin, President of the Manchouli Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Mayor of the Manchouli Municipal People's Government, He Guang, Deputy Secretary of the Manchouli Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Guo Jifang, Commander of the Manchouli Garrison, and more than 1,500 workers, Chinese People's Liberation Army troops, government cadres, students, and citizens. Soviet Consul in Manchouli, Malayev, also came to welcome them.

The station was fluttering with colorful flags, and huge portraits of Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin were hung on the platform. The national flags of China and the Soviet Union were flying in the wind. Huge red banners read: "Long Live the Eternal and Profound Friendship Between the People of China and the Soviet Union" and "Sino-Soviet Friendship is a Powerful Guarantee of Peace in the Far East and the World." The train entered the station amidst music, waist drum beats, and warm applause. After the head of the Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation, Tikhonov, and the members of the delegation got off the train, a team of Young Pioneers presented them with big red flowers. At this time, the jubilant crowd enthusiastically and for a long time cheered "Stalin," "Mao Zedong," and "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship."

At the welcoming ceremony held in front of the station, Liu Guanyi, head of the welcoming delegation, and Su Lin, Mayor of the Manchouli Municipal People's Government, delivered welcoming speeches successively. Liu Guanyi said: Comrades, you have arrived in our country when the Chinese people are jubilantly welcoming the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, and when the Chinese people are preparing to hold "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" throughout the country. Your arrival will further consolidate and develop the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and will be conducive to the cause of defending peace in the Far East and the world.

In his reply, delegation leader Tikhonov expressed his gratitude to the Chinese people for their warm and fraternal welcome. Tikhonov said: Today is a great and happy day for us, because we have set foot on the land of great China. At the same time, we are honored to represent the Soviet people in participating in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" and celebrating this festival with the Chinese people. As representatives of the Soviet people coming to China and seeing the achievements of the Chinese people in all aspects, we feel particularly honored. Churaki, Deputy Chairman of the Art Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, then delivered a speech saying: We have come to China to consolidate the Sino-Soviet friendship that millions of Soviet people care about. Let us shout long live this friendship. Tikhonov and Churaki's passionate speeches won stormy applause.

After a short rest here, the delegation will take a special train to Beijing.

### 苏联艺术科学工作者代表团和苏军红旗歌舞团抵满洲里

1952-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

     苏联艺术科学工作者代表团和苏军红旗歌舞团抵满洲里
    【新华社满洲里三十一日电】以苏联拥护和平委员会主席、名诗人吉洪诺夫为首的苏联艺术科学工作者代表团和苏军红旗歌舞团一行二百九十人（名演员乌兰诺娃等三人另由莫斯科乘飞机赴北京），在三十一日上午十一时到达我国的边境城市满洲里，受到当地人民的热烈欢迎。他们是应中苏友好协会总会的邀请，前来我国参加“中苏友好月”活动的。苏联艺术科学工作者代表团包括苏联的著名科学家、音乐家、歌唱家和巴蕾舞名演员，苏军红旗歌舞团则由二百六十人组成。
    由中苏友好协会总会副总干事阎宝航、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文艺处处长宋之的、中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局联络处处长邹荻帆和中华全国文学艺术界联合会常务委员曹禺组成的欢迎团，在团长刘贯一率领下，于二十九日自北京到达满洲里迎候。
    苏联代表团抵达时，前往车站欢迎的除上述欢迎团人员外，还有中央人民政府外交部驻满洲里联络处处长曹远辉，满洲里市中苏友好协会会长、满洲里市人民政府市长苏林、中共满洲里市委副书记贺光、满洲里市警备司令郭济芳以及工人、中国人民解放军驻军、机关干部、学生和市民共一千五百多人。前往欢迎的还有苏联驻满洲里领事马列耶夫。
    车站上飘扬着各色锦旗，月台上挂着毛主席和斯大林大元帅的巨幅画像，中苏两国国旗迎风招展。巨幅的红布标语写着：“中苏两国人民永久深厚的友谊万岁”和“中苏友好是远东和世界和平的有力保证”。列车在音乐声、腰鼓声和热烈的掌声中驶入车站。苏联艺术科学工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫和团员们下车后，一队少年儿童队员为他们戴上了大红花。这时，欢腾的人群热烈地长久地欢呼“斯大林”、“毛泽东”和“中苏友好万岁”。
    在车站前举行的欢迎会上，欢迎团团长刘贯一和满洲里市人民政府市长苏林，先后致欢迎词。刘贯一说：诸位同志是在中国人民欢欣鼓舞地迎接伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年的时候、是在中国人民准备在全国范围内举行“中苏友好月”的时候到达我国的。诸位的到来，将进一步巩固和发展中苏两国人民之间的伟大友谊，有利于保卫远东与世界和平的事业。
    吉洪诺夫团长在答词中对中国人民的热烈的兄弟般的欢迎表示感谢。吉洪诺夫说：今天对我们是一个伟大幸福的日子，因为我们踏上了伟大的中国的国土。同时我们以代表苏联人民来参加“中苏友好月”和中国人民共同庆祝这一节日感到荣幸。作为苏联人民的代表到中国来，看到中国人民各方面的成就，我们特别感到光荣。苏联部长会议艺术委员会副主席楚拉基接着致词说：我们到中国来，就是为了巩固千百万苏联人民所关怀的中苏友谊，让我们来为这种友谊高呼万岁。吉洪诺夫和楚拉基的热情洋溢的演说博得暴风雨般的掌声。
    代表团在此间稍事休息后，即趁专车前往北京。
