Title: Government Administration Council Holds 160th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-11-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The 160th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 28th. The meeting approved the Provisional Regulations on the Organization of People's Militia of the People's Republic of China. Before the adoption of these provisional regulations, Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, gave an explanation. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases, and cases of appointment and removal to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一六零次会议

1952-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六零次会议
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十次政务会议在二十八日下午举行。会议通过了中华人民共和国民兵组织暂行条例。在通过这一暂行条例之前，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会聂荣臻代总参谋长曾作了说明。会议并通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
