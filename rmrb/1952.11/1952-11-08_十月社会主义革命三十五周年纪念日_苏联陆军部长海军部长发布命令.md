Title: Thirty-Fifth Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution: Orders Issued by the Soviet Minister of the Army and Minister of the Navy
Author:
Date: 1952-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th, Moscow Telegraph Agency: Soviet Minister of the Army issued the following order on November 7, 1952:

	Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers of the Army! Comrades lieutenants, colonels, and generals of the Army!

	Today, the Soviet Army, together with all the Soviet people, celebrates the thirty-fifth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

	Thirty-five years ago, under the leadership of the great Lenin-Stalin Party, the working people of our country overthrew the oppression of landlords and capitalists and established Soviet power. Since then, the Soviet people have built socialism, built their motherland into a strong country, and defended the freedom and independence of the motherland in fierce wars against enemies.

	This year, our country is celebrating the great October Revolution with new and remarkable achievements in all aspects of the national economy and culture. Inspired by the historic resolutions of the 19th Party Congress and the wise instructions of our great leader and teacher Comrade Stalin, the Soviet people are using all their strength and energy to complete and overfulfill the Stalin Fifth Five-Year Plan and are confidently advancing along the road to communism.

	The Soviet government is implementing magnificent communist construction plans and steadfastly pursuing a policy of peace and friendship with all nations.

	In contrast to the Soviet Union's peace-loving policy, which is supported by various people's democratic countries, is the aggressive policy pursued by American and British imperialism, which is preparing for a new world war.

	The Soviet people are vigilantly watching the conspiracies of the warmongers, constantly consolidating the national defense of the Soviet state, and strengthening our motherland's preparedness to give any aggressor a crushing blow at any time.

	Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers! Comrades lieutenants, colonels, and generals! On the occasion of the thirty-fifth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, I salute you and congratulate you. I wish you further achievements in your service and studies. Constantly improve your military and political knowledge, master new combat techniques and weapons, strictly abide by army rules and iron discipline; never relax your vigilance, and strengthen military preparedness in every way.

	To celebrate this National Day, I order: Today, November 7th, in our capital Moscow, in the capitals of the Union Republics, in Kaliningrad, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the four hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, let twenty salvoes be fired.

	Long live our powerful motherland!

	Long live the great Soviet people and the Soviet Army!
	
	Long live the Soviet government!

	Long live the Communist Party of theSoviet Union - the inspirer and organizer of all our victories!

	Glory to our dear leader, teacher, and commander - the great Stalin!

	Minister of the Army of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union
	
	Vasilevsky (signed)

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th, Moscow Telegraph Agency: The Soviet Minister of the Navy issued the following order on November 7, 1952:

	Comrades sailors and non-commissioned officers of the Navy! Comrades lieutenants, colonels, and generals of the Navy!

	Today, the Soviet Navy, together with all the Soviet people, celebrates the thirty-fifth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

	Our people are welcoming the glorious October Festival with remarkable achievements in economic and cultural construction. Armed with the historic resolutions of the 19th Party Congress and inspired by Comrade Stalin's doctrine on the road from socialism to communism, the Soviet people are confidently advancing towards their great goal—building a communist society in our country.

	All the achievements of the people of all nationalities in our country are attributed to their dear Communist Party and their own wise leader and teacher, Comrade Stalin.

	American and British warmongers are preparing a new world war.

	In sharp contrast to the war instigators is the consistent and resolute struggle of the Soviet state for peace and international cooperation.

	The Soviet Navy soldiers, carrying out the instructions of the Party, the government, and Comrade Stalin, guard the maritime borders and national interests of the socialist motherland and welcome the great October Festival with new achievements in combat and political training.

	The task of the naval personnel is to: continuously improve the combat readiness of ships and troops, unswervingly learn and master modern combat techniques, train themselves in naval exercises, enable more people to achieve excellent results in combat and political training, and further improve the navy's efficiency, vigilance, discipline, and organization.

	Comrades sailors and non-commissioned officers! Comrades lieutenants, colonels, and generals! On the occasion of the thirty-fifth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, I salute you and congratulate you. I wish you new achievements in combat and political training.

	Long live the heroic Soviet people and their navy!

	Long live our dear Soviet government!

	Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union—the organizer and inspirer of all our victories!

	Glory to our dear leader, teacher, and commander, Comrade Stalin!

	Minister of the Navy of the Soviet Union, Vice Admiral

	N. G. Kuznetsov

### 十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念日  苏联陆军部长海军部长发布命令

1952-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念日
    苏联陆军部长海军部长发布命令
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联陆军部部长于一九五二年十一月七日发布命令如下：
    陆军战士与士官同志们！陆军尉官、校官与将军同志们！
    今天，苏联陆军与全体苏联人民一道庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年。
    三十五年以前，我国劳动人民在伟大的列宁—斯大林党的领导下，推翻了地主和资本家的压迫，建立了苏维埃政权。从那时以来，苏维埃人民建设了社会主义，把自己的祖国建成一个强盛的国家，在反对敌人的激烈战争中保卫了祖国的自由和独立。
    今年我国以国民经济和文化各方面的新的显著成就来庆祝伟大的十月革命节。苏联人民在第十九次党代表大会的具有历史意义的决议的鼓舞下，在自己伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志英明指示的鼓舞下，正在以自己全部的力量和精力来完成并超额完成斯大林第五个五年计划，并且满怀信心地沿着走向共产主义的道路前进。
    苏联政府执行着宏伟的共产主义建设计划，坚持不渝地执行着和平及与各国人民友好的政策。
    与得到各人民民主国家支持的苏联爱好和平的政策相对的，是正在准备新世界大战的，美英帝国主义所执行的侵略政策。
    苏联人民警觉地注视着战争贩子的阴谋，不断地巩固苏维埃国家的国防力量，加强我们祖国为了随时给予任何侵略者以粉碎性打击的准备。
    战士与士官同志们！尉官、校官与将军同志们！值此伟大十月社会主义革命三十五周年之际，我向你们致敬并祝贺你们。祝你们在服务和学习中获得进一步的成绩。不断地提高你们的军事和政治知识，掌握新的战斗技术和武器，严格遵守陆军规则和铁的军纪；决不要放松警惕性，用一切方法加强军事准备。
    为了庆祝这个国庆日，我命令：今天，十一月七日，在我国首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴，以及在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔、敖德萨四个英雄城市各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    我们强大的祖国万岁！
    伟大的苏联人民和苏联陆军万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联共产党——我们一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者万岁！
    我们亲爱的领袖、导师和统帅——伟大的斯大林光荣！
    苏联陆军部部长、苏联元帅
    华西列夫斯基（签字）
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联海军部部长于一九五二年十一月七日发布命令如下：
    海军战士与士官同志们！海军尉官、校官与将军同志们！
    今天，苏联海军和全体苏联人民一道庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年。
    我国人民以经济和文化建设各方面的显著成就来迎接光荣的十月节。苏联人民在第十九次党代表大会的具有历史意义的决议的武装下，在斯大林同志关于从社会主义过渡到共产主义的道路的学说的鼓舞下，满怀信心地向自己的伟大目标——在我国建立共产主义社会——前进。
    我国各族人民的一切成就都要归功于他们亲爱的共产党、他们自己的英明领袖与导师斯大林同志。
    美英战争贩子正在准备新的世界大战。
    与战争挑拨者针锋相对的是苏维埃国家争取和平与国际合作的一贯的坚决的斗争。
    苏联海军战士们执行党、政府和斯大林同志的指示，警卫着社会主义祖国的海上边境和国家利益，并以在战斗和政治训练方面的新成就来迎接伟大的十月节。
    海军人员的任务是：不断提高舰只和部队的战斗准备，坚持不渝地学习掌握现代战斗技术，在海军演习中锻炼自己，使更多的人在战斗和政治训练方面取得优秀的成绩，进一步提高海军的效率、警惕性、纪律性和组织性。
    海军战士与士官同志们！海军尉官、校官与将军同志们！值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年之际，我向你们致敬并祝贺你们。祝你们在战斗训练和政治训练方面获得新的成就。
    英雄的苏联人民和他们的海军万岁！
    我们亲爱的苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联共产党——我们一切胜利的组织者和鼓舞者万岁！
    我们亲爱的领袖、导师和统帅斯大林同志光荣！
    苏联海军部部长、海军中将
    恩·格·库兹涅佐夫
