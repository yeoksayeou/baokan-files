Our Great Motherland
Shi Duoshun, a signal foreman of the Electrical Engineering Section of the Shenyang Railway Bureau, created a method for adjusting color light signals, which increased the signal's visibility from four or five hundred meters to one thousand six hundred meters. This invention provides a strong guarantee for fully loaded, over-axle transport. This is Shi Duoshun inspecting the signals.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    沈阳铁路分局电务段信号工长石多顺创造了色灯信号机调整方法，使信号的显示能力从四、五百公尺增加到一千六百公尺。这个发明给满载超轴运动提供了有力的保证。这是石多顺在检查信号的情形。
