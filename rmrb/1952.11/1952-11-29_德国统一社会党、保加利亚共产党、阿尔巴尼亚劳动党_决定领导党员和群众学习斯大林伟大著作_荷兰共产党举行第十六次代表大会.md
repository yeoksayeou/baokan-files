Title: Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Bulgarian Communist Party, Albanian Party of Labor Decide to Lead Party Members and Masses in Studying Stalin's Great Works; Communist Party of the Netherlands Holds Sixteenth Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-11-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Bulgarian Communist Party, and the Albanian Party of Labor have decided to launch a campaign to lead party members and the masses in studying Stalin's great work, "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR," and the documents of the 19th Congress. of Soviet Communist party
    The Greater Berlin Party Organization of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany held a meeting of activists in late October. Wilhelm Pieck and Otto Grotewohl, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Walter Ulbricht, General Secretary of the Central Committee, attended the meeting. Ulbricht made a report on the 19th Congress of the CPSU, in which he pointed out: In order to master the art of achieving victory and in order to make our party a party like the CPSU, our Socialist Unity Party of Germany must study the documents of this congress. Pieck and Grotewohl also made reports on the 19th Congress of the CPSU to the Advanced Marxist Party School and the activist meetings of the party in Dresden and Leipzig, respectively. The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany has now passed a resolution on studying the documents of the 19th Congress of the CPSU.
    The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held a meeting on the 5th, at which Vulko Chervenkov, General Secretary of the Central Committee, made a report on the 19th Congress of the CPSU. The meeting adopted a resolution, pointing out that the documents of this congress and Stalin's new work are of particular importance. Therefore, the Bulgarian Communist Party must take them as the basis for all theoretical and practical work. The resolution also called on party organizations and mass organizations at all levels to launch a wave of studying these documents.
    The Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor recently held a plenary session at which it heard a report by Enver Hoxha, General Secretary of the Central Committee, on the resolutions of the 19th Congress of the CPSU. He emphasized that the historic resolutions and documents of the CPSU Congress and Stalin's new works are of great significance for all activities of the Albanian Party of Labor. The Central Committee decided to widely launch activities to study these documents throughout the party and among the working people.
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Hague dispatch: The Sixteenth Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands was held in Amsterdam from the 22nd to the 25th. More than 400 representatives from eleven provinces of the Netherlands attended the congress.
    The congress was opened by Wijgner, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands. Paul de Groot, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, made a lengthy report on the political situation and the work of the Party Central Committee. After discussion, the congress unanimously approved the political line and practical work of the Party Central Committee, adopted a new party program stipulating the party's current tasks, and elected a new Central Committee.
    In his closing speech, de Groot called on Dutch communists to actively fight for peace, the independence of the motherland, and the cause of the working class, and to learn from the example of Soviet communists in all aspects. He particularly emphasized the historical significance of Stalin's speech at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, which illuminates the path of struggle for fraternal parties in all countries, including the Communist Party of the Netherlands.

### 德国统一社会党、保加利亚共产党、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党  决定领导党员和群众学习斯大林伟大著作  荷兰共产党举行第十六次代表大会

1952-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党、保加利亚共产党、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党
    决定领导党员和群众学习斯大林伟大著作
    荷兰共产党举行第十六次代表大会
    【新华社二十八日讯】德国统一社会党、保加利亚共产党和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党决定展开运动，领导党员和群众学习斯大林的伟大著作“苏联社会主义经济问题”和苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的各项文件。
    德国统一社会党大柏林市党组织十月下旬曾举行活动分子会议。德国统一社会党中央委员会主席皮克和格罗提渥、中央委员会总书记乌布利希都参加了会议。乌布利希作了关于联共第十九次代表大会的报告，他指出：为了掌握取得胜利的艺术，为了使我们的党成为一个像联共那样的党，我们德国统一社会党必须学习这次代表大会的各项文件。皮克和格罗提渥并曾分别向马克思高级党校和德累斯顿、来比锡两地党的活动分子会议作了关于联共第十九次代表大会的报告。德国统一社会党中央委员会现已通过关于学习联共第十九次代表大会文件的决议。
    保加利亚共产党中央委员会五日举行了会议，中央委员会总书记契尔文科夫在会上作了关于联共第十九次代表大会的报告。会议通过决议，指出这次代表大会的各项文件和斯大林的新著具有特殊的重要意义，因此，保加利亚共产党必须把它们作为在全部理论和实际工作中的基础。决议并号召全党各级组织和群众组织展开学习这些文件的热潮。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会最近举行了全体会议，会上听取了中央委员会总书记霍查关于联共第十九次代表大会决议的报告。他强调指出：联共代表大会的历史性决议和文件以及斯大林的新著，对于阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的全部活动有着非常重大的意义。中央委员会决定在全党和劳动人民中广泛展开学习这些文件的活动。
    【新华社二十八日讯】海牙消息：荷兰共产党第十六次代表大会于二十二日至二十五日在阿姆斯特丹举行。参加大会的，有来自荷兰十一个省份的四百多个代表。
    大会由荷兰共产党中央委员会主席魏吉纳主持开幕。荷兰共产党中央委员会总书记格鲁特就政治形势和党中央委员会的工作，作了长篇报告。大会经过讨论后，一致批准了党中央委员会的政治方针和实际工作，通过了规定党的当前任务的新党纲，并且选出了新的中央委员会。
    格鲁特在闭幕会议上发表演说，号召荷兰共产党员为和平、祖国的独立和工人阶级的事业而积极斗争，并且在各方面学习苏联共产党员的榜样。他特别着重指出斯大林在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会上的演说的历史意义，这篇演说照耀着包括荷兰共产党在内的各国兄弟党的斗争道路。
