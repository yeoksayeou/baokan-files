Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party to Hold National Conference in Mid-December
Author:
Date: 1952-11-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 2nd (TASS dispatch): "Red Right" newspaper published the decision of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party regarding the convocation of the Party's National Conference in Prague from December 16th to 17th this year. The agenda of the conference is as follows:

    1. The Party's political and organizational work, and the draft of the new Party Constitution. The report will be given by Comrade Klement Gottwald.
    2. Supplementary election to fill vacancies in the members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party.

The decision of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party stipulated the proportion of representatives and the procedures for their election.

### 捷共定于十二月中旬举行全国代表会议

1952-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共定于十二月中旬举行全国代表会议
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二日讯：“红色权利报”刊载捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团关于在今年十二月十六日到十七日在布拉格召开党的全国代表会议的决定。会议的议程如下：
    一、党的政治工作和组织工作以及新党章草案。报告人是克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志。
    二、补选捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会委员和候补委员的缺额。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团的决定规定了代表比例和代表的选举程序。
    “红色权利报”刊载了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团所提出的捷克斯洛伐克共产党党章草案。捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团建议各地方党委会组织关于党章草案的广泛讨论。
