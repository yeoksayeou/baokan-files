Date: 1952-11-26

    Our Great Motherland
    The repair team of the state-owned Lutai Farm has successfully created a spiral grain conveyor, which can transport approximately 32,000 to 50,000 jin of rice per hour, saving a great deal of manpower and enabling the harvested rice to be stored in granaries earlier. This is a scene of the farm staff installing the grain conveyor.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营芦台农场修理队创制螺旋输谷器成功，每小时可输送稻谷三万二千斤到五万斤左右，节省了许多人力，使得丰收后的稻谷能够提前入囤。这是该场工作人员正在安装输谷器的情形。
