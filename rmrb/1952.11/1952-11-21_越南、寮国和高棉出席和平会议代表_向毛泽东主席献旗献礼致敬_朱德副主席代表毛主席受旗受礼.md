Title: Vietnamese, Laotian, and Cambodian Delegates to the Peace Conference Present Flags and Gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong as a Tribute; Vice Chairman Zhu De Accepts the Flags and Gifts on Behalf of Chairman Mao

Author:

Date: 1952-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th: The head of the Vietnamese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Le Dinh Tham, the deputy head, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, the head of the Laotian delegation, Nohak Phoumsavan, and the Cambodian representative, Kao Sang, who recently arrived in Beijing due to travel delays, presented flags and gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government at 4:00 PM on the 20th to pay their respects.

Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government accepted the flags and gifts on behalf of Chairman Mao.

Present at the event were Liu GuanYi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government.

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th: The Cambodian representative to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Kao Sang, a member of the Executive Committee of the Khmer Issarak Front, arrived in Beijing a few days ago due to travel delays. Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General, and many others welcomed him at the station. (Attached picture)
	Vietnamese, Laotian, and Cambodian peace delegates present flags and gifts to Chairman Mao. Vice Chairman Zhu De (third from the left) accepts the flags and gifts on behalf of Chairman Mao. From the left, the first person is Le Dinh Tham, head of the Vietnamese delegation, the fourth is Kao Sang, the Cambodian representative, the fifth is Nohak Phoumsavan, head of the Laotian delegation, and the sixth is Nguyen Xuan Thuy, deputy head of the Vietnamese delegation.

### 越南、寮国和高棉出席和平会议代表  向毛泽东主席献旗献礼致敬  朱德副主席代表毛主席受旗受礼

1952-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    越南、寮国和高棉出席和平会议代表
    向毛泽东主席献旗献礼致敬
    朱德副主席代表毛主席受旗受礼
    【新华社二十日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的越南代表团团长黎廷探、副团长阮春水，寮国代表团团长诺哈·冯沙万，和因旅途迟滞最近抵达北京的高棉代表高桑，在二十日下午四时向我中央人民政府毛泽东主席献旗献礼致敬。
    中央人民政府朱德副主席代表毛主席受旗受礼。
    在场的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清。
    【新华社二十日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的高棉代表、高棉棉东自由民主统一战线执行委员会委员高桑，因旅途耽搁，在日前抵达北京。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，副秘书长吴茂荪等多人曾到车站欢迎。（附图片）   
     越南、寮国、高棉三国和平代表向毛主席献旗献礼。朱德副主席（左第三人）代表毛主席受旗受礼。左起第一人为越南代表团团长黎廷探、第四人为高棉代表高桑、第五人为寮国代表团团长诺哈·冯沙万、第六人为越南代表团副团长阮春水。
    新华社记者齐观山摄
