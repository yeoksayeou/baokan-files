Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Today's Broadcast: Live Coverage of Beijing Women's Welcome Meeting for Soviet Women Delegates

Author:
Date: 1952-11-09
Page: 1

Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Today's Broadcast
Live Coverage of Beijing Women's Welcome Meeting for Soviet Women Delegates

[This newspaper dispatch] Beijing – The women from all circles in Beijing will hold a grand meeting at 1:00 PM today (9th) to welcome the female delegates from the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, the Soviet Art Troupe, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation. Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the meeting live from 12:45 PM. The frequencies are 850 kilocycles and 1080 kilocycles.

### 北京人民广播电台今日广播  北京妇女欢迎苏联女代表大会实况

1952-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京人民广播电台今日广播
    北京妇女欢迎苏联女代表大会实况
    【本报讯】北京市各界妇女今（九）日下午一时举行大会，欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团、苏军红旗歌舞团和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团的女代表们。北京人民广播电台从下午十二时四十五分起转播大会实况。周率是八五○千周、一○八○千周。
