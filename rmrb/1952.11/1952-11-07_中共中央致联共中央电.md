Title: CPC Central Committee Telegram to CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 1
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:

	On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal greetings to Comrade Stalin, all comrades of the Central Commitee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people, and hails the great achievements of the Soviet Union's socialist construction over the past thirty-five years.

	The recently convened 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union marks a new stage in the development of the Soviet Union. The 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, as well as Comrade Stalin's new works published on the eve of the congress and his speech at the congress, give infinite encouragement to the great struggle of communists and working people all over the world for peace, democracy and socialism.

	We wish the Communist Party of the Soviet Union even more brilliant victories in leading the Soviet people to realize the Fifth Five-Year Plan and advance towards communism!

	We wish the Communist Party of the Soviet Union even greater victories in leading the Soviet people and the working people of the world in the cause of striving for lasting world peace!

	We wish the unbreakable friendly alliance between the Chinese and Soviet peoples to be constantly consolidated and developed!

	Long live Comrade Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world!

					The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
					November 5, 1952, in Beijing

### 中共中央致联共中央电

1952-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致联共中央电苏联共产党中央委员会：
    值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向斯大林同志、苏联共产党中央委员会全体同志和苏联人民致热烈的兄弟般的祝贺，并为三十五年来苏联社会主义建设的伟大成就而欢呼。
    新近召开的苏联共产党第十九次代表大会，标志着苏联发展的新阶段。苏联共产党第十九次代表大会以及斯大林同志在大会前夜发表的新著作和他在大会上的演说，给全世界共产主义者和全世界劳动人民争取和平、民主和社会主义的伟大斗争以无限的鼓舞。
    祝苏联共产党在领导苏联人民实现向共产主义前进的第五个五年计划的工作中获得更加辉煌的胜利！
    祝苏联共产党在领导苏联人民和全世界劳动人民争取世界持久和平的事业中获得更大的胜利！
    祝中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友好同盟不断地巩固和发展！
    全世界劳动人民伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志万岁！
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年十一月五日于北京
