Title: 35th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution: Grand Military Parade Held in Red Square, Moscow

Author:
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 1

On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, a grand military parade was held in Red Square, Moscow. Stalin and his closest comrades-in-arms on the reviewing stand above Lenin's Mausoleum (from left to right): M.S. Malinin, N.E. Basisty, P.F. Zhigarev, S.M. Budyonny, V.A. Govorov, A.M. Vasilevsky, N.A. Bulganin, J.V. Stalin, S.K. Timoshenko, G.M. Malenkov, L.P. Beria, N.S. Khrushchev, L.M. Kaganovich, V.M. Molotov, N.M. Shvernik, M.G. Pervukhin, M.Z. Saburov, A.I. Mikoyan, P.K. Ponomarenko, M.A. Suslov, M.F. Shkiryatov, A.B. Aristov, N.M. Pegov, L.I. Brezhnev. (TASS dispatch)
