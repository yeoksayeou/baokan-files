Title: U.S. Side Admits U.S. Aircraft Again Intruded into the Conference Area
Author:
Date: 1952-11-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 28th: On October 22nd, U.S. military aircraft violated the agreement on the Korean armistice negotiation conference area and intruded into the airspace above the Panmunjom conference area. On October 24th, our chief liaison officer protested to the other side. On October 28th, the U.S. chief liaison officer admitted that two U.S. aircraft had indeed flown over the conference area at that time, and expressed apologies to our side, claiming that steps had been taken to prevent similar incidents from happening again.

### 美方承认美机又侵入会场区

1952-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美方承认美机又侵入会场区
    【新华社开城二十八日电】十月二十二日，美方军用飞机违反朝鲜停战谈判会场区协议，侵入板门店会场区上空。十月二十四日我方首席联络官向对方提出抗议。十月二十八日美方首席联络官承认当时确有美方飞机两架飞越会场区，并向我方表示歉意，声称已采取步骤使类似事件不再发生。
