Title: All-China Musicians Association Hosts Banquet for Zakharov
Author:
Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 10th. The All-China Musicians Association hosted a banquet on the evening of the 9th for Zakharov, a member of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, famous composer, and secretary of the Soviet Composers Association, and held a symposium.
    The Chinese composers and singers who attended the banquet included: Lü Ji, Ma Sicong, Ma Ke, Zhao Feng, He Shide, Yu Yixuan, Li Ling, Chang Xiangyu, Qu Xixian, Xin Fengxia and more than ten others.
    At the symposium, Zakharov put forward incisive opinions on the issue of the development direction of Chinese folk music, which gave Chinese musicians a lot of inspiration.
    Zakharov expressed a strong interest in Chinese folk music and drama. He hopes that the Chinese and Soviet music circles will strengthen contacts in the future to enhance mutual understanding.

### 中华全国音乐工作者协会  设宴招待查哈罗夫

1952-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国音乐工作者协会
    设宴招待查哈罗夫
    【新华社十日讯】中华全国音乐工作者协会于九日晚设宴招待苏联文化工作者代表团团员、名作曲家、苏联作曲家协会书记查哈罗夫，并举行座谈会。
    出席宴会的我国作曲家、演唱家，有：吕骥、马思聪、马可、赵渢、何士德、喻宜萱、李凌、常香玉、瞿希贤、新凤霞等十余人。
    查哈罗夫在座谈会上，对中国民间音乐发展方向的问题提出精辟的意见，给中国音乐家们很多启示。
    查哈罗夫对中国民间音乐和戏剧，发生浓厚的兴趣。他希望中苏音乐界今后加强联系，以增进相互间的了解。
