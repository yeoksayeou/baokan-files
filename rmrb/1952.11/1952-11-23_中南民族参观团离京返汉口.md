Title: Central-South Nationalities Visiting Group Leaves Beijing for Hankou
Author:
Date: 1952-11-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 21st—The Central-South Nationalities Visiting Group, consisting of seventy-five people, left Beijing on the 20th to return to Hankou. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Fei Xiaotong, Sa Kongliao, Gan Chunlei, and Ayesha, all members of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, as well as many others.
    The Central-South Nationalities Visiting Group has visited factories, schools, and scenic spots in Wuhu, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Tianjin, Beijing, and other places. They were warmly received and welcomed by the people's governments and the masses in these areas.

### 中南民族参观团离京返汉口

1952-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中南民族参观团离京返汉口
    【新华社二十一日讯】中南区民族参观团一行七十五人于二十日离北京返回汉口。前往车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会委员费孝通、萨空了，办公厅副主任甘春雷、阿艾沙等多人。
    中南区民族参观团曾先后到芜湖、南京、上海、杭州、天津、北京等地参观了各个工厂、学校及名胜古迹。并受到各该地人民政府及群众的热情招待和欢迎。
