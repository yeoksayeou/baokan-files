Title: Central and Southern Nationalities Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-11-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: The Central and Southern District Nationalities Visiting Group, a party of seventy-five people, arrived in Beijing on the 2nd from Tianjin. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Liu Chun, Fei Xiaotong, Luo Changpei, Sa Kongliao, Zhu Zaoguan, and Weng Dujian, members of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government in Beijing; Wang Feng, Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Northwest Military and Administrative Committee; Gan Chunlei and Peng Sike, Deputy Directors of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central Committee; and student representatives from the minority nationality classes of the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Central Youth League School, and the Beijing Hui Nationality College, totaling more than one hundred people.
    The Central and Southern District Nationalities Visiting Group includes nineteen ethnic groups: Yao, She, Hui, Dong, Zhuang, Miao, Pian, Gelao, Vietnamese, Lai, Yi, Maonan, Luoluo, Li, Ling, Uyghur, Mongolian, Manchu, and Jewish. The group arrived in Hankou before National Day and left Hankou on October 5th to visit various cities such as Wuhu, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Tianjin.

### 中南民族参观团抵京

1952-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中南民族参观团抵京
    【新华社二日讯】中南区民族参观团一行七十五人于二日由天津到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府民族事务委员会在京委员刘春、费孝通、罗常培、萨空了、朱早观、翁独健，西北军政委员会民族事务委员会主任委员汪锋、中央民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷、朋斯克以及中央民族学院、中央团校少数民族班、北京回民学院学生代表等共百余人。
    中南区民族参观团包括瑶、畬、回、侗、僮、苗、偏人、?佬、越南、倈、彝、毛难、倮倮、黎、伶、维吾尔、蒙、满、犹太等十九个民族成分。该团在国庆节前抵达汉口，十月五日离开汉口，到芜湖、南京、上海、杭州、天津等各城市参观。
