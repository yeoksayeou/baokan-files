Title: Soviet Film Exhibition Adds Special Program
Author:
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: To enrich the content of the current Soviet Film Exhibition, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government have added a new Soviet documentary, "The Visit of the Chinese Farmers Delegation to the Soviet Union," and "New China Weekly No. 26," to be screened as special programs in various locations. "The Visit of the Chinese Farmers Delegation to the Soviet Union" was produced by the Soviet Documentary Film Studio. "New China Weekly No. 26" was produced by the Beijing Film Studio, and its content includes the arrival of the delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China in Moscow, Premier Zhou Enlai's return to Beijing, and the arrival of Mongolian People's Republic Premier Tsedenbal in Beijing. "The Visit of the Chinese Farmers Delegation to the Soviet Union" has been dubbed with Mandarin narration, and the China Film Distribution and Exhibition Corporation is currently reducing it to two hundred 16mm copies, which will be distributed to various locations for screening in succession.

### 苏联影片展览增加特别节目

1952-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联影片展览增加特别节目
    【新华社十四日讯】中苏友好协会总会和中央人民政府文化部，为充实此次苏联影片展览内容，又增加一部苏联新纪录片“中国农民代表团访苏经过”和“新中国周报第二十六号”，在各地作为特别节目上映。“中国农民代表团访苏经过”是苏联文献电影纪录片制片厂出品。“新中国周报第二十六号”是北京电影制片厂出品，内容为中华人民共和国政府代表团到达莫斯科、周恩来总理返回北京、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔到达北京。“中国农民代表团访苏经过”已配上华语解说，正由中国影片经理公司缩制成二百个十六粍拷贝，将陆续分发各地放映。
