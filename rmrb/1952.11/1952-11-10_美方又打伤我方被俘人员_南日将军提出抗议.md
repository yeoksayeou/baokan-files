Title: U.S. Side Again Injures Our Captured Personnel, General Nam Il Lodges Protest
Author:
Date: 1952-11-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 9th. On November 6th, the U.S. side injured twenty-one of our captured personnel at the Koje Island prisoner-of-war camp. General Nam Il, the chief representative of our delegation to the Korean armistice talks, lodged a protest with the other side on November 9th, pointing out that the other side absolutely cannot escape full responsibility for the war crime of torturing and killing prisoners of war.

### 美方又打伤我方被俘人员  南日将军提出抗议

1952-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美方又打伤我方被俘人员
    南日将军提出抗议
    【新华社开城九日电】十一月六日美方在蜂岩岛战俘营打伤我方被俘人员二十一人。朝鲜停战谈判我方代表团首席代表南日将军在十一月九日向对方提出抗议，指出对方绝不能逃脱对于虐杀战俘的战争罪行的完全责任。
