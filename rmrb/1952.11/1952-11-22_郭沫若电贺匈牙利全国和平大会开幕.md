Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Opening of the Hungarian National Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The Hungarian National Peace Congress is scheduled to open in Budapest on the 22nd. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to the Hungarian National Peace Council to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: To the Hungarian National Peace Council:

	On the occasion of the imminent opening of the Hungarian National Peace Congress, the China Peace Committee, representing the people of China who are loyal to peace and love peace, extends warm and heartfelt congratulations to you—our dear friends and comrades. As the aggressive bloc headed by the United States is increasingly intensifying its conspiracy activities to create and expand war, creating international tensions and seriously threatening the peaceful lives of the people, it is the urgent task of all progressive people to unite extensively with all kind and peace-loving people in the world to stop war and defend the peaceful and free life of all mankind. The Hungarian people, like us Chinese people, are ardent lovers of peace. The various socialist constructions you are actively carrying out have achieved great successes and will continue to achieve even greater successes, which is an excellent guarantee for the cause of peace. This Hungarian Peace Congress, held in your capital, the beautiful peaceful city of Budapest, on the eve of the World People's Peace Congress, is of great significance. Our two peoples, China and Hungary, are closely united in the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and together with the vast people of the world who love peace, are engaged in unremitting struggle. Peace will surely triumph.
	Let us, on behalf of the peace-loving people of China, warmly wish your peace congress a glorious success!
		China Peace Committee Chairman Guo Moruo

### 郭沫若电贺匈牙利全国和平大会开幕

1952-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺匈牙利全国和平大会开幕
    【新华社二十一日讯】匈牙利全国和平大会定于二十二日在布达佩斯开幕。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若致电匈牙利全国和平理事会表示祝贺。电文如下：匈牙利全国和平理事会：
    值此匈牙利全国和平大会即将开幕之际，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会代表着全中国忠诚爱好和平的人民，谨向你们——我们亲切的朋友和同志，致以热烈的和衷心的祝贺。由于以美国为首的侵略集团正在日益加紧制造与扩大战争的阴谋活动，造成了国际间的紧张局势，使人民的和平生活遭受到严重的威胁，因此广泛地团结全世界一切善良的爱好和平的人民，一致起来为制止战争，保卫全人类和平自由的生活而斗争，乃是当前所有进步人民的迫切任务。匈牙利人民和我们中国人民一样，是酷爱和平的。你们所积极进行的各项社会主义的建设已经获得了伟大的成就，并将继续获得更加伟大的成就，这正是和平事业的极好的保障。此次在世界人民和平大会召开的前夕，于贵国首都，这个美丽的和平城市——布达佩斯举行的匈牙利和平大会是具有重要意义的。我们中匈两国人民紧密地团结在以苏联为首的和平民主阵营中，与全世界爱好和平的广大人民一起，进行着坚持不懈的斗争，和平是一定胜利的。
    让我们代表全中国爱好和平的人民，热烈地预祝你们的和平大会获得辉煌的成功！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若
