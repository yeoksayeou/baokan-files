Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 158th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The 158th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 14th. At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai gave an explanation on the "Issues Concerning Changing the Institutions and Tasks of the People's Governments (Military and Political Committees) of the Greater Administrative Areas and the Adjustment of the Establishment of Provinces and Regions," and Zhang Su, Deputy Director of the North China Administrative Committee of the Government Administration Council, gave a supplementary explanation on the specific measures for adjusting the establishment of provinces and regions in the North China Region. The meeting also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, as well as motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一五八次会议

1952-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五八次会议
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十八次政务会议在十四日下午举行。会上周恩来总理作了“关于改变大行政区人民政府（军政委员会）机构与任务的问题和调整省区建制的问题”的说明，政务院华北行政委员会张苏副主任就华北区调整省区建制的具体措施作了补充说明。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
