Title: Commemorating the 35th Anniversary of the October Revolution, Anglo-Soviet Friendship Association and Others Hold Anglo-Soviet Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1952-11-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London: The Anglo-Soviet Friendship Association and the Scottish-Soviet Association will hold a "Month of Friendship with the Soviet Union" in November. On November 8th, a grand meeting commemorating the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution will be held in London's largest "Empress Hall," marking the beginning of the Friendship Month. A notice issued by the Anglo-Soviet Friendship Association and the Scottish-Soviet Association stated: "We should believe that people from all walks of life in Britain will express during the Friendship Month their increasing conviction that, in this time of peace, friendship and trade between Britain and the Soviet Union are of great significance, just as they were during the critical period of 1941-1945."

### 纪念十月革命三十五周年  英苏友协等举行英苏友好月

1952-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念十月革命三十五周年
    英苏友协等举行英苏友好月
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英苏友好协会和苏格兰—苏联协会将在十一月举行与苏联友好月。十一月八日，在伦敦最大的“女皇大厦”将举行纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年的大会，友好月即从这一天开始。英苏友好协会和苏格兰—苏联协会所发布的通告说：“我们应该相信：各阶层的英国人士在友好月将表示：他们愈益坚信：在现在和平时期，英国与苏联的友好和贸易正如在一九四一—一九四五年这个紧要关头时期一样，是有重大意义的。”
