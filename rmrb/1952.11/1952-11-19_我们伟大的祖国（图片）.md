Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-11-19
Page: 1

Holding small-scale material exchange fairs is a good way to promote the exchange of industrial and agricultural products and strengthen the worker-peasant alliance. This is a small-scale material exchange fair held in Zhangjiawan, the sixth district of Tong County, Hebei Province, after the autumn harvest. The peasants are very happy to sell cotton and buy a large number of daily necessities here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    举办小型物资交流会是促进工业品和农产品的交换、加强工农联盟的良好方法之一。这是河北省通县第六区张家湾在秋收后举办的一个小型物资交流会。农民们非常高兴在这里卖掉了棉花，买进大量的日用品。
