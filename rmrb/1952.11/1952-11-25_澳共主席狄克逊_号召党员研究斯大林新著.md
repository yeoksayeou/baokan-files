Title: Australian Communist Party Chairman Dixon Calls on Party Members to Study Stalin's New Work
Author:
Date: 1952-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, November 21st: The "Tribune" of Australia published a report by Dixon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party, at the Party's Political Bureau meeting last week. The report discussed the working results of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and analyzed the international and domestic situation in Australia.

Dixon discussed the issue of the gradual transition of the Soviet Union from a socialist society to a communist society, as well as the basic propositions of Stalin's "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR." He urged party members to study this brilliant masterpiece in depth, emphasizing that it further ideologically armed the communists.

### 澳共主席狄克逊  号召党员研究斯大林新著

1952-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    澳共主席狄克逊
    号召党员研究斯大林新著
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社悉尼二十一日讯：澳大利亚“论坛报”刊载澳大利亚共产党中央委员会主席狄克逊于上一星期在党的政治局会议中发表的报告。报告中谈及苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的工作结果，并且分析了澳大利亚的国际形势和国内形势。
    狄克逊论述了苏联从社会主义社会向共产主义社会逐渐过渡的问题以及斯大林的“苏联社会主义经济问题”一书的基本命题。他敦促党员们深刻地研究这部天才杰作，并强调说：它在思想上进一步把共产党员武装起来了。
