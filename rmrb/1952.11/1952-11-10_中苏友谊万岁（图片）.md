Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship

    The presidium of the rally of all circles in the capital celebrating the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. (From left) Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Fedorov, Head of the Soviet Film Artists Delegation; Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Alexandrov, Head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble; Soong Ching Ling, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Tikhonov, Head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation; Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Chulaki, Leader of the Soviet Art Troupe; Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Gudev, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Central People's Government; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. (Xinhua News Agency photo, Qi Guanshan)
    Soong Ching Ling, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivers a speech at the rally of all circles in the capital celebrating the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Tikhonov, Head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, speaks.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Central People's Government, delivers a speech.
    Gudev, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy, speaks.
    The opening ceremony of the Soviet Film Exhibition was held in Beijing on November 7. This is the presidium of the opening ceremony. (Xu Zhiqiang photo)
    The Soviet Film Artists Delegation was warmly welcomed by all circles in Beijing upon their arrival at the Beijing Airport. (Meng Kaifeng photo)
    On the evening of October 6, the Soviet Art Troupe and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble were warmly welcomed at their premiere performance in Beijing. This is a scene of Chinese artists and performers presenting flowers to the artists of the Soviet Art Troupe.
    Literary and art workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army present flowers to the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble.
    The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble was warmly welcomed by the masses during their performance at the Beijing Stadium on November 9. (Photos in this issue of the pictorial not otherwise credited are by our reporter Zhu Bo)

### 中苏友谊万岁（图片）

1952-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏友谊万岁
    首都各界庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年大会主席台。（左起）中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫、中央人民政府副主席朱德、苏军红旗歌舞团团长亚历山大罗夫、中苏友好协会总会副会长宋庆龄、苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫、中央人民政府毛泽东主席、中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若、苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基、中央人民政府副主席高岗、苏联大使馆临时代办顾德夫、中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞。（新华社稿，齐观山摄）
    中苏友好协会总会副会长宋庆龄在首都各界庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年大会上致词。
    苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫讲话。
    中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来致词。
    苏联大使馆临时代办顾德夫讲话。
    苏联影片展览揭幕典礼于十一月七日在北京举行。这是揭幕典礼的主席台。（徐志强摄）
    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团到达北京机场时，受到北京各界热烈欢迎。    （孟凯峰摄）
    十月六日晚，苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团在北京首次演出时，受到热烈欢迎。这是我国艺术家和演员向苏联艺术工作团的艺术家们献花的情况。
    中国人民解放军文艺工作者向苏军红旗歌舞团献花。
    苏军红旗歌舞团于十一月九日在北京体育场演出时，受到群众热烈欢迎。（本期画刊照片未署名的均为本报记者朱波摄）
