Title: My National Association of Literary Workers Hosts a Banquet for Tikhonov
Author:
Date: 1952-11-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 8th:

    Mao Dun and Zhou Yang, responsible persons of the All-China Federation of Literary Workers, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 8th to entertain Tikhonov, head of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, Deputy Secretary-General of the Soviet Writers' Union, and famous poet, and held a symposium.

    Among those participating were the famous poets, writers and literary critics Xiao San, Ai Qing, Feng Xuefeng, He Qifang, Feng Zhi, Tian Jian, Zhou Libo, Huang Mo, Wang Chaowen, and others.

    The Chinese writers spent a very meaningful evening with the famous Soviet poet, discussing issues related to literary creation and poetry. Many of Tikhonov's insightful opinions were warmly welcomed by those present.

### 我全国文学工作者协会设宴招待吉洪诺夫

1952-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国文学工作者协会设宴招待吉洪诺夫
    【新华社八日讯】中华全国文学工作者协会负责人茅盾和周扬八日晚设宴招待苏联文化工作者代表团团长苏联作家协会副总书记、名诗人吉洪诺夫，并举行座谈。
    参加者，有著名诗人、作家和文艺批评家萧三、艾青、冯雪峰、何其芳、冯至、田间、周立波、荒煤、王朝闻等。
    中国的作家们和苏联名诗人在一起度过了极有意义的一晚，畅谈了有关文艺创作和诗歌方面的一些问题，吉洪诺夫的许多精辟的意见，受到在座者的热烈欢迎。
