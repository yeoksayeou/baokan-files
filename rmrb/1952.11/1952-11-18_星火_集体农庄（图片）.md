    "Spark" Collective Farm
    Collaboration by Wu En, Chen Qi, Xu Guang, Gong Mei
    I. On both sides of the Songhua River, there is a vast grassland, commonly called "Beidahuang" (Great Northern Wilderness). This fertile land has been barren for thousands of years.

    II. "Beidahuang" has been liberated. A piece of grassland on the south bank has now become a collective farm. Under the leadership of the farm chairman, Jin Baishan, and the Communist Party branch secretary, Comrade Li Zaigen, all farm members are working happily and diligently for a greater harvest.

    III. Five years ago, the People's Government established a Huachuan Water Conservancy Farm here, and many landless immigrants came here to work.

### “星火”集体农庄（图片）
乌恩  陈其  徐光  龚枚
1952-11-18
第3版()
专栏：

    “星火”集体农庄
    乌恩　陈其徐光　龚枚合作
    一、松花江的两岸，有一片辽阔的草原，人们常叫它“北大荒”，这一片肥沃的土地，一直荒废了几千年。
    二、“北大荒”翻身了，南岸的一片草原现在变成了一个集体农庄。全体庄员在农庄主席金白山和中共支部书记李在根同志的领导下，为了更大的丰收，正在愉快紧张地劳动着。
    三、五年前，人民政府在这里成立了一个桦川水利农场，来这里劳动的是许多没有土地的移民。
