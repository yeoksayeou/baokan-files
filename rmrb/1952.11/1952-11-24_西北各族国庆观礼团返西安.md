Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups' National Day Observation Delegation Returns to Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1952-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The Northwest Ethnic Groups' National Day Observation Delegation, consisting of forty-four members, led by Deputy Heads Ma Guohan (Dongxiang ethnicity) and Chen Jiangbule (Mongolian ethnicity), returned to Xi'an on the 20th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Wang Feng, Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee's Nationalities Affairs Committee, and representatives from various organs of the Northwest Region, Shaanxi Province, and Xi'an City, totaling more than fifty people.

### 西北各族国庆观礼团返西安

1952-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    西北各族国庆观礼团返西安
    据新华社讯：西北各民族国庆节观礼代表团一行四十四人，在副团长马国汉（东乡族）、陈江布勒（蒙族）率领下，在二十日返回西安。前往车站欢迎的有：西北军政委员会民族事务委员会主任委员汪锋及西北区、陕西省、西安市各机关代表共五十多人。
