Title: Northwest Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Northwest Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, a group of ninety-six people led by Deputy Head Zha Xi Cai Wang Duo Jie (Tibetan, Chairman of the People's Government of Yushu Tibetan Autonomous Region, Qinghai Province), arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 11th. Liu Chun, Zhu Zaoguan, Sa Kongliao, Weng Dujian, and Fei Xiaotong, members of the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission in Beijing, as well as Gan Chunlei and Aiyisha, deputy directors of the General Office of the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission, Guan Bao, director of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's Beijing Office, Luo Wang, acting director, and Lian Huizhu, deputy director, of the Panchen's Beijing Office, and over a hundred students of Tibetan, Hui, Uyghur, and Kazakh ethnicities from the Central Institute for Nationalities, went to the station to welcome them.

The Northwest Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation includes representatives from all walks of life from six ethnic groups: Tibetan, Mongolian, Hui, Han, Baoan, and Salar Uyghur.

### 西北牧区各民族参观团抵京

1952-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    西北牧区各民族参观团抵京
    【新华社十二日讯】西北牧区各民族参观团一行九十六人，在副团长扎喜才旺多杰（藏族、青海省玉树藏族自治区人民政府主席）率领下，十一日上午到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会在京委员刘春、朱早观、萨空了、翁独健、费孝通，中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷、阿艾沙，内蒙古自治区人民政府驻京办事处处长官保，班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺、副处长连慧珠及中央民族学院藏、回、维吾尔、哈萨克等族学员共百余人。
    西北牧区各民族参观团，包括藏、蒙、回、汉、保安、撒里维吾尔等六个民族的各阶层代表人物。
