Title: The Political Departments of the North China Military Region Headquarters and the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, etc., Hold Celebrations for the 35th Anniversary of the October Revolution; Representatives of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble Invited to Participate and Welcomed
Author:
Date: 1952-11-09
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency Report: The Political Department of the Headquarters of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Political Department of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, and the directly subordinate organs and guard units of the Public Security Forces of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission held celebrations for the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution on the evening of the 7th.

　　More than 1,400 people participated in the celebration held by the Political Department of the Headquarters of the North China Military Region and the Political Department of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. Velsky, the leader of the dance team of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble actors Znechov, Gridnev, Gilnichnikov, and Chebyshev, were invited to participate in the meeting and were warmly welcomed. Yang Chengwu, Chief of Staff of the North China Military Region Headquarters, spoke at the meeting, calling on the North China Military Region and the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison troops to actively learn from Soviet military science and the internationalist spirit of the Soviet army, and accelerate the construction of my country's modern national defense force to defend the motherland and world peace. Velsky spoke on behalf of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble at the meeting, saying: "The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, as a representative of the Soviet army, has come to the People's Republic of China to introduce the art of the Soviet army to the Chinese people. We sincerely hope that the Chinese People's Liberation Army will further grow and achieve greater achievements in the cause of defending peace." After the speech by combat hero Wang Yougen, the Political Department of the Headquarters of the North China Military Region presented flowers to the representatives of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble and presented them with "North China Liberation Medals." At this time, thunderous applause and cheers erupted from the audience.

　　More than 1,500 people attended the celebration meeting of the directly subordinate organs and guard units of the Public Security Forces of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government. Vinogradov, the leader of the choir of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble actors Kuregin, Rebinsky, Bobkov, and Shipolov, were invited to participate in the meeting and were warmly welcomed. Li Tianhuan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Public Security Forces of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, spoke at the meeting. He congratulated the 35th anniversary of the October Revolution on behalf of all comrades of the Public Security Forces, and wished good health to Stalin and Soviet friends. Li Tianhuan called on all comrades of the Public Security Forces to earnestly learn from the Soviet army and vigilantly defend the great motherland. After the speech by combat hero Ni Wenhuan, Vinogradov, the representative of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, spoke, saying: The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble is willing to further enhance the friendly relations between the people of China and the Soviet Union through its artistic activities. The Public Security Forces presented flowers to the representatives of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and Deng Shaodong, Deputy Commander of the Public Security Forces, made a brief presentation speech and personally presented five "North China Liberation Medals" to the five Soviet friends. Kuregin, the representative of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, gave a reply speech, thanking the Public Security Forces for the gifts and enthusiastic hospitality they had given them.

### 华北军区司令部政治部和京津卫戍司令部政治部等  分别举行庆祝十月革命三十五周年大会  苏军红旗歌舞团代表应邀参加受到欢迎

1952-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    华北军区司令部政治部和京津卫戍司令部政治部等
    分别举行庆祝十月革命三十五周年大会
    苏军红旗歌舞团代表应邀参加受到欢迎
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中国人民解放军华北军区司令部政治部和京津卫戍司令部政治部、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会公安部队直属机关和警卫部队于七日晚间分别举行庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年大会。
    参加华北军区司令部政治部和京津卫戍司令部政治部庆祝大会的，共一千四百多人。苏军红旗歌舞团舞蹈队队长维尔斯基，苏军红旗歌舞团演员兹聂卓夫、葛里德聂夫、吉尔尼契尼柯夫、契比淑夫等五人应邀参加大会，受到热烈欢迎。华北军区司令部参谋长杨成武在大会上讲话，号召华北军区和京津卫戍部队要积极学习苏联的军事科学和苏联军队的国际主义精神，加速建设我国现代化国防军，以保卫祖国和世界和平。维尔斯基代表苏军红旗歌舞团在大会上讲话，他说：“苏军红旗歌舞团作为苏联军队的代表来到中华人民共和国，向中国人民介绍苏军的艺术。我们衷心地热望中国人民解放军进一步壮大和在保卫和平事业中获得更大的成就。”大会在战斗英雄王有根讲话之后，由华北军区司令部政治部向苏军红旗歌舞团代表们献花，并赠给“华北解放纪念章”。这时全场爆发震耳的鼓掌声和欢呼声。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会公安部队直属机关和警卫部队的庆祝大会，到会一千五百多人。苏军红旗歌舞团合唱队队长维诺格拉陀夫，苏军红旗歌舞团演员库雷金、雷宾斯基、保布可夫、席波洛夫等五人应邀参加大会，受到热烈欢迎。人民革命军事委员会公安部队副政治委员李天焕在大会上讲话，他代表公安部队全体同志祝贺十月革命三十五周年，祝贺斯大林和苏联朋友们的健康。李天焕号召公安部队全体同志认真地向苏联军队学习，警惕地保卫伟大的祖国。在战斗英雄倪文焕讲话后，苏军红旗歌舞团代表维诺格拉陀夫讲话，他说：苏军红旗歌舞团愿意通过自己的艺术活动，进一步增进中苏两国人民之间的友好关系。公安部队向苏军红旗歌舞团代表们献花，公安部队副司令员邓少东作简短献词，亲自把五枚“华北解放纪念章”赠给五位苏联友人。苏军红旗歌舞团代表库雷金致答词，他感谢公安部队赠给他们的礼物和热情的招待。
