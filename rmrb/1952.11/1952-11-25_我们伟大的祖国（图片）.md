    Our Great Motherland
    The advanced experience of the Soviet Union in "organizing rhythmic production according to instruction charts" has been promoted in China's machinery and electrical industries, resulting in great improvements in the management methods of these enterprises. Liu Zhenrong, a worker and member of the Communist Youth League at the Northeast Machinery Plant No. 3, has completed his task for the day according to the production instruction chart. Hu Changxing, the production team leader, is marking a red line on the production progress chart for him.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    苏联“按指示图表组织有节奏的生产”的先进经验，在我国机器、电器工业中推行的结果，使这些企业的管理方法有了很大改进。东北机械三厂的工人、青年团员刘振荣按生产指示图表完成了当天的任务，生产组长胡长兴正给他在生产进度表上划上红线。
