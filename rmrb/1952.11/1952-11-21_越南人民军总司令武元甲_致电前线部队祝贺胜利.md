Title: Vietnamese People's Army Commander-in-Chief Vo Nguyen Giap Sends Telegram to Frontline Troops Congratulating Them on Victory
Author:
Date: 1952-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 19th. Vietnamese People's Army Commander-in-Chief Vo Nguyen Giap sent a telegram to the People's Army frontline troops, congratulating them on their victory. The congratulatory telegram said: On the night of November 13th, the People's Army attacked the French military stronghold in the Phat Diem area, wiping out many enemy troops and inducing the French army to send reinforcements from Nam Dinh. On the 14th, the People's Army annihilated a French army unit that was being reinforced by water, sinking three enemy gunboats and damaging one on the Day River, causing heavy losses to the enemy. The People's Army also implemented the instruction to exploit the weakness that the enemy is not easily concealed on the plains, and annihilated the French troops who had invaded the liberated areas in an ambush. The congratulatory telegram encouraged the frontline troops to take advantage of this victory to expand the results of the battle and wipe out more French troops.

### 越南人民军总司令武元甲  致电前线部队祝贺胜利

1952-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令武元甲
    致电前线部队祝贺胜利
    【新华社二十日讯】据越南通讯社北越十九日讯：越南人民军总司令武元甲致电人民军前线部队，祝贺胜利。贺电说：人民军在十一月十三日晚袭击发艳地区的法军据点，歼灭了许多敌军，引诱敌军从南定派兵增援。十四日，人民军歼灭了由水路增援的一支法军部队，在带河上击沉了敌军炮艇三艘，击伤一艘，使敌军遭受重大损失。人民军又执行了利用在平原上的敌军不易隐蔽的弱点的指示，在伏击战中歼灭了向解放区窜犯的法军。贺电勉励前线部队乘着这次胜利，扩大战果，歼灭更多的法军。
