Title: Malik, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Receives Diplomatic Missions to Celebrate the 35th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1952-11-10
Page: 4

    Celebrating the 35th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
    Malik, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Receives Diplomatic Missions
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th] TASS Moscow, 8th: On November 7th, Ya. A. Malik, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, held a reception to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, receiving the diplomatic missions of various countries in Moscow.
    The attendees included ambassadors, ministers, and personnel from the embassies and legations of various countries in Moscow. Also present were: Bo. Spasiu, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania; Li Fuchun and Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Directors of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China; A. Sobek, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Hungary; Abul Kerez, Minister of Finance of the People's Republic of Albania; Song Shaowen, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Zheng, Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government; Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions; I.K. Viliyes, Member of the World Peace Council; A. Bertie, Vice-Chairman of the France-Soviet Union Association; K. Schildhwan, Head of the Delegation of the German-Soviet Friendship Association; Nister, Head of the Delegation of the Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association; Ru. Klenghufa-Beserova, Head of the Delegation of the Czechoslovak-Soviet Friendship Association; Wu Wangla, Head of the Delegation of the Myanmar-Soviet Cultural Association; O. Babiri, Head of the Delegation of the Italian-Soviet Union Association; Airey, Head of the Delegation of the New Zealand-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as the heads of foreign trade union and cultural delegations.
    On the Soviet side, the hosts included: M.P. Tarasov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federal Socialist Republic; Soviet Ministers B.N. Kumykin, P.B. Beshchev, I.A. Benediktov, N.K. Baibakov, K.P. Gorshenin, V.N. Stoletov, D.G. Zhimerin, P .F. Lomako, Z.A. Shashkov, I.G. Bol'shakov, Ye.I. Smirnov, G.S. Khlamov; A.F. Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; A.A. Volin, President of the Supreme Court of the USSR; G.N. Safonov, Prosecutor General of the USSR; G.M. Pushkin and P.F. Podtserob, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the USSR; M.G. Loshakov, K.I. Goval, M.R. Kuzmin, R.I. Zorin, G.Ye. Kovtov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade; D.V. Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" Committee; S.A. Gerasimov, Member of the Presidium of the Soviet Peace Committee; Admirals N.Ye. Basistiy, S.Z. Zakharov, V.A. Andreyev; Army Generals M.S. Malinin, P.F. Zhigarev, P.A. Artemyev, V.I. Kazakov; A.I. Proshlyakov, A.S. Zheltov; Vice Admirals V.A. Fokin, N.I. Vinogradov; Lieutenant Generals M.A. Shalin, P.F. Batitsky, K.R. Sinilov; D.S. Mozhirny, Rear Admiral; Senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Foreign Trade, Ministry of the Army and Ministry of the Navy; Members of the Soviet Peace Committee, prominent social figures, representatives of the press, and workers in science and the arts. (Attached picture)
    On November 7th, nearly one million people in Moscow participated in the grand parade commemorating the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The picture shows the procession of Moscow people holding portraits of Lenin and Stalin passing through Red Square.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, TASS radioteletype)

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年  苏联外交部副部长马立克招待各外交使团

1952-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年
    苏联外交部副部长马立克招待各外交使团
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科八日电：苏联外交部副部长雅·阿·马立克于十一月七日举行庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年的招待会，招待各国驻莫斯科的外交使团。
    出席招待会的有：各国大使、公使以及各国大使馆和公使馆的人员。到会的还有：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席勃·斯巴修；中华人民共和国政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春和叶季壮；匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部部长阿·索贝克；阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国财政部长阿布尔·克列兹；中华人民共和国政务院财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中央人民政府邮电部副部长王诤；世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬；世界和平理事会理事伊·科·维里盖斯；法苏协会副主席艾·柏蒂；德苏友好协会代表团团长克·希尔德璜；罗苏友好协会代表团团长尼斯特；捷苏友好协会代表团团长鲁·克伦古伐—贝塞罗伐；缅苏文化协会代表团团长吴旺拉；意苏协会代表团团长奥·巴比里；新西兰对苏友好协会代表团团长艾雷以及外国工会和文化代表团的团长们。
    苏联方面出席作陪的有：俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席姆·普·塔拉索夫，苏联部长巴·尼·库米金、波·巴·别什切夫、依·阿·贝尼迪克托夫、尼·康·巴依巴科夫、凯·普·高尔谢宁、维·恩·斯托列托夫、德·格·日梅林、普·佛·洛马科、兹·阿·沙什柯夫、伊·格·鲍尔沙科夫、叶·伊·斯米尔诺夫、格·塞·赫拉莫夫；苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书亚·佛·高尔金；苏联最高法院院长阿·阿·伏林；苏联最高检察长格·恩·萨弗洛夫；苏联外交部副部长格·马·普式金和波·费·波德切罗布；对外贸易部副部长马·格·洛夏科夫、凯·伊·戈瓦尔、马·尔·库兹明、拉·伊·佐林、格·叶·柯夫托夫；“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会主席德·维·斯科贝尔琴；苏联拥护和平委员会主席团委员斯·阿·格拉西莫夫；海军上将恩·叶·巴希斯提、斯·兹·萨哈罗夫、维·阿·安德烈耶夫；陆军上将姆·斯·马里宁、普·佛·日加列夫、普·阿·阿尔杰米也夫、维·伊·卡扎科夫；阿·伊·普罗什里亚科夫、阿·斯·热托尔夫；海军中将维·阿·伏金、恩·伊·维诺格莱多夫；陆军中将姆·阿·沙林、普·佛·巴蒂茨基、凯·尔·西尼洛夫；德·斯·莫及尔尼海军少将；苏联外交部、对外贸易部、陆军部和海军部的高级官员；苏联拥护和平委员会委员，社会名流、新闻界代表、科学和艺术工作者多人。（附图片）
