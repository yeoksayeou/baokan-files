Title: President Pieck Issues Statement on German-French Relations: Will Absolutely Not Allow the German Side to Launch a War Against the French People
Author:
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 13th. TASS dispatch: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic issued a statement to the German News Agency, stating:

    The joint struggle of French and German people against the revival of German militarism in West Germany, which equally threatens the security of both France and Germany, has created a new situation in German-French relations. The German Democratic Republic will absolutely not allow the German side to launch a war against the French people.

### 皮克总统就德法关系发表声明  决不容许德国方面对法国人民发动战争

1952-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统就德法关系发表声明
    决不容许德国方面对法国人民发动战争
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社柏林十三日讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克向德意志通讯社发表声明，声明说：
    法国和德国的人士，为了反对同样地威胁着法、德两国安全的德国军国主义在西德的复活而进行的共同斗争，已经在德、法关系上造成了新的局面。德意志民主共和国决不容许德国方面发动对法国人民的战争。
