Title: "Soviet Film Exhibition" Ready in Sixty-Six Cities Across the Country
Author: 
Date: 1952-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th—The "Soviet Film Exhibition" in sixty-six cities across the country is ready and will open simultaneously on November 7th, the beginning of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." The Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations, People's Governments, and Chinese Communist Party organizations in various regions attach great importance to the "Soviet Film Exhibition" and have made thorough preparations. The aim is to enable the broad membership of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and people from all walks of life to better understand the Soviet Union through this exhibition, thereby further consolidating the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

### 全国六十六个城市  “苏联影片展览”筹备就绪

1952-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    全国六十六个城市
    “苏联影片展览”筹备就绪
    【新华社四日讯】全国六十六个城市的“苏联影片展览”业已筹备就绪，即将于十一月七日“中苏友好月”开始时同时展出。各地中苏友好协会、人民政府和中国共产党组织对“苏联影片展览”都很重视，进行了周密的筹备工作，以期通过这次展览，使中苏友好协会的广大会员和各阶层群众更好地了解苏联，从而进一步巩固中苏两国人民之间的友谊。
