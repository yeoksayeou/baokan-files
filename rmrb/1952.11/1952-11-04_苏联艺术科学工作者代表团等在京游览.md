Title: Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation and Others Tour Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1952-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: On the morning of the 3rd, some members of the Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation toured Beihai, a famous scenic spot in Beijing. In the afternoon, all members of the Soviet Art and Science Workers Delegation and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble toured the Temple of Heaven, a famous scenic spot in Beijing.

Accompanying the tour were Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council; Ai Qing, Council Member of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Zheng Zhenduo, Member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and archaeologist; Cao Mengjun, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Qu Wu, staff member of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 苏联艺术科学工作者代表团等在京游览

1952-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术科学工作者代表团等在京游览
    【新华社三日讯】三日上午，苏联艺术科学工作者代表团部分团员游览了北京名胜北海。下午，苏联艺术科学工作者代表团和苏军红旗歌舞团全体团员游览了北京名胜天坛。
    陪同游览的有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中苏友好协会总会理事、中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、诗人艾青，中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、考古学家郑振铎，中苏友好协会总会组织部副主任曹孟君及中苏友好协会总会干事屈武。
