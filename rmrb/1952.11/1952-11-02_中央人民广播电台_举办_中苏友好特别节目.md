Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Hold "Special Sino-Soviet Friendship Program"
Author:
Date: 1952-11-02
Page: 1

    [Report from this newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to hold a "Special Sino-Soviet Friendship Program" starting from November 4th. The program will be broadcast daily from 19:00 to 20:00. The program schedule from November 4th to 8th is as follows:
    November 4th: (1) Broadcast speech by Li Jishen, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; (2) Introducing a workshop in the Soviet Union that made more than 4,000 pairs of leather shoes in a day without receiving raw materials; (3) Soviet songs: "Ode to Moscow," "Song of Peace," "For the Defense of Peace," "Song of Young Peace Fighters," "Comrades from Afar, Extend Your Hand."
    November 5th: (1) Zhang Lan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, speaks on "Learning from the Soviet People's Experience in Nation-Building"; (2) Introducing the Soviet writer Polevoy and "Soviet People"; (3) Selections from the Soviet ballet: "The Bronze Horseman."
    November 6th: (1) Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, speaks on "Learning from the Example of Soviet Intellectuals and Actively Participating in National Construction"; (2) Introduction to the Soviet film: "The Unforgettable Year 1919"; (3) Soviet songs: "Moscow-Beijing," "Ode to Stalin," "Cossack People Sing of Stalin," "Song of Peace," "Youth Unite," "Comrades, Bravely March Forward."
    November 7th: (1) News report on the capital's celebration of the October Revolution Day; (2) Soviet songs: "Song of the Double-Headed Eagle," "Ode to Stalin," "Great Stalin."
    November 8th: (1) Important news reports on Sino-Soviet friendship; (2) Live recording of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble's performance in Beijing.

### 中央人民广播电台  举办“中苏友好特别节目”

1952-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    举办“中苏友好特别节目”
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台决定自十一月四日起举办“中苏友好特别节目”。时间为每日十九时至二十时。十一月四日至八日节目如下：
    十一月四日：（一）中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深广播演讲；（二）介绍苏联一个全天没领原料做出四千多双皮鞋的车间；（三）苏联歌曲：莫斯科颂、和平之歌、为了保卫和平、青年和平战士之歌、远方同志伸出你的手来。
    十一月五日：（一）中苏友好协会总会副会长张澜讲：“学习苏联人民的建国经验”；（二）介绍苏联作家波列伏依和“苏维埃人”；（三）苏联舞剧选曲：青铜骑士。
    十一月六日：（一）中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章讲：“学习苏联知识分子的榜样，积极参加祖国建设”；（二）苏联电影介绍：难忘的一九一九年；（三）苏联歌曲：莫斯科——北京、歌颂斯大林、哥萨克人民歌颂斯大林、和平之歌、青年联合起来、同志们勇敢齐步向前。
    十一月七日：（一）首都庆祝十月革命节新闻报道；（二）苏联歌曲：双鹰之歌、斯大林颂、伟大的斯大林。
    十一月八日：（一）有关中苏友好的重要新闻报道；（二）苏军红旗歌舞团在北京演出的实况录音。
