Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Leaves for Soviet Union to Attend Celebration of October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1952-11-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st—The Chinese trade union delegation participating in the celebration of the 35th anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution has departed from Beijing by plane on November 1st. Delegation leader Liu Changsheng, deputy leader Chen Shaomin, and member Hang Peilan (Shanghai model worker) are already in the Soviet Union. Other members of the delegation are: Ma Peixun (Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions), Jin Ying (Chairman of the Liaodong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions), Wang Zhihao (Vice Chairman of the Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions), He Bo (Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions), Peng Yuan (Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions), Yan Wenjun (Director of Culture and Education Department of the Northwest General Trade Union), Zhang Zhi (Chairman of the Jilin District Committee of the Railway Trade Union), Zhao Chunqi (Shandong Provincial Model Worker), Li Xiujun (Model Worker of Tangshan Power Plant), Zhao Qingfu (Xiaofengman Model Engineer), Chen Yuxi (Wuhan Special-Class Model Worker), and translator Lao Baozhong, totaling twelve people. Those who came to the airport to see them off included Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department.

### 我工会代表团赴苏参加庆祝十月革命节

1952-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团赴苏参加庆祝十月革命节
    【新华社一日讯】参加庆祝苏联十月革命节三十五周年纪念的中国工会代表团，已在十一月一日乘飞机离京。代表团团长刘长胜、副团长陈少敏及团员杭佩兰（上海劳动模范）已在苏联。代表团其他团员为：马佩勋（山西省总工会主席）、金硬（辽东省总工会主席）、王志浩（河南省总工会副主席）、何波（云南省总工会副主席）、彭原（南京市总工会副主席）、阎文俊（西北总工会文教部长）、章智（铁路工会吉林区委员会主席）、赵春起（山东省劳动模范）、李秀俊（唐山发电厂劳动模范）、赵庆夫（小丰满模范工程师）、陈玉玺（武汉市特等劳动模范）和翻译劳保忠等十二人。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温、国际联络部副部长金子明等人。
