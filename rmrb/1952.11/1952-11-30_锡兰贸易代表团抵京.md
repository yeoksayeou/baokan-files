Title: Ceylon Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th: The Ceylon Trade Delegation, led by Mr. Fang Sika, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 29th. The purpose of the delegation's visit is to further discuss trade issues between China and Ceylon on the basis of the previous successful negotiations.

Those who greeted the delegation at the station included: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; and Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

Iftikhar Ali, Second Secretary of the Pakistan Embassy in China, also greeted the delegation at the station.

The list of the Ceylon Trade Delegation is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Fang Sika, Ceylon's Envoy to Burma
Members: Mahadeva, Rubber Controller of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Ceylon Government
Kuruppu, Official of the Ceylon Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Delegation Secretary
Perera, Fang Sika's Private Secretary

### 锡兰贸易代表团抵京

1952-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团抵京
    【新华社二十九日讯】锡兰贸易代表团在方席卡团长率领下，于二十九日下午乘火车抵达北京。代表团此行是为了在上次谈判成功的基础上进一步商谈中锡贸易问题。
    到车站迎接的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，中央人民政府外交部亚洲司副司长何英、交际处处长王倬如，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等。
    到车站迎接的还有：巴基斯坦驻华大使馆二等秘书伊夫提加·艾里。
    锡兰贸易代表团名单如下：
    团长  方席卡锡兰驻缅甸公使
    团员　马哈德瓦锡兰政府农业部橡胶管理官
    库鲁斯  锡兰外交部官员、代表团
    秘书
    波瑞拉  方席卡私人秘书
