Title: Resolution on Establishing Additional Agencies of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1952-11-17
Page: 1

Resolution on Establishing Additional Agencies of the Central People's Government
(Adopted at the 19th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on November 15, 1952)

    I. Establish the Central People's Government State Planning Commission;
    II. Establish the Central People's Government Physical Culture and Sports Commission;
    III. Establish the Central People's Government Ministry of Higher Education;
    IV. Establish the Central People's Government Committee for Eliminating Illiteracy.

### 关于增设中央人民政府机构的决议  （一九五二年十一月十五日中央人民政府委员会第十九次会议通过）

1952-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    关于增设中央人民政府机构的决议
    （一九五二年十一月十五日中央人民政府委员会第十九次会议通过）
    一、成立中央人民政府国家计划委员会；
    二、成立中央人民政府体育运动委员会；
    三、成立中央人民政府高等教育部；
    四、成立中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会。
