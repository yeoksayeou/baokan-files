Title: DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong Again Sends Telegram to the UN General Assembly, Demanding a Halt to the Atrocities of the American Interventionists and Severe Punishment for War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 11th: DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong sent a telegram on the 8th to Pearson, Chairman of the Seventh UN General Assembly, and attached the "Appeal to the People of the World" issued on the 4th by the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland of Korea. He requested Pearson to distribute this document, which exposes the atrocities of the American interventionists in intensifying the bombing of Korean cities and villages during the suspension of the Korean armistice talks, to the representatives of the UN member states. In the telegram, Pak Hon-yong once again demanded an immediate halt to the various atrocities being committed by the American interventionists in Korea and severe punishment for the American war criminals who directly commanded and carried out these atrocities.

### 朝鲜外务相朴宪永再次致电联合国大会  要求制止美国干涉者的暴行并严惩战犯

1952-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜外务相朴宪永再次致电联合国大会
    要求制止美国干涉者的暴行并严惩战犯
    【新华社平壤十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永在八日致电联合国第七届大会主席皮尔逊，并将朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会在四日发表的告世界人民书随电附去，要求皮尔逊把这个揭露美国干涉者在中止朝鲜停战谈判期间加紧轰炸朝鲜城市和乡村的暴行的文件，分发给联合国各会员国的代表们。朴宪永在电报中再一次要求立即制止美国干涉者在朝鲜制造着的各种暴行，严厉惩罚直接指挥和执行这些暴行的美国战争罪犯。
