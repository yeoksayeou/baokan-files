    Eisenhower: "Please come in! It's all cleaned up."

    The American election has just ended, and Eisenhower's promise to American voters to quickly end the Korean War is still ringing in their ears. However, American news agencies have already released news revealing that what Eisenhower and his subordinates have been secretly planning is not to seek peace in Korea, but to continue and expand America's military adventures in Korea.
