Title: My Commercial Delegation Arrives in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. The commercial delegation headed by Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government and Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 2nd to hold negotiations on trade issues between China and the Soviet Union in 1953. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Loshakov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Migunov, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union in the People's Republic of China, Lobanov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Sladkovsky, Director of the Oriental Department, Lavrishchev, Director of the Complete Equipment Department, Bastoev, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lidovsky, Deputy Director of the First Far East Department, and Sherbin, Director of the Chinese Office of the Oriental Department of Foreign Trade. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'affaires of our embassy in the Soviet Union, Li Qiang and Xu Dashen, Commercial Counselors, as well as Song Shaowen, advisor to the government delegation of the People's Republic of China who arrived in the Soviet Union in August, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, and Wang Zheng, Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government.

### 我商务代表团抵苏

1952-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我商务代表团抵苏
    【新华社莫斯科三日电】我中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮率领的商务代表团已在二日乘飞机抵达莫斯科，就一九五三年我国和苏联之间的贸易问题举行谈判。到机场欢迎的有苏联对外贸易部副部长洛夏科夫，苏联驻中华人民共和国商务代表米古诺夫，苏联对外贸易部交际司司长罗班诺夫斯基、东方司司长斯拉德考夫斯基、成套设备司司长拉伏里晓夫，外交部交际司副司长巴斯托耶夫、第一远东司副司长里多夫斯基，对外贸易部东方司中国处处长舍尔比纳。到机场欢迎的并有我国驻苏联大使馆代办戈宝权、商务参赞李强和徐达深，以及八月间到苏联的我中华人
    民共和国政府代表团顾问、政务院财政经济委员会
    秘书长宋劭文和中央人民政府邮电部副部长王诤。
