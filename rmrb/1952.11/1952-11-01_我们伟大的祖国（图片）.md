Our Great Motherland
The highest yield of rice per mu in the Bohai region this year can reach about 1,660 jin. This is the rice paddy of the Xinli Village Agricultural Production Cooperative, one of the high-yield thousand-jin villages in the suburbs of Tianjin. Agricultural labor model Wang Guicun (center) is here introducing high-yield experience to the agricultural labor model of East China.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    渤海区的水稻，今年每亩的最高产量可达一千六百六十斤左右。这是天津市郊丰产千斤村之一——新立村农业生产合作社的稻田。农业劳动模范王桂村（中）正在这里向华东农业劳动模范介绍丰产的经验。
