Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Tonight: Capital Celebrates October Revolution Day Rally

Author: 
Date: 1952-11-06
Page: 1

    Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Tonight: Capital Celebrates October Revolution Day Rally

    [Xinhua News Agency, November 5th] November 7th is the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union. On the evening of the 6th, a grand celebration rally will be held in the capital. The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast the live proceedings of the rally from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM on the 6th. The wavelengths and frequencies are: 468.7 meters, 640 kilocycles; 49.1 meters, 6100 kilocycles; 40 meters, 7500 kilocycles; 33.1 meters, 9040 kilocycles; 29.24 meters, 10260 kilocycles; 19.77 meters, 15170 kilocycles. The various programs originally scheduled to be broadcast by the station from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM on the 6th will be suspended.

### 中央人民广播电台今晚广播  首都庆祝十月革命节大会实况

1952-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台今晚广播
    首都庆祝十月革命节大会实况
    【新华社五日讯】十一月七日是苏联伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念日。六日晚上，在首都将要举行盛大的庆祝大会。中央人民广播电台决定在六日下午六时三十分到八时三十分广播大会的实况。波长和周率是：四六八点七公尺，六四零千周；四九点一公尺，六一零零千周；四十公尺，七五零零千周；三三点一公尺，九零四零千周；二九点二四公尺，一零二六零千周；一九点七七公尺，一五一七零千周。该台原定六日下午六时三十分到八时三十分广播的各项节目停止播送。
