Title: All-China Youth Federation Holds Standing Committee Meeting; Decides to Convene the Second National Youth Congress Next Spring
Author:
Date: 1952-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: The All-China Youth Federation held a meeting of its Standing Committee in Beijing on the 18th. Attendees included Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Vice-Chairmen Qian Sanqiang and Sha Qianli; Secretary-General Wu Han; Deputy Secretaries-General Chen Jiakang, Li Geng, Qian Weichang, and Ding Cong; Standing Committee members Dong Xin, Qu Tangliang, Mei Gongbin, Li Boqiu, and Qian Jiaju. Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, also attended the meeting. Liao Chengzhi reported on the work of the Federation, pointing out that since the establishment of the All-China Youth Federation in 1949, it has achieved certain successes in uniting and educating youth from all walks of life and in carrying out international youth united front work. In the future, in addition to continuing to strengthen international youth united front work, it is necessary to strengthen the united front work among youth from all walks of life in China, with particular emphasis on the youth among the national bourgeoisie, urban petty bourgeoisie, ethnic minorities, religious circles, and technical personnel. In order to summarize the work of the past three years and mobilize young people across the country to participate in the great construction of the motherland, the Standing Committee decided to convene the Second National Youth Congress in the spring of next year. At the same time, in order to strengthen regular guidance for youth work in various places and to specifically prepare for the Second National Youth Congress, the Standing Committee approved the appointment of Yu Beichen as Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 全国青联举行常委会  定明春开全国青年第二次代表大会

1952-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联举行常委会
    定明春开全国青年第二次代表大会
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会十八日在北京召开常务委员会议。出席的有中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，副主席钱三强、沙千里，秘书长吴晗，副秘书长陈家康、李庚、钱伟长、丁聪，常务委员董昕、区棠亮、梅龚彬、李伯球、千家驹等人，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记刘导生也列席会议。廖承志在会上报告了该会的工作，他指出：自一九四九年中华全国民主青年联合总会成立以来，在团结和教育各界青年及开展国际青年统一战线工作方面，取得了一定的成绩。今后除继续加强国际青年统一战线工作外，更要加强国内各界青年的统一战线工作，特别着重在民族资产阶级、城市小资产阶级、少数民族、宗教界和技术人员等方面青年中进行工作。为了总结过去三年来的工作，动员全国青年参加祖国的伟大建设，常委会决定在明年春季召开全国青年第二次代表大会。同时，为加强对各地青年工作的经常指导和具体筹备全国青年第二次代表大会，常委会通过增聘于北辰为中华全国民主青年联合总会的副秘书长。
