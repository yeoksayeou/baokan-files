Title: State Council Holds 157th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st — The 157th meeting of the Central People's Government Council was held this afternoon. At the meeting, Xie Juezai, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Internal Affairs, reported on the disaster situation and disaster relief work in 1952. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals and proposals for submission to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院举行第一五七次会议

1952-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五七次会议
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十七次政务会议在三十一日下午举行。会上中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长报告了一九五二年的灾情与救灾工作。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案。
