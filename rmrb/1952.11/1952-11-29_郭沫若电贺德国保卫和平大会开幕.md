Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram Congratulating the Opening of the German Peace Protection Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-11-29
Page: 4

    Guo Moruo Sends Telegram Congratulating the Opening of the German Peace Protection Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, 27th] The German Congress for Promoting Mutual Understanding and Protecting Peace will be held in Berlin on the 28th and 29th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram to Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Committee, to express his congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Dr. Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Committee, and to the German Peace Congress
    Representatives of all peace-loving people in Germany, gathering in Berlin to hold a great peace congress, to prepare to participate in the World People's Peace Congress to be held in Vienna, greatly encourages the Chinese people. We believe that this congress will make a great contribution to the peace, unity and independence of Germany and to the cause of world peace. On behalf of the entire Chinese people, we extend our congratulations and respects to the representatives attending the congress, and through you, to the entire German people.
    The Chinese people fervently hope and firmly believe that a united, independent, peaceful, and democratic Germany, for which the entire German people are striving and which is unanimously supported by peace-loving countries and people all over the world, will be realized, thereby ending the bloodshed in Europe and guaranteeing lasting world peace. We wish peace victory and the congress success!
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
        November 26, 1952

### 郭沫若电贺德国保卫和平大会开幕

1952-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺德国保卫和平大会开幕
    【新华社二十七日讯】德国促进互相了解与保卫和平大会将在二十八日和二十九日在柏林举行，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若特致电德国和平委员会主席腓特烈表示祝贺。电文如下：德国和平委员会主席腓特烈博士并转德国和平大会
    全德国爱好和平的人民的代表，聚集在柏林举行伟大的和平大会，来筹备参加即将在维也纳举行的世界人民和平大会，使中国人民感到很大的鼓舞。我们相信，这个大会对于德国的和平、统一和独立以及全世界的和平事业，将有极大的贡献。我们谨代表全中国人民，向出席大会的代表们，并通过各位代表向全德国人民致贺并致敬。
    中国人民热烈希望并且深信，德国全体人民所正在为之而努力奋斗，并获得全世界爱好和平的国家和人民一致支持的一个统一、独立、和平、民主的德国必能实现，从而将能终止欧洲的流血，保证世界的持久和平。祝和平胜利，大会成功！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若
        一九五二年十一月二十六日
