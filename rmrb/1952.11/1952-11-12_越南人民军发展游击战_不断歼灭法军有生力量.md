Title: Vietnamese People's Army Develops Guerrilla Warfare, Continuously Annihilating French Troops

Author:

Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, November 6th: The Vietnamese People's Army continues to develop guerrilla warfare in the rear of the invading French troops in the North Vietnamese plains, annihilating French troops by platoons and companies.

In Ninh Binh Province, the Vietnamese People's Army attacked Bich Dao, a French stronghold two kilometers southeast of Ninh Binh City, the provincial capital, on October 26th, wiping out a French company. At the same time, they heavily damaged French troops at another stronghold south of Ninh Binh City. Prior to this, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated three French platoons in a counter-"sweep" operation on October 12th.

In Ha Nam Province, the Vietnamese People's Army removed the French strongholds of Van Bei, ten kilometers north of Ha Nam City, the provincial capital, and Han Mac, fifteen kilometers southeast of Ha Nam City, on October 11th and 23rd respectively.

On the left bank of the Red River, the Vietnamese local People's Army conducted a total of twenty-one battles in the first ten days of October, killing eighty-eight French officers and soldiers, wounding three hundred, capturing thirteen, and seizing one light machine gun, thirty rifles, and fourteen boxes of ammunition.

### 越南人民军发展游击战  不断歼灭法军有生力量

1952-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军发展游击战
    不断歼灭法军有生力量
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社北越六日讯：越南人民军在北越平原侵越法军的后方继续发展游击战，歼灭成排成连的法军。
    在宁平省，越南人民军于十月二十六日袭击了该省省会宁平城东南二公里的法军据点碧桃，歼灭法军一个连，同时在宁平城南方重创另一个据点的法军。在这以前，越南人民军于十月十二日在一次反
    “扫荡”战中歼灭了法军三个排。
    在河南省，越南人民军分别于十月十一日和二十三日拔除了该省省会河南城以北十公里的法军据点万杯和河南城东南十五公里的寒幕。
    在红河左岸，越南地方人民军在十月的头十天中，一共进行了二十一次战斗，击毙法军官兵八十八名，击伤三百名，俘掳十三名，缴获轻机枪一挺、步枪三十支和弹药十四箱。
