Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Holds Peking Opera Performance to Entertain Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, Art Troupe, Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and Film Artists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters held a Peking Opera performance at Huairentang Hall on the afternoon of the 13th to entertain the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation headed by G. Tikhonov, the Soviet Art Troupe led by Churaki, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble headed by B.A. Alexandrov, and the Soviet Film Artists Delegation headed by Fedorov.

Soviet experts who are helping our country with construction in Beijing were also present.

The performance was jointly presented by the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera and the Peking Opera Troupe of the Northeast Academy of Traditional Opera. A total of three programs, including "Water Curtain Cave", "The Drunken Concubine", and "Yandang Mountain", were performed successively. They were warmly welcomed by the Soviet friends.

Famous Peking Opera actor Li Shaochun starred in "Water Curtain Cave" (a segment from "Journey to the West"). After the performance, Li Shaochun took two curtain calls amidst warm applause, and received flowers from Obraztsov, a member of the Soviet Art Troupe, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, artistic director of the Puppet Theatre, and recipient of the Stalin Prize.

Famous Peking Opera actor Mei Lanfang, famous Peking Opera actor Jiang Miaoxiang and Xiao Changhua starred in "The Drunken Concubine". They received enthusiastic cheers and thunderous applause from the Soviet friends, which lasted for a long time. After several curtain calls, Mei Lanfang and others received flowers from Ulanova, a member of the Soviet Art Troupe, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, four-time recipient of the Stalin Prize, Mikhailov, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, recipient of the Stalin Prize, and Halima Naserova, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, recipient of the Stalin Prize.

Zhang Shilin and Li Chunyuan, actors from the Northeast Academy of Traditional Opera, and others starred in "Yandang Mountain." They won warm applause from the Soviet friends. After several curtain calls, they received flowers from Peybutov, a member of the Soviet Art Troupe, singer, People's Artist of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic, recipient of the Stalin Prize, and Gaidai, singer, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, two-time recipient of the Stalin Prize.

The performance started at 2:00 PM and ended at 4:30 PM.

### 中苏友协总会举行京剧表演会  招待苏联文化工作者代表团、艺术工作团、红旗歌舞团和电影艺术工作者代表团

1952-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举行京剧表演会
    招待苏联文化工作者代表团、艺术工作团、红旗歌舞团和电影艺术工作者代表团
    【新华社十三日讯】中苏友好协会总会于十三日下午在怀仁堂举行京剧表演会，招待以吉洪诺夫为首的苏联文化工作者代表团、由楚拉基领导的苏联艺术工作团、以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团和以费道罗夫为首的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团。
    到场观看的，还有在北京帮助我国建设的苏联专家们。
    表演会由中国戏曲研究院和东北戏曲研究院京剧团联合演出。先后共表演了“水帘洞”、“贵妃醉酒”和“雁荡山”等三个节目。受到苏联友人的热烈欢迎。
    京剧著名演员李少春主演“水帘洞”（“西游记”中的一段）。李少春在演完之后，在热烈掌声中两次谢幕，并接受了苏联艺术工作团团员、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员、木偶戏剧院艺术指导、斯大林奖金获得者奥布拉兹卓夫的献花。
    京剧著名演员梅兰芳、京剧著名演员姜妙香和萧长华主演“贵妃醉酒”。受到苏联友人热烈的欢呼，雷动的掌声，经久不息。梅兰芳等在数次谢幕后，又接受了苏联艺术工作团团员、苏联人民演员、四次斯大林奖金获得者乌兰诺娃，苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者米哈伊洛夫，和苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者哈利马·纳赛罗娃的献花。
    东北戏曲研究院演员张世麟和李春元等主演“雁荡山”。博得苏联友人热烈鼓掌，他们在数次谢幕后，接受了苏联艺术工作团团员、歌唱家、阿塞拜疆加盟共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者裴布托夫和歌唱家、苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金两次获得者盖达伊的献花。
    表演会自二时开始至四时半结束。
