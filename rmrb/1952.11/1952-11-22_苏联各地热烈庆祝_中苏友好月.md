Title: Various Regions of the Soviet Union Warmly Celebrate "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1952-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: According to TASS news agency, people in various regions of the Soviet Union are carrying out various activities to celebrate the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" currently being held in China.

    In Moscow, the Film Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a celebration meeting on the 19th. Famous Soviet film actors and directors attended the meeting. Ge Baoquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, was also invited to participate. V.I. Pudovkin, Director of the Film Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, delivered the opening speech at the meeting. He emphasized that Soviet cultural workers, like all Soviet people, are enthusiastically watching the achievements of the Chinese people in science, culture, and art. He said, "We are very happy to see the great Chinese people building a new life under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, and achieving remarkable achievements in all aspects of economy and culture." Subsequently, Soviet People's Artist Sergei Gerasimov reported on the achievements of Chinese film art at the meeting. Ge Baoquan was also invited to speak at the meeting and received warm applause from the attendees. After the meeting, the Chinese feature film "Victory Reunion" and the Chinese documentary "The Army Liberating Tibet Marches On" were screened. In Leningrad, Soviet scholars have recently held many research seminars to discuss various aspects of Chinese literature and art. The scientific conference held at Leningrad University on the 19th discussed some new works on the history of Chinese economy and culture recently written by Soviet scientists.

    To celebrate "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," many reports and exhibitions introducing the achievements of the new China in politics, economy, and culture have recently been arranged in urban and rural areas of Ukraine. In Kiev, the capital of the republic, the exhibitions held in various libraries have been warmly welcomed by the audience. People visiting the Museum of Oriental and Western Art, which has a Chinese art department, have also been particularly enthusiastic these days. Many Ukrainian theaters are staging Chinese dramas. Theaters in Rovno, Dnepropetrovsk, Kirovograd, and Yenakievo are staging "Romance of the West Chamber," which has achieved great success. The Ukrainian people love Chinese literary and artistic works very much. Two hundred thousand copies of works by the Chinese literary giant Lu Xun, as well as works by Ding Ling and Zhao Shuli, have been published in Kiev. Recently, Kiev also published the opera "The White-Haired Girl" by Stalin Prize winners He Jingzhi and Ding Yi, Liu Baiyu's "Invincible Three Warriors," and a collection of short stories by modern Chinese writers. Guo Moruo's historical tragedy "Qu Yuan" is being printed.

    In Kazakhstan, the people of Alma-Ata are celebrating "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" on a large scale. The Kazakhstan Society for the Popularization of Political and Scientific Knowledge, the Kazakhstan Republic National Kirov University, and various factories and organizations in Alma-Ata have held lectures to introduce the achievements of the new China in economy and culture. Clubs and libraries have also held exhibitions to promote the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. The Kazakhstan branch of the Soviet Writers' Union is preparing to hold an evening party to celebrate "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." Soviet poets and writers will recite Chinese poets' works translated into Kazakh at the meeting.

### 苏联各地热烈庆祝“中苏友好月”

1952-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联各地热烈庆祝“中苏友好月”
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联各地人民展开各种活动，庆祝目前正在中国举行的“中苏友好月”。
    在莫斯科，苏联对外文化协会电影部十九日举行了庆祝会。到会的有著名的苏联电影演员和导演。中国驻苏联大使馆参赞戈宝权也应邀参加。苏联对外文化协会电影部主任佛·蒲多夫金在会上致开会词，他强调指出：苏联文化工作者和苏联全体人民一样，热情地注视着中国人民科学、文化和艺术方面的成就。他说：我们很高兴看到伟大的中国人民在中国共产党和它的领袖毛泽东主席的领导下建设新的生活，并在经济文化各方面获得卓越的成就。接着苏联人民艺术家塞·格拉西莫夫在会上报告了中国电影艺术的成就。戈宝权也应邀在会上发言，获得了到会者热烈的掌声。会后并放映了中国故事片“胜利重逢”和中国纪录片“解放西藏大军行”。在列宁格勒，苏联学者们最近举行了许多研究会，来讨论中国文学艺术各方面的问题。列宁格勒大学在十九日举行的科学会议，讨论了最近苏联科学家写的一些关于中国经济文化史的新著作。
    为庆祝“中苏友好月”，乌克兰城乡各地最近布置了许多介绍新中国政治、经济、文化各方面成就的报告会和展览会。在共和国首都基辅，各个图书馆所举行的展览会都受到观众的热烈欢迎。前往设有中国艺术部的东西艺术博物馆参观的人，这些天来也分外踊跃。许多乌克兰剧院都在上演中国戏剧。罗夫、第聂伯罗彼得罗夫斯克、基洛夫格勒和叶纳基伏等地的剧院正在上演“西厢记”，获得了很大成功。乌克兰人民非常喜爱中国的文艺作品。中国文豪鲁迅的作品以及丁玲、赵树理的作品已在基辅发行了二十万册。最近基辅还出版了斯大林奖金得奖人贺敬之和丁毅执笔的歌剧“白毛女”、刘白羽的“无敌三勇士”和现代中国作家的短篇小说集。郭沫若的历史悲剧“屈原”正在付印中。
    在哈萨克，阿拉木图人民正在大规模庆祝“中苏友好月”。哈萨克政治和科学知识普及协会、哈萨克共和国国立基洛夫大学、阿拉木图各工厂和机关，都举办了演讲会，介绍新中国在经济和文化方面的成就。各俱乐部和图书馆还举办了展览会，宣传中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊。苏联作家协会哈萨克分会正准备举行一个庆祝“中苏友好月”的晚会。苏联诗人和作家们将在会上朗诵由他们译成哈萨克文的中国诗人的作品。
