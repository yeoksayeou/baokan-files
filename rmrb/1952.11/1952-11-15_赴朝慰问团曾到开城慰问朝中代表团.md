Title:慰问团曾到开城慰问朝中代表团
Author: Zhou Zemin
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, November 14th: (Our reporter Zhou Zemin reports) The Second and Fifth Sub-Groups of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea visited the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations in Kaesong on October 28th. The delegation and the visiting group jointly held a meeting. At the meeting, the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations warmly welcomed the arrival of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea; the visiting group expressed cordial greetings to the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations. The meeting was attended by General Nam Il, Chief Representative of the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations, General Xie Fang, Representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Xin Shuzhi, Head of the Second Sub-Group of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, Deputy Heads Cao Xinghua and Qin Chuan, Lu Wenxin, Sun Yaohua, Tang Wuyuan, Deputy Heads of the Fifth Sub-Group, staff members of the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations, and representatives of the visiting group, totaling over 800 people.

    The meeting began with the solemn playing of the national anthems of Korea and China. First, General Nam Il delivered a welcoming speech, on behalf of the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations, expressing a warm welcome to the visiting group and gratitude for the support of the great Chinese people and the kindness of sending a delegation to Kaesong to express their regards. He said: With the strong support of the Korean and Chinese people and peace-loving people all over the world, the delegation will continue to adhere to its solemn position of fairly and reasonably resolving the Korean issue, and smash the American aggressors' conspiracy to disrupt the negotiations and expand the war. Then, Qin Chuan, Deputy Head of the Second Sub-Group of the visiting group, delivered a message of greetings. On behalf of the Chinese people, he extended cordial greetings and high respect to all members of the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations, who have steadfastly defended peace and justice, and unremittingly struggled to fairly and reasonably resolve the Korean issue and defeat all the conspiracies of the American aggressors. He said: The Chinese people are determined to give full support to the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations and strive for a fair and reasonable armistice in Korea. The anti-American and aid-Korea movement of the Chinese people will not stop until the Korean issue is fairly and reasonably resolved. Aseid (Uyghur), a representative of the ethnic minorities of Xinjiang Province from the visiting group, spoke. He expressed high respect to the delegation on behalf of all ethnic minorities. He said: We, the ethnic minorities of China, deeply know that our peaceful and happy life is inseparable from the heroic struggle of the Korean and Chinese troops on the battlefield and the delegation at the negotiating table. We are determined to give you our full support until the fair and reasonable resolution of the Korean issue.

    At the meeting, the visiting group presented the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations with a silk banner and gifts of local specialties from various parts of the motherland.

    Finally, General Xie Fang, representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers of the Korean-Chinese Delegation for the Korean Armistice Negotiations, delivered a speech, in which he first expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the care and encouragement of Chairman Mao and the people of the motherland. He said: For more than a year, with the support of the Korean and Chinese people and the support of the people all over the world, we have thoroughly defeated various conspiracies of the American aggressors at the negotiating table, just as we did on the battlefield. The great achievements of the motherland's construction in all aspects over the past three years and its deep concern for us have inspired us to continue to smash the American aggressors' conspiracy to disrupt the negotiations and expand the war, and to strive for a fair and reasonable resolution of the Korean issue.

    On October 29th, the Second and Fifth Sub-Groups of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea laid wreaths and paid tribute at the tomb of martyr Yao Qingxiang. Martyr Yao Qingxiang served as the platoon leader of the military police guarding the Kaesong Neutral Zone. He was murdered by armed personnel dispatched by the U.S. side on August 19th last year. The members of the visiting group expressed their condolences to the martyrs who sacrificed themselves for the defense of the motherland and world peace.

### 赴朝慰问团曾到开城慰问朝中代表团
周泽民
1952-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    赴朝慰问团曾到开城慰问朝中代表团
    【新华社开城十四日电】本社记者周泽民报道：中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团所属第二、第五分团曾于十月二十八日在开城慰问朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团，代表团和慰问团联合举行大会。会上，朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团热烈地欢迎中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团的到达；慰问团向朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团表示亲切的慰问。参加大会的有朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团首席代表南日将军、中国人民志愿军代表解方将军、中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团第二分团团长辛树帜、副团长曹醒华、秦川，第五分团副团长卢文新、孙耀华、唐午园及朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团的工作人员和慰问团代表共八百余人。
    大会在高奏朝中两国国歌声中开始，首由南日将军致欢迎词，他代表朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团对慰问团表示热烈的欢迎，对伟大的中国人民的支援及派遣慰问团前来开城慰问的盛意表示感谢。他说：在朝中人民及全世界爱好和平人民的大力支持下，代表团将继续坚持公平合理地解决朝鲜问题的严正立场，粉碎美国侵略者破坏谈判和扩大战争的阴谋。接着由慰问团第二分团副团长秦川致慰问词。他代表中国人民向坚守和平与正义岗位，为公平合理解决朝鲜问题，击败美国侵略者一切阴谋诡计而斗争不懈的朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团全体人员致亲切的慰问和崇高的敬意。他说：中国人民决以全力支持朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团，为争取公平合理实现朝鲜停战而斗争。朝鲜问题一日不得公平合理的解决，中国人民的抗美援朝运动决不停止。慰问团新疆省少数民族代表阿塞德（维吾尔族）讲话。他代表各少数民族向代表团致崇高的敬意。他说：我们中国少数民族都深切知道，我们和平幸福的生活是与朝中部队在战场上以及代表团在谈判桌上的英勇斗争分不开的。我们决以全力支持你们，直到朝鲜问题的公平合理解决为止。
    会上，慰问团献给朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团锦旗一面及祖国各地特产等礼品。
    最后，朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团中国人民志愿军代表解方将军致词，他首先对毛主席和祖国人民的关怀和鼓励表示衷心的感谢。他说：一年多来在朝中人民的支持和全世界人民的声援下，我们像在战场上一样，在谈判桌上彻底地打败了美国侵略者的各种阴谋。祖国人民三年来各方面建设的伟大成就和对我们的深切关怀，鼓舞着我们为继续粉碎美国侵略者破坏谈判、扩大战争的阴谋，争取朝鲜问题的公平合理的解决而奋斗。
    十月二十九日，中国人民赴朝慰问团第二、第五两分团曾到姚庆祥烈士的墓前献花圈致祭。姚庆祥烈士曾担任警卫开城中立区的军事警察排长，去年八月十九日被美方所派遣的武装人员谋杀。慰问团团员们对为保卫祖国与世界和平而牺牲的烈士表示悼念。
