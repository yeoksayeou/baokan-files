Title: Slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for the Celebration of the 35th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1952-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 30th (TASS dispatch)

    Slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for the Celebration of the 35th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution

    I. Long live the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!

    II. Long live international peace! Down with the warmongers!

    III. Fraternal greetings to all peoples who are fighting against imperialist aggressors and instigators of new wars, for peace, democracy, and socialism!

    IV. Fraternal greetings to all peoples of colonial and dependent countries who are fighting against imperialist enslavers, for freedom and national independence!

    V. Fraternal greetings to the working people of all People's Democratic countries who are victoriously building socialism!
    Long live the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the People's Democratic countries and the Soviet Union, and may this friendship and cooperation be increasingly strengthened!

    VI. Fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who have achieved new victories in building a powerful People's Democratic China!
    May the great friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union – a strong guarantee of peace and security in the Far East and the world – be increasingly consolidated and developed!

    VII. Fraternal greetings to the heroic Korean people who are waging a courageous struggle against foreign aggressors, for the freedom and independence of their motherland!

    VIII. Respect to the German people who are fighting for the establishment of a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany, and for the early conclusion of a peace treaty that meets the interests of the German people and the interests of world peace!

    IX. Respect to the fraternal Communist Parties leading the struggle of the peoples of capitalist countries, colonies, and dependent countries for peace, democracy, and socialism, and for national independence!

    X. Respect to the Yugoslav patriots who are fighting to liberate their country from the fascist oppression of the Tito-Ranković clique and from the shackles of imperialism!

    XI. Respect to the Japanese people who are waging a courageous struggle against foreign occupation, for national revival, for the freedom and independence of their motherland, and for the maintenance of peace!

    XII. Long live the friendship between the peoples of Great Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle to prevent war and strive for lasting peace throughout the world!

    XIII. Working people of all countries! If the peoples of all countries take up the cause of defending peace and persevere in this cause to the end, peace can be maintained and consolidated! Strengthen the unity of the peoples of all countries in the struggle for peace, expand and consolidate the ranks of peace defenders!

    XIV. Peace defenders of the world! Expose and smash the sinister plans of the imperialist aggressors, and do not let the warmongers use the nets of lies to trap the masses of the people, deceive them, and lure them into a new world war!

    XV. Long live the Soviet Union's foreign policy – a policy of peace, security, international cooperation, and development of relations with all countries!

    XVI. Glory to the Soviet Army and Navy, which guards the peace and security of our motherland!

    XVII. Soviet soldiers! Tirelessly enrich your military and political knowledge, and master combat techniques! Tirelessly strengthen the defense capabilities of the socialist country!

    XVIII. Long live the vigilant sentinels guarding the sacred borders of our motherland – the Soviet border guards!

    XIX. Workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! Victoriously complete the historical tasks set by the 19th Congress of the Communist Party! Achieve new victories in the construction of communism!

    XX. Working people of the Soviet Union! Launch a socialist competition throughout the country to fulfill and over fulfill the Fifth Five-Year Plan! Strive for new and powerful development of the national economy, raise the material and cultural standards of the people, and further consolidate the strength of the Soviet state!

    XXI. Workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! More boldly apply the achievements in science and technology and the advanced experience in various sectors of the national economy! Strive to continuously improve labor productivity!

    XXII. Working people of the Soviet Union! Develop production potential and make extensive use of it, and practice economy in all sectors of economic construction! Save raw materials, fuel, and electricity! Reduce production costs!

    XXIII. Male and female workers, engineers and technicians, responsible persons of various enterprises! Constantly improve the quality of products every day, and supply our industry and people with high-quality equipment, materials, and goods!

    XXIV. Working people of the Soviet Union! Victoriously complete the great construction projects of hydroelectric power stations, canals, and irrigation systems, and make new contributions to the cause of building communism with these!

    XXV. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the coal mining industry! Improve the methods of mining coal, and implement and complete the mechanization of the coal mining process by all means! Build new mines faster and better! Produce more high-quality coal for our national economy!

    XXVI. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the petroleum industry! Strive to accelerate the development of oil extraction and refining! Develop new oil fields more rapidly, and accelerate the construction of oil refineries! Produce more high-quality petroleum products!

    XXVII. Workers in power stations and power grids! Expand power generating equipment more rapidly! Adopt advanced technologies on a larger scale! Ensure a continuous supply of electricity to meet the growing needs of the national economy!

    XXVIII. Soviet metallurgical workers! Make better use of the equipment of metallurgical and mining enterprises, and implement the mechanization and automation of the production process! Adopt advanced labor methods on a larger scale! Supply the country with more pig iron, steel, rolled metal, and non-ferrous metals!

    XXIX. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the machine manufacturing industry! Master and increase the production of new types of machines, tools, and equipment more rapidly! Ensure the rapid development of machine manufacturing – the basis for the continuous and rapid technical progress of all sectors of the Soviet national economy!

    XXX. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the automobile and tractor industry! Adopt new technologies on a large scale in production, and improve the quality of products! Supply the country with more automobiles and tractors!

    XXXI. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the shipbuilding industry! Make shipbuilding technology and the quality of ships more perfect! Build new ships more rapidly! Establish a powerful fleet for the Soviet state!

    XXXII. Workers in the chemical industry! Improve production technology, increase the variety of products, and improve the quality of products! Produce more mineral fertilizers and other chemical products for the national economy!

    XXXIII. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the construction industry! Adopt industrial methods of construction on a larger scale, and make better use of machines! Reduce construction costs and improve construction quality! Build enterprises, residences, schools, and health institutions more rapidly!

    XXXIV. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the building materials industry! Increase the quantity and quality of building materials by various means! Produce new types of building materials on a large scale more rapidly, and make full use of technology!

    XXXV. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in the timber industry! Fully implement mechanization in timber production, improve production organization, and make better use of machines! Tirelessly improve labor productivity! Produce more timber for the national economy!

    XXXVI. Workers in the paper and woodworking industries! Increase the production of paper, furniture, plywood, and increase the products of hydrolysis and wood chemical industry! Improve product quality!

    XXXVII. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians in light industry! Strive to rapidly increase the output of daily necessities! Increase product variety, improve product quality, save raw materials, and reduce production costs! Produce more textiles, shoes, clothing, knitwear, and other items for the people!

    XXXVIII. Workers in the food, meat, and dairy industries! Increase the output of food, ensure the improvement of food quality and the increase of food variety! Produce more sugar, oils, meat, dairy products, and other products for the people!

    XXXIX. Fishery workers! Increase the catch of fish, develop fish farming, and improve product quality! Make better use of fishing fleets and fishing gear!

    XL. Workers in local industries and production cooperatives! Produce more consumer goods and increase the production of local building materials! Improve product quality and reduce product production costs! Better meet the daily needs of the working people!

    XLI. Soviet geologists! Expand the work of prospecting for natural wealth, and improve the technology of geological prospecting! Discover the infinite treasures under our motherland more rapidly!

    XLII. Soviet railway workers! Strive to increase the volume of railway transportation! Increase freight volume and reduce transportation costs! Accelerate the turnaround of wagons by all means! Improve service to passengers! Ensure the smooth operation of railways under winter conditions!

    XLIII. Workers of the ocean and inland river fleets! Transport goods for the national economy at a greater speed! Increase the quantity of goods transported, accelerate the turnaround of ships, and improve the work of ports and ship repair yards! Prepare for the 1953 shipping season in an exemplary manner!

    XLIV. Postal and telecommunications workers! Improve the methods of postal and telecommunications! Improve the quality of postal, telegraph, telephone, and radio work! Serve the people better!

    XLV. Agricultural laborers! Tirelessly strive to complete the main tasks in agriculture – increase the yield per unit area of all crops, further increase the number of publicly owned livestock, and greatly increase the productivity of livestock products! Create abundant food for the people and abundant raw materials for industry!

    XLVI. Male and female peasants of collective farms, workers of machine tractor stations and state farms, agricultural experts! Increase the yield of grain, cotton, sugar beets, oil crops, feed, and other crops by all means!

    XLVII. Male and female peasants of collective farms, male and female workers of state farms, livestock experts, and veterinarians! Develop publicly owned livestock breeding by all means! Ensure good livestock maintenance under winter conditions! Produce more meat, milk, wool, and other livestock products!

    XLVIII. Agricultural workers! Apply the achievements of agricultural science and the experience of advanced peasants and advanced livestock breeders on a larger scale in the production of collective farms and state farms! Constantly strive to master knowledge of agriculture and animal husbandry!

    XLIX. Male and female peasants of collective farms! Strive for the further consolidation and comprehensive development of the public economy, increase the income of collective farms and collective farmers, and strengthen labor discipline! Strictly abide by the charter of agricultural cooperatives – the basic law of collective farm life!

    L. Workers of machine tractor stations! Expand the mechanization of labor-intensive tasks in all aspects of collective farm production, and make full use of technology! Repair tractors, combines, and other agricultural machines in a timely and successful manner!

    LI. Male and female peasants of collective farms, male and female workers of cotton-growing collective farms and state farms and machine tractor stations, cotton-growing experts! Strive to complete cotton picking and cotton delivery plans most rapidly! Give our socialist motherland more cotton!

    LII. Agricultural, forestry, and irrigation workers! Expand the planting of shelterbelts and improve the maintenance of forests by all means! Develop irrigation and drainage work, and make full use of newly reclaimed land! Complete the work of converting to new irrigation systems more rapidly! Establish a solid foundation for bumper harvests every year!

    LIII. Workers in state trade and cooperative trade! Develop trade in food and manufactured goods in urban and rural areas, and expand the network of stores and public supply organizations! Improve service to Soviet consumers!

    LIV. Staff of government agencies! Improve the work of Soviet administrative agencies, strengthen state discipline, and care for the demands of the working people!

    LV. Workers of scientific institutions and higher education institutions! Carry out scientific research work by all means! More boldly launch creative criticism and self-criticism in scientific work! Strengthen the combination of science and production, and improve and expand the work of training experts for the national economy and cultural undertakings!

    LVI. Workers of literature, art, and film! Tirelessly improve the ideological and artistic level of your creations! Create works worthy of our great people!

    LVII. Male and female teachers, educators! Improve the quality of education and cultivation in schools, and arm students with the basic knowledge of science! Cultivate cultured and educated citizens of socialist society, active builders of communist society!

    LVIII. Medical workers! Improve and develop the people's health care, and improve the level of work of health care and hygiene institutions! Apply the achievements of medical science!

    LIX. Soviet trade unions! Launch socialist competition more extensively to strive to complete and over fulfill the Fifth Five-Year Plan! Promote the experience of production innovators! Tirelessly care for the further improvement of the material and cultural living standards of workers and staff!

    LX. Soviet women! Strive for the further development of the economy and culture of our great motherland! Long live Soviet women – active builders of communism!

    LXI. Long live the pioneer of young communist builders, the active assistant and reserve force of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union – the Lenin-Stalin Communist Youth League!

    LXII. Soviet young men and women! Master science, technology, and culture! Be strong, brave, and prepared to overcome any difficulties! Increase the achievements of the Soviet people in the construction of communism with your labor! Long live our glorious Soviet youth!

    LXIII. Young Pioneers and primary school students! Persistently and steadily master knowledge! Strive for achievements in learning, work, and public activities! Prepare to become true Leninists and loyal sons and daughters of our great motherland!

    LXIV. Communists and members of the Communist Youth League! March in the forefront of the warriors, and strive to complete and over fulfill the Fifth Five-Year Plan and build communism in the Soviet Union!

    LXV. Long live the friendship and glorious fortress of all nationalities of our country, the indestructible fortress of world peace – the great Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!

    LXVI. Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union – the inspirer and organizer of all our victories, the great party of Lenin and Stalin!

    LXVII. Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance towards the victory of communism!

    Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 苏联共产党中央委员会  庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年的口号

1952-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会
    庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年的口号
    一、伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年万岁！
    二、国际和平万岁！打倒战争贩子！
    三、向正在为反对帝国主义侵略者和新战争挑拨者，为和平、民主、社会主义而斗争的各族人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    四、向正在为反对帝国主义奴役者，为争取自由和民族独立而战斗的殖民地和附属国的各族人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    五、向正在胜利地建设社会主义的各人民民主国家的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    各人民民主国家和苏联的牢不可破的友谊与合作万岁，并祝这种友谊与合作日益巩固！
    六、向已在建设强大的人民民主中国的工作中取得新的胜利的伟大中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    中华人民共和国与苏联之间的伟大友谊——远东和全世界和平与安全的坚强保证——日益巩固和发展！
    七、向正在为反对外国侵略者、为祖国的自由和独立而进行英勇的斗争的英雄朝鲜人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    八、向正在为争取建立统一、独立、民主、爱好和平的德国、为争取早日缔结符合德国人民利益和世界和平利益的和约而斗争的德国人民致敬！
    九、向领导着资本主义国家的、殖民地和附属国的各族人民为争取和平、民主和社会主义、为争取民族独立而进行斗争的各兄弟共产党致敬！
    十、向正在为争取把他们的国家从铁托—兰科维奇集团的法西斯压迫下、从帝国主义的束缚下解放出来而进行斗争的南斯拉夫爱国者致敬！
    十一、向正在为反对外国占领、争取民族复兴、为争取祖国的自由和独立、为维护和平而进行英勇斗争的日本人民致敬！
    十二、英、美、苏三国人民在防止战争和争取全世界持久和平的斗争中的友谊万岁！
    十三、各国的劳动人民们！如果各国人民将维护和平的事业担当起来，并且把这一事业坚持到底，和平就能够保持和巩固！加强各国人民在争取和平的斗争中的团结，扩大并巩固和平拥护者的队伍！
    十四、全世界的和平拥护者们！揭露和粉碎帝国主义侵略者的罪恶计划，不让战争贩子们用谎言的罗网去陷害人民群众、欺骗他们、诱惑他们参加新的世界战争！
    十五、苏联的外交政策——和平、安全、国际合作、发展与各国的事务联系的政策万岁！
    十六、警卫着我们祖国的和平和安全的苏联陆军和海军光荣！
    十七、苏联的战士们！不倦地丰富你们的军事和政治知识，精通战斗技术！不倦地加强社会主义国家的国防力量！
    十八、保卫我们祖国神圣边疆的、警惕的哨兵——苏联边防军万岁！
    十九、苏联的工人、农民和知识分子们！胜利地完成共产党第十九次代表大会规定的历史任务！在共产主义建设中取得新的胜利！
    二十、苏联的劳动人民们！为完成和超额完成第五个五年计划而在全国展开社会主义竞赛！努力争取国民经济新的强大的发展，提高人民的物质和文化水平，进一步巩固苏维埃国家的力量！
    二十一、苏联的工人、农民和知识分子们！更大胆地应用科学和技术方面的成就以及国民经济各部门的先进经验！为不断提高劳动生产率而奋斗！
    二十二、苏联的劳动人民们！发挥生产潜力，并广泛地利用它们，在经济建设各部门中厉行节约！节省原料、燃料和电力！减低生产成本！
    二十三、男女工人、工程师与技师们、各企业的负责人们！每日不断地改进产品质量，以上等的设备、物资和货物供给我们的工业和人民！
    二十四、苏联的劳动人民们！胜利完成水电站、运河和灌溉系统等伟大建设工程，用这些对建设共产主义的事业作出新的贡献！
    二十五、煤矿工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！改进开采煤矿的方法，用一切办法实行并完成采煤过程的机械化！更快、更好地建造新矿场！为我国的国民经济生产更多质量很高的煤！
    二十六、石油工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！努力加速发展石油的开采和提炼工作！更迅速地开发新油田，加速建造炼油厂！生产更多的质量很高的石油产品！
    二十七、电力站和电力网的工人们！更迅速地扩充发电设备！更大规模地采用先进技术！保证以源源不断的电力供应国民经济日益增长的需要！
    二十八、苏联的冶金工人们！更好地利用冶金和采矿企业的设备，实行生产过程的机械化和自动化！更大规模地采用先进的劳动方法！供给国家更多的铣铁、钢、压延金属和有色金属！
    二十九、机器制造业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！更迅速地精通并增加新式机器、工具和设备的生产！保证高速发展机器制造业——这是苏联国民经济各部门在技术上取得不断的迅速进步的基础！
    三十、汽车和拖拉机工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！在生产中大规模地采用新技术、改进产品的质量！供给国家更多的汽车和拖拉机！
    三十一、造船业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！使造船技术和船只的质量更加完美！更迅速地建造新船只！建立苏维埃国家的强大舰队！
    三十二、化学工业的工人们！改进生产技术、增加产品的种类，提高产品的质量！为国民经济生产更多的矿物肥料和其他化学产品！
    三十三、建筑业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！更大规模地采用建筑的工业方法，更好地利用机器！减低建筑成本，改进建筑质量！更迅速地建筑企业、住宅、学校和卫生机关！
    三十四、建筑材料业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！用各种方法提高建筑材料的量和质！更迅速地大量生产新型建筑材料，充分利用技术！
    三十五、木材工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！在木材生产中全面实行机械化，改善生产组织和更好地利用机器！不倦地提高劳动生产率！为国民经济生产更多的木材！
    三十六、造纸工业和木工工业的工人们！增加纸张、家具、层板的产量，增加水解和木材化学工业的产品！提高产品质量！
    三十七、轻工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！为迅速提高日用品的产量而奋斗！增加产品种类、提高产品质量、节约原料、减低生产成本！为人民生产更多的纺织品、鞋类、衣服、针织品和其他物品！
    三十八、食品、肉类和乳品工业的工人们！增加食品的产量、保证提高食品的质量和增加食品的种类！为人民生产更多的糖、油脂、肉类、乳品和其他产品！
    三十九、渔业工人们！增加捕鱼量、发展养鱼工作、提高产品质量！更好地利用渔船队和捕鱼工具！
    四十、地方工业和生产合作社的工人们！生产更多的消费品，增加本地建筑材料的生产量！提高产品的质量和减低产品的生产成本！更好地满足劳动人民的日常需要！
    四十一、苏联地质学家们！扩大探测天然财富的工作，改进地质探测工作的技术！更迅速地发现我们祖国地下的无穷的宝藏！
    四十二、苏联铁路工人们！为增加铁路的运输量而奋斗！增加货运、减低运输成本！用一切方法加速车皮的周转！改进对乘客的服务！保证铁路在冬季条件下顺利工作！
    四十三、海洋和内河船队的工人们！以更大的速度为国民经济运送货物！增加运货数量、加速船舶的周转、改善港口和船舶修理厂的工作！以模范的方式准备一九五三年的航运！
    四十四、邮电工人们！使邮电通讯的方法更加完善！改进邮政、电报、电话和无线电工作的质量！更好地为人民服务！
    四十五、农业劳动者们！不倦地为完成农业方面的主要任务而奋斗——增加一切作物的单位面积产量，进一步增加公有牲畜的头数，同时大大提高牲畜产品的生产率！为人民创造丰足的食物，为工业创造丰足的原料！
    四十六、集体农庄的男女农民、机器拖拉机站和国营农场的工人、农业专家们！用一切方法增加谷物、棉花、甜菜、产油作物、饲料和其他作物的产量！
    四十七、集体农庄的男女农民、国营农场的男女工人、畜牧业专家和兽医们！用一切方法发展公有牲畜饲养业！保证在冬季条件下做好保养牲畜的工作！生产更多的肉类、乳类、羊毛和其他牲畜产品！
    四十八、农业工作者们！在集体农庄和国营农场的生产方面更大规模地应用农业科学的成就和先进农民及先进牲畜饲养者的经验！不断努力精通农业和畜牧业的知识！
    四十九、集体农庄的男女农民们！为公共经济的进一步巩固和全面发展而奋斗，增加集体农庄和集体农民们的收入，加强劳动纪律！严格遵守农业合作社章程——集体农庄生活的基本法律！
