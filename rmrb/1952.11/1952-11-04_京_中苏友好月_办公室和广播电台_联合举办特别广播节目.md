Title: Beijing "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Office and Radio Station Jointly Host Special Broadcast Programs
Author:
Date: 1952-11-04
Page: 1
    Beijing “Sino-Soviet Friendship Month” Office and Beijing People’s Broadcasting Station will jointly present special radio programs from 8:45 PM to 9:45 PM every evening from November 7th to 13th. The schedule is as follows:

    (1) November 7th: Wu Han, Director of the Beijing “Sino-Soviet Friendship Month” Office, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, will speak on "We must strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship and be good at learning from the Soviet Union."

    (2) November 8th: Liu Laifu, Deputy Director of the Beijing “Sino-Soviet Friendship Month” Office and Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, will speak on "Actively learn from the Soviet Union's advanced production experience to welcome the upcoming large-scale economic construction."

    (3) November 9th: Liu Yingyuan, National Labor Model and Director of Beijing Shijingshan Power Plant, will speak on "The great role of advanced Soviet production experience in the Shijingshan Power Plant."

    (4) November 10th: Yin Weichen, Beijing Agricultural Labor Model, will speak on "Conscientiously learn from the advanced Soviet agricultural production experience and strive for greater achievements in agricultural production."

    (5) November 11th: Beijing students' symposium on studying Soviet youth.

    (6) November 12th: Live recording of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble's performance in Beijing.

    (7) November 13th: Sino-Soviet friendship literary and art evening (including poetry recitations, singing, clapper talks, folk art forms, etc.).

### 京“中苏友好月”办公室和广播电台  联合举办特别广播节目

1952-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    京“中苏友好月”办公室和广播电台
    联合举办特别广播节目
    【本报讯】北京市“中苏友好月”办公室和北京人民广播电台定于本月七日至十三日每晚八时四十五分到九时四十五分联合举办的特别广播节目，现已确定如下：
    （一）十一月七日由北京市“中苏友好月”办公室主任、北京市中苏友好协会副会长、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗讲“我们要加强中苏友谊，要善于向苏联学习”。
    （二）十一月八日由北京市“中苏友好月”办公室副主任、北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫讲“积极学习苏联先进生产经验，迎接即将到来的大规模经济建设”。
    （三）十一月九日由全国劳动模范、北京石景山发电厂厂长刘英源讲“苏联先进生产经验在石景山发电厂所起的巨大作用”。
    （四）十一月十日由北京市农业劳动模范殷维臣讲“认真学习苏联农业先进生产经验，为争取农业生产的更大成绩而努力”。
    （五）十一月十一日北京学生学习苏联青年座谈会。
    （六）十一月十二日苏军红旗歌舞团在北京表演的实况录音。
    （七）十一月十三日中苏友好文艺晚会（内有诗朗诵、唱歌、快板、曲艺等）。
