Title: New Swedish Ambassador to China Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The newly appointed Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Lennart Westerlind, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 29th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of Western Europe and Africa, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 瑞典新任驻华大使  昨拜会周恩来外长

1952-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典新任驻华大使
    昨拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社二十九日讯】新任瑞典驻华特命全权大使魏斯特朗二十九日赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，西欧非洲司司长宦乡及交际处处长王倬如。
