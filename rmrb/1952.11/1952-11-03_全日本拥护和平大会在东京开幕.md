Title: All-Japan Peace Protection Congress Opens in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1952-11-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st dispatch: TASS Tokyo dispatch: The All-Japan Peace Protection Congress opened here on October 31st. Approximately 500 representatives from all over Japan participated in the meeting, including the famous peace fighters Ikuo Oyama, Yoshitaro Hirano, and Kenjuro Yanagida. The Congress heard a work report on the Asian and Pacific Region Peace Conference on October 31st.

### 全日本拥护和平大会在东京开幕

1952-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全日本拥护和平大会在东京开幕
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社东京讯：全日本拥护和平大会于十月三十一日在此间开幕，参加会议的有来自日本各地的代表五百名左右，包括著名的和平战士大山郁夫、平野义太郎及柳田谦十郎。大会在十月三十一日听取了关于亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的工作报告。
