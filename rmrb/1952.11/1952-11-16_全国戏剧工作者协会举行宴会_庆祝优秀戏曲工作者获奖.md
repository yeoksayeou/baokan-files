Title: National Drama Workers Association Holds Banquet to Celebrate Outstanding Opera Workers' Awards
Author:
Date: 1952-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: The China National Drama Workers Association held a banquet to celebrate the awarding of outstanding opera workers from various regions at the First National Opera Performance and Observation Conference, and to welcome opera workers with higher achievements from various regions to join the association as new members.

The banquet was hosted by Tian Han, Chairman of the China National Drama Workers Association, and Vice Chairmen Zhang Geng and Yu Ling. Attendees included Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Central Ministry of Culture, and figures from the literary and drama circles such as Yang Hansheng, Sha Kefu, Mei Lanfang, Hong Shen, Ma Yanxiang, Zhou Xinfang, Ma Shaobo, Chang Xiangyu, Gai Jiaotian, Zhang Decheng, Xiao Changhua, Jiang Miaoxiang, Zhang Yunxi, Qin Youmei, Ding Guoxian, and Wu Tianbao, totaling over one hundred people.

The warm and joyful atmosphere at the banquet demonstrated the fraternal friendship and unity of people in the literary and drama circles. (Attached picture)

Winners of the Honorary Award at the First National Opera Performance and Observation Conference: (From left) Gai Jiaotian, Chang Xiangyu, Cheng Yanqiu, Zhou Xinfang, Mei Lanfang, Yuan Xuefen, Wang Yaoqing. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Ge Liqun.

### 全国戏剧工作者协会举行宴会  庆祝优秀戏曲工作者获奖

1952-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国戏剧工作者协会举行宴会
    庆祝优秀戏曲工作者获奖
    【新华社十五日讯】中华全国戏剧工作者协会举行宴会，庆祝各地优秀的戏曲工作者在第一届全国戏曲观摩演出大会获奖，并欢迎各地有较高成就的戏曲工作者参加该会为新会员。
    宴会由全国戏剧工作者协会主席田汉、副主席张庚、于伶主持。出席宴会的有中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，中央文化部副部长丁西林和文艺界及戏剧界人士阳翰笙、沙可夫、梅兰芳、洪深、马彦祥、周信芳、马少波、常香玉、盖叫天、张德成、萧长华、姜妙香、张云溪、秦友梅、丁果仙、吴天保等一百多人。
    宴会上的热烈愉快的空气，表示了文艺界戏剧界人士的兄弟般的友好和团结。（附图片）
    第一届全国戏曲观摩演出大会荣誉奖的得奖者：（左起）盖叫天、常香玉、程砚秋、周信芳、梅兰芳、袁雪芬、王瑶卿。新华社记者
