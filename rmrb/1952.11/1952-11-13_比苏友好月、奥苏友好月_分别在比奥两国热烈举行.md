Title: Belgian-Soviet Friendship Month, Austrian-Soviet Friendship Month Held Enthusiastically in Belgium and Austria
Author:
Date: 1952-11-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th—TASS reports: The people of Belgium and Austria are enthusiastically celebrating Belgian-Soviet Friendship Month and Austrian-Soviet Friendship Month.

    On the 9th, the Belgian people held an evening party in the capital, Brussels, to celebrate the Belgian-Soviet Friendship Month, which began on the 7th in Belgium. More than 2,000 people attended. The Secretary-General of the Belgian-Soviet Friendship Association, Sanri, delivered a speech at the meeting, celebrating the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. He recounted the remarkable achievements of Soviet peaceful construction and emphasized the significant role the Soviet Union played in the struggle to defend peace. Subsequently, the Soviet film "Unforgettable Year 1919" was shown, receiving warm praise from the audience. Enthusiastic cheers to Stalin and the Soviet Union resounded continuously throughout the venue.

    Austria has also begun its Austrian-Soviet Friendship Month. A grand celebration was held by the working people in Vienna, the capital of Austria. Austrian Member of Parliament Fischer delivered a report at the meeting. He said: The Soviet Union is the leader of the peace camp, and it has consistently and resolutely implemented a policy of peace and consolidating friendship with all countries. Fischer concluded by saying: Friendship with the Soviet Union guarantees peace and will promote the development of Austria's peaceful industry. The conference then enthusiastically adopted a letter of salute to Stalin.

    At the same time, Vienna also held a photo exhibition entitled "200 Million People Transforming Nature," introducing the great communist construction of the Soviet Union and the improvement of the material and cultural standards of the Soviet people.

### 比苏友好月、奥苏友好月  分别在比奥两国热烈举行

1952-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    比苏友好月、奥苏友好月
    分别在比奥两国热烈举行
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社讯：比利时和奥地利人民热烈庆祝比苏友好月和奥苏友好月。
    九日，比利时人民在首都布鲁塞尔举行晚会，庆祝七日在比利时开始的比苏友好月。到会的有两千多人。比苏友好协会秘书长桑日在会上发表演说，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年。他历述苏联和平建设的卓越成就，并且着重指出苏联在保卫和平的斗争中所起的重大作用。随后会上放映了苏联影片“难忘的一九一九年”等，受到观众的热烈赞赏。会场不断响起了向斯大林和苏联致敬的热烈欢呼声。
    奥地利也开始了奥苏友好月。奥地利首都维也纳举行了劳动人民的盛大庆祝会。奥地利国会议员费希尔在会上作了报告。他说：苏联是和平阵营的领导者，它一贯坚决执行和平以及巩固与各国的友好的政策。费希尔最后说：和苏联友好就保证了和平，并且会促成奥地利和平工业的发展。大会随后热烈通过了给斯大林的致敬信。
    同时，维也纳还举行了以“二亿人改造自然”为题的图片展览，介绍苏联伟大的共产主义建设和苏联人民物质水平和文化水平提高的情况。
