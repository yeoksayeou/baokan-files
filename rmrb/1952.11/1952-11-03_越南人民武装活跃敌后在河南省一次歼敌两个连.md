Title: Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Active Behind Enemy Lines, Annihilating Two Companies of the Enemy in Henan Province in One Instance
Author:
Date: 1952-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, October 30th: The Vietnamese People's Armed Forces continue to develop guerrilla warfare behind the French army in North Vietnam. The people's armed forces in Henan Province annihilated two companies of invading French troops on October 16th and 18th. In Thai Binh Province and Hung Yen Province, the people's armed forces conducted four ambush battles in the week up to October 17th, killing, wounding, and capturing over a hundred invading French troops.

### 越南人民武装活跃敌后在河南省一次歼敌两个连

1952-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民武装活跃敌后在河南省一次歼敌两个连
    【新华社一日讯】越南通讯社北越十月三十日讯：越南人民武装继续在北越法军后方发展游击战争。河南省的人民武装，在十月十六日和十八日歼灭了侵越法军两个连。在太平省和兴安省，人民武装在到十月十七日为止的一星期中，进行了四次伏击战，击毙、击伤和俘掳侵越法军百余人。
