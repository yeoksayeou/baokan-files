Title: Japanese Coal Miners Persist in Strike Struggle
Author:
Date: 1952-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 4th: Japanese coal miners are continuing and expanding their strike struggle, demanding wage increases to improve their living conditions.

The indefinite strike launched on October 17th by workers at sixteen major coal companies is still ongoing. Meanwhile, the strike struggle of small and medium-sized coal miners in Kyushu is also continuing. More than 20,000 miners, following their strike on October 27th, held a second 24-hour strike on October 31st. According to estimates from the Kyushu Coal Miners' Union, miners' strikes in October have reduced coal production in Kyushu by approximately 850,000 tons.

### 日本煤矿工人坚持罢工斗争

1952-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    日本煤矿工人坚持罢工斗争
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：日本煤矿工人要求增加工资以改善生活的罢工斗争正在继续坚持与扩大中。
    十六家主要煤矿公司的工人从十月十七日起所举行的无一定期限的罢工迄今仍在坚持中。同时，九州中小煤矿工人的罢工斗争也在继续进行。两万多名矿工继十月二十七日的罢工以后，十月三十一日又举行了第二次的二十四小时罢工。据九州煤矿联盟估计，在十月份，煤矿工人的罢工已使九州的煤产量减少了约八十五万吨。
