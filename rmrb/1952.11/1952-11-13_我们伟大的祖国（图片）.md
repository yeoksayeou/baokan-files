Our Great Motherland
Farmers in the suburbs of Beijing warmly welcome the Soviet Art Troupe. This is a female student from the Huangtugang Village School in the suburbs of Beijing. They are making symbols of Sino-Soviet friendship, ready to take them to the welcoming ceremony.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京郊区的农民热烈欢迎苏联艺术工作团。这是京郊黄土岗村民校的女学生。她们正在制作中苏友好的标志，准备带到欢迎会上去。
