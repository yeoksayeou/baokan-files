Title: Suslov's Fiftieth Birthday Celebrated, Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1952-11-22
Page: 4

    Suslov's Fiftieth Birthday
    Awarded the Order of Lenin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st] According to TASS, Moscow, 20th: In recognition of his contributions to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet state, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has awarded the Order of Lenin to M. A. Suslov on the occasion of his fiftieth birthday.

### 苏斯洛夫五十寿辰  荣获列宁勋章

1952-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏斯洛夫五十寿辰
    荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团在米·安·苏斯洛夫五十寿辰的时候，鉴于他对苏联共产党和苏维埃国家的功绩，特授以列宁勋章。
