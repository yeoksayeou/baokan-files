Title: Xinjiang Ethnic Minorities Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1952-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd—The Xinjiang Ethnic Minorities Sports Delegation, a group of sixty-six people led by head Huang Lingyun (Han ethnicity) and deputy head Rouzi Usman (Uyghur ethnicity), arrived in Beijing on the morning of November 22nd. Greeting them at the station were Gan Chunlei and A'aisha, deputy directors of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Huang Zhong, acting secretary-general of the All-China Sports Federation, representatives from the North China Sports General Branch and the Beijing Sports Branch, as well as representatives from the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Minority Nationalities Class of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and other units, totaling over a hundred people.

The Xinjiang Ethnic Minorities Sports Delegation includes members from seven ethnic groups: Uyghur, Kazakh, Uzbek, Tatar, Russian, Sibe, and Han.

### 新疆各民族体育参观团抵京

1952-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    新疆各民族体育参观团抵京
    【新华社二十二日讯】新疆各民族体育代表参观团一行六十六人，在团长黄凌云（汉族）副团长肉孜·乌斯满（维吾尔族）率领下，于二十二日上午到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷、阿艾沙、中华全国体育总会代理秘书长黄中、华北体育总分会、北京体育分会代表以及中央民族学院、青年团中央团校少数民族班等单位代表共百余人。
    新疆各民族体育代表参观团包括维吾尔、哈萨克、乌兹别克、塔塔尔、俄罗斯、锡伯、汉族等七个民族成分。
