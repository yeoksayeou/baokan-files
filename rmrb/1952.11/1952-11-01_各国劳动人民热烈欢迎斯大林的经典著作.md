Title: Working People of All Countries Warmly Welcome Stalin's Classic Works
Author:
Date: 1952-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The working people of all countries warmly welcome J.V. Stalin's classic work, "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR." In Czechoslovakia, the Czech translation of this work has been published in Prague with a print run of 100,000 copies; in Bratislava, the Slovak translation of this work has been published. The theoretical journal of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, "New Thought," published this work. The "Iskra" Publishing House of the Hungarian Working People's Party has printed 110,000 copies of Stalin's work. The publishing house of the Bulgarian Communist Party published 100,000 copies. The Albanian newspaper "People's Voice" published this work and will also print it as a special booklet. In the Mongolian People's Republic, a book including Stalin's work and the documents of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union is in print.

The French Communist Party's organ "Communist Handbook," the German Communist Party's "Freies Volk," the Danish Communist Party's "Land og Folk," the Finnish Communist Party's "People's Tribune," and the Finnish People's Democratic League's newspaper "Free Word" have also published Stalin's great work. The Danish Communist Party's "Tiden" publishing house and Norway's "Ny Tid" publishing house are printing this work as a special booklet.

### 各国劳动人民热烈欢迎斯大林的经典著作

1952-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    各国劳动人民热烈欢迎斯大林的经典著作
    【新华社三十一日讯】各国劳动人民热烈欢迎约·维·斯大林的经典著作“苏联社会主义经济问题”。在捷克斯洛伐克，这部著作的捷克文译本已在布拉格出版，印行了十万册；在布拉的斯拉发，出版了这部著作的斯洛伐克文译本。捷克斯洛伐克共产党理论刊物“新思想报”刊载了这篇著作。匈牙利劳动人民党的“火花”出版局已将斯大林的这部著作印行了十一万册。保加利亚共产党的出版局出版了十万册。阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”刊载了这篇著作，并将印成专册。在蒙古人民共和国，一本包括斯大林这部著作和联共第十九次代表大会文件的书籍在印刷中。
    法国共产党机关刊“共产主义手册”，德国共产党的“自由人民”报，丹麦共产党的“国土与人民”报，芬兰共产党的“劳动人民报”和芬兰人民民主联盟的报纸“自由谈”，也都刊载了斯大林的这篇伟大著作。丹麦共产党的“时代”出版社和挪威的“新时代”出版社正将这部著作印成专册。
