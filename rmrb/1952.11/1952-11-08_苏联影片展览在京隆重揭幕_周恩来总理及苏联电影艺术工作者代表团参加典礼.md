Title: Soviet Film Exhibition Grandly Opens in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai and Delegation of Soviet Film Artists Attend Ceremony
Author: 
Date: 1952-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: To celebrate the 35th anniversary of the Soviet October Socialist Revolution, the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association are jointly holding a Soviet film exhibition simultaneously in 66 cities across the country from November 7th to December 6th. The grand opening ceremony of this film exhibition was held in Beijing on the afternoon of the 7th.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government attended the opening ceremony.

Also attending the ceremony were Wu Yuzhang and Guo Moruo, vice-presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, the general secretary; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission; Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Executive Yuan and Minister of Culture, along with Deputy Ministers Ding Xilin, Wang Lanxi, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau; figures from literary and artistic circles, including Tian Han, Hong Shen, et al.; film industry figures Yu Ling, Cai Chusheng, Shi Dongshan, Zhang Shuihua (director of "The White-Haired Girl"), Cheng Yin (director of "Steel Warrior"), Stalin Prize winners He Shide, Su Heqing, all the screenwriters from the Central Film Bureau Film Creation Institute, and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 1,200 people.

The head of the Soviet film artists delegation, Fedorov, who had just arrived in China to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," along with delegation members Cherkasov, Cherkov, Ladynina, Petrov, Smirnova, Pavlov, and some members of the Soviet Art Troupe and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, also attended the ceremony.

Also invited to attend the ceremony were Soviet Chargé d'Affaires to China Gudev, Chief Representative of the Soviet Film Export Company in China, Stedeff, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Law Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Rochar, North Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik, Polish Ambassador Kirillok, Indian Ambassador Raghavan, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with the title of Ambassador, König, Danish Minister Mook, Chargé d'Affaires of the Indonesian Embassy, Isha Marti, Chargé d'Affaires of the Swedish Embassy, Bevan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Czechoslovak Embassy, Jabrosky, etc.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Shen Yanbing. At the beginning of the meeting, the band played the song "Moscow-Beijing". Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-President Guo Moruo, and all members of the Soviet film artists delegation, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Gudev, etc., walked onto the stage, and the audience stood up and applauded warmly. Students from the Central Film Bureau Film School then presented flowers to all members of the Soviet film artists delegation, etc.

Guo Moruo delivered a speech at the opening ceremony. He said, "Soviet films have already become an extremely important part of the cultural life of our people. They are our good teachers and helpful friends; they educate and inspire us with the great ideological power of communism; they educate and guide us with the Soviet people's struggle experience, production and construction experience, and the brilliant heroic examples of the Soviet people, enabling us to build our country into a strong, beautiful, and bright country like the Soviet Union with a hundredfold confidence. The Soviet Union's today is our tomorrow." Guo Moruo concluded by expressing his gratitude to the Soviet film artists delegation for coming to our country to participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month."

Subsequently, Gudev was invited to speak, saying that the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese people are fully expressed in films and in many other aspects. The Soviet films to be screened during the Soviet Film Exhibition will show the Soviet workers, collective farm members, and intellectuals building a communist society; they will show the Soviet people's wartime heroism, the Soviet people's high moral standards, patriotism, and determination to strive for world peace. Soviet and Chinese filmmakers have created many excellent films through close cooperation. The Soviet Film Exhibition now opening will surely further promote the friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China and the fraternal cooperation of film workers.

Fedorov said in his speech that the Soviet people pay great attention and interest to the achievements of the Chinese people in various fields such as industry, agriculture, and culture. Chinese dramas, films, and literary works are warmly welcomed by the Soviet people. Fedorov also congratulated and paid tribute to the Chinese people and film workers.

Soviet famous actress, the actress who played the heroine in the film "Happy Life" which is loved by the Chinese people, Ladynina, said in her speech that the great power of socialist film art lies in the fact that it is valued and loved by the working people all over the world. It is our greatest happiness if the films we perform are loved by everyone. Her appearance on the stage and her speech won repeated warm applause from the audience.

After the opening ceremony, the Soviet color film "Unforgettable 1919" was screened. (See picture)

### 苏联影片展览在京隆重揭幕  周恩来总理及苏联电影艺术工作者代表团参加典礼

1952-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联影片展览在京隆重揭幕
    周恩来总理及苏联电影艺术工作者代表团参加典礼
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府文化部和中苏友好协会总会为庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年，从十一月七日起到十二月六日止在全国六十六个城市同时举行苏联影片展览。这一影片展览隆重的揭幕典礼七日下午在北京举行。
    中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理参加了揭幕典礼。
    参加典礼的还有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、郭沫若，总干事钱俊瑞；中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华；中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副主任兼文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长丁西林、电影局副局长王阑西；文艺界人士田汉、洪深等；电影界人士于伶、蔡楚生、史东山、“白毛女”的导演张水华、“钢铁战士”的导演成荫，斯大林奖金获得者何士德、苏河清，中央电影局电影创作所的全体编剧工作者等以及各民主党派、各人民团体代表共一千二百余人。
    刚来我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫，团员契尔卡索夫、契尔柯夫、拉迪尼娜、彼得罗夫、斯米尔诺娃、巴甫洛夫以及苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团的部分团员也参加了典礼。
    应邀参加典礼的还有苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫，苏联影片输出公司驻华总代表处总代表史德福，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，朝鲜大使权五稷，波兰大使基里洛克，印度大使赖嘉文，德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，丹麦公使穆克，印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂，瑞典王国大使馆临时代办贝凡，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基等。
    开幕典礼由沈雁冰主持。会议开始，乐队高奏“莫斯科——北京”乐曲。周恩来总理、郭沫若副会长等和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团全体人员、苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫等走上主席台，全场起立，热烈鼓掌。中央电影局电影学校学生随即向苏联电影艺术工作者代表团全体人员等献花。
    开幕典礼首由郭沫若致词。他说：“苏联电影对于我国人民，已经构成了文化生活中极其重要的部分。它是我们的良师和益友；它以共产主义的伟大思想力量教育和鼓舞我们；以苏联人民的斗争经验、生产建设经验、苏联人民的光辉英雄榜样，教育我们，指导我们，使我们信心百倍地把我们的国家建设得像苏联那样富强、美丽和光明。苏联的今天，就是我们的明天。”郭沫若最后向光临我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团表示感谢。
    接着顾德夫应邀讲话，他说：苏联和中国人民之间的友好与合作，在电影里面和在其他许多方面一样，充分地表现了出来。在苏联影片展览期间所要放映的苏联影片里，可以看到建设共产主义社会的苏联工人、集体农庄庄员和知识分子的表现；可以看到苏联人民战时的英勇，苏联人民高度的道德水平、爱国主义精神以及争取世界和平的决心。苏联和中国电影工作者在紧密的合作之下，已经创造了许多优秀的影片。现在开幕的苏联影片展览，一定将进一步地促进苏中两国人民的友谊和电影工作人员兄弟般的合作。
    费道罗夫在讲话中说：苏联人民以极大关切和兴趣注意中国人民在工业、农业和文化等各方面的成就。中国的戏剧、电影和文学作品，受到苏联人民的热烈欢迎。费道罗夫并向中国人民和电影工作者祝贺与致敬。
    苏联名演员、我国人民所喜爱的影片“幸福的生活”女主角的扮演者拉迪尼娜在讲话中说：社会主义的电影艺术的伟大力量，就在于它是为全世界劳动人民所重视和喜爱。如果我们演出的影片为大家所喜爱，便是我们最大的幸福。她在主席台上的出现和她的演讲博得全场一再爆发热烈的掌声。
    开幕典礼结束后，并放映了苏联彩色名片“难忘的一九一九年”。（附图片）
