Title: Czechoslovak Army Cultural Troupe and Romanian Army Song and Dance Ensemble End Their Visit and Performances in China, Departing from Shenyang and Harbin Respectively
Author:
Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The Czechoslovak "Vítězný lid Army Art Troupe" successfully concluded its visit and performances in China, holding a closing ceremony in Shenyang on the evening of the 3rd. Those attending the closing ceremony included: Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government of China, Wei Ziren, Director of the Cultural Department of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region, and representatives from cultural, educational, and artistic circles in Northeast China and Shenyang, totaling over one thousand people. Also present at the closing ceremony was Colonel Prochazka, military attaché of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in China.

At the closing ceremony, Li Zhaobing delivered a speech, warmly congratulating the Czechoslovak "Vítězný lid Army Art Troupe" on its successful performances in China.

The Czechoslovak "Vítězný lid Army Art Troupe" came to China in July this year at the invitation of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of China and visited the Chinese capital Beijing, as well as Shanghai, Nanjing, Hankou, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Tianjin, Shenyang and other cities, receiving a warm welcome from the people everywhere. The troupe gave a total of thirty-nine performances in China and held eight joint celebrations with various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, workers, and students, with a total audience of 400,000 people. During its visit to China, the troupe also created programs such as "Sino-Czech Friendship Song" and "Sino-Czech Army Joint Dance".

The "Vítězný lid Army Art Troupe" departed from China on the morning of the 4th, leaving from Shenyang.

Xinhua News Agency report: The Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Ensemble successfully concluded its visit and performances in New China, holding a closing ceremony in Harbin on the evening of the 6th. Those attending the closing ceremony included Qiang Xiaochu, Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, Wang Yilun, Mayor of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, and representatives from the military, workers, students, and cultural, educational, and artistic circles, totaling over one thousand people.

At the closing ceremony, Qiang Xiaochu delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, warmly congratulating the Romanian Army Song and Dance Ensemble on its successful performances in China.

The Romanian Army Song and Dance Ensemble visited the Chinese capital Beijing, as well as Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Hankou, Xi'an, Tianjin, Shenyang, Fushun, Harbin and other cities, giving a total of thirty-five performances with an audience of 300,000 people. During its visit to China, the Song and Dance Ensemble created programs such as "Singing of Mao Zedong" (chorus).

The Romanian Army Song and Dance Ensemble departed from China on the 7th, leaving from Harbin.

### 捷军队文工团、罗部队歌舞团  结束在我国访问演出  分别由沈阳和哈尔滨启程返国

1952-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    捷军队文工团、罗部队歌舞团
    结束在我国访问演出
    分别由沈阳和哈尔滨启程返国
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”圆满结束在我国的访问演出，于三日晚在沈阳举行闭幕式。出席闭幕式的有：我国中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳、东北军区政治部文化部部长卫孑人及东北和沈阳市的文化、教育、艺术等各界代表共一千余人。出席闭幕式的还有捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆武官普罗查上校。
    在闭幕式上，李兆炳致词，热烈祝贺捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”在我国的成功演出。
    捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”于今年七月间应我国人民革命军事委员会的邀请来到我国，曾先后访问了我国首都北京及上海、南京、汉口、杭州、南昌、广州、重庆、天津、沈阳等城市，受到各地人民的热烈欢迎。该团在我国共演出三十九场，并与中国人民解放军各部队及工人、学生举行了八次联欢会，观众共达四十万人。该团在访问中国期间还创作了“中、捷人民友谊歌”和“中、捷军队联欢舞”等节目。
    “维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”在四日晨由沈阳起程离开我国。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团胜利结束在新中国的访问演出，于六日晚在哈尔滨市举行闭幕式。参加闭幕式的有松江省人民政府主席强晓初、哈尔滨市人民政府市长王一伦及部队、工人、学生代表和文化、教育、艺术等界代表共一千多人。
    在闭幕式上，强晓初代表中央人民政府文化部致词，热烈祝贺罗马尼亚部队歌舞团在我国的成功演出。
    罗马尼亚部队歌舞团曾访问了我国首都北京及上海、南京、杭州、广州、汉口、西安、天津、沈阳、抚顺、哈尔滨等城市，共演出三十五场，观众达三十万人。在访问中国期间，歌舞团创作了“歌唱毛泽东”（合唱）等节目。
    罗马尼亚部队歌舞团已于七日由哈尔滨启程离我国。
