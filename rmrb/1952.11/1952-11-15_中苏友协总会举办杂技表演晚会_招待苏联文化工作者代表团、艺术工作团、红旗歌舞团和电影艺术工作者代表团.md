Title: China-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Acrobatic Performance Evening to Entertain Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, Art Troupe, Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and Film Art Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held an acrobatic performance evening on the 14th to entertain the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, the Soviet Art Troupe, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation.

　　The Central Acrobatic Troupe performed thirteen programs for the Soviet guests, including "Water Meteor," "Flying Meaning," "Kongzhu," "Earth Drill Ring," "Rope Skills," "Plates," "Poles," "Variety Show," "Magic," "Bowl Balancing," and "Great Martial Arts." These programs were warmly welcomed. During the performance, members of the Soviet Art Troupe, Putchin and Chetoszvili, and members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble presented flowers to the performers. After the performance, Chetoszvili and others went on stage to present flowers to all the performers, and Kuznetsov, a member of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, gave a speech thanking the acrobatic troupe for their performance and praising Chinese acrobatic art. Cries of "Long live Stalin!" and "Long live Mao Zedong!" resounded throughout the hall, and the applause lasted for a long time.

### 中苏友协总会举办杂技表演晚会  招待苏联文化工作者代表团、艺术工作团、红旗歌舞团和电影艺术工作者代表团

1952-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举办杂技表演晚会
    招待苏联文化工作者代表团、艺术工作团、红旗歌舞团和电影艺术工作者代表团
    【新华社十四日讯】中苏友好协会总会在十四日举办杂技表演晚会，招待苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团、苏军红旗歌舞团和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团。
    中央杂技团为苏联佳宾们表演了十三个节目，有“水流星”、“飞义”、“空竹”、“钻地圈”、“绳技”、“盘子”、“杠子”、“杂拌子”、“魔术”、“顶碗”和“大武术”。这些节目受到热烈欢迎。在表演进行中，苏联艺术工作团团员普奇岑和契托希维利及苏军红旗歌舞团团员曾向表演者献花。表演结束后，契托希维利等上台向全体表演者献花，苏军红旗歌舞团团员库兹涅卓夫致词感谢杂技团的表演，并赞扬中国的杂技艺术。“斯大林万岁！”“毛泽东万岁！”的呼声响彻全场，掌声经久不息。
