Title: Tianjin Held Sino-Soviet Friendship Radio Rally Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-11-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 19th: The Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Radio Rally was held yesterday afternoon. A total of more than 1.2 million citizens from all walks of life listened to the live broadcast.

    Part of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, led by Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize laureate, K. Vinogradov, were invited to attend the rally at the central venue. Other members of the ensemble went to various listening venues to meet the masses.

    The rally began with a speech by Huang Huoqing, Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions. He called on the city's people to conscientiously study and promote advanced Soviet experience, prepare for large-scale economic construction, expand the organization of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, do a good job in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," strengthen the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and defend world peace.

    Vinogradov, on behalf of the song and dance ensemble and B. A. Alexandrov, expressed his gratitude to the people of Tianjin. He said: The warm hospitality of the people of Tianjin has deeply moved us. We are confident that our performance this time will make the friendship between the two peoples more widespread and consolidated.

    The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble performed programs such as "Glory to Stalin" and "The Sun Sets" at the meeting.

    The Central Conservatory of Music, the Municipal People's Art Theater, and the famous patriotic artist Chang Xiangyu also performed at the meeting.

    The rally was filled with the enthusiasm of Sino-Soviet friendship. At Hengda Tobacco Factory, when the members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble arrived, more than 1,300 workers in the venue immediately burst into cheers of "Long Live Stalin." At Tianjin Medical College, more than 1,600 doctors and nurses from hospitals across the city participated in the listening session. When members of the song and dance ensemble arrived at this listening venue, more than 20 well-known doctors who promoted the Soviet "painless childbirth method," "tissue therapy," and "sleep therapy" came out to welcome them. Young nurses held flowers and rushed to present them to members of the song and dance ensemble.

    During the rally, many workers and suburban farmers traveled dozens of miles to the central venue to present flowers and gifts to the song and dance ensemble. Near the entrance of the venue, there was a flower presentation team of more than 2,000 people, offering thousands of bouquets of flowers to Soviet friends. Workers of the steelmaking department of Tianjin Steel Plant, who had achieved great success in learning from advanced Soviet experience, also came to present a banner and a silver shield to Soviet friends. The silver shield was engraved with "Learn your advanced experience more humbly." Liu Yinfu, a famous labor model who had visited the Soviet Union, presented a carefully embroidered "Sino-Soviet Friendship" comfort bag to Soviet friends. People from all walks of life wrote more than 23,000 letters to the rally, expressing their gratitude to Soviet friends. Many citizens requested to join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on the spot. More than 1,500 people from Hebei Avenue in the Eighth District applied for membership. Many workers, cadres, students, doctors, university professors, etc., expressed their desire to conscientiously study Stalin's "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR," and many people expressed their desire to study Russian well.

### 天津市昨天举行中苏友好广播大会

1952-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    天津市昨天举行中苏友好广播大会
    【新华社天津十九日电】天津市中苏友好广播大会，在十九日下午举行。收听广播实况的各界市民共一百二十多万人。
    俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋艺术家、斯大林奖金获得者孔·维诺格拉陀夫率领的苏军红旗歌舞团部分团员应邀出席了中心会场的大会，部分团员分赴各收听会场和群众见面。
    大会首由市总工会主席黄火青致词。他号召全市人民认真学习和推广苏联先进经验，做好大规模经济建设的准备工作；扩大中苏友好协会的组织，做好“中苏友好月”的工作，加强中苏两国人民的友谊，保卫世界和平。
    维诺格拉陀夫代表歌舞团及鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫向天津市人民表示感谢。他说：天津市人民对我们的热情款待使我们十分感动。我们确信我们这次的演出，将会使两国人民的友谊更加普及和巩固。
    苏军红旗歌舞团在会上表演了“斯大林光荣”、“太阳落山”等节目。
    中央音乐学院、市人民艺术剧院和著名的爱国艺人常香玉也在会上演出节目。
    大会始终洋溢着中苏友好的热情。在恒大烟草厂，当苏军红旗歌舞团的团员们到达时，会场里一千三百多工人立时响起“斯大林万岁”的欢呼声。在天津医学院，全市各医院的大夫、护士等一千六百多人参加收听。当歌舞团团员来到这个收听会场时，推行苏联“无痛分娩法”、“组织疗法”、“睡眠疗法”的著名大夫二十多人，出门欢迎。年青的护士们手持鲜花，争先恐后地献给歌舞团团员。
    大会进行期间，许多工人、郊区农民，从数十里外赶到中心会场，向歌舞团献花献礼。在会场的大门口附近，有二千多人的献花队，把成千束鲜花献给苏联友人。学习苏联先进经验获得巨大成就的天津钢厂炼钢部工人，也赶来向苏联友人献锦旗和银盾。银盾上面刻着“更虚心的学习你们的先进经验”。曾经访问过苏联的著名劳动模范刘荫福，把自己精心绣制的“中苏友好”慰问袋献给苏联友人。各界人民给大会写了两万三千多封信，表示对苏联友人的感谢。许多市民当场要求参加中苏友好协会，第八区河北大街就有一千五百多人提出人会要求。许多工人、干部、学生、医生、大学教授等表示要认真学习斯大林的“苏联社会主义经济问题”，有许多人表示要好好学习俄文。
