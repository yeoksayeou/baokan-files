Title: U.S. Planes Continue to Violate Our Northeast Airspace
Author:
Date: 1952-11-20
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 19th—U.S. military aircraft invading Korea continue to violate our Northeast airspace. From October 28th to November 11th, U.S. planes in 44 batches totaling 250 sorties successively intruded into the airspace over Andong City, Andong County, Changbai County, Ji'an County, Linjiang County, Kuandian County, Fusong County, Jingyu County, Fengcheng County, Huanren County in Liaodong Province, and Tumen City in Jilin Province. On November 1st, U.S. planes intruding into the airspace over Liaodong Province dropped five flares 35 kilometers southeast of Songshu Town. Our people express serious protest against the provocative acts of the American aggressors in constantly violating our airspace.

### 美机续犯我东北领空

1952-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美机续犯我东北领空
    【新华社沈阳十九日电】美国侵朝军用飞机继续侵犯我国东北领空。从十月二十八日到十一月十一日，美机四十四批共二百五十架次先后侵入我辽东省的安东市、安东县、长白县、辑安县、临江县、宽甸县、抚松县、靖宇县、凤城县、桓仁县和吉林省的图们市等地上空。十一月一日侵入我辽东省上空的美机，在松树镇东南三十五公里处投照明弹五枚。我国人民对美国侵略者不断侵犯我国领空的挑衅行为表示严重抗议。
