    Our Great Motherland
    Fu Caibao and Li Yinxing, workers at the Shanghai Beacon Manufacturing Plant, successfully manufactured double-flash and repeated-flash "acetylene buoy flashing lights." These buoy lights are indispensable for waterways, as they can indicate the location of reefs and the direction of channels in inland rivers and oceans. The picture shows Li Yinxing inspecting the repeated-flash "acetylene buoy flashing light."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海灯标制配厂工人傅财宝和李银星制造双闪型和复闪型“乙炔助航闪光灯”成功了。这种助航灯是航道上不可缺少的，它能够指点内河和海洋中暗礁的位置和航道的方向。这是李银星正在检查复闪型“乙炔助航闪光灯”的情形。
