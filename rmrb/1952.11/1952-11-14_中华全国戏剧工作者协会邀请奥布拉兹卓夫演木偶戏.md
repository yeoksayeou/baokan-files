Title: All-China Federation of Drama Workers Invites Obraztsov to Perform Puppet Show
Author:
Date: 1952-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: In order to better learn from Soviet puppet theater, the All-China Federation of Drama Workers invited Sergei Obraztsov, artistic director of the Soviet Puppet Theater, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, and Stalin Prize laureate, to perform a puppet show on the morning of the 13th. The performance was hosted by Zhang Geng, Vice President of the Central Academy of Drama. More than 300 drama, art, and music workers from the capital attended, including Wu Xue, Wang Chaowen, Hua Junwu, Zhao Feng, Huang Yuan, Sun Shen, A Jia, Yu Suoya, and others.

At the meeting, Obraztsov briefly introduced the development of Soviet theater and Soviet puppet theater. He praised the achievements of Chinese art, especially emphasizing the value of folk art. He performed nine programs, including "Moment," "I Want to Stay with You for a While," "Tiger Taming," "Lullaby," and "Wine Pouring." Each program was warmly welcomed by the audience.

Chinese folk artist Yan Fengwu performed Chinese puppet shows "Sisters Playing with Plates," "Wang Xiaoer Fighting the Tiger," and "Pigsy Seeks a Bride."

After the meeting, Obraztsov and Chinese puppet show performers took photos together as a souvenir.

### 中华全国戏剧工作者协会邀请奥布拉兹卓夫演木偶戏

1952-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国戏剧工作者协会邀请奥布拉兹卓夫演木偶戏
    【新华社十三日讯】为了更好地学习苏联木偶戏，中华全国戏剧工作者协会十三日上午邀请苏联木偶戏剧院艺术指导、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者奥布拉兹卓夫表演木偶戏。表演会由中央戏剧学院副院长张庚主持，到会的有首都戏剧、美术、音乐工作者吴雪、王朝闻、华君武、赵渢、黄源、孙慎、阿甲、余所亚等三百余人。
    奥布拉兹卓夫在会上简短地介绍了苏联戏剧发展的情况和苏联木偶戏的情况。他盛赞中国艺术的成就，特别强调民间艺术的可贵。他表演了“瞬间”、“我想和你在一起呆一会儿”、“驯虎”、“摇篮曲”、“斟酒”等九个节目。每一个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    中国民间艺人阎凤舞表演了中国木偶戏“姊妹耍盘子”、“王小二打虎”和“八戒招亲”。
    会后，奥布拉兹卓夫和中国木偶戏演员摄影留念。
