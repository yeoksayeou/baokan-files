Title: Guangxi Province Prepares to Establish Zhuang Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1952-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 23rd: Guangxi Province is currently intensifying its preparations for the establishment of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region. The Zhuang people are the largest ethnic group in Guangxi Province after the Han, with a population of over 5.4 million. The vast majority are concentrated in the western part of Guangxi Province.

The Preparatory Committee for the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region was established on October 9th, consisting of 27 members, including representatives from the Zhuang, Han, Miao, Dong, and Yao ethnic groups, as well as people from all walks of life. The Chairman of the Preparatory Committee is Qin Yingji (Zhuang), and the Vice-Chairmen are Zhao Zhuoyun (Han) and Xie Hechou (Zhuang). The Preparatory Committee has an office to handle daily affairs. The current central tasks of the Preparatory Committee are: to draft a work plan and various rules and regulations for the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, to plan the convocation of the first People's Representative Conference of all ethnic groups and all circles in the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and other related preparatory work.

The list of members of the Preparatory Committee for the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the work plan of the Preparatory Committee for the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region have been approved by the Guangxi Provincial People's Government. The work plan of the Preparatory Committee for the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region proposes: First, to convene the People's Representative Conference of all ethnic groups and all circles in the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, to establish the People's Government of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region (equivalent to an administrative office level), and to elect the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and committee members of the Autonomous Region People's Government. Once the People's Government of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region is established, it will then, according to the specific situation, systematically and plannedly assist other minority areas within the Autonomous Region to implement ethnic regional autonomy at the county and district levels or establish ethnic democratic joint governments.

### 广西省筹备成立僮族自治区

1952-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    广西省筹备成立僮族自治区
    【新华社南宁二十三日电】广西省现正加紧进行成立桂西僮族自治区的各项筹备工作。僮族是广西省各族中人口仅次于汉族的一个民族，共有五百四十多万人。绝大部分都聚居在广西省西部地区。
    桂西僮族自治区筹备工作委员会是十月九日成立的，委员会由二十七人组成。包括僮、汉、苗、侗、瑶各民族代表及各界人士。筹备会主任委员为覃应机（僮族）、副主任委员赵卓云（汉族）、谢鹤筹（僮族）。筹委会下设办公室，办理日常工作事宜。筹委会当前的中心工作是：拟定桂西僮族自治区工作方案及各种章则草案，筹划召开桂西僮族区首届各族各界人民代表会议及其他有关准备工作。
    桂西僮族自治区筹备工作委员会名单及桂西僮族自治区筹备工作委员会工作方案已经广西省人民政府批准。桂西僮族自治区筹备工作委员会工作方案中提出：首先召开桂西僮族自治区各族各界人民代表会议，成立桂西僮族自治区人民政府（相当于行署级），选举自治区人民政府正副主席及委员会委员。一俟桂西僮族自治区人民政府成立后，再按照具体情况有步骤有计划地帮助本自治区内其他少数民族聚居区实行县、区级的民族区域自治或建立民族民主联合政府。
