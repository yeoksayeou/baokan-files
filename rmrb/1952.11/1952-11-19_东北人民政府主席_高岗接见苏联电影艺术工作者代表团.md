Title: Northeast People's Government Chairman Gao Gang Receives Soviet Film Artists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 18th. Northeast People's Government Chairman Gao Gang received the head of the Soviet Film Artists Delegation, Fedorov, and delegation members Radinina, Cherkasov, Petrov, Smirnova, and Pavlov at 6:00 PM on the 18th. The Soviet Deputy Consul General in Shenyang, Vasilyev, Deputy Consul Kachilov, and the Northeast Representative of the Soviet Film Export Company, Shavin, were present as guests. Also present were Northeast People's Government Vice Chairman Lin Feng, Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association Vice President Liu Zhiming, Chinese People's Liberation Army Northeast Military Region Deputy Political Commissar Zhou Huan, and Northeast Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Zhang Weizhen.

### 东北人民政府主席  高岗接见苏联电影艺术工作者代表团

1952-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    东北人民政府主席
    高岗接见苏联电影艺术工作者代表团
    【新华社沈阳十八日电】东北人民政府主席高岗在十八日下午六时，接见苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫和团员拉迪尼娜、契尔柯夫、彼得罗夫、斯米尔诺娃、巴甫洛夫。出席作陪的有：苏联驻沈阳总领事馆副总领事华西列夫、副领事卡奇洛夫，苏联影片输出公司驻东北代表沙文。出席作陪的还有：东北人民政府副主席林枫、东北中苏友好协会副会长刘芝明，中国人民解放军东北军区副政治委员周桓，东北总工会主席张维桢。
