Title: Soviet Art Troupe Continued Performance Last Night; Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman Gao Gang, and Premier Zhou Enlai Attended
Author:
Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a performance of famous Soviet artists at 8 p.m. on the 11th, with the Soviet Art Troupe, led by Turalaki, Vice-Chairman of the Art Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, performing wonderful programs. Over 1,400 women and young people from the capital were in the audience.

    Vice-Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Gao Gang, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government Nie Rongzhen, and Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua attended the performance.

    The leader of the Soviet Art Troupe, Turalaki, was welcomed by the entire audience when he appeared at the venue.

    Each program was warmly welcomed and praised by the audience. Soviet People's Artist, Stalin Prize winner, and famous ballet dancer Ulanova and Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize winner Kondratov performed the classical dance "Serenade," composed by Schumann. This performance was particularly well-received by the audience. Ulanova and Kondratov took four curtain calls and, at the audience's request, performed a section of "Serenade" again.

    Soviet People's Artist, famous singer, and Stalin Prize winner Zoya Gaidai sang an interlude from the opera "Madame Butterfly" and "Oh, Moon, Don't Shine," composed by Lysenko, earning prolonged applause from the entire audience. After repeated curtain calls, she sang "At the Far Village Entrance," composed by Budashkin.

    People's Artist of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize winner Beybutov sang "My Soviet Azerbaijan," composed by Kuliyev, and "Song of the Cotton Picker," composed by Rustamov. After four curtain calls, he also sang the song from the Soviet film "The Peddler and the Lady," which had been shown in China, and sang the Chinese folk song "In That Distant Place" in Chinese, earning warm applause from the audience.

    People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic Sergei Obraztsov performed the puppet shows "I Want to Be With You for a While" and "Lullaby." Under repeated applause, he also performed "Come Back! I Forgive You" and "Drinking Song."

    Soviet famous violinist and international competition winner Kaveldzneva played Khachaturian's "Dance" and Tchaikovsky's "Valse Scherzo," and finally, at the request of the enthusiastic applause of the audience, also played Rimsky-Korsakov's "Flight of the Bumblebee."

    Actors Sokolova and Pomazkov of the Moscow Operetta Theatre performed a pas de deux, "Ribbon Waltz," composed by Dunayevsky. Actors Karachova and Puchin of the Moscow State Circus performed rhythmic gymnastics, and actor Chitoshvili of the Moscow State Circus performed wonderful magic. These programs earned enthusiastic applause from the audience again and again.

    The performance began at 8 p.m. and lasted for two hours and forty minutes. The atmosphere throughout the venue was extremely warm. After each program, young pioneers presented flowers to the Soviet artists to pay their respects. After the performance, the Soviet artists lined up to express their gratitude to the audience, and the entire audience stood up and applauded.

### 苏联艺术工作团昨晚继续表演  朱德宋庆龄李济深高岗副主席和周恩来总理等到场观看

1952-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术工作团昨晚继续表演
    朱德宋庆龄李济深高岗副主席和周恩来总理等到场观看
    【新华社十一日讯】中苏友好协会总会十一日晚八时举行苏联著名艺术家表演会，由苏联部长会议艺术委员会副主席楚拉基所领导的苏联艺术工作团表演精彩节目。观众有首都的妇女和青年共一千四百多人。
    中央人民政府副主席朱德、宋庆龄、李济深、高岗，中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻和总政治部副主任萧华都到场观看。
    苏联艺术工作团的领导人楚拉基在会场出现时，受到全体观众的欢迎。
    演出的每一项节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎和赞赏。苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者、著名巴蕾舞演员乌兰诺娃和俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员、斯大林奖金获得者康德拉托夫表演了古典舞舒曼作曲的“小夜曲”。这一表演特别受到了全场的热烈欢迎。乌兰诺娃和康德拉托夫四次谢幕；最后接受了观众的请求，再次表演了“小夜曲”中的一段。
    苏联人民演员、著名歌唱家、斯大林奖金获得者卓娅·盖达伊演唱了歌剧“蝴蝶夫人”中的插曲和雷辛柯作曲的“哎，月儿别放光啊”，博得全场长久的鼓掌，在一再谢幕后又演唱了布达什金作曲的“在遥远的村口”。
    阿塞拜疆加盟共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者裴布托夫演唱了库里耶夫作曲的“我的苏维埃阿塞拜疆”，鲁斯塔莫夫作曲的“采棉女之歌”。在四次谢幕之后，他又演唱了在中国放映过的苏联电影“货郎与小姐”中的歌曲，并用中国话演唱了中国民歌“在那遥远的地方”，博得观众的热烈欢迎。
    俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员谢尔盖·奥布拉兹卓夫表演了木偶戏“我想和你在一起呆一会儿”和“摇篮曲”。他也在一再的鼓掌下又表演了“回来吧！我饶恕你”和“斟酒歌”。
    苏联名提琴家、国际竞赛获奖者卡威尔兹涅娃演奏了哈查比梁作曲的“舞曲”和柴可夫斯基作曲的“圆舞曲谐谑曲”，最后并在全场热情的掌声的请求下，又演奏了李姆斯基、柯尔萨科夫作曲的“野蜂飞舞”。
    莫斯科歌舞剧院演员索科洛娃、波马兹科夫表演了双人舞杜纳耶夫斯基作曲的“丝带圆舞曲”。莫斯科国家杂技团演员卡拉巧娃和普奇岑表演了柔软体操，莫斯科国家杂技团演员契托希维利表演了精彩的魔术。这些节目都博得观众一次又一次的热烈鼓掌。
    表演会从八时开始，历时二时四十分。全场情绪始终极为热烈。每一个节目结束后，少年儿童队员向苏联艺术家们献花致敬。节目演毕，苏联的演员们列队向观众表示谢意，全场起立鼓掌。
