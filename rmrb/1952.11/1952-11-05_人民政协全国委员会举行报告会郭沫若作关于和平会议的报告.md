Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Report Meeting; Guo Moruo Reports on Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-11-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) invited Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation to the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference, on the afternoon of November 1st to give a report on the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference. More than 2,200 people attended the report meeting, including Standing Committee members and members of the CPPCC National Committee, staff members of various democratic parties and people's organizations, representatives of ethnic minorities from various regions, presidents and professors of various universities, religious figures from Beijing, as well as heads and cadres of various departments of the Central People's Government, and senior officers of the People's Liberation Army.

### 人民政协全国委员会举行报告会郭沫若作关于和平会议的报告

1952-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会举行报告会郭沫若作关于和平会议的报告
    【新华社四日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在一日下午邀请中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表团副团长郭沫若作关于亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的报告。参加报告会的，有人民政协全国委员会常务委员、委员，各民主党派、各人民团体的工作人员，各地少数民族代表，各高等学校校长、教授，北京宗教界人士，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和干部，人民解放军高级军官等共二千二百余人。
