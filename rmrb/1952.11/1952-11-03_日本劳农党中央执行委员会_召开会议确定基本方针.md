Title: Central Executive Committee of the Japan Labour-Farmer Party Holds Meeting to Determine Basic Policy
Author:
Date: 1952-11-03
Page: 4

    Japan Labour-Farmer Party Central Executive Committee
    Holds Meeting to Determine Basic Policy
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st] The Central Executive Committee of the Labour-Farmer Party held a meeting on October 20th. The meeting once again confirmed the following basic policies of the Labour-Farmer Party: to promote the conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty with Japan, abolish the San Francisco Treaty and the US-Japan "Security Treaty," oppose rearmament, uphold the peace clause of the Constitution, etc.

### 日本劳农党中央执行委员会  召开会议确定基本方针

1952-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本劳农党中央执行委员会
    召开会议确定基本方针
    【新华社一日讯】东京消息：劳农党中央执行委员会于十月二十日召开会议。会议再一次确定了下列劳农党基本方针：促进缔结全面对日和约，废除旧金山和约与美日“安全条约”，反对重整军备，拥护宪法和平条款等等。
