Title: Government Administration Council North China Administrative Committee Invites Tikhonov to Give a Report; Tikhonov, Chulaki, and Others Visit the Palace Museum

Author: 
Date: 1952-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th:

    The Government Administration Council North China Administrative Committee
    Invites Tikhonov to Give a Report
    Tikhonov, Chulaki, and Others Visit the Palace Museum

    [Xinhua News Agency, November 14th] Tikhonov, head of the Soviet cultural workers delegation and Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, gave a report at the open-air auditorium of the North China People's Revolutionary University on the 13th at the invitation of the Government Administration Council North China Administrative Committee. More than 6,400 cadres and students from ten units, including the Government Administration Council North China Administrative Committee, the Marx-Lenin Institute, the Renmin University of China, Peking University, Tsinghua University, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the North China Bureau Party School of the CCP Central Committee, the North China People's Revolutionary University, the Foreign Languages ​​School, and the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, listened to the report.

    The report meeting was presided over by Liu Xiufeng, Deputy Director of the Government Administration Council North China Administrative Committee. The report meeting began at 10:30 a.m. Liu Xiufeng delivered a welcoming speech first. Then, Tikhonov gave a report. Tikhonov first explained that there is a deep friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and this friendship has a long history. Tikhonov then said that the Soviet people pay great attention to every event in China. He said that when he set off from Moscow to China, thousands of Soviet people instructed him to say hello to the Chinese people on their behalf. This is not an ordinary courtesy, but an expression of true friendship. Tikhonov said that the people of China and the Soviet Union are both very talented, with great souls and generosity. The people of China and the Soviet Union both have a love for their motherland and a great spirit of internationalism, and they both love peaceful labor. These common points are the basis for the close unity between the people of China and the Soviet Union. Tikhonov introduced the great achievements of Soviet communist construction, and he said that the Chinese people will also build their motherland very well. Tikhonov's report gave great encouragement to everyone who listened to the report, and warm applause rang out throughout the venue. At the end of the report, the audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically, shouting:
    "Long live Comrade Stalin!" "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" "Long live the unbreakable great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union!"

    [Xinhua News Agency, November 14th] Tikhonov, head of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, Chulaki, leader of the Soviet art troupe, and seventeen members of the art troupe visited the Palace Museum on the 14th. Zheng Zhenduo, Director of the Social and Cultural Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Wang Tianmu, Deputy Director of the Second Division, and Chen Qiao, Director of the Palace Museum, accompanied the visit.

    Tikhonov, Chulaki, and members of the art troupe visited the antiques and artworks from prehistoric times to the Ming Dynasty displayed in the Hall of Supreme Harmony, the cultural relics and artworks of the Qing Dynasty displayed in the Hall of Preserving Harmony, as well as the Ceramics Gallery, Zhongcui Palace, and so on. They carefully watched and inquired from one display cabinet to another and from one exhibition room to another. They greatly admired the Chinese cultural relics and artworks. Tikhonov and Chulaki wrote their impressions in the Palace Museum's commemorative book.

    Tikhonov wrote:
    "I cannot help but be deeply amazed by the precious works created by ancient Chinese artists! These works vividly prove that when Europeans were still drinking blood, China already had a high degree of civilization. I am very excited because today these highly artistic works have become the property of all the Chinese people.
    This unparalleled people's museum in the world will always remain in my memory.
    I sincerely wish Chinese archaeologists and scientists continuous new achievements to enrich the people's museum!"

    Chulaki then wrote: "I would like to add that we are particularly pleased with the part of the Palace Museum's collection that Comrade Zheng Zhenduo personally donated to the museum."

### 政务院华北行政委员会  邀请吉洪诺夫作报告  吉洪诺夫和楚拉基等参观故宫博物院

1952-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院华北行政委员会
    邀请吉洪诺夫作报告
    吉洪诺夫和楚拉基等参观故宫博物院
    【新华社十四日讯】苏联文化工作者代表团团长、苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫十三日应政务院华北行政委员会邀请，在华北人民革命大学露天礼堂作报告。听报告的有政务院华北行政委员会、马克思列宁学院、中国人民大学、北京大学、清华大学、中央民族学院、中共中央华北局党校、华北人民革命大学、外国语学校、华北农业科学研究所等十个单位工作干部和学生共六千四百多人。
    报告会由政务院华北行政委员会副主任刘秀峰主持。上午十时半报告会开始，刘秀峰首先致欢迎词。接着，由吉洪诺夫作报告。吉洪诺夫首先说明中苏两国人民之间有着深厚的友谊，这种友谊有它悠久的历史。吉洪诺夫接着说，苏联人民以极大的注意关怀中国的每一事变。他说，当他自莫斯科启程来中国时，成千成万苏联人民向他嘱咐，要他代他们向中国人民问好。这并不是普通应酬，而是表示真正友好。吉洪诺夫说，中苏两国人民都是非常有才干的，具有伟大的灵魂和慷慨大量。中苏两国人民都有热爱祖国的感情和伟大的国际主义精神，他们都爱好和平劳动，这些共同之点，就是中苏两国人民能够亲密团结的基础。吉洪诺夫介绍了苏联共产主义建设的巨大成就，他说中国人民同样会把自己的祖国建设得非常美好。吉洪诺夫的报告，给每个听报告的人以极大的鼓舞，全场不断响起热烈的掌声。报告结束时，全场起立热烈鼓掌，高呼：
    “斯大林同志万岁！”“毛泽东主席万岁！”“中苏两国人民牢不可破的伟大友谊万岁！”
    【新华社十四日讯】苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫、苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基和艺术工作团部分团员共十七人，十四日参观故宫博物院。陪同参观的有：中央人民政府文化部社会文化事业管理局局长郑振铎、第二处副处长王天木、故宫博物院院长陈乔等。
    吉洪诺夫、楚拉基和艺术工作团团员参观了太和殿内陈列的从史前期到明代的古物和艺术品、保和殿内陈列的清代文物艺术品以及陶瓷馆、钟粹宫等。他们从一个陈列柜到另一个陈列柜，从一个陈列室到另一个陈列室，详细地观看着和询问着。他们对中国的文物艺术品极为赞美。吉洪诺夫和楚拉基在故宫博物院的纪念册上写下观后感。
    吉洪诺夫写道：
    “我对于中国古代艺术家们所创造出的珍贵作品，不能不深为惊叹！这些作品生动地证明了，当欧洲人还处于茹毛饮血的时代，中国就已经有了高度的文明。我感到非常兴奋，因为今天这些艺术高超的作品已经成为全中国人民所有。
    这个全世界无与伦比的人民的博物院，将永远留在我的记忆中。
    谨祝中国的考古家和科学家们不断取得新的成就，使人民的博物院更加充实起来！”
    楚拉基接着写道：“我要补充的是，故宫陈列的物品中，郑振铎同志个人赠送给博物院的部分，使我们特别感到满意。”
