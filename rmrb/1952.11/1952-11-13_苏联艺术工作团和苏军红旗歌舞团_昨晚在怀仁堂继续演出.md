Title: The Soviet Art Troupe and the Soviet Red Banner Song and Dance Ensemble Continued Their Performance at Huairentang Last Night
Author: 
Date: 1952-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The performance of famous Soviet artists, sponsored by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, continued at Huairentang at 8 p.m. on December 12th. The Soviet Art Troupe, led by Tsuraki, Vice Chairman of the Arts Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and the Soviet Red Banner Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by B.A. Alexandrov, performed wonderful programs. Over 1,500 people watched the performance, including Soviet experts in Beijing, working cadres of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and representatives of ethnic minorities in Beijing.

The first part of the program was performed by the Soviet Art Troupe. The program included "Prelude" and "Polonaise" performed by Emelyanova, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and pianist; "Dear Stalin," "Oh, Moon, Don't Shine" and other songs, as well as an interlude from the opera "Madame Butterfly" sung by Gaidai, People's Artist of the Soviet Union and Stalin Prize winner; the ballet "Hunter and Little Bird" performed by ballet dancers Sokolova and Pomazkov of the Moscow Opera and Ballet Theatre; "Dance" and "Valse Scherzo" performed by Kaverzeneva, international competition winner and violinist; the puppet show "I Want to Stay with You for a While," "Lullaby," and "Tiger Taming" performed by Obraztsov, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner, and artistic director of the Puppet Theatre; soft gymnastics performed by Karakchaeva and Putchitsyn of the Moscow State Circus; and "Russia," "My Soviet Azerbaijan" and other songs sung by Bebtov, People's Artist of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize winner.

The outstanding and exquisite performances of the Soviet artists were warmly welcomed by the Chinese and Soviet audiences present. After each performance, the actors had to take repeated bows amidst the audience's applause. The ballet dancers Sokolova and Pomazkov took bows as many as seven times. At the enthusiastic request of the audience, Gaidai also performed the Ukrainian comic song "Jegula", Kaverzeneva added "Flight of the Bumblebee" composed by Rimsky-Korsakov, Pebtov added an interlude from the film "The Peddler and the Lady" and sang the Chinese folk song "In That Faraway Place" in Chinese, Obraztsov added the puppet show "Wine Pouring", and Karakchaeva and Putchitsyn also performed another soft gymnastics routine. All these encore performances were very wonderful, and the audience responded with warm applause again.

After a fifteen-minute break, the second part of the performance began, performed by the Soviet Red Banner Song and Dance Ensemble. The artistic director and general conductor of the performance was B.A. Alexandrov, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize winner, the general director of dance was Vilersky, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize winner, the general director of chorus was Vinogradov, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and Stalin Prize winner, the conductor of the band was V.A. Alexandrov, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, and the announcer was Kuznetsov. The first program of the performance was "Song of Stalin," led by Gerasimov, Pechiev, Sergeev, Moskin, and Ivanov. The second program was "The East is Red," led by Putchkov and Sergeev. The third program was "Moscow-Beijing," a solo sung by Gerasimov. Then came: the chorus "The Sun Sets" (marching song), Putchkov's solo "Song of the Mayor," the chorus "Dream Song," Savchuk's solo "Red Berries" (Russian folk song), "Russian Dance," "Volga Boatmen's Song," Lazhumov, Honored Artist of the Russian Republic's solo "Song of the Young Pioneers," "Echo," Nikitin, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic's solo "The Flowers are Good in the Spring Garden" and "Blue Flower," as well as "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army," "Marching Rest" (soldier's dance), and a total of fifteen programs. They were warmly welcomed by the audience.

After each performance, the applause from the audience lasted for a long time, and the performers of the Soviet Red Banner Song and Dance Ensemble took several bows, but still could not stop the audience's applause. "The East is Red," "Moscow-Beijing," "The Sun Sets," "Red Berries," "Volga Boatmen's Song," as well as "Song of the Young Pioneers," "Echo," and "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" and other programs were performed again at the enthusiastic request of the audience. After singing the Shaanbei folk song "Blue Flower" in Chinese, Nikitin added the famous Chinese song "In February."

After the Soviet Red Banner Song and Dance Ensemble finished performing, dancers from the capital presented flowers to the performers, led by B.A. Alexandrov. All the audience stood up, and applause resounded throughout the hall.

The performance ended at 11:45 p.m.

### 苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团  昨晚在怀仁堂继续演出

1952-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团
    昨晚在怀仁堂继续演出
    【新华社十二日讯】中苏友好协会总会所主办的苏联著名艺术家表演会，于十二日晚八时在怀仁堂继续举行。由苏联部长会议艺术委员会副主席楚拉基领导的苏联艺术工作团和以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团表演了精彩的节目。观看这次表演的有在京苏联专家、各民主党派和人民团体的工作干部以及在京的少数民族代表，共一千五百余人。
    表演节目的第一部分，是由苏联艺术工作团演出。节目中有俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员、钢琴家叶美里扬诺娃演奏的“前奏曲”“波兰舞曲”；苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者盖达伊演唱的“亲爱的斯大林”、“哎，月儿别放光啊”等歌曲以及歌剧“蝴蝶夫人”中的一段插曲；巴蕾舞演员、莫斯科歌舞剧院演员索科洛娃、波马兹科夫表演的巴蕾舞“猎人与小鸟”；国际比赛得奖者、提琴家卡威尔兹涅娃演奏的“舞曲”、“圆舞曲谐谑曲”；俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者、木偶戏剧院艺术指导奥布拉兹卓夫表演的木偶戏“我想和你在一起呆一会儿”、“摇篮曲”、“驯虎”；莫斯科国家杂技团演员卡拉巧娃和普奇岑表演的柔软体操；阿塞拜疆加盟共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者裴布托夫演唱的“俄罗斯”、“我的苏维埃阿塞拜疆”等歌曲。
    苏联艺术家卓越精湛的演技，受到在场的中苏两国观众的热烈欢迎。每一个节目演出后，演员们都要在观众的一再鼓掌声中频频谢幕。巴蕾舞演员索科洛娃和波马兹科夫曾谢幕七次之多。在观众热烈要求下，盖达伊又加演了乌克兰滑稽歌曲“杰古拉”，卡威尔兹涅娃加演了李姆斯基·柯尔萨科夫作曲的“野蜂飞舞”，裴布托夫加唱了影片“货郎与小姐”中的一段插曲，并用中国话演唱了中国民歌“在那遥远的地方”，奥布拉兹卓夫加演了木偶戏“斟酒”，卡拉巧娃和普奇岑也再次表演了一段柔软体操。所有这些加演节目都十分精彩，全场观众再度报以热烈的掌声。
    休息十五分钟以后，进行表演会的第二部分，由苏军红旗歌舞团表演。表演的艺术指导兼总指挥是俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫，舞蹈总指导是俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋艺术家、斯大林奖金获得者维尔斯基，合唱总指导是俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋艺术家、斯大林奖金获得者维诺格拉陀夫，乐队指挥是俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员符·亚·亚历山大罗夫，报幕是库兹聂卓夫。表演的第一个节目是“斯大林之歌”，由格拉西莫夫、裴契叶夫、谢尔格叶夫、莫斯金、伊凡诺夫领唱。第二个节目是“东方红”，由普奇柯夫、谢尔格叶夫领唱。第三个节目是格拉西莫夫独唱“莫斯科—北京”。接着是：合唱“太阳落山”（行军歌），普奇柯夫独唱“镇长之歌”，合唱“梦想曲”，萨夫楚克独唱“红莓果”（俄罗斯民歌），“俄罗斯舞”，“伏尔加船夫曲”，俄罗斯共和国功勋演员拉茹莫夫独唱“青年团员之歌”，“回声”，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员尼基丁独唱“春天的花园花儿好”和“蓝花花”，以及“中国人民解放军进行曲”、“行军休息”（士兵舞）等，共计十五个节目。受到观众热烈欢迎。
    每个节目演出之后，全场掌声经久不息，苏军红旗歌舞团表演者几次谢幕，仍然不能中止观众的掌声。“东方红”、“莫斯科——北京”、“太阳落山”、“红莓果”、“伏尔加船夫曲”，以及“青年团员之歌”、“回声”、“中国人民解放军进行曲”等节目，都在观众的热烈要求下再次表演。尼基丁在用中国话演唱了陕北民歌“蓝花花”后，又加唱了中国名歌“二月里来”一曲。
    苏军红旗歌舞团表演完毕后，首都舞蹈工作者向以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的表演者献花。全体观众起立，掌声响遍了全场。
    表演会至十一时四十五分始告结束。
