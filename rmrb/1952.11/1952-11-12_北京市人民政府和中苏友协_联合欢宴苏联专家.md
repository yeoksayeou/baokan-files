Title: Beijing Municipal People's Government and Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Jointly Host Soviet Experts
Author:
Date: 1952-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: To celebrate the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and the opening of "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," the Beijing Municipal People's Government and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly hosted a banquet for Soviet experts working in Beijing on the evening of the 8th. A grand celebration party was also held in the evening.

Invited to attend the banquet and celebration party were Yegorov, Deputy Director of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, Tuman-Fukaiya, an expert in municipal construction, and Soviet medical experts and medical personnel from the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital.

The banquet and celebration party were presided over by Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Liu Ren, Sun Xiaocun, Cao Jinghua, and Wang Zhixiang, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Present as guests were Qian Duansheng and Jiang Guangnai, Vice Chairmen of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Standing Committee, and heads of various people's organizations.

At the celebration party, Wu Han delivered a speech first, on behalf of the Beijing Municipal People's Government and the people of the capital, congratulating the Soviet experts on the great festival and thanking them for their efforts in accelerating the capital's production and construction. Yegorov also spoke at the meeting, warmly describing the friendly cooperation between Chinese and Soviet medical personnel at the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital. Sichuan opera and Jin opera were performed at the party to entertain the Soviet experts.

### 北京市人民政府和中苏友协  联合欢宴苏联专家

1952-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人民政府和中苏友协
    联合欢宴苏联专家
    【新华社十一日讯】为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年和“中苏友好月”揭幕，北京市人民政府和北京市中苏友好协会在八日下午联合欢宴现在北京市工作的苏联专家。晚间并举行了盛大的庆祝晚会。
    应邀参加宴会和庆祝晚会的有北京苏联红十字医院副院长耶果洛夫，市政建设专家土曼副卡娅和北京苏联红十字医院的苏联医学专家及医务人员。
    宴会和庆祝晚会由北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗和北京市中苏友好协会副会长张奚若、许德珩、刘仁、孙晓村、曹靖华、王之相主持。出席作陪的有北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席钱端升、蒋光鼐和各人民团体负责人。
    庆祝晚会首先由吴晗致词，他代表北京市人民政府和首都人民，向具体帮助首都各方面工作的苏联专家祝贺伟大的节日，并感谢他们在加速首都生产建设事业上所作的努力。耶果洛夫也在会上讲了话，他亲切地叙述了北京苏联红十字医院里中苏医务人员友好合作的情景。晚会演出川剧和晋剧，招待苏联专家。
