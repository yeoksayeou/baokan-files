Title: Preview of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Exhibition Held
Author:
Date: 1952-11-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd.—The preview of the “Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Exhibition,” sponsored by the China Peace Committee, was held in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park, Beijing, on the 22nd. Among those present were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, members of the China Peace Committee in Beijing, and about one hundred people from political, economic, educational, and literary circles in the capital.

Some delegates from Burma, Chile, Colombia, Guatemala, India, Indonesia, Khmer, Laos, Nicaragua, the United States, and Vietnam, who attended the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and are currently in Beijing, also attended the preview.

Guo Moruo presided over the preview. In his speech, he said: This exhibition displays gifts presented to China by the delegations of various countries. These gifts are exquisite works of art and handicrafts. Through these gifts, not only is the friendship of the people of various countries expressed, but also cultural exchanges between countries are conducted. He also said: The photographs of various countries presented to China in the exhibition are valuable materials for the Chinese people to understand the people of various countries; the photographs about the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in the exhibition are records of the conference. Finally, he expressed his gratitude to the delegations of various countries for presenting China with various gifts and photographs.

The exhibition will be open to the public for one month starting from the 24th. All the exhibits are divided into the following sections: gifts presented to China by the delegates of various countries, photographs of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, documents of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, photographs and propaganda materials presented to China by the delegates of various countries reflecting the struggles of the people of various countries to defend peace and the achievements of peaceful construction. The exhibition also displays more than 40,000 letters and telegrams from people from all over China congratulating the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arranged in the shape of the two characters "Peace". In these letters and telegrams, the Chinese people expressed their determination to defend peace.

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议展览会举行预展

1952-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议展览会举行预展
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主办的“亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议展览会”二十二日在北京中山公园中山堂举行预展会。出席预展会的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会在京委员、以及首都政治、经济、教育、文艺各界人士约一百多人。
    现尚留北京的缅甸、智利、哥伦比亚、危地马拉、印度、印度尼西亚、高棉、寮国、尼加拉瓜、美国、越南各国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的部分代表也出席了预展会。
    预展会由郭沫若主持，他在致词中说：这次展览会陈列了各国代表团送给中国的礼物，这些礼物都是精美的艺术品和工艺品。通过这些礼物，不仅表现了各国人民的友谊，而且进行了各国的文化交流。他又说：展览会陈列的各国送给中国的照片，是中国人民了解各国人民的宝贵的资料：展览会陈列的关于亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的照片，是大会的纪录。他最后表示感谢各国代表团送给中国以各种礼物和照片。
    展览会将从二十四日起公开展出一月，全部展览品分为下列几部门：各国代表送给中国的礼物，亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议照片，亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议文献，各国代表送给中国的反映各国人民为保卫和平而斗争和和平建设成就的照片和宣传品。展览会还陈列了排成“和平”两字的中国各地人民祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的四万多封函电，在这些函电中。中国人民表示了他们保卫和平的意志。
