Title: National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany Holds Meeting to Discuss the Issue of Striving for the Restoration of German National Unity
Author:
Date: 1952-11-18
Page: 4

    National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany Holds Meeting
    Discusses the Issue of Striving for the Restoration of German National Unity
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 17th] According to TASS, Berlin: The National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany held a meeting in Berlin to discuss the resolutions of the international conference for the peaceful resolution of the German question and the program put forward by the German Communist Party for striving for the restoration of national unity.
    Prominent representatives of the national resistance movement from West Germany and West Berlin, as well as guests from the German Democratic Republic, participated in the meeting, including Professor Eitel, Deputy Director of the German Academy of Sciences, and Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic.
    Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber (formerly translated as People's Council) of the German Democratic Republic, reported to the meeting on the work of the international conference for the peaceful resolution of the German question. West German parliamentarian Fischer gave a report on the German Communist Party's program for striving for the restoration of German national unity.
    During the discussion of these two reports, Dr. Correns (Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany), Professor Eitel, Dr. Gereke (Deputy Prime Minister of Lower Saxony, West Germany), Pastor Frarick, Kohn (General Secretary of the National Front of Democratic Germany), and Grotewohl all expressed their opinions.
    Speakers expressed firm opposition to the Bonn parliament's intention to ratify the Bonn General Treaty and the Paris War Agreements, and unanimously supported the resolutions of the international conference for the peaceful resolution of the German question and the program for striving for the restoration of German national unity.
    The National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany decided to take a series of measures to widely publicize the resolutions of the international conference for the peaceful resolution of the German question and the program for striving for the restoration of German national unity in both East and West Germany.
    The National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany published a statement in the newspaper supporting the program proposed by the German Communist Party for restoring German national unity, and reiterated its determination to fight against the Bonn War Treaty. The statement emphasized that the German Communist Party's program pointed out the path of peaceful resolution of the German question and the elimination of poverty and hunger to the people of West Germany. The statement called on all patriotic people in Germany to unite as one, victoriously complete the liberation struggle, and lead the country towards prosperity. The statement also called for widespread publicity of the resolutions of the international conference for the peaceful resolution of the German question and the program of the German Communist Party, paying particular attention to exposing the aggressive nature of American policy, raising vigilance, and strengthening the struggle against the Bonn War Treaty. The statement emphasized that only when all German patriots are united in the great democratic National Front of Democratic Germany can the German people become the masters of their own destiny.

### 民主德国全国阵线全国委员会举行会议  讨论争取恢复德国全国统一问题

1952-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国全国阵线全国委员会举行会议
    讨论争取恢复德国全国统一问题
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：民主德国全国阵线全国委员会在柏林举行会议，讨论了国际争取和平解决德国问题会议的决议以及德国共产党所提出的争取恢复全国统一的纲领。
    参加这次会议的有西德和西柏林参加民族抵抗运动的著名代表以及德意志民主共和国的来宾，包括：德国科学院副院长艾泰尔教授以及德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥。
    德意志民主共和国人民议院（前译人民议会）主席狄克曼向会议作了关于国际争取和平解决德国问题会议的工作的报告。西德议会议员费施就德国共产党提出的争取恢复德国全国统一的纲领作了一个报告。
    在讨论这两个报告的时候，柯伦斯博士（民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席）、艾泰尔教授、盖雷克博士（西德下萨克森州副总理）、弗拉里克牧师、科恩（民主德国全国阵线总书记）和格罗提渥都发表了意见。
    发言的人对于波恩议会打算批准波恩一般性条约和巴黎战争协定，都表示坚决反对，他们一致拥护国际争取和平解决德国问题会议的决议以及争取恢复德国全国统一的纲领。
    民主德国全国阵线全国委员会决定采取一系列的措施，在德国东西两部广泛宣传国际争取和平解决德国问题会议的决议以及争取恢复德国全国统一的纲领。
    民主德国全国阵线全国委员会在报纸上发表了声明，支持德国共产党所提出的恢复德国全国统一的纲领，同时重申它将坚决与波恩战争条约作斗争。声明强调指出，德国共产党的纲领向西德人民指出了和平解决德国问题、消除贫穷与饥饿的道路。声明号召德国一切爱国人民团结一心，胜利地完成解放斗争，并使国家走向繁荣。声明并号召广泛宣传国际争取和平解决德国问题的决议及德国共产党的纲领，特别注意揭发美国政策的侵略本质，提高警惕并加强反对波恩战争条约的斗争。声明强调指出，只有所有德国爱国人士都团结在伟大的民主德国全国阵线里，德国人民才能成为他们自己命运的主人。
