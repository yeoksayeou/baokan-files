Title: The Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea Returns Victoriously (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1952-11-26
Page: 6

Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand celebratory banquet in Pyongyang on the evening of October 25th to celebrate the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. Invited to attend were the delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the delegation of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, led by General Leader Liu Jingfan, and representatives of the general delegation.
(Draft from the "Liberation Army Pictorial" · Photo by Meng Zhaorui)

On October 26th, the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea paid respects to Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. General Leader Liu Jingfan, on behalf of all the Chinese people, extended his highest respect and cordial greetings to General Kim Il-sung and the Korean military and people under his leadership.
(Draft from the "Liberation Army Pictorial" · Photo by Meng Zhaorui)

On October 23rd, nineteen representatives of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, headed by Liu Jingfan, met with Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The picture shows Chairman Kim Tu-bong shaking hands cordially with General Leader Liu Jingfan.
(Draft from the "Liberation Army Pictorial" · Photo by Meng Zhaorui)

Zheng Enxi, combat hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers, recipient of the Second-Class Warrior Honor Medal, and others, asked Dong Quanzhong, father of model martyr Dong Cunrui, a representative of the delegation, to sign an autograph for them as a souvenir.
(Draft from the "Liberation Army Pictorial" · Photo by Wu Qun)

On October 28th, the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea visited the Korean-Chinese delegation participating in the Korean armistice talks in Kaesong. The picture shows the delegation presenting banners and gifts to the Korean-Chinese delegation for the armistice talks.
(Xinhua News Agency draft · Photo by Song Beihang)

Zhu Jihe (left), a sharpshooter hero of an anti-aircraft gun company of the Chinese People's Volunteers, who shot down and damaged seven American aircraft, and Yao Zhankuan, the company commander, admire the gifts brought to them by the delegation.
(Xinhua News Agency draft · Photo by Wang Quanzhi)

Heroes of a certain unit of the Volunteers present flowers to the members of the North China Sub-delegation of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea. (Photo by Chen Keng)

Qian Shanguang, Zhang Zhiquan, and other members of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, held a front-line symposium with Xie Xiumei and other combat heroes of the Volunteers.
(Photo by Zhang Jianchang)

Members of the delegation visited Park Chun-wol (second row, third from left), a heroine of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and a female train driver from Kangwon Province, Korea.
(Xinhua News Agency draft · Photo by Hua Min)

The Uyghur representatives of the cultural troupe of the Northwest Sub-delegation of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea performed the "Weaving Dance" for the Volunteer heroes on the Korean front line.
(Photo by Zhang Jianchang)

Members of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea paid tribute to martyr Luo Shengjiao.
(Draft from the "Liberation Army Pictorial" · Photo by Wu Qun)

Representatives of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea visit the Korean people.
(Draft from the "Liberation Army Pictorial" · Photo by Meng Zhaorui)

Representatives of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea inspect the large number of American weapons seized by the Korean People's Army.
(Xinhua News Agency draft · Photo by Lin Lin)

Zhao Lanzhang and other members of the cultural troupe of the North China Sub-delegation of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea sang Western River Drum and other programs for the heroes on the front lines.
(Photo by Chen Keng)

Members of the East China Sub-delegation of the delegation laid flowers at the cemetery of the martyrs of the Chinese People's Volunteers who heroically sacrificed themselves for the cause of resisting America, aiding Korea, and defending Asian and world peace in a certain place in Korea. (Xinhua News Agency draft · Photo by Zhang Chongxiu)

The Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea carried out extensive consolation activities on the front lines and in the rear in Korea. The picture shows the heroes of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers wearing new clothes and holding flowers, warmly welcoming the arrival of the motherland's people's delegation. (Xinhua News Agency draft · Photo by Zhang Chongxiu)

### 中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团胜利归来（图片）

1952-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团胜利归来
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁金日成首相，为庆祝中国人民志愿军入朝作战两周年，于十月二十五日晚在平壤举行盛大的庆祝宴会。应邀出席的有中国人民志愿军代表团及中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团刘景范总团长及总团的代表们。
    （解放军画报社稿·孟昭瑞摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团于十月二十六日向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成首相致敬。总团长刘景范代表全中国人民向金日成将军和他领导下的朝鲜军民，致以崇高的敬意和亲切的慰问。
    （解放军画报社稿·孟昭瑞摄）
    十月二十三日，中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团以刘景范为首的十九位代表晋见朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会金枓奉委员长。图为金枓奉委员长与刘景范总团长亲切地握手。
    （解放军画报社稿·孟昭瑞摄）
    中国人民志愿军战斗英雄、二级战士荣誉勋章获得者郑恩喜等，请慰问团代表、模范烈士家属董存瑞的父亲董全忠签名留念。
    （解放军画报社稿·吴群摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团曾于十月二十八日在开城慰问参加朝鲜停战谈判的朝中代表团。图为慰问团向停战谈判朝中代表团献旗献礼。
    （新华社稿·宋贝珩摄）
    击落和打伤美国飞机七架的中国人民志愿军某部高射炮连射击英雄朱继和（左）及连长姚占宽在欣赏慰问团带给他们的慰问品。    （新华社稿·王权致摄）
    志愿军某部英雄们向中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团华北分团团员们献花。（陈铿摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团团员钱善光、张志全等在朝鲜前线和志愿军战斗英雄解秀梅等举行战地座谈会。    （张健昌摄）
    慰问团团员们在访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国英雄、朝鲜江原道女火车司机朴春月（左起第二排第三人）。    （新华社稿·华敏摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团西北分团文工团的维吾尔族代表，在朝鲜战地给志愿军英雄们演出了“织布舞”。    （张健昌摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团团员们公祭罗盛教烈士。    （解放军画报社稿·吴群摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团的代表们访问朝鲜人民。    （解放军画报社稿·孟昭瑞摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团代表在参观朝鲜人民军所缴获的大批美国武器。
    （新华社稿·林麟摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团华北分团文工团团员赵兰彰等在战地给英雄们演唱西河大鼓等节目。
    （陈铿摄）
    慰问团华东分团的团员们在朝鲜某地向为抗美援朝、保卫亚洲及世界和平事业而英勇牺牲的中国人民志愿军烈士们的墓地献花。（新华社稿·张崇岫摄）
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团曾在朝鲜前线和后方展开广泛的慰问活动。图为中国人民志愿军某部英雄们穿着新衣，拿着鲜花，热烈地欢迎祖国人民慰问团的到来。（新华社稿·张崇岫摄）
