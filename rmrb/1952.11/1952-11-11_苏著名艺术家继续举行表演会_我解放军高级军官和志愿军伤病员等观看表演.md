Title: Renowned Soviet Artists Continue Performances; Senior Officers of Our People's Liberation Army and Wounded and Sick Veterans of the Volunteer Army Attend Performances
Author:
Date: 1952-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Led by Chulaki, the renowned Soviet artists continued their performances at Huairentang on the evening of the 9th. Attending the performance were senior officers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, literary and art workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers recuperating in Beijing, totaling more than 1,500 people.

At 8:00 PM, Tikhonov, head of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, accompanied by Xiao Hua, deputy director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, and Qian Junrui, secretary-general of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, entered the venue, and the audience stood up and applauded warmly.

Xiao Hua spoke on stage, expressing heartfelt gratitude for the performances of the Soviet art troupe and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble. He said: The Soviet art troupe and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble are organized by well-known art masters from all over the Soviet Union and even the world. Their performances in our troops will greatly promote the learning of the Soviet Union and the Soviet army by commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, so as to accelerate the construction of our country's modern and regular national defense army.

Amidst warm applause, Tikhonov was invited to speak. On behalf of the Soviet people, Soviet cultural workers, and the Soviet cultural workers delegation, he paid tribute to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers. He said: As a soldier who participated in the October Socialist Revolution, a soldier who participated in defeating the Finnish aggressors, a soldier who participated in the Great Patriotic War, and now a soldier fighting for peace, I salute you. And congratulate the Chinese People's Volunteers on achieving greater victories.

After Tikhonov finished speaking, a young soldier of the Chinese People's Volunteers and an actor from the Art Troupe of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission walked onto the stage with flowers and presented them to Tikhonov. At this moment, thunderous applause erupted throughout the venue. Tikhonov shook hands tightly with the Chinese People's Volunteers soldier who presented the flowers. The applause in the audience became even more enthusiastic, lasting for five minutes.

The performance officially began at 8:35.

Nietchayev, an actor from the Soviet Broadcasting Committee, Ulanova, a Soviet People's Artist and Stalin Prize winner, Kondratov, an Honored Artist of the Russian Republic and Stalin Prize winner, Kogan, an international violin competition winner, Mikhailov, a Soviet People's Artist and Stalin Prize winner, Halima Nasyrova, a Soviet People's Artist and Stalin Prize winner, Chitochvili, a member of the Moscow State Circus and magician, and Tamara Hanon, a People's Artist of the Uzbek Republic and Stalin Prize winner, successively gave outstanding performances, which were warmly welcomed by the audience. After each artist's performance, they accepted flowers from the audience and took curtain calls several times amidst the warm and lasting applause of the audience. The classical dance performed by Ulanova and Kondratov won enthusiastic cheers from the audience, and they performed it again at the enthusiastic request of the masses.

Nietchayev sang the Soviet song "In Ukraine" (i.e., "How Steel Was Tempered"), which is loved by our people, in Chinese. Halima Nasyrova, a Soviet People's Artist and Stalin Prize winner, sang "Ode to Mao Zedong". The performances of these two artists were warmly welcomed by the audience.

When the performance ended at about 11:00 PM, the renowned Soviet artists appeared on the stage, waving flowers to the audience. The entire audience stood up again, enthusiastically chanting "Stalin - Mao Zedong!" The thunderous applause lasted for a long time.

### 苏著名艺术家继续举行表演会  我解放军高级军官和志愿军伤病员等观看表演

1952-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏著名艺术家继续举行表演会
    我解放军高级军官和志愿军伤病员等观看表演
    【新华社十日讯】楚拉基领导的苏联著名艺术家们，九日晚间在怀仁堂继续举行了表演会。观看此次表演的，有中国人民解放军陆、空、海军高级军官，中国人民解放军文艺工作者以及在北京休养的中国人民志愿军伤病员等，共一千五百多人。
    八时正，苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫，由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞陪同步入会场，全场起立热烈鼓掌欢呼。
    萧华上台讲话，对苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团的演出表示衷心的感谢。他说：苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团都是由全苏以至全世界闻名的艺术大师所组织的，他们在我部队中的表演，将会大大地促进中国人民解放军指挥员、战斗员学习苏联，学习苏联军队，以加速我国现代化正规化国防军的建设。
    在热烈的鼓掌声中，吉洪诺夫应邀讲话。他代表苏联人民、苏联文化工作者、苏联文化工作者代表团，向中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军致敬。他说：我作为一个曾经参加十月社会主义革命的战士、曾经参加打垮芬兰侵略者的战士、曾经参加卫国战争的战士而现在又为为和平而奋斗的战士向你们致敬。并祝贺中国人民志愿军取得更大的胜利。
    吉洪诺夫讲话完毕后，中国人民志愿军的一位青年战士和人民革命军事委员会总政治部文工团演员，手拿着鲜花走到台上献给吉洪诺夫。这时，全场掌声雷动。吉洪诺夫和献花的中国人民志愿军战士紧紧地握手。全场掌声更趋热烈，持续达五分钟之久。
    在八点三十五分正式开始表演。
    苏联广播委员会演员聂恰耶夫，苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者乌兰诺娃，俄罗斯共和国功勋演员、斯大林奖金获得者康德拉托夫，提琴家国际竞赛获奖者柯岗，苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者米哈伊洛夫，苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者哈利马·纳赛罗娃，莫斯科国家杂技团团员、魔术家契托希维利，乌兹别克共和国人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者塔马拉·哈侬，先后作了杰出的表演，受到观众热烈欢迎。每个艺术家表演之后，都接受了观众的献花，并在观众热烈的持久的鼓掌声中谢幕数次。乌兰诺娃和康德拉托夫所表演的古典舞，博得全场热烈的欢呼，他们在群众热情的请求下，又作了一次表演。
    聂恰耶夫用中国话唱了为我国人民所喜爱的苏联歌曲“在乌克兰”（即“钢铁是怎样炼成的”），苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者哈利马·纳赛罗娃歌唱“歌颂毛泽东”。这两位艺术家的表演，都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    表演会在深夜十一时许结束时，苏联著名艺术家们出现在舞台上，手中挥舞着鲜花向观众致意。全场又一次地起立，热烈欢呼“斯大林——毛泽东！”雷动的掌声经久不息。
