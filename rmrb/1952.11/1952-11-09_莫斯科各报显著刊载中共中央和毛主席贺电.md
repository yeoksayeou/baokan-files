Title: Moscow Newspapers Prominently Publish Congratulatory Telegrams from the CPC Central Committee and Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1952-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. Moscow newspapers on the 7th prominently published the two congratulatory telegrams from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman Stalin of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. The newspapers also published news of China's commemoration of the October Revolution Day. The "Pravda" published a dispatch from Beijing on the 7th about the start of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." The "Izvestia" published Wu Yuzhang's article "The Powerful Manifestation of Sino-Soviet Friendship" on the 7th.

### 莫斯科各报显著刊载中共中央和毛主席贺电

1952-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报显著刊载
    中共中央和毛主席贺电
    【新华社莫斯科八日电】莫斯科各报七日都以显著地位刊载中国共产党中央委员会和我中央人民政府毛泽东主席致苏联共产党中央委员会和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林的两个贺电。各报还刊载了我国纪念十月革命节的消息。真理报七日刊载了“中苏友好月”在北京开始的通讯。消息报七日刊载吴玉章论文“中苏友好的强大表现”。
