Title: Soviet Consulates in Various Parts of China Hold Grand Celebration Receptions
Author:
Date: 1952-11-10
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency) Soviet Consuls General in Tianjin, Shenyang, Dalian, Shanghai, and Dihua, and the Consul in Guangzhou, held grand receptions on the 7th to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union.

    Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Matveyev, held a celebration reception on the afternoon of the 7th. Invited to the reception were: Tianjin Vice Mayors Wu De and Zhou Shutao, leaders of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Tianjin, and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 200 people. Polish Vice Consul in Tianjin, Boruniski, was also invited to the banquet.
    Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang Deputy Consul General Vasilyev and his wife held a celebration banquet on the evening of the 7th. Invited to the banquet were: Northeast People's Government Vice Chairmen Lin Feng and Gao Chongmin, Second Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Zhang Xiushan, Northeast Military Region Deputy Commander He Jinnian, Deputy Political Commissar Zhou Huan, and representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and labor models, totaling more than 120 people. Kim Kui-nam, representative of the Embassy of North Korea in China stationed in Shenyang, was also invited to the banquet. During the banquet, guests frequently toasted to the health of Stalin, the health of Mao Zedong, and the ever-consolidating friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.
    Soviet Consul General in Dalian, Morozov, and Deputy Consul General Deplov, held a grand reception, inviting Ouyang Qin, Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, Han Guang, Mayor of Lüda, Vice Mayors Li Lin and Yu Guying, and heads of various people's organizations and figures from cultural and scientific circles, more than 100 people in total, to the banquet. Representatives from the Soviet Command in Lushun were also invited.
    Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Shtel, held a grand reception at noon on the 7th. Invited to this grand event were: Wu Kejian, Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee, Shanghai Vice Mayors Pan Hannian, Fang Yi, and Sheng Pihua, Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, Guo Huaruo, Commander of the Songhu Garrison Headquarters of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Shu Tong, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Chen Pixian, Fourth Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and various circles, as well as combat heroes and labor models, totaling more than 400 people. The Czechoslovak Consul General in Shanghai, the Indian Consul General in Shanghai, and personnel from the Swiss, Swedish, and Danish embassies in China stationed in Shanghai were also invited to the event. At the banquet, guests repeatedly raised their glasses to congratulate the great victory of the Soviet people and the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.
    The celebration banquet of the Soviet Consulate General in Dihua was held on the evening of the 7th. Invited to the banquet were: Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Branch of the CPC Central Committee, Zhang Bangying, Third Secretary, Saifuddin, Fourth Secretary, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, Gao Jinchun, Vice Chairman, Wang Zhen, Acting Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other party, government, and military leaders, totaling more than 100 people. The Soviet Consulate General in Dihua also screened the Soviet film "The Unforgettable Year 1919" to entertain the guests.
    The Soviet Consulate in Guangzhou held a reception on the 7th to entertain leaders of various agencies, democratic parties, people's organizations, and troops in South China, Guangdong, and Guangzhou. The banquet was hosted by Golov, Acting Consul of the Soviet Consulate in Guangzhou. Guests and hosts parted on amicable terms after a delightful time.

### 苏联驻我国各地领事馆举行盛大庆祝酒会

1952-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国各地领事馆举行盛大庆祝酒会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：苏联驻天津、沈阳、大连、上海、迪化等市总领事和驻广州领事在七日分别举行盛大的酒会，庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年。
    苏联驻天津总领事马铁夫在七日下午举行庆祝酒会，应邀赴会的有：天津市人民政府副市长吴德、周叔弢，中国人民解放军驻津部队首长和各民主党派、各人民团体的代表等共二百余人。应邀赴宴的还有波兰驻津副领事博鲁尼斯基。
    苏联驻沈阳总领事馆副总领事华西列夫和夫人在七日晚举行庆祝宴会，应邀赴会的有：东北人民政府副主席林枫、高崇民，中共中央东北局第二副书记张秀山、东北军区副司令员贺晋年、副政治委员周桓和各民主党派、各人民团体代表及劳动模范等共一百二十多人。朝鲜驻华大使馆驻沈阳代表金贵南也应邀赴宴。席间宾主频频为斯大林的健康、毛泽东的健康以及中苏两国人民日益巩固的友谊干杯。
    苏联驻大连总领事莫罗卓夫、副总领事杰普洛夫举行盛大酒会，邀请中共旅大市委书记欧阳钦，旅大市人民政府市长韩光，副市长李林、于谷莺和各人民团体负责人、文化和科学界人士一百余人赴宴。苏联驻旅顺指挥部代表也应邀到会。
    苏联驻上海总领事施捷利在七日中午举行盛大酒会，应邀参加这一盛会的有：华东军政委员会秘书长吴克坚，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、方毅、盛丕华，外事处处长黄华，中国人民解放军华东军区淞沪警备区司令部司令员郭化若，中共中央华东局宣传部部长舒同，中共上海市委员会第四书记陈丕显，各民主党派、各人民团体和各界的代表及战斗英雄、劳动模范等共四百余人。捷克斯洛伐克驻沪总领事、印度驻沪总领事及瑞士、瑞典、丹麦等国驻华使馆的在沪人员也应邀到会。在宴会上，宾主一再举杯祝贺苏联人民的伟大胜利和中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊。
    苏联驻迪化总领事馆的庆祝宴会在七日晚举行，应邀参加宴会的有：中共中央新疆分局第一书记王恩茂、第三书记张邦英、第四书记赛福鼎，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉、副主席高锦纯，中国人民解放军新疆军区代司令员王震等党政军首长一百余人。苏联驻迪化总领事馆并放映苏联影片“难忘的一九一九年”招待客人。
    苏联驻广州领事馆于七日举行酒会，欢宴华南、广东和广州市各机关、民主党派、人民团体和部队的负责人。宴会由苏联驻广州市领事馆代领事戈洛夫主持。宾主尽欢始散。
