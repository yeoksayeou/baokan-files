    Our Great Motherland
    The Shuangyashan Coal Mine in Northeast China has adopted the Soviet "Donbas" automatic combined coal mining machine, which greatly reduces the physical labor of the miners and increases the output by two times compared to the past. This is Soviet expert Xizhuofu explaining the structure and operation method of the coal cutting machine to the miners underground.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北双鸭煤矿采用苏联“顿巴斯”自动联合采煤机，大大地减轻了矿工们的体力劳动，产量比过去增加两倍。这是苏联专家西卓夫在坑下给矿工讲解截煤机的构造和操纵方法。
