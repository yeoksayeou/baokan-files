Title: The National Committee of the CPPCC Study Committee Issues a Notice Calling for the Organization of All Circles to Study the Documents of the 19th Congress of the All-Union Communist Party

Author:
Date: 1952-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Study Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference issued a notice to the study committees of the provincial and municipal CPPCCs, requesting the organization of people from all walks of life to study the main speeches and reports of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The full text of the notice is as follows: Study committees of provincial and municipal CPPCCs:

    The convening of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) is a historically significant event in the world today. In order to enable people from all walks of life to understand its content and spirit, and to cooperate with the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" study, the committee has decided to organize people from all walks of life to study the main speeches and reports of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks). The Study Committee has put forward a preliminary plan for the purpose, content, method, mode, and time of this study. We hope that you will refer to this plan to formulate specific plans and lead people from all walks of life in their studies.

    1.  Study Objectives: To gain an understanding of the current international situation and trends; to understand the superiority of the Soviet socialist system and the impact of Soviet communist construction on the future of mankind; to understand China's position and role in the current world; to understand the role of Sino-Soviet friendship in the current international situation; and to understand the tasks we should undertake in the world.

    2.  Study Content: Focus on Stalin's speech at the 19th Congress of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and Malenkov's report on the work of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (especially its first part); speeches by Molotov, Beria, Mikoyan, etc. can be used as reference documents.

    3.  Study Methods: Should be linked to reality, such as linking it to examples of the sincere and selfless assistance provided by the Soviet Union to our country over the past three years, as well as examples of the development of international relations after the war, in order to gain a deeper understanding.

    4.  Study Modes: Different regulations can be made according to different objects, generally in the form of seminars and reports, and lectures can also be held as needed.

    5.  Study Time: Generally two months, but it can be appropriately shortened or extended depending on the circumstances.

    Please let us know if you have any comments on the above preliminary plan.

    After the completion of the above study, please report the progress of the study to us.

    Study Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    November 6, 1952

### 人民政协全国委员会学习委员会发出通知  号召组织各界学习联共第十九次代表大会文件

1952-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会学习委员会发出通知
    号召组织各界学习联共第十九次代表大会文件
    【新华社八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会向各省、市协商委员会学习委员会发出通知，要求在各界人士中组织对苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的主要演说和报告的学习。通知全文如下：各省、市协商委员会学习委员会：
    苏联共产党（布）第十九次代表大会的召开是目前世界中有历史意义的事件。为了使各界人士理解其内容和精神，并为了配合“中苏友好月”的学习，本会决定在各界人士中组织对苏联共产党（布）第十九次代表大会的主要演说和报告的学习。对此次学习的目的、内容、方法、方式和时间，学习委员会已提出初步方案，望你们参照这一方案制定具体计划，领导各界人士进行学习。
    一、学习目的：为了求得了解目前国际形势及趋势；了解苏联社会主义制度的优越性和苏联共产主义建设对人类前途的影响；了解中国在当前世界中的地位和作用；了解中苏友好在目前国际形势中的作用和了解我们在世界上应担负的任务。
    二、学习内容：以斯大林在联共（布）第十九次代表大会上的演说和马林科夫在苏联共产党（布）第十九次代表大会上所作的苏联共产党（布）中央委员会的报告为主（尤应着重其第一部分）；莫洛托夫、贝利亚、米高扬等的发言可作参考文件。
    三、学习方法：应联系实际，如联系三年来苏联对我国诚挚无私的帮助的实例以及战后国际关系发展的实例等，以求得到更深刻的认识。
    四、学习方式：可根据不同对象作不同的规定，一般以座谈、报告的方式进行，亦可根据需要举办讲座等。
    五、学习时间：一般以两个月为准，但得根据情况作适当的缩短或延长。
    你们对以上初步方案如有意见望告。
    上项学习结束后，望你们将学习进行情况报告我会。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会
    一九五二年十一月六日
