Title: KMT Revolutionary Committee and China National Democratic Construction Association Issue Notices, Mobilizing Members to Participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities
Author:
Date: 1952-11-06
Page: 1

    The KMT Revolutionary Committee and China National Democratic Construction Association Issue Notices
    Mobilizing Members to Participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang recently issued a notice calling on members of organizations at all levels to actively participate in various celebration activities of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month". The notice stated: As our great motherland is about to embark on large-scale economic and cultural construction, we must mobilize the people of the whole country to learn from the Soviet Union's advanced experience in various constructions, and seriously study the experience of the Soviet Union's five-year plans in industrial, agricultural science and technology, cultural education, and national defense construction. In order to coordinate and promote the victorious progress of the great movement of "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month", the notice requires local organizations to abide by the notice of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on October 27 regarding the participation of people from all walks of life in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities, and in accordance with the publicity points and celebration slogans promulgated by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, under the unified leadership of the local consultative committees, assist the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in carrying out all activities of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month". Local organizations should organize various report meetings and forums, and participate in gatherings held by local consultative committees. At the same time, under unified leadership, arrange the study of the documents of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
    [Also news] On November 3, the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association issued a supplementary notice to organizations at all levels regarding celebrating the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union and promoting the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month". It instructed organizations at all levels to complete the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" movement as a current political task. The content of the propaganda is: (1) The influence of the October Revolution on the Chinese revolution, and the great significance of commemorating the October Revolution; (2) The Soviet Union's consistent support and selfless assistance to the people of our country over the past thirty-five years; (3) The unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union and its relationship to peace in China, the Soviet Union, and the world; (4) It should be particularly emphasized that learning from the Soviet Union's advanced production experience is an important condition for the success of our country's economic and cultural construction. The Party's internal study of this movement is tentatively set for two months. The study documents are the documents of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, especially Stalin's "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" and Malenkov's report at the Congress.

### 民革和农工民主党发出通知  动员所属参加“中苏友好月”活动

1952-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    民革和农工民主党发出通知
    动员所属参加“中苏友好月”活动
    【新华社四日讯】中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会最近发出通知，号召各级组织成员积极参加“中苏友好月”各项庆祝活动。通知说：当我们伟大祖国即将进行大规模经济建设和文化建设的前夕，我们要动员全国人民向苏联学习各种建设的先进经验，认真研究苏联几个五年计划中的工业、农业科学技术、文化教育及国防建设的经验。为着配合和推动“中苏友好月”这一伟大运动胜利地进行，通知要求各地方组织应遵照人民政协全国委员会十月二十七日关于全国各界人士参加“中苏友好月”活动的通知，并根据中苏友好协会总会颁布的宣传要点和庆祝口号，在各地协商委员会统一领导之下，协助各地中苏友好协会进行“中苏友好月”一切活动。各地方组织应组织各种报告会、座谈会，并参加当地协商委员会所举行的集会。同时在统一领导下，布置学习苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的文件。
    【又讯】中国农工民主党中央委员会十一月三日向各级组织发出关于庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年推进“中苏友好月”的补充通知，指示各级组织应把“中苏友好月”运动，当作当前这一时期的政治任务去完成。宣传内容是：（一）十月革命给予中国革命的影响，以及纪念十月革命的伟大意义；（二）三十五年来苏联对我国人民的一贯的支持和无私的援助；（三）中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊对中苏两国与世界和平的关系；（四）应特别强调学习苏联先进生产经验是我国经济、文化建设成功的重要条件。关于这一运动的党内学习，暂定为两个月。学习文件为苏联第十九次代表大会文件，特别是斯大林著的“苏联社会主义经济问题”与马林科夫在大会上的报告。
