Title: Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Foreign Minister Vyshinsky
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 1

To Comrade Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, please accept my sincere congratulations, Comrade Minister. I wish the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy even greater victories, and I wish the close, unshakable, great friendship between China and the Soviet Union will be further consolidated and developed. This great friendship is the most reliable guarantee against new war provocations and for defending peace in the Far East and the world.

    Zhou Enlai
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    November 3, 1952, Beijing

### 周恩来外长致维辛斯基外长电
周恩来
1952-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长致维辛斯基外长电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部部长维辛斯基同志：
    值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年之际，部长同志，请接受我真挚的祝贺。祝苏联的和平外交政策获得更伟大的胜利，并祝中苏两国亲密的不可动摇的伟大友谊日益巩固与发展。这种伟大友谊是反对新战争挑拨和保卫远东及世界和平的极可靠的保证。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长  周恩来
    一九五二年十一月三日北京
