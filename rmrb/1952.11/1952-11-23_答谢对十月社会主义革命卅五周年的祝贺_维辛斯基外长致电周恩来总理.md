Title: Vyshinsky Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai in Reply to Congratulations on the 35th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1952-11-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Foreign Minister Vyshinsky of the Soviet Socialist Republics sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of China, in reply to his congratulations on the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The full text is as follows:
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    Comrade Premier and Minister, please accept my heartfelt thanks for your congratulations on the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.
    A. Vyshinsky
    Moscow, November 22, 1952

### 答谢对十月社会主义革命卅五周年的祝贺  维辛斯基外长致电周恩来总理

1952-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    答谢对十月社会主义革命卅五周年的祝贺
    维辛斯基外长致电周恩来总理
    【新华社二十二日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部部长维辛斯基电谢我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来对十月社会主义革命三十五周年的祝贺。全文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来同志：
    总理兼部长同志，当伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年之际，承致祝贺，请接受我衷心的谢意。
    安·维辛斯基
    一九五二年十一月二十二日于莫斯科
