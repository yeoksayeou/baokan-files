Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Adds Russian Language "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Program
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: To report on China's "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities to the Soviet Union and to express the Chinese people's desire for eternal friendship with the Soviet people and to learn from the Soviet Union, the Central People's Broadcasting Station has specifically added a "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month Program" broadcast in Russian to the Soviet Union from November 6th to December 6th. The content of this program includes speeches by people from all walks of life in China to the Soviet people, news reports about the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," and Chinese music and songs. The broadcast time is from 2:30 to 3:00 PM Beijing time every day. The frequencies are 11690 kHz, 15060 kHz, 7500 kHz, 6100 kHz, 6050 kHz, and 640 kHz.

### 中央人民广播电台增设俄语“中苏友好月”节目

1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台增设俄语“中苏友好月”节目
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民广播电台为向苏联报道我国“中苏友好月”活动和表达我国人民对苏联人民永久友好及向苏联学习的愿望，特定于十一月六日至十二月六日，增设以俄语对苏联广播的“中苏友好月节目”。这个节目的内容有我国各界人士对苏联人民的讲话、有关“中苏友好月”的新闻报道及中国音乐和歌曲。每天广播时间是北京时间两点半到三点。周率是一一六九○千周、一五○六○千周、七五○○千周、六一○○千周、六○五○千周、六四○千周。
