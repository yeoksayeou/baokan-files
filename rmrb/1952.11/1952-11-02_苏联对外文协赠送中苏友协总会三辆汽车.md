Title: Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Presents Three Cars to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

Author:
Date: 1952-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: The Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries today presented three cars to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. At 4:00 p.m., Gu Deff, Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Zhukoff, representative of the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Beijing, personally delivered the three cars to the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and many staff members of the headquarters greeted them in front of the office building. Chargé d'Affaires Gu Deff delivered a speech amidst warm applause, saying: "On behalf of the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, I present these three Soviet cars to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hoping that they will be helpful to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in carrying out its work." Secretary-General Qian Junrui then delivered a speech. He expressed his gratitude for the gift from the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. He said: "This gift comes at the beginning of the 'Sino-Soviet Friendship Month' campaign, which is of profound significance."
    Among the three cars, one is dark green, produced by the Molotov Automobile Factory in 1952, which can carry seven or eight people and runs smoothly and quickly. The two blue ones are Victory brand cars, which are a type of car that the Soviet people very much like to use on a daily basis.

### 苏联对外文协赠送中苏友协总会三辆汽车

1952-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文协赠送中苏友协总会三辆汽车
    【新华社一日讯】苏联对外文化协会今天赠送中苏友好协会总会三辆汽车。下午四时，苏联驻华大使馆代办顾德夫、苏联对外文化协会驻北京代表茹克福等亲送这三辆汽车到中苏友好协会总会。中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞和总会工作人员多人在总会办公大楼前迎接。顾德夫代办在热烈的欢迎掌声中致词说：我代表苏联对外文化协会把三辆苏联汽车赠送给中苏友好协会总会，希望它们对中苏友好协会开展工作有所帮助。钱俊瑞总干事接着致词。他对苏联对外文化协会的馈赠表示感谢。他说：这一馈赠正逢“中苏友好月”运动开始的时候，这是有很深刻的意义的。
    三辆汽车中有一辆深绿色的，是莫洛托夫汽车厂一九五二年出品，可以乘坐七、八人，行驶时平稳快速。两辆蓝色的是胜利牌汽车，它们是苏联人民日常很喜爱使用的一种汽车。
