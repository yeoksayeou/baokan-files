Title: Moscow Radio Station Changes Mandarin Program Broadcast Time
Author:
Date: 1952-11-03
Page: 4

    Moscow Radio Station
    Changes Mandarin Program Broadcast Time
    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch] Moscow Radio Station, to adapt to the change of seasons, has changed the broadcast time of its Mandarin programs starting from October 24th, and will broadcast programs about Sino-Soviet friendship during the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" held in our country. The All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has issued a notice regarding Moscow's change of Mandarin program broadcast times, hoping that members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and people from all walks of life will organize listening. ### 莫斯科广播电台  改变华语节目广播时间

1952-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科广播电台
    改变华语节目广播时间
    【新华社二日讯】莫斯科广播电台为适应季节的变化，已自十月二十四日起改变了华语节目的广播时间，并将在我国举行“中苏友好月”期间，播送有关中苏友好的节目。中苏友好协会总会已发出关于莫斯科改变华语节目广播时间的通知，希中苏友好协会会员及各界人士组织收听。（附图片）
