Title: Chairman Mao Receives Tikhonov and Others
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received Tikhonov, head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, and delegation members Grusenko, Zakharov, Beloshapko, and Yefimov at 6 p.m. on the 6th. Also present were Chulaki, leader of the Soviet Art Troupe, troupe members Mikhailov, Ulanova, Gaidai, Nasyrova, Obraztsov, Hanon, Pebtov, Alexanderov, head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, Lomonosov, deputy head of the ensemble, Fedorov, head of the Soviet Film Artists Delegation, and delegation members Chirkov and Cherkasov.

Present were Zhu De, Song Qingling, and Gao Gang, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers, Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Culture, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government.

Also present were Gudev, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Zhukov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Shford, Chief Representative of the Soviet Film Export Corporation's Representative Office in China. ### 毛主席接见吉洪诺夫等

1952-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见吉洪诺夫等
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席六日下午六时接见苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫，团员格鲁森科、查哈罗夫、别洛沙普柯、叶菲莫夫，苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基，团员米哈伊洛夫、乌兰诺娃、盖达伊、纳赛罗娃、奥布拉兹卓夫、哈侬、裴布托夫，苏军红旗歌舞团团长亚历山大罗夫，副团长罗蒙诺索夫，苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫，团员契尔柯夫、契尔卡索夫。
    在座的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、宋庆龄、高岗，政务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若、邓小平，政务院文化教育委员会副主任兼文化部部长沈雁冰，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权。
    在座的还有苏联驻我国大使馆临时代办顾德夫，苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福，苏联影片输出公司驻华总代表处总代表史福德。（附图片）
