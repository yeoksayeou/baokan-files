Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Holds Reception to Welcome Soviet Friends; Acting Chief of General Staff Nie, Deputy Commander Chen of the Volunteer Army, and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1952-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th.
    The General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission held a reception on the evening of the 10th to welcome the Soviet cultural workers delegation headed by Tikhonov, the Soviet art workers delegation led by Tsuraki, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble headed by B.A. Alexandrov, and the Soviet film art workers delegation headed by Fedorov.
    Gudev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Zhukov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, were also invited to attend the reception.
    Those present at the reception included: Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, Minister of the General Logistics Department Yang Lisan, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department Lai Chuanzhu, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Artillery Chen Xilian, Commander of the Armored Corps Xu Guangda, Commander of the Railway Corps Teng Daiyuan, Deputy Commander of the Air Force Chang Qiankun, Deputy Commander of the Air Defense Command Tan Jiasu, Deputy Political Commissar of the Public Security Forces Li Tianhuan, Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region Zhu Liangcai, and senior officers of the Army, Air Force, and Navy.
    General Chen Geng, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, also attended the reception.
    Also present at the reception were: Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Wu Xiuquan, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han, and cultural and artistic figures from the capital and literary and artistic workers from the troops, including Xiao San, Ouyang Yuqian, Lao She, Cao Yu, Dai Ailian, Li Zhaobing, Song Zhi's, Yu Ji, Chen Qitong, Chen Bo, and Tian Hua.
    The reception began at eight o'clock and was presided over by Deputy Director Xiao Hua. He extended a warm welcome and respect to the Soviet guests who had come from afar to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." He said: For thirty-five years, the Soviet people, under the leadership of the great Stalin, have given the most cordial concern and the most powerful assistance to the Chinese people's revolutionary movement, which is an indispensable condition for our people to achieve historical victory. Commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sincerely thank the Soviet people, the Soviet government, and Marshal Stalin, and thank the Soviet experts who are working hard in our country for their brotherly help. Xiao Hua expressed his firm belief that the arrival of the Soviet guests will further enhance the unbreakable close friendship between China and the Soviet Union, and raised his glass to the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, and to the health of Comrade Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world.
    The reception proceeded in an atmosphere full of cordiality and friendship. The hosts and guests frequently toasted the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, the development and growth of the armed forces of China and the Soviet Union, the great victory of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the Korean front, the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people and founder of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the health of Comrade Stalin, China's closest friend, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, and the brilliant commander. The entire venue repeatedly erupted in storm-like applause, and repeatedly cheered "Stalin, Mao Zedong."
    After the reception, the Soviet guests attended a welcoming party jointly performed by the cultural troupes stationed in Beijing. Before the performance, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission presented gifts to the Soviet cultural workers delegation, the Soviet art workers delegation, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Soviet film art workers delegation. The sound of military music, applause, and laughter merged into one at the venue. At the welcoming party, songs such as "The East is Red" and "Ode to Stalin" were sung, and dances and the dance drama "Luo Shengjiao" were performed.

### 人民革命军事委员会总政治部  举行酒会欢迎苏联友人  聂代总参谋长、志愿军陈副司令员等出席

1952-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部
    举行酒会欢迎苏联友人
    聂代总参谋长、志愿军陈副司令员等出席
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部在十日晚间举行酒会，欢迎以吉洪诺夫为首的苏联文化工作者代表团、由楚拉基领导的苏联艺术工作团、以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团和以费道罗夫为首的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团。
    苏联驻我国大使馆临时代办顾德夫和苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福也应邀出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的有：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任萧华、总后勤部部长杨立三、总干部部副部长赖传珠、中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光、炮兵司令员陈锡联、装甲兵司令员许光达、铁道兵团司令员滕代远、空军副司令员常乾坤、防空司令部副司令员谭家述、公安部队副政治委员李天焕，华北军区政治部主任朱良才和陆、空、海军的高级军官。
    中国人民志愿军副司令员陈赓将军也参加了酒会。
    参加酒会的还有：中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，文化部部长沈雁冰，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，首都文化艺术界人士和部队文艺工作者萧三、欧阳予倩、老舍、曹禺、戴爱莲、李兆炳、宋之的、虞棘、陈其通、陈播、田华等人。
    酒会在八时开始，由萧华副主任主持。他对远道前来我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联佳宾表示热烈的欢迎和敬意。他说：三十五年来，在伟大的斯大林领导下的苏联人民，对中国人民的革命运动给予了最亲切的关怀和最有力的援助，这是我国人民获得了历史性胜利的不可缺少的条件。中国人民解放军指挥员、战斗员衷心感谢苏联人民、苏联政府和斯大林大元帅，感谢来我国辛勤工作的苏联专家们兄弟般的帮助。萧华表示深信苏联佳宾的到来，将会更加增进中苏两国牢不可破的亲密友谊，并举杯祝中苏两国人民永远的牢不可破的友谊、祝中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席的健康、祝全世界劳动人民伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志健康。
    酒会在充满亲切友爱的空气中进行，宾主频频为中苏两国人民的永久的牢不可破的友谊，为中苏两国人民武装的发展壮大，为在朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军的伟大胜利，为中国人民的伟大领袖、中国人民解放军的创建者毛泽东主席的健康，为中国最亲密的朋友、全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师、天才的统帅斯大林同志的健康干杯。全场一再迸发出暴风雨般的掌声，并经久地欢呼“斯大林、毛泽东”。
    酒会结束后，苏联佳宾们参加了驻京部队文工团联合演出的招待晚会。在演出前，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部向苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团、苏军红旗歌舞团和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团献赠礼物，会场上军乐声、掌声与欢笑声融成一片。招待晚会上演唱了“东方红”、“斯大林颂”等歌曲，表演了舞蹈和舞剧“罗盛教”等节目。
