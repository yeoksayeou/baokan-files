    List of Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation and Other Groups
    List of Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation
    Head: Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, Member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, Deputy Secretary-General of the Soviet Writers' Union, three-time Stalin Prize winner, famous poet.
    Members: Beloshapkova (female), Head of the Agronomy Department of the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy.
    Grusenko, Academic Secretary of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Doctor of Biology, two-time Stalin Prize winner.
    Zakharov, famous composer, Secretary of the Soviet Composers' Union, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, three-time Stalin Prize winner.
    Yefimov, Professor at Leningrad University, author of "Modern History of China".
    List of Soviet Art Troupe
    Leader: Chulaki, Deputy Chairman of the Art Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, composer, Secretary of the Soviet Composers' Union, three-time Stalin Prize winner.
    Members: Mikhailov, singer, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, two-time Stalin Prize winner.
    Gaidai (female), singer, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, two-time Stalin Prize winner.
    Halima Nasyrova (female), singer, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Stalin Prize winner.
    Tamara Khanum (female), singer, People's Artist of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, performer of songs and dances of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, Stalin Prize winner.
    Nechayev, singer, radio actor.
    Beibutov, singer, People's Artist of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner.
    Ulanova (female), ballet dancer, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, three-time Stalin Prize winner.
    Kondratov, ballet dancer, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner.
    Sokolova (female), ballet dancer, actress of the Moscow Operetta Theater.
    Pomazkov, ballet dancer, actor of the Moscow Operetta Theater.
    Kogan, violinist, winner of several international competitions.
    Kaverzneva (female), violinist, winner of international competitions.
    Yemelyanova (female), pianist, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic.
    Karachova (female), actress of the Moscow State Circus.
    Puchin, actor of the Moscow State Circus.
    Chitoshvili, magician.
    Obraztsov, artistic director of the Puppet Theater, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner.
    Metnik, piano accompanist.
    Maksimova (female), piano accompanist.
    Zaitseva (female), piano accompanist.
    Obraztsova (female), piano accompanist.
    Rakhimov, Honored Artist of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic.
    Dadajanov
    Bakayev
    Azimov
    The above four are Uzbek song and dance accompanists for Tamara Khanum.
    Makarova (female), wardrobe manager for Tamara Khanum.
    Administrative Staff:
    Bubenin
    Salnikov
    List of Principal Leaders of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble
    Director and Chief Conductor: Boris Alexandrov, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner, composer.
    Dance Captain: Virsky, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner.
    Choir Captain: Vinogradov, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner.
    Orchestra Conductor: Vladimir Alexandrov, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic.
    List of Soviet Film Artists Delegation
    Head: Fyodorov, Member of the Collegium of the Soviet Ministry of Cinematography and Director of the Directorate of Scientific and Educational Films.
    Members: Cherkasov, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Stalin Prize winner, starred in famous films such as "Seven Hundred Years Ago", "Ivan the Terrible", "The Brilliant Inventor".
    Ladynina, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Stalin Prize winner, starred in famous films such as "Tractor Drivers", "Siberian Symphony", "A Happy Life".
    Chirkov, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Stalin Prize winner, starred in "Maxim Trilogy", "Court of Honor", "Donbass Miners".
    Petrov, famous director, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Stalin Prize winner, directed famous films such as "Peter the Great", "Kutuzov", "The Battle of Stalingrad".
    Smirnova, screenwriter, Stalin Prize winner, wrote screenplays for "The Village Teacher", "Rural Doctor" and other films.
    Pavlov, photographer, Honored Art Worker of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Prize winner, photographed "Siberian Symphony", "A Happy Life" and other films.
