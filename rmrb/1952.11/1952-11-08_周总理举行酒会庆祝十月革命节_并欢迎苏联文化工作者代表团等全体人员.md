Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Celebrate October Revolution Day and Welcome the Entire Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation and Others
Author:
Date: 1952-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government Administration Council held a reception at 2 a.m. on the 7th to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and to welcome the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation headed by Tikhonov, the Soviet Art Workers Troupe led by Tsuraki, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble headed by Alexandrov, and the Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation headed by Fedorov.

Also invited to the reception were Godev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, embassy staff, and Zhukov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Soong Ching Ling and Gao Gang, Vice Premiers of the Central People's Government Administration Council Guo Moruo and Deng Xiaoping, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Xiao Hua, Vice Ministers of the Central People's Government Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and senior officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also present at the reception were Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Director-General, directors and staff in Beijing, and responsible persons of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Also participating in the reception were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as heads of various people's organizations and figures from the scientific and cultural circles, including Chen Boda, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, Ai Qing, Cai Chusheng, Zhou Libo, Song Zhi's, Cao Yu, Mei Lanfang, and Zhou Xinfang, among others.

During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, to congratulate the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to congratulate the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the wise leader of the Chinese people, and to congratulate the health of Marshal Stalin, the close friend of the Chinese people and the great teacher of the working people of the world.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理举行酒会庆祝十月革命节  并欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团等全体人员

1952-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理举行酒会庆祝十月革命节
    并欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团等全体人员
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在七日晨二时举行酒会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年，并欢迎以吉洪诺夫为首的苏联文化工作者代表团、由楚拉基领导的苏联艺术工作团、以亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团和以费道罗夫为首的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团。
    应邀出席酒会的，还有苏联驻我国大使馆临时代办顾德夫和大使馆人员、苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福。
    出席酒会的，有中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄、高岗，中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、邓小平，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和中国人民解放军高级军官。
    出席酒会的，还有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、总干事钱俊瑞，在京理事、干事和北京市中苏友好协会负责人。
    参加酒会的，还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，以及各人民团体负责人和科学界、文化界人士陈伯达、茅盾、周扬、丁西林、艾青、蔡楚生、周立波、宋之的、曹禺、梅兰芳、周信芳等人。
    席间宾主频频举杯，祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年，祝贺中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊，祝贺中国人民英明的领袖毛泽东主席健康，祝贺中国人民亲密的朋友、全世界劳动人民伟大的导师斯大林大元帅健康。
    酒会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
