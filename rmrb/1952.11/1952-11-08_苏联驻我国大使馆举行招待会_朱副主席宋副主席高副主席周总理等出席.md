Title: Soviet Embassy in China Holds Reception; Vice Chairmen Zhu, Song, Gao, Premier Zhou, and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1952-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Gudev, held a reception in Beijing at 5 p.m. on the 7th to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

Those attending the reception included: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Song Qingling, and Gao Gang; Secretary-General Lin Boqu; Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai; Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, and Deng Xiaoping; Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court Wu Gaizhi and Zhang Zhizhi; Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen; Deputy Chief of the General Staff Zhang Zongxun; Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua; Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy Xiao Jingguang; Commander of the Artillery Chen Xilian; Commander of the Armored Forces Xu Guangda; Deputy Commander of the Air Force Chang Qiankun; Heads of various commissions and ministries of the Central People's Government Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Bo Yibo, Ma Yinchu, Jia Tuofu, Ma Xulun, Chen Boda, Shen Yanbing, Xi Zhongxun, Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Chen Yu, Huang Jing, Zhao Erlu, Li Siguang, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Naiqi, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Li Lisan, Shi Liang, An Ziwen, Li Weihan, He Xiangning, Hu Yuzhi; as well as Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Yang Chengwu, and Vice Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, among others.

Also invited to the reception were: Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, Secretary-General Qian Junrui, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Yang Shangkun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth Liao Chengzhi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League Hu Yaobang, as well as prominent figures from the capital's cultural, scientific, and artistic circles Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Zhou Yang, Zhou Libo, Xiao San, Ai Qing, Lü Ji, Lao She, Gu Yuan, Mei Lanfang, Ouyang Yuqian, and Cai Chusheng, among others.

Diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China who attended the reception included: Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkasaihan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U La Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Rocha, Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik, Polish Ambassador Kirilok, Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with the rank of Ambassador Koenig, Danish Minister Munk, Finnish Minister Knöling, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isha Marthi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Bevan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy Jabroski, and personnel from the above-mentioned embassies.

Also invited to attend the reception were the heads and deputy heads of the business delegations from Bulgaria, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, and Hungary.

Head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation Tikhonov, leader of the Soviet Art Troupe Churaki, head of the Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble of the Soviet Army Alexandrov, head of the Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation Fedorov, and some members of the aforementioned groups also attended the reception.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the victory of the great October Socialist Revolution, to the unbreakable great friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the victory of the Soviet people's communist construction, to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the health of the Chinese people's closest friend, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, Marshal Stalin.

### 苏联驻我国大使馆举行招待会  朱副主席宋副主席高副主席周总理等出席

1952-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使馆举行招待会
    朱副主席宋副主席高副主席周总理等出席
    【新华社七日讯】苏联驻我国大使馆临时代办顾德夫，为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年，特于七日下午五时在北京举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、宋庆龄、高岗，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若、邓小平，最高人民法院副院长吴溉之、张志让，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任萧华，中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光、炮兵司令员陈锡联、装甲兵司令员许光达、空军副司令员常乾坤，中央人民政府各委、部首长张奚若、彭泽民、薄一波、马寅初、贾拓夫、马叙伦、陈伯达、沈雁冰、习仲勋、谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、陈郁、黄敬、赵尔陆、李四光、滕代远、朱学范、章乃器、李书城、梁希、李立三、史良、安子文、李维汉、何香凝、胡愈之，以及京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗等。
    应邀参加招待会的还有：中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、总干事钱俊瑞，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中国共产党中央委员会办公厅主任杨尚昆，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记胡耀邦，以及首都文化、科学、艺术界著名人士陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训、周扬、周立波、萧三、艾青、吕骥、老舍、古元、梅兰芳、欧阳予倩、蔡楚生等。

    苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫、苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基、苏军红旗歌舞团团长亚历山大罗夫、苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫及上述团体的部分团员也出席了招待会。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。席间宾主频频为伟大的十月社会主义革命的胜利，为中苏两国人民牢不可破的伟大的友谊，为苏联人民共产主义建设的胜利，为中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席的健康，为中国人民的最亲密朋友、全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师斯大林大元帅的健康而干杯。
