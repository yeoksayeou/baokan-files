Title: Moscow Holds Military Parade in Red Square Today; Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Participate in October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th: TASS Moscow, November 6th dispatch: The Moscow Garrison Commander, General Artemyev, issued an order stating that the Moscow garrison will hold a military parade in Red Square at 11:00 AM on November 7th, the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The parade will be reviewed by Marshal of the Soviet Union, Timoshenko.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th dispatch: According to TASS Moscow, Moscow is filled with a festive atmosphere on the eve of the October Revolution Day. Buildings, streets, and squares are decorated with colorful displays. Pictures, slogans, and charts are seen everywhere. Huge red flags are flying.

The heart of Moscow, Red Square, has become particularly solemn and beautiful. The buildings opposite the Kremlin wall are adorned with giant portraits of Lenin and Stalin, the national emblems of the sixteen Soviet republics, and bright red flags. The red flags bear slogans released by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to celebrate the October Revolution Day. The red star on the Kremlin clock tower shines in the twilight, and the fir trees growing along the palace wall are covered with white snow. The night scenes on the streets of Moscow are also moving. Early snow has laid a silver-white carpet on the sidewalks. People are flocking to theaters, clubs, and cultural palaces to attend celebratory evening events. Many store windows display design drawings and models of new buildings and squares in the capital. The Central Post and Telecommunications Building is decorated with 24,000 colored electric lights, making it exceptionally magnificent. On the eve of the festival, the people of Moscow have achieved new victories in peaceful labor. Workers in many factories have overfulfilled their plans for smelting large quantities of metal and producing many machine tools, various machines, and millions of meters of textiles. Construction workers have built new apartments, schools, hospitals, kindergartens, and nurseries in batches. Hundreds of workers' families have moved into well-equipped new homes on the eve of the festival. The entire city is joyfully welcoming this festival of world historical significance.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th: The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions to participate in the 35th anniversary celebration of the Soviet October Revolution, arrived in Moscow on the 4th.

### 莫斯科今日在红场举行阅兵  我工会代表团抵莫斯科参加十月革命节

1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科今日在红场举行阅兵
    我工会代表团抵莫斯科参加十月革命节
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：莫斯科卫戍司令阿尔杰米也夫上将发布命令说，莫斯科的卫戍部队将于十一月七日，伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念日上午十一时，在红场举行阅兵，由苏联元帅铁木辛哥检阅。
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十月革命节前夕莫斯科处处呈现着节日的景象。建筑物、街道和广场上都装饰得五彩缤纷。到处都看见图画、标语和图表。一幅幅巨大的红旗在飘扬着。
    莫斯科的心脏——红场变得格外庄严和美丽了。克里姆林宫墙对面的建筑物高高挂起列宁和斯大林的巨像、苏联十六个加盟共和国的国徽和鲜红的大旗。红旗上大字写着苏联共产党中央委员会发布的庆祝十月革命节的口号。克里姆林宫钟塔上的红星在暮色中闪闪发光，沿着宫墙生长的枞树覆盖着皑皑白雪。莫斯科街道上的夜景也很动人。早雪已给人行道铺上了银白色的地毯。人们川流不息地赶到剧院、俱乐部和文化宫去参加庆祝节日的晚会。许多商店的橱窗里陈列着首都新建筑物和广场的设计图样和模型。中央邮电大厦装饰着二万四千盏彩色电灯，显得分外辉煌。在节日前夕，莫斯科人民获得了和平劳动的新胜利。许多工厂的工人已经超计划熔炼了大量的金属，生产了许多工作母机、各种机器和几百万公尺的纺织品。建筑工人已建筑好了一批批的新公寓、学校、医院、幼儿园和托儿所。几百个工人家庭已在节日前夕迁入设备齐全的新居。全市居民都在欢欣鼓舞地迎接这个具有世界历史意义的节日。
    【新华社莫斯科六日电】应全苏工会中央理事会的邀请参加苏联十月革命三十五周年纪念的中国工会代表团，于四日抵达莫斯科。
