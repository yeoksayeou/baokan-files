Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Holds Soviet Artist Music Appreciation Event and Broadcast
Author:
Date: 1952-11-21
Page: 1

    Central People's Broadcasting Station
    Holds Soviet Artist Music Appreciation Event and Broadcast
    [This Newspaper reports] To meet the demands of music lovers and people from all walks of life in the capital, the Central People's Broadcasting Station will hold "Music Recording Appreciation Meetings of Famous Soviet Artists Visiting Our Country to Participate in 'Sino-Soviet Friendship Month'" at the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association venue on Nanheyan, Beijing, starting from the 23rd. The appreciation meetings are scheduled to be held twice, on the 23rd and 26th; and again on the 28th and 30th. All times will begin at 7:30 PM. Anyone wishing to attend the appreciation meetings can obtain admission tickets from the reception room of the Broadcasting Administration Bureau at No. 3 West Chang'an Street, the Second Program of Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at No. 15 Chunshu Hutong, Dongcheng, and the Third Program of Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at No. 74 Wangfudajie, Dongcheng, starting from the 22nd.
    [Another News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will hold "Music Broadcasts to Our Audience by Famous Soviet Artists" and "Music Broadcasts to Our Audience by the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble" programs starting from the 22nd. The first part of the "Music Broadcasts to Our Audience by Famous Soviet Artists" program will be broadcast on the 22nd; the second part of the program will be broadcast on the 23rd; and the "Music Broadcasts to Our Audience by the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble" program will be broadcast on the 24th, all times from 6:30 PM to 8:00 PM.

### 中央人民广播电台  举办苏联艺术家音乐欣赏会和广播

1952-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    举办苏联艺术家音乐欣赏会和广播
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台为了满足首都音乐爱好者及各界人民的要求，从二十三日起，假北京南河沿苏联对外文化协会会场举办“来我国参加‘中苏友好月’的苏联著名艺术家音乐录音欣赏会”。欣赏会定在二十三日、二十六日举行两次；二十八日和三十日再次举行。时间都从下午七时半开始。凡愿参加欣赏会者，从二十二日起，可向西长安街三号广播事业局传达室、东城椿树胡同十五号北京人民广播电台第二台、东城王府大街七十四号北京人民广播电台第三台领取入场券。
    【又讯】中央人民广播电台从二十二日起，举办“苏联著名艺术家对我国听众音乐广播”及“苏军红旗歌舞团对我国听众音乐广播”节目。二十二日播送“苏联著名艺术家对我国听众音乐广播”节目的第一部分；二十三日播送该节目的第二部分；二十四日播送“苏军红旗歌舞团对我国听众音乐广播”节目，时间都在下午六点半到八点。
