Title: Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation and Others Leave Beijing to Participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1952-11-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, the Soviet Art Troupe, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Soviet Film Artists Delegation departed from Beijing in batches on the 15th to participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities across the country.
    The head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, Tikhonov, members Grusenko and Yefimov, and the leaders of the Soviet Art Troupe, Churaki, members Mikhailov, Halima Nasyrova, Ulanova, and others, a total of thirteen people, took a plane to Hankou in the morning.
    Members of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, Zakharov and Beloshapko, and members of the Soviet Art Troupe, Gaidai, and others, a total of fourteen people, took a plane to Lanzhou in the morning.
    The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by B. A. Alexandrov, took a train to Tianjin at 1:30 p.m.
    The Soviet Film Artists Delegation, headed by Fedorov, took a train to the Northeast in the evening.
    Members of the Soviet Art Troupe, Tamara Hanon, and others, a total of seven people, took a train to the Northwest in the evening.
    Those who went to the airport and station to see them off included Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Yan Baohang, Lai Yali, Li Zhanwu, Deputy Minister Li Zhaobing of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, and figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles such as Ding Xilin, Xiao San, Zhou Libo, Cao Yu, Tian Han, and Hong Shen.
    Fominko, Chief Deputy Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Zhukoff, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also went to the station and airport to see them off.
    During their stay in Beijing, the Soviet friends widely participated in the activities of "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" and made great contributions to further promoting Sino-Soviet friendship and introducing advanced Soviet experience. Tikhonov, head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, and all members, Churaki, leader of the Soviet Art Troupe, B. A. Alexandrov, head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, and others were invited to give a total of sixteen special reports, with an audience of more than 40,000 people. The Soviet Art Troupe and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble gave a total of eleven performances, with a total audience of about 130,000 people. Soviet friends visited factories, rural areas, schools, and government offices, and met with the broad masses. Soviet scientists and artists also held sixteen symposiums with Chinese experts and scholars to exchange views on science, culture, and art. These reports, performances, meetings, and symposiums were warmly welcomed by the broad masses. Their participation in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities in various regions will greatly promote the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

### 苏联文化工作者代表团等离北京  分别赴全国各地参加“中苏友好月”活动

1952-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化工作者代表团等离北京
    分别赴全国各地参加“中苏友好月”活动
    【新华社十五日讯】苏联文化工作者代表团、苏联艺术工作团、苏军红旗歌舞团和苏联电影艺术工作者代表团十五日分批由北京启程，赴全国各地参加“中苏友好月”活动。
    苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫、团员格鲁森科、叶菲莫夫和苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基、团员米哈伊洛夫、哈利马·纳赛罗娃、乌兰诺娃等一行十三人，在上午乘飞机赴汉口。
    苏联文化工作者代表团团员查哈罗夫、别洛沙普柯和苏联艺术工作团团员盖达伊等一行十四人在上午乘飞机赴兰州。
    以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团在下午一时三十分乘火车赴天津。
    以费道罗夫为首的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团在晚间乘火车赴东北。
    苏联艺术工作团团员塔马拉·哈侬等一行七人，在晚间乘火车赴西北。
    分别前往机场和车站欢送的，有中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞、副总干事阎宝航、赖亚力、李沾吾，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳以及首都文化艺术界人士丁西林、萧三、周立波、曹禺、田汉、洪深等。
    苏联驻华大使馆首席副武官富敏科及苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福也分别前往车站和机场欢送。
    苏联友人在京期间，曾广泛地参加了“中苏友好月”的活动，对进一步促进中苏友谊和介绍苏联先进经验方面作了巨大的贡献。苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫和全体团员、苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基、苏军红旗歌舞团团长鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫等应邀共作了十六次专题报告，听众约四万余人。苏联艺术工作团和苏军红旗歌舞团共演出十一次，观众共十三万人左右。苏联友人分别访问了工厂、农村、学校和机关，与广大的群众见了面。苏联科学家和艺术家们还分别和中国的专家学者举行了十六次座谈会，交换了有关科学、文化和艺术方面的意见。这些报告、演出、会面和座谈，受到广大群众的热烈欢迎。他们到各地参加“中苏友好月”活动，必将大大地促进中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。
