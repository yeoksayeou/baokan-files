Title: Grand Gala Held in Dihua to Celebrate the October Revolution and "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1952-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, November 14th: A grand gala was held in Dihua on the evening of the 9th to celebrate the October Revolution and "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." More than 500 people attended, including Burhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, Zhang Bangying, Third Secretary of the Xinjiang Branch of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Saifuddin, Fourth Secretary, and heads of various people's organizations.

    Baranov, Acting Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Dihua, Deputy Consul General Ivanov, Soviet experts assisting in our country's construction, and Soviet staff members of the Sino-Soviet Petroleum Company, the Sino-Soviet Rare Metals and Non-Ferrous Metals Company, and the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company were all invited to attend.

    The song and dance program was performed by the Xinjiang Military Region Political Department Art Troupe which had performed in Beijing. After the song and dance program, a ball was held.

### 迪化市举行盛大联欢晚会  庆祝十月革命节和“中苏友好月”

1952-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    迪化市举行盛大联欢晚会
    庆祝十月革命节和“中苏友好月”
    【新华社迪化十四日电】迪化市九日晚举行庆祝十月革命节和“中苏友好月”的盛大联欢晚会。到会的有新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉、中共中央新疆分局第三书记张邦英、第四书记赛福鼎以及各人民团体负责人等五百余人。
    苏联驻迪化总领事馆代总领事巴兰诺夫、副总领事伊万诺夫以及帮助我国建设的苏联专家和中苏石油股份有限公司、中苏稀有金属及有色金属股份有限公司、中苏民用航空公司的苏方工作人员等均应邀到会。
    晚会由新疆军区政治部文艺工作团赴京演出队演出歌咏舞蹈节目。歌咏舞蹈节目结束后，举行舞会。
