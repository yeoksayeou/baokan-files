Title: Government Administration Council Holds 159th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd—The 159th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 21st. The meeting listened to and approved the report made by Ulanhu, Chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, on the work of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region over the past three years and the outline of future construction plans. The meeting also passed various appointment and removal proposals and proposals for submission to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一五九次会议

1952-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五九次会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十九次政务会议在二十一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了内蒙古自治区人民政府乌兰夫主席所作的内蒙古自治区人民政府三年来的工作及今后建设计划的轮廓的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
