Our Great Motherland
The Dalian seaport dock has modern mechanical loading and unloading equipment. This is a hundred-ton crane on the dock. It is loading newly built iron-hulled small boats onto ten-thousand-ton ships, ready to be transported to the interior for transporting materials on inland rivers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连海港码头拥有现代化的机械装卸设备。这是码头上的一百吨起重机。它正把新造的铁壳小轮船装到万吨轮船上去，准备运到内地，以便在内河运输物资。
