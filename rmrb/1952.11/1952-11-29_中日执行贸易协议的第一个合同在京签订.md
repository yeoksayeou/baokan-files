Title: The First Contract to Implement the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The China National Import and Export Corporation and the Japan Ba Shoji Co., Ltd. signed a barter trade contract in Beijing on the 28th. This is the first specific contract signed between China and Japan to implement the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement after the signing of that agreement.

    Signing on behalf of the China National Import and Export Corporation was Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation. Signing on behalf of Ba Shoji Co., Ltd. was Sakurai Hideo, Executive Director of Ba Shoji Co., Ltd. He is also a Director of the Sino-Japanese Trade Promotion Conference.

    Attending the signing ceremony were Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Xie Xiao Nai, Director of the Liaison Department of the Council.

### 中日执行贸易协议的第一个合同在京签订

1952-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中日执行贸易协议的第一个合同在京签订
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国进出口公司和日本巴商事株式会社于二十八日在北京签订了一项易货贸易合同。这是中日贸易协议签订后，中日双方执行这个协议所签定的第一个具体合同。
    代表中国进出口公司签字的是中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭；代表巴商事株式会社签字的是巴商事株式会社专务董事樱井英雄。他是日中贸易促进会议干事。
    参加签字仪式的有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎和该会联络部部长  谢筱迺。
