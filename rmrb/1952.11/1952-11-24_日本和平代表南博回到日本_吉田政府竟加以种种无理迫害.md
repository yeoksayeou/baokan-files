Title: Nan Bo, Japanese Peace Representative, Returns to Japan; Yoshida Government Unreasonably Persecutes Him
Author:
Date: 1952-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Nan Bo, the deputy head of the Japanese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, returned to Tokyo by plane on the 18th. He was warmly welcomed at the airport by prominent Japanese peace activists such as Yoshitaro Hirano and Masaharu Hatanaka, and by the Japanese people. The Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department searched Nan Bo's luggage at the airport and interrogated him. The Japanese government's persecution of peace representatives has aroused people's vigilance. All professors at Hitotsubashi University, where Nan Bo teaches, have stated that they will support Nan Bo's just struggle with concrete actions.

### 日本和平代表南博回到日本  吉田政府竟加以种种无理迫害

1952-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和平代表南博回到日本
    吉田政府竟加以种种无理迫害
    【新华社二十日讯】参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的日本代表团副团长南博，在十八日乘飞机回到东京，在机场受到日本著名和平人士平野义太郎、畑中政春等和日本人民的热烈欢迎。东京都警视厅的警察在机场搜查了南博的行李，并对他加以盘问。日本政府迫害和平代表的行为，已经引起人们的警惕。南博任教的一桥大学的全体教授已表示将以具体行动来支持南博的正义斗争。
