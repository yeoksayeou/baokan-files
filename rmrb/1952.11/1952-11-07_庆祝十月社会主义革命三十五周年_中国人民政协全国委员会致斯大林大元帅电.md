Title: China People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee Telegram to Marshal Stalin on the Occasion of the Thirty-Fifth Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

    Celebrating the 35th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
    China People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee
    Telegram to Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:
    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the great October Revolution, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference extends sincere and warm congratulations to you and the Soviet people.
    We wish the great Soviet people new achievements in the cause of building communism.
    We wish the unbreakable great friendship between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the People's Republic of China to be further consolidated and developed.
    The entire Chinese people firmly believe that, under your wise leadership, the struggle of progressive humanity around the world for lasting peace will surely triumph over the imperialist policy of war.
    National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    November 6, 1952

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年  中国人民政协全国委员会致斯大林大元帅电

1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年
    中国人民政协全国委员会
    致斯大林大元帅电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林同志：
    欣逢伟大的十月革命三十五周年之际，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会谨向您和苏联人民致诚挚的热烈的祝贺。
    祝伟大的苏联人民在建设共产主义的事业中获得新的成就。
    祝苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和中华人民共和国牢不可破的伟大友谊更加巩固和发展。
    全中国人民一致坚信：在您的英明领导下，全世界进步人类争取持久和平的斗争必定战胜帝国主义的战争政策。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    一九五二年十一月六日
