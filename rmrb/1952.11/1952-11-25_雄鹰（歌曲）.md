The Eagle

Editor's Note: "The Eagle" is one of Zakharov's famous songs and also one of the popular Soviet songs praising leaders. This song remarkably develops the characteristics of Russian folk choir songs, with the melody conveying a simple and affectionate eulogy to Lenin and Stalin. The National Pyatnitsky Russian Folk Choir, led by Zakharov, performed this work perfectly and made a record (catalog number B11910), which is available for singers to reference.

### 雄鹰（歌曲）

1952-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    雄鹰
    编者按：“雄鹰”是查哈罗夫的著名歌曲之一，也是苏联流行的歌颂领袖的歌曲之一。这首歌卓越地发展了俄罗斯民间合唱歌曲的特色，曲调中流露着对列宁和斯大林的朴质和亲切的歌颂。查哈罗夫所领导的国立披雅特尼茨基俄罗斯民歌合唱团十分完美地表演了这一作品，并曾灌制唱片（片号B11910），可供演唱者参考。
