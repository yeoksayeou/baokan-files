    Our Great Motherland
    In the construction of vertical shafts at the Northeast Xi'an Coal Mine, the advanced experience of the Soviet parallel operation method was studied and successfully applied. This is a scene of the shaft workers at the Xi'an Coal Mine wearing specially made waterproof rubber suits to go to work.
