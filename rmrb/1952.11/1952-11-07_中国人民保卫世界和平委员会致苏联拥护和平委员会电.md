Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Sends Telegram to Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace
Author:
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    Sends Telegram to Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace

    To the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace:
    On the occasion of the glorious 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, and at the same time on the occasion of the enthusiastic start of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" throughout China, we, on behalf of the entire Chinese people, extend our warm congratulations and highest respects to you and the entire Soviet people.
    We wish the Soviet people even greater achievements in the construction of a communist society and in the cause of defending world peace; and we wish the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples—the strong bulwark of peace in the Far East and the world—even more consolidated!
    Long live the unbreakable friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!
    Long live world peace!

    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    November 6, 1952, in Beijing

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致苏联拥护和平委员会电

1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    致苏联拥护和平委员会电苏联拥护和平委员会：
    值此伟大十月社会主义革命三十五周年的光辉纪念日，同时值此“中苏友好月”在中国全国范围内热烈开始举行的日子，我们谨代表全中国人民向你们及全体苏联人民致以热诚的祝贺和崇高的敬礼。
    谨祝苏联人民在建设共产主义社会和保卫世界和平事业中获得更加伟大的成就；并祝中苏两国人民的伟大友谊——远东和全世界和平的坚强堡垒——更加巩固！
    中苏两国人民牢不可破的友好团结万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    一九五二年十一月六日于北京
