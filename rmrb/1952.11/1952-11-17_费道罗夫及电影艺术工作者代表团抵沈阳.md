Title: Fedorov and Film Art Workers Delegation Arrive in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation, a group of six led by the delegation head Fedorov, arrived in Shenyang at 3:50 PM on the 16th. Accompanying them to Shenyang were: Tian Fang, Secretary-General of the Film Bureau of the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture, Luo Guangda, General Manager of the China Film Corporation, Wang Yang, Director of the Beijing Film Studio, and others. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Liu Zhiming, Vice President of the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association, Zhou Huan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Jiao Ruoyu, President of the Shenyang Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Bai Xi, Manager of the Northeast China Region Company of the China Film Corporation, and representatives from cultural and artistic circles in Shenyang, totaling over 1,000 people. Also present to welcome them at the station was Vasilyev, Deputy Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang, and others.

    As the Soviet Film Art Workers Delegation entered the station, the welcoming crowd erupted in sustained applause and cheers. After the delegation alighted from the train, they cordially shook hands with the welcomers. Then, a group of innocent and lively Young Pioneers rushed forward to present flowers, and the song "Moscow-Beijing" resounded throughout the station.

### 费道罗夫及电影艺术工作者代表团抵沈阳

1952-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    费道罗夫及电影艺术工作者代表团抵沈阳
    据新华社讯：苏联电影艺术工作者代表团一行六人，在该团团长费道罗夫率领下，于十六日下午三点五十分到达沈阳。陪同前往沈阳的有：中央人民政府文化部电影局秘书长田方、中国影片经理公司总经理罗光达及北京电影制片厂厂长汪洋等多人。前往车站欢迎的有：东北中苏友好协会副会长刘芝明、中国人民解放军东北军区副政治委员周桓、沈阳市中苏友好协会会长焦若愚、中国影片经理公司东北区公司经理白晞以及沈阳市文化、艺术各界代表等一千余人。前往车站欢迎的还有苏联驻沈阳总领事馆副总领事华西列夫等。
    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团进入车站时，欢迎群众发出经久不息的掌声和欢呼声。代表团下车后与欢迎者亲切地握手，接着一群天真活泼的少年儿童队员奔来献花，车站上响起一片“莫斯科——北京”的歌声。
