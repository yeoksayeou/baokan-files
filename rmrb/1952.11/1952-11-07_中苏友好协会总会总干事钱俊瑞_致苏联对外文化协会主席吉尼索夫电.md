Title: Qian Junrui, Director-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Sends a Telegram to Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Author: Qian Junrui
Date: 1952-11-07
Page: 4

To Comrade Ginisov, Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Moscow:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, I, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, extend our warmest congratulations to Comrade Stalin and the Soviet people.

    The great October Socialist Revolution established a profound friendship between the peoples of our two countries. This friendship has now become an invincible force and a strong bulwark of peace in the Far East and the world.

    May China and the Soviet Union achieve further victories in the struggle to defend international peace and strive for friendly cooperation among the peoples of all countries!

    May the cultural and intellectual exchange between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union grow day by day!

    Long live the great October Socialist Revolution!

    Long live our great teacher, Comrade Stalin!

    Qian Junrui
    Director-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    November 5, 1952

### 中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞  致苏联对外文化协会主席吉尼索夫电
钱俊瑞
1952-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞
    致苏联对外文化协会主席吉尼索夫电莫斯科苏联对外文化协会：吉尼索夫同志：
    值此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年之际，我谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员向斯大林同志和苏联人民致最热烈的祝贺。
    伟大的十月社会主义革命树立了我们两国人民之间的深厚友谊，这一友谊现在已成为不可战胜的力量，已成为远东和世界和平的坚强堡垒。
    愿中苏两国在保卫国际和平和争取各国人民友好合作的斗争中获得进一步的胜利！
    愿中苏两国人民的文化与智慧的交流日益发展！
    伟大的十月社会主义革命万岁！
    我们伟大的导师斯大林同志万岁！
    中苏友好协会总会总干事  钱俊瑞
    一九五二年十一月五日
