Title: Mongolian Scientific and Cultural Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-06-12

Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: The Mongolian Scientific and Cultural Workers Delegation, consisting of three members, who were invited to visit our country in accordance with the 1955 Executive Plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, departed Beijing by plane this morning to return home after a visit to our country lasting approximately one and a half months.

During their visit to our country, the delegation visited cultural and educational institutions in Beijing and seven other major cities, including Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Guangzhou, and had extensive contacts with people from scientific, educational, and literary and art circles in our country. The head of the delegation, educator Mashlai, held nearly twenty professional seminars and visits with people from the educational field in our country, and visited higher and secondary normal schools, teacher training colleges, overseas Chinese remedial schools, and kindergartens, gaining a systematic understanding of various aspects of education work in our country. During his visits to educational institutions, Mashlai offered many valuable suggestions, and also gave a report to people in the Beijing education field on Mongolian national education, literacy campaigns, and language reform issues.

Historian Nachagdarj had academic discussions with Chinese historians such as Professor Weng Dujian and Professor Han Rulin in Beijing, and attended academic report discussion meetings such as the "Chinese History Issues" sponsored by the Chinese Academy of Sciences. During his visits to various places, he also visited the "Exhibition of the History of the Chinese Workers' Revolutionary Movement," the "February 7th" General Strike Exhibition Room, the Lu Xun Memorial Hall, the Guangzhou Commune, and the Peasant Movement Institute organized by Chairman Mao, to learn about the historical facts and materials of modern Chinese revolutionary history.

During his visit to our country, painter Zewigzhab created a number of oil paintings with the themes of construction sites, great buildings, laboring people's production, heroic model figures, and landscapes of various places. These works were praised by Chinese artists when they were exhibited for viewing at the Art Exhibition Hall of the Chinese Artists Association on the 9th.

During the delegation's stay in Beijing, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi met with all members of the delegation, and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo also met with the delegation members and had a long conversation with them. Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo held a banquet on the evening of the 9th to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 蒙古科学文化工作者代表团离北京回国

1955-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古科学文化工作者代表团离北京回国
    新华社十日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划应邀来我国访问的蒙古科学文化工作者代表团一行三人，在我国进行了为期约一个半月的参观访问后，于十日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    代表团在我国访问期间，曾在北京和上海、武汉、重庆、广州等其他七大城市参观文化教育机关，与我国科学、教育和文艺界人士进行广泛接触。代表团团长、教育学家马什拉伊曾与我国教育界人士作了将近二十次的专业座谈和访问，先后参观了高等和中等师范学校、教师进修学院、华侨补习学校和幼儿园，对我国教育工作的各方面情况作了系统的了解。马什拉伊在参观教育机关时曾提出许多宝贵的建议，并曾向北京教育界人士作了关于蒙古的国民教育、扫盲、文字改革问题的报告。
    历史学家纳查格达尔日在北京曾与我国历史学家翁独健、韩儒林教授等作学术讨论，并出席了中国科学院主办的“中国历史问题”等学术报告讨论会。在各地参观访问期间，他又参观了“中国工人革命运动史展览”、“二·七”大罢工展览室、鲁迅纪念馆、广州公社及毛主席主办的农民运动讲习所等处，了解了中国近代革命史的史实材料。
    画家泽维格扎布在我国访问期间，以建设工地、伟大建筑物、劳动人民生产情况、英雄模范人物和各地风景为主题，创作了油画多幅。这些作品于九日在中国美术家协会美术大厅作观摩展出时，受到我国美术家的赞誉。
    代表团在京期间，国务院副总理陈毅曾接见代表团全体人员，中国科学院院长郭沫若也会见了代表团人员并与他们作长时间的谈话。教育部部长张奚若曾在九日晚举行宴会欢送代表团。
