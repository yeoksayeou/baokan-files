Title: Communist Party of India Central Committee Plenary Session Opens in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 11th (TASS): According to the "Hindustan Times," the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India opened in Delhi on June 10. The meeting will last for ten days.

This meeting will discuss issues related to the upcoming Congress of the Communist Party of India.

### 印度共产党中央全会在德里开幕

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度共产党中央全会在德里开幕
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社新德里十一日讯：据“印度斯坦时报”报道，印度共产党中央委员会全体会议六月十日在德里开幕。会议将举行十天。
    这次会议将讨论与即将举行的印度共产党代表大会有关的问题。
