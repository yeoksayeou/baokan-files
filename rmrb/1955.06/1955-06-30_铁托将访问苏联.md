Title: Tito to Visit the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, June 29th: TASS reports from Belgrade on the 28th: The Yugoslav News Agency reports: It was learned from well-informed sources that Soviet representatives, during the recent Soviet-Yugoslav talks, invited the President of the Republic, Josip Broz Tito, to visit the Soviet Union.

　　It was learned from these sources that President Tito has accepted this invitation.

### 铁托将访问苏联

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托将访问苏联
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十八日讯：南斯拉夫通讯社报道：从消息灵通人士获知，苏联代表在不久以前苏南会谈时，曾邀请共和国总统约瑟夫·布罗兹·铁托访问苏联。
    从这些人士获知，铁托总统已经接受了这个邀请。
