Title: Vice Premier Peng Dehuai Returns to Beijing from Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 3rd: TASS reported from Moscow on June 3rd: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, who had previously attended the Warsaw Conference of European countries to ensure European peace and security as an observer of the People's Republic of China, visited the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government.

    During his visit, Peng Dehuai visited scenic spots and historical sites in Moscow, as well as Leningrad, Stalingrad, and Crimea.

    Peng Dehuai met with Voroshilov, Bulganin, Molotov, Khrushchev, and Zhukov, and held talks with them.

    Peng Dehuai and his entourage departed by plane on June 2nd to return to their country.

    Present at the airport to see them off were: Soviet Minister of Defense and Marshal of the Soviet Union Zhukov, Chief of General Staff of the Soviet Army and Navy and Marshal of the Soviet Union Sokolovsky, Marshal of the Soviet Union Konev, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedrin, General of the Army Antonov, Marshal of the Air Force Rudenko, Lieutenant General Gusev, Deputy Commander of the Moscow Garrison Colonel Vereshchenko, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Kapustin, and other responsible personnel from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Soviet Ministry of Defense.

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, who was invited to participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Germany and then attended the second meeting of the European countries’ conference on ensuring European peace and security in Warsaw as an observer, visited Poland and the Soviet Union successively, and returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 3rd.

    Wan Yi, Xiao Xiangrong, Chen Chu, and Lei Yingfu returned to Beijing on the same plane. Sha Qianli and Lai Jifa, who participated in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Germany, had returned to Beijing earlier.

    Welcoming them at the airport were Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense; Chang Lifu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; as well as Su Yu, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Zhang Zongxun, Chen Geng, and Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chiefs of General Staff; Tan Zheng and Xiao Hua, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, and senior generals.

    Polish Ambassador to China Kiryluk and Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic Grutner also came to the airport to welcome them.

### 彭德怀副总理由莫斯科回到北京

1955-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀副总理由莫斯科回到北京
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：前已报道参加欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议的中华人民共和国观察员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，应苏联政府的邀请访问了苏联。
    在访问期间，彭德怀参观了莫斯科的名胜古迹，并且访问了列宁格勒、斯大林格勒和克里米亚。
    彭德怀曾拜会了伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫和朱可夫，并且和他们进行了交谈。
    彭德怀及其随员已于六月二日乘飞机启程回国。
    前往机场欢送的有：苏联国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫，苏联陆海军总参谋长、苏联元帅索可洛夫斯基，苏联元帅科涅夫，苏联外交部副部长费德林，陆军大将安东诺夫，空军元帅鲁金科，陆军上将古谢夫，莫斯科市卫戍副司令维列辛科上校，苏联外交部交际司副司长卡普斯金，苏联外交部和苏联国防部其他负责人员。
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理彭德怀应邀参加了德国解放十周年庆祝典礼以后，并以观察员身份出席了在华沙召开的欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议的第二次会议，先后访问了波兰和苏联，已在三日下午乘飞机返回北京。
    同机返京的还有万毅、萧向荣、陈楚、雷英夫。参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼的沙千里、赖际发已先期返京。
    到机场欢迎的，有国防部副部长黄克诚，国务院副秘书长常黎夫，外交部部长助理何伟，以及中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕，副总参谋长张宗逊、陈赓、张爱萍，总政治部副主任谭政、萧华和高级将领。
    波兰驻华大使基里洛克和德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳也到机场欢迎。
