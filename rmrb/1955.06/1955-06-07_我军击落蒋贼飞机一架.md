Title: Our Army Shoots Down a Chiang Bandit Plane

Author:

Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 5th. The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery forces shot down a Chiang bandit plane intruding into the Shantou area of Guangdong Province today.

　　At 15:04 today, four Chiang bandit F-47 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Shantou City, Guangdong Province. The People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery forces immediately opened fire and shot down one of them. This Chiang bandit plane crashed into the ocean twenty nautical miles south of Xiaojinmen Island, Fujian Province, trailing thick smoke.

### 我军击落蒋贼飞机一架

1955-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击落蒋贼飞机一架
    新华社广州五日电　中国人民解放军高射炮部队五日击落窜扰广东省汕头地区的蒋贼飞机一架。
    五日十五时零四分，蒋贼F—四七型飞机四架，窜扰广东省汕头市上空。人民解放军高射炮部队当即开火射击，击落其中的一架。这架蒋贼飞机冒着浓烟坠落到福建省小金门岛以南二十浬的海洋里。
