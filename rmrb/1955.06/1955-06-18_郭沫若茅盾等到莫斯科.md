Title: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and Others Arrive in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 17th. Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Mao Dun, head of the Chinese delegation to the Helsinki World Peace Conference, deputy heads Chen Shutong, Liao Chengzhi, and some members of the delegation arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 16th.  Greeting them at the airport were Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, Presidium member Korneichuk, and others, as well as Liu Xiao, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union.

The delegation will leave Moscow for Helsinki in the next few days.

### 郭沫若茅盾等到莫斯科

1955-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若茅盾等到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科十七日电　出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，中国代表团团长茅盾、副团长陈叔通、廖承志和一部分团员，在十六日下午乘飞机到达莫斯科。到机场欢迎的有苏联保卫和平委员会主席尼·吉洪诺夫、主席团委员考涅楚克等人以及我国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    代表团日内即将由莫斯科转赴赫尔辛基。
