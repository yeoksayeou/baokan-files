"Materials on the Hu Feng Counter-Revolutionary Clique" Published Today

    This newspaper reports that the three batches of materials on the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique, which were published successively in the "People's Daily," have been compiled into a book by the People's Publishing House and will be published today. The book is titled "Materials on the Hu Feng Counter-Revolutionary Clique," and its contents include: the preface and editorial of the "People's Daily," the three batches of materials on the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique, and Hu Feng's "My Self-Criticism" as an appendix. Minor textual revisions have been made to the editor's notes and footnotes to the three "materials." Some footnotes have been revised in the second "material," some footnotes have been added, and two more editor's notes have been added.
    The initial print run of this book in Beijing is 700,000 copies and is scheduled to go on sale today. In addition, it will be printed in large quantities in twenty-five large and medium-sized cities, including Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Urumqi, and Hohhot.

### “关于胡风反革命集团的材料”今日出版

1955-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    “关于胡风反革命集团的材料”今日出版
    本报讯  “人民日报”前后发表的关于胡风反革命集团的三批材料，已经汇集成书，由人民出版社于今日出版。本书定名为“关于胡风反革命集团的材料”，内容包括：“人民日报”编辑部的序言、社论、关于胡风反革命集团的三批材料，以及作为附件的胡风的“我的自我批判”。对三篇“材料”的按语和注文，作了少数文字上的修改，第二篇“材料”中修改了一些注文，增加了一些注文，又增加了两个按语。
    这本书在北京初版印行七十万册，定于今日出售。此外，并将在上海、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、乌鲁木齐、呼和浩特等二十五个大中城市大量印行。
