Title: A Batch of Displaced Overseas Chinese Expelled from Thailand Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 25th: A group of twenty-five displaced overseas Chinese who were unreasonably expelled from Thailand by the Thai government, along with thirteen of their family members, arrived in Shantou on the evening of the 23rd aboard the "Hai Huang" vessel. Local overseas Chinese affairs agencies have provided reception and resettlement for these displaced overseas Chinese.

### 被泰国驱逐的难侨一批回国

1955-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    被泰国驱逐的难侨一批回国
    新华社广州二十五日电一批被泰国政府无理驱逐出境的归国难侨二十五人连同其家属十三人，于二十三日晚乘“海皇”号轮船抵达汕头。当地侨务机构对这些难侨已给以接待和安置。
