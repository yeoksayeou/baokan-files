Title: Congratulations to Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin on His Sixtieth Birthday, Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 1

    Congratulations to Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin on His Sixtieth Birthday
    Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

    To Comrade Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin:

    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to you, a student of the great Lenin and one of the closest comrades-in-arms of Stalin, the successor to Lenin's cause, one of the outstanding leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people, and a dear friend of the Chinese people. We sincerely wish you good health and new achievements under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in the struggle to build a communist society in the Soviet Union and defend world peace.

         The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
         Beijing, June 8, 1955

### 祝贺尼·亚·布尔加宁六十寿辰  中国共产党中央委员会贺电

1955-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺尼·亚·布尔加宁六十寿辰
    中国共产党中央委员会贺电尼·亚·布尔加宁同志：
    在您六十岁寿辰之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您、伟大的列宁的学生和列宁事业承继者斯大林的最亲密的战友之一，苏联共产党和苏联人民卓越的领导者之一，中国人民所敬爱的朋友致热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。我们衷心地祝贺您身体健康，祝贺您在苏联共产党领导下，在为苏联建成共产主义社会和保卫世界和平事业的斗争中获得新的成就。
         中国共产党中央委员会  一九五五年六月八日于北京
