Title: Vice-President of the Paris City Council Receives Chinese Art Troupe; Charlie Chaplin Goes to Paris to See Performance by Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th - According to Agence France-Presse, Madame Nicole de Hauteclocque, Vice-President of the Paris City Council, received the Chinese Art Troupe on the 8th, which was invited to participate in the Second Paris International Theater Festival.

    Madame de Hauteclocque praised the Chinese Art Troupe as "perfect ambassadors of Chinese theatrical art." She said: "The French public extends its sympathy and friendly greetings to the Chinese people through you."

    Zhao Feng, the Deputy Head of the Chinese Art Troupe, expressed his gratitude on behalf of the entire troupe for the warm welcome from all sectors of French society and hoped that this performance would enhance cultural and friendly relations between the two countries.

    During the exchange of gifts, Madame de Hauteclocque presented the Chinese Art Troupe with a commemorative medal marking two thousand years of Paris, and Zhao Feng presented her with a collection of Chinese art works.

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 9th - Paris News: World-renowned film artist Charlie Chaplin flew from Switzerland to Paris and watched the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe at the Sarah Bernhardt Theatre on the 7th.

    After watching the performance, Chaplin went backstage to congratulate the Chinese actors, praising the achievements in theatrical reform since the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 巴黎市议会副议长接见我国艺术团  卓别麟去巴黎观看我艺术团表演

1955-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    巴黎市议会副议长接见我国艺术团
    卓别麟去巴黎观看我艺术团表演
    新华社九日讯　据法新社报道，巴黎市议会副议长尼科尔·德奥特克洛克夫人八日接见应邀参加巴黎第二届国际戏剧节的中国艺术团。
    德奥特克洛克夫人称赞中国艺术团是“中国戏剧艺术的完美的大使”。她说：“法国公众通过你们向中国人民致以同情和友好的问候。”
    中国艺术团副团长赵沨代表中国艺术团全体人员对法国各界人士的热情欢迎表示感谢，并且希望这次演出会增进两国之间的文化和友好关系。
    在交换礼品时，德奥特克洛克夫人把一个巴黎两千年纪念牌送给中国艺术团，赵沨赠给她一册中国艺术作品集。
    新华社布拉格九日电　巴黎消息：世界闻名的电影艺术家卓别麟自瑞士飞到巴黎，在七日观看了中国艺术团在萨拉·伯纳尔剧院的演出。
    卓别麟在观剧后赴后台向中国演员祝贺，盛赞中华人民共和国成立以来在戏剧改革工作上的成就。
