Title: French Circles Warmly Welcome Performance of Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 11th (via telegram from Paris): The Chinese Art Troupe, which participated in the Second Paris International Theatre Festival, concluded its five-day performance on the 8th.

    On the evening of the 10th, the Chinese Art Troupe held a grand reception at the "Ambassador Theatre" in Paris, attended by approximately two thousand prominent figures from various circles in Paris. The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere. Chinese actors performed songs and dances at the event.
    The Chinese Art Troupe's performance in Paris has aroused great interest from French cultural and artistic circles and the press, as well as warm welcome from audiences from all walks of life. It is considered a major event in Parisian cultural life. Almost all newspapers carried news and reviews of the Chinese Art Troupe's performance on the front page. Some audience members lingered in front of the theater for up to an hour after the show in the rain, waiting to applaud and welcome the actors. Overseas Chinese in France presented flowers to the art troupe every night, expressing that the performance made them feel proud of their motherland.
    The Chinese Art Troupe will perform again at the Palais de Chaillot in Paris starting June 16th. After the news was released, all tickets for the five-day run were sold out within a few days.

### 法国各界人士热烈欢迎中国艺术团的演出

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    法国各界人士热烈欢迎中国艺术团的演出
    新华社布拉格十一日电　巴黎消息：参加巴黎第二届国际戏剧节的中国艺术团已经在八日结束了历时五天的演出。
    十日晚间，中国艺术团在巴黎“大使剧院”举行盛大招待会，出席的有巴黎各界著名人士约两千人。招待会上充满了友好的气氛。中国演员们在会上表演了歌唱和舞蹈。
    中国艺术团在巴黎的演出引起了法国文化艺术界和新闻界的巨大兴趣以及各界观众的热烈欢迎。人们认为这是巴黎文化生活中的重大事件。几乎所有报纸都曾以第一版地位刊载中国艺术团演出的消息和评论。有些观众在散戏后冒雨逗留在戏院门前达一小时之久，等候向演员们鼓掌欢迎，旅法华侨每晚都向艺术团献花，并且表示这次演出使他们感到祖国的光荣。
    中国艺术团从六月十六日起将在巴黎夏乐宫再次公演，消息传出后，几天之内已经把五天的入场券全部售完。
