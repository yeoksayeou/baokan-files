Title: Indonesia and Czechoslovakia Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 12th. Antara News Agency reports that the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced that the Indonesian Cabinet decided on May 21st to establish diplomatic relations with the Czechoslovak Republic at the ambassadorial level. The Indonesian government will establish an embassy in Prague, and the Czechoslovak government will establish an embassy in Jakarta.

### 印度尼西亚和捷克斯洛伐克建立外交关系

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和捷克斯洛伐克建立外交关系
    新华社雅加达十二日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚外交部宣布：印度尼西亚内阁在五月二十一日决定与捷克斯洛伐克共和国建立大使级的外交关系。印度尼西亚政府将在布拉格设立大使馆，捷克斯洛伐克政府将在雅加达设立大使馆。
