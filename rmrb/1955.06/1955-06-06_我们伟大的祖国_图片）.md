    Our Great Motherland
    The Shanghai Industrial Production Achievements Exhibition Hall has opened in the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace. The hall displays more than 260 objects, models, and photographs, showing the new achievements made by Shanghai's industrial production in recent years. Visitors are viewing the precision machine tools manufactured by the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1955-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海市工业生产成绩展览馆已在上海工人文化宫开放。馆里陈列了二百六十多件实物、模型和照片，显示了几年来上海工业生产所获得的新成就。观众正在参观上海机床厂制造的精密工作母机。
