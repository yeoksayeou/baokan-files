Title: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulations on Sambuu's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, sent a telegram congratulating J. Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads:

Comrade J. Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic:

On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I extend my sincere and warm congratulations to you on behalf of the People's Republic of China and myself.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
June 27, 1955

### 毛主席电贺桑布六十寿辰

1955-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺桑布六十寿辰
    新华社二十八日讯中华人民共和国主席毛泽东致电祝贺蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布六十岁寿辰，电文如下：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布同志：
    当您六十岁寿辰之际，谨代表中华人民共和国和我个人向您致衷心的热烈的祝贺。
        中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
        一九五五年六月二十七日
