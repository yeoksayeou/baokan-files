Title: U Nu Leaves Israel for Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Jerusalem (Israeli area), June 6th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu has concluded his visit to Israel. He is scheduled to leave Israel for Yugoslavia on the 6th.
　　
U Nu and Israeli Prime Minister Moshe Sharett issued a joint statement on the 5th, stating that the governments of Burma and Israel are eager to continue working together for the economic, social, and cultural progress of both countries.

### 吴努离开以色列赴南斯拉夫

1955-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努离开以色列赴南斯拉夫
    新华社六日讯　耶路撒冷（以色列区）消息：缅甸总理吴努已经结束了他在以色列的访问。他订于六日离开以色列前往南斯拉夫访问。
    吴努和以色列总理摩西·夏里特在五日发表联合声明说，缅甸和以色列两国政府热望继续为两国的经济、社会和文化的进步而共同努力。
