    Our Great Motherland
    Maotouyang is one of the largest fishing grounds in the Zhoushan Islands. During this year's season for catching yellow croaker, fishermen from Zhejiang and Fujian provinces have had a bumper harvest, catching more than 30 million jin of yellow croaker in just over a month. The Putuo County Supply and Marketing Cooperative is sun-drying the purchased fresh fish.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    猫头洋是舟山群岛最大的渔场之一。在今年捕捉黄花鱼的季节里，来自浙江和福建的渔民们获得了大丰收，一个多月来就捕获了黄花鱼三千多万斤。普陀县供销合作社正在翻晒收购来的鲜鱼。
