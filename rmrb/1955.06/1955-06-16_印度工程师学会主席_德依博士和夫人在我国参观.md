Title: Chairman of the Indian Institute of Engineers, Dr. Dey and his wife visit China
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies, Dr. Dey, Chairman of the Indian Institute of Engineers, and his wife arrived in Beijing on June 1st and have since visited Beijing and various locations in Northeast China.

In Beijing, Dr. Dey and his wife visited the factory buildings and water facilities of the state-owned Beijing First and Second Cotton Textile Mills, as well as ancient Chinese architecture such as the Great Wall and the Temple of Heaven. They also held discussions with people in China's water conservancy engineering and municipal construction fields.

On June 7th, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen met with Dr. Dey and his wife.

In Northeast China, Dr. Dey and his wife visited factories in Shenyang, the open-pit coal mine in Fushun, and the three major projects of Anshan Iron and Steel Company.

### 印度工程师学会主席  德依博士和夫人在我国参观

1955-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工程师学会主席
    德依博士和夫人在我国参观
    新华社十五日讯　应中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会邀请的印度工程师学会主席德依博士和夫人，六月一日到达北京后，先后在北京和东北各地进行了参观。
    德依博士和夫人在北京参观了国营北京第一和第二棉纺织厂的厂房建筑和水道设备，以及长城、天坛等我国古代建筑，并曾和我国水利工程和市政建设界人士举行座谈。
    在六月七日，北京市市长彭真曾接见了德依博士和夫人。
    德依博士和夫人在东北参观了沈阳市的工厂，抚顺的露天煤矿和鞍山钢铁公司的三大工程。
