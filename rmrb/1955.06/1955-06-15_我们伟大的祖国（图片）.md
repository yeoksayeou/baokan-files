    Our Great Motherland
    After the completion of the Lanzhou-Wuwei section of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway, Wuwei has become a distribution center for material exchange in Gansu. Seamless steel pipes transported from Anshan Iron and Steel to Wuwei Station will be transferred to Yumen Oilfield and the Jiuquan Basin.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    兰新铁路兰州到武威段通车后，武威已成为甘肃物资交流的集散地。从鞍钢运到武威车站的无缝钢管，将转运到玉门油矿和酒泉盆地去。
