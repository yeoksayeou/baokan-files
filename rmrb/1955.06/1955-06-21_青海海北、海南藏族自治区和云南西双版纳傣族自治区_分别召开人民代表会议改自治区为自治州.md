Title: Qinghai Haibei and Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Regions and Yunnan Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region Convene People's Representative Meetings to Change Autonomous Regions to Autonomous Prefectures
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 3

Qinghai's Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region held its first Third People's Representative Meeting from May 15th to 21st. Ninety-eight representatives from Tibetan, Mongolian, Hui, and Han ethnic groups attended the meeting. According to the constitution, the meeting changed the Qinghai Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region to the Qinghai Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture. The meeting exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress and elected the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture. Xia Rong Ga Bu (Tibetan) was elected as the head of the Autonomous Prefecture, Wang Pengyuan (Han), Hua Baozang (Tibetan), Tong Quhu (Tibetan), Nanmu Kacaixiang (Tibetan), Gongbao Dangshiji (Mongolian), and Ma Luwen (Hui) were elected as deputy heads, and Zhou Xinren (Han) was elected as the president of the Autonomous Prefecture Intermediate People's Court.

The first Third People's Representative Meeting of Qinghai's Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Region was held in Chabcha from June 5th to 8th. According to the constitution, the meeting changed the Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Region to the Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture. The meeting exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress and elected the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture. Dander (Tibetan) was elected as the head of the Autonomous Prefecture, Wei Bingwen (Han), Qiequn Jia (Tibetan), Zhaori Jia (Tibetan), and Zongzhe (Tibetan) were elected as deputy heads, and Gazang Jia (Tibetan) was elected as the president of the Autonomous Prefecture Intermediate People's Court.

The meeting finally passed a letter of respect to Chairman Mao and a letter of condolence to all exploration personnel in the Qaidam Basin. The meeting also decided to send a delegation to express condolences to geological workers working in the Hainan area.

Yunnan Province's Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region was changed to an autonomous prefecture in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution.

The first meeting of the second session of the People's Representatives of all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region, held in Jinghong from June 1st to 8th, adopted the draft Provisional Regulations for the Organization of the People's Representative Meeting of the Autonomous Prefecture and the draft Provisional Regulations for the Organization of the People's Committee. The meeting exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress, electing Zhaocun Xin (Dai) as the head of the Autonomous Prefecture, Dao Chengzong (Dai), Che Luo, Liu Yan (Han), Dao Youliang (Dai), Dao Dongting (Dai), and Dao Zhiguo (Dai) as deputy heads, and Zhao Xili (Dai), Mu Lazi, Dao Jianmin and twenty-one others as members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture, and Dao Jiantang (Dai) as the president of the People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture.

Zhaocun Xin, Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, reported on the work of the Autonomous Region People's Government over the past two years and the tasks for the future. In his report, Zhaocun Xin proposed that the tasks for the next three years are: to continue to strengthen ethnic unity, further develop production, strive to increase grain production by 20% within three years, increase cotton production by 38%, and restore tea production to the highest annual output in history; to gradually stabilize cultivated land in mountainous areas, promote diversified operations; and to gradually complete the construction of grassroots political power, and correspondingly develop transportation, culture, education, and health undertakings. The meeting unanimously adopted Zhaocun Xin's report. Many representatives also criticized some shortcomings in the government's work in their speeches, and the representatives also put forward forty-seven proposals related to production, construction, culture, education, health, and ethnic relations.

Three hundred and eleven representatives of all ethnic groups living in Xishuangbanna attended the meeting. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海海北、海南藏族自治区和云南西双版纳傣族自治区  分别召开人民代表会议改自治区为自治州

1955-06-21
第3版()
专栏：

    青海海北、海南藏族自治区和云南西双版纳傣族自治区
    分别召开人民代表会议改自治区为自治州
    青海省海北藏族自治区五月十五日到二十一日召开了第一届第三次人民代表会议。出席会议的有藏、蒙、回、汉等民族的代表九十八人。会议根据宪法规定把青海省海北藏族自治区改为青海省海北藏族自治州。会议代行人民代表大会职权，选举成立了自治州人民委员会。选出夏茸尕布（藏）为自治州州长，王鹏远（汉）、华宝藏（藏）、同曲乎（藏）、南木卡才巷（藏）、贡保当什吉（蒙）、马录文（回）为副州长，周信仁（汉）为自治州中级人民法院院长。
    青海省海南藏族自治区第一届第三次人民代表会议六月五日到八日在恰卜恰举行。会议根据宪法规定，把海南藏族自治区改为海南藏族自治州。会议代行人民代表大会职权，选举成立了自治州人民委员会。选出丹德尔（藏）为自治州州长，魏炳文（汉）、切群加（藏）、朝日加（藏）、宗哲（藏）为副州长，尕藏加（藏）为自治州中级人民法院院长。
    会议最后通过了向毛主席的致敬电，和致柴达木盆地全体勘探人员的慰问电。会议并决定派遣代表小组前往慰问在海南地区工作的地质工作人员。
    云南省西双版纳傣族自治区依照宪法规定改为自治州。
    六月一日到八日在允景洪举行的自治区第二届各族人民代表会议第一次会议，通过了自治州人民代表会议组织暂行条例草案和人民委员会组织暂行条例草案。会议代行了人民代表大会职权，选出召存信（傣）为自治州州长，刀承宗（傣）、车罗、刘岩（汉）、刀有良（傣）、刀栋庭（傣）、刀治国（傣）为副州长，召西里（傣）、沐腊兹、刀建民等二十四人为自治州人民委员会委员，刀建堂（傣）为自治州人民法院院长。
    自治区人民政府主席召存信在会上报告了自治区人民政府成立两年多来的工作和今后工作任务。召存信在报告中提出今后三年的工作任务是：继续增强民族团结，进一步发展生产，争取三年内粮食增产百分之二十，棉花增产百分之三十八，茶叶要恢复到历史上最高年产量；山区逐步固定耕地，提倡多种经营；并逐步完成基层政权建设，相应发展交通运输、文教、卫生各项事业。会议一致通过了召存信的这个报告。许多代表在发言中对政府工作中的某些缺点也进行了批评，代表们还提出了有关生产、建设、文教卫生、民族关系等方面提案四十七件。
    出席这次会议的有居住在西双版纳的各民族的代表三百一十一人。（据新华社讯）
