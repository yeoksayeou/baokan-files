Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Receives Japanese Peace Activists Miyazaki Ryusuke and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 31st. Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," received Miyazaki Ryusuke, Chairman of the Tokyo Peace Conference of Japan, and Miyazaki Semin, a member of the Japanese Peace Protection Committee, and others this afternoon.
    Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, was present at the reception.

### 宋庆龄副委员长  接见日本和平人士宫崎龙介等

1955-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长
    接见日本和平人士宫崎龙介等
    新华社上海三十一日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者宋庆龄，在三十一日下午接见了日本东京都和平会议主席宫崎龙介和日本拥护和平委员会委员宫崎世民等。
    接见时在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会副主席金仲华。
