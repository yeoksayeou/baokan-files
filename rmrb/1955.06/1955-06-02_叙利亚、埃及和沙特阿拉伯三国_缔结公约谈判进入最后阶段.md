Title: Negotiations for Convention Among Syria, Egypt and Saudi Arabia Enter Final Stage
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 31st (TASS): According to the Arab News Agency, the talks on the conclusion of a convention between Syria, Egypt and Saudi Arabia have entered the final stage. Currently, the participants in the talks are discussing the establishment of a permanent political council and the issue of a joint defense fund.

### 叙利亚、埃及和沙特阿拉伯三国  缔结公约谈判进入最后阶段

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚、埃及和沙特阿拉伯三国
    缔结公约谈判进入最后阶段
    新华社三十一日讯　塔斯社开罗三十一日
  讯：据阿拉伯通讯社报道，关于缔结叙利亚、
  埃及和沙特阿拉伯之间的公约的会谈已进入最
  后阶段。现在，会谈参加者正在讨论关于设立常
  设政治理事会和关于共同防务基金的问题。
