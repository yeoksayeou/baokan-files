Title: Nehru Returns to Moscow After Visiting Various Parts of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st dispatch: According to TASS, the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, and his entourage, who are currently on a friendly visit to various parts of the Soviet Union, returned to Moscow by train on the morning of the 21st from Leningrad.

    On the 20th, Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage continued their tour and visits in Leningrad. In the morning, the guests visited the Stalin Machine Manufacturing Plant, where they observed the production of large-scale steam turbines and water turbines. Prime Minister Nehru was very interested in visiting a factory that assembled such enormous machines, and at the request of the workers, he signed his book "The Discovery of India," which was recently published in Russian.

    Subsequently, Nehru and others visited the Zhdanov Palace of Young Pioneers. He toured the exhibits with the children, including gifts sent to the Leningrad Young Pioneers from many countries such as India, China, and France, as well as the planetarium, the fairy tale book room, and the student works exhibition of the technical group. Prime Minister Nehru watched the children happily playing for a long time. The Young Pioneers presented Nehru with a small carved box and a model of a warship made by themselves. Nehru wished the children new achievements and gave them a small sandalwood stick.

    In the afternoon, Prime Minister Nehru visited Peterhof Palace. Thousands of tourists warmly welcomed Prime Minister Nehru. Following that, Prime Minister Nehru toured the streets and embankment streets, and visited the Peter the Great Museum in the Summer Garden.

    In the evening, Prime Minister Nehru attended a banquet hosted by Smirnov, Chairman of the Leningrad City Soviet Executive Committee.

### 尼赫鲁在苏联各地访问后回莫斯科

1955-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁在苏联各地访问后回莫斯科
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社消息：正在苏联各地作友好访问的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁和随员，二十一日晨乘车从列宁格勒返莫斯科。
    二十日，尼赫鲁总理和随员，继续在列宁格勒参观和访问。上午，客人们参观了斯大林机器制造厂。他们在这里观看了正在制造中的大型汽轮机和水轮机等。尼赫鲁总理对参观装配这么巨大的机器的工厂很感兴趣，他并且应工人们的请求在不久前用俄文出版的他的著作“印度的发现”一书上签了名。
    随后，尼赫鲁等到日丹诺夫少年先锋宫参观。他同儿童们一起参观了列宁格勒少年收到由印度、中国、法国等许多国家寄来的礼物，以及天象仪馆、童话书籍房间和技术组学生的作品展览会。尼赫鲁总理长久地观看孩子们怎样欢乐嬉戏。少年先锋队员们送给了尼赫鲁一个自己作的雕刻小匣子和一个军舰模型。尼赫鲁祝孩子们获得新的成绩，并送给了他们一根小檀木棍。
    下午，尼赫鲁总理游览了彼得宫。成千上万的游人都向尼赫鲁总理表示热烈欢迎。接着，尼赫鲁总理游览了大街和堤岸街，参观了夏季花园中的彼得一世博物馆。
    晚间，尼赫鲁总理出席了列宁格勒市苏维埃执行委员会主席斯米尔诺夫的招待宴会。
