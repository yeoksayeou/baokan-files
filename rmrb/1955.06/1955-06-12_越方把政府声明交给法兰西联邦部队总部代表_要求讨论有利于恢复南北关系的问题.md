Title: Vietnamese Side Hands Government Statement to Representative of French Federal Forces Headquarters, Requesting Discussion of Issues Conducive to Restoring North-South Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 11th: According to Vietnam News Agency, General Van Tien Dung, Chief Delegate of the General Command Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army to the Central Joint Ceasefire Commission in Vietnam, formally handed over the statement issued by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on restoring normal relations between North and South Vietnam, along with relevant documents, to General Chawat, Chief Delegate of the General Command Delegation of the French Federal Forces on the 9th, requesting him to forward it to the General Command of the French Federal Forces and the responsible authorities in South Vietnam.

General Van Tien Dung requested the Central Joint Ceasefire Commission in Vietnam to discuss and resolve issues conducive to restoring relations between North and South Vietnam.

### 越方把政府声明交给法兰西联邦部队总部代表  要求讨论有利于恢复南北关系的问题

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    越方把政府声明交给法兰西联邦部队总部代表
    要求讨论有利于恢复南北关系的问题
    新华社河内十一日电　据越南通讯社讯：驻越南中央联合停战委员会的越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇将军，在九日正式把越南民主共和国政府发表的关于恢复越南北部和南部间正常关系的声明和有关文件交给法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团首席代表夏瓦特将军，要他转交法兰西联邦部队总司令部和南越负责当局。
    文进勇将军要求越南中央联合停战委员会讨论和解决有利于恢复越南北部和南部关系的一些问题。
