Title: State Council Appoints Personnel to State Organs, Embassies Abroad, and Universities

Author: 
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on May 31, appointing a number of personnel to state organs, embassies abroad, and universities. The list is as follows:
    Liu Jie as Deputy Director of the Third Office of the State Council;
    Zhang Zhiyi as Deputy Director of the Eighth Office of the State Council;
    Liu Jingfan as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Geology, Liu Wei as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Geology;
    Ma Huizhi as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications;
    Liu Zhiming as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture;
    Xue Chunpu as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea;
    Ye Jinghao as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam;
    Du Yun as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of India;
    Zhang Xiancheng as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Sweden;
    Bi Kejing as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Burma;
    Yang Lin as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Indonesia;
    Zhang Yunxiao as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Swiss Confederation;
    Gao Shangneng as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Pakistan;
  　Xie Shouting as Commercial Counselor of the Office of the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom;
    Pu Kuican as Vice President of Yanbian University;
    Wang Manshuo as Vice President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts;
    Li Zikang as President of Shanxi Agricultural College;
    Zheng Wenqing as President of East China University of Political Science and Law, Zhao Yemin as Vice President;
    Wu Shaoqui as Vice President of Henan Agricultural College;
    Chen Yongling and Luo Xiong Cai as Vice Presidents of South China Institute of Technology;
    Li Peiwen as Vice President of South China Agricultural College;
    Sun Zhongyi as President of Guangxi Agricultural College, Yang Dehua and Chen Qingyuan as Vice Presidents.　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 国务院任命一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员

1955-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任命一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院五月三十一日发布命令，任命了一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员，名单如下：
    刘杰为国务院第三办公室副主任；
    张执一为国务院第八办公室副主任；
    刘景范为地质部副部长，刘伟为地质部部长助理；
    马辉之为交通部副部长；
    刘芝明为文化部副部长；
    薛春圃为中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    叶景灏为中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    杜毓澐为中华人民共和国驻印度共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    张先成为中华人民共和国驻瑞典王国大使馆商务参赞；
    毕可敬为中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦大使馆商务参赞；
    杨琳为中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    张云啸为中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    高尚能为中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦大使馆商务参赞；
  　谢寿天为中华人民共和国驻英国代办处商务参赞；
    朴奎灿为延边大学副校长；
    王曼硕为中央美术学院副院长；
    李子康为山西农学院院长；
    郑文卿为华东政法学院院长，赵野民为副院长；
    吴绍骙为河南农学院副院长；
    陈永龄、罗雄才为华南工学院副院长；
    李沛文为华南农学院副院长；
    孙仲逸为广西农学院院长，杨德华、陈清源为副院长。　（新华社）
