Title: Japan Peace Advocates Conference Held in Tokyo: Delegation to World Peace Congress Elected and Demands for Restoration of Diplomatic Relations with China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 8th: The Japan Peace Advocates Conference, which opened in Tokyo on June 4th, closed on June 6th. Approximately 500 delegates from all over the country participated in the conference.

    The conference elected the Japanese representatives to attend the World Peace Congress. These included Chairman of the Japan Peace Committee, Ikuo Oyama; Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Jiichiro Matsumoto; Secretary-General of the Japan National Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, Kaoru Yasui; as well as individuals from labor unions, cultural circles, and other broad sectors.

    The conference passed a resolution demanding that Japan restore diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union and China, and opposing the acceptance of enriched uranium from the United States.

    At the conference, Kaoru Yasui announced that the number of people nationwide in Japan who have signed petitions against the use of atomic weapons has increased to 23,910,000.

### 日本和平爱好者大会在东京举行  选出出席世界和平大会的代表团并要求恢复同中苏的邦交

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和平爱好者大会在东京举行
    选出出席世界和平大会的代表团并要求恢复同中苏的邦交
    新华社八日讯　东京消息：六月四日在东京开幕的日本和平爱好者大会已于六日闭幕。参加大会的有来自全国各地的大约五百名代表。
    大会选出了参加世界和平大会的日本代表，其中包括日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫、日中友好协会会长松本治一郎、日本禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会事务局长安井郁以及工会、文化界等广泛阶层的人士。
    大会通过决议，要求日本同苏联和中国恢复邦交，并且反对接受美国的浓缩铀。
    安井郁在会上宣布，目前，日本全国签名反对使用原子武器的人数已经增加到两千三百九十一万人。
