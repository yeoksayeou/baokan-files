Title: Xinjiang Sends Four Ethnic Minority Health Work Teams
Author:
Date: 1955-06-14
Page: 2

    Xinjiang Sends Four Ethnic Minority Health Work Teams

    The Xinjiang Provincial Institute of Dermatology and Venereology dispatched four ethnic minority health work teams from May 27th to June 9th to Bayingolin Mongolian Autonomous Prefecture and Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture's Toli, Zhaosu, Ussuri and other places to provide free treatment for venereal diseases to herdsmen and farmers of all ethnic groups. The work team going to Ussuri will engage in long-term treatment work there.

    In 1953 and 1954, medical personnel from the Xinjiang Provincial Institute of Dermatology and Venereology had provided the first course of treatment to more than 14,000 people suffering from venereal diseases in pastoral areas of Toli in northern Xinjiang, Bayingolin Mongolian Autonomous Prefecture, and Kuqa and other places in southern Xinjiang.

    Recently, an outpatient clinic established by the Institute of Dermatology and Venereology in Urumqi City is about to begin work. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆派出四个民族卫生工作队

1955-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆派出四个民族卫生工作队
    新疆省皮肤性病防治所从五月二十七日到六月九日派出了四个民族卫生工作队，分赴巴音郭楞蒙古族自治州和伊犁哈萨克族自治州的托里、昭苏、乌苏等地，为各族牧民和农民免费治疗性病。去乌苏的工作队将在那里从事长期的治疗工作。
    一九五三年和一九五四年中，新疆省皮肤性病防治所的医务人员曾在新疆北部的托里、巴音郭楞蒙古族自治州的牧区和新疆南部的库车等地，给一万四千多患性病的人进行了第一个疗程的治疗工作。
    最近，皮肤性病防治所在乌鲁木齐市筹设的一个门诊部，即将开始工作。　（新华社）
