Title: Qian Junrui Hosts a Banquet for the Indian Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 8th — Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of June 8th to entertain the Indian Cultural Delegation, which had been invited to visit our country.

    The entire Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by M.J. Desai, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Government's Ministry of External Affairs, and Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, were invited to the banquet.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun attended the banquet.

    Also present at the banquet were Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture and President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, and Chen Kehan, Vice Ministers of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing; as well as heads of various people's organizations and prominent figures in the capital's literary and artistic circles.

    Qian Junrui delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet, wishing the Indian Cultural Delegation success in their artistic performances in our capital and other regions.

    Desai then spoke. He said that the Indian Cultural Delegation would contribute Indian art and culture to the Chinese people in order to promote the development of Sino-Indian friendly relations.

    Guests and hosts engaged in earnest conversation during the banquet. After the banquet, an acrobatic performance was given.

### 钱俊瑞设宴招待印度文化代表团

1955-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    钱俊瑞设宴招待印度文化代表团
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞八日晚设宴招待应邀访问我国的印度文化代表团。
    以印度政府外交部副部长钱达为首的印度文化代表团全体人员和印度驻华大使赖嘉文都应邀出席了宴会。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅、秘书长习仲勋出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有，文化部副部长、中印友好协会会长丁西林，文化部副部长郑振铎、夏衍、陈克寒，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长冯基平，以及各人民团体的负责人和首都文艺界著名人士等。
    钱俊瑞在宴会上致欢迎词，预祝印度文化代表团在我国首都和各地进行的艺术表演获得成功。
    钱达接着讲话。他说，印度文化代表团将把印度的艺术和文化贡献给中国人民，并以此来促进中印友好关系的发展。
    宴会中宾主双方恳切交谈。宴会后，表演了杂技节目。
