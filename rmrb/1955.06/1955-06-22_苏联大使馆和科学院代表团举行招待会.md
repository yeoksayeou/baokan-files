Title: Soviet Embassy and Academy of Sciences Delegation Hold Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-06-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 21st: The Soviet Embassy in China and the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation jointly held a reception on the evening of the 21st for the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation's visit to China.

    At the reception, Romanin, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy, spoke first. He said that we are pleased to see that the ties between the two great countries of the Soviet Union and China are becoming increasingly close in all aspects, and we wish that this relationship will be further strengthened, developed, and consolidated.

    Academician Bardin, head of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, said that the cooperation between the Soviet Union and China in science is the greatest cooperation in the world, and this cooperation will defeat any enemy who attempts to undermine our peaceful construction.

    At the reception, Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of China, and Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, spoke successively, expressing their gratitude to the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation led by Academician Bardin for their assistance to the Chinese scientific community during their visit to China, and pointed out that Chinese scientists should strive to learn advanced Soviet scientific knowledge.

    At the venue, joyous Sino-Soviet songs were constantly broadcast through loudspeakers, and scientists from both countries spoke one after another, congratulating the closer cooperation between the scientists of the two countries.

    Among those present at the reception tonight were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Chen Boda, Zhang Jiafu, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and well-known scientists from the capital, including Zhao Zhongyao, Yan Jici, Zhou Ren, Fan Wenlan, and Luo Changpei.

### 苏联大使馆和科学院代表团举行招待会

1955-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大使馆和科学院代表团举行招待会
    新华社二十一日讯　二十一日晚，苏联驻华大使馆和苏联科学院代表团为苏联科学院代表团访问中国联合举行招待会。
    招待会上，苏联大使馆临时代办罗迈进首先讲话。他说，我们高兴地看到苏中两个伟大的国家在各方面的联系日益密切起来，我们预祝这种联系进一步加强、发展和巩固。
    苏联科学院代表团团长巴尔金院士说，苏中两国在科学事业上的合作，是世界上最伟大的合作，这种合作将会使任何企图破坏我们和平建设的敌人归于失败。
    在招待会上，我国国务院副总理陈毅、科学院副院长竺可桢先后讲话，对以巴尔金院士为首的苏联科学院代表团在访问中国期间，给中国科学界的帮助表示感谢，指出中国科学家应努力学习苏联先进的科学知识。
    会场上，扩音器不断传送着欢乐的中苏歌曲，两国科学家们纷纷讲话，祝贺两国科学家更加密切地合作。
    出席今晚招待会的，有外交部副部长张闻天，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，中国科学院副院长陈伯达、张稼夫、吴有训，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，北京市副市长吴晗以及首都著名科学家赵忠尧、严济慈、周仁、范文澜、罗常培等。
