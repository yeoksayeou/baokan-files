Title: China's Sports Delegation Goes to Prague to Participate in the Czechoslovak National Games
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: The Chinese sports delegation, consisting of six people, left Beijing by plane for Prague on the morning of the 18th to participate in the first Czechoslovak National Games. The delegation is headed by Cai Shufan and Huang Qixiang, Deputy Directors of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, as the head and deputy head respectively.

　　Lu Han, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and many others were at the airport to see them off.

　　Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Gregor, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.

　　Prior to this, the Chinese gymnastics team, consisting of 159 people, who will also participate in the games, had already left for Prague by train on June 8th. The Chinese gymnastics team will perform group exercises and Chinese folk sports programs at the games.

### 参加捷克斯洛伐克全国运动大会  我国体育代表团去布拉格

1955-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    参加捷克斯洛伐克全国运动大会
    我国体育代表团去布拉格
    新华社十八日讯参加捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的中国体育代表团一行六人十八日晨乘飞机离北京前往布拉格。代表团由中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩、黄琪翔任团长和副团长。
    到机场欢送的有体育运动委员会副主任卢汉等多人。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使格里哥尔和使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    在此以前，参加这次运动大会的由一百五十九人组成的中国体操队已在六月八日乘火车前往布拉格。中国体操队将在运动大会上表演团体操和我国的民间体育节目。
