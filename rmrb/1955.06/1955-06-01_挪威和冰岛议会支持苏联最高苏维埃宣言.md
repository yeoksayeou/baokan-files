Title: Norwegian and Icelandic Parliaments Support the Declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 31st, TASS Moscow, May 30th: On May 28th, the Norwegian Ambassador to Moscow handed the reply of the Presidium of the Norwegian Parliament to the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR of February 9th of this year to the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The reply stated:
	“The Presidium notes the importance emphasized by the Supreme Soviet of strengthening ties between parliaments of various countries. The Committee of Speakers wishes to declare that it considers it of great significance to strengthen contacts between parliamentarians of various countries and that it can contribute to the easing of international tensions.”
	The reply of the Presidium of the Norwegian Parliament has been notified to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
	Xinhua News Agency, May 29th, TASS Moscow, May 28th: According to the Icelandic Legation in Moscow, the Icelandic Ministry of Foreign Affairs has handed the full text of the February 9, 1955, declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to the Icelandic Parliament.
	The speakers of both houses of parliament stated in their reply to the Icelandic Ministry of Foreign Affairs:
  “After consulting with all parties in parliament, we believe that closer ties between parliaments of various countries and mutual visits between parliamentarians will actively promote mutual understanding and the strengthening of friendly ties between countries, and therefore we support this proposal.”
	At the request of the Icelandic Legation, the relevant Soviet authorities have conveyed the above opinions of the Icelandic Parliament to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 挪威和冰岛议会支持苏联最高苏维埃宣言

1955-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威和冰岛议会支持苏联最高苏维埃宣言
    新华社三十一日讯　塔斯社莫斯科三十日讯：五月二十八日，挪威驻莫斯科大使把挪威议会主席团对苏联最高苏维埃今年二月九日的宣言的答复交给苏联外交部。这个答复中说：
      “主席团注意到，最高苏维埃强调各国议会加强联系的意义。议长委员会愿意声明，它认为各国议会议员加强接触具有重大的意义，并能够促使国际紧张局势趋于缓和。”
    挪威议会主席团的答复已经通知苏联最高苏维埃。
    新华社二十九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：据冰岛驻莫斯科公使馆说，冰岛外交部已将一九五五年二月九日苏联最高苏维埃宣言全文交给冰岛议会。
    议会两院议长给冰岛外交部的答复中说道：
  “会同议会中各党派磋商之后，我们认为，各国议会之间更加紧密的联系，议员之间相互的访问，会积极促进各国相互谅解和友好联系的加强，因此我们支持这种主张。”
    根据冰岛公使馆的请求，苏联有关方面已将冰岛议会的上述意见转达给苏联最高苏维埃。
