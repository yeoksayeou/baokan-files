Title: Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Sastroamidjojo
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, hosted a banquet at Zhongnanhai on the evening of the 1st in honor of the Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife, the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, the Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Sukardjo Wijopranoto, and his wife, the Indonesian Ambassador to India, Bala, the Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Subandrio, the Director of the Indonesian National Planning Bureau, Djuanda, a senior official of the Foreign Economic Relations Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy, Hasuno Reksoamojo, the Prime Minister's personal physician, Sayono, the Prime Minister's adjutant, Waluyo, and the Prime Minister's personal secretary, Rachasa, as well as the military attache of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Lieutenant Colonel Salim and his wife, and First Secretary Wijowiwirdojo and his wife were all invited to attend the banquet.

Those present to accompany Chairman Mao were: Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai.

Also present were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen.

Also present were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Directors of the various offices Luo Ruiqing, Lin Feng, and Jia Tuofu, Ministers of the various ministries Xie Juezai, Zhang Naiqi, Ye Jizhuang, Chen Yu, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Fu Zuoyi, Ma Wenrui, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Li Dequan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen.

Also present were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, and Deputy Director of the General Political Department Tan Zheng.

Also present were: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference He Xiangning, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Baurhan, and Vice Presidents Hu Yuzhi and Ma Yuhuai, and Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association Dapusheng.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of warmth and friendship.

After the banquet, there was a song and dance and Peking opera performance.

### 毛主席欢宴沙斯特罗阿米佐约

1955-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席欢宴沙斯特罗阿米佐约
    新华社一日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东，一日晚在中南海欢宴印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人、印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图、印度尼西亚外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和夫人、印度尼西亚驻印度大使巴拉、印度尼西亚驻苏联大使苏班德里奥、印度尼西亚国家计划局局长朱安达、印度尼西亚经济部对外经济关系司高级官员哈苏诺·雷克梭阿莫佐、总理私人医生沙约诺、总理副官瓦鲁约、总理私人秘书拉查沙，以及印度尼西亚驻华大使馆武官沙林中校和夫人、一等秘书维约维尔多约和夫人都应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有：中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来。
    出席作陪的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真。
    出席作陪的还有：国务院副总理邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，各办公室主任罗瑞卿、林枫、贾拓夫，各部部长谢觉哉、章乃器、叶季壮、陈郁、沙千里、滕代远、朱学范、廖鲁言、傅作义、马文瑞、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若、李德全，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫和中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
    出席作陪的还有：最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕，总政治部副主任谭政。
    出席作陪的还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉，副会长胡愈之、马玉槐，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    宴会后，举行了歌舞和京剧晚会。
