Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Approves Austrian State Treaty
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th, TASS Moscow, June 11th: On June 11th, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR reviewed the Austrian State Treaty on the Restoration of an Independent and Democratic Austria, signed in Vienna on May 15, 1955, by the plenipotentiaries of the USSR, the United Kingdom, the United States, France, and Austria, submitted for approval by the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR decided to approve the Austrian State Treaty on the Restoration of an Independent and Democratic Austria, proposed by the Council of Ministers of the USSR and endorsed by the Foreign Affairs Committees of both the Union Council and the Council of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

Xinhua News Agency, TASS Moscow, June 11th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued the following decree on the approval of the Austrian State Treaty on the Restoration of Independence and Democracy:

The Austrian State Treaty on the Restoration of Independence and Democracy, signed in Vienna on May 15, 1955, proposed by the Council of Ministers of the USSR and endorsed by the Foreign Affairs Committees of both the Union Council and the Council of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, is hereby approved.

K. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
N. Pegov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
Moscow, Kremlin, June 11, 1955

### 苏最高苏维埃主席团批准奥地利国家条约

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃主席团批准奥地利国家条约
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：六月十一日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团审查了苏联部长会议提请批准的苏联、英国、美国、法国和奥地利的全权代表一九五五年五月十五日在维也纳签订的关于恢复独立和民主的奥地利国家条约。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定批准由苏联部长会议提出并经苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院两院外交委员会赞同的关于恢复独立和民主的奥地利国家条约。
    新华社讯　塔斯社莫斯科十一日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布关于批准恢复独立和民主的奥地利国家条约的命令如下：
    兹批准由苏联部长会议提出并经苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院两院外交委员会赞同的一九五五年五月十五日在维也纳签订的关于恢复独立和民主的奥地利国家条约。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书尼·别哥夫
                一九五五年六月十一日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
