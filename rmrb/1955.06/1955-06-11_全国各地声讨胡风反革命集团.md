Title: Condemnation of the Hu Feng Counter-Revolutionary Clique Throughout the Country
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: In recent days, people from all walks of life in many provinces and cities across the country have held report meetings and symposiums to continue to thoroughly launch the struggle against the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique.

Yan Dakai, Deputy Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, made a report to the heads of various departments on the crimes of denouncing the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique on the 7th. On the 8th, the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party directly under the organs organized the heads of Party and government organs to make mobilization reports on denouncing the crimes of the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique to more than 13,000 staff members of the Hebei Provincial organs directly under the organs and students of various cadre training classes in Baoding City.

More than 1,700 people from all walks of life in Henan Province and Zhengzhou City also held a gathering on the 8th. At the meeting, Song Yuxi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, made a report. He called on the people of Henan Province to take action and actively participate in the struggle to smash the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique and thoroughly eliminate the ideological influence of the Hu Feng clique in Henan Province.

On the 8th, more than 6,000 staff members of organs in Xining City held a gathering to listen to a report by Sun Junyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The report systematically exposed the counter-Party and counter-revolutionary crimes of the Hu Feng clique, calling on everyone to be vigilant and eliminate all counter-revolutionary elements.

In recent days, tens of thousands of staff members of organs, military personnel, and people from the cultural and educational circles in Hangzhou City, Zhejiang Province, and Hangzhou City have participated in the report meeting entitled "Improve Vigilance and Strive to Eliminate the Hu Feng Anti-Party Clique and Its Ideological Influence." After the meeting, some units also held symposiums.

The Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Chongqing Municipal Committee held report conferences and symposiums to denounce the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique on the 9th. More than 4,000 people from all walks of life attended the report conference. Ren Baige, Mayor of Chongqing, made a report at the meeting. He exposed and analyzed the crimes of the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique, calling on everyone to improve their vigilance and recognize the serious harm of the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique's sabotage activities.

On June 8, the Propaganda Department of the Inner Mongolia Branch of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held a report meeting attended by more than 5,000 staff members of organs, exposing the crimes of the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique. On the afternoon of the 9th, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a symposium on "The Hu Feng Counter-Revolutionary Clique Issue" for people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life.

The Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and other units held gatherings on the 8th and 9th to denounce the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique. The meeting passed the "Indictment of the Crimes of the Hu Feng Counter-Revolutionary Clique."

Since the 7th, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has organized Party reporters to successively report to about 300,000 people, including cadres of organs, staff members of state-owned and joint state-private enterprises, faculty members of schools at all levels, university and high school students, literary, scientific, and health workers, and people from democratic parties. At the request of the general public, the Wuhan Branch of the Chinese Writers Association has held eight report meetings on the crimes of the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique in the past week. On the 6th, the Fujian Provincial Organs and the Fuzhou Municipal Organs held a mobilization meeting to denounce the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique. Ye Fei, Secretary of the Fujian Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, made a report at the meeting, calling for the launch of a struggle against the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique in all departments of the province. On the afternoon of the 7th, the person in charge of the Propaganda Department of the Xinjiang Branch of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party made a report on the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique to more than 20,000 cadres of all ethnic groups in Urumqi City.

### 全国各地声讨胡风反革命集团

1955-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地声讨胡风反革命集团
    据新华社讯　连日来全国许多省市各界人民分别举行报告会和座谈会，继续深入展开对胡风反革命集团的斗争。
    中共河北省委员会副书记阎达开七日向各部门负责人作了关于声讨胡风反革命集团罪行的报告。中共河北省直属机关委员会在八日组织党政机关负责人分别向河北省直属机关工作人员和保定市各干部训练班的学员共一万三千多人，作了关于声讨胡风反革命集团罪行的动员报告。
    河南省和郑州市各界人士一千七百多人也在八日举行集会。会上，中共河南省委员会宣传部部长宋玉玺作了报告，他号召河南全省人民行动起来，踊跃参加粉碎胡风反革命集团的斗争，彻底肃清胡风集团在河南省的思想影响。
    八日，西宁市六千多名机关工作人员举行集会，听取中共青海省委员会宣传部长孙君一的报告。报告系统地揭露了胡风集团反党反革命的罪恶事实，号召大家提高警惕，肃清一切反革命分子。
    浙江省、杭州市连日来有上万名机关工作人员、部队人员和杭州市文教界人士参加了以“提高警惕，为肃清胡风反党集团及其思想影响而斗争”为题的报告会。会后，有些单位还举行了座谈会。
    中国人民政治协商会议四川省和重庆市委员会在九日分别举行声讨胡风反革命集团的报告大会和座谈会。参加报告大会的有各界人士等四千多人。重庆市市长任白戈在会上作了报告。他揭露并分析了胡风反革命集团的罪行，号召大家提高警惕，认清胡风反革命集团破坏活动的严重危害性。
    六月八日，中共中央内蒙古分局宣传部举办了五千多名机关工作人员参加的报告会，会上揭露了胡风反革命集团的罪行。九日下午，中国人民政治协商会议内蒙古自治区委员会举行了各族各界“关于胡风反革命集团问题”座谈会。
    中国人民政治协商会议广东省委员会和中国人民政治协商会议广州市委员会等单位在八、九两日分别集会声讨胡风反革命集团。会上通过了“声讨胡风反革命集团的罪行书”。
    中共上海市委员会从七日起组织党的报告员陆续向机关干部、国营和公私合营企业的工作人员、各级学校教职员、大学和高中学生、文艺和科学、卫生工作者以及民主党派人士约三十万人做报告。中国作家协会武汉分会应广大群众要求，一周以来连续举办了八次关于胡风反革命集团罪行的报告会。福建省省级机关和福州市机关六日举行声讨胡风反革命集团的动员大会。中共福建省委员会书记叶飞在会上作了报告，号召在全省各个部门展开对胡风反革命集团的斗争。中共中央新疆分局宣传部负责人七日下午向乌鲁木齐市两万多名各民族干部作了关于胡风反革命集团的报告。
