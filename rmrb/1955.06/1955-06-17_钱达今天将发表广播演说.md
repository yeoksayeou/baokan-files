Title: Chandrasekharan to Deliver Radio Address Today
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th — Mr. A. K. Chandrasekharan, head of the Indian cultural delegation, at the invitation of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, will deliver a radio address to Chinese listeners on the nationwide joint broadcast program of the People's Broadcasting Stations at 20:00 on the 17th. A recording of Mr. Chandrasekharan's speech will also be broadcast on the English broadcast program of the Central People's Broadcasting Station on the 17th.

### 钱达今天将发表广播演说

1955-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    钱达今天将发表广播演说
    新华社十六日讯　印度文化代表团团长阿·库·钱达先生应中央人民广播电台邀请，将在十七日二十时全国各地人民广播电台联播节目里，向中国听众发表广播演说。钱达先生的演说录音还将在中央人民广播电台十七日的英语广播节目中播送。
