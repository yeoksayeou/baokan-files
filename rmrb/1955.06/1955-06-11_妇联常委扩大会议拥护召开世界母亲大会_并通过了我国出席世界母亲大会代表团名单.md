Title: Women's Federation Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting Endorses the Convening of the World Mothers' Congress and Approves the List of the Chinese Delegation to the World Mothers' Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch – The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held an enlarged meeting of its Standing Committee on the 10th to discuss the issue of Chinese women participating in the World Mothers' Congress.

Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and member of the Preparatory Committee for the World Mothers' Congress, reported at the meeting on the important significance of convening the World Mothers' Congress for safeguarding world peace and protecting children, as well as the enthusiastic support of people and women around the world for the World Mothers' Congress. She also reported in detail the enthusiastic support of the Chinese people and women for the convening of the World Mothers' Congress, and the active preparations made by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children to attend the congress.

The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution endorsing the convening of the World Mothers' Congress and discussed and approved the list of the Chinese delegation to the World Mothers' Congress.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch – The resolution of the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on "Endorsing the Convening of the World Mothers' Congress."

The Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation warmly endorses the World Mothers' Congress initiated by the Women's International Democratic Federation. We firmly believe that the convening of this congress will further unite the strength of mothers around the world and make outstanding contributions to "defending children, opposing war, striving for disarmament, and strengthening friendship between people of all countries." The Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation approved the list of the delegation to the World Mothers' Congress headed by Vice-Chairman Li Dequan, and called on all Chinese women to warmly endorse and actively support the upcoming World Mothers' Congress.

### 妇联常委扩大会议拥护召开世界母亲大会  并通过了我国出席世界母亲大会代表团名单

1955-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    妇联常委扩大会议拥护召开世界母亲大会
    并通过了我国出席世界母亲大会代表团名单
    新华社十日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会在十日举行常务委员会扩大会议，讨论关于中国妇女参加世界母亲大会的问题。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记、世界母亲大会筹备委员会委员曹孟君在会上报告了召开世界母亲大会对于保卫世界和平、保卫儿童的重要意义，以及全世界人民和妇女热烈拥护世界母亲大会的情形。她还详细地报告了中国人民和妇女热烈支持世界母亲大会的召开，以及中华全国民主妇女联合会和中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会为出席这次大会积极进行筹备工作的情形。
    会议一致通过了拥护召开世界母亲大会的决议，并讨论通过了中国出席世界母亲大会代表团名单。
    新华社十日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会常务委员会扩大会议关于“拥护召开世界母亲大会”的决议。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会常务委员会扩大会议热烈拥护国际民主妇女联合会发起召开的世界母亲大会，我们深信这个大会的召开定将更进一步团聚全世界母亲的力量，为“保卫孩子、反对战争、争取裁减军备和加强各国人民之间的友谊”而作出卓越的贡献。中华全国民主妇女联合会常务委员会扩大会议批准了以李德全副主席为首的出席世界母亲大会代表团名单，并号召全中国妇女热烈拥护并积极支持即将召开的世界母亲大会。
