Title: Japanese Communist Party Leader Takenaka Tsunesaburo Released
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th— News: Under strong protest from the Japanese people, the Tokyo District Court released Takenaka Tsunesaburo, a leading cadre of the Japanese Communist Party who had been illegally arrested by the police authorities, on bail this afternoon.

### 日本共产党领导干部  竹中恒三郎获释

1955-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党领导干部
    竹中恒三郎获释
    新华社十七日讯　东京消息：在日本人民的强烈抗议下，东京地方裁判所在十七日下午已让被警察当局非法逮捕的日本共产党领导干部竹中恒三郎缴纳保证金出狱。
