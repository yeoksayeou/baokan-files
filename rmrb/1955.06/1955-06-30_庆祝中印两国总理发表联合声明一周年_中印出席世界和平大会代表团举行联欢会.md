Title: Celebration of the First Anniversary of the Joint Communiqué Issued by the Prime Ministers of China and India: Chinese and Indian Delegations to the World Peace Congress Hold a Joint Celebration

Author: 
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, June 29th: The Chinese delegation and the Indian delegation attending the World Peace Congress held a joint celebration on the 28th to commemorate the first anniversary of the joint statement of the Five Principles issued by the Prime Ministers of China and India.

Chen Shutong, Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation, and Sokhe, Deputy Head of the Indian Delegation, spoke at the celebration. They both emphasized the peaceful aspirations of the Chinese and Indian people, and pointed out the great significance of the peaceful and friendly relations between China and India, established on the basis of the Five Principles, for safeguarding peace in Asia and the world. The atmosphere of the celebration was extremely warm and friendly. Artists from the Indian delegation performed songs and other programs at the meeting. On this day, the Chinese delegation also held a joint celebration with the French delegation.

### 庆祝中印两国总理发表联合声明一周年  中印出席世界和平大会代表团举行联欢会

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中印两国总理发表联合声明一周年
    中印出席世界和平大会代表团举行联欢会
    新华社赫尔辛基二十九日电　出席世界和平大会的中国代表团和印度代表团二十八日举行联欢会，庆祝中印两国总理发表五项原则联合声明一周年纪念。
    中国代表团副团长陈叔通和印度代表团副团长索克在联欢会上讲了话。他们都强调谈到中印两国人民的和平愿望，并且指出在五项原则的基础上建立起来的中印两国的和平友好关系，对于保卫亚洲和世界和平的巨大意义。联欢会的气氛极为热烈友好。印度代表团的艺术家在会上表演了歌唱等节目。这一天，中国代表团还和法国代表团举行了联欢。
