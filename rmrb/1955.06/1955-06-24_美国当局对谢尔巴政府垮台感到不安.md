Title: US Authorities Uneasy About the Fall of the Scelba Government
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. According to a Washington correspondent for the Associated Press, the US State Department is closely watching the cabinet crisis in Italy. The resignation of Italian Prime Minister Scelba has caused regret among senior US officials, because "they have always regarded Scelba as a staunch anti-communist friend of the United States."

A United Press reporter dispatching from Rome said, "Western diplomats are disappointed with Scelba's resignation" and "uneasy about the prospect of this strategically important country falling into a prolonged crisis."

### 美国当局对谢尔巴政府垮台感到不安

1955-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国当局对谢尔巴政府垮台感到不安
    新华社二十三日讯　据美联社驻华盛顿记者报道，美国国务院密切注视着意大利的内阁危机。意大利总理谢尔巴的辞职使美国高级官员感到遗憾，因为“他们一向认为谢尔巴是美国的一个坚决的反共的朋友。”
    合众社记者自罗马发出消息说，“西方外交人士对谢尔巴的下台感到失望”，并且“对这个具有很大战略意义的国家可能陷入长期危机的前景感到不安”。
