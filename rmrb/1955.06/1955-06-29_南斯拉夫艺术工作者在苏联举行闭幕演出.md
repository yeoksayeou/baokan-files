Title: Yugoslav Artists Hold Closing Performance in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 26th (TASS dispatch): The artists of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, who were invited to visit the Soviet Union, held a closing performance at the Soviet Grand Theater on the 25th, and Soviet artists also participated in the performance. The program included works by Yugoslav and Russian musicians, as well as creations by Western European artists.

  This performance demonstrated the high level of musical proficiency and theatrical art of the Yugoslav artists. Yugoslav actors have already gained a high reputation in the Soviet Union, and this performance attracted many music lovers. This performance also reflected the mutually beneficial cultural ties between the two peoples, and Soviet artists also achieved particularly brilliant success in this performance.

  Comrades Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Pospelov, Suslov and others watched the performance. Also seated in the government seats were the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mr. D. Vidić, and his wife. Representatives of various countries' embassies in the Soviet Union also attended the performance.

### 南斯拉夫艺术工作者在苏联举行闭幕演出

1955-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫艺术工作者在苏联举行闭幕演出
    新华社二十六日讯塔斯社莫斯科讯：应邀访问苏联的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的艺术工作者二十五日在苏联大剧院举行了闭幕演出，苏联艺术家们也参加了演出。演出的节目有南斯拉夫和俄罗斯音乐家们的作品和西欧艺术家的创作。
  这次演出表现了南斯拉夫艺术家们的高度的音乐水平和演剧艺术。南斯拉夫演员们已经在苏联获得了很高的声望，这次的演出吸引了众多的音乐爱好者。这次演出还体现了两国人民相互补益的文化联系，苏联艺术家在这次演出中也获得了特别辉煌的成就。
    布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、波斯伯洛夫、苏斯洛夫等同志观看了演出。在政府席位上坐着的还有南斯拉夫驻苏联大使德·维迪兹及其夫人。前往观剧的还有各国驻苏使馆的代表。
