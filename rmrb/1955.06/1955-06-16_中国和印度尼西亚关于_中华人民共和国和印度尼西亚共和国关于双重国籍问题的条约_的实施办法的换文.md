Title: Exchange of Letters Between China and Indonesia on Implementation Measures for the "Treaty Between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Issue of Dual Nationality"
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 1

His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of China's State Council writes to Your Excellency Premier:

The People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia have signed the Treaty on the Issue of Dual Nationality on April 22, 1955. During Your Excellency's visit to the People's Republic of China, our two governments fully exchanged views and reached an understanding in Beijing on the purpose and implementation measures of this treaty. I now confirm our understanding as follows:

(1) The purpose of the aforementioned Dual Nationality Treaty is to eliminate the issue of dual nationality existing between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia. This is a problem left over by history. The solution to this problem is in the interests of the people of both our countries. To achieve the above-mentioned purpose, the two governments agree to take all necessary measures and provide various conveniences in the implementation of the aforementioned treaty so that every person with dual nationality can voluntarily choose their nationality.

(2) The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia agree that among those who simultaneously possess the nationality of the Republic of Indonesia and the nationality of the People's Republic of China, there is a category of people who, in the opinion of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia, have implicitly renounced the nationality of the People's Republic of China due to their social and political status, and can be considered to have only one nationality and not dual nationality.

People belonging to the above category, since they only have one nationality, do not need to choose a nationality according to the provisions of the Dual Nationality Treaty.

For this category of people, if they so request, they can be issued a certificate for proof.

(3) To eliminate misunderstandings about Article 14 of the aforementioned Dual Nationality Treaty regarding the twenty-year validity period, the two governments agree on the following interpretation: that is, after the expiration of the twenty-year period, those who have already chosen a nationality under the aforementioned treaty will no longer make a nationality choice.

(4) In order to implement the aforementioned treaty perfectly, the two governments agree to establish a joint committee in Jakarta composed of representatives from both the Republic of Indonesia and the People's Republic of China. The task of this committee is to discuss and stipulate the methods for implementing the aforementioned Dual Nationality Treaty.

(5) Within the two-year period for choosing a nationality, the existing status of a person with dual nationality remains unchanged until he or she chooses a nationality in accordance with the provisions of the aforementioned treaty.

If the above points are confirmed by Your Excellency, then this note and your reply will become the understanding reached between the Government of the Republic of Indonesia and the Government of the People's Republic of China regarding the implementation of the Treaty on the Issue of Dual Nationality, and will take effect simultaneously with the aforementioned treaty.

With the highest respect.

To His Excellency Ali Sastroamidjojo, Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia.
                Zhou Enlai (signed)
                Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                June 3, 1955, in Beijing

### 中国和印度尼西亚关于“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约”的实施办法的换文

1955-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中国和印度尼西亚关于“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约”的实施办法的换文
    我国国务院总理周恩来去文总理阁下：
    中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国已经在一九五五年四月二十二日签订了关于双重国籍问题的条约。在阁下访问中华人民共和国的期间，我们双方政府又在北京就这个条约的目的和实施办法充分交换了意见，并达成谅解。我现在确认我们所达成的谅解如下：
      （一）上述双重国籍条约的目的是为了消除存在于中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国之间的双重国籍问题。这是一个历史遗留下来的问题。这个问题的解决是符合于我们两国人民的利益的。为了达到上述目的，两国政府同意，在实施上述条约时，采取一切必要的措施和提供各种便利条件，使每一个具有双重国籍的人，都能够自愿地选择他们的国籍。
      （二）中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府同意：在同时具有印度尼西亚共和国国籍和中华人民共和国国籍的人当中，有一类人，根据印度尼西亚共和国政府的意见，由于他们的社会地位和政治地位，证明他们已经不言而喻地放弃了中华人民共和国的国籍，可以被认为只具有一种国籍，而不具有双重国籍。
    属于上述一类的人，既然只具有一种国籍，就不需要按照双重国籍条约的规定选择国籍。
    对于这一类人，如果他们有此要求，可以发给他们一项证件，以资证明。
      （三）为了消除对上述双重国籍条约第十四条关于二十年有效期的规定的误解，两国政府同意下列解释：即在二十年期限满后，根据上述条约已经选择了国籍的人，不再进行选择国籍。
      （四）为了完善地实施上述条约，两国政府同意在雅加达成立一个由印度尼西亚共和国和中华人民共和国双方代表组成的联合委员会，这个委员会的任务是讨论和规定有关实施上述双重国籍条约的办法。
      （五）在选择国籍的两年期限内，具有双重国籍的人，在他或她按照上述条约的规定进行选择国籍之前，他或她所处的现有地位不变。
    上述各点如果获得阁下确认，则本照会和您的复照即成为印度尼西亚共和国政府和中华人民共和国政府关于执行双重国籍问题的条约时所达成的谅解，并和上述条约同时生效。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
    此照印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约阁下
                中华人民共和国国务院总理
                      周恩来（签字）
                  一九五五年六月三日于北京
