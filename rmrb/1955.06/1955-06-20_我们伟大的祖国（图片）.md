    Our Great Motherland
    In order to further develop transportation in the Tibet region, the Lhasa to Shigatse highway began construction in late May. A large quantity of supplies and lumber needed for the road construction project is being continuously transported from Lhasa to the construction site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了进一步发展西藏地区的交通运输事业，拉萨到日喀则公路已在五月下旬开工修筑。筑路工程所需的大批物资和木料正由拉萨源源运往工地。
