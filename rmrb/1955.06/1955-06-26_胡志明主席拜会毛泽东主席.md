Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits Chairman Mao Zedong
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam paid a visit to Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China at 12:00 noon on the 25th.

Present at the meeting from the Vietnamese side were members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam: Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Le Van Hien, Minister of Finance of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce; Nguyen Van Huyên, Minister of Education; Nghiêm Xuân Yêm, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry; Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Nguyen Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of the Presidential Office; Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Medical Affairs; and Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China.

Present at the meeting from the Chinese side were: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee and Mayor of Beijing; Chen Yun and Peng Dehuai, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee; He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Bo Yibo, Director of the State Construction Commission; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, and Ji Pengfei, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs; and Luo Guibo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 胡志明主席拜会毛泽东主席

1955-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席拜会毛泽东主席
    新华社二十五日讯　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在二十五日中午十二时拜会了中华人民共和国主席毛泽东。
    拜会时越南方面在座的有：越南民主共和国政府代表团团员：越南劳动党总书记长征、越南民主共和国财政部部长黎文献、工商部部长潘英、教育部部长阮文煊、农林部部长严春庵、外交部副部长雍文谦、主席府副部长阮维祯、医济部副部长范玉石、越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢。
    拜会时中国方面在座的有：中华人民共和国副主席朱德、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长、中共中央书记处书记刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培，副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀，副总理、中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，副总理贺龙、陈毅、李富春、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武，国家建设委员会主任薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，铁道部部长滕代远，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、姬鹏飞和中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波。
