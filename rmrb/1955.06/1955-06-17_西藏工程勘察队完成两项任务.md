Title: Tibet Engineering Survey Team Completes Two Tasks
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    The Tibet Engineering Survey Team has completed the survey work for establishing a leather factory and a small iron factory in Lhasa. The survey personnel investigated raw materials, fuel, and market sales, and selected and surveyed the factory site.

### 西藏工程勘察队完成两项任务

1955-06-17
第2版()
专栏：

    西藏工程勘察队完成两项任务
    西藏工程勘察队已经完成了在拉萨设立皮革厂和小型铁工厂的勘察工作。勘察人员调查了原料、燃料和市场销售等情况，选择和勘测了设厂地址。
                                    （新华社）
