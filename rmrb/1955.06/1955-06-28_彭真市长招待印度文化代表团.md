Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Indian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a reception at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park on the evening of June 27th to welcome the Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Mr. Chand.

Amidst enthusiastic applause from park visitors, Chairman Chand, his wife, and the Indian artists entered Zhongshan Hall and warmly shook hands with everyone.

Accompanying the Indian artists at the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture and President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, heads of various departments of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and representatives from the capital's literary, artistic, scientific, and journalistic circles, as well as Indian Ambassador Raghunath.

During their twenty-day visit to China, the Indian artists have already established close and profound friendships with Chinese artists. As Peng Zhen introduced Mr. Chand and his wife to representatives from all walks of life, Chinese and Indian artists gathered to discuss the similarities between Chinese and Indian songs and dances, and to share the Indian artists' impressions of their visit to Northeast China.

At the reception, Peng Zhen, on behalf of the people of Beijing, warmly welcomed the Indian Cultural Delegation and expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the Indian artists' brilliant performances. Peng Zhen said: "We, the people of Beijing, are very honored to have the first opportunity to meet with the esteemed Indian artists and appreciate their wonderful performances." Through the Indian Cultural Delegation's performances in Beijing, the strong national style and the rich, diverse, and meticulous expressive techniques of Indian art have aroused great interest among the people of the capital. Peng Zhen expressed his firm belief that the cultural exchange between China and India and the traditional profound friendship between the people of China and India will become even closer. He congratulated the Indian artists on their successful performances and wished them good health.

In his speech, Mr. Chand thanked Beijing, this ancient city, for the warm welcome given to the Indian Cultural Delegation. He said: "We have been in China for three weeks, and every day we discover more and more of the Chinese people's love for the Indian people. We do not feel like strangers in China, because we have found a second home in the hearts of the Chinese people." Chand expressed his firm belief that cultural exchanges between China and India will increase more and more, and toasted to Sino-Indian friendly cooperation.

### 彭真市长招待印度文化代表团

1955-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长招待印度文化代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　北京市市长彭真二十七日晚在中山公园中山堂举行酒会，招待以钱达先生为首的印度文化代表团。
    在游园市民的一阵阵热烈掌声中，钱达团长和夫人及印度艺术家们步入了中山堂，和大家亲切地握手。
    和印度艺术家们一起参加这次酒会的，有国务院副总理陈毅，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，文化部副部长、中印友好协会会长丁西林，文化部副部长夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市人民委员会各部门负责人，以及首都文艺、科学、新闻界人士，还有印度大使赖嘉文等。
    印度艺术家们在我国的二十天的访问中，已经和我国的艺术家们建立了亲切深厚的友谊。当彭真介绍钱达先生和夫人同各界人士见面交谈时，中印艺术家们便聚集在一起交谈着中国歌舞和印度歌舞中的相近之处，交谈着印度艺术家们访问东北的观感。
    彭真在酒会上代表北京市各界人民热烈欢迎印度文化代表团，并衷心感谢印度艺术家们的精采表演。彭真说：我们北京市人民能够首先有机会同印度各位优秀的艺术家见面和欣赏他们的精采的演出，感到十分荣幸。通过印度文化代表团在北京的演出，印度艺术所具有的强烈的民族风格和丰富多采的、细致入微的表现手法，引起了首都人民的巨大兴趣。彭真表示深信中印两国的文化交流和中印两国人民的传统的深厚友谊将愈来愈紧密，他祝贺印度艺术家们演出的成功，祝印度艺术家们健康。
    钱达先生在发言中感谢北京这个古老的城市对印度文化代表团的热烈欢迎，他说：我们到达中国已有三个星期，每天都更多地发现中国人民对印度人民的热爱。我们在中国并不感到生疏，因为我们在中国人民的心里找到了第二个故乡。钱达表示深信中印两国文化来往将愈来愈多，并为中印友好合作干杯。
