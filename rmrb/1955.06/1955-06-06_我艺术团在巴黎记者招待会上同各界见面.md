Title: My Art Troupe Meets with Various Circles at a Press Conference in Paris
Author:
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 4th: Paris Dispatch: The Second Paris International Drama Festival held a press conference on the 2nd to introduce the Chinese Art Troupe to the press. More than 200 journalists from France and other countries attended the reception.
	The press conference was opened by Jean Dureuil, head of the International Drama Festival, who first warmly welcomed the Chinese Art Troupe's acceptance of the invitation to participate in the festival's performances. At the reception, Vercors, Honorary Chairman of the France-China Friendship Association and Chairman of the National Committee of French Writers, described his impressions of China and praised the beauty of Chinese classical drama. He said that the first performance of Chinese classical drama in Paris would arouse great interest.
	Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, delivered a speech at the reception, saying that the International Drama Festival is a good way to promote international cultural exchange. The Chinese Art Troupe is willing to contribute the classical drama art of the Chinese people to this grand festival and wishes the drama festival success.

### 我艺术团在巴黎记者招待会上同各界见面

1955-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我艺术团在巴黎记者招待会上同各界见面
    据新华社布拉格四日电　巴黎消息：巴黎第二届国际戏剧节二日举行记者招待会，介绍中国艺术团同新闻记者见面。出席招待会的有法国和其他国家的记者二百多人。
    招待会由国际戏剧节负责人让·余立安宣布开会，他首先对中国艺术团接受邀请参加戏剧节的演出表示热烈欢迎。在招待会上，法中友好协会名誉主席、法国作家全国委员会主席维尔高尔叙述了他在中国的观感，称赞中国古典戏剧的优美。他说：中国古典戏剧首次在巴黎演出，将引起巨大的兴趣。
    中国艺术团团长张致祥在招待会上致词，他说国际戏剧节是促进国际文化交流的一种很好的方式。中国艺术团愿将中国人民的古典戏剧艺术贡献给这个盛大的节日，并预祝戏剧节的成功。
