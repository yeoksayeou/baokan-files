Title: V.M. Molotov Arrives in New York and Gives Speech
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th (TASS, New York, June 15th): The Soviet delegation led by V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs, which is attending the 10th anniversary celebration of the United Nations, passed through New York on the 15th on its way to San Francisco.
    Welcoming the Soviet delegation at the port of New York were: Soviet Ambassador to the United States Zarubin, Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations Sobolev, Polish Ambassador to the United States Pasowski, Polish Permanent Representative to the United Nations Belsky, Czechoslovak Permanent Representative to the United Nations Ulrich, as well as journalists from the United States, the Soviet Union, and other countries.
    V.M. Molotov made the following statement to the journalists: "On behalf of the Soviet delegation, I extend warm greetings to the residents of the world-famous city of New York, and extend warm greetings to all in the United States who uphold lasting peace, advocate international cooperation, and consolidate friendship between the people of all countries."
    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th (TASS, New York): V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs, visited United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on June 15th.
    Molotov held a half-hour meeting with Hammarskjöld and toured the United Nations headquarters with him.

### 维·米·莫洛托夫到达纽约并发表谈话

1955-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    维·米·莫洛托夫到达纽约并发表谈话
    新华社十六日讯　据塔斯社纽约十五日讯：由苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫率领的出席联合国成立十周年纪念会的苏联代表团，在前往旧金山途中于十五日路过纽约。
    在纽约港口欢迎苏联代表团的有：苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾、苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫、波兰人民共和国驻美国大使帕索夫斯基、波兰人民共和国常驻联合国代表贝尔茨基、捷克斯洛伐克共和国常驻联合国代表乌里赫以及美国、苏联和其他国家的新闻记者。
    维·米·莫洛托夫向新闻记者发表谈话如下：“我代表苏联代表团向世界闻名的纽约市的居民热烈致意，并向美国所有维护持久和平、主张国际合作和巩固各国人民之间的友谊的人热烈致意。”
    新华社十六日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫在六月十五日访问联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
    莫洛托夫同哈马舍尔德举行了半小时的会谈，并同他一起视察了联合国总部。
