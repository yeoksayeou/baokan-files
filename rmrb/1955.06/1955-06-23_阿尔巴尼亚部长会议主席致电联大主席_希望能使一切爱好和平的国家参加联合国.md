Title: Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers Cables UN General Assembly President, Hoping to Enable All Peace-loving Countries to Participate in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st: According to the Albanian News Agency on June 20th: On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the signing of the United Nations Charter, Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, sent a telegram to Mr. Van Kleffens, President of the Ninth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, congratulating him on the United Nations commemorative meeting. The telegram stated that the Albanian government believes that if the United Nations includes all countries that meet the requirements of the United Nations Charter, it will help the United Nations fulfill its mission and enhance its prestige. The Albanian government hopes that the United Nations will take the necessary measures to enable all peace-loving countries, including the People's Republic of Albania, to participate in the United Nations without discrimination.

### 阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席致电联大主席  希望能使一切爱好和平的国家参加联合国

1955-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席致电联大主席
    希望能使一切爱好和平的国家参加联合国
    新华社二十一日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社二十日讯：在联合国宪章签订十周年之际，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡打电报给联合国大会第九届会议主席范克里芬斯，向联合国纪念会表示祝贺。电报里说，阿尔巴尼亚政府认为，如果联合国包括所有符合联合国宪章要求的国家，就有助于联合国完成它的使命和提高联合国的威信。阿尔巴尼亚政府希望联合国采取必要措施，毫无歧视地使一切爱好和平的国家，包括阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国在内，能够参加联合国。
