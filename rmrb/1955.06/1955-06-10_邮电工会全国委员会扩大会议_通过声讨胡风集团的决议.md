Title: National Committee of the Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Expanded Meeting Passes Resolution Denouncing the Hu Feng Group
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 1

    National Committee of the Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Expanded Meeting
    Passes Resolution Denouncing the Hu Feng Group
    (Reported by this newspaper) The National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union held its Second Fourth Expanded National Committee Meeting in Beijing from May 31st to June 4th. During the meeting, the attending committee members and representatives held forums and unanimously denounced the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary group. Li Jinghan, Chairman of the National Committee of the Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, delivered a speech at the meeting. The chairmen of the postal and telecommunications workers' unions from various provinces and cities also expressed their opinions, unanimously supporting the resolution of the joint expanded meeting of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Presidium of the Chinese Writers Association, demanding that the counter-revolutionary element Hu Feng be punished according to law, and that the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary group be thoroughly crushed. The meeting finally passed a resolution on denouncing the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary group.

### 邮电工会全国委员会扩大会议  通过声讨胡风集团的决议

1955-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    邮电工会全国委员会扩大会议
    通过声讨胡风集团的决议
    本报讯　中国邮电工会全国委员会在五月三十一日到六月四日，在北京召开第二届第四次全国委员扩大会议。会议进行期间，到会各委员和代表曾举行座谈会，一致声讨胡风反革命集团。会上邮电工会全国委员会主席李景韩讲了话。出席会议的邮电工会各省、市委员会的主席也发表了意见，一致拥护中国文联主席团和中国作家协会主席团联席扩大会议的决议，要求依法惩办反革命分子胡风，彻底粉碎胡风反革命集团。会议最后并通过了关于声讨胡风反革命集团的决议。
