Title: At the Meeting of the Central Joint Armistice Commission in Vietnam, the Vietnamese Side Requested Discussions on Preparations for Holding a Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 9th. According to the Vietnam News Agency, General Van Tien Dung, Chief Delegate of the Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army, handed over the statement issued on June 6th by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam regarding preparations for holding a consultative conference with the South Vietnamese authorities to discuss the issue of free general elections, to General Chawat, Chief Delegate of the Delegation of the General Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina, at the meeting of the Central Joint Armistice Commission in Vietnam on the 8th.

General Van Tien Dung also issued a statement saying: The delegation of the Vietnam People's Army, according to the instructions of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army, is formally raising this issue today at the Central Joint Armistice Commission in order to discuss the issue of preparing for the holding of a consultative conference with the delegation of the General Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina.

### 在越南中央联合停战委员会会议上  越方要求讨论关于筹备举行协商会议问题

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    在越南中央联合停战委员会会议上
    越方要求讨论关于筹备举行协商会议问题
    新华社河内九日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇将军八日在越南中央联合停战委员会的会议上，把越南民主共和国政府在六日发表的关于准备同南越当局举行协商会议以讨论自由普选问题的声明交给法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团首席代表夏瓦特将军。
    文进勇将军同时发表声明说：越南人民军代表团根据越南人民军总司令部的指示，今天在中央联合停战委员会上正式提出这个问题，以便和印度支那的法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团讨论筹备举行协商会议的问题。
