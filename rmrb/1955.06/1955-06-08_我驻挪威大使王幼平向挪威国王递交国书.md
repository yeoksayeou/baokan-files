Title: My Ambassador to Norway, Wang Youping, Presents Credentials to the King of Norway
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Wang Youping, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Norway, presented his credentials to King Haakon VII of Norway on the 6th.

Ambassador Wang Youping conveyed Chairman Mao Zedong's greetings to Haakon VII when presenting his credentials.

King Haakon VII of Norway expressed his thanks and asked Ambassador Wang Youping to convey his regards to Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 我驻挪威大使王幼平向挪威国王递交国书

1955-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻挪威大使王幼平向挪威国王递交国书
    新华社七日讯　中华人民共和国驻挪威首任特命全权大使王幼平在六日向挪威国王哈康七世递交国书。
    王幼平大使在递交国书时转达了毛泽东主席对哈康七世的问候。
    挪威国王哈康七世答谢以后也请王幼平大使代向毛泽东主席致意。
