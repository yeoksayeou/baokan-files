Title: Chinese Delegation to World Mothers' Congress Leaves for Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th – The Chinese delegation and staff, totaling thirty-two, attending the World Mothers' Congress, have departed from Beijing for Lausanne, Switzerland in three groups on June 19th, 26th, and 29th. Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, serves as the head of the delegation. Li Dequan, along with some members of the delegation, is already abroad on official duties and will arrive in Switzerland at the end of June.

The list of the Chinese delegation attending the World Mothers' Congress is as follows:
Head: Li Dequan
Deputy Heads: Luo Qiong, Cao Mengjun
Members (arranged in order of the number of strokes in the surname):
　　Wang Youlan, Shen Yuanhui, Li Youxiu, Lin Ning, Wu Quanheng
　　Yu Zhiying, Suo Longgewa
　　Ni Feijun, Hairipa Rouziyeva, Guo Xiumei, Qi Yun, Chen Cuichang, Zhang Shuyi
　　Yu Yixuan, Yang Chongrui, Yang Yunyu, Liu Lanqi, Xie Bingxin
Secretary-General: Yu Zhiying (concurrent)
Deputy Secretary-General: Wu Quanheng (concurrent)

### 我国出席世界母亲大会代表团启程去瑞士

1955-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出席世界母亲大会代表团启程去瑞士
    新华社二十九日讯　出席世界母亲大会的中国代表团及工作人员三十二人，已在六月十九日、二十六日、二十九日分三批离北京前往瑞士洛桑。中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席李德全任代表团团长。李德全同部分团员因公先在国外，将在六月底先行到达瑞士。
