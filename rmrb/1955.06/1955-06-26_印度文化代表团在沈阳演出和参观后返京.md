Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Performances and Visits in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1955-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang. More than 1,300 people in Shenyang enjoyed the musical and dance performances of the Indian Cultural Delegation on the evening of the 24th.

Before the performance began, Zhou Xinmin, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, welcomed the Indian Cultural Delegation to Shenyang on behalf of the people of the city. Head of the delegation, Mr. Chandra, thanked the people of Shenyang for their warm welcome. Then, a Young Pioneer presented flowers to Mr. Chandra.

Amidst music and applause, the purple curtain slowly rose. The first item was a sitar solo, which immediately transported the audience to a beautiful musical realm. Ms. Indrani Rehman's performance of the "Bharata Natyam," which won the first prize for national dance at the International Art Competition of the Fourth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival, was praised by the audience.

Each wonderful program performed by the Indian artists won sustained applause, and the performers repeatedly bowed to the audience.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang. Head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Mr. Chandra, and his wife, along with some members of the delegation, visited the Northeast Institute of Technology and the famous coal capital, Fushun, on the 24th and 25th.

On the morning of the 24th, the guests arrived at the Northeast Institute of Technology. Deputy Dean Wang Zhili gave the guests a brief introduction, and the guests learned that the school was a newly established school with modern teaching equipment. Mr. Chandra inquired about the national student aid and student life.

Before leaving, Mr. Chandra thanked the host, saying, "Please convey my congratulations to the youth of New China for their happy lives!"

On the 25th, Mr. Chandra and others came to Fushun, the famous coal capital. They overlooked the magnificent open-pit mine from the open-pit mine observation platform. Mr. Chandra praised the shuttle-like coal trucks and the mechanized production of electric shovels. In the fine arts and handicrafts supply and marketing production group, the guests carefully observed the coal carving process by the carving workers.

Today, another group of Indian artists visited the Northeast Conservatory of Music in Shenyang.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 25th. The Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Mr. Chandra, concluded its visit to Shenyang and left Shenyang by special train on the evening of the 25th to return to Beijing.

Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, Shi Zhan, Director of the Liaoning Provincial Bureau of Culture, Lu Kan, Director of the Shenyang Municipal Bureau of Culture, cultural and artistic groups, and representatives from all walks of life, more than 500 people, went to the station to see them off. Young Pioneers presented flowers to Mr. Chandra and his wife, and the delegation members also accepted flowers from the welcomers.

Before departing, the Director of the Shenyang Municipal Bureau of Culture, on behalf of the people of Shenyang, presented the Indian artists with spun silk, coal carvings, amber, and other handicrafts. The Indian artists also gave bronze carved handicrafts to Liu Baotian and Lu Kan.

R. Mala Tai and two others had already returned to Beijing by plane from Shenyang on the morning of the 25th.

### 印度文化代表团在沈阳演出和参观后返京

1955-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团在沈阳演出和参观后返京
    据新华社沈阳电  沈阳市一千三百多人在二十四日晚欣赏了印度文化代表团的音乐舞蹈演出。
  演出开始前，沈阳市副市长周新民代表全市人民欢迎印度文化代表团来沈阳演出。钱达团长感谢沈阳人民对他们的热烈欢迎。接着，一个少年先锋队员向钱达献了花。
    在乐声和掌声中，紫色的帷幕慢慢地拉开了。第一个节目是锡达尔独奏，它一开始就把观众带入幽美的音乐境界。在第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节国际艺术比赛中获得民族舞蹈一等奖的印·拉赫曼女士表演的“婆罗多舞”，受到观众的赞扬。
    印度艺术家们表演的每个精采节目都博得经久不息的掌声，表演者一再向群众谢幕。
    据新华社沈阳电　印度文化代表团团长钱达和夫人以及部分团员二十四日和二十五日参观了东北工学院和著名煤都抚顺。
    二十四日上午，客人们来到东北工学院。副院长汪之力向客人们作了简短的介绍，客人们知道这个学校是一个有着现代教学设备的新兴学校。钱达对有关国家助学金和学生生活方面的情况作了了解。
    临别时，钱达团长向主人道谢说：“请你转达我对新中国青年幸福生活的祝贺！”
    二十五日，钱达等来到著名煤都——抚顺。他们在露天矿参观台上，瞭望了规模宏伟的露天矿场。钱达对穿梭般的运煤车和电铲等机械化生产非常称赞。客人们在美术手工业供销生产小组，细心地观察了雕刻工人用煤精雕刻的情形。
    今天，另一部分印度艺术家们在沈阳参观了东北音乐专科学校。
    新华社沈阳二十五日电　以钱达为首的印度文化代表团结束了在沈阳的访问，二十五日晚乘专车离沈阳返北京。
    沈阳市市长刘宝田、辽宁省文化局局长施展、沈阳市文化局局长鲁坎、文化艺术团体和各界代表五百多人到车站欢送。少年先锋队员们向钱达和钱达夫人献了鲜花，代表团员们也接受了欢送者的献花。
    在离别以前，沈阳市文化局局长代表沈阳市人民向印度艺术家们赠送了茧绸和煤精、琥珀等工艺品。印度艺术家们也把铜质的雕刻工艺品送给刘宝田和鲁坎。
    拉·马拉太等三人，已在二十五日上午乘飞机由沈阳返北京。
