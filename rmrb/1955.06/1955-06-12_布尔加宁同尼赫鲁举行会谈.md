Title: Bulganin Holds Talks with Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch: According to TASS, on June 10, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin held a meeting with Indian Republic Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nehru at the Kremlin.

    Soviet Council of Ministers First Vice-Chairmen Kaganovich and Mikoyan also participated in the meeting.

    The Indian side participating in the meeting also included Indian Foreign Secretary Pillai, Director of the European Division of the Indian Republic's Ministry of Foreign Affairs Hussain, Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union K.P.S. Menon, and First Secretary of the Indian Embassy in the Soviet Union, Gaul. The Soviet side also included Soviet First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko and Kuznetsov, and Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov.

    The meeting took place in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

### 布尔加宁同尼赫鲁举行会谈

1955-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁同尼赫鲁举行会谈
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁六月十日同印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁在克里姆林宫举行了一次会谈。
    苏联部长会议第一副主席卡冈诺维奇和米高扬也参加了会谈。
    参加会谈的印度方面还有印度外交部秘书长皮莱，印度共和国外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼，印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农，印度驻苏联大使馆一等秘书高尔，苏联方面还有苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯和库兹涅佐夫，苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫。
    会谈在诚挚友好气氛中进行。
