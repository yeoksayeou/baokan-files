Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Northeast China

Author:

Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th. The Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Chand, concluded its visit to Northeast China and returned to Beijing by special train at 2:30 PM on the 26th, accompanied by Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Art Affairs of the Ministry of Culture, and G.H. Kher, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China.

Greeting them at the station were Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, Zhang Hanfu, Feng Jiping, and figures from the capital's literary and art circles, as well as personnel from the Indian Embassy in China.

### 印度文化代表团访问东北各地后回到北京

1955-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团访问东北各地后回到北京
    新华社二十六日讯  以钱达为首的印度文化代表团结束了在东北各地的访问以后，由文化部艺术事业管理局副局长周巍峙和印度驻华大使馆参赞高伯登陪同，二十六日下午二时半乘专车返抵北京。
    在车站迎接的有丁西林、夏衍、章汉夫、冯基平和首都文艺界人士等，以及印度驻华大使馆人员。
