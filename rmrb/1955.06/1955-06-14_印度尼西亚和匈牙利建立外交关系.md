Title: Indonesia and Hungary Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-06-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 13th – Antara News Agency reported on the 13th that the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced that the Indonesian Cabinet decided at its May 31st meeting to establish diplomatic relations with the Hungarian People's Republic at the ambassadorial level. The Indonesian government will establish an embassy in Budapest, and the Hungarian government will establish an embassy in Jakarta.

### 印度尼西亚和匈牙利建立外交关系

1955-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和匈牙利建立外交关系
    新华社雅加达十三日电　据安塔拉通讯社十三日报道，印度尼西亚外交部宣布，印度尼西亚内阁在五月三十一日会议上决定同匈牙利人民共和国建立大使级的外交关系。印度尼西亚政府将在布达佩斯设立大使馆，匈牙利政府将在雅加达设立大使馆。
