Title: Rakosi and Hegedus Visit Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 4th: Romanian News Agency, Bucharest, reported: Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and András Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, arrived in Bucharest on the 4th for a friendly visit.

### 拉科西和赫格居斯到布加勒斯特访问

1955-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    拉科西和赫格居斯到布加勒斯特访问
    新华社四日讯　罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西和匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席安·赫格居斯，四日抵达布加勒斯特作友好访问。
