Title: Indian President Sends Telegram to Nehru Wishing Him Success on His Visit to the Soviet Union; Nehru Leaves Prague for Moscow to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 7th: Indian President Rajendra Prasad sent a telegram to Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who is visiting the Soviet Union, wishing him success on his visit.

In a telegram, Prasad said, "I would be grateful if you would convey the greetings and best wishes of the Indian people and myself to the Chairman of the Soviet Socialist Republics. We believe that peace is necessary for the progress and prosperity of all countries and peoples in the world, and all good men and women should have a responsibility to assist in the noble efforts being made to maintain peace.

"We are fortunate that our relations with that great nation are harmonious, and we are confident that your visit of goodwill and peace will further strengthen the friendly relations between our two countries and advance the cause of peace."

In another telegram to Nehru, Prasad said: "The whole country unanimously and fully supports you, and we hope that you will return with new achievements won for the cause of peace."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 7th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru left Prague by plane on the morning of the 7th for Moscow.

Those who saw him off at the airport included Czechoslovak Prime Minister Široký, deputy prime ministers, and senior government officials.

Also at the airport to see him off were the Soviet and Chinese ambassadors to Czechoslovakia and diplomatic representatives from various countries.

Prime Minister Nehru, accompanied by Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of National Defense General Kratochvíl, reviewed the honor guard.

Subsequently, Prime Minister Široký spoke, expressing his gratitude for Nehru's visit to Czechoslovakia. Prime Minister Nehru delivered a reply.

### 印总统致电尼赫鲁预祝他访苏获得成就  尼赫鲁离开布拉格前往莫斯科访问苏联

1955-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印总统致电尼赫鲁预祝他访苏获得成就
    尼赫鲁离开布拉格前往莫斯科访问苏联
    新华社新德里七日电　印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德致电到苏联访问的印度总理尼赫鲁，预祝他的这次访问获得成就。
    普拉沙德在一份电报中说，“如果您把印度人民和我自己的问候和祝愿转达给苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟主席阁下，我将不胜感激。我们认为，和平是世界上一切国家和人民的进步和繁荣所必需的，所有善良的男女应该有责任来协助正在为保持和平而进行的崇高努力。
    “我们同那个伟大的国家的关系是融洽的，这是值得庆幸的，我们深信，您的这次亲善与和平访问将进一步加强我们两国之间的友好关系并推进和平运动。”
    普拉沙德在给尼赫鲁的另一份电报中说：“全国一致全力支持你，我们盼望你将带着为和平事业赢得的新的成就归来。”
    新华社布拉格七日电　印度共和国总理尼赫鲁在七日上午乘飞机离布拉格前往莫斯科。
    到机场欢送的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基，各位副总理和政府高级官员。
    到机场欢送的还有苏联和中国驻捷克斯洛伐克的大使和各国驻捷外交代表。
    尼赫鲁总理在捷克斯洛伐克国防部副部长克拉托赫维尔将军陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    接着，西罗基总理讲了话，对尼赫鲁的访问捷克斯洛伐克，表示感谢。尼赫鲁总理致了答词。
