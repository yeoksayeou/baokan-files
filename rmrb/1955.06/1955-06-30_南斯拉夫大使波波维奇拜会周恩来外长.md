Title: Yugoslav Ambassador Popović calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Vladimir Popović, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to China, called on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the afternoon of the 29th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present were Chen Chu, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 南斯拉夫大使波波维奇拜会周恩来外长

1955-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫大使波波维奇拜会周恩来外长
    新华社二十九日讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国首任驻我国特命全权大使弗拉吉米尔·波波维奇在二十九日下午到外交部拜会外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有我外交部苏联东欧司司长陈楚，礼宾司司长柯华。
