Title: Chinese Delegation to World Peace Congress Departs for Helsinki; World Peace Council Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo Departs from Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Mao Dun, head of the Chinese delegation, Chen Shutong, deputy head, Liao Chengzhi and some members of the delegation, who are attending the World Peace Congress in Helsinki, left Beijing by plane at 10:15 this morning for Helsinki.

　　Nguyen Xuan Thuy, deputy head of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Hwang Bong-koo, a peace figure from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who are attending the Helsinki World Peace Congress, also traveled on the same plane.

　　Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and heads of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defence of World Peace and various people's organizations.

　　Officials from the Embassies of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Mongolia and Finland also went to see them off.

　　Another part of the Chinese delegation had previously left Beijing by train, traveling via the Soviet Union to Finland.

### 我国出席世界和平大会代表团去赫尔辛基  世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若由京启程

1955-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出席世界和平大会代表团去赫尔辛基
    世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若由京启程
    新华社十五日讯　出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，中国代表团团长茅盾、副团长陈叔通、廖承志和一部分团员今天上午十点一刻乘飞机离开北京去赫尔辛基。
    同机前往的有出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的越南民主共和国代表团副团长阮春水和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和平人士黄凤九等人。
    前往机场欢送的有中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和各人民团体负责人。
    越南民主共和国、蒙古和芬兰大使馆官员也前往欢送。
    中国代表团另一部分团员已先期乘火车离京，取道苏联前往芬兰。
