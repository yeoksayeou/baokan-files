Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Chanda, Chanda Visits Relevant Leaders of China's Ministry of Culture and Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1955-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, received A.K. Chanda, head of the Indian cultural delegation and Deputy Secretary of the Ministry of External Affairs of the Indian government, and his wife, as well as the delegation's secretary, G. Singh, an official of the Ministry of External Affairs of the Indian government, and the delegation's stage manager, R. Malathi, Director and Program Director of All India Radio, at 7:30 p.m. on the 14th.

Present at the reception were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Dong Biwu, He Xiangning, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Long Yun, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife were also present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: Chanda, head of the Indian cultural delegation, accompanied by Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, paid a visit to Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin, and Xia Yan at 3:00 p.m. on the 14th; and paid a visit to Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu at 4:00 p.m.

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: Chanda, head of the Indian cultural delegation, and his wife visited Peking University on the morning of the 14th.

Jiang Longji, Vice President of Peking University, introduced the school's general situation to them.

They slowly circled the campus by car. They expressed their great fondness for the school's garden-like, quiet and beautiful environment. Head of Delegation Chanda said to a group of students who came to welcome them:

"I envy you being able to study in this beautiful environment."

Here, they also met five Indian teachers and one Indian student. They visited the library of the Department of Oriental Languages ​​together. (Attached picture)

Top: Chairman Liu Shaoqi (right) talking with Chanda (center), head of the Indian cultural delegation, and Mrs. Chanda (left).
Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue

### 刘少奇委员长接见钱达  钱达拜会我国文化部和外交部负责人

1955-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长接见钱达
    钱达拜会我国文化部和外交部负责人
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，在十四日下午七时半接见了印度文化代表团团长、印度政府外交部副部长阿·库·钱达和夫人，以及代表团秘书、印度政府外交部官员古·辛格，代表团舞台监督、全印广播电台主任兼节目指导拉·马拉太。
    接见时在座的，有国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通，国务院副总理彭德怀、陈毅、李富春、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席董必武、何香凝、鲍尔汉，国防委员会副主席龙云，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，副部长丁西林、夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫。
    接见时，印度驻华大使赖嘉文和夫人也在座。
    新华社十四日讯　印度文化代表团团长钱达由印度驻我国大使赖嘉文陪同，在十四日下午三时，拜会了文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，副部长丁西林、夏衍；下午四时，拜会了外交部副部长章汉夫。
    新华社十四日讯　印度文化代表团团长钱达和夫人在十四日上午访问了北京大学。
    北京大学副校长江隆基向他们介绍了学校的概况。
    在校园内，他们乘着汽车缓缓环行了一周。他们对学校有这种像花园一样清静而秀丽的环境表示十分喜爱。钱达团长对一群来欢迎他们的学生说：
  “我很羡慕你们，能在这个美好的环境中学习”。
    在这里，他们还会见了五位印度教师和一位印度留学生。他们一起参观了东方语言系图书馆。（附图片）
