    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers across the country are producing large quantities of grain and cash crops to support national industrial construction. Members of the Fengshou Agricultural Production Cooperative in Cai Long Township, Chao'an County, Guangdong Province, are harvesting early rice while simultaneously plowing the fields to prepare for summer planting, striving for a bumper harvest throughout the year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国农民生产大量的粮食和经济作物，支援国家工业建设。广东省潮安县蔡泷乡丰收农业生产合作社的社员在收割早稻的同时，便翻犁田地准备夏种，争取全年丰收。
