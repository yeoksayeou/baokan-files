Title: U Nu Leaves London for New York
Author:
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 24th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu left London for New York by plane on the evening of the 23rd.
	U Nu arrived in Britain on the 16th for a visit. During his visit, U Nu held talks with Eden and met with Attlee, Churchill, Mountbatten and others. The Queen of England also received U Nu.

### 吴努离开伦敦去纽约

1955-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努离开伦敦去纽约
    新华社二十四日讯　伦敦消息：缅甸总理吴努已经在二十三日晚乘飞机离开伦敦去纽约。
    吴努在十六日到英国访问。访问期间，吴努曾经同艾登会谈，同艾德礼、邱吉尔、蒙巴顿等人会晤。英国女王也接见了吴努。
