Title: Romanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cables UNGA President, Pointing Out That Romania and Other Qualified Countries Joining the UN Would Help Peace
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 22nd:  George Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, sent a telegram to Mr. Van Kleffens, President of the Ninth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, congratulating him on the tenth anniversary of the signing of the United Nations Charter. The telegram stated: The basic principles of the United Nations Charter express the desire of the people of all countries to achieve lasting peace and international cooperation among all countries on the basis of peaceful coexistence. The Romanian People's Republic unswervingly strives to strengthen cooperation with other countries and consolidate world peace. A positive resolution of the issue of the Romanian People's Republic and other qualified countries joining the United Nations would strengthen the role of the United Nations in guaranteeing peace and international security in accordance with the provisions of the Charter.

### 罗马尼亚部长会议主席致电联大主席  指出罗马尼亚和其他有资格的国家加入联合国有助于和平

1955-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚部长会议主席致电联大主席
    指出罗马尼亚和其他有资格的国家加入联合国有助于和平
    新华社二十三日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特二十二日讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治打电报给联合国大会第九届会议主席范克里芬斯，祝贺联合国宪章签订十周年纪念。电报中说：联合国宪章的基本原则表达了各国人民要求实现持久和平和各国在和平共处的基础上进行国际合作的愿望。罗马尼亚人民共和国坚持不渝地为加强同其他国家的合作、巩固世界和平而努力。罗马尼亚人民共和国和其他有资格的国家加入联合国的问题如果得到积极的解决，将可以加强联合国根据宪章的规定在保证和平和国际安全中的作用。
