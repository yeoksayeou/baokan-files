    Our Great Motherland
    Wang Wenshan (left), workshop director of the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory and representative to the National People's Congress, suggested the implementation of a "pre-repair and pre-inspection machine tool" system, which has played a significant role in protecting machinery and equipment and ensuring safe production. Here, he is inspecting the machine tools used by workers operating the four-axis gantry milling machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    沈阳第一机床厂车间主任、全国人民代表大会代表王文山（左）建议制定的“预修预检机床”制度，对保护机器设备和安全生产起了很大的作用。这是他正在检查四轴龙门铣床工人使用的机床的情形。
