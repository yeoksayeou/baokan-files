Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Begin in London on the 3rd
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 3rd: TASS London, June 2nd:  Ya. A. Malik, the Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, who was entrusted by the Soviet government to conduct negotiations on the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations, paid a return visit to the Japanese negotiating representative, Shunichi Matsumoto, on June 2nd. This visit was a courtesy call.

Negotiations on the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations will begin on June 3rd at the Soviet Embassy in London.

### 苏日谈判三日在伦敦开始

1955-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判三日在伦敦开始
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社伦敦二日讯：受苏联政府委派负责进行日苏关系正常化谈判的苏联驻英国大使雅·亚·马立克在六月二日回拜了日本谈判代表松本俊一。这次拜会是礼节上的访问。
    日苏两国关系正常化谈判将在六月三日在苏联驻伦敦大使馆内举行。
