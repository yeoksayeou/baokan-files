Title: U Nu Arrives in Israel for Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, May 30th, TASS: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu has arrived in Israel for an official visit.

According to newspaper reports, U Nu will hold talks on resolving the differences between Israel and the Arab countries.

### 吴努总理到达以色列访问

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理到达以色列访问
    新华社三十日讯　塔斯社特拉维夫三十日讯：缅甸总理吴努已经到达以色列进行正式访问。
    据报纸消息，吴努将就解决以色列和阿拉伯国家的分歧问题进行会谈。
