Title: Hatoyama Expresses Support for Sino-US Negotiations on Tension in the Taiwan Area; Shigemitsu Says Japanese Government is Studying the Issue of Establishing Consulates in Each Other's Countries Between China and Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 31st: Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama said in the House of Representatives Foreign Affairs Committee on the 30th that he supports Premier Zhou Enlai's proposal that China is willing to negotiate with the United States on the tension in the Taiwan area. He expressed his conviction that the tension in the Taiwan area can be resolved through negotiations.
　　He said that the Japanese government is very concerned about the situation in the Taiwan area and hopes that war will not break out in the Taiwan area.
　　Hatoyama Ichiro's remarks were made in response to a question from Labor-Farmer Party member Haruo Okada, who asked what attitude the Japanese government would take towards the situation in the Taiwan area.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 31st: Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu said in the House of Representatives Foreign Affairs Committee on the 30th in response to questions from opposition party members that the Japanese government is studying the issue of establishing consulates with China in each other's countries. However, he believes that there are "many difficulties" in this regard, so no decision will be made in the near future.
　　He also said that the Japanese government plans to promote trade with China and gradually normalize trade relations with China. He stated that the Japanese government will agree to Japan and China sending permanent trade representative offices to each other. However, he also stated that the Japanese government will not grant diplomatic status to the personnel of such representative offices.

### 鸠山表示支持中美谈判台湾地区紧张局势  重光说日本政府正研究中日互设领事馆的问题

1955-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山表示支持中美谈判台湾地区紧张局势
    重光说日本政府正研究中日互设领事馆的问题
    新华社三十一日讯　东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎三十日在众议院外务委员会上说：他支持周恩来总理关于中国愿意就台湾地区的紧张局势和美国进行谈判的建议。他表示他确信台湾地区的紧张局势可以通过谈判来解决。
    他说：日本政府非常关心台湾地区的形势，并且希望不要在台湾地区爆发战争。
    鸠山一郎的这番话是在回答劳农党议员冈田春夫的质询时说的。冈田春夫质询日本政府对台湾地区的局势采取什么态度。
    新华社三十一日讯　东京消息：日本外务相重光葵三十日在众议院外务委员会上答复在野党议员的质询时说：日本政府正在研究和中国互设领事馆的问题。不过，他认为在这方面“困难很多”，因此在最近期间不会作出任何决定。
    他又说：日本政府计划促进和中国的贸易，并且使和中国的贸易关系逐渐正常化。他表示日本政府将同意日本和中国互派常驻贸易代表机构。但是，他又表示日本政府不会给这种代表机构的人员以外交官的待遇。
