Title: Strive for the Establishment of an Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Alliance
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 12th - According to Antara News Agency, the preparatory meeting for the establishment of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Alliance was held in Semarang on the 7th. Rustamadji, Speaker of the Semarang Provisional Legislative Council, was elected as the Chairman of the Alliance, and Gunnawan, Chairman of the Central Java Executive Committee of the Indonesian National Party, was elected as the Vice Chairman. The meeting decided to work towards promoting mutual understanding and friendship between the people of Indonesia and the people of China.

### 争取印度尼西亚—中国友好同盟成立

1955-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    争取印度尼西亚—中国友好同盟成立
    新华社雅加达十二日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，争取印度尼西亚—中国友好同盟七日在三宝垄举行成立会议。三宝垄临时立法会议议长鲁斯塔马吉当选为同盟主席，印度尼西亚国家人民党中爪哇执行委员会主席贡纳梵当选为副主席。会议决定为促进印度尼西亚人民和中国人民之间的相互了解和友谊而努力。
