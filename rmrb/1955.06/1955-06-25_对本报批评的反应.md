Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 2
Reactions to Criticisms by This Newspaper

　　On April 23rd, this newspaper published an article entitled "Waste in the Construction of Sanatoriums in the Qingdao Area," criticizing the waste in the construction of sanatoriums in the Qingdao area by the trade union system. Now, the Labor Insurance Bureau of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent a letter of self-criticism.

　　On May 12th, this newspaper published an article entitled "Not a 'Minor Matter'," criticizing the serious waste in production at the state-owned Tianjin Printing and Dyeing Factory. Now, the factory director Qi Mingyuan has sent a letter of self-criticism.

　　On May 29th, this newspaper published an article entitled "Strange Competition," criticizing the erroneous practices in the first quarter labor emulation campaign at the Fushun Cement Plant. Now, the deputy director of the plant, Chang Tianyu, and the chairman of the trade union, He Xinghai, have sent letters of self-criticism, expressing that they will learn from this mistake, overcome capitalist management thinking, strengthen the organization and leadership of the labor emulation campaign, and identify key issues in completing the production, technology, and finance plans through economic activity analysis, and conduct evaluations on the basis of completing the state plan.

### 对本报批评的反应

1955-06-25
第2版()
专栏：对本报批评的反应

    对本报批评的反应
    四月二十三日本报发表了“青岛地区疗养院建设中的浪费”一文，批评工会系统在青岛地区建筑疗养院中的浪费。现在，中华全国总工会集体劳动保险事业管理局已来信检讨。
                    ×　×　×
    五月十二日本报发表了“不是‘小事情’”一文，批评国营天津印染厂生产中的严重浪费。现在，厂长齐铭远已来信检讨。
                    ×　×　×
    五月二十九日本报发表了“奇怪的竞赛”一文，批评抚顺水泥厂第一季度劳动竞赛中的错误做法。现在，这个厂的副厂长常天玉、工会主席贺兴海已来信检讨，并表示吸取这次错误的教训，克服资本主义经营思想，加强劳动竞赛的组织领导，通过经济活动分析，找出完成生产技术财务计划的关键问题，在完成国家计划的基础上进行评比。
