Title: Soviet Scientific and Technological Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. A Soviet delegation of eight members, led by V.I. Ovsyankin, Chairman of the Soviet group of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee and member of the Collegium of the Soviet Ministry of Construction, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 5th to participate in the second meeting of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, which is about to open in Beijing.

### 苏联科学技术代表团到京

1955-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学技术代表团到京
    新华社五日讯　前来参加即将在北京开幕的中苏科学技术合作委员会第二次会议的苏联代表团一行八人，在委员会苏联组主席、苏联建造部部务会议委员瓦·伊·奥弗西扬金率领下，在五日乘飞机到达北京。
