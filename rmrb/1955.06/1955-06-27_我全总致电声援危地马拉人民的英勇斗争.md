Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Telegram to Express Support for the Heroic Struggle of the Guatemalan People
Author:
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Latin American Labor Federation on the 24th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    On the occasion of June 27th—the first anniversary of the U.S. imperialists' armed intervention in Guatemala, the Chinese working class and laboring people extend their highest respects to the heroic Guatemalan working class and all the people who are defending the independence, democratic freedom of their country. We would be grateful if your Federation could convey to the Guatemalan working class and all the people the sympathy and concern of our working class and laboring people.

### 我全总致电声援危地马拉人民的英勇斗争

1955-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我全总致电声援危地马拉人民的英勇斗争
    新华社二十五日讯  中华全国总工会二十四日致电拉丁美洲劳工联盟，电文如下：
    值此六月二十七日——美帝国主义者武装干涉危地马拉一周年纪念日之际，中国工人阶级和劳动人民谨向捍卫自己国家的独立和民主自由的英勇的危地马拉工人阶级和全体人民致以崇高的敬意。如果贵会向危地马拉工人阶级和全体人民转达我国工人阶级和劳动人民的同情和关怀，我们殊为感谢。
