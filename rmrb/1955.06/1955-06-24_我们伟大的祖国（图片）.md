Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The advanced work method demonstration and observation meeting jointly organized by the East China Textile Administration Bureau and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the China Textile Trade Union organized Shanghai textile workers to learn from labor models and advanced workers and exchange experiences. This is a scene of Ye Yindi, a female worker and advanced worker in the roving workshop of the state-owned Shanghai No. 6 Cotton Textile Factory, demonstrating.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    华东纺织管理局和中国纺织工会上海市委员会联合举办的先进工作法表演观摩会，组织了上海市纺织工人向劳动模范和先进工作者学习、交流经验。这是国营上海第六棉纺织厂粗纱车间女工、先进工作者叶银娣正在表演的情形。
