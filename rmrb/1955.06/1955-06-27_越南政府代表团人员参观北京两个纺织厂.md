Title: Members of the Vietnamese Government Delegation Visit Two Textile Factories in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 1

    Members of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Government Delegation Visit Two Textile Factories in Beijing

    (Our newspaper dispatch) Delegation members Lê Văn Hiến, Phan Anh, Nguyễn Văn Huyên, Nghiêm Xuân Âm, and Phạm Ngọc Thạch of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, visited the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory and the No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory on the morning of the 26th. They were accompanied by Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

    At 10:30 a.m., the distinguished guests first arrived at the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory. Accompanied by Song Ting, Director of the textile factory's Construction Preparatory Office, and others, the delegation members carefully observed the trial operation of each process in the seven workshops. Workers were engaged in production in the newly built, spacious, clean, and well-ventilated workshops. The representatives were very interested in the fact that all the machinery used in this factory was manufactured in China.

    During a break after the tour, Song Ting briefed the distinguished guests on the factory's scale and construction process. The distinguished guests inquired in detail about the factory's production situation and the workers' living welfare and training. As they left the factory, the workers had already finished their shift, and many workers ran over to warmly applaud the distinguished guests to express their welcome.

    Next, the distinguished guests went to the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory for a visit. Accompanied by factory director Yang Huijie, the representatives not only visited the production in the workshops but also the factory's attached nursery and the workers' residential area. In the nursery, the representatives asked the caregivers about the babies' daily lives, and they affectionately held the children in their arms. Finally, the representatives also visited the workers' cafeteria, club, and clinic, and visited several workers' families.

### 越南政府代表团人员参观北京两个纺织厂

1955-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    越南政府代表团人员参观北京两个纺织厂
    本报讯  越南民主共和国政府代表团团员黎文献、潘英、阮文煊、严春庵和范玉石，在二十六日上午参观了国营北京第一棉纺织厂和第二棉纺织厂。陪同贵宾们参观的有我国外交部礼宾司司长柯华等。
    上午十时半，贵宾们首先来到了国营北京第二棉纺织厂。在纺织厂筹建处处长宋汀等的陪同下，代表团人员仔细地参观了七个车间各个工序的试车情况。在新建的宽大整洁、空气十分通畅的车间里，工人们正在进行生产。代表们对这个工厂里使用的全部机器都是中国自己制造的产品感到非常大的兴趣。
    参观后休息时，宋汀向贵宾们介绍了工厂的规模和筹建经过。贵宾们详细地询问了工厂的生产情况和工人的生活福利、培养训练等问题。当他们离开工厂时，工人已经下班，许多工人都跑来向贵宾们热烈地鼓掌表示欢迎。
    接着，贵宾们又前往国营北京第一棉纺织厂参观。在厂长杨慧洁陪同下，代表们除参观了各个车间的生产情况，还参观了工厂附设的乳儿室和工人的住宅区。在乳儿室里，代表们向保育人员询问乳儿们每天的生活情况，他们并亲切地把孩子们抱在手里。最后，代表们还参观了工人的食堂、俱乐部和医务所，并访问了几个工人的家庭。
