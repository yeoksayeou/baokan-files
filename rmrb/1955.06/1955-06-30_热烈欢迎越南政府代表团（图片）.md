Title: Warmly Welcome the Vietnamese Government Delegation (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 5

To warmly welcome President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led, who came to our country for an official visit at our invitation, a special plane arrived in Beijing on June 25. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, Vice Chairman, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, went to the airport to welcome President Ho Chi Minh and the distinguished guests of the delegation. The picture shows President Ho Chi Minh reviewing the honor guard. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photo

President Ho Chi Minh and the children presenting flowers. On the left is Chairman Mao Zedong. Our reporter Yuan Yiping photo

President Ho Chi Minh receiving flowers from welcoming crowds. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue photo

President Ho Chi Minh paid a visit to Chairman Mao Zedong at 12:00 noon on June 25. The picture shows Chairman Mao Zedong talking with President Ho Chi Minh and others. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photo

President Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Liu Shaoqi at Premier Zhou's reception banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo

President Ho Chi Minh and Comrade Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, at Premier Zhou Enlai's reception banquet. Hou Bo photo (Xinhua News Agency)

President Ho Chi Minh and members of the delegation visited the state-owned Beijing Southern Suburb Farm on June 28. The picture shows President Ho Chi Minh and members of the delegation visiting agricultural machinery at the farm. Our reporter Yuan Yiping photo

President Ho Chi Minh with the children of Beijing Beihai Kindergarten. Our reporter Yuan Yiping photo

Members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the Temple of Heaven's Circular Mound Altar on June 27. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo

Members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visiting the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory. Our reporter Yuan Yiping photo

Members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visited the Beijing Women's Drawnwork Production Cooperative on June 27. The picture shows members of the delegation viewing the products produced by the cooperative. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photo
