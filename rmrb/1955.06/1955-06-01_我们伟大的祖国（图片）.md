Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    All forty-four children in Class B of the sixth grade of the First Primary School affiliated with Beijing Normal University have joined the Young Pioneers, becoming the first Young Pioneer class in the entire school. The children in this class all participate in various research groups. This is a scene of the children in the Young Pioneer class learning about the automatic charging of the blast furnace in the natural science group.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京师范大学附属第一小学六年级乙班的四十四个小朋友，已全部入队，成为全校第一个红领巾班。这个班的小朋友都参加了各种各样的研究小组。这是红领巾班的小朋友们在自然科学家小组了解鼓风炉自动上料的情况。
