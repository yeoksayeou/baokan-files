Title: U.S. Continues War Preparations in Asia
Author:
Date: 1955-06-22
Page: 4

    U.S. Continues War Preparations in Asia

    U.S. equips Syngman Rhee's air force with jets
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 21st -- Seoul Dispatch: The United States handed over five F-86 Sabre jets to the Syngman Rhee clique on the 20th under the guise of "loan."
    This is the first batch of jets obtained by the Syngman Rhee air force. According to the U.S. military plan to intensify the arming of Syngman Rhee, the Syngman Rhee air force will form a Sabre jet wing this year. The U.S. is training a large number of South Korean jet pilots.

    U.S. continuously supplies warships to arm the Japanese Navy
    Xinhua News Agency, June 21st -- Washington Dispatch: A group of Japanese officers and soldiers who were trained in Connecticut, USA, left for California on the 20th to receive a submarine that the United States is leasing to Japan under the mutual "defense assistance" program. This submarine will be used to train and arm the Japanese Navy.
    Prior to this, the United States handed over two destroyer escorts to Japan on the 14th.

    U.S. will supply Pakistan with a large number of weapons
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 21st -- Karachi Dispatch: Pakistan's Minister of National Defence, General Mohammed Ayub Khan, said on the 18th that Pakistan had reached an agreement with the U.S. military aid advisory group in Pakistan on major issues of military assistance. He said that U.S. military equipment would begin to arrive in large quantities in the coming months.
    Ayub Khan also said that some Pakistani troops had been equipped with modern U.S. weapons.

### 美国在亚洲继续备战活动

1955-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美国在亚洲继续备战活动
    美国用喷气机装备李承晚空军
    新华社平壤二十一日讯　汉城消息：美国二十日以“借与”的名义交给李承晚集团五架F—86佩刀式喷气机。
    这是李承晚空军所得到的第一批喷气机。消息说，根据美国加紧武装李承晚的军事计划，李承晚空军将在今年组成一支佩刀式喷气机联队。美国正在大批训练南朝鲜的喷气机驾驶员。
    美国不断供给舰艇武装日本海军
    新华社二十一日讯　华盛顿消息：在美国康涅狄格州受训的一批日本军官和士兵，二十日已经动身前往加利福尼亚州去接受一艘美国根据共同“防御援助”计划租借给日本的潜水艇。这艘潜水艇将用来训练和武装日本海军。
    在这以前，美国曾经在十四日交给日本两艘驱逐护航舰。
    美国将以大批武器供给巴基斯坦
    新华社新德里二十一日电　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦国防部长穆罕默德·阿尤布汗将军十八日发表谈话说，巴基斯坦已经和在巴基斯坦的美国军事援助顾问团就军事援助的主要问题达成协议。他说，美国军事装备将在最近几个月内开始大批地运到。
    阿尤布汗还说，巴基斯坦的某些部队已经装备了美国的现代化武器。
