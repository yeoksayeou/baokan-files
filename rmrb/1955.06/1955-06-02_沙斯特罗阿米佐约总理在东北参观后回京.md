Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Northeast
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 1st – The Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife, along with accompanying visiting personnel, accompanied by China's Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, and his wife, as well as the Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua, returned to Beijing by plane from Shenyang on the morning of the 1st, after visiting various places in the Northeast.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included the Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Minister Chen Jiakang, Director of the General Office Dong Yueqian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and personnel from the Indonesian Embassy in China.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife, along with accompanying visiting personnel, visited Shenyang, Changchun, and Anshan on May 30th and 31st.

In Shenyang, the distinguished guests visited the Gaokan Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs and the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall. At the Gaokan Agricultural Production Cooperative, they saw well-stocked supply and marketing cooperatives, primary schools, and various new agricultural implements, and were warmly received by the farmers.

At the Changchun First Automotive Works, the distinguished guests saw that the first steam turbine generator of the thermal power plant had begun trial operation, and the workers in the tool workshop of the auxiliary factory were manufacturing various precision tools. The distinguished guests walked through the central avenue of the factory area and saw that various precision equipment aided by the Soviet Union was being installed in the various factory buildings. The distinguished guests also took a car to view the city of Changchun.

On the afternoon of the 31st, the distinguished guests visited the Iron Smelting Plant of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company in Anshan. When the distinguished guests climbed the huge ore storage tank, they saw the fourth automated blast furnace that was about to be completed. The distinguished guests came to the large rolling mill built with Soviet assistance, and Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo also looked at the huge factory building more than 400 meters long near the rolling mill. The distinguished guests also visited the automated and mechanized seamless steel pipe plant. During the visit to Anshan Iron and Steel, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo also happily watched news and photo clippings of his various activities during his visit to China.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and others in Shenyang and Anshan were also invited to attend banquets hosted by Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, and Wang Yixin, Deputy Mayor of Anshan.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在东北参观后回京

1955-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在东北参观后回京
    新华社一日讯　印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人以及随同来华访问人员，由我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人以及外交部礼宾司司长柯华陪同，在东北各地参观、访问后，已于一日上午自沈阳乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有我国外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理陈家康、办公厅主任董越千、礼宾司副司长王倬如和印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆人员。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人以及随同来华访问的人员，在五月三十日和三十一日曾分别在沈阳、长春、鞍山等地参观和访问。
    在沈阳，贵宾们参观了市郊的高坎农业生产合作社和东北工业陈列馆。在高坎农业生产合作社，他们看到了商品齐全的供销合作社、小学校和各种新式农具，受到农民的热情接待。
    贵宾们在长春第一汽车制造厂看到了热电站第一台汽轮发电机已经开始试运转，辅助工场的工具车间的工人正在制造各种精密的工具。贵宾们穿过厂区中央大道，看到各个厂房正在紧张地安装苏联援助的精密设备。贵宾们还乘车观看了长春市容。
    三十一日下午，贵宾们在鞍山参观了鞍山钢铁公司炼铁厂。贵宾们登上巨大的储矿槽时，看到了即将完工的第四座自动化炼铁炉。贵宾们来到了苏联帮助我国建设的大型轧钢厂，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理还在轧钢机附近眺望这个四百多公尺长的巨大厂房。贵宾们还参观了自动化机械化的无缝钢管厂。在鞍钢参观时，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理还高兴地观看了他到中国访问的各种活动的新闻和照片剪报。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理等在沈阳和鞍山并曾应邀出席了辽宁省省长杜者蘅和鞍山市副市长王一新的招待宴会。
