Title: Bulgarian Council of Ministers and Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Host Khrushchev and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 4th -- TASS reports: The Bulgarian People's Republic Council of Ministers and the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee held a grand reception on the 3rd to welcome Khrushchev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, who participated in the Soviet-Yugoslav talks in Belgrade, upon their arrival in Sofia.

Bulgarian attendees at the reception included: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic, Chervenkov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, G. Damyanov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Yankov, R. Damyanov, General Mikhailov, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Zhivkov, Tsankov and Staykov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Taskov, as well as ministers of various ministries, and others.

Also attending the reception were the Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Bulgaria, Prikhodov, and diplomatic envoys from other countries.

### 保部长会议和保共中央招待赫鲁晓夫等

1955-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保部长会议和保共中央招待赫鲁晓夫等
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议和保加利亚共产党中央委员会三日举行盛大的招待会，欢迎参加贝尔格莱德苏南会谈的苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬到达索非亚。
    保加利亚方面出席招待会的有：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文科夫，国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫，部长会议副主席詹科夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、米哈伊洛夫大将，保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员日夫科夫、赞科夫和斯塔伊科夫，保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记塔斯科夫以及各部部长等人。
    出席招待会的还有苏联驻保加利亚特命全权大使普里豪多夫和其他国家的外交使节。、
