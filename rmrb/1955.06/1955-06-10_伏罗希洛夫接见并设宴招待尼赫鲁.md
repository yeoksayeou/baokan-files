Title: Voroshilov Receives and Fetes Nehru

Author:

Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 9th (TASS dispatch): The President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, received Nehru, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India.

Accompanying Nehru were the Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai, and the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Menon.

Present at the meeting were First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Kuznetsov, Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov, and Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Gorkin.

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th (TASS dispatch from Moscow): The President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, hosted a banquet on June 9th in honor of Nehru, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, who is on an official visit to Moscow.

Attending the banquet were Nehru, his daughter Mrs. Gandhi, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai, Director of the European Department of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussein, the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Menon, and his wife.

On the Soviet side, those present at the banquet were Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, Puzanov, Ministers of the USSR Benediktov, Zhukov, Kabanov, Kovrigina, Mikhailov, President of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR, Nesmeyanov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Gromyko and Kuznetsov, Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Molochkov, etc.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th (TASS dispatch from Moscow): The Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, and his entourage, visited a historical aircraft factory in Moscow on June 9th and were warmly welcomed by the factory's employees.

The day before, Prime Minister Nehru had also visited the Stalin Automobile Factory in Moscow.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见并设宴招待尼赫鲁

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见并设宴招待尼赫鲁
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫接见了印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
    陪同尼赫鲁进见的有印度外交部秘书长皮莱和印度驻苏联大使梅农。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫、苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金。
    新华社九日讯　塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫九日设宴招待到莫斯科作正式访问的印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
    出席宴会的有尼赫鲁、他的女儿甘地夫人、印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度外交部欧洲司司长候赛尼、印度驻苏联大使梅农和夫人。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长别涅吉克托夫、朱可夫、卡巴诺夫、科夫里金娜、米哈伊洛夫，苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯和库兹涅佐夫，苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫，以及苏联外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫等。
    宴会在友好亲切的气氛中进行。
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：印度共和国总理尼赫鲁和他的随行人员，在六月九日参观了莫斯科的一个历史悠久的飞机工厂，受到工厂职工们的热烈欢迎。
    在前一天，尼赫鲁总理还曾参观了莫斯科的斯大林汽车工厂。
