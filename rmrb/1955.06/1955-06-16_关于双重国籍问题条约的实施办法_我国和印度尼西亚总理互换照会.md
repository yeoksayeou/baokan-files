Title: Implementation Measures for the Treaty on Dual Nationality: Exchange of Notes Between the Chinese and Indonesian Prime Ministers
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 15th -- During the visit of the Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, to China, the Premier of the State Council of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, discussed the implementation measures for the "Treaty Between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality" in Beijing, and reached an understanding after a full exchange of views. The two Prime Ministers exchanged notes in Beijing on the morning of June 3, 1955.

### 关于双重国籍问题条约的实施办法  我国和印度尼西亚总理互换照会

1955-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    关于双重国籍问题条约的实施办法
    我国和印度尼西亚总理互换照会
    新华社十五日讯　在印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约访华期间，我国国务院总理周恩来曾经和阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理，在北京商谈关于“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约”的实施办法，并在充分交换意见之后达成谅解。两国总理在一九五五年六月三日上午在北京互换照会。
