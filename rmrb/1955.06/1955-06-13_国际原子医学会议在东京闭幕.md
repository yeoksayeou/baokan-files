Title: International Atomic Medicine Conference Concludes in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 11th: The International Atomic Medicine Conference, which opened in Tokyo on May 30th to study the harmful effects of atomic and hydrogen bomb explosions on humans, closed on June 11th.

This conference was initiated by the Japanese Doctors Against Atomic and Hydrogen Weapons Committee. Scientists from ten countries, including Japan, China, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, and France, attended the conference. During the conference, these scientists studied clinical diagnostic reports of victims of the atomic bomb explosions in Hiroshima and Nagasaki and the Bikini hydrogen bomb tests, and conducted field visits to Hiroshima, Nagasaki, and other places.

At the closing meeting, the chairman of the conference, Japanese scientist Dr. Masao Tsuzuki, said that the thirteen-day conference was a success. Ugandan scientist Gale gave a speech on behalf of the scientists from countries other than Japan who participated in the conference. He said: All participants in the conference, regardless of their race and political beliefs, unanimously agreed to continue to cooperate to protect humanity, which is now threatened by terrible dangers.

Dr. Tsuzuki had suggested during the conference that a permanent international scientific body be established to continue research on radioactive diseases. At the closing meeting, he said that he would propose a plan to establish such an international body in the future.

### 国际原子医学会议在东京闭幕

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    国际原子医学会议在东京闭幕
    新华社十一日讯　东京消息：五月三十日在东京开幕的，研究原子弹和氢弹爆炸的放射作用对人类的危害的国际原子医学会议，已经在六月十一日闭幕。
    这个会议是由日本医生反对原子武器和氢武器委员会发起的。出席会议的有日本、中国、苏联、英国、法国等十个国家的科学家。在会议期间，这些科学家研究了广岛、长崎原子弹爆炸和比基尼氢弹试验的受害者的临床诊断报告，并且到广岛、长崎等地进行了实地视察。
    在闭幕会议上，会议主席、日本科学家都筑正南说，十三天的会议是成功的。乌干达科学家盖尔代表参加会议的日本以外的其他国家科学家致词。他说：所有参加会议的人，不管他们的种族和政治信仰如何，都一致同意继续合作，来保护现在受着可怕的危险的威胁的人类。
    都筑正南在会议中曾建议成立一个常设的国际科学家机构，对放射性疾病继续进行研究。他在闭幕会议上说，他将在以后提出建立这样一个国际机构的计划。
