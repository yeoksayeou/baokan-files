    Our Great Motherland
    Palm fiber woven products from Xinfan County, Sichuan Province, are famous specialties. Supply and marketing cooperatives have purchased large quantities of exquisite palm fiber cool hats, cool slippers, and various household utensils. After being sorted and arranged, these palm fiber woven products will be transported and sold to various places to meet the summer needs of the people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四川省新繁县的棕丝织品是有名的特产。供销合作社收购了大批精美的棕丝凉帽、凉拖鞋和各种现具。这些棕丝织品经过整理以后，将运销各地，供应人民夏令需要。
