    Our Great Motherland
    The Harbin Electric Machinery Factory is trial-manufacturing China's first 10,000-kilowatt hydroelectric power equipment. Workers and technicians are actively inspecting the power equipment, preparing to transport it to the Guanting Reservoir hydroelectric power station for installation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    哈尔滨电机厂正在试制我国第一套一万瓩水力发电设备。工人们和技术员积极检查发电设备，准备运到官厅水库水力发电站去进行安装。
