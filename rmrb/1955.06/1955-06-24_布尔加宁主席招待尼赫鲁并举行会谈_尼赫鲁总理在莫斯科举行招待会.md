Title: Chairman Bulganin Receives Nehru and Holds Talks; Prime Minister Nehru Holds Reception in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd dispatch from Moscow (TASS): Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, received the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, at the Kremlin on the 22nd. Talks were held during the reception, attended by Kaganovich, Mikoyan, and Khrushchev.

    On the Indian side, Nehru's entourage included Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai; Director of the European Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussain; Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon; and First Secretary of the Embassy, Gore.

    On the Soviet side, the attendees included First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Gromyko, and Kuznetsov.

    The talks proceeded in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd dispatch from Moscow (TASS): Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, hosted a banquet in the Grand Kremlin Palace on the 22nd in honor of the Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, Nehru, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union.

    Those attending the banquet included Nehru, his daughter Mrs. Gandhi, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai; Director of the European Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussain; Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon and his wife, as well as embassy staff.

    On the Soviet side, those attending the banquet included: Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Pospelov, and Suslov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, Ministers of various ministries of the USSR, generals and admirals of the Soviet Army and Navy, and heads of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Trade, etc.

    The hosts and guests exchanged toasts during the banquet.

    The banquet proceeded in an extremely warm and cordial atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd dispatch from Moscow (TASS): The Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, Nehru, who is on an official visit to the Soviet Union, held a reception on the 22nd.

    Those attending the reception from the Soviet side included: Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, Ministers of various ministries of the USSR, heads of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Defense, the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, figures from science, culture, and the arts, Soviet journalists and foreign journalists.

    Those attending the reception from the Indian side included: Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon and embassy staff, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai; Director of the European Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussain; Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and Indian journalists.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union also attended the reception.

    During the banquet, a delegation of professors and students from Moscow University presented Nehru with a certificate of Honorary Doctor of Laws from Moscow University.

### 布尔加宁主席招待尼赫鲁并举行会谈  尼赫鲁总理在莫斯科举行招待会

1955-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁主席招待尼赫鲁并举行会谈
    尼赫鲁总理在莫斯科举行招待会
    新华社二十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁二十二日在克里姆林宫接见了印度共和国总理尼赫鲁。接见时举行了会谈，参加会谈的有卡冈诺维奇、米高扬和赫鲁晓夫。
    印度方面出席会谈的还有尼赫鲁的随员印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼、印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农和大使馆一等秘书高尔。
    苏联方面出席会谈的还有苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯和库兹涅佐夫。
    会谈在诚挚和友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社二十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁二十二日在大克里姆林宫设宴招待现在苏联访问的印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
    出席宴会的有尼赫鲁、他的女儿甘地夫人、印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼、印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农和他的夫人以及大使馆人员。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、波斯伯洛夫和苏斯洛夫，苏联部长会议各副主席，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长，苏联陆海军将领，苏联外交部和对外贸易部负责人等。
    宴会时宾主互相致词。
    宴会在极其热烈和亲切的气氛中进行。
    新华社二十三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：正在苏联作正式访问的印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁于二十二日举行招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议各副主席，俄罗斯联邦共和国部长会议主席普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长，苏联外交部、国防部、文化部、对外贸易部，全苏工会中央理事会的负责人，科学、文化和艺术界人士，苏联记者和外国记者。
    印度方面出席招待会的有：印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农和大使馆人员、印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼、尼赫鲁的女儿英·甘地夫人、印度记者。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻苏联外交使节。
    在宴会上，莫斯科大学的教授和学生代表团把莫斯科大学名誉法学博士证书授予尼赫鲁。
