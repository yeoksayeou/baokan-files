Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation, consisting of twelve members headed by Wang Kunlun, returned to Beijing on the 12th after visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to attend the "May Day" celebrations and for sightseeing.

During their stay in the Soviet Union, the delegation visited Moscow, Leningrad, Stalingrad, and the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic.

On their way through Harbin, the delegation gave reports on their impressions of their visit to the Soviet Union to workers, students, and cadres of various factories, schools, organizations, and groups.

### 中苏友协访苏代表团回北京

1955-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协访苏代表团回北京
    新华社十二日讯　应苏联对外文化协会邀请去苏联参加“五一”节观礼和参观访问的中苏友好协会访苏代表团一行十二人由团长王昆仑率领在十二日回到北京。
    代表团在苏联期间，先后访问了莫斯科、列宁格勒、斯大林格勒等城市和亚美尼亚苏维埃社会主义共和国。
    代表团路过哈尔滨时，曾分别向各工厂、学校、机关、团体的工人、学生和干部们作了访苏观感的报告。
