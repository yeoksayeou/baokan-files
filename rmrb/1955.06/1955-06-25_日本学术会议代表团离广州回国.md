Title: Delegation from the Science Council of Japan Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 24th: The delegation from the Science Council of Japan, which came to our country at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the morning of the 24th after visiting various parts of our country.

The delegation from the Science Council of Japan arrived in Beijing on June 3rd. After visiting scientific and educational institutions in Beijing, meeting with Chinese scientists, and conducting academic exchange activities, the delegation departed from Beijing in three groups on June 12th and 14th to visit the Northeast, Southwest, and East China. A group of delegates led by the head of the delegation, Seiji Kaya, left Beijing for the Northeast on the 12th. Then they visited Nanjing. Takeo Kuwabara and Denzaburo Miyadi went to Sichuan for a visit on June 12th. A group of ten people, including Shigeru Nanbara, left Beijing for Shanghai on the 14th, and toured Hangzhou and Suzhou. Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children, met with Arata Osada, former President of Hiroshima University and President of the Japanese Association for the Defence of Children, and Liu Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, met with Shigeru Nanbara and Hyoei Ouchi. On June 22nd, the three groups of delegates met in Wuhan and flew to Guangzhou that afternoon. In Guangzhou, they held symposiums on natural sciences and social sciences with Guangzhou scientists and visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute.

### 日本学术会议代表团离广州回国

1955-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    日本学术会议代表团离广州回国
    据新华社广州二十四日电　应中国科学院邀请来我国的日本学术会议代表团在我国各地参观访问后，已在二十四日上午离广州经香港回国。
    日本学术会议代表团是在六月三日到达北京的。代表团在北京访问了科学教育机关，会见了我国科学界人士并进行学术交流活动后，在六月十二日和十四日先后分三批到东北、西南和华东等地参观访问。以茅诚司团长为首的一批代表，在十二日离北京赴东北。然后到南京参观访问。桑原武夫和宫地传三郎二人在六月十二日赴四川访问。南原繁等一行十人于十四日离北京到上海访问，并到杭州和苏州两地游览。中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会主席宋庆龄接见了前广岛大学校长、日本保卫儿童会会长长田新，上海市副市长刘季平接见了南原繁和大内兵卫。六月二十二日，代表团的三批代表在武汉会合，当日下午飞往广州。他们在广州曾同广州的科学界人士举行了自然科学和社会科学的座谈会，并曾到农民运动讲习所旧址等处参观。
