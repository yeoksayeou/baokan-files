Title: Swedish Parliamentary Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: According to TASS news, the Swedish parliamentary delegation headed by the Speaker of the Swedish Lower House, Nilsson, returned to Moscow on the 10th and 11th after visiting various places in the Soviet Union.

The delegation arrived in Moscow on June 1st. During their stay in Moscow, they visited the Kremlin, Moscow University, watched theatrical performances, and toured Moscow by car.

On the 4th, the delegation left Moscow for Stalingrad for a two-day visit, and then divided into three groups to visit Kiev, Stalino and Crimea, Tashkent and Samarkand, Baku, Tbilisi, Rustavi and Sochi.

The delegation visited industrial enterprises and cultural and educational institutions in various places and was warmly welcomed by the Soviet people.

### 瑞典议会代表团在苏联进行参观和访问

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典议会代表团在苏联进行参观和访问
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社消息：以瑞典下议院议长尼尔逊为首的瑞典议会代表团人员在苏联各地进行参观访问后，已于十日和十一日先后回到莫斯科。
    代表团于六月一日到达莫斯科，在莫斯科期间参观了克里姆林宫，访问了莫斯科大学，观看了戏剧，并乘汽车游览了莫斯科市容。
    代表团于四日离莫斯科往斯大林格勒参观了两天，然后分成三组分别访问了基辅、斯大林诺和克里米亚，塔什干和撒马尔罕，巴库、第比利斯、鲁斯塔威和索契等地。
    代表团参观了各地工业企业和文教机关，受到了苏联人民的热烈欢迎。
