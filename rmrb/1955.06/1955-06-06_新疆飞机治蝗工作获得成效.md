Title: Xinjiang's Aircraft Locust Control Work Achieves Results
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

From May 14th to 31st, the area treated by aircraft for locust control in Qitai, Jimusar, Hutubi, Manas, and Changji in northern Xinjiang reached 18,489 hectares, protecting 3,298 hectares of farmland. In Hutubi, Changji and other places, the mortality rate of locusts reached 100%. Miquan, Shawan, Changji and other places are also carrying out ground-based pesticide and machinery locust control work.

### 新疆飞机治蝗工作获得成效

1955-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆飞机治蝗工作获得成效
    从五月十四日到三十一日，新疆北部奇台、吉木萨尔、呼图壁、玛纳斯和昌吉等地飞机治蝗的防治面积已达一万八千四百八十九公顷，受到保护的农田有三千二百九十八公顷。呼图壁、昌吉等地蝗虫死亡率达百分之百。米泉、沙湾、昌吉等地还正在紧张地进行地面药械治蝗工作。　（新华社）
