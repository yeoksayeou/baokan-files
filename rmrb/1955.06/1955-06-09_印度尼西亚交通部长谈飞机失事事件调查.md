Title: Indonesian Minister of Transportation Discusses Investigation of Plane Crash

Author:

Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 8th—Antara News Agency reported on the 7th that Indonesian Minister of Transportation Gani told Antara reporters that the work of the Indonesian investigation committee responsible for investigating the "Kashmir Princess" plane crash was effective, and its report was completely trustworthy. This report is based on facts and is entirely technical. Gani said that the investigation results of this crash have received satisfactory responses from all parties.

### 印度尼西亚交通部长谈飞机失事事件调查

1955-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚交通部长谈飞机失事事件调查
    新华社雅加达八日电　据安塔拉通讯社七日报道，印度尼西亚交通部长干尼对安塔拉记者说，负责调查“克什米尔公主号”飞机失事事件的印度尼西亚调查委员会的工作是有成效的，它的报告是完全可以信赖的。这个报告是以事实为根据，并且完全是技术性的。干尼说，这次失事事件的调查结果已经得到了各方面满意的反应。
