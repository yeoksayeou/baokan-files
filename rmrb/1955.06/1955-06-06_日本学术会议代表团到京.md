Title: Japanese Science Council Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency: The thirteen-member delegation from the Japanese Science Council, led by its chairman, Seiji Kaya, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of June 3rd after visiting the Soviet Union.

### 日本学术会议代表团到京

1955-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日本学术会议代表团到京
    据新华社讯　在苏联访问后来我国访问的日本学术会议代表团一行十三人，在团长、日本学术会议会长芽诚司率领下已在三日下午乘飞机到达北京。
