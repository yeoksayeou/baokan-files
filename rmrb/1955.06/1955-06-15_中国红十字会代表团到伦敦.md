Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Arrives in London
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 14th. London Dispatch: The Chinese Red Cross delegation of four, led by Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross, arrived in London by plane on the 13th, at the invitation of the British Red Cross Society for a friendly visit to Britain.
    Welcoming the delegation were the Countess of Limerick, Vice President of the British Red Cross Society, and Miss Barker, the Society's International Liaison and Relief Advisor.
    Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the United Kingdom, also welcomed them at the airport.
    The delegation will begin its two-week visit to the United Kingdom.

### 中国红十字会代表团到伦敦

1955-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会代表团到伦敦
    新华社布拉格十四日电　伦敦消息：应英国红十字会邀请前往英国作友好访问的中国红十字会代表团一行四人，在中国红十字会会长李德全率领下，十三日乘飞机抵达伦敦。
    欢迎代表团的有英国红十字会副会长利麦里克伯爵夫人和该会国际联络与救济顾问巴克小姐。
    中国驻英国代办宦乡也到机场欢迎。
    代表团将开始在英国的为时两周的访问活动。
