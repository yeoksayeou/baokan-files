Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and Others Visit the Forbidden City
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 1
　
Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo of the Republic of Indonesia and his wife, along with accompanying visitors, toured the Forbidden City on the morning of June 2nd at 11:00 AM, accompanied by Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country, and others.

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and others, accompanied by Wu Zhongchao, Director of the Palace Museum, visited the three main halls: Taihe Hall, Zhonghe Hall, and Baohe Hall. In Taihe Hall, they visited the "Exhibition of Ancient Art Treasures," and saw painted pottery from 2000 BC, the Guo Ji Zi Bai Pan from 800 BC, the Lu Lengjia painting of Arhats from the Tang Dynasty, Ming Dynasty blue-and-white and polychrome porcelain, and Qing Dynasty jade carving "The Nine Elders of Huichang." Next, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and others went to the Ceramics Hall in Cining Palace, where they admired porcelain produced in the Song, Yuan, and Ming dynasties, toured the West Six Palaces, and visited the "Comprehensive Exhibition of Art from Past Dynasties." The distinguished guests also toured the Imperial Garden. Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo showed great interest in the architecture of the Forbidden City, and he and his wife took pictures in many places, including the throne in Taihe Hall and the gilded Qilin in front of the Cining Palace, as souvenirs.

Before leaving, Wu Zhongchao, Director of the Palace Museum, presented Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo with a copy of Song Dynasty paintings.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理等游览故宫

1955-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理等游览故宫
    本报讯　二日上午十一时，印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人及随同来华访问的人员，由我国外交部礼宾司司长柯华等陪同游览了故宫。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理等在故宫博物院院长吴仲超陪同下，游览了太和、中和和宝和等三大殿。在太和殿，他们参观了“古代艺术品陈列”，看到了公元两千年前的彩陶，公元八百年前的虢季子白盘，唐代的卢楞伽画罗汉像，明代的青花和彩瓷等名瓷，清代的青玉雕刻“会昌九老”等艺术品。接着，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理等到慈宁宫的陶瓷馆，观赏了宋、元、明出产的瓷器，游览西六宫，参观了“历代艺术综合陈列”。贵宾们最后还游览了御花园。沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理对故宫的建筑发生很大兴趣，他特地和他的夫人在太和殿的宝座和慈宁宫门前的镏金麒麟等许多地方摄影留念。
    临别时，故宫博物院院长吴仲超将复制宋人绘画一册赠送给沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理。
