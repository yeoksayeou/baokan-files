Title: Vice Premier Ulanfu and Others Visit the Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. In the afternoon of the 3rd, Ulanfu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Jia Tuofu, Director of the Fourth Office and Minister of Light Industry, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, as well as Deputy Directors of the Fourth Office and the State Planning Commission, and Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Textile Industry, the Ministry of Light Industry, and the Ministry of Local Industry visited the Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition.

　　Hungarian Embassy in China Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Medick, and the head of the Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition Delegation, Foldes, accompanied the visit.

### 乌兰夫副总理等参观匈纺织试验仪器展览会

1955-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    乌兰夫副总理等参观匈纺织试验仪器展览会
    新华社三日讯　三日下午，中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫，第四办公室主任兼轻工业部长贾拓夫，对外贸易部长叶季壮，以及第四办公室和国家计划委员会副主任，纺织工业部、轻工业部和地方工业部副部长参观了匈牙利纺织试验仪器展览会。
    匈牙利驻我国大使馆临时代办麦迪克、匈牙利纺织试验仪器展览会代表团团长弗尔德士陪同参观。
