Title: Molotov Receives Menon
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 17th. TASS, New York, June 16th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov received Mr. Krishna Menon, Representative of the Republic of India to the United Nations, on the 15th. The conversation lasted for one hour. Present at the conversation was A.Ya. Sobolev, Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations.

On the evening of June 15th, the Soviet delegation, headed by V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, left New York for San Francisco to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the signing of the United Nations Charter.

### 莫洛托夫接见梅农

1955-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见梅农
    新华社十七日讯　塔斯社纽约十六日讯：苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫在十五日接见了印度共和国驻联合国代表克里希纳·梅农先生。谈话进行了一小时。谈话时在座的有苏联常驻联合国代表阿·亚·索波列夫。
    六月十五日晚间，以苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫为首的苏联代表团离纽约前往旧金山，去参加联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会。
