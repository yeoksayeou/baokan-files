Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The annual Dali Materials Exchange Fair in Yunnan is especially lively this year. More than 500,000 people participated in the materials exchange fair. State-owned trading companies have set up native and special product purchasing offices here. Tibetan merchants sell valuable medicinal materials such as deer antler, fritillary bulb, cordyceps, and bear bile to state-owned trading companies.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    云南一年一度的大理物资交流会，今年特别热闹。参加物资交流会的有五十多万人。国营贸易公司在这里设立了土产、特产收购处，西藏商人把鹿茸、贝母、虫草和熊胆等名贵药材卖给国营贸易公司。
