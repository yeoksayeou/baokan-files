Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Soviet Academy of Sciences Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 19th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received Academician Bardin, head of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, and all members of the delegation on the morning of the 18th: Academician Gerasimov, Academician Kostenko, Academician Betejin, Corresponding Academician Belousov, Corresponding Academician Mishustin, Corresponding Academician Tananayev, Professor Voronin, Researcher Astafiev, and Researcher Cherkasin.

Present at the reception were Chen Boda and Zhang Jiafu, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Lu Dong, Vice Minister of Heavy Industry; Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry; and He Chang Gong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Geology.

### 陈毅副总理接见苏联科学院代表团

1955-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见苏联科学院代表团
    新华社十九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅在十八日上午接见了苏联科学院代表团团长巴尔金院士和全体团员：格拉西莫夫院士、科斯琴科院士、别捷赫金院士、别洛乌索夫通讯院士、米舒斯金通讯院士、塔纳纳耶夫通讯院士、沃罗宁教授、阿斯塔菲耶夫研究员、契尔卡申研究员。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院副院长陈伯达、张稼夫，重工业部副部长吕东，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，燃料工业部副部长刘澜波，地质部副部长何长工。
