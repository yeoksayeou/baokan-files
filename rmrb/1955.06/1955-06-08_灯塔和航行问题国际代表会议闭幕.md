Title: International Conference on Lighthouses and Navigation Issues Closes

Author:
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The International Conference on Lighthouses and Navigation Issues opened in Scheveningen, Netherlands, on May 31 and closed on June 4. Approximately 150 representatives from 26 countries participated in the conference. The conference discussed issues related to lighthouses and other navigational equipment.

The Chinese delegation, led by Sun Da Guang, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China, also attended the conference at the invitation of the Netherlands (the convening country) Coastal Lighthouse Bureau.

### 灯塔和航行问题国际代表会议闭幕

1955-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    灯塔和航行问题国际代表会议闭幕
    据新华社讯　灯塔和航行问题国际代表会议五月三十一日在荷兰斯赫维宁根开幕，六月四日闭幕。参加代表会议的有来自二十六个国家的大约一百五十名代表。会议讨论了有关灯塔和其他航行设备的问题。
    由中华人民共和国交通部部长助理孙大光率领的中国代表团也应荷兰（会议召集国）海岸灯志局的邀请出席了这次会议。
