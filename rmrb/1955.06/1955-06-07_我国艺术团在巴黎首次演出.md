Title: Chinese Art Troupe's First Performance in Paris
Author:
Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague Dispatch: A Paris dispatch reports: The Chinese Art Troupe, participating in the Second French International Drama Festival, gave its first performance at the Sarah Bernhardt Theatre in Paris on the evening of the 4th. Before the opening, Philippe, a famous French actor, read an introduction to Chinese classical drama written by Vercors, Chairman of the French Writers' Committee, and explained the plot of each program.

The performance was warmly welcomed, and the troupe took repeated curtain calls after each program. After the performance ended, Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Zhao Feng, deputy head, thanked the audience along with all the members of the troupe. The audience stood up and applauded and cheered for a long time. Many people shouted "Mao Zedong!", and the atmosphere was extremely enthusiastic.

### 我国艺术团在巴黎首次演出

1955-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在巴黎首次演出
    据新华社布拉格电　巴黎消息：参加法国第二届国际戏剧节的中国艺术团四日晚在巴黎的萨拉·伯纳特剧院举行首次演出，开幕前由法国著名演员菲利普宣读法国作家委员会主席维尔高尔所写的中国古典戏剧介绍，并且说明每一个节目的情节。
    演出受到热烈欢迎，每个节目演出都再三谢幕。演出终了后中国艺术团团长张致祥和副团长赵沨同全体团员向观众致谢，观众起立长时间鼓掌和欢呼，有许多人欢呼“毛泽东！”，情况极为热烈。
