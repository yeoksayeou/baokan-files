Title: French Writers Committee Hosts Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 15th. The French National Writers Committee held a reception on the afternoon of June 11th to host the Chinese Art Troupe. Among the more than 200 attendees were famous French writers Aragon, Triolet, Vercors, Mauriac, Gascar, Stil, and Roy. The reception was presided over by Vercors. Gascar delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the French National Writers Committee. Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, expressed his gratitude in his reply.

### 法作家委员会招待我艺术团

1955-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    法作家委员会招待我艺术团
    新华社布拉格十五日电　法国作家全国委员会在六月十一日下午举行酒会，招待中国艺术团。到会的有法国著名作家阿拉贡、特丽奥勒、维尔高尔、莫西拉克、加斯卡尔、斯梯、罗阿等二百多人。酒会由维尔高尔主持。加斯卡尔代表法国作家全国委员会致欢迎词。中国艺术团团长张致祥在答词中表示感谢。
