Title: International Atomic Medical Conference Opens in Tokyo, Sponsored by the Japanese Doctors' Committee Against Atomic and Hydrogen Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 1st: The International Atomic Medical Conference, sponsored by the Japanese Doctors' Committee Against Atomic and Hydrogen Weapons, opened in Tokyo on May 30th.

    Scientists from ten countries attended the conference, including Japan, China, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, France, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, Belgium, Chile, and Uganda. Bai Xiqing, a Chinese pathologist and director of the Chinese Medical Association, attended the conference.

    The conference will study the harm to humans from the radiation of atomic and hydrogen bomb explosions. Japanese experts will present reports to the conference on the effects of the U.S. hydrogen bomb tests conducted in Bikini Atoll last spring.

    The conference is scheduled to last two weeks. During the conference, attendees will inspect the Japanese fishing boat Fukuryu Maru, which was affected by the Bikini tests, and will also make on-site inspections in Hiroshima and Nagasaki.

### 由日本医生反对原子武器和氢武器委员会发起  国际原子医学会议在东京开幕

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    由日本医生反对原子武器和氢武器委员会发起
    国际原子医学会议在东京开幕
    新华社一日讯　东京消息：由日本医生反对原子武器和氢武器委员会发起的国际原子医学会议，五月三十日在东京开幕。
    出席会议的有日本、中国、苏联、英国、法国、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、比利时、智利和乌干达十个国家的科学家。我国病理学家、中华医学会理事白希清出席了会议。
    会议将要研究原子弹和氢弹爆炸的放射作用对人类的危害。日本专家将向会议提出关于去年春天美国在比基尼进行的氢弹试验的影响的报告。
    会议时间定为两周。出席会议的人在会议期间将视察比基尼试验时受害的日本渔船福龙丸，并将到广岛和长崎等地作实地视察。
