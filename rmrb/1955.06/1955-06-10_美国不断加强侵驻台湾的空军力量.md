Title: The United States Continues to Strengthen Its Air Force Forces Invading and Stationed in Taiwan

Author:

Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th—The United States is strengthening its air force stationed in Taiwan to support its policy of armed occupation of Taiwan.

    U.S. Fifth Air Force Commander LeMay visited Taipei on the 7th to discuss with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique the immediate establishment of a U.S. Air Force task force in Taiwan and the cooperation between the U.S. and Chiang Kai-shek's air forces. Davis, the deputy commander of the U.S. Air Force's Thirteenth Air Force, who will succeed the Fifth Air Force in invading and stationing in the Taiwan area, is preparing to go to Taiwan this week to establish the task force headquarters.

    According to the United Press on the 8th, following the U.S. Air Force, "a small number" of U.S. Marine Corps jets have also begun to use Taiwan's airports for the first time. This foreshadows the future stationing of complete air units of the U.S. Marine Corps in Taiwan.

    Moreover, according to the Washington "Star," the main U.S. bases in Taiwan also maintain ground equipment, "so that aircraft of the U.S. Air Force stationed in Okinawa can get there (Taiwan) at any time - to participate in combat immediately when necessary." The newspaper said that the planning for such "emergency situations" is very detailed, with extra gasoline pools, spare parts, tools, and working machines at appropriate bases. This equipment is all kept by Americans.

### 美国不断加强侵驻台湾的空军力量

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美国不断加强侵驻台湾的空军力量
    新华社九日讯　美国正在加强驻台湾的空军力量，来支持它武装侵占台湾的政策。
    美国第五航空队司令莱迈七日曾到台北活动，同蒋介石卖国集团讨论在台湾立即建立一支美国空军特遣部队的问题和美蒋空军进行合作的问题。即将继第五航空队侵驻台湾地区的美国空军第十三航空队的副司令戴维斯，已经准备在本周内到台湾去建立特遣部队总部。
    据合众社八日透露，继美国空军部队之后，
  “少数”美国海军陆战队的喷气机也已经开始第一次利用台湾机场进行活动。这预示美国海军陆战队将来会在台湾驻扎完整的航空部队。
    不仅如此，据华盛顿“明星报”报道，美国在台湾的主要基地还保持着地勤设备，“这样可使驻扎在冲绳岛的美国空军的飞机能够在任何时候赶到那里（台湾）——在必要时立即参加作战”。这家报纸说，应付这种“紧急情况”的计划工作非常详细，在适当的基地都有着额外的汽油池、零件、工具和工作机器。这些设备都由美国人保管。
