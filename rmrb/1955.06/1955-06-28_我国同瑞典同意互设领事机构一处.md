Title: China and Sweden Agree to Establish Consular Offices in Each Other's Countries

Author: 
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th -- The Chinese government and the Swedish government exchanged notes on consular matters on June 24th. The Chinese government agreed to Sweden establishing a consular office in Shanghai, and the Swedish government agreed to China establishing a consular office in Sweden.

### 我国同瑞典同意互设领事机构一处

1955-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国同瑞典同意互设领事机构一处
    新华社二十六日讯　我国政府同瑞典王国政府在六月二十四日就设领事问题互换照会。我国政府同意瑞典在上海设立一领事机构，瑞典政府同意我国在瑞典设立领事机构一处。
