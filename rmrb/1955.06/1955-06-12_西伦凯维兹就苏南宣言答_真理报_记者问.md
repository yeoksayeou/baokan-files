Title: Cyrankiewicz Answers "Pravda" Reporter's Questions on the Soviet-Yugoslav Declaration
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th (TASS): The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Józef Cyrankiewicz, answered some questions from a "Pravda" reporter in Warsaw regarding the declaration of the Soviet and Yugoslav governments.

    In his reply, Cyrankiewicz emphasized the great significance of the Soviet-Yugoslav talks for easing international tensions and consolidating peace in Europe and the world. He pointed out that the Polish people welcomed and supported the declaration of the Soviet and Yugoslav governments with satisfaction.

    When discussing the prospects of relations between Poland and Yugoslavia, Cyrankiewicz pointed out that the traditions of glorious revolutionary struggles link the Polish and Yugoslav workers' movements. He said that now there are all possibilities to develop cooperation between the two countries in the political, economic and cultural fields. The Polish people have always been enthusiastically concerned with the realization of these possibilities. The Polish government, on its part, has already taken some measures to normalize Polish-Yugoslav relations and create an atmosphere of friendship and cooperation between the two countries. The Polish working people fully support this effort.

    Cyrankiewicz said that the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish trade union organizations, social organizations and cultural organizations welcome with satisfaction the desire to establish and promote cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia's social organizations. At the same time, on their part, they will also strive to develop cooperation between Poland and Yugoslavia, which is beneficial to the interests of our two peoples, to the cause of peace and to coexistence between countries.

### 西伦凯维兹就苏南宣言答“真理报”记者问

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹就苏南宣言答“真理报”记者问
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹答复了“真理报”驻华沙记者就苏联南斯拉夫两国政府宣言所提出的一些问题。
    西伦凯维兹在答复中着重说明了苏南会谈对于缓和国际紧张局势、巩固欧洲和世界和平的重大意义。他指出，波兰人民满意地欢迎并且支持苏南两国政府宣言。
    西伦凯维兹在谈到波兰和南斯拉夫两国之间的关系的前途时指出，光荣的革命斗争的传统联结着波兰和南斯拉夫两国的工人运动。他说，现在具有一切可能来发展两国在政治、经济和文化方面的合作。波兰人民一向热情地关心着这些可能性得到实现。波兰政府在自己这方面已经采取了一些措施，以便使波兰—南斯拉夫关系正常化和创造两国友好和合作的气氛。波兰劳动人民完全支持这种努力。
    西伦凯维兹说，波兰统一工人党、波兰的工会组织、社会团体和文化团体对建立和促进苏联同南斯拉夫的各社会团体之间的合作的愿望表示满意的欢迎，同时，在自己这方面，也将努力使波兰同南斯拉夫之间的合作得到发展，这种合作是有利于我们两国人民的利益，有利于和平事业和国与国之间的共处的。
