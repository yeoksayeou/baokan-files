Title: Another Group of Chongqing Workers Goes to Work in Tibet
Author:
Date: 1955-06-15
Page: 2

    Another Group of Chongqing Workers Goes to Work in Tibet
    Another group of more than 130 workers and engineering technicians from Chongqing Municipality went to Tibet in batches from late May to June 10 to participate in construction work.
    More than 20 percent of these personnel are Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members. Before their departure, their respective units held warm send-off meetings. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 重庆又一批工人到西藏工

1955-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

    重庆又一批工人到西藏工作
    重庆市又有一百三十多名工人和工程技术人员，在五月下旬到六月十日分批前往西藏参加建设工作。
    这批人员中百分之二十以上是共产党员和青年团员。这些人员出发前，他们所在的单位都举行了热烈的欢送会。（新华社）
