Title: Indonesia and Hungary Prepare to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 26th: The Press Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued the following communiqué:
    The Indonesian government, through the Indonesian Embassy in Delhi, has notified the government of the Hungarian People's Republic that Indonesia is prepared to establish diplomatic relations with the Hungarian People's Republic.
    The government of the Hungarian People's Republic welcomes this step by the Indonesian government with pleasure, as it will promote the expansion of international ties and is in line with the common interests of the people of both countries.

### 印度尼西亚和匈牙利准备建交

1955-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和匈牙利准备建交
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社布达佩斯二十六日讯：匈牙利外交部新闻司发表公报如下：
    印度尼西亚政府经由印度尼西亚驻德里大使馆通知匈牙利人民共和国政府：印度尼西亚准备同匈牙利人民共和国建立外交关系。
    匈牙利人民共和国政府愉快地欢迎印度尼西亚政府的这个步骤，它将促进国际联系的扩大，并且符合于两国人民的共同利益。
