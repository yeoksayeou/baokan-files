Title: The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic Decides to Gift Part of the Machinery and Transportation Vehicles from the Czechoslovak Exhibition to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th dispatch: The government of the Czechoslovak Republic has decided to gift part of the machinery and transportation vehicles from the Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction of the Czechoslovak Republic, which recently took place in Beijing, to the government of the People's Republic of China. This includes an E25 universal excavator, a Skoda 375KVA diesel power generation set, an HVFD desktop drilling machine, an OKU35 gear milling machine, an HOJ-4 four-ton hydraulic rotary truck crane, a Skoda 706RO bus, and tractors, among other items.

The head of the Czechoslovak government delegation, Vice-Premier Ludmila Jankovcová, sent a letter to Vice-Premier Chen Yun when she was in Beijing. The original text is as follows:

Respected Comrade Chen Yun, Acting Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

The friendly relations and fraternal cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic are being consolidated and strengthened daily, for the benefit of the people of our two countries. Thanks to this fraternal cooperation and assistance from the People's Republic of China, the Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction of the Czechoslovak Republic was held in Beijing. The task of this exhibition is to promote further deepening of our cooperation and better mutual understanding.

I am honored to inform you, on behalf of the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, that the government of the Czechoslovak Republic has decided to donate some of the machinery and transportation vehicles currently on display at the Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction of the Czechoslovak Republic to the government of the People's Republic of China. Please accept these machines and transportation vehicles as a symbol of the friendship between the people of Czechoslovakia and China.

Attached is a list.

		Head of the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic
			Engineer Ludmila Jankovcová
			April 30, 1955, in Beijing

Vice-Premier Chen Yun, on behalf of our government, has replied with a letter of thanks for the gift from the Czechoslovak government.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府决定  赠送捷展览会部分机器和交通工具给我国

1955-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府决定
    赠送捷展览会部分机器和交通工具给我国
    新华社十五日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府决定把日前在北京举行的捷克斯洛伐克共和国十年社会主义建设成就展览会中的一部分机器和交通工具，包括E25型万能挖掘机，斯可达375KVA柴油发电设备，HVFD型台式钻床，OKU35型铣齿机，HOJ—4型四吨液压旋转式卡车起重机，斯可达706RO型公共汽车和拖拉机等，赠送给中华人民共和国政府。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长卢德米拉·扬科夫卓娃副总理前在北京时曾致函陈云副总理。原文如下：中华人民共和国国务院代总理尊敬的陈云同志：
    中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国之间的友好关系和兄弟合作，为了我们两国人民的利益，正在日益巩固和加强。由于中华人民共和国的这种兄弟般的合作和帮助，在北京举办了捷克斯洛伐克共和国十年社会主义建设成就展览会，这一展览会的任务是促使我们的合作进一步加深和相互间更好的了解。
    我荣幸地代表捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团通知您：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府业已决定将目前在捷克斯洛伐克共和国十年社会主义建设成就展览会上陈列的一些机器和交通工具赠送给中华人民共和国政府。请接受这些机器和交通工具作为捷、中两国人民友谊的象征。
    附清单一纸。
                捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长
                  工程师卢德米拉·扬科夫卓娃
                  一九五五年四月三十日于北京
    陈云副总理代表我国政府对捷克斯洛伐克政府的赠礼已复函致谢。
