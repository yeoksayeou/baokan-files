Title: Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan Holds Grand Banquet to Welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese Delegation to Visit Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 28th. To welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese delegation to visit our country, Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan held a grand banquet. Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Mao Zedong attended the grand banquet held by Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the evening of the 28th. The banquet was held to welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam led by him to visit China.

More than 1,000 Chinese and foreign guests attended the banquet held in the new hall of the Beijing Hotel tonight.

Truong Chinh, Le Van Hien, Phan Anh, Nguyen Van Huyen, Nghiem Xuan Yem, Ung Van Khiem, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Pham Ngoc Thach, members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, attended the banquet.

At 7:40 p.m., Chairman Ho Chi Minh, Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai entered the venue. The audience stood up and applauded warmly. Two female members of the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe presented flowers to Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

As the banquet began, the orchestra played the national anthems of China and Vietnam.

Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan stood up and delivered a speech, saying: "Today, we are very happy to welcome the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam headed by Chairman Ho Chi Minh. We are extremely pleased and honored that Chairman Mao Zedong has specially graced this banquet." He proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people and the close friend of the Vietnamese people!

Then, Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a speech, saying:

"I propose:

For the close friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people,

For peace in Asia and the world,

For the visit of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam led by him to China,

For the health of Chairman Ho Chi Minh,

To the health of the host of today's banquet - Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan!"

Chairman Ho Chi Minh raised his glass and said:

"Dear comrades and friends:

I propose:

For the further development and consolidation of the fraternal and unbreakable friendship between the people of Vietnam and China,

For the solidarity of the Vietnamese people and the people of the socialist and peaceful democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union,

For the friendship between the Vietnamese people and the people of peace-loving countries in Southeast Asia,

For peace in Asia and world peace,

To the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, the close friend of the Vietnamese people, and the friend of peace-loving people all over the world!"

When Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Ho Chi Minh began and ended their speeches, the hall erupted in warm applause.

At the banquet, Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, and Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan stood up successively and raised their glasses to wish the leaders of the two parties and countries of China and Vietnam good health.

After the banquet, the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe performed wonderful song and dance programs.

### 为欢迎胡志明主席和越南代表团访问我国  越南大使黄文欢举行盛大宴会

1955-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    为欢迎胡志明主席和越南代表团访问我国
    越南大使黄文欢举行盛大宴会
    新华社二十八日讯胡志明主席和毛泽东主席在二十八日晚出席了越南民主共和国大使黄文欢举行的盛大宴会。这次宴会是为了欢迎胡志明主席和他所率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团访问中国而举行的。
    出席今晚在北京饭店新楼大厅里举行的这次宴会的中外人士共有一千多人。
    越南民主共和国政府代表团团员长征、黎文献、潘英、阮文煊、严春庵、雍文谦、阮维祯、范玉石都出席了宴会。
    下午七时四十分，胡志明主席同毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理进入会场。这时，全场起立，热烈鼓掌。越南人民歌舞团的两位女团员向毛泽东主席和胡志明主席献花。
    宴会开始，乐队奏中越两国国歌。
    黄文欢大使首先起立致词，他说：“今天我们怀着非常愉快的心情，欢迎以胡志明主席为首的越南民主共和国政府代表团。毛泽东主席特地光临这次宴会，使我们感到万分愉快和荣幸。”他提议为中国人民伟大的领袖、越南人民亲密的朋友毛泽东主席的健康干杯！
    接着，毛泽东主席致词说：
    “我提议：
    为了中越人民亲密的友谊，
    为了亚洲和世界的和平，
    为了胡志明主席和他所率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团的访问中国，
    为了胡志明主席的健康，
    为今天宴会的主人——黄文欢大使的健康干杯！”
    胡志明主席举杯致词说：
    “亲爱的同志们，朋友们：
    我提议：
    为越中两国人民兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊进一步发展和巩固，
    为越南人民和以苏联为首的社会主义和平民主阵营人民的团结，
    为越南人民和东南亚爱好和平国家人民的友谊，
    为亚洲和平和世界和平，
    为中国人民的伟大领袖、越南人民亲密的朋友、全世界爱好和平人民的朋友毛泽东主席的健康干杯！”
    毛泽东主席和胡志明主席的致词开始和结束时，大厅里发出一阵热烈的掌声。
    在宴会上，越南劳动党总书记长征、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理、彭德怀副总理和黄文欢大使先后起立，举杯祝中越两国党和国家领导人员的健康。
    宴会结束后，越南人民歌舞团表演了精采的歌舞节目。
    今晚，中国方面出席宴会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、李维汉，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席傅作义、龙云，国务院秘书长习仲勋，各办公室主任罗瑞卿、林枫、薄一波，各部部长谢觉哉、钱瑛、章乃器、曾山、叶季壮、王鹤寿、黄敬、张霖之、陈郁、蒋光鼐、沙千里、滕代远、廖鲁言、梁希、马文瑞、杨秀峰、张奚若，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，我国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊、李克农、陈赓，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝、章伯钧，以及全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，国务院各办公室副主任，各部副部长，各委员会副主任，国防委员会委员，中国人民解放军高级将领，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，中共中央各部门负责人，各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人和代表，北京市副市长，首都科学、教育、文艺、宗教、新闻界人士。
