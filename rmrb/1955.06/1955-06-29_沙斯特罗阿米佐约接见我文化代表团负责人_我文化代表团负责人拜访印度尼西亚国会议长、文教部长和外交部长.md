Title: Sastroamidjojo Receives Head of Chinese Cultural Delegation; Head of Chinese Cultural Delegation Visits Indonesian Speaker of Parliament, Minister of Education and Culture, and Minister of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 28th: Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo received Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, and Zhou Erfu, deputy head of the delegation, on the afternoon of the 28th. Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, accompanied them during the meeting.

Prior to this, Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, and Zhou Erfu, deputy head of the delegation, accompanied by Ambassador Huang Zhen, visited Sadono, the Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 28th: Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, Zhou Erfu, deputy head of the delegation, and Hu Chunpu, secretary-general, paid a visit to Indonesian Minister of Education and Culture, Yamin, on the 27th. Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, accompanied them.

That afternoon, members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, accompanied by Nasution, Deputy Director of the Cultural Affairs Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Education and Culture, toured Jakarta.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 28th: Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, and Zhou Erfu, deputy head of the delegation, accompanied by Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, visited Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo on the 28th.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约接见我文化代表团负责人  我文化代表团负责人拜访印度尼西亚国会议长、文教部长和外交部长

1955-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约接见我文化代表团负责人
    我文化代表团负责人拜访印度尼西亚国会议长、文教部长和外交部长
    新华社雅加达二十八日电印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约在二十八日下午接见了中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和副团长周而复。陪同受接见的有中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
    在这以前，中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和副团长周而复在黄镇大使陪同下，拜访了印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺。
    新华社雅加达二十八日电中国文化代表团团长郑振铎、副团长周而复和秘书长胡春圃在二十七日拜会印度尼西亚文教部长雅明。陪同前往拜会的有中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
    当天下午，中国文化代表团人员在印度尼西亚文教部文化司副司长纳苏申陪同下，游览了雅加达。
    新华社雅加达二十八日电中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和副团长周而复二十八日在中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇陪同下，拜访了印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约。
