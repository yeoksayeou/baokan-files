Title: Welcome the Joint Statement of Bulganin and Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 23rd: The office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India issued a press release on the 23rd stating that Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, warmly welcomed the joint statement signed by the Prime Minister of India and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union in Moscow on June 22.

The press release stated: Ghosh told a reporter from the "New Age" weekly that the warm and spontaneous welcome received by Prime Minister Nehru throughout his memorable visit to the Soviet Union proves the Soviet people and government's consistent love and friendly feelings towards our country. The joint statement will strengthen this lasting friendship that binds the people of our two countries together. It will strengthen the cultural and economic ties that are being forged between the Soviet Union and the Republic of India today.

Ghosh said: "Since the joint statement declares adherence to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and since it calls for a complete ban on nuclear weapons, it will be a powerful guarantee for world peace. This will be another blow to the conspiracies of the warmongers and will be hailed by all people around the world."

The Communist Party is proud of our country's significant contribution to the cause of peace, and it is pleased that the friendship between our two countries has been consolidated in a historically significant way.

Ghosh concluded by saying that the Communist Party of India welcomes the news of the upcoming visit of Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, to India, and believes that this will further strengthen relations between India and the Soviet Union.

Indian Newspapers Publish Commentary
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 23rd: Delhi newspapers on the 23rd prominently published on the front page the news of the joint statement signed by Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Soviet Minister Chairman Bulganin in Moscow.

The "Hindustan Standard" headline spanning eight columns said: "Indo-Soviet Guarantee Efforts for World Peace."

The "Hindustan Standard" published an editorial, hailing the joint statement's reaffirmation of the current Indo-Soviet friendship based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The editorial said, "As in the past, the current Indo-Soviet friendship is built on a broad foundation of mutual cooperation." "Like our neighboring country China, we and Russia can develop a broader prospect of peaceful coexistence."

Former Governor-General of India Issues Statement
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 24th: According to a Madras report from the Press Trust of India, former Governor-General of India, Rajagopalachari, issued a statement on the 23rd welcoming the joint statement of Bulganin and Nehru.

Rajagopalachari said that the forces opposing peace are being driven into a corner.

Rajagopalachari welcomed the part of the joint statement concerning the reduction of armaments and the prohibition of nuclear weapons. He said that the Soviet Union's announcement of its advocacy for a large-scale and simultaneous reduction of conventional armaments is very important, because this leaves the American side with no further room for doubt or justification.

### 欢迎布尔加宁和尼赫鲁的联合声明

1955-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎布尔加宁和尼赫鲁的联合声明
    印度共产党总书记发表谈话
    新华社新德里二十三日电　印度共产党中央委员会办公厅在二十三日发表新闻公报说，印度共产党总书记阿约艾·高士对印度总理和苏联部长会议主席六月二十二日在莫斯科签署的联合声明表示热烈欢迎。
    新闻公报说：高士对“新世纪”周报记者发表谈话说，尼赫鲁总理在他值得纪念的在苏联的整个访问期间所受到的热烈和自发的欢迎，证明了苏联人民和政府一向对我们国家的持久的热爱和友好的感情。联合声明将加强把我们两国人民联系在一起的这种持久友谊。它将加强今天苏联和印度共和国之间正在铸成的文化和经济联系。
    高士说：“由于联合声明宣布要遵循和平共处的五项原则，由于它要求完全禁止核子武器，因此它将是对世界和平的有力保障。这将是对战争制造者的阴谋的又一次打击，并且将受到全世界所有人民的欢呼。”
    共产党对于我国对和平事业作出这个重大贡献一事感到自豪，它对我们两国之间的友谊具有历史意义地得到巩固感到高兴。
    高士最后说，印度共产党欢迎苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁将要访问印度的消息，并且相信这将使印度和苏联两国的关系更加密切。
    印度报纸发表评论
    新华社新德里二十三日电　德里各报二十三日在首页的显著地位刊载了关于印度总理尼赫鲁和苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在莫斯科签署的联合声明的消息。
    “印度斯坦旗报”的横贯八栏的标题说：“印苏保证为世界和平而努力”。
    “印度斯坦旗报”发表社论，欢呼联合声明确认了以和平共处的五项原则为基础的目前的印苏友谊。社论说，“同以往一样，现在的印苏友谊是建筑在互相合作的广泛基础上的。”“我们同俄国也正像我们同邻国中国一样，和平共处可能展开更广泛的前景。”
    前印度总督发表谈话
    新华社新德里二十四日电　据印度报业托辣斯马德拉斯消息，前印度总督拉贾戈帕拉查里二十三日发表谈话，欢迎布尔加宁和尼赫鲁的联合声明。
    拉贾戈帕拉查里说，反对和平的势力正在被逼入墙角。
    拉贾戈帕拉查里表示欢迎联合声明中关于裁减军备和禁止核子武器的部分。他说，苏联宣布主张大量地和同时地裁减常规军备是很重要的，因为这使得美国方面没有任何进一步怀疑和辩解的余地。
