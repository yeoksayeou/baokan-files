Title: South American Anglo-French Representatives' Meeting Issues Communiqué
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, June 29th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, an official communiqué was released in Belgrade on the afternoon of June 27th regarding the meeting of representatives from Yugoslavia, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France.

The communiqué stated that the representatives of the four countries, during their meeting in Belgrade from June 24th to 27th, discussed the general international situation and issues of common and direct concern.

The communiqué said: The meeting "confirmed a high degree of agreement among the four governments on the various international issues studied. In view of the relatively favorable developments in the recent international situation, this meeting is of particular importance. The four governments unanimously agreed that outstanding issues should be resolved by peaceful means and through negotiations based on full respect for and recognition of the independence, equality, right of self-defense, and collective security of all nations, in accordance with the Charter of the United Nations. They will continue to make efforts to promote such a resolution."

The communiqué stated: "The four governments expressed their firm belief that the existence of a strong and independent Yugoslavia and continued cooperation between them on a completely equal footing is a contribution to peace and stability." The communiqué praised the role of cooperation within the Balkan Alliance.

The communiqué concluded: The four governments believe that "this method of exchanging views can help promote closer understanding between them, and can also contribute to further improving the general international situation and to world peace. They believe that the good relations between them in many aspects in recent years will be maintained and further expanded."

### 南美英法代表会谈发表公报

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    南美英法代表会谈发表公报
    新华社二十九日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十八日讯：据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，贝尔格莱德二十七日下午发表了关于南斯拉夫、美国、英国、法国四国代表会谈的官方公报。
    公报说，四国代表在从六月二十四日到二十七日在贝尔格莱德举行的会谈中讨论了一般国际局势和共同直接有关的问题。
    公报说：会谈“证实四国政府对所研究的各种国际问题看法有着很大程度的一致。鉴于最近国际局势的比较有利的发展，这次会议具有特殊重要意义。四国政府一致同意：应当按照联合国宪章，用和平方法和通过以充分尊重和承认一切国家的独立、平等、自卫和集体安全的权利为基础的谈判来谋求解决悬而未决的问题。它们将继续尽力促进这种解决。”
    公报说：“四国政府表示，它们坚决相信：一个强大的和独立的南斯拉夫的存在和它们之间在完全平等的条件下继续合作，是对和平和稳定的一个贡献。”公报赞扬巴尔干联盟内的合作的作用。
    公报最后说：四国政府认为，“这种交换意见的方法可以有助于促进它们之间的更亲密的谅解，而且也可以对进一步改善一般的国际局势和对世界和平作出贡献。它们相信，近几年来它们之间在许多方面的良好关系将予以保持和进一步扩展。”
