Title: Romanian Council of Ministers Issues Resolution on Developing Sheep Husbandry

Author:

Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency - Bucharest—The Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic has issued a resolution on the development of sheep husbandry. The resolution stipulates a series of measures to strengthen state assistance to collective farms, cooperative farms, and individual farmers, so as to increase the number of sheep to 15 million by the end of 1960.

### 罗马尼亚部长会议发布发展饲羊业的决议

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚部长会议发布发展饲羊业的决议
    据新华社讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议发布了关于发展饲羊业的决议。决议规定采取一系列措施来加强国家对集体农庄、共耕社和个体农民的援助，以使羊的数目到一九六零年底增加到一千五百万只。
