Title: Yemeni Prime Minister al-Hassani Visits India
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 1st. Yemeni Prime Minister al-Hassani arrived in Calcutta on May 29th for a ten-day visit to India.

Al-Hassani told reporters that the Asian-African Conference provided Asian and African countries with a "chance to gather." He said that his country will always support India's efforts for peace. Yemen generally stands on the side of neutrality.

### 也门首相哈萨尼访问印度

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    也门首相哈萨尼访问印度
    新华社新德里一日电　也门首相哈萨尼在五月二十九日到达加尔各答，他将在印度作为期十天的访问。
    哈萨尼对新闻记者发表谈话说，亚非会议使亚非国家有“聚首的机会”。他说，他的国家将始终支持印度争取和平的努力。也门一般地是站在中立方面的。
