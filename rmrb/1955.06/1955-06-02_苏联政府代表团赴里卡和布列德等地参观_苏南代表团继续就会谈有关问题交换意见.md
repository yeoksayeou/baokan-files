Title: Soviet Government Delegation Visits Rijeka and Bled; Sino-Yugoslav Delegations Continue to Exchange Views on Relevant Issues of the Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bled, June 1st: According to TASS, the Soviet government delegation, after a two-day visit to Brioni Island, sailed on the "Jadranka" yacht to the port of Rijeka on the morning of May 31st.

    Traveling on the yacht were Khrushchev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Shepilov, Gromyko, and Kumykin, as well as members of the Yugoslav government delegation, Kardelj, Ranković, and Popović.

    Accompanied by leaders of the local shipyard, members of the Soviet and Yugoslav delegations visited various workshops of the shipyard.

    Subsequently, the two delegations traveled by car to the city of Postojna in the Socialist Republic of Slovenia.

    After arriving in Postojna, members of the Soviet and Yugoslav delegations visited a famous stalactite cave.

    The two government delegations left Postojna by car for the city of Bled. Residents of villages and cities along the way came out to welcome the motorcade. In Ljubljana, the capital of Slovenia, thousands of citizens lined the streets to welcome the two delegations.

    During the journey to Rijeka and Bled, members of the two government delegations continued to exchange views on relevant issues of the Soviet-Yugoslav talks in a spirit of mutual understanding.

### 苏联政府代表团赴里卡和布列德等地参观  苏南代表团继续就会谈有关问题交换意见

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团赴里卡和布列德等地参观
    苏南代表团继续就会谈有关问题交换意见
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社布列德一日讯：苏联政府代表团在布莱俄尼岛作了两天访问之后在五月三十一日清晨乘“雅德朗卡号”快艇赴里卡港。
    乘快艇前去的有赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、米高扬、谢皮洛夫、葛罗米柯和库米金，还有南斯拉夫政府代表团团员卡德尔、兰科维奇和波波维奇等人。
    苏联和南斯拉夫代表团人员在当地造船厂领导人员陪同下参观了造船厂的各个车间。
    随后，两国代表团乘汽车前往斯洛文尼亚人民共和国的波斯多伊纳城。
    苏联和南斯拉夫代表团人员到达波斯多伊纳后，参观了一个著名的钟乳石山洞。
    两国政府代表团人员乘汽车离开波斯多伊纳前往布列德城。沿途各乡村和城市居民都出来迎接汽车的行列。在斯洛文尼亚的首都卢布尔雅那，成千上万的公民夹道欢迎两国代表团。
    在前往里卡和布列德的旅途中，两国政府代表团成员继续在互相谅解的情况下就苏南会谈有关的问题交换意见。
