Title: Capital Holds Meeting to Commemorate "June 1st" and Protect Children

Author:

Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 31st – The capital's circles held a meeting to commemorate "June 1st" and protect children at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on May 31st. More than 1,600 people attended the meeting, including principals and teachers of middle and primary schools in Beijing, Young Pioneer instructors, childcare workers, literary and art workers, cadres of government organizations, street women, and responsible persons and representatives of central and Beijing democratic parties and people's organizations. The meeting was presided over by Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, spoke at the meeting (see page 3 of today's newspaper for a summary of the speech).

Subsequently, Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Fu Lianzhang, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee for the Protection of Children of the Chinese People and Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association, also spoke at the meeting one after another. They represented organizations such as the Democratic Women's Federation, the Trade Union, and the Youth League, expressing their warm support for the cause of education and the protection of children, and hoping that women, workers, youth, and medical workers across the country would contribute to this glorious cause in their respective work posts.

### 首都举行纪念“六一”保卫儿童大会

1955-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    首都举行纪念“六一”保卫儿童大会
    据新华社三十一日讯　首都各界纪念“六一”保卫儿童大会，五月三十一日在北京天桥剧场举行。出席大会的有北京市中、小学校长、教师，少年先锋队辅导员，保育工作者，文学艺术工作者，机关干部，街道妇女以及中央和北京市各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和代表等共一千六百多人。大会由北京市副市长吴晗主持。
    中华人民共和国教育部长张奚若在会上讲话
   （讲话摘要见今天本报第三版）。
    接着，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实、中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席、中华医学会理事长傅连暲等也先后在会上讲话，他们分别代表民主妇女联合会、工会及青年团等组织，表示热烈支持教育和保卫儿童的事业，希望全国妇女、职工、青年和医务工作者们在各自的工作岗位上，为这一光荣的事业贡献力量。
