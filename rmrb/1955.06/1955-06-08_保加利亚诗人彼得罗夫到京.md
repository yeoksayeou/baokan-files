Title: Bulgarian Poet Petrov Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Valeri Petrov, the Bulgarian poet and deputy editor of the humor newspaper "Hornet," arrived in Beijing by plane on the 5th, in accordance with the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. He will be visiting China for four months.

### 保加利亚诗人彼得罗夫到京

1955-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚诗人彼得罗夫到京
    新华社五日讯　根据中保文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚诗人、幽默报纸“大黄蜂”副总编辑瓦列里·彼得罗夫，在五日乘飞机到达北京。他将在我国作四个月的参观访问。
