Title: Moscow Holds Soviet-Indian Friendship Mass Rally
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 4

(Column)

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 22nd (TASS): A grand Soviet-Indian friendship mass rally was held at the "Dynamo" Stadium in Moscow on the 21st. Tens of thousands of Moscow residents participated in the rally, including workers from various Moscow enterprises, cadres from government offices, and workers in science and culture. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists, also attended the rally.
    When the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, and the leaders of the Soviet government appeared on the platform, all attendees stood up and applauded enthusiastically. Three thousand athletes lined up on the green lawn of the stadium, throwing bouquets of flowers to the crowd. Children presented flowers to Prime Minister Nehru and the leaders of the Soviet government as a sign of respect.
    At the beginning of the meeting, the Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, M. Yasnov, first addressed the assembly. He said that the working people of Moscow gathered here to express the warmest feelings of friendship and respect to the talented and hardworking Indian people. We know very well that the Indian people and the Indian government do not like war. The working people of Moscow and all the Soviet people wholeheartedly wish the Indian people happiness and prosperity, and wish them new achievements in the noble and just struggle for peace.
    Then, the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, delivered a speech in Hindi.
    Next, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, spoke.
    The speeches of Nehru and Bulganin were warmly welcomed by the attendees and were repeatedly interrupted by stormy and prolonged applause.

### 莫斯科举行苏印友好群众大会

1955-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行苏印友好群众大会
    据新华社二十二日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：二十一日，莫斯科“狄纳莫”运动场上举行了盛大的苏印友好群众大会。参加大会的有数万莫斯科居民。他们当中有莫斯科各企业的职工、机关的干部、科学和文化工作者等。各国驻苏联的外交使节、苏联和外国记者也参加了大会。
    当印度共和国总理尼赫鲁和苏联政府领导人在大会讲台上出现时，全体到会者起立，热烈鼓掌欢呼。排列在运动场绿色草地上的三千名运动员纷纷向群众抛掷花束。儿童们向尼赫鲁总理和苏联政府领导人献花致敬。
    开会时，莫斯科苏维埃主席米·雅斯诺夫首先向大会致词。他说，莫斯科劳动人民在这里集会，是为了向天才的和勤劳的印度人民表示最热烈的友好感情和尊敬。我们很清楚地知道，印度人民和印度政府是不喜欢战争的。莫斯科劳动人民和全体苏联人民衷心祝印度人民幸福和昌盛，祝他们在争取和平的崇高和正义的斗争中获得新的成就。
    接着，印度共和国总理尼赫鲁以印地语发表演说。
    接着，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁讲话。
    尼赫鲁和布尔加宁的讲话受到了到会者的热烈欢迎，一再被暴风雨般经久不息的掌声所打断。
