Title: China's Deputy Minister of National Defense Xiao Ke and Others Arrive in Prague
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 22nd: General Xiao Ke, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and Wang Zhao, Deputy Minister of Public Security, who were invited by the Czechoslovak government to attend the first Czechoslovak National Games, arrived in Prague by plane on the 21st.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: General Bo. Lomskey, First Deputy Minister of National Defense of Czechoslovakia, Deputy Ministers of Interior Ka. Kostal, Ke. Klima, and Le. Hlaváček, Director of the Asian Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Klička, and other senior officers of the Czechoslovak Army.

Also present to welcome them were Cao Ying, China's ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and Colonel Zhou Yongguang, military attaché.

General Xiao Ke, accompanied by General Lomskey, reviewed the guard of honor.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 22nd: The Chinese sports delegation, invited to participate in the first Czechoslovak National Games, led by Cai Shufan and Huang Qixiang, Deputy Directors of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Prague by plane on the 21st.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vá. Pleskot, Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Sports Commission, senior officials of the sports meeting, Klička, Director of the Asian Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Cao Ying, China's ambassador to Czechoslovakia.

The Chinese gymnastics team, composed of 159 people, also arrived in Prague by train.

### 我国国防部副部长萧克等到达布拉格

1955-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我国国防部副部长萧克等到达布拉格
    我国体育代表团和我国体操队也已到达
    新华社布拉格二十二日电　应捷克斯洛伐克政府的邀请参加捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的中华人民共和国国防部副部长萧克将军、公安部副部长王昭等，二十一日乘飞机到达布拉格。
    前往机场欢迎的有：捷克斯洛伐克国防部第一副部长波·洛姆斯基将军，内务部副部长卡·科斯塔尔、克·克里马和勒·赫拉瓦茨卡，捷克斯洛伐克外交部亚洲司司长克利奇卡，以及捷克斯洛伐克陆军的其他高级军官。
    前往欢迎的还有我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛和武官周永光上校。
    萧克将军在洛姆斯基将军的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    新华社布拉格二十二日电　应邀参加捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会的中国体育代表团，由中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩和黄琪翔率领，于二十一日乘飞机到达布拉格。
    前往机场欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克国家体育运动委员会主席瓦·普勒斯科特、运动大会的高级负责人，捷克斯洛伐克外交部亚洲司司长克利奇卡，和我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛。
    由一百五十九人组成的中国体操队也乘火车抵达布拉格。
