Title: Wu Nu Talks About Sino-Burmese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 31st: Burmese Prime Minister Wu Nu, on his way to visit Israel, Yugoslavia, and other countries on May 28th, told reporters at the Calcutta airport in India that the trade agreement concluded between China and Burma had greatly alleviated his country's economic crisis. He said that this crisis was caused by a large surplus of rice that was difficult to handle.

### 吴努谈中缅贸易协定

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努谈中缅贸易协定
    新华社新德里三十一日电　缅甸总理吴努五月二十八日在前往以色列和南斯拉夫等国访问途中，在印度加尔各答机场对记者们说，中缅两国缔结的贸易协定大大地缓和了他的国家的经济危机。他说，这种危机是由于难以处理的大量剩余大米所造成的。
