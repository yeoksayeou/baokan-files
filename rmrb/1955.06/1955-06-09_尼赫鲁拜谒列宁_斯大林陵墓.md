Title: Nehru Visits Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 8th: According to TASS, Indian Prime Minister Nehru visited the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum on the 8th and laid a wreath.

Subsequently, Prime Minister Nehru also visited the Kremlin. He was warmly welcomed by tourists who were visiting the Kremlin at the time.

### 尼赫鲁拜谒列宁—斯大林陵墓

1955-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁拜谒列宁—斯大林陵墓
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁八日拜谒了列宁—斯大林陵墓，并且献了花圈。
    随后，尼赫鲁总理又参观了克里姆林宫。他受到了当时在克里姆林宫参观的游人的热烈欢迎。
