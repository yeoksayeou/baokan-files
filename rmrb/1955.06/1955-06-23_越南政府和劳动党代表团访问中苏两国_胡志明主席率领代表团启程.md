Title: Vietnamese Government and Labor Party Delegation Visits China and the Soviet Union; Chairman Ho Chi Minh Leads the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 22nd: In response to invitations from the Soviet and Chinese governments, a delegation from the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnam Labor Party, led by Chairman Ho Chi Minh, departed from Hanoi at 5:00 AM on the 22nd for visits to China and the Soviet Union.

The delegation members include: Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Labor Party; Nguyen Duy Trinh, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party; Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Nghiem Xuan Yem, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry; Le Van Hien, Minister of finance; Nguyen Van Huyen, Minister of Education; Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Health.

Luo Guibo, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, accompanied Chairman Ho Chi Minh to Beijing.

Those who came to see them off included: Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong; Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army; other ministers and deputy ministers; Hoang Quoc Viet, member of the Politburo of the Vietnam Labor Party and members of the Central Committee; leaders of various democratic parties and mass organizations; and Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee, and others.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union and other countries to Vietnam, as well as the Indian Consul General in Hanoi, also came to see them off.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh delivered the following speech before leaving Hanoi:

Dear compatriots:

The Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China are our two brotherly countries.

Today, at the invitation of the governments of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the People's Republic of China, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnam Labor Party have dispatched a delegation to visit the Soviet Union and China.

I am very happy to be leading this delegation. And I am deeply convinced that this visit will further strengthen the fraternal solidarity between the Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with the people and governments of these two brotherly countries.

### 越南政府和劳动党代表团访问中苏两国  胡志明主席率领代表团启程

1955-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    越南政府和劳动党代表团访问中苏两国
    胡志明主席率领代表团启程
    新华社河内二十二日电　应苏联政府和中国政府的邀请，由胡志明主席率领的越南民主共和国政府和越南劳动党代表团，已经在二十二日晨五时从河内启程，前往中国和苏联访问。
    代表团团员有：越南劳动党总书记长征，越南劳动党中央委员会委员阮维桢，越南民主共和国工商部长潘英，农林部长严春庵，财政部长黎文献，教育部长阮文萱，外交部副部长翁文谦，卫生部副部长范玉石。
    陪同胡志明主席前往北京的有中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波。
    前往欢送的有：越南民主共和国国民大会代理主席孙德胜，副总理范文同，国防部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲，其他部长和副部长，越南劳动党政治局委员黄国越和中央委员会的委员们，各民主党派和群众团体的负责人，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴等。
    苏联和各国驻越南的外交使节和外交官员、印度驻河内总领事也前往欢送。
    胡志明主席在离开河内前发表讲话如下：
    亲爱的同胞们：
    苏联和中华人民共和国是我们的两个兄弟国家。
    今天，应苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府和中华人民共和国政府的邀请，越南民主共和国政府和越南劳动党派遣了一个代表团前往苏联和中国访问。
    我由于率领这个代表团而感到非常高兴。我并且深信，这一次的访问将会进一步地密切越南人民和越南民主共和国政府同这两个兄弟国家的人民和政府的兄弟般的团结。
