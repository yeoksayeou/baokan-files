Title: Dong Shouyi, Rong Gaotang, and Others Arrive in Paris to Attend International Olympic Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 10th: A Paris dispatch reports that Dong Shouyi, member of the International Olympic Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Olympic Committee, who is attending the 50th session of the International Olympic Committee in Paris, and Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman and Secretary-General of the Chinese Olympic Committee, and member Zhang Lianhua, who are participating in the joint meeting between the Executive Committee of the International Olympic Committee and representatives of the National Olympic Committees, arrived in Paris on the 9th.

### 董守义和荣高棠等抵巴黎  参加国际奥林匹克委员会会议

1955-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    董守义和荣高棠等抵巴黎
    参加国际奥林匹克委员会会议
    新华社布拉格十日电　巴黎消息：出席在巴黎举行的第五十届国际奥林匹克委员会会议的国际奥林匹克委员会委员、中国奥林匹克委员会副主席董守义，以及参加国际奥林匹克委员会执行委员会和各国奥林匹克委员会代表联席会议的中国奥林匹克委员会副主席兼秘书长荣高棠、委员张联华，已在九日抵达巴黎。
