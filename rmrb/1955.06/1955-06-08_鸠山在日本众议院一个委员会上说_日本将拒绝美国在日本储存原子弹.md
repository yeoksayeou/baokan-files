Title: Hatoyama Says in a Committee of the Japanese House of Representatives that Japan will refuse the United States to store atomic bombs in Japan
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th. Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama said on June 6th in response to a question at the House of Representatives Budget Committee that if the United States asks Japan to agree to store atomic bombs in Japan, the Japanese government will refuse this request.

### 鸠山在日本众议院一个委员会上说  日本将拒绝美国在日本储存原子弹

1955-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山在日本众议院一个委员会上说
    日本将拒绝美国在日本储存原子弹
    新华社七日讯　东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎六日在众议院预算委员会上答复质询时说：如果美国要求日本同意它储存原子弹的话，日本政府将拒绝这种要求。
