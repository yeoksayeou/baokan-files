Author:
Date: 1955-06-09

    Our Great Motherland
    Jia Jiqing's team at the mechanized supply station of Anshan Iron and Steel Construction Company is working hard to improve the utilization rate of equipment. Last year, their single-bucket excavator excavated more than 100,000 cubic meters of earth. The team members often discuss how to further increase the amount of earth excavated, striving to accelerate the construction of the steel capital of the motherland.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1955-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山钢铁建设公司机械化供应站贾吉庆小组努力提高设备的利用率。去年他们驾驶的单斗挖土机就挖土十万多立方公尺。组员们经常讨论怎样进一步提高挖土量，为加速祖国钢都的建设而努力。
