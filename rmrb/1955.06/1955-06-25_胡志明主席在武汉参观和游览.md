Title: President Ho Chi Minh Visits and Tours Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, June 24th. President Ho Chi Minh and members of the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today visited the construction site of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and toured the East Lake scenic area in Wuhan.

At 9:00 AM, President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members took the special ship "Yan'an" to the Yangtze River Bridge construction area to visit this huge bridge project over the Yangtze River. At that time, the workers on the construction site were working in the rain. During the visit, Peng Min, Director of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau, introduced the progress of the bridge project to President Ho Chi Minh and other members of the delegation.

In the afternoon, President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members took a car to East Lake for sightseeing. The weather was fine at this time, and the scenery of East Lake was picturesque. President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members climbed "Xingyin Pavilion" to look out over the lake and mountain scenery.

It happened to be the Dragon Boat Festival today. The delegation members specially visited the "Qu Yuan Memorial Hall" in East Lake.

Liu Zihou, Governor of Hubei Province, and Li Erzhong, Third Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit and tour today.

Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also accompanied the visit and tour.

### 胡志明主席在武汉参观和游览

1955-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席在武汉参观和游览
    新华社武汉二十四日电　胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团人员，今天在武汉参观了武汉长江大桥工地，游览了东湖风景区。
    上午九时，胡志明主席和代表团人员乘“延安”号专轮到了长江大桥施工区，参观了长江上的这一巨大的桥梁工程，这时工地上的工人正冒雨施工。在参观中，武汉长江大桥工程局局长彭敏向胡志明主席和其他团员介绍了大桥工程的进行情况。
    下午，胡志明主席和代表团人员乘车赴东湖游览。这时天气方晴，东湖景色美丽如画。胡志明主席和代表团人员登“行吟阁”，远眺湖山景色。
    恰巧今天是端阳佳节。代表团人员在东湖特别参观了“屈原纪念馆”。
    今天陪同贵宾们参观和游览的，有湖北省省长刘子厚、中共武汉市委员会第三书记李尔重。
    陪同参观和游览的还有中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、我国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波、外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
