Title: Czechoslovakia's First National Games Open
Author:
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 4

    Czechoslovakia
    First National Games Open
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 24th: The first Czechoslovak National Games opened on June 23rd at the Strahov Stadium in Prague. Representatives of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the government, headed by President Zapotocky, working people from all over Czechoslovakia, foreign athletes, and guests from the Soviet Union, China, other People's Democratic countries, and countries such as Britain, India, and Indonesia attended the opening ceremony of the Games.
    Many performances were presented on the opening day of the Games, and they were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    According to information released by the Czechoslovak State Committee for Physical Education and Sports, these Games are the final part of the Czechoslovak people's activities to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army (May 9th). The Games will be held from June 23rd to July 5th. The Games are divided into youth and adult days. A total of 300,000 young and adult athletes will participate in 28 group performances. Various competitions will also be held, including ball games, skill sports, track and field, and water sports. In addition, during the Games, the best art troupes of Czechoslovakia will also hold a folk art performance in the theater.

### 捷克斯洛伐克  首届全国运动会开幕

1955-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克
    首届全国运动会开幕
    新华社布拉格二十四日电　捷克斯洛伐克第一届全国运动大会在六月二十三日在布拉格的斯特拉霍夫运动场开幕。参加运动大会开幕仪式的有以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的代表，捷克斯洛伐克全国各地的劳动人民，外国运动员和来自苏联、中国和其他人民民主国家以及英国、印度、印度尼西亚等国的客人。
    运动大会开幕这一天，表演了许多节目，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    根据捷克斯洛伐克国家体育运动委员会发表的消息，这次运动大会是捷克斯洛伐克人民庆祝苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克十周年（五月九日）的活动的最后部分。运动大会从六月二十三日举行到七月五日。运动会分成青年少年日和成年日两部分。总计将有三十万少年和成年运动员参加二十八种集体表演，同时将举行各种球类、机巧运动、田径运动、水上运动等各项比赛，此外，在运动会期间，捷克斯洛伐克最优秀的艺术歌舞团还将在剧院举行民间艺术会演。
