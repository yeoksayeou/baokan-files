Title: Volunteer Army Units Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of the Korean War Against Aggression
Author:
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 26th. Chinese People's Volunteer Army units commemorated the fifth anniversary of the "June 25th" Korean People's War Against Aggression.

    On the 25th, Volunteer Army units generally held small-scale report meetings and symposiums. The soldiers of the Second Company of a certain Volunteer Army unit stationed in the Daede Mountain area unanimously stated at a symposium that they would guard their positions more vigilantly, improve their skills, and continue to strive to maintain the implementation of the Korean Armistice Agreement and defend peace in the Far East and the world.
    On the evening of "June 25th," the leading organs of the Volunteer Army stationed on the West Coast and the Volunteer Army engineering units helping the Korean people with construction in Pyongyang held commemorative evening parties and invited the Korean people near the station to watch films such as "Fighting Friendship" and "Resist America, Aid Korea."

### 志愿军各部队纪念朝鲜反侵略战争五周年

1955-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军各部队纪念朝鲜反侵略战争五周年
    据新华社平壤二十六日电  中国人民志愿军各部队纪念“六·二五”朝鲜人民反侵略战争五周年。
    二十五日志愿军各部队普遍举行了小型报告会和座谈会。守卫在大德山地区的志愿军某部第二连的战士们在座谈会上一致表示，要更加警惕地守卫阵地，提高本领，继续为维护朝鲜停战协定的实施、保卫远东和世界和平而奋斗。
      “六·二五”晚间，志愿军驻西海岸部队的领导机关和帮助朝鲜人民在平壤市进行建设的志愿军工程兵部队都举行了纪念晚会，招待驻地附近的朝鲜人民观看了“战斗的友谊”、“抗美援朝”等影片。
