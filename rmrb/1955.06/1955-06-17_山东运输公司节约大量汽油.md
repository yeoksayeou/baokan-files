Title: Shandong Transportation Company Saves a Large Amount of Gasoline
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

    Shandong Transportation Company Saves a Large Amount of Gasoline
    The units under the state-owned Shandong Provincial Transportation Company saved a total of 36,042 liters of gasoline and diesel for the state in May.
    In the first quarter of this year, the Shandong Provincial Transportation Company experienced significant waste in the use of gasoline and diesel. In mid-April, the company convened a meeting of the heads of motor vehicle teams from various branches to formulate measures to save gasoline and diesel. After the meeting, all units attached great importance to saving gasoline and diesel and actively implemented relevant conservation measures. For example, 61% of the vehicles in the Qingdao Transportation Branch improved their air fuel savers, so that no gasoline remained inside the fuel savers. According to statistics for 20 days in May, this saved 4,007 liters of gasoline. Currently, more than 28% of the vehicles in Shandong Province have been equipped with air fuel savers.

### 山东运输公司节约大量汽油

1955-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    山东运输公司节约大量汽油
    国营山东省运输公司所属各单位五月份为国家节约了汽油、柴油共三万六千零四十二公升。
    山东省运输公司今年第一季度在汽油和柴油的使用上有很大浪费。四月中旬，这个公司召开了各分公司汽车队队长会议，拟订了节约汽油、柴油的办法。会后，各单位对节约汽油和柴油都很重视，并积极贯彻有关节约的措施。如青岛运输分公司百分之六十一的车辆改进了空气节油器，使节油器内部不再存汽油，据五月份二十天的统计，即节约汽油四千零七公升。目前山东全省已有百分之二十八以上的车辆安上了空气节油器。     （新华社）
