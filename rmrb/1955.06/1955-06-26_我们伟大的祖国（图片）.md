    Our Great Motherland
    This year, 95,000 university students and 129,000 secondary specialized school students across the country are participating in production internships. This year's graduates of the Hanzhong Agricultural School in Shaanxi are undertaking a production internship at the Baocheng May First Agricultural Production Cooperative. The cooperative members are explaining methods of seed selection in the fields to them.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国今年有九万五千名高等学校学生和十二万九千名中等专业学校学生参加生产实习。陕西汉中农业专科学校本届毕业生在褒城五一农业生产合作社进行生产实习，社员给他们讲解田间选种的方法。
