Title: Ambassador Solman of Sweden, Head of the Diplomatic Corps Composed of Envoys from Various Countries to the Soviet Union, Congratulates Chairman Bulganin on His Birthday
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th: According to TASS, Mr. Solman, the Swedish Ambassador to the Soviet Union and the head of the diplomatic corps composed of envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, paid a visit to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, on the 11th, and on behalf of the members of the diplomatic corps in the Soviet Union and himself, congratulated Bulganin on his sixtieth birthday, wishing him health and further achievements in his national activities. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, expressed his gratitude to Solman and the members of the diplomatic corps in the Soviet Union for their congratulations.

### 由各国驻苏使节组成的外交团领袖大使  瑞典驻苏大使索尔曼向布尔加宁主席祝寿

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    由各国驻苏使节组成的外交团领袖大使
    瑞典驻苏大使索尔曼向布尔加宁主席祝寿
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：由各国驻苏使节组成的外交团的领袖大使、瑞典驻苏联大使索尔曼先生，十一日拜会苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，并代表驻苏外交团的成员和他个人祝贺布尔加宁六十寿辰，祝他健康和在他的国家活动中获得进一步的成就。苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁表示感谢索尔曼和驻苏外交团的成员对他的祝贺。
