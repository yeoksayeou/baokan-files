Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Leaves Beijing to Visit East and South China Before Returning to Indonesia
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife, who came to China at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane at 8:00 am on the 3rd to visit East China, South China, and other places before returning to their country. Mono Noto, Sukarjo Wirjopranoto and his wife, Bala, Subandrio, Juanda, Hasuno Reksoamojo, and others who accompanied Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China also flew away from Beijing on the same day. Huang Zhen, China's ambassador to Indonesia, and his wife, Dong Yueqian, director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, deputy director of the Protocol Department, accompanied Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Dong Biwu, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League Ma Xulun, Deputy Director of the China Democratic National Construction Association Zhang Naiqi, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Chief of General Staff Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China Zhang Wentian, and Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Zhang Yun.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Ministers Luo Ruiqing, Ye Jizhuang, Chen Yu, Jia Tuofu, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Fu Zuoyi, Ma Wenrui, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Li Dequan, Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other deputy ministers of various ministries and deputy directors of various committees Wang Ziyi, Yang Qiqing, Zhang Xi, Wang Shitai, Kong Yuan, Lü Dong, Cao Xiangren, Qian Zhiguang, Li Yunchang, Li Fanwu, Qian Junrui, Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Tingkai, Liu Chun, Fang Fang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison Hong Shen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ke Hua, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu and Feng Jiping.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tan Zheng, Commander of the Air Force Liu Yalou, Deputy Commander of the Navy Wang Hongkun, and Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Yang Chengwu.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Secretary-General of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Huang Qixiang, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party Chen Qiyou, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Jiu San Society Tu Changwang, Vice Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Li Chunqing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Liu Daosheng, and Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Ren.

Also at the airport to see them off were: President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Bao Erhan, Vice Presidents Hu Yuzhi and Ma Yuhuai, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Wu Han, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Tian Demin, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Yang Hansheng, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Meng Yongqian, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Deputy Directors of the China Islamic Association Yang Jingren and Da Pusheng, and Deputy Director of the China Hui Muslim Cultural Advancement Association Ma Songting.

Personnel from the Indonesian Embassy in China and Indonesian overseas Chinese also went to the airport to see them off.

The foreign envoys and diplomatic officials who went to the airport to see them off included: Burmese Ambassador Wu La Mao, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Indian Ambassador Raghvan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Romanian Ambassador Kovacs, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Finnish Ambassador Sundström, Czechoslovak Ambassador Gregor, Pakistani Ambassador Ahmed, Danish Minister Gregerse, Swiss Minister Beni, Dutch Chargé d'affaires Sman, Norwegian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Gjemmuden, Korean Democratic People's Republic Chargé d'affaires ad interim Kim Gui-nam, German Democratic Republic Chargé d'affaires ad interim Grutner, Yugoslav Chargé d'affaires Milicevic, Hungarian Chargé d'affaires Medgyes, Counselor of the British Mission Addis, and Secretary of the Swedish Embassy Hammarskjöld.

The flags of the Republic of Indonesia and China were flying at the airport.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, entered the airport. At this time, the band played the national anthems of the Republic of Indonesia and China.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo reviewed the guard of honor and accepted flowers presented by children of the capital. Then, amidst the farewell music, he boarded the plane and waved goodbye to the crowd. (Attached picture)

The picture shows children in Beijing presenting flowers to Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo at the airport as a farewell. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理离京  将在华东华南等地参观后回印度尼西亚

1955-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理离京
    将在华东华南等地参观后回印度尼西亚
    新华社三日讯　应中华人民共和国政府邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人在三日上午八时乘飞机离开北京，将在华东、华南等地参观后返国。随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理来华访问的莫诺努图、苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和夫人、巴拉、苏班德里奥、朱安达、哈苏诺·雷克梭阿莫佐等同日飞离北京。陪送阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理的有我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人、外交部办公厅主任董越千、礼宾司副司长王倬如。
