Title: China's Yue Opera Troupe to Visit Germany for Performances
Author:
Date: 1955-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe left Beijing by train at 12:00 noon on the 19th to visit the German Democratic Republic. Xu Guangping, Standing Committee Member of the National People's Congress, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and well-known writer, serves as the head of the troupe. Members include Yuan Xuefen, representative of the National People's Congress and famous actress, as well as famous actors Fan Ruijuan, Fu Quanxiang, and Xu Yulan. The troupe is traveling to the German Democratic Republic for a tour of performances in various locations, according to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and will perform plays such as "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" and "Romance of the West Chamber."

### 我国越剧团去德国作访问演出

1955-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我国越剧团去德国作访问演出
    新华社十九日讯　中国越剧团已在十九日中午十二时乘火车离北京赴德意志民主共和国。全国人民代表大会常务委员、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席、著名作家许广平担任该团团长，团员有全国人民代表大会代表、名演员袁雪芬，名演员范瑞娟、傅全香和徐玉兰等。该团是根据中德文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划到德意志民主共和国各地作巡回演出的，将要表演的有“梁山伯与祝英台”及“西厢记”等剧目。
