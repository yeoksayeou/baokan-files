Title: Indian Cultural Delegation to Visit and Perform in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 17th. Led by Chanda, the Indian Cultural Delegation, accompanied by Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Art Administration of the Ministry of Culture, and Gao Bodeng, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, left by special train at 11:50 pm on the 17th to visit and perform in various parts of Northeast China.

The Central Song and Dance Ensemble, the Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Dance School, and the Inner Mongolia Song and Dance Troupe have all sent personnel to accompany the delegation to learn from the Indian artists in various places.

When the delegation left Beijing, Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, Zhang Hanfu, Feng Jiping, and people from the cultural and artistic circles of the capital, as well as personnel from the Indian Embassy in China, went to the station to see them off.

### 印度文化代表团去东北各地作访问演出

1955-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团去东北各地作访问演出
    新华社十七日讯　以钱达为首的印度文化代表团，由文化部艺术事业管理局副局长周巍峙和印度驻华大使馆参赞高伯登陪同，在十七日晚十一时五十分乘专车到东北各地作访问演出。
    中央歌舞团、中国人民解放军总政治部文工团、北京舞蹈学校和内蒙古歌舞团，都派人随代表团到各地向印度艺术家们学习。
    代表团离开北京时，丁西林、夏衍、章汉夫、冯基平和首都文艺界人士等，以及印度驻华大使馆人员都到车站相送。
