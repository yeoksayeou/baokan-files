Title: Khrushchev, Bulganin, etc. Arrive in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest dispatch from Romanian News Agency: The members of the Soviet government delegation who participated in the Belgrade talks between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia arrived in Bucharest on the 4th. The people of Bucharest greeted with warm friendship Khrushchev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁等到达布加勒斯特

1955-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁等到达布加勒斯特
    新华社四日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：参加贝尔格莱德苏联和南斯拉夫会谈的苏联政府代表团人员四日抵达布加勒斯特。布加勒斯特人民以热烈的友情接待苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
