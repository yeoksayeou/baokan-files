Title: Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Leaves Beijing to Return to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th. After accompanying the Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, on visits to Nanjing and Shanghai, the Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Subandrio, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 7th and left Beijing by plane on the 8th to return to Moscow.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department, Vio Wijojo, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and other embassy staff.

### 印度尼西亚驻苏大使离北京返莫斯科

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚驻苏大使离北京返莫斯科
    新华社八日讯　印度尼西亚驻苏联大使苏班德里奥在随同印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约访问了南京、上海后，在七日下午回到北京，八日乘飞机离京返莫斯科。
    前往机场送行的，有外交部部长助理陈家康、礼宾司司长柯华，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办维约维尔多约和其它馆员。
