Title: German Scientists Oberdorf and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: German Democratic Republic agricultural scientists Professor Friedrich Oberdorf and Professor Rudolf Hick, who are visiting China according to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th. They will be in China for a professional visit of four weeks.

### 德国科学家奥伯多夫等到京

1955-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德国科学家奥伯多夫等到京
    据新华社讯　根据中德文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国农业科学家弗雷德里希·奥伯多夫教授和鲁道夫·希克教授，已在十五日乘飞机到达北京。他们将在我国作为期四周的专业性访问。
