Title: Shandong Wheat Harvest (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-19
Page: 5

    Shandong Wheat Harvest

    Workers at the Shandong State-owned Tannan Farm are using combine harvesters to harvest wheat. Farmers from the surrounding villages have come to observe.
  *Dazhong Daily*, Photo by Zheng Hua

    Zhao Lanting (right), female production team leader of the Liaoyuan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yaozhuang Township, Sixth District, Tai'an County, is leading female members in selecting seeds in the field.
  *Dazhong Daily*, Photo by Tang Yongping

    The wheat of the Liaoyuan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yaozhuang Township, Sixth District, Tai'an County, is growing very plump, and the cooperative members are filled with joy at the harvest.
  *Dazhong Daily*, Photo by Tang Yongping

    The transport team of the State-owned Tannan Farm is transporting wheat to the drying ground in order to turn it over to the state as soon as possible.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　*Dazhong Daily*, Photo by Zheng Hua

    Eight hundred mu of wheat at the State-owned Laiwu Farm has ripened, and the workers are working hard to harvest and transport it.
  *Dazhong Daily*, Photo by Tang Yongping

    The members of the Second Production Team of the Liaoyuan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yaozhuang Township, Sixth District, Tai'an County, are harvesting and cultivating at the same time.
　　　　　　　　*Dazhong Daily*, Photo by Tang Yongping

### 山东小麦丰收（图片）

1955-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    山东小麦丰收
    山东省国营郯南农场的工人正用联合收割机收割小麦。四乡的农民都赶来参观。
  大众日报　郑华摄
    山东省泰安县六区姚庄乡燎原农业生产合作社女生产队长赵兰亭（右）领导女社员在田间选种。
  大众日报　唐永平摄
    泰安县六区姚庄乡燎原农业生产合作社的小麦长得很饱满，社员们感到丰收的喜悦。　大众日报　唐永平摄
    国营郯南农场的运输队把小麦运到场里晒干，以便及早缴给国家。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　大众日报　郑华摄
    国营莱芜农场八百亩小麦已经成熟了，工人们正在紧张地边收边运。    大众日报　唐永平摄
    泰安县六区姚庄乡燎原农业生产合作社第二生产队的社员们正在一边收割，一边耕耘。　　　　　　　　大众日报　唐永平摄
