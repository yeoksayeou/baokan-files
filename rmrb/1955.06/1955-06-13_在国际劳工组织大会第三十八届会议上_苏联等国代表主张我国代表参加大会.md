Title: At the 38th Session of the International Labour Organisation Conference, Representatives from the Soviet Union and Other Countries Advocated for the Participation of Representatives from Our Country in the Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 4
	Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 11th: The 38th session of the International Labour Organisation Conference discussed the report of the Credentials Committee on the 10th. The report of the Credentials Committee stated that the delegations of the Soviet Union and Poland, among other countries, protested against the participation of Kuomintang elements in the International Labour Organisation. However, the committee rejected this protest, arguing that the issue of the People's Republic of China's representation in international specialized agencies, including the International Labour Organisation, "concerns the United Nations' overall decision on this issue" and that "no new developments have occurred on this issue since last year."
	During the discussion of the report, Ursak, the Norwegian government representative, stated that if the United Nations continues to delay resolving the issue of China's representation in international organizations, then the International Labour Organisation should make an independent decision next year.
	The Soviet government representative, Arutiunian, reminded the meeting that new factors have emerged now that the issue of China's representation in the International Labour Organisation is being discussed. Representatives of the People's Republic of China participated in the Asian-African Conference and the Geneva Conference. However, regrettably, the Credentials Committee did not consider these new factors. Therefore, the Soviet delegation believes that the Credentials Committee's decision is incorrect. The Soviet delegation firmly opposes the participation of the Kuomintang clique's representatives in the International Labour Organisation Conference and believes that the only legitimate representatives of China can be the representatives of the government of the People's Republic of China.
	Representatives from Poland, Czechoslovakia, Albania, and other countries also spoke out against the participation of Kuomintang elements in the International Labour Organisation.
	According to the rules of procedure, the report of the Credentials Committee was not put to a vote but was left by the meeting for reference.

### 在国际劳工组织大会第三十八届会议上  苏联等国代表主张我国代表参加大会

1955-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    在国际劳工组织大会第三十八届会议上
    苏联等国代表主张我国代表参加大会
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦十一日讯：国际劳工组织大会第三十八届会议十日讨论了资格审查委员会的报告。资格审查委员会的报告说，苏联和波兰等若干国家的代表团抗议让国民党分子参加国际劳工组织。但是，委员会拒绝了这种抗议，拒绝的借口是，关于中华人民共和国在包括国际劳工组织在内的国际专门机构里的代表权问题“关涉到联合国关于这个问题的总的决定”，并且“在这个问题上，从去年以来没有发生什么新的情况”。
    在讨论报告时，挪威政府代表乌尔萨克发言指出，如果联合国将来继续拖延关于中国在国际组织中的代表权问题的解决，那末国际劳工组织在明年应当采取独立的决定。
    苏联政府代表阿鲁纠尼安提醒会议注意：现在当讨论关于中国在国际劳工组织中的代表权问题时，已经发生了新因素。中华人民共和国政府代表参加了亚非会议和日内瓦会议。但是，遗憾的是，资格审查委员会没有考虑到这些新因素，因此，苏联代表团认为，资格审查委员会的决定是不正确的。苏联代表团坚决反对让国民党集团代表参加国际劳工组织会议，并认为，唯一合法的中国代表只能是中华人民共和国政府的代表。
    波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚等国的代表也发言反对让国民党分子参加国际劳工组织。
    根据议事规则，资格审查委员会的报告没有提付表决，而由会议留作参考。
