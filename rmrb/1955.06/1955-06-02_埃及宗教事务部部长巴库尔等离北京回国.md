Title: Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakour and Others Depart Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakour and Cairo University Constitution Professor Kamel, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by special plane on the morning of the 1st to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, Wu Kejian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chang Lifu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the National Ethnic Affairs Commission, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, Ma Yu Huai, Da Putsheng, Zhang Jie, Secretary General, Ma Songting, Deputy Director of the Chinese Hui Culture Promotion Association, Ding Yimin, Deputy Secretary-General, and religious figures from the capital Yang Mingyuan, An Shiwei, educators Li Shu, Su Keqin, Ma Jian, Pang Shiqian, and others.

### 埃及宗教事务部部长巴库尔等离北京回国

1955-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　埃及宗教事务部部长巴库尔等离北京回国
    新华社一日讯　应邀来我国访问的埃及宗教事务部部长巴库尔和开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔，在一日晨乘专机离开北京返国。
    到机场欢送的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长吴克坚，国务院副秘书长常黎夫，民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华、礼宾司副司长王倬如，北京市副市长吴晗，中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉、副主任马玉槐、达浦生、秘书长张杰，中国回民文化协进会副主任马松亭、副秘书长丁毅民，以及首都宗教界人士杨明远、安士伟，教育界人士李恕、苏克勤、马坚、庞士谦等人。
