Title: U Nu Arrives in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in Belgrade by plane on the afternoon of the 6th for a ten-day official visit at the invitation of Yugoslav President Tito.
    Welcoming Prime Minister U Nu at the airport were President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, President of the National Assembly Pijade, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council Kardelj and Ranković, Secretary General of Foreign Affairs Popović, Secretary General of National Defense Ivan Gošnjak, as well as other senior Yugoslav officials, military representatives, and cultural representatives.
    Prime Minister U Nu made a brief speech at the airport. He emphasized the friendship between Yugoslavia and Burma and expressed his hope to further enhance the economic and cultural ties between the two countries.
    Prime Minister U Nu and President Tito rode in an open car from the airport to the Jecinier Palace, where thousands of residents lined the streets to welcome them.

### 吴努到达南斯拉夫

1955-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努到达南斯拉夫
    新华社七日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社消息：缅甸总理吴努应南斯拉夫总统铁托的邀请，已经在六日下午乘飞机到达贝尔格莱德作为期十天的正式访问。
    在机场欢迎吴努总理的，有南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托、国民议会议长皮雅杰、联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔和兰科维奇、外交秘书长波波维奇、国防秘书长伊凡·戈斯尼亚克以及南斯拉夫其他高级官员、军界代表和文化界代表等。
    吴努总理在机场上发表了简短的谈话。他着重指出了南斯拉夫同缅甸的友谊，并且表示希望进一步增进两国在经济和文化方面的联系。
    吴努总理和铁托总统同乘敞篷汽车从机场到捷津尼埃宫，沿途成千上万的居民夹道欢迎。
