Title: Japanese Writer Yoshio Toyoshima Dies
Author:
Date: 1955-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 18th: Renowned Japanese writer and member of the Japan Art Academy, Yoshio Toyoshima, passed away in Tokyo on the 18th due to heart disease at the age of 65.

Toyoshima Yoshio was enthusiastic about Japan-China friendship and the peace movement during his lifetime, and served successively as Vice President and Advisor of the Japan-China Friendship Association.

### 日本文学家丰岛与志雄病逝

1955-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本文学家丰岛与志雄病逝
    新华社十八日讯　东京消息：日本著名文学家、日本艺术院会员丰岛与志雄十八日因心脏病在东京逝世，享年六十五岁。
    丰岛与志雄生前热心于日中友好及和平运动，曾先后担任日中友好协会副会长、顾问。
