Title: Indian Cultural Delegation to Visit China Soon; Sino-Indian Friendship Association and Chinese Ambassador to India Host Farewell Banquets
Author:
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 1st: According to Indian News Agency, the Indian Cultural Delegation, led by Indian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Anil Chanda, will leave Delhi for China on June 4th for a visit.

The delegation includes a small music and dance troupe consisting of forty-seven artists. They will perform classical and folk music and dances of India.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 3rd: The Sino-Indian Friendship Association held a reception in New Delhi on the afternoon of the 2nd to bid farewell to the Indian Cultural Delegation, which is about to depart for a visit to China.

In addition to the Indian Cultural Delegation, about two hundred people attended the reception, including Indian Defense Minister Katju and prominent figures from all walks of life.

Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian was also invited to attend the reception.

Dr. Gyan Chand, Chairman of the National Council of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, spoke at the reception. He said that the relationship between the people of China and India is becoming increasingly close. He expressed hope that the delegation would not only bring happiness to the Chinese people but also derive great pleasure from learning about Chinese art and culture.

The head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Indian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chanda, former Indian Ambassador to China and Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Panikkar, and Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian all spoke at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 3rd: Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian held a reception on the evening of the 2nd to bid farewell to the Indian Cultural Delegation, which is about to visit China.

The head of the delegation, Chanda, and all members of the delegation attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were Indian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Mahmud and other senior officials, as well as prominent figures from the literary and artistic circles of the Indian capital.

### 印度文化代表团即动身来我国访问  印中友协和我国驻印大使先后设宴欢送

1955-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团即动身来我国访问
    印中友协和我国驻印大使先后设宴欢送
    新华社新德里一日电　据印度新闻处消息：由印度外交部副部长阿尼尔·钱达率领的印度文化代表团将于六月四日自德里前往中国访问。
    代表团包括由四十七名艺术工作者组成的小型音乐舞蹈团。他们将表演印度古典的和民间的音乐舞蹈。
    据新华社新德里三日电　印中友好协会二日下午在新德里举行招待会，欢送即将动身到中国访问的印度文化代表团。
    出席招待会的除印度文化代表团以外，还有印度国防部长卡特朱和各界著名人士约二百人。
    中国驻印度大使袁仲贤也应邀出席了招待会。
    印中友好协会全国理事会主席吉安·昌德博士在招待会上讲了话。他说，印中两国人民之间的关系正越来越密切。他表示希望，代表团将不只使中国人民快乐，而且将从对中国艺术和文化的学习中得到很大的乐趣。
    印度文化代表团团长、印度外交部副部长钱达和前印度驻中国大使、印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席潘尼迦以及中国驻印度大使袁仲贤都在招待会上讲了话。
    新华社新德里三日电　中国驻印度大使袁仲贤二日晚举行招待会，欢送即将前往中国访问的印度文化代表团。
    代表团团长钱达和全体团员都出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有印度外交部副部长马茂德和其他高级官员，以及印度首都文学艺术界著名人士。
