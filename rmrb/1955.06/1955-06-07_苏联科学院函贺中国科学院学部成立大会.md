Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Sends Congratulatory Letter to the Founding Meeting of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Academic Divisions
Author:
Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: At the meeting of the founding of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Academic Divisions on June 6th, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, read the congratulatory letter from the Soviet Academy of Sciences to the meeting.

    The congratulatory letter first extended congratulations to the meeting and to Chinese scientists. The letter pointed out: The victory of the Chinese people has opened up extremely broad paths of development for Chinese scientists. The Chinese Communist Party and the Government of the People's Republic of China attach great importance to the development of the motherland's scientific endeavors, have established the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and regularly provide support. In a very short period of time, the research institutions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences have greatly developed, and in several aspects of research work, significant achievements have been made.

    The congratulatory letter stated: True, advanced science always serves the people and is developed in close connection with the practice of socialist construction. The congratulatory letter stated: The Chinese Academy of Sciences and all Chinese scientists have extremely important tasks before them, namely, to more deeply grasp the true scientific method of dialectical materialism, to more comprehensively understand the laws of natural and social development, so as to help the great Chinese people advance rapidly on the road of building the foundations of socialism and continuously improving the material and cultural living standards of the working people.

    The congratulatory letter stated: We believe that Chinese scientists will spare no effort to complete these great and noble tasks. Chinese scientists will, together with the great Chinese people and all progressive humanity, defend the cause of peace and tirelessly consolidate the friendship between the peoples of all countries.

    The congratulatory letter stated: Soviet scientists are watching China's scientific achievements with deep concern and sincerely congratulate Chinese scientists on further creative achievements in research work for the benefit of the Chinese people.

    The congratulatory letter finally congratulated the cooperation between Chinese and Soviet scientists for a more comprehensive and in-depth development.

    The congratulatory letter was signed by Academician A.N. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Academician A.V. Topchiev, Academic Secretary of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.

### 苏联科学院函贺中国科学院学部成立大会

1955-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院函贺中国科学院学部成立大会
    新华社六日讯　在中国科学院学部成立大会六日的会议上，中国科学院副院长吴有训宣读了苏联科学院给大会的贺信。
    贺信首先向大会和中国科学家们致贺。贺信指出：中国人民的胜利，在中国科学家面前展开了极其广阔的发展道路。中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府十分重视祖国科学事业的发展，成立了中国科学院，并经常给予支持。在很短时间内，中国科学院的研究机构有了很大发展，在研究工作的若干方面，已取得了显著成就。
    贺信说：真正的、先进的科学永远服务于人民，并且是紧密地联系着社会主义建设的实践而发展的。贺信说：在中国科学院和全体中国科学家面前有着极其重要的任务，即更深入地掌握真正的科学的辩证唯物主义的方法，更全面地认识自然和社会发展的规律，从而有助于伟大中国人民在建设社会主义基础和不断提高劳动人民的物质和文化生活水平的道路上迅速前进。
    贺信说：我们相信，中国科学家们会不遗余力地完成这些伟大而崇高的任务。中国科学家们将同伟大的中国人民和全体进步人类一起，捍卫和平事业并不倦地巩固各国人民之间的友谊。
    贺信说：苏联科学家以深切的关怀注视着中国的科学成就，并衷心祝贺中国科学家在为中国人民的利益的研究工作中获得进一步的创造性的成就。
    贺信最后祝贺中苏科学家的合作更全面更深入地发展。
    在贺信上签名的有苏联科学院院长亚·尼·涅斯米扬诺夫院士和苏联科学院主席团学术秘书长亚·乌·托普切夫院士。
