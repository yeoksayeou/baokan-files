The crops planted by the Jiangxi Provincial Agricultural Science Research Institute on more than 1,000 mu of red soil in Henggang, Nanchang County, and other places have yielded good harvests. This shows the abundant ears of rye grown on red soil. Agricultural science research institute technicians are measuring the plant height of the rye.
