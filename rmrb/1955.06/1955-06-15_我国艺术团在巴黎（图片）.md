Author:
Date: 1955-06-15
Page: 4

The Chinese Art Troupe in Paris
The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to participate in the Second International Drama Festival in Paris, arrived in Paris by train on May 31 and was warmly welcomed by the French people. The picture shows Mrs. Gaby Morlay (second from the right), President of the French National Actors' Union, and others welcoming the Chinese Art Troupe at the station. The first from the left in the front row is Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and the second is Du Jinfang, a famous Peking opera actress. Xinhua News Agency photo

The Chinese Art Troupe, participating in the Second International Drama Festival in France, held its first phase of performances in Paris from June 4th to 8th for five days, which aroused great interest from the French cultural and artistic circles and the press, as well as a warm welcome from people from all walks of life.

The actors of the Chinese Art Troupe take a bow to the enthusiastically cheering French audience. Xinhua News Agency photo

### 我国艺术团在巴黎（图片）

1955-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在巴黎
    应邀参加巴黎第二届国际戏剧节演出的中国艺术团，五月三十一日乘车到达巴黎，受到法国人民的热烈欢迎。图为法国全国演员工会主席盖比·摩雷夫人（右第二人）等在车站欢迎中国艺术团的情形。前排左起第一人是中国艺术团团长张致祥，第二人是京剧名演员杜近芳。　　　　　新华社稿
    参加法国第二届国际戏剧节的我国艺术团，已从六月四日到八日在巴黎举行了历时五天的第一期演出，引起了法国文化艺术界和新闻界的巨大兴趣以及各界人士的热烈欢迎。
    我国艺术团的演员们向热烈欢呼的法国观众谢幕。　　　新华社稿
