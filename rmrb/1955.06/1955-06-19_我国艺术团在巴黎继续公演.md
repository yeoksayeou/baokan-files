Title: Chinese Art Troupe Continues Performances in Paris
Author:
Date: 1955-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 18th: The Chinese Art Troupe, participating in the Second Paris International Drama Festival, began its second series of performances at the Théâtre National de Chaillot in Paris on the evening of the 16th, after completing its first series of performances at the Sarah Bernhardt Theatre. The performance on the evening of the 16th was once again warmly welcomed. The Théâtre National Populaire and other organizations presented flowers to the performers.

### 我国艺术团在巴黎继续公演

1955-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在巴黎继续公演
    新华社布拉格十八日电　参加巴黎第二届国际戏剧节的中国艺术团，在结束了在萨拉·伯纳尔剧院的第一期演出后，从十六日晚开始，在巴黎夏乐宫剧院举行第二期公演。十六日晚上的演出再度受到热烈欢迎。法国国家人民剧院等单位都向演员们献花致意。
