Title: Prime Minister Nehru Arrives in Prague and Receives a Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 6th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru arrived in Prague by plane on the morning of the 6th for a visit to the Czechoslovak Republic.

Those welcoming him at the airport included: Czechoslovak Prime Minister Široký, Deputy Prime Ministers Ďuriš, Kopecký, and Jankovcová, ministers of various departments, and representatives of various mass organizations.

Also welcoming him at the airport were: Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Firjubin, Chinese Ambassador Cao Ying, British Ambassador Sir Clarmonde, and diplomatic representatives from other countries.

After Prime Minister Nehru disembarked, he reviewed the guard of honor accompanied by Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of National Defense General Kratochvíl. The bands played the national anthems of the Republic of India and the Czechoslovak Republic.

Prime Minister Široký delivered a speech at the airport. He first extended his most heartfelt welcome to Nehru on behalf of the government and people of the Czechoslovak Republic.

Široký said: The Czechoslovak people have long held a sincere friendship for the Indian people. The Czechoslovak people greatly admire the role played by the Republic of India, its government, and Prime Minister Nehru in the current struggle of peace-loving humanity to avoid the danger of war and to establish and consolidate friendly and peaceful cooperative relations between countries in Asia and throughout the world.

Široký said: Our country is doing everything possible to ease international tensions and develop friendly relations between countries, regardless of differences in social and national systems. I am convinced that your visit will further strengthen the friendly relations between India and Czechoslovakia; I am convinced that it will be a positive contribution to strengthening world peace and friendship among nations.

Next, Prime Minister Nehru also gave a speech. He expressed his great gratitude to the Czechoslovak government and people for their welcome. He said he was very happy to be back in this beautiful city and country after an interval of eighteen years. He went on to say: "I am particularly pleased that, during this short stay, I will have the opportunity to meet with the leaders of your government; I hope that the relations between our two countries can become closer and our ties can grow."

Nehru concluded by saying: "Our country has always been committed to, and is still committed to, adopting peaceful methods in all matters, especially in pursuit of world peace. In this endeavor, we will always be happy to cooperate with all countries."

Accompanied by Prime Minister Široký, Prime Minister Nehru traveled by car from the airport to the city, where he was warmly welcomed by the people of Prague along the way.

### 尼赫鲁总理到达布拉格受到热烈欢迎

1955-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理到达布拉格受到热烈欢迎
    新华社布拉格六日电　印度共和国总理尼赫鲁在六日上午乘飞机抵达布拉格，来访问捷克斯洛伐克共和国。
    到机场欢迎的有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基，副总理杜兰斯基、柯别茨基和扬科夫卓娃，各部部长和各群众团体的代表。
    到机场欢迎的还有：苏联驻捷大使费留宾，中国大使曹瑛，英国大使克莫德爵士和其他国家的外交代表。
    尼赫鲁总理下机后，在捷克斯洛伐克国防部副部长克拉托赫维尔将军陪同下检阅了仪仗队。乐队奏印度共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国国歌。
    西罗基总理在机场上讲了话。他首先代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府和全体人民向尼赫鲁表示最衷心的欢迎。
    西罗基说：捷克斯洛伐克人民老早就对印度人民怀有真诚的友谊。捷克斯洛伐克人民极其钦佩印度共和国、它的政府和尼赫鲁总理在目前爱好和平人类争取避免战争危险、争取建立并巩固亚洲及全世界各国间的友好和和平合作的关系的斗争中所起的作用。
    西罗基说：我们的国家正在尽一切力量来缓和国际紧张局势，不问社会和国家制度的不同，发展各国之间的友好关系。我深信，您的访问将进一步加强印度和捷克斯洛伐克两国之间的友好关系；我深信，它将是对加强世界和平和各国友好的事业的积极贡献。
    接着，尼赫鲁总理也讲了话。他对捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民对他的欢迎表示十分感激。他说他很高兴能在十八年的间隔之后又来到这个美丽的城市和国家。他接着说：“我特别感到高兴的是，在这个短短的逗留期间，我将有机会同贵国政府的领袖们会面；我希望我们两国的关系能够更加紧密，我们的联系能够增长。”
    尼赫鲁最后说：“我们的国家一向致力于，而现在仍然致力于在一切事情上采用和平方法，特别是致力于世界和平。在这个工作中，我们将永远乐于同一切国家合作。”
     尼赫鲁总理在西罗基总理陪同下乘汽车从机场驶往城里，沿途受到布拉格人民的热烈欢迎。
