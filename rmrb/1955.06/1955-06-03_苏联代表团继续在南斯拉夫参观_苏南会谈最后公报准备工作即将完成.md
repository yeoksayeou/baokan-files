Title: Soviet Delegation Continues Visit to Yugoslavia; Preparations for Final Communiqué of Soviet-Yugoslav Talks Nearing Completion
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Zagreb, June 2nd: The members of the Soviet government delegation, Khrushchev, Bulganin, and Mikoyan, along with the members of the Yugoslav government delegation, Kardelj and Ranković, etc., after leaving Brioni Island, continued their travels throughout the country.

    On June 1st, the members of the Soviet delegation arrived in Ljubljana, the capital of the Slovenian People's Republic, where they visited the "Litostroj" factory and talked with the factory's leaders.
    Subsequently, the Soviet government delegation departed Ljubljana for Zagreb, the capital of the Croatian People's Republic, amidst a grand farewell.
    The Speaker of the Parliament of the Croatian People's Republic, Bakarić, hosted a luncheon for the members of the Soviet delegation. The luncheon took place in a warm atmosphere.
    On the evening of June 1st, the Soviet delegation returned to Belgrade.

    The Soviet government delegation and the government delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia continued to exchange views sincerely on issues of concern to both countries. Currently, preparations for the final communiqué of the Soviet-Yugoslav talks are nearing completion.

### 苏联代表团继续在南斯拉夫参观  苏南会谈最后公报准备工作即将完成

1955-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团继续在南斯拉夫参观
    苏南会谈最后公报准备工作即将完成
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社萨格勒布二日讯：苏联政府代表团人员赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁和米高扬同南斯拉夫政府代表团人员卡德尔和兰科维奇等在离开布莱俄尼岛后，继续在全国各地旅行。
    六月一日，苏联代表团人员到达斯洛文尼亚人民共和国首都卢布尔雅那，在那里参观了“里托斯特洛”工厂，并且同工厂的领导人交谈。
    随后，苏联政府代表团在隆重的欢送中离卢布尔雅那前往克罗地亚人民共和国首都萨格勒布。
    克罗地亚人民共和国议会议长巴卡里奇招待苏联代表团人员共进午餐。午餐在热烈的气氛中进行。
    六月一日晚，苏联代表团回贝尔格莱德。
    苏联政府代表团同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府代表团继续诚恳地就两国所关心的问题交换意见。目前，苏南会谈最后公报的准备工作即将完成。
