Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Nanwan Reservoir workers are actively striving to build the dam to a height of 27 meters by the end of June to ensure the safe retention of floodwaters during the flood season and to greet the "July 1st" Party's birthday. In order to speed up the construction progress, all the soil, sand, and stone materials used to build the dam are now transported by small trains and dump trucks.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    南湾水库职工积极争取在六月底把拦河坝筑到二十七公尺高，保证汛期安全拦蓄洪水，来迎接“七一”党的生日。为了加速施工进度，现在修筑拦河坝的全部土、沙、石料，都已采用小火车、斗车来运输。
