Title: Molotov Leaves San Francisco to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, June 27th. TASS, San Francisco: Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Foreign Minister Molotov left San Francisco on the evening of the 26th via New York to return to the Soviet Union. He was accompanied by Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zarubin.
    The Soviet delegation participating in the United Nations commemorative meeting is led by Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Fedrin.

### 莫洛托夫离旧金山回国

1955-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫离旧金山回国
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社旧金山讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫二十六日晚间离开旧金山经纽约回苏联。同他一起离开的有苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾。
    苏联参加联合国纪念会的代表团由苏联外交部副部长费德林领导。
