Title: President of the British Red Cross Society Hosts Reception for Chinese Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: A London dispatch reports that Sir Woolton, President of the British Red Cross Society, held a banquet on the 15th to welcome the Chinese Red Cross delegation, headed by President Li Dequan, which is visiting Britain.

Those attending to accompany the guests included British Minister of Health Iain Macleod, Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Marquess of Reading, and Vice-President of the British Red Cross Society, the Countess of Limerick.

Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Britain, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Head of Delegation Li Dequan presented gifts on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross Society to the British Red Cross Society.

### 英国红十字会会长欢宴我红十字会代表团

1955-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    英国红十字会会长欢宴我红十字会代表团
    新华社布拉格十六日电　伦敦消息：英国红十字会会长伍尔顿爵士十五日举行宴会欢迎到英国访问的、以李德全会长为首的中国红十字会代表团。
    出席作陪的有英国卫生大臣艾·诺·麦克里奥德，外交副大臣里丁侯爵和英国红十字会副会长利麦里克伯爵夫人。
    中国驻英国代办宦乡也应邀出席了宴会。
    李德全团长代表中国红十字会向英国红十字会赠送了礼物。
