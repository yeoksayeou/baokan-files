Title: Fighting Breaks Out Between Ngo Dinh Diem's Troops and Hoa Hao Troops
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 31st: According to foreign news agency reports, Ngo Dinh Diem's troops and Hoa Hao troops engaged in combat on the 29th in the area bordering South Vietnam and Cambodia. Reports indicate that the fighting was intense, involving several battalions.

According to an Agence France-Presse report on the 30th, Ngo Dinh Diem and a leader of the Hoa Hao sect, Tran Van Soai, are still bargaining over the issue of buying off Tran Van Soai's troops.

### 吴庭艳军队同和好教军队发生战斗

1955-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    吴庭艳军队同和好教军队发生战斗
    新华社河内三十一日电　据外国通讯社消息，吴庭艳军队同和好教军队二十九日在南越、柬埔寨边境地带发生了战斗。消息说，战况激烈，投入战斗的部队有好几个营。
    据法新社三十日报道，吴庭艳同和好教的一个领袖陈文帅仍在就收买陈文帅的军队的问题，进行讨价还价。
