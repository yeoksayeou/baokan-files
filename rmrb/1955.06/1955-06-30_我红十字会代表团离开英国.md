Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Leaves Britain
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    Chinese Red Cross Delegation Leaves Britain
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Chinese Red Cross delegation, which had been invited by the British Red Cross to visit Britain for a friendly visit, left Britain by plane on the 27th after a two-week visit. The Vice-President of the British Red Cross, Lady Limerick, saw them off at London Airport.
    Before the plane took off, the head of the delegation, Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross, gave a speech at the airport, expressing his belief that the Red Cross societies of China and Britain will enhance the contacts that have already been established, and sincerely hoped to continue to maintain friendly relations with each other in the future.

### 我红十字会代表团离开英国

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我红十字会代表团离开英国
    据新华社讯　应英国红十字会邀请前往英国作友好访问的中国红十字会代表团在英国访问了两周以后，已经在二十七日乘飞机离开英国。英国红十字会副会长利麦里克夫人到伦敦机场送行。
    飞机起飞前，代表团团长、中国红十字会会长李德全在机场发表了谈话，表示相信中、英两国的红十字会将增进已经建立起来的接触，并且竭诚希望今后继续维持彼此之间的友好关系。
