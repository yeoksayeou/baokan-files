Title: President Ho Chi Minh Leads Vietnamese Government Delegation Through Nanning to Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, June 23rd. The government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Nanning this morning. The delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the government of the People's Republic of China.

    Luo Guibo, our country's ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, accompanied President Ho Chi Minh and others to our country.

    Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department, greeted the distinguished guests at the Friendship Pass (Munan Pass) on the Sino-Vietnamese border. Also present at Munan Pass to welcome them were Qin Yingji, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province, and Qin Shimian, Deputy Commander of the Guangxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Nguyen Van Luu, Consul in Nanning, also greeted them at Munan Pass.

    At 6:20 this morning, the special train carrying the delegation arrived in Nanning. Vietnamese and Chinese national flags and colorful flags were hung at the station.

    Welcoming the distinguished guests at Nanning Station were Hao Zhongshi, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province, Chen Manyuan, Secretary of the Guangxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Lu Shaowu, Commander of the Guangxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Chen Feng, Mayor of Nanning, Wang Tan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangxi Provincial People's Committee, heads of various departments and local organizations of democratic parties and people's organizations in Guangxi Province and Nanning City, and people from all walks of life. Vietnamese citizens residing in the PRC were also in the welcoming crowd.

    Ten thousand people welcomed the distinguished guests at the station and along the streets.

    After alighting from the train, the delegation members cordially shook hands with the welcomers. After the national anthems of Vietnam and China were played, President Ho Chi Minh and members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor. Young Pioneers presented flowers to President Ho Chi Minh.

    President Ho Chi Minh spoke at the station. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the welcomers and wished for the further development and consolidation of the friendship between the people of Vietnam and China.

    Among the welcomers were people dressed in the costumes of various ethnic minorities, such as Zhuang, Yao, and Dong. People shouted welcome slogans: "Long live the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people!" "Long live Sino-Vietnamese friendship and cooperation!" "Long live our dear President Ho Chi Minh!" "Long live peace!" Vietnamese residents of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, wearing sun hats, enthusiastically shouted the slogan "Ho Chu Tich Muon Nam (Long Live President Ho)!"

    Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members, accompanied by Hao Zhongshi and others, traveled by car to their residence to rest. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the people of Nanning along the way. People continuously applauded or waved to the distinguished guests. As the cars passed along a street shaded by phoenix trees, the Young Pioneers on both sides waved their red scarves to welcome the delegation led by President Ho Chi Minh.

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, June 23rd. The government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Wuhan by special plane at 12:00 noon today.

    More than 3,000 people welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

    The welcomers braved the rain and waited at the airport early. When the plane, adorned with the national flags of China and Vietnam, landed at the airport, warm applause and cheers erupted from the welcoming crowd. President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members waved continuously to the welcomers after disembarking from the plane.

    President Ho Chi Minh and the delegation members were accompanied to Wuhan by Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Luo Guibo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    Welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport were Liu Zihou, Governor of Hubei Province, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Yingxi, Deputy Commander of the Hubei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Song Kanfu, Mayor of Wuhan, Li Erzhong, Third Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Yao Guang, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Hubei Provincial People's Committee, and others.

### 胡志明主席率领越南政府代表团过南宁到武汉

1955-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席率领越南政府代表团过南宁到武汉
    新华社南宁二十三日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团今日早晨到达南宁。代表团是应中华人民共和国政府的邀请到我国作正式访问的。
    我国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波陪同胡志明主席等前来我国。
    中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、礼宾司司长柯华到中越国境睦南关迎接贵宾。到睦南关迎接的还有广西省副省长覃应机、中国人民解放军广西军区副司令员覃士冕。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使黄文欢和驻南宁领事阮文琉也到睦南关迎接。
    二十三日早晨六时二十分，代表团所乘的专车到达南宁。车站上悬挂着越中两国国旗和各色彩旗。
    在南宁车站上欢迎贵宾的有广西省副省长郝中士，中共广西省委员会书记陈漫远，中国人民解放军广西军区司令员卢绍武，南宁市市长陈枫，广西省人民委员会外事处处长王谭，广西省和南宁市各机关、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体的负责人以及各界群众。欢迎的人群中还有越南民主共和国的侨民。
    在车站上和街道两旁欢迎贵宾的有一万人。
    代表团人员下车后同欢迎者亲切地握手。在奏越中两国国歌后，胡志明主席和代表团团员检阅了仪仗队。少年先锋队队员向胡志明主席献花。
    胡志明主席在车站上讲话。他对欢迎者表示衷心感谢，并祝越中两国人民的友谊进一步发展和巩固。
    在欢迎的人群中，有穿着僮、瑶、侗等各种少数民族服装的人。人们高呼欢迎口号：“中越人民的兄弟友谊万岁！”“中越友好合作万岁！”“亲爱的胡志明主席万岁！”“和平万岁！”戴着遮阳通帽的越南民主共和国侨民热烈地高呼“胡主席莫南（万岁）！”的口号。
    接着，胡志明主席和代表团人员在郝中士等陪同下乘车前往住所休息。贵宾们沿途受到南宁市人民的夹道欢迎。人们不断地向贵宾鼓掌或招手致意。当汽车经过一条有凤凰树绿荫遮盖的街道时，两旁的少先队队员们摇动着红领巾欢迎胡志明主席所率领的代表团。
    新华社武汉二十三日电　胡志明主席率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团，今日上午十二时乘专机抵达武汉。
    在机场上欢迎贵宾的有三千多人。
    欢迎的人冒着雨很早就在机场上等候。当插着中越两国国旗的飞机在机场上降落时，欢迎的人群中响起热烈的掌声和欢呼声。胡志明主席和代表团人员走下飞机后向欢迎者不住地招手。
    胡志明主席和代表团人员是由中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波、外交部礼宾司司长柯华等陪同一起到达武汉的。
    在机场上欢迎贵宾的有湖北省省长刘子厚、中共湖北省委员会第一书记王任重、中国人民解放军湖北军区副司令员李迎希、武汉市市长宋侃夫、中共武汉市委员会第三书记李尔重、湖北省人民委员会外事处处长姚广等。
