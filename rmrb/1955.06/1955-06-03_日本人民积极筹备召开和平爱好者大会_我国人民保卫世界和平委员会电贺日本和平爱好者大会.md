Title: Japanese People Actively Prepare to Convene Peace Advocates Conference; China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations to Japanese Peace Advocates Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. News: The Japanese Preparatory Committee for the World Peace Conference and people from all over Japan are actively preparing for the three-day Japanese Peace Advocates Conference to be held in Tokyo starting on June 4th. In addition to selecting the Japanese delegation to participate in the World Peace Conference, this conference will also focus on issues such as opposing the preparation for atomic war and promoting the normalization of relations between Japan and the Soviet Union and China, and discuss the issue of further promoting the Japanese peace movement. The conference will also discuss the achievements of the Asian countries conference and create conditions for the convening of the World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons to be held in Hiroshima on August 6th. As of recently, about twenty national trade unions and farmers' groups, fourteen peace organizations, and more than twenty youth, women's, and cultural groups have expressed their support for the upcoming Japanese Peace Advocates Conference. At the same time, preparatory activities are also underway in many places such as Hokkaido, Gunma Prefecture, Ibaraki Prefecture, and Osaka.
    According to the "Akahata" newspaper, by May 21st, the Japanese Preparatory Committee had already selected seventy representatives to participate in the World Peace Conference, and is continuing to select more in order to form a broad-based delegation of about one hundred people. The seventy representatives already selected include Ikio Oyama, Chairman of the Japanese Peace Protection Committee, Koichi Saionji, member of the World Peace Council, as well as members of parliament, writers, scientists, musicians, professors, students, and representatives of various mass organizations such as trade unions.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the China Peace Committee, representing all peace-loving Chinese people, sent a telegram on May 31st to congratulate the Japanese Peace Advocates Conference. The telegram said:
    We believe that the conference will make a strong contribution to the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world, opposing atomic weapons, and promoting normal relations and economic & cultural exchanges between Japan and various countries.

### 日本人民积极筹备召开和平爱好者大会  我国人民保卫世界和平委员会电贺日本和平爱好者大会

1955-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民积极筹备召开和平爱好者大会
    我国人民保卫世界和平委员会电贺日本和平爱好者大会
    新华社二日讯　东京消息：世界和平大会日本筹备会和日本各地人民，积极筹备将于六月四日开始在东京举行三天的日本和平爱好者大会。这个大会除了选派参加世界和平大会的日本代表团以外，还将以反对准备原子战争、促使日本同苏中两国关系正常化等问题为中心，讨论进一步推进日本和平运动的问题。大会还将讨论亚洲国家会议的成就，并为八月六日在广岛举行的禁止原子武器世界大会的召开创造条件。到最近为止，约有二十个全国性工会和农民团体，十四个和平团体，二十多个青年、妇女、文化团体，表示支持即将召开的日本和平爱好者大会。同时，在北海道、群马县、茨城县、大阪等许多地方，也正在进行筹备活动。
    据“赤旗报”报道，到五月二十一日，日本筹备会已经选出了七十名参加世界和平大会的代表，并且还在继续推选，以便组成一个广泛性的一百人左右的大代表团。在已选出的七十个代表中，包括日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫、世界和平理事会理事西园寺公一以及国会议员、作家、科学家、音乐家、教授、学生和工会等各种群众团体的代表。
    据新华社讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会代表爱好和平的全体中国人民于五月三十一日致电祝贺日本和平爱好者大会，电文说：
    我们相信，大会对于保卫亚洲与世界和平、反对原子武器、促进日本与各国的正常关系和经济与文化交流的事业一定会作出有力的贡献。
