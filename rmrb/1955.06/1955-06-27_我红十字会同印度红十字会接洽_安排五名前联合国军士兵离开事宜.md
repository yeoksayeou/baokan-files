Title: My Red Cross Society Contacts Indian Red Cross Society to Arrange Departure of Five Former UN Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 1

    My Red Cross Society Contacts Indian Red Cross Society
    to Arrange Departure of Five Former UN Soldiers
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th – This reporter learned from the Chinese Red Cross Society that necessary arrangements are being made by the Chinese Red Cross Society with the Indian Red Cross Society regarding the departure from China of five former UN soldiers, Lewis Griggs, Otto Bell, William Cowart, Roger Devlin, and Louis Vildienez, who have applied and been approved by the relevant Chinese authorities to leave China. Once the arrangements are finalized, they will be able to leave China.

### 我红十字会同印度红十字会接洽  安排五名前联合国军士兵离开事宜

1955-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我红十字会同印度红十字会接洽
    安排五名前联合国军士兵离开事宜
    新华社二十四日讯  本社记者从中国红十字会获悉，关于已经提出申请并获得中国有关方面批准，将要离开中国的前联合国军士兵刘易斯·格里格斯、奥托·贝尔、威廉·科华特、罗歇·德夫里安和路易·弗尔迪湼等五人离开中国的一些必要安排，正由中国红十字会同印度红十字会进行接洽，一等到接洽妥当以后，他们就可以离开中国。
