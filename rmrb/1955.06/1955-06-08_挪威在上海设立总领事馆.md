Title: Norway Establishes Consulate General in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai. With the consent of the Chinese government, the government of the Kingdom of Norway has established a Consulate General in Shanghai and appointed Bos Tai as Consul General.

### 挪威在上海设立总领事馆

1955-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威在上海设立总领事馆
    新华社上海电　挪威王国政府征得我国政府同意后，已在上海设立总领事馆，并任命鲍斯泰为总领事。
