Title: The steel plant built with Soviet assistance in India will begin production in 1958.
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 22nd - According to a report from the Indian Press Trust from Panchmahals in Madhya Pradesh, India: Soviet experts who arrived in Panchmahals on the 21st stated that the Bhilai Steel Plant, with an annual output of one million tons of steel and built with the assistance of Soviet experts, will begin production in December 1958. These Soviet experts came to Panchmahals to discuss the plan with the Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, Ravi Shankar Shukla. The blueprint of the plan will be submitted to the Indian government in October this year.

The report also stated that the machinery and equipment for the steel plant will be shipped from the Soviet Union to India in early 1956. In 1956, approximately 500 Indians will go to the Soviet Union for technical training. Around 100 Soviet technicians will arrive in India by the end of this year.

### 苏联协助印度修建的钢铁厂将在一九五八年开始生产

1955-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联协助印度修建的钢铁厂将在一九五八年开始生产
    新华社新德里二十二日讯　据印度报业托辣斯自印度中央省潘契马希发出的消息：二十一日到达潘契马希的苏联专家说，由苏联专家协助修建的年产一百万吨钢的比莱钢铁厂将在一九五八年十二月开始生产。这些苏联专家到潘契马希来，是与中央省首席部长拉维·山卡尔·舒克拉商谈计划的。计划的蓝皮书将在今年十月提交印度政府。
    消息又说，钢铁厂的机器设备将在一九五六年初自苏联运到印度。一九五六年将有五百名印度人到苏联去受技术训练。一百名左右的苏联技术人员将在今年年底到达印度。
