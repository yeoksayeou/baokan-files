Title: Soviet Delegation Travels to New York via Paris to Attend Tenth Anniversary Celebration of the Signing of the United Nations Charter
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report:

    Soviet Delegation Travels to New York via Paris to Attend Tenth Anniversary Celebration of the Signing of the United Nations Charter
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed a delegation to attend the tenth anniversary celebration of the signing of the United Nations Charter, to be held in San Francisco on June 20.

    The members of the delegation are as follows: First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov (head of the delegation), Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Fedrin, Soviet Ambassador to the United States Zarubin, and Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations Sobolev. The Soviet Council of Ministers also appointed Charapkin, Volkov, and Kharlamov as advisors to the delegation.

    The Soviet delegation, led by Molotov, departed from Moscow on the 9th and arrived in Paris on the same day. Shortly after arriving in Paris, Molotov visited the French Foreign Ministry and attended a banquet hosted by French Foreign Minister Pinay. French Prime Minister Faure also attended the banquet. After the banquet, Molotov and his party proceeded to Cherbourg to travel to New York.

### 出席联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会  苏联代表团经巴黎赴纽约

1955-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    出席联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会
    苏联代表团经巴黎赴纽约
    据新华社讯　苏联部长会议任命了参加六月二十日在旧金山举行的联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会的代表团。
    代表团成员如下：苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫（团长），苏联外交部副部长费德林，苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾和苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫。苏联部长会议还任命查拉普金、沃尔科夫和哈尔拉摩夫为代表团顾问。
    苏联代表团在莫洛托夫率领下九日已经从莫斯科启程，当日到达巴黎。莫洛托夫到巴黎后不久，就访问了法国外交部，并且出席了法国外交部长比内的宴会。法国总理富尔也出席了这个宴会。宴会以后，莫洛托夫等一行就前往瑟堡转赴纽约。
