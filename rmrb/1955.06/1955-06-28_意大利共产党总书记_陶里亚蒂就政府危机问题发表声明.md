Title: Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti Issues Statement on Government Crisis

Author:

Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, June 26th (TASS dispatch): After meeting with Italian President Gronchi regarding the government crisis, Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti issued a statement to the press, clarifying the Italian Communist Party's position on the formation of a new government and the program of the new government.

Togliatti said: "We believe that the most important thing is that the government should once again abide by the constitution that guarantees the freedom and equality of all citizens. We demand that the government never again arbitrarily, or according to certain principles of fascism, discriminate against citizens and their organizations. We believe that safeguarding the interests of workers and peasants, restoring and defending their dignity and rights, is an urgent and important task of the current democratic cause."

He continued: "We believe that Italy can no longer fail to participate in the work of easing international tensions and bringing East and West closer together. We do not understand why Italy should not recognize the government of the great People's Republic of China.

We believe that the independence of the country must be safeguarded, and that new foreign troops must never be allowed to be stationed in our country."

Togliatti said: "Whoever truly embarks on the road of social reform, and thus consolidates our democratic system according to the principles enshrined in the constitution, our party will never hinder it."

### 意大利共产党总书记  陶里亚蒂就政府危机问题发表声明

1955-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利共产党总书记
    陶里亚蒂就政府危机问题发表声明
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社罗马二十六日讯：意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂在同意大利共和国总统格隆基就政府危机进行会谈后，向报界发表声明，阐明意大利共产党在成立新政府和新政府的纲领问题上所采取的立场。
    陶里亚蒂说：“我们认为最重要的是，政府应重新遵守保障一切公民的自由和平等的宪法。我们要求，政府今后再也不得任意地，或是根据法西斯主义的某些准则，对公民和他们的组织加以歧视。我们认为，维护工农的利益，恢复和保卫他们的尊严和权利，是当前民主事业的迫切的重要工作。”
    他接着说：“我们认为，意大利再不能不参加缓和国际紧张局势和使东西方接近的工作。我们不了解，意大利为什么不应当承认伟大的中华人民共和国的政府。
    我们认为，必须维护国家的独立，决不让新的外国军队驻扎在我国。”
    陶里亚蒂说：“无论什么人，只要真正走社会改革的道路，因而走根据宪法所确定的原则巩固我们的民主制度的道路，我们党决不会加以阻难的。”
