Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Theatrical Soiree to Entertain Indian Cultural Delegation; Indian Cultural Delegation Visits Summer Palace Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1955-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th. The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a theatrical soiree on the evening of the 13th to entertain the entire Indian Cultural Delegation headed by Mr. Chand.

At the soiree, famous Yue Opera actors Yuan Xuefen, Fan Ruijuan, and Fu Quanxiang performed "Broken Bridge," a scene from "The Legend of the White Snake"; famous Peking Opera actors Tan Fuying and Qiu Shengrong performed "The Empty City Stratagem"; and famous Peking Opera actors Mei Lanfang and Jiang Miaoxiang performed "The Goddess of the Luo River."

The wonderful performances of the Chinese drama artists were warmly welcomed and appreciated by the Indian artists. After each performance, the Indian artists went on stage to present flowers to the performers. After the famous performing artist Mei Lanfang performed, Mrs. Chand personally went on stage to present flowers to Mei Lanfang. Mr. and Mrs. Chand had a cordial conversation with Mei Lanfang backstage and took pictures together. Chand also asked Mei Lanfang to sign an autograph as a memento.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the performance were Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture and President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Xia Yan and Chen Kehan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

On the evening of the 9th, the entire Indian Cultural Delegation watched a song and dance performance.

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th. The head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Mr. Chand and his wife, along with members of the delegation, accompanied by Chinese singers and dancers, visited the Summer Palace on the 13th. Head of Delegation Chand said it was his "most enjoyable day."

At noon, the Indian artists, together with Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, as well as young Chinese artists, ascended the peak of Longevity Hill. They admired the Jingfu Pavilion, then passed through the Wisdom Sea to the Fragrant Buddha Pavilion amidst the verdant greenery. From here, they overlooked the summer scenery of the Summer Palace.

After lunch at the Tingliguan Restaurant, India's famous dancer Indrani Rahman performed Bharatanatyam dance, and two students from the Beijing Dance School performed Northeast Yangko dance.

In the afternoon, Indian and Chinese artists took a painted boat together to tour Kunming Lake. Many people sang and danced lightly on the bow of the boat, and many put their feet in the lake water, teaching each other songs from their respective countries. A burst of happy laughter constantly echoed on the shimmering lake.

While cruising the lake, India's famous dancer, K. Kutty, spoke to Chinese dancers about the history of Indian classical music. In the garden, the famous Chinese dancer Chen Jinqing specially bought Beijing specialty handicrafts as gifts for the Indian artists.

On the way back, Head of Delegation Chand and his wife visited the scenic Five Pagoda Temple. This temple was built in the early Yongle period of the Ming Dynasty, modeled after the Buddha Gaya Temple in India. When Head of Delegation Chand saw the Bengali script carved on the pagoda, he said that he was very happy to see the script of his hometown in distant Beijing.

On the morning of the 12th, the Indian artists also visited the Yonghe Temple and the Beihai Park.

### 文化部举行戏剧晚会招待印度文化代表团  印度文化代表团昨天游览了颐和园

1955-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部举行戏剧晚会招待印度文化代表团
    印度文化代表团昨天游览了颐和园
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国文化部在十三日晚举行戏剧晚会，招待以钱达先生为首的印度文化代表团全体人员。
    晚会上，由著名越剧演员袁雪芬、范瑞娟和傅全香演出了“白蛇传”中的一节“断桥”，著名京剧演员谭富英、裘盛戎等演出了“空城计”，著名京剧演员梅兰芳、姜妙香等演出了“洛神”。
    中国戏剧家们的精采表演受到印度艺术家们的热烈欢迎和赞赏，每一节目表演后，印度艺术家们都登台向表演者献花。当著名表演艺术家梅兰芳表演后，钱达夫人亲自登台向梅兰芳献花。钱达和夫人在后台和梅兰芳作了亲切的谈话，并在一起照了像。钱达还请梅兰芳签字留念。
    陪同贵宾们观剧的，有文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，副部长、中印友好协会会长丁西林，文化部副部长夏衍、陈克寒，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长王昆仑等。
    在九日晚间，印度文化代表团全体人员曾观看了歌舞表演。
    新华社十三日讯　印度文化代表团团长钱达和夫人以及团员们由中国歌唱家、舞蹈家们陪同，在十三日，游览了颐和园。钱达团长说，这是他“最愉快的一天”。
    中午，印度艺术家们和中印友好协会会长、文化部副部长丁西林以及中国青年艺术家们一同登上了万寿山的高峰，他们瞻仰了景福阁，然后经过智慧海，到万绿丛中的佛香阁，在这里，他们俯瞰了颐和园的夏日景色。
    在听鹂馆进餐后，印度著名的舞蹈家印·拉赫曼表演了婆罗多舞，北京舞蹈学校两位学生表演了东北秧歌舞。
    午后，印度和中国艺术家们一同乘画舫游昆明湖。很多人在船头上轻歌曼舞，很多人把脚放在湖水里互相教唱着两国的歌曲。碧波微荡的湖上，不断地响起了一片愉快的笑声。
    游湖时，印度著名的舞蹈家克·库蒂向中国舞蹈家们谈到了印度古典音乐的历史。在园中，中国著名舞蹈家陈锦清还特地买了北京特产工艺品赠送给印度艺术家们。
    在归来的路上，钱达团长和夫人游览了名胜五塔寺。这是明永乐初年仿照印度佛骨伽耶寺而建造的。钱达团长在看到了刻在塔上的孟加拉文字时说，他很高兴能够在遥远的北京看到他家乡的文字。
    十二日上午，印度艺术家们还参观了雍和宫，游览了北海公园。
