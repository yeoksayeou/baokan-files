Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Cables Congratulations to French National Day Rally Against Racial Discrimination and for Peace

Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th—The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace cabled congratulations to the French National Day Rally Against Racial Discrimination, Anti-Semitism, and for Peace, scheduled for the 12th. The cable said: "The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace extends warm congratulations to your rally. Colonialists' traditional policies and actions of racial segregation and discrimination create hatred and division among all ethnic groups, violate human equality and basic human rights, and endanger world peace. The Chinese people are willing to work together with the French people and all peace-loving people in the world to eradicate the scourge of racial discrimination and to safeguard human rights and world peace. We wish the rally complete success."

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  电贺法国反对种族歧视争取和平全国日大会

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    电贺法国反对种族歧视争取和平全国日大会
    新华社十一日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会电贺定于十二日举行的法国反对种族歧视、反对排犹主义争取和平全国日大会。电文说：“中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨向你们的大会致以热烈的祝贺。殖民主义者传统的种族隔离和歧视的政策和行为，造成各族人民的仇恨与分裂、侵犯人类的平等与基本人权，并危害世界的和平。中国人民愿与法国人民以及世界一切爱好和平人民共同努力，为根除种族歧视的祸害、为维护人权与世界和平而奋斗。谨预祝大会获得圆满成就。”
