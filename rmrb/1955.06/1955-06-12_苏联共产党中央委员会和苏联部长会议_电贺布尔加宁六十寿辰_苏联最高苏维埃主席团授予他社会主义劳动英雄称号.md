Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Congratulate Bulganin on his Sixtieth Birthday; The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Confers on Him the Title of Hero of Socialist Labor
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th. TASS, Moscow, June 11th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to N.A. Bulganin, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: Comrade Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin:

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union extend warm congratulations to you, a loyal student of Lenin and a comrade-in-arms of Stalin, and an outstanding activist of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, on your sixtieth birthday.

	The Communist Party and the Soviet people highly value the contributions you have made in the struggle to build the Soviet state and socialist industrialization, in the struggle to defeat the fascist bandits, and in the struggle to strengthen the economic and defense capabilities of our motherland.

	In every post of Party work and state work, you have dedicated all your energy to the great cause of communist construction with tireless energy and a high degree of principle.

	We sincerely wish you, our friend and comrade, our dear Nikolai Alexandrovich, good health and longevity, and wish you continued outstanding work for the happiness of the Soviet people and for the cause of communism.
				The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
				The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th. TASS, Moscow, June 11th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union’s order on awarding N.A. Bulganin the title of Hero of Socialist Labor:

	To congratulate the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, on his sixtieth birthday and to commend his outstanding contributions to the Communist Party and the Soviet people, Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin is hereby awarded the title of Hero of Socialist Labor, and he is awarded the Order of Lenin and the "Sickle and Hammer" Gold Medal.
				Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
					K. Voroshilov
				Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
					N. Begov
				June 10, 1955, Moscow Kremlin
(Attached Picture)
	N.A. Bulganin

### 苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议  电贺布尔加宁六十寿辰  苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予他社会主义劳动英雄称号

1955-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议
    电贺布尔加宁六十寿辰
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予他社会主义劳动英雄称号
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议致电尼·亚·布尔加宁，祝贺他的六十寿辰。贺电全文如下：尼古拉·亚历山德罗维奇·布尔加宁同志：
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议在您六十岁寿辰的时候向您，列宁的忠实学生和斯大林的战友，共产党和苏维埃国家的杰出活动家致热烈的祝贺。
    共产党和苏联人民非常珍视您在争取建设苏维埃国家和社会主义工业化的斗争中、在战胜法西斯强盗的斗争中和在加强我们祖国的经济力量和国防力量的斗争中所建树的功绩。
    在党的工作和国家工作的各个岗位上，您以不倦的精力和高度的原则性把自己的全付精力献给伟大的共产主义建设事业。
    我们衷心地祝您，我们的朋友和同志，我们亲爱的尼古拉·亚历山德罗维奇健康长寿，祝您继续为苏联人民的幸福、为共产主义事业作出卓著成效的工作。  苏联共产党中央委员会  苏联部长会议
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十一日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团关于授予尼·亚·布尔加宁社会主义劳动英雄称号的命令：
    为祝贺苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁六十寿辰和表彰他对共产党和苏联人民的卓越功绩，特授予尼古拉·亚历山德罗维奇·布尔加宁社会主义劳动英雄的称号，并且授予他列宁勋章和“镰刀与锤子”金质奖章。  苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席  克·伏罗希洛夫  苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书  尼·别哥夫  一九五五年六月十日　莫斯科克里姆林宫（附图片）
    尼·亚·布尔加宁
