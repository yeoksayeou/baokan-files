Title: Chen Yi Receives Mongolian Science and Culture Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 8th

    Chen Yi Receives Mongolian Science and Culture Workers Delegation

    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the Mongolian Science and Culture Workers Delegation leader Mashlai, and members Master of History Nachagdarri, and painter Zewigehzabu at 2 p.m. on the 8th.

    Present at the reception were Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, Director of the Institute of Philosophy of the Academy of Sciences Pan Zinian, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association Liu Kaiqu, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Chen Zhongjing and others.

    Also present was the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Ochirbat.

### 陈毅接见蒙古科学文化工作者代表团

1955-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见蒙古科学文化工作者代表团
    新华社八日讯　国务院副总理陈毅在八日下午二时接见了蒙古科学文化工作者代表团团长马什拉伊和团员历史学硕士纳查格达尔日、画家泽维格扎布。
    接见时在座的有教育部部长张奚若，外交部部长助理陈家康，科学院哲学研究所所长潘梓年，中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠，对外文化联络局副局长陈忠经等。
    在座的还有蒙古人民共和国驻华大使奥其尔巴特。
