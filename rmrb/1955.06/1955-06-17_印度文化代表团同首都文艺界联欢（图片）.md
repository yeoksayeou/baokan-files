Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Holds Joint Celebration with Capital's Literary and Art Circles (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 3

The Indian Cultural Delegation visited the China National Academy of Chinese Theatre Arts and Beijing Dance Academy on June 16, and held discussions and a joint celebration with people from our country's literary and art circles.

    Below: When the delegation visited the China National Academy of Chinese Theatre Arts, the students specially performed the Water Sleeve Dance for the Indian artists.
  Our reporter Yuan Yiping (photographer)

    People from our country's literary and art circles held discussions and a joint celebration with Indian artists at the Beijing International Club.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue (photographer)

    Our country's literary and art circles presented gifts to the Indian Cultural Delegation. The recipient on the right in the picture is Mrs. Chanda of the Indian Cultural Delegation.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue (photographer)

    The children of the Beijing Dance Academy warmly welcomed the Indian artists.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie (photographer)

### 印度文化代表团同首都文艺界联欢（图片）

1955-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团同首都文艺界联欢
    印度文化代表团在六月十六日参观了中国戏曲学校、北京舞蹈学校，并同我国文艺界人士举行了座谈和联欢。
    下图：代表团在参观中国戏曲学校时，学生们特地为印度的艺术家们表演了水袖舞。
  本报记者　袁毅平摄
    我国文艺界人士同印度艺术家在北京国际俱乐部举行座谈和联欢。
                      新华社记者　吴化学摄
    我国文艺界向印度文化代表团献礼。图右受礼者为印度文化代表团钱达夫人。
                      新华社记者　吴化学摄北京舞蹈学校的小朋友们热烈欢迎印度的艺术家们。
                                        新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
