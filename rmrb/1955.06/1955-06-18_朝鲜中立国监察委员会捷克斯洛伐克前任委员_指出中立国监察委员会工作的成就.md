Title: Former Czechoslovak Member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea Points Out Achievements of the NNSC's Work
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 15th. According to a report in the "People's Defense Daily" on the 15th, General Hečko, the former Czechoslovak member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission (NNSC) in Korea, said when interviewed by a reporter from Czechoslovak Radio in Pyongyang: "Despite all the differences of opinion, the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission has been reaching resolutions for many months, and has reached resolutions on some important and complex issues."

General Hečko pointed out: "The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission provides practical evidence every day to prove that the existence of international institutions composed of representatives of countries with different social systems is possible, necessary, and beneficial."

### 朝鲜中立国监察委员会捷克斯洛伐克前任委员  指出中立国监察委员会工作的成就

1955-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中立国监察委员会捷克斯洛伐克前任委员
    指出中立国监察委员会工作的成就
    新华社布拉格十五日电　据“人民防线报”十五日报道，朝鲜中立国监察委员会的前任捷克斯洛伐克委员赫契柯将军在平壤接见捷克斯洛伐克电台的记者时说：“尽管有一切意见分歧，中立国监察委员会许多月来一直在达成决议，并且在重要而复杂的一些问题上也达成了决议。”
    赫契柯将军指出：“中立国监察委员会每天都在提供实际的证据来证明：由不同社会制度的国家的代表们所组成的国际机构的存在，是可能的、必要的和有益的。”
