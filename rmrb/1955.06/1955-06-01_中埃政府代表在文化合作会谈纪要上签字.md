Title: Chinese and Egyptian Government Representatives Sign Minutes of Cultural Cooperation Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 31st. Representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Egypt held a signing ceremony in Beijing on the afternoon of the 31st for the minutes of their talks on cultural cooperation between the two countries. These minutes will be submitted to the two governments for review and final decision.

The signing ceremony took place at 5 p.m. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, signed the minutes on behalf of the Chinese side, and Bakur, Minister of Religious Affairs of the Republic of Egypt, signed the minutes on behalf of the Egyptian side.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Higher Education Huang Songling, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council He Chengxiang, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Huang Hua, Chairman of the China Islamic Association Burhan, Vice Chairman Da Pongsheng, and President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan.

The Egyptian side participating in the signing ceremony included Bakur, Minister of Religious Affairs of the Republic of Egypt, and Kamel, Professor of Constitutional Law at Cairo University.

At the signing ceremony, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Bakur, Minister of Religious Affairs of the Republic of Egypt, spoke successively. They congratulated the success of the talks and wished the cultural exchange and friendship between China and Egypt would develop day by day. (Attached picture)

In the back row from left to right: Zhang Hanfu, Zhang Wentian, Kamel, Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, Burhan. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie.

### 中埃政府代表在文化合作会谈纪要上签字

1955-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃政府代表在文化合作会谈纪要上签字
    新华社三十一日讯　中华人民共和国政府代表和埃及共和国政府代表就两国文化合作的会谈纪要，三十一日下午在北京举行签字仪式。这个会谈纪要将分别提交两国政府加以审核和作出最后决定。
    签字仪式在下午五时举行。代表中国方面在会谈纪要上签字的是中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，代表埃及方面在会谈纪要上签字的是埃及共和国宗教事务部部长巴库尔。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长丁西林、郑振铎，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，高等教育部副部长黄松龄，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉、副主任达浦生，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南。
    埃及方面参加签字仪式的，有埃及共和国宗教事务部部长巴库尔和开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔。
    在签字仪式上，中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞和埃及共和国宗教事务部部长巴库尔先后讲话，他们祝贺这次会谈的成功，并祝中埃两国间的文化交流和友谊日益发展。（附图片）
