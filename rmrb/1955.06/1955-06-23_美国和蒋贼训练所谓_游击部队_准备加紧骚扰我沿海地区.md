Title: The US and Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Train So-Called "Guerrilla Forces" Preparing to Intensify Harassment of Our Coastal Areas
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The US imperialists and the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique are training so-called "guerrilla forces" on islands such as Jinmen (Quemoy) and Mazu (Matsu), preparing to intensify harassment of our mainland coastal areas.

According to Chiang Kai-shek bandit "Central News Agency" reports, the Chiang Kai-shek "guerrilla forces" on Jinmen and Mazu Islands have recently "increased sharply in number" and have "completed all basic training and begun to receive rigorous combat training." The report said that the US-Chiang Kai-shek have established a "Guerrilla Command Center" responsible for directly commanding these Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops to harass our mainland coastal areas.

According to captured Chiang Kai-shek soldiers, the training and command of the Chiang Kai-shek "guerrilla forces" are the responsibility of the American intelligence agency "Western Enterprises Corporation Jinmen Branch" and the Chiang Kai-shek "Jinmen Defense Department."

### 美国和蒋贼训练所谓“游击部队”  准备加紧骚扰我沿海地区

1955-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    美国和蒋贼训练所谓“游击部队”
    准备加紧骚扰我沿海地区
    新华社二十日讯　美帝国主义和蒋介石卖国集团正在金门、马祖等岛上训练所谓“游击部队”，准备加紧骚扰我国大陆沿海地区。
    蒋贼“中央社”消息，金门、马祖岛上的蒋贼
  “游击部队”最近“数目激增”，已经“完成一切基本训练，并开始接受严格的战斗训练”。消息说，美蒋已设立了“游击指挥中心”，负责直接指挥这些蒋贼部队窜扰我国大陆沿海地区。
    据被俘蒋贼军士兵供称，蒋贼“游击部队”的训练和指挥，是由美国特务机关“西方企业公司金门分公司”和蒋贼“金门防卫部”负责的。
