Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Departs for Visit to Britain; Chinese Red Cross Donates Funds to Aid Earthquake Victims in Greece

Author:

Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th—The Chinese Red Cross delegation of four people, led by Chairman Li Dequan, departed by plane on the morning of the 8th for a friendly visit to Britain at the invitation of the British Red Cross. Representatives from the Ministry of Public Health of the People's Republic of China, the National Headquarters of the Chinese Red Cross Society, and the Beijing Branch, among other units, were at the airport to see them off. The delegation will spend two weeks visiting Britain.

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th—In mid-April 1955, a series of intense earthquakes struck the city of Volos in central Greece and the surrounding area, leaving more than 60,000 people homeless. After learning of this situation, the Chinese Red Cross Society sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Greek Red Cross on June 8th, through the League of Red Cross Societies, expressing its sympathy and condolences. At the same time, it also donated 20,000 Swiss francs to the Greek Red Cross for the purpose of aiding the disaster victims.

### 中国红十字会代表团启程去英国访问  中国红十字会捐款救济希腊地震区灾民

1955-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会代表团启程去英国访问
    中国红十字会捐款救济希腊地震区灾民
    新华社八日讯　应英国红十字会邀请前往英国作友好访问的中国红十字会代表团一行四人，由李德全团长率领，在八日晨乘飞机启程。到机场送行的有中华人民共和国卫生部、中国红十字会总会和北京市分会等单位的负责人。代表团将在英国作为时两个星期的访问。
    新华社八日讯　一九五五年四月中旬，希腊中部瓦洛斯城及其附近地区连续发生激烈的地震，无家可归的灾民达六万多人。中国红十字会得悉这一情况后，在六月八日通过红十字会协会致电希腊红十字会中央委员会表示同情的慰问，同时还捐赠希腊红十字会两万瑞士法郎作为救济灾民之用。
