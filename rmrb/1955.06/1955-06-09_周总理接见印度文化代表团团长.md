Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Head of Indian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, the Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Indian Government, Anil Kumar Chanda and his wife, as well as the delegation's secretary, an official of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Indian Government, Gurbachan Singh, and the delegation's stage manager, Director and Program Director of All India Radio, Ram Marathe, at 7:10 p.m. on the 8th.

Present at the reception were Chen Yi, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Hanfu, Qian Junrui, Ding Xilin, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, and Zhou Weizhi.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, was also present at the reception. (Attached pictures)
　　
Right picture: Premier Zhou Enlai meets with Anil Kumar Chanda, head of the Indian Cultural Delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo.

Below picture: Anil Kumar Chanda (first from right), head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, and members, accompanied by Ding Xilin (second from left), President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, are warmly welcomed by the people of Beijing as they walk out of the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo.

### 周总理接见印度文化代表团团长

1955-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度文化代表团团长
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在八日下午七时十分接见了印度文化代表团团长、印度政府外交部副部长阿尼尔·库马尔·钱达和夫人，以及代表团秘书、印度政府外交部官员古尔巴昌·辛格，代表团舞台监督、全印广播电台主任兼节目指导拉姆·马拉太。
    接见时，在座的有陈毅、习仲勋、章汉夫、钱俊瑞、丁西林、郑振铎、夏衍、周巍峙。
    接见时，印度驻华大使赖嘉文也在座。（附图片）
