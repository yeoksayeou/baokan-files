Title: Vietnam "People's Daily" Publishes Editorial Commenting on the Significance of Strengthening the Great Friendship Between Vietnam and the Soviet Union and China

Author:

Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 23rd: The Vietnamese "People's Daily" published an editorial on June 23rd entitled "Strengthening the Great Friendship Between Vietnam and the Soviet Union and China."

The editorial said that in order to strengthen the great friendship between Vietnam and the two brotherly countries of the Soviet Union and China, which has always existed, a delegation headed by President Ho Chi Minh visited the Soviet Union and China. The editorial pointed out that the Vietnamese people received constant encouragement from the people of the Soviet Union and China during the sacred war of resistance, and at the Geneva Conference, the Soviet and Chinese delegations helped restore peace in Indochina. Under the circumstances of Vietnam's peace having been restored, this visit is of extremely important historical significance and will further develop the great friendship that has always existed between Vietnam and the Soviet Union and China.

The editorial pointed out that the friendship between Vietnam and the Soviet Union and China, as well as other brotherly countries, is one of the factors in the victory of the Vietnamese revolution. Continuously strengthening the strength and fighting will of the Vietnamese people, continuously strengthening the fraternal friendship between Vietnam and the Soviet Union, China, and other brotherly countries, and continuously developing and consolidating friendly relations with the world's peace-loving forces are a strong guarantee for the final victory of Vietnam's cause of peace, unification, independence, and democracy.

The editorial concluded by saying that the news of President Ho Chi Minh leading a delegation to visit the Soviet Union and China is great good news for all the people of Vietnam.

### 越南“人民报”发表社论  评加强越南同苏中两国伟大友谊的意义

1955-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”发表社论
    评加强越南同苏中两国伟大友谊的意义
    新华社河内二十三日电　越南“人民报”二十三日发表了一篇社论，题为“加强越南同苏联和中国之间的伟大友谊”。
    社论说，为了加强越南同苏联和中国两个兄弟国家之间历来就具有的伟大友谊，以胡志明主席为首的代表团访问苏联和中国。社论指出，越南人民在神圣的抗战期间，曾得到苏联和中国人民不断的鼓舞，在日内瓦会议上，苏联和中国代表团又帮助恢复了印度支那的和平。在越南的和平已经恢复的情况下，这一次的访问具有极其重大的历史意义，它将使越南同苏联和中国历来就具有的伟大友谊获得进一步的发展。
    社论指出，越南同苏联和中国以及其他兄弟国家之间的友谊是越南革命胜利的因素之一。不断加强越南人民的力量和奋斗意志，不断加强越南同苏联、中国和各兄弟国家之间的兄弟友谊，不断发展和巩固与世界爱好和平力量的友好关系，是越南的和平、统一、独立、民主事业最后胜利的坚强保证。
    社论最后说，胡志明主席率领代表团前往苏联和中国访问的消息，对越南全体人民是一个大喜讯。
