Title: Anshan Uses Belt Conveyor to Pour Concrete
Author: Feng Shiming
Date: 1955-06-15
Page: 2

    Anshan Uses Belt Conveyor to Pour Concrete

    The Anshan Iron and Steel Construction Company's rolling mill project sites are using belt conveyors to pour concrete, further mechanizing the concrete engineering construction process.

    In the past, the rolling mill project company used handcarts or mine cars to transport concrete, which required a lot of labor. Now, concrete is unloaded from dump trucks onto a receiving platform and then fed by belt conveyor through a series of chutes directly into the pouring location. This simplifies the intermediate manual handling process, greatly reducing the physical labor of workers and improving work efficiency. According to statistics, using a belt conveyor equipped with a vibrating chute to transport concrete, it only takes three people seven minutes to pour 1.5 cubic meters of concrete. If handcarts are used, it would take ten workers fifteen minutes. Using belt conveyors to pour concrete also eliminates the need for scaffolding, reduces the footprint, simplifies operation, and saves costs.
                                      (Feng Shiming)

### 鞍山用皮带输送机浇灌混凝土
冯世明
1955-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

    鞍山用皮带输送机浇灌混凝土
    鞍山钢铁建设公司轧钢工程公司各工地利用皮带输送机浇灌混凝土，使混凝土工程施工过程进一步机械化。
    过去轧钢工程公司浇灌混凝土，用手推车或矿车输送，劳动量很大。现在，混凝土从自卸汽车卸在受料台上，用皮带输送机经过串桶直接泻入浇灌地点，这样就简化了中间人工输送手续，大大地减少了工人的体力劳动，提高了工作效率。据统计，利用皮带输送机并配有震动溜槽输送混凝土，三个人操作，每浇灌一点五立方公尺混凝土，只需要七分钟；如果用手推车，十个工人，还要用十五分钟。使用皮带输送机浇灌混凝土，还可以不搭脚手架，占地面积小，操作简单，费用节省。
                                      （冯世明）
