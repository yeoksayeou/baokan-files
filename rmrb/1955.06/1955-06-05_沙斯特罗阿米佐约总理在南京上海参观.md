Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Visits Nanjing and Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, his wife, and accompanying personnel on their visit to China, stopped in Nanjing and Shanghai for sightseeing on their way back home.

On the afternoon of the 3rd, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his party arrived in Nanjing by plane from Beijing. En route, the distinguished guests overlooked the Huai River harnessing project above Runheji. In Nanjing, the guests, accompanied by Mayor Peng Chong, laid a wreath at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, toured Xuanwu Lake, attended a banquet hosted by Jiangsu Province Governor Hui Yuyu, and participated in an evening party, watching a Wuxi opera performance.

In Shanghai, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, his wife, and accompanying personnel paid a visit on the 4th, to Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, who is currently in Shanghai, and had lunch together.

On the 4th, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, his wife, and accompanying personnel also visited the Shanghai No. 1 Department Store and the China Welfare Institute's Children's Palace. At the Children's Palace, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife watched with great interest the children from the Children's Palace's amateur theater troupe choir and dance troupe perform the dance "Spring Flowers" and sing songs, and accepted red scarves presented by two Young Pioneers in the children's hall. On the evening of the 4th, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife were invited to attend a banquet hosted by Shanghai Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo. In addition, some of the accompanying personnel also visited the Yangshupu Power Plant in Shanghai.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在南京上海参观

1955-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在南京上海参观
    新华社四日讯　印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人及随同来华访问人员，在返国途中路经南京、上海两地参观。
    三日中午，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理等由北京乘飞机到达南京。途中，贵宾们在润河集上空俯瞰了治淮工程。在南京市，贵宾们由市长彭冲陪同到中山陵献花圈，游览玄武湖，并出席了江苏省省长惠浴宇举行的招待宴会，参加了招待晚会，观看锡剧。
    在上海，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人以及随同来华访问人员四日拜会了现在上海的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，并共进午餐。
    四日，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人及随同来华访问人员还参观了国营上海市第一百货商店和中国福利会少年宫。在少年宫，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人以极大兴趣观看了少年宫业余剧团合唱队、舞蹈队孩子们表演的舞蹈“春天的鲜花”和歌唱，并在少年厅接受了两个少先队员献的红领巾。四日晚，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人应邀出席了上海市副市长许建国的招待宴会。此外，部分随同来华访问人员还在上海参观了杨树浦发电厂。
