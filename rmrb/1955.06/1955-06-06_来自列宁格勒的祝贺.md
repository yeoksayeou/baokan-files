    Congratulations from Leningrad
    After the successful trial production of China's first 6,000-kilowatt steam turbine by the Shanghai Turbine Factory, the news reached the Soviet Union. The turbine manufacturers of the Stalin Metal Plant in Leningrad, through the Leningrad branch of the "Latest News" editorial department of the Broadcasting Administration of the Ministry of Culture of the Soviet Union, wrote to this newspaper to congratulate China on this victory on the industrial front. The letter is published below:

    Please convey the warm congratulations of the Leningrad turbine manufacturers to the machine builders of China on the occasion of the production of China's first steam turbine. We wish you new productive and creative achievements for the benefit of the great Chinese people, and new technological achievements in the struggle for China's industrialization.

    We will continue to be ready to fully assist our dear friends, the Chinese turbine manufacturers, and share our experience with you.

    Long live the friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union!

    Representatives of the turbine manufacturers of the Leningrad "Stalin Metal Plant": Dolinski, Director of the Turbine Workshop; Yefimov, Party Organizer of the Workshop; Mikhailov, Welding and Assembly Worker; and Ivanov, Foreman

### 来自列宁格勒的祝贺

1955-06-06
第6版()
专栏：读者来信专页

    来自列宁格勒的祝贺
    上海汽轮机厂试制成功我国第一台六千瓩汽轮机后，消息传到了苏联，列宁格勒斯大林五金工厂轮机制造者，经过苏联文化部广播管理总局  “最后消息”编辑部列宁格勒分部向本报写信，祝贺我国工业战线上的这个胜利，现将来信发表如下：
    由于中国第一台汽轮机的出厂，请向中国的机器制造者们转达列宁格勒轮机制造者们的热烈祝贺。我们预祝您们为伟大的中国人民的福利取得生产上的、创造性的新成绩，在争取中国工业化的斗争中取得新的技术成就。
    我们今后仍将随时准备全力帮助我们亲爱的朋友——中国的轮机制造者，并把我们的经验告诉你们。
    中苏两大民族的友谊万岁！
    列宁格勒“斯大林五金工厂”轮机制造者代表：汽轮机车间主任道林斯基、车间党组织员叶菲莫夫、焊接装配工人米哈伊洛夫和工长伊万诺夫
