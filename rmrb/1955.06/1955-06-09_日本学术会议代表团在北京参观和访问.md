Title: Japanese Academic Conference Delegation Visits and Observes in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 7th – The Japanese Academic Conference Delegation, invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit our country, has been visiting and observing in Beijing and holding discussions with Chinese scientists in recent days.

    On the evening of June 5th, the entire delegation attended a banquet hosted by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, accompanied by more than 40 people from academic circles in Beijing. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also attended the banquet.

    The entire delegation visited Peking University and also visited Tsinghua University, Beijing College of Geology, Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, and Beijing Mining Institute. They also engaged in academic exchanges with the Institutes of Philosophy, History, Botany, Entomology, etc. under the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as well as academic research institutions such as Beijing Medical College and the Chinese Union Medical College. Delegation leader Mao Chengshi also gave a report at the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    Nanbara Shigeru, former President of Tokyo University, who arrived in Beijing on the 6th, also held discussions with personnel from the Third Institute of History and the Institute of Linguistics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Individual members of the delegation also visited the Ministry of Water Resources, the Beijing Municipal Sewerage Engineering Bureau, the Supreme People's Court, the Chinese Writers Association, and state-owned farms.

    In addition, the delegation also visited scenic spots such as the Palace Museum, the Summer Palace, Beihai Park, and the Temple of Heaven.

### 日本学术会议代表团在北京参观和访问

1955-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    日本学术会议代表团在北京参观和访问
    新华社七日讯　应中国科学院邀请前来我国访问的日本学术会议代表团到达北京后，连日在京参观访问并与我国科学界人士座谈。
    六月五日晚，代表团全体人员出席了中国科学院院长郭沫若举行的宴会，由在京的学术界人士四十多人作陪。中国红十字会会长李德全、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南等也出席了宴会。
    代表团全体人员访问了北京大学，并分别访问了清华大学、北京地质学院、北京钢铁工业学院、北京矿业学院等高等学校。并分别与中国科学院所属哲学、历史、植物、昆虫等研究所和北京医学院、中国协和医学院等学术研究机关进行了学术交流活动。茅诚司团长并在中国科学院物理研究所做了报告。
    六日到北京的前东京大学校长南原繁也同中国科学院历史研究所第三所和语言研究所的人员进行座谈。个别团员还访问了水利部、北京市下水道工程局、最高人民法院、中国作家协会和国营农场等。
    此外，代表团还游览了故宫博物院、颐和园、北海公园、天坛等名胜。
