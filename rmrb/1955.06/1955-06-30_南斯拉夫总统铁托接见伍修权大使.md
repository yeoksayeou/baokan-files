Title: Yugoslav President Tito Receives Ambassador Wu Xiuquan
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, June 29th: Yugoslav Federal People's Republic President Tito received Wu Xiuquan, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Yugoslav Federal People's Republic, on the 27th. During the meeting, Ambassador Wu Xiuquan presented gifts from Chairman Mao Zedong to President Tito on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong. President Tito had a cordial conversation with Ambassador Wu Xiuquan.

### 南斯拉夫总统铁托接见伍修权大使

1955-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫总统铁托接见伍修权大使
    新华社二十九日讯　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托在二十七日接见了中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国大使伍修权。接见时，伍修权大使代表毛泽东主席向铁托总统赠送礼物。铁托总统同伍修权大使进行了亲切的交谈。
