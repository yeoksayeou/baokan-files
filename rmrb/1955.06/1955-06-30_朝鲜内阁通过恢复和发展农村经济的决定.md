Title: North Korean Cabinet Passes Decision on Restoring and Developing Rural Economy
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea passed a decision on measures for the rapid restoration and development of the rural economy on the 24th, in order to promote the further high tide of the North Korean national economy and further improve the lives of farmers, and to enable agricultural production to catch up with the requirements of industrial development.
    The decision stipulates that the irrigation system will be expanded, the work of restoring and protecting arable land and the work of reclaiming wasteland and alkaline land will also be carried out on a large scale. At the same time, the reconstruction of the Hungnam, Bongung, and Sooncheon chemical fertilizer factories will be accelerated immediately so that they can produce more than 110,000 tons of fertilizer in the first half of 1956.
    The Cabinet also decided that in order to organizationally and economically strengthen and consolidate agricultural production cooperatives, leadership over them will be strengthened, and their livestock power will be supplemented and they will be provided with the best quality livestock. At the same time, measures will be taken to establish more agricultural machinery service stations in nine regions including North Pyongan Province, South Pyongan Province, Kangwon Province, North Hamgyong Province and South Hamgyong Province this year, and to ensure the quantity and quality of agricultural machinery production.
    At the same time, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order to amend some agricultural tax-in-kind rates. This order stipulates that the agricultural tax on dry rice paddies will be reduced by 2 percent, and the tax on fields with poor productivity in highlands and mountainous areas will be reduced by 8 percent.

### 朝鲜内阁通过恢复和发展农村经济的决定

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁通过恢复和发展农村经济的决定
    据新华社讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁二十四日通过了关于迅速恢复和发展农村经济的措施的决定，以促进朝鲜国民经济进一步高涨和进一步改善农民生活，并且使农业生产赶上工业发展的要求。
    决定规定，灌溉系统将要扩大，恢复和保护可耕地的工作以及开垦荒地和碱地的工作也将大规模进行。同时，将立刻加速进行兴南、本宫和顺川等化学肥料工厂的重建工程，以使它们能够在一九五六年上半年生产出十一万多吨的肥料。
    内阁同时决定，为了在组织上和经济上加强和巩固农业生产合作社，对于它们的领导工作将加强，并且将补充它们的牲畜力和向它们提供质量最好的家畜。同时要采取措施在今年以内在平安北道、平安南道、江原道、咸镜北道和咸镜南道等九个地区设立更多的农业机器代耕站，并且要保证农业机器生产的数量和质量。
    与这同时，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会发布了一项修正部分农业实物税率的政令。这项政令规定旱稻田的农业税减低百分之二，高原和山区生产力很差的田地减少百分之八。
