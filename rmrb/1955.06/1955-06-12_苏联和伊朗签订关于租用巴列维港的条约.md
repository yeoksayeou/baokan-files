Title: USSR and Iran Sign Treaty on Leasing the Port of Pahlavi
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, June 10th (TASS): Representatives of the Soviet Caspian Shipping Administration and the Iranian Ministry of Agriculture signed a treaty on June 9th at the Iranian Ministry of Foreign Affairs regarding the Soviet Union's lease of land and other port properties in the Iranian port of Pahlavi, with a lease term of ten years.
    The treaty was signed by A. K. Igolkin, Chief of the General Office of the Soviet Caspian Shipping Administration in Iran, and Yusuf Amir Barvis, Director of the National Land Administration of the Iranian Ministry of Agriculture.
    Trade circles in Tehran believe that the signing of this treaty will promote the development of economic relations between the Soviet Union and Iran.

### 苏联和伊朗签订关于租用巴列维港的条约

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和伊朗签订关于租用巴列维港的条约
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社德黑兰十日讯：苏联里海区国家航运管理局的代表和伊朗农业部的代表六月九日在伊朗外交部签订了关于苏联租用伊朗的巴列维港的土地和其他港口财产的条约，租约以十年为期。
    这个条约由苏联里海区国家航运管理局驻伊朗总办事处处长阿·康·伊戈尔金和伊朗农业部国家土地管理局局长优素福·阿密尔·巴尔维斯签署。
    德黑兰贸易界人士认为，这个条约的签订将促进苏联和伊朗经济关系的发展。
