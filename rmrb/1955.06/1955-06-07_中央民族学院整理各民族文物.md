Title: Central Institute for Nationalities Organizes Ethnic Minority Cultural Relics
Author:
Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    The Central Institute for Nationalities has collected more than 30,000 ethnic minority cultural relics and pictures, which are now being cataloged and organized. These cultural relics include precious revolutionary relics, handicrafts, clothing, recreational items, and production tools. Among the revolutionary relics are knives and uniforms used in the Yao uprising in 1933, "Public Notices of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army" preserved by the Yao people, and copper coins used in revolutionary base areas during the Second Revolutionary Civil War. Other cultural relics include photographs of statues of Princess Wencheng and Songtsen Gampo, presented by the Dalai Lama, symbolizing the friendship and unity between the Han and Tibetan peoples, and the "Four Brothers Harmoniously" picture, presented by the Panchen Erdeni, symbolizing the cooperation of all ethnic groups. Among the ethnic minority scripts and documents with academic research value are the hieroglyphs of the Naxi people, the reversed script of the Shui people, the classics of the Tibetan, Yi, and Dai ethnic groups, and rubbings of steles and other materials of great value for studying the history of the Yao and Hui ethnic groups. The Central Institute for Nationalities also preserves more than 500 cultural relics of the Gaoshan people in Taiwan, including the historically significant "Snake Totem Treasure Pot" and some wood carvings.

### 中央民族学院整理各民族文物

1955-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

    中央民族学院整理各民族文物
    中央民族学院搜集了三万多件各民族的文物和图片，现在正进行编目和整理工作。这些文物中，有珍贵的革命文物、手工艺品、服装、文娱用品和生产工具等。在革命文物中，有一九三三年瑶族起义使用过的刀和号衣，有瑶族人民保存下来的“中国工农红军布告”和第二次国内革命战争期间革命根据地所使用的铜币等。其他文物中有达赖喇嘛赠送的象征着汉藏族友好、团结的文成公主和松赞堪布的塑像的照片，班禅额尔德尼赠送的象征各民族合作的“四兄弟和睦图”等。在富有学术研究价值的各民族文字及文献中，有纳西人的象形文字，水家的反书文字，藏族、彝族和傣族的经典，以及研究瑶族、回族历史有很大价值的碑刻拓片等资料。中央民族学院还保存有台湾高山族的文物五百多件，其中有历史悠久的“蛇图腾宝壶”和一些木刻等。  （新华社）
