Title: Indian President Prasad Condemns "Strength" Policy
Author:
Date: 1955-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 21st – According to Indian news agency reports: Indian President Dr. Rajendra Prasad said at a public gathering in Hyderabad on the 19th that some people, disregarding the obvious threat of war, are advocating a policy of strength. But India will strive for peace through practical actions based on the Five Principles.

President Prasad also mentioned India's economic achievements since gaining independence in his speech. He pointed out India's progress in increasing food production, increasing textile exports, and constructing railways and metallurgical factories.

### 印度总统普拉沙德谴责“实力”政策

1955-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德谴责“实力”政策
    新华社新德里二十一日电　据印度新闻处消息：印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士十九日在海得拉巴的一次公众集会上说，有些人不顾明显的战争威胁，正在那里鼓吹实力政策。但印度要通过五项原则用实际行动来谋取和平。
    普拉沙德总统在他的演讲中还提到印度自获得独立以来在经济方面取得的成就。他指出了印度在增加粮食生产、增加纺织品输出以及在铁路和冶金工厂建设方面的进展。
