Title: Austrian Parliament's Two Houses Unanimously Approve Austrian State Treaty; National Council Unanimously Passes Resolution Declaring Austria's Permanent Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 9th - News: Austrian Republic President Körner signed the ratification certificate of the Austrian State Treaty on the afternoon of June 8th, after the Austrian Parliament's two houses approved the Austrian State Treaty.

The National Council and Federal Council of the Austrian Parliament held meetings on the 7th and 8th respectively, discussed the Austrian State Treaty, and unanimously approved the treaty.

At its meeting on the 7th, the National Council also unanimously passed a resolution declaring Austria's permanent neutrality, based on a proposal jointly put forward by the chairmen of the four parliamentary groups.

### 奥地利议会两院一致批准奥地利国家条约  国民院一致通过了宣布奥地利永久中立的决议

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利议会两院一致批准奥地利国家条约
    国民院一致通过了宣布奥地利永久中立的决议
    新华社九日讯　维也纳消息：奥地利共和国总统柯尔纳在奥地利议会两院批准奥地利国家条约后已于六月八日下午签署了这个条约的批准证书。
    奥地利议会国民院和联邦院先后在七日和八日举行会议，讨论了奥地利国家条约，并且一致批准了这个条约。
    国民院在七日的会议上还根据四个议会党团主席联合提出的提案，一致通过了宣布奥地利永久中立的决议。
