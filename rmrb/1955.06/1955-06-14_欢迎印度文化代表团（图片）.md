Title: Welcoming the Indian Cultural Delegation (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-14
Page: 5

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, held a reception on the evening of June 10 to entertain the Indian Cultural Delegation, which was invited to visit our country. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai and the head of the delegation, Anil Kumar Chanda, in a cordial conversation at the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie.

The Indian Cultural Delegation, invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on June 8, led by the head of the delegation, Mr. Anil Kumar Chanda, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of External Affairs of the Republic of India. When the head of the delegation, Chanda (first person on the left in the front row), and the members of the delegation stepped out of the airport, they were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Beijing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie.

Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of June 8 in honor of the Indian Cultural Delegation. The picture shows the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Chanda (left), the famous Chinese Peking Opera actor Mei Lanfang (center), and the Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Qian Junrui (right), at the banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie.

The Indian Cultural Delegation held its first musical and dance performance at the Beijing People's Theater on the evening of June 12. A performance opening ceremony was held before the performance began. The picture shows Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture and President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, delivering a welcome speech on behalf of the Chinese people to the Indian artists at the opening ceremony. Seated on the stage (from left to right): Dr. Ram Marathe, Director and Program Director of All India Radio, Mr. Gurbachan Singh, Official of the Ministry of External Affairs of the Republic of India and Secretary of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Mrs. Raghvan, wife of the Indian Ambassador to China, Mrs. Chanda, wife of the head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Mr. Raghvan, Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. Anil Kumar Chanda, head of the Indian Cultural Delegation and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of External Affairs of the Republic of India, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Chen Jinqing, Deputy Principal of Beijing Dance School, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Mei Lanfang, President of the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera, and Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association. Our reporter Na Yi.

The head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Chanda, his wife, and some members of the delegation visited the Central Academy of Fine Arts on the morning of June 11. The picture shows Jiang Feng, Vice President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts, accompanying the distinguished guests on a visit to the Academy.
                            Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie

Chorus: "Song of Sino-Indian Friendship," "Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and Indian People!" Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue

Bharatanatyam Dance. Performer: Indrani Rehman.
    "Bharatanatyam" is the oldest and purest classical dance in India. This is a solo dance from southern India, performed by women. It begins as a pure dance performance, followed by dramatic expressions. This newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping.

Chen Jinqing (left), Deputy Principal of Beijing Dance School and dancer, female writer Xie Bingxin (center), and Indian dancer, “Bharatanatyam” performer Indrani Rehman (right) have a cordial conversation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue.

Ballet: "The Boon." Performers: Female actor: Shirin Vajifdar, male actor: Krishna Kutty Panikkar. This ballet (Kathakali school) depicts a demon king who receives a terrible boon that allows him to kill anyone he touches on the head. As the demon king wreaks havoc and creates death, the god Vishnu transforms into a beautiful woman, gains the demon king's trust, and causes him to kill himself, thus good triumphs over evil. Our reporter Na Yi.

Punjabi Song. Singers: Deepak and Manohar, Drummer: Banaram, Flutist: Mangal Singh.
    The gist of the lyrics is as follows: Our land is joyful, with five mighty rivers flowing through it; here are beautiful women with hair like dark clouds; and stalwart, brave, and handsome men, eager for adventure, always ready to sacrifice everything for the motherland. This newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping.

The head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Chanda, his wife, and some members of the delegation toured the Forbidden City on June 10. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie.

Kathak Dance. Performer: Arula Da Guha.
    This is a graceful dance from northern India, with elegant and rhythmic footwork, in sync with the drumbeat. The dancer creates rhythmic dance postures, accompanied by musicians. This newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping.

Prayer Song. Singer: De V. Paluskar, Sarangi player: Ram Narayan, Drummer: Munne Khan.
    This is a famous song written four hundred years ago by a female poet, Mira. All her songs are in praise of the pastoral god Krishna. This song says: "Come to the banks of the Ganges and Yamuna, and meet the pastoral god Krishna." Our reporter Na Yi.

Handkerchief Dance. Performers: Members of the Little Ballet Troupe. The Handkerchief Dance is a dance popular among the working people of Gujarat in western India. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue.

### 欢迎印度文化代表团（图片）

1955-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    欢迎印度文化代表团
    周恩来总理在六月十日晚举行酒会招待应邀来我国访问的印度文化代表团。图为周恩来总理同团长阿尼尔·库马尔·钱达在酒会上欢谈。　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    应邀来我国访问的印度文化代表团，在团长、印度共和国外交部副部长阿尼尔·库马尔·钱达先生率领下，六月八日由广州乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长钱达（前排左第一人）和团员等步出机场时，受到北京市各界人民的热烈欢迎。　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞六月八日晚设宴招待印度文化代表团。图为在宴会上的印度文化代表团团长钱达（左）、中国著名京剧演员梅兰芳（中）和文化部代理部长钱俊瑞（右）。　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    印度文化代表团六月十二日晚在北京人民剧场举行首次音乐歌舞表演会，表演会开始之前，举行了演出开幕仪式。图为文化部副部长、中印友好协会会长丁西林在开幕仪式上代表中国人民向印度的艺术家们致词欢迎。坐在台上的（自左至右）：全印广播电台主任兼节目指导拉姆·马拉太博士，印度共和国外交部官员、印度文化代表团秘书古尔巴昌·辛格先生，印度驻我国大使赖嘉文夫人，印度文化代表团团长钱达夫人，印度驻我国大使赖嘉文先生，印度文化代表团团长、印度共和国外交部副部长阿尼尔·库马尔·钱达先生，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，北京舞蹈学校副校长陈锦清，文化部副部长夏衍，中国戏曲研究院院长梅兰芳，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥。　本报记者　纳一摄
    印度文化代表团团长钱达和夫人等一行在六月十一日上午参观和访问了中央美术学院。图为中央美术学院副院长江丰正陪同贵宾们参观美术学院。
                            新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    合唱：印中友好歌、印中人民友好万岁！　新华社记者　吴化学摄
    婆罗多舞（巴拉达那蒂亚舞）　表演者：印德拉尼·拉赫曼。
    “婆罗多”舞是印度最古老最纯粹的古典舞蹈。这是印度南方的一种单人舞，由女子表演。开始时是纯粹的舞蹈表演，接着有戏剧的表情。本报记者　袁毅平摄
    北京舞蹈学校副校长、舞蹈家陈锦清（左）、女作家谢冰心（中）同印度舞蹈家、“巴拉达那蒂亚”舞的表演者印德拉尼·拉赫曼（右）欢谈。　新华社记者　吴化学摄
    巴蕾舞剧：赏赐　表演者：女演员：希林·瓦吉弗达尔，男演员：克里希纳·库蒂·潘尼迦。这一巴蕾舞剧（卡塔卡利舞派），描写一个魔王得到一种可怕的赏赐，使他只要在任何人头顶上一摸，那人就会死去。当这魔王正在进行破坏、制造死亡的时候，毘湿奴神变成美女，取得了魔王的信任，并使他自己杀死自己，于是善良战胜了邪恶。　本报记者　纳一摄
    旁遮普之歌　演唱者：迪帕克和马诺哈尔，击鼓者：巴纳拉姆，吹笛者：曼加尔·辛格。
    歌词大意如下：我们的国土是欢乐的，这里有五条滚滚大河；这里有美丽的妇女，头发像乌云；还有魁梧、勇敢、英俊的男子，他们乐于冒险，随时准备着为祖国牺牲一切。本报记者　袁毅平摄
    印度文化代表团团长钱达和夫人及代表团部分人员六月十日在故宫游览。　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    卡塔克舞　表演者：阿鲁拉达·古哈。
    这是印度北方的一种优美舞蹈，步法婀娜而有节奏，和鼓声合拍。舞蹈者创造着有节奏的舞姿，由音乐家伴奏。本报记者　袁毅平摄
    祈祷之歌　演唱者：德·维·帕卢斯卡尔，萨朗基演奏者：拉姆·纳拉因，击鼓者：蒙内·康恩。
    这是四百年前一位女诗人米拉所作的名歌。她所作的歌曲都是歌颂牧神黑天的。这首歌道：“来到恒河和赞木纳河畔，见见牧神黑天。”　本报记者　纳一摄
    手帕舞　表演者：小巴蕾舞剧团的团员。手帕舞是流行于印度西部古加拉特劳动人民中间的一种舞蹈。新华社记者　吴化学摄
