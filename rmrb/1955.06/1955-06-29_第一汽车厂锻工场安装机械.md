Title: Installation of Machinery in the Forging Workshop of the First Automobile Plant
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 2

    Installation of Machinery in the Forging Workshop of the First Automobile Plant

    The forging workshop of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant began installing machinery on June 1. The forging workshop belongs to the hot processing system of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant and is the sixth workshop in the entire plant to begin installing machinery. The forging workshop includes four sections: flat forging, die forging, spring, and heat treatment, mainly producing crankshafts, connecting rods, and various gears for automobiles. The machinery installed here is mostly the latest high-grade precision machine tools from the Soviet Union, including a flat forging machine for three-inch steel, with a pressure of 500 tons.

### 第一汽车厂锻工场安装机械

1955-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

    第一汽车厂锻工场安装机械
    第一汽车制造厂的锻工场已经在六月一日开始安装机械。锻工场属于第一汽车制造厂热加工系统，是全厂开始安装机械的第六个工场。锻工场包括平锻、模锻、弹璜、热处理等四个部分，主要生产汽车上的曲轴、连杆和各种齿轮。在这里安装的机械，大部分是来自苏联的最新型的高级精装机床，其中平锻三寸钢料的平锻机，压力有五百吨。
