Title: Our Air Force and Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units Shot Down and Damaged Five Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes
Author:
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, June 27th – Our People's Air Force shot down one Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane and damaged two Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes today.

　　At approximately 9:00 AM, a fighter squadron of our People's Air Force was conducting patrol activities over the coastal areas of Zhejiang and Fujian. At 9:07 AM, our aircraft discovered two American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 jet fighters harassing our forces 15 kilometers southeast of the Taishan Islands. Our aircraft immediately engaged in attack. After more than ten minutes of aerial combat, the enemy's lead aircraft was hit and caught fire, the fuselage exploded, and it crashed into the sea 15 kilometers south of the Taishan Islands. The other enemy jet fighter was also seriously damaged by our aircraft and fled south of the Taishan Islands, emitting smoke.

　　At 10:20 AM on the same day, a fighter squadron of our People's Air Force, while conducting patrol activities in the airspace over the sea near the Matsu Islands, discovered an American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit PBY aircraft intruding into our coastal area from the airspace 10 kilometers east of the Matsu Islands. Our aircraft immediately attacked the enemy aircraft. After being severely damaged by our aircraft, this enemy aircraft was forced to land on the northeast beach near the lighthouse on Dongquan Island.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, June 27th – The anti-aircraft artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front damaged two Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes on the 23rd and 24th.

　　At 9:54 AM on the 24th, two Chiang Kai-shek bandit T-33 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Fuzhou City. Our anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire fiercely, hitting and damaging one of them.

　　At 10:18 AM on the 23rd, two Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 aircraft invaded the airspace over Fuzhou City, and one of them was damaged by our anti-aircraft artillery units.

### 我空军和高射炮部队击落击伤蒋贼飞机五架

1955-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军和高射炮部队击落击伤蒋贼飞机五架
    新华社福建前线二十七日电　我人民空军今日击落蒋贼飞机一架，击伤蒋贼飞机二架。
    上午九时许，我人民空军某部歼击机队在浙江和福建沿海一带上空进行巡逻活动。九时零七分，我机在台山列岛东南十五公里处发现有美制蒋贼F—八四型喷气机二架正向我进行骚扰活动。我机当即进行攻击，空战十多分钟，敌机长机被我击中起火，机身爆炸，落入台山列岛以南十五公里的海中。另一架敌喷气机也被我机击成重伤，冒着烟向台山列岛以南逃去。
      同日十时二十多分，我人民空军某部歼击机队在马祖岛附近海面上空进行巡逻活动时，发现美制蒋贼PBY型飞机一架由马祖岛以东十公里处海面上空向我沿海地区进扰。我机当即向敌机进行攻击。这一架敌机被我机击成重伤后，被迫降落在东犬岛东北沙滩上灯塔附近。
    新华社福建前线二十七日电　福建前线中国人民解放军高射炮部队二十三、二十四两日击伤蒋贼飞机两架。
    二十四日九时五十四分，蒋贼T—三三型飞机两架窜扰到福州市上空，我高射炮部队当即猛烈开火，打伤其中一架。
    二十三日十时十八分，蒋贼F—八四型飞机两架侵入福州市上空，被我高射炮部队击伤其中一架。
