Title: U.S. Prepares to Intensify Production of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 10th: The testimonies of Lewis Strauss, Chairman of the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission, and other officials before the House Appropriations Subcommittee, published on the 8th, indicate that the United States is preparing to intensify the production of atomic weapons. They said that the Atomic Energy Commission is requesting the House Appropriations Subcommittee to approve a 30 percent increase in funding for atomic weapons for the next fiscal year, beginning July 1st. A memorandum from the Atomic Energy Commission stated that the proposed increase in weapons funding is due to the need to increase the production quantity of weapons. Strauss said in his testimony that the Atomic Energy Commission plans to increase the stockpile of various types of atomic weapons.

Strauss also said that despite objections from many countries, the United States will continue to conduct atomic weapons tests in the Pacific.

### 美国准备加紧制造原子武器

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国准备加紧制造原子武器
    新华社十日讯　华盛顿消息：八日公布的美国政府原子能委员会主席斯特劳斯和其他官员在众议院拨款小组委员会会议上的证词说明，美国正在准备加紧制造原子武器。他们说，原子能委员会要求众议院拨款小组委员会同意把今年七月一日开始的下一财政年度的原子武器经费增加百分之三十。原子能委员会的备忘录中说，建议增加武器经费，是由于要增加武器的生产数量。斯特劳斯在他的证词中说，原子能委员会计划增加储存各种各样的原子武器。
    斯特劳斯还说，尽管有许多国家表示反对，美国仍将继续在太平洋进行原子武器试验。
