Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Paris
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 1st. The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to participate in the Second International Theatre Festival in Paris, arrived in Paris by train at 7:00 PM on May 31st, led by the head of the troupe, Zhang Zhixiang, and the deputy head, Zhao Feng.
    Those who welcomed the troupe at the station included: Mr. Yu Lian, Director of the Second International Theatre Festival; Mr. Fosinard, Member of the French National Assembly and former Minister; Mr. Grenier, Member of the National Assembly; Madame Gaby Morlay, Chairwoman of the National Actors' Union; Professor Deléhelle, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the France-China Friendship Association; the widow of the late famous poet Paul Éluard; Madame Plévenon, Principal of the Sèvres Higher Normal School for Girls; Jacques Mayoux, Professor of Literature at the University of Paris; Soria, President of the Paris Literary and Artistic Society; Val, General Manager of "Chant du Monde" record company; and representatives from Pathé Records.
    The art troupe received a warm welcome at the station and accepted bouquets of flowers. Head of the troupe Zhang Zhixiang addressed the welcomers, expressing his belief that the Chinese Art Troupe's visit would contribute to mutual understanding between the peoples of China and France, and to friendly relations between the cultural and artistic circles of the two countries.

### 中国艺术团到达巴黎

1955-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中国艺术团到达巴黎
    新华社布拉格一日电　应邀参加巴黎第二届国际戏剧节演出的中国艺术团在团长张致祥、副团长赵沨率领下于五月三十一日下午七时乘车到达巴黎。
    到车站欢迎的有：第二届国际戏剧节主任余立安，法国国民议会议员、前部长弗西纳尔，国民议会议员格雷尼埃，全国演员工会主席盖比·摩雷夫人，法中友好协会执行委员会主席德莱希教授，已故名诗人埃吕阿的夫人，赛佛女子高级师范学校校长普勒农夫人，巴黎大学文学教授雅克·麦尤，巴黎文学艺术社社长索里亚，“世界之歌”唱片公司总经理瓦尔和百代唱片公司代表等。
    艺术团在车站上受到热烈欢迎，并且接受了献花。张致祥团长向欢迎者致词，表示相信中国艺术团的访问将有助于中法两国人民间的相互了解和两国文化艺术界之间的友好联系。
