Title: The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam Issues Appeal to the People, Supporting the Vietnamese Government's Statement on Holding Consultative Conferences; Vietnam Lien Viet Front Issues Statement Calling on All Vietnamese People to Respond to Government Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th. The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued an appeal to the people on June 7th, supporting the statement issued by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on June 6th regarding preparations to hold consultative conferences with representative responsible authorities in South Vietnam from July 20th, 1955, to discuss the issue of free general elections.

The appeal to the people stated that this statement by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has received enthusiastic approval and support from the Vietnamese people. This is the fervent desire of the Vietnamese people from North to South, regardless of social class or religious belief. It is also the desire of peace-loving people in all countries around the world. The National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, elected by all the people from North to South and symbolizing the iron will of all the people to strive for unification, has consistently supported this position of striving for unification and will earnestly realize the people's wishes.

The National Assembly earnestly calls on representatives and people of all strata throughout the country to unite as one and work hand in hand to wage a resolute struggle to ensure that the general elections are held as scheduled on the date stipulated in the Geneva Accords.

The appeal to the people stated that at present, the American imperialists are plotting to permanently divide Vietnam, turning South Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos into military bases to provoke a new war. The appeal to the people calls on the people of the whole country to maintain vigilance and wage a resolute struggle to crush all treacherous plots.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th. The Vietnam Lien Viet Front issued a statement on June 7th, expressing support for the statement by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam regarding preparations to hold consultative conferences with the South Vietnamese authorities to discuss the issue of free general elections.

The statement said that in view of the sacred and supreme interests of the entire nation and in order to safeguard world peace, the Vietnam Lien Viet Front requests that the representative responsible authorities in South Vietnam send their representatives to contact the representatives of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and prepare to hold consultative conferences from July 20th, 1955, to discuss the issue of unifying Vietnam through free general elections held throughout the country.

The statement called on people of all strata in the northern and southern parts of the country and overseas Vietnamese, regardless of age, gender, religious belief, ethnicity, or political affiliation, to respond to the statement of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The statement called on the people to work to restore normal relations between North and South Vietnam and to create conditions for people from mass organizations or social strata in the two regions to make contact and exchange views.

The statement said that the Vietnam Lien Viet Front is deeply convinced that the just struggle of the Vietnamese people will receive the active support of the people of fraternal countries; the Vietnam Lien Viet Front calls on the French people and all peace-loving people around the world to continue to support the Vietnamese people's struggle for holding consultative conferences, for holding free general elections in accordance with the provisions of the Geneva Accords, and for achieving the unification of Vietnam.

The statement was signed by the Vietnam Lien Viet Front and the political parties and people's organizations belonging to the Lien Viet Front, including the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnam Democratic Party, the Vietnam Socialist Party, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Vietnam National Farmers Liaison Committee, the Vietnam Youth League, the Vietnam Women's Union, the Vietnam Association of Literary and Art Workers, and the Vietnam Journalists Association, and others.

### 越南国民大会常务委员会发表告人民书  支持越南政府关于举行协商会议的声明  越联战线发表声明号召全越人民响应政府声明

1955-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会常务委员会发表告人民书
    支持越南政府关于举行协商会议的声明
    越联战线发表声明号召全越人民响应政府声明
    新华社河内八日电　越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会在六月七日发表告人民书，支持越南民主共和国政府在六月六日发表的关于准备从一九五五年七月二十日起同南越有代表性的负责当局举行协商会议以讨论自由普选问题的声明。
    告人民书说，越南民主共和国政府的这个声明，受到了越南人民的热烈赞同和拥护。这是从北到南不分社会阶层或宗教信仰的越南人民的热切愿望。这也是世界各国爱好和平的人民的愿望。由从北到南的全体人民选出、象征着全体人民争取统一的钢铁意志的越南民主共和国国民大会，一贯地拥护这一争取统一的立场，并且将认真地实现人民的愿望。
    国民大会热切地号召全国各地的代表们和各阶层人民团结一致，携手为争取在日内瓦协定规定的日期如期举行普选而进行坚决的斗争。
    告人民书说，目前，美帝国主义者正在阴谋永远分裂越南，把南越、柬埔寨和老挝变成军事基地以挑起新的战争。告人民书号召全国人民保持警惕并且进行坚决的斗争来粉碎一切背信弃义的图谋。
    新华社河内八日电　越南国民联合战线在六月七日发表声明，表示支持越南民主共和国政府关于准备同南越当局举行协商会议以讨论自由普选问题的声明。
    声明说，鉴于全国人民神圣的和最高的利益，为了维护世界和平，越南国民联合战线要求南越有代表性的负责当局派遣他们的代表同越南民主共和国政府的代表进行接触，准备从一九五五年七月二十日起举行协商会议来讨论通过在全国举行自由普选的方式统一越南的问题。
    声明号召国内北部和南部各个不同阶层的人民和在国外的越侨不分年龄、性别、宗教信仰、民族和政治关系，响应越南民主共和国政府的声明。声明号召人民为恢复北越和南越间的正常关系而努力，并且为两个地区各群众组织或各社会阶层的人民进行接触和交换意见创造条件。
    声明说，越南国民联合战线深信越南人民的正义斗争将会得到各兄弟国家人民的积极支持；越南国民联合战线号召法国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民继续支持越南人民争取举行协商会议、争取根据日内瓦协定的规定举行自由普选和实现越南统一的斗争。
    在这个声明上签字的有越南国民联合战线和属于国民联合战线的各政党和人民团体，其中包括越南劳动党、越南民主党、越南社会党、越南总工会、越南全国农民联络委员会、越南青年联盟、越南妇女联合会、越南文学艺术工作者协会和越南新闻记者协会等。
