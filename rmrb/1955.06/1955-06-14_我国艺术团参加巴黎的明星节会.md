Title: Our Country's Art Troupe Participates in the Paris Festival of Stars
Author:
Date: 1955-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 13th: A Paris dispatch reports that the Chinese Art Troupe participated in the Paris Festival of Stars on the afternoon of the 11th.

French President Coty and Premier Faure attended the Festival of Stars. The Chinese Art Troupe was introduced to President Coty first.

Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, on behalf of all members, thanked President Coty for the friendly reception they received during their performance in France. He also expressed the wish to enhance the friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and French people.

President Coty expressed his great pleasure at the Chinese Art Troupe's participation in this grand festival.

### 我国艺术团参加巴黎的明星节会

1955-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团参加巴黎的明星节会
    新华社布拉格十三日电　巴黎消息：中国艺术团十一日下午参加了巴黎的明星节会。
    法国总统戈蒂和总理富尔出席了明星节会。中国艺术团被首先介绍给戈蒂总统。
    中国艺术团团长张致祥代表全体团员对这次在法国演出得到友好的接待向戈蒂总统致谢。他并且表示了增进中法两国人民友谊和文化交流的愿望。
    戈蒂总统对中国艺术团参加这次盛大节会表示非常高兴。
