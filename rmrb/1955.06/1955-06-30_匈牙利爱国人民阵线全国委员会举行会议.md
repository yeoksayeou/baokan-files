Title: Hungarian Patriotic People's Front National Council Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front held a meeting on the 24th.

Mátyás Rákosi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, attended the meeting.

The Secretary-General of the National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front, Ernő Mihályfi, delivered a report on the activities of the Patriotic People's Front since its congress in October 1954 and its future tasks. He said that the resolutions of the Hungarian Working People's Party in March and April of this year had stopped the right-leaning errors. According to the decision of the Presidium of the National Council of the Patriotic People's Front, we have begun to eliminate these errors. Since the congress, the concept of uniting all progressive and democratic forces has been increasingly strengthened. New forces have been mobilized to actively participate in political and social life.

After discussion, the meeting adopted his report.

### 匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会举行会议

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会举行会议
    据新华社讯　匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会于二十四日举行会议。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会书记马·拉科西出席了这次会议。
    匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会秘书长埃诺·米哈伊费就爱国人民阵线自一九五四年十月举行大会以来的活动和它今后的任务作了报告。他说，劳动人民党今年三月和四月的决议制止了右倾的错误。根据爱国人民阵线全国委员会主席团的决定，我们已经开始清除错误。自从代表大会以后，团结一切进步和民主力量的观念已愈来愈加强。已经动员了新的力量积极参加政治和社会生活。
    会议经过讨论后通过了他的报告。
