Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and Yugoslav President Tito Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, June 11th: TASS Belgrade, June 11th dispatch: According to an official communique released in Belgrade, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, who is visiting Yugoslavia, and President Josip Broz Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, began talks on June 10th.

### 缅甸总理吴努和南斯拉夫总统铁托会谈

1955-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努和南斯拉夫总统铁托会谈
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德十一日电：据贝尔格莱德发表的官方公报说，访问南斯拉夫的缅甸联邦总理吴努和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统约瑟夫·布罗兹·铁托在六月十日开始了会谈。
