Title: Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Visit China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-17
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will send a government delegation to visit our country.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 16th (TASS): The Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will arrive in Moscow in the first half of July. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union officially at the invitation of the Soviet government.

### 越南民主共和国政府代表团将访问中苏两国

1955-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府代表团将访问中苏两国
    新华社十六日讯　越南民主共和国政府应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，将派政府代表团来我国访问。
    新华社十六日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：越南民主共和国政府代表团将在七月上半月到莫斯科。代表团是应苏联政府的邀请到苏联作正式访问的。
