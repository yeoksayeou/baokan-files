Title: Heads of China-India, China-Myanmar Friendship Associations Send Congratulatory Messages to Heads of India-China, Myanmar-China Friendship Associations on the Anniversary of the China-India, China-Myanmar Prime Ministers' Joint Statements

Author: 
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: Heads of China-India, China-Myanmar Friendship Associations Send Congratulatory Messages to Heads of India-China, Myanmar-China Friendship Associations on the Anniversary of the China-India, China-Myanmar Prime Ministers' Joint Statements

    The full text of the congratulatory telegram from Ding Xilin, President of the China-India Friendship Association, and Chen Hansheng, Vice President, to the India-China Friendship Association is as follows:
India
New Delhi
Mr. Sundarlal, Dr. Gyan Chand, Dr. Panikkar, India-China Friendship Association:

    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of China and India, the Chinese people express their warm congratulations. The Five Principles enunciated in the joint statement provide a basis for peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems and have been praised and supported by an increasing number of peace-loving countries and peoples. The China-India Friendship Association is holding a celebration meeting in Beijing on June 27th. We hereby send this telegram to inform you and wish further development of friendly cooperation between China and India.

                                    Ding Xilin, President of the China-India Friendship Association
                                              Chen Hansheng, Vice President
　June 27, 1955

    Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: The full text of the congratulatory telegram from Zheng Zhenduo, President of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, and Qian Weichang, Vice President, to the Myanmar-China Friendship Association is as follows:
Myanmar
Yangon
Dr. Tekin Kodomae, Dr. Xinbo, Myanmar-China Friendship Association:

    It has been one year since the Prime Ministers of China and Myanmar issued a joint statement. The Five Principles enunciated in the joint statement are not only supported by the people of China and Myanmar but are also considered by more and more countries and peoples in the world as the basic principles guiding international relations. The China-Myanmar Friendship Association is holding a celebration meeting in Beijing on June 27th. We hereby send this telegram to inform you and wish the friendly cooperation between China and Myanmar will develop day by day.

                            Zheng Zhenduo, President of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association
                                      Qian Weichang, Vice President
　June 27, 1955

### 中印、中缅友协负责人致电印中、缅中友协负责人  庆贺中印、中缅总理发表联合声明周年

1955-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中印、中缅友协负责人致电印中、缅中友协负责人
    庆贺中印、中缅总理发表联合声明周年
    新华社二十七日讯　中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙致印中友好协会贺电全文如下：印度  新德里  印中友好协会森德拉尔先生、吉安·昌德博士、潘尼迦博士：
    欣逢中印两国总理发表联合声明一周年，我国人民表示热烈庆贺。联合声明所揭示的五项原则，为不同社会制度的国家之间的和平共处提供了基础，受到了愈来愈多的爱好和平的国家和人民的赞许和支持。中印友好协会特于六月二十七日在北京举行庆祝大会，谨电奉告，并祝中印两国之间的友好合作得到进一步的发展。
                                    中印友好协会会长　丁西林
                                              副会长　陈翰笙
　一九五五年六月二十七日
    新华社二十七日讯　中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长致缅中友好协会贺电全文如下：缅甸　仰光　缅中友好协会德钦哥都迈博士、信波博士：
    中缅两国总理发表联合声明，已届一年。联合声明所揭示的五项原则，不仅为中缅两国人民所拥护，而且被世界上越来越多的国家和人民认为是指导国际间关系的基本原则。中缅友好协会特于六月二十七日在北京举行庆祝大会，谨此电告，并祝中缅两国友好合作日益发展。
                            中缅友好协会会长　郑振铎
                                      副会长　钱伟长
　一九五五年六月二十七日
