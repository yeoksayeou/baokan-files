Title: Photo Exhibition of My Country Held in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency: A photo exhibition entitled "Achievements of New China on the Socialist Road," sponsored by the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture, recently opened in Stalin Park, Bucharest. The photos on display illustrate the work and life of the Chinese working people.

### 布加勒斯特举办我图片展览会

1955-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    布加勒斯特举办我图片展览会
    据新华社讯罗马尼亚对外文化协会主办的“新中国沿着社会主义道路前进中的成就”图片展览会最近在布加勒斯特斯大林公园内开幕。展览的图片说明了中国劳动人民的工作和生活情况。
