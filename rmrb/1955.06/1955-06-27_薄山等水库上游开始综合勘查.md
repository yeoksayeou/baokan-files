Title: Comprehensive Survey of the Upper Reaches of the Boshan and Other Reservoirs Begins
Author: Wang Bingwen
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 2

To prevent soil erosion in the upper reaches of the Huai River and prolong the lifespan of the completed water conservancy projects, the Henan Provincial People's Committee dispatched a large number of technical personnel in April to organize the Henan Provincial Reservoir Upper Reaches Comprehensive Survey Team. The team will conduct comprehensive surveys and planning in the upper reaches of the Baisha, Boshan, Shimantan, and Banqiao reservoirs. At present, the survey team has completed the comprehensive survey of 850 square kilometers in the upper reaches of the Baisha Reservoir. The team is carrying out an overall basin plan, and will propose the first phase of work volume and the implementation plan for the entire basin, prioritizing urgent work. In late June, the survey work of the upper reaches of the Boshan Reservoir will begin. It is expected that the survey of all 2,700-plus square kilometers in the upper reaches of the four reservoirs will be completed before the end of the year.

### 薄山等水库上游开始综合勘查
王炳文
1955-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

    薄山等水库上游开始综合勘查
    为了防止淮河上游水土流失，延长已建成的水利工程的寿命，四月份，河南省人民委员会抽调大批技术人员，组织了河南省水库上游综合性勘查队，前往已建成的白沙、薄山、石漫滩和板桥四个水库上游进行综合性的勘查及规划工作。目前，勘查队已完成了白沙水库上游八百五十平方公里的综合性勘查工作，全队正在进行流域性总体规划，并将在规划中提出第一期工作量和整个流域先后缓急工作实施计划，六月下旬开始薄山水库上游的勘查工作。预计在年底以前，将全部完成四个水库上游二千七百多平方里面积的勘查任务。（王炳文）
