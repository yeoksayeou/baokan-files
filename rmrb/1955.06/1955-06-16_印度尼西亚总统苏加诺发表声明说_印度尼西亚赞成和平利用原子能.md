Title: Indonesian President Sukarno Issues Statement Saying Indonesia Supports the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 14th (TASS): President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia issued a statement on June 14th. The statement said that Indonesia supports the peaceful use of atomic energy. Sukarno said: "All mankind expresses its protest against the use of atomic bombs, we support such protests, and we fervently hope that atomic energy will be used for peaceful purposes."
    It is reported that Indonesia has expressed its agreement to participate in the international conference on the peaceful use of atomic energy.

### 印度尼西亚总统苏加诺发表声明说  印度尼西亚赞成和平利用原子能

1955-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺发表声明说
    印度尼西亚赞成和平利用原子能
    新华社十四日讯　塔斯社雅加达十四日讯：印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺十四日发表声明。声明中说，印度尼西亚赞成和平利用原子能。苏加诺说：“全人类表示抗议使用原子弹，我们支持这样的抗议，我们热烈希望：原子能用于和平用途。”
    据悉，印度尼西亚表示同意参加和平利用原子能国际会议。
