Title: To Commemorate the Fallen Soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, the Korean Cabinet Passes a Decision to Build a China-Korea Martyrs Memorial Tower
Author:
Date: 1955-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 25th: On the fifth anniversary of the June 25th Day of Struggle Against American Imperialism, the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea passed a decision to build memorial towers to commemorate the fallen soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in the righteous war. The memorial tower for the Korean People's Army will be built on Liberation Hill in Pyongyang, and the memorial tower for the Chinese People's Volunteers will be built on the northern slope of Moranbong Peak in Pyongyang.

Construction of the memorial towers began on June 25, 1955, and will be completed on June 25, 1956.

### 为纪念朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在抗美战争中的阵亡将士  朝鲜内阁通过决定建立中朝烈士纪念塔

1955-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    为纪念朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在抗美战争中的阵亡将士
    朝鲜内阁通过决定建立中朝烈士纪念塔
    新华社平壤二十五日电　在六月二十五日对美帝国主义者斗争日第五周年的时候，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁通过了决定，要建立纪念塔来纪念朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在正义战争中的阵亡将士。朝鲜人民军的纪念塔将建立在平壤解放山上，中国人民志愿军的纪念塔将建立在平壤牡丹峰的北面山坡上。
    纪念塔在一九五五年六月二十五日开始施工，将在一九五六年六月二十五日竣工。
