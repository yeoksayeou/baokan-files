Our Great Motherland
While the rice paddies in northern China are still lush green, the early rice crop has already been harvested in the Hainan Island area in the south. Members of the Tongfeng Agricultural Production Cooperative in the eastern suburbs of Haikou City are happily threshing the grain with threshing machines.
