Title: The Soviet Union Signs Agreements with Hungary and Bulgaria on Aiding the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 15th: According to TASS, delegations of prominent scientists and engineers from the Hungarian People's Republic and the Bulgarian People's Republic arrived in Moscow at the end of May to hold talks with Soviet scientists representing the Soviet Union on the issue of Soviet assistance to Hungary and Bulgaria in the peaceful use of atomic energy.

During the talks, scientists from the Soviet Union, Hungary, and Bulgaria discussed the design, production, and supply of experimental atomic reactors and basic particle accelerator equipment, as well as issues related to training personnel and organizing scientific research in these countries in the fields of nuclear physics and the use of radioactive isotopes in medicine, biology, and other scientific and technological sectors.

As a result of the talks, agreements were signed. The agreements stipulate that relevant Soviet institutions will be responsible for completing design work for the Hungarian People's Republic and the Bulgarian People's Republic, as well as supplying equipment for experimental atomic reactors and basic particle accelerators, providing necessary scientific and technical data on atomic reactors and particle accelerators free of charge, providing technical assistance in the installation and use of these devices by dispatching Soviet experts, and supplying the necessary quantity of fissionable materials and other materials required for atomic reactors and research work. In addition, the Soviet Union will train experts sent by Hungary and Bulgaria to become scientific workers and engineers in the fields of nuclear physics, radiochemistry, the use of isotopes in science and technology, and atomic reactor technology.

During their stay in the Soviet Union, the Hungarian and Bulgarian delegations visited scientific institutions in Moscow, Leningrad, and other cities that are researching the peaceful use of atomic energy, as well as experimental atomic reactors, basic particle accelerators, and atomic power plants that are in operation.

### 就帮助和平使用原子能问题  苏联同匈牙利保加利亚两国签订协定

1955-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    就帮助和平使用原子能问题
    苏联同匈牙利保加利亚两国签订协定
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：由匈牙利人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国的著名科学家和工程师组成的代表团，于五月底来到莫斯科，就苏联帮助匈、保两国和平使用原子能问题，同代表苏联方面的苏联科学家进行了会谈。
    在会谈过程中，苏、匈、保三国科学家讨论了实验性原子堆和基本粒子加速器设备的设计、生产和供应的问题，以及有关培养干部问题、有关在这些国家内组织核子物理方面和在医学、生物学和其他科学技术部门使用放射性同位素的科学研究工作的问题。
    会谈结果，签订了协定。协定规定，苏联有关机构将负责为匈牙利人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国完成设计工作，以及供应实验性原子堆和基本粒子加速器的设备，无代价地提供关于原子堆和粒子加速器的必要的科学技术资料，通过派遣苏联专家的办法在这些设备的安装和使用方面给予技术帮助，供应原子堆和研究工作所需的必要数量的可分裂物质和其他物质。此外，苏联将把匈、保两国派来的有关专家培养成为核子物理方面、放射化学方面、在科学和技术中使用同位素方面以及原子堆技术方面的科学工作人员和工程师。
    匈、保两国代表团在苏联期间，曾参观了莫斯科、列宁格勒和其他城市的正在研究和平使用原子能的科学机关，以及正在工作中的实验性原子堆、基本粒子加速器和原子能发电站。
