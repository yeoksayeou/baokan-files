Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Korean Education Delegation; Korean Education Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th. Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received the Korean Education Delegation, led by Li Le-yan, Vice Minister of Education of Korea, and all members of the delegation on the morning of the 20th. The delegation was in China for a visit according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; Sun Wenshu, Assistant Minister of Education; and Ai Dayan, Director of the Department of Student Affairs of the Ministry of Higher Education.

Also present at the reception was the Korean Ambassador to China, Choi Il.

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th. The Education Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Li Le-yan, Vice Minister of Education of Korea, consisting of fifteen members, who came to China according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by train on the 20th to return home after a visit of more than a month in our country.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Wei Que, Vice Minister; Sun Wenshu, Assistant Minister; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Huang Songling, Vice Minister; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; and Weng Dujian, Director of the Beijing Education Bureau, and others.

Also present at the station to see them off were Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff.

During its stay in Beijing, the delegation had extensive contact with people from educational circles in our country, and they exchanged information and experience on the educational work of the two countries. The delegation also visited schools, scientific research institutions, factories, and construction sites in Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Changsha, and Wuhan, and toured scenic spots and historical sites in various places. The delegation visited Chairman Mao Zedong's hometown—Shaoshan Village, Xiangtan County, Hunan Province, and the First Normal School of Changsha, Qingshuitang, etc., with great enthusiasm. When the delegation arrived in Nanjing, it laid wreaths at the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Memorial Tower and the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to pay tribute.

The delegation also exchanged gifts with the Ministry of Higher Education and the Ministry of Education of our country. Among the gifts they presented was a banner from the teachers of Pyongyang to the teachers of Beijing, and the teachers of Beijing sent two boxes of self-made instruments to the educational circles of Pyongyang.

During the delegation's stay in Beijing, Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education, and Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, respectively held welcome and farewell banquets to entertain all members of the delegation.

On their way home, the delegation will also visit Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan, and other places.

### 陈毅副总理接见朝鲜教育考察团  朝鲜教育考察团离北京回国

1955-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见朝鲜教育考察团
    朝鲜教育考察团离北京回国
    新华社二十日讯国务院副总理陈毅在二十日上午接见了根据中朝文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的朝鲜教育考察团团长、朝鲜教育省副相李乐彦和代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有教育部部长张奚若、高等教育部部长杨秀峰、对外文化联络局局长洪深、外交部部长助理陈家康、教育部部长助理孙文淑、高等教育部留学生管理司司长艾大炎。
    接见时在座的还有朝鲜驻华大使崔一。
    新华社二十日讯根据中朝文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国的以朝鲜教育省副相李乐彦为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育考察团一行十五人，在我国进行了为期一个多月的参观访问后，在二十日乘火车离京返国。
    前往车站欢送的有教育部部长张奚若、副部长韦悫、部长助理孙文淑，高等教育部部长杨秀峰、副部长黄松龄，对外文化联络局局长洪深，北京市教育局局长翁独健等。
    前往车站欢送的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一和使馆人员。
    考察团在北京期间，曾和我国的教育界人士作了广泛的接触，互相介绍了两国教育工作的情况和经验。考察团并曾在北京、南京、上海、杭州、长沙和武汉等城市参观了学校、科学研究机关和工厂、工地，并游览了各地的名胜古迹。考察团以极大的热情访问了毛泽东主席的故乡——湖南省湘潭县韶山村和长沙第一师范学校、清水塘等处。考察团到南京时，曾到雨花台革命烈士纪念塔和中山陵献花圈致敬。
    考察团并曾和我国高等教育部、教育部互赠礼品。他们所赠礼品中有平壤市教师赠给北京市教师们的锦旗一面，北京市教师把自制的仪器两箱送给平壤市教育界。
    考察团在京期间，教育部部长张奚若和高等教育部部长杨秀峰，曾分别举行欢迎、欢送宴会招待考察团全体人员。
    考察团在回国途中，还要到天津、沈阳、鞍山等地参观访问。
