Title: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairmen of the Two Chambers Separately Receive and Host the Swedish Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, received the Swedish Parliamentary Delegation on the 13th, which came to visit the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

Voroshilov delivered a speech welcoming the delegation. He said: I am convinced that your visit to the Soviet Union will further consolidate the friendly relations between our two peoples and will contribute to the cause of maintaining peace.

The head of the Swedish Parliamentary Delegation, Speaker of the Swedish Lower House, Nilsson, delivered a reply.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Volkov, and Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Lacis, held a reception on the 13th to host the Swedish Parliamentary Delegation.

Present at the reception were Mikoyan, Pervukhin, and Saburov, Deputy Chairmen of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kuratov and Tarasov, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Pankratova, Chairmen of the Standing Committees of the two chambers of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Ministers of various ministries of the Soviet Union, Marshals of the Soviet Union, and Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Begov, etc.

Also present at the reception was the Swedish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Sohlman.

Volkov and the head of the Swedish Parliamentary Delegation, Nilsson, spoke at the reception.

Volkov said that your visit to our Soviet Union is a significant event. I am convinced that your visit to our country will help consolidate the good-neighborly relations between Sweden and the Soviet Union, and develop and deepen the friendship and mutual respect between our two peoples. During your short visit to the Soviet Union, you have the opportunity to see with your own eyes that our Soviet people are engaged in peaceful construction work, and they hope to live a peaceful and harmonious life together with the people of all countries, including our neighbor, the Swedish people.

Nilsson spoke about the impressions of the Swedish delegation's visit to the Soviet Union. He said that the Soviet Union is rapidly advancing due to the people's love of labor. Everywhere we look, we see evidence that the Soviet people are working hard to strive for security, oppose new wars, and guarantee lasting peace. Nilsson said that we are convinced that the people of all countries will be able to achieve mutual understanding, full trust, and cooperation in the future. The great Soviet people can make great contributions to ensuring such an ideal future for all mankind.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席和两院主席  分别接见和招待瑞典议会代表团

1955-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席和两院主席
    分别接见和招待瑞典议会代表团
    据新华社讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，十三日接见了应苏联最高苏维埃邀请前来苏联访问的瑞典议会代表团。
    伏罗希洛夫致词欢迎代表团。他说：我深信，你们的访问苏联将更加巩固我们两国人民的友好关系，将有助于维护和平的事业。
    瑞典议会代表团团长、瑞典下议院议长尼尔逊致答词。
    据新华社讯　苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯十三日举行招待会，招待瑞典议会代表团。
    出席招待会的有米高扬、别尔乌辛和萨布罗夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席库拉托夫和塔拉索夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员潘克拉托娃，苏联最高苏维埃两院各常设委员会主席，苏联各部部长，苏联元帅，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫等。
    出席招待会的还有瑞典驻苏联大使索尔曼。
    沃尔科夫和瑞典议会代表团团长尼尔逊先后在招待会上讲了话。
    沃尔科夫说，你们到我们苏联来访问是一件意义重大的事件。我深信，你们的访问我国有助于巩固瑞典和苏联之间的善邻关系，发展和加深我们两国人民之间的友谊和相互尊重。你们在访问苏联的短短时期内，有机会亲眼看到了我们苏联人民正在从事和平的建设劳动，他们希望同各国人民，包括我们的邻国瑞典人民在内，一道过和平和和睦的生活。
    尼尔逊谈到了瑞典代表团访问苏联的观感。他说，苏联由于人民的热爱劳动正在迅速前进。我们到处看到的东西都证明，苏联人民在努力争取安全、反对新战争和保障持久和平。尼尔逊说，我们深信各国人民将来必定能够实现互相谅解、充分信任和合作。伟大的苏联人民能够作出伟大的贡献，保障全人类获得这样一种理想的前途。
