Title: Khrushchev, Bulganin, and Others Arrive in Sofia for Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 3rd: According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, Sofia, Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, who participated in the Soviet-Yugoslav talks in Belgrade, arrived in Sofia, the capital of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, on the 3rd for a friendly visit.

### 赫鲁晓夫布尔加宁等到达索非亚访问

1955-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫布尔加宁等到达索非亚访问
    新华社三日讯　据保加利亚通讯社索非亚讯：参加贝尔格莱德苏联和南斯拉夫会谈的苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，在三日到达保加利亚人民共和国首都索非亚作友好访问。
