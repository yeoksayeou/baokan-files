Title: Christian Democrat Segni of Italy Mandated to Form Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1955-06-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, June 27th. TASS reported from Rome on June 26th that, according to the Ansa news agency, Italian President Gronchi received Christian Democrat Segni on the evening of the 26th and entrusted him with the task of forming a new government to replace the outgoing government of Scelba. Segni accepted the President's mandate and promised to present his opinion before June 30th after consulting with various political parties.

### 意天主教民主党人塞尼受命组阁

1955-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    意天主教民主党人塞尼受命组阁
    新华社二十八日讯　塔斯社罗马二十七日讯：据安莎通讯社报道，意大利共和国总统格隆基二十六日晚接见天主教民主党人塞尼并委托他组织新政府以代替已经辞职的谢尔巴政府。塞尼接受了总统的委托，并且答应在同各政党协商后在六月三十日以前提出自己的意见书。
