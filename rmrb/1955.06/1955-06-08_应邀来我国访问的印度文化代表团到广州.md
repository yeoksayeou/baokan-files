Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou on Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 7th: The Indian Cultural Delegation, consisting of 54 members, led by Anil Kumar Chanda, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of External Affairs of the Republic of India, arrived in Guangzhou by special train at 4:40 PM this afternoon, upon invitation to visit China.

    Zhu Dan, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of China, went to Shenzhen Railway Station to welcome the delegation.

    Upon the delegation's arrival in Guangzhou, more than 1,000 people welcomed them at the station, including Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the People's Committee of Guangdong Province, Sun Leyi, Secretary-General of the People's Committee of Guangzhou, and representatives from cultural and artistic circles. Members of the delegation received flowers offered by the welcoming crowd as they stepped off the train.

    At 7:40 PM, Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, received Anil Kumar Chanda, head of the delegation, and his wife at the Yingbin Hotel. Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, accepted the eight mango trees presented by Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of China, and expressed his gratitude.

    At 8:00 PM, all members of the delegation attended a banquet given by Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou.

### 应邀来我国访问的印度文化代表团到广州

1955-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问的印度文化代表团到广州
    新华社广州七日电　应邀来我国访问的印度文化代表团一行五十四人，在团长、印度共和国外交部副部长阿尼尔·库马尔·钱达率领下，七日下午四时四十分乘专车到达广州。
    我国文化部艺术事业管理局副局长朱丹曾前往深圳车站迎接代表团。
    当代表团到达广州时，在车站上欢迎的有广州市市长朱光、广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗、广州市人民委员会秘书长孙乐宜和文化艺术界代表共一千多人。代表团人员下车后，接受了欢迎的人们的献花。
    晚上七时四十分，广东省省长陶铸在迎宾馆接见代表团团长阿尼尔·库马尔·钱达和夫人，广州市市长朱光代表我国国务院总理周恩来接受印度共和国总理尼赫鲁赠送的八棵芒果树，并表示谢意。
    晚八时，代表团全体人员应邀出席了广州市市长朱光的宴会。
