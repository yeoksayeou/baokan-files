Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 5th: The Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife, along with his entourage, arrived in Guangzhou at 4:07 PM on the 5th via a special plane from Shanghai on their way back home after their visit to China. Accompanying Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo to Guangzhou were Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to the Republic of Indonesia, and his wife, as well as Dong Yueqian, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province; Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee; Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou; and Wen Niansheng, Deputy Commander of the Guangzhou Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others.

On the evening of the 5th, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, his wife, and the members of his delegation were invited to attend a banquet hosted by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理到广州

1955-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理到广州
    新华社广州五日电　印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人及随同来华访问人员，在返国途中于五日下午四时零七分由上海乘专机飞抵广州。陪同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理到广州的，有我国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使黄镇和夫人，外交部办公厅主任董越千、礼宾司副司长王倬如。
    到机场欢迎的，有广东省省长陶铸，广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗，广州市市长朱光，中国人民解放军广州军区副司令员文年生等。
    五日晚，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和他的夫人以及随同访华人员，应邀出席了广东省省长陶铸的宴会。
