Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation from Polish Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1955-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received Academician Wieczbicicki, head of the delegation from the Polish Academy of Sciences, and delegation members Academician Kuratowski, Corresponding Academician Nowacki, and Professor Rozinski, on the morning of the 7th.

Present at the reception were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见波兰科学院代表团

1955-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见波兰科学院代表团
    新华社七日讯　国务院副总理陈毅于七日上午接见了波兰科学院代表团团长维日比茨基院士和团员库拉托夫斯基院士、诺瓦茨基通讯院士、罗津斯基教授。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院副院长吴有训，对外文化联络局副局长陈忠经等。
