Title: The Vietnamese People Strive to Restore Production (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-26
Page: 5
The Vietnamese people, under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh, finally achieved a great victory in restoring peace to Indochina after eight years of long and arduous struggle. On October 10th last year, the Vietnamese People's Army liberated the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam - Hanoi. This is the beautiful scenery of Hoan Kiem Lake in Hanoi.

    After the liberation of Hanoi, the workers of the Hanoi Electricity Company greatly increased their enthusiasm for production. This is the deputy engineer of the power distribution plant, Nguyen Van Ha (left), doing an experiment with technicians to find underground lines using electricity meters.

    The Boi Thuong irrigation project in Thanh Hoa Province, central Vietnam, is one of the largest irrigation restoration projects in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This project can irrigate 120,000 hectares of rice fields. This is the concrete dam, more than 100 meters long, across the Chu River in the Boi Thuong irrigation project.

    The Vietnamese people are actively repairing the water conservancy and irrigation system in order to quickly restore agricultural production. This is an overview of the construction site of the Tac Kuong sluice gate.

    Workers at the Gia Lam train repair factory in Gia Lam city near Hanoi are actively repairing locomotives.

    Farmers in Hai Duong province of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are carrying out the first summer harvest after the ceasefire in Vietnam.

### 越南人民努力恢复生产（图片）

1955-06-26
第5版()
专栏：
越南人民努力恢复生产
    越南人民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，经过了八年长期的艰苦斗争，终于获得了恢复印度支那和平的伟大胜利。去年十月十日越南人民军解放了越南民主共和国的首都——河内。这是河内还剑湖滨的美丽景色。
    河内电力公司工人在河内解放后，生产积极性有了大大的提高。这是配电厂的副工程师阮文河（左）同技术人员正在做用电计表寻找地下线的实验。
    越南中越清化省境内的沛上水利工程，是越南民主共和国最大的水利修复工程之一。这项工程能灌溉十二万公顷的稻田。这是沛上水利工程中横拦朱江的长达一百多公尺的混凝土水坝。
    越南人民正在积极修复水利灌溉系统，以便迅速恢复农业生产。这是拓况水闸工地的概貌。
    河内附近嘉林城的嘉林火车修理厂的工人在积极修理机车。
    越南民主共和国海阳省的农民正在进行越南停战后的第一个夏收。
