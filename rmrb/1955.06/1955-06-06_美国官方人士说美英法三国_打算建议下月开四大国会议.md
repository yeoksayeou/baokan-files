Title: US Official Says US, Britain, and France Plan to Suggest a Four-Power Conference Next Month
Author:
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 5th (TASS): According to a United Press Washington dispatch, official sources say that the United States, Britain, and France intend to suggest that the Soviet Union hold a four-power summit in Geneva starting July 18th.

### 美国官方人士说美英法三国  打算建议下月开四大国会议

1955-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    美国官方人士说美英法三国
    打算建议下月开四大国会议
    新华社五日讯　塔斯社纽约五日讯：据合众社华盛顿消息，据官方人士说，美国、英国和法国打算建议苏联从七月十八日开始在日内瓦举行四大国政府首脑会议。
