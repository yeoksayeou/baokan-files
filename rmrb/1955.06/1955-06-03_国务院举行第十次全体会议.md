Title: State Council Holds Tenth Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. The tenth plenary meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of May 31st. The meeting approved the "Report of the Chinese Academy of Sciences on the Preparation and Convening of the Inaugural Meeting of the Academies" and the "List of Academicians of the Chinese Academy of Sciences"; approved the "Report of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China on the Work of Resettling Demobilized Construction Soldiers," and adopted the "Decision of the State Council on the Work of Resettling Demobilized Construction Soldiers"; approved the "Summary of Sports Work in 1954 and Report on Work Tasks for 1955" and the "Main Points of the Work Plan of the Sports Commission for 1955" of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China; approved the "Report of the Ministry of Supervision of the People's Republic of China on the Fourth National Supervision Work Conference," the "Report of the Ministry of Supervision on People's Supervision Work in 1954," and the "Report of the Ministry of Supervision on the Tasks and Specific Work of Supervision Work in 1955"; approved the "Report of the Central Handicraft Industry Administration Bureau on the Fourth National Handicraft Production Cooperation Conference"; and adopted the "Provisions of the State Council on the Reporting System for the Work of the People's Committees of Provinces, Autonomous Regions, and Municipalities Directly Under the Central Government," the "Provisions of the State Council on the Reporting System for the Work of Departments Under its Jurisdiction," and the "Decision of the State Council on Placing the Four Counties of Qin County, Hepu, Lingshan, and Fangcheng and Beihai City in Qinzhou Special District of Guangxi Province Under the Leadership of Guangdong Province." In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 国务院举行第十次全体会议

1955-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第十次全体会议
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第十次会议在五月三十一日下午举行。会议批准了“中国科学院关于筹组学部的经过和召开学部成立大会的报告”和“中国科学院学部委员名单”；批准了“中华人民共和国内务部关于安置复员建设军人工作的报告”，通过了“国务院关于安置复员建设军人工作的决议”；批准了“中华人民共和国体育运动委员会关于一九五四年体育工作的总结和一九五五年工作任务的报告”，和“体育运动委员会一九五五年工作计划要点”；批准了“中华人民共和国监察部关于第四次全国监察工作会议的报告”、“监察部关于一九五四年人民监察工作的报告”和“监察部关于一九五五年监察工作的任务和具体工作的报告”；批准了“中央手工业管理局关于第四次全国手工业生产合作会议的报告”；通过了“国务院关于各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会工作报告制度的规定”、“国务院关于所属各部门工作报告制度的规定”和“国务院关于将广西省钦州专区的钦县、合浦、灵山、防城四县和北海市划归广东省领导的决定”。此外，会议通过了各项任免案。
