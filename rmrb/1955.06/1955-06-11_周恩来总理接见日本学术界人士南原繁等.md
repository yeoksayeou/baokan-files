Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Academic Figures Shigeru Nanbara and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received prominent Japanese academic figures Shigeru Nanbara and Hyoe Ouchi at 10 p.m. on the 9th. They were invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit our country.

### 周恩来总理接见日本学术界人士南原繁等

1955-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本学术界人士南原繁等
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在九日晚十时接见了应中国科学院邀请前来我国访问的日本学术界知名人士南原繁和大内兵卫。
