Title: Negotiations on the Normalization of Relations Between the Soviet Union and Japan Officially Begin
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, June 4th: TASS London, June 4th -- According to an earlier agreement reached between the Soviet government and the Japanese government, Soviet representative Ya. A. Malik and Japanese representative Shunichi Matsumoto began negotiations in London on June 1, 1955, on the issue of normalizing Soviet-Japanese relations.
    On June 3, the Soviet and Japanese representatives held a meeting at the Soviet Embassy in London, during which they exchanged greetings and reached an agreement on the future procedures of the negotiations.

### 苏日两国关系正常化问题的谈判正式开始

1955-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日两国关系正常化问题的谈判正式开始
    新华社四日讯　塔斯社伦敦四日讯：根据苏联政府同日本政府早先达成的协议，苏联代表雅·亚·马立克同日本代表松本俊一于一九五五年六月一日在伦敦开始就苏日关系正常化的问题进行谈判。
    六月三日，苏日双方代表在苏联驻伦敦大使馆举行会议，会上双方互相致辞，并就今后谈判程序达成了协议。
