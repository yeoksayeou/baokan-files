Title: Romanian National Assembly Approves Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 31st - According to TASS, the Romanian People's Republic National Assembly convened in Bucharest on May 30th.

    Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers and head of the Romanian government delegation to the Warsaw Conference, delivered a report on the issue of ratifying the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw. He emphasized that the Warsaw Treaty is a truly peaceful and defensive treaty, entirely serving the interests of European peace and security. The Romanian government, after signing the Warsaw Treaty, reiterated its determination to participate in all international measures aimed at guaranteeing peace and international security in a spirit of genuine cooperation. We firmly believe that the Romanian people, who love peace and freedom and recognize their domestic and international responsibilities, will gloriously fulfill the tasks before them, and will support the Warsaw Treaty by engaging in heroic labor for the consolidation and prosperity of the motherland, for the strengthening of national defense, and for the victory of peace and international cooperation.

    Romanian composer and parliamentarian M. Sokol, representing the National Assembly's Foreign Affairs Committee, delivered a supplementary report at the meeting. He recommended the ratification of the Warsaw Treaty.

    Subsequently, the meeting commenced a discussion on Gheorghiu-Dej's report. All speakers welcomed the Warsaw Treaty and warmly supported the proposal for its ratification.

    The National Assembly unanimously approved the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw.

    On the evening of the same day, the meeting began its second agenda item, with Finance Minister D. Petrescu delivering a report on the Romanian People's Republic's 1955 national budget.

### 罗国民议会批准八国友好合作互助条约

1955-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    罗国民议会批准八国友好合作互助条约
    新华社三十一日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会会议五月三十日在布加勒斯特开幕。
    罗马尼亚部长会议主席、出席华沙会议的罗马尼亚政府代表团团长乔治乌—德治在会上作了关于批准在华沙签订的八国友好合作互助条约的问题的报告。他强调指出：华沙条约是真正的和平的防御性的条约，它完全是为欧洲和平和安全的利益服务的。罗马尼亚政府在签订华沙条约后再次表示：决心本着真正合作的精神参加旨在保障和平和国际安全的一切国际措施。我们深信，认识到自己的国内和国际责任的、爱好和平和自由的罗马尼亚人民，将光荣地完成自己面临的任务，将为祖国的巩固和繁荣、为加强国防力量、为和平和国际合作的胜利而进行英勇的劳动，来支持华沙条约。
    罗马尼亚作曲家、议员马·索科尔代表国民议会外交委员会在会上做了补充报告。他建议批准华沙条约。
    接着，会议就乔治乌—德治的报告展开了讨论。所有发言者都欢迎华沙条约，并热烈支持关于批准这一条约的建议。
    国民议会一致批准了在华沙签订的八国友好合作互助条约。
    同日晚，会议开始进行第二项议程，财政部长杜·佩特雷斯库在会上作了关于罗马尼亚人民共和国一九五五年国家预算的报告。
