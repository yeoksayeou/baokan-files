Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Heads to Berlin to Attend the Congress of the Free German Trade Union Federation
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 9th. At the invitation of the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Chinese trade union delegation, consisting of four members and led by Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, departed by plane this morning via Moscow for Berlin to attend the Fourth Congress of the Free German Trade Union Federation.

### 参加自由德国工会联合会代表大会  中国工会代表团动身去柏林

1955-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    参加自由德国工会联合会代表大会
    中国工会代表团动身去柏林
    新华社九日讯　应自由德国工会联合会的邀请，前往参加自由德国工会联合会第四届代表大会的中国工会代表团一行四人，由中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温率领，在九日晨乘飞机取道莫斯科前往柏林。
