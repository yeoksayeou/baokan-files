Title: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 1

    To Comrade N.A. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics,
    Dear Comrade N.A. Bulganin,
    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, please allow me, in the name of the government and people of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, to extend heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, a prominent activist and leader of the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet state, and a close friend of the Chinese people. You have made outstanding contributions to the cause of Soviet communist construction and world peace and human progress with all your energy and rich experience in political, economic, and military activities. I wish you good health and long life, and wish you even greater achievements in your work to seek the happiness of the Soviet people and the prosperity of the Soviet state, and in the cause of defending world peace.
          Zhou Enlai
         June 8, 1955, in Beijing

### 中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来贺电

1955-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来贺电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席亲爱的尼·亚·布尔加宁同志：
    在您六十寿辰时，请允许我代表中华人民共和国政府和人民并以我个人名义向您、苏联共产党和苏维埃国家杰出的活动家和领导者之一、中国人民的亲密朋友，致衷心的、热烈的祝贺。您以自己的全部精力和丰富的政治、经济和军事活动的经验，为苏联共产主义建设和世界和平、人类进步的事业作出了卓越的贡献。谨祝您健康长寿，祝您在谋取苏联人民的幸福和苏维埃国家的繁荣的工作中，在保卫世界和平的事业中，获得更大的成就。
          周恩来
         一九五五年六月八日于北京
