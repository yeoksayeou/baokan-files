Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Holds Reception in Warsaw; Warsaw People Hold Mass Rally to Welcome Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 26th: Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru held a reception in Warsaw on the afternoon of the 25th.

    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut; Chairman of the Council of State, Aleksander Zawadzki; Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz; and other leaders of the Polish United Workers' Party and the government attended the reception. Representatives from Polish scientific and cultural circles, as well as Polish and foreign journalists, were also present.

    On the Indian side, those in attendance included Prime Minister Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi; the Indian Ambassador to Poland, K.P.S. Menon; the Director of the European Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussain; and Indian journalists visiting Poland.

    Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to Poland also attended the reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 26th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru returned to Warsaw on the afternoon of the 25th after visiting various places in Poland.

    On the morning of the 25th, Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage traveled by car from Stalinogród to visit Kraków, an ancient cultural city of Poland, and were warmly cheered by urban and rural people lined up along the highway.

    Accompanied by the Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, Nehru visited the ancient buildings of Kraków, the city of Nowa Huta near Kraków, and the Lenin Metallurgical Combine located there.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Indian Prime Minister Nehru watched the famous Polish opera "Halka" at the Warsaw Opera House on the 25th.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Warsaw City People's Council held a special meeting on the 25th and, based on the proposal of Aleksander Albrecht, Chairman of the Presidium of the Warsaw City People's Council, unanimously decided to confer the title of Honorary Citizen of the City of Warsaw on Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru, who is currently visiting Poland.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the people of Warsaw held a mass rally on the 25th to welcome Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru, who is visiting Poland.

    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut; Chairman of the Council of State, Aleksander Zawadzki; Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz; First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Hilary Minc and Marian Nowak; Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Konstanty Rokossowski, Józef Jozwiak-Witold, Eugeniusz Gede and Piotr Wapot; Vice-Chairman of the Council of State, Ignar; Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Naszkowski; and others attended the rally.

    Aleksander Albrecht, Chairman of the Presidium of the Warsaw City People's Council, delivered the opening speech at the rally on behalf of the Warsaw City People's Council and the Warsaw Committee of the Polish National Front.

    Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, spoke at the meeting, expressing the Polish people's sympathy and support for the Indian people's victorious struggle for independence.

    Then, Prime Minister Nehru spoke amidst stormy applause, discussing the importance of the struggle for peace and India's tradition of peace.

    At the end of the rally, the assembled masses cheered for Prime Minister Nehru, the friendship between Poland and India, and world peace, the cheers lasting for a long time.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁在华沙举行招待会  华沙人民举行欢迎尼赫鲁的群众大会

1955-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁在华沙举行招待会
    华沙人民举行欢迎尼赫鲁的群众大会
    新华社华沙二十六日讯  印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁二十五日下午在华沙举行招待会。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特、国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和波兰统一工人党和政府的其他领导人都出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有波兰科学文化界的代表，波兰和外国的记者。
    印度方面出席招待会的有尼赫鲁总理的女儿英·甘地夫人，印度驻波兰大使克·普·西·梅农和印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼和访问波兰的印度记者。
    各国驻波兰的外交使节和武官也出席了招待会。
    新华社华沙二十六日电  据波兰通讯社报道：印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁在波兰各地参观后，已在二十五日下午回到华沙。
    二十五日早晨，尼赫鲁总理和他的随行人员从斯大林诺格罗德乘车前往波兰文化古城克拉科夫访问，沿途受到排在公路旁的城乡人民的热烈欢呼。
    尼赫鲁在波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹的陪同下，参观了克拉科夫的古代建筑物、克拉科夫附近的诺瓦胡塔城和那里的列宁冶金联合企业。
    据新华社讯  印度共和国总理尼赫鲁二十五日在华沙歌剧院看了波兰著名歌剧“哈尔卡”。
    据新华社讯  华沙市人民会议二十五日举行特别会议，根据华沙市人民会议主席团主席阿尔布列赫特的建议，一致决定把华沙市荣誉公民称号授给正在访问波兰的印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
    据新华社讯  华沙人民二十五日举行群众大会，欢迎访问波兰的印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁先生。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议第一副主席明兹和诺瓦克，部长会议副主席罗科索夫斯基、约兹维亚克—韦托德、盖德和瓦波特，国务委员会副主席伊格纳尔、外交部代理部长纳斯科夫斯基等都出席了大会。
    华沙市人民会议主席团主席阿尔布列赫特代表华沙市人民会议和波兰全国阵线华沙委员会在大会上致开幕辞。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在会上讲了话，表达了波兰人民对印度人民争取独立的胜利斗争的同情和声援。
    接着，尼赫鲁总理在暴风雨般的掌声中讲了话，谈到了为和平而斗争的重要性和印度的和平传统。
    在大会结束时，到会群众为尼赫鲁总理、波印两国的友谊和世界和平而欢呼，欢呼声历久不息。
