Title: New China's Children Grow Up in Happiness (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 5

Mao Zedong with children. Photo by Lü Houmin
Cultivating children's habit of loving labor. This is children at Guangzhou Nursery School watering flowers. Xinhua News Agency photo
New China's children, loved by the country and their parents, live happily and receive good education whether at home or in nurseries and kindergartens. This is children at Anshan No. 4 Kindergarten role-playing as doctors examining dolls.
Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Shaoqing photo
The Young Pioneers organization in Beijing is constantly developing, and now more than 150,000 children have joined the Young Pioneers organization. This is a Red Scarf class at the Second Affiliated Elementary School of Beijing Normal University. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Lu Xuezhis photo
Children of the kindergarten section of Hongguang Elementary School in Tianjin are doing an air bath. Our reporter Gao Liang photo
Children of ethnic minorities are growing up happily in the big family of the motherland. These are the Young Pioneers of the No. 1 Elementary School in Turpan County, Xinjiang Province, listening to horticultural technicians explain the growth of grapes. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Ping photo
Young Pioneers of the Nationalities School of the Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Xikang Province are writing letters to the Chinese People's Liberation Army uncles who liberated Yijiangshan Island, hoping that they will liberate Taiwan as soon as possible so that the children of Taiwan can live a happy life like them. They are reading with joy the reply from soldiers of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army guarding Yijiangshan Island. Cheng Minggui photo
Under the education of schools and the Young Pioneers organization, children not only actively study their lessons, but also carry out many beneficial extracurricular activities. Children in Beijing are carrying out voluntary tree planting. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Xu Bingxing photo
On the morning of June 1st this year, Chinese children and foreign children held a party at the Beijing Hotel to celebrate International Children's Day. The children, dressed in beautiful festive clothes, danced and sang to their hearts' content in the beautiful music, celebrating their own festival. Our reporter Na Yi photo

### 新中国儿童在幸福中成长(图片）

1955-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    新中国儿童在幸福中成长
    毛主席和少年儿童在一起。  吕厚民摄
    培养孩子们热爱劳动的习惯。这是广州市保育院的孩子正在浇花。  新华社稿
    新中国的孩子们，得到国家和父母的爱护，无论在家庭里，或者是托儿所和幼儿园里，孩子们都过着幸福的生活，受到良好的教养。这是鞍山市第四幼儿园的孩子们在扮演医生给娃娃看病。
                                    新华社记者　王少清摄
    北京市的中国少年先锋队组织不断地发展着，现在已有十五万多名少年儿童加入了少年先锋队组织。这是北京师范大学第二附属小学的红领巾班。　新华社记者　卢学志摄
    天津市红光小学幼儿部的孩子们正在作空气浴。  本报记者　高粮摄
    少数民族的儿童，在祖国的大家庭里幸福地成长着。这是新疆省吐鲁番县第一小学的中国少年先锋队队员们，正在听园艺技术人员讲解葡萄的生长情况。　新华社记者　王平摄
    西康省藏族自治州民族学校的中国少年先锋队队员们，写信给解放一江山岛的中国人民解放军叔叔，希望他们早日解放台湾；让台湾的儿童和他们一样地过着幸福的生活。他们正在怀着欣慰的心情阅读守卫在一江山岛上的中国人民解放军某部战士的复信。  程明贵摄
    儿童们在学校和中国少年先锋队组织的教育下，除了积极学习功课之外，还开展了许多有益的课外活动。北京市的儿童们正在进行义务植树。　 新华社记者　徐炳兴摄
    今年六月一日上午，北京的中国儿童同外国儿童在北京饭店举行联欢会，庆祝“六一”国际儿童节。孩子们穿着节日的美丽的衣裳，在优美的音乐声中，尽情歌舞，欢度自己的节日。  本报记者　纳一摄
