Title: Chairman Zawadzki Hosts Banquet for Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-06-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 25th: According to the Polish Press Agency, Warsaw: The Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, Zawadzki, and his wife hosted a banquet on the 24th in honor of the Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, Nehru.

    Among the Indian guests attending the banquet were: the Indian Prime Minister's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, the Indian Ambassador to Poland, K.P.S. Menon, and the Director of the European Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussein.

    The Polish side attending the banquet included: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Beirut, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Cyrankiewicz, First Deputy Chairman Minc, and Nowak, etc.

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 25th: According to the Polish Press Agency, Warsaw: The Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, Nehru, and his entourage visited Warsaw and some cities in Upper Silesia, Poland's largest industrial area, on the 24th.

    On the morning of the 24th, Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage visited the construction in the city center of Warsaw, visited the construction site of a huge stadium, and a newly built largest residential area. Subsequently, the distinguished guests went to visit the Zeran automobile factory. At the entrance of the factory, Prime Minister Nehru was warmly welcomed by the Minister of the Automobile Industry of the Polish People's Republic, Tokarski, and the factory's director and representatives of the workers, and accepted flowers from a young female worker dressed in national costume. Prime Minister Nehru visited the various production workshops of the factory and visited the various processes of automobile production in the factory with great interest. After leaving the Zeran automobile factory, the guests visited the reconstructed Old Town of Warsaw and the Palace of Culture and Science named after Stalin, which is under construction. Finally, they also visited the construction site of a new residential area under construction.

    On the afternoon of the 24th, Nehru and others flew to Stalinogród in order to visit the Upper Silesian industrial area. At the Stalinogród airport, Prime Minister Nehru was welcomed by the Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic and Minister of Mining, Jaroszewicz, and the residents of Stalinogród.

    The distinguished guests then proceeded to visit some towns in the Upper Silesian industrial area. After visiting the Zgoda Technical Equipment Factory in Świętochłowice, they returned to Stalinogród to visit the Beirut Young Pioneers Palace there. In this Young Pioneers Palace, the distinguished guests visited the children's studios and theaters, as well as the works of children's ballet, choirs, young architects, sculptors, and painters, and also received some souvenirs made by the children themselves for Prime Minister Nehru and Mrs. Indira Gandhi. That evening, Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage attended a reception held for him by the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Voivodeship Council of Stalinogród, Niechporek.

    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: According to the British Information Service, London: The British Prime Minister's Office announced on the afternoon of the 23rd: Prime Minister Eden has invited Indian Prime Minister Nehru to visit London. Nehru has accepted the invitation and will arrive in London on July 8th and leave on July 10th.

### 萨瓦茨基主席设宴招待尼赫鲁总理

1955-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基主席设宴招待尼赫鲁总理
    新华社华沙二十五日电　据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基和他的夫人，二十四日设宴招待印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
    出席宴会的印度客人还有：印度总理的女儿英·甘地夫人、印度驻波兰大使克·普·西·梅农、印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼。
    波兰方面出席宴会的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特、波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、第一副主席明兹和诺瓦克等。
    新华社华沙二十五日电　据波兰通讯社华沙讯：印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁和他的随行人员二十四日在华沙和波兰最大的工业区上西里西亚的一些城市参观。
    二十四日上午，尼赫鲁总理和他的随行人员在华沙参观了市中心区的建设情况，参观了一个巨大的运动场的建筑工地和一个新近建成的最大的住宅区。随后，贵宾们前往泽兰汽车工厂参观。在这个工厂的门口，尼赫鲁总理受到波兰人民共和国汽车工业部部长托卡尔斯基和这个工厂的厂长及职工代表们的热烈欢迎，并接受了一个穿着民族服装的青年女工的献花。尼赫鲁总理参观了这个工厂的各个生产车间，极有兴趣地参观了这个工厂的汽车生产的各个过程。从泽兰汽车工厂出来，客人们去访问重建的华沙旧城和正在建设中的斯大林文化科学宫。最后，他们又参观了一个正在建设的新住宅区的建筑工地。
    二十四日下午，尼赫鲁等乘飞机前往斯大林诺格罗德，以便参观上西里西亚工业区。在斯大林诺格罗德机场上，尼赫鲁总理受到波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席兼采矿部部长雅罗谢维奇和斯大林诺格罗德居民的欢迎。
    贵宾们接着就前往上西里西亚工业区的一些城镇参观。他们在参观了斯维托吉洛维兹的兹戈达技术装备工厂后，就回到了斯大林诺格罗德，去参观那里的贝鲁特少年先锋宫。在这个少年先锋宫里，贵宾们参观了儿童们的工作室和戏院，以及儿童巴蕾舞、歌咏队、青年建筑家、雕刻家和画家的作品，还接受了儿童们赠送给尼赫鲁总理和英·甘地夫人一些他们自己做的纪念品。这一天晚上，尼赫鲁总理和他的随行人员出席了斯大林诺格罗德市沃伊沃德雪普人民议会主席团主席湼希波雷克为他举行的招待会。
    新华社二十四日讯　据英国新闻处伦敦消息：英国首相府二十三日下午宣布：艾登首相已经邀请印度总理尼赫鲁访问伦敦。尼赫鲁已经接受了这个邀请，他将在七月八日到达伦敦，在七月十日离开。
