Title: Multiple Fragrant Plants Discovered on Yunnan Border

Author:

Date: 1955-06-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

Multiple Fragrant Plants Discovered on Yunnan Border

    Scientific workers from the Kunming Workstation of the Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences have discovered many plants that can be used to make fragrances on the Yunnan border.

    It is estimated that there are about 1,000 kinds of fragrant plants growing in the forest areas of the Yunnan border. Among them, geranium, strawberry chrysanthemum, and citronella are all high-quality fragrant plants that can be used to make cosmetics, and they are widely distributed and abundant in production. Strawberry chrysanthemum and geranium are rare plants. General fragrant plants only take the petals to extract the fragrance, while strawberry chrysanthemum and geranium can extract the fragrance from the leaves. They contain a lot of essential oils and are very valuable.

    Currently, the workstation is continuing its investigation and preparing to cultivate fragrant plants and trial-produce fragrances on a large scale in the future.

### 云南边疆发现多种香料植物

1955-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

    云南边疆发现多种香料植物
    中国科学院植物研究所昆明工作站的科学工作者在云南边疆发现了许多可以制造香料的植物。
    据估计，生长在云南边疆森林地区的香料植物约有一千种左右，其中香叶天竹葵、草莓菊、香茅等，都是能够制造化妆品用的上等香料植物，而且产地较广，产量丰富。草莓菊、香叶天竹葵是稀有的植物，一般的香料植物只采取花瓣榨取香汁，而草莓菊、香叶天竹葵却可以采叶提炼香汁，含香精量很多，它的价值很高。
    现在，这个工作站还在继续调查，准备将来大量栽培香料植物和试制香料。　（新华社）
