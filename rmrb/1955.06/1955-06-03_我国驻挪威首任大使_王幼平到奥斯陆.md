Title: China's First Ambassador to Norway, Wang Youping, Arrives in Oslo
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, June 2nd: Wang Youping, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Norway, along with Counselor Zang Junyu and others, arrived in Oslo on the 1st.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Onshaug; diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Poland, and Czechoslovakia to Norway; and Chairman of the China Committee of the Norwegian Cultural Association for People's Democratic Countries, Solheim, and others.

Chu Qiyuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Norway, and all the embassy staff also went to the station to greet them.

### 我国驻挪威首任大使　王幼平到奥斯陆

1955-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻挪威首任大使
　　王幼平到奥斯陆
    新华社二日讯　奥斯陆消息：中华人民共和国驻挪威王国首任特命全权大使王幼平偕参赞臧君宇等人，在一日抵达奥斯陆。
    前往车站欢迎的有：挪威外交部礼宾司代理司长翁沙格，苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克驻挪威的外交使节，挪威对人民民主国家文化协会中国委员会主席索尔赫姆等人。
    我国驻挪威大使馆临时代办褚启元和全体馆员也到车站迎接。
