Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Arrives in Warsaw for Visit, Cyrankiewicz Hosts Nehru and Holds Talks, Nehru Visits Zawadzki and Naszkowski
Author:
Date: 1955-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 24th - Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru arrived in Warsaw on the 23rd at the invitation of the Government of the Polish People's Republic.

Accompanying Prime Minister Nehru were his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi; Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Pillai; the Indian Ambassador to Poland, Mr. K.P.S. Menon; and the Director of the European Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Hussain.

Welcoming him at the airport were: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Mr. Bierut; Chairman of the Council of State, Mr. Zawadzki; Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mr. Cyrankiewicz; Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Mr. Mazur; First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mr. Minc and Mr. Nowak; Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Mr. Rokossowski, Mr. Jozwiak-Witold, Mr. Gede, Mr. Jaroszewicz, Mr. Indrychowski, and Mr. Wapot; and representatives of leading bodies of various political parties and social organizations, as well as representatives from the scientific and cultural spheres.

Representatives from all walks of life in Warsaw gathered in front of the airport to welcome him.

Also welcoming Nehru were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from some countries stationed in Poland.

After Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage arrived at the airport, the national anthems of the Republic of India and the Polish People's Republic were played.

Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage were warmly welcomed as they disembarked. Mrs. Cyrankiewicz presented a bouquet of roses to Prime Minister Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi. Young Pioneers from Warsaw schools presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Mr. Nehru and Mrs. Indira Gandhi warmly thanked the children who surrounded them.

Nehru said in a speech at the airport, "I am very happy to be able to come to your capital, meet with the leaders of your country, and see your country. On behalf of the people of my country, I extend my best wishes to the Polish people and wish that friendship and cooperation between our two countries will last forever."

Cyrankiewicz then delivered a speech, expressing gratitude for Prime Minister Nehru's kind congratulations. He said, "On behalf of the Polish government and people, I warmly and gladly welcome Prime Minister Nehru's visit to Poland. The Polish people watch with warm sympathy the Indian people's struggle for freedom and independence." He said, "The whole world knows that Prime Minister Nehru is a staunch fighter for peace and has realized the peaceful ideals of the Indian people in practice. Therefore, we have particularly sincere feelings for our dear guest and are particularly happy that Prime Minister Nehru has come to Poland."

Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage, accompanied by Cyrankiewicz, Bierut, Zawadzki, and other Polish representatives, traveled from the airport in convertibles to the residence arranged for Prime Minister Nehru. The people of Warsaw greeted them with cheers and applause along the way.

According to Xinhua News Agency: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, Polish People's Republic Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, held a grand reception at the Polish Council of Ministers Building on the evening of the 23rd to mark the visit of Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nehru to Poland.

Nehru, accompanied by Cyrankiewicz, traveled in a convertible to the Council of Ministers Building, and was warmly cheered by the citizens of Warsaw along the way.

The Indian side attending the reception included Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Indian Ambassador to Poland K. Menon, and Director of the European Department of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussain.

The Polish side attending the reception included Polish United Workers' Party and state leaders Bierut, Zawadzki, Minc, Nowak, Ochab, Mazur, Rokossowski, etc., ministers of government departments, generals of the Polish army, representatives of leading bodies of various political parties and social organizations, and representatives of scientific and cultural circles.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in Poland also attended the reception.

Cyrankiewicz and Nehru spoke at the meeting successively. They expressed their belief that this visit will deepen mutual understanding, promote friendly cooperation between the two countries, and further strengthen the friendly ties between the two peoples.

The famous Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble performed wonderful programs for the distinguished guests.

Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru visited Polish People's Republic Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz on the 23rd. Talks were held during the visit.

Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jawaharlal Nehru visited Polish People's Republic Chairman of the Council of State Zawadzki on the 23rd.

During the visit, Nehru handed over a letter written by Indian President Prasad to Zawadzki himself. In the letter, President Prasad wished that Nehru's visit to Poland would further strengthen the friendly relations between India and Poland and promote the peace movement.

On the same day, Nehru also visited Polish Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Naszkowski.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁到达华沙访问  西伦凯维兹招待尼赫鲁并举行会谈  尼赫鲁拜访萨瓦茨基和纳斯科夫斯基

1955-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁到达华沙访问
    西伦凯维兹招待尼赫鲁并举行会谈
    尼赫鲁拜访萨瓦茨基和纳斯科夫斯基
    新华社华沙二十四日电　印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁应波兰人民共和国政府的邀请，在二十三日到达华沙。
    随同尼赫鲁总理前来的有他的女儿英·甘地夫人，印度外交部秘书长皮莱，印度驻波兰大使克·普·西·梅农和印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼。
    到机场欢迎的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记处书记马茹尔，部长会议第一副主席明兹和诺瓦克，部长会议副主席罗科索夫斯基、约兹维亚克—韦托德、盖德、雅罗谢维奇、英德里霍夫斯基和瓦波特，各个政党和社会团体领导机构以及科学文化界的代表。
    华沙各界人民的代表齐集在机场前面欢迎。
    欢迎尼赫鲁的还有一些国家驻波兰的外交使节和使馆人员。
    在尼赫鲁总理和随行人员到达机场以后，乐队奏起了印度共和国和波兰人民共和国两国国歌。
    印度总理和随行人员下机时受到热烈欢迎。西伦凯维兹的夫人向尼赫鲁总理的女儿英·甘地夫人献了一束玫瑰花。华沙学校的少年先锋队队员向贵宾们献花。尼赫鲁先生和英·甘地夫人热烈地感谢围着他们的孩子们。
    尼赫鲁在机场发表谈话说，我很高兴我能来到你们的首都，同你们国家的领导人员会面，并看到你们的国家。我代表我国人民向波兰人民致最好的祝贺，祝我们两国之间永远保持友谊和合作。
    西伦凯维兹接着致词，表示感谢尼赫鲁总理的亲切的祝贺。他说，我代表波兰政府和人民热诚高兴地欢迎尼赫鲁总理访问波兰。波兰人民以热烈的同情注视着印度人民为自由和独立而进行的斗争。他说，全世界都知道尼赫鲁总理是一个争取和平的坚强战士，在实践中实现了印度人民的和平理想，因此我们对于我们的亲爱的贵宾有着格外真挚的感情，对于尼赫鲁总理来到波兰感到格外高兴。
    尼赫鲁总理和他的随行人员同西伦凯维兹、贝鲁特、萨瓦茨基和其他波兰代表从飞机场乘敞篷汽车到为尼赫鲁总理安排的住所。沿途华沙人民以欢呼和鼓掌来迎接他们。
    据新华社讯　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁访问波兰，二十三日晚间在波兰部长会议大厦举行了盛大的招待会。
    尼赫鲁在西伦凯维兹的陪同下乘坐敞篷汽车前往部长会议大厦，沿途受到华沙市民的热烈欢呼。
    印度方面出席招待会的有尼赫鲁的女儿英·甘地夫人、印度驻波兰大使克·梅农和印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼。
    波兰方面出席招待会的有波兰统一工人党和国家的领导人贝鲁特、萨瓦茨基、明兹、诺瓦克、奥哈布、马茹尔、罗科索夫斯基等，政府各部部长，波兰军队的将领、各政党和社会团体领导机构的代表以及科学文化界的代表。
    各国驻波兰的外交使节和武官也出席了招待会。
    西伦凯维兹和尼赫鲁在会上先后讲了话。他们表示相信这一次的访问将加深彼此的了解、促进两国的友好合作和进一步密切两国人民之间的友好联系。
    波兰著名的玛佐夫舍歌舞团为贵宾们演出了精彩的节目。
    印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁二十三日拜访了波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹。在拜访时举行了会谈。
    印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁二十三日拜访了波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基。
    尼赫鲁在拜访时把印度共和国总统普拉沙德写给萨瓦茨基的一封信交给了他本人。普拉沙德总统在信中预祝尼赫鲁的访问波兰将进一步加强印波两国的友好关系和促进和平运动。
    同日，尼赫鲁还拜访了波兰外交部代理部长纳斯科夫斯基。
