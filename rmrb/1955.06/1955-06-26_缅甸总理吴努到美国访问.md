Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Visits the United States
Author:
Date: 1955-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 25th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in the United States on the 24th for a visit. U Nu arrived in New York by plane from London on the 24th and is scheduled to travel to Washington on the 29th.

### 缅甸总理吴努到美国访问

1955-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努到美国访问
    新华社二十五日讯　纽约消息：缅甸总理吴努二十四日到美国访问。吴努在二十四日从伦敦乘飞机到达纽约，预定在二十九日前往华盛顿。
