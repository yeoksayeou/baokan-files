Title: Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Menon Hosts Banquet for Nehru's Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 9th – According to TASS, the Ambassador of the Republic of India to the Soviet Union, Menon, held a banquet on June 8th for the official visit of the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, to the Soviet Union.

    Nehru and his entourage attended the banquet.

    The Soviet side was represented by Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, Soviet ministers Zhukov, Kovrigina, Mikhailov, and Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Republic of India, Menshikov, and others.

### 印度驻苏大使梅农为尼赫鲁访苏举行宴会

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度驻苏大使梅农为尼赫鲁访苏举行宴会
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：印度共和国驻苏联大使梅农为印度共和国总理尼赫鲁到苏联作正式访问，在六月八日举行了宴会。
    尼赫鲁和他的随员出席了宴会。
    苏联方面出席的有布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫、苏联各部部长朱可夫、科夫里金娜、米哈伊洛夫和苏联驻印度共和国特命全权大使曼希科夫等。
