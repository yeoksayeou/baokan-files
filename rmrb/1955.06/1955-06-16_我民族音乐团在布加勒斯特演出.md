Title: Our National Music Ensemble Performs in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-06-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 14th: Bucharest Dispatch: The Chinese National Music Ensemble, currently visiting Romania at the invitation of the Romanian Foreign Cultural Association, gave its first performance in Bucharest on the evening of the 12th.
　　Before the performance, Jan Mihai, representative of the Romanian Foreign Cultural Association, extended a warm welcome to the Chinese artists.
　　The program included classical and folk music from various parts of China. The performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 我民族音乐团在布加勒斯特演出

1955-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我民族音乐团在布加勒斯特演出
    新华社布拉格十四日电　布加勒斯特消息：应罗马尼亚对外文化协会的邀请目前正在罗马尼亚访问的中国民族音乐团，十二日晚间在布加勒斯特举行首次演出。
    演出前，罗马尼亚对外文化协会代表扬·米哈伊向中国艺术家表示热烈的欢迎。
    演出的节目包括中国各地的古典音乐和民间音乐。演出受到观众的热烈欢迎。
