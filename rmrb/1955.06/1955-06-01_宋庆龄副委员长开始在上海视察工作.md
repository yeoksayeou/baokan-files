Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Begins Inspection Work in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 1

    Shanghai Dispatch from Our Newspaper, May 31st: Soong Ching Ling, Deputy to the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has begun in-depth inspection work in factories in Shanghai.
    On May 26th, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling arrived at the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory. The factory director, Huang Zonglin, the Secretary of the CCP Party Committee, Xia Zhengyan, and the Chairman of the Trade Union, Zhu Yunxian, reported on the general situation and production of the factory, as well as labor protection and employee welfare. They accompanied her on a tour of the factory's South Spinning Workshop, South Weaving Workshop, clinic, nursery, club, library, and single dormitory. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling was extremely pleased to see the healthy and chubby children in the nursery.
    On May 27th, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling held a four-hour discussion with more than ten people, including elderly and young workers, technical and management personnel, and labor models Zhu Fadi and Wang Guifen, from the factory. Everyone spoke enthusiastically at the meeting, telling Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling about the various achievements in production, and also talking about the current waste of manpower and materials in the factory. The workers also introduced their cultural life at the meeting. During the discussion, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling raised questions about the current unified purchase and sale of grain in the workers' hometowns, the workers' cultural life, political study, and the workers' marriages. These questions were all answered one by one. That afternoon, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling went to Caoyang New Village, where the workers' residences are located, to inspect the area. She visited the homes of advanced worker Yang Fuzhen, worker Xu Hedi, Gu Hongzhen, and others, inquired about their living conditions, and had cordial conversations with them. When Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling bid farewell to Yang Fuzhen's family, she said, "I wish you even more happiness in the future!"
    Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling's inspection work in Shanghai is continuing.

### 宋庆龄副委员长开始在上海视察工作

1955-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长开始在上海视察工作
    本报上海三十一日电　全国人民代表大会代表、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄已经开始在上海深入工厂视察工作。
    五月二十六日，宋庆龄副委员长到达国营上海第一棉纺织厂。厂长黄宗霖、中共党委书记夏正言和工会主席诸韵仙等分别汇报了工厂的概况和生产情形，以及劳动保护和职工福利情况，并陪同参观了该厂南纺车间、南织车间、卫生室、托儿所、俱乐部、图书室和单人宿舍等。宋庆龄副委员长在托儿所里看到了长得白胖健康的孩子，极为高兴。
    二十七日宋庆龄副委员长又同这个厂的老年、青年工人和技术、管理人员以及劳动模范朱法弟、王桂芬等十多人举行了四个小时的座谈会。会上大家都热情地发言，向宋庆龄副委员长讲述了生产上的各种成就，也谈了厂里目前存在的人力和物力等的浪费现象。工人们在会上还介绍了自己的文化生活。宋庆龄副委员长在座谈中提出了有关目前工人们家乡的粮食统购统销、工人的文化生活、政治学习以及工人的婚姻等问题，这些问题都一一得到回答。当天下午，宋庆龄副委员长前往这个厂的工人住宅所在地曹杨新村视察，访问了先进工作者杨富珍、工人徐和弟、顾红珍等的家庭，询问他们的生活情况，并进行了亲切的交谈。宋庆龄副委员长在和杨富珍的家庭告别时说：“祝你们今后更幸福！”
    宋庆龄副委员长在上海的视察工作正在继续进行中。
