Title: Woodcut Exhibition from Our Country Held in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 19th. A Chinese woodcut exhibition, sponsored by the Polish Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, opened in Warsaw on June 18th. The exhibition is divided into three exhibition halls, displaying seventy-four polychrome and black-and-white woodcuts from the recent three years by thirty-six modern Chinese woodcut artists.

### 华沙举办我国木刻展览会

1955-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    华沙举办我国木刻展览会
    新华社华沙十九日电波兰对外文化合作委员会主办的中国木刻展览会于六月十八日在华沙开幕。展览会共分三个展览室，展出的作品包括三十六位现代中国木刻家最近三年来的七十四幅套色和黑白木刻。
