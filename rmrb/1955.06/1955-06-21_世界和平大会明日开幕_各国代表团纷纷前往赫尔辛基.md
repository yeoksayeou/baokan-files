Title: World Peace Congress to Open Tomorrow; Delegations from Various Countries Head to Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Many members of the World Peace Council attending the World Peace Congress have arrived in Helsinki, including: Secretary-General Jean Laffitte (France), Isabelle Blume (Belgium), Duncan Jones (England), Panteleimon Ponomarenko (Soviet Union), Li Yimang (China), Felice Villerazzi (Italy), Alfredo Varela (Argentina), Jorge Zalamea (Colombia).

Xinhua News Agency: The Soviet delegation to the World Peace Congress departed from Moscow on the 19th to head to Helsinki.

The delegation includes Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, writers A. Korneichuk, Ilya Ehrenburg, Alexander Fadeyev, Konstantin Fedin, Alexei Surkov, Wanda Wasilewska, and renowned scientist Academician A. Nesmeyanov.

Viktor Lācis, Chairman of the Nationalities Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, is also going to Helsinki to attend the conference at the invitation of the World Peace Council.

Xinhua News Agency: The Indonesian delegation to the World Peace Congress left Jakarta for Helsinki on the 18th. The delegation includes Professor Poerbodiningrat, Chairman of the Indonesian National Peace Committee, Vice Chairman Setiadjid, and prominent figures from various circles.

Xinhua News Agency: Japanese representatives to the World Peace Congress are departing in succession. On the 18th, another 34 representatives left Tokyo by plane for Helsinki. Among the representatives are Ikuo Yasui, Secretary-General of the National Council for the Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and Professor at Hosei University.

The first group of 12 Japanese representatives to the World Peace Congress left Tokyo for Helsinki on the 16th.

Xinhua News Agency: The Italian National Peace Council announced that the Italian delegation to the World Peace Congress includes more than 100 representatives and observers, representing all political factions except for the extreme right wing.

Xinhua News Agency: Belgium is actively preparing to participate in the World Peace Congress. The Belgian delegation to the conference will consist of twenty people from different parties, trade unions, and religions, including Baron Houtart, Titri, Secretary of the Belgian National Peace Committee, Mrs. Gorojang, a trade union worker, and Dourjo, Chairman of the Liège Committee for the Protection of National Schools.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: The Indian delegation to the World Peace Congress left New Delhi for Helsinki on the evening of the 14th.

The Indian delegation consists of more than 90 people, including Professor D.D. Kosambi, Vice Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Dr. Anand, Academician Soke, Dr. Meghnad Saha, Member of the Indian Parliament, S.A. Dange, General Secretary of the All-India Trade Union Congress, and other prominent figures from various circles.

Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, said at a press conference held in New Delhi on the 14th that the attitude of the Indian delegation at the World Peace Congress will be based on the resolutions passed at the Asian Countries Conference held in New Delhi and the Asian-African Conference held in Bandung. He added that the Indian delegation will draw the world's attention to the Goa issue and the freedom of colonial peoples.

Kitchlew said that so far, two million signatures have been collected in India for the appeal against the preparation of atomic warfare. This fully reflects the fact that the entire Indian people support the complete prohibition of nuclear weapons and favor peaceful methods to resolve all disputes.

### 世界和平大会明日开幕  各国代表团纷纷前往赫尔辛基

1955-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平大会明日开幕
    各国代表团纷纷前往赫尔辛基
    据新华社讯出席世界和平大会的许多世界和平理事会理事已经到达赫尔辛基，其中有：总书记让·拉斐德（法国）、伊莎贝丽·布伦姆（比利时）、邓肯·琼斯（英国）、潘捷列蒙·古利亚耶夫（苏联）、李一氓（中国）、弗尔雅列齐（意大利）、艾尔弗雷多·瓦雷拉（阿根廷）、豪尔赫·萨拉米亚（哥伦比亚）。
    据新华社讯出席世界和平大会的苏联代表团十九日由莫斯科动身前往赫尔辛基。
    代表团人员中有苏联保卫和平委员会主席尼·吉洪诺夫、作家亚·考涅楚克、伊·爱伦堡、亚·法捷耶夫、康·费定、阿·苏尔科夫、凡·华西列夫斯卡娅以及著名科学家亚·涅斯米扬诺夫院士等。
    苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席维·拉齐斯应世界和平理事会邀请，也前往赫尔辛基参加大会。
    据新华社讯印度尼西亚出席世界和平大会代表团已在十八日离开雅加达去赫尔辛基。代表团包括印度尼西亚全国和平委员会主席普波迪宁格拉特教授，副主席塞第亚狄和各界著名人士。
    据新华社讯日本出席世界和平大会的代表正在陆续启程。十八日，又有三十四名代表乘飞机离东京前往赫尔辛基。代表中有日本禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会事务局长、法政大学教授安井郁等。
    日本出席世界和平大会的第一批代表十二人，已在十六日离东京去赫尔辛基。
    据新华社讯意大利全国和平理事会宣布，参加世界和平大会的意大利代表团有一百多个代表和观察员，他们代表除了极端右翼派别以外的所有政治派别。
    据新华社讯比利时正在为参加世界和平大会积极进行准备工作。参加大会的比利时代表团将由分属不同党派、工会和宗教的二十个人组成，其中有：阿拉男爵，比利时全国和平委员会书记蒂特日特，工会工作者戈罗让夫人和列日保护国立学校委员会主席道尔若等。
    新华社新德里电印度出席世界和平大会代表团已在十四日晚离新德里去赫尔辛基。
    印度代表团共九十多人，其中包括全印和平理事会副主席高善必教授，阿南德博士，索克院士，印度国会议员麦格纳德·萨哈博士，全印总工会总书记斯·阿·丹吉以及其他各界著名人士。
    全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士十四日在新德里举行的记者招待会上说，印度代表团在世界和平大会上采取的态度将以在新德里召开的亚洲国家会议和在万隆召开的亚非会议所通过的决议为基础。他又说，印度代表团将促使世界注意果阿问题和殖民地人民的自由的问题。
    克其鲁说，到现在为止，在印度已为反对准备原子战争的呼吁书征集了两百万个签名。这完全反映了这个事实：全体印度人民支持完全禁止核子武器，并且赞成以和平的方法来解决一切争端。
