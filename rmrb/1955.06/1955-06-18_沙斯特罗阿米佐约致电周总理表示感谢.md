Title: Sastroamidjojo Cables Premier Zhou to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Ali Sastroamidjojo, Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, who visited our country at the invitation of our government, cabled Premier Zhou Enlai of our State Council after returning to Jakarta on June 7, expressing his heartfelt thanks for the warm hospitality of our government and people. The cable from Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo was delivered to Premier Zhou Enlai by Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, on June 14. The full text of the cable is as follows:
    "We arrived safely in Jakarta on the evening of June 7, and the journey was smooth. I would like to take this opportunity to express, on behalf of my wife and entourage, and in my own name, my deepest gratitude once again for the warm hospitality of your government and people. I believe that this first visit of mine to the People's Republic of China has achieved the results we expected, and I need not reiterate this point. I sincerely hope that the good relations between our two countries, strengthened by Your Excellency's visit to Indonesia and my visit to China, will be consolidated and developed. Finally, my colleagues and I express our warmest thanks for the souvenirs you have given us, which have eased the sadness we felt when leaving your country."

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约致电周总理表示感谢

1955-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约致电周总理表示感谢
    新华社十六日讯　应我国政府邀请前来我国访问的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约在六月七日返抵雅加达后，特电我国务院周恩来总理对我国政府和人民的盛情招待表示衷心的感谢。阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理的电文于六月十四日由印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图面交周恩来总理。电文全文如下：
    “我们已在六月七日晚上安抵雅加达，一路平安。我愿立即借此机会代表我的夫人和随行人员并以我本人的名义，对于贵国政府和人民的盛情招待，再次表示最深切的感谢。我相信这次我对中华人民共和国的第一次访问，获得了我们所预期的成就，对于这一点我已无需重述。我非常诚恳地希望，由于阁下对印度尼西亚的访问和我对中国的访问而加强了的贵我两国间的良好关系将得到巩固和发展。最后，我和我的同行人员对您所惠赠的纪念品表示最热诚的谢意，这些纪念品减轻了我们离开贵国时的惜别之情。”
