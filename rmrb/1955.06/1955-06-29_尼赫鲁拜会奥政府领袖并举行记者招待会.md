Title: Nehru Visits Austrian Government Leaders and Holds Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, June 28th: According to TASS, Vienna, June 28th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru paid visits to Austrian President Körner, Chancellor Raab, and Vice-Chancellor Schärf on the 26th in Vienna.
    The Austrian government hosted a banquet to welcome the Indian Prime Minister.
    On the 27th, Nehru held a press conference. Reporters raised questions about peaceful coexistence, disarmament, the reunification of Germany, and the development of India.
    In answering questions, Nehru emphasized that he believed the idea of peaceful coexistence would receive widespread popular support.
    Nehru said that now, for the first time in many years, major powers have achieved a certain degree of agreement on the issue of disarmament, and therefore there is every reason to hope that an agreement can finally be reached on this issue.
    On the 27th, Nehru traveled to Salzburg to attend a meeting of Indian ambassadors to European countries.

### 尼赫鲁拜会奥政府领袖并举行记者招待会

1955-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁拜会奥政府领袖并举行记者招待会
    新华社二十八日讯　据塔斯社维也纳二十八日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁二十六日在维也纳拜会了奥地利总统柯尔纳、总理拉布和副总理谢尔夫。
    奥地利政府设宴欢迎印度总理。
    二十七日尼赫鲁举行了记者招待会。记者们提出了关于和平共处、裁军、恢复德国统一和印度国家的发展等问题。
    尼赫鲁在回答问题时着重指出，他相信，和平共处的思想将会受到人民的广泛支持。
    尼赫鲁说，现在，大国对裁军问题的看法多年来第一次取得了一定程度的接近，因而完全有根据可以希望，在这个问题上将终于能达成协议。
    二十七日，尼赫鲁前往萨尔斯堡出席印度驻欧洲各国大使的会议。
