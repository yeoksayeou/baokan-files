Title: V.M. Molotov Arrives in San Francisco and Delivers Speech; Meets with UN Secretary-General, Hosts Yugoslav Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th. TASS, San Francisco, June 19th: TASS special correspondent reports that the Soviet delegation, led by V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, arrived in San Francisco on the 18th to attend the 10th anniversary commemoration of the signing of the United Nations Charter.

Welcoming Molotov and members of the Soviet delegation at the station were Mr. Denu, representative of the UN Secretary-General and Director of the Protocol Department of the UN Secretariat, Mr. Peirce, representative of the US President and Secretary of State and Deputy Director of the Protocol Division of the State Department, and Mr. Roy Gross, representative of the city of San Francisco and Chairman of the San Francisco Preparatory Committee for the UN Tenth Anniversary Commemoration.

Molotov delivered the following speech upon arriving in San Francisco:

	“I thank you for your care for the Soviet delegation arriving here. Please allow me, through you, to extend warm congratulations to the residents of San Francisco – the city where the United Nations was established.”

Molotov and the members of the Soviet delegation stopped in Chicago for sightseeing on their way from New York to San Francisco, and visited a number of enterprises.

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th. TASS, San Francisco, June 20th: Special correspondent reports that V.M. Molotov, head of the Soviet delegation to the 10th anniversary commemoration of the signing of the United Nations Charter, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, met with UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on June 19th. The meeting took place in the UN Secretary-General's office, and Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Sobolev, also attended the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th. TASS, San Francisco, June 20th: TASS special correspondent reports: V.M. Molotov, head of the Soviet delegation to the 10th anniversary commemoration of the signing of the United Nations Charter, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet on the 19th for the delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia attending the commemoration.

The Yugoslav side attending the banquet included: Yugoslav Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs K. Popović, Yugoslav Ambassador to the United States L. Mates, and Yugoslav Permanent Representative to the United Nations J. Brilej.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedin, Ukrainian Republic Minister of Foreign Affairs L.F. Palamarchuk, Byelorussian Republic Minister of Foreign Affairs K.V. Kiselev, Soviet Ambassador to the United States G.N. Zarubin, and Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations A.Ya. Sobolev.

UN Under-Secretary-General I.S. Chernyshev also attended the banquet.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 维·米·莫洛托夫到达旧金山并发表谈话  同联合国秘书长会谈，招待南斯拉夫代表团

1955-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    维·米·莫洛托夫到达旧金山并发表谈话
    同联合国秘书长会谈，招待南斯拉夫代表团
    新华社二十日讯塔斯社旧金山十九日讯：塔斯社特派记者报道，以苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫为首的苏联代表团十八日到达旧金山，参加联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会。
    在车站上欢迎莫洛托夫和苏联代表团团员的有联合国秘书长的代表、联合国秘书处交际部主任德努，美国总统和国务卿的代表、国务院交际司副司长佩尔斯，旧金山市代表、联合国十周年纪念会旧金山市筹备委员会主席罗·格罗斯。
    莫洛托夫在抵达旧金山时发表谈话如下：
      “我感谢你们对到达这里的苏联代表团的照顾。请允许我通过你们，向旧金山——这个联合国在这里建立的城市的居民致以热烈的祝贺。”
      莫洛托夫和苏联代表团人员在从纽约到旧金山途中曾经在芝加哥停留观光，并且参观了许多企业。
    新华社二十日讯塔斯社旧金山二十日讯：特派记者报道，前来参加联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会的苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫在六月十九日同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德举行了会谈。会谈是在联合国秘书长办事处进行的，参加会谈的还有苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫。
    新华社二十日讯塔斯社旧金山二十日讯：塔斯社特派记者报道：苏联出席联合国宪章签订十周年纪念会的代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫十九日设宴招待南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国出席纪念会的代表团。
    南斯拉夫方面出席宴会的有：南斯拉夫外交秘书长科·波波维奇、南斯拉夫驻美国大使利·马蒂斯、南斯拉夫常驻联合国代表约·布里列伊。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：苏联外交部副部长费德林、乌克兰共和国外交部长勒·弗·帕拉马尔楚克、白俄罗斯共和国外交部长库·维·基谢列夫、苏联驻美国大使格·纳·查鲁宾、苏联常驻联合国代表阿·亚·索波列夫。
    出席宴会的还有联合国副秘书长伊·斯·契尔尼谢夫。
    宴会在友好气氛中进行。
