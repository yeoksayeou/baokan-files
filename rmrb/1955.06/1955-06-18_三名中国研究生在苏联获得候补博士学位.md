Title: Three Chinese Graduate Students Obtain Candidate of Sciences Degrees in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-18
Page: 2

(TASS, Leningrad) Three young Chinese graduate students at the Pavlov Institute of Physiology in Leningrad recently obtained Candidate of Sciences degrees.

	The Chinese graduate student Hua Guang, formerly a soldier in the Chinese People's Liberation Army, is now studying Pavlov's method of conditioned reflexes under the guidance of Professor I. Kurzin. He has already published two scientific works. His dissertation this time is on an important issue in modern physiology: "The Permeability of Glandular Tissue in Cases of Impaired Higher Nervous Activity." Hua Guang used the radioactive phosphorus tracer method in his research. He has identified several factors regarding medullary activity that are very useful for both scientific and clinical aspects.

	The graduate student Xu Ke, also formerly a soldier in the Chinese People's Liberation Army, is now a well-trained scientific worker. He has made further studies on Pavlov's theory of cortical inhibition, making valuable contributions to the study of higher nervous activity.

	Mei Zhentong's dissertation received high praise. Under the guidance of the famous physiology professor F. Mayorov, she prepared a paper discussing some important issues in the physiology of the medulla oblongata. She is a graduate of Peking University. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 三名中国研究生在苏联获得候补博士学位

1955-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

    三名中国研究生在苏联获得候补博士学位
    据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：列宁格勒巴甫洛夫生理学研究所最近有三名中国青年研究生取得了候补博士学位。
    中国青年研究生华光以前是中国人民解放军战士，现在在伊·库尔津教授的指导下研究巴甫洛夫的条件反射的方法，他已发表了两篇科学著作。这次的论文是谈现代生理学上的一个重要问题“在高级神经活动破坏了的情况下腺组织的渗透性”。华光在进行研究时使用了放射性磷的示踪原子法。他确定了一些对科学和临床方面都很有用的关于延髓活动的因素。
    研究生徐科以前也是一个中国人民解放军的战士，现在已是一个有素养的科学工作者了。他对巴甫洛夫关于皮质抑制的学说作了进一步的研究，对研究高级神经活动有很宝贵的贡献。
    梅镇彤的论文获得了很高的评价。她在著名的生理学教授费·马伊奥罗夫的指导下，准备了一篇谈延髓生理学上的一些重要问题的论文。她是北京大学的毕业生。　（新华社）
