Title: Czechoslovak Republic Presents Power Generation Equipment to Zhangguozhuang, Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th -- The Czechoslovak Republic has donated a generator and auxiliary electrical lighting equipment to the Zhangguozhuang Township Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing.

　　On June 5th, seven Czechoslovak experts and staff members spent the whole day installing the equipment for the cooperative. That evening, the newly installed generator started up, and the lights in the cooperative's office, medical station, cultural station and other public places shone brightly. Radios and record players also played pleasant music. That night, the members read newspapers and books under electric light in the reading room for the first time.

　　Guo Rong, director of the Zhangguozhuang Township Agricultural Production Cooperative, said that they would strive for a bumper harvest in production to repay the brotherly friendship of Czechoslovakia.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共和国  赠送北京张郭庄发电设备

1955-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共和国
    赠送北京张郭庄发电设备
    新华社十三日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国把一部发电机和附属电气照明设备赠送给北京郊区张郭庄乡农业生产合作社。
    六月五日，捷克斯洛伐克七个专家和工作人员整天替这个合作社安装这些设备。当晚，新装的发电机就响了，合作社办公室、医疗站、文化站等公共场所的电灯放出了光明，收音机和电唱机也放出了悦耳的音乐。这天夜里社员们第一次在阅览室电灯光下阅读书报。
    张郭庄乡农业生产合作社主任郭荣表示，要在生产中争取丰收，来答谢捷克斯洛伐克兄弟般的友谊。
