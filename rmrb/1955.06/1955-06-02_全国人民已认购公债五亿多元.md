Title: The People of the Whole Country Have Subscribed to More Than 500 Million Yuan of Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1955-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch:

    By May 15th, the people of the whole country had subscribed to more than 500 million yuan of the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds, reaching 86% of the total issuance. Among them, the subscription figure of staff and workers has exceeded the original sales plan by 31%; the payment figure of the military system has exceeded the original sales plan by 53%; the subscription figure of the industrial and commercial sectors reached 89.42% of the original sales plan; and the subscription figure of urban residents and others reached 94.65% of the original plan. In rural areas, because of busy spring plowing and other work in the past few months, except for some areas where promotion has been carried out, most areas are preparing to promote sales after the summer harvest, and a few areas will also promote sales after the autumn harvest in conjunction with the purchase of grain and other cash crops.
  People from all walks of life have paid more than 180 million yuan in public bonds, accounting for more than 35% of the total subscription.

### 全国人民已认购公债五亿多元

1955-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民已认购公债五亿多元
    新华社一日讯　到五月十五日统计，全国人民已认购一九五五年国家经济建设公债五亿多元，达发行总额的百分之八十六。其中职工认购数字已超过原定推销计划百分之三十一；部队系统的缴款数字已超过原定推销计划百分之五十三；工商界认购数字达原定推销计划百分之八十九点四二；市民及其他认购数字达原定计划百分之九十四点六五。农村因为这几个月来忙于春耕生产和其他工作，除部分地区进行了推销外，大部分地区准备在夏收后推销，少数地区还将在秋收后结合粮食和其他经济作物的收购进行推销。
  各界人民已缴公债款一亿八千多万元，达认购总数的百分之三十五以上。
