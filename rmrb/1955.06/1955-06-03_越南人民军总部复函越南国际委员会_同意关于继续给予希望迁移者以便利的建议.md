Title: General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Replies to the International Commission for Vietnam, Agreeing to Recommendations on Continuing to Provide Facilities for Those Wishing to Migrate
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 1st: According to Vietnam News Agency on June 1st: The International Supervisory and Control Commission for Vietnam sent a letter to the Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army on May 28th, suggesting that both sides continue to grant permission and provide facilities to those who wish to go to North or South Vietnam until July 20, 1955.

The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army replied to the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Vietnam on May 31st, expressing its recognition of the main points of the recommendations in the International Commission's letter of May 28th, which had been reached in oral agreements. The reply also pointed out that the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army believes that these recommendations, which are in line with the spirit of the statement issued by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on February 4th and the decision made on May 18th regarding the free movement between the northern and southern regions, do not constitute an amendment to paragraph D of Article 14 of the Geneva Accords.

### 越南人民军总部复函越南国际委员会  同意关于继续给予希望迁移者以便利的建议

1955-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总部复函越南国际委员会
    同意关于继续给予希望迁移者以便利的建议
    新华社河内一日电　据越南通讯社一日讯：越南国际监督和监察委员会在五月二十八日致函越南人民军总司令部联络代表团，建议双方继续给那些希望前往北越或南越的人以许可和便利，直到一九五五年七月二十日为止。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团在五月三十一日函复越南国际监督和监察委员会，对于就国际委员会五月二十八日信件中的建议的主要点所达成的口头协议表示认可。复信中并指出，越南人民军总司令部认为，符合越南民主共和国政府就南北两地区间自由移动问题在二月四日所发表的声明和在五月十八日所作出的决定的精神的这些建议，并不构成对日内瓦协定第十四条丁款的修正。
