Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Ali Expresses Hope to Meet with Bulganin
Author:
Date: 1955-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, June 25th: According to a report from the “Dawn” correspondent in Delhi, Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali, when answering questions from reporters in Calcutta on the 24th, expressed his hope to meet with the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin. He said that personal meetings between heads of government are "always beneficial."
    When talking about the joint statement of Bulganin and Nehru, Ali said that this statement "to a great extent helps to eliminate the incorrect understanding of many facts." The Pakistani Prime Minister welcomed the opinion in the statement that one country's non-interference in another country's internal affairs should include economic, political, and ideological aspects.
    In answering another question, Muhammad Ali said that the joint statement of Bulganin and Nehru "demonstrated a sincere attitude."

### 巴基斯坦总理  阿里表示希望同布尔加宁会晤

1955-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理
    阿里表示希望同布尔加宁会晤
    据新华社讯  据塔斯社喀喇蚩二十五日讯：据巴基斯坦“黎明报”驻德里记者报道，巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里二十四日在加尔各答回答记者问时，表示希望同苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁会晤，他说，政府首脑的亲自会晤“总是有好处的”。
    阿里在谈到布尔加宁和尼赫鲁的联合声明时说，这个声明“在极大程度上有助于消除对许多事实的不正确的了解”。巴基斯坦总理欢迎声明中的这一意见，即一国不干涉另一国的内政应当包括经济、政治和意识形态各方面。
    穆罕默德·阿里在答复另一个问题时说，布尔加宁和尼赫鲁的联合声明“证明了真诚态度”。
