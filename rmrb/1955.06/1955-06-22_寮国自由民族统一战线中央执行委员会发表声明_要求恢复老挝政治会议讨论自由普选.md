Title: Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee Issues Statement Demanding Resumption of Lao Political Conference to Discuss Free and Fair Elections
Author:
Date: 1955-06-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 21st: Vietnam News Agency reports that on June 15th, Prince Souphanouvong, representing the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, issued a statement demanding that the Lao Royal Government resume political talks with the Lao Fighting Units and hold free and fair elections with the participation of the Lao Fighting Units.
      The statement said that the Lao Patriotic Front advocates achieving national unity through consultation and cooperation, and the procedures and rules for free and fair elections must be discussed and agreed upon by representatives of both sides. Only in this way can the realization of universal suffrage conform to the legal principles and spirit of the Geneva Accords, and conform to the wishes and true interests of all the people of Laos.

### 寮国自由民族统一战线中央执行委员会发表声明  要求恢复老挝政治会议讨论自由普选

1955-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国自由民族统一战线中央执行委员会发表声明
    要求恢复老挝政治会议讨论自由普选
    新华社河内二十一日电　越南通讯社报道，六月十五日，苏发努冯亲王代表寮国自由民族统一战线中央执行委员会发表声明，要求老挝王国政府同寮国战斗部队恢复政治会议，并且举行有寮国战斗部队参加的自由普选。
      声明说，寮国自由民族统一战线主张通过协商合作的方法实现国家统一，关于自由普选的手续和规则必须由双方代表讨论并取得同意。只有这样实现普选才符合日内瓦协定的法理和精神，符合老挝全体人民的愿望和真正利益。
