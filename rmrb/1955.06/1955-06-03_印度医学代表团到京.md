Title: Indian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Indian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Indian Medical Delegation, consisting of five members led by head A. Huzha, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the first, at the invitation of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China, to visit and give lectures in our country.

### 印度医学代表团到京

1955-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度医学代表团到京
    据新华社讯　应中华人民共和国卫生部邀请前来我国访问及讲学的印度医学代表团一行五人，由团长阿护迦率领，在一日下午乘机到达北京。
