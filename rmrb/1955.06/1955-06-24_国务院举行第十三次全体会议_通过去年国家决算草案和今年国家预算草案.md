Title: State Council Holds Thirteenth Plenary Meeting; Approves Draft State Final Accounts for Last Year and Draft State Budget for This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd—The Thirteenth Plenary Meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 22nd. After listening to explanations from Li Xiannian, Minister of Finance, the meeting approved the report on the State Final Accounts for 1954 and the State Budget for 1955, as well as the Draft State Final Accounts of the People's Republic of China for 1954 and the Draft State Budget of the People's Republic of China for 1955. It was decided to submit the aforementioned documents to the Second Session of the First National People's Congress for review and approval.

    The meeting also decided to submit to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval the establishment of the Ministry of Coal Industry, the Ministry of Electric Power Industry, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, and the Ministry of Agricultural Products Procurement, and to abolish the Ministry of Fuel Industry.

    In addition, the meeting approved various appointments and removals from office.

    Resolution of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on Approving the Draft State Final Accounts of the People's Republic of China for 1954 and the Draft State Budget of the People's Republic of China for 1955

    (Adopted at the Thirteenth Plenary Meeting of the State Council on June 22, 1955)

    The Thirteenth Plenary Meeting of the State Council, after listening to the explanation by Minister of Finance Li Xiannian, approved the report on the State Final Accounts for 1954 and the State Budget for 1955, the Draft State Final Accounts of the People's Republic of China for 1954, and the Draft State Budget of the People's Republic of China for 1955. The meeting decided to submit the Draft State Final Accounts of the People's Republic of China for 1954 and the Draft State Budget of the People's Republic of China for 1955 to the Second Session of the First National People's Congress for review and approval.

### 国务院举行第十三次全体会议  通过去年国家决算草案和今年国家预算草案

1955-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第十三次全体会议
    通过去年国家决算草案和今年国家预算草案
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第十三次会议在二十二日下午举行。会议在听了财政部部长李先念的说明后，通过了关于一九五四年国家决算和一九五五年国家预算的报告和中华人民共和国一九五四年国家决算草案和中华人民共和国一九五五年国家预算草案，并决定将上述文件提请第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议审查批准。
    会议还决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准成立煤炭工业部、电力工业部、石油工业部和农产品采购部，撤销燃料工业部。
    此外，会议通过了各项任免案。
    中华人民共和国国务院关于通过中华人民共和国一九五四年国家决算草案和中华人民共和国一九五五年国家预算草案的决议
    （一九五五年六月二十二日国务院全体会议第十三次会议通过）
    国务院全体会议第十三次会议在听取了财政部李先念部长的说明后，通过了关于一九五四年国家决算和一九五五年国家预算的报告，中华人民共和国一九五四年国家决算草案和中华人民共和国一九五五年国家预算草案。会议决定将中华人民共和国一九五四年国家决算草案和中华人民共和国一九五五年国家预算草案提请第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议审查批准。
