Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Bakur and Kamel
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 31st. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held a banquet on the evening of the 31st to entertain the Minister of Religious Affairs of Egypt, Bakur, and the Professor of Constitutional Law at Cairo University, Kamel.

Present as guests were: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Lin Feng, Director of the Second Office; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Li Dequan, Minister of Health; Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; Zhang Xi, Ding Xilin, Zheng Zhenduo, Huang Songling, and Yang Jingren, Vice Ministers of various ministries and Deputy Directors of various commissions; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council; Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor.

Also present as guests were: Shao Lizi, Zhou Jianren, and Hu Yuzhi, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Huang Qixiang, Wang Shao'ao, Tu Changwang, Chen Qiyou, and Li Chunqing, responsible persons of various democratic parties.

Also present as guests were: Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association; Ma Yu Huai and Da Putsheng, Vice Directors; Bai Shouyi and Ma Songting, Vice Directors of the Chinese Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association; Xirao Jiatsuo, Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Yang Hansheng, Vice President; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Zizhong, Zhao Puchu, and Juezan, religious figures from the capital; Jiang Nanxiang and Su Keqin, figures from the education community, and others.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理设宴招待巴库尔和卡迈尔

1955-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待巴库尔和卡迈尔
    新华社三十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在三十一日晚举行宴会，招待埃及宗教事务部部长巴库尔和开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔。
    出席作陪的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，第二办公室主任林枫，粮食部部长章乃器，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，卫生部部长李德全，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，各部副部长和各委员会副主任张玺、丁西林、郑振铎、黄松龄、杨静仁，对外文化联络局局长洪深，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，北京市市长彭真，副市长吴晗。
    出席作陪的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员邵力子、周建人、胡愈之，各民主党派负责人黄琪翔、王绍鏊、涂长望、陈其尤、李纯青。
    出席作陪的还有：中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉，副主任马玉槐、达浦生，中国回民文化协进会副主任白寿彝、马松亭，中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，以及首都宗教界人士王梓仲、赵朴初、巨赞，教育界人士蒋南翔、苏克勤等。
    宴会在友好空气中进行。
