Title: Chairman of the Institution of Engineers (India) Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-06-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Dr. Dey, Chairman of the Institution of Engineers (India), and his wife, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on June 1st.

### 印度工程师学会主席到京

1955-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工程师学会主席到京
    据新华社讯　应中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会邀请来我国参观访问的印度工程师学会主席德依博士和他的夫人在六月一日乘飞机抵达北京。
