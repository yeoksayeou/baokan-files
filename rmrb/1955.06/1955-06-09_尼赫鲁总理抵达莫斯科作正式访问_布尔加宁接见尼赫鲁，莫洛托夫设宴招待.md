Title: Prime Minister Nehru Arrives in Moscow for Official Visit; Bulganin Receives Nehru, Molotov Hosts Banquet
Author:
Date: 1955-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th dispatch from TASS: The Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Republic of India, Jawaharlal Nehru, and his entourage, arrived in Moscow on the 7th for an official visit.

At 6:00 PM, the plane landed at the Central Airport. The flags of the Republic of India and the Soviet Union were flying at the airport.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, ministers and vice-ministers of various departments, representatives of the working people of Moscow, and newspaper reporters. Also present to welcome them were the Ambassador of the Republic of India to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon, the Soviet Ambassador to the Republic of India, Menshikov, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Moscow.

Approximately three hundred people went to the airport to welcome them.

Young Pioneers from Moscow presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Children from the Indian Embassy in the Soviet Union presented flowers to Bulganin and Prime Minister Nehru.

A guard of honor was lined up at the airport. The band played the national anthems of the Republic of India and the Soviet Union. Bulganin and Nehru reviewed the guard of honor.

Prime Minister Nehru delivered a speech at the airport. He said, "I have long hoped to come to the Soviet Union. Today, this wish has been realized. I have come here as a traveler with the best wishes for your government and people. I have come here to know you better and more fully. I also believe that my visit here will consolidate our relations. I thank you for giving me such a warm and friendly reception."

Then Nehru and Bulganin, and others, left the airport by car for the prepared residence. Prime Minister Nehru was warmly welcomed by tens of thousands of workers in Moscow along the way. Banners were hung everywhere on both sides of the street with slogans: "Salute Prime Minister Nehru! Welcome! Welcome!" Welcomers held bouquets and shouted slogans: "Congratulations on the growing Soviet-Indian friendship, congratulations on the prosperity of the Indian people, congratulations on the achievements of the cause of peace."

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th dispatch from TASS: On the morning of June 8th, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, received the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru. Accompanying Prime Minister Nehru was the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon.

Chairman Bulganin and Prime Minister Nehru held talks. Present at the talks were the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, and the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Republic of India, M.A. Menshikov.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th dispatch from TASS: On the morning of June 8th, the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, received the Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th dispatch from TASS: The Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov hosted a banquet at noon on the 8th to entertain the Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, who is on an official visit to Moscow.

Those attending the banquet included Nehru, his daughter Mrs. Gandhi, the Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pillai, the Director of the European Department of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hussein, and the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Menon, and his wife.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Soviet Ministers Benediktov, Kabanov, Kovrigina, Mikhailov, Soviet Deputy Foreign Ministers Gromyko and Kuznetsov, the Soviet Plenipotentiary Ambassador to the Republic of India, Menshikov, and the Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Molochkov, and others.

The banquet took place in a particularly cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 尼赫鲁总理抵达莫斯科作正式访问  布尔加宁接见尼赫鲁，莫洛托夫设宴招待

1955-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理抵达莫斯科作正式访问
    布尔加宁接见尼赫鲁，莫洛托夫设宴招待
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：印度共和国总理兼外交部长贾·尼赫鲁和他的随行人员于七日抵达莫斯科作正式访问。
    下午六时，飞机在中央机场降落。机场上悬挂着印度共和国和苏联两国国旗。
    前往机场欢迎的有：布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、各部部长、副部长、莫斯科劳动人民代表、报纸记者。前往欢迎的还有印度共和国驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农、苏联驻印度共和国大使曼希科夫以及各国驻在莫斯科的外交使节。
    前往机场欢迎的共约有三百人。
    莫斯科少先队员向贵宾献花。印度驻苏大使馆的儿童向布尔加宁和尼赫鲁总理献花。
    机场上排列着仪仗队。乐队奏起印度共和国和苏联国歌。布尔加宁和尼赫鲁检阅了仪仗队。
    尼赫鲁总理在机场上发表了讲话。他说，我很久以来就希望到苏联来。今天这个愿望实现了。我是作为一个带着对你们的政府和人民的最好的愿望的旅行者到这里来的。我到这里来，是为了更好地、更多地了解你们。我也相信，我到这里来将巩固我们的关系。我感谢给我这样热烈友好的接待。
    接着尼赫鲁和布尔加宁等乘车离开机场，前往预先准备好的住所。尼赫鲁总理沿途受到了莫斯科成千成万劳动者的热烈欢迎。街道两旁到处悬挂着横幅大标语：“向尼赫鲁总理致敬！欢迎！欢迎！”欢迎者拿着花束，高呼口号：祝贺日益巩固的苏印友谊，祝贺印度人民繁荣，祝贺和平事业的成就。
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：六月八日上午，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁接见了印度共和国总理尼赫鲁。陪同尼赫鲁总理前来的有印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农。
    布尔加宁主席和尼赫鲁总理进行了会谈。会谈时在座的有苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫和苏联驻印度共和国特命全权大使米·阿·曼希科夫。
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：六月八日上午，苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫接见了印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
    新华社八日讯　塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联外交部长莫洛托夫八日中午设宴招待到莫斯科作正式访问的印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁。
  出席宴会的有尼赫鲁、他的女儿甘地夫人、印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度共和国外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼、印度共和国驻苏联大使梅农和夫人。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，苏联各部部长别涅吉克托夫、卡巴诺夫、科夫里金娜、米哈伊洛夫，苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯和库兹涅佐夫，苏联驻印度共和国特命全权大使曼希科夫，以及苏联外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫等。
    宴会在特别亲切和友好的气氛中进行。
