Title: South, U.S., British, and French Representatives Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-06-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, June 26th—TASS Belgrade dispatch: According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, representatives from Yugoslavia, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France began talks in Belgrade on the 24th. Participating in the talks were Yugoslav Deputy Foreign Secretary Prica and the Western countries' ambassadors to Belgrade, Liddleberger (United States), Roberts (United Kingdom), and Couve de Murville (France).

    The Yugoslav News Agency pointed out that the agenda of the talks is as follows: 1. Regarding the overall international situation; 2. Regarding current mutual relations issues.

### 南、美、英、法四国代表会谈

1955-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    南、美、英、法四国代表会谈
    新华社二十六日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：据南斯拉夫通讯社消息，南斯拉夫、美国、英国和法国的代表二十四日在贝尔格莱德开始会谈。参加这一会谈的有南斯拉夫外交副秘书长普里查和西方国家驻贝尔格莱德大使立德尔贝格（美国）、罗伯茨（英国）和古勒（法国）。
    南斯拉夫通讯社指出，会谈的议程如下：一、关于总的国际局势，二、关于现时的相互关系的问题。
