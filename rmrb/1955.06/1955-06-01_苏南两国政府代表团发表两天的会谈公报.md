Title: Soviet and Yugoslav Government Delegations Issue Communiqué on Two-Day Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-06-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Brioni, May 31st (TASS dispatch): The following is the communiqué on the talks between the Soviet and Yugoslav government delegations:

    On May 29th and 30th, 1955, the Government Delegation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, which arrived at Brioni Island, continued talks with the Government Delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    The two sides continued to exchange views on issues concerning Soviet-Yugoslav relations in an atmosphere of mutual understanding.

    On May 29th, members of the Yugoslav delegation accompanied the Soviet government delegation on a visit to Brioni Island, touring its scenic spots.

    On May 30th, members and advisors of the Soviet delegation held talks with members and advisors of the Yugoslav delegation on issues concerning the two countries.

    On the same day, Khrushchev, Bulganin, and Tito visited the ports of Pula and Rovinj along the Istrian Peninsula aboard the yacht "Podgorka."

    Members and advisors of both delegations attended a breakfast and lunch hosted by Yugoslav President Josip Broz Tito.

    On May 31st, the Soviet government delegation was scheduled to leave Brioni Island and continue its visit to Yugoslavia.

### 苏南两国政府代表团发表两天的会谈公报

1955-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南两国政府代表团发表两天的会谈公报
    新华社三十一日讯　塔斯社布莱俄尼岛三十日讯：苏联政府代表团和南斯拉夫政府代表团会谈公报如下：
    一九五五年五月二十九日和三十日，到达布莱俄尼岛的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府代表团同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府代表团继续会谈。
    双方在互相谅解的气氛下继续就苏南关系问题交换意见。
    五月二十九日，南斯拉夫代表团团员陪同苏联政府代表团访问布莱俄尼岛，游览岛上的名胜。
    五月三十日，苏联代表团的团员和顾问同南斯拉夫代表团的团员和顾问就关系两国的一些问题进行会谈。
    同日，赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁以及铁托乘“山麓号”快艇访问了伊斯的里雅半岛沿海的普拉港和罗文港。
    双方代表团团员和顾问参加了南斯拉夫总统约·布·铁托宴请的早餐和午餐。
    五月三十一日，苏联政府代表团要离开布莱俄尼岛，并继续在南斯拉夫访问。
