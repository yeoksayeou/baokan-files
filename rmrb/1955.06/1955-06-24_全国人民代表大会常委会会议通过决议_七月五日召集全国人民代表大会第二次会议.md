Title: National People's Congress Standing Committee Meeting Passes Resolution: Second Session of the National People's Congress to Convene on July 5th
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 17th meeting on June 23rd.

    The meeting passed a resolution to convene the Second Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China in Beijing on July 5, 1955, and stipulated that representatives of the National People's Congress should register before July 1st.

    Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, gave an explanation at the meeting regarding the draft of the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy of the People's Republic of China, the draft of the 1954 State Final Accounts of the People's Republic of China, and the draft of the 1955 State Budget of the People's Republic of China, all of which are to be submitted to the Second Session of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

    The meeting also passed a resolution on the interpretation of legal issues.

    Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on Convening the Second Session of the First National People's Congress
    (Adopted at the 17th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 23, 1955)

    The 17th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress resolves: To convene the Second Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China in Beijing on July 5, 1955. Representatives of the National People's Congress should register before July 1st.

    Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the Interpretation of Legal Issues
    (Adopted at the 17th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 23, 1955)

    1.  All matters concerning the need for further clarification of the boundaries of legal and decree provisions themselves, or for supplementary provisions, shall be interpreted separately by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress or stipulated by laws and decrees.

    2.  All matters concerning how to specifically apply laws and decrees in the trial process shall be interpreted by the Adjudication Committee of the Supreme People's Court.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会会议通过决议  七月五日召集全国人民代表大会第二次会议

1955-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会会议通过决议
    七月五日召集全国人民代表大会第二次会议
    新华社二十三日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在六月二十三日举行第十七次会议。
    会议通过决议，定于一九五五年七月五日在北京召集中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议，并规定全国人民代表大会代表在七月一日以前报到。
    国务院周恩来总理在会上对将要提请全国人民代表大会第二次会议审定的中华人民共和国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划草案以及中华人民共和国一九五四年国家决算草案和中华人民共和国一九五五年国家预算草案作了说明。
    会议还通过了关于解释法律问题的决议。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于召集第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的决议
    （一九五五年六月二十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十七次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十七次会议决议：定于一九五五年七月五日在北京召集中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议，全国人民代表大会代表应于七月一日以前报到。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于解释法律问题的决议
    （一九五五年六月二十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十七次会议通过）
    一、凡关于法律、法令条文本身需要进一步明确界限或作补充规定的，由全国人民代表大会常务委员会分别进行解释或用法令加以规定。
    二、凡关于审判过程中如何具体应用法律、法令的问题，由最高人民法院审判委员会进行解释。
