Title: The Indonesia-China Friendship Association in Yogyakarta, Indonesia, is Established
Author:
Date: 1955-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th—The Indonesia-China Friendship Association in Yogyakarta, Indonesia, was officially established on the 17th, with Professor Poerbatjaraka serving as chairman.

### 印度尼西亚日惹的印度尼西亚—中国友好协会成立

1955-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚日惹的印度尼西亚—中国友好协会成立
      新华社雅加达二十日电印度尼西亚日惹的印度尼西亚—中国友好协会十七日正式成立，由普波迪宁格拉特教授担任主席。
