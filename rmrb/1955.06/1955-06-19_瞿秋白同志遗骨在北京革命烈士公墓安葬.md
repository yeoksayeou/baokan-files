Title: Comrade Qu Qiubai's Remains Interred at the Beijing Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery
Author:
Date: 1955-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: The remains of Comrade Qu Qiubai, who was murdered by the Kuomintang reactionaries in Changting County, Fujian Province, twenty years ago, were interred at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Beijing on the 18th.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China specially chose June 18th, the 20th anniversary of Comrade Qu Qiubai's heroic sacrifice, to inter his remains. Comrade Qu Qiubai's remains were transported from Changting County, Fujian Province, to Beijing in 1954.

At 9:00 a.m., the interment ceremony began amidst mournful music. Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, presided over the ceremony, with Dong Biwu, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Lu Dingyi, Zhou Jianren, Ye Shengtao, and Xu Guangping as accompanying mourners. Yang Zhihua, Comrade Qu Qiubai's wife, and many of his former comrades-in-arms also attended the interment ceremony.

Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, delivered a report on Comrade Qu Qiubai's life at the interment ceremony.

Comrade Qu Qiubai's tomb is surrounded by evergreen pines and cypresses, and lush green poplars. A portrait of Comrade Qu Qiubai was placed in front of the tomb, surrounded by colorful blooming flowers.

After Comrade Qu Qiubai's remains were placed in the tomb, Comrade Zhou Enlai laid a wreath made of white peonies in front of the tomb. At this time, the band played the Internationale, and those attending the interment ceremony gathered around the tomb, mourning this great warrior of the Chinese working class with infinite admiration and remembrance.

Comrade Qu Qiubai was born in Changzhou, Jiangsu Province, in 1899. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1922 and was elected as a member of the Party Central Committee in 1923. In 1927, he was elected as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. In 1928, he attended the Sixth Congress of the Communist International in Moscow and was elected as a member of the Executive Committee of the Communist International and a member of the Presidium. In 1933, Comrade Qu Qiubai entered the Central Revolutionary Base Area centered in southern Jiangxi Province and western Fujian Province. On February 23, 1935, he was arrested by the Kuomintang bandit army in Wuping County, Fujian Province. He heroically sacrificed his life in Changting County, Fujian Province, on June 18th. ### 瞿秋白同志遗骨在北京革命烈士公墓安葬

1955-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    瞿秋白同志遗骨在北京革命烈士公墓安葬
    据新华社十八日讯　二十年前在福建省长汀县被国民党反动派杀害的瞿秋白同志的遗骨，十八日在北京八宝山革命烈士公墓安葬。
    六月十八日是瞿秋白同志英勇就义二十周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特地选择了这个日子来安葬他的遗骨。瞿秋白同志的遗骨是在一九五四年由福建省长汀县运来北京的。
    上午九时，安葬仪式在哀乐声中开始。由中国共产党中央书记处书记周恩来主祭，董必武、康生、彭真、陆定一、周建人、叶圣陶、许广平陪祭。瞿秋白同志的夫人杨之华和他的生前战友多人也参加了安葬仪式。
    中共中央宣传部部长陆定一在安葬仪式上作了关于瞿秋白同志生平的报告。
    瞿秋白同志的墓地在长青的松柏和葱绿的杨树环绕之中，墓前摆着瞿秋白同志的遗像，周围饰满了各色盛开的鲜花。
    瞿秋白同志的遗骨入穴后，周恩来同志将一个由白色牡丹扎成的花圈献在墓前。这时，乐队奏起国际歌，参加安葬仪式的人围绕在墓地的周围，他们怀着无限敬仰和怀念的心情，吊念这位中国工人阶级的伟大战士。
    瞿秋白同志是江苏常州市人，生于一八九九年，一九二二年加入中国共产党，一九二三年被选为党的中央委员。一九二七年被选为中共中央书记。一九二八年，他在莫斯科出席了共产国际第六次代表大会，并被选为共产国际执行委员会委员和主席团委员。一九三三年，瞿秋白同志进入以江西省南部和福建省西部为中心的中央革命根据地。一九三五年二月二十三日，他在福建省武平县被国民党匪军逮捕。六月十八日在福建省长汀县英勇就义。（附图片）
