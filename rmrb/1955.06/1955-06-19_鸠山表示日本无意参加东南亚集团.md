Title: Hatoyama Indicates Japan Has No Intention of Joining Southeast Asia Group
Author:
Date: 1955-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 18th: Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama said on the 17th in the Senate Foreign Affairs Committee that he currently has no plans for Japan to join the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

Hatoyama's remarks were made in response to an inquiry from a Liberal Party senator regarding whether the Japanese government intends to join the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

### 鸠山表示日本无意参加东南亚集团

1955-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山表示日本无意参加东南亚集团
    新华社十八日讯　东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎十七日在参议院外务委员会说，他目前并没有关于日本参加东南亚条约组织的计划。
    鸠山的这番话是用来答复自由党参议员所提出的日本政府是否想参加东南亚条约组织的质询的。
