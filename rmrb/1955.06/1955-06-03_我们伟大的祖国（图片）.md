Author:
Date: 1955-06-03

    Our Great Motherland
    A train power plant has appeared in Jiamusi City, Heilongjiang Province. The complete set of equipment for this train power plant was supplied to our country by the Soviet Union. The carriages contain equipment such as boilers, turbine generators, repair shops, offices, canteens, and dormitories for on-duty personnel. This is a train power plant composed of carriages.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    黑龙江省佳木斯市出现了一座列车发电厂。这座列车发电厂的全套设备都是苏联供给我国的。车厢内有锅炉、透平发电机、修理厂、办公室、食堂和值班人员宿舍等设备。这是由一节节车厢组成的列车发电厂。
