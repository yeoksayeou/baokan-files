Title: Our Representatives Travel to Argentina to Participate in the Argentine International Trade Promotion Committee Conference
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 10th: In response to an invitation to participate in the Second Conference of the Argentine International Trade Promotion Committee, which is about to convene in Buenos Aires, the Chinese representatives—Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; Xu Shengwu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Chen Fengping, Deputy Director of the Economic Research Office of the same council—departed from Prague on June 8th for Buenos Aires.

### 参加阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会大会  我国代表前往阿根廷

1955-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    参加阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会大会
    我国代表前往阿根廷
    新华社布拉格十日电　应邀参加即将在布宜诺斯艾利斯召开的阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会第二届大会的中国代表，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉、中国国际贸易促进委员会联络部副部长徐绳武、该会经济研究室副主任陈凤平等人，已在六月八日离布拉格前往布宜诺斯艾利斯。
