Title: Premier Zhou Receives Polish Science and Technology Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the Polish Science and Technology Delegation at 11:30 AM on June 9th. The delegation was in China to participate in the Second Session of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee on Technical and Technical Science Cooperation. The head of the delegation was Mi. Lesz, Vice Chairman of the State Economic Planning Commission of the Polish People's Republic. The members included: Le. Kasman, Vice Minister of Light Industry; Ju. Burkin, Vice Minister of Land and Air Transportation; S. Pierzhenka, Vice Minister of Metallurgy; S. Bukovsky, Vice Minister of Shipping; Z. Koi, Assistant Minister of the Machine Building Industry; and D. Deoretsky, Secretary of the delegation.
    Present at the reception were Wang Shoudao, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee on Technical and Technical Science Cooperation and Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Fan Ziwem, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, both members of the Chinese group.
    Also present at the reception was Maiman, Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic in China.

### 周总理接见波兰科学技术代表团

1955-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见波兰科学技术代表团
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在九日上午十一时三十分接见来我国参加中波技术和技术科学合作联合常任委员会第二届会议的波兰科学技术代表团团长、波兰人民共和国国家经济计划委员会副主席米·莱什，团员——轻工业部副部长莱·卡斯曼、陆空运输部副部长尤·布尔金、冶金部副部长斯·彼尔任卡、航运部副部长斯·布考夫斯基、机器制造工业部部长助理兹·科伊和代表团秘书达·德奥列茨基。
    接见时在座的有中波技术和技术科学合作联合常任委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国国务院第六办公室主任王首道，中国组委员——对外贸易部副部长范子文、外交部部长助理何伟。
    接见时，波兰人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办马伊曼也在座。
