Title: Prime Minister Nehru Leaves Moscow for Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-06-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 23rd, TASS dispatch: The Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, Nehru, his daughter Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and accompanying personnel, left Moscow for Warsaw at 9:00 a.m. on June 23rd after paying an official visit to the Soviet Union.
    The leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government who went to the Central Airport to see Nehru off included: Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, ministers of various Soviet ministries, representatives of the working people of Moscow, personnel of the Indian Embassy in the Soviet Union, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Moscow, and Soviet and foreign journalists.
    Bulganin and Nehru delivered speeches at the airport.
    Accompanying Nehru to Warsaw were Molochkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and K.P.S. Menon, the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

### 尼赫鲁总理离开莫斯科去华沙

1955-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理离开莫斯科去华沙
    新华社二十三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：印度共和国总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁、他的女儿英·甘地夫人和随行人员在苏联作了正式访问后于二十三日上午九时离莫斯科去华沙。
    到中央机场欢送尼赫鲁的有苏联共产党和政府的领导人：布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，苏联各部部长，莫斯科劳动人民的代表，印度驻苏联大使馆人员，各国驻莫斯科的使节，苏联和外国的记者。
    布尔加宁和尼赫鲁在机场上发表了讲话。
    陪同尼赫鲁去华沙的有苏联外交部交际司司长莫洛奇科夫和印度驻苏大使克·普·西·梅农。
