Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Holds Talks with Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo of the Republic of Indonesia, who came to our country for a visit at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, held talks with Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on the mornings of May 27 and 28, the afternoon of May 29, and the afternoon of June 2.

Juanda, Director of the National Planning Bureau of the Republic of Indonesia, and Hasuno Reksoamojo, a senior official of the Foreign Economic Relations Department of the Ministry of Economy of the Republic of Indonesia, who accompanied Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China, held several talks with Zhang Xi and Xue Muqiao, Deputy Directors of the State Planning Commission of our country, Secretary-General Wang Guangwei, and Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, and Vice Minister Kong Yuan, from May 27 to 29, respectively.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理同周恩来总理会谈

1955-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理同周恩来总理会谈
    新华社三日讯　应中华人民共和国政府邀请前来我国访问的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约，曾在五月二十七日上午，二十八日上午，二十九日下午，六月二日下午，同中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来进行了会谈。
    随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理访华的印度尼西亚共和国国家计划局局长朱安达和印度尼西亚共和国经济部对外经济关系司高级官员哈苏诺·雷克梭阿莫佐，曾在五月二十七日至二十九日，分别同我国国家计划委员会副主任张玺、薛暮桥，秘书长王光伟和我国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长孔原，进行了数次会谈。
