Title: Prime Minister Nehru Thanks Chairman Bulganin for the Welcome and Hospitality Received During His Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-06-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 28th: According to TASS, Indian Prime Minister Nehru sent a telegram from Warsaw on the 25th to Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin, expressing gratitude for the welcome and hospitality received during his visit to the Soviet Union.

The telegram said: "At the end of my visit to the Soviet Union, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to Your Excellency and your colleagues for all the care, kind welcome and cordial hospitality you have extended to me. I would also like to let you know how touched I was by the spontaneous affection shown to me by the Soviet people wherever I went; the places I have visited and the people I have spoken to will be etched in my memory forever. If my visit has helped to deepen our mutual understanding of our views, to promote our friendship and our commitment to common well-being, then I will feel that I have been adequately rewarded.

I look forward to meeting you in India."

### 对在苏联访问期间所受到的欢迎和招待  尼赫鲁总理致电布尔加宁主席表示感谢

1955-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    对在苏联访问期间所受到的欢迎和招待
    尼赫鲁总理致电布尔加宁主席表示感谢
    新华社二十八日讯据塔斯社莫斯科讯：印度共和国总理尼赫鲁二十五日从华沙致电苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，对在苏联访问期间所受到的欢迎和招待表示感谢。
    电文说：我在结束了对苏联的访问后，想对您阁下和您的同事所给予我的一切关怀、善意的欢迎和殷勤的招待表示真诚的感谢。我还想告诉您知道，我该是如何地为我每到一个地方时苏联人民所对我表示的那种自发的热爱所感动；我所访问过的地方和同我所交谈过的人，将在我的记忆中永志不忘。如果我的访问有助于使我们的观点得到更深刻的互相谅解、促进了我们的友谊以及我们致力于共同幸福的决心，那末我就将感到：我获得了足够的酬报。
    我等待着同您在印度会见。
