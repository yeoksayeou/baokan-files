Title: Polish Academy of Sciences Delegation and Science and Technology Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th. The Polish Academy of Sciences delegation, which was invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit our country, left Beijing on the 10th to return home. The Polish delegation visited Beijing, Harbin, Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities in our country, and visited the research institutions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, such as the Institute of Mathematics and the Institute of Civil Engineering and Architecture, as well as the universities in the above-mentioned cities. The delegation also visited factories and mines in Anshan, Fushun and other places, and the Wuhan Bridge construction site. The scientists of the delegation also held many seminars with scientists from all over our country, and gave academic reports on engineering structure, mathematics and mechanics. They participated in the inaugural meeting of the academic departments of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing, and Academician Wierzbicki, the head of the delegation, also gave an academic report at the meeting.

Before leaving Beijing, the Polish Academy of Sciences delegation held talks with representatives of the Chinese Academy of Sciences on the cooperation plan between the two academies, and signed the minutes of the talks on the cooperation plan between the two academies on the afternoon of the 8th. Academician Wierzbicki, head of the Polish Academy of Sciences delegation and a civil engineer, put forward specific opinions on the development direction and long-term plan of the Institute of Civil Engineering and Architecture of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th. The head of the Polish Science and Technology Delegation, Mi. Lesz, and others left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 10th to return to their country after coming to Beijing to participate in the second meeting of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee on Technical and Technical Science Cooperation and signing the protocol.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Wang Shoudao, Chairman of the Chinese Group and Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council, all members of the Chinese Group, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei, Vice Minister of Light Industry Wang Xinyuan, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, Majman, and others.

### 波兰科学院代表团和科学技术代表团回国

1955-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰科学院代表团和科学技术代表团回国
    新华社十一日讯　应中国科学院邀请前来我国访问的波兰科学院代表团已在十日离开北京回国。波兰代表团在我国访问了北京、哈尔滨、沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、广州、武汉、南京、上海、杭州等城市，参观了中国科学院的数学研究所、土木建筑研究所等研究机构和上述各城市的高等学校，代表团还参观了鞍山、抚顺等地的厂矿和武汉大桥工地。代表团的科学家并与我国各地的科学家举行过多次座谈，作了有关工程结构方面、数学与力学方面的学术报告。他们在北京参加了中国科学院学部成立大会，团长维日比茨基院士并在会上作了学术报告。
    波兰科学院代表团在离开北京前，曾与中国科学院的代表进行了关于中波两国科学院合作计划的会谈，并在八日下午签订了关于两国科学院合作计划会谈纪录。波兰科学院代表团团长、土木工程学家维日比茨基院士对中国科学院土木建筑研究所的发展方向与远景计划提出了具体的意见。
    新华社十一日讯　波兰科学技术代表团团长米·莱什等来北京参加中波技术和技术科学合作联合常任委员会第二届会议并签订了议定书后，已在十日上午乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有中国组主席、国务院第六办公室主任王首道和中国组全体委员，外交部部长助理何伟，轻工业部副部长王新元，以及波兰驻华大使馆临时代办马伊曼等。
