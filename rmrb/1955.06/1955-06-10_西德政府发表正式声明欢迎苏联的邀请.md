Title: West German Government Issues Official Statement Welcoming Soviet Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1955-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin (via TASS), June 9th: The West German Federal Government has issued an official statement welcoming the Soviet invitation.

    The statement says: The Federal Government welcomes the Soviet Union's note of June 7 to the Federal Government regarding the proposal to establish diplomatic, trade, and cultural relations between the Soviet Union and the Federal Republic of Germany. The note raises various questions that require preliminary study, and this study has already begun. It is hoped that the result of the study will be that it is appropriate for the Federal Chancellor to hold consultations with Soviet government officials.

### 西德政府发表正式声明欢迎苏联的邀请

1955-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    西德政府发表正式声明欢迎苏联的邀请
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：西德联邦政府发表正式声明，欢迎苏联的邀请。
    这个声明说：联邦政府欢迎苏联六月七日致联邦政府的照会中所提的关于苏联和德意志联邦共和国建立外交、贸易和文化关系的建议。照会提出了各种问题，有必要对这些问题进行初步的研究，而这种研究已经开始了。可以希望，研究的结果将会是：联邦总理同苏联的政府人士举行商谈是适宜的。
