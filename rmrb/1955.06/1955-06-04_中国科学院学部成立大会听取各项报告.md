Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Inaugural Meeting Heard Various Reports
Author:
Date: 1955-06-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: The inaugural meeting of the Chinese Academy of Sciences' Academic Divisions heard several important reports over the two days of June 2nd and 3rd.

    Chen Yi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, delivered a report at the afternoon session on the 3rd on several important policies of the Party and the government and the current international situation, and gave important instructions on holding a successful inaugural meeting of the Academic Divisions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    On the morning of the 2nd, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, delivered a report on the work of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    Zhang Jiafu, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, presented a draft outline of the First Five-Year Plan of the Chinese Academy of Sciences on behalf of the Standing Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, requesting discussion by the scientists present. Zhang Jiafu explained the issues of concentrating efforts on the dominant aspects of science and focusing on the development of scientific undertakings, the development and regional distribution of scientific research institutions, and the speed of scientific development in the draft outline of the First Five-Year Plan of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    On the afternoon of the 2nd, the four academic divisions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences held separate meetings. Wu Youxun, Director of the Department of Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry; Zhu Kezhen, Director of the Department of Biology and Earth Sciences; Yan Jici, Director of the Department of Technical Sciences; and Pan Zinian, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences, each delivered reports on the work of their respective academic divisions to the members.

    On the morning of the 3rd, Academician Bardin, head of the delegation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, delivered a report entitled "Scientific Research Work in Metallurgical Problems in the Soviet Union"; Academician Wierzbicki, head of the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences and Vice President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, delivered a report entitled "Numerical Methods on the Stability Problems of Certain Engineering Structures."

### 中国科学院学部成立大会听取各项报告

1955-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院学部成立大会听取各项报告
    新华社三日讯　中国科学院学部成立大会在二日三日两天内听取了多项重要报告。
    中国共产党中央委员会委员、国务院副总理陈毅在三日下午的大会上作了关于当前党和政府的几项重要政策和目前国际形势的报告，并对开好中国科学院学部成立大会作了重要指示。
    二日上午，中国科学院院长郭沫若作了关于中国科学院工作的报告。
    中国科学院副院长张稼夫代表中国科学院院务常务委员会向大会提出了中国科学院第一个五年计划纲要草案，要求到会科学家讨论。张稼夫对中国科学院第一个五年计划纲要草案中关于集中力量于科学的主导方面和重点发展科学事业问题、关于科学研究机构的发展和地区分布问题以及关于科学事业发展速度问题作了说明。
    二日下午，中国科学院的四个学部分别开会。物理学数学化学部主任吴有训、生物学地学部主任竺可桢、技术科学部主任严济慈、哲学社会科学部副主任潘梓年分别向各学部的委员们作了关于学部工作的报告。
    三日上午，苏联科学院代表团团长、苏联科学院副院长巴尔金院士作了题为“苏联冶金问题中的科学研究工作”的报告；波兰科学院代表团团长、波兰科学院副院长维日比茨基院士作了题为“关于某些工程结构稳定性问题的数值法”的报告。
