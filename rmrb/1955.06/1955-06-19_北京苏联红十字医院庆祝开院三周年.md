Title: Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital Celebrates its Third Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1955-06-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 18th. The Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital held a celebration meeting on June 18th to celebrate its third anniversary.

    More than 600 people, including all Chinese and Soviet staff members of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital and guests, attended the celebration meeting. Vasnov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Sgaqo, and Soviet medical experts in Beijing also attended the celebration meeting.

    Bazhenov, the director of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, gave a report on the work of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital in the past three years at the celebration meeting. In his report, he praised the hospital's outstanding staff. Vice-Director Zhu Zhongli also gave a report at the meeting, in which she cited many vivid and specific facts to introduce the advanced medical ideas, work style, and conscientious service attitude of the Soviet medical staff. She also expressed, on behalf of the Chinese staff, her gratitude to the Soviet government and Soviet experts for their great contribution to the Chinese people over the past three years.

    Wu Yuzhang, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting. He said: Over the past three years, the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital has made rapid development and great achievements. Soviet experts have contributed outstanding wisdom and hard work to the health of our people and the construction of the people's health cause. Wu Yuzhang, in the name of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, expressed heartfelt thanks to the Soviet experts and the Soviet government.

    Peng Zemin, Vice-President of the Red Cross Society of China, Cui Yitian, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Public Health of the People's Republic of China, Xue Zizheng, Vice-Mayor of Beijing, and Yan Jingqing, Director of the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Public Health, also spoke at the meeting.

    At the meeting, representatives of hospitalized patients presented flowers and gifts to the Soviet experts. In the past three years, patients from all over the country have visited the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital more than 267,000 times, and more than 3,900 patients have been hospitalized for treatment. More than 3,700 patients have been discharged from the hospital after a rapid recovery. Patients who came to the hospital received careful treatment and cordial care from Soviet experts, and many patients suffering from difficult and complicated diseases or diseases that other hospitals considered difficult to treat were satisfactorily treated in this hospital.

### 北京苏联红十字医院庆祝开院三周年

1955-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    北京苏联红十字医院庆祝开院三周年
    新华社十八日讯　北京苏联红十字医院六月十八日举行了开院三周年庆祝大会。
    参加庆祝会的有北京苏联红十字医院的全体中苏工作人员和来宾共六百多人。苏联驻我国大使馆参赞华士诺夫、司高磋以及在京的苏联医学专家等也参加了庆祝大会。
    北京苏联红十字医院院长巴任诺夫在庆祝大会上作了关于北京苏联红十字医院三年来工作情况的报告，他在报告中表扬了医院优秀的工作人员。副院长朱仲丽也在大会上作了报告，她列举很多生动具体的事实介绍了苏联医务人员先进的医疗思想、工作作风和认真负责的服务态度。她并代表中国工作人员感谢苏联政府和苏联专家三年来对中国人民的伟大贡献。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章在大会上讲话。他说：三年来，北京苏联红十字医院有了迅速的发展和巨大的成绩。苏联专家为我国人民的健康和人民卫生事业的建设贡献了卓越的智慧和辛勤的劳动。吴玉章以中苏友好协会总会的名义，向苏联专家和苏联政府表示衷心的感谢。
    中国红十字会总会副会长彭泽民、中华人民共和国卫生部副部长崔义田、北京市副市长薛子正和北京市公共卫生局局长严镜清也在大会上讲了话。
    大会上，住院病人代表向苏联专家献花献礼。三年来，全国各地的病人到北京苏联红十字医院的就诊次数达二十六万七千多次，住院治疗的病人有三千九百多人，已有三千七百多病人迅速恢复健康后出院。来医院就诊的病人得到苏联专家认真的治疗和亲切的照顾，很多患疑难病症或其他医院认为难治的病症在这个医院里都得到了满意的治疗。
