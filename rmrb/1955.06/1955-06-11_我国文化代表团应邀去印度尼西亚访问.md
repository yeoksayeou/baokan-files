Title: China's Cultural Delegation Invited to Visit Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1955-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: The Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by head of delegation Zheng Zhenduo, was invited by the government of the Republic of Indonesia to visit and perform in Indonesia, and departed from Beijing on the 10th. Those who came to see them off included Vice Ministers of Culture Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, China's Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Bao Erhan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Wu Huazhi, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Wiyo Wiryodoyo, and others.

The China-Indonesia Friendship Association held a tea party on the evening of the 6th, and the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Wiyo Wiryodoyo, held a banquet on the evening of the 9th to bid farewell to the Chinese Cultural Delegation.

The Chinese Cultural Delegation will visit and perform in Indonesia for a period of two months. The delegation consists of seventy-seven people, led by Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture and writer, as the head of the delegation, and Zhou Erfu, writer, as the deputy head of the delegation. The members of the delegation include China's famous singers, dancers, and Peking opera actors. The delegation will perform in Indonesia, including singing programs such as "China-Indonesia Friendship Song" and folk songs from all over China, dance programs such as Plate Dance, Long Silk Dance, Zu Bei Sun, Lotus Dance, classical dance dramas such as Havoc in Heaven, San Cha Kou, and Stealing the Immortal Herb, as well as Chinese national instrumental music performances.

### 我国文化代表团应邀去印度尼西亚访问

1955-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团应邀去印度尼西亚访问
    新华社十日讯　应印度尼西亚共和国政府邀请前往印度尼西亚访问和演出的中国文化代表团在团长郑振铎率领下，已在十日从北京启程。前往欢送的有文化部副部长丁西林、夏衍，我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇，外交部第二亚洲司司长章文晋，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长吴化之，以及印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办维约维尔多约等人。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会曾在六日晚举行茶会，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办维约维尔多约在九日晚举行宴会，欢送中国文化代表团。
    中国文化代表团将在印度尼西亚作为期两月的访问演出。代表团共七十七人，由文化部副部长、文学家郑振铎任团长，作家周而复任副团长。团员包括我国著名的歌唱家、舞蹈演员和京剧演员。代表团将在印度尼西亚表演的，有“中国印度尼西亚友好歌”和中国各地民歌等歌唱节目，有盘子舞、长绸舞、祖背孙、荷花舞等舞蹈节目，有闹天宫、三岔口、盗仙草等古典舞剧，还有中国民族器乐演奏。
