Title: Soviet Government Delegation Returns to Moscow from Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: The Soviet government delegation, headed by Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and First Secretary of The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and including Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Mikoyan, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, returned to Moscow on June 5 after holding talks with the government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and visiting Sofia and Bucharest on the way back to their country. (Other members of the Soviet government delegation had returned to Moscow earlier.)

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest Dispatch from Romanian News Agency: Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and András Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, left Bucharest on the 5th after visiting Romania.

Xinhua News Agency reported from Romanian News Agency: At the invitation of Romania, Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and Secretary Jiří Hendrych left Bucharest by plane on the afternoon of June 5.

### 苏联政府代表团从布加勒斯特回到莫斯科

1955-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团从布加勒斯特回到莫斯科
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫率领的、包括苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬的苏联政府代表团，在同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府举行了谈判并在回国途中访问了索非亚和布加勒斯特后，于六月五日回到莫斯科（苏联政府代表团的其他团员已先期回莫斯科）。
    据新华社讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西和匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席安·赫格居斯在罗马尼亚访问后，已于五日离开布加勒斯特。
    据新华社讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：应邀到罗马尼亚访问的捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记安·诺沃提尼和书记伊·亨德里赫已在六月五日下午乘飞机离开布加勒斯特。
