Title: Zhengzhou Pesticide Factory's Second Phase Expansion Begins
Author:
Date: 1955-06-05
Page: 2

    Zhengzhou Pesticide Factory's Second Phase Expansion Begins
    The first phase of expansion of the Zhengzhou Pesticide Factory, a local state-owned enterprise, has been basically completed recently. Now, the second phase of expansion of this factory has also started construction. In the second phase of expansion, the factory will continue to build some factory buildings and warehouses, and install Raymond pulverizers and other new machines capable of producing 60,000 kilograms of powder per day. After the completion of the entire expansion project at the end of this year, the main production process of the factory will be mechanically operated, which can increase the original production capacity by more than two times.
    From January to mid-May this year, the factory has supplied 10,478,100 kilograms of wettable and various ingredient content 666 powder to the countryside. This powder sells well in twenty provinces including Anhui, Guangdong, Liaoning, Inner Mongolia, Sichuan, and Shaanxi, and is very popular among farmers. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 郑州农药厂第二期扩建开始

1955-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    郑州农药厂第二期扩建开始
    地方国营郑州农药厂的第一期扩建工程已在最近基本完成。现在，这个厂扩建的第二期工程也开始施工了。这个厂在扩建的第二期工程中，要继续建筑一些厂房和仓库，并安装一天能生产六万公斤粉剂的雷蒙式粉碎机和其他新式的机器。扩建工程在今年年底全部完成后，这个厂的主要生产过程将由机械操作，可比原来的生产能力提高二倍以上。
    从今年一月到五月中旬，这个厂供应农村的可湿性的和各种成分含量不等的六六六粉剂，已经有
  一千零四十七万八千一百多公斤。这种粉剂畅销到安徽、广东、辽宁、内蒙古、四川、陕西等二十个省份，很受农民欢迎。　（据新华社讯）
