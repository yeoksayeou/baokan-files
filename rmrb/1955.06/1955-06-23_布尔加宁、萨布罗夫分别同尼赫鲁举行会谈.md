Title: Bulganin and Saburov Hold Talks with Nehru Respectively
Author:
Date: 1955-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 22nd - According to TASS, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin held talks with Indian Republic Prime Minister Nehru in the Kremlin on June 21st.

Kaganovich, Mikoyan, and Khrushchev also participated in the talks.

On the Indian side, Nehru's entourage included Indian Foreign Secretary Pillai, Indian Foreign Ministry's Director of European Affairs Hussain, Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union K.P.S. Menon, and First Secretary of the Embassy, Gore. On the Soviet side, First Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko and Kuznetsov also attended the talks.

The talks were conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 22nd - According to TASS, Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman and Chairman of the State Economic Committee for the National Economic Annual Plan of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Saburov, held lengthy talks with Indian Republic Prime Minister Nehru on June 21st.

Participating in the talks were Nehru's entourage, including Indian Foreign Secretary Pillai, Indian Foreign Ministry's Director of European Affairs Hussain, Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union K.P.S. Menon, and First Secretary of the Embassy, Gore.

Also participating in the talks were Chairman of the State Committee for Long-Term National Economic Planning of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Baibakov, Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection, Antropov, First Deputy Foreign Minister of the Soviet Union, Kuznetsov, Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov, and others.

### 布尔加宁、萨布罗夫分别同尼赫鲁举行会谈

1955-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁、萨布罗夫分别同尼赫鲁举行会谈
    新华社二十二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁同印度共和国总理尼赫鲁二十一日在克里姆林宫举行了会谈。
    卡冈诺维奇、米高扬和赫鲁晓夫也参加了会谈。
    印度方面出席会谈的还有尼赫鲁的随员印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼、印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农和大使馆一等秘书高尔。苏联方面出席会谈的还有苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯和库兹涅佐夫。
    会谈在诚挚和友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社二十二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联部长会议国民经济年度计划国家经济委员会主席萨布罗夫二十一日和印度共和国总理尼赫鲁进行了长时间的会谈。
    参加会谈的有尼赫鲁的随员印度外交部秘书长皮莱、印度外交部欧洲司司长侯赛尼、印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农和大使馆一等秘书高尔。
    参加会谈的还有苏联部长会议国民经济长期计划国家委员会主席巴伊巴科夫、地质和矿藏保护部长安特罗波夫、苏联外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫、苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫等。
