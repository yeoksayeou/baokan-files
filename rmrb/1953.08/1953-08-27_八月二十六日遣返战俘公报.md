Title: Public Communiqué on the Repatriation of Prisoners of War on August 26th
Author:
Date: 1953-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 26th: On the 26th, our side repatriated 150 non-Korean prisoners of war and 250 Korean prisoners of war to the other side. Among the non-Korean prisoners of war were 133 American and 17 British prisoners. The total number of prisoners of war repatriated by our side to the other side was 400.

The other side repatriated 2,398 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side.

### 八月二十六日遣返战俘公报

1953-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    八月二十六日遣返战俘公报
    【新华社开城二十六日电】二十六日，我方遣送给对方非朝鲜籍战俘一百五十名，朝鲜籍战俘二百五十名。在非朝鲜籍战俘中包括美国籍一百三十三名，英国籍十七名。我方遣送给对方的战俘总数是四百名。
    对方遣送给我方朝鲜人民军被俘人员两千三百九十八名。
