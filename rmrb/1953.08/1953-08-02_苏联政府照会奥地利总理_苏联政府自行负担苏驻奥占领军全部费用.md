Title: Soviet Government Note to Austrian Chancellor: Soviet Government to Bear All Expenses of Soviet Occupation Troops in Austria
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, July 31st) TASS Vienna, July 31st: Soviet High Commissioner to Austria, I.I. Ilyichev, delivered the following note from the Soviet government to Austrian Chancellor Raab on July 30th:

"In view of the wishes expressed by the Austrian government, the Soviet government considers it possible, from August 1, 1953, to bear all expenses for maintaining Soviet occupation troops in Austria itself, thereby reducing this expenditure item in the Austrian government's budget."

### 苏联政府照会奥地利总理  苏联政府自行负担苏驻奥占领军全部费用

1953-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府照会奥地利总理
    苏联政府自行负担苏驻奥占领军全部费用
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社维也纳七月三十一日讯：苏联驻奥地利高级专员伊·伊·伊利切夫在七月三十日将苏联政府的下列照会送交奥地利总理拉布：
    “鉴于奥地利政府所表示的愿望，苏联政府认为可以从一九五三年八月一日起，由苏联政府自行负担维持苏联驻奥地利占领军的全部费用，从而减除奥地利政府预算中的这一项费用的开支。”
