    Our Great Motherland
    The construction of three major basic construction projects of the state-owned Yunnan Tin Corporation's thermal power plant, concentrator, and aerial transport cableway will play a huge role in the development of tin mine production. The concentrating capacity of this newly built concentrator is close to two-thirds of the company's existing concentrating capacity. Workers are installing shaking tables here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营云南锡业公司火电厂、选矿厂和架空运输索道等三大基本建设工程的兴建，对于发展锡矿生产将起巨大作用。这座新建的选矿厂，它的选矿能力接近于该公司现有选矿能力的三分之二。工人正在这里安装摇床。
