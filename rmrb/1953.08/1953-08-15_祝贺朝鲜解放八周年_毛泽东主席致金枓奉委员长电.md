Title: Congratulations on the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea - Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to Chairman Kim Tu-bong
Author:
Date: 1953-08-15
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
    Telegram from Chairman Mao Zedong to Chairman Kim Tu-bong, President of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:
    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, please accept my heartfelt congratulations to the entire Korean people and to you.
    I wish the heroic Korean people, who have triumphed over the American imperialist aggressors, great achievements in their efforts to prevent the recurrence of the war of aggression and to establish a peaceful, unified, and independent new Korea.
                         Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                           August 12, 1953, in Beijing
    Telegram from Premier Zhou Enlai to Premier Kim Il-sung:
    To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:
    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, I extend my warm congratulations to the Korean people, the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and to you.
    The heroic Korean people will receive the active support and assistance of the entire Chinese people in the cause of restoring war wounds and the national economy and striving for the peace, unification, and independence of Korea.
    I wish the close friendship between the peoples of China and Korea, forged in blood in the common struggle against aggression and in defense of peace, will be forever consolidated.
              Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                            August 12, 1953, in Beijing

### 祝贺朝鲜解放八周年  毛泽东主席致金枓奉委员长电

1953-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺朝鲜解放八周年
    毛泽东主席致金枓奉委员长电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉同志：
    值此朝鲜解放八周年之际，请接受我对全体朝鲜人民及您的衷心祝贺。
    祝战胜了美国帝国主义侵略者的英雄的朝鲜人民，在努力防止侵略战争之再起和建立和平、统一、独立的新朝鲜的事业中获得伟大的成就。
                     中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
                           一九五三年八月十二日于北京
    周恩来总理致金日成首相电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志：
    值此朝鲜解放八周年之际，谨向朝鲜人民、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和您致以热烈的祝贺。
    英雄的朝鲜人民在恢复战争创伤和国民经济及争取朝鲜的和平、统一与独立的事业中，将得到全中国人民的积极支持和帮助。
    祝朝中两国人民在反对侵略、保卫和平的共同斗争中以鲜血结成的亲密友谊永远巩固。
              中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理  周恩来
                            一九五三年八月十二日于北京
