Title: Meeting Held by Korean Military Armistice Commission to Determine Early Establishment of Demarcation Line Markers
Author:
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 1st. The senior members of both sides of the Korean Military Armistice Commission approved the agreement reached by their staff on July 31 regarding the specifications of the military demarcation line markers and the methods for their erection and supervision, at the commission's fifth meeting on August 1. Both sides are now stepping up preparations to begin erecting the military demarcation line markers as soon as possible.
    The sixth meeting of the Military Armistice Commission is scheduled to be held at 11:00 a.m. on August 3.

### 朝鲜军事停战委员会举行会议  确定尽早树立军事分界线标志

1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事停战委员会举行会议
    确定尽早树立军事分界线标志
    【新华社开城一日电】朝鲜军事停战委员会双方首席委员，于八月一日该委员会第五次会议上，批准了双方参谋人员于七月三十一日就军事分界线标志物的规格及其树立与监督办法所达成的协议。双方现正加紧进行各项准备，以便尽速开始树立军事分界线标志物。
    军事停战委员会第六次会议定于八月三日上午十一时举行。
