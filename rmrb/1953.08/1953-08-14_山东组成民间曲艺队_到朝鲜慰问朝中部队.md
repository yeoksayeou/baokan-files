Title: Shandong Forms Folk Art Troupe to Comfort Korean and Chinese Troops

Author: 
Date: 1953-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 13th—People from all walks of life in Shandong Province have voluntarily formed a folk art troupe to visit and comfort the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army in Korea.

    The art troupe has prepared many wonderful performance programs. Most of these programs have Shandong folk characteristics. Among them are the famous Shandong Qinshu and Shandong Kuaishu.

    The art troupe is composed of twenty people, most of whom are outstanding artists from Shandong. Among them are Deng Juru, a 59-year-old Shandong Qinshu artist, and Ding Qingwu, a 14-year-old primary school student and amateur Shandong Kuaishu performer from Jinan. A Guan Sheng (wind instrument) orchestra formed by the Buddhist community in Jinan also accompanied them. The art troupe set off for Korea on the evening of the 9th. Feng Yizhi, director of the Literature and Art Department of the Propaganda Department of the Shandong Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Ding Zhigang, deputy director of the Shandong Provincial People's Government Cultural Affairs Bureau, and others saw them off at the station.

### 山东组成民间曲艺队  到朝鲜慰问朝中部队

1953-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    山东组成民间曲艺队
    到朝鲜慰问朝中部队
    【新华社济南十三日电】山东省各界人民自动组成民间曲艺队，前往朝鲜慰问中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。
    曲艺队准备了许多精彩的演出节目。这些节目绝大部分都带有山东民间色彩。其中有著名的山东琴书、山东快书。
    曲艺队由二十人组成，大部人员都是山东出色的艺人。其中有五十九岁的山东琴书艺人邓九如，济南市十四岁的小学生、山东快书业余说唱者丁庆武等。济南佛教界组成的管笙乐队也随同前往。曲艺队已在九日晚启程赴朝。中共中央山东分局宣传部文艺处处长冯毅之、山东省人民政府文化事业管理局副局长丁志刚等都到车站送行。
