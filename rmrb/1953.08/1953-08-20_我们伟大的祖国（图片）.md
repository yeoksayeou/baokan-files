Our Great Motherland
Tens of thousands of railway employees and laborers on the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway are overcoming numerous difficulties, advancing towards the Wushaoling Mountains, which rise more than 3,000 meters above sea level, the six-kilometer-wide Heihe Beach, and the uninhabited grasslands and Gobi Desert for hundreds of miles. This is the Zhuanglang River Bridge (between Yongdeng County and Gulang County in Gansu Province), the largest bridge project on the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway in 1953. The bridge pier project has been completed, and bridge construction is now underway.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    兰新铁路的数万铁路员工和民工正在克服重重困难，向拔海三千多公尺的乌鞘岭、宽达六公里的黑河滩、百里无人烟的草滩和戈壁滩进军。这是兰新铁路一九五三年最大的一座桥梁工程——庄浪河大桥（在甘肃永登县和古浪县之间）。桥墩工程已经完工，现在正在架桥。
