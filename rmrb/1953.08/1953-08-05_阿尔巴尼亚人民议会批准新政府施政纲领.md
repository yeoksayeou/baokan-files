Title: Albanian People's Assembly Approves New Government's Program
Author:
Date: 1953-08-05
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Tirana) According to TASS, the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania held its fifth session on the 1st.

    The meeting approved the resignation of Omer Nishani from the post of Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and elected Haxhi Lleshi as Chairman of the People's Assembly.

    The People's Assembly discussed the resolutions of the joint meeting held on July 23 by the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, and the Presidium of the People's Assembly, adopted the decree on simplifying government institutions, and adopted the decree amending Article 71 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of Albania and other decrees. The People's Assembly unanimously approved the above-mentioned joint meeting's resolution authorizing Enver Hoxha to reorganize government institutions.

    Enver Hoxha then presented to the People's Assembly the list of the new government, which had been approved by the joint meeting, and reported on the new government's program. After discussion, the People's Assembly approved the list of the new government and its program.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会批准新政府施政纲领

1953-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会批准新政府施政纲领
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会在一日举行了第五次会议。
    会议批准了欧默尔·尼沙尼辞去人民议会主席团主席的职务，并选举哈奇·列希为人民议会主席。
    人民议会讨论了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议和人民议会主席团在七月二十三日举行的联席会议的各项决议，通过了关于简化政府机构的法令，并且通过了修改阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国宪法第七十一条的法令及其他法令。人民议会一致批准上述联席会议关于授权恩维尔·霍查改组政府机构的决议。
    恩维尔·霍查接着向人民议会提出了业经联席会议通过的新政府名单，并且报告了新政府的施政纲领。人民议会经讨论后，批准了新政府名单和施政纲领。
