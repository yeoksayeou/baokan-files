Title: Receptions Held by Our Embassies in the Soviet Union, Korea, Mongolia, and Germany to Celebrate the 26th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 2nd: To celebrate the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Major General Ji He, Military Attaché of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union, held a reception on the evening of the 1st. Among those invited were Soviet Marshal Budyonny, Air Force Marshal Zhavaronkov, Army General Kurasov, Lieutenant General Sinilov, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko, Deputy Ministers of the Soviet Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade Borisov, and people from all walks of life in the Soviet Union. Also invited were ambassadors, ministers, chargés d'affaires, military attachés, and counselors from people's democratic countries and other countries that have diplomatic relations with our country. Charge d'Affaires ad interim Ge Baoquan and personnel of our embassy in the Soviet Union also attended.

During the reception, Marshal Budyonny and Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko raised their glasses to congratulate the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Chinese people, and wished good health to the leader of the Chinese people, Comrade Mao Zedong.

Military Attaché Ji He raised his glass to congratulate the Soviet Communist Party, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people, and wished good health to the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government headed by Comrade Malenkov.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 1st: Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our embassy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a banquet in Pyongyang on the afternoon of August 1st to entertain leading figures from the Korean party, government, and military, as well as people from all walks of life. Among those invited to the banquet were: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; Park Jeong-ae, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, and Park Ui-wan; Hong Ki-ju, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; Kim Dal-hyun and Lee Young, Vice Chairmen of the Supreme People's Assembly; heads of various provinces of the Korean Cabinet and various democratic parties and social organizations; General Nam Il, chief representative of the Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean armistice talks; and more than 300 senior generals of the Korean People's Army. Also invited to the banquet were Soviet and other people's democratic countries' envoys and embassy staff stationed in Korea. Yuan Shengping and over 30 representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers were also invited to participate.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of warmth, solidarity, and friendship. Wonderful performances were given by the Chinese People's Volunteers' cultural troupe during the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: Ulaanbaatar Dispatch: Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our embassy in Mongolia, held a banquet on the evening of the 1st to entertain Mongolian party, government, and military leaders in celebration of the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Among those invited to the banquet were: Mongolian Prime Minister Tsedenbal, Deputy Prime Ministers Shirindib, Luvsan, Maidar, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural Zedib, and ministers of various ministries of the Mongolian government, generals of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army, and over 200 other people. Soviet and Korean ambassadors to Mongolia and embassy staff were also invited to attend. At the meeting, guests and hosts frequently raised their glasses to toast the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, Prime Minister Tsedenbal, and Chairman Malenkov, to toast the consolidation of the socialist camp of peace and democracy headed by the Soviet Union, and to toast the friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere, and a wonderful performance was given by the cultural troupe of the Mongolian Ministry of National Defense after the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 2nd: Our diplomatic mission in the German Democratic Republic held a banquet on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Among those attending the banquet were: Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic Dieckmann, Prime Minister Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Ministers Ulbricht, Nuschke, and other senior government officials and representatives of people's organizations, totaling over 200 people.

Soviet High Commissioner to Germany Ambassador Semyonov and Deputy High Commissioner Yudin attended the banquet.

Heads of diplomatic missions from various people's democratic countries were also invited to attend.

During the banquet, guests and hosts toasted Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, who founded and led the glorious Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army. Guests and hosts toasted President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic, and to the leaders of the Soviet government headed by Malenkov, and to the great Soviet Army.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and pleasant atmosphere.

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年  我驻苏、朝、蒙、德使馆举行招待会

1953-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年
    我驻苏、朝、蒙、德使馆举行招待会
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年，中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆武官吉合少将在一日晚举行招待会。应邀到会的有苏联元帅布琼尼，空军元帅热沃隆科夫，陆军大将库拉索夫、中将西尼洛夫，苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，苏联国内和对外贸易部副部长波里索夫和苏联社会各界人士。应邀到会的还有人民民主国家和其他与我国有邦交关系的各国大使、公使、代办、武官、参赞。出席者还有我驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权和大使馆人员。
    席间布琼尼元帅和葛罗米柯副外长举杯向中国共产党、中国人民解放军、中国人民祝贺，并祝中国人民领袖毛泽东同志健康。
    吉合武官举杯向苏联共产党、苏联政府、苏联人民祝贺，并祝以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党和政府的领袖们健康。
    招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。
    【新华社平壤一日电】我国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆代办甘野陶为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年，于八月一日下午在平壤市举行宴会，招待朝鲜党政军首长和各界人士。应邀出席宴会的有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱，内阁副首相崔庸健、崔昌益、郑一龙、朴义琓，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长洪箕畴，最高人民会议副议长金达铉、李英，朝鲜内阁各省及各民主政党、社会团体负责人，朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团首席代表南日大将和朝鲜人民军高级将领等三百多人。应邀出席宴会的还有苏联和各人民民主国家驻朝鲜使节和使馆人员。中国人民志愿军代表袁升平等三十多人也应邀参加。
    宴会在洋溢着热烈、团结、友爱的气氛中进行。席间由中国人民志愿军文工团演出精彩节目。
    【新华社二日讯】乌兰巴托消息：我驻蒙古大使馆临时代办陈应为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年，于一日晚举行宴会招待蒙古党政军首长。应邀出席宴会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，副总理锡林迪布、鲁布桑、迈达尔，大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布及蒙古政府各部部长、蒙古人民革命军将领等二百余人。苏联、朝鲜驻蒙大使及使馆人员也应邀出席。会上宾主频频举杯祝毛泽东主席、泽登巴尔总理、马林科夫主席的健康，祝以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营的巩固，祝中蒙两国人民的友谊。宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，会后并由蒙古国防部军队文工团演出了精彩节目。
    【新华社柏林二日电】我驻德意志民主共和国外交使团在一日晚举行宴会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年。出席宴会的有：德意志民主共和国人民议院主席狄克曼，总理格罗提渥，副总理乌布利希、努舍克和其他政府高级官员及人民团体的代表等共二百余人。
    苏联驻德高级专员谢麦诺夫大使和副高级专员尤金出席了宴会。
    各人民民主国家外交使团团长也都应邀到会。
    席间，宾主为创立并领导了光荣的中国人民解放军的毛泽东主席和朱德总司令干杯，为英雄的中国人民解放军干杯。宾主为德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克干杯，并为以马林科夫为首的苏联政府领袖及伟大的苏军干杯。
    宴会在友好与愉快的空气中进行。
