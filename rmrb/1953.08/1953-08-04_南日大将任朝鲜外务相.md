Title: General Nam Il Appointed as Foreign Minister of Korea

Author:

Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 3rd: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 3rd: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea appoints General Nam Il as the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 南日大将任朝鲜外务相

1953-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    南日大将任朝鲜外务相
    【新华社平壤三日电】朝鲜中央通讯社三日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会发表政令：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会任命南日大将为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相。
