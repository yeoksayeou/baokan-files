Title: Lhasa Grandly Celebrates "August First"
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 2nd. The People's Liberation Army of the Tibet Military Region and people from all walks of life in Lhasa held a grand "August First" celebration meeting in the square in front of the Potala Palace on the 1st. At the meeting, Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, spoke, congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteers on their great victories on the Korean front and the People's Liberation Army in Tibet on their great achievements on the production and construction front. Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, called on all commanders and fighters to actively study politics and culture, improve military technology, further unite the Tibetan people, work hard in production and road construction, and always maintain a high degree of vigilance to strictly guard against all kinds of evil conspiracies by imperialist instigation and sabotage. Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, a Kalon of the Tibetan local government, also spoke at the meeting. He praised the glorious achievements made by the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Tibet in implementing the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet over the past two years.
　　On the afternoon of the same day, the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Lhasa also held military-civilian symposiums and get-togethers at their respective garrisons. The film projection team of the Political Department of the Tibet Military Region showed the citizens various films about the growth and strengthening of the People's Liberation Army.

### 拉萨隆重庆祝“八一”

1953-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨隆重庆祝“八一”
    【新华社拉萨二日电】中国人民解放军西藏军区部队和拉萨市各界人民一日在布达拉宫前的广场上，举行了隆重的“八一”庆祝大会。会上，中央人民政府代表张经武将军讲话，他祝贺中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线所取得的伟大胜利，和进藏人民解放军在生产建设战线上所获得的巨大成就。西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军号召全体指战员积极学习政治文化，提高军事技术，进一步团结藏族人民，努力生产、修路，并随时保持高度警惕，严防帝国主义挑拨破坏的各种罪恶阴谋。西藏地方政府噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美也在会上讲话。他赞扬人民解放军进藏部队两年来在执行和平解放西藏办法的协议方面获得的辉煌成绩。
    当天下午，驻拉萨人民解放军部队，还分别在驻地举行了军民座谈会和联欢晚会。西藏军区政治部的电影放映队，向市民放映有关人民解放军成长壮大的各种影片。
