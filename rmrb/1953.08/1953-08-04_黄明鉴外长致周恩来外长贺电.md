Title: Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam's Congratulatory Telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author: Huang Mingjian
Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 1

    Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam's Congratulatory Telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
    To Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
    The news of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement and the end of the Korean War has brought boundless joy to the people of Vietnam.
    This is a great victory for the Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers, and also a great victory for the world's peace and democracy camp.
    Please allow me, on behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to congratulate the Chinese people on the great achievements they have made in the cause of resisting American aggression and aiding Korea, defending China, and defending world peace.
    Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, August 1, 1953

### 黄明鉴外长致周恩来外长贺电
黄明鉴
1953-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    黄明鉴外长致周恩来外长贺电中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来：
    当朝鲜停战协定签字和朝鲜战争结束的消息传到越南之后，引起越南人民无限的欢欣。
    这是朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军的巨大胜利，同时也是世界和平民主阵营的巨大胜利。
    请让我代表越南民主共和国政府，祝贺中国人民在抗美援朝、保卫中国和保卫世界和平的事业中所获得的伟大的成就。
    越南民主共和国政府外交部部长  黄明鉴  一九五三年八月一日
