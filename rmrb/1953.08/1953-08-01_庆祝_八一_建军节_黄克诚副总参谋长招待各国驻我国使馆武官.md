Title: Celebrating the "August 1st" Army Day: Deputy Chief of the General Staff Huang Kecheng Hosts Military Attaches from Various Countries' Embassies in China
Author:
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

    Celebrating "August 1st" Army Day
    Deputy Chief of the General Staff Huang Kecheng Hosts Military Attaches from Various Countries' Embassies in China
    Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: To celebrate the 26th anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's "August 1st" Army Day, Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, held a reception on the evening of the 31st to host military attaches from various countries' embassies in China.
    Those invited to attend the reception included: the Soviet Union's newly appointed military attache to China, Lieutenant General Petrosevsky, and the former military attache to China, Lieutenant General Kotov, Polish military attache Colonel Monath, Czechoslovak military attache Colonel Procha, Indonesian military attache Lieutenant Colonel Sharlin, and many deputy military attaches from various countries.
    Accompanying them were: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and more than 90 senior officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's land, sea, and air forces.
    The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. After the banquet, the General Political Department's Cultural Troupe performed musical and dance programs for entertainment.

### 庆祝“八一”建军节  黄克诚副总参谋长招待各国驻我国使馆武官

1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝“八一”建军节
    黄克诚副总参谋长招待各国驻我国使馆武官
    【新华社三十一日讯】为庆祝中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十六周年，中央人民政府人民革
    命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚于三十一日晚举行招待会，招待各国驻我国使馆武官。
    应邀出席招待会的有：苏联新任驻我国武官彼特罗塞夫斯基中将和前任驻我国武官柯托夫中将，
    波兰武官莫纳特上校，捷克斯洛伐克武官普罗查上校，印尼武官沙林中校及各国副武官多人。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋
    长张宗逊、总政治部副主任甘泗淇，以及中国人民解放军陆海空军高级干部九十余人。
    宴会充满了热烈友好的气氛。会后由总政治部文工团演出音乐舞蹈节目助兴。
