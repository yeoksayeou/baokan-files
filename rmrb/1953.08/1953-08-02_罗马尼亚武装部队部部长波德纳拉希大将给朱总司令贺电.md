Title: General Bodnăraș, Minister of the Armed Forces of Romania, Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu
Author:
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1

To Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

    On the occasion of the glorious 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the soldiers of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic extend warm congratulations to the brave soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    General Bodnăraș, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic
    Bucharest, July 31, 1953

### 罗马尼亚武装部队部部长波德纳拉希大将给朱总司令贺电
波德纳拉希
1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚武装部队部部长波德纳拉希大将给朱总司令贺电中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    值此光荣的中国人民解放军建军二十六周年纪念之际，罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队的军人们谨向中国人民解放军勇敢的战士们致以热烈的祝贺。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部部长波德纳拉希大将
    一九五三年七月三十一日于布加勒斯特
