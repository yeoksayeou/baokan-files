Title: On the US Side's Unreasonable Restriction of the Activities of Our Red Cross Representatives: Our Side Sends a Written Invitation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to Investigate
Author:
Date: 1953-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 22nd: At the secretaries' meeting of the Military Armistice Commission on the 22nd, the Korean and Chinese side submitted a letter of invitation to the US side, requesting the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to investigate the US side's violation of the armistice agreement and its imposition of various unreasonable obstructions and restrictions on the activities of the Korean and Chinese Red Cross representatives. The letter of invitation requested the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to dispatch three mobile neutral nation inspection teams to the Koje Island POW camp, the Yeongdeungpo and Imjin River bridge POW transfer stations to investigate the US side's unreasonable obstruction and restriction of Korean and Chinese Red Cross representatives in providing humanitarian services to prisoners of war.

The two sides of the Military Armistice Commission agreed on the 21st to have the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission investigate this incident. The US Secretary, Colonel Coleman, stated at the secretaries' meeting of the Military Armistice Commission today that he would submit this letter of invitation to the US Senior Member, Brigadier General Bryan, as soon as possible and provide a reply as soon as possible.

### 关于美方无理限制我红十字会代表活动事件  我方书面邀请中立国监察委员会调查

1953-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    关于美方无理限制我红十字会代表活动事件
    我方书面邀请中立国监察委员会调查
    【新华社开城二十二日电】在二十二日军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，朝中方面以请求中立国监察委员会调查美方违反停战协定、对朝中红十字会代表施以种种无理阻挠和限制的事件的邀请书递交美方。邀请书请求中立国监察委员会派遣三个机动中立国视察小组，前往巨济岛战俘营、永登浦和临津江桥的战俘转运站，就美方无理阻挠和限制朝中红十字会代表为战俘提供人道主义服务的事件进行调查。
    军事停战委员会双方在二十一日同意由中立国监察委员会对这一事件进行调查。美方秘书长柯尔曼今天在军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上表示，他将尽快地把这一邀请书送交美方首席委员勃里安，并尽快地提出回答。
