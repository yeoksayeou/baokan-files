Title: Returning Liberated Personnel Welcomed Home with Enthusiasm and Care (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 4

Returning Liberated Personnel Welcomed Home with Enthusiasm and Care

In accordance with the Korean Armistice Agreement, the repatriation of prisoners of war eligible for direct repatriation began on August 5th. Returning to the embrace of the motherland, our captured personnel have been warmly welcomed.
                                (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yang Limen)

    Du Ping, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteers (right), cordially comforts the returning captured personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteers in Kaesong.
                                 (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Jie)

    Having suffered all kinds of torment from the American side, our captured personnel, upon returning to the embrace of the motherland, threw away the clothing issued by the American side, sang revolutionary songs loudly, and cheered "Long live the motherland!"
                                 (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Qian Sijie)

    Our returning personnel, upon returning to the embrace of the motherland, immediately received good treatment and recuperation, deeply experiencing the warmth of the motherland.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Cai Zhongzhi)

### 回到祖国的我方被俘人员受到热情的欢迎和亲切的照顾（图片）

1953-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    回到祖国的我方被俘人员受到热情的欢迎和亲切的照顾
    根据朝鲜停战协定，应予直接遣返的战俘八月五日起开始遣返。回到祖国怀抱的我方被俘人员受到热烈的欢迎。
                                （新华社记者  杨礼门摄）
    志愿军政治部副主任杜平（右）在开城亲切慰问归来的志愿军被俘人员。
                                 （新华社记者  张杰摄）
    受尽美方折磨的我方被俘人员在回到祖国怀抱后，纷纷把美方发给的衣物抛掉，高唱革命歌曲，欢呼祖国万岁。
                                 （新华社记者  钱嗣杰摄）
    我方归来人员回到祖国怀抱后，立即得到良好的治疗和休养，深深体会到祖国的温暖。
                                  （新华社记者  蔡忠植摄）
