    Our Great Motherland
    The kelp, which breeds along the coast of Shandong, has reached the harvesting season. This is a group of female workers from the processing group of the state-owned Shandong Aquatic Product Breeding Farm. They are drying kelp on the beach.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在山东沿海一带繁殖的海带，已经到了收割的季节了。这是国营山东水产养殖场加工组的女工。她们正在海滩上进行海带的晒干工作。
