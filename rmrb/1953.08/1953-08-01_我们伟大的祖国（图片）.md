    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Liberation Army is actively studying the advanced experience and military science knowledge of the Soviet Army, in order to quickly master modern combat technology and move towards the goal of building a modern and regular national defense force. This is a scene from an exercise by a unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军积极学习苏军先进经验和军事科学知识，以求迅速地掌握现代化的战斗技术，向着建设现代化正规化国防军的目标迈进。这是中国人民解放军某部演习时的一个场面。
