Title: Government Administration Council Holds its 186th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st — The 186th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 20th. The meeting listened to and approved the report on farmland water conservancy work made by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy of the Central People's Government. The meeting also approved the list of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一八六次会议

1953-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八六次会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十六次政务会议在二十日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的关于农田水利工作的报告。会议并通过了提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的各项名单。
