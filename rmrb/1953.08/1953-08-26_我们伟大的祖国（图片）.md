    Our Great Motherland
    Students from the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Engineering have been engaged in production learning at the Anshan Iron and Steel Company for more than a year and have acquired a great deal of practical knowledge. This is a group of students from the ironmaking and steel rolling group of the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Engineering visiting the Anshan Iron and Steel Company. They are studying how this bridge-shaped giant crane works.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京钢铁学院的学生在鞍山钢铁公司进行了一年多的生产学习，获得了许多实际知识。这是在鞍山钢铁公司参观的北京钢铁学院炼铁轧钢小组的学生。他们正在研究这座桥形的巨大起重机怎样工作。
