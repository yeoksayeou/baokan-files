Title: USSR State Political Literature Publishing House Publishes Malenkov's Speech as a Separate Booklet
Author:
Date: 1953-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: The USSR State Political Literature Publishing House has published Comrade G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, speech at the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR as a separate booklet. Five million copies of the booklet were printed.

### 苏联国家政治书籍出版局  出版马林科夫演说单行本

1953-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国家政治书籍出版局
    出版马林科夫演说单行本
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：苏联国家政治书籍出版局已将苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫同志在苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议上的演说印成单行本出版，单行本共印了五百万册。
