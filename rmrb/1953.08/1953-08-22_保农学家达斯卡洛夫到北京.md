Title: Bulgarian Agronomist Daskalov Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-08-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 20th—Bulgarian agricultural scientist Academician Hristo Daskalov arrived in Beijing by plane on the 20th. He is here to study agriculture in China according to the annual cultural cooperation implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Daskalov is an Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and is currently the Director of the Institute of Crop Breeding of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences. He is also a member of the National Assembly of the People’s Republic of Bulgaria and a member of the Bulgarian National Committee for the Defense of Peace. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Li Shijun, Director of the Plant Protection Department of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, Zeng Xianpu, Deputy Director of the Special Products Department, Lin Rong, Deputy Director of the Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Chen Lingfeng, Deputy Director of the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, and Zou Difan of the Bureau of External Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Cultural and Educational Affairs. Counsellor Tzalvulanov and Secretary Grigolov of the Bulgarian Embassy in China also went to welcome him.

### 保农学家达斯卡洛夫到北京

1953-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    保农学家达斯卡洛夫到北京
    【新华社二十日讯】保加利亚农业科学家赫里斯多·达斯卡洛夫院士于二十日乘飞机到达北京。他是根据中保文化合作协定本年度文化合作执行计划前来我国考察农业的。达斯卡洛夫是保加利亚科学院院士，现任保加利亚科学院植物培植学院院长，他并且是保加利亚人民共和国国民议会议员及保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会委员。前往机场欢迎的有中央人民政府农业部植物保护司司长李世俊、特产司副司长曾宪朴、中国科学院植物研究所副所长林镕、华北农业科学研究所副所长陈凌风及政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局邹荻帆等。保加利亚驻华大使馆参赞察尔伏拉诺夫及秘书格里郭洛夫亦前往欢迎。
