Title: Italian Gasperi Government Falls

Author:

Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, August 30th) TASS, Paris, August 28th: According to reports from Rome: The Italian Gasperi government has been overthrown due to a parliamentary vote. Two hundred and eighty-two members of parliament cast votes of no confidence in the government, while only two hundred and sixty-three members of parliament cast votes of confidence, and thirty-seven members of parliament abstained.

### 意大利加斯贝利政府垮台

1953-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利加斯贝利政府垮台
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十八日讯：据罗马消息：意大利加斯贝利政府由于议会的投票而被推翻。二百八十二名议员对政府投了不信任票，投信任票的只有二百六十三名议员，另有三十七名议员弃权。
