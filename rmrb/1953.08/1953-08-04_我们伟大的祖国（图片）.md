Our Great Motherland
Since the Fuxin Haizhou Open-Pit Coal Mine began production on July 1st, thanks to the active efforts of the workers, the time for electric shovel excavation has been shortened, and the July production task was completed nine days ahead of schedule while ensuring quality. These trains are transporting selected coals of various types to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    阜新海州露天煤矿自七月一日开始生产以来，由于职工的积极努力，缩短了电铲采掘的时间，并在保证质量的条件下，提前九天完成了七月份的生产任务。这些火车正把精选的各种煤炭运往各地。
