Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee Issues Decrees on Establishing the Victory Memorial Tower of the Liberation War, etc.
Author:
Date: 1953-08-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 19th: According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency on the 18th, in order to forever commemorate the historic victory of the Korean people's Fatherland Liberation War against American imperialism and its lackeys and in defense of their fatherland's freedom and independence, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued two decrees on the 13th, deciding to build the Victory Memorial Tower of the Fatherland Liberation War in Pyongyang and to issue the Fatherland Liberation War Medal.
    The medal will be awarded to the following personnel: personnel of the Korean People's Army, the Ministry of Interior Guard, and the guerrillas who achieved glorious victory in the Fatherland Liberation War through heroic and selfless struggle; demobilized soldiers and personnel who served in military organs; all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who participated in the Korean People's Fatherland Liberation War; workers, technicians, and staff members of Korean military organs and industrial transportation departments; rural laborers and experts in the agricultural sector; personnel in the fields of science, art, literature, and education; personnel of state power organs, party organizations, and social groups.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 19th: According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency on the 18th, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on the 13th "Concerning the Establishment of a Memorial Tower to Commemorate the Meritorious Deeds of the Brave Warriors of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army."
    The decree states that in the just Fatherland Liberation War waged by the Korean people to defend their fatherland's freedom and independence, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, with its heroic and brave struggle, gave great assistance to the Korean People's Army and assisted the Korean people in achieving a historic victory. In order to forever commemorate the immortal meritorious deeds of the brave warriors of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who fought heroically alongside the Korean People's Army and sacrificed themselves in the battles that severely struck the enemy, it is specially decided to build a memorial tower in Pyongyang to commemorate the meritorious deeds of the brave warriors of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 19th: According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency on the 18th, in view of the victorious realization of the Korean armistice, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea issued decrees on the 13th, announcing the lifting of the two decrees issued by the Standing Committee on June 27th and July 1st, 1950, namely "Concerning the Declaration of a State of War in a Portion of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" and "Concerning the Declaration of Mobilization Throughout the Democratic People's Republic of Korea."

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会发布  建立解放战争胜利纪念塔等政令

1953-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会发布
    建立解放战争胜利纪念塔等政令
    【新华社平壤十九日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社十八日报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会为了永远纪念朝鲜人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗和保卫自己祖国自由独立的祖国解放战争的历史性胜利，在十三日分别发布两项政令，决定在平壤市建立祖国解放战争胜利纪念塔，并颁发祖国解放战争纪念章。
    纪念章将授给下列人员：在祖国解放战争中以英勇忘我的斗争取得光荣胜利的朝鲜人民军、内务省警备队和游击队的人员；复员军人和曾在军事机关服务的人员；参加朝鲜人民祖国解放战争的中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员；朝鲜军事机关和产业运输部门的工人、技术人员和职员；农村劳动人民和农业部门的专家；科学、艺术、文学、教育等部门的工作人员；国家政权机关、党组织及社会团体的工作人员。
    【新华社平壤十九日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社十八日报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，十三日发布“关于建立纪念中国人民志愿军勇士们功勋的追慕塔”的政令。
    政令说，在朝鲜人民为保卫祖国自由独立而进行的正义的祖国解放战争中，中国人民志愿军以自己的英雄和勇敢的斗争，给予朝鲜人民军以巨大的援助，协助朝鲜人民取得了历史性的胜利。为了永远纪念和朝鲜人民军一起英勇战斗、在沉重打击敌人的战斗中牺牲的中国人民志愿军勇士们的不朽功勋，特决定在平壤市建立纪念中国人民志愿军勇士们功勋的追慕塔。
    【新华社平壤十九日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社十八日报道：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会，鉴于朝鲜停战的胜利实现，在十三日分别发布政令，宣布解除常任委员会于一九五零年六月二十七日和七月一日先后发布的“关于在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国一部分地区宣布战时状态”和“关于在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国全区宣布动员”的两项政令。
