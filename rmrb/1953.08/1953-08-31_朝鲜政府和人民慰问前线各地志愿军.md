Title: Korean Government and People Extend Condolences to Volunteer Army on Various Fronts
Author:
Date: 1953-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 28th: The consolation groups dispatched by the government and people of all circles of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea departed from Pyongyang in mid-August to extend condolences to the various units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the front lines.

	A sub-group led by Li Qingyuan, Director of the Department of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, arrived at a unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on August 17th to carry out consolation work. A grand welcoming meeting was held by a unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. At the meeting, the unit leader, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the unit, expressed warm welcome and heartfelt gratitude to the consolation group and paid lofty respect to Marshal Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people. In his congratulatory message, Li Qingyuan, Director of the Department of Social Sciences, warmly congratulated the unit on the brilliant achievements it had made in the anti-aggression war. He said: The Korean people will never forget the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The Korean people are determined to further fight to safeguard the independence and freedom of their motherland and to vigorously carry out post-war reconstruction work. Some members of the Korean Railway Art Troupe, who had visited our country, performed multiple consolation performances in this unit.

	Another unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, which had made outstanding achievements in the counterattack south of Kimhwa, also received warm condolences from another sub-group of the Korean consolation group and representatives of the people of North Pyongan Province and farmers' art troupe in mid-August. Representatives of the consolation group and the heroes and meritorious persons of this unit held cordial discussions together. At the symposium, more than 20 heroes and meritorious persons reported their heroic deeds and expressed their determination to fight to the end for the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue. Representatives of the Korean consolation group, female model farmer Zheng Zaiyu, female model worker Zhao Jingchun from a timber factory in Sinuiju, and others all expressed at the symposium that they would learn from the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's high degree of patriotism and internationalism and strive to participate in the restoration and construction of their own motherland's industry and agriculture.

### 朝鲜政府和人民慰问前线各地志愿军

1953-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府和人民慰问前线各地志愿军
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和各界人民派遣的慰问团，于八月中旬由平壤出发，分赴前线各地慰问中国人民志愿军各部队。
    由朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部部长李清源率领的一个分团，在八月十七日到达中国人民志愿军某部进行慰问工作。中国人民志愿军某部举行了盛大的欢迎大会。会上，部队首长代表所属部队全体指挥员、战斗员对慰问团的到来，表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的感谢，并向朝鲜人民领袖金日成元帅致崇高的敬意。社会科学部部长李清源在致慰问词中，热烈祝贺这支部队在反侵略战争中建立的辉煌功绩。他说：朝鲜人民永远不会忘记中国人民志愿军的伟大功勋。朝鲜人民决心进一步为维护自己祖国的独立、自由，和大力进行战后恢复工作而斗争。曾经访问我国的朝鲜铁道艺术团的部分团员，在这支部队中进行了多次慰问演出。
    在金城以南反击战中战功卓著的另一支中国人民志愿军部队，也在八月中旬受到了朝鲜慰问团另一个分团和平安北道各界人民代表、农民艺术队的热烈慰问。慰问团的代表们和这支部队的英雄、功臣们一起，进行了亲切的座谈。在座谈会上，有二十多位英雄、功臣报告了他们的英雄事迹，并且表示了他们要为朝鲜问题的和平解决而奋斗到底的决心。朝鲜慰问团代表、女模范农民郑在玉、新义州某制材工厂女劳动模范赵京顺等都在座谈会上表示要学习中国人民志愿军高度的爱国主义和国际主义精神，努力参加自己祖国工农业的恢复和建设工作。
