Title: The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party Holds Enlarged Plenary Session; Romanian Communist Party General Secretary Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Delivers Important Report at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 22nd: The Romanian newspapers published the communique of the enlarged plenary session of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held on August 19th and 20th.

The plenary session heard the report by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Party Central Committee, entitled "The Party's Tasks in Developing the National Economy and Further Improving the Material and Cultural Standards of the Working People." After discussion, the plenary session unanimously adopted the program of economic measures proposed in the report.

The plenary session unanimously adopted a resolution on improving the work of the Party and decided to convene the Romanian Workers' Party Congress in March 1954.

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央举行扩大全会  罗共总书记乔治乌—德治在会上作了重要报告

1953-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党中央举行扩大全会
    罗共总书记乔治乌—德治在会上作了重要报告
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特二十二日讯：罗马尼亚各报刊载了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会在八月十九日和二十日举行的扩大全体会议的公报。
    全体会议听取了党中央委员会总书记乔治乌—德治所作题为“党在发展国民经济和进一步提高劳动人民物质和文化水平方面的任务”的报告。全体会议经讨论后一致通过了报告中所提出的经济措施的纲领。
    全体会议一致通过关于改进党的工作的决议，并决定在一九五四年三月召开罗马尼亚工人党代表大会。
