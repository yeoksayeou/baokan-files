Title: USSR Minister of Defense Issues Order Commemorating Aviation Day
Author:
Date: 1953-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 9th: TASS Moscow, August 9th—The Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, issued the following order on August 9th to celebrate Aviation Day:

    Pilots and navigators, engineers and technicians, air gunners and radio operators, aviation mechanics and motor vehicle operators, workers in the aviation industry!

    Comrades soldiers and sailors, sergeants and petty officers! Comrades officers and generals of the Army and Navy!

    Today, the Army and Navy of the Soviet Union, together with all the working people of our country, celebrate Aviation Day.

    On this Aviation Day, I extend my greetings and congratulations to you.

    I wish all personnel of Soviet aviation and all workers of the aviation industry new and greater achievements in further developing and strengthening the air power of our motherland.

    To commemorate Aviation Day, I order: Today, August 9, 1953, twenty salvoes shall be fired in our motherland's capital, Moscow, and the capitals of all the Union Republics.

    Long live the Soviet Air Force!

    Long live the Soviet people and their heroic Army and Navy!

    Long live the Soviet government!

    Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

### 苏联国防部部长发布庆祝航空节命令

1953-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部部长发布庆祝航空节命令
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁，在九日发布庆祝航空节的命令如下：
    驾驶员和领航员、工程师和技术员、空军射击手和无线电报务员、航空机械士和摩托手、航空工业的工作人员们！
    战士和海员、军士和士官同志们！校尉和陆海军将军同志们！
    今天，苏联的陆海军和我国全体劳动人民一道庆祝航空节。
    在航空节这一天，我向你们致意和祝贺。
    我祝苏联航空事业的全体人员和航空工业的全体工作人员，在进一步发展和加强我们祖国的空军力量方面获得新的、更大的成就。
    为了纪念航空节，我命令：今天，一九五三年八月九日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科及各加盟共和国的首都，各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    苏联空军万岁！
    苏联人民及其英勇的陆海军万岁！
    苏联政府万岁！
    苏联共产党万岁！
