Title: Twenty-Fourth Day Communiqué on Repatriation of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-08-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 24th: On the 24th, our side repatriated 150 non-Korean prisoners of war to the other side (all American), and 250 Korean prisoners of war. The total number of prisoners of war repatriated by our side to the other side was 400.
　　Today, the other side repatriated 2,398 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side, including two wounded and sick personnel.
　　According to Yu Zaiyong and Zhi Guohuan, who returned from repatriation today, on the morning of the 14th, when our captured personnel arrived in Incheon by boat from Bongam Island, a group of Syngman Rhee special agents disguised as civilians threw stones at them. Yu Zaiyong and Zhi Guohuan were injured. In addition, three of our captured personnel had their heads broken and fainted due to excessive bleeding. They were sent to the U.S. Navy Hospital in Incheon. However, these three seriously injured captured personnel were suddenly taken away from the hospital by the U.S. side one night, and their whereabouts are unknown.

### 二十四日遣返战俘公报

1953-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    二十四日遣返战俘公报
    【新华社开城二十四日电】二十四日，我方遣送给对方非朝鲜籍战俘一百五十名（全是美国籍），朝鲜籍战俘二百五十名。我方遣送给对方的战俘总数是四百名。
    今天对方遣送给我方朝鲜人民军被俘人员两千三百九十八名，包括伤病人员两名。
    据今天遣返归来的于在永、智国焕说，十四日早晨，我方被俘人员乘船从蜂岩岛到达仁川时，有一群化装为平民的李承晚特务向他们投掷石块，于在永、智国焕被打伤，另外三名我方被俘人员头部也被打破，因流血过多而昏倒后，被送到仁川美军海军医院，但这三名伤势严重的我方被俘人员在一天夜间突然被美方从医院运走，去向不明。
