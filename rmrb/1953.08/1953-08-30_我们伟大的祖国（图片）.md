    Our Great Motherland
    The Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region in Yunnan Province convened its first People's Representative Conference of all ethnic groups in mid-July and established the People's Government of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region. People of all ethnic groups and circles in Luxi County, Yunnan Province, gathered in Mangshi on July 24 to celebrate. This is a Dai dance program performed at the meeting - "Peacock Dance."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区已于七月中旬召开了首届各族各界人民代表会议，成立了德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府。云南省潞西县各族各界人民于七月二十四日在芒市集会庆祝。这是在会上演出的傣族舞蹈节目——“孔雀舞”。
