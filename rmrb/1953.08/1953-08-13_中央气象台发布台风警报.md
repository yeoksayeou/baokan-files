Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Warning
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 1

    Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Warning
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] At 5:30 PM on the 12th, the Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning:
    At 2:00 PM on the 12th, the estimated location of the typhoon center was approximately 300 kilometers southeast of the Dongsha Islands in the sea (19 degrees North latitude, 119 degrees East longitude), with an estimated maximum wind speed of 25 meters per second. At 200 kilometers from the center, the wind speed was 14 to 18 meters per second. The typhoon center is currently moving westward at a speed of approximately 20 to 24 kilometers per hour, and it is expected that by 2:00 PM on the 13th, the typhoon center will reach the sea approximately 300 kilometers directly south of Hong Kong (19.5 degrees North latitude, 114 degrees East longitude). Currently, there are strong winds of approximately level 6 to 7 in the Taiwan Strait and the northern part of the South China Sea.

### 中央气象台发布台风警报

1953-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布台风警报
    【新华社十二日讯】十二日下午五时三十分，中央气象台发布台风警报：
    十二日下午二时，台风中心位置估计在东沙群岛东南约三百公里处的海面上（北纬十九度，东经一百一十九度），最大风速估计为每秒二十五公尺。距中心二百公里处，风速为每秒十四到十八公尺。目前台风中心约以每小时二十到二十四公里的速度向西移动，预计十三日下午二时，台风中心将抵香港正南约三百公里处的海面上（北纬十九点五度，东经一百一十四度）。目前台湾海峡和南海北部有六到七级左右的大风。
