Title: Malenkov Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao on "August 1st" Army Day
Author: Ge·Malenkov
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

    Malenkov Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao on "August 1st" Army Day
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    On the occasion of the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Comrade Chairman, please accept my heartfelt congratulations. I hope that the glorious armed forces of the People's Republic of China will achieve further consolidation in the cause of defending the motherland and maintaining world peace.
    G.M. Malenkov
    Moscow, July 31, 1953

### 马林科夫致电毛主席祝贺“八一”建军节
格·马林科夫
1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    马林科夫致电毛主席祝贺“八一”建军节北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    值此中华人民共和国人民解放军建军二十六周年之际，主席同志，谨向您致衷心的祝贺。希望中华人民共和国光荣的武装力量在保卫祖国及维护世界和平的事业中获得更进一步的巩固。
    格·马林科夫
    一九五三年七月三十一日于莫斯科
