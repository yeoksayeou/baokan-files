Title: Bulgarian Deputy Foreign Minister Congratulates Our Country on the Korean Armistice; Belgian Communist Party Issues Communiqué Welcoming Korean Armistice
Author:
Date: 1953-08-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 4th: Sofia News: Bulgarian People's Republic Deputy Foreign Minister Lyu Gerasimov, on behalf of Foreign Minister Min Neichev, congratulated Charge d'Affaires Wang Chongli of our embassy in Bulgaria on July 30 on the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement. In his congratulations, Lyu Gerasimov stated: The signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement is a tremendous victory. Bulgaria will stand with China to fight for the cause of defending and consolidating peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 4th: On July 30, the "Red Flag" newspaper published a communiqué from the Political Bureau of the Belgian Communist Party, welcoming the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement.
    The Political Bureau's communiqué stated: The Political Bureau welcomes the Korean armistice with joy. This is a victory for peace over the war forces centered on the American imperialists. Until the very last minute, the war forces tried to make the armistice talks fail, but they had to give up only because of the resolute actions of all peace-loving forces.
    The communiqué continued: Although the peace fighters have won this victory at Panmunjom, everyone must remain vigilant. Because although the war-mongers have been defeated, they have not given up their dreams of conquering the Far East and dominating the world. The communiqué pointed out: The current task of peace fighters is to use the victory in Korea to expand the peace front and intensify its activities, so that the spirit of consultation and cooperation will finally overcome the policies of provocation and hegemony.

### 保加利亚副外长向我国祝贺朝鲜停战  比利时共产党发表公报欢迎朝鲜停战

1953-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚副外长向我国祝贺朝鲜停战
    比利时共产党发表公报欢迎朝鲜停战
    【新华社四日讯】索非亚讯：保加利亚人民共和国外交部副部长柳·格拉西莫夫于七月三十日代表外交部部长明·涅伊切夫向我驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办王崇理祝贺朝鲜停战协定的签订。柳·格拉西莫夫在祝贺中表示：朝鲜停战协定的签订是一个巨大的胜利。保加利亚将与中国在一起为保卫和巩固和平事业而奋斗。
    【新华社柏林四日电】七月三十日“红旗报”发表比利时共产党政治局的公报，欢迎朝鲜停战协定签字。
    政治局的公报说：政治局以欢欣的心情欢迎朝鲜停战。这是和平对以美帝国主义者为核心的战争势力的胜利。直到最后一分钟为止，战争势力还竭力想使停战谈判失败，只是由于所有和平力量的坚决行动，他们才不得不罢手。
    公报接着说：尽管和平战士在板门店取得了这个胜利，但是大家仍然不能丝毫放松警惕。因为战争贩子虽然遭到了失败，但是并未放弃他们征服远东和称霸世界的梦想。公报指出：和平战士目前的任务是，利用在朝鲜取得的胜利，扩大和平阵线，加紧它的活动，使协商合作的精神最后压倒挑衅和称霸的政策。
