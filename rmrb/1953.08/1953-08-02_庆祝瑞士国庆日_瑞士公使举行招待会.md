Title: Celebrating Swiss National Day: Swiss Minister Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. The Swiss Minister to China, Mr. René Zoellner, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 1st to celebrate Swiss National Day.

Those invited to attend included: Ma Xulun, Minister of Higher Education of the Central People's Government; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 庆祝瑞士国庆日  瑞士公使举行招待会

1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝瑞士国庆日
    瑞士公使举行招待会
    【新华社一日讯】瑞士驻我国公使任佐立于一日下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝瑞士国庆日。
    应邀赴会的有：中央人民政府高等教育部部长马叙伦、外交部副部长章汉夫、人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    各国驻我国外交使节和使馆人员也应邀赴会。
    招待会自始至终在友好的气氛中进行。
