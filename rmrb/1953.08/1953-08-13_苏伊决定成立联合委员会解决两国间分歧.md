Title: Soviet-Iranian Decision to Establish Joint Committee to Resolve Bilateral Differences
Author:
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 10th (TASS): To consolidate the existing friendly relations between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and Iran, the two governments have decided to establish a Soviet-Iranian Joint Committee in Tehran, composed of representatives from both countries, to eliminate all differences on issues such as finance and borders.

### 苏伊决定成立联合委员会解决两国间分歧

1953-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏伊决定成立联合委员会解决两国间分歧
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：为了巩固苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和伊朗两国之间现有的友好关系，双方政府已决定在德黑兰成立一个包括两国代表的苏伊联合委员会，以便消除如财政和边界等问题上的一切分歧。
