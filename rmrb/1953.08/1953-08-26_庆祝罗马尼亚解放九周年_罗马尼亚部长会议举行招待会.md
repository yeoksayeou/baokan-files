Title: Reception Held by Romanian Council of Ministers to Celebrate the Ninth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1953-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 25th: According to the Romanian News Agency, the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic held a reception on the evening of the 23rd to celebrate the ninth anniversary of Romania's liberation. Those present included: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers; Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly; Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers Iosif Chișinevschi, Gheorghe Apostol, Chivu Stoica, and Gheorghe Vidrașcu; M. Constantinescu, Chairman of the State Planning Committee; General E. Bodnăraș, Minister of the Armed Forces; Foreign Minister Simion Bughici, heads of various government departments, and many cultural and artistic workers.

Also attending the reception were diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in Romania, as well as M.B. Mitin, Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; William Gallacher, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the British Communist Party; E. Branco, a well-known Brazilian peace fighter; Friedrich Ebert, member of the Political Bureau of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Mayor of Greater Berlin; Lambin, Secretary of the Nord Provincial Committee of the French Communist Party; J. Dénis, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth; and Henri Martin, a well-known French peace fighter.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放九周年  罗马尼亚部长会议举行招待会

1953-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚解放九周年
    罗马尼亚部长会议举行招待会
    【新华社二十五日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议在二十三日晚举行招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚解放九周年。到会的有：罗马尼亚部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治，国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查，部长会议副主席约·基辛湼夫斯基、格·阿波斯托尔、基·斯托伊卡和格·维德拉希库，国家计划委员会主席米·康斯坦丁尼斯库，武装部队部部长埃·波德纳拉希大将，外交部部长西·布吉奇，政府各部门首长，以及文化、艺术工作者多人。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻罗马尼亚的外交官员，以及苏联科学院院士麦·布·米丁，英国共产党执行委员会主席威·加拉赫，巴西著名和平战士埃·布兰柯，德国统一社会党政治局委员、大柏林市市长弗雷德里希·艾柏特，法国共产党诺尔省省委书记朗本，世界民主青年联盟总书记雅·德尼，法国著名和平战士亨利·马丁等人。
