Title: Enthusiastic Support-the-Army and Give-Preferential-Treatment-to-Families-of-Revolutionary-Armymen Activities Sweeping the Nation

Author: 
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: In Beijing, Shenyang, Chongqing, Xi'an, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Nanjing, Taiyuan, Harbin, Dihua, GuiSui, and Ulanhot, people from all walks of life enthusiastically carried out activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of revolutionary armymen around the "August 1st" Army Day.

    In Beijing, the Beijing Municipal People's Government and the Beijing Branch of the Resist-America-Aid-Korea Association jointly formed a delegation to express their regards to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army soldiers who were recuperating in the capital on "August 1st" Army Day. The delegation included responsible persons and staff members from the Beijing Municipal People's Government, district people's governments, various democratic parties, and people's organizations. Led by Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and Qian Duansheng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal People's Congress Consultative Committee, the delegation visited eight hospitals on July 31st and August 1st, expressing verbal greetings, holding symposiums, organizing entertainment evenings, and distributing consolation money to the recuperating soldiers.

    In Shenyang, the people's governments of each district held joint military-government-civilian get-togethers on "August 1st" Army Day. The heads of each district reported on the preferential treatment work in their districts over the past six months and discussed opinions on supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of revolutionary armymen. Many model family members of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers, as well as combat heroes from the army, also reported on their production, study, and living situations at the meeting. Residents of streets and alleys throughout the city, carrying colorful flags and beating drums and gongs, delivered a large number of gifts to the families of revolutionary martyrs and the families of soldiers. Three delegations and performance teams composed of representatives from all walks of life in Shenyang City, carrying a large number of gifts, visited the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army in various hospitals before "August 1st" Army Day.

    In Chongqing, on the day before "August 1st" Army Day, more than 40 units, including government agencies, democratic parties, and people's organizations, organized four visiting teams to express their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army in suburban hospitals and sanatoriums. On the night of "August 1st," more than 20,000 soldiers and civilians held a grand garden party in the city's Laboring People's Cultural Palace.

    In Xi'an, residents of each district, organized into visiting groups based on the jurisdiction of the local police station, carried a large number of condolence letters and visited each of the more than 4,000 families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers in the city. The Xi'an Municipal Committee for Preferential Treatment Work and related units organized six visiting groups to express their regards to the recuperating soldiers of the Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army in hospitals and sanatoriums in the city. The Ethnic Affairs Committee of the Xi'an Municipal People's Government also held a symposium for the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers of fraternal nationalities in the city to solicit their opinions and requests. The families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers of various fraternal nationalities spoke one after another, expressing their gratitude to the People's Government and people from all walks of life for their care.

    In Wuhan, a delegation composed of people from all walks of life to express their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, as well as revolutionary disabled soldiers, visited various hospitals, sanatoriums, and schools for revolutionary disabled soldiers in groups for days leading up to the "August 1st" Army Day. The delegation was divided into five groups. In many hospitals and sanatoriums, rallies were held to express their regards, and consolation items were distributed. Literary and art workers accompanying the delegation also performed many cultural and entertainment programs.

    In Guangzhou, many government agencies, factories, and schools organized visiting teams to express their regards to the troops stationed near their residences, revolutionary disabled soldiers, and families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers. In Guangzhou's North District, West District, Henan District, and many streets, military-government-civilian symposiums or get-togethers have been held one after another in recent days. The Guangzhou Municipal People's Government also allocated a special fund of more than 100 million yuan to be distributed to the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers who are currently experiencing financial difficulties.

    In Nanjing, nine visiting teams jointly formed by representatives from the military, government agencies, and people from all walks of life extended congratulations to the hospitalized wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army and the stationed troops, and the literary and art teams performed consolation performances. On August 1st, more than 10,000 family members of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers were given free admission to six state-owned movie theaters. The city's theaters also provided free performances. Xuanwu Lake was open to revolutionary soldiers and the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers for free visits on August 2nd and 3rd. Various districts also held military-civilian get-togethers, symposiums, and photo exhibitions. The Nanjing Municipal Committee for Supporting the Army and Giving Preferential Treatment to Families of Revolutionary Armymen will conduct an inspection of preferential treatment work from August 5th to 15th, in conjunction with the current central work.

    In Taiyuan, on the eve of "August 1st," people from all walks of life and stationed troops, family members of revolutionary martyrs, and soldiers, etc., totaling more than 10,000 people, held a grand evening party. A delegation composed of representatives from all walks of life in the province and city, carrying a large number of consolation items, visited various medical units to express their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army. Various stationed troop units also held symposiums with local residents.

    In Harbin, people from all walks of life held a grand celebration and get-together on "August 1st" Army Day. At the meeting, consolation items were distributed to the families of 65 meritorious soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the families of 147 meritorious soldiers of the Volunteer Army. The families of the meritorious soldiers also received their loved ones' meritorious service reports and plaques presented to them by the people of the city at the meeting. The famous Peking Opera actor Tan Fuying performed wonderful programs for the families of the meritorious soldiers.

    In Dihua, a delegation composed of representatives from various government agencies, organizations, and ethnic groups in Xinjiang Province and Dihua City extended their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the families of the Volunteer Army on "August 1st" Army Day. Various districts in Dihua City also organized visiting groups to express their regards to the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers in their districts and local stationed troops, and held military-civilian get-together evenings.

    People from all walks of life in Guisui City and Ulanhot City also organized visiting delegations to express their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army. On August 1st, Guisui City held a celebration meeting to commend 84 Volunteer Army soldiers who had performed meritorious service in Korea and issued honorary plaques and honorary medals to the families of the meritorious soldiers. A delegation composed of leaders of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government and the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government, carrying consolation items, went to the hospital to express their regards to the Volunteer Army wounded and sick soldiers returning home for recuperation.

### 全国各地热烈进行拥军优属活动

1953-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地热烈进行拥军优属活动
    据新华社讯：北京、沈阳、重庆、西安、武汉、广州、南京、太原、哈尔滨、迪化、归绥、乌兰浩特各界人民，在“八一”建军节前后，热烈进行了拥军优属活动。
    北京市人民政府和北京市抗美援朝分会联合慰问团在“八一”建军节，代表首都二百多万人民，慰问正在首都休养的中国人民志愿军和中国人民解放军战士。参加慰问团的有北京市人民政府、区人民政府和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人与工作人员。慰问团由北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗，北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席钱端升等率领，于七月三十一日和八月一日分别到八个医院进行慰问，除对休养员们作口头慰问、举行座谈会外，还组织了文娱晚会，发送了慰问金。
    在沈阳市，各区人民政府在“八一”节当天，分别召开了军政民联欢大会，由各区区长报告了半年来本区的优抚工作，并座谈了对拥军优属工作的意见。许多烈属、军属模范和部队战斗英雄也在会上报告了自己生产、学习和生活的情况。全市各街巷的居民，打着彩旗，敲着锣鼓，把大批慰问品送给革命烈士家属、军人家属。由沈阳市各界人民代表组成的三个慰问团和演出队，在“八一”节前夕，携带大批慰问品，分赴各医院慰问解放军和志愿军的伤病员。
    重庆市在“八一”节前一天，各机关、民主党派、人民团体等四十多个单位组织了四个慰问队，到郊区医院、疗养院慰问解放军和志愿军伤病员。“八一”之夜，军民两万多人在该市劳动人民文化宫举行了盛大的游园晚会。
    在西安市，各区居民以派出所区域为单位，分别组织了慰问组，携带大批慰问信，向全市四千余革命烈士和革命军人家属，逐户进行慰问。西安市优抚工作委员会和有关单位，组织了六个慰问组，向驻在市内各医院、疗养院的志愿军、解放军休养员进行慰问。西安市人民政府民族事务委员会还召开了在该市的兄弟民族烈属、军属座谈会，征求他们的意见和要求。各兄弟民族的烈属、军属纷纷发言，感激人民政府和各界人民对他们的关怀。
    武汉市各界人民组成的慰问中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军伤病员、革命残废军人代表团，在“八一”建军节前夕，连日分组到各医院、疗养院和革命残废军人学校进行慰问。慰问团分五组进行慰问。在许多医院和疗养院里，都举行了慰问大会，并分发慰问品。随同慰问团的文艺工作人员还表演了许多文化娱乐节目。
    在广州市，许多机关、工厂和学校，都纷纷组织慰问队，慰问他们住地附近的驻军、革命残废军人和烈、军属。广州市的北区、西区、河南区和许多街道，连日来纷纷举行军政民座谈会或联欢会。广州市人民政府还特别拨款一亿多元，发给目前生活上有困难的革命烈士家属和革命军人家属。
    在南京市，由部队、机关和各界人民代表联合组成的九个慰问队，分别向志愿军、解放军住院伤病员和驻军祝贺，并由文艺队进行慰问演出。八月一日，全市一万多烈属、军属受到了六大公营电影院免费招待映出。全市剧院也免费招待演出。玄武湖在八月二、三日，免费供革命军人和烈、军属游览。各区还分别召开军民联欢会、座谈会和图片展览会。南京市拥军优属委员会在八月五日到十五日，将结合当前中心工作，进行优抚工作检查。
    太原市在“八一”前夕，各界人民和驻军、革命烈士、军人家属等一万多人举行了盛大的联欢晚会。由省、市各界代表组成的访问团，携带大批慰问品，分赴各医疗单位，向解放军和志愿军伤病员进行慰问。各驻军单位还和当地居民举行座谈会。
    在哈尔滨市，各界人民在“八一”节那天，举行了盛大的庆功联欢大会，大会向解放军六十五名功臣的家属和志愿军一百四十七名功臣的家属分赠慰问品。功臣家属们在大会上还接到了他们的亲人的立功喜报和全市人民送给他们的功臣匾。著名京剧演员谭富英并为功臣家属演出了精彩节目。
    在迪化市，由新疆省、迪化市各机关、团体及各族各界代表组成的慰问团，在“八一”节向解放军伤病员和志愿军家属进行慰问。迪化市各区也组织了慰问小组向本区烈属、军属和当地驻军进行慰问，并召开了军民联欢晚会。
    归绥市和乌兰浩特市各界人民，也组织慰问团向解放军、志愿军的伤病员进行慰问。八月一日归绥市举行了庆功大会，给八十四位在朝鲜立过功的志愿军庆功并发给功臣家属光荣匾和光荣纪念章。由内蒙古自治区人民政府和绥远省人民政府首长等组成的慰问团，携带慰问品，前往医院慰问归国休养的志愿军伤病员。
