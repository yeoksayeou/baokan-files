Title: Formation of New Indonesian Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 1st: TASS reports from Jakarta: After a government crisis lasting nearly two months, a new cabinet headed by Ali Sastroamidjojo, former Indonesian ambassador to the United States, Mexico, Canada, and other countries, was announced on July 30th.

　　The personnel of the new government are representatives of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and small parties. Representatives of the Masyumi Party (Islamic Party), the majority party, and left-wing parties did not participate in the new cabinet. Note: The previous Wilopo cabinet resigned on June 2nd. Since then, there have been several attempts to form a cabinet, but none have succeeded.

### 印度尼西亚新内阁组成

1953-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新内阁组成
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社雅加达讯：经过近两个月的政府危机之后，以曾任印度尼西亚驻美国、墨西哥、加拿大等国大使的阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约为首的一个新的内阁，在七月三十日宣布组成。
    新政府的人员都是印尼国民党和小党派的代表。新内阁没有作为多数党的马斯友美党（回教党）和左翼政党的代表参加。按：前任韦洛坡内阁是在六月二日辞职的。在这以后，曾有多次组阁活动，但都没有成功。
