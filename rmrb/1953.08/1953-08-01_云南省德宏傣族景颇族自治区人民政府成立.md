Title: Establishment of the People's Government of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region in Yunnan Province
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 3

    The first session of the People's Representatives Conference of all ethnic groups and circles in the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province concluded victoriously on July 24. The People's Government of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region was officially established. The conference exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress and elected Dao Jingban (Dai nationality) as Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, and Gong Shou, Duo Yong'an, Kan Jingtai (all Dai nationality), Lei Chunguo, Si Nashan, Pai Qiren (all Jingpo nationality), and Duan Huamin (Han nationality) as Vice Chairmen. Zao Du (Jingpo nationality) and 37 others were elected as government members. The Autonomous Region Consultative Committee was also established at the same time. The meeting elected Si Hongsheng (Dai nationality) as Chairman, Duo Yingpei (Dai nationality), Fang Hualong (Dai nationality), Na Paidu (Jingpo nationality), Pai Zhala (Jingpo nationality), Jiang Jiajie (Han nationality), Xiang Lao Zuo (Achang nationality), Dong Mingwei (Lisu nationality) as Vice Chairmen, and Dao Liangsheng (Dai nationality) and 49 others as members. A solemn oath-taking ceremony was held. Dao Jingban, in his speech at the oath-taking ceremony, expressed his determination to follow the Common Program under the leadership of the Communist Party and the higher-level People's Government and to strive for the construction of the autonomous region.
    The seven-day representative conference marked the further consolidation of the unity and friendship of the people of all ethnic groups. At the meeting, a full discussion was held on the central topic of further strengthening ethnic unity. Leading figures of all ethnic groups with ties to the masses, such as Dao Jingban, Si Hongsheng, Pai Qiren, and Si Nashan, all spoke at the meeting, expressing their determination to all the representatives. The conference unanimously adopted the Patriotic Unity Convention of the People of All Ethnic Groups in the Autonomous Region.
    In order to systematically improve the production level of the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region and gradually improve the people's living standards, the meeting also focused on discussing production issues. After full consultation, the meeting also unanimously adopted the "Provisional Organizational Regulations of the People's Government of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province" and the "Outline of the Autonomous Region People's Government's Work Plan for the Next Two Years," determining that strengthening ethnic unity and developing agricultural production would be the central tasks for the future, and that financial, economic, cultural, educational, and health work would be carried out in a planned manner, and that efforts would be made to consolidate the border defenses of the motherland.
    At the closing ceremony of the meeting, Guo Yingqiu, Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial People's Government, delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Provincial People's Government, wishing the autonomous region's work a victory. The meeting finally unanimously passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and the Chinese People's Volunteers. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府成立

1953-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府成立
    云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区首届各族各界人民代表会议，已在七月二十四日胜利闭幕。德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府，宣布正式成立。会议代行了人民代表大会的职权，选出刀京版（傣族）为自治区人民政府主席，龚绶、多永安、衎景泰（以上傣族）、雷春国、思纳山、排启仁、（以上景颇族）段华民（汉族）为副主席。早堵（景颇族）等三十七人为政府委员。自治区协商委员会也同时成立。会议选出了思鸿升（傣族）为主席，多英培
    （傣族）、方化龙（傣族）、纳排堵（景颇族）、排扎腊（景颇族）、蒋家杰（汉族）、项老佐（阿昌族）、栋明尉（傈僳族）为副主席，刀良生（傣族）等四十九人为委员。并隆重举行了宣誓就职仪式。刀京版在宣誓就职仪式上讲话，表示今后决心在共产党和上级人民政府的领导下，遵循共同纲领，为自治区的建设而努力。
    历时七天的代表会议，标志着各族人民友爱团结的更加巩固。会上，曾以进一步加强民族团结为中心议题进行了充分的讨论。各族与群众有联系的领袖人物刀京版、思鸿陞、排启仁、思纳山等，都在会上发言，向全体代表表示决心。会议一致通过了自治区各族人民爱国团结公约。
    为了有步骤地提高自治区各族人民的生产水平，逐步改善人民生活，会议还着重地讨论了生产问题。会议经过充分协商后，还一致通过了“云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府暂行组织条例”和“自治区人民政府今后两年施政纲要”，确定以加强民族团结和发展农业生产为今后中心任务，并有计划地开展财经、文教、卫生工作，并努力巩固祖国边防。
    在会议闭幕式上，云南省人民政府副主席郭影秋，代表中共云南省委员会和省人民政府致贺辞，预祝自治区工作胜利。会议最后一致通过向毛主席和中国人民志愿军致敬电。（新华社）
