Title: Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau Holds Celebration for Romanian Liberation Day; "Photo Exhibition on the 9th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation" Opens Today in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-08-23
Page: 1

    The Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau Holds Celebration for Romanian Liberation Day
    "Photo Exhibition on the 9th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation" Opens Today in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd) August 23rd is the 9th anniversary of the Liberation of Romania. The Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a celebration at the Beijing Youth Palace on the evening of the 22nd. Present at the meeting were Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of Culture, Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education, Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau, heads of various people's organizations, and more than 800 people from cultural, educational, and other circles in the capital. Invited to the meeting were Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries to China.
    The celebration opened solemnly with the national anthems of Romania, China, and the Soviet Union. First, Deputy Minister Wei Que delivered a speech. He extended warm congratulations to the government and people of the Romanian People's Republic. He described in detail the brilliant achievements the Romanian people have made in political, economic, and cultural construction over the past nine years, and described the increasing development of economic and cultural exchanges between China and Romania. He said that the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of fraternal countries is based on the great internationalist principles of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and is therefore unbreakable. The power generated by this friendship is truly invincible.
    Next, Ambassador Guo Zuowen spoke. He pointed out that thanks to the victory of the heroic Soviet army over fascism, the Romanian people were liberated. The major achievements the Romanian people have made in the past nine years under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party and the people's leader Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej are inseparable from the Soviet Union's multifaceted and enormous assistance. He said that the increasing strengthening of friendly cooperation between Romania and China is enhancing the strength of the peace and democracy camp headed by the great and invincible Soviet Union.
    After the meeting, Chinese and Romanian poetry recitations, song performances, dances, and Peking opera were performed.
    In addition: The "Photo Exhibition on the 9th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation," sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau, is scheduled to open on the 23rd in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. The exhibits include more than 300 photographs on Romania's construction achievements and social life, and various reproductions of folk art.

### 我对外文化联络事务局  举行罗马尼亚解放日庆祝会  “罗马尼亚解放九周年图片展览”今日在北京开幕

1953-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我对外文化联络事务局
    举行罗马尼亚解放日庆祝会
    “罗马尼亚解放九周年图片展览”今日在北京开幕
    【新华社二十二日讯】八月二十三日是罗马尼亚解放九周年纪念日。我中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特于二十二日晚在北京青年宫举行庆祝会。到会的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长丁西林，教育部副部长韦悫，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，中国科学院副院长吴有训，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，各人民团体负责人以及首都文化教育及其他各界人士八百余人。应邀到会的有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使郭佐文和苏联及各人民民主国家驻中国的外交使节。
    庆祝会在罗、中、苏三国国歌声中隆重开幕。首先由韦悫副部长讲话。他向罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。他详细地叙述了罗马尼亚人民九年来在政治、经济和文化建设等方面所获得的辉煌成就，并叙述了中罗两国间的经济与文化交流日益发展的情况。他说，中国人民和兄弟国家人民之间的友谊，是根据马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的伟大的国际主义的原则而建立起来的，因而是牢不可破的。这种友谊产生的力量是真正无敌的。
    接着，郭佐文大使讲话。他指出，由于英雄的苏联军队对于法西斯主义的胜利，罗马尼亚人民获得了解放，九年来罗马尼亚人民在罗马尼亚工人党和人民领袖乔治乌—德治领导下所取得的重大成就，又是和苏联多方面的巨大援助分不开的。他说，罗中两国之间友好合作的日益加强，增强着以伟大的不可战胜的苏联为首的和平民主阵营的力量。
    会后，演出了中罗两国诗歌朗诵、歌曲演奏、舞蹈和京剧等文艺节目。
    又讯：我对外文化联络事务局主办的“罗马尼亚解放九周年图片展览”，定于二十三日在北京中山公园开幕。展览品中包括关于罗马尼亚建设成就和社会生活的图片三百余幅，复制民间艺术品多种。
