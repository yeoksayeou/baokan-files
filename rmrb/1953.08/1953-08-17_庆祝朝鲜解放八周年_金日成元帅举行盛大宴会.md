Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung Holds Grand Banquet to Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-08-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 16th: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand banquet on the afternoon of the 15th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, hosting representatives of the Korean military and civilians, and foreign guests stationed in Pyongyang, with over 300 people in attendance. Korean attendees included: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; Park Jung-ai, Park Chang-ok, and Kim Il, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; and Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choe Yong-gon, Choe Chang-ik, Chung Il-yong, and Park Ui-wan.
    Invited to the banquet were representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations in Korea, high-ranking generals of the People's Army, Heroes of the Republic, Labor Heroes, and representatives of workers, farmers, women, youth, cultural and educational workers, and artists from the Pyongyang area. Foreign guests invited to the banquet included: Soviet Ambassador to Korea, S.P. Suzdalev; Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy, Gan Yetao; diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries; and journalists from various countries stationed in Pyongyang. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation, including combat heroes and meritorious individuals from the Volunteer Army who came to Pyongyang to attend the Korean Hero and Model Conference, led by Chief Delegate General Li Zhimin, were invited to attend the banquet.
    At the start of the banquet, Marshal Kim Il-sung raised his glass and said: "We are celebrating the eighth anniversary of the heroic Soviet army's liberation of Korea in an environment where the Fatherland Liberation War has already achieved victory. We deeply express our gratitude to the Soviet people, the Soviet government, and the Soviet Communist Party. I propose a toast to the health of Comrade Malenkov, the genius student of Lenin and the loyal comrade-in-arms of Stalin." Marshal Kim Il-sung also said: "In the anti-imperialist liberation struggle in the East, the Korean people and the Chinese people have a long-standing friendship. Especially in the Korean People's Fatherland Liberation War against American imperialist aggression, the great Chinese people have given us great assistance, and in our most difficult times, sent their outstanding sons and daughters to form the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to the Korean front, using their blood to aid us, driving the enemy south of the 38th parallel, and ensuring our victory. Even today, after the armistice, they continue to give us great assistance. We express our warm gratitude to the great Chinese people, the Chinese government, and the Chinese Communist Party. I propose a toast to the health of Comrade Mao Zedong, the respected leader of the Chinese people." Subsequently, Marshal Kim Il-sung successively proposed toasts to the leaders of the various People's Democratic countries, and to the health of all the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army and all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝朝鲜解放八周年  金日成元帅举行盛大宴会

1953-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜解放八周年
    金日成元帅举行盛大宴会
    【新华社平壤十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅，为了庆祝朝鲜解放八周年，于十五日下午举行盛大宴会，招待朝鲜军民代表和驻平壤的外宾，参加者三百多人。朝鲜方面出席作陪的有：最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一，内阁副首相崔庸健、崔昌益、郑一龙、朴义琓等。
    应邀出席宴会的有：朝鲜各民主党派、人民团体代表，人民军高级将领，共和国英雄，劳动英雄和平壤地区工人、农民、妇女、青年、文化教育工作者、艺术工作者的代表等。应邀出席宴会的外宾有：驻朝鲜的苏联大使苏兹达列夫，中国大使馆代办甘野陶，各人民民主国家外交使节，各国驻平壤的记者等。中国人民志愿军代表团，包括来平壤参加朝鲜英雄模范大会的志愿军战斗英雄和功臣们，在首席代表李志民将军率领下，应邀出席宴会。
    宴会开始时，金日成元帅举杯说：我们在祖国解放战争业已取得胜利的环境中，庆祝英雄的苏联军队解放朝鲜的八周年纪念。我们对于苏联人民、苏联政府和苏联共产党深深地表示感谢。我提议：为列宁的天才学生、斯大林的忠实战友马林科夫同志的健康而干杯。金日成元帅又说：在东方反帝国主义的解放战争中，朝鲜人民和中国人民有着历史悠久的友谊。特别是在反对美帝国主义侵略的朝鲜人民祖国解放战争中，伟大的中国人民给予我们很大的援助，并在我们最困难的时期，以自己优秀的儿女组成中国人民志愿军，派到朝鲜前线来，以鲜血援助我们，把敌人驱逐到三八线以南，保障了我们的胜利。在停战后的今天，仍在继续给予我们很大的援助。我们向伟大的中国人民、中国政府、中国共产党表示热烈的感谢。我提议：为中国人民敬爱的领袖毛泽东同志的健康而干杯。接着，金日成元帅依次提议为各人民民主国家的领袖，为朝鲜人民军全体将士和中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员的健康而干杯。
    宴会在热烈、友好的气氛中进行。
