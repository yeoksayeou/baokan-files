Title: General Peng Dehuai Receives the Title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" at an Award Ceremony in Pyongyang

Author:

Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 31st: The ceremony at which the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea bestowed the supreme honor – the glorious title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" – upon General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, was solemnly held in Pyongyang at 6:00 PM on July 31st. Among those attending the ceremony were: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army; General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers; and Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea. Also participating in the ceremony were Park Jung-ai, Park Chang-ok, and Kim Il, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, and Park Ui-wan; heads of various departments and ministries of the Cabinet and other party and government leaders; and General Nam Il, Chief Delegate of the Korean and Chinese Delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations, as well as many senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

The ceremony began with Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, reading the decree of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea on awarding General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" and the Order of the National Flag, First Class, and the Gold Star Medal.

Then, Chairman Kim Du-bong of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea personally pinned the brilliant Gold Star Medal on the left breast of General Peng Dehuai's uniform. At this moment, the audience applauded warmly in congratulations. Marshal Kim Il-sung warmly shook hands with General Peng Dehuai.

Finally, General Peng Dehuai stood up and delivered a speech of thanks, saying: On behalf of the Chinese People's Volunteers, I thank the Korean people and the heroic and tenacious Korean People's Army; I thank the Korean people for their enthusiastic support and care for the Chinese People's Volunteers; I thank the Korean Workers' Party for their care for the Chinese People's Volunteers in all aspects, which enabled the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army to fight side by side and achieve victory; I thank the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for its constant care for all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers and for me personally. Let me shout: Long live the Democratic People's Republic of Korea! Long live the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party! Long live the heroic and tenacious Korean People's Army! Long live Marshal Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people!

After General Peng Dehuai finished his speech of thanks, the ceremony concluded with warm applause from the entire audience.

### 彭德怀将军在平壤市举行的授奖典礼上  接受“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号

1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀将军在平壤市举行的授奖典礼上
    接受“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会以最高荣誉——“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”的光荣称号赠予中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军的典礼，于七月三十一日下午六时在平壤市隆重举行。参加典礼的有：朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅、中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军、朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉。参加典礼的还有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一，内阁副首相崔庸健、崔昌益、郑一龙、朴义琓，内阁各相等党政首长和朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团首席代表南日大将及朝鲜人民军高级将领等多人。
    典礼首先由朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会书记长康良煜朗读朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会关于赠予中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军以“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”的称号及一级国旗勋章、金星奖章的政令。
    接着，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉亲自把灿烂辉煌的金星奖章佩戴在彭德怀将军军装的左胸前。这时，全场热烈鼓掌祝贺。金日成元帅与彭德怀将军热烈握手。
    最后，彭德怀将军起立致谢词说：我代表中国人民志愿军，感谢朝鲜人民和英勇顽强的朝鲜人民军；感谢朝鲜人民对中国人民志愿军的热情支援和爱护；感谢朝鲜劳动党对中国人民志愿军在各方面的关怀，使中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军并肩作战获得了胜利；感谢朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府对中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员和对我个人的经常照顾。让我高呼：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国万岁！朝鲜劳动党中央万岁！英勇、顽强的朝鲜人民军万岁！朝鲜人民领袖金日成元帅万岁！
    彭德怀将军致谢词毕，典礼在全场的热烈鼓掌声中宣告礼成。
