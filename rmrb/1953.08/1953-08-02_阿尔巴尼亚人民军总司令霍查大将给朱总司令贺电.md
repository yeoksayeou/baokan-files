Title: General Hoxha, Commander-in-Chief of the Albanian People's Army, Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author: Hoxha

Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1

To Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, please allow me, on behalf of the People's Army of the People's Republic of Albania and in my own name, to extend to you and the brave Chinese People's Liberation Army our most sincere and fraternal greetings. The soldiers, non-commissioned officers, officers, and generals of the Albanian People's Army, together with the entire Albanian people, are watching with great concern the great achievements of the great Chinese people in building a new life and in heroically resisting the sinister conspiracies of the enemies of peace and socialism—the American imperialists and their lackeys. The Albanian People's Army wholeheartedly wishes the Chinese People's Liberation Army further achievements in combat and political training, following the example and experience of the glorious Soviet Army.

    General Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania and Commander-in-Chief of the Albanian People's Army
    Tirana, July 31, 1953

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民军总司令霍查大将给朱总司令贺电
霍查
1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民军总司令霍查大将给朱总司令贺电中国人民解放军朱德总司令：
    值此中国人民解放军建军节之际，请允许我代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民军并以我个人的名义，向您及勇敢的中国人民解放军致以最衷心的、兄弟般的敬礼。阿尔巴尼亚人民军的战士、士官、军官和将军们与阿尔巴尼亚全体人民，极其关怀地注视着伟大的中国人民在建设新生活方面以及在英勇地反击和平与社会主义的敌人——美国帝国主义者及其仆从们的罪恶阴谋方面所获得的伟大成就。阿尔巴尼亚人民军全心全意地预祝中国人民解放军按照光荣的苏联军队的榜样与经验，在战斗与政治锻炼上获得更进一步的成就。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席兼阿尔巴尼亚人民军总司令霍查大将
        一九五三年七月三十一日于地拉那
