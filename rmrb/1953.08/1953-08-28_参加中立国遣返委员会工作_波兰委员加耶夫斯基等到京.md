Title: Polish Commissioner Gajewski and Others Arrive in Beijing to Participate in Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission Work
Author:
Date: 1953-08-28
Page: 1

    Participating in the Work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission
    Polish Commissioner Gajewski and Others Arrive in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 27th] Upon the invitation of both sides of the Korean armistice, the Polish Commissioner and Minister Plenipotentiary Gajewski, and political advisor Tajkotinski, who are participating in the Korean armistice Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, have arrived in Beijing.
    Those who went to the airport to greet them included: Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Jiang Keding, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Ma Zhenwu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
    Xu Zhe, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and Gula, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic in China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 参加中立国遣返委员会工作  波兰委员加耶夫斯基等到京

1953-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中立国遣返委员会工作
    波兰委员加耶夫斯基等到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】应朝鲜停战双方邀请，参加朝鲜停战中立国遣返委员会工作的波兰委员、全权公使加耶夫斯基和政治顾问泰科琴斯基已抵达北京。
    赴机场迎接的有：中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长沈平，人民革命军事委员会对外联络处副处长蒋克定和北京市人民政府外事处处长马振武等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲和波兰人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办古拉也到机场迎接。
