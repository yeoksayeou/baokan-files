Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Holds Second Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 2nd

    The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission held its second meeting on the 2nd. The meeting decided that each Neutral Nations Inspection Team should be composed of two officers from each neutral nation, i.e., eight officers in total from the four neutral nations. The meeting also exchanged views on issues such as transportation, communication, and the safety of personnel for the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams. As of the 2nd, sixty-three personnel from the neutral nations of Czechoslovakia and Poland had arrived in Kaesong.

### 中立国监察委员会举行第二次会议

1953-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会举行第二次会议
    【新华社开城二日电】中立国监察委员会在二日举行第二次会议。会议决定每一中立国视察小组由每一中立国各派二名军官，即四个中立国共派八名军官组成。会议并对中立国视察小组的交通、通讯及小组人员的安全等问题交换了意见。到二日为止，捷克斯洛伐克和波兰两中立国的人员已有六十三人到达开城。
