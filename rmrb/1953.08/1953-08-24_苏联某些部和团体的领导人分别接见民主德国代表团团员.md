Title: Leaders of Certain Soviet Ministries and Organizations Meet with Members of the German Democratic Republic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 23rd (TASS): On August 22nd, leaders of certain Soviet ministries and social organizations separately met with members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic and held talks. The Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, M.Z. Saburov, met with the Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the German Democratic Republic, B. Leuschner, and talked with him. The Minister of Ferrous Metallurgy Industry, I.F. Tevosian, met with the Minister of Metallurgy and Mining Industry of the German Democratic Republic, F. Selbmann. The Minister of Construction, N.A. Dygai, spoke with the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Construction of the German Democratic Republic, Dr. L. Bolz. The Minister of Agriculture and Agricultural Storage of the USSR, A.I. Kozlov, met with the Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, H. Rau. The Director of the State Bank, V.F. Popov, met with the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Finance, Dr. H. Loch. The Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, N.M. Shvernik, met with the Chairman of the Federation of Free German Trade Unions, H. Warnke, and spoke with him. The Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, A.N. Shelepin, met with the Chairman of the Central Council of the Free German Youth League, E. Honecker. The Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, N.V. Popova, met with the representative of the German Democratic Women's Federation, I. Tiele. Deputy Prime Minister O. Nuschke of the German Democratic Republic visited Nikolai, Archbishop of Krutitsy and Kolomna. The conversations took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 苏联某些部和团体的领导人分别接见民主德国代表团团员

1953-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联某些部和团体的领导人分别接见民主德国代表团团员
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日电：八月二十二日，苏联某些部和社会团体的领导人分别接见德意志民主共和国政府代表团的团员，并进行谈话。苏联部长会议国家计划委员会主席马·扎·萨布罗夫接见德意志民主共和国国家计划委员会主席布·劳施纳并与他谈话。钢铁工业部长伊·费·捷沃西安接见德意志民主共和国冶金与采矿工业部长弗·赛尔柏曼。建造部长尼·亚·德加伊与德意志民主共和国副总理兼建设部长洛·博尔茨博士谈话。苏联农业和农业储存部长阿·伊·科兹洛夫接见农林部长汉·赖希尔特。国家银行行长维·费·波波夫接见副总理兼财政部长汉·洛赫博士。全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克接见自由德国工会联合会主席赫·瓦恩克并与他谈话。苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记亚·纳·谢列平会晤了自由德国青年联盟中央理事会主席埃·昂纳克。苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席尼·瓦·波波娃会晤了德国民主妇女联合会代表伊·蒂勒。德意志民主共和国副总理奥·努舍克教授访问了克卢提柴和科洛勉斯基的大主教尼古拉。交谈在友好的气氛中进行。
