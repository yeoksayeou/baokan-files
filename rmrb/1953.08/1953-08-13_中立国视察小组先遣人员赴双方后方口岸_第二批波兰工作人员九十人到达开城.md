Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Advance Personnel Head to Both Sides' Rear Ports; Second Group of Ninety Polish Staff Arrives in Kaesong
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 12th: According to the Korean Armistice Agreement, the advance personnel of the ten Neutral Nations Supervisory Commissions, responsible for supervising and inspecting the rotation of military personnel and the replacement of weapons and ammunition entering Korea at specific ports in the rear areas of both hostile sides in Korea, departed from Panmunjom at noon on August 12th. They are heading to Sinuiju, Chongjin, Hungnam, Manpo, and Sinanju in the military control area of the Korean-Chinese side, and Incheon, Daegu, Busan, Gangneung, and Gunsan in the military control area of the United Nations Command. The remaining personnel of each Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission will also leave Panmunjom around the 15th of this month, heading to the aforementioned ports respectively.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 12th: The advance personnel of the four neutral nations, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Sweden, and Switzerland, of the ten Neutral Nations Supervisory Commissions, departed from Panmunjom this afternoon, heading separately to specific ports in the rear areas of both sides. The advance personnel of the five groups stationed in our area, totaling fifty-five people, were accompanied by Senior Colonel Zhu Ran, Secretary-General of our side of the Military Armistice Commission, and Senior Colonel Assistant Secretary-General Xu Ming, departing from Panmunjom by car at 2:00 PM to Kaesong Railway Station, and then transferring to trains to Sinuiju, Chongjin, Hungnam, Manpo, and Sinanju.

When the advance personnel of the four neutral nations departed, Lieutenant General Blech of Czechoslovakia and Major General Ungrat, the Polish members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, went to see them off and directed the deployment of the departure preparations.

Also seeing them off were General Li Sang-cho, Chief Commissioner of our side of the Military Armistice Commission, and Generals Ding Guoyu, Park Il-young, and Chai Chengwen. General Li Sang-cho, representing the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, welcomed the neutral nations personnel working in our area and wished them success in their work.

Before the departure of all the advance personnel, Major General Ungrat, the Polish member and chairman of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission's August 12th meeting, held a reception at the Armistice Agreement signing hall in Panmunjom, treating the advance personnel with Polish-produced famous wines, fruits, and pastries.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 11th: Ninety Polish staff members participating in the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission arrived in Kaesong on the morning of the 11th. General Park Il-young, Commissioner of our side of the Military Armistice Commission, and others welcomed them at the station.

The first group of staff members from the Czechoslovak and Polish neutral nations arrived in Kaesong on July 30th. Together with those arriving today, there are now a total of one hundred and fifty-three people. The remaining follow-up personnel will arrive successively in the near future.

### 中立国视察小组先遣人员赴双方后方口岸  第二批波兰工作人员九十人到达开城

1953-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国视察小组先遣人员赴双方后方口岸
    第二批波兰工作人员九十人到达开城
    【新华社开城十二日电】根据朝鲜停战协定，负责在朝鲜敌对双方各后方特定口岸，监督和视察进入朝鲜的军事人员的轮换与武器弹药的替换的十个中立国视察小组，其先遣人员已在八月十二日下午自板门店出发，分赴朝中方面军事控制地区内的新义州、清津、兴南、满浦、新安州各口岸及联合国军方面军事控制地区内的仁川、大邱、釜山、江陵、群山各口岸。各该中立国视察小组的其他人员，也将在本月十五日左右离板门店，分别前往上述各口岸。
    【新华社开城十二日电】十个中立国视察小组的捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、瑞典和瑞士四中立国先遣人员，十二日下午从板门店出发，分头前往双方后方的特定口岸。派驻我方地区的五个小组的先遣人员五十五人，在下午二时由军事停战委员会我方秘书长朱然上校和上校助理秘书长徐鸣陪同，从板门店乘汽车到开城火车站，再换乘火车前往新义州、清津、兴南、满浦和新安州五个口岸。
    在四中立国先遣人员出发时，中立国监察委员会捷克斯洛伐克委员布莱希中将，波兰委员翁格洛夫斯基少将都前往送行，并指挥部署出发的各项工作。
    前往送行的还有军事停战委员会我方首席委员李相朝将军，委员丁国钰将军、朴一英将军、柴成文将军。李相朝将军代表朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，对在我方地区工作的中立国人员表示欢迎，并祝他们工作成功。
    在全体先遣人员出发前，中立国监察委员会十二日会议的主席波兰委员翁格洛夫斯基少将在板门店的停战协定签字大厅举行酒会，以波兰出产的名酒、果品和点心款待先遣人员。
    【新华社开城十一日电】参加中立国监察委员会工作的波兰工作人员九十人，在十一日上午到达开城。到车站欢迎的有军事停战委员会我方委员朴一英将军等人。
    捷克斯洛伐克和波兰两中立国的首批工作人员是在七月三十日到达开城的，连同今天到达的已共有一百五十三人。其他的后续人员均将在最近陆续到达。
