Title: Korean National Congress of Combat Heroes Opens in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1953-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 18th: The Korean National Congress of Combat Heroes, held to exchange and summarize combat experience, opened in Pyongyang on the 17th. The session will last for three days.

Attending the congress were 170 "Republic Heroes" and 200 model combatants from various branches of the Korean People's Army. Prominent Korean model labor heroes from industry and agriculture, and some family members of "Republic Heroes" were also invited to attend. A Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat hero delegation of 40 members, led by团长 Zhou Zhi, was also invited to attend.

On the morning of the first day of the congress, as the combat heroes of the Korean and Chinese People's Armies rode to the venue, they were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Pyongyang. The masses waiting in front of the Central Committee building of the Labor Party rushed forward to offer flowers and pay their respects to the heroes.

The presidium of the congress was composed of Pak Chong-ae and Kim Il, vice-chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Labor Party, Marshal Choe Yong-gon, Minister of National Defense, Vice-Minister Kim Ung, and Zhou Zhi, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat hero delegation. After the congress began, more than 100 Young Pioneers poured into the venue to present flowers to the heroes of the Korean and Chinese People's Armies. Representatives of people from all walks of life in Pyongyang also delivered congratulatory speeches and presented flags to the congress.

Marshal Choe Yong-gon gave a report on the domestic situation and military issues. He described the great victories of the Korean and Chinese People's Armies over the past three years. He pointed out that as of July 25, 447 "Republic Heroes" and more than 514,000 recipients of medals had emerged in the Korean People's Army. He called on the People's Army to further strengthen its forces and actively participate in the construction of the motherland.

Zhou Zhi, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat hero delegation, delivered a congratulatory speech to the congress on behalf of all members and combat heroes of the Volunteer Army, which was warmly applauded by the audience. Then, amidst music and thunderous applause, the Volunteer Army combat heroes presented 19 silk banners from the Volunteer Army leadership, various units, and many heroic companies to the congress.

The meeting on the 17th ended after male and female students from the Kim Chaek Revolutionary Martyrs' Children's Academy delivered congratulatory speeches and presented flags.

### 朝鲜全国战斗英雄大会在平壤开幕

1953-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜全国战斗英雄大会在平壤开幕
    【新华社平壤十八日电】为了交流和总结战斗经验而举行的朝鲜全国战斗英雄大会，十七日在平壤市开幕。会期是三天。
    参加大会的，有朝鲜人民军各兵种部队的“共和国英雄”一百七十人，模范战斗员二百人。应邀参加大会的有朝鲜著名的工农业英雄模范和一部分“共和国英雄”的家属。中国人民志愿军战斗英雄代表团一行四十人，在团长周秩率领下，也应邀参加。
    大会第一天的早晨，当朝中人民军队的战斗英雄们乘车去会场时，一路上受到平壤各界人民的热烈欢迎。等候在劳动党中央本部门前的群众，争先恐后地涌向前去，向英雄们献花致敬。
    大会主席团由朝鲜劳动党中央副委员长朴正爱、金一，民族保卫相崔庸健次帅、副相金雄及中国人民志愿军战斗英雄代表团团长周秩等人组成。大会开始后，一百多个少年团员涌进会场，向朝中人民军队英雄们献花。平壤市各界人民的代表也先后向大会致祝词并献旗。
    崔庸健次帅在会上作了关于国内形势和军队问题的报告。他叙述了朝中人民军队三年来的伟大胜利。他指出：到七月二十五日为止，朝鲜人民军中已涌现了四百四十七名“共和国英雄”和五十一万四千多名受勋者。他号召人民军进一步加强自己的力量，并积极参加祖国的建设。
    中国人民志愿军战斗英雄代表团团长周秩代表志愿军全体人员和战斗英雄向大会致祝词，受到全场热烈的鼓掌。接着志愿军战斗英雄们，在音乐声和暴风雨般的掌声中，把志愿军领导机关、各部队和许多英雄连队的十九面锦旗献给大会。
    十七日的会议在金策革命烈士子女学院的男女学员们致祝词并献旗之后结束。
