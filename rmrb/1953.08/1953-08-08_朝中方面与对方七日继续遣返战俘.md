Title: Korean-Chinese Side Continues to Repatriate Prisoners of War to the Other Side for Seven Days
Author:
Date: 1953-08-08
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 7th -- On August 7th, the Korean-Chinese side repatriated the following prisoners of war to the other side: 244 Korean prisoners of war and 150 non-Korean prisoners of war, totaling 394 people. Among the non-Korean prisoners of war, there were 81 Americans, 25 British, 25 Turks, 12 Filipinos, and 7 Colombians.
    The other side repatriated to our side: 2,151 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army, including 352 sick and wounded captured personnel, and 600 captured personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteers, totaling 2,751 people.

### 朝中方面与对方七日继续遣返战俘

1953-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝中方面与对方七日继续遣返战俘
    【新华社开城七日电】八月七日，朝中方面遣送给对方的战俘有：朝鲜籍战俘二百四十四人，非朝鲜籍战俘一百五十人，共三百九十四人。在非朝鲜籍战俘中，包括美国籍八十一人，英国籍二十五人，土耳其籍二十五人，菲律宾籍十二人，哥伦比亚籍七人。
    对方遣返给我方的有：朝鲜人民军被俘人员二千一百五十一人，内有病伤被俘人员三百五十二人，中国人民志愿军被俘人员六百人，共二千七百五十一人。
