Title: Yellow River Midstream Water Level Rises Sharply; People Along the River Intensify Dyke Protection

Author:

Date: 1953-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, August 28th: The people in the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River are nervously preparing for the first major flood of the autumn flood season, intensifying the protection of the dikes. In the middle reaches of the Yellow River, upstream of Longmen, it has been raining continuously in recent days. On the 26th, the water level of the Yellow River at Longmen rose to near the highest peak during the early August flood season, marking the first major flood of the autumn flood season. At 9:00 AM on the 28th, the flood peak had entered Qinchang, Henan Province. The Yellow River Flood Control Headquarters has urgently telegraphed the Henan and Shandong Provincial Yellow River Flood Control Headquarters, instructing the two provinces to pay attention to defense, ensure the safety of the dikes, and protect the autumn harvest. Currently, the people of all counties along the river in Henan and Shandong provinces are making full preparations. Flood control organizations at all levels are actively deploying, sending large numbers of cadres and people along the river to inspect the dikes, defend in sections, and strive to overcome this flood.

### 黄河中游水位猛涨  沿河人民加紧护堤

1953-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    黄河中游水位猛涨
    沿河人民加紧护堤
    【新华社开封二十八日电】黄河中、下游人民正紧张迎接秋汛中首次最大洪水，加紧防护堤防。黄河中游龙门以上最近连日降雨，二十六日龙门黄河水位上涨到接近于八月初伏汛中最高洪峰，为秋汛中首次最大洪水。二十八日上午九时洪峰已进入河南省秦厂。黄河防汛总指挥部已紧急电告河南、山东两省黄河防汛指挥部，指示两省注意防守，确保大堤安全，保卫秋季丰收。现在，河南、山东两省沿河各县人民，都正在作充分准备。各级防汛机构都积极部署，派出大批干部和沿河人民上堤巡查、分段防守，为战胜这次洪水而努力。
