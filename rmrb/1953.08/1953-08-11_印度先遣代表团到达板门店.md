Title: Indian Advance Delegation Arrives at Panmunjom
Author:
Date: 1953-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 10th: The Indian advance delegation, led by Mr. R. K. Nehru, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and Major General Thorat, Chief of Staff of the Indian Army, arrived at Panmunjom at 10:00 AM on August 10th to make necessary preparations for India's participation in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission stipulated in the Korean Armistice Agreement. They met with Lieutenant General Li Sang Cho, Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, and General Ding Guoyu to arrange for the Indian advance delegation to depart for the headquarters of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers on August 11th for a visit.

### 印度先遣代表团到达板门店

1953-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    印度先遣代表团到达板门店
    【新华社开城十日电】为印度参加朝鲜停战协定所规定的中立国遣返委员会作必要准备的印度先遣代表团一行，在印度外交部外事秘书鲁·克·尼赫鲁先生和印度陆军参谋长托拉特少将率领下，在八月十日上午十时到达板门店，与朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝中将和委员丁国钰将军会晤，就印度先遣代表团于八月十一日出发前往朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军总部访问一事作了安排。
