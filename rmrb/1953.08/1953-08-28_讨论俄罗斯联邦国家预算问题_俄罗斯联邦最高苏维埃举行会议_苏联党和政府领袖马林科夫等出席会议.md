Title: Discussion of the Russian Federation's National Budget Issues, Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Held, Soviet Party and Government Leaders Malenkov and Others Present

Author: 
Date: 1953-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th dispatch: According to TASS, the Third Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation has been held in the Moscow Kremlin since the 25th.

The first day of the session was held on the afternoon of the 25th. When Soviet Communist Party and government leaders G. M. Malenkov, V. M. Molotov, N. S. Khrushchev, K. Y. Voroshilov, N. A. Bulganin, L. M. Kaganovich, A. I. Mikoyan, M. Z. Saburov, M. G. Pervukhin, M. A. Suslov, P. K. Ponomarenko, P. N. Pospelov, N. N. Shatalin, and others appeared on the government platform, all the delegates and guests responded with enthusiastic applause.

I. Fadeyev, Minister of Finance of the Russian Federation, delivered a report on the Russian Federation's 1953 national budget and the implementation of the 1951-1952 national budget. He pointed out that the peoples of all nationalities of the Russian Federation had made remarkable progress in developing the socialist economy and culture. The 1953 national budget submitted by the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation to the Supreme Soviet for consideration totals 57,052.2 million rubles, an increase of 3,292.4 million rubles over last year. Budget revenue from the socialist economy is increasing significantly, while revenue from residents is decreasing. This year, the total agricultural tax of the Russian Federation will be reduced by a total of 2.4 billion rubles, and next year it will be more than 62 percent lower than in 1952. The budget allocates 51,917.3 million rubles to industry, agriculture, other sectors of the national economy, and social and cultural undertakings, accounting for more than nine-tenths of all expenditures. At the same time, the budgets of the 55 border regions and states and 12 autonomous republics of the Russian Federation are also constantly increasing. Compared with the pre-war period, these budgets have more than doubled.

S. Afanasyev, Chairman of the Budget Committee, then gave a supplementary report on the implementation of the Russian Federation's 1951-1952 national budget.

The meeting continued on the morning of the 26th, beginning the discussion on the report on the national budget. Representatives from the Moscow, Stalingrad, Chelyabinsk, and other states and the Buryat-Mongolian Autonomous Republic spoke at the meeting. They spoke about the achievements of the republic in various fields and warmly supported the draft budget for 1953 put forward by the Council of Ministers.

### 讨论俄罗斯联邦国家预算问题  俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃举行会议  苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等出席会议

1953-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

    讨论俄罗斯联邦国家预算问题
    俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃举行会议
    苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等出席会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社消息：俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃第三次会议，自二十五日起在莫斯科克里姆林宫举行。
    第一日会议在二十五日下午举行。苏联共产党和政府领袖格·马·马林科夫、维·米·莫洛托夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、克·叶·伏罗希洛夫、尼·亚·布尔加宁、拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇、阿·伊·米高扬、马·扎·萨布罗夫、米·格·别尔乌辛、米·安·苏斯洛夫、潘·康·波诺马连科、彼·恩·波斯伯洛夫、纳·纳·夏塔林等出现在政府席上时，全体代表和来宾报以热烈的掌声。
    俄罗斯联邦财政部部长伊·法捷耶夫在会上作了关于俄罗斯联邦一九五三年国家预算和一九五一—一九五二年国家预算执行情况的报告。他指出，俄罗斯联邦各族人民在发展社会主义经济和文化方面有了显著的进展。俄罗斯联邦部长会议提交最高苏维埃会议审议的一九五三年国家预算，总额达五百七十亿五千二百二十万卢布，比去年增加了三十二亿九千二百四十万卢布。预算从社会主义经济得来的收入正在大大增加，从居民得来的收入正在减低。今年，俄罗斯联邦农业税总额共将减少二十四亿卢布，明年将比一九五二年减低百分之六十二以上。预算拨给工业、农业、国民经济其他部门及社会和文化事业的经费为五百十九亿一千七百三十万卢布，占全部支出十分之九以上。与此同时，俄罗斯联邦五十五个边疆区和州、十二个自治共和国的预算也不断增加。和战前比较，这些预算已增为两倍多。
    预算委员会主席谢·阿凡纳耶夫接着作了关于一九五一—一九五二年俄罗斯联邦国家预算执行情况的补充报告。
    二十六日上午继续开会，开始讨论关于国家预算的报告。在会上发言的有莫斯科、斯大林格勒、车里雅宾斯克等州和布利亚蒙古自治共和国的代表。他们谈到了共和国各方面的成就，并热烈拥护部长会议提出的一九五三年预算草案。
