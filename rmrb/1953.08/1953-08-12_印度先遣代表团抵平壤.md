Title: Indian Advance Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1953-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 11th: An Indian advance delegation, led by Mr. R.K. Nehru, Secretary for External Affairs of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Major General Thorat, Chief of Staff of the Indian Army, arrived in Pyongyang by train from Kaesong at 7:00 PM on the 11th for a visit to make the necessary preparations for India's participation in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission stipulated by the Korean Armistice Agreement.

Those who went to East Pyongyang Station to welcome the delegation included: Korean People's Army representative, General Kim Kwang-hyup, Chief of General Staff of the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea Li Dong-geon, representative of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front Kim Min-san, and Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee, Zheng Yan-biao.

The headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers sent General Gao Cunxin, Commander of the Artillery, and four others to the station to welcome the delegation.

After alighting from the train, the members of the Indian advance delegation shook hands with the welcomers and received bouquets from female soldiers of the Korean People's Army.

### 印度先遣代表团抵平壤

1953-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    印度先遣代表团抵平壤
    【新华社平壤十一日电】为印度参加朝鲜停战协定所规定的中立国遣返委员会作必要准备的印度先遣代表团一行，在印度外交部外事秘书鲁·克·尼赫鲁先生和印度陆军参谋长托拉特少将率领下，在十一日下午七时从开城乘火车到达平壤，进行访问。
    前往东平壤车站欢迎的有：朝鲜人民军代表、朝鲜人民军最高司令部总参谋长金光侠将军，朝鲜外务省副相李东建，朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线代表金民山，平壤市人民委员会委员长郑然彪。
    中国人民志愿军司令部派炮兵司令员高存信将军等五人到车站欢迎。
    印度先遣代表团人员在下车后和欢迎者握手，并接受了朝鲜人民军女战士的献花。
