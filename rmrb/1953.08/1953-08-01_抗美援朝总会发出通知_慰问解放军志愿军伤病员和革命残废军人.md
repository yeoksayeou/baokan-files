Title: General Association to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea Issues Notice to Comfort PLA and CPV Wounded, Sick, and Disabled Veterans
Author:
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: To commemorate the 26th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day, the Chinese People's General Association to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea issued a notice on July 21st to the general and sub-associations in major administrative regions and the provincial and municipal sub-associations to express condolences to all wounded and sick soldiers and revolutionary disabled veterans of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The notice instructed the local sub-associations to select representatives to carry letters of sympathy written by the local governments and people to the hospitals, veterans' schools, sanatoriums, rehabilitation centers, and veterans' farms and ranches where the wounded and sick soldiers are located to express their condolences to the wounded and sick soldiers, disabled veterans, and other personnel. The notice also instructed the local sub-associations to actively assist relevant departments in implementing the notice of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government on preferential treatment work, and entrusted the local hospitals for wounded and sick soldiers, veterans' schools, veterans' sanatoriums, rehabilitation centers, veterans' farms, ranches, and other institutions to distribute 50,000 yuan (RMB) per person to the wounded and sick soldiers, revolutionary disabled veterans, and recuperating personnel.

### 抗美援朝总会发出通知  慰问解放军志愿军伤病员和革命残废军人

1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝总会发出通知
    慰问解放军志愿军伤病员和革命残废军人
    【新华社三十一日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会为纪念“八一”建军节二十六周年，于七月二十一日向各大行政区总分会各省市分会发出慰问中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军全体伤病员和革命残废军人的通知。通知指示各地分会选派代表，携带当地政府与人民所写的慰问信，至伤病员所在医院、荣军学校、疗养院、教养院及荣军农场、牧场向伤病员和残废军人等人员进行慰问。通知同时指示各地分会应与各有关部门积极协助贯彻中央人民政府内务部关于优抚工作的通知，并委托各地伤病员医院、荣军学校、荣军疗养院、教养院、荣军农场牧场等机关向伤病员、革命残废军人和休养员等人员每人发给人民币五万元。
