Title: According to the Armistice Agreement and the Regulations of the Military Armistice Commission, Our Side Dispatches Civil Police to Work in the Demilitarized Zone
Author:
Date: 1953-08-15
Page: 4

    According to the Armistice Agreement and the Regulations of the Military Armistice Commission
    Our Side Dispatches Civil Police to Work in the Demilitarized Zone
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 14] According to the provisions of the Armistice Agreement and the agreement reached at the 11th Secretaries Meeting of the Military Armistice Commission, the Korean-Chinese side dispatched 800 civil police officers to the area north of the Military Demarcation Line in the Demilitarized Zone from 10:00 to 19:00 on August 10th. These officers will assist in handling civil affairs and are responsible for maintaining order in our side of the Demilitarized Zone. The civil police officers dispatched by our side have been stationed in the areas distributed by the Joint Observer Group of the Military Armistice Commission, and guard posts and patrol posts have been established in necessary locations and channels.
    The time when the US side's civil police will enter the area south of the Military Demarcation Line in the Demilitarized Zone has not yet been determined.

### 根据停战协定及军事停战委员会的规定  我方派出民政警察进入非军事区工作

1953-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    根据停战协定及军事停战委员会的规定
    我方派出民政警察进入非军事区工作
    【新华社开城十四日电】朝中方面根据停战协定及军事停战委员会第十一次秘书长会议上的协议的规定，于八月十日十时至十九时派出民政警察八百人进入非军事区军事分界线以北部分，协助办理民政事宜和负责维持我方非军事区内的秩序，进入非军事区的我方民政警察已按照军事停战委员会联合观察小组分布的地区分别驻扎，在必要的地点和通道内设立岗哨和巡逻哨。
    美方民政警察进入非军事区军事分界线以南部分的时间尚未确定。
