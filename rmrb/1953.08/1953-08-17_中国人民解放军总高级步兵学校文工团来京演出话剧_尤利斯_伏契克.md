Title: The Cultural Troupe of the General Senior Infantry School of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Performs the Play "Julius Fucik" in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-08-17
Page: 3

Culture Briefs

    The Cultural Troupe of the General Senior Infantry School of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Performs the Play "Julius Fucik" in Beijing
    To commemorate the tenth anniversary of the heroic sacrifice of the immortal communist fighter and Czechoslovak People's Hero Julius Fucik, the Art Troupe of the General Senior Infantry School of the Chinese People's Liberation Army came to Beijing to perform the four-act, twelve-scene play "Julius Fucik," adapted by Soviet playwright Yu. Polyakovsky based on Fucik's "Report from the Gallows." The entire play fully expresses Fucik's high patriotism and internationalism of the Czechoslovak people.
    The troupe held a preview performance on August 3rd at the rehearsal hall of the General Political Department's Cultural Troupe. Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, ministers of various departments of the Military Commission, and figures from the capital's literary and art circles watched the preview performance. The play has been officially performed at the Labor Theater since August 8th.

### 中国人民解放军总高级步兵学校文工团来京演出话剧“尤利斯·伏契克”

1953-08-17
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    中国人民解放军总高级步兵学校文工团来京演出话剧“尤利斯·伏契克”
    为了纪念不朽的共产主义战士、捷克斯洛伐克人民英雄尤利斯·伏契克英勇就义十周年，中国人民解放军总高级步兵学校文艺工作团，来京演出苏联剧作家尤·波掠柯夫斯基根据伏契克所著“绞刑架下的报告”改编的四幕十二场话剧“尤利斯·伏契克”。全剧充分表现了伏契克的捷克斯洛伐克人民高度爱国主义和国际主义的精神。
    该团曾于八月三日在总政治部文工团排演场举行预演，中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、军委各部部长和首都文艺界人士都观看了预演。该剧自八月八日起在劳动剧场正式演出。
