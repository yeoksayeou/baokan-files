Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Tracks Laid to Huazang Temple
Author:
Date: 1953-08-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 26th. The Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway continues to extend westward from Yongdeng, and the tracks have been laid to Huazang Temple in the Tianzhu Tibetan Autonomous Region. To date, 132 kilometers of track have been laid for the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway, completing 72 percent of the annual track-laying task. The railway is now continuing to advance towards the pastoral grasslands. The laying of tracks to the Tianzhu Tibetan Autonomous Region was warmly welcomed by the Tibetan, Han, Tu, Hui, and Mongolian people. They and the railway construction workers held a celebration meeting of several thousand people. At the meeting, Zhang Xianbin (Tibetan), the head of the Second District of the Tianzhu Autonomous Region, and the Living Buddha of Huazang Temple presented "Hada" to the railway construction workers to pay their respects.

### 兰新铁路铺轨到华藏寺

1953-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    兰新铁路铺轨到华藏寺
    【新华社兰州二十六日电】兰新铁路继续从永登向西延伸，路轨已铺到天祝藏族自治区的华藏寺。至此，兰新铁路已铺好轨道一百三十二公里，完成全年铺轨任务的百分之七十二。现在铁路正继续向牧区草原前进。路轨铺到天祝藏族自治区后，受到藏、汉、土、回、蒙各族人民的热烈欢迎。他们和修筑铁路的工人一起，开了一个几千人的庆祝大会。会上天祝自治区第二区区长张显斌（藏族）和华藏寺的活佛都向筑路工人献“哈达”致敬。
