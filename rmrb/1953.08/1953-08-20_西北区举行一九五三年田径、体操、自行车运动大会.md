Title: Northwest Region Holds 1953 Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games
Author:
Date: 1953-08-20
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    The 1953 Northwest Region Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games were held in Xi'an from August 10th to August 17th. Participating in this conference were delegations from five provinces, including Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, Ningxia, and Xinjiang, as well as the city of Xi'an, comprising a total of 351 athletes from twelve ethnic groups including Han, Hui, Uyghur, Tibetan, Mongol, and Solon. During the conference, sports observation groups from Gansu and Shaanxi, and representatives from Ningxia, also participated in ethnic-style sports performances.

    The results of the competition showed that progress has been made in all events compared to the past. In the thirty-six track and field events, fifteen broke the Northwest Region record from last year. Among them, Li Yongqing, Li Ying, and Ma Chengxiang, who won first, second, and third place respectively in the men's 200-meter low hurdles race, broke the 1952 national record of 28.8 seconds with times of 27 seconds, 28 seconds, and 28.4 seconds respectively. In the women's hand grenade throw, Tibetan athlete Ka Maoji achieved a score of 43.36 meters, and Uyghur athlete Halida achieved a score of 42.93 meters, both breaking the 1952 national record of 39.81 meters.

    At the end of the conference, seventy-three Northwest Region athletes, including Ka Maoji, Halida, Li Yongqing, and Li Ying, were selected to attend the National Track and Field, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games.

### 西北区举行一九五三年田径、体操、自行车运动大会

1953-08-20
第3版()
专栏：

    西北区举行一九五三年田径、体操、自行车运动大会
    一九五三年西北区田径、体操、自行车运动大会八月十日到八月十七日在西安举行。参加这次大会的有陕西、甘肃、青海、宁夏、新疆等五省和西安市的代表团，包括汉、回、维吾尔、藏、蒙、索伦等十二个民族的运动员共三百五十一人。大会上还有甘肃、陕西体育参观团和宁夏代表参加民族形式体育表演。
    大会比赛结果，各项成绩都比以前有了很大进步。在三十六项田径赛中，有十五项突破去年西北区的纪录。其中男子二百公尺低栏比赛第一名李永清、第二名李英、第三名马呈祥，分别以二十七秒、二十八秒、二十八秒四的成绩打破了一九五二年全国二十八秒八的纪录。女子手榴弹掷远藏族运动员卡毛吉的成绩是四十三点三六公尺，维吾尔族运动员哈利达成绩是四十二点九三公尺，都打破了一九五二年全国三十九点八一公尺的纪录。
    大会最后选出了出席全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会的西北区选手卡毛吉、哈利达、李永清、李英等七十三人。   （新华社）
