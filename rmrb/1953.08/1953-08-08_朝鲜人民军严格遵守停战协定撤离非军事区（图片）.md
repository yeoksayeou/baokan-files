Title: Korean People's Army Strictly Observes the Armistice Agreement and Withdraws from the Demilitarized Zone (Photos)
Author: Xue Yin
Date: 1953-08-08
Page: 4

    Korean People's Army Strictly Observes the Armistice Agreement and Withdraws from the Demilitarized Zone
    Soldiers of an artillery unit of the Korean People's Army in a certain location on the front line celebrate the great victory before withdrawing from the demilitarized zone.

    The Korean People's Army, guarding the "Heartbreak Ridge" area on the eastern front of Korea, strictly observes the Armistice Agreement and erects signs at the Military Demarcation Line.

    An artillery unit of a certain unit of the Korean People's Army withdraws from the demilitarized zone and transfers to a new position.

    The scene of a certain unit of the Korean People's Army guarding the front line of the famous heroic position - "Heartbreak Ridge" - withdrawing from the demilitarized zone.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency reporter Xue Yin photographed)

### 朝鲜人民军严格遵守停战协定撤离非军事区（图片）
雪印
1953-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军严格遵守停战协定撤离非军事区
    前线某地的朝鲜人民军炮兵部队的战士们在撤离非军事区前庆祝伟大的胜利。
    守卫在朝鲜东线“伤心岭”地区的朝鲜人民军，严格遵守停战协定，在军事分界线处树立标志牌。
    朝鲜人民军某部的炮兵部队撤离非军事区，向新阵地转移。
    守卫在著名的英雄阵地——“伤心岭”前线的朝鲜人民军某部撤离非军事区的情形。
                                  （新华社记者    雪印摄）
