Title: "Hindustan Standard" Publishes Editorial, Accusing the U.S. of Trying to Sabotage Political Conference
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 2nd. "Hindustan Standard" published an editorial on July 31st stating: The attitude of the United States towards the upcoming political conference to discuss the Korean issue seems to be: "If the negotiations do not proceed exactly as Dulles hopes, the United States will go its own way." The editorial said that the United States is apparently "almost single-mindedly intent on implementing its pre-prepared plan to sabotage the Korean negotiations."

### “印度斯坦旗报”发表社论  指责美方要破坏政治会议

1953-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    “印度斯坦旗报”发表社论
    指责美方要破坏政治会议
    【新华社新德里二日电】“印度斯坦旗报”在七月三十一日发表社论说：美国对于即将来临的讨论朝鲜问题的政治会议所抱的态度似乎是这样：“如果谈判不能完全按照杜勒斯所希望的那样进行，美国就要自行其是”。社论说，美国显然“几乎是一心一意地要实现它事先准备好的破坏朝鲜谈判的计划”。
