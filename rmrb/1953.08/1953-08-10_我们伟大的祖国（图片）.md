    Our Great Motherland
    The Jinxi Chemical Plant is a large-scale comprehensive chemical plant. It produces large quantities of solid caustic soda and liquid caustic soda, large quantities of bleaching powder, chlorobenzene, hydrochloric acid, and carbolic acid. These are all products necessary for the development of light industry. This is the newly built bleaching powder tower, which has increased the maximum output of bleaching powder by five times compared to the original design output. Workers are transporting the finished bleaching powder from the narrow-gauge railway.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    锦西化工厂是一个综合性的大型化工厂。这里生产着大量的固体火碱和液碱、大量的漂白粉、氯化苯、盐酸和石炭酸。这些都是发展轻工业所必需的产品。这是新建的漂粉塔，它使漂白粉的最高产量已较原设计产量增加了五倍。工人们正把制成的漂白粉从轻便铁道上运出去。
