Author:
Date: 1953-08-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The municipal construction project underway in Yining City, Xinjiang Province, will make Yining City even more beautiful. This Iskhak Bek Club was built to commemorate the revolutionary hero Iskhak Bek. Iskhak Bek was one of the outstanding military leaders during the Three Districts Revolution in Xinjiang, and he made great contributions to uniting the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang against reactionary rule. Now, people of all ethnic groups in Yining City come here to play on holidays.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省伊宁市正在进行的市政建设工程，将使伊宁市变得更加美丽。这座依斯哈克拜克俱乐部是为了纪念革命英雄依斯哈克拜克而修建的。依斯哈克拜克是新疆三区革命时的杰出军事领袖之一，他对团结新疆各族人民反对反动统治的斗争有很大的贡献。现在伊宁市的各族人民每逢假日都到这里来游玩。
