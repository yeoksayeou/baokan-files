Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Cooperation Agreement on Preventing Crop Pests and Diseases
Author:
Date: 1953-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th
China and Czechoslovakia Sign Cooperation Agreement on Preventing Crop Pests and Diseases

[Xinhua News Agency, August 18th] The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic signed a Cooperation Agreement on Preventing Crop Pests and Diseases in Beijing on August 18th. The agreement stipulates that both parties will cooperate in preventing the spread of dangerous pests and diseases of crops and in measures to protect crops.

Representing the Chinese government in signing the agreement was Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Representing the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic was Mr. Kang Sala, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Zhang Linchi, Vice Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government; Zhang Jianping, Deputy Director of the General Office; Li Shijun, Director of the Plant Protection Department; Wen Ning, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Liao Tiren, Deputy Director of the General Bureau of Commodity Inspection of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; and plant pathology and quarantine experts Dai Fanglan and Zhang Ruozhuo. On the Czechoslovak side were Mr. Shimi Ci, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Republic Embassy in China; Mr. Sha Er, Commercial Counselor; and Mr. Handel, Second Secretary.

### 中捷签订预防农作物虫害与病害合作协定

1953-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷签订预防农作物虫害与病害合作协定
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府与捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府于八月十八日在北京签订了预防农作物虫害与病害合作协定。该协定规定双方在防止农作物危险病虫害传播和保护农作物的措施方面进行合作。
    代表中国政府在这个协定上签字的是中华人民共和国中央人民政府农业部部长李书城，代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府签字的是捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华特命全权大使康萨拉。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中央人民政府农业部副部长张林池、办公厅副主任张建平、植物保护司司长李世俊，外交部苏联东欧司副司长温宁，对外贸易部商品检验总局副局长廖体仁及植物病理和检疫专家戴芳澜、张若蓍等人。捷克斯洛伐克方面有捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆参赞石密茨、商务参赞沙尔及二等秘书汉德尔。
