Title: Commemorating the Immortal Achievements of the Soviet Army in Liberating Korea: Korean Cabinet Directs Reconstruction of the "August 15th" Liberation Memorial Towers
Author:
Date: 1953-08-11
Page: 4

    Commemorating the Immortal Achievements of the Soviet Army in Liberating Korea
    Korean Cabinet Directs Reconstruction of the "August 15th" Liberation Memorial Towers
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th] According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency on the 9th: In order to forever commemorate the immortal achievements of the great Soviet Army – the liberators of the Korean people – the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has issued a decision instructing all regions to rebuild the "August 15th" Liberation Memorial Towers. These Liberation Memorial Towers were almost entirely destroyed by the American invading army during the war.
    The Cabinet decision stipulates that the State Construction Committee and the People's Committees of each province, city, and county should create design plans for the reconstruction of the "August 15th" Liberation Memorial Towers, and construction will begin after approval by the Cabinet.

### 纪念苏联军队解放朝鲜的不朽功勋  朝鲜内阁指示重建“八·一五”解放纪念塔

1953-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念苏联军队解放朝鲜的不朽功勋
    朝鲜内阁指示重建“八·一五”解放纪念塔
    【新华社平壤十日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社九日报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁为了永远纪念朝鲜人民的解放者——伟大的苏联军队不朽的功勋，特发布决定，指示各地重建“八·一五”解放纪念塔。这些解放纪念塔在战争期间几乎全遭美国侵略军破坏。
    内阁决定中规定由国家建设委员会和各道、市、郡人民委员会做出重建“八·一五”解放纪念塔的设计方案，在内阁批准后即开始施工。
