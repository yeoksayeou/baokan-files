Title: World Youth Festival Continues with Various Artistic Performances
Author:
Date: 1953-08-06
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 5th) The young artists from various countries participating in the Fourth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship held various performances on the 3rd. Soviet young artists gave their first performance at the New Opera House. Those who attended included Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, the Romanian party and government leaders headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania, and journalists. In the performance, the Moscow students' chorus, the Siberian folk song chorus, the performance of the Moscow Conservatory of Music student symphony orchestra, and solo performances were praised.

The young artists from China, France, and Japan held performances on the 3rd. The young people from France, Italy, Britain, Finland, Hungary, and other countries who attended warmly welcomed every program of the performances. Young artists from Austria and Indonesia also held performances on the 3rd.

The art performances held every night attracted a large number of spectators. The halls of the theaters in Bucharest could not accommodate them, and the open-air theaters were also crowded with thousands of spectators.

The International Friendship Sports Games of the Festival opened on the 4th at the Bucharest Stadium. After the athletes participating in the competition paraded in the stadium, M. Nixon, Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, delivered a speech, expressing his belief that this sports competition would strengthen the relationship, friendship, and trust among young people from various countries. Romanian athletes then performed bicycle and equestrian performances.

Young people from various countries continue to hold get-togethers. Youth from New Zealand and Malaya, youth from Switzerland and South Africa, and youth from Korea and Latin American countries held gatherings in a friendly atmosphere.

### 世界青年联欢节继续进行各种艺术表演

1953-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青年联欢节继续进行各种艺术表演
    【新华社五日讯】布加勒斯特消息：参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的各国青年艺术家们，三日举行各种表演。苏联青年艺术家们在新歌剧院第一次演出。前往观看的有罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席彼得鲁·格罗查、以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚的党和政府的领袖、各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节和新闻记者等。在演出中，莫斯科学生的合唱、西伯利亚民歌合唱、莫斯科音乐学校学生交响乐队的演奏、独唱都受到赞扬。
    中国、法国和日本的青年艺术家们在三日举行表演，前往观看的法国、意大利、英国、芬兰、匈牙利等国青年热烈欢迎演出的每一个节目。奥地利和印度尼西亚的青年艺术家在三日也举行了表演。
    每天晚上举行的艺术表演，吸引了大量观众。布加勒斯特各剧场的大厅容纳不下了，在露天剧场的四周也挤满了成千的观众。
    联欢节的国际友谊体育比赛四日在布加勒斯特体育场开幕。参加比赛的运动员在场内游行后，世界民主青年联盟书记马·尼克松发表演说，他表示相信这次体育比赛将加强各国青年间的关系、友谊和信任。罗马尼亚选手接着举行了自行车和马术表演。
    各国青年继续举行联欢活动。新西兰和马来亚青年、瑞士和南非青年、朝鲜和拉丁美洲国家青年间，在友好的气氛中举行了联欢会。
