    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's state-owned Shanghai Precision Medical Equipment Factory has successfully trial-produced and begun producing important medical equipment – large X-ray machines. This is the newly manufactured large X-ray machine from the factory. Technicians are calibrating it.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国国营上海精密医疗器械厂试制重要的医疗器械——大型爱克斯光机已经成功，并且开始生产了。这是该厂新造的大型爱克斯光机。制配工人正在校验。
