Title: Neutral Nations Inspection Teams Stationed at Our Rear Ports Begin Work
Author:
Date: 1953-08-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 21st: The advance personnel of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams stationed at our side's five designated rear ports have begun inspection work.
    Members of the Neutral Nations Inspection Team stationed in Sinuiju, accompanied by Korean-Chinese liaison officers, inspected the perimeter of the port on the 20th and met with the airport director and the railway stationmaster. Members of the Neutral Nations Inspection Team stationed in Hungnam began inspecting the perimeter of the port on the 19th, and inspected the airport, port, and railway station on the 20th. Members of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams stationed in Chongjin, Manpo, and Sinanju also conducted similar inspections on the 19th and 20th. During the inspections, local officials comprehensively answered the various questions raised by the team members.
    The purpose of these inspections by the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams is mainly to understand the scope of their respective inspection areas and the relevant situation of the inspection sites.
    After the first inspection, except for the team stationed in Chongjin, which has not yet submitted a report, the advance personnel of the teams stationed in Sinuiju, Hungnam, Manpo, and Sinanju expressed satisfaction with the results of the inspection in their reports to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, and stated that the advance personnel of each team have established close cooperative relations with the Korean-Chinese liaison officers.

### 驻我方后方口岸的中立国视察小组开始工作

1953-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    驻我方后方口岸的中立国视察小组开始工作
    【新华社开城二十一日电】到达我方五个特定后方口岸的中立国视察小组先遣人员已开始进行视察工作。
    驻新义州的中立国视察小组的组员在朝中联络军官陪同下，于二十日视察了该口岸的周界，并会见了该地飞机场场长和火车站站长；驻兴南的中立国视察小组的组员于十九日开始视察了该口岸周界，二十日视察了飞机场、港口和火车站。驻清津、满浦和新安州的中立国视察小组的组员也都先后在十九日和二十日作了同样的视察。在视察时，当地负责人员详尽地答复了组员们所询问的各项有关的问题。
    各中立国视察小组这次视察的目的，主要是了解他们各自负责的视察区域范围及视察地点的有关情况。
    第一次视察结束以后，除驻清津的小组尚未有报告外，驻新义州、兴南、满浦和新安州的小组先遣人员都在给中立国监察委员会的报告中，表示对视察的结果感到满意，并称各小组的先遣人员已与朝中联络军官建立了密切合作的关系。
