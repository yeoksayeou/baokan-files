Title: August 23rd Communiqué on the Repatriation of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-08-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 23rd: On August 23rd, our side repatriated 150 non-Korean prisoners of war (all of whom were American) and 250 Korean prisoners of war to the other side. The total number of prisoners of war repatriated by our side to the other side was 400.
    Today, the other side repatriated 2,398 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side.
    Among the returning captured personnel of the Korean People's Army today, there were three new wounded individuals. They were stabbed with bayonets by the US military yesterday afternoon at the Munsan train station while singing patriotic songs. As soon as these three wounded returnees arrived in our side's handover area, they went into the joint Red Cross team tent to accuse the US military of its atrocities.

### 八月二十三日遣返战俘公报

1953-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    八月二十三日遣返战俘公报
    【新华社开城二十三日电】二十三日，我方遣返给对方非朝鲜籍战俘一百五十名（全系美国籍），朝鲜籍战俘二百五十名。我方遣返给对方的战俘总数是四百名。
    今天对方遣返给我方朝鲜人民军被俘人员两千三百九十八名。
    今天归来的朝鲜人民军被俘人员中又有三名新的受伤者。他们是昨天下午在汶山车站唱爱国歌曲时被美军用刺刀刺伤的。这三名受伤的归来人员刚到达我方交接区，便走进联合红十字会小组帐篷，控诉美军的暴行。
