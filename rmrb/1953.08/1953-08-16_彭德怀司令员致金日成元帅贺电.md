Title: Commander Peng Dehuai's Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1953-08-16
Page: 1

    Commander Peng Dehuai's Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Kim Il-sung
    To Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army:
    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, I extend warm congratulations to the Korean People's Army and to you.
    I wish the heroic Korean People's Army even greater strength and great achievements in the cause of preventing the recurrence of wars of aggression and striving for a peaceful, unified, and independent new Korea.
    Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Peng Dehuai
    August 15, 1953

### 彭德怀司令员致金日成元帅贺电

1953-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀司令员致金日成元帅贺电朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅：
    值此朝鲜解放八周年之际，谨向朝鲜人民军和您致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝英雄的朝鲜人民军，在防止侵略战争再起和争取和平、统一、独立的新朝鲜的事业中，将更加强大和获得伟大的成就。
    中国人民志愿军司令员  彭德怀                                                            
    一九五三年八月十五日
