Title: Exchange of Materials on Prisoner of War Deaths Between Our Side and the US Side
Author:
Date: 1953-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 24th -- The Korean-Chinese side has, in accordance with the provisions of the armistice agreement, submitted to the United Nations Command all materials and cemetery materials that it could provide within the limits of practical possibility regarding the deceased prisoners of war from the United Nations forces. According to these materials, the number of deceased prisoners of war from the United Nations forces, including those killed by United Nations aircraft bombings, totaled 1,651, including 601 of Korean nationality and 1,050 of non-Korean nationality.

The United Nations Command has also submitted death lists of prisoners of war from the Korean-Chinese side on three separate occasions, totaling 8,463 people, including 7,981 prisoners of war from the Korean People's Army and 482 prisoners of war from the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 我方和美方交换关于战俘死亡材料

1953-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我方和美方交换关于战俘死亡材料
    【新华社开城二十四日电】朝中方面已按照停战协定的规定，将它在实际可能办到的范围内所能提供的关于联合国军死亡被俘人员的全部材料及墓地材料，提交给联合国军方面。根据这些材料，联合国军被俘人员死亡者，包括为联合国军飞机所炸死者在内，共一千六百五十一人，其中朝鲜籍六百零一人，非朝鲜籍一千零五十人。
    联合国军方面也曾先后三次提出朝中被俘人员的死亡名单，共八千四百六十三人，其中朝鲜人民军被俘人员七千九百八十一人，中国人民志愿军被俘人员四百八十二人。
