Title: Tibetan Delegation Sends Letter to the Central People's Government's Nationalities Affairs Commission Thanking the Central People's Government and Fraternal Ethnic Groups for their Concern

Author: 
Date: 1953-08-29
Page: 3

    The Tibetan Delegation, led by Liu Xia Tudeng Taba, Deputy Leaders Danba Riji, Dingjia Duoji Jicun, and all members of the delegation, after concluding their report-delivery activities, wrote a letter on August 18th to the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, thanking the Central People's Government and leaders of various regions, as well as the people of all fraternal ethnic groups, for their concern and love. The letter stated: "All members of the Tibetan Delegation have now victoriously returned to Lhasa and Xigaze, and have conveyed in detail to the Tibetan local government and people of all circles, both monks and laypersons, the immense care and concern shown to us by the Central People's Government during our time in the motherland, the instructions for future work in the Tibetan region, and the great achievements in all aspects of construction witnessed during our three years of visiting various parts of the motherland. All sectors expressed great joy after listening to our report. Thus, we have victoriously completed our major task." They further stated in the letter: "Amidst boundless joy, we deeply remember the warm reception and assistance from the people of all fraternal ethnic groups and the leaders of various regions, and extend to them our sincere gratitude."
    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 西藏致敬团写信给中央人民政府民族事务委员会  感谢中央人民政府和各兄弟民族的关怀

1953-08-29
第3版()
专栏：

    西藏致敬团写信给中央人民政府民族事务委员会
    感谢中央人民政府和各兄弟民族的关怀
    西藏致敬团团长柳霞·土登塔巴，副团长丹巴日杰、定甲·多吉吉村和该团的全体团员，在结束他们的传达报告活动之后，在八月十八日写信给中央人民政府民族事务委员会，感谢中央人民政府和各地区首长以及各兄弟民族人民对他们的关怀和热爱。信中说：西藏致敬团全体团员，现已胜利返回拉萨、日喀则，并将我们在祖国内地期间，中央人民政府对我们的无比爱护和关怀，对西藏地区今后工作的指示，以及到各地参观祖国三年来各方面建设的伟大成就等情况，都向西藏地方政府和各界僧俗人民作了详细的传达报告，各方面在听取了我们的报告之后，都报以极大的欢欣。这样，就胜利地完成了我们的重大任务。他们在信中又说：我们在无限的快慰之中，深深地记着各兄弟民族人民和各个地区首长对我们的热烈招待和帮助，并对他们致以至诚的感谢。
    （新华社）
