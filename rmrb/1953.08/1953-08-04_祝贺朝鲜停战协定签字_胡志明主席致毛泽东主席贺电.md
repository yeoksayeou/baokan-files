Title: Congratulations on the Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author: Hu Zhiming
Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    Upon learning the news of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement and the end of the Korean War, the people of Vietnam feel boundless joy.
    This is a great victory for the Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers. It is also a great victory for the world peace and democracy camp.
    On behalf of the Vietnamese people and the Vietnamese government, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese people on the glorious victory they have achieved in the cause of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, defending China, and safeguarding world peace.
    Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, August 1, 1953

### 祝贺朝鲜停战协定签字  胡志明主席致毛泽东主席贺电
胡志明
1953-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺朝鲜停战协定签字
    胡志明主席致毛泽东主席贺电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    欣悉朝鲜停战协定签字和朝鲜战争结束的消息，越南人民感到无限的欢欣。
    这是朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军的伟大胜利。同时也是世界和平民主阵营的伟大胜利。
    我谨代表越南人民和越南政府，衷心祝贺中国人民在抗美援朝、保卫中国和保卫世界和平的事业中所获得的辉煌胜利。
    越南民主共和国政府主席  胡志明  一九五三年八月一日
