Title: World Youth Festival International Friendship Sports Games Continue, Results of Finals in Some Track and Field and Swimming Events Announced, Chinese Athlete Wu Chuanyu Wins First Place in Men's 100m Backstroke
Author:
Date: 1953-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 10th: In the men's 100m backstroke event at the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival sports competition, the final was held at 4:00 PM on the 9th. Chinese athlete Wu Chuanyu won first place with a time of 1 minute 8.4 seconds. Hungary won second place, and Poland won third place. When the results were announced, the entire audience stood up and cheered, and the referee personally hung the gold medal around Wu Chuanyu's neck. At this time, the band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China, and the national flags of China, Hungary, and Poland were raised to the top of the flagpoles amidst the majestic singing.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Bucharest Dispatch: The international friendship sports competition of the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival continues intensely. The results of the finals of some track and field and swimming events are as follows:

Men's Javelin: First place: Kuznetsov (Soviet Union), score: 74.76 meters. Second place: Tublenko (Soviet Union). Third place: Drillingwink (Poland).

Men's 400m: First place: Ignatiev (Soviet Union), score: 46.8 seconds. Second place: Filjo (Czechoslovakia). Third place: Pilagas (Soviet Union).

Men's 400m Relay: First place: German Democratic Republic team, score: 46.5 seconds.

Women's 400m: First place: Solopova (Soviet Union), score: 56.3 seconds. Second place: Pletneva (Soviet Union). Third place: Jurewitz (German Democratic Republic).

Women's 80m Hurdles: First place: Golubnichaya (Soviet Union), score: 11.2 seconds. Second place: Giamats (Hungary). Third place: Lubinchevya (Soviet Union).

Men's 200m: First place: Janiczek (Czechoslovakia), score: 21 seconds. Second place: Ignatiev (Soviet Union). Third place: Schroeder (German Democratic Republic).

Men's Long Jump: First place: Vrdolski (Hungary), score: 7.63 meters. Second place: Leskiewicz (Soviet Union). Third place: Ilinfeld (German Democratic Republic).

Women's 800m: First place: Otkalenko (Soviet Union), score: 2 minutes 10.5 seconds. Second place: Chernoschik (Soviet Union). Third place: Hazi (Hungary).

Women's 200m: First place: Shirlinger (German Democratic Republic), score: 23.9 seconds. Second place: Henigina (Soviet Union). Third place: Kagel (German Democratic Republic).

Men's Discus: First place: Klitz (Hungary), score: 49.27 meters. Second place: Krivonosov (Soviet Union). Third place: Mirta (Czechoslovakia).

Men's 50,000m Walk: First place: Roda (Hungary), score: 4 hours 26 minutes 18.6 seconds. Second place: Baboi (Romania). Third place: Iamish (Soviet Union).

Swimming Competition:

Women's 100m Butterfly: First place: Skle (Hungary), score: 1 minute 18.1 seconds. Second place: Litomeritzky (Hungary).

Men's 100m Breaststroke: First place: Minachin (Soviet Union), score: 1 minute 13.8 seconds. Second place: Sobav (Soviet Union).

Women's 100m Freestyle: First place: Valeria Kingi, score: 1 minute 6.5 seconds. Second place: Initim (Hungary).

Men's 400m Relay: First place: Hungary team, score: 4 minutes 27 seconds. Second place: Soviet Union team.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Bucharest Dispatch: The Chinese delegation participating in the sports competition of the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival continues to hold basketball and volleyball matches with other national teams. In the men's volleyball preliminary round, on August 7, China vs. Denmark, the result was 3-0, China won. On the 9th, China vs. Iran, the result was 3-0, China won. In the women's basketball preliminary round, China vs. Bulgaria, the result was 42-85, Bulgaria won.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 10th: The recent competition results of the Chinese sports delegation participating in the sports competition of the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival are as follows: Men's basketball, the Chinese team has played three games. On the 8th, China won both games against Lebanon and Germany. On the 9th, China vs. North Korea, North Korea won. Women's basketball, China vs. Bulgaria, Bulgaria won; China vs. the Soviet Union, the Soviet Union won. Men's volleyball, China played three games, China won against Denmark and Iran, and lost to the Soviet Union. Women's volleyball, China vs. Poland, Poland won; China vs. Austria, China won.

### 世界青年联欢节国际友谊体育比赛继续进行  田径赛和游泳比赛一部分项目决赛结果公布  我国运动员吴传玉获得男子一百公尺仰泳第一名

1953-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青年联欢节国际友谊体育比赛继续进行
    田径赛和游泳比赛一部分项目决赛结果公布
    我国运动员吴传玉获得男子一百公尺仰泳第一名
    【新华社布加勒斯特十日电】第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节体育比赛中的男子一百公尺仰泳，于九日下午四时决赛。中国运动员吴传玉以一分八秒四的成绩获得第一名，匈牙利获得第二名，波兰获得第三名。当宣布决赛结果时，全场观众起立欢呼，裁判员将金质奖章亲自挂在吴传玉的胸前。这时，乐队奏起了中华人民共和国国歌，中国、匈牙利、波兰三面国旗在雄壮的歌声中升到旗竿顶上。
