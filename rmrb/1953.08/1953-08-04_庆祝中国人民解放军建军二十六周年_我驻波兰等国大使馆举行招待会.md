Title: Receptions Held by Chinese Embassies in Poland and Other Countries to Celebrate the 26th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    To celebrate the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the embassies of our country in Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, India, and other countries held grand receptions on August 1st.

    Attending the reception held by the Chinese Embassy in Poland were approximately 200 people, including Polish government and military leaders, heads of various people's organizations, and envoys and military attachés from the Soviet Union and other countries. Polish Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, Dworakowski, Gede, and Nowak, Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions Klosiewicz, and Soviet Ambassador to Poland Popov all attended the reception. After the banquet, the Chinese color documentary "'August 1st' Sports Meet" was screened.

    Senior Colonel Yang Yazhong, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, held a reception on the evening of the 1st. Among the more than 200 guests invited to the meeting were: Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký, Deputy Premier Jaromír Ďuranský, Deputy Premier and Minister of National Defense General Alexej Čepička, and Deputy Premier Antonín Novotný. Also present were Soviet military attaché to Czechoslovakia Lieutenant General Gusev and other foreign diplomatic personnel and military attachés stationed in Czechoslovakia. The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. After the meeting, the Czechoslovak color documentary of the "Vít Nejedlý Army Art Ensemble's" performance in China last year was screened. To celebrate the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the "Vít Nejedlý Army Art Ensemble" performed at the "Winter Sports Stadium" on July 31st.

    The reception at the Chinese Embassy in Romania was hosted by Ambassador Wang Youping and Military Attaché Fu Donghua. Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, and others attended the reception. The reception was held in a warm atmosphere. Youth representatives from various countries participating in the Third World Youth Congress and the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival were also invited to attend. Members of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe participating in the festival performed wonderful programs at the meeting.

    Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Hungary, held a reception at the Park Club in Budapest on the evening of the 1st. Among the 250 guests invited to the reception were: Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party Mátyás Rákosi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Republic István Dobi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Imre Nagy, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Interior Ernő Gerő, and Minister of National Defense Lieutenant General István Bata. Also invited to the reception were generals of the Hungarian People's Army, representatives of people's organizations, outstanding figures in culture and production, and diplomatic personnel and military attachés from other countries stationed in Budapest. The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. After the meeting, the Chinese color documentary "'August 1st' Sports Meet" was screened.

    Attending the reception held by the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria were approximately 150 people, including Vice-Chairmen of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers Damyanov, Yugov, Traikov, and Lukanov, and Minister of National Defense General Panchevski.

    Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India, held a banquet on the evening of the 1st. Invited to attend were Indian Deputy Minister of Defense Sardar Surjit Singh Majithia and Indian army, navy, and air force generals and senior officers, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs R.K. Nehru, and Chief Minister of Delhi State Chaudhry Brahm Prakash. President of the All-India Peace Council Kitchlu, Vice-President Gyan Chand, and Secretary-General of the Indian Red Cross Society Balwant Singh Puri were also invited to attend. Envoys to India from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and other countries that have diplomatic relations with our country were also invited to the banquet. Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian also attended the banquet.

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年  我驻波兰等国大使馆举行招待会

1953-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年
    我驻波兰等国大使馆举行招待会
    据新华社讯：为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年，我国驻波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、保加利亚、印度等国的大使馆，在八月一日，分别举行了盛大的招待会。
    出席我国驻波兰大使馆招待会的有波兰政府、军队首长，各人民团体负责人和苏联等国驻波使节、武官等共约二百人。波兰部长会议副主席西伦凯维兹、德沃拉科夫斯基、盖德、诺瓦克，波兰工会中央理事会主席克沃谢维奇和苏联驻波兰大使波波夫都出席了招待会。宴会后并放映了中国五彩记录片
    “‘八一’运动大会”。
    我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆武官杨亚中上校于一日晚举行招待会。应邀到会的二百多位来宾中有：捷克斯洛伐克总理威廉·西罗基，副总理雅罗米尔·杜兰斯基，副总理兼国防部部长阿列克谢·契皮茨卡上将和副总理安托宁·诺沃提尼。到会的还有苏联驻捷大使馆武官古谢夫中将和其他各国驻捷外交人员和武官。招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。会后放映了捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”去年在中国演出的五彩记录片。为庆祝中国人民解放军建军节，“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”曾于七月三十一日在“冬季运动场”演出。
    我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆招待会由王幼平大使和傅东华武官主持。罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席格罗查，部长会议主席乔治乌—德治等都出席了招待会。招待会在热烈的气氛中进行。参加第三届世界青年代表大会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的各国青年代表也应邀出席。参加联欢节的中国青年艺术团团员并在会上表演精采节目。
    我国驻匈牙利大使馆武官张秉玉上校于一日晚在布达佩斯的公园俱乐部举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的二百五十位来宾中有：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会书记马加斯·拉科西，共和国主席团主席伊斯特万·道比，部长会议主席伊姆雷·纳吉，部长会议第一副主席兼内务部部长埃诺·格罗，国防部部长伊斯特万·巴塔中将。应邀出席招待会的还有匈牙利人民军将领、人民团体代表、文化和生产方面的杰出人物以及其他国家驻布达佩斯外交人员和武官。招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。会后放映了中国五彩记录片“‘八一’运动大会”。
    出席我国驻保加利亚大使馆招待会的有保加利亚部长会议副主席达米扬诺夫、于哥夫、特莱科夫、卢卡诺夫，国防部部长潘切夫斯基上将等约一百五十人。
    我国驻印度大使馆武官朱开印上校在一日晚举行宴会。应邀出席的有印度国防部副部长萨达尔·苏吉特·辛格·马吉蒂亚和印度陆海空军将领及高级军官，印度外交部外事秘书鲁·克·尼赫鲁，德里邦首席部长乔度里·德拉姆·布加拉希。全印和平理事会主席克其鲁、副主席吉安·昌德，印度红十字会秘书长巴尔万特·辛格·普里等人也应邀出席。应邀出席宴会的还有苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利及其他与我国有外交关系的国家驻印度的使节。我国驻印度大使袁仲贤也出席了宴会。
