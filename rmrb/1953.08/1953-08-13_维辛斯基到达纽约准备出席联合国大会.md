Title: Vyshinsky Arrives in New York to Attend UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 12th: TASS, New York, August 12th: Andrei Yanuarevich Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union and Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations, arrived in New York on August 11th to prepare for the Third Session of the Seventh United Nations General Assembly, which will convene on August 17th due to the conclusion of the Korean Armistice Agreement.
    The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Ukrainian Republic and head of the Ukrainian Republic delegation to the UN General Assembly, A.M. Baranovky, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Byelorussian Republic and head of the Byelorussian Republic delegation, K.V. Kiselev, arrived in New York on the same day.

### 维辛斯基到达纽约准备出席联合国大会

1953-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基到达纽约准备出席联合国大会
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社纽约十二日讯：苏联外交部第一副部长、苏联驻联合国常任代表安·扬·维辛斯基于八月十一日抵纽约，准备出席由于朝鲜停战协定缔结而将在八月十七日召开的联合国第七届大会第三次会议。
    乌克兰共和国外交部长、乌克兰共和国出席联合国大会代表团团长安·麦·巴拉诺夫斯基及白俄罗斯共和国外交部长、白俄罗斯共和国代表团团长库·维·基谢列夫同日抵达纽约。
