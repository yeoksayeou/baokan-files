Title: British Government Sends New Negotiator to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-08-25
Page: 1
　
　　British Government Sends New Negotiator to Beijing
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 23rd] Mr. Lan Lainier, the British government's negotiator dispatched to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations, was recalled and left Beijing for his country on June 19th. Mr. Du Weilian, the new negotiator dispatched by the British government, arrived in Beijing on the 23rd of this month. Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet him.

### 英国新派遣谈判代表到北京

1953-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    英国新派遣谈判代表到北京
    【新华社二十三日讯】英国政府为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判而派遣之谈判代表蓝来讷先生，已奉调于六月十九日离京返国。英国政府新派遣之谈判代表杜维廉先生，已于本月二十三日抵京。我外交部交际处副处长沈平曾赴站迎接。
