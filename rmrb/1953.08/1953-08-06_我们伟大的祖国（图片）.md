Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-08-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to cultivate outstanding worker-peasant cadres and advanced industrial workers, and to make them new-type intellectuals and talents for building a new China, the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government have established many worker-peasant rapid middle schools. This is Wang Jingbin, Tian Guiying, Bi Yulan, and Zou Guilan, who are studying at the worker-peasant rapid middle school attached to the Northeast Institute of Technology. They are taking a walk on campus after class.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国共产党和人民政府为了培养优秀的工农干部和先进的产业工人，使他们成为新型的知识分子，成为建设新中国的人材，创办了许多工农速成中学。这是在东北工学院附设的工农速成中学学习的王静彬、田桂英、毕玉兰和邹桂兰。她们在下课后正在校园内散步。
