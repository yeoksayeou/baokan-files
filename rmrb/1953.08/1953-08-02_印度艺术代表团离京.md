Title: Indian Art Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st - The Indian Art Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Beijing on the 1st to continue performances and visits in Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Liu Guanyi; Deputy Secretary-General Wu Maosun; Director of the Organization Department Xu Baoju; Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng; and Chinese literary and artistic figures Zheng Zhenduo, Hong Shen, Zhu Ming, Ye Sheng Lan, Yuan Shihai, Du Jinfang, as well as Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Kameta Togo, Hui Tun, and Van To Le Li. All members of the delegation accepted flowers presented by students from the Beijing Opera Experimental School who came to the station to see them off.

During their stay in Beijing, the Indian Art Delegation held several music and dance performances and visited the Palace Museum, the Temple of Heaven, Beihai Park, the Summer Palace, as well as schools, mosques, rural areas, and the Guanting Reservoir. They also watched Peking Opera, Chinese films, song and dance performances, and acrobatics. The delegation's performances and visits in Beijing were warmly welcomed.

### 印度艺术代表团离京

1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    印度艺术代表团离京
    【新华社一日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请前来我国的印度艺术代表团，在一日离开北京去天津、南京、上海、杭州和广州等地继续进行演出和访问。
    到车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘宁一、刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪、组织部长许宝驹，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙和我国文学艺术界人士郑振铎、洪深、朱明、叶盛兰、袁世海、杜近芳，以及亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、惠吞、万徒勒里。代表团全体团员接受了到车站欢送的北京戏曲实验学校学生们献花。
    印度艺术代表团在北京期间曾举行多次音乐舞蹈表演，并参观了故宫、天坛、北海、颐和园以及学校、清真寺、农村和官厅水库，观看了京剧、中国电影、歌舞和杂技。代表团在北京的演出和访问受到热烈欢迎。
