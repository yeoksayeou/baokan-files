Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling Receives Indian Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 12th: Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, who is currently in Shanghai, watched the first performance in Shanghai by the Indian Art Delegation, which is visiting Shanghai, on the evening of the 11th. Vice Chairman Soong also received Sachin Sengupta, head of the Indian Art Delegation, and Valahtol Narayana Menon, a member of the Indian Art Delegation and a famous Indian poet, on the afternoon of the 12th.

### 宋庆龄副主席接见印度艺术代表团

1953-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副主席接见印度艺术代表团
    【新华社上海十二日电】现在在上海的中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄，在十一日晚间观看了正在上海进行访问的印度艺术代表团在上海的首次演出。宋副主席并在十二日下午接见了印度艺术代表团团长萨钦·森古普塔和印度艺术代表团团员、印度著名诗人瓦拉托尔·纳拉雅纳·梅农。
