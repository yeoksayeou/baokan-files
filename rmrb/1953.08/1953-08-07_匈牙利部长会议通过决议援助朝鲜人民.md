Title: Hungarian Council of Ministers Passes Resolution to Aid the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1953-08-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 5th: TASS reports that the Hungarian People's Republic Council of Ministers has passed a resolution regarding aid for the reconstruction of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The resolution states that today, as the Korean people face the task of restoring their ravaged homeland's economy, the Hungarian People's Republic Council of Ministers once again expresses the solidarity of the entire Hungarian people with the Korean people and has decided to provide brotherly assistance to the Korean people.
    This year, Hungary will supply the Democratic People's Republic of Korea with industrial equipment and consumer goods, and will provide technical assistance in rebuilding destroyed cities and villages, as well as restoring factories.

### 匈牙利部长会议通过决议援助朝鲜人民

1953-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利部长会议通过决议援助朝鲜人民
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯五日讯：匈牙利人民共和国部长会议通过一项关于援助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国复兴的决议。决议说，今天当朝鲜人民面临恢复他们被蹂躏的祖国的经济的任务时，匈牙利人民共和国部长会议再一次表示匈牙利全体人民对朝鲜人民的声援，决定以兄弟般的援助给予朝鲜人民。
    今年匈牙利将以工业设备和消费品供应给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国，并将在重建被摧毁的城市和乡村以及恢复工厂方面提供技术援助。
