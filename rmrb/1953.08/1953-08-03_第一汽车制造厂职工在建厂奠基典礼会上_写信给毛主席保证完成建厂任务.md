Title: First Automobile Works Employees Write to Chairman Mao at Groundbreaking Ceremony, Guaranteeing Completion of Construction Task
Author:
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 1

    First Automobile Works Employees Write to Chairman Mao at Groundbreaking Ceremony, Guaranteeing Completion of Construction Task
    [This newspaper dispatch] The entire staff of the First Automobile Works, at the groundbreaking ceremony held on July 15th, wrote a letter to Chairman Mao, guaranteeing the completion of the construction task on schedule. The original letter is as follows:
    Respected Chairman Mao:
    We held the groundbreaking ceremony for the first automobile factory in New China with great excitement. The first automobile factory was decided to be built under your care and guidance. You have given us many specific instructions, requiring us to earnestly ensure project quality and complete the project plan on time, which has redoubled our determination and strength. All of our employees regard it as an unparalleled honor to be able to dedicate ourselves to the construction of the country's first automobile factory, and we sincerely guarantee to you: We will certainly act as masters of the country, develop initiative and creativity, continuously improve our political consciousness and technical level, learn from advanced Soviet experience, and implement the suggestions of Soviet experts, ensuring that the project is done "well, quickly, economically, and safely." Under the care of the Party and you, with the enthusiastic and selfless assistance of the great friendly country, the Soviet Union, and under the direct leadership of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government, we will certainly be able to overcome difficulties and successfully complete the construction task on schedule, paving the way for the automobile industry of New China, in order to accelerate the industrialization of the motherland.
    We sincerely wish you good health and extend our highest revolutionary salute.
    The entire staff of the First Automobile Works
    July 15, 1953

### 第一汽车制造厂职工在建厂奠基典礼会上  写信给毛主席保证完成建厂任务

1953-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    第一汽车制造厂职工在建厂奠基典礼会上
    写信给毛主席保证完成建厂任务
    【本报讯】第一汽车制造厂全体职工，在七月十五日举行的建厂奠基典礼会上，曾写信给毛主席，保证按期完成建厂任务。原信如下：敬爱的毛主席：
    我们以万分兴奋的心情，为新中国第一座汽车制造厂举行奠基典礼。第一座汽车制造厂是在您关怀与指导之下决定建立的。您给了我们许多具体的指示，要求我们切实保证工程质量，按时完成工程计划，这更加百倍地增强了我们的决心和力量。我们全体职工都以能够献身国家第一座汽车制造厂的建设为无尚光荣，我们忠诚地向您保证：我们一定以国家主人翁的姿态，发扬积极性和创造性，不断提高政治觉悟与技术水平，学习苏联先进经验，贯彻执行苏联专家建议，一定把工程做到“好、快、省、安全”。在党和您的关怀下，在伟大的友邦苏联热情无私的援助下，在中央人民政府第一机械工业部的直接领导下，我们一定能够克服困难，完满地按期完成建厂任务，为新中国的汽车工业开辟道路，以加速祖国的工业化。谨祝您身体健康，并致崇高的革命敬礼
    第一汽车制造厂全体职工  一九五三年七月十五日
