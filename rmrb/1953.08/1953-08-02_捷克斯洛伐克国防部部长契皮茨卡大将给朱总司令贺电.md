Title: Czechoslovak Defense Minister General Čepička Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author: Čepička
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1

To the most beloved General Comrade:

On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of all personnel of the Czechoslovak Army, extend to you our heartfelt, comradely greetings and sincere congratulations. The Czechoslovak People's Army is proud to maintain a fighting friendship with the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Chinese People's Liberation Army, which has fraternal ties with the Soviet Army, is an unshakable fortress for the free and peaceful life of the Chinese people.

I wish you and all the personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army many further achievements in the cause of consolidating the national defense and national strength of the People's Republic of China.

General Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic

### 捷克斯洛伐克国防部部长契皮茨卡大将给朱总司令贺电
契皮茨卡
1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克国防部部长契皮茨卡大将给朱总司令贺电最敬爱的将军同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军节纪念，我谨代表捷克斯洛伐克军队全体人员向您致以衷心的、同志般的敬礼和真诚的祝贺。捷克斯洛伐克人民军以与中国人民解放军保持着战斗的友谊而引为自豪，与苏联军队有着兄弟般联系的中国人民解放军是中国人民的自由与和平生活的不可动摇的堡垒。
    谨祝您及中国人民解放军全体人员在巩固中华人民共和国国防力量与国家实力的事业中获得许多更进一步的成就。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡大将
