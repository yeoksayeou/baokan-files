Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Polish Member Political Advisor Kowalczyk Passes Through Beijing to Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 1

    Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Polish Member Political Advisor
    Kowalczyk Passes Through Beijing to Korea
    【Xinhua News Agency, 3rd】 Józef Kowalczyk, Plenipotentiary Minister and political advisor to the Polish member of the Korean Armistice Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, has arrived in Beijing and has departed for Korea.

### 中立国监察委员会波委员政治顾问  考瓦尔切克过北京去朝鲜

1953-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会波委员政治顾问
    考瓦尔切克过北京去朝鲜
    【新华社三日讯】朝鲜停战中立国监察委员会波兰委员的政治顾问全权公使约瑟夫·考瓦尔切克已经抵达北京，并已动身前往朝鲜。
