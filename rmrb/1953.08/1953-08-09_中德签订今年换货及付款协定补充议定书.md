Title: China and Germany Sign Supplementary Protocol to This Year's Barter and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The People's Republic of China Central People's Government and the German Democratic Republic government, based on the spirit of friendly mutual assistance between the two peoples, signed a supplementary protocol to the 1953 Exchange of Goods and Payment Agreement in Beijing on August 8th.

The protocol stipulates that the People's Republic of China will, within 1953, supplement the supply of goods worth over 50 million rubles to the German Democratic Republic, including furs, eggs, animal fats, canned meat, and other foodstuffs.

Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Chinese side. Mr. Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador, signed on behalf of the German side.

### 中德签订今年换货及付款协定补充议定书

1953-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中德签订今年换货及付款协定补充议定书
    【新华社八日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府与德意志民主共和国政府之间，根据两国人民的友好互助的精神，于八月八日在北京签订了一九五三年交换货物及付款协定的补充议定书。
    该议定书规定，中华人民共和国将于一九五三年以内，补充供应德意志民主共和国以价值五千余万卢布的皮毛、蛋品、动物脂肪、肉类罐头及其他食品等货物。
    代表我方签字者，为中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒。代表德方签字者，为德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希。
