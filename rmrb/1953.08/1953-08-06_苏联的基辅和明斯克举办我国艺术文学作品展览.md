Title: Soviet Union's Kiev and Minsk Host Exhibitions of Chinese Art and Literature
Author:
Date: 1953-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th: According to TASS news, some museums and libraries in Kiev and Minsk, Soviet Union, have recently held exhibitions introducing Chinese artworks and literary works.

    The National Museum of Western and Eastern Art in Kiev, the capital of the Ukrainian Republic, displayed many paintings representing ancient and modern Chinese art. The museum also held mobile exhibitions of Chinese artworks in cultural palaces, clubs, and cinemas of various enterprises in Kiev. These exhibitions were warmly welcomed by the working people of Kiev. Every day, hundreds of people visit the Chinese art exhibits at the homes of art workers and railway workers.

    In Minsk, the capital of the Belorussian Republic, a district library held an exhibition introducing Chinese literary works. The exhibition displayed works by Chinese writers that have been translated into Russian and Belarusian, as well as works by Soviet writers about New China. The library is also preparing a reader discussion meeting on modern Chinese literature.

### 苏联的基辅和明斯克举办我国艺术文学作品展览

1953-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联的基辅和明斯克举办我国艺术文学作品展览
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联的基辅和明斯克的一些博物馆和图书馆，最近举办了介绍中国艺术品和文学作品的展览会。
    乌克兰共和国首都基辅的国立西方和东方艺术博物馆，陈列了许多代表古代和现代中国艺术的图画。这个博物馆还在基辅各企业的文化宫、俱乐部和电影院内，举办了中国艺术品的流动展览会。这些展览会受到基辅劳动人民的热烈欢迎。每天都有几百人到艺术工作者和铁路工人之家参观中国艺术展览品。
    在白俄罗斯共和国首都明斯克，有一个区图书馆举办了一次介绍中国的文学作品的展览会。展览会上陈列着已经译成俄文和白俄罗斯文的中国作家的作品，和苏联作家的有关新中国的著作。这个图书馆还在筹办关于现代中国文学的读者讨论会。
