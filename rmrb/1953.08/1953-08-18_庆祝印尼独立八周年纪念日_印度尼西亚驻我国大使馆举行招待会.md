Title: Indonesian Embassy in Our Country Holds Reception to Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
Author:
Date: 1953-08-18
Page: 1
　
    To Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
    Indonesian Embassy in Our Country Holds Reception
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in our country, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 17th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of Indonesian independence.
    Those invited to attend included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Guo Moruo, Vice Premier, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and more than 200 people including heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
    Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries to China.
    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
    In addition: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of our country, and Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent telegrams to President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia and Minister of Foreign Affairs Sunario on the 15th, respectively, to congratulate them on the eighth anniversary of Indonesian independence.

### 庆祝印尼独立八周年纪念日  印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆举行招待会

1953-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝印尼独立八周年纪念日
    印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆举行招待会
    【新华社十七日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻我国大使馆代办伊沙·玛蒂于十七日晚在北京举行招待会，庆祝印度尼西亚独立八周年纪念日。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理郭沫若，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百余人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节及外交官。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长，在十五日分别致电印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和外交部部长苏纳约，祝贺印度尼西亚独立八周年纪念日。
