    Our Great Motherland
    The Fuchun River is located in the upper reaches of the Qiantang River in Zhejiang Province. Its beautiful scenery is famous throughout the country, and the two banks are rich in products. Now, ships transporting materials frequently travel on this river.

    我们伟大的祖国
    富春江在浙江省钱塘江的上游，山水秀丽驰名全国，两岸物产富饶。现在这条江上经常繁忙地行驶着运输物资的船只。
