Title: Celebrating the Ninth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation Romanian Ambassador Guo Zuowen Holds Reception Liu Shaoqi Attends as Well as Ambassadors to China from Various Countries

Author:

Date: 1953-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: To celebrate the ninth anniversary of the Soviet army's liberation of Romania, Romanian Ambassador to China Guo Zuowen held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of August 23rd.

Invited to attend were: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council; Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; responsible persons of various people's organizations; figures from the cultural and artistic circles; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and more than 500 others. Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend.

Ambassador Guo Zuowen and Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered speeches successively, mutually wishing the permanent friendship between the Romanian and Chinese people, greater achievements for the two peoples in national construction, and wishing the strength of the world peace and democracy camp to grow stronger day by day. Soviet Ambassador to China Kuznetsov also delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the reception, the guests and hosts frequently toasted to the friendship between the Romanian and Chinese people, to the health of the leaders of the two peoples, and to the health of the great Soviet Union and its leaders.

A dance was held after the meeting.

Also: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council sent telegrams separately to P. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, and Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, expressing their congratulations.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放九周年  罗马尼亚大使郭佐文举行招待会  刘少奇副主席及各国驻华使节应邀出席

1953-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚解放九周年
    罗马尼亚大使郭佐文举行招待会
    刘少奇副主席及各国驻华使节应邀出席
    【新华社二十三日讯】为庆祝苏联军队解放罗马尼亚九周年，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使郭佐文于二十三日下午在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：我中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院副总理邓小平，最高人民法院副院长张志让，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊，外交部副部长伍修权及中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人，文化艺术界人士，以及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等五百多人。应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    郭佐文大使和刘少奇副主席相继致词，互祝罗中两国人民的永久友谊，两国人民在国家建设中获得更大的成就，并祝世界和平民主阵营的力量日益强大。苏联驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫也在会上致词祝贺。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。席间，宾主频频为罗中两国人民的友谊、为两国人民的领袖的健康而干杯，为伟大的苏联及其领袖们的健康而干杯。
    会后并举行舞会。
    又讯：我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来曾分别致电罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查和罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，表示祝贺。
