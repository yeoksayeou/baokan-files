Title: China's Student Delegation Leaves Beijing for Warsaw to Attend the Third World Student Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Third World Student Congress will be held in Warsaw on August 27th. The Chinese student delegation attending the congress departed Beijing by train on August 15th.

The delegation includes thirty official representatives and one non-voting delegate. The representatives are student representatives and student workers from universities and specialized colleges in China, including student representatives from minority ethnic groups such as Tibetan, Mongolian, Uyghur, and Zhuang, as well as student representatives who are Christian and Catholic.

### 参加第三届世界学生代表大会  我国学生代表团离京赴华沙

1953-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    参加第三届世界学生代表大会
    我国学生代表团离京赴华沙
    据新华社讯：第三届世界学生代表大会将于八月二十七日在华沙举行。参加大会的中国学生代表团已在八月十五日乘火车离京。
    代表团包括正式代表三十人，列席代表一人。代表们是我国大学与专门学院的学生代表及学生工作者，其中有藏、蒙、维吾尔、僮等少数民族的学生代表，还有信仰基督教和天主教的学生代表。
