Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-08-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Many students in schools have achieved excellent results. They are happily spending their summer vacation. This Young Pioneer is a student at the primary school attached to the Beijing Machine Factory for children of employees. This semester, she received a score of five in her major subjects. Her grandfather and mother are very happy to be looking at her report card.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    许多学校的学生都获得了很好的成绩。他们正在愉快地度着暑假。这个少年先锋队员是北京机器厂附设职工子弟小学的学生。这学期她的主要功课都得了五分。她的祖父和母亲都非常高兴地在看她的成绩单。
