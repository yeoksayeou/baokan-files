Title: The Fifth Group of Repatriated Japanese Nationals Leaves Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th. The fifth group of 1,192 Japanese nationals concentrated in Tianjin who wished to return to their country departed from Tanggu New Port on the Japanese ship "Kouan Maru" at 10:00 AM on the 6th to return to Japan. Staff members from the Tianjin Branch of the Chinese Red Cross Society went to the dock to see them off.

### 第五批回国日侨离天津

1953-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    第五批回国日侨离天津
    【新华社天津七日电】集中在天津的第五批愿意回国的日本侨民一千一百九十二人，于六日上午十时乘日本轮船“兴安丸”离塘沽新港回日本，中国红十字会天津市分会工作人员曾前往码头欢送。
