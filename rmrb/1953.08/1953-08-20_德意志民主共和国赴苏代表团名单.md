Title: List of Delegation from the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1953-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 18th (TASS): The German News Agency published a press release from the Prime Minister's Office of the German Democratic Republic, as follows:

    The special meeting of the Council of Ministers on August 17, 1953, selected the delegation to go to Moscow at the invitation of the Soviet Government. The list is as follows:
    I. Otto Grotewohl – Prime Minister; II. Walter Ulbricht – Deputy Prime Minister; III. Otto Nuschke – Deputy Prime Minister; IV. Dr. Lothar Bolz – Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Construction; V. Dr. Hans Loch – Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Finance; VI. Professor Correns – Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany; VII. Bruno Leuschner – Chairman of the State Planning Commission; VIII. Herbert Warnke – Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation; IX. Erich Honecker – Chairman of the Central Council of the Free German Youth League; X. Hans Reichelt – Minister of Agriculture and Forestry; XI. Kurt Gregor – Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade. XII. Fritz Selbmann – Minister of Metallurgical and Mining Industries; XIII. Rudolf Appelt – Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic in Moscow; XIV. Ilse Thiele – Representative of the Democratic Women's League of Germany; XV. Peter Florin – Official of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    The secretary of the delegation is Ludwig Eisseman.
    Ferdinand Thun, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, accompanied the delegation.

### 德意志民主共和国赴苏代表团名单

1953-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国赴苏代表团名单
    【新华社讯】塔斯社柏林十八日电：德意志通讯社发表了德意志民主共和国总理新闻处的公报，内容如下：
    一九五三年八月十七日部长会议特别会议选出了应苏联政府之邀到莫斯科去的代表团，名单如下：
    一、奥托·格罗提渥——总理；二、瓦尔特·乌布利希——副总理；三、奥托·努舍克——副总理；四、洛塔·博尔茨博士——副总理兼建设部长；五、汉斯·洛赫博士——副总理兼财政部长；六、柯伦斯教授——民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席；七、布伦诺·劳施纳——国家计划委员会主席；八、赫伯特·瓦恩克——自由德国工会联合会主席；九、埃里希·昂纳克——自由德国青年联盟中央理事会主席；十、汉斯·赖希尔特——农林部长；十一、库尔特·格里哥尔——对外与对内贸易部长。十二、弗里茨·赛尔柏曼——冶金与采矿工业部部长；十三、鲁道夫·阿佩尔特——德意志民主共和国驻莫斯科外交使团团长；十四、伊尔泽·蒂勒——德国民主妇女联合会代表；十五、彼得·佛罗林——外交部官员。
    代表团秘书是卢德韦希·埃塞曼。
    随代表团同去的有德意志民主共和国外交部交际司司长斐迪南德·图恩。
