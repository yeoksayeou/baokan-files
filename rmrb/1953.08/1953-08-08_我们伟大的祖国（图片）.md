    Our Great Motherland
    The Duliujian River harnessing project in Hebei Province has been completed. It has completely eliminated the threat of floods to Tianjin, the first major industrial city in North China, and the Jinpu Railway, the main transportation line between the north and south of our country, and greatly reduced the water disasters in the lower reaches of the Daqing River. This is a corner of the Duliujian River Flood Diversion Sluice.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省独流灭河工程已经完工。它完全解除了华北第一个大工业城市——天津和我国南北主要交通干线津浦铁路的洪水威胁，并大大减轻了大清河下游的水患。这是独流灭河进洪闸的一角。
