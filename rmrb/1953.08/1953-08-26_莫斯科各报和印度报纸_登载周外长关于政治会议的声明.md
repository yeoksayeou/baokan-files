Title: Moscow Newspapers and Indian Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement on the Political Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-08-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 25th: Moscow newspapers on August 25th all published the statement by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, regarding the issue of the political conference.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 25th: Indian newspapers on August 25th all published on the front page the statement of August 24th by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, regarding the issue of the political conference.

### 莫斯科各报和印度报纸  登载周外长关于政治会议的声明

1953-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报和印度报纸
    登载周外长关于政治会议的声明
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】八月二十五日，莫斯科各报都刊载了中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来关于政治会议问题的声明。
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】二十五日，印度各报均在第一版登载我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来八月二十四日关于政治会议问题的声明。
