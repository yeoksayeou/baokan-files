    Our Great Motherland
    The technical exchange between various ethnic groups in our country has been widely developed with the needs of national construction. Eighty-one young people from the Uygur and Hui ethnic groups in faraway Xinjiang have come to the Suzhou Local State-Owned No. 1 Silk Factory to study silk reeling technology. They will take the technology they have learned to the newly built silk factory in Xinjiang. These Uyghur girls are learning silk reeling techniques on the reeling machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各族人民间的技术交流，随着国家建设的需要有了广泛的开展。从遥远的新疆来到苏州地方国营第一丝厂学习的新疆维吾尔族和回族青年八十一人，正在努力学习缫丝技术。他们要把学好的技术带到新疆新建的丝厂去。这几位维吾尔族的姑娘正在立缫车上学习缫丝技术。
