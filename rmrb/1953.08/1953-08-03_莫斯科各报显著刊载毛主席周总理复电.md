Title: Moscow Newspapers Prominently Publish Mao Zedong and Zhou Enlai's Reply Telegram
Author:
Date: 1953-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd - Moscow newspapers on the 2nd all published on the front page in a prominent position the reply telegram from Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council to Chairman Malenkov of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Foreign Minister Molotov.
