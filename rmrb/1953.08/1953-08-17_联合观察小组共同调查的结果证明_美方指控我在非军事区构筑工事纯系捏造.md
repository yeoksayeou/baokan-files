Title: The Joint Observation Team's Joint Investigation Proves that the U.S. Accusation that I Constructed Fortifications in the Demilitarized Zone is Pure Fabrication
Author:
Date: 1953-08-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 16th. At the 11th meeting of the Military Armistice Commission on August 13th, the U.S. Chief Commissioner, General Bryan, accused: On August 9th, personnel from our side "had begun to construct several fortifications" in the vicinity of 38 degrees 22 minutes 5 seconds North latitude, 128 degrees 9 minutes 45 seconds East longitude, within the Demilitarized Zone. The Military Armistice Commission immediately ordered the Ninth Joint Observation Team stationed there to conduct a joint investigation at 17:45 on the afternoon of August 14th. The results of the investigation proved that the U.S. accusation was purely fabricated. The Ninth Joint Observation Team has submitted a joint report on the results of the joint investigation to the Military Armistice Commission.

### 联合观察小组共同调查的结果证明  美方指控我在非军事区构筑工事纯系捏造

1953-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    联合观察小组共同调查的结果证明
    美方指控我在非军事区构筑工事纯系捏造
    【新华社开城十六日电】八月十三日，军事停战委员会第十一次会议上，美方首席委员勃里安指控：八月九日我方人员曾在北纬三十八度二十二分五秒，东经一百二十八度九分四十五秒附近非军事区内“已着手构筑若干工事”。军事停战委员会即令驻在该地的第九联合观察小组于八月十四日下午十七时四十五分前往进行共同调查。调查结果证明美方的指控纯属捏造。第九联合观察小组已就共同调查的结果，向军事停战委员会提出联合报告。
