Title: Celebrating the Signing of the Armistice Agreement and Commemorating the "August 1st" Army Day: Volunteer Army Leadership Organ Holds Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a celebration meeting on the evening of July 30th to warmly celebrate the great victory of the Korean and Chinese people in defending peace, and also to commemorate the "August 1st" Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Those attending the celebration meeting included General Deng Hua, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, General Yang Dezhi, Chief of Staff General Li Da, Director of the Political Department Li Zhimin, Deputy Director Zhang Nansheng, and representatives from various departments of the Volunteer Army leadership organ, totaling more than 700 people. The Korean People's Delegation, led by Kim Min-san, Minister of the Social Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, also attended the celebration meeting. General Deng Hua spoke at the meeting. He described the great victories achieved by the Korean and Chinese people's forces in the anti-aggression war over the past three years, warmly congratulated the glorious victory of the realization of the Korean armistice, and called on all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to maintain high vigilance, continue to strengthen the fighting solidarity between the Chinese and Korean people, and strive to realize all the terms of the armistice agreement and achieve a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. After a speech by Kim Min-san, head of the Korean People's Delegation, representatives of the Pyongyang City People and representatives of the Korean People's Army presented flags to the leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army at the meeting. The meeting passed congratulatory telegrams to the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and to Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army.

### 庆祝停战协定的签订和纪念“八一”建军节  志愿军领导机关举行庆祝大会

1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝停战协定的签订和纪念“八一”建军节
    志愿军领导机关举行庆祝大会
    据新华社讯：中国人民志愿军领导机关于七月三十日晚间，举行庆祝大会，热烈庆祝朝中人民保卫和平的伟大胜利，同时并纪念中国人民解放军“八一”建军节。出席庆祝大会的有：中国人民志愿军副司令员邓华将军、杨德志将军，参谋长李达将军，政治部主任李志民将军、副主任张南生将军及志愿军领导机关各部门代表等共七百多人。朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会部部长金敏山率领的朝鲜人民代表团也参加了庆祝大会。邓华将军在会上讲话。他叙述了三年来朝中人民部队在反侵略战争中所获得的伟大胜利，并热烈祝贺朝鲜停战实现的光荣胜利，号召中国人民志愿军全体指战员保持高度警惕，继续加强朝中两国人民的战斗团结，为实现停战协定的全部条款，争取朝鲜问题的和平解决而奋斗到底。朝鲜人民代表团团长金敏山讲话后，平壤市人民代表和朝鲜人民军代表在会上分别向中国人民志愿军领导机关献旗。大会通过了致中国人民抗美援朝总会和朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅的贺电。
