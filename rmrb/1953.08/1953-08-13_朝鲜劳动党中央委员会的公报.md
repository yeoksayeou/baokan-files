    Communiqué of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea
    The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea held its sixth plenary meeting from August 5th to 9th. The meeting discussed the following issues: 1. The struggle to restore and develop the post-war people's economy after the signing of the armistice agreement and the Party's future tasks; 2. The recent anti-Party and anti-state incident involving the spy gang of Li Chengye, Pei Zhe, Park Seung-won, Yoon Soon-da, Zhao Il-ming, and Li Kang-guk, and the suicide of Xu Jiayi; 3. Organizational issues.
    Comrade Kim Il-sung delivered a report on the first issue, and Comrade Park Jeong-ae delivered a report on the second issue. After the reports, the participants enthusiastically discussed these two issues and adopted corresponding resolutions.
    Regarding organizational issues, the meeting passed the following resolutions:
    I. To remove Zhu Ninghe, Zhang Shiyu, Park Heon-young, Kim Oh-seong, An Gi-seong, Kim Gwang-su, and Kim Eung-bin, etc., the anti-Party, anti-state saboteurs and factionalists, from the Central Committee and expel them from the Party, and to remove former alternate Central Committee member Kwon Oh-jik from the alternate membership and expel him from the Party.
    II. To remove Gu Jae-su, Li Tianzhen, Zhao Fuli, and Li Jushang from the Central Committee for failing to demonstrate dedication and loyalty to the Party and the state during the Fatherland Liberation War.
    III. To supplement alternate members of the Central Committee, Comrades Li Young-seop, Hwang Tae-seong, Park Kyung-su, Ryu Chuk-un, and Yoon Hyung-sik as members of the Central Committee.
    IV. To abolish the Party Central Organization Committee and elect the Standing Committee of the Party Central Committee, composed of Kim Il-sung, Kim Doo-bong, Park Jeong-ae, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Park Young-bin, Choi Won-taek, Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, Kim Hwang-il, Kang Mun-seok, Kim Seung-hwa, Kim Kwang-hyup, Park Kum-cheol, and Nam Il.
    V. To elect the Political Committee of the Party Central Committee, composed of Kim Il-sung, Kim Doo-bong, Park Jeong-ae, Park Chang-ok, and Kim Il.
    VI. To remove Comrade Jang Soon-myung from the post of Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee and Comrade Li Ki-seok from the post of Vice-Chairman, and to elect Comrade Kim Eung-ki as Chairman.
    VII. To abolish the secretariat system of the Party Central Committee and decide to rename Comrades Park Jeong-ae, Park Chang-ok, and Kim Il, who hold the positions of secretaries, as Vice-Chairmen of the Party Central Committee.
    VIII. The Committee for Revision of the Constitution of the Workers' Party of Korea shall be composed of the following members: Kim Il-sung (Chairman), Park Jeong-ae, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Park Young-bin, Li Ki-seok, Kim Hwang-il, Jung Il-ryong, Kim Kwang-hyup, Li Kwon-mu, Han Seol-ya, Kang Mun-seok, Hwang Tae-seong, Kim Yeol, Go Bong-gi, Kim Seung-hwa, and Park Kum-cheol.
    IX. To approve the appointments of Comrade Kim Chang-man as Minister of Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Comrade Kim Hwang-il as Minister of Labor, Comrade Kim Min-san as Minister of Social Affairs, and Comrade Li Cheong-won as Minister of Social Sciences.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的公报

1953-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的公报
    从八月五日到九日，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行了第六次全体会议。会议讨论了如下的问题：一、停战协定签订后为恢复发展战后人民经济的斗争和党今后的任务；二、关于最近在党内发生的李承烨、裴哲、朴胜源、尹淳达、赵一明、李康国等间谍匪帮反党、反国家事件和许嘉谊自杀事件；三、组织问题。
    金日成同志作了关于第一个问题的报告，朴正爱同志作了关于第二个问题的报告。报告以后，由与会者对这两个问题热烈进行讨论，并通过了相应的决定。
    关于组织问题，会议通过了如下的决定：
    一、决定将反党、反国家的破坏暗害分子、宗派分子们——朱宁河、张时雨、朴宪永、金午星、安基成、金光洙、金应彬等，从党中央委员中除名并清除出党，将前候补中央委员权五稷从候补委员中除名并清除出党。
    二、将在祖国解放战争时期没有对党和国家发挥献身性和忠实性的具在洙、李天镇、赵福礼、李周祥从党中央委员中除名。
    三、将党中央委员会候补委员李英暹、黄泰成、朴京洙、柳祝运、尹亨植同志补充为党中央委员。
    四、废除党中央组织委员会，选出以金日成、金枓奉、朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一、朴永彬、崔元泽、崔昌益、郑一龙、金黄一、姜文锡、金承化、金光侠、朴金喆、南日同志为成员的党中央常务委员会。
    五、选出以金日成、金枓奉、朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一同志为成员的党中央政治委员会。
    六、解除党中央检阅委员会委员长张顺明同志、副委员长李基锡同志的职务，推选金应基同志为委员长。
    七、废除党中央委员会的书记制，决定将担任书记职务的朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一同志改称为党中央委员会副委员长。
    八、朝鲜劳动党党章修改委员会，由以下的成员组成：金日成（委员长）、朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一、朴永彬、李基锡、金黄一、郑一龙、金光侠、李权武、韩雪野、姜文锡、黄泰成、金烈、高凤起、金承化、朴金喆同志。
    九、批准任命金昌满同志为朝鲜劳动党中央委员会宣传鼓动部部长，金黄一同志为劳动部部长，金民山同志为社会部部长，李清源同志为社会科学部部长。
