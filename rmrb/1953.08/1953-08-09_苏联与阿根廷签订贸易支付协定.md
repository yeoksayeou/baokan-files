Title: Soviet Union and Argentina Sign Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th August: TASS reported that the Soviet Union and Argentina have issued a communique on the signing of a trade and payment agreement between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Argentine Republic.

The communique announced that on August 5th of this year, the Soviet Union and Argentina signed a trade and payment agreement.

According to this agreement, the Soviet Union will supply Argentina with a large amount of oil and coal, as well as industrial equipment, including equipment and materials for the oil, coal and power industries, railway equipment, tractors and agricultural machinery, on a deferred payment basis. In addition, the Soviet Union will also supply Argentina with raw materials and metallurgical industry products, including rolled metal, pig iron, steel plates, rails, axles, boiler tubes and boiler plates, and oil pipes for the petroleum industry. Soviet goods also include chemicals, pharmaceuticals, asbestos, precision instruments and petroleum products.

In the first year of the agreement's entry into force, Argentina will mainly export linseed oil and wool to the Soviet Union, accounting for more than 50% of the total value of goods stipulated for export to the Soviet Union. Argentina's exports also include leather, sheepskin, lard, cheese and canned meat, mutton and pork, etc.

### 苏联与阿根廷签订贸易支付协定

1953-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联与阿根廷签订贸易支付协定
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联与阿根廷发表了关于苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟与阿根廷共和国签订贸易支付协定的公报。
    公报宣布，今年八月五日，苏联与阿根廷签订了一项贸易支付协定。
    根据这个协定，苏联将按分期偿付办法供给阿根廷以大量的石油和煤以及工业装备，包括石油、煤矿和动力工业用的装备与材料、铁路装备、拖拉机和农业机器。除此以外，苏联还将供给阿根廷原料与冶金工业产品，包括延压用的金属、生铁、钢板、铁轨、轮轴、汽锅管与汽锅板、石油工业用的油管。苏联货物中还包括化学品、药品、石棉、精密仪器和石油产品。
    在协定生效的第一年内，阿根廷将输往苏联的货物中，主要是亚麻油与羊毛，占规定输往苏联的货物总值的百分之五十以上。阿根廷输出的货物还包括皮革、羊皮、猪油、干酪和罐头肉、羊肉和猪肉等等。
