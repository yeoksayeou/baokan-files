Title: Celebrating the Fiftieth Anniversary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union: Soviet Newspapers Publish the CPC Central Committee's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 31st: The Soviet newspaper "Pravda" and all Moscow newspapers on July 31st published the congratulatory messages from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the occasion of the fiftieth anniversary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. "Pravda" also published on July 31st a summary of the Chinese people's commemoration of the fiftieth anniversary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the editorial of the Beijing "People's Daily", as well as reports on newspapers publishing the "Outline of Propaganda for the Fiftieth Anniversary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and other commemorative articles.

### 庆祝苏联共产党五十周年  苏联各报刊载中共中央贺电

1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏联共产党五十周年
    苏联各报刊载中共中央贺电
    【新华社莫斯科三十一日电】苏联“真理报”和莫斯科所有报纸三十一日都登载了中国共产党中央委员会、波兰统一工人党中央委员会和捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会向苏联共产党中央委员会祝贺苏联共产党五十周年的电文。“真理报”三十一日还刊载了中国人民纪念苏联共产党五十周年和北京“人民日报”的社论摘要及各报登载“苏联共产党中央委员会庆祝苏联共产党五十周年宣传提纲”和其他纪念文章的情形。
