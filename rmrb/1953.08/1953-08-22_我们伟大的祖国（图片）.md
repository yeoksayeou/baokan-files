Our Great Motherland
	During the holidays, the geology groups of various middle schools in Beijing went on geological trips to the suburban mines, increasing their knowledge of geology. This is a group of students from Beijing No. 12 Girls' Middle School. They are on a geological trip to Hongshankou in the western suburbs. After observing the strata, they are discussing with the counselor.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市各中等学校的地质小组，在假期到郊区各矿山举行地质旅行，增加了他们对地质方面的常识。这是北京市第十二女子中学的学生。她们在西郊红山口举行地质旅行。她们在观察了岩层以后，正在和辅导员进行讨论。
