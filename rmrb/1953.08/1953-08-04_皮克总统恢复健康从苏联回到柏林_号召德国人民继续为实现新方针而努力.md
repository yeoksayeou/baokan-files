Title: President Pieck Returns to Berlin from Soviet Union After Recovering Health; Calls on German People to Continue Efforts to Achieve New Policy

Author:

Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 2nd: TASS—President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic returned to Berlin today from the Soviet Union after recovering his health.

According to the press office of the Premier of the German Democratic Republic, a delegation from the People's Chamber and the government greeted President Pieck in Frankfurt an der Oder on the Polish-German border. Tens of thousands of Berlin residents and personnel from the government of the German Democratic Republic, led by Premier Otto Grotewohl, welcomed President Pieck at the Berlin East Station. Also present to welcome him were Soviet High Commissioner to Germany Semenov, Deputy High Commissioner Yudin, and representatives of the diplomatic missions of the various People's Democratic countries.

Premier Grotewohl and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Walter Ulbricht, spoke at the welcoming ceremony, congratulating President Pieck. President Pieck thanked the people of Berlin for their warm welcome. President Pieck especially mentioned that he fully agreed with the new policy of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the government of the German Democratic Republic. He called on all patriotic Germans to continue their efforts to implement the new policy, improve the living conditions of residents, strive for German unification, and safeguard peace.

### 皮克总统恢复健康从苏联回到柏林  号召德国人民继续为实现新方针而努力

1953-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统恢复健康从苏联回到柏林
    号召德国人民继续为实现新方针而努力
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社柏林二日讯：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克恢复健康后，于今日从苏联返抵柏林。
    据德意志民主共和国总理新闻处消息，人民议院和政府曾派代表团在波德边境上的奥得河畔法兰克福迎接皮克总统。前往柏林东站欢迎皮克总统的有数万个柏林居民和以总理奥托·格罗提渥为首的德意志民主共和国政府人员。前往欢迎的还有：苏联驻德高级专员谢麦诺夫、副高级专员尤金和各人民民主国家外交使团的代表。
    总理格罗提渥和德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记瓦尔特·乌布利希在欢迎会上讲话，向皮克总统祝贺。皮克总统答谢柏林人民对他的亲热的欢迎。皮克总统特别谈到他完全同意德国统一社会党和德意志民主共和国政府的新方针。他号召德国所有的爱国人士继续为实现新的方针、为改善居民的生活条件、为争取德国统一以及为保障和平而努力。
