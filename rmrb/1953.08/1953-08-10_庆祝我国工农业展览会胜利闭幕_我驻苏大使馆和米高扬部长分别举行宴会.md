Title: Celebrating the Successful Closing of China's Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition: Receptions Held by Our Embassy in the Soviet Union and Minister Mikoyan Respectively
Author:
Date: 1953-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. After the "People's Republic of China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition" successfully concluded on the evening of August 4th, Charge d'Affaires ad interim Ge Baoquan of our embassy in the Soviet Union, together with Exhibition Hall Director Yi Qintang, hosted a Chinese dinner on the evening of the 5th to entertain the responsible personnel of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Soviet Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade, the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, and all Soviet personnel who participated in the exhibition, to express their gratitude. The attendees included: Soviet Deputy Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade Kumykin, Member of the Collegium of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Podtserob, Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce Nesterov, as well as many responsible personnel from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Soviet Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade, and the Soviet Chamber of Commerce.

On the evening of the 7th, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan, and Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, Nesterov, jointly held a grand reception to celebrate the successful closing of China's Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition. More than 500 people attended. The Soviet side included: Minister of Railways Beshchev, Minister of Coal Industry Zasyadko, Minister of Oil Industry Baibakov, Minister of Chemical Industry Tikhomirov, Minister of Transport and Heavy Machine Building Nosenko, Minister of Justice Gorshenin, Minister of Culture Ponomarenko, Marshal of the Soviet Union Budyonny, Member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Podtserob, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet Yasnov, First Deputy Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union Kabanov and Deputy Ministers Kumykin, Zherolkonkov, Borisov, Sotov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Semicastny, Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce Nesterov, as well as responsible personnel from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Soviet Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade, the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, and people from Soviet social organizations and cultural and artistic circles. The Chinese side invited to attend included: Charge d'Affaires ad interim Ge Baoquan of our embassy in the Soviet Union, Exhibition Hall Director Yi Qintang, Commercial Counselor Li Qiang, Xu Dashen, Military Attaché Major General Ji He, as well as all staff members of the embassy, all Chinese staff members who participated in the exhibition, and Chinese students from Moscow and Leningrad who assisted with the exhibition.

At the banquet, Mikoyan first raised his glass to celebrate the success of the exhibition, to congratulate the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government, and to wish Comrade Mao Zedong good health. Member of the Collegium of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Podtserob wished Premier Zhou Enlai good health, Soviet Deputy Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade Kumykin wished Vice Premier Chen Yun good health, Marshal Budyonny wished Commander-in-Chief Zhu De good health, and Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce Nesterov wished Minister Ye Jizhuang good health. In his toast, Charge d'Affaires ad interim Ge Baoquan pointed out: When the exhibition closed, the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the government, headed by Comrade Malenkov, visited the exhibition, which was a great honor for the Chinese people. The inscriptions they left in the exhibition's guest book will inspire the Chinese people who are engaged in large-scale national economic construction. In his toasts, Ge Baoquan wished good health to the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the government headed by Comrade Malenkov, good health to Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs Molotov, good health to Soviet Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade Mikoyan, and good health to the ministers present.

A concert was held before and after the banquet. Tamara Khanum, a famous Soviet singer who came to China last year to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," sang the Shaanxi folk song "Abundant Food and Clothing." Chinese students performed the Yangko dance. The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 庆祝我国工农业展览会胜利闭幕  我驻苏大使馆和米高扬部长分别举行宴会

1953-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国工农业展览会胜利闭幕
    我驻苏大使馆和米高扬部长分别举行宴会
    【新华社莫斯科九日电】“中华人民共和国工农业展览会”在八月四日晚闭幕后，我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权，在五日晚会同展览会馆馆长衣钦堂设中国晚宴，招待苏联外交部、苏联国内和对外贸易部、苏联商会的负责人员，及参加展览会工作的全体苏联人员，以表示谢意。出席宴会的有：苏联国内和对外贸易部副部长库米金、苏联外交部部务会议委员波德采罗布、苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫，以及苏联外交部、苏联国内和对外贸易部和苏联商会负责人员多人。
    七日晚，苏联部长会议副主席、苏联国内和对外贸易部部长米高扬又和苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫联名举行盛大的招待会，庆祝我国工农业展览会的胜利闭幕。到会的有五百多人。苏联方面出席的有：交通部部长别什切夫，煤矿工业部部长扎霞迪科，石油工业部部长巴伊巴科夫，化学工业部部长季霍米罗夫，运输和重型机器制造工业部部长诺森科，司法部部长戈尔谢宁，文化部部长波诺马连科，苏联元帅布琼尼，外交部部务会议委员波德采罗布，莫斯科苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，苏联国内和对外贸易部第一副部长卡巴诺夫和副部长库米金、热沃隆科夫、波里索夫、索托夫，列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记谢米察斯特尼，苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫以及苏联外交部、苏联国内和对外贸易部、苏联商会负责人员，和苏联社会团体与文化艺术界人士。我方应邀出席的有：我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权，展览会馆馆长衣钦堂，大使馆商务参赞李强、徐达深，武官吉合少将，以及大使馆全体工作人员、我国参加展览会的全体工作人员和此次协助展览会工作的莫斯科与列宁格勒两地的中国留学生。
    宴会上，米高扬首先举杯祝展览会的成功，祝贺中国共产党和中央人民政府，并祝毛泽东同志的健康。苏联外交部部务会议委员波德采罗布祝周恩来总理健康，苏联国内和对外贸易部副部长库米金祝陈云副总理健康，布琼尼元帅祝朱德总司令健康，苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫祝叶季壮部长健康。戈宝权临时代办在敬酒时指出：当展览会闭幕的时候，以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党和政府领袖们到展览会参观，对于中国人民是莫大的光荣。他们在展览会意见簿上所留下的题词，将鼓舞正在从事大规模国家经济建设的中国人民。戈宝权先后在敬酒中，祝以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党和政府领袖们的健康，祝苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫健康，祝苏联国内和对外贸易部部长米高扬健康，祝在座的各部部长的健康。
    宴会前后曾举行音乐会。曾在去年来我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联著名歌唱家塔马拉·哈侬在会上演唱了陕北民歌“丰衣足食”。中国留学生表演了秧歌舞。宴会始终洋溢着热烈友好的气氛。
