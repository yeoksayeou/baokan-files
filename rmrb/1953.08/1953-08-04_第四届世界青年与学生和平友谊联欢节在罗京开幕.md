Title: The Fourth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship Opens in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1953-08-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 2nd: The Fourth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship held its opening ceremony on the 2nd at the "August 23rd" Cultural and Sports Park in Bucharest. The vast sports field was filled with approximately 100,000 Romanian working people and foreign guests. At 4 p.m., a spirited march resounded, and the parade of youth delegations from various countries began. The parade of youth representatives from 106 countries took nearly two hours to complete. Approximately 28,000 young men and women of different nationalities shouted "Peace and Friendship!" as they passed the rostrum. The sports field was continuously filled with thunderous applause.

Jacques Denis, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, welcomed the youth delegations from various countries on behalf of the International Preparatory Committee of the Festival. He said: To express the will to strive for peace and the aspirations of young people around the world, you have crossed oceans and mountains from all corners of the world to come here. We unanimously shout: Peace and Friendship! We want peace, because peace means happiness for young people.

Next, Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania, congratulated the festival on behalf of the Romanian people.

After Groza's speech, a group of athletes holding short batons for the International Peace and Friendship Relay Race appeared on the sports field. They ran to the rostrum and handed the batons to Jacques Denis. Jacques Denis then announced the official opening of the festival. To the rhythm of the World Federation of Democratic Youth anthem, the festival flag was slowly raised. Several thousand white doves were released into the sports field. The festival began.

### 第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节在罗京开幕

1953-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节在罗京开幕
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特二日讯：第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节二日在布加勒斯特“八月二十三日”文化体育公园举行开幕典礼。广大的运动场内挤满了约十万名罗马尼亚劳动人民和外国来宾。下午四时响起了嘹亮的进行曲，各国青年代表团的游行开始了。来自一百零六个国家的青年代表的游行队伍走了将近两小时才走完。大约二万八千名不同国籍的男女青年走过主席台时高呼：“和平与友谊！”运动场上不断响起暴风雨般的掌声。
    世界民主青年联盟总书记雅克·德尼代表联欢节国际筹备委员会向到会的各国青年代表团表示欢迎。他说：为了表达争取和平的意志和全世界青年的愿望，你们渡过重洋，越过高山，从世界各个角落来到了这里。我们一致高呼：和平与友谊！我们要和平，因为和平意味着青年获得幸福。
    接着，罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席彼得鲁·格罗查代表罗马尼亚人民向联欢节祝贺。
    格罗查的演说结束后，运动场上出现了一群手持国际和平友谊接力跑短棍的运动员。他们跑到主席台前，把短棍交给雅克·德尼。接着雅克·德尼宣布联欢节正式开始。合着世界民主青年联盟盟歌的节奏，联欢节的会旗徐徐升起。运动场上放出了几千只白鸽。联欢节开始了。
