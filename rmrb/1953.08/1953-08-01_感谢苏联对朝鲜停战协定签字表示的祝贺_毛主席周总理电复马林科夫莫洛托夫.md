Title: Thanks to the Soviet Union for congratulating on the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement - Telegram reply from Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou to Malenkov and Molotov
Author: Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai
Date: 1953-08-01
Page: 1

To Comrade Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Comrade Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union:

    We sincerely thank the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union and you for your congratulations on the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement.

    The realization of the Korean armistice is a great victory for the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. The unremitting efforts of the Soviet government and the Soviet people to peacefully resolve the Korean issue have played a significant role in ending the Korean War.

    The Chinese people will forever unite with the great Soviet people, the heroic Korean people, and all peace-loving people around the world, and continue to work to consolidate and defend peace in the Far East and the world.

    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    July 31, 1953, Beijing

### 感谢苏联对朝鲜停战协定签字表示的祝贺  毛主席周总理电复马林科夫莫洛托夫
毛泽东  周恩来
1953-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢苏联对朝鲜停战协定签字表示的祝贺
    毛主席周总理电复马林科夫莫洛托夫苏联部长会议主席马林科夫同志：苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫同志：
    衷心感谢苏联各族人民和您们在朝鲜停战协定签字之际所表示的祝贺。
    朝鲜停战的实现是以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的伟大胜利。苏联政府和苏联人民为和平解决朝鲜问题所作的不懈努力，对于朝鲜战争的结束起了巨大的作用。
    中国人民将与伟大的苏联人民、英雄的朝鲜人民以及全世界爱好和平的人民永远团结在一起，为巩固与保卫远东及世界和平的事业而继续努力。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来
    一九五三年七月三十一日北京
