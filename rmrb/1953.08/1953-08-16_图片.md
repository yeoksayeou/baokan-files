    Chinese People's Volunteer Army female prisoner of war Yang Yuhua (center) returns to the embrace of the motherland, carrying the national flag she sewed herself. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Jie photo)
    Korean People's Army female prisoners of war return to the embrace of their motherland, bringing back their national flags and huge banners. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Limen photo)
    General Du Ping, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, comforts Yang Yuhua. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi photo)
    After disembarking, the Korean People's Army female prisoners of war who returned to the embrace of their motherland unfurled a banner with red characters, which read "Long Live the Brotherly Chinese People." This was written in blood after they pierced their fingers. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi photo)

### 图片

1953-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军女被俘人员杨玉华（中）回到祖国怀抱，她带着自己缝制的祖国国旗。  （新华社记者  张杰摄）
    朝鲜人民军女被俘人员返回祖国怀抱，她们带回来祖国的旗帜和巨幅的标语。  （新华社记者  杨礼门摄）
    中国人民志愿军政治部副主任杜平将军慰问杨玉华。    （新华社记者  蔡忠植摄）
    返回祖国怀抱的朝鲜人民军女被俘人员们，下车后展开了一条写有红字的标语，上面写的是“兄弟的中华人民万岁”，这是她们刺破自己的手指用鲜血写成的。（新华社记者  蔡忠植摄）
