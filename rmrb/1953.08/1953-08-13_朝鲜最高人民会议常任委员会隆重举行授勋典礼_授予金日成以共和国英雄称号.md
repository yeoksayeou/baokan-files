Title: Grand Ceremony Held by the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea to Award Medals, Kim Il-sung Awarded the Title of Hero of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1953-08-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea solemnly held an awarding ceremony on the 9th to bestow the highest honor – the glorious title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" – upon Premier of the Cabinet and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, Marshal Kim Il-sung.

    The ceremony was attended by Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; Park Jung-ai, Park Chang-ok, and Kim Il, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Choe Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, and Park Ui-wan, Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet; and many other party and government leaders and senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

    At the beginning of the ceremony, Ryu Chi-kyong, Director of the Political Affairs Department of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, read out the decree awarding Marshal Kim Il-sung the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea." Then, amidst thunderous applause, Chairman Kim Tu-bong presented Marshal Kim Il-sung with the First Class National Flag Order and the Gold Star Medal.

    At the ceremony, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly also awarded various medals to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Park Jung-ai and Park Chang-ok, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Jung Il-ryong and Choe Chang-ik, and Park Ui-wan Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet, and other party and government leaders and senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

    After the awarding of medals, Chairman Kim Tu-bong delivered a speech. He pointed out that in the just Fatherland Liberation War of the Korean people, Marshal Kim Il-sung, who led the entire party members, the entire people, and the heroic soldiers of the People's Army to victory, was honored with the glorious title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea," and the entire Korean people unanimously expressed warm welcome and congratulations. He said that all our brilliant victories in the just Fatherland Liberation War were achieved under the outstanding leadership of Comrade Kim Il-sung.

    Chairman Kim Tu-bong also extended warm congratulations to all the award recipients who had made special contributions in the war and encouraged them to work harder and strive for new victories in the struggle to rebuild the motherland after the war.

    Kim Seung-seop, Chairman of the People's Committee of North Pyongan Province, delivered a reply speech on behalf of all the award recipients. He first expressed warm congratulations to Marshal Kim Il-sung. He said that whether in the dark period of Japanese imperialist rule, in the period of peaceful construction after liberation, or in the period of the Fatherland Liberation War, the name of Marshal Kim Il-sung has always symbolized the people's light, happiness, and freedom, and symbolized brilliant victory. Finally, he vowed to repay the cultivation and care of the Workers' Party and Marshal Kim Il-sung by successfully completing the post-war national economic recovery and construction plan.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会隆重举行授勋典礼  授予金日成以共和国英雄称号

1953-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会隆重举行授勋典礼
    授予金日成以共和国英雄称号
    【新华社平壤十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在九日隆重举行以最高荣誉——“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”的光荣称号授予内阁首相、朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅的授勋典礼。
    参加典礼的有：最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一，内阁副首相崔昌益、郑一龙、朴义琓等党政首长和朝鲜人民军高级将领等多人。
    授勋典礼开始时，由最高人民会议常任委员会政房长柳致景宣读授予金日成元帅以“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号的政令。接着，在全场雷动的掌声中金枓奉委员长把一级国旗勋章和金星奖章授予金日成元帅。
    在典礼上，最高人民会议常任委员会同时分别授予最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴昌玉，内阁副首相郑一龙、崔昌益、朴义琓等党政首长和朝鲜人民军高级将领以各种勋章。
    授勋毕，金枓奉委员长发表演说。他指出，在朝鲜人民正义的祖国解放战争中，带领着全体党员、全体人民和英雄的人民军将士走向胜利的金日成元帅荣膺“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”的光荣称号，全朝鲜人民一致表示热烈的欢迎和祝贺。他说，在正义的祖国解放战争中，我们的一切辉煌胜利，都是在金日成同志的卓越领导下获得的。
    金枓奉委员长又向全体在战争中建立了特殊功勋的受勋者们致热烈的祝贺，并鼓励他们在战后重建祖国的斗争中，更加努力，争取新的胜利。
    平安北道人民委员会委员长金承燮代表全体受勋者致答词。他首先向金日成元帅表示热烈祝贺。他说，无论在日本帝国主义黑暗统治时期，在解放后的和平建设时期，和在祖国解放战争时期，金日成元帅的名字，永远象征着人民的光明、幸福和自由，象征着光辉灿烂的胜利。最后，他宣誓要以胜利完成战后国民经济的恢复建设计划来报答劳动党和金日成元帅的培养和爱护。
