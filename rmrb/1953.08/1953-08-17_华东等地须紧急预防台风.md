Title: Eastern China and other regions must urgently prepare for typhoon
Author:
Date: 1953-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued an urgent typhoon warning at 6:00 PM today:

At 2:00 PM today, the center of the strong typhoon was located approximately 200 kilometers northeast by east of Taipei (at 25.8 degrees North latitude and 123.8 degrees East longitude), with a maximum wind speed near the center of approximately 60 meters per second (force 12). At a distance of approximately 600 kilometers from the center, the wind speed reached 14 to 17 meters per second (force 7). The typhoon center is currently moving northwest by west at a speed of 22 to 26 kilometers per hour. The maximum wind speed in the East China Sea and the northern part of the Taiwan Strait has reached force 12. It is expected that the typhoon center will make landfall near Wenzhou Bay at around 4:00 AM tomorrow (the 17th). It is expected that by 8:00 PM tomorrow, the typhoon center will be near the border between Zhejiang and Anhui. It is predicted that within 24 hours, the coastal areas from the mouth of the Yangtze River to Fuzhou will generally experience winds increasing to force 10 or higher. Northeastern Jiangxi, northern Fujian, the entire province of Zhejiang, and the southeastern part of Anhui and southern Jiangsu south of the Huai River will all experience strong winds of force 6 or 7 or higher. All of the above areas must take urgent precautions.

### 华东等地须紧急预防台风

1953-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    华东等地须紧急预防台风
    【新华社十六日讯】十六日下午六时中央气象台发布台风紧急警报：
    今日下午二时，强台风中心位置约在台北东北偏东二百公里的海面上（即北纬二十五点八度，东经一百二十三点八度），中心附近最大风速约为每秒六十公尺（十二级）。距中心约六百公里的海面上，风速达每秒十四到十七公尺（七级）。目前台风中心正以每小时二十二到二十六公里的速度向西北偏西方向进行，东海海面及台湾海峡北部最大风速已达十二级，预计明日（十七日）晨四时左右，台风中心将在温州湾附近登陆。预计明日下午八时，台风中心将到浙江与安徽的交界地附近。预计二十四小时内，从长江口到福州之间的沿海地区，风力将普遍增加到十级以上。江西东北部、福建北部、浙江全省及淮河以南的安徽东南部与江苏南部一带，都有六、七级以上的大风。以上地区，均须作紧急预防。
