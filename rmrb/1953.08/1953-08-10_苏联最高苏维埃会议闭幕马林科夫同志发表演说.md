Title: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Concludes Session Comrade Malenkov Delivers Speech
Author:
Date: 1953-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: According to TASS, the joint session of the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union was held for the last time in the Kremlin on the 8th. When G.M. Malenkov, V.M. Molotov, N.S. Khrushchev, K.Y. Voroshilov, N.A. Bulganin, L.M. Kaganovich, A.I. Mikoyan, M.Z. Saburov, and M.G. Pervukhin appeared on the government platform, all the representatives and guests at the joint session warmly applauded.

The meeting continued to discuss the Soviet Union's 1953 state budget. Latvian representative Jan Kanberzin first spoke at the meeting.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union G.M. Malenkov then delivered a speech. The entire audience listened intently to Malenkov's speech. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by storms of prolonged applause.

After Malenkov delivered his speech, the discussion of the report on the Soviet Union's 1953 state budget came to an end. Soviet Minister of Finance A. Zverev said in his address: The opinions and questions raised by the representatives in their speeches will be considered and studied by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

Subsequently, the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union approved the Soviet Union's 1953 budget. The budget revenue was 544,264,720,000 rubles, and the expenditure was 530,532,048,000 rubles, with revenue exceeding expenditure by 13,732,672,000 rubles.

The joint session of the two houses also unanimously passed the Law on the Soviet Union's 1953 State Budget, approved the Agricultural Tax Law and the decrees issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and amended Article 126 of the Soviet Constitution. With this, the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union was declared closed.

### 苏联最高苏维埃会议闭幕马林科夫同志发表演说

1953-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃会议闭幕马林科夫同志发表演说
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院八日在克里姆林宫联合举行了末次会议。当格·马·马林科夫、维·米·莫洛托夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、克·叶·伏罗希洛夫、尼·亚·布尔加宁、拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇、阿·伊·米高扬、马·扎·萨布罗夫、米·格·别尔乌辛出现在政府席上时，联席会议的全体代表和来宾们热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    会议继续讨论苏联一九五三年国家预算，拉脱维亚代表扬·卡恩别尔津在会上首先发言。
    苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫接着发表演说。全场聚精会神地听取了马林科夫的演说。他的演说一再为暴风雨般的经久不息的掌声打断。
    马林科夫发表演说之后，苏联一九五三年国家预算报告的讨论就告结束。苏联财政部部长阿·兹维列夫致词说：代表们在发言中提出的意见和问题，将由苏联部长会议考虑和研究。
    接着，苏联最高苏维埃会议批准了苏联一九五三年预算。预算收入为五千四百四十二亿六千四百七十二万卢布，支出为五千三百零五亿三千二百零四万八千卢布，收入超过支出一百三十七亿三千二百六十七万二千卢布。
    两院联席会议并一致通过了苏联一九五三年国家预算法，批准了农业税法和苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布的法令，修改了苏联宪法第一百二十六条。至此，苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议宣告闭幕。
