    Our Great Motherland
    The electrical irrigation project in Erling District, Danyang County, Jiangsu Province, has been completed, irrigating 130,000 mu of farmland and alleviating the local drought threat. This is a control gate set up to regulate the water level of the irrigation canal. The farmers on duty are checking the speed and height of the water flow on the gate.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江苏省丹阳县珥陵区的电力灌溉工程已全部完工，可灌溉农田十三万亩，解除了当地干旱的威胁。这是为了调节灌溉渠道水位而设的节制闸。值班的农民正在闸上查看水流的速度和水位的高低。
