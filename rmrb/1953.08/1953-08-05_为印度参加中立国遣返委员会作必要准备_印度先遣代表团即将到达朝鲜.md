Title: Making Necessary Preparations for India's Participation in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission: Indian Advance Delegation Soon to Arrive in Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-08-05
Page: 1

    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 4th] An advance delegation of thirteen members from India, led by Mr. R.K. Nehru, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and Major General Thorat, Chief of Staff of the Indian Army, is scheduled to arrive in Korea around August 9th to make necessary preparations for India's participation in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission stipulated in the Korean Armistice Agreement. They are scheduled to visit the headquarters of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers on August 11th.

    In addition to Mr. R.K. Nehru and Major General Thorat, the Indian advance delegation includes Mr. Puri, Mr. Mullick, Mr. Ahuja, seven military officers, and Mr. Nair.

### 为印度参加中立国遣返委员会作必要准备  印度先遣代表团即将到达朝鲜

1953-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    为印度参加中立国遣返委员会作必要准备
    印度先遣代表团即将到达朝鲜
    【新华社开城四日电】为印度参加朝鲜停战协定所规定的中立国遣返委员会作必要准备的印度先遣代表团一行十三人，在印度外交部外事秘书鲁·克·尼赫鲁先生与印度陆军参谋长托拉特少将率领下，将于八月九日左右到达朝鲜，并定于八月十一日访问朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的总部。
    印度先遣代表团除鲁·克·尼赫鲁先生与托拉特少将外，尚有普里先生、莫立克先生、阿护迦先生、七位军官和奈尔先生。
