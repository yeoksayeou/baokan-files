Title: Chinese Embassies in Burma, Sweden, Switzerland, and Denmark Hold Army Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1953-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: To celebrate the 26th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, our embassies in Burma and Sweden, and our legations in Switzerland and Denmark, held receptions separately.

    Our embassy in Burma held a reception in Rangoon on the evening of August 1st. Among those invited were Brigadier General Kyaw Win, Director of the Burmese Army's Military Affairs Department, Lieutenant Colonel Than Pe, Commander of the Navy, Lieutenant Colonel Clift, Commander of the Air Force, and Wu Tun Xin, Secretary of the Political Affairs Department of the Burmese Federal Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as senior officials, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, India, Pakistan, Indonesia, Ceylon and other countries to Burma, totaling more than 70 people.

    Our embassy in Sweden held a reception in Stockholm on July 31st. Among those present were officials from the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Defense, personnel and military attachés from the embassies of the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries in Sweden, as well as personnel and military attachés from the embassies of India, Switzerland, Indonesia and other countries in Sweden, totaling more than 100 people. The chairman and some central committee members of the Swedish Communist Party also attended the reception.

    Colonel Zhang Ying, military attaché of our legation in Switzerland, held a reception on the evening of July 31st. More than 100 people attended, including Swiss military and government officials, and diplomatic personnel from several countries to Switzerland. The film "Victory After Victory" was shown after the reception.

    The military attaché of our legation in Denmark held a reception on the evening of August 1st. About 250 people were invited, including Danish military and government officials, and diplomatic envoys from several countries to Denmark.

### 我驻缅甸瑞典瑞士丹麦使馆举行建军节招待会

1953-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻缅甸瑞典瑞士丹麦使馆举行建军节招待会
    据新华社讯：我国驻缅甸、瑞典大使馆和驻瑞士、丹麦公使馆，为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十六周年，分别举行了招待会。
    我国驻缅甸大使馆于八月一日晚在仰光举行招待会。应邀出席的有：缅甸陆军军务署长觉温准将、海军司令丹培中校、空军司令克里夫特中校以及缅甸联邦政府外交部政务司秘书吴吞新等高级官员，还有苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、印度、巴基斯坦、印尼、锡兰等国驻缅甸外交使节等共七十多人。
    我驻瑞典大使馆于七月三十一日在斯德哥尔摩举行招待会。到会的有瑞典外交部和国防部的官员、苏联和人民民主国家驻瑞典使馆的人员与武官以及印度、瑞士、印尼等国家驻瑞典使馆人员及武官等一百余人。瑞典共产党主席及一部分中央委员也出席了招待会。
    我国驻瑞士公使馆武官张英上校在七月三十一日晚举行招待会。出席的有瑞士军队和政府官员、若干国驻瑞士外交人员等一百多人。会后放映了影片“南征北战”。
    我国驻丹麦公使馆武官在八月一日晚举行招待会。应邀出席的有丹麦军队和政府官员以及若干国家驻丹麦外交使节等共二百五十人。
