Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 187th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-08-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The 187th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 27th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, on the basic situation of postal and telecommunications work and the work deployment for the second half of 1953, and approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第一八七次会议

1953-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八七次会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十七次政务会议在二十七日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府邮电部朱学范部长所作的关于邮电工作基本情况与一九五三年下半年工作部署的报告，并通过了各项任免案。
