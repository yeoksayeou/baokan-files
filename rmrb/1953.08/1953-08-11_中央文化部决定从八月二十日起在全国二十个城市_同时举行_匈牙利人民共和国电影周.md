Title: The Central Ministry of Culture Decides to Hold "Hungarian People's Republic Film Week" Simultaneously in Twenty Cities Nationwide Starting August 20th

Author:
Date: 1953-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th: In order to strengthen cultural exchange between China and Hungary and further consolidate the friendly cooperation between the two countries, the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government has decided to simultaneously hold "Hungarian People's Republic Film Week" in twenty major cities across the country from August 20th to August 29th. The cities in the Northeast region participating in the screenings are Shenyang, Anshan, Harbin, Lüda, and Changchun; in the North China region, Beijing, Tianjin, and Taiyuan; in the East China region, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, and Jinan; in the Central South region, Wuhan, Changsha, Nanchang, and Guangzhou; in the Southwest region, Chongqing and Chengdu; and in the Northwest region, Xi'an and Lanzhou.

The Hungarian films to be screened during the film week include six titles: "Underground Colony," "Fire," "Two Worlds," "Baptism of Fire," "Matty the Goose-boy," and "Katalin's Marriage." The content of these films includes depictions of the Hungarian people's heroic struggle against the various conspiracies and sabotage of American imperialism; portrayals of the new atmosphere and happy life in Hungarian factories and rural areas after liberation, as well as the labor competitions and agricultural production cooperative movements launched to faithfully implement government policies; and comedy films adapted from classical works, depicting the peasants' wit, bravery, simplicity, and kindness, while satirizing the landlords' stupidity, baseness, shamelessness, and incompetence. Through these films, we can see the heroic image of the Hungarian people in their struggle against aggression and in defense of peace, as well as in their selfless labor in building a socialist society.

### 中央文化部决定从八月二十日起在全国二十个城市  同时举行“匈牙利人民共和国电影周”

1953-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部决定从八月二十日起在全国二十个城市
    同时举行“匈牙利人民共和国电影周”
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府文化部为加强中匈两国间的文化交流，并进一步巩固中匈两国的友好合作，特决定从八月二十日起到八月二十九日止，在全国二十个大城市同时举行“匈牙利人民共和国电影周”。东北区参加映演的城市为沈阳、鞍山、哈尔滨、旅大、长春，华北区为北京、天津、太原，华东区为上海、南京、杭州、济南，中南区为武汉、长沙、南昌、广州，西南区为重庆、成都，西北区为西安、兰州。
    电影周中放映的匈牙利影片共有六部，即：“地下殖民地”、“火”、“两个世界”、“战斗的洗礼”、“牧鹅少年马季”和“卡塔琳的婚姻”。这些影片的内容，一部分是表现匈牙利人民为反对美国帝国主义的各种阴谋破坏而作的英勇斗争；一部分是描写解放后的匈牙利工厂、农村中的新生气象和幸福生活，以及为忠实执行政府政策所展开的劳动竞赛和农业生产合作社运动；还有根据古典作品改编，表现农民的机智、勇敢、纯朴、善良以及讽刺地主的愚蠢、卑鄙、无耻而又无能的喜剧电影。通过这些影片，我们可以看到匈牙利人民在反对侵略、保卫和平的斗争中以及在建设社会主义社会的忘我劳动中的英勇形象。
