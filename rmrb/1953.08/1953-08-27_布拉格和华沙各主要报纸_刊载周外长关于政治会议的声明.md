Title: Major Newspapers in Prague and Warsaw Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement on Political Conference

Author:
Date: 1953-08-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 26th: Major newspapers in Prague and Warsaw prominently published the statement by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, on the issue of the political conference on the 25th.

### 布拉格和华沙各主要报纸  刊载周外长关于政治会议的声明

1953-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    布拉格和华沙各主要报纸
    刊载周外长关于政治会议的声明
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】布拉格和华沙的各主要报纸，二十五日都以显著地位，刊载我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来关于政治会议问题的声明。
