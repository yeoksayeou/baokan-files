Title: August 22nd Communique on Repatriation of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 22nd: On the 22nd, our side repatriated 137 non-Korean prisoners of war to the other side (including 37 wounded and sick prisoners of war), and 300 Korean prisoners of war. Among the non-Korean prisoners of war were 94 American prisoners of war, 23 British prisoners of war, 13 Canadian prisoners of war, 1 Turkish prisoner of war, 2 French prisoners of war, 3 Australian prisoners of war, and 1 Colombian prisoner of war. The total number of prisoners of war repatriated by our side to the other side was 437.

Today, the other side repatriated 2,402 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side.

### 八月二十二日遣返战俘公报

1953-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    八月二十二日遣返战俘公报
    【新华社开城二十二日电】二十二日，我方遣送给对方非朝鲜籍战俘一百三十七名（包括伤病战俘三十七名），朝鲜籍战俘三百名。在非朝鲜籍战俘中包括美国籍战俘九十四名、英国籍战俘二十三名、加拿大籍战俘十三名、土耳其籍战俘一名、法国籍战俘二名、澳大利亚籍战俘三名、哥伦比亚籍战俘一名。我方遣返给对方的战俘总数是四百三十七名。
    今天对方遣送给我方朝鲜人民军被俘人员二千四百零二名。
