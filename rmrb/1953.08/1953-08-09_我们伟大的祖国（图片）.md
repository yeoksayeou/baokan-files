Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-08-09
Page: 1

Our great motherland
　　In coordination with the development of the entire national economy and to meet the people's growing material needs, China's light industry is developing and growing stronger. The state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory has printed and dyed a large number of printed fabrics to supply the needs of urban and rural areas. This is a scene of female workers in the factory's cloth inspection workshop at work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    配合整个国民经济的发展和满足人民日益增长的物质生活的需要，我国的轻工业正在发展壮大。国营上海第一印染厂印染了大量花布，供给城市和农村的需要。这是该厂检布车间的女工正在工作的情形。
