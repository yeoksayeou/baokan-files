Title: Presidium of the Polish Council of Ministers Passes Decision on Assisting North Korea in Restoring the National Economy
Author: 
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 31st: Polish newspapers published the decision of the Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic regarding assistance to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in restoring its national economy.
    The decision stated that the Polish people, who had experienced immense suffering and destruction during the period of Nazi aggression and occupation, particularly sympathize with and understand the efforts of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to restore its cities, villages, and industrial enterprises that were barbarously destroyed by the aggressors.
    In order to express the attitude of the Polish people, the Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic adopted a decision on July 28, 1953, regarding assistance to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in restoring its national economy.
    The forms of assistance will include: supplying an appropriate quantity of machinery, equipment, and other materials, as well as providing the technical assistance needed to restore a number of industrial enterprises and cities.

### 波兰部长会议主席团  通过关于援助朝鲜恢复国民经济的决定

1953-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰部长会议主席团
    通过关于援助朝鲜恢复国民经济的决定
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社华沙七月三十一日讯：波兰报纸刊载了波兰人民共和国部长会议主席团通过的关于援助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国恢复国民经济的决定。
    决定说，曾在希特勒分子侵略和占领期中经历了无限苦难、遭受了巨大破坏的人民波兰，特别同情并了解朝鲜民主主义人民共和国旨在恢复曾遭侵略者野蛮地摧毁的城市乡村和工业企业的努力。
    波兰人民共和国部长会议主席团为了表示波兰人民的态度，特于一九五三年七月二十八日通过了一项关于援助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国恢复国民经济的决定。
    援助方式将包括：供应适当数量的机器、设备及其他物资，以及提供恢复若干工业企业和城市所需的技术援助。
