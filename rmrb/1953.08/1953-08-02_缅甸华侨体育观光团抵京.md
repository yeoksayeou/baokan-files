Title: Overseas Chinese Sports and Sightseeing Delegation from Burma Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-08-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Burmese Overseas Chinese Sports and Sightseeing Delegation, a group of forty people led by Head of Delegation Lin Rongquan, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou on the evening of the 1st. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Lu Xinyuan and Chen Manyun, Deputy Directors of the General Office of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Wang Jiyuan, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Propaganda Department, Zhuang Mingli, Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Fei Zhendong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, as well as more than 100 representatives from the All-China Sports Federation, the North China Sports Federation, the Indonesian Returned Football Team, and Overseas Chinese students.

### 缅甸华侨体育观光团抵京

1953-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨体育观光团抵京
    【新华社一日讯】缅甸华侨回国体育观光团一行四十人，在团长林荣泉率领下，于一日晚由广州乘车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府华侨事务委员会办公厅副主任卢心远、陈曼云、文教宣传司副司长王纪元、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员庄明理，北京归国华侨联谊会副秘书长费振东，以及中华全国体育总会、华北体育总分会和印尼回国球队、华侨学生代表共一百多人。
