    Our Great Motherland
    Xinjiang Nationalities Institute is a place for training teachers for secondary schools and other professional cadres. Excellent children from twelve ethnic groups from both sides of the Tianshan Mountains study here. In the past two years, more than 250 young people of all ethnic groups have left the school and taken up various positions in the construction of the motherland. This is a corner of the campus. Students are happily chatting here after class.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆民族学院是培养中等学校师资和其他专业干部的场所。在这里学习的有来自天山南北十二个民族的优秀子弟。两年以来，已有二百五十余名各族青年，离开学校走上祖国建设的各个岗位了。这是校园的一角。同学们课后正在这里愉快地谈心。
