Title: The First and Second Joint Observer Teams Meet for the First Time in Panmunjom
Author:
Date: 1953-08-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 13th: The chief members and all staff of both sides of the Joint Observer Teams One and Two subordinate to the Military Armistice Commission met for the first time at Panmunjom at 10:00 a.m. on the 12th. During the meeting, both sides calibrated their respective work sectors and specific responsibilities, and preliminarily exchanged views on the working methods of the two teams in performing their duties in the future.

The Joint Observer Teams Three to Ten have all gone to their respective responsible sections at the end of last month to perform their duties. The first and second teams can officially start working as soon as the Military Armistice Commission reaches an agreement on the "Regulations for Civil Navigation on the Han River Estuary."

### 联合观察小组第一、二两组  双方人员在板门店首次会晤

1953-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    联合观察小组第一、二两组
    双方人员在板门店首次会晤
    【新华社开城十三日电】军事停战委员会所属联合观察小组第一、二两组的双方首席组员及全体工作人员，于十二日上午十时在板门店首次会晤。会晤时，双方校正了各自的工作地段及具体职责，并对该两小组将来执行职司的工作方法等问题初步交换了意见。
    联合观察小组第三至第十小组均已于上月底先后分赴各自负责的地段执行职司。第一、二两个小组一俟军事停战委员会对“汉江口民用航运规则”达成协议后，即可正式开始工作。
