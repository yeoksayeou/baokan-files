Title: Both Sides Have Exchanged Materials on Deceased POWs
Author:
Date: 1953-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 10th—At the Military Armistice Commission secretaries' meeting on August 6th, both sides, in accordance with the provisions of sub-item 58 of the Armistice Agreement, exchanged materials on POWs who died while in custody. The death list of non-Korean POWs handed over by our side to the other side numbered 481. Since our side had already provided the other side with information on 569 deceased POWs on December 26, 1951, the total death list of non-Korean POWs provided by our side who died while in custody is 1,050. The death list of our captured personnel handed over by the other side totaled 8,440.

### 双方已交换死亡的战俘材料

1953-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    双方已交换死亡的战俘材料
    【新华社开城十日电】在八月六日军事停战委员会参谋会议上，双方根据停战协定第五十八款子项的规定，交换了关于在被收容期间死亡的战俘材料。我方交给对方的非朝鲜籍战俘的死亡名单为四百八十一名。按一九五一年十二月二十六日我方已提供对方五百六十九名死亡战俘资料，因此我方提供的在被收容期间的非朝鲜籍战俘的死亡名单共为一千零五十名。对方交来我方被俘人员的死亡名单则共为八千四百四十名。
