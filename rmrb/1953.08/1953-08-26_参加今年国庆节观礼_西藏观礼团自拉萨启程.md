Title: The Tibetan Delegation to the National Day Observation Ceremony Departs from Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1953-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Tibetan delegation, consisting of thirty-two members, to attend this year's National Day observation ceremony, led by Doga Pengtso Rapgye (Kalön), First Deputy Head Ngawang Jinba (Great Lama of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentse), Second Deputy Head Gagang Losang Rentzin, Third Deputy Head Achin (i.e., the Living Buddha Erqiu, Abbot of Aba Zhacang of Tashilhunpo Monastery), and Fourth Deputy Head Zimukaimo Caiwang Dondrup, departed from Lhasa in batches on the 3rd, 6th, and 17th of this month. Representatives from the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Tibetan Local Government, the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Friendship Association, and the Preparatory Committee of the Patriotic Women's Friendship Association went to the suburbs to see them off. The Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army also hosted banquets for all members of the delegation.

### 参加今年国庆节观礼  西藏观礼团自拉萨启程

1953-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    参加今年国庆节观礼
    西藏观礼团自拉萨启程
    【新华社讯】参加今年国庆节观礼的西藏观礼团一行三十二人，在团长朵噶·彭错饶杰（噶伦）、第一副团长纳旺金巴（班禅堪布会议厅大喇嘛）、第二副团长噶章·罗桑仁增、第三副团长阿钦（即札什伦布寺阿巴扎仓的法台）俄曲活佛、第四副团长孜木凯墨·彩旺顿珠等率领下，已于本月三日、六日、十七日，分批自拉萨出发。中国人民解放军西藏军区和西藏地方政府、拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会、爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会都派代表到市郊欢送。中国人民解放军西藏军区并分别设宴为观礼团全体团员饯行。
