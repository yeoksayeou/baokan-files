Title: Celebrating "August 1st" and the Korean Armistice: Rikaze Holds Rally and Parade
Author:
Date: 1953-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Over 3,000 people from the Rikaze organs and troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) Tibet Military Region and people from all walks of life, both monks and laypeople, held a rally on the 1st in the newly built stadium in the city to celebrate "August 1st" Army Day and the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement. Liang Xuanxian and Chen Zizhi, responsible cadres of the PLA stationed in Rikaze, and Zhasake Jinmei and Zhasala Min·Yixi Chuchen of the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall, spoke at the meeting.

Amidst warm applause, the conference received congratulatory gifts and letters from Panchen Erdeni and Lama Wang Lejie of Tashilhunpo Monastery to the People's Liberation Army. The conference adopted a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and the Chinese and Korean People's Armies. After the conference, the attending masses held a celebratory demonstration and parade. In addition, the sports meet jointly held by the Tibet Military Region troops and people from all walks of life in Rikaze also began on this day.

### 庆祝“八一”和朝鲜停战  日喀则各界集会游行

1953-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝“八一”和朝鲜停战
    日喀则各界集会游行
    据新华社讯：中国人民解放军西藏军区驻日喀则机关部队和各界僧俗人民三千多人，一日在市区新建的运动场上举行集会，庆祝“八一”建军节和朝鲜停战协定签字。驻日喀则人民解放军负责干部梁选贤、陈子植和班禅堪布会议厅札萨计晋美、札萨拉敏·益喜楚臣都在会上讲了话。
    大会在热烈掌声中收到班禅额尔德尼及札什伦布寺大喇嘛汪乐阶献给人民解放军的慰问品和慰问信。大会通过向毛主席和中朝人民军队的致敬电。大会结束后，到会群众举行了庆祝示威游行。此外，西藏军区部队和日喀则各界人民联合举行的运动会，也在这一天开始。
