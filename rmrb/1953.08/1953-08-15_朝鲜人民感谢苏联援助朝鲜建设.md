Title: Korean People Thank the Soviet Union for Assisting in the Construction of Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-08-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: The Korean people are filled with excitement and gratitude for the Soviet government's decision to allocate 1 billion rubles to assist Korea's economic recovery and construction. In recent days, factories, rural areas, mines, government offices, schools, and military units in various locations have widely held mass rallies to warmly thank the Soviet government for its selfless assistance.

    In Pyongyang, newspapers with special editions were posted everywhere, featuring a large photograph of Malenkov and the section of his speech at the closing session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union concerning the 1 billion ruble aid to the Korean people for economic recovery and construction. The faces of people reading the special editions revealed boundless joy and gratitude. A group of Young Pioneers reading the special edition in front of the Moranbong Theater excitedly agreed to write letters to the Soviet Young Pioneers to express their thanks. Li Fengshi, a road worker who was building "Stalin Road," said upon hearing the news: "In order to help the Korean people quickly recover from the wounds of war and live a peaceful and happy life as soon as possible, the Soviet people have given us all possible assistance. I must learn from the Soviet people's experience in post-war recovery and construction and exert all my enthusiasm in the work of rebuilding the democratic capital Pyongyang, to build 'Stalin Road' even better and more beautiful."
    All the workers of the Samsin Coal Mine held a mass rally on the evening of the 11th. When the secretary of the Party branch committee spoke about the Soviet government's aid of 1 billion rubles to the Korean people for recovery and construction, the whole audience applauded warmly. After Li Yunheng, the Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Pyongyang City Committee of the Labor Party, read Malenkov's speech at the closing session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, all the workers participating in the meeting unanimously expressed their gratitude to the Soviet people and their determination to contribute to the great cause of national recovery and construction. The meeting passed a letter of thanks to Malenkov amidst thunderous cheers.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: Pyongyang newspapers published editorials on the 11th, warmly thanking the Soviet government for its decision to aid the Korean people's economic recovery and construction with 1 billion rubles.
    The "Rodong Sinmun" editorial said: The Korean people will never forget the fraternal Soviet people who liberated the Korean people from the rule of Japanese imperialism. In the struggle against US imperialist aggression in Korea, the Soviet Union made consistent efforts to peacefully resolve the Korean issue. The editorial pointed out: This great fraternal assistance given by the Soviet people is a huge force to help Korea recover from the wounds of war. The Korean people once again express their national gratitude to the Soviet people. The editorial emphasized: The Korean people will further strengthen the friendly unity with the Soviet people and do their utmost to achieve greater victories in the great cause of restoring and developing the post-war national economy.
    The "Democratic Korea" editorial said: When the Korean people were in a difficult situation in the Fatherland Liberation War against US imperialist aggression, Comrade Stalin sent 50,000 tons of grain to the Korean people in 1952; today, as we begin the post-war recovery and construction, the Soviet people are giving us great assistance. The Korean people will reciprocate the great friendship and assistance of the Soviet people by establishing greater labor achievements and promoting the prosperous and democratic development of the motherland.

### 朝鲜人民感谢苏联援助朝鲜建设

1953-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民感谢苏联援助朝鲜建设
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜人民对苏联政府拨款十亿卢布援助朝鲜恢复经济建设的决定，充满了兴奋和感激。各地的工厂、农村、矿山、机关、学校和部队最近几天广泛举行群众大会，热烈感谢苏联政府的无私援助。
    在平壤，到处张贴着报纸号外，号外上刊登着马林科夫的大幅照片和他在苏联最高苏维埃闭幕会议上的演说中关于以十亿卢布援助朝鲜人民恢复经济建设的部分。阅读号外的人们，脸上都流露着无比的欢欣和感激。牡丹峰剧场门口阅读号外的一群少年团员在读完了号外时，兴奋地互相约定写信向苏联少年先锋队队员致谢。正在修筑“斯大林路”的修路工人李奉世听见这个消息时说：“苏联人民为了帮助朝鲜人民迅速恢复战争创伤，早日过和平幸福的生活，给了我们一切可能的援助。我一定要好好学习苏联人民的战后恢复建设的经验，在重建民主首都平壤的工作中，发挥一切热情，把‘斯大林路’修得更好，更美丽。”
    三神煤矿全体职工在十一日晚上举行了群众大会。党支部委员长谈到苏联政府以十亿卢布援助朝鲜人民恢复建设时，全场热烈鼓掌。在劳动党平壤市委员会宣传部部长李运亨朗读了马林科夫在苏联最高苏维埃闭幕会议上的演说后，参加大会的全体职工一致表示向苏联人民致谢和对祖国恢复建设的伟大事业的决心。大会在雷动的欢呼声中通过了给马林科夫的感谢信。
    【新华社平壤十四日电】平壤各报在十一日发表社论，热烈感谢苏联政府以十亿卢布援助朝鲜人民恢复经济建设的决定。
    “劳动新闻”的社论说：朝鲜人民永远不会忘记把朝鲜人民从日本帝国主义的统治下解放出来的兄弟般的苏联人民。在反对美帝国主义侵略朝鲜的斗争中，为了和平解决朝鲜问题，苏联做了始终一贯的努力。社论指出：这一次苏联人民所给予的伟大的兄弟般的援助，是帮助朝鲜恢复战争创伤的巨大力量。朝鲜人民对苏联人民再一次致以全民族的感谢。社论强调指出：朝鲜人民将进一步加强和苏联人民的友好团结，尽全力争取在恢复和发展战后人民经济的伟大事业中，取得更大的胜利。
    “民主朝鲜报”的社论说：当朝鲜人民在反对美帝国主义侵略的祖国解放战争中，处在困难的境遇时，斯大林同志在一九五二年送给朝鲜人民五万吨粮食；今天，当我们着手进行战后恢复建设事业的时候，苏联人民又给我们巨大的援助，朝鲜人民将以建立更大的劳动功勋，促进祖国富强的民主发展，来答谢苏联人民的伟大友谊和援助。
