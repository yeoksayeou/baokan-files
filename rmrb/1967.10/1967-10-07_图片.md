Title: Vietnamese Democratic Republic Acrobatic Troupe's First Performance at the Great Hall of the People
Author: Liu Zhenxiang
Date: 1967-10-07
Edition: 4

On October 6, the acrobatic troupe from the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, coming from the frontlines of the anti-American struggle, held their first performance at the Great Hall of the People. After the performance, Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, and Comrade Nguyen Van Hien, along with Comrade Nguyen Van Dan, the troupe leader, walked onto the stage to congratulate the successful performance.  Photographer: Liu Zhenxiang, People's Daily reporter.
