Title: The President of Mali Receives Our Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1967-10-09
Edition: 5

The President of Mali, Modibo Keita, received the Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Mali, Liu Helin, in the afternoon of the 4th and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

### 马里总统接见我临时代办

1967-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    马里总统接见我临时代办
    新华社巴马科七日电　马里总统莫迪博·凯塔在四日下午接见了中国驻马里大使馆临时代办刘和林，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
