Title: Chinese Friendly Trade Delegation Visits Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1967-10-22
Edition: 6

A Chinese friendly trade delegation, led by its head and deputy director of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Jia Shi, departed Beijing by airplane today to visit Pakistan.

### 我友好贸易代表团访问巴基斯坦

1967-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    我友好贸易代表团访问巴基斯坦
    新华社二十一日讯　中国友好贸易代表团，在团长、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主任贾石的率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往巴基斯坦进行访问。
