Title: Commemorating the 17th Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' Entry into the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1967-10-26
Edition: 6

Today marks the 17th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into the Korean War. The acting chargé d'affaires of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's embassy in China, Kim Jae Suk, held a cocktail party and film screening in honor of this occasion. Relevant officials from our side, including Li Tianyou, Han Nianlong, Zhang Chuming, and Ding Xilin, were invited to attend.

### 纪念中国人民志愿军赴朝作战十七周年  朝鲜驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会

1967-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念中国人民志愿军赴朝作战十七周年
    朝鲜驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会
    新华社二十五日讯　今天是中国人民志愿军赴朝作战十七周年纪念日，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑为此举行酒会和电影招待会。我有关方面负责人李天佑、韩念龙、张池明、丁西林等应邀出席。
