Title: Delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Visits the Vietnamese Ministry of National Defense
Author:
Date: 1967-10-09
Edition: 5

	By the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee member and National Defense Committee member Yang Dezhi, a delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, accompanied by Vietnamese Labor Party Central Committee member and President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Huang Guoyue, and Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Huang Jun, visited the Ministry of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the afternoon of October 3.

	The Vietnamese Labor Party Central Political Bureau alternate member and Chief of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, General Vo Nguyen Giap, the Party Central Committee alternate member and Deputy Director of the General Political Department, Major General Le Quang Dao, military hero Chen Heng, and some senior officials of the Vietnam People's Army warmly welcomed the delegation.

	The Chinese military attaché to Vietnam, Yuan Yunlou, went with the delegation.

### 中越友协代表团访问越国防部

1967-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    中越友协代表团访问越国防部
    新华社河内五日电  由中共中央委员、国防委员会委员杨得志率领的中越友协代表团，在越南劳动党中央委员、越中友协会长黄国越，越中友协秘书长黄俊陪同下，三日下午访问了越南民主共和国国防部。
    越南劳动党中央政治局候补委员、越南人民军总参谋长文进勇上将，党中央候补委员、总政治局副主任黎光道少将，军队英雄陈亨和越南人民军一些高级干部热烈地迎接了代表团。
    中国驻越南大使馆代武官袁云楼同代表团一起前往。
