	Brief News

	The Asian-African Journalists' Association Sends Delegation to Visit Arab Countries

	The Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists' Association's delegation to visit Arab countries departed from Beijing by plane on the 12th, heading to the Middle East and North Africa for a friendly visit.

	The delegation members who left Beijing today include Sugiyama Ichiro (Japan), Uma Said (Indonesia), Said Salim Abdullah (Tanzania), and Zhang Guoqing (China). The other two members of the delegation, Ahmed Gora Ibrahim (Azania) and Xiong Zhongqi (China), are already abroad.

	Palestinian Arab Guerrillas Launch Guerrilla Warfare

	The Palestinian Arab People's Organization's guerrilla forces are waging guerrilla warfare against the Israeli aggressors who have occupied Palestine, continuously striking new blows against the enemy. On the evening of October 3, Arab guerrillas blew up the residence of Israeli military chief Rabin in the Arab section of Jerusalem. Recently, explosive devices planted by Arab nationalists were also discovered at the residence of Israeli Prime Minister Eshkol in Tel Aviv and the American Library. The Israeli authorities and their American masters were extremely alarmed. At the end of September, a group of Arab guerrillas ambushed an Israeli military armored vehicle on the West Bank of the Jordan River at Ein Jabr (between Nablus and Mount Gaza), killing seven Israeli soldiers.

	Ecuadorian Government Demands U.S. Recall Ambassador

	Due to a dispute between the Ecuadorian ruling clique and American imperialism over the implementation of the "Alliance for Progress" plan, the Ecuadorian Foreign Ministry issued a statement on the 8th demanding that the U.S. government recall the U.S. Ambassador to Ecuador, Winthrop Cole.

	U.S. Ambassador Cole left Quito for his home country on the 9th as requested by the Ecuadorian government.

	Students in Puerto Rico's San Juan Protest for Independence

	Students in the capital of Puerto Rico, San Juan, took to the streets in the evening of the 1st to protest against U.S. colonial rule over Puerto Rico and demand independence. Despite the brutal suppression by the police, the protesting students attacked three department stores operated by American capital that exploit the blood and sweat of the Puerto Rican people with Molotov cocktails. Puerto Rico has been a U.S. colony since 1898.

	Indian Army Invades Pakistani-Controlled Area in Kashmir

	The Pakistani armed forces issued a news release on the 10th stating that on the morning of the 9th, an Indian army company, under the cover of machine gun and mortar fire, crossed the ceasefire line in Kashmir and occupied a position in the Pakistani-controlled area of Kashmir. On the 10th, the Indian army was driven back. Until the afternoon of the 10th, the Indian side continued shelling, but there were no casualties on the Pakistani side.

　　简明新闻
    亚非记协派代表团访问阿拉伯国家
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处访问阿拉伯国家代表团十二日乘飞机离开北京，前往中东和北非进行友好访问。
    今天离京的代表团成员有杉山市平（日本）、乌玛·赛义德（印尼）、赛义德·萨利姆·阿卜杜拉（坦桑尼亚）和张国庆（中国）等。代表团另两个成员艾哈迈德·高拉·易卜拉欣（阿扎尼亚）和熊仲琪（中国）已在国外。
    巴勒斯坦阿拉伯突击队展开游击战
    巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民组织的突击队对侵占巴勒斯坦的以色列侵略者进行游击战，不断给敌人以新的打击。十月三日晚上，阿拉伯突击队爆炸了以色列军队参谋长拉宾在耶路撒冷阿拉伯城的住宅。在特拉维夫的以色列总理艾希科尔的住宅和美国图书馆里最近也发现阿拉伯民族主义者安置的爆炸物，以色列当局及其美国主子因此极为惊慌。九月底，一批阿拉伯突击队在约旦河西岸埃因角（纳布卢斯和加泽姆山之间）伏击了以色列军队的一辆装甲车，打死七名以色列士兵。
    厄瓜多尔政府要求美政府召回大使
    由于厄瓜多尔统治集团同美帝国主义在贯彻执行“争取进步联盟”计划上发生争执，厄瓜多尔外交部八日发表公报，要求美国政府召回美国驻厄瓜多尔大使温伯莱·科尔。
    美国大使科尔根据厄瓜多尔政府的要求已经在九日离开基多回国。
    波多黎各圣胡安学生示威要求独立
    波多黎各首都圣胡安的学生一日晚间上街举行示威，强烈抗议美国对波多黎各的殖民统治，坚决要求波多黎各独立。示威学生不顾警察的残暴镇压，用燃烧弹袭击了三家美国资本经营的吮吸波多黎各人民血汗的百货公司。波多黎各是在一八九八年沦为美国殖民地的。
    印军侵犯克什米尔巴基斯坦控制区
    巴基斯坦武装部队十日发表新闻公报说，九日上午，印度军队一个连，在机关枪和迫击炮火的掩护下，越过克什米尔停火线，占领了克什米尔巴基斯坦控制区内的一个阵地。十日，印度军队被赶了回去。直到十日下午，印度方面继续开炮射击，巴基斯坦方面没有伤亡。
                          （据新华社讯）
