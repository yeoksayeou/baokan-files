Title: Comrade Shehu Leads the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Back to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-10-12
Edition: 1

	Comrade Shehu Leads the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Back to Beijing
	Comrades Zhou Enlai, Xie Fuzhi, and Yang Chengwu Welcome Them at the Airport
	Comrades Zhang Chunqiao and Yao Wenyuan See Them Off at the Airport in Shanghai

	Xinhua News Agency, October 11th: Led by Comrade Muhammad Shehu, the Albanian Party and Government Delegation concluded their visits to Jinan, Qingdao, Dazhai, Taiyuan, Yan'an, Wuhan, and Shanghai. Tonight, accompanied by Comrades Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, Luo Guibo, and others, they returned to Beijing by special plane from Shanghai.

	Comrades Zhou Enlai, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, and others went to the airport to give them a warm welcome.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Shanghai, October 11th: Led by Comrade Shehu, the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Comrades Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, Luo Guibo, and others, departed from Shanghai by special plane tonight to Beijing. Thousands of revolutionary masses and PLA soldiers and officers in Shanghai, filled with revolutionary fervor, gathered at the brightly lit airport to give a warm send-off to their close comrades from the front lines of the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle.

	Leaders of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, leaders of the PLA Navy, Air Force, and Army in Shanghai, Comrades Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Liao Zhengguo, Rao Shouquan, Zhou Jianping, Wang Shaoyong, and Ma Tiancui, as well as leaders of various revolutionary mass organizations, were at the airport to see off the Albanian Party and Government Delegation.

	Albanian students studying in Shanghai were also present at the airport to see them off.

	This afternoon, some members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China and left enthusiastic inscriptions. The delegation members also visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition and gave high praise to the Shanghai proletariat for holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, embodying the spirit of self-reliance and striving for progress.

### 谢胡同志率阿党政代表团回到北京　周恩来谢富治杨成武等同志到机场热烈欢迎　阿党政代表团离开上海时张春桥姚文元等同志到机场热烈欢送

1967-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　谢胡同志率阿党政代表团回到北京
　　周恩来谢富治杨成武等同志到机场热烈欢迎
　　阿党政代表团离开上海时张春桥姚文元等同志到机场热烈欢送
    新华社十一日讯　以穆罕默德·谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，结束了在济南、青岛、大寨、太原、延安、武汉、上海等地的参观访问后，今晚由康生、李富春、刘宁一、刘晓、罗贵波等同志陪同，乘专机从上海回到北京。
    周恩来、谢富治、杨成武等同志前往机场热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚同志。
    据新华社上海十一日电　由谢胡同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，今晚在康生、李富春、刘宁一、刘晓、罗贵波等同志陪同下，乘专机离开上海前往北京。上海数千革命群众和解放军指战员满怀战斗的激情，在灯火辉煌的机场，热烈欢送来自反帝反修斗争前线的亲密战友。
    上海市革命委员会负责人、中国人民解放军驻沪陆海空三军负责人张春桥、姚文元、廖政国、饶守坤、周建平、王少庸、马天水等同志以及各革命群众组织负责人，在机场热烈欢送阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团。
    到机场欢送的，还有阿尔巴尼亚在上海的进修生。
    今天下午，阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团部分同志参观了中国共产党第一次全国代表大会会址，并热情题词留念。代表团同志还参观了上海工业展览会，对上海工人阶级高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，发扬自力更生，奋发图强的革命精神，给以热烈的赞扬。
