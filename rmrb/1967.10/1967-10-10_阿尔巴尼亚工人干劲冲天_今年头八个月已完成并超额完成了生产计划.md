Title: Albanian Workers' Enthusiasm Soars
Author:
Date: 1967-10-10
Edition: 6

	Albanian Workers' Enthusiasm Soars
	Production Plans Exceeded in First Eight Months of the Year

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Tirana, Albanian workers have shown tremendous enthusiasm, achieving significant successes in production and construction in the first eight months of this year, completing and exceeding their production plans.

	Workers in the petroleum industry have, in eight months, supplied the state with 3.8% more crude oil, 6.7% more gasoline, and 1.9% more diesel than planned.

	Construction workers have widely launched labor competitions, completing approximately 8% more work in the first eight months of this year compared to the same period last year.

	Transportation enterprises have also completed their eight-month plans with excellent results. Road transport workers have completed 112.4% of their production targets. Young volunteers on the railways have worked with high enthusiasm, having laid 20 kilometers of roadbed and 11 kilometers of track so far.

	Trade enterprises and institutions have completed 102.1% of their sales plans. To fulfill the tasks assigned by the Fifth Congress of the Labor Party, they have provided the people with 6.7% more goods than in the same period last year.

### 阿尔巴尼亚工人干劲冲天  今年头八个月已完成并超额完成了生产计划
柳中央
1967-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚工人干劲冲天
    今年头八个月已完成并超额完成了生产计划
    据新华社地拉那电  据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚工人干劲冲天，今年头八个月在生产和建设战线上取得了很大成就，完成并超额完成了生产计划。
    石油工业工人在八个月中，超计划供应国家原油百分之三点八，汽油百分之六点七，柴油百分之一点九。
    建筑企业工人广泛开展了劳动竞赛，在今年头八个月完成的工作量，超过去年同一时期的大约百分之八。
    交通企业也以优良成绩完成了八个月的计划。公路运输工人完成生产指标的百分之一百一十二点四。铁路青年志愿人员以高度的热情进行工作，到目前为止已铺好了二十公里的路基和十一公里铁轨。
    贸易企业和贸易机构已经完成了销货计划的百分之一百零二点一。为了完成劳动党第五次代表大会交给他们的任务，他们已经为人民提供了比去年同期多百分之六点七的商品。（附图片）
