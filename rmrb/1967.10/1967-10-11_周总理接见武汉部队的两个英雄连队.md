Premier Zhou Receives Two Hero Companies from the Wuhan Military Region

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, October 10th - Premier Zhou Enlai today received the entire company of the hero company where the late combat hero Huang Jiguang served, and the 8th Company which was awarded the first-class merit in the Battle of Upper Gangling, as well as some leading cadres from the Wuhan Military Region.

During the reception, the soldiers waved copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and repeatedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand, ten thousand years!"

Present at the reception were Wu Faxian, Commander of the People's Liberation Army Air Force, and leaders of the Wuhan Military Region, Zhang Siyuan and Liu Feng, among others. After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai posed for a group photo with the soldiers.

On October 10th, Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, received the entire company of the hero company where the late combat hero Huang Jiguang served, and the 8th Company which was awarded the first-class merit in the Battle of Upper Gangling, as well as some leading cadres from the Wuhan Military Region. During the reception, the soldiers waved copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and excitedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand, ten thousand years!"

(Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian, telephoto)

### 周总理接见武汉部队的两个英雄连队

1967-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见武汉部队的两个英雄连队
    新华社武汉十日电　周恩来总理今天在武汉接见了中国人民解放军驻武汉部队某部战斗英雄黄继光生前所在连和在上甘岭战役中荣获特等功的八连的全体同志，以及驻武汉部队的部分负责干部。
    接见时，指战员们挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，一遍又一遍地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”
    接见时在场的，有中国人民解放军空军司令员吴法宪同志以及武汉部队负责人曾思玉、刘丰同志等。接见后，周恩来总理和指战员们合影留念。（附图片）
    中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来同志十月十日在武汉接见了中国人民解放军驻武汉部队某部战斗英雄黄继光生前所在连和在上甘岭战役中荣获特等功的八连的全体同志，以及驻武汉部队的部分负责干部。接见时，指战员们挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”
