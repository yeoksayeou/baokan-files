Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets Sudanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today received the delegation of the Sudan-China Friendship Association, led by Chairman of the Sudan-China Friendship Association Abdel Makid Abdel Rahim and Secretary-General of the Khartoum Sudan-China Friendship Association Mirghani Ali Mustafa. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见苏丹客人

1967-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见苏丹客人
    新华社三日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了由迈达尼苏丹—中国友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·马基德·阿卜杜勒·拉欣姆和喀土穆苏丹—中国友好协会秘书长米尔加尼·阿里·穆斯塔法率领的苏丹—中国友好协会代表团，同他们进行了友好的交谈。
