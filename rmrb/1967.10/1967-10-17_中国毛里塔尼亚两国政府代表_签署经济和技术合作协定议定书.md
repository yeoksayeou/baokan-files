Title: Representatives of the Governments of China and Mauritania Sign Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1967-10-17
Edition: 4

Representatives of the governments of the People's Republic of China and the Islamic Republic of Mauritania signed the protocol of the economic and technical cooperation agreement between the government of the People's Republic of China and the government of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania on the morning of the 14th in Nouakchott, the capital of Mauritania.

This agreement was signed in Beijing in February of this year.

### 中国毛里塔尼亚两国政府代表  签署经济和技术合作协定议定书

1967-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国毛里塔尼亚两国政府代表
　　签署经济和技术合作协定议定书
    新华社十五日讯　努瓦克肖特消息：中华人民共和国政府的代表和毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府的代表，十四日上午在毛里塔尼亚首都努瓦克肖特签署了中华人民共和国政府和毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府经济和技术合作协定的议定书。
    这个协定是今年二月在北京签订的。
