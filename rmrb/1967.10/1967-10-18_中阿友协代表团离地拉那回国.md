Title: Delegation of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association Leaves Tirana for Home
Author:
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 6

The delegation of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Minister of Defense, has concluded a three-week friendly visit to Albania. The delegation also participated in the celebrations marking the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Carrying with them the deep affection of the Albanian people for Chairman Mao, the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese people, the delegation departed from Tirana by air at noon on the 17th and returned home.

Those who came to the airport to see off the delegation included: Muftiu, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and First Secretary of the Tirana Municipal Committee; Kellezi, a candidate member of the Political Bureau and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association; Dum, a candidate member of the Political Bureau and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army; Chako, a member of the Central Committee and Director of the Political Department of the People's Army; Maliqi, Deputy Foreign Minister; and Treska, Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, along with several hundred people from various circles.

Today, the airport building was adorned with large portraits of the great leaders of the peoples of China and Albania, Chairman Mao and Comrade Hoxha, and a large banner reading "Long Live Albanian-Chinese Friendship!" At the airport, the crowd held up portraits of Chairman Mao and Comrade Hoxha, the national flags of the two countries, and banners reading "Long Live Albanian-Chinese Friendship!" They warmly saw off the Chinese guests.

Muftiu and other comrades escorted Wang Shusheng and others to the aircraft door, shook hands, embraced, and bid a reluctant farewell. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the guests.

Those who went to the airport to see off the delegation also included Li Tingquan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Military Attaché Li Yifan.

### 中阿友协代表团离地拉那回国

1967-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　中阿友协代表团离地拉那回国
    新华社地拉那十七日电　由中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领的中阿友协代表团在对阿尔巴尼亚进行了三个星期的友好访问，并参加了阿尔巴尼亚人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年的庆祝活动以后，满载着阿尔巴尼亚人民对中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席、中国共产党和中国人民的深情厚谊，于十七日中午乘飞机离开地拉那回国。
    到机场热烈欢送代表团的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、地拉那市委第一书记穆夫蒂乌，政治局候补委员、阿中友协主席凯莱齐，政治局候补委员、人民军总参谋长杜米，中央委员、人民军政治部主任恰科，外交部副部长马利列，对外文委主席特雷斯卡等，以及各界群众数百人。
    今天，机场大厦上高悬中阿两国人民的伟大领袖毛主席和霍查同志的巨幅画像和“阿中友谊万岁！”的巨幅标语。在机场上，欢送的群众手中高举着毛主席和霍查同志的画像、中阿两国国旗和“阿中友谊万岁！”等标语牌，热烈欢送中国客人。
    穆夫蒂乌等同志把王树声等同志送到机舱旁，同他们紧紧握手拥抱，依依惜别。一群少先队员向客人们献了鲜花。
    前往机场欢送代表团的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃、武官李一凡等。
