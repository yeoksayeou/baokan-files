Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Delegations from Congo (Brazzaville) and Pakistan
Author: Qian Sijie, Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 5

	Top left image: Our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao on October 3rd received the First Secretary, Prime Minister, and Head of Government of the National Revolutionary Movement of the Congo (Brazzaville) Ambrus Numa Zalay and the entire delegation of the National Revolutionary Movement and Government of the Congo (Brazzaville) led by him, as well as the entire delegation of the Youth Organization of the National Revolutionary Movement of the Congo (Brazzaville) led by Ganza Amil.		Xinhua News Agency Photographer Qian Sijie

	Top image: Chairman Mao and Prime Minister Numa Zalay warmly shake hands. Xinhua News Agency Photographer Qian Sijie

	Left image: Our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao on October 3rd received the Minister of Information and Broadcasting of Pakistan, Haji Shahabuddin (front left four), and the entire delegation of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation led by him.		Reporter Lü Xiangyou
