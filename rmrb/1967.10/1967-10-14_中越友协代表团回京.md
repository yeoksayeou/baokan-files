Title: Delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-10-14
Edition: 6

A delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, led by Yang Dezhi, was invited to participate in the activities celebrating the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Vietnam and conducted a friendly visit to Vietnam. Today, they returned to Beijing by plane, carrying the deep affection of the Vietnamese people for the Chinese people.

Relevant officials Li Tianyou, Han Nianlong, Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

The Vietnamese Embassy's Counselor Le Zhongshi and Military Attaché Chen Wenpeng also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 中越友协代表团回京

1967-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　中越友协代表团回京
    新华社十三日讯　由杨得志率领的中越友协代表团，应邀参加了越南人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年活动，并在越南进行了友好访问之后，满载越南人民对中国人民的深情厚谊，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    有关方面负责人李天佑、韩念龙、楚图南、丁西林等前往迎接。
    越南驻中国大使馆参赞黎终始，武官陈文彭等也前往迎接。
