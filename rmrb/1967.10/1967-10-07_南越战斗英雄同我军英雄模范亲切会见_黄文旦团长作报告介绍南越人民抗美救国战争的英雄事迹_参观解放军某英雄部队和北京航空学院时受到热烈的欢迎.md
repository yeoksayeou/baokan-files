Title: South Vietnamese Combat Heroes Meet with Chinese Military Heroes and Exemplars
Author:
Date: 1967-10-07
Edition: 4

South Vietnamese combat heroes met with Chinese military heroes and exemplars. Comrade Huang Wendan, leader of the delegation, gave a report introducing the heroic deeds of the South Vietnamese people in their anti-American national salvation war. They were warmly welcomed during their visit to a heroic unit of the People's Liberation Army and the Beijing Institute of Aeronautics.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on October 5th, a delegation led by combat hero Huang Wendan from the front lines of the anti-American struggle in South Vietnam, consisting of battle heroes and American-killing heroes of the National Liberation Front, met with heroes and exemplars of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and active learners of Chairman Mao's works in the afternoon. They also held a discussion.

Heroes and exemplars of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as active learners of Chairman Mao's works, have long been eager to meet their South Vietnamese comrades-in-arms to learn from them. In the afternoon, when the Vietnamese comrades arrived at the meeting place, renowned active learner of Chairman Mao's works, Liao Chujian, Zhang Chunyu, deputy squad leader of a certain unit of the Railway Corps who was awarded the title of "Iron-Road Corps Hard-Boned Soldier," and Bin Ruowen and Liu Yingjun, who were awarded the first-class combat meritorious medals for bravely shooting down an invading American-made U-2 aircraft on September 8th this year, as well as the hero Nian Siwang, all went forward to shake hands with the Vietnamese comrades, expressing warm welcome.

At the discussion, Huang Wendan, who had participated in more than 140 battles and annihilated more than 100 American aggressors, and the outstanding American-killing hero, 18-year-old female soldier Wu Shixue, and the American-killing hero Li Zhiyuan, detailed their battle exploits and the great victories achieved by the South Vietnamese people in their anti-American national salvation war through people's war.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on October 6th, the delegation led by combat hero Huang Wendan from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam visited a heroic unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Beijing. Huang Wendan, the leader of the delegation, enthusiastically said at the end of the visit that the armed forces of South Vietnam are proud to have the Chinese People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, as their comrades-in-arms.

This morning, when the South Vietnamese heroes who had killed Americans came to the garrison of this heroic unit, they were warmly welcomed by the officers and soldiers.

The South Vietnamese comrades participated in a study session on Chairman Mao's works here. The officers, soldiers, and livestock keepers of this unit introduced how they actively learn and apply Chairman Mao's works, eliminate selfishness and establish public spirit, and uphold the absolute authority of Mao Zedong Thought, serving the people wholeheartedly. The South Vietnamese heroes were moved by these deeds and highly praised the officers and soldiers of this heroic unit for their boundless love for Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought.

The South Vietnamese combat heroes from the front lines of the anti-American national salvation war watched infantry live-fire shooting and other military performances, as well as the rice fields, rice processing factories, weapon repair factories, and pig farms operated by the unit.

Comrade Huang Wendan, at the invitation, gave a report to the officers and soldiers, introducing the heroic deeds of the South Vietnamese people in their anti-American national salvation war.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on October 5th, the delegation led by Comrade Huang Wendan from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam visited the Beijing Institute of Aeronautics and was warmly welcomed.

At the welcoming ceremony, Comrade Huang Wendan spoke. He said that the Vietnamese people have the determination and strength to defeat the American aggressors until there is not a single American on Vietnamese soil.

At the ceremony, the Vietnamese comrades presented precious gifts brought from the anti-American front lines to the Beijing Institute of Aeronautics. The responsible person of the Beijing Institute of Aeronautics presented Chairman Mao badges and red armbands to the Vietnamese comrades. After the ceremony, the Vietnamese comrades and revolutionary teachers and students representatives had a friendly discussion and also viewed the revolutionary big-character posters and big-character reports.

### 南越战斗英雄同我军英雄模范亲切会见  黄文旦团长作报告介绍南越人民抗美救国战争的英雄事迹  参观解放军某英雄部队和北京航空学院时受到热烈的欢迎

1967-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    南越战斗英雄同我军英雄模范亲切会见
    黄文旦团长作报告介绍南越人民抗美救国战争的英雄事迹  参观解放军某英雄部队和北京航空学院时受到热烈的欢迎
    据新华社五日讯　由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的来自反美斗争最前线的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团的战斗英雄和歼美勇士，今天下午同中国人民解放军的英雄模范、学习毛主席著作积极分子亲切会见，并举行座谈。
    中国人民解放军的英雄模范和学习毛主席著作积极分子，早就渴望见到越南南方的英雄战友们，向他们学习。下午，当越南战友来到会见地点时，著名的学习毛主席著作积极分子廖初江，荣获“铁道兵硬骨头战士”光荣称号的铁道兵某部副班长张春玉，以及在今年九月八日英勇击落入侵美制蒋匪U—2型飞机的一等战斗功臣宾若文和刘英俊式的英雄年四旺等，迎上前去，同越南战友亲切握手，表示热烈欢迎。在座谈会上，曾经参加过一百四十多次战斗，消灭了一百多名美国侵略军的战斗英雄黄文旦，优秀歼美勇士、年仅十八岁的女战士吴氏雪和歼美勇士黎志愿等，详细介绍了他们的战斗事迹，和越南南方人民在抗美救国战争中运用人民战争取得的伟大胜利。
    据新华社六日讯　由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，今天参观了中国人民解放军驻北京某英雄部队。黄文旦团长在参观结束时热情地说，越南南方解放武装力量，为有用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民解放军做为自己的战友，感到无比的自豪。
    今天上午，当越南南方歼美杀敌英雄们来到某英雄部队驻地时，受到指战员们极其热烈的欢迎。
    越南南方战友们在这里参加了毛主席著作学习讲用会。这支部队的干部、战士和饲养员，介绍了他们如何活学活用毛主席著作，破私立公，树立毛泽东思想的绝对权威，完全彻底为人民服务的事迹。越南南方的英雄们为这些事迹所感动，他们热烈赞扬这支英雄部队的干部和战士无限热爱毛主席，无限热爱毛泽东思想。
    来自抗美救国战争前线的越南南方的战斗英雄们，观看了步兵实弹射击等军事表演，以及部队经营的稻田、稻米加工厂、武器修备厂、养猪场等。
    黄文旦团长应邀向指战员们作报告，介绍了越南南方人民抗美救国战争的英雄事迹。
    据新华社五日讯　黄文旦团长率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，今天上午访问了北京航空学院，受到热烈欢迎。
    黄文旦团长在欢迎会上讲了话。他说，越南人民有决心和力量打败美国侵略者，直到越南土地上没有一个美国鬼子为止。
    会上，越南战友把从反美前线带来的珍贵礼品送给北京航空学院。北京航空学院负责人向越南战友赠送了毛主席像章和红袖章。会后，越南战友和革命师生代表一起亲切座谈，还观看了革命的大批判专栏和大字报。
