Title: Burmese Patriotic Overseas Chinese Hold Rally to Celebrate National Festival
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 7

YANGON, Oct. 2 (Xinhua) — On the occasion of the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the motherland, more than 1,000 representatives of patriotic overseas Chinese in Burma held a rally in Yangon on the morning of October 2 to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Chargé d'affaires Xiao Ming of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, along with other embassy staff, Chinese experts in Burma, and representatives from other Chinese institutions, joined the patriotic overseas Chinese in celebrating this grand festival.

At the rally, patriotic overseas Chinese sang "The East Is Red" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" and repeatedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

They wished our great leader Chairman Mao a long life and good health!

In his speech, Xiao Ming conveyed the joyous news of Chairman Mao's inspection of North China, Central China, and East China during the great cultural revolution to the overseas Chinese. He also introduced the excellent situation facing the great motherland. In his speech, he also condemned the Burmese reactionaries for instigating an anti-China and anti-Chinese trend.

Representatives from various sectors of the patriotic overseas Chinese expressed their warmest congratulations on the great achievements made by the motherland in the past 18 years under the guidance of the great leader Chairman Mao in the socialist revolution and socialist construction.

At the rally, patriotic overseas Chinese youth performed programs praising Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought. They sang in unison, "With firm resolve, fearing no sacrifice, overcoming all difficulties, and striving for victory."

### 缅甸爱国华侨集会欢庆祖国的佳节  肖明代办在会上谴责缅甸反动派掀起反华排华逆流

1967-10-03
第7版()
专栏：

    缅甸爱国华侨集会欢庆祖国的佳节
    肖明代办在会上谴责缅甸反动派掀起反华排华逆流
    新华社二日讯　仰光消息：在祖国的无产阶级文化大革命取得伟大胜利的时候，缅甸爱国华侨代表一千多人一日上午在仰光集会，欢庆中华人民共和国成立十八周年。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办肖明和大使馆的其他成员、在缅甸的中国专家代表和其他中国机构的代表同爱国侨胞一起欢庆这个盛大的节日。
    在集会上，爱国华侨齐唱《东方红》和《大海航行靠舵手》，一次又一次地高呼：毛主席万岁！万万岁！
    敬祝我们的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
    肖明在集会上讲话时，把我们伟大领袖毛主席在祖国无产阶级文化大革命形势空前大好的时候视察华北、中南和华东地区的喜讯告诉侨胞，并且向侨胞们介绍了伟大祖国面临的一片大好形势。他在讲话中还谴责缅甸反动派发动反华排华逆流。
    爱国华侨的各界代表在讲话中对祖国十八年来遵循着伟大领袖毛主席所指出的方向，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就表示最热烈的祝贺。
    在集会上，爱国华侨少年表演了歌颂毛主席和毛泽东思想的节目。他们齐唱：“下定决心，不怕牺牲，排除万难，去争取胜利。”
