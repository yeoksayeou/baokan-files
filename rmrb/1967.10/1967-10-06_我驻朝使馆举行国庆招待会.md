Title: The Chinese Embassy in North Korea Holds a National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-10-06
Edition: 6

	The Chinese Embassy in North Korea held a reception.

	Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang - On the evening of September 30, the Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Wang Peng, held a reception to enthusiastically celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Vice Premiers Li Zhongyu and Zheng Zhunze of the North Korean Cabinet, Vice Chairman Bai Nan Yun of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Chairman Xu Zhe of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy Foreign Minister Kim Jae-bong, Deputy Minister of People's Armed Forces Choi Byung-hak, and Deputy Minister of Trade Hong Hyong-bok attended the reception at the invitation. Ambassadors from various countries in North Korea also attended the reception at the invitation.

	Wang Peng, the Charge d'Affaires, and Vice Premier Li Zhongyu both spoke at the reception.

	Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang - On the evening of September 29, the Korea-China Friendship Association held a reception to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Those who attended the reception included Vice Chairman Bai Nan Yun of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Deputy Foreign Minister Kim Jae-bong, and Vice Chairman Kim Kwang-saek of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

	Wang Peng, the Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and others attended the reception at the invitation.

	On the evening of September 28, the Korea-China Friendship Cooperative Farm in Juk-an-ri, Sun-an-gun also held a celebration event.

### 我驻朝使馆举行国庆招待会

1967-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻朝使馆举行国庆招待会
    新华社平壤电　中国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆临时代办王彭九月三十日晚上举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。
    朝鲜内阁副首相李钟玉、郑准泽，最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长白南云，对外文化联络委员会委员长徐哲，外务省副相金载凤，民族保卫省副相池炳学上将，贸易省副相洪铉八等应邀出席了招待会。各国驻朝使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    王彭临时代办和李钟玉副首相分别在招待会上讲了话。
    新华社平壤电　朝中友好协会九月二十九日晚上举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。
    出席招待会的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长白南云、外务省副相金载凤、对外文化联络委员会副委员长金光燮等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭等应邀出席了招待会。
    二十八日晚上，顺安郡宅安里朝中友谊合作农场也举行了庆祝活动。
