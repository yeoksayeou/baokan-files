	Mao Zedong Quotations
	The Communist Party and people of China and Albania should unite, all Marxist-Leninists throughout the world should unite, and all revolutionary people throughout the world should unite. We must overthrow imperialism, overthrow modern revisionism, and overthrow all reactionaries. A new world without imperialism, without capitalism, and without systems of exploitation must be established.

专栏：

　　毛主席语录
    中阿两党、两国人民团结起来，全世界马克思列宁主义者团结起来，全世界革命人民团结起来，打倒帝国主义，打倒现代修正主义，打倒各国反动派。一个没有帝国主义、没有资本主义、没有剥削制度的新世界，一定要建立起来。
                      给阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的贺电
