Title: The Economy and Politics in the Era of Proletarian Dictatorship
Author: Liénǐn
Date: 1967-10-25
Edition: 4

Under the dictatorship of the proletariat, the exploiting classes, namely the landlord and capitalist classes, have not yet disappeared and cannot disappear all at once. The exploiters have been defeated, but they have not yet been eliminated. Their resistance is strengthened a thousandfold precisely because of their defeat.
