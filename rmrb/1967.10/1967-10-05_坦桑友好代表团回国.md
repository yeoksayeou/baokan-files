Title: Tanzanian Friendship Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1967-10-05
Edition: 8

By Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd

The Tanzanian friendship delegation, led by the Minister of Land, Settlement, and Water Development, Babu, returned home by plane today. At the airport, the Tanzanian guests and the revolutionary masses of the capital who came to see them off studied Chairman Mao's quotations together and shouted in unison: "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Tanzania!" "Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!"

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water and Electric Power Du Xingyuan, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Xun Xiang, and others. Ambassador Mwaluko also came to the airport to see them off.

### 坦桑友好代表团回国

1967-10-05
第8版()
专栏：

    坦桑友好代表团回国
    据新华社三日讯　由坦桑尼亚土地、定居和水利开发部长巴布率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。在机场上，坦桑尼亚客人和前来欢送的首都革命群众一起学习毛主席语录，一同高呼：“中国坦桑尼亚两国人民的友谊万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”“伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万万岁！”到机场欢送的，有水电部副部长杜星垣，外交部部长助理宦乡等。姆瓦卢科大使也到机场欢送。
