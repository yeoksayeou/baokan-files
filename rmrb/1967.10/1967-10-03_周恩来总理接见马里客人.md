Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Guests from Mali
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou Enlai received guests from Mali this afternoon. The guests were the Director of the Office of the President of Mali, Bakary Diallo, and his wife. Zhou Enlai had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were relevant officials from our side, as well as the Chargé d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China, Senanta.

October 2, Premier Zhou Enlai received the Director of the Office of the President of Mali, Bakary Diallo (second from the left), and his wife. The photo shows them posing together during the reception. Photographed by our reporter Wang Dong.

### 周恩来总理接见马里客人

1967-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见马里客人
    新华社二日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了马里总统府办公厅主任巴卡拉·迪亚洛和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，我有关方面的负责人，以及马里驻中国大使馆临时代办塞南塔在座。（附图片）
    十月二日，周恩来总理接见了马里总统府办公厅主任巴卡拉·迪亚洛（左二）和夫人。图为接见时合影。本报记者　王东摄
