Title: Comrade Xie Hu Visits Qingdao to Watch Naval Performance; Qingdao Revolutionary Committee Hosts Banquet for Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1967-10-08
Edition: 3

	With Comrade Xie Hu at the head, the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Kang Sheng, Liu Xiao, Wang Xiaoyu, and the responsible comrades of the naval and land forces stationed in Qingdao, Yi Yaocai and Li Shuqing, visited the naval forces stationed in Qingdao this afternoon and watched a splendid naval performance. They were warmly welcomed.

	After the visit, a grand welcoming ceremony was held on the wharf. After the responsible comrade of the Qingdao naval forces, Yi Yaocai, delivered a welcome speech, Comrade Xie Hu made an impassioned speech to the naval officers and soldiers gathered on the wharf. He highly praised the powerful People's Liberation Army Navy of China for turning the great thoughts of Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong into material force and achieving great success in completing the tasks assigned by Chairman Mao. He was extremely pleased with the great role played by the great army founded by Chairman Mao in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Comrade Xie Hu sincerely wished the naval officers and soldiers to strengthen their combat effectiveness and achieve greater victories in completing the tasks assigned by the great leader, the founder of the People's Liberation Army, Chairman Mao.

	Comrade Xie Hu also conveyed, amidst thunderous applause, the fraternal sentiments of the Albanian people and the Albanian People's Army towards the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army.

	After his speech, Comrade Xie Hu presented a red banner symbolizing the eternal friendship between the parties, peoples, and armies of China and Albania to the People's Liberation Army naval forces stationed in Qingdao.

	This morning, some members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Comrades Kang Sheng, Liu Xiao, Wang Xiaoyu, and Yang Baohua, visited the Fourth Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory, the First Woodworking Factory in Qingdao, and the Li Jia Xia Village Brigade in the suburban Laoshan County.

	The Qingdao Municipal Revolutionary Committee held a grand banquet this evening to warmly welcome the comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Xie Hu.

	Present at the banquet were Comrade Kang Sheng, Comrade Liu Xiao, Comrade Wang Xiaoyu, and the responsible comrades of the Qingdao Municipal Revolutionary Committee, the land and naval forces stationed in Qingdao, and various revolutionary mass organizations.

	The banquet hall was filled with the atmosphere of close unity between the two parties and peoples of China and Albania. At the beginning of the banquet, Yang Baohua, the main responsible comrade of the Qingdao Municipal Revolutionary Committee, delivered a speech. He, on behalf of the Qingdao Municipal Revolutionary Committee and the people of the entire city, extended the warmest welcome and deepest thanks to the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Xie Hu. He also paid high tribute to the great leader of the Albanian people, the close friend of the Chinese people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, to the Albanian Party of Labor, and to the heroic Albanian people.

	Comrade Xie Hu spoke amidst warm applause. He pointed out that Albania has always supported the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and will continue to do so. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is also the revolution of the Albanian people and the revolution of all the people of the world. Comrade Xie Hu said that the great achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China inspire the Albanian people to carry out more profound revolutionary struggles. He sincerely wished the Chinese people greater victories under the brilliant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought.

	Some members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation visited the Qingdao Cotton Mill No. 9 this afternoon and were warmly welcomed by thousands of revolutionary workers from the entire mill.

	The entire mill held a grand and enthusiastic welcoming ceremony for the Albanian guests. Comrade Tonin Jakova, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Secretary-General of the Central Council of Trade Unions, and member of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, and Wang Xiaoyu, Chairman of the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee, delivered speeches at the meeting. They warmly praised the fighting friendship and unity between the peoples of China and Albania.

### 谢胡同志在青岛观看海军表演  青岛市革命委员会欢宴阿尔巴尼亚战友

1967-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志在青岛观看海军表演
    青岛市革命委员会欢宴阿尔巴尼亚战友
    据新华社青岛七日电　以谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，今天下午在康生、刘晓、王效禹和驻青岛海、陆军负责人易耀彩、李水清等同志的陪同下，访问了驻青岛海军部队，观看了精彩的海上表演，受到了极其热烈的欢迎。
    访问结束后，码头上举行了隆重的欢迎仪式，在驻青岛海军负责人易耀彩致欢迎词后，谢胡同志向聚集在码头的海军指战员们发表了热情洋溢的讲话。他高度赞扬强大的中国人民解放军海军，把伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛泽东思想化为物质力量，在完成毛主席交付的任务中取得了巨大的成就。他对毛主席缔造的伟大的军队，在无产阶级文化大革命中发挥的巨大作用，感到无比的高兴。谢胡同志衷心地祝愿海军指战员们在加强自己的战斗力，完成伟大领袖、中国人民解放军的缔造者毛主席提出的任务中取得更大的胜利。
    谢胡同志还在暴风雨般的掌声中转达了阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民军，对中国人民和中国人民解放军的兄弟情谊。
    谢胡同志在讲话以后，向人民解放军驻青岛海军部队赠送了一面象征中阿两党、两国人民和军队永恒友谊的红色锦旗。
    今天上午，阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团部分成员，由康生、刘晓、王效禹、杨保华等同志陪同，分别参观访问了四方机车车辆工厂，青岛第一木器厂和郊区崂山县中韩人民公社李家下庄大队。
    据新华社青岛七日电　青岛市革命委员会今晚举行盛大宴会，热烈欢迎以谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的战友们。
    出席宴会作陪的有：康生同志，刘晓同志，王效禹同志，以及青岛市革命委员会、驻青岛陆海军部队和各革命群众组织的负责人。
    宴会大厅洋溢着中阿两党、两国人民亲密团结的气氛。宴会开始，青岛市革命委员会主要负责人杨保华首先致词，他代表青岛市革命委员会和全市人民，向以谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团表示最热烈的欢迎和最衷心的感谢，并向阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友恩维尔·霍查同志，向阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，向英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民，致以崇高的无产阶级文化大革命的敬礼。
    谢胡同志在热烈的掌声中讲话。他指出，阿尔巴尼亚始终支持无产阶级文化大革命，并且支持到底。中国无产阶级文化大革命，也是阿尔巴尼亚人民的革命，也是全世界人民的革命。谢胡同志说，中国无产阶级文化大革命的巨大成就，鼓舞着阿尔巴尼亚人民更深入地进行革命斗争，他衷心地祝愿中国人民在毛泽东思想光辉照耀下，取得更大的胜利。
    据新华社青岛六日电　阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团部分成员今天下午参观访问了青岛国棉九厂，受到全厂数千名革命职工极其热烈的欢迎。
    全厂职工为阿尔巴尼亚客人举行了热烈隆重的欢迎大会。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、工会中央理事会总书记、阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团成员托宁·雅科瓦和山东省革命委员会主任王效禹在会上讲了话。他们热烈赞扬中阿两国人民的战斗友谊和团结。
