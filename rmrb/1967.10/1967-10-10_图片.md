Title: Military and Revolutionary Masses of Wuhan Welcome Albanian Delegation
Author: Yu Chengjian
Date: 1967-10-10
Edition: 4

    The People's Liberation Army of China in Wuhan and the revolutionary masses of Wuhan held a grand meeting on October 9 to warmly welcome the Albanian Party and Government delegation led by Comrade Xhafer Spasiu. The image shows the People's Liberation Army of China in Wuhan and the revolutionary masses of Wuhan presenting a flag to the Albanian Party and Government delegation led by Comrade Xhafer Spasiu.

    Xinhua News Agency Photographer: Yu Chengjian (Telephoto)
