Title: Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade Comrade Lin Biao Receive Friends from Albania, Vietnam, and Luxembourg

Author:

Date: 1967-10-08

Edition: 1

The great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade Comrade Lin Biao received friends from Albania, Vietnam, and Luxembourg.

Comrade Zhou Enlai and Comrade Chen Boda participated in the reception.

Chairman Mao and Comrade Lin Biao, along with others, briskly entered the reception hall, instantly creating a scene of joy and thunderous applause. The Albanian comrades, overcome with emotion, repeatedly shouted, "Mao Zedong, Mao Zedong," "Marxism-Leninism"; the Vietnamese comrades enthusiastically and rhythmically shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao," "Long live Chairman Mao"; the Luxembourg friends, with deep feelings, said, "We wish Chairman Mao eternal life!"

A Xinhua News Agency report dated October 7 states: Our most beloved great leader, the red sun in the hearts of the revolutionary people of the world, Chairman Mao, along with his close comrade Comrade Lin Biao, as well as Comrades Zhou Enlai and Chen Boda, received friends from Albania, Vietnam, and Luxembourg who are visiting Beijing. The great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao and Comrade Lin Biao, briskly entered the brightly lit reception hall, joyfully meeting the Albanian comrades from the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist front, the Vietnamese comrades from the anti-American armed struggle front, and the Luxembourg friends. Instantly, the reception hall was filled with joy and thunderous applause. The Albanian comrades waved their red "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," repeatedly shouting, "Mao Zedong, Mao Zedong," "Marxism-Leninism," "Friendship is the guarantee"; the Vietnamese comrades enthusiastically applauded, rhythmically shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao," "Long live Chairman Mao"; the Luxembourg friends held "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," deeply saying, "We wish Chairman Mao eternal life!"

Subsequently, Chairman Mao, Comrade Lin Biao, Comrade Zhou Enlai, and Comrade Chen Boda took group photos with the friends from various countries. Those who accompanied the reception included Comrades Nie Rongzhen, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, and Wang Dongxing. The foreign friends who were received included: the "One Hand with a Pickaxe, One Hand with a Gun" amateur art troupe from Tirana, Albania, led by Mu. Gerchali; the Albanian news delegation led by Na. Nataneili; the Albanian consumer cooperative delegation led by Lei. Chailai; the delegation of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association and Albanian printing experts led by Lai. Biduo; the delegation of the intermediate department of universities in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam led by Chen Song; the delegation of the Vietnam News Agency led by Tao Song; the delegation of Vietnamese journalists led by Li Min; the Vietnamese Democratic Republic acrobatic troupe led by Wu Yuying, and representatives of the Vietnamese Light Industry Company; and the delegation of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association led by Adolf Frank.

### 伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志接见了阿尔巴尼亚、越南和卢森堡的朋友

1967-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志接见了阿尔巴尼亚、越南和卢森堡的朋友
    周恩来同志陈伯达同志一起参加接见
    毛主席和林彪同志等健步来到接见大厅，顿时一片欢腾，掌声雷动。阿尔巴尼亚同志激动地一遍又一遍地高呼：“毛泽东、毛泽东”、“马克思列宁主义”；越南同志热烈地有节奏地高呼：“万岁，毛主席”、“万岁，毛主席”；卢森堡朋友深情地说：“我们祝愿毛主席万寿无疆！”新华社七日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖、世界革命人民心中的红太阳毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志，以及周恩来同志、陈伯达同志，今天下午接见了正在北京访问的阿尔巴尼亚、越南和卢森堡的朋友。伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席和林彪同志等，健步来到灯火辉煌的接见大厅，十分高兴地会见来自反帝反修前线的阿尔巴尼亚同志，来自反美武装斗争前线的越南同志以及卢森堡的朋友。顿时，接见大厅里一片欢腾，掌声雷动。阿尔巴尼亚同志们激动地挥动红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，一遍又一遍地高呼：“毛泽东、毛泽东”、“马克思列宁主义”、“友谊就是保证”；越南同志们热烈鼓掌，有节奏地高呼：“万岁，毛主席”、“万岁，毛主席”；卢森堡朋友手捧《毛主席语录》，深情地说：“我们祝愿毛主席万寿无疆！”随后，毛主席和林彪同志、周恩来同志、陈伯达同志，同各国朋友分别合影。陪同接见的，有聂荣臻、谢富治、杨成武、粟裕、汪东兴等同志。被接见的各国朋友有：由穆·格尔查利率领的阿尔巴尼亚地拉那“一手拿镐、一手拿枪”业余艺术团，由纳·纳塔奈利率领的阿尔巴尼亚新闻代表团，由列伊兹·柴里拉伊率领的阿尔巴尼亚消费合作社代表团，由莱·培多率领的阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会代表团和阿尔巴尼亚印刷专家；由陈宋率领的越南民主共和国大学中专部代表团，由陶松率领的越南通讯社代表团，由黎民率领的越南新闻工作者代表团，由吴玉英率领的越南民主共和国杂技团，和越南轻工业品公司代表；由阿道夫·弗兰克率领的卢森堡—中国友好协会代表团。（附图片）
