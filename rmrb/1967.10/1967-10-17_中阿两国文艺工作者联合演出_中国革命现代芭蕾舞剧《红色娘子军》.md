Title: Joint Performance by Chinese and Albanian Artists: The Revolutionary Modern Ballet "Red Detachment of Women"
Author:
Date: 1967-10-17
Edition: 4

Joint Performance by Chinese and Albanian Artists:
The Revolutionary Modern Ballet "Red Detachment of Women"

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, October 16 -- The Albanian amateur art troupe "One Hand Holding a Pickaxe, One Hand Holding a Gun" and the Chinese Workers', Peasants', and Soldiers' Ballet Troupe jointly performed the revolutionary modern ballet "Red Detachment of Women" in the capital yesterday and today. The performance was enthusiastically welcomed by the revolutionary masses of the capital.

At the end of today's performance, the artists from both countries sang "The Internationale" with the entire audience and shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" Many spectators said after watching the performance that the joint performance by the artists of the two countries demonstrated that the combat friendship between the peoples of China and Albania is higher than the Himalayas and deeper than the Eastern Sea.

After the performance, the artists from both countries gathered together to discuss their impressions. The Albanian actor Z. Hazo, who played Wu Qinghua, and the participant A. Ali Aj said excitedly that through the joint performance, it is clear that the peoples of Albania and China are true friends because we share the same ideals. We will continue to fight for these ideals. The Chinese actors expressed their determination to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and to forever fight alongside the Albanian people, achieving victory together.

### 中阿两国文艺工作者联合演出  中国革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》

1967-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　中阿两国文艺工作者联合演出
　　中国革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》
    新华社十六日讯　阿尔巴尼亚地拉那“一手拿镐、一手拿枪”业余艺术团和中国工农兵芭蕾舞剧团，昨天和今天在首都联合演出中国革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》，受到首都革命群众极其热烈的欢迎。
    在今天演出结束时，中阿两国文艺工作者同全场观众一起高唱《国际歌》，高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”许多观众看了演出以后说，中阿两国文艺工作者的同台演出，显示了中阿两国人民的战斗友谊比喜马拉雅山还要高，比东海还要深。
    演出结束后，中阿两国文艺工作者聚集在一起畅谈演出的感想。扮演吴清华的阿尔巴尼亚演员佐·哈佐和参加演出的阿·阿利阿伊激动地说，通过同台演出，说明我们阿中两国人民是真正的朋友，因为我们有着共同的理想。我们要朝着这种理想继续战斗下去。中国演员表示要高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，永远同阿尔巴尼亚人民战斗在一起，胜利在一起。
