Title: As Israel Launches New War Provocations, the U.S. Impudently Announces Resumption of Arms Sales to Israel
Author:
Date: 1967-10-26
Edition: 5

As the U.S. imperialist tool, Israel, issued a new round of war cries, the U.S. State Department brazenly announced on the 24th that it would resume arms sales to Israel. On the same day, Israel launched a new large-scale military provocation against the United Arab Republic.

According to the 25th issue of the UAR's *Al-Ahram* newspaper, the United States will deliver fifty military aircraft to Israel.

This U.S. action came immediately after Israeli Foreign Minister Abba Eban held a secret meeting with U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk in Washington on the 23rd. On the 24th, U.S. President Lyndon B. Johnson also held a secret meeting with Eban that lasted over an hour.

Since the Israeli destroyer *Eilat* was sunk by the UAR navy on the 21st for violating UAR territorial waters, Israeli military and political leaders have been issuing war cries, threatening to "settle accounts" with the UAR. On the 23rd, U.S. State Department spokesman Robert McCloskey warned in a threatening tone that the UAR navy's sinking of the Israeli destroyer *Eilat* was a "lack of restraint" and a "regrettable and very unfortunate" action.

On the same day the U.S. announced its resumption of arms sales to Israel, Israeli forces shelled the Suez port and its refinery and oil storage facilities in the afternoon. The UAR armed forces retaliated, destroying ten Israeli tanks, five military vehicles, and other military equipment, and shooting down a jet aircraft.

### 在以色列发动新战争挑衅时  美帝悍然宣布恢复向以色列提供军火

1967-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    在以色列发动新战争挑衅时
    美帝悍然宣布恢复向以色列提供军火
    新华社二十五日讯　在美帝的侵略工具以色列发出一片新的战争叫嚣声中，美国国务院在二十四日悍然宣布恢复向以色列提供军火。就在同一天，以色列向阿联发动了新的大规模军事挑衅。
    据阿联《金字塔报》二十五日揭露，美国将交给以色列五十架军用飞机。
    美国这个行动，是紧接在以色列外交部长埃班二十三日到华盛顿同美国国务卿腊斯克举行秘密会谈后采取的。在二十四日，美国总统约翰逊又同埃班举行了一小时多的密谈。
    自以色列的驱逐舰“埃拉特号”在二十一日因侵入阿联领海被阿联海军击沉后，以色列军政头目不断发出战争叫嚣，叫嚷要同阿联“算账”。美国国务院发言人麦克洛斯基二十三日用威胁口吻说，阿联海军击沉以色列驱逐舰“埃拉特号”是“缺乏克制态度”的作法，是“可悲的、非常令人遗憾的”。
    就在美国宣布向以色列提供军火的同一天下午，以色列军队炮轰阿联苏伊士港以及这个城市的炼油厂和油库。阿联武装部队进行还击，击毁以色列十辆坦克、五辆军用车辆和其他一些军事装备，击落一架喷气式飞机。
