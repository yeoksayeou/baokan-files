Title: Welcome the Return of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation from Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-10-20
Edition: 5

The Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to warmly welcome the delegation led by Comrade Yang Dezhi, which had just returned from a visit to Vietnam.

At the reception, Comrade Yang Dezhi spoke, expressing gratitude to the Vietnamese comrades for their warm reception of the delegation. He also introduced the glorious victories achieved by the heroic Vietnamese people under the leadership of Chairman Ho Chi Minh in their struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation, as well as the touching stories of the Vietnamese people's love for our great leader Chairman Mao. He said that the delegation had participated in activities celebrating our national day in various places in Vietnam, and these activities fully demonstrated that the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam is unbreakable. He expressed that the Chinese people resolutely support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation until the complete defeat of the U.S. aggressors.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Quan attended the reception upon invitation and delivered a speech. He highly evaluated the significance of the visit to Vietnam by the delegation led by Comrade Yang Dezhi. He said that this visit further strengthened the fraternal friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China, and embodied the great support of the 700 million Chinese people, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation. Finally, he wished the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and socialist construction in China even greater victories.

Li Xilin, a member of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association, also spoke at the reception.

The reception featured screenings of films such as "A Shining Example, A Great Initiative," which praised the victories of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

Those present at the reception today also included relevant leaders such as Chu Tuonan, Han Nianlong, and representatives of the revolutionary masses of the proletariat.

Representatives from the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 欢迎中越友协代表团访越归来  中越友协举行招待会

1967-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　欢迎中越友协代表团访越归来
　　中越友协举行招待会
    新华社十九日讯　中越友协今天下午举行招待会，热烈欢迎由杨得志同志率领的中越友协代表团访越归来。
    杨得志同志在招待会上讲话，感谢越南同志对代表团的热情接待，并介绍了英雄的越南人民在胡志明主席的领导下所取得的抗美救国战争的辉煌胜利，以及越南人民热爱我们伟大领袖毛主席的动人事迹。他说，我们代表团在越南各地参加了庆祝我国国庆的活动，这些活动都充分地显示了中越两国人民的战斗友谊是牢不可破的。他表示中国人民坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争，直到彻底打败美国侵略者。
    越南驻中国大使吴明鸾应邀出席了招待会，并讲了话。他高度评价以杨得志同志为首的中越友协代表团访问越南的意义。他说，这次访问进一步加强了越中两国人民兄弟般的友谊，体现了在毛主席领导下的七亿中国人民对越南人民抗美救国战争的巨大支持。最后，他祝愿中国无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义建设取得更伟大的胜利。
    中越友协理事丁西林在招待会上也讲了话。
    招待会上放映了《光辉的榜样，伟大的创举》等歌颂毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利的影片。
    出席今天招待会的还有楚图南、韩念龙等有关方面负责人和无产阶级革命派代表。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黎松山也应邀出席了招待会。
