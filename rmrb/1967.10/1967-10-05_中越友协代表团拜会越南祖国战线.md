Title: Delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Calls on the Vietnam Fatherland Front
Author:
Date: 1967-10-05
Edition: 8

A delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, led by Yang Dezhi, paid visits to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and the Vietnam Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee on September 28. The delegation was warmly welcomed by Huang Guoyue, Sun Guanghuai, Chen Huiliang, Nguen Vanhuan, and He Gui, among other relevant officials from Vietnam.

In the evening, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association hosted a banquet in honor of the delegation.

The banquet was held in an atmosphere filled with the close friendship and fighting unity between the peoples of China and Vietnam.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, the Vietnam Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the Vietnam-China Friendship Association jointly held a film screening event on September 28 to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 中越友协代表团拜会越南祖国战线

1967-10-05
第8版()
专栏：

    中越友协代表团拜会越南祖国战线
    新华社河内电　由杨得志率领的中越友好协会代表团，九月二十八日先后拜会了越南祖国战线中央委员会、越中友好协会和越南对外文化联络委员会。代表团受到了黄国越、孙光阀、陈辉燎、阮文煊、何桂等越南有关方面负责人的热烈欢迎。
    晚间，越中友协设宴招待代表团。
    宴会是在充满中越两国人民的亲密友谊和战斗团结的气氛中进行的。
    据新华社河内电　越南对外文化联络委员会、文化部和越中友好协会九月二十八日联合举行电影招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。
