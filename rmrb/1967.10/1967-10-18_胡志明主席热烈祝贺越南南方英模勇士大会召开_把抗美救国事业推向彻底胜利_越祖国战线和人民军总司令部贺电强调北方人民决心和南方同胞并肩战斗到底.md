Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Warmly Congratulates the Opening of the Second Conference of Heroic Soldiers of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 5

Chairman Ho Chi Minh Warmly Congratulates the Opening of the Second Conference of Heroic Soldiers of South Vietnam

Push the Anti-American National Salvation Cause to Complete Victory

The Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and the General Headquarters of the People's Army Send Telegrams Emphasizing the Determination of the Northern People to Fight Side by Side with Their Southern Compatriots to the End

	NEW DELHI, October 17 - According to reports from local newspapers, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to warmly congratulate the opening of the Second Conference of Heroic Soldiers of the People's Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam during the conference period.

	In the telegram, Chairman Ho Chi Minh warmly praised the outstanding achievements of the heroic soldiers attending the conference in the anti-American national salvation cause.

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh pointed out, "The first conference of heroes held in 1965 was a conference to defeat the 'special war' of the American aggressors. This conference of heroes will be a conference to defeat their 'limited war' and realize the political program of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam."

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh affectionately encouraged the heroic soldiers to continue to make progress and become the locomotive leading people to create more brilliant new achievements.

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh pointed out, "The American aggressors have suffered a crushing defeat, but they are still obstinate and have not given up their plot to invade the South, bomb the North, and permanently divide our country. The anti-American national salvation war of our entire people will still encounter many difficulties, but our people will certainly defeat the American aggressors and their lackeys, liberate the South, defend the North, and ultimately achieve the peaceful reunification of the motherland."

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh expressed his firm belief that "under the glorious banner of the National Liberation Front, the southern compatriots and soldiers, with their heroic spirit and rich experience, will certainly achieve more and greater victories, pushing the anti-American national salvation cause to complete victory."

	NEW DELHI, October 17 - According to reports from local newspapers, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and the General Headquarters of the People's Army recently sent telegrams to warmly congratulate the opening of the Second Conference of Heroic Soldiers of the People's Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam.

	In the telegram, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front expressed its firm belief that the military and people of the South, under the wise leadership of the National Liberation Front, and with the ever-increasing support of the people of the world, will certainly advance to victory, defeat all the brutal and cunning plots of American imperialism and its lackeys, and achieve greater victories in the winter and spring of 1967-1968.

	The telegram emphasized that the 17 million compatriots in the North, regardless of the passage of time, have only one heart, standing side by side with their southern compatriots, resolutely fighting to the end to liberate the South, defend the North, and ultimately reunify the motherland.

	The General Headquarters of the People's Army stated in the telegram that all officers and soldiers of the armed forces in the North are determined to learn from your unyielding spirit, your readiness to sacrifice for the revolution at any time, and your brave and united spirit. Together with the 17 million compatriots in the North, we are determined to fully carry out the sacred duty of the rear to the front, stand side by side with the heroic military and people of the South, and resolutely and thoroughly defeat the American aggressors.

### 胡志明主席热烈祝贺越南南方英模勇士大会召开　把抗美救国事业推向彻底胜利　越祖国战线和人民军总司令部贺电强调北方人民决心和南方同胞并肩战斗到底

1967-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席热烈祝贺越南南方英模勇士大会召开
　　把抗美救国事业推向彻底胜利
　　越祖国战线和人民军总司令部贺电强调北方人民决心和南方同胞并肩战斗到底
    新华社河内十七日电　据此间报纸报道，越南民主共和国主席胡志明在越南南方人民解放武装力量举行第二届英模勇士大会期间，打电报热烈祝贺这个大会的召开。
    胡主席在电报中热烈赞扬出席大会的英模勇士们在抗美救国事业中创建的出色战功。
    胡主席指出，“一九六五年召开的南方第一届英雄大会，是战胜美国强盗的‘特种战争’的大会。这次英雄大会将是战胜它们的‘局部战争’，实现越南南方民族解放阵线的政治纲领的大会。”
    胡主席亲切地勉励英模勇士们不断进步，继续成为引导人们创立更多辉煌的新战功的火车头。
    胡主席指出，“美国强盗已经遭到惨败，但它仍然顽固，还未放弃侵略南方、轰炸北方、长期分割我国的阴谋。我国全体人民的抗美救国战争还会遇到许多困难，但我国人民必将战胜美国侵略强盗及其走狗，解放南方，保卫北方，进而和平统一祖国。”
    胡主席表示坚信，“南方同胞和战士们在民族解放阵线的光荣旗帜下，以自己的英勇精神和丰富的经验，必将取得更多更大的胜利，把抗美救国事业推向彻底胜利。”
    新华社河内十七日电　据此间报纸报道，越南祖国战线中央委员会和越南人民军总司令部，不久以前分别打电报热烈祝贺越南南方人民解放武装力量第二届英模勇士大会的召开。
    越南祖国战线中央委员会在电报中表示坚信：南方军民在民族解放阵线的英明领导下，在全世界人民的日益巨大的支援下，必将乘胜前进，战胜美帝国主义及其走狗的一切残暴和狡猾阴谋，在一九六七——一九六八年冬春季里取得更大的战功。
    电报强调说，越南北方一千七百万同胞，任凭海枯石烂，只有一条心，同南方同胞并肩在一起，坚决战斗到底，以解放南方，保卫北方，进而统一祖国。
    越南人民军总司令部在电报中说，北方人民武装力量全体干部和战士心向骨肉的南方，决心努力学习你们坚贞不屈、随时准备献身革命的榜样，以及勇敢、团结的精神。我们同一千七百万北方同胞一起，决心大力发扬革命英雄主义精神，全心全力完成大后方对大前线的神圣义务，同英雄的南方军民并肩在一起，坚决、彻底地战胜美国侵略强盗。
