Title: Yan'an Military and Civilians Hold Grand Gathering to Welcome Comrade Aliya and Others
Author:
Date: 1967-10-08
Edition: 3

Yan'an military and civilians held a grand gathering to welcome Comrade Aliya and others
Li Fuchun and Aliya speak, enthusiastically praising the fighting friendship between China and Albania

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, October 7th: The revolutionary masses of the revolutionary holy land of Yan'an and the officers and soldiers of the Yan'an Military Sub-district of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a grand meeting today at the former site of the Seventh National Congress of the Communist Party of China—the Central Hall of Yangjialing—to warmly welcome Ramiz Aliya, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, and the members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation.

	Comrades Li Fuchun, Liu Ningyi, Luo Guibo, and others attended the meeting. Comrades Li Fuchun and Aliya both spoke at the meeting (full text on page 4), enthusiastically praising the revolutionary traditions of the revolutionary holy land of Yan'an and enthusiastically extolling the fighting friendship and close unity between the two parties, two countries, and two peoples of China and Albania. Their speeches were received with extremely enthusiastic applause.

	Today's meeting was filled with a revolutionary atmosphere of proletarian internationalist struggle. The stage was adorned with a large banner reading "Warmly Welcome the Albanian Party and Government Delegation" and with placards bearing quotations from Chairman Mao and Comrade Hoxha. When Comrade Aliya and others arrived at the venue accompanied by Comrade Li Fuchun, the crowd inside and outside the venue was packed, forming a welcoming line, with cheers and applause echoing through the air.

	At the meeting, a responsible comrade from the Yan'an Military Sub-district of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of the Yan'an military and civilians, expressed a warm welcome to the Albanian comrades from the front lines of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism.

	During the meeting, Comrade Aliya, on behalf of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, presented a red banner symbolizing the great friendship between the peoples of China and Albania to the people of Yan'an. The people of Yan'an presented "Quotations from Chairman Mao," Mao badges, and Yan'an revolutionary commemorative badges to the Albanian comrades. At this moment, the entire venue repeatedly chanted, "Long live Marxism-Leninism! Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" At the end of the meeting, the entire venue sang the "Internationale" in unison.

	After the meeting, a welcome banquet was held for the Albanian comrades.

### 延安军民盛会欢迎阿利雅等同志  李富春阿利雅同志讲话，热烈颂扬中阿战斗友谊

1967-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    延安军民盛会欢迎阿利雅等同志
    李富春阿利雅同志讲话，热烈颂扬中阿战斗友谊
    据新华社延安七日电　革命圣地延安的革命群众和中国人民解放军延安军分区的指战员，今天在具有伟大历史意义的中国共产党第七次代表大会的旧址——杨家岭中央礼堂，隆重举行大会，热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记拉米兹·阿利雅同志和阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的同志。
    李富春、刘宁一、罗贵波等同志出席了大会。李富春同志和阿利雅同志先后在大会上发表了讲话（全文见第四版），热烈赞颂革命圣地延安的革命传统，热烈颂扬中阿两党、两国和两国人民的战斗友谊和亲密团结。他们的讲话受到了极其热烈的欢迎。
    今天的大会洋溢着无产阶级国际主义战斗的革命的气氛。会场主席台上悬挂着“热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团”的巨幅标语和毛主席、霍查同志的语录牌。当阿利雅等同志在李富春同志陪同下来到会场的时候，会场内外挤满群众，夹道欢迎，欢呼声、鼓掌声响成一片。
    在大会上，中国人民解放军延安军分区的负责人代表延安军民，对来自反帝、反修前线的阿尔巴尼亚战友表示热烈的欢迎。
    会上，阿利雅同志代表阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团把一面象征中阿两国人民伟大友谊的红色锦旗送给延安人民。延安人民向阿尔巴尼亚战友赠送了《毛主席语录》、毛主席像章以及延安革命纪念章。这时，全场不断高呼：“马克思列宁主义万岁！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”大会结束时，全场齐声高唱《国际歌》。
    会后，为阿尔巴尼亚同志们举行了欢迎宴会。
