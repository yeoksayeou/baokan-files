Title: Under the Chairmanship of Comrade Hoxha, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party Holds Its Third Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1967-10-16
Edition: 5

	Under the chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha,
	the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party
	Holds Its Third Plenary Session

	New China News Agency, Tirana, October 15

	According to a report from local newspapers on October 15, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party held its fifth term third plenary session on October 13 and 14 under the chairmanship of the First Secretary of the Party Central Committee, Comrade Enver Hoxha.

	The plenary session heard the report by Comrade Koço Theodhosi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, on behalf of the Political Bureau, titled "On the Work Done and Measures to be Taken in Fully Utilizing the Production Capacity of the Machine-Building Industry and in Specializing and Further Coordinating the Machine-Building Industry." It also heard the report by Comrade Adil Çarçani, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, on behalf of the Political Bureau, titled "On the Work of Party Organizations, Mass Organizations, and State Organs in Promoting Inventions, Rationalization Proposals, and Economy in Industry and Construction, and on the Further Measures to be Taken to Advance This Movement."

	The plenary session conducted extensive discussions on the two reports, unanimously adopted the two reports, and made corresponding resolutions.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Party Central Committee, also spoke at the plenary session.

### 在霍查同志主持下  阿劳动党中央召开第三次全体会议

1967-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　在霍查同志主持下
    阿劳动党中央召开第三次全体会议
    新华社地拉那十五日电　据这里的报纸十五日报道：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会十月十三日和十四日在党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志的主持下，召开了第五届第三次全体会议。
    全会听取了党中央政治局候补委员科乔·蒂奥多西同志代表中央政治局所作的“关于在充分利用机械工业的生产能力以及使机械工业专业化和进一步协作方面已进行的工作和应该采取的措施”的报告，听取了中央政治局委员阿迪尔·查尔查尼同志代表中央政治局所作的“关于党组织、群众组织和国家机关在工业和建筑业中开展发明创造、合理化建议和节约的工作以及为推进这一运动必须采取的进一步措施”的报告。
    全会就这两个报告进行了广泛的讨论，一致通过了这两个报告，并且作出了相应的决议。
    党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志也在全会上讲了话。
