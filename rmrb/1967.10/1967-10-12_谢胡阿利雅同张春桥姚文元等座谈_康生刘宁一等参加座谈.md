Title: Xie Hu and Aliya Hold Talks with Zhang Chunqiao and Yao Wenyuan
Author:
Date: 1967-10-12
Edition: 2

Xie Hu and Aliya, along with other members of the Albanian Party and Government delegation, held talks with leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, and Liao Zhengguo, this afternoon.

Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, and Luo Guibo also participated in the talks.

Zhang Chunqiao and Yao Wenyuan briefed the Albanian comrades on the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in Shanghai. Xie Hu and Aliya highly praised and enthusiastically commended the achievements of the revolutionary masses and revolutionary rebels in Shanghai during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The talks were filled with the spirit of proletarian internationalism and revolutionary unity.

### 谢胡阿利雅同张春桥姚文元等座谈  康生刘宁一等参加座谈

1967-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　谢胡阿利雅同张春桥姚文元等座谈
　　康生刘宁一等参加座谈
    新华社上海十一日电　谢胡同志和阿利雅同志以及阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团部分团员，今天下午同上海市革命委员会负责人张春桥、姚文元、廖政国等同志举行座谈。
    康生、刘宁一、刘晓、罗贵波等同志参加了座谈会。
    张春桥、姚文元等同志向阿尔巴尼亚同志们介绍了上海市无产阶级文化大革命的情况。谢胡同志和阿利雅同志对上海的无产阶级革命派和革命群众，在无产阶级文化大革命中所取得的成就作了高度评价和热情赞扬。座谈会充满无产阶级国际主义精神和革命团结的气氛。
