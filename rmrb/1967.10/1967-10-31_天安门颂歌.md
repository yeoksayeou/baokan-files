Title: Ode to Tiananmen
Author: Yīsīmǎyī Ēr Kǎdáláilè
Date: 1967-10-31
Edition: 5

	Ode to Tiananmen
	From the Tiananmen Gate to the vast Tiananmen Square, Albanian youth poet Ismail Kadare has raised a surging red revolutionary wave. A thousand waves and ten thousand flowers cascade endlessly; what other torrent in the world can be so tumultuous and vast? I stand on the red viewing platform, gazing at the boiling sea, recalling the past: the Long March army passes before my eyes, the marching bonfires flicker with golden light. Millions of red flags dance in the sky, the blood of revolutionary martyrs dyes the red flags bright. How many heroic sons and daughters have sacrificed their lives for the revolution, their glorious deeds forever etched in our hearts. How can we forget the revolutionary torch? How can we not sing loudly for the martyrs? How can we forget the most magnificent red sea in the world today! The revolution marches forward bravely, the revolutionary torrent shakes the world. Mountains bow before it, rivers flow according to its will. The revolution is like an invincible army, enemies tremble before it. The revolution marches forward bravely, nothing in the world can stop it. Great helmsman Chairman Mao steers the ship, the future of the revolution is incredibly brilliant. Ah, where else in the world is there such a surging torrent, it forever shines with red light in the hearts of hundreds of millions of people!

### 天安门颂歌
依斯玛依尔·卡达莱
1967-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    天安门颂歌
    阿尔巴尼亚青年诗人　依斯玛依尔·卡达莱从天安门城楼到辽阔的天安门广
  场，掀起了澎湃的革命的红色巨浪。千层浪万重花一泻千里，世界上还有什么样的巨流能够如此
  奔腾浩荡。我站在红色的观礼台上，望着沸腾的海洋把往事回想：长征的大军在我面前走过，进军的篝火闪烁着金色的光芒。千万杆红旗漫天飘舞，革命先烈的鲜血把红旗染得鲜
  亮。多少英雄儿女为革命捐躯，壮志凌云的光辉业绩永远记在我们
  心上。我们怎么能够忘记那革命的火
  炬，我们怎么能不为先烈放声歌唱？我们又怎么能忘记啊，今天世界上最壮丽的红色海洋！革命奋勇向前，革命的洪流把世界震荡。高山向它低头，河流按着它的意志流淌。革命象天下无敌的雄师，敌人在它的面前吓得胆战心慌。革命奋勇向前，世界上没有什么可以把它阻挡。伟大舵手毛主席领航掌舵，革命的前程无比辉煌。啊，世界上哪里还有这样沸腾的巨
  流，它在亿万人的心中永远闪烁着红色
  的光芒！
