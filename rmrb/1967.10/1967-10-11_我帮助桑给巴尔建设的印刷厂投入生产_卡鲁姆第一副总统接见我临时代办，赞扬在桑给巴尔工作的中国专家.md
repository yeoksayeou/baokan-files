Title: The Printing Factory Assisted by China in Zanzibar Begins Production
Author:
Date: 1967-10-11
Edition: 5

The printing factory assisted by China in Zanzibar has begun production. The first vice president of Tanzania, Karume, received the acting charge d'affaires of China, Zhou Boqing, and praised the Chinese experts working in Zanzibar.

New China News Agency, Dar es Salaam, October 7th: The first vice president of Tanzania, Karume, presided over the ceremony for the commencement of production at a modern printing factory built with the assistance of China in Zanzibar on the 6th. Attending the ceremony were ministers of Zanzibar, members of the Revolutionary Council, Zanzibar printing workers, and over 200 people including the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, Zhou Boqing, the Chinese Consul in Zanzibar, and Chinese experts.

In his speech at the ceremony, Karume said that the Tanzanian people are in the process of overcoming all the hardships left by colonialism. He hopes that the new printing factory will make a valuable contribution to realizing the people's wishes.

Zhou Boqing also spoke. He said, "Following the great leader Chairman Mao's directive to serve the people of the world, Chinese experts feel proud to be able to contribute to the development of the national economy and culture of the people of Tanzania and Zanzibar."

After the ribbon-cutting ceremony, Karume, accompanied by the head of the Chinese printing expert group, inspected the printing machinery and the entire process of producing photoengraving plates. This printing factory is the first modern facility on Zanzibar Island with complete equipment. In addition to being able to print 20,000 newspapers daily, it can also print color pictures, educational maps, textbooks, and other materials. The completion of this project will help the people of Zanzibar develop their national economy and culture according to the principle of self-reliance.

New China News Agency, Zanzibar: On the morning of the 5th, the first vice president of Tanzania, Karume, received the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, Zhou Boqing, who was visiting Zanzibar, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

During the reception, Karume thanked China for its aid to Tanzania and praised the Chinese experts working in Zanzibar.

### 我帮助桑给巴尔建设的印刷厂投入生产　卡鲁姆第一副总统接见我临时代办，赞扬在桑给巴尔工作的中国专家

1967-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　我帮助桑给巴尔建设的印刷厂投入生产
　　卡鲁姆第一副总统接见我临时代办，赞扬在桑给巴尔工作的中国专家
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆七日电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆六日主持了中国帮助桑给巴尔建设的一座现代化印刷厂开始投入生产的仪式。出席仪式的有桑给巴尔部长、革命委员会成员、桑给巴尔印刷工人以及正在桑给巴尔访问的中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍、中国驻桑给巴尔领事和中国专家等二百多人。
    卡鲁姆第一副总统在仪式上发表讲话说，坦桑尼亚人民正处在克服殖民主义者遗留下来的一切苦难的过程中，他希望新的印刷厂“在实现人民的愿望中做出宝贵的贡献”。
    周伯萍也讲了话。他说：“中国专家遵循伟大领袖毛主席关于为世界人民服务的教导，为能够对发展坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔人民的民族经济和民族文化贡献一分力量而感到骄傲。”
    在剪彩之后，卡鲁姆在中国印刷专家小组组长的陪同下参观了印刷机器和照相锌版的整个制作过程。这座印刷厂是桑给巴尔岛上第一座设备完善的现代化印刷厂。它每天除了能够印刷二万份报纸以外，还能够印刷彩色图片、教学用地图、教科书等。这一工程的完成将有助于桑给巴尔人民按照自力更生的原则来发展民族经济和文化。
    新华社桑给巴尔电坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆五日上午在这里接见了最近在桑给巴尔访问的中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，卡鲁姆感谢中国对坦桑尼亚的援助，他还赞扬了在桑给巴尔工作的中国专家。
