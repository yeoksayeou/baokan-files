Title: Many Friends from Various Countries Attend the National Day Reception at Our Embassy in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1967-10-06
Edition: 6

	Many friends from various countries attended the National Day reception held by our embassy in Yugoslavia. The hearts of the world's people yearning for China cannot be blocked.

	BEOGRAD, Oct. 4 (New China News Agency) — The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, Yu Lizhao, held a reception on September 29 to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Attending the reception were people from various circles and responsible persons from relevant departments in Yugoslavia, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from many countries stationed in Yugoslavia. Before this reception, the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia also held a film and wine reception.

	At the reception and the film and wine reception, many friends from various countries enthusiastically praised the great leader Chairman Mao and highly commended the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. A worker said, "The path of Mao Zedong is the best! The best!" A Yugoslav friend said, "The Chinese path is the only path to achieve communism." Another worker, after seeing a picture of Chairman Mao with the masses, excitedly said, "Truly a great leader, a great people, a great Beijing." A young person said, "The whole world has friends of China. Although some people are trying to sabotage this friendship, the hearts of the world's people yearning for China cannot be blocked."

	At the reception, many guests requested Mao Zedong's works, portraits, and badges.

### 许多国家朋友出席我驻南使馆国庆招待会  世界人民向往中国的心是封锁不住的

1967-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    许多国家朋友出席我驻南使馆国庆招待会
    世界人民向往中国的心是封锁不住的
    新华社贝尔格莱德四日电　中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办于立暄九月二十九日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。出席招待会的有南斯拉夫各界人士和有关方面负责人，许多国家驻南外交使节和外交官员。在这次招待会之前，中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆还举行了电影酒会。
    在招待会和电影酒会上，许多国家的朋友热情歌颂伟大领袖毛主席，高度赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命。一位工人说：“毛泽东的道路最好！最好！”一位南斯拉夫朋友说：“中国的道路是唯一能达到共产主义的道路”。另一位工人看了毛主席和群众在一起的图片后激动地说：“真是伟大的领袖，伟大的人民，伟大的北京”。一位青年说：“全世界都有中国的朋友。尽管有人在破坏这种友谊，但是，世界人民向往中国的心是封锁不住的”。
    在招待会上，很多客人索取了毛主席著作、像片和像章。
