Title: Self-Designed and Manufactured Generator Set
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 4

The first and largest capacity steam turbine generator set in Jiangxi Province has successfully been connected to the power grid and put into operation at the Nanchang Power Plant. The installation quality has reached the national excellent standard.

This generator set was designed and manufactured entirely by China. Its production will further meet the electricity needs of Nanchang's industrial and agricultural production in the region.

### 自己设计制造的发电机组

1967-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　自己设计制造的发电机组
    江西省第一台最大容量的汽轮发电机组在南昌发电厂顺利并入电网，投入运行。安装质量达到国家优良标准。
    这台发电机组是我国自行设计、自行制造的。它的投产将使南昌市的电力进一步满足南昌地区工农业生产的用电需要。
