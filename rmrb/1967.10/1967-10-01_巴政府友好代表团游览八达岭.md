Title: Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Visits Badaling
Author:
Date: 1967-10-01
Edition: 7

The Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation, led by Pakistan's Minister of Information and Broadcasting, Khawaja Shahabuddin, and all members of the delegation, visited the Great Wall—Badaling today. They also toured the Ding Tomb and the Shisanling Reservoir. The Pakistani guests were warmly welcomed by the local people.

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, also accompanied them on the visit.

### 巴政府友好代表团游览八达岭

1967-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    巴政府友好代表团游览八达岭
    新华社三十日讯　由巴基斯坦新闻和广播部长赫瓦贾·夏哈布丁率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团全体成员，今天游览了长城——八达岭。还参观了定陵和十三陵水库。巴基斯坦客人在这里受到群众的热烈欢迎。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也陪同参观游览。
