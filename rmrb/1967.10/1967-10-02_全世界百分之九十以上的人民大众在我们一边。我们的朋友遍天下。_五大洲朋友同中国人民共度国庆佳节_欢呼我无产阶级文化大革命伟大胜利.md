Title: Over 90% of the World's People Stand with Us. Our Friends Are Everywhere. Friends from Five Continents Celebrate China's National Day with the Chinese People and Hail the Great Victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution

Author:

Date: 1967-10-02

Edition: 6

In the moment of the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution that has shaken the world, on the eve of the great festival of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the people of the capital, with hearts full of boundless excitement and joy, warmly welcome the close comrades-in-arms from the front lines of the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle, the heroes from the front lines of the anti-American armed struggle, all friends from the front lines of the anti-imperialist struggle in Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and all friendly representatives and friends from the political, military, economic, cultural, and grassroots levels of various countries who have brought deep friendship to the Chinese people. "Over 90% of the world's people stand with us. Our friends are everywhere." The people of the capital, following Chairman Mao's teachings, extend a warm and enthusiastic welcome to the comrades, fellow fighters, and friends from various countries; together with the friends from various countries, they celebrate the great situation of the revolutionary struggle of the people of the world against imperialism, modern revisionism, and the reactionaries of all countries, and celebrate the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in our country.

With deep affection, friends from afar have come to our country to participate in the National Day activities. They include the close comrades-in-arms of the Chinese people, the delegation of the Party and Government of the People's Republic of Albania led by Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Muhammad Shehu, and members of the delegation including Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Ramiz Alia, and others.

Invited to participate in our National Day activities are also friends from the front lines of the anti-imperialist struggle in Africa, the delegation of the National Revolutionary Movement and Government of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) led by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement, Prime Minister, and Head of Government, Ambroise Noumazalaye.

Friends from the front lines of the anti-American armed struggle, the Vietnamese brothers, led by Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Le Quoc Vy, and Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Nguyen Huu Tho, have come to participate in the National Day activities with our people.

The heroic messengers of the people of South Vietnam, led by the fighting hero Nguyen Van Dan, have brought fraternal fighting friendship to the Chinese people. They have been warmly welcomed by the revolutionary masses of the capital.

### 全世界百分之九十以上的人民大众在我们一边。我们的朋友遍天下。　五大洲朋友同中国人民共度国庆佳节  欢呼我无产阶级文化大革命伟大胜利

1967-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    全世界百分之九十以上的人民大众在我们一边。我们的朋友遍天下。
    五大洲朋友同中国人民共度国庆佳节  欢呼我无产阶级文化大革命伟大胜利
    新华社一日讯　在震撼世界的中国无产阶级文化大革命取得伟大胜利的时刻，在中华人民共和国成立十八周年伟大节日的前夕，首都人民怀着无比兴奋和喜悦的心情，欢迎来自反帝反修斗争前线的亲密战友，欢迎来自反美武装斗争前线的英雄，欢迎所有来自亚非拉反帝斗争前线的朋友，欢迎一切给中国人民带来深厚友情的各国政治、军事、经济、文化和民间的友好代表团和朋友。“全世界百分之九十以上的人民大众在我们一边。我们的朋友遍天下。”首都人民按照毛主席的教导，向来自各国的战友、同志和朋友表示亲切热烈的欢迎；各国朋友同首都人民一起共同欢庆全世界人民反对帝国主义、现代修正主义、各国反动派的革命斗争的大好形势，欢庆我国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利。
    带着深情厚谊不远万里来到我国参加国庆活动的有中国人民亲密的战友，由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，和代表团团员阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记拉米兹·阿利雅等。
