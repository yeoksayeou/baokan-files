Title: Foreign Guests Watch the Revolutionary Modern Peking Opera "Sha Jianhu"
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 6

Foreign guests visiting China for friendly exchanges and participating in National Day activities watched the revolutionary modern Peking opera "Sha Jianhu" performed by the Beijing Peking Opera Troupe No. 1 at an evening gala tonight.

The foreign guests who watched the performance today included: the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Shehu, the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam led by Huang Wendan, the Director of the Office of the Presidency of Mali, Bakara Diallo, as well as members of the National Revolutionary Movement and Government Delegation of the Congo (Brazzaville), the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation, and the Tanzanian Friendship Delegation.

Li Fuchun, Guo Moruo, Zhou Jianren, and others accompanied the foreign guests to watch the performance.

The evening gala was jointly organized by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the China International Friendship Association. The revolutionary modern Peking opera "Sha Jianhu" was warmly received by the foreign guests.

### 各国来宾观看革命现代京剧《沙家浜》  李富春等同志陪同外宾观看演出

1967-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    各国来宾观看革命现代京剧《沙家浜》
    李富春等同志陪同外宾观看演出
    新华社二日讯　前来我国进行友好访问和参加国庆活动的来自世界各国的外宾，今天晚上出席文艺晚会，观看了由北京京剧一团演出的革命现代京剧《沙家浜》。
    今天观看演出的外宾有：以谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，以黄文旦为首的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，马里总统府办公厅主任巴卡拉·迪亚洛，以及刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团、巴基斯坦政府友好代表团、坦桑尼亚友好代表团的成员。
    李富春、郭沫若、周建人等陪同各国外宾观看演出。
    文艺晚会是由对外文委和对外友协联合举办的。革命现代京剧《沙家浜》，受到了各国外宾的欢迎。
