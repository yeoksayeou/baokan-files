Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets Colombian and Chilean Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo met with the Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation led by Pablo Casas Santofimio and the Chilean Friendship Delegation led by Sergio Robledo, as well as the Vice Chairman of the Chile-Valparaiso Chinese Friendship Association, Antonio Zhou, this afternoon. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长  接见哥伦比亚和智利客人

1967-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长
    接见哥伦比亚和智利客人
    新华社二日讯　郭沫若副委员长今天下午接见了以巴勃罗·卡萨斯·桑托菲米欧为首的哥伦比亚文化友好代表团，以塞尔希奥·罗勃亚德为首的智利友好代表团和智利瓦尔帕来索智中文协副主席安东尼奥·周，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
