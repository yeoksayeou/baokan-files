Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Guests Depart Shanghai to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1967-10-19
Edition: 6

Members of the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Revolutionary Movement and Government Delegation, Gabriel Bukaka and Felix Tati-Gustave, who continued their visit to China, concluded their friendly visit to Hangzhou and Shanghai. They departed Shanghai by airplane on the evening of the 17th to return home.

Leaders of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, including Wang Hongwen, and Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gong Dafei, went to the airport to see them off. The Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Bazanga, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 刚果（布）客人离上海回国

1967-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　刚果（布）客人离上海回国
    新华社上海十七日电  留在中国继续进行访问的刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团部分团员加布里埃尔·布卡和费利克斯·塔蒂—古斯塔夫等五人，结束了在杭州、上海的友好访问，于十七日晚上乘飞机离开上海回国。
    上海市革命委员会领导成员王洪文，外交部非洲司副司长宫达非等到机场欢送。刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也到机场送行。
