Title: Comrade Xie Hu and the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Depart Beijing for a Visit to Other Parts of the Country
Author:
Date: 1967-10-05
Edition: 6

	With Comrade Xie Hu, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, as the head of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, the delegation left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit various parts of China.

	One group, led by Comrade Xie Hu, with part of the delegation members, was accompanied by Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and advisor to the Central Cultural Revolution Group.

	The other group, led by Comrade Aliya, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party and a member of the Secretariat, with part of the delegation members, was accompanied by Comrade Li Fuquan, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice-Premier of the State Council, and Comrade Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 谢胡同志和阿党政代表团离京到外地访问  康生、李富春和刘宁一同志分别陪同前往

1967-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志和阿党政代表团离京到外地访问
    康生、李富春和刘宁一同志分别陪同前往
    新华社四日讯　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，今天上午分两路乘专机离开北京前往我国各地访问。
    一路由谢胡同志率领代表团部分团员，由中共中央政治局常委、人大常委会副委员长、中央文革小组顾问康生同志陪同前往。
    一路由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记阿利雅同志率领代表团部分团员，由中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理李富春同志和中共中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长刘宁一同志陪同前往。
