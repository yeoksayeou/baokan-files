Title: Vice Premier Li Fuqun and Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi Meet Danish Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-10-29
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Li Fuqun and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Ningyi met with Danish friends, including Mrs. Aapel and two others, this evening. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Zhou Yiping.

### 李富春和刘宁一接见丹麦朋友

1967-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    李富春和刘宁一接见丹麦朋友
    新华社二十八日讯　国务院副总理李富春和人大常委会副委员长刘宁一今晚接见丹麦朋友阿佩尔夫人等三人，同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，对外友协副会长周一萍在座。
