Title: The Great Dawn Has Arrived
Author: Ālièkèsī Qiàqí
Date: 1967-10-11
Edition: 5

Look, the great dawn has arrived.
I am becoming younger and younger.
The world before my eyes
Is seeking the true path.

People clench their fists and march forward...
The red flag of the proletariat
Is flying high in the skies of China and Albania.
What a vivid red flag,
It reddens our hearts and dyes our thoughts.

The red flag flutters in the wind, and we charge forward bravely...
Mao Zedong and Enver,
Our two parties walk the same path,
Our two parties share the same language.

Like two strong mountain eagles,
Proudly soaring in the sky.
Let us hasten forward, spur on our horses.
Look, the vast army has reached the summit,
To turn the ideal into reality, the beautiful dream of a new world.

Look, a blazing fire burns in our hearts,
To burn down all traitors, capitalists, and all harmful creatures...

China—Albania,
We clasp our hands tightly,
Let this great bridge span the globe.

Ah, what a magnificent bridge,
Let a few worms see it and tremble in their dark holes.
We are the proletariat.
Your death knell has sounded, bloodthirsty capitalism.

China—Albania,
Our friendship, woven with garlands of victory, is boundless.
China, Albania, never to be separated.
Only by uniting together,
Can we connect the seas and oceans;
Only by uniting together,
Can we plant the red flag of victory throughout the world.

### 伟大的黎明来到了
阿列克斯·恰奇
1967-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　伟大的黎明来到了
　　阿尔巴尼亚　阿列克斯·恰奇
　　看，伟大的黎明来到了。
　　我变得越来越年轻。
　　我眼前的世界啊，
　　正在把真正的道路找寻。
　　人们握紧拳头，迈步前进……
　　无产阶级的红旗，
　　在中国和阿尔巴尼亚的上空，高高飘扬。
　　多么鲜艳的红旗呀，
　　你映红了我们的心，染红了我们的思想。
　　红旗迎风招展，我们奋勇向前……
　　毛泽东和恩维尔啊，
　　我们两党走着共同的道路，
　　我们两党有着共同的语言。
　　就象两只雄健的山鹰，
　　在高空中骄傲地飞旋。
　　让我们飞速前进，快马加鞭。
　　看，浩浩荡荡的大军，登上了顶峰，
　　要把理想变为现实，那新世界的美梦。
　　看，我们胸中燃起了熊熊烈火，
　　要统统烧掉那叛徒、资本主义和一切害人虫……
　　中国—阿尔巴尼亚，
　　我们紧握双手，
　　让这伟大的桥梁跨越全球。
　　啊，多么壮丽的桥梁，
　　让一小撮蛆虫看见她，在黑洞里发抖。
　　我们是无产阶级。
　　你们的丧钟敲响了，嗜血的资本主义。
　　中国—阿尔巴尼亚，
　　我们用胜利的花环结起的友谊，宽广无际。
　　中国、阿尔巴尼亚，永不分离。
　　我们只有团结在一起，
　　才能把大海大洋连结起来；
　　我们只有团结在一起，
　　才能把胜利的红旗插遍整个世界。
