Title: The Polish People's Love for Chairman Mao and Their Praise for the Chinese Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-10-19
Edition: 5

The Polish people have an infinite love for Chairman Mao and enthusiastically praise the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. The image shows the Polish people, with hearts full of boundless admiration, watching a needlework portrait of the great leader Chairman Mao titled "On the Eve of the Decisive Battle." (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
