Title: Albania Signs Agreement for Economic Aid to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-10-15
Edition: 6

Albania signs agreement for economic aid to Vietnam

	HANOI, Oct. 13 (New China News Agency) — According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency, the agreement on Albania's economic aid to Vietnam for 1968 was signed here on the 11th.

	The head of the Vietnamese government's economic delegation, Minister of the Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Chen Youyi, and the head of the Albanian government's economic delegation, Albanian Ambassador to Vietnam Xiefuqi·Hekali, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

	Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Nguyen Zheng, and Albanian Ambassador Xiefuqi·Hekali signed the 1968 trade and payment agreement between the two countries on the 11th.

	Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Weizhen attended the signing ceremony.

	The Vietnamese "People's Daily" published an article on the 13th expressing warm thanks to the Albanian Labor Party, government, and people for their sincere and valuable support and assistance to the Vietnamese people. The "People's Daily" said that the Albanian Labor Party, government, and people have consistently sympathized with and supported the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. The signing of this agreement marks the further development of the fighting friendship and unity between the two countries.

### 阿尔巴尼亚对越南无偿经济援助协定签字

1967-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚对越南无偿经济援助协定签字
    新华社河内十三日电　据越南通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚政府一九六八年对越南无偿经济援助协定十一日在这里签字。
    越南民主共和国政府经济代表团团长、越南民主共和国总理府部长陈友翼和阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚驻越南大使谢夫切特·赫卡利分别代表两国政府在上述协定上签了字。
    越南民主共和国对外贸易部副部长阮征和阿尔巴尼亚大使谢夫切特·赫卡利十一日签订了两国一九六八年换货和支付协定。
    副总理阮维桢出席了签字仪式。
    越南《人民报》十三日就上述无偿经济援助协定的签订发表文章，表示热烈感谢阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、政府和人民对越南人民所给予的真诚而宝贵的支持和援助。《人民报》说，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、政府和人民一贯同情和支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。这个协定的签订，标志着两国战斗友谊和团结的进一步的发展。
