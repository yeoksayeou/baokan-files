Title: Brave Combat and Severe Punishment of American Air Pirates: Haiphong Military and Civilians Shoot Down 48 US Aircraft in 40 Days
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 6

Brave Combat and Severe Punishment of American Air Pirates:
Haiphong Military and Civilians Shoot Down 48 US Aircraft in 40 Days

According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 12th, the heroic port city of Haiphong in North Vietnam has shown extraordinary bravery and tenacity in punishing American air pirates. In the 40 days from August 31st to now, they have defeated the long-term continuous attacks by American air pirates on Haiphong, achieving a brilliant victory by shooting down 48 US aircraft and eliminating and capturing a large number of American air pirates.

In an article published on the 11th, the Vietnam People's Army Newspaper warmly praised the brilliant victory of Haiphong military and civilians. The article said, "The various armed forces in Haiphong, with the wholehearted assistance of the people of Haiphong, have fought well and grown rapidly. No matter how cunning and vicious the American air pirates are, they cannot escape the severe punishment of the Haiphong military and civilians. In these 40 days, the number of US aircraft that have crashed on Haiphong land and the number of American air pirates that have been captured or eliminated far exceeds that of previous battles."

### 英勇战斗严惩美国空中强盗  越南海防军民四十天击落美机四十八架

1967-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　英勇战斗严惩美国空中强盗
    越南海防军民四十天击落美机四十八架
    据新华社河内十二日电　越南北方英雄的海港城市——海防军民，在严惩美国空中强盗的战斗中，表现了英勇顽强的精神。在从八月三十一日到现在的四十天中，他们战胜了美国空中强盗对海防进行的长时间的连续的袭击，取得了极其辉煌的胜利，共击落美国飞机四十八架，消灭和活捉大批美国飞贼。
    越南《人民军队报》在十一日发表的一篇述评中，热烈赞扬海防军民的辉煌胜利。述评说，海防各种武装力量在海防人民全心全意的协助下，打得很好，成长很快。不论美国空中强盗怎样狡猾凶恶，都逃不脱海防军民的严厉惩罚。这四十天来，坠落在海防陆地上的美国飞机和被活捉、被消灭的美国飞贼远远超过了以前的战斗。
