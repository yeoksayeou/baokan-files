Title: Premier Zhou Invites President Dadah to Attend Art Evening, Mauritania Delegation Warmly Welcomed at Beijing Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers Institute of Physical Culture
Author:
Date: 1967-10-22
Edition: 3

	Premier Zhou En-lai tonight invited President Moktar Ould Daddah of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania and the Mauritania delegation headed by him to attend an art evening.

	President Daddah and his party, accompanied by Premier Zhou En-lai, Vice-Premier Li Fu-chun, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Kuo Mo-jo, Liu Ning-yi and Chou Chien-jen, enjoyed the revolutionary symphony "Sha Chia P'ing" performed by the Central Philharmonic Society. At the end of the performance, the whole audience sang "The East Is Red," and the performers waved red "Quotations from Chairman Mao Tse-tung," shouting: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand times long live Chairman Mao!"

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated October 21, President Moktar Ould Daddah of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania and the Mauritania delegation headed by him visited the Beijing Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers Institute of Physical Culture this morning and were warmly welcomed by revolutionary teachers, students, staff, and Red Guards.

	President Daddah, accompanied by Vice-Chairman Kuo Mo-jo, arrived at the Institute of Physical Culture. People gathered at the school gate, holding up portraits of Chairman Mao and waving "Quotations from Chairman Mao Tse-tung," shouting slogans to express their warm welcome to the guests. After listening to a report on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution at the Institute of Physical Culture, President Daddah spoke to the revolutionary teachers, students, and Red Guards. He said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is being carried out under the leadership of the great, wise, and respected Chairman Mao Tse-tung. Your introduction has enabled us to learn about the very educational Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution."

	President Daddah and other Mauritania guests watched the teachers and students perform physical exercises in a spacious and bright gymnasium. The exercises included collective gymnastics "Quotations from Chairman Mao Tse-tung" and "We Are the Red Guards of Chairman Mao." After the performance, President Daddah shook hands with the students and congratulated them on their successful performance. The students pinned shining Mao badges on President Daddah and other Mauritania friends and expressed their determination to carry out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end under the guidance of the great leader Chairman Mao and to better support the revolutionary struggles of the African people and the people of the world.

	When President Daddah bid farewell to the revolutionary teachers and students, the people shouted in unison: "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Mauritania peoples! Firmly support the anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggles of the Mauritania people! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! Long live Chairman Mao, ten thousand times long live Chairman Mao!"

### 周总理邀请达达赫总统等贵宾出席文艺晚会  毛里塔尼亚代表团参观北京工农兵体育学院受到热烈欢迎

1967-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理邀请达达赫总统等贵宾出席文艺晚会
    毛里塔尼亚代表团参观北京工农兵体育学院受到热烈欢迎
    新华社二十一日讯　周恩来总理今晚邀请毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国总统莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫和由他率领的毛里塔尼亚代表团，出席文艺晚会。
    达达赫总统等由周恩来总理、李富春副总理和人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、刘宁一、周建人陪同，欣赏了中央乐团演出的革命交响乐《沙家浜》。演出结束时，全场高唱《大海航行靠舵手》，演员们挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，高呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    据新华社二十一日讯　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国总统莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫和由他率领的毛里塔尼亚代表团，今天上午参观了北京工农兵体育学院，受到革命师生员工和红卫兵小将的热烈欢迎。
    达达赫总统由郭沫若副委员长陪同来到体育学院时，人们聚集在学校门口，高举着毛主席画像，挥动着《毛主席语录》，高呼口号，向客人表示热烈的欢迎。达达赫总统听取了体育学院文化大革命的情况后，向革命师生和红卫兵代表讲了话。他说，中国的文化大革命是在伟大的、英明的、享有盛望的毛泽东主席领导下进行的。你们的介绍使我们了解了很有教育意义的文化大革命的情况。
    达达赫总统和其他毛里塔尼亚客人，在宽阔明亮的体操馆里观看了师生们表演的体育节目，其中有集体体操“毛主席语录操”，“我们是毛主席的红卫兵”等。表演结束，达达赫总统和同学们亲切握手，祝贺他们表演的成功。同学们把金光闪闪的毛主席像章佩戴在达达赫总统等毛里塔尼亚朋友胸前，并且表示，一定要遵照伟大领袖毛主席的教导，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，更好地支援非洲人民和全世界人民的革命斗争。
    达达赫总统在和革命师生告别时，人们齐声高呼：“中国毛里塔尼亚人民的友谊万岁！”“坚决支持毛里塔尼亚人民的反帝反殖斗争！”“无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”“毛主席万岁，万万岁！”
