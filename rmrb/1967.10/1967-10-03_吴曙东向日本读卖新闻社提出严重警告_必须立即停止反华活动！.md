Title: Wu Xudong Issues Serious Warning to Japan's Yomiuri Shimbun
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 7

Wu Xudong, representative of the Liaozhengzhi Office's liaison office in Tokyo, on September 28 issued a serious warning to the representative of Japan's Yomiuri Shimbun. He strongly condemned the newspaper's disregard for the strong opposition of the peoples of China and Japan, and its invitation to the anti-China tool of the U.S. and British imperialists and the Indian reactionaries, the Tibetan rebel Dalai Lama, to Japan to engage in conspiratorial activities.

Wu Xudong pointed out: "Recently, Yomiuri Shimbun has taken charge of Dalai's 'visit' to Japan. This is a heinous anti-China act, a blatant provocation against China. We absolutely cannot tolerate this."

Wu Xudong said: "I am authorized to issue a serious warning to your newspaper: You must immediately take effective measures to stop and cancel this anti-China activity. Otherwise, the serious consequences that arise from this must be borne by you."

### 吴曙东向日本读卖新闻社提出严重警告  必须立即停止反华活动！

1967-10-03
第7版()
专栏：

    吴曙东向日本读卖新闻社提出严重警告
    必须立即停止反华活动！
    新华社东京二日电　廖承志办事处驻东京联络事务所代表吴曙东，九月二十八日向日本读卖新闻社代表提出严重警告，严词斥责读卖新闻社不顾中日两国人民的强烈反对，“邀请”美英帝国主义和印度反动派手中的反华工具中国西藏叛匪达赖到日本进行阴谋活动的行径。
    吴曙东指出：“最近读卖新闻社包揽达赖到日本‘访问’，这是恶劣的反华行径，是对中国的明目张胆的挑衅。对此，我们绝对不能容忍。”
    吴曙东说：“我受权向你社提出严重警告：你们必须立即采取有效措施，停止和取消这一反华活动。否则，由此而引起的严重后果，必须由你们承担。”
