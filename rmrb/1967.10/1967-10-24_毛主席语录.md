	Imperialism fears most the awakening of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and the awakening of the peoples of all countries in the world. We must unite and drive U.S. imperialism back to its own home in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

	From "Several Important Conversations with People from Asia, Africa, and Latin America"

### 毛主席语录

1967-10-24

专栏：

    毛主席语录

      《同亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人士的几次重要谈话》
