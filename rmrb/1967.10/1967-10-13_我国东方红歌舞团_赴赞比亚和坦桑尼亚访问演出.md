Title: The Chinese Red East Song and Dance Troupe Visits Zambia and Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 6

The Chinese Red East Song and Dance Troupe, consisting of 53 members, at the invitation of the governments of the Republic of Zambia and the United Republic of Tanzania, led by Director Xu Ping, departed Beijing on October 7 to conduct friendly visits and performances. Relevant officials from our side and Tanzanian Ambassador to China Mwaluko were present at the airport to see them off.

### 我国东方红歌舞团  赴赞比亚和坦桑尼亚访问演出

1967-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　我国东方红歌舞团
　　赴赞比亚和坦桑尼亚访问演出
    新华社讯　中国东方红歌舞团一行五十三人，应赞比亚共和国和坦桑尼亚联合共和国政府的邀请，在团长徐平的率领下，于十月七日离京前往进行友好访问演出。我有关方面负责人和坦桑尼亚驻华大使姆瓦卢科等到机场送行。
