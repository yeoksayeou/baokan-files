Title: Revolutionary Workers of Sun Village Coal Mine
Author: Hua Aibai
Date: 1967-10-28
Edition: 3

Revolutionary workers of the Sun Village Coal Mine are committed to carrying out the revolution above ground and producing coal below ground, using every possible means to produce more raw coal for the country. This is a scene of revolutionary workers from the Sun Village Coal Mine studying methods to further improve coal mining techniques.
