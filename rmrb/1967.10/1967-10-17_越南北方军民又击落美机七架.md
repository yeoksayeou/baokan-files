Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven More U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-10-17
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down seven more U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, October 14th — According to reports from here, on the 14th, the military and civilians of Hai Phong City and Thanh Hoa Province in North Vietnam each shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft. On the 13th, the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area also shot down one aircraft.

	According to supplementary reports, on the 7th, 10th, and 12th, four more U.S. aircraft were shot down in Nghe An Province, Quang Binh Province, and the Vinh Linh area. Among them, an unmanned aircraft was shot down by the militia in Bu Gia Map District, Quang Binh Province.

	According to another report, the coastal artillery unit of Quang Binh Province, in cooperation with other local armed forces, damaged one U.S. warship on the 12th.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机七架

1967-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民又击落美机七架
    新华社河内十四日电　据这里报道，越南北方海防市和清化省军民十四日各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，永灵地区军民十三日也击落一架。
    据补充报道，七日、十日和十二日在义安省、广平省和永灵地区还击落四架美国飞机，其中一架无人驾驶飞机是被广平省布泽县的民兵击落的。
    另据报道，广平省沿海炮兵部队与当地其他武装力量合作，于十二日击伤美国军舰一艘。
