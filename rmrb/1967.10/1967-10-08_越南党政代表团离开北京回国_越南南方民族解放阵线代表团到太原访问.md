Title: Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation Leaves Beijing to Return Home; Vietnamese National Liberation Front Delegation Visits Taiyuan
Author:
Date: 1967-10-08
Edition: 3

The Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, led by Comrade Le Quang Dao, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, left Beijing by special plane this morning, carrying with them the deep friendship and solidarity of the Chinese people for the Vietnamese people.

Central leaders and leaders from relevant departments, including Nie Rongzhen, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, and Guo Moruo, went to the airport to see them off.

Also present at the airport to bid farewell were leaders from relevant departments and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Fang Yi, Li Qiang, Ding Xilin, Li Tianyou, Yuan Ziqin, Dong Yueqian, and Nie Yuanzi.

The delegation from the Vietnamese National Liberation Front, led by Huang Wendan, and the deputy head of the Vietnamese National Liberation Front's permanent representative office in China, Nguyen Minh Phuong, as well as diplomatic staff from the Vietnamese Embassy in China, were also at the airport to see them off.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated October 7, the delegation from the Vietnamese National Liberation Front, led by the combat hero Huang Wendan, departed Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit other parts of the country.

Central leaders and leaders from relevant departments, including Nie Rongzhen, Guo Moruo, Su Yu, and revolutionary masses and militia representatives from the capital, went to the airport to see them off.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Quan, members of the Vietnamese National Liberation Front's permanent representative office in China, and Vietnamese friends in Beijing were also at the airport to see them off.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated October 7 from Taiyuan, over ten million revolutionary masses and soldiers from the People's Liberation Army of China in Taiyuan gathered at the airport and along the streets to warmly welcome the delegation from the Vietnamese National Liberation Front, led by the combat hero Huang Wendan, who have come from the frontlines of the anti-American struggle.

Liu Ge Ping, Political Commissar of the Beijing Military Region, First Political Commissar of the Shanxi Military Region, member of the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee, and Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee, along with Deputy Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee Yuan Zhen, responsible officials from the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanxi, and leaders from revolutionary mass organizations, went to the airport to welcome the Vietnamese comrades.

Vietnamese students studying in Taiyuan also went to the airport to welcome them.

Liu Ge Ping hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the delegation from the Vietnamese National Liberation Front.

The banquet was filled with the internationalist fighting camaraderie between the two countries. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses, wishing the people of South Vietnam greater victories in their struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, and wishing for the further strengthening and consolidation of the brotherly friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam.

Liu Ge Ping, in his speech at the banquet, warmly praised the heroic Vietnamese people for creatively demonstrating the immense power of people's war in the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, achieving an unprecedentedly good situation. He said, the people of Shanxi are thrilled by every victory achieved by the Vietnamese people. He firmly believed that the Vietnamese people, with their spirit of decisive battle and decisive victory, would definitely be able to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors and achieve the final victory.

Huang Wendan, in his speech at the banquet, said that the delegation's visit to Taiyuan was like coming to a secure rear base. The victories achieved in South Vietnam in striking at American imperialism are great, and every victory in South Vietnam includes the contributions of the people of Shanxi. Huang Wendan, on behalf of Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho and the 14 million people of South Vietnam, expressed heartfelt thanks to the people of Shanxi! He also praised the great achievements of the people of Shanxi in various fields and wished them greater victories.

### 越南党政代表团离开北京回国  越南南方民族解放阵线代表团到太原访问

1967-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    越南党政代表团离开北京回国
    越南南方民族解放阵线代表团到太原访问
    新华社七日讯　以越南劳动党中央  政治局委员、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅同志为首的越南党政代表团，满载中国人民对越南人民深厚的战斗友谊，今天上午乘专机离开北京回国。
    中央负责同志和有关方面负责同志聂荣臻、杨成武、粟裕、郭沫若等，前往机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有各有关方面负责人和北京市革命委员会的负责人方毅、李强、丁西林、李天佑、袁子钦、董越千、聂元梓等。
    正在我国访问的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团团长黄文旦、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆外交官员，也到机场欢送。
    据新华社七日讯　由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，今天下午乘飞机离开北京到外地访问。
    中央领导同志和有关方面领导同志聂荣臻、郭沫若、粟裕和首都革命群众、首都民兵代表到机场欢送。
    越南驻中国大使吴明鸾，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团成员和在京的越南朋友，也到机场欢送。
    据新华社太原七日电　太原市革命群众和中国人民解放军陆海空三军指战员十多万人今天聚集在太原机场和街头，热烈欢迎来自反美斗争最前线的亲密战友、英雄的越南南方人民的使者——由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团。
    中国人民解放军北京部队政治委员、山西省军区第一政治委员、中共山西省核心小组组长、山西省革命委员会主任刘格平，山西省革命委员会副主任袁振，中国人民解放军驻山西部队的负责人谢振华、山西省军区负责人曹玉清和革命群众组织的负责人等前往机场欢迎越南南方战友。
    在太原的越南实习生也到机场迎接。
    刘格平同志今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎越南南方民族解放阵线代表团。
    宴会上，洋溢着中越两国人民国际主义的战斗情谊。席间，宾主频频举杯，祝越南南方人民在抗美救国斗争中取得更大的胜利，祝中越两国人民的兄弟友谊不断加强和巩固。
    刘格平在宴会上热烈赞扬英雄的越南人民在抗美救国战争中创造性地发挥了人民战争的无比威力，打出了一片空前大好的形势。他说，山西人民对越南人民所取得的每一个胜利，都感到欢欣鼓舞。他坚信，越南人民发扬决战决胜的精神，一定能够彻底打败美国侵略者，夺取最后的胜利。
    黄文旦团长在宴会上的讲话中说，代表团来到太原访问，就象来到了巩固的后方一样。我们在越南南方打击美帝国主义取得了很大的胜利，越南南方取得的每一个胜利，都包括山西人民的贡献。黄文旦团长代表阮友寿主席，代表越南南方一千四百万人民，向山西人民表示诚挚的谢意！他还赞扬了山西人民在各方面取得的伟大成就，并祝愿他们取得更大的胜利。
