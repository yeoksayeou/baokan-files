Title: Premier Zhou Continues Talks with President Daddah
Author:
Date: 1967-10-23
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou En Lai continues talks with President Daddah of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania in a friendly atmosphere this afternoon.

President Daddah of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania and the delegation he heads, accompanied by Vice Premier Li Fu Chun, visited the Sino-Albanian Friendship People's Commune this morning and were warmly welcomed by the commune members. When President Daddah arrived at the Sino-Albanian Friendship People's Commune, the commune members held high the portrait of our great leader Chairman Mao, waving the national flags of China and Mauritania, and shouting slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Mauritania!" "Firmly support the anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggles of the Mauritanian people!" "Down with U.S. imperialism!" "Down with Soviet revisionism!" "Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live our great leader Chairman Mao, ten thousand times ten thousand years!" President Daddah learned about the commune members' efforts to seize the revolution and promote production, and also visited the commune's dairy farm, horse farm, duck farm, and carpet factory.

In the afternoon, President Daddah and other Mauritanian friends, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhou Jian Ren, visited the Summer Palace.

### 周总理同达达赫总统继续会谈  毛里塔尼亚贵宾参观中阿友好公社

1967-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同达达赫总统继续会谈
    毛里塔尼亚贵宾参观中阿友好公社
    新华社二十二日讯　国务院总理周恩来同毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国总统莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫，今天下午继续在友好的气氛中举行会谈。
    新华社二十二日讯　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国总统莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫和由他率领的毛里塔尼亚代表团，今天上午由李富春副总理陪同，参观了中阿友好人民公社，受到公社社员的热烈欢迎。达达赫总统来到中阿友好人民公社的时候，社员们高举我们伟大领袖毛主席的画像，挥动着中毛两国国旗，不断地高呼：“中国毛里塔尼亚两国人民的友谊万岁！”“坚决支持毛里塔尼亚人民的反帝反殖斗争！”“打倒美帝！”“打倒苏修！”“无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”“我们伟大领袖毛主席万岁，万万岁！”达达赫总统了解了公社社员抓革命、促生产的情况，还参观了公社的奶牛场、养马场、饲鸭场和地毯厂等。
    下午，达达赫总统和其他毛里塔尼亚朋友由周建人副委员长陪同，游览了颐和园。
