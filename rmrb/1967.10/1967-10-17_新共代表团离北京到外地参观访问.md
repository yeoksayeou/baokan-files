Title: Delegation of the New Zealand Communist Party Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Other Places
Author:
Date: 1967-10-17
Edition: 4

A delegation of the New Zealand Communist Party, led by John Foldes, a member of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, came to participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The delegation left Beijing today to visit other places and then return to their country.

Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to the airport to see them off.

### 新共代表团离北京到外地参观访问

1967-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　新共代表团离北京到外地参观访问
    新华社十五日讯　由新西兰共产党全国委员会委员约翰·福尔兹率领的新西兰共产党代表团，前来参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年庆祝活动后于今天离开北京到外地参观访问，然后回国。
    中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志到机场欢送。
