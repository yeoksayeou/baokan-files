Title: Our Most Beloved Great Leader, Chairman Mao, Meets with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation
Author: Lü Xiāng Yǒu
Date: 1967-10-01
Edition: 5

Our most, most revered great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received the Albanian Party and Government Delegation in the afternoon of September 30.

	Left photo: Chairman Mao poses for a group photo with Albanian guests. The fifth person from the left in the front row is Comrade Shehu.

	Bottom left photo: Chairman Mao shakes hands warmly with Comrade Shehu.

On September 30, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held a grand reception at the Great Hall of the People to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Comrades Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and others attended the reception. Guests from five continents around the world also attended the reception. The bottom photo shows the full scene of the reception.

The photographs in this section were taken by reporter Lü Xiāng Yǒu of this newspaper.
