Title: Chairman Mao Meets Friends from Congo (Brazzaville) and Pakistan

Author:

Date: 1967-10-04

Edition: 2

Chairman Mao Meets Friends from Congo (Brazzaville) and Pakistan

Chairman Mao, in separate meetings with the delegation from the National Revolutionary Movement and the government of Congo (Brazzaville), the Pakistani government's friendly delegation, and the delegation from the youth organization of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), warmly shook hands with Prime Minister Numa Dazale, Minister Shahabuddin, Gancia Ami, and all members of their delegations, expressing his welcome.

	New China News Agency, October 3rd: Our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao today in the afternoon met with the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), Prime Minister and Head of Government, Ambassador Numa Dazale, and the entire delegation from the National Revolutionary Movement and the government of Congo (Brazzaville) led by him.

	During the meeting, Chairman Mao warmly shook hands with Prime Minister Numa Dazale and other Congolese friends, expressing his welcome.

	Subsequently, Chairman Mao had a friendly and cordial conversation with Prime Minister Numa Dazale and members of the delegation from the National Revolutionary Movement and the government of Congo (Brazzaville): Gabriel Bouka, Felix Tati-Gustave, Andre Ombesa, and Edward Ange Panki.

	Accompanying the meeting were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and Deputy Foreign Minister Xu Yixin.

	The Congolese Ambassador to China, Bazanga, was also present.

	New China News Agency, October 3rd: Our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao today in the afternoon met with Pakistani Minister of Information and Broadcasting Shahabuddin and his wife, as well as the entire delegation from the Pakistani government's friendly delegation led by Shahabuddin.

	During the meeting, Chairman Mao warmly shook hands with Minister Shahabuddin and other Pakistani friends, expressing his welcome.

	Accompanying the meeting were Premier Zhou Enlai and Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong.

	The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, was also present.

	New China News Agency, October 3rd: Our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao today in the afternoon met with the entire delegation from the youth organization of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville) led by Ily Gancia Ami.

	The Congolese youth friends, holding the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," entered the meeting hall. Chairman Mao warmly shook hands with them, expressing his welcome. The Congolese youth friends excitedly waved their red revolutionary books and chanted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao!"

	Accompanying Chairman Mao to meet with the Congolese youth friends were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and others.

### 毛主席接见刚果（布）和巴基斯坦朋友

1967-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见刚果（布）和巴基斯坦朋友
    毛主席在分别接见刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团、巴基斯坦政府友好代表团、刚果（布）全国革命运动青年组织代表团的时候，同努马扎莱总理、夏哈布丁部长、冈齐阿米和他们率领的代表团全体成员，一一亲切握手，表示欢迎。
    新华社三日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，今天下午接见了刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会第一书记、总理、政府首脑安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱，和由他率领的刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团全体成员。
    接见时，毛主席同努马扎莱总理和其他刚果（布）朋友，一一亲切握手，表示欢迎。
    随后，毛主席同努马扎莱总理以及刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团团员：加布里埃尔·布卡、费利克斯·塔蒂—古斯塔夫、安德烈·翁贝萨、爱德华·昂热·彭基，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理李先念，外交部副部长徐以新。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也在座。
    新华社三日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，今天下午接见了巴基斯坦新闻和广播部长赫瓦贾·夏哈布丁和夫人，以及由夏哈布丁率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团全体成员。
    接见时，毛主席同夏哈布丁部长和巴基斯坦其他朋友，一一亲切握手，表示欢迎。
    陪同接见的，有国务院总理周恩来，外交部副部长韩念龙。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也在场。
    新华社三日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，今天下午接见了由伊利·冈齐阿米率领的刚果（布）全国革命运动青年组织代表团全体成员。
    刚果（布）青年朋友手持《毛主席语录》，来到接见大厅。毛主席同他们一一亲切握手，表示欢迎。刚果（布）青年朋友们兴奋地挥动红彤彤的革命宝书，高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    同毛主席一起接见刚果（布）青年朋友的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理李先念等。
