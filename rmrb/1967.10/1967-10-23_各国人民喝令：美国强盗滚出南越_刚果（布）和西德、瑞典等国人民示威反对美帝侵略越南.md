Title: People of Various Countries Demand: U.S. Bandits Get Out of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-10-23
Edition: 6

According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, October 17th: The youth organization of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville) held a rally on the evening of the 15th, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam and firmly supporting the Vietnamese people's just struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

Matumba Pollo, Chairman of the Brazzaville Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville) Youth Organization, called in his speech: "All the peoples of the world, all the youth of the world, African youth, take action, take action, to drive the U.S. bandits out of Vietnam!" He pointed out: "All over the world, small wars against imperialism are breaking out everywhere, and all these small fronts are merging into a powerful front against U.S. imperialism."

The representative of the General Union of Students of Congo (Brazzaville) emphasized: The people's war waged by the Vietnamese people will certainly defeat U.S. imperialism. The struggle must continue, without compromise. We believe that the Vietnamese people will surely achieve final victory.

The chargé d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), Long Thuan Phuc, gave a brief introduction to the situation of the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation at the rally.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, October 21st: Youth, students, and residents from all over West Germany and West Berlin held demonstrations and rallies for two consecutive days, strongly protesting against U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam and supporting the struggle of the American people against this war of aggression.

In Hamburg, 2,000 residents held a mass meeting and torchlight procession on the evening of the 20th. The demonstrators made a paper tiger representing U.S. imperialism, with the face of U.S. leader Johnson and the words "Johnson is a paper tiger" written on its body. They carried this paper tiger in the procession and burned it in front of one of the U.S. aggression institutions, the "American House."

In Frankfurt, Hanover, Munich, Stuttgart, Göttingen, Nuremberg, Freiburg, and Constance in West Germany, many young people, students, and residents also held protest demonstrations on the 20th and 21st.

In West Berlin, 10,000 young people, students, and residents formed a grand procession on the afternoon of the 21st, with the flag of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the portrait of Vietnamese leader Ho Chi Minh at the forefront, holding a massive demonstration. The procession carried red flags and banners, with a huge banner prominently displayed: "Yankees, get out of Vietnam!"

According to Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, October 21st: Five thousand people held a demonstration in the Swedish capital on the afternoon of the 21st, opposing U.S. aggression against Vietnam.

Some African Americans, Africans, and Latin Americans joined the Swedish people in the demonstration. They marched in lines on the main streets of Stockholm, shouting slogans: "Down with U.S. imperialism!" "Yankees, get out of Vietnam!"

A resolution was passed at the rally held after the march, condemning U.S. imperialism and expressing solidarity with the Vietnamese people.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, October 22nd: More than 15,000 Danish citizens held a demonstration in the capital Copenhagen on the 21st, protesting against U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam.

The demonstrators held up signs with slogans such as "U.S. imperialism get out of Vietnam," "Johnson is a murderer," and marched to the U.S. Embassy to protest against U.S. aggression in Vietnam. They burned more than twenty U.S. flags in front of the U.S. Embassy and threw stones at the U.S. Embassy, smashing the windows.

According to Xinhua News Agency, London, October 20th: When British Prime Minister Wilson arrived in the fishing port of Hull in northern England on the 20th, about 200 students held a demonstration, opposing the British government's policy of supporting U.S. aggression against Vietnam.

The students held up signs saying "Wilson supports the massacre in Vietnam" and shouted "Wilson, get out!"

### 各国人民喝令：美国强盗滚出南越  刚果（布）和西德、瑞典等国人民示威反对美帝侵略越南

1967-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    各国人民喝令：美国强盗滚出南越
    刚果（布）和西德、瑞典等国人民示威反对美帝侵略越南
    据新华社布拉柴维尔十七日电  刚果（布）全国革命运动青年组织十五日晚上举行集会，强烈谴责美帝国主义侵略越南，并坚决支持英雄的越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争。
    刚果（布）全国革命运动青年组织布拉柴维尔委员会主席马图姆巴·波洛在集会上讲话呼吁：“全世界各国人民，全世界青年，非洲青年，都行动起来，行动起来，迫使美国强盗滚出越南。”他指出：“全世界到处都爆发了构成许多小战线的反对帝国主义的小战争，所有这些小战线正汇合成为反对美帝国主义的强大战线。”
    刚果（布）学生总联合会的代表强调指出：越南人民进行的人民战争，必将战胜美帝国主义。斗争必须继续下去，决不妥协。我们相信越南人民一定会取得最后的胜利。
    越南驻刚果（布）大使馆代办龙顺福在集会上就越南人民抗美救国斗争的形势作了简要介绍。
    据新华社波恩二十一日电　西德全国各地和西柏林的青年、学生以及广大居民连续两天纷纷示威、集会，强烈抗议美帝国主义侵略越南，支持美国人民反对这场侵略战争的斗争。
    在汉堡，二千名居民二十日晚上举行了群众大会和火炬游行。示威者用纸扎成一只代表美帝国主义的老虎，纸老虎的面部是美帝头目约翰逊的脸像，身体上面写道：“约翰逊是纸老虎！”他们抬着这只纸老虎游行，并且在美国侵略机构之一“美国之家”门前把它烧毁。
    在西德的法兰克福、汉诺威、慕尼黑、斯图加特、哥丁根、纽伦堡、弗赖堡和君士坦丁等地，二十日和二十一日也有许多青年、学生和居民举行抗议示威。
    在西柏林，一万名青年、学生和居民二十一日下午排成浩浩荡荡的队伍，以越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜和越南人民领袖胡志明主席的画像为先导，举行了声势浩大的示威游行。示威队伍打着红旗和标语牌，一幅巨大的标语赫然在目：“美国佬从越南滚出去！”
    据新华社斯德哥尔摩二十一日电 　瑞典首都二十一日下午举行了五千人的示威，反对美国侵略越南。
    一些美国黑人、非洲人和拉丁美洲人同瑞典人并肩参加了示威。他们在斯德哥尔摩的通衢大道上列队前进，沿途高呼口号：“打倒美帝国主义！”“美国滚出越南！”
    游行后举行的集会上通过了一个决议，谴责美帝国主义，并且表示同越南人民团结在一起。
    据新华社斯德哥尔摩二十二日电　一万五千多名丹麦公众二十一日在首都哥本哈根举行示威，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    示威者高举写着“美帝国主义从越南滚出去”、“约翰逊杀人犯”等口号的标语牌，到美国大使馆去抗议美帝国主义在越南的侵略罪行。他们在美国大使馆前面烧了二十多面美国星条旗，并且向美国使馆扔石头，砸碎了美国使馆的玻璃窗。
    据新华社伦敦二十日电　当英国首相威尔逊二十日到达英格兰北部的渔港赫尔的时候，约二百名学生举行示威，反对英国政府支持美国侵略越南的政策。
    学生们打着“威尔逊支持在越南的屠杀”的标语牌，并高呼：“威尔逊滚蛋！”
