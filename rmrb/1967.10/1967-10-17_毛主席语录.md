Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-10-17
Edition: 1

Our task is to lead a nation of tens of millions of people in an unprecedented, great struggle. Therefore, the task of universally and deeply studying the theories of Marxism-Leninism is, for us, a major problem that urgently needs to be solved and to which we must devote considerable effort.

						*From "The Position of the Communist Party of China in the National War"*

### 毛主席语录

1967-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席语录
    我们的任务，是领导一个几万万人口的大民族，进行空前的伟大的斗争。所以，普遍地深入地研究马克思列宁主义的理论的任务，对于我们，是一个亟待解决并须着重地致力才能解决的大问题。
                        《中国共产党在民族战争中的地位》
