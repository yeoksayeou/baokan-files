Title: Premier Zhou, Kang Sheng, Li Fuquan Meet with Shehu and Hu and Other Comrades
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 1

	Premier Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and advisor to the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and Li Fuquan, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, continued their talks with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Muhamed Shehu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania.

	The talks were conducted in a very sincere and cordial atmosphere.

	Present at the talks from the Chinese side were Comrades Liu Ningyi, Fang Yi, Liu Xiao, Luo Guibo, Li Tianyou, and other relevant officials; from the Albanian side were members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Comrades Ramiz Alia, Mihal Kacani, Rahman Perlak, Tonin Jakova, Akim Merko, Foto Cami, Piro Bita, and Vasili Natanaeli.

### 周恩来康生李富春同谢胡等同志会谈

1967-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来康生李富春同谢胡等同志会谈
    新华社十二日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来同志，中共中央政治局常委、人大常委会副委员长、中央文革小组顾问康生同志，中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理李富春同志，同阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，今天继续举行了会谈。
    会谈在极其真挚亲切的气氛中进行。
    参加会谈的，中国方面有各有关方面负责人刘宁一、方毅、刘晓、罗贵波、李天佑等同志；阿尔巴尼亚方面有阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团成员拉米兹·阿利雅、米哈拉奇·齐奇什蒂、拉赫曼·佩尔拉库、托宁·雅科瓦、阿基姆·梅罗、福托·恰米、皮罗·比塔和瓦西里·纳塔奈利同志。
