Title: Leaders from Various Countries Send Telegrams to Congratulate the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 7

The leadership of our country continues to receive telegrams from leaders of various countries congratulating the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

In his telegram to Chairman Mao, President Michel Micombero of the Republic of Burundi said: "I am very pleased to take this opportunity on the historic occasion of your national day to express the deepest sympathy and boundless admiration of the people of Burundi to the Chinese people and their wise leader. The people of Burundi are delighted by the fraternal relations between our two countries and wish for these relations to be further strengthened."

In his telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai, President Abdul Rahman al-Iryani of the Yemen Arab Republic said: "On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, on this day when all the fighting peoples of the world raise their voices in praise, allow me, dear friend, on behalf of the government and people of the Yemen Arab Republic, and in my personal capacity, to extend my most sincere congratulations and deepest respect to you, to the government and people of China, and to the great leader Mao Zedong who is in the midst of the struggle. The friendly relations and solid friendship between our two peoples will forever inspire the peoples of all countries who yearn for freedom and independence."

The telegram said: "I will never forget the selfless and effective assistance provided by the friendly people of China and their consistent support for our revolution. Please accept my most sincere wishes for your health and the happiness of the Chinese people."

In his telegram to Premier Zhou, Prime Minister Yusuf Zu'ayyin of the Syrian Arab Republic extended his most sincere congratulations to the government of China and the friendly people of China, and sincerely wished for the continuous progress and prosperity of the Chinese people.

Prime Minister Muhammad Haji Ibrahim Egal of the Somali Republic sent a telegram to Premier Zhou, wishing the People's Republic of China prosperity and progress.

Telegrams were also received from King Sisavang Vatthana of Laos, Emir Sabah al-Salim al-Sabah of Kuwait, the State Council and the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, the Chairman and the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, the Government of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, the Revolutionary Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Presidium of the National Assembly and the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Presidium of the Great People's Khural and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, President Urho Kekkonen of the Republic of Finland, Prime Minister Per Borten of the Kingdom of Norway, Prime Minister Jens Otto Krag of the Kingdom of Denmark, Prime Minister Tage Erlander of the Kingdom of Sweden, Chairman Roger Bonvin of the Federal Council of Switzerland, and President Zakir Husain of the Republic of India.

### 各国领导人纷纷来电  祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年

1967-10-03
第7版()
专栏：

    各国领导人纷纷来电
    祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年
    新华社二日讯　我国领导人继续收到各国领导人发来的祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年的电报。
    布隆迪共和国总统米歇尔·米孔贝罗在给毛主席的贺电中说：“我十分高兴借这一国庆纪念的历史性节日的机会，向中国人民及其英明的导师表达布隆迪人民最深切的同情和无限的钦佩。布隆迪人民为我们两国之间的兄弟般的关系感到欢欣鼓舞，并祝愿这种关系进一步加强。”
    阿拉伯也门共和国总统阿卜杜拉·萨拉勒在给周恩来总理的贺电中说：“在庆祝中华人民共和国国庆的时候，在世界上所有战斗的人民齐声赞扬的节日里，亲爱的朋友，请允许我代表阿拉伯也门共和国政府和人民，并以我个人名义，向阁下，向中国政府和人民，向斗争中的勇士毛主席致以最衷心的祝贺和最诚挚的敬意。我们两国人民之间的友好关系和牢固的友谊，将永远鼓舞着渴望自由和独立的各国人民。”
    贺电说：“我不会忘记友好的中国人民所提供的无私的有效的援助和她一贯支持我国革命的立场。请接受我对阁下和毛主席的最诚挚的祝愿，祝你们身体健康，祝中国人民幸福。”
    阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理优素福·扎因在给周总理的贺电中向中国政府和友好的中国人民致以最诚挚的祝贺，并衷心祝愿中国人民日益进步繁荣。
    索马里共和国总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒在给周总理的贺电中祝中华人民共和国繁荣进步。
    发来贺电的还有老挝国王西萨旺·瓦达纳，科威特国埃米尔萨巴赫·萨利姆·萨巴赫，波兰人民共和国国务委员会和部长会议，德意志民主共和国国务委员会主席和部长会议主席，捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国政府，匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府，保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团和部长会议，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议，苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和部长会议，芬兰共和国总统乌尔霍·吉科宁，挪威王国政府首相佩尔·博顿，丹麦王国政府首相茵斯·奥托·克拉克，瑞典王国政府首相塔格·埃兰德，瑞士联邦委员会主席罗歇·邦万，印度共和国总统扎基尔·侯赛因。
