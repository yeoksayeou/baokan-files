	The heroic people of Albania have become a great beacon of socialism in Europe.

	The revisionist leadership clique of the Soviet Union, the Tito clique of Yugoslavia, and all sorts of traitorous and scab cliques pale in comparison to you. They are but a handful of dust, while you stand as mountains that touch the sky.

		Telegram of Congratulations to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour
