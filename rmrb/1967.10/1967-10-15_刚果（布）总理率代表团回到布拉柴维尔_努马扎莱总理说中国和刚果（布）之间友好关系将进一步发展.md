Title: Prime Minister of Congo (Brazzaville) Leads Delegation Back to Brazzaville
Author:
Date: 1967-10-15
Edition: 6

	The Prime Minister of Congo (Brazzaville) leads delegation back to Brazzaville
	Prime Minister Noumazalaye says relations between China and Congo (Brazzaville) will further develop
	Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, October 13th

	The delegation led by the First Secretary of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), Prime Minister, and Head of Government, Ambroise Noumazalaye, after a friendly visit to China, returned to Brazzaville by plane on the morning of the 13th.

	Those who welcomed Prime Minister Noumazalaye and the delegation members at the airport included members of the Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), Gabriel Opangui and Aubert Lounda, Minister of Finance Edward Ebaka-Babaka, Minister of Justice Francois Makosso, Minister of Health Simon Gokana, Minister of Information Pierre Mwowama, Chairman of the Economic and Social Committee Bongo Nouara, Chairman of the Guidance Committee of the Youth Organization of the National Revolutionary Movement Djavara Anghe, as well as other high-ranking officials of the Congolese (Brazzaville) party and government and leaders of mass organizations.

	The Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chen Guang, also went to the airport to welcome them.

	Diplomats from various countries were also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

	When Prime Minister Noumazalaye answered questions from reporters at his residence, he expressed his gratitude to the Chinese government for inviting the delegation of the National Revolutionary Movement and Government of Congo (Brazzaville) to participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. He said: The delegation had contact with the working people, Red Guards, and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army of China, and had the most free discussions with workers in various fields. We returned from China with a very good impression.

	When talking about the relations between Congo (Brazzaville) and China, Prime Minister Noumazalaye said: "The relations between the People's Republic of China and Congo (Brazzaville) are as friendly as can be. During our stay in China, we obtained the most obvious proof. We believe that the visit we just completed will further develop the friendly cooperative relations that already exist between the People's Republic of China and Congo (Brazzaville)."

	He also said that the People's Republic of China is an increasingly powerful socialist country, and all countries must consider the importance of the People's Republic of China.

### 刚果（布）总理率代表团回到布拉柴维尔  努马扎莱总理说中国和刚果（布）之间友好关系将进一步发展

1967-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　刚果（布）总理率代表团回到布拉柴维尔
    努马扎莱总理说中国和刚果（布）之间友好关系将进一步发展
    新华社布拉柴维尔十三日电　由刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会第一书记、总理、政府首脑安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱率领的刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团，在对中国进行友好访问后，十三日上午乘飞机回到布拉柴维尔。
    到机场欢迎努马扎莱总理和代表团成员的，有刚果（布）全国革命运动政治局委员加布里耳·奥彭吉和奥贝·伦达、财政部长爱德华·埃布卡—巴巴卡、司法部长弗朗索瓦·马科索、卫生部长西蒙·戈卡纳、新闻部长皮埃尔·姆沃瓦马、经济和社会委员会主席邦戈·努阿拉、全国革命运动青年组织指导委员会主席贾瓦拉·昂热，以及刚果（布）党和政府的其他高级官员和群众组织的负责人。
    中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国外交使节。
    努马扎莱总理在官邸回答记者提出的问题时，表示感谢中国政府邀请刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年庆典。他说：代表团同中国劳动人民、红卫兵、人民解放军战士进行了接触，同各方面的劳动者进行了最自由的讨论。我们带着非常好的印象从中国返回本国。
    在谈到刚果（布）、中两国的关系时，努马扎莱总理说：“中华人民共和国和刚果（布）之间的关系是再亲切友好不过了。在中国逗留期间，我们获得了最明显的证明。我们认为，我们刚刚结束的访问，将使中华人民共和国和刚果（布）之间已有的友好合作关系得到进一步发展。”
    他还说，中华人民共和国是一个越来越强大的社会主义国家，各国必须考虑到中华人民共和国的重要性。
