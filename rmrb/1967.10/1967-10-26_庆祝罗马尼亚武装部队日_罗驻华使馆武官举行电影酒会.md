Title: Celebrating the Romanian Armed Forces Day: Romanian Military Attaché Hosts Film and Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-10-26
Edition: 6

Romanian military attaché Colonel Gheorghe Georgiu, to celebrate the Romanian People's Republic Armed Forces Day, hosted a film and reception this evening. Deputy Minister of National Defense Xu Yuyu and officials from the National Defense Ministry General Office, various headquarters of the People's Liberation Army, and various military branches, including Wang Xinting, Yuan Ziqin, Zhang Tianyun, and Lu Yang, were invited to attend the event.

Also in attendance were Deputy Foreign Minister Luo Guibo and Vice Chairman of the China Foreign Affairs Committee Chu Tu-nan.

Romanian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Istrati also attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys from various countries in China were also invited to attend.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队日  罗驻华使馆武官举行电影酒会

1967-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队日
    罗驻华使馆武官举行电影酒会
    新华社二十五日讯  罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆武官乔治乌中校，为庆祝罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队日，今晚举行电影酒会。国防部副部长粟裕和国防部办公厅、中国人民解放军各总部、各军种负责人王新亭、袁子钦、张天云、路扬等应邀出席了酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的，还有外交部副部长罗贵波和对外文委副主任楚图南。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代出席了酒会。各国驻华使节也应邀出席了酒会。
