Title: Soldiers of the "Red First Company" Carry Out Revolutionary Criticism
Author: Meng Qingbiao
Date: 1967-10-29
Edition: 2

The soldiers of the "Red First Company" are taking "Fight Selfishness and Criticize Revisionism" as their guiding principle. They are extensively carrying out revolutionary criticism. The training ground has also become a battlefield for revolutionary criticism.
