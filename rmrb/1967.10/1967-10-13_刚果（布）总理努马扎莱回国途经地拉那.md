Title: Prime Minister Noumazalaye of Congo (Brazzaville) Passes Through Tirana on His Way Home
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 6

The delegation led by Ambrwos Noumazalaye, the first secretary of the Central Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), the prime minister and head of the government, returned home via Tirana after concluding a friendly visit to China. On the afternoon of the 11th, they made a brief stopover at Tirana Airport.

Those who went to the airport to welcome and see off the delegation included Vice Premier of the Council of Ministers of Albania Adil Carcani, Foreign Minister Nasi, Secretary of the Presidium of the People's Assembly Klozi, Secretary of the Council of Ministers Kashabi, and Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Assembly Qiu.

Li Tingqian, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, also went to the airport to welcome and see off the delegation.

A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to Prime Minister Noumazalaye and other members of the delegation at the airport.

### 刚果（布）总理努马扎莱回国途经地拉那

1967-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　刚果（布）总理努马扎莱回国途经地拉那
　　新华社地拉那十二日电　由刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会第一书记、总理、政府首脑安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱率领的刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团，在结束了对中国的友好访问后，十一日下午在回国途中路经地拉那，在地拉那机场作了短暂的停留。
    前往机场迎接和欢送的有，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼、外交部长纳赛、人民议会主席团秘书克洛西、部长会议秘书卡沙比和地拉那人民会议执行委员会副主席齐乌等。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃也到机场迎送。
    一队少先队员在机场向努马扎莱总理和代表团其他成员献了花。
