Title: President Dah Dah Holds Grand Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1967-10-24
Edition: 3

Further Strengthen the Friendly Cooperation Between China and Mauritania

President Dah Dah Holds Grand Farewell Banquet

President Dah Dah Praises the Great Leader Chairman Mao's Personally Initiated and Led Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in His Speech

Premier Zhou En-lai Asks President Dah Dah to Convey the Friendship of 700 Million Chinese People to the Hardworking and Brave People of Mauritania

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 23rd, the Head of State of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, Moktar Ould Dah Dah, held a grand farewell banquet in Beijing this evening.

President Dah Dah and Premier Zhou En-lai both spoke at the banquet (full texts to be published separately). In his speech, President Dah Dah enthusiastically praised the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by our great leader Chairman Mao, and wished for further strengthening of the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries of Mao and China.

In his speech, Premier Zhou En-lai asked President Dah Dah and other Mauritania friends to convey the friendship of the 700 million Chinese people under the leadership of our great leader Chairman Mao to the hardworking and brave people of Mauritania. He said, the Chinese people will forever be reliable friends and brothers of the Mauritania people, and the Chinese people sincerely wish the Mauritania people victory in their anti-imperialist struggle.

Those invited to the banquet included Vice Premier Li Fu-chun and Xie Fu-zhi of the State Council, Vice Chairman of the Central Cultural Revolution Group and responsible person of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee Zhang Chun-qiao, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Mo-ruo, Liu Ning-yi, Zhou Jian-ren, Vice Minister of National Defense Su Yu, heads of various government departments and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of the Beijing Revolutionary Committee and revolutionary mass organizations of the capital. Also in attendance were China's Ambassador to Mauritania Lü Zhi-xian. Staff of the Mauritania Embassy in China and envoys from various countries to China also attended the banquet.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 23rd, the Head of State of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, Moktar Ould Dah Dah, and the delegation he led, this afternoon, accompanied by Premier Zhou En-lai, visited the Capital Steel Company and were warmly welcomed by the revolutionary workers.

When President Dah Dah arrived at the Capital Steel Company, the welcoming crowd, stretching about two kilometers along both sides of the road, erupted in excitement. People waved the national flags of both countries, beat drums, and shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Mauritania," "Firmly support the anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggle of the Mauritania people," and "Long live Chairman Mao," warmly welcoming the Mauritania guests.

President Dah Dah listened to the introduction by a representative of the Capital Steel Company and visited the iron smelting, steel smelting, and rolling mills. The guests were enthusiastically welcomed wherever they went.

President Dah Dah spoke. He said, today we came here to see your achievements and received your warm reception, for which we express our thanks.

President Dah Dah also said, we should also thank you. Through your introduction, we know how great the achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao are.

When President Dah Dah and other Mauritania friends left the Capital Steel Company, the revolutionary workers lined the route to see them off.

Those who accompanied the visit today included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Peng-fei and China's Ambassador to Mauritania Lü Zhi-xian.

Moktar Ould Dah Dah, the Head of State of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, held a grand farewell banquet on October 23rd. The picture shows President Dah Dah and Premier Zhou En-lai at the banquet. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Xin-qing.

### 达达赫总统举行盛大告别宴会

1967-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    进一步加强中国和毛里塔尼亚两国的友好合作关系
    达达赫总统举行盛大告别宴会
    达达赫总统在讲话中热情称赞我们伟大的领袖毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命
    周恩来总理在讲话中请达达赫总统把七亿中国人民的情谊转达给勤劳勇敢的毛里塔尼亚人民
    据新华社二十三日讯　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国国家元首莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫总统，今天晚上在北京举行盛大的告别宴会。
    达达赫总统和周恩来总理在宴会上讲了话（全文另发）。达达赫总统在讲话中热情称赞我们伟大领袖毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命，并祝愿毛中两国的友好合作关系进一步加强。
    周恩来总理在讲话中请达达赫总统等毛里塔尼亚朋友，把我们伟大领袖毛主席领导下的七亿中国人民的情谊，转达给勤劳勇敢的毛里塔尼亚人民。他说，中国人民永远是毛里塔尼亚人民可信赖的朋友和兄弟，中国人民衷心祝愿毛里塔尼亚人民在反帝斗争的道路上，胜利前进。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有国务院副总理李富春、谢富治，中央文革小组副组长、上海市革命委员会负责人张春桥，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、刘宁一、周建人，国防部副部长粟裕，政府各部门、中国人民解放军的负责人，北京市革命委员会和首都革命群众组织的代表。出席宴会的，还有中国驻毛里塔尼亚大使吕志先。毛里塔尼亚驻中国大使馆人员，各国驻中国使节也出席了宴会。
    新华社二十三日讯　毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国国家元首莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫总统和由他率领的毛里塔尼亚代表团，今天下午由周恩来总理陪同，参观了首都钢铁公司，受到广大革命职工的热烈欢迎。
    当达达赫总统来到首都钢铁公司时，聚集在马路两旁的长约两公里的欢迎队伍沸腾起来了。人们挥动着两国国旗，敲锣打鼓，高呼“中国毛里塔尼亚两国人民的友谊万岁”“坚决支持毛里塔尼亚人民的反帝反殖斗争”“毛主席万岁”等口号，热烈欢迎毛里塔尼亚贵宾。
    达达赫总统听取了首都钢铁公司代表的介绍，参观了炼铁、炼钢、轧钢等车间。贵宾们每到一处，都受到热烈欢迎。
    达达赫总统讲了话。他说，今天我们到你们这里来，看一看你们取得的成就，受到了你们的热情接待，我们表示感谢。
    达达赫总统还说，我们还要感谢你们，通过你们的介绍，我们知道了在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下的无产阶级文化大革命取得了多么伟大的成就。
    达达赫总统和毛里塔尼亚其他朋友离开首都钢铁公司时，广大革命职工夹道欢送。
    今天陪同参观的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，我国驻毛里塔尼亚大使吕志先等。（附图片）
    毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国国家元首莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫总统，十月二十三日举行盛大告别宴会。图为达达赫总统和周恩来总理在宴会上。新华社记者　王新庆摄
