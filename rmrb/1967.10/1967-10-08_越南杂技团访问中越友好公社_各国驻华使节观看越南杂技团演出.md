Title: Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe Visits Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Commune
Author:
Date: 1967-10-08
Edition: 7

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Acrobatic Troupe, led by Director Wu Yu Ying, visited the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship People's Commune today.

The Vietnamese comrades listened to the introduction by the commune's leaders about the commune members' study and application of Chairman Mao's works and the commune's production situation. They also visited the poor and lower-middle peasants and labored together with them. The Vietnamese cultural warriors performed excellent acrobatic acts for the commune members.

Various foreign envoys stationed in China watched the performance by the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Acrobatic Troupe at the Capital Theater this evening.

Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee Chu Tu Nan accompanied the foreign envoys to watch the excellent performance by the Vietnamese cultural warriors. Vietnamese Democratic Republic Ambassador to China Vu Minh Loan also watched today's performance.

### 越南杂技团访问中越友好公社  各国驻华使节观看越南杂技团演出

1967-10-08
第7版()
专栏：

    越南杂技团访问中越友好公社
    各国驻华使节观看越南杂技团演出
    据新华社七日讯　由吴玉英团长率领的越南民主共和国杂技团，今天访问了中越友好人民公社。
    越南战友们听取了中越友好公社负责人介绍的社员活学活用毛主席著作情况和公社生产情况，并且访问了贫下中农，和贫下中农一起劳动。越南文艺战士为社员演出了精采的杂技节目。
    据新华社七日讯　各国驻华使节今晚在首都剧场观看了越南民主共和国杂技团的演出。
    对外文委副主任楚图南，陪同各国使节观看了越南文艺战士的精彩演出。越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也观看了今天的演出。
