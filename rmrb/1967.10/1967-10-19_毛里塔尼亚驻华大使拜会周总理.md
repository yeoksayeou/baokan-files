Title: Mauritania's Ambassador Calls on Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1967-10-19
Edition: 6

Mauritania's first ambassador to China, Harich, paid a call on Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon.

The New China News Agency reported on the 18th that Harich, the first ambassador from Mauritania to China, paid a call on Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon. Premier Zhou had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Harich.

### 毛里塔尼亚驻华大使拜会周总理

1967-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　毛里塔尼亚驻华大使拜会周总理
    新华社十八日讯　毛里塔尼亚首任驻中国大使哈尔希今天下午拜会国务院总理周恩来。周总理同哈尔希大使进行了友好的谈话。
