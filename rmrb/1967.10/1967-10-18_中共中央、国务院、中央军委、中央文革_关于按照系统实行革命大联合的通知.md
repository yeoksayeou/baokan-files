Title: Notice from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group on Implementing Revolutionary Great Unification by System
Author:
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 1

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group

	Notice on Implementing Revolutionary Great Unification by System

	Xinhua News Agency, October 17th

	Notice on Implementing Revolutionary Great Unification by System

	To the revolutionary committees (preparatory groups) and military control committees of various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, and to the various mass organizations via the various military regions and provincial military regions:

	(1) All factories, schools, departments, and enterprise units must, under the principle of revolution, implement revolutionary great unification by system, by industry, and by class, in order to facilitate the establishment of the revolutionary three-in-one combination, to facilitate the progress of criticism, struggle, and transformation in various units, and to facilitate the promotion of revolution, production, work, and preparedness for war.

	(2) All revolutionary mass organizations should take Mao Zedong Thought as their basis, engage in thorough consultation, and handle matters according to different specific situations in accordance with Chairman Mao's aforementioned instructions. All organizations that cross industries should, on the principle of voluntarism, make necessary adjustments by industry.

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group

	October 17, 1967

### 中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革　关于按照系统实行革命大联合的通知

1967-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革
　　关于按照系统实行革命大联合的通知
　　新华社十七日讯　关于按照系统实行革命大联合的通知各省、市、自治区革命委员会（筹备小组）、军管会，各大军区、省军区并转各群众组织：（一）各工厂、各学校、各部门、各企业单位，都必须在革命的原则下，按照系统，按照行业，按照班级，实现革命的大联合，以利于促进革命三结合的建立，以利大批判和各单位斗批改的进行，以利于抓革命、促生产、促工作、促战备。（二）所有革命群众组织都应该以毛泽东思想为基础，经过充分协商，按照不同的具体情况，遵照毛主席上述的指示办理。一切跨行业的组织，都应该在自愿的原则下，按行业进行必要的调整。
　　中共中央、国务院中央军委、中央文革
    一九六七年十月十七日
