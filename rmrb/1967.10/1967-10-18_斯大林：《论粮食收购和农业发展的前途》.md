Title: Stalin: On Grain Procurement and the Future of Agricultural Development
Author: 斯大林
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 4

There are two paths to establishing large-scale agricultural economies: one is the capitalist path, which leads to the bankruptcy of a large number of farmers and the establishment of capitalist estates that exploit labor; the other is the socialist path, which involves uniting small peasant economies into large-scale collective economies, ensuring that farmers do not go bankrupt and that labor is not exploited.

### 斯大林：《论粮食收购和农业发展的前途》

1967-10-18
第4版()
专栏：七亿人民都来做批判家

　　斯大林：《论粮食收购和农业发展的前途》
　　建立大规模农业经济的道路有两条：一条是资本主义的道路，是使大批农民破产而建立对劳动进行剥削的资本主义大田庄的道路；一条是社会主义的道路，是把小农经济联合为大规模集体经济的道路，是农民不会遭到破产、劳动也不会受到剥削的道路。
