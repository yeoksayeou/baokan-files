Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Receives the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-10-11
Edition: 5

Premier Pham Van Dong Receives the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation
The Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Hanoi for Home

	Hanoi, October 9 (New China News Agency) — Politburo member of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, received the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association delegation led by Yang Dezhi, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the National Defense Council, on October 8. Present at the reception were Vietnamese officials including Huang Guoyue, Chunshui, Sun Guangfai, Huang Jun, and Wu Guowei.

	The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Lu Weizhao, was also present.

	Premier Pham Van Dong had a cordial and friendly conversation with Yang Dezhi and other members of the delegation.

	Hanoi, October 10 (New China News Agency) — The Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association delegation led by Yang Dezhi, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the National Defense Council, has left Hanoi for home. The delegation, at the invitation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, participated in the activities celebrating the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and conducted a friendly visit. Those who saw them off included Vietnamese officials Huang Guoyue, Nguyen Van Huan, Sun Guangfai, Huang Jun, Nguyen Jishi, Chen Weixing, and Li Nan Sheng. The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Lu Weizhao, was also present.

### 范文同总理接见中越友协代表团  中越友协代表团离河内回国

1967-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　范文同总理接见中越友协代表团
    中越友协代表团离河内回国
    新华社河内九日电　越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国政府总理范文同，八日接见了由中共中央委员、国防委员会委员杨得志率领的中越友协代表团。接见时在座的有黄国越、春水、孙光阀、黄俊、武国威等越南有关方面负责人。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊也在座。
    范文同总理同杨得志同志以及代表团其他成员进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    据新华社河内十日电　由中共中央委员、国防委员会委员杨得志率领的中越友协代表团，已经离开河内回国。代表团应越中友协的邀请，参加了越南人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年的活动，并进行了友好访问。前往送行的有黄国越、阮文煊、孙光阀、黄俊、阮基石、陈维兴、黎南胜等越南有关方面负责人。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊也前往送行。
