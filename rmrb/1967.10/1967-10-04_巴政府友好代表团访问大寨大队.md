Title: A Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Visits the Dazhai Brigade
Author:
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 6

A Pakistani government friendship delegation led by Khawaja Shabuddin visited the Dazhai Brigade of Xiyang County, Shanxi Province, today. At the end of the visit, the Pakistani guests and the brigade members together shouted: "Long live the Dazhai spirit!" "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan!" "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

Chen Yonggui, the secretary of the Dazhai Brigade Party Committee, introduced to the guests the achievements made by the brigade members in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, following Chairman Mao's teachings, and displaying the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle.

After the tour, Minister Khawaja Shabuddin praised the Dazhai Brigade as a "great model of collective strength."

### 巴政府友好代表团访问大寨大队

1967-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    巴政府友好代表团访问大寨大队
    据新华社二日讯　由赫瓦贾·夏哈布丁率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团，今天访问了山西省昔阳县大寨公社大寨大队。访问结束时，巴基斯坦客人和大寨大队的社员们共同高呼：“大寨精神万岁！”“中巴两国人民的友好万岁！”“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    大寨大队党支部书记陈永贵，向客人们介绍了大寨大队社员活学活用毛主席著作，按照毛主席的教导，发挥自力更生和艰苦奋斗的革命精神所取得的成绩。
    赫瓦贾·夏哈布丁部长在参观后，赞扬大寨大队是发挥集体力量的“伟大的典范”。
