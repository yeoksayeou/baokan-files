Title: Albanian Comrades Visit the Sino-Albanian Friendship Commune
Author:
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 6

Albanian Comrades Visit the Sino-Albanian Friendship Commune

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated October 3, the members of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Commune in the suburbs of Beijing today warmly welcomed the Albanian Party and Government Delegation as if welcoming close relatives.

	This morning, Mihalachi Zicisti, a member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and the First Secretary of the Korca District Committee, and other Albanian comrades, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and other comrades, came to the Sino-Albanian Friendship Commune. The commune members held high portraits of Chairman Mao and Comrade Enver Hoxha, waved the national flags of the two countries, chanted slogans, and beat drums and gongs to enthusiastically welcome the Albanian comrades.

	The responsible comrades of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Commune introduced the excellent situation of revolution and production in the commune to the Albanian comrades. After the visit, the Albanian comrades enthusiastically praised the achievements of the commune members in grasping the revolution and promoting production.

	The commune members held a welcome meeting, enthusiastically welcoming the glorious envoys from the great socialist beacon in Europe, Albania. At the meeting, the comrades from the two countries toasted to the unbreakable fighting friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples of China and Albania. The comrades from the two countries expressed that no matter what happens in the world, the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples of China and Albania will definitely stand together, fight together, and win together. The comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation presented two banners symbolizing the revolutionary unity and fighting friendship between the peoples of the two countries to the Sino-Albanian Friendship Commune.

### 阿尔巴尼亚同志访问中阿友好公社

1967-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚同志访问中阿友好公社
    据新华社三日讯　北京郊 区中阿友好人民公社社员今天象欢迎亲人一样，热烈地欢迎前来公社作客的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的同志们。
    今天上午，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、科尔察区委第一书记米哈拉奇·齐奇什蒂等阿尔巴尼亚同志，由外交部副部长罗贵波等同志陪同来到中阿友好人民公社。社员们高举毛主席画像和恩维尔·霍查同志画像，挥动着中阿两国国旗，高呼口号，敲锣打鼓，热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    中阿友好人民公社负责同志向阿尔巴尼亚同志介绍了这个公社革命和生产的大好形势。阿尔巴尼亚战友们参观以后，热烈赞扬社员们在抓革命、促生产方面所取得的成绩。
    社员们举行了欢迎会，热烈欢迎来自欧洲的一盏伟大的社会主义的明灯阿尔巴尼亚的光荣使者。会上，中阿两国同志共祝中阿两党、两国、两国人民之间的牢不可破的战斗友谊万古长青。中阿两国同志表示：不管世界上发生什么事情，我们两党、两国、两国人民，一定团结在一起、战斗在一起、胜利在一起。阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团同志把两面象征着中阿两国人民革命团结和战斗友谊的锦旗，赠给中阿友好人民公社。
