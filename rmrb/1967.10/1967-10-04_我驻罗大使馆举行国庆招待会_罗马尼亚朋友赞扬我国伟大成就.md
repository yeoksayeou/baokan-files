Title: Chinese Embassy in Romania Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 7

The Chinese Embassy in Romania held a reception to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 30th: The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, Li Bin, held a reception on the evening of the 29th to warmly celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attending the reception were: Member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and First Deputy Premier of the Council of Ministers, Berlad Ainu; Member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and First Deputy Premier of the Council of Ministers, Virdeu; Alternate Member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Deputy Premier of the Council of Ministers, Fazekash; Alternate Member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Central Committee Secretary, Manesku; Member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Chairman of the Romanian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Pas; Deputy Foreign Minister Marin, and responsible officials from central organizations, mass organizations, and cultural, artistic, and news circles in Romania.

Envoys from several countries to Romania also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

At the reception, many Romanian friends enthusiastically praised the great achievements of our country over the past 18 years and sincerely wished the great leader of the world revolutionary people, Chairman Mao, eternal life.

At the reception, guests eagerly viewed the exhibits in the banquet hall, which reflected the world revolutionary people's love for Chairman Mao and the unprecedented proletarian cultural revolution in our country. They, with boundless admiration, requested Mao's works, the red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and Mao's badges and photographs.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 30th: The Romanian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a meeting on the afternoon of the 29th at the Trade Union Theater in Bucharest to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

More than 600 representatives from various circles in Bucharest attended the celebration.

Members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and First Deputy Premier of the Council of Ministers, Berlad Ainu, Deputy Foreign Minister Marin, Vice Chairman of the State Committee for Culture and Art, Gheorghe, and Chairman of the Romanian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Pas, attended the celebration.

Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, Li Bin, and others were invited to attend the celebration.

Vice Chairman of the Romanian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Livzeanu, and Chargé d'Affaires Li Bin spoke at the meeting.

### 我驻罗大使馆举行国庆招待会  罗马尼亚朋友赞扬我国伟大成就

1967-10-04
第7版()
专栏：

    我驻罗大使馆举行国庆招待会
    罗马尼亚朋友赞扬我国伟大成就
    据新华社布加勒斯特三十日电　我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌二十九日晚上举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。
    出席招待会的有：罗马尼亚共产党中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席伯尔拉德亚努，罗共中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席维尔德茨，罗共中央候补执行委员、部长会议副主席法泽卡什，罗共中央候补执行委员、中央书记曼内斯库，罗共中央委员、对外文协主席帕斯，外交部副部长马林，以及罗马尼亚中央机关群众团体负责人和文化艺术新闻界人士等。
    一些国家的驻罗使节也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在热烈、友好的气氛中进行的。
    在招待会上，许多罗马尼亚朋友热烈赞扬我国十八年来取得的伟大成就，并衷心祝愿世界革命人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆。
    在招待会上，来宾们争着观看了陈列在宴会厅里的反映世界革命人民热爱毛主席以及有关我国史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命的图片。他们以无限敬佩的心情，索取毛主席著作、红彤彤的《毛主席语录》以及毛主席像章和照片。
    据新华社布加勒斯特三十日电　罗马尼亚对外文协二十九日下午在布加勒斯特工会剧院举行集会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。
    布加勒斯特各界六百多名代表出席了庆祝集会。
    罗马尼亚共产党中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席伯尔拉德亚努，外交部副部长马林，国家文化和艺术委员会副主席乔治乌以及对外文协主席帕斯等出席了庆祝集会。
    我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌等应邀出席了庆祝集会。
    罗马尼亚对外文协副主席利韦泽亚努和李斌临时代办先后在集会上讲了话。
