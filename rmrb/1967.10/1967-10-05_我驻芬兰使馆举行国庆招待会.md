Title: The Chinese Embassy in Finland Holds a Reception for National Day
Author:
Date: 1967-10-05
Edition: 8

The Chinese Embassy in Finland held a reception on the evening of October 2 to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. More than 340 guests attended the reception.

Among the attendees were Finnish Acting Foreign Minister and Minister of Education Reino Oittinen, Minister of Justice Aarre Simoinen, Chief of the General Staff Aimo, and other high-ranking officials. Representatives of Finnish workers and youth, as well as prominent figures from various sectors, were also present. Diplomatic envoys from various countries in Helsinki also attended the reception.

Many guests expressed their admiration for Chairman Mao and wished him eternal life. They eagerly requested copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," works by Chairman Mao, and Chairman Mao badges. The guests also enthusiastically praised the great victories of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 我驻芬兰使馆举行国庆招待会

1967-10-05
第8版()
专栏：

    我驻芬兰使馆举行国庆招待会
    新华社四日讯　赫尔辛基消息：中国驻芬兰大使馆临时代办李春田二日晚在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年。三百四十多位来宾出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的有：芬兰代理外交部长、教育部长雷诺·奥伊蒂宁，司法部长阿雷·西莫宁，参谋总长阿里莫等高级官员，以及芬兰工人和青年代表、各界知名人士等。各国驻赫尔辛基的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    许多来宾热情地表示他们对毛主席的敬爱，并祝毛主席万寿无疆。他们纷纷索取《毛主席语录》、毛主席著作和毛主席像章。来宾们还热情赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利。
