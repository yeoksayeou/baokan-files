Title: Total Lunar Eclipse Today
Author:
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 2

Today, there will be a total lunar eclipse. According to a message from the Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a total lunar eclipse will occur this evening, and it will be visible across the entire country except for the far western parts of Xinjiang and Tibet. The initial phase (beginning of the partial eclipse) will start at 16:25, the total eclipse will begin at 17:45, the maximum eclipse will occur at 18:15, the end of the total eclipse will be at 18:46, and the end of the partial eclipse will be at 20:05. The far eastern parts of Northeast China will be able to see the initial phase, and almost the entire country will see the moon rising while the eclipse is already in progress, meaning the moon will rise while still in eclipse.

### 今日有月全食

1967-10-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　今日有月全食
    新华社南京十七日电　据中国科学院紫金山天文台消息，本月十八日晚将发生月全食，全国除新疆、西藏的极西部以外都可以看到。初亏（偏食开始）十六时二十五分，食既（全食开始）十七时四十五分，食甚（月食程度最甚）十八时十五分，生光（全食终了）十八时四十六分，复圆（偏食终了）二十时零五分。我国东北地区的极东部可以看到初亏，全国绝大部分在月出时月食已经开始，即月亮带食而出。
