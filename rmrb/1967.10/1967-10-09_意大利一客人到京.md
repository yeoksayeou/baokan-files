Title: An Italian Visitor Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-10-09
Edition: 5

	An official from the Italy-China Friendship Association for the Promotion of Cultural Relations, Maria Regis, the president of the Italian Eastern Publishing House, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 7th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 意大利一客人到京

1967-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利一客人到京
    新华社八日讯　意大利促进同中华人民共和国文化友好关系协会负责人之一、意大利东方出版社社长玛丽娅·雷吉斯夫人，应中国人民对外文化友好协会的邀请，七日乘飞机到达北京。
