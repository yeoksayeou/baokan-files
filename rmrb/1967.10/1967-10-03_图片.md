Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Tanzanian Friendship Delegation
Author: Qian Sijie
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 6

On October 2, Premier Zhou Enlai received a Tanzanian friendship delegation led by Babu, the Minister of Land, Settlement, and Water Development of the United Republic of Tanzania. The photo shows the group posing together during the reception. Babu, the minister, is the third person from the left.  Xinhua News Agency photographer Qian Sijie.
