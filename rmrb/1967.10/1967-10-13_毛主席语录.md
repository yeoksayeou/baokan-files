    Quotations from Chairman Mao
    The truth of Marxism-Leninism is on our side. The international proletariat is on our side. The oppressed nations and peoples are on our side. More than ninety percent of the world's people are on our side. Our friends are all over the world. We are not afraid of isolation, and we will never be isolated. We are invincible. A small handful of miserable anti-China and anti-Albania elements are doomed to failure.

    Telegram to the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania
