Title: Albanian Government Delegation Arrives in Karachi for Friendly Visit at the Invitation of the Pakistani Government
Author:
Date: 1967-10-16
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Pakistani government, an Albanian government delegation led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Muhammad Shehu, arrived in Karachi, Pakistan's largest city, on October 15 via a special plane from China. They were warmly welcomed by Pakistani President Ayub Khan and other government leaders, as well as people from various sectors.

The delegation is in Pakistan at the invitation of the Pakistani government for a friendly visit.

When Chairman Shehu, Aliya, and other members of the delegation stepped off the plane, President Ayub Khan warmly shook hands with each of them. Pakistani youth presented flowers to the Albanian guests.

Pakistani people from various sectors who came to the airport to welcome the Albanian delegation enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Pakistan-Albania friendship!"

The airport was adorned with the national flags of Albania and Pakistan. The band played the national anthems of both countries. Chairman Shehu, accompanied by President Ayub, inspected the honor guard.

Subsequently, Chairman Shehu was introduced to the government ministers and other high-ranking officials, members of the National Assembly and Provincial Assembly, and local notables present at the airport. As Chairman Shehu shook hands with them, they enthusiastically placed garlands around his neck.

Chairman Shehu, accompanied by President Ayub, then drove to the presidential palace where the Albanian guests were staying. As the convoy passed through the streets, many people stood on both sides of the road, cheering and waving at the Albanian guests.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the Albanian delegation included: Pakistani Finance Minister Uqaili, Foreign Secretary Yusuf, Admiral Ahsan, Chief of Naval Staff, Director of the Protocol Department of the Foreign Ministry, Tajabji, and Commissioner of Karachi, Syed Dabar Ali Shah.

Albanian Ambassador to Pakistan, Ulvi Lulo, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation also included: Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, Xu Ying, Consul of the Chinese Consulate in Karachi, Shen Peiwen, and many other diplomatic representatives from various countries.

### 应巴基斯坦政府邀请进行友好访问  阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团抵达卡拉奇

1967-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　应巴基斯坦政府邀请进行友好访问
　　阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团抵达卡拉奇
    新华社卡拉奇十五日电　由阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团，十五日从中国乘专机抵达巴基斯坦最大的城市卡拉奇，受到巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗和其他政府领导人以及各界人士的热烈欢迎。
    代表团是应巴基斯坦政府的邀请前来巴基斯坦进行访问的。
    当谢胡主席、阿利雅和代表团其他成员走下飞机时，阿尤布·汗总统同他们一一热烈握手。巴基斯坦少年向阿尔巴尼亚贵宾献了花。
    在机场欢迎阿尔巴尼亚代表团的巴基斯坦各界人士，热情高呼“巴基斯坦—阿尔巴尼亚友好万岁！”
    机场上飘扬着阿尔巴尼亚和巴基斯坦两国国旗。乐队演奏了两国国歌。谢胡主席在阿尤布总统的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    然后，谢胡主席被介绍给在机场上的政府部长和其他高级官员、国民议会和省议会议员、当地的各界知名人士。在谢胡主席同他们握手时，他们纷纷给他戴花环。
    谢胡主席在阿尤布总统陪同下驱车前往阿尔巴尼亚贵宾下榻的总统府。当车队经过大街时，许多群众站在街道两旁向阿尔巴尼亚贵宾欢呼和挥手。
    前往机场欢迎阿尔巴尼亚代表团的还有：巴基斯坦中央财政部长乌凯利、外交部外事秘书优素福、海军总司令阿赫森海军中将、外交部礼宾司司长特雅布吉、卡拉奇区专员赛义德·达巴尔·阿里·沙阿。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻巴基斯坦大使乌尔维·卢洛也前往机场迎接。
    前往机场欢迎的还有中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英、中国驻卡拉奇领事沈培文和其他许多国家的外交代表。
