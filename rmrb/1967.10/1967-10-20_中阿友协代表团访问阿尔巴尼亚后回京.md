Title: Delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association Returns to Beijing After Visiting Albania
Author:
Date: 1967-10-20
Edition: 5

The delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Comrade Wang Shusheng, was invited to participate in the activities celebrating the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Albania. After completing a friendly visit to heroic Albania, the delegation returned to Beijing by plane today. Responsible officials from relevant departments in China, representatives of revolutionary masses in the capital, and Albanian Ambassador to China Nataneli welcomed them at the airport.

### 中阿友协代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚后回京

1967-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　中阿友协代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚后回京
    新华社十九日讯　由王树声同志率领的中阿友协代表团，应邀参加了阿尔巴尼亚人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年活动，并在英雄的阿尔巴尼亚进行了友好访问之后，今天乘飞机回到北京。我各有关方面负责人、首都革命群众代表和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利曾到机场迎接。
