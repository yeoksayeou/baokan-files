Title: China and Pakistan Hold Ceremony for Border Trade Exchange of Letters
Author:
Date: 1967-10-25
Edition: 5

	China and Pakistan held a ceremony for the exchange of letters regarding border trade between Pakistan's Gilgit and Baltistan regions and China's Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region on October 21 in Rawalpindi.

	The Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Pakistan, Xu Ying, signed on behalf of the Chinese government, and the Joint Secretary of Pakistan's Ministry of Commerce, Jafare, signed on behalf of the Pakistani government.

### 中巴举行两国边境地区贸易换函仪式

1967-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    中巴举行两国边境地区贸易换函仪式
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十三日电　中国和巴基斯坦二十一日在这里举行了巴基斯坦吉尔吉特和巴勒堤地区同中国新疆维吾尔自治区之间边境贸易换函仪式。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英代表中国政府，巴基斯坦商业部联合秘书贾法雷代表巴基斯坦政府，在换函上签了字。
