Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk and Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization Ahmed Shukeiri Send Congratulatory Messages on the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 6

	Prince Norodom Sihanouk Sends Congratulatory Message on the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
	Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization Ahmed Shukeiri Sends Congratulatory Message on the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China

	Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai received a congratulatory telegram from Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, on the occasion of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China,
	Beijing:

	On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I am pleased to extend my warmest congratulations and best wishes to you and the people of Cambodia.

	I take this opportunity to reaffirm my belief that the fraternal relations between our two countries will develop on the basis of the principles of the Bandung Conference and the spirit of complete unity in the struggle against U.S. imperialism.

	I once again express my sincere thanks to you, the government of your country, and the people of China for the invaluable assistance you have provided to our country's construction and defense efforts. This support from China is a great encouragement to our efforts on the path of development and a great encouragement to our struggle to defend our independence, which is under threat.

	Norodom Sihanouk
	Head of State of Cambodia
	Phnom Penh, September 30, 1967

	Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai received a congratulatory telegram from Ahmed Shukeiri, Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization, on the occasion of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The telegram said, "The friendship of the Chinese people and their support for the struggle of the Palestinian Arab people will forever be a shining beacon, illuminating the path to liberation and struggle."

	The telegram said, "Today, as the Palestinian people celebrate this festival, we are extremely proud of the great revolutionary significance that unites our revolutionaries in the struggle against world imperialism. Our people on the occupied land are now shedding their blood for freedom, and they can defend freedom. As long as we persist in armed struggle, we will surely achieve victory."

	The telegram also requested Premier Zhou Enlai to convey his respects and greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and the great people of China.

### 西哈努克亲王电贺我国国庆  巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里电贺我国庆

1967-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王电贺我国国庆
    巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里电贺我国庆
    新华社三日讯　周恩来总理收到了柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王发来的祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年的电报。贺电全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆之际，我高兴地向阁下表示热烈的祝贺以及我本人和高棉人民最良好的祝愿。祝伟大的中国人民幸福、昌盛。
    我借此机会向您重申，我相信我们两国之间的兄弟关系，将在万隆会议原则和在反对美帝的斗争中的完全团结一致的精神的基础上获得发展。
    我向阁下、向贵国政府和中国人民再次表示衷心的感谢，感谢你们对我国建设和国防事业所提供的极其珍贵的援助。中国这一支持，是对我们在发展道路上所作的努力的巨大鼓舞，也是对我们为了保卫受到威胁的独立而进行的斗争的巨大鼓舞。
                          柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克
                               一九六七年九月三十日于金边
    新华社二日讯　周恩来总理收到了巴勒斯坦解放组织主席艾哈迈德·舒凯里发来的祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年的电报。
    贺电说，“中国人民的友谊及其对巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民的斗争的支持，将永远成为光辉的灯塔，照耀着解放和斗争的道路。”
    贺电说，“巴勒斯坦人民今天在欢呼这一节日的时候，对使我们两国革命者在反对世界帝国主义的斗争中联结在一起的重大革命意义感到极为骄傲。在被占领的土地上的我国人民，现在正在为自由付出自己的鲜血，他们能够保卫自由。只要我们把武装斗争进行到底，我们必将取得胜利。”
    贺电还请周恩来总理向毛泽东主席和伟大的中国人民转达他的敬意和问候。
