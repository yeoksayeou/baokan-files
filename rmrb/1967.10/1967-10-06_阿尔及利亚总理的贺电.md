Title: Greetings from the Algerian Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1967-10-06
Edition: 6

	Premier Zhou Enlai received a congratulatory telegram from Algerian People's Democratic Republic Prime Minister and Chairman of the National Revolutionary Council Houari Boumedienne, congratulating the People's Republic of China on the 18th anniversary of its founding. The telegram said, "The great achievements made by the People's Republic of China since its establishment vividly demonstrate the creativity of a people with a bright future. These great achievements serve as a source of hope and inspiration for the African, Asian, and Latin American countries that are engaged in arduous struggles against colonialism and imperialism and for political and social reforms."

### 阿尔及利亚总理的贺电

1967-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚总理的贺电
    新华社五日讯　周恩来总理收到阿尔及利亚民主人民共和国总理、全国革命委员会主席胡阿里·布迈丁发来的贺电，祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年。贺电说，中华人民共和国自成立以来所取得的巨大成就，生动地证明了前途光明的人民所具有的创造性。这些巨大的成就，对于为反对殖民主义和帝国主义、从事政治和社会改革而进行艰苦斗争的非洲、亚洲和拉丁美洲各国来说，是希望和鼓舞的源泉。
