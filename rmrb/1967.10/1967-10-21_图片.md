Title: Workers of the Tianjin Third Woolen Textile Mill Criticize the Crimes of the Chinese Khrushchev
Author: Wáng Xùdōng
Date: 1967-10-21
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers of the Tianjin Third Woolen Textile Mill have thoroughly criticized the heinous crimes of the Chinese Khrushchev, who in 1949, at the predecessor of this mill, the Dongya Woolen Textile Mill, shamelessly advocated the development of capitalism.
