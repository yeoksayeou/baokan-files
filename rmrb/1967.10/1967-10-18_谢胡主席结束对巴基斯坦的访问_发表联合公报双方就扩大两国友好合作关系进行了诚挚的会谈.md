Title: Chairman Shehu Ends Visit to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 6

By a delegation led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Muhammad Shehu, at the invitation of the Pakistani government, visited Pakistan from October 15th to 17th and departed from Karachi by special plane on the morning of the 17th.

Those who went to the airport to see off the Albanian government delegation included Pakistani Minister of Finance Uqaili and other high-ranking military and government officials.

Also present at the airport to see off the Albanian government delegation were the Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Pakistan, Xu Ying, Chinese Consul in Karachi, Shen Peiwen, and diplomatic representatives from other countries in Karachi.

At the same time, the Albania-Pakistan Joint Communiqué was issued here today. The communiqué said that during his stay in Pakistan, Chairman Shehu was warmly welcomed and received by the Pakistani government and people everywhere. The communiqué said that during the visit, the President of Pakistan and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania held sincere talks in a spirit of mutual understanding on further expanding the friendly cooperation between Albania and Pakistan.

The communiqué said that both sides were pleased to note that the relations between the two countries have developed on the basis of sovereign equality, non-interference in internal affairs, and mutual benefit, in the spirit of friendship and cooperation. Both sides agreed to explore the possibility of further expanding cooperation, especially in the economic and trade sectors.

The communiqué said that the Albanian side reiterated that Albania firmly supports the right of self-determination of the people of Jammu and Kashmir.

The communiqué concluded by saying that Chairman Shehu, on behalf of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania and the Albanian government, invited Pakistani President Muhammad Ayub Khan to visit Albania. President Ayub Khan gladly accepted the invitation. The date of the visit will be decided through diplomatic channels in the future.

According to a report from Karachi on the 16th, Pakistani President Ayub Khan held talks with Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Muhammad Shehu, in a friendly atmosphere at the presidential palace in the afternoon of the 15th.

Participants from the Pakistani side included: Foreign Secretary Yusuf, Chief Secretary to the President Fida Hassan, and Director-General of the Foreign Ministry Kamruddin.

Participants from the Albanian side included: Member of the People's Assembly Ramiz Alia, Member of the People's Assembly Miharachi Qishqisti, Political Commissar of the Coastal Defense Forces Rahman Perlak, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Gjirokastra District Telo Mezhini, Albanian Ambassador to Pakistan Ulvi Lulo, and Deputy Director-General of the Second Department of the Foreign Ministry Musin Kroj.

On the evening of the 15th, President Ayub Khan hosted a banquet for the Albanian government delegation led by Chairman Shehu. At the banquet, President Ayub Khan and Chairman Shehu toasted to the prosperity and flourishing of Albania and Pakistan.

### 谢胡主席结束对巴基斯坦的访问　发表联合公报双方就扩大两国友好合作关系进行了诚挚的会谈

1967-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　谢胡主席结束对巴基斯坦的访问
　　发表联合公报双方就扩大两国友好合作关系进行了诚挚的会谈
    据新华社卡拉奇十七日电　由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团，应巴基斯坦政府的邀请，在十五日至十七日访问了巴基斯坦以后，于十七日上午乘专机离开卡拉奇。
    前往机场欢送阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团的有巴基斯坦中央财政部长乌凯利和政府其他高级军政官员。
    前往机场欢送阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团的还有，中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英、中国驻卡拉奇领事沈培文，以及其他国家驻卡拉奇的外交代表。
    同时，今天在这里发表了阿尔巴尼亚—巴基斯坦联合公报。公报说，谢胡主席在巴基斯坦逗留期间，到处受到巴基斯坦政府和人民的热诚欢迎和友好接待。公报说，在访问期间，巴基斯坦总统和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席以互相谅解的精神就进一步扩大阿尔巴尼亚和巴基斯坦之间的友好合作关系进行了诚挚的会谈。
    公报说，双方满意地指出，两国之间的关系在两国的主权平等、互不干涉内政和互利的原则的基础上，本着友好与合作的精神得到了发展。双方同意探求进一步扩大合作，特别是在经济和贸易方面的合作的可能性。
    公报说，阿尔巴尼亚方面重申，阿尔巴尼亚坚决支持查谟和克什米尔人民的自决权利。
    公报最后说，谢胡主席代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团和阿尔巴尼亚政府，邀请巴基斯坦总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅访问阿尔巴尼亚。阿尤布总统欣然接受了这一邀请。访问的日期将在以后通过外交途径决定。
    据新华社卡拉奇十六日电　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统阿尤布·汗，十五日下午在总统府同阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡在友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    巴基斯坦方面参加会谈的有：外交部外事秘书优素福，总统首席秘书菲达·哈桑，外交部司长卡迈鲁丁。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加会谈的有：人民议会议员拉米兹·阿利雅，人民议会议员米哈拉奇·齐奇什蒂，海防部队政委拉赫曼·佩尔拉库，纪诺卡斯特区人民会议执行委员会主席泰洛·麦齐尼，阿尔巴尼亚驻巴基斯坦大使乌尔维·卢洛和外交部第二司司长穆辛·克罗伊。
    十五日晚上，阿尤布·汗总统为谢胡主席率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团举行了宴会。宴会上，阿尤布·汗总统和谢胡主席互相为阿尔巴尼亚和巴基斯坦的繁荣昌盛祝酒。
