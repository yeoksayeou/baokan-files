Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Leaders and Representatives of Revolutionary Organizations in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1967-10-11
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou met with leaders and representatives of revolutionary mass organizations in Wuhan this morning. These organizations include "Steel General Headquarters," "Steel Second Command," "Steel September 13," "New Hua Workers," "New Lake University," "New Hua Farmers," "Three Commands Revolutionary Alliance," "Workers' Revolutionary Headquarters," "Middle School Red Alliance," and "Public Security United Command."

The New China News Agency reported from Wuhan that Premier Zhou had a warm conversation with them. After the meeting, Premier Zhou took a group photo with everyone.

Present at the meeting were Wang Fucheng, Commander of the People's Liberation Army Air Force, and leaders of the Wuhan Military Region, Zhang Siyu and Liu Feng.

In Wuhan, Premier Zhou also met with representatives of the revolutionary masses from Hunan Province.

### 周总理接见武汉革命组织负责人和代表

1967-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见武汉革命组织负责人和代表
    新华社武汉十日电　周恩来总理今天上午在武汉接见了“钢工总”、“钢二司”、“钢九·一三”、“新华工”、“新湖大”、“新华农”、“三司革联”、“工造总司”、“中学红联”、“公安联司”等革命群众组织的负责人和代表。接见时，周恩来总理同他们进行了亲切的交谈。接见后，周总理和大家在一起合影留念。
    接见时在场的，有中国人民解放军空军司令员吴法宪同志以及武汉部队负责人曾思玉、刘丰同志等。
    在武汉，周恩来总理还接见了湖南省无产阶级革命派的代表。
