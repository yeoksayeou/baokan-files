Title: Shukeiri Emphasizes Palestinian People's Rejection of UN's So-Called Middle East "Solution"
Author:
Date: 1967-10-25
Edition: 5

Shukeiri emphasizes Palestinian people's rejection of UN's so-called Middle East "solution"
Rejects any submissive solution

New China News Agency, Algiers. Recently, in Cairo, the Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization, Shukeiri, told a reporter from the Algerian News Agency: "We reject any solution to the Middle East situation that harms the Palestinian people."

Shukeiri made these remarks in the context of the recent intensified collusion between imperialism and revisionism, who, behind the backs of the Palestinian people, are concocting a so-called "solution" within and outside the United Nations.

He emphasized: "No country can speak on behalf of the Palestinian people. The Palestinian people do not depend on any country."

He said: "If the Arab countries, for certain reasons, accept the ceasefire decision of the United Nations Security Council, the Palestinian people will not abide by this decision."

Shukeiri stressed that the Israeli aggressor forces must unconditionally withdraw from the Arab territories they have occupied, and "under no circumstances can this issue be linked to the Palestinian issue."

Shukeiri condemned Israel's plot to establish a so-called "Palestinian government" in the occupied areas.

He said, the Palestine Liberation Organization "rejects any government that is submissive to Israel and is disguised as adhering to the principles of good neighborliness or any colonialist principles."

Shukeiri said: "We insist on our position. The Palestinians in the West Bank and Gaza have the trust and support of the Arab nation. The Palestinian people will one day free themselves from deep suffering and completely control their own destiny."

Shukeiri emphasized that the struggle against Israeli occupation will intensify. He said: "Our patriotic actions will be better carried out with the strengthening of support for our cause from the Arab world and the rest of the world."

### 舒凯里强调巴勒斯坦人民反对联合国炮制所谓中东“解决方案”  决不接受任何屈从性解决办法

1967-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    舒凯里强调巴勒斯坦人民反对联合国炮制所谓中东“解决方案”
    决不接受任何屈从性解决办法
    新华社阿尔及尔电巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里最近在开罗对阿尔及利亚新闻社记者说：“我们拒绝任何损害巴勒斯坦人民的对中东局势的解决办法。”
    舒凯里是在谈到帝国主义和修正主义最近加紧勾结，背着巴勒斯坦人民在联合国内外炮制所谓“解决方案”时说这番话的。
    他强调说：“任何国家都不能代表巴勒斯坦人民说话，巴勒斯坦人民不依附于任何国家。”
    他说：“如果阿拉伯国家为了某些理由而接受联合国安理会的停火决定，巴勒斯坦人民决不遵守这个决定。”
    舒凯里强调指出，以色列侵略军必须无条件地撤出他们侵占的阿拉伯领土，而且“无论如何不能把这个问题同巴勒斯坦问题联系起来”。
    舒凯里谴责以色列企图在侵占区中搞一个所谓“巴勒斯坦政府”的阴谋。
    他说，巴勒斯坦解放组织“拒绝这种屈从于以色列和披着所谓符合睦邻原则的外衣或符合任何殖民主义原则的政府”。
    舒凯里说：“我们坚持我们的立场。外约旦和加沙的巴勒斯坦人得到阿拉伯民族的信任和支持。巴勒斯坦人民总有一天会摆脱深重的苦难，完全自由地掌握自己的命运。”
    舒凯里强调巴勒斯坦人民反对以色列占领的斗争必将加强。他说：“我们的爱国行动将随着阿拉伯和全世界对我们事业的援助的加强而更好地开展起来。”
