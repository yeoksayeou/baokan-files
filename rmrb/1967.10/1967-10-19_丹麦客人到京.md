Title: Danish Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-10-19
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, three Danish friends, including Mrs. Apel, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 17th for a friendly visit to China.

### 丹麦客人到京

1967-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　丹麦客人到京
    新华社十八日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的丹麦友好人士阿佩尔夫人等三人，十七日晚到达北京。
