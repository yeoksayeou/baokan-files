Title: Launching a Campaign to Kill the Enemy and Achieve Meritorious Deeds to Commemorate the Revolutionary Holiday with Excellent Results
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 6

Launched a campaign to kill the enemy and achieve meritorious deeds to commemorate the revolutionary holiday with excellent results

Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Eliminated Over 600 Enemies in September

Military and Civilians in Khammouane and Other Provinces Shot Down and Destroyed Nine Enemy Aircraft

	New China News Agency, Kang Khai, October 11th - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station on the 10th, the military and civilians of various liberated areas in Laos, in order to create achievements to commemorate the Lao Revolutionary Holiday (October 12th), launched a campaign to kill the enemy and achieve meritorious deeds in September, achieving tremendous results.

	According to incomplete statistics, the military and civilians of various liberated areas in Laos engaged in more than 100 battles with the enemy during this period, eliminating 633 enemies, shooting down and destroying 10 enemy aircraft, sinking eight motorboats and patrol boats, blowing up two ammunition depots, and seizing 104 various weapons.

	Another report: On September 17th, the military and civilians of Khammouane Province bravely intercepted the enemy in the Huai Khot area, killing 26 and injuring many.

	The guerrilla forces and local troops of Phongsaly and Xam Neua provinces recently eliminated and captured 35 bandits in anti-bandit operations.

	According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the military and civilians of Khammouane Province in Laos shot down one American aircraft on September 9th, and on September 16th, they shot down two American aircraft. On September 20th, the military and civilians of Sam Neua Province shot down an invading AD-6 type American aggressor aircraft, and on September 21st, the patriotic military and civilians of Sam Neua Province continued to severely punish the invading American air aggressors, shooting down two F-105 type aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Kang Khai, October 11th - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the patriotic military and civilians of Laos shot down and destroyed three enemy aircraft on September 30th. That morning, when two enemy aircraft had just landed at the Bachap airport in Khammouane Province, they were suddenly attacked by the local patriotic military and civilians. These two aircraft were immediately blown to pieces by artillery fire. On the same day in the afternoon, the military and civilians of Ban Faxai also shot down one enemy helicopter.

### 开展杀敌立功竞赛以优异战绩纪念革命节　老挝爱国军民九月份歼敌六百多　川圹等省军民击落击毁敌机九架

1967-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　开展杀敌立功竞赛以优异战绩纪念革命节
　　老挝爱国军民九月份歼敌六百多
　　川圹等省军民击落击毁敌机九架
    新华社康开十一日电  据巴特寮电台十日广播：老挝各解放区军民为了创造成绩纪念老挝革命节（十月十二日），九月份开展杀敌立功竞赛，取得了巨大战果。
    据不完全统计，老挝各解放区军民在此期间同敌人作战一百多次，歼敌六百三十三名，击落击毁敌机十架，击沉汽艇和机动艇八艘，炸毁弹药库两座，缴获各种武器一百零四件。
    另讯：川圹省军民九月十七日在会握地区英勇截击敌人，打死二十六名，打伤多名。
    丰沙里和甘蒙两省游击队和地方部队最近在剿匪战斗中共消灭和俘虏土匪三十五名。
    据新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播，老挝川圹省爱国军民九月九日击落美机一架，九月十六日击落美机两架。老挝桑怒省军民九月二十日击落入侵的AD—6型美国强盗飞机一架，九月二十一日桑怒省爱国军民继续严惩入侵的美国空中强盗，击落美国F—105型飞机两架。
    新华社康开十一日电据巴特寮电台广播，老挝爱国军民九月三十日击毁和击落敌机三架。当天上午，当敌人的两架飞机刚在川圹省巴恰机场降落时，就遭到当地爱国军民的突然袭击。这两架飞机当场被炮火打得粉身碎骨。同日下午，班法巴苏地区军民也击落敌军直升飞机一架。
