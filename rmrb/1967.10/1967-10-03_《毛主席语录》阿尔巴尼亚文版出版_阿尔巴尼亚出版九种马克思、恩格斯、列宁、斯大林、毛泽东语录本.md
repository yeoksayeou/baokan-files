Title: The Albanian Edition of Chairman Mao's Quotations Published
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 6

The Albanian edition of "Chairman Mao's Quotations" has been published. Albania has published nine collections of quotations from Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, October 2 -- Amidst the triumphant songs of our country's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and during the days when our people enthusiastically welcomed the visit of the Albanian Party and Government delegation led by Comrade Shehu, the Albanian edition of "Chairman Mao's Quotations" has recently been published by the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House. This is a great joy in the political life of the peoples of China and Albania, and a brilliant manifestation of the fighting friendship between the two countries.

	The translation and publication of the Albanian edition of "Chairman Mao's Quotations" were carried out with the enthusiastic assistance and close cooperation of the Albanian Labor Party. The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, led by the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, sent Comrades Moisi and Mushi, two experts, to China. The two experts actively and enthusiastically participated in the translation work of "Chairman Mao's Quotations."

	Our translators and revolutionary workers in the publishing and printing industries who participated in the work of the Albanian edition of "Chairman Mao's Quotations" completed this glorious task in a short period of time, with boundless love for our great leader Chairman Mao and deep class feelings for learning and propagating Mao Zedong Thought.

	The Albanian edition of "Chairman Mao's Quotations" with a red plastic cover has added the congratulatory telegram from the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Labor Party before the main text.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, Tirana, October 2 -- Albania has recently published nine collections of quotations from Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong, including "On the Dictatorship of the Proletariat," "The Party Line and the Mass Line," "On Democratic Centralism in the Party," "On Emphasizing Politics," "On Self-Reliance," "On Religion," "On the Army and National Defense," "On Youth," and "On Women." These nine red-covered collections of quotations have been warmly welcomed by the broad revolutionary masses and revolutionary cadres in Albania. These nine collections of quotations, published by the "Naim Frasheri" Publishing House of Albania, contain a total of more than 1,160 quotations from Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and our great leader Chairman Mao.

	The quotations of the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, Chairman Mao, occupy a very important position in these nine collections of quotations.

### 《毛主席语录》阿尔巴尼亚文版出版  阿尔巴尼亚出版九种马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林、毛泽东语录本

1967-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    《毛主席语录》阿尔巴尼亚文版出版
    阿尔巴尼亚出版九种马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林、毛泽东语录本
    新华社二日讯　在我国无产阶级文化大革命取得伟大胜利的凯歌声中，在我国人民热烈欢迎谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访华的日子里，阿尔巴尼亚文版《毛主席语录》日前由北京外文出版社出版。这是中阿两国人民政治生活中的大喜事，是中阿战斗友谊的又一光辉体现。
    《毛主席语录》阿文版的翻译和出版工作，是在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的热情帮助和密切合作下进行的。中国人民最亲密的朋友、阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央，派了莫依修同志和穆希同志两位专家来华。两位专家积极地热情地参与了《毛主席语录》的翻译工作。
    参加《毛主席语录》阿文版工作的我国翻译人员和出版、印刷战线上的革命职工，怀着对我们伟大领袖毛主席的无限热爱，怀着学习和宣传毛泽东思想的深厚的阶级感情，在较短的时间内，完成了这项光荣任务。
    红色塑料封面的《毛主席语录》阿文版，正文前，增加了中国人民伟大领袖毛主席致阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的贺电。
    新华社地拉那二日电　阿尔巴尼亚最近出版了马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林、毛泽东《论无产阶级专政》、《党的路线，群众路线》、《论党的民主集中制》、《论突出政治》、《论自力更生》、《论宗教》、《关于军队和国防》、《关于青年》和《关于妇女》等九种语录本，受到阿尔巴尼亚广大革命群众和革命干部的热烈欢迎。这九本红色封面的语录是由阿尔巴尼亚“纳伊姆·弗拉什里”出版社出版的，共收集了马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林和我们伟大领袖毛主席的语录共一千一百六十多条。
    当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛主席的语录，在这九本语录中占有很重要的地位。
