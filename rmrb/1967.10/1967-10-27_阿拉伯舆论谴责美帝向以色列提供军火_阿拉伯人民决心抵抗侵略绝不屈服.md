Title: Arab Public Opinion Condemns U.S. Imperialism's Supply of Arms to Israel; Arab People Resolute in Resisting Aggression, Refusing to Surrender
Author:
Date: 1967-10-27
Edition: 5

Arab public opinion condemns U.S. imperialism's resumption of arms supply to its tool of aggression, Israel, and denounces Israel's new act of aggression in shelling the Suez city of the United Arab Republic. The official spokesman of the United Arab Republic and public opinion in some Arab countries have expressed their condemnation on October 25.

The official spokesman of the United Arab Republic, Chairman of the UAR News Management Committee, Zaid, said at a press conference in Cairo on the afternoon of October 25 that on the same day when the Israeli aggressor forces launched a new military attack on Suez, the U.S. government announced the provision of new weapons to Israel. The U.S. not only supports the Israeli aggressors diplomatically but also decides to give them weapons. We regret this.

Zaid emphasized that the U.S. is providing weapons to those who are still occupying our territory and killing our people.

Zaid said, "We believe that Israel's recent military provocations and war cries are coming from Washington or at least instigated by Washington."

The Syrian newspaper *Al-Ba'ath* said, "The U.S. provision of weapons to Israel is aimed at making Israel continue its aggression against Arab countries, which began on June 5, and to carry out the imperialist-Zionist plan."

Another Syrian newspaper, *Al-Thawra*, said, "The basic purpose of the U.S. decision is to strengthen Israel's military preparations and encourage it to take new aggressive actions."

The UAR's *Al-Ahram* newspaper, in condemning Israel's new act of aggression, said that this shelling "clearly shows how Israel is resolutely pursuing its hostile and aggressive policy." "The Arab people will never yield to Israel's policy of threats."

The Iraqi newspaper *Al-Thawra al-Arabiya* condemned Israel's new act of aggression against the UAR. It warned Israel: It must remember that the Arab people are determined to resist aggression and are prepared to give the enemy a lesson.

### 阿拉伯舆论谴责美帝向以色列提供军火  阿拉伯人民决心抵抗侵略绝不屈服

1967-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯舆论谴责美帝向以色列提供军火
    阿拉伯人民决心抵抗侵略绝不屈服
    新华社二十六日讯  阿联官方发言人和阿拉伯一些国家的舆论二十五日纷纷指责美帝国主义恢复向其侵略工具以色列提供军火，并谴责以色列侵略军炮轰阿联苏伊士城的新侵略行动。
    阿联官方发言人、阿联新闻管理委员会主席扎耶特二十五日下午在开罗举行的记者招待会上说，就在以色列侵略军对苏伊士发动新的军事攻击的同一天，美国政府宣布向以色列提供新的武器。美国不仅在外交上支持以色列侵略者，而且还决定给它武器。我们对此表示遗憾。
    扎耶特着重指出，美国向仍然占领着我们的领土和不断杀害我们人民的人提供武器。
    扎耶特说，我们认为，以色列最近的军事挑衅和好战的叫嚣都是来自华盛顿或者至少是受到华盛顿的唆使的。
    叙利亚《复兴报》说，美国向以色列提供武器的行动“是要以色列继续进行它在六月五日开始的对阿拉伯国家的侵路，要它执行帝国主义—犹太复国主义的计划”。
    叙利亚的另一家报纸《革命报》说，美国的决定的基本目的是为了加强以色列的军事准备并且鼓励它采取新的侵略行动。
    阿联《金字塔报》在谴责以色列的新侵略行动时说，这次炮轰“清楚地说明，以色列是如何坚决地奉行它的敌对和侵略的政策”。“阿拉伯人民绝不向以色列的威胁政策屈服。”
    伊拉克《阿拉伯革命报》谴责以色列对阿联的新的侵略行动。它警告以色列：它必须记住，阿拉伯人民决心抵抗侵略，并且准备给予敌人以教训。
