Title: Long Live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China!
Author: Laisi·Peiduo
Date: 1967-10-11
Edition: 5

The red torrent surges forward, and countless red flags flutter in the wind. Today, all of China is adorned in brilliant red, and the nation echoes with a thunderous cry: Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

The helmsman of the revolution, Chairman Mao, ascends the Tiananmen Gate, and a million people roar with joy. The entire nation raises a single voice: Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

The magnificent Tiananmen Square is transformed into a sea of flowers. Red blooms cover China's mountains, plains, and villages. The red ocean roars before our eyes, waves crashing with songs: Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

Today, on the square, on the streets, and across the vast expanse of China, only one song resonates—the song of revolution, stirring the hearts of workers, peasants, Red Guards, and soldiers. People gaze up at the Tiananmen Gate. Our Chinese brothers cast warm glances at us, and I salute them. I look at the disciplined ranks of soldiers, awaiting the command to march forward.

The great helmsman, Chairman Mao, waves to the people from the Tiananmen Gate. Friends from all over the world express their deepest revolutionary sentiments in various languages: Long live Chairman Mao! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

I, a soldier from Albania, stand on the reviewing stand, my heart beating with Chairman Mao. The red torrent flows ceaselessly, and the people's cheers echo like thunder. Chairman Mao has led the Chinese people from victory to victory.

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China shakes the earth, striking fear into the hearts of the enemy. The red torrent surges forward with the force of a tidal wave. Chairman Mao stands on the gate, steering China forward with giant strides. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is like a speeding locomotive, crushing all the enemy's schemes and plots, sweeping them away. Revolutionaries from the five continents raise their voices in different languages: Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

On this festive night, Beijing appears even more majestic and splendid. The revolutionary victory song shakes the earth and sky! Colorful fireworks bloom like countless flowers in the sky, and searchlights illuminate the entire city of Beijing. Here, the night is as bright as day. I look at the countless red flags on the ground, and international friends gaze at the red flags dancing in the wind! These friends include guerrillas from South Vietnam, heroes from Latin America, revolutionaries from Africa and Japan, brave warriors from South Asia, and friends of the Chinese people, all gathered in Beijing in October. Everyone shouts together, praising the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China.

With the great helmsman, Chairman Mao, at the helm, the revolutionary ship sails forward against the wind and waves! People shout with joy, and I express the deepest revolutionary feelings of the Albanian people for Chairman Mao and the Chinese people in the loudest voice. We shout a thousand times:

Long live Chairman Mao!
Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

### 万岁，中国无产阶级文化大革命！
莱索尔·培多
1967-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　万岁，中国无产阶级文化大革命！
　　阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会代表团团长　莱索尔·培多
    红色的巨流汹涌奔腾，千万面红旗迎风飘扬。今天，全中国到处都披上了鲜艳的红装，举国上下发出惊天动地的呼声：万岁，无产阶级文化大革命！
    革命舵手毛主席登上了天安门，广场上百万人欢声雷动。全中国发出同一个声音啊！万岁，无产阶级文化大革命！
    宏伟的天安门广场变成一片花海。红花染遍了中国的山河、平原和村城。红色的海洋在眼前咆哮，波涛滚滚响歌声：万岁，无产阶级文化大革命！
    今天，广场上，道路上，中国广阔的大地上只响彻着一支歌——革命之歌，激荡着工人、农民、红卫兵和战士的心胸。人们仰望着天安门城楼。中国兄弟们向我们投来了热情的目光，我向他们致敬。我望着那队列整齐的战士，他们等待着指挥员发出前进的号令。
    伟大的舵手毛主席在天安门城楼上向人民群众挥手致意，来自世界各地的友人用多种语言表达出最美好的革命感情：万岁，毛主席！万岁，无产阶级文化大革命！
    我，一名阿尔巴尼亚战士，站在观礼台上，心儿在毛主席身边跳动。红色的巨流奔腾不息，人们的欢呼声如同风暴雷鸣。过去和今天毛主席把中国人民率领，从胜利走向胜利。
    中国无产阶级文化大革命震撼着大地，敌人被吓得落魄丧魂。那红色的巨流啊！以排山倒海之势向前进。毛主席站在城楼上掌舵，中国以巨人的步伐向前飞奔。中国无产阶级文化大革命象急驰猛过、势不可挡的火车头，它把敌人的一切阴谋诡计碾得粉碎，扫除干净。五大洲的革命者用各种不同的语言共声高呼：万岁，无产阶级文化大革命！
    节日的夜晚，北京显得更加庄严伟丽，革命的凯歌震撼着大地和天空！彩色斑斓的焰火象万朵鲜花在天空盛开，探照灯照亮整个北京城。在这里，夜晚和白天一样一片光明。我望着大地上数不尽的五星红旗，国际友人仰望天空中的红旗舞东风！这些友人有的是来自越南南方的游击队员，有的是拉丁美洲的英雄。那非洲和日本的革命者，南亚的勇敢战士和中国人民的朋友也都一起聚集在十月的北京城。人们一齐欢呼，共同把中国无产阶级文化大革命的胜利战果赞颂。
    伟大舵手毛主席来掌舵，革命航船乘风破浪向前进！人们尽情的欢呼，我也用最响亮的声音表达出阿尔巴尼亚人民对毛主席、对中国人民的革命深情。我们千百次地高呼啊：
    万岁，毛主席！
    万岁，无产阶级文化大革命！
