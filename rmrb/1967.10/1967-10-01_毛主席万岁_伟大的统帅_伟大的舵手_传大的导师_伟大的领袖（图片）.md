Title: Long Live Chairman Mao
Author: Lü Xiangyou, Jiang Tao
Date: 1967-10-01
Edition: 4

Our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao are in such good health, and their steps are so vigorous, which gives us boundless strength. We must redouble our efforts to study and apply Chairman Mao's works, closely follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployments, and achieve a thorough victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

	Our dearest Red Sun Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao, as well as Comrade Zhou Enlai, are on Tiananmen.
