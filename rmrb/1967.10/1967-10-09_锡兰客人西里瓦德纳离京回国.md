Title: Sri Lankan Guest Sirimavo Bandaranaike Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1967-10-09
Edition: 5

Sri Lankan Guest Sirimavo Bandaranaike Leaves Beijing for Home

New China News Agency, October 5th: Sri Lankan Freedom Party member and leader of the Sri Lankan opposition in the parliament, Sirimavo Bandaranaike, left Beijing for her home country on the 4th.

Sirimavo Bandaranaike came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a friendly visit.

She visited Beijing, Jinan, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, among other cities, and participated in the national day celebrations marking the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 锡兰客人西里瓦德纳离京回国

1967-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰客人西里瓦德纳离京回国
    新华社五日讯　锡兰自由党议员、锡兰议会反对党领袖西里瓦德纳四日离开北京回国。
    西里瓦德纳是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    他先后访问了北京、济南、上海和杭州等城市，并参加了中华人民共和国成立十八周年国庆典礼。
