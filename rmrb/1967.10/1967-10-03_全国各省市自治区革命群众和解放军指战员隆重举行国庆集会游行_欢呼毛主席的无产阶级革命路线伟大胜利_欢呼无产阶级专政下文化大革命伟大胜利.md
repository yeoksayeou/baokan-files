Title: Nationwide Celebrations for the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 2

Revolutionary masses and PLA soldiers across the country held grand rallies and parades to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, hailing the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line and the great victory of the Cultural Revolution under the dictatorship of the proletariat.

The revolutionary masses and PLA soldiers in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions held grand rallies and parades on October 1 to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. They enthusiastically hailed the great victory of the Cultural Revolution in China, the great victory of socialist revolution and socialist construction, and the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line. The broad revolutionary masses and PLA soldiers resolutely declared that they would hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, and under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, in accordance with the principle of "criticizing the self and opposing revisionism," launch a broader and deeper wave of revolutionary criticism, revolutionary unity, and revolutionary "three-in-one" combination. They will consolidate and strengthen the dictatorship of the proletariat, further implement Chairman Mao's great call for "supporting the army and loving the people" and "grasping revolution and promoting production," and achieve a complete victory in the Cultural Revolution.

    长　沙
