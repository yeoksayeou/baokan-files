Title: A Soviet Revisionist Propaganda Poster
Author:
Date: 1967-10-27
Edition: 6

This poster, published by the Soviet State Publishing Committee, features a ruble at its center. It propagates the reactionary ideology that the bourgeoisie believes in—money can buy everything. This is a poisonous weed of revisionist thought.
