Title: Photograph of China's Hydrogen Bomb Explosion
Author:
Date: 1967-10-01
Edition: 8

To our great teacher, great leader, great commander-in-chief, and great helmsman Chairman Mao! To the great Chinese People's Liberation Army, which Chairman Mao personally founded and personally leads, and which Vice Chairman Lin directly commands, on its 40th anniversary! To the 18th anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China!

The workers, engineers, technicians, scientists, and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who participated in the research, design, production, and testing of the hydrogen bomb respectfully dedicate this photograph.

September 30, 1967
(The photographs in this issue are provided by Xinhua News Agency)
