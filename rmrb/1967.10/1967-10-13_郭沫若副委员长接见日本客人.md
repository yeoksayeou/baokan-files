Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-10-13
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met with a delegation from the Japanese China News Service led by Jiren Chiwei this evening and had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十二日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天晚上接见了由持永只仁率领的日本中国通讯社访华小组，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
