Title: The Newly Adapted Play "Huai Shu Zhuang" is Excellent
Author: chen yong gui
Date: 1967-10-08
Edition: 6

	The newly adapted play "Huai Shu Zhuang" is excellent
	Chen Yonggui

	In the current nationwide wave of military and civilian efforts to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, launching a great criticism, a great unity, and a new high tide of supporting the military and loving the people, I am pleased to have seen the performance of the newly adapted play "Huai Shu Zhuang" by the revolutionary cultural warriors of the Beijing garrison of the People's Liberation Army. This play is well-adapted. It is a victory for Mao Zedong Thought and another contribution by the Beijing garrison's revolutionary cultural warriors to the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	The newly adapted "Huai Shu Zhuang" is a play that emphasizes politics and truly meets the needs of the countryside. The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants will all praise it. Because it vividly portrays the firm will of the broad masses of community members who never forget class struggle and resolutely take the socialist road; it fully demonstrates the superiority of the collective economy; it truthfully portrays the proletarian feelings of the poor and lower-middle peasants who love Chairman Mao, love Mao Zedong Thought, and are loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. It powerfully refutes the reactionary fallacies of the Chinese Khrushchev, such as the theory of the "extinction of class struggle" and the development of the "three horses, one plow, and one cart" rich peasant economy, greatly boosting the morale of the poor and lower-middle peasants and thoroughly crushing the arrogance of the class enemy.

	The newly adapted "Huai Shu Zhuang" is a powerful weapon for criticizing the black "Reminiscences" of the Chinese Khrushchev and a good teaching material. This play, armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, exposes the living facts of the two-line struggle in the countryside through the method of presenting facts, powerfully exposing the Chinese Khrushchev's conspiracy to restore capitalism in the countryside. This play has great and profound educational significance.

	In the newly adapted "Huai Shu Zhuang," the poor and lower-middle peasants have become the masters of the stage. The play is performed very realistically and warmly, as if it were written and performed by the peasants themselves. The scholars and beauties have been removed from the stage, and history, which was once turned upside down, has been turned right side up again. It is truly excellent!

	The newly adapted "Huai Shu Zhuang" fully demonstrates the boundless creativity of the people. The portrayal of the characters in this play is very good and successful. This shows that the revolutionary cultural warriors of the Beijing garrison have listened to Chairman Mao's words and are striving to learn from the workers, peasants, and soldiers, create for them, serve them, and serve proletarian politics. They have made rapid progress. I believe they will continue to strive and improve, creating more and better new plays to serve the workers, peasants, and soldiers and serve proletarian politics.

### 新改编的话剧《槐树庄》好得很
陈永贵
1967-10-08
第6版()
专栏：

    新改编的话剧《槐树庄》好得很
　　陈永贵
    在当前全国军民紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，掀起革命的大批判，革命的大联合和拥军爱民的新高潮中，我高兴地看到了中国人民解放军驻京部队革命文艺战士演出的新改编的话剧《槐树庄》，这个戏改得好。这是毛泽东思想的胜利，是驻京部队革命文艺战士对无产阶级文化大革命的又一个贡献。
    新改编的《槐树庄》，是一个突出政治，真正适应农村需要的好戏。广大贫下中农看了都会称赞的。因为，它把广大的社员念念不忘阶级斗争，坚决走社会主义道路的坚强意志集中地表现出来了；把集体经济的优越性充分地表示了出来；把贫下中农无限热爱毛主席，无限热爱毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席的革命路线的无产阶级感情如实地表现出来了。它有力地驳斥了中国赫鲁晓夫的“阶级斗争熄灭”论和发展“三马一犁一车”富农经济等反革命谬论，大长了贫下中农的志气，大灭了阶级敌人的威风。
    新改编的《槐树庄》，是批判中国赫鲁晓夫黑《修养》的有力武器，是教育群众的好教材、活教材。这个戏以战无不胜的毛泽东思想为武器，通过摆事实的方法，把农村的两条道路、两条路线斗争的活生生的事实都摆出来了，有力地揭穿了中国赫鲁晓夫在农村复辟资本主义的阴谋。这个戏教育意义很大，很深刻。
    在新改编的《槐树庄》中，贫下中农成了文艺舞台的主人。戏演得很逼真，很亲切，好象是农民自编自演的。工农兵上了台，才子佳人滚下来。被颠倒的历史颠倒过来了，真是好得很！
    新改编的《槐树庄》充分显示了人民群众有无限的创造力。这个戏对人物的描写是很好的，很成功的。这说明驻京部队革命文艺战士听了毛主席的话，他们是在努力地向工农兵学习，为工农兵创作，为工农兵服务，为无产阶级政治服务，提高得很快。我相信他们一定会不断努力，不断提高，创造出更多更好的新戏来，为工农兵服务，为无产阶级政治服务。
                            （新华社）
