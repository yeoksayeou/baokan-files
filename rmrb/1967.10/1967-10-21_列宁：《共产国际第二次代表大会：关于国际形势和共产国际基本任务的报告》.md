Title: Lenin: Report on the International Situation and the Basic Tasks of the Communist International at the Second Congress of the Communist International
Author: Liening
Date: 1967-10-21
Edition: 4

Opportunism is our main enemy. The opportunism of the upper strata in the workers' movement is not proletarian socialism, but bourgeois socialism. It has been proven that maintaining the bourgeoisie through the activities of opportunist elements within the workers' movement is even better than the bourgeoisie doing it themselves. This is our main enemy, and we must defeat this enemy.
