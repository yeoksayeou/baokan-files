Title: Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Issues Statement to Celebrate the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1967-10-09
Edition: 5

    Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Issues Statement to Celebrate the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
    China's Revolution Provides an Example for All Revolutionaries Worldwide
    Praises the Significance of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution for the Future of the World Revolution

    Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Rome News: The Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) issued a statement to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, pointing out: "All true Marxist-Leninists in the world must learn from the rich experience of the Chinese revolution and draw strength from it."

    The latest issue of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) organ, the *New Unity*, published this statement under the headline "Long Live the People's Republic of China." The statement emphasizes that the Chinese revolution has proven the principle that "political power grows out of the barrel of a gun." All true communists and Marxist-Leninists in the world must deeply understand the historical experience of the Chinese revolution under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought in order to correctly carry out the revolution.

    The statement praises the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution initiated and led by Comrade Mao Zedong. The statement points out that under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has solved the decisive problem of how to consolidate the power after the proletariat seizes it, prevent capitalist restoration, and push the revolution forward. Therefore, the attitude towards the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a decisive factor in distinguishing true Marxists and Marxist-Leninists from enemies of Marxism-Leninism and pseudo-revolutionaries.

    The statement points out that imperialism, revisionism, and all reactionaries are trying to distort the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, but this does not in the least diminish its universal significance. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is not only of decisive significance for building socialism and communism in China, but also for the future of the world revolution. The statement stresses that under the guidance of the great Mao Zedong Thought, the Chinese revolution provides a lasting and universally significant example for all true Marxist-Leninists in the world to carry out revolutionary struggles.

### 意共（马列）为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年发表声明  中国革命为全世界革命者提供了范例  赞扬中国文化大革命对世界革命的前途具有决定性意义

1967-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    意共（马列）为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年发表声明
    中国革命为全世界革命者提供了范例
    赞扬中国文化大革命对世界革命的前途具有决定性意义
    新华社五日讯　罗马消息：意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）书记处为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年发表声明指出：“全世界一切真正的马克思列宁主义者都必须从中国革命的丰富经验中吸取教益和力量”。
    意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）机关报《新团结报》最近一期在《中华人民共和国万岁》的标题下刊载了这一声明。声明强调说，中国革命证实了“枪杆子里面出政权”的原理。全世界一切真正的共产党人和马克思列宁主义者要正确地进行革命，就必须更加深刻地领会在毛泽东思想指导下的中国革命的历史经验。
    声明赞扬由毛泽东同志亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命。声明指出，在毛泽东思想指引下的中国无产阶级文化大革命，解决了无产阶级夺取政权后如何巩固政权、防止资本主义复辟和把革命推向前进这个决定性的问题。因此，对待无产阶级文化大革命的态度，是区分真正的马克思主义者和马克思列宁主义的敌人以及假革命者的决定性因素。
    声明指出，帝国主义、修正主义和一切反动派极力歪曲中国无产阶级文化大革命，但是，这丝毫无损于中国无产阶级文化大革命的普遍意义。中国无产阶级文化大革命不仅对于在中国建设社会主义和共产主义，而且还对全世界的革命前途具有决定性意义。声明强调指出，在伟大的毛泽东思想指导下的中国革命，为全世界一切真正马克思列宁主义者进行革命斗争提供了一个具有永久、普遍意义的范例。
