Title: The First Volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published in Burmese and Indonesian; "Quotations from Chairman Mao" Published in Thai, Hindi, and Other Foreign Languages
Author:
Date: 1967-10-20
Edition: 1

The first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in Burmese and Indonesian, and "Quotations from Chairman Mao" in Thai, Hindi, Urdu, Mongolian, Norwegian, Portuguese, Esperanto, and Hausa, have been published by the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House.

The first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in Thai, Japanese, Vietnamese, Spanish, Arabic, Russian, German, Korean, Italian, and Persian will also be published in succession. The recently published Burmese version is a 32-opener, with a red plastic cover, and each volume is divided into upper and lower parts; the Indonesian version is a 16-opener, available in both hardcover and paperback.

The English, French, Spanish, Russian, German, Japanese, Arabic, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Burmese, Swahili, Albanian, Italian, Persian, and Nepali versions of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," totaling fifteen languages, have been published since last September. The Lao, Bengali, Korean, and Tamil versions will also be published in succession. The design of the foreign language versions is the same as the Chinese version.

The English and Japanese versions of "Selected Readings from the Works of Mao Zedong" will be published in the fourth quarter of this year. The English version is a 16-opener, and the Japanese version is a 32-opener, available in both hardcover and paperback.

### 《毛泽东选集》第一卷缅甸、印尼文版出版　《毛主席语录》泰、印地等多种外文版出版

1967-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　《毛泽东选集》第一卷缅甸、印尼文版出版
　　《毛主席语录》泰、印地等多种外文版出版
    新华社十九日讯　《毛泽东选集》第一卷的缅甸、印度尼西亚文版和《毛主席语录》的泰、印地、乌尔都、蒙古、挪威、葡萄牙、世界语和豪萨文版，已由北京外文出版社出版。
    《毛泽东选集》第一卷的泰、日、越、西班牙、阿拉伯、俄、德、朝鲜、意大利、波斯等文版，也将陆续出版。最近出版的缅文版为三十二开本，红塑料封面，每卷分上下册；印度尼西亚文版为十六开本，分精装和平装两种。
    《毛主席语录》的英、法、西班牙、俄、德、日、阿拉伯、越、印度尼西亚、缅甸、斯瓦西里、阿尔巴尼亚、意大利、波斯和尼泊尔等十五种文版，自去年九月份起，已先后出版。老挝、孟加拉、朝鲜和泰米尔文版，也将陆续出版。外文版的装帧与中文版相同。
    《毛泽东著作选读》的英、日文版，于今年第四季度出版。英文版为十六开本，日文版为三十二开本，分精装和平装两种。
