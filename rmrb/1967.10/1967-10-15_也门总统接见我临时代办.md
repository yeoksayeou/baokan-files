Author:
Date: 1967-10-15
Edition: 6

Yemeni President Receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires

SANAA, Oct. 13 (Xinhua) — President of the Yemen Arab Republic, Abdul Rahman al-Iryani, received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Yemen, Zheng Kangping, on the evening of October 10. The two had a very friendly conversation.

President al-Iryani said to Zheng, "Please convey my and the fighting people of Yemen's greetings to Chairman Mao and the fighting people of China."

### 也门总统接见我临时代办

1967-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　也门总统接见我临时代办
    新华社萨那十三日电　阿拉伯也门共和国总统萨拉勒十日晚上接见了中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平，并同他进行了十分友好的谈话。
    萨拉勒总统对郑康平临时代办说：“请转达我和战斗的也门人民对毛主席和战斗的中国人民的问候。”
