Title: Stalin: On Some Questions of Leninism
Author: 斯大林
Date: 1967-10-17
Edition: 4

	Stalin: On Some Questions of Leninism

	Can the bourgeois old system be fundamentally transformed without a violent revolution and without the dictatorship of the proletariat? Clearly, it cannot. If one thinks that such a revolution can be carried out within the framework of bourgeois democracy, which is suitable for bourgeois rule, in a peaceful manner, then that person is either mentally deranged, suffering from a nervous breakdown, or openly betraying the proletarian revolution.

### 斯大林：《论列宁主义的几个问题》

1967-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　斯大林：《论列宁主义的几个问题》
    没有暴力革命，没有无产阶级专政，能不能对资产阶级旧制度进行根本的改造呢？显然是不能的。如果以为可以在适合于资产阶级统治的资产阶级民主制范围内，用和平方式来进行这样的革命，那不是精神错乱，神经失常，就是公然无耻地背叛了无产阶级革命。
