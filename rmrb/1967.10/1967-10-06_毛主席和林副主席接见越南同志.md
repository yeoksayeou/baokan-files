Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Vietnamese Comrades
Author: Lv Xiangyou
Date: 1967-10-06
Edition: 1

Our great leader and his close comrade-in-arms warmly welcomed the Vietnamese party and government delegation from the front lines of the anti-American armed struggle and had an extremely cordial conversation with them.

Our great leader and his close comrade-in-arms warmly shook hands with the heroic fighters from the front lines of the anti-American patriotic war in South Vietnam. Amidst enthusiastic applause, Huang Wendan, the head of the delegation, presented a banner from the National Liberation Front to Chairman Mao. Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Our most beloved and respected great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, received today in the afternoon Li Qingyi, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Huang Wenhuan, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the entire delegation of the Vietnamese party and government led by them. During the reception, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin warmly shook hands with Li Qingyi, Huang Wenhuan, and other comrades, expressing their warm welcome to the Vietnamese comrades from the front lines of the anti-American armed struggle. Subsequently, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin had an extremely cordial conversation with Li Qingyi, Huang Wenhuan, and members of the Vietnamese party and government delegation Li Ban and Wu Mingluan. Those present at the reception included Zhou Enlai, Nie Rongzhen, Yang Chengwu, and Su Yu.

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Our most beloved and respected great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, received today in the afternoon the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam led by the heroic fighter Huang Wendan. During the reception, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin warmly shook hands with the heroic fighters from the front lines of the anti-American patriotic war in South Vietnam, expressing their welcome. Amidst enthusiastic applause, Huang Wendan, the head of the delegation, presented a banner from the National Liberation Front to Chairman Mao. The banner was embroidered in both Vietnamese and Chinese, reading: "The combat unity between the people of South Vietnam and the people of China is unbreakable." Subsequently, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin had an extremely cordial conversation with Huang Wendan, the head of the delegation, and members of the delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong, Zhang Ping, Zhuang Xianshen, Li Zhiyuan, Wu Shixue, Nguyen Ngoc Boi, Nguyen Thanh Quang, and Pei Wenbing. Those present at the reception included Zhou Enlai, Nie Rongzhen, Yang Chengwu, and Su Yu.

Our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin received the entire delegation of the Vietnamese party and government led by Li Qingyi (front left four) and Huang Wenhuan (front right four).

### 毛主席和林副主席接见越南同志
吕相友
1967-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和林副主席接见越南同志
    周恩来、聂荣臻、杨成武、粟裕同志陪同接见
    我们伟大的领袖和他的亲密战友，同黎清毅、黄文欢等同志一一握手，热烈欢迎来自反美武装斗争前线的越南党政代表团，并同他们进行了极其亲切的谈话。
    我们伟大的领袖和他的亲密战友，同来自反美爱国战争前线的越南南方战斗英雄亲切握手。在热烈掌声中，黄文旦团长把民族解放阵线一面锦旗献给毛主席。新华社五日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖、中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今天下午接见了越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅同志，越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会副主席黄文欢同志，以及由他们率领的越南民主共和国党政代表团全体成员。接见时，毛主席和林彪同志，同黎清毅、黄文欢等同志，一一亲切握手，热烈欢迎来自反美武装斗争前线的越南同志。随后，毛主席和林彪同志同黎清毅同志、黄文欢同志，以及越南党政代表团团员李班、吴明鸾同志，进行了极其亲切的谈话。陪同接见的，有周恩来、聂荣臻、杨成武、粟裕同志。新华社五日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖、中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今天下午接见了由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团全体成员。接见时，毛主席和林彪同志，同来自反美爱国战争前线的越南南方的战斗英雄们，亲切握手，表示欢迎。在一片热烈的掌声中，黄文旦团长把越南南方民族解放阵线的一面锦旗献给毛主席。锦旗上用越中两国文字绣着：“越南南方人民和中国人民的战斗团结是牢不可破的。”随后，毛主席和林彪同志同黄文旦团长，以及代表团成员阮明芳、张平、庄贤笙、黎志愿、吴氏雪、阮玉裴、阮成广、裴文炳，进行了极其亲切的谈话。同毛主席和林彪同志一起接见的，有周恩来、聂荣臻、杨成武、粟裕同志。（附图片）
    我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，接见由黎清毅同志（前左四）和黄文欢同志（前右四）率领的越南党政代表团全体成员。
    我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，接见由战斗英雄黄文旦（前左五）率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团全体成员。　本报记者　吕相友摄
