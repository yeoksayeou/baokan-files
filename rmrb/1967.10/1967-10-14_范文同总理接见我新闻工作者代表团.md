Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-10-14
Edition: 6

Premier Pham Van Dong of the Vietnamese Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau and the Government received the Chinese journalists delegation led by Guo Longchun on the 12th. Premier Pham Van Dong had a cordial conversation with the delegation. Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee Vu Quoc Oai and others. Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Vietnam Lu Weizhao was also present.

### 范文同总理接见我新闻工作者代表团

1967-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　范文同总理接见我新闻工作者代表团
    新华社河内十三日电　越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、政府总理范文同，十二日接见了由郭龙春率领的中国新闻工作者代表团。范文同总理同代表团进行了亲切的交谈。接见时在座的有越南对外文化联络委员会副主任武国威等。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊也在座。
