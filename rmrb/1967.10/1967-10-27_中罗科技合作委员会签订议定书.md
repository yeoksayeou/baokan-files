Title: The Sino-Romanian Science and Technology Cooperation Committee Signs a Protocol
Author:
Date: 1967-10-27
Edition: 5

The Sino-Romanian Science and Technology Cooperation Committee's twelfth session was held in Beijing from October 12 to 23, where a protocol was signed. Signing on behalf of China was the deputy chairman of the Chinese side of the committee and vice-minister of the Petroleum Industry, Sun Xiaofeng. Signing on behalf of Romania was the vice-chairman of the Romanian State Committee for Construction, Building, and Planning, Bubuca Vasile.

The Romanian delegation departed Beijing on October 25 to return home.

### 中罗科技合作委员会签订议定书

1967-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗科技合作委员会签订议定书
    新华社二十五日讯　中罗科学技术合作委员会第十二届会议于十月十二日至二十三日在北京召开并签订了议定书。在议定书上签字的，中国方面是委员会中国组代理主席、石油工业部副部长孙晓风；罗马尼亚方面是罗马尼亚国家建设、建筑和规划委员会副主席布伯卡·瓦西莱。
    罗马尼亚代表团已在十月二十五日离开北京回国。
