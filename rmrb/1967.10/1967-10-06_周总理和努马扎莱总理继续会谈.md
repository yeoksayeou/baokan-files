Title: Premier Zhou and Premier Noumazalaye Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1967-10-06
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Ambroise Noumazalaye of the Republic of Congo (Brazzaville) continued their talks this afternoon. The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理和努马扎莱总理继续会谈

1967-10-06
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理和努马扎莱总理继续会谈
    新华社五日讯　国务院总理周恩来同刚果共和国（布拉柴维尔）总理安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱，今天下午继续进行会谈。会谈在友好气氛中进行。
