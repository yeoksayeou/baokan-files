Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-10-04
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou today afternoon met with Pakistani Minister of Information and Broadcasting Khawaja Shahabuddin and his wife, as well as members of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation led by Shahabuddin. They had a cordial and friendly conversation. Attending the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan was also present.

### 周总理接见巴基斯坦政府友好代表团

1967-10-04
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴基斯坦政府友好代表团
    新华社三日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了巴基斯坦新闻和广播部长赫瓦贾·夏哈布丁和夫人，以及由夏哈布丁率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有外交部副部长韩念龙。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也在座。
