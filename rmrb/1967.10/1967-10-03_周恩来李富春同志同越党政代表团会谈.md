Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Li Fuchun Meet with the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation
Author: Sang Fubao
Date: 1967-10-03
Edition: 6

	Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Li Fuchun Meet with the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation

	Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd—Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, and Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun, held talks this afternoon with the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation led by Politburo Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Le Quoc Vui, and Politburo Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Van Huan.

	The talks were conducted in a very friendly atmosphere, reflecting the fighting friendship between the two parties, two countries, and two peoples of China and Vietnam.

	Present at the talks from the Chinese side were: Su Yu, Fang Yi, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, and other relevant leaders; from the Vietnamese side were: Delegates Le Ban, Vu Minh Loan, and others from the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation. (Photo attached)

	The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Li Fuchun holding talks with the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation led by Le Quoc Vui and Nguyen Van Huan.

### 周恩来李富春同志同越党政代表团会谈
桑福宝
1967-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来李富春同志同越党政代表团会谈
    新华社二日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来同志，中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理李富春同志，今天下午同以越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅同志，越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会副主席黄文欢同志为首的越南党政代表团举行会谈。
    会谈在十分亲切的气氛中进行，体现了中越两党、两国和两国人民的战斗友谊。
    参加会谈的，中国方面有：粟裕、方毅、韩念龙、李强等有关方面负责人；越南方面有：越南党政代表团团员李班、吴明鸾等。（附图片）
    图为周恩来、李富春同志同以黎清毅、黄文欢同志为首的越南党政代表团会谈。
