Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Meets with Japanese Guests

New China News Agency, October 29th — Premier Zhou Enlai met with the members of the 16th delegation to China from the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox faction) led by Yoshida Hōsei this evening and had a very friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Zhou Yiping and others. (Photo attached)

The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai posing with Japanese friends. Yoshida Hōsei is the fourth person from the left in the front row.	New China News Agency photographer Wang Xinqing

### 周总理接见日本客人

1967-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本客人
    新华社二十九日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了由吉田法晴率领的日中友协（正统）总部第十六次访华代表团的成员，同他们进行了十分友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有周一萍等。（附图片）
    图为周恩来总理接见日本朋友时合影。前左四是吉田法晴。				　新华社记者　王新庆摄
