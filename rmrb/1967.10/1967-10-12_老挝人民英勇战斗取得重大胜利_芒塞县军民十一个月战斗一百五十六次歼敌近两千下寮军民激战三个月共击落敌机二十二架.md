Lao People's Heroic Struggles Achieve Major Victories

	Moung Se County Military and Civilians Fight 156 Battles in 11 Months, Annihilate Nearly 2,000 Enemies

	Lao Civilians Fight Bravely for 3 Months, Shoot Down 22 Enemy Aircraft

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, October 11th, according to the Pathet Lao radio broadcast, the Lao patriotic military and civilians have been fighting bravely, annihilating a large number of enemies, and achieving major victories.

	The report said that from October 13th last year to September 17th this year, the patriotic military and civilians of Moung Se County fought 156 large and small battles with the enemy, killing and dissolving 1,849 enemy soldiers, including 1,231 killed, 470 wounded, and 114 captured.

	The patriotic military and civilians of this county also shot down 5 American planes and damaged 6; they sank 25 enemy motorboats and motorized boats and damaged 23; they seized more than 600 weapons, including 49 various types of cannons and 83 machine guns; in addition, they also seized a large amount of military supplies.

	The patriotic military and civilians of the Nam Bo region in Lao's Luang Prabang Province, on September 17th and 18th, consecutively attacked two enemy camps stationed in the Banla Kong and Mou Lo regions southwest of Nam Bo, killing 120 enemy soldiers, severely damaging one of the camps, and seizing and destroying many weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

	In recent times, the patriotic military and civilians in the Nam Bo region have been very active. They killed and wounded 132 enemy soldiers in the first half of September alone.

	According to another broadcast, the guerrillas and local forces of Luang Prabang Province also consecutively attacked and intercepted the enemy on September 18th and 19th, killing and wounding 40 enemy soldiers.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 10th, according to the Pathet Lao radio broadcast on September 9th, the patriotic military and civilians in the southern region of Lao fought bravely in the first three months of the rainy season (from June to August), fiercely striking down American air pirates, shooting down a total of 22 American planes (including the previously announced 12 planes).

### 老挝人民英勇战斗取得重大胜利　芒塞县军民十一个月战斗一百五十六次歼敌近两千下寮军民激战三个月共击落敌机二十二架

　　老挝人民英勇战斗取得重大胜利
　　芒塞县军民十一个月战斗一百五十六次歼敌近两千下寮军民激战三个月共击落敌机二十二架
    据新华社康开十一日电　据巴特寮电台广播，老挝爱国军民坚持战斗，歼灭了大量敌人，取得重大胜利。
    报道说，从去年十月十三日到今年九月十七日，芒塞县爱国军民同敌人进行大小战斗一百五十六次，消灭和瓦解敌人一千八百四十九名，其中包括打死一千二百三十一名，打伤四百七十名，俘虏一百一十四名。
    这个县的爱国军民还击落美国飞机五架，击伤六架；击沉敌军汽艇和机动艇二十五艘，击伤二十三艘；缴获武器六百多件，其中包括各种炮四十九门和机枪八十三挺；此外还缴获了大批军用物资。
    老挝琅勃拉邦省南博地区军民，在九月十七和十八日两天中，接连袭击盘踞在南博西南的班拉孔和木洛地区的两营敌军，共消灭敌人一百二十名，重创其中的一个营，缴获和击毁许多武器、弹药和军用物资。
    最近一个时期，爱国军民在南博地区十分活跃。他们仅在九月上半月，就打死打伤敌人一百三十二名。
    另据广播，琅勃拉邦省游击队和地方部队，九月十八日和十九日也接连袭击和截击敌人，共打死打伤敌人四十名。
    新华社河内十日电据巴特寮电台九日补充报道，老挝下寮地区爱国军民在雨季头三个月（从六月到八月）英勇奋战，痛击美国空中强盗，共击落美机二十二架（包括以前公布的十二架）。
