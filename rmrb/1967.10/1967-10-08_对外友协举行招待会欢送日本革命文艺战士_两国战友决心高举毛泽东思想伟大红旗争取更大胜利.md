Title: Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Holds Reception to See Off Japanese Revolutionary Artists
Author:
Date: 1967-10-08
Edition: 7

Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Holds Reception to See Off Japanese Revolutionary Artists
Two Countries' Comrades Resolve to Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought and Strive for Greater Victories

According to Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: "Long live our dearest and most beloved Red Sun, Chairman Mao! Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought! Long live the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Japan!" At the reception held by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to see off the Japanese Hishakuza Theater Troupe, Chinese and Japanese revolutionary artists repeatedly shouted these slogans, encouraging each other to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought in the future revolutionary struggle, to adhere to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line in literature and arts, and to strive for new and greater victories.

Hishakuza Theater Troupe leader Fujikawa Natsuko spoke passionately, saying that the great leader Chairman Mao's health is so robust, which is our greatest happiness and the greatest happiness for the revolutionary people of the world. We sincerely wish Chairman Mao eternal life!

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo attended the reception.

Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Chu Tu-nan, spoke first at the reception. He warmly congratulated the success of Hishakuza's visit to China, praising them for spreading the revolutionary spirit of rebellion among the Japanese people, and for their eager study of Chairman Mao's works, resolutely advancing in the direction indicated by Chairman Mao.

The delegation of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, currently visiting Beijing, was also invited to the reception. The delegation leader, Kawahara Kuma no Suke, expressed in his speech that they would definitely join forces with the comrades of Hishakuza to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought in Japan, to fiercely oppose the U.S.-Japan reactionaries, to fiercely oppose Soviet modern revisionism, and to fiercely oppose the revisionist group of the Japanese Communist Party led by Miyamoto.

According to Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The Japanese Hishakuza Theater Troupe visited the revolutionary holy land of Yan'an from October 3rd to 5th, with boundless reverence, they toured the places where the great leader Chairman Mao had lived and worked.

During their visit to Yan'an, they learned that many of Chairman Mao's brilliant works were written in Yan'an. With immense respect, they enthusiastically praised the great leader Chairman Mao as the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, and praised Chairman Mao for his brilliant and comprehensive inheritance and defense of Marxism-Leninism.

### 对外友协举行招待会欢送日本革命文艺战士  两国战友决心高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗争取更大胜利

1967-10-08
第7版()
专栏：

    对外友协举行招待会欢送日本革命文艺战士
    两国战友决心高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗争取更大胜利
    据新华社六日讯　“敬祝我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席万寿无疆！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”“中日两国人民的战斗友谊万岁！”在对外友协为欢送日本齿轮座剧团举行的招待会上，中日两国革命文艺战士一次又一次地高呼着这些口号，互相勉励在今后的革命斗争中，要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚持毛主席的革命文艺路线，不断争取新的更大的胜利。
    齿轮座剧团团长藤川夏子在讲话中满怀激情地说，伟大领袖毛主席的身体那样健康，是我们最大的幸福，是世界革命人民的最大幸福。我们衷心祝愿毛主席万寿无疆！
    人大常委会副委员长郭沫若出席了招待会。
    对外友协会长楚图南在招待会上首先讲话。他热烈祝贺齿轮座访问中国的成功，赞扬他们传播了日本人民的革命造反精神，赞扬他们如饥似渴地学习毛主席著作，坚决沿着毛主席指引的方向前进。
    正在北京访问的日中文化交流协会代表团应邀参加了招待会。代表团团长河原崎虎之助在讲话中表示，他们一定要和齿轮座的同志们一道，在日本高高举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，大造美日反动派的反，大造苏联现代修正主义的反，大造日共宫本修正主义集团的反。
    据新华社六日讯　日本齿轮座剧团十月三日到五日访问了革命圣地延安，以无限敬仰的心情，参观了伟大领袖毛主席当年居住和工作过的地方。
    在访问延安的日子里，他们了解到毛主席的光辉著作，有许多就是在延安写成的。他们怀着无比崇敬的心情，热情称颂伟大领袖毛主席是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者，称颂毛主席天才地、全面地继承和捍卫了马克思列宁主义。
