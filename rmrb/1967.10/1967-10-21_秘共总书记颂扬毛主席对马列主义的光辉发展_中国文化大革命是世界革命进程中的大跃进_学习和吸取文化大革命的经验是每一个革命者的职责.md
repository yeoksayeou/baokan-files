Title: The General Secretary of the Peruvian Communist Party Praises Chairman Mao's Brilliant Development of Marxism-Leninism
Author:
Date: 1967-10-21
Edition: 5

The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Peruvian Communist Party, Parades, in a letter congratulating the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, praised Mao Zedong Thought as the contemporary Marxism-Leninism and lauded Chairman Mao's personally initiated and led Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution as a brilliant development of Marxism-Leninism.

Parades wrote in the congratulatory letter to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China: "The People's Republic of China was established through a protracted and arduous people's revolutionary war under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and Comrade Mao Zedong. Mao Zedong Thought is the contemporary Marxism-Leninism, shining brilliantly over the entire world."

The letter stated, "The 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China is being celebrated under the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution led by the Communist Party of China and Comrade Mao Zedong is a great leap forward in the world revolutionary process, marking a new stage. This Cultural Revolution has struck a blow at imperialism headed by the United States and its running dogs, as well as modern revisionism."

The letter pointed out: "The enemies of the Chinese people and the oppressed peoples and nations of the world, 'are being exposed as paper tigers and will be reduced to ashes in the hatred of the world's revolutionary people led by Marxist-Leninist parties and guided by Mao Zedong Thought.'"

The letter condemned the criminal acts of U.S. imperialism, Soviet revisionism, and the reactionaries who act as their tools in surrounding and threatening China, and also condemned U.S. imperialism's aggression against China's neighboring country, Vietnam. The letter stated: "The great People's Republic of China, which has now become a powerful base for world revolution, is invincible and indestructible. The crimes and threats of U.S. imperialism can only make the revolutionary fires of the peoples of various countries burn more fiercely. Therefore, People's China, the heroic Chinese people, and the heroic Vietnamese people are not isolated. We, the revolutionaries of the whole world, are prepared to fight to the death with imperialism and its running dogs, including revisionism."

"The duty of every revolutionary is to learn from and draw lessons from the experience of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. This Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, guided by the thought of Comrade Mao Zedong, is a brilliant development of Marxism-Leninism.

"In terms of mobilizing the broad masses of the people, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has provided an example of how a Communist Party should lead the people in revolution. The Marxism-Leninism developed by Comrade Mao Zedong is guiding the world revolution, deeply penetrating the hearts of the people, and becoming a force more powerful than nuclear weapons."

### 秘共总书记颂扬毛主席对马列主义的光辉发展  中国文化大革命是世界革命进程中的大跃进  学习和吸取文化大革命的经验是每一个革命者的职责

1967-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    秘共总书记颂扬毛主席对马列主义的光辉发展
    中国文化大革命是世界革命进程中的大跃进
    学习和吸取文化大革命的经验是每一个革命者的职责
    据新华社十八日讯　秘鲁共产党中央委员会总书记帕雷德斯在一封祝贺中华人民共和国成立十八周年的信中，称颂毛泽东思想是当代的马克思列宁主义，并赞扬毛主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命是对马克思列宁主义的光辉发展。
    帕雷德斯在这封写给中共中央的贺信中说：“中华人民共和国是在中国共产党和毛泽东同志领导下经过艰巨长期的革命人民战争建立起来的，毛泽东思想是当代的马克思列宁主义，金光闪闪地普照着全世界。”
    信中写道，中华人民共和国成立十八周年是在无产阶级文化大革命胜利的情况下庆祝的。“中国共产党和毛泽东同志领导的这场无产阶级文化大革命，在世界革命进程中是一个大跃进，是一个新阶段。”这场文化大革命打击了以美国为首的帝国主义及其走狗和现代修正主义。
    信中指出：中国人民以及世界被压迫人民和被压迫民族的敌人，“正在被暴露成是真正的纸老虎，这些敌人必将在由马克思列宁主义政党领导下和在毛泽东思想指导下的世界人民的仇恨中被世界革命的烈火化成灰烬。”
    信中谴责美帝国主义、苏联修正主义及充当其工具的反动派包围、威胁中国的罪恶行为，并且谴责美帝国主义对中国的邻邦越南发动的侵略战争。信中写道：“现在已经成为世界革命强大根据地的伟大的人民中国，是不可战胜的，是不可摧毁的。美帝国主义的罪行和威胁只能使各国人民革命的烈火越烧越旺。因此，人民中国，英雄的中国人民和英雄的越南人民不是孤立的。我们全世界的革命者准备与帝国主义及其走狗，包括修正主义，进行殊死的斗争。”
    “我们大家，世界的每一个革命者都应该到处用血和火保卫人民中国这个世界无产阶级革命的先锋队、这个世界革命的根据地、这个进行民族解放战争的各国人民的后方，在自己的每一个国家进行革命，因为民族解放斗争和民族解放战争是相互支援和相互保卫的，无产阶级革命是相互支持的。”
    信中指出：“学习和吸取中国无产阶级文化革命的经验是每一个革命者的职责，在毛泽东同志的思想指导下的这场无产阶级文化大革命，是对马克思列宁主义的光辉发展。
    “在深入广大人民群众方面，无产阶级文化大革命提供了一个共产党人应如何领导人民群众进行革命的典范。毛泽东同志发展了的马克思列宁主义思想，指导着世界革命，深入人心，成了比核武器更加强大的力量。”
