Title: Somali Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-10-25
Edition: 5

	According to the 1967 execution plan of the China-Somalia cultural cooperation agreement, a Somali scientific and technological delegation led by head of delegation Abdullashi Sara Ali arrived in Beijing on October 21 at the invitation of the Chinese Association for Science and Technology.

### 索马里科技代表团抵京

1967-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    索马里科技代表团抵京
    新华社讯　根据中国—索马里文化合作协定一九六七年执行计划，以团长阿卜杜拉希·萨拉·阿里为首的索马里科学技术代表团，应中国科学技术协会的邀请于十月二十一日到达北京。
