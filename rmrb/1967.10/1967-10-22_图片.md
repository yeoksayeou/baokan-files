Title: The First Day of School at Shanghai Guangling Road Primary School
Author: Tang Yuren
Date: 1967-10-22
Edition: 3

On the first day of school at Shanghai Guangling Road Primary School, the teacher and students proudly held up the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and first of all, wished the great leader Chairman Mao a long life!
