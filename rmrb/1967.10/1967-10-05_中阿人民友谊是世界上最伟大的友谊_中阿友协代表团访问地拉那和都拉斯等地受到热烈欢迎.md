Title: The Friendship Between Chinese and Albanian Peoples is the Greatest in the World
Author:
Date: 1967-10-05
Edition: 8

The friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples is the greatest in the world. The delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Minister of National Defense, visited Tirana and Durres, among other places, and was warmly welcomed.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 3rd: The delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Minister of National Defense, participated in the mass rally held in Durres, Albania, to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The delegation also visited and inspected the city, receiving an extremely warm welcome from local party and government officials and the broad masses of the people.

In the afternoon of the first day, when the delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association arrived in Durres, accompanied by Chairman Kelaizi of the Albania-China Friendship Association and other comrades, they were greeted by local party and government officials and people from all walks of life. Subsequently, the delegation, together with people from all walks of life and the crew of the Chinese ship "Jiujiang" docked at the port of Durres, participated in the grand rally celebrating the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

At the celebration, the Director of the Durres Port Bureau, Matagi, and Wang Shusheng, the head of the delegation, both spoke.

Matagi enthusiastically praised the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and warmly sang the praises of the revolutionary friendship and combat unity between the peoples of Albania and China.

In his speech, Wang Shusheng introduced the achievements made in the socialist revolution and socialist construction in the 18 years since the founding of the People's Republic of China, as well as the great victories of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao. He also highly praised the great achievements made by Albania in its revolutionary movement. He said that the friendship between the peoples of China and Albania was forged and tempered by the great leaders Mao Zedong and Enver Hoxha, and is based on Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. It is the greatest friendship in the world. It is deeper than the Adriatic Sea and higher than the Himalayas.

The delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association presented a banner embroidered with the words "Long Live the Revolutionary Friendship and Combat Unity Between the Peoples of China and Albania!" to the people of Durres.

Previously, the delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association also visited the ancient city of Elbasan in central Albania.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana: The delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Minister of National Defense, brought the deep affection of the Chinese people for the Albanian people and visited the Albanian capital, Tirana, on September 28th and 29th. They were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the people in Tirana.

The delegation visited the Tractor Parts Factory, the "Muhamed Shehu" Military Academy, and the "Tirana" Daily Necessities Factory in Tirana, and celebrated the National Day of our country with the members of the "New China" Agricultural Cooperative.

### 中阿人民友谊是世界上最伟大的友谊  中阿友协代表团访问地拉那和都拉斯等地受到热烈欢迎

1967-10-05
第8版()
专栏：

    中阿人民友谊是世界上最伟大的友谊
    中阿友协代表团访问地拉那和都拉斯等地受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社地拉那三日电  由中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领的中阿友协代表团，参加了阿尔巴尼亚都拉斯市举行的庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年的群众大会，并在这个城市进行了参观和访问，受到当地党政负责人和广大群众极其热烈的欢迎。
    一日下午，当中阿友协代表团在阿中友协主席凯莱齐等同志陪同下来到都拉斯市时，受到当地党政负责人及各界群众的夹道欢迎。接着，代表团同各界群众以及停泊在都拉斯港的中国轮船“九江”号船员一起，参加了欢庆中华人民共和国成立十八周年的盛大集会。
    在庆祝大会上，都拉斯港务局局长马塔吉和王树声团长先后讲了话。
    马塔吉热烈赞扬史无前例的中国文化大革命，并热情歌颂阿中两国人民之间的革命友谊和战斗团结。
    王树声团长在讲话中介绍了中华人民共和国成立十八年来在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得的成就和毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利。他并且高度赞扬阿尔巴尼亚在革命化运动中取得的巨大成就。他说，中阿两国人民的友谊是伟大领袖毛泽东同志和霍查同志亲手缔造和锤炼起来的，是建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的基础上的，是世界上最伟大的友谊。它比亚得里亚海还深，比喜马拉雅山还高。
    中阿友协代表团把一面绣有“中阿两国人民的革命友谊和战斗团结万岁！”字样的锦旗赠送给都拉斯人民。
    在这以前，中阿友协代表团还访问了阿尔巴尼亚中部古城爱尔巴桑。
    据新华社地拉那电　由中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领的中阿友协代表团，带着中国人民对阿尔巴尼亚人民的深情厚谊，九月二十八日和二十九日访问了阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那，受到地拉那广大群众十分热烈的欢迎。
    代表团访问了地拉那拖拉机配件工厂、“穆罕默德·谢胡”军事学院和“地拉那”日用品工厂并同“新中国”农业社社员一起欢度我国国庆。
