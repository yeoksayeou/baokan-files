Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Kang Sheng Hold Talks with Comrade Shehu-led Albanian Party and Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-10-02
Edition: 6

	New China News Agency, October 1st: Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Advisor to the Central Cultural Revolution Group Kang Sheng, held talks with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Mehmet Shehu, in Beijing yesterday.

	The talks were conducted in a very friendly and cordial atmosphere, fully reflecting the revolutionary unity and combat friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of the two countries, which is based on Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism.

### 周恩来康生同志同谢胡同志为首的阿党政代表团继续举行会谈

1967-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来康生同志同谢胡同志为首的阿党政代表团继续举行会谈
    新华社一日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来同志，中共中央政治局常委、人大常委会副委员长、中央文革小组顾问康生同志，同阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，昨天在北京继续举行会谈。
    会谈在极为亲切友好的气氛中进行，充分地体现了中阿两党、两国和两国人民之间的建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义基础上的革命团结和战斗友谊。
