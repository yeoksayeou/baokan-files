Title: Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1967-10-06
Edition: 1

We are now in a new and great era of world revolution. The revolutionary storms in Asia, Africa, and Latin America will certainly deliver a decisive and destructive blow to the entire old world. The great victory of the Vietnamese people in their war of resistance against American aggression and for national salvation is a powerful proof of this.

		Telegram to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour
