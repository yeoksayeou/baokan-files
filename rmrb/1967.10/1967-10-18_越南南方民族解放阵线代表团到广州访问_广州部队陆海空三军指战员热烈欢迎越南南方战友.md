Title: Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Visits Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1967-10-18
Edition: 5

Guangzhou's revolutionary masses and the People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, numbering over 2,000, gathered at the airport this noon to warmly welcome the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, led by combat hero Nguyen Van Danh, who came to Guangzhou for a visit from the frontline of the anti-American struggle.

Comrade Chen Yu, along with responsible officials of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou and the Guangdong Military Region, Jiang Xiao-yuan, Chen De, Li Hua-min, Li Fu-yao, Zhang Shi-jie, and combat heroes and relevant department heads from the army, navy, and air force, went to the airport to warmly welcome the South Vietnamese comrades. The Guangzhou Baiyun Airport today presented a lively scene. The welcoming crowd held portraits of Chairman Mao and portraits of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho, raised Chairman Mao's quotations and banners reading "The Vietnamese people will definitely win, U.S. imperialism will definitely be defeated," warmly welcoming the heroic messengers of the South Vietnamese people.

Tonight, the responsible officials of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Kong Shi-quan and Wen Yu-cheng, met and hosted a banquet for the entire delegation. Comrades Chen Yu, Jiang Xiao-yuan, Chen De, and others attended the banquet.

Kong Shi-quan spoke at the banquet. He said, "Chairman Mao teaches us: 'We Chinese people are firmly supporting the just struggle of the South Vietnamese people.' The military and civilians of Guangdong Province, like the military and civilians across the country, resolutely follow Chairman Mao's words and pledge to be the strong backing of the Vietnamese people."

Nguyen Van Danh, the delegation leader, also spoke at the banquet. He said, "In this city of Guangzhou, rich in revolutionary history, the beloved Chairman Mao cultivated many cadres for the Chinese revolution. Over forty years ago, our great leader of the entire Vietnamese nation, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and other outstanding revolutionary cadres of the Vietnamese people, lived and worked in this city of revolutionary significance for the Vietnamese revolution and the Chinese revolution." Nguyen Van Danh enthusiastically praised the brilliant achievements of the Chinese people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction under the leadership of the beloved Chairman Mao and the great support given to the Vietnamese people's war against U.S. aggression for national salvation.

The Vietnamese Consul General in Guangzhou, Du Van Minh, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation and attended tonight's banquet.

New China News Agency Shanghai, October 17—The delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, led by Nguyen Van Danh, concluded their visit to Shanghai and departed this morning by special plane to Guangzhou. Several thousand military and civilians in Shanghai went to the airport to warmly see them off.

Responsible officials of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, and the army, navy, and air force, including Liao Zheng-guo, Wang Shao-yong, Mei Jia-sheng, and Zheng Chang-hua, as well as responsible officials of various revolutionary mass organizations, went to the airport to warmly see off the South Vietnamese comrades. Those who went to the airport to see them off also included Vietnamese students and trainees in Shanghai.

### 越南南方民族解放阵线代表团到广州访问　广州部队陆海空三军指战员热烈欢迎越南南方战友

1967-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南南方民族解放阵线代表团到广州访问
　　广州部队陆海空三军指战员热烈欢迎越南南方战友
    据新华社广州十七日电　广州市革命群众和中国人民解放军驻广州部队陆海空三军指战员二千多人，今天中午聚集在机场热烈欢迎来自反美斗争最前线的亲密战友——由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团前来广州访问。
    陈郁同志，中国人民解放军驻广州部队和广东省军区负责人江燮元、陈德、李化民、李福尧、张实杰等，以及陆海空战斗英雄和有关部门负责人，前往机场热烈欢迎越南南方战友。广州白云机场今天呈现一派欢腾景象。欢迎群众手持毛主席画像以及胡志明主席和阮友寿主席画像，高举毛主席语录牌以及写有“越南人民必胜，美帝国主义必败”的横额，热烈欢迎英雄的越南南方人民的使者。
    今晚，人民解放军驻广州部队负责人孔石泉、温玉成会见并宴请代表团全体成员。陈郁、江燮元、陈德等同志出席了宴会。
    孔石泉在宴会上讲话。他说，毛主席教导我们：“我们中国人民是坚决支持越南南方人民的正义斗争的。”广东省军民和全国军民一样，坚决听毛主席的话，誓作越南人民的坚强后盾。
    黄文旦团长在宴会上讲话。他说，在这个革命史迹很多的广州市，敬爱的毛主席为中国革命培养了许多农民运动骨干。四十多年前，我们越南全民族的伟大领袖胡志明主席和越南人民其他优秀革命干部，曾在这个具有革命历史意义的广州市生活过，并为越南革命和中国革命工作过。黄文旦团长热烈赞扬中国人民在以敬爱的毛主席为首的中国共产党领导下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业中取得的辉煌成就和给越南人民抗美救国战争的巨大支持。
    越南驻广州总领事杜文敏也到机场欢迎，并参加了今晚的宴会。
    新华社上海十七日电　由黄文旦团长率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，结束了在上海的访问，今天上午乘专机前往广州。上海数千军民到机场热烈欢送。
    上海市革命委员会负责人、人民解放军驻上海陆海空三军负责人廖政国、王少庸、梅嘉生、郑长华等以及各革命群众组织负责人，前往机场热烈欢送越南南方战友。到机场欢送的，还有越南在上海的留学生和实习生。
