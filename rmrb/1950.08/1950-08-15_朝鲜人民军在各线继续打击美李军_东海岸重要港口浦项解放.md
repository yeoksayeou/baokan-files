Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Strike the US-Lee Forces on All Fronts; Important East Coast Port of Pohang Liberated
Author:
Date: 1950-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 14th – The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 13th that People's Army units on all fronts continue to engage in fierce battles with US military units and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army.

    On the east coast front, after liberating the Gigei district, a certain unit of the People's Army, after encircling and annihilating the enemy who continued to resist stubbornly in the vicinity of the area, completely liberated Pohang, an important port and strategic location on the east coast, on August 10.

    In the battle to liberate Pohang, the People's Army forces killed or wounded more than 500 enemy soldiers, captured more than 400 enemy soldiers (including many US soldiers), seized 40 vehicles, 2 mobile cannons, 4 anti-tank guns, military supply warehouses, and many other weapons and ammunition.

    The People's Army forces in the area are currently pursuing the fleeing enemy. There were no changes on other fronts. (Picture attached)

### 朝鲜人民军在各线继续打击美李军  东海岸重要港口浦项解放

1950-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线继续打击美李军
    东海岸重要港口浦项解放
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十三日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，继续与美军部队和李承晚残余伪军展开猛烈的战斗。
    在东海岸方面，解放杞溪区后继续前进的人民军某部，在围歼了在该地区附近继续顽抗的敌人后，于八月十日完全解放了东海岸的重要港口和战略要地浦项。
    人民军部队在解放浦项的战斗中，毙、伤敌人五百余名，俘敌四百余名（包括许多美军），缴获汽车四十辆，机动炮二门、战防炮四门、军需仓库及其他各种武器、弹药等甚多。
    该地区的人民军部队，现正在追击逃敌。其他战线无变化。（附图片）
