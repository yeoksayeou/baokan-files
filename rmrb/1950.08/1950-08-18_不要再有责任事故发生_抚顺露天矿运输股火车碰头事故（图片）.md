　　No More Preventable Accidents
　　-- Train Collision Accident at Fushun Open-Pit Mine Transportation Division
　　By Liu Kui
　　7. When the train was being pushed from Daguantun towards Section One West, it was running extremely fast due to the steep slope, which surprised everyone.
　　8. Between Daguantun and Section One West, there was a wooden post indicating "Stop and Proceed." The safety regulations stipulated that the train must stop at this point before proceeding. However, Nie Changman did not stop and continued straight, causing the train to accelerate even further.
　　9. Flagman Yang Zhaosheng noticed a train stopped on the track ahead due to a power outage. He urgently waved the flag, signaling Nie Changman to apply the brakes.

　　刘馗作
　　7、当车由大官屯往一段西推行，因坡度较大，列车跑的飞快，人们都很惊讶。
　　8、在大官屯至一段西之间，立有“停车再开”木桩。保安制度规定，列车到此必须停止后再开。可是，聂长满没有停车，直开下去，车的进行速度越发快起来了。
