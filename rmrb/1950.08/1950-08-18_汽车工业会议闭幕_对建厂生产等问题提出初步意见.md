Title: Automotive Industry Conference Concludes, Presents Preliminary Opinions on Issues such as Factory Construction and Production

Author:

Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Automotive Industry Conference convened by the Central People's Government's Ministry of Heavy Industry on August 2nd concluded on the 12th. The conference studied the current situation of the automotive industry and discussed preliminary opinions on issues such as the site selection for new factory construction, the construction timeline and steps, vehicle models, output, the allocation and ordering of equipment, unified leadership of existing parts factories, the manufacturing of tractors, and the recruitment and training of technical personnel. These opinions will be submitted to the Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee for further review and decision.

### 汽车工业会议闭幕  对建厂生产等问题提出初步意见

1950-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　汽车工业会议闭幕
    对建厂生产等问题提出初步意见
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府重工业部八月二日召开的汽车工业会议，已于十二日闭幕。会议研究了汽车工业的现况，对新厂建设的厂址，建厂时间和步骤，车型、产量、设备的调配与订购，现有配件厂的统一领导，拖拉机的制造，技术人员的吸收和训练等问题，均讨论出初步意见。这些意见将提供政务院财经委员会作进一步审核后决定。
