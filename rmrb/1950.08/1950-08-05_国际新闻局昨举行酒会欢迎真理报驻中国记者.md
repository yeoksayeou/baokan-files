Title: International News Administration Holds Reception to Welcome Pravda Correspondent in China
Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th: The Central People's Government News Administration's International News Bureau held a reception on the 4th to welcome Visokostrovsky, the correspondent from the Soviet Union's Pravda stationed in China, who recently arrived in Beijing. Those attending included Fan Changjiang and Sa Kongliao, deputy directors of the News Administration, heads of the International News Bureau and various news organizations in the capital, and over forty correspondents stationed in China from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, and other countries. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Magnien, a reporter from the French newspaper L'Humanité, who had just returned from covering the Korean War, also rushed to attend and reported on the heroic struggle of the Korean people and the brutal acts of the U.S. military that he witnessed in Korea.

### 国际新闻局昨举行酒会欢迎真理报驻中国记者

1950-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际新闻局昨举行酒会欢迎真理报驻中国记者
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府新闻总署国际新闻局，于四日举行酒会，欢迎最近来京的苏联真理报驻中国记者维索科司卓夫斯基。出席新闻总署副署长范长江、萨空了，国际新闻局和首都各新闻单位负责人，及苏联、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克等国驻中国记者等四十余人。酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。甫自朝鲜采访归来的法国人道报记者马尼安亦赶来参加，并在席上报告了他在朝鲜目击的朝鲜人民的英勇斗争和美军的残暴行为。
