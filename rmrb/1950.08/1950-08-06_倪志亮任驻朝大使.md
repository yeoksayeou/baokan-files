Title: Ni Zhiliang Appointed Ambassador to Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Central People's Government has appointed General Ni Zhiliang as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 倪志亮任驻朝大使

1950-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　倪志亮任驻朝大使
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府已任命倪志亮将军为我国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首任特命全权大使。
