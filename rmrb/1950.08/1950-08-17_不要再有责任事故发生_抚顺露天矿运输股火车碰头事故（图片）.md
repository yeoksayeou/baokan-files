　　4. On March 22nd of this year, Nie Changman started the engine without inspecting the locomotive when he took over the shift.
　　5. After passing through six turnaround lines, he noticed the braking was slow, but he didn't inspect or repair it, and continued to push forward rapidly.
　　6. When going downhill, he tested the train's air brakes, and applied slow, continuous braking three times in one second, rendering the brakes ineffective.

　　不要再有责任事故发生
　　——抚顺露天矿运输股火车碰头事故
　　刘馗作
　　4、今年三月二十二日，聂长满当接班时，没有检查车机，就开动了。
　　5、经过六处折返线，发觉制动慢，但没有检查修理，依然很急地向前推进。
