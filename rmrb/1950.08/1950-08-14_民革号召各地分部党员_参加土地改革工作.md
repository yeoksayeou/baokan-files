Author:
Date: 1950-08-14
Page: 1

　　The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Calls on Party Members in Various Branches to Participate in Land Reform Work
    【Xinhua News Agency, 13th】 The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang has issued instructions to its local branches, calling on party members to actively participate in land reform work.
    The instructions stipulate that the committee's organizations at all levels in areas implementing land reform this year should list mobilizing party members to participate in land reform work as one of their central tasks. The instructions also point out that party members applying to participate in land reform work should first consider whether they have the determination and physical strength to endure hardship; once they decide to participate, they must persevere to the end. In their work, they must unconditionally implement the laws and policies of land reform and obey unified leadership.

### 民革号召各地分部党员  参加土地改革工作

1950-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革号召各地分部党员
    参加土地改革工作
    【新华社十三日讯】中国国民党革命委员会顷向各地方分部发出指示，号召党员踊跃参加土地改革工作。
    指示中规定在今年实行土地改革地区的该会各级组织，应将发动党员参加土地改革工作列为中心工作之一。指示并指出：申请参加土地改革工作的党员，事先应考虑自己有无刻苦耐劳的决心和体力，一经决定参加，必须贯彻始终。在工作中必须无条件执行土地改革的法令、政策和服从统一的领导。
