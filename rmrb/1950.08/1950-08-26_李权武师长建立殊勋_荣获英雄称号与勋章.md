Title: Commander Li Quanwu Establishes Meritorious Service, Awarded Hero Title and Medal
Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 4

　　Commander Li Quanwu Establishes Meritorious Service
    Awarded Hero Title and Medal
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 25th] The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 21st, awarding the title of Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, along with the Order of the National Flag, First Class, and a Gold Star Medal, to Li Quanwu, Commander of the 4th Guards Division of the People's Army of Seoul. The order stated: Commander Li Quanwu, "in the just war for the unification, freedom, and independence of the fatherland and against the armed aggression of the American imperialists, led his troops with outstanding strategy and skillful command, establishing heroic feats in battle."

### 李权武师长建立殊勋  荣获英雄称号与勋章

1950-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　李权武师长建立殊勋
    荣获英雄称号与勋章
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会二十一日发布命令，以朝鲜人民共和国英雄称号与一级国旗勋章、金星奖章各一枚，授予人民军近卫汉城第四师师长李权武。命令指出：李权武师长“在为祖国统一、自由、独立和反对美帝国主义者武装侵略的正义战争中，以优秀的战略和熟练的指挥，领导自己的部队在战斗中建立了英雄的伟勋”。
