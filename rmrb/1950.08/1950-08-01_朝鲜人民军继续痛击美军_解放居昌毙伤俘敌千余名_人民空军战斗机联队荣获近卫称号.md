Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Heavily Strike the US Military; Liberation of Geochang Annihilates More Than a Thousand Enemies; People's Air Force Fighter Regiment Awarded Guards Title
Author:
Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 31st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in a war report on the evening of the 30th that People's Army units on all fronts continued to advance in fierce battles with the US military. People's Army units attacking the Geochang area defeated the enemy's stubborn resistance and completely liberated Geochang County in northwestern Gyeongsangnam-do on the 29th. In the battle to liberate Geochang, People's Army units killed and wounded more than 1,000 enemy soldiers, captured more than 100 alive (most of whom were US military officers and soldiers), and seized 45 vehicles, 8 machine guns, and many other rifles, carbines, and other spoils of war.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 31st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in a war report on the morning of the 30th that People's Army units on all fronts continued to engage in fierce battles with US military units. The People's Army in the Hamyang County area of Gyeongsangnam-do, after annihilating the enemy forces near Anui, liberated the Anui District of Hamyang County, Gyeongsangnam-do on the 28th, killing, wounding, and capturing many enemy soldiers and seizing various spoils of war.

On the 29th, seven US bombers attacked Pyongyang. A squadron of the People's Army Air Force engaged the enemy aircraft in air combat, forcing the enemy aircraft to flee without achieving their objectives.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 31st: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 28th, awarding the title of Guards Unit to the People's Army Air Force Fighter Regiment because, in the just war for the unification, freedom, and independence of the fatherland and against the armed aggression of US imperialism, it provided air cover for the People's Army ground forces to advance smoothly and cross the river, bombed enemy forward and rear reserves and transportation lines, demonstrated bravery in air combat, inflicted heavy blows on the enemy, and established special meritorious service in ensuring the comprehensive victory of the front-line troops.

### 朝鲜人民军继续痛击美军  解放居昌毙伤俘敌千余名  人民空军战斗机联队荣获近卫称号

1950-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军继续痛击美军
    解放居昌毙伤俘敌千余名
    人民空军战斗机联队荣获近卫称号
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三十日晚发表战报称：各战线的人民军部队在与美军展开激烈的战斗中继续挺进。进攻居昌方面的人民军部队挫败了敌人的顽抗，于二十九日完全解放了庆尚南道西北的居昌县。人民军部队在解放居昌的战斗中，毙、伤敌军一千余名，活捉一百余名（其中大部分是美军官兵），缴获汽车四十五辆，机枪八挺及其他步枪卡宾枪等战利品甚多。
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三十日晨发表战报称：各战线的人民军部队继续和美军部队展开激烈的战斗。庆尚南道咸阳县方面的人民军在歼灭安义附近的敌军后，于二十八日解放了庆尚南道咸阳县的安义区，毙、伤、俘敌军甚众，卤获各种战利品。
    二十九日，美军轰炸机七架袭击平壤，人民军空军一个区队与敌机展开空战，迫使敌机未能实现其目的而逃窜。
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会二十八日发布命令，以近卫部队的称号，授予人民军空军战斗机联队，因为它在为祖国统一自由独立和反对美帝国主义武装进攻的正义战争中，从空中掩护人民军地面部队顺利进军和渡江作战，轰炸敌前线与后方的预备队及运输线，在空战中发挥勇敢性，予敌人以沉重打击，并在保障前线军队全面胜利中，建立了特殊功勋。
