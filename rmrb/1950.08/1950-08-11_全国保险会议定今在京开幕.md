Title: National Insurance Conference Scheduled to Open Today in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 2

　　[News from our newspaper] The General Company of the People's Insurance Company of China is scheduled to convene the Second National Insurance Conference in Beijing on August 11. The conference will exchange and summarize work experience from various regions and determine the future direction and tasks of insurance work. The conference is scheduled to last 21 days. Participants will include heads of regional companies and branch offices, as well as relevant business and accounting personnel. During the conference, a National Insurance Accounting Conference will also be held to discuss improvements to the insurance accounting system.
                (Insurance Company Communications Group)

### 全国保险会议定今在京开幕

1950-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国保险会议定今在京开幕
    【本报讯】中国人民保险公司总公司，定于八月十一日在京召开第二次全国保险会议。会上将交流和总结各地工作经验，并确定保险工作今后的方针和任务。会期预定二十一天。与会者包括各地区公司、分公司负责人及有关业务和会计人员。开会期间将同时举行全国保险会计会议，讨论保险会计制度的改善问题。
                （保险公司通讯小组）
