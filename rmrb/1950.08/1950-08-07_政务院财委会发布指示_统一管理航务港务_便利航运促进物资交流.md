Title: State Council Financial and Economic Committee Issues Instructions to Unify the Management of Navigation and Port Affairs, Facilitate Shipping, and Promote Material Exchange
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 5th — In order to strengthen the management of navigation and port affairs, facilitate shipping, and promote the exchange of goods, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government issued instructions on July 26 regarding the unified management of navigation and port affairs. The instructions stipulate the establishment of a Navigation Affairs General Bureau under the Ministry of Transport of the Central People's Government. Regional port bureaus will be established in important ports across the country, such as Tianjin, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Qingdao, and Dalian, as unified management agencies for navigation and port affairs. Port sub-bureaus or offices may be established in other ports as needed, under the leadership of the aforementioned important regional port bureaus. At the same time, the Ministry of Transport of the Central People's Government will begin collecting various materials related to the management of navigation and port affairs, drafting management rules, regulations, and systems, to be submitted to the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council for verification and approval, and then to the State Council for approval and promulgation.

### 政务院财委会发布指示  统一管理航务港务  便利航运促进物资交流

1950-08-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　政务院财委会发布指示
    统一管理航务港务
    便利航运促进物资交流
    【新华社五日讯】为加强航务及港务工作的管理，以便利航运，促进货物的交流，中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会特于七月二十六日发布关于统一航务港务管理的指示。指示规定在中央人民政府交通部之下设立航务总局，在国内各重要港埠，如天津、广州、上海、青岛、大连等地设立区港务局，作为统一航务和港务管理的机构；在其他港口得视需要设港务分局或办事处，受上述重要港埠区港务局之领导。同时，由中央人民政府交通部着手搜集有关航务及港务管理的各种资料，拟具管理的章则、法规和制度，经政务院财政经济委员会核准并报政务院批准公布施行。
