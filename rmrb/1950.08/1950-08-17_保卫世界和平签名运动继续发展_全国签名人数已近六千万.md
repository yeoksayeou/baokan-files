Title: World Peace Signature Campaign Continues to Develop; National Signature Count Nears Sixty Million

Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The signature campaign to defend world peace is continuing to develop across the country. According to recent incomplete statistics, 59,286,179 people nationwide have signed the peace declaration. The peace signature campaign in the northeastern provinces has penetrated the countryside, with 19,079,000 signatures collected in the region, accounting for 45% of the region's total population. In the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, 365,800 people have signed, accounting for 16% of the region's population. The number of signatures in East China has reached 19,747,000, accounting for 15% of the region's population. The number of signatures in North China is 8,400,717, accounting for 13.5% of the region's population, of which 1,433,717 people in Beijing have signed. Farmers' signatures in Shanxi Province account for 70% of the province's signatures. As of July 21, 6,922,216 people in the Central and Southern region have signed, accounting for 5% of the region's population, of which the number of signatures in Wuhan has accounted for more than 80% of the city's population. As of July 19, 1,283,952 people in the Northwest region had signed. As of July 21, 2,838,849 people in the Southwest region had signed. In addition, there are 642,345 signatures from readers who wrote to the People's Daily in Beijing.

### 保卫世界和平签名运动继续发展  全国签名人数已近六千万

1950-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫世界和平签名运动继续发展
    全国签名人数已近六千万
    【新华社十六日讯】保卫世界和平签名运动正在全国各地继续发展。截至最近的不完全统计，全国已有五千九百二十八万六千一百七十九人在和平宣言上签名。东北各省的和平签名运动已深入农村，该区签名人数已达一千九百零七万九千人，占全区人口总数的百分之四十五。内蒙自治区已有三十六万五千八百人签名，占全区人口百分之十六。华东区的签名人数已达一千九百七十四万七千人，占全区人口百分之十五。华北各地的签名人数为八百四十万零七千零一十七人，占全区人口百分之十三点五，其中北京市已有一百四十三万三千七百一十七人签名。山西省农民签名人数已占全省签名人数的百分之七十。中南区截至七月二十一日止，已有六百九十二万二千二百一十六人签名，占全区人口百分之五，其中武汉市的签名人数已占全市人口百分之八十以上。西北区截至七月十九日止，已有一百二十八万三千九百五十二人签名。西南区截至七月二十一日止，已有二百八十三万八千八百四十九人签名。此外，尚有各地读者向北京人民日报写信签名者六十四万二千三百四十五人。
