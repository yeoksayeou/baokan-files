Title: Beijing People's Bank Opens Airmail Remittance Service
Author:
Date: 1950-08-14
Page: 6

The People's Bank of Beijing has opened an airmail remittance service. All procedures are the same as for postal remittances, only with the addition of the word "airmail" on the remittance application form. Airmail remittances will be charged an additional 3,800 yuan for airmail postage, according to postal rates for airmail letters. All departments under the bank can handle this service. Currently, remittance destinations include Taiyuan, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Jiuquan, Dihua, Hankou, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Chengdu, Guiyang, Kunming and eleven other locations and their neighboring areas. (Beijing News Agency)

### 京人民银行开办航空汇款

1950-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　京人民银行开办航空汇款
    人民银行北京分行已开办航空汇款，一切手续与信汇同，仅在汇款申请书上加注“航空”字样即可。航空汇款另照邮局航空平信收三千八百元，该行所属各部处均可办理；目前通汇地点为太原、西安、兰州、酒泉、迪化、汉口、广州、重庆、成都、贵阳、昆明等十一地及其邻近地点。（京市新闻处）
