Title: Introduction to the Contents of the Thirty-first Issue of "New Era" Weekly
Author:
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 6

The English edition, Issue 31, of the "New Era" weekly published in Moscow has arrived in Beijing. The main contents of this issue include "Policy of Peace and Policy of War" (Editorial), "American Interventionists in Korea" (Korotkov), "The Situation in the Philippines" (Rabitsky), "An Open Letter to the Readers of the Swiss 'Vaterland Newspaper'" (Ivanova), "Symphony of Creation" (Chumitelko), "Notes of a Turkish Teacher" (Makar), etc. In the column "Reports from the Peace Front," there is a special article by Vishinsky: "The Border of the Peace Camp Extends into Spain," and there are also more than a dozen photos of "Latvia, Estonia, and Lithuania in the Past Ten Years."

### 《新时代》周刊三十一期内容介绍

1950-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　《新时代》周刊三十一期内容介绍
    莫斯科出版的《新时代》周刊英文版第三十一期已到京。这一期主要内容有《和平政策与战争政策》（社论），《美国干涉者在朝鲜》（考罗特科夫），《菲律宾现状》（拉比茨基），《给瑞士〈瓦特朗报〉读者的一封公开信》（伊凡诺娃），《创造的交响乐》（秋米特尔科），《一个土耳其教师的笔记》（玛喀尔）等。在《来自和平战线的报道》栏内有维逊的专文：《和平阵营的边界伸入了西班牙》，另外还有《十年来的拉特维亚、爱沙尼亚和立陶宛》照片十余帧。
