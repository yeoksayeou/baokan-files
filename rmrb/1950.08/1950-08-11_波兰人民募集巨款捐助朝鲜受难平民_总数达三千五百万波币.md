Title: Polish People Raise Huge Sums to Donate to Korean Suffering Civilians, Totaling 35 Million Polish Zloty

Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 4

Supporting the Just Struggle of the Korean People!

　　Polish People Raise Huge Sums to Donate to Korean Suffering Civilians
    Totaling 35 Million Polish Zloty
    [Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 9th] According to TASS, Warsaw, 9th: The Polish people are widely raising funds to relieve Korean civilians who have suffered from the barbaric bombing by American aircraft. Enterprises and institutions in the province of Piotrków Trybunalski held more than 2,000 rallies protesting the American aggression against Korea, with 180,000 participants. They have already raised 4 million Polish zlotys. Agricultural laborers in the province of Katowice have raised approximately 1 million zlotys. Together with donations raised by trade union organizations across the country, the total has reached 35 million zlotys (approximately 2.8 billion RMB).

### 波兰人民募集巨款捐助朝鲜受难平民  总数达三千五百万波币

1950-08-11
第4版()
专栏：声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争！

　　波兰人民募集巨款捐助朝鲜受难平民
    总数达三千五百万波币
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社华沙九日讯：波兰人民广泛募集款项，救济因美国飞机野蛮轰炸而受害的朝鲜平民。彼得哥煦省的企业与机关，举行了二千余次抗议美国侵略朝鲜的集会，参加者十八万人。他们已募集了波币四百万兹罗提。卡托维兹省的农业劳动者募集了约一百万兹罗提。连同各地工会组织所募集的捐款，总数已达三千五百万兹罗提（约合我国人民币二十八亿元）。
