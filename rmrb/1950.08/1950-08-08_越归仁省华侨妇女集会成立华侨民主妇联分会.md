Title: Overseas Chinese Women's Gathering in Quy Nhon Province, Vietnam, Establishes Overseas Chinese Democratic Women's Federation Branch
Author:
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vietnam, 5th: Over one hundred overseas Chinese women gathered in the provincial capital of Quy Nhon Province in central-southern Vietnam on June 12th to establish a local branch of the Overseas Chinese Democratic Women's Federation. An executive committee of the Women's Federation was also elected at the meeting.

### 越归仁省华侨妇女集会成立华侨民主妇联分会

1950-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　越归仁省华侨妇女集会成立华侨民主妇联分会
    【新华社讯】越南通讯社中越五日电：一百多名华侨妇女于六月十二日在中越南部归仁省省会集会，成立了华侨民主妇女联合会地方分会。会上并选出妇联执行委员会。
