Title: Vietnamese Democratic Youth League, Dutch Women's Federation Voice Support for Korean People's Liberation War, Send Respectful Greetings to Korean Youth and Women Respectively
Author:
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th (via Tass): Vietnam News Agency reports that the Vietnamese People's Democratic Youth Federation sent a telegram to the North Korean Democratic Youth League stating: "Vietnamese youth warmly congratulate the Korean youth and all the people on their heroic struggle against the Syngman Rhee puppet government and the American aggressors.

We firmly believe that your people, fighting for their own liberation, will surely achieve victory under the leadership of General Kim Il-sung.

Your struggle for national liberation, for the reunification of the country, and for the defense of peace is the same as our people's and our youth's struggle against the French colonial masters and the American aggressors. This is a struggle for a just cause, a struggle in which all the people participate and which receives the active support of democratic forces around the world.

Victory will belong to us."

Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 6th (via Tass): Newspapers report: The Dutch Women's Federation protests against the American armed intervention in Korea. In a telegram to Korean women, the Dutch Women's Federation pays tribute to the heroic Korean people who are fighting for freedom.

The Dutch Women's Federation also urges American women to intensify the fight for peace and to demand the return of American soldiers from Korea and the punishment of the executioners who have caused so much suffering to the Korean people.

### 越南民主青联、荷兰妇联  声援朝鲜人民解放战争  分别电朝鲜青年和妇女致敬

1950-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南民主青联、荷兰妇联
    声援朝鲜人民解放战争
    分别电朝鲜青年和妇女致敬
    【新华社讯】塔斯社上海五日电：越南通讯社讯，越南人民民主青年联合会致电北朝鲜民主青年联合会称：“越南青年热烈祝贺朝鲜青年和全体人民对李承晚傀儡政府和美国侵略者所进行的英勇斗争。
    我们坚信，为自身解放而奋斗的贵国人民在金日成将军的领导之下一定得到胜利。
    你们为民族解放、为国家的重新统一并为保卫和平而进行的斗争跟我国人民及我国青年对法国殖民地主人及美国侵略者而进行的斗争是同样的，这是一个为正义事业而进行的斗争，这个斗争全体人民都参加而且获得全世界民主势力的积极支持。
    胜利必将属于我们。”
    【新华社讯】塔斯社海牙六日电：各报报道：荷兰妇女联合会抗议美国武装干涉朝鲜。荷兰妇联在致朝鲜妇女的电文中，向那些为自由而战的英勇的朝鲜人民致敬。
    荷兰妇联并且敦促美国妇女们，加紧争取和平，起来要求将美军士兵从朝鲜调回国内，惩罚那些使朝鲜人民受了这么多苦难的刽子手们。
