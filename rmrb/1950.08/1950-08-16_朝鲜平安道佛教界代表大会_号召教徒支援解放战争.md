Title: Korean Pyongan Province Buddhist Community Congress Calls on Believers to Support the War of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

　　Korean Pyongan Province Buddhist Community Congress
    Calls on Believers to Support the War of Liberation
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch from Pyongyang, 12th] TASS reports from Pyongyang on the 12th: Representatives of Buddhists and Buddhist organizations in Pyongan Province convened a congress here a few days ago. They resolutely expressed their determination to fight alongside all Korean people to defeat the American aggressors and for the unification and independence of the motherland.
    Pyong Man-ha (transliteration), representative of the Buddhist Association of Pyongan Province, stated in his report: "We condemn the barbaric acts of the American aggressors. They indiscriminately bomb our peaceful cities and villages, slaughtering civilians. All Buddhists should rise up to support the righteous war of liberation, and unite to fight for the independence and unification of the motherland, so that we can quickly clear all American aggressors from our land."
    The participants of the congress unanimously passed a resolution calling on all Buddhists to fight against American aggression in Korea, and to use all their strength to support the front lines and consolidate the rear.

### 朝鲜平安道佛教界代表大会  号召教徒支援解放战争

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜平安道佛教界代表大会
    号召教徒支援解放战争
    【新华社讯】塔斯社平壤十二日电：平安道的佛教徒及佛教团体代表数日前在此间召开大会。他们坚决表示与所有朝鲜人民一道，为击退美国侵略者，及祖国的统一与独立而斗争。
    佛教会平安道分会代表平万河（译音）在报告中称：“我们斥责美国侵略者的野蛮行为。他们滥肆轰炸我们的和平城市与乡村，屠杀平民。所有佛教徒应该起来支持正义的解放战争，为了祖国的独立与统一而一致奋起，这样才可以迅速地在我国的土地上清除所有的美国侵略者。”
    参加大会的人们一致通过一项决议，要求所有佛教徒为反抗美国侵略朝鲜而斗争，用一切力量支援前线和巩固后方。
