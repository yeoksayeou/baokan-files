Title: World Student Congress Opens in Prague on the 14th; Grohman Addresses Congress, Saluting Democratic Students Worldwide
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 15th: The Second World Student Congress opened in Prague, Czechoslovakia, on the 14th. Representatives of student and youth organizations from 65 countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Korea, various European People's Democracies, India, South Africa, and Latin American countries, are attending.

    Grohman, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the International Union of Students, delivered the opening address, saluting the representatives of democratic students worldwide and guests representing various international democratic organizations. Hrzal, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Youth League, extended greetings to the congress on behalf of Czech youth.

    The audience heard a speech by Bæuereson, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, who urged the youth of the world to unite more closely and fight for lasting peace and friendship between all nations. Paulérial (French), a member of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of the Defenders of Peace, Supka (Czech), Vice-Chairman of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Banerji (Indian), a representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and Fierge, Deputy Prime Minister of Czechoslovakia, all delivered speeches at the meeting.

### 世界学生代表大会十四日在捷京开幕  格罗曼致词向全世界民主学生致敬

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界学生代表大会十四日在捷京开幕
    格罗曼致词向全世界民主学生致敬
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十四日讯：世界学生第二届代表大会已于十四日在捷京布拉格开幕。出席的有苏联、中国、朝鲜、欧洲各人民民主国家、印度、南非和拉丁美洲各国等六十五个国家的学生与青年团体的代表。
    国际学生联合会执委会主席格罗曼致开幕词，向全世界民主学生的代表和代表各国际民主团体的来宾致敬。捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟主席赫兹拉尔代表捷克青年向大会致贺词。
    会众听取了世界民主青年联盟主席鲍埃逊的演说，他敦促全世界的青年更紧密地团结起来，为持久的和平与各国的友谊而斗争。世界拥护和平大会常设委员会委员鲍立尔（法籍），世界工联副主席苏普卡（捷籍），国际民主妇联代表班勒依（印度籍）和捷克斯洛伐克副总理费林格，均在会上发表了演说。
