Title: Korean People's Army on the East Coast Annihilates Over 1,000 Enemies; Liberation of Key Transportation Hub Yiheung Northeast of Daegu; Six American Planes Shot Down During Raids on Ranam and Chongjin Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 29th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of the 28th that People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with stubborn enemy forces on all fronts. American troops and the remnant puppet army of Syngman Rhee launched frenzied counterattacks, but the People's Army dealt them heavy blows and launched vigorous offensives.

	The People's Army units attacking in the Yiheung area liberated Yiheung (northeast of Daegu), a heavily fortified stronghold and key transportation hub of the enemy. In the battle to liberate Yiheung, the People's Army units killed, wounded, and captured a large number of enemy troops, and seized a large amount of war materiel.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 29th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a communique on the evening of the 28th that People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with American troops and the remnant puppet army of Syngman Rhee on all fronts. On the east coast, People's Army units attacking repelled the enemy's frantic resistance under the cover of aircraft and warships, broke through the enemy's defensive positions, and continued their offensive. The People's Army inflicted heavy casualties on the enemy in the fighting on the east coast, killing and wounding over 800 enemy officers and soldiers, and capturing over 300 alive. They seized eight cannons of various calibers, four flamethrowers, as well as light and heavy machine guns, automatic rifles, carbines, radios, various ammunition, and other war materiel in large quantities.

	People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down three American B-29 bombers and three fighter planes that raided the Ranam and Chongjin (North Hamgyong Province) areas from August 17th to August 22nd.

### 东海岸朝鲜人民军歼敌千余  大邱东北解放交通要地义兴  美机侵袭罗南清津地区被击落六架

1950-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　东海岸朝鲜人民军歼敌千余
    大邱东北解放交通要地义兴
    美机侵袭罗南清津地区被击落六架
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十八日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与顽抗的敌军继续激战。美军部队和李承晚残余伪军疯狂反攻，人民军予以沉重打击，并展开猛烈的进攻。
    在义兴方面进攻的人民军部队，解放了敌军坚固设防据点与交通要地义兴（大邱东北）。在解放义兴的战斗中，人民军部队毙、伤、俘敌军甚多，缴获大批战利品。
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十八日晚发表公报称：人民军部队在各战线上，与美军和李承晚残余伪军继续进行猛烈的战斗。在东海岸方面进攻的人民军部队，击退在飞机和舰船掩护下疯狂反抗的敌军，突破了敌人的防御阵地，继续进攻。人民军在东海岸方面的战斗中，给予敌人以沉重的打击。毙伤敌官兵八百余名，活捉三百余名。缴获各种口径炮八门、火焰喷射器四具，及轻重机枪、自动步枪、卡宾枪、电台、各种弹药等战利品甚多。
    人民军高射炮部队，自八月十七日起至八月二十二日止，击落侵袭罗南、清津（咸镜北道）地区的美Ｂ——二九型轰炸机三架及战斗机三架。
