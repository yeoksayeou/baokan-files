Title: Australian Sydney Dockworkers Refuse to Transport U.S. Aggression Against Korea's Military Supplies
Author:
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sydney Dispatch, August 26th: TASS Sydney News: On August 23rd, a group of dockworkers in Sydney, Australia, refused to load weapons and ammunition destined for the American aggressors in Korea and left the ship carrying the weapons.
　　The Daily Telegraph stated: Due to the workers' refusal to load the ship, there was insufficient manpower, delaying the sailing schedule of the weapon-carrying vessel.

### 澳大利亚悉尼码头工人  拒运美国侵略朝鲜军火

1950-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　澳大利亚悉尼码头工人
    拒运美国侵略朝鲜军火
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社悉尼讯：八月二十三日，澳大利亚悉尼一批码头工人，拒绝装载运给在朝鲜的美国侵略者的武器和弹药，并离开装运武器的轮船。
    每日电讯称：由于工人们拒绝装船，人工不足，延迟了运武器船只的航期。
