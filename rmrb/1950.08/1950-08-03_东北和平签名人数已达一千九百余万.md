Title: Northeast Peace Signature Campaign Reaches 19.07 Million

Author:

Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 2nd. Over 19,079,000 people in Northeast China have signed the Peace Declaration. The peace signature campaigns in some provinces and cities have been successfully concluded or are about to conclude, while the remaining provinces and cities are continuing to deepen this campaign in conjunction with the anti-American aggression movement. The signature campaign in Heilongjiang Province concluded in late July. A total of 2,600,116 people in the province signed, accounting for more than 46 percent of the total population. The signature campaigns in Songjiang and Rehe provinces are nearing completion. According to incomplete statistics, more than 2.65 million people have signed in Songjiang Province, including more than 550,000 in Harbin City alone, accounting for more than 71 percent of the city's population. In Rehe Province, the number of signatories has reached 1.51 million, and the campaign is currently being continued in rural areas in conjunction with the anti-American aggression movement, with completion scheduled for early August.

### 东北和平签名人数已达一千九百余万

1950-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北和平签名人数已达一千九百余万
    【新华社沈阳二日电】东北人民已有一千九百零七万九千余人在和平宣言上签名。个别省、市的和平签名运动已经胜利结束或即将结束，其余省、市正把这一运动和反对美国侵略运动相结合继续深入展开。黑龙江省的签名运动已于七月下旬结束。该省签名人数共计二百六十万零一百一十六人，占总人口百分之四十六以上。松江、热河两省的签名运动即将结束。据不完整统计，松江省已有二百六十五万余人签名，其中哈尔滨市即有五十五万余人，占全市人口百分之七十一强。热河省签名人数已达一百五十一万人，现正结合反对美国侵略运动在农村中继续进行，预定在八月上旬结束。
