Title: International Bookstore Sells Persian (Farsi) and Korean Books

Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 6

The International Bookstore sells Persian (Farsi) and Korean Books
    In addition to distributing Soviet editions in Chinese, Russian, English, French, German, Japanese, and Polish, the International Bookstore recently received a batch of Persian (Farsi) books from Moscow, including Lenin and Stalin's works "State and Revolution," "Imperialism, the Highest Stage of Capitalism," "Problems of Leninism," "Marxism and the National Question," "Foundations of Leninism," "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)," "Dialectical and Historical Materialism," and "A Short Biography of Stalin," as well as "Selected Poems of Pushkin," "Selected Poems of Lahuti," "The Soviet National Anthem," and "The Unvanquished" by Gorbatov. This is the first time that Persian (Farsi) books have arrived in Beijing.

    In addition, the store recently received a Korean edition of Lenin's work "Imperialism, the Highest Stage of Capitalism." Readers can purchase them at the Beijing branch of the store on Wangfujing Street.

### 国际书店发售回文朝鲜文书籍

1950-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　国际书店发售回文朝鲜文书籍
    国际书店除发行苏联版中、俄、英、法、德、日、波兰等文出版物外，最近又从莫斯科运到波斯文（回文）书籍一批，包括列宁与斯大林的著作《国家与革命》，《帝国主义是资本主义发展的最高阶段》，《列宁主义问题》，《马克思主义与民族问题》，《列宁主义基础》，《联共（布）党史》，“辩证唯物主义与历史唯物主义》及《斯大林传略》，《普希金诗选》，《拉胡吉诗选》，《苏联国歌》，郭尔巴托夫著的《宁死不屈》等。按波斯文（回文）书籍到京这还是第一次。
    又该店近又运到朝鲜文列宁著作一种《帝国主义是资本主义发展的最高阶段》。读者可到王府井大街该店北京分店购买。
