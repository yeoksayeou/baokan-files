Title: Beijing 7,000 Students Rally to Celebrate the World Student Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: Students in Beijing held a rally today at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace to celebrate the convocation of the Second World Student Congress. More than 7,000 students from major universities and middle schools in Beijing attended. Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Xu Liqun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department, Qian Sanqiang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and Li Xiuzhen, Vice Chairman of the All-China Student Federation, attended and delivered speeches.

　　Gu Qizong, Chairman of the Beijing Student Federation, first delivered the opening address, representing the students of Beijing in congratulating the success of the Second World Student Congress.

　　Liao Chengzhi, in his speech, called on students across the country, under the leadership of the World Student Union, to unite closely with students around the world and wage a resolute struggle against American imperialism and its reactionary running dogs in various countries.

　　The conference concluded by passing a congratulatory telegram to the Second World Student Congress.

### 北京七千学生集会庆祝世界学代大会

1950-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京七千学生集会庆祝世界学代大会
    【新华社十八日讯】北京市学生为庆祝世界学生第二届代表大会的召开于今日在北京市劳动人民文化宫举行集会。到会有北京市各大、中学学生七千余人。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记廖承志，宣传部副部长许立群，中国民主青年联合会副主席钱三强、中华全国学生联合会副主席李秀贞均出席致词。
    北京市学联主席古奇踪首先致开会辞，代表北京学生祝贺世界学生二届代表大会的成功。
    廖承志在讲话中号召全国学生在世界学联领导下，和世界学生亲密团结起来，向美帝国主义及其各国走狗反动派作坚决的斗争。
    大会最后通过致世界学生第二届代表大会贺电。
