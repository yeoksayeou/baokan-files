Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Advance, Liberating Key Location of Tabu-dong, Inflicting Heavy Damage on the Main Force of the U.S. 25th Division
Author:
Date: 1950-08-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 19th dispatch: TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the morning of August 18th: The People's Army continues to fiercely fight against the U.S. military and the Syngman Rhee troops on all fronts. The People's Army has shattered the U.S. military's attempts to resist with the support of aircraft and is now continuing its offensive.

　　The People's Army units advancing towards Tabu-dong (north of Daegu) liberated Tabu-dong, a major enemy defense stronghold and an important transportation hub.

　　In the battles in this area, the People's Army units inflicted heavy losses on the enemy.

　　The People's Army units advancing along the southern coast continue to vigorously attack the U.S. 25th Division and Marine Corps units.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 19th dispatch: TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the evening of August 18th: The People's Army units repelled the fierce counterattacks of the U.S. and Syngman Rhee troops and continued to advance on all fronts.

　　The People's Army advancing along the southern coast heavily damaged the main force of the U.S. 25th Division, supported by tank units and Marine Corps.

　　The People's Army units advancing on the east coast repelled several enemy counterattacks, and the offensive is continuing.

　　Fierce battles are also taking place on other fronts.

### 朝鲜人民军继续推进  解放要地多富洞  重创美军第二十五师主力

1950-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军继续推进
    解放要地多富洞
    重创美军第二十五师主力
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八月十八日晨宣布：人民军在各线继续与美军及李承晚军队激战。人民军粉碎了美军在飞机支持下进行抵抗的企图，现正继续进攻中。
    向多富洞（按在大邱之北）推进的人民军部队，解放了敌方主要防御据点和重要交通中心多富洞。
    在这方面的战斗中，人民军部队使敌军遭受重大损失。
    沿南部海岸推进的人民军部队，继续猛力攻击美国第二十五师及海军陆战队部队。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八月十八日晚宣布：人民军部队击退了美国和李承晚军队的猛烈反攻，继续在各线推进。
    在南部海岸推进的人民军重创以坦克部队和海军陆战队支持的美国第二十五师主力。
    在东海岸推进的人民军部队击退了敌人若干次反攻，攻势正继续中。
    其他战线上同样在进行着激战。
