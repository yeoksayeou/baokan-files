Title: Forty-Fifth Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passes Decision on Rewarding Inventions and Guaranteeing Invention Rights and Provisional Regulations
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its forty-fifth plenary meeting at 3:00 PM today. The meeting discussed and approved the "Decision on Rewarding Inventions, Technical Improvements and Rationalization Proposals Related to Production" and the "Provisional Regulations on Guaranteeing Invention Rights and Patent Rights", and also approved several appointments and removals.

### 政务院四十五次会议  通过关于奖励发明与保障发明权的决定和暂行条例

1950-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院四十五次会议
    通过关于奖励发明与保障发明权的决定和暂行条例
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院于今日下午三时举行第四十五次政务会议，讨论通过了“关于奖励有关生产的发明、技术改进及合理化建议的决定”和“保障发明权与专利权暂行条例”，并通过任免事项多起。
