Title: Tianjin Rally Celebrates August 1st; 40,000 Demonstrate and March

Author:

Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 1st: Over 40,000 people from all walks of life in Tianjin solemnly held a rally to celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day and to oppose the American aggression against Taiwan and Korea. Mayor Huang Jing spoke at the meeting. He said: The people of Tianjin are determined to unite with the people of the whole country and the whole world to crush any aggression and provocation by American imperialism, resolutely liberate Taiwan and Tibet, and support the liberation struggle of the Korean people. Yang Chengwu, commander of a certain corps stationed in Tianjin, said: Despite the clamor and preparation for expanding the war by American imperialism, victory will surely belong to the people of the world. We Chinese people must plant the five-star red flag on Taiwan! Representatives of Korean residents, Kim Bong-tae, Li Zhucheng, director of the preparatory committee of the Tianjin branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Meng Qiujiang, director of the temporary working committee of the Tianjin branch of the China Democratic League, combat hero Huang Shutian, and Wang Laowu, vice chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, spoke successively. The speakers unanimously expressed their firm belief in liberating Taiwan, supporting the Korean people's war of liberation, and defending world peace. After the meeting, 40,000 people held a demonstration and march.

### 天津集会庆祝八一  四万人示威游行

1950-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津集会庆祝八一
    四万人示威游行
    【新华社天津一日电】津市各界四万余人隆重举行庆祝“八一”建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜大会。会上黄敬市长讲话。他说：天津人民坚决与全国全世界人民团结起来，粉碎美帝国主义的任何侵略挑衅，坚决解放台湾、西藏及支持朝鲜人民的解放斗争。驻津某兵团杨成武司令员讲话说：尽管美帝国主义叫嚣和准备扩大战争，但胜利一定是属于世界人民的。我们中国人民一定要把五星国旗插到台湾去！相继讲话的有朝鲜侨民代表金凤台、民主建国会天津分会筹委会主任李烛尘、民主同盟天津支部临时工委会主任孟秋江、战斗英雄黄树田、市总工会副主席王老五等。讲话的人一致表示了他们对解放台湾、支援朝鲜人民的解放战争和保卫世界和平的坚决信念。会后四万人举行示威游行。
