Title: Lanzhou, Dihua, Xining, Yinchuan: Military and Civilians Warmly Celebrate Army Day; People of All Ethnic Groups Unite Closely Against American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 3rd. Military personnel and civilians in the provincial capitals of Gansu, Xinjiang, Qinghai, and Ningxia held demonstration parades to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army and to oppose the bandit behavior of the United States in invading Taiwan and Korea. At a mass rally in Lanzhou, more than 30,000 military personnel and civilians unanimously expressed their determination to unite closely, strengthen the suppression of bandits, eliminate special agents, and work hard in production to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Tibet and Taiwan and to support the heroic struggle of the Korean people and army against the American aggressors. After the congress adopted messages of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, General Kim Il-sung, and the Beijing Anti-American Aggression Movement Committee, the Gansu Provincial Branch of the China Democratic League presented a flag to the People's Liberation Army. After the meeting, the military and civilians held a grand demonstration parade led by more than 80 publicity floats.
	At the demonstration in Dihua, people of all ethnic groups passed telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, General Kim Il-sung, and Professor Curie, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Paris World Congress for the Defense of Peace, expressing that the 5.3 million people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang resolutely support world peace and oppose any act of aggression by the United States. Prior to this, people's organizations and agencies in Dihua jointly organized a comfort group to visit the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army in the hospital. Family women of all ethnic groups greeted "August 1st" with practical actions to help the troops rush to make winter clothes.
	Nearly 10,000 people held a memorial meeting in Xining, Qinghai. Representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life participated in the review of the local garrison.
	In addition to holding a memorial meeting, Yinchuan, Ningxia, held a torch parade of more than 10,000 people in the evening.
	In addition, the People's Liberation Army, which is scattered in rural areas throughout the Northwest and is responsible for production, railway construction, and bandit suppression, held military conferences in units of regiments, battalions, and companies during the festival. The heads of political organs reported on the history of the army's establishment, and held military-civilian get-togethers with local residents to celebrate their festival.

### 兰州、迪化、西宁、银川  军民热烈庆祝建军节  各族人民亲密团结反对美国侵略

1950-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　兰州、迪化、西宁、银川
    军民热烈庆祝建军节
    各族人民亲密团结反对美国侵略
    【新华社西安三日电】甘肃、新疆、青海、宁夏等四省省会军民，于八一建军节分别举行了示威游行大会，庆祝人民解放军建军二十三周年，和反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的强盗行为。在兰州市举行的群众大会上，三万余军民一致表示要亲密团结，加强剿匪、肃清特务、努力生产，以支援人民解放军解放西藏、台湾，并支援朝鲜军民反击美国侵略者的英勇斗争。大会通过了向毛主席、朱总司令、金日成将军及北京反美侵略运动委员会等致敬的电文后，由民盟甘肃省支部向人民解放军献旗。会后，与会军民以八十余辆宣传彩车作先导，举行了盛大的示威游行。
    迪化市各族各界人民在示威大会上通过了致毛主席、朱总司令、金日成将军和致巴黎世界拥护和平大会常设委员会主席居里教授的通电，表示新疆各族五百三十万人民坚决拥护世界和平，反对美国的任何侵略行为。在此以前，迪化市各人民团体、机关曾联合组织慰问团，赴医院慰问解放军的伤病员。各族家庭妇女以协助部队赶制冬装的实际行动，迎接“八一”节。
    青海西宁市举行了近万人的纪念大会，各族各界代表参加了检阅当地驻军的典礼。
    宁夏银川市除举行纪念大会外，入晚，举行了万余人的火炬大游行。
    此外，分散驻在西北各地乡村中担任生产、修筑铁路和剿匪任务的人民解放军，在节日以团、营、连为单位分别召开军人大会，由政治机关首长报告建军历史，并与驻地居民举行军民联欢大会，欢度自己的节日。
