Title: Haikou Military and Civilians Rally and March: Standing on the Forefront of National Defense to Support the Liberation of Taiwan; Nanning All Circles Also Rally in the Rain
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 3rd: Haikou Dispatch: More than 20,000 military personnel and civilians in Haikou City, Hainan Administrative Region, held a rally and demonstration on the 1st to celebrate Army Day and oppose the American aggression against Taiwan and Korea. The military and civilians present expressed extreme indignation at the US imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea. The conference sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, stating that the military and civilians of Hainan, standing on the forefront of national defense, will unite to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan. The conference also passed a telegram of salute to the heroic Korean military and civilians. After the meeting, a demonstration was held. In the three days prior to this, more than 2,000 people from the Hainan District Summer Study Group, the Hainan Military Region Cultural Troupe, the PLA Unit Cultural Troupe, and the propaganda teams of various agencies and organizations were dispatched continuously to carry out propaganda activities in the streets and villages. Twenty units including the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Youth League, and the Democratic Women's Federation have held forums in recent days to oppose American aggression.

Another dispatch from Nanning: People from all walks of life in Nanning City gathered on the 1st in the rain to warmly celebrate Army Day. The leaders of the Guangxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, and the Guangxi Military Region Headquarters respectively wrote letters to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, expressing their respect and extending cordial greetings to the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers. On the eve of August 1st, more than 20,000 military personnel and civilians in the city held a torch demonstration parade to oppose American aggression against Taiwan and Korea.

### 海口军民集会游行  站在国防最前线支援解放台湾  南宁市各界亦冒雨集会

1950-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　海口军民集会游行
    站在国防最前线支援解放台湾
    南宁市各界亦冒雨集会
    【新华社汉口三日电】海口讯：海南行政区海口市二万余军民于一日举行庆祝建军节和反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜的示威大会。与会军民对美帝国主义侵略台湾朝鲜的行为表示极度愤慨。大会致电毛主席、朱总司令，表示站在国防最前线的海南军民，要团结一致支持人民解放军解放台湾。大会并通过了向英勇奋战的朝鲜军民的致敬电文。会后，举行了游行示威。在此以前的三天中，海南区暑期学习团学员、海南军区文工团、解放军某部文工团及各机关团体的宣传队共二千余人，连续出动，在街头和乡村展开宣传活动。市总工会、青年团、民主妇联等二十单位，连日分别召集了反对美国侵略的座谈会。
    又南宁讯：南宁市各界于一日冒雨集会，热烈庆祝建军节。中共广西省委、广西省人民政府暨广西军区司令部各首长分别写信向毛主席、朱总司令和人民解放军全体指战员致敬，并向烈军属致亲切慰问。该市两万余军民曾于八一前夕举行了反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的火炬示威大游行。
