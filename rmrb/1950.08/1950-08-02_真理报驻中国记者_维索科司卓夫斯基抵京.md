Title: Pravda Correspondent to China, Visokostrovsky, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. - Visokostrovsky, correspondent to China for the Soviet newspaper Pravda, arrived in Beijing on July 31st.
