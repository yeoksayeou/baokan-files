Title: China People's Anti-American Aggression Committee, China Peace Committee Joint Statement of Protest
Author:
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The China People's Anti-American Aggression Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee and the China Peace Committee jointly issued a statement protesting against American planes intruding into our country's airspace and strafing our people. The statement is as follows:

    On August 27, American planes repeatedly invaded our country's Northeast airspace and strafed Linjiang, Andong, and other places, killing three of our compatriots and injuring twenty-one. The Chinese people cannot but lodge a serious protest with the U.S. government against this provocative and brutal act of the U.S. aggression forces in Korea, and demand that the United Nations Security Council immediately take effective measures to sanction the U.S. for this crime of invading Chinese airspace and killing Chinese people, and to make the U.S. government completely withdraw its aggressive forces in Korea in order to facilitate the peaceful mediation of the Korean issue by the United Nations.
   China People's Anti-American Aggression Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee
   China Peace Committee
    August 28

### 中国人民反对美国侵略委员会  中国保卫世界和平大会委员会联合发表抗议声明

1950-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民反对美国侵略委员会  中国保卫世界和平大会委员会联合发表抗议声明
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会、中国保卫世界和平大会委员会联合发表声明，抗议美机侵入我国领土上空扫射我国人民，声明如下：
    美国飞机在八月二十七日多次侵入我国东北领空，并在临江、安东等地扫射，使我国同胞死三人，伤二十一人。中国人民对于美国侵略朝鲜军队这种挑衅和残暴行为，不能不向美国政府提出严重抗议，并要求联合国安全理事会立即采取有效措施，来制裁美国这种侵入中国领空残杀中国人民的罪行，并使美国政府完全撤退其在朝鲜的侵略军队，以利联合国和平调处朝鲜问题。
   中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会
   中国保卫世界和平大会委员会
    八月二十八日
