Title: Czechoslovak Government, Hungarian Peace Committee Cable to UN Security Council Demanding Cessation of US Indiscriminate Bombing of Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: A comprehensive TASS dispatch: The Czechoslovak government cabled the UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie on the 18th, protesting the US government and other Security Council members under the influence of the US government's opposition to the representatives of the Korean people presenting their opinions in the Security Council. It also cabled the President of the Security Council, Malik, demanding a halt to the brutal bombing of peaceful Korean residents by the American aggressors.
　　The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League and many other social organizations, including the Free Fighters League and the Catholic Action Association, have also cabled the Security Council, demanding that the US Air Force cease indiscriminate bombing of peaceful Korean residents and stop the slaughter of innocent Korean people.
　　The Hungarian Peace Committee and the Hungarian Reformed Church have also cabled the Security Council, making the same protest.

### 捷政府、匈和委会电安理会  要求制止美国滥炸朝鲜

1950-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷政府、匈和委会电安理会
    要求制止美国滥炸朝鲜
    【新华社二十二日讯】综合塔斯社消息：捷克斯洛伐克政府于十八日致电联合国秘书长赖伊，抗议美国政府以及其他在美国政府影响之下的安理会理事国反对朝鲜人民代表在安理会中陈述意见的立场，并致电安理会主席马立克，要求制止美国侵略者对朝鲜和平居民的残暴轰炸。
    捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟中央委员会及其他许多社会团体，包括自由战士联盟和天主教行动协会在内，也纷纷致电安理会，要求制止美国空军滥炸朝鲜和平居民并停止屠杀朝鲜无辜人民。
    匈牙利和平委员会及匈牙利基督新教会也致电安理会，提出同样的抗议。
