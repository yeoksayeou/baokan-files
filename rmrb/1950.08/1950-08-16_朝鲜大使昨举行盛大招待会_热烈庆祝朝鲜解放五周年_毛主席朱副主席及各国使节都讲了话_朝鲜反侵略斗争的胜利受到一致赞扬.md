Title: Korean Ambassador Hosts Grand Reception to Warmly Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of Korean Liberation; Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Zhu, and Envoys from Various Countries Deliver Speeches; Victory of the Korean Anti-Aggression Struggle Unanimously Praised
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Zhouyuan, held a grand reception at 8 p.m. today to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China was invited to attend. Those invited to attend included Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Huang Yanpei, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and heads of various people's organizations, totaling more than 500 people.

Diplomatic envoys to China who were invited to attend included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Polish Ambassador Burgin, Head of the German Diplomatic Mission Koenig, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy Hašek, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy Fekete, Counselor of the Mongolian Embassy Labdan, and others.

In the center of the venue hung the national flags of Korea, the Soviet Union, and China, as well as portraits of General Kim Il-sung, Marshal Stalin, and Chairman Mao Zedong. There were also huge slogans such as "Commemorate the fifth anniversary of the 'August 15th' liberation of the Korean people and fight to the end to eliminate the American imperialist bandits who are invading Korea!", "Long live General Kim Il-sung, the beloved leader and national hero of the Korean people!", "Long live Marshal Stalin, the great teacher of peace-loving people all over the world and the benefactor of the liberation of the Korean people!", "Long live Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people!", "Long live the friendship and unity between the Korean and Chinese people!"

At the reception, Ambassador Li Zhouyuan delivered a speech first. Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China also spoke. Others who spoke included: Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Polish Ambassador to China Burgin, Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China Koenig, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China Hašek, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China Fekete, and Counselor of the Mongolian Embassy in China Labdan.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

At the end of the reception, films commemorating the fourth anniversary of the "August 15th" liberation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Korean people's current war of liberation against American imperialism and the Li Seung-man clique were shown. (Photo attached)

### 朝鲜大使昨举行盛大招待会  热烈庆祝朝鲜解放五周年  毛主席朱副主席及各国使节都讲了话  朝鲜反侵略斗争的胜利受到一致赞扬

1950-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜大使昨举行盛大招待会
    热烈庆祝朝鲜解放五周年
    毛主席朱副主席及各国使节都讲了话  朝鲜反侵略斗争的胜利受到一致赞扬
    【新华社十五日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊为庆祝朝鲜解放五周年，特于今日下午八时举行盛大招待会。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席应邀出席。应邀与会者尚有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、副总理董必武、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，暨中央人民政府各部门首长及各人民团体负责人等共五百余人。
    各国驻华外交使节应邀与会者有苏联大使罗申、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、波兰大使布尔金、德意志外交使团团长柯尼希、捷克大使馆临时代办哈谢克、匈牙利大使馆临时代办费凯尔特、蒙古大使馆参赞拉布丹等。
    会场正中悬挂朝鲜、苏联、中国三国国旗及金日成将军、斯大林大元帅与毛泽东主席肖像，并悬有“纪念朝鲜人民‘八、一五”解放五周年，为消灭侵略朝鲜的美帝强盗战斗到底！”、“朝鲜人民敬爱的领袖民族英雄金日成将军万岁！”、“全世界爱好和平人民的伟大导师、朝鲜人民的解放恩人斯大林大元帅万岁！”、“中国人民伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！”“朝中两国人民友谊团结万岁！”等巨幅标语。
    在招待会上，李周渊大使首先致词。中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席、朱德副主席都讲了话。此外讲话者有：苏联驻华大使罗申、波兰驻华大使布尔金、罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科、德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团团长柯尼希、捷克驻华大使馆临时代办哈谢克、匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞费凯尔特、蒙古驻华大使馆参赞拉布丹。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
    招待会最后放映朝鲜民主主义人民共和国纪念“八、一五”解放四周年及朝鲜人民现正进行的反对美帝国主义及李承晚匪帮的解放战争的影片。（附图片）
