Title: All-Japan Trade Union Liaison Council Launches "Anti-War Peace Month"
Author:
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 4
Countries Defending World Peace Movement

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th dispatch: The All-Japan Trade Union Liaison Council held a meeting on July 19th to discuss the current activity guidelines. The meeting resolved to designate the month from July 25th to August 25th as "Anti-War Peace Month," and to further expand the Stockholm Appeal signature campaign in response to the World Federation of Trade Unions' call to support the Korean people.

### 日全国工会联络协议会发起“反战和平月”

1950-08-19
第4版()
专栏：各国保卫世界和平运动

　　日全国工会联络协议会发起“反战和平月”
    【新华社十八日讯】东京迟到消息：日本全国工会联络协议会曾于七月十九日召开会议，讨论当前的活动方针。会上决议自七月二十五日起至八月二十五日为止的一个月为“反战和平月”，进一步展开斯德哥尔摩宣言签名运动，以响应世界工会联合会关于支援朝鲜人民的号召。
