Title: Greek Peasant Party Holds Central Plenary Session; Adopts Resolution on Party Tasks; Striving for Peace and Opposing Imperialist Aggression Plots
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 11th: The "Free Greece" radio station broadcast the resolution on the Party's tasks adopted by the Fourth Central Plenary Session of the Greek Peasant Party, stating: Today, the primary task of the Peasant Party is to raise the level of rural struggle to that of the urban working people. To fight for peace and to eliminate the danger of turning Greece into a bridgehead and battlefield for imperialist aggression is one of the central tasks of the Party. The Peasant Party reiterates: No Greek patriot will fight against the People's Democratic countries or against the Soviet Union.

The Peasant Party must organize the peasants to fight for their democracy and freedom, for amnesty, to eliminate all concentration camps, to disband the Royalist-fascist forcibly armed peasant troops, and to allow the peasants' sons to be demobilized. This struggle must be closely linked with the struggle waged by the working class and urban working people.

On the basis of the worker-peasant alliance, uniting all democratic forces in Greece to participate in a broad democratic front is the only guarantee for the development of national democratic forces. The Peasant Party must do its utmost to unite the democratic, progressive, and patriotic forces in the countryside into this front.

### 希农民党举行中央全会  通过党的任务决议  争取和平反对帝国主义侵略阴谋

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　希农民党举行中央全会
    通过党的任务决议
    争取和平反对帝国主义侵略阴谋
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社索非亚十一日讯：“自由希腊”电台广播希腊农民党第四次中央全会通过的关于党的任务的决议称：今天，农民党的主要任务，就是要把农村斗争的水平，提高到城市劳动人民斗争的水平。为争取和平而斗争，为消灭把希腊变成帝国主义侵略的桥头堡和战场的危险而斗争，是党的中心任务之一。农民党再次宣称：没有一个希腊爱国者会与人民民主国家作战，会与苏联作战。
    农民党必须组织农民起来斗争，争取他们的民主、自由，争取大赦，消灭一切集中营，解散保皇法西斯强迫武装起来的农民队伍，使农民的儿子们得以复员。必须与工人阶级和城市劳动人民所进行的斗争紧密联合，以开展这一斗争。
    在工农联盟的基础上，联合希腊一切民主力量参加广泛的民主阵线，是全国民主力量发展的唯一保证。农民党必须尽力把农村中的民主、进步与爱国的力量团结到这个阵线中。
