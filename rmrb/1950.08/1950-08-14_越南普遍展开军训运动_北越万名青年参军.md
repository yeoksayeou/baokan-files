Title: Military Training Movement Universally Unfolded in Vietnam; 10,000 Youths in North Vietnam Enlist in the Army
Author:
Date: 1950-08-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 13th. Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam Third War Zone, 12th: In the first half of this year, 10,000 youths in the Third War Zone of North Vietnam have joined the Vietnamese regular army and militia. This includes 1,580 high school students. Currently, the military training movement is being universally carried out in various regions; military training courses are now being implemented in the three provinces of Ha Nam, Nam Dinh, and Ha Dong, south and southwest of Hanoi.

### 越南普遍展开军训运动  北越万名青年参军

1950-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南普遍展开军训运动
    北越万名青年参军
    【新华社十三日讯】越南通讯社北越第三战区十二日讯：本年上半年，北越第三战区已有一万名青年参加越南正规军及民军。其中包括高中学生一千五百八十名。目前，军事训练运动正在各地普遍展开；在河内以南及西南的河南、南定及河东三省内，军事训练课程现正实施中。
