Title: Land Reform Preparations Completed in Most Newly Liberated Areas of South Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st—According to the "Labor News" here, the peasants in the newly liberated areas of South Korea are enthusiastically supporting the land reform decree promulgated by the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Republic on July 4 for implementation in the newly liberated areas. Most areas have made all the necessary preparations, and the distribution of land will begin on August 1.

　　The "Land Reform Working Committees" (organizations at the county and district level leading land reform work) and the "Rural Committees" (village level committees for land distribution) in Gyeonggi Province have been basically organized. Many villages in Hwanghae Province, Gyeonggi Province, and southern counties of Gangwon Province have elected "Rural Committees" under the guidance of the "Land Reform Working Committees," in accordance with the land reform decree and its implementing regulations.

　　The Rural Committees that have been formed are currently investigating the land that should be confiscated without compensation from American imperialists, the Lee Seung-man puppet government, and landlords, as well as investigating the number of poor peasants and farm laborers' households and population that should receive land without compensation.

### 南朝鲜大部新区已完成土改准备

1950-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜大部新区已完成土改准备
    【新华社平壤一日电】据此间《劳动新闻》报道：南朝鲜新解放地区的农民，热烈拥护共和国最高人民会议常任委员会七月四日颁布的在新解放地区实施土地改革的法令。大部分地区已作好了一切准备工作，八月一日即将开始分配土地。
    京畿道内“土地改革工作委员会”（县区级领导土改工作的组织）与“农村委员会”（各乡的分地委员会），已基本上组织完毕。黄海道、京畿道与江原道南部各县的许多乡，已在“土改工作委员会”指导下，根据土地改革法令与实施细则选举了“农村委员会”。
    已组成的农村委员会，目前正在调查应被无偿没收的曾为美帝国主义者、李承晚傀儡政府以及地主所有的土地，并调查应无偿分得土地的贫雇农的户数与人口等。
