Title: Su HePing Work Team Movement: Many Enterprises Exceed Plans in One Month
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 5th: According to TASS, Moscow, August 3rd: As Soviet working people conclude the signing of the peace appeal, they are joyfully summarizing the gains made over the past month working in the Stakhanovite "Peace Work Teams."

　　In Zhdanov City in the Urals, twenty factories have become Stakhanovite enterprises. Steelworkers in Magnitogorsk smelted eight furnaces of steel using high-speed methods on August 2nd alone. The work team led by Stalin Prize winner Kafarov, petroleum worker in Baku, has completed the production plan for the first eight months of this year one month ahead of schedule. In many enterprises in Leningrad, the month of the Peace Work Team movement was a month in which workers exceeded their production plans; the Kirov factory alone produced hundreds of tons of extra-plan metal. The production of non-ferrous metals at the "Red Electrician" factory has exceeded the July plan by 6.2 percent.

　　The tractor team led by the famous Socialist Labor Hero and member of the Soviet Peace Committee, Angelina, completed the grain harvest one week earlier than last year on the "Zaporozhets" collective farm. On some fields in this collective farm, the harvest reached 240 poods per hectare (approximately 510 jin per mu).

### 苏和平工作班运动一月  许多企业超额完成计划

1950-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏和平工作班运动一月
    许多企业超额完成计划
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：苏联劳动人民在结束和平签名之时，正欢快地总结过去一个月来，他们在斯塔哈诺夫式的“和平工作班”中工作的收获。
    乌拉尔的日丹诺夫城，已有二十个工厂成为斯塔哈诺夫式的企业。马格尼托哥尔斯克的炼钢工人，仅在八月二日一天，就以高速方法熔炼了八炉钢。巴库的石油工人、斯大林奖金获得者卡法罗夫所领导的工作队，已提前一个月完成了今年头八个月的生产计划。在列宁格勒的许多企业中，和平工作班运动的一个月就是工人们超额完成生产计划的一个月，单是基洛夫工厂，就已生产了数百吨超计划的金属。“红色选举者”工厂的非铁金属生产，已超过七月份计划的百分之六点二。
    著名的社会主义劳动英雄、苏联拥护和平委员会委员安吉利娜所领导的拖拉机队，在“萨波罗什人”集体农场内，较去年早一个星期完成了谷物收获工作。在这个集体农场中，有些田地每公顷的收获量竟达二百四十普特（约合每市亩收获五百一十市斤。）
