Title: Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition Extended for Three Days Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-08-28
Page: 6

The National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition has attracted over 300,000 visitors as of August 27th. Due to repeated requests for an extension from various parties, the organizing committee has decided to extend the exhibition in Halls 2, 3, and 4 for three days, from August 28th to August 30th. People from all walks of life who have not yet seen the exhibition should seize the time to visit.

### 卫生医药展览会今起延期三天

1950-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　卫生医药展览会今起延期三天
    全国卫生医药展览会截至八月二十七日止观众已超过三十万人之多。各方仍再三要求延期，该会为适应观众要求，决定将第二、三、四馆延期三天，由八月二十八日起至八月三十日止，尚未看到展览的各界人士，可抓紧时间前往参观。
