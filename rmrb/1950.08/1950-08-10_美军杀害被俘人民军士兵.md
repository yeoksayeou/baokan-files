Title: U.S. Military Kills Captured People's Army Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1950-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 8th (TASS): American newspapers published some news about the U.S. military's atrocities in Korea and the massacre of North Korean soldiers. An Overseas News Agency reporter, in describing the battle for Hill 180, reported: Two enemy soldiers were captured in that battle. The two prisoners refused to provide any information, only giving their names and ages. U.S. Colonel Emmerich, in response to reporters' inquiries, declared that the two prisoners "will be killed within a few hours."

### 美军杀害被俘人民军士兵

1950-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　美军杀害被俘人民军士兵
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社纽约八日讯：美国报纸登载了一些关于美军在朝鲜的暴行和虐杀北朝鲜士兵的消息。海外通讯社记者在描述一八○高地战争时报道：在该役中俘敌军两名。此两俘虏拒绝供给任何情报，仅仅说出他们的姓名和年龄。美国艾麦利奇上校在答复记者询问时宣称，此两俘虏“将在数小时内被杀掉”。
