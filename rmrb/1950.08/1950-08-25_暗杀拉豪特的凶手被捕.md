Title: Assassin of Lahaut Arrested
Author:
Date: 1950-08-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 23rd (TASS dispatch): According to the report of *l'Humanité*, under the pressure of the widespread protests of the people of Belgium and the whole world, the Belgian authorities have been forced to arrest the assassin of Lahaut, Chairman of the Belgian Communist Party. The two assassins of Lahaut were both fascists and belonged to the Leopoldists.
    *l'Humanité* pointed out: In Belgium, the conspiracy against democratic organizations has been exposed, and caches of various weapons have also been discovered. The newspaper emphasized that only united action by the masses can force the ruling class of Belgium to take necessary measures against fascist conspirators and assassins.

### 暗杀拉豪特的凶手被捕

1950-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　暗杀拉豪特的凶手被捕
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十三日讯：据人道报消息，在比利时全国和全世界人民广泛抗议的压力下，比利时当局已被迫将暗杀比利时共产党主席拉豪特的凶手逮捕。暗杀拉豪特的两名凶手都是法西斯主义者，并属于利奥波德派。
    人道报指出：在比利时，对民主组织实行的阴谋已经被揭穿了，各种武器的贮藏所也被发现了。该报强调说：只有群众一致的行动，才可以迫使比利时的统治阶级对法西斯阴谋者们和暗杀的凶手们采取必要的措施。
