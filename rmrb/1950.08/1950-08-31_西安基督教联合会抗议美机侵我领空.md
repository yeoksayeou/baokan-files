Title: Xi'an Christian Association Protests US Aircraft Intrusion into Chinese Airspace
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Xi'an Christian Association, regarding the brutal act of American airplanes intruding into our country's airspace and killing our compatriots, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou on the 29th. The telegram stated: We firmly oppose American imperialist planes invading the Northeast and strafing Chinese compatriots; and on behalf of all Christians in Xi'an, we support Premier Zhou's protest to American imperialism. We vow to fight to the end to safeguard China's territorial sovereignty and defend world peace.

### 西安基督教联合会抗议美机侵我领空

1950-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　西安基督教联合会抗议美机侵我领空
    【新华社三十日讯】西安市基督教联合会为美国飞机侵入我国领空，残杀我同胞的暴行，于二十九日致电毛主席、周总理。电文称：我们坚决反对美帝飞机侵略东北，扫射中国同胞；并代表西安全体基督教徒拥护周总理向美帝所提抗议。誓为维护中国领土主权，保卫世界和平奋斗到底。
