Title: "Soviet Union Introduction" Lecture to be Held Today at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 6

　　"Soviet Union Introduction" Lecture to be Held Today at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

　　The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a "Soviet Union Introduction" lecture at 7:30 pm today (August 29th) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Comrade Feribov will speak on: "Lenin-Stalin's National Policy and the Friendship of the Soviet Nationalities." Those holding admission tickets and "Soviet Union Introduction" long-term lecture passes may attend.

　　If branches of the Friendship Association wish to attend, they may obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from the branch to the association at 2 Yuhe Bridge.
      (Chen Yingnan)

### “苏联介绍”讲演会今在苏对外文协举行
陈应楠
1950-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　“苏联介绍”讲演会今在苏对外文协举行
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会，定于今日（二十九日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行“苏联介绍”讲演会，请费里伯夫讲：“列宁——斯大林的民族政策及苏联各民族的友谊”。凡持有入场券和“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各支会如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信，往御河桥二号该会索取入场券。
      （陈应楠）
