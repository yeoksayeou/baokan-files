Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Our Ambassador to Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th (Delayed Dispatch) Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received Comrade Ni Zhiliang, our country's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Korea, for the first time at 1:00 PM on the 15th. Foreign Minister Park Heon-yeong of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was also present.

### 金日成首相接见我驻朝大使

1950-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　金日成首相接见我驻朝大使
    【新华社平壤十八日电】（迟到）朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成首相，于十五日下午一时首次接见我国驻朝鲜特命全权大使倪志亮将军。在座者尚有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外相朴宪永。
