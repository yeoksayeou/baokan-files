Title: Beijing Municipal Sports Association Holds Football Matches
Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 6

To raise funds for the "Sunday Football Match," the Beijing Municipal Branch of the All-China Sports Federation will hold football matches at Fu Jen Catholic University on Sunday, August 20th (postponed for one week in case of rain). The schedule is as follows:

　　2:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Amateur - Xiyan; 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Ziguang - Julian; 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM: Brothers - Lihua. Ticket sales will begin at 9:00 AM on Thursday, August 17th. The ticket price is 2,000 yuan per ticket. Tickets will be sold at the following eight locations: Beijing Municipal Sports Association, Youth Service Department, Laboring People's Cultural Palace, First Cultural Center, Second Cultural Center, Tonglai Sporting Goods Company, Zhitong Sporting Goods Company, and Chunhe Sporting Goods Company.
          (Beijing Municipal News Agency)

### 京市体育分会举办足球比赛

1950-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　京市体育分会举办足球比赛
    中华全国体育总会北京市分会为了筹募“星期球赛”基金，定于八月二十日星期日（遇雨顺延一周）下午在辅仁大学集办足球比赛，程序如下：
    二时——三时半：业余—西燕；三时半——五时：紫光——剧联；五时—六时半：兄弟——利华。八月十七日（星期四）上午九时开始售票，票价每张贰千元。售票地点为：京市体育分会，青年服务部，劳动人民文化宫，第一文化馆，第二文化馆，同来体育用品公司，志同体育用品公司，春和体育用品公司等八处。      
          （京市新闻处）
