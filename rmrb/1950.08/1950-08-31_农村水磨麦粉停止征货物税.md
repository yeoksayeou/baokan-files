Title: Rural Water-Powered Wheat Flour Exempted from Commodity Tax
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The General Taxation Bureau of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government, in order to reduce the burden on farmers and promote the utilization of water power, has submitted a request to the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government and obtained approval to exempt the commodity tax on wheat flour ground by water-powered stone mills scattered in rural areas. It has also notified local tax authorities to implement this measure from the date of the order.

### 农村水磨麦粉停止征货物税

1950-08-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　农村水磨麦粉停止征货物税
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府财政部税务总局为减轻农民负担并提倡利用水力起见，已呈经中央人民政府财政部批准对分散在农村中的水力发动石磨麦粉停征货物税，并已通知各地税务机关于令到之日实行。
