　　Beijing Children and Youth, Issue 53, Published
    The 53rd issue of "Beijing Children and Youth" was published on July 26th. The main contents of this issue include "The Story of General Kim Il-sung," which introduces the story of the Korean people's leader, General Kim Il-sung, fighting the Japanese in Northeast China. There is a story picture "Hua Hua Attends a Campfire" introducing the campfire of the Beijing Summer Camp for Children, an interesting children's story "Xiao Wu and the Pencil," and a short poem "Frog."

### 北京少年儿童第五十三期出版

1950-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京少年儿童第五十三期出版
    《北京少年儿童》第五十三期已于七月二十六日出版。该期主要内容有《金日成将军的故事》是介绍朝鲜人民领袖金日成将军在东北抗日打游击的故事。有介绍京市暑期少年营的营火会的故事画《华华参加营火会》和有趣的儿童故事《小五和铅笔》及短诗《青蛙》。
