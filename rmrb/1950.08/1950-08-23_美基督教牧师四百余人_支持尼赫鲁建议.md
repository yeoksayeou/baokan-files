Title: More Than 400 American Christian Pastors Support Nehru's Proposal
Author:
Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 21st: TASS, New York, August 20th: Four hundred and sixty-nine American Protestant missionaries and pastors (including many bishops and religious publishers) have jointly issued a statement supporting the demand for a "fair solution" to the Korean issue. The statement said: "We support any efforts aimed at a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, such as the proposal made by Prime Minister Nehru, which includes the suggestion to allow representatives of the People's Republic of China to participate in the United Nations in accordance with the UN Charter, and other steps necessary to restore the United Nations as an effective mediation body." The statement pointed out: "American people who favor friendship with all countries in the world need to make active efforts to create an atmosphere that strongly compels our government to fully support attempts to peacefully resolve the Korean issue." The statement has been sent to Truman, Acheson, Austin, Lie, and Malik, respectively.

### 美基督教牧师四百余人  支持尼赫鲁建议

1950-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　美基督教牧师四百余人
    支持尼赫鲁建议
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约二十日讯：美国基督教新教传教士牧师四百六十九人（包括许多主教及宗教报刊的发行人在内），联名发表一项支持要求“公正解决”朝鲜问题的声明。该声明称：“我们赞成任何旨在和平解决朝鲜问题的努力，例如尼赫鲁总理所提的建议，其中包括按照联合国宪章使中华人民共和国代表参加联合国的建议，以及其他使联合国恢复为一有效的调停机关所必须的种种步骤。”该声明指出：“赞成世界各国友好的美国人民，需要进行积极的努力，以造成一种气氛，强使我国政府充分支持和平解决朝鲜问题的尝试。”该声明已分别送达杜鲁门、艾奇逊、奥斯汀、赖伊与马立克。
