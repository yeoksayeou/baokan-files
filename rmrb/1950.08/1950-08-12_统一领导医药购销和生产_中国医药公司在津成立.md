Title: Unified Leadership of Pharmaceutical Purchasing and Sales and Production: China National Pharmaceutical Corporation Established in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 3

　　Unified Leadership of Pharmaceutical Purchasing and Sales and Production
    China National Pharmaceutical Corporation Established in Tianjin

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 10th – The China National Pharmaceutical Corporation was formally established in Tianjin on the first of this month. Its mission is to unify the leadership of national pharmaceutical purchasing, sales and production, and to promote the development of the Chinese pharmaceutical industry.

    Note: Since the First National Health Administration Conference in October of last year decided to establish the China National Pharmaceutical Corporation, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government has begun to establish the organization of pharmaceutical companies from the bottom up in various places. As of now, the Northeast has established a regional company, under which there are 25 branches and pharmacies in places such as Lushun-Dalian, Harbin, Changchun, and Jilin; in North China, Tianjin, Beijing, and Suiyuan branch companies have been established. Furthermore, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government has appointed Meng Qian as the General Manager of the China National Pharmaceutical Corporation and Luo Huichun as the Deputy Manager, both of whom assumed their positions on August 1st.

### 统一领导医药购销和生产  中国医药公司在津成立

1950-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　统一领导医药购销和生产
    中国医药公司在津成立
    【新华社天津十日电】中国医药公司已于本月一日在天津正式成立。其任务为统一领导全国医药的购销和生产，推动中国制药事业的发展。
    按：自去年十月间第一次全国卫生行政会议决定成立中国医药公司以来，中央人民政府卫生部即在各地自下而上地开始建立医药公司的组织。截至目前为止，东北已成立了区公司，其下并设立旅大、哈尔滨、长春、吉林等分公司及药房等共二十五处；在华北已成立了天津、北京、绥远三分公司。又，中央人民政府卫生部已任命孟谦为中国医药公司总经理，罗惠春为副经理，都已于八月一日到职视事。
