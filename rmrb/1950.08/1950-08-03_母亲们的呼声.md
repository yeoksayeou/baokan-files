Title: Mothers' Voices
Author: Ke Neiqiefu
Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 5

　　Mothers' Voices
    Ke Neiqiefu
    From the towering podium,
      The simple and solemn words of mothers rang out:
    Curse those damned enemies,
      Who want to snatch away our precious sons again!
　
    We want to raise our sons
      In a paradise of peace and happiness;
    May the clear sky be bright forever,
      And not be filled with the smoke of great war.
　
    We want to raise great poets, great musicians,
      And renowned labor heroes
    For the Soviet motherland,
      -- A happy and proud motherland.
　
    French women, Spanish women,
      And Polish mothers,
    Should resonate with the passionate voices of Russian mothers,
      Then your voices will be even louder.
　
    The sacred words of mothers calling for peace,
      This is a simple yet solemn roar...
    It surpasses the threats of bankers,
      It surpasses the salvo of any cannon!
　
    In the streets and alleys, in every household,
      People will hear the magnificent song:
    Down with the new war instigators! Fight for honor,
      For freedom, for world peace!
        (Translated by Yun)

### 母亲们的呼声
科内契夫
1950-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　母亲们的呼声
    科内契夫
    从高耸着的讲坛上传出了
      母亲们简单而森严的语句：
    咒骂那班该死的敌人吧，
      又想把我们的宝贝儿子夺走！
　
    我们要在和平幸福的乐园，
      从摇篮内把我们的儿子养大；
    愿晴朗的天空永远明媚，
      莫让它迷漫在大战烽烟里。
　
    我们要为苏维埃的祖国，
      ——以幸福而自豪的祖国，
    养育出大诗人，大音乐家，
      和鼎鼎大名的劳动英雄。
　
    法国的妇女，西班牙的妇女，
      还有波兰的老母亲，
    要与俄国母亲们的热烈呼声共鸣，
      那你们的呼声才更加响亮。
　
    母亲们呼吁和平的神圣语句，
      这是简单而又森严的怒吼……
    它胜过银行家们的威胁，
      它胜过任何大炮的齐鸣！
　
    在大街小巷，家家户户，
      人们都会听到雄壮的歌声：
    打倒新战争挑拨者！为荣誉，
      为自由，为全世界和平而奋斗！
        （云译）
