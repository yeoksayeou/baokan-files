Title: The Third Cultural Center Holds Photo Exhibition
Author: Xuan Huaiyuan
Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 6

　　The Third Cultural Center Holds Photo Exhibition
    The Beijing Municipal Third Cultural Center will hold two photo exhibitions starting today: one is a photo exhibition on the Korean War of Liberation, featuring twenty photos depicting the heroic posture of the North Korean armed forces, the enthusiasm of the Korean people in supporting the People's Army, the impoverished life of the South Korean people under the reactionary rule of Syngman Rhee, and the photos of the Rhee gang's brutal killing of patriotic South Korean people. This will be exhibited on Xidan North Avenue. The other is a systematic photo exhibition of forty-two photos introducing the Soviet Trade Unions, featuring the rights and obligations enjoyed by Soviet workers and the Law on Labor Protection for Women, etc. This will be exhibited inside the center on Xixie Street. All circles are welcome to visit. (Xuan Huaiyuan)

### 第三文化馆举办照片展览
宣怀远
1950-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　第三文化馆举办照片展览
    北京市立第三文化馆今日起举办两项照片展览：一为朝鲜解放战争照片，内容有北朝鲜武装的雄姿、朝鲜人民拥护人民军的热情、南朝鲜人民在李承晚反动统治下的穷困生活与李匪残酷杀害南朝鲜爱国人民的照片共计二十张，在西单北大街展览。另一为介绍苏联职工会的有系统的照片四十二张，内容有苏联工人所享受的权利与义务及妇女劳动保护法等，在西斜街馆内展览，欢迎各界前往参观。（宣怀远）
