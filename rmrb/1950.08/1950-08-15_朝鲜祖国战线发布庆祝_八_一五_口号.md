Title: Korean Fatherland Front Issues Slogans Celebrating "August 15th"
Author:
Date: 1950-08-15
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: The Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front has issued slogans commemorating the fifth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation, calling on all the Korean people to rise up together and fight to completely eliminate the enemy and liberate all of the country's territory. The commemorative slogans first pay tribute to the great Soviet Red Army, the Soviet people, and the leader of the Soviet people, Stalin. The slogans call on the heroic People's Army to "engage in a brutal battle with the enemy, defend the motherland's independence, freedom, and honor, and further enhance the momentum of wiping out the enemy!" "Wipe out the enemy more ruthlessly! Pursue the enemy and do not give them a chance to breathe!" "Annihilate the American aggressors!" "Quickly rescue the people in the yet-to-be-liberated areas!" The slogans also call on the heroic guerrillas to cooperate with the People's Army and "strike the enemy behind enemy lines!" The slogans issue calls to all the people on the front lines, in the rear, those liberated, and those awaiting liberation—workers, farmers, youth, women, artists, entrepreneurs, and merchants: "Contribute everything to the front lines!" "Wipe out the enemy completely!" The slogans point out: "The enemy is cunning and vicious. We must be more vigilant!" "Expose and punish rumor-mongers, saboteurs, assassins, secret agents, and spies in a timely manner, and further consolidate our rear!" The slogans finally call on all party members and members of patriotic political parties and social organizations to further strengthen the Fatherland Front, unite more firmly around the government of the Republic, and rise up together to eliminate the armed forces of American imperialist aggression and the Syngman Rhee bandit gang!

### 朝鲜祖国战线发布庆祝“八·一五”口号

1950-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜祖国战线发布庆祝“八·一五”口号
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会顷发布“八、一五”解放五周年纪念口号，号召朝鲜全体人民一致奋起为干净消灭敌人、为解放全部国土而奋斗。纪念口号首先向伟大的苏联红军、苏联人民、苏联人民领袖斯大林致敬。口号号召英雄的人民军“与敌人展开残酷的战斗，坚守祖国的独立、自由和荣誉，更加提高扫荡敌人的气势！”“要更无情地扫荡敌人！追击敌人，不要让它有喘息机会！”“消灭美国侵略者！”“迅速拯救尚未解放地区的人民！”口号并号召英勇的游击队员们配合人民军，“在敌人后方打击敌人！”口号向前线、后方、已解放的和待解放的全体人民——工人、农民、青年、妇女、艺术家、企业家、商人们发出号召：“为前线贡献一切！”“把敌人消灭干净！”口号指出：“敌人是狡猾而凶恶的。应该更加提高警惕性！”“及时揭发并惩办造谣分子、破坏分子、暗害分子，密探及间谍，更加巩固我们的后方！”口号最后号召爱国政党及社会团体所有党员会员，更加强化祖国战线，更坚强地团结在共和国政府周围，为消灭美帝国主义侵略武装和李承晚匪帮而一致奋起！
