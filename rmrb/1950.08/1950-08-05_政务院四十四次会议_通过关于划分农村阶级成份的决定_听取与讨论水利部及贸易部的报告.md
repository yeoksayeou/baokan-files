Title: The 44th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passed the "Decision on Classifying Rural Class Components" and Listened to and Discussed Reports from the Ministry of Water Resources and the Ministry of Trade
Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The 44th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:00 PM on the 4th. The meeting passed the "Decision on Classifying Rural Class Components". The meeting listened to and discussed the report made by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government, on the recent flood situation and inspection of river works; and after listening to the report made by Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, on the National Import and Export Trade Conference, it discussed and approved the report. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment cases and submitted appointment cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院四十四次会议  通过关于划分农村阶级成份的决定  听取与讨论水利部及贸易部的报告

1950-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院四十四次会议
    通过关于划分农村阶级成份的决定
    听取与讨论水利部及贸易部的报告
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第四十四次政务会议于四日下午三时举行。会议通过了关于划分农村阶级成份的决定。会议听取与讨论了中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的关于最近水灾情况及视察河工的报告；并在听取中央人民政府贸易部叶季壮部长所作的关于全国出进口贸易会议的报告后，讨论和批准了该报告。此外，会议通过任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
