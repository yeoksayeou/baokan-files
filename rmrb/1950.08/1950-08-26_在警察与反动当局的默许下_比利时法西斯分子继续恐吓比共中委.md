Title: With the tacit consent of the police and reactionary authorities, Belgian fascists continue to intimidate members of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, August 23rd: TASS reports: Belgian fascists, with the tacit consent of the police and authorities, continue to use terrorist tactics against democratic groups.
　　A "World Daily" reporter from Brussels reports that many members of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party have received threatening letters vowing to kill them.

### 在警察与反动当局的默许下  比利时法西斯分子继续恐吓比共中委

1950-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　在警察与反动当局的默许下
    比利时法西斯分子继续恐吓比共中委
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社海牙二十三日讯：比利时的法西斯分子得到警察与当局的默许，继续对民主团体采取恐怖手段。
    “世界日报”记者自布鲁塞尔来讯称，比利时共产党中央委员会的许多委员，都曾接到要将他们置于死地的恐吓信。
