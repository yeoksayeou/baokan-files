Title: Chinese Christians Issue Declaration Opposing US Imperialism's Sabotage of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: Thirty-one Chinese Christian representatives, including Wang Guoxiu and Ai Niansan, jointly published a declaration in the Shanghai Ta Kung Pao on August 1st, opposing the US invasion of Taiwan and Korea. The full text of the declaration is as follows:

　　US imperialism has frantically launched a war of aggression against Taiwan and Korea in the Far East. We Chinese Christians must, together with the people of China and the world, resolutely oppose this act of aggression by US imperialism, which is undermining world peace and disregarding international credibility.

　　We firmly support Chairman Mao and Foreign Minister Zhou's solemn statements regarding the US imperialist aggression. Taiwan is Chinese territory; the US government's dispatch of the Seventh Fleet to Taiwan in an attempt to prevent us from liberating Taiwan is an infringement upon China's territory and sovereignty, which the Chinese people cannot tolerate. We will enthusiastically support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan; we believe that the Chinese people have sufficient strength to complete this sacred task.

　　We express our highest respect for the heroic struggle of the Korean people and the brilliant victories they have achieved. We firmly believe that the Korean people will surely achieve final and complete victory and liberate their motherland from the clutches of the imperialists.

　　We express our extreme anger and serious protest against the US imperialist planes' indiscriminate bombing of peaceful cities and villages in Korea and the mass slaughter of innocent Korean people, women, and children. Truman's seeking God's blessing for US imperialist aggression in Korea is simply a blasphemous fantasy. The God of justice and love, whom every devout Christian worships, will never bless a bloodstained butcher and his accomplices. On the contrary, Truman and other masterminds of US imperialist crimes will surely suffer God's severe and merciless judgment.

　　The US imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea has thoroughly exposed the ferocious and evil true face of US imperialism, completely bankrupting its touted freedom and democracy. It also makes us clearly realize that opposing US imperialism and defeating US imperialist aggression is the common task of the people of the world.

　　Our opposition to US imperialism is not opposition to the good people of America. We want to call on American Christians to stand with us to oppose US imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea, to oppose Truman and all warmongers sending young people from the US and other countries to the front lines as cannon fodder, to oppose the US planes' massacre of the Korean people, and to take effective action to force US imperialists to end this criminal act of aggression.

　　The US military's repeated defeats in Korea fully demonstrate the fragility and incompetence of US imperialism, and also fully demonstrate the greatness of the people's power. Therefore, it is entirely possible for the people of the world to unite, defeat US imperialist aggression, and crush all US imperialist attempts to create war. We are willing to contribute our greatest strength to fight for the realization of this goal.

### 中国基督教人士发表宣言  反对美帝国主义破坏世界和平

1950-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国基督教人士发表宣言
    反对美帝国主义破坏世界和平
    【新华社三日讯】中国基督教代表人物王国秀、艾年三等三十一人八月一日在上海大公报联名发表宣言，反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜。宣言全文如下：
    美帝国主义已经疯狂地在远东发动了对台湾朝鲜的侵略战争。我们中国基督徒要与全中国，以及全世界的人民，一同坚决地反对美帝这一个破坏世界和平，蔑视国际信义的侵略行动。
    我们坚决地拥护毛主席和周外长对美帝侵略行动的严正声明，台湾是中国的领土；美国政府派遣第七舰队到台湾去，企图阻止我们解放台湾，这是侵犯了中国的领土与主权，是中国人民所不能容忍的。我们要热烈地支援人民解放军解放台湾；我们相信中国人民有充分的力量，去完成这个神圣的任务。
    我们对朝鲜人民的英勇斗争，和他们已经获得的辉煌的战果，表示最崇高的敬意。我们深信，朝鲜的人民，必定能够取得最后的，完全的胜利，把他们的祖国，从帝国主义者的魔手中解放出来。
    我们对美帝飞机滥炸朝鲜和平城市、乡村，大量屠杀朝鲜无辜人民与妇女儿童的罪恶行动，表示极度的愤怒，和严重的抗议。杜鲁门为美帝在朝鲜的侵略，祈求上帝的祝福，这简直是一个亵渎的妄想。每一个虔诚的基督教徒所崇奉的公义的慈爱的上帝，是决不会祝福一个满手血腥的屠夫和他的爪牙工具的。相反的，杜鲁门和其他美帝罪行的主谋者，是必定要遭受上帝严酷无情的审判的。
    美帝对台湾朝鲜的侵略，彻底地暴露了美帝穷凶极恶的真面目，使它所标榜的自由民主，完全破产，也使我们清楚地认识，反对美帝国主义，打败美帝国主义的侵略，是全世界人民的共同任务。
    我们反对美帝国主义，并不是反对美国良善的人民。我们要号召美国的基督徒，同我们站在一起，反对美帝国主义对台湾朝鲜的侵略，反对杜鲁门和一切战争贩子们把美国和其他各国青年送到前线当炮灰，反对美国飞机对朝鲜人民的屠杀，并用有效的行动，迫使美帝国主义者终止这一个罪恶的侵略行为。
    美国军队在朝鲜的节节惨败，充分地证明了美帝的脆弱无能，也充分地证明了人民的力量的伟大。因此，全世界的人民团结起来，击败美帝的侵略，粉碎美帝一切制造战争的企图，是完全可能的。我们愿意拿出我们最大的力量，为实现这个目标而奋斗。
    王国秀  中华基督教女青年会全国协会执行委员会主席
