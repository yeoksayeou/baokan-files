Title: National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition Opens Today
Author: Liu Rentao
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 3

　　National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition Opens Today

　　[This Newspaper's Report] The first National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition is scheduled to open today (6th). This exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government and the Health Department of the Military Commission. The purpose is to summarize the experience of health work, criticize the old society's medical and pharmaceutical measures, introduce the actual situation of the new China's health and pharmaceutical construction, and the heroic and arduous struggle of the medical workers of the People's Liberation Army in the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation. At the same time, it will publicize the direction of the future construction of the people's health and pharmaceutical undertakings. The exhibits come from all over the country. The materials collected by major administrative regions and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and various provinces in North China have exceeded 10,000 pieces. After careful selection, they are displayed in four pavilions: health, education, medicinal materials, and health services, including drawings, photographs, tables, models, objects, specimens, literature, etc.

　　The exhibition address is the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace (Tai Miao). It is scheduled to open at 9:00 AM today. Minister Li Dequan of the Central Ministry of Health will cut the ribbon, and the heads of various departments of the Central People's Government will speak. All staff members of the Central Ministry of Health and the Health Department of the Military Commission, attendees from all over the country to participate in the National Health Conference, teachers and students from medical, pharmaceutical, obstetrical, and nursing schools in Beijing, workers in traditional Chinese and Western medicine, and guests from various agencies and organizations will participate.

　　The exhibition is scheduled to be held for 20 days, open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM every day. Admission tickets for citizens will be uniformly distributed by the Beijing Municipal People's Government. Children under the age of ten are not admitted.

　　On the 3rd of this month, the conference held a preview. Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the People's Liberation Army and Director Guo Moruo of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government visited the meeting. Commander-in-Chief Zhu highly appreciated the rescue events in previous battles exhibited, especially the health service models in the Langya Mountain guerrilla warfare, tunnel warfare, and Huaihai front warfare. Director Guo Moruo and his wife, Yu Liqun, visited the health pavilion for four hours, inquiring in detail about each exhibit. He hoped that the exhibition could be popularized throughout the country as one of the tools for people's health education.
                (Liu Rentao)

### 全国卫生医药展览会今开幕
刘任涛
1950-08-06
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　全国卫生医药展览会今开幕
    【本报讯】第一届全国卫生医药展览会定今（六）日开幕。此次展览会系由中央人民政府卫生部与军委卫生部联合主办。其目的在总结卫生工作经验，批判旧社会的医药措施，介绍新中国卫生医药建设实况和人民解放军的医务工作者在抗日及解放战争中英勇地艰苦地斗争情形。同时宣传今后人民卫生医药事业建设的方向。展览品来自全国各地，由各大行政区及内蒙自治区、华北各省收集的材料已超过万件以上，经精选后分为卫生、教育、药材、卫勤四大馆陈列，包括有图画、照片、表格、模型、实物、标本、文献等。
    展览地址在京市劳动人民文化宫（太庙），预定今日上午九时开幕，届时将由中央卫生部李德全部长行剪彩礼，并有中央人民政府各部门首长讲话，中央卫生部与军委卫生部全部工作人员和来京参加全国卫生会议的出席人以及京市的医、药、产、护院校师生，中西医药业工作者，各机关团体的来宾参加。
    展览会预定展出二十天，每日上午九时至下午五时开放。市民参观券由北京市人民政府统一分配发给。十岁以下儿童谢绝入场。
    本月三日，该会曾举行预展。人民解放军朱总司令和中央人民政府文化教育委员会郭沫若主任曾莅会参观。朱总司令对展出的历次战役中的救护事迹，特别是狼牙山游击战、地洞战和淮海阵地战中的卫生勤务模型，备加赞赏。郭沫若主任偕夫人于立群曾在卫生馆参观四小时之久，对每一展品垂询甚详。他希望展览会能普及全国各地，作为人民卫生教育的工具之一。
                （刘任涛）
