Title: US Isolationist Congressman Attacks Truman, Believes Korean War Policy Will Lead US to Disaster
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 4

　　US Isolationist Congressman Attacks Truman
    Believes Korean War Policy Will Lead US to Disaster
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] According to an Agence France-Presse report on the 10th: American isolationist, Republican Senator Bridges, admitted in a speech to the Hampton Beach Republican Club on the 10th that the number of American casualties in Korea was "frighteningly high". He attacked the Truman administration's policies, saying that these policies were leading the United States to disaster.

### 美孤立派议员攻击杜鲁门  认为侵朝政策将美国导向灾难

1950-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　美孤立派议员攻击杜鲁门
    认为侵朝政策将美国导向灾难
    【新华社十二日讯】据法新社十日讯：美国孤立主义分子、共和党参议员勃里奇十日在罕普顿矶共和党俱乐部发表演说时承认：美军在朝鲜伤亡数目之高，“殊为可怕”。他攻击杜鲁门政府的政策说：此种政策正将美国导向灾难。
