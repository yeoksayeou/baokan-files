Title: Korean People's Army Repulses Frantic Counterattacks by American and Rhee Troops in the Waegwan Area; Thirteen American Planes Shot Down in Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1950-08-22
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced on the morning of the 20th that the People's Army units in the Waegwan area fiercely attacked the frantic counterattacks by American troops and the remnants of Rhee Syng-man's puppet army, supported by tanks and air force units.
　　In the battles in this area, the People's Army artillery units concentrated their fire on enemy tank units, destroying many enemy tanks and inflicting heavy losses on enemy personnel, repelling the enemy's counterattacks.
　　The People's Army units attacking on the southern coast engaged in fierce battles with the routed American 25th Division troops, who were stubbornly resisting while relying on favorable geographical conditions such as high ground and rivers.
　　American air forces continued their blind and barbarous bombing of peaceful rural areas and cities.
　　On August 19th, twelve American planes raided Seoul, indiscriminately strafing with machine guns. The People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units in the area concentrated their fire, shooting down five enemy planes outside Seoul.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced on the evening of the 20th that the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units had dealt a heavy blow to the American air force units that were blindly bombing peaceful towns and villages. The People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down one American B-29 bomber that raided Wonsan. On August 18th, they shot down four B-29 bombers and one fighter plane that raided Seoul. On August 19th, as already reported, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down five American planes that raided Seoul. On the same day, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down one American plane that raided Kaesong.
　　On August 20th, American planes raided Pyongyang five times in order to carry out blind and inhuman bombing. However, due to the fierce attacks by the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units, they failed to achieve their goals and fled. The People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down one enemy plane that raided Pyongyang.

### 朝鲜人民军在倭馆方面  击退美李军疯狂反攻  各地击落美机十三架

1950-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在倭馆方面
    击退美李军疯狂反攻
    各地击落美机十三架
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十日晨发表战报称：倭馆方面的人民军部队，猛烈打击由坦克和空军部队掩护的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军的疯狂反攻。
    人民军炮兵部队在该地区的战斗中，集中射击敌坦克部队，毁敌坦克多辆，并予敌有生力量以巨大损失，击退了敌人的反攻。
    在南海岸方面进攻中的人民军部队，向依靠高地、河川等有利地理条件顽抗的美军第二十五师溃败部队，进行激烈战斗。
    美航空部队对和平的农村和城市，继续进行盲目的、野蛮的轰炸。
    八月十九日，美机十二架侵袭汉城，以机枪进行盲目扫射。该地人民军高射炮部队予以集中射击，在汉城郊外击落敌机五架。
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十日晚发表战报称：人民军高射炮部队给予盲目轰炸和平城乡的美军航空部队以巨大打击。人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭元山市的美轰炸机Ｂ—２９一架。八月十八日，击落侵袭汉城市的Ｂ—２９轰炸机四架和驱逐机一架。八月十九日，如已经报道的，人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭汉城的美机五架。同日，人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭开城的美机一架。
    八月二十日，美机为了进行盲目的残无人道的轰炸，侵袭平壤市五次。但由于遭到人民军高射炮部队的猛烈攻击，未能达到目的而逃去。人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭平壤市的敌机一架。
