Title: Korean Residents in Beijing Gather to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of National Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of National Liberation
　　Korean Residents in Beijing Gather
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] All Korean residents in Beijing held a gathering on the afternoon of the 15th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the "August 15th" Korean Liberation. The meeting was presided over by Jiang Caishun. Che Dazheng delivered a report at the meeting. He elaborated on the achievements of democratic construction in northern Korea over the past five years, and expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the great Soviet army and Marshal Stalin for liberating Korea. Che Dazheng vehemently criticized the US aggressors for their direct armed invasion of Korea and the crimes of indiscriminate bombing and massacre of peaceful Korean residents. After recounting the glorious victory of the heroic Korean People's Army in liberating vast territories in the southern part of the country, he called on all overseas Chinese to strengthen their support for the people's liberation war being waged in their motherland, and to consolidate the friendship and unity between the people of Korea and China. The meeting concluded with the enthusiastic passing of a congratulatory telegram to General Kim Il-sung amidst warm applause.

### 庆祝祖国解放五周年  旅京朝鲜侨民集会

1950-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝祖国解放五周年
    旅京朝鲜侨民集会
    【新华社十六日讯】旅京全体朝鲜侨民于十五日下午举行了庆祝“八、一五”朝鲜解放五周年大会。大会由姜彩顺主持。车达成在会上作了报告。他详述了朝鲜北部五年间民主建设的成果，并向解放朝鲜的伟大苏联军队及斯大林大元帅致衷心的感谢。车达成猛烈抨击美国侵略者直接武力侵略朝鲜及滥肆轰炸和屠杀朝鲜和平居民的罪行。他在叙述英勇的朝鲜人民军解放了祖国南部广大国土的光辉胜利后，号召全体侨民加强声援正在祖国进行的人民解放战争，并巩固朝鲜和中国两国人民间的友谊团结。大会最后在热烈鼓掌声中通过了致金日成将军的贺电。
