Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Enters Magdalena Town
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Thirty-first Dispatch: According to Agence France-Presse, Manila, 29th Dispatch: A unit of the Philippine People's Liberation Army entered Magdalena Town in Laguna Province on the 29th. The armed police guarding the town fled upon hearing the news. After seizing many weapons and spoils, the People's Liberation Army voluntarily withdrew from the town.

### 菲律宾人民解放军攻入马奇达伦纳镇

1950-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲律宾人民解放军攻入马奇达伦纳镇
    【新华社三十一日讯】据法新社马尼拉二十九日讯：菲律宾人民解放军一部于二十九日攻入内湖省的马奇达伦纳镇。守卫该镇的武装警察，闻风逃去。人民解放军在缴获许多武器和战利品后，主动地退出了该镇。
