Title: Indonesian People Voice Support for Korean People's Struggle, Protest U.S. Imperialist Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 3rd: TASS reported from Indonesia: The Indonesian people are vehemently protesting against the American intervention in Korea and the American planes' indiscriminate bombing of peaceful residents in Korea. According to a reporter for "World Business Daily": Twenty-nine Indonesian and Overseas Chinese groups in Medan recently established a Peace Committee. The committee sent a telegram to Lie, protesting against the use of the United Nations flag to mask American aggression against Korea. The reporter also said: Not long ago, two hundred union branches sent representatives to attend the East Sumatra Trade Union Congress, where the congress urged the Indonesian government to condemn American aggression and demanded the withdrawal of American troops from Korea.

### 印尼人民声援朝鲜人民斗争  抗议美帝侵略朝鲜

1950-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼人民声援朝鲜人民斗争
    抗议美帝侵略朝鲜
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社海牙二日讯：据印尼消息：印尼人民猛烈地抗议美国干涉朝鲜和美国飞机滥炸朝鲜的和平居民，据《世界商报》记者报道：棉兰二十九个印尼的和华侨的团体，最近设立了和平委员会，委员会曾致电赖伊，抗议用联合国旗帜来掩饰美国侵略朝鲜。这位记者又说：不久前有二百个工会分会，派出代表出席东苏门答腊的工会代表大会，大会敦促印尼政府斥责美国的侵略，并要求美军撤出朝鲜。
