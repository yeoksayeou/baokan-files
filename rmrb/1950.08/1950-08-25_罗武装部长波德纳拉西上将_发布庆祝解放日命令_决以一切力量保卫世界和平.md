Title: Romanian Armed Forces Minister General Bodnăraş Issues Order Celebrating Liberation Day, Resolves to Defend World Peace With All Strength
Author: 
Date: 1950-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 24th: Romanian News Agency, Bucharest reports: Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic, General Bodnăraş issued the following order on August 23rd, Romanian Liberation Day:

Soldiers, sergeants, quartermasters, officers, and generals of the Army and Navy!

Our country's working people, proud of the arduous struggles experienced over the past six years, sincerely grateful to the great Stalin and the Soviet Union for their continuous brotherly assistance, and faithfully loving the Workers' Party that leads the Romanian people, will today celebrate the historical anniversary of liberation from fascist enslavement with even more new achievements.

Our country's working people are fighting for peace and are determined to defend peace with all their strength. The sabotage activities of the American and British warmongers and their tool, the Tito gang, have been resolutely resisted by our people.

Our people warmly salute the heroic Korean people; warmly salute the people all over the world who are fighting against the aggression of American, British and French imperialists; warmly salute the working class of France, Italy, Belgium and other countries who are fighting against the enslavement of Marshallization, as well as the progressive mankind all over the world; extend warm fraternal greetings to the glorious Soviet people and the great leader Stalin, to the great Chinese people who have won victory in the struggle against imperialism and for freedom and independence, and to the people's democracies.

### 罗武装部长波德纳拉西上将  发布庆祝解放日命令  决以一切力量保卫世界和平

1950-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗武装部长波德纳拉西上将
    发布庆祝解放日命令
    决以一切力量保卫世界和平
    【新华社二十四日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部部长波德纳拉西上将，于八月二十三日罗马尼亚解放日发布命令如下：
    陆海军士兵、军士、军需、军官和将军们！
    以过去六年来所经历的艰巨斗争而自豪的我国劳动人民，衷心感激一直不断给予我们以兄弟援助的伟大斯大林和苏联，忠诚热爱领导罗马尼亚人民的工人党，今天将以更多的新成就来庆祝自法西斯奴役下获得解放的历史纪念日。
    我国劳动人民正为和平而进行斗争，而且决心以一切力量保卫和平。美、英战争贩子及其工具铁托匪帮的阴谋破坏行动，已遭到我国人民坚决的反击。
    我国人民向英勇的朝鲜人民热烈致敬；向全世界为反对美、英、法帝国主义侵略者而斗争的人民，向为反对马歇尔化的奴役而斗争的法、意、比等国工人阶级，以及全世界的进步人类热烈致敬；向光荣的苏联人民及伟大领袖斯大林，向在反对帝国主义、争取自由与独立的斗争中获得胜利的伟大的中国人民，向各人民民主国家致以热烈的兄弟敬礼。
