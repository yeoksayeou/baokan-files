　　Photo Exhibition Held at the First Cultural Center
    To celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day, the First Cultural Center of Beijing Municipality is holding a photo and picture exhibition in the center's exhibition hall from August 1st to August 15th. Exhibition content: (1) The growth and strengthening of the Chinese People's Liberation Army – historical achievements from the Autumn Harvest Uprising in 1927 to the present; (2) Female hero Zhao Yiman; (3) The Five Warriors of Langya Mountain; (4) Revived in the Blood of Friendship; (5) Production situation in Zhangjiakou in 1950; (6) Transforming Lazy People, and more. (Yi Guan Tongxun Zu)

### 第一文化馆举办照片展览
一馆通讯组
1950-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　第一文化馆举办照片展览
    北京市立第一文化馆为了庆祝“八一”建军节自八月一日起到八月十五日止，在该馆陈列室内举办照片画片展览。展览内容：（一）中国人民解放军的成长壮大——自一九二七年秋收起义至今的史绩；（二）女英雄赵一曼；（三）狼牙山五战士；（四）在友谊的血液里复活；（五）一九五○年张家口生产情况；（六）改造懒汉等多幅。（一馆通讯组）
