Popular Science Lecture to be Held Tomorrow: Lecture No. 12
　　The Popular Science Lecture, co-sponsored by the Youth Service Department and the Science Popularization Bureau, will now be co-sponsored by the Youth Service Department, the Science Popularization Bureau, and the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace. The twelfth lecture is scheduled to be held at 7:00 p.m. on August 12 (Saturday) in the theater of the Labor People's Cultural Palace. Hu Wencheng will speak on "General Applications of Chemistry." The films "Agriculture and Soil" and "Fire" will be shown in conjunction with the lecture. All sectors are welcome to attend. Admission tickets can be collected during opening hours at the Youth Service Department on August 11 (Friday) (group tickets can be collected upon presentation of a letter of introduction). (China Youth Agency)

### 大众科学讲座明日举行第十二讲

1950-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座明日举行第十二讲
    青年服务部与科学普及局合办的大众科学讲座，今后改由青年服务部、科学普及局和北京市劳动人民文化宫三方面合办。第十二讲定于八月十二日（星期六）下午七时在劳动人民文化宫剧场举行，请胡文澄讲：“化学的一般应用”。并配合讲题放映电影“农业与土壤”和“火”。欢迎各界听讲，入场券可于八月十一日（星期五）在青年服务部开放时间内领取（团体票凭介绍信领取）              （中国青年社）
