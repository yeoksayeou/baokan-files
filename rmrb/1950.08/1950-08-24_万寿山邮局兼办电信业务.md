Title: Wanshoushan Post Office Simultaneously Handles Telecommunications Services
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 6

　　Wanshoushan Post Office Simultaneously Handles Telecommunications Services
　　The Wanshoushan Post Office has been simultaneously handling telecommunications services since the 14th of this month. It retails local and long-distance telephone calls, and accepts and sends domestic ordinary and express telegrams. People from all walks of life and citizens visiting the park should take note and utilize these services.

### 万寿山邮局兼办电信业务

1950-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　万寿山邮局兼办电信业务
    万寿山邮局，自本月十四日起兼办电信业务，零售市内及长途电话，并收拍国内寻常及加急电报，各界及游园市民注意利用。
