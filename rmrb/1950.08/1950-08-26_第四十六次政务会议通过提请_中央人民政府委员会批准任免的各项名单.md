Title: The 46th Government Affairs Council Approves the List of Appointments and Removals Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval
Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd—The 46th meeting of the Government Administration Council passed the lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval:
   I. Supplementary List of Staff for the Northwest Military and Political Committee
    Director of the Financial and Economic Committee: Peng Dehuai (concurrently)
    (Relieve Jia Tuofu of his current position as Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee.)
    Deputy Director: Jia Tuofu (concurrently)
    (Relieve Zhang Zongxun of his current position as Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee.)
   II. List of President and Vice President of Northern Jiaotong University
    President: Mao Yisheng, formerly President of Tangshan Engineering College of Jiaotong University.
    Vice President: Jin Shixuan, formerly a Counselor in the Ministry of Communications of the Kuomintang government.
