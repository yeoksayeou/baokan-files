　　Celebrating "August 1st"
    Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
    A rally of 40,000 people from all walks of life in the capital celebrating the "August 1st" Army Day of the People's Liberation Army and opposing American aggression against Taiwan and Korea was held on August 1st at the Taihe Gate Square in the Forbidden City.

    Participating in this rally were the People's Liberation Army's naval, land, and air forces and public security forces, as well as representatives of workers, students, and staff members of various organs in the capital.

    When Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army delivered a speech to the assembly, the entire audience cheered for a long time. Participating in the review parade were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various ministries, commissions, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government. Also present were leaders of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and organizations in Beijing. In addition, there were members of the Hong Kong and Kowloon industrial circles' Northeast visiting group, Australian trade union representatives, Indonesian trade union representatives, and a youth delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The parade, led by the naval, land, and air forces of the People's Liberation Army, proceeded through the Meridian Gate, Tiananmen Square, and the Gate of China towards Dongjiaomin Lane, Xijiaomin Lane, Dongsi Archway, and Xisi Archway. Broadcast vehicles broadcast the speeches from the rally to the citizens along the streets. Wherever the parade passed, citizens cheered and paid their respects. (Attached photos)

### 庆祝“八一”  反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜（图片）

1950-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　庆祝“八一”
    反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜
    首都各界四万人庆祝人民解放军“八一”建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜的示威大会，于八月一日在故宫太和门广场举行。
    参加这一示威大会的有人民解放军海陆空军部队和公安部队，以及首都的工人，学生，各机关工作人员代表。
    中国人民解放军朱德总司令向大会发表演说时，全场欢呼，经久不息。参加检阅游行队伍的有中国人民解放军各高级将领，中央人民政府各院、部、会、署、行首长。各民主党派，各人民团体，以及北京各机关团体的负责人。还有港九工业界东北参观团团员和澳洲工会代表，印尼工会代表和越南民主共和国的青年代表团。
    游行队伍以人民解放军海陆空军为前导，经午门、天安门、中华门向东交民巷、西交民巷、东四牌楼、西四牌楼行进。广播车则在街道旁边向市民群众广播着大会的演说。游行队伍行经之处，市民均夹道欢呼致敬。（附图片）
