Title: Our Ambassadors to Hungary and Sweden, Huang Zhen and Geng Biao, Arrive in Moscow Together
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th—Huang Zhen, our Ambassador to Hungary, and Geng Biao, our Ambassador to Sweden, arrived in Moscow together on the afternoon of the 10th. They will soon proceed to Budapest, the capital of Hungary, and Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, respectively, to assume their posts.

### 我驻匈及驻瑞典大使黄镇耿飚联袂抵莫斯科

1950-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻匈及驻瑞典大使黄镇耿飚联袂抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科十一日电】我国驻匈牙利大使黄镇及我国驻瑞典大使耿飚，于十日下午联袂抵达莫斯科，即将分别转赴匈牙利首都布达佩斯与瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩莅任。
