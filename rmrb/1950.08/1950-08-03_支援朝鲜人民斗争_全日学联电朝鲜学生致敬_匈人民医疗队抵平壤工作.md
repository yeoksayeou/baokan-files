Title: Supporting the Korean People's Struggle: All-Japan Student Federation Sends Greetings to Korean Students; Hungarian People's Medical Team Arrives in Pyongyang to Work
Author:
Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 4

　　Supporting the Korean People's Struggle
    All-Japan Student Federation Sends Greetings to Korean Students
    Hungarian People's Medical Team Arrives in Pyongyang to Work
    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] Tass reported from Tokyo on the 1st: The Central Committee of the All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations sent a telegram to the students of Kim Il-sung University in Pyongyang to express their respects. The telegram reads: Under the banner of the International Student Union, 250,000 students belonging to the All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations express their sincere admiration for the heroic struggle of the Korean people, and extend their heartfelt congratulations. The telegram concludes: The decisive struggle waged by the Korean people, especially Korean students, for the independence and unification of Korea, has set an example for Japanese students.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd] The Hungarian People's Medical Team, voluntarily organized by Hungarian medical personnel, arrived in Pyongyang on July 29th. The team will treat wounded Korean People's Army soldiers and Korean people injured in the barbaric bombing by American planes in Korea.

### 支援朝鲜人民斗争  全日学联电朝鲜学生致敬  匈人民医疗队抵平壤工作

1950-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　支援朝鲜人民斗争
    全日学联电朝鲜学生致敬
    匈人民医疗队抵平壤工作
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社一日电引东京消息：全日学生自治会联合会中央委员会通电平壤金日成大学学生致敬。电文写道：在国际学生联合会的旗帜下，二十五万名属于全日学生自治会联合会的学生，对朝鲜人民的英勇斗争表示真挚的钦佩。并向他们作衷心的祝贺。电文最后说：朝鲜人民，特别是朝鲜学生，为争取朝鲜的独立和统一而作的决定性斗争，为日本学生树立了榜样。
    【新华社平壤二日电】匈牙利医务人员自动组织的匈牙利人民医疗队，已于七月二十九日抵平壤。该队将在朝鲜医治光荣负伤的朝鲜人民军战士及在美机野蛮轰炸下受伤的朝鲜人民。
