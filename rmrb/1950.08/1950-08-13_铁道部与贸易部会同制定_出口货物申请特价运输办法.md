Title: The Ministry of Railways and the Ministry of Trade Jointly Formulate Measures for Applying for Special Freight Rates for Export Goods
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 2
　　
　　The Ministry of Railways and the Ministry of Trade Jointly Formulate
    Measures for Applying for Special Freight Rates for Export Goods
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. In order to reduce the cost of export goods and encourage exports, the Ministry of Railways and the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government have jointly formulated provisional measures for public and private export goods to apply for special freight rates, which will be implemented from July 1st. According to the measures announced by the Ministry of Railways, regarding the types of goods eligible for special freight rates and the preferential standards, from July 1st of this year to the end of June next year, the full-car freight for tea, tea bricks, raw silk (including spun silk, waste silk, twisted silk, dried warp, and native silk), almonds, raw hides, and egg products will be reduced by 50% of the current freight rates. The full-car freight for wool, camel hair, tung oil, vegetable oil, tea oil, peanut oil, cottonseed oil, medicinal materials, casings, walnuts, fresh eggs, antimony, and cement will be reduced by 30% of the current freight rates. In addition, the freight for special handicrafts transported from Beijing to Tianjin for export, whether in full carload or less-than-carload shipments, will continue to be reduced by 40% of the freight rates as stipulated last October.

### 铁道部与贸易部会同制定  出口货物申请特价运输办法

1950-08-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　铁道部与贸易部会同制定
    出口货物申请特价运输办法
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府铁道部及贸易部为减低出口货物成本，以鼓励出口起见，特会同制定公私营出口货物申请特价运输暂行办法，于七月一日起开始实行。特价运输货品种类及优待标准，根据铁道部公布的办法，自今年七月一日至明年六月底止，茶叶、茶砖、生丝（包括绢丝、下脚丝、捻线丝、干经、土丝）、杏仁、元皮、蛋制品的整车运费，均按现行运价减低百分之五十。羊毛、驼毛、桐油、青油、茶油、花生油、棉子油、药材、肠衣、核桃仁、鲜蛋、锑、洋灰等整车运费，均按现行运价减低百分之三十。此外，由北京运至天津出口的特种手工艺品，不论整车或零担运费，仍照去年十月规定减低百分之四十的运费。
