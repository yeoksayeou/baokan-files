　　Brief News

　　East China Revolutionary University Political Research Institute and others Commence Classes
　　The Political Research Institute of East China People's Revolutionary University (students are from various democratic parties and university professors) and the first, second, and fifth departments of the university (students are young intellectuals, mainly training land reform cadres) solemnly held their opening ceremony in Suzhou on the 20th of this month. Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, and Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairman, both attended and spoke. Responsible persons from relevant departments and democratic figures from various parties attended to offer congratulations.

　　Xiamen Overseas Chinese Establish Federation
　　The Xiamen Overseas Chinese Federation was officially established on August 4th, with six branches under its jurisdiction: Malaya, the Philippines, Indonesia, Burma, Vietnam, and Siam. The federation's current main task is to help returned overseas Chinese and their families solve various difficulties, and to introduce the situation of the motherland to overseas Chinese. The preparatory committee of the Xiamen Overseas Chinese Federation was established with the help of the Xiamen Municipal People's Government after the liberation of Xiamen in October last year. In the past ten months, it has actively served overseas Chinese and helped many returned overseas Chinese solve various difficult problems.

### 简讯

专栏：简讯

　　简讯
    华东革大政治研究院等开学
    华东人民革命大学政治研究院（学员为各民主党派人士及大学教授）及该校第一、二、五各部（学员为知识青年，以训练土地改革干部为主）已于本月二十日在苏州隆重举行开学典礼。华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、副主席马寅初均到会讲话。有关部门负责人及各党派民主人士等均到会致贺。
    厦门市归国华侨成立联合会
    厦门市归国华侨联合会已于八月四日正式成立，下辖马来亚、菲律宾、印尼、缅甸、越南、暹罗等六个分会。该会目前的主要任务是帮助归国华侨及华侨眷属解决各种困难，并向海外侨胞介绍祖国情况。厦门市归国华侨联合会筹备会在去年十月厦门解放后，即在该市人民政府帮助下成立，近十个月来曾积极为侨胞服务，帮助许多归国侨胞解决了各种困难问题。
