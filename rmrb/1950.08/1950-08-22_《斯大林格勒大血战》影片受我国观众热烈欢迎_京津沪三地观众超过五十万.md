Title: "The Great Battle of Stalingrad" Film Warmly Welcomed by Chinese Audiences; Over 500,000 Viewers in Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai
Author: Wang Tianyi
Date: 1950-08-22
Page: 3

　　"The Great Battle of Stalingrad" Film Warmly Welcomed by Chinese Audiences
    Over 500,000 Viewers in Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai
    [Our Newspaper] The famous Soviet war history film "The Great Battle of Stalingrad" has been warmly welcomed by audiences in Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai since its release. As of the 10th, the audience in the three cities has exceeded 500,000, setting the highest box office record for any film since liberation. The film was produced by a Soviet film studio and directed by the renowned director Petrov, based on the "Soviet Chronicle." With a spirit of seeking truth from facts, the film profoundly depicts the actual situation of the Battle of Stalingrad in World War II, demonstrates the Soviet people's love for their motherland and their heroic spirit in defending their country amidst hardship, and even more so, demonstrates the wise leadership of Marshal Stalin. It is an immortal record in modern history. The film has won the "Marshal Stalin" Crystal Globe Award. The Northeast Film Studio of the Central Film Bureau of our country has translated the film into Mandarin dialogue.
      (Wang Tianyi)

### 《斯大林格勒大血战》影片受我国观众热烈欢迎  京津沪三地观众超过五十万
汪天一
1950-08-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　《斯大林格勒大血战》影片受我国观众热烈欢迎
    京津沪三地观众超过五十万
    【本报讯】苏联著名战争历史影片《斯大林格勒大血战》，在北京、天津、上海三市放映以来，获得广大观众的热烈欢迎。截至十日止，三地观众已超过五十万，创解放以后各影片售票的最高纪录。按该片是苏联制片厂出品，系根据《苏联年史》，由名导演彼得洛夫导演。片中以实事求是的精神，深刻地描写了第二次世界大战中斯大林格勒战役的实况，表现了苏联人民热爱祖国，在艰苦中英勇保卫祖国的精神，更表现了斯大林大元帅的英明领导。是近代史里一页不朽的纪录。该片曾荣获“斯大林大元帅”水晶地球仪奖。我国中央电影局东北制片厂将该片翻译成了华语对白。
      （汪天一）
