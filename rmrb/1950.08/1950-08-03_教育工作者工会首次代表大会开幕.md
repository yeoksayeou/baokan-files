Title: First Congress of the Educational Workers Union Opens

Author:

Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The first National Congress of the Chinese Educational Workers Union, marking the great unity of 700,000 educational workers nationwide, opened in Beijing on the 2nd. More than 300 representatives from North China, Northeast China, East China, Central South China, Southwest China, and Northwest China attended the meeting. Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the Presidium of the Congress, delivered the opening speech. Then, Guo Moruo, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Affairs Council, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, Vice Minister Wei Que, and Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students Federation, delivered speeches. From the 3rd, the congress will respectively report and discuss the Educational Workers Union's guiding principles, tasks, organizational charter, membership benefits, and study issues. It will also elect the leading body of the Chinese Educational Workers Union - the National Committee.

### 教育工作者工会首次代表大会开幕

1950-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　教育工作者工会首次代表大会开幕
    【新华社二日讯】标志着全国七十万教育工作者大团结的中国教育工作者工会第一次全国代表大会于二日在北京开幕。到会的有华北、东北、华东、中南、西南、西北各地区代表三百余人。大会首先由大会主席团吴玉章主席致开幕词，接着由中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会主任郭沫若、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、中央人民政府教育部部长马叙伦、副部长韦悫、中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定等讲话。大会从三日起将就教育工作者工会的方针任务、组织章程、会员福利与学习等问题分别进行报告与讨论。并选举中国教育工作者工会的领导机构——全国委员会。
