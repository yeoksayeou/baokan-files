Title: Chinese and Vietnamese Student Delegations Arrive in Moscow; Our Ambassador to Romania, General Wang Youping, Also Arrives in Soviet Capital

Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th: The Chinese student delegation and the Vietnamese student delegation, on their way to Prague, Czechoslovakia, to attend the Second World Student Congress, arrived in Moscow on the 3rd. Representatives of young workers and students from universities, middle and primary schools in Moscow went to the station to welcome them. Representatives of the Young Pioneers presented flowers to the delegations.

The Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, Pishlyak, delivered a welcoming speech, praising the heroic struggle and diligent study of the Chinese and Vietnamese students. Yang Cheng, the head of the Chinese delegation, said in his reply: Chinese students will learn from the glorious example of Soviet youth and the Soviet people in building a new society. Huang Mingzheng, the head of the Vietnamese delegation, said that the Vietnamese people and Vietnamese students will fight to the end for the liberation of Vietnam. Finally, everyone sang the "Democratic Youth March" together, and the station was filled with an atmosphere of international youth friendship and unity.

The Chinese and Vietnamese student delegations will tour Moscow for several days.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, General Wang Youping, arrived in Moscow on the 3rd and will proceed to Bucharest, the capital of Romania, on the 6th to assume his post.

### 我国与越南学生代表团抵莫斯科  我驻罗大使王幼平将军亦抵苏京

1950-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国与越南学生代表团抵莫斯科
    我驻罗大使王幼平将军亦抵苏京
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】赴捷京布拉格出席世界学生第二届代表大会的中国学生代表团与越南学生代表团，已于三日抵达莫斯科。莫斯科青年工人与大中小学学生的代表均往车站欢迎，并由少年先锋队代表向代表团献花。
    苏联青年反法西斯委员会副主席彼斯利亚克致欢迎辞，赞誉英勇斗争与刻苦学习的中国学生与越南学生。中国代表团团长杨诚致答词称：中国学生将学习苏联青年与苏联人民建设新社会的光辉榜样。越南代表团团长黄明正致词，表示越南人民与越南学生将为解放越南而战斗到底。最后全体合唱民主青年进行曲，车站上充盈着国际青年友好团结的气氛。
    中国与越南学生代表团将在莫斯科观光数日。
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国首任特命全权大使王幼平将军，于三日抵达莫斯科，将于六日转赴罗京布加勒斯特莅任。
