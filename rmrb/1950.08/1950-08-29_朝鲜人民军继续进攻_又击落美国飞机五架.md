Title: Korean People's Army Continues Offensive; Shoots Down Five More American Planes
Author:
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 28th. The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report this morning, the 27th, that: The People's Army units are continuing to engage in fierce battles with enemy forces on all fronts. American troops and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army are attempting to halt the advance of the People's Army units, launching repeated counter-offensives on all fronts. The People's Army units are continuing their offensive, repelling the counter-attacking enemy forces, and inflicting losses on the enemy's manpower and equipment.

On the southern coastal front, the People's Army units, having repelled the American counter-offensive, are conducting a fierce offensive campaign.

The People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units inflicted significant losses on the American air force, which has been raiding daily. On the 24th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down two American fighter planes in the Nampo area.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 28th. The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 27th that: The People's Army units are continuing to engage in fierce battles with enemy forces on all fronts. The People's Army units repelled the counter-offensives of American troops and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

On the 26th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down two American fighter planes raiding Hamhung and one American fighter plane raiding the Kimchon area.

### 朝鲜人民军继续进攻  又击落美国飞机五架

1950-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军继续进攻
    又击落美国飞机五架
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十七日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续与敌军进行激战。美军部队与李承晚残余伪军企图阻止人民军部队的进攻，在各战线一再发动反攻。人民军部队继续进攻，击退反攻敌军，并使敌人有生力量与装备受到损失。
    在南海岸战线上，击退美军反攻的人民军部队进行着猛烈的进攻战。
    人民军高射炮部队予连日侵袭的美国空军以重大损失。二十四日，人民军高射炮部队在南浦区击落美战斗机二架。
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十七日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与敌军继续展开猛烈的战斗。人民军部队击退美军部队和李承晚残余伪军的反攻，予敌人以重大损失。
    二十六日，人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭咸兴的美军战斗机二架及侵袭金川地区的美军战斗机一架。
