Title: "Korean People's Declaration" Signature Campaign: All Korean People Respond Enthusiastically; Workers and Peasants Express Determination to Increase Production to Support the Front

Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 23rd: The Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front issued a letter to the entire nation on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation Day, calling on the people of the whole country to sign the "Korean People's Declaration," demanding that the United Nations stop the armed intervention of American imperialism and publicly try the national traitors. This call has been enthusiastically responded to by the people in the new and old liberated areas of Korea. As of the 18th, the total number of people in various parts of the country who have signed the "Korean People's Declaration" has exceeded one million.

The working people in various parts of Korea have been holding mass rallies for days to enthusiastically launch the signature campaign. At mass rallies in cities and villages that have been subjected to indiscriminate bombing by American aircraft, people from all walks of life unanimously shouted the slogan "The United Nations must immediately stop the American intervention in Korea." Women, youth, and religious figures also unanimously stated at mass rallies that they would hand over the national traitors, such as the head of the traitorous clique, Syngman Rhee, to the people for public trial.

Workers in factories, mines, and enterprises have signed the "Korean People's Declaration" one after another, resolutely stating that they will increase production to support the front.

Farmers have expressed their determination to harvest crops early and pay the tax in kind in advance to support the front.

### “朝鲜人民声明书”签名运动  全朝鲜人民热烈响应  工人农民表示决增产支前

1950-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　“朝鲜人民声明书”签名运动
    全朝鲜人民热烈响应
    工人农民表示决增产支前
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会于“八一五》解放五周年纪念日所发表的告全国人民书中，号召全国人民在《朝鲜人民声明书”上签名，要求联合国制止美帝国主义的武装干涉，并公审民族叛徒。这一号召得到朝鲜新老解放区人民的热烈响应。截至十八日止，全国各地人民在“朝鲜人民声明书”上签名的总人数已在一百万人以上。
    朝鲜各地的劳动人民连日召开群众大会，热烈展开签名运动。在遭受美机滥肆轰炸的城市和农村的群众大会上，各阶层人民一致高呼“联合国要立即制止美国干涉朝鲜”的口号。妇女、青年、宗教人士也在群众大会上一致表示要将卖国头子李承晚等民族叛徒交由人民公审。
    各工厂、矿山、企业的工人们纷纷在“朝鲜人民声明书”上签名，坚决表示以增加生产来支援前线。
    农民表示要早日收割庄稼，提前缴纳现物税去支援前线。
