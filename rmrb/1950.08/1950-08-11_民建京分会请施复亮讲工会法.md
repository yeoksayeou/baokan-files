Title: Minjian Beijing Branch Invites Shi Fuliang to Lecture on the Trade Union Law
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 6

　　Minjian Beijing Branch
　　Invites Shi Fuliang to Lecture on the Trade Union Law
　　Because the broad masses of industry and commerce requested a deeper understanding of the newly promulgated Trade Union Law of the People's Republic of China, the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association has specially invited Shi Fuliang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, to give a special lecture at the Xiehe Auditorium at 7:30 p.m. on the 12th. People from all walks of life who wish to attend can be introduced by their organizations, groups, or enterprise administrative departments, as well as by members and friends of the association, and go to the association to collect admission tickets in advance.

### 民建京分会请施复亮讲工会法

1950-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　民建京分会
    请施复亮讲工会法
    民主建国会北京市分会因广大工商界要求深入了解新颁布的中华人民共和国工会法，特请中央人民政府劳动部副部长施复亮于十二日下午七时半到协和礼堂做专题讲解。各界人士如愿往听讲。可由机关团体或企业行政部门以及会员会友介绍，先到该会洽领入场券。
