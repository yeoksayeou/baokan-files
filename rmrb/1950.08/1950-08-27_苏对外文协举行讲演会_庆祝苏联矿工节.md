Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Holds Lecture to Celebrate Soviet Miners' Day
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 6

    Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Holds Lecture
    to Celebrate Soviet Miners' Day
    Today (the 27th) is Soviet Miners' Day. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association will hold a lecture at 7:30 PM today at 19 Nanheyan to celebrate this glorious holiday. Chekanov will speak on "The Stakhanovite Movement in the Soviet Union." Anyone holding an admission ticket or a "Soviet Introduction" long-term lecture pass may attend.
    If any branch of the Friendship Association wishes to attend the lecture, they may obtain admission tickets from the association at 2 Yuheqiao with a letter of introduction from the branch.
    (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协举行讲演会  庆祝苏联矿工节
陈应楠
1950-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    苏对外文协举行讲演会
    庆祝苏联矿工节 
    今日（二十七日）是苏联矿工节，北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为庆祝这一个光辉的节日，定于今日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请切卡诺夫讲“斯达汉诺夫运动在苏联”。凡持有人场券或“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各支会如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信往御河桥二号该会索取人场券。（陈应楠）
