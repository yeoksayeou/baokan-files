Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth to Send Delegation to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The World Federation of Democratic Youth, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, will soon send a delegation to visit our country. In addition to representatives from the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, the delegation will be composed of representatives from more than 30 member countries, including the socialist country of the Soviet Union, various People's Democratic countries, as well as representatives from capitalist countries and oppressed national countries. The delegation will arrive in the capital of China in early September and will stay in China for about a month.

### 世界民主青年联盟将派代表团来我国访问

1950-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界民主青年联盟将派代表团来我国访问
    【新华社十八日讯】世界民主青年联盟应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合总会的邀请，即将派遣代表团来我国访问。代表团的组成，除世界民主青年联盟和国际学生联合会的代表外，并有三十余个会员国的代表参加，内中包括社会主义国家苏联、各人民民主国家以及资本主义国家和被压迫民族国家的代表。代表团将在九月初旬抵达中国首都，在中国约有一个月的逗留。
