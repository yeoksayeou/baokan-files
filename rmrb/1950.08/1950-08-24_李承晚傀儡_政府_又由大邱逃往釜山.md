Title: Syngman Rhee Puppet "Government" Flees from Daegu to Busan Again
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 4

　　Syngman Rhee Puppet "Government" Flees from Daegu to Busan Again

　　[Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd] According to the Kuomintang Central News Agency: The South Korean Syngman Rhee puppet "government" fled from Daegu to Busan on the 19th and announced Busan as its "temporary capital." This is the third time the Syngman Rhee "government" has fled; the previous two times were to Daejeon and Daegu. The so-called "embassies" of the United States and Chiang Kai-shek's remnant bandits stationed in South Korea have also fled to Busan with the Syngman Rhee "government."

### 李承晚傀儡“政府”又由大邱逃往釜山

1950-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　李承晚傀儡“政府”又由大邱逃往釜山
    【新华社二十三日讯】据国民党中央社消息：南朝鲜李承晚傀儡“政府”已于十九日自大邱逃抵釜山，并宣布釜山为其“临时首府”。这是李承晚“政府”的第三次逃亡，前两次是逃到大田和大邱。美国及蒋介石残匪驻南朝鲜的所谓“大使馆”，已随李承晚“政府”逃抵釜山。
