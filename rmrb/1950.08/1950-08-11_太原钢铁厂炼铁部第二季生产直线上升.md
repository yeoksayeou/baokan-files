Title: Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant Iron Smelting Department's Production Rises Sharply in the Second Quarter

Author:

Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 2

Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant Iron Smelting Department's Production Rises Sharply in the Second Quarter

[This newspaper Taiyuan News] Since April, the Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant's Iron Smelting Department has seen a straight upward trend in production quantity and quality, exceeding the second quarter's production targets. In April, the department exceeded the target by 19.48% in quantity and 54.38% in quality; in May, the quantity exceeded the target by 20.15% and the quality exceeded the target by 81.95%; in June, the quantity exceeded the target by 37.93% and the quality exceeded the target by 110.07%. On June 14th, the department also created a new record of daily output exceeding equipment capacity by 24%, an unprecedented achievement since the iron furnace was put into operation. This is a major victory on the production front.
        (Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant Iron and Steel Small Newspaper Correspondent Group)

### 太原钢铁厂炼铁部第二季生产直线上升

1950-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　太原钢铁厂炼铁部第二季生产直线上升
    【本报太原讯】太原钢铁厂炼铁部自四月份以来，生产数量质量呈直线上升，超额完成第二季生产任务。该部四月份在数量上超过任务百分之十九点四八，质量超过任务百分之五十四点三八；五月份数量超过任务百分之二十点一五，质量超过任务百分之八十一点九五；六月份数量超过任务百分之三十七点九三，质量超过任务百分之一百一十点○七。六月十四日，该部又创造了超过设备能力百分之二十四的日产量，为炼铁炉开炉以来空前未有的新纪录。这是生产战线上的一大胜利。
        （太原钢铁厂钢铁小报通讯组）
