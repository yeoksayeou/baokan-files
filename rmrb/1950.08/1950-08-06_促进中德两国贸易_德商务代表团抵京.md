Title: Promoting Trade Between China and Germany: German Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 1

　　Promoting Trade Between China and Germany
    German Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 5th] To promote trade between China and the German Democratic Republic, the German Democratic Republic, with the consent of our Central People's Government, has dispatched a business delegation led by Mr. Ziller to China for trade negotiations. The delegation arrived in Beijing by plane at 11 o'clock this morning. Those who went to welcome them included Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Lin Haiyun, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade, Siradzki, Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China, Grutner, Counselor of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic in China, Dulber, Commercial Counselor, Nikolai, Deputy Commercial Representative of the Soviet Embassy in China, Gusto, Commercial Commissioner of the Polish Embassy, László, Commercial Commissioner of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and Madar, Acting Commercial Commissioner of the Czechoslovak Embassy.

### 促进中德两国贸易  德商务代表团抵京

1950-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　促进中德两国贸易
    德商务代表团抵京
    【新华社五日讯】为了促进中德两国贸易，德意志民主共和国于取得我中央人民政府同意后，已派遣由齐勒先生率领之商务代表团来华商谈。该团已于今晨十一时乘机抵京。前往欢迎者有我外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、交际处处长王倬如、贸易部国外贸易司司长林海云，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆参赞席拉德，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团参赞葛鲁特纳，商务参赞杜尔伯，苏联驻华大使馆商务副代表倪古莱夫，波兰大使馆商务专员顾士多，匈牙利驻华大使馆商务专员拉兹罗，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆代理商务专员马达尔。
