Title: South Korean Science, Culture and Arts Circles Convene Anti-American Imperialism Aggression Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th. Over five hundred scientists, cultural figures, and artists from the southern half of Korea convened an uprising conference in Seoul on the 3rd to oppose American imperialism's armed aggression against Korea. In their speeches, South Korean scientists and artists unanimously expressed their determination to bravely participate in the battle and strive for the reunification and independence of the motherland, as well as the free development of national culture, science, and art. The conference resolved to support the ever-victorious People's Army with all its strength to secure victory in the war. The conference sent telegrams of respect to Marshal Stalin, General Kim Il-sung, and the World Peace Congress.

### 南朝鲜科学文化艺术界召开反对美帝侵略大会

1950-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜科学文化艺术界召开反对美帝侵略大会
    【新华社平壤十一日电】朝鲜南半部的科学家和文化艺术界五百余人，三日在汉城召开反对美帝国主义武装侵略朝鲜的奋起大会。南朝鲜科学家与艺术家们在发言中，一致表示要勇敢地参加战斗，为祖国的统一，独立与民族文化、科学、艺术的自由发展而斗争。大会决议以一切力量支援百战百胜的人民军，以争取战争的胜利。大会通电斯大林大元帅，金日成将军以及世界拥护和平大会致敬。
