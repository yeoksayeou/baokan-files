Title: Korean People's Army Shatters Enemy Counterattacks, Inflicts Heavy Losses on Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1950-08-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 27th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of the 26th that the People's Army troops on all fronts had shattered the counterattacks of the U.S. military and the remnants of the Li Chengwan (Syngman Rhee) puppet army, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy's manpower and equipment.

　　The People's Army troops advancing on the southern coast are heavily striking U.S. infantry and naval forces, continuing fierce fighting.

　　People's guerrillas, responding to the People's Army's offensive, have launched active operations around Ulsan, Dongnae, and Busan, inflicting significant losses on the enemy.

　　The People's Army Air Force units attacked enemy assembly points, enemy positions, military facilities, and transportation routes, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 27th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the evening of the 26th that the People's Army troops continued to engage in fierce fighting with enemy troops attempting to counterattack on all fronts.

　　The People's Army troops that liberated Uiseong, Gunwi, and Dabudong continued their offensive. The U.S. military and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army on this front continued to mount stubborn counterattacks to stop the People's Army's offensive. However, the People's Army troops repelled the enemy's counterattacks, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

　　On August 26th, U.S. bombers and fighter planes invaded the Pyongyang, Munpyeong, Wonsan, Suan, and Nampo areas several times, indiscriminately carrying out barbarous bombing, causing many casualties among peaceful residents.

### 朝人民军粉碎敌反击  予敌军以巨大的损失

1950-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军粉碎敌反击  予敌军以巨大的损失
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十六日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上粉碎了美军部队和李承晚残余伪军的反击，予敌有生力量和装备以巨大的损失。
    在南海岸方面进攻中的人民军部队，沉重地打击美军步兵和海军部队，继续激烈的战斗。
    人民游击队响应人民军的进攻，在蔚山、东莱、釜山周围，展开积极的活动，给予敌人以重大的损失。
    人民军空军部队攻击敌人的结集点及敌军阵地、军用设施、运输道路，予敌以重大的损失。
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十六日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与试图反击的敌军，继续激战。
    解放义城、军威、多富洞的人民军部队，继续进攻。该阵线的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，为了阻止人民军的进攻，继续顽强的反击。但人民军部队击退了敌人的反击，予敌人以巨大的损失。
    八月二十六日，美军轰炸机和战斗机侵袭平壤、文坪、元山、遂安、南浦地区数次，盲目地进行野蛮的轰炸，使和平居民伤亡甚多。
