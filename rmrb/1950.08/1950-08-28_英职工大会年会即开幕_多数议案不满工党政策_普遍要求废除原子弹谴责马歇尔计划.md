Title: British Trades Union Congress Annual Meeting to Open; Majority of Motions Dissatisfied with Labour Party Policies; Universal Demand to Abolish the Atomic Bomb and Condemn the Marshall Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 26th (TASS dispatch): The General Council of the British Trades Union Congress announced the agenda for the 82nd annual meeting of the Trades Union Congress on August 22nd. This year's annual meeting will open in Brighton on September 4th.

　　The agenda includes 76 motions, reflecting the anxiety of British trade union members about the current policies of the Labour government.

　　Of the ten motions concerning international issues, six call for the abolition of the atomic bomb. A motion from the Association of Scientific Workers calls for the control of atomic energy and the prohibition of atomic weapons.

　　Organizations such as the National Union of Metal Mechanics, the National Union of Vehicle Builders, and the Tobacco Workers' Union have all submitted motions calling for the prohibition of the atomic bomb.

　　The motion on international trade submitted by the National Union of Foundry Workers points out: "Establishing unconstrained trade with other countries, including China, the Soviet Union, and Eastern European democratic countries, is the most reliable guarantee of peace." The union condemns "any restrictions on such trade relations imposed by the Marshall Aid program, and any external interference in British affairs."

　　Of the ten motions concerning profits, prices, and wages, four call for the abolition of the wage freeze policy and express concern about rising prices and employer profits.

### 英职工大会年会即开幕  多数议案不满工党政策  普遍要求废除原子弹谴责马歇尔计划

1950-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　英职工大会年会即开幕
    多数议案不满工党政策
    普遍要求废除原子弹谴责马歇尔计划
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英国职工大会最高委员会于八月二十二日公布职工大会第八十二届年会的议程，本届年会将于九月四日在布来屯开幕。
    议程中包括七十六项议案，这些议案反映出英国工会会员对工党政府现行政策的焦虑。
    关于国际问题的十项议案中，有六项要求废除原子弹。科学工作者协会的一项议案，要求管制原子能和禁止原子武器。
    全国五金技工协会、全国造车工人联合会及烟草工人工会等团体，都提出要求禁止原子弹的议案。
    全国铸造工人混合工会所提出的关于国际贸易的议案指出：“与别的国家，包括中国、苏联和东欧民主国家在内建立毫无约束的贸易，乃是和平的最可靠的保证。”该工会谴责“马歇尔援助所强迫施行的对这种贸易关系的任何限制以及任何外来的势力对英国事务的干涉”。
    关于利润、物价与工资问题的十项议案中，有四项要求废止工资冻结的政策，并对物价及雇主利润的提高表示忧虑。
