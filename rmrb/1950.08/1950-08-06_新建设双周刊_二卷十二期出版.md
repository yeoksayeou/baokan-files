Date: 1950-08-06

New Construction Bi-weekly
Volume 2, Issue 12 Published
　　
The latest issue of "New Construction" Bi-weekly, Volume 2, Issue 12, has been published. The main articles in this issue include: "On the Illegality of U.S. Imperialist Interference in China and Korea" (Li Haopei), "Looking at the So-called Third World War from the International Situation" (Meng Xianzhang), "Characteristics of the People's Liberation Army" (Wu Qi), "The People's Liberation Army on the Production Front" (Shu Sheng), "The Question of Adjusting Industry and Commerce" (Zhang Naiqi), "The Individual Economy of Peasants" (Economics Department of Peking University), "Land Reform and the Development of Agricultural Production" (Shi Chu), "The Transformation of the Banking Industry in Tianjin" (Zhang Gaofeng), "China's Liquid Fuel Problem" (Zeng Zhaolun), and several communications and reports.

### 新建设双周刊  二卷十二期出版

1950-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　新建设双周刊
    二卷十二期出版
    《新建设》双周刊，二卷十二期已出版，这一期的要目计有：“论美帝干涉中国及朝鲜的非法”（李浩培），“从国际形势看所谓第三次世界大战”（孟宪章），“人民解放军的特点”（吴齐），生产战线上的人民解放军（蜀生），调整工商业问题（章乃器），农民个体经济（北大经济系系会），土地改革与发展农业生产（石础），转变过程中的天津银钱业（张高峰）中国的液体燃料问题（曾昭抡）及通讯报导等多篇。
