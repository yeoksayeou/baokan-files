Title: Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Holds Reception to Welcome Representatives of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
Author: 
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 10th: According to TASS, the representatives of the Chinese Academy of Sciences who participated in the celebration of the 250th anniversary of the German Academy of Sciences, Professors Yun Ziqiang and Wang Ganchang, stopped over in Moscow for sightseeing on their way back to China. The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a reception on the 8th to warmly welcome them. Counselor Ge Baoquan of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union was also invited to attend.

At the meeting, Academician Bardin, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, gave the Chinese scientists a detailed introduction to the organization of scientific research in the Soviet Union, the work of the various institutes and laboratories of the Academy of Sciences, the training of scientific personnel, and the construction of scientific institutions. The Chinese scientists expressed their gratitude to the Soviet scientists for their assistance and support, and expressed the hope to further strengthen the creative ties between the scientists of China and the Soviet Union.

### 苏科学院主席团举行招待会  欢迎我国科学院代表

1950-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏科学院主席团举行招待会
    欢迎我国科学院代表
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：中国科学院参加庆祝德意志科学院二百五十周年的代表团代表恽子强、王淦昌两教授，于返国途中在莫斯科逗留观光。苏联科学院主席团特于八日举行招待会热烈欢迎，中国驻苏大使馆参赞戈宝权亦应邀参加。
    苏联科学院副院长巴尔丁院士在会上向中国科学家详尽介绍了苏联科学研究工作的组织，科学院各研究所和实验室的工作情况，科学干部的训练，以及科学机关的建设等。中国科学家致谢苏联科学家给予他们的援助与支持，并希望更进一步加强中苏两国科学家之间的创造性联系。
