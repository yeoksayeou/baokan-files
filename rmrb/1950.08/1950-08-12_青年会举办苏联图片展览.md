Title: YMCA Holds Soviet Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 6

　　YMCA Holds Soviet Photo Exhibition
    The YMCA on Mishi Street in this city will hold a Soviet Socialist Construction Photo Exhibition at the association from 9:00 am to 7:00 pm daily from August 11th to 15th. The content includes: 1. May Day in the Soviet Union, 2. Soviet General Elections, 3. Soviet Culture and Education, 4. The Estonian Republic, 5. Maternal and Child Health Care, 6. New Soviet Shops. All sectors are welcome to visit.

### 青年会举办苏联图片展览

1950-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　青年会举办苏联图片展览
    本市米市大街青年会定八月十一日至十五日每日上午九时至下午七时在该会举行苏联社会主义建设图片展览。内容计有：一、五一在苏联，二、苏联大选，三、苏联文化教育，四、爱沙尼亚共和国，五、妇婴保健，六、苏维埃的新商店。欢迎各界前往参观。
