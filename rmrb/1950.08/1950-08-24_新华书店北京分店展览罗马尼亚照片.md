Title: Xinhua Bookstore Beijing Branch Exhibits Romanian Photos
Author: Xinhua Bookstore Correspondence Group
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 6

　　The Xinhua Bookstore Beijing Branch Exhibits Romanian Photos
    To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic, the Xinhua Bookstore Beijing Branch is exhibiting Romanian photos in its storefront windows from the 23rd. The photos include content on industry, agriculture, children's education, social welfare, and the peace movement, mainly introducing Romania's construction experience. There are more than sixty photos in total, with detailed explanations.
   (Xinhua Bookstore Correspondence Group)

### 新华书店北京分店展览罗马尼亚照片
新华书店通讯组
1950-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　新华书店北京分店展览罗马尼亚照片
    新华书店北京分店为庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国建国六周年。于二十三日起，在门市橱窗展览罗马尼亚的照片，包括工业、农业、儿童教育、社会福利及和平运动等内容，主要在介绍罗马尼亚的建设经验。共计六十余幅，并有详细说明。
   （新华书店通讯组）
