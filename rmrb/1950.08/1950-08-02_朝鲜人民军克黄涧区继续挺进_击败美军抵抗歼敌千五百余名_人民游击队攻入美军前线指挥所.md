Title: Korean People's Army Captures Hwanggan District and Continues Advance; Defeats American Resistance, Eliminates Over 1,500 Enemy Troops; People's Guerrillas Attack American Forward Command Post
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 31st that: People's Army units on all fronts are continuing to engage in fierce battles with the American forces. The People's Army, having liberated the Hwanggan district belonging to Yeongdong County at the southern end of North Chungcheong Province, defeated the resistance of the American troops in the southeast of Hwanggan, inflicting heavy blows on the enemy. In this battle, the People's Army killed or wounded more than 1,500 enemy soldiers, seized three 105mm cannons, five 81mm mortars, more than one hundred 60mm mortars, six 57mm guns, twenty-one vehicles, and a large number of other light machine guns, carbines, rifles, radios, and other spoils of war.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of July 31st that: Frontline People's Army units continued to advance while engaging in fierce battles with the American forces. A certain unit of the People's Army, having liberated Yeongdong, captured the Hwanggan district belonging to Yeongdong County near Chupung Pass in the southern part of North Chungcheong Province on the 29th. In this battle, more than 300 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, and two 105mm cannons, three 60mm guns, fifteen vehicles, and other light and heavy machine guns and other spoils of war were seized.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st: According to Associated Press reporter Mr. Kim, who is embedded with the American troops: Korean People's guerrillas attacked a forward command post of the American First Cavalry Division on July 31st. The guerrillas fired fiercely at a building. Several officers who escaped from the windows were shot by machine guns. According to United Press reporter Mr. Cary Shearer: In this incident, one American major, two American soldiers, and one Korean puppet army lieutenant were killed, and seven other American officers were injured.

### 朝鲜人民军克黄涧区继续挺进  击败美军抵抗歼敌千五百余名  人民游击队攻入美军前线指挥所

1950-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军克黄涧区继续挺进
    击败美军抵抗歼敌千五百余名
    人民游击队攻入美军前线指挥所
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三十一日晚发表战报称：各战线上的人民军部队，继续和美军展开着激烈的战斗。解放忠清北道南端永同县所属黄涧区的人民军，在黄涧的东南部击败美军部队的抵抗，予敌人以沉重打击。人民军在这一战斗中，毙伤敌一千五百余名，缴获一零五公厘炮三门，八一迫击炮五门，六零迫击炮一百余门，五七炮六门，汽车二十一辆及其它轻机枪卡宾枪步枪电台等战利品甚多。
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七月三十一日晨发表战报称：前线人民军各部队在与美军展开激战中继续向前推进。解放永同的人民军某部，二十九日攻克忠清北道南部秋风岭附近永同县所属黄涧区。在这一战斗中，毙、伤敌三百余名，缴获一零五公厘炮两门，六零炮三门，汽车十五辆及其他轻重机枪等战利品甚多。
    【新华社一日讯】据美联社随美军记者金氏报道：朝鲜人民游击队于七月三十一日袭入美国第一骑兵师的一个前线指挥所。游击队向一所建筑猛烈射击。几个从窗子里逃出来的军官遭到机枪的射击。据合众社记者卡里歇尔报道：在这一事件中，美军少校一名、美国士兵二名及朝鲜伪军中尉一名被击毙，另有美军官七人受伤。
