Title: MacArthur Persecutes Japanese Progressive Journalists; International Association of Journalists Protests
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 5th: The Secretariat of the International Association of Journalists has telegraphed MacArthur, strongly protesting his arbitrary order to dismiss a large number of progressive journalists from Japanese newspapers, news agencies, and radio stations. The telegram demands an immediate end to this persecution and calls for the immediate resumption of publication of progressive newspapers.

### 麦克阿瑟迫害日进步记者  国际记者协会提出抗议

1950-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟迫害日进步记者
    国际记者协会提出抗议
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社布拉格三日讯：国际新闻记者协会秘书处顷致电麦克阿瑟，强烈抗议他的解雇大批日本报纸、通讯社及广播电台的进步记者的专横命令。该电要求立即中止这种迫害行动，并要求准许进步报纸立即复刊。
