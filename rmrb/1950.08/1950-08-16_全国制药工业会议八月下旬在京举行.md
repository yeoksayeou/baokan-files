Title: National Pharmaceutical Industry Conference to be Held in Beijing in Late August
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. In order to cooperate with the National Health Conference currently being held and to promote the construction of national health work, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government and the Ministry of Light Industry have decided to convene the National Pharmaceutical Industry Conference in Beijing on August 25. The conference will discuss the future production policy of the pharmaceutical industry, division of labor and manufacturing methods, and other issues. In addition to inviting representatives from public and private pharmaceutical factories across the country to participate, the conference will also invite the Health Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission to provide joint guidance.

### 全国制药工业会议八月下旬在京举行

1950-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国制药工业会议八月下旬在京举行
    【新华社十一日讯】为配合正在举行的全国卫生会议，推进全国卫生工作建设，中央人民政府卫生部、轻工业部已决定于八月二十五日在北京召开全国制药工业会议。会议将讨论制药工业今后生产方针、分工制造办法等问题。会议除将分邀全国各地公、私营制药工厂代表参加外，并将邀请中国人民革命军事委员会卫生部会同指导。
