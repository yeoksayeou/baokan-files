Title: Xinhua Bookstore Distributes Separate Copies of This Publication
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 5

Starting from the fifteenth issue, this publication will also be printed as a separate sixteen-page edition, distributed by the General Administration Office of Xinhua Bookstore, with a basic price of 0.6 yuan per issue. Those who wish to subscribe to or purchase individual copies of this publication should contact their local Xinhua Bookstore directly.

### 新华书店发行本刊单行本

1950-08-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　新华书店发行本刊单行本
    本刊自第十五期起，每期另印十六开单行本，委托新华书店总管理处发行，每期基价六角。订阅或零购本刊者，请迳向当地新华书店接洽。
