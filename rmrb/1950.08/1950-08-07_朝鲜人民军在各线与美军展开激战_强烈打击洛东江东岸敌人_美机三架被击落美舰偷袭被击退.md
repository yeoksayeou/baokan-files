Title: Korean People's Army Engages in Fierce Battles with US Troops on All Fronts; Inflicts Heavy Blows on Enemy on East Bank of the Nakdong River; Three US Aircraft Shot Down; US Naval Vessel's Sneak Attack Repelled
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th—The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in a war report on the evening of the 5th that People's Army units continue to engage in fierce battles with US troops on all fronts. The enemy, having suffered heavy blows from People's Army units in Jinju, Hapcheon, Gimcheon, Andong, and other places and forced to retreat, continues to put up stubborn resistance in the area east of the Nakdong River, taking advantage of the favorable terrain. People's Army units, in close coordination, are inflicting heavy blows on the enemy in this area. The People's Army Air Force detachments continue to engage in fierce battles with the numerically superior US Air Force, covering the advance of the People's Army ground forces, and boldly attacking enemy rear military installations and supply lines, inflicting heavy damage on the enemy.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th—The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in a war report on the morning of the 6th that People's Army units continue to engage in fierce battles with US troops on all fronts. US aircraft and naval fleets continue to bomb and shell peaceful cities and rural areas extensively, destroying schools, hospitals, cultural facilities, and residences.

On the 5th, a US warship attempted a sneak attack near Baengnyeong Island on the west coast. Under the intense bombardment of the People's Army coastal artillery, the enemy warship failed to achieve its objective and fled south in panic.

On the 5th, eight US bombers once again invaded Nampo City and were shot down by People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units. On the same day, four US bombers also attacked Haeju City, but were thwarted by the fire of the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units.

### 朝鲜人民军在各线与美军展开激战  强烈打击洛东江东岸敌人  美机三架被击落美舰偷袭被击退

1950-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线与美军展开激战
    强烈打击洛东江东岸敌人
    美机三架被击落美舰偷袭被击退
    【新华社平壤六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部五日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续与美军部队展开激战。在晋州、陕川、金泉、安东等地遭到人民军部队沉重打击而败退的敌人，在洛东江东岸地带利用有利的地理条件，继续进行着顽抗。人民军各部队在紧密的协同动作下，对该地区的敌人，予以强烈的打击。人民军空军区分队与数量上占优势的美国空军，继续展开剧烈战斗，掩护着人民军地面部队的进攻，果敢地袭击敌人后方的军事设施和供给线，予敌人以重创。
    【新华社平壤六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部六日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续与美军部队展开着激烈的战斗。美机群与舰队对和平城市与农村，继续大肆轰炸与炮击，破坏着学校、病院、文化设施及住宅。
    五日，美军舰一艘偷袭西海岸白翎岛附近。在人民军海岸炮队的猛烈轰击下，敌舰未达目的而仓皇向南逃窜。
    五日，美轰炸机八架再度侵袭南浦市，被人民军高射炮部队击落三架。同日，海州市也有美轰炸机四架来袭，但由于人民军高射炮部队的射击而未得逞。
