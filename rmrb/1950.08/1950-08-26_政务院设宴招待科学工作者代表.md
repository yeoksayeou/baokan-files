Title: Government Administration Council Hosts Banquet for Scientific Workers Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 1

　　Government Administration Council Hosts Banquet for Scientific Workers Representatives

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet on the evening of the 25th for all the representatives attending the National Congress of Natural Science Workers of China. The heads of various ministries, commissions, academies, administrations, and banks under the Financial and Economic Committee and the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council also attended. After the banquet, a Peking opera performance was held.

### 政务院设宴招待科学工作者代表

1950-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院设宴招待科学工作者代表
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院于二十五日晚设宴招待中华全国自然科学工作者代表会议的全体代表。政务院财政经济委员会及文化教育委员会所属各部、会、院、署、行的首长均与会。宴后，并举行京剧晚会。
