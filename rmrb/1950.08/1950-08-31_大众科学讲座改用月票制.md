Title: Public Science Lectures Adopt Monthly Ticket System
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 6

The public science lectures will adopt a monthly ticket system starting in September, with four lectures per month (held every Saturday). Workers, soldiers, organizations, groups, and individuals who wish to attend the lectures are requested to register at the Youth Service Department (16 East Chang'an Avenue) from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM and 7:30 PM to 9:30 PM today. Tickets will be available from August 31st to September 2nd. The lecture topics and speakers are as follows:

	September 2nd: Liu Liangji will speak on "Salt."
	September 9th: Wang Longfu will speak on "Coal."
	September 16th: Chen Jianhou will speak on "Soybeans."
	September 23rd: Wang Jinlin will speak on "Paper."

### 大众科学讲座改用月票制

1950-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座改用月票制
    大众科学讲座自九月份开始改用月票制，每月讲四次（每星期六举行）。希望工人、部队、机关、团体及个人愿参加听讲者，于今日下午三时至六时，晚七时半至九时半到青年服务部（东长安街十六号）登记。领票日期自本月三十一日起至九月二日止。讲题程序及主讲人如下：
    九月二日，刘良楫讲：“盐”。九月九日，王隆甫讲：“煤”。九月十六日，陈建侯讲：“大豆”。九月二十三日，王金林讲：“纸”。
