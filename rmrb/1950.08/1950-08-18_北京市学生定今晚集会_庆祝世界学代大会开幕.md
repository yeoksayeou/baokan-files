Title: Beijing Students to Hold Rally Tonight to Celebrate the Opening of the World Student Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 1

　　【Our News】Beijing university and middle school students are scheduled to hold a "Celebration of the Opening of the Second World Student Congress" at the Labor People's Cultural Palace at 7:00 PM this evening (the 18th) to express their warm support for the Second World Student Congress which began in Prague on the 14th and for the International Union of Students, and to express their determination to work hard for lasting world peace under the leadership of the International Union of Students. Five thousand students will participate in the conference. Liao Chengzhi, Xu Liqun, Qian Sanqiang and others will attend and speak. After the meeting, there will be singing, poetry recitations, movies and other programs.

### 北京市学生定今晚集会  庆祝世界学代大会开幕

1950-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市学生定今晚集会
    庆祝世界学代大会开幕
    【本报讯】北京市大、中学生定今（十八）日下午七时在劳动人民文化宫举行“庆祝世界学生第二次代表大会开幕大会”，以表示对十四日在布拉格开始举行的世界学生第二次代表大会和对国际学联的热烈拥护，并表示在世界学联领导之下，坚决为争取世界持久和平而努力。大会将有五千学生参加。廖承志、许立群、钱三强等将出席讲话。会后并有歌咏、诗朗诵、电影等节目。
