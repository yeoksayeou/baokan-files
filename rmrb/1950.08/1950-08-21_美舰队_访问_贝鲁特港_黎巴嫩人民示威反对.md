Title: US Fleet "Visits" Beirut Port, Lebanese People Demonstrate in Opposition
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 17th: Lebanese progressives and working people have expressed great indignation at the "visit" of the United States Navy's Sixth Fleet to Beirut Port. The aforementioned US fleet recently made an unauthorized "visit" to the port. On the eve of the US fleet's departure, the people of Beirut held a demonstration of over three thousand people. The demonstrators protested against turning Lebanon into a US military base and protested against the American aggression in Korea.

### 美舰队“访问”贝鲁特港  黎巴嫩人民示威反对

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　美舰队“访问”贝鲁特港
    黎巴嫩人民示威反对
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社大马士革十七日讯：黎巴嫩进步人士和劳动人民，对于美国海军第六分舰队“访问”贝鲁特港一事，甚表愤慨。上述美舰队曾于最近擅自“访问”该港。在美舰队驶离该港前夕，贝鲁特人民群众曾举行示威，参加者达三千余人。示威群众抗议将黎巴嫩沦为美国军事基地，同时抗议美国侵略朝鲜。
