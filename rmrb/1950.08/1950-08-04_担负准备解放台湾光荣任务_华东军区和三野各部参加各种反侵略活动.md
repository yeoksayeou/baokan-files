Title: Undertaking the Glorious Task of Preparing to Liberate Taiwan, East China Military Region and Third Field Army Units Participate in Various Anti-Aggression Activities
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 3rd. The People's Liberation Army's East China Military Region and the Third Field Army's land, sea, and air forces, which are tasked with preparing to liberate Taiwan and defend the southeast coast, launched extensive celebrations for the "August 1st" Army Day. Troops stationed in major cities such as Shanghai, Nanjing, and Hangzhou participated in local people's rallies and demonstrations celebrating Army Day and opposing American aggression against Taiwan and Korea. Many unit leaders and combat heroes gave speeches at these meetings, resolutely expressing the determination of all commanders and fighters to repel US imperialist aggression against Taiwan, and extending warm respect to the Korean people. Before and after the festival, various units conducted education on the history of the army and education on glorious traditions within the troops, and launched a movement against American aggression against Taiwan and Korea. On August 1st, newspapers of the East China Military Region's various units published special commemorative issues. Various armies under a certain army group held general competitions in military, political work, culture, and sports before and after the festival. A certain army held a large review at the regimental level. The People's Liberation Army units on the Jinmen front also held enthusiastic celebrations in their respective garrisons. A certain unit engaged in arduous road construction work in a certain area unanimously expressed at the Army Day celebration rally that they would work hard to complete the road construction task as soon as possible.

### 担负准备解放台湾光荣任务  华东军区和三野各部参加各种反侵略活动

1950-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　担负准备解放台湾光荣任务
    华东军区和三野各部参加各种反侵略活动
    【新华社南京三日电】担负准备解放台湾和保卫东南沿海国防任务的人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军所属陆、海、空军部队在“八一”建军节展开了广泛的庆祝活动。驻上海、南京、杭州等大城市的部队，都参加了当地人民庆祝建军节、反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜的示威大会和示威游行。许多部队首长和战斗英雄模范们在这些大会上讲了话，代表全体指战员坚决表示了击退美帝国主义侵略台湾的决心，并向朝鲜人民致热烈的敬意。各部在节日前后，曾在部队中进行了建军历史教育和光荣传统教育，并展开反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动。八一，华东军区各部队报纸均出版了纪念专刊。某兵团所属各军在节日前后普遍举行了军事、政治工作、文化、体育等项比赛。某军以团为单位举行了大检阅。金门前线的人民解放军各部也在驻地分别举行了热烈的庆祝。正在某地从事艰苦的修路工程的某部，在庆祝建军节大会上一致表示要努力劳动，争取早日完成筑路任务。
