Title: North Korean Farmers' Delegation Goes to Southern Land Reform Areas to Work
Author:
Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd. The Korean Central News Agency reports: In order to congratulate the farmers in the newly liberated areas of South Korea who are carrying out land reform, the North Korean Farmers' Alliance has organized a farmers' congratulatory delegation. The delegation consists of thirty-five agricultural labor heroes, including model farmer Zhang Zhijia, who was awarded the Republic's Medal of Merit, and dozens of members of the farmers' theater troupe. Carrying a large number of photographs introducing the democratic construction of the northern half of the Republic and related to farmers, they departed on the 27th in three teams to Gyeonggi-do, South Gangwon-do, and Hwanghae-do, etc.

### 朝鲜北部农民组祝贺团赴南部土改区进行工作

1950-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜北部农民组祝贺团赴南部土改区进行工作
    【新华社平壤二日电】朝鲜中央通讯社讯：北朝鲜农民同盟为了对南朝鲜新解放地区正进行着土地改革的农民表示庆贺，特组织了农民祝贺团。该团由荣获共和国功劳奖章的模范农民张智甲等三十五名农业劳动英雄与农民剧团团员数十人组成，携带大批介绍共和国北半部民主建设及与农民有关的照片，于二十七日分三队赴京畿道，南江原道与黄海道等地。
