Title: World Peace Congress Standing Committee Cables Japanese Peace Association Inviting Japan to Attend Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 4

　　World Peace Congress Standing Committee Cables Japanese Peace Association
    Inviting Japan to Attend Second World Peace Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th] Tokyo dispatch: The Japan Peace Protection Association recently received a letter from Raphael, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the World Congress in Defense of Peace, inviting Japan to send 40 representatives to the Second World Congress in Defense of Peace to be held in Warsaw on October 16.

### 世界和大常委会电日和平协会  邀请日本出席二届和大

1950-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和大常委会电日和平协会
    邀请日本出席二届和大
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：日本保卫和平协会近接到世界拥护和平大会常设委员会总书记拉斐德来函，邀请日本派代表四十人，出席十月十六日在华沙召开的第二届世界拥护和平大会。
