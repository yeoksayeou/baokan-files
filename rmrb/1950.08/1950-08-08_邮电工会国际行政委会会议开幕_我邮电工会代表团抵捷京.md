Title: International Administrative Committee Meeting of Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Opens; Our Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union delegation, headed by Zhu Xuefan, passed through Moscow on the 3rd and arrived in Prague on the afternoon of the 6th to attend the Administrative Committee meeting of the Postal, Telegraph and Telephone International, an industrial department of the World Federation of Trade Unions, held in Prague. The chairman of the Czechoslovak Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, Kola, and the Deputy Minister of Posts, Remoser, welcomed them at the station.
　　The Administrative Committee meeting of the Postal, Telegraph and Telephone International opened on the 7th. The main agenda of the meeting includes the struggle of international postal and telecommunications workers for peace, and the economic and social rights of postal and telecommunications workers in capitalist and colonial countries.
　　The delegation will then go to Salzwedel and Berlin in East Germany to attend the Free German Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Congress and the Third Free German Trade Union Congress as guests.

### 邮电工会国际行政委会会议开幕  我邮电工会代表团抵捷京

1950-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　邮电工会国际行政委会会议开幕
    我邮电工会代表团抵捷京
    【本报讯】新华社消息：以朱学范为首的中国邮电工会代表团于三日途经莫斯科，已于六日下午抵达布拉格，出席在布拉格召开的世界工联产业部之一邮电工人工会国际的行政委员会会议。捷克斯洛伐克邮电工会主席科拉及邮政部副部长雷摩瑟均往车站欢迎。
    邮电工人工会国际的行政委员会会议已于七日开幕。会议主要议程为国际邮电工人为和平而斗争，和资本主义国家与殖民地国家的邮电工人的经济社会权利等问题。
    该代表团随后将赴东德沙尔兹魏德尔与柏林，以来宾资格出席自由德国邮电工会代表大会和自由德国第三次工会代表大会。
