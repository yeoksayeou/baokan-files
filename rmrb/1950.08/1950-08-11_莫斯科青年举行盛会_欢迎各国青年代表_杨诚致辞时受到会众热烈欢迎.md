Title: Moscow Youth Hold Grand Gathering to Welcome Youth Representatives from Various Countries; Yang Cheng's Speech Receives Enthusiastic Welcome from the Audience
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: According to TASS, Moscow - Two thousand youth representatives from Moscow held a grand gathering at the Trade Union Building on the 5th to welcome the student delegations from China, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia, who were passing through Moscow en route to the Second World Student Congress in Prague, as well as the youth delegations from the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, France, and other countries currently visiting Moscow.

The renowned Moscow Stakhanovite worker, Kolabelnikova, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the young workers of Moscow. Yang Cheng, head of the Chinese student delegation, addressed the assembly amidst fervent applause, stating that the participation of student representatives from China, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam in this grand gathering symbolized the further development and consolidation of the peace camp. When Yang Cheng shouted "Long live Mao Zedong!" and "Long live Stalin!", thunderous cheers erupted in the hall.

Representatives from various countries and Mikhailov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of the Soviet Union, also delivered speeches at the meeting, calling for the consolidation of the unity of progressive youth around the world.

### 莫斯科青年举行盛会  欢迎各国青年代表  杨诚致辞时受到会众热烈欢迎

1950-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科青年举行盛会
    欢迎各国青年代表
    杨诚致辞时受到会众热烈欢迎
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科两千青年代表于五日在工会大厦举行盛会，欢迎出席布拉格世界学生第二次代表大会道经莫斯科的中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古的学生代表团，及现在访问莫斯科的德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克，法国等青年代表团。
    著名的莫斯科斯塔哈诺夫式工人科拉贝尔尼科娃，代表莫斯科青年工人致欢迎辞。中国学生代表团团长杨诚在会众狂热的欢迎中致辞称：中国、朝鲜、蒙古和越南的学生代表参加这个盛会，象征着和平阵营的进一步发展与巩固。当杨诚高呼“毛泽东万岁”，“斯大林万岁”时，大厅中迸发出雷动的欢呼。
    各国代表及苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会书记米哈依洛夫，均在会上发表演说，号召巩固全世界进步青年的团结。
