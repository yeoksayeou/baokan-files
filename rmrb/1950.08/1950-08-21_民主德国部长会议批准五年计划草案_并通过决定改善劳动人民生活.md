Title: The Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic Approves Draft Five-Year Plan and Passes Resolution to Improve the Lives of the Working People
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 19th, TASS reported: The German News Agency reported that the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic approved the draft Five-Year Plan proposed by the German Socialist Unity Party. The Council of Ministers has instructed the Planning Department of the German Democratic Republic, in conjunction with other ministries and state governments, to draft a law on the Five-Year Plan based on this draft.
    In order to prepare for the completion of the Five-Year Plan in industries such as mining, metallurgy, power, hardware, and shipbuilding, the Council of Ministers decided to immediately make a number of large investments to increase production capacity. Secondly, in accordance with the recommendations of the Third Congress of the German Socialist Unity Party, the Council of Ministers passed a resolution on improving the living standards of the working people.

### 民主德国部长会议批准五年计划草案  并通过决定改善劳动人民生活

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　民主德国部长会议批准五年计划草案
    并通过决定改善劳动人民生活
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社柏林十九日讯：德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国部长会议批准了德国统一社会党提出的五年计划草案。部长会议已训令德意志民主共和国计划部会同其他各部及各州政府，以这个草案为基础，起草关于五年计划的法律。
    为了准备在采矿、冶金、动力、五金和造船等工业方面完成五年计划，部长会议决定立即进行若干大宗的投资，以增加生产能力。其次，部长会议按照德国统一社会党第三次代表大会的建议，通过了关于改进劳动人民生活水准的决定。
