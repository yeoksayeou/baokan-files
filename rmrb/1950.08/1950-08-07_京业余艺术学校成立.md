Title: Beijing Amateur Art School Established
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 3

　　Beijing Amateur Art School Established
    [This newspaper dispatch] According to the Beijing Municipal News Agency: The Beijing Amateur Art School has been established. The school's purpose is to serve workers, peasants, and soldiers with literature and art, to meet the needs of the masses of people in Beijing, to train amateur art cadres from among workers and students, and to improve the political and professional level of opera workers.
    The school is jointly sponsored by the Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Beijing People's Art Theater, with the assistance of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Beijing Fine Arts Studio, and is led by the Beijing Municipal Amateur Education Work Committee. The School Affairs Committee is the highest administrative organ, with Li Bozhao, Wang Yaping, Jin Ziguang, Zhao Shuli, Lu Su, Hu Man, Ren Hong, Shu Qiang, Wang Songsheng, Zhang Menggeng, Zu Tiangong, and Li Bokang as twelve members, Li Bozhao as the chairman, and Wang Yaping and Jin Ziguang as the vice-chairmen.
    The school stipulates a quota of 1,400 students (400 in the worker department, 900 in the student department, and 100 in the opera department). Anyone who voluntarily participates in the study of any art department such as literature, drama, music, fine arts, dance, or opera, and who is recommended by their department or passes the examination, can become a student of the school.

### 京业余艺术学校成立

1950-08-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　京业余艺术学校成立
    【本报讯】据京市新闻处讯：北京业余艺术学校已成立了，该校的宗旨是本着文艺为工农兵服务的方针，适应北京市人民大众的需要，培养工人、学生的业余艺术干部，并提高戏曲工作者的政治业务水平。
    该校系由市文联和北京人民艺术剧院联合主办，由市总工会、团市工委和北京美术工作室协助，由北京市业余教育工作委员会领导。校务委员会为最高行政机关，李伯钊、王亚平、金紫光、赵树理、芦肃、胡蛮、任虹、舒强、王松声、张梦庚、祖田工、李伯康等十二人为委员，李伯钊为主任委员，王亚平、金紫光为副主任委员。
    该校规定学员名额为一千四百名（工人部四百名，学生部九百名，戏曲部一百名）。凡自愿参加文学、戏剧、音乐、美术、舞蹈、戏曲等任一艺术部门学习，并经其所在部门保送或考试及格者，均可为该校学员。
