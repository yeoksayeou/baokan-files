Title: Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee Sends Congratulations; Chair and Vice-Chair of China's Democratic Women's Federation Reply with Thanks
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Cai Chang, Chair of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chair, received a congratulatory telegram from Nadezhda Parfyonova, Vice-Chair of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, congratulating China on the "August 1st" Army Day. They replied with a telegram of thanks. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:
    (1) Telegram from Parfyonova:
    To Cai Chang and Deng Yingchao of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Beijing:
    We warmly congratulate you and the female soldiers - participants in the Chinese People's Liberation Movement - on the 23rd anniversary of the People's Liberation Army. The Chinese People's Liberation Army vigilantly guards the interests of your country, and they have won the glory of immortal victories in the struggle against the Kuomintang clique, foreign imperialists, and aggressors. We congratulate you on achieving new successes in further strengthening the power of your country.
    Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
    Long live the heroic Chinese women!
    With warm greetings!  Nadezhda Parfyonova
    (2) Reply telegram from Cai Chang and Deng Yingchao:
    To Madam Parfyonova:
    We and the female soldiers participating in the Chinese People's Liberation Movement sincerely thank you for your congratulations on the "August 1st" Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. We will further strengthen our unity, learn from the heroic fighting spirit of the Soviet sisters in the anti-fascist war and in the great construction for peace, and we will work harder to completely eliminate the remaining Chiang Kai-shek bandit gangs, liberate Taiwan and Tibet, and strive to defend lasting world peace.
    Long live the close friendship between Chinese and Soviet women!
            Cai Chang Deng Yingchao

### 苏联妇女反法西斯委员会来电祝贺我国民主妇联主席副主席复电致谢

1950-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联妇女反法西斯委员会来电祝贺我国民主妇联主席副主席复电致谢
    【新华社三十一日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超，收到苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席巴菲诺娃祝贺我国“八一”建军节的贺电，并复电致谢，两电电文如下：
    （一）巴菲诺娃来电：
    北京中华全国民主妇女联合会蔡畅、邓颖超：
    我们向您们以及女战士们——中国人民解放运动的参加者们热烈祝贺人民解放军二十三周年纪念。中国人民解放军非常警觉地守卫着贵国的利益，他们在反对国民党集团、外国帝国主义者及侵略者的斗争中，曾获得不朽的胜利的光荣。我们祝贺您们在进一步加强贵国的威势中获得新的成就。
    中国人民解放军万岁！
    英勇的中国妇女万岁！
    致热烈的敬礼！      纳杰日达·巴菲诺娃
    （二）蔡畅、邓颖超复电：巴菲诺娃夫人：
    我们和参加中国人民解放运动的女战士们诚恳地感谢你们在中国人民解放军“八一”建军节的祝贺。我们要更加强团结，学习苏联姐妹们曾在反对法西斯战争中和在为和平的伟大建设中英勇奋斗精神，我们要更加努力，为了彻底消灭蒋介石残余匪帮，解放台湾、西藏，为了保卫世界持久和平而奋斗。
    中苏妇女亲密友谊万岁！
            蔡畅  邓颖超
