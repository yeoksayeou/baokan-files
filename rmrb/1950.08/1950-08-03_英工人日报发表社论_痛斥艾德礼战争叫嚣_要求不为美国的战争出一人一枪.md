Title: British "Worker's Daily" Publishes Editorial Denouncing Attlee's Warmongering; Demands Not to Contribute a Single Man or Gun to America's War
Author:
Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 2nd, TASS dispatch: The *Worker's Daily* published an editorial exposing the essence of Attlee's broadcast, stating: "Never have more clever words been abused to defend such a vile and filthy undertaking. ... What the people need is peace, not war. But Attlee's appeal calls for a world war so that the United States can achieve its goal of conquering the world. Under pressure from the United States, he is gradually dragging Britain into a global conflict, yet he is concealing from the British people the terrible risks and suffering caused by this sinful policy. He depicts a purely fictional worldwide communist conspiracy, yet he refuses to face the reality of Nehru and Stalin's peace proposals, which will end the Korean War and save our youth from pointless sacrifice.

Attlee says: Communism threatens Britain's economic recovery. However, it is not communism that is undermining our recovery, destroying social services, and destroying the possibility of realizing our housing construction plans, but precisely the massive new rearmament plan currently being implemented under American orders. Attlee is actually defending the war!

We demand that he defend peace! Not a single man or gun for MacArthur's war! Support the Stalin-Nehru peace proposals! No rearmament for America's war! Save world peace!"

### 英工人日报发表社论  痛斥艾德礼战争叫嚣  要求不为美国的战争出一人一枪

1950-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　英工人日报发表社论
    痛斥艾德礼战争叫嚣
    要求不为美国的战争出一人一枪
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社伦敦一日讯：工人日报发表社论，揭露艾德礼广播的本质称：“从来没有比这更巧妙的辞句，被滥用来辩护这么卑鄙龌龊的勾当了。……人民需要的是和平，而不是战争。但是，艾德礼的呼吁却号召世界大战，使美国能达到其征服世界的目的。在美国的压力下，他正在把英国逐步拖入世界冲突中，然而，他却把这种罪恶的政策所招致的可怖的冒险与痛苦瞒着英国人。他描绘纯系虚构的全世界规模的共产党阴谋，然而，他却拒绝面对尼赫鲁和斯大林和平建议的现实，这些建议将结束朝鲜战争，且将拯救我们的青年免受无谓的牺牲。
    艾德礼说：共产主义威胁英国的经济复兴。然而，破坏我们的恢复工作，毁坏社会服务事业，并摧毁实现我们的房屋建筑计划之可能的，并不是共产主义，而恰恰是目前在美国命令下执行庞大的、新的重整军备计划。艾德礼竟为战争辩护！
    我们要求他为和平辩护！不为麦克阿瑟的战争出一人一枪！拥护斯大林——尼赫鲁的和平建议！不为美国的战争重整军备！拯救世界和平！”
