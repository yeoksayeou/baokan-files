Title: Northwest Ethnic Youth Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-08-20
Page: 1
　　
　　【Xinhua News Agency, 19th】 The Northwest Ethnic Youth Visiting Group, consisting of 49 people, led by the head of the group, Silamujiang (Kazakh), and deputy heads Wang Tianyuan (Han), Meng Bolin (Mongolian), and Ma Zuling (Hui), arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 18th. Representatives from eight organizations, including the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, and the Beijing Students' Federation, went to the station to welcome them and presented flowers to the visiting group.

　　The visiting group consists of representatives from various ethnic groups who recently attended the Third Northwest Youth Congress and the First Northwest Student Congress, including youth from various circles of the Hui, Uygur, Kazakh, Tibetan, Mongolian, Tatar, Tu, Uzbek, Russian, Xibe, and Han ethnic groups. The group will visit schools, factories, and scenic spots in Beijing, Tianjin, and other places.

### 西北各族青年参观团抵北京

1950-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族青年参观团抵北京
    【新华社十九日讯】西北各族青年参观团一行四十九人，由团长斯拉木江（哈萨克族）及副团长王天元（汉族）、孟柏林（蒙族）、马祖灵（回族）率领，已于十八日晚抵达北京。中央人民政府民族事务委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、青年团北京市工作委员会、北京市民主妇女联合会及北京市学生联合会等八团体代表前往车站欢迎，并向参观团献花。
    参观团系由最近出席西北青年第三届代表大会及西北学生第一届代表大会的各族代表组成，包括回族、维吾尔族、哈萨克族、藏族、蒙族、塔塔尔族、土族、乌兹别克族、俄罗斯族、锡泊族及汉族等十一个民族的各界青年。该团将参观北京、天津等地的学校、工厂、名胜等。
