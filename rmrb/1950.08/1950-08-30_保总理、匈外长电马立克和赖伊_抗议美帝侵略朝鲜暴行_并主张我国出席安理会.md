Title: Bulgarian Premier, Hungarian Foreign Minister Cable Malik and Lie, Protesting U.S. Imperialist Aggression Against Korea and Advocating China's Attendance at the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th dispatch: TASS dispatch: Bulgarian Council of Ministers Chairman Chervenkov and Hungarian Foreign Minister Károly Gyula have sent telegrams to Security Council President Malik and United Nations Secretary-General Lie respectively, lodging a serious protest against the United States' aggression against Korea and the atrocities of the U.S. Air Force indiscriminately bombing peaceful Korean residents.

In his telegram, Chervenkov, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, expressed full support for the Soviet Union's proposals in the Security Council and denounced the barbaric atrocities of the U.S. armed forces in wantonly bombing peaceful Korean residents. The telegram pointed out: The American aggressors have violated the United Nations Charter, violated the main principles of international law, and Chapter III of the Geneva Convention on the protection of civilians. The Bulgarian government firmly maintains that the United Nations must immediately take measures to stop the insane bombing of Korean residential areas and peaceful residents.

In his telegram, Károly Gyula pointed out: The government of the People's Republic of Hungary believes that the Korean question is a pressing issue in current international relations that must be resolved without delay. Therefore, the Hungarian government fully supports the Soviet Union's proposals in the Security Council and believes that it is necessary to: 1. Hear the opinions of representatives of the Korean people. 2. End the Korean War and withdraw foreign troops currently in Korea. 3. Allow representatives of the People's Republic of China to participate in the Security Council. The telegram also requested the Security Council to take measures to immediately stop the bestial bombing of the Korean people by the American aggressors.

### 保总理、匈外长电马立克和赖伊  抗议美帝侵略朝鲜暴行  并主张我国出席安理会

1950-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　保总理、匈外长电马立克和赖伊
    抗议美帝侵略朝鲜暴行
    并主张我国出席安理会
    【新华社二十九日讯】综合塔斯社讯：保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫，匈牙利外交部长卡莱·古拉，顷分别致电安全理事会主席马立克与联合国秘书长赖伊，严重抗议美国侵略朝鲜与美国空军对朝鲜和平居民滥肆轰炸的暴行。
    契尔文科夫在其电文中代表保加利亚人民共和国政府，对苏联在安理会上的建议表示完全支持，并痛斥美国武装部队野蛮地轰炸朝鲜和平居民的兽行。电文指出：美国侵略者已破坏了联合国宪章，违反了国际法的主要原则和关于保护平民的日内瓦公约第三章。保加利亚政府坚决主张联合国必须立即采取措施，制止对朝鲜各居民区及和平居民的疯狂轰炸。
    卡莱·古拉在其电文中指出：匈牙利人民共和国政府认为朝鲜问题乃是目前国际关系间急如燃眉的问题，必须毫不迟延地加以解决，因此匈牙利政府完全支持苏联在安全理事会上的建议，认为必须：一、听取朝鲜人民代表申述意见。二、终止朝鲜战争，撤出现在在朝鲜的外国军队。三、准许中华人民共和国代表参加安全理事会。电文并要求安理会采取措施，立即制止美国侵略者对朝鲜人民的兽性轰炸。
