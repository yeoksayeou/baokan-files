Title: Romanian Education Leaps Forward: Enrollment of Workers' and Peasants' Children Increases by 34 Percent Compared to Last Year
Author:
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 4

　　Romanian Education Leaps Forward
    Enrollment of Workers' and Peasants' Children Increases by 34 Percent Compared to Last Year

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 5th: TASS reports from Bucharest on the 5th: According to the Romanian News Agency, since the publication of the resolution on educational reform, Romania has achieved significant accomplishments in education over the past two years. This year, the number of children of workers and laboring peasants enrolled in schools has increased by 34 percent compared to the 1948-1949 academic year. Special attention has been paid to the development of vocational training, as well as intermediate and advanced technical education.

    The number of students in primary and secondary schools has reached 2,180,000, and the number of university students exceeds 48,600, compared to only 20,700 in 1944.

    Currently, there are 969 primary and secondary boarding schools nationwide, compared to only 559 in 1948. Children of ethnic minorities account for 300,700 students in primary and secondary schools.

    Most importantly, the curriculum has been reformed. Departments of social sciences—Marxism-Leninism, political economy, dialectical materialism, and historical materialism—have been established in all faculties of higher education institutions.

### 罗马尼亚教育突飞猛进  入学工农子女较去年增百分之三十四

1950-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚教育突飞猛进
    入学工农子女较去年增百分之三十四
    【新华社讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特五日电：据罗马尼亚电讯社报道：自从关于教育改革的决议公布以后，两年以来，罗马尼亚已在教育上获得重大成就。今年，工人和劳动农民们入学的子女，较一九四八——一九四九学年增加百分之三十四。发展职业训练以及中级和高级的技术教育，都受到特别的注意。
    初等和中等学校的学生数目达二百一十八万名，大学学生的数目超过四万八千六百名，而在一九四四年只有二万零七百名。
    现今全国有九百六十九所初等和中等的寄宿学校，而在一九四八年只有五百五十九所。少数民族的儿女有三十万零一千七百名学生入初等和中等的学校。
    最重要的是课程的改革，高等教育机关的各系中都设立了社会科学的课程——马列主义，政治经济学，辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义。
