　　Congratulations on the Sixth Anniversary of the Liberation of Romania
    Chairman Mao Cables Chairman Parhon
    Premier Zhou Cables Chairman Groza
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] August 23rd marks the sixth anniversary of the liberation of the Romanian People's Republic. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council sent telegrams to Constantin I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of that country, and Petru Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, respectively, to express their congratulations. The telegrams are as follows:
   (1) Chairman Mao's congratulatory telegram to His Excellency Constantin I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Romania, I, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the Romanian people. I also deeply believe that the Romanian people will achieve further success in economic and cultural construction.
          Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                           August 21, 1950, in Beijing
   (2) Premier Zhou's congratulatory telegram to His Excellency Petru Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Romania, I warmly congratulate the Romanian people on their happiness and progress.
  Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                           August 21, 1950, in Beijing

### 祝贺罗马尼亚解放六周年  毛主席电巴洪主席  周总理电格罗查主席
周恩来  毛泽东
1950-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺罗马尼亚解放六周年
    毛主席电巴洪主席
    周总理电格罗查主席
    【新华社二十二日讯】八月二十三日为罗马尼亚人民共和国解放六周年纪念。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与政务院周恩来总理分别致电该国国民议会主席团康斯坦丁·依·巴洪主席及部长会议伯特鲁·格罗查主席，表示祝贺。电文如下：
   （一）毛主席贺电罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团康斯坦丁·依·巴洪主席阁下：
    我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府及中国人民，值此罗马尼亚解放六周年之际，谨向阁下及罗马尼亚人民致祝贺之忱。并深信罗马尼亚人民在经济、文化的建设上将有更进一步的成功。
          中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
                           一九五○年八月二十一日于北京
   （二）周总理贺电罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议伯特鲁·格罗查主席阁下：
    欣逢罗马尼亚解放六周年之际，我热烈祝贺罗马尼亚人民的幸福与进步。
  中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长  周恩来
                           一九五○年八月二十一日于北京
