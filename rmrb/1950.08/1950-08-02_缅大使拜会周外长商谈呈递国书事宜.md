Title: Burmese Ambassador Visits Foreign Minister Zhou to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st — U Myint Toon, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to our country, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 6:00 PM today to discuss matters concerning the presentation of his credentials. Those present included Qiao Guanhua, Acting Director of the Asian Department, Chen Jiakang, Deputy Director, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 缅大使拜会周外长商谈呈递国书事宜

1950-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　缅大使拜会周外长商谈呈递国书事宜
    【新华社一日讯】缅甸联邦驻我国首任特命全权大使吴敏登于今日下午六时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，就呈递国书问题有所商谈。在座者尚有亚洲司代司长乔冠华、副司长陈家康、交际处长王倬如。
