Title: Korean People's Army Liberates Waegwan on the East Bank of the Nakdong River; Ten-Day Operation on the South Coast Annihilates Over 6,000 American Troops
Author:
Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd (Delayed Dispatch) The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the evening of the 17th that People's Army units on all fronts continued to engage in fierce battles with American troops and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army, who were stubbornly resisting under the cover of tanks, armored vehicles, and artillery units.

The People's Army units advancing towards Waegwan in Gyeongsangbuk-do launched a fierce attack on American troops and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army, who were attempting to resist by utilizing the high ground and favorable geographical conditions along the Nakdong River and its east bank, and by establishing defensive positions using fortifications, minefields, and other artificial and natural obstacles. On August 15th, they liberated Waegwan, an important defensive stronghold of the enemy.

In the battle to liberate Waegwan, People's Army units killed, wounded, and captured a large number of enemies. They seized a large amount of war booty, including five tanks, twelve 105mm guns, twenty-five rocket launchers, eighty-one mortars, vehicles, and other weapons and ammunition.

On August 13th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down two American B-29 bombers that were raiding the Bakcheon area of ​​North Pyongan Province.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd (Delayed Dispatch) The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the evening of the 18th that People's Army units attacking on the southern coast gave the main force of the American 25th Division, which continued to resist under the cover of powerful tank units and the navy, a heavy and decisive blow, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. From August 2nd to 12th, People's Army units in the area killed or wounded over 5,000 enemy troops and captured over 1,000. War booty seized included: twenty-nine heavy tanks, fifteen 155mm guns, twenty-four 105mm guns, seven 76mm guns, forty 60mm guns, thirty-two rocket launchers, seven other guns, thirty anti-aircraft machine guns, forty-three heavy machine guns, ten light machine guns, over four hundred automatic rifles, over two thousand rifles, over four hundred vehicles, two flamethrowers, and a large amount of various shells and ammunition.

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the morning of the 21st that People's Army units on all lines were fiercely fighting enemy troops who were conducting frantic resistance. American and Li Chengwan troops, supported by aircraft, tanks, and artillery, were resisting stubbornly, attempting to recover their huge losses. People's Army units repelled the enemy's attempts to launch counter-offensives and dealt new blows to the enemy.

The People's Army units advancing along the southern coast are inflicting losses on the resisting American 25th Division and Marine Corps units and continue to advance, pursuing the defeated enemy.

On August 20th, heavy machine guns of the People's Army ground forces shot down two American fighter planes that were raiding Sariwon.

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the evening of the 21st that People's Army units on all lines continued to engage in fierce battles with the resisting American and Li Chengwan troops. American and Li Chengwan puppet troops mobilized all their forces and were conducting fierce counter-offensives everywhere with the support of aircraft, tanks, and artillery. People's Army units continuously struck the enemy, causing them to suffer losses.

In order to cover up the failures of the American troops on the front lines, the American air force has been more intensely and brutally bombing Korean cities and villages. On August 20th, American planes barbarously bombed Haeju, Wonsan, Pyongyang, and rural areas of South Pyongan Province.

As a result of these barbaric bombings, civilian houses in some cities and farmhouses in rural areas were destroyed.

### 洛东江东岸朝鲜人民军解放倭馆  南海岸十天作战歼美军六千余人

1950-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　洛东江东岸朝鲜人民军解放倭馆
    南海岸十天作战歼美军六千余人
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】（迟到）朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十七日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，继续与在坦克、装甲车及炮兵部队的掩护下进行顽抗的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军展开激战。
    向庆尚北道倭馆方面进攻中的人民军部队，对利用洛东江及其东岸一带的高地和有利的地理条件，利用防御工事、地雷网及其他人工与天然障碍物建立防御阵地企图顽抗的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，施以猛烈攻击，于八月十五日解放了敌人的重要防御据点——倭馆。
    人民军部队在解放倭馆的战斗中，毙、伤、俘敌甚众。缴获战利品极多，内有坦克五辆，一○五公厘炮十二门、火箭炮二十五门、八一迫击炮、汽车及其他武器、弹药等。
    八月十三日人民军高射炮部队击落空袭平安北道博川地区的美军Ｂ——二十九型轰炸机两架。
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】（迟到）朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十八日晚发表战报称：在南海岸方面进攻的人民军部队，给予在有力的坦克部队及海军掩护下继续顽抗的美军第二十五师主力以沉重的决定性打击，使敌人遭受重大损失。自八月二日至十二日，人民军在该地区共毙、伤敌军五千余名，俘敌一千余名。缴获战利品如下：重型坦克二十九辆、一五五公厘炮十五门、一○五公厘炮二十四门、七六公厘炮七门、六○炮四十门、火箭炮三十二门、其他炮七门、高射机关枪三十挺、重机关枪四十三挺、轻机关枪十挺、自动枪四百余支、步枪二千余支、汽车四百余辆、火焰喷射器二个及其他各种炮弹与弹药甚多。
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十一日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各线与进行疯狂抵抗的敌军激战。美军和李承晚军在飞机、坦克和大炮的支援下进行顽抗，企图挽回他们的巨大损失。人民军部队击退敌人发动反攻的企图，并给予敌人新的打击。
    沿南海岸前进的人民军部队正使进行顽抗的美国第二十五师和海军陆战队部队蒙受损失，并继续前进，追击败退的敌军。
    八月二十日，人民军地面部队的重机枪，击落空袭沙里院的美国战斗机两架。
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十一日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各线继续对进行顽抗的美国和李承晚军展开激战。美军和李承晚伪军动员了他们的全部力量，并在飞机、坦克和大炮的支援下到处进行着猛烈的反攻。人民军部队给予敌军以不断打击，使之遭到损失。
    为了掩盖前线美军的失败，美国空军更加猛烈地、残暴地轰炸朝鲜的城市与乡村。八月二十日，美国飞机野蛮地轰炸海州、元山、平壤和平安南道的乡村地区。
    由于这些野蛮的轰炸，若干城市中的民房和乡村地区的农庄被炸毁。
