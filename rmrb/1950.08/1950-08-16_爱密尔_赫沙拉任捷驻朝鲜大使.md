Title: Emil Hršel Appointed Czechoslovak Ambassador to North Korea
Author: 
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 15th: TASS reports that the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Gottwald, appointed Emil Hršel as the Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of Czechoslovakia to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the 14th. Ambassador Hršel will travel to Pyongyang to assume his post soon.

### 爱密尔·赫沙拉任捷驻朝鲜大使

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　爱密尔·赫沙拉任捷驻朝鲜大使
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德，于十四日任命爱密尔·赫沙拉为捷克斯洛伐克驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使。赫沙拉大使不日即赴平壤莅任。
