Title: Speech by Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August:

Speech by Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, at the Beijing Celebration of "August 1st" Army Day and Demonstration Against U.S. Aggression in Taiwan and Korea:

Comrades:

We, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation, on behalf of the democratic youth of the entire nation, extend our highest revolutionary salute to all commanders and fighters of the Army, Air Force, and Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, directly led by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu!

For the past twenty-plus years, Chinese youth have been the most loyal and active supporters of the People's Liberation War. In the future, they will also be the most loyal and active supporters of completing the liberation of Taiwan and Tibet, and carrying the People's Liberation War through to the end. We ardently love world peace, and we have the confidence and strength to safeguard world peace. However, today, the United States shamelessly invades our territory of Taiwan, sticking its pig snout into China. We, the Chinese youth, declare today that we will inherit the glorious tradition of the past thirty years, resolutely unite, and support the Central People's Government and the Chinese People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan. To smash any act of imperialist aggression against our country, and to consolidate the People's Republic of China, we will be ready to contribute everything we have.

On the "August 1st" Army Day, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation, on behalf of the democratic youth of the entire nation, congratulate the Korean People's Army on its brilliant victory, and extend fraternal revolutionary greetings to all armed comrades of the Army, Air Force, and Navy of the Korean People's Army, who, under the direct command of General Kim Il-sung, are heroically and victoriously pursuing the American invading troops!

Chinese youth congratulate our Korean brothers who are waging a war of national liberation, and wish them even more glorious victories in the near future!

At the same time, we congratulate the victorious development of the Vietnamese People's Liberation War, and salute the commanders and fighters of the Vietnamese People's Army who are heroically waging a war of national liberation under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh!

On the "August 1st" Army Day, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation extend our respects to the heroic Soviet armed forces who defeated the German, Italian, and Japanese fascist bandits, and who today have become the vanguard in defending world peace, providing boundless confidence and hope to the people of the world! We warmly extend our highest respect to their great and wise leader, Marshal Stalin!

Long live the People's Republic of China!

Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

Long live Chairman Mao!

Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu!

Long live General Kim Il-sung!

Long live President Ho Chi Minh!

Long live Marshal Stalin!

Long live the world peace and democracy camp!

### 全国民主青联主席  廖承志的演说

1950-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国民主青联主席
    廖承志的演说
    【新华社一日讯】中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志在北京各界庆祝“八一”建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会上的演说：同志们：
    我们，中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合总会谨代表全国民主青年，向毛主席、朱总司令直接领导下的中国人民解放军的陆军、空军和海军的全体指战员，致以最崇高的革命敬礼！
    中国青年在过去二十多年，都是人民解放战争的最忠实和最积极的支持者。在今后，也将是完成解放台湾、西藏，将人民解放战争彻底进行到底的最忠实最积极的支持者。我们酷爱世界和平，而且我们有信心和力量来保卫世界和平，但是，今天美国竟然无耻地侵略我国领土台湾，竟然将猪鼻子伸进中国来了。我们中国青年在今天宣布，我们一定继承三十年来的光荣传统，坚决团结起来，拥护中央人民政府和中国人民解放军解放台湾。为了粉碎帝国主义对我国的任何侵略行为，为了巩固中华人民共和国，我们将时刻准备着贡献我们的一切。
    中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合总会在“八一”建军节日，谨代表全国民主青年，祝贺朝鲜人民军辉煌的胜利，并向在金日成将军直接指挥下，英勇地、胜利地追击着美国侵略军队的朝鲜人民军的陆军、空军和海军的全体武装同志，致以兄弟的革命敬礼！
    中国青年祝贺进行着民族解放战争的朝鲜兄弟们，在不远的将来，获得更光辉的胜利！
    同时，我们祝贺越南人民解放战争胜利的发展，并向在胡志明主席领导下，英勇地进行民族解放战争的越南人民军的指战员同志致敬！
    中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合总会在“八一”建军节日，谨向击败德国、意大利、日本法西斯强盗，今天成为保卫世界和平的前卫，在全世界人民面前提供了无穷尽信心和希望的苏联武装英雄们致敬！我们热烈地向他们伟大的、英明的领导者斯大林大元帅致崇高的敬礼！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民解放军万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
    朱总司令万岁！
    金日成将军万岁！
    胡志明主席万岁！
    斯大林大元帅万岁！
    世界和平民主阵营万岁！
