Title: Heavy Casualties Among U.S. Troops Invading Korea; U.S. Imperialism Conceals the Truth, Provoking Internal Attacks
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th Dispatch: The American aggressors' concealment of the number of American military casualties in the Korean War has provoked internal attacks. According to a TASS dispatch citing the *New York Herald Tribune*, Republican Senator Bridges rebuked the U.S. military command for concealing the number of American military casualties in the Korean War. He said, "If we knew the whole truth about the number of our troops killed and wounded in the Korean War, we would be frightened. The number of casualties is being concealed. The truth about what we have done in Korea in recent weeks is terribly tragic, and once it is revealed to us, it will be chilling."
　　According to a United Press dispatch, after Bridges' rebuke, a U.S. Army Department spokesman on the 10th still sophistically claimed that it "has not concealed the casualty figures for the Korean War." However, when someone mentioned Bridges' condemnation, he had to declare: "MacArthur's headquarters is accelerating the confirmation and announcement of casualty figures."
　　According to Agence France-Presse, American radio commentator Pearson admitted in the *American Mirror* on the 7th that U.S. official communiqués only acknowledged 25 percent of the U.S. deaths and injuries in Korea.

### 侵朝美军死伤惨重  美帝隐瞒真实情况引起内部攻击

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　侵朝美军死伤惨重
    美帝隐瞒真实情况引起内部攻击
    【新华社十四日讯】美国侵略者隐瞒美军在朝鲜作战中的死伤人数，已引起其内部的攻击。据塔斯社电引《纽约先驱论坛报》消息：共和党参议员勃里奇斥责美军统帅部隐瞒美军在朝鲜作战中的死伤人数。他说：“如果我们知道了关于我军在朝鲜战争中死伤人数的全部真实情况，我们一定会被吓得发抖。伤亡人数是被隐瞒起来了。我军的死伤惨重得可怕，最近几个星期来，我们在朝鲜所干的事情的真相，一旦对我们公布出来，那会使人胆寒的。”
    据合众社电称，在勃里奇的斥责之后，美国陆军部发言人于十日仍狡赖地宣称：它“没有隐瞒朝鲜战争的伤亡数字”。但是，当有人提到勃里奇的谴责时，他又不得不宣称：“麦克阿瑟总部正加速证实与宣布伤亡人数。”
    另据法新社报道，美国广播评论员皮尔逊七日在《美国镜报》上承认：美国官方公报只承认了美国在朝鲜死伤的人数百分之二十五。
