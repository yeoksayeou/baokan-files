Title: Finance Committee Releases Measures: Preferential Treatment for Unemployed Workers Taking Trains Home to Participate in Production
Author:
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 2

　　Finance Committee Releases Measures
    Preferential Treatment for Unemployed Workers Taking Trains Home to Participate in Production
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] To alleviate the difficulties faced by unemployed workers in various cities returning to their hometowns to participate in production, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government issued the "Measures for Preferential Treatment for Unemployed Workers in Various Cities Returning Home to Participate in Production by Train" on July 28th. The measures stipulate that during the months of August and September this year, all unemployed workers in various cities who have been reviewed and qualified according to the Interim Measures for Relieving Unemployed Workers and have received registration certificates issued by the Unemployment Relief Offices shall be entitled to a 60% discount on train fares when taking trains home to participate in production; family members accompanying them shall also be granted the same preferential treatment. Unemployed workers returning home to participate in production must hold an introduction document from the unemployment relief committee of each city, proving that they are indeed returning home to participate in production, when purchasing preferential tickets.

### 财委会发布办法  优待失业工人乘坐火车还乡生产

1950-08-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　财委会发布办法
    优待失业工人乘坐火车还乡生产
    【新华社六日讯】为减少各城市失业工人返乡生产时的困难，中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会于七月二十八日发布“各城市失业工人还乡生产乘坐火车优待办法”。办法规定：在今年八、九两月内，凡各城市依照救济失业工人暂行办法经审查合格领有失业工人救济处发给登记证之失业工人，乘坐火车还乡生产时，票价一律按四折优待；眷属随行者，亦予同样优待。还乡生产之失业工人购买优待票时，须持有各城市失业工人救济委员会之介绍文件，证明确为还乡生产者。
