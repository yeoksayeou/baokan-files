Title: Korean People's Army Repulses Enemy Counter-Attack; Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units Shoot Down Five More American Planes
Author: 
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war bulletin on the morning of the 25th that People's Army units had repulsed enemy counter-attacks on all fronts and continued to launch attacks.

American planes continue to carry out barbaric bombing raids on the northern and southern regions of the Republic, destroying peaceful towns and villages and slaughtering innocent residents. People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units bravely engaged the invading enemy planes, inflicting heavy losses on them.

On the 21st, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down an American fighter plane that invaded the Nampo area; on the 23rd, they shot down two American fighter planes that invaded the Kimchon area.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war bulletin on the evening of the 25th that People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the enemy on all fronts. American troops and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army concentrated all their forces to launch a counter-attack, but suffered setbacks under the fierce blows of the People's Army.

On the front lines, the morale of the American troops is declining daily. In the Waegwan area, a squad of thirteen men from the 5th Artillery Regiment of the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division, led by their squad leader, surrendered to People's Army units with all their weapons.

On August 24th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units engaged American bombers that invaded Pyongyang, shooting down two enemy B-29 bombers.

### 朝人民军击退敌反攻  高射炮部队又击落美机五架

1950-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军击退敌反攻
    高射炮部队又击落美机五架
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十五日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上击退敌军的反攻，并继续展开攻击。
    美机对共和国南北部各地区继续进行野蛮的轰炸，破坏和平城乡，屠杀无辜居民。人民军高射炮部队勇敢地迎击来袭敌机，使敌机遭受巨大损失。
    人民军高射炮部队，于二十一日击落侵袭南浦地区的美战斗机一架；二十三日击落侵袭金川方面的美战斗机二架。
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十五日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，与敌人继续进行猛烈的战斗。美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，集中全部兵力进行反攻，但在人民军的猛烈打击下，遭受挫折。
    在前线，美军士气逐日低落，倭馆方面，美军第一骑兵师第五炮兵团所属的一个班十三人，在班长率领下，携带全部武器，向人民军部队投降。
    八月二十四日，人民军高射炮部队迎击来袭平壤的美军轰炸机，击落敌Ｂ——２９式轰炸机二架。
