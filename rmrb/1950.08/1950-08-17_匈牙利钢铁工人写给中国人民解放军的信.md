Title: Letter from Hungarian Steelworkers to the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 3

　　People's Garden

　　Letter from Hungarian Steelworkers to the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    Editor's Note: The letter from Hungarian steelworkers to the Chinese People's Liberation Army was sent on July 14th. However, due to travel delays, we only received it recently.
    Dear Comrades,
    As you celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of your army, all the men and women workers of the A II factory of the Mátyás Rákosi United Steel Works pay their respects with warm affection to the glorious Chinese People's Liberation Army! We congratulate you on liberating your motherland from the shackles of imperialism. At the same time, we guarantee that together with our advanced and great ally and defender of world peace, the Soviet Union, we will build a socialist society.
    Long live the glorious Chinese People's Liberation Army!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!
    Long live the great Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries that are friendly to the Soviet Union!
    Long live the great Stalin, the genius leader of the world's proletariat!
      Two hundred and twenty-nine men and women workers from the United Steel Works in Sunte Sepete, Budapest, Hungary (signed)
        Signature Collector: (Hungary) Johann Fogorasi
              July 14th

### 匈牙利钢铁工人写给中国人民解放军的信

1950-08-17
第3版()
专栏：人民园地

　　匈牙利钢铁工人写给中国人民解放军的信
    编者按：匈牙利钢铁工人给中国人民解放军的信，是在七月十四日发出的。但因旅途的耽搁，我们在最近才接到。
    亲爱的同志们：
    在你们庆祝你们二十三周年建军纪念日的时候，玛提阿拉可西联合钢铁厂ＡⅡ厂所有的男女工人们以热烈的友爱向光荣的中国人民解放军致敬！我们祝贺你们把你们的祖国从帝国主义的束缚下解放出来。同时，我们保证和我们先进的伟大盟国和世界和平保卫者苏联一起，建设起社会主义的社会来。
    光荣的中国人民解放军万岁！
    毛泽东同志万岁！
    伟大的苏联以及和苏联友好相处的人民民主国家万岁！
    全世界无产阶级的天才领导者伟大的斯大林万岁！
      匈牙利布达佩斯孙特赛培特地方联合钢铁厂男女工人二百二十九人（签名）
        签名收集者：（匈牙利）约翰·佛戈拉西
              七月十四日
