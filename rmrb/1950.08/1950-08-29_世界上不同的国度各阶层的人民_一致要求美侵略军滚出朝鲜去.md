Title: People of All Strata in Different Countries of the World Unanimously Demand That the U.S. Aggressor Troops Get Out of Korea

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: Comprehensive TASS news: The National Congress of the National Front of Democratic Germany, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Greater Berlin Peace Committee, the German Cultural Association, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Anti-Fascist Fighters Alliance, the Executive Committee of the Soviet-Bulgarian Association Federation, the Sofia Cultural Workers Club, the Czechoslovak Orthodox Church and other various churches, the French National Railway Workers Federation, the Executive Committee of the Italian National Guerrilla Association, and the National Anti-Fascist Association have all passed resolutions or sent telegrams to Malik, Chairman of the Security Council, and UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie, expressing their angry protests against the bestial crimes of the United States' aggression against Korea and the barbaric bombing of peaceful cities and villages in Korea by American aircraft, and demanding that all foreign troops conducting a war of conquest against the Korean people withdraw from Korea.

The National Congress of the National Front of Democratic Germany pointed out in its resolution: "The events in Korea are a warning to us, and they task us with selflessly defending peace and exposing and thwarting the war policy pursued by the aggressors." The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany emphasized "Korea is for Koreans! U.S. troops get out of Korea!" The protest telegram from the Greater Berlin Peace Committee expressed extreme indignation at the U.S. command's plan to destroy ten of the largest cities in Korea with intensive bombing. Sofia's cultural workers demanded an immediate halt to the barbaric bombing that is destroying the lives and cultural treasures of the Korean people. People of all strata in Hungary deeply resent the atrocities committed by U.S. troops in Korea. Construction workers in Budapest stated that they would intensify their work for peace and increase labor productivity as a protest against the atrocities committed by U.S. troops in Korea.

The French National Railway Workers Federation, representing 247,000 members, protested the indiscriminate bombing of Korea by American aircraft and demanded the immediate withdrawal of U.S. aggressor troops from Korea. The Italian National Guerrilla Association and the National Anti-Fascist Association said in a telegram to the Security Council that the United States' actions in Korea were "an insult to civilization."

### 世界上不同的国度各阶层的人民  一致要求美侵略军滚出朝鲜去

1950-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界上不同的国度各阶层的人民
    一致要求美侵略军滚出朝鲜去
    【新华社二十八日讯】综合塔斯社消息：民主德国全国阵线全国代表大会、德国统一社会党中央委员会、大柏林和平委员会、德国文化联盟、保加利亚反法西斯战士联盟中央委员会、保苏协会联合会执行委员会、索非亚文化工作者俱乐部、捷克斯洛伐克正教教会和其他各种教会、法国全国铁路员工联合会、意大利全国游击队协会执行委员会和全国反法西斯主义者协会，都纷纷通过决议或致电安理会主席马立克和联合国秘书长赖伊，对美国侵略朝鲜和美国飞机野蛮轰炸朝鲜的和平城市和乡村的兽性罪行，表示愤怒的抗议，并要求一切对朝鲜人民进行征服战争的外国军队撤出朝鲜。
    民主德国全国阵线全国代表大会在决议中指出：“朝鲜的事件对于我们乃是一个警告。同时责成我们忘我地保卫和平，揭发并挫败侵略者奉行的战争政策。”德国统一社会党中央委员会强调“朝鲜是朝鲜人的！美军滚出朝鲜去！”大柏林和平委员会的抗议电中，对美国统帅部计划以密集轰炸炸毁朝鲜十座最大的城市极表愤慨。索非亚文化工作者要求立即阻止毁灭朝鲜人民生命与文化宝藏的野蛮轰炸。匈牙利各阶层人民对美军在朝鲜暴行深切痛恨。布达佩斯的建筑工人们表示将加紧为和平而工作，提高劳动生产率，作为对美军在朝暴行的抗议。
    法国全国铁路员工联合会代表二十四万七千名会员，抗议美国飞机滥炸朝鲜，要求美国侵略军队立即自朝鲜撤退。意大利全国游击队协会及全国反法西斯主义者协会在致安理会电中说：美国在朝鲜的行动是“对文明的侮辱”。
