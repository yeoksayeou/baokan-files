　　Production Plan for Matches, Oils and Fats, and Cigarettes for the Second Half of 1950

　　At present, the state-owned economy is moving towards a planned economy, and private enterprises also hope that the government will help them gradually embark on the road of planned production. The match, oil and fat, and cigarette industries have, through professional meetings of public and private enterprises convened by the government's competent departments, separately determined the production plans for the second half of this year:
