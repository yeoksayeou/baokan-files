Title: Polish Publishing Industry Thrives: Nearly Sixty Million Books Printed in First Half of Year
Author:
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 4
　　
　　Polish Publishing Industry Thrives
    Nearly Sixty Million Books Printed in First Half of Year
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] TASS, Warsaw, August 5th: The Polish News Agency, citing statistics from the Central Publishing Bureau, reported that in the first six months of this year, Poland published 2,542 books and pamphlets, totaling 59 million copies.

    One million copies of Marxist-Leninist classics were printed.

    Works by Soviet writers were printed in 2.5 million copies, of which 70% were literary works. The number of works printed from the German Democratic Republic has also increased substantially.

    In the second quarter of this year, the Republic Publishing Bureau published five million copies of school textbooks.

### 波兰出版事业蓬勃发展  上半年印行书籍近六千万册

1950-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰出版事业蓬勃发展
    上半年印行书籍近六千万册
    【新华社讯】塔斯社华沙五日电：波兰通讯社根据中央出版局的统计数字报道称：在本年最初六个月内，波兰出版了二千五百四十二种书籍和小册子，共印行五千九百万册。
    马列主义经典的著作印行了一百万册。
    苏联作家的作品印行了二百五十万册，其中百分之七十是文学著作。德意志民主共和国的著作，印行的数目也有大量的增加。
    在本年第二季，共和国出版局出版了五百万册学校教科书。
