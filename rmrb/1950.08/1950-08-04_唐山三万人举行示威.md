Title: 30,000 People Hold Demonstration in Tangshan
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 1

　　[News report] Over 30,000 soldiers and civilians in Tangshan City solemnly held a rally on August 1st to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and to demonstrate against U.S. imperialist aggression in Taiwan and Korea. Giant portraits of Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Marshal Stalin, and General Kim Il-sung were hung high at the venue. The five-star national flag and the Korean flag fluttered throughout the entire area. The conference was declared open after a 23-gun salute. Commander Han of a certain PLA unit stationed in Tangshan pointed out in his speech: The PLA's 23 years of development, from small to large, from weak to strong, is inseparable from the correct leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and the great unity of the people across the country under the banner of Chairman Mao. He encouraged all troops stationed there to continue to promote the spirit of hard struggle of the past 23 years, stand on the battle post, study Mao Zedong Thought, study modern military science, study culture, defend the national defense, and fight to the end for the independence, autonomy, and prosperity of New China, and for the lasting peace of the world. Li Liguo, Secretary of the Tangshan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, called on the people of Tangshan to do a better job in production and work to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan and to support the heroic struggle of the Korean people and army. Subsequently, Wang Yuqi, a people's hero from a certain unit of the stationed troops, Korean friend Kim Young, and representatives from workers, farmers, military families, students, and business circles all spoke at the meeting. Finally, the women's federation representative, amidst the melodious military music, presented flowers to the People's Liberation Army and passed a telegram from the conference to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Korean General Kim Il-sung. After the meeting, all the soldiers and civilians began a demonstration parade, which stretched for seven or eight li.

### 唐山三万人举行示威

1950-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　唐山三万人举行示威
    【本报讯】唐山市军民三万余人于八月一日隆重举行庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十三周年及反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会。会场高悬毛主席、朱总司令、斯大林大元帅、金日成将军的巨像。五星国旗、朝鲜国旗飘扬全场。大会在礼炮连鸣二十三响后宣布开幕。驻唐某军韩军长在讲话中指出：人民解放军二十三年来的建军，由小到大，由弱到强，是与共产党、毛主席的正确领导及在毛主席旗帜下的全国人民大团结分不开的。他勉励全体驻军今后要继续发扬二十三年来的艰苦奋斗作风，站在战斗的岗位上，学习毛泽东思想，学习近代化军事科学，学习文化，保卫国防，为新中国的独立、自主、繁荣，为全世界的持久和平奋斗到底。中共唐山市委书记李力果号召唐市人民要把生产、工作搞的更好，来支援人民解放军解放台湾和声援朝鲜军民的英勇斗争。接着，驻军某部人民功臣王玉岐、朝鲜友人金荣，以及工人、农民、军属、学生、工商界代表等都纷纷在大会上讲了话。最后，由妇联代表，在悠扬的军乐声中，向人民解放军献花，并通过大会给毛主席、朱总司令、朝鲜金日成将军的通电。会后全体军民开始示威游行，游行行列长达七、八里。
