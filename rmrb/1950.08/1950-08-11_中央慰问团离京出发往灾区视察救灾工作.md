Title: Central慰问团 Departs Beijing to Inspect Disaster Relief Work in Disaster Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Central慰问团 (comfort mission) to慰问 (express sympathy to) disaster victims and inspect disaster relief work in disaster areas such as northern Anhui and Henan, departed Beijing on the 9th. The团 (mission) is headed by Peng Zemin, Vice Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council, with Pu Huarin, Director of the General Office of the Supervisory Committee of the China People's Relief Association, and Gan Zhongdou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central Ministry of Internal Affairs, as Deputy Heads. In addition, there are also State Council Counselor Zhang Yuanshan, Jiao Shizhai, Financial and Economic Commission Specialist He Weicheng, Political and Legal Committee Counselor Qiu Elun, and personnel sent by the Central Ministry of Water Resources, Ministry of Health, Ministry of Agriculture, All-China Women's Federation and other units.

### 中央慰问团离京出发往灾区视察救灾工作

1950-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央慰问团离京出发往灾区视察救灾工作
    【新华社十日讯】前往皖北、河南等灾区慰问灾民并视察救灾工作的中央慰问团已于九日离京出发。该团由政务院政法委员会副主任彭泽民为团长，中国人民救济总会监察委员会办公厅主任浦化人及中央内务部办公厅副主任甘重斗为副团长，此外尚有政务院参事章元善、焦实斋，财经委员会专员贺维城，政法委员会参事丘锷仑，及中央水利部、卫生部、农业部、全国妇联等单位派出人员。
