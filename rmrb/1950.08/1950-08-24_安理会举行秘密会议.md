Title: Security Council Holds Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 22nd (TASS): Security Council President Malik released a communique after the Security Council members held a secret meeting on the afternoon of the 21st, which was distributed by the United Nations Secretariat. The communique is as follows: "The Security Council members informally exchanged views on the issue of inviting representatives from North and South Korea on the 21st. No agreement was reached on this issue."

### 安理会举行秘密会议

1950-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　安理会举行秘密会议
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社纽约二十二日讯：安理会主席马立克在安理会理事国于二十一日下午举行秘密会议后发表公报，由联合国秘书处散发。公报如下：“安理会理事国之间于二十一日就邀请南北朝鲜代表的问题非正式地交换了意见。关于这一问题并未获致任何协议。”
