Title: US Imperialism Plots to Seize Indonesian Military Bases, Cochrane Reportedly Consulted with Sukarno and Hatta
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 4

　　US Imperialism Plots to Seize Indonesian Military Bases
    Cochrane Reportedly Consulted with Sukarno and Hatta
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd Dispatch] American imperialism is actively carrying out activities to seize military bases in Indonesia. According to a United Press report from Jakarta, July 31st: Recently, the US Ambassador to Indonesia, Cochrane, held a series of consultations with Indonesian President Sukarno and Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hatta, requesting to "lease" three military bases in the northeastern part of the Indonesian archipelago. Sukarno and Hatta replied to Cochrane that "If the United States supports Indonesia's claims to Dutch New Guinea, it will have a favorable impact on the United States' request for Indonesian bases."

### 美帝图攫取印尼军事基地  柯契伦曾与苏加诺、哈达会商

1950-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝图攫取印尼军事基地
    柯契伦曾与苏加诺、哈达会商
    【新华社三日讯】美帝国主义正积极进行在印尼攫取军事基地的活动。据合众社雅加达七月三十一日讯：最近美国驻印尼大使柯契伦曾与印尼总统苏加诺、总理兼外长哈达举行一连串的会商，要求“租借”印尼群岛东北部的三个军事基地。苏加诺与哈达回答柯契伦说：“美国如果支持印尼对荷属新几内亚的要求，将会对美国对印尼基地的要求，发生良好的影响。”
