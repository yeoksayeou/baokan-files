Title: Labor People's Cultural Palace to Host Grand Joint Celebration for Soldiers, Workers, and Students Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 6

The Beijing Youth Summer Life Committee, the General Political Department of the Military Commission Youth Department, the North China Military Region Political Department, the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League Beijing Municipal Committee, the Beijing Students' Federation, the Beijing Sports Branch, and the Labor People's Cultural Palace will jointly host a social gathering and garden party at the Labor People's Cultural Palace on August 13 (Sunday) to enrich the cultural and recreational life of the troops, workers, and students, and to strengthen mutual understanding and ties. Approximately 35,000 soldiers, workers, students, and some comrades from government organizations will participate.

The celebration will begin at 3:00 PM. In addition to exhibitions of weapons and achievements in production and study, as well as photos of combat, production, and study life, there will be over 140 entertainment, dance, and competitive game stations, as well as sports activities such as basketball and volleyball games, tug-of-war competitions, and skills demonstrations.

On the morning of the same day, there will also be a symposium for soldiers, workers, and students to introduce the conditions of combat, production, and study life. Many combat heroes and labor heroes will participate.

	(Summer Committee Publicity and Communications Group)

### 劳动人民文化宫明日战士工人学生大联欢

1950-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　劳动人民文化宫明日战士工人学生大联欢
    为了丰富部队、工人、学生的文娱生活，加强彼此间的了解与联系，北京市青年暑期生活委员会、军委总政治部青年部、华北军区政治部、市总工会、团市工委、市学联、京体育分会、劳动人民文化宫等单位将在八月十三日（星期日）在劳动人民文化宫举办联欢游园大会。届时将有三万五千战士、工人、学生和一部分机关同志参加。
    联欢从下午三点开始，除展览武器及生产、学习成绩和战斗、生产、学习生活照片外，并有娱乐、舞蹈、竞赛游戏站一百四十余处，篮、排球赛、拔河比赛、技巧表演等体育活动。
    当日上午还有战士、工人和学生的座谈会，介绍战斗、生产、学习生活的情况。其中将有许多战斗英雄、劳动英雄参加。
          （暑委会宣通组）
