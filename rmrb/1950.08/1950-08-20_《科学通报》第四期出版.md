*Scientia* (Science Bulletin) Issue 4 Published

　　*Scientia* (Science Bulletin), compiled and printed by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was published on August 15th. The contents include: "Can Human Power Overcome Hail?" by Zhu Kezhen; "Soil Science and Agricultural Construction" by Feng Zhaolin; "Soviet Genetics - The Focus of Debate" by Nuzhdin; "Stalin and Michurin's Agricultural Biology" by Lysenko; a brief account of the First Plenary Session of the Academy of Sciences; recent developments at the Northeast Institute of Science; recent developments at the Institute of Modern History; how the old farmer Huang Xingwang of Wayaobao masters the development stages of tobacco leaves; and several issues that should be paid attention to when exploring oil in northern Shaanxi.

### 《科学通报》第四期出版

1950-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　《科学通报》第四期出版
    中国科学院编印的《科学通报》第四期已于八月十五日出版，内容有竺可桢的《人力能克服冰雹吗？》冯兆林的《土壤科学与农业建设》，努日金的《苏联的遗传学——争论的焦点》，李森科的《斯大林和米邱林的农业生物学》及科学院第一次院务会议简述，东北科学研究所最近动态，近代史研究所近况，瓦窑堡老农黄兴旺是怎样掌握烟叶的发育阶段，勘探陕北石油应注意的几个问题等。
