Title: Expressing the Firm Will to Defend World Peace: 1,460,000 People in Beijing Have Signed; Over 1,200,000 People in Xinjiang Province Have Signed
Author:
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 1

　　Expressing the Firm Will to Defend World Peace
　　Beijing has one million, four hundred and sixty thousand signatures
　　Xinjiang Province has reached one million and two hundred thousand signatures
　　[Our Newspaper's Report] According to Xinhua News Agency: 1,464,743 people from all walks of life in Beijing have signed the Peace Declaration. This includes 71,604 industrial workers, 57,056 students, 618,388 urban residents, 332,260 suburban residents (most of whom are farmers), 45,157 industrial and commercial figures, 159,500 cadres from the Central People's Government and directly subordinate organizations of Beijing, members of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and 180,778 people from all walks of life who signed at the People's Daily signing station and through reader correspondence. Thus far, the total number of peace signatories in Beijing has reached approximately 70% of the city's total population.
　　People from all walks of life in Beijing expressed their firm will to defend world peace in the peace signature campaign. 3,509 peace signatories from the Beijing Clothing Factory, accounting for more than 95% of the factory's total employees. All the shop employees in the photography industry in Beijing's Second District signed their names and engraved a seal with the six characters "Defend World Peace," which was printed on every photo bag to promote peace signing to customers. Their actions influenced workers and capitalists in fourteen other industries, who also adopted the same method for peace propaganda.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 18th] The signature campaign for the defense of world peace in Xinjiang Province has expanded from larger cities to small and medium-sized cities and rural areas. As of August 15th, 1,249,448 people of all ethnic groups and from all walks of life in the entire region have signed, accounting for 23.5% of the province's total population. Among them, the Uyghur and Kazakh people account for more than 70% of the signatories. In Dihua City, the number of people of all ethnic groups and from all walks of life who have signed has reached 91,178, accounting for more than 76% of the city's total population. During the "Week of Opposition to American Aggression against Taiwan and Korea" from July 26th to August 2nd, the Dihua branch of the China Peace Committee, the Xinjiang branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Xinjiang People's Democratic League, and various people's organizations, troops, and schools organized more than 40 propaganda teams, using ethnic song and dance, street lectures, and other methods to carry out propaganda, enabling people of all ethnic groups and from all walks of life to further recognize the aggressive crimes of American imperialism in undermining world peace. As a result, more than 10,000 people enthusiastically signed the Peace Declaration in various languages. The peace signature campaign in various parts of southern Xinjiang has penetrated into the countryside. According to incomplete statistics from the three administrative regions of Kashgar, Shache, and Hotan alone, the number of signatories has reached 770,587, most of whom are Uyghur farmers. Eighty percent of the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xinjiang have signed.

### 表示保卫世界和平的坚决意志  北京已有一百四十六万人签名  新疆省签名人数已达一百二十余万人

1950-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　表示保卫世界和平的坚决意志
    北京已有一百四十六万人签名
    新疆省签名人数已达一百二十余万人
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：北京各界人民已有一百四十六万四千七百四十三人在和平宣言上签名。其中包括产业工人七万一千六百零四人，学生五万七千零五十六人，城区市民六十一万八千三百八十八人，郊区市民三十三万二千二百六十人（其中大部分是农民），工商界人士四万五千一百五十七人，中央人民政府及北京市各直属机关干部，各民主党派和各人民团体人员十五万九千五百人，在人民日报签名站和读者通信签名的各界人民十八万零七百七十八人。至此，北京和平签名者总数已达全市总人口百分之七十左右。
    北京各界人民在和平签名运动中表示了保卫世界和平的坚决意志。北京被服厂和平签名者有三千五百零九人，占全厂职工人数百分之九十五以上。北京市第二区照像业全体店员工人都签了名，并且刻了一个书写着“保卫世界和平”六个字的图章，印在每一个装照片的纸袋上，向顾客们进行和平签名宣传。他们的行动影响了其他十四个行业的工人和资本家，也采用同样方式进行和平宣传。
    【新华社迪化十八日电】新疆省的保卫世界和平签名运动已由较大的城市扩展到各中小城市和农村。截至八月十五日止，全疆各族各界人民签名者已有一百二十四万九千四百四十八人，占全省人口总数的百分之二十三点五。其中维吾尔族和哈萨克族人民占了签名人数的百分之七十以上。迪化市各族各界人民签名的已达九万一千一百七十八人，占全市人口总数的百分之七十六以上。该市在七月二十六日至八月二日的反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动周中，中国保卫世界和平大会委员会迪化分会、中苏友好协会新疆分会、新疆人民民主同盟总会及各人民团体、部队、学校等共组织了四十余个宣传队，采用民族歌舞、街头讲演等方式进行宣传，使各族各界人民进一步认识了美帝国主义破坏世界和平的侵略罪行，因此有一万余人踊跃地用各种不同文字在和平宣言上签了名。南疆各地的和平签名运动已深入农村，仅根据喀什、莎车、和阗三个行政区的不完全统计，签名人数已达七十七万零五百八十七人，其中大多数为维吾尔族农民。驻新疆的人民解放军，已有百分之八十的指战员签了名。
