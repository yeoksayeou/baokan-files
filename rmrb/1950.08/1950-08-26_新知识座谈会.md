Title: New Knowledge Forum

Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 8

　　New Knowledge Forum
    The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association is scheduled to hold its 84th New Knowledge Forum at the Xiehe Auditorium at 7:30 PM on Saturday, August 26th. Professor Wang Tieya, Deputy Director of the Research Committee of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and Chairman of the Department of Political Science at Peking University, is invited to speak on "The United Nations Problem." Anyone wishing to attend the lecture may be introduced by members, friends, or organizations to obtain a ticket from the China Democratic National Construction Association in advance.
     (Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会

1950-08-26
第8版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会定于八月二十六日（星期六）下午七时三十分，在协和礼堂举行第八十四次新知识座谈会，敦请中国人民外交学会研究委员会副主任、北京大学政治系主任王铁崖教授主讲“联合国问题”。凡愿参加听讲的，可由会员会友或机关团体介绍，先到民建会领取听讲券。
     （桢）
