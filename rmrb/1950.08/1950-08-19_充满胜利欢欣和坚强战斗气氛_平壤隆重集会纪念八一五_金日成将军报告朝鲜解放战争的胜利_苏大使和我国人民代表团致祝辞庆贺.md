Title: Pyongyang Solemnly Gathers to Commemorate August 15th, Filled with Victory and Strong Fighting Atmosphere. General Kim Il-sung Reports on the Victory of the Korean War of Liberation. Soviet Ambassador and Our People's Delegation Offer Congratulations.
Author:
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th—Pyongyang citizens from all walks of life solemnly held a rally on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation.

Attending were more than a thousand representatives from Pyongyang, including workers, farmers, soldiers, students, women, staff, cultural and artistic workers, representatives of various democratic parties and social organizations, as well as delegations from the newly liberated areas. Foreign guests included embassy staff from the Soviet Union, China, Hungary, and Mongolia stationed in Korea, the Chinese People's Delegation, members of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation, and the Hungarian medical team.

When the presidium of the conference, headed by Korean people's leader General Kim Il-sung, appeared on the rostrum, all attendees stood up and responded with stormy applause and cheers.

After the conference began, representatives of the Korean working people first presented flowers to General Kim Il-sung and Chairman Kim Tu-bong as a tribute. They then successively presented flowers to Soviet Ambassador Sidorov, Chinese People's Delegation leader Guo Moruo, Chinese Ambassador Ni Zhiliang, Hungarian Minister Sandor, and Mongolian Ambassador Zamsolem.

Amidst warm applause and cheers, General Kim Il-sung began his report on the situation in North and South Korea over the past five years and the heroic Korean people's victorious and just war of national liberation. He announced that the heroic Korean People's Army, with the support of all the Korean people, in just one and a half months since the turn to the counteroffensive, had already liberated 90 percent of the land and 92 percent of the population in the southern half of Korea, killing, wounding, and capturing a total of 70,000 invading troops of American imperialists and their stooge, the Syngman Rhee puppet army.

Subsequently, Soviet Ambassador Sidorov, Chinese People's Delegation leader Guo Moruo, Hungarian Minister Sandor, and Mongolian Ambassador Zamsolem delivered congratulatory speeches. The speakers unanimously expressed warm congratulations and respect to the Korean People's Army and its wise leader, General Kim Il-sung.

Amidst cheers and applause, our People's Delegation presented General Kim Il-sung with a silk banner inscribed with "Long Live the Victory of the Korean People," and presented gifts to the heroic Korean People's Army. Deputy leader Li Lisan delivered a congratulatory speech when presenting the banner and gifts.

Finally, amidst thunderous applause, the entire audience stood up and passed a letter of respect to the leader of the working people of the world and the Korean people's dearest friend, Grand Marshal Stalin.

The conference proceeded throughout in an atmosphere filled with the joy of victory, strong fighting will, and internationalist spirit.

### 充满胜利欢欣和坚强战斗气氛  平壤隆重集会纪念八一五  金日成将军报告朝鲜解放战争的胜利  苏大使和我国人民代表团致祝辞庆贺

1950-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　充满胜利欢欣和坚强战斗气氛
    平壤隆重集会纪念八一五
    金日成将军报告朝鲜解放战争的胜利
    苏大使和我国人民代表团致祝辞庆贺
    【新华社平壤十八日电】平壤市各界人民已于十五日晚隆重举行庆祝“八·一五”解放五周年大会。
    出席者有平壤地区的工人、农民、战士、学生、妇女、职员、文化艺术工作者、各民主政党、社会团体的代表，以及来自新解放区的代表团共千余人。外宾出席者有驻朝鲜的苏联、中国、匈牙利、蒙古等国的大使馆人员、中国人民代表团、苏联工会代表团与匈牙利医疗队的团员们。
    当以朝鲜人民领袖金日成将军为首的大会主席团出现在主席台上时，与会者全体起立，报以暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。
    大会开始后，首由朝鲜劳动人民代表向金日成将军和金枓奉委员长献花致敬。他们接着依次向苏联大使西地科夫、中国人民代表团团长郭沫若、中国大使倪志亮、匈牙利公使鲜道尔及蒙古大使扎姆索伦献花。
    金日成将军在热烈的掌声与欢呼声中开始了关于五年来南北朝鲜形势和英勇的朝鲜人民胜利进行正义的民族解放战争的报告。他宣布英雄的朝鲜人民军，在全体朝鲜人民的支援下，在转入反攻后不过一个半月的期间，业已解放了朝鲜南半部百分之九十的土地和百分之九十二的人口，毙、伤、俘美帝国主义者的侵略军队及其走狗李承晚伪军共达七万名。
    继由苏联大使西地科夫、中国人民代表团团长郭沫若、匈牙利公使鲜道尔、蒙古大使扎姆索伦致祝辞。祝贺者一致对朝鲜人民军及其英明领袖金日成将军表示热烈的庆贺与敬意。
    我国人民代表团在欢呼声和掌声中，向金日成将军献赠锦旗一面，上书“朝鲜人民胜利万岁”，并向英勇的朝鲜人民军献赠礼物。李立三副团长在献旗、献礼时，发表了祝辞。
    大会最后在雷动的掌声中，全场起立通过了给全世界劳动人民的领袖和朝鲜人民最亲爱的朋友斯大林大元帅的致敬电。
    大会始终在充满胜利的欢欣、坚强的战斗意志及国际主义精神的气氛中进行。
