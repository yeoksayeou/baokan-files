Title: Central and Southern Military and Political Committee Allocates Grain to Relieve Henan Flood Victims; Central Disaster Area慰问团 Arrives in Kaifeng
Author:
Date: 1950-08-23

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 17th: The Civil Affairs Department of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, in order to relieve the victims of the flood-stricken areas of Henan Province, has recently decided to allocate another 10 million jin of grain, in addition to the 15 million jin of relief grain previously allocated to the province, for use as relief grain and seed grain loans. At the same time, the Civil Affairs, Agriculture and Forestry, Water Conservancy, and Health Departments and the Counselor's Office of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee have jointly formed a慰问组, carrying a large number of medicines, and set off on the 14th of this month to Henan to cooperate with the Central慰问团 in visiting the seriously affected areas of Huangchuan and Xinyang to慰问 the disaster victims and treat their illnesses.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 18th: The Central People's Government and the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee's disaster area慰问团 arrived in Kaifeng on August 15th. Peng Zemin, head of the Central Disaster Area慰问团 and Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and Liu Fei, Minister of Water Conservancy of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, listened to the report of the Henan Provincial People's Government on the disaster situation and disaster relief measures, and gave specific instructions. The慰问团 will depart on the 18th to visit the disaster victims in the severely affected areas of Huangchuan and Xinyang Prefectures, and conduct in-depth inspections to understand the disaster situation and disaster relief work.

### 中南军政委员会拨发粮食救济河南灾民  中央灾区慰问团抵开封

1950-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中南军政委员会拨发粮食救济河南灾民
    中央灾区慰问团抵开封
    【新华社汉口十七日电】中南军政委员会民政部为救济河南省受水灾地区的灾民，继前此拨付该省救济粮一千五百万斤之后，最近将再增拨粮食一千万斤，作为救济粮和贷种粮。同时，中南军政委员会民政、农林、水利、卫生四部及参事室各单位，更合组一慰问组，携带大批医药，于本月十四日首途赴豫配合中央慰问团前往潢川、信阳等重灾区慰问灾民，并为灾民治疗疾病。
    【新华社开封十八日电】中央人民政府及中南军政委员会的灾区慰问团已于八月十五日抵达开封。中央灾区慰问团团长、中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会副主任彭泽民和中南军政委员会水利部部长刘斐等，听取了河南省人民政府关于灾情及救灾措施的报告，并予以具体指示。慰问团将于十八日分赴潢川、信阳两专区的重灾地区慰问灾民，并深入视察，以了解受灾情况和救灾工作。
