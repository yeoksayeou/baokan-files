Title: Central Nationalities Affairs Commission Welcomes Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission held a gathering on the 10th to welcome the Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation. Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council, Dong Biwu, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, and Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Zhang Zhizhong, also attended the meeting. Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, delivered an opening speech, representing the government in welcoming the visiting delegation to Beijing. He said that the staff of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission were willing to learn much knowledge from the representatives of the visiting delegation, and hoped that the delegation would reflect the problems and opinions existing among the various ethnic groups in the Northwest to the central government as much as possible. Ma Fuchen, head of the visiting delegation, and deputy heads Niza Buding, and Tibetan representative Ji Wumei, among others, stated in their speeches that they were willing to work under the banner of Chairman Mao to realize the sincere unity of all ethnic groups in China.

### 中央民族事务委员会欢迎西北各族参观团

1950-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央民族事务委员会欢迎西北各族参观团
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府民族事务委员会于十日集会欢迎西北各民族参观团。政务院董必武副总理、郭沫若副总理、西北军政委员会张治中副主席也到会。民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉致开会词时，代表政府对参观团来京表示欢迎。并说中央民族事务委员会的工作人员愿向参观团各位代表学习许多知识，希望参观团把西北各民族中所存在的问题和意见尽量向中央反映。参观团团长马辅臣，副团长尼扎布丁、藏族人士计吾美等在讲话中，都表示愿在毛主席的旗帜下，为实现中国各民族的精诚团结而努力。
