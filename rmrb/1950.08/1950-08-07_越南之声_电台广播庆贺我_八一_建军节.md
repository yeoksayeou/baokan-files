Title: "Voice of Vietnam" Radio Broadcast Celebrates Our "August 1st" Army Day

Author:

Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam dispatch from Vietnam News Agency, 3rd: "Voice of Vietnam" radio station broadcast on the 1st, celebrating the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After recounting the tremendous development of the Chinese People's Liberation Army since the Nanchang Uprising in 1927, the radio commentator expressed confidence that the Chinese People's Liberation Army will surely liberate Taiwan and Tibet, and will contribute to the defense of world peace.

### “越南之声”电台广播庆贺我“八一”建军节

1950-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　“越南之声”电台广播庆贺我“八一”建军节
    【新华社三日讯】据越南通讯社北越讯：“越南之声”电台在一日的广播中庆贺中国人民解放军的建军节。电台评论员在叙述自一九二七年南昌起义以来中国人民解放军所获得巨大发展以后，表示确信中国人民解放军定能解放台湾和西藏，并将对保卫世界和平有所贡献。
