Title: Pyongyang Newspapers Publish Full Text of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's "August 1st" Speech
Author:
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 5th. The "Democratic Korea" newspaper, the government organ of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the "Labor News" newspaper, the central organ of the Workers' Party of Korea, both published the full text of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's August 1st speech at the Beijing rally celebrating the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army in prominent positions on their international pages on the 4th.

### 平壤各报全文刊载朱总司令“八一”演说

1950-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　平壤各报全文刊载朱总司令“八一”演说
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府机关报《民主朝鲜》报、劳动党中央机关报《劳动新闻》报，均于四日以国际版显要地位，全文刊登朱德总司令八月一日在北京市庆祝人民解放军建军二十三周年大会上的演说。
