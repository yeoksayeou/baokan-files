Title: The Sunday Literary Lecture: Wei Chenxu to Speak This Week
Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 6

　　Sunday Literary Lecture
    Wei Chenxu to Speak This Week
    The Sunday Literary Lecture, sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Wei Chenxu this week, with a lecture entitled: "On Problems of Dramatic Creation." After the lecture, Yu Yunfang, Song Zhicheng, and Huo Chunshan will perform Beijing Drum Song: "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix." Address: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: Sunday morning at 9:30 AM. Admission with long-term or temporary tickets. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Service Department on Saturday (group tickets can be obtained on Friday).

### 星期文艺讲座  本周魏晨旭主讲

1950-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    本周魏晨旭主讲
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部主办的星期文艺讲座，本周请魏晨旭主讲，讲题：《关于戏剧创作问题》。会后由于韵芳宋志诚霍春山先生表演京韵大鼓：《百鸟朝凤》。地址：青年宫交谊厅，时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场，临时券可于礼拜六至服务部领取（团体票可于礼拜五往领）。
