Title: East China and Central-South Military Regions and the Third and Fourth Field Armies Prepare to Elect Model Hero Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-08-22
Page: 1
　　
　　East China and Central-South Military Regions and the Third and Fourth Field Armies
    Prepare to Elect Model Hero Representatives
    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] The East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, along with the Central-South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army, are preparing to elect representatives of combat heroes and labor models. The political departments of both military regions have issued instructions to their subordinate units, stipulating specific methods for electing representatives and pointing out that this election should be used to motivate commanders and fighters to carry forward the spirit of revolutionary heroism. The East China Military Region and the Third Field Army are scheduled to hold a Hero and Model Representative Conference on September 5th to select representatives from the military region to attend the National Combat Hero Representative Conference and the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representative Conference. The work of exhibiting the battle achievements of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army is also being actively prepared.
    In addition: The Songhu Garrison Troops held a celebration of meritorious persons and awards to model workers for eight days from August 3rd to 10th, and held a grand award ceremony. At the meeting, the meritorious and model personnel formulated their own progress plans and resolved to strive for more achievements in the great task of liberating Taiwan and defending the motherland.

### 华东中南两军区暨三野四野  准备选举英模代表

1950-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东中南两军区暨三野四野
    准备选举英模代表
    【新华社十五日讯】人民解放军华东军区暨第三野战军和中南军区暨第四野战军各部正进行选举战斗英雄、劳动模范代表的准备工作。两军区的政治部均向所属部队发出了指示，规定选举代表的具体办法，并指出应通过此次选举以激励指战员们发扬革命英雄主义的精神。华东军区和第三野战军定于九月五日举行英雄模范代表大会，产生该军区出席全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的代表。华东军区和第三野战军的战绩展览工作也正在积极准备中。
    又讯：淞沪警备部队曾于八月三日至十日的八天中举行庆贺功臣、奖励模范大会，并举行了隆重的给奖典礼。会上功臣模范们订出自己的进步计划，决心在解放台湾，保卫祖国的伟大任务中争取功上加功。
