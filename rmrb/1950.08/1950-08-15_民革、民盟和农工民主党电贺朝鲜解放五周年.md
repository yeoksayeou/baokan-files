Title: RCCK, CDL, and CACDP Send Telegrams Congratulating North Korea on the Fifth Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-08-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 15th:

　　Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, sent a telegram congratulating North Korea on the fifth anniversary of its liberation. The telegram reads as follows:
To Chairman Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

　　On the occasion of the great and glorious fifth anniversary of the liberation of your country, the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, following the peace-loving and democratic countries of the world and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, extends its most sincere congratulations to all the people of your country, and wishes your country a speedy completion of peace, unification, independence, and freedom. The direct aggression of the U.S. imperialists against your country has been defeated by the heroic Korean People's Army. In a very short period, we will see the liberation of all the Korean people, and then we will hold a more grand celebration.

　　With the highest respect!

　　Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, and Shen Junru, Vice Chairman, sent a telegram to Chairman Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea congratulating him on the fifth anniversary of the liberation of North Korea. The telegram reads as follows:
Chairman Kim Il-sung:

　　Today is the anniversary of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of your country. We would like to extend our sincere and warm congratulations to you and the people of your country! Thanks to the arduous struggle of the Korean people under your leadership, your country has been liberated from the iron hooves of Japanese imperialism! Now the U.S. imperialists are following in the footsteps of the Japanese imperialists, attempting to hinder your country's independence, unification, and freedom. The people of your country are engaged in a great and just war against the U.S., and have achieved brilliant results. We firmly believe that the heroic People's Army under your leadership will be able to expel the U.S. imperialists from the country, complete the great cause of your country's unification and independence! Strengthen the power of the world peace and democracy camp. Wish you victory!

　　Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League Shen Junru, Vice Chairman

　　This Newspaper: Zhang Bojun, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, sent a special telegram to General Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The congratulatory telegram stated:
To Ambassador Li Zhouyuan, forwarded to General Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

　　At this time, when the people of your country are engaged in a great and just war to defend the democratic independence and unification of the motherland, on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of your country, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations on behalf of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party! Thanks to your and the Korean Labor Party's wise leadership, as well as the heroic resistance of the Korean people and the People's Army, the conspiracy of the US and Li has been quickly defeated, giving the fascist bandits the punishment they deserve, and a complete victory is about to be won.

　　You are now fighting the most arduous and heroic battle against the most ferocious enemies of world peace and democracy—the U.S. imperialists and their running dogs. The Chinese people extend fraternal support and condolences to your great cause.

　　Zhang Bojun, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party
August 14, 1950

### 民革、民盟和农工民主党电贺朝鲜解放五周年

1950-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　民革、民盟和农工民主党电贺朝鲜解放五周年
    【新华社十五日讯】中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深电贺朝鲜解放五周年，电文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议金日成主席：
    在贵国伟大光荣解放五周年的今天，中国国民党革命委员会，随同全世界和平民主国家和中华人民共和国中央人民政府之后，谨致最真挚的赤诚向贵国全体人民申贺，并祝贵国和平、统一、独立、自由的迅速完成。现在美帝国主义对贵国施行的直接侵略，已经被英勇的朝鲜人民军所击败了。在很短的期内，我们可以看到全朝鲜人民的解放，那时我们再举行更盛大的庆祝。
    谨致最高敬礼！
    中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深
    【新华社十五日讯】中国民主同盟主席张澜、副主席沈钧儒顷致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议金日成主席祝贺朝鲜解放五周年。电文如下：金日成主席：
    今天是贵国解放五周年纪念日期，我们谨向你及贵国人民致诚恳热烈的贺忱！由于你领导的朝鲜人民的艰苦奋斗，使贵国从日本帝国主义的铁蹄之下，得到了解放！如今美帝又继日帝的后尘，妄图阻挠贵国的独立统一与自由。贵国人民正在从事伟大的、正义的抗美战争，并已获得辉煌的战绩。我们坚决相信在你领导之下的英勇的人民军，必能驱逐美帝出境，完成贵国的统一独立的大业！加强世界和平民主阵容的力量。敬祝胜利！
    中国民主同盟主席张澜  副主席沈钧儒
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党中央委员会主席章伯钧特电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成将军庆贺朝鲜解放五周年纪念。贺电中称：李周渊大使先生转朝鲜民主主义共和国部长会议主席金日成将军：
    当此，贵国全国人民为保卫祖国的民主独立和统一而进行伟大的正义的战争之际，欣逢贵国解放五周年纪念，谨代表中国农工民主党向您致崇敬的庆贺！由于您与朝鲜劳动党的英明领导，以及朝鲜人民和人民军的英勇抗战，已经迅速地击败了美李的阴谋，给予法西斯匪徒以应得的打击，行将赢得全面的胜利。
    你们现在是在向全世界和平民主的最凶顽敌人——美帝及其走狗，进行最艰苦最英勇的战斗，中国人民对于你们这伟大事业，致以兄弟的声援和慰问。
        中国农工民主党中央委员会主席章伯钧
              一九五○年八月十四日
