Title: Chairman Mao Fetes Burmese Ambassador; Burmese Ambassador Pays Courtesy Calls on Our Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 1

　　Chairman Mao Fetes Burmese Ambassador
    Burmese Ambassador Pays Courtesy Calls on Our Government Leaders
    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th] Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet for the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Myint Toon, and all embassy staff at 7:30 PM on the 10th. Invited to attend were Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu and Directors Mao Bingnan and Yan Baohang of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Director Yu Xinqing of the Bureau of Protocol of the Central People's Government, and Acting Director Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director Chen Jiakang, Director Wang Zhuoru, and Deputy Director Shen Ping of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th] U Myint Toon, the Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, presented his credentials on the 7th. In recent days, he has paid courtesy calls on Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei, and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席欢宴缅大使  缅大使分谒我政府首长

1950-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席欢宴缅大使
    缅大使分谒我政府首长
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于十日下午七时半宴缅甸驻我国大使吴敏登及全体馆员。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席、刘少奇副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席，林伯渠秘书长，外交部章汉夫副部长、毛炳南主任、阎宝航副主任，中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长及外交部亚洲司乔冠华代司长、陈家康副司长、交际处王倬如处长、沈平副处长等。
    【新华社十日讯】缅甸联邦驻我国大使吴敏登七日呈递国书后，日前曾分谒朱德副主席、刘少奇副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席，黄炎培副总理，外交部章汉夫副部长等。
