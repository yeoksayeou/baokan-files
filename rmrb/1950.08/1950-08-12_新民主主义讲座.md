Date: 1950-08-12

　　New Democracy Lectures
    The New Democracy Lectures sponsored by the Beijing YMCA are scheduled for 7:30 PM on Saturday, August 12th. Mr. Zhao Yizhi, representative of the Beijing business community, and Mr. Zhao Fusan, representative of the youth, will report on the "Process of the Second Session of the Third All-Circles People's Representative Conference of Beijing". All circles are welcome to attend the lectures.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座定八月十二日（星期六）晚七时半请京市工商界代表赵宜之先生、青年代表赵复三先生报告“北京市第二届第三次各界人民代表会议经过”。欢迎各界前往听讲。
