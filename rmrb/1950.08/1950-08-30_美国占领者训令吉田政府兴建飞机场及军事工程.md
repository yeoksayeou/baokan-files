Title: American Occupation Authorities Order the Yoshida Government to Build Airfields and Military Projects
Author:
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 26th, TASS dispatch from the 25th: Japanese banking circles report that the American occupation authorities have ordered the Japanese government to undertake large-scale construction of airfields and other military projects, at a total cost of 3.5 billion yen (at the official exchange rate of 360 yen to one US dollar). Not long ago, the American government also ordered the Japanese Ministry of Construction to restore all former Japanese military airfields. According to Japanese government sources, the cost of this project is estimated at 2.27 billion yen. Currently, fourteen airfields have been restored and expanded with funds provided by the American occupation authorities.

### 美国占领者训令吉田政府兴建飞机场及军事工程

1950-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国占领者训令吉田政府兴建飞机场及军事工程
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社二十五日讯：日本银行界人士消息，美国占领当局已训令日本政府大兴土木，建筑飞机场和其他军事工程，费用共计三十五亿日元（按官方汇率为三百六十日元合一美元）。不久以前，美国政府并曾命令日本政府建设省恢复日本以前的一切军用飞机场。据日本政府人士估计，这笔工程的费用为二十二亿七千万日元。目前，由美国占领当局供给经费所恢复和扩充的飞机场，已达十四个。
