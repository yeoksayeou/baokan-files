Title: Supporting the Just Struggle of the Korean People, Italian Sailors Refuse to Transport American Imperialist Accomplices to Korea, Exhibition on the Korean War Held in Prague
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 4

　　Supporting the Just Struggle of the Korean People
    Italian Sailors Refuse to Transport American Imperialist Accomplices to Korea Exhibition on the Korean War Held in Prague
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] According to a report from the Italian ANSA news agency relayed by TASS: Italian sailors on the "Anna Salan" ship, chartered by the so-called International Refugee Organization, which recently arrived in Melbourne, Australia, resolutely refused to set sail after learning that the organization intended to use the ship to transport "Australian nurses" to Korea to support the American war of aggression. The International Refugee Organization was forced to cancel its decision to send the ship to Korea.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] TASS, Prague: The exhibition on the Korean War, sponsored by the Czechoslovak Ministry of Information and Culture, opened in Prague on the 3rd. The exhibits fully reflect the struggle of the Korean people for independence, freedom, and unification. The exhibition has attracted a large audience.

### 声授朝鲜人民正义斗争  意籍水手拒运美帝帮凶赴朝  布拉格举办朝鲜战争展览会

1950-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　声授朝鲜人民正义斗争
    意籍水手拒运美帝帮凶赴朝  布拉格举办朝鲜战争展览会
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社引意大利安莎通讯社的消息称：最近驶抵澳大利亚墨尔本的所谓国际难民组织所租用的“安娜·萨兰”号轮船上的意大利籍水手，在获悉该组织要用该船载运“澳大利亚护士”到朝鲜去支援美国的侵略战争后，坚决拒绝开船。国际难民组织不得不取消将该船开赴朝鲜的决定。
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克宣传与文化部主办的朝鲜战争展览会，于三日在布拉格开幕。各项展览品充分反映了朝鲜人民为争取独立、自由与统一而进行的斗争。展览会吸引了大量观众。
