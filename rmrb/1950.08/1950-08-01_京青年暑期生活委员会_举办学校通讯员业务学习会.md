Title: Beijing Youth Summer Life Committee Holds Training Session for School Correspondents

Author:

Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 7

Beijing Youth Summer Life Committee
Holds Training Session for School Correspondents

The first training session for school correspondents, sponsored by the Beijing Youth Summer Life Committee, will be held at 9:00 AM on August 2nd in the auditorium of the Communist Youth League Municipal Committee at No. 8 East Chang'an Street. Comrade Lu Hao of the People's Daily will speak on "How to Conduct Interviews." School correspondents and students interested in journalistic writing are welcome to attend.

(Summer Committee Propaganda and Communication Group)

### 京青年暑期生活委员会  举办学校通讯员业务学习会

1950-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

　　京青年暑期生活委员会
    举办学校通讯员业务学习会
    北京市青年暑期生活委员会主办的第一次学校通讯员业务学习会，定于八月二日上午九时，在东长安街八号团市工委礼堂举行。由人民日报社陆灏同志讲：“怎样进行采访”。欢迎学校通讯员及爱好通讯写作的同学们听讲。
        （暑委会宣通组）
