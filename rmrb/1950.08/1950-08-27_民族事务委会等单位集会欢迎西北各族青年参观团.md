Title: Nationalities Affairs Commission and Other Units Hold Gathering to Welcome Northwest Ethnic Groups Youth Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency: To welcome the Northwest Ethnic Groups Youth Visiting Group, which arrived in Beijing on the 18th, the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation, and the Beijing Students' Federation, held a joint gathering on the 24th.

### 民族事务委会等单位集会欢迎西北各族青年参观团

1950-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　民族事务委会等单位集会欢迎西北各族青年参观团
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：为欢迎十八日抵京的西北各族青年参观团，中央人民政府民族事务委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、青年团北京市工作委员会、北京市民主妇女联合会、北京市民主青年联合会筹委会、北京市学生联合会等九单位，二十四日曾联合举行集会。
