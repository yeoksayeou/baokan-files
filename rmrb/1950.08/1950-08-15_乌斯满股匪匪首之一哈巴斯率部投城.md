Title: Habas, One of the Bandit Leaders of Usman's Bandits, Leads His Troops to Surrender to the City
Author:
Date: 1950-08-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 14th.  Dispatch from Dihua: After the Usman bandit gang in Xinjiang was basically annihilated by the People's Liberation Army, the scattered remnant bandits have reached a dead end. Habas, one of the key accomplices of the Usman bandits, surrendered on July 13th with more than 20 remaining bandits.

### 乌斯满股匪匪首之一哈巴斯率部投城

1950-08-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　乌斯满股匪匪首之一哈巴斯率部投城
    【新华社西安十四日电】迪化讯：新疆乌斯满股匪经人民解放军基本剿灭后，零星残匪已走头无路。乌匪重要帮凶之一的哈巴斯已于七月十三日率残匪二十余人投降。
