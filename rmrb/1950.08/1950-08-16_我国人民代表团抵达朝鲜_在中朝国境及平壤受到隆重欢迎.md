Title: Our People's Delegation Arrives in Korea; Receives Grand Welcome at Sino-Korean Border and in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 1

　　Our People's Delegation Arrives in Korea
    Receives Grand Welcome at Sino-Korean Border and in Pyongyang

    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th] The Chinese People's Delegation of twenty-three members, led by head and deputy head Guo Moruo and Li Lisan, arrived here on the morning of the 14th to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation.

    The Chinese People's Delegation received a grand welcome. The welcoming delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, consisting of Vice Premier Hong Myong-hui, Minister of Culture and Propaganda Heo Jeong-suk, Director of the Cultural Training Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense Seo Hwi, Director of the Operations Bureau Jeon Woo, Vice Chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea Won Dong-geun, and Chairman of the Democratic Youth League of North Korea Hyeon Jeong-min, went to Sinuiju on the Sino-Korean border to welcome our People's Delegation. Ni Weiting, Counselor of our Embassy in Korea, and Wen Shizhen, Chief Representative of the Northeast People's Government's Commercial Delegation in Korea, also went to Andong to greet them.

    On the morning of the 14th, those who went to Pyongyang Station to welcome our People's Delegation included: Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Jeong Jun-taek, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of Korea, Park Dong-cheol, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Tae Seong-su, Vice Minister of Culture and Propaganda, Kim Seong-hak, Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee, Choi Kyung-deok, Chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea, and Kang Jin-gan, Chairman of the Farmers' Union of North Korea. Organizations such as the Democratic Women's League of North Korea presented flowers to the delegation. Ni Zhiliang, Ambassador of our country to Korea, Counselors Chai Junwu and Xue Zonghua, and all the staff of the embassy, as well as Ma Yusheng, Vice Chairman of the General Federation of Overseas Chinese in North Korea, also went to the station to welcome them.

    On the evening of the 13th, when the delegation entered Korea and arrived in Sinuiju, the People's Committee of North Pyongan Province held a grand welcoming ceremony.

### 我国人民代表团抵达朝鲜  在中朝国境及平壤受到隆重欢迎

1950-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国人民代表团抵达朝鲜
    在中朝国境及平壤受到隆重欢迎
    【新华社平壤十五日电】来朝鲜参加庆祝“八·一五”解放五周年的中国人民代表团一行二十三人，在正副团长郭沫若、李立三的率领下，于十四日晨抵达此间。
    中国人民代表团受到了隆重的欢迎。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国副首相洪命熹、文化宣传部长许贞淑、民族保卫部文化训练局局长徐辉、作战局长全宇、北朝鲜职业总同盟副委员长元东根、北朝鲜民主青年同盟委员长玄正民等所组成的欢迎代表团，曾赴中朝国境新义州欢迎我国人民代表团。我国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞倪蔚庭、东北人民政府驻朝鲜商业代表团首席代表文士桢等亦曾赴安东迎接。
    十四日晨，赴平壤车站欢迎我国人民代表团者有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会书记长康良煜、朝鲜国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽、外交部副部长朴东楚、文化宣传部副部长太成洙、平壤市人民委员会委员长金成学、北朝鲜职业总同盟委员长崔璟德、北朝鲜农民同盟委员长姜镇干等。北朝鲜民主妇女同盟等团体并向代表团献花。我国驻朝鲜大使倪志亮，参赞柴军武、薛宗华，暨全体馆员，北朝鲜华侨联合总会副委员长马玉声等，亦均往车站欢迎。
    十三日晚，代表团进入朝鲜国境抵达新义州时，朝鲜平安北道人民委员会曾举行盛大的欢迎会。
