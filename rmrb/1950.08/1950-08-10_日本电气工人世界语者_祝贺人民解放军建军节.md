Title: Japanese Electrical Workers Esperantists Congratulate the People's Liberation Army on Army Day

Author:

Date: 1950-08-10
Page: 1

　　Japanese Electrical Workers Esperantists
    Congratulate the People's Liberation Army on Army Day
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Preparatory Committee of the Esperanto Association of the All-Japan Electrical Industry Labor Union sent a letter to the Chinese People's Liberation Army on August 1st, congratulating them on the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the army. The full text is as follows: Respected comrades of the People's Liberation Army:
    On August 1st, a day of great significance for the Chinese revolution, we, the electrical industry workers of Japan who are Esperantists, extend our warm and joyful greetings to you.
    Your astonishing success in the Chinese revolution gives us, the peace-loving working class of Japan, great encouragement and great strength, and it will forever be our eternal hope.
    Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army! Long live the Chinese Communist Party! Long live the People's China! Long live lasting world peace!
      Preparatory Committee of the Esperanto Association of the All-Japan Electrical Industry Labor Union
                Mizuta, Yamamoto (signed)

### 日本电气工人世界语者  祝贺人民解放军建军节

1950-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　日本电气工人世界语者
    祝贺人民解放军建军节
    【本报讯】全日本电气工业劳动组合世界语学会筹备委员会，于八月一日致函中国人民解放军，祝贺建军二十三周年纪念。全文如下：敬爱的人民解放军同志们：
    在八月一日，这个对于中国革命极有意义的日子，我们全日本电气工业中的工人世界语者们向你们致热烈的充满愉快的敬礼。
    你们在中国革命中的惊人成功给我们日本喜爱和平的工人阶级以极大的鼓励和极大的力量，而且它永远会是我们永恒的希望。
    中国人民解放军万岁！中国共产党万岁！人民中国万岁！世界持久和平万岁！
      全日本电气工业劳动组合世界语学会筹备委员会
                水田、山本（签名）
