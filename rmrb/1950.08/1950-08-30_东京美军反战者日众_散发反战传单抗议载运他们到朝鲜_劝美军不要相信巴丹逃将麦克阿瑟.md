Title: Growing Anti-War Sentiment Among US Troops in Tokyo: Distributing Anti-War Leaflets, Protesting Transportation to Korea, Urging US Troops Not to Trust MacArthur, the Escapee of Bataan
Author:
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 27th: According to a TASS report from Tokyo: More and more American soldiers are against the war. Several incidents have occurred where American soldiers have refused to be sent to Korea. Anti-war leaflets have been distributed among American soldiers in Tokyo, protesting against being transported to Korea. The leaflets urged soldiers not to trust "Mac" (their nickname for MacArthur), because he had fled from Bataan in the Philippines during the previous war, abandoning the soldiers.

1950-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　东京美军反战者日众
    散发反战传单抗议载运他们到朝鲜
    劝美军不要相信巴丹逃将麦克阿瑟
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社引东京消息称：美国士兵反对战争的越来越多。美国士兵拒绝被派赴朝鲜的事件已发生若干起。东京的美国士兵中间曾有人散发反战传单，抗议载运他们至朝鲜。传单敦促士兵不要相信“麦克”（他们对麦克阿瑟的称呼），因为他在前一次大战中曾从菲律宾的巴丹逃走而将士兵抛弃不顾。
