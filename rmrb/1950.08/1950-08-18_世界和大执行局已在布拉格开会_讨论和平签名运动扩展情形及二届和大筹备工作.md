Title: World Peace Congress Executive Bureau Meets in Prague to Discuss Expansion of Peace Signature Campaign and Preparations for Second World Peace Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th: The Executive Bureau meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace convened on the 16th in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia. Attendees included Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Standing Committee; Giuseppe Di Vittorio (General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions), Pietro Nenni (leader of the Italian Socialist Party), John Desmond Bernal (British physics professor), as well as Soviet representatives Ilya Ehrenburg, Alexander Korneichuk, Wanda Wasilewska, Chinese representatives Wu Youxun, Hua Luogeng, and over fifty representatives from European, American, and African countries.

The meeting is scheduled for three days. The first day's agenda includes a report from the Secretariat of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress and discussions on the expansion of the worldwide peace signature campaign. The main agenda for the second day is to discuss preparations for the Second World Peace Congress to be held in Warsaw in mid-October, as well as the congress journal. The third day will be devoted to discussing the World Peace Fund issue and making resolutions.

### 世界和大执行局已在布拉格开会  讨论和平签名运动扩展情形及二届和大筹备工作

1950-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和大执行局已在布拉格开会
    讨论和平签名运动扩展情形及二届和大筹备工作
    【新华社布拉格十七日电】世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局会议于十六日在捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格开会。出席者有常委会主席约里奥——居里，副主席赛扬（世界工联总书记），南尼（意大利社会党领袖），贝尔纳（英国物理学教授），及苏联代表爱伦堡、高涅楚克、华西列夫斯卡，中国代表吴有训、华罗庚以及欧洲、美洲和非洲各国代表共五十余人。
    会议为期三日。第一日议程为和大常设委员会秘书处报告，及讨论全世界和平签名运动的扩展情形。第二日主要议程为讨论十月中旬在华沙举行的第二届世界和平大会的筹备工作，及和大会刊等工作。第三日将讨论世界和平基金问题并作出决议。
