Title: Celebrating "August 1st" Army Day: Commander-in-Chief Zhu Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. To celebrate the August 1st Army Day, Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a reception banquet at 8:00 p.m. on August 1st. Those attending the celebration banquet included Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff; senior military officers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; heads of ministries, commissions, institutes, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government; and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 300 people.
Foreign guests invited to attend this grand celebration banquet included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Military Attaché Lieutenant General Kotov, Czech Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burkin, Swedish Ambassador Amaston, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Tun, Danish Minister Munk, German Chargé d'Affaires Grutena, Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires Fekete, and others, totaling more than 130 people.
The banquet proceeded in a warm atmosphere. Those present frequently toasted the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, the wise leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the great leader of the world's peace-loving people, Marshal Stalin.

### 庆祝“八一”建军节  朱总司令举行招待会

1950-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝“八一”建军节
    朱总司令举行招待会
    【新华社一日讯】为庆祝八一建军节，中国人民解放军朱德总司令特于八月一日晚八时举行招待宴会。出席庆祝宴会的，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深、张澜，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，代参谋总长聂荣臻及中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官、中央人民政府各部、会、院、署、行首长，各民主党派和各人民团体负责人等共三百余人。
    应邀参加这一盛大庆祝宴会的外宾有：苏联大使罗申、武官柯托夫中将，捷克大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，印度大使潘尼迦。波兰大使布尔金，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，缅甸大使吴敏登，丹麦公使穆克，德国临时代办葛鲁特纳，匈牙利临时代办费凯特等一百三十余人。
    宴会在热烈的气氛中进行，与宴者频频为英勇的中国人民解放军干杯，为中国人民的英明领袖毛主席、朱总司令干杯，为世界爱好和平人民的伟大领袖斯大林大元帅干杯。
