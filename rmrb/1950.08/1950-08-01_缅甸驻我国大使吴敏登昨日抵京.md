Title: Burmese Ambassador to China U Myint Thein Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 4

　　Burmese Ambassador to China U Myint Thein Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 31st] U Myint Thein, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Burma to China, and his wife, accompanied by Ma Muming, Chief of the Foreign Affairs Section of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government Foreign Affairs Office, and U Khun, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy in China, arrived in Beijing at 7 p.m. today. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Qiao Guanhua, Acting Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Tang Yanjie, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Danish Minister Munk, Hungarian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Fekete, Counselor Simonyi, First Secretary of the Romanian Embassy Mentianu, Second Secretary of the Polish Embassy Dai Peilaxinski, News Commissioner Luo Buman, Third Secretary of the Czech Embassy Luo Dai, British Government Representative Hutchison, and Dutch Government Representative Hou Weimin.

### 缅甸驻我国大使吴敏登昨日抵京

1950-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸驻我国大使吴敏登昨日抵京
    【新华社三十一日讯】缅甸驻我国首任特命全权大使吴敏登及夫人，由天津市人民政府外侨事务处交际科长马牧鸣及缅甸驻我国代办吴群陪同，于今日下午七时抵京。赴站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司代司长乔冠华、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部参谋长唐延杰，苏联大使罗申，印度大使潘尼迦，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，丹麦公使穆克，匈牙利大使馆临时代办费凯特、参赞希蒙尼，罗马尼亚大使馆一等秘书孟天努，波兰大使馆二等秘书戴裴拉辛斯基、新闻专员罗步曼，捷克大使馆三等秘书罗戴，英国政府代表胡阶森，荷兰政府代表侯卫民等。
