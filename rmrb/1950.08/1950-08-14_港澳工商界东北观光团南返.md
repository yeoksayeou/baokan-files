Title: Hong Kong and Macau Business Community Observation Group Returns South

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 13th: The Hong Kong and Macau business community observation group has left Guangzhou on the 11th to return to Hong Kong and Macau. The group arrived in Guangzhou on the 9th of this month and was warmly received by the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal People's Governments and business community representatives. The group leader, Huang Changshui, deputy leaders Mo Yinggui, Chen Junleng and the other 22 members issued a written statement after returning to Hong Kong. In their written statement, they summarized their impressions from visiting Guangzhou, Hankou, Tianjin, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Benxi, Fushun, Anshan, Dalian, Lushun, Beijing, Shanghai and other cities over the past two months, and praised the motherland for its progress and the bright future for industrial and commercial development. Private industrial and commercial enterprises in various regions, with the support and leadership of the government and public enterprises, are all profitable and continuously improving and expanding.

### 港澳工商界东北观光团南返

1950-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　港澳工商界东北观光团南返
    【新华社广州十三日电】港澳工商界东北观光团一行已于十一日离穗分别返抵港澳。该团于本月九日南返抵达广州，曾受到广东省、广州市人民政府及工商界人士的热烈招待。该团团长黄长水，副团长莫应桂、陈君冷等一行二十二人返香港后发表了书面谈话。他们在书面谈话中概述他们两个多月中观光广州、汉口、天津、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、本溪、抚顺、鞍山、大连、旅顺、北京、上海等城市所得的印象，并赞扬祖国正在进步，工商业发展前途光明远大。各地私营工商业在政府和公营企业扶助与领导下，都有利可图，不断提高和扩展。
