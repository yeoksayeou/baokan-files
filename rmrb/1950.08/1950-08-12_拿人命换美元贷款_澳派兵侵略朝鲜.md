Title: Trading Lives for Dollar Loans: Australia Sends Troops to Invade Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 4

　　Trading Lives for Dollar Loans
　　Australia Sends Troops to Invade Korea
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 11th] The Menzies government of Australia is sacrificing lives to support the United States' invasion of Korea in exchange for dollar loans to salvage its looming economic collapse. According to a Dentsu report: After Australian Prime Minister Menzies personally went to Washington in late July to discuss matters with U.S. officials, a dollar loan agreement has recently been concluded. It is widely believed that the amount of the loan may exceed 100 million pounds.
　　Opposition leader Chifley issued a statement on the 5th, accusing the Australian government of using its troops at the disposal of the United States in exchange for dollar loans.

### 拿人命换美元贷款  澳派兵侵略朝鲜

1950-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　拿人命换美元贷款
    澳派兵侵略朝鲜
    【新华社十一日讯】澳大利亚孟席斯政府不惜以派兵支援美国在朝鲜的侵略行动来换取美元贷款以挽救其面临的经济崩溃的危机。据电通社消息：在澳总理孟席斯于七月下旬亲往华盛顿和美国官方进行商谈之后，最近已缔结了美元贷款协定。众信，贷款的数目可能超过一亿英镑。
    澳反对党领袖齐佛莱于五日发表声明，指责澳政府不该以军队供美国驱使以换取美元贷款。
