Title: Publication of the Twenty-First Issue of "Chinese Children and Youth"
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 8

The twenty-first issue of "Chinese Children and Youth" has been published. The contents of this issue include: "The Japanese People Stand Up" (a comic strip); Wang Dazun: "The Rabbit with Three Feet"; Xie Jian'san: "How to Make Mosquito Incense"; Gong Jue (translator): "The Little Girl of Paris" (fiction); Zhang Baokang (translator): "The Working People on the Sea" (team life); Ren Dalin: "A Bullet" (fairytale); Kang Tuo: "The Logic of Ruthlessness" (fable); Zuo Lin, excerpt eighteen from the story of the New Fourth Army's vanguard team: "Who Says Resistance Against Japan is Illegal"; and Men Sucen: "Eating Dumplings" (children's song), etc.

### 中国少年儿童第二十一期出版

1950-08-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　中国少年儿童第二十一期出版
    《中国少年儿童》第二十一期已出版，本期内容有：《日本人民站来了》（连续画）；汪达尊：《三双脚的兔儿》；谢建三：《怎样做蚊烟香》；龚爝译：《巴黎的小女孩》（小说）；张保康译：《海上的劳动人民》（队的生活）；任大霖：《一粒子弹》（童话）；康濯：《狠的道理》（寓言），左林著新安诱行团的故一之十八：《谁说抗日不合法》，及门肃岑：《吃饺子》（儿歌）等。
