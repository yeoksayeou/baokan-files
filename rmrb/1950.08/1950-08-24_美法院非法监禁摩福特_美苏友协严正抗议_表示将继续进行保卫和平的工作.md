Title: US Court Illegally Imprisons Morford; US-Soviet Friendship Association Lodges Strong Protest; States It Will Continue Work to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 19th: TASS reported from New York on the 19th: Representatives of the National Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association told reporters that the association's Executive Director, Morford, has been ordered to enter prison on August 28th for "contempt" of the House Un-American Activities Committee. This order was issued after US Supreme Court Chief Justice Vinson rejected a request for bail pending Supreme Court review of Morford's case.

Morford's lawyer insisted that the actions of representatives of the Un-American Activities Committee were unconstitutional. He pointed out that working for American-Soviet friendship is not a "subversive" or anti-American activity.

The National Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association issued the following statement to the press regarding the decision to imprison Morford: "Executive Director Morford and the Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association continue to believe that our work for American-Soviet friendship is in the supreme interest of the American people. We believe now, as we always have, that such understanding and friendship between the two countries is the greatest guarantee of achieving peace and saving ourselves and the rest of humanity from destruction by atomic war. We will continue our work to defend peace."

### 美法院非法监禁摩福特  美苏友协严正抗议  表示将继续进行保卫和平的工作

1950-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　美法院非法监禁摩福特
    美苏友协严正抗议
    表示将继续进行保卫和平的工作
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约十九日讯：美苏友好协会全国理事会的代表告各报记者称，该会执行理事摩福特已奉命于八月二十八日入狱，因为他“蔑视”众院非美活动委员会。此项命令是在美国最高法院审判长文森驳回关于在最高法院审理摩福特的案件之前先予保释的要求以后发出的。
    摩福特的律师坚认非美活动委员会代表的行为是不合宪法的，他指出：为美苏友好而工作，并不是“颠覆性”的或是反美的活动。
    美苏友好协会全国理事会特就监禁摩福特的决定向报界发表如下声明：“执行理事摩福特和美苏友好协会理事会依然认为，我们为美苏友好而做的工作，是符合美国人民之无上利益的。我们现在仍和以前一样相信，两国之间的这种谅解与友谊，是获致和平以及使我们自身与其余的人类不致遭受原子战争毁灭的最大的保障。我们将继续进行保卫和平的工作。”
