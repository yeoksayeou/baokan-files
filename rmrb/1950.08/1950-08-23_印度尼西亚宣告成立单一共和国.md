Title: Indonesia Declares Establishment of a Unitary Republic
Author:
Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 4

　　Indonesia Declares Establishment of a Unitary Republic
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 22nd] Jakarta Dispatch: The unified Indonesian nation was declared established in Jakarta a few days ago (August 15th), and the former Federal Republic of Indonesia no longer exists. The new nation is named the "Republic of Indonesia" and is divided into ten provinces. With the declaration of the new nation, the Hatta government has resigned.

### 印度尼西亚宣告成立单一共和国

1950-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚宣告成立单一共和国
    【新华社二十二日讯】雅加达讯：几天前（八月十五日）统一的印度尼西亚国家在雅加达宣告成立，以前的印尼联邦共和国不再存在了。这个新的国家定名为“印度尼西亚共和国”，共分十省。由于新的国家宣布成立，哈达政府已经辞职。
