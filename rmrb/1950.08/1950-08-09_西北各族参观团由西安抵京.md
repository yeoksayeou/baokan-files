Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing from Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency report: The Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, which departed from Xi'an on the 4th of this month, arrived in Beijing on the 8th. The delegation consists of 38 members, including committee members and attendees who recently participated in the first meeting of the Northwest Military and Political Committee's Ethnic Affairs Committee, as well as worker representatives from Xinjiang Province. It includes nine ethnic groups: Hui, Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, Kyrgyz, Salar, Tu, and Han, led by Head of Delegation Ma Fuchen (Hui), Deputy Heads Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan), Nizabuding (Uyghur), He Zhaolin (Mongolian), and Zong Qun (Han). The purpose of the delegation's visit to Beijing is to pay respects to the Central People's Government and to visit Beijing and Tianjin. When the delegation arrived in Beijing, Yang Jingren, Director of the General Office of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission, and others went to the station to welcome them.

### 西北各族参观团由西安抵京

1950-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族参观团由西安抵京
    【本报讯】新华社消息：本月四日由西安启程来京的西北各族参观团，已于八日到达北京。该团系由最近出席西北军政委员会民族事务委员会首届会议之委员及列席人士，新疆省工人代表等三十八人组成，包括回族、蒙族、藏族、维吾尔族、哈萨克族、柯尔克斯族、撒拉族、土族及汉族等九个民族，由团长马辅臣（回族），副团长黄正清（藏族）、尼扎布丁（维吾尔族）、何兆麟（蒙族）、宗群（汉族）等率领。该团来京目的为向中央人民政府致敬，并参观京津各地。该团抵京时，中央民族事务委员会办公厅主任杨静仁等多人前往车站欢迎。
