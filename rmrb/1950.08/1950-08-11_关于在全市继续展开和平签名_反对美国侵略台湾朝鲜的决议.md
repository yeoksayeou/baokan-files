Title: Resolution on Continuing to Launch Peace Signatures Throughout the City to Oppose US Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 1

[Beijing Municipal News Agency] The Beijing Municipal Second People's Representative Conference, Third Session, passed a resolution on continuing to launch a signature campaign to defend peace and oppose US aggression against Taiwan and Korea. The full text is as follows:

　　American imperialism has launched armed aggression against Korea, obstructed our country's liberation of Taiwan, interfered in the liberation movements of the Vietnamese and Filipino people, and undermined peace in Asia and the world, thus destroying the United Nations Charter. In order to defend our country's territorial sovereignty, liberate Taiwan, and support Korea, we must unite with the people of all of China, all of Asia, and the whole world to resolutely smash the conspiracy of US imperialism to instigate a third world war.

　　The people of Beijing have responded to the call of the World Peace Congress, and 1,126,822 people have already signed the peace declaration. Now, in order to continue mobilizing the people of the city to participate in the struggle against American imperialist aggression and to strike at all provocations by the American aggressors! The conference decided to continue launching a peace signature campaign throughout the city.

### 关于在全市继续展开和平签名  反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜的决议

1950-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　关于在全市继续展开和平签名  反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜的决议
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市第二届第三次各界人民代表会议通过关于继续展开保卫和平签名运动，反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的决议，全文如下：
    美国帝国主义发动武装侵略朝鲜，阻挠我国解放台湾，干涉越南、菲律宾人民的解放运动，破坏亚洲与世界的和平，破坏了联合国宪章。为了保卫我国的领土主权，解放台湾，为了声援朝鲜，我们必须与全中国、全亚洲和全世界的人民团结一致为坚决粉碎美帝国主义挑动第三次世界战争的阴谋而奋斗。
    我们北京市人民响应世界和平大会的号召，已经有一百一十二万六千八百二十二人在和平宣言上签名。现在为了继续动员全市人民参加反对美国帝国主义侵略的斗争，打击美国侵略者的一切挑衅！会议决定继续在全市展开保卫和平的签名运动。
