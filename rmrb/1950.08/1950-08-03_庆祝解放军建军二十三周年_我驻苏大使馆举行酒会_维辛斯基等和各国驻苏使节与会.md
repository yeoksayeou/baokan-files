Title: Chinese Embassy in Soviet Union Holds Reception to Celebrate 23rd Anniversary of PLA Founding; Vyshinsky and Other Ambassadors to Soviet Union Attend
Author:
Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd -- Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and General Bian Zhangwu, Military Attaché, held a grand reception in Moscow on July 31st to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to entertain people from all walks of life in the Soviet Union and diplomatic envoys of various People's Democratic countries to the Soviet Union. Distinguished guests attending the reception included Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, Deputy Minister Zorin, Deputy Minister of the Army Marshal Sokolovsky, Admiral Nevsky, Moscow Garrison Commander Lieutenant General Sinilov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade Kumykin, Borisov, Losakov, and Yeremin, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Denisov, TASS Director-General Bargunov, diplomatic envoys of various People's Democratic countries: Korean Ambassador Chu Ning-ho, Czechoslovak Ambassador Kleibich, Bulgarian Ambassador Blagoeva, Romanian Ambassador Bujic, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Abel, Hungarian Ambassador Zoklaraton, Albanian Minister Natanaeli, Polish Chargé d'affaires Zambrovich, Mongolian Chargé d'affaires Agilbishi, as well as Koenig, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China, who was passing through Moscow on his way back to his post, and more than 300 others.
　　The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere. The participants frequently toasted the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, the invincible Soviet Army, the Korean People's Army, which is heroically fighting against the American aggressors, Chairman Mao Zedong, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, and the leader of the peace-loving people of the world, Marshal Stalin.

### 庆祝解放军建军二十三周年  我驻苏大使馆举行酒会  维辛斯基等和各国驻苏使节与会

1950-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝解放军建军二十三周年
    我驻苏大使馆举行酒会
    维辛斯基等和各国驻苏使节与会
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】中华人民共和国驻苏大使王稼祥暨武官边章五将军，为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十三周年纪念，于七月三十一日在莫斯科举行盛大酒会，招待苏联各界人士和各人民民主国家驻苏使节。与会贵宾有苏联外交部长维辛斯基，副部长佐林，陆军部副部长索科洛夫斯基元帅，海军上将聂夫清科，莫斯科卫戍司令西尼洛夫中将，对外贸易部副部长库米金、鲍里邵夫、洛萨柯夫、叶列明，苏联对外文化协会主席捷尼索夫，塔斯社社长巴尔古诺夫；各人民民主国家的外交使节：朝鲜大使朱宁河，捷克斯洛伐克大使克莱比赫，保加利亚大使布拉果耶娃，罗马尼亚大使布吉克，德意志民主共和国外交使团团长阿贝尔，匈牙利大使佐克拉顿，阿尔巴尼亚公使纳塔奈利，波兰临时代办赞布罗维奇，蒙古代办阿吉尔毕希，以及道经莫斯科返任的德意志民主共和国驻中国外交使团特命全权大使柯尼希等三百余人。
    酒会在友好热烈的气氛中进行。与会者频频为英勇的中国人民解放军，为不可战胜的苏军，为正在对美国侵略者进行英勇战斗的朝鲜人民军，为毛泽东主席，为朱德总司令，为世界爱好和平人民的领袖斯大林大元帅干杯。
