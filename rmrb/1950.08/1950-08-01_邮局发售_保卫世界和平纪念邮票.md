Title: Post Office Issues Commemorative Stamps for Defending World Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 7

　　Post Office Issues
    Commemorative Stamps for Defending World Peace
    In order to oppose the American aggression against Taiwan and Korea, the General Post Office has specially issued the "Commemorative Stamps for Defending World Peace." The design of the stamp is a dove of peace. It is divided into three types: 400 yuan (brown), 800 yuan (green), and 2,000 yuan (blue). The Beijing Postal Administration will begin selling them on August 1st.

### 邮局发售  保卫世界和平纪念邮票

1950-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

　　邮局发售
    保卫世界和平纪念邮票
    邮政总局为了反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜。特地发行了“保卫世界和平纪念邮票”。该项邮票图案为一和平之鸽。计分四百元（棕色），八百元（绿色），二千元（蓝色）三种。北京邮政管理局将于八月一日开始发售。
