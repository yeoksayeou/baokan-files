Title: Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 4th: The Vietnam General Confederation of Labor celebrated its fourth anniversary on July 20th.

　　This organization of Vietnamese workers has actually existed since 1924. After the August Revolution in Vietnam in 1945, it emerged from the underground and became the "Vietnam General Confederation of Labor" on July 19, 1946, with approximately 300,000 members. The General Confederation of Labor joined the World Federation of Trade Unions on June 19, 1947.

### 越总工会成立四周年纪念

1950-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　越总工会成立四周年纪念
    【新华社六日讯】电通社仰光四日讯：越南总工会于七月二十日庆祝它成立四周年纪念日。
    这个越南工人的组织，实际上自从一九二四年以来便已存在。一九四五年越南的八月革命后，它即从地下出现，在一九四六年七月十九日成为“越南总工会”，拥有三十万左右会员。总工会自一九四七年六月十九日起，加入世界工联。
