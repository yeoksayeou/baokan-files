Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Respectively Sent Telegrams of Condolence on the Passing of Vice Chairman Ma Bu Fang
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou of the State Council, on the 11th, respectively sent telegrams to the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, expressing condolences on the passing of Vice Chairman Ma Bu Fang of the Provincial People's Government. The two telegrams are as follows:

	(1) Chairman Mao's telegram of condolence to the Qinghai Provincial People's Government and forwarded to the family of Vice Chairman Ma Bu Fang:
	Vice Chairman Ma Bu Fang's passing is deeply mourned. Special telegram to express condolences.
		Mao Zedong, August 11th

	(2) Premier Zhou's telegram of condolence to the Qinghai Provincial People's Government and forwarded to the family of Vice Chairman Ma Bu Fang:
	Shocked to learn of Vice Chairman Ma Bu Fang's passing, I express my condolences.
		Zhou Enlai, August 11th

### 毛主席周总理分别致电  悼唁马朴副主席的逝世

1950-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席周总理分别致电
    悼唁马朴副主席的逝世
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府毛主席、政务院周总理十一日曾分别致电青海省人民政府，对省人民政府马朴副主席的逝世表示悼念。两电如下：
        （一）毛主席唁电青海省人民政府并转马朴副主席家属：
    马朴副主席病逝，殊深悼念，特电致唁。
        毛泽东八月十一日
        （二）周总理唁电青海省人民政府并转马朴副主席家属：
    惊悉马朴副主席病逝，谨致悼唁。
          周恩来八月十一日
