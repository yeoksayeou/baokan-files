Title: MacArthur's Cable Confirms Conspiracy to Invade Our Taiwan

Author:
Date: 1950-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: MacArthur's cable to the Overseas War Veterans has completely confirmed the U.S. imperialist conspiracy to invade our Taiwan. According to a Taiwan radio broadcast quoting an Associated Press dispatch from Chicago on the 25th, MacArthur, in his cable to the Overseas War Veterans Association, emphatically emphasized the military importance of Taiwan's position. MacArthur blatantly stated, "If we retain the Pacific defense line, including Taiwan, then we may obtain peace." He also said, "From this 'natural defense line' in the Pacific, we can use air power to control every Asian seaport from Vladivostok to Singapore." He further pointed out that Taiwan is the central hub of this defense line.

### 麦克阿瑟的一个电报证实了侵我台湾阴谋

1950-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟的一个电报证实了侵我台湾阴谋
    【新华社二十七日讯】麦克阿瑟给海外作战退役军的电报，完全证实了美帝国主义侵略我台湾的阴谋。据台湾电台引美联社芝加哥二十五日电称：麦克阿瑟在他给海外作战退役军人协会的电报中，曾极力强调台湾地位在军事上的重要性。麦克阿瑟明目张胆地说：“如我们保有包括台湾在内的太平洋防线，则我们可能获得和平。”并说：“从此一太平洋‘天然防线’，我们可用空军控制自海参崴至新加坡每一亚洲海港。”他并指出台湾是这条防线的总枢纽。
