Title: Philippine Liberation Army Grows Stronger, Launches Attacks in Seven Provinces, Briefly Occupies Two Provincial Capitals
Author:
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 4

　　Philippine Liberation Army Grows Stronger
    Launches Attacks in Seven Provinces, Briefly Occupies Two Provincial Capitals
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 28th] According to Agence France-Presse and United Press reports: The attacks launched by the Philippine Liberation Army against the American puppet Quirino clique on the evening of the 25th have, as of the morning of the 27th, expanded to seven provinces including Tarlac, Pampanga, Nueva Ecija, Laguna, Cavite, Nueva Vizcaya, and Bulacan. Seven hundred Liberation Army soldiers, after raiding Santa Cruz, the capital of Laguna province, on the evening of the 25th, occupied the city for a full day and night, opening and releasing all the prisoners in the provincial jail. Six other towns in Laguna province were also attacked. On the morning of the 27th, the Liberation Army raided San Rafael, the capital of Bulacan province. The puppet municipal government buildings in that city and in Santiago, eastern Luzon, were both raided. Local puppet regimes in other areas have also been hit.

### 菲解放军声势更加壮大  在七个省发动攻击  一度占领两个省会

1950-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲解放军声势更加壮大
    在七个省发动攻击  一度占领两个省会
    【新华社二十八日讯】据法新社及合众社讯：菲律宾解放军于二十五日晚对美国傀儡季里诺集团发动的攻击，截至二十七日晨止，已扩大到丹辘、邦邦牙、新怡西夏、内湖蜂牙丝芝、新米斯皆耶及武拉干等七个省。解放军七百名在二十五日晚袭入内湖省的首府圣踏格罗斯后，曾占领该市达一昼夜，打开并释放了省监狱的全部犯人。内湖省的另六个城镇亦受到袭击。二十七日晨，解放军袭入武拉干省会圣拉费尔。该市及吕宋东部的圣地也哥市的傀儡市政府大厦均被袭入。其他各地的地方傀儡政权亦均受到打击。
