Title: Second World Student Congress to Open Mid-Month; Music and Dance Competition to be Held

Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 4th: The Second World Student Congress is scheduled to be held in Prague, Czechoslovakia, from August 14th to 24th. During the Congress, a competition for the title of "Honorary Student of the Second World Student Congress" will be held. Participants will perform piano, violin, folk instruments, and song and dance. There will be three prizes for each competition, with the prizes being honorary certificates. The winner of the first prize will be awarded the title of "Honorary Student of the Second World Student Congress."

### 二届世界学代会月中开幕  将举行音乐歌舞荣誉比赛

1950-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　二届世界学代会月中开幕
    将举行音乐歌舞荣誉比赛
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：世界学生第二届代表大会定于八月十四日到二十四日在捷京布拉格举行。在大会期间将举行“世界学生第二届代表大会荣誉学生”称号的赛会。参加比赛者将表演钢琴、提琴、民间乐器和歌舞。各项比赛均有三个奖，奖品为荣誉奖状，获得第一奖者将荣膺“世界学生第二届代表大会荣誉学生”的称号。
