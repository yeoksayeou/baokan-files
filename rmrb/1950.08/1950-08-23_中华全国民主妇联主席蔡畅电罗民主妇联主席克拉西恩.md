Title: Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Cables Romanian Democratic Women's Federation President, Craciun
Author: Cai Chang
Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd. Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, recently sent a telegram to Mrs. Craciun, President of the Romanian Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating her on the sixth anniversary of Romania's liberation. The telegram reads as follows: Mrs. Craciun:

　　On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of your country's liberation, we Chinese women extend our heartfelt congratulations to the Romanian people and women. You have already achieved great results in the work of building your motherland. This strengthens the forces defending world peace.

　　We wish you even greater achievements. Chinese women will unite more closely with Romanian women and do our utmost to defend world peace.

　　Cai Chang
August 22, 1950

### 中华全国民主妇联主席蔡畅电罗民主妇联主席克拉西恩
蔡畅
1950-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国民主妇联主席蔡畅电罗民主妇联主席克拉西恩
    【新华社二十二日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅顷致电罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会主席克拉西恩祝贺罗马尼亚解放六周年纪念。电文如下：克拉西恩夫人：
    欣逢贵国解放六周年纪念，我们中国妇女谨向罗马尼亚人民与妇女致衷心的祝贺。你们已在建设祖国的工作中获得了伟大成绩。这即是加强保卫世界和平的力量。
    谨祝你们获得更大的成就。中国妇女将与罗马尼亚妇女更亲密的团结一致，尽最大努力保卫世界和平。
    蔡畅
  一九五○年八月二十二日
