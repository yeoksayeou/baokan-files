Title: Greeting the Peace Conference: Polish Laboring People Intensify Production, Launching Labor Emulations to Guarantee Fulfillment of Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Warsaw Dispatch: In order to greet the Polish Peace Conference that will open on September 1st, the Polish laboring people have put forward thousands of new production guarantees.

　　All employees of the "Fuchs" factory in Warsaw decided to increase labor productivity from August 11th to 31st to overproduce products worth 14.5 million zlotys (approximately 1 billion RMB) beyond the plan.

　　Miners of the Dores coal mine in Lower Silesia enthusiastically responded to the call of the miners of the Fozolek coal mine, guaranteeing to complete this year's coal production plan by the beginning of December, and to hand over 64,750 tons of coal over the plan by the end of the year. The miners of Saburka will hand over 20,313 tons of coal over the plan this month.

　　Among workers in metallurgy, textile, food and other industries, an expanding labor competition has been launched to celebrate the opening of the Polish Peace Conference. The Soyka youth work team of the "Bo Gui" metallurgical plant guarantees to exceed the production plan by 25 percent every month.

### 迎接拥护和平大会  波兰劳动人民加紧生产  纷纷展开劳动竞赛保证完成生产计划

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　迎接拥护和平大会
    波兰劳动人民加紧生产
    纷纷展开劳动竞赛保证完成生产计划
    【新华社十六日讯】华沙消息：为了迎接九月一日开幕的波兰拥护和平大会，波兰劳动人民已提出了成千的新的生产保证。
    华沙“甫其士”工厂的全体职工，决定在八月十一日到三十一日间，以增加劳动生产率来超计划生产价值波币一千四百五十万兹罗提（约合人民币十亿）的产品。
    下西里西亚的多列士煤矿矿工们，热烈地响应佛佐勒克煤矿工人的号召，保证要在十二月初完成本年产煤计划，并在年终超计划缴纳六万四千七百五十吨煤。萨伯尔卡的矿工们，本月内将超计划缴纳二万零三百一十三吨煤。
    在冶金、纺织、食品及其他工业的工人中，已为庆祝波兰拥护和平大会开幕而展开了日益扩大的劳动竞赛。“波贵”冶金工厂的索伊卡青年工作队，保证每月超过生产计划百分之二十五。
