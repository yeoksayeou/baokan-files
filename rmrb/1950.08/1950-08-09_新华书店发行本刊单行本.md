Title: Xinhua Bookstore Distributes Single Copies of This Publication
Author:
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 5

The People's Daily will also publish single copies of each issue, printed in a sixteen-page format. The distribution is entrusted to the General Administration of Xinhua Bookstore. The base price for each issue is sixty cents. Those who wish to subscribe to or purchase individual copies of this publication, please contact your local Xinhua Bookstore.

### 新华书店发行本刊单行本

1950-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　新华书店发行本刊单行本
    本刊每期另印十六开单行本，委托新华书店总管理处发行，每期基价六角。订阅或零购本刊者，请迳向当地新华书店接洽。
