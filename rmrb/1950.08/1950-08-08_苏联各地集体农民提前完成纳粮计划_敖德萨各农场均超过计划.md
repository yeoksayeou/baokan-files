Title: Collective Farmers in Various Regions of the Soviet Union Complete Grain Delivery Plans Ahead of Schedule; Farms in Odessa All Exceed Plans
Author:
Date: 1950-08-08
Page: 4

　　Collective Farmers in Various Regions of the Soviet Union Complete Grain Delivery Plans Ahead of Schedule
    Farms in Odessa All Exceed Plans
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 7th, Dispatch from TASS, Moscow] Moscow newspapers on the 5th published the first batch of reports to Stalin from Soviet collective farmers on completing grain delivery plans ahead of schedule, these being farmers from the Moldavian Republic, Krasnodar Krai, and Odessa Oblast.
    The Moldavian Republic has completed 102.9% of its delivery plan, excluding corn. The plan for wheat and rye was completed by 109.9%. This year's wheat delivery increased by 5,787,000 poods (approximately 189,582,000 jin) compared to last year.
    In Krasnodar Krai of the Russian Federation, the workers of collective farms, state farms, and machine tractor stations completed the grain delivery plan (excluding corn and rice) on the 2nd. They also successfully completed the harvesting work. The grain obtained by collective farmers this year is far more than in previous years.
    Collective farms and state farms in Odessa Oblast have completed 114.2% of their delivery plan. The wheat plan was completed by 120.7%. This year's wheat delivery is 11,747,000 poods (approximately 384,830,700 jin) more than last year.
    News of completing grain delivery plans ahead of schedule is flooding into Moscow from all over.

### 苏联各地集体农民提前完成纳粮计划  敖德萨各农场均超过计划

1950-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联各地集体农民提前完成纳粮计划
    敖德萨各农场均超过计划
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科各报于五日刊载了第一批苏联集体农民向斯大林所作提前完成谷物缴纳计划的报告，他们是摩尔达维亚共和国、克拉斯诺达边疆区及敖德萨州等处的农民。
    摩尔达维亚共和国除玉蜀黍外，已完成缴纳计划的百分之一○二点九。其中小麦和裸麦计划完成了一○九点九。今年缴纳的小麦较去年增加了五百七十八万七千普特（约合一亿八千九百五十八万二千市斤）。
    在俄罗斯联邦的克拉斯诺达边疆区，集体农民、国营农场及机器拖拉机站的工人们，已在二日完成了谷物缴纳计划（玉蜀黍及稻米除外）。他们并已顺利地完成了收割工作。集体农民今年获得的谷物，远较往年为多。
    敖德萨州的集体农场和国营农场，已完成缴纳计划的百分之一一四点二。其中小麦的计划完成了百分之一二○点七。今年缴纳的小麦，较去年多一千一百七十四万七千普特（约合三亿八千四百八十三万零七百市斤）。
    报告提前完成纳粮计划的消息，正从各地潮水般地涌向莫斯科。
