Title: Chinese Art Exhibition Opens in Kyiv
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd, Kyiv: A three-month Chinese art and crafts exhibition opened on July 29th in Kyiv, the capital of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic. The exhibition includes paintings by famous ancient Chinese artists, such as Li Longmian's paintings of horses and Huang Quan's figures; pottery from the Wei, Jin, and Six Dynasties, sculptures from the Five Dynasties, and various types of porcelain; contemporary artist Shi Lu's color woodcut "Chairman Mao Meets Labor Heroes," as well as portraits of Lenin and Stalin painted by Chinese artists. There are many visitors.

### 基辅中国艺术品展览会开幕

1950-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　基辅中国艺术品展览会开幕
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社基辅讯：为期三个月的中国美术及工艺品展览会，已于七月二十九日在苏联乌克兰共和国的首都基辅开幕。展览会上陈列有中国古代名家绘画，有李龙眠画马与黄筌的人物；有魏晋六朝的陶器，五代的雕刻和多种瓷器；有当代艺术家石鲁的套色木刻“毛主席和劳动英雄的会见”，以及中国画家所作的列宁和斯大林的画像。参观的人很多。
