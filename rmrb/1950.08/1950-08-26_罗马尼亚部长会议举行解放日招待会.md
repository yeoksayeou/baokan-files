Title: Romanian Council of Ministers Holds Liberation Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 4
　
　　Romanian Council of Ministers Holds Liberation Day Reception
    [Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 25th] The Romanian News Agency, Bucharest, reported: To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Romania by the Soviet Army, the Romanian Council of Ministers held a reception at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the evening of the 23rd. Attending the reception were delegations from various countries celebrating the liberation of Romania, ambassadors and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in Romania, members of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, key government officials and members of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, as well as representatives from various people's organizations, generals and officers of the armed forces, representatives from the cultural and artistic circles, and labor heroes. The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere, and Romanian and Soviet songs were performed afterwards.

### 罗马尼亚部长会议举行解放日招待会

1950-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚部长会议举行解放日招待会
    【新华社二十五日讯】罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚部长会议为庆祝苏军解放罗马尼亚六周年纪念日，于二十三日晚在外交部举行招待会。出席参加庆祝罗马尼亚解放日的各国代表团，各国驻罗使节及外交使团人员，罗马尼亚国民议会主席团委员，政府要员和罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会委员，以及各人民团体的代表，武装部队的将军与军官，文化艺术界代表和劳动英雄等多人。招待会在热烈气氛中进行，会后并演奏了罗马尼亚与苏联歌曲等节目。
