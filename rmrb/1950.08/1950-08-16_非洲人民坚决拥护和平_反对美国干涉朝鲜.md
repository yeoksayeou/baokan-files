Title: African People Firmly Support Peace and Oppose US Interference in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: Reuter, New York, August 7th: According to消息 received by the here the African Affairs Committee, the people of the Union of South Africa, Uganda in East Africa, and Sierra Leone in West Africa strongly oppose the armed intervention of the United States in Korea. The African Affairs Committee has received a resolution passed by the Executive Committee of the West African Civil Liberties and Defence League, stating: If the Korean war develops into an aggressive world war, the African people will not participate in any form. The League guarantees full support for the Korean people's struggle for freedom. The African Affairs Committee also received a telegram from a mass meeting held in Johannesburg, South Africa, on July 16. It said: "Africans, Indians, and all colored people in South Africa support peace. We want peace, not war." The conference demanded that British, French, and American troops immediately withdraw from Korea, Vietnam, Malaya, and all other colonies.

### 非洲人民坚决拥护和平  反对美国干涉朝鲜

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　非洲人民坚决拥护和平
    反对美国干涉朝鲜
    【本报讯】据新华社十二日讯：电通社纽约七日讯：据此间非洲事务委员会接到的消息，南非联邦、东非的乌干达及西非的塞拉勒窝的人民，强烈反对美国在朝鲜的武装干涉。非洲事务委员会曾接到西非公民自由与国防同盟执行委员会所通过的决议，内称：如果朝鲜战事演变成侵略性的世界战争，非洲人民将不以任何形式参战。该同盟保证充分支援朝鲜人民争取自由的斗争。非洲事务委员会还接到南非约翰内斯堡七月十六日举行的群众大会所发的通电。其中说：“非洲人、印度人及南非的一切有色人民都拥护和平。我们要和平，不要战争。”大会要求英、法、美军队立即自朝鲜、越南、马来亚及其他一切殖民地撤退。
