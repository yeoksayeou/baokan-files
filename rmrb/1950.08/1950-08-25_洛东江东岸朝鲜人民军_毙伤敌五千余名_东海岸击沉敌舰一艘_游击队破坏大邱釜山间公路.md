Title: Korean People's Army on the East Bank of the Nakdong River Kills and Wounds Over 5,000 Enemies; Sinks an Enemy Ship on the East Coast; Guerrillas Disrupt Highway Between Daegu and Busan
Author:
Date: 1950-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 24th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 23rd that the People's Army units on all fronts repelled the resistance and counterattacks of the US military and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army under the cover of aircraft, inflicting enormous losses on the enemy's personnel and equipment. The People's Army units advancing victoriously and the People's Army units on the southern coast continued to strike the enemy heavily and continued their offensive.

The US Navy continues to intrude into the coastline north of the 38th parallel, barbarously shelling our residential areas.

On August 20th, the US destroyer number 726 intruded into the sea near Bupyeong-ri (transliteration) on the east coast, attempting to shell our residential areas. Our People's Army coastal artillery units bombarded the ship with concentrated fire, hitting it 27 times. As a result, the ship exploded violently 20 miles south of Bupyeong-ri and finally sank.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 24th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 23rd that the People's Army units on all fronts were fighting fiercely, striking the frantic counterattacks of the US military and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army. The People's Army units repelled the enemy's counterattacks and inflicted huge losses.

In the area east of the Nakdong River, the People's Army units inflicted huge losses on the US military's personnel and equipment. In the fighting, over 5,000 enemies were killed or wounded, and many spoils of war were captured.

The People's guerrillas between Daegu and Busan responded to the People's Army's offensive and launched fierce activities. The guerrillas disrupted the highway between Daegu and Busan behind enemy lines, disrupting the enemy's rear and achieving great results.

### 洛东江东岸朝鲜人民军  毙伤敌五千余名  东海岸击沉敌舰一艘  游击队破坏大邱釜山间公路

1950-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　洛东江东岸朝鲜人民军
    毙伤敌五千余名
    东海岸击沉敌舰一艘
    游击队破坏大邱釜山间公路
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十三日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上击退在飞机掩护下的美军和李承晚残余伪军的抵抗和反击，并予敌人的有生力量和装备以巨大的损失。解放军威后继续挺进的人民军部队和在南海岸方面的人民军部队，沉重地打击敌人，继续展开攻击。
    美海军继续侵入三八线以北的海岸线，野蛮地炮击我居民地区。
    八月二十日，美国驱逐舰七二六号一艘，侵入东海岸富坪里（译音）附近的海面，企图炮击我居民地区。我人民军海岸炮兵部队以集中火力，轰击该舰，命中二十七发，结果该舰在富坪里以南二十英里之处，发生巨大爆炸，终于沉没。
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十三日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上打击美军部队和李承晚残余伪军的疯狂反击，继续展开激烈的战斗。人民军部队击退了敌人的反击，并予以巨大的损失。
    在洛东江东岸地区，人民军部队予美军有生力量与装备以巨大的损失。在战斗中，毙伤敌五千余名，并缴获战利品甚多。
    大邱、釜山间的人民游击队响应人民军的进攻，展开猛烈的活动。游击队在敌后破坏大邱、釜山间的公路，扰乱敌人的后方，收到巨大的效果。
