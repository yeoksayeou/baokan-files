Author:
Date: 1950-08-24

　　Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
    Romanian Ambassador Holds Reception
    Chairman Mao Attends Upon Invitation
    [Xinhua News Agency, 23rd dispatch] The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Romania to China, Mr. Rudenko, held a grand reception in Beijing at 8:00 PM on the 23rd to celebrate the sixth anniversary of Romania's liberation.
    Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China attended upon invitation. Also invited were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan; Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu; Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu and Huang Yanpei; President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru; and more than 400 heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and leading figures of various people's organizations.
    Diplomatic envoys of various countries who attended upon invitation included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burkin, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Head of the German Diplomatic Mission Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Burmese Ambassador Wu Min Deng, Danish Minister Mook, and Chargé d'Affaires of the Czechoslovakian Embassy Hasek, among others.
    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Afterwards, Romanian newsreels "August 23rd, 1949" and "Danube-Black Sea Canal" were shown. (Attached picture)

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放六周年  罗大使举行招待会  毛主席应邀出席

1950-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝罗马尼亚解放六周年
    罗大使举行招待会
    毛主席应邀出席
    【新华社二十三日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使鲁登科，为庆祝罗马尼亚解放六周年纪念，特于二十三日下午八时在京举行盛大招待会。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席应邀出席。应邀与会者尚有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深、张澜；中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠；政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，副总理董必武、黄炎培；最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，及中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体负责人等四百余人。
    各国外交使节应邀出席者有苏联大使罗申，朝鲜大使李周渊，印度大使潘尼迦，波兰大使布尔金，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，德意志外交使团团长柯尼希，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汉，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，缅甸大使吴敏登，丹麦公使穆克，以及捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办哈谢克等人。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。会后并放映了罗马尼亚的新闻影片《一九四九年的八月二十三日》和《多瑙河——黑海运河》。（附图片）
