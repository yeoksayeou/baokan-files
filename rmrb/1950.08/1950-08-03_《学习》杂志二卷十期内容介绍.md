Title: Introduction to the Contents of "Study" Magazine, Volume 2, Number 10
Author:
Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 6

The tenth issue of "Study" magazine, Volume 2, was published on August 1st. This issue features Comrade Chen Boda's "On Learning." The full text is divided into four sections: 1. The question is "willingness to learn" or not? 2. Two learning methods; 3. Is it adopting a consultative attitude or a worshipping attitude? 4. When to learn? It contains very insightful discussions on the attitude and method of learning. In addition, the main articles include: Jun Lin's "New Contributions to Marxist-Leninist Theory," introducing Stalin's recently published two articles, "On Marxism in Linguistics" and "On Some Problems of Linguistics"; Shuang Yun's "The Marxist Military Line," which is a study note on Chairman Mao's "Strategic Problems of China's Revolutionary War"; Wu Jiang's "Opposing Bureaucracy," which theoretically analyzes the emergence of bureaucracy and bureaucratism, the characteristics of bureaucratism, the impact of bureaucratism on our national work, and the ways to overcome bureaucratism; Wang Huide's "The Status of Workers in Capitalist Society" (introducing Chapter 5 of Leontiev's Political Economy); Song Yunbin's "Three Stages of Language Learning," providing excellent reference opinions for young writers. This issue's "Readers' Forum" column publishes four articles: "Opposing 'Acute Illness' in Theoretical Learning," "On 'Boldly Putting Forward Negative Opinions'," "How to Make Informal Talks and Central Speeches Beneficial to Collective Learning," and "How I Take Reading Notes." This issue's "Question and Answer" column, regarding land reform issues, answers the question of "Why rich peasants could not join peasant associations before and during the land reform." It also answers two questions of political economy, one question of social development history, one question of the Chinese revolution, and one question about Marr's linguistic theory.

### 《学习》杂志二卷十期内容介绍

1950-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　《学习》杂志二卷十期内容介绍
    《学习》杂志二卷十期已于八月一日出版。这一期发表了陈伯达同志的《关于学习》。全文共有四节：一、问题在于“肯不肯学”？二、两个学习方法；三、是采取商量的态度，还是拜菩萨的态度；四、什么时候去学？对学习的态度和方法有极精辟的论述。此外，主要文章有：君麟的《马列主义理论的新贡献》，介绍斯大林最近发表的《论马克思主义在语言学中的问题》及《论语言学的一些问题》两篇文章：双云的《马克思主义的军事路线》，是读毛主席《中国革命战争的战略问题》的笔记；吴江的《反对官僚主义》，从理论上分析官僚制与官僚主义的产生，官僚主义的特色，官僚主义对我们国家工作的影响，以及克服官僚主义的办法；王惠德的《资本主义社会中工人的地位》（介绍列昂节夫政治经济学第五章）；宋云彬的《语文学习三阶段》，向青年学习写作者提供了很好的参考意见。这一期的《读者笔谈》栏，发表了四篇文章：反对理论学习中的《急性病》，关于《大胆提出反面意见》，怎样使漫谈和中心发言有利于集体学习，我怎样做阅读笔记。这期的《问题解答》栏，关于土改问题的，解答了《富农为什么在土地改革前和土地改革中不能加入农民协会》的问题，另外解答了两个政治经济学的问题，一个社会发展史的问题，一个中国革命问题的问题，一个关于马尔语言学说的问题。
