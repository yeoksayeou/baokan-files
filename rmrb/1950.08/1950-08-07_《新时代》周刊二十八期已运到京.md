Title: "New Era" Weekly Issue 28 Has Arrived in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 6

　　The International Bookstore has recently received the English edition of the Moscow-published "New Era" Weekly, issue number 28. Its main contents include: "American Aggressors and the United Nations," "Korea," "News from the Peace Camp," "Finnish Right-Wing Socialist Leaders Serve the Warmongers," "International Life," "Impressions of a Trip to China (The South Gate Triangle Area of China)," etc. There are also photographs of Soviet citizens signing the petition to ban atomic weapons, ten photographs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and a supplement containing the document "On Soviet and American Interference in Korea."

### 《新时代》周刊二十八期已运到京

1950-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　《新时代》周刊二十八期已运到京
    国际书店最近运到莫斯科出版《新时代》周刊英文版第二十八期。其主要内容有：《美国侵略者与联合国》，《朝鲜》，《和平阵营消息》，《芬兰右翼社会党首领们服务于战争贩子》，《国际生活》，《中国旅行印象记（中国南关三角地带》等文。并有苏联公民响应禁用原子武器签名运动照片及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国照片十幅，与《有关苏联和美国干涉朝鲜》文件附册一本。
