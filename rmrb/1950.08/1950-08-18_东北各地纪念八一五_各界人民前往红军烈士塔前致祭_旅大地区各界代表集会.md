Title: Northeast China Commemorates August 15th: People from All Walks of Life Pay Tribute at the Red Army Martyrs Tower; Representatives from Various Circles in the Lüda Area Gather

Author:
Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang Dispatch: People in Northeast China enthusiastically commemorated the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Northeast China on August 15th. On the morning of the 15th, over a thousand representatives from various organizations, groups, and military units in Shenyang City held a memorial ceremony at the Soviet Red Army Martyrs Memorial Tower. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shenyang Branch, the Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League held lectures and gatherings on the evening of the 14th, and invited Soviet expatriates to participate. Provincial capitals also held celebrations on the 15th. Representatives from all walks of life in cities such as Jilin, Andong, Chengde, Harbin, and Ang’angxi went to the local Soviet Red Army Martyrs Memorial Towers to lay flowers and pay their respects. The Liaodong Provincial Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association invited Soviet experts and local government leaders to a joint soiree on the evening of the 14th. Harbin City held a celebration attended by over 20,000 people, featuring wonderful Chinese, Soviet, and Korean song and dance performances.

Over a thousand representatives from various circles in the Lüda area held a meeting at the Dalian Cultural Palace on the 15th to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the August 15th Liberation. Ouyang Qin, Secretary of the CPC Lüda District Committee, delivered a speech at the meeting. Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Chairman of the Lüda Administrative Office, Major General Razzazov, representative of the Soviet Garrison Command, and Zhao Guilan, a model factory protector, also spoke successively.

The people of Northeast China, who participated in the peace signature campaign and the movement against American aggression in Taiwan and Korea, strengthened their friendly feelings towards the great Soviet Union and became more determined to defend world peace while celebrating the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Northeast China.

### 东北各地纪念八一五  各界人民前往红军烈士塔前致祭  旅大地区各界代表集会

1950-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北各地纪念八一五
    各界人民前往红军烈士塔前致祭
    旅大地区各界代表集会
    【本报讯】据新华社沈阳电：东北各地人民热烈纪念“八一五”东北解放五周年。十五日晨，沈阳市各机关、团体、部队代表千余人，在苏联红军烈士纪念塔前举行祭墓典礼。中苏友好协会沈阳市分会及市文联、青年团市团委等单位均于十四日晚分别举行讲演会和联欢会，并邀请苏联侨民参加。各省省会也于十五日分别举行了庆祝活动。吉林、安东、承德、哈尔滨、昂昂溪等市各界人民代表，均前往当地的苏联红军烈士纪念塔献花致敬。辽东省中苏友好协会分会，于十四日晚邀请苏联专家及当地政府首长举行联欢晚会。哈尔滨市举行了两万余人的庆祝大会，会上表演了中、苏、朝等国的精彩歌舞节目。
    旅大地区各界代表千余人，十五日在大连市文化宫举行“八一五”解放五周年纪念大会。中共旅大区党委书记欧阳钦在会上发表演说外，旅大行署副主席乔传珏、苏联驻军指挥部代表拉兹扎佐夫少将及护厂模范赵桂兰等亦相继讲话。
    参加了和平签名运动和反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动的东北人民，在庆祝东北解放五周年的时候，愈益加强了对伟大苏联的友好感情，并更加坚定了保卫世界和平的决心。
