Title: National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China Calls on Organizations at All Levels to Launch Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 4

　　National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China
    Calls on Organizations at All Levels to Launch Peace Signature Campaign
    [Dispatch from this newspaper] The National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China issued a notice on August 22nd calling on all levels of organizations of the Textile Workers' Union of China to regard the peace signature campaign as one of the most important tasks at present. In accordance with the spirit of the appeal from the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace Congress, it is necessary to cultivate thousands of publicity and mobilization personnel to serve the peace signature movement, conduct extensive and in-depth publicity and mobilization work, and strive to mobilize all 470,000 textile workers and their families across the country to participate in the signature campaign.
     (National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China)

### 我国纺织工会全国委会  号召各级组织发动和平签名

1950-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国纺织工会全国委会
    号召各级组织发动和平签名
    【本报讯】中国纺织工会全国委员会于八月二十二日发出通知号召中国纺织工会各级组织必须把和平签名工作当作目前最重要的工作之一，要根据中国保卫世界和平大会委员会号召的精神，培养千万个为和平签名运动服务的宣传鼓动人员，广泛深入地进行宣传动员工作，争取发动全国四十七万纺织职工及其家属全部参加签名。
     （中国纺织工会全国委员会）
