Title: Speech by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China and Great Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch:

　　Speech by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace and Great Assembly Committee

　　Chairman Guo Moruo of the China Peace and World Security Conference Committee delivered a speech at the celebration of the "August 1st" Army Day demonstration against the American invasion of Taiwan and Korea:

　　Today is the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. In the history of the Chinese revolution, this day clearly marked the division between "armed revolution against armed counter-revolution." Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, after a long and arduous struggle, our armed revolutionary forces basically eliminated the armed counter-revolutionary forces on the mainland. The Chinese revolution has achieved a brilliant and unparalleled victory. We pay the highest respect to the heroic and skilled People's Liberation Army, to the Chinese Communist Party that serves the people wholeheartedly, and to the great people's leader, Chairman Mao.

　　The armed counter-revolutionary forces used to have an absolute advantage, but today only a small group of desperate fugitives remain, fleeing to Taiwan, attempting to prolong their lives.

　　The backing of the counter-revolutionary forces—American imperialism—once supported the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang with great power on the mainland, and has suffered a tragic defeat, but it still refuses to learn its lesson and refuses to let go even today.

　　On June 27, US President Truman issued a blatant and shameless statement: American imperialism is carrying out open armed aggression against Korea and our Taiwan. The US Seventh Fleet has accepted Truman's order and is operating in and around the Taiwan Strait, attempting to obstruct our liberation of Taiwan.

　　After suffering a major blow in Korea, American imperialism is attempting to transfer the troops of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang to fight in Korea, which is absolutely intolerable to all the Chinese people and all the people of Asia. We declare that American imperialism should bear full responsibility for the serious consequences caused by all these events.

　　Chairman Mao and Foreign Minister Zhou have already made clear and solemn statements on Truman's statement on June 28, which are unanimously supported by all the Chinese people. All of us Chinese people must "unite and make full preparations to defeat any provocation by American imperialism." All of us Chinese people must "be of one heart and one mind, and fight to the end to liberate Taiwan from the hands of American aggressors."

　　Today is Army Day, and all of us Chinese people must solemnly pledge on this glorious anniversary: We must unanimously support the heroic and skilled People's Liberation Army, smoothly liberate Taiwan, and plant our five-star red flag all over Taiwan Island, Penghu Island, and Kinmen Island, completing the complete liberation of Chinese territory.

　　In the face of our heroic and invincible People's Liberation Army, any natural or artificial obstacles cannot be stopped.

　　The Yellow River, the Yangtze River, and the Qiongzhou Strait cannot stop us, and neither can the Taiwan Strait. The six billion US dollars and countless airplanes, cannons, tanks, and warships that American imperialism previously used to help the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang could not stop us, and neither will the Seventh Fleet.

　　American imperialism's aggressive behavior has already been severely punished by the Korean people in Korea, and will undoubtedly be severely punished by our Chinese people in Taiwan. This is the just deserts of American imperialism—aggressors should bear all the consequences of aggressive behavior.

　　All the Chinese people unite to oppose the aggression of American imperialism!

　　Liberate Taiwan and capture Chiang Kai-shek alive!

　　Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

　　Long live the Chinese Communist Party!

　　Long live Chairman Mao!

### 中国和大委员会主席  郭沫若的演说

1950-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国和大委员会主席
    郭沫若的演说
    【新华社一日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若在庆祝“八一”建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会上的演说：
    今天是中国人民解放军建军节第二十三周年纪念。这在中国革命史上，是“武装的革命反对武装的反革命”明朗地划分的日子。在毛主席和中国共产党的领导之下，经过了坚苦卓绝的长期斗争，我们的武装的革命势力把武装的反革命势力，在大陆上基本肃清了。中国革命已经获得了辉煌无比的胜利。我们对于英勇善战的人民解放军，对于全心全意为人民服务的中国共产党，对于伟大的人民领袖毛主席致崇高的敬礼。
    武装的反革命势力在从前是一直占着绝对的优势的，但在今天只剩下一小撮的釜底游魂，逃到台湾去，企图苟延残喘了。
    反革命势力的靠山——美国帝国主义，在大陆上曾经以很大的力量支持蒋介石匪帮，已经遭受了悲惨的失败，但它却死不肯接受教训，今天都还不肯放手。
    六月二十七日美国总统杜鲁门竟发表了狂妄无耻的声明：美国帝国主义对于朝鲜和我们的台湾实行公开的武装侵略。美国的第七舰队已经接受了杜鲁门的命令，在台湾海峡及其附近一带活动，想阻挠我们对于台湾的解放。
    美帝国主义在朝鲜遭受了重大的打击之后，企图调运蒋介石匪帮的军队去朝鲜打仗，这是我们全中国人民和全亚细亚人民所绝对不能容忍的。我们声明，所有这些事件所引起的严重后果，美帝国主义是应负完全责任的。
    毛主席和周外长在六月二十八日对于杜鲁门的声明都已经有了明确而严正的表示，是全中国人民所一致拥护的。我们全中国人民要“团结起来，进行充分的准备，打败美帝国主义的任何挑衅”。我们全中国人民要“万众一心，为从美国侵略者手中解放台湾而奋斗到底”。
    今天是建军节，我们全中国人民更要在这个有光荣历史的纪念日，一致宣誓：我们要一致支援英勇善战的人民解放军，顺利地解放台湾，把我们的五星红旗插遍台湾岛、澎湖岛、金门岛，完成中国领土的全部解放。
    在我们英勇无敌的人民解放军面前，任何天然的或人为的阻碍都是抵挡不住的。
    黄河、长江、琼州海峡抵挡不住，台湾海峡也同样抵挡不住。美帝国主义以前帮助蒋介石匪帮的六十亿美元和无数的飞机大炮坦克军舰抵挡不住，第七舰队也同样抵挡不住。
    美帝国主义的侵略行为，在朝鲜已经受到了朝鲜人民的严重的惩治，在台湾毫无疑问也将受到我们中国人民的严重的惩治。这是美帝国主义的罪有应得——侵略者应该担负侵略行为的一切后果。
    全中国人民团结起来反对美帝国主义的侵略！
    解放台湾，活捉蒋介石！
    中国人民解放军万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
