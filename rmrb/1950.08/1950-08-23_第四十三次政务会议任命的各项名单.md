Title: List of Appointments Approved by the 43rd Government Administration Council

Author:
Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: The following is the list of appointments approved by the 43rd Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government:

  I. List of Staff Members of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government
    Director of the General Radio Administration: Li Qiang (Current Director of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director: Lu Zongcheng (Formerly a management engineer at the Shanghai International Radio Station).
    Director of the Long-Distance Telecommunications Administration: Wang Zigang (Current Deputy Director of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director: Hou Deyuan (Current Deputy Chief of the Technical Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
   　　　Hu Mingshi (Current Director of the Tianjin Telecommunications Bureau).
    Director of the City Telephone Administration: Meng Guimin (Current Chief of the Business Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Director of the Supply Bureau: Zhao Zhigang (Current Deputy Director of the Postal Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director: Li Yincang (Current Deputy Chief of the Equipment Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director of the Planning Department: Huang Ruzu (Current Deputy Chief of the Technical Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Director of the Personnel Department: Wang Shengang (Current Chief of the Personnel Section of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director: Sheng Tong (Current Deputy Chief of the Personnel Section of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Liang Maocheng (Current Deputy Chief of the Cadre Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director of the Finance Department: Huang Guanqin (Current Chief of the General Affairs Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Yu Xiumin (Current Chief of the Accounting Section of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Chief of the Labor and Wages Section: Zhou Ying (Female) (Former Chief of the Labor and Wages Section of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Chief of the International Relations Section: Wang Zigang (Current Deputy Director of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
    Deputy Director of the Telecommunications Technology Research Institute: Wu Shuoping (Current Deputy Chief of the Technical Section of the Telecommunications Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government).
   II. List of Presidents and Vice-Presidents of Regional Branches of the People's Bank of China
    President of the East China Regional Branch: Chen Mu (Former President of the East China Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    Vice-President: Xie Shoutian (Former Vice-President of the East China Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    President of the Central South Regional Branch: Chen Xiyu (Former Manager of the Central South Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    Vice-President: He Youqi (Former Deputy Manager of the Central South Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    President of the Southwest Regional Branch: Deng Chenxi (Former Manager of the Southwest Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    Vice-President: Zhang Maofu (Former Deputy Manager of the Southwest Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    President of the Northwest Regional Branch: Huang Yaguang (Former Manager of the Northwest Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
    Vice-President: Qiao Peixin (Former Deputy Manager of the Northwest Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China).
   III. List of President and Vice-Presidents of the Northeast Bank
    President of the Northeast Bank: Wang Qizhi (Current Deputy Manager of the Northeast Bank).
    Vice-President: Shen Yujie (Former Deputy Manager of the Northeast Bank).
    Zhang Shichang (Former Deputy Manager of the Northeast Bank).
