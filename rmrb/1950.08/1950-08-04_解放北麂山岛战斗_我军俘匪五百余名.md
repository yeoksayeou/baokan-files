Title: Battle to Liberate North Qieshan Island: Our Army Captures Over 500 Bandits
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 1

　　Liberation of North Qieshan Island Battle
    Our Army Captures Over 500 Bandits
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 3rd] In the battle to liberate North Qieshan Island, off the coast of Oujiang River in Zhejiang Province, on the 15th of last month, a unit of the People's Liberation Army's Third Field Army captured a total of 574 bandits, including Li Dongfan, the deputy commander of the bandit "Southeast Anti-Communist National Salvation Army," Li Dongming, the commander of the bandit's First Column, Zhou Fengwu, the chief of staff, and Zhou Kunyu, the deputy commander of the bandit's Eighth Column. In addition, 30 bandits were killed or wounded. Two speedboats, one American-made rocket launcher, fifteen light and heavy machine guns, 710 various guns, and some other military supplies were seized.

### 解放北麂山岛战斗  我军俘匪五百余名

1950-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放北麂山岛战斗
    我军俘匪五百余名
    【新华社南京三日电】人民解放军第三野战军一部于上月十五日解放浙江瓯江口外北麂山岛的战斗中，计俘匪“东南反共救国军”副军长李东藩、匪第一纵队指挥官李东明、参谋长周凤梧、匪第八纵队副指挥官周坤愈以下五百七十四名。另毙伤匪三十名。缴获汽艇二艘、美式火箭炮一门、轻重机枪十五挺、各种枪支七百一十支及其他军用物资一部。
