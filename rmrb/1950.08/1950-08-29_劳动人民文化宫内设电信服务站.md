Title: Telecommunications Service Station Established in the Working People's Cultural Palace
Author: 
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 6

To facilitate workers and citizens visiting the Cultural Palace, the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau has added a telecommunications service station inside the palace, starting from the 29th of this month. It will sell local and long-distance telephone services, and accept ordinary domestic telegrams for dispatch.

专栏：
