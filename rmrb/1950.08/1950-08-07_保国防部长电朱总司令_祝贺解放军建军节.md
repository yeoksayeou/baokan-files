Title: Bulgarian Defense Minister Sends Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu Congratulating the PLA on Army Day

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De received a congratulatory telegram from Bulgarian Defense Minister Panchevski on the "August 1st" Army Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

On the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of the combatants and commanders of the Bulgarian People's Army and myself, I extend my highest and heartfelt congratulations to you.

I wish you even greater victories in the struggle to liberate all of Chinese territory from the obstruction of Anglo-American imperialists and to defend the peaceful construction of the great Chinese people.

Bulgarian Defense Minister
Lieutenant General Panchevski

### 保国防部长电朱总司令  祝贺解放军建军节

1950-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　保国防部长电朱总司令
    祝贺解放军建军节
    【新华社五日讯】朱德总司令收到保加利亚国防部长潘雪夫斯基祝贺“八一”建军节的贺电，电文如下：中国人民解放军总部朱德总司令：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十三周年之际，谨代表保加利亚人民军队的战斗员和指挥员们以及我自己向您致以崇高的衷心的庆祝。
    谨祝你们在从英美帝国主义者阻挠下解放全部中国领土和捍卫伟大的中国人民的和平建设斗争中获得更大的胜利。
              保加利亚国防部长
              陆军中将潘雪夫斯基
