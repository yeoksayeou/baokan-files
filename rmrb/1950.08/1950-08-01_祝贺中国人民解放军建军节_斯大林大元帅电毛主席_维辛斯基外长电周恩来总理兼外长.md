Title: Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Marshal Stalin Cables Chairman Mao. Foreign Minister Vyshinsky Cables Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai.
Author:
Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st—Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received a congratulatory telegram from Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on the anniversary of the founding of our army. The telegram reads as follows:
To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

　　On the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China, please accept my sincere congratulations and warm wishes.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yo. Stalin
　　【Xinhua News Agency, 31st】Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received a congratulatory telegram from Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky on the anniversary of the founding of our army. The telegram reads as follows:
To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

　　On the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China, please accept my congratulations, Mr. Premier.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　An. Vyshinsky

### 祝贺中国人民解放军建军节  斯大林大元帅电毛主席  维辛斯基外长电周恩来总理兼外长

1950-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺中国人民解放军建军节
    斯大林大元帅电毛主席
    维辛斯基外长电周恩来总理兼外长
    【新华社三十一日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席接获苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅祝贺我国建军节的贺电，电文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    值此中华人民共和国人民解放军建军二十三周年纪念之际，请接受我衷心的庆贺与热烈的祝福。
          约·斯大林
    【新华社三十一日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理，接获苏联外长维辛斯基祝贺我国建军节的贺电，电文如下：北京中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来先生：
    值此中华人民共和国人民解放军建军二十三周年纪念之际，总理先生，请您接受我的祝贺。
            安·维辛斯基
