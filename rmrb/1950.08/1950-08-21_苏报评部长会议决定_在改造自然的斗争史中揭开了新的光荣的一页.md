Title: Su Bao Comments on the Ministerial Council Decision: Opening a New Glorious Page in the History of the Struggle to Transform Nature
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: TASS Moscow dispatch: All newspapers here on the 18th published the decision of the Soviet Ministerial Council on adopting new irrigation systems on their front pages, accompanied by commentary. The People's Truth newspaper published an editorial stating: This decision occupies an important position in the measures taken by the Party and the government to improve the efficiency of socialist agriculture. In the history of the Soviet people's struggle to transform nature and use the powerful forces of nature for the benefit of communist construction, this decision has opened a new glorious page. It will enable collectively farmed peasants to achieve new victories in the struggle to further develop socialist agriculture and guarantee a bumper harvest of agricultural products in our country.

　　News newspaper published an editorial emphasizing: This decision once again demonstrates the concern of the Soviet government, the Communist Party, and Comrade Stalin for further improving socialist agriculture.

### 苏报评部长会议决定  在改造自然的斗争史中揭开了新的光荣的一页

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏报评部长会议决定
    在改造自然的斗争史中揭开了新的光荣的一页
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：十八日此间各报皆以首页地位刊载苏联部长会议关于采用新的灌溉系统的决定，并予评论。真理报发表社论称：这一决定在党和政府所采取的提高社会主义农业效率的措施中，占有重要地位。在苏联人民改造自然并使用自然界强大力量以利共产主义建设的斗争史中，这一决定揭开了新的光荣的一页。它将使集体耕种的农民们在进一步发展社会主义农业和保证我国农产品丰收的斗争中，获得新的胜利。
    消息报发表社论强调称：该项决定再度显示了苏联政府、共产党和斯大林同志对于进一步提高社会主义农业所表示的关怀。
