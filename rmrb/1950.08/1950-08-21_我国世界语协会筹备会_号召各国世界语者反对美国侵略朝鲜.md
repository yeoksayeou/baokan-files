Title: Preparatory Committee of the All-China Esperanto League Calls on Esperantists of All Countries to Oppose US Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Esperanto League issued a declaration to Esperantists of all countries regarding the US imperialist war of aggression against Korea. The full text is as follows:

　　US imperialism has launched a war of aggression against the Korean people, carrying out brutal destruction and massacres, destroying peace in the Far East and threatening world peace.

　　We call on Esperantists in China and all countries around the world to expose the conspiracies and atrocities of US imperialism, support the Korean people's just war of liberation against aggression, expand the peace signature campaign, and call on peace-loving people to oppose US imperialism and force it to withdraw from Korea. Only in this way can peace in Korea and Asia be restored, and world peace be guaranteed.

　　We express our warm respect to the Korean people who are fighting for national liberation and wish them final victory in their heroic struggle.

　　Preparatory Committee of the All-China Esperanto League

### 我国世界语协会筹备会  号召各国世界语者反对美国侵略朝鲜

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　我国世界语协会筹备会
    号召各国世界语者反对美国侵略朝鲜
    【新华社十八日讯】中华全国世界语协会筹备会为美帝国主义侵略朝鲜的战争发表致各国世界语者宣言一件，全文如下：
    美帝国主义对朝鲜人民发动了侵略的战争，进行了残酷的破坏和屠杀，破坏了远东的和平并威胁着世界和平。
    我们号召全中国及世界各国的世界语者们揭穿美帝国主义的阴谋与兽行，支援朝鲜人民反侵略的正义的解放战争，扩大和平签名运动，号召爱好和平的人类反对美帝国主义，使它退出朝鲜。只有这样朝鲜与亚洲的和平才能恢复，世界和平才得到保障。
    我们对为民族解放而斗争的朝鲜人民表示热烈的敬意，并愿他们在其英勇的斗争中取得最后的胜利。
     中华全国世界语协会筹备会
