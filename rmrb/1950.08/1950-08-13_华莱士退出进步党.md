Title: Wallace Quits Progressive Party
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 4

　　Wallace Quits Progressive Party
    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch] Tass, New York, 9th Dispatch: Progressive Party National Secretary Baldwin announced: Only two members of the Progressive Party National Committee resigned due to disagreeing with the committee's recent statement opposing American military intervention in Korea. These two resigning members are Wallace and Yale Law School Professor Emerson. Baldwin stated: Progressive Party organizations in twenty-one states all agree with the National Committee's statement. Baldwin also stated: The fight will continue with greater strength than ever before, until the world can once again unite and regain peace.

### 华莱士退出进步党

1950-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　华莱士退出进步党
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约九日讯：进步党全国书记鲍尔温宣称：进步党全国委员会只有两位委员因不同意委员会最近反对美国军事干涉朝鲜的声明而退党。这两位退党的委员是华莱士与耶鲁大学法学院教授伊莫逊。鲍尔温称：进步党二十一个州的组织均赞同全国委员会的声明。鲍尔温又称：战斗将以比任何时候都大的力量继续下去，直至世界能够再度团结，重获和平时为止。
