Title: Soviet Peace Signature Campaign Successfully Concludes: Over 110 Million People Participated

Author:

Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to a TASS report from Moscow, the Soviet Peace Advocacy Committee issued a statement on the 3rd, announcing the successful conclusion of the signature campaign in the Soviet Union for the Stockholm Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Partisans for Peace. With the exception of a few remote areas where signatures are nearing completion, a total of 115,527,940 Soviet people signed the peace declaration from June 30th to August 1st. Through this unanimous signing action, the entire adult population of the Soviet Union has demonstrated their firm will to oppose war, demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, and condemn the government of any country that first uses atomic weapons as war criminals.

The statement concluded by saying: After the end of the peace signature campaign, the people of all nationalities in the Soviet Union, under the leadership of the Soviet government, will continue to be at the forefront of the struggle to consolidate world peace, as in the past.

### 苏联和平签名运动胜利结束  一亿一千余万人参加了签名

1950-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联和平签名运动胜利结束
    一亿一千余万人参加了签名
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联拥护和平委员会三日发表声明称：在苏联为世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩宣言征集签名的运动，已告结束。除某些边远地区的签名即将完成外，自六月三十日起至八月一日止，苏联人民总计有一亿一千五百二十七万五千九百四十人在和平宣言上签了名。苏联全体成年人已用一致签名的行动，表现了他们反对战争，要求禁止原子武器，并斥责首先使用原子武器的任何国家的政府为战犯的坚定意志。
    声明末称：和平签名运动结束以后，苏联各族人民将在苏联政府领导下，像过去一样继续走在巩固世界和平的斗争的最前列。
