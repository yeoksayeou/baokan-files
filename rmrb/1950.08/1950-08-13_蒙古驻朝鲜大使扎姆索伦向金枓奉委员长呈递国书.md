Title: Mongolian Ambassador to North Korea, Zhamtsarangiin Sambuu, Presents Credentials to Chairman Kim Tu-bong
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 11th: Zhamtsarangiin Sambuu, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, along with First Secretary Khorloo, Sangiymidov, and attendants, arrived in Pyongyang on the 10th. Welcoming them at the station were Park Tong-chul, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Cho Chung-hwa, Director of Protocol; Kim Sung-hak, Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee; Sidorov, the Soviet Ambassador to Korea; Chai Junwu, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Korea; and Shandor, the Hungarian Minister to Korea, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 12th: Zhamtsarangiin Sambuu, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, accompanied by First Secretary Khorloo, presented his credentials to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the 11th. Present were Park Tong-chul, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea; Kang Ryang-wook, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; and Cho Chung-hwa, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others.

### 蒙古驻朝鲜大使扎姆索伦向金枓奉委员长呈递国书

1950-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古驻朝鲜大使扎姆索伦向金枓奉委员长呈递国书
    【新华社平壤十一日电】蒙古人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首任特命全权大使扎姆索伦·桑布，偕一等秘书霍尔诺、桑居米多夫及随员等于十日抵达平壤。到车站欢迎者有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外交部副部长朴东楚，仪礼司长赵清和，平壤市人民委员长金成学，苏联驻朝鲜大使西地科夫，我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办柴军武，匈牙利驻朝鲜公使鲜道尔等人。
    【新华社平壤十二日电】蒙古人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首任特命全权大使扎姆索伦偕一等秘书霍尔诺，十一日向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉呈递国书。在场者有朝鲜外交部副部长朴东楚，最高人民会议常任委员会书记长康良煜，外交部仪礼司司长赵清和等。
