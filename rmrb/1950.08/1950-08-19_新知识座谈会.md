Title: New Knowledge Seminar

Author:

Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 6

The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association will hold its 83rd New Knowledge Seminar at 7:30 PM today (the 19th) at the Xiehe Auditorium. Zhu Chuan, Director of the Financial Management Department of the Head Office of the People's Bank of China, will report on the "National Financial Conference." All are welcome to attend the lecture. The association has also prepared temporary admission tickets, which can be obtained by contacting them with a letter.

(Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会

1950-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会今（十九）日下午七点半，在协和礼堂举行第八十三次新知识座谈会，请人民银行总行金融管理处朱川处长报告“全国金融会议情况”，望大家踊跃参加听讲，该会另备有临时入场券，各界可持函洽领。
          （桢）
