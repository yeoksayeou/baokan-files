Title: Korean People's Army Annihilates an Enemy Regiment on the East Coast; Advancing Troops Surround a Large Enemy Force and are in the Process of Eliminating Them
Author:
Date: 1950-08-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th: TASS, Pyongyang, 9th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war bulletin on the evening of the 8th that units of the People's Army continued to engage in fierce battles with US troops and the Syngman Rhee puppet army on all fronts. People's Army units advancing south of Cheongsong on the eastern coast have completely annihilated one enemy regiment. On the same front, advancing units of the People's Army have surrounded a large enemy force and are now engaged in combat to eliminate it. As a result of operations in this area, the 18th enemy regiment, one motorized unit, one artillery division, and several other units have been routed. Captured war materiel includes four 105mm howitzers and 70 vehicles.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th: TASS, Pyongyang, 8th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war bulletin on the morning of the 8th that units of the People's Army continued to engage in fierce battles with US troops on all fronts. The People's Army Air Force bravely engaged US aircraft, and continued to bomb enemy concentration areas, rear lines of communication, and military targets, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. In Gyeongsangnam-do and Gyeongsangbuk-do, guerrillas intensified their activities to support the offensive of the People's Army units and continued to harass the enemy's rear.

　　US military aircraft continue to barbarously bomb towns and cities in both North and South Korea, including the Pyongyang area. On the afternoon of August 7, more than thirty four-engine American aircraft dropped hundreds of bombs on Pyongyang and its suburbs during a three-hour air raid. As a result, buildings housing newspapers, hospitals, cultural and educational institutions, and many residences were destroyed. Casualties included patients and elementary school students.

### 朝鲜人民军在东海岸歼敌一团  前进部队包围大队敌人正消灭中

1950-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在东海岸歼敌一团
    前进部队包围大队敌人正消灭中
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社平壤九日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八日晚发表战报称：人民军各部在全线续与美军及李承晚伪军进行激战。在青松以南、东部海岸推进的人民军部队，已完全歼灭敌军一团。在同一方面，人民军前进部队包围了大队敌军，现已开始战斗，以期消灭该部敌军。该地作战结果，已击溃敌军第十八团、摩托化部队一团、炮兵一师以及其他若干单位。战利品中包括一○五公厘炮四门及汽车七十辆。
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社平壤八日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八日晨发表战报称：人民军各部继续在全线与美军进行激战。人民军的空军英勇地与美机交锋，并继续轰炸敌军集结地带、敌后交通线及军事目标，使敌方遭到重大损失。在庆尚南道与庆尚北道，游击队为支持人民军各部的进攻，加紧活动，继续困扰敌军后方。
    美军飞机不断野蛮地轰炸南北朝鲜各城镇，包括平壤地区。八月七日下午，三十余架四引擎的美国飞机于历时三小时的空袭中，在平壤及其郊区投弹数百枚。结果报馆、医院、文教机关等的房屋及许多住宅被毁。伤亡者包括病人及小学生。
