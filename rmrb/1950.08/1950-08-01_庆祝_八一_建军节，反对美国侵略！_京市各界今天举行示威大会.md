Title: Celebrating "August 1st" Army Day, Oppose American Aggression! Beijing's Circles Hold Demonstration Rally Today
Author:
Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 1
　
　　Celebrating “August 1st” Army Day, Oppose American Aggression!
    Beijing's Circles Hold Demonstration Rally Today

    [News from Beijing News Agency] Today (August 1st) at 8:00 AM, people from all walks of life in Beijing will hold a rally in front of the Taihe Gate inside the Meridian Gate to celebrate "August 1st" Army Day and oppose American aggression in Taiwan and Korea. It is estimated that 40,000 troops, workers, students, and staff from various organizations will participate in the rally. A demonstration parade will follow the meeting. The parade route is from the Meridian Gate, and splits into two routes at the Chinese Gate, one going east via Dongjiaomin Lane and Dongdan to Dongsi, and the other going west via Xijiaomin Lane and Xidan to Xisi. Each route will then return to its respective location.

    [Further News] From 7:30 AM to 10:00 AM on August 1st, the Central People's Broadcasting Station's domestic and international services will simultaneously broadcast the live coverage of the "Beijing's Circles Celebrating 'August 1st' Army Day and Opposing American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea Demonstration Rally." Mayor of Beijing, Nie Rongzhen will deliver the opening remarks at the rally, and there will be many important speeches. Speakers include Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De; Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Guo Moruo; Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Li Jishen; Chairman of the China Democratic League, Zhang Lan; Convenor of the Standing Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Huang Yanpei; Korean Ambassador, Li Zhouyuan; Lin Kengsheng of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Ningyi; Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Cai Chang; and Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Liao Chengzhi. The live broadcast of the rally will also be broadcast simultaneously by people's radio stations across the country.

### 庆祝“八一”建军节，反对美国侵略！  京市各界今天举行示威大会

1950-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝“八一”建军节，反对美国侵略！
    京市各界今天举行示威大会
    【京市新闻处讯】今日（八月一日）上午八时，北京各界将在午门内太和门前举行庆祝“八一”建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会，预计参加大会的部队、工人、学生及机关团体工作人员将达四万人。会后并举行示威游行。游行路线系由午门出场，至中华门分东西两路进行，东路经东交民巷、东单至东四；西路经西交民巷、西单至西四。然后各路自行返归驻地。
    【又讯】八月一日上午七时三十分至十时，中央人民广播电台国内台及国际台将同时转播“北京各界庆祝‘八一’建军节、反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会”实况。大会由北京市聂荣臻市长致开会词，并有许多重要演讲。演讲人有中国人民解放军总司令朱德、中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若、中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、中国民主同盟主席张澜、中国民主建国会常务委员会召集人黄炎培、朝鲜大使李周渊、台湾民主自治同盟林铿生、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记、中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志等。大会实况转播节目并将由全国各地人民广播电台联播。
