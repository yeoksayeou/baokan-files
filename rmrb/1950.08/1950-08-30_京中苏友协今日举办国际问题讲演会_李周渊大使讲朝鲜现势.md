Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold International Affairs Lecture Today; Ambassador Li Zhouyuan to Speak on the Current Situation in Korea
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 6

　　Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold International Affairs Lecture Today
    Ambassador Li Zhouyuan to Speak on the Current Situation in Korea
    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is scheduled to hold a lecture on "International Affairs" at 7:30 pm today (August 30th) at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall. Mr. Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, will speak on "The Korean People's Struggle for the Unification and Independence of the Fatherland." After the meeting, a newsreel will be shown of the recent victorious advance of the Korean People's Army, liberating major cities and defeating the American army and the Li Seung-man bandit gang. If it rains, the date will be postponed.
     (Chen Yingnan)

### 京中苏友协今日举办国际问题讲演会  李周渊大使讲朝鲜现势
陈应楠
1950-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　京中苏友协今日举办国际问题讲演会
    李周渊大使讲朝鲜现势
    北京市中苏友好协会定于今日（三十日）下午七时半在中山公园音乐堂举办“国际问题”讲演会，请朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊先生讲《朝鲜人民为了祖国的统一独立而斗争》。会后并放映最近朝鲜人民军胜利进军解放各大城市，击溃美军及李承晚匪帮的新闻记录片。如果下雨，改期举行。
     （陈应楠）
