Title: Our Country's Ambassador to Romania, Wang Youping, Arrives in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency: Bucharest, August 8th – According to the Romanian News Agency on the 8th: The staff of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China, led by Ambassador Wang Youping, arrived in Bucharest on the evening of the 8th. Representatives of the Romanian People's Republic's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, members of the diplomatic missions of various countries in Romania, and delegations from various mass organizations in Bucharest welcomed them at the station. The welcomers greeted the representatives of the heroic Chinese people with prolonged applause.
　　
Voiculescu, Chairman of the Provisional Committee of the Bucharest Municipal People's Council, extended warm respects to Ambassador Wang and all the staff on behalf of the citizens of Bucharest.

### 我国驻罗马尼亚大使  王幼平抵达布加勒斯特

1950-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国驻罗马尼亚大使
    王幼平抵达布加勒斯特
    【本报讯】新华社消息：据罗马尼亚通讯社八日讯：由王幼平大使率领的中华人民共和国大使馆工作人员，已于八日晚抵达布加勒斯特。到车站欢迎者有罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部代表，各国驻罗外交使团人员以及布加勒斯特各群众团体的代表团。欢迎者以历久不息的鼓掌欢迎英雄的中国人民的代表。
    布加勒斯特市人民议会临时委员会主席沃伊库列斯库代表布加勒斯特市的公民，向王大使暨全体人员致热烈的敬意。
