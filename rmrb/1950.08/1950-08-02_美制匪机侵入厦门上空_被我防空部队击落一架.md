Title: American-made Bandit Plane Intrudes Over Xiamen, One Shot Down by Our Air Defense Forces
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 31st -- On the morning of July 29th, at approximately 7:00 AM, two American-made Kuomintang bandit planes intruded over Xiamen. Under the intense firing of our ground air defense forces, one P-47 fighter plane was shot down, the fuselage was shattered, and the pilots were all burned to death. The other bandit plane fled in panic.

### 美制匪机侵入厦门上空  被我防空部队击落一架

1950-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　美制匪机侵入厦门上空
    被我防空部队击落一架
    【新华社福州三十一日电】七月二十九日上午七时许，美制国民党匪机两架侵入厦门上空，在我地面防空部队密集射击下，Ｐ四七型战斗机一架被击落，机身粉碎，驾驶员全被烧死。另一架匪机即仓惶逃去。
