    Weekly Literary Lecture
    The weekly literary lecture co-organized by the Popular Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department will feature Yang Shaoxuan as the speaker this week. The topic will be: "People's Life and Opera Literature." After the lecture, Yu Yunfang, Lu Chunshan, and Song Zhicheng will perform Jingyun Dagu: "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix." Address: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: Sunday morning at 9:30 AM. Admission with long-term or temporary tickets.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部合办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请杨绍萱主讲，讲题：“人民生活与戏曲文学”。会后由于韵芳、露春山、宋志成演唱京韵大鼓：“百鸟朝凤”。地址：青年宫交谊厅。时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场。
