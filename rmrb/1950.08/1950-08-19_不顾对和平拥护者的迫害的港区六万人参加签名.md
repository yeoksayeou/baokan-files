Title: 60,000 People in the Hong Kong Area Participate in Signing, Undeterred by Persecution of Peace Advocates
Author:
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 17th, TASS: According to reports from Trieste: The peace movement in the city of Trieste and the entire Zone A of the Free Territory of Trieste, undeterred by the persecution of peace advocates and the malicious propaganda by reactionary newspapers against the struggle for peace and the demand to ban atomic bombs, is attracting an increasing number of participants.
    Sixty thousand people have signed the Stockholm Appeal. More than ninety percent of workers in basic industries and dockworkers have signed the Stockholm Appeal. The signature campaign is also progressing smoothly among railway workers and workers in various municipal enterprises.
    In many villages inhabited by Slovenians, ninety-seven to one hundred percent of the residents have already signed. Numerous peace rallies have been held in various districts and villages of the Trieste headquarters under the slogan "Trieste Needs Peace."

### 不顾对和平拥护者的迫害的港区六万人参加签名

1950-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　不顾对和平拥护者的迫害的港区六万人参加签名
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社索非亚十七日讯：据的里雅斯特来讯：的里雅斯特城和的里雅斯特自由区甲区全区的和平运动，不顾对和平拥护者的迫害，和反动报纸对和平斗争和禁止原子弹的要求进行恶意的宣传，参加的群众已日渐增多。
    在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名者已有六万人。基本工业的工人和码头工人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名者已在百分之九十以上。征集签名运动在铁路工人和各市政企业的工人中间也在顺利进行着。
    在斯洛文人所居住的许多村庄内，百分之九十七到百分之百的居民都已经签了名。的里雅斯特本部各区和各村庄在“的里雅斯特需要和平”的口号下举行了无数次的和平大会。
