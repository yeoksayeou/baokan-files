Title: "August 1st" Issue of Xikang Daily Published in Ya'an
Author:
Date: 1950-08-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 13th: The Xikang Daily was launched on August 1st in Ya'an. The newspaper is published daily in a four-page format.

### 西康日报“八一”在雅安创刊

1950-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　西康日报“八一”在雅安创刊
    【新华社重庆十三日电】西康日报已于八月一日在雅安创刊。该报每日出四开一张。
