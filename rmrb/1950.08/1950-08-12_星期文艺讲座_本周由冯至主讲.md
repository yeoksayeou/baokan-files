Title: Sunday Literary Lecture: This Week, Feng Zhi to Lecture

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-12
Page: 6

　　Sunday Literary Lecture
    This Week, Feng Zhi to Lecture
    The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly organized by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Feng Zhi this week. The topic is: "The Peace Movement in Eastern European Democratic Countries" – A demonstration of reportage literature. Those from all walks of life who wish to attend the lecture can obtain admission tickets at the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座  本周由冯至主讲

1950-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    本周由冯至主讲
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合举办的星期文艺讲座，本周请冯至主讲，讲题是：“东欧民主国家的和平运动”。——一个报告文学的示范。各界愿往听讲者，可于星期六往青年服务部领取入场券。
