Title: National Trade Union Women Workers' Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1950-08-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The National Trade Union Women Workers' Conference convened by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions opened in Beijing on the 16th. The central task of the conference is to determine the tasks and methods of women workers' work in the future, and secondly, to study how to carry out the work of staff and workers' families based on the existing experience in various regions.
    At the meeting held on the 16th, Cai Chang, Minister of the Women Workers' Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered the opening speech. Mao Qihua, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, Deng Yingchao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the China Textile Trade Union, also spoke at the meeting. Yang Zhihua, Deputy Minister of the Women Workers' Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, gave a report on the work of women workers in the past year.
    The speech by Mao Qihua, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, fully demonstrated the People's Government's concern for women workers. He asked the representatives attending the meeting to provide more opinions and feedback on the situation to the Ministry of Labor. Deng Yingchao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, pointed out that the Women's Federation and the Women Workers' Department of the Trade Union should further strengthen cooperation on the basis of existing work, so as to give play to the leading role of women workers in women's work. Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, affirmed the achievements made in the work of women workers in the past year, and he explained that strengthening the work of women workers is to strengthen the work of the trade union. Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the China Textile Trade Union, pointed out that in the work of the Textile Trade Union, due to the good work of women workers, the trade union work has achieved certain results, and she pointed out the importance of family work based on the actual work experience of the Textile Trade Union.
    Fifty-one representatives attended the meeting, including the heads of the women workers' departments of trade unions in various regions and industries, as well as women workers' committee members of some grassroots organizations. The meeting is scheduled to close on the 22nd.

### 全国工会女工工作会议开幕

1950-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国工会女工工作会议开幕
    【新华社十六日讯】中华全国总工会召开的全国工会女工工作会议已于十六日在北京开幕。会议的中心任务是确定今后女工工作的任务和作法，其次是根据各地已有的经验，研究如何开展职工家属工作等问题。
    在十六日举行的会议上，中华全国总工会女工部部长蔡畅致开幕词。中央人民政府劳动部副部长毛齐华，中华全国民主妇联副主席邓颖超，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中国纺织工会主席陈少敏均在会上讲话。中华全国总工会女工部副部长杨之华在会上作了一年来女工工作的报告。
    中央人民政府劳动部副部长毛齐华的讲话，充分表现了人民政府对女工的关切。他要求到会代表向劳动部提意见多反映情况。中华全国妇女联合会副主席邓颖超指出：民主妇联与工会的女工部应在现有的工作基础上进一步密切合作，以发挥女工在妇女工作中的领导作用。中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一肯定了一年来女工工作已有的成绩，他说明加强女工工作就是加强工会工作。中国纺织工会主席陈少敏指出：在纺织工会工作中，由于作好了女工工作，因而使工会工作获得了一定的成绩，她并根据纺织工会的实际工作的经验指出家属工作的重要性。
    出席这次会议的代表有各地区各产业工会女工部长及一部分基层组织的女工委员共五十一人。会议预定在二十二日闭幕。
