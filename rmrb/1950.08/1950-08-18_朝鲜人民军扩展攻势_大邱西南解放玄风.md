Title: Korean People's Army Expands Offensive: Hyunpung Liberated Southwest of Daegu
Author:
Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th: TASS, Pyongyang, 16th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of August 16th: The People's Army units continue to carry out offensive battles on all fronts. The People's Army units advancing towards Hyunpung crushed the enemy's stubborn resistance and liberated Hyunpung, an important stronghold of the enemy's defense line southwest of Daegu, on August 12th.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in a battle report on the evening of August 16th: People's Army units are continuing to launch fierce attacks on all fronts, giving powerful blows to the stubborn U.S. military units and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army. On the east bank of the Nakdong River, the People's Army units repelled the counter-attack by the U.S. infantry and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army under the cover of U.S. tank and artillery units, inflicting great damage on the enemy. In the battles in this area, the People's Army units killed, wounded, and captured a large number of enemy troops, seizing seven 105mm guns, ten tanks, three armored vehicles, fifteen 81mm mortars, twelve other large and small guns, more than forty trucks, and some other weapons and ammunition.

### 朝鲜人民军扩展攻势  大邱西南解放玄风

1950-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军扩展攻势
    大邱西南解放玄风
    【新华社平壤十七日电】塔斯社平壤十六日电：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八月十六日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各线继续进行攻势战。向玄风推进的人民军部队击溃敌军的顽抗，于八月十二日解放了大邱西南敌军防线的重要据点玄风。
    【新华社平壤十七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十六日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上予顽抗的美军部队与李承晚残余伪军以强有力的打击，继续进行猛烈的进攻。人民军部队在洛东江东岸，击退了美军坦克部队与炮兵部队掩护下的美军步兵部队及李承晚残余伪军的反攻，予敌人以莫大损伤。人民军部队在该地区的战斗中，毙伤与俘虏敌军甚众，缴获一零五公厘炮七门，坦克车十辆，装甲车三辆，八一迫击炮十五门，其他大小炮十二门，汽车四十余辆及其他武器弹药一部。
