Title: This Publication Also Printed as a Separate Booklet for Distribution
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 5

　　This Publication Also Printed as a Separate Booklet for Distribution
    Starting from issue 57, each issue of this publication will also be printed as a separate 16mo booklet, entrusted to the Xinhua Bookstore General Administration for distribution, with a base price of six jiao per issue. Those who wish to subscribe to or purchase individual copies of this publication should contact local Xinhua Bookstores.

### 本刊另印单行本发行

1950-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

　　本刊另印单行本发行
    本刊自五十七期起每期均另印成十六开单行本，委托新华书店总管理处发行，每期基价六角。订阅或零购本刊者，请到各地新华书店接洽。
