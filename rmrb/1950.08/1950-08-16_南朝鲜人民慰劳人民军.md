Title: South Korean People Console the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th (TASS): Pyongyang Radio broadcast: Farmers in various districts of southern Korea are sending grain and vegetables to the People's Army, which liberated them from the Syngman Rhee fascist police regime. Farmers in 13 districts of Gyeonggi Province have already sent 96,000 kilograms of grain and a large amount of vegetables.

### 南朝鲜人民慰劳人民军

1950-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜人民慰劳人民军
    【新华社讯】塔斯社平壤十二日电：平壤电台广播：朝鲜南部各区农民都把谷物和蔬菜送给把他们从李承晚法西斯警察政权下解放出来的人民军。京畿道十三区农民已送出九万六千公斤的谷物和大量的蔬菜。
