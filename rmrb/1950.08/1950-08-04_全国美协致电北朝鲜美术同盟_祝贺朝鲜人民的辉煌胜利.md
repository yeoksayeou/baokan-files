Title: All-China Federation of Art Circles Sends a Telegram to the North Korean Fine Arts Association Congratulating the Korean People on Their Brilliant Victory
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 3

　　The All-China Federation of Art Circles Sends a Telegram to the North Korean Fine Arts Association
    Congratulating the Korean People on Their Brilliant Victory

    [Reported by this newspaper] The All-China Federation of Art Workers Associations sent a telegram to the North Korean Fine Arts Association on the 1st of this month, stating:

    "We warmly congratulate the Korean people on the brilliant victory they have achieved in the liberation war against aggression and for the independence and unification of their motherland, and extend our highest respect.

    The heroic struggle of the Korean people has shattered the aggression and conspiracies of US imperialism and its lackeys against the people of Asia, and dealt a powerful blow to the war criminals who attempt to undermine world peace.

    The Chinese people and all peace-loving, democratic, and free people around the world stand on your side and are enthusiastically supporting you.

    We, on behalf of the Chinese art workers, solemnly express to you: We will do everything in our power to support your just war and will respond to US imperialism's aggression against China by liberating Taiwan.

    The Korean people and the Chinese people will surely achieve victory."

### 全国美协致电北朝鲜美术同盟  祝贺朝鲜人民的辉煌胜利

1950-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国美协致电北朝鲜美术同盟
    祝贺朝鲜人民的辉煌胜利
    【本报讯】中华全国美术工作者协会于本月一日致电北朝鲜美术同盟称：
    “我们热烈祝贺朝鲜人民在反对侵略争取祖国独立与统一的解放战争中所获的辉煌的胜利，并致以最崇高的敬意。
    朝鲜人民的英勇斗争，粉碎了美帝国主义及其奴仆们对亚洲人民所施的侵略和阴谋，给企图破坏世界和平的战犯们一个有力的打击。
    中国人民及全世界爱好和平，民主，自由的人民，都站在你们这一边，都在热诚地声援着你们。
    我们代表中国的美术工作者向你们郑重表示：我们将尽一切力量，支援你们所进行的正义战争。并将以解放台湾，答复美帝国主义对中国的侵略。
    朝鲜人民和中国人民一定会取得胜利。”
