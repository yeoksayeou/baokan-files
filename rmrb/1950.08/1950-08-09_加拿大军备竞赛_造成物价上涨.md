Title: Canada's Arms Race Causes Price Increases
Author:
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, 6th (TASS): Canada's arms race has caused the prices of major necessities to rise. According to figures from the Statistics Bureau, the food price index increased from 209 to 214.3 in June. Rent has also increased. In July, the overall cost of living index reached a record level. Newspapers predict that prices will continue to increase.

### 加拿大军备竞赛  造成物价上涨

1950-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　加拿大军备竞赛
    造成物价上涨
    【新华社讯】塔斯社渥大瓦六日电：加拿大的军备竞赛已使主要必需品价格上升。依照统计局的数字，在六月间，食物价格的指数从二○九增加到二一四·三。房租也提高了。在七月间，生活费用的总指数达到创纪录的水平。各报预料物价还要继续增加。
