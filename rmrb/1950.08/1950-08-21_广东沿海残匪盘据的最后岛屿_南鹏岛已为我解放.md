Title: The Last Island in Guangdong Coastline Held by Remnant Bandits, Nanpeng Island, Has Been Liberated by Us
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5

　　The Last Island in Guangdong Coastline Held by Remnant Bandits
    Nanpeng Island Has Been Liberated by Us
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 20th] A unit of the People's Liberation Army Fourth Field Army liberated Nanpeng Island (located in the sea southeast of Yangjiang) on the morning of the 9th, the last island in the Guangdong coastline held by Kuomintang remnant bandits. After landing, the People's Liberation Army annihilated over 400 remnant bandits on the island in just two hours of combat.

### 广东沿海残匪盘据的最后岛屿  南鹏岛已为我解放

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　广东沿海残匪盘据的最后岛屿
    南鹏岛已为我解放
    【新华社广州二十日电】人民解放军第四野战军某部九日晨解放广东沿海国民党残匪盘据的最后岛屿南鹏岛（在阳江东南海上）。解放军在登陆后仅历两小时的战斗，即歼岛上残匪四百余人。
