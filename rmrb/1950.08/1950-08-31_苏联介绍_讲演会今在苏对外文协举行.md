Title: "Soviet Union Introduction" Lecture to be Held Today at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 6

    "Soviet Union Introduction" Lecture to be Held Today at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Union's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries have scheduled a "Soviet Union Introduction" lecture for today (August 31st) at 7:30 PM at No. 19 Nanheyan. Mr. 谢列金 [Xiè Lièjīn] will speak on "The Soviet Union's Struggle for Peace" (one aspect of Soviet foreign policy). All those holding admission tickets or "Soviet Union Introduction" long-term lecture passes may attend.
    If any branch of the Friendship Association wishes to attend, they may obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch at No. 2 Yuheqiao, Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### “苏联介绍”讲演会今在苏对外文协举行

1950-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

　　“苏联介绍”讲演会今在苏对外文协举行
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（三十一日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行“苏联介绍”讲演会，请谢列金讲“苏联为和平而斗争”（苏联对外政策的一方面），凡持有入场券或“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各支会如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信前往御河桥二号北京市中苏友协索取入场券。
