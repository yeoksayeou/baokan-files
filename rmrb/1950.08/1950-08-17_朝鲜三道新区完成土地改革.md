Title: Land Reform Completed in Three Newly Liberated Provinces of Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th (TASS dispatch): The land reform in the newly liberated rural areas of Gyeonggi Province, Gangwon Province, and Hwanghae Province in Korea has been successfully completed. The peasants who obtained land warmly thank the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for giving them land and freedom, and promise to supply more grain to the front lines.

Currently, land reform is being implemented in Chungcheongbuk-do, Chungcheongnam-do, and Jeollanam-do. Preparations for land reform have also begun in the liberated areas of Gyeongsangnam-do and Gyeongsangbuk-do, and the peasants are actively participating.

### 朝鲜三道新区完成土地改革

1950-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜三道新区完成土地改革
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社平壤十五日讯：京畿道、江原道及黄海道新解放区乡村的土地改革，已经胜利完成。获得土地的农民热烈感激给他们以土地和自由的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府，并保证以更多的粮食供给前线。
    现在，忠清北道、忠清南道和全罗南道正实行土地改革。庆尚南道与庆尚北道解放了的地区也已着手准备土地改革，农民都积极参加。
