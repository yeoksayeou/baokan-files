Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Invited Ambassador Li Zhouyuan to Give a Speech Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 1

　　Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Invited Ambassador Li Zhouyuan to Give a Speech Yesterday
    [Our Newspaper] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held an international affairs lecture at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall at 7:30 pm on the 30th, inviting Mr. Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, to speak on "The Korean People's Struggle for the Unification and Independence of the Motherland." At the meeting, Professor Xu Deheng, Vice President of the Association, first delivered an opening speech, and briefly reported on the heroic struggle of the Korean people he witnessed during his trip to Korea, and his impressions of the trip. Ambassador Li then gave a speech, in which he gave a detailed report on the heroic struggle of the Korean people since "August 15th" and the peaceful construction of People's Korea. After the meeting, a newsreel of the Korean People's Liberation War was shown.
    Before yesterday's meeting, many comrades were unable to attend the lecture due to the rain. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to broadcast the full text of Ambassador Li's speech on the 30th on the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station from 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm tomorrow (September 1st). Please pay attention to listening.

### 北京市中苏友好协会昨邀李周渊大使讲演

1950-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市中苏友好协会昨邀李周渊大使讲演
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会于三十日晚七时半在中山公园音乐堂举行国际问题讲演会，请朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊先生讲“朝鲜人民为祖国的统一独立而斗争”。会上首先由该会副会长许德珩教授致开会词，同时简短报告了此次去朝鲜见到的朝鲜人民英勇的斗争事迹，和此行的观感。继由李大使讲演，他对“八一五”以来朝鲜人民英勇的斗争与人民朝鲜的和平建设，作了详尽的报告。会后并放映了朝鲜人民解放战争的新闻记录片。
    昨日开会前，因雨许多同志未能前往听讲，北京市中苏友协定于明日（九月一日）晚八时至九时在北京市人民广播电台，将李大使三十日的讲词全文广播，望大家注意收听。
