Title: Sukarno and Hatta Send Telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Thanking Them for Congratulations on Indonesian Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th: President of the Republic of Indonesia, Azmed Sukarno, and Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mohammad Hatta, via the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of their country, telegraphed Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on August 23rd, respectively, to express their gratitude for their telegrams of congratulations on the country's Independence Day.

### 苏加诺哈达电毛主席周总理  感谢对印尼独立纪念日的祝贺

1950-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏加诺哈达电毛主席周总理
    感谢对印尼独立纪念日的祝贺
    【新华社三十日讯】印度尼西亚共和国总统阿兹默德·苏加诺及总理兼外交部部长莫罕默德·哈达，经该国外交部于八月二十三日分别电复我中央人民政府毛泽东主席及周恩来总理兼外长，对他们电贺该国独立纪念日表示感谢。
