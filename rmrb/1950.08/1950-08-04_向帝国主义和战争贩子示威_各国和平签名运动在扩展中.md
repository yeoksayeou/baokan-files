Title: Demonstrating Against Imperialism and Warmongers: The Peace Signature Campaign Expands in Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 4

　　Demonstrating Against Imperialism and Warmongers
　　The Peace Signature Campaign Expands in Various Countries
　　Spain
　　[News from This Newspaper] Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 31st, Electric News Agency report: The Spanish Communist Party's "Workers' World" newspaper published in Paris announced that among the Spanish Republican exiles scattered around the world, 216,452 have already signed the Stockholm Appeal.
　　Austria
　　TASS, Vienna: The Austrian people are continuing to collect signatures for the Stockholm Appeal with perseverance. As of July 28th, a total of 600,973 people in Austria had signed the declaration.
　　United States
　　TASS, New York, July 30th: The Daily Worker reported that despite the vicious terror of the police and fascist thugs, over 12,000 peace signatures had been collected in the southern states by July 15th. In Winston-Salem, South Carolina, 1,000 signatures were collected from workers at the Reynolds Tobacco Company in just a few hours. The Communist Party's Texas Secretary, Green, told a Daily Worker reporter that despite the reactionaries' attempts to intimidate the local population, the peace movement is unfolding in the state.
　　Netherlands
　　TASS, The Hague, July 30th: The movement to collect signatures for the Stockholm Appeal in the Netherlands is expanding in scale. As of July 30th, a total of 238,867 signatures had been collected nationwide. When signing the Stockholm Appeal, the Dutch working people expressed their angry protests against the American intervention in Korea.
　　Greece
　　TASS, Athens, July 30th: It is reported that the secretly circulated youth newspaper "Laifendia" published the full text of the Stockholm Appeal. The newspaper stated that the movement to collect signatures for the Stockholm Appeal has begun in Greece despite all oppression, and that the Democratic Front of Young Peace Supporters has played an active role in this movement. The newspaper pointed out that young peace supporters have collected signatures for the Stockholm Appeal among their families, at their workplaces, and in educational institutions. For example, in Athens, three young peace supporters collected 160 signatures in just two days.
　　India
　　According to TASS, Delhi, July 30th: The Delhi Standing Committee for Peace convened a joint meeting of progressive public organizations here. The meeting resolved to establish a committee in Delhi with the aim of: regularly reporting to the Indian people on the struggles of the peoples of Asian countries, especially Korea, Vietnam, Malaya, the Philippines, and Indonesia, for national independence and against imperialist oppression; further strengthening the struggle against imperialist armed intervention in these countries; exerting pressure on the Indian government to prevent it from directly or indirectly assisting the imperialists in oppressing the Asian people; and also to organize a mass movement demanding the immediate withdrawal of armed forces from Korea by the United States and other countries. The resolution demanded that the Indian government immediately refuse to support the Security Council's resolution on the Korean issue.

### 向帝国主义和战争贩子示威  各国和平签名运动在扩展中

1950-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　向帝国主义和战争贩子示威
    各国和平签名运动在扩展中
    西班牙
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：电通社巴黎七月三十一日讯：西班牙共产党在巴黎出版的《工人世界》报宣布，在散处全世界各地的西班牙流亡共和分子中，已有二十一万六千四百五十二人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签了名。
    奥地利
    塔斯社维也纳讯：奥地利人民正继续以坚忍不拔的精神为斯德哥尔摩宣言征集签名。截至七月二十八日止，全奥已有六十万零九千一百七十三人在宣言上签了名。
    美国
    塔斯社纽约三十日讯：工人日报报道：不顾警察与法西斯暴徒的恶毒恐怖，到七月十五日为止，南部诸州已征集了一万二千余人的和平签名。在南卡罗林纳州温斯顿·萨莱姆，数小时之内即在雷诺烟草公司的工人中征集了一千人的签名。共产党得克萨斯州书记革林告工人日报记者说，尽管反动派企图恫吓当地人民，和平运动仍在该州展开。
    荷兰
    塔斯社海牙三十日讯：荷兰为斯德哥尔摩宣言征求签名的运动，规模日益扩大。截至七月三十日为止，全国共已征集了二十三万八千八百六十七人的签名。荷兰劳动人民在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名的时候，对于美国干涉朝鲜一事表示愤怒的抗议。
    希腊
    塔斯社雅典三十日讯：据悉，秘密发行的青年报纸《莱芬迪亚报》曾刊载斯德哥尔摩宣言全文，该报称，为斯德哥尔摩宣言征集签名的运动，不顾一切压迫也已在希腊开始，而青年和平拥护者民主阵线在这个运动中起了积极的作用。该报指出，青年和平拥护者曾在他们的家属中间，在他们的工作地点以及在教育机关中，为斯德哥尔摩宣言征集签名。例如在雅典，三位青年和平拥护者单在两天之内就征集了一百六十人签名。
    印度
    据塔斯社德里三十日讯：德里常设和平委员会曾在此间召开进步的公共团体联席会议。会议决议在德里设立一个委员会，目的在于：经常向印度人民报告亚洲各国人民尤其是朝鲜、越南、马来亚、菲律宾和印尼等国的人民为争取民族独立和反对帝国主义压迫而进行的斗争的消息；进一步加强反对帝国主义武装干涉这些国家的斗争；对印度政府施用压力，使它不能直接或间接地帮助帝国主义者压迫亚洲人民；同时也为了组织一个群众运动，要求美国和其他国家的武装力量立即自朝鲜撤退。决议要求印度政府立即拒绝支持安全理事会关于朝鲜问题的决议。
