Title: China's First Ambassador to North Korea, General Ni Zhiliang, Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 12th: General Ni Zhiliang, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, along with Wu Xiaoda, Second Secretary of the Embassy, and nine other staff members, arrived here at 5:30 this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Park Dong-chu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, and Kim Seong-hak, Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee.

Also present at the station to welcome them were Soviet Ambassador to Korea, Sidorov, Mongolian Ambassador to Korea, Zhamsooron, and Hungarian Minister to Korea, Szandor.

Chai Junwu, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and all the staff members of the embassy, Wen Shizhen, Chief Representative of the Northeast People's Government Commercial Delegation to Korea, Ma Yusheng, Vice Chairman of the North Korean Chinese Overseas Chinese Federation, and others also went to the station to welcome them.

Three representatives from the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, including Gao Jingren, went to Sinuiju on the Sino-Korean border to greet Ambassador Ni Zhiliang and his party.

### 我国首任驻朝鲜大使  倪志亮将军抵平壤

1950-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国首任驻朝鲜大使
    倪志亮将军抵平壤
    【新华社平壤十二日电】我国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首任特命全权大使倪志亮将军，偕大使馆二等秘书吴晓达及随员等一行九人，于十二日晨五时半抵达此间。前往车站欢迎者有朝鲜外交部副部长朴东楚，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会书记长康良煜，平壤市人民委员会委员长金成学等人。
    前往车站欢迎者尚有苏联驻朝鲜大使西地科夫，蒙古驻朝鲜大使扎姆索伦，匈牙利驻朝鲜公使鲜道尔。
    我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办柴军武暨全体馆员，东北人民政府驻朝鲜商业代表团首席代表文士桢，北朝鲜华侨联合总会副委员长马玉声等，亦往车站欢迎。
    朝鲜外交部高景仁等三代表，曾赴新义州中朝国境迎接倪志亮大使等一行。
