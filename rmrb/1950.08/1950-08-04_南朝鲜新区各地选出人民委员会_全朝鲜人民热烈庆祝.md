Title: People's Committees Elected in Newly Liberated Areas of South Korea; Whole Korean People Warmly Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: According to a Pyongyang broadcast cited by TASS, the entire Korean people warmly celebrate the election of people's committee members in the newly liberated areas of South Korea. The election of district and township people's committees in the newly liberated areas of Gyeonggi Province, Hwanghae Province, and southern Gangwon Province have all concluded. The representatives elected to the people's committees include people from all democratic classes. For example, among the five hundred and forty-nine representatives of the ninety-five township people's committees in Guhuang District (transliteration), there are eighty-two workers, four hundred and twenty-three peasants, seventeen staff members, two merchants, and one entrepreneur.

### 南朝鲜新区各地选出人民委员会  全朝鲜人民热烈庆祝

1950-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜新区各地选出人民委员会
    全朝鲜人民热烈庆祝
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社引平壤广播，全朝鲜人民热烈庆祝南朝鲜解放区各地人民委员的选举。京畿道、黄海道及江原道南部新解放地区的区、乡人民委员会的选举事宜，均已结束。选入人民委员会的代表包括各民主阶层人士。如谷黄区（译音）九十五个乡人民委员会的五百四十九个代表中，有工人八十二人，农民四百二十三人，职员十七人，商人二人及企业家一人。
