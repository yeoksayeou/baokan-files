Title: Grand Rally Held in Prague Celebrates the Victorious Conclusion of the Student Representative Assembly - "U.S. Get Out of Korea" Resounds Throughout the Venue
Author:
Date: 1950-08-26
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 25th: TASS Prague dispatch: Residents and youth of Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, held a grand mass rally on the evening of the 23rd at the Old Town Square in the city center to celebrate the victorious conclusion of the Second World Student Congress. Participants carried various banners with slogans demanding that American troops withdraw from Korea and stop American intervention.

　　Groman, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the International Union of Students, delivered a speech stating: The Second World Student Congress has powerfully demonstrated the unyielding determination of progressive students from all countries to struggle wholeheartedly for lasting peace.

　　D'Arboussier, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee, Boerenson, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and Yershova, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol and a member of the Soviet student delegation, also delivered speeches.

　　The speech by Yang Cheng, head of the Chinese delegation and newly elected Vice-Chairman of the International Union of Students, was warmly welcomed. Kang Buk-ki, head of the Korean student delegation, delivered a speech amidst enthusiastic applause, recounting once again the heroic struggle of the Korean people.

　　Hollmann, the American progressive student representative and Vice-Chairman of the International Union of Students, emphasized in his speech: The members of the American delegation pledge to redouble their efforts to work closely with peace-loving people in the United States and around the world.

　　The audience participating in the mass rally unanimously adopted a resolution protesting the American aggression against Korea to the United Nations Security Council with great enthusiasm.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 25th: The delegations of various countries attending the Second World Student Congress received grand farewell gifts from Czech youth on the 23rd. Minutes before the closing of the congress, more than 40 members of the Czechoslovak Youth League, led by two motorcycles, carrying gifts such as wireless radios, cameras, and sketchbooks, marched into the venue of the congress in a column. Amidst warm applause, they distributed various gifts to the student delegations of various countries. Two of the motorcycles were given to the student representatives of China and Korea, respectively.

　　Groman, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the International Union of Students, warmly celebrated the achievements of this congress in his closing remarks and presented the flag of the International Union of Students to the student representatives of the Soviet Union, China, and more than 40 countries that are striving to carry out the peace signature campaign.

### 布拉格举行盛大集会  庆祝学代会胜利闭幕  “美国退出朝鲜”呼声响彻全场

1950-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　布拉格举行盛大集会
    庆祝学代会胜利闭幕
    “美国退出朝鲜”呼声响彻全场
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格的居民与青年，于二十三日晚，在市中心旧市政厅广场举行盛大的群众大会，庆祝第二届世界学生代表大会的胜利闭幕。与会者携着各式各样的标语牌，写着要求美国军队退出朝鲜，制止美国干涉行为的口号。
    国际学联执委会主席格罗曼发表演说称：第二届世界学生代表大会已有力地证明了世界各国的进步学生为持久和平而全力斗争的不屈不挠的决心。
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局副主席达波赛，世界民主青联主席鲍埃逊，以及苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会书记之一、苏联学生代表团团员叶尔硕娃，均发表了演说。
    中国代表团团长、新当选的国际学联副主席杨诚的讲演，受到热烈的欢迎。朝鲜学生代表团团长康北继在热烈的掌声中发表了演说，再次讲述了朝鲜人民的英勇斗争。
    美国进步学生代表、国际学联副主席荷尔曼发言强调称：美国代表团的团员们，誓将加倍努力与美国及全世界爱好和平的人民密切合作。
    参加群众大会的会众，以高涨的热情一致通过了向联合国安理会抗议美国侵略朝鲜的决议。
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】出席第二届世界学生代表大会的各国代表团，于二十三日接受了捷克青年的隆重的临别赠礼。在大会闭幕前数分钟，捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟盟员四十余人，以两辆机器脚踏车为前导，携带着无线电机、照相机及画册等礼物，列队进入代表大会的会场。他们在热烈的掌声中把各种礼品分赠给各国学生代表团。其中的两辆机器脚踏车分别送给了中国与朝鲜的学生代表。
    国际学联执委会主席格罗曼，在闭幕词中热烈庆祝本届代表大会的成就，并将国际学联会旗，分别赠与苏联、中国和四十余个努力进行和平签名运动的国家的学生代表。
