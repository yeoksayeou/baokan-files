Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Executive Bureau Meeting Passes Declaration on Korean Situation; Firmly Demands Cessation of War, Suspension of Bombing of Civilians, Withdrawal of Foreign Troops, and Hearing of Statements from Both Sides.
Author:
Date: 1950-08-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 19th: TASS, Prague, 19th -- The Executive Bureau meeting of the World Peace Council Standing Committee recently passed a declaration regarding the Korean situation. The declaration will be submitted to the United Nations Security Council and governments of all countries.

　　The declaration states: "The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council Standing Committee, holding its meeting at a time when the Korean War is ongoing, fully recognizes the immense danger presented by this conflict. The wanton bombing of peaceful residents, causing large numbers of casualties and destruction, has shocked the Executive Bureau. The Executive Bureau firmly demands that the Security Council consider the will of the Korean people and seek a final resolution to this conflict based on the following points: cessation of hostilities and immediate suspension of bombing of civilians, withdrawal of all foreign troops, and hearing of statements from representatives of both sides. The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council Standing Committee is convinced that it is presenting the will of hundreds of millions of people in making the above suggestions. It is also convinced that it represents the expectations of people throughout the world who long to maintain and consolidate peace."

### 世界和大常委会执行局会议  通过关于朝鲜事件的声明  坚决要求停止战争，中止轰炸平民，撤退外国军队，  听取双方代表申述意见。

1950-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和大常委会执行局会议
    通过关于朝鲜事件的声明
    坚决要求停止战争，中止轰炸平民，撤退外国军队，  听取双方代表申述意见。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社布拉格十九日讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局会议顷通过一项关于朝鲜事件的声明。该声明将递交联合国安全理事会及世界各国政府。
    声明称：“世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局在朝鲜战争正在进行的时刻举行会议，它充分认识到这一冲突所具有的巨大危险。大肆轰炸和平居民造成了大量的伤亡和破坏，使本执行局为之震惊。本执行局坚决要求安理会考虑朝鲜人民的意志，以下列各点为基础，寻求最后解决这一冲突的途径：停止战事并立即中止轰炸平民，撤退一切外国军队并听取双方代表申述意见。世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局确信：它提出上述建议是代表亿万人民的意志的。它也确信：它代表了全世界渴求保持并巩固和平的人民的期望。”
