Title: Vice Chairman Liu's Land Reform Report Published in Full by Pravda
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 1st. Pravda published on July 31st, on one and a half pages, the report on land reform by Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, delivered at the Second Session of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 刘副主席土改报告  真理报全文刊载

1950-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　刘副主席土改报告
    真理报全文刊载
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】真理报于七月三十一日以一页半的篇幅，刊登中华人民共和国中央人民政府副主席刘少奇在中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会第二次会议上所作关于土地改革问题的报告。
