Title: Polish Trade Unions Donate Funds to Support the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1950-08-10
Page: 4

　　Polish Trade Unions Donate Funds to Support the Korean People
　　[Dispatch from Warsaw by this newspaper] During the "Week to Support the Korean People," Polish trade unions collected 24 million zlotys (Polish currency) as a fund to support the Korean people. These donations will be used to purchase urgently needed supplies for the Korean people and will be shipped to Korea immediately.

### 波各工会捐款支援朝鲜人民

1950-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　波各工会捐款支援朝鲜人民
    【本报讯】据波兰大使馆华沙讯：波兰各工会在“支援朝鲜人民周”中捐集了二千四百万兹罗提（波币）作为支援朝鲜人民的基金。此项捐款将用以购买朝鲜人民所急需的物资，并将立即运往朝鲜。
