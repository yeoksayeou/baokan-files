Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Vigorously Attack on All Fronts; Liberation of Key Location Goseong in the South Sea; Guerrilla Forces Strengthen Activities Behind Enemy Lines to Coordinate with the Offensive
Author:
Date: 1950-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 15th that the People's Army units on all fronts are fiercely striking the US military units and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army, and continuing their offensive.

The People's Army units attacking in the South Sea area repelled several counterattacks by US tank units and infantry units, and liberated Goseong in Gyeongsangnam-do, the enemy's main stronghold in the South Sea area, on the 12th.

In the battle to liberate Goseong, the People's Army units killed, wounded, and captured many enemies. The spoils of war seized were as follows: nine 155mm cannons, twelve 105mm cannons, thirteen tanks, 157 vehicles, and a large number of weapons and ammunition.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 15th that the People's Army units are inflicting heavy blows on US military units and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army on all fronts and continuing to launch vigorous attacks. The People's Army units continue to inflict great losses on US military units and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army in their advance. Guerrilla forces are responding to the People's Army units' offensive and are carrying out active operations behind enemy lines. Guerrilla forces are disrupting and harassing enemy rear facilities and transportation in areas such as Goryeong, Daegu, Miryang, Gyeongju, and Ulsan, strengthening their activities and inflicting great losses on the enemy. A unit of guerrillas ambushed a US engineering unit near the Nakdong River in Ogok District, Goryeong County, killing and wounding more than 60 US soldiers.

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: TASS, Pyongyang, 15th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 15th that the People's Army units are continuing to attack fiercely on all fronts. Guerrilla forces are strengthening their activities behind enemy lines to assist the People's Army's offensive. In Miryang, Heju (transliteration), Ulsan, and other areas, guerrilla forces are destroying roads and other targets behind enemy lines, causing the enemy to suffer serious losses.

### 朝鲜人民军各线继续猛攻  南海方面解放要地固城  游击队加强敌后活动配合进攻

1950-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军各线继续猛攻
    南海方面解放要地固城
    游击队加强敌后活动配合进攻
    【新华社平壤十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，猛烈打击美军部队与李承晚残余伪军，继续进攻。
    在南海方面进攻的人民军部队，击退美军坦克部队和步兵部队的数次反攻，于十二日解放了敌人在南海方面的主要据点、庆尚南道的固城。
    人民军部队在解放固城的战役中，毙、伤、俘敌甚多。缴获战利品如下：一五五公厘炮九门、一零五公厘炮十二门、坦克十三辆、汽车一五七辆及大批武器、弹药。
    【新华社平壤十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上予美军部队和李承晚残余伪军以沉重打击，继续展开猛攻。人民军部队在进军中继续予美军部队和李承晚残余伪军以莫大损失。游击队响应人民军部队的进攻，在敌人后方展开着积极的活动。游击队在高灵、大邱、密阳、庆州、蔚山等地区破坏与扰乱着敌后的设施和交通，加强自己的活动，予敌人以莫大损失。游击队一部在高灵县午谷区洛东江附近奇袭美军工兵部队。毙伤美军六十余名。
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社平壤十五日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晨发表战报称：人民军部队继续在各战线上猛烈进攻。游击队正加强敌后活动来协助人民军进攻。在密阳、赫州（译音）、蔚山以及其他地区，游击队正在敌后破坏公路及其他目标，使敌人遭受严重损失。
