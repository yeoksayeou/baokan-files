Title: The 44th Government Affairs Council Approved the List of Appointments to be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval
Author:
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The 44th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government approved the list of appointments for the Mayor and members of the Benxi Municipal People's Government, which will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval, as follows:
Mayor: Wang Yubo, formerly Mayor of Benxi Municipal People's Government.
Members: Eighteen persons (in order of the number of strokes in their surnames)
　　Ding Xiu: Currently Deputy Secretary of the Benxi Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　Ren Zhiyuan: Currently Chairman of the Benxi Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.
　　Qu Fuming: Coal miner.
　　Tong Maoting: Currently Manager of Benxi Zhenyuan Iron Factory.
　　Li Yaguang: Currently Secretary of the Benxi Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　Li Ning: Currently Director of the Benxi Municipal Public Security Bureau.
　　Lin Nan (female): Currently Director of the Benxi Municipal Women's Federation.
　　Jin Yucheng: Doctor.
　　Jin Guang: Currently Secretary of the Benxi Municipal Work Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
　　Wei Ruo (female): Currently Deputy Director of the Benxi Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education.
　　Zhang Jian: Currently Director of the Benxi Municipal Bureau of Commerce.
    Yang Wei: Currently Manager of Benxi Coal and Iron Company.
    Jia Dingxun: Currently Director of the Steelmaking Shop of the Special Steel Plant of Benxi Coal and Iron Company.
    Jin Shuliang: Currently Chief Engineer of Benxi Coal and Iron Company.
    Liu Xiangming: Currently Secretary of the Benxi Municipal People's Government.
    Liu Yuanliang: Currently Director of the Benxi Municipal Labor Bureau.
    Yan Chunzhi: Currently Engineer of Benxi Coal and Iron Company.
    Han Dianming: Currently Director of Teaching Affairs of Benxi Middle School.
