Title: National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition Offers Free Admission for Convenience, Extends Daily Hours

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-16
Page: 6

　　National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition Offers Free Admission for Convenience, Extends Daily Hours
    The National Health and Pharmaceutical Exhibition, in order to meet the demands of the general public and facilitate visits from all walks of life, will extend its daily visiting hours from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, effective immediately. Admission is free for all. However, to visit the Military Health Service Pavilion, a letter of introduction from the relevant agency, unit, school, or organization is required. (Publicity and Guidance Section of the Health Exhibition)

### 全国卫生医药展览会为便利参观一律免票  并延长每日开放时间

1950-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　全国卫生医药展览会为便利参观一律免票
    并延长每日开放时间
    全国卫生医药展览会为适应广大群众要求，便于各界人士参观起见，自即日起将每天参观时间延长，由上午九时至下午六时止。并一律免票入场。惟参观军队卫生勤务馆，须有各该机关、部队、学校、团体的介绍信。（卫展会宣传指导股）
