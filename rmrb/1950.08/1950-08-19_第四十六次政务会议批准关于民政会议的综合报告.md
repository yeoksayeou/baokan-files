Title: The 46th Government Affairs Council Approves Comprehensive Report on the National Civil Affairs Conference
Author: 
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The 46th meeting of the Central People's Government Council was held at 3:30 PM on the 18th. After listening to and discussing the comprehensive report on the National Civil Affairs Conference made by Xie Juezai, Minister of the Interior of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the report. The meeting also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, and motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 第四十六次政务会议批准关于民政会议的综合报告

1950-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　第四十六次政务会议批准关于民政会议的综合报告
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第四十六次政务会议，于十八日下午三时半举行。会议在听取与讨论了中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长所作的关于全国民政会议的综合报告后，批准了该报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
