Title: Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Invites Indian Ambassador Panikkar to Give a Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 30th:

The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs held its second lecture meeting on August 30th, and specially invited Indian Ambassador to China, Panikkar, to give a lecture entitled: "The Political Dynamics of Modern India." More than 110 members of the institute attended the meeting, which was chaired by the institute's Vice President, Hu Yuzhi. Ambassador Panikkar's lecture lasted two hours, and he gave a very detailed introduction to the political and economic situation of modern India. The lecture was conducted in a friendly atmosphere, and the audience was very interested in the content of the lecture.

### 中国人民外交学会邀请印度潘尼迦大使讲演

1950-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民外交学会邀请印度潘尼迦大使讲演
    【新华社三十日讯】中国人民外交学会于八月三十日举行第二次讲演会，特请印度驻中国大使潘尼迦讲演，讲题为：《现代印度的政治动力》。出席该会会员一百一十余人。由该会副会长胡愈之任主席。潘尼迦大使讲演历两小时，对于现代印度之政治经济情况介绍甚详。讲演会在友好的气氛中进行，听众对于讲演内容颇感兴趣。
