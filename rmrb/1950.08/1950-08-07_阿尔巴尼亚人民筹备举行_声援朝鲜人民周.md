Title: Albanian People Prepare to Hold "Week of Solidarity with the Korean People"
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 4th: According to comprehensive reports from TASS, the Albanian people are scheduled to hold a "Week of Solidarity with the Korean People" from August 8th to 15th. The Albanian Democratic Front, the Albanian Committee for the Defense of Peace, and organizations such as the Writers Association, Journalists Association, Labor Youth League, and Women's Federation have formed a central steering committee to carry out preparatory work.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民筹备举行“声援朝鲜人民周”

1950-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚人民筹备举行“声援朝鲜人民周”
    【新华社四日讯】综合塔斯社地拉那消息：阿尔巴尼亚人民定于八月八日至十五日举行“声援朝鲜人民周”。阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线，阿尔巴尼亚拥护和平委员会，以及作家协会，记者协会，劳动青年联合会，妇女联合会等团体，已组成中央指导委员会，进行筹备工作。
