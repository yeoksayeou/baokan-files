Title: Congratulations on the 23rd Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army; The Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party Sends a Telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Marshal Vasilevsky Sends a Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch:

　　Congratulations on the 23rd Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
    The Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party Sends a Telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
    Marshal Vasilevsky Sends a Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received a congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the Army Day. The telegram reads as follows: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    On the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union extends its warm respect to the Communist Party of China, the organizer of the Chinese People's Liberation War.
    We wish the People's Liberation Army further victories in its glorious struggle to consolidate the People's Republic of China and strive for peace and democracy.
                The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] Commander-in-Chief Zhu De received a congratulatory telegram from Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Army of the Soviet Union, on the Army Day. The telegram reads as follows: To Mr. Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China:
    On the anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China, please accept my friendly congratulations on behalf of the Soviet Army and myself.
    A. Vasilevsky

### 祝贺人民解放军建军二十三周年  联共中央致电中共中央  华西列夫斯基元帅致电朱总司令

1950-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺人民解放军建军二十三周年
    联共中央致电中共中央
    华西列夫斯基元帅致电朱总司令
    【新华社一日讯】中国共产党中央委员会接获苏联共产党中央委员会祝贺建军节的贺电，电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十三周年纪念之际，苏联共产党中央委员会向中国人民解放战争的组织者——中国共产党致热烈的敬意。
    谨祝人民解放军在巩固中华人民共和国与争取和平民主事业的光荣斗争中获得更进一步的胜利。
                苏联共产党中央委员会
    【新华社一日讯】朱德总司令接获苏联陆军部部长华西列夫斯基元帅祝贺建军节的贺电，电文如下：中华人民共和国人民解放军总司令朱德先生：
    值此中华人民共和国人民解放军建军纪念日，谨以苏联军队及我个人的名义，请您接受我友谊的祝贺。
    安·华西列夫斯基
