Title: New Knowledge Forum to Host Xiao Hua Tomorrow Evening to Discuss the Story of the Long March
Author:
Date: 1950-08-04
Page: 6

The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association has scheduled its 81st New Knowledge Forum for 7:30 PM on Saturday, August 5th, at the Xiehe Auditorium on East Third Street. In conjunction with the August 1st Army Day, we have invited Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Military Commission, to speak on "The Story of the Long March." Individuals from all walks of life who wish to attend can obtain admission tickets through introductions from members, friends, or affiliated organizations at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong.

### 新知识座谈会明晚请萧华讲关于长征的故事

1950-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会明晚请萧华讲关于长征的故事
    民主建国会北京市分会定于五日（星期六）下午七时半，在东单三条协和礼堂举行八十一次新知识座谈会，为配合八一建军节，这次特请军委会总政治部副主任萧华讲《关于长征的故事》。各界人士如要听讲，可由会员会友或机关团体单位介绍，到无量大人胡同二十四号领取入场券。
