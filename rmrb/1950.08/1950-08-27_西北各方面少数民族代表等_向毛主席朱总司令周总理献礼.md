Title: Representatives of Various Ethnic Minorities in Northwest China Present Gifts to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, received flowers, gifts, flags, and dedicatory speeches from representatives of various ethnic minorities in Northwest China, as well as from the All-China Conference of Scientific Workers Representatives and the First National Health Conference. At the grand gift presentation and tribute-paying ceremony held on the afternoon of the 20th, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De addressed the more than 900 representatives present. Among the gifts presented by the representatives of various ethnic groups in Northwest China were precious products from the border regions such as saffron, deer antlers, Tibetan incense, and Tibetan musk pills.

### 西北各方面少数民族代表等  向毛主席朱总司令周总理献礼

1950-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各方面少数民族代表等
    向毛主席朱总司令周总理献礼
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席，中国人民解放军朱德总司令，政务院周恩来总理，曾接受西北各方面少数民族代表及中华全国科学工作者代表会议和第一届全国卫生会议的献花、献礼、献旗、献词。在二十日下午举行的这一隆重献礼致敬大会上，朱德总司令曾向到会的九百余代表讲话。西北各方面民族代表所献的礼物中，有藏红花、鹿角、藏香、藏麝香丸等边陲名贵产物。
