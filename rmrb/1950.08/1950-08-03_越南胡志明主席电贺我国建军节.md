Title: President Ho Chi Minh of Vietnam Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Our Army Day

Author:

Date: 1950-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd—Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De have received a congratulatory telegram from President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating them on Army Day. The telegram reads: Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu:

On the occasion of the glorious festival of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of the Vietnamese government, people, and army, extend to you infinite warm and affectionate congratulations.

Ho Chi Minh
August 1st

### 越南胡志明主席电贺我国建军节

1950-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南胡志明主席电贺我国建军节
    【新华社二日讯】毛泽东主席、朱德总司令顷接获越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席祝贺建军节的贺电，电文如下：毛主席，朱总司令：
    欣逢中国人民解放军的光荣节日，我谨代表越南政府、人民及军队向你们致无限热诚和亲爱的庆祝。
            胡志明
                  八月一日
