Title: Our Army Liberates Xiyang Island Outside Sandu Ao Port in Northeast Coastal Fujian
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 1

　　Fujian Northeast Coastal Sandu Ao Port Outer
    Our Army Liberates Xiyang Island
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 23rd] A unit of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army liberated Xiyang Island outside Sandu Ao Port in northeast coastal Fujian on the 12th of this month. Most of the remnant bandits and secret agents who fled to the island were wiped out. When the People's Liberation Army occupied the island, they also repelled the fierce attack of four bandit gunboats.

### 福建东北沿海三都澳港外  我军解放西洋岛

1950-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　福建东北沿海三都澳港外
    我军解放西洋岛
    【新华社南京二十三日电】人民解放军第三野战军一部，于本月十二日解放福建东北沿海三都澳港外的西洋岛。亡命该岛的残余土匪特务大部就歼。人民解放军在占领该岛时，并击退了匪炮艇四艘的猛烈攻击。
