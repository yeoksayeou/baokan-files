Title: DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong Calls Lai Yi, Demanding the Transmission of the Report on the Atrocities of the American and Li Syngman Gangs to All UN Member States
Author:
Date: 1950-08-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 28th: DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong telegraphed UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie on the 22nd, requesting Lie to transmit the first investigation report of the Investigation Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front on the atrocities of the American interventionists and the Li Syngman bandit gang to all member states.

Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong also telegraphed Malik, the President of the United Nations Security Council, requesting that the first report be added to the materials on the atrocities committed by the American interventionists in Korea.

### 朝外长朴宪永致电赖伊  要求向联合国各会员国传达美李暴行调查报告

1950-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝外长朴宪永致电赖伊
    要求向联合国各会员国传达美李暴行调查报告
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永，于二十二日致电联合国秘书长赖伊，要求赖伊向各会员国传达朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线调查委员会关于美国干涉者与李承晚匪帮暴行的调查报告第一号。
    外务相朴宪永同时并致电联合国安全理事会主席马立克，要求把该第一号报告附加于美国干涉者对朝鲜暴行的资料中。
