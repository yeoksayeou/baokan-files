Title: Austrian Democratic Women's League Voices Support for Heroic Korean Women
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th. The Austrian Democratic Women's League sent a letter to the North Korean Democratic Women's League on July 17th, denouncing American imperialism's aggression against Korea and expressing support for the heroic Korean women. The letter stated: "In this time of terror, danger, and utmost urgency, we, the women and mothers of Austria, stand by your side. We will concentrate all our efforts on publicizing the truth about Korea in our country, and continue to collect 100,000 more signatures for the ban on atomic bombs."

### 奥地利民主妇女联盟声援英勇的朝鲜妇女

1950-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　奥地利民主妇女联盟声援英勇的朝鲜妇女
    【新华社布拉格六日电】奥地利民主妇女联盟于七月十七日致函北朝鲜民主妇女同盟，斥责美国帝国主义侵略朝鲜并声援英勇的朝鲜妇女。函中表示：“在这恐怖、危险而万分紧急的时刻，我们奥地利的妇女和母亲们是站在你们一边的。我们将集中一切力量在我们的国家里宣传朝鲜的真相，并为禁止原子弹而继续再征集十万人的签名。
