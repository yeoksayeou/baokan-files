Title: Mongolian Chairman G. Bumatsende Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Congratulations on Mongolian National Day; Marshal Khorloogiin Choibalsan Sends Reply Telegram to Premier Zhou, Expressing Gratitude

Author:
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai have respectively received reply telegrams from G. Bumatsende, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Marshal Khorloogiin Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the same country. Chairman Bumatsende and Marshal Choibalsan expressed their gratitude to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou for their congratulatory messages on the 29th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Chairman G. Bumatsende's telegram to Chairman Mao:

His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to you and the friendship of the Chinese people for your congratulations on the further victories of the Mongolian people on the 29th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

On behalf of the Mongolian people and the Presidium of the Little Khural, and in my personal capacity, I sincerely wish the great Chinese people further victories.

G. Bumatsende, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic

Ulaanbaatar, July 10, 1950

Marshal Khorloogiin Choibalsan's telegram to Premier Zhou:

His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to Your Excellency the Premier for your congratulations on the happiness of the Mongolian people on the occasion of the 29th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

On behalf of the Mongolian people and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and in my personal capacity, I sincerely wish the great Chinese people further victories in their struggle for the complete liberation of all of China and for the construction of a free and independent People's Republic of China.

Marshal Khorloogiin Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic

Ulaanbaatar, July 10, 1950

### 蒙古布曼曾迪主席  电谢毛主席祝贺蒙古国庆  乔巴山元帅复电周总理致谢

1950-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古布曼曾迪主席
    电谢毛主席祝贺蒙古国庆
    乔巴山元帅复电周总理致谢
    【新华社八日讯】毛泽东主席和周恩来总理分别收到蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席格·布曼曾迪及该国部长会议主席郝·乔巴山元帅的复电。布曼曾迪主席和乔巴山元帅对毛主席与周总理电贺蒙古人民革命二十九周年纪念事表示感谢。
    格·布曼曾迪主席致毛主席电：
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东阁下：
    对您在蒙古人民革命二十九周年纪念日为蒙古人民更进一步的胜利的祝贺，谨向您和中国人民的友谊致以衷心谢意。
    我代表蒙古人民及小呼拉尔主席团以个人名义衷心地希望伟大的中国人民更进一步的胜利。
            蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席  格·布曼曾迪
              一九五○年七月十日于乌兰巴特尔
    郝·乔巴山元帅致周总理电：
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来阁下：
    对您在蒙古人民革命二十九周年纪念日之际，为蒙古人民幸福的祝贺，谨向总理阁下致以衷心的感谢。
    我代表蒙古人民和蒙古人民共和国政府以个人名义，衷心地希望伟大的中国人民在为彻底解放全中国及为建设自由独立的中华人民共和国斗争中更进一步地获得胜利。
    蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席  元帅郝·乔巴山
        一九五○年七月十日于乌兰巴特尔
