Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Members Kim Sam-ryong and Lee Joo-ha Murdered; Murderer Now Captured by the Ministry of Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1950-08-10
Page: 4

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th】Kim Sam-ryong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Lee Joo-ha, member of the Central Committee, who were arrested in Seoul on March 27th of this year, were murdered by the Lee Seung-man gang on June 26th. Their remains were discovered on August 3rd. The murderer who killed them has already been captured by the Korean Ministry of Internal Affairs. On August 4th, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party issued an obituary to all party members and the people of the whole country, stating: "We must avenge them and completely wipe out the American aggressors and the Lee Seung-man gang from our territory. The arduous struggle and heroic sacrifice of Comrades Kim Sam-ryong and Lee Joo-ha will become an example for all party members and patriotic people of the whole country. Their spirit will live forever."

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员  金三龙李舟河被害  凶手现已被内务部捕获

1950-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜劳动党中央委员
    金三龙李舟河被害
    凶手现已被内务部捕获
    【新华社平壤七日电】今年三月二十七日在汉城被捕的朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员金三龙和中央委员李舟河，已于六月二十六日被李承晚匪帮杀害了。他们的遗体已于八月三日发现。杀害他们的凶手，业已被朝鲜内务部捕获。八月四日，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会向全体党员及全国人民发出讣告，内称：“我们一定要替他们报仇，将美国侵略者和李承晚匪帮从我国领土上全部消灭干净。金三龙、李舟河两同志的艰苦奋斗与英勇牺牲的全部生涯，将成为我党全体党员与全国爱国人民的榜样。他们的精神永垂不朽。”
