Title: Former Chief of Staff of the South Korean Puppet "National Defense Army", Chae Byeong-deok, Killed in Hadong
Author:
Date: 1950-08-02
Page: 4

　　South Korean Former Puppet "National Defense Army" Chief of Staff
    Chae Byeong-deok Killed in Hadong
    [Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch] According to a Central News Agency (Nationalist) report: Zhang Yichun, the "Commander-in-Chief of the Army, Navy, and Air Force" of the remnants of the Syngman Rhee troops, confirmed that Chae Byeong-deok, the former Chief of Staff of the South Korean puppet "National Defense Army" who was dismissed due to defeat, was killed in action on the 28th in Hadong (Gyeongsangnam-do). After being dismissed, Chae Byeong-deok served as the puppet "Commander of the 3rd Army Corps," responsible for defending southwestern Korea.

### 南朝鲜前伪“国防军”参谋总长  蔡秉德在河东被击毙

1950-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜前伪“国防军”参谋总长
    蔡秉德在河东被击毙
    【新华社三十一日讯】据国民党中央社消息：李承晚残余部队的“海陆空军总司令”张义宽证实：因战败而被免职的南朝鲜伪“国防军”前参谋总长蔡秉德，已于二十八日在河东（庆尚南道）之役中被击毙。蔡秉德在被免职后，曾任伪“第三军团司令”，负责防守朝鲜西南部。
