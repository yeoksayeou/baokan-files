Title: Sixth District Trade Union Establishes Workers' Clinic
Author: Li Shixiu
Date: 1950-08-06
Page: 3

　　Sixth District Trade Union Establishes Workers' Clinic
    [This Newspaper] In accordance with the resolution of the District People's Representative Conference, the Sixth District Trade Union Office of Beijing Municipality has established a workers' clinic. The address of the clinic is No. 24 Hufangqiao. The clinic is open for consultation from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and from 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM daily (closed on Sunday afternoons).
                (Li Shixiu)

### 第六区工会成立工人诊疗所
李世秀
1950-08-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　第六区工会成立工人诊疗所
    【本报讯】京市六区工会办事处执行区人民代表会议决议，成立了工人诊疗所，该所地址在虎坊桥二十四号，每日上午八时至十二时，下午二时至六时（星期日下午停诊）诊疗。
                （李世秀）
