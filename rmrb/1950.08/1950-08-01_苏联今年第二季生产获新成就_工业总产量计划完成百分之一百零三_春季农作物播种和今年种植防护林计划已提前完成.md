Title: The Soviet Union Achieves New Achievements in Production in the Second Quarter of This Year; Total Industrial Output Plan Fulfilled by 103 Percent; Spring Crop Sowing and This Year's Protective Forest Planting Plan Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-08-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th (TASS): The Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has just released a communiqué on the results of the implementation of the National Economic Development Plan for the second quarter of 1950, the contents of which are as follows:

    (1) Industry: The total industrial output plan for the second quarter was fulfilled by 103 percent. The industrial output plan for the first half of this year has already been overfulfilled. The total industrial output of the Soviet Union is now 21 percent higher than in the second quarter of last year.

    The percentages of production volume of major industrial products in the second quarter compared with the same quarter last year are as follows: pig iron 122, steel 119, rolled metal 116, coal 112, oil 117, electricity 115, main-line electric locomotives 135, main-line freight cars 117, trucks 126, passenger cars 150, buses 132, ball bearings 133, steam turbines 169, metal-cutting machine tools 110, tractors 116, mineral fertilizers 118, cement 126, cotton fabrics 102, linen fabrics 122, meat 156, confectionery 122, canned food 136, and the output of other industrial products has also increased significantly.

    The utilization of industrial equipment has made significant progress compared with the same quarter last year. In the second quarter, many industrial units used less raw materials, fuel, and electricity than in the same quarter last year. The goal of reducing industrial production costs has been exceeded. Production costs in the second quarter, calculated at comparable prices, have been reduced by 6 percent.

    (2) Agriculture: The spring crop sowing plan has been fully completed, and the sowing time has been shortened. The quality of spring farmland work has been improved. The total sown area this year has increased by approximately six million hectares compared to last year. Nearly 70,000 combine drivers and co-drivers, as well as many other agricultural workers, have received technical training. The supply of various agricultural machines by the state has increased by 20 to 160 percent respectively. Crop conditions are good in various regions, and harvesting is proceeding smoothly in the southern regions.

    This year's protective forest planting plan has been completed ahead of schedule. This spring, more than 700,000 hectares of protective forest belts were planted in the grassland and forest-grassland zones of the European part of the Soviet Union, exceeding one and a half times the area afforested last spring.

    The number of livestock on collective farms and state farms has increased significantly. The merger of small collective farms in some areas to engage in large-scale operations has improved the level of agriculture.

    (3) Transportation: The railway transportation plan for the second quarter was fulfilled by 104 percent. The inland waterway transportation plan was fulfilled by 99 percent. The maritime freight transportation plan was fulfilled by 105 percent.

    (4) Basic Construction Projects: Increased by 31 percent in the second quarter of this year compared with the same period last year.

    (5) Trade: Due to the further reduction in prices of food and manufactured goods on March 1 this year, the volume of trade has further expanded. The volume of state and cooperative retail trade in the second quarter increased by 30 percent compared with the same period last year, with food sales increasing by 25 percent and manufactured goods increasing by 37 percent.

    (6) Number of Employees and Labor Productivity: The number of employees in the second quarter increased by 2.4 million compared with the same quarter last year. 1.9 million people were added to industry, agriculture, timber, construction, and transportation; 300,000 people were added to education, scientific research, and medical institutions.

    The labor productivity of industrial workers increased by 12 percent compared with the second quarter of last year, and the labor productivity of construction workers increased by 13 percent.

    (7) Education, Science, and Health: In the second quarter, nearly 500,000 specialized personnel (including correspondence students) graduated from higher education institutions, technical schools, and specialized schools. The number of graduates from seven-year schools, secondary schools, and schools for young workers and farmers increased by 25 percent compared to last year.

    The number of books published in the first half of this year increased by 15 percent compared with the first half of last year. In the second quarter, the distribution network of hospitals, epidemic prevention agencies, and sanatoriums has been further expanded, and a large number of new medical equipment has been obtained. Ten million workers took paid vacations in the second quarter.

### 苏联今年第二季生产获新成就  工业总产量计划完成百分之一百零三  春季农作物播种和今年种植防护林计划已提前完成

1950-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联今年第二季生产获新成就
    工业总产量计划完成百分之一百零三
    春季农作物播种和今年种植防护林计划已提前完成
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联部长会议中央统计局，顷发表关于一九五○年第二季发展国民经济计划执行结果的公报，内容如下：
    （一）工业方面：第二季工业总产量计划完成了百分之一百零三。今年上半年的工业产量计划业已超计划完成。现在苏联全部工业总产量较去年第二季增加了百分之二十一。
    第二季重要工业产品的生产量与去年同季相较，其百分比如下：铣铁一二二，钢一一九，辗金属一一六，煤一一二，石油一一七，电力一一五，干线电气机车一三五，干线货车一一七，载重汽车一二六，小客车一五○，公共汽车一三二，球轴承一三三，蒸气透平一六九，金属切割工作母机一一○，拖拉机一一六，矿物肥料一一八，水泥一二六，棉织品一○二，麻织品一二二，肉类一五六，糖果一二二，罐头食品一三六，其他各项工业品产量亦有大量增加。
    工业装备的利用，较去年同季有重大进步。第二季内许多工业单位使用的原料、燃料与电力，已较去年同季减少。减低工业生产成本的目标已被超过。第二季内生产成本按比较价格计算已减低了百分之六。
    （二）农业方面：春季农作物播种计划已全部完成，并且缩短了播种时间。春季农田工作质量已经提高。今年的全部播种面积，较去年增加了约六百万公顷。将近七万名联合机司机与副司机及其他许多农业工人，受到了提高技术的训练。国家供应的各种农业机器，分别增加了百分之二十到百分之一百六十不等。各地农作物情况良好，在南部地区，收割工作正顺利进行中。
    今年种植防护林的计划业已提前完成。今年春季，在苏联欧洲部分的草原与森林草原地带种植了七十多万公顷的防护林带，超过去年春季植林面积的一倍半。
    集体农场与国家农场的牲畜头数已大大增加。若干地区的小型集体农场，由于进行合并以从事大规模经营，业已提高了农业的水平。
    （三）运输方面：第二季的铁道运输计划完成了百分之一百零四。内河运输计划完成了百分之九十九。海上货运计划完成了百分之一百零五。
    （四）基本建设工程方面：今年第二季较去年同期增加了百分之三十一。
    （五）贸易方面：由于本年三月一日再次减低食品和制成品的价格，贸易额已进一步扩大。第二季的国家及合作社零售贸易额较去年同期增加了百分之三十，食物销售量增加了百分之二十五，制成品增加了百分之三十七。
    （六）职工人数与劳动生产率：第二季的职工人数较去年同季增加了二百四十万人。在工业、农业、木材、建筑业与运输业中增加了一百九十万人；在教育、科学研究与医药机关中增加了三十万人。
    产业工人的劳动生产率，比去年第二季增加了百分之十二，建筑业工人的劳动生产率增加了百分之十三。
    （七）教育、科学、卫生方面：第二季内在高等教育机关、技术学校和专门学校毕业的专门人材（包括函授学生）将近五十万人。七年制学校与中等学校以及青年工人和农民学校的毕业生，较去年增加了百分之二十五。
    今年上半年的书籍刊行数额较去年上半年增加了百分之十五。在第二季内，医院、防疫机关与休养所的分布网已进一步扩大，并且获得了大量的新式医学设备。第二季照领工资度假的工人达一千万人。
