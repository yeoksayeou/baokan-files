Title: Five Million Koreans Sign Petition Demanding U.S. Troops Withdraw from Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th: TASS reports: As of August 21st, over five million Koreans have signed the "Korean People's Declaration." This declaration demands the immediate withdrawal of U.S. troops from Korea and the trial of ten major national traitors. The collection of signatures for the declaration has been completed in Pyongyang, Hungnam (transliteration), Chongjin, Sinuiju, and other major industrial centers, as well as in the liberated towns of Seoul, Ongjin, Kaesong, Incheon, Suwon, Gongju, and many other places.

　　Many former members of the South Korean "National Assembly" who have turned to the people and the motherland have also signed the declaration. The collection of signatures is still in progress.

### 朝鲜五百万人签名  要求美军撤出朝鲜

1950-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜五百万人签名
    要求美军撤出朝鲜
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：截至八月二十一日止，五百万以上的朝鲜人民已在“朝鲜人民声明书”上签名。这一声明书要求美国军队立即从朝鲜撤退，并审判十名主要民族叛徒。宣言签名的收集工作，已在平壤、兴南（译音）清津、新义州和其他巨大工业中心以及在解放了的城镇汉城、瓮津、开城、仁川、水原、公州和其他许多地方完成。
    投向人民和祖国的许多前南朝鲜“国民议会”的议员，也都在宣言上签了名。收集签名的工作仍在进行中。
