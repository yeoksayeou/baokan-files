Title: Our Ambassador to North Korea, Ni Zhiliang, Visits the North Korean Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1950-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 14th. Our first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, General Ni Zhiliang, accompanied by Chargé d'Affaires Rong Junwu, paid his first visit to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, Lin Xianyong, at 7:00 PM on the 12th to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials.

### 我国驻朝鲜大使倪志亮拜会朝鲜外长

1950-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国驻朝鲜大使倪志亮拜会朝鲜外长
    【新华社平壤十四日电】我国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首任特命全权大使倪志亮将军，于十二日晚七时，偕代办荣军武，首次拜会朝鲜外交部部长林宪永，商谈有关呈递国书事宜。
