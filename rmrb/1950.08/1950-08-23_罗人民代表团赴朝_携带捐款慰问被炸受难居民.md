Title: Romanian People's Delegation Visits North Korea, Bringing Donations to Console Residents Affected by Bombing

Author:

Date: 1950-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 21st: The Romanian News Agency reports from Bucharest: A delegation of working people from the Romanian People's Republic departed for North Korea on the evening of the 19th. The delegation consists of Polila, Executive Member of the General Confederation of Labor and editor of "Trade Union Life," and Oilien, Executive Member of the General Confederation of Labor and Chairman of the Trade Union of Workers in State Administrative Organs, among others. They will present a donation of 77 million lei from the Romanian working people to the Korean trade unions to aid women, children, the elderly, and the weak who have suffered from the American imperialists' criminal bombing.

### 罗人民代表团赴朝  携带捐款慰问被炸受难居民

1950-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗人民代表团赴朝
    携带捐款慰问被炸受难居民
    【新华社二十一日讯】罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国劳动人民代表团于十九日晚离此赴朝鲜。该团系由总工会执行委员、《工会生活》报编辑波利拉和总工会执行委员、国家行政机关工作者工会主席奥伊连等组成。他们将把罗马尼亚劳动人民七千七百万列伊的捐款，赠给朝鲜工会，以救济遭受美帝国主义罪恶轰炸而受难的妇孺老弱。
