Title: Hungarian Film and Theater Workers Rally to Protest US Imperialist Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 8th: A mass rally organized by the Hungarian Drama and Film Workers Union protested against US imperialist aggression against Korea and expressed solidarity with the heroic Korean people fighting for freedom and independence.

　　Somlai, an actor at the National Theatre and winner of the Kossuth Prize, stated in a speech at the rally that US imperialist aggression against Korea has aroused anger in the hearts of every upright person.

　　The working people of Hungary are actively supporting the Korean people's struggle for freedom. Somlai urged all artists to join the ranks of the international peace movement.

### 匈影剧工作者集会抗议美帝侵略朝鲜

1950-08-11
第4版()
专栏：声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争！

　　匈影剧工作者集会抗议美帝侵略朝鲜
    【新华社讯】塔斯社布达佩斯八日电：匈牙利戏剧电影工作者联合会所组织的群众大会抗议美帝对朝鲜的侵略，并对为自由与独立而奋斗的英勇的朝鲜人民表示声援。
    国立剧院演员和科苏斯奖金获得者索姆拉伊在大会发表演说称：美帝对朝鲜的侵略，已在每一个正直人的心中引起了愤怒。
    匈牙利的劳动人民正在积极地支持朝鲜人民为自由而进行的斗争。索姆拉伊敦促所有的艺术工作者参加国际和平运动的队伍。
