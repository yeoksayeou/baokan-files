　　Daily Report of Flood Season Water Levels, August 17th
　　Note: The Datong zero point shall be the standard for all stations in North China and the Yellow River stations (Luokou shall be based on the Qingdao zero point), the Wusongkou zero point shall be the standard for all stations in the Yangtze River, the abandoned Yellow River mouth zero point shall be the standard for the Huaihe River and the Grand Canal, the Guangxi assumed zero point shall be the standard for Wuzhou, and the Dalian annual average sea level shall be the standard for Northeast China.
