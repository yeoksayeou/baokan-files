Title: Beijing Ganshiqiao Post Office and Tianqiao and Other Post Booths Extend Business Hours
Author:
Date: 1950-08-07
Page: 6

To facilitate citizens, the business hours of the nine post booths in Tianqiao, Dingfu Street, Wenjin Street, Gulou, Xidan, Renmin Zaixue, Peking University, and Guang'anmen Street in this city have been changed to 8:00 am to 12:00 pm and 3:00 pm to 6:00 pm on weekdays; Sundays will be 8:00 am to 11:00 am, and closed in the afternoon.

In addition, starting from August 1st, the Ganshiqiao Post Office will extend its weekday business hours to 8:00 pm and add telegraphic transfer services to benefit the masses.

### 京市甘石桥邮局及天桥等处邮亭  延长办公时间

1950-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　京市甘石桥邮局及天桥等处邮亭
    延长办公时间
    为便利市民起见，本市天桥、定阜大街、文津街、鼓楼、西单、人民在学、北京大学及广安门大街九个邮亭平常日办公时间，改为上午八时至十二时，下午三时至六时；星期日为上午八时至十一时，下午休息。
    又甘石桥邮局自八月一日起，将平常日办公时间延长于下午八点止，并添办电汇业务，以利群众。
