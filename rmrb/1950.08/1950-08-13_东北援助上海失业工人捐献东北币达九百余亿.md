Title: Northeast Aids Shanghai Unemployed Workers with Donations of Over 90 Billion Northeast Currency
Author:
Date: 1950-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 11th—The donation campaign in Northeast China to aid unemployed workers in Shanghai has concluded. As of July 22nd, the six provinces, four directly-administered municipalities, and the Lüda region of Northeast China have donated a total of 96,025 million Northeast currency, of which Shenyang city accounted for 25.2 billion, ranking first among all regions in terms of donation amount. The donation has now been fully remitted to the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions.

### 东北援助上海失业工人捐献东北币达九百余亿

1950-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北援助上海失业工人捐献东北币达九百余亿
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】东北各地援助上海失业工人的献工捐款运动已经结束。截至七月二十二日止，全东北六省，四直辖市及旅大地区，共捐献东北币九百六十亿二千五百余万元，其中沈阳市即占二百五十二亿元，居各地捐款数的首位。现该项捐款，已全部汇寄上海总工会。
