Title: China People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Holds Lecture Meeting, Invites Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon to Speak
Author:
Date: 1950-08-11
Page: 1

　　China People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Holds Lecture Meeting
    Invites Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon to Speak
    [This newspaper dispatch] The China People's Institute of Foreign Affairs held a lecture meeting on August 9th, specially inviting Lee Joo-yeon, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, to speak on the topic: "The Korean People's Fight Against the American Imperialists' Robber-like Aggression to Complete the Independence and Unification of the Motherland." More than one hundred and ten members attended the meeting, including Shao Lizi. In his speech, Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon narrated the process of the establishment of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the astonishing achievements in economic construction within five years, pointed out the various crimes of the American imperialists and their running dog, the Li Seung-man bandit gang, in invading Korean territory and brutally harming the Korean people, exposed the American imperialists' conspiracy to use the United Nations to attack the Korean people, and provided a detailed analysis of the factors that ensure the Korean people will be able to defeat the American imperialists.

### 中国人民外交学会举行演讲会  邀朝鲜李周渊大使讲演

1950-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民外交学会举行演讲会
    邀朝鲜李周渊大使讲演
    【本报讯】中国人民外交学会于八月九日举行讲演会，特请朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李周渊讲演，讲题为：《朝鲜人民反对美帝强盗般的侵略，为完成祖国独立统一而斗争。》出席该会会员邵力子等一百一十余人。李周渊大使讲演中叙述了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立的经过及五年内经济建设的惊人成就，指出美帝国主义及其走狗李承晚匪帮侵略朝鲜领土、残害朝鲜人民的种种罪行，揭露美帝国主义利用联合国进攻朝鲜人民的阴谋，并详尽分析朝鲜人民必能战胜美帝国主义的因素。
