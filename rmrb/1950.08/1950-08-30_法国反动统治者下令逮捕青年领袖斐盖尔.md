Title: French Reactionary Rulers Order Arrest of Youth Leader Figuères
Author:
Date: 1950-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 29th: Paris progressive newspapers reported on the 28th that the French government has actually ordered the arrest of Figuères, leader of the French Progressive Youth Movement, Secretary-General of the Republican Youth League of France, and Director of the youth newspaper "Avant-Garde," because he published an interview with Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 法国反动统治者下令逮捕青年领袖斐盖尔

1950-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国反动统治者下令逮捕青年领袖斐盖尔
    【本报讯】据新华社布拉格二十九日电：巴黎各进步报纸二十八日报道：法国政府竟下令逮捕法进步青年运动领袖、法兰西共和青年联盟总书记和青年界报纸《前卫报》社长斐盖尔，因为他发表了越南民主共和国主席胡志明的访问记。
