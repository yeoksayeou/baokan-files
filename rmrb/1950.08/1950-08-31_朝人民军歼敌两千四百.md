Title: Korean People's Army Annihilates 2,400 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1950-08-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 29th that the People's Army troops repelled the counter-attacks of the US military and the Syngman Rhee puppet army on all fronts, inflicting significant blows on the enemy. The People's Army units liberating Uiheung crushed the enemy's stubborn resistance and continued their offensive. In the fighting in this area, the People's Army killed or wounded more than 800 enemy officers and soldiers, captured more than 200 enemy soldiers, and seized the following spoils of war: three tanks, five 105mm guns, four rocket launchers, twelve anti-aircraft machine guns, more than fifty light machine guns, and other weapons and ammunition.
　　American aircraft continued to carry out barbaric and indiscriminate bombings of peaceful cities and villages in both North and South Korea. Several cities and villages were destroyed, and many peaceful residents were killed or wounded.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a communique on the evening of the 29th that the People's Army troops repelled the fierce counter-attacks of the US military and the Syngman Rhee puppet army on all fronts, inflicted blows on the enemy, and continued to engage the enemy in fierce battles.
　　The People's Army units liberating Yiheung inflicted heavy losses on the enemy's manpower and equipment in fierce battles, and are now continuing to advance southward. The People's Army units operating south of Yiheung killed or wounded more than 1,200 enemy soldiers and captured more than 200 enemy officers and soldiers. They seized thirty-five cannons, fifty heavy machine guns, five hundred rifles, and a large amount of other equipment. Currently, the People's Army units operating in this area are continuing to strike the enemy and advance southward.

### 朝人民军歼敌两千四百

1950-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军歼敌两千四百
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十九日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上击退美军部队和李承晚伪军的反攻，予敌以重大打击。解放军威的人民军部队击溃敌军的顽抗，继续展开攻势。在该方面的战斗中，人民军毙伤敌军官兵八百余人，俘敌二百余人，缴获战利品如下：坦克三辆，一○五公厘炮五门，火箭炮四门，高射机枪十二挺，轻机枪五十余挺以及其他武器、军火。
    美国飞机继续对朝鲜南北部的和平城乡进行野蛮的盲目轰炸。若干城市和乡村被炸毁，和平居民伤亡甚众。
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十九日晚发表公报称：人民军部队在各战线上击退美军和李承晚伪军的猛烈反攻，并予敌人以打击后，继续与敌进行激战。
    解放义兴的人民军部队，在猛烈的战斗中予敌军有生力量和装配以重大损失，现正继续向南推进。在义兴以南作战的人民军部队，毙伤敌军一千二百多名，俘敌官兵二百多名。缴获大炮三十五门、重机枪五十挺、步枪五百支以及大批其他装备。目前在这一带作战的人民军部队正在继续打击敌人，并向南推进。
