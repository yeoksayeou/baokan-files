Title: Korean People's Army Continuously Repulses US Army Counterattacks; Breaks Through US 25th Division's Position on the South Coast
Author:
Date: 1950-08-24
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd -- The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 22nd that the People's Army units on all fronts fiercely attacked the counter-offensives of the US military units and the remnants of the Li Chengwan (Syngman Rhee) puppet army, which were covered by aircraft and armored units. In order to recover from their defeat, the enemy continued to launch frantic counterattacks on certain fronts. However, the People's Army units, in close coordination, continued to repel the enemy's counterattacks, inflicting huge losses on the enemy.

The People's Army units attacking on the South Coast broke through the positions of the US 25th Division and the US Marine Corps and continued to launch fierce attacks.

The People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units inflicted heavy losses on the US Air Force, shooting down three US fighter planes that invaded Sinanju. In the Pyongyang area, one US fighter plane was shot down.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd -- The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 22nd that the People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the US military units and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army on all fronts. The US military units and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army, covered by tanks and armored units, as well as the so-called police teams and youth defense corps who were forcibly mobilized, concentrated all their strength and attempted to put up a desperate resistance. The People's Army units inflicted heavy blows on the enemy.

US aircraft have been invading various regions in southern and northern Korea for days, continuing to carry out indiscriminate and barbaric bombings. On August 19, more than 50 US aircraft bombed Chengjin (Kimchaek), dropping more than 500 bombs in the city center, destroying many houses, schools, hospitals, theaters, and cultural and educational institutions, causing numerous casualties among residents.

### 朝鲜人民军连续击退美军反击  南海岸突破美军第廿五师阵地

1950-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军连续击退美军反击
    南海岸突破美军第廿五师阵地
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十二日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上猛烈地打击由飞机和装甲部队掩护的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军的反攻。敌人为挽回自己的败退，继续在某些战线上展开疯狂的反击。但人民军部队在紧密的协同作战下，继续击退敌人的反击，予敌人以巨大损失。
    在南海岸方面进攻中的人民军部队，突破美军第二十五师和美海军陆战队的阵地，继续展开激烈的攻击。
    人民军高射炮部队予美军航空部队以巨大的损失，击落侵袭新安州的美战斗机三架。在平壤地区，击落美战斗机一架。
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十二日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与美军部队和李承晚残余伪军继续展开猛烈的战斗。由坦克和装甲部队掩护下的美军部队与李承晚残余伪军，以及强制动员来的所谓警察队和青年防卫队，集中全部力量，图作拚死的顽抗。人民军部队予敌人以深重的打击。
    美国飞机连日侵袭朝鲜南部和北部各地区，继续进行盲目的野蛮的轰炸。八月十九日，五十余架美机空袭了城津，在市中心投弹五百余枚，炸毁许多住房、学校、病院、剧场以及文化教育机关，居民伤亡甚众。
