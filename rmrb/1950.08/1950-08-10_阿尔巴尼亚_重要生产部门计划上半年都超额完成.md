Title: Albania: Important Production Sectors Exceed Half-Year Plans
Author:
Date: 1950-08-10
Page: 4

　　Tirana, August 7th, TASS dispatch citing the Albanian News Agency: The Statistical Bureau of the Albanian State Planning Commission has recently released a communique regarding the execution results of the Albanian national economic development plan for the first half of 1950. The communique states that compared to the same period in 1949, the output of various important production sectors is as follows: the total production of the mining industry is 165 percent, petroleum is 127 percent, ore is 254 percent, and the manufacturing industry is 133 percent. The communique points out that the total area for planting major crops this year has greatly exceeded that of 1949. State-owned farms and machine tractor stations have made significant progress. The total volume of freight transported in the transportation industry has increased. The total amount of projects completed by the Ministry of Construction is 143 percent of that in the first half of last year. The total trade volume is 158 percent of the same period last year. The number of industrial workers has surged.

### 阿尔巴尼亚  重要生产部门计划上半年都超额完成

1950-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚
    重要生产部门计划上半年都超额完成
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社地拉那七日电引阿尔巴尼亚通讯社消息：阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会统计局顷发表了关于一九五○年上半年发展阿尔巴尼亚国民经济计划执行结果的公报，公报称：与一九四九年同期相较，各项重要生产部门的产量如下：采矿工业的总生产量百分之一六五，石油百分之一二七，矿砂百分之二五四，制造工业百分之一三三。公报指出：今年种植主要农作物的总面积，已大大超过了一九四九年。国营农场和机器拖拉机站，已有了显著的进步。运输业中的货运总量已有增加。营造工业部所完成的工程总量为去年上半年的百分之一四三。贸易总量为去年同期的百分之一五八。产业工人人数已经激增。
