　　Lecture on New Democracy
　　The New Democracy lecture series, organized by the Beijing YMCA, will feature Mr. Ke Li, Secretary of the All-China Sports Federation, speaking on "National Sports in New Democracy" at the YMCA on Saturday, August 19th, at 7:30 PM. All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座，定八月十九日（星期六）晚七时半请全国体育总会秘书轲犁先生在该会讲：“新民主主义的国民体育”。欢迎各界前往听讲。
