Title: Korean People's Army Liberates Uiseong, Cheongsong, and Gunwi; Annihilates 6,600 Enemies in the Andong Area; Eliminates and Sweeps Away the Majority of the U.S. Mechanized Regiment in the Uiseong Area

Author:

Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 8th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 7th that People's Army troops continued to engage in fierce battles with U.S. military units and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army on all fronts. The People's Army units attacking the Uiseong area eliminated and swept away the majority of the U.S. mechanized regiment stubbornly resisting in the area, and on the 5th, liberated Uiseong County in Gyeongsangbuk-do, an enemy operational stronghold and transportation hub. As a result of fierce battles between the People's Army units continuing to advance in the Andong area and the enemy, over 6,000 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, over 600 were captured alive, and the following war trophies were seized: 4 tanks, 17 armored vehicles, 3 57mm cannons, 25 60mm rocket launchers, 10 mortars, 60 other cannons, 14 anti-aircraft machine guns, over 20 light and heavy machine guns each, over 900 rifles, and many other vehicles, radios, automatic guns, carbines, artillery shells, etc.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 8th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 7th that the People's Army units advancing on the east coast defeated the U.S. military units and remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army continuing to stubbornly resist in the mountainous areas near Cheongsong, and liberated Cheongsong County in Gyeongsangbuk-do on August 5th.
    On the same day, another unit of the People's Army liberated Gunwi County, an important strategic stronghold of the enemy, an outpost for defending Daegu, and 40 kilometers directly north of Daegu. In this battle, many enemy soldiers were killed, wounded, or captured, and a large number of war trophies were seized.
    On the 6th, U.S. planes attacked Pyongyang, Sariwon, and other places three times, carrying out indiscriminate bombing and strafing of residents, causing a large number of homes to be destroyed and many residents to be injured or killed.

### 朝鲜人民军解放义城青松军威  安东方面歼敌六千六百  义城方面击灭与扫荡美机械化联队大部

1950-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军解放义城青松军威
    安东方面歼敌六千六百
    义城方面击灭与扫荡美机械化联队大部
    【新华社平壤八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与美军部队和李承晚残余伪军继续展开着猛烈的战斗。进攻义城方面的人民军击灭与扫荡了在该区顽抗的美军机械化联队的大部，五日解放了敌人的作战据点和交通要道庆尚北道的义城县。在安东方面继续进攻中的人民军部队与敌人展开激战的结果，打死、打伤敌军六千余名，活捉六百余名，缴获战利品如下：坦克四辆，装甲车十七辆，五七公厘炮三门，六零公厘火箭炮二十五门。迫击炮十门，其他炮六十门，高射机关枪十四挺，轻重机枪各二十余挺，步枪九百余支，其他汽车、电台、自动枪、卡宾枪、炮弹等甚多。
    【新华社平壤八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七日晨发表战报称：在东海岸方面进攻的人民军部队，击破在青松附近山岳地带继续顽抗的美军部队与李承晚残余伪军，于八月五日解放了庆尚北道的青松县。
    同日，人民军另部解放了敌军重要战略据点、防御大邱的前哨基地、大邱正北四十公里的军威县。在这一战役中，毙伤俘敌军甚多，缴获大批战利品。
    六日，美机三次袭击平壤、沙里院等地，对居民施行盲目炸射，使为数极多的住宅遭到破坏，居民伤亡甚众。
