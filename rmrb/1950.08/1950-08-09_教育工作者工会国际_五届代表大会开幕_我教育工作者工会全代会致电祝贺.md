Title: Educational Workers' Union International Fifth Congress Opens; Our National Congress of Educational Workers' Union Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author: 
Date: 1950-08-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: TASS reports that the Fifth Congress of the Educational Workers' Union International, a sector of the World Federation of Trade Unions, opened in Vienna on the 6th. Participating were delegations of educational workers from the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Mongolia, and countries such as the United States, France, Italy, Norway, and Australia.

　　World Federation of Trade Unions Vice President Soubbotka, in his address, pointed out that the Educational Workers' International faces significant tasks. It must continuously strengthen the solidarity of educational workers' unions, organize work to strive for economic, social, and cultural needs, and play an effective role in the struggle to defend peace.

　　The congress will last five days, and delegates will listen to and discuss the administrative committee's work report and a report on the struggle for peace.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The First National Congress of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union has sent a telegram to congratulate the Fifth Congress of the Educational Workers' Union International, currently being held in Vienna, Austria. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the Presidium and all delegates of the Fifth Congress of the Educational Workers' Union International!

　　As the highest leading body of educational workers' unions worldwide, the Fifth Congress of the Educational Workers' Union International, opens in Vienna, the First National Congress of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union is being held in Beijing. We, on behalf of all educational workers in China, extend our warm congratulations to the congress. The Chinese Educational Workers' Union vows to unite with educational workers' unions of all countries, under the unified leadership of the Educational Workers' Union International, to defend lasting world peace and people's democracy, oppose the US imperialist plot to create war, better educate our future generations, and defend the vital interests of educational workers.

### 教育工作者工会国际  五届代表大会开幕  我教育工作者工会全代会致电祝贺

1950-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　教育工作者工会国际
    五届代表大会开幕
    我教育工作者工会全代会致电祝贺
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社讯，世界工联产业部之一、教育工作者工会国际的第五届代表大会，于六日在维也纳开幕。与会的有苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古及美、法、意、挪威、澳大利亚等国的教育工作者代表团。
    世界工联副主席苏普卡在会上致辞时指出：教育工作者国际正面临着重大的任务。它必须不断加强教育工作者工会的团结，组织为争取经济、社会和文化需要而进行的工作。必须在维护和平的斗争中发挥有效的作用。
    大会会期五日，代表们将听取并讨论行政委员会的工作报告和关于争取和平的报告。
    【新华社八日讯】中国教育工作者工会第一次全国代表大会顷致电祝贺现在奥京维也纳召开的教育工作者工会国际第五届代表大会。电文如下：教育工作者工会国际第五届代表大会主席团暨全体代表同志们！
    当着全世界教育工作者工会的最高领导机关——教育工作者工会国际第五届代表大会在奥京开幕的时候，中国教育工作者工会第一次全国代表大会正在北京开会。我们谨代表中国全体教育工作者向大会致热烈的祝贺。中国教育工作者工会誓与各国教育工作者工会一道，在教育工作者工会国际的统一领导下，为保卫世界持久和平与人民民主，反对美帝国主义制造战争的阴谋，更好地教育我们的后一代，并保卫教育工作者切身利益而奋斗。
