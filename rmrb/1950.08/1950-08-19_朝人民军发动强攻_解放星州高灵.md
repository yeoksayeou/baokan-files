Title: Korean People's Army Launches Strong Offensive, Liberates Seongju and Goryeong
Author:
Date: 1950-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 18th: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the morning of the 17th that People's Army units continued to launch powerful offensives on all fronts against the US military units and the remnant troops of the Syngman Rhee puppet army, who were attempting to concentrate massive forces and resist stubbornly. People's Army units advancing in the Seongju and Goryeong areas defeated the enemy's stubborn resistance and liberated the enemy's important defensive strongholds, the county towns of Seongju and Goryeong (note that both cities are located on the west bank of the Nakdong River, southwest of Waegwan). In the battles to liberate Seongju and Goryeong, People's Army units captured many war trophies and killed, wounded, and captured a large number of enemy soldiers.

### 朝人民军发动强攻  解放星州高灵

1950-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军发动强攻
    解放星州高灵
    【新华社平壤十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十七日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上对妄图集中庞大的兵力竭力顽抗的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，继续发动强大攻势。在星州、高灵方面进攻中的人民军部队，挫败了敌人的顽抗，解放了敌人的重要防御据点星州与高灵两县城（按两城均在洛东江西岸，倭馆西南）。人民军部队在解放星州、高灵的战斗中，缴获许多战利品，毙伤俘敌军甚众。
