Title: Korean People's Army Advances on All Fronts Towards the Nakdong River; Captures Strategic Stronghold of Andong, Annihilating 2,700 Enemy Troops; Liberates Hamchang District, Eliminating a Large Part of the U.S. 35th Regiment; Advances in Jinju and Gimcheon Areas After Overcoming Stubborn Enemy Resistance
Author:
Date: 1950-08-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the evening of the 3rd that the People's Army units on all fronts have frustrated the stubborn resistance of U.S. military units and remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army, and continue to advance. The People's Army units attacking towards Andong, taking advantage of favorable geographical conditions, annihilated the U.S. military that continued to resist, and completely liberated Andong (approximately 80 kilometers north of Daegu), an important city in Gyeongsangbuk-do and a crucial military strategic stronghold of the enemy, on August 1st.

　　In the battle to liberate Andong, the People's Army units killed or wounded over 1,500 enemy soldiers, captured over 1,200 alive, and seized 13 U.S.-made heavy artillery pieces, 6 105mm cannons, over 900 rifles, and a large number of other spoils such as cars, machine guns, radios, and ammunition.

　　On the same day, another unit of the People's Army attacking towards Hamchang liberated the Hamchang district, which belongs to Sangju County, Gyeongsangbuk-do.

　　In the battle to liberate Hamchang, the People's Army units eliminated the majority of the U.S. 25th Division's 35th Regiment and the main force of the puppet army's Western Corps. Many enemy soldiers were captured. Four 105mm cannons, ten mortars, over 200 carbines, ten heavy machine guns, pistols, radios, and countless other spoils of various ammunition were seized, and over 20 enemy vehicles were destroyed.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of the 3rd that the People's Army units on all fronts are continuing to launch fierce battles against U.S. military units and the remaining Syngman Rhee puppet army. In the area of Jire (note - Jire is approximately 18 kilometers southwest of Gimcheon County, Gyeongsangbuk-do), the People's Army repeatedly engaged in fierce battles with the U.S. military and the Syngman Rhee puppet army, breaking through the repeated stubborn resistance of the U.S. and Rhee forces, inflicting heavy blows on the enemy, killing or wounding over 500 enemy soldiers, and seizing ten mortars, 35 vehicles, 15 rocket launchers, over ten heavy machine guns and anti-aircraft machine guns each, and many other rifles, automatic rifles, carbines, ammunition, and so on.

　　The People's Army, having liberated Jinju, continues to advance while overcoming the enemy's resistance and various difficulties.

### 朝鲜人民军全线向洛东江推进  攻占战略据点安东歼敌两千七  解放咸昌区消灭美军第三十五团大部  晋州及金泉方面击破敌军顽抗继续前进

1950-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军全线向洛东江推进
    攻占战略据点安东歼敌两千七
    解放咸昌区消灭美军第三十五团大部
    晋州及金泉方面击破敌军顽抗继续前进
    【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上挫败美军部队与李承晚伪军残余的顽抗，继续向前推进。向安东方面进攻的人民军部队，利用有利的地理条件，消灭了继续顽抗的美军，于八月一日完全解放了敌人重要的军事战略据点庆尚北道的重要城市安东（大邱以北约八十公里）。
    在解放安东的战斗中，人民军部队毙、伤敌军一千五百余名，活捉一千二百余名，缴获美式重炮十三门、一零五公厘炮六门，步枪九百余支及其它汽车、机关枪、电台、弹药等战利品甚多。
    同日，向咸昌方面进攻的人民军另部，解放了庆尚北道尚州县所属的咸昌区。
    在解放咸昌的战斗中，人民军部队消灭了美军第二十五师三十五团的大部和伪军西部军团的主力部队。俘掳敌军甚多。缴获一零五公厘炮四门、迫击炮十门、卡宾枪二百余支、重机枪十挺、手枪、电台、各种弹药等战利品无数，并击毁敌汽车二十余辆。
    【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上对美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，继续展开猛烈战斗。在知礼方面（按——知礼在庆尚北道金泉县西南约十八公里），人民军与美军及李承晚伪军反复展开激战，击破美李军的数次顽抗，予敌人以沉重打击，毙、伤敌军五百余名，缴获迫击炮十门、汽车三十五辆、火箭炮十五门、重机枪与高射机关枪各十余挺，及其他步枪、自动枪、卡宾枪、弹药等甚多。
    解放晋州的人民军，在击破敌人的抵抗与克服各种困难中继续前进。
