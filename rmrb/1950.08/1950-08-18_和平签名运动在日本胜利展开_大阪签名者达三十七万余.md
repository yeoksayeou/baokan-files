Title: Peace Signature Campaign Victoriously Unfolds in Japan; Over 370,000 Signatures Collected in Osaka
Author:
Date: 1950-08-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: A delayed dispatch from Tokyo: The Stockholm Appeal signature campaign is victoriously unfolding in Japan. The Osaka region is especially active. As of July 1st, the Peace Signatures collected in Osaka by the League for the Defense of Democracy alone have reached over 248,000. The signatures collected by the Osaka local organization of the Youth Homeland Front had reached about 130,000 by mid-July, not including the signature ballots directly submitted to the central Youth Homeland Front. Well-known figures in the cultural and educational circles who have signed the declaration include: Hiroshi Suekawa, President of Ritsumeikan University, Yasushi Tsuneto, President of Osaka Municipal University, poet Juzo Ono, Koji Fushimi, Professor of the Faculty of Science of Osaka University, and many others.

### 和平签名运动在日本胜利展开  大阪签名者达三十七万余

1950-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　和平签名运动在日本胜利展开
    大阪签名者达三十七万余
    【新华社十七日讯】东京迟到消息：斯德哥尔摩宣言签名运动，正在日本胜利展开。大阪地区尤其活跃。截至七月一日止，仅民主主义拥护同盟在大阪所征集的和平签名，已达二十四万八千余个。青年祖国战线大阪地方组织所征集的签名，在七月中旬已达十三万个左右，而直接送交青年祖国战线中央的签名票，尚未计算在内。在宣言上签名的文化教育界知名人士有：立命馆大学校长末川博、大阪市立大学校长恒藤泰、诗人小野十三郎、大阪大学理学院教授伏见康治等多人。
