Title: Korean People's Army Strikes Back Against Enemy Counterattacks; Liberates Gimje and Continues Southward Advance
Author:
Date: 1950-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th, TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on the morning of the 19th: The People's Army units are fiercely battling the U.S. military and the Syngman Rhee puppet army on all fronts, inflicting severe losses on the enemy.

	The People's Army units that liberated Gimje (phonetic) continued their offensive in the area south of Gimje, inflicting heavy losses on the Syngman Rhee puppet army's 5th Division.

	Along the southern coast, People's Army units continued to advance, inflicting a devastating blow on the retreating U.S. 25th Division.

	The People's Army units operating on the east coast are fighting fiercely, striking at enemy counterattacks.

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th, TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on the evening of the 19th: People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the stubbornly resisting enemy on all fronts. American and Syngman Rhee's troops are fighting rabidly with the support of the U.S. Air Force.

	On the east coast, the enemy launched a counterattack, attempting to regain several strongholds occupied by the People's Army units, but under the blows of the People's Army, they are currently retreating with heavy losses.

	U.S. aircraft barbarously bombed peaceful cities and villages in the rear. On August 18th, U.S. aircraft brutally attacked many residential areas in South Pyongan Province, South Hamgyong Province, and Gangwon Province. As a result, many houses and cultural and educational institutions were destroyed. Many civilians were killed and wounded.

### 朝鲜人民军打击敌人反攻  解放今义继续南进

1950-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军打击敌人反攻
    解放今义继续南进
    在南海岸方面亦正继续向前挺进
    予美军第二十五师以毁灭性打击
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十九日晨宣布：人民军部队在各线对美军及李承晚伪军进行激战，使敌军蒙受严重的损失。
    解放今义（译音）的人民军部队继续在今义以南的地区进攻，使李承晚傀儡军第五师遭到巨大损失。
    沿南海岸，人民军部队继续向前挺进，予败退的美军第二十五师部队以毁灭性的打击。
    在东海岸作战的人民军部队，打击着敌人的反攻，进行激战。
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十九日晚宣布：人民军部队在各线继续对进行顽抗的敌军展开激战。美国的和李承晚的军队正在美空军的支持下狂暴地战斗着。
    在东海岸，敌军进行反击，图重获人民军部队占领了的若干据点，但是，在人民军的打击下，目前正在败退中，损失巨大。
    美国飞机野蛮轰炸后方的和平城市与乡村。八月十八日，美国飞机残暴地袭击平安南道、咸镜南道和江原道内的许多居民区。结果许多房屋、文化与教育机关被炸毁。平民伤亡很多。
