Title: Heze People's Bank Develops Remittance Business
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 2

[Ji Lu Yu Electric] The Heze Office of the People's Bank of China has recently seen its remittance business grow daily and its area gradually expand. In addition to conducting remittances with Jinan, Jinan, Liaocheng and Puyang under normal circumstances, it also began remittances with Linqing, Tianjin, Xuzhou and Handan in early April. In order to facilitate the trade of merchants by adjusting a large number of materials, the office is actively preparing to establish remittance relations with Beijing, Shijiazhuang, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Shangqiu, Cao County, Shan County and other places.

### 菏泽人民银行汇兑业务开展

1949-04-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　菏泽人民银行汇兑业务开展
    【冀鲁豫电】中国人民银行菏泽办事处，近来汇兑业务日益开展，地区逐渐扩大，除济宁、济南、聊城、濮阳四处按照正常情况进行汇兑外，现与临清、天津、徐州、邯郸亦于四月初开始通汇。办事处为大量调剂物资便利商民交易，正在积极筹备和北平、石门、郑州、开封、商邱、曹县、单县等地建立通汇关系。
