Title: All India Trade Union Congress to be Held in Bombay, Discussing the Tasks of the Indian Working Class
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi: The annual meeting of the All India Trade Union Congress will be held in Bombay from May 27th to May 30th. The meeting will discuss the current political and economic situation, as well as the tasks of the Indian working class.

### 全印职工大会将在孟买举行　讨论印工人阶级任务

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　全印职工大会将在孟买举行
  　讨论印工人阶级任务
    【新华社北平二十一日电】塔斯社德里讯：全印职工大会年会将于五月二十七日至五月三十日在孟买举行。该会将讨论目前的政治与经济形势，以及印度工人阶级的任务。
