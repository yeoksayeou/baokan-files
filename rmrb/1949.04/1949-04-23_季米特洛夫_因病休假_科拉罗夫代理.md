Title: Dimitrov Takes Sick Leave, Kolarov to Act as Proxy
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 21st — TASS, Sofia, 19th: Bulgarian official sources have announced that Premier Dimitrov is taking sick leave. During the Premier's leave, his duties will be carried out by Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Kolarov.

### 季米特洛夫　因病休假　科拉罗夫代理

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　季米特洛夫
  　因病休假
  　科拉罗夫代理
    【新华社北平二十一日电】塔斯社索非亚十九日讯：保加利亚官方顷发表公告称：季米特洛夫总理因病休假。在总理休假期间，其职务由副总理兼外交部长科拉罗夫代理。
