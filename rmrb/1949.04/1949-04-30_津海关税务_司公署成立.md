Title: Tianjin Customs Tax Bureau Established
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Customs Tax
  　Bureau Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 28th] The Tianjin Customs Tax Bureau was established on the 27th. Tax Commissioner Zhu Jianbai explained that the general task of the Customs is to manage import and export goods and collect customs duties. In the future, efforts should be made to increase customs revenue under the principles of facilitating foreign trade, developing production, and promoting economic prosperity.

### 津海关税务　司公署成立
新华社
1949-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　津海关税务
  　司公署成立
    【新华社天津二十八日电】天津海关税务司公署于二十七日成立。税务司朱健白说明海关的总任务是管理进出口货物和征收关税。今后应在畅通对外贸易，发展生产、繁荣经济的原则下，争取增加关税收入。
