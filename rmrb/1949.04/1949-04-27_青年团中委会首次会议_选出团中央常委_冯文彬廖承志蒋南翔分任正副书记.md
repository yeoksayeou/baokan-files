Title: First Meeting of the Central Committee of the Youth League: Election of Standing Committee Members, Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, and Jiang Nanxiang Appointed as Secretary and Deputy Secretaries
Author:
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 26th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, elected on the 18th of this month at the First National Congress of the Youth League, held its first plenary meeting in Beiping the other day. Twenty-five central committee members and ten alternate central committee members attended the plenary session, electing Comrade Feng Wenbin as chairman. The meeting elected the following nine comrades as members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Youth League: Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, Jiang Nanxiang, Qian Junrui, Gao Tang, Li Chang, Song Yiping, Lu Ping, and Han Tianshi; and Comrades Chen Jiakang and Zhang Ben (female) as alternate members of the Standing Committee, forming the Central Standing Committee. Feng Wenbin was elected as the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and Liao Chengzhi and Jiang Nanxiang as Deputy Secretaries. The meeting discussed various cases submitted by the Congress to the Central Committee for handling, and approved: a notification from the Central Committee of the League to the league committees above the prefectural committee level in various regions to establish league schools where possible, and the division of labor in the training plans of league schools at all levels; a plan to be uniformly formulated by the Central Committee regarding the publication of regular publications of the Central Committee of the League and league committees at all levels, and the editing of various youth reading materials; a public solicitation and unified regulation by the Central Committee of various proposals and temporary motions, including the League emblem, League flag, League certificate, and League song, totaling fifteen items, to be handled separately by the Standing Committee. The meeting decided that the second plenary meeting of the Central Committee will be held in six months, convened by the Central Standing Committee.

### 青年团中委会首次会议  选出团中央常委  冯文彬廖承志蒋南翔分任正副书记

1949-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中委会首次会议
  　选出团中央常委
    冯文彬廖承志蒋南翔分任正副书记
    【新华社北平二十六日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会自本月十八日在青年团第一次全国代表大会上选举产生后，于日前在北平举行第一次全体会议。全会到有中央委员二十五人，候补中央委员十人，推冯文彬同志主席。会议上选举出：冯文彬、廖承志、蒋南翔、钱俊瑞、高棠、李昌、宋一平、陆平、韩天石等九同志为青年团中央委员会的常务委员，陈家康、张本（女）两同志为候补常务委员，组成中央常务委员会；并选举冯文彬为青年团中央委员会书记，廖承志、蒋南翔为副书记。会议讨论了代表大会提交中央委员会处理之各项案件，计通过：由团中央通知各地地委以上的团委在可能情况下开办团校，以及各级团校在训练计划上的分工，由团中央统一拟定关于出版团中央及各级团委的定期刊物与编辑各种青年读物之计划，由团中央公开征求并统一规定青年团之团徽、团旗、团证、团歌等各种提案及临时动议共十五件，提交常委会分别办理。会议决定第二次中央委员会全体会议于半年后举行，由中央常务委员会召集之。
