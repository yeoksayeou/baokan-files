Title: Small Units of Seven or Fewer People in Cities to Implement Package Supply System
Author:
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 3

　　Small Units of Seven or Fewer People in Cities
  　Implementing Package Supply System
    [This Newspaper's Report] The North China People's Government, in order to implement a package supply system of 140 catties of millet per person per month for small units of seven or fewer people in cities, specifically notified the municipal governments of Beiping, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Tangshan, and Taiyuan, as well as the administrative offices of various provinces, the various organs at the North China level, and the various directly subordinate organs of the various departments of the North China People's Government on the 31st of last month, stating: In order to facilitate work, reduce administrative hassles, and under the principle of not increasing financial expenditures, it is now stipulated that small units of seven or fewer people in cities such as Beiping, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Taiyuan, and Tangshan, and organs with seven or fewer people on the supply system, may implement a package supply system. The standard is 140 catties of millet per person per month, which includes all expenses for the purchase of cooking utensils, the hiring of cooks, and personal allowances, except for clothing expenses. It is hoped that this will be implemented accordingly!

### 城市七人以下小单位　实行包干供给制

1949-04-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　城市七人以下小单位
  　实行包干供给制
    【本报讯】华北人民政府为城市七人以下之小单位实行每人每月一百四十斤小米的包干供给制，特于上月三十一日通知北平、天津、张家口、唐山、太原各市政府，及各行署省府、华北级各机关、华北人民政府各部门各直属机关称：为便利工作，减少事务麻烦，并在不增加财政开支的原则下，兹规定北平、天津、张家口、太原、唐山等城市七个人以下之小单位，和供给制人员在七人以内之机关，均可实行包干供给制，其标准，每人每月小米一百四十斤，除服装费外，其他炊具购买，伙夫雇用，个人津贴等，均包括在内，希即依照执行！
