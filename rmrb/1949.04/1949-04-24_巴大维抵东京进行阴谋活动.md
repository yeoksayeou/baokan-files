Title: Badawi Arrives in Tokyo to Engage in Conspiracy Activities
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 3

　　Badawi Arrives in Tokyo to Engage in Conspiracy Activities
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 22nd] Badawi, the former head of the U.S. military advisory group in Nanjing, has recently returned to Tokyo, Japan, and has been appointed as the commander of the U.S. 7th Division under MacArthur's command. It is generally believed that Badawi's appointment in Tokyo is related to MacArthur's conspiratorial activities against China.
　　Another dispatch: L. Randolph Prewitt, the director of the China branch of the U.S. Economic Cooperation Administration, completed all preparations in the United States to continue assisting the Kuomintang reactionaries and returned to Shanghai from the United States on the 21st.

### 巴大维抵东京进行阴谋活动

1949-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴大维抵东京进行阴谋活动
    【新华社北平二十二日电】前美国驻南京军事顾问团团长巴大维又于最近返回日本东京，并被任为麦克阿瑟统辖下的美军第七师师长。一般认为巴大维之在东京任职，与麦克阿瑟对中国的阴谋活动有关。
    另讯：美国经合总署中国分署署长莱普汉，在美完成了继续援助国民党反动派的一切准备工作以后，已于二十一日由美返抵上海。
