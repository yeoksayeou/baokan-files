Title: Filming the "Advance to the South": Northeast Photography Team Arrives at the Central Plains Front

Author:

Date: 1949-04-13
Page: 3

　　Filming the "Advance to the South"
  　Northeast Photography Team Arrives at the Central Plains Front
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 12th] The 12th and 18th teams of the Northeast Film Studio's news photography team have arrived at the Central Plains front a few days ago to prepare for filming newsreels of the advance to the South.

### 把“江南进军”摄入镜头　东北摄影队抵中原前线

1949-04-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　把“江南进军”摄入镜头
  　东北摄影队抵中原前线
    【新华社中原十二日电】东北电影制片厂新闻摄影队第十二、十八两队，已于日前到达中原前线，准备拍摄向江南进军的新闻片。
