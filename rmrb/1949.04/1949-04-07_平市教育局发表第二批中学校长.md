Title: The Ping City Education Bureau Announces the Second Group of Middle School Principals
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 2

　　Ping City Education Bureau Announces Second Group of Middle School Principals

　　[This newspaper reports] The Beiping Municipal People's Government Education Bureau announced yesterday (the 5th) the second group of middle school principals in this city. The list is as follows: Xue Chengye, Principal of the Municipal Second Middle School; Sun Zhentao, Principal of the Municipal Third Middle School; Tong Shaozong, Principal of the Municipal Sixth Middle School; Wang Zaitian, Principal of the Municipal Seventh Middle School; Liang Yiqiu, Principal of the Municipal First Girls' Middle School; Peng Hong＠, Principal of the Municipal Fourth Girls' Middle School; Cao Anli, Principal of the Municipal Senior Industrial School; Shi Zongshu, Principal of the Municipal Senior Commercial Vocational School; and Chu Shu, Principal of the Hebei Provincial Women's Vocational School. Except for Tong Shaozong, all have assumed their new positions.

　　(Bai Sheng)

### 平市教育局发表第二批中学校长
柏生
1949-04-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市教育局发表　第二批中学校长
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府教育局昨（五）日发表本市第二批中学校长，名单如下：市立第二中学校长薛成业、市立第三中学校长孙震涛、市立第六中学校长佟绍宗、市立第七中学校长王在田、市立第一女子中学校长梁以俅、市立第四女子中学校长彭鸿＠、市立高级工业学校校长曹安礼、市立高级商业职业学校校长施宗恕、河北省立女子职业学校校长褚曙等九人。除佟绍宗外，均已分别就任新职。　　　　
　　（柏生）
