Title: Unified Management of Public Enterprises: Beijing Establishes Enterprise Bureau
Author: Meng Bei
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 2

　　Unified Management of Public Enterprises
　　Beijing Establishes Enterprise Bureau

　　[This Newspaper] To unify the management of public enterprises under the jurisdiction of Beijing Municipality, the Municipal People's Government has merged the former Beiping Municipal Enterprise Company and the main units under the former Beiping Municipal Public Works Bureau to establish the Enterprise Bureau. The Qinghe Woolen Mill and Yanjing Paper Mill, taken over by the Materials Takeover Committee, are also placed under the administration of the Bureau. As for the Park Management Section, Vehicle Registration Section, Metrology Verification Institute, and Market Management Section formerly under the Public Works Bureau, they are assigned to the Construction Bureau and the Industry and Commerce Bureau, respectively. Currently, most of the thirteen units under the Bureau, including the Tram Company, Water Company, Bus Company, and No. 1 Soy Sauce Factory, have started operations and resumed production.
　　(Meng Bei)

### 统一管理各公营企业　北平成立企业局
孟北
1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　统一管理各公营企业
  　北平成立企业局
    【本报讯】为了统一管理北平市所属的公营企业，市人民政府将原伪北平市企业公司及伪北平市公用局所属的主要单位合并成立企业局。物资接管委员会所接管的清河制呢厂、燕京造纸厂，也划归该局管理。至于原伪公用局所属的公园管理科、车辆登记科、度量衡检定所、市场管理科等部门，则分别划到建设局和工商局。目前该局所属的电车公司、自来水公司、公共汽车公司及第一酱油厂等十三个单位，都已大部开工，恢复生产。
  　（孟北）
