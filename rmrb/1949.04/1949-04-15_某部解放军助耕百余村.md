Title: A PLA Unit Helps Over a Hundred Villages with Spring Planting
Author:
Date: 1949-04-15
Page: 2

　　A PLA Unit Helps Over a Hundred Villages with Spring Planting
　　[This newspaper dispatch] The commanders and fighters of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in the suburbs of Peiping, upon the arrival of the spring planting season, voluntarily organized 2,589 people, 444 warhorses, and 30 carts into numerous spring planting assistance teams. They actively helped the people with spring planting, without accepting any remuneration. In five days, they helped more than 160 villages and 1,966 households, the vast majority of whom were families of military martyrs and industrial workers, and poor and lower-middle peasants, as well as some middle peasants. They delivered a total of 9,450 cartloads of manure, cleared livestock manure corrals 316 times, hauled 204 cartloads of soil for bedding, pounded manure 10,000 times, carried 5,000 shoulder-pole loads of manure, plowed 338 mu of land, and harrowed 4,818 mu of land.

### 某部解放军助耕百余村

1949-04-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　某部解放军助耕百余村
    【本报讯】驻平郊的人民解放军某部指战员在春耕到来时，自动组织了二五八九人和四四四匹战马，三十台车，结成许多助耕队，积极助民春耕，不收任何报酬，五天中，共帮助了一百六十多个村子，一千九百六十六户，内绝大部分是军工烈属和贫雇农，和一部分中农。共送粪九四五○车，起牲口粪圈三一六次。拉土垫圈二○四车，捣粪一万次，担粪五千担，耕地三三八亩，耙地四八一八亩。
