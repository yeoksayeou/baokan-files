Title: Northeast Peasants' Union Sends Telegram to Congratulate Paris Peace Congress, Protests Reactionary Measures of French Government
Author:
Date: 1949-04-22
Page: 3
　　
　　Northeast Peasants' Union
  　Telegraphically Congratulates Paris Peace Congress
  　Protests Reactionary Measures of French Government
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 18th] The Peasants' Unions of 201 counties and 4 banners in the Northeast Liberated Area today jointly sent a telegram to congratulate the upcoming Paris Peace Congress and protest the French government's measures to limit the number of representatives from various countries. The telegram stated: "The World Peace Congress convening in Paris fully demonstrates the firm will and great strength of the people of the world against imperialism and war. In order to defend the freedom and rights we have already obtained, we are willing to resolutely unite with all peace-loving forces in the world to jointly prevent the danger of a new war of aggression. We protest the French government's shameless action of restricting the number of representatives from various countries attending the Peace Congress. We hope that the Congress will overcome the obstruction of the reactionaries and achieve success."

### 东北农民联合会　电贺巴黎和平大会　抗议法政府反动措施

1949-04-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北农民联合会
  　电贺巴黎和平大会
  　抗议法政府反动措施
    【新华社沈阳十八日电】东北解放区二百零一个县和四个旗的农民联合会，本日联合致电祝贺即将开幕的巴黎和平大会，并抗议法国政府限制各国代表人数的措施。电称：世界拥护和平大会在巴黎集会，充分表现了世界人民反帝反战的坚强意志和伟大力量。为了保卫我们现在所已经得到的自由权利，我们愿意坚决与全世界一切爱好和平力量团结一致，来共同制止新的侵略战争的危险。我们抗议法国政府限制各国出席和平大会的代表人数的无耻行动。希望大会战胜反动派的阻挠而得到成功。
