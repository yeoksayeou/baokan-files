Title: World Peace Congress Simultaneously Held in Paris and Prague
Author:
Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 1

　　World Peace Congress
　　Paris and Prague Simultaneously Held
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th】According to Agence France-Presse, Prague, 19th dispatch: It was announced here today that simultaneously with the World Peace Congress opening in Paris tomorrow, the representatives of various countries who were refused entry visas by the French government will hold a peace conference in Prague. The announcement stated that all members of the obstructed delegations will participate in the peace conference to be held here tomorrow, which is part of the Paris Peace Congress.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th】According to a Polish News Agency report: A communique from the World Federation of Democratic Youth pointed out that representatives from fifty youth organizations worldwide are actively participating in the preparations for the upcoming World Peace Congress. Youth from Belgium, Switzerland, Britain, and France will hold a relay race during the peace congress. The World Federation of Democratic Youth, together with the International Union of Students, will also hold an international exhibition to celebrate the World Peace Congress in Paris.

### 世界拥护和平大会　巴黎布拉格同时举行

1949-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界拥护和平大会
  　巴黎布拉格同时举行
    【新华社北平二十日电】据法新社布拉格十九日电：此间今日宣布：与明日在巴黎开幕的世界拥护和平大会同时，被法国政府拒绝发给入境签证的各国代表，将在布拉格举行和平大会。公告说，被阻的各国代表团的一切团员，将参加明日在此举行的和平大会，这一大会是巴黎和平大会的一部分。
    【新华社北平十九日电】据波兰通讯社消息：世界民主青年联盟的公报指出：全世界五十个青年组织的代表，积极参加行将到来的世界拥护和平大会的筹备工作。比利时、瑞士、英国和法国的青年将在和平大会开会期间举行接力赛跑。世界民主青年联盟将会同国际学联，也将举行国际展览会，以庆祝巴黎的世界拥护和平大会。
