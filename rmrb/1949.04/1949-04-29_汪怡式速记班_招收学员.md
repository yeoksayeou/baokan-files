Title: Wang Yi-style Shorthand Class招生 Recruits Students
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 2

　　Wang Yi-style Shorthand Class
  　Recruits Students
    [This newspaper] (news) The "Wang Yi-style" Mandarin shorthand class in Ping City has begun enrolling students. According to Mr. Wang Yi, Mandarin shorthand uses dots and lines to represent sounds, and each hundred characters can be recorded with only sixty strokes. The school is located in the private Yide Primary School in Nanfengsheng Hutong, Xisi, Fourth District.

### 汪怡式速记班　招收学员

1949-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　汪怡式速记班
  　招收学员
    【本报讯】平市“汪怡式”国语速记班开始招生。据汪怡先生谈：国语速记用点线表音，每百字用六十笔即可记出。校址在四区西四南丰盛胡同私立艺德小学内。
