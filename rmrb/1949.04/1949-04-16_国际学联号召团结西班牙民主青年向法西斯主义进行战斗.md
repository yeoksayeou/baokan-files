Title: International Union of Students Calls for Solidarity with Spanish Democratic Youth in the Fight Against Fascism
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 14th: The International Union of Students has issued a letter to student organizations in its 54 member countries, calling on them to express their full solidarity and concern for Spanish democratic youth on the 14th of this month—the Day of Solidarity with Spanish Democratic Youth. The International Union of Students also expressed its conviction that the Day of Solidarity will be a powerful international demonstration reflecting the determination of progressive students to fight against fascism.

### 国际学联号召团结西班牙民主青年向法西斯主义进行战斗

1949-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际学联号召团结西班牙民主青年向法西斯主义进行战斗
    【新华社北平十四日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：国际学联发表告五十四个会员国学生团体书，号召他们在本月十四日——与西班牙民主青年团结日，向西班牙民主青年表示完全的团结一致、休戚相关。国际学联并表示确信团结日将是反映进步学生决心对法西斯主义进行斗争的强大国际示威运动。
