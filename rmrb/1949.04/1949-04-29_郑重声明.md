Author: Bao Yongshan
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 4

Solemn Declaration

　　Due to carelessness, I lost my Municipal Third Hospital work badge number eighteen. I do not know who picked it up and is falsely using my name to spread rumors and deceive people. In addition to declaring it void again, I will not be responsible for any acts of impersonation discovered thereafter.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Bao Yongshan
