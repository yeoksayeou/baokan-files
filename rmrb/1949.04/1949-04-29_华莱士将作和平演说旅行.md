Title: Wallace to Make Peace Speech Tour
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th. TASS, Washington: American Progressive Party leader Wallace announced to reporters that he plans to begin a fifteen-city peace speaking tour on the evening of the 27th, ending the trip with a rally at Madison Square Park in New York on May 16th. Wallace had invited British Member of Parliament Zilliacus and French National Assembly Member Goebell to the United States to jointly conduct the peace tour, but the US authorities refused to issue them entry visas. Wallace has protested this reactionary behavior to the Department of Justice.

### 华莱士将作和平演说旅行

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　华莱士
  　将作和平演说旅行
    【新华社北平二十七日电】塔斯社华盛顿讯：美国进步党领袖华莱士对记者宣布：他打算在二十七日晚间开始十五个城市的和平演讲旅行，并在五月十六日在纽约麦迪逊公园举行大会结束此行。华莱士曾邀请英国下院议员齐里雅库斯和法国国民议会议员高比尔赴美一同进行和平旅行，但美当局拒绝发给入境护照签证，华莱士已向司法部抗议此种反动行为。
