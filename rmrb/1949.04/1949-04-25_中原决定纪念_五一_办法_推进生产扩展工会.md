Title: Central Plains Decides on Methods for Commemorating "May Day"; Promoting Production and Expanding Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 1

　　Central Plains Decides on Methods for Commemorating "May Day"
    Promoting Production and Expanding Trade Unions
    [Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 24th] The Preparatory Committee of the Central Plains Federation of Trade Unions convened a meeting on the 20th of this month with the responsible comrades of the CCP committees and trade unions of Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Luoyang and other cities, and decided that the commemoration of "May Day" Labor Day should achieve two goals: First, to further advance the production work of various cities. Second, to mobilize all party cadres to strengthen the concept of relying on the working class, to closely connect the Party with the workers, and to expand trade union organizations.

### 中原决定纪念“五一”办法  推进生产扩展工会

1949-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原决定纪念“五一”办法
    推进生产扩展工会
    【新华社郑州二十四日电】中原职工总会筹委员于本月二十日召集开封、郑州、洛阳等城市的中共党委与工会负责同志开会，决定纪念“五一”劳动节要达到两个目的：一、把各城市的生产工作向前推进一步。二、对全党干部进行动员，加强依靠工人阶级的观念，密切党与工人的联系，扩大工会组织。
