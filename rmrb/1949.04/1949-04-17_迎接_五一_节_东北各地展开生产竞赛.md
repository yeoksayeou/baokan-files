Title: Welcoming the "May Day" Festival: Production Competitions Unfold Across Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1949-04-17
Page: 1

　　Welcoming "May Day"
　　Northeast China Launches Production Competition
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 16th] To welcome the "May Day" Labor Day, employees of state-owned enterprises in Harbin, Andong, Changchun, Jilin, Qiqihar and other places have launched production competitions. At the beginning of last month, the Harbin Tram Factory started a production merit campaign, proposing a plan to produce 33 trams before "May Day," and on the first day of this month, it has begun to fully implement the contract system. The waterworks guarantees that it will re-install electrical water delivery before the "May Day" festival. The Yuhua Paper Mill in Jilin Province, on the second day of launching the production competition, produced more than 1,100 catties of paper in twenty-one hours, more than doubling the average daily output of 500 catties in March. In addition, state-owned enterprises in Andong, Changchun, Qiqihar and other places have also formulated production plans and launched competitions.

### 迎接“五一”节　东北各地展开生产竞赛

1949-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　迎接“五一”节
  　东北各地展开生产竞赛
    【新华社东北十六日电】哈尔滨、安东、长春、吉林、齐齐哈尔等地公营企业职工，展开生产竞赛，迎接“五一”劳动节。哈尔滨电车厂上月初开始了生产立功运动，提出“五一”以前，出车三十三台的计划，并且在本月一日，已经开始全面实行包乘制。自来水厂保证在“五一”节以前，从新补装电气送水。吉林省裕华造纸厂，在展开生产竞赛第二天，二十一个钟头，就出了一千一百余斤纸，超过三月份每天平均产量五百斤的一倍。此外安东、长春、齐齐哈尔等地公营企业，也纷纷订出生产计划，展开竞赛。
