Notice from the Beiping Municipal Trading Company

To support industrial production and organize the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, the cotton yarn and cloth previously managed by the General Merchandise Branch will be spun off into a separate Cotton Yarn and Cloth Branch, which officially commenced operations on April 18th. We wholesale various fabrics and cotton yarn and exchange raw materials for finished products. We hereby announce this.

Cotton Yarn and Cloth Company Address: No. 38, Sanlihe, Qianwai
Telephone: (7) 2177 (7) 2147
