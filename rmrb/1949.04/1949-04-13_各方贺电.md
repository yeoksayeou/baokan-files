Date: 1949-04-13

　　Congratulatory Messages from Various Parties

To the First National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League:

    The grand gathering of national advanced youth representatives will become the core for uniting and educating the vast youth population, fighting heroically for the complete victory of the People's Liberation War and the construction of a new China. We respectfully wish the conference a successful outcome.

    Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, Zhang Jichun, and Li Da, leading all commanders and fighters of the Second Field Army, respectfully congratulate you. April 1st

To all representatives of the First National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League:

    Chinese youth have been extremely brave and active in the struggle against feudalism, bureaucratic capitalism, and imperialism, both on the front lines and in the rear of the People's Liberation War, making great contributions to the cause of people's liberation. We believe that after the establishment of the New Democratic Youth League and its central organization, the enthusiasm and organization of the youth will be further enhanced. The youth will not only be a great force in the people's liberation struggle but also a great shock force in national construction. We are delighted to attend the opening of the conference and send our congratulations.

    Lin Biao, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Fourth Field Army, and Luo Ronghuan, Political Commissar, leading all commanders and fighters, respectfully congratulate you. April 10th

Dear Representatives:

    Just as the Chinese People's Liberation Army is about to cross the Yangtze River, and on the eve of the victory of the national people's liberation struggle, we are extremely excited to hear the news that the First National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League is about to open in Beiping! The China New Democratic Youth League is an organization of all progressive youth who are willing to fight to the end for the cause of New Democracy. It will inherit and carry forward the glorious tradition of Chinese youth, along with Chinese workers, peasants, and all patriotic democratic classes since the May Fourth Movement, who have been unyielding in their heroic struggle for China's independence, freedom, democracy, and peace, resolutely opposing imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism, becoming the backbone of the nation's youth in every fighting position. The congress will surely lead the Chinese youth to further unite, closely unite with the national workers, peasants, and all democratic classes, resolutely fight for the cause of liberating all of China, support the People's Liberation Army in crossing the Yangtze River, firmly support the eight conditions put forward by Chairman Mao, completely crush the false peace conspiracy of the reactionary traitorous government in Nanjing, eliminate the heinous rule of the Kuomintang reactionaries, and achieve a truly permanent democratic peace. We hereby send our congratulations and wish the conference a victory!

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　April 8th

### 各方贺电

专栏：

　　各方贺电中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会：
    全国先进青年代表大集会，将成为团结与教育广大青年的核心，为争取人民解放战争的彻底胜利和建设新中国而英勇战斗。敬祝大会胜利成功。
    刘伯承、邓小平、张际春、李达率第二野战军全体指战员敬贺    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
                                             四月一日中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国青年在反对封建、反对官僚资本、反对帝国主义的斗争中，在人民解放战争的前线与后方，都是十分勇敢、十分积极的，对人民解放事业作了伟大的贡献。我们相信，新民主主义青年团及其中央组织成立之后，青年们的积极性与组织性会更大的发挥起来，青年们不但是人民解放斗争中的巨大力量，而且会是国家建设中的巨大突击力量。欣逢大会开幕，特电祝贺。
    中国人民解放军第四野战军司令员林彪政治委员罗荣桓率全体指战员敬贺
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　四月十日亲爱的代表们：
    正当中国人民解放大军即将南渡长江，全国人民解放斗争将要胜利的前夜，我们听到中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会即在北平揭幕的消息，异常兴奋！中国新民主主义青年团，是中国一切愿为新民主主义事业奋斗到底的进步青年的组织，它将继承与发扬“五四”以来中国青年与中国工人、农民及一切爱国的民主阶层，百折不挠地为中国独立、自由、民主、和平，坚决反对帝国主义，反对封建主义，反对官僚资本主义而英勇奋斗的光荣传统，成为全国青年在各个战斗岗位上的中坚力量。大会必能领导中国青年更进一步的团结起来，与全国工人农民及一切民主阶层紧密联合，为解放全中国事业，坚决奋斗，支援人民解放军南渡长江，坚决拥护毛主席提出的八项条件，彻底粉碎南京反动卖国政府的假和平阴谋，消灭国民党反动派的万恶统治，实现真正永久的民主的和平。特此电贺，并祝大会胜利！
    中华全国总工会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　四月八日
