Title: North Korean Democratic Youth League Protests Against South Korean Reactionary Government's Arrest of Youth Group Leaders
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

　　North Korean Democratic Youth League
  　Protests Against South Korean Reactionary Government's Arrest of Youth Group Leaders
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st] TASS, Pyongyang, 20th: The North Korean Democratic Youth League held a mass rally at the Pyongyang National Theater to protest the South Korean reactionary authorities' arrest of forty youth group leaders. The assembly passed a letter to Korean youth, urging South Korean youth to actively struggle against the American reactionaries and the Syngman Rhee puppet government.

### 北鲜民主青年同盟　抗议南鲜反动政府逮捕青年团体领袖

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　北鲜民主青年同盟
  　抗议南鲜反动政府逮捕青年团体领袖
    【新华社北平二十一日电】塔斯社平壤二十日讯：北朝鲜民主青年同盟在平壤国家剧院召开群众大会，抗议南朝鲜反动当局逮捕四十名青年团体的领袖。大会通过告朝鲜青年书，敦促南朝鲜青年积极对美国反动派及李承晚傀儡政府斗争。
