Title: Commemorating the Historic Crossing of the Yangtze: Third Field Army Calls on Entire Army to Report on "A Day of the Crossing"
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

　　To Commemorate the Historic Crossing of the Yangtze
  　Third Field Army Calls on Entire Army to Report on "A Day of the Crossing"
    [Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, 22nd] In order to forever commemorate the historical feat of the People's Liberation Army's southward expedition across the Yangtze River, the Political Department of the Third Field Army and the Xinhua News Agency's Third Field Army Branch initiated an "A Day of the Crossing" reporting campaign throughout the army before the crossing. They called on personnel from all departments to report on what they saw and heard during a certain day within the first three days from the start of the crossing.

### 纪念渡江历史壮举　第三野战军号召全军报道“渡江的一日”

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念渡江历史壮举
  　第三野战军号召全军报道“渡江的一日”
    【新华社蚌埠二十二日电】为了永远纪念人民解放军渡江南征的历史壮举，第三野战军政治部与新华社第三野战总分社于渡江前发起全军报道“渡江的一日”，号召各部门人员将开始渡江之日起三天内的某一天中所见所闻报道出来。
