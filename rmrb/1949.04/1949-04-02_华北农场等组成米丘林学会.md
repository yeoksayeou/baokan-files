Title: North China Farm and Others Form Michurin Society
Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 2

　　【Shijiazhuang News】The Agricultural Research Office of North China Agricultural College and North China Farm established the "Michurin" Society in mid-March, with more than 200 participants. At the meeting, Gao Huimin, director of the Agronomy Department of the Agricultural College, introduced the simple story of Michurin, a Soviet railway worker who spent decades of hard work cultivating many new crop seeds.

### 华北农场等组成米丘林学会

1949-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北农场等组成米丘林学会
    【石家庄讯】华北农学院农业研究室及华北农场，于上月中旬成立“米丘林”学会，参加者二百余人。会上农学院农艺系主任高惠民介绍了这位苏联铁路职工出身，用了几十年苦工，培育出很多新生作物种子的生物科学家米丘林的简单事迹。
