Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram to Spanish Democratic Youth, Expressing Solidarity and Resolute Struggle
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 20th: The All-China Students' Federation, in response to the call from the International Union of Students to its member student organizations: "On April 14th – the Day of Solidarity with Spanish Democratic Youth, express solidarity and shared destiny with Spanish Democratic Youth," has issued the following telegram:

"To the Spanish Democratic Youth via the International Union of Students: Let us unite more closely, along with democratic youth around the world, to struggle for the destruction of fascist rule, and for the independence, democracy, and peace of our motherland."

Furthermore: The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram to the Polish National Union of Student Organizations on the 16th, congratulating them on the successful conclusion of the Polish National Union of Student Organizations Congress on the 13th. The telegram stated: "To the Polish National Union of Student Organizations in Warsaw: We extend our heartfelt congratulations on your successful congress and express our respect for your heroic struggle to implement the six-year plan to lay the foundation for socialism."

### 我全国学联致电西班牙民主青年　表示团结一致坚决奋斗

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　我全国学联致电西班牙民主青年
  　表示团结一致坚决奋斗
    【新华社北平二十日电】中华全国学生联合会为响应国际学联对会员国学生团体发出的号召：“在四月十四日——与西班牙民主青年团结日，向西班牙民主青年表示团结一致、休戚相关。”特发出如下的电报：
    “国际学联转西班牙民主青年，让我们更紧密地团结起来，与全世界民主青年一道，为摧毁法西斯的统治，为祖国的独立、民主、和平而奋斗。”
    又讯：中华全国学生联合会于十六日致电波兰全国学生团体联合会，庆贺于十三日闭幕的波兰全国学生团体联合会代表大会，电文称：“华沙波兰全国学生团体联合会：对你们成功的大会谨致衷心的祝贺，并对你们为实现奠定社会主义基础的六年计划所进行的英勇斗争，特致敬意。”
