Title: Ji-Lu-Yu District Group Transportation Company
Author:
Date: 1949-04-18
Page: 2

　　[Ji-Lu-Yu Electric] The Ji-Lu-Yu Administrative Office established the state-owned "Ji-Lu-Yu Heze Automobile Transportation Company" in early April. The company now has buses and freight vehicles going to Jining, Yanzhou, Jinan, Tianjin, Linqing, Handan, Kaifeng, Shangqiu and other places, undertaking two-way and one-way cargo transport for public and private enterprises, as well as business travel.

### 冀鲁豫区组运输公司

1949-04-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫区组运输公司
    【冀鲁豫电】冀鲁豫行政公署于四月上旬成立国营“冀鲁豫菏泽汽车运输公司”。该公司现设有开往济宁、兖州、济南、天津、临清、邯郸、开封、商邱等地之班车及货运车，承揽公私企业的双程及单程货物包运及商旅来往。
