Announcement of the People's Government of Beiping Municipality (No separate document will be issued) Municipal Finance and Taxation Document No. 1

Concerning the announcement of the fixed salary income tax rate:

It has been determined by this Government that the fixed salary income tax rate for civil servants, teachers, police officers, and employees of public and private enterprises in this city will be collected starting from April of this year. The relevant matters for attention are hereby announced separately for the reference and implementation of all organs, schools, and public and private enterprises.

1.  Salary income tax will be levied starting from April of this year.

2.  The salary income tax will be calculated based on the equivalent of the monthly salary income in terms of kilograms of millet, and collected in currency converted at the market price at the time of salary payment. If the salary is originally paid in currency converted from the price of millet, the tax amount will be calculated based on the millet price at the time of payment.

3.  The temporary tax exemption point for salary income tax is 250 kilograms of millet. Those with income below 250 kilograms are exempt from tax. Those with income above 250 kilograms will be taxed on the excess part according to a progressive tax rate ranging from 4% to 15%. The tax brackets and calculation formulas are shown in the table below:

    Salary Income (Millet)　　Tax Rate　　Tax Calculation Formula (X = Salary Income)
    251 kg - 350 kg　　4%　　0.04X - 10 kg
    351 kg - 450 kg　　5%　　0.05X - 13.5 kg
    451 kg - 600 kg　　7%　　0.07X - 22.5 kg
    601 kg - 750 kg　　9%　　0.09X - 34.5 kg
    751 kg - 900 kg　　12%　　0.12X - 57 kg
    Above 900 kg　　15%　　0.15X - 84 kg

　　Note: 1. Fractions of a kilogram of salary income will not be taxed.
　　　　  2. Round to two decimal places.

4.  For employees of public and private enterprises whose food and lodging are provided by the factory or shop, the value of food and lodging shall be calculated as 150 kilograms of millet. If their net salary exceeds 100 kilograms of millet per month, the excess part will be taxed at the same rate as above.

5.  Salary income tax shall be withheld by each salary-paying unit at the time of salary payment. Within five days after salary payment, the withheld tax amount shall be declared to the Municipal Taxation Bureau in accordance with the prescribed form. The Taxation Bureau will issue a payment voucher for immediate payment into the treasury.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mayor: Ye Jianying
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Mayor: Xu Bing
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　April 24, 1949

Note: 1. The address of the Taxation Bureau is No. 1 Donggong'an Street, Qiannei. 2. Withholding lists and reporting forms for salary and remuneration income tax are available for sale at Yibaozhai on Yangmeizhu Slanting Street and Xinghua Printing House on the same street.

### 北平市人民政府公告（不另行文）　府财税字第一号

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　北平市人民政府公告（不另行文）　府财税字第一号
    为公告定额薪资所得税税率由
    查本市公教警人员及公私事业职员之定额薪资所得税税率业经本府确定自本年四月份开始征收兹将应行注意事项分别公告于后希各机关学校公私事业查照办理
    一，薪资所得税自本年四月份开征。
    二，薪资所得税按每人每月所得薪资折合小米斤数为计算标准以发薪当时市价折合货币征收之其薪资如本系按米价折发货币者则以发给时所折米价计算税额。
    三，薪资所得税暂定二百五十斤小米为免税点二百五十斤以下者免税二百五十斤以上者就其超过部份计征税率按百分之四至百分之十五超额累进其税级及计算公式如左表：
    新资收入数  　兑率　　税额计算公式（Ｘ＝薪资收入数）
    ２５１斤—３５０斤　　４％　　．０４Ｘ—１０斤
    ３５１斤—４５０斤　　５％　　．０５Ｘ—１３．５斤
    ４５１斤—６００斤　　７％　　．０７Ｘ—２２．５斤
    ６０１斤—７５０斤　　９％　　．０９Ｘ—３４．５斤
    ７５１斤—９００斤　　１２％　　．１２Ｘ—５７斤
    ９００斤以上者　　１５％　　．１５Ｘ—８４斤
　说明　１．薪资收入零数不及一斤者不征
　 　   ２．小数点第二位四舍五入
    四、公私事业职工之食宿由厂店供给者其食宿以一百五十斤小米计其净得薪资每月超过一百斤小米者就其超过部份课税其税率与上同
