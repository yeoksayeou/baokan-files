Title: Romania Changes Government Personnel
Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, April 27th: Bucharest - The Presidium of the Romanian Parliament has appointed former Minister of Petroleum and Mining, Constantin Nistor, as Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and Simion Tighe and Bită Balasinu as Vice-Chairmen. First Deputy Prime Minister Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and Finance Minister Vasile Luca have been relieved of their concurrent positions as Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the State Planning Commission. Gheorghe Vasilichi has been appointed as the Minister of Petroleum and Mining. The same order also appointed Bobescu Dorinu as Minister of Education, Constantin Mătescu as Deputy Minister of Petroleum and Mining, and Florica Miceanschi as Deputy Minister of Education.

### 罗马尼亚　更动政府人员

1949-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚
  　更动政府人员
    【新华社北平二十七日电】布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚议会主席团任命前石油与采矿部部长康斯坦丁尼斯库为国家计划委员会主席，西米昂·泰格和比特·巴拉西努两人为副主席。原兼国家计划委员会正副主席的第一副总理御治与财长卢加则解除兼职。石油和采矿部长一职由乔治·瓦西里继任。同一命令并任命波比斯库·道里努为教育部部长，康斯坦丁·马特斯库为石油与采矿部副部长，佛洛里卡·米金西斯为教育部副部长。
