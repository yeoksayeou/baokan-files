Title: Beiping Postal Administration to Accept International Mail Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 2

　　Beiping Postal Administration
  　Began Accepting International Mail Yesterday
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Beiping Postal Administration and its subordinate bureaus in this city have received orders to resume accepting international regular and express letters and express registered letters from the 20th. In addition to the ordinary postage, each regular and express letter will be charged an additional 18 yuan in People's Currency, and each express registered letter will be charged an additional 36 yuan. These international express letters are limited to being sent to countries that handle express services.

### 平市邮政管理局　昨起收寄国际函件

1949-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市邮政管理局
  　昨起收寄国际函件
    【本报讯】本市北平邮政管理局及所属各局顷奉令自廿日起恢复收寄国际平快函件及快递挂号函件。平快函件除普通资费外，每件另加人民券十八元，快递挂号函件除普通资费外，每件另加三十六元。该项国际快递函件，系以寄交办理快递事务各国为限。
