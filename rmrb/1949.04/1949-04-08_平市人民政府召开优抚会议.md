Title: The Ping City People's Government Convenes a Meeting on Preferential Treatment
Author: Jiang Xia
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 2

　　The Ping City People's Government Convenes a Meeting on Preferential Treatment
    [Our Newspaper] The Civil Affairs Bureau of the Ping City People's Government convened a meeting yesterday on preferential treatment for the families of martyrs, soldiers, and workers. The meeting was held to investigate and understand the situation of preferential treatment work in various districts of Ping City and to solicit opinions from various districts on the work. According to incomplete statistics, there are currently more than 2,700 households of martyrs', soldiers', and workers' families in Ping City.
  　(Jiang Xia)

### 平市人民政府召开优抚会议
江夏
1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市人民政府召开优抚会议
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府民政局昨日召开烈、军、工属优抚会议，调查了解平市各区优抚工作情形，征求各区对优抚工作之意见。据不完全统计，北平市现有烈、军、工属共二千七百多户。
  　（江夏）
