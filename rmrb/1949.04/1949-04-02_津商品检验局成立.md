Title: Tianjin Commodity Inspection Bureau Established
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 1

Tianjin Commodity Inspection Bureau Established

    The Tianjin Commodity Inspection Bureau was officially established on April 1st. In order to safeguard the reputation of domestic products in the international market and prevent the intrusion of inferior foreign goods into our domestic market, the bureau will implement inspection of import and export commodities.

### 津商品检验局成立

1949-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　津商品检验局成立
    天津市商品检验局，定于四月一日正式成立。为维护国产品在国际市场的信誉，并防止国外劣等货物侵入本国市场，该局对进出口商品将施行检验。
