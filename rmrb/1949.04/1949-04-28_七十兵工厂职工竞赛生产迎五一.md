Title: Seventy Ordnance Factory Workers Launch Production Competition to Welcome May Day
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 2

　　【This Newspaper News】Workers at the Seventy Ordnance Factory are using labor competitions to welcome May Day. After the First Workshop and the Sixth Workshop signed a competition agreement on the 26th, they launched the competition the next day. The original competition condition was to exceed the original production task by more than double before May Day, with good quality and saving of raw materials. Based solely on the competition situation yesterday, it is possible to exceed three to four times the original amount. For example, worker Ma Yangling from the First Workshop made ×× launching tube outer skins in one day, exceeding the plan by four times. Worker Dong Liansheng from the Sixth Workshop made ×× tube body outer skins in one day, exceeding three times the original plan. The competition between the two workshops is now in full swing.

### 七十兵工厂职工竞赛生产迎五一

1949-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　七十兵工厂职工竞赛生产迎五一
    【本报讯】七十兵工厂职工用劳动竞赛来迎接“五一”劳动节。一所与六所二十六日签了竞赛书后，第二天就展开竞赛。原来竞赛条件是在“五一”节前超过原生产任务的一倍以上，并且质量好，节省原料；仅根据昨日一天的竞赛情况，可能超过三倍到四倍，如一所工人马扬岭一天就做出发射筒外皮××个，即超过计划四倍。六所工人董连生一天就做成筒体外皮××个，超过三倍。现两所竞赛已热烈展开。
