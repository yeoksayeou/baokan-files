Title: The Soviet Komsomol Helps Teachers and Students Make Progress Together in Schools
Author:
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 8th: Moscow Dispatch: Yershova, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol, reported on the work of the Komsomol in schools at the 11th Congress of the organization on the 2nd. Yershova said: The main task of the Komsomol in schools is to help teachers promote the progress of students and to improve the work of communist education for young people. The congress discussed Yershova's report for three days and passed it on the 5th.

Pravda published an editorial on the 6th entitled "The Komsomol and Schools." The editorial pointed out that this decision passed by the Komsomol Congress marks the transfer of the Komsomol's attention to schools, to the education and training of the new generation. The editorial said that the Soviet Union implements universal compulsory education, and there are now 34 million students studying in seven-year primary schools and ten-year secondary schools. This number shows how great the achievements of Soviet education are. These schools have become a path for enlightening communist education for the younger generation. The editorial said that the main thing required of the Komsomol is to be the Party's assistant, to educate the younger generation in the spirit of communism, in the spirit of Soviet patriotism, and in the spirit of perseverance and advancement in schools in various ways, and to cultivate them into fighters who are not afraid of all difficulties and pains and who selflessly dedicate themselves to the cause of Lenin and Stalin.

### 苏联青年团在学校中　帮助老师同学共同努力进步

1949-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联青年团在学校中
  　帮助老师同学共同努力进步
    【新华社北平八日电】莫斯科讯：苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会书记之一叶尔硕娃于二日在该团第十一届代表大会上报告青年团在学校中的工作。叶尔硕娃说：共产主义青年团在学校中的主要任务，是帮助教师促进同学们的进步，并改进以共产主义教育青年的工作。大会对叶尔硕娃的报告讨论了三天，在五日通过了这个报告。
    真理报在六日发表社论，题目是“共产主义青年团与学校”。社论指出：共产主义青年团代表大会所通过的这个决定，标志着青年团已把注意力转移到学校、转移到对新的一代的教育和培养。社论说：苏联实行着普遍的强迫教育，现在共有三千四百万学生在七年制的初级学校和十年制的中等学校里学习，这个数字说明了苏联教育事业的成就是多么巨大。这些学校已经变成对青年的一代进行启蒙共产主义教育的通路。社论说：要求青年团的主要事情，是做共产党的助手，在学校中用各种方法以共产主义的精神、以苏维埃爱国主义的精神、以坚忍前进的精神教育年青的一代，把他们培养成不怕一切困难和痛苦的、忘我地献身于列宁斯大林事业的斗士。
