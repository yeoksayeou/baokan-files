　　Today's Dictionary
　　Autoclave - A device used by health departments to sterilize using steam. After 15 minutes at a steam pressure of 15 pounds and a temperature of 121 degrees (Celsius), all bacteria can be killed.
　　Physiological saline solution - Human blood contains a certain amount of salt (sodium chloride), and this saline solution contains the same amount of salt as blood (0.85 percent), hence the name physiological saline solution. Physiological saline solution is often used for intravenous injections for those with massive hemorrhages or poisoning, to replenish blood and detoxify.

### 今日辞典

1949-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　今日辞典
    高压消毒器——是卫生部门利用水蒸气来消毒的一种器具。在蒸气压力十五磅、温计一百二十一度（摄氏）时间十五分钟以后，一切细菌皆可杀死。
    生理食盐水——人体血液里含有一定量的盐（氯化钠），而这种食盐水与血液所含的盐量相等（百分之零点八五），故称生理食盐水。生理食盐水多用做对大出血者或中毒病者的体内注射用，可以补血解毒。
