Title: North Bank of the Yangtze River in Eastern Hubei, We Captured Huangmei and Guangji
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 1

　　North Bank of the Yangtze River in Eastern Hubei
  　We Captured Huangmei and Guangji
    [Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Beiping, broadcast on the 11th] Central Plains News: A unit of the People's Liberation Army occupied the two county towns of Huangmei and Guangji on the north bank of the Yangtze River in eastern Hubei on the 9th. The enemy entrenched in the two cities fled in panic.

### 鄂东长江北岸　我克黄梅广济

1949-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　鄂东长江北岸
  　我克黄梅广济
    【北平新华广播电台十一日广播】中原讯：人民解放军一部九日进占湖北东部长江北岸的黄梅和广济两座县城，盘踞两城的敌人，狼狈逃窜。
