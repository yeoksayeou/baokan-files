Title: People's Bank of Beiping Branch Opens Twenty-Two Remittance Points
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 2

　　People's Bank of Beiping Branch
　　Opens Twenty-Two Remittance Points
    [Our Newspaper] To regulate finance in inland North China and between North China and Northeast China, and to facilitate mutual trade between liberated areas, the People's Bank of Beiping Branch has opened twenty-two remittance points in the past month and a half. Electric remittance methods were adopted between Beiping and Tianjin starting April 1st. The bank's remittance business is rapidly expanding, greatly benefiting the organization of urban and rural material exchange. According to recent information, the People's Bank of Beiping handles remittance points in: Tianjin, Shijiazhuang, Tangshan, Zhangjiakou, Xuanhua, Baoding, Cangxian, Hejian, Anguo, Xinji, Zhuoxian, Handan, and Shanhaiguan, totaling thirteen locations. The East City Office handles remittance points in: Dexian, Cangxian, Xinji, and Bozhen, totaling four locations. The West City Office handles remittance points in Wangdu, Yixian, Huailai, Huolu, Wanxian, Baoding, Zhuoxian, Zhangjiakou, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, and Tianjin, totaling eleven locations. The South City Office currently handles remittance points in Dingxian and Gaoyang, totaling two locations. (Yu)

### 人民银行北平分行　开辟通汇点廿二处
宇
1949-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行北平分行
  　开辟通汇点廿二处
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行为调节华北内地及华北与东北等地区金融，便利各解放区间相互贸易，在一个半月期间，先后开辟了通汇点二十二处。平津间并于四月一日开始采用电汇办法。该行汇兑业务迅速开展，对组织城乡物资交流俾益甚大。根据最近情况，人民银行北平经办的通汇点有：天津、石家庄、唐山、张家口、宣化、保定、沧县、河间、安国、辛集、涿县、邯郸、山海关等十三处。东城办事处经办通汇点：德县、沧县、辛集、泊镇等四处。西城办事处经办通汇点有望都、易县、怀来、获鹿、完县、保定、涿县、张家口、唐山、石家庄、天津等十一处。南城办事处目前经办通汇点有定县、高阳两处。  （宇）
