Title: Atomic Bomb Is Nothing Special! Piglet in Bikini Test Still Safe and Sound
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 3

　　Atomic Bomb Is Nothing Special!
　　Bikini Test Piglet Still Safe and Sound
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 9th] United Press message: Piglet No. 311, which was tested in the atomic bomb test on Bikini Atoll in the Pacific in 1946, was recently examined by the U.S. Naval Laboratory and found to be completely normal and healthy. "Its well-being has greatly surprised atomic bomb experts, as it was expected that no animal close to the center of the atomic bomb explosion would survive."

### 原子弹不过如此！　比基尼试验中猪仔迄今无恙

1949-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　原子弹不过如此！
  　比基尼试验中猪仔迄今无恙
    【新华社北平九日电】合众社消息：一九四六年在太平洋比基尼岛屿原子弹试验中受试的第三一一号猪仔，近经美海军实验室检查后，认为完全正常健全。“其安然无恙，使原子弹专家亦大感惊奇。因当时预料在贴近原子弹爆炸中心的动物，绝无幸存者。”
