Title: Exchange Rate Outside the District: One to One Hundred and Forty
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate Outside the District
  　One to One Hundred and Forty
    The Bank of China in Ping City today announced the exchange rate between Beiping and Shanghai for outside the district as one to one hundred and forty.

### 区外汇兑一比一四零

1949-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑
  　一比一四零
