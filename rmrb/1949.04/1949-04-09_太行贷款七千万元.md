Title: Taihang Loans Seventy Million Yuan
Author:
Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 2

Taihang Loans Seventy Million Yuan
    [Taihang Telegram] The Taihang Administrative Office has decided to issue agricultural loans of seventy million yuan (People's Currency), instructing that they must be used for agricultural production.

### 太行贷款七千万元

1949-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　太行贷款七千万元
    【太行电】太行行政公署决定发放农业贷款七千万元（人民券），指示必须用于农业生产。
