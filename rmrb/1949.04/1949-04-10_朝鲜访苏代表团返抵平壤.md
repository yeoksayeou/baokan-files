Title: Korean Delegation to the Soviet Union Returns to Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 3

　　Korean Delegation to the Soviet Union
  　Returns to Pyongyang
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: Pyongyang Radio broadcast: The delegation of the Government of the Korean People's Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, returned to Pyongyang at 9:00 PM on the 7th. The delegation was welcomed at the airport by representatives of the people.

### 朝鲜访苏代表团返抵平壤

1949-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜访苏代表团
  　返抵平壤
    【新华社北平九日电】平壤电台广播：以金日成总理为首的朝鲜人民民主共和国政府访苏代表团一行，已于七日下午九时返抵平壤。代表团在机场受到广大人民代表的欢迎。
