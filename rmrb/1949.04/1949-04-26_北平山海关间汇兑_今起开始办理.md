Title: Remittances Between Beiping and Shanhaiguan to Begin Today
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 2

　　Remittances Between Beiping and Shanhaiguan to Begin Today
  　[Reported by this newspaper] Remittances between Beiping and Shanhaiguan will begin today (the 26th). The People's Bank of Beiping Branch will accept remittances to Shanhaiguan. The exchange rate is 1/1000, and there is no limit to the amount of each remittance.
(Yu)

### 北平山海关间汇兑  今起开始办理
宇
1949-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平山海关间汇兑
  　今起开始办理
    【本报讯】北平与山海关间汇兑，今（二十六）日起开始。凡汇往山海关之款项，人民银行北平分行即可接受办理。汇率为千分之十，每次汇款额不限。    （宇）
