Title: Korean People's Assembly Approves Soviet-Korean Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, reported to the Third Supreme People's Assembly on the results of the Korean government delegation's visit to the Soviet Union on the 24th, namely the conclusion of the economic and cultural cooperation agreement between Korea and the Soviet Union. During the discussion, the representatives unanimously praised the cooperation agreement and emphasized the selfless nature of the Soviet loan. The Assembly unanimously approved the work of the government delegation in the Soviet Union.

### 朝鲜人民议会批准苏鲜协定

1949-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民议会批准苏鲜协定
    【新华社北平二十六日电】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜人民民主共和国总理金日成二十四日在第三届最高人民议会报告朝鲜政府代表团访苏的成果，即缔结了朝鲜与苏联间经济文化合作协定。在讨论中代表们发言一致赞扬这个合作协定，并着重指出苏联贷款的大公无私的性质。议会一致批准政府代表团在苏联的工作。
