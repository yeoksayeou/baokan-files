Title: Tearing Up Yan Xishan's "Portrait"
Author:
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 4

　　Tearing Up Yan Xishan's "Portrait"
　　　　The east of Taiyuan city is mountains,
　　　　West of the Fen River, still mountains;
　　　　Taiyuan city is like a pot bottom,
　　　　Lonely placed on the Fen River beach.
　　　　Yan Xishan, himself says:
　　　　"Taiyuan's situation is like a person,
　　　　The east mountain is like Taiyuan's head,
　　　　The hands are the north and south airports;
　　　　Shizuizi and Fengge Ridge,
　　　　Are like eyes, high and bright,
　　　　Two feet stretched out in the west of Fen River,
　　　　The inside of Taiyuan city is like the five internal organs."
　　　　The image painted by the bandit Yan himself,
　　　　Has been torn up more than half,
　　　　Both hands have been completely severed,
　　　　Both arms are injured,
　　　　Both eyes have been gouged out long ago,
　　　　The head has left the shoulders.
　　　　Artillery is架 above the head,
　　　　Shells hit the internal organs,
　　　　The Yan bandits are hungry and panicking,
　　　　The belly is gaping open, the five internal organs are finished!
　　　　　　(Reprinted from "People's Soldiers")

### 扯破阎锡山“画像”

1949-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　扯破阎锡山“画像”
    太原城东是大山，
    汾河往西还是山；
    太原城好像个锅底，
    孤孤摆在汾河滩。
    阎锡山、自已讲：
    “太原形势像人样，
    东山好比太原头，
    手是南北飞机场；
    石咀子和凤阁梁，
    好比眼睛高又亮，
    两脚伸在汾河西，
    太原市内像五脏”。
    阎贼自己画的像，
    已被扯破多半张，
    两手干脆被断，
    两条胳膊受伤，
    两只眼睛早剜掉，
    脑袋已离开肩膀。
    大炮架在头顶上，
    炮弹打到内五脏，
    阎匪饥饿又恐慌，
    肚皮瞠开五脏完！
         （转载“人民子弟兵”）
