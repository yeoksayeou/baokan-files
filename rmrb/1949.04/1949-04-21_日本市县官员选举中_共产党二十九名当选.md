Title: In Japanese Municipal and Prefectural Official Elections, 29 Communists Elected
Author:
Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 3

　　Japanese Municipal and Prefectural Official Elections
  　29 Communists Elected
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 20th】United Press, Tokyo - The Japanese Communist Party's Akahata newspaper reported that in the recent municipal and prefectural official elections, 29 of the 133 candidates from the Japanese Communist Party were elected. The newspaper also stated that this result shows that the strength of the Japanese Communist Party has increased since the general election in January.

### 日本市县官员选举中　共产党二十九名当选

1949-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本市县官员选举中
  　共产党二十九名当选
    【新华社北平二十日电】合众社东京讯，日共赤旗报称：在最近市、县官员选举中，日本共产党一百三十三名候选人中有二十九名当选。该报又称，这一结果显示：日共力量自一月份大选以来，又有增加。
