Title: Ping City Labor Groups Invited to Discuss Preparations for Commemorating "May 1st" and "May 4th"
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 1

　　【Our Newspaper News】The Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of "May 1st" and "May 4th" in Beiping City and the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping City Federation of Trade Unions, in order to actively prepare for the commemoration of "May 1st" and "May 4th," specially invited thirty-two trade union groups and units, including the Preparatory Committee of the Pingjin Railway Workers' General Union, the Mentougou Workers' General Union, and the Telecommunications Bureau Workers' Union, yesterday (the 22nd) to discuss relevant preparatory work, and to study the specific work of the "May 1st" parade and the arrangement of the venue.

### 平市职工团体应邀商讨　筹备纪念“五一”“五四”

1949-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市职工团体应邀商讨
  　筹备纪念“五一”“五四”
    【本报讯】北平市纪念“五一”“五四”筹备会与北平市职工总会筹委会为积极筹备纪念“五一”“五四”，特于昨（二十二）日邀请本市平津铁路职工总会筹委会、门头沟职工总会、电信局职工会等三十二个职工团体单位，商讨有关筹备工作，并研讨“五一”游行及布置会场等各项具体工作。
