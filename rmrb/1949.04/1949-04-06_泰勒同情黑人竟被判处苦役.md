Title: Taylor Sentenced to Hard Labor for Sympathizing with Blacks
Author:
Date: 1949-04-06
Page: 3

　　Taylor Sentenced to Hard Labor for Sympathizing with Blacks
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 4th: TASS, April 2nd: Taylor, the Progressive Party's vice-presidential candidate in the last election and current Senator, has been sentenced to hard labor and fined for opposing anti-black segregation laws. He was previously convicted of a "crime" for deliberately entering a venue through an entrance reserved for blacks in May of last year, and subsequently appealed. On March 31st, the Alabama Court of First Instance again sentenced Taylor to 180 days of hard labor and a fine of fifty dollars. Taylor has posted a two-hundred-dollar bail pending a further appeal.

### 泰勒同情黑人竟被判处苦役

1949-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　泰勒同情黑人竟被判处苦役
    【新华社北平四日电】塔斯社二日讯：上次大选中进步党副总统候选人，现任参议员泰勒。因反对反黑人的隔离法而被判处苦役和罚金。他曾因去年五月故意经由只限黑人使用的入口处进入一个会场而被判“罪”，后不服上诉。上月三十一日，阿拉巴马州第一审法院再次判处泰勒以一百八十天的苦役，并课以五十元的罚金。泰勒业以二百元保证金保释，以待再提起上诉。
