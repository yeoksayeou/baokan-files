Title: Philippine People's Anti-Japanese Army Attacks Near Manila
Author:
Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Manila on the 9th, the Philippine People's Anti-Japanese Army attacked Hibuspulin in Bulacan Province on the 7th. This place is the most famous waterfall town in Luzon, only dozens of miles north of Manila. News revealed that the defending troops did not dare to fight and abandoned the town to flee. According to previous reports from Reuters, the People's Anti-Japanese Army launched extensive attacks in the neighboring province of Nueva Ecija at the end of March.

### 马尼拉附近菲民抗军出击

1949-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　马尼拉附近
  　菲民抗军出击
    【新华社北平十日电】据路透社马尼拉九日讯，菲律宾民抗军曾于七日袭入布拉干省的希布斯普林，该地为吕宋最著名的瀑布镇，在马尼拉以北仅数十哩。消息宣露：守军不敢应战，弃镇逃跑。据路透社前此报道，民抗军于三月末在布拉干的邻省蕊伊瓦西哈广泛出击。
