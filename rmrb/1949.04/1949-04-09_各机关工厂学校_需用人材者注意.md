　　Attention All Institutions, Factories, and Schools Needing Personnel

　　[Ping City News] The Ping City People's Government Civil Affairs Bureau Social Services Department has released the following statistics for the number of unemployed persons registered in March:

　　Mechanical workers: 182, Ordnance factory workers: 164, Auto mechanics and drivers: 568, Railway employees: 1,657, Tram workers: 18, Printing workers: 316, Printing type casters: 55, Sewing workers: 757, Textile workers: 172, Telecommunications workers: 229, Electrical machinery workers: 59, Electricians: 104, Civil engineers: 151, Leather makers: 13, Chemical industry workers: 37, Agricultural technicians: 28, Paper makers: 6, Shoe makers: 43, Childcare workers: 30, Draftsmen: 58, Optical instrument workers: 1, Aeronautical engineers: 6, Common laborers (independent producers): 116, Cultural and educational workers: 105, Other: 64, Cooks: 60, Civil servants: 249, Medical staff: 37, Typists: 115, Shipping workers: 1, Accountants: 42, Cooperative workers: 2, Waterworks workers: 27, Mining and metallurgy workers: 18, Wine makers: 3, Ironworkers: 231, Railway workers (handled by the Changxindian Labor Union): 502, Total: 6,226. Any institutions, schools, factories, or individuals wishing to employ personnel from the above list may contact Comrade Zheng Bin at the Social Services Department for assistance.
