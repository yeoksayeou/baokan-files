Title: He Xiangning Hosts Women's Federation Executive Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 1
　　
　　He Xiangning Hosts Women's Federation Executive Committee Members
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] Ms. He Xiangning, Honorary Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, hosted a reception for the executive committee members of the federation at her residence at 10:00 AM yesterday (the 15th). More than 30 people attended, including Qu Mengjue, Kang Keqing, Zhang Qinqiu, Lei Jieqiong, and Liu Wang Liming.

### 何香凝招待妇联执委

1949-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　何香凝招待妇联执委
    【本报讯】全国民主妇联名誉主席廖夫人何香凝先生，于昨日（十五日）上午十时，在她的寓所招待该会执行委员，计到区梦觉、康克清、张琴秋、雷洁琼、刘王立明等三十余人。
