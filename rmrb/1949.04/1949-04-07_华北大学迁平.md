Title: North China University Moves to Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 6th: North China University, originally located in Zhengding, has decided to move to Beiping. The university headquarters arrived a few days ago, and 5,000 new students recruited in the Beiping and Tianjin areas have already enrolled. After the university moves to Beiping, some students will remain in Zhengding to continue their studies.

### 华北大学迁平

1949-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北大学迁平
    【新华社北平六日电】原设正定之华北大学决定迁平，校部已于日前抵达，该校在平津招收之新生亦已有五千人入学。该校迁平后，部分学生仍将留正定学习。
