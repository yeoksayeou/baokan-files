Title: Vietnamese People's Guerrillas Bravely Attack the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 3

　　Vietnamese People's Guerrillas
  　Heroically Attack the Enemy
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th] According to Agence France-Presse, Saigon: In the past forty-eight hours, the Vietnamese People's Guerrillas have carried out several brave attacks near Kapuskagang (Southeast of Saigon). It is claimed that the guerrillas have seized firearms and ammunition from a French military stronghold in the suburbs.

### 越人民游击队英勇袭敌

1949-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　越人民游击队
  　英勇袭敌
    【新华社北平十二日电】据法新社西贡讯：在过去四十八小时里，越南人民游击队在卡普斯加格（西贡东南）附近进行数次勇敢的袭击。据称：在城郊一法军据点的枪支和弹药已被游击队夺去。
