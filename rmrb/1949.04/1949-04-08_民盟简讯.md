Title: China Democratic League Briefs
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 1

　　China Democratic League Briefs
　　The China Democratic League's Peiping (Beiping) City branch began a general registration of League members last week and plans to convene a Peiping City League members' conference within this month to elect the League's city executive committee.
　　Liang Shuming, former Standing Committee member of the China Democratic League, has withdrawn from the League. Liang declared his withdrawal from the League in October 1947, and made another statement of withdrawal in the newspaper in January of this year.
　　Zhou Xinmin, a member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, brought three large boxes of Western medicine when he came to Peiping, representing the League headquarters' donation to the People's Liberation Army.

### 民盟简讯

1949-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟简讯
    民盟北平市支部，上周开始进行盟员总登记，计划于本月内召开平市盟员大会，选举该盟市执委会。
    前民盟中常委梁漱溟已脱离民盟。梁曾于一九四七年十月声明退盟，今年一月又在报上再作退盟声明。
    民盟中委周新民来平时，携来西药三大箱，代表该盟总部赠送人民解放军。
