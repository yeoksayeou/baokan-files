Title: Joint Statement by Chinese Democratic Parties Opposing the North Atlantic Treaty

Author:

Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 3rd: The Chinese democratic parties issued a joint statement on the 3rd opposing the North Atlantic Treaty. The full text is as follows: The North Atlantic Treaty, to be signed on April 4th by the governments of the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, France, Norway, Denmark, Iceland, the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, Portugal, and Italy, is a treaty that aims to incite a new world war of aggression and endangers human peace and security. Considering that world peace is indivisible, the safety and security of the peoples of the East and the West are interdependent, and that the governments of the United States and the United Kingdom are in fact striving to expand the model of the North Atlantic Treaty to the Pacific region, the Chinese democratic parties hereby express their resolute opposition to the North Atlantic Treaty and any other similar aggressive schemes.

The Chinese democratic parties condemn the aggressive policies of the United States imperialist government and its vassals, which betray the Potsdam Agreement and other agreements guaranteeing international peace, betray the United Nations Organization, and betray the peaceful aspirations of the people of all countries. The Chinese democratic parties express warm sympathy and loyal support for the struggle of the world's peace and democracy forces, led by the Soviet Union, against the danger of a new war. The Chinese democratic parties firmly believe that this struggle will subject the imperialists who instigate war and their running dogs in various countries to the condemnation of the peace-loving people of the world and isolate them, and will frustrate the conspiracy of a new world war of aggression. We believe that the world belongs to the people, not to the imperialists and their running dogs. The rampant and unbridled behavior of the imperialists and their running dogs is only a temporary phenomenon; all their conspiracies and tricks, along with themselves, will be exposed and abandoned by the people.

The Chinese democratic parties, while representing the great Chinese people, solemnly declare: If the imperialist aggressor bloc dares to provoke this reactionary war that endangers the people of the whole world, then we will unite the people of the whole country, abide by Dr. Sun Yat-sen's immortal will, adopt necessary methods, and join hands with China's ally, the Soviet Union, and the peace and democracy forces of all countries, to resolutely fight against the initiators of the war of aggression, defeat the aggressors, overthrow the entire imperialist system, and realize the liberation of all mankind and lasting peace. We believe that if war breaks out, the imperialist aggressor countries will certainly be the losers, and the anti-imperialist, invaded countries will certainly be the victors. This prospect has already been pointed out to the people of the world by the experience of World War II.

### 中国各民主党派联合声明反对北大西洋公约

1949-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国各民主党派联合声明反对北大西洋公约
    【新华社北平三日电】中国各民主党派于三日发表反对北大西洋公约的联合声明全文如下：美、加、英、法、挪、丹、冰、荷、比、卢、葡、意等国政府将于四月四日签订的北大西洋公约，是一个以挑动新的世界侵略战争为目的的危害人类和平安全的条约。鉴于世界和平不可分割，东西各民族人民安危与共，并且事实上美英等国政府也正在力图将北大西洋公约的模型扩张到太平洋方面来，中国各民主党派，特对北大西洋公约及其他任何类似的侵略方案，表示坚决的反对。
    中国各民主党派谴责美国帝国主义政府及其附庸背叛波茨坦协定和其他保障国际和平的协定、背叛联合国组织、背叛各国人民和平愿望的侵略政策。中国各民主党派对于以苏联为首的世界和平民主势力反对新战争危险的奋斗，表示热烈的同情和忠诚的拥护。中国各民主党派坚信，这种奋斗将使策动战争的帝国主义者及其在各国的走狗们受到全世界和平人民的责备而陷于孤立，将使新的世界侵略战争的阴谋陷于破产。我们认为世界是人民的，不是帝国主义者及其走狗们的。帝国主义者及其走狗们的横行与猖獗，不过是暂时的现象，他们的一切阴谋诡计，连同他们本身在内，均将被人民所揭露，所抛弃。
    中国各民主党派同时代表伟大的中国人民郑重宣告：如果帝国主义侵略集团竞敢挑动这个危害全世界人民的反动的战争，那么我们将团结全国人民，遵守孙中山先生的不朽遗嘱，采用必要的方法，与中国的盟友苏联和各国和平民主势力，携手并进，向侵略战争的发动者作坚决的斗争，打败侵略者，推翻整个帝国主义制度，实现全人类的解放和永久的和平。我们认为，如果战争爆发，帝国主义的诸侵略国一定是失败者，反帝国主义的诸被侵略国一定是胜利者。这个前途，已经被第二次世界大战的经验向全世界人民指明出来了。
