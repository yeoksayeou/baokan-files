Title: Mudanjiang Workers Repair "Youth" Locomotive
Author:
Date: 1949-04-22
Page: 3
　
　　Mudanjiang Workers
  　Repair "Youth" Locomotive
    [Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 19th] Workers at the Mudanjiang Locomotive Depot of the Harbin Railway Administration repaired a locomotive, named it "Youth," and dedicated it to the First National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. The locomotive had been abandoned for four years and was severely damaged. This time, the workers of the Mudanjiang Locomotive Depot used their spare time to repair it in a rush, and it took a month to completely repair it. The traction force reaches 1,325 tons, making it one of the best quality locomotives in the section. Currently, the locomotive is operated by three young drivers and six Youth League members, who are working hard to create new records in operation.

### 牡丹江工人　修复“青年号”机车

1949-04-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　牡丹江工人
  　修复“青年号”机车
    【新华社哈尔滨十九日电】哈尔滨铁路管理局牡丹江机务段工人修复机车一台，命名为“青年号”，献给中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会。该机车原已弃置四年，破损很大，此次经牡丹江机务段工友利用工作余暇突击修理，历时一个月乃完全修复。牵引力达一千三百二十五吨，成为该段机车中质量最好者之一。现包乘该机车的为三位青年司机和六位青年团员，他们正积极工作，准备在运转上创造新纪录。
