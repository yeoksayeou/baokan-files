Title: Kuomintang Bandit Gang Massacres Political Prisoners
Author:
Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 1
　　
　　Kuomintang Bandit Gang Massacres Political Prisoners
    [North Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, 11th] According to reliable sources in Shanghai published in Hong Kong newspapers: Chen Zitao, former chief editor of the Shanghai "Wen Cui" weekly, and Luo Hemin, former reporter of the Guoxin News Agency and chief editor of the Hengyang Kaiming Daily, were secretly strangled to death in Nanjing prison by the Kuomintang reactionaries at 10 PM on December 27th of last year, and were buried together at Yuhuatai. Another two groups of political prisoners were also killed one after the other, one group of seventy-two people, and another group of twelve people, all of whom were buried alive. Among them, one woman and her child both perished. Prior to this, Xinhua News Agency reported that after Li Zongren ordered the release of political prisoners, the Kuomintang reactionaries' crimes of massacring political prisoners were continuing in Shanghai, Xi'an and other places.

### 国民党匪帮惨杀政治犯

1949-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党匪帮惨杀政治犯
    【北平新华广播电台十一日广播】据香港报纸刊载上海可靠消息：前上海“文萃”周刊主编陈子涛和前国新社记者衡阳开明日报总编辑骆何民在去年十二月二十七日夜十时，在南京监狱中被国民党反动派秘密绞杀，一齐被埋在雨花台。另有两批政治犯，也都先后被杀害，一批七十二名，另一批十二名，都是被活埋的。其中有一女姓和她的孩子都一同遇难。前此新华社曾报导：在李宗仁命令释放政治犯以后，国民党反动派惨杀政治犯的罪行，在上海、西安及其它各地尚在继续。
