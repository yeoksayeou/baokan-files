Title: Opposing the Kuomintang's Creation of Famine: Teacher and Student Strike in Yulin Schools
Author:
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 2nd: According to delayed news from Yulin: More than 700 students and faculty members from schools such as Yulin Middle School, Yulin Vocational School, and Yulin Normal School held a three-day strike from the 13th to the 16th of last month to oppose the severe famine caused by the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang. The Yulin Kuomintang reactionary authorities dispatched a large number of military police and special agents to monitor and guard the school gates and streets, and issued a notice prohibiting people from gathering.

### 反对国民党制造饥饿　榆林各学校罢教罢课

1949-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　反对国民党制造饥饿　榆林各学校罢教罢课
    【新华社延安二日电】据榆林迟到消息：榆林中学、榆林职业中学和榆林师范学校等校学生和教职员七百余人，为反对国民党反动统治所造成的严重饥饿状态，曾于上月十三日到十六日举行罢课罢教三天。榆林国民党反动当局出动大批军警特务在校门及各街道监视警戒，并布告禁止人民集会。
