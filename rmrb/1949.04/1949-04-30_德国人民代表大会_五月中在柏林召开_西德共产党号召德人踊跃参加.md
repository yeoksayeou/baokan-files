Title: German People's Congress to be Held in Berlin in Mid-May; West German Communist Party Calls on Germans to Actively Participate
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 3

　　German People's Congress
  　To be Held in Berlin in Mid-May
    West German Communist Party Calls on Germans to Actively Participate
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th] Detchutsche News Agency reports: The Central Committee of the West German Communist Party issued a statement calling on the German people to fight against the establishment of a separate West German state and the "Occupation Statute." The statement emphasized that all those who cherish the survival and welfare of the German people should send representatives to the German People's Congress to be held in Berlin on May 15th and 16th. The statement condemned the leaders of the Christian Democratic Party, the Christian Social Union, and the German Social Democratic Party for accepting the "Occupation Statute" and the decision to establish a separate West German state.

### 德国人民代表大会　五月中在柏林召开  西德共产党号召德人踊跃参加

1949-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　德国人民代表大会
  　五月中在柏林召开
    西德共产党号召德人踊跃参加
    【新华社北平二十九日电】德意志通讯社消息：西德共产党中央委员会发表文告，号召德国人民为反对建立西德分立国家及“占领法规”而斗争。文告强调一切珍惜德国人民的生存和福利的人，都应派遣代表出席定于五月十五、十六两日在柏林举行的德国人民代表大会。文告谴责基督教民主党，基督教社会同盟和德国社会民主党的领袖接受“占领法规”和西德分立国家的决定。
