Title: Tianjin Establishes Securities Exchange
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Establishes
  　Securities Exchange
    【Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 28th】The Tianjin Municipal People's Bank, in order to effectively manage the financial market and guide floating capital into legitimate channels, has decided to establish the Tianjin Securities Exchange. The original Tianjin Securities Exchange has been notified by the Military Control Commission's Financial Takeover Office to cease operations and liquidate. Government shares will be taken over by the office, and private shares will be returned to their original owners.

### 天津成立　证券交易所

1949-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津成立
  　证券交易所
    【新华社天津二十八日电】津市人民银行为有效地管理金融市场，引导游资用于正当途径，顷决定成立天津市证券交易所。原天津证券交易所，已由军管会金融接管处通知其结束清理。其中官股由该处接管，私股发还原主。
