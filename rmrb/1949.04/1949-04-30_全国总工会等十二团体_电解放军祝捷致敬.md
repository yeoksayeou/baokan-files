Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other twelve organizations send a telegram to the People's Liberation Army to congratulate them on their victory and pay their respects
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 28th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other twelve people's organizations recently jointly sent a telegram to congratulate the great victory of the liberation of Nanjing. The original telegram is as follows: Generals Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, and Zhang Jichun of the Second Field Army, Generals Chen Yi, Rao Shushi, Su Yu, and Tan Zhenlin of the Third Field Army, Generals Lin Biao and Luo Ronghuan of the Fourth Field Army, and to all commanders and soldiers of the Yangtze River Front: Under the wise strategic guidance of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the People's Liberation Army crossed the Yangtze River and, with an overwhelming force, liberated Nanjing, the center of the Kuomintang's counter-revolutionary rule for twenty-two years, and many famous cities and important towns along the Yangtze River within three days. The Kuomintang's counter-revolutionary rule has been declared dead from this point forward, and the Chinese people's democratic revolution will soon achieve complete victory. As the good news comes, the people are rejoicing. We would like to extend our congratulations and respects to the heroic People's Liberation Army, and call on compatriots from all walks of life across the country to unite as one, actively support this victorious advance, quickly, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely eliminate the remaining forces of the Kuomintang bandits, and win the final victory of liberating all of China! All-China Federation of Trade Unions, China Liberated Areas Youth Federation, All-China Students' Federation, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, China Association of Scientific Workers, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, North China Literary and Art Circles Association, China Association of Academic Workers, China Liberated Areas Journalists Association, China Youth Journalists Society, Shanghai People's Organizations Joint Committee, Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff Union, April 27th.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 28th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other twelve people's organizations jointly sent a telegram to congratulate the great victory of Taiyuan. The original telegram is as follows: Generals Xu Xiangqian, Zhou Shidi, and Luo Ruiqing, and to all commanders and soldiers of the Taiyuan Front:
　　The liberation of Taiyuan finally ended the nearly forty years of criminal rule in Shanxi by the war criminal Yan Xishan, who dared to resist stubbornly, and declared the complete liberation of North China. We are overjoyed by this great victory, and we would like to extend our congratulations and respects to the heroic and skilled People's Liberation Army on the Taiyuan Front, and call on compatriots from all walks of life across the country to unite as one, actively support the front, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, quickly, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely eliminate all the remaining forces of the Kuomintang bandit army who dare to resist stubbornly, and advance towards the final liberation of all of China!
　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions, China Liberated Areas Youth Federation, All-China Students' Federation, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, China Association of Scientific Workers, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, North China Literary and Art Circles Association, China Association of Academic Workers, China Liberated Areas Journalists Association, China Youth Journalists Society, Shanghai People's Organizations Joint Committee, Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff Union, April 27th.

### 全国总工会等十二团体　电解放军祝捷致敬

1949-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会等十二团体
  　电解放军祝捷致敬
    【新华社北平二十八日电】中华全国总工会等十二个人民团体顷联名致电祝贺解放南京大捷，原电如下：第二野战军刘伯承、邓小平、张际春将军，第三野战军陈毅、饶漱石、粟裕、谭震林将军，第四野战军林彪、罗荣桓将军，并转长江前线全体指战员同志：人民解放军在毛主席、朱总司令英明战略指导之下，横渡长江，以排山倒海之势在三天内就解放了国民党二十二年来反革命统治中心南京和长江沿岸的许多名城重镇，国民党反革命统治从此宣告灭亡，中国人民民主革命迅将取得完全胜利。捷报传来，万众欢腾。我们谨向英勇的人民解放军祝捷致敬，并号召全国各界同胞团结一致，积极支援这一胜利的进军，迅速、彻底、干净、全部地消灭国民党匪帮的残余势力，赢得解放全中国的最后胜利！、中华全国总工会、中国解放区青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国科学工作者协会、中华全国文艺协会、华北文艺界协会、中国学术工作者协会、中国解放区新闻记者联合会、中国青年记者学会、上海人民团体联合会、北平市中小学教职员联合会筹委会、四月二十七日
    【新华社北平二十八日电】中华全国总工会等十二个人民团体联名电贺太原大捷。原电如下：徐向前、周士弟、罗瑞卿诸将军并转入原前线全体指战员同志：
    太原的解放，最后结束了敢于顽抗的战犯阎匪锡山在山西近四十年的罪恶统治，使华北由此宣告完全解放。我们为这一伟大的胜利欢欣鼓舞，谨向英勇善战的太原前线人民解放军祝捷致敬，并号召全国各界同胞，团结一致，积极支援前线，在毛主席、朱总司令领导之下，迅速、彻底、干净、全部地消灭国民党匪军一切敢于顽抗的残余势力，为最后解放全中国胜利前进！
    中华全国总工会、中国解放区青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国科学工作者协会、中华全国文艺协会、华北文艺界协会、中国学术工作者协会、中国解放区新闻记者联合会、中国青年记者学会、上海人民团体联合会、北平市中小学教职员联合会筹委会　　　　　四月二十七日
