Title: National Shanxi University Decides to Move and Merge on May 3rd
Author: Liu Ye
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 2

　　National Shanxi University Decides to Move and Merge on May 3rd
    [Our Dispatch] With the liberation of Taiyuan, the Education Department of the North China Government has specifically decided that the National Shanxi University, which remained in Beiping, will move back to Taiyuan on May 3rd. Yesterday (the 28th), an advance team composed of personnel from the Education Department and teachers and students of the university has already departed. Shanxi University is currently suspending classes and actively preparing for the relocation. (Liu Ye)

### 国立山西大学决于五三迁并
刘野
1949-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　国立山西大学决于五三迁并
    【本报讯】随着太原的解放，华北政府教育部特决定留在北平的国立山西大学于五月三日迁回太原。昨（二十八）日由教育部及该校师生组成的先遣小组已起程。现山大停课，积极筹备迁校事宜。　（刘野）
