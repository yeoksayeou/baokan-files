Title: Greek Civil Servant Strike Victory
Author:
Date: 1949-04-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th. According to Agence France-Presse: The Greek civil servant strike, which paralyzed Greek public services, has been victorious. The civil servant union announced on the 14th that it had accepted the settlement conditions proposed by the mediation committee, including an increase of 50 percent in the living allowance for the months of April, May, and June. Civil servants will also receive an extra half-month's Easter bonus.

### 希公务员罢工胜利

1949-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　希公务员罢工胜利
    【新华社北平十五日电】据法新社讯：曾使希腊公用事业陷于麻痹状态的希腊公务员大罢工已获得胜利。公务员职工会于十四日宣布接受调解委员会提出的解决条件，其中有增加四、五、六三个月的生活津贴各百分之五十。公务员并将领到额外半个月的复活节奖金。
