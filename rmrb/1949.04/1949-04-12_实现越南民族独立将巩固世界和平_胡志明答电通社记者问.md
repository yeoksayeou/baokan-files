Title: Achieving Vietnamese National Independence Will Consolidate World Peace - Ho Chi Minh Answers Questions from the Electric News Agency

Author:

Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10th: According to a report from the Electric News Agency in Prague: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam stated on the 7th, in response to questions from an Electric News Agency reporter, that the war in Vietnam could be stopped immediately when France recognizes Vietnam's true unification and true independence. Asked whether the war in Vietnam would threaten world peace, Ho Chi Minh replied, "French colonialism will be a permanent threat to world peace. The realization of Vietnamese national independence will consolidate world peace." Ho Chi Minh declared that the reason for Vietnam's military success is that all Vietnamese people are united in the struggle against French colonialism to achieve the true independence and unification of their homeland.

### 实现越南民族独立将巩固世界和平　胡志明答电通社记者问

1949-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　实现越南民族独立
  　将巩固世界和平
  　胡志明答电通社记者问
    【新华社北平十日电】据电通社布拉格讯：越南民主共和国总统胡志明七日在答电通社记者询问时称：在法国承认越南的真正统一与真正独立时，越南战事便可立即停止。问：越南战事是否会威胁到世界和平？胡志明答称：“法国的殖民主义将成为世界和平的永久威胁。越南民族独立的实现将巩固世界和平。”胡志明宣称：越南在军事方面成功的原因，是越南全体人民都团结在反对法国殖民主义争取其祖国的真正独立与统一的斗争里。
