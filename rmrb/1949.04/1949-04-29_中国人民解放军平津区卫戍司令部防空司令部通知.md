Title: Notice from the Air Defense Command of the Pingjin Area Garrison Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 1

　　Notice from the Air Defense Command of the Pingjin Area Garrison Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    Defense Communication Letter No. 1
    In order to ensure the accuracy of air raid alarm signals, in addition to the alarm signals stipulated in Public Notice No. 3 of the Garrison Command, alarm light ball stations have been set up in the main streets of the city. The air raid alarm signals are now specified as four types:
    A. Preparatory Alarm… … … Hang one red ball (with a light inside the ball at night).
    B. Emergency Alarm… … … Hang two red balls.
    C. Imminent Air Raid Alarm… … … Two red balls lowered.
    D. All Clear Alarm… … … Hang one long green light.
    In addition, after the city issues an air raid alarm signal, all devices that produce sounds similar to the alarm signal (such as train whistles, factory whistles, etc.) must be stopped to avoid misunderstandings.
    This is hereby notified.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Office of the Pingjin Area Air Defense Command

### 中国人民解放军平津区卫戍司令部防空司令部通知

1949-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军平津区卫戍司令部防空司令部通知
    防通字第一号
    为保证空袭警报信号确实起见，除按卫布字第三号布告所规定之警报信号外，特在市内各主要街区设置警报灯球台，现规定空袭警报信号共为四种。
    甲、预备警报………挂红球一个（夜间球内置灯）。
    乙、紧急警报………挂两红球。
    丙、临空警报………两红球降下。
    丁、解除警报………挂长绿灯一个。
    另本市发出防空警报信号后，凡有类似警报信号声音之器具（如火车笛、工厂笛……等）须一律停止使用以免误会。
    特此通知
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　平津区防空司令部办公室
