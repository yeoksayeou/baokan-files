Title: Shandong People's Government Moves Office to Jinan
Author:
Date: 1949-04-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 16th – The Shandong Provincial People's Government moved its office to Jinan City on the 7th of this month. On the evening of the 13th, the Provincial Government held a cadres' meeting at the People's Printing Plant, attended by more than 900 people from the heads of various departments, ministries, banks, and bureaus. Chairman Kang Sheng pointed out in his speech that in urban work, the Shandong Provincial People's Government must wholeheartedly rely on the working class, unite with other working people, win over intellectuals, and strive to win over as many petty bourgeoisie and liberal bourgeoisie elements as possible who can cooperate with us, to jointly build the people's city, develop industry, and strengthen the leadership role of the city over the countryside and industry over agriculture.

### 山东人民政府迁至济南办公

1949-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　山东人民政府迁至济南办公
    【新华社济南十六日电】山东省人民政府已于本月七日迁来济南市办公。十三日晚，省府假人民印刷场召开干部大会，到各厅、部、行、局长以下九百余人。康生主席讲话中指出，山东省人民政府在城市工作中必须全心全意地依靠工人阶级，团结其他劳动群众，争取知识分子，争取尽可能多的能和我们合作的小资产阶级及自由资产阶级分子，以共同建设人民的城市，发展工业，加强城市对乡村，工业对农业的领导作用。
