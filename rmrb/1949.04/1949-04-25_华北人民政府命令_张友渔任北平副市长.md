Title: Order of the North China People's Government: Zhang Youyu Appointed Deputy Mayor of Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 1

　　Order of the North China People's Government
    Zhang Youyu Appointed Deputy Mayor of Beiping
    [Our Newspaper] According to the order of the North China People's Government on the 21st, Deputy Mayor of Beiping Xu Bing has been assigned another post, and the position of Deputy Mayor will be succeeded by Zhang Youyu.

### 华北人民政府命令  张友渔任北平副市长

1949-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府命令
    张友渔任北平副市长
    【本报讯】据华北人民政府二十一日命令，北平市副市长徐冰另有任用，副市长一职由张友渔继任。
