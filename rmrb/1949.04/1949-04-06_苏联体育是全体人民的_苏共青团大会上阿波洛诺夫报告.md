Title: Soviet Sports Belong to All the People: Apolonov's Report at the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Congress
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-06
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: Moscow Dispatch: The 11th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League continued to discuss the report of the Central Committee of the League on April 1st.

　　Apolonov, Chairman of the Sports Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, also participated in the discussion. He said: In the Soviet country, sports are an inseparable part of the communist education of the people, and are a means of making working people healthy and cultivating them to work for the motherland and defend the motherland. In contrast to the sports of capitalist countries, which serve the interests of the ruling group, Soviet sports, from the first day of its existence, have belonged to all the people.

### 苏联体育是全体人民的  苏共青团大会上阿波洛诺夫报告

1949-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联体育是全体人民的
  　苏共青团大会上　阿波洛诺夫报告
    【新华社北平三日电】莫斯科讯：苏联共产主义青年团第十一届代表大会四月一日继续讨论团的中央委员会的报告。
    苏联部长会议体育委员会主席阿波洛诺夫也参加了讨论。他说：在苏维埃国度中，体育是以共产主义培育人民的不可分的一部分，而且是使劳动人民健康和培养他们为祖国劳动和保卫祖国的手段。资本主义国家的体育是为统治集团的利益服务的，与此相反，苏联的体育从它存在的第一天起，就是全体人民的。
