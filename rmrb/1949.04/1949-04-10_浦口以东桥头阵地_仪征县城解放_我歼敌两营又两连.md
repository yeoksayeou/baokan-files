Title: Bridgehead east of Pukou, Yizheng County liberated, We annihilated two enemy battalions and two companies
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, 9th. The People's Liberation Army liberated Yizheng County, an important bridgehead east of Pukou held by the Kuomintang army, and Damatou south of the city on the 7th. On the 8th, Siyuangou, south of the city, was also captured. Two companies of the Fourth Army guarding the city and two battalions of the county security regiment were completely annihilated. More than 300 enemies in Siyuangou were captured.

### 浦口以东桥头阵地　仪征县城解放　我歼敌两营又两连

1949-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　浦口以东桥头阵地
  　仪征县城解放
  　我歼敌两营又两连
    【新华社蚌埠九日电】人民解放军于七日解放浦口以东国民党军重要桥头阵地仪征县城及该城以南的大码头，八日续克该城以南的泗源沟。该城守敌第四军两个连及县保安团两个营全部被歼。泗源沟之敌被俘三百余。
