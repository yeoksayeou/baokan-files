Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Will Be Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 1

　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation Will Be Officially Established
    [This Newspaper's Report] The highest leading organization of Chinese women's mass organizations—the All-China Democratic Women's Federation—is about to be formally established. Yesterday, the delegations of the First National Congress of Chinese Women, based on the election law of the Executive Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation passed by the congress on the first day, discussed the guiding principles and methods of the election, and pre-selected candidates for formal executive committee members and alternate executive committee members. The representatives carefully studied the qualifications and lists of the candidates. By 7:00 PM, seventy-three official executive committee candidates had been approved. The formal election was conducted at 8:00 PM that afternoon.

### 中华全国民主妇联会将正式成立

1949-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国民主妇联会将正式成立
    【本报讯】中国妇女群众组织的最高领导机构——中华全国民主妇女联合会即将正式成立。昨日中国妇女第一次全国代表大会各代表团根据一日大会所通过的中华全国民主妇女联合会执行委员会选举法，分别讨论选举的方针方法，并预选正式执委及候补执委的候选人，代表认真研究候选人的条件、名单。直到下午七时为止，已通过了正式执委候选人七十三名，当天下午八时进行正式选举。
