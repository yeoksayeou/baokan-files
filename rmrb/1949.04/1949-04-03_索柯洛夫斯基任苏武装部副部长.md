Title: Sokolovsky Appointed Deputy Minister of Soviet Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 3

　　Sokolovsky
  　Appointed Deputy Minister of Soviet Armed Forces
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 31st] TASS, Moscow, 29th: The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union recently appointed Marshal of the Soviet Union, Sokolovsky, as First Deputy Minister of the Soviet Armed Forces, and relieved him of his duties as Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Occupation Forces in Germany and Governor-General of the Soviet Military Administration in Germany. The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union appointed General of the Army Chuikov as Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Germany. General Chuikov is a famous hero of the defense of Stalingrad, and served as a Major General Military Attaché at the Soviet Embassy in China during the War of Resistance against Japan.

### 索柯洛夫斯基任苏武装部副部长

1949-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　索柯洛夫斯基
  　任苏武装部副部长
    【新华社北平三十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十九日讯：苏联部长会议顷任命苏联元帅索柯洛夫斯基为苏联武装部队部第一副部长，解除其德境苏联占领军总司令及德境苏联军政府总督之职。苏联部长会议任命陆军上将崔可夫为德境苏军总司令。按崔可夫上将是著名的保卫斯大林格勒的英雄将领，抗战时期曾在苏联驻华大使馆任中将武官。
