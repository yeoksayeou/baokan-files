　　Today's Dictionary
    Ridge—In the fields, crops are planted in rows. Farmers commonly refer to a row as "one ridge."
    Credit Department—Refers to one of the business departments of a production cooperative or supply and marketing cooperative with a certain scale. Its task is to regularly handle all deposit, loan, and other credit matters for its members, in order to seek welfare for the members.

### 今日辞典

1949-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　今日辞典
    垄——田里庄稼，都是一行一行的种植着，农民俗称一行即谓“一垄”。
    信用部—是指具有一定规模的生产合作社与供销合作社的业务部门之一，其任务是经常地为社员办理一切存、放款等信用事宜，俾便为社员谋福利。
