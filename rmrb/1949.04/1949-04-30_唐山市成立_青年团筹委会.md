Title: Tangshan City Establishes Youth League Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 2

　　Tangshan City Establishes
　　Youth League Preparatory Committee
    [Tangshan Electric] The Tangshan New Democratic Youth League City Preparatory Committee has been formed, under the leadership of the Jidong Youth League Preparatory Committee, to uniformly lead the city's League building work. Song Minzhi, Hu Ming and other fifteen people are members of the preparatory committee. The first preparatory meeting was held on the 18th of this month, and the guiding principles and tasks for future League building were determined.

### 唐山市成立　青年团筹委会

1949-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　唐山市成立
  　青年团筹委会
    【唐山电】唐山新民主主义青年团市筹委会已组成，在冀东团筹委会领导下，统一领导全市建团工作。以宋敏之、胡明等十五人为筹备委员。第一次筹备会议于本月十八日召开，确定了今后建团的方针与任务。
