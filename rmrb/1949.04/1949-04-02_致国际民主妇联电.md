Title: Telegram to the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 1

To Mrs. [Eugénie] Cotton and the Women's International Democratic Federation:

　　For the first time in history, 460 representatives of Chinese women gathered in Peiping, the newly liberated ancient cultural capital, to convene the First National Women's Congress on March 25. All of our representatives excitedly and warmly extend our cordial and lofty respects to you!

　　In the past three years, you have actively opposed the instigators of a new world war and defended peace and democracy for the happiness of the world's people and the safety of women and children, gaining the support of women all over the world. You have become a great force in the world's democratic camp.

　　All of us representatives present, representing the women of all of China, hereby guarantee to you: We will resolutely implement the declaration and resolutions of the Second Congress of the Women's International Democratic Federation, support the World Peace Congress to be held in Paris in April, and we will work with the Chinese people to eliminate the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang, which brings disaster to women and children, expel American imperialism from China, complete the nationwide revolutionary victory, seek the realization of true peace, and establish an independent and free people's democratic republic, in order to achieve the complete liberation of Chinese women. At the same time, we will do our best to strengthen the ties of the democratic women's movement in Asian countries, and more actively exert the power of Asian women to strive for the realization of lasting world peace.

　　We believe that Chinese women will continue to receive the loving assistance of the Women's International Democratic Federation in the struggles to come.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The First National Congress of Chinese Women

### 致国际民主妇联电

1949-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　致国际民主妇联电
    戈登夫人及国际民主妇女联合会：
    中国妇女在历史上第一次齐集了四百六十位代表，于三月二十五日在新解放的文化古都北平召开第一次全国妇女代表大会，我们全体代表兴奋热烈地向你们致亲切崇高的敬礼！
    三年来你们为世界人类的幸福，妇女和儿童的安全，积极反对世界新战争的挑拨者，保卫和平与民主，得到了全世界妇女的拥护，你们已成为世界民主阵营中一支伟大的力量。
    我们到会的全体代表，代表全中国的妇女，特向你们保证：我们要坚决执行国际民主妇联第二次代表大会的宣言及决议，拥护四月在巴黎召开的世界拥护和平大会，我们将努力与中国人民一道，消灭使妇女儿童遭受灾难的国民党反动统治，驱逐美帝国主义出中国，完成全国性的革命胜利，求得真正和平的实现，建立独立自由的人民民主共和国，以求得中国妇女的彻底解放。同时当尽我们最大的努力，加强亚洲各国民主妇女运动的联系，更积极发挥亚洲妇女的力量，为实现世界持久和平而奋斗。
    我们相信中国妇女在今后的斗争中，必将继续得到国际民主妇女的友爱的帮助。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　中国妇女第一次全国代表大会
