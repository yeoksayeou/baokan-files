North China Military Region Logistics Department Notice

　　It is hereby notified that from April 29th to May 4th, ammunition and waste products will be destroyed at Hongshankou in the western suburbs of Beiping. It is hoped that all our agencies, troops, and citizens will not be alarmed when they hear the sound. This notice is hereby given.

　　　　　　North China Military Region Logistics Department, April 28th, Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of China
