Title: One Year Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship: Stalin and Dimitrov Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th—According to TASS, Moscow, 9th: On the occasion of the first anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Bulgarian Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance, Bulgarian Prime Minister Dimitrov sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin, thanking the Soviet Union for its assistance in the further political, economic, and cultural development of Bulgaria over the past year, and wishing the further development and consolidation of the friendship and cooperation between the two peoples. In his reply telegram, Stalin wished the Bulgarian people further success.

### 苏保友好条约一周年　斯大林季米特洛夫互电致贺

1949-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏保友好条约一周年
  　斯大林季米特洛夫互电致贺
    【新华社北平十三日电】据塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：值苏保友好互助条约签订一周年之际，保加利亚总理季米特洛夫特电斯大林大元帅，感谢苏联于一年来对保加利亚在政治、经济及文化进一步发展上的帮助，并祝两国人民友谊合作的进一步发展和巩固。斯大林在复电中祝保加利亚人民获得更进一步的成功。
