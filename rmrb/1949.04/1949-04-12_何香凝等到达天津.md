Title: He Xiangning and Others Arrive in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 11th] Democratic personage He Xiangning and five others arrived in Tanggu on the 9th aboard the ship "Atlantic" from Hong Kong, and took a car to Tianjin at 7 pm that day. Lian Yinong, Director of the Municipal Government's Reception Office, and others went to Tanggu to welcome them. He and the others are scheduled to go to Beiping within the next few days.

### 何香凝等到达天津

1949-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　何香凝等到达天津
    【北平新华广播电台十一日消息】民主人士何香凝等六人，从香港乘大西洋号轮船，九日到达塘沽，当天下午七时乘车到天津。市政府交际处长连以农等曾前往塘沽欢迎。何氏等定日内赴平。
