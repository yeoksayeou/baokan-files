Title: Tianjin-Beijing Railway Administration Notice
Author:
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 4

Notice from the Tianjin-Beijing Railway Administration:

　　(1) By order of the Ministry of Railways, this bureau will adjust passenger ticket prices starting April 25th.
　　(2) The passenger ticket base price is now two yuan per person per kilometer, with a minimum chargeable distance of ten kilometers. Distances exceeding ten kilometers will be charged incrementally in units of five kilometers.
　　(3) Any ticket price with a remainder less than five yuan will be rounded up to five yuan.
　　(4) Express train fees are the same for all classes: 100 yuan for 200 kilometers, 200 yuan for 201 to 400 kilometers, and 300 yuan for 401 to 600 kilometers.
　　(5) Platform tickets are ten yuan each.
　　(6) Examples of the new ticket prices are as follows: ① Beijing to Tianjin East Station, ordinary ticket 280 yuan, express ticket 380 yuan ② Beijing to Tangshan, ordinary ticket 530 yuan, express ticket 730 yuan ③ Beijing to Shanhaiguan, ordinary ticket 840 yuan, express ticket 1140 yuan ④ Beijing to Zhangjiakou, ordinary ticket 410 yuan, express ticket 610 yuan ⑤ Beijing to Zhuozhou, ordinary ticket 150 yuan ⑥ Tianjin East Station to Cangzhou, ordinary ticket 260 yuan.
　　(7) Attention is requested from all sectors of society.

### 平津铁路管理局启事

1949-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　平津铁路管理局启事
    （一）本局奉铁道部令自四月二十五日起调整客票票价
    （二）客票基数每人每公里改为二元计费里程起码十公里，超过十公里按每五公里为单位递进计费。
    （三）客票尾数不足五元进作五元。
    （四）快车费不分等级，二百公里收人民券一百元，二○一公里至四○○公里收二百元，四○一公里至六○○公里收三百元。
    （五）月台票每张十元。
    （六）新票价举例如次：①北平至天津东站普通票价二百八十元快车票价三百八十元②北平至唐山普通票价五百三十元快车票价七百三十元③北平至山海关普通票价八百四十元快车票价一千一百四十元④北平至张家口普通票价四百十元快车票价六百十元⑤北平至涿县普通票价一百五十元⑥天津东站至沧县普通票价二百六十元。
    （七）请各界人士注意
