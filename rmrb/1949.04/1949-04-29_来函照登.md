Letters to the Editor

　　Regarding the article "Restrictions on the Use of Civilian Labor During Spring Plowing" published on page one of the People's Daily on the 19th, please correct item four in the first point, "Military Industrial Martyrs' Families," to "Families of Martyrs of Military Industry."

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Office of the North China People's Government
