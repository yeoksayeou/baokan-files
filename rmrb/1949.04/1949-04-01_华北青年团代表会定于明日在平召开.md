Title: North China Youth League Congress Scheduled to Open Tomorrow in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-04-01
Page: 1

　　The North China Youth League Congress Scheduled to Open Tomorrow in Beiping
    [From Our Newspaper] To welcome the national victory, better organize the youth of North China to actively support the war, develop production, and study hard, the First Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League of North China is scheduled to open in Beiping tomorrow (April 2nd). Since March 24th, fifty-nine representatives (including eleven female representatives) from the nine regions of North China, namely Chahar, Suiyuan-Mongolia, Taihang, Taiyue, Central Hebei, Eastern Hebei, Southern Hebei, Hebei-Shandong-Henan, and Taiyuan; fifteen representatives (including four female representatives) from the three cities of Shijiazhuang, Beiping, and Tianjin; fourteen representatives (including one female representative) from directly subordinate organs and schools of the North China Government, including North China University, North China People's Revolutionary University, North China Agricultural College, Jiaotong College, Finance and Economics College, the Engineering College of the Enterprise Department, and Yucai Middle School; and seven people including Lu Ping and Xu Shiping from the Preparatory Committee of the New Youth League of North China, totaling ninety-five people, have arrived in Beiping successively. These representatives of young workers, farmers, and student league members have been exchanging experiences in establishing the League in various regions, and raising various relevant issues in recent days, so as to summarize the experiences of various regions in the congress and determine the future work direction. The congress will also elect the North China Committee of the China New Youth League, and select representatives to attend the National Congress of the China New Youth League. The congress is expected to last four days.

### 华北青年团代表会定于明日在平召开

1949-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北青年团代表会定于明日在平召开
    【本报讯】为迎接全国胜利形势，更好的组织华北青年积极支援战争，发展生产，努力学习，中国新民主主义青年团华北第一届代表大会定于明日（四月二日）在北平召开。自三月二十四日起，华北察哈尔、绥蒙、太行、太岳、冀中、冀东、冀南、冀鲁豫、太原等九地区代表五十九人（内女代表十一人），石家庄、北平、天津三市代表十五人（内女代表四人），华北政府直属机关学校，华北大学、华北人民革命大学、华北农学院、交通学院、财经学院、企业部工学院、育才中学等代表十四人（内女代表一人）及新青团华北筹委会陆平、许世平等七人，以上共九十五人，已先后到达北平。这些青年工人、农民、学生团员的代表，连日来交换各地建团经验，提出各种有关问题，以便在大会中总结各地经验，确定今后工作方针。大会并将选举中国新青团华北委员会，和选派出席中国新青团全国代表大会代表。大会预计将开四天。
