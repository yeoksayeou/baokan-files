Title: Coal Mining
Author: Jing Chun, Chun Jiao
Date: 1949-04-17
Page: 4

　　Coal Mining
　　West Mountain Coal Mine Correspondence Group, Jing Chun, Chun Jiao
　　You swing the hammer, I hold the drill,
　　Clang—clang, clang—clang,
　　Drilling in with every swing,
　　Hold the drill steady, strike the hammer true,
　　Fear not the coal face, hard and tough.
　　Load the dynamite, light the fuse,
　　Boom, bang, crash…
　　The entire tunnel fills with smoke.
　　Braving the smoke, we drill inwards,
　　Basket after basket, loaded into the coal car, (1)
　　Whoosh, clunk, clunk, clunk…
　　Pushed to the underground station. (2)
　　The electric bell rings,
　　The coal cars rumble,
　　Unlatching and latching without delay,
　　Loading the cages is even faster,
　　One shift produces two hundred and forty cars! (3)
　　Note (1) Coal car
　　Note (2) Underground station—underground parking lot
　　Note (3) One shift—eight hours, two hundred and forty cars, equivalent to one hundred and forty-four tons.

### 采煤
景椿　春蛟
1949-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　采煤
    西山煤矿通讯组  景椿  春蛟
    你打锤，我把钎，
    叮——当，叮——当，
    一锤一锤往里钻，
    钎把稳，锤打准，
    那怕煤壁硬又坚。
    装火药，点引线，
    轰，当，哗啦……
    满洞尽是烟。
    冒着烟往里钻，
    一筐一筐背入罐，（一）
    呼，噜，噜，噜，噜……
    推到下把钩。（二）
    当当电铃响，
    镐车滚滚转，
    摘钩挂钩不迟延，
    装罐笼更不慢，
    一班出他二百四十罐！（三）
    注（一）罐——煤车
    注（二）下把钩——井下停车场
    注（三）一班——八小时二百四十罐，合一百四十四吨。
