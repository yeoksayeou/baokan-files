Title: Hebei Electric Power Corporation to Begin Inventory Today

Author:

Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 2

  　Hebei Electric Power Corporation
    To Begin Inventory Today
    [This newspaper report] The Hebei Electric Power Corporation held an inventory meeting yesterday morning (20th), deciding to begin the inventory in groups starting today (21st). Nineteen employees have been selected and divided into four groups: General Affairs, Accounting, Material Warehouse, and Hebei Electric Power Office, to assist in the inventory work. (Ming)

### 冀北电力总公司  今起开始清点

1949-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

  　冀北电力总公司
    今起开始清点
    【本报讯】冀北电力公司总公司昨（二十）日上午召开清点大会，决于今（二十一）日起分组开始清点，并抽出职工十九名，分总务、会计、材料库及冀电处四组，协助清点工作。（鸣）
