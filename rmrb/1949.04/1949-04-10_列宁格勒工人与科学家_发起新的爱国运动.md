Title: Leningrad Workers and Scientists Launch New Patriotic Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 3

　　Leningrad Workers and Scientists
  　Launch New Patriotic Movement
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th] TASS, Moscow: The newspaper published on the 2nd a letter to Stalin passed by the Leningrad City and Leningrad Oblast industrial workers, scientists, and technicians conference. The letter indicates that the Leningrad scientific community has launched a new patriotic movement, striving for close and effective connections between scientists and industrial workers. The conference discussed ways to expedite the implementation of the tasks set by Stalin for the further development of Leningrad's industry.

### 列宁格勒工人与科学家　发起新的爱国运动

1949-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　列宁格勒工人与科学家
  　发起新的爱国运动
    【新华社北平五日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：二日报纸刊登列宁格勒市与列宁格勒州产业工人、科学家与技师会议通过的给斯大林的信，信中表明列宁格勒科学团体已发动一个新的爱国运动，争取科学家与产业工人之间密切的有效的联系。会议讨论了尽速执行斯大林规定的进一步发展列宁格勒工业任务的办法。
