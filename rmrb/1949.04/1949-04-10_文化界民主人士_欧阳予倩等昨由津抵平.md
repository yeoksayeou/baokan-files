Title: Democratic Figures in Cultural Circles Ouyang Yuqian and Others Arrive in Beiping from Tianjin Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 1

　　Democratic Figures in Cultural Circles
  　Ouyang Yuqian and Others Arrive in Beiping from Tianjin Yesterday
    [By Our Newspaper] Democratic figures in cultural circles, playwright Ouyang Yuqian, journalists Sa Kongliao and Jin Zhonghua, and musician Ma Sicong, arrived in Beiping from Tianjin at 4:00 PM on the ninth.

### 文化界民主人士　欧阳予倩等昨由津抵平

1949-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　文化界民主人士
  　欧阳予倩等昨由津抵平
    【本报讯】文化界民主人士，戏剧家欧阳予倩，新闻界萨空了、金仲华及音乐家马思聪等四人于九日下午四时由津抵平。
