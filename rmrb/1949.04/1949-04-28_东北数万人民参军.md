Title: Tens of Thousands of People in Northeast China Join the Army
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 1

　　【Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, 27th broadcast】 Frontline along the Yangtze River reports: The people in the newly liberated areas of Anqing and Wangjiang are actively supporting the People's Liberation Army. In Anqing and Wangjiang counties, more than 400 people transported supplies day and night to the south bank of the Yangtze River in just two days. They all firmly stated that they must support the Liberation Army in liberating all of China.
　　【Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, 27th broadcast】 Beiping News: People in various liberated areas are enthusiastically joining the People's Liberation Army. Recently, tens of thousands of people from the Northeast Military Region have joined the Liberation Army. In the Luzhongnan area, more than 20,000 people from eight local troops have gloriously been promoted into the main force. Now, five new guard regiments have been re-established in the entire area.

### 东北数万人民参军

1949-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北数万人民参军
    【北平新华广播电台二十七日广播】长江前线消息：安庆、望江新解放区人民，积极支援人民解放军。安庆、望江两县在两天当中，就有四百多人昼夜向长江南岸运送物资，他们都坚决表示，一定要支持解放军解放全中国。
    【北平新华广播电台二十七日广播】北平消息：解放区各地人民热烈参加人民解放军。东北军区最近已有数万人参加解放军。鲁中南区有八个地方部队两万多人，已经光荣升入主力军。现在全区已又重新建立五个警卫团。
