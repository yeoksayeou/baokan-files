Title: Puerto Rican Communist Party Statement Opposes US Imperialist War Preparations
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 3

　　Puerto Rican Communist Party Statement
  　Opposes US Imperialist War Preparations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th] The Puerto Rican Communist Party issued a statement opposing the aggression and new war preparations of US imperialism. According to a report from the Electronegative News Agency: The statement from the Puerto Rican Communist Party stated: "The interests of our people are different from the aggressive goals of US imperialism. Our main task at this moment is to wage a struggle against unemployment, exploitation, hunger, poverty, and all the suffering that the United States inflicts on Puerto Rico." The declaration also said: We vow to fight with all our strength. For the sake of peace, independence, and the happiness of future generations in Puerto Rico and elsewhere, we must oppose all imperialist aggression (Note: Puerto Rico is a US territory, seized by the United States from Spain in the Spanish-American War in 1899).

### 波多黎各共产党声明　反对美帝准备战争

1949-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　波多黎各共产党声明
  　反对美帝准备战争
    【新华社北平二十八日电】波多黎各共产党发表声明，反对美国帝国主义的侵略和新战争准备。据电通社消息：波多黎各共产党的声明内称：“我们人民的利益和美国帝国主义的侵略目的不同。此刻我们的主要任务就是进行反对失业、剥削、饥饿、穷困和美国加于波多黎各的种种痛苦的斗争。”宣言又说：我们誓以全力进行斗争。为了波多黎各及其他各处的和平、独立与子子孙孙的幸福，我们要反对一切帝国主义的侵略（按波多黎各为美国领土，系美国在一八九九年美西战争中由西班牙手中夺得）。
