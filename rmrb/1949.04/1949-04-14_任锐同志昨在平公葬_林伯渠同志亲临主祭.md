Title: Comrade Ren Rui Given Public Funeral in Beiping; Comrade Lin Boqu Attended as Chief Mourner
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 1
　　Comrade Ren Rui Given Public Funeral in Beiping

　　Comrade Lin Boqu Attended as Chief Mourner

　　[This newspaper’s report] The Beiping Municipal People's Government held a public funeral yesterday (the 13th) for female revolutionary Comrade Ren Rui at the Wan'an Public Cemetery in Xiangshan. More than 60 people attended the public memorial, including Lin Boqu, Luo Mai, Liu Ren (female), and democratic personage Zhang Bojun. Comrade Lin Boqu officiated as chief mourner. Comrades Luo Mai and Liu Ren reported on Comrade Ren's revolutionary life, and Mr. Zhang Bojun reported on her heroic deeds during the early stages of her participation in the revolution. Comrade Ren Rui passed away in Tianjin on the 10th due to illness, and her remains were moved from Tianjin to Beiping on the 12th.

### 任锐同志昨在平公葬　林伯渠同志亲临主祭

1949-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　任锐同志昨在平公葬
  　林伯渠同志亲临主祭
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府于昨（十三）日在香山万安公墓公葬任锐女革命家，参加公祭的有林伯渠、罗迈、刘仁（女）和民主人士章伯钧等六十余人。林伯渠同志主祭。罗迈、刘仁两同志报告任同志的革命生平，章伯钧先生报告其参加革命初期的英勇事迹。按任锐同志于十日在津因病逝世，遗体于十二日由津移平。
