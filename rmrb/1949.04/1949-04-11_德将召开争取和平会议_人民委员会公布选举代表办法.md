Title: Germany to Convene Conference for Peace; People's Committee Announces Methods for Electing Representatives
Author:
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: TASS, Berlin: The Secretariat of the German People's Committee has announced the methods for electing representatives to the Third German People's Congress for Unity and Justice and Peace. According to these methods, the five states in the Soviet occupation zone and Greater Berlin will elect 1,500 representatives on May 15th and 16th, and the three Western German occupation zones will elect 500 representatives.

### 德将召开争取和平会议　人民委员会公布选举代表办法

1949-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　德将召开争取和平会议
  　人民委员会公布选举代表办法
    【新华社北平九日电】塔斯社柏林讯：德国人民委员会书记处公布了选举出席第三届德国争取团结与正义的和平人民代表大会的办法。按照该办法，苏占区的五个州和大柏林市将在五月十五、十六两日选举一千五百个代表，三个西德占领区选举五百个代表。
