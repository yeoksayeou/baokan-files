Title: Qian Junrui Denounces U.S. Imperialism's Aid to Chiang at Press Conference in Prague
Author:
Date: 1949-04-22
Page: 3

　　At Press Conference in Prague
  　Qian Junrui Denounces U.S. Imperialism's Aid to Chiang
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 20th] According to Agence France-Presse, Prague, 19th: Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation to the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Paris, condemned at a press conference here today that U.S. imperialism's "aid" to Chiang Kai-shek, amounting to six billion yuan, was aimed at making China a U.S. colony. In recalling the victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Qian Junrui expressed that all of China will be liberated in the near future. He said: "In this way, the Chinese people will give a resounding answer to all Marshall Plans and Truman Doctrines." Qian Junrui concluded: "The peace-loving people's camp headed by the Soviet Union is stronger than the imperialist camp, and the strength of imperialism is a paper tiger."

### 在布拉格记者招待会上　钱俊瑞痛斥美帝援蒋

1949-04-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　在布拉格记者招待会上
  　钱俊瑞痛斥美帝援蒋
    【新华社北平二十日电】据法新社布拉格十九日电：巴黎世界拥护和平大会中国代表团秘书长钱俊瑞，今在此地记者招待会上谴责美帝国主义给蒋介石数达六十亿元的“援助”，是要使中国成为美国的殖民地。钱俊瑞在追述中国人民解放军的胜利时，表示全中国将在不久的将来解放。他说：“这样，中国人民就给一切马歇尔计划与杜鲁门主义一个响亮的回答”。钱俊瑞结语称：“以苏联为首的爱好和平人民阵营比帝国主义阵营更为强大，帝国主义的力量是纸老虎”。
