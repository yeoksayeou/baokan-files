Title: Workers at the 70th Ordnance Factory Establish Technical Study Class
Author: Guo Maolin
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 2

　　Workers at the 70th Ordnance Factory
  　Establish Technical Study Class
    [Ping City News] Li Boren, Zhang Deshan, and other workers from the stamping department of the 70th Ordnance Factory, recognizing the importance of technology, voluntarily initiated the establishment of a technical study class. Twenty-four people have already signed up. It is scheduled to have technicians explain subjects such as mathematics, drafting, and mechanical principles after work every afternoon. They not only study themselves but also welcome other workers to sign up and participate. (Guo Maolin)

### 七十兵工厂工人　成立技术研究班
郭茂林
1949-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　七十兵工厂工人
  　成立技术研究班
    【平市讯】七十兵工厂冲压部工友李博仁、张德山等，感于技术的重要，自动发起成立一个技术研究班，报名的已有二十四人，定于每天下午下班后请各技术员讲解数学、制图、机械原理等课目，他们不但自己学习，而且很欢迎其他工友报名参加。    （郭茂林）
