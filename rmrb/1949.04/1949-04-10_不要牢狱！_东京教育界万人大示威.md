Title: "No Prisons!" Massive Demonstration by Tokyo's Education Sector

Author:

Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 3

　　“No Prisons!”
  　Massive Demonstration by Tokyo's Education Sector
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 9th] According to Agence France-Presse, Tokyo, April 8th: Under the auspices of the Japan Teachers Union, ten thousand Japanese people demonstrated on the streets of Tokyo and in front of the Imperial Palace today, protesting the reduction of education funding. Demonstrators held flags and signs with the slogans: We want to build schools, not prisons; Down with the so-called "University Bill" and "Local Civil Service Bill."

### “不要牢狱！”　东京教育界万人大示威

1949-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　“不要牢狱！”
  　东京教育界万人大示威
    【新华社北平九日电】据法新社东京八日讯：在日本教师联合会主持下，今日有一万日人在东京大街上和皇宫门前示威，抗议削减教育经费。示威者手执旗帜与标语牌，写着：我们要建立学校，不要牢狱；打倒所谓“大学法案”及“地方公务员法案”。
