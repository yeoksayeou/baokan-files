Title: Inter-district Exchange Rate Increased Again
Author:
Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 2

　　Inter-district Exchange Rate Increased Again
    [Ping City News] The Bank of China's inter-district exchange rate was re-established today (9th) at one to seventy-five, an increase of 36.5 percent compared to the previous rate.

### 区外汇兑再度提高

1949-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑  再度提高
    【平市讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价今日（九日）重订为一比七十五，较前再提高百分之三十六点五。
