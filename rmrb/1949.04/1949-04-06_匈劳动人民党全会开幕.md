Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Plenary Session Opens
Author:
Date: 1949-04-06
Page: 3

　　Hungarian Working People's Party
  　Plenary Session Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 4th] Budapest dispatch: The plenary session of the leading organ of the Hungarian Working People's Party opened here on April 2nd. General Secretary Rákosi will deliver a political report, and the Chairman of the Hungarian State Planning Office, Vidar, will give a report on the new five-year plan.

### 匈劳动人民党全会开幕

1949-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈劳动人民党
  　全会开幕
    【新华社北平四日电】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党领导机关的全会于四月二日在此间开幕。总书记拉科西将作政治报告，匈牙利国家计划处主席维达尔作关于新五年计划的报告。
