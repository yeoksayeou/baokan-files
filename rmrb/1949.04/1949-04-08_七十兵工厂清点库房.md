Title: Seventieth Arsenal Takes Stock of Warehouse
Author: Guo Maolin
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 2

　　Seventieth Arsenal Takes Stock of Warehouse
    ▲The Seventieth Arsenal has formally established a inventory committee to begin taking stock of the warehouse. Before the inventory, three rules were proposed: (1) Nothing should be given to others privately; (2) No bribes should be accepted; (3) Nothing should be taken for oneself. Every thread and needle belongs to the people. The inventory began on the 6th.
    (Guo Maolin)

### 七十兵工厂清点库房
郭茂林
1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：职工生活

　　七十兵工厂清点库房
    ▲七十兵工厂正式成立清点委员会开始清点库房。清点前提出约法三章：（一）任何东西不私送别人；（二）不受贿；（三）任何东西不私自据为己有。一针一线归人民，六日已开始清点。
    （郭茂林）
