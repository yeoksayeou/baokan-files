Title: North China and Shanghai Sign Postal Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 1

　　North China and Shanghai
  　Sign Postal Agreement
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 28th] After more than ten days of discussion and research, Su Youlu and Cheng Anyu, representatives of the North China Postal and Telecommunications Bureau, and Mei Yifan, Weng Haoying, Shen Xin, and Li Xiong, representatives of the Shanghai Postal Bureau, have formulated provisional measures for postal communication and officially signed an agreement on the 27th.

### 华北与上海　签订通邮协定

1949-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北与上海
  　签订通邮协定
    【新华社北平二十八日电】华北邮电总局代表苏幼麓、成安玉与上海邮政总局代表梅贻璠、翁灏英、沈鑫、李雄等经十余天讨论与研究，已制定通邮暂行办法，于二十七日正式签署协定。
