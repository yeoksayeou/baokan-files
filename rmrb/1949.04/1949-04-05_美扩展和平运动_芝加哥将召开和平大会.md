Title: US Expands Peace Movement: Chicago to Hold Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-04-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 4th: The Polish Press Agency, New York, reports: The National Council of Arts, Sciences and Professions will convene a peace conference in Chicago. Shapley, the initiator of the New York Peace Conference, will attend and participate. The conference will protest the U.S. State Department's order forcing Eastern European representatives attending the New York Peace Conference to leave the United States.

### 美扩展和平运动　芝加哥将召开和平大会

1949-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　美扩展和平运动　芝加哥将召开和平大会
    【新华社北平四日电】波兰通讯社纽约讯：艺术、科学和自由职业者全国委员会将在芝加哥召集和平大会。纽约和平大会的发起人沙普莱将出席参加。大会将抗议美国国务院逼迫出席纽约和平大会的东欧代表离美的命令。
