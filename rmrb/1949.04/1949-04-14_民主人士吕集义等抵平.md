Title: Democratic Personages Lü Jiyi and Others Arrive in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 1

　　Democratic Personages
  　Lü Jiyi and Others Arrive in Beiping
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Democratic personages Lü Jiyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang; Tan Dongjing and Li Zhisong, representatives of the China Democratic Promotion Association; and Zhou Yuexiang, wife of Li Jishen, and Luo Xiou, wife of Cai Tingkai, arrived in Beiping from Tianjin at 4:00 PM today.

### 民主人士吕集义等抵平

1949-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　民主人士
  　吕集义等抵平
    【本报讯】民主人士国民党革命委员会副秘书长吕集义、国民党民主促进会代表谭冬菁、李致颂暨。李济深夫人周月乡、蔡廷锴夫人罗西欧等于本日下午四时由津抵平。
