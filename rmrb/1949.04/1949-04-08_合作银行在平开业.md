Title: Cooperative Bank Opens in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 2

　　Cooperative Bank Opens in Beiping
    [Report from this newspaper] The Cooperative Bank, prepared by the North China People's Government, officially opened in Beiping on the 7th. From morning to noon, people from all walks of life and colleagues in the industry came to offer their congratulations in an endless stream. The bank's business department and its affiliated offices and sub-branches also commenced operations on the same day. To expand its business, the bank has decided to set up branches throughout North China. The Tianjin branch is ready and will open in the near future. The Shijiazhuang office is also being organized.

### 合作银行在平开业

1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　合作银行在平开业
    【本报讯】华北人民政府筹办之合作银行于七日在平正式开业。由晨至午，各界及同业前往致贺者络绎不绝。该行营业部所属各办事处、分理处，亦于同日开始营业。该行为扩展业务，并决定在华北各地筹设分支机构。天津分行已筹备就绪，短期内可开业。石家庄办事处亦在筹组中。
