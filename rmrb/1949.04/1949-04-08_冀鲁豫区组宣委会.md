　　Ji-Lu-Yu District Organization and Propaganda Committee
    [Ji-Lu-Yu Dispatch] In order to unify the leadership of the propaganda and education work at the district party committee level in the Ji-Lu-Yu District, the Ji-Lu-Yu Propaganda and Education Committee was organized on the 24th of last month. The committee is composed of one representative from each of the following: the Propaganda Department of the District Party Committee, the Propaganda Department of the Military Region, the Education Department of the Administrative Office, the Propaganda Committees of the Workers', Peasants', Youth, and Women's organizations, the Literary Association, the newspaper office, and the Propaganda Department of the Heze County Party Committee. Under the leadership of the Propaganda Department of the District Party Committee, and in accordance with the instructions of the District Party Committee, it promotes various district-wide propaganda activities carried out by its subordinate units (it does not have direct leadership over the organizations at the prefectural, county, or sub-district levels led by each unit). All joint propaganda and education activities concerning the entire district, the formulation, planning, and inspection of propaganda guidelines for a certain period shall be discussed and decided upon by the Propaganda and Education Committee, and each participating unit shall ensure its implementation.

### 冀鲁豫区组宣委会

1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫区组宣委会
    【冀鲁豫电】冀鲁豫区为统一领导区党委一级的宣教工作，特于上月二十四日组织冀鲁豫宣教委员会。该委员会系由区党委宣传部、军区宣传部、行署教育处、工、农、青、妇宣传委员、文协、报社及菏泽县委宣传部等各派代表一人组成，在区党委宣传部领导下，按照区党委指示，推动所属各单位进行各种带有全区性的宣传活动（对各单位所领导的地、县或分区各级组织，不发生直接领导关系），凡有关全区共同性的宣教活动，一定时期的宣传方针的制订、计划及检查，统一由宣教委员会讨论、决定，并分别由各参加单位保证贯彻执行。
