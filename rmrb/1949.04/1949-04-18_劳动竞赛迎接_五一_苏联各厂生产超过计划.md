Title: Labor Competition to Greet "May Day": Soviet Factories Exceed Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1949-04-18
Page: 3
　　
　　Labor Competition to Greet "May Day"
　　Soviet Factories Exceed Production Plans
    【Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 16th】 International Labor Day on May 1st is approaching. The Soviet working people are, according to their glorious tradition, launching a broad socialist labor competition to produce more steel, coal, lathes, machines, locomotives, automobiles, tractors, and textiles as gifts to welcome May Day. TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Kuznetsk steel plants exceeded their production plans in the first three months of this year. Pig iron production increased by 12.1 percent compared to the same period last year. Blast furnace workers at the Magnitogorsk steel plants guaranteed to produce 1,000 tons of pig iron above plan before May Day and guaranteed to save 20 kilograms of coking coal per ton of pig iron during iron smelting. Steelworkers at the plant are also increasing the number of high-speed smelting operations and improving the quality of steel. Miners in the Kuznetsk coal mining area have already exceeded the plan for the first three months. Coal mines in the Donets Basin have also launched a competition to greet May Day. Workers at the Kolomna Locomotive Factory launched a new competition, namely, using saved raw materials, fuel, and electricity to produce above plan. Among them, they guaranteed to produce twelve more mainline locomotives this year using this method. The Sormovo Factory guaranteed to produce five more locomotives and three steam turbines. Thousands of workers at the Stalin Automobile Factory exceed the plan by 100 to 150 percent every day. They decided to produce hundreds of trucks and buses above plan before May Day and dedicate them to the country. Workers at the Stalingrad Tractor Factory guaranteed to produce dozens of tractors before May Day. Workers at the Moscow Ball Bearing Factory guaranteed to Stalin that they would reach the production level of the Five-Year Plan before May Day. Workers at the Krasin Radio Equipment Factory guaranteed to produce thousands more newly designed radios and amplifiers before May Day.

### 劳动竞赛迎接“五一”　苏联各厂生产超过计划

1949-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　劳动竞赛迎接“五一”
  　苏联各厂生产超过计划
    【新华社北平十六日电】五一国际劳动节，就要到来了。苏联劳动人民正按着他们光荣的传统，发动广泛的社会主义劳动竞赛，生产更多的钢铁、煤炭、车床、机器、火车头、汽车、拖拉机、纺织品，作为迎接五一节的礼物。塔斯社莫斯科讯：库兹涅茨克各钢铁厂在今年头三个月中生产超过了计划。铣铁产量比去年同一时期增加了百分之十二点一。马格尼托哥斯克各钢铁厂的鼓风炉工人，保证在五一节以前超计划生产铣铁一千吨，并且保证在炼铁时每吨铣铁节省焦煤二十公斤。该厂炼钢工人也正在增加高速度冶炼的次数，并提高钢的质量。库兹涅茨克煤矿区各处煤矿矿工，已经超过了头三个月的计划。顿尼兹区煤矿也发动了迎接五一节的竞赛。克诺洛姆纳机车制造厂工人发动了一种新的竞赛，即利用节省下来的原料、燃料和电力进行超计划的生产。其中保证今年用这种方法多生产十二台干线机车。索尔摩沃工厂保证多生产五台机车和三艘汽轮。斯大林汽车工厂成千的工人，每天都超过计划百分之一百到一百五十，他们决定在五一节以前超计划生产成百辆的卡车和公共汽车献给国家。斯大林格勒拖拉机工厂的工人们，保证在五一节以前生产几十辆拖拉机。莫斯科滚珠工厂工人，向斯大林保证在五一节以前达到五年计划的生产水平。克拉辛无线电器材厂工人，保证在五一节以前多生产成千新设计的无线电机和扩音器。
