Title: Exchange Rate for Areas Outside the District Today at 1 to 200
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate for Areas Outside the District Today at 1 to 200
　　[Our Paper's News] Due to the continued devaluation of the Kuomintang's Gold Yuan, the exchange rate for areas outside the district at the Bank of China has been raised to 1 to 200 starting from the 16th, an increase of 42.8% compared to the previous rate.

### 区外汇兑今起一比二百

1949-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑今起一比二百
    【本报讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价，由于国民党金圆券的继续贬值，自十六日起提高为一比二○○，较前次牌价又上升百分之四二点八。
