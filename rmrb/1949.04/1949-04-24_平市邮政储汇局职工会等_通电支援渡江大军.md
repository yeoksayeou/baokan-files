Title: Beijing Postal Savings Bureau Union and Others Send Telegrams to Support the Yangtze River Crossing Army
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 1

　　The Beijing Postal Savings Bureau Union and Others
  　Send Telegrams to Support the Yangtze River Crossing Army
    [Report from this newspaper] The Nanjing Kuomintang government's rejection of signing the domestic peace agreement has aroused the indignation of the people of Beijing. The Savings Division of the Beijing Postal Workers' Union has sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army. The telegram stated: "Advancing into the South of the Yangtze River is the unanimous call of the people of the whole country. We warmly support the wise order of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu to advance, and resolutely support the heroic Liberation Army advancing south." The Professors' Association of Beijing Railway College, Yenching University, and the Joint Association of Assistant Researchers of the Beijing Research Institute, the Joint Association of Lecturers and Teaching Assistants of Peking University, Tsinghua University, Normal University, Railway College, and Yanjing University, etc., jointly sent a telegram to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army saying: "The people of the South of the Yangtze River are anxiously waiting for you to liberate them. Your footsteps represent peace, happiness, and light. You will annihilate all enemies who dare to resist. We must make every effort to support you." In addition, the Student Union of Railway College, students of China University, and citizens of the first and fourth districts of Beijing have written letters expressing their support for the order to advance and supporting the PLA's southern expedition.

### 平市邮政储汇局职工会等　通电支援渡江大军

1949-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市邮政储汇局职工会等
  　通电支援渡江大军
    【本报讯】南京国民党政府拒绝签订国内和平协定，激起了平市人民的愤慨。北平邮政职工会储汇分会顷致电毛主席朱总司令及解放军全体指战员，电文称：“进军江南是全国人民一致的呼声，我们热烈拥护毛主席及朱总司令英明的进军命令，并坚决支援南进的英勇的解放军。”北平铁道学院、燕京大学教授会及北平研究院助理研究员联合会、北大、清华、师大、铁道学院、燕大讲师助教联合会等联合致电人民解放军各野战军全体指战员说：“江南的人民正在焦急的等待你们去解放，你们的足迹就是和平、幸福、光明，你们将歼灭所有敢于抵抗的敌人，我们必须尽一切努力来支援你们”。此外，铁道学院学生会、华大学生、平市内一区及内四区的市民均纷纷写信表示拥护进军命令，支援解放军南征。
