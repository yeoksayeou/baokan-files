Title: People's Bank Posts Exchange Rates, Buys Gold and Foreign Currency
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 2

　　[News from this newspaper] The Peiping branch of the People's Bank of China officially posted exchange rates and began buying gold, US dollars, and Hong Kong dollars on the 6th. The initial exchange rates were set as follows: Gold (pure gold) at 28,000 yuan (People's Currency) per tael, US dollars at 600 yuan (People's Currency) per dollar, and Hong Kong dollars at 110 yuan (People's Currency) per dollar.

### 人民银行挂牌　收兑黄金外币

1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行挂牌　收兑黄金外币
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北平分行于六日起正式挂牌收兑黄金、美钞、港币，首次订定之牌价为：黄金（纯金）每两兑人民券二万八千元，美钞每元兑人民券六百元，港币每元兑人民券一百一十元。
