Title: Education Bureau Continues to Announce Principals of Various Middle Schools in the City
Author: Wang Ming
Date: 1949-04-13
Page: 2

　　Education Bureau Continues to Announce Principals of Various Middle Schools in the City
    [Our Newspaper] The Ping City People's Government Education Bureau continues to announce the list of principals and deans of middle schools in the city as follows: Xu Dai'ao is the principal of the First Municipal Middle School, Li Shilian is the Dean of Studies, Du Junhui is the principal of the Second Municipal Girls' Middle School, Guan Ruoran is the Dean of Studies, Gong Shuhe is the Deputy Dean of Studies, and Wang Yizhi is the Deputy Dean of Studies of the Third Municipal Girls' Middle School. Ma Yuhuai is appointed as the principal of National Chengda Normal School, Yang Mingde as the vice principal, and Li Shanzhen as the Dean of Studies.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wang Ming)

### 教育局继续宣布本市各中学校长
王明
1949-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　教育局继续宣布本市各中学校长
    【本报讯】平市人民政府教育局继续宣布本市中学校长及教导主任名单如下：徐歹傲为市立第一中学校长、李士濂为教导主任、杜君惠为市立第二女子中学校长、管若然为教导主任、弓叔和为副教导主任、王一知为市立第三女子中学副教导主任。代派马玉槐为国立成达师范学校校长，杨明德为副校长，李善珍为教导主任。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王明）
