Title: Exchange Rate for Areas Outside the Region: Starting Today, One to Four Thousand

Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate for Areas Outside the Region
　　Starting Today, One to Four Thousand
　　[This newspaper] According to the Bank of China, the exchange rate for areas outside the region will be revised to 4,000 yuan to one unit starting today (April 30).
