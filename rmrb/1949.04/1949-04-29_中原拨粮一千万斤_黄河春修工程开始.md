Title: Central Plains Allocates 10 Million Jin of Grain, Yellow River Spring Repair Project Begins
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, April 28th: The Central Plains Temporary People's Government has allocated 10 million jin of grain as funding for this year's Yellow River spring repairs. The project will repair and reinforce the dangerous sections of the Yellow River along 312 li from Guangkukou River in Henan Province to Simingtang in Kaocheng. The project in the Hebei-Shandong-Henan area plans to consolidate and expand 67 dangerous sections and 978 rows of diversion structures. The standards for reinforcing dikes will be 1.7 meters above the highest flood level of 1937. Currently, the people on both banks of the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River are working with the Yellow River Commission's Flood Prevention Department to prepare materials, reinforce dikes, and rectify dangerous areas as part of the spring repair project.

### 中原拨粮一千万斤　黄河春修工程开始

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　中原拨粮一千万斤
  　黄河春修工程开始
    【新华社郑州二十八日电】中原临时人民政府顷拨粮一千万斤作为今年黄河春修费用，修装自河南省广武枯河以下至考城四明堂三百十二里间的黄河险工。冀鲁豫区的工程，计划巩固和增修险工六十七处，埽坝九百七十八道，复堤以高出民国二十六年洪水最高水位一公尺七寸为标准。现沿黄河中下游两岸人民正协同黄委会修防处进行备料、复堤、整险等春修工程。
