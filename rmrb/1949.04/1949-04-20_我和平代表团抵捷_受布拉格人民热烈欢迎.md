Title: Our Peace Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia, Warmly Welcomed by the People of Prague
Author:
Date: 1949-04-20
Page: 1

　　Our Peace Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia
  　Warmly Welcomed by the People of Prague
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 19th】According to a report from the Electric News Agency in Prague on the 18th: The Chinese delegation representing all the Chinese people to attend the World Congress for the Defense of Peace in Paris arrived in Prague on the 17th. Seven members of the delegation had already arrived in Prague last week. Prague Mayor Vacek, Frank, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Preparatory Committee for the Paris Congress, Professor Brusok, representative of the Czech Oriental Institute and Chairman of the Czech-China Association, the President of Charles University in Prague, and many Czech writers and journalists were present to welcome the Chinese delegation upon its arrival. The Czech welcoming representative emphasized the great significance of the Chinese people's victory in his speech. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation and a distinguished Chinese scholar and writer, expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the Czechoslovak government and people in his reply. He said: He firmly believes that the Chinese delegation, in cooperation with delegations from Czechoslovakia and other countries, will surely accomplish its mission of peace. Li Dequan, a Chinese representative and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Democratic Women's Federation, praised the contributions of Czech women in the implementation of the Czech Two-Year and Five-Year Plans in her speech.

### 我和平代表团抵捷　受布拉格人民热烈欢迎

1949-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我和平代表团抵捷
  　受布拉格人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社北平十九日电】据电通社布拉格十八日讯：代表全中国人民出席巴黎世界拥护和平大会的中国代表团于十七日到达布拉格。代表团中的七个团员已于上星期到达布拉格。布拉格市长瓦塞克、捷克出席巴黎大会筹备委员会主席弗兰克、捷克东方学院代表兼捷克——中国协会主席勃鲁索克教授、布拉格查利士大学校长和许多捷克作家与记者都在中国代表团到达时，前往欢迎。捷克欢迎代表在演说中强调中国人民胜利的巨大意义。中国代表团团长、卓越的中国学者与作家郭沫若在答词中对捷克斯洛伐克政府与人民的热情接待，表示感谢。他说：他深信中国代表团在捷克及其他国家的代表团合作之下，必将完成它的和平使命。中国代表及中国民主妇联副主席李德全在她的演说中赞扬捷克妇女在实施捷克两年与五年计划中的贡献。
