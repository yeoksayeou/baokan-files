Author:
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 1

North China University Notice, April 3rd

    Enrollment for the Second Division

    The list of successful candidates for the second re-examination of our university's Second Division was announced on February 25th. It is now decided that registration and enrollment for admitted students will take place on April 5th and 6th at the Second Division (inside the Temple of Agriculture). Admitted students are requested to arrive on time.

### 华北大学通告　四月三日  第二部入学

1949-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北大学通告　四月三日
    第二部入学
    本校二部第二次复试已于二月二十五日发榜，兹决定于四月五、六两日在该部（先农坛内）报到注册入学，希录取诸生，准时前往为要。
