Title: Cooperative Bank Provides Loans to Support Chicken Hatching Industry in Tong County
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 2

　　Cooperative Bank Provides Loans
  　To Support Chicken Hatching Industry in Tong County
    [Report from this newspaper] The Tong County office of the business department of the Cooperative Bank issued 160,000 yuan in small industrial loans to support the county's chicken hatching industry. Tong County has a highly developed chicken hatching industry, with no fewer than a thousand people across the county engaging in this as a side occupation.
　　(Jian, Yu)

### 合作银行放贷款扶植通县孵鸡业

1949-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　合作银行放贷款
  　扶植通县孵鸡业
    【本报讯】合作银行营业部所属通县办事处，发放了十六万元小本工业贷款，扶植该县的孵鸡业。通县孵鸡业最为发达，全县以此为副业者不下千人。
　　 （建、宇）
