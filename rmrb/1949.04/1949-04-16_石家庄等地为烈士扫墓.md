Title: Shijiazhuang and Other Places Sweep Tombs for Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 2
[Comprehensive Report by This Newspaper] On the 5th of this month, Qingming Festival, Shijiazhuang, Zhangjiakou and other places held public memorial ceremonies for the revolutionary martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves for the people. More than 900 representatives from all walks of life in Shijiazhuang gathered in front of the Martyrs' Monument in Victory Park on the 5th to sweep the tombs. Representatives from the Shijiazhuang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Shijiazhuang Municipal People's Government participated in the memorial ceremony. Zhangjiakou City solemnly held a public memorial ceremony in Victory Square on the 6th, with the participation of over 14,000 representatives from all walks of life and citizens. The memorial platform was filled with elegiac couplets and eulogies. The conference opened with a salute, followed by a moment of silence and the presentation of wreaths to the martyrs. The political commissar of the military region, the mayor, and representatives of the martyrs' families spoke in turn. The conference concluded with resounding slogans. In Nangong, Southern Hebei, 5,000 people participated in the public memorial ceremony for martyrs on the 5th. Before the meeting, everyone shouted slogans: Fulfill the will of the martyrs, completely crush the Kuomintang reactionaries' peace conspiracy, and resolutely carry the revolution through to the end.

### 石家庄等地为烈士扫墓

1949-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　石家庄等地为烈士扫墓
    【本报综合报导】本月五日清明节，石市、张垣等地公祭为人民英勇牺牲的革命先烈。石市各界代表九百余人于五日齐集胜利公园烈士塔前，举行扫墓，中共石家庄市委和石市民主政府均有代表参加致祭。张家口市六日于胜利广场隆重举行公祭，到会各界代表及市民共一万四千余人。公祭台满布挽联、挽词，大会于礼炮中开幕，向烈士静默、献花圈后，军区政委、市长、烈属代表依次讲话，大会在雄壮口号中散会。冀南南宫五日公祭烈士，参加者五千余人。会前全体高呼：完成烈士遗志，彻底粉碎国民党反动派的和平阴谋，坚决将革命进行到底。
