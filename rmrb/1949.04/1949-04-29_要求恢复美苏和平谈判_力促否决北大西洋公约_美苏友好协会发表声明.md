Title: Demanding the Resumption of US-Soviet Peace Negotiations and Striving to Reject the North Atlantic Treaty: US-Soviet Friendship Association Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 3

　　Demanding the Resumption of US-Soviet Peace Negotiations
  　Striving to Reject the North Atlantic Treaty
    US-Soviet Friendship Association Issues Statement
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 28th] TASS, New York dispatch: The National Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association issued a statement on the 25th, demanding the rejection of the North Atlantic Treaty. The statement pointed out that the North Atlantic Treaty and the plan to arm Europe mean the manufacturing of weapons, the reduction of living standards, and a sharp militarization in all aspects. The Council strongly urged people's organizations to hold protest rallies, write to Truman and Congress, strive to reject the North Atlantic Treaty, and resume negotiations with the Soviet Union to peacefully resolve all differences.

### 要求恢复美苏和平谈判　力促否决北大西洋公约  美苏友好协会发表声明

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　要求恢复美苏和平谈判
  　力促否决北大西洋公约
    美苏友好协会发表声明
    【新华社北平二十八日电】塔斯社纽约讯：美苏友好协会全国理事会于二十五日发表声明，要求否决北大西洋公约。声明指出，北大西洋公约与武装欧洲的计划，意味着武器的制造，生活水平的降低以及在一切方面急剧的军事化。理事会力促人民组织抗议大会，致函杜鲁门与国会，力促否决北大西洋公约，并与苏联恢复和平解决一切分歧的谈判。
