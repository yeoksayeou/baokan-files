Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 2

Literary Writer Zhao Shuli Gave a Speech at the Art Academy Yesterday
[Report from this newspaper] Renowned literary writer Zhao Shuli, at the invitation of the Beiping Art Academy, gave a speech at the school's auditorium at 1:30 PM yesterday (the 26th), titled "How Art Serves the Masses." In his speech, he particularly emphasized that artists should go deep into the masses to portray the lives of the masses, seeking improvement through popularization.

(Bai Sheng)

### 文艺作家赵树理昨在艺专演讲
柏生
1949-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　文艺作家赵树理昨在艺专演讲
    【本报讯】著名文艺作家赵树理应北平艺专之请，于昨（二十六）日下午一时半在该校大礼堂讲演，题为“艺术怎样为大众服务”，讲演中特别强调艺术家应深入到群众中去表现群众生活，在普及中求提高。
　　　　　　（柏生）
