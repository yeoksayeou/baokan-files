Title: Creating an Underwater Forest: Yantai Coastal Area Increases Kelp Cultivation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 3

　　Creating an Underwater Forest
  　Yantai Coastal Area Increases Kelp Cultivation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Yantai, 28th] The Yantai Aquatic Products Cultivation Farm has formulated a plan to cultivate kelp (seaweed) and other algae in the vast shallow sea area of 10 million square meters along the coast of Yantai, Weihaiwei, Liugong Island, Yangma Island, Kongtong Island, and Dashi Island, using rocks and seabed gravel, to gradually create an underwater forest.

### 创造海中林　烟台沿海增殖昆布

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　创造海中林
  　烟台沿海增殖昆布
    【新华社烟台二十八日电】烟台水产养殖场顷作出计划，决定在烟台、威海卫沿岸及刘公岛、养马岛、崆峒岛、大石岛一带一千万平方公尺广阔的浅海区，用自岩石及海底石砾，养殖昆布（即海带）等藻类，逐渐创造海中林。
