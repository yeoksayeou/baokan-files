Title: Third Session of the Second UN General Assembly Opens
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 7th: New York Dispatch: The Third Session of the Second UN General Assembly opened in New York on the 5th. The meeting was chaired by the President of the Assembly, Australian Foreign Minister Evatt, and lasted only thirty minutes. The Assembly's Steering Committee also met on the same day.

### 三届二次联大开幕

1949-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　三届二次联大开幕
    【新华社北平七日电】纽约讯：联合国第三届第二次大会已于五日在纽约开幕，由大会主席、澳外长伊瓦特主持，历时仅三十分钟。大会的指导委员会亦于同日集会。
