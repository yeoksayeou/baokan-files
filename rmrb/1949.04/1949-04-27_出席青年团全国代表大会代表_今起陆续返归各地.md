Title: Delegates to the National Congress of the Communist Youth League to Return to Their Respective Regions Starting Today
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 2

　　Delegates to the National Congress of the Communist Youth League to Return to Their Respective Regions Starting Today
    [Our Newspaper] The youth league delegates who attended the First National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League will return to their respective regions starting today. After the closing of the congress, they paid their respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, listened to a report from Comrade Zhou Enlai, a member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, regarding the analysis of the current situation and the work of the Youth League, and the delegations exchanged experiences from various regions. Special symposiums were convened on urban youth league work, rural youth league work, etc., and detailed studies were conducted on how to convey the resolutions of the congress and implement the resolutions and spirit of the congress into practical work. (Jin Feng)

### 出席青年团全国代表大会代表　今起陆续返归各地
金凤
1949-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　出席青年团全国代表大会代表
  　今起陆续返归各地
    【本报讯】出席新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会的青年团代表，今日起陆续返归各地。大会闭幕后，他们曾谒见了毛主席及朱总司令，听取了中共中央委员周恩来同志关于目前形势分析及青年团工作的报告，各代表团相互交流了各地经验。召集了城市青年团工作、乡村青年团工作等专题座谈会，并详细研究如何传达大会决议，把大会的决议与精神贯彻到实际工作中去。（金凤）
