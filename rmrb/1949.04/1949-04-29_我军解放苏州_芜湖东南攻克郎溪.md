Title: Our Army Liberates Suzhou; Langxi Captured Southeast of Wuhu
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 28th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing eastward along the Nanjing-Shanghai Railway, liberated Suzhou on the morning of the 27th, and annihilated two enemy battalions in Fengqiao Town, west of the city. Suzhou is the connecting point of the Nanjing-Shanghai and Suzhou-Jiaxing (Xing) roads, and is a vital military location. The State of Wu during the Spring and Autumn Period once established its capital here, hence it is also known as Wu County.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 28th: The People's Liberation Army in the Wuhu area is advancing rapidly southward at a speed of 130 li per day, and liberated Langxi, southeast of Wuhu, on the morning of the 27th.

### 我军解放苏州　芜湖东南攻克郎溪

1949-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军解放苏州
  　芜湖东南攻克郎溪
    【新华社长江前线二十八日电】沿京沪路东进的人民解放军于二十七日晨解放苏州，并于城西枫桥镇歼敌两个营。苏州为京沪路与苏嘉（兴）路之连接点，系军事要地。春秋时吴国曾建都于此，故又称吴县。
    【新华社长江前线二十八日电】芜湖方面人民解放军以每日一百三十里的速度向南疾进，在二十七日上午解放芜湖东南的郎溪。
