Title: Value-Guaranteed Savings Today's Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 2

　　Value-Guaranteed Savings
  　Today's Exchange Rate
    The Savings Department of the People's Bank of Peiping Branch announces that the standard physical unit exchange rate for value-guaranteed savings today (April 26th) is 22.19 yuan.
