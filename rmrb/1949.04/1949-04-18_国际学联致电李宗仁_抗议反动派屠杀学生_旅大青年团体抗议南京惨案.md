Title: All-China Students' Federation Cables Li Zongren Protesting Reactionary Faction's Massacre of Students; Lüda Youth Organizations Protest Nanjing Massacre
Author:
Date: 1949-04-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: The International Students' Federation in Prague on the 15th cabled Li Zongren, protesting the Kuomintang reactionary faction's brutal massacre of students in Nanjing and demanding punishment for the murderers of the students.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 17th: The Preparatory Committee of the Lüda New Democratic Youth League, the Lüda Youth Federation, and the Lüda Student Federation jointly issued a statement of support, protesting the Nanjing massacre and expressing solidarity with the students of Nanjing. In addition, the Lüda Teachers' Federation and professors from various universities in the area also expressed their protest against the Nanjing massacre.

### 国际学联致电李宗仁  抗议反动派屠杀学生　旅大青年团体抗议南京惨案

1949-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际学联致电李宗仁
    抗议反动派屠杀学生
  　旅大青年团体抗议南京惨案
    【新华社北平十六日电】布拉格十五日消息：国际学联顷致电李宗仁，抗议国民党反动派屠杀南京学生的暴行，要求惩办屠杀学生的凶手。
    【新华社大连十七日电】旅大新民主主义青年团筹委会、旅大青年联合会，旅大学生联合会联合发表声援书、抗议南京惨案，声援南京学生。另旅大教师联合会及该地区各大学教授亦对南京惨案表示抗议。
