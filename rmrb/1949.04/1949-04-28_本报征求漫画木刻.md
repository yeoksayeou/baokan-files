This Newspaper Seeks Cartoons and Woodcuts

　　This newspaper welcomes artistic works such as various cartoons, comic strips, and woodcuts. Those who wish to draw or carve are invited to contact the editorial department of this newspaper for consultation.
  　　　　
  　People's Daily

### 本报征求漫画木刻

1949-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　本报征求漫画木刻
    本报欢迎绘刻各种漫画、连环画、木刻等艺术作品，如愿绘刻者，请与本报编辑部洽商，为荷。　　　　
  　人民日报社
