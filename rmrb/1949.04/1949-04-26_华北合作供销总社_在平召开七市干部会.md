Title: North China Cooperative Marketing and Supply General Headquarters Holds Cadre Meeting of Seven Cities in Ping

Author: Yu
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 2

　　North China Cooperative Marketing and Supply General Headquarters
  　Holds Cadre Meeting of Seven Cities in Ping

    [Our Dispatch] The joint conference of responsible cadres from the Ping, Tianjin, Tangshan, Baoding, Zhangjiakou, Taiyuan, and Shijiazhuang city cooperative marketing and supply general headquarters, convened in Ping by the North China Cooperative Marketing and Supply General Headquarters, commenced yesterday (the 25th). The meeting is scheduled for three days. In addition to summarizing the experience of urban cooperatives, it will also discuss and resolve issues such as the organization and tasks of production cooperatives, the business of marketing and supply cooperatives, and urban-rural relations, which are universally present in various regions.

　　(Yu)

### 华北合作供销总社  在平召开七市干部会
宇
1949-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北合作供销总社
  　在平召开七市干部会
    【本报讯】华北合作供销总社在平召开之平、津、唐、保、张、并、石、七个城市供销合作总社负责干部联席会议，已于昨（二十五）日开始。会期预定三天。除总结城市合作社经验外，并研讨及解决各地普遍存在的生产合作社的组织与任务，供销合作社业务及城乡关系等问题。
　　（宇）
