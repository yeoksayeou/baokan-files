Title: Czechoslovakia and Norway Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 5th. TASS reports: Czechoslovakia and Norway signed a trade agreement in Prague on the 1st. The agreement is valid until the end of February 1950. The total trade volume is approximately 1.4 billion Koruna (Czech currency). Norway will supply Czechoslovakia with fish and fish products, chemicals, etc., while Czechoslovakia will supply Norway with textiles, pottery, glass, shoes, and products from the metallurgical and machine industries.

### 捷挪签订贸易协定

1949-04-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷挪签订贸易协定
    【新华社北平五日电】塔斯社讯：捷克斯洛伐克与挪威一日于布拉格签订贸易协定。该协定有效期限为至一九五零年二月底止。贸易总额约达十四亿科伦纳（捷币）。挪威将以鱼和鱼类产品、化学品等交付捷克斯洛伐克，捷将以纺织品、陶器、玻璃、鞋类、冶金和机器工业的产品供给挪威。
