Title: Unite to Stop Wars of Aggression! The French Communist Party Calls on Workers to Actively Participate in the May Day Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 3

　　Unite to Stop Wars of Aggression!
  　The French Communist Party Calls on Workers to Actively Participate in the May Day Demonstration
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th] TASS, Paris: The Political Bureau of the French Communist Party held a meeting on the 27th and passed a resolution calling on all working people to participate in the demonstration organized by the French General Confederation of Labor on May 1st. The resolution pointed out: "May Day" will not only be a festival of struggle to satisfy the demands of the working people, but also a festival of struggle against the war preparations policy of imperialism and its agents. The Political Bureau called on the French people to unite and use a powerful "May Day" demonstration to prove their strength in stopping the warmongers and their indomitable will to uphold peace.

### 团结起来阻挡侵略战争！　法共号召工人踊跃参加“五一”示威

1949-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　团结起来阻挡侵略战争！
  　法共号召工人踊跃参加“五一”示威
    【新华社北平二十九日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国共产党政治局于二十七日举行会议，通过决议，号召一切劳动人民于五月一日参加法国总工会所组织的示威。决议指出：“五一”将不仅是争取满足劳动人民要求的斗争节日，而且也将是反对帝国主义及其代理人备战政策的斗争节日。政治局号召法国人民团结起来，以强有力的“五一”示威证明他们阻挡战争贩子的力量和拥护和平的不屈不挠的意志。
