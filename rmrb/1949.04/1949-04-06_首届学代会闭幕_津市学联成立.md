Title: First Student Congress Concludes; Tianjin Student Federation Established
Author:
Date: 1949-04-06
Page: 3

[Tianjin Dispatch] The first Tianjin Student Representative Congress concluded on the 4th. The congress lasted four days, during which it adopted "The Current Tasks of the Tianjin Student Movement" and the "Charter of the Tianjin Student Federation," and formally established the Tianjin Student Federation. Liu Yan, Wu Longwen, Xu Shuzhen, and fourteen others were democratically elected as executive committee members. Zhang Jieying and two others were elected as alternate executive committee members. The congress unanimously passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, all democratic parties, and all people's organizations. It also telegraphed its support for the resolutions of the All-China Students' Federation and the North China Students' Federation regarding the student movement, and endorsed the World Peace Congress to be held in Paris.

### 首届学代会闭幕　津市学联成立

1949-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　首届学代会闭幕　津市学联成立
    【天津电】天津市学生第一届代表大会已经在四日闭幕。大会共开会四天，大会中通过“天津学生运动的当前任务”和“天津学生联合会章程”，并正式成立天津市学生联合会。民主选出刘炎、吴隆文、徐淑珍等十七人为执行委员。张捷英等二人为候补执行委员。大会一致通过向中国共产党毛主席朱总司令和人民解放军全体指战员，各民主党派，各人民团体通电致敬，并且通电拥护全国学联和华北学联关于学生运动的决议，拥护即将在巴黎召开的世界拥护和平大会。
