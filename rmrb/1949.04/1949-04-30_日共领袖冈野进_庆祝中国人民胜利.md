Title: Japanese Communist Leader Okano Susumu Celebrates the Victory of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 28th: According to an Agence France-Presse dispatch from Tokyo: Japanese Communist Party leader Nozaka Sanzō (Okano Susumu) congratulated the victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army when he received a reporter from the Japanese Communist Party's "Akahata" newspaper on the 27th. Nozaka believes that the instability of the Japanese ruling system will become increasingly severe. He also said: The industrialization of the new China is also possible without American "aid."

### 日共领袖冈野进　庆祝中国人民胜利

1949-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　日共领袖冈野进
  　庆祝中国人民胜利
    【新华社北平二十八日电】据法新社东京讯：日本共产党领袖野坂参三（冈野进）二十七日在接见日共“赤旗报”记者时，庆贺中国人民解放军的胜利。野坂认为，日本统治制度的不稳，将愈来愈甚。他又说：没有美国的“援助”，新中国的工业化也是可能的。
