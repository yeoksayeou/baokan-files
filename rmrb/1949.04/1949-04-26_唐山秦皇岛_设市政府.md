Title: Tangshan, Qinhuangdao: Municipal Governments Established
Author:
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 1

　　Tangshan, Qinhuangdao
  　Municipal Governments Established
    [From Our Paper] The Jidong Administrative Office recently submitted a request to the North China People's Government and received approval to rename the Tangshan Military Control Commission Municipal Office as the Tangshan Municipal People's Government; and the Qinyu Municipal Government as the Qinhuangdao Municipal People's Government.

### 唐山秦皇岛　设市政府

1949-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　唐山秦皇岛
  　设市政府
    【本报讯】冀东行政公署顷向华北人民政府呈准将唐山市军管会市政办事处改名为唐山市人民政府；秦榆市政府改名为秦皇岛市人民政府。
