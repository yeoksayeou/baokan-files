Title: Czech Military Tribunal
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 3

　　TASS, Prague: The Czechoslovakian National Military Tribunal recently sentenced two American spies: Sergeant Alexander Jones of the American European Occupation Army and soldier Lerence Hill of the American European Occupation Army. Jones and Hill illegally crossed the Czechoslovakian border in December 1948 to conduct espionage activities. Jones and Hill were uncovered and arrested by security agencies while engaging in espionage. The court hearing fully proved their guilt of espionage. Jones and Hill have been sentenced to ten and twelve years of imprisonment respectively.

### 捷军事法庭

1949-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷军事法庭
    【新华社北平一日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克国家军事法庭最近判决了两名美国间谍——美国欧洲占领军中士亚历山大·琼斯和美国欧洲占领军士兵勒伦斯·希尔。琼斯和希尔系于一九四八年十二月非法越过捷克斯洛伐克边界，进行间谍活动。琼斯和希尔在进行间谍活动时，被保安机关破获而予以逮捕。庭讯充分证明了他们犯有间谍罪。琼斯和希尔已分别被处十年与十二年徒刑。
