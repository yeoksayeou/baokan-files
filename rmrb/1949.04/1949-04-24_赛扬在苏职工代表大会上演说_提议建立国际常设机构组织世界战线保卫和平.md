Title: Saillant Speaks at Soviet Trade Union Congress, Proposes Establishing International Standing Organization to Organize World Front to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 3
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 23rd (Dispatch from Moscow): When Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, addressed the 10th Congress of Soviet Trade Unions on the 21st, he said that he represented the World Federation of Trade Unions in proposing to the World Peace Congress the establishment of a permanent international committee to carry out the activities of the world front for the cause of peace. He also suggested that the World Federation of Trade Unions, the Women's International Democratic Federation, and the World Federation of Democratic Youth should have representatives participate in this permanent international committee to ensure the main foundation for the cause of peace. Saillant declared: The international working class will always do everything in its power to fight for the cause of peace. Today, more than ever, trade unions in all countries of the world need to unite. Relying on their own unity, the world working class can confidently and successfully defend the cause of world peace.

### 赛扬在苏职工代表大会上演说  提议建立国际常设机构组织世界战线保卫和平

1949-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　赛扬在苏职工代表大会上演说
    提议建立国际常设机构组织世界战线保卫和平
    【新华社北平二十三日电】莫斯科讯：世界职工联合会总书记赛扬二十一日在苏联职工会第十届代表大会上致词时说，他代表世界工联向世界拥护和平大会提议：建立一个常设的国际委员会，以进行为和平事业而斗争的世界战线的活动。他又建议，世界工联、国际民主妇联及世界民主青联应有代表参加这个常设的国际委员会，以确保为和平事业而斗争的主要基础。赛扬宣称：国际工人阶级将始终尽一切力量为和平事业而斗争。世界各国职工会在今天比以往任何时候更加需要团结，依靠本身的团结，世界工人阶级是能够很有把握、很成功地保卫世界和平事业的。
