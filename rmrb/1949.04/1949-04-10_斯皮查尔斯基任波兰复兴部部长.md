Title: Spychalski Appointed Minister of Reconstruction of Poland
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 9th: TASS Warsaw dispatch: The Polish President Bierut, on the 6th, granted Kaszorowski's request to relieve him of his duties as Minister of Reconstruction, and agreed to Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz's recommendation to appoint Spychalski (former Deputy Minister of National Defense) as Minister of Reconstruction, and appointed Ochab as First Deputy Minister of National Defense, and appointed Army Commander Popławski as Second Deputy Minister. The former mayor of Łódź, Eugeniusz Stawiński, was appointed Minister of Light Industry.

### 斯皮查尔斯基任波兰复兴部部长

1949-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯皮查尔斯基
  　任波兰复兴部部长
    【新华社北平九日电】塔社华沙讯：波兰总统贝鲁斯特于六日准卡索罗夫斯基之请，解除其复兴部长战务，并同意总理西伦凯维兹的建议，任命斯皮查尔斯基（前国防部副部长）为复兴部部长，任命奥哈布为国防部第一副部长，任命陆军司令波鲁劳斯基为第二副部长。前罗兹市长尤金约斯。斯拉文斯基被任命为轻工业部部长。
