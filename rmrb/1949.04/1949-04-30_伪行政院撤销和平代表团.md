Title: Puppet Executive Yuan Revokes Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 1

　　Puppet Executive Yuan
  　Revokes Peace Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 28th] He Yingqin, who fled to Guangzhou, convened a meeting on the 27th in the name of the puppet Executive Yuan President, attended by more than a dozen reactionary elements, including Jia Jingde, Li Han hun, Zhang Zhiben, Duanmu Jie, Bai Yunti, Dai Kuisheng, Li Jilan, Dong Lin, Huo Huan, Huang Shao Gu, Ni Jiong Sheng, and others, resolving to "revoke the government (referring to the already-collapsed Nanjing Kuomintang reactionary government) peace negotiation delegation," and resolving to "completely cease postal and telegraphic remittances to the CCP area (referring to the Chinese People's Liberation Area)."

### 伪行政院撤销和平代表团

1949-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　伪行政院
  　撤销和平代表团
    【新华社北平二十八日电】逃到广州的何匪应钦，二十七日以伪行政院长的名义，召集了贾景德、李汉魂、张知本、端木杰、白云梯、戴愧生、李及兰、董霖、霍桓、黄少谷、倪炯声等十几名反动分子开会，决议“撤销政府（按指已经灭亡的南京国民党反动政府）和平商谈代表团”，并决议“对中共区（按指中国人民解放区）邮电汇兑一律停止”。
