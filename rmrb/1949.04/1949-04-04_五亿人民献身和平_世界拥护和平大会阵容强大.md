Title: Five Hundred Million People Dedicate Themselves to Peace; World Peace Congress Boasts a Strong Lineup
Author:
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 3

　　Five Hundred Million People Dedicate Themselves to Peace
  　World Peace Congress Boasts a Strong Lineup
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: According to the Polish News Agency, Paris: As of the 1st, delegations representing 500 million people have expressed their willingness to attend the World Peace Congress scheduled to be held in Paris on April 20th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd: TASS, Paris: The Preparatory Committee of the World Peace Congress issued a public statement on April 1st, reporting that in the past 24 hours, the Preparatory Committee had received declarations from numerous individuals and organizations in the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Germany, Mexico, Venezuela, Israel, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, and Ireland, expressing their dedication to the cause of peace and their desire to participate in the work of the Congress. These groups and individuals include the Netherlands Trade Union Confederation, the German Farmers' Mutual Aid League with 542,000 members, leaders of Venezuelan workers' groups, and miners of the Romanian People's Republic.

### 五亿人民献身和平　世界拥护和平大会阵容强大

1949-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　五亿人民献身和平
  　世界拥护和平大会阵容强大
    【新华社北平三日电】据波兰通讯社巴黎讯：截至一日为止，已经有代表五万万人的代表团，表示愿意出席定于四月二十日在巴黎召开的世界拥护和平大会。
    【新华社北平二日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：世界拥护和平大会筹备委员会于四月一日发表公报，报告在过去二十四小时中，筹备委员会即收到了英国、荷兰、德国、墨西哥、委内瑞拉、以色列、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚和爱尔兰的许多个人和团体的声明，表示献身和平事业及希望参加大会工作。这些团体和个人中有荷兰总工会，拥有五十四万二千会员的德国农民互助同盟，委内瑞拉工人团体的领袖，罗马尼亚人民共和国的矿工。
