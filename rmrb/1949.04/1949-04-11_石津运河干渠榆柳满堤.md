Title: Stone-Jing Canal Main Canal: Elms and Willows Line the Banks
Author: Shen Lin
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 2

　　Stone-Jing Canal Main Canal
  　Elms and Willows Line the Banks
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Stone-Jing Canal Irrigation Project Engineering Office is cultivating timber to protect the canal bank slopes. Along the main canal (i.e., the Stone-Jing Canal diversion route), on both banks, from Zhaolingpu Village in the Sixth District of Shijiazhuang City in the east to Baichigan Village in the Fourth District of Huolu County in the west, a total length of approximately thirty-seven li, six rows of elm, locust, and willow trees have been planted, totaling 21,592 trees, and more than 32,000 hemp plants have been sown. The section from Huangbizhuang to Baichigan is planned for summer sowing, and a portion of the tree seeds has already been prepared.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Shen Lin)

### 石津运河干渠榆柳满堤
沈林
1949-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　石津运河干渠
  　榆柳满堤
    【本报讯】石津运河灌溉工程工程处培植木材，保护渠道岸坡，将干渠（即石津运河导水路）两岸东自石家庄市六区赵陵铺村，西至获鹿县四区百尺杆村，全长约三十七里的岸坡植榆、槐、柳树六行，共二万一千五百九十二棵，并种大麻子三万二千余棵。黄壁庄至百尺杆段准备夏季播种，目前已准备好树籽一部。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（沈林）
