Title: Value-Guaranteed Savings: Today's Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 2

Value-Guaranteed Savings
　Today's Exchange Rate
    The People's Bank of Peiping Branch Savings Department announces today's (April 27th) standard commodity unit exchange rate for value-guaranteed savings as 126.90 yuan.
