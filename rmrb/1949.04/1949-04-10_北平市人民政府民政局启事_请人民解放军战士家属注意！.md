Notice from the Civil Affairs Bureau of the Peiping Municipal People's Government

Attention People's Liberation Army Soldier Family Members!

　　It has been found that in recent months, some of the notification letters for families of People's Liberation Army soldiers sent by various PLA units could not be delivered due to incomplete addresses. A list is hereby published in the newspaper. Please ask the families of the revolutionary soldiers listed to obtain proof from their respective street governments and come to the bureau to register. After verification, they will obtain military family status and enjoy preferential treatment.
