Title: Poland and Soviet-Occupied Germany Sign Trade Treaty
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 1st—Berlin: A trade treaty for 1949 between Poland and the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany was signed here on March 29. The treaty stipulates that the amount of goods exchanged between the two parties has reached 152 million US dollars, which is twice that of last year. Poland will exchange coal, coking coal, iron, metal appliances, fish, canned food and other goods with the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany for machinery, mining equipment and precision instruments for the printing industry, optical instruments, chemicals, etc.

### 波兰与德境苏占区签订贸易条约

1949-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　波兰与德境苏占区签订贸易条约
    【新华社北平一日电】柏林讯：波兰与德境苏占区之间一九四九年度的贸易条约，于三月二十九日在此间签字。该条约规定双方换货数额已达一亿五千二百万美元，为去年度的两倍。波兰将以煤、焦煤、铁、金属器具、鱼类、罐头食品及其他货物等与德境苏占区交换机器、采矿设备与印刷业精确仪器、光学仪器、化学品等。
