Title: Henan Military Region Local Armed Forces Eliminate 600 Bandits in Five Days
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

　　Henan Military Region Local Armed Forces
  　Eliminate 600 Bandits in Five Days
    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 22nd—The local armed forces of the Henan Military Region of the People's Liberation Army are actively suppressing bandit armed forces lurking in various places. In five days, from the 8th to the 13th, they eliminated more than 600 bandits. The Luoyang Sub-district local armed forces successively suppressed the bandit gangs of Li Tiankui and Yang Chunting in Lushi, Yiyang, and Yiyang areas on the 8th, 9th, and 11th, capturing 298 enemies and killing or wounding more than 60. On the 12th and 13th, the Nanyang Sub-district local troops suppressed the bandit gangs of Zhang Wanqing and Pei Chenggui in the Deng County area, capturing the bandit leader Pei Chenggui and more than 200 others, and killing or wounding more than 40. A certain unit of the Shanzhou Sub-district attacked the bandits of Shangge in Lushi, capturing 31 bandits. In the above battles, a total of 3 60mm mortars, 4 light and heavy machine guns, more than 230 rifles and carbines, and many other military supplies were seized.

### 河南军区地方武装　五天歼土匪六百

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　河南军区地方武装
  　五天歼土匪六百
    【新华社中原二十二日电】人民解放军河南军区地方武装积极清剿潜伏各地的土匪武装。八日至十三日五天内，共歼灭土匪六百余人。洛阳分区地方武装于八、九、十一三日，先后向卢氏、伊阳及宜阳地区之股匪李天魁、杨春庭等部清剿，俘敌二百九十八名，毙伤六十余人。十二、十三两日，南阳分区地方部队清剿邓县地区之张万清、裴成贵等股匪，俘匪首裴成贵以下二百余人，毙伤四十余人。陕州分区某部向卢氏上葛土匪进击，俘匪三十一名。在以上各项战斗中共缴获六○炮三门，轻重机枪四挺，步马枪二百三十余支及其他军用品甚多。
