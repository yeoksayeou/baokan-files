Title: Nine Kuomintang Air Force Personnel Arrive in Liberated Area from Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 1
　　
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 15th] Yan'an News: Kuomintang Air Force Captain Wu Zhixi, Military Assistant Wang Jiaji, and Meteorological Sergeant Mi Qiaoqun, along with six others, departed from Chengdu and other places and safely arrived in the Northwest Liberated Area on the 15th of last month, preparing to participate in the construction of the People's Air Force. Wu Zhixi and others' righteous actions have been welcomed by the military and civilians in the liberated areas.

### 国民党空军九人自成都抵解放区

1949-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党空军九人自成都抵解放区
    【北平新华广播电台十五日广播】延安消息：国民党空军上尉吴志浠、军佐王家骥和气象军士宓翘群等九人，从成都等地起身，已在上月十五日平安到达西北解放区，准备参加人民空军的建设事业。吴志浠等这种正义的行动，受到解放区军民的欢迎。
