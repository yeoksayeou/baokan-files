Title: Chairman Mao Zedong's Reply to Fu Zuoyi: Reactionary Kuomintang civil and military officials who sincerely repent and show factual performance are welcome.
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 2nd: Chairman Mao Zedong replied to Fu Zuoyi's telegram of April 1st. The telegram reads: General Fu Zuoyi:

　　I have read your telegram of April 1st. The Nanjing Kuomintang reactionary government's policy of launching a counter-revolutionary civil war is completely wrong. The government must be held responsible for the enormous disasters suffered by the Chinese people as a result of this counter-revolutionary civil war over the past few years. However, those civil and military officials of the reactionary Kuomintang government who have implemented this policy, as long as they recognize right and wrong, sincerely repent, act in good faith, and show factual performance, thereby promoting the cause of people's liberation and facilitating the peaceful resolution of domestic problems, will be welcomed by us, regardless of who they are. You, General, have contributed to the peaceful settlement of the Beiping issue. We believe it is excellent and should be welcomed that you, General, are willing to stand on the side of the people in the future and participate in the construction of the New Democracy.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, April 2, 1949

### 毛主席电复傅作义  反动的国民党文武官员，只要翻然悔悟，确有事实表现，均表欢迎。

1949-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电复傅作义
    反动的国民党文武官员，只要翻然悔悟，确有事实表现，均表欢迎。
    【新华社北平二日电】毛泽东主席对于傅作义四月一日通电发出复电。电文称：傅作义将军：
    四月一日通电读悉。南京国民党反动政府发动反革命内战的政策，是完全错误的。数年来中国人民由于这种反革命内战所受的浩大灾难，这个政府必需负责。但是执行这个政策的国民党反动政府的文武官员，只要他们认清是非，翻然悔悟，出于真心实意，确有事实表现，因而有利于人民解放事业之推进，有利于用和平方法解决国内问题者，不问何人，我们均表欢迎。北平问题的和平解决，贵将军与有劳绩。贵将军复愿于今后站在人民方面，参加新民主主义的建设事业，我们认为这是很好的，这是应当欢迎的。
  　　　　　　　毛泽东　一九四九年四月二日
