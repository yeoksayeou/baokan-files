Title: Philippine People's Anti-Japanese Army Strikes: Many Enemies Killed in Central Luzon
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd -- According to a Reuters dispatch from Manila on the 31st, the Philippine People's Anti-Japanese Army has simultaneously launched attacks from its bases in Nueva Ecija Province, the center of its activities in Central Luzon, killing and wounding many members of the puppet government's constabulary, security forces, and reactionary landlord armed militias.

### 菲民抗军出击　吕宋中部毙敌甚多

1949-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　菲民抗军出击
  　吕宋中部毙敌甚多
    【新华社北平二日电】据路透社引马尼拉三十一日的消息称：菲律宾民抗军已自吕宋中部民抗军的活动中心兰瓦伊西哈省的各据点同时出击，毙伤傀儡政府的警备队、保安队与地主反动武装民团人员多名。
