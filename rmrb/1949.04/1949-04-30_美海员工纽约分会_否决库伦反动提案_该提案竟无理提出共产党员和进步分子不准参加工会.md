Title: US Maritime Union, New York Branch, Rejects Cowley's Reactionary Proposal - The proposal unreasonably suggests that Communists and progressives should not be allowed to join the union.
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 29th. Radioteletype from New York: The New York branch of the National Maritime Union of America overwhelmingly rejected the reactionary proposal of Cowley, chairman of the union's national committee, on the 26th. This proposal suggested that Communists and progressives should not be allowed to join the union.

### 美海员工纽约分会　否决库伦反动提案  该提案竟无理提出共产党员和进步分子不准参加工会

1949-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　美海员工纽约分会
  　否决库伦反动提案
    该提案竟无理提出共产党员和进步分子不准参加工会
    【新华社北平二十九日电】电通社纽约讯：美国全国海员工会纽约分会二十六日以压倒多数否决了该会全国委员会主席库伦的反动提案。这一提案提出共产党员和进步分子不准参加工会。
