Title: North China New Youth League Congress Hears Report on Establishment of North China League
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 1

　　North China New Youth League Congress Hears Report on Establishment of North China League
    [Our newspaper's dispatch] The first Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China in North China continued yesterday (April 3rd). Comrade Lu Ping, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Youth League of North China, delivered a report on the establishment of the Youth League in North China and the tasks for future work. It will be collectively discussed today.
    　　(Jin Feng)

### 华北新青团代表大会听取华北建团报告
金凤
1949-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北新青团代表大会听取华北建团报告
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团华北第一届代表大会昨日（四月三日）继续开会，由青年团华北筹委会主任陆平同志作了一个关于华北青年团的建立情况及今后工作任务的报告。今日将集体讨论。
    　　（金凤）
