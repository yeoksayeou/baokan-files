Title: The U.S. Economy is Becoming Increasingly Bleak
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: Polish News Agency, Warsaw Dispatch: Italian famous writer Prezzolini pointed out in his Washington correspondence: The prospects for the American economy are becoming increasingly bleak. Film companies are closing down one after another. Railroads are laying off thousands of workers every day. The same situation exists in the automobile industry. Orders for the textile industry have been greatly reduced.

### 美国经济日趋暗淡

1949-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国经济日趋暗淡
    【新华社北平十七日电】波兰通讯社华沙讯：意大利名作家普列左里尼在其华盛顿通讯中指出：美国经济的远景日益暗淡，影片公司一个接着一个地关门，铁路上每天都要解雇成千的工人，汽车工业也存在着同样的情况，纺织工业的订货单已经大大地减少了。
