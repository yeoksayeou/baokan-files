Title: Correspondence

Author:

Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 1

To the Communication Workers and all worker comrades of factories in Shijiazhuang:
    We have heard that you have launched a new production increase movement on the basis of the original production increase competition to welcome the "May 1st" Labor Day. We hope you can organize and report on it in a timely manner.
    To the first congress of the Ordnance Bureau of the Public Enterprise Department of the North China Government: The telegram from your congress has been received, but it lacks a report on the congress's situation. We hope that after receiving this letter, you can make a detailed report immediately and send it to our agency as soon as possible.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our Newspaper Correspondence Section

### 通讯往来

1949-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来石家庄市各工厂通讯员及全体工人同志：
    听说你们已在原有增产竞赛基础上发动了新的增产运动，用以迎接“五一”劳动节。希望你们能及时的组织报导。
    华北政府公营企业部兵工局首届大会鉴：寄来的大会通电已收到，但缺少大会情况的报导，希望见信后能即刻作一详细的报导，并尽快给本社寄来为盼。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
