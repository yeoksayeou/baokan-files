Title: Italian General Confederation of Labor Calls on the Working Class to Unite with the Broad Masses to Demonstrate for Peace on May Day
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 17th: TASS, Rome: The Italian General Confederation of Labor issued a statement emphasizing the special significance of this year's International May Day. It calls on all Italian trade union organizations to unite with the broad masses of the Italian people, regardless of political or religious beliefs, to participate in the May Day demonstrations under the slogans of peace, work, and unity. The General Confederation of Labor has instructed its local unions to establish special committees in urban and rural areas to prepare for the demonstrations.

### 意总工会号召工人阶级　团结全国广大群众五一示威争取和平

1949-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　意总工会号召工人阶级
  　团结全国广大群众五一示威争取和平
    【新华社北平十七日电】塔斯社罗马讯：意大利总工会发表公告，强调指出今年国际五一节的特别意义，号召意大利一切工会团体团结意大利广大群众，不分政治和宗教信仰，在和平，工作和团结的口号下，参加五一节示威，总工会责成各工会在城乡地区建立特别委员会，准备示威。
