Title: Another Kuomintang Plane Defects

Author: 
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 29th. Another Kuomintang "B-25" type No. 940 medium bomber has defected to join the People's Liberation Army. The plane was flown by Captain Liang Huifu, a pilot of the 9th Squadron of the 1st Group of the Kuomintang Air Force, from Hankou on April 7th and arrived safely in the Liberated Area.

### 国民党飞机又一架起义

1949-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党飞机又一架起义
    【新华社北平二十九日电】国民党“Ｂ２５”式九四零号中型轰炸机一架起义参加人民解放军。该机系于四月七日由国民党空军一大队九中队上尉飞行员梁惠福驾驶，由汉口起飞安抵解放区。
