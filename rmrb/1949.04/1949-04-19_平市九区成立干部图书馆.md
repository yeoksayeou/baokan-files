Title: Ping City's Ninth District Establishes Cadre Library
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 2

　　[Ping City News] The Propaganda Department of the Ninth District Committee, in order to meet the learning needs of cadres, recently established a cadre library. A portion of books and magazines were purchased from Xinhua Bookstore for comrades to borrow. Daily reading time is from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. The library also publishes an irregular learning newsletter, where comrades can raise questions for discussion at any time, or write out their reading experiences for everyone's reference.

### 平市九区成立干部图书馆

1949-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市九区成立干部图书馆
    【平市讯】九区区委宣传部适应干部学习要求，近成立干部图书馆。由新华书店购到一部分书籍和杂志，供同志们借阅。每日阅读时间是下午五时至七时。该馆还办了份不定期的学习通讯报，同志们可随时发现问题提出讨论，或将阅读心得写出供大家参考。
