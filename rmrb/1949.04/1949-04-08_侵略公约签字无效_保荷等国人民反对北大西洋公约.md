Title: Aggression Pact Signing Invalid: People of Bulgaria, Netherlands, and Other Countries Oppose North Atlantic Treaty
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 7th -- Reports from various sources: People in Bulgaria, the Netherlands, Belgium, Egypt, and other countries are actively opposing the signing of the North Atlantic Aggression Pact. The Bulgarian National Congress for the Defense of Peace sent a letter to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie, pointing out that the pact violates the fundamental principles of the United Nations Organization. It demanded that the UN implement the resolution condemning war propaganda and adopt the Soviet proposal on reducing armaments and banning atomic weapons.

　　Meetings of construction workers in Amsterdam, workers at the Zaandam-Abatta factory, and a conference in Velsen, Netherlands, have all passed resolutions demanding that the government abolish the North Atlantic Pact. An editorial in the Dutch Communist Party's newspaper, De Waarheid, emphasized that the people did not authorize Foreign Minister Stikker to sign the North Atlantic Pact. Stikker represents the capitalist groups, and his signature has no validity. The Dutch people do not recognize this signature and do not recognize the aggressive North Atlantic Pact.

　　The Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party passed a resolution condemning the Spaak government's participation in the North Atlantic Pact and urging workers to oppose the government's policy of expanding armaments, as the burden of this policy will fall on the working class and taxpayers.

　　A demonstration against the participation of French North Africa in the North Atlantic Pact took place in Cairo, Egypt, on the 5th.

### 侵略公约签字无效　保荷等国人民反对北大西洋公约

1949-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　侵略公约签字无效
  　保荷等国人民反对北大西洋公约
    【新华社北平七日电】各方报导：保、荷、比、埃等国人民，纷纷反对北大西洋侵略公约的签订。保加利亚全国拥护和平大会，致函联合国秘书长莱氏，指出该公约是违反联合国组织的根本原则的，要求联合国实行谴责战争宣传的决议，以及采纳苏联关于减缩军备和禁用原子武器的提案。
    荷兰阿姆斯特丹的建筑工人和散顿阿伯塔根工厂工人的集会以及威林根的大会，都通过了决议，要求政府废除北大西洋公约。荷共机关报真理报的社论着重指出：人民没有授权外长斯蒂克在北大西洋公约上签字。斯蒂克是代表资本家集团的，他的签字没有任何效力。荷兰人民不承认这个签字，不承认侵略性的北大西洋公约。
    比利时共产党中央委员会通过决议，斥责斯巴克政府参加北大西洋公约，并敦促工人反对政府扩充军备的政策，因为这种政策的负担都要落在劳动阶级和纳税人的身上。
    埃及的开罗于五日发生反对法属北非参加北大西洋公约的示威。
