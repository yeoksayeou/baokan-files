Title: Ping City Establishes Hui People's Workers Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 2

　　Ping City Establishes Hui People's Workers Committee
    [This newspaper's report] The "Beijing Municipal People's Government Hui People's Work Committee" has been established by order. Ma Yuhuai has been appointed as the chairman, and Jin Yaru and Ma Chunguang have been appointed as members. The address is No. 100 Guang'anmen Inner Street, Northwestern Middle School, telephone number 3-3579. It has now started operations.

### 平市成立回民工委会

1949-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市成立回民工委会
    【本报讯】“北平市人民政府回民工作委员会”已奉令成立，派马玉槐为主任委员，金雅如、马春光为委员。会址在本市广安门内大街门牌一百号。西北中学内，电话三局三五七九号。现已开始办公。
