Title: Beijing Municipal Projects to Commemorate "May Day" and "May Fourth"
Author:
Date: 1949-04-22
Page: 1

　　Beijing Municipal Projects to Commemorate "May Day" and "May Fourth"
    [Report from this newspaper] The Beijing Municipal "May Day" and "May Fourth" Preparatory Committee held its second meeting yesterday (the 21st), resolving that rallies be held separately for "May Day" and "May Fourth". For "May Day" alone, seven groups will perform plays and Yangko dances. Movie theaters will offer free movie screenings to workers, and the Education Bureau will hold speech contests. Additionally, a "May Fourth" historical materials exhibition will be held at Peking University, and an exhibition will be held at the North China Book and Newspaper Agency in Zhongshan Hall. (Ming)

### 平市纪念“五一”“五四”项目

1949-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市纪念“五一”“五四”项目
    【本报讯】平市“五一”、“五四”筹委会昨（二十一日）召开第二次会议，决议“五一”、“五四”分别举行大会，仅“五一”即有七个团体演出话剧及秧歌舞，各电影院将免费招待工人看电影，教育局并举办讲演比赛。另在北大举行“五四”史料展览，在中山堂华北书报社展览。　　（鸣）
