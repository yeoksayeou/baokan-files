Title: TASS Refutes Reuters' Rumors
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, April 18th: TASS, Moscow, April 17th: On April 13th, British Reuters broadcast a message claiming that "Russian planes" appeared over Iran and Iraq last week. This message was published in some reactionary newspapers in Iran. TASS is instructed to deny this ridiculous fabrication by Reuters.

### 塔斯社驳斥路透社造谣

1949-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　塔斯社驳斥路透社造谣
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：四月十三日英国路透社广播消息，硬说：“俄国飞机”上星期出现于伊朗与伊拉克上空。该消息发表在伊朗的某些反动报纸上。塔斯社奉命否认路透社这一可笑的捏造。
