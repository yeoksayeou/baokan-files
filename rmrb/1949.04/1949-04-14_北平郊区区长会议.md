Title: Beiping Suburban District Chiefs Conference
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 1

　　Beiping Suburban District Chiefs Conference
    [Our Newspaper] The Beiping Municipal People's Government Suburban District Committee held a conference of district chiefs from various suburban districts at the City Government at 2:00 PM on the 13th. Attendees included more than ten units from Xiangshan, Qinglongqiao, Shijingshan, Fengtai, Nanyuan, Mentougou, and the Cooperative Bank in the Beiping suburbs. The meeting was chaired by Dong Ruqin, Deputy Director of the Civil Affairs Bureau. The main topics discussed were: land survey and registration in the suburbs, cooperative production loans, the organization of village administration, and how to solve the current spring famine in the suburbs. Comrade Yu Mian from the Cooperative Bank reported at the meeting that the Beiping Municipal Cooperative Bank office has been established and is preparing to issue agricultural loans in coordination with the spring plowing. The meeting will continue on the afternoon of the 14th.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 北平郊区区长会议
柏生
1949-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平郊区区长会议
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府郊区委员会于十三日下午二时假市府召开各郊区区长会议，出席者计有平郊香山、青龙桥、石景山、丰台、南苑、门头沟及合作银行等十余单位，由民政局副局长董汝勤主席，会中主要讨论：郊区的土地调查登记、合作生产贷款、村政权的组织、如何解决郊区目前春荒等四项问题。合作银行于勉同志在会上报告北平市已成立合作银行办事处，准备配合春耕，发放农贷。会议将于十四日下午继续举行。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
