Title: North China Tax Bureau Convenes Meeting of Managers of Publicly Operated Distilleries
Author:
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 1

　　North China Tax Bureau
  　Convenes Meeting of Managers of Publicly Operated Distilleries
    [News from this newspaper] The North China Tax Bureau convened a meeting of managers of publicly operated distilleries from across the region, which was held on the 10th at the bureau's offices in Nanbingmasi, Beiping. Fifty-seven managers and representatives from areas including Ji-Lu-Yu, Taihang, Taiyue, Chahar, Jizhong, Jinan, Jidong, Shijiazhuang, Suiyuan, as well as Ping and Tianjin, attended. The meeting is scheduled to last for ten days. In addition to reviewing past work, the meeting will discuss and resolve important issues such as the export of alcoholic beverages, monopoly operations, production and sales, tax rates, and market allocation.
　　　　　　　　　　(Jian, Yu)

### 华北税务总局召开公营酒厂经理会

1949-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北税务总局
  　召开公营酒厂经理会
    【本报讯】华北税务总局所召开的全区公营酒厂经理会议，已于十日在北平南兵马司该局内举行。冀鲁豫、太行、太岳、察哈尔、冀中、冀南、冀东、石家庄、绥远、以及平、津等地出席经理、代表共五十七人，会期预定十天。此次会议除检讨以往的工作外，将研讨并解决今后酒类出口、专营、产销、税率及市场分配等重要问题。　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　（建、宇）
