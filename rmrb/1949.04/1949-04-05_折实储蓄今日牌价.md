Title: Savings Deposits Indexed to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-04-05
Page: 2

　　Savings Deposits Indexed to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate
    The Savings Department of the People's Bank of Beiping Branch announces that today's (5th) standard unit exchange rate for savings deposits indexed to real value is 90.95 yuan.
