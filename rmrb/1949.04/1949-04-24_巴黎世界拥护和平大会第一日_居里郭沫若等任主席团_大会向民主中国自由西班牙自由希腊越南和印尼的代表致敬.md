Title: First Day of the World Congress of Partisans of Peace in Paris: Joliot-Curie, Guo Moruo, and Others Serve on the Presidium; Congress Pays Tribute to Representatives of Democratic China, Free Spain, Free Greece, Vietnam, and Indonesia

Author: 
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: On the first day (20th) of the World Congress of Partisans of Peace in Paris, the congress first elected its presidium, which included Frédéric Joliot-Curie, French Member of Parliament Aragon, Martin Andersen of Denmark, World Federation of Democratic Youth President Bauereson, British Dean Burr, Mexican General Lázaro Cárdenas, World Federation of Democratic Women President, Mrs. Gordon, Soviet Fadeyev, Chinese Guo Moruo, Italian Nenni, American John Rogge, World Federation of Trade Unions President Saillant and others.
　　The congress then began with an opening address by Chairman Joliot-Curie. He paid tribute to the delegates on behalf of the organizing committee and millions of French people, and especially emphasized paying tribute to the representatives of Democratic China, Free Spain, Free Greece, Vietnam and Indonesia.

### 巴黎世界拥护和平大会第一日　居里郭沫若等任主席团  大会向民主中国自由西班牙自由希腊越南和印尼的代表致敬

1949-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴黎世界拥护和平大会第一日
  　居里郭沫若等任主席团
    大会向民主中国自由西班牙自由希腊越南和印尼的代表致敬
    【新华社北平二十二日电】巴黎世界拥护和平大会第一日（二十日）会议，首先选出大会的主席团，其中有约里奥·居里、法国民议会议员阿拉贡、丹麦的马丁·安得生、世界青年联盟主席鲍埃逊、英国丁·勃尔、墨西哥拉柴罗·加顿纳斯将军、世界民主妇女联盟主席戈登夫人、苏联法捷耶夫、中国郭沫若、意大利南尼、美国约翰·罗治、世界工联主席赛扬等人。
    大会继即由居里主席致开幕词。他代表筹委会及法国千百万人民向到会代表们致敬，并特别强调向民主中国、自由西班牙、自由希腊、越南及印尼的代表们致敬。
