Title: Second Installment of Marshall Aggression Plan Funds Approved; Truman Actually Signs Again
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

　　Second Installment of Marshall Aggression Plan Funds Approved
  　Truman Actually Signs Again
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 21st] According to the US Information Service: US President Truman signed the second installment of the Marshall Aggression Plan Act on the 19th, stipulating US$5.43 billion for the implementation of the aggression plan for the next fifteen months (April 3, 1949 to June 30, 1950). The actual appropriation of expenses still requires separate approval by Congress. However, before Congress actually appropriates funds, the Economic Cooperation Administration can borrow US$1 billion from the Reconstruction Finance Corporation as a revolving fund.
    At the same time, this bill signed by Truman also stipulates that the US$45.5 million of the US$270 million "Aid to China Act" that has not yet been used can continue to be used until February 15 of next year to encourage the Kuomintang reactionaries to make a desperate struggle.

### 第二期马歇尔侵略计划拨款　杜鲁门竟又签字

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　第二期马歇尔侵略计划拨款
  　杜鲁门竟又签字
    【新华社北平二十一日电】据美国新闻处讯：美总统杜鲁门已于十九日签署第二期马歇尔侵略计划法案，规定以五十四亿三千万美元，为今后十五个月（一九四九年四月三日至一九五○年六月三十日）执行侵略计划的经费。实际费用的指拨，仍需国会另案通过。但在国会实际拨款以前，经合总署可向复兴银公司借款十亿美元，作为活动基金。
    同时，杜鲁门签署的这一法案，并规定二亿七千万“援华法案”中尚未动用的四千五百万美元，可继续运用至明年二月十五日，以鼓励国民党反动派作垂死挣扎。
