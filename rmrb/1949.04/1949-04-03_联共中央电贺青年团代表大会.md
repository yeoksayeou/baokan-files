Title: CPSU Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Youth League Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 2nd: Moscow Dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a congratulatory telegram to the 11th Congress of the Communist Youth League, stating: The Communist Youth League is the vanguard of Soviet youth, a reliable reserve force and loyal assistant of the Bolshevik Party. Nurtured and guided by Lenin and Stalin, the Communist Youth League has traversed a great and glorious path, cultivating millions of patriotic Soviet youth and active builders of communist society, and has won the respect and love of the people with its patriotic activities. Today, the Soviet people are solving the great task of building communism, and the role of the Communist Youth League in educating the younger generation has even greater importance. The Communist Youth League must cultivate fearless, vigorous, and enthusiastic fighters among the youth, fighters who will fight for the freedom and honor of our motherland, for the cause of the Lenin-Stalin Party, and for the victory of communism.

### 联共中央电贺青年团代表大会

1949-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　联共中央电贺青年团代表大会
    【新华社北平二日电】莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会电贺共产主义青年团第十一届代表大会称：共产主义青年团是苏联青年的先锋队，是布尔什维克党可靠的后备军和忠实的助手。共产主义青年团受到列宁和斯大林的培育和指导，经历了伟大、光荣的道路，培育了千百万苏维埃青年爱国者和共产主义社会的积极建设者，并以其爱国活动，赢得了人民的尊敬与热爱。今天苏联人民正在解决建设共产主义的伟大任务，共产主义青年团在教育年青一代中的作用，已有更巨大的重要性，共产主义青年团必须在青年中培养大无畏的、朝气蓬勃的、兴致勃勃的战士，为我们祖国的自由与荣誉，为列宁斯大林党的事业，为共产主义的胜利而斗争的战士。
