Title: Welcome Suggestions for Municipal Administration
Author:
Date: 1949-04-01
Page: 4

　　Social Services

　　To Mr. Hu Dingjun, Mr. Li Hongjiong, Ms. Guan Huiqing, and Mr. Guo Jingsen:
　　We have received your suggestions regarding the municipal administration of Beiping. They have been forwarded to the relevant departments for study and improvement. We greatly welcome your enthusiastic concern for the construction of Beiping and hope you will offer more opinions in the future.

　　To Mr. Yu Shizhou:
　　Your suggestion regarding the government's tree planting is very valuable. We have forwarded your opinion to the Department of Agriculture and Forestry of the North China Government. We would like to express our gratitude to you here. We hope that you will continue to provide constructive opinions to accelerate the progress of our construction work.

　　To Mr. Wang Yichen:
　　The suggestions in your letter are very good. We have forwarded them to the relevant departments for study and to improve our work. We hope that you will continue to provide constructive opinions for the work of the city of Beiping.
　　　　　　　　　　Social Services Group

### 欢迎你们对市政建议

1949-04-01
第4版()
专栏：社会服务

　　欢迎你们对市政建议
    胡鼎钧  李洪迥、关惠卿  郭景森诸先生：
    你们对北平市政的建议，已收到。并已转达有关各机关研究改进。你们对北平建设工作如此热情关怀，我们是很欢迎的，并希望你们以后更多的提出意见。于世胄先生：
    你对政府植树的建议，很宝贵，我们已将你的意见转达华北政府农林部。在此，特致谢意。今后尚望多多提供建设意见、俾使建设事业突飞猛进。王颐晨先生：
    来信所提建议很好，我们已转达有关机关研究，以改进工作。今后尚望继续为北平市工作提供建设意见。
    　　　　　社会服务组
