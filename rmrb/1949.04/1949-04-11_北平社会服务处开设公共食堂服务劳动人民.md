Title: Beiping Social Service Center Opens Public Canteen to Serve the Working People
Author:
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 2

　　Beiping Social Service Center
  　Opens Public Canteen to Serve the Working People
    [Beiping News] To serve the working masses, the Beiping Social Service Center recently opened a public canteen near Qianwai Damochang. It offers two types of meals, with prices ranging from a minimum of 15 yuan per person per "dun" (likely a unit of weight or serving size) to a maximum of 50 yuan, which is quite economical. Since its opening, more than one hundred working people have been going there to eat every day.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Beiping Social Service Center)

### 北平社会服务处开设公共食堂服务劳动人民

1949-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平社会服务处
  　开设公共食堂服务劳动人民
    【平市讯】为服务劳动大众，平市社会服务处近在前外打磨场开设了一个公共食堂，备有两种饭食，最低每人每吨十五元，最高五十元，颇为经济。开张以来每天要有一百多劳动人民前往用餐。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（北平社会服务处）
