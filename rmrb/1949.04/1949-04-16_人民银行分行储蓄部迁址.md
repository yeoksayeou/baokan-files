Title: People's Bank Branch Savings Department Relocates
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 2

　　People's Bank Branch
  　Savings Department Relocates
    [Ping City News] Since its opening, the People's Bank Savings Department in this city has been well-received for its "Shishi" (real value) savings. Due to recent business expansion, it will relocate to the former site of the Zhongnan Bank at 27 Xiheyan on the 18th to conduct business. The original site will be converted into the Second Business Office. Existing depositors can still handle deposit and withdrawal procedures at the original site.

### 人民银行分行储蓄部迁址

1949-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行分行
  　储蓄部迁址
    【平市讯】本市人民银行储蓄部自开业以来，折实储蓄颇受欢迎，近因业务扩大，将于十八日迁西河沿二十七号原中南银行旧址营业，原址改为第二营业室。原储款各户，存取手续，仍在原址办理。
