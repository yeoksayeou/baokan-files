Title: Czechoslovak Premier Issues Call: Workers of the World, Unite to Prevent Imperialist War

Author:

Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 8th: TASS, Prague reports: Czechoslovak Premier and Chairman of the Trade Union, Zápotocký, recently stated that the unity of the working class, preparing for battle, is the main force capable of preventing new aggression by imperialists against peace-loving nations. On the eve of the economic crisis, capitalists seek to weaken the fighting strength of the working class and make its will subject to the interests of monopoly capital. The international laboring people's movement to defend peace is not only fighting to maintain peace, but also to defend the rights of the laboring people and improve their living standards. The upcoming World Congress of Supporters of Peace will be a serious blow to war instigators. It will also be a serious blow to the reactionary forces in Czechoslovakia who are clamoring for a new war to break out.

### 捷总理发出号召　工人阶级团结起来阻止帝国主义战争

1949-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷总理发出号召
  　工人阶级团结起来
  　阻止帝国主义战争
    【新华社北平八日电】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克总理兼职工会主席萨波托斯基最近发表谈话说。工人阶级团结起来，进行战斗准备，是能够阻止帝国主义对爱好和平民族进行新侵略的主要力量。在经济危机前夕，资本家力求削弱工人阶级的战斗力量和使它的意志服从于垄断资本的利益。国际劳动人民的保卫和平运动，不仅是为保持和平，而且也是为保卫劳动人民的权利和提高生活水平而斗争。即将来临的世界拥护和平大会将是对战争挑拨者的严重打击。它也将是对喧嚷新战争要爆发的捷克斯洛伐克反动派的严重打击。
