Title: Beijing Prepares for Industrial Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 2

(Beijing News) Since liberation, the city's industry has gradually recovered, and the production situation has also improved. Currently, under the leadership of the Bureau of Industry and Commerce, the city's Industrial Association is stepping up preparations for an industrial exhibition. The exhibition is scheduled to begin on June 6th of this year at the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park. A sales area will be set up to sell products. The collection of exhibits has already begun, including products, models, charts, photographs, texts, and other industrial-related items.

### 平市筹备工业展览

1949-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市筹备工业展览
    【平市讯】本市工业自解放以来，逐渐恢复，生产情形亦日见好转。现本市工业会在工商局领导下，正加紧筹备工业展览会。时间定于本年六月六日开始，地点拟在中山公园中山堂。并设售品所，附售产品。目前已开始征集展览品。分产品、模型、图表、照片、文字及其他有关工业的种种。
