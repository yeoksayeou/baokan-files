Title: Soviet-Albanian Agreement Signed: USSR to Loan Equipment and Materials to Albania
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, Fourteenth: Moscow dispatch: TASS announced on the 11th that the governments of the Soviet Union and Albania had signed an agreement in Moscow whereby the Soviet Union would loan equipment and materials to Albania, and a barter treaty for 1949. The Albanian government delegation left the Soviet Union on the 11th to return home. Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, sent a telegram to Stalin before his departure, expressing deep gratitude. The telegram stated: The enormous assistance given by the Soviet Union to Albania in the economic, cultural, and educational fields constitutes a strong foundation for Albania's construction of socialism.

### 苏阿签订协定苏将贷阿装备物资

1949-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏阿签订协定苏将贷阿装备物资
    【新华社北平十四日电】莫斯科讯：塔斯社于十一日发表苏联与阿尔巴尼亚两国政府的公报，宣布阿政府代表团已在莫斯科与苏联签订了苏联以装备与物资贷给阿尔巴尼亚的协定，及一九四九年的易货条约。阿政府代表团于十一日离苏返国。阿部长会议主席霍查临行时特电斯大林表示深切的感谢。电文中说：苏联在经济、文化和教育方面给予阿国的巨大帮助，构成了阿国建设社会主义的强有力的基础。
