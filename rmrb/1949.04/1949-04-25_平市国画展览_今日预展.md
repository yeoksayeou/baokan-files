Title: Ping City Chinese Painting Exhibition - Preview Today
Author:
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 2

Ping City Chinese Painting Exhibition
    Preview Today
    [Our Newspaper] The colleagues in the Chinese painting circle of Beiping City have been preparing for two months and plan to hold a new Chinese painting exhibition. More than 100 paintings have now been completed. For the sake of observation and learning, a preview is specifically scheduled from 1:00 PM to 6:00 PM on the 25th (Monday) at the Hua Da Fine Arts Team, No. 12 Caoduo Hutong, Beikou, Beichi Zi. Criticisms are welcome.

### 平市国画展览  今日预展

1949-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市国画展览
    今日预展
    【本报讯】北平市国画界同人经两个月的筹备，拟举行一新国画展览会，今已作成画件一百余幅。兹为观摩起见，特定于二十五日（星期一）午后一时至六时假北池子北口草垛胡同十二号华大美工队举行预展，欢迎批评。
