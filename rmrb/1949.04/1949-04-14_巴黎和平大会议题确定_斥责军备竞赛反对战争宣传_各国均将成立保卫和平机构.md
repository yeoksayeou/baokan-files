Title: Paris Peace Congress Agenda Set: Condemning the Arms Race, Opposing War Propaganda; All Countries Will Establish Peace Protection Organizations
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: According to the Polish News Agency from Paris: The Preparatory Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, in view of the increasing number of people applying to participate in the Congress, decided at its meeting on the 8th to extend the session by one day, i.e., the Congress will be held for six days starting from April 20th. At the same time, the Preparatory Committee decided to put the following topics up for discussion at the Peace Congress: condemning the arms race and increasing war budgets, the role of the United Nations in safeguarding peace, the struggle against war propaganda, respecting the sovereignty and independence of all nations, world economic relations, women's participation in the peace movement, organizing peace supporters, and establishing peace protection organizations in various countries.

### 巴黎和平大会议题确定  斥责军备竞赛反对战争宣传　各国均将成立保卫和平机构

1949-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴黎和平大会议题确定
    斥责军备竞赛反对战争宣传　
    各国均将成立保卫和平机构
    【新华社北平十二日电】据波兰通讯社巴黎讯：世界拥护和平大会筹委会鉴于申请参加大会的人数越来越多，于八日会议中决议将会期延长一天，即大会将自四月二十日起举行六天。筹委会同时决定将下列议题在和平大会中提出讨论：斥责军备竞赛及增加战争预算，联合国在保卫和平中的作用，反对战争宣传的斗争，尊重各民族的主权与独立，世界经济关系，妇女参加和平运动，组织和平拥护者，并在各国成立保卫和平的机构。
