Title: Romania Regularly Elects People's Jurors; Exploiters of Others' Labor Cannot Participate
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 20th: The Romanian News Agency reports: Romania will hold elections for people's jurors nationwide from May 10th to June 10th. This is a major event for the Romanian working people. The election law stipulates that the minimum age for electors is eighteen, and the minimum age for those elected is twenty-three. Anyone who has engaged in or is engaged in any exploitation of others' labor cannot participate in the election or be elected as a people's juror. Candidates will be nominated by the Romanian Workers' Party, the provisional committees of the People's Councils in various regions, the Labor Federation, local trade union groups, and other mass organizations. The election will be guided by a committee composed of the President of the Court and representatives of the Romanian Workers' Party and the Labor Federation. According to the new law of the judicial administrative organs, people's jurors who belong to the ranks of urban and rural working people will participate in the trials of all criminal and civil courts except the Supreme Court.

### 罗定期选举人民陪审官　凡剥削他人劳动者不能参加

1949-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗定期选举人民陪审官
  　凡剥削他人劳动者不能参加
    【新华社北平二十日电】罗马尼亚通讯社讯：罗马尼亚将于五月十日至六月十日在全国举行人民陪审官的选举，这是罗马尼亚劳动人民的重大事件。选举法规定：选举人最低年龄为十八岁，被选人最低年龄为二十三岁。凡曾进行或正在进行任何剥削他人劳动的人，不能进行选举或被选为人民陪审官。候选人将由罗工人党，各地的人民会议临时委员会，劳工联合会，各地工会团体和其他群众组织提出。选举将由法院院长及罗工人党与劳工联合会的代表所组成的委员会指导进行。按照司法行政机关的新法律，凡属于城乡劳动人民队伍的人民陪审官，将参加最高法院以外的一切刑事与民事法庭的裁判。
