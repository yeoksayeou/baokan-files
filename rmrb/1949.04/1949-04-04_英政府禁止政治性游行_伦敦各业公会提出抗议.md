Title: British Government Bans Political Marches, London Trade Unions Protest
Author:
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 3rd, Electric News Agency London: The Executive Committee of the London Trades Council protested on the 1st against the Home Office's decision to ban political marches in London for three months. The protest stated: Due to this ban, in Europe, only Britain, Franco's Spain, and Royal Fascist Greece are prohibiting workers from marching on May Day.

### 英政府禁止政治性游行　伦敦各业公会提出抗议

1949-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　英政府禁止政治性游行　伦敦各业公会提出抗议
    【新华社北平三日电】电通社伦敦讯：伦敦各业公会委员会执委会于一日抗议内政部禁止三个月内在伦敦举行政治性游行的决定。抗议宣称：由于这一禁令，欧洲只有英国、佛朗哥、西班牙、和保皇法西斯的希腊，禁止工人在五一节游行。
