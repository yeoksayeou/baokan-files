Title: Photos of Children's Life in Liberated Areas on Display at Zhongshan Park Today
Author:
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 1

　　Photos of Children's Life in Liberated Areas on Display at Zhongshan Park Today
    [Our Paper] To celebrate "April 4th" Children's Day, the North China Pictorial Agency of the Political Department of the North China Military Region has specially selected various photos related to children's lives in the liberated areas and will hold an open-air photography exhibition at Zhongshan Park in Ping City today. All circles of children in Ping City are welcome to visit collectively.

### 解放区儿童生活照片今日在中山公园展览

1949-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放区儿童生活照片今日在中山公园展览
    【本报讯】为了庆祝“四四”儿童节，华北军区政治部华北画报社特选出有关解放区的儿童生活照片多种，于今日在平市中山公园举行露天摄影展览，欢迎平市各界儿童集体参观。
