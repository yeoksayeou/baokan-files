Title: Intra-Regional Exchange Rate: 1 to 3,000 Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 2

　　Intra-Regional Exchange Rate
    1 to 3,000 Starting Today
    [Our Newspaper] The Bank of China's intra-regional exchange rate will be adjusted to 1 to 3,000 starting today (April 27th), an increase of 1,000 yuan compared to the previous rate.

### 区外汇兑  今起一比三千

1949-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑
    今起一比三千
    【本报讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价自今（二十七日）起改定为一比三、○○○，较前牌价再上提千元。
