Title: Cultivating Children's Interest in Literature: Soviet Union Holds Children's Book Movement Week
Author:
Date: 1949-04-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, March 31st: Moscow, March 28th: The Soviet Union is holding a Children's Book Movement Week. The aim is to popularize children's books and cultivate children's interest in literature. Millions of children who are on school holidays are participating in this movement week. Before the start of the movement week, many children's readers' meetings were held in various places, where children presented their favorite books and discussed the latest works of writers. On the first day of Children's Book Week in Moscow, children held a meeting at the Trade Union Building, where they met writers and visited the children's book exhibition. Various children's clubs, cultural palaces, and large enterprises have arranged poetry recitals, concerts, and performances of the best dramas for children.

### 培养儿童对文学的兴趣　苏举行儿童读物运动周

1949-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　培养儿童对文学的兴趣
  　苏举行儿童读物运动周
    【新华社北平三十一日电】莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联正举行儿童读物运动周。目的是普及儿童读物，培养儿童对文学的兴趣。正在度着学校假期的千百万儿童们都参加这个运动周。运动周开始以前，各地举行了许多儿童读者会议，儿童们在会议上提出他们最爱读的书籍，并且讨论作家最近的著作。在莫斯科儿童读物周的第一天，儿童们在职工会大厦举行了会议，他们在那儿会见了作家们，并且参观了儿童读物展览。各儿童俱乐部、文化宫和大企业，都给儿童们安排了诗歌朗诵会、音乐会，并演出最好的戏剧。
