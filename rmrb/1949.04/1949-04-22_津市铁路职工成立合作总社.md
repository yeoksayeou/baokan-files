Title: Tianjin Railway Workers Establish Cooperative General Association
Author:
Date: 1949-04-22
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 18th. The consumer cooperatives of 26 units affiliated with the Tianjin Railway Bureau, including the North Station, East Station, South Station, East Station Inspection Section, Tianjin Locomotive Factory, and East Station Locomotive Depot, have jointly established the Tianjin Railway Workers' Consumer Cooperative General Association. The General Association has a total of 42,690 members, including 10,330 employees and 32,360 employee family members. Previously, the 26 units' cooperatives had distributed a total of 782,015 jin of grain to their members, generally at prices 10% lower than market prices.

### 津市铁路职工成立合作总社

1949-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市铁路职工成立合作总社
    【新华社天津十八日电】天津路局所属北站、东站、南站、东站检车段、天津机厂、东站机务段等二十六个单位的消费合作社已联合成立天津铁路职工消费合作总社，总社社员共四万二千六百九十人，其中职工一万零三百三十人，职工家属三万二千三百六十人。前此二十六个单位的合作社配售给社员的粮食共计已达七十八万二千零十五斤，一般较市价便宜百分之十。
