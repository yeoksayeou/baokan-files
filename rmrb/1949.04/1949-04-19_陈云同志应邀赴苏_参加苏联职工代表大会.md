Title: Comrade Chen Yun Invited to USSR to Attend Soviet Trade Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 1

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Radio Broadcast, 18th】 Moscow Dispatch: Chen Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, has arrived in Moscow at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Unions to attend the Tenth All-Union Trade Union Congress which is scheduled to open on the 19th. Delegations and representatives from the trade unions of Australia, Mongolia, Korea, Bulgaria, Austria, Romania, Albania, Hungary, and other countries have arrived in Moscow one after another to attend the congress.

### 陈云同志应邀赴苏　参加苏联职工代表大会

1949-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈云同志应邀赴苏
  　参加苏联职工代表大会
    【北平新华广播电台十八日广播】莫斯科讯：中华全国总工会主席陈云，应苏联职工会中央委员会的邀请已经到达莫斯科，参加正在十九号召开的全苏联职工会第十届代表大会。澳大利亚、蒙古、朝鲜、保加利亚、奥地利、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利等国家职工会的代表团和代表，已纷纷到达莫斯科参加大会。
