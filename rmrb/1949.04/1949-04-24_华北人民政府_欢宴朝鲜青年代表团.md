Title: North China People's Government Hosts Banquet for Korean Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 1

　　North China People's Government
  　Hosts Banquet for Korean Youth Delegation
    [This Newspaper's report] The North China People's Government hosted a banquet for the Korean Youth Delegation at 5:00 PM on the 21st. Attendees included the delegation leader Zhang Ruizhen, Li Jinshun, Li Chonggen, and others. Feng Wenbin, Li Chang, Song Yiping, Gao Tang, and other members of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League were invited to accompany them. Before the banquet, Chairman Dong Biwu had a cordial conversation with the comrades of the Korean Youth Delegation about the various aspects of the Korean people's democratic movement and youth life. Chairman Dong delivered a speech at the banquet, expressing his welcome and wishing the youth of China and Korea close unity. Zhang Jinzhen, the leader of the Korean Youth Delegation, also delivered a speech on the spot to express his gratitude.

### 华北人民政府　欢宴朝鲜青年代表团

1949-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府
  　欢宴朝鲜青年代表团
    【本报讯】华北人民政府于二十一日下午五时，宴请朝鲜青年代表团，到代表团团长张瑞振、李金顺、李重根等。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员冯文彬、李昌、宋一平、高棠等被邀作陪。宴前，董必武主席与朝鲜青年代表团诸同志，欢谈朝鲜人民民主运动及青年生活的各种情况。董主席并在宴会席上致词，表示欢迎之意，瑞中朝两国青年亲密团结并祝。朝鲜青年代表团团长张进振亦即席讲话表示谢意。
