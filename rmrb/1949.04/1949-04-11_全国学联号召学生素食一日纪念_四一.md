Title: All-China Students' Federation Calls on Students to Observe a Vegetarian Day in Commemoration of "April 1st"
Author:
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 10th—The All-China Students' Federation recently received a telegram from the Nanjing Area University and College "April 1st" Deceased Students Funeral Committee, which stated in brief: "The April 1st Massacre has aroused nationwide indignation. This committee has scheduled a memorial service for the deceased martyrs on the afternoon of the 11th of this month. We respectfully request that you observe a vegetarian diet for one day and observe three minutes of silence at noon for immediate mourning." The All-China Students' Federation hereby calls on students nationwide to observe a vegetarian diet for one day on the 11th, and observe three minutes of silence at noon (areas receiving the news can schedule a date after receiving the news) to show their mourning for the "April 1st" deceased students, and to support the Nanjing students in their struggle for genuine peace.

### 全国学联号召学生素食一日纪念“四一”

1949-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学联号召学生素食一日纪念“四一”
    【新华社北平十日电】中华全国学生联合会顷接南京区大学及专科以上学校“四一”死难同学治丧委员会的电报，略谓：“四一血案，举国震愤，本会订于本月十一日下午追悼死难烈士，敬乞于该日素食一天，中午默哀三分钟，即时追悼”。中华全国学生联合会，特号召全国同学于十一日素食一天，中午默哀三分钟（接讯地区可于接讯后订期举行之）以示对“四一”死难同学之追悼，并支持南京同学，争取真正和平之斗争。
