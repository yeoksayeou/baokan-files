Title: Stalin Elected Honorary Chairman at the Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League

Author:
Date: 1949-04-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 31st: Moscow Dispatch: The 11th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League (VLKSM), representing nine million members, opened on the 29th in the magnificent hall of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union in the Kremlin, where it usually convenes. After the opening of the congress by Nikolai Mikhailov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Young Communist League, the young men and women representatives erupted in stormy cheers: "Glory to our dear Stalin!" "Long live Stalin, the best friend of youth!" These slogans, shouted in dozens of languages of the Soviet nationalities, resounded throughout the venue. The congress elected the presidium of the congress and the honorary presidium, and elected Stalin as honorary chairman. The congress adopted the following agenda: the report of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, the report on the Central Auditing Commission, the report on the work of the Young Communist League in schools, revision of the regulations of the Young Communist League, and the election of the Central Committee and the Auditing Commission of the League.

### 苏联青年团代表大会上斯大林被选为荣誉主席

1949-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联青年团代表大会上斯大林被选为荣誉主席
    【新华社北平三十一日电】莫斯科讯：代表着九百万团员的苏联共产主义青年团第十一届代表大会，二十九日在克里姆林宫内苏联最高苏维埃通常开会的宏伟大厅中开幕。大会由共产主义青年团中央委员会书记尼科拉伊·米哈依诺夫揭幕后，男女青年代表们就发出了暴风雨似的欢呼：“光荣归于我们亲爱的斯大林！”“青年最好的朋友斯大林万岁！”这些用苏联各民族几十种语言呼喊的口号声，响彻会场。大会选出了大会主席团与名誉主席团，并选举斯大林为荣誉主席。大会通过日程如下：苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会的报告，关于中央监察委员会的报告，关于学校中共产主义青年团工作的报告，修改共产主义青年团的团规，选举团的中央委员会和监察委员会。
