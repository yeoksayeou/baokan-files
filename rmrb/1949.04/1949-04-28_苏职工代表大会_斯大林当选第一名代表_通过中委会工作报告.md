Title: Soviet Workers' Congress: Stalin Elected First Representative, Approves Central Committee Work Report
Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 3

　　Soviet Workers' Congress
  　Stalin Elected First Representative
  　Approves Central Committee Work Report
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 27th: Moscow dispatch: The discussion of the report of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Union at the Tenth Congress of the Trade Union, which lasted for four days, ended on the 23rd. A total of sixty-eight people spoke or made special reports during the discussion. Representatives of the central bodies of various foreign trade unions also delivered speeches at the meeting, expressing congratulations and hopes. Finally, Kuznetsov, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Union, made a concluding report. He pointed out that under the conditions of the Soviet Union's victorious advance towards communism, the role of trade unions as a school of communism has been infinitely increased. We must hold higher the banner of socialist competition to complete the Stalin's post-war five-year plan ahead of schedule, further improve labor productivity, and improve product quality. We have a responsibility to further improve the living standards of the working people and maximize the satisfaction of their daily life and cultural needs. Kuznetsov then expressed his sincere thanks to the fraternal representatives of the central bodies of various foreign trade unions on behalf of the congress. He said, "The participation of the fraternal delegations in the Tenth Congress of the Soviet Trade Union will be conducive to the further development of the world working class in its struggle for lasting peace and democracy." Subsequently, the congress unanimously adopted a resolution on the report of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Union, approving the political line and practical work of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Union. The meeting then began to review the second item on the agenda - the report on the trade union charter. The report was made by Osipov, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Union.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 27th: Moscow dispatch: Popova, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the Soviet Trade Union, reported on the 21st at the Tenth Congress of the Soviet Trade Union on behalf of the Credentials Committee that Stalin, the leader of the Soviet people, was elected as the first representative. Stalin's comrades-in-arms were also elected as representatives to the congress. A total of one thousand three hundred and forty-three representatives were elected to attend the congress, representing 28.5 million members of the Soviet Trade Union (in 1938, the number of members of the Soviet Trade Union was 23.8 million). The representatives attending the congress included thirty-two nationalities, fifty-one Heroes of the Soviet Union and Heroes of Socialist Labor, twenty-eight Stalin Prize winners, and many well-known Stakhanovite movement activists. Among the representatives attending the congress were five hundred and seventeen women.

### 苏职工代表大会　斯大林当选第一名代表　通过中委会工作报告

1949-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏职工代表大会
  　斯大林当选第一名代表
  　通过中委会工作报告
    【新华社北平二十七日电】莫斯科讯：苏联职工会第十届代表大会对该会中央委员会报告的讨论，历时四日，已于二十三日结束，在讨论中发言或作专题报告的共有六十八人。各外国职工会中央机构的代表，也在会上致词，表示祝贺和希望。最后由苏联职工会中央委员会主席库兹涅佐夫作总结报告。他指出：在苏联胜利地迈向共产主义的条件下，职工会作为共产主义学校的作用业已无限量地增大了。我们必须把提早完成斯大林战后五年计划、进一步提高劳动生产率、提高产品质量的社会主义竞赛的旗帜更高地举起来。我们有责任进一步提高劳动人民的生活水准，最大限度地满足他们日常生活的与文化的需要。库兹涅佐夫继即代表大会向各外国职工会中央机构的兄弟代表们表示诚挚的谢意。他说：“各兄弟代表团参加苏联职工会第十届代表大会，将有利于世界工人阶级争取永久和平与民主的更进一步发展。”接着，大会一致通过关于苏联职工会中央委员会报告的决议，批准苏联职工会中央委员会的政治路线与实际工作。会议继即开始审议议程的第二项——关于职工会章程的报告。该报告是由苏联职工会中央委员会书记之一奥希波夫所作。
    【新华社北平二十七日电】莫斯科讯：苏联职工会中央委员会书记之一波波娃，二十一日在苏联职工会第十届代表大会上，代表资格审查委员会报告说：苏联人民的领袖斯大林被选为第一名代表。斯大林的战友们也被选为出席大会的代表。被选举出席大会的共有一千三百四十三名代表，他们代表着苏维埃职工会二千八百五十万会员（在一九三八年，苏联职工会会员数为二千三百八十万）。参加大会的代表，包括三十二个民族，有五十一位苏联英雄和社会主义劳动英雄，二十八位斯大林奖金荣获者，许多著名的斯塔哈诺夫运动者。出席大会的代表当中有五百十七位妇女。
