Title: Taiyuan Shanxi Daily Published; Xinhua News Agency Taiyuan Branch Enters City to Send稿 (manuscripts/news reports)
Author:
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 3

　　Taiyuan Shanxi Daily Published
　　Xinhua News Agency Taiyuan Branch Enters City to Send 稿
    [Taiyuan Branch Telegram] Shanxi Daily was published in Taiyuan on the morning of the 26th. The newspaper is a four-page broadsheet. The Taiyuan Branch of Xinhua News Agency entered the city and began sending 稿 (manuscripts/news reports) on the day of Taiyuan's liberation. The Taiyuan Xinhua Broadcasting Station is rushing to repair its machinery. Once the power is on, it can begin broadcasting.

### 太原山西日报出版  新华社太原分社入城发稿

1949-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　太原山西日报出版
  　新华社太原分社入城发稿
    【太原分社电】山西日报二十六日晨在太原出版，该报系对开四版，新华社太原分社则于太原解放的当日，已入城发稿，太原新华广播电台，正在抢修机件，电力一到，即可开始播音。
