Title: Current Exchange Rate for Commodity-linked Savings
Author:
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 2
　　
　　Commodity-linked Savings　Today's Exchange Rate
    The Savings Department of the People's Bank of China, Beiping Branch, announces that the standard unit exchange rate for commodity-linked savings for today (the 7th) is 90.90 yuan.
