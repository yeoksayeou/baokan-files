Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government Establishes Office in Beiping
Author: Yang Ping
Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 1

　　Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government Establishes Office in Beiping

　　[This newspaper dispatch] In order to assist the Beiping Military Control Commission in handling various issues related to Inner Mongolia, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government has decided to establish a resident office in Beiping. Gao Boze Bu, Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of the government, has been appointed as the director of the office. The office has been registered with the Beiping Military Control Commission and was officially established on the 5th of this month, starting operations at 38 Dafosi West Street, Beiping.
    (Yang Ping)

### 内蒙自治政府平办事处成立
杨平
1949-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　内蒙自治政府平办事处成立
    【本报讯】内蒙自治政府为协助平市军管会处理有关内蒙古各项问题，决定成立该府驻平办事处，任命该府农牧部高博泽布部长为办事处主任。该处已经平市军管会备案，于本月五日正式成立，开始在平市大佛寺西街三十八号办公。
    （杨平）
