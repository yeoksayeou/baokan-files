Title: CCP Central Committee Decides to Appoint Nie Rongzhen as Peace Talk Representative; Nanjing Reactionary Government Delegation Arrives in Beiping Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 1

　　CCP Central Committee Decides
　　Add Nie Rongzhen as a Representative for Peace Talks
　　Nanjing Reactionary Government Delegation Arrives in Beiping Yesterday
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 1st] The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to appoint Nie Rongzhen as a representative to hold peace talks with the Nanjing Kuomintang government delegation, and has decided to appoint Qi Yanming as the secretary-general of the delegation.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 1st] The Nanjing Kuomintang reactionary government's peace delegation, consisting of Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Zhang Shizhao, Huang Shao＠, Li Zheng, Liu Fei, and 19 advisors and staff members, arrived in Beiping at 3:10 pm today. The delegation's secretary-general, Lu Yuwen, and his party of ten arrived yesterday afternoon.

### 中共中央决定  加派聂荣臻为和谈代表　南京反动政府代表团昨抵平

1949-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央决定
    加派聂荣臻为和谈代表
  　南京反动政府代表团昨抵平
    【新华社北平一日电】中共中央决定加派聂荣臻为与南京国民党政府代表团举行和平谈判的代表，并决定以齐燕铭为代表团秘书长。
    【新华社北平一日电】南京国民党反动政府和谈代表团张治中、邵力子、章士钊、黄绍＠、李蒸、刘斐及顾问随员等一行十九人于本日下午三时十分到达北平，该代表团秘书长卢郁文等一行十人已于昨日下午到达。
