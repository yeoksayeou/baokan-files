Title: Inter-regional Exchange Rate Increased by 100 Percent
Author: 
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 2

　　Inter-regional Exchange Rate Increased by 100 Percent
　　[This newspaper] The exchange rate for inter-regional remittances at the Bank of China is revised today (the 25th) to 1 to 2,000, doubling the previous rate (the 23rd).

### 区外汇兑上提百分之百

1949-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑上提百分之百
    【本报讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价今日（二十五日）改订为一比二、○○○，较上次（二十三日）再提高一倍。
