Title: Central Plains Liberates Guangshui Station; Hanchuan and Yingshan County Towns Both Captured
Author:
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 6th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Guangshui Station, an important town south of Wusheng Pass on the southern section of the Pinghan Railway, on the 3rd, annihilating a part of the Kuomintang army's First Army Group, Twenty-ninth Army. Sixteen artillery pieces of various types were seized. Detailed battle results are still being investigated.
    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 6th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Hanchuan County, west of Hankou, on the 26th of last month, capturing more than 100 enemies. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Yingshan County, southwest of Xinyang, on the 30th of last month, capturing Peng Jiwu, the deputy commander of the 48th Regiment of the Henan Security Forces, and more than 30 others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, 6th: The People's Liberation Army captured Tuqiao and Liujiadu, southwest of Wuwei on the north bank of the Yangtze River, on the 2nd, annihilating a battalion, two companies, and a machine gun platoon of Kuomintang bandit troops.

### 中原解放广水车站　汉川应山两县城同告攻克

1949-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原解放广水车站
  　汉川应山两县城同告攻克
    【新华社中原六日电】人民解放军于三日解放平汉路南段武胜关以南重镇广水车站，歼灭国民党军第一兵团二十九军一部，缴获各种炮十六门，详细战果正清查中。
    【新华社中原六日电】人民解放军于上月二十六日解放汉口以西的汉川县城，俘敌一百余人。解放军另部于上月三十日解放信阳西南的应山县城，俘敌河南保安四十八团团附彭继武以下三十余人。
    【新华社蚌埠六日电】解放军于二日攻克无为西南长江北岸的土桥和刘家渡，共歼灭国民党匪军一个营、两个连和一个机枪排。
