Title: Huiwen Holds Charity Basketball Game to Raise Funds to Aid Impoverished Students
Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 2

　　【Ping City News】The charity basketball fundraising tournament to aid impoverished students, sponsored by the Sports Committee of Huiwen Middle School in this city, will be held at 2:00 PM this afternoon (2nd) in the school's gymnasium. It is reported that various strong teams in the city have been invited to participate.

### 募款救济清寒同学　汇文举行篮球义赛

1949-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　募款救济清寒同学　汇文举行篮球义赛
    【平市讯】本市汇文中学体育委员会主办之救济清寒同学篮球募捐义赛，今（二）日下午两点在该校体育馆举行。闻本市各劲旅均被邀参加。
