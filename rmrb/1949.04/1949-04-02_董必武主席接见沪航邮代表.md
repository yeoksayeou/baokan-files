Title: Chairman Dong Biwu Receives Representatives from Shanghai Aviation and Postal Services

Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 1

　　North China One Dispatch: Mr. Lei Zhongren, representative of the Shanghai aviation industry, and Mr. Mei Yi@, representative of the postal service, along with a delegation of six, arrived in Beiping yesterday to discuss air and postal services with the Liberated Areas. Chairman Dong Biwu of the North China People's Government received them at 6:00 PM on the first.

### 董必武主席接见沪航邮代表

1949-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　董必武主席接见沪航邮代表
    【华北一日电】与解放区洽商通航、通邮之沪航空界代表雷仲仁与邮政界代表梅贻＠等一行六人，于昨日抵平。华北人民政府董必武主席于一日下午六时予以接见。
