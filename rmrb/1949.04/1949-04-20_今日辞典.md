　　Today's Dictionary
    Kilogram - is a unit of measurement commonly used in all countries around the world. Its standard is the weight of one cubic decimeter of water at four degrees Celsius (because the volume of water is smallest at four degrees Celsius). One kilogram is equal to two Chinese "jin."

    公斤——是世界各国通用的衡度的一种。它的标准是一立方公寸水在寒暑表摄氏四度时的重量（因水的体积在摄氏四度时最小）。一公斤合中国二市斤。
