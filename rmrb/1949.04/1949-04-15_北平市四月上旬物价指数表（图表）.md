Author:
Date: 1949-04-15
Page: 2

　　Price Index Table of Beiping City in Early April
　　
　　Commodity　　　　　　Index
　　Food Grains　　　　　１６２.９０
　　Non-staple Food　　　　１１９.６０
　　Cotton Cloth　　　　　１３４.４５
　　Sundry Goods　　　　　１０２.５５
　　Dyes　　　　　　　　　７８.５０
　　Fuels　　　　　　　　１５３.１０
　　Other Industrial Products　１４５.８０
　　General Index　　　　１２８.１３
    
    Note:
    １. This index uses the average price in February 1949 as the base period = 100.
    ２. This index is calculated using a simple geometric mean.
    ３. The categories include the following types of goods:
    　Ａ. Grains – Tongfen noodles, wheat, rice, millet, Yuan corn, corn flour, sorghum, sesame.
    　Ｂ. Non-staple food – sesame oil, white oil, edible salt, pork, dry vermicelli, chicken eggs.
    　Ｃ. Cotton cloth – Second Factory Five Blessings Cloth, Flying Green, Flying Blue, 32-count Shi Quan yarn, 20-count San Ding yarn, cotton.
    　Ｄ. Sundry goods – Mobil kerosene, Xiang Feng matches, 45-vehicle white sugar, brown sugar, Triangle soda ash, Ding Guang soap, Elephant cigarettes.
    　Ｅ. Dyes – Gongzi vulcanized green, Red Elephant vulcanized green.
    　Ｆ. Fuels – Mentougou lump coal, Mentougou raw coal, Kailuan raw coal.
    　Ｇ. Other industrial products – newsprint, pink stationery, Shengli tires, Warrior sneakers.
