Title: Radio Communication Between Beiping and Nanjing Increased to Four Hours Daily; Telegram Service Between Beiping and Taiyuan Restored; Long-Distance Telephone Service Between Beiping and Zhangjiakou Open
Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 2

　　Radio Communication Between Beiping and Nanjing
  　Daily Increased to Four Hours
    Telegram Service Between Beiping and Taiyuan Restored
  　Long-Distance Telephone Service Between Beiping and Zhangjiakou Open
    [Report from this newspaper] Following the liberation of Nanjing and Taiyuan, the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau decided to increase radio communication between Beiping and Nanjing by two hours, making it a total of four hours (from 10:00 to 12:00 and 14:00 to 16:00). Telegram service with Taiyuan has also been restored. Wireless telephone service will be restored in a day or two, once the lines in Taiyuan City are repaired. The Beiping Postal Administration has also announced the rapid restoration of transportation between Beiping and Taiyuan, which will be directly transported via the Zhengtai Railway. In addition, telegram and telephone services to Qingdao, Hankou, and Xi'an in the Kuomintang-controlled areas have been temporarily suspended and will be rapidly restored after liberation.
    [Another report] Long-distance telephone service between Beiping and Zhangjiakou has now been repaired and opened for express business calls starting from the 26th.

### 北平南京间无线电通话　每日增至四小时  平并间电报亦已恢复　平张间长途电话通话

1949-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平南京间无线电通话
  　每日增至四小时
    平并间电报亦已恢复
  　平张间长途电话通话
    【本报讯】南京、太原解放后，北平电讯局决定北平、南京、间无线电通话增加两小时，连过去一共四小时（从十时到十二时，十四时至十六时）。与太原间电报亦已恢复。无线电话俟一两天太原市线路修好，即可恢复。北平邮政管理局也宣布迅速恢复平并间交通运输，将由正太铁路直接运递。此外，国民党统治区青岛、汉口、西安三地电报、电话，均暂告中断，解放之后也将迅速恢复。
    【又讯】北平与张家口长途电话现已修通，已于二十六日起开放特快商话。
