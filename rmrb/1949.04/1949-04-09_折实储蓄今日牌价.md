Title: Indexed Savings Account Exchange Rate Today
Author:
Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 2

　　Indexed Savings Account　Today's Exchange Rate
    The People's Bank of Beiping Branch Savings Department announced that today's (9th) standard unit exchange rate for indexed savings is 92.10 yuan.
