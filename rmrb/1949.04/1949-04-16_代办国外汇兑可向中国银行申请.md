Title: Foreign Exchange Services Can Be Applied for at the Bank of China
Author:
Date: 1949-04-16
Page: 2

　　Foreign Exchange Services
  　Can Be Applied for at the Bank of China
    [Ping City News] The Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China stipulates that all banks in the city with branches or agencies abroad, which handle foreign exchange with a reputation for good faith, and are willing to abide by all policies and decrees of the People's Government in accordance with the "Provisional Measures for Foreign Exchange Management in North China," can go to the Bank of China in this city to collect application forms and apply for foreign exchange business.

### 代办国外汇兑可向中国银行申请

1949-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　代办国外汇兑
  　可向中国银行申请
    【平市讯】中国人民银行北平分行规定凡在国外有分支行或代理机构，经营外汇向著信誉之本市各银行，按照华北区外汇管理暂行办法，愿遵守人民政府一切政策法令者，均可于最近前往本市中国银行领取申请书，申请代办国外汇兑业务。
