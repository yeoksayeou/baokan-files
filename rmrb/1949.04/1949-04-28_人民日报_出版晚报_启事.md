Title: People's Daily to Publish Evening News Announcement

Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 1

　　People's Daily
  　Publishing Evening News
  　Announcement
  　(1)
    In order to meet the requirements of the broad readership, this newspaper will launch "People's Evening News" on May 1st. It will be published daily, four pages in size. In addition to reporting important news from home and abroad, as well as North China and this city, it will also publish news from factories, schools, municipal administration, commerce, etc., as well as entertainment programs, advertisements, announcements, etc. In order to help workers, students, shop assistants, and citizens understand various policies and laws, a special column will be opened to answer various questions related to learning and life. We hope that people from all walks of life will contribute excellent works from time to time and offer advice!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　People's Daily Editorial Office
    (2)
    This newspaper will publish "People's Evening News" on May 1st, with four pages daily. Subscriptions are welcome from all sectors.
    Price: Five yuan per issue, one hundred and fifty yuan per month. Subscribers please contact the newspaper's distribution department and offices.
    Telephone number: 2-4173
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　People's Daily Business Department

### 人民日报　出版晚报　启事

1949-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民日报
  　出版晚报
  　启事
  　（一）
    本报为适应广大读者要求，定于五月一日创刊“人民晚报”，日出四开一张，除报导国内外及华北、本市要闻外，并刊登工厂、学校、市政、商业等新闻及娱乐节目、广告、启事等，为帮助工人、学生、店员及市民了解各种政策法令，将辟专栏，解答有关学习及生活上之各种问题。尚希各界时赐佳作，并提赐意见！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　人民日报总编室
    （二）
    本报于五月一日出版“人民晚报”，每日四开一张，欢迎各界订阅。
    价目：每期五元，每月一百五十元。定阅者请向本报发行部及办事处接洽。
    电话号码二，四一七三　
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　人民日报经理部
