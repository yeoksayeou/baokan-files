Title: Communist Party of the Philippines and People's Guerrilla Headquarters Voice Support for Printing Workers' Strike
Author:
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: According to a Reuters dispatch: CPP General Secretary Balgos and Philippine People's Guerrilla Commander Taruc simultaneously issued statements in support of the major strike by Philippine printing factory workers. They paid tribute to the strikers, pointing out that their current struggle is part of the common struggle against imperialism. It is reported that the major strike by Philippine printing workers began on the 11th, involving nineteen printing factories.

### 菲共与人民游击队总部  声援印刷工人罢工

1949-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　菲共与人民游击队总部
  　声援印刷工人罢工
    【新华社北平二十四日电】据路透社讯：菲共总书记巴尔哥斯与菲人民游击队总司令塔鲁克同时发表声明，声援菲印刷厂工人的大罢工。他们向罢工者致敬，指出他们当前的斗争，乃是反对帝国主义共同斗争的一部分。按菲印刷工人大罢工开始于十一日，参加的有十九个印刷厂。
