Title: Ping City Student Federation Executive Committee Initiates the Organization of the City Youth Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-11
Page: 1

　　Ping City Student Federation Executive Committee
  　Initiates the Organization of the City Youth Federation
    [This Newspaper News] The Beiping Student Federation held its sixth executive committee meeting yesterday morning (the 10th). It was resolved to initiate the organization of the Beiping City Youth Federation to further unite the city's young workers, students, women, professional youth, and Youth League members. It was also decided to unite the city's youth to prepare for an expanded commemoration of the first "May Fourth" Youth Day after the liberation of Beiping. The meeting requested the Youth Workers Department of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, the Young Women's Department of the Municipal Women's Federation Preparatory Committee, the Youth League Beiping Municipal Preparatory Committee, and other professional youth organizations to quickly select preparatory committee members to jointly establish the Beiping Municipal Democratic Youth Federation Preparatory Committee with the preparatory committee members selected by the Federation.

### 平市学联执委会发起组织市青联

1949-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市学联执委会
  　发起组织市青联
    【本报讯】北平学联昨（十）日上午开第六次执委会。决议发起组织北平市青年联合会，以进一步团结全市青年工人、学生、妇女、职业青年及青年团员。并决定联合全市青年，筹备扩大纪念北平解放后的第一个“五四”青年节。会议要求市总工会筹委会青工部、市妇联筹委会青年妇女部、青年团北平市筹委会及其他职业青年团体赶快选出筹备委员，与该会选出的筹备委员共同成立北平市民主青年联合会筹委会。
