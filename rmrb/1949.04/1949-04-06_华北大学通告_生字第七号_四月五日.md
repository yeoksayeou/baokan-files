　　North China University Notice 　 Sheng Zi No. 7 April 5th
    The office location of the Foreign Language Research Office of our university, which is recruiting Russian translators, was originally at No. 6, East Fourth Street. It has now been moved to No. 31 Qianmian Hutong. Those interested in Russian translation are kindly requested to contact us there.

1949-04-06

　　华北大学通告 　 生字第七号　四月五日
