Title: Severe Protest by Tianjin News Circles Against French Government's Restrictions on Entry of Peace Representatives
Author:
Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 1

[Report from this newspaper] Tianjin news circles issued a letter of protest against the unreasonable actions of the French reactionary government in restricting the entry of peace representatives. The original text is as follows:

To Mr. Aragon, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Paris:

We are extremely indignant at the unreasonable act of the French government in limiting the number of representatives sent by various countries to Paris to attend the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, and we hereby express our serious protest to the French government.

The Congress of Supporters of Peace, held in Paris on behalf of peace-loving people all over the world, demonstrates the deep hatred of the people of all countries for aggressive war and their strong determination to defend peace. Any sabotage and obstruction cannot weaken but only strengthen this hatred and determination. The French government's decision to restrict the entry of peace representatives from various countries is completely unreasonable and reactionary. It only exposes the fear of peace by the French reactionaries and exposes the French government's complete obedience to Washington.

We demand that the French government immediately revoke this decision to limit the number of peace representatives allowed to enter the country. At the same time, we hope that the French people, in order to maintain the traditional spirit of freedom-loving of the French nation, will unite to resist and urge the French government to abolish this restriction, reject the instructions of the American reactionaries, allow representatives from various countries to enter the country, and launch the great work of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Paris.

Opposing aggressive war and defending world peace is the unanimous demand of the people of the world today. Any sabotage or obstruction cannot block the unity of the world's peace forces. Chinese journalists, and the Chinese people as a whole, stand completely with the representatives attending the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. We believe that your great work will surely be completed victoriously, so that all new war aggression and provocation conspiracies will be defeated.

Signatories (arranged by the number of strokes in the surname): Wang Chun, Wang Yunsheng, Wang Daren, Wang Dingkun, Shi Ximin, Zhu Muzhi, An Gang, Jiang Heng, Du Bo, Di Chaobai, Li Zhuang, Li Yaqun, Li Qianfeng, Jin Zhonghua, Zhou You, Wu Lengxi, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Hu Zhongchi, Hong Yilong, Ma Jianmin, Fan Changjiang, Xu Maijin, Xu Yian, Xu Zhucheng, Yuan Bo, Gao Zuchang, Chen Kehan, Chen Shi'wu, Zhang Rong, Mei Yi, Zhang Panshi, Zhang Gengsheng, Zhang Longdong, Zhang Zhaolin, Feng Shiyun, Peng Zigang, Yang Gang, Huang Zhuoming, Sun Chengpei, Li Shu, Zhao Yimin, Zhao Xiaozhang, Zhao Chaogou, Liu Xiling, Liu Shiping, Liu Zunqi, Han Jingfeng, Xie Gongwang, Chu Anping, Sa Kongliao, Wei Keming, Xiao Feng, Gu Ming.

### 对法政府限制和平代表入境　平市新闻界严重抗议

1949-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　对法政府限制和平代表入境
  　平市新闻界严重抗议
    【本报讯】平市新闻界为反对法国反动政府限制和平代表入境事，发表抗议书，原文如下：
    巴黎世界拥护和平大会筹委会主席阿拉贡先生：
    我们对于法国政府限制各国派来巴黎出席世界拥护和平大会的代表人数这一无理举动，感到极大的愤慨，为此要向法国政府表示严重的抗议。
    代表全世界爱好和平人士在巴黎举行的拥护和平大会，显示着世界各国人民对于侵略战争的深刻憎恨与保卫和平的坚强决心。任何破坏与阻挠，都不能减弱而只能加强这种憎恨和决心。法国政府限制各国和平代表入境这一决定，是完全无理的和反动的，它只有暴露法国反动派的害怕和平，暴露法国政府完全听命于华盛顿。
    我们要求法国政府立即撤消这个限制和平代表入境人数的决定。同时，我们希望法兰西人民，为了保持法兰西民族爱好自由的传统精神，一致起来抗争，促使法国政府取消这种限制，拒绝美国反动派的指使，允许各国代表入境，展开巴黎世界拥护和平大会的伟大工作。
    反对侵略战争，保卫世界和平，是今天世界人民的一致要求。任何破坏阻挠，都不能阻隔世界人民和平力量的团结。中国的新闻记者，中国全国人民，完全和出席世界拥护和平大会的代表站在一起。我们相信你们的伟大工作一定能够胜利完成，使所有新战争侵略的挑拨阴谋都归于失败。
    签名者（以姓的笔划排列）：王春、王芸生、王达仁、王定坤、石西民、朱穆之、安岗、江横、杜波、狄超白、李庄、李亚群、李千峰、金仲华、周游、吴冷西、胡乔木、胡愈之、胡仲持、洪一龙、马建民、范长江、徐迈进、徐亦安、徐铸成、袁勃、高祖长、陈克寒、陈适五、章容、梅益、张磐石、张更生、张隆栋、张兆麟、冯诗云、彭子冈、杨刚、黄卓明、孙承佩、黎澍、赵毅敏、赵孝章、赵超构、刘希玲、刘实平、刘尊棋、韩劲风、谢公望、储安平、萨空了，魏克明、萧风、顾明。
