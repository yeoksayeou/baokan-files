Title: Hong Kong's Wah Kiu Yat Po, Wen Wei Po, and Ta Kung Pao Initiate Anti-War Petition in Response to the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 9th: Hong Kong News: In response to the Paris World Peace Congress, Hong Kong's Wah Kiu Yat Po, Wen Wei Po, and Ta Kung Pao are jointly launching a signature campaign against the U.S. imperialists' instigation of a new world war. The signed petitions will be submitted to the World Peace Congress, which is scheduled to open in Paris on April 20th. Signatories pledge not to cooperate with those responsible for the war (should war break out). They also call on the Paris Peace Congress to establish a permanent organization to maintain peace.

### 香港华商报文汇报大公报　发起签名反战　响应世界拥护和平大会

1949-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　香港华商报文汇报大公报
  　发起签名反战
  　响应世界拥护和平大会
    【新华社北平九日电】香港消息：香港华商报、文汇报、大公报为响应巴黎世界拥护和平大会，正在联合发动反对美帝国主义发动新世界战争的签名运动。签名书将送交行将于四月二十日在巴黎开幕的世界拥护和平大会。签名者保证决不与应对战争负责的人合作（假如战争爆发的话）。并要求巴黎和平大会成立维护和平的永久性组织。
