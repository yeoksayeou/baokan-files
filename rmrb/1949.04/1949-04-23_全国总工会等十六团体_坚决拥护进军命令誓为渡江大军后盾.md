Title: Sixteen National Organizations Including the All-China Federation of Trade Unions: Firmly Support the Advance Order, Vow to Be the Strong Backing for the Yangtze River Crossing Army
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, April 22nd: Sixteen people's organizations, including the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, in response to the order issued yesterday by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu to the People's Liberation Army, specifically representing the national workers, youth, women, and cultural education circles, jointly sent a telegram to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army field armies, expressing their firm support for the order of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and vowing to be the strong backing for the People's Liberation Army which is crossing the Yangtze River to liberate all of China. The original text of the telegram is as follows:

To all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army field armies:

The order issued by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on April 21st to thoroughly and completely eliminate the reactionary troops and bring the war criminals to justice is entirely in line with the wishes and interests of the people of the whole country.

We, representing the sixteen people's organizations from the national workers, youth, women, and cultural education circles, enthusiastically respond to this order and call on the people of all circles throughout the country to support this great order with all their strength. We firmly believe that with the unanimous support of the people of the whole country, the victory of the Liberation Army in eliminating the Kuomintang reactionary troops and liberating all of China will soon be realized.

Respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu!

Respects to all the heroic commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army!

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation of Liberated Areas, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the China Association for Scientific Workers, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the North China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Association of Academic Workers, the China Liberated Areas Journalists Association, the China Young Journalists Society, the Shanghai People's Organizations Joint Committee, the Peking University Professors Association, the Tsinghua University Professors Association, the Yenching University Professors Association, the Normal University Professors Association, the Preparatory Committee of the Peiping Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff Union.

### 全国总工会等十六团体　坚决拥护进军命令誓为渡江大军后盾

1949-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会等十六团体
  　坚决拥护进军命令誓为渡江大军后盾
    【新华社北平二十二日电】中华全国总工会等十六个人民团体，为响应毛主席、朱总司令昨日向人民解放军发布的命令，特代表全国工人、青年、妇女及文化教育界，联合致电中国人民解放军各野战军全体指战员，表示坚决拥护毛主席、朱总司令的命令，誓为正在南渡长江解放全中国的人民解放军的后盾。兹志其原电如下：
    人民解放军各野战军全体指战员：
    毛主席、朱总司令于四月二十一日向人民解放军发布的彻底、完全地消灭反动军队、缉拿战争罪犯归案法办的命令，是完全符合全国人民的愿望和利益的。
    我们代表全国工人、青年、妇女及文化教育各界的十六个人民团体，热烈地响应这个命令，并号召全国各界人民，以一切力量来拥护这个伟大的命令，并且深信解放大军在全国人民的一致拥护下，消灭国民党反动军队、解放全中国的胜利，很快就能实现。
    向毛主席、朱总司令致敬！
    向英勇的人民解放军全体指战员致敬！
    中华全国总工会、中国解放区青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华科学工作者协会、中华全国文艺协会、华北文艺界协会、中国学术工作者协会、中国解放区新闻记者联合会、中国青年记者学会、上海人民团体联合会、北京大学教授联谊会、清华大学教授联谊会、燕京大学教授联谊会、师范大学教授会、北平市中小学教职员联合会筹委会。
