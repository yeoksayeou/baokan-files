Title: Socialist Unity Party of Germany Issues May Day Call: Fight for Peace and Oppose War; French Laboring People Organize May Day Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1949-04-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: TASS, Berlin: The Socialist Unity Party of Germany recently issued a May Day appeal. The Socialist Unity Party calls on the German people to fight for Germany's unity and a just peace, and against the instigators of imperialist war. It calls for solidarity and friendship with the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries. The May Day appeal also protests against the "Ruhr Statute" and the "Occupation Statute," and against the colonial enslavement of West Germany. The Socialist Unity Party calls on the working people in the Soviet occupation zone to actively participate in the struggle to complete and surpass the two-year plan for developing the economy of the Soviet occupation zone.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st: TASS, Paris: The Executive Bureau of the French General Confederation of Labor met on the 30th to prepare for the May Day celebration and called on the French working people to organize powerful May Day demonstrations. These demonstrations will be carried out under the following slogans: fight for peace and friendship between all countries, realize the economic and social demands of the working class, oppose unemployment and the "Marshall Plan," fight for national independence, oppose reaction, defend democracy and trade union freedom, release the miners and all working people who have been sentenced to imprisonment for exercising the right to strike, and strive for the fraternal unity of the French and world working class.

### 德统一社会党“五一”号召　争取和平反对战争  法劳动人民布置五一大示威

1949-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　德统一社会党“五一”号召
  　争取和平反对战争
    法劳动人民布置五一大示威
    【新华社北平六日电】塔斯社柏林讯：德国统一社会党顷发表五一节号召。统一社会党号召德国人民为争取德国的统一及公正的和平，反对帝国主义战争的挑拨者而斗争，号召与苏联及人民民主国家团结友好。五一号召并抗议“鲁尔法规”与“占领法规”，抗议西德的殖民地奴役。统一社会党号召苏占区的劳动人民积极参加斗争，完成并超过发展苏占区经济的两年计划。
    【新华社北平一日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国总工会执行局三十日开会筹备纪念五一节，并号召法国劳动人民布置强大的五一示威。这些示威将在下列口号下进行：争取和平与各国间的友谊，实现工人阶级的经济与社会要求，反对失业和“马歇尔计划”，争取民族独立，反对反动，保卫民主与工会的自由，释放因使用罢工权而被判处徒刑的矿工和一切劳动人民，争取法国与全世界工人阶级的兄弟团结。
