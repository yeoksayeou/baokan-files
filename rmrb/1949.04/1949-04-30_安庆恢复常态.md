Title: Anqing Returns to Normal

Author:

Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Anqing, 29th. In the newly liberated city of Anqing, the electric power company and the water plant are supplying electricity and water as usual. More than forty primary and secondary schools have mostly resumed classes. Anhui University also moved back into the city on the 24th to prepare for the resumption of classes. "Anqing News" was published on the 25th.

### 安庆恢复常态

1949-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　安庆恢复常态
    【新华社安庆二十九日电】新解放的安庆城，电灯公司，自来水厂照常供应水电，四十余中小学已大部复课，安徽大学亦于二十四日搬回城内，准备复课“安庆新闻”已于二十五日出版。
