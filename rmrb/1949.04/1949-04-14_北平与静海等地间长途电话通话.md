Title: Long-Distance Telephone Service Between Beiping and Jinghai and Other Places

Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 2

　　Long-Distance Telephone Service Between Beiping and Jinghai and Other Places
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Beiping Telecommunications Bureau announced yesterday: Wired long-distance telephone service between Beiping and Jinghai, Yangcun, Beitang, and Hangu officially opened at 00:00 on the 13th of this month. Each call will be three minutes, costing 72 yuan in People's Currency (78 yuan for Jinghai). It is fast, economical, accurate, and convenient. All users are encouraged to take advantage of it.

### 北平与静海等地间长途电话通话

1949-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平与静海等地间
  　长途电话通话
    【本报讯】北平电信局昨发表：北平与静海、杨村、北塘、汉沽四地间有线长途电话，自本月十三日零时起正式通话，每次三分钟，计人民券七十二元（静海每次七十八元）。迅速经济，准确便利，请各界用户注意利用。
