Title: Dalian University Opens; Current Student Body at 1,300

Author:

Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, April 26th: Dalian University, after several months of preparation, officially opened on the 15th of this month. The university is formed by merging the former Industrial Specialized School, Electrical Specialized School, Medical College, Russian Language Specialized School, Scientific Research Institute, and Hygiene Research Institute. It currently has two colleges – Engineering and Medicine – and a Russian language specialization course, focusing on cultivating talent in machinery, electrical engineering, communications engineering, mining and metallurgy, civil engineering, and medical and health workers. The duration of study is two years for preparatory courses, three years for undergraduate courses, and two years for the Russian language specialization course. The university currently has 1,300 students.

### 大连大学开学　现有学生千三百名

1949-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　大连大学开学
  　现有学生千三百名
    【新华社大连二十六日电】大连大学经数月筹备，已于本月十五日开学。该校系由原工业专门学校、电气专门学校、医学院和俄语专门学校、科学研究院、卫生研究所等合并组成。暂设工学、医学两院及俄语专修科，着重培养机械、电气、通讯工程、采矿冶金、土木工程等人材和医疗卫生工作者。修业年限为预科二年，本科三年，俄语专修科二年。全校现有学生一千三百名。
