Title: The People's Will! Wanted: Chen Daqing
Author:
Date: 1949-04-01
Page: 1

　　The People's Will!
  　Wanted: Chen Daqing
    [Report from this newspaper] The crime of the Kuomintang bandit gang in shooting and killing Shanghai transportation workers Gu Bokang, Zhong Quanzhou, and Wang Yuan on February 17th has aroused angry protests from workers, students, and people from all walks of life in Beiping, demanding severe punishment for the murderers, as reported in previous newspapers. Now, the Jibei Electric Power Company, the United University Alumni Association, the Tsinghua University Electrical Engineering Society, the Tsinghua University Student Union, the Southwest Associated University School of Science and Engineering Class of 1945, and eleven other academic organizations, along with Wang Dachun and sixty-five others, have suggested to the North China People's Government that Chen Daqing, the pseudo-Songhu Garrison Commander and one of the murderers of the massacre, be placed on the list of war criminals and ordered to be arrested and brought to justice.

### 人民公意！　通缉陈大庆

1949-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民公意！
  　通缉陈大庆
    【本报讯】国民党匪帮二月十七日枪杀上海交通工人顾伯康、钟泉周、王元三人的罪行，曾激起北平工人、学生及各界人民愤怒抗议，要求严惩凶手，已志前报。兹又有冀北电力公司、联大校友会、清华大学电机工程学会、清华大学学生自治会，西南联大理工学院一九四五级级会等十一个学术团体及王大纯等六十五人向华北人民政府建议，将惨案的凶手之一伪淞沪警备司令陈大庆列入战犯名单，通令严缉归案。
