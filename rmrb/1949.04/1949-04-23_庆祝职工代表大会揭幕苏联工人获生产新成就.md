Title: Celebrating the Opening of the Trade Union Congress, Soviet Workers Achieve New Production Successes
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, April 21st: TASS reports: To celebrate the opening of the Tenth Congress of Soviet Trade Unions, Soviet workers have achieved new successes in the labor competition initiated for this purpose. Miners in many mines in Kuznetsk have increased coal production to the level planned for the 1950 production plan. Hundreds of Soviet coal mining enterprises have completed their coal mining plans for the fourth month. The labor productivity of steelworkers at the blast furnace of the "Serov" factory in the Urals has exceeded the level that should be reached in 1950, with pig iron exceeding 25 percent, steel exceeding 21 percent, and rolled steel exceeding 27 percent. Employees of the Moscow Stalin Automobile Factory produced more than 250 heavy trucks in the first quarter of this year than planned. Three thousand Stakhanovite workers at the Gorky Molotov Automobile Factory have completed their personal five-year plan goals. All personnel at the factory have exceeded the first quarter plan, accelerated the turnover of working capital, and saved 15 million rubles.

### 庆祝职工代表大会揭幕苏联工人获生产新成就

1949-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝职工代表大会揭幕苏联工人获生产新成就
    【新华社北平二十一日电】塔斯社讯：为庆祝苏联职工会第十届代表大会的开幕，苏联工人已在为此发起的劳动竞赛中赢得了新的成就。康兹涅茨克许多矿坑的矿工们，已把煤产量提高到预定为一九五○年生产计划的水平。苏联千百个采煤企业，已完成了第四个月的采煤计划。乌拉尔“塞罗夫”工厂鼓风炉的钢铁工人的劳动生产率，已超过应于一九五○年到达偏水平，铣铁超过百分之二十五，钢超过百分之二十一，辗钢超过百分之二十七。莫斯科斯大林汽车工厂的职工，在本年第一季生产了超过计划的二百五十辆以上的大卡车。高尔基城莫洛托夫汽车工厂的三千名斯塔哈诺夫运动者，已完成了他们个人的五年计划目标。该厂的全体人员，都已超过了第一季计划，而且加速了流动资金的周转，并节省了一千五百万卢布。
