Title: Revolutionary Order in Nanjing Gradually Established; Railway to Zhenjiang Reopens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 1

[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, April 27th: Revolutionary order in Nanjing is being gradually established. After the People's Liberation Army entered the city on the 23rd, workers and students from universities and middle schools have actively assisted the PLA in establishing revolutionary order. Some students and staff from schools such as Central University have voluntarily taken on tasks such as patrolling, guarding, and checking IDs. Urban traffic has begun to recover.

[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Zhenjiang, April 27th: The Zhenjiang Military Control Commission has been established upon the order of the Political Department of the Third Field Army Headquarters. Yuan Zhongxian is the director, and the commission has already started work.

[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, April 27th: The Nanjing-Zhenjiang section of the Nanjing-Shanghai Railway has reopened to traffic. Thanks to the active protection of the employees, the locomotives, carriages, bridges, and tracks on this section are in good condition and undamaged. Traffic resumed the day after the liberation of Nanjing.

### 南京革命秩序逐步建立　到镇江的铁路恢复通车

1949-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京革命秩序逐步建立
  　到镇江的铁路恢复通车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京二十七日消息：南京革命秩序正逐步建立中，解放军在二十三日进入市区后，职工们和大中学学生亦积极协助解放军建立革命秩序。中央大学等校部分学生和员工，并自动担任巡逻放哨盘查等任务，市内交通已开始恢复。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】镇江二十七日消息：镇江军事管制委员会，已奉第三野战军司令部政治部命令成立，主任为袁仲贤，并已开始办公。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京二十七日消息：京沪铁路南京镇江段已恢复通车，该段机车车箱桥梁路轨，由于员工们积极保护，完好无损，南京解放后第二天就恢复通车。
