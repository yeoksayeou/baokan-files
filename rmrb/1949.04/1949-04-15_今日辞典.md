　　Today's Dictionary
    Yard—A British unit of measurement. Each yard equals three feet, or approximately 2 Chinese feet, 8 cun, 5 fen, and 7 li.
    Tall-stalk crops—A type of plant, such as sorghum, corn, millet...etc. In addition to being edible, the stalks can be burned as firewood.

　　今日辞典
    码—英国度名。每码计英尺三尺，合中国市尺二尺八寸五分七厘多。
    高秆作物—是植物类别的一种，如高粱、玉蜀黍、谷……等。其籽粒除供食用外，秸秆可当柴烧。
