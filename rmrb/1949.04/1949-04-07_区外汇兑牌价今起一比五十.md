Title: Exchange Rate Outside the Liberated Area to be 50 to 1 Starting Today
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate Outside the Liberated Area to be 50 to 1 Starting Today
    [This Newspaper's Report] Due to the Kuomintang reactionaries' continuous and rampant inflation in the ruling area, the People's Bank's exchange rate for areas outside the liberated area will be raised again to 50 to 1 starting from the 7th, an increase of 11 percent compared to the previous rate. (Yu)

### 区外汇兑牌价今起一比五十
宇
1949-04-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑牌价　今起一比五十
    【本报讯】由于国民党反动派统治区域物价赓续狂涨，人民银行区外汇兑挂牌从七日起再度提高为一比五十，较前次牌价又提高百分之十一。  （宇）
