Title: Indonesian Guerrillas Intensify Activities in West Java, Attacking Dutch Positions Every Night
Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 3

　　Indonesian Guerrillas
  　Intensify Activities in West Java
  　Attacking Dutch Positions Every Night
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th] The Hague dispatch: Dutch newspapers reported on the 26th that Indonesian guerrillas operating in West Java are intensifying their activities. Armed conflicts continue to occur in the Modjokerto area, the Solo area, and within the city. Indonesian troops attack Dutch positions every night.

### 印尼游击队　在西爪哇加紧活动　每天晚上袭击荷军阵地

1949-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼游击队
  　在西爪哇加紧活动
  　每天晚上袭击荷军阵地
    【新华社北平二十七日电】海牙讯：荷兰报纸二十六日载称：在西爪哇活动的印尼游击队正加紧活动。在莫德约克托地区、梭罗地区和城内，继续发生武装冲突。印尼军每天晚上袭击荷军阵地。
