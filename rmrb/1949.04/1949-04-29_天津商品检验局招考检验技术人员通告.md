Tianjin Commodity Inspection Bureau Announces Recruitment of Inspection Technicians

In order to strengthen scientific work, the following methods are used to recruit various inspection technicians:

I. Qualifications: Applicants should be under 40 years of age, in good health, with no bad habits, a clean history, and a graduate of a domestic or foreign university or college, or have equivalent qualifications, and possess one of the following specialized skills to apply for the examination:

(1) Organic chemical analysis (2) Pharmaceutical analysis (3) Industrial raw material analysis (4) Cotton grading and identification (5) Fur grading and identification

II. Application Procedures: From the date of this announcement until May 5th, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM daily, bring a 2-inch half-length photo, detailed resume, and credentials to the Secretariat of Tianjin Commodity Inspection Bureau at 75 Tai'erzhuang Road, Tianjin, to register.

III. Examination: The examination will be divided into written, oral, and experimental tests. The dates will be notified separately.

IV. Remuneration: Those who are admitted will receive treatment in accordance with the salary standards stipulated by the Bureau.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director Wang Zhongxiu
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Director Zhang Fusheng
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　April 27th, 38th Year of the Republic of China

### 天津商品检验局招考检验技术人员通告

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　天津商品检验局招考检验技术人员通告
    为加强科学工作特招考各项检验技术人员其办法如下：
    一、资格　年龄在四十岁以下身体健康无不良嗜好历史清白曾在国内外大学专科毕业或同等学力具有下列专门技术之一者均可报名应试
    （１）有机化学分析（２）医药分析（３）工业原料分析（４）棉花分级鉴定（５）毛皮分级鉴定
    二、报名手续　自登报之日起至五月五日止每日上午九时至下午四时携带二寸半身照片详细履历及证件至天津台儿庄路七十五号天津商品检验局秘书室报名
    三、考试　分笔试口试实验其日期另行通知
    四、待遇　录取者按本局薪给标准规定待遇
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　局长　王种秀
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　副局长　张复生
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年四月二十七日
