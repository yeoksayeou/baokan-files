Title: US Strike Statistics for the Past Year: 3,400 Strikes, 2 Million People

Author:
Date: 1949-04-27
Page: 3

　　US Strike Statistics for the Past Year
  　Number of Strikes: 3,400
  　Number of People: 2 Million
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 26th] Diantong Agency, Washington: The US Department of Labor statistics reveal that in the past year, the United States has experienced 3,419 strikes, an average of more than nine per day, with a total of 2 million workers participating.

### 美国一年来罢工统计　次数：三千四　人数：二百万

1949-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国一年来
  　罢工统计
  　次数：三千四
  　人数：二百万
    【新华社北平二十六日电】电通社华盛顿讯：美国劳工部统计透露，在过去一年中，美国发生罢工三千四百一十九次，即平均每天九次以上，参加工人共达二百万名。
