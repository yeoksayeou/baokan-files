Title: Kuomintang-Controlled Area Agricultural Scientists Liang Xi and Others Arrive in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 1

　　Agricultural Scientists from Kuomintang-Controlled Area
  　Liang Xi and Others Arrive in Beiping
    [This newspaper's dispatch] Liang Xi, an agricultural scientist from the Kuomintang-controlled area and head of the Chinese Agricultural Society, and psychologist Pan Shu, arrived in Beiping the day before yesterday (the 24th).

### 国统区农学家梁希等抵平

1949-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　国统区农学家
  　梁希等抵平
