Title: Zhangjiakou Railway Sub-bureau Opens Workers' Training Class
Author:
Date: 1949-04-18
Page: 2

　　Zhangjiakou Railway Sub-bureau Opens Workers' Training Class
    [Zhangjiakou News] In order to raise the class consciousness and working ability of employees, the Zhangjiakou Sub-bureau of the Pingjin Railway Administration held the first phase of the workers' training class on March 26. 172 employees received training for a period of five days, which ended on the 30th of last month. The second phase of the training class of the bureau will begin on April 10.

### 张垣铁路分局开办工训班

1949-04-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　张垣铁路分局开办工训班
    【张垣讯】为提高职工的阶级觉悟和工作能力，平津铁路管理局张家口分局，于三月二十六日召开第一期职工训练班，受训员工一百七十二名，学习期限五天，于上月三十日结束。该局第二期训练班将于四月十日开始。
