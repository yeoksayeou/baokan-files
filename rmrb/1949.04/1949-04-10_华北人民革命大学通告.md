Title: North China People's Revolutionary University Notice
Author:
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 4

　　North China People's Revolutionary University Notice
    This school has decided to hold the eighth examination on April 12th and 13th. All registered students (numbered from 101 to 600 in the "Lin" series) are expected to attend the examination at the China University examination hall. The examination dates and subjects are listed below:
    April 12th, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM: Examination of Chinese Language, Political Common Sense, Chinese and Foreign History and Geography, Mathematics.
    April 13th, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM: Oral Examination and Physical Examination.
    April 10th, 38th Year of the Republic of China.

### 华北人民革命大学通告

1949-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　华北人民革命大学通告
    本校决定四月十二十三两日举行第八次考试，希报名各生（临字一○一号至六○○号）均到中国大学考场应考兹将考试日期课目列后：
    四月十二日上午九时至下午五时考试国文、政治常识、中外史地、数学。
    四月十三日上午九时至下午五时口试及体格检查。
    中国民国三十八年四月十日。
