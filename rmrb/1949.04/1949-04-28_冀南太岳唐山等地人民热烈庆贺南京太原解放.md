Title: People in Southern Hebei, Taiyue, Tangshan, and other places enthusiastically celebrate the liberation of Nanjing and Taiyuan
Author:
Date: 1949-04-28
Page: 1

People in Southern Hebei, Taiyue, Tangshan, and other places enthusiastically celebrate the liberation of Nanjing and Taiyuan

[Reported by this newspaper] After the news of the liberation of Nanjing and Taiyuan spread, people throughout North China were overjoyed, sending telegrams to congratulate and holding meetings to celebrate, all expressing their unwavering and enthusiastic support for the revolutionary war.

The Southern Hebei District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Southern Hebei Administrative Office, and the Headquarters and Political Department of the Southern Hebei Military Region, on the 25th, jointly sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, congratulating them on the great victory in liberating Nanjing and Taiyuan, and expressing that under the correct leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the Party, government, army, and people of the entire Southern Hebei District will work and produce even harder in the future to support the People's Liberation Army in completely, cleanly, and entirely eliminating the remnants of the reactionaries, and strive to build a new democratic republic and defend world peace.

After hearing the news of the victory, the masses in various agencies, schools, factories, shops, and districts and villages in the Taiyue District, the Taiyue Xinhua Daily's liaison section's phone was constantly busy reporting the victory news to all parties, until the extra edition was issued. The mimeograph comrades of the Taiyue Postal Administration were particularly careful when printing the victory news, printing it better than ever, and in greater quantities, hoping that the news could be spread to every corner, so that everyone would know.

When the Tangshan Daily distributed a large number of extra editions to the citizens, everyone showed extreme excitement, with gongs, drums, and slogans ringing out. The faculty and students of the Railway Institute organized a yangko team overnight. Workers expressed their determination to support the crossing-the-river army and liberate all of China through hard work. When the workers in the steel plant heard the news of the victory, they were so excited that they volunteered to work two extra hours to celebrate.

### 冀南太岳唐山等地人民热烈庆贺南京太原解放

1949-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀南太岳唐山等地人民热烈庆贺南京太原解放
    【本报讯】南京、太原解放消息传出后，我全华北各地人民无不欢欣鼓舞，通电祝贺、开会庆祝，无不同声表明坚决热烈支援革命战争的忠诚。
    中共冀南区党委、冀南行署、冀南军区司令部及政治部，于二十五日，联名致电毛主席、朱总司令及全体人民解放军指战员，庆贺解放南京太原的大胜利，并表示在毛主席、朱总司令的正确领导下全冀南区的党、政、军、民今后更加努力工作、生产，以支援人民解放军彻底、干净、全部消灭反动派的残余，为建设新民主主义共和国，保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    太岳区各机关、学校、工厂、商店及各区村的群众在听到胜利消息以后，太岳新华日报通联科的电话一直忙着向各方报告着胜利消息，一直到发出号外。太岳邮政管理局的油印同志，在印刷胜利捷报时，刻写的特别清，印得比每次都好，而且多，希望这次消息能传到每一个角落，使每一个人都知道。
    当唐山日报把大批号外传到市民的手里时，人人都表现了极度的兴奋，锣鼓及口号声响成一片。铁路学院的教职学员，连夜组织了秧歌队。工人们纷纷表示以努力生产来支援渡江大军，解放全中国。制钢厂的各组工人听到胜利消息时兴奋得纷纷加义务工两小时来庆祝。
