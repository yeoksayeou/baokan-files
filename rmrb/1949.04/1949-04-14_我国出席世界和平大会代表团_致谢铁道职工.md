Title: Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Congress Thanks Railway Workers
Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 1

[Reported by this newspaper] The entire Chinese delegation to the World Peace Congress sent a letter on April 2 to Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan, forwarded to the Northeast Railway Bureau and the Pingjin Railway Bureau, expressing their respect for the service spirit of the railway workers along the way. The letter stated: "This time, our delegation was assigned to participate in the World Peace Congress in Paris. We are grateful for the special car provided by your ministry and for Comrade Zhou Li's escort and care. Thanks to the efforts of Comrade Zhou and the various railway bureaus along the line, as well as the special train staff, we were able to reach our destination before the deadline... We would like to express our sincerest gratitude to your ministry and are willing to learn from your arduous and dedicated service spirit, continue to serve the people, and strive for the success of the World Peace Congress!"
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Trace)

### 我国出席世界和平大会代表团　致谢铁道职工

1949-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国出席世界和平大会代表团
  　致谢铁道职工
    【本报讯】出席世界和平大会中国代表团全体团员于四月二日致函铁道部滕代远部长，并转东北铁路总局与平津铁路局，对沿途铁路职工的服务精神，表示敬意。该函称：“这次本团奉派赴巴黎参加世界和平大会，承蒙贵部派车专送，并派周力同志随车照顾，由于周同志及沿线各路局以及专车职工的努力，我们都能在限定日期以前到达目的地…，谨向贵部表示最诚挚的谢意，并愿学习你们艰苦卓绝的服务精神，继续为人民服务，争取世界和平大会的成功！”
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（迹）
