Title: Nanjing Kuomintang Reactionary Government Peace Negotiation Delegates to Arrive in Beiping Today; Zhang Zhizhong Visits Chiang Kai-shek in Xikou Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1949-04-01
Page: 1

　　Nanjing Kuomintang Reactionary Government
    Peace Negotiation Delegates to Arrive in Beiping Today
  　Zhang Zhizhong Visits Chiang Kai-shek in Xikou Before Departure
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 31st] The Nanjing Kuomintang reactionary government decided on the 24th and 29th to send Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Huang Shao＠, Zhang Shizhao, Li Zheng, and Liu Fei as representatives to hold peace talks with the Chinese Communist Party. They have decided to arrive in Beiping on April 1st. Zhang Zhizhong, who was designated as the chief representative, went to Xikou on the 29th by order of the pseudo-Acting President Li Zongren and the pseudo-Executive Yuan President He Yingqin to meet the number one war criminal, pseudo-President Chiang Kai-shek, to "request instructions," and returned to Nanjing on the 30th.

### 南京国民党反动政府  和谈代表今将来平　张治中临行往溪口见蒋贼

1949-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京国民党反动政府
    和谈代表今将来平
  　张治中临行往溪口见蒋贼
    【新华社北平三十一日电】南京国民党反动政府已于二十四日及二十九日先后决定派张治中、邵力子、黄绍＠、章士钊、李蒸、刘斐为与中国共产党举行和平谈判的代表，并已决定于四月一日来平。被指定为首席代表的张治中，曾于二十九日奉伪代总统李宗仁、伪行政院长何应钦之命，往溪口见第一号战争罪犯伪总统蒋介石“请求指示”，于三十日返回南京。
