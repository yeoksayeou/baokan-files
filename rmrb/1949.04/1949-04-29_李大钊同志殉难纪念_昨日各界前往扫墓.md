Title: Commemoration of Comrade Li Dazhao's Martyrdom: All Circles Visited the Tomb Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 1

　　Commemoration of Comrade Li Dazhao's Martyrdom
　　Yesterday All Circles Visited the Tomb
　　[Our Newspaper] Yesterday (the 28th) was the anniversary of Comrade Li Dazhao's martyrdom. Responsible comrades from the Party, government, military, civilian, and academic circles in Peiping, as well as democratic figures, went to the Wanan Public Cemetery in the Western Hills of Peiping at 9:30 AM to sweep the tomb. Participants included: Chairman Dong Biwu, Comrade Lin Boqu, Commander Nie, Comrade Peng Zhen, Mayor Ye Jianying, Wu Yuzhang, Yang Xiufeng, Fan Wenlan, Luo Mai, Zhou Yang, Zhao Zhensheng, Zhao Yimin, Xiao Ming of the Peiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Xiuyan of the Peiping Municipal Women's Federation, and democratic figures such as Shen Junru, Ma Xulun, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Bojun, and Tan Pingshan. Comrade Lin, a close comrade-in-arms of Comrade Dazhao during his lifetime, delivered a speech in front of the tomb after the ceremony, in which he cordially recalled Comrade Dazhao's revolutionary life and deeds.

### 李大钊同志殉难纪念　昨日各界前往扫墓

1949-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　李大钊同志殉难纪念
  　昨日各界前往扫墓
    【本报讯】昨日（二十八日）为李大钊同志殉难纪念日，在平的党政军民学名界负责同志及民主人士于九时半前往北平西山万安公墓扫墓。参加者有：董必武主席、林伯渠同志，聂司令员、彭真同志、叶剑英市长、吴玉章、杨秀峰、范文澜、罗迈、周扬、赵振声、赵毅敏，北平市总工会萧明，北平市妇联会张秀岩等同志及民主人士沈钧儒、马叙伦、黄炎培、章伯钧、谭平山等先生，林老为大钊同志生前亲密战友，于举行仪式后，即在墓前发表讲话，他亲切的回忆了大钊同志的生平革命事迹。
