Title: Exchange Rate Outside the Region: 1 to 120
Author:
Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate Outside the Region: 1 to 120
    [This newspaper reports] The Bank of China's exchange rate for outside the region will be increased to 1 to 120 starting today (12th).
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Jian, Yu)

### 区外汇兑一比一二○

1949-04-12

    【本报讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价于今日（十二日）起提高为一比一二○。
