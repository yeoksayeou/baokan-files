Title: Fushun Refractory Brick Factory Doubles Production
Author:
Date: 1949-04-04
Page: 3

　　Fushun Refractory Brick Factory Doubles Production
    [Xinhua News Agency, Fushun, 2nd] The refractory brick factory, which is of great significance to heavy industrial construction, has rapidly resumed production. When the factory started work on March 1, it only produced 10 tons of bricks per day, but now it has reached 20 tons.

### 抚顺耐火砖厂生产提高一倍

1949-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　抚顺耐火砖厂生产提高一倍
    【新华社抚顺二日电】对重工业建设有重大意义的耐火砖工厂已迅速恢复生产。该厂于三月一日开工时、每日仅出砖十吨，现则达二十吨。
