Title: National New Youth League Congress to be Held in Beiping on the 10th; Over Two Hundred Representatives from Various Regions Have Already Registered
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 1

　　National New Youth League Congress
  　Will Be Held in Beiping on the 10th
  　Over Two Hundred Representatives from Various Regions Have Already Registered
    [Our Newspaper's Report] The first National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League will officially open in Beiping on the tenth of this month. As of yesterday (the 6th), 226 representatives from various regions have registered for the congress, including 55 representatives from the Northeast Liberated Area, 54 representatives from the East China delegation, 30 representatives from the Central Plains, Northwest, and a portion of the military, and 87 representatives from the North China delegation, which is currently in session. (Jin Feng)

### 全国新青团代表会将于十日在平举行　各地代表已有二百余人报到
金凤
1949-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国新青团代表会
  　将于十日在平举行
  　各地代表已有二百余人报到
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团全国第一次代表大会将于本月十日在平正式开幕。到昨（六日）日为止，各地代表向大会报到者已有二百二十六人，计为东北解放区代表团五十五人，华东代表团五十四人，中原、西北及部队一部分代表三十人，及正在开会中的华北代表团八十七人。  （金凤）
