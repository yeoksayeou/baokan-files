Title: Neiqiu First District Eliminates Cabbage Worms
Author: Neiqiu County Government Cooperative Section
Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 2

　　Neiqiu First District Eliminates Cabbage Worms
    [Taihang News] After a cabbage worm infestation occurred in eleven villages, including Sanchongfeng, in the first district of Neiqiu County in the middle of last month, the county government immediately organized cadres to lead the masses in capturing and beating the worms. After five days of effort, the worm infestation has been eliminated.
    [Another News] Following the elimination of the worm infestation in the first district, on the 29th of last month, a worm infestation was discovered in 1,192 mu of oilseed rape land in seventeen villages in the second district of Neiqiu. The government is now organizing all efforts to capture and beat the worms.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Neiqiu County Government Cooperative Section)

### 内邱一区扑灭菜虫
内邱县政府合作科
1949-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　内邱一区扑灭菜虫
    【太行讯】内邱一区三中封等十一村于上月中旬发生油菜虫害后，县府立即组织干部领导群众捕打，经过五天的努力，虫害已捕灭。
    【又讯】继一区虫害消灭后，上月二十九日，内邱二区有十七个村一千一百九十二亩油菜地里发现虫害，现政府正在组织全力捕打中。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（内邱县政府合作科）
