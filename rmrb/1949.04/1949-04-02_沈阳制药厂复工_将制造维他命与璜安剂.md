Title: Shenyang Pharmaceutical Factory Resumes Production: To Manufacture Vitamins and Sulfa Drugs
Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 31st: The large-scale Shenyang Chemical Pharmaceutical Factory, including its main factory and five branch factories, officially resumed production last month. The main factory's laboratory has researched and developed manufacturing methods for vitamins and various sulfa drugs, and production will begin soon. The other five branch factories under the factory are also accelerating their recovery.

### 沈阳制药厂复工　将制造维他命与璜安剂

1949-04-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　沈阳制药厂复工
  　将制造维他命与璜安剂
    【新华社沈阳三十一日电】规模宏大的沈阳化学制药厂总厂及五个分厂，已于上月正式恢复生产。总厂化验室已研究出维他命及各种璜安制剂的制造方法，即将开始制造。该厂所属的另外五个分厂，现亦在加紧恢复中。
