　　Reading the Newspaper Little Dictionary
　　National Council of the Arts, Sciences and Professions—This organization was formed in 1944 by representatives of the American arts, sciences, and professions. It has more than 18,000 members and branches in 15 states in the United States, but its main areas of activity are in cities such as New York, Hollywood, and Chicago.
　　Communist Party of India—The Communist Party of India currently has 55,000 members. Under its direct leadership are more than one million organized masses in the Indian Trade Union, Farmers' Association, and Student Federation. It has also established a liberated area including five million people in Hyderabad State, implementing land reform and resisting the military counter-offensive of the Nehru reactionary government.

### 读报小辞典

1949-04-05
第3版()
专栏：读报小辞典

　　读报小辞典
    艺术、科学、自由职业者全国委员会——是美国的艺术界、科学界及自由职业界的代表于一九四四年组成。会员有一万八千余人，在美国十五州设有分会，但主要活动地区则为纽约、好莱坞以及芝加哥等都市。
    印度共产党　——印度共产党现有党员五万五千人。在他直接领导下有印度工会、农会、学联会等一百多万有组织的群众。在海得拉巴土邦内并建立了一处包括五百万人的解放区，实行了土地改革，抗击了尼赫鲁反动政府的军事反攻。
