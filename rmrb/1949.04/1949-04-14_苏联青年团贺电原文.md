Author:
Date: 1949-04-14
Page: 1

　　Telegram of Congratulations from the Soviet Youth League (Komsomol)
　　[Our Newspaper Dispatch] The First National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League yesterday received a congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League to the Congress. The original telegram is as follows:
    Dear friends,
    The Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, on behalf of millions of members of the Young Communist League of the Soviet Socialist Federal Republic, extends heartfelt greetings to you and the Chinese democratic youth you represent on the occasion of the opening of your First National Congress.
    Our youth are paying close attention to the heroic struggle of the Chinese people for their freedom and national independence. The brilliant victory of the Chinese people in this struggle is a victory for the camp of democracy and progress, and an extremely important contribution to the common cause of striving for peace and opposing the instigators of a new war of imperialism. The Soviet youth praise the heroic and unyielding spirit of the Chinese democratic youth, from whose ranks have emerged thousands upon thousands of male and female heroes of the liberation war.
    We are confident that your Congress will take a further step forward in strengthening the unity of progressive Chinese youth and in solving the great task of building a free, independent, and democratic China under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.
    Dear friends, we wish you the greatest success in the struggle for the cause of the people, for lasting peace and democracy, for national independence, and for the freedom and happiness of our youth.
    Long live the further consolidation and expansion of the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet youth!
    　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　April 11, 1949

### 苏联青年团贺电原文

1949-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联青年团贺电原文
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团第一次代表大会昨日接获全苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会给大会的贺电，原电如下：
    亲爱的朋友们：
    全苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会代表苏维埃社会主义联邦共和国的千百万共产主义青年团员，在贵团第一次代表大会开幕之际，向你们及你们所代表的中国民主青年致衷心的敬礼。
    我国青年极其关心地注意中国人民为了自己的自由及民族独立而进行的英勇斗争。中国人民在这一斗争中的光辉的胜利就是民主与进步阵营的胜利，也就是在争取和平反对帝国主义的新战争挑拨者的共同事业中一个极其重要的贡献。苏联青年们赞颂中国民主青年的英勇刚毅的精神，从他们的行列中产生了成千成万的解放战争的男英雄和女英雄们。
    我们确信你们的大会将在加强中国进步青年的团结事业中、将在中国共产党领导下解决建设自由独立民主中国的伟大事业中，向前更进一步。
    亲爱的朋友们，我们预祝你们在为人民的事业、为巩固的和平与民主、为民族的独立、为我们青年的自由与幸福的斗争中获得最大的成功。
    中苏青年的友谊更加巩固与扩大万岁！
    　　　　　　　　全苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年四月十一日
