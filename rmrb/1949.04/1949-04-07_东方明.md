Title: Eastern Brightness
Author: Liu Yazi
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 4

　　Eastern Brightness
    Liu Yazi
    The sun comes out, the East is bright,
    We have a Commander-in-Chief.
    “Lofty positions are not my wish,
    I am just a soldier of the people”!
    (Note) The second half of the poem uses the original lines of Commander-in-Chief Zhu and myself, I dare not take credit for it, so I note it here.
    Yazi's record.

### 东方明
柳亚子
1949-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　东方明
    柳亚子
    太阳出来东方明，
    我们有个总司令。
    “麟台云阁非吾愿，
    咱就人民子弟兵”！
    （注）下半首即用朱总司令和我的原句，不敢掠美，附志于此。
    亚子记。
