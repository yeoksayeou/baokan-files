Title: He Xiangning and Others Arrive in Beiping Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-04-13
Page: 1

　　He Xiangning and Others Arrive in Beiping Yesterday

　　[Our Newspaper] Madam Liao Zhongkai, He Xiangning, together with her daughter Liao Mengxing, and democratic figures Zhang Wen (Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, Democratic League), Guo Guanjie (Third Party), and Su Yanbin (Democratic League), arrived in Beiping at 4 PM on the 12th from Tianjin, accompanied by Comrade Liao Chengzhi. Comrades Dong Biwu, Ye Jianying, Li Weihan, Deng Yingchao, and democratic figures Shen Junru, Zhu Xuefan, Liu Qingyang, and others went to the train station to welcome them. Madam He Xiangning expressed her great joy at arriving in the liberated area to participate in the construction of the new China.

### 何香凝等昨抵平

1949-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　何香凝等昨抵平
    【本报讯】廖仲恺夫人何香凝先生，偕女公子廖梦醒，暨民主人士张文（国民党革命委员会、民主同盟）、郭冠杰（第三党）、苏延宾（民主同盟）等于十二日下午四时由廖承志同志陪伴从天津抵平。董必武、叶剑英、李维汉、邓颖超等同志、及民主人士沈钧儒、朱学范、柳亚子、刘清扬等前往车站欢迎，何香凝先生对抵达解放区参加新中国的建设工作表示极为愉快。
