Title: North China University Notice, April 3rd, Second Department Enrollment
Author:
Date: 1949-04-05
Page: 1

　　North China University Notice, April 3rd
    　Second Department Enrollment
    The list of successful candidates for the second entrance re-examination of our university's Second Department was announced on March 25th. It is hereby decided that registration and enrollment will take place on April 5th and 6th at the Second Department (inside the Temple of Agriculture). We hope that all admitted students will arrive on time.

### 华北大学通告　四月三日  第二部入学

1949-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

　 　华北大学通告   四月三日
    　第二部入学
    本校二部第二次复试已于三月二十五日发榜，兹决定于四月五、六两日在该部（先农坛内）报到注册入学，希录取诸生，准时前往为要。
