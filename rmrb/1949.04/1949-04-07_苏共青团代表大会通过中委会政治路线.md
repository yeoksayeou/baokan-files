Title: Komsomol Congress Approves Central Committee's Political Line
Author:
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 3
　　
　　Soviet Komsomol Congress
  　Approves Central Committee's Political Line
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 5th] Moscow dispatch: The 11th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League (Komsomol) of the Soviet Union continued its discussion on the 2nd of the reports of the Central Committee and the Central Auditing Committee of the Komsomol. Bokryshkin, a famous air force officer who had been awarded the title of "Hero of the Soviet Union" three times, pointed out in his speech the active role of the Komsomol in the construction of the Soviet Air Force. After the discussion, Mikhailov (formerly mistranslated as Mikhailov), the General Secretary of the Communist Youth League, made a summary. The congress then unanimously adopted the political line in the report of the Central Committee of the Komsomol and its work, and also adopted the report of the Central Auditing Committee.

### 苏共青团代表大会通过中委会政治路线

1949-04-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏共青团代表大会
  　通过中委会政治路线
    【新华社北平五日电】莫斯科讯：苏联共产主义青年团第十一届代表大会，二日继续讨论青年团中央委员会与中央监察委员会的报告，曾经三次荣获“苏联英雄”头衔的著名的空军人员卜克里施金，在发言中指出青年团在建设苏联空军中的积极作用。讨论结束后，由共产主义青年团总书记米哈依洛夫（前误为米哈依诺夫）作了总结，大会继即一致通过共青团中委会报告中的政治路线和它的工作并通过中央监察委员会的报告。
