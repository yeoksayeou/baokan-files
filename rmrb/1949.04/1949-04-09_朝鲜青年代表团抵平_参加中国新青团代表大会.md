Title: Korean Youth Delegation Arrives in Beiping to Attend Chinese New Youth League Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 8th. The international comrades-in-arms – the Korean Youth Delegation – who will attend the First National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, arrived in Beiping at 9:20 this morning. The delegation consists of three members: Jang Se-jin, Vice Chairman of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, and Lee Jung-geun (worker representative), and Lee Geum-soon (student representative). Feng Wenbin, Jiang Nanxiang, and Song Yiping, representatives of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Xie Bangding, representative of the All-China Student Federation, Xiao Song, representative of the Beiping Municipal Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, and more than 200 representatives from North China, Central Plains, Northeast China, East China, Northwest China and other places attending this First National Congress of the Youth League, went to Beiping East Railway Station to welcome them. Jiang Nanxiang delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and various youth organizations welcoming the delegation at the station, saying: "The arrival of the Korean representatives will surely further enhance the understanding and unity between the youth of China and Korea." Jang Se-jin, of the Korean delegation, delivered a reply, congratulating the historic victory of the Chinese People's Liberation War, and emphasizing that this victory was the result of the correct leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, and the Chinese Communist Party. After the speeches, the youth representatives of the two countries sang the Internationale together at the station.

### 朝鲜青年代表团抵平　参加中国新青团代表大会

1949-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜青年代表团抵平
  　参加中国新青团代表大会
    【新华社北平八日电】参加中国新民主主义青年团全国第一次代表大会的国际战友——朝鲜青年代表团在今天上午九时二十分到达北平。代表团由北朝鲜民主青年同盟副委员长张瑞振和李重根（工人代表）、李金顺（学生代表）三人组成。中国新民主主义青年团筹委会代表冯文彬、蒋南翔、宋一平，中华全国学生联合会代表谢邦定，新民主主义青年团北平市筹委会代表萧松和参加此次青年团全国第一次代表大会的华北、中原、东北、华东、西北等地代表二百余人，齐赴北平东车站欢迎，蒋南翔代表中国新民主主义青年团筹委会和到车站欢迎的各个青年组织向朝鲜代表团致欢迎词说：“朝鲜代表们的到来，一定会更加增进中国朝鲜两国青年的了解和团结。”朝鲜代表团张瑞振致答辞，他祝贺中国人民解放战争的历史性的胜利，着重说明这种胜利是由于中国人民领袖毛泽东主席和中国共产党正确领导的结果。致词毕，两国青年代表，在车站齐唱国际歌。
