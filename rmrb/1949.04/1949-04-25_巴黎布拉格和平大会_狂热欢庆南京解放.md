Title: Paris and Prague Peace Conferences Enthusiastically Celebrate the Liberation of Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 1

　　Paris and Prague Peace Conferences
    Enthusiastically Celebrate the Liberation of Nanjing
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 24th: Prague dispatch: After the chairman of the Prague Peace Conference announced the news of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's liberation of Nanjing, the entire conference hall immediately erupted into fifteen minutes of fervent cheering. The 44-member Chinese delegation was overjoyed and sang "Long Live Free China" loudly. Guo Moruo, the head of the Chinese delegation, declared: "The victory of the Chinese people is the victory of the entire peace camp."
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 24th: Paris dispatch: When the news of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's liberation of Nanjing reached the Paris World Peace Congress on the 23rd, and was announced by Professor Crowther, the head of the British delegation and chairman of the conference, all the representatives stood up and cheered fervently for two minutes. According to foreign journalists, this was the most enthusiastic scene in the four days since the opening of the conference.

### 巴黎布拉格和平大会  狂热欢庆南京解放

1949-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴黎布拉格和平大会
    狂热欢庆南京解放
    【新华社北平二十四日电】布拉格讯：布拉格和平大会主席宣布中国人民解放军解放南京的消息后，大会全场立刻爆发了十五分钟的狂热欢呼。中国代表团四十四人欢欣鼓舞，高声歌唱“自由中国万岁”歌。中国代表团团长郭沫若宣称：“中国人民的胜利是整个和平阵营的胜利。”
    【新华社北平二十四日电】巴黎讯：当中国人民解放军解放南京的消息传到二十三日巴黎世界拥护和平大会会场，由大会主席英国代表团团长克劳瑟教授加以宣布时，全体代表起立狂热地欢呼达两分钟之久。据外国记者报道，这是大会开幕以来四天中最热烈的场面。
