Title: Qinyang Peasants Apply Fertilizer and Weed Wheat
Author:
Date: 1949-04-05
Page: 2

(Taihang News) Qinyang peasants are actively weeding wheat seedlings and applying top dressing fertilizer. In the Xiaoweiying Village of the Second District, more than 200 semi-able-bodied laborers have organized to weed wheat and apply top dressing, freeing up male full laborers for transportation. Xiwancun Village has already applied 7,000 dan of top dressing fertilizer, irrigated more than 4,200 mu of wheat, and weeded more than 4,900 mu of wheat. In the entire Second District, more than 30,000 mu of the total 40,000 mu of wheat have been weeded, and more than 20,000 mu have been top-dressed. (Jin)

### 沁阳农民追肥锄麦

1949-04-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　沁阳农民追肥锄麦
    【太行讯】沁阳农民积极锄麦苗、上追肥。二区校尉营村半劳力二百余人组织起来进行锄麦、上追肥，腾出男全劳力搞运输。西万村已上追肥七千余担，并已浇麦四千二百多亩，锄麦四千九百余亩。全二区四万余亩麦子已锄三万余亩，已上追肥二万余亩。（进）
