Title: Ping Sui Railway Workers Hold First Representative Conference
Author: Lin Wei
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 2

　　Ping Sui Railway Workers
  　Hold First Representative Conference
    [Zhangjiakou News] The Zhangjiakou Branch of the Pingjin Railway Workers' General Union held its first worker representative conference for the Ping Sui Line on the 25th. Attendees included representatives from 42 units such as Nankou, totaling 87 people. The conference's agenda included: electing representatives to attend the North China Workers' Representative Conference; electing visiting delegations to attend the Northeast Workers' Representative Conference and model worker conference; conveying the opinions from the Pingjin Railway General Union's worker representative forum; and widely soliciting opinions from worker representatives on worker affairs and administrative management. The visiting delegation to the Northeast departed on the 25th. (Lin Wei)

### 平绥路职工　开首次代表会议
林巍
1949-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　平绥路职工
  　开首次代表会议
    【张家口讯】平津铁路职工总会张家口职工分总会，于二十五日召开平绥线首次职工代表会议。到会者计有南口等四十二个单位，代表八十七人。会议内容：选举出席华北职工代表大会代表，并选举参观团出席东北职工代表会和劳模会议，传达平津铁路总工会职工代表座谈会意见，广泛征求职工代表对职工工作和行政管理工作的意见。赴东北的参观团已于二十五日起程。    （林巍）
