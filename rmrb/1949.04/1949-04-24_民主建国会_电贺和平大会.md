Title: China National Democratic Construction Association Cables Congratulations to Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 3

　　China National Democratic Construction Association
  　Cables Congratulations to Peace Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd] The China National Democratic Construction Association recently sent a cable to the Paris World Peace Congress to express congratulations. The original cable is as follows:
    To the Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Congress, forwarded to the World Peace Congress:
    As the Chinese People's Liberation War has achieved a decisive victory, and the great New Democratic construction is about to begin, the representatives of peace-loving countries around the world are holding a peace conference in Paris. We, the Chinese people, firmly support the propositions and decisions of the conference. We hereby send a cable to express our congratulations and wish the conference a complete success.

### 民主建国会　电贺和平大会

1949-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　民主建国会
  　电贺和平大会
    【新华社北京二十二日电】民主建国会顷电巴黎世界拥护和平大会致贺，原电如下：
    中国出席世界拥护和平大会代表团转世界拥护和平大会：
    当此中国人民解放战争获得了决定性的胜利，伟大的新民主主义建设即将开始之际，全世界爱好和平国家的代表在巴黎举行拥护和平大会，我们中国人民坚决地支持大会的主张和决定。特电申贺，敬祝大会圆满成功。
