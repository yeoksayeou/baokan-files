Title: French Young Workers Love Peace, Oppose De Gaulle's Hackneyed Tune of Invading Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1949-04-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 4th -- Paris dispatch: The Central Bureau of the French Young Workers' Union issued a statement on the 2nd, expressing indignation at De Gaulle's recent remarks urging the strengthening of the war of aggression against Vietnam. The statement pointed out: Young workers love peace, are unwilling to defend the interests of large colonial companies, and are unwilling to oppress other nations.

### 法青年工人爱好和平　反对戴高乐侵越滥调

1949-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　法青年工人爱好和平　反对戴高乐侵越滥调
    【新华社北平四日电】巴黎讯：法国青年工人工会中央局二日发表声明，对戴高乐日前发表的敦促加强侵略越南战争的谈话，表示愤慨。声明指出：青年工人爱好和平，不愿保卫大殖民公司的利益，也不愿压迫其他民族。
