Title: Countries Intensify Selection of Representatives to Attend World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 10th—As the date of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, to be held in Paris on April 20th, approaches, some countries that have decided to attend are stepping up the selection of delegations to attend the congress. The delegations from Hungary and Albania have already been selected. The Hungarian delegation, composed of twenty prominent cultural workers, will be led by Révai, a member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Working People's Party. The Albanian delegation, headed by Konovida, President of the Albanian Academy of Sciences, includes Kapo, Secretary of the Albanian Women's League, and Shutriqi, Secretary-General of the Writers' Union, among others. In addition, the Executive Committee of the French General Confederation of Labor has approved the decision to attend the World Congress of Supporters of Peace and has called on all organizations belonging to the General Confederation to step up preparations for the congress. Various labor mass organizations in Britain are also actively selecting representatives to attend the meeting.

### 各国加紧选派代表出席世界和平大会

1949-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　各国加紧选派代表出席世界和平大会
    【新华社北平十日电】随着四月二十日在巴黎举行的世界拥护和平大会会期的迫近，决定与会的一些国家正在加紧选派出席大会的代表团。匈牙利与阿尔巴尼亚的代表团业已选出。由匈牙利著名文化工作者二十人组成的代表团，将由匈劳动人民党政治局委员里瓦伊率领出席大会。以阿尔巴尼亚科学院院长科诺维达为首的阿国代表团，包括阿妇女联盟书记卡波、作家联盟总书记舒特利基等人。此外，法国总工会执行委员会已批准出席世界拥护和平大会的决定，并号召一切属于总工会的组织，加紧大会的筹备工作。英国各劳动群众团体亦正在积极地选派赴会的代表。
