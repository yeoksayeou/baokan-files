Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang to Prepare the Establishment of the Peiping Branch

Author: 
Date: 1949-04-26
Page: 1

　　Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
  　Preparing to Establish the Peiping Branch
    【Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, April 24th】The central organization personnel of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang have arrived in Peiping and attach great importance to the work of the Peiping Municipal Branch of the Committee. The Standing Committee of the Committee has passed a resolution designating Li Xijiu, Zhu Yunshan, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Cisheng, Wu Maosun, Xu Baokui, Wang Jingru, Zhou Fanwen, and Su Congzhou as the nine members of the preparatory committee for the Peiping Municipal Branch. They have established secretarial, registration, and review groups, and are now actively carrying out membership registration and other preparatory work.

### 中国国民党革命委员会　筹组北平分会

1949-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国国民党革命委员会
  　筹组北平分会
    【新华社北平二十四日电】中国国民党革命委员会中央机构人员来平后，对该会平市分会工作，颇为重视。该会中常会曾通过决议，指派李锡九、朱蕴山、陈劭先、陈此生、吴茂荪、许宝骙、王静如、周范文、苏从周等九人组成平市分会筹备委员会，设立秘书、登记、审查各组，现正积极进行登记会员及其他筹备工作。
