Title: Penicillin
Author: Lin Fenzi
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 4

Medical Q & A

　　Penicillin Editor:
　　I often see "Penicillin" in newspapers. I don't know the properties and usage of this drug. Please explain in detail.　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lin Fenzi
    Answer: Penicillin is also known as Penicillium. It is one of the antibiotic drugs. It is extracted from the culture medium of Penicillium＋Noaum. There are currently four kinds of antibiotic components: F, G, K, X. Among them, K has the weakest therapeutic effect. Penicillin can be taken orally (but in larger doses) and used for intramuscular and intravenous injection. Penicillin's indications:
    A. Symptoms for which penicillin is definitely effective, all staphylococcal infections with or without bacteremia:
    1. Acute and chronic osteomyelitis, abscesses of soft tissues, meningitis, suppurative pneumonia, empyema, wound infections and burns, endocarditis.
    2. All anaerobic clostridium infections, gas gangrene, malignant edema.
    3. All hemolytic streptococcal infections combined with bacteremia and all severe local infections, cellulitis, streptococcal pneumonia, empyema, puerperal sepsis, peritonitis.
    4. All pneumococcal infections.
    5. All gonococcal infections, gonococcal arthritis, gonococcal ophthalmia, endocarditis, epididymitis, peritonitis.
    B. Penicillin is also effective but still needs consideration for the following diseases:
    Syphilis, actinomycosis, diphtheria.
    C. Diseases for which penicillin is ineffective:
    1. All gram-negative bacilli, such as typhoid, dysentery bacilli, E. coli, cholera, plague bacteria.
    2. Filterable infections: influenza, lupus vulgaris, sleeping sickness.
    3. Tuberculosis, acute lupus erythematosus, systemic lupus erythematosus, ulcerative colitis, malaria, cancer.
    Methods of using Penicillin:
    Oral administration: Ordinary penicillin preparations are not very effective orally because they are destroyed by gastric acid; however, there are now penicillin tablets with buffering agents for oral administration, so that gastric acid does not affect them. However, the oral dose must be 4-5 times larger than the injection dose. Each tablet usually contains at least 50,000 units and is only suitable for mild cases. Take it half an hour before meals or two hours after meals.
    Injection: Generally, it is a dry powder divided into several kinds of 20, 10, 50, 1 million units and 300,000 units of oil-wax agent. Before use, the powder must be dissolved in sterile distilled water or physiological saline or 5% glucose solution to a certain concentration, and injected into muscles, veins, subcutaneously, into the spinal cavity, or other body cavities.
    Intramuscular injection is the most commonly used, and the deltoid, gluteal, and thigh muscles are the most suitable. Each CC of injection solution contains at least 20,000 units, once every three hours.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Health Department of the North China People's Government

### 盘尼西林

1949-04-29
第4版()
专栏：医药问答

　　盘尼西林编者先生：
    在报纸上时常看到“盘尼西林”，不知该药的性质及用法如何？请详解答。    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　林芬兹
    答：盘尼西林（Ｐｅｎｉｃｉｌｌｉｎ）又名青霉菌素，是属于抗生性药物之一种。它是由Ｐｅｎｉｃｉｌｌｉｕｍ＋Ｎｏａｕｍ之培养液提炼而得。抗生性成份，现已有四种：Ｆ，Ｇ，Ｋ，Ｘ。其中以Ｋ的疗效最弱。盘尼西林可以口服（但需量较大）及用以肌肉静脉注射。盘尼西林的适应症：
    甲、青霉素确实有效之症状，一切葡萄状球菌传染合并或不合并菌血症：
    １、急性慢性骨髓炎、软部组织的脓肿、脑膜炎、化脓性肺炎、脓胸、创伤传染及火伤、心内膜炎。
    ２、一切嫌气性梭菌传染、气体坏疸、恶性水肿。
    ３、一切合并菌血症之溶血性链球菌传染及一切严重的局部传染、蜂窝织炎、链菌性肺炎、脓胸、产褥脓毒症、腹膜炎。
    ４、一切肺炎球菌传染。
    ５、一切淋球菌传染、淋球菌性关节炎、淋菌性眼炎、心内膜炎、副睾丸炎、腹膜炎。
    乙、青霉素亦属有效而尚待考虑之病症：
    梅毒、放线菌病、白喉。
    丙、青霉菌素无效的病症：
    一、一切革拉氏阴性杆菌，如伤寒、赤痢杆菌、大肠杆菌、霍乱、鼠疫菌。
    二、滤过性传染：流感、天冷疮、嗜眠性脑膜炎。
    三、结核病、急性偻林致斯、红斑性狼疮、溃疡性大肠炎、疟疾、癌症。
    盘尼西林之应用方法：
    口服法　普通的青霉制剂口服无大效，因它会被胃酸破坏；但现有加有缓冲剂之青霉素内服片，使胃酸不致影响。惟内服量须大于注射４—５倍，普通每片至少含有五万单位，并亦只适应于轻症，饭前半小时或饭后二小时内服。
    注射法　普通是干燥粉末分２０．１０．５０．１００万单位数种及３０万单位之油蜡剂，用前须将粉末以无菌蒸溜水或生理盐水或５％葡萄糖液溶化为一定浓度，注射于肌肉、静脉、皮下、背椎腔或其他体腟中。
    肌肉注射最为常用，以三角肌、臀肌及大腿肌为最宜。注射液每ＣＣ至少含２０．０００单位，隔三小时一次。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北人民政府卫生部
