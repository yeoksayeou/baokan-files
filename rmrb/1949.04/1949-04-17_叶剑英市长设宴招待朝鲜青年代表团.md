Title: Mayor Ye Jianying Hosts Banquet for Korean Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-17
Page: 1

　　Mayor Ye Jianying Hosts Banquet
　　To Welcome the Korean Youth Delegation
　　[This newspaper report] On the evening of the 15th, the Mayor of Beiping, Ye Jianying, hosted a banquet at the Six Nations Hotel to welcome the Korean Youth Delegation consisting of Zhang Ruizhen, Li Chonggen, and Li Jinshun. General Lin Biao and General Nie Rongzhen were also present, extending a warm welcome and raising their glasses to congratulate the eternal unity of the people and youth of China and Korea.

### 叶剑英市长设宴招待朝鲜青年代表团

1949-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　叶剑英市长设宴
  　招待朝鲜青年代表团
    【本报讯】十五日晚，北平市长叶剑英在六国饭店设宴招待朝鲜青年代表团张瑞振、李重根、李金顺。林彪将军、聂荣臻将军均出席参加，深致欢迎之意，并举杯祝贺中鲜两国人民及青年团结万岁。
