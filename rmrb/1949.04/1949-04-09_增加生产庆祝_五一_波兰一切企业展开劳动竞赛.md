Title: Increasing Production to Celebrate "May Day": All Enterprises in Poland Launch Labor Emulation Drives
Author:
Date: 1949-04-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, Eighth Dispatch: TASS, Warsaw: Labor emulation drives to celebrate the May First International Labor Day are unfolding in all enterprises in Poland. Workers at the Badari Metallurgical Factory have undertaken several specific tasks and decided to involve all workers in the factory in the labor emulation drive, striving to greatly exceed production quotas. Workers at the Lublin Tannery decided to increase production to celebrate May Day, guaranteeing the completion of the year's production by October. Workers at the Electrical Equipment Factory decided to complete the Three-Year Plan by May 1st this year and save 5 million Polish zlotys. Workers across the country have made similar pledges.

### 增加生产庆祝“五一”　波兰一切企业展开劳动竞赛

1949-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　增加生产庆祝“五一”
  　波兰一切企业展开劳动竞赛
    【新华社北平八日电】塔斯社华沙讯：为庆祝五一国际工人节而进行的劳动竞赛，在波兰的一切企业中展开。巴达里冶金工厂的职工已承担若干具体的任务，并决定使工厂全体工人卷入劳动竞赛，争取大大超过生产定额。卢布林制革厂工人决定增加生产来庆祝五一节，保证在十月完成全年的生产。电气装备工厂工人决定在今年五月一日完成三年计划，并节约波币五百万。全国各地工人都作出类似的保证。
