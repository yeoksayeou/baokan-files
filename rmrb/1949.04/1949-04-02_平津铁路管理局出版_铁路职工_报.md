Title: Pingjin Railway Administration Publishes "Railway Worker" Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1949-04-02
Page: 2

Pingjin Railway Administration
　　Publishes "Railway Worker" Newspaper
    [This newspaper] The Pingjin Railway Administration will publish the "Railway Worker" newspaper starting from the 1st. The main purpose is to reflect the production, learning, and lives of all employees of the Pingjin Railway. The newspaper is tentatively scheduled to be published every five days, typeset, and octavo in size.
　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　(Qing)

### 平津铁路管理局出版“铁路职工”报

1949-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津铁路管理局
  　出版“铁路职工”报
    【本报讯】平津铁路管理局从一日起出版“铁路职工”报。主要目的是反映平津铁路全体职工们的生产、学习和生活的情况。该报暂定为五日刊，铅印，八开。  　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　（青）
