Title: Exchange Rate Between Shanghai and Liberated Areas Further Increased to 1:300 Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-04-19
Page: 2

　　[Ping Shi News] The People's Bank of China's exchange rate between Shanghai and Beiping (Peiping) will be further increased to 1:300 starting from the 19th.
