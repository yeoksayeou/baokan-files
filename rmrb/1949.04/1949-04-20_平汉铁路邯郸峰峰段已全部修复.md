Title: Pinghan Railway邯郸-峰峰 Section Fully Repaired
Author: You Min
Date: 1949-04-20
Page: 1

　　Pinghan Railway
  　邯郸-峰峰 Section Fully Repaired
    [Jinan Telegram] The Handan to Fengfeng section of the Pinghan Railway, the trunk line of forty kilometers, with a total length of forty-three kilometers, was fully repaired by March 23. Thanks to the enthusiastic creativity of the workers, the peasants' contribution of buried parts and equipment, and the hard work style of the cadres, the project was completed one month ahead of the original plan to complete the entire project by the end of April. Light and fast trains are now used to transport passengers, merchants, goods, and coal.
　　　　　　　　　　　(You Min)

### 平汉铁路邯郸峰峰段已全部修复
幼民
1949-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　平汉铁路
  　邯郸峰峰段已全部修复
    【冀南电】平汉铁路邯郸至峰峰段，干线四十公里，全长四十三公里，至三月二十三日已全部修复。由于依靠职工们的热情创造、农民贡献埋藏的零件器材，与干部的艰苦作风，比四月底全部结束工程的原计划，提前了一月完成，现在已用轻便快车，运送客商货物及煤炭。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（幼民）
