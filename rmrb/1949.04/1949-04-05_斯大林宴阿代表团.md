Title: Stalin Hosts Albanian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-05
Page: 3
　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: Moscow dispatch: On the 1st, Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet in the Kremlin for the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, including General Hoxha, Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, and Koleka, Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and other high-ranking officials.
