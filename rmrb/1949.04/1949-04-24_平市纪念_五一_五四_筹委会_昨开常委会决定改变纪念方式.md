Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 1
	
	Preparatory Committee for Commemorating "May 1st" and "May 4th" in Beiping Holds Standing Committee Meeting, Decides to Change Commemoration Method

	[Our Paper's News] In order to adapt to the current situation in Beiping, the Preparatory Committee for the "May 1st" and "May 4th" Commemoration in Beiping held its third Standing Committee meeting yesterday (23rd). It was resolved to cancel the "May 1st" and "May 4th" memorial rallies, and instead hold a city-wide representative memorial meeting and decentralized activities. Under the unified leadership of the Preparatory Committee, the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, the Pingjin Railway Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, the Beiping Student Federation, and the Beiping Municipal People's Government Education Bureau will each take overall responsibility. The content of the commemoration will be enriched through various means, such as holding seminars, lectures, evening parties, and ball games. The Propaganda Committee's activities will be held as usual.
					(Bai Sheng)

### 平市纪念“五一”“五四”筹委会　昨开常委会决定改变纪念方式
柏生
1949-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市纪念“五一”“五四”筹委会
  　昨开常委会决定改变纪念方式
    【本报讯】为了适应北平目前情况，北平市“五一”“五四”纪念会筹委会昨（二十三）日召开第三次常委会。决议“五一”“五四”两天纪念大会停开，举行全市代表纪念会及分散性活动。在筹委会统一领导下，分别由北平市职工总会筹委会、平津铁路总工会筹委会、北平市学联、北平市人民政府教育局负总责任。并以各种方式充实纪念内容，如召开座谈会、讲演会、晚会、球赛活动等。宣委会活动照旧举行。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
