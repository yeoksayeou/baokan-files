Title: Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission Announces Provisional Regulations on Railway Military Transportation
Author:
Date: 1949-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, April 22nd: The Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, in order to ensure the smooth progress of railway military transportation and the rational use of railway transportation without wasting railway transport capacity, has specially formulated the Provisional Regulations on Railway Military Transportation (published on page four of today's newspaper) and ordered its strict implementation by its subordinates on April 10th. The original text of the order is as follows:

Order of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission

1. The Provisional Regulations on Railway Military Transportation are hereby formulated and promulgated for implementation from today.

2. All field armies and military region organs and troops under the People's Liberation Army, as well as the Railway General Bureau, Railway Administration Bureaus, and stations under the Ministry of Railways of this Commission, must strictly implement these regulations and shall not violate them.

3. From the date of promulgation of these regulations, all military transportation regulations and measures previously issued by military organs and railway bureaus that conflict with these regulations shall be invalidated.

Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Peng Dehuai
April 10th, Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of China

### 中国人民革命军事委员会　公布铁路军运暂行条例

1949-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民革命军事委员会
  　公布铁路军运暂行条例
    【新华社北平二十二日电】中国人民革命军事委员会为保证铁路军事运输的顺畅和合理使用铁路运输而不浪费铁路运输力，特制定铁路军运暂行条例（载本报今日四版），于四月十日命令所属切实执行。命令原文如下：
    中国人民革命军事委员会命令
    一、兹制定铁路军运暂行条例，自即日起公布施行。
    二、凡属人民解放军所属之野战军及各级军区机关部队及本委员会铁道部所属之铁路总局、铁路管理局、车站必须切实执行本条例，不得违反。
    三、自本条例公布之日起，各军事机关、各铁路局以前颁布之军运条例及办法，凡与本条例抵触者一律作废。
    主席毛泽东　副主席朱德　刘少奇　周恩来　彭德怀
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年四月十日
