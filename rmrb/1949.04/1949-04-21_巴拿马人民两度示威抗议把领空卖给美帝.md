Title: Panamanian People Demonstrate Twice to Protest Selling Airspace to American Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1949-04-21
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: Reports from various sources: The Panamanian people held demonstrations on the 9th and 18th, protesting against the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the US-Panama Aviation Agreement. The government dispatched mounted police to suppress the protests and used tear gas, sabers, and gunfire to attack patriotic masses. Several demonstrators were injured. Note: The reactionary Panamanian Congress, disregarding the people's will to safeguard sovereignty and independence, passed the US-Panama Aviation Agreement on the 12th, selling airspace rights and retaining US Air Force bases, providing US imperialism with "convenience" to "protect" the Panama Canal.

### 巴拿马人民两度示威抗议把领空卖给美帝

1949-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴拿马人民两度示威抗议把领空卖给美帝
    【新华社北平十九日电】各方报道：巴拿马人民在九日和十八日两度举行示威运动，抗议北大西洋公约和美巴航空协定。政府出动骑警镇压，并以催泪弹、马刀和开枪等暴力方法，向爱国群众进攻。示威者有数人受伤。按：巴拿马反动议会曾不顾人民维护主权独立的意志，于十二日通过了出卖领空权的美巴航空协定，保留美国空军基地。使美帝国主义获得“保护”巴拿马运河的“便利”。
