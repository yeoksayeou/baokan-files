Title: Announcement from the No. 1 Branch of North China Agricultural Machinery Factory
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 3

　　Announcement from the No. 1 Branch of North China Agricultural Machinery Factory:
　　One, our factory manufactures "sprayers" and "dusters." Please place orders in advance from all regions.
　　Two, our factory produces "Rotenone Powder" (Delice Powder), a special effect medicine for killing aphids (mealybugs), flea beetles (cabbage worms), and red spiders (fire dragons, red mites).
　　Three, our factory produces poisonous powder and liquid for killing mole crickets. It is easy to use and highly effective.
　　Four, our factory still has various insecticides and fungicides such as "Lead Arsenate," "Calcium Arsenate," "Ceresan," "Long Mercury," "Bordeaux Powder," etc. In particular, "Ceresan" is most effective in preventing millet head blight (blast, looking old, exposed hearts) and various crop smuts [covered smut, bunt].
　　Five, our factory also produces "Insect Enemy" liquid to kill mosquito and fly eggs, etc.
    We welcome introductions and adoptions from local governments and agricultural organizations.
　　　　　　　　　　　Factory Address: No. 9, Luchang Street, North Gate of the Temple of Agriculture, Beiping.
　　　　　　　　　　　Telephone: (3) 4397 (3) 2566

### 华北农业机械厂第一分厂启事

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　华北农业机械厂第一分厂启事
    一、本厂制造“喷雾器”“喷粉器”请各地预先订购
    二、本厂出品杀除蚜虫（腻虫蜜虫）猿叶虫（白菜牛子）和红蜘蛛（火龙红沙）的特效药“鱼滕粉”（地力斯粉）
    三、本厂出品毒杀蝼蛄的药粉和药水，使用方便，效力宏大
    四、本厂尚存各种杀虫杀菌药剂如“砒酸铅”“砒酸钙”“赛拉散”“龙汞”“波尔多粉”等，特别是“赛拉散”防治谷子白发病（枪杆，看谷老，露心）和各种庄稼的黑穗病【黑疸乌霉】最有效。
    五、本厂还制喷杀蚊蝇蛋等的“虫敌”药水
    欢迎各地政府及农业机关团体介绍采用
