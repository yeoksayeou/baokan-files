Title: Peking University New Youth League General Branch Established
Author: Zhang Zhenming
Date: 1949-04-07
Page: 2

　　Peking University New Youth League General Branch Established
    ▲The General Branch of the New Youth League of Peking University held its establishment meeting on the afternoon of the 6th, inviting professors and students to participate. A recreational evening party was held at 7:30 PM, with wonderful programs such as "Anti-Overturning Struggle" and marching dance.
　　　
　　(Zhang Zhenming)

### 北大新青团总支成立
张震明
1949-04-07
第2版()
专栏：学校生活

　　北大新青团总支成立
    ▲北大新青团总支部六日下午开成立大会，请教授和同学参加。晚七时半举行游艺晚会，有“反翻摆斗争”、进军舞等精彩节目。　　　
　　（张震明）
