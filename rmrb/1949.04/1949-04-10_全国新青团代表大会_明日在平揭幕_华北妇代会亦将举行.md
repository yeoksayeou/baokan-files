Title: National New Youth League Congress to Open Tomorrow in Beiping; North China Women's Congress Also to Be Held
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-04-10
Page: 1

　　National New Youth League Congress
  　to Open Tomorrow in Beiping
  　North China Women's Congress Also to Be Held
    [Our Dispatch] The First National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League will open in Beiping tomorrow (the 11th). A preparatory meeting will be held today (the 10th) in the auditorium of the Beiping Art Academy to approve the presidium of the congress and the agenda.
    Since the preparatory committee issued a notice, determining that the first National Congress of the Youth League would be held in Beiping, and deciding on the method of selecting representatives with each province or province-level city as a unit, electing one representative for every two thousand League members, and each preparatory committee above the provincial level could send two to three representatives, Youth League member representatives from various regions have been reporting in since April 5th. As of today, 245 representatives have reported, representing (according to incomplete statistics) 179,000 League members. (Jin Feng)
    [Our Dispatch] In order to convey the resolutions of the National Women's Congress and study and discuss women's work in North China, the North China Women's Representative Conference, jointly prepared by the Women's Federations of the Jin-Cha-Ji and Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu border regions, is scheduled to be held in Beiping on April 15th, with registration beginning on the 13th. The main contents of the conference are: (1) Report on the current political situation and tasks; (2) Conveying the resolutions of the National Women's Congress; (3) Report on women's work in North China. The first preparatory meeting was held on April 5th. In order to facilitate the advancement of work, the conference has established a secretariat, an organization department, and a research group, which are now carrying out their work separately and actively preparing for the conference.

### 全国新青团代表大会　明日在平揭幕　华北妇代会亦将举行
金凤
1949-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国新青团代表大会
  　明日在平揭幕
  　华北妇代会亦将举行
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会明（十一日）日在平揭幕。今（十日）在北平艺术专科学校大礼堂开预备会议，将通过大会主席团及议事日程等。
    自从筹委会发出通知，确定在北平召开青年团第一次全国代表大会，决定代表产生办法以省或相当于省的市为单位，每两千团员选出代表一人，省以上的各级筹委会可选派代表两人至三人后，四月五日起，各地区的青年团员代表纷纷报到，至今日为止，报到代表有二百四十五人。代表团员（据不完全的统计）十七万九千人。　　　　　　　　　　（金凤）
    【本报讯】为传达全国妇代大会决议，研究讨论华北妇女工作，由晋察冀、晋冀鲁豫两边区妇联负责筹备召开的华北妇女代表会议，定于四月十五日在北平举行，十三日开始报到。会议主要内容为：（一）目前政治形势与任务的报告；（二）传达全国妇代大会的决议；（三）华北妇女工作报告。并于四月五日已开过第一次筹备会。为便利工作之推行，大会设有秘书处、组织部、资料研究组，现已分别进行工作，积极筹备大会事宜。
