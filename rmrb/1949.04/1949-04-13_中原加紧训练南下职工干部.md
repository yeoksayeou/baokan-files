Title: Central Plains Intensifies Training of Southern-Bound Employee Cadres
Author:
Date: 1949-04-13
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 12th—In order to strengthen the training of employee cadres heading south, the Central Plains Bureau of the CPC Central Committee has established the Central Plains Employee Cadre School, which opened on the 29th of last month. Of the 206 graduates from the first brigade of the Kaifeng Employee School, 120 have been approved to go south. Students in the second and third brigades are now doubling their efforts in their studies, preparing to go south together after graduation.

### 中原加紧训练南下职工干部

1949-04-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　中原加紧训练南下职工干部
    【新华社中原十二日电】中共中央中原局为加强训练南下职工干部，特创办中原职工干部学校，并已于上月二十九日开学。开封职工学校第一大队毕业学员二百零六人中，有一百二十人已获准南下。二、三大队学生现正加倍学习，准备毕业后相随南进。
