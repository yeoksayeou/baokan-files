Title: Tsinghua Held Its 38th Anniversary Celebration Yesterday
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-04-25
Page: 2

Tsinghua Held Its 38th Anniversary Celebration Yesterday

[Our Newspaper] Yesterday (the 24th), Tsinghua University held its 38th anniversary celebration and invited alumni to return to the school. This was Tsinghua's first alumni homecoming day after liberation. More than a thousand alumni returned, accounting for more than two-thirds of the total.

(Bai Sheng)

### 清华昨日举行卅八周年校庆

1949-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　清华昨日举行卅八周年校庆
    【本报社】昨（二十四）日清华大学举行三十八周年校庆祝纪念，招待校友返校，这是解放后清华第一个返校节。一千多个返校的占三分之二以上。
