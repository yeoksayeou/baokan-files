Title: People's Bank of China Approves Juxingcheng to Handle Out-of-District Foreign Exchange

Author:

Date: 1949-04-12
Page: 2

The People's Bank of China in this city has approved Juxingcheng as an authorized bank for out-of-district foreign exchange transactions, effective yesterday, the 11th. Including the previously approved Jin Cheng, Yanye, China Industrial, Xinhua Trust, and Shanghai Commercial, there are now six authorized banks.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Jian, Yu)

### 中国人民银行核准聚兴诚办区外汇兑

1949-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行核准聚兴诚办区外汇兑
    【本报讯】本市中国人民银行区外汇兑指定银行，于昨十一日起又核准聚兴诚一家。连前次核准之金城、盐业、中国实业、新华信托、上海商业，已有六家。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　（建、宇）
