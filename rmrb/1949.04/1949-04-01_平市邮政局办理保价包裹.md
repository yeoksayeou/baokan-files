Title: Ping City Post Office Handles Insured Packages
Author:
Date: 1949-04-01
Page: 2

　　Ping City Post Office
  　Handles Insured Packages
    [Ping City News] The Beiping Postal Administration and its affiliated branch offices have resumed handling "insured package" services since March 29th. Packages sent to various bureaus in the Liberated Areas can be insured at the request of the sender. Valuable items must be insured. In addition to the regular postage and minimum fee, an insurance fee of 3% of the estimated value will be charged. If the insured package is lost, the post office will compensate the full insured value.

### 平市邮政局办理保价包裹

1949-04-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市邮政局
  　办理保价包裹
    【平市讯】北平邮政管理局及所属各支局，自三月二十九日起恢复办理“保价包裹”业务，凡寄往解放区各局之包裹，如寄件人就请保价者，可予照办，惟贵重物品必须保价，该项保价包裹，除收取普通资费及起码费外，并按估价百分之三收取保价费，该项保价包裹，如有损失，邮局即按所保价值全部补偿。
