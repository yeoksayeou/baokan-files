　　Circular of the Railway Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of China
    Yung Ji Gao Zi No. 1, April 27th, 38th Year of the Republic of China
    In order to fulfill the purpose of railways serving the people and to facilitate the preparation of freight transportation plans, it is hoped that all public and private enterprises and merchants consigning goods will fill out the attached freight transportation plan form before the 15th of each month for the following month. If the estimated transportation volume is less than 30 cars, submit it to the transportation office of the responsible bureau of the originating station. If the transportation volume is more than 30 cars, send it directly to the Transportation Bureau of the Ministry in Peiping to facilitate the planned allocation of vehicles and benefit transportation. This is hereby announced.
