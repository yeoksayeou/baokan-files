Title: Charles University of Prague Awards Honorary Doctorate to Guo Moruo
Author:
Date: 1949-04-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 28th: Agence France-Presse, Prague: Charles University here awarded an honorary doctorate on the 27th to Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation and Chinese writer and scientist attending the World Congress of Defenders of Peace. Czechoslovak government officials and many figures from the scientific and cultural circles attended the award ceremony.

### 捷京查理士大学　赠郭沫若名誉博士学位

1949-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷京查理士大学
  　赠郭沫若名誉博士学位
    【新华社北平二十八日电】法新社布拉格讯：此间查理士大学于二十七日以名誉博士学位赠予出席世界拥护和平大会的中国代表团团长、中国作家与科学家郭沫若氏。捷政府官员及科学界文化界许多人士，都参加了赠授典礼。
