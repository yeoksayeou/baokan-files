Title: Malayan People's Guerrilla Force Ambush British Troops Near Ipoh
Author:
Date: 1949-04-24
Page: 3

　　Malayan People's Guerrilla Force
  　Ambush British Troops Near Ipoh
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 23rd] According to a United Press report: The Malayan People's Guerrilla Force ambushed a British police patrol near Ipoh in Perak on the 20th. The guerrillas bombarded the police patrol with hand grenades, rifles, and Sten guns, and engaged in fierce fighting with the reinforcing 4th Hussars Regiment. The Hussars Regiment used flamethrowers for the first time in combat against the guerrillas.

### 马来亚人民游击队　怡保附近伏击英军

1949-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　马来亚人民游击队
  　怡保附近伏击英军
    【新华社北平二十三日电】据合众社讯：马来亚人民游击队二十日在霹雳州的怡保附近伏击英警察巡逻队。游击队以手榴弹、步枪与司登枪轰击警察巡罗队，并与增援的第四胡萨兵团展开激战。胡萨兵团在对游击队作战中，第一次使用喷火器。
