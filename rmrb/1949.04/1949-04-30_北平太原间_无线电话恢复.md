Title: Wireless Telephone Service Restored Between Beiping and Taiyuan
Author:
Date: 1949-04-30
Page: 2

　　Beiping-Taiyuan
　　Wireless Telephone Service Restored
　　[Beiping News] Following the liberation of Taiyuan, telegraph communication between the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau and Taiyuan has been restored. As of the 28th of this month, wireless telephone service between Beiping and Taiyuan has also been restored, and commercial calls are now open. Calling hours are from 10:00 to 11:00 AM and 4:00 to 5:00 PM daily. The sound is clear, and connections are quick. The charge for a regular call is only 126 yuan. (Military Control Commission of the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau)

### 北平太原间　无线电话恢复

1949-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平太原间
  　无线电话恢复
    【平市讯】自太原解放后，北平电信局与太原之电报通讯业经恢复。自本月二十八日起，北平至太原之无线电话亦告恢复，并开放商话。通话时间为每日上午十时至十一时及下午四时至五时，声音清晰，接通迅速，寻常叫号通话每次仅收费一百二十六元。　（军管北平电信局）
