Title: Egypt Controls Sinai Peninsula Battlefield; Shot Down Seven Israeli Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st dispatch from Cairo: The Egyptian Army General Headquarters announced today that after fierce fighting with the invading Israeli forces, the Egyptian army has controlled the battle situation in the Sinai Peninsula.

The announcement stated that the safety of the Suez Canal and ships passing through it is not threatened. The announcement said that the Egyptian army is capable of defending the canal under any circumstances.

A communiqué issued by the Egyptian Army General Headquarters on the evening of October 30 stated that the Egyptian army repelled an attack by Israeli forces in the Quseima area of the Sinai Peninsula, inflicting heavy losses on the Israeli forces. The communiqué announced that seven Israeli aircraft had been shot down.

On the other hand, the Israeli government announced on October 30 that Israeli forces had occupied Quseima in the Sinai Peninsula. Quseima is on the highway to Ismailia.

### 埃及已经控制西奈半岛战局  击落以色列飞机七架

1956-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及已经控制西奈半岛战局
    击落以色列飞机七架
    新华社31日讯　开罗消息：埃及军队总司令部今天发表公报说，埃及军队在同以色列入侵部队作了激烈的战斗以后，已经控制住了西奈半岛的战局。
    这项公报说，苏伊士运河和通过运河的船只的安全没有受到威胁。公报说，埃及军队在任何情况下都有能力来保卫运河。
    埃及军队总司令部10月30日晚上发表的公报说，埃及军队在西奈半岛的库塞马地区击退了以色列军队的一次进攻，以色列军队受到重大损失。这项公报宣布，已经有七架以色列飞机被击落。
    另一方面，以色列政府10月30日宣布说，以色列军队已经占领了西奈半岛上的库塞马。库塞马在通往伊士美利亚的公路线上。
