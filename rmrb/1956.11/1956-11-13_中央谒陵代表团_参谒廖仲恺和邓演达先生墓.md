Title: Central Pilgrimage Delegation Visits the Tombs of Mr. Liao Zhongkai and Mr. Deng Yanda
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th -- This afternoon, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and representatives of the Central Pilgrimage Delegation commemorating the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth, accompanied by Hui Yuyu, Governor of Jiangsu Province, paid their respects and homage to the tombs of Mr. Liao Zhongkai and Mr. Deng Yanda after visiting the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum.

Those who visited Mr. Liao Zhongkai's tomb included Soviet representative Cherepanov, the chief advisor to the Whampoa Military Academy founded by Mr. Sun Yat-sen thirty years ago, Ma Kun, the former adjutant to Mr. Sun Yat-sen from Canada, as well as Liao Zhongkai's son, Liao Chengzhi, and daughter, Liao Mengxing.

The visitors also laid wreaths at the tombs of Mr. Liao Zhongkai and Mr. Deng Yanda.

### 中央谒陵代表团  参谒廖仲恺和邓演达先生墓

1956-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央谒陵代表团
    参谒廖仲恺和邓演达先生墓
    新华社南京12日电　今天下午，中华人民共和国副主席、中国共产党中央委员会副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国农工民主党中央委员会主席章伯钧和孙中山诞辰九十周年纪念中央谒陵代表团的代表等，由江苏省省长惠浴宇陪同，在晋谒中山陵以后分别到廖仲恺先生墓和邓演达先生墓瞻仰吊念。
    到廖仲恺先生墓前参谒的有：苏联代表、三十年前孙中山先生所创办的黄埔军校总顾问契列班诺夫、加拿大的前中山先生副官马坤，还有廖仲恺先生的儿子廖承志、女儿廖梦醒。
    参谒者还在廖仲恺先生墓前和邓演达先生墓前献了花圈。
