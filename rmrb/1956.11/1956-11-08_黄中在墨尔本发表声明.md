Title: Huang Zhong Issues Statement in Melbourne
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, November 7th: Huang Zhong, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation to the 16th Olympic Games, said here on the evening of November 6th that unless the International Olympic Committee (IOC) rescinds its decision to allow Taiwan to send a team to the 16th Olympic Games, China will not participate in the 16th Olympic Games.

At a press conference, Huang Zhong issued a statement to reporters from the Australian Broadcasting Corporation and local newspapers, explaining the reasons as follows:

	I. The International Olympic Committee's recognition of Taiwan as an Olympic Committee is a violation of the Olympic Charter. The Chinese Olympic Committee has long been a member of the International Olympic Organization. The Olympic Charter stipulates that a country can only have one Olympic Committee and can only send one delegation. The Chinese Olympic Committee invited athletes from Taiwan Province to participate in the national Olympic trials in order to form a unified Chinese Olympic delegation.

	II. This practice of the International Olympic Committee has caused the problem of creating "two Chinas" in sports, which the Chinese people resolutely oppose.

	III. The Chinese people cannot tolerate the decision of the Executive Committee of the International Olympic Committee to change the name of the People's Republic of China to "Beijing China".

### 黄中在墨尔本发表声明

1956-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    黄中在墨尔本发表声明
    新华社墨尔本7日电　中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会代表团秘书长黄中11月6日晚上在这里说，除非国际奥林匹克委员会取消准许台湾派遣一个队参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会的决定，否则中国不参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会。
    黄中在一次记者招待会上向澳大利亚广播公司和当地报纸的记者发表了一项声明，声明中说明理由如下：
    一、国际奥林匹克委员会承认台湾为奥林匹克委员的做法是违反奥林匹克宪章的。中国奥林匹克委员会早就是国际奥林匹克组织的会员。奥林匹克宪章规定，一个国家只能有一个奥林匹克委员会，只能派遣一个代表队。中国奥林匹克委员会曾邀请台湾省的运动员参加全国的奥林匹克选拔赛，以便组成一个统一的中国奥林匹克代表队。
    二、国际奥林匹克委员会的这种做法，引起了在体育运动方面制造“两个中国”的问题，这是中国人民所坚决反对的。
    三、中国人民不能容忍国际奥林匹克委员会执行委员会把中华人民共和国的名称改为“北京中国”的决定。
