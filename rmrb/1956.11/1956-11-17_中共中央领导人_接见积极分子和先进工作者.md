Title: Central CCP Leaders Receive Activists and Advanced Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, this afternoon received all the representatives attending the National Congress of Socialist Construction Activists of Martyrs' Families, Military Dependents, Disabled Soldiers, and Demobilized Soldiers.
    Xie Juezai, Minister of Internal Affairs, and others were also present at the reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, this afternoon received all the representatives attending the Representative Conference of Activists of the Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.  Luo Ruiqing, Commander of the Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Minister of Public Security, and others were present at the reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, this afternoon received all the representatives attending the First National Representative Conference of Advanced Grain Workers. Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food, and others were present at the reception.

### 中共中央领导人  接见积极分子和先进工作者

1956-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人
    接见积极分子和先进工作者
    新华社16日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、陈云，总书记邓小平，今天下午接见了出席全国烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人社会主义建设积极分子大会的全体代表。
    接见时，内务部长谢觉哉等也在座。
    新华社16日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、陈云，总书记邓小平，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军公安军积极分子代表会议的全体代表。接见的时候，中国人民解放军公安军司令员、公安部部长罗瑞卿等也在座。
    新华社16日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、陈云，总书记邓小平，今天下午接见了出席第一次全国粮食先进工作者代表会议的全体代表。接见的时候，粮食部部长章乃器等也在座。
