Title: Hungary Gradually Returns to Normal Order
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 7th (TASS dispatch): The domestic situation in Hungary is gradually returning to normal.

　　The capital, Budapest, and most areas of the provinces are showing calm.

　　The Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government organs are taking various measures to ensure the uninterrupted supply of goods to the residents.

### 匈牙利逐渐恢复正常秩序

1956-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利逐渐恢复正常秩序
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯7日讯：匈牙利国内情况逐渐恢复正常。
    首都布达佩斯和各省大多数地区呈现安宁。
    匈牙利工农革命政府机关正采取各种措施保证把物品不间断地供应给居民。
