Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Watches Soviet Circus Performance
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Vice Chairman Zhu De watched the performance of the Soviet Circus tonight and received the head of the circus, Asanov, and the main performers of the circus during the intermission.

### 朱副主席观看苏联马戏团演出

1956-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席观看苏联马戏团演出
    新华社27日讯　朱德副主席今晚观看了苏联马戏团的演出，并且在休息时间接见了马戏团团长阿萨诺夫和马戏团的主要演员。
