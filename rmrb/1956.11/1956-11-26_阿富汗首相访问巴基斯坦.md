Title: Afghan Prime Minister Visits Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 25th: Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud arrived in Karachi by special plane from Kabul on the afternoon of the 24th. At the invitation of the Pakistani government, he will pay an eight-day official visit to the country.

### 阿富汗首相访问巴基斯坦

1956-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相访问巴基斯坦
    新华社卡拉奇25日电　阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德在24日下午乘专机从喀布尔到达卡拉奇。他应巴基斯坦政府的邀请，将在这个国家作八天的正式访问。
