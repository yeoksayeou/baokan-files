Title: Egyptian Intelligence Chief States UN Forces Must be Stationed with Egypt's Consent
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 10th (TASS dispatch): The Middle East News Agency reports that the Director of Egyptian Intelligence, Abdul Kader Hatem, said that the Egyptian government agrees in principle to the organization of an international police force and is now studying the list of countries participating in this force.

He added that Egypt has requested the UN Secretary-General to provide some clarifications and explanations before making a final response.

Hatem pointed out that the Egyptian government, throughout the entire day of November 9th, engaged with UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld through Egypt's Permanent Representative to the UN, Omar Lutfi, to determine the areas where the international police force would be stationed.

Hatem emphasized that it can be seen from the report submitted by Hammarskjöld to the UN General Assembly on November 7th that the international police force can only be formed with the consent of the Egyptian government, and the location of the international police force must also be determined with the prior consent of the Egyptian government.

Hatem said that Hammarskjöld pointed out that the international police force will be stationed on the armistice line, which is stipulated according to the 1949 Egypt-Israel armistice agreement. Hammarskjöld explained in the report that the function performed by the international police force is temporary. This function will be terminated at the same time as the end of the state of emergency. This force will not carry out any military tasks and has no power to interfere in matters related to the privileges and sovereignty of the host country.

### 埃及情报司司长表示  联合国部队驻地须经埃及同意

1956-11-12
第7版()
专栏：

    埃及情报司司长表示
    联合国部队驻地须经埃及同意
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社开罗10日讯：中东通讯社报道，埃及情报司司长阿卜杜勒·卡德尔·哈特姆说，埃及政府原则上同意组织国际警察部队，现在正在研究参加这支部队的国家的名单。
    他又说，埃及已经要求联合国秘书长作一些阐明和解释，然后再作最后答复。
    哈特姆指出，埃及政府在11月9日一整天通过埃及驻联合国常驻代表欧默尔·卢特菲，为确定国际警察部队驻扎地区一事同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德进行接触。
    哈特姆着重指出，从哈马舍尔德11月7日向联合国大会所提出的报告中可以看出，国际警察部队只有经埃及政府同意才可以组成，而国际警察部队的驻地也同样要经过埃及政府事先同意才能确定。
    哈特姆说，哈马舍尔德指出，国际警察部队将驻扎在停战线上，这条停战线是根据1949年埃及—以色列停战协定所规定的。哈马舍尔德在报告中说明，国际警察部队所执行的职能是临时性的。这种职能在非常状态结束的同时宣告停止。这支部队不执行任何军事任务，也没有权力干预有关驻在国特权和主权的问题。
