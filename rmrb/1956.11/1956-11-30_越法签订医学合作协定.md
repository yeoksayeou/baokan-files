Title: Vietnam and France Sign Medical Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency report: Following recent talks, the delegations from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and France issued a joint communique stating that two agreements on medical cooperation between the two countries, lasting five years, were signed in Hanoi on the 26th. The first agreement, effective from January 1, 1957, stipulates that the Saint Paul Hospital in Hanoi will become a joint hospital. The other agreement outlines the forms of cooperation between the two countries in cancer research.

### 越法签订医学合作协定

1956-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    越法签订医学合作协定
    据新华社讯　越南民主共和国和法国代表团最近会谈后发表联合公报说，为期五年的关于两国之间的医学合作的两个协定26日在河内签字。第一个协定，从1957年1月1日起，河内的圣保罗医院将成为一个联合医院。另一个协定规定两国之间在癌症研究方面进行合作的形式。
