Title: ACFTU Sends Telegram to Secretary General of the Egyptian Trade Union Congress

Author:

Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) today sent a telegram to Kamal, Secretary General of the Egyptian Republic Trade Union Congress, supporting the just struggle of the Egyptian people.

The telegram said: All Chinese workers express boundless indignation at the unjust act of armed aggression against Egypt by British and French colonialists. We firmly protest against the aggressive crimes of the British and French governments against the Egyptian people. We, on behalf of millions of workers across the country, once again express our full support for the heroic struggle of the Egyptian people under the leadership of President Nasser to defend national independence and state sovereignty. We firmly believe that final victory will surely belong to the Egyptian people.

### 全总致电埃及工会大会总书记

1956-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全总致电埃及工会大会总书记
    新华社2日讯　中华全国总工会今天打电报给埃及共和国工会大会总书记卡迈勒，支持埃及人民的正义斗争。
    电报说：中国全体职工对英、法殖民主义者武装侵略埃及的不义行为，表示无比的义愤。我们坚决抗议英、法政府对埃及人民的侵略罪行。我们代表全国千百万职工对埃及人民在纳赛尔总统领导下进行的保卫民族独立和国家主权的英勇斗争，再一次表示全力的支持。我们深信最后胜利必定属于埃及人民。
