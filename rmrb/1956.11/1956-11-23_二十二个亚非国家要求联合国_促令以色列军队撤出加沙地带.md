Title: Twenty-Two Asian and African Nations Call on the United Nations to Urge Israeli Troops to Withdraw from the Gaza Strip
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 22nd: Representatives of twenty-two Asian and African nations at the United Nations requested Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the evening of the 21st to strive to urge Israeli troops to withdraw from the Gaza Strip.

In their joint letter, they stated: "The Israeli occupying forces have used methods of intimidation, implemented policies of terror, and committed acts of massacre, resulting in hundreds of casualties; near-famine conditions and a lack of supplies have further exacerbated the already miserable situation of refugees and residents in that area.

"The non-withdrawal of Israeli troops and the aforementioned distressing acts flagrantly violate the United Nations General Assembly resolutions of November 3rd and 7th, 1956, and violate the basic principles of the United Nations Charter, as well as the principles of justice and human rights."

The representatives of the following countries signed the letter to Hammarskjöld: Afghanistan, Cambodia, Egypt, India, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Nepal, the Philippines, Sudan, Thailand, Yemen, Burma, Ceylon, Indonesia, Iran, Pakistan, Morocco, Saudi Arabia, Syria, and Tunisia.

### 二十二个亚非国家要求联合国  促令以色列军队撤出加沙地带

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    二十二个亚非国家要求联合国
    促令以色列军队撤出加沙地带
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：联合国二十二个亚非国家的代表21日晚上要求秘书长哈马舍尔德努力促使以色列军队撤出加沙地带。
    他们在联名信上说：“以色列占领军使用了威吓的方法，实行了恐怖政策，和犯下了屠杀罪行，结果使成百的人伤亡；近乎饥荒的情况和缺乏供应的情况已经使那个地区的难民和居民本来就很悲惨的境况更趋恶化了。
    “以色列军队的不撤退和上述令人痛心的行为彰明较著地违反了1956年11月3日和7日的联合国大会的决议，并且违反了联合国宪章的基本原则以及正义和人权的原则。”
    在致哈马舍尔德的信上签字的有下列国家的代表：阿富汗、柬埔寨、埃及、印度、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、尼泊尔、菲律宾、苏丹、泰国、也门、缅甸、锡兰、印度尼西亚、伊朗、巴基斯坦、摩洛哥、沙特阿拉伯、叙利亚和突尼斯。
