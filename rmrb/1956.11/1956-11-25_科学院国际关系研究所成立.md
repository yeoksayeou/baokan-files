Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Establishes Institute of International Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences Institute of International Relations was established in Beijing today.

    This institute is an academic institution specializing in the study of international issues. It will systematically study international issues to meet the needs of our country's foreign policy and the needs of our people to understand the development of the international situation.

    In the process of preparation, the institute has formulated a twelve-year plan for the study of international relations and has gathered scientific researchers studying international issues from all aspects. It also plans to build a relatively complete specialized library for studying international issues in the next few years.

    Zhang Wentian, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, was invited to speak at today's inauguration ceremony.

    The director of the institute is Meng Yongqian, and the deputy directors are Chen Hansheng and Liu Simu.

### 科学院国际关系研究所成立

1956-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    科学院国际关系研究所成立
    新华社24日讯　中国科学院国际关系研究所今天在北京成立。
    这个研究所是专门研究国际问题的学术机构。它将系统地研究国际问题以适合我国对外政策的需要，适合我国人民关心国际形势发展的需要。
    在筹建过程中，这个研究所已拟定了研究国际关系的十二年规划，并从各方面调集了一些研究国际问题的科学工作人员。还准备在今后几年内筹建一个比较完备的研究国际问题的专业图书馆。
    中共中央政治局候补委员、外交部副部长张闻天应邀在今天的成立大会上讲了话。
    这个研究所的所长是孟用潜，副所长是陈翰笙、刘思慕。
