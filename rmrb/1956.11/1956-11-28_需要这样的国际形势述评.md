Title: Need Such International Situation Commentaries
Author: Yang Guang, Bai Xingliang
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 8

Column: Readers, Authors, Editors

    Need Such International Situation Commentaries
    The two international situation commentaries published in your newspaper on October 24th and November 22nd summarized the new developments in China's foreign relations and the international situation in recent months, helping those who regularly study current events to make a well-founded and analytical summary. At the same time, it also helps people who are usually too busy with work to read the newspaper carefully, to understand the recent international situation more systematically in a short amount of time. We hope that you can publish similar international situation commentaries every certain period.
              (Yang Guang, Bai Xingliang)

### 需要这样的国际形势述评
杨光  白兴亮
1956-11-28
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    需要这样的国际形势述评
    10月24日和11月22日你报发表的两篇国际形势述评，概述了几个月来我国对外关系和国际形势的新发展，帮助那些经常学习时事的人们做了一次既有根据又有分析的总结；同时也帮助了平日因为工作太忙，无暇仔细阅报的人们，花不多的时间就能比较系统地了解最近的国际形势。希望你们能每隔一定的时期，登一次类似这样的国际形势述评。
              （杨光、白兴亮）
