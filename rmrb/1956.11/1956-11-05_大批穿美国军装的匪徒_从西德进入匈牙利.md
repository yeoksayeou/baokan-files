Title: A Large Number of Bandits in U.S. Military Uniforms Enter Hungary from West Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 4th. TASS, Vienna, 3rd -- The "Voice of the Austrian People" newspaper reporter said in a dispatch from the Austrian-Hungarian border that the Nikolsdorf city border hotel "is like a transit station, batches of Hungarian-speaking people in American military uniforms are constantly coming here from West Germany. Their passports are made in West Germany, and everyone carries marching equipment."
    The newspaper also said that a leaflet calling for "volunteers willing to go to Hungary" has appeared in Vienna.

### 大批穿美国军装的匪徒  从西德进入匈牙利

1956-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    大批穿美国军装的匪徒
    从西德进入匈牙利
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社维也纳3日讯：“奥地利人民之声报”记者在从奥地利和匈牙利边境发来的通讯中说，尼克尔斯多夫市的国境饭店“就像是一个过境转运站，一批批说匈牙利话、穿美国军装的人不断地从西德到这里来。他们的护照是西德制发的，每个人都带有行军装备”。
    这家报纸还说，维也纳已经出现了一种号召“愿意到匈牙利去的志愿者”的传单。
