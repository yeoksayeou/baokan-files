Title: Commending Model Deeds, Exchanging Work Experience: Public Security Forces Active Elements Representative Conference Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Public Security Forces Active Elements Representative Conference opened in Beijing today. Attending the conference were 319 outstanding public security soldiers and representatives from 44 outstanding units. These representatives came from all parts of the country, including the Pamir Plateau, the Qaidam Basin, the Yunnan border, the Inner Mongolia grasslands, the Changbai Mountain forests, and the Fujian front. Among them were public security officers and soldiers of Han, Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Miao, Zhuang, Dong, Yi, Korean, Dai, Uygur, Kawa, and Jingpo nationalities.

Cheng Shicai, Deputy Commander of the Public Security Forces, spoke at the meeting, saying that in the past few years, under the leadership of the Party and the country, the Public Security Forces have carried out arduous struggles,打击了企图进行破坏活动的特务、匪徒和帝国主义的间谍分子, and has打击了企图进行破坏活动的特务、匪徒和帝国主义的间谍分子the security of the country's important industries and railway transportation, and maintained urban and rural public security and the interests of the people. In the future, the border defense forces of the Public Security Forces must be vigilant and strictly guard against any possible infiltration by special agents and spies of imperialism and the Chiang Kai-shek clique in areas where they may infiltrate; in areas adjacent to peaceful and neutral countries, they must fully embody the spirit of developing friendly and good-neighborly relations.

Cheng Shicai then said that the purpose of this meeting is to commend the exemplary deeds of the active elements who have emerged in the Public Security Forces in the task of safeguarding the motherland's construction, exchange experiences, further develop the positive factors of the troops, and better complete the glorious task of safeguarding the motherland's socialist construction.

Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, attended today's opening ceremony and gave instructions to the active elements present.

In the afternoon, Ouyang Yi, Director of the Political Department of the Public Security Forces, delivered a report entitled "Strive to Better Defend the Socialist Construction of the Motherland," and typical reports by representatives of active elements began.

### 表扬模范事迹　交流工作经验  公安军积极分子代表会议在京开幕

1956-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    表扬模范事迹　交流工作经验
    公安军积极分子代表会议在京开幕
    新华社15日讯　中国人民解放军公安军积极分子代表会议今天在北京开幕。出席这次会议的有三百十九名优秀公安战士和四十四个优秀单位的代表。这些代表们来自帕米尔高原、柴达木盆地、云南边疆、内蒙古草原、长白山森林以及福建前线等全国各个地方。他们当中包括汉、蒙、回、藏、苗、僮、侗、彝、朝鲜、傣、维吾尔、佧佤、景颇等民族的公安军军官和士兵。
    公安军副司令员程世才中将在会上讲话，他说，几年来公安军在党和国家的领导下，进行了艰苦的斗争，打击了企图进行破坏活动的特务、匪徒和帝国主义的间谍分子，保卫了国家的重要工业和铁路交通运输的安全，维护了城乡治安和人民的利益。今后公安军边防部队在帝国主义、蒋介石集团的特务和间谍分子可能潜入的地带，必须提高警惕、严加防范；在与和平中立国家毗连的地带，要充分体现出发展友好睦邻关系的精神。
    程世才中将接着说，这次会议的目的，是为了表扬公安军在执行保卫祖国建设事业的任务中所涌现出来的积极分子的模范事迹，交流经验，进一步发扬部队的积极因素，更好地完成保卫祖国社会主义建设的光荣任务。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将出席了今天的开幕式，向到会的积极分子们作了指示。
    下午，公安军政治部主任欧阳毅中将作了“为更好地保卫祖国的社会主义建设而奋斗”的报告，并且开始了积极分子代表的典型报告。
