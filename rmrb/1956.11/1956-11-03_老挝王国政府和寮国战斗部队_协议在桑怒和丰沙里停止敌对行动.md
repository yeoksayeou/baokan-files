Title: Lao Kingdom Government and Lao Fighting Units Agree to Cease Hostilities in Sam Neua and Phongsaly

Author:

Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 2nd: According to a broadcast by Vientiane Radio today, the Lao Kingdom Government and the Lao Fighting Units signed an agreement on the 1st regarding the cessation of hostilities in the provinces of Sam Neua and Phongsaly. These two provinces are the areas where the Lao Fighting Units are concentrated.

At the signing ceremony, the head of the Lao military delegation, Phoumi Vongvichit, delivered a speech expressing satisfaction with the results of the month-long meeting. He said: Although some difficulties were encountered at times, both sides have resolved the issues based on the actual situation and mutual concessions. He said: In this spirit, the remaining issues will also be properly resolved.

The head of the Kingdom Government military delegation, Ouane Rattikone, also expressed satisfaction with the results of the meeting in his speech. He said: Although these results are preliminary, they will help both sides reach further agreements.

### 老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队  协议在桑怒和丰沙里停止敌对行动

1956-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队
    协议在桑怒和丰沙里停止敌对行动
    新华社河内2日电　据万象电台今天广播，老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队已经在1日签订关于在桑怒和丰沙里两省停止敌对行动问题的协议。这两省是寮国战斗部队集结地区。
    在签订协议的会上，寮国军事代表团团长冯·西巴色发表讲话，对一个多月来会议的成果表示满意。他说：尽管有时遇到一些困难，但双方已经根据现实情况和互相让步来解决问题。他说：根据这种精神，遗留的问题也将能够继续得到妥善的解决。
    王国政府军事代表团团长温·拉迪功在讲话中也对会议结果表示满意。他说：尽管这些结果是初步的，但是它将帮助双方取得进一步协议。
