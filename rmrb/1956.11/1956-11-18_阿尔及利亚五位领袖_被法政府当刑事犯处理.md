Title: Five Algerian Leaders Treated as Criminals by the French Government
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 6

　　Algerian Five Leaders
　　Treated as Criminals by the French Government
　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 16th: The French government authorities have rejected the request of the five leaders of the Algerian National Liberation Front, who were kidnapped to France, to be treated as political prisoners.
　　These five leaders were kidnapped by the French authorities by bandit means when they were on a Moroccan plane from Rabat to Tunis, and they are being held as "criminals" in the "Santé" prison in Paris.

### 阿尔及利亚五位领袖  被法政府当刑事犯处理

1956-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚五位领袖
    被法政府当刑事犯处理
    据新华社17日讯　据塔斯社巴黎16日讯：法国政府当局拒绝了被它绑架到法国去的阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的五个领导人提出给他们以政治犯待遇的要求。
    这五个领袖是在乘摩洛哥飞机从拉巴特动身到突尼斯去的时候被法国当局用盗匪手段绑架去法国的，他们被作为“刑事犯”囚禁在巴黎“桑台”监狱里。
