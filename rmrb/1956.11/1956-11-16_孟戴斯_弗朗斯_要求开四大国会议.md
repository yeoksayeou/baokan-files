Title: Mendès France Calls for a Four-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: Paris dispatch: Pierre Mendès France, leader of the Republican Radical and Radical Socialist Party, delivered a speech at a meeting of a provincial organization of the party on the 13th, drawing everyone's attention to the dangerous consequences of the Anglo-French armed intervention in Egypt. France must proactively demand the convening of a Four-Power or Five-Power conference to resolve outstanding issues.

### 孟戴斯—弗朗斯  要求开四大国会议

1956-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    孟戴斯—弗朗斯
    要求开四大国会议
    据新华社15日讯　巴黎消息：共和激进和激进社会党领袖孟戴斯—弗朗斯13日在这个党的一个省组织的会议上发表演说，提请大家注意英法武装干涉埃及的危险后果。法国必须主动要求召开四大国或五大国会议来解决悬而未决的问题。
