Title: Arab Countries Take Action to Support Egypt's Resistance: Syria Announces National Mobilization; Jordan Severs Diplomatic Relations with France and Warns Britain Not to Use Bases in Jordan; Libya Declares No Foreign Troops on Its Territory Allowed to Participate in the War of Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. Damascus Dispatch: The Syrian Supreme Defense Council, after holding a meeting on October 31st, announced a state of emergency and implemented general mobilization throughout the country.

    Meanwhile, according to a report in "Today's Newspaper" on October 31st, Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali said, "Syria will fulfill the obligations stipulated in the military agreement between Syria, Egypt, and Jordan." He said that Syria has "taken all necessary measures to stand with its sister country, Egypt."

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. Amman Dispatch: Jordan Radio broadcast today that Jordan has severed diplomatic relations with France.

    Jordanian Prime Minister Nabulsi summoned the British Ambassador to Jordan on the evening of October 31st and told him that Jordan will never allow Britain, under any circumstances, to use Jordanian territory and airports as military bases to launch aggression against Egypt or any Arab country.

    Nabulsi emphasized that Jordan reserves the right to take free action in the event that Britain uses Jordanian military bases as bases of attack.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. Tripoli Dispatch from TASS: The Libyan government issued a statement on October 31st, stating that the Libyan government resolutely condemns the aggression against the fraternal state of Egypt and sincerely wishes to provide practical and effective assistance to Egypt and other Arab countries in their cause of resisting the aggressors.

    The statement emphasized that Libya will not allow the use of foreign troops stationed on its territory to attack any Arab country.

    Libyan Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sahili, issued the above statement at a press conference held on October 31st.

    The Libyan government's statement also said: "The Libyan government supports the resolution of all international problems within the framework of the United Nations, in accordance with the principles of the United Nations Charter."

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. Baghdad Dispatch from AFP, October 31st: The Iraqi cabinet has decided to lodge a protest with Britain and France against their attitude towards Egypt, which undermines the United Nations Charter.

    The cabinet also decided that Iraq is ready to cooperate at any time with all Arab countries providing assistance to Egypt, including military assistance.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. Beirut Dispatch from TASS, November 1st: According to newspaper reports, the US Embassy in Lebanon has requested the Lebanese Foreign Ministry to allow certain US military ships to enter the port of Beirut.

    Lebanese Prime Minister Yafi resolutely rejected this unreasonable request.

### 阿拉伯国家采取行动支援埃及抗战  叙利亚宣布全国总动员　约旦同法国断绝邦交并警告英国不得使用在约旦的基地　利比亚声明不许境内外国驻军参加侵埃战争

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家采取行动支援埃及抗战
    叙利亚宣布全国总动员　约旦同法国断绝邦交并警告英国不得使用在约旦的基地　利比亚声明不许境内外国驻军参加侵埃战争
    新华社1日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚最高国防委员会在10月31日举行会议后，宣布全国进入紧急状态并且实行总动员。
    同时，据“今天报”10月31日报道，叙利亚总理阿萨利说，“叙利亚将履行叙利亚、埃及和约旦三国军事协定所规定的义务”。他说，叙利亚已经“采取了一切必要的措施来同姐妹国家埃及站在一起”。
    新华社1日讯　安曼消息：约旦电台今天广播，约旦已经同法国断绝外交关系。
    约旦首相纳布西在10月31日晚上召见了英国驻约旦大使，并且告诉他，约旦将永远不允许英国在任何情况下利用约旦的国土和机场作为军事基地，来进行对埃及或任何阿拉伯国家的侵略。
    纳布西强调说，约旦保留有在英国利用约旦的军事基地作为攻击基地的情况下，采取自由行动的权利。
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社的黎波里1日讯：利比亚政府10月31日发表声明说，利比亚政府坚决谴责对兄弟之邦埃及的侵略，并且衷心愿意对埃及和其他阿拉伯国家反击侵略者的事业给以实际有效的援助。
    声明强调利比亚决不允许运用驻在它的领土上的外国军队来进攻任何一个阿拉伯国家。
    利比亚外交部代理部长萨希利在10月31日举行的记者招待会上发布了上述声明。
    利比亚政府的这一声明还说：“利比亚政府拥护根据联合国宪章原则在联合国范围内解决一切国际问题。”
    新华社1日讯　据法新社巴格达10月31日消息：伊拉克内阁已经决定向英法提出抗议，反对它们对埃及采取破坏联合国宪章的态度。
    内阁同时决定，伊拉克准备随时同对埃及提供援助的所有阿拉伯国家进行合作，包括军事援助在内。
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特1日讯：据报纸报道，美国驻黎巴嫩大使馆要求黎巴嫩外交部允许美国某些军用船只驶入贝鲁特港。
    黎巴嫩总理雅非断然地拒绝了这项无理要求。
