Title: British Troops Attack Yemen Border City
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: New York dispatch: Yemen's Chargé d'Affaires, Zabarah, on behalf of the government of the Kingdom of Yemen, notified the member states of the United Nations that British troops had attacked Yemeni cities bordering the Aden Protectorate. This British act of aggression occurred when Yemen's King Ahmad announced that more than 20,000 Yemenis had volunteered to assist Egypt. On the same day, the head of the Yemeni delegation to the United Nations General Assembly, Saif al-Islam al-Hassani, wrote to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, accusing the British authorities in Aden of concentrating troops in some areas of the southern Yemeni border in the past few days, and accusing the British Air Force of constantly violating Yemeni airspace without scruples.

### 英国军队进攻也门边境城市

1956-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    英国军队进攻也门边境城市
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：也门代办扎巴拉赫以也门王国政府的名义通知联合国各会员国说，英国军队已经进攻了同亚丁保护地接界的也门城市。英国的这次侵略行为是在也门艾哈迈德国王宣布有两万多也门人志愿援助埃及的时候发生的。也门出席联合国大会代表团团长赛义弗·伊斯兰·哈萨尼在同一天曾经写信给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，指责亚丁的英国当局过去几天中在也门南部边界的一些地区集结军队，并且指责英国空军毫无顾忌地不断侵犯也门领空。
