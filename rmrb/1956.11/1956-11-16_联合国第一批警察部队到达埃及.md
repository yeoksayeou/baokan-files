Title: The First UN Police Force Arrives in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th. The first contingent of the United Nations Police Force arrived in Egypt this morning.

A UN spokesman said that the ninety-five officers and soldiers, transported by three Swiss aircraft, landed at Abu Suweir Airport, ten miles west of Ismailia.

He said the contingent included four Danish officers and forty-one Danish soldiers, six Norwegian officers and forty-four Norwegian soldiers.

According to plans, the UN Police Force will be stationed in a former British barracks near Abu Suweir Airport, which can accommodate approximately one thousand people.

### 联合国第一批警察部队到达埃及

1956-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国第一批警察部队到达埃及
    新华社15日讯　开罗消息：联合国警察部队的第一批人员已经在今天上午到达埃及。
    一个联合国发言人说，由三架瑞士飞机载运的九十五名官兵在伊士美利亚以西十英里的阿布苏埃尔机场降落。
    他说，这批部队包括四名丹麦军官和四十一名丹麦士兵，六名挪威军官和四十四名挪威士兵。
    根据计划，联合国警察部队将驻扎在阿布苏埃尔机场附近的一个前英国兵营里，这个兵营约可容纳一千人。
