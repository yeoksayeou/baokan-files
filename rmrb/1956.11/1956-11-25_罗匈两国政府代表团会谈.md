Title: Romanian and Hungarian Government Delegations Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 23rd: According to TASS, the government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic has arrived in Budapest from Bucharest and is holding talks with the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government.

The delegation consists of Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party, Prime Minister Stoica, and other Romanian leaders.

### 罗匈两国政府代表团会谈

1956-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    罗匈两国政府代表团会谈
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯23日讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团已经由布加勒斯特到达布达佩斯，并且正在同匈牙利工农革命政府进行谈判。
    代表团由罗马尼亚工人党第一书记乔治乌—德治、部长会议主席斯托伊卡和罗马尼亚其他领导人组成。
