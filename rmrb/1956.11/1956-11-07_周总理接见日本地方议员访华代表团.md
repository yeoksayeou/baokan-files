Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Local Assembly Members' Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the head of the Japanese Local Assembly Members' Delegation to China, Fumio Kawabata, the deputy head, Takeo Urabe, and all members of the delegation this afternoon.

At the same time, Premier Zhou Enlai also received Kazuo Suzuki, executive director of the Japan-China Trade Association, and four Japanese journalists.

In the evening, all members of this delegation left Beijing by train to visit various parts of China. (Attached photo)

### 周总理接见日本地方议员访华代表团

1956-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本地方议员访华代表团
    据新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本地方议员访华代表团团长川端文夫、副团长浦部武夫和全团人员。
    同时，周恩来总理还接见了日本中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄和四位日本新闻记者。
    当晚，这个代表团的全体人员乘火车离开北京去中国各地参观。（附图片）
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在11月6日，接见了日本地方议员访华代表团。图为接见时情形。
