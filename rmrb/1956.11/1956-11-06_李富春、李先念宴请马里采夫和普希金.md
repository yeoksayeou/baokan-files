Title: Li Fuchun and Li Xiannian Host Malysev and Pushkin at a Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Premier Li Fuchun and Li Xiannian of the State Council, in celebration of the 39th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, hosted a banquet this evening for the General Cultural and Educational Advisor and the Deputy General Economic Advisor of the State Council and all the staff of their offices.

    The banquet was held in a hall decorated with small lanterns of various colors. The hall was filled with a cordial and joyful atmosphere. Hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to celebrate this great festival of the world's proletariat.

    Li Fuchun invited everyone to toast the 39th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union, the prosperity of the socialist camp, gratitude to the Soviet experts for their assistance in China's socialist construction, and the victory achieved in suppressing the Hungarian reactionary forces with Soviet assistance.

    Pushkin, Deputy General Economic Advisor of the State Council, on behalf of all Soviet economic system experts, congratulated China on its new achievements in all aspects.

    Malysev, the General Cultural and Educational Advisor of the State Council, also spoke at the banquet. He proposed a toast to the greater achievements of the Chinese people in building socialism. He wished China's science and culture further prosperity.

    Also present were Lin Feng, Director of the Second Office of the State Council, Bo Yibo, Director of the Third Office, Jia Tuofu, Director of the Fourth Office, and Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office.

    During the banquet, Soviet and Chinese comrades performed many artistic programs.

    After the banquet, a film night and dance were held.

### 李富春、李先念宴请马里采夫和普希金

1956-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    李富春、李先念宴请马里采夫和普希金
    新华社5日讯　国务院副总理李富春、李先念为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年，在今天晚上宴请国务院文教总顾问、经济副总顾问和他们办公室的全体工作人员。
    宴会在一个挂满了各种颜色的小灯笼的大厅里举行。大厅里充满了亲切和欢乐的气氛。主人和客人频频举杯祝贺这个全世界无产阶级的伟大的节日。
    李富春请大家为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年，为中苏两国牢不可破的友谊，为社会主义阵营的繁荣，为感谢苏联专家对我国社会主义建设的援助，为在苏联援助下扑灭匈牙利反动势力所取得的胜利干杯。
    国务院经济副总顾问普希金代表全体苏联经济系统的专家祝贺中国在各方面取得新的成就。
    国务院文教总顾问马里采夫也在宴会上讲了话。他提议为中国人民在建设社会主义事业中获得更大的成就而干杯。他祝中国科学文化进一步繁荣。
    出席作陪的有国务院第二办公室主任林枫、第三办公室主任薄一波、第四办公室主任贾拓夫、第六办公室主任王首道等。
    在宴会进行中，苏联同志和中国同志表演了许多文艺节目。
    宴会结束后，举行了电影晚会和舞会。
