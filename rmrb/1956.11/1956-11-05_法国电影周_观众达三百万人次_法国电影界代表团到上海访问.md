Title: "French Film Week" Attracts Three Million Viewers; French Film Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th - The "French Film Week," held successively in ten cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Xi'an, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Shenyang, Harbin, and Urumqi since mid-October, has concluded, attracting three million viewers.

French films have left a wonderful impression on Chinese audiences. During the film week, many literary publications and newspapers published film reviews, praising the high artistic standards of French cinema.

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th - André Barrenton, head of the French film delegation invited to China to participate in the "French Film Week," along with delegation members Alexandre Mnouchkine, Pierre Gout, and Yves Ciampi, left Beijing today for a visit to Shanghai.

While in Beijing, they participated in the "French Film Week" activities and held symposiums with Beijing film professionals to introduce the current state of film work in China and France, expressing their shared desire for friendly cooperation in the film industry. Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, met with all members of the delegation.

On the evening of the 3rd, Wang Lanxi, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Film Bureau, hosted a farewell banquet for the delegation, attended by Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, and more than 20 people from the Beijing film industry.

### “法国电影周”观众达三百万人次  法国电影界代表团到上海访问

1956-11-05
第7版()
专栏：

    “法国电影周”观众达三百万人次
    法国电影界代表团到上海访问
    新华社4日讯　从10月中旬起先后在北京、上海、天津、武汉、西安、广州、重庆、沈阳、哈尔滨、乌鲁木齐等十个城市举行的“法国电影周”，已相继结束，观众达三百万人次。
    法国影片已经留给中国观众美好的印象。电影周举行期间，许多文艺刊物和报纸发表了影评，赞扬法国电影艺术的高度水准。
    新华社4日讯　应邀来我国参加“法国电影周”活动的法国电影界代表团团长安德烈·巴朗，以及团员亚历山大·慕努施金、比埃尔·古罗和益夫·向毕等人在今日离北京赴上海参观访问。
    他们在北京参加了“法国电影周”的活动，并和北京电影界人士举行座谈会，相互介绍中、法两国电影工作现状，共同表示了双方在电影事业方面友好合作的愿望。文化部副部长夏衍接见了全团人员。
    3日晚，文化部部长助理、电影事业管理局局长王阑西设宴为代表团送行，文化部副部长张致祥和北京电影界人士二十多人出席作陪。
