Title: Shimen Reservoir Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 12th - Hubei Province's largest reservoir, the Shimen Reservoir, has recently been completed. A dam as high as 35.35 meters connects the mountains on both sides of the Zhaizi River. The narrow Xiaoshimen entrance has been excavated into a wide spillway. The Zhaizi River water, which used to harm the people, will forever become a source of happiness for the people.

　　The Shimen Reservoir is located in Zhongxiang County and is a comprehensive utilization mountain valley reservoir for flood control, irrigation, and power generation. The reservoir can store 130 million cubic meters of water. With this reservoir, the vast area east of the Hanshui Yaodi and north of the Tianmen River will be free from water and drought disasters. In addition to irrigating the existing 150,000 mu of paddy fields, it can also convert 200,000 mu of dry land into paddy fields. At the same time, this reservoir can also generate more than 400 kilowatts of electricity.

　　The construction of this reservoir started in January of this year.

### 石门水库建成

1956-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

    石门水库建成
    新华社武汉12日电　湖北省目前最大的水库——石门水库，最近已经完工。一座高达三十五点三五公尺的大坝，把寨子河两岸的山峰连成一体。狭窄的小石门口，被开凿成一条宽阔的溢洪道。过去经常为害人民的寨子河水，将永远成为人民幸福的源泉。
    石门水库在钟祥县境，是一座防洪、灌溉、发电等综合利用的山谷水库。水库内可以蓄水一亿三千万立方公尺。有了这座水库，汉水遥堤以东、天门河以北的广大地区将从此免除水、旱灾害，除灌溉现有十五万亩水田外，还可以使二十万亩旱地改为水田。同时，这座水库还能发电四百多瓩。
    这座水库是今年1月开工的。
