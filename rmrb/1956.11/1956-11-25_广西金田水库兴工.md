Title: Construction Begins on Guangxi Jintian Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 3

    Construction Begins on Guangxi Jintian Reservoir
    (Reported by this newspaper) Construction is underway on the largest reservoir in Guangxi Province – the Jintian Reservoir – near Jintian Township, Guiping County, Guangxi Province, the base of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom uprising.
    The entire length of the canal for this reservoir is 512 li, with a total of 1,785 large and small ancillary projects. After the completion of this project, it can irrigate 130,000 mu of paddy fields and prevent drought in 32 townships and towns, as well as general flooding in areas such as Nanmu.

### 广西金田水库兴工

1956-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

    广西金田水库兴工
    本报讯　在太平天国起义的根据地——广西省桂平县金田乡附近，正在兴建一个广西省最大的水库——金田水库。
    这个水库全渠线长五百十二里，大小附属工程共一千七百八十五座。这个工程完工后，可以灌溉水田十三万亩，可以防治三十二个乡、镇的旱灾和南木等区的一般水患。
