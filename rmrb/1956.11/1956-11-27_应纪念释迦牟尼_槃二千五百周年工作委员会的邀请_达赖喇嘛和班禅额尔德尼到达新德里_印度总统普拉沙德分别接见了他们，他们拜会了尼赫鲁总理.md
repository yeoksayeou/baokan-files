Title: At the Invitation of the Working Committee for the Commemoration of the 2500th Anniversary of the Nirvana of Sakyamuni, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Arrive in New Delhi. Indian President Prasad Receives Them Separately; They Call on Prime Minister Nehru.
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 26th: At the invitation of the Working Committee for the Commemoration of the 2500th Anniversary of the Nirvana of Sakyamuni, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni arrived in New Delhi on the afternoon of November 25th on two separate airplanes.

Greeting them at Palam Airport were: the Chairman of the Working Committee, Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan; the Honorary Chairman of the Working Committee, Indian Prime Minister Nehru; Chairman of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, U Nu; Speaker of the Indian People's Assembly, Ayyangar; Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru; Buddhist elders from various countries; Indian government officials, members of parliament, and many prominent figures from all walks of life. Also welcoming them at the airport were the Chinese Buddhist Delegation headed by Xirao Jiacuo, overseas Chinese, and diplomatic representatives from various countries.

Twenty thousand Delhi citizens had gathered at the airport long before the planes arrived. The airport was decorated with flags and banners. When the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni emerged from the cabins, they were greeted with enthusiastic cheers.

Prime Minister Nehru presented hadas to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, expressing his welcome. Both the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni reviewed the guard of honor.

When the Dalai Lama arrived at Palam Airport, accompanied by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to India, Radhakrishnan delivered a welcoming speech, saying that it was a historically significant event for the Dalai Lama to be with them. He extended the warmest welcome to the Dalai Lama on behalf of the Indian government. He expressed his hope that the Dalai Lama's visit would strengthen the cultural and spiritual ties that closely unite India and China.

In his reply, the Dalai Lama expressed his gratitude to the Indian leaders and people for their warm welcome.

When Panchen Erdeni arrived on another plane, Speaker of the People's Assembly, Ayyangar, delivered a welcoming speech, saying that today was a historically significant day for India. Panchen Erdeni also expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome he had received.

The Dalai Lama's mother and sister also arrived in Delhi on the same day.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 26th: Indian President Prasad received the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni separately at the Presidential Palace on the afternoon of the 25th.

When Prasad received the Dalai Lama, Vice President Radhakrishnan was present. Accompanying the Dalai Lama were the Chief Sutra Instructor, Lingcang Living Buddha, Deputy Sutra Instructor, Chijiang Living Buddha, and Kalon Ape Awang Jinmei, among others.

When Prasad received Panchen Erdeni, the Vice President was also present. Accompanying Panchen Erdeni were Zasa Jandong Jigme, Sutra Instructor Enjiu Living Buddha, and Grand Lama Tangqu Yuanpei, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 26th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni paid a visit to Indian Prime Minister Nehru this morning.

Subsequently, they participated in a symposium discussing "The Contribution of Buddhism to Art, Literature, and Philosophy." The symposium was jointly organized by the Working Committee for the Commemoration of the 2500th Anniversary of the Nirvana of Sakyamuni and the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization.

Prime Minister Nehru delivered the opening address at the symposium. The Chinese Buddhist Delegation headed by Master Xirao Jiacuo, as well as representatives from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, also participated in the symposium. ### 应纪念释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年工作委员会的邀请  达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到达新德里  印度总统普拉沙德分别接见了他们，他们拜会了尼赫鲁总理

1956-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应纪念释迦牟尼涅槃二千五百周年工作委员会的邀请
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到达新德里
    印度总统普拉沙德分别接见了他们，他们拜会了尼赫鲁总理
    新华社新德里26日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼应纪念释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年工作委员会的邀请，分乘两架飞机在11月25日下午先后到达新德里。
    在巴兰机场上迎接他们的有：工作委员会的主席、印度副总统拉达克里希南，工作委员会的名誉主席、印度总理尼赫鲁，缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努，印度人民院议长阿延加尔，印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁，各国的佛教长老，印度的政府官员、议员和各阶层的许多著名人士。在机场上欢迎的还有以喜饶嘉错为首的中国佛教代表团、华侨和各国外交代表。
    两万德里市民在飞机到达德里以前很久就聚集在机场上。机场上装饰着旗帜和彩幡。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼从机舱里走出来的时候，受到了热烈的欢呼。
    尼赫鲁总理向达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼献了哈达，向他们表示欢迎。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼都检阅了仪仗队。
    当达赖喇嘛在中国驻印度大使潘自力的陪同下到达巴兰机场时，拉达克里希南致欢迎词说，达赖喇嘛和我们在一起是一个具有历史意义的事件。他代表印度政府向达赖喇嘛表示最热烈的欢迎。他表示希望达赖喇嘛的访问将加强使印中两国紧密团结起来的文化和精神上的联系。
    达赖喇嘛致答词时对印度领袖和人民对他的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    当班禅额尔德尼乘另一架飞机到达时，人民院议长阿延加尔致欢迎词说，对印度来说，今天是有历史意义的一天。班禅额尔德尼也对他所受到的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    达赖喇嘛的母亲和姊姊也在同一天到达德里。
    新华社新德里26日电　印度总统普拉沙德25日下午在总统府分别接见了达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    普拉沙德在接见达赖喇嘛时，在座的有副总统拉达克里希南。陪同达赖喇嘛前往的有正经师林仓活佛，副经师赤江活佛和噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美等。
    普拉沙德在接见班禅额尔德尼的时候，副总统也在座。陪同班禅额尔德尼前往的有扎萨詹东·计晋美，经师恩久活佛和大喇嘛唐曲源培等。
    新华社新德里26日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼今天上午拜会了印度总理尼赫鲁。
    随后，他们参加了讨论“佛教对艺术、文学和哲学的贡献”的座谈会。座谈会是由纪念释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年工作委员会同联合国教育、科学及文化组织合作举办的。
    尼赫鲁总理在座谈会上致开会词。以喜饶嘉错大师为首的中国佛教代表团以及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国的代表也参加了座谈会。（附图片）
