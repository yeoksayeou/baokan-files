Title: "Flora of Guangzhou" Published
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency: "Flora of Guangzhou" Published

	The "Flora of Guangzhou," written by a dozen or so botanists from South China, has been published. The book contains a total of 1.5 million words and 415 illustrations. Almost all plants that can be seen in Guangzhou, whether wild or cultivated, are recorded in this book. It was compiled by scientists from the South China Botanical Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, with the assistance of many local scientists, over a period of four years.

### “广州植物志”出版

1956-11-27
第7版()
专栏：

    “广州植物志”出版
    新华社讯　华南十几位植物学家编写的“广州植物志”出版了。全书共一百五十万字，有四百一十五幅插图。凡是在广州可以看到的植物，无论是野生或是人工栽培的，差不多在这部书里都有记载。它是中国科学院华南植物研究所的科学家们在当地许多科学家的协助下，用了四年时间才编成的。
