Title: Celebrating the 39th Anniversary of the Great October Revolution; Meetings Held in Shenyang, Lhasa, and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Shenyang, Xi'an, Chongqing, Lhasa, Urumqi and other cities held meetings successively on November 6th and 7th to celebrate the October Revolution Day.

    At the meeting held in Shenyang, the person in charge of the Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang, Soviet experts in Shenyang, and the person in charge of the Korean Consulate General in Shenyang were all invited to participate.
    Jiao Ruoyu, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Shenyang, expressed his heartfelt gratitude and high respect to the Soviet people and government for helping our country carry out socialist construction.
    Yerizaveiting, the Soviet Consul General in Shenyang, spoke at the meeting. That evening, Yerizaveiting and his wife held a celebration banquet to entertain people from all walks of life in Shenyang.
    At the celebration meeting in Xi'an, Fang Zhongru, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, listed many specific facts to illustrate the Soviet Union's selfless assistance to China, and pointed out the importance of the unity between China and the Soviet Union and the unity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, regarding the recent international events.
    Soviet expert Doropchev was invited to speak at the meeting. He introduced to the attendees the brilliant achievements of the Soviet Union in the 39 years since its founding.
    At the meeting held in Chongqing, Li Tangbin, President of the Chongqing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Ren Baige, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, both emphasized in their speeches that the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples must be greatly strengthened and consolidated.
    Balishakov, a representative of the Soviet experts in Chongqing, also spoke amidst warm applause. He said that the great achievement of the great October Socialist Revolution is the international friendship between the peoples of all countries. A strong friendship unites all socialist countries. Brotherly friendship unites the Chinese and Soviet peoples forever. We regard this friendship as the source of all achievements of our two peoples and a guarantee of lasting world peace.
    The Lhasa celebration meeting was held in the Lhasa Auditorium, which was decorated with colorful flags.
    Apei Awang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, spoke first at the meeting. He described the brilliant achievements of the Soviet people in building socialism and communism and the many brotherly and selfless assistance they have given to our country's construction since the victory of the October Socialist Revolution 39 years ago. Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke about the brilliant example of the Soviet Union in resolving ethnic issues.
    More than 700 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and Urumqi held a meeting on the morning of the 7th to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Aisihaiti, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, spoke at the meeting. He expressed his deep gratitude for the selfless assistance given by the Soviet Union to China. He asked everyone to better learn from the Soviet Union and specifically apply the Soviet Union's construction experience and scientific and technological achievements to practice. He particularly pointed out that learning from the spirit of friendship and unity of all ethnic groups in the Soviet Union is of great significance for the close unity of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang to build socialism.
    Belov, the Deputy Consul of the Soviet Consulate General in Urumqi, spoke at the meeting. He said: We Soviet people in China are very excited to see the remarkable achievements the Chinese people have made in socialist construction. He said: The Soviet people are determined to fight alongside the Chinese people to defend world peace. In concluding his speech, Belov shouted: Long live the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people - a powerful factor for world peace!

### 庆祝伟大的十月革命节三十九周年  沈阳、拉萨等地举行大会

1956-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝伟大的十月革命节三十九周年
    沈阳、拉萨等地举行大会
    据新华社讯　沈阳、西安、重庆、拉萨、乌鲁木齐等城市，在11月6日、7日，先后举行大会庆祝十月革命节。
    在沈阳举行的大会上，苏联驻沈阳总领事馆负责人、在沈阳的苏联专家和朝鲜驻沈阳总领事馆负责人都应邀参加了大会。
    沈阳市中苏友好协会会长焦若愚对苏联人民和政府帮助我国进行社会主义建设，表示衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。
    苏联驻沈阳总领事耶里扎微丁，在会上讲了话。当晚，耶里扎微丁和夫人，并举行庆祝宴会，招待沈阳各界人士。
    在西安市的庆祝大会上，中共陕西省委员会书记、中共西安市委员会第一书记方仲如列举了许多具体事实说明苏联对中国的无私援助后，并就最近国际间发生的事件，指出中苏两国团结和以苏联为首的社会主义各国团结一致的重要性。
    苏联专家多罗普切夫应邀在会上讲了话，他向到会者介绍了苏联建国三十九年来的辉煌成就。
    在重庆市举行的大会上，重庆市中苏友好协会会长李唐彬、中共重庆市委员会书记任白戈都在讲话中强调必须大力加强和巩固中苏两国人民的友谊。
    在重庆的苏联专家代表巴利沙柯夫也在热烈的掌声中讲了话。他说，伟大的十月社会主义革命的巨大成果，就是各国人民间的国际友谊，坚强的友谊，使社会主义阵营各国团结一致。兄弟般的友谊把中苏两国人民永久地团结在一起。我们把这种友谊看作是我们两国人民所有成就的源泉，是世界持久和平的保证。
    拉萨的庆祝大会是在彩旗飘扬的拉萨大礼堂举行的。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美首先在会上讲话。他叙述了自十月社会主义革命胜利后三十九年来，苏联人民在建设社会主义、共产主义事业中所获得的辉煌成就以及对我国建设事业所作的许多兄弟般的无私的援助。中共西藏工委副书记范明在讲话中谈到了苏联在解决民族问题上的光辉范例。
    新疆维吾尔自治区和乌鲁木齐各族各界人士七百多人，在7日上午举行了庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年大会。
    新疆维吾尔自治区副主席艾斯海提在大会上讲了话。他对苏联给予中国的无私援助，表示深切感激。他要求大家更好地学习苏联，把苏联的建设经验和科学技术成就，具体地应用到实践中来。他特别指出，学习苏联各民族的友爱团结精神，对于我国新疆各民族紧密团结建设社会主义具有很重要的意义。
    苏联驻乌鲁木齐总领事馆副领事别罗夫在大会上讲了话。他说：我们在中国的苏联人，看到中国人民在社会主义建设中所获得的卓越成就，感到非常兴奋。他说：苏联人民坚决地和中国人民一道，为保卫世界和平而奋斗。别罗夫在结束讲话时高呼：世界和平的强大因素——中苏人民的兄弟友谊万岁！
