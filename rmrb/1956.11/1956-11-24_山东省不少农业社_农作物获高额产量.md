Title: Many Agricultural Cooperatives in Shandong Province Achieve High Crop Yields
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Many agricultural production cooperatives in Shandong Province have achieved high yields this year.

The Advanced Agricultural Cooperative in Qiaozhuang Township, Zhucheng County, produced an average of 917.5 jin of corn per mu on 91 mu of land, with 6 mu averaging 1,350 jin per mu. The Lighthouse First Agricultural Cooperative in Fan County planted over 950 mu of sweet potatoes this spring, with yields exceeding 5,000 jin per mu, and 35 mu yielding 10,800 jin per mu. The Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in Ziyang County produced an average of 1,055 jin of millet per mu on 7 mu of land.

The Lu Wei Agricultural Cooperative in Gaotang County produced an average of 84 jin of ginned cotton per mu on 17,181 mu of cotton, with 3,320 mu averaging 114.5 jin of ginned cotton per mu. The Agricultural Cooperative in Xinfali Township, Leling County, produced over 100 jin of ginned cotton per mu on 2,100 mu of cotton fields.

### 山东省不少农业社  农作物获高额产量

1956-11-24
第3版()
专栏：

    山东省不少农业社
    农作物获高额产量
    据新华社讯　山东省今年有不少农业生产合作社获得了高额产量。
    诸城县乔庄乡先进农业社，有九十一亩玉米平均每亩产到九百一十七斤半，其中的六亩平均产到一千三百五十斤。范县灯塔第一农业社今年春季种植的九百五十多亩甘薯，每亩产量都在五千斤以上，其中有三十五亩每亩产一万零八百斤。滋阳县友谊农业社有七亩谷子平均每亩产到了一千零五十五斤。
    高唐县鲁伟农业社一万七千一百八十一亩棉花，平均每亩产皮棉八十四斤，其中有三千三百二十亩平均每亩产皮棉一百十四斤半。乐陵县新发李乡农业社有两千一百亩棉田每亩产皮棉一百斤以上。
