Title: Moscow Holds Report Meeting Celebrating the October Revolution Festival
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th dispatch: According to TASS reports: The Moscow City Labor People's Deputies Soviet, together with party organizations, social groups, and representatives of the Soviet Army, held a celebration meeting for the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution on the afternoon of November 6th at the Lenin Central Stadium Sports Palace in Moscow.

    More than 10,000 people attended the celebration meeting, including workers from various factories in Moscow, construction workers, enterprise leaders, scientific and artistic workers, Party and Soviet workers, Soviet military personnel, and many foreign delegations in the Soviet Union and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union.

    The magnificent hall was brightly lit. The rostrum was decorated with bright red flags, with a statue of Lenin in the center.

    At 18:10, the leaders of the Soviet Party and government appeared on the rostrum, and people welcomed them with warm applause.

    Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delivered a report entitled "The Thirty-ninth Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution" amidst enthusiastic applause from the audience. (The report will be issued separately.)

    After the closing of the celebration meeting, a grand performance was held.

### 莫斯科举行庆祝十月革命节的报告大会

1956-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行庆祝十月革命节的报告大会
    据新华社7日讯　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃会同党组织、社会团体、苏军代表11月6日下午在莫斯科的列宁中央运动场体育宫举行了十月社会主义革命三十九周年庆祝大会。
    有一万多人参加了庆祝会，其中有莫斯科各工厂里的职工、建筑工人、企业领导人、科学艺术工作者、党和苏维埃的工作人员、苏军人员以及许多在苏联的外国代表团和各国驻苏联的外交使节。
    宏伟的大厅里灯火通明。主席台上装饰着鲜红的旗子，中间是列宁的雕像。
    十八时十分。苏联党和政府的领导人出现在主席台上，这时人们以热烈的掌声欢迎他们。
    苏联共产党中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫在全场热烈的掌声中作了以“伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年”为题的报告。（报告另发）
    庆祝会闭幕后，举行了盛大的演出会。
