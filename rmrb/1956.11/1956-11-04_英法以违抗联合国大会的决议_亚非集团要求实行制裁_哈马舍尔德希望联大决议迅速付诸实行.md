Title: England, France, and Israel Defy UN General Assembly Resolution; Asian-African Group Demands Sanctions; Hammarskjöld Hopes for Swift Implementation of UNGA Resolution
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: TASS, New York, November 3rd: According to an Associated Press report, Abdoh, the Iranian representative to the United Nations, submitted a memorandum to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on behalf of the Asian-African countries. The memorandum stated that Britain, France, and Israel had not fulfilled the resolutions of the UN General Assembly's special emergency session. Representatives of Asian-African countries requested that the UN General Assembly's special emergency session take firm measures to stop the aggression against Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel. According to the Associated Press, representatives of many Asian-African countries demanded that military, economic, and financial sanctions be imposed on the aggressors.

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd, New York Dispatch: UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld today sent the UN General Assembly's resolution demanding an immediate ceasefire and the withdrawal of troops invading Egypt to Israel, Egypt, France, and Britain.

On November 2nd, Hammarskjöld also issued a statement on the resolution passed by the United Nations, expressing hope that the countries concerned would respect the resolution. He said: "I believe that the resolution, which received unprecedented overwhelming support in the United Nations yesterday, is something that strengthens the organization and gives its principles and purposes new meaning. I also share the hope of the UN General Assembly that the ceasefire, the cessation of troop entry into the region, and the withdrawal of all troops behind the armistice line will be swiftly implemented."

### 英法以违抗联合国大会的决议  亚非集团要求实行制裁  哈马舍尔德希望联大决议迅速付诸实行

1956-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    英法以违抗联合国大会的决议
    亚非集团要求实行制裁
    哈马舍尔德希望联大决议迅速付诸实行
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社纽约3日讯：据美联社消息，伊朗驻联合国代表阿卜杜代表亚非国家把一份备忘录交给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。备忘录说，英、法两国和以色列没有履行联合国大会特别紧急会议的决议。亚非国家代表要求联合国大会特别紧急会议采取坚决措施，制止英法两国和以色列对埃及的侵略。美联社消息说，许多亚非国家的代表要求对侵略者施行军事、经济和财政上的制裁。
    据新华社3日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德今天把联合国大会要求立刻停火和把侵入埃及的军队撤出埃及的决议发给以色列、埃及、法国和英国。
    哈马舍尔德11月2日还就联合国通过的决议发表声明，表示希望有关国家尊重这个决议。他说：“我认为，昨天在联合国中得到空前的绝大多数票支持的决议，是加强这个组织并且使它的原则和宗旨具有新的意义的一桩事。我也同联合国大会一样希望：停火、军队停止进入这个地区以及把一切军队撤退到停战线后面去等事，都将迅速实行。”
