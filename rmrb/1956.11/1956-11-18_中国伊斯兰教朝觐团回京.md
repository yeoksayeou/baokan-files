Title: Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 16th. The last group of members of the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation, led by Acting Head of the Delegation Li Shu and Deputy Head of the Delegation Anwar, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th. Dapusheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Ma Songting, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Hui Culture Association, and many well-known figures from the Islamic community in Beijing were at the airport to greet them.

The Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation departed at the end of June this year. After completing their Hajj pilgrimage in Mecca, they visited eight Islamic countries, including Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Syria, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Tunisia, and Afghanistan. Head of the Delegation Bao Erhan and some members had already returned to China in separate groups.

### 中国伊斯兰教朝觐团回京

1956-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教朝觐团回京
    新华社16日讯　中国伊斯兰教朝觐团的最后一批人员由代理团长李恕、副团长安瓦尔率领，16日下午乘飞机回到北京。中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、中国回民文化协会副主任马松亭，以及北京的伊斯兰教界知名人士多人曾到机场迎接。
    中国伊斯兰教朝觐团是在今年6月末出国的。他们在麦加完成了朝觐任务以后，访问了沙特阿拉伯、埃及、叙利亚、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、突尼斯、阿富汗等八个伊斯兰教国家。团长包尔汉和部分团员已经分批先行回国。
