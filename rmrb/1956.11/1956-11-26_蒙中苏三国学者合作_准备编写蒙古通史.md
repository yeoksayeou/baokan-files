Title: Mongolian, Chinese, and Soviet Scholars Cooperate to Prepare for Writing a Comprehensive History of Mongolia
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 24th: A resolution from the working conference of Mongolian, Chinese, and Soviet historians on the collaborative writing of a three-volume comprehensive history of Mongolia was signed in Ulaanbaatar on the 23rd.

The resolution stipulates that the period from 1957 to 1959 will be the preparation stage, and 1960 to 1965 will be the stage for writing and completing the publication. A meeting will be held next year to discuss the writing outline.

The resolution also includes a content plan for the writing and the specific division of labor among the scholars of the three countries.

### 蒙中苏三国学者合作  准备编写蒙古通史

1956-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙中苏三国学者合作
    准备编写蒙古通史
    新华社乌兰巴托24日电　蒙、中、苏三国历史学家合作编写三卷本的蒙古通史工作会议的决议，23日在乌兰巴托签字。
    决议规定从1957到1959为准备阶段，1960到1965年为进行编写和完成出版阶段。明年将开会讨论编写提纲。
    决议还附有编写的内容计划和三国学者的具体分工。
