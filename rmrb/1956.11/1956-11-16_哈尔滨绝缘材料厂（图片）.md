Title: Harbin Insulating Materials Factory (Picture)
Author: Yu Min
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 2

    Harbin Insulating Materials Factory
    Harbin Insulating Materials Factory is the first modern insulating materials manufacturing plant in our country. The picture shows workers in the pressing workshop using a 500-ton oil press to press rubberized cloth boards.
                             Photo by Yu Min (Manuscript from Heilongjiang Daily)

### 哈尔滨绝缘材料厂（图片）
于敏
1956-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨绝缘材料厂
    哈尔滨绝缘材料厂，是我国第一座现代化的绝缘材料制造厂。图为压制车间的工人，正在用五百吨的油压机压制胶布板。
                             于敏摄（黑龙江日报社稿）
