Title: Highway on the Plateau
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shua Jing Si, 26th. The Long Tang Highway on the grassland of the northwestern Sichuan plateau has recently been completed.

　　This highway starts from Longri on the Cheng (du) A (ba) highway, follows the Bai River, a tributary of the upper reaches of the Yellow River, passes through marshlands and endless grasslands, and reaches Tangke, a grassland town on the Yellow River, with a total length of 128 kilometers.

　　There are more than 10 million mu of arable land on the grassland of the northwestern Sichuan plateau. The completion of the Long Tang Highway will greatly promote the further development of the grassland.

### 高原上的公路

1956-11-27
第2版()
专栏：

    高原上的公路
    新华社刷经寺26日电　四川西北高原草地上的龙唐公路，已经在最近修筑完工。
    这条公路从成（都）阿（坝）公路上的龙日起，顺着黄河上游的支流白河，穿过沼泽地带和一望无际的草原，到达黄河边的草地市镇——唐克，全长一百二十八公里。
    四川西北高原草地上，约有一千多万亩的可耕地。龙唐公路修成后，对草地的进一步开发，有很大作用。
