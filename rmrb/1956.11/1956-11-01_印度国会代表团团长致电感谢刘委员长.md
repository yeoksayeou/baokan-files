Title: Head of Indian Parliamentary Delegation Sends Telegram to Thank Chairman Liu

Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Ayengar, the head of the Indian Parliamentary Delegation, sent a telegram to Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, before leaving Hong Kong to return to his country, expressing his gratitude for the warm hospitality received during his visit to China. The telegram said: Please accept our heartfelt thanks for the warm and multifaceted care you have given us.

### 印度国会代表团团长致电感谢刘委员长

1956-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    印度国会代表团团长致电感谢刘委员长
    新华社31日讯　印度国会代表团团长阿延加尔，在离开香港回国之前，致电我国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长，对在中国访问期间受到的热诚招待，表示感谢。电文说：请接受我们对你给我们的热烈和多方面的关怀，致以衷心的感谢。
