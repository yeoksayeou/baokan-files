Title: Governments of Various Countries Condemn Anglo-French Aggression Indonesian Foreign Minister States Inability to Sit Idly By Pakistani Prime Minister Opposes Use of Force in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 1st: Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani said this morning that the Indonesian government will not hesitate to take clear action against Israel's attack on Egypt. He said that the Indonesian government believes that the security of Egypt is also the security of Indonesia.

The Foreign Minister told approximately one thousand demonstrators, mostly youth and students, that yesterday when he received the British and French ambassadors in his office, he had already expressed to them that Indonesia would not sit idly by if Egypt were attacked.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 1st: Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy returned to Karachi on October 31st after his invited visit to China.

Answering questions from reporters at the airport, the Prime Minister said that Israel's recent acts of aggression have disrupted peace in the Middle East and have created a very serious and explosive situation. He said that Pakistanis have always sought not to confuse the issue of Israel with the Suez Canal dispute. He said: "Undoubtedly, I do not want the situation there to be resolved by any country using force."

### 各国政府纷纷谴责英法侵略  印度尼西亚外长表示不能坐视不管  巴基斯坦总理反对在中东使用武力

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    各国政府纷纷谴责英法侵略
    印度尼西亚外长表示不能坐视不管
    巴基斯坦总理反对在中东使用武力
    据新华社雅加达1日电　印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼今天上午说，印度尼西亚政府将毫不犹豫地对于以色列攻击埃及采取明确的行动。他说，印度尼西亚政府认为，埃及的安全也是印度尼西亚的安全。
    外交部长告诉大约一千名大多数是青年和学生的示威者说，昨天他在他的办公厅接见英国和法国大使的时候已经向他们表示，一旦埃及遭到攻击，印度尼西亚不会坐视不管。
    据新华社卡拉奇1日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底应邀访问中国以后，在10月31日回到卡拉奇。
    总理在机场回答记者提出的问题时说，以色列最近的侵略行动已经破坏了中东地区的和平，并且已经造成了一种非常严重和爆炸性的局势。他说，巴基斯坦人一向力求不要把以色列问题和苏伊士运河纠纷混淆起来。他说：“无疑，我不希望那里的局势由任何国家使用武力的办法来解决。”
