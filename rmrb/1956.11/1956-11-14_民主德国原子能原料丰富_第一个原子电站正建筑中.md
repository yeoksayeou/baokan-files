Title: East Germany Rich in Atomic Energy Raw Materials; First Atomic Power Station Under Construction
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 3

    East Germany Rich in Atomic Energy Raw Materials
    First Atomic Power Station Under Construction
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 13th: According to the German News Agency, East German Deputy Prime Minister Selbmann spoke at the opening ceremony of the “Soviet Exhibition of Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy” in Leipzig on the 11th about East Germany's plans for the peaceful use of atomic energy. East Germany is the largest uranium producer in Europe. He said that East Germany would establish a nuclear center station equipped with research reactors and cyclotrons. This nuclear center station may begin operations in 1957. He also announced that construction work on Germany's first atomic power station is already underway, and this power station will have a generating capacity of 70,000 kilowatts.

### 民主德国原子能原料丰富  第一个原子电站正建筑中

1956-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    民主德国原子能原料丰富
    第一个原子电站正建筑中
    新华社柏林13日电　据德意志通讯社报道：民主德国副总理赛尔布曼11日在莱比锡“苏联和平利用原子能展览会”的开幕仪式上谈到了民主德国的和平利用原子能的计划。民主德国是欧洲产铀最多的国家。他说：民主德国要建立一个配备有研究反应器和回旋加速器的核子中心站。这个核子中心站可能在1957年开始工作。他还宣布，德国第一个原子发电站的建筑工作已经在进行，这个发电站的发电能力将有七万瓩。
