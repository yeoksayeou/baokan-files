Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, Announces China's Gift of 30 Million Rubles Worth of Materials and Cash

Author:

Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on the 6th to Comrade János Kádár, Premier of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, congratulating the establishment of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government and announcing the Chinese government's decision to provide a gratuitous and unconditional gift of materials and cash worth 30 million rubles to the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government as a friendly aid and support to the fraternal Hungarian people. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade János Kádár, Premier of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, Budapest:

    We are delighted to learn that the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government has been formed and has taken control of the entire situation in Hungary. The government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people extend our warm and heartfelt congratulations to the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government and the Hungarian people.

    With the assistance of the Soviet Union, the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government has swiftly crushed the frantic attacks of the reactionary restoration forces. This great victory has protected the Constitution of the Hungarian People's Republic solemnly adopted in 1949 and the socialist system stipulated in the Constitution, thereby protecting the most fundamental利益 of the Hungarian people.

    The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people are extremely concerned about the difficulties encountered by the Hungarian people. The Chinese government has now decided to provide a gratuitous and unconditional gift of materials and cash worth 30 million rubles to the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government as a friendly aid and support to the fraternal Hungarian people.

    We wish that the long-standing friendship between the peoples of China and Hungary will continue to develop and consolidate.

                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                        November 6, 1956, Beijing

### 周总理电贺匈牙利工农革命政府  宣布我赠送匈牙利三千万卢布的物资和现金

1956-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺匈牙利工农革命政府
    宣布我赠送匈牙利三千万卢布的物资和现金
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来6日打电报给匈牙利工农革命政府总理亚诺什·卡达尔同志，祝贺匈牙利工农革命政府的成立，并宣布中国政府决定以价值三千万卢布的物资和现金无偿地、不附任何条件地赠送给匈牙利工农革命政府，作为对兄弟的匈牙利人民的友好援助和支持。电报全文如下：布达佩斯匈牙利工农革命政府总理亚诺什·卡达尔同志：
    欣悉匈牙利工农革命政府已经组成，并且控制了匈牙利整个局面，中华人民共和国政府和中国人民谨向匈牙利工农革命政府和匈牙利人民表示热烈的、衷心的祝贺。
    匈牙利工农革命政府在苏联援助下，迅速地击溃了反动复辟势力的猖狂进攻。这个巨大的胜利保护了1949年庄严通过的匈牙利人民共和国宪法和宪法所规定的社会主义制度，从而也就保护了匈牙利人民最根本的利益。
    对于匈牙利人民所遭遇到的困难，中华人民共和国政府和中国人民极为关怀。中国政府现决定以价值三千万卢布的物资和现金无偿地、不附任何条件地赠送给匈牙利工农革命政府，作为对兄弟的匈牙利人民的友好援助和支持。
    祝中匈两国人民之间的久已存在的友谊继续发展和巩固。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                        1956年11月6日于北京
