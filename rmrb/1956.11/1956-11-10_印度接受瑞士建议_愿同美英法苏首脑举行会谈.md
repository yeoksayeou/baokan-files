Title: India Accepts Swiss Proposal, Willing to Hold Talks with Leaders of US, UK, France, and Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th, Bern dispatch: A communique issued by the Swiss government on November 7th stated that India has accepted Switzerland's proposal to participate in a meeting with the heads of government of the United States, Britain, France, and the Soviet Union.

The communique said that Indian Prime Minister Nehru welcomed the Swiss proposal and said that every effort should be made to avoid the disaster of another world war.

Nehru said that he would definitely participate in the proposed talks if the other four invited governments accepted and desired India's participation.

### 印度接受瑞士建议  愿同美英法苏首脑举行会谈

1956-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度接受瑞士建议
    愿同美英法苏首脑举行会谈
    新华社8日讯　伯尔尼消息：瑞士政府11月7日发表的一项公报说，印度已经接受瑞士要求它参加同美国、英国、法国和苏联的政府首脑举行一次会谈的建议。
    公报说，印度总理尼赫鲁对瑞士的建议表示欢迎，并且说应该作出一切努力来避免爆发另一次世界大战的灾害。
    尼赫鲁说，他一定参加这个建议中的会谈，如果其他四个被邀请的政府接受并且希望印度参加的话。
