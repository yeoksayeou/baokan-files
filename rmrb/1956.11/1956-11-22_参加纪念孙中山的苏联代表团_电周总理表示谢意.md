Title: Soviet Delegation Participating in Sun Yat-sen Commemoration Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Expressing Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1956-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - The Soviet delegation invited to China to participate in the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Premier of the State Council, expressing their gratitude upon their return home.
The telegram stated: "We were invited to China to participate in the 90th anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. As we are about to return to our country, we would like to express our heartfelt thanks to you and all the members of the commemorative committee for your invitation and the cordial reception we received during our stay in your beautiful country. Our delegation requests you to convey our deep gratitude and best wishes to Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Comrade Zhu De, and others for the exceptionally kind care they extended to the delegation."

### 参加纪念孙中山的苏联代表团  电周总理表示谢意

1956-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    参加纪念孙中山的苏联代表团
    电周总理表示谢意
    新华社21日讯　应邀来我国参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念活动的苏联代表团在回国的时候，致电中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席、国务院总理周恩来表示谢意。
    电文说：“我们应邀来中国参加孙中山博士诞辰九十周年纪念活动，当此归国之际，谨向您和纪念委员会的全体人员对你们的邀请和当我们在你们美好国家里逗留时所给予的殷勤接待表示衷心的感谢。我们代表团请您代向毛泽东同志、刘少奇同志、朱德同志等人致深切的谢意和祝福，感谢他们对代表团所给予的异常亲切的关怀。
