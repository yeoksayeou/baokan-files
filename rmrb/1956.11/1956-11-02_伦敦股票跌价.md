Title: London Stock Prices Fall

Author:

Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 31st: News of Britain's intention to take advantage of Israel's invasion of Egypt to send troops into the Suez Canal Zone triggered a wave of price drops on the London stock market. The value of oil stocks alone fell by more than 50 million pounds.

### 伦敦股票跌价

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    伦敦股票跌价
    据新华社31日讯　伦敦消息：英国要利用以色列侵略埃及的机会进兵苏伊士运河区的消息传出以后，伦敦股票市场掀起了一阵跌风。光是石油股票价格就下跌了五千多万英镑。
