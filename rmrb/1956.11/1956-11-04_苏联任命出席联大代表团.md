Title: Soviet Union Appoints Delegation to UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd (TASS): The governments of the Soviet Union, the Ukrainian Republic, and the Belarusian Republic have appointed delegations to the eleventh session of the United Nations General Assembly, which will open in New York on November 12.

	The head of the Soviet delegation is Foreign Minister Shepilov, the deputy head is First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Kuznetsov, and the members of the delegation are Soviet Ambassador to the United States Zarubin, Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations Sobolev, and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kazakh Republic, Tazhibayev.

	The head of the Ukrainian Republic delegation is the Ukrainian Republic Minister of Foreign Affairs, Palamarchuk.

	The head of the Belarusian Republic delegation is Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Belarusian Republic, Kiselev.

### 苏联任命出席联大代表团

1956-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联任命出席联大代表团
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：苏联、乌克兰共和国和白俄罗斯共和国政府任命了出席将于11月12日在纽约开幕的联合国大会第十一次会议的代表团。
    苏联代表团的团长是外交部长谢皮洛夫，副团长是外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫，代表团团员有，苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾、苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫和哈萨克共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长塔日巴耶夫。
    乌克兰共和国代表团团长是乌克兰共和国外交部长帕拉马尔楚克。
    白俄罗斯共和国代表团团长是白俄罗斯共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长基谢廖夫。
