Title: Hammarskjöld Extends Deadline for Arranging Ceasefire in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, November 6th, New York: The United Nations Secretariat announced on the evening of November 4th that Secretary-General Hammarskjöld had extended the deadline for arranging a ceasefire in Egypt. According to the United Nations General Assembly resolution of November 3rd, the Secretary-General has the responsibility to arrange and achieve a ceasefire with the parties concerned within twelve hours. Hammarskjöld has now extended the twelve-hour deadline to midnight New York time on November 4th.
    Hammarskjöld also decided to submit a report to the United Nations General Assembly on November 5th regarding the implementation of the ceasefire resolution.

### 哈马舍尔德延长在埃及安排停火期限

1956-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德延长在埃及安排停火期限
    新华社6日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书处11月4日晚上宣布，秘书长哈马舍尔德已经延长安排在埃及停火的期限。根据联合国大会11月3日的决议秘书长有责任在十二小时内同有关各方安排和获致停火。目前哈马舍尔德已经把十二小时的期限延长到11月4日纽约时间午夜。
    哈马舍尔德还决定在11月5日向联合国大会提出关于停火决议执行情况的报告。
