Title: Ambassador Popović Holds Reception to Celebrate Yugoslav National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th – Over four hundred Chinese and foreign guests gathered at the Yugoslavian Embassy this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

National leaders Zhu De, Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Shen Junru, Dong Biwu, and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian attended the reception held by Yugoslav Ambassador Popović.

Vice Premier Chen Yun of the State Council, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, extended congratulations to the Yugoslav people and the Yugoslav government at the reception.

Chen Yun spoke about the further development of the friendship between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples over the past year. He said that the continuous development and consolidation of friendship and cooperation between the two governments and peoples are of great significance to the socialist construction of our two countries and the cause of maintaining world peace. He believes that with the joint efforts of the two governments and peoples, the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will be further strengthened.

Chen Yun proposed a toast to the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples, to the prosperity and happiness of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, and to the health of President Tito.

Before Chen Yun's speech, the host of the reception, Ambassador Popović, spoke. The Ambassador took the opportunity to express the sincere friendship of the Yugoslav people to the Chinese people, and the Yugoslav people's admiration for the great task of peaceful socialist construction undertaken by the Chinese people and the enviable successes achieved and to be achieved.

The Ambassador also expressed the Yugoslav government and people's desire and firm determination to further develop the existing friendly relations between China and Yugoslavia for the benefit and happiness of the two peoples, based on the principles of peaceful coexistence and socialist solidarity. He wished the eternal and sincere friendship between China and Yugoslavia a steady increase and development.

The Ambassador concluded by inviting his Chinese and foreign guests to join him in a toast to the health and longevity of Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the prosperity of the Chinese people.

Among the Ambassador's guests were heads of various departments of state organs, generals, people from all walks of life, and diplomats from various countries stationed in China.

### 波波维奇大使举行招待会  庆祝南斯拉夫国庆

1956-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    波波维奇大使举行招待会
    庆祝南斯拉夫国庆
    新华社29日讯　有四百多位中外人士今天下午聚集在南斯拉夫大使馆，欢庆南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国庆。
    国家的领导人员朱德、陈云、彭德怀、沈钧儒、董必武和外交部代部长张闻天等都出席了南斯拉夫大使波波维奇举行的这个招待会。
    国务院陈云代总理在招待会上代表中国人民和中国政府，对南斯拉夫人民和南斯拉夫政府表示祝贺。
    陈云谈到了过去一年多来，中南两国人民之间的友谊进一步发展的情况。他说，两国政府和人民之间的友好和合作的不断发展和巩固，对于我们两国的社会主义建设和维护世界和平的事业，都有重大的意义。他相信，在两国政府和人民的共同努力之下，两国间的友好合作关系，还会得到进一步的加强。
    陈云提议为中南两国人民的兄弟友谊，为南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的繁荣和幸福，为铁托总统的健康干杯。
    在陈云讲话以前，招待会的主人波波维奇大使讲了话。大使利用招待会的机会对于中国人民表示南斯拉夫人民的诚挚的友好热情，对于中国人民从事和平建设社会主义的伟大任务及所获得的和将要获得的令人羡慕的成功表示南斯拉夫人民的敬佩。
    大使还表达了南斯拉夫政府和人民对于为了中南两国人民的利益和幸福，根据和平共处和社会主义团结的原则，进一步发展中南两国已经存在的友好关系的愿望和坚定的决心。他祝中南两国永恒而诚挚的友谊稳步地增长和发展。
    大使最后请他的中外客人们和他一起为毛泽东主席的健康和万寿无疆，为中国人民的繁荣干杯。
    在大使的客人中，还有国家机关各部门负责人，将军们，社会各界人士，以及各国驻中国的外交官们。
