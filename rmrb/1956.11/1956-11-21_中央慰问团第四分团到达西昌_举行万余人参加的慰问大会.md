Title: Central Delegation Fourth Sub-Delegation Arrives in Xichang, Holds Rally of Over Ten Thousand People

Author:

Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 20th. The Fourth Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation held a rally in Xichang County, Sichuan Province, on the 19th, attended by over 15,000 people.

　　At the rally, Huang Jue'an, head of the Fourth Sub-Delegation, extended cordial greetings on behalf of the Central Committee and Chairman Mao to the people of all ethnic groups, the troops, and the cadres of the Xichang Special District, and elaborated on the Central Committee's principles and policies on completing the democratic reform work in ethnic minority areas through peaceful consultation. The delegation presented gifts to local leading organs, mass organizations, and responsible cadres of the Yi, Tibetan, and other ethnic groups in these units.

　　At the meeting, the persons in charge of the Xichang Prefectural Committee of the Communist Party of China and the PLA troops stationed in Xichang, as well as representatives of the Yi, Tibetan, and other ethnic groups, all spoke. They expressed their gratitude for the concern of the Central Committee and Chairman Mao and stated that they would resolutely complete the democratic reform work in all ethnic minority areas.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 20th. Over 7,000 Yi, Tibetan, and Han people in Xichang Special District, Sichuan Province, warmly welcomed the arrival of the Fourth Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation in Xichang on the 17th.

　　After the delegation received flowers and hadas from the Yi, Tibetan, and Mongolian students of the Xichang Nationalities Cadre School, Huang Jue'an, head of the Fourth Sub-Delegation, spoke. He congratulated the ethnic minority areas of Xichang on the great achievements they have made in recent years under the leadership of the Party and the People's Government, and expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm welcome from the Party, government, army, people's organizations, and fraternal ethnic groups of Xichang Special District.

　　Subsequently, the delegation members passed through a welcoming procession that stretched for several miles. On the evening of the 17th, people from all walks of life in the special district held a grand welcome party for the arrival of the delegation.

### 中央慰问团第四分团到达西昌  举行万余人参加的慰问大会

1956-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中央慰问团第四分团到达西昌
    举行万余人参加的慰问大会
    据新华社成都20日电　中央慰问团第四分团19日在四川省西昌县举行了有一万五千多人参加的慰问大会。
    在大会上，第四分团团长黄觉庵代表中央和毛主席向西昌专区各族人民、部队和机关干部表示亲切的慰问，并阐述了中央关于和平协商完成民族地区民主改革工作的方针政策。慰问团向当地领导机关和群众团体以及这些单位的彝、藏等族负责干部赠送了礼品。
    会上，中共西昌地委和中国人民解放军驻西昌部队的负责人及彝、藏等族代表都讲了话。他们感谢中央和毛主席的关怀，表示要坚决完成各民族地区的民主改革工作。
    据新华社成都20日电　四川省西昌专区七千多彝、藏、汉族人民，17日热烈欢迎中央慰问团第四分团到达西昌。
    慰问团接受西昌民族干部学校的彝、藏、蒙古族的学员献花和献哈达后，慰问团第四分团团长黄觉庵讲话。他祝贺西昌民族地区几年来在党和人民政府领导下所取得的巨大成就，并衷心感谢西昌专区党、政、军、人民团体和兄弟民族的热情欢迎。
    接着，慰问团人员通过长达数里的欢迎行列。十七日晚间，专区各界人民为慰问团的到来举行了盛大的欢迎晚会。
