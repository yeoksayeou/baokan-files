Title: Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic Decides: The Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government is the Highest National Administrative Organ
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 12th: The Czechoslovak News Agency, citing a Budapest radio report, said that the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic has decided that, pending the convocation of the Hungarian National Assembly to make certain amendments to the Constitution, the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government is the highest national administrative organ. The Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government is appointed by the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic. The government itself is responsible for leading the work of all departments of national administration.

### 匈牙利人民共和国主席团决定  工农革命政府是最高国家行政机关

1956-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民共和国主席团决定
    工农革命政府是最高国家行政机关
    新华社布拉格12日电　捷克斯洛伐克通讯社引布达佩斯电台的消息说，匈牙利人民共和国主席团决定：在匈牙利国民议会开会对宪法作某些修改之前，匈牙利工农革命政府是最高国家行政机关。工农革命政府是由匈牙利人民共和国主席团任命的。政府本身负责领导国家行政各个部门的工作。
