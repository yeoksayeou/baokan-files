Title: U.S. Officials Confirm Israeli Invasion of Egypt with British and French Consent; But U.S. Defense Department Officials Say Military Aid to Britain and France Will Not Be Interrupted
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st Dispatch from Washington: U.S. officials confirmed on October 31st that thirty-six hours before the Israeli forces attacked Egypt, Israel had notified the British and French military attachés stationed in Tel Aviv about its intended attack.

News broadcaster Edward Murrow said in his CBS news broadcast on October 30th that Israel's attack on Egypt was carried out according to an "arrangement between Britain, France, and Israel."

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st Dispatch from Washington: A senior official of the U.S. Department of Defense said that the United States currently has no plans to interrupt military aid to Britain and France.

He said that the U.S. State Department is studying how to handle military aid to Britain and France since Britain and France took military action against Egypt, but at the same time, the Department of Defense has no intention of weakening the military strength of the North Atlantic Alliance.

### 美官员证实以色列在英法同意下入侵埃及  但美国防部官员却说不中断对英法的军事援助

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    美官员证实以色列在英法同意下入侵埃及
    但美国防部官员却说不中断对英法的军事援助
    新华社1日讯　华盛顿消息：美国官员们10月31日证实：在以色列部队进攻埃及的三十六小时前，以色列曾经把它要进攻的事通知英国和法国驻在特拉维夫的陆军武官。
    新闻广播员爱德华·穆罗10月30日在他的哥伦比亚广播公司新闻广播中说，以色列对埃及的进攻是根据“英、法和以色列之间的安排”进行的。
    新华社1日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部的一个高级官员说，美国目前没有中断对英法的军事援助的打算。
    他说，美国国务院正在研究自从英法对埃及采取军事行动以后如何处理对英法的军事援助问题，但是同时，国防部没有削弱北大西洋联盟军事力量的打算。
