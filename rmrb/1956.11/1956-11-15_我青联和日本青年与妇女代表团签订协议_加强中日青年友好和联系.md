Title: All-China Youth Federation and Japanese Youth and Women Delegation Sign Agreement to Strengthen China-Japan Youth Friendship and Ties
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The All-China Youth Federation and the Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China signed an agreement in Beijing this morning on strengthening friendship and ties between the youth of the two countries.

Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Tamotsu Ida, head of the Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective parties.

Hu Yaobang and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and Ding Cong, Guan Shixiong, and Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretaries-General of the All-China Youth Federation, attended the signing ceremony.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The full text of the agreement between the All-China Youth Federation and the Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China on strengthening friendship and ties between the youth of the two countries is as follows:

The Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China, dispatched by the All-Japan Youth and Women Conference and the Youth and Women Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and the All-China Youth Federation, held consultations in Beijing on strengthening friendship and ties between the youth of the two countries. As a result, they reached a complete consensus on the following points and signed this agreement:

	1. The All-China Youth Federation will regularly exchange representatives with the All-Japan Youth and Women Conference and the Youth and Women Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (hereinafter referred to as "both parties").

	2. Both parties will exchange activity materials, documents, books, and other materials of youth organizations in China and Japan.

	3. Both parties will actively carry out various activities aimed at deepening friendship between the youth of Asian and African countries.

	4. Both parties will actively participate in various peace activities in the world and enhance friendly relations with youth around the world.

		November 14, 1956, Beijing
		All-China Youth Federation
			Chairman: Liao Chengzhi
		Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China
			Head: Tamotsu Ida

### 我青联和日本青年与妇女代表团签订协议  加强中日青年友好和联系

1956-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我青联和日本青年与妇女代表团签订协议
    加强中日青年友好和联系
    新华社14日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会和日本青年与妇女访华代表团，今天上午在北京签订关于加强两国青年友好和联系的协议。
    全国青联主席廖承志和日本青年与妇女访华代表团团长井田保代表双方签字。
    参加签字仪式的，有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、胡克实，全国青联副秘书长丁聪、关世雄、王传彬等人。
    新华社14日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会和日本青年与妇女访华代表团关于加强两国青年友好和联系的协议，全文如下：
    全日本青年、妇女会议和日本工会总评议会青年、妇女协议会派遣的日本青年与妇女访华代表团和中华全国民主青年联合会就加强两国青年友好和联系问题，在北京进行了协商，结果在下列各点上完全取得一致的意见，并签订本协议：
    1、中华全国民主青年联合会将和全日本青年、妇女会议和日本工会总评议会青年、妇女协议会（以下简称双方）经常互相交换代表。
    2、双方将互相交换中日两国青年团体的活动资料、文件、图书等。
    3、双方将积极地进行旨在加深亚非各国青年之间的友好的各种活动。
    4、双方将积极参加世界上的各种和平活动并和全世界青年增进友好关系。
        1956年11月14日　于北京
      中华全国民主青年联合会
                主席　廖承志
    日本青年与妇女访华代表团
                团长　井田保
