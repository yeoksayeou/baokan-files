Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Syrian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation, which is currently in China on a friendly visit.

In his speech at the banquet, President Zhang Xiruo said: "The Syrian people and the Chinese people have always been very friendly. We believe that this visit to China by the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation will further enhance the friendly relations between China and Syria."

Referring to the Syrian government's decision to stand with Egypt in resisting Anglo-French aggression, President Zhang Xiruo said, "The Chinese people deeply admire this action."

He added, "This struggle of the Arab people is a just struggle. We believe that a just struggle will surely achieve final victory."

The head of the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation, Secretary-General of the People's Party, Ali Buzao, said in his reply that he was "happy and excited" to be able to personally visit China to observe its development and achievements in various fields.

He said: "Since setting foot on Chinese soil, we have heard even more exciting news, that is, the Chinese people have held grand parades and demonstrations to support the Arab people's struggle for independence and freedom."

"We believe that the Chinese people, who have already gained liberation and freedom, must have a very high level of political awareness. As long as we unite, we will definitely be able to defeat imperialism."

Also present at the banquet were Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, and Qiao Guanhua, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association.

### 张奚若欢宴叙利亚议员访华团

1956-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    张奚若欢宴叙利亚议员访华团
    新华社5日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在今晚设宴欢迎现在中国进行友好访问的叙利亚议员访华团。
    张奚若会长在会上致词说：“叙利亚人民和中国人民一向是很友好的。我们相信，这次叙利亚议员访华团来中国访问，一定会更进一步增进中叙两国的友好关系。”
    在谈到叙利亚政府决定和埃及站在一起反抗英法侵略时，张奚若会长说，“中国人民对这种举动感到十分钦佩。”
    他又说，“阿拉伯人民这种斗争是一种正义的斗争。我们相信，正义的斗争是一定会得到最后胜利的。”
    叙利亚议员访华团团长、人民党总书记阿里·布佐在致答词中表示能亲自到中国考察各方面的发展和成就“感到愉快和欣奋”。
    他说：“自从踏上中国国土后，我们听到了更兴奋的消息，就是中国人民举行盛大游行示威支持阿拉伯人民为独立自由所进行的斗争。”
    “我们相信，在已获得解放和自由的中国人民，他们的政治觉悟一定很高，只要我们团结起来，我们是一定能够战胜帝国主义的。”
    出席宴会的还有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪；全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深；中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通；中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生等人。
