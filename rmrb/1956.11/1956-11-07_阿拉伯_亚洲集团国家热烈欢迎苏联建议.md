Title: Arab-Asian Group Countries Warmly Welcome Soviet Proposal
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch: According to a United Press report, a spokesman for the Arab-Asian group of nations at the United Nations stated on the 5th that they warmly welcomed the Soviet proposal for joint Soviet-American armed forces to stop the Anglo-French aggression against Egypt.

    The spokesman said that the Arab-Asian group considers the Soviet proposal to be a "very good" suggestion.

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch, New York News: Indian Minister Without Portfolio Menon arrived in New York by plane from London on November 5th. Upon arrival, he held talks with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld.

    After the meeting, Menon said that if the Soviet Union and the United States joined their forces to stop the aggression in the Middle East, it would be "good news" "for the whole world."

    According to British newspapers, before Menon arrived in New York, he told reporters at London Airport regarding the armed aggression against Egypt that the people of India were angry about the aggression in which Britain participated.

### 阿拉伯—亚洲集团国家热烈欢迎苏联建议

1956-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯—亚洲集团国家热烈欢迎苏联建议
    新华社6日讯　据合众社消息，联合国阿拉伯—亚洲集团国家的一位发言人5日对苏联提出的由苏美联合武装力量制止英法对埃及的侵略的建议表示热烈欢迎。
    这个发言人说，阿拉伯—亚洲集团认为苏联的这个建议是一个“非常好”的主张。
    新华社6日讯　纽约消息：印度不管部长梅农11月5日从伦敦乘飞机到达纽约。到达后，他同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德进行了会谈。
    梅农在会谈后说，如果苏联和美国联合它们的兵力来制止中东的侵略，这“对全世界来说”将是个“好消息”。
    据英国报纸报道，梅农到纽约前曾经在伦敦机场就埃及遭到武装侵略一事向记者发表谈话说，印度全国的人对于有英国参加的这次侵略感到愤怒。
