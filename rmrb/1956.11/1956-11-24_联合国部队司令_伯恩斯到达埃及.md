Title: UN Forces Commander Burns Arrives in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Abu Suweir, Egypt, November 23rd: UN Emergency Force Commander Major General Burns arrived in Abu Suweir, Egypt on November 22nd. He stated that he would temporarily establish the UN Emergency Force headquarters in Cairo. Burns also said that the troops provided by UN member states are already sufficient for their current mission. He said that the UN Emergency Force is primarily a "symbol," but it is prepared to fight if attacked. He also pointed out that clearing the Suez Canal is not part of his mission. He said: "After the Israeli army withdraws from the Sinai Peninsula, the UN forces will be stationed along the demarcation line."

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 23rd: A spokesperson for UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld said on the 21st that the United Nations has so far agreed to allow troops from eight countries to participate in the UN International Police Force.

These countries are: Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Canada, Colombia, India, and Yugoslavia. These countries have provided a total of 4,479 officers and soldiers to participate in this force.

### 联合国部队司令  伯恩斯到达埃及

1956-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国部队司令
    伯恩斯到达埃及
    据新华社23日讯　埃及阿布苏威尔消息：联合国紧急部队司令伯恩斯少将11月22日到达埃及的阿布苏威尔。他说，他暂时将在开罗设立联合国紧急部队司令部。伯恩斯又说，联合国会员国所提供的部队已经足以应付他们目前的任务。他说，联合国紧急部队主要是一种
  “象征”，但是如果受到攻击，它是准备战斗的。他并且指出，清理苏伊士运河不是他的任务的一部分。他说：“在以色列军队撤出西奈半岛以后，联合国部队将沿着分界线驻军。”
    据新华社23日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的发言人21日说，到目前为止，联合国已经同意让八个国家的部队参加联合国国际警察部队。
    这些国家是：挪威、瑞典、丹麦、芬兰、加拿大、哥伦比亚、印度和南斯拉夫。这些国家一共提供了四千四百七十九名官兵参加这支部队。
