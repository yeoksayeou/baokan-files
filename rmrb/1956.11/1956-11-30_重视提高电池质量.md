Title: Emphasize Improving Battery Quality
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 2

Column: Reaction to Criticism

    Emphasize Improving Battery Quality
    A letter from the Local State-Owned Shanxi Chemical Factory: The criticism of our factory's low battery quality made by Comrade Zheng Yangmin, published on page two of the People's Daily on October 18th, is correct. The main reason for the low quality is that our factory leaders have a mentality of valuing quantity over quality. To thank the readers for their help, we will further improve our business management and focus on improving product quality in the future.

### 重视提高电池质量

1956-11-30
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    重视提高电池质量
    地方国营山西化工厂来信：10月18日人民日报二版发表郑仰民同志对我厂电池质量低劣的批评是正确的。造成质量低劣的主要原因是我厂领导上存在着重量轻质的思想。为了感谢读者对我们的帮助，今后我们要进一步改善企业管理，重视提高产品质量。
