Title: Ceylonese Prime Minister Says Britain, France and Israel Should Obey UN Resolution and Withdraw Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 19th: Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike said at Manchester Airport in Britain on November 18 that the current Middle East situation is still "very dangerous and disturbing," and the only way to prevent further deterioration of the Middle East situation is that "Britain, France, (and) Israeli (troops) should obey the United Nations resolution and withdraw."

Bandaranaike was passing through Britain as a special spokesperson for the Colombo countries to attend the United Nations General Assembly. He said that he would meet with British Prime Minister Eden in London to discuss the Suez Canal situation and the issue of British bases in Ceylon.

### 锡兰总理说英法以应服从联大决议撤军

1956-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理说英法以应服从联大决议撤军
    新华社19日讯　伦敦消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克11月18日在英国曼彻斯特机场说，目前的中东局势依然是“很危险和令人不安”的，防止中东局势进一步恶化的唯一办法就是“英国、法国、以色列（军队）应该服从联合国的决议撤退”。
    班达拉奈克是作为科伦坡国家特别发言人去出席联合国大会时途经英国的。他说，他将在伦敦同英国首相艾登会谈苏伊士运河局势和英国在锡兰的基地问题。
