Title: Chinese Buddhist Delegation Goes to Nepal to Attend World Buddhist Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. A Chinese Buddhist delegation of fifteen members left Beijing by plane this morning for Kathmandu, the capital of the Kingdom of Nepal, to attend the Fourth World Buddhist Conference.

    The head of the Chinese Buddhist delegation is Master Xirao Jiacuo, President of the Buddhist Association of China. The deputy heads are Gara Cang Living Buddha, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Layman Zhao Puchu, and Xia Rong Ga Bu Living Buddha of Qinghai. After attending the World Buddhist Conference, Xirao Jiacuo, Gara Cang, and Zhao Puchu will travel to India to participate in the activities commemorating the 2,500th anniversary of Sakyamuni’s Nirvana.

### 我佛教代表团赴尼泊尔参加世界佛教大会

1956-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

    我佛教代表团赴尼泊尔参加世界佛教大会
    新华社9日讯　中国佛教代表团一行十五人今晨乘飞机离开北京去尼泊尔王国首都加德满都参加第四届世界佛教徒大会。
    中国佛教代表团的团长是中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错大师，副团长是中国佛教协会副会长噶喇藏活佛、赵朴初居士和青海夏荣尕布活佛。喜饶嘉错、噶喇藏和赵朴初三人在参加世界佛教徒大会之后将转赴印度参加释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念活动。
