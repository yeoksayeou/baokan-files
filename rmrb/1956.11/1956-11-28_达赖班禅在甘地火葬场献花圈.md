Title: Dalai Lama, Panchen Lama Lay Wreath at Gandhi's Cremation Site
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 27th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni visited Raj Ghat this morning and laid a wreath at Gandhi's cremation site.

Subsequently, they received public homage at Hyderabad House and Kota House respectively. Large tents were erected in the courtyards of both buildings, with felt carpets laid inside. Approximately three hundred Buddhists gathered in each tent to listen to lectures.

### 达赖班禅在甘地火葬场献花圈

1956-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖班禅在甘地火葬场献花圈
    新华社新德里27日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼今天上午访问了拉志加特，并且在甘地火葬场献了花圈。
    随后，他们分别在海得拉巴大厦和科塔大厦接受公众参拜。在这两座大厦的庭院里都搭起了巨大的帐篷，里面铺着绒毡。每个帐篷里都集聚了大约三百名佛教徒来听讲经。
