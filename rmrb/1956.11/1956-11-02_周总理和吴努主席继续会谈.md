Title: Premier Zhou and Chairman U Nu Continue Talks
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, U Nu, continued their talks this afternoon.

### 周总理和吴努主席继续会谈

1956-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理和吴努主席继续会谈
