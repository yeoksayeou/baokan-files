Title: Butler States Anglo-French Troop Withdrawal Has Preconditions
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

    Butler States
    Anglo-French Troop Withdrawal Has Preconditions
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 22nd: In the British House of Commons on the 20th, when asked when the Anglo-French forces would withdraw from the Suez Canal area, British Acting Prime Minister Butler replied that Eden had announced on November 6 that the canal zone would only be handed over when the international police force could "effectively perform its tasks."

### 巴特勒表示  英法撤军有先决条件

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    巴特勒表示
    英法撤军有先决条件
    据新华社22日讯　伦敦消息：20日在英国下院，有人问英法军队什么时候才从苏伊士运河地区撤走，英国代理首相巴特勒回答道，艾登已经在11月6日宣布过，只有在国际警察部队能够“有效地执行它的任务”的时候才会把运河区移交过去。
