Title: Soviet Legal Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd. The Soviet legal workers delegation, led by Soviet Deputy Procurator-General Mishutin, has concluded its visit to China and departed from Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Gao Kelin, Zhang Zhizhiang, and Ma Xiwu, Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court; Tan Zhengwen and Huang Huoxing, Deputy Procurators-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Shi Liang, Minister of Justice; Zheng Shaowen, Chen Yangshan, and Min Ganghou, Vice Ministers of Justice; Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General; and many people from the Chinese political and legal circles.

Sudarikov, Cultural Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

During the month, the delegation visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other cities, and had extensive contact with central and local legal workers. The delegation gave six academic lectures and participated in nearly fifty professional seminars.

### 苏联法律工作者访华代表团离京回国

1956-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联法律工作者访华代表团离京回国
    新华社2日讯　以苏联副总检察长米舒金为首的苏联法律工作者访华代表团，结束了在我国的访问活动，已在今晨乘飞机离京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有最高人民法院副院长高克林、张志让、马锡五，最高人民检察院副检察长谭政文、黄火星，司法部部长史良、副部长郑绍文、陈养山、闵刚侯，中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子、副秘书长戈宝权，以及我国政法界人士多人。
    苏联驻我国大使馆文化参赞苏达里柯夫也到机场欢送。
    在一个月的时间里，代表团先后访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州、广州等城市，同中央和地方法律工作者作了广泛的接触，代表团曾作了六次学术性的讲演，分别参加了近五十次的专业座谈会。
