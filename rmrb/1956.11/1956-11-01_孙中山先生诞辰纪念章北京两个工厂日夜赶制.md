Title: Two Beijing Factories are Working Day and Night to Produce Commemorative Medals for Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Two factories in Beijing, the Beijing Metal Arts Factory and the Joint State-Private Partnership Medal Factory, have begun producing commemorative medals for the 90th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth.

　　The commemorative medal was designed by the famous sculptor and professor of the Central Academy of Arts and Design, Zheng Ke. It features a relief of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's portrait. The medal is made of bronze, plated with gold, and sprayed with a transparent varnish. The back is inscribed with the words "In Commemoration of the 90th Anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth".

　　The Beijing Metal Arts Factory plans to produce 15,000 of these commemorative medals, which will be presented to attendees at the 90th anniversary commemorations of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Nanjing on November 12th. The Joint State-Private Partnership Medal Factory initially plans to produce 200,000 medals, which will be sold at the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park and at the Biyun Temple in the Western Hills in Beijing.

　　In order to rush the production of these products, the factories have adopted a two-shift production system, day and night. The workers have pledged to complete the task on time, with guaranteed quality and quantity.

### 孙中山先生诞辰纪念章北京两个工厂日夜赶制

1956-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    孙中山先生诞辰纪念章北京两个工厂日夜赶制
    新华社31日讯　孙中山先生诞辰九十周年的纪念章已经由北京市金属工艺工厂、公私合营证章厂开始制作。
    纪念章是由著名的雕塑家、中央工艺美术学院教授郑可设计的，上面是孙中山先生头像浮雕。纪念章由紫铜制成，外镀赤金，并喷透明的清照光漆，背面有纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年的字样。
    这种纪念章，北京市金属工艺工厂计划生产一万五千枚，将于11月12日在北京、上海、广州、南京的孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念会上送给参加的人作为纪念。公私合营证章厂初步计划生产二十万枚，将在北京中山公园中山堂和西山碧云寺出售。
    工厂为了赶制这批产品，采取日夜两班生产的办法。工人们提出了按期、按质按量完成任务的保证。
