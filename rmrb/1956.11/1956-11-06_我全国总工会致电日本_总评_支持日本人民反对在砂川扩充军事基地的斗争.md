Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Japanese "Sohyo" in Support of the Japanese People's Struggle Against the Expansion of Military Base in Sunagawa
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), and forwarded it to the Liaison Council of Japanese Groups Supporting the Sunagawa Struggle, supporting the struggle of the Japanese people against the expansion of the military base in Sunagawa.
    The telegram said: Chinese workers have always supported the Japanese people's struggle against foreign military bases. Chinese workers have learned through their own newspapers, magazines, and broadcasts all the details of the heroic struggle recently waged by the Japanese people against the expansion of the military base in Sunagawa.
    Chinese workers, who love peace and are dedicated to peaceful construction, resolutely oppose this military base policy that endangers peace in Asia and the world, and resolutely support the just struggle of the Japanese people to safeguard their national independence and dignity, safeguard their vital interests, and defend peace in Asia and the world.
    We congratulate the Japanese people on the victory already achieved in their united struggle against the expansion of the military base in Sunagawa. We are well aware that your struggle against the military base policy is a long-term and arduous one; however, we are also firmly convinced that with the enhanced unity of the Japanese people, and the sympathy and support of peace-loving countries and people all over the world, the final victory will surely belong to the heroic Japanese people.
    We extend our cordial greetings to the Japanese compatriots who were wounded in the struggle.

### 我全国总工会致电日本“总评”  支持日本人民反对在砂川扩充军事基地的斗争

1956-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会致电日本“总评”
    支持日本人民反对在砂川扩充军事基地的斗争
    新华社4日讯　中华全国总工会打电报给日本工会总评议会并转日本各团体支援砂川斗争联络会议，支持日本人民为反对在砂川扩充军事基地所进行的斗争。
    电报说：中国工人一向支持日本人民为反对外国军事基地的斗争。中国工人已通过自己的报纸、刊物、广播获悉日本人民最近为反对在砂川扩充军事基地所进行的英勇斗争的全部情况。
    热爱和平和献身于和平建设的中国工人，坚决反对这种危害亚洲与世界和平的军事基地政策，坚决支持日本人民为维护自己的民族独立与尊严、维护自己的切身利益和保卫亚洲与世界和平的正义斗争。
    祝贺日本人民团结一致的为反对在砂川扩充军事基地的斗争中已获得的胜利，我们深知你们为反对军事基地政策的斗争是长期的、艰苦的；但是，我们也深信，日本人民团结的增强，全世界爱好和平各国及广大人民的同情和支援，最后的胜利一定是属于英勇斗争的日本人民的。
    谨向在斗争中流血受伤的日本同胞致以亲切的慰问。
