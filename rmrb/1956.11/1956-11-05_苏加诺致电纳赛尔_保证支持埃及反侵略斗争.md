Title: Sukarno calls Nasser, guarantees support for Egypt's anti-aggression struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 5

    Sukarno calls Nasser
    Guarantees support for Egypt's anti-aggression struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 3rd -- Indonesian President Sukarno telegraphed Egyptian President Nasser today, guaranteeing that the Indonesian people support Egypt in its anti-aggression struggle.

### 苏加诺致电纳赛尔  保证支持埃及反侵略斗争

1956-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺致电纳赛尔
    保证支持埃及反侵略斗争
    新华社雅加达3日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在今天打电报给埃及总统纳赛尔，保证印度尼西亚人民在埃及的反侵略斗争中支持埃及。
