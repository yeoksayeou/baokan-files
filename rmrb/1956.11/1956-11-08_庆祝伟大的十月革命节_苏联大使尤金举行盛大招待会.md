Title: Celebrating the Great October Revolution Day: Soviet Ambassador Yudin Holds Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th – Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, held a grand reception tonight to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai attended tonight's reception.

Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, congratulated the Soviet government and people on the great festival of the October Revolution and thanked the Soviet government and people for their fraternal and sincere assistance to China. Premier Zhou said that the Chinese people, based on their own personal experience, deeply know that the great and eternal friendship between China and the Soviet Union is completely in line with the fundamental interests of our two peoples and all progressive humanity. Further consolidating and developing this friendship will greatly enhance the power of peace, democracy, and socialism and make significant contributions to safeguarding world peace and the cause of human progress.

Ambassador Yudin, in his speech at tonight's reception, first said that the Soviet people have embarked on the path of building a new life based on the principles of peace, democracy, and socialism for thirty-nine years. The Ambassador said that the Soviet Union is engaged in a selfless struggle for the cause of peace and democracy, that is, for the noble cause of justice, and has won the deep sympathy and respect of hundreds of millions of people around the world. The People's Republic of China, a great socialist country in the East, together with the People's Democratic countries of Europe and Asia, are advancing hand in hand in the family of equal sovereign states. Their unity is a decisive condition for the achievements in the struggle to safeguard peace among all peoples. Ambassador Yudin concluded by saying that we are very pleased that the Chinese people are celebrating our national day so excitedly and with warm, fraternal feelings, thereby emphasizing that the victory of our proletarian revolution is of great world historical significance.

At the end of the speeches by Ambassador Yudin and Premier Zhou Enlai, all Chinese and foreign guests stood up to toast the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, the great unity of the socialist countries, and the victory of world peace and democracy.

Also invited to attend the reception were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Marshal Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; He Xiangning, Zhang Bojun, and Bao Erhan, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; responsible persons from various departments of the Chinese state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations; people from all walks of life; and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China. ### 庆祝伟大的十月革命节  苏联大使尤金举行盛大招待会

1956-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝伟大的十月革命节
    苏联大使尤金举行盛大招待会
    新华社7日讯　苏联驻中国大使尤金在今天晚上举行盛大招待会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年。
    朱德副主席，刘少奇委员长，周恩来总理出席了今晚的招待会。
    周恩来总理在招待会上代表中国政府和人民向苏联政府和人民祝贺十月革命节这个伟大的节日，感谢苏联政府和人民对中国的兄弟般的真诚的援助。周总理说，中国人民根据自己的亲身经验，深知中苏两国的伟大的永恒的友谊是完全符合于我们两国人民和全体进步人类的根本利益的。进一步巩固和发展这个友谊将会极大地增强和平、民主和社会主义的力量，并且为维护世界和平和人类进步事业作出重大贡献。
    尤金大使在今晚的招待会上首先致词说，苏联人民走上建设以和平、民主和社会主义的各项原则为基础的新生活的道路已经有三十九年了。大使说，苏联为和平、民主的事业，也就是为崇高的正义事业进行着忘我的斗争，获得了全世界亿万人民的深切同情和尊敬。东方的一个伟大的社会主义国家——中华人民共和国以及欧洲和亚洲各人民民主国家同苏联一起，在平等的主权国家的大家庭里共同携手前进。他们的团结一致是维护各国人民间的和平的斗争之所以取得成就的一个决定性的条件。尤金大使最后说，使我们感到非常欣慰的是中国人民是这样兴奋地并且怀着热烈的兄弟般的情感来庆祝我们的国庆节日，并从而强调说明我国无产阶级革命胜利是具有伟大的世界历史意义的。
    尤金大使和周恩来总理讲话结束时，全体中外来宾都起立为中苏两国人民牢不可破的伟大友谊、为社会主义各国人民的伟大团结、为世界和平和民主的胜利干杯。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、李富春、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、叶剑英元帅、程潜、张治中、傅作义、龙云，外交部副部长张闻天，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝、章伯钧、包尔汉，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，中国国家机关各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体负责人，社会各界人士，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。（附图片）
