Title: Nehru Decides to Visit the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 14th: Indian Ambassador to the United States, Mehta, said after visiting US President Eisenhower on the 13th that President Eisenhower and Prime Minister Nehru have agreed to meet in Washington. He said that New Delhi and Washington will issue a joint statement this week clarifying the specific dates of the visit.

### 尼赫鲁决定访问美国

1956-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁决定访问美国
    新华社14日讯　华盛顿消息：印度驻美大使梅达13日在访问美国总统艾森豪威尔以后说，艾森豪威尔总统和尼赫鲁总理已经商定在华盛顿会晤。他说，新德里和华盛顿在这个星期内将发表公报说明明确的访问日期。
