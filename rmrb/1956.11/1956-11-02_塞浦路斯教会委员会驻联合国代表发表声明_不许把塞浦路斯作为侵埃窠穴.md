Title: Representative of the Cypriot Church Committee to the United Nations Issues Statement: No Allowing Cyprus to be Used as a Nest for Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 1st: Zenon Rossides, the representative of the Cypriot Church Committee stationed at the United Nations, issued a statement on October 31st, condemning Britain and France for using Cyprus as a military headquarters to direct the attack on Egypt. The statement said that in doing so, Britain and France, without obtaining the consent of the island's residents, exposed the island to terrible war disasters.

The statement pointed out that the current situation proves that Britain's refusal to allow Cyprus self-determination is not for the "interests of defending the free world" as Britain claims, but for the purpose of aggression.

### 塞浦路斯教会委员会驻联合国代表发表声明  不许把塞浦路斯作为侵埃窠穴

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯教会委员会驻联合国代表发表声明
    不许把塞浦路斯作为侵埃窠穴
    新华社1日讯　纽约联合国消息：塞浦路斯教会委员会派驻在联合国的代表泽农·罗西迪斯10月31日发表声明，谴责英法把塞浦路斯当作指挥进攻埃及的军事总部。声明说，这样，英法就在没有取得这个岛上的居民的同意的情况下，使这个岛面临着可怕的战争灾难。
    声明指出：目前的情况证明，英国拒绝让塞浦路斯自决，并不是为了英国所诳称的“保卫自由世界的利益”，而是为了侵略目的。
