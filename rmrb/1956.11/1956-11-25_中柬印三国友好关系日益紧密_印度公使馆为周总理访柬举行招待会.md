Title: China-Cambodia-India Friendly Relations are Growing Closer; Indian Legation Holds Reception for Premier Zhou's Visit to Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 24th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Sihanouk attended a reception held by the Indian Legation for the Chinese Premier on the evening of the 23rd.

More than a thousand people in Phnom Penh, hearing the news, gathered outside the Indian Legation to cheer Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Sihanouk. Many climbed onto buses and telephone poles in order to see them.

When Premier Zhou and Prince Sihanouk arrived at the Indian Legation, Indian girls presented them with garlands.

In his speech, Zhou said, "India, Cambodia, and China have established close and friendly relations on the basis of The Panchsheel (Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence)." He praised India's role in restoring peace in Indochina. Premier Zhou said, "India has also provided beneficial assistance to the development of friendly ties between China and Cambodia." He believes that the friendly relations between these three countries are in the interests of the people of China, Cambodia, and India.

Indian Chargé d'affaires Krishnan said in welcoming the distinguished guests that the Indian government and people are watching the "continuous development of friendly relations" between China and Cambodia under the wise guidance of Zhou Enlai and Sihanouk with deep friendly feelings and great satisfaction.

After Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception, people stood on both sides of the road to welcome him as he drove back to the Royal Palace. As Premier Zhou's motorcade passed, people cheered and applauded enthusiastically.

### 中柬印三国友好关系日益紧密  印度公使馆为周总理访柬举行招待会

1956-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中柬印三国友好关系日益紧密
    印度公使馆为周总理访柬举行招待会
    新华社金边24日电　周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王23日晚上出席了印度公使馆为中国总理举行的招待会。
    金边一千多群众知道这个消息以后，聚集在印度公使馆的外面向周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王欢呼。很多人爬上公共汽车和电线杆，以便能够看到他们。
    当周总理和西哈努克亲王到达印度公使馆的时候，印度的女孩子向他们献上花环。
    周恩来在讲话时说，“印度、柬埔寨和中国在和平共处的五项原则的基础上已经建立了紧密和友好的关系。”他赞扬印度对恢复印度支那和平所起的作用。周总理说，“印度对发展中国和柬埔寨之间的友好联系也提供了有益的帮助。”他认为，这三个国家的友好关系是符合中国、柬埔寨和印度人民的利益的。
    印度代办克里希南在欢迎贵宾时说，印度政府和人民怀着深厚的友好心情和极大的满意注视着在周恩来和西哈努克的英明指导下，中国和柬埔寨之间“友好关系的不断发展”。
    周恩来总理参加招待会以后，乘车回王宫的时候，人民群众又站立在马路两旁欢迎他。当周总理一行的汽车通过的时候，人们热烈地向他们欢呼和鼓掌。
