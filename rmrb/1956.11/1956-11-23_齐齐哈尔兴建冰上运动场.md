Title: Qiqihar to Build Ice Sports Field
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Qiqihar, 22nd. A large ice sports field is under construction in Qiqihar City, Heilongjiang Province. This sports field has an area of 35,000 square meters, and a wide circular 400-meter ice race track is built inside. It is surrounded by a fifteen-tiered semi-circular grandstand that can accommodate 20,000 spectators. The venue has lighting equipment, so competitions can also be held at night.
    It is estimated that this project can be completed by mid-December.

### 齐齐哈尔兴建冰上运动场

1956-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    齐齐哈尔兴建冰上运动场
    新华社齐齐哈尔22日电　黑龙江省齐齐哈尔市兴建一座大型冰上运动场。这个运动场面积有三万五千平方公尺，场内建有宽阔的环形四百公尺冰面竞赛跑道。周围设有十五层阶梯式半圆形看台，可以容纳两万观众。场内有照明设备，夜间也能比赛。
    这项工程估计到12月中旬可以全部完工。
