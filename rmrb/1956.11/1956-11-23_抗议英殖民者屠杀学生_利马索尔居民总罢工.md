Title: Limassol Residents on General Strike to Protest British Colonialists' Massacre of Students
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, 22nd: Residents of Limassol on the island of Cyprus held a general strike on the 21st to protest the British colonialists' atrocities in killing Cypriot students.
    On this day, shops and offices in Limassol were closed. Hundreds of people working on the construction site of the British Middle East Joint Military Headquarters also joined the strike.

### 抗议英殖民者屠杀学生  利马索尔居民总罢工

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议英殖民者屠杀学生
    利马索尔居民总罢工
    新华社22日讯　尼科西亚消息：塞浦路斯岛上的利马索尔居民在21日举行总罢工，抗议英国殖民者杀害塞浦路斯学生的暴行。
    在这一天，利马索尔的商店和办事机关都关了门。数以百计的正在英国中东军联合司令部建筑工地上作工的人也参加了罢工。
