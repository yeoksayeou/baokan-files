Title: British Garrison Oil Depot in Libya Destroyed; British Military Outpost Near Aden Attacked
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th: Cairo dispatch: According to a Cairo Radio broadcast on November 7th, the oil depot of the British troops stationed in Libya has been destroyed, resulting in the loss of three-quarters of the British oil storage in Libya.

Cairo Radio also broadcast that the British military outpost, Murilas, in the Aden Protectorate was attacked by local people. Murilas is close to the Yemeni border.

Damascus Radio broadcast on the same day that large-scale mass demonstrations had taken place in the British colony of Aden and the Aden Protectorate in support of the Egyptian people. British military vehicles were attacked near Aden.

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th: Amman dispatch: The Jordanian Army Headquarters announced on November 7th that Jordanian army anti-aircraft guns shot down an Israeli reconnaissance plane that invaded Jordanian airspace on the morning of November 7th.

Jordanian officials said that this was the second Israeli plane shot down over Jordanian airspace in two days.

### 利比亚英国驻军的石油库被炸毁  亚丁附近英军前哨据点遭到袭击

1956-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    利比亚英国驻军的石油库被炸毁
    亚丁附近英军前哨据点遭到袭击
    新华社8日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台11月7日广播，英国驻利比亚军队军营的石油库已被炸毁，英军在利比亚的石油储存有四分之三因此损失。
    开罗电台广播又说，英军在亚丁保护地内的前哨据点穆里拉斯受到了当地群众的袭击。穆里拉斯靠近也门边境。
    大马士革电台在同一天广播说，在英国的亚丁殖民地和亚丁保护地都发生了大规模的群众示威，声援埃及人民。英国军用车曾经在亚丁附近受到袭击。
    新华社8日讯　安曼消息：约旦陆军总部11月7日宣布，约旦陆军高射炮11月7日上午击落了一架侵入约旦领空的以色列侦察机。
    约旦官员说，这是在两天以内在约旦领空击落的第二架以色列飞机。
