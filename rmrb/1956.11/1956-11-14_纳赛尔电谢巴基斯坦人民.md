Title: Nasser Sends Telegram to Thank the People of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 13th -- Egyptian President Nasser recently sent a telegram to Bhashani, leader of the Pakistan People's League, thanking the People's League for initiating "Egypt Week" in Pakistan.
    Nasser said in the telegram: "The Egyptian government and people are proud to have Pakistani brothers and their admirable support."

### 纳赛尔电谢巴基斯坦人民

1956-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔电谢巴基斯坦人民
    新华社13日讯　卡拉奇消息：埃及总统纳赛尔最近打电报给巴基斯坦人民联盟领袖巴沙尼，感谢人民联盟在巴基斯坦发起“埃及周”。
    纳赛尔在电报中说：“埃及政府和人民因为有巴基斯坦兄弟和他们的值得赞美的支援而自豪。”
