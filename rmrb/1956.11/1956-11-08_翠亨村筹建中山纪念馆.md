Title: Preparing to Build Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Cuiheng Village
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 6th. Zhongshan County, Guangdong Province, has decided to build a Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall near Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Cuiheng Village to display Sun Yat-sen's relics and related revolutionary historical materials.

The Memorial Hall will be built on the open space to the left of Sun Yat-sen's former residence, covering an area of approximately six mu. Currently, relevant departments have dispatched a working group to Cuiheng Village to carry out preparatory work.

The management personnel of Sun Yat-sen's former residence have re-arranged the former residence based on the opinions of the elderly in the village and the results of various investigations, so that the furnishings of the former residence can be restored to the way they were during Mr. Sun Yat-sen's lifetime. The historical materials of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's life, which were exhibited in the former residence last year, are planned to be transferred to the Memorial Hall. More than 600 citrus trees have been planted on the open space near the former residence, which will be developed into a garden.

### 翠亨村筹建中山纪念馆

1956-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    翠亨村筹建中山纪念馆
    新华社广州6日电　广东省中山县决定在翠亨村孙中山先生故居附近筹建中山先生纪念馆，陈列孙中山先生遗物及有关的革命史料。
    纪念馆将建筑在孙中山先生故居左侧的空地上，占地约六亩。目前，有关部门已经派出一个工作组到翠亨村进行筹备工作。
    孙中山先生故居的管理人员已经根据村中老人的意见和各方调查的结果，将故居进行了重新布置，使故居陈设能恢复中山先生生前的样子。去年在故居展出的中山先生生平史料则准备移交给纪念馆。故居附近的空地上目前已经种植了六百多株柑橘树，准备辟成一个花园。
