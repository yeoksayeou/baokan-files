Title: Pakistani People Contribute Money and Effort to Support Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 8th -- People from all over Pakistan are enthusiastically donating money and signing up to join volunteer service teams to support Egypt.

The National Headquarters of the Pakistan Red Cross Society has begun raising an emergency relief fund for the Egyptian people, with a target of one million rupees. The President of the Red Cross Society, Syed Wajid Ali, said in Karachi on the evening of November 6 that the Pakistan Red Cross Society will also send a medical team to Egypt in the shortest possible time.

The Mayor of Karachi has launched a Mayor's Fund to help Egypt, and he himself donated two thousand rupees.

Colonel Hassan, Joint Secretary-General of the Pakistan People's Alliance and Convener of Pakistani Volunteers Against British and French Aggression in Egypt, announced in Karachi on November 6 that since he issued a call at a public gathering on November 2, three hundred volunteers have applied for service so far.

### 巴基斯坦人民  出钱出力支援埃及

1956-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦人民
    出钱出力支援埃及
    新华社卡拉奇8日电　巴基斯坦各地人民踊跃捐款和报名参加支援埃及的志愿人员服务队。
    巴基斯坦红十字会全国总部已经开始为埃及人民募集一笔紧急救济基金，目标是一百万卢比。红十字会会长赛义德·瓦吉德·阿里11月6日晚上在卡拉奇说，巴基斯坦红十字会还将在最短期内派遣一支医疗队到埃及去。
    卡拉奇市市长发起了一项帮助埃及的市长基金，他本人捐了二千卢比。
    巴基斯坦人民联盟联合秘书长、巴基斯坦反对英法侵略埃及志愿人员召集人哈桑上校11月6日在卡拉奇宣布，在11月2日他在一个公众集会上发出号召以后，到现在为止已经有三百名志愿人员申请服役。
