Title: Jiangsu Province, Nanjing Municipal People's Committee and CPPCC Committee Host Banquet for Central Mausoleum Pilgrimage Delegation and Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th: The Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee, Nanjing Municipal People's Committee, Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC jointly hosted a banquet this evening to entertain the Central Mausoleum Pilgrimage Delegation and guests from eight countries including the Soviet Union who came to Nanjing to visit the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum.

Zhu De, head of the Central Mausoleum Pilgrimage Delegation, spoke at the banquet. He expressed his gratitude to the foreign guests who came to Nanjing to visit the mausoleum, and to the people of Nanjing for their good protection and repair of the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum.

Next, the head of the Soviet delegation, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the CPSU, Moskatov, spoke. He said that Mr. Sun Yat-sen was a great patriot. Lenin had a very high evaluation of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and the Soviet people, like the Chinese people, admire and love Mr. Sun Yat-sen. He went on to say that Mr. Sun Yat-sen firmly opposed colonialism, but now Britain, France, and Israel are invading Egypt. He expressed his firm support for the just struggle of the Egyptian people. Finally, he thanked them for inviting him to China to participate in the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

The head of the Mongolian delegation, Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers and member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Avirmed, said that the Chinese people love Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and the Mongolian people also love Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Mr. Sun Yat-sen is the great son of the Chinese people. Jiichiro Matsumoto, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, also spoke at the banquet, saying that to achieve true peace and eliminate war, China and Japan must be friendly.

The banquet ended at about 8 p.m.

### 江苏省、南京市人民委员会和政协委员会  设宴招待中央谒陵代表团和外宾

1956-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    江苏省、南京市人民委员会和政协委员会
    设宴招待中央谒陵代表团和外宾
    据新华社南京12日电　江苏省人民委员会、南京市人民委员会、政协江苏省委员会和政协南京市委员会今晚联合设宴招待到南京参谒中山陵的中央谒陵代表团和苏联等八个国家到南京谒陵的来宾。
    中央谒陵代表团团长朱德在宴会上讲话。他对到南京谒陵的外国来宾们表示感谢，对南京人民良好地保护并且修理了中山陵表示感谢。
    接着，苏联代表团团长、苏共中央检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫讲话。他说，中山先生是一个伟大的爱国主义者。列宁对中山先生有极高的评价，苏联人民和中国人民一样地崇敬和热爱中山先生。他接着说，中山先生坚决反对殖民主义，但是现在英国、法国和以色列正在侵略埃及，他表示要坚决支持埃及人民的正义的斗争。最后他对邀请他来中国参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念表示感谢。
    蒙古代表团团长、蒙古部长会议副主席、蒙古人民革命党中央委员阿维尔齐德讲话，他说中国人民热爱孙中山先生，蒙古人民也一样热爱孙中山先生，孙中山先生是中国人民的伟大儿子。日本中国友好协会会长松本治一郎也在宴会上讲了话，他说要得到真正的和平，消灭战争，中日两国必须友好。
    宴会在当晚八时许结束。
