Title: Under US Direction, Colombian Government Persecutes the People
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th, Bogota dispatch: According to a report in the Colombian "Truth Daily," secretly published in Bogota, under the direction of the US military mission, the Colombian government has carried out brutal massacres of farmers who oppose the government's autocratic actions and is carrying out large-scale persecution of patriotic and democratic forces.

The "Truth Daily" reported that "A 'punitive expedition' sent to the southern Torima region not long ago killed 496 farmers there, including some elderly people and children. The newspaper stated that thousands of political prisoners are being held in Colombian prisons; as long as an informer provides information, military courts arrest and interrogate people and subject them to ruthless persecution.

### 在美国的指使下  哥伦比亚政府残害人民

1956-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    在美国的指使下
    哥伦比亚政府残害人民
    新华社25日讯　波哥大消息：据波哥大秘密出版的哥伦比亚“真理报”报道：在美国军事使团的指使下，哥伦比亚政府对那些反对政府专制行为的农民进行了残酷的屠杀，并且正在大规模迫害爱国民主力量。
    “真理报”的消息说，“不久以前派往托里马南部地区的一支“讨伐队”在那里杀害了四百九十六个农民，其中包括一些老人和小孩。报纸说，哥伦比亚监狱里正关着成千上万的政治犯；只要有告密者报信，军事法庭就抓人审讯，并且加以无情的迫害。
