Title: Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Taoist Association Established
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Twenty-three well-known figures from the Taoist community across the country recently held a meeting in Beijing to prepare for the establishment of the Chinese Taoist Association.

During the meeting, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, gave a report on the state's policy of freedom of religious belief, expressing support for the establishment of the Chinese Taoist Association.

The attending initiators exchanged opinions enthusiastically on relevant issues and studied and discussed in detail the purposes, tasks, and organization of the preparation for the establishment of the Chinese Taoist Association.

The meeting unanimously adopted the "Initiating Statement for the Chinese Taoist Association."

The initiating statement said: "In order to unite Taoists across the country to further contribute our strength in loving the motherland, actively participating in socialist construction, and safeguarding world peace, and in order to assist the government in implementing the policy of freedom of religious belief and further promote the excellent traditions of Taoism, we feel that there is a need for a national Taoist organization. Therefore, we initiate the establishment of the Chinese Taoist Association." The initiating statement expressed the hope that Taoist priests and practitioners in various regions would actively sponsor and enthusiastically support the establishment of this organization.

The meeting approved the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Taoist Association. Yue Chongdai was appointed as the chairman of the preparatory committee, and Chen Yingning and Meng Minghui were appointed as vice-chairmen, responsible for carrying out the preparatory work.

### 中国道教协会筹备委员会成立

1956-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中国道教协会筹备委员会成立
    新华社26日讯　全国道教界知名人士二十三人最近在北京举行会议，筹备成立中国道教协会。
    会议期间，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘就国家的宗教信仰自由政策作了报告，表示支持成立中国道教协会。
    到会的各位发起人对有关问题热烈地交换了意见，详细地研究和讨论了筹组中国道教协会的目的、任务和组织等事宜。
    会议一致通过了“中国道教协会发起书”。
    发起书中说：“为了团结全国道教徒在爱护祖国、积极参加社会主义建设和保卫世界和平的事业中进一步贡献我们的力量，为了协助政府贯彻宗教信仰自由政策，并进一步发扬道教优良传统，我们觉得需要有一个全国性的道教组织，因此我们发起成立中国道教协会。”发起书表示希望各地道长、司玄积极赞助并热烈支持成立这一组织。
    会议通过成立了中国道教协会筹备委员会。推定岳崇岱为筹备委员会主任委员，陈樱宁、孟明慧为副主任委员，负责进行筹备工作。
