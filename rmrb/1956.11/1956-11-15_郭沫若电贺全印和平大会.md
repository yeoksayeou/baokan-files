Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram of Congratulations to All-India Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, today sent a telegram congratulating the All-India Peace Conference to be held in Calcutta, India on the 15th. The telegram reads as follows: To the All-India Peace Council, forwarded to the All-India Peace Conference:
    I would like to express, on behalf of the Chinese people, the highest respect and warm support to the All-India Peace Conference, and through the delegates of the conference, to the peace-loving people of India.
    At a time when British and French colonialists and their tool, the Israeli government, are disregarding the warnings of world opinion, and carrying out armed aggression against Egypt, seriously threatening peace in Asia and Africa and the entire world, your convening of the All-India Peace Conference is extremely timely and meaningful. We firmly believe that your conference will make a significant contribution to mobilizing the masses to condemn aggression, oppose war, defend peace, and support the struggle of Egypt. The Chinese people are forever willing to work together with the Indian people to strengthen the unity of the people of Asian and African countries, defeat colonialist aggression, and safeguard world peace. I wish the conference success!
    China Peace Committee
                    Chairman, Guo Moruo
                November 14, 1956

### 郭沫若电贺全印和平大会

1956-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺全印和平大会
  新华社14日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天致电祝贺将于15日在印度加尔各答召开的全印和平大会。电文如下：全印和平理事会转全印和平大会
    我谨代表中国人民向全印和平大会并通过大会的代表们向爱好和平的印度人民表示崇高的敬意和热烈的支持。
    正当英、法殖民主义者和它们的工具以色列政府不顾世界舆论的警告，武装侵略埃及，严重威胁亚非两洲以及全世界的和平的时候，你们召开了全印度的和平大会，这是极为适时和极有意义的。我们深信，贵大会在动员群众谴责侵略、反对战争、保卫和平、支援埃及的斗争上，一定能作出巨大的贡献。中国人民永远愿与印度人民一道，为加强亚非各国人民的团结，击败殖民主义的侵略、维护世界和平而共同奋斗。　谨祝大会成功！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                    主席　郭沫若
                1956年11月14日
