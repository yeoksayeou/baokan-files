Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Receives Moskatov
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, today received the Soviet delegation headed by Moskatov, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, who were invited to China to participate in the celebrations of the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Present at the reception were Deng Yingchao and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
　　The Soviet delegation returned to Beijing from Shanghai yesterday afternoon.

### 朱副主席接见莫斯卡托夫

1956-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见莫斯卡托夫
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国副主席朱德今天接见了应邀来我国参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念活动的、以苏联共产党中央检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫为首的苏联代表团。接见时在座的有邓颖超和中苏友好协会总会理事会副秘书长戈宝权。
    苏联代表团是昨天下午从上海回到北京的。
