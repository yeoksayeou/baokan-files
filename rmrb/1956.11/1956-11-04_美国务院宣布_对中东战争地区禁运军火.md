Title: U.S. State Department Announces Arms Embargo on Middle East War Zone
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 3rd: The U.S. State Department announced on November 2nd a ban on the shipment of any U.S. military supplies to the Middle East war zone, including Israel and Egypt.

For a long time, the United States has not shipped any military equipment to Egypt, while Israel has consistently received U.S. military supplies.

State Department Press Officer White, when announcing the decision to halt military supplies, referred to the corresponding resolution passed by the United Nations General Assembly on the evening of November 1st. He also said that he could not explain why Egypt, which was under attack, was also included in the embargo.

### 美国务院宣布  对中东战争地区禁运军火

1956-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    美国务院宣布
    对中东战争地区禁运军火
    新华社3日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院11月2日宣布禁止向中东的战争地区，包括以色列和埃及运送任何美国军用物资。
    长久以来，美国已经没有运送任何军事装备给埃及，而以色列却一直得到美国军事物资的供应。
    国务院新闻发布官怀特在宣布关于停运军事物资的决定的时候提到联合国大会11月1日晚上通过的相应决议。他还说，他不能解释为什么被攻击的埃及也包括在禁运的范围之内。
