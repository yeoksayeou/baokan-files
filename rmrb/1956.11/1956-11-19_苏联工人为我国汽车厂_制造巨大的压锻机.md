Title: Soviet Workers Manufacture Huge Press Forging Machine for China's Automobile Factory
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 5

Moscow, November 18th (via our correspondent) - The Likhachev Automobile Factory in Moscow is nearing completion of assembling a super-sized press forging machine. This press forging machine is specially manufactured for China's First Automobile Works. The pressure of the press forging machine is 3,500 tons. The welded seams of this machine are 17 kilometers long. It weighs 782 tons and its height is as high as a five-story building. Transporting this machine will require at least twelve to fourteen specialized railway cars. The workers decided to complete the assembly of this new press forging machine ahead of schedule and ship it to China before the New Year.

### 苏联工人为我国汽车厂  制造巨大的压锻机

1956-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联工人为我国汽车厂
    制造巨大的压锻机
    本报莫斯科18日电　莫斯科利哈乔夫汽车制造厂最近将要完成一架超型特大的压锻机的装配工作。这架压锻机是为我国第一汽车制造厂专门制造的。压锻机的压力是三千五百吨，这部机器的焊接缝，全长有十七公里，它的重量是七百八十二吨，它的高度有五层楼那样高。运输这部机器至少要十二—十四个专门的车皮。工人们决定提前完成这个新压锻机的装配工作，并在新年以前运送到中国。
