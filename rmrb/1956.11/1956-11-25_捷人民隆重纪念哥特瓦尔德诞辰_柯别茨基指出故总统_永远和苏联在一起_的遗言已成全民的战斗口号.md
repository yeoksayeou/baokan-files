Title: Czechoslovak People Grandly Commemorate Gottwald's Birth Anniversary, Kopřiva Points Out Late President's Testament "Forever With the Soviet Union" Has Become National Battle Cry
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 24th: The Czechoslovak people grandly commemorated the sixtieth anniversary of the birth of the late President Gottwald on November 23rd.

　　A memorial evening was held in Prague on the evening of the 23rd. Kopřiva, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, reported on Gottwald's life and deeds at the evening. He said that Gottwald was a loyal Leninist and an enthusiastic fighter for communism and for the consolidation and unbreakable friendship with the Soviet Union. Gottwald once said: "We will always be with the Soviet Union and will never change," and this sentence has now become the battle cry of all the Czechoslovak people. Kopřiva pointed out that when Comrade Gottwald was leading the country's economic development, he timely noticed the imbalance in socialist economic development. Kopřiva said that by working hard to overcome these imbalances that were recognized in a timely manner, we were able to avoid various economic imbalances that could have caused unfortunate consequences.

　　Kopřiva also talked about the Hungarian events. He said that in the Hungarian events, we have a deeper understanding of the significance of the Soviet Union to the socialist cause.

　　In Gottwald's birthplace, Dědice village, the unveiling ceremony of the Gottwald monument was held on the 23rd. A delegation from the Communist Party and the government, headed by Prime Minister Široký, participated in the unveiling ceremony.

### 捷人民隆重纪念哥特瓦尔德诞辰  柯别茨基指出故总统“永远和苏联在一起”的遗言已成全民的战斗口号

1956-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    捷人民隆重纪念哥特瓦尔德诞辰
    柯别茨基指出故总统“永远和苏联在一起”的遗言已成全民的战斗口号
    新华社布拉格24日电　11月23日，捷克斯洛伐克人民隆重纪念故总统哥特瓦尔德六十周年诞辰。
    在布拉格，23日晚间举行了纪念晚会。捷共中央政治局委员柯别茨基在晚会上报告了哥特瓦尔德的生平事迹。他说，哥特瓦尔德是忠实的列宁主义者，是为共产主义、为同苏联的巩固和牢不可破的友谊而奋斗的热情战士。哥特瓦尔德曾经说过：“我们永远和苏联在一起，任何时候也不改变”，这句话现在已经成为全体捷克斯洛伐克人民的战斗口号。柯别茨基指出，哥特瓦尔德同志在领导国家经济发展的时候，曾经及时注意到社会主义经济发展的不平衡情况。柯别茨基说，由于努力克服了这些及时认识到的不平衡情况，我们才能够避免了引起不幸后果的各种经济不平衡现象。
    柯别茨基还谈到了匈牙利事件。他说，在匈牙利事件中，我们更加深刻地认识到苏联对于社会主义事业的意义。
    在哥特瓦尔德的诞生地德迪泽村，23日举行了哥特瓦尔德纪念碑揭幕式。以西罗基总理为首的共产党和政府的代表团参加了揭幕仪式。
