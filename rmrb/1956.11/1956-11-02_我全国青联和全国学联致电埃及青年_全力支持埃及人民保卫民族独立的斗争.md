Title: All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation Send Telegrams to Egyptian Youth, Fully Supporting the Egyptian People's Struggle to Defend National Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation today sent telegrams to Cairo, expressing extreme anger at the armed aggression of Egypt by Britain and France, and expressing full support for the heroic struggle of Egyptian youth and the Egyptian people.
    The All-China Youth Federation's telegram to the Supreme Council for Youth Welfare and Sports of Egypt and the Youth Department of the Egyptian Liberation Conference stated: "All Chinese youth, with extreme anger, protest against the British and French governments' issuance of a so-called 'ultimatum' to the Egyptian government and the bombing of the sacred territory of Egypt, this imperialist bandit act, and the armed invasion of Egypt by the Israeli government under the manipulation of colonialists. On behalf of the 120 million youth of China, we once again express our full support for the heroic struggle of Egyptian youth and the Egyptian people, under the leadership of President Nasser, to defend national independence and national sovereignty. We deeply believe that your just struggle, with the support of all peace-loving and just people in the world, will surely achieve final victory."
    The All-China Students' Federation's telegram to Cairo University and the General Union of Egyptian University Students also expressed great indignation and solemn protest against the imperialist bandit acts of Britain and France and the armed aggression against Egypt by the Israeli government under the instigation of colonialists. The telegram stated: "The students of China, together with the people of our country, firmly support the just struggle of Egyptian students and the Egyptian people, under the leadership of President Nasser, to defend national independence and national sovereignty. We warn the imperialists and their vassals, those who play with fire will burn themselves! The final victory will surely belong to Egypt."

### 我全国青联和全国学联致电埃及青年  全力支持埃及人民保卫民族独立的斗争

1956-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国青联和全国学联致电埃及青年
    全力支持埃及人民保卫民族独立的斗争
    新华社1日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会今天分别打电报给开罗，对英法两国武装侵略埃及的行为表示万分愤怒，对埃及青年和埃及人民的英勇斗争表示全力支持。
    中华全国民主青年联合会给埃及青年福利及体育最高理事会和埃及解放大会青年部的电报说：“全中国青年以万分愤怒的心情，抗议英、法两国政府对埃及政府提出所谓
  ‘最后通牒’及轰炸埃及神圣国土这一帝国主义强盗行为和以色列政府在殖民主义者摆弄下对埃及所进行的武装侵犯。我们代表全中国一亿二千万青年对埃及青年及埃及人民在纳赛尔总统领导下所进行的保卫民族独立和国家主权的英勇斗争，再一次表示全力的支持，深信你们的正义斗争，在全世界所有爱好和平与正义的人民的支持下，必将获得最后的胜利。”
    中华全国学生联合会给开罗大学和埃及大学生总联合会的电报，也对英、法两国的帝国主义强盗行为和以色列政府在殖民主义者的唆使下对埃及进行的武装侵略，表示极大的愤慨和严正的抗议。电报说：“全中国的学生和我国人民一道坚决支持埃及学生和埃及人民在纳赛尔总统领导下所进行的保卫民族独立和国家主权的正义斗争。我们警告帝国主义者和它的附庸，玩火者必自焚！最后的胜利必定是属于埃及的。”
