Title: Soviet Union Invites American Athletes to Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, 16th: The chairman of the Soviet Sports Association, Konstantin Krubin, formally invited American track and field athletes to visit Moscow in 1957 this morning in Melbourne.

This invitation was extended by Krubin to the leader of the American track and field team at the Olympic Village in Melbourne.

Dan Ferris, one of the heads of the American Amateur Athletic Union, stated that he accepted the invitation.

### 苏联邀请美国运动员访问

1956-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联邀请美国运动员访问
    据新华社16日讯　墨尔本消息：苏联体育协会主席康斯坦丁·克鲁宾今天上午在墨尔本正式邀请美国田径运动员在1957年访问莫斯科。
    这个邀请是克鲁宾在墨尔本的奥运村向美国田径队的领队提出的。
    美国业余体育协会负责人之一丹·费里斯表示接受这个邀请。
