Title: Henan Disaster Victims Move into New Homes
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 6

    Henan Disaster Victims Move into New Homes
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, November 10th: Up to now, more than 500,000 rooms of houses flooded and collapsed in the Henan flood-stricken areas have been repaired, and the vast majority of disaster victims have moved into new homes.

### 河南灾民迁入新居

1956-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    河南灾民迁入新居
    据新华社郑州10日电　到现在为止，河南水灾地区已修复被淹倒塌的房屋五十多万间，绝大部分灾民都已搬进了新房。
