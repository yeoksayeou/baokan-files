Title: Czechoslovak Folk Art Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The "Czechoslovak Folk Art Exhibition" organized by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations opened today at Daoning Zhai in Beihai Park, Beijing.
    The exhibition displays Czechoslovak folk costumes, woven goods, daily necessities, toys, puppets, and pictures of Czechoslovak folk dances, etc. The exhibition is expected to be on display in Beijing for three weeks.
    Those who visited the exhibition today included Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, Cai Ruohong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, people from literary and art circles and other circles, and reporters.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor, and many foreign diplomatic officials in China also visited the exhibition.

### 捷民间艺术展览会在北京开幕

1956-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

    捷民间艺术展览会在北京开幕
    新华社9日讯　对外文化联络局举办的“捷克斯洛伐克民间艺术展览会”今天在北京北海公园道宁斋开幕。
    展览会展出了捷克斯洛伐克的民间服装、编织品、生活用品、玩偶、木偶和捷克斯洛伐克民间舞蹈的图片等。展览会预计在北京展出三个星期。
    今天到展览会参观的有文化部副部长张致祥，中国美术家协会副主席蔡若虹，文艺界及其他各界人士和记者等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使格里哥尔和许多国家驻中国的外交官员也参观了展览会。
