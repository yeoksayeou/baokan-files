Title: Speech by Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 3

    Speech by Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
    Chairman, dear comrades:
    I am speaking on behalf of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League. I fully endorse the Chairman's speech just now. Today, there is also a team of Taiwanese people in Tiananmen Square. The people of Taiwan are united with the people of the motherland in opposing the Anglo-French aggression against Egypt and supporting Egypt's just war of resistance against aggression.
    British and French imperialism is already an old-brand imperialism, and its crimes are known to all. The people of Taiwan are familiar with the face of imperialism, we know its ugliness, loathsomeness and hatred. We must fight against imperialism. Let us hold this rally and demonstration, let us crush it to death with our footsteps, let us crush it to death!
    Egypt is China's brother. In ancient times, Egypt, Babylon, India and China were four brothers. We believe that Egypt's just struggle will surely achieve final victory. Today we march and demonstrate, let us pay tribute to our brothers and sisters in Egypt, let us express to Egypt: the Chinese people are your reliable friends, we support Egypt. The hundreds of thousands of people in Tiananmen Square represent the will of China's 600 million people, we support Egypt. We stand with the Egyptian people and wage a tenacious struggle against the aggressors until final victory.
    Let us shout:
    Oppose Anglo-French aggression against Egypt!
    Support Egypt's just struggle!
    Long live world peace!

### 台湾民主自治同盟主席  谢雪红的讲话

1956-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

    台湾民主自治同盟主席
    谢雪红的讲话
    主席，亲爱的同志们：
    我代表台湾民主自治同盟讲话。我完全拥护刚才主席的发言。今天，在天安门广场也有我们台湾人的队伍。台湾人民和祖国人民一致，反对英法侵略埃及，支持埃及抵抗侵略的正义战争。
    英法帝国主义已经是老牌帝国主义，它们的罪恶天下皆知。台湾人民熟识帝国主义的面貌，我们知道它的丑恶，可憎和可恨。我们要和帝国主义作斗争。我们今天开大会，示威游行，让我们用我们的脚步把它踩死，把它踩死吧！
    埃及是中国的兄弟。在古代，埃及、巴比伦、印度和中国就是四个兄弟。我们相信埃及的正义斗争一定要取得最后胜利。我们今天游行示威，让我们向埃及的兄弟姊妹们致敬，让我们向埃及表示：中国人民是你们可靠的朋友，我们支持埃及。天安门广场几十万人的队伍代表着中国六万万人民的意志，我们支持埃及。我们和埃及人民站在一起，向侵略者作顽强的斗争，到最后胜利为止。
    让我们高呼：
    反对英法侵略埃及！
    支持埃及的正义斗争！
    世界和平万岁！
