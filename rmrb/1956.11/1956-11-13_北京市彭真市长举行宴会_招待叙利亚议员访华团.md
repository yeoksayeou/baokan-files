Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet to Welcome Syrian Parliamentary Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a banquet tonight to welcome the Syrian parliamentary delegation headed by Ali Bouzo.

The hosts and guests sat around the table, warmly discussing friendship. Peng Zhen said at the banquet that Syria is our beloved brother, and China and Arab countries are closely related in all aspects. He said that the Chinese people, as well as the people of Beijing, resolutely support the just struggle of the Egyptian people against the Anglo-French imperialist aggression. He proposed a toast to the prosperity of Syria, to the friendship between China and Syria, to the friendship between the Chinese and Arab peoples, to the great unity of all peace-loving people in the world, to the eternal coexistence of all nations in the world, and to the peaceful coexistence of all countries.

Head of the delegation, Ali Bouzo, spoke at the meeting, expressing his gratitude to the host for the warm reception on behalf of the delegation. He proposed a toast to the growing friendship and increasing unity between China and Syria.

Abd al-Halim Qaddur, a member of the delegation and one of the leaders of the Syrian Ba'ath Party, said that since arriving in this friendly country, we have felt so much friendship and warmth, and we feel that we are among friends who understand us very well. He said that the friendship between China and Syria is for close cooperation and the defense of peace. Because the cause of world peace depends on the unity of the people of Asian and African countries. The unity between the Chinese people and the Arab people is the foundation for defending our national independence and consolidating peace. He also said that the delegation's visit to China will help the friendship between the Arab people and the Chinese people. He proposed: a toast to the friendship between the people of all countries, especially between Syria and China, and to the generous and hospitable Mayor of Beijing and the people of Beijing.

Also present at tonight's banquet were Beijing Vice Mayors Wu Han and Yue SongSheng, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Ren, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Vice Presidents Zhou Gengsheng, Qian Duansheng, Luo Longji, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association Ma Yu Huai, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

### 北京市彭真市长举行宴会  招待叙利亚议员访华团

1956-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长举行宴会
    招待叙利亚议员访华团
    新华社12日讯　北京市市长彭真今晚举行宴会，招待以阿里·布佐为首的叙利亚议员访华团。
    宾主们围桌而坐，畅叙友谊。彭真在宴会上说，叙利亚是我们亲爱的兄弟，中国和阿拉伯国家之间在各方面息息相关。他说，中国人民以及北京市人民坚决支持埃及人民的反抗英法帝国主义侵略的正义斗争。他提议为叙利亚的繁荣，为中叙两国的友谊，为中国阿拉伯人民间的友谊，为全世界一切爱好和平人民大团结，为世界各民族永远共存，为各国和平共处而干杯。
    阿里·布佐团长在会上讲话，代表访华团向主人盛情招待表示感谢。他提议为叙中两国友谊的日益增长和两国团结的日益加强而干杯。
    访华团团员、叙利亚复兴社会党领导人之一阿卜杜勒·哈利姆·卡杜尔讲话说，从我们到这个友好国家后，我们就感受到非常多的友情和温暖，就感觉到我们是在非常了解我们的朋友之中。他说，中叙两国的友谊是为了密切合作和保卫和平。因为，世界和平事业有赖于亚非各国人民的团结。中国人民和阿拉伯人民间的团结就是保卫我们国家的独立和保卫和平的巩固基础。他又说，访华团这次访问中国将有助于阿拉伯人民和中国人民间的友谊。他提议：为各国人民间首先为叙利亚同中国间的友谊，为慷慨好客的北京市长和北京市人民干杯。
    出席今晚宴会的，有北京市副市长吴晗、乐松生，政协北京市委员会主席刘仁，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长周鲠生、钱端升、罗隆基，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任马玉槐以及北京各界人士等。
