Title: Soviet Circus Performers Watch Performance by Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Soviet circus performers watched a performance of song and dance programs by the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe this evening. At the enthusiastic request of the Soviet circus performers and other audience members, many programs were re-performed.
    At the end of the performance, the Soviet circus performers went on stage to present flowers to the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe. The artists from the two countries warmly shook hands.

### 苏联马戏团演员观看匈人民军文工团演出

1956-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联马戏团演员观看匈人民军文工团演出
    新华社19日讯　今天晚上，苏联马戏团演员们观看了匈牙利人民军文工团演出的歌舞节目。在苏联马戏团的演员和其他观众热情的要求下，许多节目都作了重演。
    演出结束，苏联马戏团演员们登台向匈牙利人民军文工团献花，两国艺术家们亲切地握手。
