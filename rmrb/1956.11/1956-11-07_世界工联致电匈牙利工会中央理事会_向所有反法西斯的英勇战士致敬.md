Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Message to the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions, Saluting All the Heroic Anti-Fascist Fighters

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 6th, Prague dispatch: The World Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram from Prague on November 5th to the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions, which stated:
    Dear Comrades!
    The World Federation of Trade Unions and the working people of the world salute all the fighters who participated in suppressing the fascist counter-revolutionary elements—the workers, peasants, and intellectuals of your country. The appalling and bloody acts that attempted to undermine the people's democracy and the socialist foundations of Hungary with the support of the entire international reactionaries show that anti-fascist vigilance must be heightened, and all the organized forces of the working class must be universally united.
    Long live the unity of the Hungarian working people in the struggle for socialism, democracy, the happiness of the Hungarian people, against fascism and international reactionaries—the warmongers! Long live international workers' solidarity!
                Representative of the World Federation of Trade Unions, General Secretary Louis Saillant

### 世界工联致电匈牙利工会中央理事会  向所有反法西斯的英勇战士致敬

1956-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联致电匈牙利工会中央理事会
    向所有反法西斯的英勇战士致敬
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社布拉格讯：世界工会联合会11月5日在布拉格打电报给匈牙利工会中央理事会，电报说：
    亲爱的同志们！
    世界工会联合会和全世界劳动人民向所有参加镇压法西斯反革命分子的斗争的战士——贵国的工人、农民和知识分子致敬。企图在整个国际反动派支持下破坏匈牙利的人民民主和社会主义基础的骇人听闻的血腥行为表明，必须提高反法西斯的警惕，工人阶级一切有组织的力量必须普遍团结起来。
    匈牙利劳动人民在为社会主义、民主、匈牙利人民的幸福，反对法西斯主义和国际反动派——战争贩子的斗争中的团结万岁！国际工人团结万岁！
                世界工会联合会代表、总书记　路易·赛扬
