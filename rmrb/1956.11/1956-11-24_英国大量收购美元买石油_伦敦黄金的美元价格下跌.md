Title: Britain Buys Large Amounts of Dollars to Purchase Oil; Dollar Price of Gold in London Falls
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, 22nd: Reports indicate that the massive acquisition of dollars by Britain to pay for oil imports from the Americas has caused the dollar price of gold on the London market to fall to its lowest point since the gold market reopened two and a half years ago.
　　According to reports, the dollar price of gold on the 20th was $34.95 per ounce, while it was $35.07 several weeks prior.

### 英国大量收购美元买石油  伦敦黄金的美元价格下跌

1956-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

    英国大量收购美元买石油
    伦敦黄金的美元价格下跌
    新华社22日讯　伦敦消息：为了偿付美洲的石油进口而大量收购美元的情况，使得伦敦市场上黄金的美元价格下跌到两年半前黄金市场重新开放以来的最低点。
    据报道，20日黄金的美元价格是每盎司三四·九五美元，而几个星期以前是三五·零七美元。
