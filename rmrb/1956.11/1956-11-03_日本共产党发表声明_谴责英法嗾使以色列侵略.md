Title: Japanese Communist Party Issues Statement Condemning Britain and France for Instigating Israeli Aggression
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st. The Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party published a statement in today's "Akahata" newspaper, demanding that Israel immediately withdraw its troops to its territory, and opposing armed intervention by Britain and France.

　　The Japanese Communist Party said in a statement that Britain and France instigated Israel to invade Egypt and interfered in Egypt's internal affairs. The statement pointed out that Britain and France attempted to regain the Suez Canal by force.

### 日本共产党发表声明  谴责英法嗾使以色列侵略

1956-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    日本共产党发表声明
    谴责英法嗾使以色列侵略
    新华社1日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央常任干部会在今天的“赤旗报”上发表声明，要求以色列立即把它的军队撤回它的领土，并且反对英国和法国进行武装干涉。
    日本共产党在声明中说，英国和法国嗾使以色列侵略埃及，并且干涉埃及的内政。声明指出，英国和法国企图用武力重新夺回苏伊士运河。
