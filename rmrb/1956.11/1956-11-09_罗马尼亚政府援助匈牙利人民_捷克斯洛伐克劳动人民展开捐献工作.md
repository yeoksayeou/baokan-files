Title: Romanian Government Aids Hungarian People; Czechoslovakian Working People Launch Donation Drive
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 4

    Romanian Government Aids Hungarian People
    Czechoslovakian Working People Launch Donation Drive
    Xinhua News Agency, November 7th, Bucharest dispatch from TASS: In response to the request from the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government for fraternal aid to the Hungarian working people, the Romanian government has decided to provide emergency assistance to friendly People's Democratic Hungary. The aid consists of petroleum products, building materials, medicines, and other materials with a total value of 13 million lei.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 7th, Prague dispatch: Workers in many factories and enterprises throughout Czechoslovakia are donating funds to help the Hungarian working people quickly heal the wounds inflicted by the counter-revolutionaries.
    Steelworkers at the Stalingrad Machine Factory in Prague have called on steelworkers across the country to organize special shifts in November in order to donate the wages from these shifts to the Hungarian working people. In Pilsen, workers in the heavy machinery workshop of the Lenin Factory have suggested that the entire factory add a "solidarity shift."
    Miners in Lesaki have decided to donate a quarter of the bonuses from their special shifts to purchase medical supplies for injured Hungarian workers. Workers at the Lebriz Food Enterprise unanimously decided to donate 25,000 crowns (Czech currency) from their factory director's fund to provide relief to the children of those who were killed while defending the Hungarian People's Democratic system. Members of unified agricultural cooperatives have also expressed their willingness to aid the Hungarian people with agricultural products.
    According to a broadcast by Radio Prague on the 6th, the Czechoslovak government has decided to establish a "Solidarity Fund for Aiding the Hungarian Working People" to be responsible for transferring all donated money and grain to the Hungarian government.

### 罗马尼亚政府援助匈牙利人民  捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民展开捐献工作

1956-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府援助匈牙利人民
    捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民展开捐献工作
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特7日讯：罗马尼亚政府为答复匈牙利工农革命政府关于给予匈牙利劳动人民以兄弟援助的请求，决定给予友好的人民民主的匈牙利以紧急的援助。援助的物资有共值一千三百万列伊的石油产品、建筑材料、医药品和其他物资。
    新华社布拉格7日电　捷克斯洛伐克全国各地的许多工厂和企业的工人纷纷捐献资金，帮助匈牙利劳动人民尽速医治反革命分子给他们带来的创伤。
    布拉格的斯大林格勒机器工厂的冶炼工人呼吁全国各地冶炼工人在11月份组织特别的班次，以便把这些班次的工资捐给匈牙利劳动人民。在比尔森，列宁工厂的重型机器车间的工人已经建议全厂工人加一个“团结班”。
    勒萨基的矿工们已经决定捐赠特别班的奖金的四分之一，供给受伤的匈牙利工人购买医药用品。列勃里兹食品企业的工人们一致决定，从他们的厂长基金中捐赠二万五千克朗（捷币），救济因保卫匈牙利人民民主制度而被害的人的子女。统一农业合作社的社员们也表示用农产品援助匈牙利人民。
    据布拉格电台6日宣布，捷克斯洛伐克政府已经决定设立“援助匈牙利劳动人民团结基金会”，负责把所有捐献的金钱和粮食转交给匈牙利政府。
