Title: France's Foreign Trade Deficit Increases
Author:
Date: 1956-11-29
Page: 6

    France's Foreign Trade Deficit Increases
    Xinhua News Agency, November 28th, TASS Paris, November 27th: According to the French newspaper "Le Monde," France's foreign trade deficit has increased significantly recently.
    If the deficit was 37.8 million francs in August this year, it increased to 39.9 million francs in September, and reached an unprecedented figure of 51.6 million francs in October.

### 法国对外贸易逆差增加

1956-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    法国对外贸易逆差增加
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社巴黎27日讯：据法国“世界报”报道，最近，法国对外贸易的逆差大大增长。
    如果在今年8月赤字是三千七百八十万法郎，那么在9月它就增加到了三千九百九十万法郎，而在10月里则达到了一个空前的数字——五千一百六十万法郎。
