Title: Syrian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Syrian Parliamentary Delegation, led by Mr. Ali Buzo, Secretary-General of the People's Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association and head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation that visited Syria, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and figures from the Islamic community.

Far, the Egyptian Commercial Representative in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

List of the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation:

　　Head: Ali Buzo (People's Party)
　　Members: Abdul Samad Futayeh (Second Deputy Speaker of Parliament, Constitutional Group), Abdul Hamid Duedari (People's Party), Jihad Howan (Chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee, Independent), Ali Dandashi (Independent), Rateb Husami (First Secretary of Parliament, People's Party), Musalam Haddad (People's Party), Daffy Bakar (People's Party), Hussein Shabani (Democratic Group), Abdul Halim Kadur (Arab Renaissance Socialist Party), Mazhar Shurbaji (National Party), Jamil Hilo (People's Party), Abdul Hadi Abasa (Socialist Party), Abdul Wahab Hudo (National Party), Hagop Marshew (Democratic Group)
　　Secretary: Georges Abu Jamra

### 叙利亚议员访华团到达北京

1956-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    叙利亚议员访华团到达北京
    新华社4日讯　叙利亚议员访华团在团长、人民党总书记阿里·布佐的率领下，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。访华团是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国作友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长胡愈之、秘书长吴茂荪，中国伊斯兰教协会主任，前访问叙利亚的中国文化艺术代表团团长包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，以及伊斯兰教界人士等。
    埃及驻中国商务代表法尔也到机场欢迎。
