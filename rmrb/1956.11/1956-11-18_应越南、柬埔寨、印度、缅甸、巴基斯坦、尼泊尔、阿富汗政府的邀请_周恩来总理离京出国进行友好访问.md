Title: At the Invitation of the Governments of Vietnam, Cambodia, India, Burma, Pakistan, Nepal, and Afghanistan, Premier Zhou Enlai Leaves Beijing for a Friendly Visit Abroad
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th—The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China issued the following press communique on the 17th:

    At the invitation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Government of the Republic of India, the Government of the Union of Burma, the Government of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, and the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, will pay a friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Republic of India, the Federation of Burma, the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the Kingdom of Nepal, and the Kingdom of Afghanistan, starting from the second half of November.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 17th—Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai left Beijing by special plane at 8:40 this morning, invited to pay a friendly visit to Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Republic of India, the Federation of Burma, the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the Kingdom of Nepal, and the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

    Accompanying him on the visit were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the Premier's Office Zhang Yan, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gong Peng, and other accompanying personnel.

    Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Hoang Van Hoan, and Head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation, Yang An, left Beijing on the same plane.

    Those who saw him off at the airport included Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and Ulanhu, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Ji Pengfei, and Zhang Hanfu, and others.

    Foreign diplomatic officials who saw him off at the airport included: Pakistan Ambassador Ahmed, Afghan Ambassador Samad, Indonesian Ambassador Wirjopranoto, Egyptian Ambassador Hassan Ragab, Burmese Charge d'Affaires U Ba Maung, Indian Charge d'Affaires Bahadur Singh, Vietnamese Democratic Republic Charge d'Affaires Zhou Liang and others.

    On the evening of the 16th, a joint celebration was held in Beijing with people from China, Vietnam, India, Burma, Pakistan, Afghanistan, and Cambodia participating. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Pakistan Ahmed, Ambassador of India R.K. Nehru, Ambassador of Afghanistan Samad, Charge d'Affaires of Burma U Ba Maung, Head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation Yang An, and others attended the reception. (Attached picture)

    Premier Zhou waving goodbye to well-wishers before leaving Beijing.

### 应越南、柬埔寨、印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、尼泊尔、阿富汗政府的邀请  周恩来总理离京出国进行友好访问

1956-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    应越南、柬埔寨、印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、尼泊尔、阿富汗政府的邀请
    周恩来总理离京出国进行友好访问
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国外交部17日发表新闻公报如下：
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，应越南民主共和国政府、柬埔寨王国政府、印度共和国政府、缅甸联邦政府、巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国政府、尼泊尔王国政府和阿富汗王国政府的邀请，将从11月下半月起到越南民主共和国、柬埔寨王国、印度共和国、缅甸联邦、巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国、尼泊尔王国和阿富汗王国作友好访问。
    新华社17日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天上午八时四十分乘专机离开北京，应邀前往越南民主共和国、柬埔寨王国、印度共和国、缅甸联邦、巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国、尼泊尔王国和阿富汗王国进行友好访问。
    随同前往访问的有国务院副总理贺龙，外交部部长助理乔冠华，总理办公室副主任张彦，外交部新闻司司长龚澎和其他随行人员。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢和柬埔寨经济代表团团长杨安同机离开北京。
    到飞机场送行的，有国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、乌兰夫，秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、李维汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞、章汉夫等。
    到机场送行的各国外交官员有：巴基斯坦大使阿哈默德，阿富汗大使萨马德，印度尼西亚大使维约普拉诺托，埃及大使哈桑·拉加卜，缅甸临时代办吴巴茂，印度临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格，越南民主共和国临时代办周亮等人。
    16日晚间，在北京举行了有中国、越南、印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、阿富汗、柬埔寨等七个国家的人士参加的联欢酒会。周恩来总理、贺龙副总理，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，巴基斯坦大使阿哈默德，印度大使拉·库·尼赫鲁，阿富汗大使萨马德，缅甸临时代办吴巴茂，柬埔寨经济代表团团长杨安等出席了酒会。（附图片）
    周总理离京前向欢送者挥手告别。
