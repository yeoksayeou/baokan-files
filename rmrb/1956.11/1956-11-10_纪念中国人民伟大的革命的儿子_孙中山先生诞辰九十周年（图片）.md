Author: Lin Li Wu Erxiang Zhang Huchen Yu Chuangshuo Lu Yongzhen
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 8

In Memory of the Great Revolutionary Son of the Chinese People
—The Ninetieth Anniversary of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen
Mr. Sun Yat-sen's office in Guangzhou.
In 1905, Mr. Sun Yat-sen with members of the Tongmenghui in Japan. The seventh person from the left in the second row is Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
A photo of Mr. Sun Yat-sen taking office as the Provisional President in Nanjing on January 1, 1912. The fifth person from the left in the front row is Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
Mr. Sun Yat-sen at the Whampoa Military Academy in Guangzhou in 1924.
In 1924, Mr. Sun Yat-sen and his wife, Madam Soong Ching Ling, on a ship on their way north to Tianjin.
Mr. Sun Yat-sen accepted the advice of the Chinese Communist Party and adopted the three major revolutionary policies of alliance with Russia, cooperation with the Communist Party, and assistance to farmers and workers. In January 1924, the First National Congress of the Chinese Kuomintang was held in Guangzhou. The picture shows Mr. Sun Yat-sen addressing the meeting.
A photo of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and Mr. Liao Zhongkai.
Sun Yat-sen personally supervised the campaign to suppress the rebellion of the warlords of Guangdong and Guangxi. The picture shows Sun Yat-sen (first person on the right) personally directing operations on the front line in Huizhou, Dongjiang when Chen Jiongming betrayed the revolution.
Mr. Sun Yat-sen (second person on the right) at a mass rally in Guangzhou in 1924 to mourn Lenin.
Mr. Sun Yat-sen hosting a banquet for Soviet friends.
When the first airplane was manufactured in our country, Mr. Sun Yat-sen and his wife, Madam Soong Ching Ling, attended the test flight ceremony together. The plane was named "Soong Ching Ling." The picture shows Mr. Sun Yat-sen and his wife, Madam Soong Ching Ling, posing in front of the plane.
Cuiheng Village in Zhongshan County, Guangdong Province, is the hometown of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. This is Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Cuiheng Village.
Our reporters Lin Li and Wu Erxiang took the photos.
Mr. Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Shanghai. Photo by Zhang Huchen (News Daily)
The study in Mr. Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Shanghai. The pens, inkstones, and stationery on the desk are arranged as they were when Mr. Sun Yat-sen was alive. Photos by Yu Chuangshuo and Lu Yongzhen

### 纪念中国人民伟大的革命的儿子——孙中山先生诞辰九十周年（图片）
林里　吴尔祥  章虎臣  俞创硕　陆永桢
1956-11-10
第8版()
专栏：

    纪念中国人民伟大的革命的儿子
    ——孙中山先生诞辰九十周年
    孙中山先生在广州的办公室里。
    1905年，孙中山先生在日本与同盟会会员合影。第二排左起第七人是孙中山先生。
    1912年1月1日孙中山先生在南京就任临时大总统时留影。前排左起第五人是孙中山先生。
    1924年，孙中山先生在广州黄埔军官学校。
    1924年，孙中山先生北上到天津的时候，同夫人宋庆龄先生在船上合影。
    孙中山先生接受中国共产党的建议，采取联俄、联共、扶助农工的革命三大政策。1924年1月，在广州召开了中国国民党第一次全国代表大会。图为孙中山先生在会上致词。
    孙中山先生和廖仲恺先生的合影。
    孙中山亲自督师讨伐粤桂军阀的叛乱。图为陈烱明叛变革命的时候，孙中山（右第一人）亲临东江惠州前线指挥作战。
    1924年，孙中山先生（右第二人）在广州追悼列宁的群众大会上。
    孙中山先生宴请苏联友人。
    在我国修造出第一架飞机时，孙中山先生同夫人宋庆龄先生一起参加了试航典礼。这架飞机被命名为“宋庆龄号”。图为孙中山先生和夫人宋庆龄先生在飞机前合影。
    广东省中山县翠亨村是孙中山先生的故乡。这是翠亨村中山故居。
    本报记者　林里　吴尔祥摄
    孙中山先生在上海的故居。  章虎臣摄（新闻日报社稿）
    孙中山先生上海故居的书室。书桌上的笔砚文具，都照先生生前办公时的样子摆设着。　　　　　　俞创硕　陆永桢摄
