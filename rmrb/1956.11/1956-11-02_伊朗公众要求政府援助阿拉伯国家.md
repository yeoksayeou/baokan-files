Title: Iranian Public Demands Government Aid to Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, October 31st (TASS): Iranian newspapers have published news of the Israeli invasion of Egypt on the front page. "People's Echo" pointed out that the Iranian public expressed indignation at the Israeli invasion of Egypt and demanded that the government and parliament pass emergency resolutions to aid Arab countries and stop Israeli aggression.

### 伊朗公众要求政府援助阿拉伯国家

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    伊朗公众要求政府援助阿拉伯国家
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社德黑兰10月31日讯：伊朗报纸在第一栏刊载了以色列侵犯埃及的消息。“人民回声报”指出，伊朗公众对以色列侵犯埃及这件事表示愤慨，并且要求政府以及议会通过援助阿拉伯国家和制止以色列侵略的紧急决议。
