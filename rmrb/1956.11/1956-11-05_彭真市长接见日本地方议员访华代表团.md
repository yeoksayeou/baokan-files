Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Receives Japanese Local Assembly Members Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen received the Japanese Local Assembly Members Delegation to China, led by the delegation head, Fumio Kawabata, and all members of the delegation, this evening.

    Present at the meeting were Vice Mayors Zhang Youyu and Le Songsheng, and Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xiao Fangzhou, among others.

### 彭真市长接见日本地方议员访华代表团

1956-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    彭真市长接见日本地方议员访华代表团
    新华社4日讯　北京市市长彭真今天晚上接见了日本地方议员访华代表团团长川端文夫和代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有副市长张友渔、乐松生和中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长萧方洲等人。
