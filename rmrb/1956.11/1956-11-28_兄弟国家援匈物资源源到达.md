Title: Brotherly Countries' Aid to Hungary Arrives in a Steady Stream
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 5
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 27th: TASS reports: It has been officially announced here that, up to November 24th, 2,290 train cars of food, provided as free aid by socialist countries to the Hungarian People's Republic, had arrived in Hungary. This included 336 train cars of sugar, 47 train cars of canned goods, 671 train cars of flour, 254 train cars of pork and beef, 226 train cars of edible salt, 358 train cars of wheat, 82 train cars of butter, 25 train cars of rice, 134 train cars of potatoes, and 40 train cars of cooking oil.

### 兄弟国家援匈物资源源到达

1956-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家援匈物资源源到达
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：这里正式宣布，到11月24日为止，社会主义国家对匈牙利人民共和国提供的无偿援助的食物，已经有二千二百九十车皮运到了匈牙利。其中包括三百三十六车皮砂糖、四十七车皮罐头、六百七十一车皮面粉、二百五十四车皮猪肉和牛肉、二百二十六车皮食盐、三百五十八车皮小麦、八十二车皮黄油、二十五车皮大米、一百三十四车皮马铃薯和四十车皮油脂。
