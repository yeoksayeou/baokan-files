Title: Materials on Sun Yat-sen's Life and Deeds: New Discoveries in Beijing Recently
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Recently, a batch of valuable materials related to the life and deeds of Mr. Sun Yat-sen have been discovered. Among them are photographs of the list of alternate members of the Central Executive Committee, including Comrades Mao Zedong, Lin Boqu, and Qu Qiubai, elected at the First National Congress of the Kuomintang in 1924, which were made from Mr. Sun Yat-sen's calligraphy; there is a photograph of the elegiac couplet "Friend and Teacher of the Nation," written by Mr. Sun Yat-sen in 1924 to mourn Lenin, complete with upper and lower inscriptions; there is a photograph of the original telegram sent by Mr. Sun Yat-sen to Lenin in 1918 to congratulate the victory of the October Socialist Revolution; there are also photographs of the original site of the revolutionary organization during the first Guangzhou Uprising in 1895 and propaganda materials and books and periodicals related to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary work.

The collector of these materials is Wang Gengxiong, a student at the Central Academy of Arts and Crafts. Wang Gengxiong has been collecting materials related to Mr. Sun Yat-sen since he was fifteen years old—from 1937 to the present, a period of nineteen years.

### 有关中山先生生平事迹资料  北京最近有新发现

1956-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    有关中山先生生平事迹资料
    北京最近有新发现
    新华社3日讯　新近又发现了一批有关孙中山先生生平事迹的珍贵资料。其中有1924年国民党第一次全国代表大会上选出的包括毛泽东、林伯渠、瞿秋白等同志在内的中央执行委员候补人名单的照片，它是由孙中山先生的墨迹摄制而成的；有1924年孙中山先生为追悼列宁而亲笔写的带有上下款的挽额——“国友人师”的照片；有1918年孙中山先生为祝贺十月社会主义革命胜利拍给列宁的电报原文的照片；还有1895年第一次广州起义时革命机关原址的照片和孙中山先生从事革命工作的宣传品、书刊等资料。
    这批资料的收藏者，是中央工艺美术学院学生王耿雄。王耿雄从他十五岁——1937年起到现在十九年间，一直都在留心搜集有关孙中山先生的资料。
