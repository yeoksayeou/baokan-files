Title: In Rafah, Gaza Strip, Israel Massacres Arab Civilians Dying of Hunger
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: According to an Associated Press dispatch from Tel Aviv on the 16th: Israeli occupation forces in Rafah, in the Egyptian Gaza Strip, used force to suppress Arab people demonstrating for food. The report stated that Israeli troops may have shot and killed fifty Arabs and wounded at least twenty-five.

It is reported that the United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East has protested to the Israeli military governor of the Gaza Strip, Gaon.

### 在加沙地区的拉法镇  以色列屠杀阿拉伯饥民

1956-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    在加沙地区的拉法镇
    以色列屠杀阿拉伯饥民
    据新华社17日讯　据美联社特拉维夫16日消息：以色列占领军曾经在埃及加沙地区的拉法镇，使用武力镇压为要求粮食而示威的阿拉伯人。消息说，以色列军队开枪打死的阿拉伯人可能有五十人，最少打伤二十五人。
    据报道，联合国近东巴基斯坦难民救济及就业局曾向加沙区以色列军事总督加昂提出抗议。
