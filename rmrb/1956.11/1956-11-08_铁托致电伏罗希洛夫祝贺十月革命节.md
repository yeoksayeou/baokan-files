Title: Tito Sends Congratulations to Voroshilov on the Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th. According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, Yugoslav President Tito sent a telegram to Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, on November 6th, congratulating him on the thirty-ninth anniversary of the October Revolution.

The telegram said: "On the occasion of celebrating the thirty-ninth anniversary of the great October Revolution, please allow me, on behalf of the people of all nationalities of Yugoslavia and in my own name, to extend my heartfelt congratulations and respect to you and the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, and to wish you further achievements in building your socialist country, strengthening the friendly relations between our two countries, and strengthening cooperation with peace-loving people all over the world."

### 铁托致电伏罗希洛夫祝贺十月革命节

1956-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托致电伏罗希洛夫祝贺十月革命节
    新华社7日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社消息，南斯拉夫总统铁托6日致电苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，祝贺十月革命三十九周年。
    贺电说：在庆祝伟大的十月革命三十九周年的时候，请允许我代表南斯拉夫各族人民并且以我个人的名义，向你和苏联各族人民致以衷心的祝贺和敬意，并且祝贺你们在建设你们的社会主义国家、加强我们两国的友好关系和加强同全世界各国爱好和平的人民的合作各方面取得进一步的成就。
