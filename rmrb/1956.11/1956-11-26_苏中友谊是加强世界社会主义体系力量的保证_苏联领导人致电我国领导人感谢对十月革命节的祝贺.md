Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship is a Guarantee for Strengthening the Power of the World Socialist System. Soviet Leaders Send a Telegram to Our Country's Leaders Thanking Them for Their Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th -- Voroshilov, Bulganin, and Shepilov sent a telegram to Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai on the 24th, thanking them for their congratulations on the anniversary of the October Revolution. The telegram reads as follows:
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China;
To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress;
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
On the occasion of the thirty-ninth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, in accepting your congratulations and friendly wishes, we, on behalf of the Soviet people and the Soviet government, and in our own names, express our heartfelt gratitude to you, dear comrades, and through you, to the Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China.
The Soviet people are sincerely delighted with the great achievements made by the fraternal Chinese people, who are bravely and confidently advancing along the road of building socialism.
The Soviet people believe that the hard-working and talented Chinese people, closely united in the people's democratic united front, will achieve new and outstanding achievements in all aspects of socialist construction and will build China into a powerful socialist industrial country.
A fraternal and unbreakable friendship has been established between the peoples of our two countries. This friendship is a guarantee for further strengthening the power of the world socialist system and a guarantee for lasting world peace. We firmly believe that the friendly and close cooperative relations existing between our two countries will continue to develop and consolidate in the future for the benefit of the peoples of both countries and for the consolidation of world peace.
  K. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
  N. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR
  D. Shepilov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR
 November 24, 1956
 At the Moscow Kremlin

### 苏中友谊是加强世界社会主义体系力量的保证  苏联领导人致电我国领导人感谢对十月革命节的祝贺

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏中友谊是加强世界社会主义体系力量的保证
    苏联领导人致电我国领导人感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
    新华社25日讯　伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁和谢皮洛夫24日致电毛泽东、刘少奇和周恩来，感谢他们对十月革命节的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志：中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年的时候，承致祝贺和友好的愿望，仅代表苏联人民、苏联政府，并以我们自己的名义向你们，亲爱的同志，并通过你们向中国人民和中华人民共和国政府表示衷心的感谢。
    苏联人们对于勇往直前地、满怀信心地沿着建设社会主义的道路向前迈进的兄弟般的中国人民所取得的伟大成就感到衷心的欣喜。
    苏联人民相信，紧密地团结在人民民主统一战线中的勤劳而富有天才的中国人民，将在社会主义建设的一切方面取得新的卓越的成就，并将把中国建设成为一个强大的社会主义工业国。
    我们两国人民之间已经建立起兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊。这一友谊是进一步加强世界社会主义体系力量的保证，也是世界持久和平的保证。我们坚信，我们两国之间所存在的友好和紧密合作的关系，今后仍将为两国人民的利益和巩固世界和平的利益而继续发展和巩固。
  苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席　伏罗希洛夫
            苏联部长会议主席　布尔加宁
                苏联外交部长　谢皮洛夫
                            1956年11月24日
                        于莫斯科克里姆林宫
