Title: In order to complete the first year's task of the Sixth Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, the Soviet Komsomol calls for the implementation of All-Union Labor Work Shifts
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 21st (TASS): The Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union announced that it supports the proposals made by many Komsomol organizations and calls on Soviet youth to start implementing All-Union Labor Work Shifts at various work posts to strive to complete the first year's task of the Sixth Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

The Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol issued this call in its open letter to all Komsomol members and youth of the Soviet Union on winning the Order of Lenin. The letter stated that completing and overfulfilling the tasks of the Sixth Five-Year Plan is your best contribution to the motherland and the best answer to the highest award.

The open letter condemned the Anglo-French-Israeli aggression against Egypt and called on youth to strengthen the eternal and unbreakable friendship with the youth of great China and the people's democratic countries, and with the peace-loving youth of all countries.

The letter concluded: We must constantly encourage and motivate ourselves and each other to always be the reliable, fighting, and infinitely loyal assistants of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

### 为了提前完成第六个五年计划第一年任务  苏联共青团号召实行全苏劳动工作班

1956-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    为了提前完成第六个五年计划第一年任务
    苏联共青团号召实行全苏劳动工作班
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科21日讯：苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会宣布，它支持许多共青团组织提出的建议，号召苏联青年在各种工作岗位上开始实行争取提前完成第六个五年计划第一年任务的全苏劳动工作班。
    苏联共青团中央在它就荣获列宁勋章致苏联全体共青团员和青年的公开信中发出这一号召。信中说，完成和超额完成第六个五年计划的任务就是你们给祖国的最好贡献，就是对最高奖励的最好回答。
    公开信谴责英、法、以侵略埃及的行动，号召青年加强同伟大中国和各人民民主国家的青年、同一切国家爱好和平的青年之间永恒的、牢不可破的友谊。
    信中最后说：我们要一次再一次地自励和互勉，永远成为苏联共产党的可靠的、战斗的、无限忠诚的助手！
