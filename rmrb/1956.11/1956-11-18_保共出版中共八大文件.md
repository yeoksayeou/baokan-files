Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Publishes Documents from the Eighth National Congress of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 17th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reported on the 16th that the Bulgarian Communist Party Publishing Bureau recently published a large number of separate editions of Mao Zedong's opening address at the Eighth National Congress of the Chinese Communist Party and Liu Shaoqi's political report on behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party at the Eighth National Congress of the CPC.
