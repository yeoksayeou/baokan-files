Title: Egyptian Government Issues Statement: Strict Vigilance Against Britain and France Resuming War
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 29th: The Egyptian government broadcast a statement on Cairo Radio on the 28th. The statement reminded world opinion to "beware of the danger of renewed hostilities in Egypt due to the refusal of Britain, France and Israel to withdraw from Egyptian territory."

The statement said that Egypt "will not sacrifice its independence to maintain world peace." The Egyptian government will hold "the British government solely responsible" for "the unfortunate consequences of the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt, for the continuing crimes in Port Said and the Sinai Peninsula, and for the occupying forces' failure to withdraw from Egyptian territory."

The statement then elaborated on how Britain helped Israel invade Egypt and how Britain perfidiously launched an attack on Egypt.

The statement also pointed out that after the ceasefire, "in Port Said, Gaza, Rafah, El Arish, and Khan Yunis, (British and French troops) committed atrocities and persecutions against civilians."

### 埃及政府发表声明  严防英法重新挑起战争

1956-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及政府发表声明
    严防英法重新挑起战争
    新华社29日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府28日在开罗电台广播了一项声明。声明提醒全世界舆论，“谨防埃及境内由于英法以拒绝撤出埃及领土而重新发生敌对行动的危险”。
    声明说，埃及“不会为了维持世界和平而牺牲自己的独立”。“对于英法以侵略埃及，对于仍旧在塞得港和西奈半岛继续发生罪行，对于占领军未能从埃及领土撤退的不幸后果”，埃及政府将“唯英国政府是问”。
    声明接着详细地谈到了英国怎样帮助以色列侵略埃及以及英国如何背信弃义地向埃及发动进攻的情况。
    声明并且指出，停火以后，“在塞得港、加沙、拉法、埃尔阿里希和汉尤尼斯等地（英法军队）都对平民犯下了暴行，进行了迫害。”
