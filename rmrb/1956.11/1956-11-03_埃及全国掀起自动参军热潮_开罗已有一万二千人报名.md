Title: Egypt Sees Nationwide Surge in Voluntary Military Enlistment; 12,000 Register in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November. Cairo dispatch: A wave of voluntary enlistment is sweeping Cairo. The Egyptian National Liberation Army Command announced that by 12:30 Greenwich Mean Time on October 31st, approximately 10,000 men and 2,000 women had registered.

The Command announced that large-scale voluntary enlistment is continuing throughout Egypt.

The Command issued a communique calling on Egyptians to voluntarily enlist in the army to defend their homeland.

The Egyptian Red Crescent has appealed to the people to donate plasma to those soldiers who are defending Egyptian territory.

### 埃及全国掀起自动参军热潮  开罗已有一万二千人报名

1956-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及全国掀起自动参军热潮
    开罗已有一万二千人报名
    新华社2日讯　开罗消息：自动参军的热潮弥漫着开罗。埃及民族解放军司令部宣布，到10月31日格林威治时间十二点三十分为止，大约有一万名男子和二千名女子报了名。
    司令部宣布，大规模的自动参军正在埃及全国各地继续进行。
    司令部发表了公报，要求埃及人自动参军，保卫祖国。
    埃及红新月会已经向人民呼吁捐输血浆给那些正在保卫埃及国土的战士。
