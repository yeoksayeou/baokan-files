Title: U.S. Navy stirs up trouble, engages in extensive activities in the Mediterranean and Persian Gulf

Author:

Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 27th: U.S. naval authorities announced on the 23rd that the U.S. Navy's first missile-equipped ship, the "Boston," left Norfolk on the 23rd to join the U.S. Sixth Fleet in the Mediterranean.

The naval authorities also announced on the same day that the destroyer-tender "Prairie" had arrived in the Persian Gulf and would become the flagship of the U.S. Middle East Naval Force Commander.

### 美国海军兴波作浪  在地中海和波斯湾大肆活动

1956-11-28
第6版()
专栏：

    美国海军兴波作浪
    在地中海和波斯湾大肆活动
    新华社27日讯　华盛顿消息：美国海军当局23日宣布，美国海军的第一艘装有导弹的舰只“波士顿号”23日离开诺福克前去参加地中海的美国第六舰队。
    海军当局同一天还宣布，驱逐舰—补给舰“普拉利号”已经抵达波斯湾，这艘军舰将成为美国驻中东海军部队司令的旗舰。
