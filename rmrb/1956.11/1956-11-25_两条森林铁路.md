Title: Two Forest Railways
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 24th - Two forest highways are being constructed in the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Sichuan Province.

    One is the Ranghei Highway, from Xia Rang Kou (more than twenty miles west of Shua Jing Si) on the Cheng (Du) A (ba) Highway to Heishui, with a total length of one hundred and twenty kilometers. The other is the Ma Dan Highway, starting from Maerkang, the terminus of the Shua (Jing Si) Ma (Erkang) Highway, passing through Zhuosijia, Dajin, Xiaojin to Danba in the Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture; currently, the section from Maerkang to Songgang is being constructed.

    Currently, about six thousand laborers of various ethnic groups are braving the severe cold, building roads on the frozen ground in mountain valleys at an altitude of three thousand five hundred to four thousand meters above sea level.

    Heishui, Maerkang, Zhuosijia, Dajin, Xiaojin and other counties have very rich primeval forests.

### 两条森林铁路

1956-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

    两条森林铁路
    新华社成都24日电　四川省阿坝藏族自治州正在兴修两条森林公路。
    一条是壤黑公路，从成（都）阿（坝）公路上的下壤口（刷经寺西二十多里）到黑水，全长一百二十公里。一条是马丹公路，从刷
  （经寺）马（尔康）公路的终点马尔康起，经绰斯甲、大金、小金到甘孜藏族自治州的丹巴；目前修的是马尔康到松冈这一段。
    现在，约有六千名各族民工，正在拔海三千五百到四千公尺的山谷间，冒着严寒在冻地上筑路。
    黑水、马尔康、绰斯甲、大金、小金等县，都有非常丰富的原始森林。
