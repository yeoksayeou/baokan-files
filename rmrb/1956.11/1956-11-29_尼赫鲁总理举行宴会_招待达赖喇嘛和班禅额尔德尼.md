Title: Prime Minister Nehru Hosts Banquet in Honor of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1956-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 28th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru held a banquet on the evening of November 27th to honor the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni.

More than two hundred guests attended the banquet. The host and guests engaged in cordial conversation and listened to musical performances together.

The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni delivered their first public speeches after arriving in New Delhi at a reception held in their honor by the Indian Buddhist Association that afternoon. The Dalai Lama said, "We must not only strive to propagate faith in the Buddha, but also practice his teachings of compassion and non-violence, because only in this way can we achieve world peace and brotherhood." Panchen Erdeni said, "We should pray and strive to promote our religious culture, world peace, and better understanding among all countries in the world."

The reception was presided over by Indian Minister of Defense, Kailash Nath Katju. Buddhist delegations from various countries participating in the 2,500th anniversary of Sakyamuni Buddha's Nirvana were invited to attend the reception. The delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also presented the Indian Buddhist Association with a replica of the famous Hanoi Pagoda.

That morning, Prime Minister Nehru visited the Dalai Lama at Hyderabad House and then visited Panchen Erdeni at Kota House.

### 尼赫鲁总理举行宴会  招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼

1956-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理举行宴会
    招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    新华社新德里28日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在11月27日晚上举行宴会招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    两百多位客人出席了这次宴会。主人和客人们进行了亲切的交谈，并且在一起听了音乐演奏。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼当天下午在印度佛教协会为他们举行的招待会上发表了他们到达新德里以后的第一次公开演说。达赖喇嘛说：“我们不但必须努力宣扬信奉佛祖，而且还要实行他的慈悲和非暴力的教义，因为只有这样做才能实现全世界的和平和兄弟之谊。”班禅额尔德尼说：“我们应该祈祷和努力促进我们的宗教文化、世界和平和全世界各国之间的更好的谅解。”
    主持这次招待会的是印度国防部长凯·纳·卡特朱。参加释迦牟尼涅槃二千五百周年纪念的各国佛教代表团都应邀参加了招待会。越南民主共和国的代表团还向印度佛教协会赠送了河内著名佛塔的复制品。
    当天上午，尼赫鲁总理曾经到海得拉巴大厦访问了达赖喇嘛，随后前往科塔大厦访问了班禅额尔德尼。
