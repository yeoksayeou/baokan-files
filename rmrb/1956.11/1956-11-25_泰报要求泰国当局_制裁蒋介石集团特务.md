Title: Thai Newspaper Demands Thai Authorities Sanction Chiang Kai-shek Clique Secret Agents
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 6

    Thai Newspaper Demands Thai Authorities
    Sanction Chiang Kai-shek Clique Secret Agents
    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, November 24th: Many Thai newspapers are demanding that Thai authorities strictly stop the illegal activities of Chiang Kai-shek clique secret agents in Thailand.
      "Tu Hua News" published a commentary on November 19th saying that the time has come for Thailand to seriously and resolutely deal with the activities of Taiwanese Kuomintang secret agents who violate Thai law. Similarly, the time has also come for the Thai government to re-examine its diplomatic relations with the "Taiwan government."
    The "Liberty Communications" editorial on November 16th pointed out that the Chiang Kai-shek clique's establishment of secret organizations in Thailand is illegal, and the activities of this organization will create turmoil in Thailand in the days to come. The editorial reminded the Thai authorities to take the recent Kowloon riots as a warning.

### 泰报要求泰国当局  制裁蒋介石集团特务

1956-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    泰报要求泰国当局
    制裁蒋介石集团特务
    据新华社24日讯　曼谷消息：泰国许多报纸都要求泰国当局严厉制止蒋介石集团特务在泰国的非法活动。
      “图画新闻”11月19日发表的一篇评论说，现在已经到了泰国必须认真坚决对付违反泰国法律的台湾国民党特务的活动的时候了。同样，现在也到了泰国政府应当重新审查同“台湾政府”的外交关系的时候了。
    “自由通讯报”11月16日的社论指出，蒋介石集团在泰国建立秘密组织是违法的，这个组织的活动，将会在未来日子里在泰国制造出纷乱事件来。社论提醒泰国当局应以最近发生的九龙暴乱事件为戒。
