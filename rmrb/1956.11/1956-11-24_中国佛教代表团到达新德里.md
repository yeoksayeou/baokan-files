Title: Chinese Buddhist Delegation Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 23rd – The Chinese Buddhist Delegation, led by Master Xiraojiacuo, arrived in New Delhi on the evening of November 22nd after attending the Fourth World Buddhist Conference in Kathmandu. The delegation is here at the invitation of the Shakyamuni Nirvana Working Committee to participate in the 2,500th anniversary commemoration of Shakyamuni's Nirvana. This working committee is chaired by Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan.

The celebrations commemorating Shakyamuni's Nirvana include: a public gathering in New Delhi on November 24th, with a speech by Indian President Dr. Prasad; and a three-day Buddhist symposium from November 26th to 29th. Prominent Buddhists from twenty-five countries have accepted the working committee's invitation to attend the symposium. In addition to the Chinese Buddhist Delegation headed by Master Xiraojiacuo, the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini will also participate in the Shakyamuni Nirvana commemoration activities. Other prominent Buddhists who are on their way to attend the symposium include former Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Cambodian Prince Sihanouk, the Maharaja of Sikkim, Mongolia's Tuvshinhingiin Gombojab, the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Thich Tri Do, and the Soviet Union's Gabyishi Lama.

### 中国佛教代表团到达新德里

1956-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国佛教代表团到达新德里
    新华社新德里23日电　中国佛教代表团在团长喜饶嘉错大师的率领下在加德满都参加了第四届世界佛教徒大会以后，已经在11月22日晚间到达新德里。代表团这次是应释迦牟尼涅槃工作委员会的邀请到这里来参加释迦牟尼涅槃二千五百周年纪念的。这个工作委员会由印度副总统哈达克里希南博士担任主席。
    纪念释迦牟尼涅槃的节日包括：11月24日在新德里举行公共集会，由印度总统普拉萨德博士发表演说；从11月26日到29日举行三天的佛教座谈会。已经有二十五个国家的著名佛教徒接受了工作委员会的邀请来参加这次座谈会。除了以喜饶嘉错大师为首的中国佛教代表团之外，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼也将参加释迦牟尼涅槃的纪念活动。其他正在前来参加座谈会的著名佛教徒还有前缅甸总理吴努、柬埔寨的西哈努克亲王、锡金的马哈拉加、蒙古的图夫申兴·贡博杜、越南民主共和国的释智度和苏联的加比希喇嘛。
