Title: Pictures
Author: Zhang Yixue, Zhu Yu
Date: 1956-11-29
Page: 4

    The Third Sub-delegation of the Central Delegation arrived in Shua Jing Si (刷经寺), the location of the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee of Sichuan Province, on November 15th. The delegation members visited and expressed their regards to the Tibetan, Qiang, and Hui people in Shua Jing Si, and were warmly welcomed by the local people of all ethnic groups. The picture shows the Tibetan people welcoming members of the Central Delegation.
                                     Photo by Zhang Yixue (Xinhua News Agency Manuscript)
    The acrobatics team of the Third Sub-delegation of the Central Delegation performed in Shua Jing Si. This is a scene of acrobatic team members performing on a tightrope. Photo by Zhu Yu (Xinhua News Agency Manuscript)
