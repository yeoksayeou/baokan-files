Title: Soong Ching-ling Organizes Important Documents of Mr. Sun Yat-sen
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 3rd: Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling recently organized a collection of important documents of Mr. Sun Yat-sen in Shanghai that she has treasured for more than thirty years. She has sent these documents to the Preparatory Committee for the Ninetieth Anniversary of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen in Beijing.

Among the important documents sent to Beijing are the handwritten manuscript of "The Fundamentals of National Reconstruction" by Mr. Sun Yat-sen, a book of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's handwritten manuscripts totaling sixty pages, more than one hundred precious photographs of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary activities during his lifetime, and photographs of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and his wife together. Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling also organized a Chinese map drawn by Mr. Sun Yat-sen himself in Shanghai and the English manuscript of the "Industrial Plan" written in Shanghai.

While organizing the documents, Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling personally rearranged the furnishings in the former residence of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, keeping the living room, dining room, study, and bedroom in their original style and shape.

Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling also wrote inscriptions for the upcoming publication of "Selected Works of Sun Yat-sen" and Mr. Sun Yat-sen's posthumous writing "The Fundamentals of National Reconstruction."

### 宋庆龄整理出中山先生的重要文献

1956-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄整理出中山先生的重要文献
    新华社上海3日电　宋庆龄副委员长最近在上海整理出一批珍藏三十多年的孙中山先生的重要文献。她已把这些文献寄给在北京的孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会。
    在这批送往北京的重要文献中，有孙中山先生亲笔写的“建国大纲”手稿，孙中山先生的手札墨迹一本共六十页，孙中山先生生前革命活动珍贵照片一百多张，以及中山先生和夫人合拍的照片等。宋庆龄副委员长还整理出孙中山先生在上海亲手画的一张中国地图和在上海著作的“实业计划”的英文底稿。
    与整理文献的同时，宋庆龄副委员长亲自重新布置了中山故居里的陈设，使客厅、餐厅、书室、寝室保持着原来的风格和形状。
    宋庆龄副委员长还为即将出版的孙中山选集和孙中山先生的遗墨“建国大纲”题了词。
