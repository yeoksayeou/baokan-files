Title: Indian Children in Beijing Celebrate Children's Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Today is Children's Day in India, and also the sixty-seventh birthday of Indian Prime Minister Nehru. Indian children in Beijing invited children from Asian and African countries in Beijing to hold a gathering at the International Club this afternoon to celebrate this festival.

The Indian children in Beijing, thinking of the plight of the Egyptian children, presented some gifts to the Egyptian children at the meeting to express the Indian children's friendship and sympathy for the Egyptian children.

### 在京印度儿童欢度儿童节

1956-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    在京印度儿童欢度儿童节
    据新华社14日讯　今天是印度的儿童节，同时也是印度总理尼赫鲁的六十七岁生日。在北京的印度儿童，今天下午邀请在北京的亚洲和非洲国家的儿童在国际俱乐部举行集会，欢度这个节日。
    在北京的印度儿童想到埃及儿童的遭遇，在会上把一些礼物赠给了埃及儿童，以表示印度儿童对埃及儿童的友爱和同情。
