Title: The Soviet Union and Afghanistan Sign Communiqué on Prime Minister Daud's Visit to the Soviet Union, Reaffirming Both Sides' Desire for World Peace and Expressing Their Intention to Strengthen Friendly Cooperation

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st: According to TASS, the Soviet Union and Afghanistan signed a communiqué on October 30 regarding Afghan Prime Minister Daud's visit to the Soviet Union. Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Bulganin, and Afghan Prime Minister Daud, signed the communiqué respectively.

The communiqué stated that during the visit of the Afghan government leaders to the Soviet Union, they held talks with the Soviet government leaders in a fully friendly and harmonious atmosphere. After the talks, they reaffirmed the joint statement signed on December 18, 1955, by Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Bulganin, during his visit to Kabul. They noted with satisfaction that the implementation of the points in this statement has already brought benefits to the people of both countries.

The leaders of the Soviet Union and Afghanistan once again declared their hope to consolidate world peace and security based on mutual respect and trust between the people and countries of the world. They agreed that all international relations issues should be resolved through friendly consultation, and all disputes should be resolved peacefully on the basis of mutual respect and understanding.

In the spirit of respecting fundamental human rights and the right to self-determination of all nations and countries, the leaders of the Soviet Union and Afghanistan reaffirmed their determination to support the legitimate aspirations of all people and countries striving for political and economic independence.

The communiqué concluded by stating that the leaders of the Soviet Union and Afghanistan declared their sincerity in strengthening the friendly and sincere relations between the two countries and confirmed that the basis for assistance and cooperation should be expanded in improving the economic living standards of the people of both countries, in an atmosphere of goodwill and good relations between the two friendly neighbors, based on the principle of mutual respect.

After the signing ceremony, Bulganin held a grand reception to entertain Daud. Both the host and guest spoke at the reception.

### 苏联和阿富汗签署关于达乌德首相访苏公报  重申双方争取世界和平的愿望并表示要加强两国友好合作

1956-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联和阿富汗签署关于达乌德首相访苏公报
    重申双方争取世界和平的愿望并表示要加强两国友好合作
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和阿富汗10月30日签署了关于阿富汗首相达乌德访问苏联的公报。苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和阿富汗首相达乌德分别在公报上签了字。
    公报说，阿富汗王国政府领导人在访问苏联期间同苏联政府领导人在充分友好和融洽的气氛中进行了会谈。会谈之后，他们重申了1955年12月18日苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在访问喀布尔时所签署的联合声明。他们满意地认为，这一声明中的各点的实现已经给两国人民带来了好处。
    苏阿两国领导人再次声明，希望在世界各国人民和各个国家相互尊重和信任的基础上巩固世界和平和安宁。他们一致认为，所有国际关系问题都应当通过友好协商方式解决，各种争端都应当采取和平方法在相互尊重和谅解的基础上调解。
    苏阿两国领导人本着应该尊重基本人权和各个民族、各个国家的自决权的信念，再次表示决心支持为政治独立和经济独立而斗争的各国人民和各个国家的合理愿望。
    公报最后说，苏阿两国领导人声明有诚意加强两国友好真诚的关系，并且确认应该根据相互尊重的原则在两个友好邻国的善意和良好关系的气氛中，在提高两国人民经济生活水平方面扩大帮助和合作的基础。
    签字仪式结束后，布尔加宁举行盛大的招待会，招待达乌德，宾主双方都在招待会上讲了话。
