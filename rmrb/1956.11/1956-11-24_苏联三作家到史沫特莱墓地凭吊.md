Title: Three Soviet Writers Pay Respects at Smedley's Grave

Author:

Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Soviet writers, Polevoi, Galin, and Zalygin, who came to China to participate in the 20th Anniversary Commemoration of Lu Xun's Death, paid their respects at the grave of the American revolutionary female writer Agnes Smedley in the suburbs of Beijing this afternoon.

    Polevoi and others laid a wreath of flowers at Smedley's tomb. Two white silk ribbons were hung on the wreath, reading:

    "To a glorious colleague—Agnes Smedley" and "Respectfully offered by the delegation of Soviet Writers."

    Xiao San, a member of the Council of the Chinese Writers Association, and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, accompanied them.

### 苏联三作家到史沫特莱墓地凭吊

1956-11-24
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联三作家到史沫特莱墓地凭吊
    新华社20日讯　前来中国参加鲁迅逝世二十周年纪念活动的苏联作家波列沃依、加林、扎雷金，今天下午到在北京市郊的美国革命女作家史沫特莱的墓地凭吊。
    波列沃依等在史沫特莱的墓前献了鲜花篮，花篮上挂着的两条白绸带上写着：
    “光荣的同行——史沫特莱”和“苏联作家代表团敬挽”。
    陪同前往凭吊的有中国作家协会理事萧三和中苏友好协会总会理事会副秘书长戈宝权等。
