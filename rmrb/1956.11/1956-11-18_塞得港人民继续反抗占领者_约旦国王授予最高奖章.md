Title: Port Said People Continue to Resist Occupiers; Jordanian King Awards Highest Medal
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th, TASS, Cairo, November 16th: "Al-Gomhouria" writes that despite the terror and persecution tactics adopted by the British and French troops, the residents of Port Said continue to resist the British and French occupiers. Slogans denouncing the aggressors who infringe upon the freedom of the Egyptian people, as well as slogans calling on everyone to resist, appear daily on the walls of houses and on sidewalks.

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: According to Cairo Radio broadcast on November 16th, Jordanian King Hussein has awarded the highest Jordanian honor, the Order of al-Nahda, to the people of Port Said who valiantly resisted the British and French aggression.

### 塞得港人民继续反抗占领者  约旦国王授予最高奖章

1956-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    塞得港人民继续反抗占领者
    约旦国王授予最高奖章
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社开罗16日讯：“共和国报”写道，尽管英国和法国的军队采取恐怖和迫害的手段，塞得港的居民继续对英法占领者进行反抗。在房屋的墙壁上和人行道上每天都出现斥责侵犯埃及人民自由的侵略者的口号，以及号召大家进行抵抗的口号。
    新华社17日讯　据开罗电台11月16日广播，约旦国王侯赛尼已经把约旦的最高荣誉——纳赫达奖章授予英勇抵抗英法侵略的塞得港人民。
