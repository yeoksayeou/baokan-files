Title: All-China Youth Federation and CYL Central Committee Host Banquet to Welcome French Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The All-China Youth Federation and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a banquet this evening to welcome the French Youth Delegation.

    Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and Raymond Mischel, head of the French Youth Delegation, delivered speeches at the banquet. They both expressed the hope that the visit of the delegation would greatly strengthen and develop the friendship between the youths of the two countries.

    The delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon. The delegation consists of eighteen people, including twelve French youth organizations. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 全国青联和团中央设宴欢迎法青年代表团

1956-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联和团中央设宴欢迎法青年代表团
    新华社26日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎法国青年代表团。
    青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅和法国青年代表团团长雷蒙·米歇尔先后在宴会上致词，他们一致表示希望通过代表团的这次访问，将大大加强和发展两国青年间的友谊。
    代表团是在昨天下午到达北京的。代表团共十八人，包括法国十二个青年组织，他们是应全国青联邀请来中国进行友好访问的。
