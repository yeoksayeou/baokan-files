Title: Sudanese Volunteers Prepare to Go to Egypt; Afghan Prime Minister Says He Will Give Egypt Necessary Aid
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th, dispatch from Cairo by TASS: Egypt’s "Republic Newspaper" published news from Khartoum stating that the Sudanese Trade Union Federation organized a team of one thousand people, preparing to participate in the Egyptian people’s struggle against the British, French, and Israeli aggression.

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th, dispatch from Kabul by TASS: Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud said in a speech on Kabul Radio on the evening of the 13th that the Afghan government is making every effort to stop the aggression in Egypt. He said that in order to realize the people's wishes, the government will give Egypt the necessary aid.

The Prime Minister pointed out that the perfidious attack on Egypt has aroused great anger throughout the country. Considering the mood of the people, the Afghan government has informed the Egyptian people, the United Nations, and the world that the Afghan people sympathize with the Egyptian people.

The Prime Minister also said that groups and residents from all over the country are constantly sending many applications, suggesting aid to Egypt.

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th, dispatch from Cairo by TASS: According to the Middle East News Agency: The Sudanese government passed a resolution deciding to donate 100,000 head of livestock to Egypt to help Egypt recover from the losses caused by imperialist aggression.

### 苏丹志愿人员准备赴埃及  阿富汗首相表示将给埃及必需的援助

1956-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹志愿人员准备赴埃及
    阿富汗首相表示将给埃及必需的援助
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社开罗14日讯：埃及“共和国报”刊载了从喀土穆来的消息说，苏丹工会联合会组织了一千人的队伍，准备参加埃及人民反对英、法和以色列侵略的斗争。
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔14日讯：阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德13日晚上在喀布尔广播电台发表演说时说，阿富汗政府正竭力促使停止在埃及的侵略。他说，为了实现人民的愿望，政府将给埃及必需的援助。
    首相指出，对埃及的背信弃义的进攻在全国各地激起了极大的愤怒。阿富汗政府考虑到人民的情绪，已经告知埃及人民、联合国和全世界：阿富汗人民是同情埃及人民的。
    首相还说，全国各地团体和居民不断送来许多申请书，建议援助埃及。
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社开罗15日讯：据中东通讯社消息：苏丹政府通过决议，决定赠给埃及十万头牲畜，帮助埃及恢复由于帝国主义侵略而受到的损失。
