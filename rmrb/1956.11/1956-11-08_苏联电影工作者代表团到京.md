Title: Soviet Filmmakers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Following the first group of the Soviet Filmmakers Delegation which arrived in Beijing on the 5th, the delegation member, Honored Artist of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, director of films such as "Donbass Miners," "Ordinary Soldier," and "Decline of the House of Artamonov," Rukov, and delegation member, film actor Panich, arrived in Beijing on the 6th. Delegation member, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, leading actor in films such as "An Unfinished Story" and "Othello," Bondarchuk, and delegation member, leading actress in films such as "This Must Not Be Forgotten" and "Silvery Dust," Ushakova, also arrived in Beijing on the 7th.
    As of now, all members of the Soviet Filmmakers Delegation have arrived in Beijing.

### 苏联电影工作者代表团到京

1956-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联电影工作者代表团到京
    新华社7日讯　继5日到达北京的苏联电影工作者代表团首批人员之后，代表团团员、乌兹别克人民共和国功勋艺术家、“顿巴斯矿工”、“普通一兵”和“没落之家”等影片的导演鲁柯夫和团员、电影演员潘尼奇在6日到达北京。团员、苏联人民演员、“没有说完的故事”、“奥赛罗”等影片的男主角邦达尔丘克和团员、“不能忘记这件事”、“银灰色的粉末”等影片的主要演员乌沙阔娃也在7日到达北京。
    至此，苏联电影工作者代表团全体人员都已到京。
