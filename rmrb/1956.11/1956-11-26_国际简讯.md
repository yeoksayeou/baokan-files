International Briefs

    Uruguay Decides to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Bulgaria
    According to a report in Montevideo's "El Día," the Uruguayan government has accepted the proposal from the government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria regarding the mutual exchange of diplomatic representatives.

    Vietnam-Bulgaria Diplomatic Representations Upgraded to Ambassadorial Level
    The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced on the 25th that, according to the recent decision of the governments of Vietnam and Bulgaria, the diplomatic representative of Vietnam in Sofia and the diplomatic representative of Bulgaria in Hanoi will be upgraded to the ambassadorial level.

    Albania and Yugoslavia Sign Aviation Agreement
    According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, Albania and Yugoslavia signed an agreement in Tirana on November 23rd for the establishment of air traffic between the two countries.

    Czechoslovakia and Sweden Sign Trade Protocol
    A protocol agreed upon by Czechoslovakia and Sweden concerning the adjustment of trade relations from November 1956 to October 1957 was signed in Prague on November 22nd.

    According to a list of goods for further increased trade volume, Czechoslovakia will import from Sweden machinery and equipment for the paper and cellulose industries, various machines and instruments, ball bearings, chemical industry products, cellulose, paper, leather, iron ore, cooking oil, etc.

    Sweden will import from Czechoslovakia various machines, especially machine tools, instruments, vehicles, tractors, textiles, rolled products, glass, hops, malt, etc.

    Soviet Expert Committee Heads to Cambodia
    According to an agreement reached in Moscow between the Soviet government and Cambodian Prince Sihanouk, a Soviet expert committee has departed from Moscow for Cambodia. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1956-11-26
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    乌拉圭决定与保加利亚建交
    据蒙得维的亚“晨报”报道，乌拉圭政府接受了保加利亚人民共和国政府关于两国互换外交代表的建议。
    越保外交代表升格为大使级
    越南民主共和国外交部25日宣布，根据越南和保加利亚两国政府最近的决定，越南驻索非亚的外交代表和保加利亚驻河内的外交代表将升格为大使级。
    阿南两国签订航空协定
    据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫11月23日在地拉那签订了阿南两国建立航空交通的协定。
    捷瑞签订换货议定书
    捷克斯洛伐克和瑞典两国商定的1956年11月到1957年10月调整换货关系的议定书，11月22日在布拉格签字。
    根据进一步增加换货数量的货物清单，捷克斯洛伐克将从瑞典进口造纸工业和纤维素工业的机器和设备、各种机器和仪器、滚珠轴承、化学工业产品、纤维素、纸张、皮革、铁矿砂、食油等。
    瑞典将从捷克斯洛伐克进口各种机器，特别是工作母机、仪器、车辆、拖拉机、纺织品、压延品、玻璃、啤酒酒花、麦芽等。
    苏联专家委员会前往柬埔寨
    根据苏联政府和柬埔寨西哈努克亲王在莫斯科达成的协议，一个苏联专家委员会已从莫斯科动身前往柬埔寨。　（新华社）
