Title: Britain Wants Western European Countries to Rely on the United States for Oil Supply; Switzerland Restricts Oil Consumption
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 17th: British Chancellor of the Exchequer Macmillan said on the 15th that the European oil supply crisis caused by the Middle East events can only be resolved by relying on the Organization for European Economic Cooperation, which is composed of seventeen countries.

Macmillan is in Paris attending a meeting of the Organization for European Economic Cooperation.

It is said that the executive committee of the Organization for European Economic Cooperation has submitted a request to the United States for oil supply, and a special American agency is considering "how to meet" this request.

Xinhua News Agency, Bern, 17th: The Swiss government issued a decree on the 16th stipulating that, starting from November 18th, the use of any motor vehicles on holidays is prohibited in order to save gasoline.

### 英国要西欧国家靠美国供应石油  瑞士限制石油消费

1956-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    英国要西欧国家靠美国供应石油
    瑞士限制石油消费
    新华社17日讯　巴黎消息：英国财政大臣麦克米伦15日说，因为中东事件而引起的欧洲石油供应危机，只有依靠由十七个国家组成的欧洲经济合作局机构去设法解决。
    麦克米伦正在巴黎参加欧洲经济合作局的会议。
    据说，这个欧洲经济合作局的执行委员会已经向美国提出供应石油的要求，美国的一个专门机构正在考虑“怎样来满足”这个要求。
    新华社17日讯　伯尔尼消息：瑞士政府在16日颁布了一项法令，规定从11月18日开始，禁止在假日使用任何机动车辆，以节省汽油。
