Title: Various Circles in Vietnam Enthusiastically Prepare to Welcome Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th -- Vietnam is enthusiastically preparing to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai, who will arrive in Vietnam tomorrow for a visit.

Today, Vietnam's "People's Daily" and "Hanoi Daily" both published biographies of Premier Zhou Enlai. The "People's Daily" also published a large photograph of Premier Zhou Enlai and excerpts from a New China impression written by Hoang Dao Thuy, a literary and artistic worker of the Vietnamese People's Army.

The "Xin Yue Hua Bao" also published a speech by Bui Ky, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Vietnam.

Workers and students in Hanoi are preparing to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's visit. Workers at the Gia Lam train repair factory are busy writing slogans and building decorative arches. Workers at the "Thong Nhat" match factory, newly built with Chinese assistance this year, have decided to launch a short-term labor competition to express their welcome.

### 越南各界  热烈准备欢迎周总理

1956-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    越南各界
    热烈准备欢迎周总理
    新华社河内17日电　越南热烈准备欢迎将在明天到达越南访问的周恩来总理。
    越南“人民报”、“河内日报”今天都登载了周恩来总理的传记。“人民报”还刊出周恩来总理的大幅照片和越南人民军文艺工作者黄道翠所写的访问新中国印象记片段。
    “新越华报”也刊登了一篇越中友好协会会长裴纪发表的欢迎周恩来总理访问越南的谈话。
    河内的工人、学生正在筹备欢迎周恩来总理前来访问。嘉林火车修理厂的工人忙着写标语、搭彩坊。今年在中国帮助下新建的“统一”火柴厂的工人决定发动短期劳动竞赛来表示欢迎。
