Title: Japanese Fukuoka University Students Hold Demonstration Protesting U.S. Deployment of Aircraft Capable of Carrying Atomic Bombs
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 15th: Approximately one thousand students from five universities in Fukuoka, Kyushu, held a rally in Fukuoka City on the afternoon of the 14th to oppose the stationing of six U.S. aircraft capable of carrying atomic bombs at the nearby Itazuke Air Base.

    After passing a resolution, the students held a demonstration march in front of the U.S. Consulate in Fukuoka City.

    The first batch of six U.S. F-100 Super Sabre jet fighters arrived at Itazuke Air Base at 1:00 PM on the 14th. They are the first new type of fighter aircraft to be equipped by the U.S. Far East Air Force. On the 13th, approximately two hundred Japanese university students already held a rally to oppose this U.S. measure.

### 日本福冈大学生举行示威游行  抗议美国派驻能装原子弹的飞机

1956-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    日本福冈大学生举行示威游行
    抗议美国派驻能装原子弹的飞机
    新华社15日讯　东京消息：九州岛福冈的五个大学的学生约一千人14日下午在福冈市举行大会，反对美国的六架能够装载原子炸弹的飞机驻在附近的板付空军基地。
    在通过了决议以后，学生们到福冈市的美国领事馆门前举行了示威游行。
    美国第一批六架F一○○超佩刀式喷气式战斗机是在14日下午一点到达板付空军基地的。它们是第一次配备在美国远东空军的新式战斗机。13日已经有大约二百个日本大学生举行大会，反对美国的这种措施。
