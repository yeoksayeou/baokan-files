Title: Leaders of Fourteen National People's Organizations Hold Joint Meeting, Decide to Establish China-Egypt Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The leaders of fourteen national people's organizations in China held a joint meeting in Beijing this afternoon. The meeting decided to establish the China-Egypt Friendship Association and approved Bao Erhan as the President and Da Pusheng as the Vice President.

The fourteen sponsoring people's organizations are: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, China Islamic Association, China Peace Committee, Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, All-China Youth Federation, Red Cross Society of China, Chinese Medical Association, All-China Sports Federation, China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and China Political Science and Law Association.

Today's joint meeting was presided over by Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The charter of the China-Egypt Friendship Association, adopted at the meeting, states that the purpose of the China-Egypt Friendship Association is to promote cultural exchange between China and Egypt, enhance mutual understanding between the peoples of China and Egypt, and develop the fraternal friendship between the two countries. The main task of the China-Egypt Friendship Association at present is to support the just struggle of the Egyptian people for independence and sovereignty.

The list of candidates for President, Vice President, Directors, and Secretary-General of the China-Egypt Friendship Association, unanimously approved by the joint meeting, is as follows:

President: Bao Erhan, Vice President: Da Pusheng

Directors: Wang Manshuo, Wang Shouren, Wang Xixiong, Zohara, Imin Mahsum, Zhu Dan, He Gongkai, Li Shu, Li Chao, Shen Kefei, Xiao Fangzhou, Chen Baichen, Hu Lansheng, Hu Zhongchi, Xia Nai, Ma Hanbing, Ma Jian, Mei Ru'ao, Ling Qihan, Cao Mengjun, Huang Zhong, Zeng Zhaolun, Zhang Zhirang, Zhang Changcheng, Tong Baoling, Yang Jingren, Yang Yinliu, Yang Huilin, Dong Xin, Zhao Bingnan, Zhao Wangyun, Jian Bozan, Saifuddin, Gu Yizhi.

Secretary-General: Yang Huilin

The directors of the China-Egypt Friendship Association held their first meeting this afternoon, and the meeting decided to hold the inaugural meeting of the China-Egypt Friendship Association on the 10th.

### 我国十四个人民团体负责人举行联席会议  决定成立中国埃及友好协会

1956-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    我国十四个人民团体负责人举行联席会议
    决定成立中国埃及友好协会
    新华社8日讯　中国十四个人民团体的负责人今天下午在北京举行联席会议，会议决定成立中国埃及友好协会，并通过包尔汉为会长、达浦生为副会长。
    十四个发起的人民团体是：中国人民对外文化协会、中国伊斯兰教协会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民外交学会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中国红十字会总会、中华医学会、中华全国体育总会、中国国际贸易促进委员会和中国政治法律学会。
    今天的联席会议由中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙主持。会议通过的中国埃及友好协会章程说，中国埃及友好协会的宗旨是，促进中埃两国文化交流，增进中埃两国人民的相互了解，发展中埃两国兄弟般的友好关系。当前中埃友好协会的主要工作是支援埃及人民争取独立和主权的正义的斗争。
    联席会议一致通过的中国埃及友好协会会长、副会长、理事和总干事人选名单如下：
    会长：包尔汉、副会长：达浦生
    理事：王曼硕、王守仁、王西雄、左哈拉、伊明·马合苏木、朱丹、何功楷、李恕、李超、沈克非、肖方洲、陈白尘、胡兰生、胡仲持、夏鼐、马寒冰、马坚、梅汝璈、凌其翰、曹孟君、黄中、曾昭抡、张志让、张长城、童葆苓、杨静仁、杨阴浏、杨慧琳、董昕、赵炳南、赵望云、翦伯赞、赛福鼎、顾逸之。
    总干事：杨慧琳
    中国埃及友好协会的理事们在今天下午举行了第一次会议，会议决定在10日举行中埃友好协会成立大会。
