Title: All-China Women's Federation and Others Send Telegram to Egyptian Women
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Chinese National Committee for the Defence of Children jointly sent a telegram to Egyptian women, expressing firm support for the just struggle of the Egyptian people.
　　
The telegram said: "The British and French colonialists, ignoring the just calls of the people of the world, have brazenly launched an armed invasion of Egypt. This sinful act of harming Egyptian women and children has aroused unparalleled anger among hundreds of millions of Chinese women and mothers."

The telegram said: "You - the sisters of Egypt, under the leadership of President Nasser, have joined the men in a heroic battle to defend your homeland and protect your independent and free life for generations to come. Hundreds of millions of Chinese women and mothers give firm support to your heroic struggle today. The Egyptian people's just action of demanding national independence and autonomy cannot be overwhelmed by any force. Victory must belong to the glorious Egyptian people!"

### 全国妇联等致电埃及妇女

1956-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联等致电埃及妇女
    新华社2日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会和中国保卫儿童全国委员会联合致电埃及妇女，表示坚决支持埃及人民的正义斗争。
    电文说：“英法殖民主义者，不顾全世界人民的正义呼声，竟悍然发动对埃及的武装侵略。这种残害埃及妇女儿童的罪恶行为，激起了全中国妇女和母亲们无比的愤怒。”
    电文说：“你们——埃及的姊妹们已经在纳赛尔总统的领导下，与男子们并肩地为保卫家乡，保卫世世代代独立自由的生活，投入了英勇的战斗。我们亿万个中国妇女和母亲们，对你们今天所进行的英勇斗争，给予坚决的支持。埃及人民这种要求国家独立自主的正义行动，是任何武力也不能压倒的。胜利一定属于光荣的埃及人民！”
