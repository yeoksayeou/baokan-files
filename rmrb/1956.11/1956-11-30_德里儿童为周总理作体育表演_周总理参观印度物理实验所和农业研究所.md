Title: Delhi Children Perform Sports Demonstration for Premier Zhou; Premier Zhou Visits Indian Physics Laboratory and Agricultural Research Institute
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 29th - Premier Zhou Enlai this morning watched a sports demonstration by three thousand Delhi children organized by the Indian National Discipline Scheme.

Premier Zhou Enlai was accompanied by Prime Minister Nehru and the Dalai Lama, as well as Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru. They all greatly admired the various programs of the performance, as well as the well-disciplined and composed formations of the boys and girls.

The audience cheered loudly when they saw Premier Zhou Enlai beating the Lezim (similar to the flower-stick used for entertainment by people in Premier Zhou's hometown of the Huai River basin) along with the children who were collectively performing Lezim while dancing to the rhythm of Indian flutes and drums.

When Premier Zhou Enlai arrived at the review stand, female students presented him with garlands and scattered flower petals on the distinguished guests. Premier Zhou Enlai reviewed the guard of honor. The band played the national anthems of China and India.

After watching the sports demonstration, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a brief speech. Premier Zhou Enlai said, "We are very happy to meet you here, and I would like to extend my congratulations on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government."

He continued, "You are the sons and daughters of the great Indian people, you are cared for and trained by the government under the guidance of Prime Minister Nehru, you have collective life, you receive good training, you have good learning opportunities, you have the conditions for future work, you have a happy today, and you will have a tomorrow to serve your motherland. I now bring you the brotherly and sisterly friendship of the Chinese youth."

Premier Zhou Enlai concluded by shouting: "Long live great India! Long live the sons and daughters of the great Indian people!" He also shouted "Jai Hind!" (Long live India) in Hindi.

The girls presented gifts to Premier Zhou Enlai. Premier Zhou Enlai took photos with the children.

The aim of the Indian National Discipline Scheme is to cultivate the younger generation's discipline and good citizenship through physical exercise programs, organized sports, personal leadership training, and patriotic education. These boys and girls are orphans and children of displaced people who became refugees due to the 1947 partition of the country caused by the conflict between Hindus and Muslims instigated by the colonialists.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 29th - After watching the children's sports demonstration at ten o'clock this morning, Premier Zhou Enlai then visited the Indian National Physical Laboratory. Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru accompanied Premier Zhou on the visit. Dr. Krishnan, the director of the laboratory, and other personnel received Premier Zhou.

Premier Zhou Enlai was very interested in the various tasks of the laboratory. He asked questions of the laboratory personnel.

A large number of the laboratory staff came to welcome him, and Premier Zhou greeted them with his hands clasped together.

On both sides of the road that Premier Zhou Enlai's car passed, many people gathered, shouting "Hindi-Chini bhai bhai" (Indians and Chinese are brothers).

Afterwards, Premier Zhou Enlai visited the nearby Indian Agricultural Research Institute. He was accompanied by Jain, the Minister of Food and Agriculture of India, and Rai Choudhuri, the head of the chemistry department of the institute.

Premier Zhou Enlai visited the cattle farm and saw the special breed "Sahiwal cattle".

Premier Zhou Enlai also visited the institute's work on using radioactive isotopes to improve agriculture.

### 德里儿童为周总理作体育表演  周总理参观印度物理实验所和农业研究所

1956-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    德里儿童为周总理作体育表演
    周总理参观印度物理实验所和农业研究所
    新华社新德里29日电　周恩来总理在今天上午参观了印度根据国民纪律培养计划组织起来的三千名德里少年儿童的体育表演。
    陪同周恩来总理参观的有尼赫鲁总理和达赖喇嘛，以及中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁。他们都非常欣赏表演的各项节目，以及男女少年儿童的纪律严明、态度沉着的分列式队伍。
    参观的人在看到周恩来总理随着印度笛子和鼓的拍子，同一边跳舞一边集体表演莱齐姆（同周总理故乡淮河流域人民娱乐用的花棍相似）的少年儿童们一道打着莱齐姆的时候，他们大家都高声欢呼起来。
    周恩来总理到达检阅场的时候，女学生们给他戴上了花环，并且把花瓣撒在贵宾们的身上。周恩来总理检阅了仪仗队。乐队奏了中国和印度的国歌。
    在观看体育表演以后，周恩来总理发表了简短的讲话。周恩来总理说，我们很高兴地在这里会见你们，我首先代表中国人民和中国政府祝贺你们。
    他接着说，你们是伟大的印度人民的儿女，你们受到了在尼赫鲁总理指导下的政府的照顾和训练，你们得到了集体生活，你们受到了很好的训练，你们得到了很好的学习机会，你们得到将来工作的条件，你们有了快乐的今天，你们将有为你们祖国效劳的明天，未来是属于你们的。我现在谨把中国青年兄弟般的、姐妹般的友谊带给你们。
    周恩来总理最后高呼：伟大的印度万岁！伟大的印度人民的儿女们万岁！他并且用印地语高呼“斋兴德！”（印度万岁）。
    女孩子们向周恩来总理献了礼物。周恩来总理同少年儿童们一起照了像。
    印度国民纪律培养计划的目的是通过体育锻炼计划、有组织的运动、个人领导才能的锻炼和爱国主义教育，来培养年青的一代的纪律思想和良好的公民品质。这些男女少年儿童都是孤儿和那些流离失所的人们的孩子，这些人由于殖民主义者挑起的印度教徒和穆斯林的冲突所引起的1947年国家分治而成为难民。
    据新华社新德里29日电　周恩来总理今天上午十时在参观了少年儿童体育表演之后，接着参观了印度国立物理实验所。陪同周总理参观的有中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁。实验所所长克里希南博士和其他人员接待了周总理。
    周恩来总理对实验所的各项工作感到很大的兴趣。他向实验所的人员提出了问题。
    实验所的大批工作人员都来欢迎他，周总理向他们双手合十致意。
    在周恩来总理的汽车经过的道路两旁，聚集着很多人，他们高呼“印地秦尼巴依巴依”（印中人民是兄弟）。
    随后，周恩来总理参观了附近的印度农业研究所。陪同他参观的有印度粮食和农业部部长贾殷和这个研究所的化学部主任拉伊·乔杜里。
    周恩来总理参观了牛场，看了特别品种“萨希瓦尔牛”。
    周恩来总理还参观了研究所的利用放射性同位素来改进农业的工作。
