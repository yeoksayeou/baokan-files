Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Arrives in Leningrad for Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation, headed by Peng Zhen, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation, accompanied by Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, arrived in Leningrad from Moscow on the 18th.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the train station by the leaders of the Leningrad Party and Soviet, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation in Leningrad, representatives of various social organizations, and working people.

Acting Chairman of the Leningrad City Soviet Executive Committee, Stel'chalkovskij, delivered a welcome speech to the delegation.

Following this, Peng Zhen delivered a reply speech. He pointed out that thirty-nine years ago, a cannon shot fired here shook the entire world and opened a new century in human history. From that time on, the brilliance of the socialist revolution illuminated the path of the Chinese people towards victory. The working people of Leningrad demonstrated great heroism during the Civil War and the Patriotic War, and rapidly restored and developed the city's construction after the war, making it one of the largest industrial centers. These are examples for the Chinese people to follow.

On the same day, the delegation toured the city streets, visited the cruiser "Aurora" and the Young Pioneers Palace, and toured the underground tramway. In the evening, they watched an opera.

### 我全国人民代表大会代表团  到达列宁格勒访问

1956-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

    我全国人民代表大会代表团
    到达列宁格勒访问
    新华社莫斯科19日电　以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫陪同下，18日从莫斯科到达列宁格勒。
    代表团在火车站受到了列宁格勒党和苏维埃领导人、在列宁格勒的苏联最高苏维埃代表和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表、各社会团体的代表和劳动人民的热烈欢迎。
    列宁格勒市苏维埃执行委员会代理主席斯特尔查尔科夫斯基向代表团致欢迎词。
    接着，彭真致答词。他指出，三十九年前，就是在这里发出的一声炮响，震动了整个世界，并且在人类的历史上打开了新的世纪，从那个时候开始，社会主义革命的光辉便照亮了中国人民走向胜利的道路。列宁格勒劳动人民在国内战争和卫国战争时期，表现了伟大的英雄主义气概，并且在战后迅速地恢复和发展了城市的建设，使它成为最大的工业中心之一。这些都是中国人民的榜样。
    当天，代表团游览了市街，访问了“阿芙乐尔号”巡洋舰和少年先锋宫，参观了地下电车道。晚上，他们看了歌剧。
