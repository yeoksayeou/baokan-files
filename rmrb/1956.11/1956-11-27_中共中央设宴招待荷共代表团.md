Title: CPC Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Delegation from the Communist Party of the Netherlands

Author:

Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China hosted a banquet this afternoon to welcome the delegation from the Communist Party of the Netherlands, including the head of the delegation, Annie Averink, and members Gerard Maas and Gerrit Taverlis, who are visiting China.

Attending the banquet were Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Chen Yun, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Yang Shangkun, and Liu Ningyi, members of the CPC Central Committee; and Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, Xiong Fu, and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

### 中共中央设宴招待荷共代表团

1956-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央设宴招待荷共代表团
    新华社26日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天下午设宴招待前来访问中国的荷兰共产党代表团团长安妮·阿夫林克，团员盖拉德·玛斯和盖雷特·台夫里斯。
    出席宴会的有中共中央副主席刘少奇、朱德、陈云，中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥、杨尚昆、刘宁一，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、熊复、李启新等同志。
