Title: Soviet Film Week Opens in Beijing; First Group of Soviet Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing and Attends Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The opening ceremony of Soviet Film Week was held solemnly this evening at the Capital Cinema in Beijing.

The first group of the Soviet film workers delegation, which arrived in Beijing this afternoon, attended the opening ceremony.

Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture and Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, said at the opening ceremony: The holding of this Soviet Film Week is just before the 39th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, let us express our heartfelt congratulations to this festival that all working people around the world feel happy about. Qian Junrui expressed his sincere welcome to the Soviet film workers delegation. He said: From the founding of New China to the end of 1955, Soviet films were screened nearly two million times in China, with an audience of more than 1.16 billion people. The arrival of this delegation, composed of outstanding entrepreneurs and artists, will surely greatly inspire Chinese film audiences and provide Chinese filmmakers with many lessons.

Sudalikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, spoke at the opening ceremony, saying that this Soviet Film Week, held on the eve of the October Revolution Day, vividly demonstrates the growing cultural ties between the Soviet Union and China, and shows the great and sincere friendship between the peoples of the two countries. He hoped that Chinese audiences would offer criticisms and suggestions on Soviet films, and wished the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples would forever prosper.

Wang Lanxi, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Film Bureau, introduced the members of the Soviet film workers delegation to the audience. They are: Karashnikov, head of the delegation and Deputy Director of the General Administration of Film Production of the Soviet Ministry of Culture; delegation member, Soviet People's Artist, and the female lead of the films "Mother" and "Rural Teacher," Maretskaya; and the female lead of the films "Young Guard" and "Unwanted Son-in-Law," Mordyukova. When introduced, the Soviet film artists sitting on the rostrum repeatedly stood up to thank the enthusiastic applause of the Chinese audience, and Chinese film workers also presented them with flowers.

Karashnikov then spoke. He extended friendly greetings to Chinese film workers and audiences on behalf of Soviet film workers. Maretskaya and Mordyukova warmly greeted the Beijing audience. Maretskaya said to the audience in Chinese, "Hello," and "Thank you," and the audience once again expressed their welcome with thunderous applause.

Among those attending the opening ceremony tonight were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shao Lizi, as well as Chinese film figures Cai Chusheng, Situ Huimin, Wang Yang, Sha Meng, and more than 1,300 audience members from all walks of life.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

After the opening ceremony, the Soviet color feature film "Mother," based on Gorky's novel of the same name, was screened.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The first group of the Soviet film workers delegation, invited to China to participate in the Soviet Film Week, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who arrived in Beijing today were Karashnikov, head of the delegation and Deputy Director of the General Administration of Film Production of the Soviet Ministry of Culture; delegation member, Soviet People's Artist, and the female lead of the films "Rural Teacher" and "Mother," Maretskaya; and Soviet film actress, the female lead of the films "Young Guard" and "Unwanted Son-in-Law," Mordyukova.

Greeting the delegation at the airport today were Wang Lanxi, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Film Bureau, Liao Jingtian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as famous figures from the Chinese film industry.

The delegation consists of seven people. In addition to those who arrived in Beijing today, there are also Uzbek SSR Honored Artist and film director Rukov, famous Soviet film actor and the male lead of the film "Unfinished Story," Bondarchuk, as well as film actors Ushakova and Pannichi, who will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 苏联电影周开幕式在北京举行  苏联电影工作者代表团首批人员到达北京并出席了开幕式

1956-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联电影周开幕式在北京举行
    苏联电影工作者代表团首批人员到达北京并出席了开幕式
    新华社5日讯　苏联电影周的开幕式今天晚上在北京首都电影院隆重举行。
    今天下午到达北京的苏联电影工作者代表团首批人员出席了开幕式。
    文化部副部长、中苏友好协会总会理事会秘书长钱俊瑞在开幕式上说：这次苏联电影周的举办正值伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年的前夕，让我们向这个全世界劳动人民都感到喜悦的节日表示衷心的祝贺。钱俊瑞对苏联电影工作者代表团表示诚挚的欢迎。他说：从新中国成立到1955年底，苏联影片在中国放映将近二百万场，观众达十一亿六千万多人次，这次由卓越的事业家和艺术家组成的代表团的到来，一定能够大大鼓舞中国的电影观众，并将给中国电影工作者很多教益。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫在开幕式上讲了话，他说，在十月革命节前夕举行的这次苏联电影周，鲜明地表示了苏中两国日益增长的文化联系，显示出两国人民伟大真诚的友谊。他希望中国观众能对苏联影片提出批评和建议，并祝苏、中两国人民牢不可破的友谊永远繁荣。
    文化部部长助理、电影事业管理局局长王阑西介绍苏联电影工作者代表团人员同观众见面。他们是：代表团团长、苏联文化部影片生产总管理局副局长卡拉什尼科夫，团员、苏联人民演员、影片“母亲”、“乡村女教师”的女主角玛列茨卡娅，影片“青年近卫军”、“不称心的女婿”的女主角莫尔久阔娃。当介绍的时候，坐在主席台上的苏联电影艺术家们不断起立，答谢中国观众的热烈鼓掌，中国电影工作者还向他们献了鲜花。
    卡拉什尼科夫接着讲话。他代表苏联电影工作者向中国电影工作者和观众致以友谊的问候。玛列茨卡娅和莫尔久阔娃热情洋溢地向北京观众问好。玛列茨卡娅用中国话向观众亲切地说：“你们好”、“谢谢你”，全场观众再一次用雷动的掌声向他们表示欢迎。
    今天晚上出席开幕式的有国务院副总理贺龙，中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，以及中国电影界人士蔡楚生、司徒慧敏、汪洋、沙蒙等和各界观众共一千三百多人。
    各国驻华外交使节也应邀参加了开幕式。
    在开幕式以后，放映了根据高尔基同名小说拍摄的苏联彩色故事片“母亲”。
    新华社5日讯　应邀来中国参加苏联电影周活动的苏联电影工作者代表团首批人员今天下午坐飞机到达北京。
    今天到达北京的有代表团团长、苏联文化部影片生产总管理局副局长卡拉什尼科夫，团员、苏联人民演员、影片“乡村女教师”、“母亲”的女主角玛列茨卡娅，苏联电影演员、影片“青年近卫军”、“不称心的女婿”的女主角莫尔久阔娃。
    今天到飞机场欢迎代表团的有文化部部长助理、电影事业管理局局长王阑西，中苏友好协会总会理事会副秘书长廖经天，以及中国电影界著名人士。
    代表团共有七人，除今天到达北京的以外，还有乌兹别克人民共和国功勋艺术家、电影导演鲁柯夫，苏联著名电影演员、影片“没有说完的故事”的男主角邦达尔丘克，以及电影演员乌沙阔娃、潘尼奇将在日内到达北京。
