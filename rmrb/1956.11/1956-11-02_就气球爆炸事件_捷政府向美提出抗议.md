Title: Czechoslovak Government Protests to the U.S. Regarding Balloon Explosion Incident
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 1st: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Czechoslovak Foreign Ministry delivered a note to the U.S. Embassy in Prague on October 30th, lodging a strong protest regarding the recent incident in which balloons sent by the American organization, the so-called "Free Europe Committee," exploded over Slovakia.

The note stated that this activity, which has already caused several tragic incidents in Czechoslovakia, not only violates international civil aviation conventions, but also the United Nations Charter.

The Czechoslovak government stated that if these activities are not stopped, the government will have to take further measures and consider submitting this issue to the Eleventh United Nations General Assembly.

### 就气球爆炸事件  捷政府向美提出抗议

1956-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    就气球爆炸事件
    捷政府向美提出抗议
    新华社布拉格1日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克外交部10月30日送交美国驻布拉格大使馆一份照会，就最近所谓“自由欧洲委员会”这个美国组织放送的气球在斯洛伐克上空爆炸的事件提出强硬抗议。
    照会说，这个已经在捷克斯洛伐克造成将几起惨痛的不幸事件的活动，不仅违反国际民航公约，也违反联合国宪章。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府声明，如果这些活动不予制止，政府将不得不采取进一步的措施，并且考虑把这个问题提交第十一届联合国大会。
