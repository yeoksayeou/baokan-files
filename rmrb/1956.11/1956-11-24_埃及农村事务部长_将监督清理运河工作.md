Title: Egyptian Minister of Rural Affairs to Oversee Canal Clearing

Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 23rd. Cairo, 22nd, announced that the Egyptian Minister of Rural Affairs, Abdel Latif Boghdadi, will be responsible for overseeing the clearing of the Suez Canal. Boghdadi is also responsible for guiding the reconstruction and redesign plans for Port Said.

### 埃及农村事务部长  将监督清理运河工作

1956-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及农村事务部长
    将监督清理运河工作
    新华社23日讯　开罗消息：据开罗22日宣布，埃及农村事务部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·波格达迪将负责监督清理苏伊士运河的工作。波格达迪还负责指导重建和重新设计塞得港的计划。
