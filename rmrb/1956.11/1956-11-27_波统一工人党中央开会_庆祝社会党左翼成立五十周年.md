Title: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Meets to Celebrate 50th Anniversary of the Founding of the Left Wing of the Socialist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 26th – According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party convened a meeting in Warsaw on the 25th to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the founding of the left wing of the Polish Socialist Party.

    Members and secretaries of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party attended the meeting.

    Ochab, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, delivered a report at the meeting. In his report, he emphasized the contributions made by the left wing of the Polish Socialist Party in developing the Polish workers' revolutionary movement.

    At the meeting, the Polish State Council awarded government prizes to some veteran members of the left wing of the Polish Socialist Party who actively participated in the Polish revolutionary movement.

    The left wing of the Polish Socialist Party was a component part of the Polish Communist Workers' Party, which was established in 1918. In 1948, it merged with the Polish Workers' Party to become the Polish United Workers' Party.

### 波统一工人党中央开会  庆祝社会党左翼成立五十周年

1956-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    波统一工人党中央开会
    庆祝社会党左翼成立五十周年
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会25日在华沙召开庆祝波兰社会党左翼成立五十周年大会。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局的委员和书记处的书记出席了大会。
    波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员奥哈布在会上作了报告。他在报告中着重指出了波兰社会党左翼在发展波兰工人革命运动方面所作出的贡献。
    在大会上，波兰国务委员会把政府的奖励授给一些积极参加波兰革命运动的波兰社会党左翼老党员。
    波兰社会党左翼是1918年成立的波兰共产主义工人政党的一个组成部分。1948年和波兰工人党合并成为波兰统一工人党。
