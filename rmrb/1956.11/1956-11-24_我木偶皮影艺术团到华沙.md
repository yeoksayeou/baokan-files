Title: My Puppet Shadow Art Troupe Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 23rd. The Chinese Puppet and Shadow Art Troupe arrived in Warsaw by train on the 22nd from Czechoslovakia. The troupe is visiting Poland at the invitation of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Arts and will perform in Poland for two weeks.

### 我木偶皮影艺术团到华沙

1956-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我木偶皮影艺术团到华沙
    新华社华沙23日电　中国木偶皮影艺术剧团22日由捷克斯洛伐克乘车到达华沙。剧团是应波兰文化艺术部的邀请来波兰的，将在波兰作两星期的访问演出。
