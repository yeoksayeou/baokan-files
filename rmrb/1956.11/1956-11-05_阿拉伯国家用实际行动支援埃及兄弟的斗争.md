Title: Arab Countries Support Egyptian Brothers' Struggle with Practical Actions
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Cairo dispatch: According to a Middle East News Agency dispatch from Khartoum, the Sudanese government has banned British and French planes carrying troops and civilians from flying over Sudan. A British plane carrying troops to Aden was ordered to fly back to Malta.
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: Beirut dispatch: Lebanese Minister of State Saeb Salam said today that Lebanon will refuse to supply fuel to British and French warships attacking Egypt if these warships moor in Lebanon.
He also announced that Lebanon has recalled its ambassadors to London and Paris. He declined to say whether this meant Lebanon might sever diplomatic relations with Britain and France.
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: TASS Beirut, November 3rd: On November 2nd, the Lebanese Parliament unanimously passed a resolution condemning the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt and supporting the struggle of fraternal Egypt. The resolution also requires the government to take necessary measures in accordance with the United Nations Charter and the Arab States Joint Defense Treaty.
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: TASS Beirut, November 2nd: According to newspaper reports, Iraq has promulgated a decree entering a state of war.
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Baghdad dispatch: Iraqi Crown Prince Abdul Ilah sent a telegram to British Prime Minister Eden on November 3rd in his personal capacity, condemning the "hostile actions of Britain and France against the brotherly Egyptian people."
Abdul Ilah demanded that Eden immediately cease the current military operations on Egyptian territory.
At the same time, the situation in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, is becoming more tense, and many parliamentarians are demanding a reconsideration of Iraq's relations with Britain. The Iraqi government announced martial law on November 3rd, banning all demonstrations and gatherings, and prohibiting the carrying of weapons. The Iraqi Ministry of Education also ordered the suspension of classes at all levels of schools for a week on the same day. The government also warned that if prices continue to rise, it will implement control over food throughout the country.
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: TASS Beirut, November 3rd: According to newspaper reports, representatives of all political parties and organizations in Jordan sent a telegram to Iraqi King Faisal II, in which they asked the King to withdraw from the Baghdad Pact and help Egypt in its struggle against British and French aggression.
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Damascus dispatch: The Syrian government took over all British and French schools in Syria on November 3rd.
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Bahrain dispatch: A general strike and demonstrations have taken place in the oil-rich Bahrain Islands in the Persian Gulf since the morning of November 2nd to protest against the British and French aggression against Egypt. The strike was still in progress on the 3rd.
Police authorities in the Emirate of Bahrain announced that riots occurred in Manama, the capital of Bahrain, on November 2nd and 3rd, and some British businesses were burned down. Police authorities have issued martial law and announced that anyone on the street will be arrested, and those who resist arrest will be shot.
British troops have been deployed to the outskirts of Manama to suppress demonstrations against the aggression against Egypt.

### 阿拉伯国家用实际行动支援埃及兄弟的斗争

1956-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家用实际行动支援埃及兄弟的斗争
    苏丹禁止英法飞机过境
    新华社4日讯　开罗消息：据中东通讯社喀土穆消息说，苏丹政府已经禁止英法装载军队和平民的飞机飞经苏丹。有一架装载军队前往亚丁的英国飞机被勒令飞回马耳他岛。
    黎巴嫩不供给英法军舰油料
    新华社3日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩国务部长赛布·萨拉姆今天说，黎巴嫩将拒绝向攻击埃及的英法军舰供给油料，如果这些军舰在黎巴嫩碇泊的话。
    他还宣布，黎巴嫩已经召回它驻在伦敦和巴黎的大使。他拒绝说明这是否意味着黎巴嫩可能同英法断绝外交关系。
    据新华社4日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特3日讯：11月2日黎巴嫩议会一致通过了谴责英法和以色列侵略埃及和支持兄弟埃及的斗争的决议。决议并要求政府根据联合国宪章和阿拉伯国家集体安全公约采取必要措施。
    伊拉克宣布进入战争状态
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特2日讯：据报纸报道，伊拉克已经颁布了进入战争状态法令。
    新华社4日讯　巴格达消息：伊拉克王储阿卜杜勒·伊拉在11月3日以私人名义打电报给英国首相艾登，谴责“英法对兄弟埃及人民的敌对行动”。
    阿卜杜勒·伊拉要求艾登立即停止目前在埃及领土上的军事行动。
    同时，伊拉克首都巴格达的情况正更加紧张，许多议员要求重新考虑伊拉克同英国的关系。伊拉克政府已经在11月3日宣布实行戒严令，禁止一切游行示威和集会，并且禁止携带武器。伊拉克教育部在同一天下令各级学校停课一星期。政府还警告说，如果物价继续上涨，将对全国的粮食实行统制。
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特3日讯：据报纸报道，约旦所有政党和团体的代表给伊拉克国王费萨尔二世打了电报，他们在电报中要求国王脱离巴格达条约，帮助埃及反抗英法侵略的斗争。
    叙利亚政府接管英法学校
    新华社4日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚政府在11月3日接管了在叙利亚的全部英国和法国学校。
    巴林群岛人民举行总罢工
    新华社4日讯　巴林消息：波斯海湾上盛产石油的巴林群岛从11月2日早晨起发生了总罢工和示威，抗议英法侵略埃及。罢工到3日仍在进行中。
    巴林酋长国警察当局宣布，巴林首府马纳马在11月2日和3日发生了骚动，一些英国企业被焚毁。警察当局已经颁布戒严令，并且宣布任何在街上的行人都将被逮，拒捕者将受枪击。
    英军已经开到马纳马近郊来镇压反抗侵略埃及的示威群众。
