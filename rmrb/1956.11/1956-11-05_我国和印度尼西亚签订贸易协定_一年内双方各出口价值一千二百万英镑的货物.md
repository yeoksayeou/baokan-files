Title: China and Indonesia Sign Trade Agreement: Each Side to Export Goods Worth £12 Million Within One Year
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia, in order to develop trade relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, recently held trade negotiations in Beijing. After frank consultations, the two sides, on November 3, 1956, signed the Trade Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesian.

    Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China signed on the behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and Soekardjo Wirjopranoto, Head of the Trade Delegation of the Republic of Indonesia and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China, signed on the behalf of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia. The Chinese side attending the signing ceremony included Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. The Indonesian side included Taher Ibrahim, Deputy Head of the Trade Delegation, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Economy, and seven members of the delegation.

    The Trade Agreement stipulates that trade between the two countries shall be based on the principle of balanced imports and exports. The term of the agreement is one year, during which each side shall export goods worth £12 million. The commodities exported by the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Indonesia are: machinery and tools, chemical raw materials, minerals, electrical equipment, building materials, cotton cloth, floral print fabrics, cotton yarn, silk, paper and stationery, daily necessities, food, sporting goods, rice, etc. The commodities exported by the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China are: rubber, copra, coconut oil, white sugar, brown sugar, coffee, pepper, betel nuts, spices, timber, quinine, rattan, etc.

    Simultaneously with the signing of the Trade Agreement, the two sides exchanged three notes. One of the notes extends the validity of the Payment Agreement signed by the two governments on September 1, 1954, to October 31, 1957. The second note stipulates that the trade balance owed by the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China in the implementation of the Trade Protocol signed on September 1, 1954, will be repaid by the Republic of Indonesia with exports of various goods over the next three years. The third note stipulates the strengthening of economic and technical cooperation between the two countries. The Government of the People's Republic of China is prepared to provide the Government of the Republic of Indonesia with certain light industrial equipment needed for economic development within the scope of its possible supply and is prepared to provide assistance in sending experts to Indonesia and accepting Indonesian interns to study in the People's Republic of China.

    The negotiations between the two sides were conducted in a friendly atmosphere. The above agreements will effectively strengthen the economic cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia and further promote the friendly relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia. This is fully in line with the resolutions and spirit of the Asian-African Conference on economic cooperation and is conducive to promoting prosperity and peace in Asia and the world.

### 我国和印度尼西亚签订贸易协定  一年内双方各出口价值一千二百万英镑的货物

1956-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    我国和印度尼西亚签订贸易协定
    一年内双方各出口价值一千二百万英镑的货物
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府为了在平等互利的基础上发展两国间的贸易关系，最近在北京举行了贸易谈判，双方经过坦率协商以后，于1956年11月3日签订了中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府之间的贸易协定。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮代表中华人民共和国政府签字，印度尼西亚共和国政府贸易代表团团长、印度尼西亚共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使苏加佐·维约普拉诺托代表印度尼西亚共和国政府签字。参加签字仪式的，中华人民共和国方面有：对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮等；印度尼西亚共和国方面有：贸易代表团副团长、经济部对外贸易署署长塔黑尔·伊伯拉钦姆和代表团团员七人。
    贸易协定规定两国的贸易以进出口平衡为原则，协定的期限是一年，在协定有效期内双方各出口价值一千二百万英镑的货物。中华人民共和国对印度尼西亚共和国出口的商品是：机器和工具、化工原料、矿产品、电工器材、建筑材料、棉布、花裙布、棉纱、丝绸、纸张和文教用品、日用百货、食品、体育用品、大米等。印度尼西亚共和国对中华人民共和国出口的商品是橡胶、椰干、椰子油、白糖、土糖、咖啡、胡椒、槟榔、香料、木材、奎宁、藤等。
    和贸易协定签订的同时，双方交换了三个照会。照会之一将两国政府在1954年9月1日签订的支付协定的有效期延长到1957年10月31日止。照会之二规定在执行1954年9月1日签订的贸易议定书中，印度尼西亚共和国积欠中华人民共和国的贸易差额将由印度尼西亚共和国在今后三年内出口各种货物偿还。照会之三规定加强两国间的经济和技术合作，中华人民共和国政府准备在可能供应的范围内，供给印度尼西亚共和国政府发展经济所需要的某些轻工业设备，并准备在派遣专家前往印度尼西亚和接受印度尼西亚实习生来中华人民共和国进修方面给予帮助。
    双方的谈判是在友好气氛中进行的。以上各种协议，将有力地加强中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国的经济合作，进一步促进中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国的友好关系。这是完全符合亚非会议有关经济合作的决议和精神，并且有利于促进亚洲和世界的繁荣和和平。
