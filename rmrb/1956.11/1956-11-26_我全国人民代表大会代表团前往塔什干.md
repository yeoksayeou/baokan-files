Title: Our National People's Congress Delegation Leaves for Tashkent
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 25th: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation left Moscow for Tashkent on November 25th aboard a "Tu-104" jet airliner.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 25th: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation, which is currently visiting Moscow, visited the Atomic Energy Power Station of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the 24th.

After the visit, Peng Zhen, head of the delegation, told Soviet reporters that Soviet scientists had made meritorious contributions because they had utilized atomic energy for peaceful purposes. He wished Soviet scientists further achievements in the peaceful use of atomic energy.

### 我全国人民代表大会代表团前往塔什干

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国人民代表大会代表团前往塔什干
    新华社莫斯科25日电　11月25日，中国全国人民代表大会代表团乘“Ty—104”喷气式客机离莫斯科前往塔什干。
    据新华社莫斯科25日电　正在莫斯科访问的中国全国人民代表大会代表团在24日参观了苏联科学院原子能发电站。
    参观后，代表团团长彭真对苏联记者说，苏联科学家创立了功勋，因为他们把原子能利用于和平的目的。他祝苏联科学家们在和平利用原子能方面取得进一步的成就。
