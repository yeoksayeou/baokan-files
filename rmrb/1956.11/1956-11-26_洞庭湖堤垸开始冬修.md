Title: Dongting Lake Levees Start Winter Repairs
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha Telegram　Over eighty thousand laborers are now engaged in winter repair work on the levees and embankments along Dongting Lake in Hunan Province. In Anxiang, Huarong, and other counties where work started earlier, over 40% of the earthwork task for winter levee and embankment repair has already been completed. Eight counties, including Changde and Anxiang, have eliminated over 14,200 hidden dangers in the levees.

### 洞庭湖堤垸开始冬修

1956-11-26
第3版()
专栏：

    洞庭湖堤垸开始冬修
    据新华社长沙电　湖南省洞庭湖滨的堤防上，现在有八万多民工投入了堤垸的冬季整修工程。开工较早的安乡、华容等县，冬季整修堤垸的土方任务已经完成40％以上。常德、安乡等八县已经消灭了堤防隐患一万四千二百多处。
