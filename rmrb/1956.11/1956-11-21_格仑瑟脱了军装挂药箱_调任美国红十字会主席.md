Title: Glencorse Takes Off the Uniform and Hangs Up the Medicine Kit: Transferred to the Position of Chairman of the American Red Cross
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 20th: Outgoing Supreme Commander of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization forces, American General Alfred M. Gruenther, has been appointed Chairman of the American Red Cross.
	Gruenther was formally selected by the Board of Governors of the American Red Cross, but the announcement was made by the White House, as Eisenhower is the honorary chairman of the organization.
	Another American General, Lauris Norstad, will succeed Gruenther as Supreme Commander of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization forces.

### 格仑瑟脱了军装挂药箱  调任美国红十字会主席

1956-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    格仑瑟脱了军装挂药箱
    调任美国红十字会主席
    新华社20日讯　华盛顿消息：即将离职的北大西洋集团军队总司令、美国将军艾尔弗雷德·马·格仑瑟已经被任命为美国红十字会主席。
    格仑瑟是由美国红十字会理事会正式选任的，但是由白宫加以宣布，因为艾森豪威尔是这个组织的名誉主席。
    将接替格仑瑟任北大西洋集团军队总司令的是另一位美国将军劳理斯·诺斯塔德。
