Title: Egypt and Australia Sever Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 7th: According to an official announcement, Egypt severed diplomatic relations with Australia on November 6th.

### 埃澳断绝外交关系

1956-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    埃澳断绝外交关系
    新华社7日讯　开罗消息：据官方宣布，埃及在11月6日同澳大利亚断绝了外交关系。
