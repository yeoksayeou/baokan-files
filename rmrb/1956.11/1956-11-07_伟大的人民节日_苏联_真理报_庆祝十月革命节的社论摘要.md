Title: Great People's Festival: Abstract of "Pravda" Editorial Celebrating the October Revolution Day in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: According to TASS Moscow, 6th: The Soviet "Pravda" today published an editorial entitled "Great People's Festival" to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Revolution.

The editorial said that the Soviet state is entering its glorious fortieth year as a strong socialist country with a highly developed economy and advanced culture. The October Revolution was a true people's revolution that achieved victory under the principles of realizing proletarian internationalism and the international unity and international peace of the working people. The October Revolution opened a new era, an era in which mankind was liberated from social oppression and colonial national oppression and developed all its creative forces.

The editorial said that progressive people in all countries recognize the greatest world-historical significance of the October Revolution and its powerful influence on the course of social development. The cause of the October Revolution has been developed in the labor and struggle of the peoples of the socialist family, united by the principles of proletarian internationalism and the common idea of building a socialist society. The October Revolution Day is a great festival for the working people of all countries.

The editorial said that implementing the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU and realizing the Sixth Five-Year Plan for developing the national economy will further consolidate the material and technical foundation of the Soviet Union. The measures taken by the party are to further develop Soviet democracy, and these measures have aroused new upsurges in the initiative, political activism, and labor activism of workers, farmers, and the Soviet intelligentsia.

The editorial concluded by saying that in foreign affairs, the Communist Party and the Soviet government have consistently adhered to the unwavering principles of maintaining peace and preventing war, and the principles of peaceful coexistence, cooperation, and friendship among all countries.

Our country establishes mutual relations with all countries of the socialist family on the basis of complete equality, and believes that it is its internationalist duty to consolidate the fraternal cooperation and mutual assistance of socialist countries for the great cause of peace and socialism.

Under the banner of Marxism-Leninism, under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Soviet people are moving forward to the victory of communism with full confidence.

### 伟大的人民节日  苏联“真理报”庆祝十月革命节的社论摘要

1956-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

    伟大的人民节日
    苏联“真理报”庆祝十月革命节的社论摘要
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：苏联“真理报”今天以“伟大的人民节日”为题发表社论，庆祝十月革命三十九周年。
    社论说，苏维埃国家作为一个经济高度发达、文化先进的强大社会主义国家进入自己光荣的第四十年。十月革命是在实现无产阶级国际主义原则、在实现劳动人民国际团结和国际和平的原则下取得胜利的真正人民的革命，十月革命开辟了新的纪元，人类摆脱社会压迫和殖民主义民族压迫而获得解放以及发展自己一切创造力量的纪元。
    社论说，各国进步的人士都承认十月革命的最伟大的世界历史意义和它对社会发展过程的强大影响。十月革命事业在由无产阶级国际主义原则和建设社会主义社会的共同思想团结起来的社会主义大家庭各国人民的劳动和斗争中获得了发展。十月革命节是各国劳动人民伟大的节日。
    社论说，执行苏共第二十次代表大会决议，实现发展国民经济的第六个五年计划，将更加巩固苏联的物质技术基础。党所采取的措施是为了进一步发展苏维埃民主，这些措施激起了工人、农民、苏维埃知识分子的主动性、政治积极性和劳动积极性的新高涨。
    社论最后说，在外交方面，共产党和苏联政府一贯遵循维护和平和防止战争的坚定不移的原则、遵循和平共处、各国合作和友好的原则。
    我们国家在完全平等的基础上同社会主义大家庭各国建立相互关系，认为为了伟大的和平和社会主义事业而巩固社会主义国家的兄弟合作和互助，是自己的国际主义职责。
    在马克思列宁主义的旗帜下，在共产党的领导下，苏联人民满怀信心地向共产主义的胜利前进。
