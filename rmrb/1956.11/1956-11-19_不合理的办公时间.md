Title: Unreasonable Office Hours
Author: Hong Ye
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 4

Column: Voice of the Masses

    Unreasonable Office Hours
    The autumn office hours stipulated by Pingyuan County, Shandong Province, are very unreasonable: 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM for study, 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM for office work, 12:30 PM to 1:30 PM for rest, 1:30 PM to 4:30 PM for office work, and 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM is still office time in the evening.
    In this way, our time is divided into fragmented pieces, affecting everyone's after-work study. We have raised our opinions on this several times, but it has never attracted the attention of the leadership.
                        (Hong Ye)

### 不合理的办公时间
鸿野
1956-11-19
第4版()
专栏：群众呼声

    不合理的办公时间
    山东平原县秋季办公时间规定得很不合理：上午七点到九点学习，九点到十二点半办公，十二点半到一点半休息，一点半到四点半办公，晚上六点半到八点半仍是办公时间。
    这样，把我们的时间都分得零零碎碎，影响了大家的业余进修。我们对此曾几次提出了意见，但始终没有引起领导上的重视。
                        （鸿野）
