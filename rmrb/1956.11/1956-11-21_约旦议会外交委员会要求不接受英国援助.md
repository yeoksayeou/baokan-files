Title: Jordanian Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee Requests Rejection of British Aid
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 6

    Jordanian Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee Requests Rejection of British Aid
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 19th – According to TASS, Amman reports: The Jordanian Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee passed a resolution to refuse British financial "aid." This resolution will be submitted to parliament for discussion.

### 约旦议会外交委员会要求不接受英国援助

1956-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦议会外交委员会要求不接受英国援助
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社大马士革19日讯：安曼消息，约旦议会外交委员会通过了关于拒绝接受英国财政“援助”的决议。这项决议将提交议会讨论。
