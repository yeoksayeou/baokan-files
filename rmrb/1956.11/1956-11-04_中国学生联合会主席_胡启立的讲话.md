Title: Speech by Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 3

    Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation
    Hu Qili's Speech
    Chinese students have never been afraid of any violent force. During the "May Fourth" Movement, we bravely shouted the slogan "Down with imperialism!" Now the imperialists have been driven out of mainland China, but Britain and France are killing and setting fires in Egypt! Comrades, students, can we ignore this behavior of the imperialists?
    The bombs dropped by British and French planes in Egypt fall on the Egyptian people, and at the same time, they explode in our hearts. This banditry of the British and French imperialists reminds us of the cruel oppression suffered by the Chinese people before liberation. An unparalleled flame of anger burns in our hearts. We will never allow the British and French imperialists to run rampant. Chinese students will never stand idly by in the face of the evil deeds of the British and French aggressors. We must dedicate all our strength to support the just struggle of the Egyptian people.
    We must seriously warn the British and French imperialists that in today's surging anti-colonialist tide, it is a dream to think that airplanes and cannons can hinder the progress of the wheels of history. History will not go backwards. It will not be the peace-loving people who are crushed, but the aggressors themselves. History is destined for the colonialist and imperialist systems to perish, and the people who defend national independence and love peace will surely achieve final victory!
    Dear Egyptian brothers, fight bravely! Chinese students and 600 million Chinese people stand with you, and the peace-loving people of the world stand with you. Victory belongs to you!
    Long live the heroic Egyptian people!

### 中国学生联合会主席  胡启立的讲话

1956-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

    中国学生联合会主席
    胡启立的讲话
    中国学生从来是不怕任何强暴力量的，“五四”运动时我们就曾经勇敢地喊出过“打倒帝国主义”的口号，现在帝国主义者已经被赶出中国大陆，但是英、法又在埃及杀人放火！同学们、同志们，我们能对帝国主义者这种行为置之不理吗？
    英国法国的飞机在埃及丢下的炸弹炸在埃及人民的身上，同时也就是炸在我们的心里，英法帝国主义者这种强盗行径使我们回忆起解放前中国人民所身受过的残酷压迫，在我们的心里燃起了无比愤怒的火焰，我们决不容许英、法帝国主义者横行霸道。中国学生决不对英法侵略者的罪恶行为袖手旁观，我们一定要献出全部力量，支援埃及人民的正义斗争。
    我们要严重警告英法帝国主义者，在反殖民主义的怒潮汹涌澎湃的今天，如果还以为用飞机大炮就能阻碍历史车轮的前进，那就是作梦。历史是不会倒退的，被粉碎的将不是爱好和平的人民群众，而正是侵略者自己。历史注定了殖民主义和帝国主义制度一定要灭亡，保卫民族独立和爱好和平的人民一定会取得最后的胜利！
    亲爱的埃及弟兄们，英勇的战斗罢！中国学生和六亿中国人民和你们站在一起，世界上爱好和平的人民和你们站在一起，胜利是属于你们的！
    英雄的埃及人民万岁！
