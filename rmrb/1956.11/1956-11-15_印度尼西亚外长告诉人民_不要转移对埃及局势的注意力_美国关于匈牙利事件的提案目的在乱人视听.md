Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Tells People Not to Divert Attention from the Situation in Egypt; U.S. Proposal on Hungarian Events Aims to Confuse the Public

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 14th - Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani requested on the evening of the 12th that the Indonesian people not allow their attention to be diverted from the Middle East crisis.

He told a plenary session of the parliament that the situation in Egypt has a direct impact on Indonesia's security; this is "due to its geographical proximity and the principles it is fighting for, as well as the high-pressure tactics used by Britain and France."

He said that it is precisely for this reason that "the government asks to continue to pay attention to the further development of the Middle East situation and to ensure that our attention is not diverted from it."

In a speech to the parliament on the Hungarian events, Abdulgani explained that when the U.S. proposal on the Hungarian events was submitted to the United Nations General Assembly for a vote, Indonesia abstained because the draft resolution was largely intended to divert international attention from the much more serious issue of British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt.

He said that it would be irreconcilable if Indonesia were to approve a draft resolution that "does not substantially reflect the entire situation in Hungary" together with Britain, France, and Israel, who were declared aggressors.

### 印度尼西亚外长告诉人民  不要转移对埃及局势的注意力  美国关于匈牙利事件的提案目的在乱人视听

1956-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长告诉人民
    不要转移对埃及局势的注意力
    美国关于匈牙利事件的提案目的在乱人视听
    新华社雅加达14日电　印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼12日晚上要求印度尼西亚人民不要让自己的注意力从对中东危机上转移开去。
    他对国会全体会议说，埃及的形势对印度尼西亚的安全有直接的影响，这是“由于它在地理上接近和为之奋斗的原则，同时也由于英国和法国所使用的高压手段”。
    他说，正是为了这个缘故，“政府要求继续注意中东形势的进一步发展，并且使得我们对它的注意力不要转移”。
    阿卜杜加尼在向国会发表的关于匈牙利事件的演说中解释说，当美国提出的关于匈牙利事件的提案在联合国大会提付表决的时候，印度尼西亚弃权，这是因为这个决议草案在很大程度上是用来转移国际上对于更要严重得多的问题，即英、法、以侵略埃及问题的注意力的。
    他说，如果印度尼西亚和被宣布为侵略者的英国、法国和以色列一道批准“实质上并没有反映匈牙利整个形势”的决议草案，那是不调和的。
