Title: Israeli Troops Massing on Syrian and Jordanian Borders; Syria Asks the UN for a Swift and Impartial Investigation; Announces No Repair of Oil Pipelines Until Aggressor Troops Withdraw from Egypt

Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: Damascus News: Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar has once again telegraphed UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, pointing out that Israeli troops are massing on the Israeli-Syrian armistice line and the Israeli-Jordanian armistice line. Bitar also requested a "swift and impartial" inspection of the demilitarized zone and the Israeli-occupied territories.

It is also reported that Syrian Army Chief of Staff General Toufik Nizamuddin arrived in Amman, the capital of Jordan, on the 25th. He will hold talks with Jordanian military authorities.

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: TASS Beirut, November 26th: According to the Lebanese "Al Ayam" newspaper, the Lebanese Chargé d'affaires in Amman said in a report submitted to the Lebanese Foreign Minister that the situation there is becoming increasingly serious due to the concentration of Israeli troops on the Jordanian-Israeli border. The report calls for immediate measures to ease the situation on the Jordanian-Israeli border.

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: Damascus News: Syrian Minister of Public Works Majiddin Jabri issued a statement on November 26th, saying that the Syrian government insists on its decision not to allow repairs to the Iraq Petroleum Company's pipeline until foreign troops withdraw from Egyptian territory.

The minister made this statement after meeting with representatives of the oil company. The Iraq Petroleum Company's pipeline through Syria was cut off after the start of the Anglo-French military aggression against Egypt.

### 以色列军队陈兵叙约边境  叙利亚要联合国迅速进行公正调查  并宣布在侵略军撤出埃及前不准修理油管

1956-11-28
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列军队陈兵叙约边境
    叙利亚要联合国迅速进行公正调查
    并宣布在侵略军撤出埃及前不准修理油管
    新华社27日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚外交部长比塔尔再次打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，指出以色列军队正在以色列—叙利亚停战线和以色列—约旦停战线上集结。比塔尔并且要求“迅速而又公正地”对非军事区和以色列所占据的内地进行视察。
    另悉，叙利亚陆军参谋长图菲克·尼扎姆丁将军已经在25日到达约旦首都安曼。他将同约旦军事当局举行会谈。
    新华社27日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特26日讯：据黎巴嫩“军旗报”报道，黎巴嫩驻安曼代办在一份送交黎巴嫩外交部长的报告中说，由于以色列在约旦—以色列的边境上集结军队，那里的情况日趋严重。报告要求立即采取措施缓和约旦—以色列边境上的局势。
    新华社27日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚公共工程部部长麦吉德丁·贾布里11月26日发表声明说，叙利亚政府坚持它的决定，在外国军队撤出埃及领土之前不允许进行修理伊拉克石油公司的输油管的工作。
    这位部长在接见石油公司的代表以后发表了这个声明。伊拉克石油公司经过叙利亚的输油管是在英法侵略埃及的军事行动开始以后被切断的。
