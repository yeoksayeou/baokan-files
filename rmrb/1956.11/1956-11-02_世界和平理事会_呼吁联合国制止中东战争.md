Title: World Peace Council Calls on United Nations to Stop the Middle East War
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, October 31st: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council has telegraphed the United Nations, requesting it to find ways to halt military actions in the Middle East.

The telegram states: The World Peace Council is now reflecting public opinion's unease about the sudden tension in the Near East. This unease is particularly acute because in the past two years the people of all countries have initially affirmed the positive results of easing international tensions, and they have the right to expect further easing of international tensions.

The telegram states that efforts by the United Nations have given hope for a peaceful resolution to the Middle East situation and the Suez issue, while the use of force against Egypt could trigger a war, the consequences and scope of which are unpredictable.

The World Peace Council requests the United Nations to use its full prestige to halt military actions and resolutely calls on the countries concerned to resolve their differences through negotiations.

The World Peace Council states that it will fully support the United Nations' initiatives aimed at halting military actions and holding negotiations.

### 世界和平理事会  呼吁联合国制止中东战争

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会
    呼吁联合国制止中东战争
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社维也纳10月31日讯：世界和平理事会书记处打电报给联合国，要求它设法制止中东的军事行动。
    电报说：世界和平理事会现在反映社会舆论对近中东突然紧张的局势的不安情绪。这种不安情绪之所以特别严重，是因为各国人民在过去两年中已经初步肯定了缓和国际紧张局势的良好结果，他们有权利期待进一步缓和国际紧张局势。
    电报说，联合国方面的努力会使得中东局势和苏伊士问题有希望获得和平解决，而使用武力对待埃及却可能引起战争，这场战争的后果和范围是无法预料的。
    世界和平理事会要求联合国运用它的全部威望来制止军事行动，并且坚决号召有关各国通过谈判解决它们之间的分歧。
    世界和平理事会表示，它将以全力支持联合国的目的在于制止军事行动和举行谈判的倡议。
