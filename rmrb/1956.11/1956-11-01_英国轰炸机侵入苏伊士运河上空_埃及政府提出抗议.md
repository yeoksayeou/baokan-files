Title: British Bombers Invade Suez Canal Airspace, Egyptian Government Protests
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 5

    British Bombers Invade Suez Canal Airspace
    Egyptian Government Protests
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 31st: The Egyptian Foreign Ministry announced that the Egyptian government lodged a protest with Britain on October 30th regarding the intrusion of two British bombers into the airspace above the Suez Canal.
    According to the Middle East News Agency, two British "Canberra" jet bombers intruded into the airspace over the Suez Canal area. One of them was hit by Egyptian anti-aircraft fire and landed in Jordan after being damaged.

### 英国轰炸机侵入苏伊士运河上空  埃及政府提出抗议

1956-11-01
第5版()
专栏：

    英国轰炸机侵入苏伊士运河上空
    埃及政府提出抗议
    新华社31日讯　开罗消息：埃及外交部宣布，埃及政府在10月30日已经就英国两架轰炸机侵入苏伊士运河上空一事向英国提出抗议。
    据中东通讯社报道，两架英国“堪培拉”式喷气轰炸机曾经侵入苏伊士运河地区上空飞行，其中有一架被埃及高射炮火击中。这架被击伤的飞机降落在约旦。
