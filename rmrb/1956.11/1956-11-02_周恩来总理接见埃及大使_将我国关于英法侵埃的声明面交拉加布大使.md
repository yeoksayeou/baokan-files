Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Egyptian Ambassador and Personally Hands Over China's Statement on the Anglo-French Invasion of Egypt to Ambassador Ragab
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Ragab, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Egypt, on the afternoon of the 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai personally handed over the statement of the Government of the People's Republic of China regarding the Anglo-French armed invasion of Egypt to Ambassador Ragab and held talks with Ambassador Ragab.

### 周恩来总理接见埃及大使  将我国关于英法侵埃的声明面交拉加布大使

1956-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见埃及大使
    将我国关于英法侵埃的声明面交拉加布大使
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，于1日下午接见埃及共和国特命全权大使拉加布，将中华人民共和国政府关于英、法武装侵略埃及的声明，面交拉加布大使，并与拉加布大使进行了交谈。
