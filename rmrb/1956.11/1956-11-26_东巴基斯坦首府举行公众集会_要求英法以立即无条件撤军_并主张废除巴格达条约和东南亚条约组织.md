Title: Public Gathering Held in East Pakistan's Capital, Demanding Immediate and Unconditional Withdrawal of Troops by Britain, France, and Israel, and Advocating for the Abolition of the Baghdad Pact and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 24th: The Pakistan Democratic Party convened a public gathering in Dhaka on the evening of the 23rd to commemorate Anti-Imperialism Day. The gathering passed a resolution demanding the immediate and unconditional withdrawal of all troops of Britain, France, and Israel from Egyptian territory.

The gathering also demanded that the British, French, and Israeli aggressors not only compensate the Egyptians for the loss of life and property but also compensate the world for the losses suffered in trade.

The gathering called for the abolition of the Baghdad Pact, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, and all military alliance treaties, urging the Pakistani government to implement an independent foreign policy to ensure close cooperation with all countries, especially Asian and African countries.

The Central Executive Committee of the Pakistan Ulama Council passed a resolution in Karachi on the same day. The resolution congratulated the people of Pakistan for the valuable assistance given to the cause of the Egyptian people and urged them to continue the struggle.

The resolution also called on the government to withdraw from the British Commonwealth and expel Britain from the Baghdad Pact.

### 东巴基斯坦首府举行公众集会  要求英法以立即无条件撤军  并主张废除巴格达条约和东南亚条约组织

1956-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦首府举行公众集会
    要求英法以立即无条件撤军
    并主张废除巴格达条约和东南亚条约组织
    据新华社卡拉奇24日电　巴基斯坦民主党为了纪念反对帝国主义日，23日晚上在达卡召集了一次公众集会。集会通过决议，要求英法以的全部军队立即无条件地撤出埃及领土。
    集会还要求英法以侵略者不仅要赔偿埃及人的生命和财产的损失，而且还要赔偿全世界在贸易上所遭受的损失。
    集会要求废除巴格达条约、东南亚条约组织和一切军事同盟条约，促请巴基斯坦政府实行独立的外交政策，以便保证同所有的国家，特别是亚非国家进行密切的合作。
    巴基斯坦贤哲会中央执行委员会同一天在卡拉奇通过一个决议。决议祝贺巴基斯坦人民给与埃及人民的事业的宝贵帮助，并且要求他们继续进行斗争。
    决议还要求政府脱离英联邦，并且把英国逐出巴格达条约。
