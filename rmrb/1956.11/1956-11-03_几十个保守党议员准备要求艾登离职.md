Title: Dozens of Conservative MPs Prepare to Demand Eden's Resignation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 2nd. Altringham, a member of the British Conservative Party, revealed to the "News Chronicle" that at least thirty Conservative MPs who oppose the government's policy towards Egypt are preparing to propose a motion calling for Prime Minister Eden's resignation.

### 几十个保守党议员准备要求艾登离职

1956-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    几十个保守党议员准备要求艾登离职
    新华社2日讯　伦敦消息：英国保守党党员奥耳特林尼姆向“新闻纪事报”透露，至少有三十个反对政府对埃及政策的保守党议员在准备提出一项要艾登首相离职的动议。
