Title: Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, issued a statement today protesting the armed aggression against Egypt by British and French imperialism.

    Li Jishen said: "We feel boundless anger at the criminal actions of British and French armed aggression against Egypt; in the 1950s, the British and French imperialists actually adopted such shameful gunboat policy to invade a sovereign country. This is a serious provocation to all mankind, and an insult to world civilization!"
    Li Jishen said: "Egypt's territorial sovereignty has been seriously violated, and peace in the Middle East and the world is facing a serious threat. We demand that the United Nations Security Council immediately take effective measures to resolutely stop the aggression of Britain, France and their tool, Israel. Whether the United Nations can assume the responsibility of sanctioning aggressor countries and maintaining peace is facing a severe test!
    All peace-loving and justice-upholding countries and people in the world unanimously support Egypt's just struggle to resist aggression and defend peace. Since the Suez Canal issue arose, we, the Chinese people, have resolutely stood by Egypt's side. We pledge to do our utmost to support the Egyptian people, and we believe that the Egyptian people's just struggle will surely be victorious. Here, we warn the British and French governments to quickly pull back from the brink, otherwise, they will set themselves on fire and regret it too late!"

### 中国国民党革命委员会主席  李济深发表谈话

1956-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中国国民党革命委员会主席
    李济深发表谈话
    新华社2日讯　中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深今天发表谈话，抗议英、法帝国主义对埃及的武装侵略。
    李济深说：“我们对于英法武装侵略埃及的罪恶行动感到无比的愤怒；在二十世纪五十年代，英法帝国主义者居然还采取这种可耻的炮舰政策来侵犯一个主权国家，这是对全人类的一个严重挑衅，是对世界文明的一种玷辱！”
    李济深说：“埃及的领土主权已经受到严重的侵犯，中东及世界和平正遭到严重的威胁，我们要求联合国安全理事会立即采取有效的措施，坚决制止英法及其工具以色列的侵略行动。联合国能不能负起制裁侵略国家、维持和平的责任，正面临着严重的考验！
    全世界爱好和平主张正义的国家和人民一致支持埃及反抗侵略保卫和平的正义斗争。我们中国人民自苏伊士运河问题一发生，就坚决站在埃及一边，我们誓以全力支援埃及人民，并相信埃及人民的正义斗争一定胜利。在这里，我们警告英法政府赶快悬崖勒马，否则，必将引火自焚，后悔莫及！”
