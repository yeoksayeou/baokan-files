Title: Spring
Author: Dashidorzhiin Natsagdorj
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 8

    Spring
    Mongolian People's Republic　Dashidorzhiin Natsagdorj
    Editor's Note: November 17th marks the 50th anniversary of the birth of Dashidorzhiin Natsagdorj (1906-1937), a founder of modern Mongolian literature. To commemorate this writer, we are publishing a translation of his poem "Spring," written in 1937.
    Thousands upon thousands of springs
      radiate light under the eternal sky,
    In the beautiful country of Mongolia,
      changing, year after year.
    In springtime, carefree and at ease,
      the human heart is peaceful and serene—
    The seedlings of grasses and trees have turned green,
      and horses neigh happily.
    When the sun sends its warmth,
      the jade-green snow is melting away,
    The world is also changing its appearance,
      truly taking on a new look.
    When the forests put forth green leaves,
      children jump and frolic about—
    Even the aged and hoary elders
      grow young again.
    The wild geese on the grasslands cackle,
      they will fly here to be our guests,
    The herders sit in their tents
      listening to them, deeply moved.
    The small river in the valley begins to flow murmuring,
      the stream from the source also begins to sing softly,
    Echoing the music of spring,
      flocks of lambs bleat on the grassland.
    How pure is the breath that blows,
      how warm, how sweet-scented,
    It reminds us of the past,
      awakening so many thoughts within us.
    The young people on the grasslands decorate
      our wealthy, temporary yurts,
    Fathers and mothers bend their backs,
      above the cradles of their children.
      (Translated by Lu Yong) (With picture)
    Dashidorzhiin Natsagdorj

### 春天
达什多仁·那蔡都日
1956-11-17
第8版()
专栏：

    春天
    蒙古人民共和国　达什多仁·那蔡都日
    编者按　11月17日是蒙古人民共和国作家达什多仁·那蔡都日（1906—1937）诞生五十周年，为了纪念这位蒙古现代文学的奠基人，现将他在1937年写的诗“春天”译出发表。
    千千万万个春天
      在永恒的天空下放射出光芒，
    在美丽的国家蒙古，
      变换着，一年又一年。
    春天里自由自在，
      人的心灵和平宁静——
    草木的幼苗已经发绿，
      马儿欢快地嘶鸣。
    当太阳送来了温暖，
      绿玉色的雪正在消融，
    世界也在改变面貌，
      真正焕然一新。
    当树林发出绿色，
      孩子们乱跳欢蹦，——
    即使年迈苍苍的老人
      也要变得年轻。
    草原上的大雁咯咯地叫着，
      它们要飞到我们这里来作客，
    牧人们坐在帐篷里
      听着它们，深深感动。
    山谷里的小河开始潺潺流淌，
      源头的流水也开始淙淙低唱，
    应和着春天的音乐，
      群群羊羔在草原上咩咩地叫着。
    吹来的气息多么洁净，
      多么温暖，多么甜香，
    它使我们想起过去，
      唤起我们这么多的思想。
    草原上的青年人装点着
      我们的富有的临时篷帐，
    父亲和母亲弯下了腰身，
      在孩子的摇篮之上。
      （卢永译）（附图片）
    达什多仁·那蔡都日
