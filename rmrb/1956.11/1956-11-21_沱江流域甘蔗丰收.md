Title: Abundant Sugarcane Harvest in the Tuo River Basin
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 20th -- The 400,000 mu of sugarcane in the Tuo River basin in Sichuan has yielded a bumper harvest, with an average yield of 6,000 jin per mu, an increase of about 60% over last year, the highest yield since liberation. Some agricultural cooperatives have achieved an average yield of about 10,000 jin per mu.
Currently, harvesting is underway in various locations.

### 沱江流域甘蔗丰收

1956-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    沱江流域甘蔗丰收
    新华社成都20日电　四川沱江流域四十万亩甘蔗丰收，每亩平均产量达六千斤，比去年增产60％左右，是解放以来产量最高的一年。有些农业社每亩平均产量达到一万斤左右。
    目前各地正在紧张收割。
