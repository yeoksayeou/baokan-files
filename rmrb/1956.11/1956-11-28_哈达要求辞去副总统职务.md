Title: Hatta Requests to Resign as Vice President
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 27th - Jakarta newspapers today published Vice President Hatta's letter to Parliament Speaker Sadono. In the letter, Vice President Hatta informed the Speaker that he would formally resign from December 1st of this year.
    According to newspaper reports, a copy of this letter was also sent to President Sukarno and the Cabinet.
    The Indonesian Nationalist Party expressed "strong disagreement" with Hatta's resignation. It is reported that some parties support Hatta's resignation, while others do not.

### 哈达要求辞去副总统职务

1956-11-28
第6版()
专栏：

    哈达要求辞去副总统职务
    据新华社雅加达27日电　雅加达各报今天刊登了哈达副总统致国会议长沙多诺的信。哈达副总统在信里通知议长说，他将从本年12月1日起正式辞职。
    据报纸消息说，这封信的副本还抄送给了苏加诺总统和内阁。
    印度尼西亚国民党对哈达的辞职表示“非常不同意”。据悉，有些党派表示赞成哈达辞职，另外有一些党派不赞成。
