Title: Deng Xiaoping Receives International Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th - Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping received the International Youth Delegation this morning and answered questions raised by the delegation members. Accompanying him were Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Hu Yaobang, Director of the Xinhua News Agency Wu Lengxi, and Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhao Yimin, among others.

### 邓小平接见国际青年代表团

1956-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平接见国际青年代表团
    新华社17日讯　国务院副总理邓小平今天上午接见了国际青年代表团，解答了代表团团员们提出的问题。陪同接见的，有高等教育部部长杨秀峰、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、新华通讯社社长吴冷西和中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏等人。
