Title: Molotov Appointed Minister of State Control

Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 21st: TASS reported from Moscow on the 21st that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union appointed Comrade V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, as Minister of State Control of the Soviet Union, and therefore relieved Comrade Zhevronkov of his post as Minister of State Control of the Soviet Union.

### 莫洛托夫任国家监察部部长

1956-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫任国家监察部部长
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科21日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命苏联部长会议第一副主席维·米·莫洛托夫同志为苏联国家监察部部长，因此免去热沃隆科夫同志的苏联国家监察部部长职务。
