Title: King Suramarit Cables Chairman Mao to Thank Him for Congratulations on Cambodian Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 1
    
Xinhua News Agency, 24th. King Suramarit of Cambodia sent a cable to Chairman Mao Zedong to thank him for his congratulations on the third anniversary of Cambodian independence. The cable is as follows:

Your Excellency:

I am deeply moved by your congratulatory message of November 7, 1956, sent in your personal name and in the name of the friendly people of China, to me and the people of my country on the occasion of the third anniversary of Cambodian independence. I sincerely thank you and ask you to accept the congratulations of the Cambodian people and myself on your personal happiness and the prosperity of the entire Chinese people. I am convinced that the friendly relations between our nations will improve day by day.

                                        Norodom Suramarit
                                    Phnom Penh, November 18, 1956

### 苏拉玛里特国王电毛主席  感谢对柬埔寨独立节的祝贺

1956-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉玛里特国王电毛主席
    感谢对柬埔寨独立节的祝贺
    新华社24日讯　柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特回电感谢毛泽东主席祝贺柬埔寨第三届独立节。电文如下：阁下：
    我对您在1956年11月7日以您个人名义及中国友邦人民的名义，在柬埔寨第三届独立节之际向我和我国人民所致的贺电深为感动。我诚恳地感谢您，并请接受柬埔寨人民和我对您个人幸福及全中国人民的富强的祝贺。我深信我们民族间的友好关系将日渐改进。
                                        诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特
                                    1956年11月18日于金边
