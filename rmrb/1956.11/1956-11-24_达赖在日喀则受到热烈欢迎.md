Title: Dalai Lama Receives Warm Welcome in Xigaze
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, November 23rd: The Dalai Lama, accompanied by the Panchen Erdeni, paid homage to the mausoleum stupa of the Ninth Panchen Lama at the Zhaxilhunbu Monastery in Xigaze on the 21st. The Dalai Lama recited scriptures, presented a hada, and performed a pilgrimage in front of the mausoleum stupa.

The Dalai Lama arrived in Xigaze that afternoon. This was his first visit to Xigaze, and he will travel to India with the Panchen Erdeni to attend the 2,500th anniversary commemoration of the passing of Sakyamuni.

Upon the Dalai Lama's arrival in Xigaze, he was warmly welcomed by more than 40,000 monks and laypeople in the Xigaze region, with the welcoming procession stretching for ten li. The Panchen Erdeni personally welcomed the Dalai Lama at the west gate of Zhaxilhunbu Monastery. They exchanged greetings by touching their heads together, wishing each other peace, and then entered the Gongse Palace hand in hand to rest. In the evening, the Panchen Erdeni held a banquet to entertain the Dalai Lama and his entourage.

### 达赖在日喀则受到热烈欢迎

1956-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖在日喀则受到热烈欢迎
    新华社拉萨23日电　达赖喇嘛在班禅额尔德尼陪同下，21日在日喀则札什伦布寺朝拜了第九世班禅的陵塔。达赖喇嘛在陵塔前诵经、献哈达和进行了朝拜。
    达赖喇嘛是在当天下午到达的，他还是第一次到日喀则，他将和班禅额尔德尼一道前往印度参加释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念大会。
    达赖喇嘛到达日喀则时，受到日喀则地区四万多僧俗人民欢迎，欢迎行列长达十里。班禅额尔德尼亲自在札什伦布寺西门欢迎达赖喇嘛，他们见面时行了碰头礼，互祝平安，随即携手进入公色宫殿休息。晚间，班禅额尔德尼举行了宴会，招待达赖喇嘛和他的随行人员。
