Title: French People Hold Anti-Fascist Struggle Day; Soviet Communist Party Sends Telegram to French Communist Party Expressing Brotherly Solidarity
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 14th (TASS): The French people nationwide, responding to the call of the General Confederation of Labor (CGT), held an anti-fascist struggle day on the 13th. The CGT had called on all French workers, regardless of their union affiliation or differing political views, to act together to block the path of fascism and prevent reactionary elements from infringing upon the people's democratic organizations.
    On this day, personnel from many French enterprises and institutions organized rallies and meetings, temporarily ceased work, and are taking other measures to strengthen the united front of the working people and prevent fascist provocations.
    The French CGT once again issued a call on the 13th, urging the working class to stop fascist elements from attacking labor committees, trade unions, or other democratic organizations' meeting places and the activists of these organizations.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 14th (TASS): Today, "Pravda" published a telegram sent by the Central Committee of the CPSU to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party.
    In the telegram, the Central Committee of the CPSU stated: "We express the fraternal solidarity of the Soviet Communists with our French Communist comrades in their struggle against the provocative attacks of fascist thugs."
    "By giving the enemies of French democracy and the freedom of the people the response they deserve, the French Communists, the working class, and all progressive people are making an important contribution to the common cause of defending peace and socialism. The Soviet people praise their heroic and tenacious spirit."
    "We firmly believe that the united action of the French Communists, Socialists, and all democratic people will surely block the path of reactionary and fascist forces."

### 法国人民举行反法西斯斗争日  苏共致电法共表示兄弟的声援

1956-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    法国人民举行反法西斯斗争日
    苏共致电法共表示兄弟的声援
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：法国全国响应总工会的号召在13日举行了反法西斯斗争日。总工会曾经号召法国全体劳动人民，不分他们属于那一个工会，也不管在种种政治问题上有什么不同的看法，大家共同行动，堵住法西斯主义的道路，并且防止反动分子侵犯人民的民主组织。
    这一天，法国许多企业部门和机关的人员组织了集会和会议，暂时停止了工作，并且正在采取其它措施来加强劳动人民的统一战线，防止法西斯的挑衅。
    法国总工会在13日再次发出号召，号召工人阶级制止法西斯分子袭击劳工委员会、工会或其他民主组织的会所和这些组织的积极分子。
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：今天“真理报”刊载了苏共中央拍给法国共产党中央委员会的一份电报。
    苏共中央在电报中说：我们表示，在我们的法国共产党同志为反对法西斯暴徒的挑衅性进攻而进行的斗争中，苏联共产党人给予他们兄弟的声援。
    法国共产党人、工人阶级和一切进步人士，正在给予法国民主和人民自由的敌人应有的反击，从而为捍卫和平和社会主义的共同事业作出重要贡献。苏联人民赞扬他们这种英勇顽强的精神。
    我们坚定地相信，法国共产党人、社会党人和一切民主人士团结一致的行动，一定可以堵住反动和法西斯势力的道路。
