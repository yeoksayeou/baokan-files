Title: Aidit Sends Telegram to Kádár to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 6th. Antara News Agency reported on November 6th that the General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, sent a telegram to the First Secretary of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Kádár, saying that the Indonesian Communists congratulate the Hungarian people on their victory. The Hungarian people have successfully suppressed the counter-revolutionaries' open attempt to restore capitalism in Hungary. This victory has strengthened the Arab countries' struggle against the Anglo-French aggression.

The telegram also stated that the Indonesian Communists fully support the Hungarian Revolutionary Government's policy of requesting Soviet military assistance.

Aidit said that the Soviet army has helped the Hungarian people achieve liberation for the second time.

### 艾地致电卡达尔表示祝贺

1956-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    艾地致电卡达尔表示祝贺
    新华社雅加达6日电　安塔拉通讯社11月6日报道，印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地在给匈牙利劳动人民党第一书记卡达尔发出的一封电报中说，印度尼西亚共产党人祝贺匈牙利人民的胜利。匈牙利人民已经成功地镇压了反革命分子打算在匈牙利使资本主义复辟的公开企图。这个胜利已经加强了阿拉伯国家反对英法侵略的斗争。
    电报又说，印度尼西亚共产党人完全支持匈牙利革命政府请求苏军援助的政策。
    艾地说，苏联军队已经是第二次帮助匈牙利人民获得解放。
