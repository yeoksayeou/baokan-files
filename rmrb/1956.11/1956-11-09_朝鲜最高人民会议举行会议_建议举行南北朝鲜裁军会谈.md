Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Holds Meeting; Suggests North-South Korean Disarmament Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 7th: The Korean Supreme People's Assembly held a meeting in Pyongyang from November 5th to 7th. At the opening session, Chairman Li Young delivered a report on the visit of the delegation of the Korean Supreme People's Assembly to the Soviet Union. He said that this visit further strengthened the friendship and cooperative relations between the people of Korea and the Soviet Union, and at the same time helped to consolidate and develop the internationalist unity between the two countries, making contributions to the cause of safeguarding peace.
    Finally, the meeting adopted a resolution on the report of the delegation of the Supreme People's Assembly to the Soviet Union and a declaration in support of the Soviet Supreme Soviet's appeal to the parliaments of all countries for disarmament. This declaration condemned the Anglo-French aggression against Egypt.
    The meeting also adopted a letter to the South Korean National Assembly and people from all walks of life, calling on the Korean people to oppose Syngman Rhee's policy of expanding arms and preparing for war, and suggesting that the South Korean National Assembly and various social strata send representatives to jointly discuss holding North-South Korean disarmament talks and other issues.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议举行会议  建议举行南北朝鲜裁军会谈

1956-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议举行会议
    建议举行南北朝鲜裁军会谈
    新华社平壤7日电　朝鲜最高人民会议11月5日到7日在平壤举行，在开幕会上李英议长作了关于朝鲜最高人民会议代表团访问苏联的报告。他说，这次访问进一步加强了朝苏两国人民之间的友谊和合作关系，同时有助于两国之间国际主义团结的巩固和发展，对维护和平事业作出贡献。
    最后，会议通过了关于最高人民会议访苏代表团的报告的决议和支持苏联最高苏维埃致各国议会的裁军呼吁书的宣言。在这项宣言中谴责了英法对埃及的侵略行为。
    会议还通过了给南朝鲜国会和各阶层人士的信，号召朝鲜人民反对李承晚的扩军备战政策，建议南朝鲜国会和各社会阶层派出代表共同磋商举行南北朝鲜裁军会谈等问题。
