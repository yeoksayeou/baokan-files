Title: A Blood Debt of British Colonialists: Hundreds of Patriots Brutally Murdered in Cyprus in One Year
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 6

    A Blood Debt of British Colonialists
    Hundreds of Patriots Brutally Murdered in Cyprus in One Year
    Xinhua News Agency, November 28th -- According to a TASS report: November 26th marked the one-year anniversary of the British colonial authorities declaring a "state of emergency" in Cyprus. According to newspaper materials, in this year, eight local anti-British patriotic fighters were sentenced to death by the British authorities under the regulations of the "state of emergency," twenty-one people were sentenced to life imprisonment, and two hundred and sixteen people were sentenced to imprisonment for varying terms, their imprisonment periods totaling six hundred and sixty-one years.

### 英国殖民者的一笔血债  在塞浦路斯一年残害数百爱国者

1956-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

    英国殖民者的一笔血债
    在塞浦路斯一年残害数百爱国者
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社报道：11月26日是英国殖民当局在塞浦路斯宣布“紧急状态”的一周年。据报纸材料，在这一年中，有八个当地的反英爱国志士被英国当局根据“紧急状态”时期的条例判处了死刑，二十一人被判处无期徒刑，二百十六人被判处了不同期限的监禁，他们的监禁期加在一起长达六百六十一年。
