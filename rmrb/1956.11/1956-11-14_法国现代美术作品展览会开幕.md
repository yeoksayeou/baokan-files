Title: French Modern Art Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: An exhibition of reproductions of oil paintings, pastels, watercolors, and tapestry designs by famous French modern artists Picasso, Maurice Utrillo, Raoul Dufy, Georges Rouault, Henri Matisse, Jean Lurçat, Pierre Bonnard, Marquet, Fernand Léger, and others, has been on display at the China Artists Association Exhibition Hall in Beijing since the 10th of this month. The exhibition is scheduled to end on November 25th.

### 法国现代美术作品展览会开幕

1956-11-14
第7版()
专栏：

    法国现代美术作品展览会开幕
    据新华社讯　本月10日起在北京中国美术家协会展览馆展出法国现代著名画家毕加索、摩利斯·郁特利罗、拉奥·杜飞、乔治·鲁奥、亨利·马蒂斯、让·吕尔萨、披爱尔·勃那尔、马尔盖、费尔南·雷瑞等的油画、粉画、水彩、壁毡设计的复制品。展览会预定在11月25日结束。
