Title: High-Aged Members of the Central Research Institute for Culture and History Commemorate Mr. Sun Yat-sen

Author:

Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Over sixty high-aged members of the Central Research Institute for Culture and History held a commemorative meeting this morning for the 90th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. The venue was filled with articles, poems, calligraphy and paintings created by the elderly members to commemorate Mr. Sun Yat-sen. The commemorative meeting was presided over by Ye Gongchuo, Vice-Director of the Central Research Institute for Culture and History.

The elderly members who participated in the commemorative meeting were all contemporaries of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Some had followed Mr. Sun Yat-sen for many years, while others had direct or indirect contact with him. Everyone had vivid memories of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary deeds during his lifetime. At the commemorative meeting, all the elderly members stood and paid their respects to the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Ye Gongchuo and many other elderly members spoke at the meeting, expressing their respect and remembrance for Mr. Sun Yat-sen. (Photo attached)

"Exhibition of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Life and Deeds" opened on November 12th in the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. The picture shows the entrance of the exhibition hall.

### 中央文史研究馆的高龄老人纪念孙中山先生

1956-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

    中央文史研究馆的高龄老人纪念孙中山先生
    新华社12日讯　中央文史研究馆六十多位高龄老人，在今天上午举行孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念会。会场上布满了老人们为了纪念孙中山先生而作的文章和诗词书画。纪念会由中央文史研究馆副馆长叶恭绰主持。
    参加纪念会的老人们，都是孙中山先生同时代的人，其中有的追随中山先生多年，有的直接或间接地接触过中山先生，大家对孙中山先生生前革命事迹记忆犹新。在纪念会上，全体老人起立向孙中山先生遗像致敬。叶恭绰和许多老人都在会上讲了话，表示对孙中山先生的崇敬和怀念之情。（附图片）
    “孙中山先生生平事迹展览会”11月12日在北京中山公园内中山堂开幕。图为展览会场入口处。
            新华社记者　傅　军摄
