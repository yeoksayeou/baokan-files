Author: Bai Shui, Mingde
Date: 1956-11-27

    Dividing the Family
  　Bai Shui, Mingde
    In many areas, there is a lack of new moral education for farmers, and some farmers still retain strong selfish and self-interested views. When they divide the family property, brothers and sisters-in-law compete for property, but they shirk the responsibility of supporting their parents.

### 分家（图片）
白水　明德
1956-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    分家
  　白水　明德
    有不少地区对农民缺乏新的道德教育，有的农民仍然保存着浓厚的自私自利的观点。他们在分家时，兄弟妯娌之间互相争抢财产，但对应尽的供养父母的责任，又互相推诿。
