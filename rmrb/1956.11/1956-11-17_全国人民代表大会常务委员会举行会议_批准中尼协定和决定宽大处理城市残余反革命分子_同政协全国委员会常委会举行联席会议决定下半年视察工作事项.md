Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting: Approves Sino-Nepalese Agreement and Decides on Lenient Treatment of Urban Remaining Counter-Revolutionaries; Holds Joint Meeting with CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee to Decide on Inspection Matters for the Second Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The 51st meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) and the 31st meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) National Committee were held jointly on the 16th.

The joint meeting heard an explanation from Zhang Su, a member and Deputy Secretary-General of the NPC Standing Committee, discussed the inspection work for the second half of 1956, and passed a notice to NPC deputies and CPPCC National Committee members regarding relevant matters for the inspection work in the second half of 1956. The notice stated: The inspection work for NPC deputies and CPPCC National Committee members will commence on November 20th and will last for approximately one month; deputies and members who are old and frail or unable to leave their work posts may conduct inspections locally or not participate in inspections; those working and studying in schools may conduct inspections during the winter vacation; the inspection items, locations, targets, as well as the cadres and masses to be received, shall be selected independently by the deputies and members; in order to deeply understand the situation and discover problems, the inspection areas and items should not be too numerous, and attention should be paid to listening to the opinions of the people and lower-level cadres; inspection work by deputies and members may be conducted jointly or separately; deputies and members inspecting the same area should, as far as possible, conduct joint inspections; problems discovered by deputies and members during the inspection work may be handed over to the relevant local authorities for handling, and may also be reported to the NPC Standing Committee for handling if necessary; reports may or may not be written after the inspection, and they may be written collectively or individually, covering comprehensive issues or individual issues.

The 51st meeting of the NPC Standing Committee, after hearing an explanation from Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, deliberated on the State Council's proposal to establish the Third Ministry of Machine Building, decided to establish the Third Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China, and approved the list of appointments and removals proposed by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai. The meeting also approved the list of appointments and removals proposed by the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

After hearing an explanation from Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, the 51st meeting of the NPC Standing Committee passed a resolution on approving the agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal on maintaining friendly relations and on commerce and transportation between the Tibet region of China and Nepal. After the meeting, Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong approved the aforementioned agreement in accordance with the decision of the 51st meeting of the NPC Standing Committee.

The 51st meeting of the NPC Standing Committee, after hearing an explanation from Minister of Public Security Luo Ruiqing, passed a decision on leniently treating and resettling urban remaining counter-revolutionary elements. The meeting also passed a decision that all supervision of counter-revolutionary elements shall be sentenced by the People's Courts.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  批准中尼协定和决定宽大处理城市残余反革命分子  同政协全国委员会常委会举行联席会议决定下半年视察工作事项

1956-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    批准中尼协定和决定宽大处理城市残余反革命分子
    同政协全国委员会常委会举行联席会议决定下半年视察工作事项
    新华社16日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第三十一次会议十六日举行联席会议。
    联席会议在听取了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、副秘书长张苏的说明后，讨论了1956年下半年视察工作问题，通过了发给全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员的1956年下半年视察工作的有关事项的通知。这个通知说：全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员的视察工作在11月20日开始，视察时间为一个月左右；年老体弱或者不能离开工作岗位的，可以就地视察或者不参加视察，在学校工作和学习的，可以在寒假期间进行视察；视察的项目、地点、对象以及接见的干部和群众都由代表和委员自行选定；为了深入地了解情况和发现问题，视察的地区和项目不宜太多，并应注意听取人民群众和下层干部的意见；代表和委员视察工作可以联合进行，也可以单独进行，到同一地区视察的代表和委员，能够联合视察的，尽可能联合起来进行视察；代表和委员在视察工作中发现的问题可以交由当地有关机关处理，必要时也可以报告全国人民代表大会常务委员会处理；视察以后可以写报告，也可以不写，可以集体写，也可以个人写，可以写全面的问题，也可以写个别的问题。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议在听取了国务院总理周恩来的说明后，审议了国务院提请决定设立第三机械工业部的议案，决定设立中华人民共和国第三机械工业部并且通过了国务院总理周恩来提请决定任免的名单。会议还通过了最高人民检察院提请批准任免的名单。
    在听取了外交部副部长章汉夫说明后，全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议通过了关于批准中华人民共和国和尼泊尔王国保持友好关系以及关于中国西藏地方和尼泊尔之间的通商和交通的协定的决议。会后，中华人民共和国主席毛泽东已根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议的决定，批准了上述协定。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议在听取了公安部部长罗瑞卿的说明后，通过了关于宽大处理和安置城市残余反革命分子的决定。会议还通过了关于对反革命分子的管制一律由人民法院判决的决定。
