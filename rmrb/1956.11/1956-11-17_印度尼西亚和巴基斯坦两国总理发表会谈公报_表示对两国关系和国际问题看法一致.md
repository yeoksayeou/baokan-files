Title: Indonesian and Pakistani Prime Ministers Issue Joint Communiqué, Expressing Agreement on Bilateral Relations and International Issues

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 16th: Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo left Karachi this morning to return to Jakarta.

The Indonesian Prime Minister, who arrived here yesterday, held talks with Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy and other Pakistani leaders.

A joint communiqué issued last night stated: "The two Prime Ministers discussed issues of mutual concern to Indonesia and Pakistan. They reached a consensus on measures that could be taken to develop closer political, economic, and cultural relations between Indonesia and Pakistan. They further discussed the current international situation and issues related to world peace and international cooperation, on which their views were consistent."

The communiqué stated: "The Indonesian Prime Minister personally handed over to the Pakistani Prime Minister the joint statement recently issued in Delhi following the Colombo Powers conference of the Prime Ministers of Burma, Ceylon, Indonesia, and India. The two Prime Ministers took this opportunity to discuss this joint statement. It was agreed that the Pakistani government would present its views to the Indonesian Prime Minister so that these views could be conveyed to the other Colombo Powers participating in the Delhi conference."

The communiqué stated that the Indonesian Prime Minister invited the Pakistani Prime Minister to visit Indonesia. The Pakistani Prime Minister has gladly accepted the invitation.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 15th: Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, who is visiting Pakistan at the invitation of the Pakistani government, arrived in Karachi from New Delhi this morning.

Upon arrival at the airport, Sastroamidjojo told reporters that he hoped this visit would allow him to become more familiar with Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy and provide him with an opportunity to exchange views with the Pakistani Prime Minister and other leaders on "issues of common concern to both countries and some international issues that are now troubling us."

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo said that the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of India, Ceylon, Burma, and Indonesia in New Delhi on November 14th would have its impact on international affairs because "we have worked hard for peace in the spirit of the Bandung Declaration."

Sastroamidjojo also said that he hoped to inform the Pakistani Prime Minister of the nature of the discussions at the Colombo Powers conference.

### 印度尼西亚和巴基斯坦两国总理发表会谈公报  表示对两国关系和国际问题看法一致

1956-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和巴基斯坦两国总理发表会谈公报
    表示对两国关系和国际问题看法一致
    新华社卡拉奇16日电　印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约今天上午离开卡拉奇回雅加达。
    昨天到达这里的印度尼西亚总理同巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底以及其他巴基斯坦的领袖进行了会谈。
    昨天晚间发表的一个联合公报说：“两位总理讨论了同印度尼西亚和巴基斯坦共同有关的问题。他们也就为了在印度尼西亚和巴基斯坦之间发展更密切的政治、经济和文化关系而可能采取的措施取得了一致的意见。他们进一步讨论了目前的国际形势以及有关世界和平和国际合作的问题，在这些问题上他们的看法是一致的。”
    公报说：“印度尼西亚总理亲自把缅甸、锡兰、印度尼西亚和印度的总理最近在德里举行科伦坡国家会议之后发表的联合声明交给了巴基斯坦总理。两位总理借此机会讨论了这个联合声明。经取得协议，巴基斯坦政府将向印度尼西亚总理提出它的看法，以便把这种看法转达参加德里会议的其他科伦坡国家。”
    公报说，印度尼西亚总理邀请巴基斯坦总理访问印度尼西亚。巴基斯坦总理已经欣然接受邀请。
    新华社卡拉奇15日电　应巴基斯坦政府邀请到巴基斯坦访问的印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约今天上午从新德里到达卡拉奇。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约在机场上对记者说，他希望他这次访问将会使他同巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底更加熟悉，并且可以使他有机会同巴基斯坦总理以及其他领袖就两国“共同关心的问题以及现在使我们伤脑筋的一些国际问题”交换意见。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理说，印度、锡兰、缅甸和印度尼西亚总理们11月14日在新德里发表的联合声明将会在国际事务中有它的影响，因为“我们已经根据万隆宣言的精神努力谋求和平”。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约还说，他希望把这次科伦坡国家会议讨论的性质告诉巴基斯坦总理。
