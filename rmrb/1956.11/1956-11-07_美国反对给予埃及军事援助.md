Title: The United States Opposes Providing Military Aid to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Washington dispatch: The U.S. government stated on the evening of the 5th that it opposes the Soviet Union's proposal for the Soviet Union and the United States to jointly provide military aid to Egypt to stop aggression.

White House Press Secretary Hagerty issued a statement saying that any plan to use Soviet troops in the Middle East would be opposed by the United States, and it is "impossible to imagine" that the United States would agree to any such proposal.

### 美国反对给予埃及军事援助

1956-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    美国反对给予埃及军事援助
    新华社6日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府在5日晚上声明，它反对苏联关于苏美两国共同给予埃及军事援助以制止侵略的建议。
    白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂发表声明说，任何在中东使用苏联军队的打算都将遭到美国的反对，“不可能想像”美国会同意任何这类的建议。
