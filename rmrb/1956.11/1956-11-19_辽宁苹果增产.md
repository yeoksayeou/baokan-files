Title: Liaoning Apple Production Increases

Author:

Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 18th: Liaoning has had a bumper harvest of apples, with total production expected to reach more than 178,000 tons, an increase of more than 20,000 tons compared to last year. More than 48,000 tons of apples have already been transported and sold to markets across the country.

This year, the supply range of Liaoning apples will be wider than in previous years. People in most provinces and cities across the country will be able to enjoy Liaoning apples.

### 辽宁苹果增产

1956-11-19
第3版()
专栏：

    辽宁苹果增产
    新华社沈阳18日电　辽宁苹果丰收，总产量可以达到十七万八千多吨，比去年增产两万多吨。现在已经有四万八千多吨苹果运销到国内各地市场。
    今年辽宁苹果的供应范围将比往年扩大。全国绝大部分省、市人民都可以吃到辽宁苹果。
