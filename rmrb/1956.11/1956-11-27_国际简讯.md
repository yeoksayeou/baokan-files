International Briefs

    Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe Performs in Leningrad
    The Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe's tour in Leningrad concluded on the 25th, with all five performances being a great success.

    East Germany Gifts Four Fishing Vessels to Vietnam
    The German Democratic Republic presented four fishing vessels to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the 25th in Haiphong. A representative from the Vietnamese Ministry of Industry thanked the German Democratic Republic for its selfless assistance at the handover ceremony.
    These four fishing vessels, each with a deadweight of thirty-eight tons, are the first new-style fishing vessels using marine machinery owned by the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Soviet Military Delegation Departs Yugoslavia for Home
    The Soviet military delegation, having visited Yugoslavia for twenty days at the invitation of the Yugoslav government, left Belgrade for home on November 25th.

    Yugoslav Mining Experts to Work in Burma
    Five Yugoslav mining experts recently arrived in Rangoon. They will mainly be engaged in the exploration of copper mines in northern Burma.

    Czechoslovak Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Germany for Home
    The Czechoslovak parliamentary delegation, led by Chairman of the National Assembly, Fierlinger, returned to Prague from the German Democratic Republic on the 26th. They arrived in Berlin on the 18th for a friendly visit.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1956-11-27
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    上海京剧院在列宁格勒演出
    上海京剧院在列宁格勒的巡回演出已经在25日结束，前后的五次演出都获得了很大的成功。
    民主德国赠送越南四艘渔轮
    德意志民主共和国赠给越南民主共和国的四艘渔轮25日在海防移交。越南工业部代表在移交仪式上感谢民主德国的无私援助。
    这四艘各载重三十八吨的渔轮是越南民主共和国拥有的第一批用海洋机器捕鱼的新式渔轮。
    苏联军事代表团离南回国
    苏联军事代表团应邀到南斯拉夫访问了二十天以后，11月25日离开贝尔格莱德回国。
    南斯拉夫采矿专家到缅甸工作
    五个南斯拉夫采矿专家最近到达仰光。他们主要是将着手进行缅甸北部的铜矿的勘探工作。
    捷议会代表团离德回国
    捷克斯洛伐克议会代表团在国民议会主席费林格率领下，26日从德意志民主共和国回到布拉格。他们是18日到柏林作友好访问的。
                      （新华社）
