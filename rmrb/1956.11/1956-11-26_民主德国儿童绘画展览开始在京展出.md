Title: Exhibition of Children's Paintings from the German Democratic Republic Opens in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th. An exhibition of children's paintings from the German Democratic Republic opened today at the Water Pavilion Exhibition Hall in Zhongshan Park, Beijing.

In these works, children from the German Democratic Republic, aged four to eighteen, depict a wide variety of themes—their own lives, games, festivals, and the construction work of their motherland. All the works express the children's lively and cheerful optimistic attitude.

Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children Kang Keqing, Committee members Shen Zijiou and Liao Mengxing, and art teachers from some primary and secondary schools in Beijing visited the exhibition today.

German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China Gipner, First Secretary of the Embassy Deichs, and members of the German Democratic Republic Education Delegation visiting our country also visited the exhibition. This exhibition is being held in accordance with the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 民主德国儿童绘画展览开始在京展出

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国儿童绘画展览开始在京展出
    新华社24日讯　德意志民主共和国儿童绘画展览今天在北京中山公园水榭展览厅开始展出。
    四岁到十八岁的德意志民主共和国儿童们在这些作品中描绘出了多种多样的主题——他们自己的生活、游戏、节日，以及他们祖国的建设工作。全部作品表现出了孩子们活泼愉快的乐观态度。
    教育部副部长韦悫，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清、委员沈兹九、廖梦醒，以及北京市部分中小学的美术教师今天参观了展览。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使纪普纳、大使馆一等秘书戴克斯，以及应邀在我国访问的德意志民主共和国教育考察团人员也参观了展览。这次展览是按照中德文化合作协定1956年执行计划规定举办的。
