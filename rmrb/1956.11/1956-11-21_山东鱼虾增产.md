Title: Shandong Increases Fish and Shrimp Production
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 20th: Shandong Province's fish production has increased compared to last year. According to statistics from the Shandong Provincial Aquatic Products Bureau, from the beginning of the year to the end of October, the province harvested a total of 264,770 tons of fish and shrimp, an increase of 6.29% compared to the same period last year.

This year's abundant aquatic harvest in Shandong Province was achieved on the basis of realizing the collectivization of fisheries throughout the province. At present, there are 806 fishery production cooperatives in the province, with cooperative membership accounting for 92% of the total number of fishing households in the province, of which 83% participate in advanced cooperatives. These fishery cooperatives have all added fishing gear and carried out production reforms this year.

### 山东鱼虾增产

1956-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    山东鱼虾增产
    据新华社济南20日电　山东省鱼产量比去年增加。据山东省水产局统计，从年初到10月底，全省共捕获鱼虾二十六万四千七百七十吨，比去年同期增加了6.29％。
    今年山东省的水产丰收，是在全省实现渔业合作化的基础上获得的。现在，全省共有八百零六个渔业生产合作社，入社渔户占全省渔户总数的92％，其中参加高级社的渔户占83％。这些渔业社今年都增添了捕鱼工具和进行了生产改革。
