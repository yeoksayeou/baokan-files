Title: Yugoslav Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The head of the Yugoslav Economic Delegation, Kiro Gligorov, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. The delegation will discuss the conclusion of a trade agreement for 1957 with the Chinese government.
    Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and representatives from the Ministry of Communications and the People's Bank of China were at the airport to welcome them.
    Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, was also at the airport to welcome them.
    Members of the delegation have arrived in Beijing successively in the past few days.

### 南斯拉夫经济代表团到达北京

1956-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫经济代表团到达北京
    新华社26日讯　南斯拉夫经济代表团团长基罗·格里戈罗夫在今日下午到达北京。代表团将和中国政府商谈缔结1957年度贸易协定等问题。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部副部长孔原和交通部、中国人民银行的代表。
    南斯拉夫驻我国大使波波维奇也在机场欢迎。
    代表团团员已在日前陆续到达北京。
