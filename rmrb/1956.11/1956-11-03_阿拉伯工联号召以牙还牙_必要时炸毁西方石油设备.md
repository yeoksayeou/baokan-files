Title: Arab Labor Union Calls for Tit-for-Tat, If Necessary, Blow Up Western Oil Facilities
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 2nd – The International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions called on Arab countries on November 1st to blow up Western oil facilities if necessary, to cut off oil transportation.

This trade union federation, which has great influence in Arab countries, also called on Arab countries to destroy all foreign military bases that could be used against Egypt.

The call of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions was broadcast by Cairo Radio on November 1. The Federation called for three major actions to be taken immediately:

(1) "Do not let a single drop of oil flow to the invading army or the army attempting to invade, even if this leads to the destruction of oil facilities in all Arab countries—Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Syria, Lebanon, Yemen, and Muscat, etc."

(2) "Blow up foreign military bases in Arab countries that can be used for aggression."

(3) "Immediately stop the shipment and supply work of ships and aircraft of aggressor countries."

### 阿拉伯工联号召以牙还牙  必要时炸毁西方石油设备

1956-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯工联号召以牙还牙
    必要时炸毁西方石油设备
    新华社2日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯工人国际联合会11月1日号召阿拉伯国家在必要的时候炸毁西方的石油设备，以断绝石油的输送。
    这个在阿拉伯国家中有很大影响的工会联合会还号召阿拉伯国家毁掉可以被用来对付埃及的一切外国军事基地。
    阿拉伯工人国际联合会的号召11月1日由开罗电台广播。联合会号召马上采取三大行动：
    （一）“不要让一滴石油流到侵略军或者企图进行侵略的军队那里，即使这会导致在所有阿拉伯国家——沙特阿拉伯、伊拉克、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、也门和穆斯喀特等地——的石油设施的炸毁。”
    （二）“炸毁可以被用来进行侵略的设在阿拉伯国家里的外国军事基地。”
    （三）“立即停止侵略国的船只和飞机的装运和供应工作。”
