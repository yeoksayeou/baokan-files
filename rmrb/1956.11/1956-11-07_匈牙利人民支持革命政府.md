Title: Hungarian People Support the Revolutionary Government
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 5th: The Hungarian people are widely supporting the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, according to TASS.

According to reports from various regions, order and tranquility are being restored. Workers' councils in Szombathely, Nagykanizsa, Szolnok, and other cities have issued statements supporting the Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government. Industrial enterprises in many cities have begun work, and public transportation has resumed. Railway transport has been restored in some states. The Pécs radio station in southern Hungary broadcast a letter to the working people of Baranya County from the county party committee of the Working People's Party and the county council. The letter said that the discredited Nagy government had been overthrown and that the Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, composed of Kádár and others, would realize the legitimate demands of the working people after the restoration of domestic order. It also called on all regions to support the revolutionary government.

### 匈牙利人民支持革命政府

1956-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民支持革命政府
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯5日讯：匈牙利人民广泛支持匈牙利工农革命政府。
    据各地消息，秩序和安宁正在恢复中。松巴西利、那吉卡尼萨、索尔诺克和其它城市的工人委员会纷纷发表声明，支持工农革命政府。许多城市的工业企业已经开始工作，公共运输也恢复了。有一些州的铁路运输已经恢复了。匈牙利南部的贝奇电台广播了巴拉涅亚州劳动人民党党委和州会议的告劳动人民书。告人民书说，威信扫地的纳吉政府被推翻了，由卡达尔等人组成的工农革命政府在国内秩序恢复以后，将实现劳动人民正当的要求。它同时号召各地支持革命政府。
