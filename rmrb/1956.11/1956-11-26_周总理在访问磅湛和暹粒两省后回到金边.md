Title: Premier Zhou Returns to Phnom Penh After Visiting Kampong Cham and Siem Reap Provinces
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, November 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage returned to Phnom Penh at 5:00 PM today after visiting Kampong Cham and Siem Reap provinces. On the 24th, they made this visit aboard two special planes belonging to the King and Queen of Cambodia, accompanied by Prince Sihanouk and Royal Army Chief of Staff Lon Nol.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage visited the Chup Rubber Plantation in eastern Kampong Cham Province. They toured the rubber latex processing factory, rubber plantations, and a nursery of rubber saplings that could supply 12,000 hectares of rubber plantations. According to the French owner, this rubber plantation is the second largest in the world.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage traveled by car from Siem Reap to the ruins of Angkor, the ancient capital city founded in the 9th century. They visited Angkor Wat and Angkor Thom. There are magnificent ancient stone buildings and towering ancient pagodas here, with Buddhist statues and exquisite reliefs carved on the walls of the cloisters. They are still preserved today, becoming a brilliant monument to Cambodian architectural art.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long were given a grand welcome by the local governments and people of the two provinces. When Premier Zhou's plane landed at Kampong Cham Airport, the governors of the two provinces stepped forward to welcome him, placing garlands around Premier Zhou's neck and presenting him with bouquets.

In his speech to the welcoming crowd, Premier Zhou Enlai thanked the people of the two places for their reception. He congratulated the people of Kampong Cham Province for their economic development; and praised the people of Siem Reap Province for maintaining and developing their national culture despite a long period of foreign rule.

When Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage disembarked at Phnom Penh Airport today, honor guards presented arms in salute. Tens of thousands of people lined the streets to welcome them back to Phnom Penh.

### 周总理在访问磅湛和暹粒两省后回到金边

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在访问磅湛和暹粒两省后回到金边
    新华社金边25日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员访问了磅湛省和暹粒省以后，已经在今天下午五时回到金边。24日，他们乘坐柬埔寨国王和王后的两架专机，在西哈努克亲王和皇家陆军参谋长朗诺的陪同下作了这次访问。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员来到磅湛省东部的舒普橡胶园。他们参观了橡胶乳加工工厂、橡树园，以及可供应一万二千公顷的橡胶园的橡树苗圃。据法国园主说，这个橡胶园是世界上第二个最大的。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员乘汽车从暹粒前往建立于九世纪的吴哥古都的遗址。他们参观了吴哥寺和吴哥城。这里有宏伟的古代石砌建筑物和耸立的古塔，回廊四壁刻有佛像和精美的浮雕。它们至今还保存着，成为柬埔寨建筑艺术辉煌的纪念物。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理受到了两个省的地方政府和人民的盛大欢迎。当周总理所乘的飞机在磅湛机场着陆的时候，两个省的省长上前欢迎，给周总理戴上花环，并且送上花束。
    周恩来总理在对欢迎他的群众讲话时感谢两个地方人民的接待。他对于磅湛省人民的经济发展表示祝贺；对于暹粒省人民虽然经过长期的外国统治仍然保持和发展了民族文化，表示赞扬。
    当周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员今天回到金边机场下机时，仪仗队举枪致敬。成千上万的人排列在街道上欢迎他们回到金边。
