Title: A British Bomber Intruded into Syrian Airspace and Was Shot Down
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th dispatch from Damascus: The Damascus Radio broadcast on November 6th that Syrian jet fighters shot down a British "Canberra" twin-engine jet bomber that had intruded into Syrian airspace.

The broadcast said that Syrian jet fighters fired on the invading British jet bomber, and the British plane immediately caught fire and crashed into the border area between Syria and Lebanon.

The broadcast said that two British pilots who parachuted from the destroyed bomber have been arrested.

### 一架英国的轰炸机侵入叙领空被击落

1956-11-08
第6版()
专栏：

    一架英国的轰炸机侵入叙领空被击落
    新华社7日讯　大马士革消息：据大马士革电台11月6日广播，叙利亚喷气战斗机击落了一架侵入叙利亚领空的英国“堪培拉”双引擎喷气轰炸机。
    广播说，叙利亚喷气战斗机向前来袭击的英国喷气轰炸机开火，这架英国飞机随即着火坠到叙利亚和黎巴嫩边境地区。
    广播说，从这架被击毁的轰炸机上跳伞下来的两名英国驾驶员已被逮捕。
