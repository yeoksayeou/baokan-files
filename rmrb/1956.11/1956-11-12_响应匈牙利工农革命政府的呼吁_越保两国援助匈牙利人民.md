Title: Responding to the Appeal of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, Vietnam and Bulgaria Aid the Hungarian People
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 8

    Responding to the Appeal of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government
    Vietnam and Bulgaria Aid the Hungarian People
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th - The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has announced that it will donate goods worth three million rubles to the Hungarian people.
    Prime Minister Pham Van Dong sent a telegram to Prime Minister Kádár yesterday, congratulating him on the establishment of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government and announcing the above-mentioned aid. The telegram said: The Vietnamese people warmly congratulate the Hungarian people on their victory and wish the Hungarian people greater achievements in implementing the fifteen-point program of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government.
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Sofia, 10th (Bulgarian Telegraph Agency) - The Bulgarian Council of Ministers issued a communiqué stating that, in response to the appeal of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government and to express the Bulgarian people's warm sympathy for the fraternal Hungarian people, the Bulgarian government has decided to provide the Hungarian people with gratuitous fraternal assistance, immediately transporting the following materials to Hungary: 1,000 tons of sugar, 700 tons of rice, 1,000 tons of kidney beans, 100 tons of biscuits, 5,000 tons of cement, 2,000 cubic meters of broadleaf sawn timber, 2,000 cubic meters of broadleaf planks, 30,000 cubic meters of firewood, 26,000 tons of coal, and 3,000 tons of crude oil.
    In order to continue providing the Hungarian working people with medicines and other industrial and food products, the Central Council of the Bulgarian Red Cross has decided to raise funds to aid the fraternal Hungarian people through donations from the entire nation.

### 响应匈牙利工农革命政府的呼吁  越保两国援助匈牙利人民

1956-11-12
第8版()
专栏：

    响应匈牙利工农革命政府的呼吁
    越保两国援助匈牙利人民
    据新华社河内10日电　越南民主共和国政府宣布把价值三百万卢布的物资赠送给匈牙利人民。
    范文同总理昨天打电报给卡达尔总理，祝贺匈牙利工农革命政府成立，并且宣布上述援助。电文中说：越南人民热烈祝贺匈牙利人民的胜利，并且祝匈牙利人民在实现匈牙利工农革命政府的十五点纲领中取得更多的成就。
    新华社11日讯　据保加利亚通讯社10日讯：保加利亚部长会议发表公报说，保加利亚政府为响应匈牙利工农革命政府的呼吁和表示保加利亚人民对匈牙利兄弟人民的热烈的同情，决定给予匈牙利人民以无偿的兄弟援助，把下列物资立刻运往匈牙利：食糖一千吨、大米七百吨、菜豆一千吨、饼干一百吨、水泥五千吨、阔叶树锯材两千立方公尺、阔叶树木板两千立方公尺、劈柴三万立方公尺、煤两万六千吨、原油三千吨。
    为了今后继续给匈牙利劳动人民以药品和其他工业品和食品的援助，保加利亚红十字会中央理事会决定用全国人民捐献的办法来筹募救济匈牙利兄弟人民的基金。
