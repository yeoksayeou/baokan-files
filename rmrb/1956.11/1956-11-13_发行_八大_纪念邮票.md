Title: Issuing Commemorative Stamps for the "Eighth National Congress"
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency reported: The commemorative stamps for the "Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China" issued by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications went on sale on November 10th.

This set of stamps has three denominations and one design, printed in three different colors: dark green, bright red, and purplish red.

The design features Tiananmen in the very center, with a gear directly below it. Linked by silk ribbons, agricultural crops extend symmetrically to the left and right. The words "Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China" are written horizontally at the top.

### 发行“八大”纪念邮票

1956-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    发行“八大”纪念邮票
    据新华社讯　邮电部发行的“中国共产党第八次全国代表大会”纪念邮票，在11月10日开始发售。
    这一组邮票共三种面值，是一个图案，印成墨绿、大红、紫红三种不同的颜色。
    图案的正当中是天安门，下边正中有一个齿轮，用丝绶连结着农作物对称地向左右两边伸开，上边横写“中国共产党第八次全国代表大会”字样。
