Title: Indian Military Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: The Indian military goodwill delegation headed by General J.N. Chaudhuri, left Beijing by plane at 8:00 this morning, and will return home via Guangzhou.

Colonel Gan Mai, Deputy Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India, and Colonel Wang Qinwu of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense, accompanied the delegation to Guangzhou.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 1st: The Indian military goodwill delegation headed by General J.N. Chaudhuri, made a stopover in Wuhan this morning on its way from Beijing to Guangzhou by special plane.

Kong Qingde, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan, hosted a luncheon for the distinguished guests at the airport restaurant.

Among those present at the meal were Xiong Fei, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, and many generals and colonels from various services and arms of the garrison.

Afterwards, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Commander Kong Qingde, visited the construction site of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge.

At 2:20 p.m., the distinguished guests left Wuhan for Guangzhou by plane.

### 印度军事代表团离北京回国

1956-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印度军事代表团离北京回国
    据新华社1日讯　以卓·纳·乔杜里将军为首的印度军事友好访华代表团，今天上午八时乘飞机离开北京，将取道广州回国。
    中国驻印度副武官甘迈上校、国防部外事处王秦五上校陪同代表团去广州。
    新华社武汉1日电　以卓·纳·乔杜里将军为首的印度军事友好访华代表团，今日上午自北京乘专机去广州途中，曾在武汉停留。
    中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长孔庆德中将在机场餐厅设便宴招待贵宾。
    陪同进餐的有武汉市熊飞副市长和驻军各军种、兵种的将军们和校官们多人。
    接着，贵宾们在孔庆德中将陪同下，到武汉长江大桥工地参观。
    下午二时二十分，贵宾们离武汉飞广州。
