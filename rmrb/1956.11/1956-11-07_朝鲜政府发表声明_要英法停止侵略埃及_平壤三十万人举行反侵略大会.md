Title: The Korean Government Issues a Statement Demanding that Britain and France Cease Their Aggression Against Egypt; 300,000 People in Pyongyang Hold an Anti-Aggression Rally
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang Telegram – The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a statement on the 3rd, stating that the military actions taken by Britain and France against Egypt are acts of aggression against Egypt and a blatant violation of the United Nations Charter.

　　The statement said that the Korean government firmly demands that the British and French governments immediately cease their military actions against Egypt and promptly begin peaceful consultations on the Suez Canal issue, based on the national interests of the Egyptian people.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang Telegram – Over 300,000 people in Pyongyang held a rally on the morning of the 4th to oppose the British and French imperialist aggression against Egypt. The meeting passed a resolution protesting against the British and French governments' aggression against Egypt.

　　After the meeting, a parade was held. The marching crowds shouted slogans, demanding that the British and French governments immediately cease their acts of aggression against Egypt, and also demanding that the American aggressors get out of Korea.

　　Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Kim Tu-bong, and other state leaders, as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties and social organizations, attended the meeting.

### 朝鲜政府发表声明  要英法停止侵略埃及  平壤三十万人举行反侵略大会

1956-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府发表声明
    要英法停止侵略埃及
    平壤三十万人举行反侵略大会
    据新华社平壤电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府3日发表声明说，英法对埃及采取的军事行动是对埃及的侵略行为，是对联合国宪章的公然违反。
    声明说，朝鲜政府坚决要求英法政府立即停止对埃及的军事行动，在符合埃及人民的民族利益的基础上，迅速开始和平协商苏伊士运河问题。
    据新华社平壤电　平壤三十多万人民4日上午举行反对英法帝国主义侵略埃及的大会。会上通过了抗议英法政府侵略埃及的决议书。
    会后举行了游行，游行的队伍高呼口号，要求英法政府立即停止对埃及的侵略行动，并且要求美国侵略者滚出朝鲜。
    金日成首相、金枓奉委员长等国家领导人和各民主党派、社会团体负责人出席了大会。
