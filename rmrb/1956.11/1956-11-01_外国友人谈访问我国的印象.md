Title: Foreign Friends Talk About Their Impressions of Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 6

Indian Speaker of the People's House

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 29th - According to the Press Trust of India, the Speaker of the People's House, Mr. Ayengar, head of the Indian Parliamentary Delegation who just returned from a visit to China, said in Calcutta that China has a unified government and it is very efficient. Throughout China, there is no phenomenon of personal rule, and the government is working to strengthen its ties with the people, so that people from all walks of life can cooperate with the government.
    He said that a characteristic of China is that there is a coalition government in which some ministers are held by people other than communists.
    Ayengar also mentioned that Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, has accepted an invitation to visit India after the Indian general election next year.

Finnish Speaker of Parliament

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 29th - Helsinki News: The Finnish Parliament organized a report meeting on the overseas visits of the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation on the 25th. Speaker of Parliament Sukselainen reported on his impressions of visiting China.
    He said that the delegation conducted extensive tours and visits in China, saw China's agriculture and rapidly developing modern industry, and also saw the former national capitalists. They are very satisfied with the current situation. In agriculture, the state is using all means to support the new agricultural program.
    Sukselainen also talked about Chairman Mao Zedong's meeting with the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation and the delegation's attendance at the session of the National People's Congress of China, where they heard Premier Zhou Enlai's report. He said that the general impression is that Chinese politicians are wise and energetic people. China is in the midst of a climax of construction and the realization of new aspirations.

### 外国友人谈访问我国的印象

1956-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    外国友人谈访问我国的印象
    印度人民院议长
    新华社新德里29日电　据印度报业托辣斯消息，刚从中国访问回国的印度国会代表团团长、人民院议长阿延加尔在加尔各答说，中国有着统一的政府，它的效率很高。在全中国，没有个人统治的现象，政府正努力加强和人民的联系，使各阶层的人民和政府合作。
    他说，中国的一个特征是，那里存在着一种联合政府，有些部长是由共产党以外的人士担任的。
    阿延加尔还提到，中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇已经接受邀请，将在明年印度大选结束后访问印度。
    芬兰议会议长
    新华社布拉格29日电　赫尔辛基消息：芬兰议会在25日组织了关于芬兰议会代表团出国访问的报告会。议长苏克西莱宁报告了访华观感。
    他说，代表团在中国进行了广泛的参观和访问，看到了中国的农业和正在迅速发展的现代工业，也看到了过去的民族资本家。他们对目前的情况很满意。在农业方面，国家正在用一切办法来支持新的农业纲要。
    苏克西莱宁还谈到毛泽东主席接见芬兰议会代表团和代表团出席中国人民代表大会会议听到周恩来总理作报告的情况。他说，总的印象是中国政治家都是明智和精力充沛的人。中国全国正处于建设和实现新的愿望的高潮中。
