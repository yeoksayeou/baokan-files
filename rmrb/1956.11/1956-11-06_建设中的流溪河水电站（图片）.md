Title: The Liuxi River Hydropower Station Under Construction (Photo)
Author: Chen Fubei
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 3

    The Liuxi River Hydropower Station Under Construction
    A hydroelectric power station is being built on the upper reaches of the Liuxi River in the north of Guangzhou. This hydropower station was surveyed and designed by Chinese engineering and technical personnel under the guidance of Soviet experts. Its installed capacity is over 40,000 kilowatts. The picture shows a corner of the Liuxi River Hydropower Station construction site.
                                 Photo by Chen Fubei (Southern Daily)

### 建设中的流溪河水电站（图片）
陈福北
1956-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

    建设中的流溪河水电站
    在广州市北部流溪河的上游，正兴建一个水力发电站。这个水电站是在苏联专家指导下，由我国工程技术人员勘测设计的。它的装机容量在四万瓩以上。图为流溪河水电站工地的一角。
                                 陈福北摄（南方日报社稿）
