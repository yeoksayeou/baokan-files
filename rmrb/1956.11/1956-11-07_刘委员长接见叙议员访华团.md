Title: Chairman Liu Receives Syrian Parliamentary Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th—Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Syrian parliamentary delegation headed by Ali Buzo this afternoon.

The hosts and guests exchanged views on the situation in the Middle East. Chairman Liu Shaoqi expressed that the Chinese people resolutely support the just struggle of the Egyptian people and all Arab people against the Anglo-French imperialist aggression. Head of Delegation Ali Buzo expressed his gratitude for this.

Present at the meeting were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Su and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association.

During the meeting, the delegation presented gifts to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. (Attached picture)

### 刘委员长接见叙议员访华团

1956-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    刘委员长接见叙议员访华团
    新华社6日讯　刘少奇委员长今天下午接见了以阿里·布佐为首的叙利亚议员访华团。
    宾主就中东形势进行了交谈。刘少奇委员长表示，中国人民坚决支持埃及人民以及全体阿拉伯人民反抗英法帝国主义侵略的正义斗争。阿里·布佐团长对此表示感谢。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、李维汉、陈叔通，副秘书长张苏、余心清，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉。
    接见时，访华团向刘少奇委员长赠送了礼品。（附图片）
