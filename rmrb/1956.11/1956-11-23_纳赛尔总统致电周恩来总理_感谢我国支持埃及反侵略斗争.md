Title: President Nasser Cables Premier Zhou Enlai, Thanking Our Country for Supporting Egypt's Anti-Aggression Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: The Embassy of the Republic of Egypt in China today delivered a telegram from President Nasser to Premier Zhou Enlai to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China. The full text of President Nasser's telegram is as follows:

    Your telegram has deeply moved me and the Egyptian people.

    Your support for our struggle to safeguard freedom and independence has strengthened our confidence in our just cause. This makes us even more determined to crush the imperialist attempts by Britain, France, and their henchman Israel to infringe upon Egyptian sovereignty.

    The support given by peace-loving people and governments of all countries to the struggle of our people against imperialist conspiracies, to safeguard their survival, and to defend their freedom, strengthens our belief that the future of mankind will shine with the light of peace, because the barbaric aggression of Britain, France, and Israel against the peaceful people of Egypt, and their actions that completely disregard all humanitarian considerations, highlight the world's people's love of peace and hatred of aggression. This once again proves that world public opinion supports the struggle of small countries to safeguard freedom and independence.

    I thank you and the great Chinese people for the sincere help of the cash donation, and I thank you again for your cooperation in our struggle against imperialist conspiracies.
　　　　　
　 Gamal Abdel Nasser

### 纳赛尔总统致电周恩来总理  感谢我国支持埃及反侵略斗争

1956-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统致电周恩来总理
    感谢我国支持埃及反侵略斗争
    新华社22日讯　埃及共和国驻中国大使馆今天将纳赛尔总统致周恩来总理的电报一份，送交中华人民共和国外交部，纳赛尔总统的电报全文如下：
    你的来电深深地感动了我和埃及人民。
    你们对于我们为维护自由和独立而进行的斗争所给予的支持，加强了我们对自己的正义事业的信心。这使我们更有决心去粉碎英、法和他们的走卒以色列侵犯埃及主权的帝国主义企图。
    爱好和平的各国人民和政府对于我国人民面对帝国主义的阴谋，为维护自己的生存和捍卫自己的自由而进行的斗争所给予的支持，加强了我们的信念：人类的未来将闪耀着和平的光芒，因为英、法和以色列对埃及和平人民所进行的野蛮侵略，它们所采取的完全不顾一切人道考虑的行为，衬托出了世界人民对和平的热爱和对侵略的憎恨。这再一次证明：世界舆论是支持小国为维护自由和独立而进行的斗争的。
    我感谢你和伟大的中国人民赠送现金的真诚的帮助，并且再次感谢你们在我们反对帝国主义阴谋的斗争中所给予的合作。　　　　　
　  加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
