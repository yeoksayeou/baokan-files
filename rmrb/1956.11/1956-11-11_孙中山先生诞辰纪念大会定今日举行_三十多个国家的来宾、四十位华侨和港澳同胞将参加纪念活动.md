Title: Commemorative Meeting for the Birthday of Mr. Sun Yat-sen Scheduled for Today; Guests from More Than Thirty Countries, Forty Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Will Participate in the Commemorative Activities

Author:
Date: 1956-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. A commemorative meeting for the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen will be solemnly held in Beijing on the 11th.

The meeting will be attended by members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, representatives of the National People's Congress in Beijing, representatives of various democratic parties, people's organizations, government departments, universities and colleges in Beijing, and representatives of ethnic minorities. Representatives of overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots who were invited by the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen to participate in the commemorative meeting have arrived in Beijing successively.

Guests from more than thirty countries have been invited to participate in the commemorative meeting.

The Preparatory Committee also decided that on the 12th, the day of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birthday, commemorative meetings will be held in major cities across the country, provincial capitals, and autonomous region capitals.

Recently, various mass commemorative activities have been widely held in Beijing and throughout the country. The Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, and Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou have been renovated and are ready for people from all walks of life to visit.

The State Council recently issued a notice that on the anniversary of his birth, national flags will be widely flown in all municipalities directly under the central government, cities where provincial capitals are located, autonomous region capitals, and Lhasa to show commemoration.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. More than 150 guests from more than thirty countries will participate in the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen in Beijing.

As of the evening of the 10th, the foreign guests who have arrived in Beijing upon invitation include: U Ba Swe, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association; Thakin Khin Zaw, Director of the Burma People's Cultural Center; Ma Kun, former adjutant of Mr. Sun Yat-sen from Canada; Zeng Zhusen, Dean of the Chinese Department of the University of Indonesia; Mrs. Rasuna Said, member of the Indonesian Constituent Assembly; Matsumoto Jiichiro, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Miyazaki Seimin, a prominent figure in Japanese society; the North Korean delegation headed by Baek Nam-un, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; the Mongolian delegation headed by Avirmed, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia and member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party (members include Mrs. Sukhbaatar, member of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia and member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party); Duran, representative of the Swiss Red Cross, the Standing Committee of the International Red Cross, the Red Cross Society, and the International Committee of the Red Cross; Charles Coundrin, a British mechanical engineer; Phan Bi Thuan, a member of the National Assembly of Vietnam, and others. The Soviet delegation and the Tunisian delegation who will come to participate in the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen are on their way to Beijing.

Also participating in the commemorative activities are politicians, social activists, peace advocates, scientists, writers, and artists from various countries in Beijing. They come from the following countries: Soviet Union, Albania, Afghanistan, Australia, Austria, Bulgaria, Burma, Canada, Czechoslovakia, Denmark, Egypt, West Germany, Finland, France, East Germany, Greece, Hungary, India, Italy, Japan, North Korea, Mexico, Mongolia, the Netherlands, Norway, Pakistan, Poland, Romania, Sweden, Syria, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Vietnam. In addition, foreign guests from Guatemala and Thailand will participate in the commemoration of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birthday in Guangzhou.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Forty overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots from India, Indonesia, Burma, Japan, and Vietnam, who were invited by the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen to participate in the commemorative activities for Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birthday, have arrived in Beijing one after another.

The overseas Chinese who came back from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are He Qiu, Liang Jinjie, and Li Shiquan. He Qiu was the former vice chairman of the Haiphong League in Vietnam. The overseas Chinese who came back from India include Tan Yunshan, Dean of the Chinese College of the International University of India, and Liang Zizhi, and other seven people.

The Hong Kong and Macao compatriots who have returned are He Xian, Ke Ping, and other three people. He Xian was once the president of Kiang Wu Hospital founded by Mr. Sun Yat-sen in Macao and is currently the president of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce.

Dr. Huang Xinfang, who once led the student Northern Expedition team, and Zhou Ming, of the Sichuan Railway Protection Comrades Association, and other fourteen people arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 10th. Macao News: People from all walks of life in Macao are preparing to commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

The "Preparatory Committee of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce for the 90th Anniversary of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen" has been established.

The Macao Federation of Trade Unions and the Macao Democratic Women's Federation have decided to hold meetings on the 11th and 12th respectively to commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

In addition, teachers from various schools in Macao and the Macao Student Union have also formed preparatory committees for the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, preparing to hold commemorative activities separately.

### 孙中山先生诞辰纪念大会定今日举行  三十多个国家的来宾、四十位华侨和港澳同胞将参加纪念活动

1956-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    孙中山先生诞辰纪念大会定今日举行
    三十多个国家的来宾、四十位华侨和港澳同胞将参加纪念活动
  新华社10日讯　孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念大会11日将在北京隆重举行。
    大会将有中国人民政协全国委员会在京委员、全国人民代表大会在京代表、各民主党派、各人民团体以及政府各部门、北京各高等院校和少数民族的代表参加。应孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会邀请参加纪念大会的海外华侨和港澳同胞的代表，已陆续到京。
    有三十多个国家的来宾应邀参加纪念大会。
    纪念筹备委员会还决定，12日——孙中山先生诞辰那一天，全国各大城市和省会所在地及自治区首府所在地，将普遍举行纪念大会。
    北京和全国各地最近还广泛举行了各种群众性的纪念活动。北京、南京、上海、广州的中山纪念堂、中山陵、中山故居已整修一新，准备各界人民前往参谒。
    国务院最近发出通知，在诞辰纪念日，全国各直辖市、省会所在的城市、自治区首府所在地及拉萨等地，将普遍悬挂国旗，以示纪念。
    新华社10日讯　三十多个国家的一百五十多位来宾，将在北京参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念活动。
    到10日晚止，已经应邀到达北京的外宾有：缅中友好协会主席信波，缅甸人民文化馆主任德钦觉盛，加拿大的前中山先生副官马坤，印度尼西亚大学汉学院院长曾珠森，印度尼西亚制宪会议委员拉苏娜·赛依德夫人，日中友好协会会长松本治一郎，日本社会名流宫崎世民，以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院院长白南云为首的朝鲜代表团，以蒙古部长会议副主席、蒙古人民革命党中央委员阿维尔齐德为首的蒙古代表团（团员中有蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团委员、蒙古人民革命党中央委员苏赫—巴特尔夫人等人），瑞士红十字会、国际红十字会常设委员会、红十字会协会、红十字国际委员会代表杜郎，英国机械工程师堪勒斯·康德林，越南国会议员潘必遵等。前来参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念活动的苏联代表团和突尼斯代表团正在来北京途中。
    参加纪念活动的还有在北京的各国政治家、社会活动家、和平人士、科学家、作家、艺术家等。他们来自下列国家：苏联、阿尔巴尼亚、阿富汗、澳大利亚、奥地利、保加利亚、缅甸、加拿大、捷克斯洛伐克、丹麦、埃及、西德、芬兰、法国、民主德国、希腊、匈牙利、印度、意大利、日本、朝鲜、墨西哥、蒙古、荷兰、挪威、巴基斯坦、波兰、罗马尼亚、瑞典、叙利亚、英国、美国和越南等。此外，还有来自危地马拉和泰国的外宾将在广州参加孙中山先生诞辰纪念活动。
    新华社10日讯　应孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会的邀请，参加中山先生诞辰纪念活动的来自印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、日本、越南各地华侨和港澳同胞共四十人，已先后到达北京。
    从越南民主共和国回来的华侨有何球、梁锦杰、李世泉三人，何球是原越南海防同盟会副会长。从印度回来的华侨有印度国际大学中国学院院长谭云山、梁子质等七人。
    港澳同胞已回来的有何贤、柯平等三人，何贤曾经是孙中山先生在澳门创办的镜湖医院的院长，现任澳门中华总商会会长。
    曾经领导学生北伐队的黄新房博士和四川保路同志会的周明等十四人在今晚乘火车到达北京。
    据新华社广州10日电　澳门消息：澳门各界人民正在筹备纪念孙中山先生诞生九十周年。
    “澳门中华总商会纪念孙中山先生诞生九十周年筹备委员会”已经成立。
    澳门工会联合总会、澳门民主妇女联合会决定在11日、12日分别集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。
    此外，澳门各校教师和澳门学联也分别组成了纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年筹委会，准备分别举行纪念活动。
