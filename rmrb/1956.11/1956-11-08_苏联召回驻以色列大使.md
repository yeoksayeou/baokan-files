Title: The Soviet Union Recalls Its Ambassador to Israel
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th – TASS reports: According to a decision by the Soviet government, the Soviet Ambassador to Israel, Abramov, has been ordered to leave Israel and return to Moscow immediately. Abramov informed the Israeli Foreign Ministry of this order on November 6th. Abramov left Israel on the same day.

### 苏联召回驻以色列大使

1956-11-08
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联召回驻以色列大使
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：根据苏联政府的决定，苏联驻以色列大使阿布拉莫夫奉命离开以色列，立刻回莫斯科。阿布拉莫夫在11月6日把这个命令通知了以色列外交部。阿布拉莫夫在当天离开以色列。
