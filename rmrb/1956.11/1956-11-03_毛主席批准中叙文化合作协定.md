Title: Chairman Mao Approves Sino-Syrian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to the decision of the 49th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, ratified the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Syrian Republic on October 22, 1956.

### 毛主席批准中叙文化合作协定

1956-11-03
第3版()
专栏：

    毛主席批准中叙文化合作协定
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第四十九次会议的决定，于1956年10月22日批准了中华人民共和国政府和叙利亚共和国政府文化合作协定。
