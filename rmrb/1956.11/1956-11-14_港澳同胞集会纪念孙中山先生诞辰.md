Title: Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Gather to Commemorate Sun Yat-sen's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 13th: Hong Kong news: Compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macao have been gathering since November 11th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

	The Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions held a symposium on November 11th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth, attended by more than 100 representatives from various trade unions. Chen Yaocai, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, said at the meeting that Sun Yat-sen fought heroically for China's national independence, democratic unity for forty years and made great contributions to the Chinese revolution. He said that today the people of the whole country are fighting to build a socialist motherland, and we should learn from Sun Yat-sen's great revolutionary spirit to build a unified, prosperous and happy country.

	The Hong Kong Chinese Reform Association also held a memorial meeting on November 12th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth, with the participation of more than 200 people from all walks of life. Chen Pishi, Chairman of the meeting and a barrister in Hong Kong, delivered a speech, focusing on Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary ideals and the Three Principles of the People and their realization in New China today. He said that we can say to Chinese people overseas or in Taiwan that if they believe in Sun Yat-sen and his doctrines, then they should join us in building New China, and they can return to the mainland to see who is truly realizing Sun Yat-sen's ideals.

	Fei Yimin, the director of the Hong Kong "Ta Kung Pao," who was invited to attend the meeting, said in his speech that today, when our territory Taiwan is still occupied by foreign forces and world peace is still threatened, each of us, in order to truly commemorate Sun Yat-sen, must learn from his revolutionary spirit and strive for the socialist construction of the motherland and for the lasting peace of the world. Only in this way can we live up to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's expectations for the compatriots throughout the country.

	Tang Bingda, a business representative who attended the memorial meeting, said, "I hope that all compatriots with patriotic ideas and who support Mr. Sun Yat-sen will strengthen their unity in the future under the correct call of 'one family of patriots' and 'patriotism knows no order' to contribute to the peaceful reunification and great construction of the motherland."

	The Macao Chinese Education Association, the Democratic Women's Federation, the Chinese Students' Federation, the Art Research Association, and workers from many industries in Macao held separate gatherings on November 11th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 13th: Hong Kong news: The Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce held a memorial meeting on the afternoon of November 12th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. More than 500 people attended the memorial meeting, including the heads of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, the Kiang Wu Hospital, the Tung Sin Tong Charitable Society, representatives of various organizations, veteran members of the Tongmenghui, and friends and fellow villagers of Sun Yat-sen during his lifetime.

	Ma Man Kei, Vice Chairman of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, said in his speech: "Mr. Sun Yat-sen was a great patriot who loved China and saved China all his life. All Chinese people should love the motherland, realize the reunification of the motherland under the banner of 'one family of patriots,' and build the motherland into a more prosperous and powerful country."

	A dinner party was held after the memorial meeting.

### 港澳同胞集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰

1956-11-14
第2版()
专栏：

    港澳同胞集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰
    据新华社广州13日电　香港消息：香港和澳门各界同胞从11日开始，纷纷集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。
    港九工会联合会在11日举行了有各个工会代表一百多人出席的孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念座谈会。港九工会联合会理事长陈耀材在会上讲话说，孙中山先生为中国民族独立、民主统一英勇奋斗了四十年，对中国革命有很大贡献。他说，今天全国人民正在为建设社会主义的祖国而奋斗，我们应当学习孙中山先生的伟大革命精神，来建设统一富强幸福的国家。
    香港华人革新协会在12日也举行了孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念会，这个纪念会有各界人士二百多人参加。会议主席、香港大律师陈丕士首先致词，他着重介绍了孙中山先生的革命理想和革命的三民主义，今天在新中国获得实现的情况。他说，我们可以对在海外或台湾的中国人说，如果他们信仰孙中山和信奉孙中山的学说，那么他们就应当同我们一起建设新中国，他们可以回到大陆上看看是谁在真正地实现孙中山先生的理想。
    应邀参加会议的香港“大公报”社长费彝民发言说，在我们领土台湾还有外力侵占和世界和平仍受到威胁的今天，我们每个人，为了真正纪念孙中山先生，必须学习他的革命精神，努力为祖国社会主义建设、为争取世界持久和平而奋斗，这样才算不辜负中山先生当年对全国同胞的期望。
    参加这个纪念会的工商界人士汤秉达说，我希望一切具有爱国观念和拥护孙中山先生的同胞，今后在爱国一家和爱国不分先后的正确号召下，加紧团结起来，为祖国和平统一和伟大建设献出力量。
    澳门中华教育会、民主妇女联合会、中华学生联合会、美术研究会和澳门许多行业的工人，都在11日分别集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。
    据新华社广州13日电　香港消息：澳门中华总商会在12日下午举行孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念会。参加纪念会的有澳门中华总商会和镜湖医院、同善堂的负责人、各社团代表、同盟会老会员以及孙中山先生生前友好同乡等五百多人。
    澳门中华总商会副理事长马万祺在会上致词说：孙中山先生是伟大爱国者，一生热爱中国，拯救中国。凡是中国人都应热爱祖国，在爱国一家旗帜下，实现祖国统一，将祖国建设得更加富强。
    在纪念会后还举行了聚餐。
