Title: Pakistani President and Prime Minister to Visit Three Middle Eastern Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 15th: Pakistani President Mirza and Prime Minister Suhrawardy will leave here on November 17th to visit Iraq, Turkey, and Saudi Arabia.

According to the Urdu daily "Jang" on the 14th, Mirza and Suhrawardy are preparing to persuade Arab countries to believe that the Baghdad Pact is not against the Arabs but is in their interests.

The newspaper said that political figures here have expressed dissatisfaction with the upcoming visit, pointing out that if the President and Prime Minister want to dispel the misunderstandings of Arab countries and truly support Egypt, they should immediately go to Cairo to meet with Egyptian President Nasser and then formulate a plan to deal with the current situation.

### 巴基斯坦总统和总理  将到三个中东国家访问

1956-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统和总理
    将到三个中东国家访问
    据新华社卡拉奇15日电　巴基斯坦总统米尔扎和总理苏拉瓦底将于11月17日离开这里前往伊拉克、土耳其和沙特阿拉伯访问。
    据乌尔都文日报“战斗报”14日报道，米尔扎和苏拉瓦底准备去说服阿拉伯国家，使它们相信巴格达条约并不是反对阿拉伯人的，而是合乎他们的利益的。
    这家报纸说，这里的政界人士对即将进行的这次访问表示不满，他们指出，如果总统和总理想消除阿拉伯国家的误解，并且真正要支持埃及的话，他们应当立即到开罗去同埃及总统纳赛尔会谈，然后制定出一个对付当前局势的方案。
