Title: Britain Reinforces Aden
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: TASS Cairo, November 14th: According to "Al-Ahram", Yemen's Minister to Cairo, Sayyid Abu Taleb, said that according to Yemeni sources, British army and air force units are being transferred to the British protectorate of Aden in batches.

### 英国增兵亚丁

1956-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    英国增兵亚丁
    据新华社15日讯　塔斯社开罗14日讯：据“金字塔报”报道，也门驻开罗公使赛义德·阿布·塔列布说，据也门消息，英国陆军和空军部队一批批调到英国保护地亚丁去。
