Title: First Team of Czechoslovak Miners Sets World Record in Tunneling
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th — The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the miner team (56 people) from the "North" mine of the Jáchymov mines in Czechoslovakia broke the world record for tunneling on November 7th. They tunneled 1,021.3 meters in one month, breaking the world record of 1,014 meters held by American miners.
　　This achievement is due to the team's genuine enthusiasm and great efforts in trying out new drilling machines. The team's experience will be promoted in all mines in Jáchymov and other mines and coal mines across the country.

### 捷一矿工小组  创巷道掘进世界纪录

1956-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    捷一矿工小组
    创巷道掘进世界纪录
    新华社布拉格8日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克的雅希莫夫矿“北面”矿井的矿工小组（五十六人）11月7日打破了巷道掘进世界纪录，在一个月之内掘进了一千零二十一点三公尺，打破了美国矿工保有的一千零十四公尺的世界纪录。
    这个成就是由于小组试用新的钻机而发挥了真正的热情和极大的努力。这个小组的经验将在雅希莫夫矿的所有矿井和全国其他矿山和煤矿推广。
