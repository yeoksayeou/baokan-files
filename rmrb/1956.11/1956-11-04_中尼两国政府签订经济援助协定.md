Title: China and Nepal Governments Sign Economic Aid Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. During the visit of His Excellency Tanka Prasad Acharya, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, to the People's Republic of China, the governments of China and Nepal held discussions on the issue of China assisting the Kingdom of Nepal in its economic construction, and signed the China-Nepal Economic Aid Agreement on this issue in Beijing on October 7th. In addition, the governments of China and Nepal also exchanged notes on the issue of foreign exchange stipulated in Article 1 of the aforementioned Economic Aid Agreement and on the issue of granting foreign exchange facilities to Nepalese merchants in the Tibet region of China. (The full text of the Economic Aid Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, as well as the two notes exchanged by both parties, can be found on page 3)

### 中尼两国政府签订经济援助协定

1956-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中尼两国政府签订经济援助协定
    新华社3日讯　在尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅阁下访问中华人民共和国期间，中尼两国政府曾就中国协助尼泊尔王国进行经济建设问题进行商谈，并于10月7日在北京就此问题签订了中国尼泊尔经济援助协定。此外，中尼两国政府还就上项经济援助协定第一条所规定的现汇问题以及关于给予在中国西藏地方的尼泊尔商人以外汇便利的问题互换了照会。（中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府经济援助协定以及双方互换的两个照会全文见第三版）
