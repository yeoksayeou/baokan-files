Title: A "Black Shop" in Vienna: Hungarian Terrorists Establish a Lair Within
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: According to a TASS dispatch from Berlin: The "Neues Deutschland" correspondent in Hungary pointed out in an article that Hungarian counter-revolutionaries have established a command headquarters in Vienna.

The author of the article quoted the confession of a captured Budapest-Austria shuttle bus driver, who said that the terrorists' command headquarters is located in the Regina Hotel in Vienna, and the people in the headquarters are all Hungarian fugitives.

The leader of this fugitive command is Ferenc Peter, the former leader of the Hungarian Fascist Party. Zoltan Kovacs, the editor-in-chief of the Hungarian group of the American "Radio Free Europe," also lives in this hotel.

### 维也纳一家“黑店”  匈恐怖分子设寨其中

1956-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    维也纳一家“黑店”
    匈恐怖分子设寨其中
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：“新德意志报”驻匈牙利记者在一篇文章中指出，匈牙利反革命分子在维也纳设有一个司令部。
    文章的作者引述了一个被逮捕的布达佩斯—奥地利班车司机的供词，他说，在维也纳的勒吉那饭店里设有恐怖分子的司令部，司令部里的人都是一些匈牙利的亡命者。
    这个亡命者司令部的首领是过去的匈牙利法西斯党党魁费伦茨·彼得。在这个饭店里还住着美国“自由欧洲”电台匈牙利组的总编辑佐尔坦·科瓦奇。
