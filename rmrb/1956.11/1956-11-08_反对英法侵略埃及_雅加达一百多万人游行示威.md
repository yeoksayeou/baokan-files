Title: Protesting the Anglo-French Aggression Against Egypt: More Than One Million People March in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 7th: On the morning of November 7th, 1,050,000 people in Jakarta participated in a demonstration against the Anglo-French aggression against Egypt. The demonstrators smashed the British Information Service library and burned the British flag.

    The French Embassy in Indonesia was protected by the police and suffered no damage. A demonstrator climbed onto the wall of the French Embassy's dormitory, tore the French tricolor flag to shreds, and shouted, "Long live Egypt!"

    The demonstrators held up portraits of Egyptian President Nasser and waved colorful flags and banners with slogans such as: "Anglo-French Imperialists, get out of Asia and Africa!" "Long live the unity of the Asian and African people!" "Support the Egyptian people!" "Eden and Mollet, get out, you are murderers!" and so on.

    Before the demonstration, a huge mass rally was held on Independence Square. Representatives of workers, women, youth, students, and writers from Jakarta expressed their determination to fully support the Egyptian people's struggle against aggression.

    The Egyptian Ambassador to Indonesia, An Rose, spoke at the meeting and was warmly welcomed by the masses.

    The conference passed a resolution demanding joint action with the Egyptian government and people until all British, French, and Israeli troops withdraw from Egyptian territory.

    The resolution supports the Indonesian government's statement on the Middle East situation, the parliament's resolution on the Middle East situation, and the government's efforts to hold a Colombo Powers conference in New Delhi. The resolution calls for swift action through diplomatic channels, international organizations, parliamentary relations, and other means to crush the aggression of Britain, France, and Israel.

### 反对英法侵略埃及  雅加达一百多万人游行示威

1956-11-08
第6版()
专栏：

    反对英法侵略埃及
    雅加达一百多万人游行示威
    新华社雅加达7日电　11月7日早晨，雅加达有一百零五万人参加了反对英法侵略埃及的示威游行。示威者捣毁了英国新闻处的图书馆，并且烧毁了英国的国旗。
    法国驻印度尼西亚大使馆的房子在警察保护下没有受到损失。一个示威者爬上法国大使馆宿舍的围墙上，把法国的三色旗撕得粉碎，并且高呼“埃及万岁”。
    示威者高举埃及总统纳赛尔的画像，并且挥舞着彩色的旗子和口号牌。这些口号是：“英法帝国主义者滚出亚洲和非洲！”“亚非人民团结万岁！”“支持埃及人民！”“艾登和摩勒滚蛋，你们是杀人犯！”等等。
    在示威游行之前，独立广场上举行了规模巨大的群众集会。雅加达的工人、妇女、青年、学生和作家的代表们都表示决心充分支持埃及人民反对侵略的斗争。
    埃及驻印度尼西亚大使安罗西在会上讲话，受到了群众的热烈欢迎。
    大会通过决议，要求同埃及政府和人民采取联合行动直到英国、法国和以色列的一切部队从埃及领土上撤退为止。
    决议支持印度尼西亚政府关于中东局势的声明、国会关于中东局势的决议，和政府为了在新德里举行科伦坡国家会议所作的努力。决议要求通过外交途径、国际组织、议会关系和其他方法迅速采取行动，粉碎英国、法国和以色列的侵略。
