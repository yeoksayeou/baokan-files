Title: Meeting Between Leaders of the CPSU and the CPN
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th -- TASS reported: Representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held talks on the 12th with the leaders of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, who are currently visiting Moscow.
　　The representatives of the CPSU Central Committee were: Pospelov, Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Ponomarev and Kuusinen, members of the Central Committee.
　　The CPN leaders who participated in the talks were: Groot, General Secretary of the Central Committee, Kuijper and Hruenendeik, members of the Central Committee.
　　They discussed issues of common concern in a friendly atmosphere.

### 苏共和荷共领导人会谈

1956-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    苏共和荷共领导人会谈
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会的代表12日同正在莫斯科访问的荷兰共产党的领导人举行了会谈。
    苏共中央的代表是：苏共中央书记波斯伯洛夫、中央委员波诺马烈夫和库西宁。
    参加会谈的荷共领导人是：中央委员会总书记格鲁特，中央委员克伊佩尔、赫鲁念杰伊克。
    他们在友好的气氛中讨论了一些共同关心的问题。
