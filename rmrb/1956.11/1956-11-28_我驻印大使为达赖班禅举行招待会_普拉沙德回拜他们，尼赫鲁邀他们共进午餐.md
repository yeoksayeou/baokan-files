Title: My Ambassador to India Holds Reception for Dalai Lama and Panchen; Prasad Pays Return Visit; Nehru Invites Them to Lunch

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 27th: The Chinese Ambassador to India, Pan Zili, held a reception on the evening of the 26th to mark the visit of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni to India.

Among those invited were Indian Prime Minister Nehru, former Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, senior Indian officials and members of parliament, Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council Kitchlew, Chairman of the India-China Friendship Association Sundarlal, prominent figures from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India, totaling more than 300 people.

The Chinese Buddhist delegation, headed by Master Xirao Gyatso, and other national Buddhist representatives who participated in the commemoration of the 2,500th anniversary of the Nirvana of Sakyamuni also attended the reception.

The reception was held in the colorfully decorated garden of the Chinese Embassy. Hosts and guests chatted happily. Accompanied by Ambassador Pan Zili and Nehru and U Nu, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni walked around the reception venue. The head of the Soviet Buddhist delegation, Gabishi, and the head of the Mongolian Buddhist delegation, Tuvshinhing Gombojab, also presented hadas to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni.

That afternoon, President Prasad paid a return visit to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni.

At noon, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni were invited to lunch with Prime Minister Nehru. The Sikkim Maharaja also joined them for lunch.

### 我驻印大使为达赖班禅举行招待会  普拉沙德回拜他们，尼赫鲁邀他们共进午餐

1956-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印大使为达赖班禅举行招待会
    普拉沙德回拜他们，尼赫鲁邀他们共进午餐
    新华社新德里27日电　中国驻印度大使潘自力为达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼访问印度，在26日晚上举行招待会。
    应邀参加的有印度总理尼赫鲁，前缅甸总理吴努，印度高级官员和议员们，印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁，全印和平理事会主席克其鲁，印中友好协会主席森德拉尔，各界著名人士和各国驻印度外交使节等共三百多人。
    以喜饶嘉错大师为首的中国佛教代表团和参加释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念的其他各国佛教代表也参加了招待会。
    招待会是在装饰得五彩缤纷的中国大使馆花园中举行的。主人和客人们愉快地交谈。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在潘自力大使和尼赫鲁、吴努的陪同下在招待会场中绕行一周。苏联佛教代表团团长加比希和蒙古佛教代表团团长图夫申兴·贡博杜还向达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼献了哈达。
    当天下午，普拉沙德总统曾经回拜达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    在中午的时候，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼应邀同尼赫鲁总理共进午餐。在一起进午餐的还有锡金的大君。
