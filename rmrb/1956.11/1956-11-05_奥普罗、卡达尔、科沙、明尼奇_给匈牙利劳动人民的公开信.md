Title: Appro, Kadar, Kossa, Minnich - Open Letter to the Hungarian Working People
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th - Budapest dispatch: On November 4th, an “Open Letter to the Hungarian Working People” from four Hungarian political activists was published here. They are: Antal Apro, Janos Kadar, Istvan Kossa, and Ferenc Munnich.

    The content of this letter is as follows:

    “Our patriots! Worker and peasant brothers!

    We, Antal Apro, Janos Kadar, Istvan Kossa, and Ferenc Munnich, former ministers of the Imre Nagy government, declare that we withdrew from the government on November 1, 1956, severed all ties with this government, and initiated the establishment of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government.

    We have taken this important step because we realize that within the Nagy government, which has fallen under the influence of reactionary forces and become powerless, we can no longer oppose the increasingly dangerous counter-revolution, which threatens the demise of our People's Republic, the worker-peasant regime, and our socialist achievements.

    They murdered many of our respected warriors who had fought for decades: Imre Mezo, Secretary of the Budapest Party Committee, Comrade Kalamar, an old fighter of the Csepel workers' movement, and Sandor Sziklai, curator of the Museum of Military History. In addition to these people, they also massively murdered other prominent sons of the working class and peasants.

    We have become members of a government incapable of action, and we cannot continue to do nothing and allow counter-revolutionary terrorists and bandits to savagely murder our best worker and peasant brothers under the guise of democracy, plunging our peaceful citizens into terror, creating anarchy in the country, and subjecting our entire people to the shackles of counter-revolution for a long time.

    Hungarian working people, patriots, our worker brothers, comrades!

    We are determined to do everything in our power to fight against the reactionary, fascist threat, against the murderous gangs.

    We call on all the loyal sons of our People's Republic, all those who support socialism, especially the Communists, workers, miners, peasants, and outstanding sons of the intelligentsia, to support all measures of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government. Support the liberation struggle of the entire people.”

### 奥普罗、卡达尔、科沙、明尼奇  给匈牙利劳动人民的公开信

1956-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    奥普罗、卡达尔、科沙、明尼奇
    给匈牙利劳动人民的公开信
    新华社布拉格4日电　布达佩斯消息：11月4日，这里发表了匈牙利四个政治活动家“给匈牙利劳动人民的公开信”。他们是：安托尔·奥普罗、亚诺什·卡达尔、伊斯特万·科沙和费伦茨·明尼奇。
    这封信的内容如下：
    “我们的爱国者！工人和农民兄弟们！
    我们，前伊姆雷·纳吉政府的部长安托尔·奥普罗、亚诺什·卡达尔、伊斯特万·科沙、费伦茨·明尼奇声明，我们已经于1956年11月1日退出政府，同这个政府割断一切关系，并且倡议建立匈牙利工农革命政府。
    我们之所以采取这个重要步骤，是由于意识到，在已经落入反动势力影响下而变得无能为力的纳吉政府内部，我们再也没有办法反对反革命危险，而这种危险越来越大，使我们的人民共和国、工农政权，我们的社会主义成果遭到复灭的威胁。
    他们杀害了我们的许多曾经在数十年中进行斗争的可敬的战士：布达佩斯党委员会书记伊姆雷·麦泽，契佩尔工人运动的老战士卡拉马尔同志，军事史博物馆的馆长山多尔·西克拉伊。除了这些人以外，他们还大规模地杀害了工人阶级和农民的其他有声望的儿子。
    我们已经成了无能行动的政府的成员，我们不可能继续无所作为而听任反革命恐怖分子和匪徒们在民主的掩饰下野蛮地杀害我们最优秀的工农弟兄，使我们的和平的公民陷入恐怖中，在国内造成无政府状态，使我们全体人民长期处于反革命的桎梏中。
    匈牙利劳动人民、爱国者、我们的工人兄弟、同志们！
    我们决定要尽我们一切力量同反动的、法西斯的威胁性危险、同屠杀人民的匪帮斗争。
    我们号召我们人民共和国的一切忠实的儿子、一切拥护社会主义的人们，首先是共产党人、工人、矿工、农民和知识界的优秀儿子支持匈牙利工农革命政府的一切措施。支持整个人民的解放斗争。”
