Title: Tianyang Has Abundant Crystal
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Baise, 18th: The prospecting work of the Tianyang Crystal Mine in Guangxi Province, one of the larger crystal mining areas in our country, has been basically completed recently. The geological workers in charge of the exploration are writing the final geological report for handover to the state for mining.

The crystal contained in the Tianyang Crystal Mine is a colorless crystal. The mining area is very large, the reserves are abundant, and the quality of the crystal is also very good. This mining area began preliminary surveys and exploration in January 1954 and September 1955.

The Youjiang River basin of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture in Guangxi Province has abundant crystal resources. In addition to the Tianyang Crystal Mining Area, crystal ore outcrops have been discovered in Baise, Debao, Fengshan, Lingle and other counties.

### 田阳水晶多

1956-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

    田阳水晶多
    新华社百色18日电　我国当前比较大的一个水晶矿区——广西省田阳水晶矿的勘探工作，在最近基本结束。负责勘探的地质工作者正在编写最后的地质报告书，以便移交国家进行开采。
    田阳水晶矿蕴藏的水晶是一种无色水晶。矿区面积很大，藏量丰富，水晶的质量也很好。这个矿区是在1954年1月和1955年9月开始进行普查和勘探的。
    广西省桂西僮族自治州右江流域，有丰富的水晶资源。除了田阳水晶矿区以外，在百色、德保、凤山、凌乐等县，都有水晶矿苗发现。
