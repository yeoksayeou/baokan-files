Title: Flames of Anger: British Oil Pipeline in Northern Lebanon Blown Up

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 6

    Flames of Anger
    British Oil Pipeline in Northern Lebanon Blown Up
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 6th: According to news obtained here from the headquarters of the Iraqi Petroleum Company, a section of the Iraqi Petroleum Company's oil pipeline laid in northern Lebanon was blown up and caught fire on the morning of November 5th. This is the first time that the British-dominated oil company's oil pipeline in Lebanon has been sabotaged.
    At least three sections of the Iraqi Petroleum Company's oil pipeline in Syria have been blown up.
    A spokesman for the company said that since the three locations of the oil pipeline in Syria were destroyed on November 2nd, the oil pipeline's oil transportation work has been at a standstill.

### 愤怒的火焰  黎巴嫩北部英国油管被炸毁

1956-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    愤怒的火焰
    黎巴嫩北部英国油管被炸毁
    新华社6日讯　贝鲁特消息：据这里的伊拉克石油公司总部获得的消息，敷设在黎巴嫩北部的伊拉克石油公司油管在11月5日早晨有一段被炸毁起火。这家英国资本占统治地位的石油公司在黎巴嫩的油管遭到破坏，这次是第一次。
    在叙利亚的伊拉克石油公司油管已经至少有三段被炸毁。
    这个公司的发言人说，自从11月2日在叙利亚境内的油管三处被毁以来，油管的输油工作一直陷于停顿。
