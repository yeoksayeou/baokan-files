Title: Chairman Mao Receives Japanese Okayama Prefecture Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the entire delegation of the Japanese Okayama Prefecture Academic and Cultural Delegation, headed by Chief Delegate Hideichi Hayashi and Deputy Chief Delegate Sukeyoshi Kawasaki, at the Qin Zheng Dian this evening.

Also present at the reception were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; and Wang Tuo, Director of the Foreign Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. (Attached picture)
Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, received the Japanese Okayama Prefecture Academic and Cultural Delegation on the 18th. The picture shows a group photo taken during the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Lü Houmin

### 毛主席接见日本冈山县代表团

1956-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见日本冈山县代表团
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席今晚在勤政殿接见了以林秀一团长、川崎佑宣副团长为首的日本冈山县学术文化代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有，中国科学院院长郭沫若，中国科学院副秘书长谢鑫鹤，中国科学院对外联络局局长王拓。（附图片）
