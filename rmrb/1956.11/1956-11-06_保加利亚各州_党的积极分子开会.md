Title: Bulgarian States’ Party Activists Hold Meetings
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 5th: TASS reports from Sofia on November 4th: According to the decision of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Bulgarian states have held meetings of party activists. Members of the Party Central Committee gave reports on the international situation and the tasks of the party at the meetings.
    First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Zhivkov, delivered a speech at the meeting of party activists in Sofia Oblast on November 3rd. The meeting expressed great anger at the counter-revolutionary elements' attempt to overthrow the Hungarian People's regime and remove Hungary from the peaceful and socialist camp.
    Similar meetings were also held in the cities of Ruse, Stara Zagora, Blagoevgrad, and Kolarovgrad. These meetings held in various places have become a demonstration of the solidarity and unity of the Bulgarian Communist Party around the Central Committee.

### 保加利亚各州  党的积极分子开会

1956-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    保加利亚各州
    党的积极分子开会
    据新华社5日讯　塔斯社索非亚4日讯：根据保加利亚共产党中央委员会的决定，保加利亚各州举行了党的积极分子会议，党中央委员会的委员分别在会上作了关于国际局势和党的任务的报告。
    保共中央第一书记日夫科夫11月3日在索非亚州党的积极分子会议上发表了演说。会议对反革命分子企图推翻匈牙利的人民政权、使匈牙利脱离和平和社会主义阵营的阴谋表示极大的愤怒。
    在鲁西、斯塔拉萨哥拉、布拉戈耶夫格勒、科拉罗夫格勒等城市也举行了同样的会议。各地举行的这些会议已经成为保加利亚共产党团结和统一在中央委员会的周围的示威。
