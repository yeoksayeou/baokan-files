On November 18th, over two thousand students from various middle schools in Beijing held a get-together at the Beijing Hotel with some representatives attending the National Congress of Martyrs' Families, Military Dependents, Disabled Servicemen, and Demobilized Servicemen's Socialist Construction Activists. The picture shows students from the Beijing Midwifery School greeting Rong Guanxiu, the mother of soldiers.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu photographed.
