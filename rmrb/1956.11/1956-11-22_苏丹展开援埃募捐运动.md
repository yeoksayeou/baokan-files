Title: Sudan Launches Fundraising Campaign to Aid Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-22
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 20th: A widespread fundraising campaign to aid Egypt has been launched in Sudan.
    The Sudanese government has allocated 20,000 Egyptian pounds and 10,000 head of livestock as a fund to aid Egypt. Leaders of various religious sects and political parties, as well as representatives from the business community, have donated large sums of money. The Sudanese Trade Union Federation has passed a resolution for workers to donate one day's wages to aid Egyptian residents.

### 苏丹展开援埃募捐运动

1956-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹展开援埃募捐运动
    据新华社21日讯　塔斯社开罗20日讯：苏丹广泛展开了援助埃及的募捐运动。
    苏丹政府拨出了二万埃镑和一万头牲畜作为援助埃及的基金。各教派、政党的领袖们和商界的代表们捐献了巨额的款项。苏丹工会联合会通过了一项职工捐献一日工资援助埃及居民的决议。
