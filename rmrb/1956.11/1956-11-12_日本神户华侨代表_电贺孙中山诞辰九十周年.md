Title: Japanese Kobe Overseas Chinese Representatives Send Telegram Congratulating Sun Yat-sen's 90th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. Five representatives of overseas Chinese in Kobe, Japan, sent a telegram back to China to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

    Commemorating the 90th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth, we pledge to inherit the legacy of the martyrs with a spirit of unity and patriotism.
		
 Kobe Overseas Chinese Representatives: Zhan Tingying, Huang Hongji, Chen Yifang, Wu Yuchen, Chen Deren.

### 日本神户华侨代表  电贺孙中山诞辰九十周年

1956-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本神户华侨代表
    电贺孙中山诞辰九十周年
    新华社11日讯　日本神户华侨代表五人打电报回国，纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。电报全文如下：
    纪念孙中山先生九十诞辰，我们誓以团结爱国精神，继承先烈遗志。
  神户华侨代表　詹廷英、黄鸿记、陈义方、吴玉臣、陈德仁。
