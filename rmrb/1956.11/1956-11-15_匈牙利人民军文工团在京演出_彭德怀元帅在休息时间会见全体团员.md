Title: Hungarian People's Army Ensemble Performs in Beijing, Marshal Peng Dehuai Meets with All Members During Intermission
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th - The Hungarian People's Army Ensemble gave its first performance in Beijing tonight.

Among those who watched the performance were Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Ke, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Liu Yalou (Air Force General), Luo Shunchu (Navy Vice Admiral), Xiao Xiangrong (Lieutenant General), Liu Zhijian (Lieutenant General), Chen Yi (Major General), Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and more than a thousand literary and art workers from the capital and troops stationed in Beijing.

The fourteen programs performed by the Hungarian artists tonight were met with enthusiastic applause from the audience. At the audience's enthusiastic request, the a cappella "Echo," the chorus "Join Our Ranks," and the dance "Magnet Can Dance" were re-performed or other songs were added. In particular, the Chinese songs "The Half Moon Climbs Up," "Guerrilla March," and "Dialogue of Flowers" were sung with clear pronunciation, fully grasping the emotions of the songs themselves, arousing great interest among the audience. The soldier's dance "Morning in the Barracks" was full of life and also received a warm welcome.

Before tonight's performance, Marshal Peng Dehuai met with Colonel Sokoly István, the leader of the ensemble, Major Melek László, the head of the ensemble, and other leaders. During the intermission, Marshal Peng Dehuai and the generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army went on stage to meet all the members of the ensemble. In a short speech, Marshal Peng Dehuai highly praised the programs performed by the Hungarian artists for their strong national style and extremely high artistic level, and thanked them for bringing the friendship of the Hungarian people. He expressed that the Chinese people will always stand together with the Hungarian people. At the end of the performance, more than two hundred literary and art workers from the capital and the Chinese People's Liberation Army went on stage to present flowers. (Attached picture)

### 匈牙利人民军文工团在京演出  彭德怀元帅在休息时间会见全体团员

1956-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民军文工团在京演出
    彭德怀元帅在休息时间会见全体团员
    新华社14日讯　今天晚上，匈牙利人民军文工团，作了来到北京后的第一次演出。
    观看演出的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理、国防部长彭德怀元帅，中国人民解放军高级将领萧克上将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、刘亚楼空军上将、罗舜初海军中将、萧向荣中将、刘志坚中将、陈沂少将，中华人民共和国文化部副部长张致祥和首都及驻京部队文艺工作者一千多人。
    匈牙利艺术家们今天演出的十四个节目，每一个都博得观众热烈鼓掌。在观众的热情要求下，无伴奏合唱“回声”、合唱“参加我们的队伍”、舞蹈“磁罐舞”等节目都作了重演，或者加演了别的歌曲。特别是中国歌曲“半个月亮爬上来”、“游击队队歌”、“对花”，演唱时咬字清楚，充分掌握了歌曲本身的情绪，引起观众们极大兴趣。士兵舞“兵营里的早晨”富有生活气息，也受到热烈欢迎。
    今晚演出前，彭德怀元帅接见了文工团领队索柯洛伊·衣施多万上校、团长梅莱克·拉斯洛少校及其他领导人员。在演出休息时间，彭德怀元帅和中国人民解放军的将军们上台会见了文工团的全体团员。彭德怀元帅在简短的讲话中，对匈牙利艺术家们演出的具有浓厚民族风格和极高艺术水平的节目大加赞赏，并感谢他们带来匈牙利人民的友情，他表示中国人民将永远和匈牙利人民站在一起。演出结束，首都的文艺工作者和中国人民解放军的文艺工作者二百多人登台献花。（附图片）
