Title: Protest Against British and French Aggression Against Egypt: Mass Rally Held in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 5
    Protest Against British and French Aggression Against Egypt
    Mass Rally Held in Delhi
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 5th – On the afternoon of November 4th, two mass rallies were held in Delhi to protest against the British and French aggression against Egypt.
    At the Ramlila Ground, a mass rally hosted by the Delhi State Congress Committee passed a resolution condemning the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt. The resolution demanded that Britain, France, and Israel immediately withdraw their troops from Egyptian territory in accordance with the resolutions of the United Nations General Assembly.
    On the same day, the Communist Party of India, the Praja Socialist Party, and the All India People's Democratic Front jointly held a mass rally at Gandhi Square. The conference passed a resolution calling on all political parties in India to unite and establish an organization to aid Egypt, and called on people to join volunteer armies to help the Egyptian people in their struggle for freedom. The conference also demanded that the Indian government sever its ties with the Commonwealth and nationalize British investments in India.

### 抗议英法侵略埃及  德里举行群众集会

1956-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    抗议英法侵略埃及
    德里举行群众集会
    新华社新德里5日电　11月4日下午，在德里举行了两个群众大会，抗议英法对埃及的侵略。
    在拉姆利拉广场，由德里邦国大党委员会主持的一个群众大会通过了谴责英国、法国和以色列侵略埃及的决议。决议要求英国、法国和以色列立即遵照联合国大会的决议从埃及领土上撤出它们的军队。
    同一天，印度共产党、人民社会党和全国民主阵线联合在甘地广场举行了群众大会。大会通过决议，要求印度各政党团结起来成立援助埃及的机构，并且号召参加志愿军来帮助埃及人民进行争取自由的斗争。大会还要求印度政府割断它同英联邦的关系，把英国在印度的投资收归国有。
