Title: US and Canada Jointly Launch First Scientific Rocket
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Toronto dispatch: The first scientific rocket in the joint contribution of the United States and Canada to the International Geophysical Year was launched on October 17 from the Muskeg area of the northern Canadian plains.
    Seven more rockets will be launched before November 20.
    The rocket launched on October 17 was 7.5 meters long and weighed 567 kilograms. It was loaded with scientific recording instruments to collect materials on atmospheric density, air pressure, temperature, the Earth's magnetic field, solar and ultraviolet rays, ionization density, and cosmic radiation phenomena. It rose to a height of about one hundred miles.

### 美加两国联合射出第一个科学火箭

1956-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美加两国联合射出第一个科学火箭
    新华社9日讯　多伦多消息：美国和加拿大对国际地球物理学年所作的联合贡献中的第一个科学火箭，已经在10月17日从加拿大北部大平原默斯凯格地区发射出去。
    在11月20日以前，还将发射另外七个火箭。
    10月17日发射的那个火箭，有七点五公尺长，五百六十七公斤重，它装载着科学纪录仪器，以收集关于大气密度、气压、温度、地球磁场、太阳和紫外光线、电离密度和宇宙放射现象的材料。它上升的高度约达一百英里。
