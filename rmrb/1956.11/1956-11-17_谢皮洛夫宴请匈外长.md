Title: Shepilov Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 15th. TASS, New York, 15th: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov hosted a luncheon on the 14th in honor of Hungarian People's Republic Foreign Minister Horvath.

### 谢皮洛夫宴请匈外长

1956-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫宴请匈外长
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社纽约15日讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫14日设午宴招待匈牙利人民共和国外交部长霍瓦特。
