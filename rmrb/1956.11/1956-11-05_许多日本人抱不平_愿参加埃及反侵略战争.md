Title: Many Japanese Feel Indignant and Wish to Join Egypt's War Against Aggression
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 3rd - Egyptian Ambassador to Japan, Osman Abed, said at a press conference today that the Egyptian Embassy has received many letters from Japanese people applying to join Egypt's war against British and French aggression. He said that many Japanese students have also written to encourage Egypt's resistance to British and French aggression. He thanked the Japanese people for their moral support for the Egyptian people.

### 许多日本人抱不平  愿参加埃及反侵略战争

1956-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    许多日本人抱不平
    愿参加埃及反侵略战争
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：埃及驻日本大使欧斯曼·艾贝德今天在记者招待会上说，埃及大使馆收到了许多日本人申请参加埃及反抗英法侵略的战争的信。他说，还有许多日本学生来信对埃及反抗英法侵略表示鼓励。他感谢日本人民对埃及人民的道义支持。
