Title: Khrushchev Receives Stoica, Bulganin Hosts Banquet for Romanian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th dispatch from Moscow: TASS reports: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Stoica, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, on the 28th. During the conversation, the two sides discussed issues of concern to the Soviet Union and Romania, as well as the consolidation and further development of relations between the two countries. The conversation took place in an extremely cordial and comradely atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th dispatch from Moscow: TASS reports: Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet on the 28th in honor of the government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th dispatch from Moscow: TASS reports: The Soviet government delegation and the Romanian government delegation held talks at the Kremlin on the 27th. The talks took place in a sincere, friendly, and fully mutually understanding atmosphere. During the talks, both sides discussed issues of concern to both countries.

Advisors have been entrusted by both sides to draft the necessary documents based on their proposals, so that the two delegations can discuss them at future meetings.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见斯托伊卡  布尔加宁欢宴罗政府代表团

1956-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见斯托伊卡
    布尔加宁欢宴罗政府代表团
    据新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫28日接见了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局委员、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席斯托伊卡。双方在谈话中谈到了苏联和罗马尼亚所关心的一些问题以及巩固和进一步发展两国关系的问题，谈话是在极其恳切的和同志式的气氛中进行的。
    据新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁28日设宴招待以部长会议主席斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团。
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府代表团同罗马尼亚政府代表团27日在克里姆林宫进行会谈。会谈是在诚恳、友好和完全相互谅解的气氛中进行的。会谈中双方讨论了两国关心的问题。
    双方已经委托顾问就双方的建议拟定出必要的文件，以便两国代表团在以后的会议上讨论。
