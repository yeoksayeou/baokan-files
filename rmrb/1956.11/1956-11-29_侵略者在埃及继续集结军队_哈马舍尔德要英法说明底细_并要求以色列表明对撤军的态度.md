Title: Aggressors Continue to Mass Troops in Egypt; Hammarskjöld Demands Explanation from Britain and France; Asks Israel to Clarify its Position on Troop Withdrawal
Author:
Date: 1956-11-29
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 28th, New York Dispatch: United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld notified the United Nations General Assembly on the 27th that he would ask Britain and France to clarify reports about their continued massing of troops in Egypt. Hammarskjöld's action was taken after a request from Indian representative Menon.

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th, New York Dispatch: United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld has once again requested that Israel clarify its position on the withdrawal of Israeli troops from Egypt.

Earlier, Israeli Foreign Minister Meir had written to Hammarskjöld, requesting that he resume talks to discuss the so-called "Israeli proposals for withdrawing its troops and the role of the United Nations police force."

In a letter to Meir on the 26th, Hammarskjöld stated that he hoped that Israel would first put forward some concrete proposals, "clarifying its views," before such discussions could take place, as this would be helpful in resuming the talks.

### 侵略者在埃及继续集结军队  哈马舍尔德要英法说明底细  并要求以色列表明对撤军的态度

1956-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    侵略者在埃及继续集结军队
    哈马舍尔德要英法说明底细
    并要求以色列表明对撤军的态度
    据新华社28日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德27日通知联合国大会，他将要求英法两国澄清关于它们在埃及继续集结军队的消息。哈马舍尔德这个行动是在印度代表梅农提出要求以后采取的。
    据新华社28日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德再次要求以色列就以色列部队撤出埃及一事表明它的态度。
    早些时候，以色列外交部长梅厄曾经写信给哈马舍尔德，要求和他恢复会谈，以便讨论所谓“以色列对撤退它的部队所提出的建议和联合国警察部队的作用”的问题。
    哈马舍尔德在26日给梅厄的信中表示，他希望在进行这种讨论之前，以色列首先提出一些具体的建议，“澄清一下它的看法”，这样做将会对恢复会谈有帮助。
