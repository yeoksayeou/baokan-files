Title: At the First Day of the Arab Summit, Lebanese President Calls for United Support for Egypt Against Britain and France
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, November 14th: Arab heads of state held a meeting on November 13th in Beirut, the capital of Lebanon. This is the first such meeting of Arab nations in ten years.

    Participating in the summit were the Presidents of Lebanon and Syria, the Kings of Saudi Arabia, Iraq, and Jordan, the Prime Minister of Libya, the Crown Prince and Foreign Minister of Yemen, and the Egyptian Ambassador to Lebanon representing President Nasser. The Chairman of the Sudanese Sovereignty Council, Mugrabi, also participated as an observer.

    At the opening session, Lebanese President Camille Chamoun called on Arab countries to unite in support of Egypt in defending the Suez Canal and resisting British and French intervention.

    Syrian President Shukri al-Quwatli, in a passionate speech at the meeting, said that the despicable aggression of Britain, France, and Israel against Egypt had consolidated the unity of millions of Arabs.

    A communique issued after the meeting stated that the first session had established a committee composed of the Prime Ministers and Premiers of each participating country to study the agenda with the assistance of representatives and to report to the Kings and Presidents scheduled to meet on the 14th.

    The meeting is scheduled to last two or three days.

### 在阿拉伯国家首脑会议第一天会议上  黎巴嫩总统呼吁联合支持埃及抵抗英法

1956-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    在阿拉伯国家首脑会议第一天会议上
    黎巴嫩总统呼吁联合支持埃及抵抗英法
    据新华社14日讯　贝鲁特消息：阿拉伯各国首脑11月13日在黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特举行会议。这是阿拉伯国家十年来第一次举行这样的会议。
    参加首脑会议的有黎巴嫩和叙利亚的总统，沙特阿拉伯、伊拉克和约旦的国王，利比亚的首相，也门的皇太子兼外交大臣，以及纳赛尔总统的代表埃及驻黎巴嫩大使。苏丹主权委员会主席穆格拉比也以观察员的身份参加。
    黎巴嫩总统夏蒙在开幕会议上呼吁阿拉伯国家在埃及保卫苏伊士运河、抵抗英法干涉方面联合起来支持埃及。
    叙利亚总统库阿特利在会上发表的一篇激烈的演说中说，英法以三国对埃及的卑鄙侵略已经巩固了千百万阿拉伯人的团结。
    会后发表的公报说，第一次会议已经成立了一个由各参加国的总理和首相组成的委员会，以便由他们在代表们的帮助下研究议程，并向预定在14日举行会议的国王和总统提出报告。
    会议预定举行两三天。
