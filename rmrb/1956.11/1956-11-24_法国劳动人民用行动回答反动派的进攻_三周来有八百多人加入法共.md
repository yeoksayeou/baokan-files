Title: French Laboring People Respond to Reactionary Attacks with Action: Over 800 Join the French Communist Party in Three Weeks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 23rd - According to a TASS report from Paris on November 22nd: Many French working people are responding to the rampant attacks of reactionary forces and fascists by joining the Communist Party. According to "l'Humanité," more than 800 people in twenty-three provinces have joined the French Communist Party in the past three weeks.

The French people are determined to respond to the Communist Party's resolution and raise funds to repair the houses destroyed by the fascists. In less than two weeks, the working people have already donated 7.5 million francs. Fundraising is still ongoing.

### 法国劳动人民用行动回答反动派的进攻  三周来有八百多人加入法共

1956-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

    法国劳动人民用行动回答反动派的进攻
    三周来有八百多人加入法共
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社巴黎22日讯：法国许多劳动人民用参加共产党的行动来回答反动势力和法西斯分子的猖狂进攻。据“人道报”报道，最近三周以来，在二十三个省里就有八百多人加入法国共产党。
    法国人民决心响应共产党的决议，募集款项来修缮遭到法西斯分子破坏的房子。在不到两星期的时间里，劳动人民已经捐献了七百五十万法郎。现在募捐还在继续进行。
