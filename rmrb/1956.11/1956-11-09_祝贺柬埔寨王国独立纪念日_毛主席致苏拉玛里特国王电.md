Title: Congratulations on the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Chairman Mao's Telegram to King Suramarit
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia
    Chairman Mao's Telegram to King Suramarit
    To His Majesty Norodom Suramarit, King of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    On the occasion of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and the Cambodian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the cooperative and friendly relations between China and Cambodia will be further strengthened and developed.
                           Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                                    November 7, 1956, Beijing

### 祝贺柬埔寨王国独立纪念日  毛主席致苏拉玛里特国王电

1956-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺柬埔寨王国独立纪念日
    毛主席致苏拉玛里特国王电
    柬埔寨王国
    国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下：
    当柬埔寨王国独立纪念日的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向您和柬埔寨人民致热烈的祝贺。祝中柬两国合作和友好的关系进一步加强和发展。
                           中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                    1956年11月7日于北京
