Title: Huangtankou Hydropower Station Under Construction (Picture)
Author: Mu Ying, Mi He
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 2

    Huangtankou Hydropower Station Under Construction
    A large-scale hydroelectric power station is under construction in Huangtankou, Wuxi River, Zhejiang Province. Once completed, the power generation capacity of this power station will be equal to the total existing power generation capacity of Zhejiang Province.
                                Photo by Mu Ying and Mi He (Zhejiang Daily)

### 兴建中的黄坛口水电站（图片）
沐鹰  米河
1956-11-15
第2版()
专栏：

    兴建中的黄坛口水电站
    浙江省乌溪江黄坛口正在兴建一座大型的水力发电站。这座电站建成后，它的发电能力等于浙江省现有发电量的总和。
                                沐鹰、米河摄（浙江日报社稿）
