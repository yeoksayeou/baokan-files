Title: TASS Denies Rumors: Refutes the Absurd Claim that Soviet Troops Stationed in Hungary and Romania are Moving Towards the Yugoslav Border
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: TASS, Moscow, November 22nd: TASS refutes rumors: The American news agencies United Press and Associated Press have released news stating that the Soviet Union has moved twenty new infantry divisions into Hungary, and that Soviet troops stationed in Hungary and Romania are moving towards the Yugoslav border. These reports also state that Soviet troops have already landed at Bulgarian Black Sea ports.

TASS is authorized to state that the information released by the aforementioned news agencies does not correspond to the facts. These reports are completely provocative and malicious lies fabricated by groups hostile to the Soviet Union, and their aim is to complicate the international situation and undermine the cause of international peace and security.

### 塔斯社辟谣  驳斥所谓驻匈罗两国的苏军向南斯拉夫边境移动的鬼话

1956-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    塔斯社辟谣
    驳斥所谓驻匈罗两国的苏军向南斯拉夫边境移动的鬼话
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社莫斯科22日电：塔斯社辟谣：美国的合众社和美联社发布消息说，苏联把二十个新的步兵师开进匈牙利，苏联驻匈牙利和罗马尼亚的军队正向南斯拉夫边界移动。这些消息还说，苏联军队已经在保加利亚的黑海港口登陆。
    塔斯社受权声明，上述通讯社发布的消息同事实不符。这些消息是敌视苏联的集团捏造的彻头彻尾的挑拨性恶意谎话，它们的目的是要使国际局势复杂化，破坏国际和平和安全事业。
