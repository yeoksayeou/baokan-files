Title: Produce More and Better Generators for Jiefang Brand Automobiles (Picture)
Author: Sun Baopu
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 2

    Produce More and Better Generators for Jiefang Brand Automobiles
    In order to support the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant in producing Jiefang brand automobiles, the Boshan Electric Motor Factory in Shandong increased its production of automobile DC generators this year by 120% compared to last year. The picture shows workers conducting final tests on a batch of automobile DC generators. Photo by Sun Baopu (Draft from Dazhong Daily)

### 为解放牌汽车生产更多更好的发电机（图片）
孙保朴
1956-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

    为解放牌汽车生产更多更好的发电机
    山东博山电机厂为了支援第一汽车制造厂生产解放牌汽车，今年生产的汽车直流发电机，比去年增加了120％。这是工人们正在进行一批汽车直流发电机的最后试验。　孙保朴摄（大众日报社稿）
