Title: Nie Rongzhen and Bo Yibo Appointed Vice Premiers of the State Council, Chen Yun, Vice Premier, Concurrently Appointed Minister of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. According to the decision of the 51st meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, appointed Nie Rongzhen and Bo Yibo as Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yun concurrently as Minister of Commerce of the People's Republic of China, Yang Yichen as Minister of Urban Services of the People's Republic of China, and Song Renqiong as Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building.

### 聂荣臻薄一波任国务院副总理  陈云副总理兼任商业部部长

1956-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻薄一波任国务院副总理
    陈云副总理兼任商业部部长
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议的决定，任命聂荣臻、薄一波为中华人民共和国国务院副总理，陈云兼任中华人民共和国商业部部长，杨一辰为中华人民共和国城市服务部部长，宋任穷为第三机械工业部部长。
