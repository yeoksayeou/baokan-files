Title: Ministry of Culture and Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Host Banquet for Soviet Film Delegation; Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing Receives Soviet Film Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Ministry of Culture and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the entire Soviet film workers delegation.

    Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing gave a welcoming speech before the banquet began and expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the selfless assistance of the Soviet film industry to China's film industry.

    Go Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters, also expressed a warm welcome to the delegation in his speech.

    The head of the delegation, Kalashnikov, also raised his glass in thanks. He said that although he has only been in your beautiful capital for a few days, he has deeply felt the profound and most precious friendship of the Chinese people towards the Soviet Union.

    Shen Yanbing and Kalashnikov also exchanged toasts at the banquet, toasting to the greater achievements of the film industries of China and the Soviet Union.

    Young film actor Li Baiwan also toasted the entire delegation on behalf of the Chinese youth, saying that the vast number of Chinese youth have received much education from Soviet films. Soviet People's Artist Bondarchuk, who played the role of the Ukrainian poet Shevchenko on the screen, recited Shevchenko's poems in Ukrainian during the banquet.

    Sudalikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Soviet expert of the Ministry of Culture, Shevelin, also spoke at the banquet.

    More than eighty people attended the banquet, including well-known figures in the Chinese film industry and Soviet experts from the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture.

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing received the entire Soviet film workers delegation at the Beijing Hotel today.

    Shen Yanbing and the head of the delegation, Kalashnikov, as well as all members of the delegation had a cordial conversation.

### 文化部中苏友协欢宴苏电影团  文化部部长沈雁冰接见苏联电影工作者代表团

1956-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

    文化部中苏友协欢宴苏电影团
    文化部部长沈雁冰接见苏联电影工作者代表团
    新华社9日讯　今晚，苏联电影工作者代表团全体人员出席了文化部和中苏友好协会总会为欢迎他们而举行的宴会。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在宴会开始前致欢迎词，并且对苏联电影界对中国电影事业的无私的帮助表示衷心的感谢。
    中苏友好协会总会理事会副秘书长戈宝权也在讲话中对代表团表示了热诚的欢迎。
    代表团团长卡拉什尼科夫也举杯答谢。他说，来在你们美丽的首都虽然只有几天，可是已经深深感到中国人民对苏联的深厚的最珍贵的友谊。
    沈雁冰和卡拉什尼科夫并在宴会上互致祝词，为中、苏两国电影事业得到更巨大的成就而干杯。
    青年电影演员李百万并代表中国青年向代表团全体人员敬酒致意，他说，因为中国广大青年曾经在苏联影片中得到许多教育。苏联人民演员银幕上的乌克兰诗人谢夫钦科的扮演者邦达尔丘克在席间用乌克兰语朗诵了谢夫钦科的诗篇。
    出席宴会的苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫、文化部苏联专家谢维林等人也在宴会上讲话。
    出席宴会的有中国电影界著名人士和文化部电影事业管理局苏联专家等八十多人。
    新华社9日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天在北京饭店接见苏联电影工作者代表团全体人员。
    沈雁冰和代表团团长卡拉什尼科夫以及全体团员亲切地进行了交谈。
