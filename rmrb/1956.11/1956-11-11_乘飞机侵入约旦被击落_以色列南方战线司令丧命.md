Title: Shot Down While Invading Jordan by Plane, Commander of Israel's Southern Front Dies

Author:

Date: 1956-11-11
Page: 5

    Shot Down While Invading Jordan by Plane
    Commander of Israel's Southern Front Dies
    Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 10th -- According to a broadcast by Jordanian radio on the 9th, Colonel Asaf Simhoni, commander of the Israeli army's Southern Front who commanded the fighting in the Sinai Desert, was killed when the plane he was on was shot down by Jordanian anti-aircraft guns while invading Jordanian airspace. Simhoni is dead.
    The broadcast stated that the Israeli plane was shot down on November 7th, and Israeli official sources have already acknowledged the incident.

### 乘飞机侵入约旦被击落  以色列南方战线司令丧命

1956-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    乘飞机侵入约旦被击落
    以色列南方战线司令丧命
    新华社10日讯　安曼消息：据约旦电台9日广播，指挥以色列军队在西奈沙漠上作战的南方战线司令阿萨夫·西姆霍尼上校乘坐的飞机在侵入约旦领空时被约旦高射炮击落。西姆霍尼已经毙命。
    广播说，这架以色列飞机是在11月7日被击落的，以色列的官方消息已经承认了这件事。
