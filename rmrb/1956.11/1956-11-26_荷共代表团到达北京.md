Title: Dutch Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: The Dutch Communist Party delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to visit and tour China, consisting of delegation leader Annie Averink and members Gerard Maas and Gerrit Taveres, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and General Secretary of the Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Li Chuli and Li Qixin, responsible personnel of the Central Committee's organs.

### 荷共代表团到达北京

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    荷共代表团到达北京
    新华社25日讯　应中共中央邀请前来中国进行访问和参观的荷兰共产党代表团团长安妮·阿夫林克和团员盖拉德·玛斯、盖雷特·台夫里斯一行三人已经在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中共中央政治局委员、中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央委员、中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央机关负责工作人员李初梨、李启新。
