Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Visits the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 17th: The delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, headed by Peng Zhen, with Li Jishen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Bojun, and Hu Ziang as deputy heads, and all members of the delegation, visited the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation this morning, accompanied by Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Tarasov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, received the delegation.

Tarasov welcomed the delegation on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation. He and various ministers introduced the basic situation of the Russian Federation, described the elections and powers of the Supreme Soviet, and answered many questions raised by the delegation members regarding the connection between Soviet representatives and the masses, legislation, and the work of various ministries.

### 我全国人民代表大会代表团  访问俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃

1956-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国人民代表大会代表团
    访问俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃
    据新华社莫斯科17日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团团长彭真、副团长李济深、程潜、章伯钧、胡子昂和全体团员今天上午在中国驻苏联大使刘晓陪同下，访问了俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃。
    俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫接见了代表团。
    塔拉索夫代表俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃向代表团表示欢迎。他并和各位部长介绍了俄罗斯联邦的基本情况，介绍了最高苏维埃的选举和职权，并且回答了代表团人员提出的关于苏维埃代表和群众的联系、关于立法和各部工作等许多问题。
