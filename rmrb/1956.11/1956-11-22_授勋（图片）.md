    Awarding Medals
    Fang Cheng
    The German fascist war criminal Hess, who was sentenced to life imprisonment, demanded to be released on the grounds that the crimes he committed were the same as those committed by the British and French aggressors, but the British and French aggressors were not sentenced to imprisonment.
