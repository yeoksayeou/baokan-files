Title: Soviet Circus Troupe Extends Greetings to PLA Troops Stationed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, November 8th. The Soviet Circus Troupe held a performance for over 6,000 soldiers of the People's Liberation Army land, sea, and air forces stationed in Beijing on the afternoon of November 8th.

	This performance for the Chinese People's Liberation Army soldiers was in keeping with the tradition of Soviet artists performing for Soviet soldiers every October during the anniversary of the Socialist Revolution.

	At the beginning of the performance, Soviet circus performers threw bouquets of flowers to the soldiers in the surrounding stands. The artistic director of the circus, Sudakevich, made brief speeches at the beginning and end of the performance. He said that as they celebrated the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet artists extended their greetings to the glorious Chinese capital and the great Chinese people and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and wished the Chinese People's Liberation Army soldiers even greater achievements in their work for the prosperous future of China.

	The Soviet artists performed more than ten acts, including aerial acrobatics, equestrianism, strength juggling, trapeze artists, and lion-bear performances, which were warmly welcomed by the soldiers.

	After the performance, all the members of the Soviet Circus Troupe met with the audience. The entire audience stood up and applauded the Soviet artists' wonderful performance once again. Chen Yading, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke on behalf of the soldiers, thanking the Soviet artists for their warm greetings to the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 苏联马戏团慰问解放军驻京部队

1956-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联马戏团慰问解放军驻京部队
    新华社8日讯　苏联马戏团8日下午为中国人民解放军陆海空军驻北京部队战士六千多人举行表演会。
    这是按照每逢十月社会主义革命节，苏联艺术家们就要为苏军战士进行慰问演出的传统而为中国人民解放军战士们举行的一次表演会。
    表演会开始，苏联马戏团演员把一束束鲜花掷给四周看台上的战士们。马戏团艺术指导苏达凯维奇在表演开始前和结束时作了简单的致词。他说，当庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年的时候，苏联艺术家们向光荣的中国首都和伟大的中国人民、中国人民解放军战士致以敬礼，并祝中国人民解放军战士们在为中国的繁荣未来所进行的工作中获得更大的成就。
    苏联艺术家们表演了空中体操、马术、力量杂耍、空中飞人、耍狮熊等十多个节目，受到全场战士的热烈欢迎。
    节目表演完毕，苏联马戏团全体演员同观众会见。全场起立，再一次为苏联艺术家的精采表演鼓掌。中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长陈亚丁代表战士们讲了话，感谢苏联艺术家对中国人民解放军的热情的慰问。
