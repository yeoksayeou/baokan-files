Title: Some Italians and Canadians Volunteer to Help Egypt Resist Anglo-French Aggression
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 2nd: The Egyptian Embassy in Italy announced on November 1st that some Italians have already expressed their willingness to help Egypt resist the Anglo-French aggression.

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, November 2nd: Egyptian Ambassador to Canada, Hussein El-Khatib, told a United Press reporter on November 1st that the Egyptian Embassy had received applications from "many Canadians" volunteering to help Egypt resist the Anglo-French aggression.

### 有些意大利人和加拿大人  志愿帮助埃及抵抗英法侵略

1956-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    有些意大利人和加拿大人
    志愿帮助埃及抵抗英法侵略
    新华社2日讯　罗马消息：埃及驻意大利大使馆11月1日宣布：已经有一些意大利人表示志愿帮助埃及抵抗英法的侵略。
    新华社2日讯　渥太华消息：埃及驻加拿大大使侯赛尼·哈蒂布11月1日在接见合众社记者的时候说，埃及大使馆已经接到了“许多加拿大人”志愿去帮助埃及抵抗英法侵略的申请。
