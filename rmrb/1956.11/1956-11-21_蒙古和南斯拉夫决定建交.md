Title: Mongolia and Yugoslavia Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 20th: Mongolian newspapers reported today that Mongolia and Yugoslavia have recently decided, after negotiations, to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives.

### 蒙古和南斯拉夫决定建交

1956-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古和南斯拉夫决定建交
    新华社乌兰巴托20日电　蒙古报纸今天报道，蒙古和南斯拉夫两国最近经过谈判，决定建立外交关系，并且互派外交代表。
