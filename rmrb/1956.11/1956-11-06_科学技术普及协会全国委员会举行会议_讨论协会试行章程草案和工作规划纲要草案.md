Title: National Committee of the Association for Science and Technology Popularization Holds Meeting to Discuss Draft Articles of Association and Draft Outline of Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: The National Committee of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization held its second plenary meeting of the first National Committee in Beijing today to discuss the draft articles of association for the association on a trial basis and the draft outline of the association's work plan for the period from 1957 to 1962.

    The meeting also heard a report on the association's work over the past six years. The report pointed out that, with the exception of Tibet and Taiwan, which is yet to be liberated, science popularization association branches have been established in all provinces, cities, and autonomous regions across the country, and the number of members nationwide has grown to over 110,000. More than 90,000 science lectures and more than 3,000 exhibitions have been held across various regions. Four national popular science journals and more than 2,000 popular science booklets and lecture materials have been published.

    A total of sixty national committee members and branch representatives attended the meeting, and two hundred and five people attended as observers.

### 科学技术普及协会全国委员会举行会议  讨论协会试行章程草案和工作规划纲要草案

1956-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    科学技术普及协会全国委员会举行会议
    讨论协会试行章程草案和工作规划纲要草案
    据新华社5日讯　中华全国科学技术普及协会今天在北京举行第一届全国委员会第二次全体会议，讨论了协会试行章程草案和协会在1957年到1962年期间的工作规划纲要草案。
    会议并且听取了协会六年来的工作报告。报告中指出：除西藏和待解放的台湾外，全国各省、市、自治区都已经成立了科普协会分会，全国会员已发展到十一万人以上。各地进行了科学讲演九万多次，展览三千多次。出版了四种全国性的通俗科学期刊，两千多种通俗科学小册子和讲演资料。
    出席会议的全国委员和分会代表共有六十人，列席会议的有二百零五人。
