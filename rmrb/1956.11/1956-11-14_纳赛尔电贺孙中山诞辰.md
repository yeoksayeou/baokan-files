Title: Nasser Sends Telegram Congratulating on Sun Yat-sen's Birthday

Author:

Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Director of the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth. The full text is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Director of the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen:

Due to the special circumstances currently facing Egypt, it is impossible to send a delegation to attend the commemoration of the 90th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Today, we express our special respect to the admirable son of the great Chinese people, whose struggle has inspired and empowered not only his people but also all oppressed and awakened people around the world. The Egyptian Ambassador to China will attend the commemoration of the 90th anniversary of the birth of this great son of the Chinese people, who fought against colonialism for freedom and dignity for forty years.

Gamal Abdel Nasser.

### 纳赛尔电贺孙中山诞辰

1956-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔电贺孙中山诞辰
    新华社13日讯　埃及总统加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔给孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会主任周恩来的电报，全文如下：孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会主任：周恩来阁下：
    由于埃及目前所处的特殊情况，以致不能派遣一个代表团前来参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念大会。我们在今天对伟大的中国人民的可敬的儿子表示特别的敬意，他的斗争不但给了他的人民，而且也给予全世界一切被压迫和觉悟了的人民以鼓舞和力量。埃及驻中国大使将参加纪念这位在四十年中为争取自由和尊严而进行反殖民主义斗争的中国人民的伟大儿子诞生九十周年的纪念会。
    加麦尔·阿卜杜勒，纳赛尔。
