Title: Syrian President Kuwatly Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st. Syrian President Shukri al-Kuwatly, who is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and personally by Voroshilov, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of October 31.

Welcoming him at the Central Airport were Voroshilov, Bulganin, Shepilov, Tarasov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, ministers, representatives of the working people, and foreign diplomatic envoys.

Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, said in his speech that the Soviet people deeply sympathize with the struggle of the heroic Syrian people and the people of other Arab countries. The people of these countries are striving to consolidate peace and international security, to eliminate the remaining poisons of colonial rule, to consolidate their national independence, and to achieve the comprehensive development of their countries. He said that we are pleased that the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Syria are expanding day by day, and the friendship between the Soviet and Syrian people is as strong as Damascus steel. Voroshilov concluded by saying that he believes that the President's visit will be a new and important stage in the development of Soviet-Syrian friendly relations and will be an important contribution to world peace.

President Kuwatly said in his reply that he was very pleased to visit this country which has shattered the shackles of imperialism, liberated itself, and pointed the way to the people of other countries. He said that we have a long-standing friendship with the Soviet Union, and this visit is to further strengthen and develop this friendly relationship. He finally thanked the Soviet people for their warm welcome and reception along the way.

### 叙总统库阿特利到达莫斯科

1956-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    叙总统库阿特利到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科1日电　应苏联最高苏维埃主席团和伏罗希洛夫个人的邀请访问苏联的叙利亚总统舒·库阿特利，10月31日下午乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    到中央机场欢迎的，有伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、谢皮洛夫、俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫、各部长、劳动人民代表以及各国外交使节等。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫致词说，苏联人极其同情英勇的叙利亚人民和其他阿拉伯国家人民的斗争。这些国家的人民正在为巩固和平和国际安全、为消除殖民统治的余毒、为巩固本国的民族独立、为本国的全面发展而奋斗。他说，我们感到满意的是，苏联和叙利亚的友好关系日益扩大，苏叙两国人民的友谊像大马士革钢一样的坚实。伏罗希洛夫最后说，我相信，总统先生的这次访问在苏叙友好关系的发展中将是一个新的重要阶段，将是对世界和平的重要贡献。
    库阿特利总统致答词说，我访问这个已经打碎了帝国主义枷锁的、解放了的、并且向其他国家人民指出道路的国家感到很高兴。他说，我们同苏联有着悠久的友谊，我们这次访问是为了进一步加强和发展这种友好关系。他最后感谢沿途受到苏联人民的热烈欢迎和接待。
