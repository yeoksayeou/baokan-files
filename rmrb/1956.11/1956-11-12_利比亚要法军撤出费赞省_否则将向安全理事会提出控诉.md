Title: Libya Demands French Troops Withdraw from Fezzan Province; Otherwise, It Will File a Complaint with the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, November 11th: "Al Raid" newspaper reports that the French government has notified the Libyan government that it agrees to withdraw a portion of French troops from Fezzan Province. The newspaper states that the Libyan government has rejected this suggestion, declaring its determination to achieve the complete withdrawal of French troops from Fezzan Province.

The newspaper specifically points out that if France does not completely withdraw French troops from this province within the timeframe stipulated by the Franco-Libyan treaty, the Libyan government will bring this issue before the Security Council.

### 利比亚要法军撤出费赞省  否则将向安全理事会提出控诉

1956-11-12
第7版()
专栏：

    利比亚要法军撤出费赞省
    否则将向安全理事会提出控诉
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社的黎波里11日讯：“拉伊德报”报道，法国政府已经通知利比亚政府，它同意从费赞省撤出一部分法国军队。报纸说，但是利比亚政府拒绝接受这个建议，它声明要实现使法国军队全部撤出费赞省的决心。
    报纸特别指出，如果法国不在法国—利比亚条约所规定的期限内将法国军队全部撤出这个省，利比亚政府将把这个问题提到安全理事会去。
