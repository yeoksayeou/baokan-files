Title: Our Military Delegation Visits Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 8th. Sofia dispatch: The Chinese military delegation headed by General Deng Hua, at the invitation of the Bulgarian Ministry of National Defense, arrived in Sofia by plane from Yugoslavia on November 3rd for a visit. That evening, Bulgarian Minister of National Defense Panchevski hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation. Starting from the 5th, the delegation split into three groups—naval, land, and air—to begin their tours and visits.

### 我军事代表团到保加利亚访问

1956-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团到保加利亚访问
    新华社布拉格8日电　索非亚消息：由邓华上将率领的中国军事代表团，应保加利亚国防部的邀请，在11月3日由南斯拉夫乘飞机到达索非亚进行访问。当天晚上，保加利亚国防部长潘切夫斯基设宴欢迎代表团。从5日起，代表团即分海、陆、空三组开始进行参观和访问。
