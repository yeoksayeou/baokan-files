Title: Yugoslav Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th. The Yugoslav film delegation, invited to China to attend the opening ceremony of the Yugoslav Film Week and to pay a friendly visit, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

    The head of the delegation is Samuilo Amadzai, Chairman of the Management Committee of the Yugoslav Film Production Association and Manager of the Newsreel Company. The members of the delegation are: Georgi Skrigin, Secretary-General of the Yugoslav Film Workers Association and director of films such as "The Two of Them"; Vladimir Pogacic, Director of the Yugoslav Film Archive, screenwriter, and director of films such as "Old Grudges and New仇"; Julifa Starich, film actress and female lead in films such as "Decisive Moment"; Dragutin Todic, film actor and one of the male leads in the film "The Two of Them"; and Ivan Marinček, cinematographer for films such as "The Adventures of Little Warrior" and "Decisive Moment".

    The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Situ Huimin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, Zhang Jichun, Director of the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture, and Chinese film actors, and received flowers from Chinese film workers.

### 南斯拉夫电影代表团到达北京

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫电影代表团到达北京
    新华社25日讯　应邀来中国参加南斯拉夫电影周开幕式并进行友好访问的南斯拉夫电影代表团一行六人今天下午到达北京。
    代表团团长是南斯拉夫电影生产联谊会管理委员会主席和新闻影片公司经理萨穆伊洛·阿玛达伊。团员是：南斯拉夫电影工作者联谊会秘书长、影片“他俩”等的导演格奥尔吉·斯克里京，南斯拉夫电影资料馆馆长、电影剧本作家、“旧恨新仇”等影片的导演符拉迪米尔·泡加契奇，电影演员、“当机立断”等影片的女主角尤利法·斯塔里奇，电影演员、影片“他俩”的男主角之一德拉古廷·托迪奇，“小勇士历险记”、“当机立断”等影片的摄影师伊万·马临切克。
    代表团人员在飞机场上受到文化部电影事业管理局副局长司徒慧敏，北京市文化局局长张季纯以及中国电影演员们的热烈欢迎，并且接受了中国电影工作者的献花。
