Title: Six Writers Visit the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia Separately
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency News:

    Zhang Jin, director of the Chinese Writers Association and writer, and poets Kuang Min and Ge Bizhou, left Beijing by plane for Moscow on the 15th. They are going for a friendly visit under the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Liu Baiyu, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association and writer, and writers Wu Zuxiang and Kong Luosun left Beijing by plane for Belgrade on the 10th and will pay friendly visits to various parts of Yugoslavia.

### 六位作家分别赴苏联和南斯拉夫访问

1956-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    六位作家分别赴苏联和南斯拉夫访问
    新华社讯　中国作家协会理事、作家章靳以，诗人厂民、戈壁舟15日从北京乘飞机赴莫斯科。他们是根据中苏文化合作协定1956年执行计划前去作友好访问的。
    中国作家协会书记处第一书记、作家刘白羽，作家吴组缃、孔罗荪在10日从北京乘飞机赴贝尔格莱德，将在南斯拉夫各地作友好访问。
