Title: Order and Calm Being Restored in Various Parts of Hungary
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 7th: According to TASS, Budapest, 6th: Order and calm are gradually being restored in various parts of Hungary. According to a broadcast by Budapest Radio, Székesfehérvár, southwest of Budapest, is very calm, and the workers and staff of various factories and enterprises have already started working.

    The schools in Szolnok will reopen on November 7.

    In Nyíregyháza, in eastern Hungary, the last group of counter-revolutionaries has been disarmed. Order is being restored here.

    The Workers' Revolutionary Committee of Szabolcs-Szatmár-Bereg County called on workers throughout the state to resume work.

    In view of the difficult situation caused by the counter-revolutionary riots in the country, the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government announced that November 7 would not be a holiday.

    "Free People's Daily," the central organ of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, was published on November 6. The newspaper published the "Letter to the Hungarian People" and many other proclamations of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government. It also published the "Letter to the Hungarian People" from the headquarters of the Soviet troops stationed in Hungary.

    "Free People's Daily" reported on the meeting between Kádár, Prime Minister of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, and the working people of Szolnok.

### 匈牙利各地正恢复秩序和平静

1956-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利各地正恢复秩序和平静
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯6日讯：匈牙利各地正在逐渐恢复秩序和平静。据布达佩斯电台广播，布达佩斯西南的塞克什费黑瓦尔已经十分平静，各工厂企业的职工都已经开始工作。
    索尔诺克的各学校将在11月7日复课。
    在匈牙利东部的尼雷吉哈佐，最后的一批反革命分子已经被解除了武装。这里的秩序正在恢复。
    萨伯尔齐—沙特马尔州的工人革命委员会号召全州工人恢复工作。
    鉴于反革命暴动在国内所造成的困难情况，匈牙利工农革命政府宣布11月7日不休假。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央机关报“自由人民报”11月6日出版了。报纸刊登了匈牙利工农革命政府的“告匈牙利人民书”等许多文告。还登载了驻匈牙利苏军司令部“告匈牙利人民书”。
    “自由人民报”报道了匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔同索尔诺克市劳动人民会见的时候谈话的情况。
