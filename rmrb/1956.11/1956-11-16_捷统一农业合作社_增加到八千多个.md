Title: Czech Unified Agricultural Cooperatives Increased to Over Eight Thousand
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 15th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency: As of October 31st, the number of unified agricultural cooperatives in Czechoslovakia has increased to 8,166. The total land they cultivate accounts for 36.14% of the total agricultural land area included in the state's planned economy.

During the period from January to October this year, a total of 1,323 new cooperatives were established nationwide, with 38,000 members and 192,000 hectares of land. The original cooperatives also added 14,000 new members and more than 170,000 hectares of land.

### 捷统一农业合作社  增加到八千多个

1956-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    捷统一农业合作社
    增加到八千多个
    据新华社布拉格15日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：截至10月31日为止，捷克斯洛伐克的统一农业合作社增加到了八，一六六个。它们耕种的土地总共占列入各州计划经济范围内的农业土地总面积的36.14％。
    在今年1月到10月这段期间，全国总共成立了一千三百二十三个新社，它们拥有三万八千个社员和十九万二千公顷的土地。原有的合作社还增加了一万四千个新社员，十七万多公顷的土地。
