Title: Soviet and Polish Delegations Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th - TASS: On November 17th, the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government continued talks with the delegation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Government of the Polish People's Republic.

Participating in the talks on the Soviet side were Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Mikoyan, and Saburov. On the Polish side were: Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and Jędrychowski.

Present at the talks on the Soviet side were: Alternate Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union Marshal Zhukov, Minister of Railways Beshchev, Minister of Finance Zverev, Minister of Foreign Trade Kabanov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Patolichev, and Soviet Ambassador to Poland Ponomarenko, etc.; on the Polish side were also: Members of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Szyr, Wierna, Trąmpczyński, and Kotlicki.

The two delegations listened to and discussed the suggestions of experts from both countries. Further useful exchanges of views were made on the issues raised by both sides. The participants in the talks agreed to issue a joint statement.

### 苏联和波兰代表团继续会谈

1956-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和波兰代表团继续会谈
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：11月17日，苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府代表团同波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰人民共和国政府代表团继续会谈。
    参加会谈的，苏联方面有赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、米高扬和萨布罗夫。波兰方面有：哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹和英德里霍夫斯基。
    会谈时在座的，苏联方面有：苏共中央委员会主席团候补委员、苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅、交通部长别谢夫、财政部长兹维列夫、对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫和外交部副部长帕托利切夫、苏联驻波兰大使波诺马连科等；波兰方面还有：波兰统一工人党中央委员西尔、维尔娜、特朗普琴斯基和科特利次基。
    双方代表团听取和讨论了两国专家的建议。对双方提出来的问题作了进一步有益的交换意见。会谈参加者同意发表一项联合声明。
