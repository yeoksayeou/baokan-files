Title: Nehru Disagrees with UN Supervision of Hungarian Elections
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 13th - Prime Minister Nehru said on the 12th: "The substantive part of the United Nations resolution on the Hungarian issue is incorrect."
    The substantive part of this resolution, which interferes in Hungary's internal affairs, calls for the withdrawal of Soviet troops and the holding of so-called "free elections" in Hungary under United Nations supervision.
    He made these remarks to reporters at the airport on the morning of the 12th upon returning to New Delhi from Calcutta.

### 尼赫鲁不同意联合国监督匈牙利选举

1956-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁不同意联合国监督匈牙利选举
    新华社新德里13日电　尼赫鲁总理12日说：“联合国关于匈牙利问题决议的实效部分是不对的。”
    这个干涉匈牙利内政的决议的实效部分要求撤退苏联军队和在匈牙利举行由联合国监督的所谓“自由选举”。
    他是在12日早上从加尔各答回到新德里的时候在机场上对新闻记者说这番话的。
