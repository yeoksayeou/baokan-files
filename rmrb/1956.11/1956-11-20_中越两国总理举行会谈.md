Title: Chinese and Vietnamese Premiers Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 19th. Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, received Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, at 3 p.m. today.

    The two Premiers held talks, discussing several issues concerning the two countries.

    Participating in the talks on the Vietnamese side were also Vice-Premiers Phan Ke Toai, Vo Nguyen Giap, several government ministers, and the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, and others; on the Chinese side were also Vice-Premier He Long, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Luo Guibo, and others.

    The talks proceeded in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 中越两国总理举行会谈

1956-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中越两国总理举行会谈
    新华社河内19日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同在今天下午三时接见了中华人民共和国总理周恩来。
    两位总理举行了会谈，讨论了有关两国的若干问题。
    参加会谈的，越南方面还有副总理潘继遂、武元甲，几位政府部长和越南驻中国大使黄文欢等；中国方面还有副总理贺龙、中国驻越南大使罗贵波等。
    会谈在十分亲切友好的气氛中进行。
