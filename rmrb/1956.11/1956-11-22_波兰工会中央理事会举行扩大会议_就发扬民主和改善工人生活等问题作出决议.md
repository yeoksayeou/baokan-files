Title: Polish Central Council of Trade Unions Holds Expanded Meeting, Adopts Resolutions on Issues Such as Promoting Democracy and Improving Workers' Lives
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-22
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, November 21st. According to a report by the Polish Press Agency: The Ninth Expanded Plenary Meeting of the Polish Central Council of Trade Unions passed a resolution requesting that trade unions send representatives to parliament, and requiring the government to consult with trade unions before making any major decisions on economic measures.

    The resolution also expressed the hope to improve the living conditions of Polish workers, and to ensure that there would be no more violations of the rule of law, collective contracts, and the legitimate rights and interests of the working class. It should be clearly stipulated the principles for issuing alimony, pensions, and family allowances.

    The resolution also suggested that trade union organizations guarantee to help employees in the work of establishing workers' autonomous institutions, and support the initiatives of the working people aimed at strengthening enterprise autonomy. The federations of various industrial trade unions are scheduled to hold a special congress before the second quarter of next year.

    This meeting of the Polish Central Council of Trade Unions was held in Warsaw from November 16th to 19th.

    TASS, Warsaw, November 19th. The Ninth Expanded Plenary Meeting of the Polish Central Council of Trade Unions was held in Warsaw from November 16th to 19th.

    The plenary meeting discussed the work of the Central Council in the past period, and formulated the tasks of the trade union movement in accordance with the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Meeting of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. Speakers at the plenary discussion criticized the work of the Trade Union Central Council and proposed ways to improve trade union work in enterprises and further promote internal trade union democracy. The issue of striving for further improvement of the material living conditions of the working people occupied a particularly important place in the discussion.

    Matewin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and Jaroszewicz, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, spoke at the meeting. The meeting passed a resolution defining the tasks of further developing the Polish trade union movement.

    The plenary meeting elected a new Presidium of the Polish Central Council of Trade Unions consisting of thirty-eight members. Loga-Sowinski was elected Chairman of the Trade Union Central Council.

### 波兰工会中央理事会举行扩大会议  就发扬民主和改善工人生活等问题作出决议

1956-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰工会中央理事会举行扩大会议
    就发扬民主和改善工人生活等问题作出决议
    新华社21日讯　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰工会中央理事会第九次扩大全体会议通过了一项决议，要求让工会派代表出席议会，并且要求政府在采取经济措施方面的任何重要决定以前，应该与工会商量。
    决议还希望改善波兰工人的生活条件，并且希望今后不再有破坏法制、集体合同和工人阶级正当权益的事情发生，应该明确规定发给赡养费、养老金和家庭津贴的原则。
    决议还建议工会组织保证在建立工人自治机构的工作中帮助职工，支持劳动人民以加强企业自治为目的的倡议。各产业工会的联合会定于明年第二季度以前举行一次特别代表大会。
    波兰工会中央理事会的这一次会议是从11月16日到19日在华沙举行的。
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社华沙19日讯：波兰工会中央理事会第九次扩大全体会议11月16日到19日在华沙举行。
    全体会议讨论了中央理事会在过去一个时期的工作，并且根据波兰统一工人党中央委员会第八次全体会议的决议拟定了工会运动的任务。在全会讨论时发言的人批评了工会中央理事会的工作，提出了改进企业中的工会工作、进一步发扬工会内部民主的办法。关于为进一步提高劳动人民的物质生活条件而奋斗的问题在讨论中占有特别重要地位。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记马特温和部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇在会上发了言。会议通过了一项规定进一步发展波兰工会运动的任务的决议。
    全体会议选出了由三十八人组成的新的波兰工会中央理事会主席团。洛加—索文斯基当选为工会中央理事会主席。
