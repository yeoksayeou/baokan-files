Title: Kachin State Leaders of Burma Leave Beijing for Visits to Various Places

Author:

Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th November. The Kachin State leaders of the Union of Burma, U Zanta Sin, Shinwa Nau, Duwa Zao Lang, and the Kachin State Secretary U San Lone, accompanied by Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit various places.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Dong Yueqian, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

During their stay in Beijing, U Zanta Sin and others visited some factories and schools and toured the capital's scenic spots and historical sites.

### 缅甸克钦邦领导人离北京赴各地参观

1956-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦领导人离北京赴各地参观
    新华社7日讯　缅甸联邦克钦邦领导人吴赞塔信、申瓦闹、杜瓦藻朗和克钦邦秘书吴珊龙等一行四人，由外交部礼宾司副司长王雨田陪同，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京到各地参观。
    到机场送别的，有外交部办公厅主任董越千，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋和礼宾司副司长马振武等人。
    吴赞塔信等在北京期间，曾经参观了一些工厂和学校，游览了首都的名胜古迹。
