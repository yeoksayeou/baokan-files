Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Arrive in Gangtok, Sikkim
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 24th. According to the Indian Press Trust, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni arrived in Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, today, en route to New Delhi, India, to attend the 2,500th Anniversary of Sakyamuni’s Nirvana.

The Maharaja of Sikkim, in accordance with Sikkimese tradition, greeted the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni at a location one mile outside of Gangtok.

### 达赖和班禅到达锡金首府甘托

1956-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖和班禅到达锡金首府甘托
    新华社新德里24日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在前往印度新德里参加释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念大会的途中在今天到达锡金的首府甘托。
    锡金的大君马哈拉加依照锡金的传统到离甘托一英里以外的地方，迎接达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
