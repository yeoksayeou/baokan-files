Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Regarding All Instances of Control of Counter-Revolutionary Elements to be Judged by the People's Courts
(Adopted at the 51st Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on November 16, 1956)
    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress decided at its 51st meeting on November 16, 1956:
    From now on, the control of counter-revolutionary elements and other criminals shall be judged by the People's Courts in accordance with the law and executed by the public security organs.
    During the period of control, if a controlled person is found to have committed new crimes, requiring an extension of the control period, or if, due to good behavior and meritorious service, it is necessary to shorten the control period or revoke the control in advance, it must also be judged or ruled by the People's Court in accordance with the law.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于对反革命分子的管制一律由人民法院判决的决定（1956年11月16日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议通过）

1956-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于对反革命分子的管制一律由人民法院判决的决定
      （1956年11月16日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十一次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年11月16日第五十一次会议决定：
    今后对反革命分子和其他犯罪分子的管制，一律由人民法院依法判决，交由公安机关执行。
    在管制期间的被管制分子，如果被发现有新的犯罪行为，需要延长管制期限，或者因为表现良好，立有功绩，需要缩短管制期限或者提前撤销管制，也须经人民法院依法判决或者裁定。
