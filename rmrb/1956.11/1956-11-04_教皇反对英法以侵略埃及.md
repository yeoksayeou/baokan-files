Title: Pope Opposes Anglo-French Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 3rd: Vatican sources have expressed opposition to the Anglo-French-Israeli aggression against Egypt. Pope Pius XII, in a circular to all Christian archbishops worldwide, said: "When reasonable people sincerely desire peace, they should come together to resolve the serious problems related to them, and hope to resolve them justly, rather than using violence."

### 教皇反对英法以侵略埃及

1956-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    教皇反对英法以侵略埃及
    据新华社3日讯　据塔斯社罗马3日讯：梵蒂冈人士表示反对英法以三国侵略埃及的行动。教皇庇护第十二在致全世界基督教大主教的通函中说：“当理智的人们真诚地盼望和平的时候，他们要聚合起来，以解决同他们有关系的严重问题，并且希望正义地解决，而不是用暴力来解决。”
