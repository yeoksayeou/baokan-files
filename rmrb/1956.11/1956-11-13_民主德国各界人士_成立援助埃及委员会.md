Title: People from All Circles in the German Democratic Republic Establish Committee to Aid Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 4

    People from All Circles in the German Democratic Republic
    Establish Committee to Aid Egypt
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 11th, TASS: Prominent social figures, responsible government personnel, and representatives from the scientific, cultural, and trade union sectors of the German Democratic Republic, after a meeting in Berlin, established the "Friendship and Aid to Egypt Committee." Professor Brugsch was elected as chairman of the committee.

### 民主德国各界人士  成立援助埃及委员会

1956-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国各界人士
    成立援助埃及委员会
    据新华社12日讯　塔斯社柏林11日讯：民主德国著名社会人士、政府负责人员以及科学界、文化界和工会的代表在柏林举行会议后，成立了“友谊援助埃及委员会”。布鲁格希教授当选为委员会主席。
