Title: Guo Moruo and Li Siguang Telegraph Egyptian Scientific Circles Supporting the Anti-Aggression Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 1

    To the Egyptian Council of Science and Scientific Associations:
    We, Chinese scientists, along with all the Chinese people, feel boundless anger towards the Anglo-French imperialist aggression against Egypt and Israel's armed provocation. This barbaric act of aggression not only destroys peace in the Asian-African region, but also seriously threatens world peace and the safety of billions of people.
    The Chinese people established the Chinese People's Committee to Support Egypt Against Aggression on the 8th, and decided to organize manpower and material resources to realize the volunteer support for Egypt. All Chinese scientific workers are members of the committee.
    We, Chinese scientific workers, firmly support the just struggle of the Egyptian people to defend national independence and oppose aggression. We believe that the forces of peace will surely achieve final victory.
		　Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
                   Li Siguang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies
		　November 13, 1956

### 郭沫若李四光电埃及科学界支持反侵略斗争

1956-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若李四光电埃及科学界支持反侵略斗争埃及科学理事会及科学协会
    中国的科学工作者和全体中国人民一道，对英、法帝国主义者的侵略埃及和以色列的武装挑衅行为感到无比的愤怒。这种野蛮的侵略行为不仅破坏了亚非地区的和平，并且严重地威胁着世界和平和亿万人的安全。
    中国人民已于8日成立中国人民支援埃及反抗侵略委员会，决组织人力物力实现支援埃及的志愿，全体中国科学工作者均为该委员会的成员。
    我们中国的科学工作者坚决支持埃及人民保卫民族独立、反对侵略的正义斗争。我们相信和平力量必将取得最后的胜利。	         　中国科学院院长  郭沫若
                   中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席　李四光		　1956年11月13日
