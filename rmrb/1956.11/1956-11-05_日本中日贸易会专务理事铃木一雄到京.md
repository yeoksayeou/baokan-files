Title: Japan-China Trade Association Executive Director Kazuo Suzuki Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Kazuo Suzuki, Executive Director of the Japan-China Trade Association, and his secretary, Kazuyo Morikawa, arrived in Beijing on the 3rd at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 日本中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄到京

1956-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    日本中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄到京
    新华社4日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问的日本中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄和秘书森川和代，于3日到达北京。
