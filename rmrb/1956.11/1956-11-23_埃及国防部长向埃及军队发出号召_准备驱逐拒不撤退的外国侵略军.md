Title: Egyptian Defense Minister Issues Call to Egyptian Army: Prepare to Expel Foreign Aggressor Troops Refusing to Withdraw
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd, Cairo News: According to Cairo Radio, Egyptian Defense Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Army, Amer, called on the Egyptian army on November 21st to maintain readiness. He said that if the imperialists do not leave, they must fight for the liberation of Egypt.

Amer told the troops withdrawing from the Sinai Peninsula: "If it were not for the intervention of imperialist countries in these battles, you would have been able to completely annihilate the enemy." He said: "I have unlimited confidence and trust in all personnel of the armed forces."

Amer pointed out that the main reason for withdrawing from the Sinai Peninsula was "to preserve our army in order to meet the main enemy, and to enable the army to fight again under better conditions." He said: "Some of the equipment we lost is easy to replenish. We must keep the Egyptian soldiers, and we have successfully implemented this withdrawal operation."

### 埃及国防部长向埃及军队发出号召  准备驱逐拒不撤退的外国侵略军

1956-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及国防部长向埃及军队发出号召
    准备驱逐拒不撤退的外国侵略军
    新华社22日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台广播，埃及国防部长兼陆军总司令阿密尔11月21日号召埃及军队保持准备。他说，如果帝国主义者不离开，就要为解放埃及而斗争。
    阿密尔对从西奈半岛撤退的军队说：“如果不是帝国主义国家在这些战斗中进行干涉，你们是能够完全歼灭敌人的。”他说：“我对武装部队的全体人员有无限的信心和信任。”
    阿密尔指出，撤出西奈半岛的主要原因是“保存我们的军队以便迎击主要的敌人，并且使军队能够在较好的条件下再进行战斗”。他说：“我们损失的一些装备很容易补充。我们要保持埃及的士兵，我们成功地实行了这个撤退行动。”
