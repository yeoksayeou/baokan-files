Title: My Olympic Athletes Protest the International Olympic Committee's Conspiracy to Create "Two Chinas"
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 8th. Chinese Olympic athletes support the decision of the Chinese Olympic Committee not to participate in the 16th Olympic Games until the issue of the International Olympic Committee revoking Taiwan's right to send athletes independently is resolved. Chinese athletes express great indignation at the International Olympic Committee's conspiracy to create "two Chinas." Shi Wanchun, center forward of the Chinese football team, said: Recently, some people have been trying to create "two Chinas" in international organizations, but this conspiracy has long been seen through by the awakened Chinese people, and we will never fall for it. I fully endorse and support the Chinese Olympic Committee's decision not to participate in this Olympic Games and its protest to the International Olympic Committee. Chen Jingkai, the world record holder in the snatch with 133 kg in the lightest weight class of weightlifting, said: Not participating in this Olympic Games only means one less opportunity for me to win glory for the motherland. In a recent practice test, my total score reached 320 kg, and my snatch was 135 kg. However, in order to safeguard the interests of the motherland, we will never let the International Olympic Committee's conspiracy to create "two Chinas" succeed. I will work harder to prepare for winning greater honor for the motherland in the future.

The basketball player of the Chinese Olympic team, Wang Zhaoyu, the gymnast Chen Xiaozhang, the shooter Zhang Hong, and the track and field athlete Jiang Yumin, have all written to the newspaper to express their opinions and determination. They all protest the International Olympic Committee's conspiracy to create "two Chinas."

This newspaper reports that more than 300 representatives of sports workers in Beijing gathered at Shichahai Sports Field in Beijing on the afternoon of the 7th to protest the International Olympic Committee's conspiracy to create "two Chinas."

All athletes, referees, and staff participating in the 1956 Guizhou Provincial Zonal Basketball and Volleyball Championships in Guiyang also sent a telegram on November 4th, protesting the International Olympic Committee's political conspiracy to create "two Chinas."

### 我奥林匹克选手  抗议国际奥委会制造“两个中国”的阴谋

1956-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    我奥林匹克选手
    抗议国际奥委会制造“两个中国”的阴谋
    新华社广州8日电　中国奥林匹克选手拥护中国奥林匹克委员会在国际奥林匹克委员会取消台湾单独派运动员参加第十六届奥运会权利的问题没有解决以前，不参加这一届奥林匹克运动会的决定。中国选手们对国际奥委会制造“两个中国”的阴谋表示十分愤慨。中国足球队的中锋史万春说：最近以来，不断有人想在国际组织中制造“两个中国”，可是这种阴谋早就被觉醒的中国人民所识破，我们绝不会上这种当。我完全拥护和支持中国奥林匹克委员会不参加这一届奥林匹克运动会的决定和对国际奥林匹克委员会的抗议。最轻量级举重双手挺举一百三十三公斤的世界纪录保持者陈镜开说：不参加这一届奥林匹克运动会对我来说只不过是少了一次为祖国争光的机会。我在最近一次练习测验中，总成绩达到了三百二十公斤，双手挺举一百三十五公斤。但是为了维护祖国的利益，我们绝不让国际奥林匹克委员会制造“两个中国”的阴谋得逞。我要更进一步勤学苦练，准备将来为祖国争取更大的荣誉。
    中国奥林匹克运动队的篮球选手王兆钰、体操选手陈孝彰、射击选手张鈜和田径选手姜玉民等人都纷纷把自己的意见和决心写给报社，他们都对国际奥林匹克委员会制造“两个中国”的阴谋表示抗议。
    本报讯　北京市体育工作者代表三百余人，7日下午在北京什刹海体育场集会，抗议国际奥林匹克委员会制造“两个中国”的阴谋。
    正在贵阳市参加1956年贵州省分区篮排球锦标赛的全体运动员、裁判员和工作人员也在11月4日发出电报，抗议国际奥林匹克委员会制造“两个中国”的政治阴谋。
