Title: Past Stamps Commemorating Sun Yat-sen to be Organized and Issued by Stamp Company
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The China National Stamp Corporation has recently compiled five sets of previously issued stamps commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and has decided to supply these five sets of stamps in Beijing and Shanghai simultaneously on the day the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications issues stamps commemorating the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth (mid-November).

These five sets of stamps total thirty-nine types, one of which was issued in 1912. This is the first set of stamps commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen, with a total of twelve stamps, each of a different color. The center of this stamp is printed with a half-length portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and below it is printed "In Memory of the Xinhai Revolution" (in English).

### 过去纪念孙中山的邮票  集邮公司将整理发行

1956-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    过去纪念孙中山的邮票
    集邮公司将整理发行
    新华社3日讯　中国集邮公司最近整理出五套过去发行的纪念孙中山先生的邮票，并且决定在邮电部发行纪念孙中山先生诞生九十周年邮票的当天（十一月中旬），在北京、上海同时供应这五套邮票。
    这五套邮票共有三十九种，其中有一套是在1912年发行的。这是第一套纪念孙中山先生的邮票，共有十二枚，每枚的颜色都不同。这种邮票的中央印着孙中山先生的半身像，下面印着“纪念辛亥革命”（英文）。
