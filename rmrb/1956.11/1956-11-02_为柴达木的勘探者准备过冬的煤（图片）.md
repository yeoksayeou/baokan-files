Title: Preparing Coal for Prospectors in Qaidam to Get Through the Winter (Picture)
Author: Yuan Shaoke
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 2

    Preparing Coal for Prospectors in Qaidam to Get Through the Winter
    In the mountains northeast of Da Qaidan in Qinghai, there is a high-quality anthracite mine - Yeyang Coal Mine. In order to supply the people prospecting for oil in the basin with coal to get through the winter, the handicraft industry cooperative is now mining here. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Shaoke photographed.

### 为柴达木的勘探者准备过冬的煤（图片）
袁少柯
1956-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

    为柴达木的勘探者准备过冬的煤
    在青海大柴旦东北的山中，有一个质量很好的无烟煤矿——野羊煤矿。为了把这里的煤供给在盆地里勘探石油的人们过冬，现在手工业联社正在这里进行开采。　新华社记者　袁少柯摄
