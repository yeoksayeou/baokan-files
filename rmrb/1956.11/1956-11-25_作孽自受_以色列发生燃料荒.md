Title: Reap What You Sow: Fuel Shortage in Israel
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, November 23rd: Due to the severe threat of fuel shortages, the Israeli government will implement a series of strict measures to restrict the use of fuel and electricity, starting from November 25th.

　　The "Jerusalem Post" wrote that the government instructed the "Palestine Electric Company," which supplies the majority of the country's electricity needs, to cut electricity production by half.

　　The newspaper wrote that public bus transportation in the country has been greatly reduced, and the use of cars is also restricted.

### 作孽自受  以色列发生燃料荒

1956-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    作孽自受
    以色列发生燃料荒
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社特拉维夫23日讯：由于缺乏燃料的严重威胁，以色列政府将从11月25日开始对使用燃料和电力实行一系列严格限制的措施。
    “耶路撒冷邮报”写道，政府指示供应国内绝大部分电力需要的“巴勒斯坦电力公司”把电力生产缩减一半。
    报纸写道，国内公共汽车运输大大减少，汽车使用也受到限制。
