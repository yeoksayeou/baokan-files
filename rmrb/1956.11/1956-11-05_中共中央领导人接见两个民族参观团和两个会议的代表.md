Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Two National Minority Visiting Groups and Representatives of Two Conferences
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 4th.
The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairman Zhu De, General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, and members of the Political Bureau Lin Boqu and Li Fuchun, received the Gansu Province Agricultural Area National Minority Religious Figures Visiting Group and the Qinghai Province Pastoral Area Minority Nationalities Visiting Group this morning. They also successively received representatives attending the National First Congress of Activists for the Popularization of Science and Technology Among Workers and the representatives attending the National Conference of Advanced Workers in Credit Settlement.
Li Jishen, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun and others also participated in the receptions.

### 中共中央领导人接见两个民族参观团和两个会议的代表

1956-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见两个民族参观团和两个会议的代表
    新华社4日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，总书记邓小平，政治局委员林伯渠、李富春，在今天上午接见了甘肃省农业区民族宗教人士参观团和青海省牧业区少数民族参观团。同时还先后接见了出席全国第一次职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会的代表和出席全国信贷结算先进工作者代表会议的代表。
    李济深、陈叔通、章伯钧等也分别参加了接见。
