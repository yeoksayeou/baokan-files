Title: National Congress of Activists of Revolutionary Martyrs’ Families Continues; Ministry of National Defense and General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Jointly Host Banquet for Congress Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Continuing to follow Chairman Mao's instruction of "Carry forward the revolutionary tradition and strive for greater glory," and to make greater contributions to the cause of socialist construction. This is the guarantee made to the congress by twelve activists participating in the National Congress of Activists in Socialist Construction of Revolutionary Martyrs’ Families, Military Dependents, Disabled Soldiers, and Demobilized Soldiers in their speeches to the congress on the 14th, 15th, and 16th.

Pan Xiaojing, a demobilized soldier who now works in a tin mine at Yunnan Tin Company, said in his speech that the achievements I have made in the past are only a small part of the great socialist cause. I guarantee that in the future I will better follow Chairman Mao's instructions, overcome arrogance and impetuosity, closely unite with the masses, strive to learn technology and improve production operation methods, and always maintain the glorious title of advanced producer. Pan Xiaojing has been successively named an activist in socialist construction for young people in Gejiu City, an advanced producer in Gejiu City, and an activist in socialist construction for demobilized soldiers in the province and city.

Hua Wenqing, a military dependent from Guizhou Province, has been engaged in education for thirty-three years and has been rated as an outstanding teacher. In his speech, he said that in the future, I will continue to learn from the masses and strive to cultivate more talents for socialist construction for the country in my educational work.

Ji Jinsheng, a disabled soldier from Jiangsu Province who had both legs amputated, guaranteed to the congress in his speech that he would humbly learn in the future and constantly improve his political and cultural level; never regard himself as a hero, never be arrogant and complacent, humbly listen to the opinions of the masses, better play an active leading role, and conscientiously do his job well. Ji Jinsheng is now the party branch secretary and director of the agricultural cooperative's supervision committee. In his speech to the congress, he said that he guarantees to bring the spirit of this congress back and implement it among the families of revolutionary martyrs, military dependents, disabled soldiers, and demobilized soldiers, so that it can be transformed into their actions and play a greater role in the cause of socialist construction.

The congress speeches ended today. A total of forty-five people spoke at the congress.

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Ministry of National Defense and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly held a banquet at noon today to entertain all the representatives attending the National Congress of Activists in Socialist Construction of Revolutionary Martyrs’ Families, Military Dependents, Disabled Soldiers, and Demobilized Soldiers.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense, extended a warm welcome to the fathers and mothers of the martyrs and to all the activists on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army at the banquet, and he raised his glass to wish everyone good health and success in their work.

Before the banquet, Marshal Peng Dehuai met with Deng Fangzhi, the mother of martyr Huang Jiguang, Hu Wenxiu, the mother of martyr Liu Hulan, Dong Quanzhong, the father of martyr Dong Cunrui, Luo Diekai, the father of martyr Luo Shengjiao, Peng Laotaitai, the mother of revolutionary martyr Peng Pai, and more than 30 others. Marshal Peng Dehuai had a cordial conversation with them. (Attached picture)

Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received the representatives to the National Congress of Activists in Socialist Construction of Revolutionary Martyrs’ Families, Military Dependents, Disabled Soldiers, and Demobilized Soldiers on November 16. The picture shows Chairman Mao Zedong shaking hands with the mother of martyr Peng Pai (center) during the reception. Photo by Bai Shizao (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 烈军属积极分子代表大会继续进行  国防部和解放军总政治部联合举宴招待大会代表

1956-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    烈军属积极分子代表大会继续进行
    国防部和解放军总政治部联合举宴招待大会代表
    新华社16日讯　继续遵照毛主席“发扬革命传统，争取更大光荣”的指示，在社会主义建设事业中作出更大的贡献。这是参加全国烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人社会主义建设积极分子大会的十二位积极分子在十四、十五、十六三天的大会发言中，向大会提出的保证。
    现在云南锡业公司一家锡矿作工的复员军人潘孝敬在会上发言说，过去我所取得的成绩还只是伟大的社会主义事业中的一小点。我保证今后要更好地遵循毛主席的指示，要戒骄戒躁，紧密团结群众，努力学习技术和改进生产操作方法，永远保持先进生产者的光荣称号。潘孝敬曾先后被评为个旧市青年社会主义建设积极分子、个旧市先进生产者和省、市的复员军人社会主义建设积极分子。
    贵州省军属花文卿已从事教育工作三十三年，曾被评为优秀教师。他在发言中说，今后我还要继续向群众学习，努力在教育工作岗位上为祖国培养更多的社会主义建设人才。
    被截去两腿的江苏省残废军人季金生在发言时向大会提出保证说，他保证今后虚心学习，不断提高自己的政治、文化水平；决不以功臣自居，决不骄傲自满，虚心倾听群众意见，更好地发挥积极带头作用，兢兢业业地做好工作。季金生现在农业社里担任党的分支部书记和农业社监察主任。他在大会发言中说，保证将这次大会精神带回去，贯彻到烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人中去，使之变成他们的行动，在社会主义建设事业中发挥更大的作用。
    大会发言在今天结束。在大会上发言的总共有四十五人。
    新华社16日讯国防部和中国人民解放军总政治部今天中午联合举行宴会，招待出席全国烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人社会主义建设积极分子大会的全体代表。
    国防部部长彭德怀元帅在宴会上代表中国人民解放军向在座的烈士们的父亲和母亲以及各位积极分子表示热诚的欢迎，他举杯祝大家身体健康和工作顺利。
    宴会前，彭德怀元帅接见了黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝、刘胡兰烈士的母亲胡文秀、董存瑞烈士的父亲董全忠、罗盛教烈士的父亲罗迭开、彭湃先烈的母亲彭老太太等三十多人。彭德怀元帅亲切地和他们进行了交谈。（附图片）
    中共中央主席毛泽东、副主席刘少奇、周恩来、陈云，总书记邓小平在11月16日接见了全国烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人社会主义建设积极分子大会的代表。图为接见时毛泽东主席和彭湃烈士的母亲（中）握手。　白世藻摄（新华社稿）
