Title: The Romanian Workers' Party, Government, and Parliament Congratulate the Establishment of the Hungarian Revolutionary Government
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: According to a report by the Romanian News Agency on the 5th, the "Spark" newspaper published today a congratulatory letter from the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Romanian Government, and the Presidium of the National Assembly to the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, the Hungarian working class, and all the people. The congratulatory letter expressed sincere congratulations on the establishment of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government.

The congratulatory letter pointed out: The Romanian people fervently sympathize with the just struggle of the fraternal Hungarian people and fully believe that the Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, which has won the widespread support of the masses, will surely victoriously realize the program it has announced.

The Central Council of Romanian Trade Unions also issued a congratulatory letter to the Hungarian trade union organizations, trade union activists, workers, engineers, and others.

### 罗马尼亚工人党、政府和议会  祝贺匈牙利革命政府成立

1956-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党、政府和议会
    祝贺匈牙利革命政府成立
    据新华社6日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社5日讯：“火花报”今天登载了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会、罗马尼亚政府和国民议会主席团给匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府、匈牙利工人阶级和全体人民的贺信。贺信表示衷心祝贺匈牙利工农革命政府的成立。
    贺信指出：罗马尼亚人民热切地同情兄弟匈牙利人民的正义斗争，并且完全相信，取得人民群众广泛支持的工农革命政府一定能胜利地实现它所公布的纲领。
    罗马尼亚工会中央理事会也发出了给匈牙利工会组织、工会积极分子、工人、工程师等的贺信。
