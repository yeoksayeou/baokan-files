Title: Indian President Prasad Receives Premier Zhou Enlai; Prime Minister Nehru Holds Luncheon to Entertain Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 29th. Indian President Rajendra Prasad received Premier Zhou Enlai at the Presidential Palace at 12:50 noon today.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the State Council Premier's Office Zhang Yan, and Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gong Peng.

During the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome given to him by the Indian government and people.

President Prasad told Premier Zhou Enlai that he had read the report of the Indian agricultural delegation to China, and that he was currently considering taking measures based on the report.

Premier Zhou Enlai said that China and India have many similarities and can therefore exchange experiences. He said that the Chinese agricultural science delegation sent to India had also learned a lot from India.

President Prasad asked Premier Zhou to convey his regards to the Chinese people.

After arriving in New Delhi on November 28, Premier Zhou Enlai called on Indian Prime Minister Nehru that evening. Prime Minister Nehru hosted a luncheon at the Presidential Palace today to entertain Premier Zhou Enlai.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 29th. On the evening of the 28th, Indian Prime Minister Nehru had dinner with Premier Zhou Enlai.

Prior to this, Nehru accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage to watch a shadow play performed by the famous Indian dancer Uday Shankar.

### 印度总统普拉沙德接见周恩来总理  尼赫鲁总理举行午宴招待周恩来总理

1956-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德接见周恩来总理
    尼赫鲁总理举行午宴招待周恩来总理
    新华社新德里29日电　印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德今天中午十二时五十分在总统府接见了周恩来总理。
    陪同受接见的有我外交部部长助理乔冠华、国务院总理办公室副主任张彦和外交部新闻司司长龚澎。
    在谈话的时候，周恩来总理对于印度政府和人民给他的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    普拉沙德总统对周恩来总理说，他看过印度到中国考察农业的代表团的报告书，他说，目前正在考虑根据这个报告书采取措施。
    周恩来总理说，中印两国有很多方面是相似的，因此可以交流经验。他说，中国派到印度去的农业科学考察团也从印度学到了很多东西。
    普拉沙德总统请周总理代他向中国人民致意。
    周恩来总理11月28日到达新德里后，在当天晚上拜会了印度总理尼赫鲁。尼赫鲁总理今天在总统府举行午宴招待周恩来总理。
    新华社新德里29日电　28日晚，印度总理尼赫鲁同周恩来总理共进晚餐。
    在这以前，尼赫鲁陪同周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员观看了印度著名舞蹈家乌·山卡尔表演的影子戏。
