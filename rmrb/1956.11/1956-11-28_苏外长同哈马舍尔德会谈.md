Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Sepilov Holds Talks with Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 27th: According to TASS, Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov met with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on November 26th. During the meeting, they discussed certain issues related to the future work of the Eleventh United Nations General Assembly. Present at the meeting were Kuznetzov and Sobolev of the Soviet delegation, as well as United Nations Under-Secretary-General Tchernychev.

On the same day, Shepilov met with Menon, the head of the Indian delegation to the United Nations General Assembly, and had a lengthy conversation with him about the situation of the Eleventh General Assembly session. Kuznetzov was also present at the conversation.

### 苏外长同哈马舍尔德会谈

1956-11-28
第6版()
专栏：

    苏外长同哈马舍尔德会谈
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫11月26日同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德会谈。会谈期间讨论了同十一届联合国大会今后工作有关的某些问题。会谈时在座的有：苏联代表团人员库兹涅佐夫、索波列夫以及联合国副秘书长切尔内晓夫。
    同一天，谢皮洛夫会见了印度出席联合国大会代表团团长梅农，并同他就第十一届大会会议情况作了长时间谈话。谈话时库兹涅佐夫也在座。
