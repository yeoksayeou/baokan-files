Title: Chen Geng Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Indian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a cocktail party at 6:00 PM tonight to bid farewell to the Indian Military Friendship Delegation visiting China.

    General Chen Geng said in his speech at the cocktail party: The Indian Military Friendship Delegation is about to leave Beijing and return home tomorrow. I would like to ask General Chaudhuri to convey the respect of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to all officers and soldiers of the Indian army, and wish the Indian Military Friendship Delegation a safe journey.

    General J.N. Chaudhuri also spoke, repeatedly thanking all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for their close friendship.

    Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru and his wife, and Military Attaché Malik were invited to attend tonight's cocktail party.

    Also attending the cocktail party were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and military attachés of various countries stationed in China.

    After the cocktail party, a dance party was held.

### 陈赓设宴欢送印度军事代表团

1956-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    陈赓设宴欢送印度军事代表团
    据新华社31日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将今晚六时举行酒会欢送印度军事友好访华代表团。
    陈赓大将在酒会上致词说：印度军事友好访华代表团明天就要离开北京回国了；我请乔杜里将军向印度军队全体官兵转达中国人民解放军全体官兵对他们的敬意，并祝印度军事友好访华代表团旅途平安。
    卓·纳·乔杜里将军也讲了话，他一再感谢中国人民解放军全体官兵对他们的亲密友谊。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁及夫人和武官马立克应邀出席了今晚的酒会。
    出席酒会的还有中国人民解放军的高级将领和各国驻华武官。
    酒会完毕后接着举行了舞会。
