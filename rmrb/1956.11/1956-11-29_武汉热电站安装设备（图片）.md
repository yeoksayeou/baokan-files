Title: Installation of Equipment at Wuhan Thermal Power Plant (Photo)
Author: Li Deyi
Date: 1956-11-29
Page: 2

Wuhan Thermal Power Plant Installing Equipment
The equipment installation project at the Wuhan Thermal Power Plant is adopting the Soviet Union's advanced experience—pre-assembly and then overall installation. The work that originally required two years is expected to be completed in seven months. The equipment installation is now underway. This is a giant gantry crane lifting a filter to prepare for installation assembly. (Photo by Li Deyi, Hubei Daily)

### 武汉热电站安装设备（图片）
李德义
1956-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

    武汉热电站安装设备
    武汉热电站的设备安装工程，由于采用了苏联的先进经验——预先组合然后整体安装的方法，原来需要两年的工作，预计七个月就可以完成。现在设备安装正在紧张地进行。这是巨型的龙门吊车，正起吊一个过滤器，为安装组合作好准备。　李德义摄（湖北日报社稿）
