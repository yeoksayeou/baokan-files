Title: Japanese Hydraulic Science Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The Japanese Hydraulic Science Delegation, led by Taniguchi Saburo, consisting of six members, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, has concluded its investigations in various parts of China. They departed from Beijing by plane today and will return home via Guangzhou.

On the evening of the 23rd, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a farewell banquet.

### 日本水利科学代表团回国

1956-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    日本水利科学代表团回国
    新华社24日讯　应中国科学院邀请来中国访问的以谷口三郎为首的日本水利科学访华代表团一行六人，已结束在中国各地的考察，今天乘飞机离北京，将经过广州返国。
    23日晚，中国科学院副院长吴有训曾设宴送别。
