Title: Speech by Ma Xulun, Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 2

Comrades! Compatriots!

The British and French imperialists have completely torn up the United Nations Charter, abandoned all international law, instigated Israel to attack Egypt, and used this as an excuse to launch a large-scale armed invasion of Egypt. This is not only a serious violation of Egypt's territorial sovereignty but also a serious destruction of world peace. This is absolutely intolerable to the Egyptian people and also intolerable to all peace-loving people. Now the brothers and sisters across Egypt have been urgently mobilized, standing at the forefront of anti-colonialism, fighting to defend their sacred territory and sovereignty, fighting to safeguard their glorious national independence and freedom, and also fighting to defend world peace. We, the people of Beijing and the people of all of China, will never stand idly by. All members of the China Association for Promoting Democracy fully support our government's solemn statement of November 1. With unparalleled anger, we strongly protest and condemn the towering crimes of the British and French imperialists in invading Egypt and destroying peace. We firmly stand with all Chinese compatriots and all peace-loving people around the world to fully support the heroic and just struggle of our Egyptian brothers and sisters.

### 中国民主促进会主席  马叙伦的讲话

1956-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会主席
    马叙伦的讲话
    同志们！同胞们！
    英法帝国主义者完全撕毁了联合国宪章，背弃了一切国际公法，唆使以色列进攻埃及，并且以此为借口向埃及发动大规模的武装进攻，这不仅是对埃及的领土主权的严重侵犯，也是对世界和平的严重破坏。这是埃及人民所决不能容忍的，也是所有爱好和平的人民所决不能容忍的。现在埃及全国的兄弟姊妹们已经紧急的动员起来，站在反殖民主义的最前线，为保卫自己神圣的领土和主权而战，为维护自己光荣的民族独立和自由而战，也是为保卫世界和平而战，我们北京市人民和全中国人民决不置身事外。我们中国民主促进会全体会员完全拥护我国政府11月1日的严正声明，我们以无比愤怒的心情强烈抗议和谴责英法帝国主义者侵略埃及和破坏和平的滔天罪行，我们坚决和全中国同胞一起，和全世界一切爱好和平的人民一起，全力支持埃及的兄弟姊妹们英勇的正义的斗争。
