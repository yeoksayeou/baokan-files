Title: Dutch Foreign Minister Seeks Skin from a Flint, Begging the United States to Alleviate Western Europe's Oil Shortage
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 24th: Dutch Foreign Minister Joseph Luns met with U.S. Acting Secretary of State Herbert Hoover Jr. on the 23rd, requesting that the United States transfer oil from the Western Hemisphere to Western Europe to alleviate the oil shortage there.

Luns said he does not believe the United States must require Britain and France to withdraw their troops from Egypt before providing oil aid.

### 荷兰外交大臣与虎谋皮  乞求美国解救西欧油荒

1956-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    荷兰外交大臣与虎谋皮  乞求美国解救西欧油荒
    新华社24日讯　华盛顿消息：荷兰外交大臣约瑟夫·伦斯23日会见了美国代理国务卿小胡佛，要求美国从西半球转运石油到西欧去解救那里的石油荒。
    伦斯说，他不相信美国一定要英法先从埃及撤兵，然后才给以石油援助。
