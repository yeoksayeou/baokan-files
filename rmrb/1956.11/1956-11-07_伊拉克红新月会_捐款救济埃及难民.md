Title: Iraqi Red Crescent Society Donates Funds to Aid Egyptian Refugees
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 6

    Iraqi Red Crescent Society
    Donates Funds to Aid Egyptian Refugees
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 6th: It was announced here on November 5th that the Iraqi Red Crescent Society has donated £10,000 to help the victims in Egypt who are suffering from the Anglo-French aggression.

### 伊拉克红新月会  捐款救济埃及难民

1956-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克红新月会
    捐款救济埃及难民
    新华社6日讯　巴格达消息：据这里11月5日宣布，伊拉克红新月会已经捐助一万镑，帮助遭受英法侵略的埃及的受难者。
