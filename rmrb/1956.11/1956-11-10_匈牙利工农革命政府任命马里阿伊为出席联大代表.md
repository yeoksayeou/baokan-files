Title: Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government Appoints Marjai as Delegate to the UN General Assembly
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, New York dispatch: The Premier of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, János Kádár, and Foreign Minister Imre Horváth notified United Nations General Assembly Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld on November 8th that the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government had appointed József Marjai as the Hungarian delegate to the emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly and the eleventh session of the United Nations General Assembly, which is scheduled to open on November 12th.
    Before Marjai arrives, János Szabó will represent Hungary at the United Nations General Assembly.

### 匈牙利工农革命政府任命马里阿伊为出席联大代表

1956-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利工农革命政府任命马里阿伊为出席联大代表
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔和外交部长伊姆雷·霍瓦特11月8日通知联合国大会秘书长哈马舍尔德说，匈牙利工农革命政府已经任命约瑟夫·马里阿伊为出席联合国大会紧急会议和订于11月12日开幕的第十一届联合国大会的匈牙利代表。
    在马里阿伊到达之前，由雅诺希·萨波代表匈牙利出席联合国大会。
