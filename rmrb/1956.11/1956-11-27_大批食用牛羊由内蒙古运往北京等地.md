Title: Large Quantities of Edible Cattle and Sheep Transported from Inner Mongolia to Beijing and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, November 26th. From November 1st to 24th, over 126,000 head of edible cattle and sheep were transported from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region to Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Liaoning, Hebei, and Shanxi. Currently, there are still over 364,000 head of cattle and sheep being gathered from various pastoral areas to cities like Hohhot, Hailar, and Jining. After being processed into frozen meat in these locations, they will continue to be transported to the aforementioned regions, including Beijing.

From January to October this year, the Autonomous Region has transported over 161,000 head of edible cattle and over 370,000 sheep to support major cities and industrial and mining areas. Both figures represent more than double the volume transported during the same period last year, and the supply scope has also expanded compared to last year.

### 大批食用牛羊由内蒙古运往北京等地

1956-11-27
第3版()
专栏：

    大批食用牛羊由内蒙古运往北京等地
    新华社呼和浩特26日电　从11月1日到24日，内蒙古自治区有十二万六千多头食用牛羊运往北京、天津、上海、辽宁、河北和山西等地。现在，还有三十六万四千多头牛羊，正从各牧区集中到呼和浩特、海拉尔、集宁等城市，在这些地方加工成冻肉以后，将继续运往上述北京等地区。
    今年1月到10月，自治区外运支援各大城市和工矿区的食用牛已达十六万一千多头，羊三十七万多只，都比去年同时期外运量增加一倍多，供应范围也比去年扩大了。
