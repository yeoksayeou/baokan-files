Title: Egyptian Chief Liaison Officer Discusses UN Emergency Force Mission
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 17th - Egyptian Chief Liaison Officer Brigadier General Hilmi, responsible for welcoming the UN Emergency Force, told reporters, "The UN Emergency Force will not remain in the Canal Zone after the withdrawal of the British and French forces. The first task of the UN Emergency Force is to ensure the prompt withdrawal of the British and French forces from the area they occupy, from Port Said to Cap. After this, the UN Emergency Force will proceed to the Egyptian-Israeli border."
    The Egyptian officer said, "What I mean is that they will be deployed along the line from Gaza to Rafah to Auja, and ensure the complete withdrawal of Israeli forces from the Sinai Peninsula."
    The line from Gaza to Rafah refers to the Gaza Strip.
    Hilmi, who is also the Chief of Staff of the Egyptian Army's Eastern Command, confirmed that Egypt has given priority to clearing operations in the Canal Zone.
    The second contingent of the UN Emergency Force arrived at Abu Suweir, ten miles west of Ismailia, Egypt, on the 16th. This contingent included fifty-five Colombian officers and soldiers. They arrived on Swiss aircraft from Capodichino Airport near Naples, Italy.
    The first contingent of the UN Emergency Force, composed of Norwegian and Danish troops, arrived at Abu Suweir on the 15th.
    Lieutenant Colonel Mu (Norway), Deputy Commander of the UN Emergency Force, said: "The UN Emergency Force will begin work within a week."

### 埃及首席联络官谈联合国紧急部队任务  首先要保证英法军队尽快撤出塞得港  然后进驻埃以边境并使以军撤离西奈半岛

1956-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及首席联络官谈联合国紧急部队任务
    首先要保证英法军队尽快撤出塞得港
    然后进驻埃以边境并使以军撤离西奈半岛
    新华社17日讯　开罗消息：负责迎接联合国紧急部队的埃及首席联络官希尔米准将对记者们说，“联合国紧急部队在英法军队撤出以后将不继续留在运河区，联合国紧急部队的第一个任务是要保证英法军队尽快撤出他们所占领的塞得港到卡普的这一地带。在这以后，联合国紧急部队将前往埃及—以色列边境。”
    这位埃及军官说，“我的意思是说，他们将部署在从加沙到拉法到奥贾的这一条线上，并且保证使以色列部队完全撤出西奈半岛。”
    所谓从加沙到拉法这一条线就是指的加沙地带。
    希尔米也是埃及军队东部司令部的参谋长，他证实埃及已经对运河地区的清除工作给予优先的考虑。
    联合国紧急部队第二批人员在16日到达埃及伊士美利亚以西十英里的阿布苏威尔。这批人员包括五十五名哥伦比亚官兵。他们是从意大利那不勒斯附近的卡波迪基诺机场乘瑞士飞机到达的。
    由挪威和丹麦部队组成的联合国紧急部队第一批人员，已经在15日到达阿布苏威尔。
    联合国紧急部队副司令穆上校（挪威）说：“联合国紧急部队将在一星期内开始工作”。
