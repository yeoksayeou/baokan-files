Title: Nasser Declares State of War Throughout the Country
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: Cairo dispatch: Egyptian President Nasser announced on November 1st that the entire country is in a state of war. Nasser is now the military commander of Egypt.

### 纳赛尔宣布全国处于战争状态

1956-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔宣布全国处于战争状态
    新华社2日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统纳赛尔在11月1日宣布全国处于战争状态。现在纳赛尔是埃及的军事统帅。
