Title: Algerian Insurgents Continuously Attack French Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 24th -- French newspapers report that Algerian insurgents and French troops continue to engage in combat.

　　On the 22nd, near Palestro, a unit of French troops was ambushed, resulting in twelve French soldiers killed and seven wounded.

　　On the 21st, in the provinces of Aumale and Constantine, Algerian insurgents and French troops clashed repeatedly. Reports indicate that French troops stationed in Aumale province have begun scheduled "sweeping operations."

　　Newspapers reported that forty-nine Algerians were killed in the military operations of the past two days.

### 阿尔及利亚起义者不断打击法国军队

1956-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚起义者不断打击法国军队
    新华社24日讯　巴黎消息：据法国报纸报道，阿尔及利亚起义者和法国军队继续进行战斗。
    22日，在巴勒斯特罗附近，法军一支部队遭到伏击，有十二名法军士兵被打死，七名受伤。
    21日，在阿兰省和君士坦丁省，阿尔及利亚起义者和法国军队不断发生冲突。有消息说：驻阿兰省的法军已经开始按期进行“扫荡战”。
    报纸报道，在近两天的军事行动中，有四十九个阿尔及利亚人被打死。
