Title: Arab and Islamic Countries Unite in Solidarity, Strongly Supporting Egyptian Brothers in Resisting Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, November 8th: According to TASS, the Syrian Parliament has passed a government proposal to increase defense spending. During the parliamentary session, members strongly condemned the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt. Parliamentarian Khaled al-Azem stated that the entire Syrian people, government, and parliament firmly stand with Egypt. Parliamentarian Ahmed Ismail called for mobilizing all forces to assist Egypt.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, Damascus: The Egyptian Ambassador to Syria stated in Damascus on November 8th that he had received thousands of applications from Syrians expressing their willingness to support the Egyptian people's struggle.

    Saudi Arabia Refuses to Supply Oil to Britain and France

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, Jeddah: According to a broadcast from Mecca Radio on the 8th, ARAMCO had already stopped exporting oil to Britain and France on the 7th. Mecca Radio stated that the suspension of oil shipments was in accordance with Saudi Arabia's severance of diplomatic relations with Britain and France and the ban on exporting Saudi Arabian oil products to these two countries.

    Saudi Arabia has cut off oil supplies to the British refinery in Bahrain. The British refinery in Bahrain, which previously obtained crude oil from the Saudi Arabian pipeline, had a refining capacity of 200,000 barrels per day, while local crude oil production was only 30,000 barrels.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, Jeddah: King Saud of Saudi Arabia has donated 2 million riyals (600,000 US dollars) to the Egyptian Red Crescent to help victims of the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt.

    Jordanian Citizens Demand the Abolition of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: TASS reported from Reuters, Amman, that thousands of Jordanian citizens on November 8th resolutely demanded that the Jordanian government abolish the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty to protest British aggression against Egypt. Jordanians also demanded that the government establish diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

    The report said that thousands of telegrams were sent to the Soviet government, expressing gratitude for the Soviet government's support of Egypt and the Arab people against imperialism.

    50,000 Indonesians Sign Up as Volunteers

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 9th: According to the Indonesian News Agency on the 9th, Egyptian Ambassador to Indonesia, Dr. Ali Fahmi Anrosi, announced that 50,000 Indonesians had signed up to volunteer to help Egypt in its resistance.

    In response to reporters' questions, the Egyptian Ambassador said that his government had instructed him to consult with the Indonesian government on the issue of Indonesian volunteers going to Egypt. He said that his government had also requested medical assistance from the Indonesian government.

    In Java and Sumatra, local workers have taken boycott actions against British and French enterprises and plantations.

    Pakistani People Respond to Egypt's Call

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 9th: The Pakistani people, responding to the Egyptian government's call for assistance, have donated money and goods and signed up to join the ranks of volunteers assisting Egypt.

    An Egyptian Embassy spokesperson said on the 8th that the Egyptian Embassy had begun registering Pakistani volunteers willing to serve Egypt.

    The Chief Minister of West Pakistan, Sahib Khan, announced on November 7th the establishment of an Egypt War Victims Relief Fund. The West Pakistan government has also decided to send a medical team to Egypt. The Red Crescent Society held a meeting in Lahore on the 7th and decided to provide Egypt with trained volunteers in military and civil defense aspects. The first group of volunteers to go to Egypt will consist of fifteen people.

    The East Pakistan government is also planning to send a medical team to Egypt.

    The Islamic Scholars Association announced in Karachi on the 7th that they had made arrangements to dispatch a fully equipped medical team to assist their Egyptian brothers.

    In Quetta, the Islamic Alliance branch established two workstations on the 7th to recruit volunteers and collect material aid for Egypt.

    Afghanistan to Send Volunteer Army

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: Kabul Radio broadcast on the evening of November 7th that Afghanistan would send a volunteer army of 1,000 people to fight alongside Egypt.

    According to a report from a "Al-Ahram" reporter in New York on the 8th, the United States is putting pressure on the Afghan government to prevent Afghanistan from sending a volunteer army to Egypt. The "Al-Ahram" reporter also said that 200,000 Afghans have expressed their willingness to go to Egypt to resist British, French, and Israeli aggression.

### 阿拉伯和伊斯兰教国家敌忾同仇  大力支援埃及兄弟反抗侵略者

1956-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯和伊斯兰教国家敌忾同仇
    大力支援埃及兄弟反抗侵略者
    叙利亚议会通过增加国防费用
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社大马士革8日讯：叙利亚议会通过了一项增拨国防费用的政府提案。在议会会议上，议员严厉谴责英、法、以色列对埃及的侵略。议员哈勒德·阿泽姆说，全叙利亚人民和政府和议会坚决站在埃及方面。议员艾哈迈德·伊斯梅尔呼吁动员一切力量援助埃及。
    新华社9日讯　大马士革消息：埃及驻叙利亚大使11月8日在大马士革说，他已经收到了数千封叙利亚人寄来的申请书，他们表示志愿支援埃及人民的斗争。
    沙特阿拉伯不向英法供给油料
    据新华社9日讯　吉达消息：据麦加电台8日广播说，阿美石油公司已经在7日停止输出石油给英国和法国。麦加电台说，停运石油是根据沙特阿拉伯同英法断绝外交关系和禁止输出沙特阿拉伯的石油制品给这两个国家的命令而进行。
    沙特阿拉伯已断绝了对巴林群岛英国炼油厂的石油供应。过去从沙特阿拉伯输油管取得原油的巴林群岛英国炼油厂每天能炼油二十万桶，而本地只出产原油三万桶。
    新华社9日讯　吉达消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特已经捐赠二百万里亚尔（六十万美元）给埃及红新月会，以帮助在英国、法国和以色列对埃及侵略下的受难者。
    约旦公民要求废除英约条约
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社讯：据路透社安曼消息，数千约旦公民11月8日坚决要求约旦政府废除英国—约旦条约，表示抗议英国侵略埃及。约旦人还要求政府同苏联和中华人民共和国建立外交关系。
    消息说，有成千封电报拍给苏联政府，表示感谢苏联政府支持埃及和阿拉伯人民反对帝国主义的斗争。
    五万印度尼西亚人报名当志愿兵
    据新华社雅加达9日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社9日报道，埃及驻印度尼西亚大使阿里·法哈米·安罗西博士宣布，已经有五万名印度尼西亚人报名要当志愿兵去帮助埃及抗战。
    埃及大使在回答记者问题时说，他的政府已经训令他就印度尼西亚志愿人员前往埃及问题同印度尼西亚政府进行接洽。他说，他的政府还请印度尼西亚政府给以医药的援助。
    在爪哇和苏门答腊，当地工人对英、法企业和种植园采取了抵制行动。
    巴基斯坦人民响应埃及的呼吁
    据新华社卡拉奇9日电　巴基斯坦人民响应埃及政府要求援助的呼吁，纷纷捐献金钱和物品，并且报名参加援助埃及的志愿人员的队伍。
    埃及大使馆发言人8日说，埃及大使馆已经开始登记愿意为埃及效力的巴基斯坦志愿人员。
    西巴基斯坦首席部长萨希布汗11月7日宣布设立埃及战争受害者救济基金。西巴基斯坦政府并且已经决定派遣医疗队到埃及去。红新月会7日在拉合尔举行会议，决定向埃及提供军事方面和民防方面的受过训练的志愿人员。第一批前往埃及的志愿人员将由十五人组成。
    东巴基斯坦政府也正在计划派医疗队到埃及去。
    伊斯兰教贤哲会7日在卡拉奇宣布，他们已经作好安排，即将派遣一支配备齐全的医疗队去援助埃及兄弟们。
    在基达，伊斯兰教联盟支部7日设立了两个工作站，招收志愿人员并且收集捐给埃及的物质援助。
    阿富汗将派遣志愿军
    据新华社9日讯　喀布尔电台11月7日晚广播说，阿富汗将派遣一支由一千个人组成的志愿军去和埃及一起作战。
    据开罗“金字塔报”记者8日从纽约发出的一条消息说，美国正在对阿富汗政府施加压力，阻挠阿富汗派遣志愿军前往埃及。“金字塔报”记者还说，阿富汗已经有二十万人表示志愿到埃及反抗英、法和以色列的侵略。
