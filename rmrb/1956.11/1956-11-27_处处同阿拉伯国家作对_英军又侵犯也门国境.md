Title: Acting Against Arab Countries Everywhere: British Troops Invade Yemen's Border Again
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 6

    Acting Against Arab Countries Everywhere
    British Troops Invade Yemen's Border Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 26th: TASS reported from Cairo on the 25th that "The Republic" newspaper reported that the Yemeni legation in Cairo had received news of another British military invasion of the Yemeni border. British troops stationed in the Aden Protectorate invaded the Al-Obeidi region in southeastern Yemen, attempting to bring the residents of this region under the control of the British administration in Aden.

### 处处同阿拉伯国家作对  英军又侵犯也门国境

1956-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    处处同阿拉伯国家作对
    英军又侵犯也门国境
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社开罗25日讯：“共和国报”报道，也门驻开罗公使馆收到了有关英国军队又一次侵犯也门国境的消息。驻扎在亚丁保护地的英国军队侵入了也门东南部分的厄洛贝特地区，企图使这个地区的居民处于亚丁的英国行政当局的管制之下。
