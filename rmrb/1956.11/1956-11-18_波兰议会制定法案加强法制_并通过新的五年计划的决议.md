Title: Polish Parliament Enacts Laws to Strengthen the Legal System and Adopts Resolutions on a New Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 17th: After passing a series of bills at its tenth session on the 15th, the Polish Parliament announced a temporary recess of the plenary session until it reconvenes on November 19th.
    On this day, the parliament passed a bill to strengthen the national legal system, stipulating that the state should be directly responsible for illegal actions by various state institutions.
    The parliament also passed bills organizing the Planning Commission under the Council of Ministers and abolishing the original State Economic Planning Commission. The bill pointed out that the abolition of the State Economic Planning Commission signifies the end of the era of economic centralization in Poland. The Planning Commission, under the close supervision of the government, will not have administrative powers.
    The parliament also passed some bills and motions changing and establishing state organs.
    On the 13th, the Polish Parliament passed a resolution on the Five-Year Plan (1956-1960) for the development of the national economy, and also approved the amendments passed by the Finance and Budget Committee.

### 波兰议会制定法案加强法制  并通过新的五年计划的决议

1956-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰议会制定法案加强法制
    并通过新的五年计划的决议
    新华社华沙17日电　波兰议会第十届会议在15日的会议上通过了一系列的法案以后，宣布全体会议暂时休会到11月19日再开。
    议会在这一天通过了一项加强国家法制的法案，这项法案规定国家应该对各种国家机构的违法行动直接负责。
    议会还通过了组织隶属于部长会议的计划委员会和取消原来的国家经济计划委员会的法案。法案指出，取消国家经济计划委员会意味着波兰经济集中化时代的结束。计划委员会将在政府的密切监督之下，不具有管理的权力。
    议会还通过了一些改变和建立国家机关的法案和动议。
    在13日，波兰议会通过了关于发展国民经济的五年计划（1956—1960年）的决议，同时还批准了财政和预算委员会通过的修正案。
