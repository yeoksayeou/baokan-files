Title: Yugoslav Government Expresses Sympathy for Egypt, Hopes All Countries Will Jointly Stop Anglo-French Aggression
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Yugoslav government issued a statement on the 1st, according to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, stating that the Yugoslav people and the Yugoslav government are deeply disturbed and indignant at the Anglo-French invasion of Egypt and the barbaric bombing of Cairo.

The statement said: The Yugoslav government, on behalf of the Yugoslav people, expresses its sympathy for the Egyptian people and their government.

The statement concluded: The Yugoslav government hopes that the people of all countries in the world and their governments will unanimously condemn the act of aggression and make joint efforts to find ways to stop the aggression. To achieve this goal, the Yugoslav government has instructed its permanent representative to the United Nations to request that a special session of the United Nations General Assembly be convened as soon as possible.

### 南斯拉夫政府表示同情埃及希望各国共同制止英法侵略

1956-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫政府表示同情埃及希望各国共同制止英法侵略
    新华社2日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫政府1日发表声明说，南斯拉夫全国人民和南斯拉夫政府，对于英法开始侵略埃及和野蛮地轰炸开罗深感不安和愤慨。
    声明说：南斯拉夫政府代表南斯拉夫人民表示同情埃及人民和他们的政府。
    声明最后说：南斯拉夫政府希望世界各国人民和他们的政府一致谴责侵略行动并且进行共同努力来设法制止侵略。为了达到这个目的，南斯拉夫政府已经指示它驻联合国常任代表要求尽速召开一次联合国大会特别会议。
