Title: Indonesian Government Representative Sudarsono Arrives in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 29th - Sudarsono, the representative of the government of the Republic of Indonesia and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, arrived in Budapest on November 27th. Czátorday, Director of the Protocol Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, welcomed him.

Sudarsono has already paid a courtesy call on Sárka, Deputy Minister of the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 印度尼西亚政府代表  苏达索诺到布达佩斯

1956-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府代表
    苏达索诺到布达佩斯
    新华社布达佩斯29日电　印度尼西亚共和国政府代表、特命全权大使苏达索诺在11月27日到达布达佩斯。匈牙利外交部交际司司长察托尔道伊前往欢迎。
    苏达索诺已经拜会了匈牙利外交部副部长萨尔卡。
