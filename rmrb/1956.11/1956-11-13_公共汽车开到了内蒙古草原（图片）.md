Title: The Bus Reaches the Inner Mongolian Grasslands (Picture)
Author: Sun Gengshen
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 6

    The Bus Reaches the Inner Mongolian Grasslands
    In order to improve the transportation conditions for herdsmen on the Inner Mongolian grasslands, the state has added several buses this year to the highways from Hailar to Sanhe, and from Hailar to Dongxinba Banner. As you can see from this picture, the herdsmen are very fond of these comfortable and beautiful new vehicles.
        Photo by Sun Gengshen (Contribution from Hulunbuir Newspaper)

### 公共汽车开到了内蒙古草原（图片）
孙更申
1956-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    公共汽车开到了内蒙古草原
    为了改善内蒙古草原上牧民们的交通条件，今年国家在海拉尔到三河、海拉尔到东新巴旗两条公路上，新添了几辆公共汽车。从这张图片上可以看到，牧民们是多么喜爱这舒适美丽的新车。　　　　　		孙更申摄（呼伦贝尔报社稿）
