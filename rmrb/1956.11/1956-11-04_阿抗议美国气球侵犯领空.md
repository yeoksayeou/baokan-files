Title: Albania Protests US Balloons Violating Airspace
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd - According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, on October 26th, balloons released by the US authorities from West Germany were discovered over several towns and residential areas in Albania. These balloons pose a significant threat to local air traffic and the lives and property of residents.
    The Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the government, has sent a letter of protest to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, requesting that necessary measures be taken to prevent the US government from repeating such hostile acts in the future.

### 阿抗议美国气球侵犯领空

1956-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    阿抗议美国气球侵犯领空
    据新华社2日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：10月26日，在阿尔巴尼亚好些城镇和居民区的上空发现有美国当局从西德境内放出的气球。这些气球对当地的空中交通和居民生命财产是重大威胁。
    阿尔巴尼亚外交部奉政府的命令，已经向联合国秘书长发出抗议信，要求采取必要的措施，使美国政府今后不再重复这种敌对的行为。
