Title: Shanghai, Tianjin and Other Places Hold Meetings to Celebrate the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 6th: Over a thousand representatives from all walks of life in Shanghai held a grand celebration meeting tonight to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

    Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Osipov, and consulate staff, Soviet experts in Shanghai, and consular personnel and experts from other brother countries were invited to attend the celebration meeting.

    The celebration meeting was presided over by Wei Wenbo, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.

    Shi Ximin, Vice President of the Shanghai Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a report at the meeting. He said that the people of Shanghai, like the people of the whole country, are celebrating this great festival with infinite enthusiasm. The bright road opened up by the October Socialist Revolution is the road that human history and social development must inevitably take. The Soviet Union is a beacon on the road to communism for all mankind.

    Shi Ximin emphasized that it is extremely important to strengthen the unity and cooperation between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

    Amidst warm applause, Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Osipov, also spoke at the meeting. He thanked the Shanghai Party, government organs, and the people for their warmth and fraternal friendship. He said that the Chinese people's unwavering march on the road of socialist construction is a testament to the power and invincibility of the ideas of the October Socialist Revolution.

    After the celebration meeting, guests and hosts watched the Soviet film "Mother" together.

    Xinhua News Agency: Tianjin, November 6th: People from all walks of life in Tianjin gathered at the People's Auditorium tonight to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. More than 1,500 people, including leading members of the Tianjin Party and government organs, workers, cadres, students, writers, scientists, and representatives of ethnic minorities, attended the meeting.

    Yan Jibalov, Director of the Tianjin Office of the Soviet Trade Representative Office in China, and all staff, Soviet experts in Tianjin, and more than 80 staff members of various people's democratic countries in Tianjin, also attended tonight's grand event.

    More than 1,400 representatives from all walks of life in Lushun-Dalian City held a meeting tonight to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

    Soviet Consul General in Dalian, Belyanov, and many Soviet experts in Lushun-Dalian City were invited to attend the celebration meeting.

    People from all walks of life in Wuhan City also held a meeting on the 6th to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. Those who attended the meeting included leading members of the Hubei Province and Wuhan Party and government organs, and representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 1,000 people. Soviet experts in Wuhan City were also invited to attend the meeting.

    Urumqi City held a joint celebration party on the evening of the 5th to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The party was jointly organized by the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Autonomous Region's Department of Culture.

    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, November 6th: Chongqing City is widely carrying out publicity activities to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

    In the past few days, a new batch of pictures introducing the October Revolution Day, the Soviet Union's Sixth Five-Year Plan, and the achievements of the Soviet Union's economic and cultural construction have been placed in the picture display windows at Chongqing's stations, docks, and public places, attracting thousands of viewers every day.

    The suburbs of Chongqing City held an exhibition of pictures and models of Soviet collective farms. More than 30,000 members of agricultural production cooperatives visited the exhibition.

### 上海天津等地举行大会庆祝十月革命节

1956-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    上海天津等地举行大会庆祝十月革命节
    据新华社上海6日电　上海市各界代表一千多人，今晚举行盛大庆祝会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年。
    苏联驻上海总领事奥西波夫和领馆人员、在沪的苏联专家和其他兄弟国家的领事人员及专家，都应邀参加了庆祝大会。
    庆祝会由中共上海市委书记处书记魏文伯主持。
    上海市中苏友好协会副会长石西民在大会上作了报告。他说，上海人民和全国人民一样，抱着无限热烈的心情来庆祝这个伟大的节日。十月社会主义革命所开辟的光明道路，是人类历史和社会发展必然要走的道路。苏联就是全人类走向共产主义道路上的一座灯塔。
    石西民强调指出加强中苏两国人民团结合作是极为重要的。
    在热烈的掌声中，苏联驻上海总领事奥西波夫也在大会上讲了话。他感谢上海党、政机关和人民对他们的热情和兄弟般的友好。他说，中国人民坚定不移地走上了社会主义的建设道路，是十月社会主义革命思想的力量及其不可战胜的证明。
    庆祝会结束后，宾主一起观看了苏联电影“母亲”。
    据新华社电　天津市各界人民今晚在人民礼堂集会庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年。天津市党和政府领导机关的负责人、工人、干部、学生、作家和科学家及少数民族的代表共一千五百多人出席了大会。
    苏联驻华商务代表处驻天津办事处处长燕吉巴罗夫和全体工作人员、在天津的苏联专家以及各人民民主国家在天津的工作人员等八十多人，也参加了今晚的盛会。
    旅大市各界代表一千四百多人今天晚上举行大会，庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年。
    苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫和旅大市的苏联专家多人应邀参加了庆祝大会。
    武汉市各界人民也在6日举行大会庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年。参加大会的有湖北省和武汉党和政府领导机关的负责人及各界代表一千多人。在武汉市的苏联专家也应邀参加大会。
    乌鲁木齐市在5日晚上举行庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年联欢晚会。这个晚会是中苏友好协会新疆维吾尔自治区分会和自治区文化厅联合举办的。
    据新华社重庆6日电　重庆市广泛展开庆祝十月社会主义革命三十九周年的宣传活动。
    这几天，在重庆市车站、码头、公共场所的图片宣传橱窗里，新换上了一批介绍十月革命节、苏联第六个五年计划以及苏联经济、文化建设成就等的图片，这些图片每天都吸引着成千的观众。
    重庆市郊区举办了苏联集体农庄的图片和模型展览。有三万多名农业生产合作社社员参观了这个展览。
