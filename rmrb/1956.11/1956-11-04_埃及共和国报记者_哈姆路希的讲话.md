Title: Speech by Hamroush, Reporter for the Egyptian Republic Newspaper
Author: Hamuluxi
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 2

Comrades:

	I extend to you the respects of a free man to his free brothers.

	I extend to you sincere respect, the respect of a brother from a free country, I extend respects to the free country - our Chinese brothers.

	I extend to you respects, the respects of a brother in a country that is fighting to brothers who have undergone a long struggle.

	I extend to you respects, and respects for your support of your brothers, your brothers are struggling against the forces of evil, against the forces of imperialism, on the land of peace-loving country - Egypt.

	I thank you for this feeling, thank you for your full support. This feeling and support is not a strange thing to our Chinese brothers, because China has undergone a long struggle for its own freedom, sovereignty and unity.

	Today the Egyptian people are fighting against the forces of evil, against imperialism, and against fascism represented by two imperialist countries. I don't want to say two countries, but the two governments of Britain and France.

	Today your Egyptian brothers are fighting against British and French fascism.

	Today your Egyptian brothers are fighting against the forces of evil, against the forces of imperialism.

	Today your Egyptian brothers are fighting to safeguard world peace.

	Today your free Egyptian brothers, under the leadership of the free people's leader Nasser, are fighting for freedom and to defend their homeland.

	Today your Egyptian brothers are fighting, resisting the fascist imperialist war launched by Eden and Mollet.

	Today, as your brothers fight, they are convinced that they are not isolated in the struggle. Yes, they believe that all the free and peace-loving people of the world are supporting them, enabling them to stand strong and powerful in the face of the ferocious imperialism.

	Imperialism used the Israel it created to invade the borders of Egypt. British and French imperialism, under the pretext of protecting the Suez Canal, invaded Egyptian sovereignty. British and French planes bombed an Egyptian ship parked in the Suez Canal, and the navigation of the canal was completely destroyed.

	This is the so-called protection of freedom of navigation in the canal in the logic of imperialism.

	Now British and French planes are bombing peaceful Egyptian cities, airports, factories, and hospitals.

	But I can definitely tell everyone: Britain and France will never be able to destroy the spirit of the people under any circumstances, will never be able to destroy the people's confidence, will never be able to destroy the people's determination to fight to defend their homeland.

	The spirit of the people will never be destroyed, and victory will belong to the people.

	Because of our determination to fight, because of our unity against the forces of evil, against the forces of imperialism, victory must belong to us.

	With your support, with the support of the peace-loving people of all countries, victory must belong to us.

	Victory belongs to us because we are defending the just cause we believe in and are fighting to achieve the goals of this cause.

	Comrades, I salute you, thank you, thank you for your support, thank you for the support of your wise government, and through you I salute the peace lovers and peace advocates everywhere.

	Long live the friendship between the people of China and Egypt!

	Long live the great unity of the anti-imperialist struggles of Asian and African countries!

	Long live the people who love peace and defend peace!

	Long live the people of all countries in their struggle for peace!

### 埃及共和国报记者  哈姆路希的讲话

1956-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    埃及共和国报记者
    哈姆路希的讲话
    同志们：
    我向你们致以自由者对自由弟兄们的敬意。
    我向你们致以诚挚的敬意，这是一个自由国家的弟兄，我向自由国家——中国弟兄们致敬意。
    我向你们致敬，这是正在进行斗争的国度里的一个弟兄向经过长期斗争的弟兄们的敬意。
    我向你们致敬，并且向你们对自己弟兄们的支持致敬，你们的弟兄们正在爱好和平的国家——埃及的土地上同恶势力、同帝国主义势力做斗争。
    我感谢你们的这种情感，感谢你们的完全支持。这种情感和支持对我们的中国弟兄来说并不是什么奇怪的事情，因为中国为了自己的自由、主权和统一曾经进行了长期的斗争。
    今天埃及人民正在向恶势力、向帝国主义作斗争，正在向由两个帝国主义国家代表着的法西斯主义作斗争。我不想说是两个国家，而是英、法两个政府。
    今天你们的埃及弟兄正在进行着反对英法法西斯的斗争。
    今天你们的埃及弟兄正在进行着反对恶势力、反对帝国主义势力的斗争。
    今天你们的埃及弟兄正在为了维护世界和平进行着斗争。
    今天你们的自由的埃及弟兄们正在自由的人民领袖纳赛尔的领导下，进行着争取自由、保卫国土的斗争。
    今天你们的埃及弟兄正在进行斗争，抵抗艾登和摩勒所发动的法西斯帝国主义战争。
    今天，当你们的弟兄们进行斗争的时候，他们深信他们在斗争中并不是孤立的。是的，他们相信全世界爱好自由和平的人民都在支持他们，使他们坚强地有力地站在凶恶的帝国主义面前。
    帝国主义利用他一手制造的以色列侵犯埃及的国境，英法帝国主义以保护苏伊士运河为借口侵犯埃及的主权，英法飞机炸毁了停在苏伊士运河的一艘埃及船只，运河的航行完全被破坏了。
    这就是帝国主义逻辑里的所谓保护运河航行自由。
    现在英法飞机正在轰炸埃及的和平城市、飞机场、工厂和医院。
    但是我肯定的告诉大家：英法两国在任何情况下绝对毁灭不了人民的精神，绝对毁灭不了人民的信心，绝对毁灭不了人民为保卫国土而战斗的决心。
    人民的精神是绝对不会毁灭的，胜利将是属于人民的。
    由于我们斗争的决心，由于我们团结一致反对恶势力、反对帝国主义势力，所以胜利一定是属于我们的。
    有了你们的支持，有了爱好和平的各国人民的支持，胜利一定是属于我们的。
    胜利是属于我们的，因为我们是在保卫我们相信的正义事业，是在为了实现这个事业的目标而斗争。
    同志们，我向你们致敬，感谢你们，感谢你们的支持，感谢你们的明智的政府的支持，并通过你们向各个地方的和平爱好者和和平拥护者致敬。
    中埃两国人民的友谊万岁！
    亚非各国反帝斗争的大团结万岁！
    爱好和平保卫和平的人们万岁！
    各国人民争取和平的斗争万岁！
