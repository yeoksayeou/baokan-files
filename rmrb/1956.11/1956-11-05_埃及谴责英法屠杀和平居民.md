Title: Egypt Condemns Anglo-French Massacre of Peaceful Residents
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th, Washington dispatch: The Egyptian Embassy in Washington issued a public statement on November 3rd condemning the armed forces of Britain and France for massacring peaceful residents of Egypt.

    The public statement, citing a statement from Cairo, pointed out that the residences, hospitals and religious temples in Egyptian cities and villages have become targets of Anglo-French attacks. Egyptian women and children are currently suffering cruel massacres without exception, which are incompatible with the spirit of civilization.

### 埃及谴责英法屠杀和平居民

1956-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及谴责英法屠杀和平居民
    新华社4日讯　华盛顿消息：埃及驻华盛顿大使馆11月3日发表公报，谴责英国和法国的武装力量屠杀埃及的和平居民。
    公报援引来自开罗的声明指出：埃及城市和乡村的住宅、医院和宗教寺院都成了英法攻击的目标。埃及妇女和儿童目前正毫无例外地遭受着与文明精神不能相容的残酷的屠杀。
