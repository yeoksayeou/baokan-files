Title: Mei Yi Hosts Banquet for Delegation of East German Journalists

Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 8th November. Mei Yi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, held a banquet this evening to welcome the delegation of journalists from the German Democratic Republic who are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 7th. The delegation consists of five people. The head of the delegation is Georg Klaus, a member of the editorial board of "Neues Deutschland". The members include Leonhard Helmschrott, editor-in-chief of "Bauernecho" and vice-chairman of the German Journalists Association, writer Adolf Endler, Walter Heilig, editor-in-chief of the Central Picture Agency, and Günter Schabowski, deputy editor-in-chief of the "Tribüne".

Those present at the banquet tonight included heads of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and various news organizations in Beijing.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 梅益设宴招待民主德国新闻工作者代表团

1956-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    梅益设宴招待民主德国新闻工作者代表团
    新华社8日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长梅益今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请来中国访问的德意志民主共和国新闻工作者代表团。
    代表团是在7日到达北京的。代表团共五人。团长是“新德意志报”编委格奥赫·克劳斯，团员有“农民回声报”主编、德意志新闻工作者协会副主席利昂哈特·赫姆斯罗特，作家阿道尔夫·格尔次，中央图片社主编瓦耳特·海利希，论坛报副总编辑京特·夏博夫斯基。
    出席今晚宴会的，有外交部新闻司和北京市各新闻单位负责人。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
