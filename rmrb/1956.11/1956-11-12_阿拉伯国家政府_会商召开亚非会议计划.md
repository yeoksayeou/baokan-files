Title: Arab Governments Confer on Plans to Convene Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Cairo dispatch: According to a report by the Middle East News Agency on the 10th, Arab governments are meeting in Cairo to discuss plans for holding a second Asian-African conference in Cairo.

### 阿拉伯国家政府  会商召开亚非会议计划

1956-11-12
第7版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家政府
    会商召开亚非会议计划
    新华社11日讯　开罗消息：据中东通讯社10日报道，阿拉伯国家政府正在开罗会谈关于在开罗举行第二次亚非国家会议的计划。
