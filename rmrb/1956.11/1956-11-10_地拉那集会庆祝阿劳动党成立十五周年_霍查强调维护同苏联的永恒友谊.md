Title: Tirana Rally Celebrates 15th Anniversary of Albanian Labor Party; Hoxha Emphasizes Maintaining Eternal Friendship with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, Tirana dispatch: According to a TASS report from Tirana: November 8th was the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Labor Party. On this day, a grand celebration rally was held in Tirana.

    Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, delivered a report at the meeting. He explained the glorious path taken by the heroic Albanian Labor Party since its founding and the achievements made by the Albanian people.

    Hoxha emphasized that the Albanian Labor Party and the people will continue to strengthen and maintain the eternal and unbreakable friendship with the Soviet Union, and strengthen fraternal ties with socialist countries.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th, Moscow dispatch: According to a TASS report from Moscow: Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, wrote an article in "Pravda" on the 8th to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Labor Party.

    Hoxha pointed out that the Albanian people have made tremendous achievements, which are the result of the correct and experienced leadership of the Albanian Labor Party. It is under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party, and with the assistance of the Soviet Union, that the Albanian people have established a people's government and are now living freely and independently. The Albanian people harbor unwavering, deep gratitude and love for the Soviet Union. We hate and despise those who slander the outstanding construction work of socialist life in the Soviet Union.

    Hoxha wrote that the Albanian Labor Party has relied on Leninism in the construction of the people's democratic regime, both in the past and now. For our party and people, the example of the Soviet Union is the greatest example worthy of our party and people to learn from forever. Our party and people will also utilize the experience of any other country that builds socialism based on Marxist-Leninist theory. Hoxha then talked about the achievements made by the Albanian people in socialist construction.

### 地拉那集会庆祝阿劳动党成立十五周年  霍查强调维护同苏联的永恒友谊

1956-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    地拉那集会庆祝阿劳动党成立十五周年
    霍查强调维护同苏联的永恒友谊
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社地拉那讯：11月8日是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立十五周年纪念日。在这一天，地拉那举行了隆重的庆祝大会。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查在会上作了报告。他说明了英雄的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党自建党以来所走过的光荣道路和阿尔巴尼亚人民所获得的成就。
    霍查强调说，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和人民今后将继续加强和维护同苏联的永恒的、牢不可破的友谊，加强同社会主义国家的兄弟联系。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查8日在“真理报”撰文，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立十五周年。
    霍查指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民取得了巨大的成就，这些成就是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党正确的有经验的领导的结果。正是在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在苏联的援助下，阿尔巴尼亚人民才创立了人民政权，现在自由自主的生活着。阿尔巴尼亚人民对苏联怀着不变的深刻的感激和热爱的感情。我们仇视和鄙视那些诽谤苏联社会主义生活的卓越建设工作的人。
    霍查写道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党在人民民主政权建设中过去和现在都是依靠着列宁主义的。对于我们党和人民来说，苏联的榜样是永远值得我们党和人民去学习的最伟大的榜样。我们党和人民也将运用其他任何一个根据马克思列宁主义学说建设社会主义的国家的经验。霍查接着谈到阿尔巴尼亚人民在社会主义建设中所取得的成就。
