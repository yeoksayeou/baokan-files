　　International Briefs

　　Our Art Troupe Performs in Democratic Germany
　　After concluding its tour performances in various parts of West Germany, the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, on the 26th, having traveled by train from Hamburg, West Germany. Starting from the 29th, the Chinese Art Troupe will perform in Democratic Germany for three weeks.

　　Japanese Automotive Industry Delegation to Visit Our Country
　　According to an AFP dispatch from Tokyo, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers Association told AFP reporters on the 26th that Japan will send an automotive industry delegation to China early next year to investigate the market and study possible technical connections. The organization of Japanese automakers said that the delegation will consist of approximately sixteen representatives from major Japanese automobile companies.

### 国际简讯

1956-11-29
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    我艺术团到民主德国演出
    中国艺术团结束了在西德各地的访问演出后，在26日应邀从西德的汉堡乘火车来到德意志民主共和国首都柏林。从29日起，中国艺术团将在民主德国演出三个星期。
    日本汽车业代表团将访我国
    据法新社东京消息，日本汽车工业会26日告诉法新社记者说，日本将在明年初派遣一个汽车工业代表团到中国去访问，以便调查市场和研究可能的技术联系。这个日本汽车制造商的组织说，代表团将由日本大的汽车公司的约十六名代表组成。
