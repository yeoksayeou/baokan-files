Title: Aggressors' Murderous Intent Unabated, Britain Continues to Send Reinforcements to the Middle East, Israel Amasses Troops on the Syrian-Jordanian Border
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd dispatch from Damascus: Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar told reporters on the 21st that British and French troops are continuing to reinforce Cyprus and are conducting military buildups. He said that Arab countries must remain vigilant and be in a state of general mobilization.
    Bitar said: "The aggressor countries are delaying their withdrawal from Egyptian territory because they are attempting to impose (their) solutions on Egypt and other Arab countries.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 21st dispatch from TASS, London, November 20th: British newspapers are reporting that Britain continues to increase its armed forces in the Middle East. According to the "Times", on the evening of the 19th, a transport ship "Empire Clyde", loaded with infantry, engineers, artillery units and medical personnel, set sail from Liverpool. On the same evening, another transport ship "Empire Orwell", carrying engineer and administrative personnel units, set sail from Southampton. The "Daily Telegraph and Morning Post" pointed out that these ships were "transporting reinforcements to the Middle East."
    Xinhua News Agency, November 21st dispatch from TASS, Athens, November 20th: According to reports from Athens, after the ceasefire in Egypt, the Israeli army headquarters concentrated troops on the borders of its two other neighboring Arab countries—Jordan and Syria. Israel has dispatched tank units, artillery, and mechanized infantry to these areas, already mobilizing about ten brigades, as well as air force units prepared to coordinate with ground forces. Israel is also conducting additional mobilization, conscripting reservists into the army.

### 侵略者杀心未戢  英国继续不断增兵中东地区  以色列在约叙边境集结军队

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    侵略者杀心未戢
    英国继续不断增兵中东地区  以色列在约叙边境集结军队
    新华社22日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚外交部长比塔尔21日对记者说，英法军队现在正继续增援塞浦路斯，并且进行军事集结。他说，阿拉伯国家必须保持警惕，并且要处于总动员状态。
    比塔尔说：“侵略国迟迟不从埃及领土撤出军队，因为它们企图把（它们的）解决办法强加在埃及和其他阿拉伯国家身上。
    据新华社21日讯　据塔斯社伦敦20日讯：英国报纸报道英国继续在中东地区增加武装部队的情况。据“泰晤士报”消息，19日晚上有一艘装载步兵、工兵和炮兵分队以及医务人员的运输舰“帝国克莱德号”从利物浦启碇。同一天晚上另一艘载着工兵和行政人员分队的运输舰“帝国奥威尔号”从南安普顿启碇。“每日电讯与晨邮报”指出这些舰只是“运送增援部队去中东地区的”。
    据新华社21日讯　据塔斯社雅典20日讯：据在雅典获得的消息，以色列军队司令部在埃及境内停火之后把军队向它的另外两个相邻的阿拉伯国家——约旦和叙利亚的边境上集中。以色列向这些地区派遣战车部队、炮兵、机械化步兵，已经调动的约有十个旅，还有准备用来配合地面部队的空军。以色列还正在进行补充动员，征集预备役军人入伍。
