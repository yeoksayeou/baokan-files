Title: Israel Presents Absurd Conditions for Withdrawal to the UN
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 22nd - On the evening of November 21st, the Israeli delegation to the United Nations General Assembly issued a memorandum from Israel to Hammarskjöld in New York. The memorandum stated that its troops would withdraw from Egypt when the International Emergency Force had made "satisfactory arrangements to ensure Israel's security, prevent the recurrence of danger or the threat of aggression, and hostile actions on land and sea."

### 以色列向联合国  提出荒谬的撤军条件

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列向联合国
    提出荒谬的撤军条件
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：11月21日晚间以色列出席联合国大会的代表团在纽约发表了以色列给哈马舍尔德的一项备忘录。备忘录说，在国际紧急部队作出了“令人满意的安排来保证以色列的安全，防止再发生危险或进攻的威胁，以及陆上和海上的敌对行动”时，它的部队将撤出埃及。
