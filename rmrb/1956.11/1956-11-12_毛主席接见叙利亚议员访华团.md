Title: Chairman Mao Receives Syrian Parliamentary Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation headed by Ali Buzo this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman, and Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

During the meeting, the delegation presented gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 毛主席接见叙利亚议员访华团

1956-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见叙利亚议员访华团
    新华社11日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了以阿里·布佐为首的叙利亚议员访华团。
    接见时，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、副委员长彭真，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。
    接见时，访华团向毛泽东主席赠送了礼品。
