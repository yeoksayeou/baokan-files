Title: Bolivian Scholar De Kroell Dies in Plane Crash En Route to China
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: Leo Pucher de Kroell, the renowned Bolivian professor of archeology and anthropology who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, unfortunately died in a plane crash on November 24th while en route to China. Leo Pucher de Kroell was one of the passengers on the Czechoslovakian plane that unfortunately crashed en route from Zurich to Prague on the 24th. De Kroell was a professor at the Tomás Frías University in Bolivia and had been engaged in research on the origin of the American race for many years. He had planned to explore the question of the American race originating in Asia during this visit.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, jointly sent a telegram to the President of Tomás Frías University in Bolivia, expressing their deep condolences on De Kroell's unfortunate death on behalf of Chinese cultural circles and scientists.

### 玻利维亚学者德克罗耳  来我国途中坠机遇难

1956-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚学者德克罗耳
    来我国途中坠机遇难
    据新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的玻利维亚著名的考古学和人类学教授莱奥·普切尔·德克罗耳，11月24日在来中国途中因飞机失事不幸遇难。莱奥·普切尔·德克罗耳是24日从苏黎士往布拉格途中不幸失事的捷克斯洛伐克飞机上的乘客之一。德克罗耳是玻利维亚的托马斯·弗里亚斯大学的教授，多年以来一直从事研究美洲人种的起源问题。他曾经准备在这次访问中探讨关于美洲人种渊源于亚洲的问题。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若和中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南联名打电报给玻利维亚的托马斯·弗里亚斯大学校长，代表中国文化界和科学家对德克罗耳不幸遇难表示沉痛的哀悼。
