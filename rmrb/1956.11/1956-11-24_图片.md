Title: Countless Egyptian People in Port Said Displaced and Homeless
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 6

Countless Egyptian people in Port Said have been displaced and made homeless. The picture shows an Egyptian woman carrying her young children, leaving her destroyed home in grief and indignation. (Xinhua News Agency)
