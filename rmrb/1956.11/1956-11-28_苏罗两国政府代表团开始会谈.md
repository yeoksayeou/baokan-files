Title: Soviet-Romanian Government Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 27th: TASS, Moscow, November 27th: Talks between the Soviet government delegation and the government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic began this morning in the Kremlin.

    The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin (head of the delegation), First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, and Saburov.

    The Romanian side participating in the talks included: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Stoica (head of the delegation), Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Bîrlădeanu, Chairman of the State Planning Committee, Marin, Minister of Finance, Mănescu, and Minister of Foreign Trade, Popescu.

    Also present at the talks were: Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the USSR, Baybakov, Minister of Finance, Zverev, Minister of Foreign Trade, Kabanov, Soviet Ambassador to Romania, Yepishev, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Patolichev, and others.

### 苏罗两国政府代表团开始会谈

1956-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏罗两国政府代表团开始会谈
    新华社27日讯　塔斯社莫斯科27日讯：苏联政府代表团和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团之间的会谈今天上午在克里姆林宫开始举行。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁（代表团团长）、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和萨布罗夫。
    罗马尼亚方面参加会谈的有：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席斯托伊卡（代表团团长）、部长会议副主席伯尔拉德亚努、国家计划委员会主席马林、财政部长曼尼斯库、对外贸易部长波佩斯库。
    会谈时在座的有：苏联国家计划委员会主席巴伊巴科夫、财政部长兹维列夫、对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫、苏联驻罗马尼亚大使叶皮谢夫、苏联外交部副部长帕托利切夫等。
