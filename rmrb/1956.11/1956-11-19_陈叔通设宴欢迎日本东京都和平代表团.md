Title: Chen Shutong Hosts Banquet for Japanese Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Delegation headed by Hachiro Miura. Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and Japanese peace figures in Beijing, Togo Kameda, and Korean Hwang Bong-gu, were invited to accompany them.

### 陈叔通设宴欢迎日本东京都和平代表团

1956-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    陈叔通设宴欢迎日本东京都和平代表团
    新华社16日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通今晚设宴欢迎以三浦八郎为首的日本东京都和平代表团。应邀作陪的有北京市副市长吴晗和在京日本和平人士龟田东伍、朝鲜黄凤九等。
