Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th November - Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimov, was recalled and left Beijing for home on the 20th. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, went to the station to see him off. Diplomatic envoys and staff from various countries' embassies in China, as well as all personnel from the Bulgarian Embassy in China, also went to see him off.

### 保大使迪莫夫返国

1956-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    保大使迪莫夫返国
    据新华社20日讯　保加利亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使迪莫夫奉调于20日离开北京返国。前往车站送行的有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如、苏联东欧司副司长李汇川。各国驻华使节、使馆人员和保加利亚驻华大使馆全体人员也前往送行。
