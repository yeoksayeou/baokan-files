Title: U.S. Government Opposes British and French Use of U.S.-Supplied Weapons in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 5th: The U.S. government issued a warning to Britain and France on November 3rd, opposing their use of weapons supplied by the U.S. to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization in the Middle East.

The U.S. took this action shortly after British Defense Minister Head admitted in the House of Commons that U.S.-aided equipment was used in the British and French air raids against Egypt.

### 美国政府反对英法在中东用美援武器

1956-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    美国政府反对英法在中东用美援武器
    新华社5日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府11月3日向英法两国发出警告，反对他们把美国供给北大西洋公约区域的武器用于中东。
    美国采取这个行动是在英国国防大臣赫德在下院承认美国援助的装备在英法对埃及进行空袭时被使用以后不久宣布的。
