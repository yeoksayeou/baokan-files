Title: Israeli Unity Association Condemns Government Aggression Against Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, November 20th: The council of the Israeli Unity Association issued a statement condemning Israel for joining forces with those "attempting to dominate other countries and exploit the people of other nations" in the aggression against Egypt. The association includes university students and professors of various political persuasions from the University of Jerusalem.

The statement also condemned the crimes committed by the Israeli military authorities in the occupied Egyptian territories and demanded that the criminals who murdered peaceful Arab residents be punished.

### 以色列团结协会  谴责政府侵略埃及

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列团结协会
    谴责政府侵略埃及
    据新华社21日讯　据塔斯社特拉维夫20日讯：以色列的团结协会理事会发表声明，谴责以色列同那些“企图统治其他国家和剥削别国人民”的力量联合起来侵略埃及。参加这个协会的有耶路撒冷大学中抱有各种不同政治信仰的大学生和教授。
    这篇声明同时谴责以色列军事当局在被侵占的埃及领土上所犯的罪行，并且要求惩办杀害阿拉伯和平居民的罪犯。
