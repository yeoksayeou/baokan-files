Title: Yang Hansheng Hosts Japanese Literary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 12th to welcome the Japanese visiting literary delegation (formerly known as the Japanese Writers Delegation).
　　At the banquet, Yang Hansheng extended a warm welcome to the prominent figures of the Japanese literary and art circles for their visit to China. Kiyoshi Aono, President of the Japan Art Academy and literary theorist, expressed at the banquet his hope that there would be more contact between the literary and art circles of Japan and China in the future.

### 阳翰笙宴日本文学代表团

1956-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    阳翰笙宴日本文学代表团
    据新华社12日讯　中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙12日晚间设宴欢迎日本访华文学代表团（前曾称为日本作家代表团）。
    阳翰笙在宴会上，对日本文艺界著名人士这次来中国访问，表示热诚的欢迎。日本文艺家协会会长、文艺理论家青野季吉在宴会上，希望今后日、中两国文艺界之间能有更多的接触。
