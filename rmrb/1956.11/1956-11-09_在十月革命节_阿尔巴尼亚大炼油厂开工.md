Title: Albanian Major Oil Refinery Starts Operation on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 7th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, on November 7th, in Salek, Elbasan District of Albania, the oil refinery, one of the largest factories in the Second Five-Year Plan, has started operation.

This large-scale oil refinery was built with Soviet assistance. It includes thirty-nine different factories, covering an area of approximately thirty-three hectares. This factory is of great significance to Albania's economic development. From 1957, it will be able to refine all of the country's crude oil, and by 1960, the amount of crude oil refined will reach 3.76 times that of 1955.

### 在十月革命节  阿尔巴尼亚大炼油厂开工

1956-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    在十月革命节
    阿尔巴尼亚大炼油厂开工
    新华社8日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那7日讯：11月7日，在阿尔巴尼亚爱尔巴桑区的萨列克，第二个五年计划中最大工厂之一的炼油厂已经开工。
    这个规模宏大的炼油厂是在苏联援助之下建立起来的。它包括三十九个不同的工厂，占地约三十三公顷。这座工厂对阿尔巴尼亚的经济发展有很重大的意义。从1957 年起，它就能够精炼国内的全部原油，到1960年，精炼原油的数量将达到1955年的三点七六倍。
