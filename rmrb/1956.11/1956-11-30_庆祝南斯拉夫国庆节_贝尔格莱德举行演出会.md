Title: Belgrade Holds Performance to Celebrate Yugoslavia's National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, Nov. 29th: A performance was held at the National Theater in Belgrade on the evening of the 28th to celebrate Yugoslavia's National Day on November 29th, according to Yugoslav News Agency.
    President of the Federal National Assembly, Pijade, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council, Djurakovic, Secretary of State for National Defense, Gosnjak, other senior Yugoslav officials and representatives of various groups attended the performance.
    Similar celebrations were held throughout Yugoslavia on this day. Belgrade and other republic capitals also fired celebratory salutes on the evening of the 28th to mark the occasion.

### 庆祝南斯拉夫国庆节  贝尔格莱德举行演出会

1956-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝南斯拉夫国庆节
    贝尔格莱德举行演出会
    新华社29日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德讯：28日晚间，贝尔格莱德的民族剧院举行了演出会，庆祝11月29日南斯拉夫国庆节。
    联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰、联邦执行委员会副主席乔拉柯维奇、国防国务秘书长戈什尼亚克，其他南斯拉夫高级官员和各团体的代表出席了演出会。
    这一天，南斯拉夫各地也举行了类似的庆祝活动。贝尔格莱德和其他共和国首都还在28日晚间鸣放礼炮以示庆祝。
