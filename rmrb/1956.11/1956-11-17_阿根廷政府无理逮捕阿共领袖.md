Title: Argentine Government Unjustifiably Arrests Communist Leader

Author:

Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 16th, Buenos Aires Dispatch: Argentine government police suddenly raided the headquarters of the Argentine Communist Party on the early morning of the 14th and arrested fifteen leaders of the Argentine Communist Party.

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th, Buenos Aires Dispatch: The Argentine interim government promulgated another anti-communist measure on the 13th, announcing the prohibition of the Argentine Communist Party from participating as a political party in the elections to be held in 1957. This government had previously announced that Argentina would hold its first presidential election since the overthrow of the Peron regime in 1955 sometime in 1957.

### 阿根廷政府无理逮捕阿共领袖

1956-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷政府无理逮捕阿共领袖
    新华社16日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷政府警察在14日清晨突然袭击阿根廷共产党总部，并且逮捕走了十五名阿共领袖。
    据新华社15日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷临时政府13日又颁布了一项反共措施，宣布禁止阿根廷共产党作为一个政党参加在1957年举行的选举活动。这个政府过去曾经宣布，在1957年内，阿根廷将举行自1955年庇隆政权被推翻以后的第一次总统大选。
