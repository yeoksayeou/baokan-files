Title: Dalai and Panchen Lamas to Visit India to Attend the 2,500th Anniversary of Shakyamuni's Nirvana

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 1

    Invited to Attend the 2,500th Anniversary of Shakyamuni's Nirvana
    Dalai and Panchen Lamas to Visit India
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The Dalai and Panchen Lamas have been invited to attend the 2,500th anniversary of Shakyamuni's Nirvana in India.
    The commemoration of the 2,500th anniversary of Shakyamuni's Nirvana will be held in India in late November this year. The Indian Shakyamuni Nirvana 2,500th Anniversary Working Committee, with Indian Prime Minister Nehru as honorary chairman and Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan as chairman, has invited the Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso and the Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen to India to attend the commemoration.
    The Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso and the Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen have accepted the invitation and are about to depart for India.

### 应邀参加释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念  达赖和班禅即将去印度

1956-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念
    达赖和班禅即将去印度
    新华社16日讯　达赖、班禅应邀参加印度释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念。
    今年11月下旬将在印度举行释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年的纪念活动。以印度尼赫鲁总理为名誉主席和副总统拉达克里希南博士为主席的印度释迦牟尼?槃二千五百周年纪念工作委员会，邀请了达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞前往印度参加此项纪念活动。
    达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞已经接受了邀请，他们即将起程前往印度。
