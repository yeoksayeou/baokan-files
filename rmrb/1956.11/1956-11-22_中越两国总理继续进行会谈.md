Title: Chinese and Vietnamese Premiers Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 21st. Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai and Vietnamese Premier Pham Van Dong continued their talks this afternoon, discussing issues concerning both countries.
    Participating in the talks from the Vietnamese side were also Deputy Prime Ministers Phan Ke Toai and Vo Nguyen Giap, several government ministers, and the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, among others; from the Chinese side were also Deputy Premier He Long and the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Luo Guibo, among others.
    The talks proceeded in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 中越两国总理继续进行会谈

1956-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中越两国总理继续进行会谈
    新华社河内21日电　中国总理周恩来和越南总理范文同在今天下午继续进行会谈，讨论有关两国的一些问题。
    参加会谈的，越南方面还有副总理潘继遂、武元甲，几位政府部长和越南驻中国大使黄文欢等；中国方面还有副总理贺龙和中国驻越南大使罗贵波等。
    会谈在十分亲密友好的气氛中进行。
