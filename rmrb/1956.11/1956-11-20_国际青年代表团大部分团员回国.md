Title: Most Members of International Youth Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Most members of the International Youth Delegation, a total of twenty people, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their countries. A few members of the delegation will continue to visit various parts of our country.
　　Yesterday evening, the All-China Youth Federation held a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation. Before leaving Beijing, the delegation visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places, and had extensive contact with Chinese youth.

### 国际青年代表团大部分团员回国

1956-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

    国际青年代表团大部分团员回国
    新华社19日讯　国际青年代表团的大部分团员共二十人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团还有少数团员将继续到我国各地进行参观访问。
    昨天晚上，中华全国民主青年联合会举行宴会为代表团饯行。离京前，代表团曾经到沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、天津、上海、杭州、广州等地进行了参观访问，并且和中国青年进行了广泛的接触。
