Title: Further Strengthen the Party Ranks to Defend Socialist Achievements: East German Working People Enthusiastically Request to Join the Socialist Unity Party

Author:

Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 12th: According to a report in "Neues Deutschland" on the 11th, a large number of non-Party workers, farmers, intellectuals, and other laborers in East Germany are actively requesting to join the Socialist Unity Party of Germany to answer the provocations of international reactionary forces and further strengthen the Party ranks to defend socialist achievements. In October alone, 11,700 outstanding non-Party laborers were admitted as probationary members of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. Among these new Party members, approximately 8,000 are workers and staff members in the industrial sector.

This vital new force of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany demonstrated their patriotism and internationalism when applying to join the Party. For example, Matthias Wiener, a worker from Greifenheinichen, wrote in his application: "Whoever opposes the worker-peasant government of Hungary opposes the German Democratic Republic. I am determined to use all my strength to defend the German Democratic Republic. Therefore, I am applying to join the Socialist Unity Party of Germany."

### 进一步壮大党的队伍　保卫社会主义成果  民主德国劳动人民踊跃要求加入统一社会党

1956-11-13
第3版()
专栏：

    进一步壮大党的队伍　保卫社会主义成果
    民主德国劳动人民踊跃要求加入统一社会党
    新华社柏林12日电　据“新德意志报”11日报道：民主德国广大的非党工人、农民、知识分子和其它劳动者纷纷要求加入德国统一社会党，以回答国际反动势力的挑衅，进一步壮大党的队伍来保卫社会主义的成果。仅仅在10月份一个月内，就有一万一千七百名优秀的非党劳动者被吸收为德国统一社会党预备党员。在这批新入党的劳动者当中约有八千人是产业部门的工人和职员。
    德国统一社会党的这支生力军在申请入党的时候表现了他们的爱国主义和国际主义精神。例如，格莱芬海尼森的工人马蒂阿斯·维纳在自己的入党申请书上写道：“谁反对匈牙利的工农政权，谁就是反对德意志民主共和国。我决心用我的全部力量来捍卫德意志民主共和国。因此，我申请加入德国统一社会党。”
