Title: Twenty-One Asian and African Nations Call on the UN to Order Britain, France, and Israel to Immediately Withdraw Troops from Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd, New York Dispatch: Representatives of twenty-one Asian and African nations to the United Nations put forward a proposal on the evening of the 22nd, requesting the United Nations General Assembly to once again demand that France, Britain, and Israel immediately withdraw their troops from Egypt.

The proposing countries are: Afghanistan, Burma, Cambodia, Ceylon, Egypt, Ethiopia, India, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Nepal, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Syria, Tunisia, and Yemen.

The full text of the proposal is as follows:

“The General Assembly,

“Noting the Secretary-General's report on compliance with the General Assembly resolutions of November 2 and 7.

“Recalling the resolution of November 7, 1956, requesting Israel to withdraw its forces immediately behind the demarcation lines established by the 1949 Armistice Agreement,

“Further recalling that the above resolution also requested France and the United Kingdom to withdraw their forces immediately from Egyptian territory in accordance with previous resolutions,

“1. Notes with great concern that, although considerable time has elapsed since the General Assembly requested compliance with the resolutions and the immediate withdrawal of Israeli, French and United Kingdom forces through repeated resolutions, it has still not been complied with,

“2. Reaffirms its demand that Israel, France, and the United Kingdom comply immediately with the resolutions of November 2 and 7,

“3. Requests the Secretary-General to communicate this resolution urgently to the parties concerned and to report on its implementation without delay.”

### 二十一个亚非国家要求联合国  责成英法以立即把军队撤出埃及

1956-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

    二十一个亚非国家要求联合国
    责成英法以立即把军队撤出埃及
    新华社23日讯　纽约消息：联合国二十一个亚非国家的代表22日晚上提出提案，要求联合国大会再次要求法国、英国和以色列立即把军队撤出埃及。
    提案的发起国是：阿富汗、缅甸、柬埔寨、锡兰、埃及、埃塞俄比亚、印度、印度尼西亚、伊朗、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、摩洛哥、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、沙特阿拉伯、苏丹、叙利亚，突尼斯和也门。
    提案全文如下：
    “大会
    “注意到秘书长关于11月2日和7日大会决议是否得到遵守的报告。
    “回忆到1956年11月7日要求以色列立即把它的部队撤到1949年停战协定所确定的分界线后面的决议，
    “进一步回忆到上述决议也要求法国和联合王国遵照以前的决议立即把它们的部队撤出埃及领土，
    “一、非常忧虑地注意到，虽然大会要求遵守决议和以色列、法国和联合王国部队立即撤退的屡次的决议通过以后已经经过相当长的时间，但是仍然没有被遵守，
    “二、重申它要以色列、法国和联合王国立即遵守11月2日和7日的决议，
    “三、要求秘书长赶紧把这个决议通知有关方面，并且毫不迟延地就这个决议的履行情况提出报告。”
