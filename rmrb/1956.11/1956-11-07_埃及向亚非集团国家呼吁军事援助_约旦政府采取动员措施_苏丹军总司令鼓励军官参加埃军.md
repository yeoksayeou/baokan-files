Title: Egypt Appeals to Afro-Asian Group for Military Assistance; Jordanian Government Takes Mobilization Measures; Sudanese Army Commander-in-Chief Encourages Officers to Join Egyptian Army
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th, New York dispatch: Abdel Mohamed Hassak, a press officer for the Egyptian delegation to the United Nations, announced on the evening of November 5th that Egypt had written a letter appealing to all countries of the Afro-Asian Group to promptly provide Egypt with military assistance to help it resist the aggression of Britain, France, and Israel.

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th, Amman dispatch: The Jordanian government announced on November 5th the mobilization of the public for non-military resistance movements, and ordered secondary school students to receive military training and establish recruitment stations so that every citizen could contribute to defending their country.

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th, Cairo dispatch: Residents of Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, have been continuously holding demonstrations since November 2nd, protesting the Anglo-French aggression against Egypt. The demonstrating masses shouted slogans such as "Down with the pirates!"

According to the Middle East News Agency, the Commander-in-Chief of the Sudanese Armed Forces encouraged Sudanese officers on the 4th to join the Egyptian army in fighting against the aggressors.

### 埃及向亚非集团国家呼吁军事援助  约旦政府采取动员措施　苏丹军总司令鼓励军官参加埃军

1956-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及向亚非集团国家呼吁军事援助
    约旦政府采取动员措施　苏丹军总司令鼓励军官参加埃军
    新华社6日讯　纽约消息：埃及驻联合国代表团新闻发布官阿卜杜勒·穆罕默德·哈萨克5日晚上宣布：埃及已经写信呼吁亚非集团所有国家迅速给予埃及以军事援助，帮助它抗击英国、法国和以色列的侵略。
    新华社6日讯　安曼消息：约旦政府在11月5日宣布动员公众进行非军事的抵抗运动，并且下令中学学生受军事训练和建立募兵站，以使每一个公民都能贡献力量保卫自己的国家。
    新华社5日讯　开罗消息：苏丹首都喀土穆的居民从11月2日以来，就不断举行示威游行，抗议英法侵略埃及。游行的群众高呼“打倒海盗！”等口号。
    据中东通讯社报道，苏丹武装部队总司令4日鼓励苏丹军官去参加埃及军队同侵略者作战。
