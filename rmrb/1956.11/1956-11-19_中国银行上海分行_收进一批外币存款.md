Title: Bank of China Shanghai Branch Receives a Batch of Foreign Currency Deposits
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 18th. Since the Bank of China Shanghai Branch began offering interest-bearing "foreign currency deposit" services on October 15th, it has, within one month, received a batch of British pounds and Hong Kong dollars deposited by depositors from Hong Kong and other places, equivalent to RMB 25,600.
    The interest-bearing "foreign currency deposit" service offered by the Bank of China Shanghai Branch has received attention from all parties, and many people at home and abroad have written to inquire about deposit methods.

### 中国银行上海分行  收进一批外币存款

1956-11-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中国银行上海分行
    收进一批外币存款
    新华社上海18日电　中国银行上海分行自10月15日办理有息“外币存款”业务以来，一个月中已经收存了香港等地存户储存的英镑、港币一批，等于二万五千六百元人民币。
    中国银行上海分行举办的有息“外币存款”业务，受到各方面的重视，国内外许多人都写信来询问存款的办法。
