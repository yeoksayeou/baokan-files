Title: Indonesian Cabinet Meeting Concludes Lubis Attempted to Launch Coup
Author:
Date: 1956-11-29
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 28th: The Indonesian cabinet issued a communique today after a meeting, stating: "The cabinet meeting concluded that a series of agreements, preparations, and practical actions were undertaken, led by Lubis, to launch a coup."

The communique said: "The measures taken so far by the Army Chief of Staff and the Supreme Military Prosecutor were known and agreed to by the government in advance. The government ordered the Army Chief of Staff and the Supreme Military Prosecutor to continue taking measures in accordance with military law and army disciplinary regulations. The government will promptly issue a related statement to the parliament."

### 印度尼西亚内阁会议得出结论  卢比斯曾企图发动政变

1956-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁会议得出结论
    卢比斯曾企图发动政变
    新华社雅加达28日电　印度尼西亚内阁今天举行会议后发表公报说：“内阁会议得出结论，曾经产生一连串的协议、准备工作和实践行动，由卢比斯领导发动政变。”
    公报说：“陆军参谋长和最高军事检察官迄今所采取的措施事前都为政府所知悉和同意。政府命令陆军参谋长和最高军事检察官根据军法和军队纪律法规继续采取措施。政府将迅速向国会发表有关的声明。”
