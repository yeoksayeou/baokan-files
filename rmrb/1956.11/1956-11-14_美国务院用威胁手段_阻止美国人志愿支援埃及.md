Title: U.S. State Department Uses Threats to Prevent Americans from Voluntarily Supporting Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 11th. The U.S. State Department threatened the American people on November 10th, forbidding them from voluntarily supporting Egypt.

The State Department said that if anyone volunteers to go to Egypt to fight, they will lose their citizenship.

### 美国务院用威胁手段  阻止美国人志愿支援埃及

1956-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    美国务院用威胁手段
    阻止美国人志愿支援埃及
    新华社11日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院11月10日威胁美国人民，不许他们志愿支援埃及。
    国务院说，如果有人志愿到埃及去作战的话，他们将丧失他们的国籍。
