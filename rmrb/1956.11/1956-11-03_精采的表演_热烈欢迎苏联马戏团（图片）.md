Title: Wonderful Performance – Warmly Welcome the Soviet Circus (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 8

    Wonderful Performance
    ——Warmly Welcome the Soviet Circus
    The Soviet Circus, invited to visit our country, gave its opening performance at the Beijing Gymnasium on the evening of the 1st.
    The wonderful performances of the Soviet circus artists were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Right picture: At the opening performance, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of our country, delivered a welcome speech.
    Bottom picture: Juggler: Honored Artist of the Moldavian Soviet Socialist Republic, Vsevolod Herts. All photos on this page are taken by our reporter.
    Left picture: Lion-Bear Act. Performer: Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Ivan Ruban
    Right picture: Tightrope Walking. Performer: Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, F. Selbina.
    Bottom picture: Intermission Comedy.
  Performer: Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Boris Vyatkin
    Left picture: Ladder Skills.
  Performer: Honored Artist of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, Mirayev.
    Bottom picture: Ball Skills Performer: Malyenkov.
    Right picture: Martial Arts. Performers: Zapashny Brothers
    Bottom picture: Aerial Gymnastics. Performer: Mchinskaya
    Left picture: Dog Act: Performer: Victoria Olkhovikova
    Top first person: Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic: Boris Vyatkin Top second person: Honored Artist of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic: Mirayev Top: Honored Artist of the Moldavian Soviet Socialist Republic: Vsevolod Herts First person from the left: Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic: Ivan Ruban Left: Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic: F.
