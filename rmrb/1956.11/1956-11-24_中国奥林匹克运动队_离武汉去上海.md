Title: Chinese Olympic Team Leaves Wuhan for Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 21st: The Chinese Olympic Team left Wuhan for Shanghai by ship this morning. A total of five teams, including track and field, gymnastics, basketball, soccer, and weightlifting, departed from here to Shanghai.
    The above five teams gave six wonderful performances for more than 50,000 Wuhan spectators. In the last exhibition game on the 19th, the national basketball team defeated the Nanjing Military Team again with a score of 94 to 61. In addition, the track and field, gymnastics, and weightlifting teams in the delegation were also invited to the Wuhan Institute of Physical Education yesterday to give demonstration performances.

### 中国奥林匹克运动队  离武汉去上海

1956-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国奥林匹克运动队
    离武汉去上海
    新华社武汉21日电　中国奥林匹克运动队今天上午乘轮船离开武汉去上海。从这里去上海的共有田径、体操、篮球、足球和举重等五个代表队。
    上述五个代表队为五万多武汉观众作了六场精采的表演。国家篮球队在19日最后一场表演比赛中，又以九十四比六十一战胜了南京部队队。此外，代表团中的田径、体操和举重等代表队，昨天还应邀到武汉体育学院作了示范表演。
