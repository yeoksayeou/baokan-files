Title: Albania Reduces Prices, Will Raise Wages of Low-Paid Workers Starting Next Year
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 27th - Tirana, November 26th dispatch: The Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania and the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party have announced a decision to lower retail prices of industrial goods and grain.

The decision states that the Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Labor Party, considering the victorious completion of the national plan and following the instructions of the Third Party Congress on continuously improving the material and cultural living standards of the working people in urban and rural areas, have decided to lower retail prices of industrial goods and grain starting from November 26, 1956. According to this decision, grain prices will be reduced by 5% to 26.6%, clothing prices will be reduced by 4.3% to 64%, and the prices of furniture, building materials, other industrial products, and tickets for artistic performances will be reduced by 6% to 60%, respectively.

The decision points out that this price reduction will give residents an annual benefit of more than 750 million leks (Albanian currency).

The newspapers also published the decision of the Albanian Council of Ministers, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions on raising the wages of low-paid staff members. This decision stipulates that the wages of low-paid workers will be increased by 41.4% starting from January 1, 1957.

According to newspaper reports, the Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Labor Party have also decided to increase the amount of preferential treatment for the families of those who sacrificed themselves in the national liberation war, servicemen, and workers, starting from January 1, 1957.

### 阿尔巴尼亚降低物价  明年起提高低薪职工的工资

1956-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚降低物价
    明年起提高低薪职工的工资
    新华社布拉格27日电　地拉那26日消息：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会公布了关于降低工业品和粮食的零售价格的决定。
    决定中说，部长会议和劳动党中央委员会考虑到国家计划的胜利完成和遵循第三次党代表大会关于不断提高城乡劳动人民物质和文化生活水平的指示，决定从1956年11月26日起降低工业品和粮食的零售价格。根据这项决定，粮食价格降低5％到26.6％，服装价格减低4.3％到64％，家具、建筑材料和其他工业品的价格以及艺术演出的票价等分别减低6％到60％不等。
    决定指出，这一次降低价格使居民一年中可以得到七亿五千万里克（阿币）以上的进益。
    各报还刊载了阿尔巴尼亚部长会议、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和阿尔巴尼亚总工会中央理事会关于提高低薪工作人员工资的决定。这项决定规定从1957年1月1日起把低薪职工的工资提高41.4％。
    据报纸报道，部长会议和劳动党中央委员会还决定从1957年1月1日起提高在民族解放战争中牺牲人员的家属、军人以及职工的优抚金的数额。
