Title: The Great Soviet People March Forward on the Road to Communism (Pictures)
Author: Xie Ming, Ivanov-Alliluyev, Shpilov, Yevgeniy, Klippenitser, Chernishev, Kagiyev, Zaitsev
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 8

The great Soviet people are advancing on the road to communism.

　　During the Sixth Five-Year Plan, the Soviet Union will greatly improve the level of agricultural mechanization. These are a large number of tractors manufactured by the nationally renowned Stalingrad Tractor Factory, ready to be shipped to all parts of the Soviet Union.

　　The giant Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station on the Volga River will be fully completed during the Sixth Five-Year Plan. Its power generation capacity is 2.1 million kilowatts. This is the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station under construction. Photo by Xie Ming (TASS draft)

　　Moscow, the capital of the Soviet Union, will build new residences with a living area of 1.36 million square meters this year. This is a new residential area under construction in the southwest district of Moscow.
　　　　　　　　Photo by Ivanov-Alliluyev
　　　　　　　　　　　　(TASS draft)

　　Soviet scientists are conducting a large number of comprehensive scientific expeditions in the Arctic, where they have established drifting ice scientific stations. This is the "Arctic No. 6" drifting ice scientific station newly built this spring. Photo by Shpilov (TASS draft)

　　According to the resolution of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet Union opened 285 boarding schools this year. These are the children in the boarding school getting ready in the morning.
　　　　Yevgeniy Photo (TASS draft)

　　In July of this year, the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union passed a new preferential pension law. This law greatly increased the amount of pensions. This is Polikarpov, director of the Social Insurance Department of Yashkino Village, Chkalov Oblast, issuing pension certificates to workers receiving pensions.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Klippenitser (TASS draft)

　　This year, the Soviet Union achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest of grain, especially in the newly reclaimed areas in the east. This is the Budyonny Collective Farm in the Krasnoyarsk Territory harvesting wheat using the segmented harvesting method.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Chernishev (Originally published in Soviet Pictorial)

　　In recent years, many engineering and technical personnel and workers from our country have interned in Soviet factories to learn advanced production experience from Soviet friends. This is a group of comrades from the Lanzhou Petroleum Machinery Factory of our country who are interning at the Baku Schmidt Factory in the Soviet Union, talking with Soviet friends during a break.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Kagiyev (TASS draft)

　　This year is the year with the highest cotton output in Soviet history. The cotton harvest in the Central Asian region is abundant, and large quantities of cotton are continuously being sent to procurement stations. This is a procurement station in the Tajik Republic, unloading the delivered cotton and stacking it into cotton stacks. Photo by Zaitsev (TASS draft)

### 伟大的苏联人民在共产主义的道路上迈进（图片）
谢明  伊万诺夫—阿里鲁耶夫  什比洛夫  叶夫皆里新  里皮尼采尔  契尔内舍夫  卡吉耶夫  萨依采夫
1956-11-07
第8版()
专栏：

    伟大的苏联人民在共产主义的道路上迈进
    苏联在第六个五年计划中，要大大提高农业机械化的水平。这是全国闻名的斯大林格勒拖拉机制造厂制造的大批拖拉机，正准备运往全苏各地。
    伏尔加河上巨大的古比雪夫水电站将在第六个五年计划期间全部建成。它的发电能力是二百一十万瓩。这是正在建筑中的古比雪夫水电站。　谢明摄（塔斯社稿）
    苏联首都莫斯科今年将要建筑居住面积达一百三十六万平方公尺的新住宅。这是莫斯科西南区正在建筑中的新住宅。
        伊万诺夫—阿里鲁耶夫摄
                  （塔斯社稿）
    苏联的科学家们正在北极进行着大量的综合科学考察工作，他们在那里建立了浮冰科学站。这是今年春天新建的“北极六号”浮冰科学站。　什比洛夫摄（塔斯社稿）
    根据苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议，苏联今年开办了二百八十五所寄宿学校。这是寄宿学校的孩子们清晨起身后正在梳洗。
      叶夫皆里新摄（塔斯社稿）
    今年7月苏联最高苏维埃通过了一项新的优抚金法案。这项法案大大提高了养老金的金额。这是迟卡洛夫州雅什金诺村社会保险部主任波里卡尔波夫向领取养老金的工人颁发养老金证明书。
                                克里皮尼采尔摄（塔斯社稿）
    今年苏联获得了空前未有的谷物大丰收，东部开荒地区收成特别好。这是克拉斯诺雅尔斯克边疆区布琼尼集体农庄正在用分段收割法收割麦子。
    契尔内舍夫摄（原载苏联画报）
    几年来我国有不少工程技术人员和工人在苏联的工厂中实习，向苏联朋友们学习先进的生产经验。这是我国兰州石油机械厂在苏联巴库施米特工厂实习的同志，在休息的时候和苏联朋友们谈话。
                                    卡吉耶夫摄（塔斯社稿）
    今年是苏联历史上棉花产量最高的一年。中亚细亚地区的棉花获得丰收，大批棉花接连不断地送到采购站。这是塔吉克共和国的一个采购站，正把送来的棉花卸下车来，堆成棉花垛。萨依采夫摄（塔斯社稿）
