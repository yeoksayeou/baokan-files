Title: European and Asian Socialist Countries Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The working people of European and Asian socialist countries warmly celebrated the 39th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party held a celebration meeting for the October Revolution anniversary on the evening of the 6th in the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw. All members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and more than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Warsaw attended the meeting. The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, presided over the opening of the meeting and delivered a speech. The Chairman of the Polish State Council, Zawadzki, made a report on the October Revolution anniversary. They both emphasized the important significance of the October Revolution to Poland, and the close unity, friendship and cooperation between Poland and the Soviet Union and between the two parties. Zawadzki said that the unity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union is of decisive significance at present for defending world peace and progress, for the defense strength of the socialist camp, and for defending the achievements of the working class and the masses.

The Central Committee of the German United Socialist Party, the GDR Council of Ministers and the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany held a celebration meeting for the October Revolution anniversary on the 6th at the Capital Theater in Berlin. Mueckenberger, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the German United Socialist Party, made a report.

Four thousand working people in Bucharest held a meeting on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Revolution. Romanian Workers' Party and government leaders such as Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and Stoica attended the celebration meeting. Pavelescu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, made a report celebrating the October Revolution anniversary.

In Bulgaria, a grand celebration rally was held at the People's Theater in Sofia on the evening of the 6th. Ganovski, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and academician of the Academy of Sciences, made a report at the meeting. Working people in other cities and villages in Bulgaria also held celebration meetings and evening parties these two days.

In Czechoslovakia, to celebrate the October Revolution anniversary, the national Czech-Soviet Friendship Month began on the 7th. The traditional Peace and Friendship Relay Race also began on this day. On the eve of the festival—November 6, the National Theater in Prague held a celebration performance.

On the eve of the October Revolution anniversary, employees of many factories, members of agricultural producers' cooperatives, and people from literary and art circles in Albania wrote letters to Soviet friends, expressing their gratitude for the Soviet Union's friendship and assistance, and congratulating the Soviet people on their new achievements.

People from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a gathering on the 6th to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Revolution. The meeting was presided over by Li Yuqiu, Deputy Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korea-Soviet Cultural Association. Premier Kim Il-sung and other Korean state leaders, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to North Korea, attended the meeting. At the meeting, Kim Chang-man, Deputy Chairman of the Korean Workers' Party, made a report, and Soviet Ambassador to North Korea Ivanov also spoke.

In Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, a celebration rally and parade of more than 50,000 people was held on November 7. Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, told the masses gathered in Sukhbaatar Square about the great achievements of the Soviet people and the construction achievements made by the Mongolian people with the help of the Soviet Union.

The Ministry of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association jointly held a meeting in Hanoi on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the October Revolution anniversary. At the meeting, Yang Bai Mei, Vice President of the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association, made a report celebrating the festival. Popov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Vietnam, also spoke.

### 欧亚社会主义国家庆祝十月革命节

1956-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    欧亚社会主义国家庆祝十月革命节
    据新华社7日讯　欧洲和亚洲社会主义国家的劳动人民热烈庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会6日晚间在华沙文化科学宫举行了十月革命节庆祝大会。波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局全体委员和华沙各界代表三千多人参加了大会。波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡主持大会开幕，并且发表了演说。波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基作了关于十月革命节的报告。他们都特别强调十月革命对波兰的重要意义，强调波苏两国和两党之间的亲密团结友谊和合作。萨瓦茨基说，以苏联为首的社会主义国家的团结对于保卫世界和平和进步、对于社会主义阵营的防御力量、对于保卫工人阶级和人民群众所取得的成果，在目前具有决定性的意义。
    德国统一社会党中央委员会、德意志民主共和国部长会议和民主德国全国阵线全国委员会6日在柏林的首都剧院举行了庆祝十月革命节的大会。德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局候补委员穆肯柏格尔作了报告。
    布加勒斯特的四千个劳动人民6日晚间举行了庆祝十月革命三十九周年的大会。罗马尼亚工人党和政府的领导人乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡等出席了庆祝大会。工人党中央委员会政治局委员帕伏列斯库作了庆祝十月革命节的报告。
    在保加利亚，6日晚在索非亚的人民剧院举行了盛大的庆祝集会。保共中央委员、科学院士加诺夫斯基在会上作了报告。保加利亚其他城市和农村的劳动人民在这两天也纷纷举行了庆祝会和晚会。
    在捷克斯洛伐克，为了庆祝十月革命节，从7日起全国开始举行捷苏友好月。传统的和平与友谊接力赛也在这一天开始举行。在节日前夕——11月6日，布拉格的民族剧院举行了庆祝节日的演出会。
    在十月革命节前夕，阿尔巴尼亚许多工厂的职工、农业生产合作社社员和文学艺术界人士等纷纷写信给苏联朋友，表示感谢苏联的友谊援助，并且祝贺苏联人民获得新的成就。
    平壤各界在6日举行集会，庆祝十月革命三十九周年。大会由朝苏文化协会中央委员会副委员长李诩求主持。金日成首相等朝鲜国家领导人以及各国驻朝鲜外交使节都出席了大会。大会上，朝鲜劳动党副委员长金昌满作了报告，苏联驻朝鲜大使伊凡诺夫也讲了话。
    在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托，11月7日举行了五万多人的庆祝集会和游行。蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布向聚集在苏赫巴托尔广场上的群众讲述了苏联人民的伟大成就，和蒙古人民在苏联帮助下取得的建设成就。
    越南民主共和国文化部和越苏友好协会6日晚间在河内联合举行了庆祝十月革命节的大会。会上，越苏友好协会副会长杨白梅作了庆祝节日的报告。苏联驻越南大使馆临时代办波波夫也讲了话。
