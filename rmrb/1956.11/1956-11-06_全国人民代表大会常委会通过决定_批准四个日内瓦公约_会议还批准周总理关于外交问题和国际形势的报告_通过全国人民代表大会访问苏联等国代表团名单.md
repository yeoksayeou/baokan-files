Title: NPC Standing Committee Passes Decision Approving Four Geneva Conventions; Meeting Also Approves Premier Zhou's Report on Diplomatic Issues and the International Situation; Passes List of NPC Delegation to Visit the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 50th meeting on the afternoon of the 5th.

The meeting reviewed the proposal submitted by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for a decision to approve the 1949 Geneva Conventions, and adopted four decisions to approve the Geneva Convention of August 12, 1949, for the Amelioration of the Condition of the Wounded and Sick in Armed Forces in the Field, the Geneva Convention of August 12, 1949, for the Amelioration of the Condition of Wounded, Sick and Shipwrecked Members of Armed Forces at Sea, the Geneva Convention of August 12, 1949, relative to the Treatment of Prisoners of War, and the Geneva Convention of August 12, 1949, relative to the Protection of Civilian Persons in Time of War. In the decisions on approving the aforementioned four Geneva Conventions, the meeting also made reservations on certain articles of the aforementioned four Geneva Conventions. After the meeting, Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, in accordance with the decision of the 50th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved the aforementioned four Geneva Conventions.

The meeting listened to and discussed Premier Zhou Enlai's report on diplomatic issues and the current international situation. Premier Zhou Enlai made a report at the meeting on the Sino-Burmese border issue, the current situation in the Middle East, the Hungarian issue, and the relationship between socialist countries. The meeting gave principled approval to Premier Zhou Enlai's diplomatic report.

The meeting also approved the list of the delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China to visit the Soviet Union, Romania, and Czechoslovakia.

Members of the State Council, responsible individuals from the Supreme People's Court and the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and members from the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting today as observers.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会通过决定  批准四个日内瓦公约  会议还批准周总理关于外交问题和国际形势的报告　通过全国人民代表大会访问苏联等国代表团名单

1956-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会通过决定
    批准四个日内瓦公约
    会议还批准周总理关于外交问题和国际形势的报告　通过全国人民代表大会访问苏联等国代表团名单
    新华社5日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在5日下午举行第五十次会议。
    会议审议了国务院总理周恩来提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定批准1949年日内瓦公约的议案，通过了四个决定，决定批准1949年8月12日改善战地武装部队伤者病者境遇之日内瓦公约、1949年8月12日改善海上武装部队伤者病者及遇船难者境遇之日内瓦公约、1949年8月12日关于战俘待遇之日内瓦公约、1949年8月12日关于战时保护平民之日内瓦公约。会议在关于批准上述四个日内瓦公约的决定中，对上述四个日内瓦公约的若干条款还作了保留。会后，中华人民共和国主席毛泽东已根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十次会议的决定，批准了上述四个日内瓦公约。
    会议听取和讨论了周恩来总理关于外交问题和目前国际形势的报告。周恩来总理就中缅边界问题、中东目前形势、匈牙利问题和社会主义国家的关系问题在会上作了报告。会议对于周恩来总理的外交报告，作了原则的批准。
    会议还通过了中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会访问苏联、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克代表团的名单。
    国务院组成人员，最高人民法院、最高人民检察院负责人和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员列席了今天的会议。
