Title: Nearly Two Thousand Beijing Youth Celebrate with Artillery Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: Nearly two thousand Beijing youth held a celebration today with a certain artillery unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed locally. At the meeting, representatives of the troops reported to the youth on the glorious traditions of the People's Liberation Army and the heroic fighting deeds of their unit, and warmly welcomed the Beijing youth who will enlist in the army this year. Representatives of the Beijing youth also spoke at the meeting.

After the meeting, in the clubs, sports grounds, and shooting ranges of the military camp, groups of young people and soldiers could be seen engaging in various celebration activities. The youth were most interested in watching the artillerymen's heavy artillery operation performance. When the performance ended, some young people happily rode on the gun barrels, and many surged onto the gun carriages, shaking hands with the soldiers and laughing. More than twenty young people also participated in live ammunition shooting.

### 北京近两千青年和炮兵部队联欢

1956-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    北京近两千青年和炮兵部队联欢
    据新华社25日讯　北京市近二千青年今天和驻在当地的中国人民解放军某炮兵部队举行了联欢会。会上，部队代表向青年们报告了人民解放军的光荣传统和本部队的英勇斗争事迹，并且对本年度应征入伍的北京市青年表示热烈欢迎。北京市青年代表在会上也讲了话。
    会后，在军营的俱乐部里、运动场上和靶场上，到处可以看到一群群的青年和士兵们在亲密地进行各种联欢活动，青年们最感到兴趣的是参观炮兵们的重炮操作表演。当表演结束的时候，有的青年高兴地骑上炮筒，很多人涌上炮车握着战士们的手欢笑。有二十多个青年还参加了实弹射击。
