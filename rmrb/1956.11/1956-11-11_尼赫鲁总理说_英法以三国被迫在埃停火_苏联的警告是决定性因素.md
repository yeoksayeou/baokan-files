Title: Prime Minister Nehru Says Britain, France and Israel Were Forced to Cease Fire in Egypt; Soviet Warning Was Decisive Factor
Author:
Date: 1956-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 9th - According to the Indian Press Trust, Indian Prime Minister Nehru said today at a meeting of the Indian National Congress Working Committee that, in his view, the recent warnings issued by Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin to Britain, France, and Israel were the most decisive factor leading to the ceasefire in Egypt by these three countries.

It is said that Nehru also said, when referring to recent international developments, that the current situation remains tense and difficult.

He said India would contribute to the planned UN Emergency Force.

The Indian National Congress Working Committee held a two-hour meeting in Bombay this morning to discuss the international situation.

### 尼赫鲁总理说  英法以三国被迫在埃停火  苏联的警告是决定性因素

1956-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理说
    英法以三国被迫在埃停火  苏联的警告是决定性因素
    新华社新德里9日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度总理尼赫鲁今天在印度国大党工作委员会的一个会议上说，在他看来，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁最近向英、法、以三国提出的警告，是导致这三个国家在埃及停火的最有决定性的因素。
    据说，尼赫鲁在提到最近的国际事态发展时还说，现在的局势仍然是紧张和困难的。
    他说，印度将对计划中的联合国紧急部队作出贡献。
    印度国大党工作委员会今天早晨在孟买举行了两小时的会议来讨论国际局势。
