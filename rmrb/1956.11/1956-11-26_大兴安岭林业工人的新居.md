Title: New Homes for Forestry Workers in the Greater Khingan Mountains
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Yakeshi, November 25th: By mid-November this year, over 1,500 households of forestry industry workers in the Greater Khingan Mountains had moved into new homes.

    The Greater Khingan Mountains forest industry system has built more than 65,000 square meters of residential housing for families this year. By the end of the year, approximately 2,000 households of forest industry workers will be able to live in new houses. The new houses built this year are mostly equipped with fire walls and heated brick beds suitable for the climatic conditions of the forest area, and are installed with ceilings, floors, and bright glass windows. Most of these residences are built near the production sites.

### 大兴安岭林业工人的新居

1956-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

    大兴安岭林业工人的新居
    据新华社牙克石25日电　大兴安岭森林工业职工，到今年11月中旬为止先后有一千五百多户搬进新居。
    大兴安岭森林工业系统今年建设了六万五千多平方公尺的家属住宅。到年末，森林工业职工约有二千多户可以住进新房屋。今年建设的新住宅大都设有适合林区气候条件的火墙，火炕，并且安装了天棚、地板和明亮的玻璃窗。这些住宅大部分建立在生产现场的附近地方。
