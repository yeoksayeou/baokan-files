Title: Kachin State Leader U Zanhta Hsin and Others from Burma Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: Kachin State Minister U Zanhta Hsin, Chairman of the Burmese Union Kachin State Culture and Economy Association and Member of Parliament Shinwa Nau, former Kachin State Minister and Kachin State Administrative Chairman Duwa Zaulang, Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Union Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ba Lin Dun, and Kachin State Secretary U San Long, a group of five people, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 31st. Burmese Ambassador to China U La Maung and Director of the Research Department of the Burmese Union Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Qin Niu also arrived on the same plane.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Chun.

Judge of the Burmese Union High Court U Min Deng and First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China U Ba Maung also welcomed them at the airport.

### 缅甸克钦邦领导人吴赞塔信等到达北京

1956-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦领导人吴赞塔信等到达北京
    新华社31日讯　缅甸克钦邦领导人克钦部部长吴赞塔信、缅甸联邦克钦邦文化经济协会主席、国会议员申瓦闹、前克钦部部长兼克钦部政务主席杜瓦藻朗和缅甸联邦外交部常务秘书巴林顿、克钦邦秘书吴珊龙等一行五人，在31日下午乘飞机到达北京。缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和缅甸联邦外交部研究司司长吴钦纽也同机到达。
    到机场欢迎的，有外交部副部长章汉夫和民族事务委员会副主任刘春等人。
    缅甸联邦高级法院法官吴敏登和缅甸驻华大使馆一等秘书吴巴茂也到机场欢迎。
