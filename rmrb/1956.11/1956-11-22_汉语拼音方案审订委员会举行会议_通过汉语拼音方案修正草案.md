Title: Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee Holds Meeting, Passes Revised Draft of Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme
Author:
Date: 1956-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - The Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee, at its fourth meeting held today, passed the revised draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme. This revised draft will be submitted to the State Council for approval and promulgation.

Today's meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Review Committee. Present were Vice Chairmen Zhang Xiruo, Hu Qiaomu, and fourteen committee members.

The revised draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme passed this time is the first revision style of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme.

Since October 17th, the Review Committee has held four consecutive meetings, listened to the explanation of the Chinese Character Reform Committee's Pinyin Scheme Committee on the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme issue, and conducted thorough research and discussion on the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme and the first and second revision styles, as well as the schemes proposed by Ding Xilin, Wei Que, and Li Jinxi, members of the Pinyin Scheme Committee.

During the meeting, the Review Committee invited people from the linguistic, educational, scientific and technological, literary and art, news, publishing, and translation circles in Beijing, as well as people's organizations and military units, to organize multiple symposiums, and solicited opinions on the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme from linguistic circles in various places through correspondence. Among the 234 people who expressed opinions in the Beijing symposiums and through correspondence in various places, 136 people supported the first revision style, accounting for 58.1% of the total.

### 汉语拼音方案审订委员会举行会议  通过汉语拼音方案修正草案

1956-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    汉语拼音方案审订委员会举行会议
    通过汉语拼音方案修正草案
    新华社21日讯　汉语拼音方案审订委员会在今天召开的第四次会议上，通过了汉语拼音方案修正草案。这个修正草案将提请国务院批准公布。
    今天的会议由审订委员会主任郭沫若主持。到会的有副主任张奚若、胡乔木和委员十四人。
    这次通过的汉语拼音方案修正草案是汉语拼音方案修正第一式。
    审订委员会自10月17日以来，相继举行了四次会议，听取了中国文字改革委员会拼音方案委员会关于汉语拼音方案问题的说明，就汉语拼音方案草案和修正第一式、第二式，以及拼音方案委员会委员丁西林、韦悫、黎锦熙三人提出的方案，进行了充分的研究和讨论。
    审订委员会在会议期间，曾邀请在北京的语言学界、教育界、科学技术界、文艺界、新闻界、出版界、翻译界，以及人民团体和部队的人士组织了多次座谈会，并向各地语言学界用通信方式征集了对汉语拼音方案的意见。在参加北京座谈和各地通信时表示意见的二百三十四人中，有一百三十六人赞成修正第一式，即占总数58.1％。
