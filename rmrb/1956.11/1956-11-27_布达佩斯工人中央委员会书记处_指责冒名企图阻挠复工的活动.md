Title: Secretariat of the Budapest Workers' Central Committee Accuses Imposters of Attempting to Obstruct Resumption of Work
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 25th (TASS dispatch): Budapest Radio broadcast a notice from the Secretariat of the Budapest Workers' Central Committee. The notice pointed out that certain individuals and groups, not implementing the decision to resume work, were issuing irresponsible calls in the name of the Budapest Workers' Central Committee and the workers' committees of individual enterprises. The notice reminded the working people of the entire country to be aware that the purpose of these provocative calls was to cause panic and disrupt the ranks of the workers.

The Budapest Workers' Central Committee believes that its call issued on November 23 regarding the necessity to continue the production work that had already begun throughout the country remains valid.

The notice pointed out that the Budapest Workers' Central Committee would continue talks with the government on November 26.

Hungarian Prime Minister Kádár spoke to representatives of the workers' committees of major enterprises in Budapest at the Parliament Building on the 25th.

Newspapers and radio stations continue to report on the resumption of work in enterprise departments across the country. On the 24th, 90% of the miners in Ostrava began work.

Hungarian Minister of the Armed Forces and Public Security, Münnich, expressed his gratitude to the recently established Hungarian Armed Forces officer corps and the staff of the police organs for their efforts in maintaining domestic order and for victoriously suppressing the sabotage of terrorists, bandits, and looters.

### 布达佩斯工人中央委员会书记处  指责冒名企图阻挠复工的活动

1956-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    布达佩斯工人中央委员会书记处
    指责冒名企图阻挠复工的活动
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯25日讯：布达佩斯电台广播了布达佩斯工人中央委员会书记处的通告。通告指出，不执行复工决定的某些个人和团体以布达佩斯工人中央委员会和个别企业的工人委员会的名义发出了不负责任的号召。通告提醒全国劳动人民注意，这些挑拨性号召的目的是引起惊慌和破坏工人队伍。
    布达佩斯工人中央委员会认为它在11月23日发表的关于必须在全国继续已经开始的生产工作的号召仍然有效。
    通告指出，布达佩斯工人中央委员会将于11月26日继续同政府进行会谈。
    匈牙利总理卡达尔25日在议会大厦向布达佩斯各大企业的工人委员会的代表们讲了话。
    报纸和无线电台继续报道全国各地企业部门恢复工作的情况。24日在俄斯特有90％的矿工开始了工作。
    匈牙利武装部队和公安部部长明尼赫向最近建立起来的匈牙利武装部队军官团以及警察机关的工作人员表示感谢，因为他们努力维持国内秩序，并且胜利地扑灭了恐怖分子、匪徒和抢劫者的捣乱。
