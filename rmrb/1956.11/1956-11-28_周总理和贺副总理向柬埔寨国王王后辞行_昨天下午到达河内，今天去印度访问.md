Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Bid Farewell to the King and Queen of Cambodia; Arrived in Hanoi Yesterday Afternoon, Leaving for India Today
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 27th – Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage bid farewell to King Suramarit and the Queen of Cambodia at 12 noon today.

The King and Queen awaited the guests in the Throne Hall, with the Royal Guards lined up outside the hall. As Zhou Enlai and his party arrived, the band played the national anthems of China and Cambodia.

Zhou Enlai presented gifts to the King and Queen. The King and Queen also presented gifts to Zhou Enlai, He Long, and their entourage, and asked Zhou Enlai to pass on the gifts they gave to Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Zhu De.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 27th – Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage left Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia, at 2 p.m. today. They will travel to India via Hanoi.

As they left the Royal Palace where they had stayed for five days in cars, King Suramarit and the Queen stood on the steps of the Throne Hall, waving goodbye. Royal Guards lined all the roads within the palace, presenting arms in salute. As when they arrived for their visit five days ago, countless citizens of Phnom Penh lined the roads to the airport, bidding farewell to the distinguished guests.

Thousands of people also gathered at the airport to say goodbye to the Chinese Premier, including representatives of overseas Chinese from all over Cambodia.

All senior officials of the Royal Government and foreign diplomatic representatives in Phnom Penh were present, seated on the platform specially built for receiving distinguished guests.

As Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Sihanouk arrived at the airport in a car, the band played the national anthems of China and Cambodia.

Then, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long reviewed the guard of honor and shook hands with the well-wishers to say goodbye.

After they ascended the platform, Prime Minister San Yun delivered a farewell speech. He emphasized the significance of the Chinese Premier's visit in strengthening Sino-Cambodian friendship. He wished that China and Cambodia would always be companions in good and bad times.

In his congratulatory speech, Zhou Enlai once again expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Royal Government and people of Cambodia for their grand and warm reception. He expressed his belief that the friendship between Cambodia and China could not only become a model of international cooperation and friendship between peoples, but would also certainly make important contributions to peace in Asia and the world.

Prince Sihanouk and all the well-wishers present at the scene continued to wave at the airport after the plane carrying Premier Zhou Enlai took off, and only left the airport after the plane had disappeared into the distance.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th – Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage arrived in Hanoi by plane from Phnom Penh this afternoon. They will leave Hanoi tomorrow for a visit to India.

Present at the airport to welcome them were Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Vietnamese Ambassador to China Hoang Van Hoan, the Indian Consul-General in Hanoi, and other individuals.

Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo was also at the airport to welcome them.

### 周总理和贺副总理向柬埔寨国王王后辞行  昨天下午到达河内，今天去印度访问

1956-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和贺副总理向柬埔寨国王王后辞行
    昨天下午到达河内，今天去印度访问
    新华社金边27日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理和随行人员，在今天中午十二时向柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特和王后辞行。
    国王和王后在御座宝殿上等候客人，殿外排列着御林军，在周恩来等一行到达的时候，乐队奏了中国和柬埔寨的国歌。
    周恩来向国王、王后赠了礼。国王和王后也向周恩来、贺龙和随行人员赠了礼，并且请周恩来把他们赠给毛泽东主席和朱德副主席的礼物转交给他们。
    新华社金边27日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理等一行在今天下午二时离开柬埔寨首都金边。他们将经河内前往印度访问。
    在他们坐着汽车离开他们住了五天的王宫的时候，苏拉玛里特国王和王后一道站在御座宝殿台阶上向他们挥手送别。宫内所有的道路上都有御林军列队行军礼致敬。和五天以前他们来这里访问的时候一样，无数的金边市民排列在通往机场的道路上欢送贵宾们。
    机场上也聚集了成千上万的人，向中国总理告别，其中有从柬埔寨各地来的华侨代表。
    王国政府全部高级官员和在金边的外国外交代表几乎都来了，他们坐在为接待贵宾而特别建筑起来的礼台上。
    在周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王坐在一辆汽车里来到机场的时候，乐队奏起了中国和柬埔寨的国歌。
    接着，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理检阅了仪仗队，并且和欢送的人们握手告别。
    在他们登上礼台以后，桑云首相致了欢送词。他强调指出中国总理这次访问对加强中柬友谊的意义。他祝中国和柬埔寨在好的和坏的日子里，都永远是同伴。
    周恩来在致祝词的时候再一次对柬埔寨王国政府和人民隆重而热情地接待，表示衷心的感谢。他表示相信柬埔寨和中国的友谊不仅可以成为国际合作和各国人民友好的榜样，而且还一定能对亚洲和世界和平作出重要的贡献。
    西哈努克亲王和所有在场欢送的人们在周恩来总理乘坐的飞机飞升上空以后，还在机场上不断挥手致意，一直等飞机远去以后才离开机场。
    新华社河内27日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员今天下午乘飞机从金边抵达河内。他们将在明天离开河内去印度访问。
    在机场欢迎的有范文同总理、越南驻中国大使黄文欢、印度驻河内总领事和其他人士。
    中国驻越南大使罗贵波也在机场欢迎。
