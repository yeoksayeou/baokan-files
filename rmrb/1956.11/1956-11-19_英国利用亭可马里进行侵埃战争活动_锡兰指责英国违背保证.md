Title: Britain Used Trincomalee for Aggression Against Egypt; Ceylon Accuses Britain of Violating Guarantees
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 18th, Colombo dispatch: A senior official of the Ceylon Ministry of Defense said on November 17th that the Ceylonese government had received a report from the Commander of the Ceylonese Naval Fleet, De Mel, accusing Britain of using the Ceylonese naval base, Trincomalee, for war activities during the attack on Egypt.

The official said that this action by Britain violated its past guarantees not to use Ceylonese bases in the Suez Canal dispute.

The report said that the British cruiser "Newfoundland" had docked in Trincomalee to refuel before participating in the war in Egypt.

### 英国利用亭可马里进行侵埃战争活动  锡兰指责英国违背保证

1956-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    英国利用亭可马里进行侵埃战争活动
    锡兰指责英国违背保证
    据新华社18日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰国防部一位高级官员11月17日说，锡兰政府接到了锡兰海军舰队司令德梅耳的一份报告，指责英国在进攻埃及期间使用锡兰海军基地亭可马里进行战争活动。
    这位官员说，英国的这一行动违反了它过去提出的在苏伊士运河争端中不使用锡兰基地的保证。
    这份报告说，英国巡洋舰“纽芬兰号”在参加埃及战争以前曾经停泊在亭可马里添加燃料。
