Title: Plant Quarantine and Plant Protection Stations Universally Established in Hebei Province
Author: Liang Zhaobin
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 3

To actively carry out plant quarantine and plant protection work, plant quarantine and plant protection stations have been universally established in the ten special districts under the jurisdiction of Hebei Province. The main tasks of these stations are to conduct plant quarantine, investigate plant quarantine objects and general pests of diseases, insects, birds and animals, and eliminate serious crop diseases and insect pests. (Liang Zhaobin)

### 河北省普遍建立植物检疫和植物保护站
梁兆斌
1956-11-05
第3版()
专栏：

    河北省普遍建立植物检疫和植物保护站
    本报讯　为了积极开展植物检疫和植物保护工作，河北省所属十个专区普遍建立了植物检疫和植物保护站。这些站的主要任务是进行植物检疫，进行植物检疫对象和一般病虫鸟兽害的调查，消灭严重的农作物病虫害。　　　（梁兆斌）
