Title: Visiting the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
Author: Wu Boxiao
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 8

Visiting the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
    Wu Boxiao
    Let the footsteps be light, very light,
    Without a trace of sound;
    Millions of hearts beating gently,
    A long line slowly advancing.
    This is an extraordinary meeting,
    This is a golden moment;
    Like the red May Day every year,
    People are receiving the mentors' review.
    Lenin and Lenin's comrades and students,
    Are resting quietly, sleeping;
    As if considering something,
    Their faces are as fresh as the morning.
    Looking up at the mentors' remains,
    Unable to blink an eye;
    Seeing that slightly bent right hand about to reach out,
    That sleeping giant about to awaken.
    I seemed to hear earnest instructions,
    In the deep, deep silence;
    Those instructions are silent words,
    But everyone understands that profound meaning.
    Walking out of the solemn and clean tomb passage,
    The long line still maintains its solemnity;
    Unable to speak, unable to sing,
    Silently exchanging that pure language.
    That language is a call to life and labor,
    That language is an international song;
    People come with different beliefs,
    People will return with that common language.
    Whether men or women, from the countryside or the city,
    Millions of people share one feeling, one thought;
    Whether white or black, from the Rhine or the Nile,
    All pray in their hearts: Long live! Great mentor.
    I, this traveler from the East, am also full of thoughts,
    These thoughts are not sadness or melancholy;
    But because the motherland has realized Lenin's ideals,
    I feel unparalleled excitement, glory, and happiness.
    Still walking in the line, I made a wish:
    For the people, I should do more, do more work.
    In Lenin's immortal cause,
    Even being the smallest cog is not in vain.
        October 24, 1956, night in Moscow

### 谒列宁——斯大林墓
吴伯箫
1956-11-30
第8版()
专栏：

    谒列宁——斯大林墓
    吴伯箫
    让脚步轻轻地，轻轻地，
    不要有一丝儿声音；
    千万颗心脉脉地跳动，
    长长的行列在缓缓行进。
    这是不平凡的会晤，
    这是黄金一样的时刻；
    像年年红色的五月一日，
    人们在接受导师的检阅。
    列宁和列宁的战友、学生，
    都在静静地休息，睡眠；
    又仿佛在考虑什么事情，
    脸色都像早晨一样新鲜。
    瞻仰导师的遗容，
    舍不得眨一眨眼睛；
    眼看那微屈的右手就要伸出，
    那沉睡的巨人就要苏醒。
    我仿佛听见了谆谆的嘱咐，
    在深沉的深沉的静默里；
    那嘱咐是无声的语言，
    但是谁都懂得那深邃的意义。
    从庄严整洁的墓道里走出，
    长长的行列仍然保持着肃穆；
    说不出话，也唱不出歌，
    默默交流的是那纯洁的言语。
    那言语是生活和劳动的号召，
    那言语是一首国际歌曲；
    人们带着不同的信念走来，
    人们将带着那共同的言语归去。
    不管是男的女的，来自乡村还是城市，
    千万人是一种感情，一种心思；
    不管是白种人黑种人，来自莱茵河还是尼罗河，
    都在内心里祷念着：万岁！伟大的导师。
    我这东方的游人也满怀心事，
    这心事不是悲哀或者忧郁；
    而是为祖国实现了列宁的理想，
    感到无比的兴奋、光荣和幸福。
    还走在行列里我就许下了心愿：
    为人民我应当多做些，多做些工作。
    在列宁不朽的事业里
    作一个最小的螺丝钉也不算白活。
        1956年10月24日夜于莫斯科
