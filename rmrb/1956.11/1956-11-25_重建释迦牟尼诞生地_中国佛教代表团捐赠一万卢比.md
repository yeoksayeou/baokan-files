Title: Rebuilding the birthplace of Sakyamuni: Chinese Buddhist Delegation Donates 10,000 Rupees
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 4

    Rebuilding the birthplace of Sakyamuni
    Chinese Buddhist Delegation Donates 10,000 Rupees
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi dispatch: Kathmandu news: The Chinese Buddhist delegation attending the Fourth World Buddhist Conference donated 10,000 Rupees on November 20th to be used for the reconstruction of Lumbini, the birthplace of Sakyamuni. The Soviet Union also donated 3,300 Rupees.
    The Chinese Buddhist delegation also donated 2,000 Rupees for the reconstruction of the Hiroshima Pagoda. Prior to this, Japan had passed a resolution in a Diet session recommending the reconstruction of the Hiroshima Pagoda.

### 重建释迦牟尼诞生地  中国佛教代表团捐赠一万卢比

1956-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    重建释迦牟尼诞生地
    中国佛教代表团捐赠一万卢比
    新华社新德里讯　加德满都消息：参加第四届世界佛教徒大会的中国佛教代表团11月20日捐助一万卢比作为重建释迦牟尼诞生地兰毗尼之用。苏联也捐助了三千三百卢比。
    中国佛教代表团还捐赠两千卢比作为重建广岛大塔之用。在这之前，日本在一次国会会议上曾经通过了建议重建广岛大塔的决议。
