Title: Nehru Again Condemns Military Treaties for Creating War Disasters, Believes Baghdad Pact is the Root of Middle East Disputes
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 12th – Indian Prime Minister Nehru said on November 11th, "The only way for countries to get along well, the only way to handle international relations, is the method we call Panchsheel, or the ten principles adopted at Bandung (Conference)."

Speaking at the closing session of the three-day National Committee of the Congress Party in Calcutta, Nehru once again denounced military treaties. He said: "I don't know how much life is left in the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization and the Baghdad Pact. I think they are already dead."

Nehru continued that, whether in Europe or Asia, these treaty systems do not lead to security, peace, and cooperation between countries, but rather incite emotions of war and violence.

Nehru cited Egypt as an example: "Most of the disputes in West Asia occurred when the Baghdad Pact was formed. This treaty divided Arab countries into two groups." He said: "From then on, other things followed one after another, step by step, and the current events are all consequences of the Baghdad Pact."

Nehru said: "I hope it will be universally recognized that small countries should also be allowed to decide their own destiny just like big countries. And powerful countries will not interfere in small countries. They can help small countries, but they must not suppress and coerce small countries with armed forces or other methods."

Nehru hoped that the spirit of the Bandung Conference would fill the Colombo Countries Conference held on November 12th, and that this spirit would spread to and influence other countries in the world.

### 尼赫鲁再斥军事条约制造战祸  认为巴格达条约是中东纠纷的根源

1956-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁再斥军事条约制造战祸
    认为巴格达条约是中东纠纷的根源
    新华社新德里12日电　印度总理尼赫鲁11月11日说，“各国相处得好的唯一办法，在处理国际关系上应当采取的唯一办法，是我们叫作潘查希拉的办法，或者是在万隆（会议）采取的十项原则”。
    尼赫鲁在加尔各答举行了三天的国大党全国委员会的闭幕会议上讲话时再一次指责军事条约。他说：“我不知道东南亚条约和巴格达条约还有多少生气存在。我认为他们是已经死亡的了。”
    尼赫鲁接着说，无论在欧洲或亚洲，这些条约体系并不是引向安全、和平和各国之间的合作，而是激起战争和暴力的情绪。
    尼赫鲁举埃及为例说：“在西亚的这个纠纷大部分是从巴格达条约形成的时候发生的。这个条约把阿拉伯国家分成两个集团。”他说：“从此其他事情接着来了，一步跟着一步，目前发生的事都是随着巴格达条约而产生的结果。”
    尼赫鲁说：“我希望普遍认识到，应该让小国也像大国一样去决定它们自己的命运。而强大的国家将不会干涉小国。他们可以帮助小国，但是，他们一定不能用武装力量或其他方法来压制和强迫小国。”
    尼赫鲁希望万隆会议精神将会充满在11月12日举行的科伦坡国家会议，而这一精神将会传布到并且影响到世界其他国家。
