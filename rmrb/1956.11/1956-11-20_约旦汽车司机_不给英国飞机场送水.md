Title: Jordanian Car Drivers Refuse to Deliver Water to British Airfields

Author:

Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 6

    Jordanian Car Drivers
    Refuse to Deliver Water to British Airfields
    Xinhua News Agency, November 18th: According to the Jordanian Automobile Drivers Union's decision to boycott Britain, Jordanian car drivers are refusing to deliver water to British airfields in Jordan.

### 约旦汽车司机  不给英国飞机场送水

1956-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦汽车司机
    不给英国飞机场送水
    据新华社18日讯　根据约旦汽车司机工会关于抵制英国的决定，约旦汽车司机们拒绝把水送给在约旦的英国飞机场。
