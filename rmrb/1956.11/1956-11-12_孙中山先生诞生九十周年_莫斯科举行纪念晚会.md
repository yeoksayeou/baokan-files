Title: Moscow Holds Commemorative Soiree for the 90th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th. The Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge and the Institute of Sinology of the Soviet Academy of Sciences jointly held a soiree in the Central Lecture Hall on November 10th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of the great Chinese democratic revolutionary Sun Yat-sen.

　　Among those attending the soiree were Soviet cultural workers, workers, and university students. Staff members of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union also attended the soiree.

　　Academician M.B. Mitin of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, in presiding over the opening of the soiree, said that Sun Yat-sen was the great son of the Chinese people. His name is precious to all Eastern peoples who have shaken off the shackles of colonialism and to all peoples who are fighting against colonialism.

　　After recounting the role played by Sun Yat-sen in the Chinese working people's struggle for liberation, Academician Mitin said that the great Russian October Socialist Revolution, which Sun Yat-sen regarded as humanity's great hope, had a great influence on him. Sun Yat-sen was a great and unwavering friend of the Soviet people. He always emphasized that the Chinese working people must form an alliance with the world's first worker-peasant state, the Soviet Union. Now, Sun Yat-sen's ideals and goals have become a reality. The friendship between China and the Soviet Union has become a pillar of peace and democracy in the Far East and the world.

　　Subsequently, Xie Ning, Deputy Doctor of Philosophy, and Vinogradov, Deputy Doctor of History, respectively gave reports on "The Great Chinese Democratic Revolutionary Sun Yat-sen" and "Lenin on Sun Yat-sen." A. Klimov, Deputy Doctor of History, recounted his memories of Sun Yat-sen at the meeting.

　　A performance was held at the end of the soiree.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th. Soviet newspapers today published articles commemorating the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

　　"Pravda" published a photograph of Sun Yat-sen and an article by Gudev entitled "The Great Son of the Chinese People." In this article, the author detailed Sun Yat-sen's life and career.

　　"Izvestia" and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" also published photographs of Sun Yat-sen and published commemorative articles entitled "The Great Son of the Chinese People" and "The Great Petrel of the Chinese Revolution," respectively.

### 孙中山先生诞生九十周年  莫斯科举行纪念晚会

1956-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    孙中山先生诞生九十周年
    莫斯科举行纪念晚会
    据新华社莫斯科11日电　苏联政治与科学知识普及协会和苏联科学院汉学研究所11月10日在中央讲堂联合举办晚会，纪念伟大的中国民主主义革命家孙中山诞辰九十周年。
    出席这次晚会的，有苏联文化工作者、工人和大学青年。中国驻苏联大使馆的工作人员也出席了晚会。
    苏联科学院院士马·鲍·米丁在主持晚会开幕的时候讲话说，孙中山是中国人民伟大的儿子。他的名字，对所有摆脱了殖民主义束缚的东方人民和一切为反对殖民主义而斗争的人民都是宝贵的。
    米丁院士讲述了孙中山在中国劳动人民争取解放的斗争中所起的作用以后说，被孙中山认为人类伟大的希望的俄国伟大十月社会主义革命对他起了很大的影响。孙中山是苏联人民的忠实到底的伟大朋友。他始终强调：中国劳动人民必须同世界上第一个工农国家苏联结成同盟。现在，孙中山的理想和奋斗目标已经成为现实了。中国同苏联的友谊已成为远东和世界和平和民主的支柱。
    接着，由哲学副博士谢宁和历史学副博士维诺格拉多夫分别作了“伟大的中国民主主义革命家孙中山”和“列宁论孙中山”的报告。历史学副博士阿·克里莫夫在会上叙述了他对孙中山的回忆。
    晚会在最后举行了演出。
    据新华社莫斯科11日电　苏联各报今天载文纪念孙中山诞辰九十周年。
    “真理报”登载了孙中山的照片和顾德夫的文章“中国人民伟大的儿子”。作者在这篇文章中详细地介绍了孙中山的生平和事业。
    “消息报”和“共青团真理报”也分别登载了孙中山的照片和发表了纪念文章“中国人民的伟大的儿子”和“中国革命伟大的海燕”。
