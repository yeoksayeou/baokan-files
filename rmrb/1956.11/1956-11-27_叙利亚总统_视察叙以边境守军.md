Title: Syrian President Inspects Troops on the Syrian-Israeli Border
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 26th. Syrian President Shukri al-Quwatli, accompanied by General Tawfiq Nizam al-Din, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, inspected troops stationed on the Syrian-Israeli border on November 25th. Prior to this, the President also inspected the military training camps for men and women of the People's Resistance Organization.

    A Syrian official said they were very satisfied with the high morale of the troops. These soldiers demonstrated their strong will to defend the country's territorial integrity and oppose any conspiracies by imperialists.

    According to a report from International News Service in Jerusalem on the 24th, Turkish troops are mobilizing along the Syrian border.

### 叙利亚总统  视察叙以边境守军

1956-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总统
    视察叙以边境守军
    新华社26日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总统库阿特利在武装部队总司令图菲克·尼扎姆丁将军陪同下，11月25日视察了驻在叙利亚—以色列边境上的军队。在这以前，总统还视察了人民抵抗组织的男女军事训练营。
    一位叙利亚官方人士说，他们对于军队高涨的士气感到非常满意。这些军人显示出他们对保卫国家领土完整和反对帝国主义者的任何阴谋的坚强意志。
    另据国际新闻社耶路撒冷24日消息说，土耳其军队正在沿着叙利亚的边境调动。
