Title: Anglo-French Aggressor Troops Continue Provocations in Egypt, Israeli Foreign Minister Declares Intention to Seize the Gaza Strip
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: TASS, Cairo, November 11th: According to the Egyptian Armed Forces General Command, on November 10th, an enemy jeep approached Egyptian positions. Soldiers in the jeep opened fire on Egyptian sentries, who immediately returned fire. The exchange resulted in the death of two enemy soldiers.
The report states that on the evening of November 9th, two enemy aircraft conducted reconnaissance over Egyptian military positions in the Ismailia district. After the Egyptian Armed Forces opened fire, both enemy aircraft were shot down.

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Jerusalem dispatch: Israeli Foreign Minister Golda Meir stated on November 10th that Israel intends to occupy the Gaza Strip, Egyptian territory, for a long period.
Speaking at a gathering of the majority party, the Israeli Labor Party, she described the Gaza Strip as "an inseparable part of Israel."
The Israeli government has stated that it is prepared to withdraw its troops from Egyptian territory after the arrival of United Nations forces. Meir's speech is inconsistent with the aforementioned promises of the Israeli government.

### 英法侵略军在埃及继续挑衅  以色列外长扬言要霸占加沙地带

1956-11-12
第7版()
专栏：

    英法侵略军在埃及继续挑衅
    以色列外长扬言要霸占加沙地带
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社开罗11日讯：据埃及武装部队总司令部消息，11月10日，敌军吉普车一辆驰近埃及阵地，车上士兵向埃及哨兵开火，埃及哨兵立即回击。双方互相射击的结果，敌军死二人。
    消息说，11月9日晚间敌军飞机两架在伊士美利亚区埃军阵地上空进行侦察。埃及武装部队开火后，两架敌机都被击落。
    据新华社11日讯　耶路撒冷消息：以色列外交部长果尔达·梅厄夫人在11月10日表示，以色列打算长期占有埃及的领土加沙地带。
    她在多数党以色列工党的一次集会上演说时，把加沙地带说成是“以色列不可分割的一部分”。
    以色列政府曾经表示它准备在联合国部队到达后撤回在埃及境内的军队。梅厄夫人的演说同上述以色列政府的诺言是不一致的。
