Title: Israeli Prime Minister Publicly States Intention to Seize the Sinai Peninsula and Opposes UN Emergency Force Deployment
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th dispatch from Israeli-occupied Jerusalem: Following Israel's notification to the United Nations on November 5th of its agreement to a ceasefire in Egypt, Israeli Prime Minister and Minister of Defense David Ben-Gurion stated on November 7th that Israel intends to permanently occupy the Egyptian Sinai Peninsula and the Gaza Strip, which it recently invaded.

He told the Israeli Parliament: "The (1949) Armistice Agreement signed with Egypt is dead and buried and cannot be resurrected, therefore the ceasefire line between Israel and Egypt is no longer valid."

He implied that Israel does not recognize the 60,000 square kilometer Sinai Peninsula as an indivisible part of Egyptian territory. He said: "Our army has not invaded Egyptian territory, nor have they attempted to do so."

This was Ben-Gurion's first statement to Parliament since Israel launched its armed aggression against Egypt. Ben-Gurion also threatened to launch further acts of aggression against Egypt. He said he hoped that Israel would not be forced to "return to Egypt" in the future.

Ben-Gurion also stated that Israel opposes the deployment of the United Nations Emergency Force to carry out its mission. He said: "We will never agree to a foreign army stationed along the Israeli border or stationed in areas occupied by Israel."

### 以色列总理公然说要霸占西奈半岛  并反对联合国紧急部队去执行任务

1956-11-09
第3版()
专栏：

    以色列总理公然说要霸占西奈半岛
    并反对联合国紧急部队去执行任务
    新华社8日讯　以色列区耶路撒冷消息：以色列在11月5日通知联合国同意在埃及停火后，以色列总理兼国防部长本—古里安11月7日表示，以色列企图永久霸占它最近侵占的埃及的西奈半岛和加沙地带。
    他向以色列议会说：“（1949年）同埃及签订的停战协定已经死亡和埋葬而不能复活，因此以色列和埃及之间的停战线不再有效。”
    他暗示以色列不承认六万方公里的西奈半岛是埃及领土的不可分割的部分。他说：“我们的军队没有侵犯埃及的领土，他们也没有企图那样做。”
    这是以色列发动对埃及的武装侵略以后，本—古里安第一次在议会发表声明。本—古里安还威胁要对埃及发动进一步的侵略行动。他说，他希望以色列在将来不至于被迫要“回到埃及”。
    本—古里安还表示以色列反对联合国紧急部队去执行任务。他说：“我们决不同意一支外国军队沿着以色列边境驻扎，或者驻扎在以色列占领的地区内。”
