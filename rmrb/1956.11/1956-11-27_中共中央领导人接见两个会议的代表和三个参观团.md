Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CPC Receive Representatives of Two Conferences and Three Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1956-11-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th.

    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, received this morning the representatives attending the National Congress of Basic-Level Trade Union Cadres of Joint State-Private Enterprises and the representatives attending the First National Congress of the China Light Industry and Food Industry Trade Union. They also received all members of the Jilin Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Group, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Judicial Cadres Visiting Group, and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Forestry Visiting Group.
    During the reception, Musa Yefu, the head of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Judicial Cadres Visiting Group, presented Chairman Mao with a Uygur hat.

### 中共中央领导人接见两个会议的代表和三个参观团

1956-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见两个会议的代表和三个参观团
    新华社26日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，副主席朱德、陈云，总书记邓小平，在今天上午接见了出席全国公私合营工会基层干部大会的代表和出席中国轻工业、食品工业工会第一次全国代表大会的代表，同时还接见了吉林省各民族参观团、新疆维吾尔自治区司法干部参观团和新疆维吾尔自治区林业参观团的全体人员。
    接见的时候，新疆维吾尔自治区司法干部参观团团长木沙也夫把一顶维吾尔族帽子献给了毛主席。
