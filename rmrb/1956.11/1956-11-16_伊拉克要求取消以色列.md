Title: Iraq Demands the Annulment of Israel
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 15th, Baghdad dispatch: An Iraqi government memorandum published on November 13th stated: "The Iraqi government is fully convinced that eradicating Israel is the only practical way to maintain peace and order in the Middle East. Otherwise, Israel will continue to pursue its policy of aggression."
　　The Iraqi government has sent this memorandum to the foreign diplomatic representatives in Iraq and to the Arab heads of state who are meeting in Beirut. The Iraqi representative to the United Nations has been ordered to bring this matter before the United Nations General Assembly.

### 伊拉克要求取消以色列

1956-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克要求取消以色列
    新华社15日讯　巴格达消息：伊拉克政府11月13日发表的一个备忘录说：“伊拉克政府完全相信，拔除以色列是维护中东和平和秩序的唯一切实可行的办法。不然，以色列将继续推行它的侵略政策”。
    伊拉克政府已经把这份备忘录送给各国驻伊拉克的外交代表和正在贝鲁特举行会议的阿拉伯首脑们。在联合国的伊拉克代表已经奉命把这个问题提到联合国大会。
