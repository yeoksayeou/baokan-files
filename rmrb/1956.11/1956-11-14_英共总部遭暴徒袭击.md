Title: British Communist Party Headquarters Attacked by Rioters
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 13th: A group of rioters, using the pretext of the failure of the counter-revolutionary elements' riot in Hungary, attacked the London headquarters of the British Communist Party on the evening of November 11th, smashing the windows of the building.
    On the same day, reactionaries also held demonstrations near the "Daily Worker" newspaper office and threatened to burn down the "Daily Worker."

### 英共总部遭暴徒袭击

1956-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    英共总部遭暴徒袭击
    据新华社13日讯　伦敦消息：一批暴徒借口匈牙利反革命分子制造的暴乱遭到失败，在11月11日晚间袭击了伦敦英国共产党总部，打破了这座建筑物的窗子。
    同一天，反动分子还在“工人日报”报馆附近举行了示威，并且恫吓说要烧掉“工人日报”。
