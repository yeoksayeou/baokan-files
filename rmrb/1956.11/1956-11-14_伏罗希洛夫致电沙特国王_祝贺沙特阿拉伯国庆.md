Title: Voroshilov Sends Telegram to Saudi King Congratulating Saudi Arabia on National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to King Saud ibn Abd al-Aziz Al Saud of Saudi Arabia, congratulating Saudi Arabia on its National Day, wishing the Saudi Arabian people prosperity and achievement, and wishing further development of the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Saudi Arabia. He also expressed his belief that, at this critical moment when colonial countries have launched a military aggression against Egypt, the Arab people and other peace-loving people in the world will surely do their utmost to stop the aggression and restore peace and security in the Middle East.

### 伏罗希洛夫致电沙特国王  祝贺沙特阿拉伯国庆

1956-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫致电沙特国王
    祝贺沙特阿拉伯国庆
    据新华社13日讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫打电报给沙特阿拉伯国王沙特·伊本·阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·费萨尔·沙特，祝贺沙特阿拉伯国庆节，祝沙特阿拉伯人民繁荣和获得成就，并且祝苏联和沙特阿拉伯的友好关系获得进一步发展。他还表示相信，在殖民国家对埃及发动军事侵略的严重的关头，阿拉伯人民和世界上其他爱好和平的人民必定会竭尽全力来制止侵略和恢复中东的和平和安全。
