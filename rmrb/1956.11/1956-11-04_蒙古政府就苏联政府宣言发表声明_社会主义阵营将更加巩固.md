Title: Mongolian Government Issues Statement on Soviet Government Declaration; Socialist Camp Will Be Further Consolidated
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 3rd: The Government of the Mongolian People's Republic issued a statement on November 2nd regarding the Soviet government's declaration of October 30th, stating that the Mongolian government believes the Soviet government's declaration is of extreme importance for further developing and consolidating cooperation among socialist countries, and for establishing unwavering unity based on common goals and common interests.

The statement said that some errors that existed in the past in the relations between individual socialist countries are being gradually eliminated, which will make the great socialist camp even more consolidated.

The statement said that Mongolia and the Soviet Union have thirty-five years of friendship, which has been developed and consolidated under the principles of complete respect for national sovereignty, mutual assistance, and mutual cooperation. In Mongolia's construction, the Soviet Union has provided and is providing invaluable assistance.

The statement said that the Mongolian government, on behalf of its people, resolutely condemns the attempts by international reactionary forces to weaken the friendship between socialist countries and to undermine the foundations of the people's democratic system in some people's democratic countries. The Government of the Mongolian People's Republic is convinced that the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between countries of the socialist camp will continue to strengthen and develop in order to achieve our common goals and to consolidate peace on the principles of peaceful coexistence among all nations.

### 蒙古政府就苏联政府宣言发表声明  社会主义阵营将更加巩固

1956-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府就苏联政府宣言发表声明
    社会主义阵营将更加巩固
    新华社乌兰巴托3日电　蒙古人民共和国政府11月2日就苏联政府10月30日的宣言发表声明说，蒙古政府认为苏联政府的宣言对进一步发展和巩固社会主义各国间的合作，对建立在共同目标和共同利益上的不可动摇的一致性具有极为重要的意义。
    声明说，过去在个别社会主义国家间相互关系中存在的某些错误正在逐步消除，这将使伟大的社会主义阵营更加巩固。
    声明说，蒙古和苏联有着三十五年友谊，这种友谊是在完全尊重国家主权、互相帮助和互相合作的原则下发展和巩固起来的。在蒙古的建设中，苏联给予了并且正在给予不可估价的帮助。
    声明说，蒙古政府代表自己的人民坚决谴责国际反动势力企图削弱社会主义国家间的友谊和动摇某些人民民主国家人民民主制度基础的行为。蒙古人民共和国政府坚信，社会主义阵营国家之间的牢不可破的友谊和合作将不断加强和发展，以便达到我们的共同目标、以便在各国人民和平共处的原则上巩固和平。
