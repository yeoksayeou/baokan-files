Title: Hanoi Holds Mass Rally to Welcome Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 19th: One hundred thousand citizens of Hanoi held a grand mass rally this morning at the famous Ba Dinh Square to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.

On the square, decorated with banners reading "Welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Vietnam" and "Long Live the Unbreakable Friendship Between Vietnam and China," gathered representatives of workers, suburban farmers, students, government employees, ethnic minorities, and other people from all walks of life in Hanoi. In addition, there were more than 3,000 overseas Chinese who had come from various provinces to participate.

At precisely 9:00 AM, when Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Pham Van Dong appeared on the rostrum, the crowd enthusiastically cheered them, waving Vietnamese and Chinese national flags and flowers. Accompanying them on the rostrum from the Vietnamese side were: Deputy Prime Ministers Phan Ke Toai and Vo Nguyen Giap, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, Vietnamese Ambassador to China Hoang Van Hoan, and Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee Tran Duy Hung. From the Chinese side were: Deputy Premier He Long, Qiao Guanhua, Zhang Yan, Gong Peng, and Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo.

Tran Duy Hung first welcomed Zhou Enlai on behalf of the people of the Vietnamese capital and asked him to convey the respect of the people of Hanoi to the people of Beijing, the Chinese people, and Chairman Mao Zedong.

Subsequently, Premier Pham Van Dong and Premier Zhou Enlai spoke at the meeting one after another. (For the full text of the speeches, see page two.)

When Premier Zhou Enlai finished speaking, the crowd in the square cheered him enthusiastically. Premier Zhou Enlai finally walked to the right side of the rostrum and waved to the foreign diplomatic envoys and Chinese experts standing below the rostrum.

The rally ended at 9:45 AM, and the masses participating in the rally shouted the slogan "Long Live Vietnamese-Chinese Friendship" and gradually left along the tree-lined boulevards.

### 河内举行群众大会欢迎周恩来总理

1956-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    河内举行群众大会欢迎周恩来总理
    新华社河内19日电　河内十万市民今天早晨在著名的巴亭广场上举行盛大的群众大会欢迎周恩来总理。
    在装饰有“欢迎周恩来总理访问越南”、“越中牢不可破的友谊万岁”横幅标语的广场上，聚集有河内工人、郊区农民、学生、机关职员、少数民族和其他各界人民代表。此外还有从各省赶来参加的华侨三千多人。
    九时正，当周恩来总理和范文同总理出现在主席台上的时候，群众摇着越中两国国旗和鲜花向他们热烈欢呼。随同登上主席台的，越南方面有：副总理潘继遂、武元甲，外交部副部长雍文谦，越南驻中国大使黄文欢和河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴。中国方面有：贺龙副总理、乔冠华、张彦、龚澎和中国驻越南大使罗贵波。
    陈维兴首先代表越南首都人民欢迎周恩来，并且请他把河内人民的敬意带给北京人民、中国人民和毛泽东主席。
    随后，范文同总理和周恩来总理相继在会上讲了话。（讲话全文见第二版）
    周恩来总理讲话结束的时候，广场上的群众向他热烈欢呼。周恩来总理最后走到主席台的右边，向站在主席台下面的各国外交使节和中国专家招手致意。
    大会在九时四十五分结束后，参加大会的群众高呼着“越中友谊万岁”的口号，沿着林荫大道陆续离去。
