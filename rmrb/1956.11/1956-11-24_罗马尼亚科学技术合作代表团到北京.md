Title: Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Anton Constantinescu, Deputy Minister of Chemical Industry and Chairman of the Romanian Group of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 22nd to attend the fourth meeting of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Zhou Wenlong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee and Vice Minister of Petroleum Industry, Zhang Qinqiu, member and Vice Minister of Textile Industry, and others. The Romanian Ambassador to China, Chioroiu, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 罗马尼亚科学技术合作代表团到北京

1956-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚科学技术合作代表团到北京
    新华社23日讯　前来我国参加中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第四届会议的罗马尼亚科学技术合作代表团，在代表团团长、中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会罗马尼亚组主席、化学工业部副部长安东·康斯坦丁聂斯库率领下已经在22日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、石油工业部副部长周文龙，委员、纺织工业部副部长张琴秋等多人。罗马尼亚驻华大使乔洛尤也前往机场迎接。
