Title: Congratulations from Foreign Friends
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 4
    
    Former President of Mexico's Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: Former President of Mexico, Lázaro Cárdenas, sent a telegram congratulating the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of Sun Yat-sen's Birthday:
    On the occasion of the ninetieth anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth, I would like to join everyone in expressing our respect for his contributions to the implementation of social reforms for the people.
        Lázaro Cárdenas
    President of the Belgian Red Cross's Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: The President of the Belgian Red Cross, Frédéric de Mérode, sent a telegram to the Red Cross Society of China, congratulating the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the Red Cross Society of China, Beijing
    The Belgian Red Cross sincerely pays its respects to the great pioneer Sun Yat-sen.
    Frédéric de Mérode
    Representative of International Red Cross Institutions' Congratulatory Letter
    Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: Mr. Durand, the representative of the International Committee of the Red Cross in Hanoi, who was invited to attend the commemorative meeting for the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth, wrote a letter to the Red Cross Society of China today, congratulating the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth on behalf of some international Red Cross institutions.
    Durand said in his letter: "The Red Cross Society of China kindly invited representatives from the Standing Committee of the International Red Cross Conference, the League of Red Cross Societies, and the International Committee of the Red Cross to attend this commemorative meeting. In order to thank you for this invitation, the International Committee has entrusted me to represent these international Red Cross institutions in expressing their congratulations and best wishes on the occasion of the ninetieth anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth."
    Durand also expressed his honor and pleasure in being able to attend the commemorative meeting for the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth in his letter.
    Exhibitions and Lectures Held in Various Parts of the Soviet Union to Commemorate Sun Yat-sen
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 14th: The ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth is widely commemorated throughout the Soviet Union.
    An exhibition of pictures about Sun Yat-sen has recently been held in the Lenin State Library in Moscow. The exhibition features Russian translations of Sun Yat-sen's works and collections of speeches. A photograph of Sun Yat-sen on display was printed in Moscow in 1925. Below the photo are moving words from Sun Yat-sen's will to the Central Executive Committee of Soviet Russia before his death.
    The State Museum of Oriental Cultures in Moscow also held an exhibition on Sun Yat-sen.
    The Kharkov Scientific Library displayed Lenin's and Mao Zedong's evaluations of Sun Yat-sen, as well as Sun Yat-sen's letters to the Soviet Russian Central Committee and his comrades, at a book exhibition commemorating the ninetieth anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. Part of the exhibition material illustrates the realization of Sun Yat-sen's ideals in present-day China, as well as the achievements of socialist construction and the friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.
    In recent days, members of the Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge have given lectures on Sun Yat-sen's life and his revolutionary path in many cultural palaces and clubs.
    Soviet newspapers such as "Izvestia," "Red Star," "Soviet Culture," "Labor News," and "Literary Gazette" have universally published articles commemorating the ninetieth anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

### 国外友人的祝贺

1956-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    国外友人的祝贺
    墨西哥前总统的贺电
    新华社15日讯　墨西哥前总统拉萨罗·卡德纳斯来电祝贺孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。电文如下：
    孙中山诞辰纪念筹备委员会主任：
    值此孙逸仙博士诞辰九十周年的机会，我谨和大家一起，对于他为人民实行社会改革的功绩，表示尊崇。
        拉萨罗·卡德纳斯
    比利时红十字会会长的贺电
    新华社15日讯　比利时红十字会会长弗雷德里克·德梅罗德致电中国红十字会总会祝贺孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念。电文如下：
    北京中国红十字会总会
    比利时红十字会诚挚地向伟大先行者孙逸仙表示敬意。
    弗雷德里克·德梅罗德红十字会国际机构的代表的祝贺信
    新华社15日讯　应邀参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念大会的红十字会国际委员会河内代表杜朗，今天写信给中国红十字会总会，代表红十字会的一些国际机构，对孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念表示祝贺。
    杜朗在信中说：“中国红十字会总会惠然邀请了国际红十字大会常设委员会、红十字会协会和红十字会国际委员会的代表们参加这次纪念会。为了答谢这个邀请，国际委员会委托我代表这些红十字会国际机构，以它们的名义在孙逸仙博士诞生九十周年之际表达它们的庆贺和祝愿。”
    杜朗在他的信中还对他能够参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念大会表示荣幸和高兴。
    苏联各地纪念孙中山举办展览会讲演会
    新华社莫斯科14日电　苏联各地广泛纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。
    在莫斯科列宁国家图书馆里，最近举行了有关孙中山的图片展览会。展览会上展出了译成俄文的孙中山的著作和演说集。展出的一幅孙中山相片还是1925年在莫斯科印制的。相片下面是孙中山逝世前给苏俄中央执行委员会的遗书中的动人的话。
    莫斯科的国立东方文化博物馆也举行了有关孙中山的展览会。
    哈尔科夫科学图书馆在纪念孙中山九十周年诞辰举办的图书展览会上，展出了列宁和毛泽东对于孙中山的评价以及孙中山给苏俄中央和战友们的信件。展览会的一部分材料说明孙中山的理想在现在的中国的实现，也说明了社会主义建设的成就和中苏两国人民的友好合作。
    这几天来，苏联政治与科学知识普及协会会员们分别在许多文化宫和俱乐部，作关于孙中山的生平和他的革命道路的讲演。
    苏联的报纸如“消息报”、“红星报”、“苏维埃文化报”、“劳动报”、“文学报”等，普遍刊载了纪念孙中山诞辰九十周年的文章。
