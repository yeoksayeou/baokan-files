Title: Bumper Harvests of Chinese Medicinal Cornelian Cherries and Atractylodes Macrocephala
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 20th. The Jiande Special District of Zhejiang Province has achieved bumper harvests of two Chinese medicinal materials, cornelian cherries and Atractylodes Macrocephala. It is estimated that the output of cornelian cherries is about 100,000 jin, an increase of 70,000 jin over last year, and the output of Atractylodes Macrocephala is about 45,000 jin, an increase of 25,000 jin over last year.
　　Cornelian cherries and Atractylodes Macrocephala are precious Chinese medicines and tonics. Cornelian cherries can also be used as delicacies at banquets, and at present, Atractylodes Macrocephala is only produced in Yuqian County in Jiande Special District.

### 中药萸肉和于术丰收

1956-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    中药萸肉和于术丰收
    新华社杭州20日电　浙江建德专区的萸肉和于术两种中药丰收。据估计，萸肉产量约十万斤，比去年增产七万斤，于术产量约四万五千斤，比去年增产两万五千斤。
    萸肉、于术是名贵中药，又是补品。萸肉还可以用作筵席上的珍品，于术目前全国还只有建德专区的于潜县出产。
