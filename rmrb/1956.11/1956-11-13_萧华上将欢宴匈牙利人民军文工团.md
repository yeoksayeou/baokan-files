Title: General Xiao Hua Hosts a Banquet for the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, which arrived in Beijing after visiting and performing in various parts of China.

Attending the banquet were Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Lieutenant General Wang Huaiqiu, Director of the Political Department of the Air Force, heads of various departments of the General Political Department, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and representatives of literary and artistic workers from the capital and troops stationed in Beijing.

Gao Huade, Chargé d'Affaires of the Hungarian People's Republic in China, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

General Xiao Hua said at the banquet: We treat you as brothers, partners, and comrades-in-arms in the socialist family. Your artistic performances and visits have left a very deep and good impression on the people and the army in various places, especially when your motherland was in distress, you still insisted on performing for us, which moved us deeply. In his speech, General Xiao Hua emphatically mentioned that recently, under the leadership of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, the Hungarian working people quelled the painful rebellion, severely hitting imperialism, and warmly congratulated the Hungarian working people and all patriots for this.

Colonel Szokolai István, leader of the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, repeatedly thanked the Chinese people for the warm reception and profound friendship extended to the ensemble during its visit to China. On behalf of all members of the ensemble, he warmly congratulated the Chinese workers, peasants, and intellectuals who are building socialism, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army who are defending socialist construction, for their achievements.

The Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble arrived in Beijing by train this morning, where they were warmly welcomed by more than 1,500 literary and artistic workers from the capital and troops stationed in Beijing at the station. Before arriving in Beijing, the ensemble had visited and performed in Shenyang, Anshan, Lushun-Dalian, Tianjin, Jinan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other cities in China.

### 萧华上将欢宴匈牙利人民军文工团

1956-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

    萧华上将欢宴匈牙利人民军文工团
    据新华社12日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎在我国各地访问演出后来到北京的匈牙利人民军文工团。
    陪同出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，空军政治部主任王辉球空军中将，总政治部各部门负责人和中华人民共和国文化部副部长张致祥，北京市副市长冯基平，以及首都和驻京部队文艺工作者的代表多人。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻中国代办高华德及使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    萧华上将在宴会上讲话说：我们是把你们当作兄弟、伙伴和社会主义大家庭里的战友来接待的。你们的艺术表演和访问，给各地人民和军队留下了极深、极好的印象，特别是当你们祖国遭受危难的情况下，仍坚持为我们演出，使我们深为感动。萧华上将在讲话中着重提到，最近匈牙利劳动人民在工农革命政府领导之下，平息了令人痛心的叛乱，严重地打击了帝国主义，为此向匈牙利劳动人民和全体爱国者热烈祝贺。
    匈牙利人民军文工团领队索柯洛伊·衣施多万上校在宴会上讲话中，一再感谢中国人民在文工团访华期间所给予的热情接待和深厚友谊。他代表文工团全体同志，热烈祝贺正在建设社会主义的中国工人、农民、知识分子和正在保卫社会主义建设的中国人民解放军所取得的成就。
    匈牙利人民军文工团是今天上午乘火车来到北京的，他们在车站上受到首都和驻京部队文艺工作者一千五百多人的热烈欢迎。文工团在到达北京之前，已在我国沈阳、鞍山、旅大、天津、济南、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉等城市作了访问演出。
