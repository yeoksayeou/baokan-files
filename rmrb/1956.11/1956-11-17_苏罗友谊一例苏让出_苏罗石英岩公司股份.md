Title: An Example of Soviet-Romanian Friendship: The Soviet Union Transfers Shares in the Soviet-Romanian Quartzite Company
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, November 14th: TASS reports that the Soviet government has decided to transfer the Soviet shares in the "Soviet-Romanian Quartzite" company to the Romanian government. An agreement on the transfer was signed by the two governments in Bucharest on October 22nd.

    The agreement stipulates that the Soviet shares in the aforementioned company transferred to Romania will be compensated by Romania on preferential terms. The payment will be made in installments over several years.

### 苏罗友谊一例苏让出  苏罗石英岩公司股份

1956-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    苏罗友谊一例苏让出
    苏罗石英岩公司股份
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社布加勒斯特14日讯：苏联政府决定把“苏罗石英岩”公司中的苏联股份移交给罗马尼亚政府，有关移交的协定，两国政府已经于10月22日在布加勒斯特签字。
    协定规定，移交给罗马尼亚的上述公司中的苏联股份，将按照优惠条件由罗马尼亚方面给以补偿。款项将分数年偿付。
