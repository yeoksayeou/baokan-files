Title: Colombo Powers to Meet in New Delhi Next Week to Discuss Anglo-French-Israeli Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 6th: According to the Press Trust of India, the Colombo Powers conference will be held in New Delhi on November 12th to discuss the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt.

Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike are expected to arrive in New Delhi on November 11th for the meeting. Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe is already in India and is currently attending the Asian Socialist Conference in Bombay. No response has yet been received from Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy, who is traveling to Tehran today to attend a meeting of the four Muslim nations of the Baghdad Pact.

This Colombo Powers conference was proposed by Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo.

### 讨论英法以侵略埃及问题  科伦坡国家下周在新德里开会

1956-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    讨论英法以侵略埃及问题
    科伦坡国家下周在新德里开会
    新华社新德里6日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，科伦坡国家会议将在11月12日在新德里开会，讨论英国、法国和以色列侵略埃及的问题。
    印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约和锡兰总理班达拉奈克可望在11月11日到达新德里开会。缅甸总理吴巴瑞已经在印度，他目前正在孟买参加亚洲社会党会议。还没有接到巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底的答复，他今天前往德黑兰出席巴格达条约四个穆斯林国家的会议。
    这次科伦坡国家会议是印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约建议举行的。
