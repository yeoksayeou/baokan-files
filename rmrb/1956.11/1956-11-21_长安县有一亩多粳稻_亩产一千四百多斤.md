Title: More than one mu of Japonica rice in Chang'an County yields over 1,400 catties per mu
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 3

    Chang'an County has more than one mu of Japonica rice yielding over 1,400 catties per mu
    (Our newspaper's report) More than one mu of Japonica rice in the Youth Production Team of the No. 1 Jiulian Agricultural Cooperative in Chang'an County, Shaanxi Province, yielded an average of 1,424 catties and 12 taels per mu, breaking the highest unit area yield of rice in the province's history. In addition, another one mu and two li of Japonica rice in this production team yielded an average of 1,246 catties and 6 taels per mu.

### 长安县有一亩多粳稻  亩产一千四百多斤

1956-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    长安县有一亩多粳稻
    亩产一千四百多斤
    本报讯　陕西省长安县九联第一农业社青年生产队的一亩一分多粳稻，平均亩产一千四百二十四斤十二两，打破了全省历年来的水稻的最高单位面积产量。这个生产队另外还有一亩零二厘粳稻，平均亩产一千二百四十六斤六两。
