Title: Supporting the Hungarian People to Overcome Difficulties: Czechoslovak Government Decides to Donate 90 Million Crowns Worth of Supplies; First Batch of Supplies from Democratic Germany Arrives in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th - According to a broadcast from Prague Radio, Czechoslovak Premier Široký received Hungarian Ambassador Gábor on the 13th and informed him that:

Based on fraternal friendship and the hopes of the Czechoslovak people, the Czechoslovak government has decided to assist the Hungarian people with goods worth a total of 90 million crowns (Czech currency), to be managed by the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government. Orders have already been issued to expedite the delivery of these goods to the Hungarian People's Republic.

These supplies include 5,000 tons of flour, 5,000 tons of potatoes, 100 tons of meat, 5,000 tons of cement, 10,000 tons of lime, 325,000 square meters of roofing materials, 100,000 square meters of window glass, 2,000 cubic meters of round logs, 3,000 cubic meters of sawn timber, 3,600 tons of wood fuel, pharmaceuticals worth a total of 17.5 million crowns, and textiles, footwear, tableware, and other daily necessities worth a total of 32.4 million crowns.

In addition, the Czechoslovak government has instructed the Minister of Foreign Trade to ensure the timely fulfillment of obligations under the 1956 Czech-Hungarian trade agreement.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th - According to TASS, Berlin, 14th: Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic said in a telegram to Premier Kádár of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government that the government of the German Democratic Republic has decided to immediately provide the Hungarian working people with gratuitous assistance in the form of goods worth a total of 22 million marks.

According to the German News Agency, the first batch of aid to Hungary included 100,000 square meters of window glass, 5,000 tons of diesel engine fuel, 410,000 pairs of shoes, 10,000 blankets, 1,500 tons of pasta and canned goods.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 14th - According to a report in the "Neues Deutschland" on the 13th: The first batch of aid from the German Democratic Republic to Hungary arrived in Budapest on the 11th along with a delegation from the German Red Cross. Subsequent aid shipments will be dispatched successively in the coming days.

The broad working people of the German Democratic Republic have launched a surge of support for the fraternal Hungarian people. Since the establishment of the "Committee for Assisting Socialist Hungary" on November 10th, it has continuously received large amounts of cash and daily necessities from working people across the country. The State Broadcasting Committee alone has raised 2.7 million marks in cash. Miners from the Borna brown coal mine in the Leipzig district raised 30,000 marks to donate to their Hungarian worker brothers. Members of agricultural cooperatives and individual farmers have sent their agricultural products, livestock, and trucks. Workers and technicians from many factories have requested to go to Hungary to help repair factory machinery and equipment.

The "Committee for Assisting Socialist Hungary" has dispatched a delegation to Hungary to directly understand what the Hungarian people need most in restoring construction and establishing normal life, in order to provide more practical assistance to the Hungarian people.

### 支援匈牙利人民战胜困难  捷政府决定赠送九千万克朗的物资  民主德国第一批物资运到布达佩斯

1956-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    支援匈牙利人民战胜困难
    捷政府决定赠送九千万克朗的物资  民主德国第一批物资运到布达佩斯
    新华社布拉格14日电　据布拉格电台广播，捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基13日接见匈牙利大使加波尔，通知他说：
    捷克斯洛伐克政府根据兄弟之谊和捷克斯洛伐克人民的希望，决定用总值九千万克朗（捷币）的物资援助匈牙利人民，把这些物资交由匈牙利工农革命政府处理，并已下令把这些物资赶快运往匈牙利人民共和国。
    这些物资包括有面粉五千吨、马铃薯五千吨、肉一百吨、水泥五千吨、石灰一万吨、屋顶材料三十二万五千平方公尺、窗玻璃十万平方公尺、圆木两千立方公尺、锯好的木材三千立方公尺、木材燃料三千六百吨、总值为一千七百五十万克朗的药品，以及总值为三千二百四十万克朗的纺织品、鞋类、餐具和其他日用品。
    此外，捷克斯洛伐克政府还责成对外贸易部长保证按时完成捷匈1956年贸易协定中的义务。
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社柏林14日讯：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥在给匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔的电报中说，德意志民主共和国政府决定立即给匈牙利劳动人民总值为二千二百万马克的货物的无偿援助。
    据德意志通讯社报道，第一批运给匈牙利的援助物资中有十万平方公尺窗玻璃、五千吨柴油发动机甲燃料、四十一万双鞋、一万条毛毯、一千五百吨面食品和罐头。
    新华社柏林14日电　据“新德意志报”13日报道：德意志民主共和国援助匈牙利的第一批物资已经在11日随德国红十字会代表团一起到达布达佩斯。后续的援助物资将在日内陆续启运。
    民主德国广大的劳动人民掀起了支援兄弟的匈牙利人民的热潮。“援助社会主义匈牙利委员会”从11月10日成立以来，不断地收到各地劳动人民送来的大量现金和生活用品。单是国家广播事业委员会一个单位就已经募集了二百七十万马克的现金。来比锡专区波尔纳褐煤矿的矿工们募集了三万马克赠送给匈牙利的工人兄弟。农业合作社的社员和个体农民送来了他们的农产品、家畜和载重卡车。许多工厂的工人和技术人员纷纷要求到匈牙利去帮助修复工厂的机器设备。
    “援助社会主义匈牙利委员会”已经派遣了一个代表团到匈牙利去直接了解匈牙利人民在恢复建设和建立正常生活方面最需要的是什么，以便更加切合实际地援助匈牙利人民。
