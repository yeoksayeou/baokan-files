Title: Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu Meets with British Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, met with the British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, at noon today. Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, on behalf of the Chinese government, lodged a serious protest and warning with the British and French governments regarding the armed invasion of Egypt by the British and French governments, and handed the "Protest of the Government of the People's Republic of China to the Governments of Great Britain and France" to Mr. Trevelyan, asking him to immediately forward it to the British government and the French government.

Mr. Trevelyan agreed to immediately forward the aforementioned protest to the British government and the French government.

### 章汉夫副部长约见英国代办

1956-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    章汉夫副部长约见英国代办
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫今天中午约见了英国驻中国代办欧念儒。章汉夫副部长代表中国政府对英法政府武装进犯埃及的行为向英、法政府提出严重的抗议和警告，并把“中华人民共和国政府给英、法两国政府的抗议”交给欧念儒，要他立即转给英国政府和法国政府。
    欧念儒答应把上述抗议立即转交英国政府和法国政府。
