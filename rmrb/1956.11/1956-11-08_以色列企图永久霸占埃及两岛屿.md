Title: Israel Attempts to Permanently Seize Two Egyptian Islands
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: Tel Aviv dispatch: Israeli Prime Minister David Ben-Gurion announced on the evening of November 6th that Israel intends to permanently occupy the Egyptian islands of Tiran and Sanafir, which it recently seized.

Tiran and Sanafir Islands are located at the mouth of the Gulf of Aqaba, near the southern tip of Egypt's Sinai Peninsula, about 180 kilometers from Israel's southernmost point.

In a radio address, Ben-Gurion stated that he was unwilling to return the two islands to Egypt. He announced that the two islands were Israeli "territory."

He said that Tiran Island would be renamed Yotvata. (Attached picture)

### 以色列企图永久霸占埃及两岛屿

1956-11-08
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列企图永久霸占埃及两岛屿
    新华社7日讯　特拉维夫消息：以色列总理本—古里安11月6日晚宣布，以色列打算永久占有它最近占领的埃及岛屿蒂朗岛和塞纳菲尔岛。
    蒂朗岛和塞纳菲尔岛都在亚喀巴湾口，靠近埃及西奈半岛南端，离以色列最南端有约一百八十公里。
    本—古里安在一篇广播演说中表示他不愿意把这两个岛屿交还埃及。他宣布这两个岛屿是以色列的“领土”。
    他说，蒂朗岛将被命名为尤特瓦特。（附图片）
