Title: Burmese Acting Prime Minister Concerned About Middle East Situation, Summons Envoys from the United States, Britain, Egypt, France, Israel, and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 2nd: The Burmese Ministry of Information released a press communique on the evening of November 1st, stating that Burmese Acting Prime Minister Zaw Khun Cho summoned the American ambassador, the Egyptian minister, the French minister, the Israeli minister, the chargé d'affaires of the British embassy, and the chargé d'affaires of the Soviet embassy this morning, and discussed the current developments in the Middle East situation with each of them.

Zaw Khun Cho expressed in the discussions that Burma is deeply disturbed by the changes in the Middle East situation, as this constitutes a real threat to world peace.

### 缅甸代理总理关切中东局势  分别召见美英埃法以苏等国使节

1956-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸代理总理关切中东局势
    分别召见美英埃法以苏等国使节
    据新华社仰光2日电　缅甸宣传部在11月1日晚上发表一项新闻公报说：缅甸代理总理藻昆卓在今天上午分别召见了美国大使、埃及公使、法国公使、以色列公使、英国大使馆临时代办和苏联大使馆临时代办，同他们每个人讨论了目前中东的局势发展。
    藻昆卓在讨论中表示，缅甸对中东局势的转变深为不安，因为这已构成对世界和平的实际威胁。
