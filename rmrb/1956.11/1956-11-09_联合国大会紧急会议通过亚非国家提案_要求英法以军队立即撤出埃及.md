Title: UN General Assembly Emergency Session Passes Asian-African Nations Proposal Demanding Immediate Withdrawal of British, French, and Israeli Troops from Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 8th dispatch from New York: The United Nations General Assembly continued its emergency session on the afternoon of November 7th. The session passed the Asian-African nations' proposal demanding that the aggressive troops of Britain, France, and Israel withdraw from Egypt, requesting that Israel immediately withdraw its troops to behind the armistice line stipulated in the February 24, 1949, armistice agreement, and demanding that Britain and France immediately withdraw all troops from Egyptian territory. The session also passed a proposal from Argentina and other countries to organize an international emergency force.
    The voting results for the Asian-African nations' proposal were: sixty-five votes in favor, one vote against (Israel), and ten abstentions. The countries abstaining were Australia, Belgium, France, Laos, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Portugal, South Africa, and the United Kingdom.
    The voting results for the proposal to organize an international emergency force were: sixty-four votes in favor, none against, and twelve abstentions. The countries abstaining were: Albania, Bulgaria, Belarus, Czechoslovakia, Egypt, Hungary, Israel, Poland, Romania, Ukraine, South Africa, and the Soviet Union.

### 联合国大会紧急会议通过亚非国家提案  要求英法以军队立即撤出埃及

1956-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    联合国大会紧急会议通过亚非国家提案
    要求英法以军队立即撤出埃及
    据新华社8日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会11月7日下午继续举行紧急会议。会议通过了亚非国家要求英、法、以三国侵略部队撤出埃及的提案，要求以色列立即把它的军队撤退到1949年2月24日停战协定规定的停战线后面，要求英法立即从埃及领土撤出全部军队。会议还通过了阿根廷等国家关于组织国际紧急部队的提案。
    亚非国家提案的表决结果是：六十五票赞成，一票反对（以色列），十票弃权。弃权的国家是澳大利亚、比利时、法国、老挝、卢森堡、荷兰、新西兰、葡萄牙、南非和英国。
    关于组织国际紧急部队的提案的表决结果是：六十四票赞成，没有人反对和十二票弃权。弃权的国家是：阿尔巴尼亚，保加利亚，白俄罗斯，捷克斯洛伐克，埃及，匈牙利，以色列，波兰，罗马尼亚，乌克兰，南非，苏联。
