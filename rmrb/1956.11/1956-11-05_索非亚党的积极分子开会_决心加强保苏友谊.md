Title: Sofia Party Activists Meet, Determined to Strengthen Bulgarian-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 3rd (TASS dispatch): The Sofia City Party organization of the Bulgarian Communist Party held a meeting of party activists in Sofia on November 2nd. First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, Zhivkov, delivered a report on the international situation and the tasks of the party. The meeting demonstrated that the communists of the Bulgarian capital are closely united around the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and demonstrated their firm will and determination to fulfill the tasks put forward by the party. Sofia city party activists expressed their determination to repel any attempts against the people's democratic system and against the achievements of the people, and are prepared to strengthen Bulgarian-Soviet friendship and friendship with all people's democratic countries.

### 索非亚党的积极分子开会  决心加强保苏友谊

1956-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    索非亚党的积极分子开会
    决心加强保苏友谊
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社索非亚3日讯：保加利亚共产党索非亚市党组织11月2日在索非亚举行了党的积极分子会议。保共中央第一书记日夫科夫就国际形势和党的任务作了报告。会议表明了保加利亚首都的共产党员紧密地团结在保加利亚共产党中央委员会的周围，表明了他们完成党所提出的任务的坚定意志和决心。索非亚市党的积极分子表示决心打退任何反人民民主制度以及反对人民成果的企图，准备加强保苏友谊以及同一切人民民主国家的友谊。
