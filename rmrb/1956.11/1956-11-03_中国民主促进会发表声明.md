Title: China Association for Promoting Democracy Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The China Association for Promoting Democracy issued a statement tonight condemning the armed aggression of British and French imperialism against Egypt, warning the British and French governments to immediately cease their aggression against Egypt. The statement said that all members of the China Association for Promoting Democracy support the Chinese government's statement on the British and French armed aggression against Egypt, and firmly stand with all the people of our country and the people of Asian and African countries to fully support the Egyptian people's just struggle to defend their sacred territory and national independence.

　　Tonight, the China Association for Promoting Democracy also sent four representatives to the Egyptian Embassy in China to deliver a telegram supporting the Egyptian people. The telegram said, "Your struggle is just and has the support of the people of Asian and African countries and peace-loving people all over the world. You will surely win the final victory."

　　These two documents were adopted at a joint meeting of the Central Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy and the Council of the Beijing Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy held tonight. Wang Shao'ao, Zhou Jianren, Xu Guangping, Lin Handa, vice-chairmen of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, and some central committee members and members of the Beijing Branch spoke at the meeting, expressing their full support for the Egyptian people until they achieve final victory.

### 中国民主促进会发表声明

1956-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会发表声明
    新华社2日讯　中国民主促进会今晚发表谴责英、法帝国主义武装侵略埃及的声明，警告英、法政府立即停止对埃及的侵略。声明表示中国民主促进会的全体会员拥护中国政府关于英法武装侵略埃及的声明，坚决地和我国全体人民一起，和亚非各国人民一起，全力支持埃及人民为保卫其神圣国土和民族独立而进行的正义斗争。
    中国民主促进会今晚还推派四位代表到埃及驻华大使馆送交了一封支持埃及人民的电报。电报说，“你们的斗争是正义的，得到亚非各国和全世界爱好和平人民的支持，你们一定获得最后胜利。”
    这两个文件是在今晚举行的中国民主促进会中央委员会和中国民主促进会北京市分会理事联席会议上通过的。中国民主促进会副主席王绍鏊、周建人、许广平、林汉达和一些中央委员、北京市分会的理事在会上发言，表示全力支援埃及人民，直到他们获得最后胜利为止。
