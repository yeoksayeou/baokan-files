Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Tan Yunshan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Tan Yunshan, Dean of the Chinese Institute of International University of India, and his daughter Tan Wen on the evening of the 14th.
