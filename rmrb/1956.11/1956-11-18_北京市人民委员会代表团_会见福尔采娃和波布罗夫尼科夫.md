Title: Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation Meets Furtseva and Bobrovnikov
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 17th: The members of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation, who are currently visiting the Soviet Union, met with Furtseva, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the Moscow City Committee, and Bobrovnikov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, at the Moscow City Soviet Building on the 16th. Bobrovnikov then hosted a banquet for the delegation.

### 北京市人民委员会代表团  会见福尔采娃和波布罗夫尼科夫

1956-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人民委员会代表团
    会见福尔采娃和波布罗夫尼科夫
    据新华社莫斯科17日电　正在苏联访问的北京市人民委员会代表团人员16日在莫斯科市苏维埃大厦会见了苏共中央委员会书记处书记、莫斯科市委员会书记福尔采娃和莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫。波布罗夫尼科夫接着设宴招待了代表团。
