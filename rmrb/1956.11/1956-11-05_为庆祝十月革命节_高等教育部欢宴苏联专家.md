Title: Ministry of Higher Education Hosts Banquet for Soviet Experts to Celebrate October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: To celebrate the 39th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Ministry of Higher Education hosted a banquet at noon today to entertain all Soviet experts in Beijing who have been invited to China to help build higher education and secondary specialized education.

Experts from fraternal countries such as Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania，the German Democratic Republic, the Mongolian People’s Republic, the Democratic People’s of Korea, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam who were invited to China to assist in the construction of higher and secondary specialized education were also invited to the banquet.

Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, said at the banquet: In recent years, China's higher education has developed rapidly, and secondary specialized education has also made great progress. This is inseparable from learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and inseparable from the sincere and selfless help of Soviet experts and experts from various fraternal countries. In the future, we must continue to adhere to the policy of learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and other countries and combining it with China's actual situation. Yang Xiufeng said: The friendship between us will grow with the development of our common cause.

Maritsev, General Consultant of the State Council for Culture and Education, and Degujin, Acting Chief Consultant of the Ministry of Higher Education, also spoke at the banquet.

Yang Fangzhi, Director of the State Council's Bureau of Foreign Experts, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Higher Education, and heads of universities and secondary specialized schools that have hired experts were also present today. More than 500 people attended the banquet.

### 为庆祝十月革命节  高等教育部欢宴苏联专家

1956-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    为庆祝十月革命节
    高等教育部欢宴苏联专家
    新华社4日讯　高等教育部为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年，今天中午设宴招待应聘来中国帮助建设高等教育和中等专业教育的全体在京苏联专家。
    应聘来中国帮助建设高等教育和中等专业教育的波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国等兄弟国家的专家也应邀参加了宴会。
    高等教育部部长杨秀峰在宴会上说：几年来，中国高等教育事业有了飞跃的发展，中等专业教育也有了很大的进步，这是和学习苏联先进经验分不开的，是同苏联专家以及各兄弟国家的专家们真诚无私的帮助分不开的。今后我们必须继续坚持学习苏联和其他国家的先进经验并且同中国实际相结合的方针。杨秀峰说：我们之间的友谊将会随着我们共同的事业的发展而日益增进。
    国务院文教总顾问马里采夫，高等教育部代理首席顾问德古今也在宴会上讲了话。
    今天出席作陪的有国务院外国专家局局长杨放之，高等教育部的副部长，以及各聘有专家的高等学校和中等专业学校的负责人等。出席宴会的共有五百多人。
