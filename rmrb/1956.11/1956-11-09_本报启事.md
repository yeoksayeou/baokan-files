This Newspaper's Announcement

    Recently, there have been many important international and domestic current affairs news items. For the convenience of editing and publishing, the layout of this newspaper will be temporarily adjusted as follows: pages one and two will be the news pages, pages three and four will be the international pages, page five will be the domestic politics page, page six will be the domestic economics page, and pages seven and eight will remain the same.

### 本报启事

1956-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    本报启事
    近来国际国内的重要时事新闻很多，为了编辑和出版的方便，对本报版面暂时作如下调整：第一、第二版是要闻版，第三、第四版国际版，第五版国内政治版，第六版国内经济版，第七、第八版照常。
