Title: Japanese Writers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. The Japanese Writers Delegation, a group of eleven, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 10th.

The delegation is in China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The heads of the delegation are: Aono Suekichi, Chairman of the Japan Literary Artists Association and literary critic; Koji Uno, a famous writer; and Mantaro Kubota, Chairman of the Japan Theater Association. Members of the delegation include Seiichiro Hori, Hisao Koshi, Hachiro Hiraiwa, Akira Shiraishi, Morimichi Uno, Koichi Kubono, Yukitakeshi Izumino, and Nori Izui.

### 日本作家代表团到达北京

1956-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    日本作家代表团到达北京
    新华社11日讯　日本作家代表团一行十一人在10日晚上到达北京。
    代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来中国访问的。代表团的团长是：日本文艺家协会会长、文学评论家青野季吉，著名作家宇野浩二，日本演剧协会会长久保田万太郎等三人，团员有堀诚一郎、越寿雄、平岩八郎、白石彰、宇野守道、久保野耕一、泉野之武、泉井乃利等。
