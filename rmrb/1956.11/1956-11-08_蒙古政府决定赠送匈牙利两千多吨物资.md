Title: Mongolian Government Decides to Donate More Than 2,000 Tons of Supplies to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 7th. The Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic telegraphed the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government on the 6th, saying that the Mongolian people congratulated the establishment of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government. The telegram also stated that in order to help the Hungarian people restore normal life as soon as possible, the Mongolian government decided to provide free assistance to the Hungarian people with 2,000 tons of wheat, 100 tons of meat, and 15 tons of sausage.

The telegram said that the Mongolian people fully believe that the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, leading the working people and uniting all progressive forces, with the help of socialist countries, will surely be able to crush the reactionary forces and achieve new achievements in the cause of building socialism.

### 蒙古政府决定赠送匈牙利两千多吨物资

1956-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府决定赠送匈牙利两千多吨物资
    新华社乌兰巴托7日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议6日打电报给匈牙利工农革命政府说，蒙古人民祝贺匈牙利工农革命政府的成立。电报并且说，为了帮助匈牙利人民早日恢复正常生活，蒙古政府决定无偿地援助匈牙利人民两千吨小麦、一百吨肉和十五吨香肠。
    电报说，蒙古人民完全相信，匈牙利工农革命政府领导劳动人民，团结一切进步力量，在社会主义国家的帮助下，一定能够粉碎反动势力，并且在建设社会主义事业中获得新的成就。
