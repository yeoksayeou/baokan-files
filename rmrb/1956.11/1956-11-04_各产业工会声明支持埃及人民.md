Title: Various Industrial Unions Issue Statements Supporting the Egyptian People
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd November: China's various industrial unions have successively issued statements and telegrams to relevant Egyptian unions, supporting the just struggle of the Egyptian people.

    The National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, in its statement, representing 1.04 million railway workers nationwide, expressed its willingness to do its utmost to support the Egyptian people.

    The National Committees of the Chinese Electric Power Union, the Chinese Textile Union, the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Union, and the Chinese Commerce Union have all sent telegrams to relevant Egyptian unions expressing their firm support for the Egyptian people.

    The National First Advanced Workers Conference for Popularizing Science and Technology Among Employees, currently being held in Beijing, also passed a protest telegram against the armed aggression of Egypt by British and French imperialism.

### 各产业工会声明支持埃及人民

1956-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    各产业工会声明支持埃及人民
    新华社3日讯　中国各产业工会纷纷发表声明和致电埃及各有关工会，支持埃及人民的正义斗争。
    中国铁路工会全国委员会在声明中代表全国一百零四万铁路职工表示愿意尽最大努力来做埃及人民的后盾。
    中国电业工会、中国纺织工会、中国邮电工会、中国商业工会的全国委员会都分别打电报给埃及的有关工会对埃及人民表示坚决支持。
    正在北京举行的全国第一次职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会也通过了反对英法帝国主义武装侵略埃及的抗议电。
