Title: Ongniud Banner Cultivates a Large Number of Technicians
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot: Ongniud Banner in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has trained 1,500 technicians in veterinary medicine, breeding, feeding, lambing, and other fields among farmers and herdsmen. Among them, 946 are Mongolian. Now, every pastoral and agricultural production cooperative has its own technician. This year, they have treated more than 300,000 head of cattle and sheep with copper sulfate irrigation and anthrax vaccinations, and cured more than 3,000 sick livestock.

### 翁牛特旗培养大批技术员

1956-11-17
第3版()
专栏：

    翁牛特旗培养大批技术员
    据新华社呼和浩特电　内蒙古自治区翁牛特旗已经在农牧民中培养了一千五百名兽医、配种、饲养、接羔等方面的技术员。其中有九百四十六名是蒙古族。现在，每一个牧业和农业生产合作社都有了自己的技术员。今年经他们灌硫酸铜、注射炭疽预防针的牛羊有三十多万头次，治愈的病畜有三千多头。
