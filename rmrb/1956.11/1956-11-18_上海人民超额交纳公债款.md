Title: Shanghai People Oversubscribe to Public Bonds

Author:

Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th—According to statistics from the Shanghai Municipal Committee for Promoting the Sale of National Economic Construction Bonds as of October 10th, the people of Shanghai have already paid 72.51 million yuan for the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds, exceeding the city's planned sales target.

This year, more than 1.3 million workers and staff members in the city purchased public bonds, and the public bond funds they have paid have increased by about 10% compared with the actual purchase amount last year.

### 上海人民超额交纳公债款

1956-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    上海人民超额交纳公债款
    新华社上海16日电　据上海市推销国家经济建设公债委员会办公室到10月10日止的统计，上海市人民已经交纳1956年国家经济建设公债款七千二百五十一万元，超过了全市的计划推销数额。
    今年全市有一百三十多万个职工购买公债，他们已交的公债款比去年的实际购买数增加10％左右。
