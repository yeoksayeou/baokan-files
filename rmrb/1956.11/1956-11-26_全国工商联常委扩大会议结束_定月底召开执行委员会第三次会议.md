Title: National Federation of Industry and Commerce Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting Concludes; Executive Committee's Third Meeting Scheduled for End of Month
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 24th - The Sixth (Enlarged) Meeting of the Standing Committee of the First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce concluded on the afternoon of November 24th after five days of group discussions. At the plenary meeting held today, the drafts of documents such as Chairman Chen Shutong's report to the Second National Congress of Members, the three-year work report, the interim measures for the industrial and commercial sector's mutual aid fund, and the constitution of the Federation of Industry and Commerce were adopted.

The meeting also approved the agenda for the Third Meeting of the First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. This meeting will be held in Beijing on November 30th.

The main task of this enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee was to prepare for the Second National Congress of Members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. There will be 1,400 delegates and observers attending this Congress, all of whom are representatives from various aspects of the industrial and commercial sector: there are advanced producers and advanced workers from the industrial and commercial sector; there are private-sector heads, technicians, and those holding leadership positions in departments and workshops in joint state-private enterprises; there are also small vendors and individual handicraftsmen; there are young industrialists and businessmen, as well as female industrialists and businesswomen; there are also industrialists and businessmen from ethnic minorities and other people who have made contributions to the industrial and commercial sector.

### 全国工商联常委扩大会议结束  定月底召开执行委员会第三次会议

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联常委扩大会议结束
    定月底召开执行委员会第三次会议
    新华社24日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会常务委员会第六次（扩大）会议，经过五天小组讨论后，在11月24日下午结束。在今天举行的全体会议上，通过了陈叔通主任委员在第二届会员代表大会上的报告、三年工作报告、工商界生活互助金暂行办法及工商业联合会章程等文件的草案。
    会议还通过了全国工商联第一届执行委员会第三次会议的议程。这个会议将在11月30日在北京召开。
    这次常务委员会扩大会议的任务，主要是给全国工商联第二届会员代表大会作好准备工作。这届会员代表大会出席代表及列席人员将有一千四百人，都是工商界各方面的代表人物：有工商界的先进生产者及先进工作者；有合营企业的私方负责人、技术人员和担任科室车间领导职务的人员，也有小商小贩和个体手工业者；有青年工商业者，也有女工商业者；还有少数民族工商业者及其他对工商界有贡献的人士。
