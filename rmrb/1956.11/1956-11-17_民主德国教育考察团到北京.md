Title: East German Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. A four-member education delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Isolde Oschmann, Director of the Preschool Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane at 2:30 p.m. on the 15th.

The East German education delegation is visiting China in accordance with the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement. They will investigate the situation of general education and early childhood education in our country.

Greeting them at the airport were Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Trade Union Xiao Xiangping, and others.

### 民主德国教育考察团到北京

1956-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国教育考察团到北京
    新华社15日讯　德意志民主共和国教育考察团四人，在团长、德意志民主共和国教育部学前教育司司长依苏尔德·奥希曼率领下，15日下午二时半乘飞机到达北京。
    德意志民主共和国教育考察团是按照中德文化合作协定1956年执行计划规定来中国的。他们将在我国考察普通教育和幼儿教育的情况。
    到机场欢迎的有教育部副部长韦悫、中国教育工会全国委员会副主席萧项平等。
