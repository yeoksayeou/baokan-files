Title: Ambassador Yudin's Speech at the Reception Celebrating the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1956-11-08
Page: 2

Comrades and friends,
Esteemed guests:
    As the Ambassador and citizen of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, I would first like to express my gratitude to all the guests present at this banquet for their concern for the national holiday of the Soviet people.
    Thirty-nine years have passed since the victorious Great Revolution, when our working people overcame domestic reactionary forces and embarked on a new life based on the principles of peace, democracy, and socialism.
    The Soviet people, strictly and unswervingly adhering to these principles, overcoming enormous difficulties, and removing obstacles in the path of progress, have built a great multinational socialist country, freed their motherland from its former economic backwardness, and transformed it into a powerful industrial and collective agricultural nation, developing all branches of science, technology, and culture and making them the property of all the people.
    The Soviet Union's selfless struggle for the cause of peace and democracy, for the noble cause of justice, has won it the deep sympathy and respect of hundreds of millions of people around the world. The great socialist country of the East—the People's Republic of China—together with the people's democratic countries of Europe and Asia, are advancing hand in hand in a family of equal and sovereign states.
    Their unity is a decisive condition for the achievements in the struggle to safeguard peace among all nations. No conspiracy of the hostile forces of imperialism and counter-revolution can undermine the foundations of friendship and cooperation between the socialist countries, primarily because their unity is not based on some temporary, fleeting circumstances, but on the fundamental and vital interests of the people of each country. To strengthen this friendship and cherish it as the apple of one's eye is the highest patriotic and internationalist duty of every citizen of these socialist countries.
    The lofty ideals of the struggle for peace, democracy, and social progress bring the people of the socialist countries closer to the people of India, Burma, Egypt, Indonesia, and all other Eastern countries that have won their freedom and independence in the arduous struggle against colonialism!
    Our sympathy and support are entirely with the heroic Egyptian people and the peoples of other Arab countries who are bravely resisting the attacks of imperialist aggressors. We are confident that the freedom-loving Egyptian people will triumph, their just cause will succeed, and the bloody adventure instigated by the imperialists in the Near East will inevitably fail.
    The Soviet people have deep respect for the people of all countries in the world who strive for business-like cooperation and jointly fight against the dark reactionary forces that are provoking military conflicts and bloodshed with their provocative actions.
    We are celebrating the thirty-ninth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution in Beijing, the capital of this great and friendly country—the People's Republic of China. We are very gratified that the Chinese people are celebrating our national holiday with such excitement and with warm, fraternal feelings, thereby emphasizing the great, world-historical significance of the victory of our proletarian revolution.
    Long live the victory of peace and democracy throughout the world!
    Long live the great unity of the people of the socialist countries!
    May the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China—a great force in the struggle for peace and the happiness of all nations—be increasingly consolidated and last forever!

### 在庆祝十月革命节的招待会上  尤金大使的讲话

1956-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    在庆祝十月革命节的招待会上
    尤金大使的讲话
    亲爱的同志们和朋友们，
    尊敬的来宾们：
    作为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的大使和公民，我首先要向出席这次宴会的全体来宾表示谢意，感谢你们对苏联人民国庆节日的关切。
    自从我国劳动人民战胜国内的反动势力取得了伟大的革命胜利、走上了建设以和平、民主和社会主义的各项原则为基础的新生活的道路以来，从那个具有重大意义日子起已经过了三十九年了。
    苏联人民严格地始终不渝地坚持这些原则，克服了巨大的困难并扫除横在前进道路上的障碍之后，建立了伟大的多民族的社会主义国家，使自己的祖国摆脱了以往的经济落后状态，并成为一个强大的工业和集体农业的国家，使科学技术和文化的各个部门都获得了发展，并使他们成为全体人民的财产。
    苏联为和平、民主的事业，也就是为崇高的正义事业而进行着忘我的斗争，这就使它获得了全世界亿万人民的深切同情和尊敬。东方的一个伟大的社会主义国家——中华人民共和国以及欧洲和亚洲各人民民主国家同苏联在一起，在平等的主权国家的大家庭里共同携手前进。
    他们的团结一致是维护各国人民间的和平的斗争之所以取得成就的一个决定性的条件。帝国主义和反革命的敌对势力的任何阴谋都不能破坏各社会主义国家之间的友谊和合作的基础，这首先是因为他们的团结不是以一些一时的、短暂的情况为基础的，而是建筑在各国人民根本的切身利益的基础之上的。加强这种友谊并像保护自己的眼睛一样加以爱护，这是我们这一个社会主义国家的每一个公民最高的爱国主义和国际主义的责任。
    为争取和平、民主和社会进步而斗争的崇高思想使各社会主义国家的人民同印度、缅甸、埃及、印度尼西亚的人民以及那些在对殖民主义者进行艰苦斗争中取得了自己的自由和独立的所有其它东方国家的人民互相接近起来了！
    我们的同情和支持完全是在勇敢地抗击着帝国主义侵略势力的进攻的英勇的埃及人民及其它阿拉伯国家人民这方面的。我们深信爱好自由的埃及人民是会取得胜利的，他们的正义事业是会成功的，帝国主义者所策动的在近东的血腥的冒险是一定会遭到失败的。
    苏联人民对于那些力求进行事务上的合作并对正在以自己的挑衅行为引起军事冲突和流血事件的黑暗反动势力进行共同斗争的世界各国人民是满怀敬意的。
    我们是在这个伟大的友好国家——中华人民共和国的首都北京，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年。使我们感到非常欣慰的是中国人民是这样兴奋地并且怀着热烈的兄弟般的情感来庆祝我们的国庆节日，并从而强调说明我国无产阶级革命胜利是具有伟大的、世界历史意义的。
    全世界和平和民主胜利万岁！
    社会主义各国人民伟大的团结万岁！
    愿苏联和中华人民共和国两国人民的牢不可破的友谊和合作——争取和平和各国人民的幸福的斗争中的伟大力量——日益巩固并万世长存！
