Title: Gathering of American Progressives Commemorates the 23rd Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the United States and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th - According to TASS, more than six hundred American progressives held a celebration in New York to commemorate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Union and the 23rd anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the United States and the Soviet Union.

    The celebration was initiated by the National Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association. Prominent American progressive social activists, including Muriel Draper, Chairman of the National Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association, and black singer Paul Robeson, spoke at the meeting.

    The speakers mentioned the achievements of the Soviet Union on the path of peace and progress and called on the American people to strengthen their friendship with the Soviet Union.

    A group of Hungarian reactionary elements who had fled to the United States attempted to disrupt the meeting but failed.

### 纪念美苏建交二十三周年  美国进步人士集会

1956-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念美苏建交二十三周年
    美国进步人士集会
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：美国六百多名进步人士在纽约举行了苏联建国三十九周年和美、苏建交二十三周年的庆祝会。
    庆祝会是由美苏友好协会全国委员会创议举行的，美国著名的进步社会活动家、美苏友好协会全国委员会主席摩弗德，黑人歌唱家罗伯逊等都在会上讲了话。
    讲话的人都提到苏联在和平和进步的道路上所获得的成就，并且号召美国人民加强同苏联的友谊。
    一批逃亡在美国的匈牙利反动分子企图破坏会议，但是没有得逞。
