Title: Egyptian Red Crescent Society Cables Thanks to Chinese Red Cross for Donation and Support
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Egyptian Red Crescent Society sent a telegram to the Chinese Red Cross on the 25th, expressing gratitude for the donations from the Chinese people to the Egyptian people.
    The Chinese Red Cross previously sent a telegram to the Egyptian Red Crescent Society, expressing support for the just struggle of the Egyptian people. The telegram mentioned that the Chinese Red Cross had donated RMB 100,000 (equivalent to 170,068 Swiss Francs) to the Egyptian Red Crescent Society to purchase medical supplies needed by the Egyptian people.

### 埃及红新月会电谢我红十字会捐款支援

1956-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及红新月会电谢我红十字会捐款支援
    新华社27日讯　埃及红新月会25日打电报给中国红十字会，对中国人民给予埃及人民的捐助表示感谢。
    中国红十字会不久前曾经打电报给埃及红新月会，表示支持埃及人民的正义斗争。电报提到中国红十字会已经捐给埃及红新月会人民币十万元（合一七○，○六八个瑞士法郎），用来购买埃及人民所需要的医药物资。
