Title: Israeli Newspapers Express Concern About England and France Pulling Chestnuts out of the Fire for Them
Author:
Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, November 3rd: Some Israeli newspapers and radio stations are feigning indifference to the Anglo-French-Israeli aggression against Egypt. The "Jerusalem Post" said in an editorial that the resolution of the United Nations General Assembly Emergency Session was "merely a recommendation that is not binding on anyone."

Despite this, the newspaper could not help but admit: "The opposition of Asian and African countries is an unpleasant blow to Israel."

The "Haaretz" published a message from the Star News Agency on November 3, reflecting a concern: "England and France are only using Israel for their current plans, leaving Israel to the mercy of fate, regardless of the fact that Israel will be subjected to even stronger hatred from Arab countries." "Haaretz" said that the war against Egypt is only beneficial to Britain.

### 替英法火中取栗以色列报纸表示忧虑

1956-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    替英法火中取栗以色列报纸表示忧虑
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社特拉维夫3日讯：以色列一些报纸和电台对于英、法和以色列侵略埃及的行动装做若无其事的样子。“耶路撒冷邮报”在社论中说，联合国大会紧急会议的决议“不过是一个对谁都没有约束力的建议”。
    尽管如此，这家报纸终究不能不承认：“亚非国家的反对态度，对以色列来说是一个不愉快的打击。”
    “国土报”11月3日刊登的斯塔通讯社的一条消息反映出了这样一种忧虑：“英法只是为了自己目前的打算，利用以色列，让以色列去听任命运的摆布，不顾以色列会遭到阿拉伯国家更加强烈的憎恨。”“国土报”说，反对埃及的战争只对英国有利。
