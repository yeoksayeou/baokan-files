Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Soviet Experts
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet at the Academy on the afternoon of the 5th at 6:00 PM in honor of the Soviet experts who have been invited to work in China, to celebrate the thirty-ninth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

Representatives from the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences delegation and the Polish Academy of Sciences delegation who are currently visiting China, as well as Mongolian historians, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Among those accompanying the guests at the banquet were Zhang Jingfu, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, heads of various departments, and Chinese scientists.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, and the development of science in China and the Soviet Union.

### 郭沫若设宴招待苏联专家

1956-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴招待苏联专家
    新华社6日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年，5日下午六时在科学院设宴招待应聘来我国工作的在京苏联专家。
  正在我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团及波兰科学院代表团的代表，和蒙古历史学家等，也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的有中国科学院副院长张劲夫、竺可桢、吴有训，各学部负责人和中国科学家。
    席间，宾主频频为中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊干杯，为中苏两国科学的发展干杯。
