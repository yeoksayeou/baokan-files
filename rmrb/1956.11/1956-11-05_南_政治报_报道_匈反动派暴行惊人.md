Title: Southern "Political Newspaper" Reports: Hungarian Reactionary Atrocities are Appalling
Author:
Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th: Belgrade dispatch: The Yugoslavian "Political Newspaper" published a dispatch from Budapest on November 3rd, pointing out that in Budapest, the reactionary faction's shocking atrocities—especially against communists—have not stopped. The reporter wrote that this situation is continuing, which is a clear sign of the rise of extreme right-wing elements who believe that the time for them to act has come. It is known that in the past few days, many communists have been brutally murdered. The newspaper also pointed out that there are many signs that fascist behavior is rapidly intensifying.

### 南“政治报”报道  匈反动派暴行惊人

1956-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    南“政治报”报道
    匈反动派暴行惊人
    新华社布拉格4日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫“政治报”11月3日刊载的一篇布达佩斯通讯指出，在布达佩斯，反动派的骇人听闻的暴行——特别是对共产党人——还没有停止。记者写道，这种情况还在继续着，这是极右翼分子抬头的确切标志，这些分子认为，有利于他们进行活动的时机来到了。大家知道，在过去几天中，有不少共产党人遭到残杀。报纸还指出，有许多迹象说明，法西斯行为在迅速加强。
