Title: At the Invitation of the CPC Central Committee, Italian Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Peng Zhen Holds Banquet to Welcome All Members of the Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. The Italian Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation, consisting of eight members, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, Chen Zhengren, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Xiandong and Cai Ziwei, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, and Xiong Fu, a responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee.

In the evening, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, held a banquet to welcome all the guests of the Italian Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation.

The list of members of the Italian Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation is as follows:

Luigi Locolatolo, Member of the Central Committee of the Italian Socialist Party, Head of the Rural Work Department of the Italian Socialist Party

Gino Bertoldi, Member of the Central Committee of the Italian Socialist Party, Secretary of the Verona Municipal Committee of the Socialist Party in Veneto Province

Vincenzo Millilo, Member of the Central Committee of the Italian Socialist Party, Vice Chairman of the National League of Cooperatives, former Senator

Renato Tramontani, Deputy Secretary of the Italian National Agricultural Workers' Union

Giuseppe Collia, Member of the Sardinian Regional Council

Angelo Ganazzoli, Secretary of the Palermo Municipal Committee of the Socialist Party in Sicily Province

Marino Papucci, Secretary of the Pisa Municipal Committee of the Socialist Party in Tuscany Province

Giorgio Vigeti, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Bologna Provincial Committee of the Socialist Party, Head of the Bologna Provincial Farmers' Organization

### 应中共中央邀请来我国访问  意大利社会党农业考察团到京  彭真举行宴会欢迎考察团全体人员

1956-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国访问
    意大利社会党农业考察团到京
    彭真举行宴会欢迎考察团全体人员
    新华社31日讯　意大利社会党农业考察团一行八人在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。考察团是应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来中国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真，中共中央委员刘宁一，候补中央委员陈正人，中华人民共和国农业部副部长杨显东、蔡子伟，中共中央负责工作人员熊复。
    晚间，中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真举行宴会，欢迎意大利社会党农业考察团的全体客人们。
    意大利社会党农业考察团人员名单如下：
    路易吉·洛科拉托洛　意大利社会党中央委员，意大利社会党农村工作部负责人
    吉诺·贝托耳迪　意大利社会党中央委员，维纳托省维罗纳市社会党市委书记
    文琴佐·米利洛　意大利社会党中央委员，合作社全国联盟副主席，前参议员
    雷纳托·特拉蒙塔尼　意大利全国农业工人工会副书记
    朱塞佩·科利亚　撒丁省地方议会议员
    安杰洛·加纳佐利　西西里省巴勒莫市社会党市委书记
    马里诺·帕普奇　托斯加纳省彼萨市社会党市委书记
    乔治·维杰提　波伦亚省社会党省委书记处书记，波伦亚省农民组织负责人
