Title: Chinese Fisheries Association Delegation Visits Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The nine-member delegation of the Chinese Fisheries Association to Japan left Beijing by train this morning for a visit to Japan.

The delegation is visiting Japan at the invitation of the Japan-China Fisheries Council.

The delegation is headed by Yang Yu, director of the China Fisheries Association. During their visit to Japan, they will discuss some specific issues in the implementation of the Sino-Japanese Fisheries Agreement, and visit major facilities of Japanese fishery bases and related fishery production issues.

### 我国渔业协会访日代表团去日本

1956-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

    我国渔业协会访日代表团去日本
    新华社17日讯　中国渔业协会访日代表团一行九人已在今日上午乘火车离北京前往日本访问。
    这个代表团是应日本日中渔业协议会的邀请去日本访问的。
    代表团由中国渔业协会主任杨煜担任，他们在访日期间，将就中日渔业协定执行中的一些具体问题进行商谈，并参观访问日本渔业基地的主要设施以及有关渔业生产方面的问题。
