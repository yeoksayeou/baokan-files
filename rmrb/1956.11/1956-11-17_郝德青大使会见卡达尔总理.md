Title: Ambassador Hao Deqing Meets with Premier Kádár
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 16th: Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, Hao Deqing, met with Kádár, Premier of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, on the afternoon of the 14th, and presented Premier Zhou Enlai's congratulatory telegram to the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government to Premier Kádár.

Ambassador Hao Deqing and Premier Kádár spoke for approximately two hours. Present at the meeting was Münnich (formerly translated as Minichi), Deputy Premier and Minister of Armed Forces and Public Security of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government.

### 郝德青大使会见卡达尔总理

1956-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    郝德青大使会见卡达尔总理
    新华社布达佩斯16日电　中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青在14日下午会见了匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔，并且将周恩来总理致匈牙利工农革命政府的贺电递交卡达尔总理。
    郝德青大使和卡达尔总理谈了约两小时的话。在座的有匈牙利工农革命政府副总理兼武装部队和公安部长明尼赫（前译为明尼奇）。
