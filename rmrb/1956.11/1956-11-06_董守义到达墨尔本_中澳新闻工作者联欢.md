Title: Dong Shouyi Arrives in Melbourne; Sino-Australian Journalists' Gathering

Author:

Date: 1956-11-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, 5th: Dong Shouyi, Chinese member of the International Olympic Committee and Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation to the 16th Olympic Games, and Huang Zhong, Secretary-General of the delegation, arrived in Melbourne on the 4th.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, 5th: Chinese journalists who are in Melbourne to cover the 16th Olympic Games attended a conference held by the Victorian Branch of the Australian Journalists Association at the Cerberus Club in Melbourne on the 3rd and were warmly welcomed.
    
    The Chinese journalists delivered a congratulatory message to the conference and presented gifts to the conference on behalf of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 董守义到达墨尔本  中澳新闻工作者联欢

1956-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    董守义到达墨尔本
    中澳新闻工作者联欢
    据新华社墨尔本5日电　国际奥林匹克委员会中国委员、中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会代表团副团长董守义和代表团秘书长黄中已经在4日到达墨尔本。
    据新华社墨尔本5日电　前往墨尔本报道第十六届奥林匹克运动会的中国记者3日参加了澳大利亚新闻工作者协会维多利亚州分会在墨尔本赛耳的克俱乐部举行的大会，并受到热烈欢迎。
    中国记者向大会致贺词并代表中国新闻工作者联谊会向大会赠送礼物。
