Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Watches Performance by the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. On the evening of the 18th, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, watched a comprehensive song and dance program performed by the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe.

During the intermission, Vice Chairman Zhu De met with the leaders of the song and dance troupe and had a cordial conversation with them. After the performance, Vice Chairman Zhu De, accompanied by Gao Ende, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, went on stage and shook hands with the members of the song and dance troupe one by one.

On the 17th and 18th, the Hungarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe performed simultaneously in three theaters in Beijing. The comprehensive song and dance program was one of the performances. The other two performances were by individual performance teams and included piano, harp, and solo vocal performances.

### 朱德副主席观看匈牙利人民军文工团演出

1956-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席观看匈牙利人民军文工团演出
    新华社18日讯　18日晚上，中华人民共和国朱德副主席观看了匈牙利人民军文工团演出的综合歌舞节目。
    在演出休息时间，朱德副主席接见了文工团的领导人员，并且同他们亲切地谈了话。演出结束，朱德副主席在匈牙利驻中国临时代办高恩德陪同下，上台和文工团团员们一一握手。
    17日和18日，匈牙利人民军文工团在北京三个剧场同时演出，综合歌舞节目是其中的一场。另外两场是单项演出队的演出和钢琴、竖琴、独唱节目。
