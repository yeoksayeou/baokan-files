Title: Egypt Seizes British and French Banks in Egypt and Takes Over British and French Oil Enterprises in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd dispatch from Cairo: The Egyptian government today seized the British and French banks in Egypt.
	These banks included Barclays Bank, Ottoman Bank, Credit Lyonnais, and Comptoir National d'Escompte de Paris.
	The doors of these banks were closed, and Egyptian police guarded them.
	Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd dispatch from Cairo: The Egyptian government took over the British and French oil enterprises in Egypt on November 1st.
	An Egyptian government communique stated that the Minister of Industry and Commerce took over two British companies and one French company in Egypt. They are Cory Brothers, the Anglo-Egyptian Oil Company, and the Société Egyptienne Générale de Pétrole.
	From November 1st, the Egyptian army has been responsible for protecting all public utilities.

### 埃及查封英法在埃的银行  并接管了英法在埃及的石油企业

1956-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及查封英法在埃的银行
    并接管了英法在埃及的石油企业
    新华社2日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府今天查封了在埃及的英国和法国的银行。
    在这些银行中包括巴尔克莱银行，沃特门银行，里昂信贷银行，巴黎国家贴现银行。
    这些银行的大门被封闭，门前有埃及警察守卫。
    新华社2日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府11月1日接管了英法在埃及的石油企业。
    埃及政府发布的一份公报说，工商部长接管了在埃及的两家英国公司和一家法国公司。它们是谷牌石油公司、英埃石油公司和法埃石油公司。
    从11月1日起，埃及陆军担负了保护一切公用事业的责任。
