Title: Egyptian Railway Transportation Fully Restored; Cairo International Airport Reopened
Author:
Date: 1956-11-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 12th: The Egyptian Railway Administration announced on November 10th that all railway transportation in Upper and Lower Egypt has been restored.

    The Egyptian Ministry of Transportation pointed out that the short-term interruption of railway transportation was due to British and French bombing, but it was quickly repaired by the Egyptian Engineer Corps.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: According to Cairo Radio on November 11th, Cairo International Airport has begun to resume air traffic operations on some international routes.

    The Egyptian government on November 11th has allowed Trans World Airlines, KLM Royal Dutch Airlines, Air India International, and Swissair to resume their air traffic operations to or from Cairo during the period between sunrise and sunset.

    Cairo International Airport was closed after the outbreak of the war of aggression against Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel.

### 埃及铁路运输全部恢复  开罗国际机场重新开放

1956-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及铁路运输全部恢复  开罗国际机场重新开放
    新华社12日讯　开罗消息：埃及铁路管理机构11月10日宣布，上埃及和下埃及的全部铁路运输已经恢复。
    埃及交通部指出，铁路运输短期中断是由于英、法轰炸造成的，但是它很快地被埃及工兵团修复了。
    新华社12日讯　据开罗电台11日宣布，开罗国际机场开始恢复某些国际航线的通航业务。
    埃及政府11日已经允许美国环球航空公司、荷兰皇家航空公司、印度国际航空公司和瑞士航空公司在日出和日落这段时间内恢复它们到开罗或者从开罗起飞的通航业务。
    开罗国际机场是在英法和以色列侵略埃及的战争爆发以后关闭的。
