Title: The Vietnam People's Army Sent Two Letters to the International Commission Requesting Investigation into South Vietnam's Continued Illegal Importation of Military Supplies
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 30th -- According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency on the 30th: The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent two letters to the International Commission in Vietnam on October 24th and 26th, requesting the International Commission to investigate the continued illegal importation of military supplies into South Vietnam.

    The two letters pointed out that on August 20th, an American ship unloaded nearly 800 tons of military aircraft equipment and ammunition, as well as other military supplies, at Saigon Port. On August 27th, another American ship transported nearly 500 tons of artillery and mortar shells, as well as many parts for these two types of weapons, to Nha Trang Port. Two days later, a U.S. Navy warship unloaded nearly 100 tons of armored speedboat parts and more than 200 tons of machine gun bullets, submachine gun bullets, and rifle bullets, as well as military communications equipment, at Saigon Port.

### 越南人民军两次致函国际委员会  要求调查南越继续非法运入军用物资

1956-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    越南人民军两次致函国际委员会
    要求调查南越继续非法运入军用物资
    新华社河内30日电　据越南通讯社30日讯：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团先后在10月24日和26日写了两封信给越南国际委员会，要求国际委员会对继续非法将军用物资运入南越的行动进行调查。
    这两封信件指出，在8月20日，一只美国船在西贡港卸下了将近八百吨军用飞机的装备和弹药以及其它军用物资。在8月27日，另一只美国船把将近五百吨大炮和迫击炮的炮弹以及这两种武器的许多零件运到芽?港。两天以后，一艘美国海军军舰又在西贡港卸下了将近一百吨的装甲汽艇的零件和两百多吨的机枪子弹、冲锋枪子弹和步枪子弹以及军用通讯器材。
