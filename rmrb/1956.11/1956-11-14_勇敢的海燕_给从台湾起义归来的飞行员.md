Title: Brave Petrel - To the Pilots Who Returned from Taiwan in Uprising
Author: Chen Shanwen
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 8

    Brave Petrel
    -- To the Pilots Who Returned from Taiwan in Uprising
    Chen Shanwen
    A sorrowful petrel flies across the strait
    Leaving the lonely forest
    Leaving the sinful island
    Coming to the homeland longed for day and night
    A brave petrel returns to the homeland
    The phoenix trees are as brilliant as flames
    Jasmine bushes exude the fragrance of the morning
    The green land opens her broad embrace
    A silver petrel lands on the beautiful land of the homeland
    A prodigal son returns to his mother's embrace
    How much blessing, how much longing!
    How much courage does kindness and happiness require?
    The brave petrel is no longer sad
    How happy is the brave petrel!
    My good brothers,
    My brothers return to this boiling and fascinating land of the motherland

### 勇敢的海燕——给从台湾起义归来的飞行员
陈善文
1956-11-14
第8版()
专栏：

    勇敢的海燕
    ——给从台湾起义归来的飞行员
    陈善文
    忧伤的海燕飞过海峡
    离开那寂寞的森林
    离开那罪恶的孤岛
    来到日夜怀念的家乡
    勇敢的海燕回到家乡
    凤凰木像火焰一样灿烂
    茉莉丛飘着早晨的清香
    绿油油的土地敞开她宽阔的胸膛
    银色的海燕飞落在美丽家乡的土地上
    浪荡的儿子回到母亲的怀抱
    多少祝福，多少的想望啊
    善良和幸福需要多少勇敢
    勇敢的海燕不再忧伤
    勇敢的海燕多么快乐
    我的好兄弟啊
    我的兄弟回到这沸腾的迷人的祖国土地上
