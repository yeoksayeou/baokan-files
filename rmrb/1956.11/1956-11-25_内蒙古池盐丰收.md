Title: Inner Mongolia Achieves Bumper Harvest of Pond Salt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, November 24th. Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has achieved a bumper harvest of pond salt, with its output now ranking first among all pond salt producing regions in the country.

    Inner Mongolia is rich in pond salt reserves, with more than 170 natural salt ponds of various sizes distributed across the grasslands of Bayannur, Xilingol and other areas. The reserves of just three state-owned salt fields and three local state-owned salt fields amount to over 100 million tons. In addition to supplying the region itself, Inner Mongolia is also preparing to supply 130,000 tons of pond salt to Gansu, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Hebei and other regions this year.

### 内蒙古池盐丰收

1956-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古池盐丰收
    新华社呼和浩特24日电　内蒙古自治区池盐丰收，产量已经居全国各地池盐产量的第一位。
    内蒙古地区池盐蕴藏量很丰富，有一百七十多个大小天然盐池，分布在巴彦淖尔、锡林郭勒等草原上，仅三个国营盐场和三个地方国营盐场的蕴藏量就有一亿多吨。内蒙古出产的池盐除了供应本区以外，今年还准备供应甘肃、陕西、山西、河北等地十三万吨。
