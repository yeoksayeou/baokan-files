Title: Defend Austrian Neutrality - Austrian Communist Party Chairman Issues Call

Author:

Date: 1956-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, November 6th: According to TASS, Vienna, November 6th: Austrian Communist Party Central Committee Chairman Koplenig, speaking at a district representatives meeting of the party about Austria's policy on the Hungarian events, said: Now it is necessary to unite all forces within the country to defend our country's neutrality and safeguard democracy and independence. Koplenig called on people with various political viewpoints to work together to prevent Austria's neutrality from being undermined and to protect the democratic rights of the working people.

### 保卫奥地利中立  奥共主席发出号召

1956-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    保卫奥地利中立
    奥共主席发出号召
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社维也纳6日讯：奥地利共产党中央委员会主席柯普勒尼希在党的一个区代表会议上谈到奥地利对匈牙利事件的政策的时候说：现在必须团结国内的一切力量，来捍卫我国的中立，保障民主和独立。柯普勒尼希号召具有各种不同政治见解的人共同来防止奥地利的中立遭到破坏和保护劳动人民的民主权利。
