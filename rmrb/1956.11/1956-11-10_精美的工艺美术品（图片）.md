    Exquisite Arts and Crafts

    In October of this year, the Sichuan Provincial Bureau of Culture and the Bureau of Handicraft Industry held the "Sichuan Provincial Arts and Crafts Exhibition" in Chengdu. The exhibits included more than 1,500 pieces of pottery, lacquerware, dyeing and weaving, bamboo ware, metal crafts, palm and grass weaving, and handicrafts from ethnic minority areas. The left picture shows exquisite embroidery, and the right picture shows beautiful and practical Sichuan ceramics.
           Photographed by Qian Yihua and Li Xuan (Manuscript from Sichuan Daily)

### 精美的工艺美术品（图片）
钱一华  李煊
1956-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    精美的工艺美术品
    今年10月，四川省文化局和手工业管理局，在成都举办了“四川省工艺美术品展览会”。展品有陶器、漆器、染织、竹器、金属工艺，棕草编织及少数民族地区的工艺品等一千五百余件。左图是精美的刺绣，右图是美观适用的四川陶瓷。
           钱一华、李煊摄（四川日报社稿）
