Title: India Prepares to Welcome Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 25th: The Indian capital of New Delhi and other major cities are making preparations to receive Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long.
    The Indian government is making the final arrangements for the Chinese Premier's visit.
    A group of specialized decorators are busy decorating the road from the airport to the Presidential Palace, where the national flags of India and China will be hung high on both sides of the road. Banners are being hung in the middle of the road with slogans welcoming the Chinese Premier and hailing the close friendship between India and China. Public buildings are also being dressed up in flowers and flags.
    More than three thousand students are practicing according to the "National Physical Training Program" in order to perform sports demonstrations to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.
    People's organizations across the country are also actively preparing to participate in mass rallies to welcome the Chinese Premier. The Indian Railway Authority is preparing to add many special trains to transport people from nearby areas such as Punjab and Uttar Pradesh to Delhi.
    In Bombay, Madras, and Calcutta, public gatherings and grand banquets have also been arranged to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai. The All-India China Friendship Association plans to hold receptions in Delhi and Bombay to welcome Premier Zhou, and will also organize banquets to welcome him.

### 印度准备欢迎周恩来总理

1956-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度准备欢迎周恩来总理
    新华社新德里25日电　印度首都新德里和其他大城市正在进行接待周恩来总理、贺龙副总理的准备工作。
    印度政府对中国总理的访问，正在作最后的安排。
    一批专门的装饰工匠正在忙于装饰从飞机场到总统府的道路，在这条路的两边将高挂印中两国的国旗。在道路中间正在悬挂横幅，上面写着欢迎中国总理和欢呼印中亲密友谊的口号。公共建筑物也正在披上鲜花和旗帜的盛装。
    有三千多名学生正在根据“国民锻炼计划”进行演习，以便进行体育表演来欢迎周恩来总理。
    全国各地的人民团体也在积极地准备着参加欢迎中国总理的群众集会。印度铁路当局准备增开许多次专车载运从旁遮普和北方省等附近地区前来德里的人。
    在孟买、马德拉斯和加尔各答，也已经安排了欢迎周恩来总理的公众集会和盛大宴会。印中友好协会全国委员会预定在德里和孟买举行欢迎周总理的招待会，还将组织欢迎他的宴会。
