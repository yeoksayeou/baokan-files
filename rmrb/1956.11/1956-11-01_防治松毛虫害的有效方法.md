Title: Effective Methods for Preventing and Controlling Pine Caterpillar Pests
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 3

The Forest Management Bureau of the Ministry of Forestry conducted an initial experiment in the Dabie Mountains of Henan Province this autumn using (26)111-A insecticidal smoke to kill *Pinus massoniana* pine caterpillars. The results of the experiment proved that this method is very effective in killing young pine caterpillars.

The pine caterpillar is the most damaging pest to forests in our country, with a large area of occurrence and rapid reproduction, making prevention and control difficult. The successful experiment of using insecticidal smoke to kill pine caterpillars is of great significance for protecting the country's forest resources.

### 防治松毛虫害的有效方法

1956-11-01
第3版()
专栏：

    防治松毛虫害的有效方法
    本报讯　林业部森林经营局从今年秋季在河南省大别山区初次进行了（二六）一一一A杀虫烟雾剂杀灭马尾松毛虫的试验。试验结果证明，使用这种方法杀灭幼龄期的松毛虫很有效。
    松毛虫是我国森林的为害最大的害虫，发生面积大、繁殖快，而防治又很困难。利用杀虫烟雾剂杀灭松毛虫的试验成功，对于保护祖国森林资源有重要意义。
