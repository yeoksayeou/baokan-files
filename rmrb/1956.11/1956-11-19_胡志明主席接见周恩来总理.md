Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-11-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and He Long, Qiao Guanhua, Zhang Yan, and Gong Peng, who accompanied Zhou Enlai on his visit to Vietnam, at the Presidential Palace from 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM today.

Present at the reception were Pham Van Dong, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Phan Ke Toai, Deputy Premier and Minister of the Interior; Vo Nguyen Giap, Deputy Premier and Minister of National Defense; Nguyen Duy Trinh, Secretary-General of the Presidential Palace; and Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Also present were Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Hoang Van Hoan, Vietnamese Ambassador to China.

### 胡志明主席接见周恩来总理

1956-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见周恩来总理
    新华社河内18日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在今天下午三点到三点三十分在主席府接见了中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和随同周恩来访问越南的贺龙、乔冠华、张彦、龚澎。
    接见时在座的有越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长范文同、副总理兼内务部长潘继遂、副总理兼国防部长武元甲、主席府秘书长阮维祯、外交部副部长雍文谦。
    中国驻越南大使罗贵波和越南驻中国大使黄文欢也在座。
