Title: National Congress of Active Workers in Popular Science for Employees Concludes: Further Clarifying the Guidelines for Popular Science Work: To Be More Closely Integrated with Production and Practice, and to Strive for Breadth

Author:

Date: 1956-11-05
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The first National Congress of Active Workers in Popular Science and Technology for Employees concluded today, the 3rd. Mathematician Hua Luogeng introduced his methods of diligent self-study at the meeting on the 3rd. During the six days from the opening of the congress on October 29th to its closing today, fifty-one representatives of active workers who spoke at the meeting and thirty-three representatives of active workers who submitted written speeches introduced their experiences, gains, and moving deeds in learning and popularizing scientific and technological knowledge, and organizing popular science work. They also conveyed the urgent calls of the broad masses of employees in various places to learn scientific and technological knowledge. The congress successively listened to reports and special lectures by Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Mao Yisheng, Vice Chairman of the National Science Popularization Association; Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Qian Xuesen, a famous expert in aerodynamics; Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation; Hua Luogeng, a famous mathematician; and Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. These reports and lectures clarified the important significance of popularizing scientific and technological knowledge among male and female employees for production and construction and the people's lives, the relationship between popular science work and scientific research, and the steps towards marching towards science, and introduced the new achievements of science and technology in the world.

The congress further clarified that the guidelines for popular science work are: it must be more closely integrated with production, combined with practice, and strive for breadth.

On the afternoon of the 2nd, more than a thousand representatives attending the congress unanimously passed a protest resolutely protesting the armed invasion of Egypt by Britain and France. The representatives expressed their willingness to contribute all their strength to support Egypt.

Today, the congress passed a letter to employees across the country, calling on male and female employees to work hard to learn scientific and technological knowledge.

After the closing of the congress, the representatives will also hold symposiums with relevant government departments and scientific organizations in Beijing, visit factories and scientific research institutions, and tour places of interest.

### 全国职工科普工作积极分子大会闭幕  进一步明确科普工作的方针是：更加密切结合生产和实际，力求广泛

1956-11-05
第7版()
专栏：

    全国职工科普工作积极分子大会闭幕
    进一步明确科普工作的方针是：更加密切结合生产和实际，力求广泛
    新华社3日讯　全国第一次职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会3日闭幕。数学家华罗庚在3日的会上介绍了他刻苦自学的方法。大会从10月29日开幕到今天闭幕的六天中，在会上发言的五十一个积极分子代表和提出书面发言的三十三个积极分子代表介绍了他们学习和普及科学技术知识、组织科学技术普及工作的经验、收获和动人事迹，并且转达了各地广大职工迫切要求学习科学技术知识的呼声，大会先后听取了全国总工会主席赖若愚、国务院副总理李富春、全国科普协会副主席茅以升、中国科学院副院长竺可桢、著名空气动力学家钱学森、全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、著名数学家华罗庚和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实的报告和专题讲演。这些报告和讲演阐明了向男女职工普及科学技术知识对生产建设和人民生活的重要意义、科学技术普及工作同科学研究的关系以及向科学进军的步骤等，并且介绍了世界上科学技术的新成就。
    大会进一步明确了科学技术普及工作的方针是：必须更加密切结合生产，结合实际，力求广泛。
    2日下午，参加大会的一千多个代表一致通过了坚决抗议英法武装侵略埃及的抗议书。代表们表示愿意为支援埃及贡献一切力量。
    今天，大会通过了给全国职工的一封信，号召男女职工努力学习科学技术知识。
    大会闭幕以后，代表们还要在北京分别同政府有关部门和科学团体举行座谈会，参观工厂和科学研究机构，游览名胜。
