Title: The Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units Reach Agreement on Peaceful Neutrality

Author: 
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd - According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio today: The Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units reached an agreement on the issue of peaceful neutrality yesterday. This is the initial result of the political delegations of both sides meeting since September 25th.

### 老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队  就和平中立问题达成协议

1956-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队
    就和平中立问题达成协议
    新华社河内3日电　据万象电台今天广播：老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队已经在昨天就和平中立问题达成了协议。这是双方政治代表团从9月25日开会以来的初步结果。
