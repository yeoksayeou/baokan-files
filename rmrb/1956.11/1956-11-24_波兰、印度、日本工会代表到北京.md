Title: Polish, Indian, and Japanese Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Polish Seamen's Union delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 21st. The delegation of four was led by Kowalski Stanislav, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Polish Seamen's Union. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Seamen's Union.
　　The delegation includes experts and advanced producers. They will visit ports throughout China and disseminate their advanced experiences.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Two representatives of the All-India Food, Tobacco, and Hotel Workers Federation arrived in Beijing on the 20th. The two representatives are: Saksena, representative of the Indian Sugar Industry Union, and Nankia, General Secretary of the Indian Food and Railway Supply Personnel Union. They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Light Industry Union, after visiting the Soviet Union.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. The Japanese Coal Miners' Union delegation, invited by the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train this morning, led by Koga Sadamu (團長古贺定), Sato Hideo (副团长佐藤英雄), Katsuno Noboru (胜野登), and Secretary-General Shitaomi Hirofumi (秘书长下见弘法).

### 波兰、印度、日本工会代表到北京

1956-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰、印度、日本工会代表到北京
    新华社21日讯　波兰海员工会代表团在21日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团一行四人，由波兰海员工会全国委员会副主席柯瓦尔斯基·斯坦尼斯拉夫率领。他们是应中国海员工会邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    代表团中有专家和先进生产者。他们将到我国各地海港访问，并将传播他们的先进经验。
    新华社21日讯　全印食品、烟草、饭店工人联合会代表团代表两人，已在20日到北京。这两位代表是：印度糖业工会代表萨克森纳、印度食品及铁路供应人员工会总书记南基亚。他们是应中国轻工业工会全国委员会的邀请，在访问苏联以后，来中国访问的。
    新华社22日讯　应中国煤矿工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的日本煤矿工会代表团，在团长古贺定，副团长佐藤英雄、胜野登，秘书长下见弘法的率领下，在今天早上乘火车到达北京。
