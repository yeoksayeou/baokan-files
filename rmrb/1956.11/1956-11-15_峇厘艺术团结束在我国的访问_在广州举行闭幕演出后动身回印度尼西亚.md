Title: Balinese Art Troupe Concludes Visit to China, Departs for Indonesia After Closing Performance in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 14th. The Indonesian Balinese Art Friendship Delegation, after a two-month visit and performance tour in nine major cities in China, left Guangzhou today to return home. Last night, the Indonesian artists held a closing ceremony and their final performance of this visit at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Guangzhou.

At the closing ceremony, Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang and delegation leader Saleh Umar spoke successively. Afterwards, the Indonesian artists performed eleven wonderful programs for more than 5,000 spectators.

### 峇厘艺术团结束在我国的访问  在广州举行闭幕演出后动身回印度尼西亚

1956-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    峇厘艺术团结束在我国的访问
    在广州举行闭幕演出后动身回印度尼西亚
    据新华社广州14日电　印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团，在我国九个大城市进行了两个月的访问和演出后，今天离开广州回国。昨天晚上，印度尼西亚艺术家们在广州市中山纪念堂举行了这次访问的闭幕式和最后一场演出。
    在闭幕式上，广州市市长朱光和访问团团长萨勒·乌玛尔先后讲话。接着，印度尼西亚艺术家们为五千多观众演出了十一个精采的节目。
