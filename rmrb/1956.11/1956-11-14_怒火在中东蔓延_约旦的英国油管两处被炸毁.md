Title: Fury Spreads in the Middle East: Two Sections of British Pipeline in Jordan Destroyed
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 13th: Two sections of the Iraq Petroleum Company's pipeline passing through Jordanian territory were blown up on the evening of November 12th. This pipeline runs from Kirkuk in Iraq to the Israeli port of Haifa.

The explosions occurred about eight miles from Irbid in northern Jordan. The explosions caused fires.

### 怒火在中东蔓延  约旦的英国油管两处被炸毁

1956-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    怒火在中东蔓延
    约旦的英国油管两处被炸毁
    新华社13日讯　安曼消息：通过约旦国境的伊拉克石油公司的油管在11月12日晚有两处被炸毁。这条油管是从伊拉克的克尔库尔通到以色列港口海法的。
    爆炸地点离约旦北部的伊尔比德约八英里。爆炸后起了火。
