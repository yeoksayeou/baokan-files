Economic Aid Agreement Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal

    The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, in order to promote friendly relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal and strengthen the friendship between the peoples of the two countries, have, on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence (Panchsheel), concluded this Agreement, the articles of which are as follows:

    Article 1 The People's Republic of China shall, within three years after the signing and entry into force of this Agreement, provide the Kingdom of Nepal with gratuitous assistance of 60 million Indian Rupees. Of the 60 million Indian Rupees, one-third shall be given in cash in installments, and two-thirds shall be given in machinery, equipment, materials and other commodities needed by the Kingdom of Nepal and available from the People's Republic of China. The machinery, equipment, materials and other commodities to be provided as assistance shall be agreed upon separately by the two governments.

    Article 2 The economic assistance provided by the Government of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal shall be without any conditions and without sending technical personnel to Nepal. The funds and materials provided as assistance shall be used entirely freely by the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, and the Government of the People's Republic of China shall not interfere.

    Article 3 The implementing agencies of this Agreement shall be the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China on the Chinese side, and the Ministry of Planning and Development of the Kingdom of Nepal on the Nepalese side.

    Article 4 This Agreement shall enter into force on the date of signature.

    This Agreement was signed in Beijing on October 7, 1956, in duplicate, each in Chinese, Nepali and English, all three texts being equally authentic.

    For the Government of the People's Republic of China
    Minister of Foreign Trade
    Ye Jizhuang (signature)

    For the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal
    Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Nepal to the People's Republic of China
    Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana (signature)

    Exchange of Letters between China and Nepal on the Phased Disbursement of Cash under Economic Assistance

    (I) Note from Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, to Lieutenant General Rana, Nepalese Ambassador to China

    Your Excellency:

    In accordance with the provisions of Article 1 of the "Economic Aid Agreement between The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal," the People's Republic of China will provide the Kingdom of Nepal with 20 million Indian Rupees in cash in installments.

    I have the honor to propose that 10 million Indian Rupees in cash be provided in the first quarter of the 1956-57 fiscal year and another 10 million Indian Rupees in cash be provided in the first quarter of the 1957-58 fiscal year. If Your Excellency agrees, please confirm.

    Please accept the assurances of my highest consideration.

    To
    His Excellency Lieutenant General Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana

### 中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府经济援助协定

1956-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府经济援助协定
    中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府，为了促进中华人民共和国和尼泊尔王国之间的友好关系和加强两国人民的友谊，在和平共处的五项原则（潘查希拉）的基础上，签订本协定，条文如下：
    第一条　中华人民共和国在本协定签字生效后的三年内，无偿地援助尼泊尔王国六千万印度卢比。在六千万印度卢比中，三分之一分期给予现汇，三分之二给予尼泊尔王国所需要的和中华人民共和国可能供应的机器、设备、材料和其他商品。援助的机器、设备、材料和其他商品，由两国政府另行商定。
    第二条　中华人民共和国政府给予尼泊尔王国的经济援助不附带任何条件和不派遣技术人员前往尼泊尔。援助的款项和物资完全由尼泊尔王国政府自由使用，中华人民共和国政府不加干涉。
    第三条　本协定的执行机构，中华人民共和国方面是中华人民共和国对外贸易部；尼泊尔王国方面是尼泊尔王国计划和发展部。
    第四条　本协定自签字之日起生效。
    本协定于1956年10月7日在北京签订，共两份，每份都用中文、尼泊尔文和英文写成，三种文本具有同等效力。
