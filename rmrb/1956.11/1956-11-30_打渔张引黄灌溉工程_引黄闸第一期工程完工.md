Title: Dayuzhang Yellow River Diversion Irrigation Project: First Phase of Yellow River Diversion Sluice Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-11-30
Page: 3

    Dayuzhang Yellow River Diversion Irrigation Project
    First Phase of Yellow River Diversion Sluice Completed

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 27th: The first phase of construction of the Yellow River diversion sluice at the head of the Dayuzhang Yellow River Diversion Irrigation Project in Shandong Province was completed on the 25th and will soon release water for irrigation.

    The Yellow River diversion sluice is 89.6 meters long and 9 meters high, with a diversion capacity of 120 cubic meters per second under normal low water levels of the Yellow River. This phase of construction started in early April this year. In addition to the Yellow River diversion sluice, there are more than 6,200 medium and small-sized buildings, including the main canal hub, diversion sluice, control sluice, and bridges. The completed earthwork for this phase is over 18 million cubic meters, which can irrigate more than 400,000 mu of farmland.

### 打渔张引黄灌溉工程  引黄闸第一期工程完工

1956-11-30
第3版()
专栏：

    打渔张引黄灌溉工程
    引黄闸第一期工程完工
    据新华社济南27日电　山东省境内打渔张引黄灌溉工程的渠首引黄闸第一期建筑工程25日完工，即将放水浇地。
    这座引黄闸全长八十九点六公尺，高九公尺，引水量在黄河常年枯水位的情况下为一百二十秒公方。这期建筑工程是在今年4月初开工的，它除了引黄闸以外，还有总干渠枢纽、引水闸、节制闸、桥梁等中小型建筑物六千二百多座。这一期工程完成的土方有一千八百多万公方，可以灌溉四十多万亩田。
