Title: Premier Zhou Holds Farewell Reception in Phnom Penh; Chairman of the Cambodian International Supervisory Committee Hosts Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1956-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 27th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China held a grand farewell reception at the Government Building in Phnom Penh at 9:00 PM on November 26th.

More than 400 people attended the reception, including Prince Sihanouk, Prince Penn Nouth, Prime Minister San Yun, and almost all senior officials of the Royal Government of Cambodia, as well as diplomatic representatives and members of economic delegations from various countries stationed in Phnom Penh.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Sihanouk delivered speeches at the reception (see page 4). After the reception, a Chinese film was screened. The reception proceeded in a very warm and friendly atmosphere and did not end until after 1:00 AM.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 26th. The Chairman of the Cambodian International Supervisory and Inspection Committee, K.E. Dass, held a cocktail party for Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to the Kingdom of Cambodia at 7:00 PM on November 26th. Prime Minister San Yun and senior government officials of the Kingdom of Cambodia, as well as envoys from various countries stationed in Phnom Penh and people from all walks of life in Phnom Penh, attended the cocktail party. The cocktail party proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理在金边举行告别招待会  柬埔寨国际监察委员会主席招待周总理

1956-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在金边举行告别招待会
    柬埔寨国际监察委员会主席招待周总理
    新华社金边27日电　中华人民共和国总理周恩来在11月26日晚上九时假金边政府大厦举行盛大的告别招待会。
    出席招待会的有西哈努克亲王、宾努亲王、桑云首相和几乎全部柬埔寨王国政府的高级官员，各国驻金边的外交代表、经济代表团团员等四百多人。
    周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王在招待会上先后讲了话（见第四版）。会后放映了中国电影。招待会在十分热烈友好的空气中进行，直到深夜一点以后才结束。
    新华社金边26日电　柬埔寨国际监督和监察委员会主席克·恩·达斯为周恩来总理访问柬埔寨王国在11月26日晚上七时举行酒会。出席酒会的有柬埔寨王国政府首相桑云和政府高级官员，以及各国驻金边的使节和金边各界人士。酒会在友好的气氛中进行。
