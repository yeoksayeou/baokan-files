Tibetan Sakya Proverbs
　　The hard-earned achievements of kind-hearted people,
　　Can be destroyed completely by a single word from the wicked;
　　The crops that farmers painstakingly plant year after year,
　　Can be destroyed cleanly by hail in an instant.
　　　　　＊
　　Ignorant people are single-mindedly seeking personal ease,
　　But the result is always pain and suffering;
　　Isn’t it seen that people plagued by illness,
　　Harm their own lives in order to escape suffering?!
　　　　　＊
　　Horseshoe prints are easily filled with water,
　　Small warehouses are easily filled with belongings,
　　Narrow gardens are easily sown with seeds,
　　Narrow-minded people are easily intoxicated by a little knowledge.
　　　　　＊
　　People without learning are especially jealous of scholars;
　　People even consider the seedlings growing on the snow mountains in winter to be an ominous sign!
　　　　　　　　　　Wang Yao, selected and translated

### 西藏萨迦格言

1956-11-19
第8版()
专栏：

    西藏萨迦格言
    善心的人们辛辛苦苦的成绩，
    恶人用一句话就能破坏无余；
    农民们成年累月辛苦种的庄稼，
    冰雹用一刹那工夫能毁灭干净。
          ＊
    愚昧的人一心在寻求个人安乐，
    结果却都是得到痛苦和痛苦；
    不是见到被病魔纠缠的人们，
    为了摆脱痛苦而戕害了自己生命？！
          ＊
    马蹄痕印很容易被水灌满，
    小小仓库很容易被财物装满，
    狭狭田园很容易被种籽撒满，
    量窄的人很容易被一点知识所陶醉。
          ＊
    没有学问的人们，对学者特别嫉恨；
    雪山冬天长出的禾苗，人们还认为是不吉之兆哩！
                        王尧选译
