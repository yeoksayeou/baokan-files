Title: Syria Announces Severing Diplomatic Relations with Britain and France; Libyan King Issues State of Emergency Decree Nationwide; All Arab Heads of State Agree to Hold Emergency Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd dispatch: Damascus dispatch: The Syrian government announced on November 2nd the severing of diplomatic relations with Britain and France due to the Anglo-French aggression against Egypt. The Syrian Foreign Ministry summoned the British and French diplomatic envoys in Damascus on the afternoon of November 2nd and notified them of this decision by the Syrian government.
　　
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd dispatch: According to TASS, Damascus, November 3rd: The Syrian cabinet passed a resolution: to appoint Syrian Army Chief of Staff Marshal Nizam al-Din as Commander-in-Chief of the National Armed Forces; to recall the Syrian ambassadors to London and Paris; and to take necessary measures to strengthen the country's air defense capabilities.
　　The above decisions were made when the Syrian cabinet discussed the political situation caused by the British, French, and Israeli attack on Egypt and measures to strengthen the country's defense capabilities.
　　
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd dispatch: According to TASS, Tripoli, November 2nd: The King of Libya issued a decree on November 2nd placing the entire country in a state of emergency.
　　
According to a broadcast by Tripoli Radio, Libyan Prime Minister Halim, after British Foreign Secretary Lloyd guaranteed that British troops and military bases in Libya would not be used against Egypt, requested the British command, through the British Ambassador to Libya, to allow Libyan military observers to inspect British military bases within Libya in order to supervise Britain's fulfillment of its promise. Halim said he hoped Britain would respond to this request no later than November 2nd.
　　
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd dispatch: Beirut dispatch: A Lebanese Foreign Ministry spokesman said on the evening of November 2nd that all Arab Kings and Heads of State had accepted Lebanese President Chamoun's invitation to convene an emergency meeting to study the situation caused by the attack on Egypt.

### 叙利亚宣布同英法两国断绝邦交  利比亚国王颁布全国处于紧急状态令  全部阿拉伯国家元首同意开紧急会议

1956-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚宣布同英法两国断绝邦交
    利比亚国王颁布全国处于紧急状态令
    全部阿拉伯国家元首同意开紧急会议
    新华社3日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚政府在11月2日宣布同英国和法国断绝外交关系，因为英法侵略埃及。叙利亚外交部在2日下午召见了英国和法国驻大马士革的外交使节，把叙利亚政府的这项决定通知了他们。
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社大马士革3日讯：叙利亚内阁通过决议：任命叙利亚军队总参谋长尼扎姆丁元帅为国家武装部队总司令；撤回叙利亚驻伦敦和巴黎大使；采取必要措施加强国家的防空力量。
    上述决定是在叙利亚内阁讨论由于英国、法国和以色列进攻埃及所引起的政治局势和加强国家防御力量措施的时候作出的。
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社的黎波里2日讯：利比亚国王11月2日颁布了全国处于紧急状态的法令。
    据的黎波里电台广播，利比亚首相哈利姆在英国外交大臣劳埃德保证不使用英国在利比亚的军队和军事基地去反对埃及之后，通过英国驻利比亚大使要求英国司令部准许利比亚军事观察员视察利比亚境内的英国军事基地，以便监督英国履行自己的诺言。哈利姆说，希望英国最晚不迟于11月2日答复这个要求。
    据新华社3日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩外交部发言人11月2日晚上说，所有阿拉伯国家的国王和元首都接受了黎巴嫩总统夏蒙关于召开紧急会议来研究由于进攻埃及所引起的局势问题的邀请。
