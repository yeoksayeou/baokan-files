Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Tan Yunshan
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- Chairman Mao Zedong received Tan Yunshan, Dean of the China Academy of India International University, and his daughter this afternoon.

    Chairman Liu Shaoqi was also present at the reception.

### 毛泽东主席接见谭云山

1956-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见谭云山
    新华社11日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了印度国际大学中国学院院长谭云山和他的女儿。
    接见的时候，刘少奇委员长也在座。
