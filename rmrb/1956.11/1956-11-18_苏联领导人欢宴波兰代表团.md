Title: Soviet Leaders Host Banquet for Polish Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 17th: Khrushchev, Voroshilov, and Bulganin hosted a breakfast banquet in the Kremlin on November 16th to welcome the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee and the Polish government delegation that is currently visiting Moscow.
    The Polish side attending the banquet included: Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and Jędrychowski.
    The Soviet side attending as hosts included: Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Zhukov, Brezhnev, Belyayev, Furtseva, and Pospelov, as well as heads of various departments of the Soviet government.
    The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere, with Khrushchev, Gomulka, and Voroshilov offering warm and friendly greetings to each other.
    On the evening of the 16th, the Polish Party and government delegation, accompanied by the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the state, went to the Bolshoi Theatre to watch Tchaikovsky's opera "The Queen of Spades."

### 苏联领导人欢宴波兰代表团

1956-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人欢宴波兰代表团
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：11月16日，赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁在克里姆林宫设早宴招待正在莫斯科访问的波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰政府代表团。
    波兰方面出席宴会的有：哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹和英德里霍夫斯基。
    苏联方面出席作陪的有：卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、朱可夫、勃列日涅夫、别利亚耶夫、福尔采娃和波斯伯洛夫以及苏联政府各部门的负责人。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，赫鲁晓夫、哥穆尔卡和伏罗希洛夫相互亲切地致词。
    16日晚间，波兰党和政府代表团在苏联共产党和国家领导人陪同下，前往苏联大剧院看了柴可夫斯基的歌剧“黑桃皇后”。
