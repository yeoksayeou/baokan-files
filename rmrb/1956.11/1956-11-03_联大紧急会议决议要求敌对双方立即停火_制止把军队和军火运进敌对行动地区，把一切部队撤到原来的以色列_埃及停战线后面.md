Title: UN General Assembly Emergency Session Resolution Demands Immediate Ceasefire by Hostile Parties
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 1

    Immediately Stop the Anglo-French Governments' Aggression Against Egypt!
    UN General Assembly Emergency Session Resolution Demands Immediate Ceasefire by Hostile Parties
    Stop Transporting Troops and Military Supplies into Areas of Hostile Action, Withdraw All Troops to the Original Israel-Egypt Armistice Line
    Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd dispatch from New York: The United Nations General Assembly Emergency Session continued its meeting on the evening of November 1st. The meeting passed by an overwhelming majority the U.S. proposal demanding an immediate ceasefire by all hostile parties. This proposal stipulates stopping the transport of troops and military supplies into areas of hostile action, and demands the immediate withdrawal of all troops to behind the armistice line stipulated by the Israel-Egypt Armistice Agreement.
    The results of the General Assembly vote: Sixty-four votes in favor of the U.S. proposal, including the United States, the Soviet Union, India, Yugoslavia, Sweden, Latin American countries, Asian-African countries, and other countries. Those opposed were France, Britain, Israel, Australia, and New Zealand. Abstentions included Belgium, Canada, Laos, the Netherlands, Portugal, and the Union of South Africa.
    Before the vote, representatives of many countries spoke out to condemn the aggressive actions of Britain, France, and Israel. (See page 7 for details of the UN General Assembly meeting)

### 联大紧急会议决议要求敌对双方立即停火  制止把军队和军火运进敌对行动地区，把一切部队撤到原来的以色列—埃及停战线后面

1956-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    立即制止英法政府对埃及的侵略！
    联大紧急会议决议要求敌对双方立即停火
    制止把军队和军火运进敌对行动地区，把一切部队撤到原来的以色列—埃及停战线后面
    据新华社2日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会紧急会议11月1日晚上继续举行会议。会议以绝大多数票通过了要求敌对各方立即停火的美国提案，这个提案规定制止把军队和军火运进敌对行动地区，并且要求立即把一切部队撤到以色列—埃及停战协定所规定的停战线后面。
    大会表决结果：赞成美国提案的有六十四票，其中包括美国、苏联、印度、南斯拉夫、瑞典、拉丁美洲国家、亚非国家以及其他国家。反对的有法国、英国、以色列、澳大利亚和新西兰。弃权的有比利时、加拿大、老挝、荷兰、葡萄牙和南非联邦。
    在表决前，许多国家的代表都发言谴责英、法和以色列的侵略行为。（联大会议详情见第七版）
