Title: Soviet Circus Opens in Beijing to Enthusiastic Applause and Laughter
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: The Soviet Circus held its opening performance at the Beijing Gymnasium tonight.

The Beijing Gymnasium, which seats over six thousand, added many temporary seats tonight, yet was still filled to capacity, with many people standing in the aisles to watch.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing said in his opening address that Soviet circus art is not only popular among the broad masses of people in the Soviet Union, but also enjoys a high reputation in many countries. The Chinese people, regardless of age or gender, are extremely interested in the Soviet Circus. In recent days, Beijing citizens have been lining up in long queues to buy tickets, some even arriving late at night to wait until dawn. To meet the enthusiastic demands of the audience, the Soviet Circus has agreed to increase the number of performances in Beijing.

Soviet Circus Director Asanov then gave a reply speech. He said: We are looking forward to meeting the Chinese audience with great excitement. During this return visit performance, mutual learning and exchange of experiences with our Chinese counterparts will further enrich the acrobatic and circus arts of the Soviet and Chinese people. Asanov said: All members of our troupe, like all the Soviet people, are filled with love and brotherly friendship for the great Chinese people.

Amidst warm applause, the artists of the circus began their wonderful performances, presenting fourteen acts tonight. These included thrilling lion and bear acts, high-altitude performances, and trampoline acts rarely seen by Chinese audiences; amusing dog training; as well as tightrope walking, ladder acrobatics, strength acrobatics, equestrianism, and martial arts. These acts, and the comic performances interspersed between the acts, repeatedly evoked enthusiastic applause and laughter.

Among those who attended the performance at the gymnasium tonight were Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Culture Qian Junrui, Xia Yan, Chen Kehan, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Bao Erhan, and Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang.

U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, who is currently visiting China, and his wife and entourage, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Li Haiqiu, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries, also attended the performance.

### 苏联马戏团在京举行开幕演出  精彩的表演不断引起观众热烈的掌声和笑声

1956-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联马戏团在京举行开幕演出
    精彩的表演不断引起观众热烈的掌声和笑声
    新华社1日讯　苏联马戏团今天晚上在北京体育馆举行开幕演出。
    拥有六千多个座位的北京体育馆今晚增加了许多临时座位，仍然座无虚席，许多人站在走道上观看。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在开幕式上致词说，苏联的马戏艺术不仅在苏联受到广大人民的欢迎，而且在许多国家享有盛誉。我国人民，不论男女老少，对苏联马戏感到极大的兴趣。几天来北京市民排着长长的行列等候买票，有的甚至深夜赶来等到天亮。为了满足观众的热烈要求，苏联马戏团已经同意增加在北京演出的场数。
    苏联马戏团团长阿萨诺夫接着致答词。他说：我们怀着极其激动的心情，期待着同中国观众会见。我们这次答访演出期间同中国同行的相互学习和经验交流，将使苏、中两国人民的杂技和马戏艺术更加丰富起来。阿萨诺夫说：我们全团人员和全体苏联人民一样，对伟大的中国人民充满热爱和兄弟般的友情。
    在热烈的掌声中，马戏团的艺术家们开始了精彩的表演，他们今天晚上表演了十四个节目。这里面有中国观众很少看到过的惊险的耍狮、熊、高空表演、蹦床；趣味横生的驯狗；以及走钢丝、梯技、力量杂耍、马术、武术等。这些节目和在节目表演空隙时间加演的滑稽表演，都不止一次地引起热烈的掌声和笑声。
    今晚到体育馆观看表演的，有国务院副总理贺龙、李富春、李先念、秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，文化部副部长钱俊瑞、夏衍、陈克寒、张致祥，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章等人。
    正在中国访问的缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努及夫人和随行人员，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员等也出席观看了表演。
