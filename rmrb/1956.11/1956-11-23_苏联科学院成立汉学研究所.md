Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Establishes Institute of Sinology
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to the Soviet "Literary Gazette," the Institute of Sinology of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has recently been established in Moscow. At present, the institute is busy formulating its work plan for next year and its five-year plan for the future.

This institute will study the achievements and experiences of China's socialist construction.

"Literary Gazette" stated that studying the various activities of the Chinese people will not only expand our understanding of this great country but will also provide rich data for solving universal scientific problems related to the history of the development of all human society.

The History Department of the institute will study Chinese history from ancient times to modern times. Soviet economists will study China's national economic issues. The Department of State Construction will pay special attention to studying China's modern social reforms and its international relations. Linguists will study Chinese national languages. The institute will publish scientific works on these issues. The Department of Literature and Culture of Chinese Nationalities will introduce to the Soviet scientific community and the general readership ancient and modern literature and social science ideas created by the Chinese people over thousands of years.

The institute has a special department responsible for translating and publishing Chinese ancient and modern historical, economic, socio-political science, and literary books and documents. In addition, a scientific intelligence department and an editing and publishing department will be established. Starting next year, the institute plans to publish a specialized Soviet journal on Sinology issues.

### 苏联科学院成立汉学研究所

1956-11-23
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院成立汉学研究所
    新华社莫斯科讯　据苏联“文学报”报道：苏联科学院汉学研究所最近已经在莫斯科成立。目前，研究所正在忙于制定明年的工作计划和今后的五年计划。
    这个研究所将研究中国社会主义建设的成就和经验。
    “文学报”说，研究中国人民的各种活动不仅将扩大我们对这个伟大国家的了解，而且也必将为解决同全人类社会发展史有关的普遍性的科学问题提供丰富的资料。
    研究所的历史部将研究中国古代一直到近代的历史。苏联经济学家将研究中国的国民经济问题。国家建设部将特别注意研究中国现代的社会改革和它的国际关系。语言学家将研究中国的民族语言。研究所将就这些问题发表科学著作。中国各民族文学和文化部将向苏联科学界和广大的读者介绍中国人民在几千年中创造的文艺和社会科学思想方面的古代文献和现代文献。
    研究所有专门部门担任翻译和发表中国古代和现代历史、经济、社会政治科学和文艺书籍文献的工作。此外，还将建立科学情报部门和编辑出版部门。从明年开始，这个研究所准备出版一种苏联研究汉学问题的专门杂志。
