Title: Afghan Prime Minister Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-11-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 31st. Afghan Prime Minister Daoud and his entourage left Moscow by plane today.

Soviet state leaders Bulganin, Kaganovich, Zhukov, and Shepilov went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Daoud and others will return home via Tashkent.

### 阿富汗首相回国

1956-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相回国
    新华社莫斯科31日电　阿富汗首相达乌德和他的随行人员在今天乘飞机离开莫斯科。
    苏联国家领导人布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、朱可夫和谢皮洛夫等到机场欢送贵宾。
    达乌德等将取道塔什干回国。
