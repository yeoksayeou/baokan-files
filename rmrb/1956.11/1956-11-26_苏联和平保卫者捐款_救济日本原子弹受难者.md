Title: Soviet Peace Defenders Donate Funds to Aid Japanese Atomic Bomb Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 6

    Soviet Peace Defenders Donate Funds
    to Aid Japanese Atomic Bomb Victims
    (Xinhua News Agency) The Presidium of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, on behalf of the Soviet peace defenders, has decided to donate 80,000 rubles as a fund to aid Japanese atomic bomb victims.
    The Presidium of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee has also decided to donate 15,000 rubles in the name of the Soviet peace defenders as a fund for activities commemorating Ikuo Oyama.

### 苏联和平保卫者捐款  救济日本原子弹受难者

1956-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联和平保卫者捐款
    救济日本原子弹受难者
    据新华社讯　苏联保卫和平委员会主席团代表苏联和平保卫者，决定捐献八万卢布作为救济日本原子弹受难者的基金。
    苏联保卫和平委员会主席团还决定以苏联和平保卫者的名义捐献一万五千卢布作为纪念大山郁夫活动基金。
