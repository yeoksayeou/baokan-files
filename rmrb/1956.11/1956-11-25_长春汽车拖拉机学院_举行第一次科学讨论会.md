Title: Changchun Automotive Tractor College Holds First Scientific Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency Report: Changchun Automotive Tractor College held its first scientific discussion meeting in mid-November. In a paper, Associate Professor Zhang Hua proposed a device that can correct the steering of cars, reduce vibration, and improve the existing problems in the current car structure. The paper "Calculation and Analysis of Heavy-Duty Vehicle Frames" by young teacher Huang Tianze proposed a new calculation method for reducing the weight of frames. Professor Dai Guirui introduced his design of a "Agricultural Internal Combustion Water Pump" which he made in his spare time. This "Agricultural Internal Combustion Water Pump" has a simple structure, large water pumping and drainage volume, low water pumping cost, and is convenient for farmers to use.

During the discussion, everyone offered amendments and exchanged experiences in scientific theoretical research and practical work.

In addition to teachers from the Changchun Automotive Tractor College, researchers, professors, and engineers from scientific research institutions, universities, and the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant in Changchun, Beijing, Harbin, and other places also attended the scientific discussion meeting, and they also presented scientific reports at the meeting.

### 长春汽车拖拉机学院  举行第一次科学讨论会

1956-11-25
第7版()
专栏：

    长春汽车拖拉机学院
    举行第一次科学讨论会
    新华社讯　本月中旬举行的长春汽车拖拉机学院第一次科学讨论会上，张华副教授在一篇论文中提出用一种装置可使汽车转向正确，减少震动，改进了目前汽车结构上存在的问题。青年教师黄天泽的“载重汽车车架计算及其分析”论文，提出了关于减轻车架重量的新的计算方法。戴桂蕊教授介绍了他利用课余时间所作的“农用内燃水泵”的设计。这种“农用内燃水泵”构造简单，抽水排水量大，抽水成本低廉，便于农民运用。
    讨论中，大家提出了修正意见，交流了科学理论研究和实际工作的经验。
    参加这次科学讨论会的除长春汽车拖拉机学院教师外，还有长春、北京、哈尔滨等地的科学研究机关、大专学校和第一汽车制造厂的研究人员、教授和工程师，他们也在会上提出了科学报告。
