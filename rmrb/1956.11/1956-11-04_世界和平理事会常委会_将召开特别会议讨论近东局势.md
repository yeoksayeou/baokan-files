Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee to Convene Special Meeting to Discuss Near East Situation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 3rd, TASS, Vienna, November 2nd: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council sent a telegram to all members of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. The telegram stated that, in view of the very serious situation in the Near East, a special meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will be held in Stockholm on November 8.

### 世界和平理事会常委会  将召开特别会议讨论近东局势

1956-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会
    将召开特别会议讨论近东局势
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社维也纳2日讯：世界和平理事会书记处给世界和平理事会常务委员会全体委员发了一封电报。电报说，鉴于近东局势非常严重，定于11月8日在斯德哥尔摩召开世界和平理事会常务委员会特别会议。
