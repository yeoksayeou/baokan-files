Title: UN Chief Truce Supervisor Protests Again Against Israeli Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jerusalem, 31st -- It was reported that United Nations Chief Truce Supervisor, Major General Burns, on the evening of October 30th, once again protested to Israeli Prime Minister Ben-Gurion regarding Israel's aggression against Egypt.

### 联合国首席停战监督员  再次抗议以色列进攻埃及

1956-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国首席停战监督员
    再次抗议以色列进攻埃及
    据新华社31日讯　耶路撒冷消息：联合国首席停战监察员伯恩斯少将在10月30日晚间就以色列进攻埃及问题，向以色列总理本—古里安再一次提出抗议。
