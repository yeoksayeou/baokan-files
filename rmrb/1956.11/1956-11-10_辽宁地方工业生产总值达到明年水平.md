Title: Liaoning's Local Industrial Output Value Reaches Next Year's Level
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to recent statistics from the Liaoning Provincial Statistics Bureau, the average total output value of more than 1,100 local state-owned, joint state-private, and cooperative factories in Liaoning Province has already reached the level of 1957 ahead of schedule.

Of the 33 major products included in the First Five-Year Plan, the output of 15 products, including double-wheel double-share plows, seeders, and ball bearings, has also reached the 1957 level ahead of schedule.

In the past three years, Liaoning's local industries have turned over more than 350 million yuan in profits to the state.

### 辽宁地方工业生产总值达到明年水平

1956-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    辽宁地方工业生产总值达到明年水平
    据新华社讯　辽宁省统计局最近统计，辽宁全省一千一百多个地方国营、公私合营和合作社营工厂的平均生产总值，已经提前达到1957年的水平。
    列入第一个五年计划的三十三种主要产品，有双轮双铧犁、播种机、滚珠轴承等十五种产品产量，也提前达到1957年的水平。
    在三年多的时间内，辽宁地方工业上缴给国家的利润有三亿五千多万元。
