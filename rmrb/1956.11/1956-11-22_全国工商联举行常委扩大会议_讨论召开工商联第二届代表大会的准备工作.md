Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Holds Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting to Discuss Preparations for the Second National Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-11-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- The First Executive Committee Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce began its Sixth (Enlarged) Meeting on November 19th. Attending the meeting were Chairman Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen Li Zhuchen, Zhang Naiqi, Meng Yongqian, Sheng Pihua, Rong Yiren, Chen Jingshe, Gong Tianmin, standing committee members, and some executive committee members in Beijing, totaling one hundred and fourteen people.
    The main purpose of this Standing Committee meeting is to prepare for the Second National Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce to be held in early December this year, and to discuss the documents and related issues of the Second National Congress.

### 全国工商联举行常委扩大会议  讨论召开工商联第二届代表大会的准备工作

1956-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联举行常委扩大会议
    讨论召开工商联第二届代表大会的准备工作
    据新华社20日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会常务委员会在11月19日开始举行第六次（扩大）会议。出席会议的有主任委员陈叔通、副主任委员李烛尘、章乃器、孟用潜、盛丕华、荣毅仁、陈经畬，巩天民及常务委员，部分在京执行委员等一百一十四人。
    这次常委会主要是为今年12月初召开的全国工商联第二届代表大会作好准备，并且要讨论第二届代表大会的文件及有关问题。
