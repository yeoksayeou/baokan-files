Title: East Germany and West Germany Sign Goods Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-11-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 15th. The government of the German Democratic Republic and representatives of the West German government signed a trade agreement on November 15th stipulating that the total value of goods exchanged between the two sides in 1957 should reach one billion accounting units.

### 民主德国和西德  签订换货协定

1956-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国和西德
    签订换货协定
    新华社柏林15日电　德意志民主共和国政府11月15日和西德政府代表签订了一项贸易协定，规定双方在1957年换货总额要达到十亿计算单位。
