Title: National People's Congress Delegation Continues Visit in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-11-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 23rd: The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation returned to Moscow from Leningrad on the 22nd.

　　On the afternoon of the same day, the delegation visited the Moscow Aircraft Manufacturing Factory. Soviet Minister of Aviation Industry Dementyev and Deputy Minister Belyansky accompanied the delegation. The factory director and chief engineer gave the delegation a detailed introduction to the factory's history and current production situation.

　　After the visit, the delegation attended a welcoming meeting held by all the factory's employees.

　　On the same day, the delegation also visited the Moscow City Soviet of Workers' Deputies. The chairman of the Soviet Executive Committee, Bobrovnikov, received the delegation. He introduced the work of the City Soviet and urban construction, and answered questions raised by the delegation members.

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: According to TASS reports: The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation continued its visits in Moscow on November 23rd. Some members of the delegation visited the All-Union Agricultural Exhibition and the All-Union Industrial Exhibition.

　　Members of the delegation also visited the Moscow City Soviet of Workers' Deputies in groups, visited the Gorki village, the site of the Lenin Museum, and the State Tretyakov Gallery. Some people visited the home of Soviet People's Artist Ulanova.

### 全国人民代表大会代表团继续在莫斯科参观

1956-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表团继续在莫斯科参观
    据新华社莫斯科23日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团22日从列宁格勒回到莫斯科。
    当天下午，代表团参观了莫斯科飞机制造工厂。陪同代表团参观的有苏联航空工业部长杰明捷夫，副部长别利安斯基。这个工厂的厂长和总工程师向代表团详细地介绍了工厂的历史和现在的生产情况。
    参观后，代表团参加了全厂职工为他们举行的欢迎大会。
    当天代表团还访问了莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃。苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫接见了代表团，他介绍了市苏维埃的工作和市政建设，并且回答了代表团员们提出的问题。
    据新华社24日讯　据塔斯社报道：中国全国人民代表大会代表团11月23日继续在莫斯科参观和访问。代表团部分人员参观了全苏农业展览会和全苏工业展览会。
    代表团人员还分批访问了莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃，参观了列宁故居博物馆所在地——高尔克村和国家特列季亚科夫图画陈列馆，有的人到苏联人民演员乌兰诺娃的家里作了客。
