Title: Premier Zhou Watches Performance by Hungarian Army Cultural Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China watched a performance by the Hungarian People's Army Cultural Troupe this evening. Also in attendance were members of the capital's literary and art circles and Soviet experts. Every performance was warmly welcomed by the audience. At the end of the performance, Soviet experts went on stage to present flowers to the Hungarian artists.

After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai met with all the members of the Hungarian People's Army Cultural Troupe and had a cordial conversation with them. (Attached photo)

### 周总理观看匈军文工团演出

1956-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理观看匈军文工团演出
    接见全体团员并且和他们亲切地谈了话
    新华社15日讯　今天晚上，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来观看了匈牙利人民军文工团的演出。在一起观看的还有首都的文艺界人士和苏联专家。演出的每一个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。演出结束，苏联专家上台向匈牙利艺术家们献花。
    在演出以后，周恩来总理接见了匈牙利人民军文工团的全体团员，并且和他们亲切地谈了话。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和匈牙利的艺术家们谈话。　新华社记者　吴化学摄
