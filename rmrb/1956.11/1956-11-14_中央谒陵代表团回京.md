Title: Central Pilgrimage Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Central Pilgrimage Delegation commemorating the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth, headed by delegation leader Zhu De and deputy leader Li Jishen, returned to Beijing from Nanjing by plane today.

The Soviet delegation that came to Nanjing for the pilgrimage went to Shanghai for a visit today, and the guests from the other seven countries have already returned to Beijing by plane.

### 中央谒陵代表团回京

1956-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中央谒陵代表团回京
    新华社13日讯　纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年中央谒陵代表团团长朱德、副团长李济深等今天乘飞机从南京回到北京。
    到南京谒陵的苏联代表团今天从南京去上海参观，其他七个国家的来宾已经乘飞机回到北京。
