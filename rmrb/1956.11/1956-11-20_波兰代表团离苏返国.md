Title: Polish Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Home
Author:
Date: 1956-11-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 19th: According to TASS, the delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee and the Polish government, headed by Gomulka, left Moscow for Warsaw by special train on the evening of the 18th after talks with the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government delegation.

### 波兰代表团离苏返国

1956-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰代表团离苏返国
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以哥穆尔卡为首的波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰政府代表团同苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府代表团会谈以后，已经在18日晚上乘专车离莫斯科回华沙。
