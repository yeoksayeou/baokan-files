Title: We Need Work Experience from Party Organizations in Government Agencies
Author: Lú Shèngyí
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 8
Column: Readers, Authors, Editors

    We Need Work Experience from Party Organizations in Government Agencies

    I do Party work in a government agency and often feel frustrated because Party work in government agencies is not easy to do well, and there are few new changes in work methods, forming an "old routine." Therefore, I urgently need more guidance and need to learn from the work of Party organizations in other government agencies. But in my impression, the People's Daily rarely publishes news and articles in this regard. I hope that in the future, my requests can be appropriately satisfied.
                (Jiangxi　Lú Shèngyí)

### 我们需要机关党组织的工作经验
卢盛仪
1956-11-02
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    我们需要机关党组织的工作经验
    我在机关里做党务工作，常常感到苦恼的是，机关党务工作不易做好，在工作方法上很少有新的变化，形成“老一套”。因此迫切需要更多的指导，需要从其他机关的党组织工作中吸取经验。但在我的印象中，人民日报很少发表这方面的消息和文章。希望今后对我们的要求，能给予适当的满足。
                （江西　卢盛仪）
