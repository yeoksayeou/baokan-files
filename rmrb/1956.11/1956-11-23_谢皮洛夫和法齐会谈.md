Title: Shepilov and Fawzi Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-11-23
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 22nd. Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov met with Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi on the 20th. They held lengthy talks and discussed in detail issues related to both countries.
　　Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Kuznetsov was present at the meeting.

### 谢皮洛夫和法齐会谈

1956-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫和法齐会谈
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫20日会见了埃及外交部长法齐。他们举行了长时间的会谈，详细地讨论了同两国有关的问题。
    会谈时在座的有苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫。
