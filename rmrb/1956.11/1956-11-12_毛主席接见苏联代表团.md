Title: Chairman Mao Receives Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Chairman Mao Zedong received this afternoon the Soviet delegation headed by Mr. Moskatov, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The delegation was invited to Beijing to participate in the activities commemorating the ninetieth anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Mr. Cherepanov, a member of the delegation, served as the chief advisor to the Whampoa Military Academy thirty years ago when Mr. Sun Yat-sen founded it.

### 毛主席接见苏联代表团

1956-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见苏联代表团
    新华社11日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了以苏联共产党中央检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫为首的苏联代表团。代表团是应邀前来北京参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念活动的。代表团团员中的契列班诺夫在三十年前孙中山先生创办黄埔军校时曾任黄埔军校总顾问。
