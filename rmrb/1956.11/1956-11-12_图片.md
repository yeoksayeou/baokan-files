Title: Picture
Author: Lü Houmin
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 5

    Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received all members of the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation to China on November 11. The picture shows a group photo taken during the meeting. The fourth person from the left in the front row is Ali Buzo, head of the Syrian Parliamentary Delegation to China. Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin photographed.
