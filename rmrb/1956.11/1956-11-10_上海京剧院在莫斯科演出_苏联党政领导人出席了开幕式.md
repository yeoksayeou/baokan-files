Title: Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe Performs in Moscow; Soviet Party and Government Leaders Attend Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 3

    Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe Performs in Moscow
    Soviet Party and Government Leaders Attend Opening Ceremony

    (Our Moscow Correspondent, November 8th) The Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe of China held the opening ceremony of its performances at the Stanislavsky and Nemirovich-Danchenko Music Theatre in Moscow on November 8th. Soviet Party and government leaders, including Khrushchev, Molotov, Malenkov, as well as Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedin, and others attended the opening ceremony. Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao and diplomatic envoys from many countries also attended the opening ceremony.

    The opening ceremony was presided over by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov. He congratulated the Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe on its successful performances in Moscow. Dean Zhou Xinfang delivered a speech in reply.

    At the opening ceremony, the Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe performed four excerpts: "Yandang Mountain," "The Drunken Concubine," "Stealing the Immortal Herb," and "Fisherman's Revenge." These performances were warmly welcomed by the audience.

    On November 8th, Zhou Xinfang and others attended the reception held by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the October Revolution.

### 上海京剧院在莫斯科演出  苏联党政领导人出席了开幕式

1956-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    上海京剧院在莫斯科演出
    苏联党政领导人出席了开幕式
    本报莫斯科8日电　中国上海京剧院于11月8日在莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基—丹钦科音乐剧院举行了演出的开幕式。苏联党和政府领导人：赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫、马林科夫和苏联文化部部长米哈依洛夫、外交部副部长费德林等人出席了开幕式。中国驻苏联大使刘晓以及很多国家的外交使节也参加了开幕式。
    开幕式由苏联文化部部长米哈依洛夫主持。他祝贺上海京剧院在莫斯科演出成功。周信芳院长致答词。
    在开幕式上，上海京剧团演出了“雁荡山”、“贵妃醉酒”、“盗仙草”、“打渔杀家”四个节目。这些节目受到了观众们的热烈欢迎。
    11月8日周信芳等出席了苏联文化部部长米哈依洛夫为庆祝十月革命三十九周年的招待会。
