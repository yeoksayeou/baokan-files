Title: Arab Airlines Abrogates Contracts for Hiring British Pilots
Author:
Date: 1956-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, November 14th: Arab Airlines announced on November 13th that it would abrogate the contracts for hiring thirteen British pilots.

    The airline's spokesperson explained that the decision was made because Arab countries would not allow British pilots to fly over their airspace.

### 阿拉伯航空公司  废除雇用英国驾驶员合同

1956-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯航空公司
    废除雇用英国驾驶员合同
    新华社14日讯　安曼消息：阿拉伯航空公司11月13日宣布，它将废除关于雇用十三名英国驾驶员的合同。
    这家航空公司的发言人解释说，作出这项决定是由于阿拉伯国家都不让英国驾驶员飞越它们的领空。
