Title: Inaugural Meeting of the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association: Telegram Protesting the Anglo-French Armed Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-11-11
Page: 1

    Inaugural Meeting of the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association
    Telegram Protesting the Anglo-French Armed Aggression Against Egypt
    The Anglo-French governments' barbaric armed aggression against Egypt not only undermines peace in the Middle East but also world peace. The 600 million Chinese people express their utmost anger and strong protest against this shameless act of aggression. We solemnly warn the Anglo-French governments: immediately stop slaughtering the Egyptian people! Immediately withdraw all invading troops! The entire Chinese people, along with the people of Asia, Africa, and other countries of the world, will never sit idly by in the face of the Anglo-French governments' crimes of aggression against Egypt. The Chinese people will use all practical and effective actions to resolutely support the Egyptian people's just struggle to defend their motherland, resist aggression, and safeguard world peace, and drive all invading troops from Egyptian territory.
      Inaugural Meeting of the China-Egypt Friendship Association
          November 10, 1956, in Beijing

### 中埃友好协会成立大会  抗议英法武装侵略埃及电

1956-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃友好协会成立大会
    抗议英法武装侵略埃及电
    英法政府对埃及野蛮的武装侵略，不仅破坏了中东的和平而且破坏了世界的和平。六万万中国人民对这种无耻的侵略行为，表示最大的愤怒和严重的抗议。我们严正警告英法政府：马上停止屠杀埃及人民！马上撤退全部侵略军队！对英法政府侵略埃及的罪行，全中国人民与亚非国家以及世界各国的人民决不会袖手旁观。中国人民将用一切实际有效的行动，坚决支持埃及人民保卫祖国，反抗侵略，维护世界和平的正义斗争，把侵略军队从埃及领土上全部赶出去。
      中国埃及友好协会成立大会
          1956年11月10日于北京
