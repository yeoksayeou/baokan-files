Title: Eden Temporarily Relieved of Prime Minister Duties, Reportedly Due to Over-Fatigue
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 6

    Eden Temporarily Relieved of Prime Minister Duties
    Reportedly Due to Over-Fatigue
    Xinhua News Agency, London, November 20th—TASS, London, November 20th: The British Prime Minister's Office officially announced that, on the advice of doctors, Eden will temporarily be relieved of his duties as Prime Minister. During this period, Butler will preside over cabinet meetings.
    The communique said, "The Prime Minister is severely over-fatigued."

### 艾登暂摆脱首相工作  据说是因为疲劳过度

1956-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    艾登暂摆脱首相工作
    据说是因为疲劳过度
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社伦敦20日讯：英国首相办公厅正式宣布，根据医生的劝告，艾登将暂时摆脱首相工作，在这个期间，由巴特勒主持内阁会议。
    公报说，“首相严重地疲劳过度”。
