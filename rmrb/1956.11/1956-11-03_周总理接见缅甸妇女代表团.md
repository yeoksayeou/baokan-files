Title: Premier Zhou Receives Burmese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-11-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received the Burmese women's delegation led by Madam Du Shengbu this afternoon.

    Those present at the reception included U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and other Burmese guests. The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife were also present.

    Also present were Li Shenzhi, Guo Moruo, and Li Weihan, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat.

### 周总理接见缅甸妇女代表团

1956-11-03
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理接见缅甸妇女代表团
    新华社2日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了以杜盛布团长为首的缅甸妇女代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和缅甸其他客人。缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和他的夫人也在座。
    在座的还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李深济、郭沫若、李维汉，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、书记处书记曹孟君等。
