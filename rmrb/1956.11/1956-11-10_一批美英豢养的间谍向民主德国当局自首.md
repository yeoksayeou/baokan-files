Title: A Group of American and British-Trained Spies Surrender to the Authorities of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1956-11-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 9th. Recently, some spies from foreign intelligence agencies have surrendered to the public security organs of the German Democratic Republic, the majority of whom are spies from American and British intelligence centers.
　　They confessed that they were ordered to collect economic and military intelligence in the German Democratic Republic. They also said that the heads of foreign intelligence agencies had recently tasked them with creating provocative incidents in the German Democratic Republic.

### 一批美英豢养的间谍向民主德国当局自首

1956-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    一批美英豢养的间谍向民主德国当局自首
    新华社柏林9日电　最近有一些外国谍报机关的间谍向民主德国的公安机关自首，其中多数是美国和英国谍报中心的间谍。
    他们供认，他们奉命在民主德国搜集了经济和军事情报。他们还说，外国谍报机关的头子最近给他们的任务是在民主德国制造挑衅事件。
