Title: People's Representatives and CPPCC Members Begin Inspection Work
Author:
Date: 1956-11-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. National People's Congress representatives and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference began their inspection work for the second half of this year today.

    Of the representatives and members in Beijing, 322 have decided to go to various places for inspection. Some of the representatives and members have already left Beijing, and the remaining representatives and members will also depart successively. They will go to 20 provinces including Guangdong and Sichuan, the three municipalities directly under the central government of Beijing, Shanghai, and Tianjin, as well as the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region for inspection. Among them, 103 representatives and members have decided to carry out inspection work in Beijing.

    Some of the representatives and members in various places have already begun their inspections.

    On the 17th, the representatives and members in Beijing held discussions in groups according to the inspection areas on issues related to inspection work.

    The items for inspection by the representatives and members this time are extremely broad, including issues in industrial and mining enterprises, agriculture, water conservancy, local transportation, disaster areas, construction of old revolutionary base areas, culture, education, health, scientific research, grain, industry and commerce, free markets, handicrafts, women, children's welfare, social welfare, municipal construction, public security, judicial procuratorial work, grassroots elections, the work of people's congresses at all levels, the work of the CPPCC and the united front, work related to ethnic minorities, and work related to overseas Chinese affairs. Many representatives and members jointly studied and determined the key inspection items before departure.

### 人民代表和政协委员  开始视察工作

1956-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    人民代表和政协委员
    开始视察工作
    新华社20日讯　全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员今年下半年的视察工作，从今天起开始进行。
    在京的代表和委员决定去各地视察工作的已有三百二十二人，其中一部分代表和委员已经先后离京，其余的代表和委员们也将陆续出发。他们将去广东、四川等二十个省，北京、上海、天津三个直辖市以及新疆维吾尔自治区视察，其中决定在北京视察工作的代表和委员有一百零三人。
    在各地的代表和委员们有的也已经开始视察。
    17日，在京的代表和委员曾按视察地区分组就视察工作中有关问题进行了座谈。
    这次代表和委员视察的项目极为广泛，其中包括工矿企业、农业、水利、地方交通、灾区情况、革命老根据地建设、文教卫生、科学研究、粮食、工商业、自由市场、手工业、妇女、儿童福利、社会福利、市政建设、公安司法检察、基层选举、各级人民代表大会工作、政协和统一战线工作、少数民族工作和侨务等方面的问题。许多代表和委员在出发前曾共同研究和确定了视察的重点项目。
