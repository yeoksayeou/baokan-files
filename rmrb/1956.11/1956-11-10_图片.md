    Commemorative Medal for the 90th Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth.
    The more than 10,000 commemorative medals for the 90th anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth, manufactured by the Beijing Metal工艺 [Gōngyì] (Arts & Crafts) Factory, have all been completed. This is a photo of the female workers in the finishing room of the Metal工艺 [Gōngyì] Factory conducting the final inspection of the commemorative medals.

                                        Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yu Huiru [喻惠如] photo
