Title: All-China Youth Federation and Communist Youth League Central Committee Hold Reception to See Off Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. The All-China Youth Federation and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League jointly held a reception at noon today to see off the delegation of the Japan Youth Organization Council and the Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception.

Premier Zhou spoke at the reception. He extended welcome and gratitude on behalf of the Chinese government and people to the visiting Japanese friends. He said that we are very happy to see more and more Japanese friends visiting China. Mutual visits and contacts between the Chinese and Japanese people can not only promote the friendship between the two peoples, but also help promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Japan. He hoped that through the visits of various delegations, the friendly relations between the Chinese and Japanese people would become closer, so that the Chinese and Japanese people could live in peace forever. Premier Zhou finally raised his glass to wish Sino-Japanese friendship forever and good health to all Japanese representatives.

The Japanese National Electrical Communication Workers' Union Delegation, the Japanese Writers Delegation, and the Japanese Okayama Prefecture Academic and Cultural Delegation, who are also visiting China, were also invited to attend the reception.

Attending the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Hu Yaobang, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Student Federation; Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; and heads of units such as the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Before the reception, 3,500 youths from all walks of life in Beijing held a meeting at the Beijing Hotel to see off the delegation of the Japan Youth Organization Council and the Japanese Youth and Women Delegation to China. Liao Chengzhi, Hu Yaobang, and the heads of the two Japanese delegations, Yoshiaki Sagae and Tamotsu Ida, spoke at the meeting.

### 全国青联和团中央举行酒会  欢送日本青年和妇女访华代表团

1956-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    全国青联和团中央举行酒会
    欢送日本青年和妇女访华代表团
    新华社11日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会今天午间联合举行酒会，欢送日本青年团协议会代表团和日本青年与妇女访华代表团。
    周恩来总理出席了酒会。
    周总理在酒会上讲了话。他代表中国政府和人民向日本朋友的访问表示欢迎和感谢。他说，我们很高兴地看到越来越多的日本朋友来中国访问。中日两国人民彼此访问和接触不仅可以促进两国人民的友谊，而且可以帮助促进中日两国邦交的恢复。他希望通过各个代表团的访问，使中日两国人民的友好关系更加密切起来，使中日两国人民能永远和平共处下去。周总理最后举杯祝中日友好万岁，祝各位日本代表的身体健康。
    前来中国访问的日本全国电气通讯工会代表团、日本作家代表团、日本冈山县学术文化代表团也应邀出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的有全国青联主席廖承志，青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、章泽、胡克实，全国学联主席胡启立，全国总工会主席赖若愚，中国科学院副院长陶孟和以及中国人民对外文化协会等单位的负责人。酒会前，北京市各界青年三千五百人在北京饭店举行大会，欢送日本青年团协议会代表团和日本青年与妇女访华代表团。廖承志、胡耀邦和日本两个代表团的团长寒河江善秋、井田保等都在大会上讲了话。
