Title: Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference Passes Resolution Demanding Governments Strive for Disarmament; Representatives from Many Countries Advocate for Inviting Our Country to Participate

Author:

Date: 1956-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd, dispatch from Bangkok: The 45th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference passed a proposal on disarmament issues on the afternoon of November 21st with 436 votes (two abstentions).

This proposal calls on national parliaments to recommend to their respective governments, particularly those participating in the Subcommittee of the United Nations Disarmament Commission, to continue striving for an agreement on disarmament issues. This agreement should stipulate significant reductions in the armed forces and conventional armaments of all countries and take the prohibition of the production, use, and testing of weapons of mass destruction as the first stage in the overall solution to the disarmament problem.

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd, dispatch from Bangkok: Representatives from parliamentary delegations of many countries participating in the 45th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference in Bangkok have requested that China be invited to join the Inter-Parliamentary Union. According to Thai newspapers, during the meetings on the 15th and 16th, representatives from the parliamentary delegations of the Soviet Union, Poland, Sweden, Burma, Pakistan, India, Indonesia, and Albania, among other countries, pointed out in their speeches that the People's Republic of China, representing six hundred million people, should be invited to participate in this international organization.

### 各国议会联盟会议通过决议  要求各国政府争取实现裁军  许多国家的代表主张邀请我国参加

1956-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    各国议会联盟会议通过决议
    要求各国政府争取实现裁军
    许多国家的代表主张邀请我国参加
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社曼谷讯：各国议会联盟第四十五届会议在11月21日下午以四百三十六票（两票弃权）通过了关于裁减军备问题的提案。
    这项提案号召各国议会向本国政府，特别向那些参加联合国裁军委员会小组委员会的政府建议继续争取就裁军问题达成协议，这项协议应当规定大大裁减各国武装部队和常规军备，并且以禁止生产、使用和试验大规模毁灭性武器作为普遍解决裁军问题过程中的第一个阶段。
    新华社23日讯　曼谷消息：参加在曼谷举行的各国议会联盟第四十五届会议的许多国家的议员团的代表，要求邀请中国加入各国议会联盟。据泰国报纸报道，在15日和16日两天的会议上，苏联、波兰、瑞典、缅甸、巴基斯坦、印度、印度尼西亚和阿尔巴尼亚等国的议员团的代表都在发言中指出，代表六亿人民的中华人民共和国应当被邀参加这个国际性的组织。
