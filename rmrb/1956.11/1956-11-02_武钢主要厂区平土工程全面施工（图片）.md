Title: Wugang Major Plant Area Leveling Project Under Full Construction (Photo)
Author: Hao Chunyi
Date: 1956-11-02
Page: 2

The leveling project for the main plant areas of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Joint Enterprise, including the steelmaking plant, refractory plant, coking plant, and sintering plant, is now under full construction. The Wugang construction site has already leveled more than three million cubic meters of loess since September of last year. This is a corner of the Wugang leveling site - huge excavators are digging soil near the ironmaking area. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hao Chunyi photo

### 武钢主要厂区平土工程全面施工（图片）
郝纯一
1956-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

    武钢主要厂区平土工程全面施工
    武汉钢铁联合企业的炼钢厂、耐火厂、焦化厂、烧结厂等主要厂区的平土工程正在全面施工。武钢工地从去年9月到现在已经平整了三百多万立方的黄土。这是武钢平土工地的一角——巨大的挖土机在炼铁区附近挖土。　新华社记者　郝纯一摄
