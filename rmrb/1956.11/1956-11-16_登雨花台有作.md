Title: An Excursion to Yuhuatai
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1956-11-16
Page: 4

An Excursion to Yuhuatai
    Li Jishen

    Yuhuatai in Bai Xia, historically a place of contention,
    The people revere merit and virtue, using it to commemorate the martyrs,
    Flowers and trees adorn the hills, the scenery is particularly beautiful,
    From now on, peace will be established, and there will be no more wars!

### 登雨花台有作
李济深
1956-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    登雨花台有作
    李济深
    白下雨花台，由来争战地，
    人民崇功德，用以纪烈士，
    花木灿山陵，风景殊胜美，
    从兹定太平，无复战争矣！
