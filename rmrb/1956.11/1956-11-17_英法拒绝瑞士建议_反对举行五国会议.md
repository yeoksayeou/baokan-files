Title: Britain and France Reject Swiss Proposal, Oppose Five-Nation Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-11-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 16th Dispatch from London: British officials announced in a public statement on the 15th that the British government had rejected the Swiss government's proposal for a high-level conference to be attended by the four major powers and India.
	Eden has written to the President of the Swiss Confederation expressing Britain's unwillingness to hold the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, 16th Dispatch from Bern: It was announced here that the French government formally replied to the Swiss Federal Government on the 15th, rejecting the Swiss government's proposal to hold a five-nation conference.

### 英法拒绝瑞士建议  反对举行五国会议

1956-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    英法拒绝瑞士建议
    反对举行五国会议
    新华社16日讯　伦敦消息：英国官方15日发表公报，宣布英国政府拒绝了瑞士政府提出的关于举行四大国和印度参加的高级会议的建议。
    英国不愿意举行会议的意见已经由艾登写信通知瑞士联邦主席。
    新华社16日讯　伯尔尼消息：此间宣布，法国政府15日正式答复瑞士联邦政府，拒绝瑞士政府的关于举行五国会议的建议。
