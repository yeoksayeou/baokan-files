Title: "White Mountain" Brand Bicycles Improve in Quality
Author:
Date: 1956-11-26
Page: 2

The "White Mountain" brand bicycles have improved in quality
    (Our newspaper's Shenyang dispatch from the 24th) In the third quarter factory competition this year among the Shanghai, Tianjin, and Shenyang bicycle factories, the product quality evaluation showed that the "White Mountain" brand bicycle from the Shenyang Bicycle Factory was awarded 3,000 yuan by the First Ministry of Machine Building because the electroplating and baking paint, among other major processes, had exceeded advanced levels. A year ago, this factory was a backward factory criticized by users as "everything but the bell rings." In the past year, in order to reverse the past poor quality situation, the factory sent people to Shanghai and Tianjin to humbly learn the advanced experience of brother factories. In electroplating and baking paint, with the help of the First Bureau of the Ministry of Machine Building, they strictly implemented the Soviet experts' suggestion of establishing scientific inspection methods. Since the second half of this year, the processing workshop has also systematically promoted eight advanced experiences such as the processing assembly line, and the quality of the factory's products has increased month by month. At present, the quality of the "White Mountain" brand bicycles produced by this factory has reached and exceeded the level of products from the other two factories, except for the mechanical processing, welding, and treatment of the flywheel and frame, which are still inferior to the "Forever" and "Flying Pigeon" brand bicycles of Shanghai and Tianjin.

### 白山牌自行车质量好转

1956-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

    白山牌自行车质量好转
    本报沈阳24日电　在上海天津沈阳三个自行车厂今年第三季度的厂际竞赛、检查产品质量的评比中，沈阳自行车厂的产品——白山牌自行车由于电镀烤漆等主要工艺已经超过先进水平，受到了第一机械工业部的三千元奖励。一年前，这个工厂还是一个被用户批评为“除了车铃不响其他零件都响”的落后工厂，一年来这个厂为了扭转过去质量不好的情况，曾经派人到上海天津虚心学习兄弟厂的先进经验，电镀烤漆方面在第一机械工业局的帮助下严格地执行了苏联专家提出的建立科学检查方法的建议，今年下半年以来加工车间还有计划地推行了加工流水线等八项先进经验，工厂产品质量逐月上升。目前这个厂生产的白山牌自行车除飞轮、架子的机械加工，和烧焊、处理等工艺还不及上海天津的永久牌飞鸽牌自行车外，其他质量已经达到和超过其他两厂的产品的水平。
