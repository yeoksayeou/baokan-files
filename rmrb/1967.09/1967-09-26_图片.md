Title: The Radiance of Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Xu Wenliang
Date: 1967-09-26
Edition: 6

The radiance of Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the entire world, awakening the oppressed peoples of all nations. Jamaican workers residing abroad draw wisdom and strength from the Little Red Book. Dear Chairman Mao! You have spoken the words in our hearts, and you have shown us the direction of our march forward.

		Xu Wenliang (Xinhua News Agency)
