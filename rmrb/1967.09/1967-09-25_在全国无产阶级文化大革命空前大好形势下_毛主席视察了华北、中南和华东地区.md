Title: Chairman Mao Inspects the North China, Central South, and East China Regions Amid the Great Situation of the Nationwide Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-09-25
Edition: 1

In the midst of the unprecedentedly good situation of the nationwide Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, our great leader Chairman Mao Zedong recently inspected the North China, Central South, and East China regions. He investigated the situation of the Cultural Revolution in the provinces and cities of Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang, and Shanghai.

Chairman Mao has now returned to Beijing.

### 在全国无产阶级文化大革命空前大好形势下　毛主席视察了华北、中南和华东地区

1967-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　在全国无产阶级文化大革命空前大好形势下
　　毛主席视察了华北、中南和华东地区
　　新华社二十四日讯　在全国无产阶级文化大革命空前大好的形势下，我们伟大的领袖毛泽东主席最近一时期视察了华北、中南和华东地区，调查了河南、湖北、湖南、江西、浙江、上海等省市文化大革命的情况。
　　毛主席现在已经回到北京。
