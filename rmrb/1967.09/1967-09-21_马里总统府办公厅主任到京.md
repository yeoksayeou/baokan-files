Title: Director of the Office of the Malian President Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-21
Edition: 4

The Director of the Office of the Malian President, Bakary Diallo, and his wife, have arrived in China for a friendly visit. They arrived in Beijing a few days ago. Relevant officials from our side and the Malian Ambassador to China, Guisse, welcomed them at the airport.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibao hosted a banquet to welcome Bakary Diallo and his wife.

### 马里总统府办公厅主任到京

1967-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　马里总统府办公厅主任到京
    新华社二十日讯　马里总统府办公厅主任巴卡拉·迪亚洛和夫人，来我国进行友好访问，已于日前到达北京。我有关方面负责人和马里驻中国大使吉塞到机场欢迎。
    外交部副部长罗贵波曾设宴欢迎巴卡拉·迪亚洛和夫人。
