Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1967-09-03
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai today received Nguyen Van Huan, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and Minister of Education of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Quyen, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Women's Union, and the entire delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association led by them. He had a very cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Sirou, Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, and other relevant officials. Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Loan was also present at the reception.

	Right photo: On September 2, Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association led by Nguyen Van Huan (front left third), Vice Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and Minister of Education of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Ho Quyen (front right second), member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Women's Union.

	Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Guiyu.

### 周恩来总理接见越中友协代表团

1967-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见越中友协代表团
    新华社二日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了越中友协副会长、越南民主共和国教育部部长阮文煊，越南劳动党中央委员、越南妇女联合会副主席何桂以及由他们率领的越中友协代表团全体成员，同他们进行了十分亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有张奚若、楚图南、丁西林等有关方面负责人。接见时越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也在座。（附图片）
    右图：九月二日，周恩来总理接见由越中友协副会长、越南民主共和国教育部长阮文煊（前左三）和越南劳动党中央委员、越南妇女联合会副主席何桂（前右二）率领的越中友协代表团全体成员。
    新华社记者　张桂玉摄
