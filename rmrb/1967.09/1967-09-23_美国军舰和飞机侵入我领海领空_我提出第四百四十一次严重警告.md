Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 441st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1967-09-23
Edition: 6

U.S. warships and aircraft intrude into our territorial waters and airspace; we issue the 441st serious warning

	New China News Agency, September 21st report: At 11:59 to 12:10 on September 21st, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 14:20 to 18:46, a U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters northeast of Pingtan Island in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 18:03 to 18:06, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace west of the Leizhou Peninsula in Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocations by U.S. military aircraft and warships, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 441st serious warning.

### 美国军舰和飞机侵入我领海领空　我提出第四百四十一次严重警告

1967-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国军舰和飞机侵入我领海领空
　　我提出第四百四十一次严重警告
    新华社二十一日讯　九月二十一日十一时五十九分至十二时十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日十四时二十分至十八时四十六分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省平潭岛东北地区领海。同日十八时零三分至十八时零六分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省雷州半岛以西地区领空。对于美国军用飞机和军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百四十一次严重警告。
