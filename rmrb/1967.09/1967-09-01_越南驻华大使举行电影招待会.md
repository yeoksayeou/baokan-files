Title: Vietnamese Ambassador Hosts Film Screening
Author:
Date: 1967-09-01
Edition: 6

Vietnamese Ambassador Hosts Film Screening

	New China News Agency, September 1st - Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Vu Minh Quan, hosted a film screening today to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Relevant officials from China, Zhang Sirou, Han Nianlong, Zhou Yiping, and Li Qiang, attended the event upon invitation.

	Members of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation, who are in Beijing to participate in activities celebrating the 22nd anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as Deputy Head of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front's Permanent Delegation to China, Nguyen Minh Phong, also attended the film screening. The event featured a documentary showcasing the victories of the people of North and South Vietnam in their struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

### 越南驻华大使举行电影招待会

1967-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南驻华大使举行电影招待会
    新华社三十一日讯　越南驻中国大使吴明鸾今天举行电影招待会，庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十二周年。我有关方面负责人张奚若、韩念龙、周一萍、李强应邀出席了电影招待会。
    正在北京参加我国庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十二周年活动的越中友协代表团全体成员，以及越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳也出席了电影招待会。电影招待会上，放映了纪录越南南北方人民抗美救国斗争胜利事迹的影片。
