Title: Vietnamese News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-03
Edition: 6

	Vietnamese News Delegation Arrives in Beijing

	New China News Agency, September 2nd: A delegation of four Vietnamese journalists, led by Li Ming, a member of the editorial board of the Vietnamese "People's Daily," arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 2nd. The delegation is visiting China on a friendly basis in accordance with the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries.

### 越南新闻代表团到京

1967-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南新闻代表团到京
    新华社二日讯　越南新闻工作者代表团一行四人，在越南《人民报》编委黎明率领下，已于二日晨到达北京。代表团是根据中越两国文化合作协定来我国进行友好访问的。
