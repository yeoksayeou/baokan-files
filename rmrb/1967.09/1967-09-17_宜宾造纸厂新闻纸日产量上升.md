Title: Yibin Paper Mill Increases Daily Production of Newsprint
Author:
Date: 1967-09-17
Edition: 4

The revolutionary rebels of the proletariat at the Yibin Paper Mill in Sichuan Province have raised high the banner of revolutionary criticism, focusing on revolution and vigorously promoting production. Since August, the daily production of newsprint has rapidly and steadily increased. Other special industrial papers have also seen increased production.

### 宜宾造纸厂新闻纸日产量上升

1967-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    宜宾造纸厂新闻纸日产量上升
    四川宜宾造纸厂的无产阶级革命派，高举革命大批判的旗帜，狠抓革命，猛促生产，从八月份以来，新闻纸日产量迅速而稳定地上升，其他特种工业用纸也都获得增产。　（据新华社）
