Title: Delegation of Vietnam News Agency Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-28
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the New China News Agency, a delegation from the Vietnam News Agency, led by its president Tao Song, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Relevant officials from our side and the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Li Zhongshi, along with other Vietnamese friends, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 越南通讯社代表团到京

1967-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应新华通讯社邀请，由越南通讯社社长陶松率领的越南通讯社代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。我有关方面负责人和越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始和其他越南朋友也到机场迎接。
