Title: The People United as One, Who Can Be Our Enemy?
Author:
Date: 1967-09-11
Edition: 6

The revolutionary teachers and students of Beijing Grassland Middle School, along with the soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army who came to the school for military training, resolutely responded to Chairman Mao's great call. They raised the banner of "supporting the army and loving the people" and carried out the movement to support the army and love the people. This is a scene of students and PLA soldiers together drafting a covenant to support the army and love the people.

New China News Agency photographer Chu Ying

The revolutionary masses of Wuhan's Third Mill love the great leader Chairman Mao's personally created and Comrade Lin Biao's personally commanded People's Liberation Army the most. The revolutionary workers of Wuhan's Third Mill and the comrades of the 8199th Unit study and fight together, establishing a deep comradeship in battle.

New China News Agency photographer Tan Xian De

The soldiers and officers of a certain unit stationed in Qinghai use Mao Zedong Thought to mobilize the masses, arm the masses, and fight shoulder to shoulder with the revolutionary masses. This is a scene of them writing big-character posters with the revolutionary masses to expose and criticize the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road.

New China News Agency photographer An Ke Ren

The People's Armed Forces Department of Zigong City, Sichuan, firmly stands on the side of the revolutionary leftists, thinking what the revolutionary leftists think and worrying about what the revolutionary leftists worry about. This is the deputy director of the Zigong City People's Armed Forces Department, Comrade Li Zongbo, propagating Mao Zedong Thought and Chairman Mao's revolutionary line to the revolutionary masses and revolutionary youngsters, receiving a warm welcome from the revolutionary masses and revolutionary youngsters.

New China News Agency photographer You Yun Gu

In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the poor and lower-middle peasants in the suburbs of Shanghai established a deep class sentiment with the People's Liberation Army. The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants expressed their determination to learn better from the PLA, assist the PLA in completing the glorious tasks of "three supports and two militaries," and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end.

Reporter Wang Jing Ren

The soldiers and officers of a certain unit stationed in Beijing firmly support the proletarian revolutionary masses, closely unite with the local proletarian revolutionary masses, fight together, and launch a high tide of great criticism and struggle against China's Khrushchev, determined to thoroughly criticize and expose China's Khrushchev politically, ideologically, and theoretically.

Photographer Yang Chang Zhong

The military and civilians on the front lines of Fujian hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, using Mao Zedong Thought to arm their minds. The military and civilians are united as one, ready at any time to give the daring enemy a devastating blow, to defend the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, to defend the dictatorship of the proletariat, and to defend the security of the motherland.

New China News Agency

### 军民团结如一人　试看天下谁能敌（图片）

1967-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    军民团结如一人　试看天下谁能敌
    北京草场地中学革命师生和来校执行军训任务的解放军指战员，坚决响应毛主席的伟大号召，高举“拥军爱民”的旗帜，深入开展拥军爱民运动。这是同学们和解放军同志在一起制订拥军爱民公约。		　新华社记者　楚　英摄
    无产阶级革命派最热爱伟大统帅毛主席亲手缔造、林彪同志直接指挥的人民解放军。武汉第三米厂的革命派工人，和八一九九部队的同志一起学习，一起战斗，建立了深厚的战斗友谊。
                        新华社记者　谭先德摄
    驻青海某部指战员，用毛泽东思想发动群众，武装群众，与革命群众并肩战斗。这是他们与革命群众一起写揭发批判党内最大的一小撮走资本主义道路当权派的大字报。
                        新华社记者　安克仁摄
    四川自贡市人民武装部坚决地站在革命左派一边，想革命派所想，急革命派所急。这是自贡市人民武装部副部长李宗伯同志向革命群众和革命小将们宣传毛泽东思想和毛主席的革命路线，受到革命群众和革命小将的热烈欢迎。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　游云谷摄
    上海郊区贫下中农在无产阶级文化大革命中，和解放军建立了深厚的阶级感情。广大贫下中农表示，要更好地向解放军学习，协助解放军完成“三支”、“两军”的光荣任务，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
                                      本报记者　王景仁摄
    驻京部队某部指战员坚定地支持无产阶级革命派，紧紧地同驻地无产阶级革命派社员团结在一起，战斗在一起，掀起了对中国赫鲁晓夫大批判、大斗争的高潮，决心从政治上、思想上、理论上彻底批倒批臭中国的赫鲁晓夫。				　杨昌忠摄
    福建前线军民高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，用毛泽东思想武装自己的头脑。军民团结如一人，随时准备给敢于来犯之敌以毁灭性打击，以保卫无产阶级文化大革命，保卫无产阶级专政，保卫祖国的安全。
　　　　　　　　　新华社稿
