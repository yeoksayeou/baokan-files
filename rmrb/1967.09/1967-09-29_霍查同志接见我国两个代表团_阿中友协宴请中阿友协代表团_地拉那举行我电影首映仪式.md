Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Two Chinese Delegations; Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association Delegation; Tirana Holds Premiere of Chinese Film
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 8

Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Two Chinese Delegations

The beloved leader of the Albanian people and the closest friend of the Chinese people, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Comrade Enver Hoxha, received on the afternoon of the 27th the delegation of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Minister of National Defense, who is currently visiting Albania. He also received the delegation of Chinese scientists.

During the reception, Comrade Enver Hoxha had a very cordial and friendly conversation with all the members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were: Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Central Committee Secretary Kadri Hazbiu, Candidate Member of the Political Bureau and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Behar Shtylla, Central Committee Member Spiro Koleka, Rector of the University of Tirana Iliaz Alliu, and Chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Teme Sejko.

Also present at the reception was the Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Tingquan.

Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association Delegation

The Executive Council of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association held a banquet on the evening of the 26th to warmly welcome the delegation of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Minister of National Defense.

At the banquet, filled with a very sincere and friendly atmosphere, Chairman Behar Shtylla of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association and Head of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association Delegation Wang Shusheng both spoke. The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the health of the great leaders Mao Zedong and Enver Hoxha, to the fighting friendship and revolutionary unity between the peoples of the two countries.

Attending the banquet were also the Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Tingquan, and Military Attache Li Yifan.

Tirana Holds Premiere of Chinese Film

To celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, two large-scale color documentaries, "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts" and "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought," were premiered on the 27th at the Partisan Cinema in Tirana.

Attending the premiere were Albanian Deputy Foreign Minister Maliqe Malile, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture Abdyl Hoxha, Vice Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Spase, and Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Council of Tirana Qirjazi, among others.

Chinese Military Attache to Albania Li Yifan also attended the premiere.

The two films shown at the premiere were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in the Albanian capital.

### 霍查同志接见我国两个代表团　阿中友协宴请中阿友协代表团　地拉那举行我电影首映仪式

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　霍查同志接见我国两个代表团
　　阿中友协宴请中阿友协代表团　地拉那举行我电影首映仪式
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民最敬爱的领袖、中国人民最亲密的朋友、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志，二十七日下午接见了正在这里访问的由中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领的中阿友协代表团，同时还接见了中国科学工作者代表团。
    接见时，霍查同志同代表团全体成员进行了极其亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、中央书记卡博，政治局候补委员、阿中友协主席凯莱齐，中央委员什图拉，地拉那大学校长伊利和对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃。
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　阿中友协理事会二十六日晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领的中阿友协代表团。
    在充满着极其真挚和友好气氛的宴会上，阿中友协主席凯莱齐和中阿友协代表团团长王树声先后讲了话。宾主双方不断为两国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志和霍查同志的身体健康，为中阿两国人民的战斗友谊和革命团结，频频干杯。
    出席宴会的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和武官李一凡。
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年，中国的两部大型彩色纪录片《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》和《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》，二十七日在地拉那游击队电影院隆重举行首映仪式。
    出席首映仪式的有，阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长马利列，教育和文化部副部长阿布迪·霍查，阿中友协副主席斯帕塞，地拉那市人民议会执行委员会副主席齐乌等。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆武官李一凡也出席了首映仪式。
    在首映仪式上放映的两部影片，受到了阿尔巴尼亚首都各界人士十分热烈的欢迎。
