Title: At the Invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, the National Revolutionary Movement and Government Delegation of Congo (Brazzaville) Will Visit China
Author:
Date: 1967-09-26
Edition: 1

At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, the National Revolutionary Movement and Government Delegation of the Republic of Congo (Brazzaville) will soon come to China for a friendly visit. The delegation is led by Ambrose Noumazalaye, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), the Prime Minister, and the head of the government.

### 应周恩来总理的邀请　刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团将来我国访问

1967-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　应周恩来总理的邀请
　　刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团将来我国访问
    新华社二十六日讯　应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，刚果共和国（布拉柴维尔）全国革命运动和政府代表团将于最近前来我国进行友好访问。这个代表团由刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会第一书记、总理、政府首脑安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱率领。
