Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Again Invade Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1967-09-10
Edition: 5

	U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces, numbering approximately thirty, invaded one kilometer into the territory of Cambodia's Kampong Cham Province, Kirirom District, from the night of the 2nd to the early morning of the 3rd. They brazenly attacked a border outpost of the Cambodian provincial defense forces, killing two soldiers and injuring eight soldiers and civilians. The outpost suffered severe damage. In this savage attack, the Cambodian military and civilians bravely resisted the invasion by the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces.

### 美国—南越伪军又侵犯柬埔寨

1967-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国—南越伪军又侵犯柬埔寨
    据新华社讯　美国—南越伪军约三十人，二日深夜至三日凌晨侵入柬埔寨境内一公里的茶胶省基里翁县，悍然袭击了柬埔寨省防军的一个边防哨所，打死省防军两名，打伤省防军和居民八名，哨所遭到了严重破坏。在这次野蛮的袭击中，柬埔寨军民对美国—南越伪军的侵袭，进行了英勇的抵抗。
