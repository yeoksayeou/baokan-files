Title: Soldiers and Red Guards United in Battle
Author: You Yun Gu
Date: 1967-09-25
Edition: 3

The soldiers and revolutionary youths of the Zizhong City People's Armed Forces Department have forged a deep bond of camaraderie through their shared struggles. This photograph captures the moment when the revolutionary youths from Zizhong City's Second Middle School and the soldiers from the Armed Forces Department engage in activities to support the military and love the people.

	Newspaper reporter You Yun Gu took this photograph.
