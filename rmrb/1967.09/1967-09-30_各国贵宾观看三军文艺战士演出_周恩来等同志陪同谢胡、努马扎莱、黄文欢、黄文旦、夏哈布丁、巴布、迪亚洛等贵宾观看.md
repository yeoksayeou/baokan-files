Title: International Guests Watch Performance by PLA Cultural Troops
Author:
Date: 1967-09-30
Edition: 1

International friends visiting China for friendly visits and to participate in National Day activities attended a grand evening of performing arts tonight, watching a revolutionary song and dance performance by the revolutionary cultural troops of the People's Liberation Army's land, sea, and air forces stationed in Beijing.

Central leaders Comrade Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Xie Fuzhi accompanied the international friends to watch the performance.

Invited to attend the evening were: the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Shehu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania; the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville) and Government Delegation led by Ambassador Nzambezi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), Prime Minister, and Head of Government; Comrade Nguyen Van Huan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Leader of the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, and other members of the delegation; the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam led by Hero of the Battlefield Nguyen Van Dan; the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation led by Minister of Information and Broadcasting Shahabuddin; the Tanzanian Friendship Delegation led by Minister of Lands, Settlement, and Water Development Babu; and Director of the Office of the President of Mali, Diallo, among others.

When Premier Zhou Enlai and others escorted the international friends from various countries into the theater, the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly for a long time, shouting in unison: "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao for ten thousand years!"

The revolutionary cultural troops of the three services tonight performed with high revolutionary enthusiasm for the international friends, including "Wishing Chairman Mao a Long Life," "The Ship Sails on the Sea with the Helmsman at the Helm," and songs composed from Chairman Mao's poems, as well as dances that embody the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line of literature and art. Every time the image of our great leader, the red sun in the hearts of the people of the world, Chairman Mao, appeared on the screen, the entire audience erupted in thunderous applause that would not stop.

The revolutionary songs and dances performed by the revolutionary cultural troops of the three services fully reflected the boundless love of all the people of China for the great leader Chairman Mao, their boundless admiration for the boundlessly brilliant Mao Zedong Thought, and the brilliant achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. The performance by the revolutionary cultural troops of the three services was warmly welcomed by the international friends.

The evening of performing arts, filled with revolutionary, friendly, and united atmosphere, ended with the singing of "The Internationale."

Today's evening was jointly organized by the Foreign Affairs Committee and the China International Friendship Association.

### 各国贵宾观看三军文艺战士演出　周恩来等同志陪同谢胡、努马扎莱、黄文欢、黄文旦、夏哈布丁、巴布、迪亚洛等贵宾观看

1967-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国贵宾观看三军文艺战士演出
　　周恩来等同志陪同谢胡、努马扎莱、黄文欢、黄文旦、夏哈布丁、巴布、迪亚洛等贵宾观看
    新华社二十九日讯　前来我国进行友好访问和参加国庆活动的国际友人，今晚出席了盛大的文艺晚会，观看了中国人民解放军陆海空三军驻京部队无产阶级革命派文艺战士演出的革命歌舞。
    中央领导同志周恩来、康生、谢富治陪同各国朋友观看了演出。
    应邀出席晚会的有：由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，由刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会第一书记、总理、政府首脑安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱率领的刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团，越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会副主席、越南党政代表团副团长黄文欢同志和代表团其他成员，由战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，由巴基斯坦新闻和广播部部长夏哈布丁率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团，由坦桑尼亚土地、定居和水利开发部长巴布率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团和马里总统府办公厅主任迪亚洛等。
    当周恩来总理等陪同来自各国的国际友人走进剧场时，全场起立，长时间地热烈鼓掌，齐声高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”
    三军革命文艺战士，今天晚上以高昂的革命豪情，为各国朋友演出了《祝毛主席万寿无疆》、《大海航行靠舵手》和为毛主席诗词谱写的歌曲，以及体现毛主席无产阶级革命文艺路线伟大胜利的舞蹈。每当天幕上映出我们伟大领袖、世界人民心中的红太阳毛主席像时，全场就响起经久不息的雷鸣般的掌声。
    三军革命文艺战士演出的这些革命歌舞，充分地反映了全中国人民对伟大领袖毛主席的无限热爱，对光焰无际的毛泽东思想的无限崇拜，反映了中国无产阶级文化大革命的辉煌成就。三军革命文艺战士的演出受到了各国朋友的热烈欢迎。
    充满革命、友谊和团结气氛的文艺晚会，在《国际歌》声中结束。
    今天的晚会是由对外文委、对外友协联合举行的。
