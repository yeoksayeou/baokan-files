Title: Delegation of the Communist Party of New Zealand Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 8

A delegation of the Communist Party of New Zealand, led by John Folds, a member of the National Committee of the Communist Party of New Zealand, arrived in Beijing on September 27th, 1967. The delegation, consisting of five members, was invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to conduct a visit and tour.

Comrade Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, welcomed the delegation and hosted a banquet in their honor.

### 新西兰共产党代表团到京　刘宁一同志欢迎并宴请新共同志

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　新西兰共产党代表团到京
　　刘宁一同志欢迎并宴请新共同志
    新华社二十八日讯　由新西兰共产党全国委员会委员约翰·福尔兹同志率领的新西兰共产党代表团一行五人，应中共中央邀请，前来我国参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年的庆祝活动和参观访问，二十七日到达北京。
    中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志欢迎并宴请了新西兰共产党代表团。
