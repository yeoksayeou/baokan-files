Title: The North Korean Ambassador Holds a Reception for National Day
Author:
Date: 1967-09-10
Edition: 5

North Korean Ambassador to China, Hyun Jun-gyeok, held a reception today to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Xie Fuzhi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, and other relevant officials attended the reception at the invitation.

Ambassador Hyun Jun-gyeok and Vice Premier Li Xiannian both spoke at the reception. Diplomats from various countries accredited to China were also present.

On September 8th, the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-North Korea Friendship Association held a reception to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜驻华大使举行国庆招待会

1967-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社九日讯　朝鲜驻中国大使玄峻极今天举行招待会，庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十九周年。
    国务院副总理李先念、谢富治，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，各有关方面负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
    玄峻极大使和李先念副总理在招待会上先后讲了话。出席招待会的还有各国驻中国的使节。
    八日，我国对外友协和中朝友协举行了招待会，庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十九周年。
