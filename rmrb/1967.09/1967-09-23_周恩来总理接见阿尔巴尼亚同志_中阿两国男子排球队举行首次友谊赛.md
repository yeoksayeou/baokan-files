Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Albanian Comrades and the First Friendly Volleyball Match Between China and Albania
Author:
Date: 1967-09-23
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Enlai received Albanian comrades
The first friendly volleyball match between China and Albania

	New China News Agency, September 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai this evening received the leaders of the Tirana volleyball and shooting teams, P. Tabia and his deputies H. Sinamadi and B. Qiuqi. Premier Zhou and the Albanian comrades had a very friendly and cordial conversation.

	Present at the reception were Vice Premier Li Xiannian and responsible comrades from relevant departments.

	Albanian Ambassador to China, Nataneli, was also present.

	According to the New China News Agency, September 22nd: The Tirana men's volleyball team of Albania and the Chinese national men's volleyball team held their first friendly match this evening at the Beijing Workers' Stadium. A grand opening ceremony was held before the match.

	Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, member of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Chen Boda, and responsible comrades from the People's Liberation Army and other relevant departments attended the opening ceremony and received the volleyball players from both countries and the Albanian shooting athletes who attended the opening ceremony.

	Albanian Ambassador to China, Nataneli, and other Albanian comrades also attended the opening ceremony.

	Tonight, the Beijing Workers' Stadium was filled with the atmosphere of unity and friendship between the peoples of China and Albania. At 8:20 PM, the athletes from both countries, holding high the portraits of Chairman Mao and Comrade Enver Hoxha, waving the national flags of China and Albania, and holding red copies of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," marched into the stadium. The orchestra played "We Are Marching on the Great Road," and the 15,000 spectators in the stadium applauded enthusiastically for a long time, shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" Then, the athletes from both countries and all the spectators read together the quotations from Chairman Mao: "'The world is so big, yet we are neighbors.' China and Albania are separated by thousands of mountains and rivers, but our hearts are connected. We are your true friends and comrades. You are also our true friends and comrades. We are not those false friends who are sweet-tongued but treacherous. Our revolutionary and fighting friendship has withstood the test of storms and tempests." They also read together the quotations from Comrade Enver Hoxha: "Our people, our party, and government will always stand shoulder to shoulder with the great Chinese people and the Chinese Communist Party on the common revolutionary road, whether in peaceful times or in stormy moments, and advance together in the struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism for the victory of revolution and socialism."

	At the opening ceremony, Vice Minister of the Foreign Cultural Relations Commission Chu Tunan and the leader of the Tirana volleyball and shooting teams, P. Tabia, spoke, warmly congratulating the eternal friendship between the peoples of China and Albania.

	The match ended with the orchestra playing the songs "Praise the Party and Enver Hoxha" and "The Ship Needs a Helmsman." (Photo attached)

	September 22nd, Premier Zhou Enlai poses for a group photo with the Albanian Tirana volleyball and shooting teams. New China News Agency photographer: Wang Xinqing.

### 周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚同志　中阿两国男子排球队举行首次友谊赛

1967-09-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚同志
　　中阿两国男子排球队举行首次友谊赛
    新华社二十二日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了阿尔巴尼亚地拉那排球队和射击队领队彼·塔比亚和副领队哈·西那马地、巴·秋奇。周总理和阿尔巴尼亚同志进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有李先念副总理和各有关方面的负责人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利也在座。
    据新华社二十二日讯　阿尔巴尼亚地拉那男子排球队和中国国家男子排球队，今天晚上在北京工人体育馆进行了首次友谊比赛。比赛前，举行了隆重的开幕式。
    周恩来总理、李先念副总理、中央文革小组成员戚本禹，中国人民解放军和其他有关方面的负责人参加了开幕式，并接见了两国排球运动员和参加开幕式的阿尔巴尼亚射击运动员。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利和其他阿尔巴尼亚同志，也参加了开幕式。
    今晚，北京工人体育馆内洋溢着中阿两国人民团结和战斗的友谊气氛。八时二十分，两国运动员高举毛主席画像和恩维尔·霍查同志的画像，打着中阿两国国旗，手持红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，列队进入比赛场地，这时乐队高奏《我们走在大路上》，全场一万五千名观众长时间地热烈鼓掌，高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”接着，两国运动员和全场观众一起朗读毛主席语录：“‘海内存知己，天涯若比邻’。中阿两国远隔千山万水，我们的心是连在一起的。我们是你们真正的朋友和同志。你们也是我们真正的朋友和同志。我们和你们都不是那种口蜜腹剑的假朋友，不是那种两面派。我们之间的革命的战斗的友谊，经历过急风暴雨的考验。”一起朗读恩维尔·霍查同志语录：“我国人民、我们党和政府同伟大的中国人民和中国共产党在共同的革命道路上前进，无论在和平的日子里，还是在暴风雨的时刻，都将永远同他们肩并肩地站在一起，在反对帝国主义和现代修正主义、争取革命和社会主义胜利的斗争中共同前进。”
    在开幕式上，对外文委副主任楚图南，阿尔巴尼亚地拉那排球队和射击队领队彼·塔比亚，先后讲了话，他们热烈祝贺中阿两国人民的兄弟友谊万古长青。
    比赛在高奏《歌颂党和恩维尔·霍查》、《大海航行靠舵手》的乐曲声中结束。（附图片）
　　九月二十二日，周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚地拉那排球队和射击队时合影。　新华社记者　王新庆摄
