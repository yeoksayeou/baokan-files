Title: Chairman of the Sudanese Supreme Council Receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Sudan
Author:
Date: 1967-09-21
Edition: 4

Chairman of the Sudanese Supreme Council Receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Sudan

According to Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, September 18 — Chairman of the Sudanese Supreme Council, Sayyid Ismail al-Azhari, received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Sudan, Xue Na, on September 18 and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

### 苏丹国家最高委员会主席接见我驻苏丹临时代办

1967-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹国家最高委员会主席接见我驻苏丹临时代办
    据新华社喀土穆十八日电　苏丹国家最高委员会主席赛义德·伊斯梅尔·阿扎里十八日接见了中国驻苏丹大使馆临时代办雪呐，并且同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
