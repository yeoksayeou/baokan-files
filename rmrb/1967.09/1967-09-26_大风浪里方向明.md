Title: Clear Direction in Stormy Seas
Author: Jiěfàngjūn Yī Zhànshì
Date: 1967-09-26
Edition: 3

	Mao Zedong inspects the Cultural Revolution,
	With a mighty hand, he points the way to victory.
	Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,
	We will forever follow you in revolution.
	Following you, we will never lose our way on the long journey!
	Following you, in stormy seas, our direction is clear!
	Following you, we firmly support the revolutionary faction!
	Following you, we will achieve new victories in the Cultural Revolution!

### 大风浪里方向明
解放军一战士
1967-09-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　大风浪里方向明
　　解放军一战士
　　毛主席视察文化大革命，
　　挥巨手指明了胜利前程。
　　毛主席啊，毛主席，
　　我们永远跟您干革命。
　　跟着您，万里航程不迷路！
　　跟着您，大风浪里方向明！
　　跟着您，坚决支持革命派！
　　跟着您，在文化大革命中立新功！
