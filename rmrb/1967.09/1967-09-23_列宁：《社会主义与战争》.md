Opportunism represents the bourgeois policy within the workers' movement, the interests of the petty bourgeoisie, and the interests of a small portion of the bourgeoisified workers who have formed an alliance with the "national" bourgeoisie. It is opposed to the interests of the proletarian masses and the oppressed masses.
