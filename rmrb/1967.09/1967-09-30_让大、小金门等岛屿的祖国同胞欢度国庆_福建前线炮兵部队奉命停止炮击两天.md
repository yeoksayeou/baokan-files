Title: Allowing Fellow Countrymen on the Islands of Greater and Lesser Kinmen to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1967-09-30
Edition: 6

The spokesman for the Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's frontline forces in Fujian announced that October 1st marks the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. To allow the fellow countrymen and Nationalist military personnel on the islands of Greater Kinmen, Lesser Kinmen, Dadan, and Erdan to celebrate National Day with the rest of the nation and to rejoice in the great victories of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the Fujian frontline artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have been ordered to cease artillery strikes on October 1st and 3rd for two days, as a gesture of concern.

### 让大、小金门等岛屿的祖国同胞欢度国庆  福建前线炮兵部队奉命停止炮击两天

1967-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    让大、小金门等岛屿的祖国同胞欢度国庆
    福建前线炮兵部队奉命停止炮击两天
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线部队司令部发言人宣布，十月一日是中华人民共和国成立十八周年国庆节，为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞和国民党军官兵同全国人民一道欢度国庆，欢呼无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在十月一日和三日两天停止炮击，以示关怀。
