Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-09-12
Edition: 1

    Our task is to lead a great nation of tens of millions of people in an unprecedented, great struggle. Therefore, the task of universally and deeply studying the theories of Marxism-Leninism is, for us, a major problem that urgently needs to be solved and to which we must devote considerable effort.

    _China's Communist Party in the National War_
