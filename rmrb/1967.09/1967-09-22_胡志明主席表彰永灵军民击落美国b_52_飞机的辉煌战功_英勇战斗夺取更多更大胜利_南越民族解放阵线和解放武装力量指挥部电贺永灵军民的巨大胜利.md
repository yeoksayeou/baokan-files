Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Commends the Heroic People and Soldiers of Vinh Linh for Shooting Down U.S. B-52 Bombers and Calls for Further Victories
Author:
Date: 1967-09-22
Edition: 6

Chairman Ho Chi Minh Commends the Heroic People and Soldiers of Vinh Linh for Shooting Down U.S. B-52 Bombers and Calls for Further Victories

The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the Command of the Liberation Armed Forces Congratulate the Great Victory of the People and Soldiers of Vinh Linh

	Hanoi, September 21 - According to reports from newspapers here on September 21, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently signed an order to award the military merit medal of the second class to a unit of the Vinh Linh garrison that shot down two U.S. B-52 bombers.

	Hanoi, September 21 - According to reports from newspapers here on September 21, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam wrote a letter on September 20 to the people and soldiers of Vinh Linh, warmly praising them for their brilliant feat of shooting down two U.S. B-52 bombers on September 17.

	The letter said, "The people, soldiers, and cadres of Vinh Linh have fought well and accurately. Vinh Linh truly deserves to be the heroic frontline of the socialist North."

	The letter encouraged the people and soldiers of Vinh Linh to uphold the traditions of unity, bravery, and strength in their fighting and production, and to achieve more and greater victories.

	Hanoi, September 20 - According to reports from newspapers here on September 20, the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army sent a telegram on September 19 to the anti-aircraft units of Vinh Linh that shot down two U.S. B-52 bombers, warmly praising their new achievements.

	The telegram said, "In the battle to severely punish the U.S. aggressors and avenge the people of Vinh Linh and the people of the North and South, you have always been vigilant, discovered the enemy in a timely manner, remained calm and brave, seized the opportunity, and fought well and accurately."

	The telegram called on the anti-aircraft units of Vinh Linh to continue to deepen their hatred for the U.S. aggressors, continuously improve their combat effectiveness, strive for greater victories, and resolutely foil all the plots of the U.S. aggressors.

	Hanoi, September 21 - According to reports from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the Command of the Liberation Armed Forces sent a telegram on September 19 to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, warmly congratulating the heroic people and soldiers of Vinh Linh on their great victory of shooting down two U.S. B-52 bombers on September 17.

	The telegram pointed out that this great victory is a new great encouragement to the people of the South. It shows that no matter what kind of modern bomber the U.S. aggressors use, they cannot escape the punishment of the Vietnamese people and soldiers. If the U.S. imperialists continue to attack the North, they will surely suffer more and more severe punishments.

	The telegram expressed the determination to actively learn from the examples of the heroic Vinh Linh and the people and soldiers of the North, to advance victoriously, and to thoroughly defeat the U.S. imperialists and their lackeys.

### 胡志明主席表彰永灵军民击落美国B—52飞机的辉煌战功　英勇战斗夺取更多更大胜利　南越民族解放阵线和解放武装力量指挥部电贺永灵军民的巨大胜利

1967-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席表彰永灵军民击落美国B—52飞机的辉煌战功
　　英勇战斗夺取更多更大胜利
　　南越民族解放阵线和解放武装力量指挥部电贺永灵军民的巨大胜利
    新华社河内二十一日电　据这里报纸二十一日报道：越南民主共和国主席胡志明，最近签署一项命令，授予击落美国B—52轰炸机两架的永灵驻军某立功单位以二级军功勋章。
    新华社河内二十一日电　据这里报纸二十一日报道：越南民主共和国主席胡志明，二十日写信给永灵区军民，热烈表扬他们十七日击落美国强盗B—52轰炸机两架的辉煌战功。
    信中说，永灵同胞、战士和干部们打得好，打得准。永灵真不愧为社会主义北方的英雄前线。
    信中勉励永灵军民在战斗和生产中发扬团结、英勇、坚强的传统，夺取更多更大的胜利。
    新华社河内二十日电　据这里报纸二十日报道：越南人民军总司令部十九日打电报给十七日击落美国B—52轰炸机两架的永灵区防空部队各有功单位，热烈表扬他们建立的新功勋。
    电报说，在严惩美国强盗、和为它们杀害的永灵人民和南北两方人民报仇的战斗中，你们时刻保持警惕，及时发现敌人，沉着、勇敢，掌握时机，打得好，打得准。
    电报号召永灵区防空部队各有功单位继续加深对美国侵略强盗的仇恨，不断提高战斗力，争取更大的胜利，坚决粉碎美国强盗的一切阴谋。
    新华社河内二十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会和解放武装力量指挥部十九日打电报给越南祖国战线中央委员会和越南人民军总司令部，热烈祝贺英雄的永灵区军民十七日击落两架美国B—52轰炸机的巨大胜利。
    电报指出，这一伟大胜利，是对南方同胞的新的巨大的鼓舞。它表明，美国强盗的任何一种现代化轰炸机，都无法逃脱越南军民的惩罚。如果美帝国主义继续袭击北方，那么，它必将遭到更多更严厉的惩罚。
    电报表示要积极学习英雄的永灵区和北方军民的榜样，乘胜前进，彻底打败美帝国主义及其走狗。
