Title: The Masses of Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Revolutionary Cadres in Hongjing Production Brigade, Shaqiu Commune, Taicang County, Jiangsu Province, Resolutely Carry Out Chairman Mao's Great Line of "Grasp Revolution, Promote Production" and Achieve Double Victory in Revolution and Production
Author: Fang Ailing
Date: 1967-09-13
Edition: 4

The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants and revolutionary cadres in Hongjing Production Brigade, Shaqiu Commune, Taicang County, Jiangsu Province, are resolutely carrying out Chairman Mao's great line of "grasp revolution, promote production." They have achieved double victories in revolution and production. The commune members are studying the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" on the cotton drying ground.

	New China News Agency reporter Fang Ailing
