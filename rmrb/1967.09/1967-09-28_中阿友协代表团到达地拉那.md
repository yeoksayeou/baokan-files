Title: Delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association Arrives in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1967-09-28
Edition: 6

The delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Minister of Defense, arrived in Tirana by airplane at noon on the 26th. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Assembly, Kelaizi, and other leaders, as well as 2,000 people from the Albanian capital.

The delegation was invited by the Albania-China Friendship Association to visit Albania and participate in the celebrations of the Chinese National Day by the Albanian people.

Today, large portraits of the great leaders of the Chinese and Albanian peoples, Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha, were hung in the airport building. The national flags of the two countries fluttered in the wind. The welcoming crowd held up portraits of Chairman Mao and Comrade Hoxha, as well as the national flags of the two countries. They also held up banners with slogans such as "Long live the unbreakable friendship between Albania and China!" "Long live the Communist Party of China led by Comrade Mao Zedong!" "Long live the Party of Labor of Albania led by Comrade Enver Hoxha!" When Comrade Wang Shusheng and other members of the delegation stepped out of the airport accompanied by Comrade Kelaizi and others, the welcoming crowd waved red flags and flowers, shouting "Enver-Mao!"

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport also included: Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, Poci, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Malile, Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Commission, Treska, Vice Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association, Dumi and Spase, Secretary of the Women's Federation, Mufitiu, and Secretary of the Tirana Municipal Committee, Yiftica. The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Tingqian, and the military attache, Li Yifan, also warmly welcomed the delegation at the airport. The delegation of Chinese scientific workers also arrived in Tirana by the same flight and was warmly welcomed by the Vice President of Tirana University, Ladovitska, and representatives of various circles in the capital.

### 中阿友协代表团到达地拉那

1967-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    中阿友协代表团到达地拉那
    据新华社地拉那二十六日电　由中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领的中阿友协代表团，二十六日中午乘飞机到达地拉那。中阿友协代表团受到阿中友协主席、地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席凯莱齐等领导人和阿尔巴尼亚首都两千名群众的极其热烈欢迎。
    代表团是应阿中友协的邀请，前来进行友好访问，并参加阿尔巴尼亚人民庆祝中国国庆活动的。
    今天，地拉那机场大楼上悬挂着中阿两国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志和霍查同志的巨幅画像。机场上中阿两国国旗迎风飘扬。欢迎的群众高举毛主席和霍查同志的画像以及中阿两国国旗。他们还举着标语牌，上面写着：“阿中牢不可破的友谊万岁！”“以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党万岁！”“以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党万岁！”当王树声同志和代表团其他成员在凯莱齐等同志的陪同下步出机场的时候，欢迎的群众挥动红旗和鲜花，高呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”。
    到机场欢迎中阿友协代表团的还有：阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部副主任波奇，外交部副部长马利列，对外文委主席特雷斯卡，阿中友协副主席杜米和斯帕塞，妇联秘书长穆夫蒂乌和地拉那市委书记伊夫蒂查。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和武官李一凡也到机场热烈欢迎代表团。中国科学工作者代表团也同机到达地拉那，受到地拉那大学副校长拉多维茨卡和首都各界群众代表的热烈欢迎。
