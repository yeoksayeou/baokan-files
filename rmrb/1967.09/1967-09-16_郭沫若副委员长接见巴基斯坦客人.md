Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-09-16
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today received Pakistani Education Secretary Shahab and his wife, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan and his wife were also present at the meeting.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见巴基斯坦客人

1967-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社十五日讯  人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了巴基斯坦教育部秘书夏哈布和他的夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗和夫人也在座。
