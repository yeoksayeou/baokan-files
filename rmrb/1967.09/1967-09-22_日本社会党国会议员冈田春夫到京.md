Title: Japanese Socialist Party Member of Parliament Okada Haruo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-22
Edition: 6

Japanese Socialist Party Member of Parliament Okada Haruo arrives in Beijing

New China News Agency, September 21st: Japanese Socialist Party Member of Parliament Okada Haruo, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, arrived in China for a friendly visit and reached Beijing on the 20th.

### 日本社会党国会议员冈田春夫到京

1967-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　日本社会党国会议员冈田春夫到京
    新华社二十一日讯　日本社会党国会议员冈田春夫应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国进行友好访问，于二十日到达北京。
