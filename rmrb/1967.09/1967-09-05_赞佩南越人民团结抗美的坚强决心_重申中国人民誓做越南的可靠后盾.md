Title: Praise for the Determination of the South Vietnamese People to Unite and Resist America
Author:
Date: 1967-09-05
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Deputy Head of Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong
Praise for the Determination of the South Vietnamese People to Unite and Resist America
Reaffirming the Chinese People's Commitment to Be a Reliable Backer of Vietnam

Nguyen Minh Phuong, representing the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, expressed deep gratitude to Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin for the support of the Chinese people and handed over the political program of the Liberation Front.

New China News Agency, September 4th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held a cordial and friendly conversation with Nguyen Minh Phuong, the acting head of the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam stationed in China, this afternoon.

During the meeting, Nguyen Minh Phuong, on behalf of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, expressed deep gratitude to Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese government for their strong support for the people of South Vietnam. Nguyen Minh Phuong also handed over the political program adopted by the extraordinary meeting of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to Premier Zhou.

Premier Zhou expressed admiration for the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam's mobilization and unity of all possible forces to participate in and support the anti-American, national salvation war, defeat the American aggressors, and thoroughly eliminate and disintegrate the puppet army until final victory is achieved.

Premier Zhou reaffirmed that the 700 million Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are determined to be a reliable backbone for the Vietnamese people; the Chinese Communist Party and government will, as always, resolutely support the Vietnamese people's anti-American, national salvation war.

Present at the meeting were Zhao Yimin and Han Nianlong. (Accompanying photograph)

### 赞佩南越人民团结抗美的坚强决心　重申中国人民誓做越南的可靠后盾

1967-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见阮明芳代理团长
　　赞佩南越人民团结抗美的坚强决心
　　重申中国人民誓做越南的可靠后盾
    阮明芳代表南越民族解放阵线中央向毛主席林副主席表示深切感谢中国人民支援，并递交解放阵线政治纲领。
    新华社四日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天下午接见了越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，阮明芳代理团长代表越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会向毛主席、林彪副主席、中国党和政府表示深切的感谢，感谢中国人民对越南南方人民的大力支援。阮明芳代理团长还向周总理递交了越南南方民族解放阵线非常会议通过的解放阵线政治纲领。
    周总理对越南南方民族解放阵线动员、团结一切可以团结的力量，参加和支持抗美救国战争，打败美国侵略者，彻底消灭和瓦解伪军，直到取得最后胜利的坚强决心，表示赞佩。
    周总理重申，用毛泽东思想武装起来的七亿中国人民，誓做越南人民的可靠后盾；中国党和政府将一如既往地坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争。
    接见时在座的有赵毅敏、韩念龙。（附图片）
    九月四日，周恩来总理接见越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳。图为周恩来总理和阮明芳代理团长进行亲切友好的谈话。新华社记者　齐铁砚摄
