Title: PLA Border Troops in Tibet Help Tibetan People with Autumn Harvest
Author:
Date: 1967-09-21
Edition: 3

The broad ranks of officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army border troops in Tibet have enthusiastically responded to the great call of the great leader Chairman Mao to "grasp revolution and promote production." In recent days, they have actively helped Tibetan people with the autumn harvest, receiving a warm welcome from the Tibetan compatriots.

At present, the highlands of Tibet are covered with golden wheat and highland barley, with thick stalks and full ears. The autumn harvest assistance teams composed of the broad ranks of officers and soldiers are marching to the fields in batches, drums and gongs resounding. In the fields, soldiers and civilians work together, sing revolutionary songs together, and everywhere is filled with deep affection between the army and the people.

### 西藏边防部队帮助藏族人民秋收

1967-09-21
第3版()
专栏：

　  西藏边防部队帮助藏族人民秋收
    人民解放军西藏边防部队广大指战员热烈响应伟大统帅毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召，连日来，积极帮助藏族人民秋收，受到藏族同胞的热烈欢迎。
    目前，西藏高原小麦、青稞秆粗穗饱，一片金黄。由广大指战员组成的助民秋收队伍，一批批地敲锣打鼓奔赴田间。田野里军民一起劳动，一起唱革命歌曲，到处洋溢着军民的深情厚谊。
