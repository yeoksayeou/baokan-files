Iraqi Ambassador to China Leaves for Home on Recall

The Iraqi Ambassador to China, Rashid, has left his post and departed from Beijing by plane on August 31st.

North Vietnam's Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three More U.S. Planes

On August 30th, the militia in the river province of Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor plane. On August 29th, the military and civilians in the river province and Haiphong city shot down one U.S. plane each.

Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Strike Back at the Enemy

In the first half of August, the guerrilla forces and local troops in the provinces of Xieng Khouang and Luang Prabang in Laos launched extensive attacks, annihilating more than 200 enemy troops invading the liberated areas.

In Xieng Khouang province, the guerrilla forces and local troops launched consecutive attacks on the invading enemy on the 6th, 9th, and 10th, killing 101 enemy troops and seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies.

In Luang Prabang province, the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys, from the 4th to the 13th, gathered a large number of troops to launch "sweeping" operations in many areas of the Xieng Ngeun district. The local military and civilians fought a fierce defensive battle, leaving the enemy disoriented, killing 77 on the spot, and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies. The guerrilla forces and local troops in the Nam Bao area annihilated 34 enemy troops in the anti-"sweeping" battles from the 1st to the 13th.

Indonesian Reactionaries Resume Relations with "Malaysia"

Following the orders of their American masters, the fascist military regime in Indonesia openly announced on August 31st the resumption of diplomatic relations with the neo-colonialist product "Malaysia." This further exposed the ugly face of this reactionary regime as an active tool of U.S. and British imperialism in Asia.

In recent days, to resume diplomatic relations with "Malaysia," the fascist military regime in Indonesia has conducted a series of intense activities. The puppet "Cooperative Parliament" of this reactionary regime passed a resolution on the 25th, demanding the resumption of relations with "Malaysia" on its "National Day" — the 31st.

### 简明新闻

1967-09-01
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

　　简明新闻
　　伊拉克驻华大使奉调离任回国
    伊拉克驻华大使拉希德奉调离任，已于三十一日乘飞机离京回国。
　　越南北方军民又击落美机三架
    越南北方河静省民兵三十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。河西省和海防市军民二十九日击落美机各一架。
　　老挝爱国军民狠狠地打击敌人
    老挝川圹、琅勃拉邦省游击队和地方部队，在八月上半月广泛出击，消灭侵犯解放区的敌人二百多名。
    川圹省游击队和地方部队，在六日、九日和十日三天中，接连打击进犯的敌人，歼敌一百零一名，缴获一批武器和军用物资。
    在琅勃拉邦省，美国强盗及其走狗，从四日到十三日纠集大批军队对川银县许多地区发动“扫荡”。当地军民进行了无比英勇的自卫战，把敌人打得晕头转向，当场消灭敌人七十七名，缴获大批武器和军用物资。南博地区游击队和地方部队，在一日到十三日的反“扫荡”战斗中，歼敌三十四名。
　　印尼反动派同“马来西亚”复交
    印度尼西亚法西斯军人政权秉承美国主子的旨意，在八月三十一日公开宣布同新殖民主义产物“马来西亚”恢复外交关系，从而进一步暴露了这个反动政权积极充当美英帝国主义在亚洲的侵略工具的丑恶嘴脸。
    几天来，为了恢复同“马来西亚”的外交关系，印尼法西斯军人政权进行了一系列紧张的活动。这个反动政权的御用工具“合作国会”二十五日通过决议，要求在“马来西亚”的“国庆日”——三十一日同它恢复外交关系。
                            （据新华社讯）
