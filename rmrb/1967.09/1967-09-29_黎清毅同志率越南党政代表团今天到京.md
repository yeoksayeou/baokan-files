Title: Comrade Le Quyet Thien Leads Vietnamese Party-Government Delegation to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China, a Vietnamese Party-Government Delegation led by Le Quyet Thien, Politburo Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, will arrive in Beijing on September 29. The delegation will participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The delegation members are:

	Le Quyet Thien, Politburo Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam,
	Li Ban, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade,
	Nguyen Minh Loan, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China.

### 黎清毅同志率越南党政代表团今天到京

1967-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　黎清毅同志率越南党政代表团今天到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会和中华人民共和国政府的邀请，由越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南党政代表团，将于二十九日到达北京。代表团将参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年的庆祝活动。
    代表团团员有：
    越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会副主席黄文欢，
    越南劳动党中央委员会候补委员、对外贸易部副部长李班，
    越南劳动党中央委员会候补委员、越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使吴明鸾。
