		Quotations from Chairman Mao

		After our socialist revolution has basically achieved victory, there are still some people in society who dream of restoring the capitalist system. They will wage struggles against the working class from all sides, including ideological struggles.

		_From "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"_

### 毛主席语录

1967-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席语录
    在我国社会主义革命取得基本胜利以后，社会上还有一部分人梦想恢复资本主义制度，他们要从各个方面向工人阶级进行斗争，包括思想方面的斗争。
                      《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》
