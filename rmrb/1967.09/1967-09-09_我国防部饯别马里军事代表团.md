Title: The Chinese Ministry of National Defense Sees Off the Malian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-09-09
Edition: 6

The Chinese Ministry of National Defense held a banquet in Shanghai this evening to see off the Malian military delegation led by Captain Diaby Sila Diallo. At the banquet, filled with a friendly atmosphere, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense Pan Zhenwu and Captain Diallo both spoke.

Pan Zhenwu said, the Chinese people, following the teachings of the great leader Chairman Mao, firmly stand on the side of the Malian people. The friendship between the military and people of China and Mali, based on opposing both old and new colonialism, will surely grow stronger day by day.

Captain Diallo, in his speech, enthusiastically praised the great leader Chairman Mao and the brilliant victories achieved by the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao. He said, Chairman Mao Zedong is not only the savior of the Chinese people but also the hope of all oppressed peoples.

Senior officials from the People's Liberation Army's land, sea, and air forces stationed in Shanghai, as well as relevant departments, attended the banquet.

### 我国防部饯别马里军事代表团

1967-09-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　我国防部饯别马里军事代表团
    据新华社上海八日电　国防部今晚在上海举行宴会，欢送由迪比·席拉·迪亚拉上尉率领的马里共和国军事代表团。在充满友好气氛的宴会上，国防部办公厅副主任潘振武和迪亚拉上尉先后讲了话。
    潘振武说，我国人民遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导，坚定不移地站在马里人民一边。中、马两国军队和人民的友谊在反对新老殖民主义基础上，必将与日俱增。
    迪亚拉上尉在讲话中，热烈赞颂伟大领袖毛主席和毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命取得的辉煌胜利。他说，毛泽东主席不仅是中国人民的救星，也是一切被压迫人民的希望。
    人民解放军驻上海陆海空三军和有关方面负责人出席了宴会。
