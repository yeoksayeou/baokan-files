Title: Iraqi Scientific and Technological Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-21
Edition: 4

An Iraqi scientific and technological delegation, led by Chairman Fadhil Taiyer of the Iraqi Republic's Supreme Scientific Committee, arrived in Beijing today by airplane to visit China in a friendly capacity.

### 伊拉克科技代表团到京

1967-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克科技代表团到京
    新华社十九日讯　前来我国进行友好访问的伊拉克共和国科学技术代表团，在团长、伊拉克共和国最高科学委员会主席法迪尔·塔伊勒率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
