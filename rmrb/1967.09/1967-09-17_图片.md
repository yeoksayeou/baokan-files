Title: PLA Unit 6299 and Revolutionary Masses United in Struggle in Wenzhou
Author: Zhang Shenming
Date: 1967-09-17
Edition: 4

    The 6299 unit of the People's Liberation Army, stationed in Wenzhou to support the left, is united in heart with the revolutionary masses of the proletariat. They fight together and labor together. This is a scene of the soldiers and officers of the 6299 unit working alongside the workers of the Wenzhou Port Bureau to unload chemical fertilizer.

    Photograph by Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Shenming.
