Title: Governments of China, Tanzania, and Zambia Sign Agreement to Build Tanzam Railway
Author:
Date: 1967-09-06
Edition: 4

The governments of the People's Republic of China, the United Republic of Tanzania, and the Republic of Zambia signed the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the United Republic of Tanzania, and the Government of the Republic of Zambia on the Construction of the Tanzania-Zambia Railway" in Beijing this evening. The signing of this agreement will further strengthen the friendship among the peoples of China, Tanzania, and Zambia, and promote the development of economic and technological cooperation among the three countries.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.

Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, signed on behalf of the Chinese government. Jamal, Minister of Finance of Tanzania, and Sikota, Minister of State of Zambia, signed on behalf of the governments of Tanzania and Zambia, respectively.

Those who attended the signing ceremony also included Yang Lin, Xie Huai-de, Xu Yixin, and Guo Lu from relevant departments of China; Mwaluko, Ambassador of Tanzania to China; and members of the Tanzania-Zambia Joint Economic Delegation.

### 中国坦桑尼亚赞比亚三国政府  签订关于修建坦赞铁路的协定

1967-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国坦桑尼亚赞比亚三国政府
　　签订关于修建坦赞铁路的协定
    新华社五日讯　“中华人民共和国政府和坦桑尼亚联合共和国政府、赞比亚共和国政府关于修建坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路的协定”，今天晚上在北京签字。这个协定的签订，将进一步加强中国、坦桑尼亚、赞比亚三国人民之间的友谊，和促进三国经济技术合作关系的发展。
    国务院总理周恩来参加了签字仪式。
    代表中国政府在协定上签字的是国务院副总理李先念。代表坦桑尼亚政府、赞比亚政府在协定上签字的，是坦桑尼亚财政部长贾马勒和赞比亚国务部长索科。
    参加签字仪式的，还有我国各有关方面的负责人杨琳、谢怀德、徐以新、郭鲁；坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科；坦桑尼亚、赞比亚联合经济代表团的成员。
