Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into China's Fujian and Zhejiang Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1967-09-28
Edition: 6

On September 25 at 4:15 AM to September 26 at 8:16 AM, a U.S. warship intruded into China's territorial waters east of Huangqi Peninsula, Fujian Province, three times. On September 25 at 6:35 AM to 3:25 PM, another U.S. warship intruded into China's territorial waters east of the Zhoushan Archipelago, Zhejiang Province, three times. In response to these military provocations by U.S. warships, the spokesperson of China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 442nd serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建、浙江领海　我提出第四百四十二次严重警告

1967-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国军舰侵入我福建、浙江领海
　　我提出第四百四十二次严重警告
    新华社二十六日讯　九月二十五日四时十五分至二十六日八时十六分，美国军舰一艘，三次侵入我福建省黄岐半岛以东领海。二十五日六时三十五分至十五时二十五分，又有美国军舰一艘，三次侵入我浙江省舟山群岛以东领海。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百四十二次严重警告。
