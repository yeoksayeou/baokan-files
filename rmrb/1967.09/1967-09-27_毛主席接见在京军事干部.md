Title: Chairman Mao Receives Military Cadres in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-27
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao received military cadres in Beijing.

Chairman Mao, radiant and full of vigor, arrived at the reception venue. The broad masses of military cadres of the People's Liberation Army enthusiastically and excitedly raised their arms to shout: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand ten thousand years!" "Wish the great leader Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!" "Forever loyal to the great leader Chairman Mao!" "Forever loyal to Mao Zedong Thought!" "Forever loyal to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!" "Wherever Chairman Mao points, we will fight to that place!" "Carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!"

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: In the unprecedentedly good situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, on the eve of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on the evening of September 26th, our great teacher Chairman Mao received military cadres from various provinces who are studying in Beijing, and military leaders from some provinces and regions who are attending meetings in Beijing.

When Chairman Mao, radiant and full of vigor, arrived at the reception venue, the entire venue erupted in a long, thunderous applause and cheers. The broad masses of military cadres of the People's Liberation Army, with extremely enthusiastic and excited hearts, raised their arms to shout: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand ten thousand years!" "Wish the great leader Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!" "Forever loyal to the great leader Chairman Mao!" "Forever loyal to Mao Zedong Thought!" "Forever loyal to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!" "Wherever Chairman Mao points, we will fight to that place!" "Carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!"

### 毛主席接见在京军事干部

1967-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见在京军事干部
    毛主席神采奕奕地来到接见会场，解放军广大干部极其热烈极其激动地振臂高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”“祝伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”“永远忠于伟大领袖毛主席！”“永远忠于毛泽东思想！”“永远忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线！”“毛主席指向那里，就打到那里！”“把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”
    新华社二十六日讯在无产阶级文化大革命空前大好的形势下，在中华人民共和国成立十八周年的前夕，九月二十六日晚，我们的伟大导师毛主席，接见了正在北京学习的各省军事干部，和在北京开会的一些省、区的军队领导人。
    当毛主席神采奕奕地来到接见会场时，全场响起了长时间的暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼，人民解放军广大干部以极其热烈、极其激动的心情振臂高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”“祝伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”“永远忠于伟大领袖毛主席！”“永远忠于毛泽东思想！”“永远忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线！”“毛主席指向那里，就打到那里！”“把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”
