Title: Workers of the East Is Red No. 2 Passenger Ship and Revolutionary Staff Hold a Criticism Meeting
Author: Zhao Jieqing
Date: 1967-09-05
Edition: 2

    Workers from the East Is Red No. 2 passenger ship construction team and revolutionary staff on board held a criticism meeting. They linked the struggle in their unit to criticize China's Khrushchev and his agents in the Wuhan area for their heinous crimes of opposing the Party, opposing socialism, and opposing Mao Zedong Thought.
