Title: Publication of "Mao Zedong on People's War" and "Long Live the Victory of People's War"
Author:
Date: 1967-09-16
Edition: 2

The great helmsman Chairman Mao's sayings on people's war—"Mao Zedong on People's War," and the reissued "Long Live the Victory of People's War" by Comrade Lin Biao, have been published by the People's Publishing House and are now on sale at the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore. They will also be distributed nationwide.

"Mao Zedong on People's War" features a portrait of our great leader Chairman Mao and a dedication by Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao: "Study Chairman Mao's works, follow Chairman Mao's words, act according to Chairman Mao's instructions, and be a good soldier of Chairman Mao's." The book also includes editorial notes from "Red Flag" magazine, "People's Daily," and "Liberation Army Daily" for the publication of Chairman Mao's sayings on people's war.

"Long Live the Victory of People's War" was first published by the People's Publishing House in September 1965. This reissue is based on the reissued version of Comrade Lin Biao's brilliant work.

### 《毛主席论人民战争》、《人民战争胜利万岁》两书出版

1967-09-16
第2版()
专栏：

    《毛主席论人民战争》、《人民战争胜利万岁》两书出版
    新华社十五日讯　伟大统帅毛主席论人民战争的语录——《毛主席论人民战争》，和重新发表的林彪同志的《人民战争胜利万岁》，由人民出版社分别出版，已在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地发行。
    《毛主席论人民战争》一书，印有我们伟大领袖毛主席的像页，和毛主席的亲密战友林彪同志的题词：“读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好战士”。书末附有《红旗》杂志、《人民日报》、《解放军报》为发表毛主席论人民战争语录写的按语。
    《人民战争胜利万岁》一书，早在一九六五年九月由人民出版社出版；这次再版是根据重新发表的林彪同志这一光辉著作排印的。
