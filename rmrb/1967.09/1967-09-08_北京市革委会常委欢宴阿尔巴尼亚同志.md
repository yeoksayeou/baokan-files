Title: Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee Standing Committee Hosts Banquet for Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1967-09-08
Edition: 5

Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee Standing Committee Hosts Banquet for Albanian Comrades

According to Xinhua News Agency, Ding Guoyi, a standing committee member of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the entire delegation of the Tirana volleyball and shooting teams from Albania, led by Pi Tabia.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Nataneli, attended the banquet.

Ding Guoyi, Ambassador Nataneli, and Pi Tabia all spoke at the banquet.

Representatives from the joint performance committee of the revolutionary masses of the army, navy, and air force in Beijing, as well as athletes, attended today's banquet to welcome the Albanian comrades. Comrades from both China and Albania engaged in friendly and cordial conversations during the banquet. The Albanian Deputy Ambassador to China, Baba Ni, also attended the banquet.

### 北京市革委会常委欢宴阿尔巴尼亚同志

1967-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

　　北京市革委会常委欢宴阿尔巴尼亚同志
    据新华社七日讯　北京市革命委员会常委丁国钰今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由彼·塔比亚率领的阿尔巴尼亚地拉那排球队和射击队全体成员。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使纳塔奈利出席了宴会。
    丁国钰同志、纳塔奈利大使和彼·塔比亚同志在宴会上讲了话。
    出席今天宴会欢迎阿尔巴尼亚同志的还有陆海空三军驻京部队无产阶级革命派文体战士联合演出委员会和运动员的代表。中阿两国同志在宴会上进行了亲切友好的交谈。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞巴巴尼也出席了宴会。
