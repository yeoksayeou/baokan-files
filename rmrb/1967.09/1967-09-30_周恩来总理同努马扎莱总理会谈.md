Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Prime Minister Numa Azali
Author:
Date: 1967-09-30
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai met with Prime Minister Ambrwose Numa Azali of the Republic of Congo (Brazzaville) today at noon for talks.

The talks were held in a friendly atmosphere.

Those present from the Chinese side included Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and relevant officials Zhang Shan, Yang Lin, Xu Yixin, and Gong Dafei. From the Congolese (Brazzaville) side, members of the National Revolutionary Movement and the government delegation included Gabriel Bouka, Felix Tati-Gustave, Andre Ombesa, Auguste Tchouafou, Jean-Michel Monbounou, and the Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Bazanga.

On September 29, Premier Zhou Enlai held friendly talks with Prime Minister Ambrwose Numa Azali of the Republic of Congo (Brazzaville). Photo by our reporter, Lü Xiangyou.

### 周恩来总理同努马扎莱总理会谈

1967-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理同努马扎莱总理会谈
    新华社二十九日讯　国务院总理周恩来同刚果共和国（布拉柴维尔）总理安布鲁瓦斯·努马扎莱，今天中午举行了会谈。
    会谈在友好气氛中进行。
    参加会谈的：中国方面有国务院副总理李先念，以及有关方面负责人曾山、杨琳、徐以新和宫达非；刚果（布）方面有全国革命运动和政府代表团成员：加布里埃尔·布卡，费利克斯·塔蒂—古斯塔夫，安德烈·翁贝萨，奥古斯特·契乌夫，让—米歇尔·蒙布努，以及刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加。（附图片）
