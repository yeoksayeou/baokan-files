Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Meets Tanzanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-09-05
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Li Xiannian today met with Peter Kisumo, Minister of Local Government and Rural Development of Tanzania, Chief Secretary Mchuiuru, Cultural Commissioner Ntiryo, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Tanzanian Ambassador to China Mwaluko was also present at the meeting. The Tanzanian guests arrived in China on September 2.

### 李先念副总理接见坦桑尼亚客人

1967-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　李先念副总理接见坦桑尼亚客人
    新华社四日讯　李先念副总理今天接见了坦桑尼亚地方政府和乡村发展部部长彼得·基苏莫，首席秘书姆丘乌鲁，文化专员恩蒂罗，和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也在座。坦桑尼亚客人是九月二日来我国的。
