Title: The Da Zhai Photo Exhibition Opens in the Capital of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1967-09-19
Edition: 5

The Da Zhai photo exhibition opened on September 16 at the municipal hall in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan. Afghan audiences showed great interest in the photos reflecting the tremendous achievements of the Chinese working people under the guidance of the great, invincible thought of Chairman Mao Zedong.

Afghan Parliament's Senate President Abdul Hadi Dawi and officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, News, and Culture attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

The colorful portrait and bust of the great leader Chairman Mao displayed at the exhibition attracted the audience. They expressed their love for the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and the works of Chairman Mao displayed at the exhibition and requested copies from the Chinese staff.

### 大寨图片展览在阿富汗首都开幕

1967-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    大寨图片展览在阿富汗首都开幕
    据新华社喀布尔十七日电　中国大寨图片展览会十六日在阿富汗首都喀布尔的市政厅开幕。阿富汗观众对展出的图片上反映的中国劳动人民在伟大的战无不胜的毛泽东思想指引下取得的巨大成就，表示极大的兴趣。
    阿富汗议会长老院议长阿卜杜勒·哈迪·达威以及外交部、新闻和文化部的一些官员出席了展览会的开幕式。
    在展览会上陈设的伟大领袖毛主席的彩色像和半身塑像吸引着观众。他们对展览会上陈列着的《毛主席语录》和毛主席著作，表示热爱并且向中国人员索取。
