Title: Norwegian Regional Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 8

At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, a delegation from the Norwegian regional trade unions, consisting of four members led by Hans Borgin, Chairman of the Norwegian Building Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing on September 27.

### 挪威地区工会代表团到京

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　挪威地区工会代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国进行友好访问的挪威地区工会代表团，一行四人，在拜鲁姆建筑工会主席汗斯·波根率领下，二十七日到达北京。
