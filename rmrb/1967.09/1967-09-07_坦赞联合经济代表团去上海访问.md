Title: Tanzanian and Zambian Economic Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1967-09-07
Edition: 6

A joint economic delegation from Tanzania and Zambia, led by Tanzania's Minister of Finance, Jamal, and Zambia's Minister of State, Soko, departed Beijing this morning for a visit to Shanghai. After completing their visit, they will return to their respective countries.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council, along with more than a thousand revolutionary masses from various sectors of the capital, went to the airport to see them off. Tanzania's Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, also went to the airport to bid farewell.

### 坦赞联合经济代表团去上海访问

1967-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　坦赞联合经济代表团去上海访问
    新华社六日讯　由坦桑尼亚财政部长贾马勒和赞比亚国务部长索科率领的坦桑尼亚、赞比亚联合经济代表团，今天上午离开北京前往上海参观访问，访问结束后回国。
    国务院副总理李先念，首都各界革命群众一千多人，到机场欢送。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科到机场送行。
