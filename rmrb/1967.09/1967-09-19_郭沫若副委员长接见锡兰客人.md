Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Sri Lankan Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-09-19
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo received Sri Lankan guests this afternoon. He had a friendly conversation with the leader of the Sri Lankan opposition party, Sirimavo Bandaranaike.

Sirimavo Bandaranaike came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to conduct a friendly visit. She will also be attending the celebration of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见锡兰客人

1967-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见锡兰客人
    新华社十八日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见锡兰自由党议员、锡兰议会反对党领袖西里瓦德纳，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    西里瓦德纳是应中国人民外交学会邀请，前来中国进行友好访问的，并将应邀参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年国庆。
