	Chairman Mao's Quotations

	The working class must transform the entire society in the course of class struggle and struggle against nature, and in doing so transform itself. The working class must continually learn in the course of work, gradually overcome its shortcomings, and never stop. — *On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People*
