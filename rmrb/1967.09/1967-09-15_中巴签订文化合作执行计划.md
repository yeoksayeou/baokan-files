Title: China and Pakistan Sign Cultural Cooperation Execution Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-09-15
Edition: 6

The 1967-1968 cultural cooperation execution plan between the People's Republic of China and Pakistan was signed in Beijing today. Zhou Yiping, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Republic's Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Shahab, Secretary of the Pakistani Ministry of Education, signed the execution plan on behalf of both sides. After the signing ceremony, Zhang Siyi, Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, hosted a banquet to welcome the Pakistani guests and celebrate the signing of the cultural cooperation execution plan. Sultan M. Khan, Pakistani Ambassador to China, attended the signing ceremony and the banquet.

### 中巴签订文化合作执行计划

1967-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    中巴签订文化合作执行计划
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国和巴基斯坦一九六七年至一九六八年度文化合作执行计划，今天在北京签字。我国对外文委副主任周一萍和巴基斯坦教育部秘书夏哈布，分别代表双方在执行计划上签字。签字以后，对外文委主任张奚若举行宴会招待巴基斯坦客人，并庆祝文化合作执行计划的签字。巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗参加了签字仪式和宴会。
