Title: Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Friendly Visit and National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1967-09-28
Edition: 1

	At the invitation of the Chinese government, a Pakistani government friendship delegation led by Khawaja Shahabuddin, Minister of Information and Broadcasting of Pakistan, will arrive in Beijing on September 28 to conduct a friendly visit to China and participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	New China News Agency, September 27th: The Pakistani government friendship delegation, led by Khawaja Shahabuddin, Minister of Information and Broadcasting of Pakistan, will arrive in Beijing on September 28 at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China to conduct a friendly visit to China and participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 应我国政府邀请前来友好访问并参加国庆　巴基斯坦政府友好代表团今日到京

1967-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　应我国政府邀请前来友好访问并参加国庆
　　巴基斯坦政府友好代表团今日到京
    新华社二十七日讯　由巴基斯坦新闻和广播部部长赫瓦贾·夏哈布丁率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团，应中华人民共和国政府邀请，前来我国进行友好访问，并参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年庆祝活动，将于二十八日到达北京。
