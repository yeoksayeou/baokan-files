Title: Comrade Kang Sheng Receives and Hosts a Banquet for the Central Delegation of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist)
Author:
Date: 1967-09-05
Edition: 3

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received and hosted a banquet for the central delegation of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) on August 30. The delegation included Jacques Jurquet, the head of the delegation and a member of the Secretariat of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist), and Régis Berjoan, a member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist). They had a warm and friendly conversation.

The other two members of the delegation, Raymond Casas, a member of the Secretariat of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist), and Alain Kastner, a member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist), had already returned to their country.

Present at the reception were Liu Ningyi, a member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Communist Party of China, and Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, among others. (Photo attached)

Comrade Kang Sheng poses for a group photo with the delegation of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist). Jacques Jurquet is on the right, third from the left, and Régis Berjoan is on the left, second from the right.

Photograph by Liu Changzhong, Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 康生同志接见并宴请法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央代表团

1967-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　康生同志接见并宴请法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央代表团
    新华社四日讯　中共中央政治局常委康生同志，八月三十日接见并宴请了在我国访问的法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央代表团团长、法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）书记处书记雅克·儒尔盖和团员、法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）政治局委员勒纪斯·贝热龙，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    代表团另外两位团员：法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）书记处书记雷蒙·卡萨斯和法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央委员阿兰·卡斯汤已先后回国。
    接见时在座的，有中共中央委员、书记处书记刘宁一和中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等。（附图片）
　　康生同志同法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央代表团同志合影。右三是雅克·儒尔盖同志，左二是勒纪斯·贝热龙同志。
