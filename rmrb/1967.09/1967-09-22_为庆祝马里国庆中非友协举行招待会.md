Title: Reception Held by the China-Africa Friendship Association to Celebrate the National Day of Mali
Author:
Date: 1967-09-22
Edition: 6

For the celebration of Mali's National Day, the China-Africa Friendship Association held a reception.

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 21st, at the reception held today by the China-Africa Friendship Association to celebrate Mali's National Day, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China, Senanta, enthusiastically praised the great leader of the people of the world, Chairman Mao, and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. He said, "Chairman Mao is not only the wise leader of the Chinese people but also the wise leader of all the peace-loving people of the world. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has not only achieved great successes but is also advancing further."

The Director of the Office of the Presidency of the Republic of Mali, Bakara Diallo, and his wife, who are currently visiting Beijing, were invited to attend the reception.

Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, spoke first at the reception, warmly praising the friendship between the peoples of China and Mali. He said, "The Chinese people will, as always, firmly support the just struggles of the peoples of Mali and other African countries."

Senanta thanked the Chinese people for their support and assistance to the Malian people. He said, "The Chinese government has sent experts to Mali and established factories, which is a great concern of the Chinese people for the Malian people and also a great concern for the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America."

After the reception, the documentary films "The Red Sun Illuminates the Ballet Stage" and "Chairman Mao Is Forever in Our Hearts" were screened.

### 为庆祝马里国庆中非友协举行招待会

1967-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

　  为庆祝马里国庆中非友协举行招待会
    据新华社二十一日讯在今天中非友协举行的庆祝马里国庆的招待会上，马里驻中国大使馆临时代办塞南塔热烈赞颂全世界人民的伟大领袖毛主席和中国无产阶级文化大革命。他说，毛主席不仅是中国人民的英明领袖，也是全世界一切爱好和平人民的英明领袖。中国无产阶级文化大革命不仅取得了成就，而且正向前发展。
    正在北京进行访问的马里共和国总统府办公厅主任巴卡拉·迪亚洛和夫人应邀出席了招待会。
    中非友协副会长丁西林在招待会上首先讲话，热情赞扬中马两国人民的友谊。
    他说，中国人民将一如既往地、坚定不移地支持马里和非洲各国人民的正义斗争。
    塞南塔感谢中国人民对马里人民的支持和帮助。他说，中国政府为马里派出了专家，建立了工厂，这是中国人民对马里人民的极大关怀，也是对亚非拉人民的极大关怀。
    招待会结束后放映了纪录影片《红太阳照亮了芭蕾舞台》和《毛主席永远和我们心连心》。
