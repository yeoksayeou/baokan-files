Title: The Vietnam-China Friendship Association Holds a Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 8

To celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the 9th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and to mark the return of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation from their visit to China, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association held a report meeting on September 27. The meeting introduced the great achievements of the Chinese people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction. Attending the meeting were Vice Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Dang Thai Mai, Head of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation to China Nguyen Van Huan, and others. The Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Vietnam, Lu Weiqiao, was also invited to attend the meeting. Nguyen Van Huan, on behalf of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation to China, delivered a report at the meeting.

### 越中友协举行报告会

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　越中友协举行报告会
    据新华社河内二十八日电为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年和庆祝中越友协成立九周年，并值越中友协代表团访华归来，越中友协二十七日举行报告会，介绍中国人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的伟大成就。出席报告会的有：越中友协副会长邓泰梅、越中友协访华代表团团长阮文煊等。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊应邀出席了报告会。阮文煊代表越中友协访华代表团在会上作了报告。
