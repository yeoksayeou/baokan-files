Title: Sri Lankan Freedom Party Member Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-28
Edition: 6

	Sri Lankan Freedom Party Member Arrives in Beijing

	Xinhua News Agency, September 26th—At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Sri Lankan Freedom Party member S. D. Bandaranaike and his wife arrived in Beijing this afternoon to conduct a friendly visit to China and participate in the celebrations of the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 锡兰自由党议员到京

1967-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰自由党议员到京
    新华社二十六日讯　锡兰自由党议员塞·迪·班达拉奈克和夫人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，前来我国进行友好访问，并参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年的庆祝活动，今天下午到达北京。
