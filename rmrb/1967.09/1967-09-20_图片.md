Title: Revolutionary Youth of Beijing's No. 23 Middle School Follow Chairman Mao's Strategic Deployment
Author: Wáng Jǐngrén
Date: 1967-09-20
Edition: 3

    The revolutionary youth of Beijing's No. 23 Middle School are closely following Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment. They firmly grasp the direction of the struggle, launching a mass criticism of the handful of power-holders within the party who are taking the capitalist road. They are achieving great revolutionary unity.

    This photo was taken by People's Daily reporter Wang Jingren.
