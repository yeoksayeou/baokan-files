Author:
Date: 1967-09-28
Edition: 6

President of Congo (Brazzaville) Visits the Textile Factory Built with Chinese Aid

Recently Receives the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Congo (Brazzaville)

	NEW YORK, Sept. 28 (Xinhua) — On September 18, President of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) Alphonse Massamba-Débat visited the construction site of the Kinsoundi Textile Factory, which was built with Chinese aid. During the visit, Massamba-Débat worked alongside Congolese workers and Chinese experts at several locations. He encouraged the Congolese workers to learn from the Chinese experts and to cooperate well. He also shook hands warmly with the Chinese experts and several times led the crowd in shouting slogans such as "Long live Sino-Congolese friendship!", "Long live the People's Republic of China!", and "Long live Chairman Mao!"

	NEW YORK, Sept. 26 (Xinhua) — On September 26, President of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) Alphonse Massamba-Débat received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chenguang.

	They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

### 刚果（布）总统参观中国援建的纺织厂　最近接见我驻刚果（布）临时代办

1967-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　刚果（布）总统参观中国援建的纺织厂
　　最近接见我驻刚果（布）临时代办
    新华社布拉柴维尔电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴十八日在这里参观了由中国援助修建的金松迪纺织厂工地。马桑巴—代巴参观时在几个场地与刚果（布）工人和中国专家一起劳动。他鼓励刚果（布）工人努力向中国专家学习和很好地合作。他还和中国专家热情握手，并且几次带头高呼：“中刚友谊万岁！”“人民中国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”等口号。
    新华社布拉柴维尔二十六日电　刚果（布）共和国总统马桑巴—代巴二十六日下午接见了中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光。
    他们作了亲切友好的交谈。
