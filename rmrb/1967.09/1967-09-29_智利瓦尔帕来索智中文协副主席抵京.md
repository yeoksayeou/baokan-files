Title: Vice Chairman of the Chile-Sino Friendship Association in Valparaiso Arrives in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1967-09-29

Edition: 8

Vice Chairman of the Chile-Sino Friendship Association in Valparaiso Arrives in Beijing

New China News Agency, September 28th: Antonio Zhou, Vice Chairman of the Chile-Sino Friendship Association in Valparaiso, arrived in Beijing recently at the invitation of the China-Latin America Friendship Association to participate in the National Day celebrations.

### 智利瓦尔帕来索智中文协副主席抵京

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    智利瓦尔帕来索智中文协副主席抵京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中拉友协邀请来我国参加国庆活动的智利瓦尔帕来索智中文协副主席安东尼奥·周已在最近抵京。
