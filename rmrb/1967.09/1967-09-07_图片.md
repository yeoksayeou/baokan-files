Title: The Three Military Services in Beijing Respond to Chairman Mao's Call for Supporting the Army and Loving the People
Author: Mèng Zōng
Date: 1967-09-07
Edition: 4

The revolutionary masses of the three military services in Beijing firmly respond to Chairman Mao's great call to support the army and love the people, and extensively carry out activities to support the army and love the people. The picture shows the propaganda troupe of the Political Department of the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army performing propaganda for the broad masses of revolutionary workers, peasants, and soldiers.
