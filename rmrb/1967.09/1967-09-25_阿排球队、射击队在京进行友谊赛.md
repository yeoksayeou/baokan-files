Title: Albanian Volleyball and Shooting Teams Hold Friendly Matches in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-25
Edition: 4

The Albanian volleyball team from Tirana and the shooting team began friendly matches with Chinese volleyball and shooting athletes in Beijing on September 21. The Albanian volleyball team from Tirana has already played two friendly matches against the Chinese national men's volleyball team and the Beijing men's volleyball team; the shooting team has conducted two days of friendly matches with the People's Liberation Army's August First Shooting Team.

Athletes from both China and Albania have learned from each other and helped each other during training and competitions. The training grounds and competition venues are filled with a spirit of unity and struggle between the athletes of the two countries.

### 阿排球队、射击队在京进行友谊赛

1967-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿排球队、射击队在京进行友谊赛
    新华社二十四日讯　阿尔巴尼亚地拉那排球队和射击队，从二十一日开始，在北京同中国排球和射击运动员连续进行了友谊比赛。阿尔巴尼亚地拉那排球队已分别同中国国家男子排球队和北京男子排球队进行了两场友谊比赛；射击队同中国人民解放军八一射击队进行了两天友谊比赛。
    中阿两国的排球和射击运动员在训练和比赛中，互相学习，互相帮助，训练场和比赛场上洋溢着中阿两国运动员团结和战斗的气氛。
