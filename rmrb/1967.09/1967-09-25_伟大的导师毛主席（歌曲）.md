Title: Great Teacher Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-09-25
Edition: 6

		Great Teacher Chairman Mao
				Words by Fu Jinceng
				Music by Sheng Mao and Tang He

The song "Great Teacher Chairman Mao" is a powerful tribute to the leadership and guidance of Chairman Mao Zedong. The lyrics, penned by Fu Jinceng, and the music, composed by Sheng Mao and Tang He, reflect the deep reverence and admiration held by the Chinese people for their beloved leader. This song encapsulates the spirit of the Cultural Revolution, emphasizing the importance of Chairman Mao's teachings and his role as the guiding light for the nation. The melody and lyrics together create a stirring anthem that inspires loyalty and dedication to the principles of Maoism.
