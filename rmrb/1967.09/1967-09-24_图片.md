Title: Revolutionary Masses in the Capital Firmly Implement Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions
Author: Shen Guirong
Date: 1967-09-24
Edition: 3

The revolutionary masses in the capital are resolutely implementing Chairman Mao's latest instructions. In the midst of the revolutionary criticism, they are achieving great unity. After the revolutionary masses of the Beijing Jianhua Casting Factory achieved great unity, they joyfully reported the good news to Chairman Mao.
