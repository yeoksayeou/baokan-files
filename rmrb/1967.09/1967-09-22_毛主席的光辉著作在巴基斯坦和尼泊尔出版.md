Title: Chairman Mao's Brilliant Works Published in Pakistan and Nepal
Author:
Date: 1967-09-22
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao's three brilliant works, "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," "On Policy," and "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation," have been translated into Urdu and recently published in West Pakistan's Lahore as three separate booklets. These works have been warmly welcomed by the public.

Each booklet features a portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong on the cover. The publisher's foreword states: Chairman Mao is the great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman of the Chinese people. These booklets contain precious and important works that will greatly benefit the people of Pakistan.

The publisher had previously published the Urdu version of "Quotations from Chairman Mao."

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, September 21st: Chairman Mao's brilliant work, "On New Democracy," has been translated into Nepali and recently published in Kathmandu by the China-Nepal Friendship Association.

### 毛主席的光辉著作在巴基斯坦和尼泊尔出版

1967-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席的光辉著作在巴基斯坦和尼泊尔出版
    新华社拉瓦尔品第电　毛主席的三篇光辉著作《中国革命和中国共产党》、《论政策》和《关于农业合作化问题》已经译成乌尔都文，最近在西巴基斯坦拉合尔分别以三本小册子的形式出版。这些著作受到公众的热烈欢迎。
    每一本小册子的书面上都有一幅毛泽东主席的侧面像。出版者在前言中写道：毛主席是中国人民的伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手。这些小册子包括了宝贵的和重要的作品，它们将大大有助于巴基斯坦人民。
    这个出版者早些时候还出版了《毛主席语录》乌尔都文版。
    新华社加德满都二十一日电　毛主席的光辉著作《新民主主义论》已译成尼泊尔文，由尼中友好协会最近在加德满都出版。
