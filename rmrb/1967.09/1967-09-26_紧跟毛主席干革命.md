Title: Follow Chairman Mao in Revolution
Author: 
Date: 1967-09-26
Edition: 2

The workers' Red Guards are firmly following Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. Under the great banner of Mao Zedong Thought, we are resolutely carrying out the great proletarian cultural revolution. We are determined to smash the bourgeois reactionary line and thoroughly expose and criticize the counter-revolutionary revisionist clique within the Party.

In the face of the complex and fierce class struggle, we must always keep in mind the teachings of Chairman Mao. We must study and apply Mao Zedong Thought conscientiously, and use it as a powerful weapon to arm our minds. Only in this way can we maintain a high degree of revolutionary vigilance and firmly grasp the revolutionary initiative.

We must closely follow the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central Cultural Revolution Group. We must resolutely implement the policies and directives of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao. We must firmly support the left, criticize the right, and thoroughly expose and criticize the counter-revolutionary revisionist clique within the Party.

We must strengthen the unity of the revolutionary masses. We must unite all genuine revolutionaries and progressive forces, and form a mighty alliance to smash the bourgeois reactionary line and thoroughly expose and criticize the counter-revolutionary revisionist clique within the Party.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary culture. We must use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle, and create a large number of revolutionary literary and artistic works that reflect the revolutionary spirit of the times. We must use these works to educate the masses, inspire their revolutionary enthusiasm, and promote the great proletarian cultural revolution.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary discipline. We must strictly follow the eight-point agreement on discipline and hygiene, and maintain a high degree of revolutionary discipline. We must oppose all forms of anarchism and ultra-leftism, and maintain the order and stability of the revolution.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary organization. We must establish and strengthen the revolutionary committees at all levels, and ensure that they are led by genuine revolutionaries. We must oppose all forms of factionalism and splittism, and maintain the unity and solidarity of the revolutionary ranks.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary ideology. We must use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle, and carry out ideological and political education among the masses. We must oppose all forms of bourgeois and feudal ideology, and promote the proletarian revolutionary ideology.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary economy. We must use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle, and carry out economic construction in accordance with the principles of self-reliance and hard work. We must oppose all forms of capitalist and revisionist economic policies, and promote the development of the socialist economy.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary military. We must use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle, and carry out military construction in accordance with the principles of people's war. We must oppose all forms of imperialist and revisionist military policies, and promote the development of the people's revolutionary military.

We must strengthen the construction of the proletarian revolutionary foreign policy. We must use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle, and carry out foreign policy in accordance with the principles of proletarian internationalism. We must oppose all forms of imperialist and revisionist foreign policies, and promote the development of the revolutionary international united front.

In short, we must firmly follow Chairman Mao's revolutionary line, and resolutely carry out the great proletarian cultural revolution. We must smash the bourgeois reactionary line and thoroughly expose and criticize the counter-revolutionary revisionist clique within the Party. We must unite all genuine revolutionaries and progressive forces, and form a mighty alliance to promote the great proletarian cultural revolution. Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Mao Zedong Thought! Long live the great proletarian cultural revolution!
