Title: Afterword
Author:
Date: 1967-09-22
Edition: 4

The poor and lower-middle peasants of the Fourth Production Brigade of Hongjing Commune, using Mao Zedong Thought as their guide and combining it with their own personal experiences, have exposed and criticized the Chinese Khrushchev. Such criticism meetings are highly commendable.

Criticism meetings held at the production brigade level are an excellent form for carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the countryside. They can fully mobilize the broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants within a basic production unit, encouraging everyone to think and speak out, pooling their collective wisdom, and thus making the criticism more detailed and thorough.

Criticism meetings held at the production brigade level are also an excellent form for the masses to study and apply Chairman Mao's works. In such mass criticism meetings, everyone uses Mao Zedong Thought as a weapon to analyze and criticize the various revisionist fallacies of the Chinese Khrushchev. This is learning through struggle and applying what one has learned in struggle, which can continuously raise the level of study and application, making Mao Zedong Thought take deeper root in people's minds.

The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants participating in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a crucial key to carrying the revolution to completion in the countryside. Let us follow the example of the poor and lower-middle peasants of the Fourth Production Brigade of Hongjing Commune, holding criticism meetings at the production brigade level, and carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution more thoroughly. Let us bury the Chinese Khrushchev completely in the vast ocean of the people's war!

### 编后

1967-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　编后
    洪泾大队第四生产队的贫下中农，以生产队为单位，运用毛泽东思想，结合自己的切身体会，对中国赫鲁晓夫进行了揭露和批判。这样的批判会，很值得提倡。
    以生产队为单位的革命批判会，是农村大批判的好形式。它可以在一个基本生产单位的范围内，充分动员广大贫下中农，人人动脑，个个动口，集思广益，从而使大批判进行得更细致、更深入。
    以生产队为单位的革命批判会，也是群众活学活用毛主席著作，自己教育自己的好形式。在这样的群众性批判会上，大家以毛泽东思想为武器，分析、批判中国赫鲁晓夫的种种修正主义谬论，这就是在斗争中学，在斗争中用，就可以不断地提高学用水平，使毛泽东思想在头脑里越来越牢固地扎下根来。
    广大贫下中农参加革命的大批判，是将农村无产阶级文化大革命进行到底的一个重要关键。让我们象洪泾大队第四生产队的贫下中农那样，以生产队为单位，更深入地开展革命的大批判，把中国赫鲁晓夫彻底埋葬在“人民战争”的汪洋大海之中！
