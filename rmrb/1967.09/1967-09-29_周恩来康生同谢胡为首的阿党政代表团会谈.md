Title: Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng Meet with Shehu-led Albanian Party and Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 1

Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Premier of the State Council, and Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and advisor to the Central Cultural Revolution Group, held talks with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Muḥammad Shehu, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, this noon.

The talks were conducted in an extremely sincere and friendly atmosphere, fully reflecting the fraternal friendship and combat unity between the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of the two countries.

Those present at the talks included:
From the Chinese side: responsible comrades from various relevant departments, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, Luo Guibo, Fang Yi, Li Tianyou, etc.;
From the Albanian side: members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, Ramiz Alia, Mihallaq Qishti, Rahman Perlaku, Tonin Jakova, Akilim Mero, Foto Çami, Piro Bita, and Vasili Natanaeli.

### 周恩来康生同谢胡为首的阿党政代表团会谈

1967-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来康生同谢胡为首的阿党政代表团会谈
    新华社二十八日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来同志，中共中央政治局常委、人大常委会副委员长、中央文革小组顾问康生同志，同阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，今天中午举行会谈。
    会谈在极其真挚亲切的气氛中进行，充分地体现了中阿两党、两国、两国人民之间的兄弟友谊和战斗团结。
    参加会谈的：中国方面有各有关方面负责人刘宁一、刘晓、罗贵波、方毅、李天佑等；阿尔巴尼亚方面有阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团成员拉米兹·阿利雅，米哈拉奇·齐奇什蒂，拉赫曼·佩尔拉库，托宁·雅科瓦，阿基姆·梅罗，福托·恰米，皮罗·比塔和瓦西里·纳塔奈利。
