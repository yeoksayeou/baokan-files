Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Bravely Fight the Enemy, Foiling Enemy "Sweeping" Operations
Sangthong Province Military and Civilians Vigorously Suppress Bandits and Destroy American Air Pirates, Resolutely Defending the Liberated Areas

According to Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, September 7th: Lao patriotic military and civilians have recently launched frequent attacks, achieving continuous victories and annihilating a large number of enemies, achieving new victories.

According to the "Voice of Laos" radio broadcast on the 6th: On August 27th, the Lao People's Liberation Army launched a surprise attack on the enemy's stronghold on Hill 1252 in Xieng Khouang Province. After a short but fierce battle, the Liberation Army captured the stronghold, annihilating all the defenders, killing 26 and capturing 7.

According to the Pathet Lao radio broadcast: On August 15th, the local forces and guerrilla units in Louangphrabang Province bravely attacked the enemy stationed in the old Khang region, annihilating and capturing 64 enemies, including the commander of the 36th Battalion of the puppet army who was killed.

In August, the military and civilians of Louangphrabang Province continued their brave fighting, foiling the enemy's multiple attacks and "sweeping" operations in the liberated areas, killing 122 enemies, wounding 148, and capturing 18.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, September 8th: According to the Pathet Lao radio broadcast on the 7th: In August, the patriotic military and civilians of Sangthong Province, in order to defend and consolidate the liberated areas, extensively carried out anti-bandit and anti-American air pirate combat activities, achieving new victories.

According to incomplete statistics, during this period, the patriotic military and civilians of this province engaged in 18 battles with the enemy, annihilating 112 bandits and shooting down 5 American aircraft.

### 老挝爱国军民英勇杀敌粉碎敌人“扫荡”  桑怒省军民大力剿匪和消灭美国空中强盗坚决保卫解放区

1967-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　老挝爱国军民英勇杀敌粉碎敌人“扫荡”
　　桑怒省军民大力剿匪和消灭美国空中强盗坚决保卫解放区
    据新华社康开七日电  老挝爱国军民最近频频出击，连战连捷，歼灭大批敌人，取得了新的胜利。
    据“老挝之声”电台六日广播：老挝人民解放军八月二十七日奇袭川圹省一二五二号高地的敌军据点，经过短时间的激战，解放军攻克了这个据点，全歼守敌，其中打死二十六名，俘虏七名。
    据巴特寮电台广播：老挝琅勃拉邦省地方部队和游击队，八月十五日英勇袭击盘踞在旧康地区的敌人，一举消灭和俘虏敌人六十四名，其中伪军第三十六营营长丧命。
    琅勃拉邦省军民八月份再接再厉，英勇作战，粉碎了敌人对解放区的多次进攻和“扫荡”，打死敌人一百二十二名，打伤一百四十八名，俘虏十八名。
    新华社康开八日电  据巴特寮电台七日广播：老挝桑怒省爱国军民为了保卫和巩固解放区，八月份广泛地开展了剿匪和消灭美国空中强盗的战斗活动，取得了新胜利。
    据不完全统计，这个省的爱国军民在这期间同敌人进行了十八次战斗，消灭土匪一百一十二名，击落美国飞机五架。（附图片）
    上图：一位老挝人民解放军战士怀着无限崇敬的心情，竖起大拇指，称颂毛主席是“东方升起的红太阳，世界革命的伟大舵手”。
    下图：守卫在富科特山前沿阵地的老挝人民解放军战士，正严阵以待，随时准备给来犯的美国强盗及其走狗以致命的打击。
