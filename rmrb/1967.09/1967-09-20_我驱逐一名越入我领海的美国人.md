Title: I Expelled an American Who Intruded into My Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1967-09-20
Edition: 5

On August 17, the American David J. Steele, aboard a yacht, intruded into the territorial waters of Hainan Island in Guangdong Province. He was immediately detained by local public security personnel. After a thorough interrogation by relevant authorities, he admitted his error of intruding into Chinese territorial waters and wrote a written self-criticism. The relevant authorities have detained him for one month, and he will be expelled from the country upon completion of his detention period today.

### 我驱逐一名越入我领海的美国人

1967-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我驱逐一名越入我领海的美国人
    新华社广州十九日电　八月十七日，美国人大卫·约·史蒂尔，乘坐游艇，越入我国广东省海南岛以南领海，当即被我当地民警拘留。经我有关当局详细审讯后，本人已承认越入我国领海的错误，并写出书面检讨。我有关当局将其拘留一月，满期后于今日驱逐出境。
