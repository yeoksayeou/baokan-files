Title: A Red Sun Rises
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 5

A red sun rises, its radiance illuminating the earth. Sing loudly of the red sun in our hearts, sing loudly of the great victory of Mao Zedong Thought. Chairman Mao! Chairman Mao! We resolutely follow you, and we vow to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!

		Lü Houmin (Photographer)

		We love to hear Chairman Mao's words, we love to read Chairman Mao's books. The revolutionary treasure is in our hands, forever following Chairman Mao to make the revolution.

		Shanxi Daily

		Chairman Mao is the red sun, illuminating the five continents and four seas. The army loves the people, the people support the army, the army and the people are united as one. Following the great leader's guidance, we march forward to victory!

		Wei Dongbing (Photographer)

		Chairman Mao's words are powerful beyond measure, each sentence is truth, each word is a torch. Print more of the red treasure, let Mao Zedong Thought's brilliance shine upon the world.

		Li Ziqing (Photographer)

		Dear Leader Chairman Mao, all ethnic peoples love you. A million red hearts turn towards the red sun, a thousand needles and a million threads embroider our intentions.

		Cheng Hanting (Photographer)

		Wearing Chairman Mao's badge on our chests, a sun that never sets rises in our hearts. Chairman Mao's teachings are in our hearts, we are pioneers in revolutionary production.

		Zhang Yaxin (Photographer)

		Every portrait of the Chairman shines brightly, illuminating our hearts. It gives us boundless strength. The revolutionary masses unite, charging towards the battlefield of the great revolutionary criticism.

		Liu Zhenxiang (Photographer)
