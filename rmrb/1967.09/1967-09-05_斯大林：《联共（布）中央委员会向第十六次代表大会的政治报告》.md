Title: Stalin: Report on the Work of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) to the Sixteenth Party Congress
Author: 斯大林 (Stalin)
Date: 1967-09-05
Edition: 4

    Some people are advocating the abandonment of the policy of complete collectivization. It is said that even within our party, there are supporters of this "idea." However, only those who, consciously or unconsciously, are in collusion with the enemies of communism could utter such words.

### 斯大林：《联共（布）中央委员会向第十六次代表大会的政治报告》

1967-09-05

专栏：

    有人发出必须放弃全盘集体化政策的论调。听说连我们党内也有这种“思想”的拥护者。但是，只有那些自觉或不自觉地和共产主义的敌人勾结在一起的人，才能说出这种话。
