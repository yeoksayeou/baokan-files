Title: Celebrating "Defend Pakistan Day" - Pakistani Military Attaché Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-09-07
Edition: 6

	The Pakistani Embassy in China held a reception to celebrate "Defend Pakistan Day" on the evening of September 6. The event was hosted by Pakistani military attachés to China, Air Force Colonel I. H. Khan and Colonel Kayum.

	Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jinguang, along with leaders from various departments, services, and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Garrison Command, including Li Tianyou, Wu Faxian, Yuan Ziqin, and Zhang Tianyun, attended the reception. Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong and other relevant officials were also present.

	Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan attended the reception.

	Invited guests also included diplomats and military attachés from various countries stationed in China.

### 庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”  巴基斯坦驻华武官举行招待会

1967-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”
　　巴基斯坦驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社六日讯　巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆空军武官伊·哈·汗空军上校和武官卡尤姆上校，为庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”，今晚举行招待会。
    国防部副部长萧劲光，中国人民解放军各总部、各军种、各兵种和北京卫戍区的负责人李天佑、吴法宪、袁子钦、张天云等，外交部副部长韩念龙，以及各有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有各国驻中国的使节和大使馆武官。
