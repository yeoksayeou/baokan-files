Title: Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-24
Edition: 5

The Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation, invited by the China-Latin America Friendship Association to participate in the National Day celebrations and a friendly visit to China, arrived in Beijing on September 21. The delegation is led by Dr. Pablo Casas Santofimio.

### 哥伦比亚文化友好代表团到京

1967-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　哥伦比亚文化友好代表团到京
    新华社二十三日讯　应中拉友协邀请来我国参加国庆活动和友好访问的哥伦比亚文化友好代表团，由团长巴勃罗·卡萨斯·桑托菲米欧博士率领，二十一日到达北京。
