Title: China's Ministry of National Defense Holds a Report Meeting Inviting Vietnamese Comrades to Share Heroic Deeds in the Vietnamese People's Struggle Against American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1967-09-16
Edition: 5

The Ministry of National Defense of China held a report meeting this afternoon, inviting members of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation, including Vietnamese Democratic Republic war hero Thai Van A and female militia member Chen Shishen, to share their heroic deeds in the struggle against American aggressors. As the delegation leader Nguyen Van Huyen, deputy leader Ho Kuei, and other delegation members entered the venue, the entire audience burst into enthusiastic applause, welcoming their Vietnamese comrades from the front lines of the anti-American struggle. The meeting was presided over by Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Xinting.

The Director of the Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Lu Yang, spoke at the meeting, warmly praising the brilliant victories achieved by the Vietnamese military and people in the war of resistance against American aggression. He also expressed that the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought and tempered by the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, firmly support the Vietnamese people's war of resistance against American aggression until the complete defeat of the American aggressors.

Thai Van A and Chen Shishen vividly introduced the heroic deeds of the Vietnamese military and people, who, by unleashing the tremendous power of people's war, achieved one victory after another. They expressed their gratitude to the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army for their great support and assistance to Vietnam, and declared their determination to defend the north, liberate the south, and ultimately unify the nation by continuing the war of resistance against American aggression to the end!

The delegation leader Nguyen Van Huyen also spoke at the meeting. He thanked the Chinese Ministry of National Defense for holding the report meeting, stating that this meeting demonstrated the great support of the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army for the Vietnamese people.

Vietnamese military attaché Colonel Chen Wenpeng also attended the report meeting.

### 我国防部举行报告会  邀请越南战友介绍越南人民反美斗争英雄事迹

1967-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我国防部举行报告会
    邀请越南战友介绍越南人民反美斗争英雄事迹
    新华社十五日讯　我国国防部今天下午举行报告会，邀请越中友协代表团成员、越南民主共和国战斗英雄泰文阿和女民兵陈氏深，报告他们同美国侵略强盗进行英勇斗争的英雄事迹。当代表团团长阮文煊、副团长何桂和代表团成员走进会场时，全场热烈鼓掌，欢迎来自反美斗争前线的越南战友。报告会由中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭主持。
    国防部办公厅主任路杨在致词中，热烈赞扬越南军民在抗美救国战争中所取得的辉煌胜利，并表示，用毛泽东思想武装起来的，经过无产阶级文化大革命锻炼的中国人民和人民解放军，坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争，直到彻底打败美国侵略者。
    泰文阿和陈氏深在报告中，生动地介绍了越南军民发挥了人民战争的巨大威力，取得一次又一次胜利的英雄事迹，感谢中国人民和人民解放军对越南的巨大支持和援助，并表示决心为保卫北方，解放南方，进而统一祖国，把抗美救国战争进行到底！
    团长阮文煊也在会上讲了话。他感谢我国国防部举行报告会，他说，这个报告会体现了中国人民和人民解放军对越南人民的巨大支持。
    越南驻华使馆武官陈文彭上校也出席了报告会。
