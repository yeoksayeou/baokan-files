	Brief News

	Vietnam's Ministry of Foreign Trade Awards Medals to Chinese Heroic Crew
	The Ministry of Foreign Trade of Vietnam held an award ceremony on September 1st, presenting medals and badges to the crew members of the Chinese cargo ships "1018" and "1019" in the South China Sea. This honor recognizes their bravery in enduring hardships and sacrifices, and their tenacious spirit in overcoming attacks by American air pirates.

	At the ceremony, Minister of Foreign Trade Phan Hien, on behalf of the Vietnamese government, handed over the medals and badges to the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Acting Charge d'Affaires Lu Weizhao, and requested him to convey them to the crew members of these two heroic ships.

	Laotian People of Xam Neua Province Repel Enemy Attacks
	In August, the patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province in Laos fought bravely, repelling multiple attacks and "sweeps" launched by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys against the liberated areas of Xam Neua. The enemy's "encroachment" plot was completely foiled. In various battles, the patriotic military and civilians annihilated 349 enemy troops, shot down two aircraft, and seized over 100 weapons and a large amount of military supplies.

	Albanian Foreign Ministry Statement Recognizes Existing Cambodian Borders
	On September 6th, the Albanian Foreign Ministry issued a statement saying, "The Albanian government supports the statements made by the Royal Government of Cambodia on November 2, 1966, May 9, 1967, and July 22, 1967, recognizing the current borders of the Kingdom of Cambodia and fully respecting its territorial integrity."

	The statement also expressed solidarity and full support for the brave Cambodian people and their government in their just struggle to defend national interests.

	Australian People Protest Government's Participation in Vietnam War
	On September 9th, over 300 Australians demonstrated on the streets of Melbourne, protesting against the Australian government's decision to follow U.S. imperialism in participating in the Vietnam War. The demonstrators carried banners opposing the Vietnam War, marching from the Parliament House to the city center, and then held a protest rally in the city square. At the rally, speakers condemned the Australian government for becoming a puppet of U.S. imperialism and an accomplice in the Vietnam War.

### 简明新闻

专栏：简明新闻

　　简明新闻
　　越南外贸部给我英雄船员授勋
    越南对外贸易部一日举行授勋仪式，授予中国南海英雄船“一零一八号”和“一零一九号”货船船员以勋章和徽章，表彰他们不怕艰苦和牺牲，顽强战胜美国空中强盗袭击的英勇精神。
    越南对外贸易部部长潘英在仪式上代表越南政府，把勋章和徽章交给中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊，并请他转交给这两艘英雄船的船员。
　　老挝川圹省军民粉碎敌人进攻
    老挝川圹省爱国军民在八月份英勇作战，击溃了美帝国主义及其走狗对川圹省解放区发起的多次进攻和“扫荡”，使敌人的“蚕食”阴谋遭到彻底破产。在各次战斗中，爱国军民共歼敌三百四十九名，击落飞机两架，缴获各种武器一百多件和许多军用物资。
　　阿外交部声明承认柬现存边界
    阿尔巴尼亚外交部六日发表声明说，“阿尔巴尼亚政府支持柬埔寨王国政府一九六六年十一月二日、一九六七年五月九日和七月二十二日的声明，承认柬埔寨王国目前的边界，完全尊重它的领土完整。”
    声明并表示声援和完全支持英勇的柬埔寨人民及其政府为保卫民族利益而正在进行的正义斗争。
　　澳人民抗议政府参加侵越战争
    三百多名澳大利亚人九日在墨尔本大街上举行示威，抗议澳大利亚政府追随美帝国主义参加侵越战争。示威者举着反对侵越战争的标语，从议会大厦一直游行到市中心，然后在市中心广场举行抗议集会。在会上，发言者谴责澳大利亚政府已经堕落成美帝国主义的傀儡，充当美帝国主义侵越战争的帮凶。　（据新华社讯）
