	Brief News

	Tanzanian Newspaper Praises China for Building Tanzania-Zambia Railway

	Tanzania's *Nationalist* newspaper published an editorial on September 7 praising China's assistance in building the Tanzania-Zambia Railway.

	The editorial stated that China's interest-free loan "is provided in a spirit of friendship. The agreement respects both the assisting and the assisted countries."

	Pathet Lao Radio Denounces Vientiane Regime's Provocation

	On the night of August 26, rioters in Vientiane threw three grenades into the residence of a representative of the Lao Patriotic Front and the residence of Prince Souphanouvong, causing some damage. Pathet Lao Radio issued a commentary saying that this is a step in the plot by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys to further complicate and intensify the situation in Laos, in order to provide an excuse for U.S. imperialism to launch new military adventures in Laos.

	Black U.S. Soldiers in Vietnam Refuse to Fight

	In August, when the liberated armed forces in South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on the U.S. forces in the Phuoc Long area of Quang Nam Province, the U.S. Marine Corps in that province hastily dispatched a company of black U.S. soldiers to provide relief. However, after the helicopters air-dropped this company of U.S. soldiers, they staged a sit-down strike, refusing to carry out combat orders. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    坦桑报纸盛赞中国援建坦赞铁路
    坦桑尼亚《民族主义者报》七日发表社论赞扬中国援助修建坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路。
    社论说，中国的无息贷款“是本着友好的精神提供的。协定尊重了援助国和受援国双方。”
    巴特寮电台谴责万象当局的挑衅
    八月二十六日夜间，万象暴徒把三颗手榴弹分别投进老挝爱国战线党代表的住所和留给苏发努冯亲王居住的房屋，造成一些损失。巴特寮电台发表评论说，这是美帝国主义及其走狗阴谋使老挝局势更加紧张和复杂，以便美帝国主义对老挝采取新的军事冒险行动的一个步骤。
    侵越美军一连黑人士兵公开拒战
    八月间，在南越广南省解放武装力量猛烈进攻先福地区的美军时，这个省的美国海军陆战队曾慌忙派遣一连黑人美军前往救援。但是，当直升飞机把这一连美军空投下来之后，他们就进行静坐斗争，拒绝执行作战命令。（据新华社讯）
