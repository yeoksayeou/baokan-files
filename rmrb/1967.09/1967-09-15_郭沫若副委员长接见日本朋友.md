Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-09-15
Edition: 6

The Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, received in the afternoon of the 13th a delegation of Chinese studies experts led by Terahata Eizo, dispatched by the General Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), and a delegation led by Watanabe Yukio, the 15th delegation from the General Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) to visit China. He also had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友

1967-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友
    新华社讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若十三日下午接见了由日中友协（正统）总部派遣的以寺广映雄为首的中国研究家代表团和以渡部行雄为首的日中友协（正统）总部第十五次访华代表团，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
