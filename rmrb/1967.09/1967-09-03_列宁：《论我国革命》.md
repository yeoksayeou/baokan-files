Title: On Our Revolution
Author: Lie Ning
Date: 1967-09-03
Edition: 4

You say that to build socialism, we need civilization. Excellent. Then why can't we first create the prerequisites for this civilization in our country, such as expelling the landlords and expelling the Russian capitalists, and then begin to move towards socialism?
