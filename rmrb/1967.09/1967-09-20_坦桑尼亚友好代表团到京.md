Title: Tanzanian Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-20
Edition: 5

Tanzanian Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 19th, at the invitation of our country, the Tanzanian Minister of Lands, Settlement, and Water Development, Babu, led the Tanzanian Friendship Delegation to visit China. They arrived in Beijing by special plane this evening. The guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by representatives from the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Red Guards. Representatives of the capital's Red Guards presented the guests with copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong" and Mao Zedong badges.

Tanzania's Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, and diplomats from Vietnam and some African countries were at the airport to welcome them.

### 坦桑尼亚友好代表团到京

1967-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚友好代表团到京
    据新华社十九日讯应邀来我国访问的坦桑尼亚联合共和国土地、定居和水力开发部长巴布率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团，今天晚上乘专机到达北京。客人在机场受到水利电力部和外交部代表以及红卫兵小将的热烈欢迎。首都红卫兵代表向客人们赠送了《毛主席语录》和毛主席像章。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科，越南和一些非洲国家使节到机场迎接。
