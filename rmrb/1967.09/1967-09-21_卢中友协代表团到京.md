Title: Delegation of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-21
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, a delegation from the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association, consisting of two members led by Secretary-General Adolf Frank, arrived in Beijing on September 19 to conduct a friendly visit to China.

### 卢中友协代表团到京

1967-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　卢中友协代表团到京
    新华社二十日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的卢森堡—中国友好协会代表团，一行二人，在秘书长阿道夫·弗兰克率领下，于十九日到达北京。
