Title: Delegation of the Sudan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 8

A delegation of the Sudan-China Friendship Association, led by Chairman Abdel Makid Abdel Rahim of the Sudan-China Friendship Association in Khartoum and Secretary-General Mirgani Ali Mustafa of the Sudan-China Friendship Association in Khartoum, arrived in Beijing on September 28. The delegation consists of nine members. They were invited by the China-Africa Friendship Association to participate in the celebrations of the National Day of the People's Republic of China and to conduct a friendly visit.

### 苏丹—中国友好协会代表团到京

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　苏丹—中国友好协会代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　由迈达尼苏丹—中国友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·马基德·阿卜杜勒·拉欣姆和喀土穆苏丹—中国友好协会秘书长米尔加尼·阿里·穆斯塔法率领的苏丹—中国友好协会代表团，一行九人，二十八日到达北京。代表团是应中非友协邀请，前来参加我国庆庆祝活动，并进行友好访问的。
