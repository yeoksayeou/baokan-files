Title: Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association Delegation Visits Albania
Author:
Date: 1967-09-24
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Albanian-China Friendship Association, a delegation from the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, consisting of five members, led by its head, Central Committee member and Deputy Minister of Defense Wang Shusheng, departed by plane from Beijing today to visit Albania and participate in the celebrations marking the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included: Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee and President of the China International Friendship Association Chu Tu-nan, and representatives of the revolutionary masses of the capital.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Albanian Ambassador to China Natanaeli and others.

The delegation also included a group of Chinese scientific workers who will visit Albania.

### 中阿友协代表团赴阿访问

1967-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　中阿友协代表团赴阿访问
    新华社二十三日讯　应阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会邀请，中阿友协代表团一行五人，在团长、中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声率领下，于今日乘飞机离京赴阿进行友好访问，并参加阿尔巴尼亚人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十八周年的庆祝活动。
    到机场送行的有：中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭，外交部副部长罗贵波，对外文委副主任、对外友协会长楚图南和首都革命群众代表等。
    到机场送行的，还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利等。
    同机赴阿尔巴尼亚访问的还有中国科学工作者代表团。
