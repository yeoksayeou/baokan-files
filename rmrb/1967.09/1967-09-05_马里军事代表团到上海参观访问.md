Title: Malian Military Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1967-09-05
Edition: 5

A Malian military delegation led by Captain Dibi Silla Diarra, Governor of Kita Province and Commander of the Military Region, arrived in Shanghai by special plane this morning.

The guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by responsible comrades of the PLA units in Shanghai, as well as by leading members of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee.

Before coming to Shanghai, the Malian military delegation had visited cities such as Jinan. During their visits, Captain Dibi Silla Diarra enthusiastically praised the great leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. He said, "Chairman Mao is the great leader of the Chinese people; he is the great leader of the people of the world. Under the guidance of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, the revolutionary struggles of the revolutionary peoples of all countries in the world will surely achieve thorough victory." The Malian guests and the PLA soldiers and revolutionary masses together repeatedly toasted to the long life of the great leader Chairman Mao!

### 马里军事代表团到上海参观访问

1967-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　马里军事代表团到上海参观访问
    据新华社上海四日电　由马里共和国基塔省长兼军区司令迪比·席拉·迪亚拉上尉率领的马里共和国军事代表团，今天上午乘专机到达上海。
    客人们在机场上，受到了中国人民解放军驻上海陆、海、空三军负责同志以及上海市革命委员会领导成员的热烈欢迎。
    马里军事代表团在去上海以前，曾到济南等地进行了参观访问。在参观访问过程中，迪比·席拉·迪亚拉上尉热情颂扬伟大领袖毛主席，热情颂扬战无不胜的毛泽东思想。他说，毛主席是中国人民的伟大领袖，他是世界人民的伟大领袖，在战无不胜的毛泽东思想指引下，世界各国革命人民的革命斗争，必将取得彻底胜利。马里客人和解放军指战员、革命群众一起，不断共祝伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！
