Title: CPC Central Office Circular: Seriously Study Comrade Jiang Qing's Speech on September 5
Author:
Date: 1967-09-17
Edition: 1

The CPC Central Office Circular issued a notice on September 9, calling on the proletarian revolutionary masses nationwide to seriously study Comrade Jiang Qing's speech on September 5.

The notice states: "On September 5, Comrade Jiang Qing's speech at the Anhui delegation meeting in Beijing correctly analyzed the current situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and clearly outlined the tasks for the proletarian revolutionary masses. All revolutionary committees, military control committees, and revolutionary mass organizations should seriously organize the listening and study of this speech recording to further grasp the direction of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution struggle pointed out by Chairman Mao, carry out thorough criticism, achieve great unity, and do a good job in supporting the army and loving the people."

### 中共中央办公厅通知  认真学习江青同志九月五日讲话

1967-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央办公厅通知
    认真学习江青同志九月五日讲话
    新华社十六日讯　中共中央办公厅在九月九日发出通知，号召全国无产阶级革命派认真学习江青同志九月五日的讲话。
    通知说：“九月五日江青同志在安徽来京代表会议上的讲话，对目前无产阶级文化大革命的形势作了正确的分析，向无产阶级革命派提出了明确的斗争任务。各革命委员会、军管会、革命群众组织，应认真组织收听这个讲话录音，进行学习，以进一步掌握毛主席给我们指出的无产阶级文化大革命斗争的大方向，搞好大批判、革命大联合，做好拥军爱民的工作。”
