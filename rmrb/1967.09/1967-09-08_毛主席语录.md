Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-09-08
Edition: 1

	We must be especially vigilant against individuals like Khrushchev who have personal ambitions and engage in conspiracies, and prevent such bad elements from usurping the leadership of the Party and the state at all levels.

### 毛主席语录

1967-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席语录
    要特别警惕象赫鲁晓夫那样的个人野心家和阴谋家，防止这样的坏人篡夺党和国家的各级领导。
    转摘自《关于赫鲁晓夫的假共产主义及其在世界历史上的教训》
