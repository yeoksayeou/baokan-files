Title: The Greatest Happiness for All the People of China and the World
Author:
Date: 1967-09-30
Edition: 1

The greatest happiness for all the people of China and the world is that our great leader and great helmsman Chairman Mao is in extremely good health and has boundless energy.

On the eve of National Day, the broad masses of soldiers and civilians joyfully celebrated this double joyous piece of good news, enthusiastically shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" "Wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!" They unanimously declared that they would definitely study Chairman Mao's works more diligently, establish the absolute authority of Mao Zedong Thought, resolutely listen to Chairman Mao's words, and resolutely carry out Chairman Mao's instructions.

New China News Agency, September 29th: The extremely inspiring news that our great leader Chairman Mao is in extremely good health and has boundless energy spread, the broad masses of soldiers and civilians in Beijing, Shanghai, and other places who are joyfully welcoming National Day erupted in cheers. They joyfully celebrated this double joyous piece of good news, enthusiastically shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" "Wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

When Zhang Chunqiao comrade announced this piece of good news at the celebration and victory meeting of a certain unit of the Air Force, the soldiers and civilians of many units in Beijing and Shanghai, as well as workers, students, and staff from factories, schools, and offices, immediately wrote big-character posters and held celebration meetings, good news meetings, and discussion meetings. Broad revolutionary masses and soldiers said: Chairman Mao is in extremely good health and has boundless energy, this is the greatest happiness for all the people of China and the world!

The soldiers of the unit that shot down the U-2 high-altitude reconnaissance plane flown by the American-made Kuomintang bandits, who received awards at the celebration meeting yesterday, were particularly moved after hearing the good news. They said that this piece of good news greatly inspired us and gave us boundless fighting strength. Chairman Mao's praise and encouragement are the greatest praise and encouragement for all the soldiers of the army. After a representative from the Second Company of a certain unit conveyed the good news reported by Zhang Chunqiao comrade, the entire company's soldiers were overjoyed. They sincerely wished Chairman Mao eternal life and immediately launched a discussion on "How should we respond to Chairman Mao's concern for us?" The soldiers said: All victories come from the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. Of all the principles, the most fundamental one is to arm our minds with Mao Zedong Thought. They swore: We must further launch a new high tide of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, promote the revolution, strengthen preparedness, and work, and be highly vigilant, "devote all our efforts to annihilate the invading enemy," and report new victories to Chairman Mao.

In the capital, the revolutionary cadres and broad masses of soldiers from the Navy, Air Force, and Beijing Military Region held a grand celebration. The soldiers of a certain company guarding Tiananmen Square day and night held a meeting in front of Chairman Mao's giant portrait at Tiananmen Square today. They solemnly swore: We are Chairman Mao's red sentinels. Wherever Chairman Mao points, we will resolutely fight there. The soldiers joyfully sang "The East Is Red" in front of Tiananmen Square and performed a self-composed song "The Guard's Heart Turns to the Red Sun." The heroes and models who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day activities and made outstanding contributions to defending the country's defense and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution also held a meeting overnight. The heroes and models who were awarded for their achievements, such as Bin Ruowen, Zhang Zishun, Lü Shanghua, Han Ruiji, and Wang Ruichang, as well as the famous "hard-bone" soldier Zhang Chunyu, said excitedly at the meeting: Chairman Mao is in extremely good health and has boundless energy, this is the greatest wish of the entire army and the nation, and it is also the greatest wish of revolutionary people all over the world. They unanimously declared that they would definitely study and apply Chairman Mao's works more diligently, establish the absolute authority of Mao Zedong Thought in their minds, resolutely listen to Chairman Mao's words, and resolutely carry out Chairman Mao's instructions, and make new contributions to the people in defending the country and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Many units of the Three Services stationed in Shanghai held celebration meetings today. The broad masses of soldiers and sailors who rushed from sea islands on speedboats, as well as workers, peasants, and students who rushed from factories, rural areas, and schools to participate in the "Three Supports, Two Militaries" work, expressed their boundless loyalty to Chairman Mao with tears of excitement at the celebration meeting. The soldiers of the "Good Eighth Company on Nanjing Road" said: Chairman Mao's health is our greatest happiness. Every one of our red hearts, which are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao, flies to Beijing. The soldiers of the East China Fleet wrote in their article praising Chairman Mao's eternal life: Chairman Mao is the brightest red sun in our hearts. Wherever he goes, the sky becomes brighter, the rivers sing, the roads become clearer, and the people gain boundless strength. We are revolutionary soldiers nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought. The vast East China Sea cannot contain our boundless love, loyalty, admiration, and respect for Chairman Mao. We swear to dedicate our loyal hearts forever to our most beloved Chairman Mao, to follow Chairman Mao forever, and to make revolution forever!

Today, the revolutionary cadres and masses of the proletariat in the capital and Shanghai are all excited and inspired by the news that Chairman Mao is in extremely good health and has boundless energy. Some took to the streets to spread the good news, put up slogans, and eagerly told the good news to more comrades. Some held celebration meetings and discussion meetings to celebrate Chairman Mao's eternal life! Many revolutionary cadres of the proletariat also swore in front of Chairman Mao's portrait to raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, closely follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, carry out a great criticism, develop and consolidate the great alliance of the revolution and the "three-in-one combination," do a good job in the struggle, criticism, and transformation of their units, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end. The revolutionary cadres of many factories, mines, and enterprises in the capital and the warriors of the "Shanghai Workers' General Headquarters" declared: We will resolutely respond to Chairman Mao's great call to "grasp the revolution and promote production," be the pioneers of the revolution and the models of production, and report revolutionary and production achievements to Chairman Mao. The Red Guards also decided to study and apply Chairman Mao's works more diligently, further carry out the great criticism and the struggle, criticism, and transformation of their units, develop and consolidate the great alliance of the revolution, develop and consolidate the "three-in-one combination" of the revolution, and make new achievements in the Cultural Revolution!

　　我们伟大的领袖伟大的统帅毛主席身体非常非常健康精力非常非常充沛
    国庆前夕，广大军民无比幸福地欢庆这个喜上加喜的特大喜讯，激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”他们一致表示，一定要更好地活学活用毛主席著作，树立毛泽东思想的绝对权威，毛主席的话坚决听，毛主席的指示坚决执行
    新华社二十九日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席身体非常非常健康，精力非常非常充沛的最最振奋人心的特大喜讯传出后，正在喜气洋洋迎接国庆的北京、上海等地的人民解放军广大指战员和革命群众，欢声雷动，四出报喜。他们无比欢欣、无比幸福地欢庆这个喜上加喜的特大喜讯，激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”
    张春桥同志在空军某部祝捷庆功大会上报告的这个特大喜讯一传出，京、沪地区的许多部队的解放军指战员和工厂、学校、机关的革命群众就纷纷写出大字报，举行庆祝会、报喜会、座谈会。广大革命群众和指战员们说：毛主席身体非常非常健康，精力非常非常充沛，这是全中国人民和全世界人民的最大幸福！
    在昨天庆功大会上受奖的击落美制蒋匪帮Ｕ—2高空侦察机的作战有功部队指战员，听到特大喜讯以后心情特别激动。他们说，这个特大喜讯极大地鼓舞了我们，给我们增加了无穷的战斗力量。毛主席对我们的嘉奖和鼓励，就是对全军指战员的最大嘉奖和最大鼓励。某部二连参加庆功大会的代表传达了张春桥同志报告的特大喜讯后，全连指战员个个欢腾，衷心祝愿毛主席万寿无疆，并且立即开展了“毛主席这样关怀我们，我们怎么办？”的讨论。指战员们说：一切胜利来自战无不胜的毛泽东思想。千条万条，最根本的一条是用毛泽东思想武装头脑。他们宣誓：一定要进一步掀起活学活用毛主席著作的新高潮，抓革命、促战备、促工作，百倍提高警惕，“全力以赴，务歼入侵之敌”，以新的战功再向毛主席报捷。
    在首都，海军、空军、北京部队领导机关无产阶级革命派和广大指战员，听到喜讯后，千歌万曲齐颂毛主席。日夜守卫在天安门前的某部一连指战员，今天在天安门前的毛主席巨幅画像下举行集会，他们庄严宣誓：我们是毛主席的红哨兵，毛主席指向那里，我们就坚决打到那里。指战员们无比喜悦地在天安门前高唱《东方红》，演唱了自编的表演唱《警卫战士心向红太阳》。来京参加国庆活动的，在保卫祖国国防、保卫无产阶级文化大革命中作出了卓越贡献的作战有功人员和英雄模范人物，也连夜集会。荣立战功的宾若文、张自顺、吕尚华、韩瑞阶、王瑞昌和著名的硬骨头战士张春玉等在会上激动地说：毛主席身体非常非常健康，精力非常非常充沛，这是全军和全国人民的最大心愿，也是全世界革命人民的最大心愿。他们一致表示，一定要更好地活学活用毛主席著作，在自己的头脑中确立毛泽东思想的绝对权威，毛主席的话坚决听，毛主席的指示坚决执行，在保卫祖国，保卫无产阶级文化大革命中为人民再立新功。
    驻沪三军的许多单位，今天都举行了庆祝集会。广大指战员和乘快艇从海岛上赶来的战士，从工厂、农村、学校赶来的参加“三支”“两军”工作的人员，在庆祝集会上含着激动的泪花，倾诉着无限忠于毛主席的激情。“南京路上好八连”的指战员们说：毛主席身体这样健康，这是我们最大最大的幸福，我们每一颗无限忠于毛主席的红心，都飞向了北京。东海舰队指战员在他们歌颂毛主席万寿无疆的文章中写道：毛主席是我们心中最红最红的红太阳，他老人家走到那里，那里的天空就更加明朗，那里的江河就欢腾歌唱，那里的道路就明亮宽广，那里的人民就有了无穷无尽的力量。我们是毛泽东思想哺育成长的革命战士，滔滔东海容不下我们对毛主席的无限热爱、无限忠诚、无限崇拜、无限敬仰的深情。我们誓把赤胆忠心永远献给我们最最敬爱的毛主席，永跟毛主席，永远闹革命！
