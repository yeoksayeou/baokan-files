Title: Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, a delegation from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam will arrive in Beijing on September 29. The delegation will participate in the celebrations marking the 18th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and will also conduct a friendly visit to our country.

### 越南南方民族解放阵线代表团今天到京

1967-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南南方民族解放阵线代表团今天到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，越南南方民族解放阵线代表团，将于二十九日到达北京。代表团将参加中华人民共和国成立十八周年的庆祝活动，并在我国进行友好访问。
