Title: Chairman Mao Returns to Beijing from an Inspection
Author: Yáng Zhìhé
Date: 1967-09-27
Edition: 3

Chairman Mao returns to Beijing from an inspection,
The joyous news reaches our camp,
The applause is like thunderous drums,
The entire army is in high spirits.

Chairman Mao returns to Beijing from an inspection,
Cadres and soldiers welcome each other with joy,
Running to spread the good news,
The mountains and rivers echo with cheers.

Chairman Mao returns to Beijing from an inspection,
Millions of smiling faces are radiant,
Four Little Red Books are worn on the chest,
A red sun rises in our hearts.

Chairman Mao returns to Beijing from an inspection,
I pledge to the leader:
Firmly grasp the correct direction,
Sail forward against the wind and waves.

Chairman Mao returns to Beijing from an inspection,
Illuminating the entire nation with red;
Hundreds of millions of soldiers and civilians hand in hand,
Forever follow you in revolution.

### 毛主席视察回北京
杨志和
1967-09-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　毛主席视察回北京
　　战士　杨志和
　　毛主席视察回北京，
　　喜讯飞到俺军营，
　　掌声如雷锣鼓响，
　　全军上下齐欢腾。
　　毛主席视察回北京，
　　干部战士喜相迎，
　　奔走相告传喜讯，
　　三山五岳起欢声。
　　毛主席视察回北京，
　　千万张笑脸喜盈盈，
　　四卷红书贴胸前，
　　一轮红日心中升。
　　毛主席视察回北京，
　　我向领袖表决心：
　　牢牢掌握大方向，
　　乘风破浪向前进。
　　毛主席视察回北京，
　　照得全国一片红；
　　亿万军民手携手，
　　永远跟您闹革命。
