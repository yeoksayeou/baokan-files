Title: Yao Wenyuan's Important Article "Critique of Tao Chu's Two Books" Published
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 2

	Yao Wenyuan's important article "Critique of Tao Chu's Two Books" has been published as a standalone book by the People's Publishing House. It is now being distributed by the Beijing New Hua Bookstore and will soon be available across the country.

### 姚文元同志的重要文章《评陶铸的两本书》出版

1967-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　姚文元同志的重要文章《评陶铸的两本书》出版
    新华社二十七日讯　姚文元同志的重要文章《评陶铸的两本书》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，由北京新华书店开始发行，并即将在全国各地陆续发行。
