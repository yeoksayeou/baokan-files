Title: The Chinese Red Cross Donates Funds to Aid Sudanese Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1967-09-15
Edition: 6

The Chinese Red Cross has donated 20,000 yuan to the people of Sudan who have been affected by recent heavy rains. On September 12, the Chinese Embassy in Sudan, on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross, handed over the funds to the Sudanese Red Crescent Society.

### 我红十字会捐款救济苏丹灾民

1967-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    我红十字会捐款救济苏丹灾民
    据新华社讯　中国驻苏丹大使馆十二日代表中国红十字会交给苏丹红新月会人民币两万元，作为中国红十字会赠送给最近因暴雨而受灾的苏丹居民的一笔捐款。
