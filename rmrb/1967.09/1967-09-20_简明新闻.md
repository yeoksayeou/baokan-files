Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1967-09-20
Edition: 5

The Arab people continue to resist the Israeli invaders.

The Arab people in the occupied Gaza Strip and the West Bank of the Jordan River, despite the frantic suppression by the U.S. imperialist tool, the Israeli authorities, continue to resist the invaders in various ways.

In the Gaza Strip, landmines planted by Arab nationalists on September 8 blew up an Israeli military vehicle, killing one Israeli soldier and injuring four others.

In the West Bank, landmines planted by Arab nationalists in the Tulkarm area on September 14 damaged an Israeli train bound for Haifa.

The Sudanese government announces the halt of imports from the United States.

Sudanese Minister of Commerce and Supply, Ahmad Sayyid Hamad, announced on the 18th that due to the U.S. attempt to impose an economic blockade on Sudan, the decision has been made to stop importing goods from the United States.

Sudanese Minister of Finance and Economy, Sharif Hussein Khindi, once again pointed out that the so-called "U.S. aid" is intended to create political influence. This "aid" is given to countries that are willing to submit to the U.S. and follow its orbit.

The Chiang Kai-shek gang's "embassy" in Saigon is blown up.

The Chiang Kai-shek gang's "embassy" in the bustling area of Saigon was blown up on the morning of the 19th. This is the Saigon people's punishment of the U.S. running dog, the Chiang Kai-shek gang.

According to Western news agencies, a two-story building of the Chiang Kai-shek gang's "embassy" was largely destroyed, with some gang members buried under the rubble and others injured.

Japanese police brutally suppress Tokyo University students.

On the night of the 13th, the Japanese reactionary authorities dispatched a large number of armed police to storm into Tokyo's Hosei University, brutally suppressing the students. A total of 285 students were arrested, and many were injured.

According to reports, the administration of Hosei University arbitrarily punished seven students on the 8th of this month, sparking the anger of the students. In recent days, Hosei University students have been fighting resolutely against the reactionary school authorities.

### 简明新闻

1967-09-20
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    阿拉伯人民继续反抗以侵略军
    被占领的加沙地区和约旦河西岸地区的阿拉伯人民不顾美帝侵略工具以色列当局的疯狂镇压，继续用各种方式反抗侵略者。
    在加沙地区，阿拉伯民族主义者埋设的地雷九月八日炸毁了一辆以色列军车，车上的一名以色列士兵被炸死，四名被炸伤。
    在约旦河西岸地区，阿拉伯民族主义者在图卡雷姆地区埋设的地雷，九月十四日炸坏了一次开往海法的以色列列车。
    苏丹政府宣布停止进口美国货
    苏丹商业和供应部长艾哈迈德·赛义德·哈马德十八日宣布，由于美国企图对苏丹实行经济封锁，因此决定停止从美国进口货物。
    苏丹财政和经济部长沙里夫·侯赛因·欣迪再次发表讲话指出，所谓“美援”是为了造成政治影响，这种“援助”是给予愿意服从美国和围着美国轨道转的国家的。
    蒋匪帮驻西贡“大使馆”被炸
    位于西贡闹市区的台湾蒋介石匪帮驻西贡
  “大使馆”，十九日上午被炸，这是西贡人民对美帝走狗蒋匪帮的惩罚。
    据西方通讯社报道，蒋匪帮“大使馆”的一幢两层大楼大部分被炸毁了，有一些蒋匪帮分子被埋在瓦砾下面，还有一些被炸伤。
    日本警察血腥镇压东京大学生
    日本反动当局十三日夜，出动大批武装警察，闯入东京法政大学，对广大学生进行血腥镇压。二百八十五名学生被捕，许多人受伤。
    据报道，东京法政大学当局本月八日无理处分了七名学生，激起了广大学生的愤怒。连日来，法大学生与学校反动当局展开了坚决斗争。
                        （据新华社讯）
