Title: Proletarian Revolutionaries in Wuhan Resolutely Follow Chairman Mao's Great Strategic Deployment
Author: Xie Fengquan
Date: 1967-09-10
Edition: 2

The proletarian revolutionaries of Wuhan City are determined to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, launching a high tide of revolutionary criticism. They will thoroughly criticize and expose the crimes of the Chinese Khrushchev and the likes of Tao Zhu and Wang Renzhong. The picture shows the proletarian revolutionaries of Wuhan Machine Tool Factory fiercely denouncing the counter-revolutionary two-faced traitor Tao Zhu's heinous crimes of opposing the Party, opposing socialism, and opposing Mao Zedong Thought.
