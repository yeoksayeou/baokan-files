Title: Our Personnel in Japan Follow Chairman Mao's Teachings, Bravely Fight Against Oppression
Author:
Date: 1967-09-18
Edition: 5

Our personnel stationed in Japan, following the great teachings of Chairman Mao, fearlessly confront oppression and bravely fight back. Together with Japanese friends who came to support them, they loudly read Chairman Mao's quotations, strongly protesting against the anti-China actions of the Sato government.

	New China News Agency (Telephoto)
