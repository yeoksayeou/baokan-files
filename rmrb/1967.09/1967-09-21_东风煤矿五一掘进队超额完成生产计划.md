Title: The Five-One Excavation Team of Dongfeng Coal Mine Exceeds Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-09-21
Edition: 3

The proletarian revolutionary faction of the Five-One Excavation Team of Dongfeng Coal Mine of Henan Huobei Mining Bureau, the "June 23rd Commune" fighters, in the struggle against the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road, represented by the Chinese Khrushchev, united with the broad revolutionary masses, focused on revolution, and vigorously promoted production. From January to August this year, they excavated 722 meters of rock tunnels, exceeding the production plan by 10 percent.

Currently, the proletarian revolutionary faction of the Five-One Excavation Team is continuing to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasizing proletarian politics, and in the struggle, gradually uniting the majority of cadres and masses, using every possible means to complete the state's tasks, achieving "revolution above ground and production below ground."

### 东风煤矿五一掘进队超额完成生产计划

1967-09-21
第3版()
专栏：

　  东风煤矿五一掘进队超额完成生产计划
    河南鹤壁矿务局东风煤矿五一掘进队的无产阶级革命派——“二七公社”战士，在向以中国赫鲁晓夫为代表的党内一小撮走资本主义道路当权派展开大批判、大斗争中，团结广大革命群众，狠抓革命，猛促生产，今年一至八月份掘进岩石巷道七百二十二米，超额百分之十完成生产计划。
    现在，五一掘进队的无产阶级革命派正继续高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出无产阶级政治，在斗争中逐步团结了干部和群众的大多数，千方百计地完成国家任务，做到了“上井搞好革命，下井搞好生产”。
                                          （据新华社讯）
