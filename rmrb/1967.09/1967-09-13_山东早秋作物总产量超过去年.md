Title: Shandong's Early Autumn Crop Yields Exceed Last Year's
Author:
Date: 1967-09-13
Edition: 4

In the midst of the triumphant songs of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Shandong Province has reported a bumper harvest of early autumn crops. At present, the province's 19 million mu of sorghum, corn, and millet have basically been harvested, with the total yield exceeding that of last year. Some regions have not seen such a good harvest in many years. The broad masses of peasants are now selecting and drying the grain, actively selling it to the state.

### 山东早秋作物总产量超过去年

1967-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    山东早秋作物总产量超过去年
    在无产阶级文化大革命节节胜利的凯歌声中，山东省传来了早秋作物丰收的喜讯。目前，全省一千九百多万亩谷子、玉米、高粱等早秋作物已经基本收割完毕，总产量超过了去年，有些地区是多年来未有的好收成。广大农民正在选晒粮食，积极向国家交售。
