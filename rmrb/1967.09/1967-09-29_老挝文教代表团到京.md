Title: Lao Delegation of Education and Culture Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-09-29
Edition: 8

A delegation of education and culture from Laos, led by Bunpeng Pantathong, arrived in Beijing today. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the China-Laos Friendship Association to participate in National Day celebrations and conduct a friendly visit.

### 老挝文教代表团到京

1967-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    老挝文教代表团到京
　　新华社二十八日讯　以奔平·潘塔翁为首的老挝文教代表团今天到达北京。代表团是应中国老挝友好协会邀请，前来我国参加国庆活动，并进行友好访问的。
