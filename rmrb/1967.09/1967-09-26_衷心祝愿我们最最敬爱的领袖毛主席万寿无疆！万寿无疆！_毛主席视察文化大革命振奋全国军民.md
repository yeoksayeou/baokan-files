Title: Heartfelt Wishes for the Long Life of Our Most Beloved Leader Chairman Mao! Long Live Chairman Mao!
Author:
Date: 1967-09-26
Edition: 1

Heartfelt wishes for the long life of our most beloved leader Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao!

Chairman Mao Inspects the Cultural Revolution, Inspiring the Nation's Military and Civilians

In the unprecedentedly good situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Chairman Mao inspected the northern, central, and eastern regions, investigating the situation of the Cultural Revolution in the provinces and municipalities of Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang, and Shanghai. The good news spread across the vast territory of our heroic motherland. Hundreds of millions of military and civilian personnel rejoiced and leaped with excitement, holding meetings and parades, determined to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment and make the Cultural Revolution even better.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 25th, the news that our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao inspected the northern, central, and eastern regions in the unprecedentedly good situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution across the country has greatly encouraged the hundreds of millions of revolutionary masses in various places. Today, from early morning to late night, the entire nation is in a state of joy. From Changbai Mountain to Hainan Island, from the shores of the East China Sea to the foot of the Tian Shan Mountains, across the vast territory of our heroic motherland, drums and firecrackers resound everywhere. "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand years!" and the song "The Great Ship Sails with the Helmsman" echo through the clouds and shake the earth.

Revolutionary cadres of the proletariat, Red Guards, and broad revolutionary masses from various places, with hearts full of boundless love, faith, loyalty, and admiration for the great leader Chairman Mao, held grand meetings, parades, and various celebrations. They enthusiastically hailed the unprecedentedly good situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao, and enthusiastically hailed the great victories achieved in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution over the past more than a year. Broad revolutionary cadres of the proletariat said excitedly that Chairman Mao's inspection is the greatest concern, support, and encouragement for us. We must resolutely follow Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, closely follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, advance victoriously, and achieve a thorough victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The news spread, and the broad revolutionary cadres and masses of the proletariat in the capital carried giant portraits of Chairman Mao, beat drums and gongs, and paraded to the location of the Party Central Committee and the State Council to report the good news. The parade teams surged onto the streets like a tide. Revolutionary workers of the Capital Steel Company said: Our heart's red sun, the reddest red sun, Chairman Mao, in the unprecedentedly good situation of the Cultural Revolution, personally went to the countryside for an inspection, issuing the latest instructions to the working class. We are indeed fortunate to have such a great and talented leader. We will follow Chairman Mao's latest instructions, and now we have already achieved revolutionary great unity. We will use the sharp weapon of Mao Zedong Thought to further carry out revolutionary great criticism, do a good job in struggle-criticism-transformation, grasp revolution, and promote production, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end. Broad Red Guards and revolutionary students in the capital's colleges and universities held celebration meetings and parades today.

In Shanghai, the source of the "January Revolution," millions of revolutionary masses cheered in unison today as Chairman Mao personally inspected various places and came to Shanghai. Members of the revolutionary organization of Shanghai's working class, "Workers' General Headquarters," said proudly that we Shanghai workers will resolutely carry out Chairman Mao's latest instructions and quickly achieve revolutionary great unity. The task before us now is to further consolidate and develop revolutionary great unity in terms of ideology, and to launch a new high tide of revolutionary great criticism on a broader and deeper scale, and to respond to Chairman Mao's greatest concern for us with practical actions! In recent periods, many Red Guards in Shanghai's schools, following Chairman Mao's teachings, have quickly united on the basis of Mao Zedong Thought by learning from the working class.

Nearly 200,000 revolutionary cadres of the proletariat, revolutionary masses, and PLA combatants in the Zhengzhou area of Henan held a grand celebration meeting and parade this morning. The responsible person of the Henan Provincial Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group and the first political commissar of the provincial military region, Comrade Liu Jianxun, said at the celebration meeting: Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao is in high spirits and in good health, which is the greatest happiness for all the people of China and the world! Chairman Mao is very concerned about the situation of the Cultural Revolution in Henan and the revolutionary great unity. This is Chairman Mao's latest instruction, and we must execute it resolutely and swiftly, quickly achieve revolutionary great unity in the revolutionary great criticism, and report the good news to Chairman Mao! More than 100,000 revolutionary cadres of the proletariat and local garrison combatants in Kaifeng also held a grand celebration meeting.

More than 100,000 revolutionary cadres of the proletariat, revolutionary masses, and garrison combatants in the Wuhan area held a grand meeting and parade today, full of revolutionary joy and fighting spirit. The political commissar of the Wuhan Garrison Command, Zhang Chunqing, said at the meeting that our great leader Chairman Mao is very concerned about the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the Wuhan area. At this moment, the entire audience of soldiers and civilians immediately became excited, with drums and gongs resounding and cheers echoing. Revolutionary workers from Wuhan Steel Company and the Wuhan railway system expressed their determination to seriously implement Chairman Mao's great policy of "grasp revolution, promote production," to complete the state plan quickly, efficiently, and economically, and to create new achievements to celebrate National Day.

Today, the entire city of Changsha is in a state of joy. Before dawn, the broad revolutionary cadres and masses of the proletariat gathered, carrying giant portraits of the great leader Chairman Mao, holding high red flags, and holding red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" from all directions, surging onto the streets like a tide. In the morning, 150,000 revolutionary masses and garrison combatants held a celebration meeting. The responsible person of the provincial revolutionary committee preparatory group, Li Yuan, said at the meeting: The great leader Chairman Mao personally came to Hunan to investigate the situation of the Cultural Revolution, which is a major event in our province's political life. Chairman Mao has shown us the way forward and the direction of struggle. We, the revolutionary cadres of the proletariat from Chairman Mao's hometown, must act most actively, swiftly, and resolutely according to Chairman Mao's latest instructions, take immediate action, and quickly achieve revolutionary great unity. The meeting passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao with thunderous applause.

Today, hundreds of thousands of revolutionary masses in Nanchang City took to the streets, holding high red flags, carrying giant portraits of Chairman Mao, and holding red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," to hold a celebration parade. The responsible person of the Jiangxi Provincial Proletarian Revolutionary Great Unity Preparatory Committee said excitedly: We, the children of Jinggangshan, will not disappoint Chairman Mao's expectations for us. We must closely follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, deepen the revolutionary great criticism, launch military support activities on a larger scale, and launch a new high tide in industrial and agricultural production, and report the new battle achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao. Revolutionary cadres of the proletariat from the Anshan Coal Mine with a glorious revolutionary tradition held a grand celebration meeting today. They said: Chairman Mao's personal inspection of Jiangxi has added boundless strength to us. We, the workers of Anshan, will closely follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, resolutely overthrow the Chinese Khrushchev, and unconditionally implement Chairman Mao's great instruction of "grasp revolution, promote production," to quickly, efficiently, and economically complete the coal production task, and repay Chairman Mao's concern for us.

More than 100,000 revolutionary cadres of the proletariat and local garrison combatants in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City held a grand meeting and parade today. Many factories and schools' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams took to the streets to spread the good news. At the expanded meeting of the provincial workers' congress preparatory committee held today, representatives from all over the province expressed their determination to better study and apply Chairman Mao's works, closely follow the great leader Chairman Mao's strategic deployment, and strive to promote the great unity of the proletarian revolutionary cadres in the province and launch a new high tide of revolutionary great criticism!

Today, tens of thousands to hundreds of thousands of people in Guiyang, Harbin, Jinan, Xining, Qingdao, and Hohhot held grand celebration meetings and parades. The chairman of the Guizhou Provincial Revolutionary Committee, Li Zaichan, said that Chairman Mao's heart is connected with the revolutionary masses. We must forever be loyal to Chairman Mao, forever loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, and forever loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. All the proletarian revolutionary cadres in our province must raise high the flag of proletarian revolutionary criticism led by Chairman Mao, overthrow the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road, and in the revolutionary great criticism, develop and consolidate the revolutionary great unity and the revolutionary "three-in-one combination," do a good job in struggle-criticism-transformation in our units, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end. The chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Revolutionary Committee and the commander of the Qinghai Military Region, Liu Xianquan, said that the great leader Chairman Mao inspected the northern, central, and eastern regions, investigating the situation of the Cultural Revolution in the provinces and municipalities of Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Zhejiang, and Shanghai, which is a great event for the proletarian revolutionary cadres, revolutionary masses of all ethnic groups, and broad PLA combatants across the country. The broad proletarian revolutionary cadres and revolutionary masses in Shanxi, Tianjin, Anshan, and other places expressed their determination to closely follow the great strategic deployment of the great commander Chairman Mao and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to a victorious end when they cheered the great news that Chairman Mao inspected the northern, central, and eastern regions.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 25th, the great news that our most beloved great teacher and leader Chairman Mao returned to Beijing after inspecting the northern, central, and eastern regions in the unprecedentedly good situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution across the country has made the broad PLA combatants immersed in boundless joy and infinite happiness all day long.

In the center of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Beijing, the revolutionary cadres of the proletariat from the PLA's army, navy, and air force headquarters in Beijing immediately became excited upon hearing this great news. The joyous crowds carried giant portraits of Chairman Mao, held high red flags, beat drums and gongs, and took to the streets to hold celebration parades. The tide of reporting good news surged towards Tiananmen and the reception station of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, from morning to night, unceasingly. The revolutionary cadres of the proletariat from the headquarters of the various military branches, the National Defense Science and Technology Commission, the Navy, the Air Force, the Beijing Military Region, the Second Artillery Corps, and the Armored Corps also held celebration meetings separately. The leaders of the three military branches also participated in the celebration meetings or parades separately.

The good news spread through the airwaves, reaching the front lines of the thousand-li coastal and border defenses, reaching the air force bases, naval vessels, high-altitude outposts, and reaching the front lines of the "three supports, two militaries" tightly united with the proletarian revolutionary cadres. Wherever the good news reached, there was joy. The broad combatants of the Nanjing, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Fuzhou, Jinan, Kunming, Lanzhou, Chengdu, Wuhan, and other military units listened to the broadcast repeatedly, with hearts full of excitement and love for the great leader Chairman Mao, and held discussions. The broad combatants of the military units in Inner Mongolia, Tibet, Xinjiang, Zhejiang, Jiangxi, Henan, Qinghai, Heilongjiang, Guizhou, and other places said excitedly when cheering that Chairman Mao personally inspected and investigated the situation of the Cultural Revolution, guiding us to break the waves and advance bravely along Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment.

第1版()
专栏：

　　衷心祝愿我们最最敬爱的领袖毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
　　毛主席视察文化大革命振奋全国军民
　　在无产阶级文化大革命空前大好形势下，毛主席视察了华北、中南和华东地区，调查了河南、湖北、湖南、江西、浙江、上海等省市文化大革命的情况。喜讯传遍英雄祖国辽阔的疆土。亿万军民欢腾跳跃，纷纷集会游行，决心紧跟毛主席伟大战略部署，把文化大革命搞得更好更好。
    据新华社二十五日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席在全国无产阶级文化大革命空前大好的形势下视察了华北、中南和华东地区的喜讯，极大地鼓舞了全国各地亿万革命群众。今天，从凌晨到深夜，举国上下，一片欢腾。从长白山到海南岛，从东海之滨到天山脚下，在我们英雄祖国辽阔的疆土上，到处锣鼓喧天，鞭炮齐鸣。“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”的欢呼声和《大海航行靠舵手》的歌声响彻云霄，震动大地。
    各地无产阶级革命派战士、红卫兵小将和广大革命群众，怀着对伟大领袖毛主席无限热爱、无限信仰、无限忠诚、无限崇拜的心情，纷纷举行盛大的集会、游行和各种庆祝活动。他们最热烈地欢呼毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命空前大好的形势，最热烈地欢呼无产阶级文化大革命一年多来取得的伟大胜利。广大无产阶级革命派战士极其兴奋地说，毛主席的视察，是对我们最大的关怀、支持和鼓舞。我们要坚决沿着毛主席的无产阶级革命路线，紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，乘胜前进，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的彻底胜利。
