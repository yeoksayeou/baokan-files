Title: "Vietnam in Struggle" Exhibition Opens at Beijing Cultural Palace
Author:
Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: The "Vietnam in Struggle" exhibition, sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, opened on the 4th at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, and Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, attended the opening ceremony. Foreign guests present included Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Soviet Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Fedelin, Mongolian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Labudan, German Democratic Republic Diplomatic Mission Chargé d'Affaires Grutner, Hungarian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Fekert, Polish Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Dowding, Indonesian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Isha Madi, Indian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Kaur, Korean Embassy Counselor Choi Young, and Burmese Embassy Third Secretary Charlie Piroo. Also in attendance were approximately 200 people from all walks of life in the capital, as well as Chinese and foreign journalists.

Huang Wen Huan, Ambassador-level Representative of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, said in his speech at the opening ceremony: This exhibition roughly reflects how the Vietnamese people, under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh and the Lao Dong Party, are fighting against the French and American imperialist aggressors. He also pointed out: Vietnam's national liberation war is arduous, but the Vietnamese people have deep confidence that they will achieve final victory in this struggle.

Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, said in his subsequent speech: This exhibition demonstrates the great achievements of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the past six years in politics, military affairs, economy, culture, and education, and shows the bravery, diligence, and wisdom of the Vietnamese people.

The exhibition mainly features photographs, as well as various Vietnamese books published under difficult material conditions. These photographs introduce the situation of President Ho Chi Minh leading the war of resistance, the founding of the Vietnam Lao Dong Party, the establishment of the Vietnam National United Front, and the establishment of the anti-imperialist alliance of the three nations of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos. They also introduce the heroism of the People's Army and guerrillas in combat, the patriotic labor enthusiasm of workers, the enthusiastic support of the front lines by peasants, women, and children, and the Vietnamese people's warm celebration of the victory of the Chinese and Soviet revolutions.

The exhibition is scheduled to close on September 28.

### “斗争中的越南”展览会在北京文化宫揭幕

1951-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    “斗争中的越南”展览会
    在北京文化宫揭幕 【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“斗争中的越南”展览会，四日在北京劳动人民文化宫揭幕。参加揭幕典礼的有政务院文化教育委员会副主任沈雁冰和外交部副部长章汉夫等人。到会的外宾有捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、苏联大使馆临时代办费德林、蒙古大使馆临时代办拉布丹、德意志民主共和国外交使团临时代办葛鲁特纳、匈牙利大使馆临时代办费凯尔特、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、印度大使馆临时代办高尔、朝鲜大使馆参赞崔英和缅甸大使馆三等秘书查理辟鲁等。与会的尚有首都各界人士和中外记者约二百人。
    越南民主共和国驻华代表团大使衔代表黄文欢在揭幕礼时致词说：这次展览会大体地反映出越南人民如何在胡志明主席及劳动党领导下和法、美帝国主义侵略者进行斗争的情形。他并指出：越南的民族解放战争是艰苦的，但越南人民深具信心：即他们一定能在这一斗争中取得最后胜利。
    政务院文化教育委员会副主任沈雁冰继致词说：这个展览会表现了越南民主共和国六年来在政治、军事、经济、文教等各方面的巨大成就，显示了越南人民的勇敢、勤劳和智慧。
    展览会以图片为主，并有在物质条件困难情况下出版的越文书籍多种。这些图片介绍了胡志明主席领导抗战，越南劳动党建党和越南国民联合战线成立及越南、柬埔寨与老挝三民族反帝联盟的成立等情形，介绍了人民军和游击队作战的英勇，工人的爱国主义劳动热情，农民、妇女和儿童等支援前线的踊跃，以及越南人民热烈庆祝中苏革命胜利的情况。
    展览会定于九月二十八日闭幕。
