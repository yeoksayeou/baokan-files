Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Holds Tea Party to Welcome Ehrenburg and Neruda
Author:
Date: 1951-09-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd dispatch: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters held a tea party in Beijing this afternoon to welcome Ehrenburg and his wife, and Neruda and his wife. Participants included Wu Yuzhang and Huang Yanpei, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters; Qian Junrui, director-general; Shen Zhiyuan, Lai Yali, and Zhao Zhongchi, deputy directors-general; and more than one hundred staff members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

    Vice President Wu Yuzhang delivered a welcoming speech, expressing his respect for Ehrenburg and Neruda's glorious contributions to the cause of defending world peace. Vice President Huang Yanpei recited a poem he dedicated to Ehrenburg and Neruda. In his speech, Ehrenburg emphasized that the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples is a true friendship that has stood the test of time. Then, Neruda recited a poem he wrote in tribute to the Chinese people (see page 3 of today's newspaper). The tea party proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 中苏友协总会举行茶会欢迎爱伦堡聂鲁达

1951-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举行茶会欢迎爱伦堡聂鲁达
    【新华社二十二日讯】中苏友好协会总会今日下午在北京举行欢迎爱伦堡夫妇、聂鲁达夫妇的茶会。参加者有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、黄炎培，总干事钱俊瑞，副总干事沈志远、赖亚力、赵仲池，以及中苏友好协会工作人员一百余人。
    吴玉章副会长致欢迎词，对爱伦堡、聂鲁达
    在保卫世界和平事业中的光辉贡献表示敬意。黄炎培副会长朗诵了他送给爱伦堡和聂鲁达的诗。爱伦堡致词时强调中苏两国人民的友谊是经历了考验的真实友谊。接着，聂鲁达朗诵了他所作的向中国人民致敬的诗篇（见今天本报第三版）。茶会始终在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
