Title: Typhoon Alert Lifted
Author:
Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 1

    Typhoon Alert Lifted
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] The Central Meteorological Observatory announced the lifting of the typhoon alert at 7:00 PM on September 4th. The typhoon's force has greatly weakened after crossing Hainan Island and landing west through Tokyo Bay, and it no longer has any impact on our country.

### 台风警报解除

1951-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    台风警报解除
    【新华社四日讯】中央气象台九月四日下午七时宣布解除台风警报。按台风自穿越海南岛、经东京湾向西登陆后，风势大为减弱，对我国已无影响。
