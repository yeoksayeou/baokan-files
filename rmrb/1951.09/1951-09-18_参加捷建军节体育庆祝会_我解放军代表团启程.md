Title: PLA Delegation Departs to Participate in Czechoslovak Army Day Sports Celebration
Author:
Date: 1951-09-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, which will participate in the Czechoslovak Republic Army Day (October 7th) sports celebration, departed from Beijing on the afternoon of the 16th for Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia. The head of the delegation is Liu Daosheng, the deputy head is Xiong Botao, the secretary-general is Li Zhaobing, and the representatives are: Jiang Xueshan, head of the Tashan Hero Regiment; Wang Yushan, a Matrosov-type hero; Zhang Ba, a blasting hero; Yu Fuxiang, a model worker and combat hero; Ding Zhihui (female), a model medical worker; Shen Yuanzhen, a representative of China's first group of female parachutists; and sports teams, totaling 59 people. The sports events to be participated in are: soccer, 3,000-meter relay race carrying submachine guns, and 5-kilometer international race, etc. On the afternoon of the 16th, when the delegation departed, more than 300 people, including heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and representatives of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, went to the station to see them off. Weisskopf, the Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, and Procházka, the Military Attaché of the Embassy, also went to the station to see them off.

### 参加捷建军节体育庆祝会  我解放军代表团启程

1951-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    参加捷建军节体育庆祝会
    我解放军代表团启程
    【新华社十七日讯】参加捷克斯洛伐克共和国建军节（十月七日）体育庆祝会的中国人民解放军代表团，已于十六日下午由北京出发前往捷克京城布拉格。代表团团长刘道生，副团长熊伯涛，秘书长李兆炳，代表有：塔山英雄团团长江雪山，马特洛索夫型英雄王玉山，爆炸英雄张八，模范工作者、战斗英雄于复祥，模范医务工作者丁志辉（女），中国第一批女跳伞员代表沈元珍，以及体育队等共五十九人。准备参加的体育项目有：足球、三千米携带手提机枪的接力赛跑，五公里国际赛跑等。十六日下午代表团启程时，赴车站欢送者有人民革命军事委员会各部负责人及陆、海、空军代表等共三百余人，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使魏斯柯普夫及大使馆武官普罗查亦到站欢送。
