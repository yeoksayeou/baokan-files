Title: U.S. "Peace Crusaders" Leader Writes to Acheson, Advocating for China and India to Attend the San Francisco Conference, Arguing for Broad Discussion and Revision of the Treaty

Author: 
Date: 1951-09-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 7th: Twelve prominent leaders of the "American Peace Crusaders" sent a telegram to U.S. Secretary of State Acheson on the 5th, requesting that the People's Republic of China and India be invited to the San Francisco Treaty of Peace Conference, and requesting that the broadest possible discussion and possible revision of the treaty be permitted. The twelve signatories to this telegram included Dr. W.E.B. Du Bois, Physics Professor Morrison, Union Leader Brison, Reverend Forbes, Reverend Evans, and Robertson. They said that the result of the proposed treaty, opposed by the largest portion of the Asian people and nations, might be: the treaty being passed mainly by European powers and the United States. The treaty cannot achieve peace, but will only create a situation where the European powers and the United States are allied with Japan against Asia.

### 美国“和平十字军”领袖致函艾奇逊  主张邀中印两国出席旧金山会议  认为必须对和约展开广泛讨论和修改

1951-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国“和平十字军”领袖致函艾奇逊
    主张邀中印两国出席旧金山会议
    认为必须对和约展开广泛讨论和修改
    【新华社八日讯】电通社纽约七日讯：十二名著名的“美国和平十字军”领袖于五日致电美国国务卿艾奇逊，要求邀请中华人民共和国及印度出席旧金山对日和约会议，并要求允许对和约展开最广泛的讨论和作可能的修改。这一电报的十二个签名者包括威廉·杜波依斯博士，物理学教授摩里逊，工会领袖勃里逊，福贝斯牧师，伊凡斯牧师及罗伯逊在内。他们说，最大部分的亚洲人民和国家反对建议中的条约的结果可能是：主要由欧洲列强和美国通过条约。和约不能获致和平，只会造成欧洲列强和美国与日本联盟反对亚洲的局面。
