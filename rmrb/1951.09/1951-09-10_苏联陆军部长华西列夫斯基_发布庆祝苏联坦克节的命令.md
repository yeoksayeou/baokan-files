Title: Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky Issues Order Celebrating Soviet Tank Day
Author:
Date: 1951-09-10
Page: 4

    Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky
    Issues Order Celebrating Soviet Tank Day
   [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 9th] TASS reports from Moscow: Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky issued an order on September 9th celebrating Soviet Tank Day. The order stated: "Comrade Tankers and Tank Manufacturers! Comrade Soldiers and Non-Commissioned Officers! Comrade Lieutenants, Colonels, and Generals! Today, the Soviet Army and all the people of our country celebrate Tank Day together, and review the achievements of tankers and tank manufacturers in fulfilling the tasks set by the Soviet government and our leader and teacher, the great Stalin. On this Tank Day, I extend my respects and congratulations to you, and wish the personnel of the armored and mechanized forces and the workers in the tank manufacturing industry even greater achievements in strengthening the combat effectiveness of the Soviet Army. To commemorate Tank Day, I order: Today, September 9, 1951, a twenty-gun salute will be fired in our country's capital, Moscow, and in the capitals of all the Union Republics."

### 苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基  发布庆祝苏联坦克节的命令

1951-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基
    发布庆祝苏联坦克节的命令
   【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基九日发布庆祝苏联坦克节的命令。命令说：“坦克手和坦克制造者同志们！战士与士官同志们！尉官、校官与将军同志们！今天，苏联军队和我国全体人民一道庆祝坦克节，并检阅坦克手和坦克制造者在执行苏联政府及我们的领袖和导师伟大的斯大林所规定的任务方面所获得的成就。值此坦克节，我向你们致敬和祝贺，并预祝装甲与机械化部队的人员和坦克制造业的工人，在加强苏联军队战斗力的工作中取得更多的成就。为了纪念坦克节，我命令：今天，一九五一年九月九日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科及各加盟共和国的首都，各鸣放礼炮二十响。”
