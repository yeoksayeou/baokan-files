Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-09-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Children in New China are universally receiving patriotic education. Their love for the motherland is growing stronger day by day. This is a scene of students from the Second Affiliated Elementary School of Beijing Normal University, surrounding a globe and listening to their teacher explain: "Our motherland is a vast and expansive country with abundant resources."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的儿童普遍地受到了爱国主义的教育。他们对祖国的爱日益增强。这是北京师范大学第二附属小学的学生，在上地理课的时候，围着地球仪听教员给他们讲解：“我们的祖国是辽阔广大物产丰富的国家。”
