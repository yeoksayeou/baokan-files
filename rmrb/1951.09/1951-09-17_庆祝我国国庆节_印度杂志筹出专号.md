Title: Indian Magazines Prepare Special Issues to Celebrate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 16th - Many Indian magazines are preparing to publish special issues about the new China to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    The Indian Communist Party's official publication "People's Power," published in Madras, has decided to dedicate its September 30th issue as a special issue commemorating the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    The Hindi monthly "New China" will be launched on October 1st in Benares, United Provinces.

    The weekly "New Generation," with a circulation of over 10,000 copies, has decided to publish a special issue of "People's China" on September 29th. It will contain articles, poems, and stories written specifically to celebrate China's National Day. The Punjabi monthly "Garden" has decided to publish a "China Special Issue" on October 1st.

    In addition, the Hindi weekly "New Malwa," published in Indore City, Central Provinces, the English monthly "Today India," published in Allahabad City, United Provinces, and the English weekly "Lightning," published in Bombay, are also preparing to publish special issues about the new China to commemorate China's National Day.

### 庆祝我国国庆节  印度杂志筹出专号

1951-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国国庆节
    印度杂志筹出专号
    【新华社新德里十六日电】为庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年，印度的很多杂志都准备出版有关新中国的专号。
    在马德拉斯出版的印度共产党机关刊“人民力量”，决定以九月三十日的一期作为纪念中华人民共和国成立两周年的专号。
    印地文月刊“新中国”将在十月一日在联合省贝拿勒斯创刊。
    销路超过一万份的“新世代”周刊，决定在九月二十九日出版“人民中国”专号。内有专为庆祝中国国庆而写的文章、诗歌和故事。旁遮普文的“花园”月刊决定在十月一日出版“中国专号”。
    此外，在中央省印度尔城出版的印地文“新马尔瓦”周刊、在联合省阿拉哈巴德城出版的英文“今日印度”月刊和孟买出版的英文“闪电”周刊，也都准备出版有关新中国的专号，以纪念中国国庆。
