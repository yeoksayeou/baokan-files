Title: Guangdong Coastal Areas Must Pay Attention to Typhoon Prevention
Author:
Date: 1951-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on September 1st: At 2:00 PM on the 1st, the typhoon was located 200 li directly north of the Philippines (i.e., 18.5 degrees north latitude, 120.5 degrees east longitude), moving in a west-northwest direction at a speed of 40 li per hour. At a distance of 130 li from the typhoon center, the maximum wind speed was 270 li per hour; at a distance of 250 li from the center, the wind speed was 180 li per hour. In the next 24 hours, it will strike 250 li directly south of Dongsha Island. The southern part of the South China Sea and the East China Sea have been affected and will experience storms and heavy rain. The coastal areas of Guangdong must pay attention to prevention.

### 广东沿海须注意预防台风

1951-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    广东沿海须注意预防台风
    【新华社一日讯】中央气象台九月一日下午八时发出台风警报：一日下午二时台风的位置在菲律宾正北二百华里（即北纬十八·五度东经一百二十·五度）的地方，正向西偏北的方向进行，每小时移动四十华里。离开台风中心一百三十华里的地方，最大风速每小时二百七十华里，离开中心二百五十华里的地方，风速是每小时一百八十华里。未来二十四小时内将要袭击东沙岛正南二百五十华里的地方。中国南海与东海之南部已受影响，将起暴风大雨，广东沿海必须注意预防。
