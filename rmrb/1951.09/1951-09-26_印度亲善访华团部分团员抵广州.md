Title: Part of the Indian Goodwill Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1951-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 25th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, the All China Federations of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All China Federation of Democratic Youth, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the head of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, Mr. Sundarlal, and some members of the delegation, arrived in Guangzhou on the 22nd and 24th. Li Zhangda, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Li Jianzhen, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Democratic Women's Federation, Yu Hui, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, and representatives from various democratic parties and circles welcomed them. After all members of the delegation arrive in Guangzhou, they will travel north to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations.

### 印度亲善访华团部分团员抵广州

1951-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度亲善访华团部分团员抵广州
    【新华社广州二十五日电】应中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会的邀请到中国来的印度亲善访华团团长森德拉尔及部分团员，已于二十二、二十四日先后抵达广州。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会广东省分会主席李章达、广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云、广州市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟、广东省民主妇女联合会筹备会主任李坚真、广州市民主妇女联合会筹备会副主任余慧、广州市副市长朱光和各民主党派、各界代表均往欢迎。该访问团团员全部到达广州后即北上赴北京，参加国庆庆典。
