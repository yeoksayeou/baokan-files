Title: Vietnamese Foreign Minister Issues Statement: Bao Dai Puppet Regime Has No Right to Represent Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, dated September 4th: Vietnamese Minister of Foreign Affairs Hoàng Minh Giám made a statement at a press conference regarding the issue of the Bao Dai puppet regime sending representatives to the San Francisco Peace Treaty. He said: "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam already declared on July 20, 1951, that the French colonialists and their puppet Bao Dai clique absolutely have no right to represent Vietnam and the Vietnamese people in discussing the issue of the Treaty of Peace with Japan. Today, we once again declare that the puppet Bao Dai and Trần Văn Hữu absolutely have no right to represent the Vietnamese people in signing any treaty with any foreign country." Hoàng Minh Giám emphasized: "The Vietnamese people and the government believe that the signature of the puppet and traitor is absolutely invalid."

### 越南外长发表声明  保大傀儡无权代表越南

1951-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    越南外长发表声明
    保大傀儡无权代表越南 【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社北越四日电：越南民主共和国政府外交部部长黄明鉴就保大傀儡派代表出席旧金山对日和约问题在记者招待会上发表谈话说：“越南民主共和国政府已经在一九五一年七月二十日声明说：法国殖民者及其傀儡保大集团绝对无权代表越南及越南人民讨论对日和约问题。今天我们再一次宣告：傀儡保大与陈文友绝对无权代表越南人民与任何外国签订任何条约。”黄明鉴强调说：“越南人民与政府认为：傀儡与卖国贼的签字是绝对无效的。”
