Title: India and Pakistan Decide to Send Delegations to International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-09-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 14th: The decision of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council on July 23rd to hold an international economic conference in Moscow has received the support of peace-loving people in India and Pakistan. Professor Huadeya, a famous Indian economist and chairman of the Indian Preparatory Committee for the International Economic Conference, announced at a press conference in Mumbai on the 10th that India will send a 15-member delegation to the international economic conference to be held in Moscow in December. He said: The preparatory committee plans to finalize the list of delegates to attend the meeting within a month.
    The Karachi Peace Committee, the capital of Pakistan, decided on the 9th to send a 10-member delegation to the International Economic Conference. A three-member preparatory committee has now been established to assist in this work.

### 印度巴基斯坦决派遣代表团出席国际经济会议

1951-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印度巴基斯坦决派遣代表团出席国际经济会议
    【新华社新德里十四日电】七月二十三日世界和平理事会执行局关于在莫斯科举行国际经济会议的决定，已获得印度和巴基斯坦两国爱好和平人民的支持。印度著名经济学家、印度国际经济会议筹备委员会主席华德亚教授于十日在孟买举行的招待记者会上宣布：印度将派遣由十五人组成的代表团出席十二月间在莫斯科举行的国际经济会议。他说：筹备委员会计划在一个月内最后决定出席会议的代表名单。
    巴基斯坦首都喀喇蚩和平委员会于九日决定派遣十人代表团出席国际经济会议。现在已成立三人筹备委员会，以协助这项工作的进行。
