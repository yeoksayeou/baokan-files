Title: Government Administration Council Holds 101st Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-09-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 7th.

    The 101st meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 7th. The meeting heard and approved the report of Chen Qiyuan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the recent disaster situation and relief work in various parts of the country. After hearing the report of Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, on the disaster situation in the Liaohe River area of ​​Northeast China, the meeting decided to organize a condolence group to visit and express sympathy. The meeting also passed a number of appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment cases to the Central People's Government for approval.

### 政务院举行第一零一次会议

1951-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一零一次会议
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一○一次政务会议于七日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府内务部陈其瑗副部长所作的关于全国各地最近灾情与救灾工作的报告。会议在听取了东北人民政府高崇民副主席关于东北辽河地区灾情的报告后决定组织慰问团前往慰问。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府批准任命案多起。
