Title: Burmese Ambassador U Myint Thein Recalled; New Ambassador U Hla Maw Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The first Burmese Ambassador to China, U Myint Thein, was recalled on June 24th. With the agreement of the Chinese government, the government of the Union of Burma appointed U Hla Maw as the Burmese Ambassador to China.

Ambassador U Hla Maw arrived in Beijing by car at 3:50 PM today. Those who went to the station to greet him included Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. In addition, there were the Indian Ambassador, the Swedish Ambassador, the Danish Minister, the Swiss Minister, the Chargé d'affaires of the Indonesian, Pakistani, Polish and Hungarian Embassies, the Counselors of the Soviet and Bulgarian Embassies, personnel from the Czech, Romanian Embassies and the Vietnamese Delegation, as well as representatives of the British, Norwegian, and Dutch governments who are here for negotiations.

### 缅甸大使吴敏登奉调返国  新任大使吴拉茂昨日抵京

1951-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸大使吴敏登奉调返国
    新任大使吴拉茂昨日抵京
    【新华社十七日讯】缅甸首任驻华特命全权大使吴敏登已于六月二十四日奉调返国。缅甸联邦政府于征得我国政府同意后，任命吴拉茂为缅甸驻我国特命全权大使。
    吴拉茂大使于今日下午三时五十分乘车抵京。赴站迎接者有我外交部办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司代司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美，此外尚有印度大使、瑞典大使、丹麦公使、瑞士公使，印尼、巴基斯坦、波兰及匈牙利大使馆的临时代办，苏联及保加利亚使馆的参赞，捷克、罗马尼亚使馆及越南代表团人员以及英国、挪威、荷兰政府谈判代表等。
