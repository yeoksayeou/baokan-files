Title: Railway Employees' Volunteer Teams from Xi'an, Qingdao, and Other Places Depart One After Another to Aid Korea in Transportation
Author:
Date: 1951-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Railway employees' volunteer transportation teams from Xi'an, Qingdao, Xuzhou, Kunming, and other places departed one after another in late August and early September to work on the front lines in Korea. Employees from each respective railway sub-bureau gave them warm send-offs. More than 3,000 employees of the Xi'an Railway Sub-bureau, at the send-off, held a parade and demonstration against the U.S. sabotage of the Korean armistice negotiations.

### 西安青岛等地铁路员工志愿援朝运输队陆续出发

1951-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    西安青岛等地铁路员工
    志愿援朝运输队陆续出发
    【新华社七日讯】西安、青岛、徐州、昆明等地铁路员工志愿援朝运输队于八月下旬和九月初陆续出发，赴朝鲜前线工作。各该铁路分局员工均热烈欢送。西安铁路分局全体员工三千余人，并于欢送时举行了反对美国破坏朝鲜停战谈判的游行示威。
