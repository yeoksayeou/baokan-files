Title: Beijing Workers Who Participated in the Anti-Japanese War Call on All Workers in the City to Resolutely Oppose American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1951-09-03
Page: 1

    [Report from this newspaper] To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the victory of the Anti-Japanese War on "September 3rd", the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions invited more than 20 workers and trade union cadres who participated in the Anti-Japanese War to a forum on September 2nd to discuss the heroic deeds of workers who persisted in production and struggle during the war. Zhang Hongshun, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, also called on everyone at the meeting to oppose the efforts of British and American imperialism to obliterate the contributions of the Chinese and Soviet people in the Anti-Japanese War, to oppose unilateral peace with Japan, and to oppose the rearmament of Japan.

### 北京曾参加抗日战争的职工号召全市工友坚决反对美国侵略

1951-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    北京曾参加抗日战争的职工号召全市工友坚决反对美国侵略
    【本报讯】为了庆祝“九三”抗日战争胜利六周年纪念日，北京市总工会于九月二日，邀请参加过抗日战争的职工和工会干部二十多人，座谈抗战时工人坚持生产和斗争的英雄事迹。市总工会张鸿舜副主席并在会上号召大家反对美英帝国主义抹杀中苏人民抗日的功勋，反对片面对日媾和，反对重新武装日本。
