Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-09-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is a batch of tractors shipped from the Soviet Union to Xinjiang according to the Sino-Soviet trade and barter agreement signed between the Central People's Government and the Soviet Union. These new agricultural machines will create favorable conditions for the development of agriculture in Xinjiang.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是根据中央人民政府和苏联签订的中苏贸易及换货协定，由苏联运抵新疆的一批拖拉机。这些新式的农业机器将为新疆农业的发展创造有利的条件。
