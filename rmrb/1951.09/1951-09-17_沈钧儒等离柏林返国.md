Title: Shen Junru and Others Leave Berlin to Return to China
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th. The Chinese delegation led by Shen Junru, which attended the Fifth Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, left Berlin on the 15th by plane via Moscow to return to China. The Minister of Justice of the German Democratic Republic, Max Fechner, and Wang Yutian, Counselor of the Chinese Diplomatic Mission to Germany, went to the airport to see them off.

### 沈钧儒等离柏林返国

1951-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    沈钧儒等离柏林返国
    【新华社柏林十六日电】沈钧儒率领的参加国际民主法律工作者协会第五届代表大会的中国代表团，已于十五日离柏林乘飞机转莫斯科返国。德意志民主共和国司法部长费希纳和我国驻德外交使团参赞王雨田等均往机场送行。
