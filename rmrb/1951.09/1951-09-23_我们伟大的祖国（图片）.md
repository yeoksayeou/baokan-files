    Our Great Motherland
    Galla chinensis is a medicinal material produced in large quantities in the southwestern provinces of China. It contains a lot of tannic acid, which can be used as raw material for organic chemical industry, dyes, medicinal raw materials, lithographic raw materials and other purposes. This is in a processing factory, where female workers are selecting a large number of galla chinensis transported from the producing areas.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    五倍子是我国西南各省大量出产的一种药材。它含有很多的单宁酸，可供有机化学工业原料、染料、医药原料、石印原料等多种用途。这是在一所加工工厂里，女工正在把大批从产地运来的五倍子加以挑选。
