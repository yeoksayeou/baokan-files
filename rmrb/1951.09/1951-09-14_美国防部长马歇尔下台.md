Title: US Defense Secretary Marshall Resigns
Author:
Date: 1951-09-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, September 13th: The US government announced on the 12th that US Defense Secretary Marshall has resigned, and the vacancy will be filled by the current Deputy Secretary of Defense, Lovett. Truman has also nominated the head of the US Economic Cooperation Administration, Foster, to be the Deputy Secretary of Defense.

### 美国防部长马歇尔下台

1951-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    美国防部长马歇尔下台
    【新华社十三日讯】华盛顿消息：美国政府十二日宣布：美国国防部长马歇尔业已辞职，遗缺由现任国防部副部长罗维特继任。杜鲁门并已提出由美国经合总署署长浮斯特任国防部副部长。
