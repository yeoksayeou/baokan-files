Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and All-China Women's Federation Send Congratulatory Messages to Bulgaria on National Day

Author: 
Date: 1951-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Women's Federation Send Congratulatory Messages to Bulgaria on National Day

    [Xinhua News Agency News] September 9th marks the seventh anniversary of Bulgaria's National Day. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Central Council of the Bulgarian Trade Unions to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: Central Council of the Bulgarian Trade Unions:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, I extend warm congratulations to the Bulgarian working people on behalf of the Chinese working people, and wish you even greater victories in the great cause of building socialism and defending world peace.
                                         All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 8th] September 9th marks the seventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. Cai Chang, Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Zola Dragoycheva, Chairperson of the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee, to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: Zola Dragoycheva, Chairperson of the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee:
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, I extend warm congratulations to the Bulgarian sisters on behalf of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. We firmly believe that in the common struggle to defend peace and oppose imperialism, the friendship between the women of China and Bulgaria will become closer and develop further.
        Cai Chang, Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                                                September 8th

### 我全国总工会和全国妇联  分别电贺保加利亚国庆

1951-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会和全国妇联
    分别电贺保加利亚国庆
    【新华社讯】九月九日是保加利亚国庆七周年，中华全国总工会特电保加利亚总工会中央理事会致贺。电文如下：保加利亚总工会中央理事会：
    兹值保加利亚人民共和国国庆日，谨代表中国劳动人民向保加利亚劳动人民致以热烈的庆贺，并祝你们在建设社会主义及保卫世界和平的伟大事业中获得更大的胜利。
                                         中华全国总工会
    【新华社八日讯】九月九日为保加利亚人民共和国成立七周年纪念，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅特电保加利亚民主妇女委员会主席左拉·特拉哥依契娃夫人致贺。电文如下：保加利亚民主妇女委员会主席左拉·特拉哥依契娃夫人：
    欣逢保加利亚人民共和国成立七周年纪念，谨代表中华全国民主妇女联合会向保加利亚姊妹们致热烈庆贺。我们深信在保卫和平与反对帝国主义的共同斗争中，中保两国妇女的友谊将更加密切与发展。
        中华全国民主妇女联合会主席  蔡  畅
                                                九月八日
