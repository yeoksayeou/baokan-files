Title: National Railway Exhibition Grandly Opens Yesterday; Vice Chairman Zhu De Personally Cuts Ribbon and Calls for Further Development and Expansion of Railway Construction
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The National Railway Exhibition, jointly organized by the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government and the National Committee of the China Railway Workers' Union, grandly opened at the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace yesterday afternoon. Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government personally attended and cut the ribbon. Those who attended the opening ceremony included Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Deputy Prime Ministers Dong Biwu and Huang Yanpei of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, Acting Chief of General Staff Nie Rongzhen of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Deputy Director Peng Zemin of the Political and Legal Committee, and other leaders of various departments of the Central People's Government, generals of the People's Liberation Army, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than two hundred people.

    In his speech at the opening ceremony, Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, pointed out that the purpose of this exhibition is to centrally demonstrate the achievements of the national railway employees in restoring and building the people's railways under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, actively utilizing their creativity, learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and with the assistance of the broad masses of workers, peasants, and the People's Liberation Army. It aims to educate railway employees and exchange experiences in production and construction. He also pointed out that this exhibition will also greatly enhance the confidence of the broad masses of people in building the motherland.

    Vice Chairman Zhu then delivered a speech, saying: Chinese railways are very important and have been doing very well in the past two years. In the future, they should be further developed and expanded, and their own equipment capabilities should be fully utilized to help other industries.

    Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, and Deputy Prime Ministers Dong Biwu and Huang Yanpei of the Government Administration Council spoke successively. They unanimously pointed out that the people's railways have achieved brilliant results in restoration and construction in the past two years, and with the joint efforts of railway workers across the country, greater development will surely be achieved in the future.

    The exhibition mainly uses models and charts, and the content is mainly divided into six parts: (1) reflecting the restoration of railways since liberation; (2) the main equipment of the people's railways; (3) inventions and creations of employees and the implementation of advanced Soviet working methods; (4) heroic struggles of employees in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea; (5) improvement of employees' cultural welfare life; (6) the situation of newly built routes.

    The exhibition is scheduled to close on October 5th.

### 全国铁路展览会昨隆重开幕  朱德副主席亲临剪彩号召进一步发展与扩大铁道建设

1951-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国铁路展览会昨隆重开幕
    朱德副主席亲临剪彩号召进一步发展与扩大铁道建设
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府铁道部与中国铁路工会全国委员会联合举办的全国铁路展览会于十五日下午在北京劳动人民文化宫隆重举行开幕典礼。中央人民政府朱德副主席亲自莅临剪彩。参加开幕典礼的有中央人民政府副主席李济深、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通、中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武、黄炎培、人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、政治法律委员会副主任彭泽民等，中央人民政府各部门首长、人民解放军将领、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人等共计二百余人。
    中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远在开幕典礼致词中指出，此次展览会的目的，是为了集中表现全国铁路员工在中国共产党和毛主席领导下，积极发挥自己的创造性，学习苏联先进经验，并在广大工人、农民、人民解放军协助下，恢复和建设人民铁路的成就，借以教育铁路员工，交流生产建设经验。他还指出，此次展览会，对于提高广大人民建设祖国的信心也会有很大作用。
    继由朱副主席致词，他说：中国铁道很重要，两年来办得很好，今后更应进一步发展与扩大，并充分发挥本身设备效能，来帮助其他工业。
    中央人民政府副主席李济深，政务院副总理董必武、黄炎培相继讲话。他们一致指出：两年来人民铁路的恢复与建设已有辉煌成绩，今后在全国铁路职工共同努力下，必能获得更大发展。
    展览会以模型、图表为主，内容主要分六部分：（一）反映铁路解放以来的修复情况，（二）人民铁路主要设备情况，（三）职工的发明创造以及学习苏联先进工作方法的实施情况，（四）职工在抗美援朝中的英勇斗争事迹，（五）职工文化福利生活的改善，（六）新建路线的情况。
    展览会预定十月五日闭幕，
