Title: Bumper Harvest of Crops in the Korean Rear Areas
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 14th: Farmers in the Korean rear areas have overcome various difficulties and diligently carried out patriotic production increase labor, resulting in a bumper harvest of crops this year. According to reports from various regions obtained by the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, the harvest volume of the third quarter crops this year has greatly exceeded the national plan. As of September 5th, millet production has increased by 19.4%; corn production has increased by 19.1%. Compared with the yield per "町步" (chōbu, approximately one hectare) in 1948, millet production has increased by 53.7%; corn production has increased by 44.9%.

Late-season crops such as sorghum and rice are also expected to have a bumper harvest. The vast number of Korean farmers, elated by the bumper harvest, are currently busy with grass cutting in preparation for the autumn harvest.

### 朝鲜后方农作物丰收

1951-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜后方农作物丰收
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜后方农民克服各种困难，辛勤地进行爱国主义增产劳动，已使本年度农作物获得丰收。据农林省所获各地报告：本年度第三季农作物收获量已大大超过国家计划。截至九月五日，谷子已增收百分之十九点四；玉蜀黍已增收百分之十九点一。和一九四八年每町步（按一町步约合一公顷）的产量相比较，谷子增产百分之五十三点七；玉蜀黍增产百分之四十四点九。
    预计晚期作物高梁、稻子等亦将丰收。因丰收而欢欣鼓舞的朝鲜广大农民现正忙于割草工作，准备秋收。
