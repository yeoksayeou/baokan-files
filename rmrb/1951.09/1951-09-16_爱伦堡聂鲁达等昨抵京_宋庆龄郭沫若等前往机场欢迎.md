Title: Ehrenburg, Neruda, and Others Arrive in Beijing Yesterday; Soong Ching-ling, Guo Moruo, and Others Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1951-09-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Ilya Ehrenburg, a member of the Soviet "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" Stalin International Prize Committee, and Soviet writer, along with Chilean writer Pablo Neruda, Ehrenburg's wife, and Neruda's wife, arrived in Beijing at 3 p.m. today. Ehrenburg and Neruda are representing the "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" Stalin International Prize Committee to present the award to Soong Ching-ling, a member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, in Beijing. They are invited by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and will stay in Beijing to attend China's National Day celebration.
    Soong Ching-ling, Guo Moruo, and Mao Dun, members of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, personally went to the airport to welcome them. Also present at the airport were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Qian Junrui, Director-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Lai Yali, Deputy Director-General; Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Qu Tangliang, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Zhou Yang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than thirty others. Fedlin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, also went to the airport to welcome them. (Attached picture)
    The picture shows Ehrenburg (first from the right) and Neruda (second from the right) arriving at Beijing Airport.

### 爱伦堡聂鲁达等昨抵京  宋庆龄郭沫若等前往机场欢迎

1951-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    爱伦堡聂鲁达等昨抵京
    宋庆龄郭沫若等前往机场欢迎
    【新华社十五日讯】苏联“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会委员、苏联作家爱伦堡、与智利作家聂鲁达、和爱伦堡夫人、聂鲁达夫人等一行，已于今日下午三时抵达北京。爱伦堡和聂鲁达是代表“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会来北京以奖金授与世界和平理事会执行局委员宋庆龄的。他们应中国人民保卫世界和平，反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若的邀请，并将留在北京参加我国国庆节庆祝大会。
    世界和平理事会执行局委员宋庆龄、郭沫若、茅盾曾亲往机场欢迎。前往机场欢迎的并有：中国人民保卫世界和平、反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞、副总干事赖亚力，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曾宪植，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会国际联络部部长区棠亮，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等三十余人。苏联大使馆参赞费德林也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
