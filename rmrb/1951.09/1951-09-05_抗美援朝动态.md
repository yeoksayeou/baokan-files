The Report-Back Work of the慰问团 Comfort Mission to Korea Has Achieved Success

The report-back work carried out by the Chinese People's慰问团 Comfort Mission to Korea in cities and counties across the country has achieved great success. As of August 20th, according to incomplete statistics from 41 cities and counties including Beijing, Tianjin, Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou, Baoding, Tangshan, Xinxiang, Gui Sui, Datong, Xuanhua, Yangyuan, Jingxing, Jiaozuo, Anyang, Jixian, Puxian, Puyang, Changyuan, Guancheng, Chaocheng, Fanxian, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhu, Wuxi, Hangzhou, Jinan, Qingdao, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Xining, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Nanning, Kaifeng, Nanchang, Chongqing, Kunming, Chengdu, Guiyang, Ya'an, and the Ulanhot City in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the leagues of Chahar and Xing'an, 32 counties in Western Sichuan, 12 counties in Southern Sichuan, and the Pearl River Special District of Guangdong, the慰问团 Comfort Mission to Korea has given more than 1,000 reports. More than 8 million people from all walks of life have directly heard the reports, and many more have indirectly heard the reports of the慰问团 Comfort Mission.

    赴朝慰问团的传达报告工作获得成绩
    中国人民赴朝慰问团在全国各市、县所进行的传达报告工作，已获得巨大成绩。截至八月二十日止，据北京、天津、太原、张家口、保定、唐山、新乡、归绥、大同、宣化、阳原、井陉、焦作、安阳、汲县、濮县、濮阳、长垣、观城、朝城、范县、上海、南京、芜湖、无锡、杭州、济南、青岛、西安、兰州、西宁、武汉、广州、南宁、开封、南昌、重庆、昆明、成都、贵阳、雅安等四十一个市县及内蒙自治区乌兰浩特市，察哈尔盟、兴安盟各旗，川西三十二个县，川南十二个县，广东珠江专区等地的不完全统计，赴朝慰问团已作了一千多次报告，直接听到报告的各阶层人民在八百万人以上，间接听到慰问团的报告的为数更多。
