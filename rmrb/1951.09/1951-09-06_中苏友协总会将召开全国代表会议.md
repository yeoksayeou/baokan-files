Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters to Convene National Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd—The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters is scheduled to convene its first National Representative Conference in Beijing, starting October 5th. The conference will summarize work experience, determine future work guidelines and tasks, discuss how to strengthen propaganda work, and will adopt a new draft of the association's charter.

    The number of delegates participating in this representative conference will be between 250 and 300. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters has notified Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations across the country to elect delegates according to the specified quotas and report to Beijing before October 3rd.

### 中苏友协总会将召开全国代表会议

1951-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会将召开全国代表会议
    【新华社二日讯】中苏友好协会总会定于十月五日起在北京召开第一次全国代表会议，总结工作经验，确定今后工作方针任务，讨论如何加强宣传工作，并将通过新的会章草案。
    参加这次代表会议的代表名额为二百五十人至三百人。中苏友好协会总会已通知全国各地中苏友好协会，依照指定名额推选代表，准于十月三日以前报到。
