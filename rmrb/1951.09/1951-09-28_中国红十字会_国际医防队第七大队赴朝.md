Title: China Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Team Seventh Detachment Departs for Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 27th. The Seventh Detachment of the China Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team departed from Tianjin on the 27th for the Korean front.

Since January of this year, the China Red Cross has successively formed six International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Teams, which have been dispatched to the Korean front in batches. Recently, in order to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, the association rapidly formed the Seventh Detachment. The captain of this detachment is Wang Changlai, a famous orthopedic expert who was the former captain of the Huai River First Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team. The members include some members of the Huai River medical and epidemic prevention service team, and medical workers who voluntarily signed up from public and private medical institutions in Guizhou, Sichuan, Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Pingyuan, Henan, Shandong, Suiyuan and other places.

Before the team's departure, the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, Tianjin medical workers and people from all walks of life held a farewell meeting.

### 中国红十字会  国际医防队第七大队赴朝

1951-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会
    国际医防队第七大队赴朝
    【新华社天津二十七日电】中国红十字会国际医防服务队第七大队，于二十七日由天津出发赴朝鲜战地。
    中国红十字总会自今年一月开始，已先后组成了六个国际医防服务队，分批赴朝鲜战地服务。最近为了加强抗美援朝运动，该会又迅速组成第七大队。该队队长由前治淮第一医防服务大队队长著名的骨科专家王昌来担任；队员中，有治淮医防服务队的部分队员，和由贵州、四川、浙江、江苏、平原、河南、山东、绥远等地公私立医疗机构志愿报名的医务工作者。
    该队出发之前，天津市人民政府与天津医务工作者及各界人士曾举行欢送会。
