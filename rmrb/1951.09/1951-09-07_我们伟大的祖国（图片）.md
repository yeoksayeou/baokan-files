    Our Great Motherland
    The pottery of Yixing, Jiangsu is a famous specialty of our country. After a year of preparation after liberation, the pottery industry in Yixing established a joint production and sales office, advocating the production of utensils needed for daily life by the masses, with self-transportation and self-sales. This is a pottery blank-making workshop in Shushan Town, Yixing. These two workers are trimming and polishing the blanks of exquisite flower pots.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江苏宜兴的陶器是我国有名的特产。解放后宜兴的陶器业经过一年的筹备，成立了产销联合营业处，提倡生产群众日常必需的器皿，自运自销。这是宜兴蜀山镇的一所陶器制坯工场。这两位工人正在修剔与磨光精致的花盆的坯子。
