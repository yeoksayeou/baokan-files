    Our Great Motherland
    The Southwest Visiting Group dispatched by the Central People's Government has arrived in the minority areas of Guishan, Yunnan. The picture shows members of the visiting group visiting a martyr's family of the A'xi ethnic group and giving them gifts.

    我们伟大的祖国
