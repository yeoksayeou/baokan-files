Title: US-Imposed Japanese Peace Treaty Violates Canadian Interests; Canadian Newspaper Demands Parliament Not Ratify It
Author:
Date: 1951-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 19th: The "Canadian Forum" solemnly requested the Canadian Parliament on the 17th: "Do not ratify the war treaty! Oppose the rearmament of Japanese militarists! Oppose the already implemented plan to rearm the German Junkers!"

The newspaper said: The US-imposed Japanese peace treaty violates Canada's interests and is a threat of new war. Japanese militarists and their masters, US imperialists, are the enemies of the people of all Asian countries. The Canadian government signed this treaty in San Francisco, and therefore bears part of the responsibility for rearming Japan and establishing a new Japanese aggressive army against China and the Soviet Union - the main goals of Japanese militarism.

The newspaper wrote: "But the gentlemen in San Francisco will not succeed. The democratic forces of the people of all Asian countries are too strong, and the unity of the Chinese and Soviet people is too close, to allow the infamous imperialist politicians and their satellite country politicians to realize their ambition of ruling the Asian continent again through their fake 'peace conference'."

### 美制对日和约违反加拿大利益   加报要求议会不予批准

1951-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    美制对日和约违反加拿大利益
    加报要求议会不予批准
    【新华社布拉格十九日电】“加拿大论坛报”于十七日向加拿大议会提出庄严的要求：“不要批准战争条约。反对重新武装日本军国主义者！反对已经在实行的重新武装德国容克家族的计划！”
    该报说：美制对日和约违反加拿大的利益，它是新战争的威胁。日本军国主义者及其主子美帝国主义者是亚洲各国人民的敌人。加拿大政府在旧金山签订了这个条约，因此对重新武装日本、对建立反对中国反对苏联——日本军国主义的主要目标——的日本新侵略军队，负有一部分责任。
    该报写道：“但是旧金山的先生们是不会成功的。亚洲各国人民的民主力量太强大了，中国人民和苏联人民的团结太紧密了，不允许声名狼籍的帝国主义政客及其卫星国政客的假‘和会’，来实现他们再度统治亚洲大陆的野心。”
