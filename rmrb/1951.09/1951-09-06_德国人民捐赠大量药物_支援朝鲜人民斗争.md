Title: The German People Donate Large Quantities of Medicine to Support the Korean People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 3

    German People Donate Large Quantities of Medicine
    To Support the Korean People’s Struggle [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th] According to a report from the German News Agency, the working people of the German Democratic Republic have shipped large quantities of medicine and textiles to support the heroic Korean people who are resisting American aggression. The items sent to Korea include: 1,893 boxes and 30 barrels of medicine, 801 pieces of textiles, clothing and shoes, 154 boxes of various donated items, and 2 ambulances equipped with the latest equipment. Since launching the aid-Korea movement in September 1950, the German people have donated a total of 8 million marks. This movement is still continuing. Workers are continuing to work overtime to support the fighting Korean people.

### 德国人民捐赠大量药物  支援朝鲜人民斗争

1951-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    德国人民捐赠大量药物
    支援朝鲜人民斗争 【新华社五日讯】据电通社柏林三日讯：德意志民主共和国的劳动人民已运出大量药品与纺织品支援英勇抗击美国侵略者的朝鲜人民。运往朝鲜的物品包括：一千八百九十三箱和三十桶药品，八百零一件纺织品、衣服和鞋袜、一百五十四箱的各种捐赠品及二辆有最新设备的救护车。德国人民自一九五零年九月展开援朝运动以来，总共已捐献了八百万马克。这个运动现在还在继续中。工人们正继续加班工作，支援战斗的朝鲜人民。
