Title: Government Administration Council Held Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st -- The Central People's Government Administration Council held its 100th meeting yesterday afternoon. The meeting listened to and approved the work report for the first half of 1951 made by Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government. The meeting also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, as well as motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院昨举行会议

1951-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院昨举行会议
   【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百次政务会议于三十一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长所作的一九五一年上半年工作报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
