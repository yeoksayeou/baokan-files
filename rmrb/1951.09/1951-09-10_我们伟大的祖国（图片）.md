    Our Great Motherland
    Strengthening engineering design is a necessary condition for ensuring national capital construction. The Zhejiang Linen Textile Factory plans to expand the factory site, increase spindles, increase looms, and increase workers within two years. This is the factory director Zhai Xiwu (left) and chief engineer Zhang Jishen studying the architectural engineering design of the factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    加强工程设计是保证国家基本建设的必要条件。浙江麻纺织厂计划在二年内扩大厂地，增加纱锭，增加布机，并增加工人。这是该厂厂长翟翕武（左）和总工程师张继善正在研究工厂的建筑工程设计。
