Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-09-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Silkworm raising is a major sideline for farmers in Zhejiang. In the past, due to the massive dumping of American silk fabrics and the oppression and exploitation by the Kuomintang, the production and sale of silkworm cocoons suffered greatly. Now, with farmers' own cooperatives, the production and sale of silkworm cocoons are becoming increasingly prosperous. This is Hong Yongquan, a member of the silkworm breeding group of the cooperative, whose family is busy preparing to send the silkworm cocoons to the cooperative for sale.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    养蚕是浙江农民的一项主要副业。过去由于美国丝织品的大量倾销和国民党的压迫剥削，以致蚕茧的产销都受到了打击。现在有了农民自己的合作社，蚕茧的产销日益畅旺了。这是参加合作社育蚕小组的洪永泉，他的一家人都正忙着准备把蚕茧送到合作社去出售。
