Title: British Communist Party Political Committee Member Allison Passes Away, CCP Central Committee Sends Condolences
Author:
Date: 1951-09-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 13th: George Allison, member of the Political Committee of the British Communist Party, Minister of the Industrial Department of the British Communist Party, and outstanding fighter of the British working class, passed away in London on the 11th due to illness.
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, expressing condolences on the death of George Allison, member of the Political Committee of the British Communist Party. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain:
    We extend our deepest condolences on the passing of George Allison, an outstanding fighter of the British working class and member of the Political Committee of the British Communist Party. Comrade Allison's spirit of fighting for the liberation of the British working class throughout his life will be immortal.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    September 13, 1951

### 英共政治委员会委员艾利逊逝世  中共中央致电吊唁

1951-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    英共政治委员会委员艾利逊逝世
    中共中央致电吊唁
    【新华社十三日讯】伦敦消息：英国共产党政治委员会委员兼英共工业部部长、英国工人阶级卓越的战士乔治·艾利逊于十一日在伦敦因病逝世。
    【新华社十三日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电英国共产党执行委员会，吊唁英共政治委员会委员艾利逊逝世。电文如下：英国共产党执行委员会：
    谨对英国工人阶级的卓越战士、英国共产党政治委员会委员乔治·艾利逊的逝世，致以深深的哀悼。艾利逊同志为英国工人阶级解放事业奋斗一生的精神永垂不朽。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五一年九月十三日
