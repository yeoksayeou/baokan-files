Title: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters to Hold Important Broadcast Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: To coincide with the second anniversary of its founding and the upcoming First National Congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters will hold important broadcasts on the Central People's Broadcasting Station from 20:30 to 21:00 on September 21st and from 20:30 to 21:00 on September 28th. The topics include "Several Questions on the Understanding of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association" by Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Headquarters, and "The Significance of Participating in the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association" by Zeng Ping, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The Association has notified Sino-Soviet Friendship Association organizations across the country to organize its members and non-member masses to listen, study conscientiously, and report on the listening effect afterwards.

### 中苏友好协会总会明天举行重要广播

1951-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会明天举行重要广播
    【新华社十九日讯】中苏友好协会总会为了配合该会成立二周年和即将召开的中苏友好协会第一次全国代表大会，定于九月二十一日二十时三十分至二十一时和二十八日二十时三十分至二十一时在中央人民广播电台举行重要广播。讲题有总会副总干事赵仲池的“对中苏友好协会认识上的几个问题”和北京市中苏友好协会副总干事曾平的“参加中苏友好协会的意义”。该会已通知全国各地中苏友好协会组织广大会员与非会员群众收听，认真学习，并于事后汇报收听效果。
