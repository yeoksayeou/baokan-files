Title: People of Cairo Hold a Mass Meeting Demanding British Troops Withdraw from Egypt
Author:
Date: 1951-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd -- According to a Moscow broadcast on the 17th, the people of Cairo, the capital of Egypt, held a mass meeting of 5,000 people in the city on the 69th anniversary of the British occupation of Cairo (in 1882).

The participants expressed their indignation at the British occupation of Egyptian territory and expressed their determination to fight to ensure the withdrawal of British troops from the Suez Canal Zone.

The conference passed a resolution condemning the government's policy of delaying the abolition of the Anglo-Egyptian Treaty signed in 1936, protesting against attempts to restrict various social freedoms through legislation, and firmly advocating the release of political prisoners.

### 开罗人民举行大会  要求英军撤出埃及

1951-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    开罗人民举行大会
    要求英军撤出埃及
    【新华社二十二日讯】据莫斯科十七日广播开罗消息：埃及首都开罗人民在英国占领开罗（在一八八二年）六十九年之际，在该城举行了五千人的群众大会。
    与会者对英国占领埃及领土表示愤慨，并表示决心为使英军从苏彝士运河区撤退而斗争。
    大会通过决议，谴责政府拖延废止一九三六年签订的英埃条约的政策，抗议通过立法来限制各种社会自由的企图，并坚决主张释放政治犯。
