Title: The Bombay India-China Friendship Association Holds "Glimpses of New China Exhibition"
Author:
Date: 1951-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th. The "Glimpses of New China Exhibition" held by the India-China Friendship Association in Bombay will be held from the 12th to the 14th in the Great Hall of the University of Bombay.

　　The exhibition features 150 large photographs depicting the thriving new life in China today. It shows the selfless and enthusiastic work of the Chinese people in building a new China, as well as the participation of women in the construction of the new China.

　　In addition to the photographs, the exhibition also includes propaganda paintings by Chinese artists depicting the Chinese people's struggle for peace and their engagement in peaceful construction.

### 孟买印中友协举办“新中国一瞥展览会”

1951-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    孟买印中友协举办“新中国一瞥展览会”
    【新华社新德里十二日电】印度孟买印中友好协会举办的“新中国一瞥展览会”，从十二日到十四日在孟买大学大礼堂举行。
    展览会有一百五十幅描写今日中国欣欣向荣的新生活的大幅照片。它显示中国人民为了建设新中国而忘我地热情地工作的情景，以及新中国的妇女们参加国家建设的情形。
    展览会除了照片外，还有中国艺术家所画的宣传画，描写中国人民争取和平的斗争与从事和平建设的情形。
