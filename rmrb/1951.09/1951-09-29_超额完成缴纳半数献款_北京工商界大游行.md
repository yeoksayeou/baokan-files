Title: Beijing Business Community Grand Parade Overfulfills Half of Donation Quota
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Beijing business community pre-emptively overfulfilled its quota of paying half of its donation by the 28th. Since the movement to complete half of the donation before National Day was initiated at the broadcast rally on the 18th of this month, as of today, the city's business community has donated a total of 31,257,510,000+ yuan to the People's Bank of China, exceeding half of the total donation amount. To this end, the Beijing business community held a "Grand Parade to Welcome National Day, Increase Production, and Donate Collectively to the National Treasury" this afternoon, and reported the good news to the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association and the Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen. When the parade of 16,000 people, led by the chief leader and Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, Fu Huating, set off from Tiananmen Square and arrived in front of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Secretary-General of the Municipal Government, Xue Zizheng, and others came to greet them. Xue Zizheng accepted the letter of congratulations from the Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce on behalf of the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association and Mayor Peng Zhen. The parade shouted slogans such as "Celebrate National Day," "Strengthen the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Movement," and "Guarantee the Early and Overfulfillment of the Entire Donation Plan," which were welcomed by the masses along the way. The parade ended at 5 p.m.

### 超额完成缴纳半数献款  北京工商界大游行

1951-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    超额完成缴纳半数献款
    北京工商界大游行
    【新华社二十八日讯】北京市工商界二十八日提前超额完成缴纳半数献款。自本月十八日在广播大会上发起国庆节前完成缴纳献款半数的运动以来，截至今日止，全市工商界向中国人民银行缴纳的献款共计三百一十二亿五千七百五十一万余元，达全部捐献额的半数以上。为此，北京市工商界特于今日下午举行“迎接国庆增产捐献集体纳库大游行”，并向中国人民抗美援朝总会、北京市人民政府彭真市长报喜。当一万六千人的游行队伍、由总领队、北京市工商界联合会主任委员傅华亭等率领，自天安门广场出发，行至北京市人民政府门前时，市政府秘书长薛子正等前来迎接。薛子正代表抗美援朝总会和彭真市长接受了市工商联合会的报喜信。游行队伍高呼：“庆祝国庆”、“加强抗美援朝运动”、“保证提前超额完成全部捐献计划”等口号，受到沿途群众的欢迎。游行至下午五时结束。
