Title: Promoting Cultural Exchange Between China and Germany: German Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-09-27
Page: 1

    Promoting Cultural Exchange Between China and Germany
    German Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 26th] In order to promote cultural exchange between China and Germany, the government of the German Democratic Republic, with the consent of our Central People's Government, has dispatched a cultural delegation to China to negotiate a cultural agreement, and has designated Mr. Koenig, the Ambassador-level head of the diplomatic mission of that country to China, as Plenipotentiary Representative. Members of the delegation, including Mrs. Rentmeister and two others, arrived in Beijing by the same plane as the People's Observation Delegation from that country at 5:00 PM on the 26th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of our Central People's Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Culture and Education, Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director, and Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Mr. Koenig, the Ambassador-level head of the German diplomatic mission to China, and others also went to welcome them.

### 促进中德两国文化交流  德文化代表团抵京

1951-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    促进中德两国文化交流
    德文化代表团抵京
    【新华社二十六日讯】为了促进中德两国文化交流，德意志民主共和国政府于征得我中央人民政府同意后，特派遣文化代表团来华商订文化协定，并指定该国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希为全权代表。该代表团团员伦特迈斯特夫人等三人已于二十六日下午五时与该国人民观礼代表团同机抵京。前往机场欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部苏欧司徐以新副司长，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局洪深局长、陈忠经副局长，外交部交际处韩叙科长等。德国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希等也前往欢迎。
