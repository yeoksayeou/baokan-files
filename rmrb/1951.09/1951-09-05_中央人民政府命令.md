    Order of the Central People's Government

    The "Provisional Organic Regulations of the People's Courts of the People's Republic of China," the "Provisional Organic Regulations of the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the Central People's Government," and the "General Rules for the Organization of People's Procuratorates at All Levels" have been passed by the 12th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee and are hereby promulgated for implementation.

    Chairman Mao Zedong
       September 4, 1951

### 中央人民政府命令

1951-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府命令
    中华人民共和国人民法院暂行组织条例及中央人民政府最高人民检察署暂行组织条例、各级地方人民检察署组织通则，业经中央人民政府委员会第十二次会议通过，特令公布施行。
    主席  毛泽东
       一九五一年九月四日
