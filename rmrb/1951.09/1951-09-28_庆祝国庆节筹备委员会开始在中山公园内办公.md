Title: Preparatory Committee for National Day Celebrations Begins Working in Zhongshan Park
Author:
Date: 1951-09-28
Page: 1
    
    Preparatory Committee for National Day Celebrations Begins Working in Zhongshan Park
    [Our Newspaper] The preparatory committee for the National Day celebrations of the People's Republic of China has begun working in Zhongshan Park. The telephone number for the General Assembly Secretariat is 3rd Bureau 1414, and the telephone number for the Command Headquarters is 3rd Bureau 1818.

### 庆祝国庆节筹备委员会开始在中山公园内办公

1951-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝国庆节筹备委员会开始在中山公园内办公
    【本报讯】庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节筹备委员会已在中山公园内开始办公，大会秘书处电话为三局一四一四，指挥部电话为三局一八一八。
