Title: Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army: General Lin Biao and others lead the entire army to send a telegram to Chairman Mao congratulating the National Day

Author:
Date: 1951-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 28th. Commander Lin Biao, Political Commissars Luo Ronghuan, Deng Zihui, and Tan Zheng of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army, led all commanders and fighters in sending a telegram to Chairman Mao, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government:

On the occasion of our National Day – the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China – we extend our warm congratulations to you and the Central People's Government with boundless excitement and sincere respect.

In the past two years, the heroic and hardworking Chinese people, under your wise leadership and that of the Central People's Government, have achieved brilliant accomplishments in political, economic, cultural, and national defense construction, and the people of the whole country have been united as never before. Through the movements of resisting America and aiding Korea, land reform, and suppressing counter-revolutionaries, the patriotic enthusiasm of the people has been further heightened, the movement to increase production in industry and agriculture has been widely launched, and the national defense of the new China has been further consolidated. The great achievements of the past two years prove that the strength of our people is great, that our nation is a very outstanding nation, and that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, we are fully capable of building our country into a prosperous and strong nation.

When we, the commanders and fighters of the entire army, are warmly celebrating our National Day, we vow to use all our determination to consolidate the victories our people have achieved, defend the motherland, defend peace in the Far East and the world, and be ready at any time to fight the enemies who dare to invade our sacred territory at your command, and strive a hundred times harder to improve ourselves and build a stronger national defense force.

Respectfully,

Lin Biao, Commander of the Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army

Luo Ronghuan, Deng Zihui, Tan Zheng, Political Commissars, and All Commanders and Fighters

### 中南军区暨第四野战军  林彪将军等率领全军电毛主席祝贺国庆

1951-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中南军区暨第四野战军
    林彪将军等率领全军电毛主席祝贺国庆
    【新华社汉口二十八日电】中南军区暨第四野战军司令员林彪，政治委员罗荣桓、邓子恢、谭政率领全体指挥员、战斗员致电毛主席，祝贺中华人民共和国成立两周年的国庆节。该电全文如下：中央人民政府毛主席：
    兹值我国国庆佳节——中华人民共和国成立两周年之际，谨以无限兴奋的心情和诚挚的敬意，向您和中央人民政府致热烈的祝贺。
    两年来，英勇勤劳的中国人民，在您和中央人民政府的英明领导下，在政治、经济、文化和国防建设方面，都获得了辉煌的成就，全国人民业已空前地团结起来。经过抗美援朝、土地改革和镇压反革命等项运动，人民的爱国热情更形高涨，工农业增产运动已普遍展开，而新中国的国防则益臻巩固。两年来的巨大成就，证明了我国人民的力量是伟大的，我们这个民族是一个非常优秀的民族，在中国共产党领导之下，我们完全能够把我们的国家建设成为一个繁荣富强的国家。
    我们全军指战员，在热烈欢庆我国国庆的时候，誓以一切决心为巩固我国人民已得的胜利，保卫祖国，保卫远东与世界和平，随时准备在您的号令下，和敢于侵犯我国神圣疆土的敌人作战，并以百倍努力，提高自己，为建设更强大的国防军而奋斗。
    中南军区、第四野战军司令员  林彪
            政治委员  罗荣桓  邓子恢  谭政暨全体指战员敬贺
