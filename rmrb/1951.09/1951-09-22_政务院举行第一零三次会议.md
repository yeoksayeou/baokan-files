Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 103rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-09-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st—The Central People's Government Administration Council held its 103rd meeting on the afternoon of the 21st. After hearing explanations from Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council, and Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, the meeting approved the "Instructions on Inspecting the Implementation of the Marriage Law." The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals, as well as proposed appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一零三次会议

1951-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一零三次会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一零三次政务会议于二十一日下午举行。会议在听取了政务院黄炎培副总理和最高人民法院张志让副院长的说明后，通过了关于检查婚姻法执行情况的指示。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
