Title: Typhoon to Affect Taiwan Strait Area
Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on August 31st: At 2:00 PM on August 31st, the center of the typhoon was located in the ocean 600 li east of the Philippine Islands (i.e., 17.3 degrees north latitude and 125 degrees east longitude), moving in a northwest-westerly direction at a speed of 36 li per hour. The maximum wind speed near the center was 300 li per hour, and at a distance of 400 li from the center, the wind speed was 180 li per hour. In the next 24 hours, it will strike 50 li directly south of Taiwan. The Taiwan Strait area will be affected by it.

### 台湾海峡一带将受台风影响

1951-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    台湾海峡一带将受台风影响
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央气象台八月三十一日下午八时发出台风警报：八月三十一日下午二时，台风中心的位置在菲律宾群岛东六百华里的海洋中（即北纬十七·三度东经一二五度）向西北西方向进行，每小时移动三十六华里，中心附近最大风速每小时三百华里，离开中心四百华里的地方，风速是每小时一百八十华里。未来二十四小时内，将要袭击台湾正南五十华里处。台湾海峡一带将要受到它的影响。
