Title: Viet People's Army Operates in South Vietnam, Eliminating Eight French Military Strongholds
Author:
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, South Vietnam, 16th: According to Vietnam News Agency's South Vietnam dispatch of the 15th, in the first half of August, the South Vietnamese People's Army local forces eliminated eight enemy military strongholds and sank six French landing craft. Six of these strongholds were in Cai Be and Cai Lay districts of My Tho province, and the other two were in Long Xuyen province. In these battles, the People's Army annihilated all enemy troops in each stronghold. The largest battle took place in Na Xa, where two companies of European and African troops and puppet troops defending this position were completely annihilated. The People's Army local forces used rocket launchers and anti-tank guns when sinking enemy landing craft.

### 越人民军在南越作战  拔除法军据点八处

1951-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越人民军在南越作战
    拔除法军据点八处
    【新华社十六日讯】据越南通讯社南越十五日讯：南越人民军地方部队于八月上半月拔除敌军据点八处，并击沉法军登陆艇六艘。这些据点有六处是在美荻省的丐跛与丐礼县，其他两处在龙州省。在这些战役中，人民军歼灭了各据点所有的敌军。最大的一次战斗发生在那沙，防守这个阵地里的欧洲非洲军队与伪军两连，全部被歼灭。人民军地方部队击沉敌军登陆艇时，曾使用火箭炮与反坦克炮。
