Title: Commemorating the Sixth Anniversary of the Victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan: Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. The telegram reads as follows:

Marshal Stalin:

On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan, I extend my warm congratulations and heartfelt thanks to you, the Soviet Armed Forces, and the Soviet people on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the entire Chinese people. The great assistance given to the Chinese people by the Soviet Union in the War of Resistance Against Japan, and the solid alliance between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China to jointly prevent the resurgence of Japanese aggression forces, have given the Chinese people infinite encouragement in the struggle against aggression forces in the Far East. Long live the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union in the just cause of opposing Japanese imperialism and safeguarding peace in the Far East!

Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, September 2, 1951

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, replied to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China with the following telegram:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Comrade Chairman, thank you for your high evaluation of the Soviet Union and its army's role in defeating the Japanese aggressor forces. The Chinese people and their Liberation Army, despite the conspiracies of the Kuomintang, played a great role in eliminating Japanese imperialists. The struggle of the Chinese people and their Liberation Army greatly facilitated the task of defeating Japanese aggressor forces. Undoubtedly, the indestructible friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China is now and will be in the future for the purpose of ensuring peace in the Far East and opposing all aggressors and warmongers. Comrade Chairman, please accept the congratulations of the Soviet Union and its army on the sixth anniversary of the liberation from the enslavement of Japanese imperialism in East Asia.

Long live the great friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!

Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, J. Stalin, 9:22 AM, September 2, 1951

### 纪念抗日战争胜利六周年  毛主席斯大林大元帅互电致贺

1951-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念抗日战争胜利六周年
    毛主席斯大林大元帅互电致贺
    【新华社二日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席于抗日战争胜利日六周年之际，致电苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅，电文如下：斯大林大元帅：
    当此抗日战争胜利日六周年之际，谨代表中国人民解放军和全中国人民向你和苏联武装部队、苏联人民致热烈的祝贺和深切的感谢。苏联在抗日战争中给予中国人民的巨大援助，以及苏联和中华人民共和国关于共同防止日本侵略势力再起的巩固同盟，使中国人民在反对远东侵略势力的斗争中，获得无限鼓舞。中苏两国在反对日本帝国主义和维护远东和平的正义事业中的伟大友谊万岁！
   中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东  一九五一年九月二日
    【新华社二日讯】苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅复电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，电文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    主席同志，感谢您对苏联及其军队在击溃日本侵略力量事业中的作用之崇高的估价。中国人民及其解放军不顾国民党的阴谋，在消灭日本帝国主义者的事业中起了巨大的作用。中国人民及其解放军的斗争，大大地便利了击溃日本侵略力量的事业。无庸置疑，苏联和中华人民共和国的不可摧毁的友谊，在现在和将来都是为着保证远东和平，反对一切侵略者及战争贩子的事业的。主席同志，请您接受苏联及其军队对东亚从日本帝国主义奴役下获得解放的六周年的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的伟大友谊万岁！
    中国人民解放军万岁！苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席约·斯大林一九五一年九月二日九时二十二分
