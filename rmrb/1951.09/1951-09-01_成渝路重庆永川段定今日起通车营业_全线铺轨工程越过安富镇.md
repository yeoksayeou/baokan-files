Title: The Chongqing-Yongchuan Section of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway Scheduled to Open to Traffic Today; Track-laying Project on the Entire Line Surpasses Anfu Town

Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, August 31st. The Chongqing to Yongchuan section of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway will open to traffic on September 1st. This section is 166 kilometers long, passing through 19 stations. One mixed passenger and freight train will operate in each direction daily. The announcement of the opening has greatly excited people from all walks of life in Sichuan. Farmers along the line believe that this event fully demonstrates Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government's care for the people of Southwest China. Industrial and commercial sectors in Chongqing, Yongchuan, Jiangjin, and other places are planning to transport goods via the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway to reduce freight costs. Currently, the track-laying project for the entire Chengdu-Chongqing Railway line has surpassed Anfu Town in Rongchang and is about to reach Longchang.

### 成渝路重庆永川段定今日起通车营业   全线铺轨工程越过安富镇

1951-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    成渝路重庆永川段定今日起通车营业
    全线铺轨工程越过安富镇
   【新华社重庆三十一日电】成渝铁路重庆至永川段自九月一日起通车营业。该段全长共一百六十六公里，沿途共经十九站，每日对开混合客货列车一班。通车营业消息公布后，四川各界人民均极兴奋。沿线农民认为这件事充分证明毛主席和中央人民政府对西南人民的关怀。重庆、永川、江津等地工商行业已计划将货物由成渝铁路运输，以减轻运费。现成渝铁路全线铺轨工程已越过荣昌安富镇，即将达到隆昌。
