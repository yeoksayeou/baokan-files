Title: Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-09-26
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, September 25th—The Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, consisting of over twenty people, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Peng Zemin, member of the Central People's Government and member of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Li Renren, Liao Chengzhi, and Zhuang Xiquan, deputy directors of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhang Youyu, deputy mayor of Beijing; as well as Wang Shao'ao, Yu Xinqing, Qian Jiaju and other representatives of government organs and democratic parties, and more than four hundred representatives of groups such as the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association and the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce.
    The Indonesian Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, which arrived in Beijing the day before yesterday, also went to welcome them.
    After the visiting group got off the train, they received flowers presented by representatives of Beijing returned overseas Chinese students amidst warm applause. A brief welcome meeting was then held at the station.
    Peng Zemin delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, extending a warm welcome to the visiting group. Yang Laoqing, the head of the visiting group, delivered a reply, saying: Overseas Chinese have always looked forward to a peaceful and strong motherland, and now this wish has finally been realized. Overseas Chinese truly feel the warmth and glory of the motherland. He expressed that the visiting group would strive to learn from the compatriots of the motherland.
    The visiting group arrived in Guangzhou on September 20. In Guangzhou, the group was warmly welcomed by the Guangdong Provincial People's Government and people from all walks of life.

### 缅甸华侨观光团抵京

1951-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨观光团抵京
    【新华社二十五日讯】缅甸华侨归国观光团一行二十余人于今日上午由广州乘车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府委员兼华侨事务委员会委员彭泽民，中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员李任仁、廖承志、庄希泉，北京市副市长张友渔，以及王绍鏊、余心清、千家驹等政府机关和民主党派代表，北京归国华侨联谊会、北京市工商联合会等团体代表四百余人。
    前天抵京的印尼华侨归国观光团亦全体前往欢迎。
    观光团下车后，在热烈的掌声中接受北京归国华侨学生代表的献花。接着在车站举行了简短的欢迎会。
    彭泽民代表中央人民政府华侨事务委员会致欢迎词，对观光团表示热烈的欢迎。观光团团长杨老清致答词说：海外侨胞一直期望着有一个和平和强盛的祖国，现在这个愿望终于实现了。海外华侨真正感受到祖国的温暖和光荣。他表示观光团将努力向祖国同胞学习。
    观光团是在九月二十日抵达广州的。在广州时，该团曾受到广东省人民政府及各界人民的热烈欢迎。
