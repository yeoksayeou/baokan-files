Title: Generals Kim Il-sung and Peng Dehuai's Reply to Ridgeway's Letter Prominently Published in Full by Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1951-09-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th. All Soviet newspapers on the 13th prominently published the full text of the reply from Generals Kim Il-sung and Peng Dehuai to Ridgeway on the 11th, and the statement from the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Negotiation Delegation on the 10th.

### 金日成彭德怀两将军复李奇微信件  苏联各报全文显著刊载

1951-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成彭德怀两将军复李奇微信件
    苏联各报全文显著刊载
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】苏联各报十三日均以显著地位登载金日成、彭德怀两将军十一日致李奇微的复信的全文和朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军谈判代表团十日的声明。
