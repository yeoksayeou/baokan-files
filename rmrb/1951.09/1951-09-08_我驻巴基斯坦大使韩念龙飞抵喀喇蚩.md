Title: My Ambassador to Pakistan, Han Nianlong, Arrives in Karachi
Author:
Date: 1951-09-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 4th. Han Nianlong, the first ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, arrived in Karachi on the 2nd. Those who greeted him at the airport included: General Rochar, the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Mr. Mostafa, Director of the Protocol Department of the Pakistani Foreign Ministry, diplomatic officials from the Soviet and Czechoslovak embassies in Pakistan, and the Polish trade representative, Zheng Weizhi, the chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, and many Chinese residents in Karachi.
    Ambassador Han made a brief statement at the airport, saying, "As the first ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, I will do my best to develop and promote friendly and cooperative relations between the peoples of the two countries."
    Ambassador Han finally wished the people of China and Pakistan new victories in their efforts to strive for peace and prosperity in Asia and the world.

### 我驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙飞抵喀喇蚩

1951-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙飞抵喀喇蚩
    【新华社新德里四日电】中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦首任大使韩念龙于二日到达喀喇蚩。到机场欢迎的有：巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查将军、巴基斯坦外交部交际司司长莫斯塔化先生，苏联、捷克斯洛伐克驻巴外交官员和波兰贸易代表，中国驻巴大使馆代办郑为之和喀喇蚩的许多中国侨民。
    韩大使在机场发表简短谈话称：“作为中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦首任大使，我将尽力发展与促进两国人民之间的友好合作关系。”
    韩大使最后预祝中、巴两国人民在争取亚洲与世界的和平与繁荣的努力中，获得新的胜利。
