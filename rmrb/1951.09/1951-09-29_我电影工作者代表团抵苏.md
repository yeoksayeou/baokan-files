Title: Our Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1951-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th - The Chinese film workers delegation of twelve, led by Yu Ling, arrived here on the evening of the 26th by plane at the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Cinema to participate in the Chinese Film Week. They were greeted at the airport by Semyonov, Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Cinema, Moskovsky, Deputy Manager of the Soviet Film Export Company, directors Varlamov, Gerasimov, Yutkevich, actress Makarova, and dozens of others. Ge Baoquan, Counselor of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, also went to welcome them. The Chinese Film Week is scheduled to be held simultaneously in the capitals of all sixteen Soviet republics and in fourteen major industrial cities such as Leningrad and Stalingrad from October 1st to 10th. "The White Haired Girl" and "The Great Unity of Chinese Nationalities" will be screened as the main films during the film week.

### 我电影工作者代表团抵苏

1951-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我电影工作者代表团抵苏
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】应苏联电影事业部邀请赴苏参加中国电影周的中国电影工作者代表团一行十二人，在团长于伶率领下已于二十六日晚飞抵此间。前往机场欢迎的有苏联电影事业部副部长塞萌诺夫，苏联电影输出公司副经理莫斯柯夫斯基，导演瓦尔拉莫夫、格拉西莫夫、尤特凯维奇，演员玛卡罗娃等数十人，我驻苏大使馆参赞戈宝权也往欢迎。中国电影周定于十月一日至十日同时在全苏十六个加盟共和国首都和列宁格勒、斯大林格勒等十四处大工业城市举行。“白毛女”及“中国民族大团结”两片将作为电影周中主片放演。
