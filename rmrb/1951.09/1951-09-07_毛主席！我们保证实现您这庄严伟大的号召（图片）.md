Title: Chairman Mao! We guarantee to realize your solemn and great call (Sketches of Harnessing the Huai River, No. 6)
Author: Deng Shu, Gu Qun
Date: 1951-09-07
Page: 3

The Central Huai River Inspection Group brought a banner with Chairman Mao's own handwriting, "We must repair the Huai River well," to all comrades participating in the Huai River harnessing work. The laborers excitedly expressed that they would answer Chairman Mao by completing the glorious task of harnessing the Huai River.
