Title: Oppose the Bloody Crimes of American Imperialism in Disrupting the Korean Armistice Negotiations!
Author: Cai Shangxiong Qian Sijie
Date: 1951-09-04
Page: 4

    Oppose the Bloody Crimes of American Imperialism in Disrupting the Korean Armistice Negotiations!
    General Li Sang-jo, representative of the Korean People's Army, speaks to the soldiers of Yao Qingxiang's platoon in front of the martyr's remains, denouncing the crimes of the American and Syngman Rhee bandit troops in destroying the neutral zone.
    General Deng Hua, representative of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, speaks at the memorial service held by people from all walks of life in Kaesong for Martyr Yao Qingxiang.
    Generals Li Sang-jo and Xie Fang visit Comrade Wang Renyuan, a patrolman of our side who was injured in an attack by the American and Syngman Rhee bandit troops who invaded the Kaesong neutral zone, at a front-line hospital.
    At 22:20 on August 22, American aircraft invaded the airspace over the Kaesong conference site, bombing and strafing near the residences of our delegation. The picture shows liaison officers from both sides investigating the fourth bomb crater at the scene.
    Photos by Cai Shangxiong and Qian Sijie, accompanying photographers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

1951-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    反对美帝国主义破坏朝鲜停战谈判的血腥罪行！
    朝鲜人民军代表李相朝将军，在姚庆祥排长遗体前，向姚烈士生前所在连队的战士讲话，痛斥美李匪军破坏中立区的罪行。
    中国人民志愿军代表邓华将军在开城各界追悼姚庆祥烈士大会上讲话。
    李相朝、解方两将军在前线某医疗所慰问被侵入开城中立区的美李匪军袭击受伤的我方巡逻队员王仁元同志。
