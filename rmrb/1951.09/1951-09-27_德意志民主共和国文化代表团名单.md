Title: List of the Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1951-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The list and resumes of the representatives of the Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic are as follows:

    Plenipotentiary Representative: Koenig, current head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, with the rank of Ambassador.

    Member: Mrs. Maria Rentmeister, current member of the State Art Affairs Committee of the German Democratic Republic.

    Robert Havemann, current professor at Humboldt University in Berlin, Chairman of the Greater Berlin Peace Committee.

    Gurt Maetzig, director of the German Film Studio, winner of the National Prize, academician of the German Academy of Arts.

### 德意志民主共和国文化代表团名单

1951-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国文化代表团名单
   【新华社二十六日讯】德意志民主共和国文化代表团代表名单及简历如下：
    全权代表：柯尼希，现任德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长。
    团员：马利亚·伦特迈斯特夫人，现任德意志民主共和国国家艺术事业委员会委员。
    罗伯特·哈福曼，现任柏林洪伯特大学教授，大柏林和平委员会主席。
    古尔特·麦契希，德国影片厂导演，国家奖金获得者，德意志艺术院院士。
