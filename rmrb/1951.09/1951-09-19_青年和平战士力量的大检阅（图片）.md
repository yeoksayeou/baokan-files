Title: A Grand Review of the Strength of Young Peace Warriors (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1951-09-19
Page: 5

The opening ceremony of the Third World Youth and Students for Peace Festival took place on August 5th in the Ulbricht Square in Berlin. Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese delegation, addressed the assembly.

Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese delegation, is with children at the children's summer camp. The Chinese delegation received enthusiastic cheers from youth representatives in the stands as they entered the assembly venue. (From right to left: Wu Han, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, Feng Wenbin, head of the delegation, Rong Gaotang, deputy head of the delegation)

The Chinese youth delegation received a warm welcome from the German people everywhere. The picture shows welcomers at the Frankfurt train station in Germany warmly shaking hands with Chinese representatives.

The Free German Youth League presents a gift to head of delegation Feng Wenbin.

Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic (third from the right) visits the Chinese photo exhibition.

Combat heroes Guo Zhongtian and Zhou Wenjiang of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army accept ambulances donated to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army by a German automobile factory.

Youth representatives of the Chinese Tibetan nationality visit West German youth injured by the U.S. occupation forces in the hospital.

Wang Jingbin, a model telephone operator of the Anshan Railway Bureau and a representative of Chinese young workers, visits a young West German female worker injured by the U.S. occupation forces in the hospital.

Members of the Soviet Young Pioneers present badges to members of the Chinese Young Pioneers.

Chinese and German Young Pioneers at the assembly venue.

Combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army lay wreaths at the Soviet Red Army Martyrs Memorial in Berlin.

The Chinese Youth Art Troupe performs the song "People of the World Are of One Mind." This song won the second prize in the song competition of the Third World Youth and Students for Peace Festival.

The Chinese Youth Art Troupe's acrobatic team performs vehicle stunts at the assembly.

The Chinese Youth Art Troupe performs the song-and-dance report "Advancing Shoulder to Shoulder." (Photo by He Jiang)

When China held a friendship gathering to receive youth representatives from New Democratic countries, Zhou Wenjiang, a combat hero of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, was lifted up amidst cheers by youth from various countries.

Photography: Jia Huamin

### 青年和平战士力量的大检阅（图片）

1951-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    青年和平战士力量的大检阅
    八月五日第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节在柏林乌布利希广场开幕时，我国代表团团长冯文彬在大会上讲话。
    我国代表团团长冯文彬在儿童夏令营和儿童在一起。我国代表团进入大会会场时，受到看台上的各国青年代表的热烈欢呼。（自右至左：我国代表团副团长吴晗，团长冯文彬，副团长荣高棠）
    中国青年代表团到处受到德国人民的热烈欢迎。图为在德国法兰克福车站的欢迎者和中国代表们热烈握手的情形。
    自由德国青年联盟向冯文彬团长献礼。
    德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥（右起第三人）参观我国图片展览会。
    志愿军战斗英雄郭忠田、周文江接受德国汽车工厂献给中国人民志愿军的救护车。
    我国藏族青年代表到医院慰问被美国占领军打伤的西德青年。
    中国青年工人劳动模范代表安东铁路局电话交换手王静彬到医院慰问被美国占领军打伤的西德青年女工人。
    苏联少年先锋队队员将纪念章赠给我国少年儿童队员。
    在大会会场中的中德两国的少年儿童队员。
    我国人民解放军和志愿军战斗英雄向柏林苏联红军烈士纪念塔献花。
    我国青年文艺工作团演唱歌曲“全世界人民心一条”。这首歌荣获第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节歌曲竞赛二等奖。
    我国青年文艺工作团杂技队在大会上表演车技。
    我国青年文艺工作团演出歌活报“并肩向前”。（何疆摄）
    我国举行招待新民主主义国家青年代表联欢大会时，中国人民志愿军战斗英雄周文江被各国青年在欢呼中高举起来。
