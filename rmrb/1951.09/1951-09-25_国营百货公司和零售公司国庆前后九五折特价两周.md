Title: State-owned Department Stores and Retail Companies Offer a 5% Discount for Two Weeks Before and After National Day

Author:
Date: 1951-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: To celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of New China, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government has decided that all commodities in the retail outlets of state-owned department stores and retail companies across the country will be sold at a special price of 5% off the current retail price for two weeks around National Day. State-owned department stores and retail companies in various regions are now actively arranging for special-priced retail work in accordance with this instruction.

### 国营百货公司和零售公司国庆前后九五折特价两周

1951-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    国营百货公司和零售公司国庆前后九五折特价两周
   【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府贸易部为庆祝新中国开国两周年纪念日，决定全国各地国营百货公司及零售公司的零售门市部所有商品，在国庆节前后按现在零售牌价九五折特价出售两星期。各地国营百货公司和零售公司现正根据这一指示，积极布置特价零售工作中。
