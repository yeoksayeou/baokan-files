Title: U.S. Aircraft and Warships Frequently Invade China's Borders
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: U.S. Air Force planes and Navy warships have been frequently invading China's borders in recent days. Incomplete statistics for the five days from August 30th to September 3rd show that U.S. aircraft violated China's airspace a total of sixteen times involving seventeen planes; U.S. warships violated China's coast a total of three times involving four ships.

	At 00:25 on August 30th, a U.S. plane intruded over the city of Ji'an County, Liaodong Province. At 7:00 AM on the same day, a U.S. plane intruded over the northeast of Chaolian Island, east of Qingdao City, Shandong Province. At 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM, and 12:00 PM on the same day, one U.S. plane each time intruded over the northwest of Bei Huangcheng Island in the Changshan Islands, north of Penglai County, Shandong Province.

	At 7:00 AM and 8:00 AM on August 31st, a U.S. plane intruded over the northeast of Chaolian Island, Shandong Province, twice.

	From 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM on September 1st, a U.S. plane intruded over Chaolian Island, Shandong Province. In addition, two U.S. bombers intruded over Xuejia Island, west of Qingdao City, Shandong Province.

	At 6:40 AM on September 2nd, a U.S. plane intruded over Chaolian Island, Shandong Province. At 20:50 on the same day, a U.S. plane intruded over the Fuyouwei area of Ji'an County, Liaodong Province.

	At 1:40 AM on September 3rd, a U.S. plane intruded over the city of Ji'an County, Liaodong Province. At 6:50 AM on the same day, two U.S. planes intruded over the northern area of Weihaiwei, Shandong Province, and the northern area of Shidao City, respectively. From 8:20 AM to 9:00 AM on the same day, a U.S. plane intruded successively over Chaid Island, Da Guan Island, northeast of Qingdao City, Shandong Province, and Chengshanjiao, belonging to Rongcheng County, at the eastern tip of the Shandong Peninsula. At 20:15 on the same day, a U.S. plane intruded over the city of Ji'an County, Liaodong Province, and then intruded over Changdianhekou, belonging to Kuandian County. From 3:00 AM to 4:00 AM on August 30th, one U.S. warship intruded into the sea north of Jiesongying, belonging to Penglai County, and two other U.S. warships intruded into the sea east of Jiesongying.

	At approximately 4:00 AM on August 31st, one U.S. landing craft intruded into the sea east of Chengshanjiao.

### 美国飞机舰艇频繁侵犯我国边境

1951-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    美国飞机舰艇频繁侵犯我国边境
    【新华社九日讯】美国空军飞机和海军舰艇，连日以来频繁地侵犯我国边境。自八月三十日到九月三日共五日内的不完全统计，美国飞机侵犯我国领空，共十六次十七架；美国军舰侵犯我国沿海共三次四舰。
    八月三十日零时二十五分，美机一架侵入我辽东省辑安县城上空。同日七时，美机一架侵入山东省青岛市以东之朝连岛东北上空。同日十时、十一时和十二时，美国飞机各一架侵入我山东省蓬莱县以北长山列岛的北隍城岛西北上空。
    八月三十一日上午七时和八时，美机一架两度侵入山东省的朝连岛东北上空。
    九月一日七时至八时，美机一架侵入我山东省的朝连岛上空。另美国轰炸机两架侵入我山东省青岛市以西的薛家岛上空。
    九月二日六时四十分，美机一架侵入我山东省的朝连岛上空。同日二十时五十分，美机一架侵入我辽东省辑安县属富有卫地方上空。
    九月三日一时四十分，美机一架侵入我辽东省辑安县城上空。同日六时五十分，美机两架分别侵入我山东省威海卫北面上空和石岛市北面上空。同日八时二十分到九时，美机一架先后侵入我山东省青岛市以东偏北的柴岛、大管岛及山东半岛东端荣成县属的成山角上空。同日二十时十五分，美国飞机一架侵入我辽东省辑安县城上空，随后又侵至宽甸县属长甸河口上空。八月三十日三时至四时，美舰一艘侵入蓬莱县属解宋营以北海面，另美舰两艘侵入解宋营以东海面。
    八月三十一日四时许，美国登陆艇一艘侵入成山角以东海面。
