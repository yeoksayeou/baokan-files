Title: Romania Holds Exhibition to Celebrate the Second Anniversary of China's Founding

Author:
Date: 1951-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 27th: The Romanian Association for Foreign Culture held an exhibition entitled "The Chinese People's Struggle to Defend Peace and Happiness" on the 25th to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The exhibits included many photographs and charts illustrating the enormous changes in New China over the past two years, the production achievements of the Chinese working people, and the heroic struggle of the Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea. Attending the opening ceremony of the exhibition were leaders of the Romanian Parliament, government, and Party, representatives of people's organizations and cultural and artistic circles, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries stationed in Romania. Makavei, Chairman of the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture, delivered the opening speech, saying, "The exhibition will enable the Romanian people to more deeply understand the achievements of the Chinese people in the struggle to defend peace and happiness. The Chinese people are about to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of their country, and the Romanian working people are overjoyed by the tremendous achievements of the Chinese people over the past two years, and wish the Chinese people greater achievements in the struggle to defend lasting peace." Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, then delivered a speech. He paid tribute to the Romanian people and pointed out that this exhibition will promote further friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Romanian peoples.

### 罗马尼亚举办展览会  庆祝我建国两周年

1951-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚举办展览会
    庆祝我建国两周年
    【新华社二十七日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚对外文化协会为庆祝中华人民共和国建国两周年纪念，于二十五日举办“中国人民为保卫和平与幸福而斗争”展览会。展览品包括许多照片和图表，说明新中国两年来的巨大变化、中国劳动人民的生产成就，和中国人民志愿军在朝鲜的英勇斗争。参加展览会揭幕礼的有：罗马尼亚议会、政府和党的领袖，人民团体和文化艺术界的代表，及苏联和各人民民主国家驻罗外交使节。罗马尼亚对外文化协会主席马卡维致开幕词，他说：“展览会可以使罗马尼亚人民更深切地了解中国人民在保卫和平与幸福的斗争中的成就。中国人民即将庆祝建国两周年纪念日，罗马尼亚劳动人民为中国人民两年来的巨大成就而欢欣鼓舞，并祝中国人民在保卫持久和平的斗争中获得更大的成就。”中国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平接着致词。他向罗马尼亚人民致敬并指出：这个展览会将促进中罗两国人民的进一步的友好合作。
