Title: Mass Rally in Central-South and Wuhan Celebrates Victory Over Japan Day
Author:
Date: 1951-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    A mass rally of people's representatives from Central-South and Wuhan, totaling about 1,000 people, was held on the 2nd to celebrate the "September 3rd" Victory Over Japan Day. Zhang Nanxian, Vice Chairman of the Central-South Military and Political Committee, pointed out in his speech that the victory of the Anti-Japanese War was achieved by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, united as one, persisting in struggle, and with the help of the Soviet Union. Today, as we commemorate "September 3rd," we must consolidate and develop Sino-Soviet friendship and strengthen our forces to oppose the aggression of American imperialism. The meeting passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Marshal Stalin, and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. After the meeting, the presidium of the conference went to the Wanguo Cemetery on Zhongshan Avenue to sweep the tombs of the fifteen Soviet volunteer air force martyrs who sacrificed their lives in combat with the Japanese imperialist air force in 1938.

### 中南、武汉各界人民代表  集会庆祝抗日胜利日

1951-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中南、武汉各界人民代表
    集会庆祝抗日胜利日
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中南、武汉各界人民代表约千人，二日集会庆祝“九三”抗日战争胜利纪念日。中南军政委员会副主席张难先在会上讲话中指出，抗日战争的胜利，是中国人民在共产党和毛主席的领导下，团结一致，坚持斗争，并在苏联的帮助下取得的。今天纪念“九三”，就要巩固和发展中苏友谊，充实力量，来反对美帝国主义的侵略。会上通过了向毛主席、朱总司令、斯大林大元帅和中国人民志愿军全体指战员致敬电。会后，大会主席团到中山大道万国公墓，为一九三八年与日本帝国主义空军作战牺牲的十五名苏联志愿空军烈士扫墓。
