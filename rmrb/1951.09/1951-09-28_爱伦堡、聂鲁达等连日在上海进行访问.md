Title: Ehrenburg, Neruda, and Others Visit Shanghai for Several Days
Author:
Date: 1951-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 27th: Ilya Ehrenburg, member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council and famous Soviet writer, and Pablo Neruda, famous Chilean poet, along with Mrs. Ehrenburg and Mrs. Neruda, are currently visiting Shanghai. On the 24th, they met with the heads of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, the Shanghai Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association, and the Shanghai Committee of the China News and Publication Workers' Union. In the afternoon, accompanied by Ding Ling, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary Workers, they visited the former residence of Lu Xun. Ehrenburg also visited the Xinhua Printing Factory, and Neruda visited the Institute of Hydrobiology. In the evening, they attended a welcome reception hosted by ten organizations, including the East China General Branch and Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions. The 25th was the 70th anniversary of Lu Xun's birth. Accompanied by Ding Ling, Xia Yan, and others, they went to Hongqiao to pay their respects at Lu Xun's tomb. Ehrenburg presented a wreath on behalf of the Soviet Writers Association, and Neruda presented a wreath on behalf of the Chilean cultural circles. In the afternoon, they visited the former residence of Sun Yat-sen on Xiangshan Road and visited the No. 1 State-owned Cotton Mill. In the evening, they attended a banquet hosted by Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, and Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government.

### 爱伦堡、聂鲁达等连日在上海进行访问

1951-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    爱伦堡、聂鲁达等连日在上海进行访问
    【新华社上海二十七日讯】世界和平理事会执行局委员、苏联名作家爱伦堡与智利名诗人聂鲁达和爱伦堡夫人、聂鲁达夫人正在上海访问。二十四日他们会晤了中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会、上海市文学艺术界联合会、中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备委员会上海分会和中国新闻出版印刷工会上海市委员会等团体的负责人。下午，他们由中华全国文学工作者协会副主席丁玲陪同参观鲁迅故居。爱伦堡并参观了新华印刷厂，聂鲁达访问了水生生物研究所。当晚他们出席了中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会华东总分会和上海分会、中苏友好协会上海分会、上海总工会等十个团体的欢迎会。二十五日是鲁迅诞生七十周年纪念日，他们由丁玲、夏衍等陪同往虹桥谒鲁迅墓，爱伦堡代表苏联作家协会、聂鲁达代表智利文化界联合会献花圈。他们在下午往香山路瞻仰了孙中山先生故居，并参观了国营棉纺第一厂。晚上参加华东军政委员会主席饶漱石和上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年的宴会。
