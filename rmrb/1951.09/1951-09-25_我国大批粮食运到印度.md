Title: Large Quantities of Chinese Grain Shipped to India
Author:
Date: 1951-09-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 24th: According to the contract signed between the Chinese and Indian governments in May, China has shipped over 300,000 tons of grain to India. The Sino-Indian contract stipulates that China will supply India with 442,000 tons of sorghum and 66,000 tons of rice. All the rice has been shipped to India. As of September 18th, the amount of sorghum shipped to India has reached 251,000 tons. Before September 30th, six more ships carrying 27,180 tons of sorghum will successively arrive in Madras and Calcutta. Another ship carrying 6,600 tons of sorghum will arrive in Calcutta on October 6th.

### 我国大批粮食运到印度

1951-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

   我国大批粮食运到印度
   【新华社新德里二十四日电】我国政府根据五月间与印度政府签订的合同而运到印度的粮食，目前已达三十多万吨。中印合同规定中国以四十四万二千吨高粱和六万六千吨大米供给印度。大米已全部运到印度。到九月十八日为止，运到印度的高粱已达二十五万一千吨。九月三十日以前，将续有六只船载运高粱二万七千一百八十吨陆续到达马德拉斯和加尔各答。另一只载运高粱六千六百吨的船将于十月六日到达加尔各答。
