Title: Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Prepare to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th. People from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macao have begun to enthusiastically prepare for the celebration of National Day. The Hong Kong business community has organized a "Preparatory Committee for the National Day Celebration," with the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, the Chinese Manufacturers' Association, the Gold and Silver Exchange and other units participating in the preparatory work. It has been decided to hold a National Day celebration meeting at noon on October 1st, and a grand social banquet in the evening. More than 40 organizations in Macao, including workers, students, business, cultural and other circles, have also jointly formed the "Macao Compatriots National Day Celebration Preparatory Committee." In addition to deciding to hold a celebration meeting, a dinner party and a recreational evening party on National Day, all shops and schools in the city will be closed for one day, and archways will be built and decorated with lanterns and colored streamers on all streets to celebrate with fireworks.

### 港澳同胞筹庆国庆节

1951-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    港澳同胞筹庆国庆节
    【新华社广州十六日电】香港、澳门各界人民开始热烈筹备庆祝国庆节。香港工商界已组织“庆祝国庆节大会筹备会”，华商总会、中华厂商联合会、金银贸易场等单位均参加了筹备工作，决定于十月一日午间举行国庆节庆祝大会，当晚举行盛大联欢宴会。澳门的工人、学生、工商界、文化界等社团共四十余个单位，亦已联合组成“澳门同胞庆祝国庆节大会筹备委员会”，除决定于国庆日举行庆祝大会、叙餐会和游艺晚会外，全埠各商店、学校均将休息一天，各街道将盖搭牌楼，张灯结彩，鸣炮志庆。
