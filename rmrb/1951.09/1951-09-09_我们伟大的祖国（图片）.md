    Our Great Motherland
    After obtaining cotton produced by farmers from various regions, cotton textile factories in Shanghai, both public and private, have resumed operations one after another. They will continue to produce cloth to supply the needs of the military and the people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海各公私营棉纺织厂在获得各地农民生产的棉花以后，已纷纷复工。他们将继续生产布匹，供给军民的需要。
