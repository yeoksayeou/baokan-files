Title: Polish Delegation Departs to Attend China's National Day Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1951-09-29
Page: 4

    Polish Delegation Departs to Attend China's National Day Ceremony
    [Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 28th] TASS Warsaw, 27th dispatch quoting the Polish Press Agency: The Polish delegation to attend the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, led by Putrament, Vice Chairman of the Polish National Peace Committee, has left Warsaw by plane for Beijing. Representatives of the Polish United Workers' Party and various people's organizations, as well as Peng Mingzhi, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, went to the airport to see them off.

### 参加我国国庆典礼  波代表团启程

1951-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    参加我国国庆典礼
    波代表团启程
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社华沙二十七日电引波兰通讯社的消息说：波兰参加中华人民共和国建国两周年国庆典礼代表团，由波兰全国和平委员会副主席普特拉曼特率领，已乘机离华沙赴北京。波兰统一工人党和各人民团体的代表以及中国驻波大使彭明治均赴机场送行。
