    Our Great Motherland
    The "Red Silk Dance" is a new type of dance formed by absorbing the excellent characteristics of Peking Opera and Ping Opera movements. It has our national form and expresses a light and joyful mood. This is the "Red Silk Dance" being performed by the cultural troupe going abroad.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “红绸舞”是吸取京戏和评戏动作的优良特点而形成的一种新的舞蹈。它具有我国的民族形式，表现出轻快、喜悦的情绪。这是出国文工团正在表演“红绸舞”。
