Title: Decision of the Committee on the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prizes

Author: 
Date: 1951-09-19
Page: 1

    Decision of the Committee on the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prizes
    Song Qingling, Chairman of the Chinese People's Relief Administration, is awarded the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize for her outstanding contribution to the struggle to safeguard and consolidate peace.
    Signed: Chairman of the Committee: Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Skobeltsyn
    Vice-Chairman: Guo Moruo (China)
    Aragon (France)
    Committee Members: Bernal (United Kingdom)
    Neruda (Chile)
    Dobski (Poland)
    Kellermann (Germany)
    Sadoveanu (Romania)
    Fadeyev (Soviet Union)
    Ehrenburg (Soviet Union)
    April 6, 1951, Moscow

    阿拉贡（法国）
