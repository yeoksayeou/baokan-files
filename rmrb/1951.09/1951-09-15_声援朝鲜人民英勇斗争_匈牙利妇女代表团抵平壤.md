Title: In Support of the Korean People's Heroic Struggle: Hungarian Women's Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1951-09-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 7th: A four-member Hungarian women's delegation, which came to Korea to support the heroic war of resistance of the Korean people, arrived in Pyongyang on the 3rd, and was warmly welcomed by Korean government leaders and representatives of various democratic parties, social organizations, and people from all walks of life.
    The Hungarian women's delegation is headed by Ms. Bugar, a member of the National Assembly of the Hungarian People's Republic. The members include Ms. Kando, Secretary-General of the Saraegeersai Committee of the Hungarian Democratic Women's League, Ms. Sarka of the Budapest Peace Protection Committee, and Ms. Obrmayo, a university professor. Accompanying them are Mr. Vig, a reporter for the Hungarian Working People's Party newspaper "Free People's Daily," and Mr. Furgatz, a film production technician. The delegation also brought a large number of gifts from the Hungarian people to the Korean people.

### 声援朝鲜人民英勇斗争  匈牙利妇女代表团抵平壤

1951-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    声援朝鲜人民英勇斗争
    匈牙利妇女代表团抵平壤
    【新华社平壤七日电】为声援英勇抗战的朝鲜人民而来朝鲜的匈牙利妇女代表团一行四人，已于三日抵达平壤，受到朝鲜政府首长与各民主政党、社会团体及各界人民代表的热烈欢迎。
    匈牙利妇女代表团由匈牙利人民共和国国民议会议员布加尔女士任团长，团员有匈牙利民主妇女联盟沙拉埃盖尔赛委员会书记长康道女士、布达佩斯保卫和平委员会沙尔卡女士、大学教授奥勃马约女士。随行者有匈牙利劳动人民党机关报《自由人民报》记者维格，电影制片技师福尔加茨等。代表团并带来了匈牙利人民送给朝鲜人民的大批礼物。
