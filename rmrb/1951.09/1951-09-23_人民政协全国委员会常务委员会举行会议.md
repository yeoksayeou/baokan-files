Title: National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Standing Committee Holds Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd—The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a meeting on the afternoon of the 22nd. The meeting adopted slogans for celebrating the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, discussed the issue of convening the third session of the National Committee in mid-October, and the issue of mobilizing staff from central and Beijing-Tianjin municipal organs, organizations, schools, and democratic parties to participate in the land reform movement.
　　Those attending the meeting included Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and Standing Committee members Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Lan, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Naiqi, Ma Xulun, Zhang Xiruo, Liu Ningyi, Deng Yingchao, Feng Wenbin, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi, and others, totaling sixteen people.
　　Those attending the meeting as observers included Xu Teli, Liu Geping, Situ Meitang, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Liu Yazi, and Zhang Dongsun, members of the Central People's Government Committee, Chen Shaoxian, Luo Longji, and Huang Shaohong, members of the Government Administration Council, and more than twenty staff members of the National Committee.

### 人民政协全国委员会常务委员会举行会议

1951-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会常务委员会举行会议
   【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会于二十二日下午举行会议。会议通过了庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年的口号，讨论了于十月中旬召开全国委员会第三次会议的问题以及动员中央和京津两市各机关、各团体、各学校、各民主党派的工作人员参加土地改革运动的问题。
    出席这次会议的，有人民政协全国委员会副主席周恩来、李济深、陈叔通，常务委员蒋光鼐、张澜、章伯钧、黄炎培、章乃器、马叙伦、张奚若、刘宁一、邓颖超、冯文彬、沈雁冰、梁希、邵力子等共十六人。
    列席这次会议的，有中央人民政府委员会委员徐特立、刘格平、司徒美堂、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、柳亚子、张东荪，政务院政务委员陈劭先、罗隆基、黄绍竑及全国委员会的工作人员等二十余人。
