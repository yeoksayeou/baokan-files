Title: China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association Hosts Banquet for Volunteer Army Representatives Chai Chuanruo and Others; Hopes They Will Convey the Nationwide Patriotic Movement to All Commanders and Fighters Upon Returning to the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1951-09-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: The China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association hosted a banquet on the evening of the 13th for ten returning representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, including Chai Chuanruo, Ji Bingqian, and Gao Chao. Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, vice-chairmen of the association, Liu Guan-yi, secretary-general, and heads of various departments attended the reception. During the banquet, vice-chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong gave short speeches. They unanimously pointed out that the Volunteer Army's returning representatives have reported the great victories of the Volunteer Army on the Korean front to the people of the whole country, which has greatly promoted the nationwide movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. These reports have promoted the production increase and donation movement and enhanced the patriotic ideology of the Chinese people. It is hoped that when the representatives return to the Korean front, they will convey to all Volunteer Army commanders and fighters the achievements of the people of the whole country in land reform, the suppression of counter-revolutionaries, and various construction undertakings, as well as the situation of the Resist America and Aid Korea movement. Volunteer Army representative Chai Chuanruo expressed his gratitude on the spot. He stated that in order to defend our great and beloved motherland, the Volunteer Army is determined to persevere and fight to the end.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  欢宴志愿军代表柴川若等  希望回到前线时，把全国人民的爱国运动情况告慰全体指战员

1951-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    欢宴志愿军代表柴川若等
    希望回到前线时，把全国人民的爱国运动情况告慰全体指战员
    【新华社十三日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会于十三日晚欢宴我国人民志愿军归国代表柴川若、嵇炳前、高巢等十人。该会副主席彭真、陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一及各部负责人均出席招待。席间，该会副主席彭真、陈叔通均作简短讲话。他们一致指出：志愿军归国代表把志愿军在朝鲜前线的伟大胜利向全国人民做了传达报告，对全国人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动起了很大的推动作用。这些报告推动了增产捐献运动，提高了我国人民的爱国主义思想，希望代表们回到朝鲜前线时，把全国人民在土地改革、镇压反革命运动和各种建设事业方面的成绩，以及抗美援朝运动的情况，告慰全体志愿军指战员同志们。志愿军代表柴川若即席致谢。他表示：为了保卫伟大可爱的祖国，志愿军誓必坚持不懈地奋斗到底。
