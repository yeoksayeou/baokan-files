    Our Great Motherland
    Do you know in what a happy environment the children of New China are growing up? Here, the children in the small class of the attached kindergarten of Beijing Normal University are imitating the actions of doctors, concentrating on giving medical treatment to their dolls!

    我们伟大的祖国
    你知道新中国的儿童是在怎样的幸福环境里生长吗？在这里，北京师范大学附设婴幼园的小班儿童，正在模仿医生的动作，聚精会神地给娃娃看病哩！
