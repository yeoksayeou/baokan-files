Title: Commemorating September 3rd: Opposition to the Illegal US-British Peace Treaty with Japan - Over Twenty Combat Heroes and Others to Hold Radio Forum Today
Author:
Date: 1951-09-03
Page: 1

    Commemorating September 3rd: Opposition to the Illegal US-British Peace Treaty with Japan
    Over Twenty Combat Heroes and Others to Hold Radio Forum Today
    [This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: To celebrate the 6th anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan on "September 3rd" and to oppose the illegal San Francisco Peace Conference with Japan convened by US-British imperialism, over twenty people, including famous combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, veteran soldiers from the War of Resistance Against Japan, returning representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and combat heroes, are scheduled to hold a radio forum at the Central People's Broadcasting Station at 10:30 AM on September 3rd. Those attending the meeting and speaking will include Jiang Xueshan, combat hero of a certain unit of the Fourth Field Army and commander of the Tashan Heroic Regiment, Wang Yushan, the famous Chinese Matrosov-style hero, and Zhang Ba, the famous explosives hero of the First Field Army, among others.

### 纪念九三反对美英非法对日媾和  战斗英雄等二十余人今日举行广播座谈会

1951-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念九三反对美英非法对日媾和
    战斗英雄等二十余人今日举行广播座谈会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：为庆祝“九三”抗日胜利六周年，反对美英帝国主义非法召集的旧金山对日和会，中国人民解放军著名的战斗英雄、抗日时期的老战士、中国人民志愿军归国代表、战斗英雄等二十余人定于九月三日上午十时三十分在中央人民广播电台举行广播座谈会。出席会议发言的有第四野战军某部战斗英雄、塔山英雄团团长江雪山，著名的中国马特洛索夫式的英雄王玉山，第一野战军著名的爆炸英雄张八等。
