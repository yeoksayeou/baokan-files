Title: L'Humanité Comments on the San Francisco Conference: The United States' Arrogant Use of "Absolute Command" Shows Its Weakness, Not Its Strength
Author:
Date: 1951-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th: The French newspaper L'Humanité said in an editorial on the 4th, entitled "The Absolute Command of San Francisco": "To use the means of 'absolute command' - the only means suitable for their totalitarian politics, the American imperialists will not hesitate to use even the most dangerous provocative acts. In order to 'prepare' for the San Francisco Conference, they used sabotage to stall the Korean armistice talks, and in these days, incidents of their violating Chinese airspace and territorial waters have also suddenly increased."

The editorial continued: Despite the fact that the Soviet delegation once again condemned the United States' sinister conspiracies and spoke out for the interests of the people of the world when attending the conference, Washington politicians still said: "We will still sign as is." "There is no doubt about this, but what about the future? Absolute command is one thing, but political reality is another. The recent exchange of congratulatory telegrams between Stalin and Mao Zedong timely reminded Truman and his negotiators, as well as the Western vassal states, of the existence of the Sino-Soviet alliance, which unites nearly 700 million people under the same socialist ideal with an unwavering will to stop aggression and defend peace in Asia."

The editorial continued: Even within the Atlantic Alliance, sharp contradictions are becoming increasingly apparent. "In this situation, many countries that may sign in San Francisco will not be without reservations in their hearts when they sign. Each of them is considering how to deceive the other."

The editorial concluded: "In this situation, the State Department's bluster, roar, and table-thumping are all understandable. This is a manifestation of fragility, not strength."

### 法国人道报评旧金山会议  美国使用“绝对命令”的蛮横手段是表现它脆弱不是表现它有力量

1951-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    法国人道报评旧金山会议
    美国使用“绝对命令”的蛮横手段是表现它脆弱不是表现它有力量
    【新华社布拉格九日电】法国人道报四日在题为“旧金山的绝对命令”的社论中说：“美帝国主义者为了使用‘绝对命令’的手段——唯一适合他们极权主义政治的手段，即使用最危险的挑衅行为也在所不惜。为了‘准备’旧金山会议，他们以暗中破坏的手段使朝鲜的停战谈判停顿，而在这些日子里，他们侵犯中国领空和领海的事件也骤然增加。”
    社论继续说：尽管苏联代表团出席会议时再次斥责美国的罪恶阴谋，为世界各国人民的利益仗义执言，但华盛顿政客还是说：“我们仍然照样签字。”“这一点固然没有疑问，但是将来怎么样呢？绝对命令是一回事，可是政治现实却是另一回事。斯大林和毛泽东最近来往的贺电，及时地向杜鲁门和他的谈判代表以及西方附庸国提醒了中苏同盟的存在，这个同盟把在同一个社会主义理想之下的近七亿人民团结在制止侵略和保卫亚洲和平的不可动摇的意志之下。”
    社论继续说：甚至在大西洋联盟以内，尖锐的矛盾也越来越明显了。“在这种情况下，许多可能在旧金山签字的国家在签字时，内心是不会没有保留的。他们每一个人都在考虑怎样欺骗对方。”
    社论末了说：“在这种情况下，国务院的色厉内荏，咆哮，拍桌子，全是可以理解的。这是一种脆弱的表现，而不是力量的表现。”
