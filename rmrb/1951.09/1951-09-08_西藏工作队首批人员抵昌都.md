Title: First Group of Tibet Work Team Arrives in Qamdo
Author:
Date: 1951-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 4th: Qamdo Dispatch: The first group of the Tibet Work Team organized by the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council arrived in Qamdo on August 24th. Vice Chairman Bonda Dorje of the Qamdo Regional People's Liberation Committee, on behalf of the Tibetan people, thanked Chairman Mao for his boundless concern for the Tibetan people and expressed warm welcome and regards to the personnel entering Tibet.

### 西藏工作队首批人员抵昌都

1951-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏工作队首批人员抵昌都
    【新华社重庆四日电】昌都讯：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会组织的西藏工作队首批人员，已于八月二十四日抵达昌都。昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任邦达多吉代表藏族人民感谢毛主席对西藏人民的无限关怀，并对入藏工作人员表示热烈的欢迎和慰问。
