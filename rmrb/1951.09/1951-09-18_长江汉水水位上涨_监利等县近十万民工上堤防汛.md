Title: Yangtze and Han Rivers Water Levels Rise; Nearly 100,000 Workers in Jianli and Other Counties Go to Embankments for Flood Control

Author: 
Date: 1951-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, September 15th: Since the 7th and 8th of this month, continuous heavy rains in Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Henan and other places have caused water levels in the tributaries of the Yangtze River basin to rise generally. People in various regions are now stepping up the defense of embankments. On the 13th and 14th, the water level of the Han River in Hubei Province exceeded last year's highest water level, and the flood peak has reached Hanchuan, Qianjiang, Mianyang and other places. The water level of the Yangtze River from Yichang to Hankou has risen by more than one meter in just a few days. Currently, nearly 100,000 workers in Jianli and other counties have gone to the embankments. Leading cadres of the Party and government in various counties have also gone to the embankments. Other regions along the Yangtze River and lakes in Hunan and Jiangxi are also stepping up flood prevention.

### 长江汉水水位上涨  监利等县近十万民工上堤防汛

1951-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    长江汉水水位上涨
    监利等县近十万民工上堤防汛
    【新华社汉口十五日电】本月七、八日以来，湖北、湖南、江西、河南等地连日大雨，长江流域各支流水位普遍上涨，各地人民现在正加紧防守堤防。十三日和十四日，湖北汉水的水位已超过去年的最高水位，洪水峰已经到达了汉川、潜江、沔阳等地。长江宜昌到汉口一段的水位，数日间就上涨了一公尺多。现在，监利等县已有近十万民工上堤。各县党、政负责干部也都上了堤。其他湖南、江西沿江沿湖的地区，也都在加紧防汛。
