Title: Vietnamese People's Delegation Visits Korea and Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-09-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Vietnamese People's Delegation, headed by Hoang Quoc Viet, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 25th after visiting Korea via Shenyang.

    The delegation arrived in Shenyang on the 23rd. On the 24th, the delegation visited the Northeast Materials Exchange Exhibition. That evening, the Northeast People's Government hosted a banquet for the delegation.

    When the delegation left Shenyang for Beijing, those who saw them off at the station included Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Zhang Xiushan, Director of the Organization Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast Branch of the Resist America and Aid Korea General Association, He Jinnian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region, and Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions.

### 越南人民代表团访问朝鲜后来京

1951-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民代表团访问朝鲜后来京
    【新华社二十五日讯】以黄国越为首的越南人民代表团，在访问朝鲜后，于二十五日下午经沈阳抵达北京。
    代表团是二十三日到沈阳的。二十四日，代表团参观了东北物资交流展览大会。当晚，东北人民政府曾设宴招待代表团。
    在代表团离沈阳赴北京时，到车站欢送的有东北人民政府副主席林枫、中共中央东北局组织部长张秀山、东北抗美援朝总分会主席高崇民、东北军区副司令员贺晋年、东北总工会主席张维桢等人。
