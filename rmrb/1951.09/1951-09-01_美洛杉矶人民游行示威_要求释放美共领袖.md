Title: Los Angeles People March and Demonstrate, Demanding the Release of American Communist Leaders
Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st dispatch from Los Angeles: The U.S. authorities' continuous and frantic persecution of the American Communist Party has aroused strong protests from the American people. According to a report from the "Worker Daily" correspondent in Los Angeles: More than 750 representatives from various mass organizations in Los Angeles recently organized a march and demonstration in front of the courthouse where the American Communist leaders are being held, protesting the illegal persecution of twelve American Communist leaders, headed by William Schneiderman, chairman of the California State Committee of the American Communist Party. The marching and demonstrating队伍 passed through the main streets of Los Angeles, chanting slogans, demanding a reduction in the bail for the twelve American Communist leaders, and calling for a struggle to win their release.

### 美洛杉矶人民游行示威  要求释放美共领袖

1951-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美洛杉矶人民游行示威
    要求释放美共领袖
    【新华社三十一日讯】洛杉矶消息：美国当局接连不断地疯狂迫害美国共产党的行动，已激起美国人民的强烈抗议。据“工人日报”驻洛杉矶记者报道：七百五十多个洛杉矶各种群众团体的代表，最近在禁闭美共领袖的法院院址前组织了游行示威，抗议非法迫害以美国共产党加利福尼亚州州委员会主席威廉·斯奈德曼为首的十二名美共领袖。游行示威的队伍穿过洛杉矶各主要街道，一路高呼口号，要求减少十二位美共领袖的保释金，并号召为争取他们的释放而斗争。
