Our Great Motherland
    The National Railway Exhibition is being held at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. Thousands of people come here to visit every day. This group of young students has once again received a patriotic education from this exhibition. They are surrounding the model of the Beidaihe Railway Workers' Sanatorium.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-29
第1版()
专栏：
我们伟大的祖国
    全国铁路展览会在北京劳动人民文化宫举行。每天都有成千成万的人来这里参观。这一群青年学生从这个展览会中又一次受到了爱国主义的教育，他们正在围观北戴河铁路工人疗养院的模型。
