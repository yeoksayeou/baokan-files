Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-09-11
Page: 1

    In our great motherland, labor mutual aid in agricultural production is also being increasingly promoted in the newly liberated rural areas. This is a group of male and female members of the mutual aid team in Daxi Township, Fourth District, Fengcheng County, Jiangxi Province. They are working hard to harvest rice.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国农业生产中的劳动互助，在新解放区农村也日益推广了。这是江西丰城县第四区大溪乡的农民互助组的男女组员。他们正在紧张地收割稻子。
