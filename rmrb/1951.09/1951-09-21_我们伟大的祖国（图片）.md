Author:
Date: 1951-09-21

    Our Great Motherland
    During the Huai River project, tens of thousands of laborers are working diligently. Protecting the health of the laborers is a very important task. The First Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team of the Red Cross Society of China is honorably undertaking this task. This is a mobile medical team of the service team applying medicine and bandaging the foot of a laborer injured on the Huai River embankment.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在治淮工程中，有成千成万的民工在辛勤地劳动着。保护民工们的健康是一件很重要的工作。中国红十字会第一医务防疫服务大队光荣地担负起这项工作任务。这是该队的一个巡回医疗组在淮河堤上为一个脚部受伤的民工敷药包扎的情形。
