Title: First National Conference on Ethnic Education Opens

Author:
Date: 1951-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st—The First National Conference on Ethnic Education, convened by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, opened in Beijing on the 20th.  Attending the conference were responsible comrades from the central government, major administrative regions, relevant provinces and cities' education administrative departments, ethnic minority education workers, and representatives from relevant departments, totaling over 120 people. Including those attending as observers, there were a total of over 140 people, including representatives from 14 ethnic groups such as Han, Mongol, Tibetan, Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, Miao, Yi, Lisu, Akha, Bai, Gaoshan, Korean, and Manchu.

    The tasks of this conference are: to further understand the situation of ethnic minority education nationwide; to summarize work and exchange experiences; and to discuss important issues such as the direction of ethnic minority education, the training of ethnic minority cadres, the content of ethnic minority education, and the establishment of ethnic education administrative institutions. In addition, the conference will exchange opinions on issues such as ethnic minority education funding, student treatment, and the creation and enrichment of written languages.

    The conference opened with a speech by Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government. He pointed out that it is an unprecedented event in the history of Chinese education to bring together responsible comrades of education administrations, ethnic education workers and ethnic education experts from all over the country to discuss the development of education for all ethnic groups. He emphasized that the educational policy for ethnic minorities should be based on the general policy of "the culture and education of the People's Republic of China are new democratic, that is, national, scientific, and mass-oriented" as stipulated in the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and combined with the characteristics of each ethnic group to develop people's education that takes New Democracy as the content and adopts forms suitable for the development of each ethnic group.

    Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, and Saifudin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, also attended the meeting to provide guidance.

### 第一次全国民族教育会议开幕

1951-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

     第一次全国民族教育会议开幕
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府教育部召开的第一次全国民族教育会议，已于二十日在京开幕。出席这次会议的有中央、各大行政区、各有关省、市教育行政部门的负责同志，少数民族教育工作者和各有关部门代表一百二十余人，连列席会议者共计一百四十余人；其中包括了汉、蒙、藏、回、维吾尔、哈萨克、苗、彝、摩些、阿西、民家、高山、朝鲜、满等十四个民族的代表。
    这次会议的任务是：进一步了解全国少数民族教育情况；总结工作，交流经验；并讨论少数民族教育的方针、培养少数民族干部、少数民族教育的内容、建立民族教育行政机构等重要问题。此外会上将交换对少数民族教育经费、学生待遇、文字的创造与充实等问题的意见。
    会议由中央人民政府教育部部长马叙伦致开幕词。他指出：集合全国各地的教育行政负责同志、民族教育工作者和民族教育专家于一堂，来商讨发展各民族的教育事业，这是中国教育史上一件空前的事情。他着重指出：少数民族的教育方针应该依据人民政协共同纲领文教政策规定的“中华人民共和国的文化教育为新民主主义的，即民族的、科学的、大众的文化教育”这个总方针，并结合各民族的特点，发展以新民主主义为内容和采取适合于各该民族发展的形式的人民教育。
    中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，民族事务委员会副主任赛福鼎也都到会指导。
