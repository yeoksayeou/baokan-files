Author: 
Date: 1951-09-30
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Tiananmen Square in Beijing, on the eve of National Day, is illuminated by countless lights, making it look exceptionally solemn and beautiful. The capital's electrical workers worked continuously for forty-eight hours to install these lights, so that people from all walks of life and the public participating in the National Day celebrations could enjoy a pleasant evening here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国庆节前夕的北京天安门，被无数盏灯光照耀，显得分外的庄严美丽。首都电气工人连续工作四十八小时来安装这些电灯，好让参加国庆典礼的各方人士和民众在这里欢度良夜。
