Title: The Dalai Lama Returns to Lhasa, Zhang Jingwu Welcomed by Officials of the Local Government of Tibet in Lhasa and People of All Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st: Lhasa News: After leaving Yadong on July 21st, the Dalai Lama returned to Lhasa on August 17th. Officials and people, both monks and laymen, from all over Lhasa went to the suburbs to welcome him.
Xinhua News Agency, 31st: Lhasa News: Zhang Jingwu, the representative of the Central People's Government to Tibet, left Yadong on July 23rd and arrived in Lhasa on August 8th. Representative Zhang was welcomed by the monks and lay officials of the local government of Tibet in Lhasa and local Tibetans, Han Chinese, and Hui people upon his arrival in Lhasa.

### 达赖喇嘛返抵拉萨  张经武受到拉萨西藏地方政府官员及各族人民欢迎

1951-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛返抵拉萨
    张经武受到拉萨西藏地方政府官员及各族人民欢迎
   【新华社三十一日讯】拉萨消息：达赖喇嘛在七月二十一日离亚东后，已在八月十七日返抵拉萨。全拉萨的僧俗官员人民，都往郊外欢迎。
   【新华社三十一日讯】拉萨消息：中央人民政府赴藏代表张经武，在七月二十三日离开亚东，八月八日抵达拉萨。张代表在抵达拉萨时，受到拉萨的西藏地方政府僧俗官员及当地藏民、汉民、回民的欢迎。
