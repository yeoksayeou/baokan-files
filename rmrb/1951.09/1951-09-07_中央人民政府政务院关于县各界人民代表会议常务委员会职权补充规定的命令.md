Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government Regarding Supplementary Provisions on the Functions and Powers of the Standing Committee of the County People's Representatives Conference

The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, in order to further clarify the functions and powers of the Standing Committee of the County People's Representatives Conference, suggests the following supplementary revisions to Article 10 of the "Organizational Rules for the County People's Representatives Conference," which lists the functions and powers of the County Standing Committee:

    I. Organize and promote representatives of the County People's Representatives Conference to convey the resolutions of the People's Representatives Conference and the policies and decrees of the County People's Government in various occasions and in various ways, and assist in their implementation.

    II. Regularly contact the representatives of the People's Representatives Conference, democratic parties, people's organizations, and democratic personages, and through them, extensively contact the people, assist the government in mobilizing the people to carry out various tasks, systematically collect opinions from all aspects, and promptly suggest that the government adopt them.

    III. Accept the people's suggestions, inquiries, requests, appeals, and criticisms, and be responsible for handling them appropriately, ensuring that they are addressed and accounted for.

    IV. Be responsible for carrying out the work of the County People's Democratic United Front, strengthening the unity and cooperation of the democratic parties, people's organizations, and democratic personages.

    V. Organize symposiums and study sessions to assist and promote the study of current events, policies, and theories by the democratic parties, people's organizations, and democratic personages.

    VI. Accept and handle matters entrusted and suggested by the Provincial (Administrative Office District) Consultative Committee.

    VII. Be responsible for the preparatory work for the next County People's Representatives Conference.

The above suggestions were discussed and approved by the 96th Government Affairs Conference of this Council, and have been reported to and approved by the Chairman of the Central People's Government. They are hereby ordered to be promulgated and implemented.

                             Premier Zhou Enlai
                                August 18, 1951

### 中央人民政府政务院关于县各界人民代表会议常务委员会职权补充规定的命令

1951-09-07
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院
    关于县各界人民代表会议常务委员会职权补充规定的命令
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会为了使县各界人民代表会议常务委员会的职权更加明确起见，建议对于“县各界人民代表会议组织通则”第十条列举的县常务委员会的职权，补充修订如下：
    一、组织与推动县人民代表会议代表，在各种场合，以各种方式，传达人民代表会议的决议及县人民政府政策法令，并协助推行。
    二、经常地联系人民代表会议代表、民主党派、人民团体、民主人士，并经过他们广泛地联系人民，协助政府动员人民进行各种工作，有计划地搜集各方面意见，及时建议政府采择。
    三、接受人民的建议、询问、要求、申诉与批评，负责加以适当的处理，务使有着落有交代。
    四、负责进行县人民民主统一战线工作，加强各民主党派、人民团体和民主人士的团结与合作。
    五、组织座谈会和学习会，协助与推动各民主党派、人民团体和民主人士关于时事、政策和理论的学习。
    六、接受与办理省（行署区）协商委员会委托事项与建议。
    七、负责进行下届各界人民代表会议的准备工作。
    上项建议，经提出本院第九十六次政务会议讨论通过，并经报请中央人民政府主席批准，特令公布施行。
                             总理  周恩来
        一九五一年八月十八日
