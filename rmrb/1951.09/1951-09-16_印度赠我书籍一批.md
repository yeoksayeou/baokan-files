Title: India Donates a Batch of Books to China
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Indian Ambassador to China, Panikkar, on the evening of the 13th, on behalf of the Indian government, donated over 500 books to Peking University, Zhongshan University, and the Ministry of Communications. The donation ceremony was held at the Beijing International Club, and the books were accepted by Minister of Education Ma Xulun on behalf of the Central People's Government. After the ceremony, the Indian Embassy in China screened an Indian film to entertain the Chinese and foreign guests present.

The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government is also preparing to donate a batch of books to Indian universities.

### 印度赠我书籍一批

1951-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度赠我书籍一批
    【新华社十五日讯】印度驻我国大使潘尼迦于十三日晚间代表印度政府，以五百余册书籍赠送给北京大学、中山大学和交通部。赠送仪式在北京国际俱乐部举行，书籍由我中央人民政府教育部部长马叙伦代表接受。仪式完毕后，印度驻我国大使馆并放映印度电影，招待到会的中外来宾。
    我中央人民政府教育部也准备以一批书籍赠与印度的大学。
