Title: Representatives of People from Old Revolutionary Base Areas in Central South and East China Arrive in Beijing; Huai River Delegation and Water Conservancy Representatives from Various Places Arrive One After Another
Author:
Date: 1951-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th - Four hundred and ninety-two representatives of the people from the old revolutionary base areas of Central South and East China, invited by the Central People's Government to participate in the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 28th. Greeting them at the station were Xie Juezai, Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Xinyu, Deputy Minister, Zhang Xiruo and Peng Zemin, Deputy Directors of the Political and Legal Committee, Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China People's Relief Administration, Fu Qiutao and Zhu Xuefan, Deputy Heads of the Central People's Government Southern Old Revolutionary Base Area Visiting Delegation, Yan Baohang, Deputy Secretary-General, Cao Mengjun, representative of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Jin Shixuan, Professor of Northern Jiaotong University, Dong Weichuan, Professor of Beijing Normal University, Li Zong'en, President of the Peking Union Medical College, staff members of various agencies, students from Beijing, and members of the Young Pioneers, totaling approximately 1,500 people. A grand welcome meeting was held at the station.

After two hundred Young Pioneers presented flowers to the representatives at the welcome meeting, Zhang Xiruo delivered a welcome speech. He said, "The contributions of the people of the old revolutionary base areas to the Chinese revolution are enormous. As the people of the whole country joyously celebrate the second National Day, we should express our deep respect and love for the people of the old liberated areas." Zheng Shaowen, a representative of the people from the old revolutionary base areas, delivered a reply, saying, "This time, the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao have invited us, the representatives of the people from the old liberated areas, to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. We feel very honored, and we guarantee to always remember Chairman Mao's words: 'Carry forward the revolutionary tradition and strive for greater glory.'"

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th - Water conservancy representatives arriving in the capital to participate in the National Day celebrations are arriving one after another. Among them, the Huai River Delegation arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 28th. Greeting them at the station were Liu Yaozhang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Water Conservancy of the Central People's Government, and more than one hundred others. The delegation includes: Li Jingping, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Huai River Harnessing Committee, Qian Zhengying, Deputy Director of the Engineering Department, Wu Meisheng, Deputy Chief Engineer of Wusong Machine Factory, Xie Hongyou, Special-Class Huai River Harnessing Hero of Runheji, Gan Caihua (female), Labor Model, Zhang Liao, Pan Fengyun, Ma Fuqian, Wang Tingxiu, Zheng Peiqi, Wang Shilan (female), Ding Guangfu, Wang Changhui, Huai River Harnessing Heroes, Liu Huaicun, Huang Yuanming (female), Model Workers, Su Zhongyue, Le Jinlin and other seventeen people, staff members of the Huai River Harnessing Committee.

Other water conservancy representatives from various places arriving in Beijing for the celebrations include: Liu Chongguang, Deputy Director of the Water Conservancy Department of the East China Military and Political Committee, Pan Zhengdao, Deputy Director of the Water Conservancy Department of the Central South Military and Political Committee, Wu Chonghua, Labor Model for Yellow River Harnessing, as well as Luo Chengfu, Gao Yuanzhen, Shi Ni'ma, Sun Baogang, Wu Kangning, Feng Binghui and many other water conservancy labor models and model workers from various places.

### 中南华东各老根据地人民代表抵京  淮河代表团及各地水利代表陆续到达

1951-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中南华东各老根据地人民代表抵京
    淮河代表团及各地水利代表陆续到达
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中央人民政府邀请来京参加国庆典礼的中南、华东各老根据地人民代表四百九十二人于二十八日上午到达北京。到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府内务部部长谢觉哉，副部长武新宇，政治法律委员会副主任张奚若、彭泽民，中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫，中央人民政府南方老根据地访问团副团长傅秋涛、朱学范，副秘书长阎宝航，中华全国民主妇联代表曹孟君，北京市副市长张友渔，北方交通大学教授金士宣，北京师范大学教授董渭川，中国协和医学院院长李宗恩，各机关工作人员、北京市学生、少年儿童队队员等约一千五百人，并在车站开了盛大的欢迎会。
    欢迎会在二百名少年儿童队队员向代表献花后，由张奚若致欢迎词。他说：“老根据地人民对中国革命的贡献是巨大的。当全国人民欢欣鼓舞庆祝第二个国庆日的时候，我们对老区人民应表示深切的敬意和热爱。”老根据地人民代表郑绍文致答词，他说：“这次中央人民政府和毛主席请我们老区人民代表来京参加国庆典礼，我们觉得非常光荣，我们保证永远记住毛主席的话：‘发扬革命传统，争取更大光荣。’”
    【新华社二十八日讯】前来首都参加国庆节观礼的水利代表陆续到达，其中淮河代表团已于二十八日上午抵京。到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府水利部办公厅主任刘瑶章等百余人。代表团包括：治淮委员会政治部副主任黎竞平、工程部副部长钱正英、吴淞机器厂副总工程师吴梅生、润河集特等治淮功臣谢洪友、劳动模范甘彩华（女）、治淮功臣张辽、潘凤云、马福乾、王庭秀、郑培起、王世兰（女）、丁广富、王长惠、模范工作者刘怀存、黄远明（女）、治淮委员会工作人员苏中岳、乐金林等十七人。
    其他各地来京观礼的水利代表，已抵京的计有：华东军政委员会水利部副部长刘宠光、中南军政委员会水利部副部长潘正道、治黄劳动模范吴崇华，以及各地水利劳动模范和模范工作者罗成富、高元贞、石呢玛、孙宝刚、吴康宁、冯炳辉等多人。
