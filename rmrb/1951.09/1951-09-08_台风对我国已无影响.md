Title: Typhoon No Longer Affecting China
Author:
Date: 1951-09-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 4th—The Central Meteorological Observatory announced the lifting of the typhoon warning at 7:00 PM on September 4th. The typhoon, after crossing Hainan Island and making landfall west of Tokyo Bay, has weakened significantly and no longer poses a threat to China.

### 台风对我国已无影响

1951-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    台风对我国已无影响
    【新华社四日讯】中央气象台九月四日下午七时宣布解除台风警报。按台风自穿越海南岛经东京湾向西登陆后，风势大为减弱，对我国已无影响。
