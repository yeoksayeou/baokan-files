Title: Soviet Union Prepares to Celebrate China's National Day by Holding Chinese Film Week; Our Film Workers Delegation to Fly to the Soviet Union Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The Chinese film workers delegation, invited by the Soviet Ministry of Cinema to participate in the Chinese Film Week in the Soviet Union, is scheduled to fly to the Soviet Union from Beijing on the 25th. The Chinese Film Week is scheduled to begin on October 1st, the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and will be held in the capitals of all sixteen Soviet republics and other major cities. The Chinese Film Week will screen the Sino-Soviet co-productions "Liberated China," "The Victory of the Chinese People," "Beautiful Rivers and Mountains," as well as the Chinese films "Daughter of China," "Invisible Front," "Boundless Radiance," "Iron Warrior," "The White Haired Girl," and "The Great Unity of Chinese Nationalities," which have already been translated into Russian in Moscow. Other films that have arrived in the Soviet Union but have not yet been translated into Russian, such as "Shangrao Concentration Camp," "New Children's Heroes," "Marching Forward with Songs," "Unite Until Tomorrow," "Green Hill Red Flag," and "The Victory of the People of Inner Mongolia," are planned to be screened on tour in the cultural palaces of major factories first.
    In order to facilitate this unprecedented film exhibition, the Soviet Ministry of Cinema specially invited China to send a delegation including film administrative cadres, directors, actors, and Stalin Prize winners to participate. The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government selected twelve film workers to form the delegation. In addition to the head and deputy heads and secretary, the remaining members of the delegation are all Stalin Prize winners, directors, and actors related to the films in this exhibition. The delegation is headed by Yu Ling, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Culture Bureau and Director of the Shanghai Film Studio, with Sha Meng, Acting Director of the Art Committee of the Northeast Film Studio, director of "Zhao Yiman" and "Shangrao Concentration Camp," and Xu Ke, Director of the Art Committee of the Beijing Film Studio and director of "Boundless Radiance," as deputy heads.

### 苏联筹庆我国国庆将举办中国电影周  我电影工作者代表团明日飞苏

1951-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联筹庆我国国庆将举办中国电影周
    我电影工作者代表团明日飞苏
    【新华社二十三日讯】应苏联电影事业部邀请赴苏参加中国电影周的中国电影工作者代表团，定二十五日由京飞苏。中国电影周定于十月一日我中华人民共和国成立二周年纪念日起，在全苏联十六个加盟共和国首都及其他大城市举行。中国电影周将放映中苏合制的“解放了的中国”、“中国人民的胜利”、“锦绣河山”以及已在莫斯科译成俄语的我国影片“中华女儿”、“无形的战线”、“光芒万丈”、“钢铁战士”、“白毛女”、“中国民族大团结”等。其他已到苏联还没有译成俄语的影片“上饶集中营”、“新儿女英雄传”、“高歌猛进”、“团结起来到明天”、“翠岗红旗”、“内蒙人民的胜利”等片，拟先在各大工厂的文化宫作巡回放映。
    为了便利举行这一空前的电影展览，苏联电影事业部特邀我国派遣一个包括电影行政干部、导演、演员及斯大林奖金获得者的代表团前往参加。中央人民政府文化部选派了十二位电影工作者组成代表团。代表团除正副团长及秘书外，其余团员均为与此次展览影片有关的斯大林奖金获得者及导演、演员。代表团由上海文化局副局长、上海电影制片厂厂长于伶任团长，东北电影制片厂艺术委员会代理主任、
    “赵一曼”、“上饶集中营”的导演沙蒙与北京电影制片厂艺术委员会主任、“光芒万丈”的导演许珂任副团长。
