Title: Declaration Supporting the Peace Pact: Over 5 Million Signatures in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 4

    Declaration Supporting the Peace Pact
    Over 5 Million Signatures in Vietnam
    [Xinhua News Agency, 31st] According to Vietnam News Agency, the Vietnam Peace Committee recently announced that as of August 15th, 5,359,359 Vietnamese people had signed the declaration supporting the Peace Pact. The committee recently received many signature books from all over the country, including many sent from French-occupied areas such as Hanoi, Saigon, and Hue.

### 拥护和平公约宣言  越五百余万人签名

1951-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    拥护和平公约宣言
    越五百余万人签名
    【新华社三十一日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南和平委员会最近公布：截至八月十五日止，越南人民在和平公约宣言上签名的已有五百三十五万九千三百五十九人。该会最近收到了许多来自全国各地的签名书，其中不少是从河内、西贡和顺化等法军占领区送来的。
