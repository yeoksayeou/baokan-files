Title: New Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung Visits Foreign Minister Zhou to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1951-09-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The newly appointed Burmese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, U Hla Maung, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:30 PM on the 20th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department. Correction: In the news item "Beijing Catholic Community Convenes Representative Conference" on the first page of this newspaper on September 15, "more than ninety percent" in the tenth line should read "more than eighty-four percent."
    In the news item "China-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Important Broadcast Tomorrow" on the first page of this newspaper on September 20, "Representative Assembly" in the third line should read "Representative Conference."

### 新任缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂拜会周外长商谈呈递国书

1951-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    新任缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂拜会周外长商谈呈递国书
    【新华社二十日讯】新任缅甸驻华特命全权大使吴拉茂于二十日下午四时三十分赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有亚洲司陈家康代司长，交际处王倬如处长。更正本报九月十五日第一版所载“北京市天主教召开代表会议”消息第十行中“百分之九十强”应为“百分之八十四强”。
    本报九月二十日第一版所载“中苏友好协会总会明天举行重要广播”消息第三行“代表大会”应为“代表会议”。
