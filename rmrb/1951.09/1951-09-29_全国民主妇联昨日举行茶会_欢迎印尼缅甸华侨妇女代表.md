Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Held a Tea Party Yesterday to Welcome Indonesian and Burmese Overseas Chinese Women Representatives
Author:
Date: 1951-09-29
Page: 1
The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party yesterday morning to warmly welcome the fourteen women representatives of the Indonesian Overseas Chinese Visiting Group and the Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group. Cai Chang, Chairman of the National Women's Federation, Vice-Chairmen Deng Yingchao and Xu Guangping, and representatives of the Beijing Women's Federation attended the welcoming party. After Xu Guangping delivered a welcoming speech, Lei Jieqiong, a Standing Committee member of the National Women's Federation, introduced the glorious achievements of the motherland under the leadership of Chairman Mao in the past two years and the active participation of Chinese women in the Resist America, Aid Korea movement. Li Mei, a representative of Indonesian Overseas Chinese women, and Wang Yizhi, a representative of Burmese Overseas Chinese women, respectively reported on the local women's movement and the overseas Chinese's wholehearted devotion to the motherland. Deng Yingchao spoke at the end. She expressed the hope that the returned Overseas Chinese sisters would understand the true situation of the motherland with a sense of ownership, and when they return overseas in the future, they will publicize the achievements of the motherland in the past two years to the Overseas Chinese sisters.

### 全国民主妇联昨日举行茶会  欢迎印尼缅甸华侨妇女代表

1951-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    全国民主妇联昨日举行茶会
    欢迎印尼缅甸华侨妇女代表
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于二十八日上午举行茶会，热烈欢迎印尼华侨归国观光团及缅甸华侨归国观光团的十四位妇女代表。全国妇联主席蔡畅，副主席邓颖超、许广平等和北京市妇联的代表都出席了这个欢迎会。许广平致欢迎辞后，全国妇联常委雷洁琼介绍二年来祖国在毛主席领导下的辉煌成就及中国妇女积极参加抗美援朝运动的情况。印尼华侨妇女代表李梅和缅甸华侨妇女代表王一芝先后报告了当地的妇女运动情况及海外华侨心心向着祖国的情形。最后由邓颖超讲话。她希望归国的华侨姊妹以主人翁的态度来了解祖国的真实情况，并在将来回到海外时，将祖国两年来的成就向华侨姊妹多作宣传。
