Title: Most Australians Oppose the US-Imposed Japanese Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1951-09-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, September 17th, via telecommunication: A recent public opinion poll shows that approximately two-thirds of the Australian people oppose the American "Japanese Peace Treaty." In the public opinion poll, one of the questions was: "Do you agree or disagree with the peace treaty with Japan that does not require Japan to pay reparations and allows Japan to have an army?" Sixty-three percent of the people from all walks of life who participated in the survey answered "disagree," while only twenty-one percent answered "agree."

### 澳大利亚多数人民反对美制对日和约

1951-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚多数人民反对美制对日和约
    【新华社十八日讯】电通社墨尔本十七日讯：最近举行的民意测验表明，澳大利亚全国人民约有三分之二反对美国“对日和约”。在民意测验中，有一个问题是：“对日和约不要日本赔偿并准许日本有军队，你是赞成这种和约呢，还是反对呢？”参加测验的各界人民百分之六十三回答“反对”，只有百分之二十一回答“赞成”。
