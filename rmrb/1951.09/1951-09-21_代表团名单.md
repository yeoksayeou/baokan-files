Title: Delegation List
Author:
Date: 1951-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The list of the People's Republic of China Cultural Delegation to India and Burma is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Ding Xielin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government and physicist;
    Deputy Head of Delegation: Li Yimang, economist;
    Members: Chen Hansheng, former professor of history at Peking University; Zheng Zhenduo, Director of the Bureau of Cultural Relics, literary critic, and novelist; Liu Baiyu, novelist (also Secretary-General of the Delegation); Feng Youlan, professor of Chinese Philosophy History at Tsinghua University; Qian Weichang, professor of physics and applied mathematics at Tsinghua University; Wu Zuoren, professor and painter at the Central Academy of Fine Arts; Di Chaobai, professor of economics at Peking University (also Deputy Secretary-General of the Delegation); Ji Xianlin, professor of the Department of Oriental Languages at Peking University; Zhang Junxiang, playwright and film director; Ye Dingyi, professor of Chinese Literature Department at Beijing Normal University; Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China (also Deputy Secretary-General of the Delegation); Chang Shuhong, painter and Director of the Dunhuang Cultural Relics Research Institute; Zhou Xiaoyan, professor of the Shanghai Branch of the Central Conservatory of Music.
    In addition to the above fifteen people, there are also six staff members accompanying the delegation.

### 代表团名单

1951-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    代表团名单
    【新华社二十日讯】赴印度和缅甸访问的中华人民共和国文化代表团名单如下：
    团长：中央人民政府政务院文化部副部长、物理学家丁燮林；
    副团长：经济学家李一氓；
    团员：前北京大学历史学教授陈翰笙，文物局局长、文学批评家、小说家郑振铎，小说家刘白羽（兼代表团秘书长），清华大学中国哲学史教授冯友兰，清华大学物理学及应用数学教授钱伟长，中央美术学院教授、画家吴作人，北京大学经济学教授狄超白（兼代表团副秘书长），北京大学东方语文学系教授季羡林，戏剧家、电影导演张骏祥、北京师范大学中国文学系教授叶丁易，中国红十字会总会副秘书长倪斐君（兼代表团副秘书长），画家、敦煌文物研究所所长常书鸿，中央音乐学院上海分院教授周小燕。
    代表团除上述十五人外，尚有工作人员六人随行。
