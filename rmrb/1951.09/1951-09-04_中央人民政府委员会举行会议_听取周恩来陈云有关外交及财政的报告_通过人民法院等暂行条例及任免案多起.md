Title: Central People's Government Committee Holds Meeting, Hearing Reports on Foreign Affairs and Finance from Zhou Enlai and Chen Yun, Passing Provisional Regulations on People's Courts and Multiple Appointment and Removal Cases

Author:
Date: 1951-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd: The Central People's Government Committee held its twelfth meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting heard a report on foreign affairs by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and a financial report by Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Administration Council and Director of the Financial and Economic Committee. After hearing explanations from Xu Deheng, Acting Chairman of the Legislative Affairs Committee of the Central People's Government, on the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for the People's Courts of the People's Republic of China," and from Li Liuru, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, on the revised draft of the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for the Supreme People's Procuratorate" and the draft of the "General Rules for the Organization of People's Procuratorates at All Levels," the meeting unanimously approved the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for the People's Courts of the People's Republic of China," the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for the Supreme People's Procuratorate," and the "General Rules for the Organization of People's Procuratorates at All Levels." The meeting also approved multiple appointment and removal cases and appointment and removal cases submitted by the State Administration Council for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

The following attended the meeting: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, and members of the Central People's Government Committee Li Lisan, He Xiangning, Wu Yuzhang, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Saifuddin, Ulanhu, Liu Geping, Ma Yinchu, Chen Yun, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Gao Chongmin, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Peng Zemin, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Mingshu, Tan Pingshan, Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun, and Long Yun, totaling thirty-four people.

The following were present at the meeting as observers: members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Presidents and members of the Supreme People's Court, Deputy Chief Procurators and members of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and senior officers of the People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, and Air Force, members of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government and heads of various committees, ministries, commissions, academies, administrations, and banks, as well as the Mayors and Deputy Mayors of Beijing and Tianjin, totaling more than one hundred people.

### 中央人民政府委员会举行会议  听取周恩来陈云有关外交及财政的报告  通过人民法院等暂行条例及任免案多起

1951-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府委员会举行会议
    听取周恩来陈云有关外交及财政的报告
    通过人民法院等暂行条例及任免案多起
   【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府委员会于三日下午举行第十二次会议。会议听取了政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来所作的外交报告和政务院副总理兼财政经济委员会主任陈云所作的财政报告。会议在听取中央人民政府法制委员会代理主任委员许德珩所作的关于“中华人民共和国人民法院暂行组织条例”的说明，和中央人民政府最高人民检察署副检察长李六如所作的关于“最高人民检察署暂行组织条例”修正案和“各级地方人民检察署组织通则”草案的说明后，全体一致通过了“中华人民共和国人民法院暂行组织条例”、“中央人民政府最高人民检察署暂行组织条例”、“各级地方人民检察署组织通则”。会议并通过任免案及政务院提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
    出席这次会议的，有：主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府委员会委员李立三、何香凝、吴玉章、彭真、薄一波、聂荣臻、周恩来、董必武、赛福鼎、乌兰夫、刘格平、马寅初、陈云、马叙伦、郭沫若、高崇民、沈雁冰、陈叔通、司徒美堂、彭泽民、张治中、傅作义、李烛尘、章伯钧、张奚若、陈铭枢、谭平山、柳亚子、张东荪、龙云等共三十四人。
    列席这次会议的，有：中国人民政协全国委员会委员，最高人民法院副院长、委员，最高人民检察署副检察长、委员，人民革命军事委员会委员和中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官，中央人民政府政务院政务委员和所属各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，以及京津两市正副市长等共一百余人。
