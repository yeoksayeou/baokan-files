Title: All-China Youth Federation, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Send Telegram to Vietnamese Youth League and Other Organizations to Extend Congratulations
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st.

    All-China Youth Federation, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Send Telegram to Vietnamese Youth League and Other Organizations to Extend Congratulations
[Xinhua News Agency, 1st] September 2nd is the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth and the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram to the Vietnam Youth League and the Vietnam National Salvation Youth Association to extend congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: Vietnam Youth League, Vietnam National Salvation Youth Association:

    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, we extend our brotherly greetings to the heroic Vietnamese youth and people, and wish you greater victories in your national liberation cause. We firmly believe that the friendship and unity forged by the youth of China and Vietnam in the struggle will be further consolidated and developed in the common struggle against US imperialism's unilateral peace treaty with Japan, the rearmament of Japan, and the defense of peace in Asia and the world.

All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
September 1, 1951

### 全国青联、青年团中央致电越南青年联盟等团体祝贺

1951-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联、青年团中央致电越南青年联盟等团体祝贺
   【新华社一日讯】九月二日为越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念日。中华全国民主青年联合总会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会特电越南青年联盟、越南青年救国会致贺。电文如下：越南青年联盟越南青年救国会：
    值此越南民主共和国六周年纪念，我们谨向英勇的越南青年和人民致以兄弟般的敬礼，并祝你们的民族解放事业获得更大的胜利。我们深信中、越两国青年在战斗中结成的友谊与团结，将在反对美帝国主义片面对日媾和、重新武装日本及保卫亚洲与世界和平的共同斗争中愈益巩固与发展。中华全国民主青年联合总会中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会  一九五一年九月一日
