Title: Feng Wenbin, Wu Han, and Others Return to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese youth delegation to the World Youth and Student Peace Festival, deputy head Wu Han, and five other members, returned to the capital by plane on the afternoon of the 5th. Liao Chengzhi, chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Qian Sanqiang, vice chairman, Jiang Nanxiang, deputy secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and representatives from the All-China Students Federation and other units, totaling more than 60 people, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 冯文彬吴晗等返抵北京

1951-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    冯文彬吴晗等返抵北京
   【新华社五日讯】参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节的我国青年代表团团长冯文彬、副团长吴晗等一行七人，于五日下午乘飞机返抵首都。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、副主席钱三强，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔，及中华全国学生联合会等单位代表六十余人。
