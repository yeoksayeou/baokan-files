    Women in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region love their motherland and are actively preparing to organize themselves for production. They are looking at pictures in "People's Pictorial" showing women across the country actively engaged in production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古自治区的妇女热爱祖国，积极准备组织起来，进行生产。她们正在看“人民画报”上刊登的全国各地妇女积极生产的图片。
