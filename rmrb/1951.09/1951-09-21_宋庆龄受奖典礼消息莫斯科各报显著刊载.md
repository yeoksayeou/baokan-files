Title: News of Soong Ching Ling's Award Ceremony Prominently Published in Moscow Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1951-09-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: Today, all Moscow newspapers prominently published news of the award ceremony for Soong Ching Ling's "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize on their front pages, along with the full text of Soong Ching Ling's acceptance speech and her photograph. The newspaper *News* published an editorial entitled "Women are Active Fighters for Peace," stating: The heroic and staunch struggles of Soong Ching Ling, Madame Gordon, and Park Chung Ae, the recipients of the Stalin International Peace Prize, are lofty examples for all upright people fighting to defend international peace and friendship. Moscow Radio also broadcast the news of Soong Ching Ling's award today.

### 宋庆龄受奖典礼消息莫斯科各报显著刊载

1951-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄受奖典礼消息莫斯科各报显著刊载
    【新华社莫斯科二十日电】今日莫斯科各报都以首页显著地位登载宋庆龄接受“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金典礼的消息，宋庆龄在受奖典礼上的答词全文和她的照片。消息报以“妇女是争取和平的积极战士”为题发表社论说：宋庆龄、戈登夫人和朴正爱这几位斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人的英勇和坚强的斗争，对于一切为保卫国际和平与友谊而斗争的正直的人们是崇高的模样。莫斯科广播电台今日也广播了宋庆龄受奖的消息。
