Title: Our Ambassador to Pakistan, Han Nianlong, Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1951-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 12th: Our first ambassador to Pakistan, Han Nianlong, presented his credentials to the Governor-General of Pakistan, Nazimuddin, at 12:30 pm on September 10th. Those attending the ceremony included the Joint Secretary of the Pakistani Foreign Ministry (the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Pakistan is currently abroad) and the Director of Protocol. When Ambassador Han presented his credentials, he delivered a eulogy to Governor-General Nazimuddin, and Governor-General Nazimuddin delivered a reply. After the ceremony, Governor-General Nazimuddin hosted a banquet for Ambassador Han, and Pakistan's Ambassador to our country, Roza, was also present.

### 我驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙呈递国书

1951-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙呈递国书
    【新华社十二日讯】喀喇蚩消息：我国首任驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙已于九月十日十二时半向巴基斯坦总督纳齐穆丁呈递国书。参加典礼者有巴基斯坦外交部联合秘书（巴基斯坦外交部部长现在国外）暨交际司司长。韩大使呈递国书时曾向纳齐穆丁总督致颂词，纳齐穆丁总督致答词。礼成后，纳齐穆丁总督欢宴韩大使，在座者尚有巴基斯坦驻我国大使罗查。
