Title: Apei and His Entourage Arrive in Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1951-09-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 23rd. Lhasa dispatch: Apei, the chief plenipotentiary representative of the Tibetan local government, and his entourage arrived in Lhasa, the seat of the Tibetan local government, on the 12th of this month. They were warmly welcomed by over a thousand officials and staff members of the Tibetan local government.
    The Central Meteorological Observatory Announces the Lifting of the Typhoon Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. The Central Meteorological Observatory announced the lifting of the typhoon warning at 7:00 AM on the 22nd. The typhoon moved northwest from the east of Luzon Island. On the 21st, when it passed between Guangzhou and Hainan Island and then moved west, its intensity greatly decreased. At 2:00 AM on the 22nd, the typhoon had dissipated.

### 阿沛等一行抵拉萨

1951-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    阿沛等一行抵拉萨
    【新华社重庆二十三日电】拉萨讯：西藏地方政府首席全权代表阿沛等一行，已于本月十二日抵达西藏地方政府所在地——拉萨，受到西藏地方政府千余名官员和职员的热烈欢迎。
    中央气象台宣布解除台风警报
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央气象台二十二日七时宣布解除台风警报。按台风自吕宋岛的东边向西北进行，二十一日穿过广州与海南岛之间再向西时强度大减，二十二日二时台风已经消灭。
