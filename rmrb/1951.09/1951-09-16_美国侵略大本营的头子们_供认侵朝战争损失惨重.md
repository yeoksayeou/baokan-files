Title: Heads of the American Aggression Headquarters Admit to Heavy Losses in the War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The American imperialists have paid a huge price in blood for their aggressive adventure in Korea. The heads of the American aggression headquarters have recently had to issue yet another lament about this. According to Agence France-Presse (AFP) in Washington, the Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, Bradley, admitted in a radio address on the evening of the 10th that "The Korean War is the most costly and bloodiest war the United States has ever fought." He called on "the American people to provide enough plasma to their soldiers fighting in the Far East to save the wounded." AFP stated: "This call was jointly issued by Bradley, Marshall, and Ridgway."

### 美国侵略大本营的头子们  供认侵朝战争损失惨重

1951-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    美国侵略大本营的头子们
    供认侵朝战争损失惨重
    【新华社十五日讯】美帝国主义在朝鲜的侵略冒险中付出了巨大的血的代价，美国侵略大本营的头子们最近不得不对此发出又一次的哀鸣。据法新社华盛顿消息，美国参谋长联席会议主席布莱德雷在十日晚发表的广播演说中供认：“朝鲜战争是美国所进行的一次代价最大、流血最多的战争”，他呼吁“美国人民必须为他们在远东作战的士兵供给足够的血浆以拯救伤兵”。法新社称：“这个呼吁是布莱德雷、马歇尔和李奇微联名发出的。”
