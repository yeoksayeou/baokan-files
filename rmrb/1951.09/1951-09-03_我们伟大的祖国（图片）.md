Author:
Date: 1951-09-03

Our Great Motherland
Wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers are very concerned about the news from the Korean front. They resent the US imperialists' conspiracy to sabotage the armistice negotiations, and are determined to return to Korea after recovering. This is a scene of the wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteers recuperating in a hospital, listening attentively to the nurse reading the newspaper.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军的伤病员，非常关心朝鲜前线的消息，他们愤恨美帝国主义阴谋破坏停战谈判，决心在伤愈后重到朝鲜去。这是在某医院中疗养的志愿军伤病员静听护士读报的情形。
