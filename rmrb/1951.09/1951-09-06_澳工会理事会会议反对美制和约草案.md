Title: Australian Trades and Labour Council Meeting Opposes U.S.-Drafted Peace Treaty

Author:

Date: 1951-09-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney dispatch from TASS: Australian working people oppose the U.S.-drafted peace treaty with Japan. According to Melbourne Radio: The Australian Trades and Labour Council meeting, which opened in Melbourne on September 3rd, also opposed the U.S.-proposed draft. This meeting, representing one million workers, has decided to notify the United Nations, U.S. President Truman, and the Australian government that the meeting opposes the U.S.-drafted peace treaty with Japan because this draft will enable Japan to rearm.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th: London dispatch: The British magazine "New Statesman and Nation" published an editorial on August 31st, advocating delaying the date of signing the peace treaty with Japan so that the new China could participate in discussions on the new treaty. The editorial said: "The countries participating in the San Francisco conference will face a major test of political skill, and if they ignore the concrete fact of China's position in Asia, they will fall into an inextricable quagmire."

The editorial believes: "A reasonable basis for resolving the problem should be to postpone the signing of the peace treaty with Japan, allow China to join the United Nations, and agree to China's participation in discussions on the new treaty. Such discussions should include Korea and Asian countries, including those who may be unwilling to sign the current treaty." The editorial emphasized: "Washington cannot ignore this powerful opposition."

### 澳工会理事会会议反对美制和约草案

1951-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    澳工会理事会会议
    反对美制和约草案
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社悉尼讯：澳大利亚劳动人民都反对美制对日和约草案。据墨尔本广播：九月三日在墨尔本开幕的澳大利亚工会理事会会议也反对美国所建议的草案。这个代表一百万工人的会议已决定通知联合国、美国总统杜鲁门和澳大利亚政府说：该会议反对美制的对日和约草案，因为这个草案将使日本能够重新武装。 【新华社布拉格四日电】伦敦消息：英国“新政治家与民族”周刊在八月三十一日发表社论，主张延缓对日和约的签字日期，以便能使新中国参加关于新的和约的商讨。该社论说：“参加旧金山会议的各国将面临一次巨大的政治手腕的考验，如果他们忽视了中国在亚洲的地位这一具体事实，他们将陷于不可自拔的厄运的泥淖中。”
    社论认为：“一个合理的解决问题的基础，应该是延期签订对日和约，让中国加入联合国，并同意中国参加对新和约的讨论。这种讨论应该有朝鲜与亚洲国家包括那些可能不愿意在目前和约上签字的国家参加。”社论着重地说：“华盛顿不能无视这种强有力的反对力量。”
