    Our Great Motherland
    The sundial is an instrument invented in our country 2,500 years ago that uses the projection of sunlight to measure the time of day. This is the sundial placed in front of the Hall of Supreme Harmony in the Forbidden City in Beijing. It was made according to the traditional method of oblique sundials since the Han Dynasty. These young students, after learning the principles of the sundial from books, came here to observe and review.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    日晷是我国二千五百年以前所发明的利用日光的投影来测定时辰的一种仪器。这是放置于北京故宫太和殿前面的日晷。它是根据汉代以来斜置式日晷的传统制法所造的。这几位青年学生从书本上学习了日晷的原理之后，来到这里进行观察和复习。
