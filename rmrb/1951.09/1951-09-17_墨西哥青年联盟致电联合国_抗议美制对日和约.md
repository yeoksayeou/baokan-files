Title: Mexican Youth League Calls on UN to Protest US-Made Japanese Peace Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, 14th - The Mexican Youth League, with 90,000 members, has sent a telegram to the United Nations protesting the Japanese Peace Treaty and demanding that the five major powers conclude a genuine peace treaty.

The telegram stated: "This treaty, designed by the American ruling clique, shows that they want to use Japan as a new springboard in a third world war. The exclusion of countries most concerned with the existence of a democratic and free Japan, such as India, China, Burma, and the Soviet Union, from this treaty shows that the American plan is against the peace of the Asian people, and then against the peace of the whole world."

The telegram demanded that the United Nations condemn the Japanese Peace Treaty and cause the governments of the signatory countries to adopt "a just peace policy, an international friendship policy, abandoning those excuses that prevent the five major powers of the United States, the Soviet Union, Britain, People's China and France from concluding a genuine peace treaty."

### 墨西哥青年联盟致电联合国  抗议美制对日和约

1951-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥青年联盟致电联合国
    抗议美制对日和约
    【新华社十六日讯】电通社墨西哥城十四日讯：拥有九万盟员的墨西哥青年联盟致电联合国，抗议对日和约，要求五大国缔结真正的和平公约。
    电文说：“由美国统治集团所设计的这个条约，表示他们想利用日本作为第三次世界大战中的新跳板。印度、中国、缅甸、苏联这些最为关心一个民主自由的日本的存在的国家被这个条约排除在外，这表明美国的计划是反对亚洲人民的和平，接着就是反对全世界的和平。”
    电文要求联合国谴责对日和约，并使签字各国政府采取“一个公正的和平政策，国际友好政策，抛弃那些阻止美国、苏联、英国、人民中国和法国五大国缔结一个真正的和平公约的借口。”
