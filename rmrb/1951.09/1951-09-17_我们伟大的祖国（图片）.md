Author:
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    When constructing the sluice gates for the Runheji diversion sluice of the Huai River, the workers displayed a high degree of enthusiasm for labor. They persisted in working even in the 125-degree heat. This is a picture of the riveters installing the sluice gates, working intensely. They wrote a slogan on the sluice gate: "Deadline not yet reached; work already good. Chairman Mao heard it, very good, very good!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在进行修建淮河润河集分水闸的闸门时，工人们发挥了高度的劳动热情。他们曾在一二五度热日下坚持工作。这是按装闸门的铆钉工人正在紧张工作。他们在闸门上写了标语：“限期没到；工作已好。毛主席听了，很好，很好！”
