Title: Slogans for Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China, Released by the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author: 
Date: 1951-09-23
Page: 1

    Slogans for Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China, Released by the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    I. Celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China!
    II. Pay tribute to the people of the whole country who oppose imperialist aggression, eliminate feudal exploitation, eradicate the remaining counter-revolutionary forces, and build a new China!
    III. Pay tribute to the People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, Air Force, People's Public Security Forces, and People's Police!
    IV. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Volunteers who have achieved great victories on the Korean front!
    V. Pay tribute to the stretcher bearers, transport teams, medical teams, railway transportation personnel, rear service personnel, and battlefield cultural workers who support the Chinese People's Volunteers!
    VI. Pay tribute to the comrades who were wounded in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea!
    VII. Eternal glory to the martyrs who sacrificed themselves for the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea!
    VIII. Pay tribute to the government and people of our great ally, the bulwark of world peace and democracy, the Soviet Union!
    IX. Long live the great and unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union!
    X. Celebrate the great victory of the Korean people's war against aggression!
    XI. Pay tribute to the heroic Korean People's Army and the Korean people!
    XII. Pay tribute to the heroic Vietnamese People's Army and the Vietnamese people!
    XIII. Pay tribute to the Japanese people who are striving for independence, democracy, and peace!
    XIV. Pay tribute to the people of Asian countries such as India, Burma, Pakistan, Iran, Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines who oppose imperialist aggression!
    XV. Pay tribute to the people of our friendly nations who stand with us in opposing imperialism: the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Albania, and Mongolia!
    XVI. Long live the friendship between the people of the United States, Britain, France, Italy, and other countries and the Chinese people in the struggle to defend peace!
    XVII. Oppose U.S. imperialism's occupation of Taiwan and aggression against Korea!
    XVIII. Oppose the illegal U.S.-British imperialist treaty with Japan! Oppose U.S. imperialism's continued occupation of Japan and rearmament of Japan!
    XIX. People of the whole country, unite and strengthen the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and defeat U.S. aggression!
    XX. Strive to promote the patriotic pact movement!
    XXI. Strive to complete the patriotic donation plan!
    XXII. Young men and women, join the National Defense Forces and consolidate national defense!
    XXIII. Eradicate counter-revolutionaries and imperialist spies!
    XXIV. Complete land reform and thoroughly eliminate the feudal land system!
    XXV. Workers, work hard in production, strengthen the patriotic labor emulation movement, strengthen the unity of employees and workers, improve production technology, improve working conditions, increase the quantity of products, improve product quality, save raw materials, reduce costs, and ensure the completion of production plans!
    XXVI. Leaders of state-owned factories and enterprises, rely on workers to run factories and enterprises well, achieve democratic reforms within factories and enterprises, strengthen business management and unified leadership, and implement economic accounting!
    XXVII. Farmers, work hard in cultivation, strengthen the patriotic production increase movement, develop labor mutual assistance and rural cooperative undertakings, improve agricultural technology, build water conservancy projects, actively plant trees, and strive to further improve the level of agricultural production!
    XXVIII. State organ staff, work hard, study policies, strictly abide by state discipline, closely contact the masses, and strive to improve the work efficiency of state organs!
    XXIX. Young students, study hard, exercise, and be prepared to serve the motherland at any time!
    XXX. Women, break the shackles of feudal ideology, actively participate in labor production and political and cultural activities, and strive to fully realize women's equal rights with men in all aspects of political, economic, cultural education, and social life!
    XXXI. Educators, do a good job in education, use revolutionary thought and the spirit of patriotism to educate young people, and cultivate talents for national construction!
    XXXII. Scientific and technological workers and medical workers, strive to master advanced theories and technologies, closely integrate with reality, and serve the people and national construction!
    XXXIII. Literary, artistic, and film workers, grasp the literary and artistic direction of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers, and strive to create literary and artistic works with correct ideological content and high artistic level!
    XXXIV. Industrialists and businessmen, implement labor-capital consultation, improve business management, develop economic undertakings that are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood, support government laws and decrees, oppose the pursuit of exorbitant profits and hoarding for speculation, actively pay taxes, and actively participate in national construction!
    XXXV. Believers of all religions, carry forward the spirit of patriotism, eliminate imperialist influence, and participate in the front against imperialism and feudalism!
    XXXVI. All democratic parties, democratic personages, and people's organizations, consolidate and develop their own organizations, and strive to strengthen the people's democratic united front and fully realize the Common Program!
    XXXVII. All nationalities of the country, unite, and strive to develop the political, economic, transportation, cultural, educational, and health undertakings in minority areas, and strive to jointly oppose imperialist aggression and build an independent, unified, and great motherland!
    XXXVIII. Overseas Chinese, unite, love the motherland, and strive to defend your legitimate rights and interests!
    XXXIX. Long live the great unity of the Chinese people!
    XL. Long live the great unity of the Asian people!
    XLI. Long live the great unity of the people of the world!
    XLII. Long live the leader and teacher of the people of the world, Marshal Stalin!
    XLIII. Long live the great People's Republic of China!
    XLIV. Long live the Communist Party of China!
    XLV. Long live the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao!
    National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
        September 22, 1951

### 中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会发布  庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年的口号

1951-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会发布
    庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年的口号
    一、庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年！
    二、向反对帝国主义侵略、消灭封建剥削、肃清反革命残余势力、建设新中国的全国人民致敬！
    三、向中国人民解放军陆、空、海军和人民公安部队、人民警察致敬！
    四、向在朝鲜前线获得伟大胜利的中国人民志愿军致敬！
    五、向支援中国人民志愿军的担架队员、运输队员、医疗队员、铁路交通人员、后方勤务人员、战地文化工作者致敬！
    六、向为抗美援朝而负伤的同志们致敬！
    七、为抗美援朝而牺牲的烈士们永垂不朽！
    八、向我们伟大的盟邦、世界和平民主的堡垒苏联的政府和人民致敬！
    九、中苏两国伟大的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    十、庆祝朝鲜人民反侵略战争的伟大胜利！
    一一、向英勇的朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民致敬！
    一二、向英勇的越南人民军和越南人民致敬！
    一三、向争取独立、民主、和平的日本人民致敬！
    一四、向反对帝国主义侵略的印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、伊朗、印度尼西亚、马来亚、菲律宾等亚洲各国人民致敬！
    一五、向与我们共同反对帝国主义的友邦德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古的人民致敬！
    一六、美、英、法、意及其他各国人民和中国人民在保卫和平的斗争中的友谊万岁！
    一七、反对侵占台湾、侵略朝鲜的美帝国主义！
    一八、反对美英帝国主义的非法对日和约！反对继续占领日本和重新武装日本的美帝国主义！
    一九、全国人民团结起来，加强抗美援朝运动，打败美国的侵略！
    二十、努力推行爱国公约运动！
    二一、努力完成爱国捐献计划！
    二二、男女青年参加国防军、巩固国防！
    二三、肃清反革命分子和帝国主义间谍！
    二四、完成土地改革，彻底消灭封建土地制度！
    二五、工人们，努力生产，加强爱国劳动竞赛运动，加强职工团结，改进生产技术，改善工作条件，增加产品数量，提高产品质量，节约原料，减低成本，保证完成生产计划！
    二六、国营工厂、企业的领导者们，依靠工人，办好工厂、企业，实现工厂、企业内的民主改革，加强经营管理和统一领导，实行经济核算！
    二七、农民们，努力耕作，加强爱国丰产运动，发展劳动互助和农村合作事业，改进农业技术，兴修水利，积极植林，为更加提高农业生产水平而奋斗！
    二八、国家机关工作人员们，努力工作，学习政策，严守国家纪律，密切联系群众，为提高国家机关的工作效率而奋斗！
    二九、青年学生们，努力学习，锻炼身体，准备随时为祖国服务！
    三十、妇女们，打破封建思想的束缚，积极参加劳动生产和政治、文化活动，为彻底实现妇女在政治、经济、文化教育、社会生活等各方面与男子平等的权利而奋斗！
    三一、教育工作者们，作好教育工作，用革命思想和爱国主义的精神去教育青年，培养国家建设人材！
    三二、科学技术工作者和医务工作者们，努力掌握进步的理论和技术，密切结合实际，为人民和国家建设服务！
    三三、文学、艺术、电影工作者们，掌握为工农兵服务的文艺方向，为创造有正确思想内容和高度艺术水平的文艺作品而努力！
    三四、工商业家们，实行劳资协商，改善经营管理，发展有利于国计民生的经济事业，拥护政府法令，反对追逐暴利和高价居奇，踊跃缴纳税款，积极参加国家建设！
    三五、各宗教的信徒们，发扬爱国精神，清除帝国主义影响，参加反帝国主义反封建主义的战线！
    三六、各民主党派，各民主人士，各人民团体，巩固和发展自己的组织，为加强人民民主统一战线，彻底实现共同纲领而奋斗！
    三七、全国各民族团结起来，努力发展少数民族地区的政治、经济、交通和文化教育卫生事业，为共同反对帝国主义侵略，建设独立、统一的伟大的祖国而奋斗！
    三八、国外华侨团结起来，爱护祖国，为保卫自己的正当权益而奋斗！
    三九、中国人民大团结万岁！
    四十、亚洲人民大团结万岁！
    四一、世界人民大团结万岁！
    四二、全世界人民的领袖与导师斯大林大元帅万岁！
    四三、伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    四四、中国共产党万岁！
    四五、中国人民伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
        一九五一年九月二十二日
