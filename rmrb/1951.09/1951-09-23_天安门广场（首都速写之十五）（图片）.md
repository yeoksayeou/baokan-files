Title: Tiananmen Square (Sketches of the Capital, No. 15) (Picture)
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1951-09-23
Page: 3

    Tiananmen Square (Sketches of the Capital, No. 15)
    By Shao Yu
    Tiananmen Square,
    Seeing you is seeing the vastness and strength of the motherland!
    The red flag burns like a flame!
    The roar of peace and justice shakes heaven and earth!
    Here,
    Confidence and strength are surging!
    It is wall after wall of bronze and iron!
    Let the imperialist warmongers tremble!
    Let us sing for the present and future of our motherland!

### 天安门广场（首都速写之十五）（图片）
邵宇
1951-09-23
第3版()
专栏：

    天安门广场（首都速写之十五）
    邵宇作
    天安门广场，
    看到您也看到祖国的辽阔坚强！
    红旗像火焰般在燃烧！
    和平，正义的吼声震动着天地！
    在这里
    是信心和力量在汹涌！
    是一道又一道的铜壁铁墙！
    让帝国主义战争贩子发抖吧！
    让我们为祖国的现在和将来歌唱！
