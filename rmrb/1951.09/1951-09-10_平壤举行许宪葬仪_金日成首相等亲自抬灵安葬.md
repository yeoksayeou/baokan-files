Title: Pyongyang Holds Funeral for Heo Heon; Premier Kim Il Sung and Others Personally Carried the Casket for Burial

Author:
Date: 1951-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 9th. The funeral of Heo Heon, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, was held here on the 7th. Premier Kim Il Sung attended in person. Also attending the funeral were Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Park Heon-young and Hong Myung-hee, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Heo Ga-i and Lee Seung-yeop, Park Jeong-ae, Cabinet Ministers, and representatives from various democratic parties, social organizations, the People's Army, and people from all walks of life, totaling more than 300 people. Ambassadors and embassy representatives from the Soviet Union, China, and other People's Democratic countries to Korea also attended the funeral.

Amidst mournful music, Kim Il Sung, Kim Tu-bong, Park Heon-young, Heo Ga-i and others personally carried Heo Heon's coffin to be buried at Moranbong, a scenic area in Pyongyang.

### 平壤举行许宪葬仪  金日成首相等亲自抬灵安葬

1951-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤举行许宪葬仪
    金日成首相等亲自抬灵安葬
    【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜最高人民会议议长许宪葬仪于七日在此间举行。金日成首相亲临参加。参加葬仪的尚有最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁副首相朴宪永、洪命熹、朝鲜劳动党中央书记许嘉谊、李承烨、朴正爱、内阁各相、以及各民主政党、社会团体、人民军与各界人民的代表共三百多人。苏联、中国及各人民民主国家驻朝使节、使馆代表也参加了葬仪。
    在哀乐声中，金日成、金枓奉、朴宪永、许嘉谊等亲自把许宪的灵柩抬往平壤市的风景区牡丹峰安葬。
