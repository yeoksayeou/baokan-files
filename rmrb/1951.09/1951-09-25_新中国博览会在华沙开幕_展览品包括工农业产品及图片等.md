Title: New China Exhibition Opens in Warsaw, Exhibits Include Industrial and Agricultural Products and Photos
Author:
Date: 1951-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 22nd: The New China Exhibition opened in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, on the 19th. More than 250 people attended the opening ceremony, including Polish Vice-Chairman of the Parliament Barcikowski, Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski, Minister of Foreign Trade Kid, Minister of Culture and Art Dybowski, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Ochab, diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries to Poland, celebrities from the Polish science, culture, and art circles, and representatives from various mass organizations in Warsaw. Polish Minister of Foreign Trade Kid delivered a speech on behalf of the Polish government, pointing out that this exhibition will enable the Polish people to have a deeper understanding of New China and promote the further development of cultural and economic cooperation between China and Poland. General Peng Mingzhi, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, said in his speech that he firmly believed that the Polish people would gain a more concrete understanding of the great achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong in politics, economy, and culture through these exhibits. The guests who attended the opening ceremony unanimously praised China's exhibits.

Note: The exhibits at the New China Exhibition were previously exhibited at the Leipzig Fair in the German Democratic Republic in March and the 52nd International Fair held in Czechoslovakia in May this year, and were warmly welcomed by the people of Germany and Czechoslovakia. The exhibits include agricultural, industrial, mining, and photographic sections. The agricultural section includes China's premium tea, soybeans, pig bristles, egg products, sausage casings, and other specialties. The industrial and mining section includes various minerals, industrial products, and handicrafts.

### 新中国博览会在华沙开幕  展览品包括工农业产品及图片等

1951-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    新中国博览会在华沙开幕
    展览品包括工农业产品及图片等
   【新华社二十二日讯】华沙消息：新中国博览会于十九日在波兰首都华沙开幕。参加开幕典礼的有波兰议会副主席巴尔西科夫斯基、外交部长斯克列杰夫斯基、对外贸易部长基德、文化与艺术部长迪鲍夫斯基、波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记奥查布、苏联及各人民民主国家驻波兰外交使节、波兰科学、文化和艺术界名流，以及华沙各群众团体的代表等二百五十余人。波兰对外贸易部长基德代表波兰政府致词，他指出：这次博览会将使波兰人民更加深刻地认识新中国，并促进波中两国文化和经济合作的进一步发展。我国驻波兰大使彭明治将军在讲话中，表示深信波兰人民通过这些展览品，将更加具体地认识到中国人民在毛泽东主席的领导下，在政治、经济与文化等方面所获得的巨大成就。参加开幕典礼的来宾们一致对我国的展览品赞美不已。
   按：新中国博览会上的展览品，曾先后在今年三月和五月间，在德意志民主共和国莱比锡博览会和捷克斯洛伐克举办的第五十二届国际博览会上展出，受到了德、捷人民的热烈欢迎。展览品包括农业、工矿及图片等部分。农业部分中有我国上等茶叶、大豆、猪鬃、蛋品、肠衣等特产。工矿部分有各种矿物、工业产品和工艺品。
