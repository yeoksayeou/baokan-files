Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Warning: Shantou, Hong Kong, and Guangzhou Areas to Expect Wind and Rain
Author:
Date: 1951-09-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 7:00 PM on the 19th: At 2:00 PM on the 19th, the typhoon was located southeast by east of Dongsha Island (i.e., 20 degrees north latitude and 119 degrees east longitude), moving in a northwest direction at a speed of 43 li per hour. Within 24 hours, the Shantou, Hong Kong, and Guangzhou areas will experience wind and rain.

### 中央气象台发出台风警报  汕头香港广州一带将有风雨

1951-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发出台风警报
    汕头香港广州一带将有风雨
    【新华社十九日讯】中央气象台十九日下午七时发出台风警报：十九日下午二时，台风的位置在东沙岛的东南偏东（即北纬二十度东经一百一十九度）向西北方向进行，每小时移动四十三华里。二十四小时内，汕头、香港、广州一带将有风雨。
