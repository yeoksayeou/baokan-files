Title: The People of the Capital Actively Prepare to Celebrate the Second National Day; A Grand Military Parade Ceremony Will Be Held in Front of Tiananmen on National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-09-20
Page: 1

    The People of the Capital Actively Prepare to Celebrate the Second National Day
    A Grand Military Parade Ceremony Will Be Held in Front of Tiananmen on National Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, 18th] People from all walks of life in the capital will enthusiastically celebrate the second National Day of the People's Republic of China on October 1st this year. Representatives from the Central People's Government and various agencies and organizations in Beijing held a preparatory meeting yesterday to celebrate the second National Day, deciding to hold a grand military parade ceremony and celebration parade in Tiananmen Square on National Day. Those invited to attend the ceremony will include specially invited international guests, combat heroes from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, people's representatives from the revolutionary old base areas in the South and North, labor models from public and private industries, agricultural labor models, Huai River harnessing labor models, representatives from various ethnic minorities in the country, and overseas Chinese returning for sightseeing.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 18th] The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government is issuing a national emblem stamp to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, which will be available for sale starting on National Day. This national emblem stamp is a rectangular shape, with the national emblem of our country as the main body, backed by a light yellow background pattern, and surrounded by a meander pattern border. The face values are 100 yuan, 200 yuan, 400 yuan, 500 yuan, and 800 yuan. The sales period will last until the end of March 1952.

### 首都人民积极筹备庆祝第二届国庆节  国庆日将在天安门前举行盛大阅兵典礼

1951-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    首都人民积极筹备庆祝第二届国庆节
    国庆日将在天安门前举行盛大阅兵典礼
    【新华社十八日讯】首都各界人民将在今年十月一日热烈地庆祝中华人民共和国第二届国庆节。中央人民政府和北京市各机关、团体代表于昨日召开庆祝第二届国庆节的筹备工作会议，决定于国庆日在天安门广场举行盛大的阅兵典礼和庆祝游行。届时参加观礼的，有特邀的国际贵宾，中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军的战斗英雄、南方和北方各革命老根据地的人民代表、公私营工业的劳动模范、农业劳动模范、治淮劳动模范、国内各少数民族的代表和华侨归国观光团等。
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府邮电部为庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年，发行国徽邮票一种，自国庆日起开始发售。此项国徽邮票为直长方形，以我国国徽为主体，背衬淡黄色地纹，四周以回纹为边框。面值分一百元、二百元、四百元、五百元、八百元五种。发售期限至一九五二年三月底止。
