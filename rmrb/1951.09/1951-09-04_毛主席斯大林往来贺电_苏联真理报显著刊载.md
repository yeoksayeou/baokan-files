Title: Chairman Mao and Stalin Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams, Prominently Published in Soviet Pravda
Author:
Date: 1951-09-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd: Pravda published on the 3rd on the front page in a prominent position the exchange of congratulatory telegrams between Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan.

专栏：

    毛主席斯大林往来贺电
