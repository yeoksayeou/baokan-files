Title: "Be Good Cadres of the People!" A Letter to All University Graduates from Students from North China Assigned to Work in Inner Mongolia

Author: 
Date: 1951-09-03
Page: 2

    "Be Good Cadres of the People!"
    From Students from North China Assigned to Work in Inner Mongolia
    To All University Graduates of 1951:
    We are the first group of 44 students from North China assigned to work in Inner Mongolia. The Inner Mongolia Personnel Bureau, following the principle of "suitable for national needs, taking into account the consistency of learning and application," has assigned us our work. We deeply feel the government's care for us.
    We students from North China, along with the people of the whole country, have received the education of the great Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic movement, and after graduation, we went through the study in the summer study group. We have a clearer understanding of the greatness and loveliness of our motherland and the arduous tasks that our youth should undertake in the great construction cause of the motherland. We assure you that we will always humbly learn, improve our political consciousness, delve into our work, overcome difficulties, and under the leadership of our wise and great leader Chairman Mao Zedong, strive to build a new Inner Mongolia and strive to be good cadres of the people. We hope that all university graduates of 1951, in different regions and different jobs, will contribute our strength for the happiness and beautiful and brilliant future of our motherland, and for our Chairman Mao!

### “做人民的好干部！”华北分配到内蒙工作的同学  给全国高等学校毕业生的信

1951-09-03
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    “做人民的好干部！”
    华北分配到内蒙工作的同学
    给全国高等学校毕业生的信全国一九五一年高等学校毕业同学们：
    我们是华北区分配到内蒙工作的第一批到达内蒙的四十四个同学。内蒙人事局按照“适合国家需要，照顾学用一致”的原则，分配了我们的工作。我们深深感到政府对我们的爱护。
    我们华北区的同学与全国人民一起，受了伟大的抗美援朝爱国主义的教育，毕业后又经过在暑期学习团的学习。我们更清楚地认识了祖国的伟大可爱和我们青年在祖国伟大建设事业中所应担当的艰巨任务。我们向你们保证，今后永远要虚心学习，提高政治觉悟，钻研工作，克服困难，在我们英明伟大的领袖毛泽东主席领导下，为建设新内蒙而奋斗，为争取做人民的好干部而奋斗。我们希望全国一九五一年高等学校毕业同学们，在不同地区，不同工作岗位上，为了我们祖国的幸福美好及光辉灿烂的未来，为了我们的毛主席而贡献出我们的力量！  赵思贵  周莲馚  叶  喜  窦伯菊  王伦平  梁仲仁  朱锡沅  徐尚荣  何文阁  李  春  展毓彭  程瑞华  方天棋  沈克明  李学海  侯培兰  赵哲瑞  薛明浚  霍宏燮  毛世錡  骆延祜  山  崑  吴乾初  周领群  高鸿昇  宋雨普  刘正和  尚  钗  施庆和  徐越化  陈国干  朱畅功  金  农  林同墉  李文显  周百成  韩晋绅  李  炳  张景堂  康振文  侯仲魁  郭寿钧  王公廉  万国志
