Title: US Intensifies Control of Japanese War Industry
Author:
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th, TASS, September 15th: The American military, with the assistance of the Yoshida puppet government, is accelerating the militarization of Japan.

    The Japanese newspaper "Yomiuri Shimbun" reported that Yoshida's chief advisor, Shirasu Jiro, held several meetings with US Assistant Secretary of the Army, Chiang Sheng, in San Francisco on the issue of arming Japan.

    American military equipment plans stipulate that the first priority is to more rapidly develop Japan's war industry.

    According to "Nihon Keizai Shimbun," previously important war industry companies -- Mitsubishi Heavy Industries and Nakajima Aircraft Company -- are now extremely eager to produce special military equipment. The United States wants to take measures to revive these two companies. They also possess wartime production capacity and the mechanical engineering personnel and technology necessary for war production.

    The "Yomiuri Shimbun" reported that the US military logistics department stationed in Japan inquired with four larger companies: Komatsu Company, East Japan Heavy Industries Company, West Japan Heavy Industries Company, and Fuji Industries Company, asking if they could manufacture $5 million worth of parts for "heavy machinery" used by the US military in Korea, Japan, and Southeast Asia.

    According to "Mainichi Shimbun," the US Air Force will use the airbases at Chitose, Misawa, Tachikawa, Kisarazu, Atsugi, Yokota, Kakamigahara, Itami, Iwakuni, and Itazuke, according to the so-called US-Japan "Security Treaty."

### 美国加紧控制日本战争工业

1951-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    美国加紧控制日本战争工业
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社十五日讯：美国军方在吉田傀儡政府的协助下，正加速使日本军国主义化。
    日本报纸“读卖新闻”报道，吉田的首席顾问白洲次郎，在旧金山时曾就武装日本的问题与美国陆军部助理部长江生举行了几次会谈。
    美国的军备计划规定首先要更迅速地发展日本的战争工业。
    据“日本经济新闻”说，以前的重要战争工业公司——三菱重工业公司和中岛飞机公司，现在极欲生产特别军备。美国想采取措施，使这两家公司复活。它们还拥有战时生产能力和进行战争生产所必需的机械工程人员和技术人员。
    “读卖新闻”报道，驻日美军后勤部询问过四家较大的公司：小松公司、东日本重工业公司、西日本重工业公司和富士产业公司，问它们是否能为在朝鲜、日本和东南亚的美军所使用的“重型机器”制造价值五百万美元的零件。
    据“每日新闻”说，根据所谓美日“安全条约”，美国空军将使用千岁、三泽、立川、木更津、厚木、横田、各务原、伊丹、岩国、板付这些空军基地。
