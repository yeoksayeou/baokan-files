Title: TASS is Authorized to Declare: Denouncing Ridgeway Headquarters and American and British Newspapers for Spreading Rumors
Author:
Date: 1951-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: TASS, August 8th dispatch: In the past few days, the press office of General Ridgeway's headquarters in Tokyo and certain American and British newspapers, acting on orders from certain quarters, have spread rumors that there are large numbers of white Soviet troops in North Korea, etc.

TASS is authorized to declare: All these and similar reports are fabrications from beginning to end, disseminated to deceive world public opinion.

### 塔斯社奉命声明  斥李奇微总部及美英报纸造谣

1951-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    塔斯社奉命声明
    斥李奇微总部及美英报纸造谣
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社八日电：在过去几天内，东京李奇微将军总部新闻处和若干美英报纸，奉某方面的命令散布谣言，说在北朝鲜有大批白种苏军等等。
    塔斯社奉命声明：所有这些以及类似的消息从头至尾都是虚构的，是为了欺骗世界舆论而散布的。
