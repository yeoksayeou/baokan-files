Title: US Chicago Veterans Demonstrate, Demanding an End to the War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 30th (TASS): "The Daily Worker" reports that veterans of World War II held a parade and rally in Chicago under the slogan "Stop the Korean War Immediately!" Demonstrators held many signs demanding the immediate conclusion of a Korean armistice agreement, a five-power conference, the release of imprisoned peace fighters, the abolition of discrimination against black people, and an end to wage freeze policies. Tens of thousands of residents gathered on the streets of Chicago's South Side as the parade passed. The demonstrators distributed leaflets calling for participation in the struggle for peace and postcards addressed to Truman demanding peace.

After the parade, a rally was held in Washington Park. Audal, secretary of the Chicago Labor Unity Committee, said in a speech at the rally: "For those who make fortunes from war, the luckiest day is the day negotiations are hindered. But the sincere demand of the American people, veterans and non-veterans, black and white, people of all faiths and political views, is: to ensure the continuation of negotiations." Jones, chairman of the district committee of the "American Women for Peace Association," also delivered a speech at the rally, saying, "We hope that the Korean peace talks will continue and that the war actions will stop."

### 美国芝加哥退伍军人示威  要求停止侵朝战争

1951-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美国芝加哥退伍军人示威
    要求停止侵朝战争
    【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社纽约三十日讯：“工人日报”报道，第二次世界大战退伍军人在“立即停止朝鲜战争！”的口号下在芝加哥举行游行示威和大会。示威者手持许多标语牌，要求立即缔结朝鲜停战协定，召开五大国会议、释放被投入监狱的和平战士、废止对黑人的歧视与停止工资冻结政策。在游行队伍通过的芝加哥南部的大街上聚集了千千万万的居民。示威者向他们散发号召参加争取和平斗争的传单与写给杜鲁门要求和平的明信片。
    游行完毕以后，接着在华盛顿公园举行了大会。芝加哥劳工团结委员会书记奥达尔在大会上讲演说：“对于那些靠战争发财的人们来说，最走运的日子是谈判受到阻碍的一天。但是美国人民、退伍军人与非退伍军人、黑人与白人、持有各种信仰与政见的人们的衷心要求是：保证谈判的继续进行”。“美国妇女争取和平协会”区会主席琼斯也在大会上发表演讲说：“我们希望朝鲜和谈继续进行并停止战争行动。”
