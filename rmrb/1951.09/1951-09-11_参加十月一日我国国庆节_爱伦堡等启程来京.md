Title: Ehrenburg and Others Depart for Beijing to Participate in China's National Day on October 1st
Author:
Date: 1951-09-11
Page: 1

    To Participate in China's National Day on October 1st
    Ehrenburg and Others Depart for Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th) The Soviet writer Ilya Ehrenburg, fighter for the world peace movement, and his wife, and the Chilean poet Pablo Neruda and his wife, left by train for Beijing on the evening of the 9th to participate in the National Day of the People's Republic of China. Those who saw them off at the station included prominent figures from Soviet cultural circles and Ge Baoquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union.

### 参加十月一日我国国庆节  爱伦堡等启程来京

1951-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    参加十月一日我国国庆节
    爱伦堡等启程来京
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】世界和平运动斗士苏联作家爱伦堡及其夫人和智利诗人聂鲁达及其夫人，于九日晚乘火车赴北京，参加中华人民共和国国庆节。到站欢送者有苏联文化界名流及我国驻苏大使馆戈宝权参事。
