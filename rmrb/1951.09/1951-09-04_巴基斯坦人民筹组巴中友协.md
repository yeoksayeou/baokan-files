Title: Pakistanis Preparing to Form Pakistan-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1951-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 2nd. The Pakistan-China Friendship Association is being actively prepared. A preparatory committee headed by Mr. Shalin Ansari is responsible for drafting the constitution and registering members.
