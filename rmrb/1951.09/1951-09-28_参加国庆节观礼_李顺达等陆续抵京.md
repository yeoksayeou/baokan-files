Title: Attending National Day Observance: Li Shunda and Others Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1951-09-28
Page: 1
    
    Attending National Day Observance
    Li Shunda and Others Arrive in Beijing One After Another
    [Xinhua News Agency, 27th] Agricultural labor models participating in the National Day celebrations have been arriving in Beijing successively since the 25th. As of now, 27 labor models from the Southwest, Central South, East China, Inner Mongolia, Shanxi, Chahar, and Pingyuan regions have reported their arrival. Li Shunda, the famous labor model from Shanxi Province who initiated the national patriotic production increase campaign; Lü Hongbin, the labor model from Shandong Province known for intensive cultivation; Qiu Guanxing, the labor model from Zhejiang Province specializing in pest control; Yang Tiancai, the labor model from Hunan Province who achieved high cotton yields and improved agricultural implements; Su Dianxuan, the famous labor model from Henan Province; Zhang Yushan, the labor model from the Southwest region; and Sandoubu, the pastoral labor model from Inner Mongolia who achieved the highest horse breeding rate, have all reported their arrival. When the labor models arrived in Beijing, Yang Xiansheng, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, and others personally welcomed them at the railway station.

### 参加国庆节观礼  李顺达等陆续抵京

1951-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加国庆节观礼
    李顺达等陆续抵京
    【新华社二十七日讯】参加国庆节观礼的农业劳动模范，已于二十五日起陆续抵京。目前报到的已有西南、中南、华东、内蒙、山西、察哈尔、平原等地的二十七位劳动模范。发起全国爱国丰产竞赛运动的山西省著名劳动模范李顺达、山东省精耕细作的劳动模范吕洪斌、浙江省治虫劳动模范邱关兴、湖南省棉花丰产和改良农具有成绩的劳动模范杨天才、河南省的著名劳动模范苏殿选，西南区劳动模范张玉山和内蒙古创造马匹最高繁殖率的牧业劳动模范散都布等都已报到。劳动模范们抵京时，中央人民政府农业部杨显东副部长等曾亲到车站欢迎。
