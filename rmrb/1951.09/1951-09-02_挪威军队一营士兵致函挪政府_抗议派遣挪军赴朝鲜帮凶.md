Title: Norwegian Army Battalion Soldiers Write to Norwegian Government Protesting Dispatch of Norwegian Troops to Aid Aggression in Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-09-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st - According to a Reuters dispatch from Oslo on August 29th as reported by L'Humanité: A newspaper in Trondheim reported that soldiers from a Norwegian army battalion stationed in northern Norway have petitioned the Norwegian Parliament and the Norwegian government, protesting the dispatch of Norwegian troops to Korea.
    The signatories to the protest demanded a referendum on the issue. They declared: "We protest against the government's decision to send troops outside Norway to wage war, whether in the name of the United Nations or otherwise."

### 挪威军队一营士兵致函挪政府  抗议派遣挪军赴朝鲜帮凶

1951-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威军队一营士兵致函挪政府
    抗议派遣挪军赴朝鲜帮凶
    【新华社布拉格一日电】据人道报所载路透社奥斯陆八月二十九日电讯：德琅索的一个报纸报道说：驻在挪威北部的一营挪威军队的士兵，上书挪威议会与挪威政府，抗议派遣挪威军队到朝鲜。
    在抗议书上签名的人，要求在这个问题上举行公民投票。他们宣称：“我们抗议政府决定派军队至挪威境外进行战争，不论这种战争是在联合国的名义下或是在其他的名义下进行的。”
