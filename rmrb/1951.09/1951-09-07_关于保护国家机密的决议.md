    Resolution on Protecting State Secrets
    (Adopted at the 25th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on July 19, 1951)
    The National Conference of Secretaries-Generals of Provincial and Municipal CPPCC Committees unanimously resolved that CPPCC organs at all levels throughout the country should strengthen the work of protecting state secrets, and in accordance with the central government's regulations on protecting secrets, conduct publicity and education among staff members of democratic parties, people's organizations, and democratic figures from all walks of life. In light of specific circumstances, they should formulate confidentiality rules or confidentiality covenants for the organs, and strictly protect state secrets in order to achieve the goal of consolidating the people's democratic dictatorship.

### 关于保护国家机密的决议

1951-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    关于保护国家机密的决议
    （一九五一年七月十九日中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第二十五次会议通过）
    全国省、市协商委员会秘书长会议，一致决议在全国各地协商机关应加强保护国家机密的工作，并根据中央人民政府的保密法令，向各民主党派，人民团体的工作人员及各界民主人士进行宣传教育，结合具体情况制定机关保密规则或保密公约，严格保护国家机密，以达到巩固人民民主专政的目的。
