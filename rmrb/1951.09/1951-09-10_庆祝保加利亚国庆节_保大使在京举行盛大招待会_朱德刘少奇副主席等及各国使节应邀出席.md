Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Holds Grand Reception in Beijing to Celebrate National Day; Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries Attend Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1951-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th—September 9th is the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Petkov, held a grand reception in Beijing.

Invited to attend were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, as well as He Xiangning, Xu Teli, Ma Xulun, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Peng Zemin, Zhang Xiruo, Liu Yazi, Chen Yu, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Luo Longji, Shao Lizi, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Lei Renmin, Liao Chengzhi, Feng Wenbin, Xiao Hua and other heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, totaling over 300 people.

Diplomatic envoys to China from various countries who were invited to attend included: Ambassador of Czechoslovakia Weiskopf, Ambassador of Romania Rudenko, Ambassador of India Panikkar, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic König, Representative of Vietnam with the rank of Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Sweden Söderblom, Minister of Denmark Munk, Minister of Switzerland Renzoli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy Fedlin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isha Mahdi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy Ahmed Ali, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy Rabdan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy U Bya, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy Fekete, Counselor of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, and staff members of various embassies.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝保加利亚国庆节  保大使在京举行盛大招待会  朱德刘少奇副主席等及各国使节应邀出席

1951-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝保加利亚国庆节
    保大使在京举行盛大招待会
    朱德刘少奇副主席等及各国使节应邀出席
    【新华社九日讯】九月九日是保加利亚人民共和国国庆节，保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使彼得科夫特在京举行盛大招待会。
    应邀出席的有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若，以及何香凝、徐特立、马叙伦、沈雁冰、陈叔通、司徒美堂、彭泽民、张奚若、柳亚子、陈郁、李书城、梁希、罗隆基、邵力子、章汉夫、伍修权、雷任民、廖承志、冯文彬、萧华等中央人民政府各部门首长，各民主党派和各人民团体负责人及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等三百余人。
    各国驻华外交使节应邀出席的有：捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、越南大使衔代表黄文欢、瑞典大使赛德勃隆、丹麦公使穆克、瑞士公使任佐立、苏联大使馆临时代办费德林、印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办阿默德·艾礼、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、蒙古大使馆临时代办拉布丹、缅甸大使馆临时代办吴辟、匈牙利大使馆临时代办费凯尔特、朝鲜大使馆参赞崔英和各国使馆人员。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
