Title: Song Qingling Hosts a Banquet for Ehrenburg and Neruda; Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Others Invited to Accompany

Author: 
Date: 1951-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Song Qingling, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," hosted a banquet tonight for Ilya Ehrenburg, a member of the "For Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize Committee and famous Soviet writer, and the Chilean poet Pablo Neruda. Invited to accompany them were Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Li Jishen, Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhizhiang, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Li Liuru, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression Guo Moruo, Vice Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Ningyi, Vice Presidents of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao and Xu Guangping, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Liao Chengzhi, Secretary of the China New Democratic Youth League Feng Wenbin, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Mao Dun and Zhou Yang, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, and more than 80 heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and responsible persons of people's organizations. Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin was also invited to attend. The banquet proceeded in a cheerful and friendly atmosphere.

### 宋庆龄欢宴爱伦堡聂鲁达  朱德副主席周恩来总理等应邀作陪

1951-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄欢宴爱伦堡聂鲁达
    朱德副主席周恩来总理等应邀作陪
    【新华社十九日讯】“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖者宋庆龄于今晚欢宴“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会委员、苏联名作家爱伦堡和智利诗人聂鲁达。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理黄炎培，最高人民法院副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长李六如，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，副主席彭真、陈叔通，中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、许广平，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，中国新民主主义青年团书记冯文彬，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾、周扬，北京市副市长张友渔以及中央人民政府各部门首长和人民团体负责人等八十余人。应邀出席者尚有苏联驻华大使罗申。宴会在欢愉友好的气氛中进行。
