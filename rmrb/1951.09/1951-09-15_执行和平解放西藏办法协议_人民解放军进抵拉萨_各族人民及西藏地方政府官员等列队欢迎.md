Title: Implementing the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet: People's Liberation Army Reaches Lhasa, People of All Ethnic Groups and Tibetan Local Government Officials Line Up to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1951-09-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 14th: Lhasa News: The vanguard unit of a certain People's Liberation Army unit, implementing the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, arrived in Lhasa on the 9th of this month, and was warmly welcomed by approximately 30,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life, including Tibetans, Han Chinese, and Hui people. As early as ten days ago, the residents of Lhasa and the Tibetan local government had prepared food and lodging for the entering People's Liberation Army. When the People's Liberation Army entered the city, people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life lined up to welcome them. Among those welcoming were officials from the Tibetan local government, monks and laypeople from the three major monasteries of Drepung, Sera, and Ganden, as well as the Tibetan local army. Welcoming monks and lay officials presented Hada scarves, flowers, and other gifts to Commander Wang Qimei of the unit.

### 执行和平解放西藏办法协议  人民解放军进抵拉萨  各族人民及西藏地方政府官员等列队欢迎

1951-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    执行和平解放西藏办法协议
    人民解放军进抵拉萨
    各族人民及西藏地方政府官员等列队欢迎
    【新华社重庆十四日电】拉萨讯：执行和平解放西藏办法协议开往西藏的人民解放军某部先遣部队，已于本月九日进抵拉萨，受到当地藏族、汉族、回族等各民族各界人民约三万人的热烈欢迎。早在十天以前，拉萨城内的居民及西藏地方政府即为入城的解放军准备好了粮食和宿营地。当解放军入城时，各民族各界人民列队欢迎，在欢迎的行列中有西藏地方政府官员和哲蚌、色拉、噶丹三大寺庙的僧俗人员，以及西藏地方的军队。欢迎的僧俗官员纷纷向该部队王其梅司令员敬献哈达和鲜花等礼品。
