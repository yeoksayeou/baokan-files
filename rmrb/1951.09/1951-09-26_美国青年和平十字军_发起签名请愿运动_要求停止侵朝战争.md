Title: American Youth for Peace Crusade Launches Petition Drive Demanding End to War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: According to a dispatch from TASS, New York: The American Youth for Peace Crusade last week launched a petition drive to sign a telegram calling for a ceasefire in Korea. The telegram requests President Truman, United Nations Secretary-General Trygve Lie, and United Nations Commander-in-Chief Ridgway to seek an immediate ceasefire in Korea and to open all avenues for peace negotiations, even going beyond military negotiations if necessary, in order to restore armistice talks and ensure their successful conclusion. The telegram warns: "We, the American youth, declare that we will not allow our hopes for peace to be shattered."

Glasgow, chairman of the American Youth for Peace Crusade, stated that as of September 20th, over one hundred youth leaders had signed the telegram, and signatures are being added daily.

The Washington State Pension Union recently unanimously passed a "Program for the Struggle for Peace" at its 14th Annual Meeting, calling for a petition drive demanding a ceasefire in Korea, the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact, and the defense of the renowned African-American scholar and peace leader Dr. W.E.B. Du Bois.

### 美国青年和平十字军  发起签名请愿运动  要求停止侵朝战争

1951-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    美国青年和平十字军
    发起签名请愿运动
    要求停止侵朝战争
    【新华社二十五日讯】据电通社纽约讯：美国青年和平十字军上周发起在一封呼吁朝鲜停火的电报上签名的运动。这封电报要求杜鲁门总统、联合国秘书长赖伊和联合国军总司令李奇微设法立即在朝鲜停火，并开放所有通往和平谈判的门路，遇必要时甚至在军事谈判以外进行努力，俾恢复休战谈判，而务使谈判胜利结束。电文警告说：“我们美国青年声明，我们决不容许打破我们对和平所抱的希望。”
    美国青年和平十字军主席格拉斯哥称：截至九月二十日止，已有一百多名青年领袖在这封电报上签名，而且每天都有人签名。
    华盛顿州养老金领取者联合会最近在其第十四届年会上，一致通过一项“为争取和平而斗争的纲领”，号召进行一次要求朝鲜停火、缔结五大国和平公约及保卫著名美国黑人学者与和平领袖杜波依斯博士的请愿运动。
