Title: Over a Hundred Volunteer Army Representatives Return Home to Participate in National Day Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1951-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 21st: Over a hundred combat hero representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who are to participate in the National Day celebrations in Beijing, have returned home from the Korean front in batches and arrived in Shenyang. Upon returning home, the representatives received a warm welcome and hospitality from people from all walks of life in the Northeast region. On the 21st, the representatives reported on the heroic deeds of the Volunteer Army on the Korean front to various institutions in Shenyang, People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Shenyang, and representatives from all walks of life. At 5 p.m. on the same day, the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Northeast People's Government, and the Northeast Military Region jointly hosted a banquet for all the representatives.

The representatives will depart for Beijing in the coming days.

### 志愿军代表百余人返国参加国庆观礼

1951-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

     志愿军代表百余人返国参加国庆观礼
    【新华社沈阳二十一日电】中国人民志愿军赴京参加国庆节观礼代表战斗英雄一百多人，已于日前自朝鲜前线先后返国，到达沈阳。代表们返国后，受到东北区各界人民的热烈欢迎与招待。二十一日代表们曾分向沈阳各机关、人民解放军驻沈阳部队和各界代表报告志愿军在朝鲜前线的英勇事迹。同日下午五时，中共中央东北局、东北人民政府、东北军区联合欢宴全体代表。
    代表们将于日内启程赴京。
