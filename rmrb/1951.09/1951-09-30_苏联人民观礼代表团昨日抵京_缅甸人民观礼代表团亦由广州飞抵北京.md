Title: Soviet People's Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Burmese People's Delegation Also Arrives in Beijing from Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1951-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th—The Soviet People's Delegation, invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, the China Peace Committee, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles to attend our National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing today. Those who greeted them at the airport included: Shao Lizi and Cao Jinghua, directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Lai Yali, Deputy Secretary-General, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, Zhang Xiruo, Standing Committee member, Zhang Bojun, member, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Qian Sanqiang and Sha Qianli, Vice Chairmen, Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department, Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government. The Hungarian People's Delegation headed by István Dobi also went to the airport to welcome them. Soviet Ambassador Roshchin and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.
   Xinhua News Agency, September 29th—The Burmese People's Delegation, invited by the China Peace Committee, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All- China Democratic Women's Federation, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles to attend our National Day celebrations, flew to Beijing from Guangzhou this afternoon. Abbas, a member of the Indian Goodwill Visiting Delegation, also arrived on the same plane. Those who greeted them at the airport included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, Zhang Xiruo, Standing Committee member, Zhang Bojun, member, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Jiebo, Executive Committee member, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Qian Sanqiang and Sha Qianli, Vice Chairmen, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department, Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Sha Kefu, Secretary, and Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government. U Bya, First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy, and Kumar, Third Secretary of the Indian Embassy, also welcomed them at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th—The list of the Soviet People's Delegation invited to attend our National Day celebrations is as follows:
    Leader: Solovyov, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
    Members: Yakovlev, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Obalin, Member of the Soviet Peace Committee, Member of the World Peace Council, and Chairman of the Political and Scientific Knowledge Popularization Association. Yunusov, Director of the Chemical Institute of the Uzbek Academy of Sciences, Professor of Tashkent State Central Asian University. Kabalevsky, composer, Honored Artist of the Russian Republic, Stalin Prize winner. Trofimenko, Chairman of the Soviet Cultural and Educational Workers' Union, Member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. Zoteyeva (female), Member of the Central Committee of the Georgian Communist Youth League, Member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Communist Youth League. Marinina (female), Hero of Socialist Labor, Stalin Prize winner.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th—The list of the Burmese People's Delegation invited to attend our National Day celebrations is as follows:
    Leader: Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, writer, historian.
    Deputy Leader: Thakin Lun, Chairman of the Burma Peace Congress,
        Chairman of the All Burma Trade Union Congress.
    Secretary: U Tun Min, Central Executive Committee Member of the Burma Peace Congress.
    Members: Thakin Ba Hein, Vice Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association.
        Ko Hla Win, Chairman of the All Burma Students Union.
        Daw Khin Khin Lay, Chairman of the Burmese Women's Liberation League.
        Ko Thein Maung (Dagon Taya), Chairman of the Burmese Writers Association. (Attached picture)
    The Soviet People's Delegation, which arrived in Beijing yesterday, is pictured with members of our Young Pioneers at the airport. The second from the right is the delegation leader, Solovyov.

### 苏联人民观礼代表团昨日抵京  缅甸人民观礼代表团亦由广州飞抵北京

1951-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联人民观礼代表团昨日抵京
    缅甸人民观礼代表团亦由广州飞抵北京
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中苏友好协会总会、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会、中华全国总工会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中华全国文学艺术界联合会的邀请，前来参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的苏联人民观礼代表团已在今日到达北京。到机场欢迎的有：中苏友好协会总会理事邵力子和曹靖华，副总干事赖亚力，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通，常务委员张奚若，委员章伯钧，秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，副主席钱三强和沙千里，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、副秘书长曾宪植、国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等人。赴机场欢迎的并有以伊斯特万尼·华斯为首的匈牙利人民观礼代表团。苏联大使罗申及使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
   【新华社二十九日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中华全国文学艺术界联合会的邀请，前来参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的缅甸人民观礼代表团在今天下午由广州飞抵北京。印度亲善访华团团员亚巴斯亦同机抵达。到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，常务委员张奚若，委员章伯钧，秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，执行委员李颉伯，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，副主席钱三强、沙千里，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，副秘书长曾宪植，国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾，秘书长沙可夫，及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等人。缅甸大使馆一等秘书吴辟和印度大使馆三等秘书古玛尔也到机场欢迎。
    【新华社二十九日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的苏联人民观礼代表团名单如下：
    团长：索洛维耶夫，全苏工会中央理事会书记。
    团员：雅科夫列夫，苏联对外文化协会副主席。奥巴林，苏联保卫和平委员会委员，世界和平理事会理事。政治科学知识普及协会主席。尤努索夫，乌兹贝克科学院化学研究所所长，塔什干国立中亚细亚大学教授。卡巴列夫斯基，作曲家。俄罗斯共和国功勋艺术工作者，斯大林奖金得奖者。特罗菲敏科，苏联文化教育工作者联合会主席。全苏工会中央理事会理事。佐捷拉娃（女），乔治亚共产主义青年团中央委员会委员，全苏列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会委员。玛里宁娜（女），社会主义劳动英雄，斯大林奖金得奖者。
