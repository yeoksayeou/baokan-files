Title: The Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-09-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: September 2nd marks the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent congratulatory telegrams to Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Hoang Minh Giam respectively.

(1) Chairman Mao's congratulatory telegram to His Excellency Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    On the occasion celebrating the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I, on behalf of the Government and people of The Peoples Republic of China, extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Vietnam, and wish the Vietnamese people, under your leadership, new victories in the struggle against imperialism for the liberation of all Vietnam.
                                            Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                                                                                        September 1, 1951, in Beijing
(2) Premier Zhou's congratulatory telegram to His Excellency Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    On the occasion celebrating the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, please accept my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, your government and the people of your country, and I wish the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Vietnam will be increasingly strengthened in the struggle to defend world peace and oppose imperialism.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                                                                                        September 1, 1951, in Beijing

### 越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念  毛主席周总理分别致电祝贺

1951-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念
    毛主席周总理分别致电祝贺
    【新华社一日讯】九月二日为越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与周恩来总理兼外交部部长分别致电越南民主共和国胡志明主席及外交部黄明鉴部长，表示祝贺。
    （一）毛主席贺电越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席阁下：
    欣逢越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府及人民，向阁下及越南人民致衷心的庆贺，并祝越南人民在阁下的领导下，为解放全越南而进行的反对帝国主义的斗争获得新的胜利。		中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席  毛泽东
            一九五一年九月一日于北京
        （二）周总理贺电越南民主共和国外交部黄明鉴部长阁下：
    欣逢越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念，请接受我对阁下，贵国政府及人民的热烈庆贺，并祝中越两国人民兄弟般的友谊在保卫世界和平及反对帝国主义的斗争中日益巩固。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长  周恩来
                                    一九五一年九月一日于北京
