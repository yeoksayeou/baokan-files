Title: Indonesian Overseas Chinese Tourist Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: An Indonesian Overseas Chinese returning tourist group of over sixty people arrived in Beijing by train from Tianjin this afternoon. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Peng Zemin, member of the Central People's Government and member of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Li Renren, Liao Chengzhi, and Zhuang Xiquan, deputy directors of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing; representatives from government agencies and democratic parties; the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Friendship Association, and representatives of Overseas Chinese students from various schools in Beijing, totaling over four hundred people.

After the tourist group disembarked, they received flowers presented by representatives of the Returned Overseas Chinese Friendship Association amidst warm applause. A brief welcome meeting was then held at the station.

Deputy Director Zhuang Xiquan delivered a welcome speech to the tourist group. He first extended a warm welcome to the tourist group on behalf of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government. He briefly introduced the Central People's Government's policy of protecting the legitimate rights and interests of Overseas Chinese and the achievements of New China in various fields. He emphasized that the achievements of the motherland are inseparable from the interests of the Overseas Chinese.

Song Zhongquan, the head of the tourist group, delivered a reply. He praised the efforts and achievements of the people of the motherland in various fields. He said: The Overseas Chinese in Indonesia love the motherland, love the people of the motherland and their leader Chairman Mao. He expressed that the Overseas Chinese must work harder to learn from their compatriots in the motherland in the future.

### 印尼华侨观光团抵京

1951-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼华侨观光团抵京
    【新华社二十三日讯】印尼华侨归国观光团一行六十余人于今日下午由天津乘车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府委员兼华侨事务委员会委员彭泽民，中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员李任仁、廖承志、庄希泉，北京市副市长张友渔，政府机关和民主党派代表，北京市工商联合会，北京归国华侨联谊会及北京各校华侨学生代表等四百余人。
    观光团一行下车后，在热烈的鼓掌声中接受了归国华侨联谊会代表的献花。接着在车站上举行了简短的欢迎会。
    庄希泉副主任委员向观光团致欢迎辞。他首先代表中央人民政府华侨事务委员会向观光团表示热烈的欢迎，他扼要地介绍了中央人民政府保护国外华侨正当权益的政策和新中国在各方面的成就。他强调祖国的成就是和华侨的利益分不开的。
    观光团团长宋中铨致答辞。他盛赞祖国人民在各方面的努力和成就。他说：印尼的华侨都热爱着祖国，热爱着祖国的人民和自己的领袖毛主席。他表示侨胞们今后需要更加努力地向祖国的同胞学习。
