Title: Shen Junru and Others Return to the Capital from Berlin
Author:
Date: 1951-09-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th.

    Shen Junru and the Chinese delegation he led, which participated in the Fifth Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers in Berlin, returned to the capital by plane at 4 p.m. on the 25th. Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Min Ganghou, Secretary-General, Zhou Xinmin, Secretary-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and Wang Huaian, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Justice, and many others.

### 沈钧儒等由柏林返抵首都

1951-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    沈钧儒等由柏林返抵首都
    【新华社二十六日讯】由沈钧儒所率领的参加柏林国际民主法律工作者协会第五届代表大会的中国代表团，于二十五日下午四时乘飞机返抵首都。前往机场欢迎者有最高人民法院副院长张志让、秘书长闵刚侯，最高人民检察署秘书长周新民，司法部办公厅主任王怀安等多人。
