Title: New Chinese Art Exhibition Held in Sofia, Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1951-09-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th. The "New Chinese Art Exhibition," sponsored by the Bulgarian Committee for Science, Art, and Culture, opened in Sofia on the 8th. Many representatives from Sofia's art and cultural circles and trade unions attended the opening ceremony. Li Muyu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, and members of the diplomatic missions of the Soviet Union, Poland, and other countries in Bulgaria also attended. The host of the exhibition, Mr. Ganovski, Chairman of the Committee for Science, Art, and Culture, said in his speech that the exhibits fully demonstrate the brilliant prospects of Chinese art and give the Bulgarian working people a deeper understanding of the new China and its rich art.

### 新中国艺术展览会在保京索非亚举行

1951-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    新中国艺术展览会在保京索非亚举行
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】保加利亚科学艺术文化委员会主办的“新中国艺术展览会”于八日在索非亚开幕。参加开幕仪式的有索非亚艺术文化界和工会代表多人。我国驻保大使馆参赞李慕禺和苏、波等国驻保外交使团人员均往参加。展览会主持人科学艺术文化委员会主席加诺夫斯基致词说：展览会上的陈列品充分显示了中国艺术的光辉远景，并且给保加利亚劳动人民一个更深刻了解新中国及其丰富艺术的机会。
