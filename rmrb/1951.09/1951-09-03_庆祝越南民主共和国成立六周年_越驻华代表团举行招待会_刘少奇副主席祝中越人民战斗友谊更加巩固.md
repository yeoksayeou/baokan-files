Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnamese Delegation to China Holds a Reception. Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi Wishes the Fighting Friendship Between the Chinese and Vietnamese People Will Be Further Consolidated.
Author:
Date: 1951-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: The Ambassador-level Representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Hoang Van Hoan, held a reception tonight to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Those invited to attend included Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Nie Rongzhen, and Li Lisan, Wu Yuzhang, Saifudin, Ma Xulun, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun, Teng Daiyuan, Chen Yu, Liang Xi, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Luo Longji, Shao Lizi, Situ Meitang and other heads of departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various democratic parties, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Zhang Youyu, and more than 300 people.

Diplomatic envoys to China who attended included: Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Hungarian Ambassador Szafrankó, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy Fedorin, Chargé d'Affaires of the Mongolian Embassy Labdan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Grutner, Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy Doding, Chargé d'Affaires of the Czechoslovak Embassy Reif, Chargé d'Affaires of the Indian Embassy Gore, Chargé d'Affaires of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy Wu Bi, Counselor of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, and other embassy staff.

At the beginning of the reception, Representative Hoang Van Hoan first gave a speech. After describing the great achievements of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in various fields over the past six years, he said: At present, American imperialism is openly interfering in Vietnam, dreaming of turning Vietnam into a springboard for aggression against New China and suppression of the people of Southeast Asia. He said that the victory of the Chinese people and the victory of the Korean and Chinese people on the Korean battlefield greatly encouraged the Vietnamese people. The Vietnamese people firmly believe that they will eventually defeat the French colonialists and American interventionists.

Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Central People's Government then gave a congratulatory speech. He said that the people of China and Vietnam have a long history of friendship, and the Chinese people deeply sympathize with the Vietnamese people's struggle for national independence and national liberation. Therefore, after the founding of the People's Republic of China, it was the first to recognize the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and established diplomatic relations with it. Finally, Vice Chairman Liu, on behalf of the Chinese people, congratulated the Vietnamese people on winning new victories in their struggle for national independence and liberation, congratulated the people of China and Vietnam on the further consolidation of their friendship in the common struggle against imperialism, and wished President Ho Chi Minh, the leader of the Vietnamese people, good health.

Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy, Fedorin, then gave a speech. He represented the Soviet people in congratulating the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on its National Day. He said: The Soviet people have always sympathized with the Vietnamese people because they are waging a heroic struggle to finally overthrow the foreign rulers.

The reception ended in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝越南民主共和国成立六周年  越驻华代表团举行招待会  刘少奇副主席祝中越人民战斗友谊更加巩固

1951-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国成立六周年
    越驻华代表团举行招待会
    刘少奇副主席祝中越人民战斗友谊更加巩固
   【新华社二日讯】越南民主共和国驻华代表团大使衔代表黄文欢为庆祝越南民主共和国成立六周年纪念，于今晚举行招待会。
    应邀出席者有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、副总理郭沫若，中国人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻及李立三、吴玉章、赛福鼎、马叙伦、沈雁冰、陈叔通、张治中、傅作义、章伯钧、张奚若、柳亚子、张东荪、滕代远、陈郁、梁希、章汉夫、伍修权、罗隆基、邵力子、司徒美堂等中央人民政府各部门首长，各民主党派负责人及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等三百余人。
    驻我国外交使节出席者有：罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、匈牙利大使夏法朗科、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、苏联大使馆临时代办费德林、蒙古大使馆临时代办拉布丹、德意志民主共和国外交使团临时代办葛鲁特纳、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办赖夫、印度大使馆临时代办高尔、印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、缅甸大使馆临时代办吴辟、朝鲜大使馆崔英参赞及其他使馆人员。
    招待会开始时，大使衔黄文欢代表首先致辞。他在叙述越南民主共和国成立六年来在各方面的巨大成就后说：目前美帝国主义正在公开地干涉越南，梦想把越南变为侵略新中国和镇压东南亚人民的跳板。他说，中国人民的胜利及朝、中人民在朝鲜战场上的胜利，大大鼓舞了越南人民。越南人民深信：他们一定能最后击败法国殖民者和美国干涉者。
    中央人民政府副主席刘少奇继起致祝词。他说，中、越两国人民在历史上有悠久的友谊，中国人民对越南人民要求民族独立与民族解放的斗争，是十分同情的。因此，中华人民共和国在成立以后，就首先承认了越南民主共和国，和它建立了外交关系。刘副主席最后代表中国人民，祝贺越南人民要求民族独立解放的斗争获得新的胜利，祝贺中、越两国人民在反对帝国主义的共同斗争中的友谊更加巩固，并祝贺越南人民领袖胡志明主席的健康。
    苏联大使馆临时代办费德林继起致辞。他代表苏联人民祝贺越南民主共和国国庆。他说：苏联人民一直同情着越南人民，因为越南人民正在为最后推翻外国统治者而进行着英勇的斗争。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中结束。
