Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Statement Condemning the Illegal Japanese Peace Treaty Published Prominently in Moscow Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1951-09-22
Page: 1

    Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Statement Condemning the Illegal Japanese Peace Treaty Published Prominently in Moscow Newspapers
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 21st: Moscow newspapers on the 20th all published in prominent positions the statement made by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, on the 18th, regarding the San Francisco Peace Treaty signed by the United States and its vassal states.
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: TASS Beijing, September 20th dispatch, fully broadcast the Xinhua News Agency's broadcast of Foreign Minister Zhou's statement regarding the San Francisco Peace Treaty signed by the United States and its vassal states.
