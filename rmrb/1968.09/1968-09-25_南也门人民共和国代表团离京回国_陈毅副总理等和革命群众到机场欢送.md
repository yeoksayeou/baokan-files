Title: Delegation of the People's Republic of South Yemen Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1968-09-25
Edition: 4

By South Yemen Foreign Minister Safi Ahmad Zalay led a delegation of the People's Republic of South Yemen, which concluded its friendly visit to China, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, and relevant officials from government departments, the People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Wang Xinting, Qiao Guanhua, Li Qiang, Xie Huaiyi, and Xiang Qian, as well as representatives from the Beijing workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams, leaders of various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, and exemplary soldiers from the Beijing garrison, went to the airport to see them off.

Minister Zalay and his delegation, en route home, stopped over in Shanghai this evening, where they were warmly seen off by several hundred revolutionary masses. Minister Zalay and his delegation, accompanied by leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Revolutionary Committee, representatives from the Shanghai workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams, leaders of various revolutionary mass organizations in Shanghai, and exemplary soldiers from the Shanghai garrison, bade farewell to the welcoming crowds. The people waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong" and the national flags of China and South Yemen, repeatedly shouting slogans: "Firmly support the anti-imperialist, anti-colonial struggle of the South Yemen people!" "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and South Yemen!" "Down with U.S.-led imperialism!" "Down with modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionist renegade clique!" "Long live the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!" "Long live the invincible Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the South Yemen people!" "Salute to President Qahtan Shaabi!" "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

When Minister Zalay and his delegation left Beijing today, envoys from some countries stationed in China also went to the airport to see them off.

### 南也门人民共和国代表团离京回国  陈毅副总理等和革命群众到机场欢送

1968-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    南也门人民共和国代表团离京回国
    陈毅副总理等和革命群众到机场欢送
    新华社二十四日讯  由南也门外交部长塞弗·艾哈迈德·扎莱率领的南也门人民共和国代表团，结束在我国的友好访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    国务院副总理陈毅和政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会等有关方面负责人王新亭、乔冠华、李强、谢怀德、向前，以及首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，北京市各革命群众组织负责成员，驻京部队五好战士代表，到机场欢送。
    扎莱部长等在回国途中，今天晚上途经上海，受到上海市数百名革命群众的热烈欢送。扎莱部长等由上海市革命委员会领导成员、上海工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表、上海市各革命群众组织负责成员、驻沪部队五好战士代表陪同，与欢送群众告别时，人们挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，挥动着中国、南也门两国国旗，不断高呼口号：“坚决支持南也门人民的反帝反殖斗争！”“中国南也门两国人民的友谊万岁！”“打倒以美国为首的帝国主义！”“打倒以苏修叛徒集团为中心的现代修正主义！”“无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！”“毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”“战无不胜的马克思主义、列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！”“南也门人民万岁！”“向卡坦·沙比总统致敬！”“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    扎莱部长等今天离开北京时，一些国家驻中国的使节，也到机场欢送。
