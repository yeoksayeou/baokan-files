Title: Fuzhou Frontline Ceases Artillery Fire for Two Days During National Day
Author:
Date: 1968-09-30
Edition: 4

A spokesman for the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Fuzhou Frontline forces announced today that October 1st marks the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. To allow the compatriots and soldiers on the islands of Jinmen, Xiaojinmen, Dadeng, and Erdeng to celebrate National Day with the rest of the nation and to rejoice in the great victories of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Fuzhou Frontline have been ordered to cease fire on October 1st and 3rd for two days, as a gesture of concern.

### 福建前线国庆期间停止炮击两天

1968-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    福建前线国庆期间停止炮击两天
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线部队司令部发言人今天宣布，十月一日是我中华人民共和国建国十九周年国庆节。为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞和蒋军的官兵们同全国人民一道欢度国庆，欢呼无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在十月一日和三日两天停止炮击，以示关怀。
