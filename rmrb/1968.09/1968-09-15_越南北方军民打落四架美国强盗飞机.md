Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four American Aggressor Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-09-15
Edition: 6

The military and civilians of North Vietnam's Vinh Linh district shot down three invading American aggressor aircraft on the 9th and 10th. On the 12th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh province shot down an F-4 fighter jet.

### 越南北方军民打落四架美国强盗飞机

1968-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民打落四架美国强盗飞机
    越南北方永灵区军民九日和十日曾接连打落三架入侵的美国强盗飞机。十二日，广平省军民打落了一架Ｆ—4战斗机。
