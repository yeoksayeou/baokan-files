Title: Chinese Technical Personnel Arrive in Kathmandu to Participate in the Construction of the Sun Koshi Hydropower Station
Author:
Date: 1968-09-21
Edition: 5

A batch of Chinese technical personnel has arrived in Kathmandu to participate in the construction of the Sun Koshi Hydropower Station, which is being built with Chinese aid.

The Sun Koshi Hydropower Station is located approximately 80 kilometers from Kathmandu. A power transmission line will be constructed from the power station to Kathmandu. On May 25 of last year, the governments of China and Nepal signed an agreement to build these projects.

Recently, Rudra Prasad Giri, Nepal's Minister of Works, Transportation, Water Resources, and Electricity, accompanied by Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, Tu Guowei, inspected the construction site.

### 参加我国援建尼泊尔水电站工程的建设  我国一批技术人员到达加德满都

1968-09-21
第5版()
专栏：

    参加我国援建尼泊尔水电站工程的建设
    我国一批技术人员到达加德满都
    新华社加德满都十八日电　中国技术人员约六十人已经先后到达加德满都，参加由中国援助兴建的尼泊尔逊科西水电站工程的建设工作。
    逊科西水电站位于离加德满都大约八十公里的地方。从水电站到加德满都将架设一条输电线路。去年五月二十五日，中国和尼泊尔两国政府签署了兴建这些工程的议定书。
    最近，尼泊尔工程、交通运输、水源和电力国务大臣鲁德拉·普拉萨德·吉里，在中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维陪同下，视察了建筑工地。
