Title: New Ambassador of Mali Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu
Author:
Date: 1968-09-17
Edition: 6

New Ambassador of Mali Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu

New China News Agency, September 16th: The new Ambassador of the Republic of Mali to China, Assane Gondo, accompanied by diplomatic staff of the Embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China today. Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Assane Gondo. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guan-hua and others.

### 马里新任驻华大使  向董必武副主席递交国书

1968-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    马里新任驻华大使
    向董必武副主席递交国书
    新华社十六日讯　马里共和国新任驻中国大使阿桑·甘多偕同大使馆外交官员，今天向中华人民共和国副主席董必武递交了国书。董必武副主席同阿桑·甘多大使进行了友好的谈话。在座的有外交部副部长乔冠华等。
