Title: "Voice of the Thai People" Strongly Supports Albania's Withdrawal from the Warsaw Pact
Author:
Date: 1968-09-27
Edition: 6

"Voice of the Thai People" radio station broadcasted an article on the 25th, firmly supporting the People's Republic of Albania's solemn declaration to withdraw from the Warsaw Pact.

The article pointed out that under the control of the Soviet revisionists, the Warsaw Pact has long betrayed the principles and purposes of Marxism-Leninism, becoming a tool for the Soviet revisionist clique to promote great power chauvinism and national egoism, used to control, plunder, and enslave the peoples of Eastern Europe. The recent invasion of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet revisionist clique, along with some member states of the Warsaw Pact organization, has exposed the aggressive and reactionary nature of the Warsaw Pact.

The article pointed out that the Warsaw Pact has become a tool for the Soviet revisionist leadership to engage in political collusion with the NATO, controlled by U.S. imperialism, and to engage in counter-revolutionary global collusion with U.S. imperialism. In recent years, the Soviet revisionist clique has been trying to link the Warsaw Pact organization with NATO, promoting so-called "European détente," helping U.S. imperialism maintain its hegemony in Western Europe, and moving troops eastward to suppress the revolutionary struggles of the Vietnamese people and the peoples of Asia and Africa. The purpose of these treacherous activities by the Soviet revisionist clique is to conspire with U.S. imperialism in an attempt to redivide the world.

The article pointed out that for a long time, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party headed by Comrade Enver Hoxha, the heroic Albanian people have been waging an uncompromising struggle against the evil activities of the Soviet revisionist clique using the Warsaw Pact, repeatedly foiling the Soviet revisionist clique's despicable plots to control, interfere with, and overthrow Albania.

The article said that the aforementioned actions of the Albanian government and the recent announcement of withdrawal from the Warsaw Pact are revolutionary actions, in accordance with the principles of Marxism-Leninism, in accordance with the interests of the proletariat, and in accordance with the interests of the revolutionary peoples of the world.

The article continued, saying that the heroic revolutionary action of the Albanian people has great historical significance. It has dealt a heavy blow to the great power chauvinism, national egoism, and mad aggressive policies of the Soviet revisionist clique, greatly encouraging the struggle of the peoples of Eastern Europe against the Soviet revisionist clique. The heroic and daring revolutionary actions of the Albanian people have shown the indomitable revolutionary spirit of the heroic Albanian people who fear no violence. The article said that the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle of the Albanian people is not isolated. All true Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary peoples of various countries firmly support the Albanian people. Under the leadership of the Communist Party of Thailand, the Thai people are waging a revolutionary armed struggle against U.S. imperialism and the Thanom-Praphat clique, firmly supporting the Albanian people's revolutionary action of withdrawing from the Warsaw Pact. The Thai people stand firmly with the Albanian people and will continue to fight shoulder to shoulder with the Albanian people.

### “泰国人民之声”坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚退出华沙条约  阿人民果敢的革命行动沉重打击苏修集团  泰国革命人民坚定地同阿尔巴尼亚人民站在一起并肩战斗

1968-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    “泰国人民之声”坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚退出华沙条约
    阿人民果敢的革命行动沉重打击苏修集团
    泰国革命人民坚定地同阿尔巴尼亚人民站在一起并肩战斗
    新华社二十六日讯　“泰国人民之声”电台二十五日广播一篇文章，坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国庄严宣布退出华沙条约的革命行动。
    文章指出，在苏修控制下的华沙条约，早已背叛马克思列宁主义的原则和目的，成为苏修叛徒集团推行大国沙文主义和民族利己主义的侵略工具，用以控制、掠夺和奴役东欧各国人民。这次苏修叛徒集团纠集了华沙条约组织中的某些成员国的修正主义集团，侵略捷克斯洛伐克，就赤裸裸地暴露了华沙条约的侵略和反动实质。
    文章指出，华沙条约已成为苏修领导集团用来同美帝控制的北大西洋集团进行政治勾结以及同美帝进行反革命全球勾结的工具。近年来，苏修叛徒集团竭力要把华沙条约组织同北大西洋集团挂钩，大搞所谓“欧洲缓和”，帮助美帝维护它在西欧的霸权，并把兵力东移，镇压越南人民和亚非各国人民的革命斗争。苏修叛徒集团这些叛卖活动的目的，就是要同美帝国主义合谋妄图重新瓜分世界。
    文章指出，长期以来，在恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导下的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民，对苏修叛徒集团利用华沙条约所干的罪恶活动进行毫不妥协的斗争，一次又一次地粉碎了苏修叛徒集团妄图控制、干涉和颠覆阿尔巴尼亚的卑鄙阴谋。
    文章说，阿尔巴尼亚政府的上述行动和这次宣布退出华沙条约的行动，是革命的行动，是符合马克思列宁主义原则的，是符合无产阶级利益的，是符合全世界革命人民利益的。
    文章接着说，阿尔巴尼亚人民的这一英勇的革命行动，具有重大的历史意义。它给了苏修集团的大国沙文主义、民族利己主义和疯狂侵略政策以沉重的打击，大大鼓舞了东欧各国人民反对苏修叛徒集团的斗争。阿尔巴尼亚人民的英勇果敢的革命行动，表现了英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民不畏任何强暴的坚贞不屈的革命精神。文章说，阿尔巴尼亚人民反帝反修的斗争并不孤立。全世界真正的马克思列宁主义者、各国革命人民坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚人民。在泰国共产党领导下，正在进行革命武装斗争、反对美帝国主义和他侬—巴博集团的泰国人民，坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚人民退出华沙条约的革命行动。泰国人民坚定地同阿尔巴尼亚人民站在一起，并坚定地同阿尔巴尼亚人民并肩战斗下去。
