Title: Delegation from the Ministry of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-09-25
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Ministry of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Deputy Minister Qu Hui Jin, arrived in Beijing on September 21 by train at the invitation of China. Luo Gui Bo, Deputy Minister, went to the station to welcome them.

### 越南民主共和国文化部代表团到京

1968-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国文化部代表团到京
    新华社讯　由瞿辉瑾副部长率领的越南民主共和国文化部代表团，应邀来我国访问，于二十一日乘火车到达北京。罗贵波副部长前往车站迎接。
