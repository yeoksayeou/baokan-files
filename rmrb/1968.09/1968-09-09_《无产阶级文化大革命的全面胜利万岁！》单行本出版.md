Title: Long Live the Complete Victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!
Author:
Date: 1968-09-09
Edition: 4

The single-volume edition of the important editorial "Long Live the Complete Victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! — Warmly Congratulate the Establishment of Revolutionary Committees in All Provinces, Municipalities, and Autonomous Regions of the Entire Country (Except Taiwan Province)" by the People's Daily and the PLA Daily has been published by the People's Publishing House. It will begin to be distributed at the New China Bookstore in Beijing on September 8 and will be successively distributed at New China Bookstores across the country.

### 《无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！》单行本出版

1968-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    《无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！》单行本出版
    新华社七日讯　《人民日报》《解放军报》的重要社论《无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！——热烈欢呼全国（除台湾省外）各省、市、自治区革命委员会全部成立》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，九月八日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
