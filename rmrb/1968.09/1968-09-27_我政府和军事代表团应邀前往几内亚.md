Title: Chinese Government and Military Delegation Invited to Guinea
Author:
Date: 1968-09-27
Edition: 6

A delegation from the People's Republic of China, led by Zhang Ting, has been invited by the Guinean government to participate in the celebrations marking the 10th anniversary of Guinea's independence. The delegation departed from Beijing by airplane today to travel to Conakry, the capital of Guinea.

Relevant officials, including Luo Guibo, Xie Huai De, Zhang Zhong Ru, and Ding Xilin, were present at the airport to see them off.

The Guinean Ambassador to China, Segou Kamara, and other diplomatic staff from the embassy were also present at the airport to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 我政府和军事代表团应邀前往几内亚

1968-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府和军事代表团应邀前往几内亚
    新华社二十六日讯　由张挺率领的中华人民共和国政府和军事代表团，应几内亚政府邀请参加几内亚共和国独立十周年庆祝活动，今天乘飞机离开北京前往几内亚首都科纳克里。
    有关方面负责人罗贵波、谢怀德、张中如、丁西林到机场送行。
    几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉和大使馆外交官员，也到机场送行。
