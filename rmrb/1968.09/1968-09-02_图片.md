Title: Revolutionary Enthusiasm in Production at the Changchun Automobile Factory
Author:
Date: 1968-09-02
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Changchun Automobile Factory in Jilin Province have directed the revolutionary enthusiasm unleashed during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution towards production, continually presenting a new and improved production landscape. Since the beginning of this year, production has shown significant monthly increases. This is a scene of revolutionary workers in the final assembly workshop hurriedly assembling buses.
