Title: Youth Returning to the Countryside Study Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Liaoyi Shi
Date: 1968-09-24
Edition: 5

    The educated youth who have returned to their hometowns in the 50 Families Production Brigade of the Xiyingzi People's Commune in Chaoyang County, Liaoning Province, have been consistently engaged in studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought. This is a scene from a study session where the educated youth discuss their experiences of learning from Chairman Mao's brilliant work "In Memory of Norman Bethune," and their reflections on becoming new socialist farmers.  Photographed by Liaoyi Shi (Xinhua News Agency)
