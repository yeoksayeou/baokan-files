Title: The First to Fourth Volumes of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published in Thai, Vietnamese, Japanese, and Indonesian
Author:
Date: 1968-09-26
Edition: 1

The first to fourth volumes of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in Thai, Vietnamese, Japanese, and Indonesian have been published by the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House. So far, the first to fourth volumes of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" have been published in eight foreign languages: Thai, Vietnamese, Burmese, Indonesian, Japanese, English, French, and Russian.

The Korean edition of the first volume, the Spanish, German, and Korean editions of the second volume, the Persian edition of "Selected Military Writings of Mao Zedong," and the Arabic edition of "Six Military Works by Chairman Mao" have also been recently published.

### 《毛泽东选集》第一卷至第四卷泰、越南、日、印尼文版出版

1968-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    《毛泽东选集》第一卷至第四卷泰、越南、日、印尼文版出版
    新华社二十五日讯　《毛泽东选集》第一卷至第四卷的泰、越南、日、印尼文版已由北京外文出版社出版。到目前为止，出齐《毛泽东选集》第一卷至第四卷的共有泰、越南、缅、印尼、日、英、法、俄八种外文版。
    《毛泽东选集》第一卷的朝鲜文版，第二卷的西班牙、德、朝鲜文版，《毛泽东军事文选》的波斯文版和《毛主席的六篇军事著作》的阿拉伯文版，也在最近出版。
