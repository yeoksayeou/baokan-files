Title: Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing for National Day Celebrations and Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1968-09-28
Edition: 1

A delegation from the Pakistani government, led by Advisor to President Ayub Khan, Syed Fida Hassan, has been invited by the government of the People's Republic of China to participate in the celebrations of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to conduct a friendly visit. The delegation will arrive in Beijing on September 28.

### 应我国政府邀请前来参加国庆和友好访问  巴基斯坦政府友好代表团今日到京

1968-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府邀请前来参加国庆和友好访问
    巴基斯坦政府友好代表团今日到京
    新华社二十七日讯　由巴基斯坦阿尤布·汗总统的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团，应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，前来我国参加中华人民共和国成立十九周年庆祝活动，并进行友好访问，将于二十八日到达北京。
