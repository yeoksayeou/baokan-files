Title: Chairman Mao's Brilliant Works Published in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1968-09-21
Edition: 5

The brilliant works of Chairman Mao, "Problems of Strategy in China's Revolutionary War" and "Analysis of the Classes in Chinese Society," have been translated into Sinhala and published by the Colombo Workers' Publishing House in Ceylon's capital, Colombo.

Another brilliant work by Chairman Mao, "The Struggle in Chingkang Mountains," has also been translated into Sinhala and published by the Colombo Publishing House.

Chairman Mao's works have been warmly welcomed by the people of Ceylon.

### 毛主席的光辉著作在锡兰出版  受到锡兰人民的热烈欢迎

1968-09-21
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席的光辉著作在锡兰出版
    受到锡兰人民的热烈欢迎
    新华社科伦坡二十日电　伟大领袖毛主席的光辉著作《目前抗日统一战线中的策略问题》和《怎样分析农村阶级》已经译成僧伽罗文，由锡兰首都科伦坡工人出版社出版。
    毛主席的另一篇光辉著作《井冈山的斗争》也已经译成僧伽罗文，并由科伦坡人民出版社出版。
    毛主席的这些著作受到了锡兰人民的热烈欢迎。
