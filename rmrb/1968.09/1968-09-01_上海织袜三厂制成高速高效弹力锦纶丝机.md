Title: Shanghai Sock Factory No. 3 Develops High-Speed, High-Efficiency Spandex Nylon Yarn Machine
Author:
Date: 1968-09-01
Edition: 4

Shanghai Sock Factory No. 3, a small workshop in a lane house, has successfully developed a high-speed, high-efficiency spandex nylon yarn machine. Several technicians and maintenance workers from the factory, following the great leader Chairman Mao's teachings of self-reliance, hard work, breaking superstitions, and liberating thoughts, have demonstrated the revolutionary spirit of the working class by daring to think, speak, act, and do. They have recently successfully tested a Chinese-style high-speed, high-efficiency spandex nylon yarn machine, providing extremely favorable conditions for the production of spandex nylon socks and other spandex nylon fabrics in China's knitting industry. The successful development of the high-speed, high-efficiency spandex nylon yarn machine is another vivid and powerful proof of Chairman Mao's instruction that "the lowly are the wisest, and the noble are the most foolish."

### 上海织袜三厂制成高速高效弹力锦纶丝机

1968-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    上海织袜三厂制成高速高效弹力锦纶丝机
    上海的一家里弄工厂——上海织袜三厂的几个工人出身的技术人员和保全工，遵循伟大领袖毛主席的自力更生，艰苦奋斗，破除迷信，解放思想的伟大教导，发扬工人阶级敢想、敢说、敢闯、敢干的革命大无畏精神，最近试制成功了一台中国式的高速、高效弹力锦纶丝机，为我国针织行业生产弹力锦纶袜以及其它弹力锦纶织物提供了极为有利的条件。高速、高效弹力锦纶丝机的试制成功，是伟大领袖毛主席所指示的“卑贱者最聪明！高贵者最愚蠢”的又一个生动有力的证明。　（据新华社讯）
