Title: Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams Enter Changchun Geological Institute
Author: Hong Shi Liu Mao Xin
Date: 1968-09-26
Edition: 3

    After the Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams entered Changchun Geological Institute, they worked together with revolutionary teachers and students. They studied Chairman Mao's works and his latest instructions carefully, focusing on the issues from their school's struggle, criticism, and reform.
