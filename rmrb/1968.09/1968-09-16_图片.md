Title: Turkish People Stage Anti-American Protest March
Author:
Date: 1968-09-16
Edition: 5

    The Turkish people held an anti-American protest march. The reactionary authorities brutally suppressed the patriotic masses. The picture shows that despite the suppression by the police and gendarmes, the angry students flooded the streets of Istanbul, protesting against the atrocities of the reactionary authorities.

    (Telephoto, Xinhua News Agency)
