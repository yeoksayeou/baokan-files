Title: Military and Civilians in South Vietnam's Quang Nam, Kon Tum, and Other Provinces Achieve New Victories
Author:
Date: 1968-09-11
Edition: 6

Military and civilians in South Vietnam's Quang Nam, Kon Tum, and other provinces have achieved new victories.

According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 9th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: From late August to early September, military and civilians in South Vietnam's Quang Nam and Kon Tum provinces achieved new victories.

From August 23 to September 1, military and civilians in Quang Nam Province continuously shelled and attacked the enemy, killing and wounding over 430 enemy personnel, including the annihilation of eight enemy platoons. The local forces and guerrilla units in Thang Binh County also destroyed 14 of the enemy's "strategic hamlets" and concentration camps, liberating compatriots who had been imprisoned by the enemy.

From August 31 to September 5, military and civilians in the Central Highlands region of Kon Tum, Gia Lai, and Dac Lac provinces continued to attack the enemy. According to incomplete statistics, they annihilated 415 enemy personnel, including nearly 200 American invaders, shot down 13 enemy aircraft, destroyed seven cannons and 22 military vehicles, burned two ammunition depots and one fuel depot, and destroyed eight bunkers and 26 barracks.

According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 8th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: Recently, military and civilians in South Vietnam's Vinh Long and Quang Ngai provinces continued to attack and annihilate many American puppet troops.

From August 21 to 27, military and civilians in Vinh Long Province engaged in nearly 100 battles with the enemy, attacking Vinh Long City, Sa Dec City, and the towns of Tam Phuoc, Duc Hoa, Hau Nghia, Phu My, Le Thuy, and Ba Ria, as well as a number of enemy strongholds and outposts. They killed and captured over 1,540 enemy personnel, including more than 130 American invaders, shot down and destroyed 40 enemy aircraft, destroyed 20 military vehicles, and sank or damaged 12 enemy ships.

On September 3, the liberation armed forces in Quang Ngai Province intercepted and annihilated three enemy main force companies in the Go Hau area, destroying and damaging six enemy armored vehicles.

On September 3, the guerrilla forces in Ninh Thuan Province, in a brave counterattack against American aggressor aircraft that came to bomb, shot down two enemy aircraft. On August 20, the guerrilla forces in Dong Xuan Commune, Binh Dinh Province, used light weapons to shoot down one American aggressor aircraft and killed and wounded 18 American aggressors.

### 南越广南、昆嵩等省军民又获新战果

1968-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    南越广南、昆嵩等省军民又获新战果
    据新华社河内九日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广南、昆嵩等省军民，在八月底到九月初期间，又取得了新的战果。
    广南省军民从八月二十三日到九月一日接连炮轰和袭击敌人，共打死打伤敌人四百三十多名，其中包括全歼敌军八个排。这个省升平县的地方部队和游击队还捣毁了敌人的“战略村”和集中营十四个，解放了被敌人囚禁的同胞。西原地区昆嵩、嘉莱和多乐省的军民，从八月三十一日到九月五日继续袭击敌人，据不完全统计，共歼敌四百一十五名，其中包括美国侵略军近二百名，击落敌机十三架，击毁大炮七门和军车二十二辆，烧毁弹药库二座和汽油库一座，击毁碉堡八座和营房二十六座。
    据新华社河内八日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方永隆、广义等省军民最近继续袭击和歼灭许多美伪军。
    永隆省军民在八月二十一日到二十七日期间同敌人进行了近一百次战斗，进攻了永隆市、沙沥市和三平、德宗、丐绒、平明、勒倭、巴更等城镇以及敌人的一批据点和哨所，消灭和俘虏敌人一千五百四十多名，其中包括美国侵略军一百三十多名，击落击毁敌机四十架，击毁军车二十辆，击沉击伤敌船十二艘。
    广义省解放武装力量九月三日在鹅黄地区截击前来窜犯的伪军，歼灭敌人三个主力连，击毁击伤敌人装甲车六辆。
    西宁省游击队九月三日在英勇反击前来轰炸的美国强盗飞机的战斗中，击落敌机两架。鹅贡省东山乡游击队八月二十日，用轻武器击落了美国强盗飞机一架，并打死打伤十八名美国强盗。
