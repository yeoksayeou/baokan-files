Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Jiang Qing's Important Speeches Published as Separate Volumes
Author:
Date: 1968-09-14
Edition: 3

New China News Agency, September 13th: The important speeches made by Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Jiang Qing on September 7th at the grand celebration held by the revolutionary masses of Beijing to mark the establishment of revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions of the country (excluding Taiwan Province) have been published as separate volumes by the People's Publishing House. They will be available for purchase starting September 13th at the New China Bookstore in Beijing and will soon be available at New China Bookstores across the country.

### 周恩来总理和江青同志重要讲话出版单行本

1968-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理和江青同志重要讲话出版单行本 
    新华社十三日讯　周恩来总理和江青同志九月七日在北京市革命群众热烈庆祝全国（除台湾省外）各省、市、自治区革命委员会全部成立大会上的重要讲话，已由人民出版社出版单行本，九月十三日起在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
