Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of the Sino-Guinea Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1968-09-16
Edition: 5

China's Chargé d'Affaires in Guinea Hosts a Reception

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, CONAKRY, September 14 — The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Guinea, Lin Bingnan, held a reception aboard the Chinese ocean liner "Wuxi," which is currently docked in Conakry harbor, on the evening of September 13. Together with Guinean friends, he enthusiastically celebrated the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Guinean Friendship Treaty.

Attending the reception were Kaita Enfamara, member of the National Political Bureau of the Guinean Democratic Party and Minister of Trade; General Dianai Lansana, member of the National Political Bureau of the Guinean Democratic Party and Minister of the People's Army and Defense of the Revolution; Kaita Mori, Secretary of State responsible for Transportation; General Kaita Numanjian, Chief of the General Staff of the Guinean People's Army; and other high-ranking officials from the government and military, as well as representatives from various sectors.

Lin Bingnan and Kaita Enfamara both spoke at the reception. They highly praised the combat friendship between the Chinese and Guinean peoples established in their common struggle against imperialism and neo-colonialism, and sincerely wished for the continued strengthening and development of the fruitful and friendly cooperation between the two countries.

In his speech, Kaita Enfamara also highly praised the effective and selfless assistance that China has provided to Guinea, and warmly praised the Chinese workers and technicians working in Guinea. He pointed out that the Guinean people are very satisfied with the Chinese workers and technicians, who have set an example for the Guinean people.

Finally, Minister Enfamara proposed a toast to the health of the great leader of the Chinese revolution, Chairman Mao Zedong.

The reception was held in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝中几友好条约签订八周年  我驻几内亚临时代办举行招待会

1968-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中几友好条约签订八周年
    我驻几内亚临时代办举行招待会
    新华社科纳克里十四日电　中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南，十三日晚在目前停泊在科纳克里港的中国远洋轮《无锡》号上举行招待会，同几内亚朋友一起，热烈庆祝中几友好条约签订八周年。
    出席招待会的有，几内亚民主党全国政治局委员、贸易部长凯塔·恩法马拉，几内亚民主党全国政治局委员、人民军和保卫革命部部长迪亚奈·兰萨纳将军，负责运输的国务秘书凯塔·莫里，几内亚人民军总参谋长凯塔·努曼迪安将军，以及政府和军队的其他高级官员和各方面人士。
    林秉南和凯塔·恩法马拉在招待会上讲了话。他们都热烈赞扬中国人民和几内亚人民在反对帝国主义和反对新老殖民主义的共同斗争中建立起来的战斗友谊，衷心祝愿两国间卓有成果的、友好的合作日益巩固和发展。
    凯塔·恩法马拉在讲话时，还高度赞扬中国给予几内亚的有效的和无私的援助，热情赞扬在几内亚工作的中国工人和技术人员。他指出，几内亚人民对中国工人和技术人员表示十分满意，中国工人和技术人员为几内亚人民树立了榜样。
    最后，恩法马拉部长提议为中国革命的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    招待会是在极其亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
