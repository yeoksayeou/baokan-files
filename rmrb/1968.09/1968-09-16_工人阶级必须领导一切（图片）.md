Title: The Working Class Must Lead Everything
Author:
Date: 1968-09-16
Edition: 6

The working class must lead everything. After the capital's workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams entered colleges and universities, they followed Chairman Mao's instructions, "To hold study classes is a good method; many problems can be solved in study classes," to help revolutionary teachers and students establish Mao Zedong Thought study classes, promoting the school's struggle, criticism, and reform.

The reporter of this newspaper took the photo.

The workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team stationed at the No. 3 Middle School of the Liaoyuan Coal Mining Bureau led the school's revolutionary teachers and students to focus on class struggle. This is the workers' propaganda team organizing all teachers and students to visit the "Mass Grave" of the Liaoyuan Coal Mine after the bitter history of miners in the old society and the current class struggle, severely criticizing the "theory of the extinction of class struggle" of the Chinese Khrushchev. The photo was taken by Jiang Zishun.

The workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams entering the education front is a earth-shaking event. The workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team of Shanghai entered Fudan University, participated in the school's struggle, criticism, and reform, and will permanently lead the school. The photo was taken by a reporter from Xinhua News Agency (telephoto).

The workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team entering Tsinghua University is determined to complete the glorious task given by Chairman Mao to re-educate intellectuals. This is the propaganda team's comrades conducting class education of "remembering bitterness and thinking sweetness" to the students of the school. The photo was taken by a reporter of this newspaper.

Soldiers from the workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams stationed in colleges and universities are studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions on educational revolution seriously with old workers. The report was provided by the Armored Forces Reporting Group.

After the capital's workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams entered colleges and universities, a new atmosphere appeared within the schools. Revolutionary teachers, students, and staff, under the leadership of the working class, strengthened their organizational discipline, determined to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, unify their thoughts, steps, and actions, and do a good job in the school's struggle, criticism, and reform.

The report was provided by the Propaganda Group of the capital's workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team stationed at Peking University.

The workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team stationed at Beijing Institute of Electric Power conducted extensive and in-depth heart-to-heart talks among revolutionary teachers and students, patiently helping them solve their ideological problems with Mao Zedong Thought, and raising their awareness of class struggle and line struggle. The photo was taken by Yang Changzhong.

After the capital's workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams entered Peking University, they organized the leaders of the school's revolutionary mass organizations to study Chairman Mao's latest instructions, conducted thorough and detailed ideological and political work among revolutionary teachers, students, and staff, and quickly achieved revolutionary unity. This is them going to the Municipal Revolutionary Committee to report their joy with drums and gongs. The photo was taken by a reporter of this newspaper.

### 工人阶级必须领导一切（图片）

1968-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    工人阶级必须领导一切
    首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队进驻大专院校后，遵照毛主席“办学习班，是个好办法，很多问题可以在学习班得到解决”的教导，帮助学校革命师生办起了毛泽东思想学习班，促进学校的斗、批、改。
    本报记者摄
    驻吉林辽源矿务局三中的工人毛泽东思想宣传队，带领学校的革命师生，狠抓阶级斗争。这是工人宣传队组织全校师生参观辽源煤矿“万人坑”后，正在结合旧社会矿工的血泪史和现实的阶级斗争，狠批中国赫鲁晓夫的“阶级斗争熄灭”论。 江志顺摄
    工人毛泽东思想宣传队进入教育阵地，是件惊天动地的大事。上海工人毛泽东思想宣传队进驻复旦大学，参加学校的斗、批、改，并且要永远领导学校。新华社记者摄（传真照片）
    进驻清华大学的首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队，决心完成毛主席交给的对知识分子进行再教育的光荣任务。这是工人宣传队的同志在向该校学生进行忆苦思甜的阶级教育。　本报记者摄
    进驻大专院校的首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队中的解放军战士，正在跟老工人一起，认真地学习毛主席关于教育革命的最新指示。
    装甲兵报道组供稿
    首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队进驻大专院校后，院校内出现了一派崭新的气象，革命师生员工在工人阶级的领导下，加强了组织性纪律性，决心紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，统一认识，统一步伐，统一行动，搞好学校的斗、批、改。
    首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队驻新北大政宣组供稿
    驻北京电力学院的首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队，在革命师生中广泛深入地开展谈心活动，耐心地帮助他们用毛泽东思想去解决自己的思想问题，提高阶级斗争和路线斗争觉悟。　杨昌忠摄
    首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队进驻北京大学以后，组织这个学校的革命群众组织负责人学习毛主席的最新指示，在革命师生员工中进行深入细致的思想政治工作，使全校很快实现了革命大联合。这是他们一起敲锣打鼓到市革命委员会报喜。　本报记者摄
