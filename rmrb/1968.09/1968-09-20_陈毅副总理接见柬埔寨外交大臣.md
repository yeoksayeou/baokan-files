Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Cambodian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1968-09-20
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Cambodian Foreign Minister

New China News Agency, September 19th — Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with Prince Norodom Phurissara, the Cambodian Foreign Minister, and his wife, who are currently in Beijing, this afternoon. They had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua and others. Cambodian Ambassador to China Zhang Gang was also present.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet to entertain the Cambodian guests.

### 陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨外交大臣

1968-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨外交大臣
    新华社十九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天下午接见正在北京的柬埔寨外交大臣诺罗敦·富里萨拉亲王和夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长乔冠华等。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待柬埔寨客人。
