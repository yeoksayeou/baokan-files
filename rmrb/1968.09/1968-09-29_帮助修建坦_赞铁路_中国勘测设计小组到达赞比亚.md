Title: Helping to Build the Tanzam Railway
Author:
Date: 1968-09-29
Edition: 6

The Chinese survey and design team for the Tanzania-Zambia Railway arrived in Zambia.

LUNAKA, Sept. 21 (Xinhua) -- Li Zhenhe, Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Zambia, held a reception on Sept. 21 in Lusaka for the Chinese survey and design team that came to help build the Tanzania-Zambia Railway.

Attending the reception were: Zambia's Minister of Finance Mwenda, Minister of Commerce, Industry and Foreign Trade Chimbala, and others.

The reception was held in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

On the same day, Minister Mwenda received some leaders and members of the Chinese survey and design team, expressing a warm welcome to their arrival.

### 帮助修建坦—赞铁路  中国勘测设计小组到达赞比亚

1968-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    帮助修建坦—赞铁路
    中国勘测设计小组到达赞比亚
    新华社讯　卢萨卡消息：中国驻赞比亚大使馆临时代办李振和，二十一日在卢萨卡为帮助修建坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路的中国勘测设计小组到达赞比亚举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有：赞比亚财政部长穆登达，商业、工业和对外贸易部长钦巴等。
    招待会在友好亲切的气氛中进行。
    当天，穆登达部长接见了中国勘测设计小组的一些负责人和成员，对他们的到达表示热烈欢迎。
