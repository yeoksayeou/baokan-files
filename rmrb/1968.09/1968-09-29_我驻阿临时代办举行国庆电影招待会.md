Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Holds Film Reception for National Day

Author:

Date: 1968-09-29

Edition: 6

For the celebration of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Li Tinguan held a film reception at the embassy on the evening of the 26th.

The reception screened Chinese films such as "The Red Lantern" with piano accompaniment, which were warmly received.

Albanian Deputy Foreign Minister Mali, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture Duma, Deputy Minister of Trade Pano, Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee Treska, as well as representatives from Albanian industrial and mining enterprises, government agencies, cultural organizations, and the people's army, attended the reception.

### 我驻阿临时代办举行国庆电影招待会

1968-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻阿临时代办举行国庆电影招待会
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃二十六日晚上在大使馆举行电影招待会。
    招待会上放映了钢琴伴唱《红灯记》等中国影片，受到热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长马利列、教育和文化部副部长杜马、贸易部副部长帕诺、对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡，以及阿尔巴尼亚工矿企业、机关、文化团体和人民军代表等，出席了招待会。
