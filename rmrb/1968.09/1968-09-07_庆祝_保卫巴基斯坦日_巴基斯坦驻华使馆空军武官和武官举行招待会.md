Title: Celebrating "Defend Pakistan Day"
Author:
Date: 1968-09-07
Edition: 5

Pakistan's Air Force Attaché and Military Attaché at the Embassy in China hold a reception

	BEIJING, Sept. 6 (Xinhua) -- Pakistan's Air Force Attaché Colonel Haidar and Military Attaché Colonel Kayum at the Embassy of Pakistan in China held a reception here tonight to celebrate "Defend Pakistan Day."

	Vice Minister of National Defense Su Yu, and responsible officials from the various headquarters, services, and arms of the People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Garrison, including Wang Xinting and Zhang Lingbin, as well as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and relevant officials, were invited to attend the reception. The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

	Pakistan's Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan attended the reception.

	Envoys and military attachés from various countries in China were also invited to attend.

### 庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”  巴基斯坦驻华使馆空军武官和武官举行招待会

1968-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”
    巴基斯坦驻华使馆空军武官和武官举行招待会
    新华社六日讯　巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆空军武官海达上校和武官卡尤姆上校，为庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”，今晚在这里举行招待会。
    国防部副部长粟裕，中国人民解放军各总部、各军种、兵种和北京卫戍区负责人王新亭、张令彬等，以及外交部副部长罗贵波和有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国的使节和大使馆武官也应邀出席。
    （附图片）
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆空军武官海达上校和武官卡尤姆上校，为庆祝“保卫巴基斯坦日”九月六日举行招待会。左起：粟裕、古拉姆·海达、王新亭、卡尤姆在招待会上。
