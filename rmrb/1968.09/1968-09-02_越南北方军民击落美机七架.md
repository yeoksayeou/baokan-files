Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-09-02
Edition: 6

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 31: It was announced here that the military and civilians of North Vietnam recently shot down three more invading American aggressor aircraft.

The military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down one American aircraft each on August 29 and 30.

On August 24, a U.S. reconnaissance aircraft was shot down by local military and civilians over the Vinh Yen area.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 29: It was announced here that on the afternoon of August 28, as soon as a U.S. aggressor unmanned reconnaissance aircraft intruded into the skies over Hanoi, it was immediately shot down by the Hanoi City air defense forces.

Prior to this, on August 26, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: It was announced here that the military and civilians of the North Vietnamese provinces of Ninh Binh and Quang Binh shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft each in battles on August 23 and 24.

### 越南北方军民击落美机七架

1968-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机七架
    据新华社河内一日电　据这里公布：越南北方军民最近又击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架。
    义安省军民八月二十九日和三十日各击落美国飞机一架。
    八月二十四日，一架美国侦察飞机在永灵区上空被当地军民击落。
    新华社河内二十九日电　据这里公布：八月二十八日下午，当一架美国强盗无人驾驶侦察机刚刚侵入河内市上空时，立即被河内市防空部队击落。
    在这以前，广平省军民在二十六日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方河静省和广平省军民，在八月二十三日和二十四日的战斗中，各打落一架美国强盗飞机。
