Title: Chairman Mao Trusts Us Workers, We Are Loyal to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-09-03
Edition: 2

	“Chairman Mao has the utmost trust in us workers, and we workers are the most loyal to Chairman Mao.”
	The revolutionary workers of the Beijing Knitting Factory are seriously studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions. They are determined to spend their entire lives reading Chairman Mao's books, listening to Chairman Mao's words, and acting according to Chairman Mao's instructions, becoming good workers for Chairman Mao.	New China News Agency photographer
