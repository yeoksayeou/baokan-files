Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Somali Guests
Author:
Date: 1968-09-16
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Somali Republic Minister of State Muhammad Sheikh Yusuf Debi, who is currently in Beijing for medical treatment, on September 14. They had a friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Zhang Sirou, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet in honor of the Somali guests.

### 陈毅副总理接见索马里客人

1968-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见索马里客人
    新华社十五日讯　陈毅副总理十四日接见了正在北京治病的索马里共和国国务部长穆罕默德·谢赫·优素夫·代比，同他进行了友好的谈话。接见时，在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。
    接见以后，陈毅副总理设宴招待索马里客人。
