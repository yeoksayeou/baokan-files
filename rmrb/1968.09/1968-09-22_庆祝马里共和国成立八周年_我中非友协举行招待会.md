Title: Celebrating the 8th Anniversary of the Republic of Mali
Author:
Date: 1968-09-22
Edition: 5

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a reception today to enthusiastically celebrate the 8th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Mali.

Vice President of the China-Africa Friendship Association Ding Xilin and the Malian Ambassador to China, Gandou, both spoke at the reception. They jointly wished for the continuous development of the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Mali in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism.

Representatives from the Capital Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team, responsible members from various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, representatives of the Five Good Soldiers from the Beijing Garrison of the People's Liberation Army, and responsible officials from relevant government departments and the Beijing Revolutionary Committee, including Qiao Guanxia, Xie Huai De, and Jia Shi, attended the reception.

The reception was also attended by invitation of the diplomatic staff from the Malian Embassy in China.

### 庆祝马里共和国成立八周年  我中非友协举行招待会

1968-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝马里共和国成立八周年
    我中非友协举行招待会
    新华社二十一日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会今天举行招待会，热烈庆祝马里共和国成立八周年。
    中非友协副会长丁西林和马里驻中国大使甘多先后在招待会上致词，他们共祝中国马里两国人民的战斗友谊在反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的斗争中不断发展。
    首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队的代表、北京市各革命群众组织的负责成员、中国人民解放军驻北京部队五好战士代表，政府有关部门和北京市革命委员会的负责人乔冠华、谢怀德、贾石等，出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有马里驻中国大使馆外交官员。
