Title: Tianjin Machine Tool Accessory Factory Produces High-Precision Adjustable Chuck
Author:
Date: 1968-09-05
Edition: 3

Workers at the Tianjin Machine Tool Accessory Factory have held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, fiercely criticized the counterrevolutionary revisionist line of China's Khrushchev, carried forward the spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle of the proletarian revolution, and successfully developed the country's first "adjustable high-precision three-jaw self-centering chuck." After trials and appraisals by some machine tool factories in Shanghai, Beijing, and Tianjin, it received high praise. Finally, with the approval of the relevant leading departments, it will be put into mass production in the third quarter of this year.

The "adjustable high-precision three-jaw self-centering chuck," commonly known as the high-precision adjustable chuck, is an important accessory for high-precision grinding machines. When installed on a grinding machine, it can adjust the center of the workpiece during the machining process, ensuring the high precision and quality of the machined workpiece.

### 天津机床附件厂制成高精度可调卡盘

1968-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

    天津机床附件厂制成高精度可调卡盘
    天津市机床附件厂的工人，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，狠批中国赫鲁晓夫的反革命修正主义路线，发扬自力更生，艰苦奋斗的无产阶级革命精神，胜利地试制成功了我国第一种“可调整高精度三不自定心卡盘”。经上海、北京、天津的一些机床厂进行试验鉴定，都得到很高的评价。最后，经有关领导部门批准，在今年第三季度正式成批生产。
    “可调整高精度三不自定心卡盘”简称高精度可调卡盘，是高精度磨床上的重要附件，把它安装在磨床上，可以调整工件在加工过程中的中心，保证加工工件的高精度高质量。
