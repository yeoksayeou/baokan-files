Title: Miners' Hearts Turned Toward Chairman Mao
Author: Zhen Yang
Date: 1968-09-22
Edition: 4

	Oil-slicked drill on my shoulder,
	Red light shining from my chest;
	For the revolution, I descend into the mine,
	With a heroic spirit to excavate treasures from the mountains!

	Blood boiling with passion, spirits soaring,
	Feet flying faster than the wind;
	My songs reach the Gate of Heavenly Peace,
	The tunnels connect to Zhongnanhai!

	Miners' hearts turn toward the Party,
	Chairman Mao's teachings forever in our hearts,
	Seize the revolution, promote production,
	Fighting spirit unyielding forever!

	Chairman Mao commands me to the battlefield,
	Clearing the path through thorns and brambles;
	Chairman Mao waves his hand, I march forward,
	Through fire and mountains, my steps never falter!

	Oil-slicked drill on my shoulder,
	Red light shining from my chest;
	Thousands of miles of tunnels connect the continents,
	Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the entire world!

### 矿山工人心向毛主席
振扬
1968-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    矿山工人心向毛主席
    湖南衡阳工人　振扬
    油光闪闪的风钻扛肩上，
    红光灿灿的宝书揣胸怀；
    我为革命下矿井，
    劈山取宝志豪迈！
    热血沸腾豪情激，
    双脚如飞比风快；
    歌声飞向天安门，
    巷道连着中南海！
    矿山工人心向党，
    毛主席教导记心怀，
    抓革命，促生产，
    斗志昂扬永不衰！
    毛主席指挥我上阵，
    披荆斩棘把路开；
    毛主席挥手我前进，
    火海刀山脚不歪！
    油光闪闪的风钻扛肩上，
    红光灿灿的宝书揣胸怀。
    千里井巷连五洲呀，
    毛泽东思想普照全世界！
