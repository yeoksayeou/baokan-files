Title: Do Not Allow the Sato Government to Turn Saga Prefecture into a U.S. Military Base in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-09-10
Edition: 6

About three thousand patriotic workers, farmers, and students from Saga Prefecture held a rally in Karatsu City on the afternoon of the 8th, strongly condemning the Sato reactionary government's tricks to move the U.S. Air Force base in Itazuke to the northern part of Saga Prefecture in the Chikushi area, attempting to quell the people's opposition.

This rally was organized by the Joint Struggle Committee Against the Relocation of the U.S. Military Base to Chikushi. Large banners were hung in the venue, reading: "Let us fight to the end to smash the U.S.-Japan Security Treaty!" and "We do not allow Saga Prefecture to become a base for invading Vietnam!"

Representative Miyazaki Shigeru of the Joint Struggle Committee spoke at the meeting, denouncing U.S. imperialism and the Sato reactionary government for dragging Japan deeper and deeper into the U.S. imperialist aggression in Asia. He said: "Whether to allow the U.S.-Japan Security Treaty or to smash it, this is a serious issue concerning the dignity of the Japanese nation. We must resolutely smash the security system and win Japan's independence and peace."

At the meeting, representatives of workers, farmers, and citizens condemned the crimes of the Japanese Communist Party's Miyamoto revisionist group, which slandered and attempted to split the anti-U.S. patriotic struggle of the people of Saga Prefecture. A member of the Miyamoto group who infiltrated the meeting openly jumped up to support the U.S.-Japanese reactionaries, and many participants shouted angrily at him: "Parliamentary fool, get out!"

After the meeting, the participants held a demonstration march.

### 不许佐藤政府把佐贺县变成侵越美军基地  日本佐贺县三千爱国工人农民学生集会

1968-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    不许佐藤政府把佐贺县变成侵越美军基地
    日本佐贺县三千爱国工人农民学生集会
    谴责日共宫本集团污蔑并妄图分裂人民群众反美爱国斗争的罪行
    据新华社九日讯　据东方通讯社报道，佐贺县大约三千名爱国工人、农民和学生，八日下午在唐津市举行集会，强烈谴责佐藤反动政府玩弄诡计，要把板付美国空军基地迁往佐贺县北部切木地区，妄图以此平息人民的反对。
    这次集会是由反对美军基地迁往切木地区共同斗争组织主持的。会场上挂着巨幅标语，上面写着：“让我们为粉碎日美安全条约斗争到底！”“我们不准把佐贺县变成侵越基地！”
    共同斗争组织的代表宫崎茂在会上发言，谴责美帝国主义和佐藤反动政府把日本越来越深地拖入美帝在亚洲进行的侵略战争中去。他说：“是容许日美安全条约还是粉碎它，在这两条道路中作出抉择，是一个有关日本民族的尊严的严重问题。我们必须坚决粉碎安全体系，赢得日本的独立与和平。”
    会上，一些工人、农民和市民的代表纷纷谴责日共宫本修正主义集团污蔑并且企图分裂佐贺县人民群众的反美爱国斗争的罪行。有一个混入大会的宫本集团分子公然跳出来为美日反动派帮腔，许多参加集会的人对他愤怒地高呼：“议会迷，滚出去！”
    会后，参加集会的人举行了示威游行。
