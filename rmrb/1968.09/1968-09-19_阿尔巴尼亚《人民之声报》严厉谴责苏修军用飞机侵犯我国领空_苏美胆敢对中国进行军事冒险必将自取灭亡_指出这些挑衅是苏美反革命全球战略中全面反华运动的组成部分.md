Title: The Albanian "People's Voice" Newspaper Severely Condemns Soviet Revisionist Military Aircraft's Invasion of Our Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-09-19
Edition: 5

The Albanian "People's Voice" Newspaper severely condemns the provocative activities of Soviet revisionist military aircraft invading China's airspace, and points out that these provocative incidents are part of the comprehensive anti-China movement in the global counter-revolutionary strategy of the Soviet Union and the United States.

The editorial article published on September 18 states that the provocative actions of the Soviet modern revisionists have aroused great indignation and hatred among the Albanian people. The Albanian people stand in solidarity with the Chinese people, resolutely condemning the Soviet revisionists' anti-China provocations.

The article points out that the Soviet revisionist military aircraft's provocations against the People's Republic of China are not accidental or isolated incidents. They are premeditated actions by the Soviet revisionist leadership and a continuation of their anti-China activities. When the Soviet revisionists and their lackeys invaded Czechoslovakia in August with a sudden attack, they especially intensified military provocations against the People's Republic of China. The connection between the invasion of Czechoslovakia and the military provocations against the People's Republic of China is obvious, part of a well-coordinated plan.

The article points out that the Soviet modern revisionists and American imperialists are using all means, from slander and provocation to outright banditry, to sabotage and obstruct the great Chinese people's progress along the revolutionary and socialist path. They attempt to strengthen the "encirclement" of China with various means, isolate China, and restore capitalism in China. The Soviet revisionists and American imperialists have high hopes for the conspirators, for the Chinese Khrushchov to seize the fortress from within. However, their plans and hopes have been shattered by the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution initiated and led by the great Marxist-Leninist Mao Zedong. The establishment of revolutionary committees throughout China marks the complete failure and bankruptcy of the Chinese Khrushchov's counter-revolutionary conspiracy, and the shameful failure of the plans and hopes of the American imperialists, Soviet modern revisionists, and all other reactionaries to obstruct the people's China from advancing along the revolutionary and socialist path.

The article further points out that the Soviet modern revisionists, in collusion with American imperialists, provoke the People's Republic of China because the People's Republic of China has become an insurmountable obstacle to their counter-revolutionary conspiracy to establish world hegemony. Because the great People's China is a powerful and loyal defender of the people of all countries, and because the great People's China is a staunch fighter against American imperialism and Soviet revisionism, for the victory of the great cause of revolution and socialism.

The article concludes by stating that no matter what tricks the Soviet modern revisionists and American imperialists play, they cannot stop the seven hundred million Chinese people from advancing victoriously along the revolutionary and socialist path. Faced with these despicable provocations from the Soviet modern revisionists, the Chinese people will further enhance their revolutionary vigilance and unite more closely around the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao. If the Soviet modern revisionists or American imperialists dare to launch a military adventure against the People's Republic of China, they will surely meet their doom.

### 阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》严厉谴责苏修军用飞机侵犯我国领空  苏美胆敢对中国进行军事冒险必将自取灭亡  指出这些挑衅是苏美反革命全球战略中全面反华运动的组成部分

1968-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》严厉谴责苏修军用飞机侵犯我国领空
    苏美胆敢对中国进行军事冒险必将自取灭亡
    指出这些挑衅是苏美反革命全球战略中全面反华运动的组成部分
    新华社地拉那十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》十八日发表编辑部文章，严厉谴责苏修军用飞机侵犯中国领空的挑衅活动，并且指出，这些挑衅事件是苏美为建立世界霸权而推行的反革命全球战略中全面反华运动的组成部分。
    文章说，苏联现代修正主义者的这些反华挑衅事件，激起了阿尔巴尼亚人民极大的愤慨和仇恨。阿尔巴尼亚人民和中国人民同仇敌忾，坚决谴责苏修的这些反华挑衅活动。
    文章指出，苏修军用飞机对中华人民共和国进行挑衅绝不是偶然的，也不是什么孤立的和出乎意料的事件。它是苏修领导集团经过预先策划而进行的，是苏修领导集团反华活动的继续。莫斯科的阴谋分子在苏修及其仆从八月间以突然袭击的方式出兵占领捷克斯洛伐克的时候，特别扩大了对中华人民共和国的军事挑衅。对捷克斯洛伐克的侵略和对中华人民共和国的军事挑衅之间的联系是明显的，是一个配合好了的计划。
    文章指出，苏联现代修正主义者和美帝国主义者都正在动用从诽谤、挑衅直至强盗般袭击的一切手段来破坏和阻挡伟大的中国人民沿着革命和社会主义道路前进。他们妄图用各种方式加强对中国的“火力包围圈”，孤立中国，在中国复辟资本主义。苏修和美帝对阴谋分子、对中国赫鲁晓夫从“内部夺取堡垒”寄予了很大的希望。但是，他们的计划和希望被由伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛泽东同志发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命粉碎了。中国全国成立革命委员会标志着中国赫鲁晓夫反革命大阴谋的彻底失败和破产，标志着美帝国主义者、苏联现代修正主义者和其他一切反动派企图阻止人民中国沿着革命和社会主义道路前进的计划和希望的可耻破产。
    文章接着指出，苏联现代修正主义者协同美帝国主义者向中华人民共和国进行挑衅，是因为中华人民共和国成了他们实现建立世界霸权的反革命阴谋的不可逾越的障碍，是因为伟大的人民中国是各国人民强大的和忠实的保卫者，是因为伟大的人民中国是反对美帝和苏修、为革命和社会主义伟大事业的胜利而斗争的坚强战士。
    文章最后指出，不管苏联现代修正主义者和美帝国主义者干什么样的勾当，他们都不能阻挡七亿中国人民在革命和社会主义道路上的胜利前进。面对苏联现代修正主义者这些可恶的挑衅，中国人民将更加加强他们的革命警惕性，更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的周围。如果苏联现代修正主义者或美帝国主义者胆敢对中华人民共和国进行军事冒险，那么他们必将自取灭亡。
