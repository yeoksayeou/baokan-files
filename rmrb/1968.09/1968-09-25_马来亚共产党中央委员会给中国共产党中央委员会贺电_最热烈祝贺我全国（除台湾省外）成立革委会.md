Title: The Central Committee of the Malayan Communist Party Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1968-09-25
Edition: 4

The Central Committee of the Malayan Communist Party sent a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party on September 15, expressing the most enthusiastic congratulations on the establishment of revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions of China (excluding Taiwan Province). The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Dear Comrades:

On the eve of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, revolutionary committees have been successfully established in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions of China (excluding Taiwan Province). This is not only a great joy for the political life of the Chinese people but also a great joy for the political life of people all over the world. All members of the Malayan Communist Party, all officers and soldiers of the Malayan National Liberation Army, and the Malayan people are filled with boundless joy and inspiration. We extend our warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the 700 million Chinese people.

Under the wise leadership of the Proletarian Headquarters headed by Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Lin Biao, the hundreds of millions of revolutionary masses have engaged in extremely fierce and complex struggles with the class enemy, achieving a great victory in the Cultural Revolution nationwide. This is a great victory for the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, a great victory for Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

This victory of great historical significance further consolidates the dictatorship of the proletariat, making China, the center and base of the world revolution, even stronger, and turning socialist China into an invincible revolutionary fortress. This is a great encouragement and support for the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of all countries.

This brilliant victory has smashed the conspiracy of a handful of counter-revolutionaries headed by the Chinese Khrushchev to restore capitalism in China, and it has also shattered the illusions of imperialism and modern revisionism in achieving a "peaceful evolution" in China. This is a severe blow to the criminal collusion of U.S. imperialism and Soviet revisionism in their attempt to redivide the world.

The Malayan Communist Party and the Malayan people regard the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by the greatest genius of our times, Chairman Mao, as a great revolution that concerns the destiny of mankind. We are deeply convinced that China, which accounts for one-fourth of the world's population, is undergoing and will undergo unprecedented great changes in all fields, which will have a profound impact on the future of the international communist movement and all mankind. We sincerely wish the Chinese people greater and more brilliant achievements in their struggle to win a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Long live the comprehensive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

Long live the banner of our times—Mao Zedong Thought!

Long live, long live, and ten thousand times long live the great teacher and great leader of the world's people, Chairman Mao!

### 马来亚共产党中央委员会给中国共产党中央委员会贺电  最热烈祝贺我全国（除台湾省外）成立革委会

1968-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚共产党中央委员会给中国共产党中央委员会贺电
    最热烈祝贺我全国（除台湾省外）成立革委会
    新华社二十四日讯　马来亚共产党中央委员会十五日给中国共产党中央委员会一封贺电，最热烈祝贺我国各省、市、自治区（除台湾省外）革命委员会全部成立。贺电全文如下：中国共产党中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    在中华人民共和国国庆十九周年将要来临的时候，中国全国（除台湾省外）各省、市、自治区全部胜利地成立了革命委员会。这不但是中国人民政治生活中的一件大喜事，同时也是各国人民政治生活中的一件大喜事。马来亚共产党全体党员、马来亚民族解放军全体指战员和马来亚人民为此感到无限欢欣鼓舞。我们向你们，并通过你们向七亿中国人民致以最热烈的祝贺。
    在以毛泽东主席为首、林彪副主席为副的无产阶级司令部的英明领导下，亿万革命群众同阶级敌人进行了异常激烈复杂的斗争，实现了全国山河一片红，在全国范围内赢得了无产阶级文化大革命的决定性胜利。这是战无不胜的毛泽东思想的伟大胜利，是毛主席无产阶级革命路线的伟大胜利。
    这个具有重大历史意义的胜利进一步巩固了无产阶级专政，使世界革命的中心和根据地——中国变得更为强大，使社会主义中国成为不可战胜的革命堡垒。这对各国人民的革命斗争是一个巨大的鼓舞和支援。
    这个辉煌的胜利粉碎了以中国赫鲁晓夫为首的一小撮反革命分子在中国复辟资本主义的阴谋，也粉碎了帝国主义和现代修正主义在中国实现“和平演变”的幻想。这对美帝、苏修加紧勾结重新瓜分世界的罪恶勾当是一个极其沉重的打击。
    马来亚共产党人和马来亚人民把当代最伟大的天才毛主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命看作是关系到人类命运的大革命。我们深信，占世界人口四分之一的中国，在各个领域中正在发生和将要发生的史无前例的伟大变革，必将对国际共产主义运动和全人类的前途发生极为深远的影响。我们最衷心地祝愿中国人民在夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的斗争中取得更加光辉灿烂的成就。
    无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！
    我们时代的旗帜——毛泽东思想万岁！
    全世界人民的伟大导师、伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！
    马来亚共产党中央委员会
    一九六八年九月十五日
