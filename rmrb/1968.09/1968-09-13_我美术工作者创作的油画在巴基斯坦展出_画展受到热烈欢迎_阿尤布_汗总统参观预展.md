Title: Chinese Artists' Oil Paintings Exhibited in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1968-09-13
Edition: 5

An exhibition of ten oil paintings created by Chinese artists opened on September 9 at the Pakistan Military Museum in Rawalpindi. These paintings vividly depict the brave struggle of Pakistani military and civilians against the Indian armed invasion in September 1965, and have been warmly welcomed by Pakistani friends.

Pakistani President Ayub Khan, accompanied by Army Chief of Staff General Yahya Khan, visited the pre-exhibition on September 6, which marked the third anniversary of India's large-scale armed invasion of Pakistan.

Pakistani Army Chief of Staff Lieutenant General Shahabzada Yakub Khan cut the ribbon at the opening ceremony.

Chinese artists, invited by Pakistan, visited the country and created ten oil paintings. The main themes of these paintings include: brave Pakistani soldiers defending the heroic city of Lahore; Pakistani soldiers repelling Indian tank attacks near Sialkot's Chawinda; soldiers crossing the Tawi River in Kashmir; and the chaotic retreat of Indian invaders, among others.

The works of Chinese artists have been highly praised by Pakistani friends. One Pakistani friend said, "These oil paintings by Chinese artists not only depict in the best artistic form the entire process of brave Pakistani soldiers defending their homeland and resisting Indian invaders, but also demonstrate the growing friendship between China and Pakistan in the face of a common enemy."

### 我美术工作者创作的油画在巴基斯坦展出  画展受到热烈欢迎　阿尤布·汗总统参观预展

1968-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我美术工作者创作的油画在巴基斯坦展出
    画展受到热烈欢迎　阿尤布·汗总统参观预展
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十一日电由中国美术工作者创作的十幅油画九日在这里的巴基斯坦军事博物馆开始展出。这些油画生动地描绘了巴基斯坦军民一九六五年九月反击印度武装侵略的英勇战斗的形象，受到巴基斯坦明友的热烈欢迎。
    巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗在陆军总司令叶海亚汗将军的陪同下，在九月六日“保卫巴基斯坦日”——印度对巴基斯坦发动大规模武装侵略三周年时，参观了油画的预展。
    巴基斯坦陆军总参谋长夏哈布扎达·雅各布·汗少将在开幕式上为画展剪彩。
    中国美术工作者曾应巴基斯坦的邀请，到巴基斯坦访问，并创作了十幅油画。中国美术工作者创作的十幅油画的主要内容有，英勇的巴基斯坦战士捍卫英雄的拉合尔市；巴基斯坦战士在锡亚尔科特附近的齐温达击退印度坦克的袭击；战士们渡过克什米尔的塔维河以及印度侵略者狼狈逃跑的场面等。
    中国美术工作者的作品受到巴基斯坦朋友的热烈赞赏。一位巴基斯坦朋友说：“中国艺术家的这些油画不仅用最好的美术形式体现了英勇的巴基斯坦士兵保卫祖国、反对印度侵略者的全部经过情形，而且也是反对共同敌人的巴中友谊日益增长的又一表现。”
