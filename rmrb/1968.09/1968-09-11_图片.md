Title: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution Promotes Rapid Development of Industry in Tibet
Author:
Date: 1968-09-11
Edition: 5

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has promoted the rapid development of industry in Tibet. Tibet, which previously had no textile industry, has established its first woolen mill during the Cultural Revolution. The picture shows a corner of the woolen mill. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
