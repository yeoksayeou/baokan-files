Title: Down with the Plundering U.S. Imperialism - Ecuadorian Students Stage Angry Protests
Author:
Date: 1968-09-08
Edition: 6

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on September 7, hundreds of students in Guayaquil, the largest city in Ecuador, recently held a march and protest to denounce the plundering of Ecuador by U.S. imperialism.

The protesting students marched through the main streets of Guayaquil, shouting slogans such as "Down with the U.S. imperialism that plunders our country's wealth" and other anti-U.S. chants. After the march, the angry students rushed towards the U.S. Consulate in Guayaquil and threw stones at it, expressing their deep hatred for U.S. imperialism.

The protesting students also held up signs reading "Traitors will be defeated," condemning the crimes of revisionist elements who attempted to sabotage this anti-U.S. march.

### 打倒掠夺成性的美帝国主义  厄瓜多尔学生愤怒示威

1968-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    打倒掠夺成性的美帝国主义
    厄瓜多尔学生愤怒示威
    据新华社七日讯　厄瓜多尔最大城市瓜亚基尔的数百名学生最近举行游行示威，抗议美帝国主义对厄瓜多尔的掠夺。
    示威学生经过了瓜亚基尔市的主要街道，他们高呼“打倒掠夺我国财富的美帝国主义”等反美口号。游行结束后，愤怒的学生奔向美国驻瓜亚基尔市的领事馆，并向它投掷石块，表示他们对美帝国主义的无比仇恨。
    示威学生还举着“叛徒必败”等标语牌，谴责修正主义分子妄图破坏这一反美游行的罪行。
