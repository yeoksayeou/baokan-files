Title: Workers and Soldiers of the Fushun Cement Factory Denounce the "Expert Management" Counter-Revolutionary Revisionist Line of the Chinese Khrushchev
Author: Liu Zhesi, Hong Tong
Date: 1968-09-12
Edition: 3

Workers of the Fushun Cement Factory and stationed soldiers together denounce the counter-revolutionary revisionist line of "expert management" advocated by the Chinese Khrushchev. This greatly promotes the daring to think and daring to act revolutionary spirit of the working class.
