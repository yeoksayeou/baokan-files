Title: The First Pharmaceutical Workshop in Zanzibar, Built with Chinese Aid, is Completed
Author:
Date: 1968-09-03
Edition: 4

The first pharmaceutical workshop in Zanzibar, built with Chinese aid, was completed on August 24. The completion ceremony was held at Lenin Hospital in Zanzibar, with the attendance of the First Vice President of Tanzania, Karume, who also delivered a speech.

This workshop, equipped with Chinese machinery and instruments, is located in a building within Lenin Hospital. It is a gift from the Chinese government to the Zanzibar government of Tanzania.

Attending the ceremony were ministers of Zanzibar, members of the Revolutionary Council, officials from the Zanzibar Ministry of Health, and medical staff from Zanzibar.

Members of the Chinese medical team working there also attended the ceremony.

In his speech, Karume, the First Vice President of Tanzania, expressed his gratitude to the Chinese government for its assistance to the people of Zanzibar. He said that this assistance would help the people of Zanzibar in their efforts to prevent diseases, achieve self-reliance, and develop the economy.

Liu Qingyou, the Deputy Consul of China in Zanzibar, formally handed over the equipment of the workshop to the Zanzibar government of Tanzania on behalf of the Chinese government.

### 我援建的桑给巴尔岛上第一个制药车间落成  坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆主持典礼，并发表讲话

1968-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我援建的桑给巴尔岛上第一个制药车间落成
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆主持典礼，并发表讲话
    据新华社桑给巴尔电　由中国帮助建立的桑给巴尔岛上的第一个制药车间，八月二十四日在坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆主持下，在这里的列宁医院举行落成典礼。
    这个用中国机器和仪器装备起来的制药车间，就设在列宁医院的一幢房子里，是中国政府赠送给坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔政府的礼物。
    出席仪式的有桑给巴尔的部长们和革命委员会的成员，还有桑给巴尔卫生部的官员和桑给巴尔的医务人员。
    在这里工作的中国医疗队人员也出席了仪式。
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆在仪式上发表讲话，感谢中国政府领导人对桑给巴尔人民的这一帮助。他说，这一帮助将有助于桑给巴尔人民在防治疾病、实现自力更生和发展经济方面的努力。
    中国驻桑给巴尔副领事刘庆有代表中国政府把这个车间的设备正式移交给坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔政府。
