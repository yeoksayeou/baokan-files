Title: The Red Sun Shines Over the Mountains and Rivers
Author:
Date: 1968-09-08
Edition: 5

The red sun shines over the mountains and rivers,
A rainbow covers the countless mountains and rivers,
Seven hundred million people all smile with joy,
Singing praises to the leader Mao Zedong.

### 红日照山河

1968-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    红日照山河
    上海第八丝织厂女工汪婉华一轮红日照山河，千山万水披彩虹，七亿人民齐欢笑，歌唱领袖毛泽东。
