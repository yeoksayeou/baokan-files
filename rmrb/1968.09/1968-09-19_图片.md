Title: Revolutionary Teachers and Students of Tianjin Textile Institute Learn from Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1968-09-19
Edition: 3

Revolutionary teachers and students of Tianjin Textile Institute have gone deep into factories, rural areas, and the military, learning from workers, peasants, and soldiers. They work together, engage in self-criticism and criticism of revisionism, and carry out educational reforms. This is a scene of revolutionary teachers and students from the Woolen Class 63 of the institute learning from veteran workers at Tianjin First Woolen Mill. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
