Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-09-19

The Party of Labour of Albania and the Albanian people have consistently held high the great red banner of Marxism-Leninism. They have resolutely opposed the policy of aggression and war of U.S. imperialism and have engaged in a struggle of principle with modern revisionism, which has the Soviet Communist Party leadership as its center.
