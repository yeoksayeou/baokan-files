Title: Algerian Ministry of Health Hosts Banquet for Chinese Medical Team
Author:
Date: 1968-09-26
Edition: 5

The second batch of the Chinese medical team, after serving the people of Algeria for more than two years, left the Algerian capital, Algiers, on the 22nd to return home.

On the eve of their departure, the Algerian Ministry of Health held a banquet to see off the entire team and presented each member with a gift.

### 阿尔及利亚卫生部宴请我医疗队

1968-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚卫生部宴请我医疗队
    据新华社阿尔及尔电　第二批中国医疗队，在阿尔及利亚为阿尔及利亚人民服务了两年多之后，于二十二日离开阿尔及利亚首都阿尔及尔回国。
    离开的前夕，阿尔及利亚卫生部设宴欢送这批中国医疗队的全体成员，并且向他们每人赠送一份礼物。
