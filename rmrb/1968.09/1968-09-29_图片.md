Title: Interpreting the Implementation of "Downsizing and Streamlining"
Author: Xiang Dongbing, Zhang Qibing
Date: 1968-09-29
Edition: 3

Leaders of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Medium Plate Mill Revolutionary Committee, with the help of the People's Liberation Army, have earnestly implemented Chairman Mao's great directive on "downsizing and streamlining." They have gone deep into the workshops to mobilize the masses to carry out a revolutionary big-character poster campaign, which has powerfully promoted the development of the revolution and production. The picture shows military representatives and revolutionary cadres discussing with workers the great significance of "downsizing and streamlining."
