Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Eight U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-09-05
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down eight U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, September 2. According to reports from here: On September 1, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	Additionally, on August 28 and 31, the military and civilians of Nghe An and Quang Binh Provinces also shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, September 3. According to reports from here: In the past three days, the military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down four more invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	On September 1, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province consecutively shot down one U.S. Navy helicopter and one U.S. F-4 fighter jet. On September 2 and 3, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down one U.S. F-8 fighter jet and one U.S. unmanned reconnaissance drone, respectively.

### 越南军民击落美机八架

1968-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机八架
    新华社河内二日电　据这里公布：越南北方广宁省军民一日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    另，八月二十八日和三十一日，义安和河静两省军民还击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    新华社河内三日电　据这里宣布：越南北方军民最近三天又击落四架入侵的美国强盗飞机。
    河静省军民一日曾接连打落一架美国海军直升飞机和一架美国F—4战斗机。义安省军民二日和三日曾分别打落一架美国F—8战斗机和一架美国无人驾驶侦察机。
