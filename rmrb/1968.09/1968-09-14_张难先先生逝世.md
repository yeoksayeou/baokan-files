Title: Mr. Zhang Nanshen Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-09-14
Edition: 4

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress announced that Zhang Nanshen passed away from illness on September 11 in Beijing.

Zhang Nanshen was a native of Stainyang County, Hubei Province. He was 95 years old.

### 张难先先生逝世

1968-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    张难先先生逝世
    新华社十三日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张难先先生，因病于九月十一日在北京逝世。
    张难先先生是湖北省沔阳县人，终年九十五岁。
