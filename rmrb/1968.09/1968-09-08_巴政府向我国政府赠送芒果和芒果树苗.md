Title: The Government of Pakistan Presents Mangoes and Mango Saplings to the Government of China
Author:
Date: 1968-09-08
Edition: 6

The Government of Pakistan has presented to the Government and people of China one hundred high-quality mangoes and one hundred mango saplings as a symbol of Pak-China friendship.

On August 31, Malik Huda Bashir, Minister of Agriculture and Food of West Pakistan, spoke at the presentation ceremony held at the airport in Lahore, the capital of West Pakistan. He said on behalf of the Central Government and people of Pakistan that the gift was presented "to express the ever-growing friendship and fraternal feelings between the two countries."

Malik Huda Bashir asked the Chinese friends to convey to the great people of China and their great leader Chairman Mao the friendly sentiments of the people, government, and President Ayub Khan of Pakistan.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, Xu Ying, spoke on behalf of the Government and people of China. He expressed thanks to the Government, people, and President Ayub Khan of Pakistan for the friendly sentiments they have shown.

### 巴政府向我国政府赠送芒果和芒果树苗

1968-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    巴政府向我国政府赠送芒果和芒果树苗
    新华社拉合尔五日电　巴基斯坦政府向中国政府和人民赠送了一百个优良品种的芒果和一百棵芒果树苗，作为巴中友谊的象征。
    西巴基斯坦政府农业和粮食部长马利克·胡达·巴希八月三十一日在西巴基斯坦首府拉合尔机场举行的赠送仪式上，代表巴基斯坦中央政府和人民发表讲话说，赠送这批礼物，“是为了表示中巴两国日益增进的友谊和兄弟情谊”。
    马利克·胡达·巴希请中国朋友向伟大的中国人民和中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席转达巴基斯坦人民、政府和阿尤布·汗总统的友好情谊。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英在讲话中代表中国政府和人民，对巴基斯坦政府、人民和阿尤布·汗总统所表示的友好情谊，表示感谢。
