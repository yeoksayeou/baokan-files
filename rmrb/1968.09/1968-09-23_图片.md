Title: Mozambican Freedom Fighters Cherish Chairman Mao's Portrait Badges
Author:
Date: 1968-09-23
Edition: 5

The Mozambican freedom fighters, who are bravely fighting to overthrow the Portuguese colonial rule backed by American imperialism, hold an immense love for the great leader Chairman Mao. This photo captures them joyfully pinning the radiant portrait badges of Chairman Mao on their comrades.
