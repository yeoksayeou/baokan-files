Title: Ode to the Red Sun (Two Poems)
Author: Mao Shiying Zheng Deming
Date: 1968-09-23
Edition: 6

	Ode to the Red Sun (Two Poems)
	Soldiers of the Liberation Army Mao Shiying Zheng Deming

	The red lighthouse stands in the east,
	The waves under the Golden Water Bridge,
	Beat against the thousand-mile-long embankment;
	The dawn on Tiananmen,
	Writes glorious verses.
	Ah! Beijing rises with the red sun,
	Its rays illuminate the golden land;
	The lights before us are the brightest,
	How can we forget Chairman Mao!
	See! Red flowers bloom at the Martyrs' Monument,
	Like the torches of the Autumn Harvest Uprising;
	Chang'an Street is the broadest,
	Etched with the footsteps of revolutionary soldiers.
	The sentry by the Zhongnanhai,
	Do you hear the urgent hoofbeats outside Luoshan Pass?
	The red lights in Huairentang shine,
	Reflecting the battle flags under Baota Mountain.
	The cypress trees at Tiananmen,
	Transform into ten-thousand-foot-long ropes to bind the enemy;
	Chairman Mao waves his hand, announcing the spring thunder,
	Sunlight covers the land of our motherland.
	Ah! The Cultural Revolution's thunder roars,
	Transforming rivers and mountains,
	Mao Zedong Thought shines with golden light,
	Illuminating the skies of the five continents and four seas.
	Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,
	You stand like a red lighthouse in the east,
	Your rays shine across the globe,
	Welcoming a brilliant new century!

	Soldiers Sew the Red Sun
	With every stitch, blood surges,
	In the heart, passion overflows;
	Soldiers sew the red sun,
	Hearts bloom with the morning sun.
	Every stitch opens the red clouds,
	The red sun rises with golden wings;
	Every thread weaves a thousand beams of light,
	The red sun in the heart shines brightly.
	Every stitch connects to Beijing,
	Beijing is home to the greatest genius of humanity;
	Every thread sews the character "loyalty,"
	Loyalty offered to our wise commander.
	Needles and threads carry a thousand meanings,
	Gold and silver threads hold endless love;
	The red sun shines brighter in the heart,
	Revolutionary passion fills the chest.
	Standing on tiptoes, looking towards Beijing,
	Tears of happiness stream down the face;
	The red heart sews the red sun,
	Rays of light shine in all directions.
	Pieces of red clouds fly in the sky,
	Sunflowers bloom towards the sun,
	Deep emotions are woven into the threads,
	The red sun illuminates the entire world.

### 红太阳颂（二首）
毛世英　郑德铭
1968-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

    红太阳颂（二首）
    解放军战士　毛世英　郑德铭
    红色灯塔东方立
    金水桥下的浪花，
    拍响千百里长堤；
    天安门上的朝霞，
    写满光辉的诗句。
    啊！北京升起红太阳，
    光芒照亮金色的土地；
    眼前灯火最辉煌啊，
    怎能忘怀领袖毛主席！
    看！烈士碑前红花开，
    好象那秋收起义的火炬；
    长安街道最广阔，
    刻满着革命战士的足迹。
    中南海边的哨兵啊，
    你可听见娄山关外马蹄急！？
    怀仁堂里红灯亮啊！
    辉映着宝塔山下的战旗。
    那天安门前的青松，
    化作万丈长缨缚仇敌；
    毛主席挥手报春雷啊，
    阳光铺满祖国的大地。
    啊！文化革命风雷激，
    万水千山改天地，
    毛泽东思想金光闪啊，
    映红五洲四海的天际。
    毛主席啊毛主席，
    您象红色灯塔东方立，
    光芒万丈照全球，
    迎来光辉灿烂的新世纪！
    战士敬绣红太阳
    全身，热血澎湃，
    胸中，豪情满怀；
    战士敬绣红太阳啊，
    心花朝阳并蒂开。
    一针针挑开满天红霞，
    红日鼓动金翅升起来；
    一线线牵来万道金光，
    心中的红太阳大放异彩。
    针针连着北京城啊，
    北京住着人类最伟大的天才；
    线线绣在“忠”字上啊，
    忠心献给我们英明的统帅。
    飞针走线千重意，
    金丝银线万般爱；
    红太阳照心心更亮，
    革命的激情满胸怀。
    踮起脚儿望北京啊，
    幸福的热泪挂满腮；
    红心绣出红太阳，
    霞光万道放光彩。
    片片红云飞天上，
    朵朵葵花向阳开，
    深情凝聚针线上啊，
    绣得红太阳照亮全世界。
