Title: Black Students in Illinois Protest and March
Author:
Date: 1968-09-12
Edition: 5

		Three hundred black students at the University of Illinois, with the support and active participation of many white students, held a protest march on campus on the 10th, strongly demanding improvements in the living conditions of black students.

		During this struggle, the students angrily tore apart a photograph of the school's reactionary president.

		The reactionary authorities in the United States, in the dead of night, sent in a large number of police to secretly enter the school and launch a sudden attack on the black students, madly arresting a large number of black students.

		At the same time, hundreds of black people in Syracuse, New York, held a protest march on the 8th, protesting against the reactionary ruling clique's racial discrimination against blacks, and engaged in fierce struggles with the police. From the night of the 9th to the morning of the 10th, black youths in Syracuse, despite police suppression, took to the streets in groups, throwing stones and bottles at the shops and cars of white racists.

### 美国伊利诺斯州黑人大学生示威游行  许多白人学生支持和积极参加黑人斗争

1968-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    美国伊利诺斯州黑人大学生示威游行
    许多白人学生支持和积极参加黑人斗争
    新华社十一日讯　华盛顿消息：美国伊利诺斯州伊利诺斯大学的三百多名黑人学生，在许多白人学生的支持和积极参加下，十日在校园内举行示威游行，强烈要求改善黑人学生的居住条件。
    在这场斗争中，学生们愤怒地把这个学校的反动校长的照片撕得粉碎。
    美国反动当局竟然在午夜后出动大批警察偷偷开进这个学校，对黑人学生进行突然袭击，疯狂地逮捕了大批黑人学生。
    与此同时，纽约州锡拉丘兹的数百名黑人群众八日举行示威游行，抗议反动统治集团对黑人的种族歧视，并同警察进行了激烈的搏斗。从九日晚到十日清晨，锡拉丘兹的黑人青少年们不顾警察的镇压，分批走上街头，用石块和瓶子猛砸白人种族主义者的商店和汽车。
