Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Guests from South Vietnam and Laos
Author:
Date: 1968-09-29
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Guests from South Vietnam and Laos

	New China News Agency, September 28th

	Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with Huoi Ba, a member of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and Nguyen Thuc Binh, a war hero from South Vietnam, who are currently in Beijing. They had a friendly conversation.

	Attending the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and others.

	Nguyen Van Khoa, Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, and Wu Mingluan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, were also present.

	After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet in honor of the Vietnamese guests.

	New China News Agency, September 28th

	Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with Xieng Khouang, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, Tham, and Noy, members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee, who are currently in Beijing. They had a friendly conversation.

	Attending the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and others.

	After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet in honor of the Lao guests.

### 陈毅副总理接见越南南方和老挝客人

1968-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见越南南方和老挝客人
    新华社二十八日讯国务院副总理陈毅，今天上午接见正在北京的越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员胡惠巴、越南南方战斗英雄黄叔伯，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长罗贵波等。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广、越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也在座。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待越南客人。
    新华社二十八日讯国务院副总理陈毅，二十七日上午接见正在北京的老挝爱国战线党中央委员会副主席西吞·库马丹，老挝爱国战线党中央常委提门和中央常委涅乌，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长罗贵波等。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待老挝客人。
