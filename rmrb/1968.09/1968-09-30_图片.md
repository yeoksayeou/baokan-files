Title: Delegation from Albania Led by Comrade Baluku Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-09-30
Edition: 4

Tens of thousands of people lined the streets to give a warm welcome to the Albanian Party and government delegation led by Comrade Baluku. The delegation, along with Chinese leaders such as Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Yao Wenyuan, who came to welcome them, posed for a group photo at the airport. Xinhua News Agency photographer.

On September 29, Premier Zhou Enlai held a grand banquet to warmly welcome Comrade Bekir Baluku and the entire delegation of the Albanian Party and government.

The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Baluku at the banquet. Xinhua News Agency photographer.

Comrade Baluku, along with Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, visited the Red Guards who came to welcome the Albanian guests and posed for a commemorative photo with them.

Xinhua News Agency photographer.
