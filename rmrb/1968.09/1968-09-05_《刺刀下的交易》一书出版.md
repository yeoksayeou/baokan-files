Title: The Transaction Under the Bayonet
Author:
Date: 1968-09-05
Edition: 2

The book "The Transaction Under the Bayonet" has been published.

New China News Agency, September 3rd: An important article by the editorial board of the People's Daily, "The Transaction Under the Bayonet," and a news report by the New China News Agency exposing the so-called "Sino-Czechoslovakia Joint Statement" issued by the Soviet revisionist clique have been published as a separate volume by the People's Publishing House. The book is titled "The Transaction Under the Bayonet." This book will be available for purchase at the New China Bookstore in Beijing starting September 4th and will soon be available at New China Bookstores nationwide.

### 《刺刀下的交易》一书出版

1968-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    《刺刀下的交易》一书出版
    新华社三日讯《人民日报》评论员重要文章《刺刀下的交易》和新华社关于揭露苏修叛徒集团抛出的所谓“苏捷会谈公报”的新闻报道，已由人民出版社出版单行本，书名为《刺刀下的交易》。这本书从九月四日起在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
