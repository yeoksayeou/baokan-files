Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-09-28
Edition: 1

	We communists are not here to seek official positions, but to make revolution. We must all have a thorough spirit of revolution. We must never for a moment fall out of touch with the masses. So long as we do not fall out of touch with the masses, we shall certainly be victorious.

### 毛主席语录

1968-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    我们共产党人不是要做官，而是要革命，我们人人要有彻底的革命精神，我们不要有一时一刻脱离群众。只要我们不脱离群众，我们就一定会胜利。
