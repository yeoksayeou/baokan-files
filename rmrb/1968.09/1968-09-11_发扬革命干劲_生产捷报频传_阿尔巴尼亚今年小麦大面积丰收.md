Title: Carry Forward the Revolutionary Spirit and Report Frequent Production Victories: Albania Achieves Large-Scale Wheat Harvest This Year
Author:
Date: 1968-09-11
Edition: 6

Albania has achieved a large-scale wheat harvest this year.

The wide masses of peasants and workers in state farms in Albania, under the leadership of the Party of Labour of Albania, have carried forward the revolutionary spirit and achieved a large-scale harvest in wheat production this year. Following the Mat district in central Albania, where the average yield of wheat was 31.5 hundredweight per hectare over one-third of the wheat sowing area, the districts of Lushnje and Tirana also reported frequent victories.

The Lushnje district achieved an average yield of 30 hundredweight per hectare over 28.5% of the wheat sowing area. The district produced 35,000 hundredweight more wheat than originally planned. The Tirana district produced 9,000 hundredweight more wheat than planned.

The Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania and the Ministry of Agriculture awarded the red flags and certificates of the socialist emulation to 31 state farms and agricultural cooperatives that achieved high wheat yields.

The Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania and the Ministry of Agriculture called on all agricultural workers to redouble their efforts, summon up their revolutionary spirit, and do a good job in the field management of corn to strive for a bumper corn harvest this year.

### 发扬革命干劲　生产捷报频传  阿尔巴尼亚今年小麦大面积丰收

1968-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    发扬革命干劲　生产捷报频传
    阿尔巴尼亚今年小麦大面积丰收
    新华社地拉那电　阿尔巴尼亚今年获得小麦大面积丰收。
    阿尔巴尼亚广大农民和国营农场职工在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，发扬革命干劲，今年，在小麦生产上取得了大面积的丰收。继位于阿尔巴尼亚中部的马蒂区，在全区三分之一的小麦播种面积上平均每公顷收获小麦三十一点五公担后，卢什涅和地拉那等地区也捷报频传。
    卢什涅区在全区百分之二十八点五的小麦播种面积上平均每公顷收获三十公担。该区比原订计划多生产了三万五千多公担小麦。地拉那区比原计划多生产了九千多公担小麦。
    阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会和农业部特授予获得小麦丰产的三十一个国营农场和农业合作社以社会主义竞赛红旗和奖状。
    阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会和农业部号召全体农业劳动者再接再厉，鼓足干劲，搞好玉米田间管理，争取今年玉米大丰收。
