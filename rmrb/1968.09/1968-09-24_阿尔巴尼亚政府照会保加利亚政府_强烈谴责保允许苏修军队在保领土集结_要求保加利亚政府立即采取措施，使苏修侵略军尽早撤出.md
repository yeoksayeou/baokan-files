Title: Albanian Government Communiqué to the Bulgarian Government
Author:
Date: 1968-09-24
Edition: 3

The Albanian government has strongly condemned the Bulgarian government for allowing a large number of Soviet revisionist troops to gather on Bulgarian territory. It demands that the Bulgarian government take immediate measures to ensure the early withdrawal of the Soviet revisionist invading forces.

	NEW DELHI, Sept. 22 (Xinhua) - The Albanian News Agency today published a telegram sent by the Albanian People's Republic government on September 21 to the Bulgarian People's Republic government. The telegram strongly condemns the Bulgarian government for allowing a large number of Soviet revisionist troops to gather on Bulgarian territory and demands that the Bulgarian government take immediate measures to ensure the early withdrawal of the Soviet revisionist invading forces.

	The telegram says: The Albanian People's Republic government has irrefutable evidence that a large number of Soviet troops of various branches have gathered on the territory of the Bulgarian People's Republic. After the invasion of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic by the armed forces of the Soviet Union, the Polish People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, and the Bulgarian People's Republic, it is clear that the purpose of the Soviet Khrushchev revisionists' armed forces stationed in Bulgaria is to promote a policy of great-power chauvinism and aggression in the Balkans and the world.

	The telegram points out: There is no doubt that the gathering of Soviet revisionist military forces on the territory of the Bulgarian People's Republic is not defensive in nature. The gathering of Soviet invading forces on Bulgarian territory is a blatant violation of the spirit and provisions of the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance signed between the Albanian People's Republic and the Bulgarian People's Republic in December 1947. In particular, the gathering of Soviet invading forces on Bulgarian territory is a blatant violation of the assurances given by the Bulgarian government under this treaty, that is, not to take actions harmful to the peoples of the Balkan countries—actions that infringe upon the independence, freedom, sovereignty, and territorial integrity of the states in this region, as well as the cause of peace and international security.

	The telegram says: Guided by the Marxist-Leninist policy of the Albanian Party of Labour, the Albanian People's Republic government, in accordance with the policy of the Party of Labour to firmly defend the freedom, independence, and sovereignty of the motherland, and the policy of not infringing upon the freedom, independence, and sovereignty of the peoples of other countries in the world, especially the peoples of the Balkan countries, and in accordance with the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance between the Albanian People's Republic and the Bulgarian People's Republic, demands that the Bulgarian government:

	1. In accordance with the Albanian-Bulgarian Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance, not allow and not participate in any actions taken against the Albanian People's Republic by anyone.

	2. Take immediate measures to ensure the early withdrawal of Soviet armed invading forces from Bulgarian territory, thereby completely eliminating this base of aggression that has been allowed to be established on Bulgarian territory, for the peace of the Balkans and the world.

	The telegram says: The Albanian People's Republic government deems it necessary to point out that, on the contrary, if the Bulgarian government violates the obligations imposed upon it by the December 1947 treaty and, at the same time, tramples upon the basic principles of relations between states by continuing to allow the Soviet army to remain on Bulgarian territory and using this territory as a base for aggression against the peoples of the Balkan countries, it will bear serious responsibility before the peoples of the world, and first and foremost before the Bulgarian people themselves.

	The telegram concludes by saying: The Albanian people believe that the fraternal Bulgarian people will continue to be true to their traditions of struggle for freedom and independence, true to the immortal Marxist-Leninist teachings of Georgi Dimitrov, and will not allow the Albanian-Bulgarian Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance to be violated, nor will they allow their homeland's territory to be used as a military base for attacking the peoples of other countries.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府照会保加利亚政府  强烈谴责保允许苏修军队在保领土集结  要求保加利亚政府立即采取措施，使苏修侵略军尽早撤出

1968-09-24
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府照会保加利亚政府
    强烈谴责保允许苏修军队在保领土集结
    要求保加利亚政府立即采取措施，使苏修侵略军尽早撤出
    新华社地拉那二十二日电　这里的报纸今天刊登了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府二十一日发出的给保加利亚人民共和国政府的一份电报照会，强烈谴责保加利亚政府让大量苏修军队在保加利亚领土上集结，并要求保加利亚政府立即采取措施，使苏修侵略军队尽早撤出。
    照会说：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府掌握了无可辩驳的材料，证实在保加利亚人民共和国的领土上集结了苏联各兵种的大量军队。在苏联、波兰人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国的军队侵略捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国以后，可以清楚地看到：苏联赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的武装力量驻扎在保加利亚的目的，在于在巴尔干和世界推行大国沙文主义侵略政策。
    照会指出：毫无疑问，苏联修正主义者的军事力量在保加利亚人民共和国领土上的集结，决不是防御性的。苏联侵略部队在保加利亚领土上的这种集结，是明目张胆地违背阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国一九四七年十二月签订的友好、合作、互助条约的精神和规定的。尤其是，苏联侵略部队在保加利亚领土上的集结，是公然违背保加利亚政府根据这一条约所作的保证的，即不采取损害巴尔干各国人民的行动——触犯这一地区的国家的独立、自由、主权和领土完整以及和平事业和国际安全的行动。
    照会说：以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的马克思列宁主义政策为指导的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，遵循劳动党的坚决维护祖国的自由、独立和主权的政策，遵循不侵犯世界其他国家人民，特别是巴尔干各国人民的自由、独立和主权的政策，根据阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国友好、合作、互助条约，特要求保加利亚政府：
    一、根据阿尔巴尼亚—保加利亚友好、合作、互助条约，不允许和不参加不论什么人针对阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国所采取的任何行动。
    二、立即采取措施使苏联武装侵略部队在尽可能早的时间内撤出保加利亚领土，从而，为巴尔干和世界的和平，全部清除被允许在保加利亚领土上设立的这个侵略温床。
    照会说：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府认为有必要指出，相反地，如果保加利亚政府违反一九四七年十二月条约所赋予它的义务，并同时践踏国与国之间关系的基本原则而继续让苏军继续留在保加利亚领土上，并把这个领土作为对巴尔干各国人民进行侵略的基地的话，它就要在世界各国人民面前，首先在保加利亚人民自己面前承担严重责任。
    照会最后说：阿尔巴尼亚人民相信，兄弟的保加利亚人民将继续忠于他们为自由和独立而斗争的传统，忠于不朽的乔治·季米特洛夫的马克思列宁主义的教导，并决不允许破坏阿尔巴尼亚—保加利亚友好、合作、互助条约，也决不允许把他们祖国的领土用来作为进攻其他国家人民的军事基地。
