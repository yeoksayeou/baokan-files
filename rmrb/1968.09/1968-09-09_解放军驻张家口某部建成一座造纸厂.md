Title: PLA Unit in Zhangjiakou Completes Paper Mill
Author:
Date: 1968-09-09
Edition: 4

Soldiers and officers of a PLA unit stationed in Zhangjiakou have, in accordance with the great leader Chairman Mao's teachings on "The People's Liberation Army should be a great school. This great school should study politics, study military affairs, study culture, engage in agricultural and sideline production, and run some small and medium-sized factories," after more than three months of arduous struggle, completed a paper mill with a daily production capacity of five to seven tons of paper. The mill has officially started production of six types of paper used for printing and publishing works such as "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong" and "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," and the quality fully meets national standards. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 解放军驻张家口某部建成一座造纸厂

1968-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    解放军驻张家口某部建成一座造纸厂
    中国人民解放军驻张家口某部指战员，遵照伟大领袖毛主席关于“人民解放军应该是一个大学校。这个大学校，要学政治，学军事，学文化，又能从事农副业生产，又能办一些中小工厂”的伟大教导，经过三个多月的艰苦奋战，建成了一座日产五至七吨纸张的造纸厂。这个厂已正式生产印制《毛主席语录》、《毛泽东选集》等著作用的六种纸张，质量完全合乎国家要求。（据新华社讯）
