Chairman Mao's Important Inscription for Japanese Worker Friends

New China News Agency, September 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong, on September 18, 1962, received the "Japanese Workers' Study Active Elements Delegation to China," and wrote an important inscription for Japanese worker friends. Tomorrow marks the sixth anniversary of Chairman Mao's inscription, and we now publish Chairman Mao's inscription.

The full text of the inscription is:

	"As long as one seriously achieves the combination of the universal truths of Marxism-Leninism with the concrete practice of the Japanese revolution, the victory of the Japanese revolution is undoubtedly guaranteed.

	At the request of the various friends of the Japanese Workers' Study Active Elements Delegation to China, this is presented to Japanese worker friends.

	Mao Zedong
	September 18, 1962"

	(Accompanying picture)

### 毛主席给日本工人朋友们的重要题词

1968-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席给日本工人朋友们的重要题词
    新华社十七日讯　毛泽东主席于一九六二年九月十八日，接见“日本工人学习积极分子访华代表团”，为日本工人朋友们做了重要题词。明天是毛主席题词六周年，现在发表毛主席的这一题词。
    题词的全文是：
    “只要认真做到：马克思、列宁主义的普遍真理与日本革命的具体实践相结合，日本革命的胜利就是毫无疑义的。
    应日本工人学习积极分子访华代表团各位朋友之命，书赠日本工人朋友们。
    毛泽东
    一九六二年，九月十八日”
