Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-09-08
Edition: 1

To establish revolutionary committees with three-in-one combinations, to carry out extensive criticism, to clean up the class ranks, to rectify the Party, to streamline organizations, to reform unreasonable rules and regulations, to transfer office staff, and to carry out the struggle, criticism, and transformation in factories—these are the main stages that we generally go through.

### 毛主席语录

1968-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    建立三结合的革命委员会，大批判，清理阶级队伍，整党，精简机构、改革不合理的规章制度、下放科室人员，工厂里的斗、批、改，大体经历这么几个阶段。
