Title: Palestinian Guerrillas Decisively Defeat Israeli Invaders
Author:
Date: 1968-09-05
Edition: 6

Palestinian guerrillas have launched over twenty attacks against the Israeli invaders, who are tools of American aggression, from August 23 to 31. They have killed and wounded more than 140 Israeli soldiers, destroyed three tanks, eight half-track vehicles, seven military vehicles, and an important factory.

The Palestinian strike forces have intensified their activities in the West Bank of the Jordan River, the Golan Heights, the Sinai Peninsula, and the areas under Israeli occupation to tie down the Israeli troops that are concentrating in the Beisan Valley. The Israeli aggressors are now using the Beisan Valley as a forward base for their new aggression against the Arab countries.

From August 23 to 31, the Palestinian strike forces carried out sixteen attacks in the areas under Israeli occupation, destroying many enemy positions.

On the night of August 23, the "Storm" unit used mortars to shell the Israeli military positions in the villages of Yusuf and Muzhaim, destroying a heavy artillery position and other facilities, and annihilating many Israeli soldiers. The "Storm" unit also attacked the enemy position on Mount Ktav, annihilating an Israeli unit there and using mortars to shell Israeli reinforcements, destroying an enemy heavy gun and a vehicle. Other Palestinian strike forces ambushed an Israeli patrol between the villages of Jibrin and Umm Qutayn, destroying the enemy's vehicle and killing five enemy soldiers.

On the nights of August 24 and 25, the "Storm" unit used rockets to attack some Israeli military positions, destroying some facilities and killing and wounding many enemy soldiers.

On the night of August 26, Palestinian strike forces destroyed an Israeli half-track vehicle in the Negev, killing all the personnel on board.

On the night of August 27, the "Storm" unit used rockets to attack the Israeli military position in Kfar Luppin, annihilating a batch of enemy soldiers.

On the night of August 28, the "Storm" unit carried out two attacks: they ambushed an Israeli patrol on the road near the village of Entebe, killing four enemy soldiers on the vehicle and destroying the enemy's patrol car; they also engaged in a battle with an Israeli patrol in Jerusalem, killing three Israeli soldiers and then safely withdrawing.

On the night of August 29, the "Storm" unit used land mines to blow up an Israeli military building on Samuel Street in Jerusalem, killing and wounding ten Israeli soldiers. Other Palestinian strike forces destroyed two Israeli half-track vehicles near the villages of Yusuf and Muzhaim, killing all the personnel on board.

On August 30 and 31, Palestinian strike forces carried out three attacks in the areas under Israeli occupation, destroying a batch of vehicles and annihilating all the enemy soldiers on board.

The "Storm" unit launched three attacks against the enemy on the West Bank of the Jordan River on August 24, 25, and 28, achieving many victories.

On the nights of August 23 and 26, Palestinian strike forces attacked the enemy in the vicinity of Dabusiya in the Golan Heights and near the east bank of the Suez Canal in the Sinai Peninsula, killing a batch of Israeli soldiers.

### 摧毁敌军阵地　伏击敌军巡逻队  巴勒斯坦游击队痛歼以色列侵略

1968-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    摧毁敌军阵地　伏击敌军巡逻队
    巴勒斯坦游击队痛歼以色列侵略军
    新华社大马士革三日电　据“法塔赫”（巴勒斯坦民族解放运动）和其他巴勒斯坦民族主义组织发表的战报，巴勒斯坦突击队，在八月二十三日到三十一日期间，对美国的侵略工具以色列发动了二十多次袭击，打死打伤一百四十多名以色列侵略军，击毁三辆坦克、八辆半履带车、七辆军车和一个重要的工厂。
    巴勒斯坦突击队在被以色列占领的约旦河西岸、戈兰高地、西奈半岛和以色列统治区加强活动，以牵制正在集结的以色列军队，特别是在贝桑河谷的以色列军队。以色列侵略者现在把贝桑河谷作为企图对阿拉伯国家发动新的侵略的前进基地。
    在八月二十三日到三十一日期间，巴勒斯坦突击队在以色列统治区发动了十六次袭击，摧毁许多敌军阵地。
    二十三日夜间，“暴风”部队用迫击炮轰击优素福村和穆兹哈伊姆的以色列军事阵地，击毁一个重炮阵地和其它设施，歼灭许多以色列军；“暴风”部队在袭击克塔夫山的敌军阵地时，消灭了那里的一支以色列军队，并用迫击炮袭击以色列援军，击毁敌军一门重炮和一辆车；其他的巴勒斯坦突击队在吉布林村和乌姆库特恩之间的公路上，伏击一支以色列巡逻队，击毁了敌人的军车，打死五名敌人。
    二十四日晚和二十五日晚，“暴风”部队用火箭袭击以色列的一些军事阵地，击毁一些设施，打死打伤许多敌人。
    二十六日晚，巴勒斯坦突击队在内格夫击毁一辆以色列半履带车，车上的人全部被打死。
    二十七日晚，“暴风”部队用火箭袭击克法鲁平的以色列军事阵地，消灭了一批敌人。
    二十八日晚，“暴风”部队发动了两次袭击：他们在恩特耶夫村的路上伏击一支以色列巡逻队时，打死了车上的四名敌人，击毁了敌人的巡逻车；他们在耶路撒冷同以色列巡逻队交战，在打死三名以色列士兵后安全撤退。
    二十九日晚，“暴风”部队用地雷炸毁耶路撒冷市塞缪尔街上的一座以色列军事建筑物，炸死炸伤十名以色列军；其他的巴勒斯坦突击队在优素福村附近和穆兹哈伊姆附近的两个地方击毁两辆以色列半履带车，车上的人全部被打死。
    巴勒斯坦突击队三十日和三十一日在以色列统治区发动三次袭击，击毁一批车辆，全歼车上敌军。
    “暴风”部队在八月二十四日、二十五日和二十八日，向约旦河西岸的敌人发动了三次袭击，取得了许多战果。
    二十三日和二十六日夜里，巴勒斯坦突击队分别在戈兰高地达布西耶附近和西奈半岛苏伊士运河东岸附近袭击敌人，打死一批以色列士兵。
