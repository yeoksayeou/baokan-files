Author:
Date: 1968-09-23
Edition: 4

South Yemen Foreign Minister Holds Reception in Beijing
Vice Premier Chen Yi and Representatives of the Capital Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team Attend the Reception

BEIJING, Sept. 22 (Xinhua) -- Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of South Yemen, Saif Ahmed Zalay, held a reception in Beijing this evening.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, representatives of the Capital Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team, responsible members of various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, representatives of the Five Good Soldiers of the Beijing garrison of the People's Liberation Army, and responsible persons from the Chinese government departments, the People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Revolutionary Committee, including Wang Xinting, Qiao Guanhua, Li Qiang, Xie Huai-de, and Xiang Qian, were invited to attend the reception. The reception was filled with a warm atmosphere of unity and friendship between the Chinese people and the Arab people, as well as the people of Asia and Africa. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial conversation with Foreign Minister Zalay and the members of the South Yemen delegation led by him.

Diplomats from various countries accredited to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 南也门外长在京举行招待会  陈毅副总理和首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表等出席招待会

1968-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    南也门外长在京举行招待会
    陈毅副总理和首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表等出席招待会
    新华社二十二日讯　南也门人民共和国外交部长塞弗·艾哈迈德·扎莱今晚在北京举行招待会。
    国务院副总理陈毅，首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，北京市各革命群众组织的负责成员，中国人民解放军驻京部队五好战士代表，以及我国政府部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会的负责人王新亭、乔冠华、李强、谢怀德、向前等，应邀出席了招待会。招待会上洋溢着中国人民同阿拉伯人民，以及亚非人民之间的团结友好气氛。陈毅副总理同扎莱外长和由他率领的南也门人民共和国代表团成员进行了亲切的谈话。
    各国驻中国使节应邀出席招待会。
