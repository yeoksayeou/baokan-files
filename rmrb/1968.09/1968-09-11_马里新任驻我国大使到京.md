Title: New Ambassador of Mali Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-09-11
Edition: 6

The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Mali to the People's Republic of China, Assane Gondo, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 马里新任驻我国大使到京

1968-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    马里新任驻我国大使到京
    新华社十日讯　马里共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使阿桑·甘多，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
