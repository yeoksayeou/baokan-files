Title: Ecuadorian People Continue Their Struggle, Strongly Condemning the Reactionary Military Police's Murder of Agricultural Workers
Author:
Date: 1968-09-06
Edition: 5

The people of Ecuador have been continuously engaged in struggles, strongly condemning the reactionary military police for killing agricultural workers. The image shows the people of Quito, the capital of Ecuador, holding up large signs with the word "Revolution," marching and demonstrating on the main streets of the city.
