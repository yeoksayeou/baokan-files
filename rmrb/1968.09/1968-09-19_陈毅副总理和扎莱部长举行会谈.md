Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Minister Zale Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1968-09-19
Edition: 5

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi held talks with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen, Safi Ahmad Zale, this morning. The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 陈毅副总理和扎莱部长举行会谈

1968-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理和扎莱部长举行会谈
    新华社十八日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天上午同南也门人民共和国外交部长塞弗·艾哈迈德·扎莱举行了会谈。会谈是在友好的气氛中进行的。
