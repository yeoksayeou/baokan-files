Title: New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-09-26
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the CPC Central Committee, a delegation from the New Zealand Communist Party arrived in Beijing to participate in the celebrations marking the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to conduct a visit and tour. The delegation, led by Joe Folds, a member of the Auckland Regional Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 应中共中央邀请前来我国参加国庆活动  新西兰共产党代表团到京

1968-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请前来我国参加国庆活动
    新西兰共产党代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　应中共中央邀请前来我国参加国庆十九周年庆祝活动和进行参观访问的新西兰共产党代表团，由新共奥克兰地区委员会委员乔埃·福尔兹率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
