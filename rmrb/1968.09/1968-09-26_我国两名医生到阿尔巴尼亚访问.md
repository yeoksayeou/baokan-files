Title: Two Chinese Doctors Visit Albania
Author:
Date: 1968-09-26
Edition: 5

Two Chinese doctors, Guo Jiajian and Chen Baotian, arrived in Tirana by plane recently. They came to Albania in accordance with the Sino-Albanian health agreement to visit. During their stay in Albania, they will exchange experiences with Albanian medical professionals and learn more about the further revolutionary development of Albania's national life and the achievements of its socialist construction.

### 我国两名医生到阿尔巴尼亚访问

1968-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

    我国两名医生到阿尔巴尼亚访问
    新华社地拉那二十二日电　根据中阿卫生协定来阿尔巴尼亚访问的中国外科医生郭加强和陈宝田最近乘飞机到达地拉那。他们在阿尔巴尼亚访问期间，将同阿尔巴尼亚医学界交流经验，了解阿尔巴尼亚国家生活进一步革命化和社会主义建设成就。
