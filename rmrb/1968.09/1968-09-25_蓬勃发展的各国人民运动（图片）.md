Title: The Thriving People's Movements Around the World
Author:
Date: 1968-09-25
Edition: 6

The thriving people's movements around the world

People in New York, USA, engage in fierce struggle with the fascist military police sent to suppress them.

Xinhua News Agency (Telephoto)

The British Labour Party government closely follows the U.S. imperialists, serving their aggressive policies, and faces strong opposition from the British working class and the broad masses. The image shows over 60,000 dock workers in London demonstrating in front of the Houses of Parliament, opposing the reactionary domestic and foreign policies pursued by the British Labour Party government.

Xinhua News Agency

Tens of thousands of workers and students in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, hold a rally and demonstration, opposing the reactionary rule of the pro-American dictatorship.

Xinhua News Agency

Workers, peasants, and students in the Iraqi capital Baghdad hold up anti-American slogans and shout slogans such as "Down with American imperialism!" and "Down with Israel!" as they demonstrate and march.

Xinhua News Agency Photographer

### 蓬勃发展的各国人民运动（图片）

1968-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    蓬勃发展的各国人民运动
    美国纽约的人民群众同前来镇压他们的法西斯军警展开激烈的搏斗。
    新华社发（传真照片）
    英国工党政府紧紧追随美帝，为美帝的侵略政策效劳，遭到英国工人阶级和广大人民群众的强烈反对。图为六万多伦敦码头工人在国会大厦前示威游行，反对英国工党政府所奉行的反动的内外政策。　
  　新华社发
    巴西里约热内卢几万工人和学生集会示威，反对巴西亲美独裁当局的反动统治。　　　新华社发
    伊拉克首都巴格达的工人、农民和学生高举着反美标语牌，喊着“打倒美帝国主义！”“打倒以色列！”等口号，示威游行。
