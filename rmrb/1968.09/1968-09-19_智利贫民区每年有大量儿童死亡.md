Title: Large Number of Children Die Annually in Chilean Slums
Author:
Date: 1968-09-19
Edition: 6

A survey report from the University of Chile reveals that, according to incomplete statistics, 45 percent of Chileans "live in substandard housing." In these residential areas, "there are no sewers, no running water, and no electricity, leading to rampant diseases and chaos." Every year, 5,000 children under the age of five in Chile die due to the "inhuman living conditions in the slums."

### 智利贫民区每年有大量儿童死亡

1968-09-19
第6版()
专栏：

    智利贫民区每年有大量儿童死亡
    智利大学一份调查报告透露，据不完全统计，智利有百分之四十五的人“居住在条件低劣的房子里”。在这些居民区，“没有下水道，没有自来水，也没有电灯，因此疫病流行和混乱不堪。”智利每年有五千名不满五岁的儿童由于“贫民区非人的生活条件而死亡”。
