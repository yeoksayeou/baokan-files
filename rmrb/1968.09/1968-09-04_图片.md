Title: African Laborers View Photos of Chairman Mao Reviewing the Cultural Revolution Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-09-04
Edition: 4

African laborers, while viewing the photo exhibition of the great leader Chairman Mao reviewing the cultural revolution forces, enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao for personally initiating and leading the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution.
