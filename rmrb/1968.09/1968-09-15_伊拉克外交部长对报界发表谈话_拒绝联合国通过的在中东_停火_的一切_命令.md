Title: Iraqi Foreign Minister Speaks to the Press
Author:
Date: 1968-09-15
Edition: 6

Iraqi Foreign Minister Speaks to the Press
Rejects All "Orders" for a Ceasefire in the Middle East Passed by the United Nations

	Newspaper reports from Baghdad state that Iraqi Foreign Minister Abdul Karim Sheikhli recently spoke to the press, saying that Iraq rejects all "orders" for a ceasefire in the Middle East passed by the United Nations since 1948. The foreign minister said, "Iraq has never recognized the 1948 'ceasefire order,' and it does not recognize any other 'ceasefire orders.'"

	He said that the current Iraqi government believes that guerrilla activities on lands occupied by Israel "reflect the will of the Palestinian Arab people to reject United Nations Security Council resolutions and all other 'peace' schemes."

	He pointed out that so-called "political solutions" to the Middle East problem are impossible and declared that Iraq supports the struggle of other Arab countries against Israeli aggression.

	Since 1948, under the manipulation of U.S. imperialism, the United Nations has passed several so-called "ceasefire orders" aimed at "legalizing" the Arab territories occupied by Israel, its tool of aggression.

Iraqi Revolutionary Command Council Decides to Take Over an American-Run University

	Newspaper reports from Baghdad state that on the 12th, the Iraqi Revolutionary Command Council issued an order deciding to take over the American-run University of Baghdad.

	The council's order requires relevant Iraqi departments to take necessary measures to nationalize the university and place it directly under government control.

	Recently, progressive students and public opinion in Iraq have repeatedly condemned U.S. imperialism for using the University of Baghdad for espionage activities.

	On August 21, the Iraqi newspaper *Revolution* published an article pointing out that the Central Intelligence Agency of the United States uses the University of Baghdad as a means of infiltration.

	The article said that the existence of a purely American-run university in Iraq is a great insult to the anti-American masses. The article demands the takeover of the University of Baghdad and calls for a reform of all its systems and curricula.

### 伊拉克外交部长对报界发表谈话  拒绝联合国通过的在中东“停火”的一切“命令”

1968-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克外交部长对报界发表谈话
    拒绝联合国通过的在中东“停火”的一切“命令”
    伊拉克革命指挥委员会决定接管一所美帝办的大学
    新华社巴格达十三日电　据巴格达报纸报道，伊拉克外交部长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·谢赫利最近对报界发表谈话说，伊拉克拒绝联合国在一九四八年以来通过的在中东实行“停火”的一切“命令”。这位外交部长说，“伊拉克从来没有承认一九四八年的‘停火令’，它也不承认任何其他的‘停火令’。”
    他说，伊拉克现政府认为，在被以色列侵占的土地上的游击队活动，“体现了巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民拒绝联合国安理会决议和其他一切‘和平’方案的意志”。
    他指出，所谓“政治解决”中东问题是不可能的，并且宣布，伊拉克支持其他阿拉伯国家抗击以色列侵略的斗争。
    一九四八年以来，联合国在美帝国主义操纵下通过了目的在于使它的侵略工具以色列所侵占的阿拉伯领土“合法化”的一些所谓“停火令”。
    新华社巴格达十三日电　据巴格达报纸报道，伊拉克革命指挥委员会十二日发布命令，宣布决定接管美帝国主义在伊拉克首都巴格达所办的希克马大学。
    这个委员会在命令中要求伊拉克有关部门采取必要的措施使这所大学民族化，把它直接置于政府的控制之下。
    最近，伊拉克进步学生和舆论纷纷谴责美帝国主义利用希克马大学在伊拉克进行间谍活动。
    八月二十一日，伊拉克《革命报》发表文章指出，美国中央情报局利用希克马大学作为它进行渗入的口子。
    文章说，在伊拉克有纯属美国办的大学的存在是对反美群众的莫大侮辱。文章要求接管希克马大学，并且对这所大学的一切制度和课程进行改革。
