Title: The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought! The Great Victory of Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Line!
Author:
Date: 1968-09-08
Edition: 1

The great victory of Mao Zedong Thought! The great victory of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line!
Millions of soldiers and civilians enthusiastically cheer the redness of the entire country!
Determined to follow Chairman Mao in completing the great task of struggle, criticism, and transformation!

The broad masses of soldiers and civilians say: The establishment of revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions (excluding Taiwan Province) demonstrates the invincible power of Mao Zedong Thought, announces the complete failure of the counter-revolutionary plot of the Chinese Khrushchev, announces the complete failure of the counter-revolutionary rumors of the U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists, and announces the complete failure of the imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries in realizing their "peaceful evolution" in China. They are determined, under the leadership of the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao, to carry forward the spirit of thorough revolution, achieve a comprehensive victory in the Cultural Revolution, and let the sunlight of Mao Zedong Thought shine even more brightly over the motherland.

	New China News Agency, September 7th: The great victory of Mao Zedong Thought resounds from the Tian Shan Mountains to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. The entire country, from the great rivers to the Great Wall, from the border to the coast, is filled with joy. Millions of soldiers and civilians are overjoyed, singing and dancing, enthusiastically cheering the establishment of revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions (excluding Taiwan Province). This is the great victory of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought! This is the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line! The broad masses of soldiers and civilians excitedly say that the entire country is red, marking the entry of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution into the great stage of struggle, criticism, and transformation across the country. We must continue to carry forward the spirit of thorough revolution of the proletariat, follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, under the command of the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, unify our thinking, unify our steps, and unify our actions to fight this battle of struggle, criticism, and transformation, and achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

	The masses in various places point out that the simultaneous birth of the revolutionary committees of the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, with the entire country red, announces the complete failure of the counter-revolutionary plot of the Chinese Khrushchev and his agents in various regions to turn the dictatorship of the proletariat into the dictatorship of the bourgeoisie, announces the complete failure of all counter-revolutionary rumors of the U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists, and announces the complete failure of the imperialists and modern revisionists in realizing their "peaceful evolution" in China. This great victory shows the invincible power of Mao Zedong Thought in the hands of millions of revolutionary masses, greatly strengthens the dictatorship of the proletariat, and greatly enriches and develops Marxism-Leninism. People shout over and over again: "Long live the comprehensive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!" "Long live the great, glorious, and correct Communist Party of China!" "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand times ten thousand years!"

	In the capital Beijing, in Shanghai, Tianjin, in Shenyang, Wuhan, Chengdu, Xi'an, Guangzhou, Xining, Shijiazhuang, Changchun, Harbin, Hohhot, Taiyuan, Changsha, Zhengzhou, Nanning, Lanzhou, Yinchuan, Kunming, Guiyang, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Jinan, Nanchang, Fuzhou, Hefei, and in the vast cities and countryside of each province, municipality, and autonomous region, the joyous news of the birth of the revolutionary committees of Tibet and Xinjiang was broadcast last night, and thousands upon thousands of soldiers and civilians took to the streets, gathered in squares, held grand rallies and parades. Cities were lit up all night, with firecrackers and drums echoing through the sky, presenting a scene of extreme festivity. At the grand celebrations held in various places, local revolutionary committee leaders spoke, and together with the broad revolutionary masses, they celebrated this great event in the political life of our people.

	In the days of celebrating great victories and welcoming new great battle tasks, the masses in various places are particularly excited, and their fighting spirit is more soaring. The joyous news reached the red Anshan mine, and the mine above and below ground immediately erupted with a chorus of "Long live Chairman Mao!" Many old miners who followed Chairman Mao in the revolution years ago, excitedly leaned on their canes and participated in the celebration held at the mine. The miners said they would definitely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, better play the leading role of the working class in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and let the sunlight of Mao Zedong Thought shine even more brightly over the motherland. The joyous news reached the historic city of Zunyi, and the people of Zunyi turned out en masse, gathering at the square where Chairman Mao led the Red Army to Zunyi to meet with the broad masses, holding an unprecedented grand rally to celebrate the great victory of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. The joyous news reached the revolutionary holy land of Yan'an, and the broad masses gathered at the foot of the Bell Tower and along the banks of the Yan River, enthusiastically celebrating the redness of the entire country. They are determined to carry forward the revolutionary spirit of Yan'an cultivated by Chairman Mao and fight a brilliant battle of struggle, criticism, and transformation. The joyous news spread, and the border and coastal defenses of the motherland immediately boiled over. The soldiers of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, who patrol the Inner Mongolia border day and night, immediately formed a propaganda team, rode their horses, and galloped across the vast grasslands for thousands of miles to spread the joyous news to one Mongolian yurt after another. At the same time, in the border town of Mangshi in Yunnan, in the fishing ports of the Zhoushan Islands, in the logging camps of the Greater and Lesser Xing'an Mountains, in the villages under the Wuzhishan Mountains, the broad masses of soldiers and civilians held celebrations. The millions of soldiers and civilians pointed out excitedly during the celebration of victory that from the January Revolution storm in Shanghai to the establishment of the revolutionary committees in Tibet and Xinjiang over twenty months, the soldiers and civilians across the country responded resolutely to the great call of the great leader Chairman Mao to "unite the revolutionary masses of the proletariat and seize power from the handful of capitalist-roaders in power within the party," and after a heroic struggle, finally won the decisive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the broad revolutionary workers in various places, who have followed Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment and advanced victoriously, playing the role of the main force, have surging emotions and enthusiastically cheer the brilliant sunlight of Mao Zedong Thought shining over the southwestern and northwestern borders of the motherland and over the entire country. They study over and over again Chairman Mao's recent instructions: "Establish revolutionary committees with the three-in-one combination, carry out great criticism, clean up the class ranks, rectify the party, streamline organizations, reform unreasonable regulations and systems, transfer office personnel, and in factories, the struggle, criticism, and transformation generally go through these stages." The broad revolutionary workers and the Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams in various places expressed their determination to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment even more closely, fully play the leading role of the working class in the Cultural Revolution and all work, and strive forward to complete the great historical task of struggle, criticism, and transformation!

	The joyous news reached the Benxi Mining Bureau in Liaoning Province, and the entire bureau's coal mines above and below ground were in a festive mood. The broad revolutionary workers and the soldiers and fighters of the stationed People's Liberation Army together held high the red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting over and over again: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand times ten thousand years!" The revolutionary workers of the Niutaitai Mine, who were preparing to go on duty at midnight, expressed at the pre-shift meeting that they would definitely fully implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions, grasp revolution and promote production, and strive forward to complete the great historical task of struggle, criticism, and transformation. In the Changchun Locomotive Works, which has achieved outstanding results in grasping revolution and promoting production, the broad revolutionary workers, after hearing the joyous news, walked all night to the city to participate in the city-wide celebration. They said, "The workers of our locomotive factory must ride the momentum of the great situation across the country to quickly launch a high tide of struggle, criticism, and transformation, and better shoulder the great historical mission of the working class leading everything."

	The Taiyuan Railway Bureau used the dispatch telephone to convey the joyous news to the broad workers along the line and to the passengers on the trains, and immediately a scene of joy and celebration appeared on the railway lines under its jurisdiction. The railway workers said, "The great leader Chairman Mao wants us, the working class, to lead everything. We must shoulder the dual burdens of grasping revolution and promoting production and will not disappoint Chairman Mao's expectations." After hearing the great joyous news, the revolutionary workers of the Yinchuan Rubber Factory immediately spread the news to every worker's family dormitory. Many workers expressed their joy with actual actions of grasping revolution and promoting production, and the entire factory exceeded the production target by more than 9%, creating a new record of daily production since the factory's establishment. The revolutionary workers said: "The entire country is red, and the task on the shoulders of the working class is not lighter but heavier. We must constantly improve our political awareness and, under the leadership of the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, seriously fight this battle of struggle, criticism, and transformation, occupy all positions with Mao Zedong Thought, and turn the great motherland into a big school of Mao Zedong Thought."

	The joyous news reached the countryside, pastures, and fishing islands across the country, and the broad poor and lower-middle peasants, herdsmen, and fishermen beat drums and gongs, held celebration rallies and parades. Chen Yonggui, deputy head of the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee and secretary of the Party branch of the Dazhai Brigade, said excitedly, "The entire country is red, which is the great victory of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. We poor and lower-middle peasants must firmly remember Chairman Mao's teachings, hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, fight this battle of struggle, criticism, and transformation, and consolidate and develop the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution." The joyous news reached the village of Xibeipo in Ping Mountain County, Hebei Province, and the entire village was filled with joy. The old poor peasants, old Party members, and revolutionary young people of this brigade immediately organized Mao Zedong Thought study classes to study and implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions on seriously carrying out struggle, criticism, and transformation.

	Li Shunda, a national model worker in agriculture and a member of the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee, said when cheering the great joyous news: "We poor and lower-middle peasants firmly support the working class leading everything and must be the most reliable allies of the working class. We must respond to Chairman Mao's call to 'definitely grasp grain, definitely grasp cotton, and definitely grasp cloth,' with greater achievements, for the liberation of Taiwan and for the construction of a powerful socialist motherland." The joyous news reached Lingbao County, Henan Province, and the broad poor and lower-middle peasants said: "Chairman Mao's leadership is truly brilliant, the entire country is red, and the Chinese Khrushchev's dream is shattered. Seven hundred million people are overjoyed." They are determined to turn Chairman Mao's latest instructions into actual actions, stride forward, and with unremitting struggle, consolidate this victory and develop this victory.

	The great leader Chairman Mao recently called on us to seriously carry out struggle, criticism, and transformation. On this great day of celebrating the redness of the entire country, members of revolutionary committees at all levels across the country reviewed Chairman Mao's latest instructions, seriously studied the important editorial "Long Live the Comprehensive Victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" published in the People's Daily and the Liberation Army Daily, and all expressed their determination to follow Chairman Mao's great teachings, resolutely implement the various proletarian policies proposed in the editorial note of the Red Flag magazine published on September 5, seriously do investigation and research, grasp typical cases, lead the broad revolutionary masses, fight this battle of struggle, criticism, and transformation, and achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

	Revolutionary teachers and students and revolutionary Red Guards in various places expressed their determination to further implement Chairman Mao's series of latest instructions, under the leadership of the working class, seriously carry out struggle, criticism, and transformation, resolutely walk the road of integrating with workers, peasants, and soldiers, and carry out the proletarian education revolution to the end.

	The heroic soldiers and fighters of the People's Liberation Army enthusiastically cheered the birth of the revolutionary committees of the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, enthusiastically cheered the redness of the entire country. From the headquarters of various military regions, various military branches, and various departments to the camp areas and the front lines of the border and coastal defenses, everywhere was in a festive mood. The border and coastal defense soldiers stationed in Yunnan, Heilongjiang, Inner Mongolia, Hainan Island, and other places, with the joyous news of victory, crossed mountains and rivers to reach remote mountain villages, fishing villages, and tents, and held celebration activities with various ethnic revolutionary masses, explaining Chairman Mao's latest instructions on seriously carrying out struggle, criticism, and transformation. The joyous news reached the Changjiang warship, which Chairman Mao had inspected, and the soldiers and fighters gathered in the cabin where Chairman Mao had stayed, enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao's great revolutionary practice, and enthusiastically cheered the great victory of the entire country being red under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought. A company of a certain unit of the Beijing Garrison, which had once been responsible for the security of the Seventh Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee, reviewed Chairman Mao's great teachings at the Seventh Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee: "The victory of the country is only the first step of the long march of ten thousand miles.... The Chinese revolution is great, but the road after the revolution is longer, the work is greater, and more arduous." They are determined to carry forward the spirit of thorough revolution of the proletariat, follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, advance victoriously, and win new victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The soldiers and fighters of the "Eighth Company of Nanjing Road," the "Model Company for Supporting the Left and Loving the People," the "Pioneer Company for Opening Mountains," the 1124 Engineering Site Heroic Collective, the company where Qiu Shaoyun was formerly stationed, and the company where the heroic soldier Liu Xuebao was stationed, as well as the representatives of the Mao Zedong Thought Study Class of the National Defense Industry, firmly expressed their determination to, under the command of the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, unify their thinking, unify their steps, and unify their actions, and in the great high tide of struggle, criticism, and transformation, perform new deeds for the people. The soldiers and fighters of the army, navy, and air force on the front lines of Fujian, in the days of enthusiastically cheering the great joy of the entire country being red, studied over and over again Chairman Mao's great teachings on "never relaxing vigilance," and are determined to defend the newly born revolutionary committees with fighting, be ready at any time to fight for the liberation of Taiwan, our sacred territory, and guard the border, coastal, and air defenses, defend the motherland, and defend the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. If the enemy dares to invade, we will definitely, thoroughly, completely, and entirely bury them in the vast ocean of people's war.

### 毛泽东思想的伟大胜利！毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利！  亿万军民最热烈欢呼全国山河一片红  决心紧跟毛主席完成斗批改伟大任务

1968-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东思想的伟大胜利！毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利！
    亿万军民最热烈欢呼全国山河一片红  
    决心紧跟毛主席完成斗批改伟大任务
  广大军民说：全国（除台湾省外）各省、市、自治区革委会全部成立，显示了战无不胜的毛泽东思想的无比威力，宣告了中国赫鲁晓夫反革命阴谋的彻底破产，宣告了美帝、苏修反革命谣言的彻底破产，宣告了帝、修、反在中国实现“和平演变”痴心妄想的彻底破产。他们决心在毛主席的无产阶级司令部的领导下，发扬彻底革命精神，夺取文化大革命的全面胜利，让毛泽东思想的阳光把祖国山河照耀得更红更红
    新华社七日讯　天山南北和西藏高原传出毛泽东思想的伟大胜利凯歌，祖国大江南北，长城内外，万里边防海防，到处一片欢腾。亿万军民欢天喜地，载歌载舞，纵情地热烈欢呼全国除台湾省以外的省、市、自治区全部成立革命委员会，是战无不胜的毛泽东思想的伟大胜利！是毛主席无产阶级革命路线的伟大胜利！广大军民激动地说，全国山河一片红，这极其壮丽的一幕，标志着无产阶级文化大革命运动在全国范围内进入了伟大的斗批改的阶段。我们一定要继续发扬无产阶级的彻底革命精神，紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，在以毛主席为首、林副主席为副的无产阶级司令部的号令下，统一认识，统一步伐，统一行动，打好斗、批、改这一仗，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利！
    各地亿万军民指出，西藏自治区革命委员会和新疆维吾尔自治区革命委员会的同时胜利诞生，祖国山河一片红，宣告了中国赫鲁晓夫及其在各地区代理人变无产阶级专政为资产阶级专政的反革命阴谋的彻底破产，宣告了美帝、苏修的一切反革命谣言的彻底破产，宣告了帝国主义和现代修正主义在中国实现“和平演变”的痴心妄想的彻底破产。这一伟大胜利，显示了战无不胜的毛泽东思想掌握了亿万革命群众的无比威力，大大加强了无产阶级专政，大大地丰富和发展了马克思列宁主义。人们一遍又一遍地尽情高呼：“无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利万岁！”“毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”“伟大的、光荣的、正确的中国共产党万岁！”“伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    在首都北京，在上海、天津，在沈阳、武汉、成都、西安、广州、西宁、石家庄、长春、哈尔滨、呼和浩特、太原、长沙、郑州、南宁、兰州、银川、昆明、贵阳、南京、杭州、济南、南昌、福州、合肥，在各省、市、自治区的广大城乡，西藏、新疆两个自治区革命委员会胜利诞生的大喜讯昨晚一广播，千千万万的军民就欢欣鼓舞地走上街头，涌向广场，举行盛大集会和游行。各个城市通宵达旦，鞭炮声、锣鼓声震天动地，呈现极其热烈的节日欢腾景象。在各地举行的盛大庆祝集会上，当地革委会负责人讲了话，同广大革命群众一起欢庆我国人民政治生活中的大喜事。
    在欢呼伟大胜利，迎接新的伟大战斗任务的日子里，各地军民心情特别激动，斗志更加昂扬。喜讯传到红色安源，井上井下，顿时爆发出一片“毛主席万岁”的欢呼声。许多当年跟随毛主席闹革命的老矿工，兴奋地拄着拐杖，参加了矿上举行的庆祝大会。矿工们表示，一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，在无产阶级文化大革命中更好地发挥工人阶级的领导作用，让毛泽东思想的阳光把祖国山河照耀得更红更红。喜讯传到历史名城遵义，遵义人民倾城出动，聚集在毛主席率领工农红军长征到遵义同广大人民见面的广场，举行了规模空前盛大的集会，庆祝毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利。喜讯传到革命圣地延安，广大军民欢集在宝塔山下，延河两岸，热烈庆祝全国山河一片红。他们决心发扬毛主席培育的延安革命精神，打一场漂亮的斗、批、改的大胜仗。喜讯传开，祖国的边疆、海防立即沸腾起来，日夜在内蒙古边境线上巡逻的人民解放军某部，连夜组成宣传队，乘上战马，奔驰千里草原，把喜讯传给附近的一个个蒙古包。就在这同一个时间，在云南的边疆城镇芒市，在舟山群岛的各个渔港，在大小兴安岭的伐木场，在五指山下的村寨，广大军民纷纷举行了庆祝集会。亿万军民在欢庆胜利时兴奋地指出，从上海“一月革命”风暴到西藏、新疆革命委员会成立的二十个月中，全国军民坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席的“无产阶级革命派联合起来，向党内一小撮走资本主义道路当权派夺权”的伟大号召，经过英勇斗争，终于夺得无产阶级文化大革命的决定性胜利。
    在无产阶级文化大革命中紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署胜利前进，充分发挥了主力军作用的各地广大革命工人，在欢呼胜利的时候，心潮澎湃，纵情欢呼毛泽东思想的灿烂阳光普照祖国的西南和西北边疆，普照祖国大地。他们一遍又一遍地学习毛主席最近指出的“建立三结合的革命委员会，大批判，清理阶级队伍，整党，精简机构、改革不合理的规章制度、下放科室人员，工厂里的斗、批、改，大体经历这么几个阶段。”各地广大革命职工和工人毛泽东思想宣传队表示，一定要更加紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，充分发挥工人阶级在文化大革命中和一切工作中的领导作用，为胜利完成斗、批、改的伟大历史任务而奋勇前进！
