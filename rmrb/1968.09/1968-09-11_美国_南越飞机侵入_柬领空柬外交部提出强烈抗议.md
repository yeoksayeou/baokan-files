Title: US-South Vietnamese Aircraft Invade Cambodian Airspace, Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1968-09-11
Edition: 6

	The Cambodian Foreign Ministry lodged strong protests with the US government and the Saigon puppet regime on August 31. The protests were in response to the outrageous provocations of US and South Vietnamese aircraft, which invaded Cambodian airspace over the province of Takeo from July 1 to 19, a total of 32 times. The ministry demanded an immediate halt to these actions.

### 美国—南越飞机侵入  柬领空柬外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越飞机侵入
    柬领空柬外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边七日电　据柬埔寨新闻社七日报道，柬埔寨外交部八月三十一日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越飞机从七月一日到十九日期间，三十二次侵入柬埔寨柴桢省领空的疯狂挑衅行径，并坚决要求立即停止这种行径。
