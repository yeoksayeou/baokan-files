Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-09-24
Edition: 1

    “Though we are separated by mountains and rivers, our hearts are as close as neighbors.” The People’s Republic of China and the People’s Democratic Republic of Algeria are separated by thousands of mountains and rivers, but our hearts are connected. We are your true friends and comrades. You are also our true friends and comrades.

### 毛主席语录

1968-09-24

专栏：

    毛主席语录
    “海内存知己，天涯若比邻”。中阿两国远隔千山万水，我们的心是连在一起的。我们是你们真正的朋友和同志。你们也是我们真正的朋友和同志。
