Title: On the Issue of Re-educating Intellectuals
Author:
Date: 1968-09-18
Edition: 2

The great leader Chairman Mao's latest instructions on re-educating intellectuals, published in the third issue of *Red Flag* magazine in 1968, along with the important article by the editorial board of *People's Daily* and *Red Flag* magazine, "On the Issue of Re-educating Intellectuals," have been published as a separate book by the People's Publishing House. Starting from the 16th, it will be available at the New China Bookstore in Beijing and will soon be available at New China Bookstores nationwide.

The investigative report from Shanghai, "The Growth of 'Barefoot Doctors' and the Direction of the Medical Education Revolution," published in the third issue of *Red Flag* magazine, and the investigative report by the investigative team of *People's Daily* and *Red Flag* magazine, "The Rural Education Revolution Must Rely on Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants," will soon be published as separate books and will be available at New China Bookstores nationwide.

### 《关于知识分子再教育问题》等出版单行本

1968-09-18
第2版()
专栏：

    《关于知识分子再教育问题》等出版单行本
    新华社十六日讯　《红旗》杂志一九六八年第三期发表的伟大领袖毛主席关于对知识分子进行再教育的最新指示以及《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志评论员的重要文章：《关于知识分子再教育问题》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，十六日起在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
    《红旗》杂志第三期发表的上海市的调查报告《从“赤脚医生”的成长看医学教育革命的方向》和《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志调查员所写的调查报告《农村的教育革命必须依靠贫下中农》，即将分别出版单行本，并在全国各地新华书店发行。
