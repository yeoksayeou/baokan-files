Title: Patriotic Military and Civilians in Southern Laos Shoot Down 34 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-09-02
Edition: 6

Patriotic Military and Civilians in Southern Laos Shoot Down 34 U.S. Aircraft

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency from Kengkay, the Pathet Lao News Agency reported: In the year up to this July, the local forces and guerrilla units in southern Laos used light weapons to shoot down 34 U.S. aggressor aircraft.

The report said that in the past year, local forces and guerrilla units extensively carried out a campaign to punish enemy aircraft using light weapons. The guerrilla units alone shot down 11 enemy aircraft using light weapons.

### 老挝下寮爱国军民击落美机三十四架

1968-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝下寮爱国军民击落美机三十四架
    据新华社康开电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：在到今年七月中旬的一年里，老挝下寮地方部队和游击队用轻武器击落了三十四架美国强盗飞机。
    报道说，在过去的一年中，地方部队和游击队广泛地开展了用轻武器惩罚敌机的运动，仅游击队就用轻武器击落了敌机十一架。
