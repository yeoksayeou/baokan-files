Title: Mozambique National Liberation Forces Shoot Down Three Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-09-27
Edition: 5

Mozambique National Liberation Forces have shot down three more enemy aircraft.

NEW DELHI, Sept. 26 (Xinhua) - Following the destruction of 12 Portuguese colonialist military aircraft on August 10, the Mozambique National Liberation Forces have recently shot down three more enemy aircraft.

According to a bulletin issued by the Mozambique National Liberation Forces in Dar es Salaam on September 18, the three enemy aircraft were shot down over the Lirandza area near Mueda while en route from the port of Amélia to the important Portuguese colonialist air base in Mueda in Mozambique.

### 莫三鼻给爱国武装又击落敌机三架

1968-09-27
第5版()
专栏：

    莫三鼻给爱国武装又击落敌机三架
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　莫三鼻给爱国武装力量继八月十日一举摧毁十二架葡萄牙殖民军飞机之后，最近又击落敌机三架。
    据莫三鼻给爱国武装力量十八日在达累斯萨拉姆发表的公报说，这三架敌机是在从阿梅利亚港飞往葡萄牙殖民军在莫三鼻给的重要空军基地姆韦达的途中，在姆韦达附近的利伦德地区上空被击落的。
