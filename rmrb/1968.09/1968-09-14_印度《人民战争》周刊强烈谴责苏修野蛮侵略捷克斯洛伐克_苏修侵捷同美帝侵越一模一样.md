Title: Indian "People's War" Weekly Strongly Condemns Soviet Revisionists' Brutal Invasion of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1968-09-14
Edition: 5

The Indian "People's War" weekly published an editorial on August 29 strongly condemning the Soviet revisionist clique's brutal invasion of Czechoslovakia.

The editorial states that the Soviet revisionist clique's invasion of Czechoslovakia is identical to American imperialism's savage invasion of Vietnam. The revisionist traitors claim that the reason for invading Czechoslovakia is that "Czechoslovakia's socialist achievements" are in danger, and that they are doing this to "defend" these "achievements." Moreover, they even say that they went there at the "invitation" of Czechoslovakia. What did American imperialism say about invading Vietnam? They also claimed it was for the people's "security," that they went there at the "request" of the Vietnamese people. In this light, it is no surprise that the Soviet revisionist traitors use the pretext of "saving" "socialist achievements."

The editorial points out that the Soviet revisionist clique has restored capitalism at home and is meddling in other countries, plundering their wealth and controlling their politics. This is imperialism. American imperialism is doing this today, and the Soviet revisionist clique is also doing this.

The editorial continues, saying that the Soviet revisionist clique has long been extracting large quantities of raw materials from Czechoslovakia, swallowing the fruits of the Czech people's labor. As the crisis in Czechoslovakia deepened, this led to changes in the ruling class, with the Dubček clique coming to power as a clear example. Dubček is no good either; he wanted to break away from the Soviet revisionist traitors, establish his own revisionist clique's control, and thus rule at will. The revisionist traitors used all kinds of tricks against Dubček, including bribery, flattery, threats, and invitations to meetings, but when threats and inducements failed, the Soviet revisionist clique led its lackeys to invade Czechoslovakia, trampling on Czechoslovakia.

The editorial states that the Czech people have not tolerated this brutal invasion and are fighting against the revisionist traitors everywhere.

The editorial points out that some reactionaries also oppose this invasion, but their opposition is not for the people; it is for the interests of the Czechoslovakian revisionist leaders, the Soviet revisionist clique, and all reactionaries in the world. Therefore, it is necessary to wage an uncompromising struggle against the revisionist traitors and open up the road to liberation for themselves.

### 印度《人民战争》周刊强烈谴责苏修野蛮侵略捷克斯洛伐克  苏修侵捷同美帝侵越一模一样

1968-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    印度《人民战争》周刊强烈谴责苏修野蛮侵略捷克斯洛伐克
    苏修侵捷同美帝侵越一模一样
    必须对修正主义叛徒集团作无情斗争，开辟自己解放自己的道路
    新华社十三日讯　八月二十九日出版的印度《人民战争》周刊发表社论，强烈谴责苏修叛徒集团野蛮侵略捷克斯洛伐克的罪行。
    社论说，苏修叛徒集团对捷克斯洛伐克的侵略同美帝国主义对越南的野蛮侵略是一模一样的。修正主义叛徒们说侵略捷克斯洛伐克的原因是“捷克斯洛伐克的社会主义成果”处于危险之中，他们是为了“保卫”这些“成果”而这样做的。不仅如此，他们甚至还说，他们是应捷克斯洛伐克的“邀请”才去那里的。美帝国主义对侵略越南是怎么说的呢？他们也说是为了人民的“安全”，是应越南人民的“请求”才带领军队去那里的。这样看来，苏联的修正主义叛徒们也使用“挽救”“社会主义成果”之类的名义，就毫不奇怪了。
    社论指出，苏修叛徒集团在国内复辟资本主义，并向其它国家插足，掠夺那些国家的财富，控制那些国家的政治。这就是帝国主义。美帝国主义今天在干这一手，苏修叛徒集团也在干这一手。
    社论接着说，苏修叛徒集团过去一直从捷克斯洛伐克取得大量的原料，吞没捷克斯洛伐克人民的血汗所得。捷克斯洛伐克的危机深化了，由于这种危机使得当权者发生更迭，杜布切克集团执政就是这方面的明显例证。杜布切克也并非什么好东西，他想摆脱苏联修正主义叛徒，建立自己修正主义集团的控制，从而随心所欲地进行统治。修正主义叛徒们对杜布切克用尽了利诱、讨好、威胁、邀请参加会议等一切阴谋手段，但当威胁和诱惑都无济于事的时候，苏修叛徒集团率领自己的娄罗们侵入捷克斯洛伐克，蹂躏了捷克斯洛伐克。
    社论说，捷克斯洛伐克人民没有容忍这种野蛮的侵略，他们在各地与修正主义叛徒们开展斗争。
    社论指出，一些反动派也反对这一侵略，但是他们的反对不是为了人民，而是为了捷克斯洛伐克的修正主义头目、苏修叛徒集团和世界一切反动派的利益。因此，必须对修正主义叛徒集团作无情的斗争，开辟自己解放自己的道路。
