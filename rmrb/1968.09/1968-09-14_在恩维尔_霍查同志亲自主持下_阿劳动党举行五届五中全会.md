Title: The Fifth Plenum of the Fifth Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania Held Under the Personal Chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha
Author:
Date: 1968-09-14
Edition: 5

The Fifth Plenum of the Fifth Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania was held on September 5 under the personal chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Party Central Committee.

The Plenum heard and discussed the report by Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Party Central Committee, on the current international situation, particularly the situation in Czechoslovakia. The Plenum also heard and discussed the report by Comrade Mehmet Shehu, member of the Political Bureau of the Party Central Committee and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, on the position of the People's Republic of Albania towards the Warsaw Pact.

The Plenum unanimously endorsed the above two reports and adopted relevant resolutions.

### 在恩维尔·霍查同志亲自主持下  阿劳动党举行五届五中全会

1968-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    在恩维尔·霍查同志亲自主持下
    阿劳动党举行五届五中全会
    全会听取和讨论了霍查同志关于当前国际形势，特别是关于捷克斯洛伐克形势的报告和谢胡同志关于阿尔巴尼亚对华沙条约立场的报告。全会一致赞同这两个报告，并作出相应的决议
    新华社地拉那十二日电　据这里的报纸十一日报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五届中央委员会第五次全体会议九月五日在党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志的主持下举行。
    全会听取和讨论了党中央第一书记霍查同志关于当前国际形势，特别是关于捷克斯洛伐克形势的报告。全会听取和讨论了党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席谢胡同志关于阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国对华沙条约的立场的报告。
    全会一致赞同上述这两个报告，并且作出了相应的决议。
