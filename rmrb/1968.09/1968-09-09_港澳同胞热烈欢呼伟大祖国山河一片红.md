Title: Hong Kong and Macao compatriots enthusiastically cheer the great motherland with a sea of red
Author:
Date: 1968-09-09
Edition: 3

The news of the birth of the Revolutionary Committees of Tibet and Xinjiang spread, and the broad masses of patriotic compatriots immediately launched unprecedented grand activities to report the good news and celebrate. They expressed their unwavering confidence: they will definitely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and achieve the final victory in the struggle against British oppression.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, September 8: The simultaneous birth of the Revolutionary Committees of the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and the establishment of Revolutionary Committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country except Taiwan Province, brought the great news to Hong Kong. The patriotic compatriots in Hong Kong and Kowloon, who are deeply engaged in the anti-British struggle, were elated and immersed in unprecedented happiness and joy. They enthusiastically cheered the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the motherland, and enthusiastically cheered the fact that after the tempering of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, our great socialist motherland has become even more solid as a rock, and the strong backing of overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots is presenting an even more majestic appearance to the people of the world. The patriotic compatriots in Hong Kong expressed their unwavering confidence: they will definitely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, achieve the final victory in the struggle against British oppression, and make greater contributions to supporting the socialist revolution and socialist construction in the motherland.

On the evening of September 6, the news of the birth of the Revolutionary Committees of the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region was broadcast to Hong Kong. Workers, peasants, fishermen, students, and patriotic compatriots from all walks of life, who had been eagerly awaiting this great news, immediately launched unprecedented grand activities to report the good news. Various reporting teams carried giant portraits of Chairman Mao, beating drums and gongs, singing and dancing, enthusiastically cheering the great motherland with a sea of red. Many patriotic compatriots were so excited that they stayed up all night. People joyfully shouted: "Long live the victory of the proletarian revolutionary line of Chairman Mao!" "Long live the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!"

On the morning of September 7, various patriotic organizations in Hong Kong and Kowloon raised the solemn and beautiful five-star red flag under the majestic song of "The East Is Red." Many patriotic trade unions, associations, schools, banks, national product companies, and theaters hung up giant red banners and held celebration meetings. The broad masses of patriotic compatriots faced Beijing and sincerely wished our dearest red sun in our hearts, our great teacher Chairman Mao, eternal life! Eternal life!

Leaders of various patriotic trade unions, schools, associations, and prominent figures from the industrial and commercial, film, and women's circles in Hong Kong and Kowloon gave speeches, enthusiastically cheering the establishment of Revolutionary Committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country except Taiwan Province. Yang Guang, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, said in his speech: The great motherland with a sea of red is a major event in the process of achieving the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution under the leadership of Chairman Mao by hundreds of millions of soldiers and people. At this unprecedentedly exciting and joyful moment, we, the working class in Hong Kong, must remember Chairman Mao's teachings, fully play the leading role of the working class, unite patriotic compatriots from all walks of life, strengthen the anti-imperialist and patriotic forces, further launch the mass movement of studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought, and strive together with the people of the whole country to oppose imperialism led by the United States, modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionist leadership clique, and reactionaries of all countries, and to achieve the final victory in the struggle against British oppression. Many anti-British fighters who have made contributions in the struggle against oppression also gave speeches, expressing their boundless joy.

The news reached Macao, and patriotic compatriots were extremely elated. Various patriotic trade unions, associations, shops, and some streets immediately hung up banners and launched grand celebration activities. Many people organized reporting teams, carrying portraits of Chairman Mao, beating drums and gongs to congratulate each other enthusiastically.

On September 8, patriotic compatriots from various circles in Hong Kong and Macao held grand meetings with nearly 2,000 and more than 1,000 people respectively, enthusiastically cheering the great socialist motherland with a sea of red. The meeting was filled with cheers and slogans, and patriotic compatriots joyfully shouted: "Long live the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" Representatives from the workers, peasants, students, cultural, women's, and industrial and commercial circles in Hong Kong and Macao gave speeches. The two meetings, amidst warm applause and cheers, respectively passed telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao.

### 港澳同胞热烈欢呼伟大祖国山河一片红

1968-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    港澳同胞热烈欢呼伟大祖国山河一片红
    西藏、新疆革委会胜利诞生的喜讯传来以后，广大爱国同胞立刻展开了空前盛大的报喜活动和庆祝活动。他们信心百倍地表示：一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，夺取反对港英迫害斗争的最后胜利
    据新华社香港八日电　西藏自治区和新疆维吾尔自治区革命委员会同时胜利诞生，全国除台湾省外的各省、市、自治区革命委员会全部成立的特大喜讯传到香港，正在深入开展反英抗暴斗争的港九爱国同胞欢欣鼓舞，沉浸在无比的幸福和欢乐中。他们热烈欢呼祖国无产阶级文化大革命所取得的伟大胜利，热烈欢呼经过无产阶级文化大革命的锻炼，我们伟大的社会主义祖国更加坚如磐石，海外华侨和港澳同胞的强大靠山，正以更加雄伟的面貌出现在全世界人民的面前。香港爱国同胞信心百倍地表示：一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，夺取反对港英迫害斗争的最后胜利，并且为支援祖国的社会主义革命和社会主义建设作出更大的贡献。
    六日傍晚，西藏自治区和新疆维吾尔自治区两个革命委员会胜利诞生的消息从电波中传到香港，早就等候着这个特大喜讯的各业工人、农民、渔民、学生和各界爱国同胞，立刻展开了空前盛大的报喜活动。各个报喜队抬着毛主席的巨幅画像，敲锣打鼓，载歌载舞，热烈欢呼伟大祖国山河一片红。许多爱国同胞兴奋得彻夜不眠。人们纵情欢呼：“毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”“伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    七日早晨，港九各爱国机构都在雄壮的《东方红》歌声中升起了庄严美丽的五星红旗。许多爱国工会、社团、学校、银行、国货公司和戏院纷纷张挂起巨幅红布标语，并分别举行了庆祝集会。广大爱国同胞面向北京，衷心祝愿我们心中最红最红的红太阳、我们的伟大导师毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
    各爱国工会、学校、社团的负责人和工商界、电影界、妇女界知名人士纷纷发表谈话，热烈欢呼全国除台湾省外的省、市、自治区全部成立革命委员会。港九工会联合会理事长杨光发表谈话说：全国山河一片红，这是亿万军民在伟大领袖毛主席领导下夺取文化大革命全面胜利进程中的重大事件。在这无比兴奋、无比欢乐的时刻，我们香港工人阶级一定牢记毛主席的教导，充分发挥工人阶级的领导作用，团结各阶层爱国同胞，壮大反帝爱国队伍，进一步掀起活学活用毛泽东思想的群众运动，同全国人民一道，为反对以美国为首的帝国主义、以苏修领导集团为中心的现代修正主义和各国反动派，为夺取反英抗暴斗争的最后胜利，而努力奋斗。在反迫害斗争中作出贡献的许多反英抗暴战士也纷纷发表谈话，表达他们无限喜悦的心情。
    喜讯传到澳门，爱国同胞无比欢欣鼓舞。各爱国工会、社团、商店和一些街道立即张挂起标语，展开盛大的庆祝活动。许多人组织了报喜队，抬着毛主席画像，敲锣打鼓地互相热烈祝贺。
    八日，香港、澳门各界爱国同胞分别举行了近两千人和一千余人的盛大集会，纵情欢呼伟大社会主义祖国锦绣山河一片红。会上，欢呼声和口号声响彻天空，爱国同胞无比兴奋地欢呼：“无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利万岁！”“伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万万岁！”港、澳工人、农民、学生界、文化界、妇女界、工商界代表分别发表了讲话。这两个大会，在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中分别通过了给毛主席的致敬电。
