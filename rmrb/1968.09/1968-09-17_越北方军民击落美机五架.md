Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-09-17
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians shot down five U.S. aircraft.

According to a report from here, on September 11, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

Prior to this, on September 8, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down two U.S. aircraft, and on August 31, the military and civilians of Vinh Phuc Province shot down one U.S. aircraft.

### 越北方军民击落美机五架

1968-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    越北方军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内电　据这里公布，越南北方广平省军民在十一日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    在这以前，义安省军民在八日击落美机两架，永灵区军民在八月三十一日击落美机一架。
