Title: A Total Solar Eclipse Will Occur Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1968-09-21
Edition: 4

Tomorrow will see a total solar eclipse. According to a message from the Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a total solar eclipse will occur on September 22. In China, a band approximately 70 to 80 kilometers wide from Zhaosu County to Heshuo County in Xinjiang will experience the total eclipse. Other regions in Xinjiang, most of Tibet and Qinghai, the western part of Gansu, the westernmost part of Inner Mongolia, and the westernmost part of Sichuan will see a partial eclipse. The eclipse will occur just before sunset. The times for the eclipse in major cities are as follows (Beijing time):

	Urumqi: Initial eclipse at 18:48, maximum eclipse at 19:50, eclipse magnitude 0.99.
	Jiuquan: Initial eclipse at 18:58, eclipse magnitude 0.42.
	Xining: Initial eclipse at 19:04, eclipse magnitude 0.1.
	Lhasa: Initial eclipse at 19:16, eclipse magnitude 0.6.

	(Note: In the above regions, except for Urumqi, the maximum eclipse occurs after sunset in the other regions, so the maximum eclipse cannot be seen, and the eclipse magnitudes are all at the time of sunset.)

### 明日将发生日全食

1968-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    明日将发生日全食
    新华社南京二十日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台消息：九月二十二日将发生日全食。我国新疆自昭苏县至和静县宽度约七八十公里的地带可以看到全食。新疆其他地区，西藏、青海的绝大部分，甘肃西部、内蒙古和四川的最西部可以看到偏食。发生日食的时间都是快要日落时。主要城市发生日食的时刻如下：（北京时间）
    乌鲁木齐，十八时四十八分初亏，十九时五十分食甚，食分为零点九九。酒泉，十八时五十八分初亏，食分为零点四二。西宁，十九时零四分初亏，食分为零点一。拉萨，十九时十六分初亏，食分为零点六。
  （注：上述地区中除乌鲁木齐以外，其它地区的食甚都发生在日没以后，不能看到食甚，食分也都是日没时的食分。）
