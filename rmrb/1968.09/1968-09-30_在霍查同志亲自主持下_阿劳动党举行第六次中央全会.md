Title: The Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania Held Under the Personal Chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha
Author:
Date: 1968-09-30
Edition: 5

The Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania was held under the personal chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, from September 27 to 28.

The plenary session heard a report by Ramiz Alia, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat, on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, entitled "On Deepening the Socialist Revolution by Waging Class Struggle and Implementing the Mass Line." After discussing the above-mentioned issues, the plenary session unanimously adopted the report and made relevant decisions.

According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania held its Sixth Plenary Session under the personal chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, from September 27 to 28.

The plenary session heard a report by Ramiz Alia, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat, on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, entitled "On Deepening the Socialist Revolution by Waging Class Struggle and Implementing the Mass Line." After discussing the above-mentioned issues, the plenary session unanimously adopted the report and made relevant decisions.

### 在霍查同志亲自主持下  阿劳动党举行第六次中央全会

1968-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

    在霍查同志亲自主持下
    阿劳动党举行第六次中央全会
    全会听取了阿利雅代表政治局所作《关于通过开展阶级斗争和贯彻群众路线，深入开展社会主义革命》的报告，一致通过了这个报告并作出了有关决定
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志的主持下，于九月二十七日至二十八日举行了第六次全会。
    全会听取了党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记拉米兹·阿利雅代表中央委员会政治局所作的《关于通过开展阶级斗争和贯彻群众路线，深入开展社会主义革命》的报告。全会在讨论了上述问题以后，一致通过了这个报告并作出了有关决定。
