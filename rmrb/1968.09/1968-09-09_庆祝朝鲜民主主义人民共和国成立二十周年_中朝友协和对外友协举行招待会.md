Title: Celebrating the 20th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
Author:
Date: 1968-09-09
Edition: 6

China-Korea Friendship Association and China International Friendship Association held a reception to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Xinhua News Agency, September 8th - The China-Korea Friendship Association and the China International Cultural Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing on the 7th to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and relevant officials attended the reception.

The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, Kim Jae-suk, and other diplomatic officials of the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

After the reception, a documentary film of our great leader Chairman Mao receiving proletarian revolutionary fighters was shown. To celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the China-Korea Friendship People's Commune held a film evening on the 8th. Kim Jae-suk, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, and other diplomatic officials of the embassy were invited to attend the film evening.

### 庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立二十周年  中朝友协和对外友协举行招待会

1968-09-09
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立二十周年
    中朝友协和对外友协举行招待会
    新华社八日讯　中国朝鲜友好协会和中国对外文化友好协会，七日在北京举行招待会，庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立二十周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，以及有关方面负责人，出席了招待会。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑和使馆外交官员应邀出席了招待会。
    会后，放映了我们伟大领袖毛主席接见无产阶级革命战士的纪录影片。为庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立二十周年，中朝友好人民公社八日举行了电影晚会。金在淑临时代办和大使馆其他外交官员应邀出席了电影晚会。
