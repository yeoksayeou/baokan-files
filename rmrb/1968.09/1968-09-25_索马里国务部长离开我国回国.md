Title: Somali State Minister Leaves China to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-09-25
Edition: 4

Somali State Minister Muhammad Sheikh Yusuf Dabi, after receiving treatment in China, left China on September 21 to return home. During his stay in China, Minister Dabi visited Beijing and Shanghai.

### 索马里国务部长离开我国回国

1968-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    索马里国务部长离开我国回国
    新华社讯　索马里国务部长穆罕默德·谢赫·优素夫·代比在中国治疗以后，二十一日离开中国回国。代比部长在中国期间曾在北京和上海进行了参观。
