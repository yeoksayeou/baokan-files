Title: Bolivian Communist Party Publishes "Chairman Mao on People's War"
Author:
Date: 1968-09-19
Edition: 5

The Bolivian Communist Party's Central Committee's Press Committee recently published, through the "Liberation" Press, the Spanish edition of "Chairman Mao on People's War." The book includes a portrait of the great leader Chairman Mao.

In the book, the publisher wrote in the "To the Reader" section: "Comrade Mao Zedong's thought—contemporary Marxism-Leninism—is a powerful revolutionary weapon in the hands of the peoples of all countries who are fighting against American imperialism and striving for national liberation.

"The party has always correctly pointed out that in its historical mission to liberate the people of Bolivia, one of its most important tasks is to promote the study of Comrade Mao Zedong's works among party members and leaders and among the masses of the people."

The article states that to firmly support the party's decision, the Bolivian Communist Party Central Committee's Press Committee published "Chairman Mao on People's War."

### 玻共出版《毛主席论人民战争》  指出毛泽东思想是各国人民反对美帝、争取民族解放的强大革命武器

1968-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    玻共出版《毛主席论人民战争》
    指出毛泽东思想是各国人民反对美帝、争取民族解放的强大革命武器
    新华社十八日讯　玻利维亚共产党中央委员会报刊委员会最近经由“解放”出版社，出版了《毛主席论人民战争》西班牙文版。书里印有伟大领袖毛主席像。
    在书中，出版者在《致读者》一文里写道：“毛泽东同志的思想——当代的马克思列宁主义，是反对美帝国主义、为争取民族解放而斗争的各国人民手中的强大革命武器。
      “我党一向正确地指出，在其解放玻利维亚人民的历史使命中，它的最重要的任务之一是，在党员和党的领导中和在人民群众中，促进对毛泽东同志的著作的学习。”
    文章说，为了坚决地支持党的这个决定，玻利维亚共产党中央委员会报刊委员会出版了《毛主席论人民战争》。
