Title: Cambodian Foreign Ministry Protests New Aggression by the U.S. and Saigon Puppet Regime
Author:
Date: 1968-09-15
Edition: 6

The Cambodian Foreign Ministry has lodged strong protests against the new acts of aggression by the U.S. and the Saigon puppet regime. According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Royal Government of Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent two separate notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime on September 9 and 10, respectively, to protest the latest acts of aggression against Cambodia.

In one of the notes, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry pointed out that on the evening of August 28, U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops invaded Cambodian territory in the province of Kampong Cham, wounding one Cambodian soldier. In another note, it was pointed out that on the evening of September 2, U.S.-South Vietnamese aircraft violated Cambodian airspace and deliberately attacked the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces, wounding one Cambodian soldier.

### 柬外交部抗议美伪集团新的侵略罪行

1968-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    柬外交部抗议美伪集团新的侵略罪行
    据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部，在九月九日和十日两次分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡政权，强烈抗议美国和西贡傀儡集团对柬埔寨的新的侵略罪行。
    柬埔寨外交部在一项照会中指出，美国—南越傀儡军队在八月二十八日晚上侵犯了柬埔寨干丹省领土，打伤一名柬埔寨士兵。在另一项照会中指出，美国—南越飞机在九月二日晚上侵犯了柬埔寨领空，对柬埔寨王家武装部队蓄意进行袭击，打伤一名柬埔寨士兵。
