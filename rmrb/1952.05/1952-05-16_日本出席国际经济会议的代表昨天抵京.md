Title: Japanese Representatives to International Economic Conference Arrive in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Japanese representatives to the Moscow International Economic Conference, Takakura Tomi and secretary Matsuyama Shigeru, as well as Japanese representatives Hoashi Kei, Miyakoshi Kisuke, and their secretary Nakao Kazuo, who were unable to attend the conference due to circumstances, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th for a visit. Ji Chaoding, member and Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, welcomed them at the airport.

### 日本出席国际经济会议的代表昨天抵京

1952-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    日本出席国际经济会议的代表昨天抵京
    【新华社十五日讯】出席莫斯科国际经济会议的日本代表高良富和秘书松山繁，以及因故未及参加会议的日本代表帆足计、宫腰喜助及其秘书中尾和夫等一行五人，在十五日下午搭飞机到达北京参观。中国国际贸易促进委员会委员兼秘书长冀朝鼎曾往机场欢迎。
