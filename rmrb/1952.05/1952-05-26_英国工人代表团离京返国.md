Title: British Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1952-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th -- The British workers delegation, invited to our country to attend the "May Day" celebrations, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 24th to return home after visiting and touring Northeast China and East China. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Li Zaiwen, director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

### 英国工人代表团离京返国

1952-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    英国工人代表团离京返国
    【新华社二十四日讯】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的英国工人代表团，在我国东北和华东等地参观访问后，在二十四日晨乘飞机离北京返国。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温等人。
