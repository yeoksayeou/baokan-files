Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 1

To Chairman Ulanhu of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:

    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you and the people of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

    I wish you continued success in building upon the glorious achievements you have made in the past five years, continued promotion of the spirit of patriotism and internationalism so highly demonstrated, and continued progress in your efforts to realize Chairman Mao's great ethnic policy, forever becoming a good example of minority ethnic regional autonomy!

    Zhou Enlai, May 1, 1952

### 周恩来总理贺电

1952-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理贺电
    内蒙古自治区人民政府乌兰夫主席：
    值兹内蒙古自治区五周年纪念佳节，我向你和内蒙古自治区人民致衷心的庆贺。
    祝你们继续发扬过去五年中已经获得的光辉成就，继续发扬高度表现了的爱国主义和国际主义的精神，并在实现毛主席的伟大民族政策的努力中，与日俱进，永远成为少数民族区域自治的良好榜样！
    周恩来  一九五二年五月一日
