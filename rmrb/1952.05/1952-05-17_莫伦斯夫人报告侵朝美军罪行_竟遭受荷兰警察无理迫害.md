Title: Madame Morel Reports on Crimes of U.S. Troops Invading Korea, Suffers Unreasonable Persecution by Dutch Police
Author:
Date: 1952-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sixteenth Dispatch: TASS, Brussels, Fourteenth Dispatch: According to "Red Flag" news: When Belgian lawyer Madame Morel went to Amsterdam to give a report on the crimes committed by American aggressors in Korea, she was persecuted by the Dutch police. As everyone knows, Madame Morel participated in the work of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers' investigation team to investigate the crimes committed by American aggressors in Korea. On May 10th, the Dutch police attempted to prevent Madame Morel from giving a public speech in Amsterdam. On May 11th, Madame Morel and her husband were subjected to the humiliation of detention and a brutal search at the border while returning to Belgium.

### 莫伦斯夫人报告侵朝美军罪行  竟遭受荷兰警察无理迫害

1952-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    莫伦斯夫人报告侵朝美军罪行
    竟遭受荷兰警察无理迫害
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社布鲁塞尔十四日讯：据“红旗报”消息：在比利时律师莫伦斯夫人前往阿姆斯特丹作关于美国侵略者在朝鲜所犯罪行的报告时，荷兰警察对她进行了迫害。大家知道：莫伦斯曾经参加国际民主法律工作者协会调查团的工作，调查美国侵略者在朝鲜犯下的罪行。五月十日，荷兰警察企图阻止莫伦斯夫人在阿姆斯特丹公开演讲。五月十一日，莫伦斯夫人和她的丈夫在回比利时的途中，在边境上遭受了拘留及粗暴搜查的侮辱。
