Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1952-05-15
Page: 1

    China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Officially Established

    (Xinhua News Agency, 12th) In order to implement the resolutions of the International Economic Conference and actively promote the development of China's international trade, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade was officially announced on the 4th and held its first meeting, which was attended by Mr. Chamberlin, Secretary-General of the International Trade Promotion Council. The council is composed of 17 members, including Nan Hanchen, President of the People's Bank of China; Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xue Muqiao, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council; Zhou Rongxin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council; Zhang Naiqi, Member of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee; Ji Chaoding, Director and Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Trade; Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of Textile Industry; Meng Yongqian, Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives; Li Zhuchen, Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce; Sheng Pi Hua, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce; Wu Yun Chu, General Manager of Tianchu Monosodium Glutamate Company; Xu Dixing, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee; Chen Hansheng, Deputy Director of the China Economic Society; Liu Ziju, Minister of Culture and Education of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation. Ji Chaoding also serves as Secretary-General, organizing the secretariat to be responsible for domestic and foreign liaison, publicity, research and other work. The address of the association is located at 28 Dongjiaominxiang, and various tasks will be carried out immediately.

### 中国国际贸易促进委员会正式成立

1952-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国际贸易促进委员会正式成立
    【新华社十二日讯】为执行国际经济会议的决议，积极推动中国国际贸易的开展，中国国际贸易促进委员会已在四日正式宣布成立，并举行第一次会议，国际贸易促进委员会秘书长香贝朗亦曾出席参加。该委员会由中国人民银行行长南汉宸、北京大学校长马寅初、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、政务院财政经济委员会秘书长薛暮桥、政务院财政经济委员会副秘书长周荣鑫、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员章乃器、中国银行董事兼副总经理冀朝鼎、贸易部副部长雷任民、纺织工业部副部长陈维稷、中华全国合作社联合总社理事会副主任孟用潜、天津市工商业联合会主任委员李烛尘、上海市工商业联合会主任委员盛丕华、天厨味精股份有限公司总经理吴蕴初、华东军政委员会财政经济委员会副主任许涤新、中国经济学会副主任委员陈翰笙、中华全国总工会文教部长刘子久、中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等十七人组成。由冀朝鼎兼任秘书长，组织秘书处，负责进行对国内外联络、宣传、研究等工作。该会会址设于东交民巷二十八号，即日开始进行各项工作。
