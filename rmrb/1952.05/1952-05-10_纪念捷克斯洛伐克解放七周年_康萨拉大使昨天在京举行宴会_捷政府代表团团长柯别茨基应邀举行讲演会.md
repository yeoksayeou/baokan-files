Title: Commemorating the Seventh Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia: Ambassador Kousal Held a Banquet in Beijing Yesterday; Head of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, Kopěcký, Was Invited to Give a Lecture

Author: 
Date: 1952-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: May 9th is the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Kousal, held a banquet in Beijing on the 9th.

Those invited to attend were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han, totaling more than 300 people.

The foreign envoys invited to the meeting were: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkasekhan, Hungarian Ambassador Szafrankó, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik, Swiss Minister Renzo Real, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Meisztrup, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Beve, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation Brunstein, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Gura, Head of the Dutch Government Negotiation Delegation Van de Put, and embassy personnel from various countries.

During the banquet, Ambassador Kousal and Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered speeches successively. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Furthermore, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, respectively sent telegrams to the President of the Czechoslovak Republic Gottwald and Foreign Minister Široký, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia.

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: At the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, the head of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, Kopěcký, gave a lecture in Beijing on the morning of the 9th, with the topic "Czechoslovakia's Advance towards Socialism in the Past Seven Years". The lecture was presided over by Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Zhou Yang. Seated on the rostrum, in addition to all members of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, were also Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of our Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of External Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council. The audience included responsible persons and cadres from Party, government, military, trade union, and cultural and educational organs, as well as students from various schools in the capital, totaling more than 1,200 people.

Kopěcký's two-hour speech was frequently interrupted by warm applause. At the end of the lecture, young pioneers from the May 1st Girls' Middle School in Beijing presented flowers to Head of Delegation Kopěcký and all members of the delegation to express their respect.

### 纪念捷克斯洛伐克解放七周年  康萨拉大使昨天在京举行宴会  捷政府代表团团长柯别茨基应邀举行讲演会

1952-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念捷克斯洛伐克解放七周年
    康萨拉大使昨天在京举行宴会
    捷政府代表团团长柯别茨基应邀举行讲演会
    【新华社九日讯】五月九日是苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克七周年纪念日。捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使康萨拉九日在北京举行宴会。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深、政务院总理兼外长周恩来、副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，以及中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派和人民团体负责人和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等三百多人。
    应邀到会的各国使节有：苏联大使罗申、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、朝鲜大使权五稷、瑞士公使任佐立、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、越南代表团临时代办周亮、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡、芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特、波兰大使馆临时代办古拉、荷兰政府谈判代表范德普和各国使馆人员。
    在宴会中，康萨拉大使和朱德副主席相继致词。宴会在热烈和友好气氛中进行。
    又讯：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长、曾分别致电捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德和外交部长西罗基，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克解放七周年。
    【新华社九日讯】捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长柯别茨基应中央人民政府文化部的邀请，于九日上午在北京举行讲演会，讲题是“捷克斯洛伐克七年来向社会主义迈进”。讲演会由中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬主持，列席于主席台上的，除捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团的全体团员外，尚有我国中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范、外交部副部长伍修权、人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深。听讲的包括党、政、军、工会及文教机关的负责人和干部，以及首都各学校的学生总共一千二百余人。
    柯别茨基历时两小时的演讲，频频被热烈的掌声所打断，讲演会结束时，并有北京市五一女中的少年儿童队员向柯别茨基团长及全体团员献花致敬。
