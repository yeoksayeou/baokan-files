Title: Banfi, Pancini, and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st Dispatch: After being invited to participate in China's commemorative meeting for the world's four great cultural figures, Italian Senator Banfi and physicist Pancini, who went to Shanghai and Hangzhou for sightseeing, returned to Beijing on the morning of the 21st. Renowned Italian film director De Santis returned to Beijing on the morning of the 19th, after visiting Shanghai a few days ago.

### 班菲潘齐尼等返北京

1952-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    班菲潘齐尼等返北京
    【新华社二十一日讯】应邀参加我国纪念世界四大文化名人纪念大会后，前赴上海、杭州参观的意大利上议员班菲、物理学家潘齐尼，在二十一日上午返抵北京。意大利名电影导演德·桑蒂在日前赴上海参观后，已在十九日上午返京。
