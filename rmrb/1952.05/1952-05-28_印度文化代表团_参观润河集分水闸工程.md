Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Visits Runhe Diversion Project
Author:
Date: 1952-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, May 27th: The Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Mrs. Pandit, visited the Runhe Diversion Project. The delegation highly praised this great project, believing that the fact that the people of New China can build such a huge project with their own strength is the pride and glory of the people of New China. The delegation returned to Bengbu on the 27th and took a car to Nanjing that day.

### 印度文化代表团  参观润河集分水闸工程

1952-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团
    参观润河集分水闸工程
    【新华社蚌埠二十七日电】以潘迪特夫人为首的印度文化代表团参观了润河集分水闸工程。代表团对这一伟大工程甚为赞扬，认为新中国人民能用自己的力量，建造这样巨大的工程，是新中国人民的骄傲和光荣。代表团在二十七日返抵蚌埠，当天乘车去南京。
