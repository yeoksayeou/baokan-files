Title: Soviet Government Delegation Arrives in Prague to Attend Celebration of the Seventh Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th Telegram (TASS): The Soviet government delegation participating in the celebrations of the seventh anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Czechoslovakia arrived in Prague on May 7th. The delegation includes Soviet Marshal Konev (head of the delegation), Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin, and Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Lavrentiev.
    The Soviet government delegation was warmly welcomed at the Ruzyne Airport in Prague, which was decorated with the Soviet and Czechoslovak flags.

### 苏联政府代表团抵布拉格  参加苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克七周年庆祝会

1952-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团抵布拉格
    参加苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克七周年庆祝会
    【新华社讯】塔斯社布拉格七日电：参加苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克七周年庆祝会的苏联政府代表团已于五月七日到达布拉格。代表团包括苏联元帅科涅夫（代表团团长）、苏联外交部副部长佐林和苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使拉夫伦捷夫。
    在苏联国旗和捷克斯洛伐克国旗交相辉映的布拉格鲁钦飞机场上，苏联政府代表团受到热烈欢迎。
