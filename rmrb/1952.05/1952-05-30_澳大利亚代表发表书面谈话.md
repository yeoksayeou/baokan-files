Title: Australian Representatives Issue Written Statement
Author:
Date: 1952-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 29th:
    The Australian representatives who came to participate in the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference issued the following written statement after arriving in Beijing:
    The Australian representatives would like to express their gratitude for the warm welcome and kind care they received upon arriving in People's China. They are particularly pleased to have been welcomed by representatives of the Chinese Young Pioneers and to have received flowers from them. The representatives unanimously stated: We have come from six thousand miles away to attend this conference because we believe that nothing is more important to the people of the world than the cause of peace. The eyes of the world are on our conference. It is extremely important that this conference make real progress in maintaining peace.

### 澳大利亚代表发表书面谈话

1952-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚代表发表书面谈话
    【新华社二十九日讯】前来参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会的澳大利亚代表们在抵达北京后发表下列书面谈话：
    澳大利亚代表们在抵达人民中国之后，受到了热烈的欢迎和亲切的照顾，谨表示感谢。他们尤其欣喜的是，受到中国少年儿童队的代表的欢迎，并接受了他们的献花。代表们一致表示：我们远从六千英里之外前来参加会议，因为我们相信：对全世界人民说来，没有什么比和平事业更为重要。全世界的目光都注视着我们的这次会议。这次会议必须在维护和平方面有真正的进展，这是极端重要的事。
