Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Czech Government Delegation to Celebrate the Signing of Four Agreements on Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Enlai, hosted a banquet on the evening of May 6th, after the signing ceremony of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Postal Agreement, the Telecommunications Agreement, and the Scientific and Technical Cooperation Agreement, to celebrate the entire membership of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation. Attendees at the banquet included all Chinese and Czech personnel who participated in the signing ceremony. During the banquet, hosts and guests frequently toasted to Sino-Czech friendship and cooperation in the fields of culture, postal service, telecommunications, science and technology, as well as to the consolidation and development of world peace. The banquet proceeded throughout in a friendly and pleasant atmosphere.

### 周恩来总理宴请捷政府代表团  庆祝中捷文化合作等四协定的签订

1952-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理宴请捷政府代表团
    庆祝中捷文化合作等四协定的签订
    【新华社六日讯】我国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来于五月六日晚在中捷文化合作协定、邮政协定、电信协定及科学与技术合作协定签字仪式举行后，特宴请捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团全体团员，以示庆贺。出席宴会者为中、捷双方参加签字仪式全体人员。席间宾主频频为中捷友好及文化、邮电、科学技术诸方面的合作以及世界和平事业的巩固与发展而干杯。宴会始终在友好愉快气氛中进行。
