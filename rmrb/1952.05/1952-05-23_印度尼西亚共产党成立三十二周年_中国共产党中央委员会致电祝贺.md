Title: The 32nd Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia - The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Message

Author:
Date: 1952-05-23
Page: 1

    The 32nd Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Message
    [This newspaper reports] To mark the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia on the 23rd, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory telegram. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia:
    On the occasion of the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sends you its warmest congratulations.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    May 20, 1952

### 印度尼西亚共产党成立三十二周年  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党成立三十二周年
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【本报讯】二十三日是印度尼西亚共产党成立三十二周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会：欣逢印度尼西亚共产党成立三十二周年纪念，中国共产党中央委员会特向你们致热烈的庆贺。
    中国共产党中央委员会    一九五二年五月二十日
