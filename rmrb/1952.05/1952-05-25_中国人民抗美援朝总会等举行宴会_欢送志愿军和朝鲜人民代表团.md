Title: China Peace Committee and Other Organizations Hold Banquet to See Off Volunteer Army and Korean People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: The China Peace Committee, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, the North China Peace Committee Branch, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and the Beijing Peace Committee Branch held a banquet at 5:30 PM on the 24th to see off the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China, who are about to return to Korea. All representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China attended. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, Zhang Xiruo, Standing Committee Member of the China Peace Committee and Chairman of the Beijing Branch, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others attended the reception. Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and the embassy's Counselor were also invited. At the beginning of the banquet, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, delivered a brief farewell speech. He said that the work of the delegation "strengthened the militant friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and inspired the people of China to increase production and practice economy, and to resist America and aid Korea." Vice Chairman Peng Zhen thanked the delegation on behalf of the people of the whole country. Finally, he said: "Please convey to the Korean People's Army, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and the Korean people the determination and confidence in victory of our people in supporting the war to resist America and aid Korea, our concern and high respect for them, and wish them new and more brilliant victories on the front lines of striving for a reasonable solution to the Korean issue and opposing aggression and defending peace." Hong Sun-chol, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, said in his reply: "We will bring back to Korea the amazing development of the new China that we have seen, and the great strength of the 500 million brothers in resisting America and aiding Korea, and convey it to the fighting Korean people and the People's Army, thereby further enhancing their confidence in victory and fighting will. We are determined to repay the noble kindness of the Chinese people to us with more heroic struggles and strive for final victory." Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, then gave a reply, thanking the Peace Committee and its branches in various places, the General Political Department and the political departments of various military regions, the people's governments at all levels, and the people of the whole country for their assistance to the delegation's work. During the banquet, everyone frequently toasted to celebrate the victory of the delegation's work, to wish the Korean and Chinese people's armies and the Korean people greater victories, and to the health of General Kim Il-sung, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Marshal Stalin.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会等举行宴会  欢送志愿军和朝鲜人民代表团

1952-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会等举行宴会
    欢送志愿军和朝鲜人民代表团
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部、华北抗美援朝总分会、北京市人民政府、北京市抗美援朝分会于二十四日下午五时半举行宴会，欢送即将返回朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团。中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表全体出席。中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员、北京市分会主席张奚若、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等出席招待。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使权五稷及大使馆参赞亦应邀出席。宴会开始，首先由中国人民抗美援朝总会彭真副主席致简短的欢送词。他说：代表团的工作“加强了中朝人民之间的战斗友谊，鼓舞了全中国人民增产节约、抗美援朝的斗志。”彭真副主席代表全国人民向代表团致谢。最后他说：“请你们把我国人民支援抗美援朝斗争的决心和胜利信心，对于朝鲜人民军、中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民的关怀和崇高的敬意带给他们，并祝他们在争取朝鲜问题的合理解决和反对侵略、保卫和平的前线上获得新的、更辉煌的胜利。”朝鲜人民访华代表团洪淳哲团长在致答词中说：“我们要把我们所看到的新中国惊人的发展面貌，和五万万兄弟的伟大的抗美援朝力量带回朝鲜，传达给正在战斗着的朝鲜人民和人民军，借此更加提高他们的胜利信心和战斗意志。我们决以更英勇的斗争和争取最后的胜利来答谢中国人民对于我们的崇高的恩情。”中国人民志愿军归国代表团李雪三团长接着致答词，他对协助代表团工作的抗美援朝总会和各地分会、总政治部与各军区政治部、各级人民政府及全国人民致谢。席间大家频频为庆祝代表团工作的胜利，为预祝朝中人民军队和朝鲜人民的更大的胜利，为金日成将军、毛泽东主席、斯大林大元帅的健康而干杯。
