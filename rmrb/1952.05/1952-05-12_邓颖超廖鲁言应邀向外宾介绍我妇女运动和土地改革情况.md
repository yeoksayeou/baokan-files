Title: Deng Yingchao and Liao Luyan Invited to Introduce China's Women's Movement and Land Reform to Foreign Guests
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency:

    Deng Yingchao, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, was invited to hold a symposium on the situation of the women's movement in New China on the morning of the 6th with trade union representatives from various countries who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations. The attendees included trade union representatives from the Soviet Union, Romania, Sweden, Czechoslovakia, the United Kingdom, India, Australia, North Korea, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, and Burma, British worker representatives, and Danish "May Day" celebration representatives. Mrs. Thornton and Kuzmink from the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Mrs. Faggi and Mrs. Roa, who came to China to attend the commemorative meeting for four great cultural figures, and the wife of Chilean painter José Venturelli also attended the symposium. In her report, Vice-President Deng Yingchao detailed how Chinese women, with the victory of the Chinese people's revolution, have achieved complete equality with men in politics, economics, culture, education, and social life. She also spoke about the important role and great achievements of Chinese women in the construction of the motherland and in the cause of defending peace. After Vice-President Deng Yingchao's report, heads of various departments of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation answered some questions raised by foreign guests.

    Xinhua News Agency: Liao Luyan, Director of the Counselor's Office of the State Council of the Central People's Government, was invited to hold a symposium on China's land reform on the afternoon of the 6th with foreign trade union representatives who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations and foreign representatives who attended the commemorative meeting for four great cultural figures. The attendees included trade union representatives from Romania, Sweden, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Vietnam, Ceylon, British worker representatives, trade union representatives from India, Australia, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, Burma, Iran, and Algeria, and representatives from the Danish "May Day" celebration delegation. Famous French and Italian figures Faggi, Roa, Banfi, Panzini, and De Sanctis, who came to Beijing to attend the commemorative meeting for four great cultural figures, and Chilean painter José Venturelli and his wife, who were visiting China, also attended the symposium. In his report, Liao Luyan specifically described the face of the old Chinese countryside, the policies and principles of land reform, the content, steps, and methods of land reform, the new face of the Chinese countryside after land reform, and its development prospects. Director Liao Luyan's report was extremely well received by foreign guests. Director Liao Luyan also answered some questions raised by foreign guests at the meeting.

### 邓颖超廖鲁言应邀向外宾介绍我妇女运动和土地改革情况

1952-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    邓颖超廖鲁言应邀向外宾介绍我妇女运动和土地改革情况
    【新华社讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超应邀在六日上午和前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的各国工会代表举行了关于新中国妇女运动情况的座谈会。到会的有：苏联、罗马尼亚、瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克、英国、印度、澳大利亚、朝鲜、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰和缅甸的工会代表，英国工人代表，及丹麦“五一”节观礼代表。世界工联亚澳联络局的桑顿夫人、库兹明克，来我国出席四大文化名人纪念会的法奇夫人，罗阿夫人，以及智利画家何塞·万徒勒里的夫人也出席了座谈会。邓颖超副主席在报告中详细地讲述了中国妇女随着中国人民革命的胜利，在政治上、经济上、文化教育上及社会生活中，获得了与男子完全平等的地位。她还讲述了中国妇女在祖国的建设工作中、在保卫和平事业中所起的重要作用和巨大成就。邓颖超副主席报告后，并由中华全国民主妇女联合会各部门负责人分别答复了外宾提出的一些问题。
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府政务院参事室主任廖鲁言，应邀在六日下午和前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的外国工会代表及参加四大文化名人纪念大会的外国代表举行了关于我国土地改革的座谈会。到会的有：罗马尼亚、瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、越南、锡兰的工会代表，英国工人代表，印度、澳大利亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰、缅甸、伊朗和阿尔及利亚的工会代表，丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团代表。来京参加四大文化名人纪念会的法国和意大利著名人士法奇、罗阿、班菲、潘齐尼、德·桑蒂，访问中国的智利画家何塞·万徒勒里夫妇也参加了座谈会。廖鲁言在报告中具体地讲述了旧中国农村的面貌，土地改革的政策和方针，土地改革的内容、步骤和方法，土地改革后中国农村的新面貌及其发展前途。廖鲁言主任的报告极受外宾欢迎。廖鲁言主任并在会上答复了外宾提出的一些问题。
