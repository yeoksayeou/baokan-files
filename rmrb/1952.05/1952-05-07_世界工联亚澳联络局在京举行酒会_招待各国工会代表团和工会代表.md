Title: Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions for Asia and Australia Holds Reception in Beijing to Entertain Trade Union Delegations and Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch. The Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions for Asia and Australia held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 6th to entertain trade union delegations and trade union representatives from various countries who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations. The reception was hosted by Liu Ningyi, Kauribannov, and Thornton, members of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau. More than 200 people attended.

Invited guests included: Soviet Trade Union Delegation, Romanian Trade Union Delegation, Danish "May Day" Celebration Delegation, Swedish Trade Union Delegation, Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation, German Democratic Republic Trade Union Delegation, Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation, Ceylon Trade Union Representatives, British Workers Delegation, batches of Indian Trade Union Delegations that arrived in China, Australian Trade Union Delegation, Korean Trade Union Delegation, Bulgarian Trade Union Delegation, Hungarian Trade Union Delegation, Polish Trade Union Delegation, Mongolian People's Republic Trade Union Delegation, Burmese Trade Union Delegation, Iranian Trade Union Representative, and Algerian Trade Union Delegation.

Also invited were: Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, heads of various industrial trade unions in our country, Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, and labor model representatives from major administrative regions across the country who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

The reception was presided over by Liu Ningyi, who announced the start, followed by a speech by Thornton. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the unity of the working class in Asia and Australia and the unity of the working class around the world, with extremely enthusiastic emotions.

At the end of the reception, a ceremony was held for the Algerian Trade Union Delegation and the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation to present gifts to each other. The Algerian Trade Union Delegation, on behalf of the dockworkers of their country, presented the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation with a silk banner; the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation presented the Algerian Trade Union Delegation with gifts such as a portrait of President Ho Chi Minh and commemorative badges. In their speeches, the trade union representatives of both countries emphasized the close unity of the working classes of the two countries to fight against imperialism and strive for national liberation, and congratulated the great unity of the working class worldwide. After the gift presentation, the representatives of the two countries warmly embraced, and applause resounded throughout the venue.

### 世界工联亚澳联络局在京举行酒会  招待各国工会代表团和工会代表

1952-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    世界工联亚澳联络局在京举行酒会
    招待各国工会代表团和工会代表
    【新华社六日讯】世界工会联合会亚澳联络局六日下午在北京举行酒会，招待前来中国参加“五一”节观礼的各国工会代表团和工会代表。酒会由亚澳联络局委员刘宁一、考里班诺夫和桑顿主持。到会的有二百多人。
    应邀参加的有：苏联工会代表团、罗马尼亚工会代表团、丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团、瑞典工会代表团、捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团、德意志民主共和国工会代表团、越南工会代表团、锡兰工会代表、英国工人代表团、先后到达中国的印度各批工会代表团、澳大利亚工会代表团、朝鲜工会代表团、保加利亚工会代表团、匈牙利工会代表团、波兰工会代表团、蒙古人民共和国工会代表团、缅甸工会代表团、伊朗工会代表和阿尔及利亚工会代表团。
    应邀参加的还有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、我国各产业工会负责人、北京市总工会副主席萧明，以及由全国各大行政区来北京参加“五一”节观礼的劳动模范代表等。
    酒会由刘宁一主持宣布开始，接着由桑顿致辞。席间宾主频频举杯祝贺亚洲澳洲工人阶级的团结和全世界工人阶级的团结，情绪极为热烈。
    酒会结束时，举行了阿尔及利亚工会代表团和越南工会代表团相互献礼的仪式。阿尔及利亚工会代表团代表本国码头工人赠送越南工会代表团锦旗一面；越南工会代表团赠给阿尔及利亚工会代表团的礼品是胡志明主席像及纪念章等。两国工会代表在致辞中强调两国工人阶级的紧密团结，为反对帝国主义、争取民族解放而斗争，并祝贺全世界工人阶级的大团结。两国代表在献礼后热烈拥抱，全场掌声雷动。
