Title: Indonesian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou by Train
Author:
Date: 1952-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 14th—The Indonesian trade union delegation, consisting of twelve members led by head of delegation Suroso, arrived in Guangzhou by train on the 13th, after the British government delayed issuing transit visas. The delegation was invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to our country to attend the May Day celebrations. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Nengxing, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Liao Limin, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; and Wu Jiemin, Director of the Culture and Education Department, and others.

### 印尼工会代表团已乘车抵达广州

1952-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼工会代表团已乘车抵达广州
    【新华社广州十四日电】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加五一节观礼的印度尼西亚工会代表团一行十二人，由团长苏罗梭率领，因英国政府迟迟不发过境签证，于十三日始乘车抵达广州。前往车站欢迎的有：广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云、市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟、省总工会筹备会秘书长陈能兴、市总工会筹备会秘书长廖立民和文教部长吴介民等。
