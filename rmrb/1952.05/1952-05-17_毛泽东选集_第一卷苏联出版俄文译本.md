Title: "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Volume I, Russian Edition Published in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-05-17
Page: 1

    "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Volume I, Russian Edition Published in the Soviet Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th] According to a TASS report from Moscow on the 15th: The Russian translation of Volume I of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has been published by the Soviet Foreign Languages Publishing House. The entire four volumes of the Selected Works will be published by the bureau in succession. The Selected Works include the most important works of Comrade Mao Zedong in various periods of the Chinese revolution. Volume I includes "On Practice," which is very famous in the Soviet Union.
    [Xinhua News Agency] The second volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" was simultaneously published in Hong Kong and Macau on April 26, and was warmly welcomed by the broad masses of patriotic compatriots. The Hong Kong "Ta Kung Pao" and "Wen Wei Po" published commentaries on the same day to express their congratulations.

### “毛泽东选集”第一卷苏联出版俄文译本

1952-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    “毛泽东选集”第一卷苏联出版俄文译本
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：“毛泽东选集”第一卷的俄文译本，已由苏联外国作品出版局出版。选集全部四卷，将由该局陆续印行。选集包括了毛泽东同志在中国革命各个时期中的最重要的著作。第一卷中包括了在苏联非常著名的“实践论”。
    【新华社讯】“毛泽东选集”第二卷已于四月二十六日同时在香港、澳门发行，受到广大爱国同胞的热烈欢迎，香港“大公报”和“文汇报”当日都发表了评论，表示庆祝。
