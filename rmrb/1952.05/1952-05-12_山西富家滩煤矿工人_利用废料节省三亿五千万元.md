Title: Workers at Shanxi Fujiatan Coal Mine Save 350 Million Yuan by Utilizing Waste Materials
Author: Zhi Jian, Wu Xiang, Wang Biao, Shi Lin, Xue Liang
Date: 1952-05-12
Page: 2

    Reader's Letters

    Workers at Shanxi Fujiatan Coal Mine
    Utilize Waste Materials to Save 350 Million Yuan
    Editor: After the "Three-Anti" movement, the employees of the Shanxi Fujiatan Coal Mine actively used their brains and found ways to save funds and create wealth for the country.

    Labor model Kang Yongxiang proposed a rationalization suggestion, saving the state over 14,000 catties of millet. Workers in the repair shop, seeing the lack of copper plates, used boiler plates destroyed by the Japanese invaders to replace copper plates, saving the state 140 million yuan. Zhang Chuanyou and Hu Ximing of the foundry group utilized scrap iron from damaged coal cars instead of good materials when manufacturing ghatas, pulleys, and track rollers. Electrician Sun Zhengta used tape to wrap old iron wires instead of electrical wires, repairing three damaged transformers and converting a damaged transformer into a pole-mounted machine. In terms of utilizing waste materials, the entire repair shop increased production and saved 357.5 million yuan for the country. Zhi Jian, Wu Xiang, Wang Biao, Shi Lin, Xue Liang

### 山西富家滩煤矿工人  利用废料节省三亿五千万元
志坚  武祥  王彪  石林  学良
1952-05-12
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    山西富家滩煤矿工人
    利用废料节省三亿五千万元
    编辑同志：山西富家滩煤矿职工在“三反”运动后，积极开动脑筋，想办法为国家节省资金，创造财富。
    劳动模范康永祥提出一件合理化建议，给国家节省小米一万四千余斤。修理场的工人看到没有铜板，便利用日寇炸毁的锅炉片代替铜板，为国家节省一亿四千万元。翻砂组张传有、胡喜铭在制造堕陀、槽轮、道中轮时，利用坏煤车废铁代替好料。电工孙正田以胶布裹在旧铁丝上，代替电线，修好三个坏变压器，还把一部坏变压器改为电杆机。全修理厂在利用废料方面，为国家增产节约三亿五千七百五十万元。志坚  武祥  王彪  石林  学良
