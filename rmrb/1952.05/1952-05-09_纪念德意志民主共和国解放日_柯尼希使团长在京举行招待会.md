Title: Commemorating the Liberation Day of the German Democratic Republic: Head of Mission König Holds Reception in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Ambassador-level Head of Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, König, held a reception in Beijing on the 8th to commemorate the Liberation Day of the German Democratic Republic.

Those invited to attend were: Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei; President of the Supreme People's Court, Shen Junru; Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Nie Rongzhen; Deputy Chief of the General Staff, Su Yu; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiuquan; Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Xiao Hua; other responsible persons from various ministries of the Central People's Government and leaders of various national people's organizations, Zhang Xiruo, Ma Yinchu, Xie Juezai, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Bojun, Liang Xi, Shen Yanbing, Li Dequan, Hu Yuzhi, Yu Xinqing, Wu Yuzhang, Feng Wenbin, Peng Zemin, as well as Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Youyu, and others.

Also invited to attend were the Ambassadors of the Soviet Union, Romania, the Mongolian People's Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Korea, Czechoslovakia and other countries to China, the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation to China, and the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China.

All members of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation headed by Kopetzky were also invited to attend.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere, and the German Democratic Republic film "Fighting Village" was shown after the reception.

### 纪念德意志民主共和国解放日  柯尼希使团长在京举行招待会

1952-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念德意志民主共和国解放日
    柯尼希使团长在京举行招待会
    【新华社八日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，为纪念德意志民主共和国解放日，特于八日在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长粟裕，外交部副部长伍修权，人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，及中央人民政府各部其他负责人与各全国性人民团体负责人张奚若、马寅初、谢觉哉、滕代远、朱学范、章伯钧、梁希、沈雁冰、李德全、胡愈之、余心清、吴玉章、冯文彬、彭泽民以及北京市副市长张友渔等。
    苏联、罗马尼亚、蒙古人民共和国、匈牙利、保加利亚、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克等国驻华大使，越南驻华代表团临时代办及波兰驻华大使馆临时代办等亦应邀出席。
    应邀出席的还有以柯别茨基为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团全体团员。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行，会后并放映德意志民主共和国影片“战斗的乡村”。
