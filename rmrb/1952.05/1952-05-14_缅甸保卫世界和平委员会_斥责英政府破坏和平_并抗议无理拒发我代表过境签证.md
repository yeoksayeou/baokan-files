Title: Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace Condemns the British Government for Undermining Peace and Protests the Unreasonable Denial of Transit Visas to Our Representatives
Author:
Date: 1952-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The British government unreasonably refused to issue transit visas to representatives of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace who were to attend the All-Burma Peace Conference held in Rangoon on May 10th. The Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace specifically sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace on the 10th, condemning the British government's act of undermining peace. The telegram is as follows: To the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace:

	Up to now, more than one thousand representatives and special invitees from all over the country and various professions have participated in the All-Burma Peace Conference. The actions of the British and Hong Kong governments in undermining peace have aroused our great anger. We believe that the actions of the British imperialists are intended to oppose world peace, conceal the exposure of the United States' germ warfare in Korea, and undermine our internal peace. We demand that the British and Hong Kong governments issue transit visas to the Chinese people's peace representatives.

	The Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace
	May 10th

### 缅甸保卫世界和平委员会  斥责英政府破坏和平  并抗议无理拒发我代表过境签证

1952-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会
    斥责英政府破坏和平
    并抗议无理拒发我代表过境签证
    【新华社十二日讯】英国政府无理拒发出席五月十日在仰光召开的全缅和平大会的中国人民保卫世界和平委员会代表的过境签证。缅甸保卫世界和平委员会十日特致电中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，斥责英国政府破坏和平的行为。电文如下。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会：
    到现在为止，参加全缅和平大会的代表和特邀代表已超过一千人，他们是来自各地和各种职业的。英国和香港政府破坏和平的行动，激起了我们极大的愤怒。我们认定，英帝国主义者的行为，是为了反对世界和平，掩饰美国在朝鲜进行细菌战的真相的暴露，并破坏我们内部的和平。我们要求英国和香港政府发给中国人民和平代表的过境签证。
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会
    五月十日
