Title: The Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region: The Central People's Government's Government Administration Council Sends a Delegation to Congratulate

Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, 30th. May 1st marks the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. The Central People's Government's Government Administration Council has sent a delegation to offer congratulations. The delegation includes Peng Zeming, Wang Baozhen, Jin Xiansheng, and Yang Chengsen, representatives of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, and Sa Kongliao (Mongolian), Mengteer (Tibetan), A'aisha (Uyghur), Yan Xinmin, and Zhang Yingda (Hui), representatives of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, totaling nine people.

The delegation arrived in Zhangjiakou, the seat of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, on the afternoon of the 29th. When the delegation arrived, Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, Wang Zaitian, member of the Inner Mongolia Branch Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and representatives from various agencies, organizations, troops, and schools in Inner Mongolia stationed in Zhangjiakou, all welcomed them at the station.

### 内蒙古自治区成立五周年  中央人民政府政务院派代表团前往祝贺

1952-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　内蒙古自治区成立五周年
    中央人民政府政务院派代表团前往祝贺
    【新华社张家口三十日电】五月一日是内蒙古自治区成立五周年纪念日，中央人民政府政务院特派代表团前往祝贺。代表团包括中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会代表彭泽民、王葆真、金献生、杨成森，民族事务委员会代表萨空了（蒙族）、孟特尔（藏族）、阿艾沙（维吾尔族）、严信民、张英达（回族）共九人。
    该团在二十九日下午抵达内蒙古自治区人民政府驻地张家口。代表团到达时，内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫、中共中央内蒙古分局委员王再天，和内蒙古驻张家口各机关、团体、部队、学校的代表等多人都到车站欢迎。
