Title: Hungarian National People's Art Troupe to Perform in Various Locations; Ministry of Culture and All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Host Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1952-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: After several large-scale performances in Beijing, the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe will soon travel to other parts of China to perform before returning to Hungary. The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, together with the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, hosted a farewell banquet on the afternoon of the 17th. Among those attending the banquet were: Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; heads of various departments under the Ministry of Culture; and over two hundred people from the literary and art circles of the capital. Huang Zhen, China's ambassador to Hungary, and his wife, who are currently in Beijing, also attended the banquet. Also invited to the banquet were: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Sziklai Ferenc, and his wife and embassy staff, and Korean dancer Choi Seung-hee, among others.

Minister Shen Yanbing delivered a speech at the meeting, extending a warm farewell to the art troupe as they leave Beijing. He pointed out that the art troupe's performances in China have been warmly welcomed by the Chinese people, and have played a role in promoting cultural exchange between the two peoples of China and Hungary.

The head of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, Székely György, then gave a reply. He expressed his gratitude for the warm hospitality the art troupe received during its stay in Beijing, and said that he would work to promote the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and Hungary after returning to his country.

The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of unity and friendship. Before the meeting, a performance of artistic programs was held, with the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe performing Chinese dances, and the Opera and Dance Theater attached to the Central Academy of Drama and the China Youth Art Theatre performing Hungarian dances. The Central Academy of Drama and other units and the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe exchanged gifts. Finally, a documentary film shot by the Beijing Film Studio about the Hungarian Art Troupe's performances in China was shown.

### 匈牙利国家人民文工团将赴各地表演  中央文化部会同全国文联设宴送行

1952-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家人民文工团将赴各地表演
    中央文化部会同全国文联设宴送行
    【新华社十七日讯】匈牙利国家人民文工团在北京作多次较大规模的演出后，即将前往我国其他地方表演，然后返回匈牙利。中央人民政府文化部会同中华全国文学艺术界联合会在十七日下午设宴送行。出席宴会的有：文化部部长、中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席沈雁冰，文化部副部长、中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，以及文化部所属各部门负责人，和首都文学、艺术界人士共二百多人。现在北京的我国驻匈牙利大使黄镇和夫人也出席了宴会。应邀赴宴的还有：匈牙利驻我国大使夏法朗柯和夫人及大使馆人员，朝鲜舞蹈家崔承喜等人。
    沈雁冰部长在会上致辞，对文工团离开北京表示热诚送别。他指出文工团在我国的表演已得到我国人民的热烈欢迎，对于中匈两国人民的文化交流起了推动作用。
    匈牙利国家人民文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊继起致答辞。他对文工团在北京期间所受到的热诚款待表示感谢，并表示将来回国后将为促进中匈两国人民的友谊和文化交流而努力。
    宴会上充满着团结友好的气氛。会前曾举行文艺节目表演，由匈牙利国家人民文工团表演中国舞蹈，并由中央戏剧学院附属歌舞剧院和中国青年剧院表演匈牙利舞蹈。中央戏剧学院等单位和匈牙利国家人民文工团互相献礼。最后并放映了北京电影制片厂所拍摄的匈牙利文工团在我国表演的纪录影片。
