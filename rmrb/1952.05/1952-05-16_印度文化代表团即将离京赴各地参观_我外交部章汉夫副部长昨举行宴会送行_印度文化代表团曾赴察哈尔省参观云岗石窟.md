Title: Indian Cultural Delegation to Depart Beijing for Visits to Various Places; Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of Our Foreign Ministry Held a Banquet Yesterday to See Them Off; Indian Cultural Delegation Visited the Yungang Grottoes in Chahar Province
Author:
Date: 1952-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: After visiting Beijing, the Indian Cultural Delegation is about to leave for visits to various places. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, specially held a banquet to see the delegation off.

Those attending the banquet included Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin, Li Dequan, Minister of the Ministry of Health, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education, Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Trade, Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China, Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others, as well as Yuan Zhongxian, our Ambassador to India, Li Yimang, Deputy Head of our former Cultural Delegation to India and Burma, and many members of the delegation in Beijing.

Indian Ambassador to China, Panikkar, and diplomatic personnel from the embassy were also invited to attend the banquet.

During the banquet, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu delivered a speech. He expressed his regret at the delegation’s departure from Beijing. He said: The Indian Cultural Delegation’s visit to our country, like our cultural delegation’s visit to India last year, has given the cultural exchange between China and India a good start and development on a new basis. I believe that through this friendly mutual visit, the friendship between the people of China and India will grow and be consolidated. The sincere cooperation between the people of the two great Asian nations will greatly contribute to the lasting peace of Asia and the world. He finally raised his glass to congratulate the success of the Indian Cultural Delegation, to congratulate the good start and development of friendship and cultural exchange between China and India on a new basis, and to congratulate the lasting peace of Asia and the world.

Mrs. Pandit, head of the Indian Cultural Delegation, said in her speech: As we now prepare to leave this beautiful and warmly welcoming city, we are full of hope, full of hope for the growth of Sino-Indian friendship and for the future of the world. We look forward to continuing to maintain contact and friendship in the cultural and other fields, a friendship brought about by the exchange of cultural delegations between the two countries. She expressed her gratitude for the hospitality received by the delegation and raised her glass to congratulate the prosperity of the People's Republic of China.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The Indian Cultural Delegation left Beijing on the 13th for Datong, Chahar Province, en route to visit the Yungang Grottoes. After completing their visit, the delegation returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 15th.

At the Yungang Grottoes, the delegation saw many stone Buddhas and reliefs from the Northern Wei Dynasty and subsequent dynasties of our country, and visited the Huayan Temple in Datong City. The Datong Municipal People's Government hosted a banquet for the delegation.

Before going to Yungang, some members of the delegation also visited the mechanized farming school affiliated with the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government in Beijing, and visited the school's chicken coops, dairy cow sheds, and pigsties.

### 印度文化代表团即将离京赴各地参观  我外交部章汉夫副部长昨举行宴会送行  印度文化代表团曾赴察哈尔省参观云岗石窟

1952-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团即将离京赴各地参观
    我外交部章汉夫副部长昨举行宴会送行
    印度文化代表团曾赴察哈尔省参观云岗石窟
    【新华社十五日讯】印度文化代表团在北京参观后，即将离京前往各地参观。我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫特举行宴会，为代表团送行。
    参加宴会者有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林，卫生部部长李德全，教育部副部长曾昭抡，贸易部副部长姚依林，中国科学院副院长竺可桢，中国人民大学校长吴玉章，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等以及我国驻印度大使袁仲贤，我国前赴印缅文化代表团副团长李一氓和在京团员多人。
    印度驻华大使潘尼迦和使馆外交人员也应邀参加宴会。
    席间，章汉夫副部长致词。他对印度文化代表团的离京，表示惜别之意。他说：印度文化代表团的访问我国，正如去年我国文化代表团访问印度一样，使中印文化交流在新的基础上有了良好的开端和发展。我相信，经过这一次友好的相互访问，中印两国人民间的友谊将日益发展和巩固。亚洲两大国家人民的真诚合作，将大有助于亚洲与世界的持久和平。他最后举杯祝贺印度文化代表团的成功，祝贺中印两国的友谊与文化交流在新的基础上的良好开端和发展，并祝贺亚洲与世界的持久和平。
    印度文化代表团团长潘迪特夫人致词说：当我们现在准备离开这个美丽的、热诚招待的城市的时候，我们满怀着希望，满怀着对中印友谊增长和对世界未来的希望。我们盼望在文化和其他领域里继续保持联系，保持友谊，这种友谊是两国交换文化代表团所带来的。她对于代表团所受到的款待表示谢意，并举杯祝贺中华人民共和国的繁荣。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
    【新华社十五日讯】印度文化代表团于十三日离京前往察哈尔省大同转往云岗石窟参观。代表团参观完毕后，已于十五日下午返抵北京。
    代表团在云岗石窟看到了我国北魏及其后各朝代的许多石佛和浮雕，并在大同市参观了市内的华严寺。大同市人民政府曾设宴招待代表团。
    在赴云岗参观以前，代表团一部分团员又曾在北京参观了中央人民政府农业部所属机耕学校，并参观了该校的鸡舍、乳牛舍和猪舍。
