Title: Southwest Ethnic Groups Delegation Returns to Chongqing; Qinghai Tibetan Delegation, Including Xiacang, Returns to Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 28th: The delegation of representatives from ethnic groups in the Southwest, including representatives from Western Sichuan, Southern Sichuan, Northern Sichuan, and Xikang, totaling 74 people, returned to Chongqing on the 25th. Chen Keda, Secretary-General of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, Sunge Bardon, Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Chongqing, and representatives from relevant agencies welcomed them at the dock. He Long and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, received these representatives on the afternoon of the 27th, encouraging them to continue their efforts in promoting ethnic unity after returning to their regions. The Southwest Ethnic Affairs Commission also invited the representatives to a forum. At the meeting, the representatives spoke enthusiastically, unanimously expressing their gratitude to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government for giving representatives of all brotherly ethnic groups the opportunity to visit the inland areas under construction. They excitedly described the great achievements they had witnessed with their own eyes in the political, economic, cultural, and other constructions of the motherland, and expressed their determination to build the minority areas as beautiful as the inland areas.
    The delegation's visit lasted more than 80 days, during which they visited factories, schools, and scenic spots in major cities such as Hankou, Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Nanjing. During the visit, they received warm receptions from the leaders of the Central People's Government and local people's governments, and enthusiastic welcomes from people of all circles in various places. They will soon return to their respective ethnic regions to report to the people of all ethnic groups. The 104 representatives from Yunnan and Guizhou, belonging to the delegation, have already returned home from Hankou separately.

    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 28th: The Qinghai Tibetan Delegation, including Xiacang (Living Buddha of Longwu Temple in Tongren County), returned to Xi'an by train on the morning of May 27th after visiting Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Meng Dingjun, Secretary-General of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Northwest Military and Administrative Committee, He Tingjun, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Northwest Military and Administrative Committee, and Losang (Tibetan), Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Xi'an, and others.

### 西南各族代表参观团返重庆  青海藏族参观团夏日仓等返西安

1952-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    西南各族代表参观团返重庆
    青海藏族参观团夏日仓等返西安
    【新华社重庆二十八日电】西南各民族晋京代表参观团所属川西、川南、川北、西康等地代表一行七十四人，已于二十五日返抵重庆。西南军政委员会民族事务委员会秘书长陈可大、西藏班禅驻重庆办事处处长孙格巴顿及各有关机关代表都到码头欢迎。西南军政委员会副主席贺龙、邓小平在二十七日下午接见了这些代表，勉励他们回去后继续致力于民族团结工作。西南民族事务委员会并邀请代表们举行座谈会。会上，代表们踊跃发言，一致感谢毛主席和中央人民政府的关怀，使各兄弟民族的代表有机会参观了正在建设中的内地。他们激动地叙述着自己亲眼见到的祖国在政治、经济、文化等各种建设上的伟大成就，并表示要把少数民族地区也建设得和内地一样美好。
    该团此次参观共历时八十多天，先后在汉口、北京、天津、上海、杭州、南京等大城市参观了工厂、学校和名胜古迹。在参观中，受到中央人民政府及各地人民政府首长的热情招待，和各地各界人民的热烈欢迎。最近他们即将返回本民族地区，向各族人民作传达报告。该团所属云南、贵州两地的代表一百零四人，已由汉口分道回乡。
    【新华社西安二十八日电】青海藏族参观团夏日仓（同仁县隆务寺活佛）等人，在北京、天津、上海、杭州等地参观后，已于五月二十七日上午乘车返抵西安。前往车站欢迎的有：西北军政委员会民族事务委员会秘书长蒙定军、西北军政委员会交际处副处长贺廷俊和西藏班禅驻西安办事处处长罗桑（藏族）等多人。
