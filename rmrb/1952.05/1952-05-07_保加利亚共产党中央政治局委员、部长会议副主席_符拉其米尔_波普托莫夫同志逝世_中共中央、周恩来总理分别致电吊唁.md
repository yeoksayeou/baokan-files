Title: Comrade Vladimir Poptomov, Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Passes Away; The CPC Central Committee and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams of Condolence Respectively
Author:
Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Council of Ministers issued a joint announcement stating: "The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Council of Ministers announce with deep sorrow that Comrade Vladimir Poptomov, an outstanding Communist activist, a loyal comrade-in-arms of Dimitrov, a long-tested fighter for the Communist cause, Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, passed away due to a long illness at 2:45 PM on May 1st of this year."

    Telegram of Condolence from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    Sofia
    Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party:
    We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Poptomov, Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party. Please accept our deepest condolences!
    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    Beijing, May 6, 1952

    Telegram of Condolence from Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council
    To Comrade Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria:
    I was shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Vladimir Poptomov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, a steadfast fighter who fought tirelessly for the cause of the Bulgarian people. I extend my deepest condolences to you, the government and people of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    Beijing, May 4, 1952

### 保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席  符拉其米尔·波普托莫夫同志逝世  中共中央、周恩来总理分别致电吊唁

1952-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席
    符拉其米尔·波普托莫夫同志逝世
    中共中央、周恩来总理分别致电吊唁
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会和部长会议发表联合通告说：“保加利亚共产党中央委员会和部长会议沉痛通告：杰出的共产党活动家、季米特洛夫的忠实战友、久经考验的共产主义事业的战士、保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员、部长会议副主席符拉其米尔·波普托莫夫久病不治，已于本年五月一日下午二时四十五分逝世。”
    中国共产党中央委员会唁电
    索非亚
    保加利亚共产党中央委员会：
    得悉保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员波普托莫夫同志病逝，谨致深切的哀悼！
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年五月六日北京
    政务院周恩来总理唁电
    保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席
    契尔文科夫同志：
    惊悉保加利亚部长会议副主席符拉其米尔·波普托莫夫同志——为保加利亚人民事业而斗争不懈的坚贞战士——不幸逝世，谨向您、保加利亚人民共和国政府及人民致沉痛的哀悼。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院
    总理周恩来
    一九五二年五月四日于北京
