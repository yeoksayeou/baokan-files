Title: Commemorating the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Sweden: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 1

    To commemorate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Sweden
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
    [Report from this newspaper] May 21st marks the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Sweden. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Sweden: On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Sweden, please accept our warm congratulations.
    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, May 19th

### 瑞典共产党成立三十五周年纪念  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典共产党成立三十五周年纪念
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【本报讯】五月二十一日是瑞典共产党成立三十五周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：瑞典共产党中央委员会：欣逢瑞典共产党成立三十五周年纪念，谨致热烈的祝贺。
    中国共产党中央委员会五月十九日
