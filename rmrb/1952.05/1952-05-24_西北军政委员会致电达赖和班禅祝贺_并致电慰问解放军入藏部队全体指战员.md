Title: Northwest Military and Political Committee Sends Telegrams of Congratulations to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, and Sends Telegrams of Condolence to All Commanders and Fighters of the PLA Troops Entering Tibet

Author: 
Date: 1952-05-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 23rd. The Northwest Military and Political Committee sent telegrams to the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and all monks and lay people of Tibet, congratulating them on the first anniversary of the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The original telegram is as follows: To the Dalai Lama, Mr. Panchen Erdeni, and to all monks and lay people of Tibet:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, we send this telegram of congratulations. The signing and implementation of this agreement has ushered the Tibetan people into a new historical period, enabling them to return forever to the great family of the People's Republic of China, free from the enslavement of imperialist aggressor forces, and to enjoy the rights of national equality together with other ethnic groups in the country, and to move towards a bright and happy future. Over the past year, the Dalai Lama and Mr. Panchen Erdeni, in close unity, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, with the assistance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and with the warm support of all monks and lay people in Tibet, are smoothly implementing the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. It is hoped that you will continue to unite all monks and lay people in Tibet and work together to thoroughly implement the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, consolidate national defense, and build a prosperous and happy new Tibet.
    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 23rd. On the eve of the first anniversary of the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, the Northwest Military and Political Committee sent a telegram of condolence to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet. The original telegram is as follows: Representative Zhang Jingwu forwards to Generals Zhang Guohua and Tan Guansan and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the signing of the "Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet," we extend our condolences and pay our sincere respects!
    Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, you have overcome all kinds of difficulties, obtained the active assistance of the Dalai Lama, Mr. Panchen Erdeni and the Tibetan local government, and the warm welcome of the Tibetan monks and lay people, and successfully entered Tibet, and with your exemplary behavior, united the vast number of monks and lay people, and achieved brilliant achievements in implementing the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. It is hoped that you will continue to work hard, and together with the Dalai Lama and Mr. Panchen Erdeni, unite all monks and lay people in Tibet, and strive to build a prosperous and happy new Tibet!

### 西北军政委员会致电达赖和班禅祝贺  并致电慰问解放军入藏部队全体指战员

1952-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    西北军政委员会致电达赖和班禅祝贺
    并致电慰问解放军入藏部队全体指战员
    【新华社西安二十三日电】西北军政委员会致电达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和西藏全体僧、俗人民，祝贺和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年。原电如下：达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼先生并转西藏全体僧俗人民：
    值此和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年之际，特电致贺。这一协议的签订和实行，使西藏人民进入了一个新的历史时期，使西藏人民永远回到中华人民共和国祖国大家庭之中，脱离帝国主义侵略势力的奴役，和国内其他民族一起共同享受民族平等的权利，走向光明和幸福的前途。一年以来达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼先生亲密团结，在毛主席和中央人民政府英明领导下，在中国人民解放军的协助下，在全藏僧俗人民的热烈支持下，正顺利地实现着和平解放西藏办法的协议。希望你们继续团结全藏僧俗人民，为彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议，巩固祖国国防、建设繁荣幸福的新西藏而共同奋斗。
    【新华社西安二十三日电】在和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年的前夕，西北军政委员会致电慰问驻藏人民解放军全体指战员。原电如下：张经武代表转张国华、谭冠三将军及驻藏人民解放军全体指战员同志们：
    值此“和平解放西藏办法的协议”签订一周年之际，谨电慰问，并致以亲切的敬意！
    你们在毛主席的英明领导下，克服了种种困难，获得达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼先生和西藏地方政府的积极协助以及西藏僧俗人民的热烈欢迎，胜利进入西藏，并以自己的模范行为，团结广大僧俗人民，为实现和平解放西藏办法的协议，获得辉煌成就。尚望继续努力，协同达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼先生团结全藏僧俗人民，为建设繁荣幸福的新西藏而奋斗！
