Title: Finnish Minister Varvanne Recalled Home; New Minister Knöring Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 28th -- Finnish Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Varvanne, has been recalled from Beijing. The Finnish government has now appointed Mr. Knöring as the Finnish Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China.
    Minister Knöring arrived in Beijing by train on May 28th. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was at the station to greet him. Also present were Mr. Brondstandt, Chargé d'affaires ad interim, and Mr. Jela, Archives Secretary, of the Finnish Legation in China.

### 芬兰公使瓦尔万尼奉调返国  新任公使克诺林昨抵北京

1952-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰公使瓦尔万尼奉调返国
    新任公使克诺林昨抵北京
    【新华社二十八日讯】芬兰首任驻华特命全权公使瓦尔万尼已奉调离北京，芬兰政府现已任命克诺林为芬兰驻华特命全权公使。
    克诺林公使于五月二十八日乘车抵京。赴车站迎接的有我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如。此外尚有芬兰驻华公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特及档案秘书耶拉。
