    Organization is Stronger than Working Alone
    Li Qun, Zhang Huaixin, Yao Heng, Ji Lin, Hao Chao, Yun Ting
    (7) Wang Quanbao continued to use his old farm tools. Looking at the mutual aid team, he said unconvinced, "Don't worry, just watch me. My grain this year won't be less than what you harvest."
    (8) The mutual aid team leader, Li Sixiao, led well. Whenever he had free time, he would gather everyone to read the newspaper and study. The political awareness of the team members gradually improved, and they knew where society was going. The newspaper said: "Love the country and grow cotton, consolidate the worker-peasant alliance..." Li Sixiao's mutual aid team actively responded to this call, revised the patriotic pact, and decided to plant thirty mu of cotton.
    (9) After democratic discussion, Li Sixiao's mutual aid team planned and divided the work: some people carried out warm water seed soaking, and some people turned the soil and applied base fertilizer.

### 组织起来比单干强（图片）
力群　张怀信　药恒  吉林  郝超  云挺作
1952-05-16
第3版()
专栏：

    组织起来比单干强
    力群　张怀信　药恒  吉林  郝超  云挺作
    七）王全保照旧使用那套老农具。他看着互助组，不服气地说：“不发愁，你们看我吧，我今年的粮食，不会比你们打得少。”
    （八）互助组长李四小领导得好，一有闲空，便集合大家来读报，进行学习。组员们的政治觉悟逐渐提高了，他们知道了社会往那里走。报上说：“爱国家种棉花，巩固工农联盟……”李四小互助组就积极地响应了这个号召，修订了爱国公约，决定种三十亩棉花。
    （九）李四小互助组经过民主讨论后，有计划地分了工：一部分人进行温汤浸种工作，一部分人翻地施基肥。
