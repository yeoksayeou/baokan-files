Title: Czechoslovakian and Polish Observation Delegations Depart Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1952-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th:

    The Czechoslovakian, Polish, and Algerian trade union delegations, and five members of the Danish "May Day" observation delegation who were invited to China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 27th to return to their countries. Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department, and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 捷波等国观礼代表离京返国

1952-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    捷波等国观礼代表离京返国
    【新华社二十七日讯】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、阿尔及利亚工会代表团和丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团团员五人已在二十七日晨乘飞机离北京回国。前往机场欢送的有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、组织部部长许之桢等人。
