Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Representatives from Various Countries' Trade Union Delegations
Author:
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government received representatives from various countries' trade union delegations who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations and held a tea party in their honor. Those present included Kauli Banno and Thornton, representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions; Shefchenko, head of the Soviet Union Trade Union Delegation; Stoia Alina, head of the Romanian Trade Union Delegation, and member Aleksink Seming; Gilde Abel, head of the Danish "May Day" Observation Delegation, and members Elsi Jensen and Ranield Andersen; Nate Senande, head of the Swedish Trade Union Delegation, and members Walter Anderson and Ronnie Öhman; Yulei Sun, head of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation; Gut Helbig, head of the German Democratic Republic Trade Union Delegation, and secretary Janna Stern; Yang Bailian, head of the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation; Saralanankara, a trade unionist from Ceylon; Frank Haywood, head of the British Workers' Delegation; Sha Shi, Yusuf, Mitra, Saksena, Chakraborty and Sha Shi's secretary Ms. Anklyese Airiya from the Indian Trade Union Delegation; Wright, head of the Australian Trade Union Delegation; Park Hyo-bin, head of the Korean Trade Union Delegation; Ivan Vassilev Mitov, head of the Bulgarian Trade Union Delegation; Bodgornik Joseph, head of the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation; Wiktor Drożyński, head of the Polish Trade Union Delegation; Ganbaosuren, head of the Mongolian People's Republic Trade Union Delegation; Deqin Qin Su, head of the Burmese Trade Union Delegation; two representatives from the Iranian Trade Union; Mussa Braham, head of the Algerian Trade Union Delegation, and members Abteer-Gadai Babu, Boalem Buyuba and Abteer-Gadai Deliday.
Also present were Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Trade Union; Dong Xin, First Deputy Director of the Policy Research Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Trade Union.
Premier Zhou Enlai answered questions raised by the representatives of various countries at the meeting.
The tea party proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理接见各国工会代表团人员

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见各国工会代表团人员
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，五日接见来中国参加“五一”节观礼的各国工会代表团人员，并举行茶会招待。到会的有世界工会联合会代表考里班诺夫和桑顿，苏联工会代表团团长谢甫钦科，罗马尼亚工会代表团团长斯托雅·爱丽娜、团员阿列克辛克·谢明，丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团团长吉尔德·阿伯尔，团员爱尔希·真森夫人和兰尼尔德·安德生夫人，瑞典工会代表团团长纳特·赛南德、团员瓦尔特·安德森和罗尼·欧曼，捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团团长尤莱·逊，德意志民主共和国工会代表团团长古特·海尔比希、秘书让纳·斯德恩，越南工会代表团团长杨白莲，锡兰工会代表撒拉南卡拉，英国工人代表团团长弗兰克·海渥德，印度工会代表团的沙氏、尤索夫、米特拉、萨克森纳、查克拉伐第和沙氏的秘书安克利丝艾丽亚女士，澳大利亚工会代表团团长莱特，朝鲜工会代表团团长朴孝斌，保加利亚工会代表团团长伊凡·瓦西列夫·米笃夫，匈牙利工会代表团团长波德哥尔尼克·约瑟夫，波兰工会代表团团长维克多·德洛日支，蒙古人民共和国工会代表团团长冈波苏仑，缅甸工会代表团长德钦钦素，伊朗工会代表二人，阿尔及利亚工会代表团团长慕撒·勃拉汉姆、团员阿勃特尔——加代·巴步、波阿雷姆·步尤巴和阿勃台尔——加代·德里代。
    在座的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏，中华全国总工会政策研究室第一副主任董昕，中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫等。
    周恩来总理在会上答复了各国代表提出的问题。
    茶会在友好气氛中进行。
