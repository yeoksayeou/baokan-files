Title: Guo Moruo and Xie Juezai Invited to Give Reports to Foreign Guests, Introducing China's Peace Movement and Political Power Construction

Author:
Date: 1952-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, was invited on the morning of the 7th to give a report to foreign guests who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations and visit, on China's peace movement. Those present included representatives of the Romanian Trade Union, Danish "May Day" observation delegation, trade union representatives from Sweden, Czechoslovakia, Vietnam, and Ceylon, British worker representatives, and trade union representatives from Australia, North Korea, Mongolia, Burma, and Algeria. Also present were: French representatives Farge and his wife, Roa, Italian representatives Banfi, Panzini, and De Santi, Chilean painter José Venturelli and his wife, who came to Beijing to attend the anniversaries of Avicenna, Da Vinci, Hugo, and Gogol, and representatives from Australia, Britain, Indonesia, Burma, India and other countries who came to China for a visit after attending the International Economic Conference. In his report, Guo Moruo introduced the Chinese people's movement to defend world peace, especially the widespread and in-depth Resist America, Aid Korea movement launched across the country since American imperialism launched the war of aggression against Korea and aggression against China's territory of Taiwan, as well as the current main tasks of the China Peace Committee. Guo Moruo's report received warm applause from the foreign guests.

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Xie Juezai, Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, was invited on the morning of the 6th to give a report on the political power of the People's Republic of China to foreign trade union representatives who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, and foreign representatives who came to Beijing to attend the memorial meeting for the four great cultural figures. Present were: Soviet trade union representatives, the Danish "May Day" observation delegation, trade union representatives from Sweden, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Vietnam, and Ceylon, British worker representatives, trade union representatives from India, Australia, North Korea, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, Burma, Iran, and Algeria, and French and Italian representatives Farge, Roa, Banfi, Panzini, and De Santi who attended the memorial meeting for the four great figures. Chilean painter José Venturelli, who was visiting China, was also invited to attend. In his report, Minister Xie Juezai explained the nature, tasks, and organizational forms of the political power of the People's Republic of China, and analyzed the leading role of the Chinese working class and its political party, the Chinese Communist Party, in the construction of political power. He pointed out that the People's Republic of China has made great achievements in political, military, economic, and cultural construction in the two years since its founding. The Chinese people have defeated the aggression of American imperialism, eliminated counter-revolutionaries on the mainland, basically completed the land reform nationwide, and industrial and agricultural production has approached or exceeded pre-war levels, and the people's material and cultural life has been significantly improved. These all prove the superiority of the people's democratic dictatorship in China. Minister Xie Juezai's report was welcomed by the foreign guests.

### 郭沫若和谢觉哉应邀向外宾作报告  分别介绍我国和平运动和政权建设情况

1952-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若和谢觉哉应邀向外宾作报告
    分别介绍我国和平运动和政权建设情况
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若七日上午应邀向前来我国参加“五一”节观礼和参观的外宾作关于中国和平运动的报告。出席的有罗马尼亚工会代表、丹麦“五一”观礼代表、瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克、越南、锡兰工会代表、英国工人代表、澳大利亚、朝鲜、蒙古人民共和国、缅甸、阿尔及利亚的工会代表。出席的还有：来北京参加阿维森纳、达·芬奇、雨果、果戈理纪念会的法国代表法奇及夫人、罗阿，意大利代表班菲、潘齐尼、德·桑蒂，智利画家何塞·万徒勒里及夫人，出席国际经济会议之后来我国参观的澳大利亚、英国、印尼、缅甸、印度等国的代表。郭沫若在报告中介绍了中国人民保卫世界和平运动，特别是自从美帝国主义发动侵略朝鲜的战争和侵略我国领土台湾以来，在全国广泛深入展开的抗美援朝运动的情况，以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的当前主要工作。郭沫若的报告获得了外宾的热烈掌声。
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府内务部部长谢觉哉，应邀在六日上午向前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的外国工会代表，以及来北京参加四大文化名人纪念大会的外国代表作了关于中华人民共和国政权问题的报告。到会的有：苏联工会代表，丹麦“五一”节观礼代表，瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、越南、锡兰工会代表，英国工人代表，印度、澳大利亚、朝鲜、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰、缅甸、伊朗和阿尔及利亚工会代表，参加四大名人纪念大会的法国和意大利代表法奇、罗阿、班菲、潘齐尼、德·桑蒂。来我国访问的智利画家何塞·万徒勒里也应邀到会。谢觉哉部长在报告中讲述了中华人民共和国的政权性质、任务和组织形式，并分析了中国工人阶级及其政党——中国共产党在政权建设中的领导作用。他指出：中华人民共和国成立两年多来在政治、军事、经济、文化建设等方面都获得了巨大的成绩。中国人民已击败了美帝国主义的侵略，在大陆上已肃清了反革命分子，全国土地改革已基本上完成，工农业生产已接近或超过了战前的水平，人民的物质文化生活已有了显著的提高。这些都证明了中国人民民主专政的政权制度的优越性。谢觉哉部长的报告受到了外宾的欢迎。
