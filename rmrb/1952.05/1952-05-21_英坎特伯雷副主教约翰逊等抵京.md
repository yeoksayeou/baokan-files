Title: Dean Johnson of Canterbury and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 1

    Dean Johnson of Canterbury and Others Arrive in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 20th] Dean Johnson of Canterbury and his wife, and Mr. Dey, a lecturer on workers' education at Oxford University, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General, Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; He Chengxiang, a religious worker; Wang Zizhong, General Secretary of the North China Christian Joint Association, and many others. (Attached picture)
    Johnson (fourth from right) and his wife, Dey (first from left), Guo Moruo (second from right), and others at the airport.
                (Photo by Liu Dongao, Xinhua News Agency)

### 英坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等抵京

1952-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    英坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等抵京
    【新华社二十日讯】英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊及夫人、牛津大学工人教育问题讲师戴伊，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，在二十日下午搭飞机抵达北京参观。前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，秘书长刘贯一，联络部副部长孟雨；中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若；宗教工作者何成湘；华北基督教联合会总干事王梓仲等多人。（附图片）
    约翰逊（右起第四人）及其夫人与戴伊（左第一人）和郭沫若（右第二人）等在机场合影。
                （新华社记者刘东鳌摄）
