Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and Korean People's Visiting Delegation Departed Beijing Yesterday to Return to Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-05-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visiting Delegation left Beijing on the afternoon of the 25th to return to Korea. Representatives from all walks of life in Beijing, including Zhang Xiruo, Xiao Hua, and Zeng Zhaolun, Wu Han, and more than 800 others, went to the station to see them off. Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, also went to the station to see them off.
    The two delegations arrived in Beijing on January 18th this year. After giving several reports to people from all walks of life in Beijing, they went to various parts of the country to visit and report. Over the past four months, the two delegations have made a total of 5,589 reports of various sizes across the country, organized 335 radio broadcasts, produced 474 written propaganda pieces, and held exhibitions of anti-US and aid-Korea materials and pictures in 31 cities, giving the broad masses of people a profound education in patriotism, internationalism, and revolutionary heroism, and promoting the Three-Anti and Five-Anti Movements that were being vigorously carried out across the country at that time. The two delegations also extensively consoled the families of revolutionary martyrs, families of revolutionary soldiers, and returned Chinese People's Volunteer Army wounded and sick soldiers recuperating in various parts of the country.

### 志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团昨天离北京返朝鲜

1952-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团昨天离北京返朝鲜
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团在二十五日下午离开北京返回朝鲜。到车站欢送的有北京各界人民代表张奚若、萧华、曾昭抡、吴晗等八百多人。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使权五稷也到站欢送。
    两代表团在今年一月十八日来到北京，向北京各界人民进行多次报告后即分赴全国各地进行访问和报告。四个多月来，两代表团在全国各地共作了五千五百八十九次大小报告，组织了三百三十五次广播会，作了四百七十四篇文字宣传，并在三十一个城市中举行了抗美援朝实物和图片展览，使广大人民受到了爱国主义、国际主义和革命英雄主义的深刻教育，推动了当时在全国各地热烈展开的三反运动和五反运动。两代表团并在全国各地广泛地慰问了革命烈士家属、革命军人家属和归国休养的中国人民志愿军伤病员。
