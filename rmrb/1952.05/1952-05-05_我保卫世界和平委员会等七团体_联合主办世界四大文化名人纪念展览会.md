Title: Seven Organizations Including the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Jointly Sponsor an Exhibition Commemorating the World's Four Great Cultural Figures
Author:
Date: 1952-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The "Exhibition Commemorating the Millennium of Avicenna's Birth, the Quincentennial of Leonardo da Vinci's Birth, the Sesquicentennial of Victor Hugo's Birth, and the Centennial of Nikolai Gogol's Death," jointly sponsored by seven organizations, including the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, the Red Cross Society of China, and the Chinese Medical Association, opened this morning at the National Beijing Library. Foreign guests invited to China to attend the commemorative meeting for the world's four great cultural figures, including Banfi, Mrs. Faggi, Mr. and Mrs. Roa, Pancini, and De Sanctis, as well as Chilean painter José Venturelli and his wife, who were invited to visit China, attended the event. Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Secretary Sha Kefu, Li Siguang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and writers Shu Qun, Feng Zhi, Wang Yeqiu, and others accompanied the foreign guests on a tour. The foreign guests highly praised the richness of the exhibits and the detailed explanations.

The exhibition displays 122 pictures and more than 300 kinds of books and periodicals in Chinese, French, English, Japanese, and other languages, introducing the life of Avicenna, Leonardo da Vinci, Victor Hugo, and Nikolai Gogol, and their brilliant contributions to human culture. In the picture section, you can see Avicenna's portrait; color reproductions of Leonardo da Vinci's immortal masterpieces "The Last Supper" and "Mona Lisa"; Victor Hugo's manuscripts; and early stage photos of Chinese drama workers performing Gogol's famous plays, etc. A collection of Leonardo da Vinci's paintings, donated by Italian philosophy professor Banfi, is also on display in a special cabinet. In the painting and periodical section, you can see various Chinese translations of works by Victor Hugo and Nikolai Gogol; some of these works had translations as early as forty or fifty years ago. Mr. Lu Xun translated and introduced Gogol's works very early on, and the exhibition specially displays his manuscript of the translation of "Dead Souls." The exhibition fully demonstrates the Chinese people's emphasis on and cherishment of human cultural heritage.

### 我保卫世界和平委员会等七团体  联合主办世界四大文化名人纪念展览会

1952-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会等七团体
    联合主办世界四大文化名人纪念展览会
    【新华社四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中国红十字会总会、中华医学会总会等七团体联合主办的“阿维森纳诞生一千周年、达·芬奇诞生五百周年、雨果诞生一百五十周年、果戈理逝世一百周年纪念展览会”于四日上午在此间国立北京图书馆开幕。应邀来中国参加世界四大文化名人纪念大会的外宾班菲、法奇夫人、罗阿和夫人、潘齐尼、德·桑蒂，及应邀在中国访问的智利画家何塞·万徒勒里和夫人都前往参加。中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会副主席茅盾、秘书长沙可夫，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席李四光及作家舒群、冯至、王冶秋等人，陪同外宾参观。外宾对展览会陈列品之丰富、说明之详尽，极为赞赏。
    展览会陈列图片一百二十二幅和中、法、英、日等国文字的书籍报刊三百余种，分别介绍阿维森纳、达·芬奇、雨果与果戈理的生平和他们对人类文化的辉煌贡献。在图片部分，可以看到阿维森纳的画像；达·芬奇艺术的不朽杰作“最后的晚餐”、“蒙娜丽莎”的彩色复制品；雨果的诗稿；我国戏剧工作者早年演出果戈理名剧的戏照等等。意大利哲学教授班菲赠送的达·芬奇画选一套，也在此设专柜展览。在画刊部分，可以看到雨果、果戈理作品的各种不同版本的中文译作；其中有的作品在距今四五十年前就有了译文。鲁迅先生很早就翻译介绍果戈理的作品，展览会特陈列了他的“死魂灵”译文的手稿。展览会充分显示了我国人民对人类文化遗产的重视和珍爱。
