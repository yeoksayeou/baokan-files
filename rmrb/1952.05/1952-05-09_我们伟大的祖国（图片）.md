    Our Great Motherland
    Improving fish farming techniques and developing freshwater fish farming in a planned way are ways to increase aquatic products. There are many swamps in the lower reaches of the Minjiang River in Fujian, which are good places for freshwater fish farming. This is a picture of farmers in Pandun Township, Minhou, Fujian, releasing fish fry into ponds.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    提高养鱼技术，有计划地发展淡水养鱼事业，是增加水产的途径之一。福建闽江下游有许多沼泽，是淡水养鱼的好地方。这是福建闽侯潘墩乡的农民，他们正在把鱼苗放到池塘里去。
