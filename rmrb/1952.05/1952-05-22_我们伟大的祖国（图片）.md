    Our Great Motherland
    Using new-style animal-drawn farm implements can save a lot of manpower. This is an animal-drawn hay rake, eight feet wide, equipped with thirty-two spring-loaded tines. Pulled by one animal, it can rake eighty mu of grass per day, saving fifteen times the labor compared to manual raking. This type of rake is most suitable for use in grasslands and pastoral areas.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    使用新式马拉农具可以节省大量人力。这是马拉搂草机，宽八呎，装有弹簧齿爪三十二个，用一头牲口拉动，每天能搂草八十亩，比人力省时十五倍。这种搂草机在草原和畜牧地区使用最为适宜。
