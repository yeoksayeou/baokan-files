Title: Presidium of the People’s Great Hural of the Mongolian People’s Republic Decides to Appoint Tsedenbal as Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 4

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 28th】 TASS Ulaanbaatar, 27th: The Presidium of the People's Great Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic has decided to appoint Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic. Tsedenbal is the General Secretary of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. Prior to this appointment, he served as Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团决定  任命泽登巴尔为部长会议主席

1952-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团决定
    任命泽登巴尔为部长会议主席
　  【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托二十七日讯：蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团决定任命尤姆热金·泽登巴尔为蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席。泽登巴尔是蒙古人民革命党总书记。在这次任命前，他担任着蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席的职务。
