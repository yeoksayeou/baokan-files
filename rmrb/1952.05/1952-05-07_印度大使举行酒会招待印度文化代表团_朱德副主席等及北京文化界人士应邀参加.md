Title: Indian Ambassador Holds Reception for Indian Cultural Delegation; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Beijing Cultural Figures Invited to Attend

Author:

Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 6th. Indian Ambassador to China, K.M. Panikkar, held a reception on the evening of the 6th to welcome the Indian Cultural Delegation.

Invited to the reception were also Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government Huang Yanpei, Minister of Trade of the Central People's Government Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Zhou Yang and Ding Xilin, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Education Wei Que and Zeng Zhaolun, other responsible persons from various ministries of the Central People's Government, and figures from Beijing's cultural circles: Ma Yinchu, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Xing Xiping, Yu Xinqing, Fan Changjiang, Chen Kehan, Chen Jiakang, Qian Duansheng, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Ouyang Yuqian, Sha Kefu, Hong Shen, Zheng Zhenduo, Li Bozhao, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, totaling over two hundred people.

Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian, and Deputy Head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to India and Burma Li Yimang, were also invited to attend the reception.

Also attending the reception were: Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko, Hungarian Ambassador to China Szafrankó, Bulgarian Ambassador to China Petkov, Burmese Ambassador to China U Myint Thein, Pakistani Ambassador to China Raza, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Mejstrup, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation Brunstand, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Gura, as well as Dutch government negotiation representative Van der Puyl, and Norwegian government negotiation representative Koht, and others. The Burmese Cultural Delegation was also invited to attend the reception. The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 印度大使举行酒会招待印度文化代表团  朱德副主席等及北京文化界人士应邀参加

1952-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    印度大使举行酒会招待印度文化代表团
    朱德副主席等及北京文化界人士应邀参加
    【新华社六日讯】印度驻华大使潘尼迦在六日晚举行酒会，招待印度文化代表团。
    应邀参加招待会的还有我中央人民政府副主席朱德，中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培，中央人民政府贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长周扬、丁西林，卫生部部长李德全，外交部副部长章汉夫，教育部副部长韦悫、曾昭抡，中央人民政府各部其他负责人及北京文化界人士：马寅初、蔡廷锴、张奚若、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、李四光、陶孟和、邢西萍、余心清、范长江、陈克寒、陈家康、钱端升、许广平、许德珩、欧阳予倩、沙可夫、洪深、郑振铎、李伯钊以及北京市副市长吴晗等共二百余人。
    我国驻印度大使袁仲贤以及我国前赴印缅文化代表团副团长李一氓也应邀参加招待会。
    参加招待会的还有：苏联驻华大使罗申、罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科、匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫、缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、越南代表团临时代办周亮、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特、波兰大使馆临时代办古拉，以及荷兰政府谈判代表范德普、挪威政府谈判代表高兰等。缅甸文化代表团也应邀参加了招待会。招待会在友好的空气中进行。
