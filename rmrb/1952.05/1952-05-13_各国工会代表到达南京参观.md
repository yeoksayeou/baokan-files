Title: Labor Union Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Nanjing for Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th. More than 120 representatives from the World Federation of Trade Unions and labor union delegations from various countries, who came to our country to attend the May 1st International Labor Day celebrations, arrived in Nanjing on the afternoon of the 12th for a visit, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China, and Xu Zhizhen, Minister of Organization of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Upon alighting from the train, the representatives were presented with flowers by representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing. The representatives paid their respects at the Yuhuatai Martyrs' Cemetery that afternoon, and attended a grand welcome reception by people from all walks of life in the evening.

### 各国工会代表到达南京参观

1952-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表
    到达南京参观
    【新华社南京十二日电】前来我国参加五一国际劳动节观礼的世界工联和各国工会代表团代表一百二十多人，十二日下午由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏和中华全国总工会组织部长许之桢陪同来南京参观。代表们下车后接受南京市各界代表献花。代表们当日下午凭吊雨花台烈士陵墓，晚间出席各界人民的盛大欢迎晚会。
