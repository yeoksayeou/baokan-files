Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Visits Northeast China and Tianjin; Chakraborty and Others Leave Xi'an for Luoyang
Author:
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 21st: After arriving in Shenyang on the 17th, the Indian Cultural Delegation has been visiting Shenyang, Fushun, and other places for several days. On the 18th, the delegation visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall and the China Medical University. The delegates viewed various industrial products at the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, as well as active models demonstrating the production processes of steel, cement, coal, and other materials. They were particularly interested in the cotton, hemp, silk, and other textiles displayed at the exhibition hall. At the China Medical University, the delegation attended a welcoming ceremony with over 1,200 faculty members and students, and visited the university's library, laboratories, and various instruments and models.

On the 19th, the delegation went to Fushun to visit the Longfeng Mine shaft coal mining equipment, the mine workers' canteen, workers' sanatorium, workers' dormitory, Fushun Municipal Workers' Sanatorium, open-pit mine, oil refinery, etc.

On the 20th, the delegation visited rural areas in the suburbs of Shenyang and the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant. In the afternoon of the same day, the head of the delegation, Mrs. Pandit, paid a visit to Gao Gang, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government.

During their visit to the Northeast, the delegation attended two welcoming banquets hosted by the Cultural Department of the Northeast People's Government, as well as the Northeast People's Government and the Shenyang Municipal People's Government. Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and Liu Zhiming, Minister of Culture, both delivered welcoming speeches at the banquets. The delegation also attended two film receptions and a song and dance evening, and Ms. Rau Shanda, a member of the delegation, performed a dance at the song and dance evening.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 21st: The Indian Cultural Delegation, led by its head Mrs. Pandit and accompanied by Chen Shuliang, Commissioner of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, arrived in Tianjin on the 21st for a visit. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Huang Jing, Fang Ji, Liang Hanbing, Yang Shixian, Li Jiye, Meng Qiujiang, and others. In the evening, Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, and Zhou Shutao, Deputy Mayor, held a reception to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 21st: Dr. Chakraborty, Professor Shi Jueyue, and Professor Peng De, members of the Indian Cultural Delegation who came to Xi'an from Beijing for a visit, left Xi'an on the 20th. Those who went to the railway station to see them off included: Zhu Maoqing, Director of the Cultural and Educational Bureau of the Xi'an Municipal People's Government, Liu Shangda, Director of the Xi'an Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, writer Zheng Boqi, historian Wu Bolun, painter Zhao Wangyun, and artist Chang Shuhong, among others. During their stay in Xi'an, Chakraborty and others visited the Huaqing Pool in Lintong, the Xingjiao Temple forty li south of Xi'an, the Stele Forest, the Great Wild Goose Pagoda, and the Small Wild Goose Pagoda, as well as attended a welcoming banquet hosted by Fang Zhongru, Mayor of the Xi'an Municipal People's Government.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, May 21st: Chakraborty, Shi Jueyue, and Peng De of the Indian Cultural Delegation arrived in Luoyang on the 20th for a visit. Ji Wenfu, Director of the Cultural Relics Preservation Committee of the Henan Provincial People's Government, and Li Ximi, Director of the Luoyang Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, among others, went to the station to welcome them. On the 21st, Chakraborty and others visited the Longmen Grottoes, a famous historical site in China, where Buddhist statues were carved during the Northern Wei Dynasty and subsequent dynasties, and also visited the nearby Guan Yu's Tomb. They left Luoyang by train that evening.

### 印度文化代表团访东北、天津  查克拉瓦底等离西安到洛阳

1952-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团访东北、天津
    查克拉瓦底等离西安到洛阳
    【新华社沈阳二十一日电】印度文化代表团在十七日抵达沈阳后，连日在沈阳、抚顺等地参观访问。十八日，代表团参观了东北工业陈列馆和中国医科大学。代表们在东北工业陈列馆参观了各种工业产品，以及表示钢铁、水泥、煤等生产过程的活动模型，他们对于陈列馆中的棉、麻、丝等纺织品特别感到兴趣。在中国医科大学，代表团出席了该校师生一千二百余人的欢迎会，参观了该校图书馆、实验室及各种仪器和模型。
    十九日，代表团前往抚顺参观了龙凤矿竖井采煤设备及该矿工人食堂、工人业余休养所、工人宿舍，抚顺市工人疗养院，露天矿，制油厂等。
    二十日，代表团参观了沈阳市郊农村和沈阳第一机器厂。同日下午，代表团团长潘迪特夫人拜访了东北人民政府主席高岗。
    代表团在东北访问期间，曾出席东北人民政府文化部以及东北人民政府、沈阳市人民政府的两次招待宴会。东北人民政府副主席林枫、文化部部长刘芝明都在宴会上致词表示欢迎。代表团还出席了两次电影招待会和一次歌舞晚会，代表团团员劳·姗达女士并在歌舞晚会上表演了舞蹈节目。
    【新华社天津二十一日电】印度文化代表团在团长潘迪特夫人率领下，在二十一日由我中央人民政府外交部专员陈叔亮陪同抵达天津参观。前往车站欢迎的有：黄敬、方纪、梁寒冰、杨石先、李霁野、孟秋江等。晚上，天津市人民政府市长黄敬、副市长周叔弢举行酒会欢迎代表团。
    【新华社西安二十一日电】由北京来西安参观的印度文化代表团团员查克拉瓦底博士、师觉月教授和彭德教授三人已于二十日离开西安。前往火车站欢送的有：西安市人民政府文教局局长朱茂青、西安市文学艺术界联合会主任刘尚达、作家郑伯奇、历史学家武伯伦、画家赵望云及艺术家常书鸿等。查克拉瓦底等在西安期间，曾访问临潼华清池、西安城南四十里的兴教寺、碑林以及大雁塔、小雁塔等著名的名胜古迹，并出席了西安市人民政府市长方仲如的欢迎宴会。
    【新华社开封二十一日电】印度文化代表团查克拉瓦底、师觉月、彭德二十日到洛阳参观。河南省人民政府文物保管委员会主任委员嵇文甫，洛阳市文学艺术界联合会主任李希泌等前往车站欢迎。二十一日查克拉瓦底等参观了我国著名历史古迹，龙门伊阙、北魏及其后各代刻造佛像，并参观了附近的关帝塚，当日晚乘车离洛。
