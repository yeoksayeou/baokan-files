Title: Shen Junru and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 4

　　Shen Junru and Others Return to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Shen Junru, Vice-Chairman of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, and Li Ke, member of the council, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th after attending the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Council meeting held in Vienna in mid-April. Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Shi Liang, Zhang Zhizhi, Wu Gaizhi, Hu Yuzhi, Wu Han, Fan Changjiang, Yan Baohang, Min Ganghou, Dong Yueqian, Liu Guanyi, Wu Maosun, and many others, representing legal workers and people's organizations in the capital.

### 沈钧儒等返抵北京

1952-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　沈钧儒等返抵北京
    【新华社三十日讯】国际民主法律工作者协会副主席沈钧儒、理事柯柏年等，在出席了四月中旬在维也纳举行的国际民主法律工作者协会理事会会议之后，已于二十九日下午乘飞机返抵北京。到机场欢迎的有首都法律工作者和人民团体的代表史良、张志让、吴溉之、胡愈之、吴晗、范长江、阎宝航、闵刚侯、董越千、刘贯一、吴茂荪等多人。
