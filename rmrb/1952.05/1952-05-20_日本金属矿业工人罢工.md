Title: Japanese Metal Mine Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 18th: 43,000 workers from Japan's five largest metal mining companies held a 24-hour strike on the 16th to demand wage increases. If the capitalists persist in refusing the workers' demands, the workers will hold another 48-hour strike on the 22nd and 23rd. Workers from other small and medium-sized metal mining companies are also planning to hold a 24-hour strike on the 22nd.

### 日本金属矿业工人罢工

1952-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本金属矿业工人罢工
    【新华社十八日讯】东京消息：日本五个最大的金属矿业公司的四万三千名工人，为了要求增加工资，在十六日举行了二十四小时的罢工。如果资方坚持拒绝工人的要求，工人将在二十二日和二十三日再举行一次四十八小时的罢工。其他中小金属矿业公司的工人也计划在二十二日举行二十四小时的罢工。
