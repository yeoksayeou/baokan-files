Title: Telegram of Greetings from Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, to the Congress
Author: Nikolaĭ Tikhonov
Date: 1952-05-05
Page: 1

    To the Beijing China People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace,
    Dear Comrade Guo Moruo:
    We sincerely thank you for inviting us to China to participate in the great commemoration of cultural celebrities. Please convey our warm congratulations to all participants of the commemoration and all peace defenders in China. The Chinese people are commemorating the great writers Nikolai Vasilievich Gogol, Victor Hugo, the brilliant painter Leonardo da Vinci, and the outstanding scientist and thinker of the Eastern people, Avicenna, together with the people of the world. Their works belong to all progressive humanity.
    May the commemoration held in Beijing for them be a new manifestation representing the peace forces of the world to promote closer friendship and cultural exchange among mankind.
    Soviet Peace Committee
    Nikolai Tikhonov, May 3rd

### 苏联拥护和平委员会主席  吉洪诺夫给大会的贺电
尼古拉·吉洪诺夫
1952-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联拥护和平委员会主席
    吉洪诺夫给大会的贺电
    北京中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    亲爱的郭沫若同志：
    我们衷心地感谢您邀请我们前往中国参加伟大的文化名人纪念会。我们请您代我们向全体参加纪念会以及中国所有的保卫和平的人致以热忱的祝贺。中国人民是与全世界人民一起来纪念伟大的作家尼古拉·华西里耶维赤·果戈理、维克多·雨果、辉煌的画家辽奥纳多·达·芬奇和东方人民杰出的科学家与思想家阿维森纳的。他们的作品属于整个进步的人类。
    愿在北京为他们而举行的纪念会，是代表全世界和平力量以促进人类更密切的友谊和文化交流的新表现。
    苏联拥护和平委员会
    尼古拉·吉洪诺夫  五月三日
