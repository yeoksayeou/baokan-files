    Our Great Motherland
    The Erlongshan Reservoir on the Dongliao River is one of the four major reservoirs in Northeast China. Under its direct control, 150,000 hectares of land were spared from flooding last year. Its storage capacity is sufficient to irrigate 30,000 hectares of rice paddies. This is the scene of the reservoir discharging water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东辽河二龙山水库是我国东北四大水库之一。在它的直接控制下，去年有十五万垧土地免于水灾。它的蓄水量足够灌溉三万垧稻田。这是水库正在放水的情形。
