Title: Seven Chinese People's Organizations Hold Receptions for Days to Entertain Foreign Guests Attending the National Day Observance and Visiting China
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd - The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All China Federation of Trade Unions , the All- China Federation of Democratic Youth , the All-China Women's Democratic Federation, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies jointly held a music and dance soiree on the evening of the 2nd to entertain foreign guests who came to China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visits. More than 700 people attended.
    Invited attendees included: Renowned French and Italian figures Facci and his wife, Roa and his wife, Banfi, Panzini, and De Santi, who came to Beijing to attend the anniversaries of Avicenna, Da Vinci, Hugo, and Gogol; World Federation of Trade Unions representatives Kaulibanov and Sandton; Trade union delegations from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Vietnam, India, and Sweden; as well as the Danish "May Day" Observation Delegation, trade union delegations from Burma and Australia, a British workers' delegation, Iranian trade union representatives, and an Algerian trade union delegation.
    Also invited were representatives from Vietnam, India, Burma, Australia, Brazil, England, Argentina, Ceylon, France, and Mexico who came to China to visit after attending the International Economic Conference.
    Also invited were: Some members of the Hungarian National Folk Ensemble who came to China to perform, and the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Kobetsky. Chilean painter José Venturelli, who came to China for a visit, was also invited to attend.
    More than 40 representatives of labor models from major administrative regions across the country who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations were also invited.
    Responsible persons from the above-mentioned seven people's organizations attended as hosts.
    More than ten programs were performed at the soiree, and each program received warm applause from the audience.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Women's Democratic Federation, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies held a soiree on the evening of the 3rd, featuring Peking Opera performed by Mei Lanfang, to entertain foreign guests who came to China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visits. More than 800 people attended.
    Invited attendees included: Representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions, trade union delegations from the Soviet Union and Romania, the Danish "May Day" Observation Delegation, trade union delegations from Sweden, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, and Vietnam, a British workers' delegation, various Indian trade union delegations who came to China one after another, trade union delegations from Australia, North Korea, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, and Burma, Iranian trade union representatives, and an Algerian trade union delegation.
    Also invited were: The Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Kobetsky; French and Italian celebrities Facci and his wife, Roa and his wife, Banfi, Panzini, and De Santi, who came to Beijing to attend the commemorative meetings of the four great cultural figures, Avicenna, Da Vinci, Hugo, and Gogol; Chilean painter Venturelli and his wife; and representatives from Australia, England, Indonesia, Burma, France, India, and Vietnam who came to China to visit after attending the International Economic Conference.
    The Hungarian National Folk Ensemble, which came to China to perform, was also invited to participate.
    Also invited were more than 50 representatives of labor models from major administrative regions across the country who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and more than 30 members of the Burmese Overseas Chinese Returnees Sightseeing Delegation.
    Responsible persons from the seven people's organizations attended the soiree to entertain the guests.
    The soiree's program was wonderful, and at the end, the foreign guests gave enthusiastic applause.

### 我国七个人民团体连日举行晚会  招待来我国观礼和参观的外宾

1952-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我国七个人民团体连日举行晚会
    招待来我国观礼和参观的外宾
    【新华社二日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会七团体，在二日晚联合举行音乐舞蹈晚会，招待来中国参加“五一”节观礼和参观的外宾。出席的有七百多人。
    应邀参加的有：来北京出席阿维森纳、达·芬奇、雨果、果戈理周年纪念大会的法国和意大利的知名人士法奇和夫人、罗阿和夫人、班菲、潘齐尼和德·桑蒂；世界工会联合会代表考里班诺夫和桑顿；苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、越南、印度、瑞典等国工会代表团，以及丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团、缅甸、澳大利亚工会代表团、英国工人代表团、伊朗工会代表和阿尔及利亚工会代表团等。
    应邀参加的还有出席国际经济会议后来我国参观的越南、印度、缅甸、澳大利亚、巴西、英国、阿根廷、锡兰、法国、墨西哥的代表。
    应邀出席的还有：来中国表演的匈牙利国家人民文工团一部分团员，以柯别茨基为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团。前来我国访问的智利画家何塞·万徒勒里也应邀出席。
    由全国各大行政区来北京参加“五一”节观礼的劳动模范的代表四十多人也应邀参加。
    出席作陪的有上述七个人民团体的负责人。
    晚会共演出了十多个节目，每个节目都获得全场热烈鼓掌。
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会七个人民团体，在三日晚举行晚会，由梅兰芳演出京剧，招待来我国参加“五一”节观礼和参观的外国来宾。出席的有八百多人。
    应邀出席的有：世界工会联合会代表，苏联、罗马尼亚的工会代表团，丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团，瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、越南的工会代表团，英国工人代表团，先后到中国来的印度的各批工会代表团，澳大利亚、朝鲜、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰、缅甸的工会代表团，伊朗工会代表和阿尔及利亚工会代表团等。
    应邀出席的还有：以柯别茨基为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团；来北京参加阿维森纳、达·芬奇、雨果、果戈理四大文化名人纪念大会的法国和意大利名流法奇夫妇、罗阿夫妇、班菲、潘齐尼、德·桑蒂；智利画家万徒勒里夫妇；参加国际经济会议之后来我国参观的澳大利亚、英国、印尼、缅甸、法国、印度、越南的代表。
    来中国表演的匈牙利国家人民文工团也应邀参加。
    应邀参加的还有由全国各大行政区来北京参加“五一”节观礼的劳动模范的代表五十人和缅甸华侨归国观光团团员三十多人。
    七个人民团体的负责人都出席晚会招待来宾。
    晚会节目精采，终场时获得外宾热烈的掌声。
