Title: China Peace Committee Invites Reporters to Visit Exhibition on U.S. Imperialist Germ Warfare Crimes
Author:
Date: 1952-05-12
Page: 1

    China People's Committee for World Peace
    Invites Reporters to Visit Exhibition on U.S. Imperialist Germ Warfare Crimes
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] The China People's Committee for World Peace invited Chinese and foreign reporters to visit the exhibition on U.S. imperialist germ warfare crimes organized by the committee on May 11th, and held a press conference. Prior to this, the following groups had visited the exhibition on U.S. imperialist germ warfare crimes: trade union delegations from various countries attending China's "May Day" celebrations, representatives from various countries attending China's commemoration of the world's four great cultural figures, representatives from various countries who came to China to visit after attending the International Economic Conference, as well as the Indian Cultural Delegation, the Burmese Cultural Delegation, and the Czechoslovak Government Delegation.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  邀记者参观美帝国主义细菌战罪行展览会

1952-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    邀记者参观美帝国主义细菌战罪行展览会
    【新华社十一日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会五月十一日邀请中外记者参观该会举办的美帝国主义细菌战罪行展览会，并举行了记者招待会。在这之前，参观过美帝国主义细菌战罪行展览会的，有：参加我国“五一”节观礼的各国工会代表团，参加我国纪念世界四大文化名人纪念会的各国代表，出席国际经济会议后来我国参观的各国代表及印度文化代表团、缅甸文化代表团和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团等。
