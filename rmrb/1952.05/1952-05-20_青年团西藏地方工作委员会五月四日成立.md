Title: Communist Youth League of China Tibet Regional Work Committee Established on May 4th
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 19th: Youths of all nationalities in Lhasa held a meeting on May 4th to warmly celebrate the "May Fourth" Youth Day and the establishment of the Communist Youth League of China Tibet Regional Work Committee. On that day, the youths, wearing new clothes, arrived at the meeting place very early. Central People's Government representative General Zhang Jingwu, Tibet Military Region Commander General Zhang Guohua, First Deputy Commander Ngabo Ngawang Jigme, and others attended the meeting to provide guidance.

At the meeting, General Zhang Jingwu, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, solemnly announced the establishment of the Communist Youth League of China Tibet Regional Work Committee. Youth representatives of all nationalities presented Hada scarves to express their congratulations.

General Zhang Jingwu then reported on the historical significance of the "May Fourth" Movement. He called on youths of all nationalities in Tibet to unite closely, study hard, enhance their revolutionary understanding, resolutely oppose imperialism, and become outstanding cadres for building a new Tibet. Ngabo Ngawang Jigme called on youths of all nationalities in Tibet to study well the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet and resolutely drive out the imperialist forces of aggression from Tibet. Tibetan youth representative Sangpo Tse said in his speech: "Today, an organization that leads and educates us has been established. We must unite closely around it and, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, build our homeland into a happy paradise."

Amid warm applause, the congress passed congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao, the Chinese People's Volunteers, and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China. The whole audience sang songs such as "The East is Red," "The Battle Song of the Chinese People's Volunteers," and "Unity is Strength" in different languages, and shouted "Long Live Chairman Mao." In the evening, youths of all nationalities held a gala evening.

### 青年团西藏地方工作委员会五月四日成立

1952-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团西藏地方工作委员会五月四日成立
    【新华社拉萨十九日电】拉萨市各民族青年在五月四日开会热烈庆祝“五四”青年节和中国新民主主义青年团西藏地方工作委员会成立。这天，青年们穿着新衣很早就赶到会场。中央人民政府代表张经武将军和西藏军区司令员张国华将军、第一副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美等都到会指导。
    会上，张经武将军代表中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会庄严宣布中国新民主主义青年团西藏地方工作委员会成立。各民族青年代表纷纷敬献哈达祝贺。
    张经武将军接着报告了“五四”运动的历史意义，他号召西藏各民族青年紧密团结起来，努力学习，提高革命认识，坚决反对帝国主义，成为建设新西藏的优秀干部。阿沛·阿旺晋美号召西藏各民族青年好好学习关于和平解放西藏办法的协议，坚决驱逐帝国主义侵略势力出西藏。藏族青年代表桑颇色在讲话中说：“今天成立了领导和教育我们的组织，我们一定要紧紧地团结在它的周围，在毛主席的英明领导下，把我们的家乡建设成幸福的乐园。”
    在热烈掌声中大会通过给毛主席、中国人民志愿军、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的致敬电。全场用不同语言高唱“东方红”、“中国人民志愿军战歌”和“团结就是力量”等歌曲，高呼“毛主席万岁”。晚间，各民族青年并举行游艺晚会。
