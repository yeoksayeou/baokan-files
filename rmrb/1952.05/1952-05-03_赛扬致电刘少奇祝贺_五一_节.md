Title: Saillant Sends Telegram to Liu Shaoqi to Congratulate Labor Day
Author: Lu Yi Saillant
Date: 1952-05-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram to Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, to congratulate him on Labor Day. The original telegram is as follows: Comrade Liu Shaoqi:
    On this Labor Day, we are pleased to extend fraternal greetings to you and all comrades. The great successes achieved by the Chinese people in the construction of New China herald prosperity and peace. Workers all over the world stand in solidarity with the Chinese people in the struggle against the imperialist savages who cannot prevent your progress.
    Best wishes,
    Louis Saillant, in Vienna

### 赛扬致电刘少奇祝贺“五一”节
路易·赛扬
1952-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    赛扬致电刘少奇祝贺“五一”节
    【新华社二日讯】世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬，致电中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇，祝贺“五一”节。原电如下：刘少奇同志：
    值此“五一”节，我们怀着愉快的心情向你以及全体同志致兄弟的敬礼。中国人民在新中国建设事业中所获得的伟大成功预告着繁荣与和平。在反对那无法阻挠你们前进的帝国主义生番的斗争中，全世界的工人是和中国人民团结一致的。
    谨致最好的愿望
    路易·赛扬  于维也纳
