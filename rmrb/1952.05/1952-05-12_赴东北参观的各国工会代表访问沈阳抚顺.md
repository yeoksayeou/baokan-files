Title: Visiting Shenyang and Fushun: Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries on a Visit to Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1952-05-12
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 9th) At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, a group of over 100 trade union delegations and trade union representatives from various countries who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, visited Shenyang on the morning of the 8th, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Traveling on the same train to Shenyang were also four representatives from India, Burma, and Indonesia who came to China for a visit after attending the Moscow International Economic Conference. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Zhiming, Vice-Chairman of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang; model workers Jiang Wanshou and Chu Hongyi, as well as heads of various departments of the Northeast and Shenyang Federations of Trade Unions, chairmen of various industrial trade unions, representatives of various people's organizations, and more than 200 others. After the representatives got off the train, they shook hands cordially with the welcomers and accepted flowers. In the afternoon of the same day, the representatives visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall. In the evening, the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions and the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions jointly hosted a banquet to entertain the representatives from various countries. Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a short welcome speech at the banquet. After the dinner, a song and dance soiree was held to entertain the guests, with performances by the Northeast People's Art Theater. The representatives from various countries will visit factories, rural areas, and schools in Northeast China.

(Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 11th) Trade union delegations and trade union representatives from various countries who came to Northeast China for a visit, as well as representatives from India, Burma, Indonesia and other countries who came to China for a visit after attending the International Economic Conference, have been visiting Shenyang and Fushun for several days, holding discussions with Chinese workers.

On the afternoon of the 8th, the representatives from various countries visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall. In this six-story building displaying various industrial product models and production charts, they saw an overview of the development of industrial construction in Northeast China. They unanimously praised it as one of the best exhibitions they had ever seen. Representatives from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries were excited about the progress of their brother countries, believing that China's achievements in industrial construction are a great contribution to world peace. Representatives from Southeast Asian countries believed that this was "the fruit of liberation." They all expressed that this visit had greatly encouraged them.

On the 9th, the representatives went to Fushun, a famous coal industry center in China, and were warmly welcomed by the local workers. Zhang Zifu, a well-known national model worker and coal mining expert, accompanied the representatives on their visit despite his illness, and discussed production experiences in detail. The representatives visited the Longfeng Mine, the oil refinery, and the world-famous open-pit mine. Trade union representatives from Poland and India, and worker representatives from the United Kingdom met with Chinese coal miners in the Longfeng Mine pit. The representatives all praised when they heard that the city's workers' daily production value this year will be two and a half times higher than in the previous year. In addition, the representatives also visited the city's workers' spare-time sanatorium, workers' convalescent home, nursery, workers' dormitory, family dormitory, workers' canteen, workers' club and other welfare facilities.

On the morning of the 10th, all the representatives visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory. They saw the workers responding to the state's call for increased production and thrift, and working intensely on production in the factory's machining, casting, and assembly workshops, expressing their great admiration. They were greatly interested in the blackboard newspapers, patriotic covenants, and union announcement boards about workers' welfare measures in the workshops. They were excited about the development of the factory's production and the improvement of workers' lives, and repeatedly congratulated the factory's workers. Trade union representatives from Mongolia and Czechoslovakia also presented the factory's workers with commemorative badges and banners.

On the afternoon of the 10th, the representatives visited the Gao Kan Village Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Jiuzhan District suburbs of Shenyang, the construction site of the Northeast Institute of Technology, the China Medical University, and the scenic spot of the Prajna Temple in the city in groups. The coal mine workers and railway workers representatives among the representatives also attended symposiums hosted by the Northeast Coal Mine Trade Union and the Northeast Regional Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, respectively.

### 赴东北参观的各国工会代表访问沈阳抚顺

1952-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    赴东北参观的各国工会代表访问沈阳抚顺
    【新华社沈阳九日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的各国工会代表团和工会代表一行一百多人，于八日上午由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一陪同前来沈阳参观。同车到达沈阳的，还有出席莫斯科国际经济会议之后来我国参观的印度、缅甸、印尼三国代表四人。前往车站欢迎的有：东北总工会主席张维桢、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会东北总分会副主席刘芝明、沈阳市长朱其文、劳动模范姜万寿、初洪义及东北、沈阳两级总工会各部门负责人、各产业工会主席、各人民团体代表等二百多人。代表们下车后与欢迎者亲切地握手，并接受了献花。当天午后，代表们参观了东北工业陈列馆。晚上，东北总工会和沈阳市总工会联合设宴招待各国代表，东北总工会主席张维桢并在席上致简短的欢迎词。晚宴后，举行歌舞晚会招待嘉宾，由东北人民艺术剧院演出节目。各国代表将参观东北的工厂、农村和学校。
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】前来东北参观的各国工会代表团、工会代表，以及出席国际经济会议后来我国参观的印度、缅甸、印尼等国代表，连日访问沈阳、抚顺，和我国工人座谈。
    八日下午，各国代表参观东北工业陈列馆，他们在这座陈列着各种工业产品模型和生产图表的六层大楼上，看到了东北工业建设发展的概貌。他们一致称赞：这是他们所见过的最好的展览之一。苏联和各人民民主国家的代表为自己兄弟国家的进展而兴奋，他们认为：中国在工业建设上的成就，是对世界和平的巨大贡献。东南亚各国代表认为：这是“解放的果实”。他们一致表示：这次参观使他们受到很大鼓舞。
    九日，代表们前往我国著名煤业中心——抚顺参观，受到当地工人的热烈欢迎。著名的全国劳动模范、采煤能手张子富抱病陪伴代表们参观，详尽地交谈着生产经验。代表们参观了龙凤矿、制油厂和举世闻名的露天矿，波兰、印度工会代表和英国工人代表并在龙凤矿矿坑中与我国采煤工人会见。代表们听到该市工人今年每人每天的生产价值将比前年增加两倍半，都一致称赞。此外，代表们还参观了该市工人业余休养所、工人疗养院、托儿所、工人宿舍、家属宿舍、工人食堂、工人俱乐部等福利设施。
    十日上午，全体代表到沈阳第一机器厂参观，他们在该厂加工、翻砂、装配三个车间里，看到工人们正在响应着国家增产节约的号召、紧张地进行生产的情景，表示非常敬佩。他们对各车间的黑板报、爱国公约、以及工会揭示牌上关于工人福利措施的通知，感到极大兴趣。他们为该厂生产的发展和工人生活的改善而兴奋，一再向该厂工人表示祝贺。蒙古工会代表和捷克斯洛伐克工会代表并向该厂工人赠送了纪念章和锦旗。
    十日下午，代表们分组参观了沈阳市郊旧站分区高坎村农业生产合作社、东北工学院建筑工地、中国医科大学和市内般若寺名胜。代表中的煤矿工人和铁路工人代表，并分别出席了东北煤矿总工会和中国铁路工会东北地区委员会所主办的座谈会。
