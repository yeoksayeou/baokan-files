Title: Burmese Labor Union Representative, Thakim Lwin Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-11
Page: 1

    Burmese Labor Union Representative
    Thakim Lwin Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th] Thakim Lwin (Vice Chairman of the All Burma Trade Union Congress), the Burmese labor union representative invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th, having been delayed due to unforeseen circumstances. Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Xiuzhu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture and Education, and others went to the airport to welcome him.

### 缅甸工会代表  德钦卢挨抵达北京

1952-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表
    德钦卢挨抵达北京
    【新华社十日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼、因故迟到的缅甸工会代表德钦卢挨（全缅工会大会副主席）十日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温、文教部副部长张修竹等人。
