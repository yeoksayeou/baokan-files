Title: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to Return to Korea; Grand Farewell Rally Held by People from All Walks of Life in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1952-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: More than 6,000 people from all walks of life in the capital held a grand rally at 8 p.m. today to bid farewell to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation to Return to Korea and the Korean People's Delegation to China, who are about to return to Korea. Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and the embassy's counselor were also invited to attend. When the delegations entered the venue, the audience applauded warmly. After the meeting began, Zhang Xiruo, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and Chairman of the Beijing Branch, delivered a farewell speech on behalf of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and the Beijing Branch, representing the people of Beijing and all the Chinese people to express their condolences and gratitude to the delegations. He hoped that the delegates would convey to the Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army the Chinese people's high respect for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean people, as well as their sincere and determined support for the Korean-Chinese People's Army and the Korean people.

    Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation to Return to Korea, and Hong Chun-chol, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delivered speeches in reply (full text on page 3). They reported in detail the rich and vivid facts they had seen in various parts of the country regarding the Chinese people's movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea, the production increase movement in industry and agriculture, the great "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" movements, and the Chinese people's love for the Volunteer Army and the Korean people. They unanimously pointed out that the unparalleled and powerful strength of the great Chinese people is a guarantee for resisting the aggression of US imperialism and defending peace in the Far East and the world. Li Xuesan and Hong Chun-chol expressed that after returning to Korea, they would widely convey the report among the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the Korean People's Army, and the Korean people, mobilizing them to resist the American aggressors more bravely and stubbornly until final victory.

    Finally, the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea presented gifts to the two delegations, and the North China General Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and the Beijing Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea presented silk banners to the two delegations. The meeting ended at about 10 o'clock, followed by an evening party.

### 志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团将返朝鲜  首都各界举行盛大欢送会

1952-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团将返朝鲜
    首都各界举行盛大欢送会
    【新华社二十四日讯】首都各界人民六千余人，今日下午八时举行大会，欢送即将返回朝鲜的中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使权五稷及大使馆参赞也应邀参加。当代表团步入会场时，到会群众热烈鼓掌欢迎。大会开始后首先由中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员、北京市分会主席张奚若致欢送词，他代表中国人民抗美援朝总会和北京市分会，代表北京市各界人民，代表全中国人民向代表团表示慰问和感谢。他希望代表们把中国人民对中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民的崇高的敬意，以及对朝中人民军队和朝鲜人民全力支援的热诚和决心，告诉给朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团李雪三团长和朝鲜人民访华代表团洪淳哲团长相继致答词（全文见第三版），他们详尽地报告了两代表团在全国各地所看到的中国人民在抗美援朝运动、工业与农业的增产运动、伟大的“三反”和“五反”运动以及中国人民对志愿军和朝鲜人民的热爱等丰富生动的事实。他们一致指出：伟大的中国人民的无比强大的力量，是反抗美帝国主义的侵略、保卫远东和世界和平的保障。李雪三团长和洪淳哲团长表示回到朝鲜以后要在中国人民志愿军、朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民中广泛地传达报告，动员他们更勇敢、更顽强地反抗美国侵略者，直到取得最后胜利。
    最后，中国人民抗美援朝总会向两代表团赠送礼物，华北抗美援朝总分会、北京市抗美援朝分会各向两代表团献锦旗各一面。大会于十时许结束，会后并举行晚会。
