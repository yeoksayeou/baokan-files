Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 134th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: The 134th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 2nd. After listening to the explanation given by Xu Zirong, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the Ministry's key work points for April, May and June of 1952.

### 政务院举行第一三四次会议

1952-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一三四次会议
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十四次政务会议于二日下午举行，听取了中央人民政府公安部徐子荣副部长所作的说明后，批准了该部一九五二年四、五、六月份工作要点。
