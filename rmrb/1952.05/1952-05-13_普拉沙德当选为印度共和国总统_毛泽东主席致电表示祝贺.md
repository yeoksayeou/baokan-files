Title: Prasad Elected President of the Republic of India Chairman Mao Zedong Sends Congratulatory Message

Author:
Date: 1952-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram congratulating Rajendra Prasad on his election as President of the Republic of India. The telegram reads: To His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:

    I am pleased to learn of Your Excellency's election as President of the Republic of India, and I would like to extend my congratulations on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

    Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
    May 11, 1952

### 普拉沙德当选为印度共和国总统  毛泽东主席致电表示祝贺

1952-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    普拉沙德当选为印度共和国总统
    毛泽东主席致电表示祝贺
    【新华社十二日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席电贺印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德当选，电文如下：印度共和国拉金德拉·普拉沙德总统阁下：
    欣悉阁下当选为印度共和国总统，谨代表中华人民共和国政府及人民表示祝贺。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席    毛泽东
    一九五二年五月十一日
