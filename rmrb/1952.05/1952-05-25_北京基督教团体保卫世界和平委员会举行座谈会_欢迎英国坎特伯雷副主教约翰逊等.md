Title: Beijing Christian Organizations Committee for the Defense of World Peace Holds Symposium Welcoming British Canterbury Vice-Dean Johnson, et al.
Author:
Date: 1952-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: The Beijing Christian Organizations Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a symposium on the afternoon of the 24th to welcome the British Canterbury Vice-Dean Johnson and his wife, and Oxford University Lecturer Day, who are visiting China. More than 30 representatives from the Chinese Anglican Church, the North China Congregational Church, the Church of Christ in China, and other Christian organizations in the capital attended the meeting. At the meeting, Wang Zizhong, Chairman of the Beijing Christian Organizations Committee for the Defense of World Peace, delivered a speech, warmly welcoming Vice-Dean Johnson, who had visited China in 1932. Subsequently, Vice-Dean Johnson and his wife, and Day delivered speeches successively. Vice-Dean Johnson said: It is a great honor for me to be able to come to China again in my lifetime. Twenty years ago, after my visit to China, I described to the British people the unparalleled beauty of your country, its architecture and countryside, and the great tradition of your thousands of years of history. Now I am here and I see the most moving thing in the world—New China. Last time I saw the great material beauty of China, and now I see its spiritual and psychological beauty. In the area from Prague to Beijing, I saw a very large group of people, 800 to 900 million. Now the great task of you 800 million people and us British people and American people is to avoid the expansion of war. We are fighting for peace, and we will win peace.

He Chengxiang, a religious worker, and Zhao Fusan, Jiang Yizhen, Wang Zizhong, Pang Zhikun, Chen Wenrun, and other representatives of Christian organizations in Beijing, then spoke about the religious policy of the New China People's Government, the Three-Self Patriotic Reform Movement of Christianity, Christians' participation in patriotic and peace-keeping movements, and the activities of Christian churches and female Christians in Beijing. They also answered questions about Chinese religion raised by Vice-Dean Johnson.

### 北京基督教团体保卫世界和平委员会举行座谈会  欢迎英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等

1952-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    北京基督教团体保卫世界和平委员会举行座谈会
    欢迎英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等
    【新华社二十四日讯】北京基督教团体保卫世界和平委员会，在二十四日下午举行座谈会，欢迎前来我国访问的英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊和夫人、牛津大学讲师戴伊。到会的有中华圣公会、华北公理会、中华基督教会和首都其他基督教团体的代表三十多人。会上北京基督教团体保卫世界和平委员会主席王梓仲致词，他热烈欢迎曾在一九三二年访问过中国的约翰逊副主教。接着，约翰逊副主教和夫人、戴伊相继发表了演说。约翰逊副主教说：我能够再度到中国来，这是我一生中很大的光荣。二十年前我访问中国回去之后，曾向英国人民叙述你们国家的无比的美丽，它的建筑和农村，以及你们数千年历史的伟大传统。现在我到这里看到了世界上最动人的事情——新中国。我上次看到了中国伟大的物质上的美丽，而现在我又看到了它的精神上、心理上的美丽。在布拉格到北京这片地区，我看到了很大的一群人，八万万到九万万人。现在你们八万万人民和我们英国人民以及美国人民的伟大任务，就是避免战争的扩大。我们为和平而斗争，而且我们将会赢得和平。
    会上继由我国宗教工作者何成湘和北京市基督教团体代表赵复三、蒋翼振、王梓仲、庞之焜、陈文润等分别讲述了新中国人民政府的宗教政策、基督教三自革新运动、基督徒参加爱国运动和保卫和平运动、北京市基督教会和女基督徒的活动情况。他们并答复了约翰逊副主教所提出的有关中国宗教的问题。
