Author:
Date: 1952-05-18

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to enable farmers in Northeast China to use new-style animal-drawn farm implements universally within five or six years, the first batch of new-style animal-drawn farm implements manufactured by the Shenyang Farm Implement Factory this year has been delivered on schedule. Workers are installing seeders here, ready to be distributed to farmers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了使东北农民能够在五、六年内普遍使用新式马拉农具，沈阳农具工厂今年制造的第一批新式马拉农具已按时出厂。工人们正在这里安装播种机，准备发给农民。
