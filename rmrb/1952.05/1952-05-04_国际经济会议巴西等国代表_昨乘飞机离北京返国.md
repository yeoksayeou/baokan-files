Title: Representatives from Brazil and Other Countries to the International Economic Conference Depart Beijing by Plane to Return Home Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Brazilian representatives, Olivieri, Quemary, Martin, and Sequinho, the British representatives Slater, Horsley, McClelland, and Silver, the Mexican representative Otilio Sere, and the French representative LeBlanc and his secretary Harkutianba, a total of eleven people who attended the Moscow International Economic Conference and then visited our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 3rd to return to their countries. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Ji Chao-ding, member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xiang Shuxiang, member of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee, Sun Xiaocun, Standing Committee member of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce. Jiang Ming, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 国际经济会议巴西等国代表  昨乘飞机离北京返国

1952-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    国际经济会议巴西等国代表
    昨乘飞机离北京返国
    【新华社三日讯】出席莫斯科国际经济会议后来我国参观的巴西代表奥立维、奎马瑞、马丁、塞基诺，英国代表斯莱特尔、霍斯莱、麦克利兰、锡尔佛，墨西哥代表奥蒂洛·希里亚，法国代表勒布朗及其秘书哈库蒂安巴等一行十一人，于三日上午搭飞机离北京回国。到机场欢送的有：国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔、民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓村、北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭。中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司副司长江明也到机场欢送。
