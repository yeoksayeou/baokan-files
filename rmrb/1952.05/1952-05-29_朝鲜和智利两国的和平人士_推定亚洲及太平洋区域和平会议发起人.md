Title: Peace Activists from Korea and Chile Designated as Initiators of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: To respond to the call for the convocation of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, peace activists from Korea and Chile have respectively designated the initiators of the conference and are carrying out preparatory work for the conference. The initiators from Korea are: Park Chung-ai, Member of the World Peace Council, Vice-Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League; Han Sorya, Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, Chairman of the Korean Federation of Literature and Art Organizations, writer; Hyun Heon, Chairman of the General Federation of Korean Trade Unions; Lee Hwan-ki, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League; and Tian Dong-hyuk, Member of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace. The Korean Preparatory Committee for the Peace Conference has decided to widely publicize the great significance of the Peace Conference to the people of the whole country, to strengthen the unity between the Korean people and the people of the countries along the Asian and Pacific coasts, to work together to strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue, to defend peace, and to stop the conspiracy of new wars of aggression. The initiators from Chile are: Guillermo del Pedregal, Member of the World Peace Council, former Minister of Finance; Olga Poblete de Espinosa, Member of the World Peace Council, Professor of Far Eastern Studies; Mireya Lafuente, Chairman of the Intellectual Workers Union, painter; and Salvador Ocampo, Senator, Secretary-General of the Chilean Trade Union. They have decided to send representatives to Beijing to attend the conference.

### 朝鲜和智利两国的和平人士  推定亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议发起人

1952-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和智利两国的和平人士
    推定亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议发起人
  　【新华社二十八日讯】朝鲜和智利两国的和平人士，为响应召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，已分别推定了会议的发起人，进行会议的准备工作。朝鲜的发起人为世界和平理事会理事、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会副委员长、朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长朴正爱，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长、朝鲜文学艺术总同盟委员长、作家韩雪野，朝鲜职业总同盟委员长玄勋，朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长李焕起和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员田东赫。朝鲜参加和平会议筹备委员会已决定向全国人民广泛宣传和平会议的重大意义，以加强朝鲜人民和亚洲及太平洋沿岸各国人民之间的团结，共同努力争取朝鲜问题的和平解决，保卫和平，并制止新侵略战争的阴谋。智利的发起人为：世界和平理事会理事、前财政部部长盖勒莫·德尔·柏德列加，世界和平理事会理事、远东问题教授奥尔加·波布列特夫人，知识工作者联盟主席、画家米来亚·拉夫恩提夫人，上议院议员、智利工会秘书长萨尔瓦多·奥坎波。他们已决定派遣代表来北京出席会议。
