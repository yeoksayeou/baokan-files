Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 25th. The Burmese Cultural Delegation, consisting of fifteen members and led by U Tun Pe, arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 25th for a visit, accompanied by Chen Ying, a special commissioner of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government. Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife also traveled with them. Greeting them at the station were Song Richang, Deputy Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Xia Yan, Director of the Bureau of Culture, and Zhang Junxiang and Zhou Xiaoyan, members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to India and Burma, who were in Shanghai. Indian Consul General in Shanghai, Mr. Merie, and his wife also welcomed them at the station. On the same morning, U Tun Pe, head of the delegation, and all the members were received by Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Central People's Government, accompanied by Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung. In the evening, Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai, hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation.

### 缅甸文化代表团到上海参观

1952-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团到上海参观
    【新华社上海二十五日电】缅甸文化代表团一行十五人，在团长宇吞帕率领下，由中央人民政府外交部专员陈应陪同，在二十五日晨到上海参观。缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂及其夫人同行。前往车站欢迎的，有华东军政委员会副秘书长宋日昌，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年，文化局局长夏衍及我国前赴印缅文化代表团在沪团员张骏祥、周小燕等。印度驻沪总领事梅里及其夫人也到车站欢迎。当日上午，该团团长宇吞帕和全体团员进见中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席，陪同进见的，为缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂。晚上，上海市人民政府陈毅市长设宴欢迎代表团。
