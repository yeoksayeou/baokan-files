Title: American Peace Fighter Paul Robeson's Fifty-Fourth Birthday: China Peace Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression sent a telegram on the 7th to congratulate the American peace fighter, famous singer Paul Robeson, on his fifty-fourth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Fast, New York, transfer to Robeson:
    On the occasion of your fifty-fourth birthday, please accept our heartfelt congratulations. Your work and struggle for world peace and the liberation of oppressed peoples are not only admired by peace-loving and freedom-loving people but also encourage all good people. We wish you success in your work and good health.
            The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, May 7th

### 美国和平战士罗伯逊五十四岁诞辰  我国保卫世界和平委员会致电祝贺

1952-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国和平战士罗伯逊五十四岁诞辰
    我国保卫世界和平委员会致电祝贺
    【新华社八日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会于七日电贺美国和平战士、名歌唱家罗伯逊五十四岁诞辰。电文如下：纽约法斯特转罗伯逊：
    值您五十四岁寿辰之际，谨致以衷心的祝贺，您为世界和平与被压迫人民的解放而工作和斗争，不仅为爱好和平与自由的人民所钦佩，并给予一切善良人民以鼓励，敬祝您工作顺利和身体健康。
            中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会五月七日
