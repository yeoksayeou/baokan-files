Title: Korean Negotiation Delegation Conference Makes No Progress
Author:
Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, May 6th. The Korean armistice negotiation delegation held another administrative meeting on the 6th. The meeting made no progress.

### 朝鲜谈判代表团大会无进展

1952-05-07

    朝鲜谈判代表团大会无进展
