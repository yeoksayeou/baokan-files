Author:
Date: 1952-05-10

    Our Great Motherland
    The Jingjiang River Diversion Project is one of China's large-scale water conservancy projects. Currently, workers and peasants in various regions are working day and night to support the Jingjiang River Diversion Project. This is a scene of workers from the Jianghan Shipbuilding Company assembling the first gate of the South Sluice of the Jingjiang River Diversion Project.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    荆江分洪工程是我国大规模的水利工程之一。现在各地的工人和农民正在日以继夜地为支援荆江分洪工程而努力工作。这是江汉船舶公司的工人正在装配荆江分洪工程南闸的第一个闸门的情形。
