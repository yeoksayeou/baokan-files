Title: Soviet and Other Countries' Trade Union Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th:

    The trade union delegations from the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, and Bulgaria, who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their countries. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department, and Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Textile Workers' Union, and others.

### 苏联等国工会代表团返国

1952-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联等国工会代表团返国
    【新华社二十八日讯】来我国参加“五一”节观礼的苏联、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚工会代表团已于二十八日晨乘飞机离北京回国。前往机场欢送的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、组织部部长许之桢和中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏等人。
