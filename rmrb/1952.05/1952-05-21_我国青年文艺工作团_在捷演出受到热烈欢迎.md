Title: China's Youth Art Troupe Receives Warm Welcome in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague: China's Youth Art Troupe, which participated in the World Youth Festival in Berlin last year and has been touring the Soviet Union and Eastern European countries, has arrived in Czechoslovakia from Bulgaria for performances. On the evening of the 12th, China's Youth Art Troupe officially performed at the Smetana Theater in Prague. Among those who attended the performance were: the Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, John; Prime Minister Zápotocký; Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Široký; Deputy Prime Minister Ďuriš; Czechoslovak government officials; members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party; and figures from the Czechoslovak cultural circles. The Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Tan Xilin, as well as diplomatic envoys from other fraternal countries, were also present. The program included singing, folk dances, classical Chinese opera, and the opera "Shoulder to Shoulder Forward." The audience gave enthusiastic applause to these programs and repeatedly requested encores. Shouts of "Stalin, Gottwald, Mao Zedong" resounded for a long time.

Prior to this, China's Youth Art Troupe had performed many times in Bratislava, the Czech-Hungarian border city of Komárno, the mining city of Kladno, and the Winter Stadium in Prague. On the 10th, they performed "The White-Haired Girl" in Prague. Each performance concluded in an atmosphere strongly expressing the friendship between the two peoples. After completing their performances in Prague, the troupe will travel to various other cities in Czechoslovakia for performances. On the 13th, members of China's Youth Art Troupe met with the Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of Czechoslovakia, Široký, and attended a banquet hosted by the Czechoslovak Minister of Education, Science, and Art, Nejedlý. On the same evening, the Czechoslovak Central Council of Trade Unions and the Czechoslovak Youth League jointly held a reception for the Chinese Youth Art Troupe, introducing the members to representatives of Czechoslovak composers, theater workers, and other art workers. During the reception, the hosts and guests performed songs, dances, and other programs.

### 我国青年文艺工作团  在捷演出受到热烈欢迎

1952-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我国青年文艺工作团
    在捷演出受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社布拉格电】参加去年在柏林举行的世界青年联欢节后在苏联和东欧各国巡回演出的我国青年文艺工作团，已自保加利亚抵捷克斯洛伐克演出。十二日晚，我国青年文艺工作团在布拉格斯米泰娜剧院正式演出。前往观剧的有：捷国民议会主席约翰、总理萨波托斯基、副总理兼外交部长西罗基、副总理杜兰斯基，捷政府官员、共产党中央委员会委员和捷文化界人士等，我驻捷大使谭希林，以及其他兄弟国家的外交使节等也都出席。演出的节目有唱歌、民间舞蹈、中国古典剧和歌剧“并肩向前”。观众对这些节目报以热烈掌声，并多次要求再演。欢呼“斯大林、哥特瓦尔德、毛泽东”的声音经久不绝。
    在此以前，我国青年文艺工作团曾在布拉的斯拉发、捷匈边境城市科马诺、采矿城市克拉德诺等以及在布拉格的冬季体育场演出多次，并于十日在布拉格演出“白毛女”。每次演出都在强有力地表现两国人民友谊的气氛中结束。该团在布拉格演毕后，将再到捷各城市去表演。我国青年文艺工作团人员十三日进见捷副总理兼外交部长西罗基，并出席了捷教育科学艺术部长尼耶德利的宴会，同日晚，捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会和捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟联合举行晚会，招待我青年文艺工作团，介绍团员们和捷克斯洛伐克作曲家、戏剧工作者及其他艺术工作者的代表会面。会上，宾主双方演出了歌唱、舞蹈和其他节目。
