Title: Algerian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing for "May Day" Observance; Italian Film Director Santi Arrives in Beijing to Attend Anniversary Commemoration of Four World Cultural Figures
Author:
Date: 1952-05-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Algerian Trade Union Delegation, comprising thirteen members, and one Iranian trade union representative, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 1st to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country. Italian film director Giuseppe De Santis also arrived at the same time to participate in the anniversary commemoration of Avicenna, da Vinci, Hugo, and Gogol.
The list of the Algerian Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
　　Head of the Delegation: Mussa Brahim, Algerian General Confederation of Labor Secretary, Tobacco Workers' Union Secretary
　　Member: Abdelkader Babou, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Algerian General Confederation of Labor, Secretary-General of the Trade Union Council of the Blida Region.
　　Boualem Bouyoub, Member of the Algerian Railway Workers' Union.
　　Pierre Liddy, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Algerian General Confederation of Labor, Secretary-General of the Algerian Gas and Electricity Workers' Union.
　　Abdelkader Derradji, Secretary-General of the Trade Union Council of the Sidi-Bel-Abbès Region.
　　Khir Bengazi, Secretary of the Trade Union Council of the Sidi-Bel-Abbès Region.
　　Baptiste Berretdot, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Algerian General Confederation of Labor, Secretary-General of the Algerian Dockworkers' Union.
　　Hamed Zitouni, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Algiers Provincial Trade Union Council.
　　Mohammed Naib, Secretary-General of the Oran Agricultural Workers' Union.
　　Ali Hababat, Secretary of the Troville Miners' Union.
　　Hamed Achour, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Algiers Metalworkers' Union.
　　Dris Menteur, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Constantine Medical Workers' Union.
　　Mohammed Bidi, Member of the Executive Bureau of the Constantine Provincial Trade Union Council.

    阿尔及利亚工会代表团一日抵京
