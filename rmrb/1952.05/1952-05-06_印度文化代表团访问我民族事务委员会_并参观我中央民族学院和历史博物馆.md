Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Visits Our Nationalities Affairs Commission and Visits Our Central Institute for Nationalities and Historical Museum
Author:
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th—The Indian Cultural Delegation visited the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 5th and visited the Central Institute for Nationalities.

Responsible comrades of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government held a forum with the delegation on nationality issues. The forum was presided over by Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission. Participants in the forum included Liu Geping (Hui), Vice Chairman of the Commission, and members Zhu Zaoguan (Miao), Liu Chun (Han), Luo Changpei (Manchu), Jian Bozan (Uyghur), Sa Kongliao (Mongolian), Yang Jingren (Hui), Zhao Fan (Han), Mengtel (Tibetan), head of the Tibetan Affairs Group, Ayesha (Uyghur), Deputy Director of the General Office, Peng Sike (Mongolian), and Luo Wang, acting director of the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office.

At the forum, Li Weihan introduced China's nationality policy to the delegation and answered various questions raised by members of the delegation.

After the meeting, the delegation went to the Central Institute for Nationalities for a visit. The delegation met with students from various ethnic minorities in China at the institute and visited the "Exhibition of Cultural Relics and Pictures of Ethnic Minorities" held by the institute.

Some members of the delegation also visited the Historical Museum on the morning of the 5th.

### 印度文化代表团访问我民族事务委员会  并参观我中央民族学院和历史博物馆

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团访问我民族事务委员会
    并参观我中央民族学院和历史博物馆
    【新华社五日讯】印度文化代表团于五日下午访问中央人民政府民族事务委员会，并参观中央民族学院。
    中央人民政府民族事务委员会负责同志与代表团举行了关于民族问题的座谈会。座谈会由民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉主持。参加座谈会的有该会副主任委员刘格平（回），委员朱早观（苗）、刘春（汉）、罗常培（满）、翦伯赞（维吾尔）、萨空了（蒙）、杨静仁（回）、赵范（汉），藏族事务组组长孟特尔（藏），办公厅副主任阿艾沙（维吾尔）、朋斯克（蒙）以及班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺。
    李维汉在座谈会上向代表团介绍了我国的民族政策，并回答了代表团团员们提出的各项问题。
    会后，代表团即前往中央民族学院参观。代表团在该院会见了中国各地少数民族学生，并参观了该院所举办的“少数民族文物图片展览”。
    代表团一部分团员在五日上午又曾参观了历史博物馆。
