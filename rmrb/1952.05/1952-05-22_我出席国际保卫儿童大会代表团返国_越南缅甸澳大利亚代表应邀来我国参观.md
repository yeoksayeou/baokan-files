Title: Delegation to International Conference for the Defense of Children Returns Home; Representatives from Vietnam, Burma, and Australia Invited to Visit China
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 1

	Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The head of the Vietnamese delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children, Ho Quy, and two representatives, Burmese representative Du Qingna, and the head of the Australian delegation, Madeline Ganster, and three representatives, arrived in Beijing on the 21st at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China. The Chinese delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children, led by Kang Keqing, also returned to Beijing at the same time. Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, Vice-Chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Fu Lianzhang and Wei Que, Vice-Chairpersons of the China National Committee for the Defense of Children, Liang Xi, Chen Qiyuan, Zhang Xiaomei, members of the committee, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from various people's organizations and government agencies, totaling more than 200 people, welcomed them at the station.

### 我出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团返国  越南缅甸澳大利亚代表应邀来我国参观

1952-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团返国
    越南缅甸澳大利亚代表应邀来我国参观
    【新华社二十一日讯】出席国际保卫儿童大会的越南代表团团长荷桂及代表二人，缅甸代表都庆纳和澳大利亚的代表团团长麦德林·甘司特尔及代表三人，应中华全国民主妇女联合会的邀请来我国参观，在二十一日到达北京。我国出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团在团长康克清率领下同时返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有：中华全国民主妇联副主席邓颖超、李德全，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席傅连暲、韦悫，委员梁希、陈其瑗、张晓梅，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温及各人民团体和各机关代表共二百多人。
