Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-05-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Tibetan people are skilled at horsemanship and archery. They often celebrate their festivals by performing equestrian archery. This is the scene of the Tibetan people of Heihe Town performing equestrian archery to welcome the People's Liberation Army when the People's Liberation Army entered Tibet last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    藏族人民擅长骑射。他们常常以表演骑射术来庆祝他们的节日。这是去年人民解放军进入西藏的时候，黑河镇的藏族人民表演骑射术欢迎人民解放军的情形。
