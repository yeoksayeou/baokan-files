Title: Iranian Labor Union Representatives Issue Statement Before Leaving China
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Iranian labor union representatives who came to China to attend the "May 1st" celebrations issued the following statement before leaving China:

Dear Comrades:

On the occasion of visiting China, the Iranian representatives personally witnessed the following achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party and its great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong:

　　1. The greatest national unity.
　　2. The great Chinese people are completely loyal to the principles of internationalism.
　　3. The Chinese people are completely loyal to a just and lasting peace.
　　4. The Chinese people's friendship and loyalty to the Soviet Union is particularly significant. As we all know, this friendship is the touchstone for all those who struggle for peace and national independence.

Regarding China's progress, we already knew a great deal, but what we have now seen with our own eyes far exceeds our imagination.

Regarding such important issues as land reform, the reform of the teaching system, the cultivation of youth, the care of children, the welfare of workers, and labor insurance, we cannot even express our general impressions, because even a brief discussion would require a lot of time and space.

We will do our best to make our Iranian people understand everything that People's China has achieved.

We will do our best to publicize our impressions to the Iranian people through articles, pamphlets, and meetings, so that they know the miracles that the Chinese people are achieving in all aspects.

### 伊朗工会代表离开我国前夕发表谈话

1952-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗工会代表离开我国前夕发表谈话
    【新华社二十八日讯】来我国参加“五一”节观礼的伊朗工会代表，在离开中国前夕，发表谈话如下：亲爱的同志们：
    伊朗代表在访问中国之际，亲眼见到了中国人民在共产党和它的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的领导下获得了下列的成就：
    一、最大的全国统一。
    二、伟大的中国人民完全忠于国际主义的原则。
    三、中国人民完全忠于正义的、持久的和平。
    四、中国人民对苏联的友谊和忠诚是特别显著的。像我们大家所知道的，这种友谊是考验所有为和平及民族的独立而斗争的人的试金石。
    关于中国已有进步一事，我们原来就知道了许多，但是我们现在所亲眼见到的却大大地超过了我们想像的范围。
    对于土地改革、教学制度的改革、青年的培养、儿童的保育、工人的福利、劳动保险等等这样一些重要的问题，我们甚至无法说出我们大体的印象，因为即使扼要地谈一谈也需要很多的时间和篇幅。
    我们将尽我们最大的努力使我们伊朗人民了解人民中国已实现的一切。
    我们将尽我们最大的努力用文章、小册子和会议等方式把我们的印象向伊朗人民宣传，使他们知道中国人民在各方面正在实现的奇迹。
