Title: Indonesian Millions of Workers Demonstrate to Celebrate May Day, Demanding Cessation of American Bacteriological Warfare in Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 7th TASS: Indonesian working people widely celebrated May Day, the first time in Indonesian history. Approximately one million Indonesian working people participated in May Day celebration rallies and demonstrations across the country, including about 500,000 demonstrators in Jakarta, about 80,000 in Makassar, about 60,000 in Palembang, and about 25,000 in Bandung. Mass rallies were also held in Semarang, Surabaya, Medan, and other cities in Indonesia. Workers throughout the country demanded improved living conditions, cessation of bacteriological warfare in Korea, and called on the people of the whole country to fight for peace. The All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions was the main organizer of these celebrations and demonstrations.

### 印尼百万劳动人民示威庆祝“五一”节  要求制止美国在朝鲜进行的细菌战

1952-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼百万劳动人民示威庆祝“五一”节
    要求制止美国在朝鲜进行的细菌战
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社雅加达七日讯：印尼劳动人民广泛庆祝“五一”节，这在印尼历史上还是第一次。印尼全国参加“五一”节庆祝大会和示威游行的劳动人民，共约一百万人，其中在雅加达示威的约有五十万人，在锡江约有八万人，在巨港约有六万人，在万隆约有两万五千人。在三宝垅、泗水、棉兰和印尼其他城市，也都举行了群众大会。全国各地的工人们都要求改善生活情况、制止在朝鲜进行的细菌战，而且还号召全国人民为争取和平而斗争。印尼总工会是这些庆祝大会和示威游行的主要组织者。
