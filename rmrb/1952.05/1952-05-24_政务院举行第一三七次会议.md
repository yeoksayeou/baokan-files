Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 137th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-05-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The 137th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 23rd. After listening to the explanation made by Li Shucheng, Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the ministry's summary of agricultural production work in 1951 and its work plan for 1952. The meeting approved several proposals for appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一三七次会议

1952-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一三七次会议
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十七次政务会议在二十三日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府农业部李书城部长所作的说明后，批准了该部关于一九五一年农业生产工作总结及一九五二年工作计划。会议通过提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
