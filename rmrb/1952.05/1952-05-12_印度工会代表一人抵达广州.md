Title: Indian Trade Union Representative Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-05-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 11th—Mr. Ram Asri, an Indian trade union representative invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in our country, arrived in Guangzhou on the 10th, his journey having been delayed due to unforeseen circumstances. Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Nengxing, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, were all at the station to welcome him.

### 印度工会代表一人抵达广州

1952-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工会代表一人抵达广州
    【新华社广州十一日电】应中华全国总工会之邀前来我国参加五一节观礼的印度工会代表蓝姆·阿斯里，旅途因事延搁，于十日始到达广州。广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云、广州市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟、广东省总工会筹备会秘书长陈能兴等均到车站欢迎。
