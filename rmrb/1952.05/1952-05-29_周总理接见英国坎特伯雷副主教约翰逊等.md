Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives British Canterbury Vice-Dean Johnson and Others
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 28th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government received British Canterbury Vice-Dean Johnson and his wife, and Oxford University Workers' Education lecturer Day, who are currently visiting China, on the afternoon of the 28th.

### 周总理接见英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等

1952-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等
  　【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在二十八日下午接见现在中国参观的英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊及其夫人、牛津大学工人教育问题讲师戴伊。
