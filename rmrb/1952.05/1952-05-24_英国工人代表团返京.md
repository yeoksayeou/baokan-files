Title: British Workers' Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The British Workers' Delegation, invited to our country to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, returned to Beijing by train on the morning of the 23rd after visiting various places in East China. Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also returned to Beijing on the same train.

### 英国工人代表团返京

1952-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    英国工人代表团返京
    【新华社二十三日讯】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的英国工人代表团在华东各地参观访问后，已于二十三日晨乘车返抵北京。中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一也同车回京。
