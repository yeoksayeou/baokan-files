Title: Soviet People Eagerly Purchase Russian Translation of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 18th. The Russian translation of the first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" was published on the 15th. The windows of major bookstores in Moscow are displaying the book, and buyers are very enthusiastic. The book is a 32mo hardcover edition, with a total of 534 pages. The words "Mao Zedong" are printed in gold in Russian on the dark blue leather cover and spine. A photo of Chairman Mao is printed at the beginning of the volume.

When the translation of the Selected Works was published, major Soviet newspapers such as Pravda and Izvestia published news reports and introduced the contents of the volume.

### 苏联人民踊跃购买“毛泽东选集”俄译本

1952-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民踊跃购买“毛泽东选集”俄译本
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】“毛泽东选集”第一卷俄文译本已在十五日出版。莫斯科各大书店的橱窗内，都陈列该书，购者甚为踊跃。该书为三十二开精装版本，厚共五三四面，在深蓝色的充皮封面及书脊上，都印有烫金的俄文毛泽东三字，卷首并印有毛主席的照片。
    选集译本出版时，苏联真理报、消息报等各大报均发表新闻，并介绍该卷的内容。
