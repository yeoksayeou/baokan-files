Title: The Evil Consequences of the US Conducting Germ Warfare in Korea: Dysentery Patients in Japan More Than Double Last Year

Author:

Date: 1952-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: The germ warfare being waged by the American aggressors in Korea continues to produce evil consequences in Japan. TASS, Tokyo, May 21st: Japan has experienced a particularly rampant outbreak of dysentery. The number of patients with the disease has more than doubled compared to last year. Democratic newspapers previously reported that the spread of dysentery in Kobe was related to the US military's use of bacteriological weapons in Korea.

### 美国在朝鲜进行细菌战的恶果  日本痢疾病患者比去年多二倍

1952-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    美国在朝鲜进行细菌战的恶果
    日本痢疾病患者比去年多二倍
    【新华社二十二日讯】美国侵略者在朝鲜进行的细菌战，正继续在日本产生恶果。塔斯社东京二十一日讯：日本已发生了特别猖獗的痢疾。该病患者已超过去年的二倍。民主报纸前曾报道，神户痢疾之所以蔓延是与美军在朝鲜使用细菌武器有关的。
