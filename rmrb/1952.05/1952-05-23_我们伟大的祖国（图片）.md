Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-05-23
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the first anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet between the Central People's Government and the Local Government of Tibet. This is a scene of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet from the Northwest, crossing mountains and rivers, holding high the red flag, and arriving in Lhasa, the seat of the Local Government of Tibet.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是中央人民政府和西藏地方政府签订关于和平解放西藏办法的协议一周年的日子。这是翻越千山万水，由西北入藏的人民解放军部队高举红旗，抵达西藏地方政府所在地拉萨城的情景。
