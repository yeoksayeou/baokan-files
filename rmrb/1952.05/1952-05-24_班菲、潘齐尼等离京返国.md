Title: Banfi, Pancini and Others Depart Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-05-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Italian Senator Banfi, physicist Pancini, and film director De Santis, who were invited to China to attend the World Celebrities Commemoration Conference, left Beijing by plane this morning, the 23rd, to return home. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were: Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and others.

### 班菲、潘齐尼等离京返国

1952-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    班菲、潘齐尼等离京返国
    【新华社二十三日讯】应邀前来我国参加世界四大文化名人纪念大会的意大利上议员班菲、物理学家潘齐尼、电影导演德·桑蒂，在二十三日上午搭飞机离开北京回国。前往机场送行的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一和联络部副部长孟雨，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫等多人。
