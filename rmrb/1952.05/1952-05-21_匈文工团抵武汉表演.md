Title: Hungarian Performing Arts Troupe Arrives in Wuhan for Performances
Author:
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, May 20th: The Hungarian People's Republic National People's Performing Arts Troupe, consisting of 137 members led by Director Szöllősi Gyula, arrived in Wuhan by special train from Beijing on the 20th, having been invited to perform in our country. Song Kanfu, Xu Maoyong, Chen Huangmei, Chen Jianshu, and representatives from various people's organizations, military units, students, and cultural and artistic circles, totaling over 1,000 people, were at the station to welcome them.

### 匈文工团抵武汉表演

1952-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    匈文工团抵武汉表演
    【新华社汉口二十日电】应邀前来我国表演的匈牙利人民共和国国家人民文工团一行一百三十七人，在团长歇勒西·纠腊率领下，于二十日自北京乘专车抵达武汉。到车站欢迎的有宋侃夫、徐懋庸、陈荒煤、陈剑修和各人民团体、部队、学生和文化艺术界代表共一千多人。
