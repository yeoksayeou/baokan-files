Title: Part of the Li Seung-man Puppet Army Officers and Soldiers Cross Over to the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 21st: TASS Pyongyang, May 19th dispatch: According to Pyongyang Radio broadcast, officers and soldiers of the Li Seung-man army are increasingly dissatisfied with the intervention war waged by the US imperialists in collusion with the Korean traitorous clique. Recently, a part of the officers and soldiers of the 7th Division of the Li Seung-man puppet army, including a battalion commander named Jin Yu (transliteration), crossed the battle line to surrender. They were warmly welcomed in Pyongyang by representatives of municipal authorities, mass organizations, working people, and soldiers of the People's Army. Jin Yu expressed his gratitude for the welcome from the residents of Pyongyang; he said that it was a great pleasure to no longer serve the despicable Li Seung-man traitorous clique. He is determined to fight selflessly for his motherland and people.

### 李承晚伪军官兵一部  向朝鲜人民军投诚

1952-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    李承晚伪军官兵一部
    向朝鲜人民军投诚
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社平壤十九日讯：据平壤电台广播，李承晚军队的官兵对美帝国主义者串通朝鲜卖国集团所进行的干涉战争日渐感到不满。最近有李承晚伪军第七师的官兵一部，其中包括一个名叫金玉（译音）的营长，越过战线前来投诚。他们在平壤受到市政机关、民众团体、劳动人民和人民军战士的代表们的热诚欢迎。金玉对平壤居民的欢迎表示感谢；他说，他能够不再为卑鄙的李承晚卖国集团服务，是一件极大的幸事。他决心要为他的祖国和人民进行忘我的战斗。
