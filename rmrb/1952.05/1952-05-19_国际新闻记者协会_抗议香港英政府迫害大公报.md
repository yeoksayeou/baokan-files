Title: International Organization of Journalists Protests British Government Persecution of Ta Kung Pao
Author: Helman Kheronique
Date: 1952-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th. On the 17th, the International Organization of Journalists issued the following protest to the British government of Hong Kong regarding the persecution of Ta Kung Pao:

To the British government of Hong Kong:

We are shocked and saddened to hear of the forced closure of Ta Kung Pao and the imprisonment of its editors. This is solely because the newspaper published a factual report on the Chinese people's protest against the Hong Kong government's bloody suppression. We consider this a blatant attack on freedom of the press; the British government of Hong Kong has absolutely no right to do this. Based on the principles of freedom of speech and expression, we demand that such rights be granted to newspapers in the territories currently under British rule.

International Organization of Journalists
President: Helman
Secretary-General: Kheronique

### 国际新闻记者协会  抗议香港英政府迫害大公报
赫尔曼  赫朗尼克
1952-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    国际新闻记者协会
    抗议香港英政府迫害大公报
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】十七日，国际新闻记者协会为香港大公报被迫害事向香港英国政府提出抗议如下：香港英国政府：
    我们听到大公报被勒令停刊和该报编辑被判徒刑的消息，感到颇为惊愕和遗憾。这只是因为那个报纸发表了一篇关于中国人民抗议香港政府血腥镇压的事实报道。我们认为这是对新闻自由的一种猖狂进攻；香港英国政府绝无任何权利这样做。根据言论自由和表达意见的自由的原则，我们要求在英国现在统治之下的地方给报纸以这种权利。
    国际新闻记者协会  主席  赫尔曼  秘书长  赫朗尼克
