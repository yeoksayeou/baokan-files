Title: Government Administration Council Holds 136th Meeting; Previous Meeting Held on May 9th
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 16th: The 136th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 16th. After hearing Vice Minister of Finance Rong Zihe's report on the collection of agricultural tax in 1951 and his opinions on the 1952 draft tax rate plan for agricultural tax in new areas, the meeting approved the 1952 draft tax rate plan for agricultural tax in new areas. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The 135th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of May 9th. After hearing the explanation given by Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council, the meeting approved the committee's main points of supervision work for April to December 1952.

### 政务院举行第一三六次会议  上一次会议已在五月九日举行

1952-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一三六次会议
    上一次会议已在五月九日举行
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十六次政务会议在十六日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府财政部戎子和副部长关于一九五一年农业税的征收情况和一九五二年新区农业税税率方案的意见后，批准了一九五二年新区农业税税率方案。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十五次政务会议于五月九日下午举行。会议在听取政务院人民监察委员会刘景范副主任所作的说明后，批准了该委一九五二年四月至十二月监察工作要点。
