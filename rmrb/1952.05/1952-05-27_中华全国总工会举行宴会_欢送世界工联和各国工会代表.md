Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Banquet to See Off Representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions and Trade Unions from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1952-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet to bid farewell to the representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions and trade unions from various countries who were invited to China by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations and are about to leave Beijing to return to their home countries. Those present at the banquet included Zhu Xuefan and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China; Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Zhizhen, Director of the Organization Department, and others. Zhu Xuefan delivered a speech on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, expressing his feelings of reluctance to part with the representatives. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the victory of the unity of the working class of the world, and the atmosphere was extremely warm. A dance party was held after the banquet to add to the enjoyment.

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: The delegation of the World Federation of Trade Unions and trade union representatives from various countries, totaling more than 120 people, who came to China to attend the "May Day" Labor Day celebrations, returned to Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 25th, accompanied by Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China, Xu Zhizhen, Director of the Organization Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others, after visiting various places in East China. Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the station to welcome them.

### 中华全国总工会举行宴会  欢送世界工联和各国工会代表

1952-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会举行宴会
     欢送世界工联和各国工会代表
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的世界工会联合会和各国工会代表，即将分别离开北京回国，中华全国总工会特举行宴会欢送。出席宴会的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、刘宁一，中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏，中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温、组织部部长许之桢等多人。朱学范代表中华全国总工会在会上致词，向代表们表示惜别之意。席间宾主频频为全世界工人阶级的团结的胜利而干杯，情绪极为热烈。会后并举行舞会助兴。
    【新华社二十五日讯】前来我国参加“五一”劳动节观礼的世界工会联合会和各国工会代表团一行一百二十多人，在华东各地参观访问后，已于二十五日下午由中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏、中华全国总工会组织部长许之桢等陪同乘车回到北京。到车站欢迎的有中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温等人。
