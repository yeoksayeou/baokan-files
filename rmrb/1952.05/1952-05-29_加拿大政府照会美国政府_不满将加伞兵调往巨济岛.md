Title: Canadian Government Protests to US Government Over Transfer of Canadian Paratroopers to Geoje Island
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The cowardly behavior of the American aggressors in ordering the transfer of Commonwealth Division troops to Geoje Island to share the blame for the American massacre of our captured personnel has aroused dissatisfaction from the Canadian government.

According to a Reuters dispatch from Ottawa on the 26th, the Canadian government has lodged a protest with the US government, expressing "displeasure and anxiety" over the order of the US Commander-in-Chief in Korea, General Clark, to transfer a Canadian paratroop company to Geoje Island. The Canadian Press report cited in the same Reuters dispatch indicates that the Canadian government's protest was due to "their belief that the Americans have handled the situation on Geoje Island very badly, and now want Canada, Britain and Greece to share the responsibility and consequences."

### 加拿大政府照会美国政府  不满将加伞兵调往巨济岛

1952-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    加拿大政府照会美国政府  不满将加伞兵调往巨济岛
    【新华社二十八日讯】美国侵略者下令调遣英联邦师部队至巨济岛以分担美方屠杀我方被俘人员的罪责的卑怯行为，已引起加拿大政府的不满。
    据路透社渥太华二十六日电透露，加拿大政府已向美国政府提出照会，对于美国侵朝军总司令克拉克把加拿大伞兵连调往巨济岛一事，表示“不悦和焦虑”。路透社同一电讯所引的加拿大通讯社的报道表明：加拿大政府提出照会表示不满的原因，是由于“他们认为美国人处理巨济岛的情势甚为糟糕，而现在却要加拿大、英国和希腊来分担责任和后果”。
