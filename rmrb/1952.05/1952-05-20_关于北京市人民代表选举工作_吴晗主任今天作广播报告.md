Title: Wu Han to Deliver Radio Address Today on Beijing Municipal People's Representative Election Work
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 1

    Concerning Beijing Municipal People's Representative Election Work
    Wu Han to Deliver Radio Address Today
    [From Our Newspaper] Wu Han, Director of the Beijing Municipal Fourth Session of People's Representatives Election Committee, is scheduled to deliver a report titled "On the Election Work of the People's Representatives Conference in the Beijing Urban Area" to the city's residents via Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, starting at 2:30 PM today. The station will broadcast simultaneously on five frequencies (850, 1080, 920, 750, and 1320 kilocycles).

### 关于北京市人民代表选举工作  吴晗主任今天作广播报告

1952-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    关于北京市人民代表选举工作
    吴晗主任今天作广播报告
    【本报讯】北京市第四届各界人民代表会议代表选举委员会主任吴晗，于二十日下午二时三十分起应邀在北京人民广播电台向全市人民作题为“关于北京市区各界人民代表会议的选举工作”的报告。该台将用五个周率（八五○，一○八○，九二○，七五○和一三二○千周）同时联播。
