Title: Secretariat of the International Union of Students Sends Telegram to All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation to Congratulate "May Fourth" Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1952-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Secretariat of the International Union of Students sent a telegram on the 3rd to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation to celebrate China's "May Fourth" Youth Day. The telegram is as follows:

All-China Democratic Youth Federation
All-China Students' Federation

On the occasion of the May Fourth Youth Day, please accept the heartfelt greetings to the youth and students of China. Students all over the world respect the glorious achievements of Chinese youth and students, together with the whole nation, in the struggle to win national independence and freedom, and their current contributions to the struggle for peace. We wish Chinese youth new achievements in the cause of building the motherland and defending world peace.

Secretariat of the International Union of Students May 3rd

### 国际学生联合会书记处  致电我全国青联和学联祝贺“五四”青年节

1952-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    国际学生联合会书记处
    致电我全国青联和学联祝贺“五四”青年节
    【新华社四日讯】国际学生联合会书记处在三日致电中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会庆祝我国“五四”青年节，电文如下：中华全国民主青年联合总会中华全国学生联合会
    值此五四青年节谨向中国青年及学生致以衷心的敬礼，全世界的学生都尊重中国青年及学生与全国一起在赢得民族独立与自由的斗争中的辉煌成就以及他们目前在争取和平斗争中所作的贡献。敬祝中国青年在建设祖国事业与保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的成就。
    国际学生联合会书记处  五月三日
