    Our Great Motherland
    The "Earth-Moon Orbiting Instrument" was created by Zhang Junde, a teacher at the Xi'an Normal University Affiliated Primary School. It plays an important role in guiding teaching and popularizing astronomical knowledge. This is a recent symposium held in Beijing, where Zhang Junde is explaining the manufacturing principles and process of the "Earth-Moon Orbiting Instrument."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “月地运行仪”是西安师范附属小学教师张俊德创造的。它对于辅导教学和普及天文知识方面起着重要的作用。这是最近在北京召开的一个座谈会，张俊德正在讲解“月地运行仪”的制作原理和过程。
