Title: Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Shanghai Yesterday for a Visit After Visiting Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 20th. More than 120 representatives from the World Federation of Trade Unions and trade unions of various countries who were invited to China to attend the May Day celebrations arrived in Hangzhou on the 18th for a visit. The Indonesian trade union delegation also arrived in Hangzhou on the 17th from Guangzhou for a visit. In addition to visiting scenic spots in West Lake, the representatives also visited the state-owned Zhejiang Linen Textile Factory. The representatives also visited the workers' sanatorium established by the Chinese Railway Trade Union in Liu Zhuang, West Lake. The clean sickrooms, excellent equipment, and beautiful environment of the sanatorium deeply impressed the representatives. Kachali, head of the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union delegation, said: "Seeing the great achievements of New China in the past two years, I am fully convinced that the construction of New China will develop rapidly in the future."
    
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th. More than 130 representatives from the World Federation of Trade Unions and trade union delegations from various countries, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in China, arrived in Shanghai this morning accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Chen Shaomin, and Xu Zhizhen for a visit. More than 3,000 people, including Wu Kejian, Pan Hannian, Sheng Pihua, Liu Changsheng, Liu Daosheng, Zhang Yun, labor models, advanced workers, and worker representatives, went to the station to welcome them. The Consul General of the Czechoslovak Consulate General in Shanghai, Srimak, also came to the station to welcome them. At the welcoming meeting held at the station, Liu Changsheng delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the workers of East China and Shanghai. Caulibonov, the representative of the World Federation of Trade Unions, replied: We warmly thank the Chinese worker brothers and people from all walks of life for their warm reception and welcome. After praising the brilliant and great achievements of New China in various construction undertakings, he said: We must tell all the people in the world: how the great Chinese people are working selflessly, and the Chinese people's desire and struggle are to defend lasting world peace.
    
That afternoon, the representatives visited the Caoyang New Village workers' residential area and the Shanghai city scene. In the evening, the East China Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions jointly hosted a banquet to welcome them. Responsible trade union cadres, labor models, and advanced workers were invited to attend.

### 各国工会代表访问杭州后昨天抵达上海参观

1952-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表访问杭州后昨天抵达上海参观
    【新华社杭州二十日电】应邀前来我国参加五一节观礼的世界工联和各国工会代表等一百二十多人，于十八日抵达杭州参观。印度尼西亚工会代表团也在十七日自广州来杭参观。代表们除游览西湖名胜外，并参观了公营浙江麻纺织厂。代表们还在西湖刘庄参观了中国铁路工会设立的职工休养所。休养所内整洁的病室、良好的设备和优美的环境给代表们以深刻的印象。印度尼西亚回教工会代表团团长卡查利说：“看到新中国两年来的伟大成就，使我充分相信今后新中国的建设一定能飞速发展。”
    【新华社上海二十日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的世界工会联合会和各国工会代表团代表一行一百三十多人，二十日上午由刘宁一、陈少敏、许之桢陪同来上海参观。前往车站欢迎的有吴克坚、潘汉年、盛丕华、刘长胜、刘导生、章蕴和劳动模范、先进工作者及工人代表共三千多人。捷驻沪总领事馆总领事斯立马克也到车站欢迎。在车站举行的欢迎会上，刘长胜代表华东和上海工人致欢迎辞。世界工会联合会代表考里班诺夫致答词说：我们热烈感谢中国工人兄弟和各界人民的热烈招待与欢迎。他在称赞了新中国各项建设事业辉煌的伟大成就以后说：我们一定要告诉全世界所有一切的人们：伟大的中国人民是如何地在忘我劳动，中国人民的愿望和斗争就是为了保卫世界持久和平。
    代表们当日下午参观了曹杨新村工人住宅区和上海市容。晚上，中华全国总工会华东办事处、上海总工会联合设宴欢迎，应邀作陪的有工会负责干部、劳动模范和先进工作者。
