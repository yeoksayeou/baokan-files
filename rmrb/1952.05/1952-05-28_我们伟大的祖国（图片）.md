    Our Great Motherland
    A mobile broadcasting vehicle is an excellent and flexible tool for propaganda and cultural entertainment. This is the propaganda vehicle of the Central-South Broadcasting Station conducting propaganda and entertainment activities at the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project site. It is warmly welcomed by military personnel and civilian workers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    巡回广播宣传车是一种流动性的宣传和文化娱乐的优良工具。这是在荆江分洪工地进行宣传和文娱活动的中南广播电台的宣传车，它受到军工、民工们的热烈欢迎。
