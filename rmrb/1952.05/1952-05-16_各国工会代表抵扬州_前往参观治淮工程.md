Title: Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Yangzhou to Visit the Huai River Harnessing Project
Author:
Date: 1952-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yangzhou, May 15th. Over one hundred and twenty trade union representatives from the World Federation of Trade Unions and various countries, who were invited to China to attend the “May Day” celebrations, arrived in Yangzhou at 1:00 PM on the 14th, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others. Representatives of people's organizations from Northern Jiangsu and Yangzhou, as well as representatives of model workers in the Huai River harnessing project, numbering over six thousand, welcomed them at the station. Upon disembarking, Young Pioneers presented flowers to the representatives individually.

    On the afternoon of the 14th, the representatives visited the exhibition on the Huai River harnessing project and attended a reception held by the Northern Jiangsu People's Administrative Office. At the reception, Hui Yuyu, Director of the Northern Jiangsu People's Administrative Office, briefly introduced the Huai River harnessing project to the representatives, pointing out the great role this project will play in the prosperous and happy future of the Chinese people. The representatives will visit the construction site of the main irrigation canal in the lower reaches of the Huai River.

### 各国工会代表抵扬州  前往参观治淮工程

1952-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表抵扬州
    前往参观治淮工程
    【新华社扬州十五日电】应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的世界工联和各国工会代表等一百二十多人，由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一等陪同，于十四日下午一时到达扬州。在车站欢迎的有苏北与扬州市各人民团体代表以及治淮劳动模范代表等六千余人。下车时，少年儿童队员曾向代表们一一献花。
    代表们在十四日下午参观了治淮工程展览会，并出席了苏北人民行政公署举行的招待会。会上，苏北人民行政公署主任惠浴宇向代表们扼要地介绍了治淮工程，并指出这项工程对中国人民富裕和幸福的将来所起的伟大作用。代表们将赴淮河下游灌溉总渠工地参观。
