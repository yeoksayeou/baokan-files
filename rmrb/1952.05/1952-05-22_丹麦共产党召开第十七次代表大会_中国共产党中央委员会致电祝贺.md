Title: The Communist Party of Denmark Convenes its 17th National Congress; The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 1

    [Report from this newspaper] The Communist Party of Denmark convened its 17th National Congress on the 22nd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory message. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Denmark, and to all the representatives of the 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark, Copenhagen: We deeply regret that we are unable to send representatives to the 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of Denmark. We wish the congress a complete success, and we wish the Danish people victory in the struggle for peace and democracy under the leadership of the Communist Party of Denmark.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    May 20, 1952

### 丹麦共产党召开第十七次代表大会  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦共产党召开第十七次代表大会
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【本报讯】二十二日丹麦共产党召开第十七次代表大会，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：哥本哈根丹麦共产党中央委员会并转丹麦共产党第十七次代表大会全体代表同志们：欣逢丹麦共产党召开第十七次代表大会，我们未能派代表参加，深以为歉。谨祝大会圆满成功，并祝丹麦人民在丹麦共产党领导下在争取和平与民主的斗争中获得胜利。    中国共产党中央委员会  一九五二年五月二十日
