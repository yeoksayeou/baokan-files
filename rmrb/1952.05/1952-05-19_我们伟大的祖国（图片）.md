    Our Great Motherland
    Carrying out aviation model activities can cultivate in youth and children excellent qualities of loving the motherland, loving labor, and loving science. During an aviation model performance held in Tiananmen Square in Beijing, these two female members of the Young Pioneers team are holding model airplanes, preparing to take off.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    开展航空模型运动，可以培养青年和少年儿童热爱祖国、热爱劳动和热爱科学的优良品质。在北京天安门广场举行航空模型表演时，这两位少年儿童队的女队员正拿着模型飞机准备起飞。
