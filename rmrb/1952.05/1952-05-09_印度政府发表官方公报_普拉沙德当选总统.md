Title: The Indian Government Issues Official Communiqué: Prasad Elected President
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 8th: The Government of the Republic of India issued an official communiqué on May 6th, announcing that Dr. Prasad has been elected as the President of India. The President was elected by an electoral college consisting of the newly elected Indian Parliament's two houses and the legislative assemblies of the provinces (states). Prior to this, Dr. Radhakrishnan, the former Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, was elected as the Vice President of India on April 25th.

### 印度政府发表官方公报  普拉沙德当选总统

1952-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府发表官方公报
    普拉沙德当选总统
    【新华社新德里八日电】印度共和国政府在五月六日发表官方公报，宣布普拉沙德博士已当选为印度总统。总统是由最近新选出的印度国会两院和各省（邦）立法议会选出的选举团所选举出来的。在这以前，前印度驻苏联大使拉德哈克里斯南博士已在四月二十五日当选为印度副总统。
