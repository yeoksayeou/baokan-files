Title: Hungarian National Folk Ensemble Leaves Shenyang for Home

Author:

Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 28th. The Hungarian National Folk Ensemble left Shenyang by train on the 28th to return home. Before their departure, the Northeast People's Government held a banquet in their honor. Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, presided over the banquet. Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, Hong Chunzhe, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, and Vasilyev, Deputy Consul of the Soviet Union in Shenyang, were also invited to attend. After the banquet, all members of the ensemble left Shenyang by train. Luo Feng, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Northeast People's Government, Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and more than 700 people from cultural and artistic circles went to the station to see them off.

After three performances in Shenyang, the Hungarian National Folk Ensemble successfully concluded its tour of China, and a grand closing ceremony was held in Shenyang on the evening of the 27th. Liu Zhiming, Minister of Culture of the Northeast People's Government, and more than 1,000 people from cultural and artistic circles attended the closing ceremony.

During their stay in Northeast China, the ensemble visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall in Shenyang and Fushun, a famous coal industry center in our country. The head of the ensemble, Scheller Gyula, and the main responsible cadres, also held discussions with Liu Zhiming, Minister of Culture of the Northeast People's Government, and Luo Feng, Deputy Minister, about their impressions of performing and visiting China.

### 匈牙利文工团离沈阳返国

1952-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利文工团离沈阳返国
    【新华社沈阳二十八日电】匈牙利国家人民文工团于二十八日乘车离沈阳回国。行前，东北人民政府设宴欢送，宴会由东北人民政府副主席林枫主持，中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三、朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲、苏联驻沈阳副领事瓦锡里也夫等均应邀参加。宴会毕，全体团员乘车离沈阳，东北人民政府文化部副部长罗烽、沈阳市副市长焦若愚以及文化艺术界七百余人前往车站欢送。
    匈牙利国家人民文工团在沈阳三次演出后，胜利结束了来中国的巡回演出，二十七日晚在沈阳隆重举行闭幕式。参加闭幕式的有东北人民政府文化部部长刘芝明和文化艺术界千余人。
    该团在东北曾参观了沈阳东北工业陈列馆和我国著名煤业中心——抚顺。团长歇勒西·纠腊及该团主要负责干部并与东北人民政府文化部部长刘芝明、副部长罗烽座谈他们来中国演出和访问的观感。
