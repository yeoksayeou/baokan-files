Title: The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government Held a Reception Yesterday for the Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government held a reception on the evening of the 2nd to welcome the Burmese Cultural Delegation headed by U Tun Paw.

The reception was hosted by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture. Those attending the reception included: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zhou Yang and Ding Xilin, Deputy Ministers of Culture; Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education; Sha Qianli, Deputy Minister of Trade; Hu Yuzhi, Director of the General Administration of Publications; and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government.

Also attending the reception were university leaders Ma Yinchu, Ye Qisun, Mao Yisheng, Lin Liru, Chen Yuan, Sun Xiaocun, Fei Xiaotong; scientists Zhu Kezhen, Pei Wenzhong; literary and art figures Zhou Libo, Lao She, Zhao Shuli, Ouyang Yuqian, Li Bozhao, Shakov, Cao Yu, Mei Lanfang, Lü Ji, Qi Baishi, Xu Guangping; as well as Li Yimang, deputy head of China's previous cultural delegation to India and Burma, and members of the delegation in Beijing, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than 150 people.

Burmese Ambassador to China U Ohn Maung and diplomatic personnel from the embassy were also invited to attend.

During the reception, Minister Shen Yanbing delivered a welcome speech, expressing his sincere welcome to the Burmese Cultural Delegation. He said: The Chinese people are willing to establish friendly relations with the people of all countries and promote mutually beneficial cultural and economic exchanges. This visit of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to our country will surely bring back this spirit of peace and friendship of our people to Burma. He said that the friendly relations between China and Burma are one of the important factors for peace in Asia.

U Tun Paw, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, then delivered a speech. He said: The Burmese Cultural Delegation was deeply impressed by everything they saw in Beijing; they were particularly impressed by the unity of the Chinese people and the achievements of Chinese industry. The Burmese Cultural Delegation will never forget the warm welcome they received everywhere. He said: China and Burma had cultural relations in the past, and they still have them now, and these relations will be further enhanced in the future. He concluded by congratulating the cultural relations and friendship between China and Burma, and congratulating the lasting peace of Asia.

The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中央人民政府文化部  昨举行酒会招待缅甸文化代表团

1952-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府文化部
    昨举行酒会招待缅甸文化代表团
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府文化部在二日晚举行酒会，招待以宇吞帕为首的缅甸文化代表团。
    招待会由文化部部长沈雁冰主持。出席招待会的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长周扬、丁西林，教育部副部长韦悫，贸易部副部长沙千里，出版总署署长胡愈之，中央人民政府办公厅副主任余心清。
    出席招待会的还有各大学负责人马寅初、叶企孙、茅以升、林砺儒、陈垣、孙晓村、费孝通，科学界人士竺可桢、裴文中，文学艺术界人士周立波、老舍、赵树理、欧阳予倩、李伯钊、沙可夫、曹禺、梅兰芳、吕骥、齐白石、许广平，以及我国前赴印缅文化代表团副团长李一氓和在京团员、北京市副市长吴晗等一百五十余人。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和大使馆外交人员也应邀到会。
    席间沈雁冰部长首先致欢迎辞，他对缅甸文化代表团表示热诚的欢迎。他说：中国人民愿与各国人民建立友好关系，促进彼此之间的、对彼此都有益的文化交流和经济关系。缅甸文化代表团此次访问我国，一定能把我国人民的这种和平友好的精神带回缅甸。他说中缅两国的友好关系是亚洲和平的重要因素之一。
    缅甸文化代表团长宇吞帕继起致辞。他说：缅甸文化代表团对于在北京所参观的一切，印象极深；对于中国人的团结一致以及中国工业上的成就尤有深刻的印象。缅甸文化代表团对于他们在各处所受到的热诚欢迎是永远不会忘记的。他说：中缅两国过去有文化关系，现在也有，这种关系将来更将有所增进。他最后祝贺中缅两国的文化关系和友谊，祝贺亚洲的持久和平。
    酒会在友好的空气中进行。
