Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Visits Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, May 22nd: The Burmese Cultural Delegation and the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, visited the famous Runheji Diversion Sluice project and then took a train to Nanjing from Bengbu on the 22nd. Wu Jue, Secretary-General of the Huai River Harnessing Committee, and Ni Bainian, Mayor of Bengbu, went to the station to see them off. When the delegation traveled to Runheji by special boat, they highly praised this great construction project of New China.

### 缅甸文化代表团赴南京参观

1952-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团赴南京参观
    【新华社蚌埠二十二日电】缅甸文化代表团和缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，参观了著名的润河集分水闸工程后，已于二十二日经蚌埠乘车去南京。治淮委员会秘书长吴觉和蚌埠市市长倪柏年曾赴车站欢送。代表团在乘专轮到润河集时，对这个新中国的伟大建设工程备加赞扬。
