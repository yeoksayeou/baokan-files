Title: My Educational Union and Ten Other Organizations Jointly Hold a Symposium to Welcome Representatives of Various Countries to the International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. My Educational Union, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and other ten cultural, educational, medical, scientific, and relief organizations jointly held a symposium on the afternoon of the 30th to welcome representatives from various countries who came to China for visits after attending the Moscow International Economic Conference. The attendees included: Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the Chinese Educational Union; Sha Kefu, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Fu Lianzhang, Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association; Su Jingguan, Chairman of the Chinese Medical Workers' Union; Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China People's Relief Administration; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology; Liang Xi, Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology Popularization; Fan Wenlan, Director of the Federation of Social Science Organizations of China; Fan Changjiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China News Publishing and Printing Union, and others. After Li Dequan delivered the opening remarks, Wu Yuzhang talked about the development and achievements of the People's Republic of China in culture, education, health, and relief in the past two and a half years.

After listening to Wu Yuzhang's report, Chamberlin, Secretary-General of the China Committee for the Promotion of International Trade, was very excited about the achievements of New China in culture and education. He said: After nearly a week of visits in New China, they believe that New China has made tremendous efforts in economic and cultural aspects since liberation and has achieved great success. Chamberlin then discussed the great significance of the International Economic Conference in promoting the development of trade and cultural relations among the people of various countries, and in conclusion, once again expressed warm congratulations on the achievements of China's economic and cultural construction.

The representatives raised many questions about the cultural and educational situation in New China, which were answered by Wu Yunfu, Zeng Zhaolun, Sha Kefu, and Fan Changjiang respectively.

### 我教育工会等十个团体联合举行座谈会  欢迎国际经济会议各国代表

1952-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　我教育工会等十个团体联合举行座谈会
    欢迎国际经济会议各国代表
    【新华社三十日讯】中国教育工会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会等十个文化、教育、医学、科学、救济团体，在三十日下午联合举行座谈会，欢迎出席莫斯科国际经济会议后前来我国参观的各国代表。到会的有：中国教育工会主席吴玉章、中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫、中华医学会总会理事长傅连暲、中国医务工作者工会主席苏井观、中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫、中国红十字会总会会长李德全、中国科学工作者联合会副主席曾昭抡、中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希、中国社会科学各团体联合会主任范文澜、中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会主任范长江等人。座谈会在李德全致开会辞后，由吴玉章谈述了中华人民共和国两年半来，在文化、教育、卫生、救济等方面的发展和成就。
    国际贸易促进委员会秘书长香贝朗在听了吴玉章的报告后，对于新中国文化教育各方面的成就，甚为兴奋。他说：他们在新中国经过将近一星期的参观之后，相信新中国在解放以来，已在经济和文化方面作了非常大的努力，并取得了非常大的成功。香贝朗接着论述了国际经济会议对于促进各国人民间贸易和文化关系的发展的巨大意义，并在结论中再次对中国经济文化建设方面的成就表示热烈的祝贺。
    代表们对于新中国的文教情况提出了许多询问，当由伍云甫、曾昭抡、沙可夫、范长江分别作了答复。
