Title: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government yesterday received delegations from Czechoslovakia, India, and Burma
Author:
Date: 1952-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received yesterday the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, headed by Mr. Kopřiva, along with all members of the delegation. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Kousal, accompanied them.
After the meeting, Mr. Kopřiva, on behalf of President Gottwald of the Czechoslovak Republic, presented Chairman Mao Zedong with a car and a car presentation ceremony was held.
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received yesterday the Indian Cultural Delegation, headed by Mrs. Pandit, along with all members of the delegation. The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. Panikkar, accompanied them.
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received yesterday the Burmese Cultural Delegation, headed by U Thun Pe, along with all members of the delegation. The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Myint Thein, accompanied them.

### 中央人民政府毛泽东主席昨接见捷印缅三国代表团

1952-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府毛泽东主席昨接见捷印缅三国代表团
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席九日接见捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长柯别茨基及全体团员。陪同进见者为捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉。
    接见后，柯别茨基代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国哥特瓦尔德总统赠送毛泽东主席汽车一辆，并举行了赠车仪式。
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席九日接见印度文化代表团团长潘迪特夫人和全体团员。陪同进见者为印度驻华大使潘尼迦。
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席九日接见缅甸文化代表团团长宇吞帕和全体团员。陪同进见者为缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂。
