Title: American Aggressor Troops Frenziedly Unleash Poison Gas Again
Author:
Date: 1952-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th—According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency on the 7th: The American aggressor troops, recklessly violating international law, continue to use poison gas on the Korean front. The areas where they are releasing poison gas are mainly on the western front.

	On April 22nd at 12:00, enemy aircraft dropped a poison gas bomb on the Daewon-dong area, seven kilometers northeast of Kaesong.

	On April 22nd, the enemy fired seven poison gas shells at the Chengnei area along the Imjin River, south of Kaesong.

	From 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM on April 23rd, the enemy fired more than 20 poison gas shells at the Seongsan area west of Cheorwon.

	At 4:30 PM on April 23rd, the enemy fired more than 50 poison gas shells at the northeastern area of Sanggeumgok, five and a half kilometers northwest of Gorangpo-ri.

	On April 25th, the enemy fired seven poison gas shells at areas south of Kaesong, including Sikhyun-ri, Hwangsan, and Seokjeong.

	On the eastern front, the enemy fired 16 poison gas shells at the Yuinsan area northwest of Mundeung-ri.

### 美国侵略军竟又疯狂施放毒气

1952-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美国侵略军竟又疯狂施放毒气
    【新华社平壤十三日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社七日消息：恣意破坏国际公法的美国侵略军继续在朝鲜前线使用毒气。其施放毒气的地区主要是西部战线。
    四月二十二日十二时敌机向开城东北七公里大院洞地区投下毒气弹一枚。
    四月二十二日，敌向开城以南临津江边城内地区发射毒气炮弹七枚。
    四月二十三日上午十时至下午三时之间，敌向铁原以西城山地区发射毒气炮弹二十多枚。
    四月二十三日下午四时半，敌向高浪浦里西偏北五公里半上金谷东北地区发射毒气炮弹五十多枚。
    四月二十五日敌向开城以南食岘里、黄山、石井等地发射毒气炮弹七枚。
    东线方面，敌曾向文登里西北鱼隐山地区发射毒气炮弹十六枚。
