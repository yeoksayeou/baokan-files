Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and Korean People's Delegation to China Arrive in Shenyang Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-05-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 26th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China arrived in Shenyang from Beijing on the 26th.
    Greeting them at the station were: Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Mo Wenhua, Director of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army; Zhu Qiwu, Mayor of Shenyang; as well as representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, troops, and schools, and Young Pioneers, totaling over 700 people. Kim Gui-nam, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China in Shenyang, also went to the station to welcome them.
    The delegates alighted from the train amidst music, slogans, and applause, and received flowers from the Young Pioneers.
    The two delegations will stay in Shenyang for several days to conduct various tours and activities.

### 志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团昨抵沈阳

1952-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团昨抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳二十六日电】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团于二十六日由北京到达沈阳。
    前往车站欢迎的有：东北抗美援朝总分会主席高崇民，人民解放军东北军区政治部主任莫文骅、沈阳市市长朱其文，及各民主党派、各人民团体、部队、学校的代表、少年儿童队员等共七百余人。在沈阳的朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞金贵南亦往车站欢迎。
    代表们在乐声、口号声和掌声中下车，接受了少年儿童队员的献花。
    两代表团将在沈阳逗留数日，进行各项参观活动。
