Title: At the Invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Reverend Johnson, Deputy Vicar of Canterbury, and Others Arrive in Beijing via Prague
Author:
Date: 1952-05-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 15th—At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Reverend Johnson, Deputy Vicar of Canterbury, and his wife, as well as Mr. Day, a lecturer on worker education issues at Oxford University, left London for Prague a few days ago. They departed Prague on the morning of the 14th to visit China and are expected to arrive in Beijing soon.

### 应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问  英坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等经布拉格来京

1952-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问
    英坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊等经布拉格来京
    【新华社布拉格十五日电】英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊和夫人、牛津大学工人教育问题讲师戴伊，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，于日前离开伦敦到布拉格，并于十四日上午离开布拉格前往中国访问，不日即可到达北京。
