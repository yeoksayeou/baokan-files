Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend Soviet "May Day" Celebrations, Farmer and Women Representatives Also Arrive One After Another
Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th - At the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Chinese Trade Union Delegation led by Liu Zihou to the Soviet Union to participate in the "May Day" celebration, the Chinese Farmers' Representative Visiting Group led by Zhang Linchi, and the Chinese delegation to the International Conference for the Defence of Children led by Kang Keqing at the invitation of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee to visit the Soviet Union, have successively arrived in Moscow to participate in the "May Day" celebration.

### 应邀参加苏联“五一”节庆典  我国工会代表团抵达莫斯科  我农民、妇女代表亦陆续到达

1952-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　应邀参加苏联“五一”节庆典
    我国工会代表团抵达莫斯科
    我农民、妇女代表亦陆续到达
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】由刘子久率领的应全苏工会中央理事会邀请赴苏联参加“五一”节庆祝典礼的我国工会代表团，由张林池率领的我国农民代表参观团、由康克清率领的应苏联妇女反法西斯委员会邀请访问苏联的我国出席国际保卫儿童大会的代表团，已陆续抵达莫斯科，参加“五一”节庆祝典礼。
