Title: The Communist Party of Israel Convenes its Twelfth National Congress; The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1952-05-29
Page: 1

　　[This News Report] The Communist Party of Israel convened its Twelfth National Congress on May 29th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory telegram. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel and to all the delegates of the Twelfth National Congress of the Communist Party of Israel, Comrades: We deeply regret that we are unable to send a delegation to attend the Twelfth National Congress of the Communist Party of Israel. We wish the conference complete success and wish the people of Israel, under the leadership of the Communist Party of Israel, victory in their struggle for national independence, democracy, and freedom, against the plans of the American warmongers to establish an aggressive Middle East bloc, and in defense of world peace.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    May 28, 1952

### 以色列共产党召开第十二次代表大会  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    以色列共产党召开第十二次代表大会
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
　  【本报讯】五月二十九日以色列共产党召开第十二次代表大会，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：以色列共产党中央委员会并转以色列共产党第十二次代表大会全体代表同志们：欣逢以色列共产党召开第十二次代表大会，我们未能派代表参加，深以为歉。谨祝大会圆满成功，并祝以色列人民在以色列共产党领导下，在争取民族独立、民主、自由，反对美国战争贩子建立中东侵略集团计划，保卫世界和平的斗争中获得胜利。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年五月二十八日
