Title: Indonesian Islamic Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Indonesian Islamic Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th. (The delegation's journey was delayed because the British government in Hong Kong obstructed their transit visas.) Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office, Zhang Xiuzhu, Deputy Director of the Department of Culture and Education, and Zhao Guoyou, a well-known national model worker, went to the airport to welcome them.

List of the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union Delegation:
	Head of Delegation: Mochtar Kachaeli, Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union
	Member: Sunaldo, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union
	Harun Rasid, Head of the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union's Press Office

### 印尼回教工会代表团昨抵达北京

1952-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼回教工会代表团昨抵达北京
    【新华社八日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国参加“五一”节观礼的印度尼西亚回教工会代表团一行三人，于八日下午乘飞机到达北京。（该团因中途被香港英国政府阻搁过境签证，以致旅途迟滞。）前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、办公厅主任栗再温、文教部副部长张修竹和全国著名劳动模范赵国有等人。
    印尼回教工会代表团名单
    团长    莫区塔尔·卡查利    印尼回教工会副主席
    团员    苏拿尔多印尼回教工会总书记
    哈伦·拉西德印尼回教工会新闻处长
