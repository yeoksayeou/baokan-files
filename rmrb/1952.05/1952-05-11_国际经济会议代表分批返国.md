Title: International Economic Conference Delegates Return Home in Batches
Author:
Date: 1952-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 10th: A group of thirteen delegates from Argentina, Canada, Chile, Venezuela, and Burma, who attended the Moscow International Economic Conference and then visited China, arrived in Guangzhou by train on the 9th and left Guangzhou for Hong Kong that evening to return to their countries. Among those who saw them off at the station were Deng Wenzhao, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce; Lin Zhicheng, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce; and Chen Junleng, Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association.

Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: A group of three people including Australian representative Hoffman, British representative Wayne, and Ceylon representative Wanigathunga, who attended the Moscow International Economic Conference and then visited China, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 10th to return to their countries. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Ji Chao Ding, a member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xiang Shuxiang, a member of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee; Sun Xiaocun, Standing Committee member of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union; and Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, and many others.

### 国际经济会议代表分批返国

1952-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    国际经济会议代表分批返国
    【新华社广州十日电】出席莫斯科国际经济会议后来我国参观的阿根廷、加拿大、智利、委内瑞拉和缅甸等国代表一行十三人，在九日乘车抵达广州，并在当晚离广州转道香港回国。到车站送行的有：广东省工商业联合会筹备会主任委员邓文钊、广州市工商业联合会筹备会主任委员林志澄、民主建国会广州市分会主任委员陈君冷等。
    【新华社十日讯】出席莫斯科国际经济会议后来我国参观的澳大利亚代表霍夫曼、英国代表韦恩、锡兰代表瓦尼加通加等一行三人，在十日上午搭飞机离北京回国。到机场欢送的有：国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔、民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓村、中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫、北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭等多人。
