Title: Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe Issues Statement Protesting US Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 4

    Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe Issues Statement
    Protesting US Germ Warfare
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe has issued a statement protesting the US's barbaric germ warfare. The full text of the statement is as follows:

    We, the members of the Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe, after two months of touring performances in China and having seen the exhibition of the US imperialists' germ warfare crimes, protest the crime of attempting to eliminate tens of thousands of peaceful people through inhuman methods; we protest the violation of international treaties and despise those who use scientific achievements and human wisdom to eliminate millions of people.

    We protest germ warfare!

### 匈国家人民文工团发表声明  抗议美国进行细菌战

1952-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    匈国家人民文工团发表声明
    抗议美国进行细菌战
    【本报讯】匈牙利国家人民文工团顷发表声明，抗议美国进行细菌战的暴行，声明全文如下：
    我们匈牙利国家人民文工团团员，经过在中国两个月的巡回演出，看过了美帝国主义细菌战罪行展览会后，我们抗议使用非人的方法，企图消灭成千成万和平人民的罪行；我们抗议对于国际条约的违反，唾弃那些利用科学成就与人类智慧来消灭千百万人们的人。
    我们抗议细菌战争！
