Title: Head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation and Burmese Ambassador to China Host Reception for Heads of Government Departments and Figures from Beijing's Cultural Circles
Author:
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, U Tun Pe, and the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, held a reception on the evening of the 5th to host heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and figures from Beijing's cultural circles.

Those invited to the reception included: Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Vice Ministers Zhou Yang and Ding Xilin; Li Dequan, Minister of the Ministry of Health; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wei Que, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education; Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Trade; as well as other heads of various ministries of the Central People's Government and cultural figures Zhang Xiruo, Xing Xiping, Hu Yuzhi, Li Siguang, Yu Xinqing, Fan Changjiang, Chen Jiakang, Ma Yinchu, Hong Shen, Zheng Zhenduo, Ouyang Yuqian, and Li Yimang, Deputy Head of our country's former cultural delegation to India and Burma, totaling over one hundred people.

Also attending the reception were: Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin; Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko; Mongolian Ambassador to China, Jalkhasaikhan; Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szafrankó; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov; Pakistani Ambassador to China, Rochar; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy, Isa Matti; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation, Zhou Liang; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation, Meisztrup; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy, Beven; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation, Brunstandt; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy, Gula; Counselor of the Indian Embassy, Gore; First Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy, Rejgle, as well as the Dutch government's negotiation representative, Van der Peppell, and the Norwegian government's negotiation representative, Goran.

Also attending the reception were the Indian Cultural Delegation and Indian trade union representatives attending the "May Day" celebrations.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸文化代表团团长和缅甸驻华大使  招待我政府各部门首长和北京文化界人士

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团团长和缅甸驻华大使
    招待我政府各部门首长和北京文化界人士
    【新华社五日讯】缅甸文化代表团团长宇吞帕和缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂于五日晚举行酒会，招待我中央人民政府各部门首长及北京文化界人士。
    应邀参加招待会的有：中央人民政府副主席李济深、中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培、中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长周扬、丁西林、卫生部部长李德全、外交部副部长章汉夫、教育部副部长韦悫、贸易部副部长姚依林以及中央人民政府各部其他负责人和文化界人士张奚若、邢西萍、胡愈之、李四光、余心清、范长江、陈家康、马寅初、洪深、郑振铎、欧阳予倩及我国前赴印缅文化代表团副团长李一氓等一百余人。
    参加招待会的还有：苏联驻华大使罗申、罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科、蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫、巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、越南代表团临时代办周亮、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡、芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特、波兰大使馆临时代办古拉、印度大使馆参赞高尔、捷克斯洛伐克大使馆一等秘书赖格勒，以及荷兰政府谈判代表范德普、挪威政府谈判代表高兰。
    参加招待会的还有印度文化代表团以及参加“五一”节观礼的印度工会代表。
    招待会在友好的空气中进行。
