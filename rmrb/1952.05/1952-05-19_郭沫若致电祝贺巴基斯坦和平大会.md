Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Pakistan Peace Conference
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1952-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th:

    Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Pakistan Peace Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, 18th] The Pakistan Peace Conference will be held soon. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram of congratulations, the text of which is as follows: Pakistan Peace Conference:
    On behalf of the Chinese people, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the Pakistan Peace Conference. At present, the U.S. government's interference in the internal affairs of Asian countries has even developed to the stage of using bacteriological weapons. The development of the Pakistan people's movement to defend peace will not only contribute to peace in Asia but also to world peace. We are willing to work closely with the peace-loving people of Pakistan to fight against bacteriological warfare and defend peace in Asia and the world. I wish the conference success.
     Guo Moruo, May 18

### 郭沫若致电祝贺巴基斯坦和平大会
郭沫若
1952-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若致电祝贺巴基斯坦和平大会
    【新华社十八日讯】巴基斯坦和平大会将于日内举行，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若特致贺电庆祝，电文如下：巴基斯坦和平大会：
    我代表中国人民谨向巴基斯坦和平大会致以热诚的祝贺。目前美国政府干涉亚洲国家内政，竟发展到使用细菌武器的阶段。巴基斯坦人民保卫和平运动的开展，不但有助于亚洲和平，并且有助于世界和平。我们愿与巴基斯坦爱好和平的人民紧密携手，为反对细菌战争、保卫亚洲及世界和平而共同奋斗。祝大会成功。
     郭沫若  五月十八日
