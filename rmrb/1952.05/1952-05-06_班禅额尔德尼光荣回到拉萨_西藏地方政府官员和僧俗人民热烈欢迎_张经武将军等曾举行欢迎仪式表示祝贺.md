Title: Panchen Erdeni Returns Gloriously to Lhasa; Local Tibetan Government Officials and Monks and Lay People Warmly Welcome Him; General Zhang Jingwu and Others Held a Welcome Ceremony to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 5th: Panchen Erdeni and all personnel of his entourage arrived in Lhasa, the seat of the Tibetan local government, on April 28th. Upon their arrival in Lhasa, officials at all levels of the Tibetan local government, the Tibetan local army, lamas from the three major monasteries, monks and lay people of Lhasa, as well as General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region, and more than 20,000 people from the Lhasa organs and troops of the People's Liberation Army, welcomed them in the eastern suburbs of Lhasa.

April 28th was filled with a festive atmosphere in Lhasa. The people of Lhasa, who had long yearned for Panchen Erdeni to return to Tibet, put on new clothes for the holiday. They cleaned the streets very thoroughly, and large banners reading "Support the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni to Unite Closely and Implement the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet" were hung in the city. Representatives of the three major monasteries and officials at all levels of the Tibetan local government paid respects to Panchen Erdeni with extremely grand ceremonies and presented him with Hada scarves.

General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region, and other leaders of the Tibet Military Region held a welcome ceremony for Panchen Erdeni. In their welcoming speeches, Generals Zhang Jingwu and Zhang Guohua expressed their condolences and congratulations to Panchen Erdeni and all personnel of his entourage for crossing snow-capped mountains and grasslands and gloriously returning to Lhasa in order to implement the agreement and liberate the Tibetan people. In his reply, Panchen Erdeni first expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Chairman Mao, the Central People's Government, the Northwest and Southwest Military and Political Committees, Representative Zhang Jingwu of the Central People's Government, and the Tibet Military Region for their care, and expressed his gratitude to the Dalai Lama and all levels of the Tibetan local government for their assistance during his return to Tibet. He then said: "In the future, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, we must unite closely with the Dalai Lama, further strengthen the unity with all fraternal ethnic groups in the country and within the Tibetan ethnic group, and strive to implement the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, build a new Tibet, and consolidate the southwest national defense of the motherland."

### 班禅额尔德尼光荣回到拉萨  西藏地方政府官员和僧俗人民热烈欢迎  张经武将军等曾举行欢迎仪式表示祝贺

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼光荣回到拉萨
    西藏地方政府官员和僧俗人民热烈欢迎
    张经武将军等曾举行欢迎仪式表示祝贺
    【新华社拉萨五日电】班禅额尔德尼及其行辕全体人员，已在四月二十八日抵达西藏地方政府所在地——拉萨。他们到达拉萨时，西藏地方政府各级官员、西藏地方部队、三大寺喇嘛、拉萨市僧俗人民，和中央人民政府代表张经武将军、西藏军区司令员张国华将军，以及人民解放军驻拉萨机关、部队等二万多人，都在拉萨市东郊欢迎。
    四月二十八日，拉萨市充满了节日气象。久已渴望班禅额尔德尼回藏的拉萨人民，都换上了过节日的新衣，他们把街道打扫得非常干净，市内挂着“拥护达赖喇嘛与班禅额尔德尼亲密团结起来实现关于和平解放西藏办法的协议”等大幅标语。三大寺代表和西藏地方政府各级官员以极其隆重的仪节，拜见了班禅额尔德尼，并向他敬献哈达。
    中央人民政府代表张经武将军、西藏军区司令员张国华将军和西藏军区各首长曾为班禅额尔德尼举行了欢迎仪式。张经武、张国华两将军在致欢迎词中，对班禅额尔德尼及其行辕全体人员为了执行协议，为了西藏民族的解放，翻越雪山草地，光荣地回到拉萨，表示慰问和祝贺。班禅额尔德尼致答词时，首先表示衷心感谢毛主席、中央人民政府、西北、西南军政委员会、中央人民政府张经武代表和西藏军区的关怀，并对达赖喇嘛和西藏地方各级政府在他回藏途中给予帮助，表示感谢。他接着说：“今后要在毛主席、中央人民政府领导下，和达赖喇嘛亲密团结，进一步加强与国内各兄弟民族和西藏民族内部的团结，为实现关于和平解放西藏办法的协议，建设新西藏，巩固祖国西南国防而努力。”
