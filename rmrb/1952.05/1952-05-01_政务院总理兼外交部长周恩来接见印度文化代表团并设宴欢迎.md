Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Receives Indian Cultural Delegation and Hosts a Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government received the Indian Cultural Delegation headed by Mrs. Pandit on the evening of the 30th and hosted a banquet to welcome them.

    The delegation was accompanied by Indian Ambassador to China, K.M. Panikkar. Present were Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, and Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing.

    Among those present at the banquet were: Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Chen Yun and Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council Lu Dingyi, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister Ding Xilin, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister Su Jinguan, Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Sha Qianli, Minister of Justice Shi Liang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Education Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, President of Peking University Ma Yinchu, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, writers Ding Ling and Lao She, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, as well as our Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian, Deputy Head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to India and Burma Li Yimang, and others, totaling eighty people.

    Indian Ambassador to China K.M. Panikkar and diplomatic personnel of the embassy were also invited to attend the banquet.

    During the banquet, the hosts and guests toasted the friendship between the Chinese and Indian people, and the happiness of the two peoples.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. (Attached picture)

### 政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来接见印度文化代表团并设宴欢迎

1952-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来接见印度文化代表团并设宴欢迎
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在三十日晚接见并设宴欢迎以潘迪特夫人为首的印度文化代表团。
    接见时代表团由印度驻华大使潘尼迦陪同进见。在座的有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部亚洲司代司长陈家康、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清。
    出席宴会作陪的有：政务院副总理陈云、郭沫若，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长丁西林，卫生部部长李德全、副部长苏井观，贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长沙千里，司法部部长史良，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，教育部副部长曾昭抡，中国科学院副院长李四光、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，北京大学校长马寅初，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，作家丁玲、老舍，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及我国驻印度大使袁仲贤和我国前赴印缅文化代表团副团长李一氓等八十人。
    印度驻华大使潘尼迦及大使馆外交人员也应邀参加宴会。
    席间，宾主为中印两国人民的友谊，为两国人民的幸福而干杯。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。（附图片）
