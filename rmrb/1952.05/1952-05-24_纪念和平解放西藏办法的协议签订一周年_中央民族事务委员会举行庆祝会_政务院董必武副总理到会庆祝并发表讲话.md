Title: Central Nationalities Affairs Commission Holds Celebration Meeting to Commemorate the First Anniversary of the Signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet; Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu Attends and Addresses the Meeting

Author:

Date: 1952-05-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission held a celebration meeting on the afternoon of the 23rd to commemorate the first anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. The meeting was attended by Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Li Siguang, Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, Vice Minister of Water Resources Li Baohua, Vice Minister of Communications Ji Fang, Deputy Director of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Geping, all committee members in Beijing, and representatives from the Ministry of Agriculture and the Ministry of Culture. Also attending the celebration meeting were Xia Ricang Living Buddha from Tongren County, Qinghai, Acting Director Luo Wang and Deputy Director Lian Huizhu of the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office and all cadres, Tibetan people in Beijing, and Tibetan students from the Central Institute for Nationalities, totaling more than 400 people. The meeting was presided over by Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission.

Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu especially attended the meeting to celebrate and delivered a speech (full text to be published separately). This was followed by speeches from Ye Jizhuang, Li Dequan, and Li Siguang. Ye Jizhuang said: "After the liberation of Tibet, state-owned trading organizations are beginning to take various effective economic measures to gradually improve the lives of the Tibetan people. In the future, we will continue to implement the instructions of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government and strive to help the restoration and development of the economic undertakings of the Tibetan people." In their speeches, Li Dequan and Li Siguang also pointed out that the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will resolutely follow Chairman Mao's instructions and continue to help the Tibetan people build a happy new life.

Also speaking at the meeting were Xia Ricang Living Buddha and Luo Wang, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office. Xia Ricang said: The Central People's Government, in order to help the Tibetan people expel the aggressive forces of imperialism and defend the national defense of the motherland, sent the People's Liberation Army and working cadres into Tibet, and from then on, the Tibetan people were liberated. This is a victory for all the Tibetan people of China. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Tibetan people will unite even more closely with the people of all nationalities in the country and strive to develop the political, economic, and cultural undertakings of the Tibetan region and build a great motherland. Luo Wang said: Under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, Tibet has been peacefully liberated, and the Panchen Lama, who had been away from Tibet for a long time, has also gloriously returned to Tibet, and is united with the Dalai Lama to jointly implement the agreement for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. ### 纪念和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年  中央民族事务委员会举行庆祝会  政务院董必武副总理到会庆祝并发表讲话

1952-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年
    中央民族事务委员会举行庆祝会
    政务院董必武副总理到会庆祝并发表讲话
    【新华社二十三日讯】为纪念和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年，中央人民政府民族事务委员会在二十三日下午举行庆祝会。参加大会的有中央贸易部叶季壮部长、中央卫生部李德全部长、中国科学院李四光副院长、中央教育部韦悫副部长、中央水利部李葆华副部长、中央交通部季方副部长、中央民族事务委员会刘格平副主任委员和全体在京委员及中央农业部和文化部的代表。参加庆祝会的还有青海同仁县夏日仓活佛、班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺、副处长联慧珠和全体干部以及在京藏族人士及中央民族学院藏族学员等，共四百多人。大会由中央民族事务委员会刘格平副主任委员主持。
    政务院董必武副总理特到会庆祝，并发表讲话（全文另发）。继由叶季壮、李德全和李四光讲话。叶季壮说：“西藏解放后，国营贸易机构正在开始采取各种有效的经济措施，以期逐渐改善西藏人民的生活。今后将继续贯彻毛主席和中央人民政府的指示，努力帮助西藏人民经济事业的恢复和发展。”李德全、李四光在讲话中也指出：中央人民政府卫生部和中国科学院将坚决遵照毛主席的指示，继续帮助西藏人民建设幸福的新生活。
    在会上讲话的还有夏日仓活佛和班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺。夏日仓说：中央人民政府为了帮助西藏人民驱逐帝国主义侵略势力，保卫祖国的国防，派遣了人民解放军和工作干部进入西藏，从此西藏人民获得了解放。这是中国全体藏族人民的胜利。在中国共产党和毛主席领导下，西藏人民将与国内各民族人民益加紧密地团结起来，为发展西藏地区的政治、经济、文化事业和建设伟大的祖国而努力。罗旺说：在中国共产党和毛主席英明领导下，西藏得到了和平解放，久离西藏的班禅也已光荣地返回西藏，并且和达赖喇嘛团结一致，共同实现和平解放西藏办法的协议。西藏人民在中国共产党和中央人民政府领导下，在先进兄弟民族的大力帮助下，将努力建设新的西藏。
    大会在热烈的掌声中结束。会后并举行聚餐和庆祝晚会。
