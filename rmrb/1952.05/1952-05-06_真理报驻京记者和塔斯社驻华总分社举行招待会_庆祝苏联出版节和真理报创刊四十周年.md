Title: Truth Daily's Beijing Correspondent and TASS China Branch Office Hold Reception Celebrating Soviet Publishing Festival and the 40th Anniversary of the Truth Daily's Founding
Author:
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. The Truth Daily's Beijing correspondent and the TASS China Branch Office held a reception in Beijing to celebrate the Soviet Bolshevik Publishing Festival and the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Truth Daily.

Those invited to attend were: Chen Boda, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee; Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Publishing Administration; Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Cultural and Educational Affairs; Fan Changjiang, President of the People's Daily; Wu Lengxi, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency; writers Liu Baiyu and Zhou Libo; and more than 150 representatives from China's news, publishing, and literary circles. Also present were personnel from the embassies of various brother countries in China and progressive journalists from various countries in Beijing.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin, spoke at the reception, congratulating the Truth Daily on better serving the working people of the world and wishing health to the publishing workers of China and the Soviet Union and the guests present.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 真理报驻京记者和塔斯社驻华总分社举行招待会  庆祝苏联出版节和真理报创刊四十周年

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    真理报驻京记者和塔斯社驻华总分社举行招待会
    庆祝苏联出版节和真理报创刊四十周年
    【新华社五日讯】五月五日是苏联布尔什维克出版节，又是真理报创刊四十周年纪念。真理报驻北京记者和塔斯社驻华总分社特在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中共中央宣传部副部长陈伯达、中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、出版总署署长胡愈之、中国人民大学校长吴玉章、中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞、外交部情报司司长龚澎、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、人民日报社社长范长江、新华通讯社副社长吴冷西、作家刘白羽、周立波以及我国新闻、出版、文艺界代表等一百五十多人，到会的还有各兄弟国家驻华使馆人员和在北京的各国进步记者。
    苏联驻华大使罗申曾在招待会上讲话，祝贺真理报更好地为世界劳动人民服务。并祝中苏两国出版工作者及到会来宾们健康。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
