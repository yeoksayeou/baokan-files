Author:
Date: 1952-05-12

    Our Great Motherland
    Cultural and educational undertakings in the minority nationality areas of our country have greatly developed. In the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, 3,774 primary schools have been established, with 307,935 students, accounting for more than 60 percent of the total number of school-age children in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the vast majority of whom are children of farmers and herdsmen. This is a picture of students attending class at the Tongliao County Senior Primary School in Jirem League.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国少数民族地区的文化教育有了很大的发展。内蒙古自治区设立了三千七百七十四所小学，有学生三十万七千九百三十五人，占内蒙古自治医学龄儿童总数的百分之六十以上，其中绝大多数是农民和牧民的子弟。这是哲里木盟通辽县高级小学学生正在上课的情形。
