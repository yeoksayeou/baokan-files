Title: Directory of Confessions of American POWs Enoch and Quinn
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: American air force navigator Enoch and pilot Quinn, captured by the Chinese People's Volunteers on the Korean battlefield, have personally written the following confessions, admitting the crime of the United States launching bacteriological warfare. These confessions are:

    I. The Truth About How American Imperialism Launched Bacteriological Warfare (Confession of American POW Enoch);
    II. Open Letter from American POW Enoch to the Chinese People's Volunteers;
    III. The Circumstances Under Which I Was Forced to Participate in the Inhumane Bacteriological Warfare Launched by Wall Street in the United States (Confession of American POW Quinn);
    IV. How I Was Forced to Participate in the Bacteriological Warfare Launched by Wall Street in the United States (Radio Broadcast by American POW Quinn).
    (The above confessions can be found on pages four, five, and six of today's newspaper)

### 美俘伊纳克和奎恩的供词目录

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美俘伊纳克和奎恩的供词目录
    【新华社五日讯】在朝鲜战场上为中国人民志愿军俘获的美国空军领航员伊纳克和驾驶员奎恩，已亲笔写了下列的供词，供认了美国发动细菌战的罪行。这些供词是：
    一、美国帝国主义如何发动细菌战争的真相（美国战俘伊纳克的供词）；
    二、美国战俘伊纳克给中国人民志愿军的公开信；
    三、我被迫参加美国华尔街发动的非人道的细菌战的经过（美国战俘奎恩的供词）；
    四、我如何被迫参加美国华尔街发动的细菌战争（美国战俘奎恩的广播稿）。
    （以上供词见今日本报四、五、六版）
