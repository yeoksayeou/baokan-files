Title: Canterbury's Vice-Dean Johnson and Others Go to Hankou
Author:
Date: 1952-05-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Canterbury's Vice-Dean Johnson and his wife, and Oxford University Workers' Education lecturer Day, who came to China for a visit, have visited various circles, schools, and historical sites since arriving in Beijing on the 20th. On the 30th, they left Beijing for Hankou, accompanied by Xu Ying from the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and will visit various parts of China. Those who saw them off at the station included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhang Xiruo and Wu Yaozong, Standing Committee Members, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, and Wang Zizhong, Chairman of the Beijing Christian Organizations for World Peace.
    On the eve of Johnson and his wife's departure from Beijing, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, held a grand banquet to welcome them. During the banquet, the guests and hosts had a pleasant conversation, and the banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 约翰逊副主教等赴汉口

1952-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    约翰逊副主教等赴汉口
    【新华社三十日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国参观的英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊及其夫人、牛津大学工人教育问题讲师戴伊自二十日到达北京后，连日访问各界、参观学校和名胜古迹，已于三十日由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部徐盈陪同离京赴汉口，将到我国各地参观访问。到车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，常务委员张奚若、吴耀宗，秘书长刘贯一，北京基督教团体保卫世界和平委员会主席王梓仲等。
    约翰逊及其夫人等离京前夕，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席彭真曾举行盛大宴会欢迎。席间宾主畅谈甚欢，宴会始终在友好的气氛中进行。
