Title: National Federation of Literary and Art Circles Holds Symposium with Hungarian National Folk Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1952-05-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a symposium with the Hungarian National Folk Ensemble this morning to discuss issues related to Chinese literature and art. Present at the meeting were: Gyula Sebestyén, leader of the Hungarian National Folk Ensemble, and 29 artistic cadres from the ensemble; Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Sha Kefu, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee members Li Bozhao, Hong Shen, and Committee members Lü Ji, Zhang Geng, Wu Xiaobang, and others. At the meeting, Zhou Yang gave a report on Chinese literature and art. He first described the development of Chinese literature and art since the May Fourth Movement, and pointed out the great influence of Chairman Mao's speech at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art in 1942 on the development of Chinese literature and art. Then, Zhou Yang answered some questions raised by the members of the folk ensemble. Zhou Yang's report attracted great attention from the members of the Hungarian National Folk Ensemble. Finally, Gyula Sebestyén, leader of the ensemble, delivered a speech expressing gratitude. The symposium will continue on the 17th, where members of the Hungarian National Folk Ensemble and Chinese literary and art workers will hold group discussions on music, dance, and other issues.

### 全国文联与匈牙利文工团举行座谈会

1952-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文联与匈牙利文工团举行座谈会
    【新华社十五日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会在十五日上午和匈牙利国家人民文工团举行座谈会，讨论中国文学艺术方面的问题。到会的有：匈牙利国家人民文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊和文工团艺术干部共二十九人；中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，秘书长沙可夫，常务委员李伯钊、洪深，委员吕骥、张庚、吴晓邦等。会上由周扬作了关于中国文学艺术问题的报告。他首先叙述了五四运动以来中国文学艺术的发展情况，指出了毛主席在一九四二年在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话对于中国文学艺术发展的伟大影响。接着周扬答复了文工团团员们所提出的一些问题。周扬的报告引起了匈牙利国家人民文工团团员们很大的注意。最后由文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊致词表示感谢。座谈会将在十七日继续举行，由匈牙利国家人民文工团团员和我国文艺工作者就音乐、舞蹈等问题进行分组讨论。
