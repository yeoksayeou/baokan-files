Title: Capital Holds Children's Day Celebration and Welcomes Delegation to International Conference for the Defense of Children
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: The capital held a celebration of "June 1st" International Children's Day and a welcoming ceremony for the delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children on the 30th. Present at the meeting were all members of the Chinese delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children, as well as Honorary Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation He Xiangning, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children Li Dequan, and child care workers, primary school teachers, and cadres from central and Beijing organizations, totaling approximately 6,000 people.

Foreign guests invited to the conference included: representatives of delegations from Vietnam, Burma, Australia, and other countries who came to China for visits after attending the International Conference for the Defense of Children, as well as Chief Delegate of the Korean delegation to the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Park Jung-ai, Mongolian representative Bazar Shirindib, Deputy Chief Delegate of the Indonesian delegation Suwati, Australian representative Ada Bronham, and others.

The meeting was opened by Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children. In her opening remarks, she first extended warm congratulations to children across the country and around the world, and extended her regards and thanks to the members of the Chinese delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children and the representatives of delegations from Vietnam, Burma, Australia, and other countries. She also expressed a warm welcome to the delegations from various countries attending the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. She said: The victory of international child protection is the victory of peace. All people and mothers who care about children should take this year's "June 1st" as a day of peace demonstration to show the strength of Asian people who love peace and oppose war. She concluded by saying that caring for children and opposing war is the common responsibility of all peace-loving people.

Subsequently, Liu Shi, Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children, reported on the proceedings and achievements of the International Conference for the Defense of Children held in Vienna in April, and discussed how to implement the resolutions of the conference in China.

Foreign guests who spoke at the meeting included: Head of the Vietnamese delegation to the International Conference for the Defense of Children, He Gui, Burmese representative Du Qingna, Head of the Australian delegation, Madeline Gans Terrell, and Deputy Chief Delegate of the Indonesian delegation to the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Suwati. In their speeches, they unanimously praised the happy lives of children in New China.

The Chief Delegate of the Korean delegation to the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Park Jung-ai, stood up and shouted "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people!"

### 首都举行庆祝儿童节大会  并欢迎我出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团

1952-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    首都举行庆祝儿童节大会
    并欢迎我出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团
    【新华社三十日讯】首都庆祝“六一”国际儿童节、欢迎出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团大会，在三十日举行。到会的有我国出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团全体人员，还有中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席李德全和中央及北京市的儿童保育工作者、小学教师、机关干部等共约六千人。
    应邀参加大会的外宾有：出席国际保卫儿童大会后来我国参观的越南、缅甸、澳大利亚等国代表团的代表，和出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的朝鲜代表团首席代表朴正爱、蒙古代表巴扎尔·锡林迪布、印度尼西亚代表团副首席代表苏瓦蒂、澳大利亚代表亚达·布龙汉等。
    大会由中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席邓颖超致开会词。她在致词中首先向全国儿童和全世界儿童致以热烈的庆祝，并对出席国际保卫儿童大会的我国代表团人员和越南、缅甸、澳大利亚等国代表团的代表致以慰劳和谢意，她还对出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各国代表团表示了热烈的欢迎。她说：国际保卫儿童的胜利就是保卫和平的胜利。所有关心儿童的人们及母亲们都要把今年的“六一”作为和平示威运动的日子，以表示亚洲爱好和平反对战争的力量。她最后说，爱护儿童反对战争是所有爱好和平的人们的共同责任。
    随后，我国出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团副团长柳湜在会上报告了四月间在维也纳举行的国际保卫儿童大会的经过及其成就，并论及如何在我国贯彻执行会议的决议。
    外宾们在会上讲话的有：出席国际保卫儿童大会的越南代表团团长荷桂、缅甸代表都庆纳、澳大利亚代表团团长麦德林·甘司特尔和出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的印度尼西亚代表团副首席代表苏瓦蒂。她们在讲话中一致赞扬新中国儿童们的幸福生活。
    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的朝鲜代表团首席代表朴正爱曾起立为中朝人民友谊万岁而高呼！
