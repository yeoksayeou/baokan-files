Title: Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union Sends a Cable to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League to Congratulate "May Fourth" Youth Day

Author: 
Date: 1952-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union sent a cable on the 3rd to the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China to celebrate China's "May Fourth" Youth Day. The text of the cable is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China:
On the occasion of the May Fourth Chinese Youth Day, the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union extends brotherly greetings to the glorious Chinese youth and their vanguard, the New Democratic Youth League, and wishes the Chinese youth new achievements in the work of building the People's Republic of China. Long live the unbreakable, brotherly friendship between the youth of China and the Soviet Union!
Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, May 3rd

### 苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会  致电我青年团中央委员会祝贺“五四”青年节

1952-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会
    致电我青年团中央委员会祝贺“五四”青年节
    【新华社四日讯】苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会在三日致电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会庆贺我国“五四”青年节，电文如下：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会：
    值此五四中国青年节，苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会向光荣的中国青年及其先锋新民主主义青年团致以兄弟般的敬礼，敬祝中国青年在建设中华人民共和国的工作中获得新的成就。中苏两国青年兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会  五月三日
