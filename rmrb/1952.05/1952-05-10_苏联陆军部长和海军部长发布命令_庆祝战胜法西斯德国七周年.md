Title: Soviet Army Minister and Navy Minister Issue Orders Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Victory over Fascist Germany
Author:
Date: 1952-05-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: TASS, Moscow, May 9th: The Soviet Army Minister issued the following order on May 9th: Comrades Soldiers and Sergeants of the Army! Comrades Officers and Generals!

Seven years ago, under the leadership of the Communist Party and our wise leader, the great strategist Comrade Stalin, the Soviet people and their armed forces victoriously concluded the Great Patriotic War, winning a complete victory over the Hitlerite aggressors who invaded our motherland's freedom and independence, and rescuing the people of Europe from the horrors of fascist enslavement.

On this Victory Day, I extend my salute and congratulations to all personnel of the Soviet Army.

In commemoration of Victory Day, I order: Today—May 9th, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the various union republics, in Kaliningrad, Lvov, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, a salute of thirty guns shall be fired to celebrate.

Long live the Soviet people and their glorious army!

Long live the Soviet government!

Long live the Communist vanguard of the Soviet people, tempered in battle, the inspirer and organizer of our victories, the great party of Lenin and Stalin!

Long live our great leader and teacher, the genius strategist, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Comrade Stalin!

Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed their lives in the struggle to defend the freedom and independence of our motherland!
                Soviet Army Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Vasilevsky

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Navy Minister issued the following order on May 9th: Comrades Sailors and Sergeants of the Navy! Comrades Naval Officers and Generals!

Today is the seventh anniversary of the victorious conclusion of the Great Patriotic War against German fascist aggression. Under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party and our wise leader, the genius commander Comrade Stalin, the Soviet people and their armed forces won a victory of world-historic significance over German imperialism, safeguarding the freedom and independence of our socialist motherland, and rescuing the people of Europe from fascist enslavement.

On this occasion of the seventh anniversary of the great victory over fascist Germany, I extend my respects and congratulations to you.

Long live the heroic Soviet people and their brave Navy!

Long live our Soviet government!

Long live the inspirer and organizer of our victories—the wise party of Lenin and Stalin!

Long live our beloved leader, the genius commander, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Comrade Stalin!

Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed their lives in the struggle for the freedom and independence of our motherland!
               Soviet Navy Minister, Vice-Admiral Kuznetsov

### 苏联陆军部长和海军部长发布命令  庆祝战胜法西斯德国七周年

1952-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联陆军部长和海军部长发布命令
    庆祝战胜法西斯德国七周年
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联陆军部长于五月九日发布命令如下：陆军战士与士官同志们！校尉与将军同志们！
    七年以前，苏联人民和他们的武装部队在共产党和我们的英明领袖、伟大战略家斯大林同志的领导之下，胜利地结束了伟大的卫国战争，赢得对侵犯我们祖国自由与独立的希特勒侵略军的完全胜利，并把欧洲各国人民从法西斯奴役的恐怖下拯救出来。
    值此胜利日，我谨向苏联陆军全体人员致敬并致贺。
    为了纪念胜利日，我命令：今天——五月九日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫，以及在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨诸英雄城市，一律鸣放礼炮三十响，以示庆祝。
    苏联人民和他们的光荣的陆军万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联人民在战斗中百炼成钢的先锋队、我们的胜利的鼓舞者与组织者、伟大的列宁与斯大林的党万岁！
    我们的伟大领袖和导师、天才战略家、苏联大元帅斯大林同志万岁！
    在保卫我们祖国的自由与独立的战斗中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
                苏联陆军部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部长于五月九日发布命令如下：海军战士与士官同志们！海军校尉与将军同志们！
    今天是反对德国法西斯侵略的伟大卫国战争胜利结束的七周年纪念日。苏联人民和他们的武装部队，在光荣的共产党和我们英明的领袖、天才的统帅斯大林同志领导之下，对德国帝国主义赢得了具有世界历史意义的胜利，维护了我们社会主义祖国的自由和独立，并把欧洲各国人民从法西斯奴役下拯救了出来。
    值此战胜法西斯德国伟大胜利七周年纪念之际，我谨向你们致敬和祝贺。
    英雄的苏联人民和他们勇敢的海军万岁！
    我们的苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——英明的列宁与斯大林的党万岁！
    我们亲爱的领袖、天才的统帅、苏联大元帅斯大林同志万岁！
    在为我们祖国的自由和独立的战斗中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
               苏联海军部长、海军中将库兹聂佐夫
