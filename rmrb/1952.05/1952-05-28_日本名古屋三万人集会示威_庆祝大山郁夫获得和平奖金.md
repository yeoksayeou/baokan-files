Title: Nagoya, Japan: 30,000 People Rally and Demonstrate to Celebrate Ikua Oyama Receiving the Peace Prize

Author:
Date: 1952-05-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 26th. TASS reports: The Akahata newspaper reported that a large rally of 30,000 people was held in Nagoya on May 17th to celebrate Professor Ikuo Oyama's receipt of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace." A large-scale demonstration was held after the meeting.

    In his speech, Ikuo Oyama emphasized the necessity for the people of Asian countries to unite and fight for peace.

### 日本名古屋三万人集会示威  庆祝大山郁夫获得和平奖金

1952-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    日本名古屋三万人集会示威
    庆祝大山郁夫获得和平奖金
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社东京二十六日讯：赤旗报报道，名古屋五月十七日举行了一次三万人的大集会，庆祝大山郁夫教授荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。会后举行了大规模示威。
    大山郁夫演说时强调亚洲各国人民为争取和平有联合起来进行斗争的必要。
