Title: Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 2

　　Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
	Dong Biwu, Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council, Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
    To the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the people of all nationalities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:
    Since the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region five years ago, it has established equal, united, friendly, and cooperative relations among all nationalities in the autonomous region and has achieved achievements in various aspects of politics, economy, and culture, thus demonstrating the brilliant victory of Chairman Mao's ethnic policy. In the work of universally implementing the policy of ethnic regional autonomy and ethnic democratic joint government in relevant regions, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has provided and will continue to provide valuable experience.
    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the establishment of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, I would like to extend my warm congratulations via telegram.
    Dong Biwu, Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    May 1, 1952
    Liu Lantao, Director of the North China Administrative Committee of the Government Administration Council, Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
    To Chairman Ulanhu of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:
    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the establishment of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations. I hope that our two regions will further strengthen ties, learn from each other, and work together to welcome the large-scale economic construction of the motherland.
    Liu Lantao, Director of the North China Administrative Committee
    May 1, 1952
    The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
    To Chairman Ulanhu of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:
    Since the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region five years ago, under the leadership of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region headed by you, it has established equal, united, and friendly cooperative relations among nationalities, developed the economic and cultural undertakings of the autonomous region, and set a good example of ethnic regional autonomy.
    The achievements of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region over the past five years fully prove the correctness of the ethnic policy of Chairman Mao, the great leader of the people of all nationalities in China. On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, we would like to express our congratulations with incomparable joy and excitement and wish you ever-increasing achievements in your continued struggle to realize Chairman Mao's ethnic policy.
    Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government
    May 1, 1952

### 祝贺内蒙古自治区人民政府成立五周年

1952-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　祝贺内蒙古自治区人民政府成立五周年
    政务院政治法律委员会主任董必武贺电
    内蒙古自治区人民政府暨内蒙古自治区各民族人民：
    内蒙古自治区成立五年以来，确立了自治区内各民族间平等、团结与友爱合作的关系，达到了政治上、经济上、文化上多方面的成就，从而显示了毛主席民族政策的光辉胜利。在各有关地区普遍推行民族区域自治和民族民主联合政府的政策的工作中，内蒙古自治区已经并将继续提供宝贵的经验。
    值兹内蒙古自治区人民政府成立五周年，特电致以热烈的祝贺。
    中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会主任  董必武
    一九五二年五月一日
    政务院华北行政委员会主任刘澜涛贺电
    内蒙古自治区人民政府乌兰夫主席：
    值此内蒙古自治区人民政府成立五周年之际，谨致衷心的祝贺。愿你我两区进一步密切联系，互相学习，为迎接祖国大规模的经济建设而共同努力。
    华北行政委员会主任刘澜涛
    一九五二年五月一日
    中央人民政府民族事务委员会贺电
    内蒙古自治区人民政府乌兰夫主席：
    内蒙古自治区成立五年以来，在以你为首的内蒙古自治区人民政府领导之下，确立了民族间平等、团结和友好合作的关系，发展了自治区的经济和文化事业，树立了民族区域自治的良好榜样。
    内蒙古自治区五年来的成就，完全证实了中国各民族人民伟大领袖毛主席民族政策的正确。值兹内蒙古自治区成立五周年，我们谨以无比愉快和兴奋的心情，申致贺意，并祝你们在继续实现毛主席民族政策的奋斗中获得与日俱增的成就。
    中央人民政府民族事务委员会
    一九五二年五月一日
