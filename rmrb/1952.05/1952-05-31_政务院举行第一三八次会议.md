Title: Government Administration Council Holds 138th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: The 138th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 30th. After listening to the explanation given by Dong Biwu, Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Central People's Government Administration Council, the meeting approved the key tasks of the committee for the second half of 1952. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Bo Yibo, Director of the Central Committee for Thrift Examination, on several issues related to the conclusion of the "Five-Anti" campaign. In addition, several appointment and removal cases were approved.

### 政务院举行第一三八次会议

1952-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一三八次会议
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十八次政务会议在三十日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会董必武主任所作的说明后，批准了该委一九五二年下半年的工作要点。会议听取和批准了中央节约检查委员会薄一波主任所作的关于结束“五反”的几个问题的报告。此外通过任免案多起。
