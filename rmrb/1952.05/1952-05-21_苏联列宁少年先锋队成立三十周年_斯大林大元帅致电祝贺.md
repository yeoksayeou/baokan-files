Title: Thirtieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Lenin Young Pioneer Organization - Marshal Stalin Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author: Sidalin
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 4

    Thirtieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Lenin Young Pioneer Organization
    Marshal Stalin Sends Congratulatory Telegram
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th] According to a TASS report from Moscow on the 19th, May 19th marks the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Lenin Young Pioneer Organization. Stalin sent a congratulatory telegram to the Lenin Young Pioneers. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Young Pioneer Organization named after Lenin, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Young Pioneers and school children.
    I wish the Young Pioneers and school children good health and success in their studies, work, and social activities.
    Let the Young Pioneer Organization continue to educate the Young Pioneers and school children to be loyal Leninists and loyal sons and daughters of our great motherland. Stalin

### 苏联列宁少年先锋队成立三十周年  斯大林大元帅致电祝贺
斯大林
1952-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联列宁少年先锋队成立三十周年
    斯大林大元帅致电祝贺
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：五月十九日是苏联列宁少年先锋队成立三十周年纪念日，斯大林致电列宁少年先锋队祝贺，电文如下：
    值此以列宁为名的少年先锋队成立三十周年的时候，我向少年先锋队队员和学校儿童致以衷心的祝贺。
    我祝贺少年先锋队队员和学校儿童健康，并在学习、工作和社会活动中获得成就。
    让少年先锋队继续把少年先锋队队员和学校儿童教育成忠实的列宁主义者、我们伟大祖国的忠诚儿女。  斯大林
