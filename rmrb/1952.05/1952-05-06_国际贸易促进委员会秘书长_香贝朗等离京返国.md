Title: Secretary-General of the International Trade Promotion Committee, Chambellan, and Others Departed Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1952-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Chambellan (France), Secretary-General of the International Trade Promotion Committee, and his wife and son, along with British representative Boughton and others, who visited China after attending the Moscow International Economic Conference, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 5th to return home. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Nan Hanchen, head of the Chinese delegation to the International Economic Conference; Ji Chao Ding, member of the International Trade Promotion Committee; Chen Hansheng, Deputy Director of the China Economic Society; Xiang Shuxiang, member of the preparatory office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee; Sun Xiaocun, Standing Committee member of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Fu Huating, Director of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, and many others. Jiang Ming, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 国际贸易促进委员会秘书长  香贝朗等离京返国

1952-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    国际贸易促进委员会秘书长
    香贝朗等离京返国
    【新华社五日讯】出席莫斯科国际经济会议之后来我国参观的国际贸易促进委员会秘书长香贝朗（法国）夫妇和公子、英国代表鲍尔顿等一行四人，在五日上午乘飞机离北京回国。到机场欢送的有：我国出席国际经济会议代表团团长南汉宸、国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎、中国经济学会副主任委员陈翰笙、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔、民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓邨、北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭等多人。中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司副司长江明也到机场欢送。
