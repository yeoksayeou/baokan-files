Title: Youth League Central Committee Hosts Hungarian National People's Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1952-05-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a banquet on the afternoon of the 14th to entertain all members of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe who came to perform in our country. Among those present at the banquet were Feng Wenbin, Jiang Nanxiang, Rong Gaotang, and others from the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League. Invited to accompany them were Sha Kefu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Jin Ziguang, Shu Mo, Zhang Lu, and many other literary and art workers from the capital. At the meeting, Feng Wenbin delivered a welcoming speech. The head of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, Heeresi Gyula, delivered a reply. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the profound friendship between the people and youth of China and Hungary, and to wish Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Rákosi good health.

### 青年团中央招待匈牙利国家人民文工团

1952-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央招待匈牙利国家人民文工团
    【新华社十四日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会在十四日下午举行宴会，招待前来我国表演的匈牙利国家人民文工团全体团员。出席宴会的有：青年团中央委员会书记处书记冯文彬、蒋南翔、荣高棠等。应邀作陪的有：中央人民政府文化部办公厅主任沙可夫，和首都文艺工作者金紫光、舒模、张鲁等多人。会上由冯文彬致欢迎辞。匈牙利国家人民文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊致答辞。席间宾主频频举杯祝贺中匈两国人民和青年的深厚友谊，祝贺斯大林、毛泽东、拉科西的健康。
