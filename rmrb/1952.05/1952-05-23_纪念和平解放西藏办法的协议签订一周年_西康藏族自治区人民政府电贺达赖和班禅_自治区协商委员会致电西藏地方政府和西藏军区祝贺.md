Title: Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet; The People's Government of the Xikang Tibetan Autonomous Region Sends Telegrams of Congratulations to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Lama; The Autonomous Region's Consultative Committee Sends Telegrams to the Local Government of Tibet and the Tibet Military Region to Extend Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1952-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kangding, May 22nd: The People's Government of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province sent telegrams to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni to congratulate them on the first anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. The original telegram is as follows:
Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni,

    On the occasion of the anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, we are delighted to hear that Panchen Erdeni has safely arrived in Lhasa and has had a cordial meeting with the Dalai Lama. The monks and laypeople of our region are spreading the news and congratulating each other, and their joy is beyond words. This is a great victory for Chairman Mao's ethnic policy and a blessing for the harmony and unity of all Tibetan people. In addition to actively preparing for the celebration, we hereby extend our congratulations on behalf of the people of the entire region, and hope that the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni will cooperate closely under the leadership of the Central People's Government, unite all Tibetan people, and thoroughly implement the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. This telegram is respectfully submitted, with wishes for your good health.

Xinhua News Agency, Kangding, May 22nd: The Consultative Committee of All Ethnic Groups and All Circles of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province sent telegrams to the Local Government of Tibet and Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Deputy Commanders Apé Awang Jigme and Garga Phuntsok Rabgye, and Political Commissar Tan Guansan, congratulating them on the first anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet.

    The telegram to the Local Government of Tibet stated: On the occasion of the anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, please accept the sincere congratulations of all the monks and laypeople of our region. The peaceful liberation of Tibet has not only enabled the Tibetan people to forever free themselves from the shackles of imperialist aggression and advance towards prosperity and happiness together with all ethnic groups of the motherland, but has also greatly promoted the progress of various constructions in our region. In order for the Tibetan people to smoothly build their own happy life and consolidate the frontier defenses of the motherland, all of us in the region are determined to make even greater efforts in the future to complete the task of supporting Tibet, and hope that you will thoroughly implement the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet under the leadership of the Central People's Government. Let us join hands and strive together to build a free and happy new Xikang and an independent and prosperous motherland.

    The telegram to Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and others stated: On the occasion of the anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, we, on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in the region, extend our deep respect to you and all the commanders and fighters of the Tibet Military Region who are bravely defending the frontier of the motherland; and congratulate the official establishment of the Tibet Military Region. We express our heartfelt gratitude for the brotherly assistance you have given to the people of our region since the advance of the army. We deeply understand the great significance of the establishment of the Tibet Military Region in protecting the territory of the motherland and the happy lives of all ethnic groups, and we will continue to mobilize the growing manpower and material resources of the region to support you. We wish you even closer unity with all the Tibetan people in the struggle to completely eliminate the aggressive forces of imperialism, consolidate national defense, and fully implement the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, and achieve new victories!

### 纪念和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年  西康藏族自治区人民政府电贺达赖和班禅  自治区协商委员会致电西藏地方政府和西藏军区祝贺

1952-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念和平解放西藏办法的协议签订一周年
    西康藏族自治区人民政府电贺达赖和班禅
    自治区协商委员会致电西藏地方政府和西藏军区祝贺
    【新华社康定二十二日电】西康省藏族自治区人民政府为纪念和平解放西藏办法协议签订一周年，致电达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼祝贺。原电如下：达赖喇嘛班禅额尔德尼
    正值和平解放西藏办法协议签订周年纪念之际，欣闻班禅额尔德尼安抵拉萨，并与达赖喇嘛亲密会晤，我区僧、俗人民奔相走告，互为传颂，喜悦之情难以言表。这是毛主席民族政策的伟大胜利和全藏人民和睦团结的福祉。我们除正积极筹备庆祝外，谨此代表全区人民敬致祝贺，并望达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在中央人民政府领导下亲睦合作，团结全藏人民，彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议。特电奉闻，并颂健康。
    【新华社康定二十二日电】西康省藏族自治区各族各界人民协商委员会致电西藏地方政府和人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华，副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美、尕噶·彭错饶杰及政治委员谭冠三等，祝贺和平解放西藏办法协议签订一周年。
    致西藏地方政府的电文说：值此和平解放西藏办法协议签订周年纪念之际，请接受我区全体僧俗人民的至诚祝贺。西藏和平解放不但使西藏人民从此永远摆脱帝国主义侵略势力的羁绊，得以和祖国各族人民一起走向繁荣幸福，而且也大大推进了我区各种建设的进程。为着西藏人民顺利地建设自己的幸福生活和巩固祖国边防，我们全区人民今后誓以更大的努力来完成支援西藏的任务，并望你们在中央人民政府的领导下，彻底实现和平解放西藏办法协议。让我们携手并进，为建设自由幸福的新康藏和独立富强的祖国而共同奋斗。
    致人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华等的电文说：兹值和平解放西藏办法协议签订周年纪念，谨代表全区各族人民向你们和英勇捍卫着祖国边防的西藏军区全体指战员致以深切的敬意；并贺西藏军区的正式成立，对你们在进军以来给予我区人民兄弟般的帮助，我们谨表衷心感谢。我们深知西藏军区的成立对保卫祖国领土和各族人民的幸福生活，具有何等重大的意义，我们将继续动员全区日益壮大的人力物力支援你们。祝贺你们更紧密地团结全藏人民，在彻底清除帝国主义的侵略势力、巩固国防和全部实现和平解放西藏办法的协议的奋斗中，取得新的胜利！
