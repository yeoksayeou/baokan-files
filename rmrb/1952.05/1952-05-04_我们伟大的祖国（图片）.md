    Our Great Motherland
    Motorcycle sports are a new sports project for the people of our country. The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation is preparing to develop this sport step by step on the basis of general physical exercises. These are the first group of young female motorcycle athletes, and they are driving "Jinggangshan" motorcycles made by our country's workers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    摩托车运动是我国人民体育的新项目之一。中华全国体育总会筹备委员会准备在一般体育运动的基础上有步骤地来开展这项运动。这是第一批女青年摩托车运动员，她们正驾驶着我国工人所制造的“井岗山”摩托车。
