Title: “Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!” Publishes Editorial Calling for a Halt to the American Aggressors' Atrocities on Geoje Island
Author:
Date: 1952-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 23rd: "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" published an editorial entitled "Stop the Atrocities of the American Imperialists!" saying: The world is shocked by the unprecedented atrocities of the American barbarians on Geoje Island. Compared to these atrocities, the tragic conditions in the death camps set up by Hitler in Buchenwald, Dachau, Mauthausen, and Auschwitz pale in comparison.

On May 7, prisoners of war in Prisoner of War Camp No. 76 detained the camp commander, General Dodd, and indignantly protested the brutal and tyrannical rule. The aggressors' atrocities on Geoje Island were not only fully proven by this, but also admitted and confirmed in writing by the American representative, General Dodd, and the new camp commander, General Colson. The open confession by Truman's generals of the heinous crimes of the American army once again exposes the bloody crimes of American imperialism.

The editorial further stated: The exposure of the terrible situation on Geoje Island has caused confusion, panic, and outrage among the American ruling clique and the entire American reactionaries. These revelations explain the true reasons for Ridgway's representatives' shameful behavior during the Panmunjom negotiations and expose their true purpose in delaying the armistice talks. At the same time, these revelations undeniably prove that the U.S. military command's forced detention of 100,000 Korean and Chinese prisoners of war is closely related to the American aggressors' conspiracy to prolong the war and expand the war to China.

The editorial emphasized: World opinion and all peace-loving people condemn the atrocities committed by the American aggressors and the unprecedented acts of abuse in human history. All people with a sense of justice resolutely demand an end to the interventionists' atrocities and a swift end to the Korean War. People of all countries consider the direct perpetrators of the Geoje Island crimes—American Generals Dodd, Colson, Boatner, Clark, and Ridgway—to be war criminals and demand that they be severely punished. The anger of the people of all countries proves that the vigilance of all peace-loving forces has increased. Hundreds of millions of people in various countries are increasingly determined to thwart the heinous conspiracy of the American warmongers, to safeguard peace. Communist and Workers' parties, all progressive people's organizations, and all peace fighters will inform the broad masses of people of the truth about Geoje Island. Let all people with a sense of justice who cherish peace pass judgment on the human enemy who plans a new world massacre, plans to enslave peace-loving people of all countries, and establish fascist reactionary rule throughout the world—the bloody American butchers.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”发表社论  号召制止美国侵略者在巨济岛的暴行

1952-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”发表社论
    号召制止美国侵略者在巨济岛的暴行
    【新华社二十六日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特二十三日讯：“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”发表题为“制止美帝国主义者的暴行！”的社论说：全世界都为美国野蛮人在巨济岛的空前暴行所震惊。和这种暴行比起来，希特勒在布痕瓦尔德、达豪、麦丹尼克和奥斯威辛所设立的死亡营中的凄惨情况都相形减色。
    五月七日，第七十六号战俘营的战俘扣留了战俘营长官杜德将军，并且愤慨地抗议凶残暴虐的统治。侵略者在巨济岛上的暴行不仅由此得到了充分证明，并且由美国的代表杜德将军和新任战俘营长官柯尔生将军书面承认和证实。杜鲁门的将军们对于美国军队的滔天罪行的公开供认，再一次暴露了美帝国主义的血腥罪行。
    社论进一步说：巨济岛恐怖情况的揭发，引起了美国统治集团以及整个美国反动派的混乱、惊惶失措和暴跳如雷。这些揭发说明了李奇微的代表在板门店谈判时的可耻行为的真正原因，暴露了他们拖延停战谈判的真正目的。同时，这些揭发也无可抵赖地证明了美军司令部强迫扣留十万名朝中战俘一事，与美国侵略者打算拖延战争并且把战争扩大到中国去的阴谋有着密切关系。
    社论强调说：世界舆论和全体爱好和平的人民，都痛斥美国侵略者所干下的暴行和人类历史上前所未有的虐待行为。一切有正义感的人民都坚决要求停止干涉者的暴行并迅速结束朝鲜战争。各国人民认为巨济岛罪行的直接执行人——美国将军杜德、柯尔生、波纳、克拉克、李奇微——是战犯，并要求严办他们。各国人民的愤怒，证明了所有爱好和平的力量的警觉性已有提高。各国亿万人民日益坚决地要挫败美国战争贩子的万恶阴谋，要维护和平。共产党和工人党、所有进步的人民团体和所有的和平战士，都将把巨济岛的事实真相告诉给广大人民群众。让珍视和平的一切有正义感的人民，来通过对策划新世界大屠杀、策划奴役爱好和平的各国人民并在全世界建立法西斯反动统治的人类之敌——血腥的美国屠夫的判决吧。
