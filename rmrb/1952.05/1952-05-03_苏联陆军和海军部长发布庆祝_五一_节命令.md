Title: Soviet Army and Navy Ministers Issue Orders Celebrating May Day
Author: Kuznetsov
Date: 1952-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. TASS dispatches: The Soviet Army Minister issued the following order celebrating May Day:

	Comrade soldiers and sergeants of the Army! Comrade officers and generals!

	Today, the Soviet people, together with their glorious Soviet Army, celebrate May Day—the international day of solidarity of the working people. We are commemorating this festival with the significant victories won under the leadership of the Bolshevik Party.

	On this occasion of May Day, I extend my greetings and congratulations to you and wish all members of the Soviet Army to make unremitting efforts to further improve their military and political knowledge during this summer training period, to tirelessly study and master weapons and combat equipment, and to strengthen discipline, vigilance, and combat readiness in every possible way.

	In commemoration of the international festival of the working people, I order: Today, May 1st, twenty salvoes of artillery will be fired in our country's capital, Moscow, in the capitals of all the Union Republics, in Kaliningrad, Lvov, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa.

	Long live the great Soviet people and their heroic army!

	Long live the Soviet government!

	Long live the party of Lenin and Stalin—the inspirer and organizer of all our victories!

	Glory to our great leader and teacher—the genius commander—Comrade Stalin!

	Soviet Army Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Vasilevsky

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. TASS dispatches: The Soviet Navy Minister issued the following order celebrating May Day:

	Comrade sailors and sergeants of the Navy! Comrade officers and generals!

	Today, our Navy, together with all the Soviet people, celebrates May Day—the international festival of the working people.

	The people of our motherland, under the leadership of the Communist Party, are greeting May Day with outstanding new achievements in strengthening the power of the Soviet state.

	The Soviet Navy units, executing the orders of our great leader and teacher, Comrade Stalin, vigilantly defend the maritime borders and national interests of our motherland, and celebrate May Day with new achievements in combat and political training.

	The task of the Navy is to further enhance vigilance, further strengthen the combat readiness of ships and units, improve their military technology day by day, cultivate outstanding soldiers in combat and political training, and strengthen military discipline and organization.

	Comrade sailors and sergeants of the Navy! Comrade officers and generals!

	On this occasion of the international festival of the working people—May Day, I extend my greetings and congratulations to you, and wish you more achievements in strengthening the combat power of our Navy.

	Long live our great Soviet motherland!

	Long live the heroic Soviet people and their Navy!

	Long live the victorious party of Lenin and Stalin!

	Glory to our great and wise leader and helmsman, Generalissimo Stalin!

	Soviet Navy Minister, Vice-Admiral Kuznetsov

### 苏联陆军和海军部长发布庆祝“五一”节命令
库兹聂佐夫
1952-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联陆军和海军部长发布庆祝“五一”节命令
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联陆军部长发布庆祝五一节命令如下：
    陆军战士与士官同志们！校尉与将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民与他们的光荣的苏联陆军共同欢庆五一节——劳动人民国际团结的节日。我们是以在布尔什维克党领导下赢得的重大胜利来纪念这个节日的。
    值此五一节，我谨向你们致敬和致贺，并祝苏联陆军全体人员在今年夏季受训期间，更加坚持不渝地使军事与政治知识日益提高，并不倦地学习掌握武器和战斗装备，用一切方法加强纪律，提高警觉和战斗准备。
    为了纪念劳动人民的国际节日，我命令：今天，五月一日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴以及在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨诸英雄城市，一律鸣放礼炮二十响。
    伟大的苏联人民和他们的英勇的军队万岁！
    苏联政府万岁！
    列宁和斯大林的党——我们的一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——万岁！
    光荣归于我们的伟大领袖和导师——天才的统帅——斯大林同志！
    苏联陆军部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部长发布庆祝五一节命令如下：
    海军战士与士官同志们！校尉与将军同志们！
    今天，我国海军与全体苏联人民一道庆祝五一节——劳动人民的国际节日。
    我们祖国的人民在共产党领导下，是以加强苏维埃国家威力方面的卓越的新成就来迎接五一节的。
    苏联海军部队执行我们伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志的命令，警惕地保卫我们祖国的海上边疆和国家利益，并以战斗和政治训练方面的新成就来庆祝五一节。
    海军的任务是进一步提高警惕性，进一步加强舰只和部队的战斗准备，逐日提高他们的军事技术，在战斗和政治训练中培养优秀战士，加强军事纪律和组织。
    海军战士和士官同志们！校尉和将军同志们！
    值此劳动人民的国际节日——五一节，我谨向你们致敬和致贺，并祝你们在加强我国海军的战斗威力方面获得更多的成就。
    我们伟大的苏维埃祖国万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们的海军万岁！
    胜利的列宁和斯大林的共产党万岁！
    光荣归于我们伟大英明的领袖和舵手斯大林大元帅！
    苏联海军部长、海军中将库兹聂佐夫
