Title: Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Luong Bang, Presents Credentials to Shvernik
Author:
Date: 1952-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dated 16th. According to a delayed dispatch from North Vietnam by the Vietnam News Agency: The Vietnamese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Luong Bang, presented his credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Shvernik, on April 25th at the Kremlin. Present at the ceremony were Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Kuraenkov, and Director of the Southeast Asia Department Bakitov.

### 越南驻苏大使阮良朋  向什维尔尼克呈递国书

1952-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南驻苏大使阮良朋
    向什维尔尼克呈递国书
    【新华社十六日讯】据越南通讯社北越迟到消息：越南驻苏联特命全权大使阮良朋已于四月二十五日在克里姆林宫向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克呈递国书。在场者有苏联外交部副部长佐林、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金、苏联外交部交际司司长库拉仁科夫和东南亚司司长巴基托夫。
