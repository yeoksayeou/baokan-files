Title: May Declaration – Celebrating May Day in Beijing
Author: Hong Chunzhe
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 3

　　May Declaration
    ——Celebrating May Day in Beijing
    Hong Chunzhe, Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China
    In an atmosphere of battle,
    The light of victory shines...
    On the distant horizon,
    Appear colorful flags fluttering in the wind;
    Fragrance pervades the mountains and fields,
    The sun's light is even more brilliant.
    This is the victorious May!
    The hundreds of millions of hearts
    That shake the earth are boiling,
    Harboring the same strong will to eliminate the enemy.
    This is the victorious May, the song of May!
    I am listening:
    People holding high the blood-stained five-star red flag,
    On the road to victory after victory,
    Singing that song.
    I am listening:
    The five hundred million people of the Mao Zedong era,
    Singing like one person
    That heroic song.
    The crowds boiling in front of Tiananmen,
    From the distant borderlands to Beijing—the heart of China,
    The thousands of roads on the mainland,
    Passing through red flags fluttering like churning waves,
    Gather the pride of building victory.
    On the vast land,
    With new determination and fighting will,
    Each heart spurts with sparks;
    On the red road,
    Long lines like surging torrents,
    March forward with a thunderous roar.
    History tells us the bloody inscription:
    "Sacrifice is glory, is victory!"
    The fighting ranks forever defending peace,
    Look! The leader's portrait hanging high on that exquisite marble railing,
    Stalin and Mao Zedong are together,
    Together with the leaders of all countries,
    From these portraits, the representatives of all countries
    See the great strength of friends all over the world uniting and advancing.
　　The ancient city of Beijing,
    Radiating the light of revolutionary tradition,
    The red pillars, the golden glazed tiles, the ancient city walls,
    Are so lovely and magnificent!
    The magnificent Tiananmen,
    Is so uplifting!
    I saw:
    Forest-like dense chimneys,
    And black smoke rising into the clouds,
    Beneath that
    Great construction is underway.
    ——Everywhere I have been is like this.
    My dearest and most precious friends,
    What valuable blood and sweat this is!
    To work for the Party and the motherland is your most sublime honor.
    Every road leads to communism,
    You and we have stepped onto the front lines together;
    For the spring of peace,
    For singing freely....
    The movement to increase production is beginning,
    Irrepressible anger is also rising;
    In the song of Resisting America and Aiding Korea,
    A pyramid of eternal glory has been established,
    Ah, a name that is praised at any time——
    The workers of New China!
    Please accept the honor sent from heroic Korea,
    Please accept the handshake sent from heroic Korea.
    Different times and places,
    Different skin colors and languages,
    From this end of the earth to that end,
    Steeled wills unite,
    In the enemy's final wail,
    In our song of victory,
    Shout! Our heroic victory, we must show off!
    The angry cries from six continents and five oceans,
    Scatter in the universe.
    This is the cry of good ordinary people,
    Demanding with infinite hatred and curses——
    "Trial the murderous American imperialists!"
    "Severely punish the public enemy of mankind, American imperialism!"
    Murderous demons listen:
    Where there are people,
    There are sharp eyes of revenge;
    People holding up the map of fighting Korea,
    Shout the May Declaration——
    May you die forever!
    The tycoons of Wall Street!
    The mad dogs in the White House!
    Everyone knows your perfidy,
    Everyone knows you are pretending,
    Your dead-end fate
    Will not be long, will not be long!
    Quickly kneel before the grim history!
    Quickly crawl into the grave you dug yourself!
    With full of enthusiasm,
    Dedicate everything to the motherland, the Party, and the leader;
    The road we took through the gunfire,
    This is the road of heroic Korea, stained with blood,
    No one can stop us from moving forward.
    The flames of Resisting America and Aiding Korea,
    Rising fiercely under the banner of Mao Zedong,
    The will of the five hundred million people is a strong will;
    The power united in May,
    Is an indestructible force.
    The boaster of "American civilization"—the germ warfare criminal,
    Listen carefully:
    From the Kremlin to the whole world,
    The call from all places——this May Declaration,
    Is issued together with us.
    You listen:
    The people are with us!
    Peace is with us!
    Victory is with us!
    In the enemy's panic and trembling,
    We have firm confidence and will.
    The May ranks move forward,
    Towards more brilliant construction,
    Towards greater victory!
    In infinite anger,
    In the even more resounding song of Resisting America and Aiding Korea,
    Forward, forward!
    Together with fighting Korea,
    Holding high the banner of victory, singing the battle song of victory,
    Forward, march forward!
    (Bing Wei, translator)

### 五月的宣言  ——庆祝五一节在北京
洪淳哲
1952-05-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　五月的宣言
    ——庆祝五一节在北京
    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长  洪淳哲
    战斗的气氛中，
    胜利的光芒闪耀着……
    遥远的地平线上，
    呈现了迎风飘荡的彩旗；
    芳香笼罩着山野，
    太阳的光芒更加辉煌。
    这是胜利的五月！
    震撼地球的
    亿万个沸腾了的心胸，
    怀着同样的消灭敌人的坚强意志。
    这是胜利的五月，五月的歌声！
    我在听：
    人们高举着鲜血染成的五星红旗，
    在百战百胜的道路上，
    高唱着的那支歌曲。
    我在倾听：
    毛泽东时代的五万万人民的队伍，
    像一个人似地唱着的
    那支英雄的歌曲。
    天安门前沸腾了的人群，
    从遥远的边疆到北京——中国的心脏，
    大陆上的千万条道路，
    穿过像波涛翻腾似的飘荡的红旗，
    汇集了建设胜利的骄矜。
    在辽阔的大地上，
    以新的决心和战斗意志，
    每个心胸都喷射着火花；
    在红色的道路上，
    狂澜汹涌似的长长行列，
    震天动地地昂然前进。
    历史告诉了我们血的铭言：
    “牺牲是光荣，是胜利！”
    永远保卫和平的战斗队伍，
    看啊！在那玲珑的大理石栏杆上高悬着的领袖像，
    斯大林和毛泽东在一起，
    和各国人民的领袖在一起，
    各国的代表们就从这些领袖像上，
    看到了全世界的朋友团结前进的伟大力量。
　   放射着革命传统的光芒的
    古香古色的北京城啊，
    红色的圆柱、金色的琉璃瓦、古老的城墙，
    是那样可爱、壮丽！
    雄伟的天安门啊，
    是那样使人心胸开阔！
    我看见了：
    树林般茂密的烟囱，
    和升入云端的黑烟，
    那底下
    正进行着伟大的建设。
    ——我到过的任何地方都是这样。
    我的最高贵最亲爱的朋友们，
    这是多么宝贵的血汗啊！
    为了党和祖国而劳动，是你们最崇高的荣誉。
    条条道路通向共产主义，
    你们和我们一起走上了前线；
    为了和平的春天，
    为了自由地歌唱……。
    增加生产的运动正在开始，
    不可抑止的愤怒也正在升涨；
    在抗美援朝的歌声中，
    建立了永远光辉的金字塔、
    啊，任何时候都被歌颂着的名字——
    新中国的工人们！
    请接受，从英雄的朝鲜送来的荣誉，
    请接受，从英雄的朝鲜送来的握手。
    时间、地点不同，
    肤色、语言不同，
    从地球的这端到那端，
    钢铁的意志团结起来，
    在敌人最后的哀鸣声中，
    在我们胜利的歌声中，
    高呼啊！我们英雄的胜利，我们要炫耀！
    六大洲、五大洋传来的愤怒呼声，
    在宇宙间散开。
    这是善良的普通人的呼声，
    以无限仇恨和诅咒要求——
    “审判杀人犯美帝国主义！”
    “严惩人类的公敌美帝国主义！”
    杀人魔鬼听着：
    那里有人，
    那里就有尖锐的复仇的眼睛；
    人们举着战斗的朝鲜地图，
    高喊出五月的宣言——
    叫你们永远死去！
    华尔街的大亨们！
    白宫里的疯狗们！
    你们的背信弃义谁都知道，
    谁都知道你们在装模作样，
    你们的穷途末路的命运，
    不会久长，不会久长！
    赶快在严峻的历史面前跪下！
    赶快钻进你自己掘好的坟墓！
    以满腔的热血，
    把自己的一切献给祖国、党和领袖；
    我们冲开炮火所走的道路，
    这是鲜血染红了的英雄的朝鲜的道路，
    谁也别想阻挡我们前进。
    抗美援朝的火焰，
    在毛泽东的旗帜下熊熊上升，
    五万万人民的意志是坚强的意志；
    五月里团结起来的力量，
    是不可摧毁的力量。
    “美国文明”的吹嘘者——细菌战犯，
    你好好地听着：
    从克里姆林到全世界，
    从所有的地方发出的呼声——这五月的宣言，
    是和我们一起发出的。
    你听着：
    人民和我们在一起！
    和平和我们在一起！
    胜利和我们在一起！
    在敌人的惊恐和战栗中，
    我们有着坚强的信心和意志。
    五月的队伍向前进，
    向着更辉煌的建设，
    向着更伟大的胜利！
    在无限的愤怒中，
    在更加高亢的抗美援朝的歌声中，
    前进啊，前进！
    和战斗的朝鲜一道，
    高举胜利的旗帜，高唱胜利的战歌，
    前进，向前进！
    （冰蔚  译）
