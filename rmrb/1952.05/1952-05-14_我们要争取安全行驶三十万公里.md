Title: We Must Strive for 300,000 Kilometers of Safe Driving

Author:

Date: 1952-05-14
Page: 6

Respected Chairman Mao: We report our production achievements to you with ardent hearts. To welcome the great "May Day" Labor Day, we carefully revised the patriotic pact in April and formulated the "Five-Five" work system. On April 25th, we completed our April production tasks ahead of schedule. Throughout the month, we exceeded the axle load 25 times, totaling 8,322 tons, with daily vehicle kilometers exceeding the bureau's target by 55.5 kilometers and technical speed exceeding the bureau's target by 2.8 kilometers. In the "Overall Red" competition, we won the most red flags, ranking first in the entire section. Since March of the year before last, we have driven safely for 200,000 kilometers.

Our achievements are inseparable from your teachings, the education of the Party and the Youth League, the help of Soviet experts, and the victory of the "Three-Anti" campaign. We use these practical actions to fiercely strike back against the American imperialist war of aggression. In mid-May, we will further enhance our enthusiasm, strive for a complete victory in the "tiger hunt," and overfulfill production targets. We will never be complacent, we will certainly correct existing shortcomings, and strive for 300,000 kilometers of safe driving.

Harbin Locomotive Depot No. 1291 Youth Chartered Car Group

### 我们要争取安全行驶三十万公里

1952-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我们要争取安全行驶三十万公里
    敬爱的毛主席：我们以热切的心情，向您报告生产成绩。为了迎接伟大的“五一”劳动节，我们在四月份慎重地修订了爱国公约，订出了“五五”工作制度。四月二十五日，我们提前完成了四月份生产任务。我们全月超轴二十五次，共八千三百二十二吨，日车公里超过局定五十五点五公里，技术速度超过局定二点八公里。在“满堂红”竞赛中，我们得红旗最多，占全段第一位。自前年三月以来，我们安全行驶了二十万公里。
    我们有这些成就，是和您的教导，党和青年团的教育，苏联专家的帮助，以及“三反”斗争的胜利分不开的。我们用这些实际行动狠狠地回击了美帝国主义的侵略战争。在五月中，我们要更进一步发挥积极性，争取“打虎”彻底胜利，超额完成生产指标。我们决不骄傲，我们一定改正现存缺点，争取安全行驶三十万公里。
    哈尔滨机务段一二九一号青年包车组
