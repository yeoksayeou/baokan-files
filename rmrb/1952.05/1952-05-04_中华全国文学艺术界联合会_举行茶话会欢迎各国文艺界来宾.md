Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Holds Tea Party to Welcome Literary and Art Guests from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1952-05-04
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 3rd) The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a tea party on the morning of the 3rd to welcome literary and art guests from various countries who were invited to participate in China's commemoration of the world's four great cultural figures (Avicenna, Da Vinci, Hugo, Gogol) and to attend China's "May Day" celebrations.

Invited guests included: President of the French Peace Council, Fache, and his wife; literary critic Roa and his wife; Italian philosopher Banfi; physicist Pancini; film director De Santis; Polish poet Mondalion; German Democratic Republic screenwriter Stern; Swedish journalist Ohman; Danish famous playwright Abell and his wife; novelist Ulf; and newspaper editor Hermann Jensen. Chilean painter José Venturelli and his wife, who are visiting China, were also invited.

Those attending as hosts included: Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Mao Dun and Zhou Yang; executive committee members Sha Kefu, Zhao Shuli, Ouyang Yuqian, Li Bozhao, Cao Yu; as well as committee members in Beijing and many people from the capital's literary and art circles.

At the meeting, Mao Dun delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Guests from various countries delivered speeches in succession. They conveyed the respect of literary and art workers from their countries to Chinese literary and art workers, and expressed their willingness to work hard to promote cultural exchange and understanding between the people of all countries. Afterwards, the guests exchanged opinions and experiences on literary and art issues with Chinese literary and art figures. At the meeting, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles distributed "Mao Zedong's Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art" (foreign language edition) and the Stalin Prize-winning works "The Sun Shines on the Sanggan River," "The Hurricane," and "The White-Haired Girl" to the literary and art guests from various countries.

### 中华全国文学艺术界联合会  举行茶话会欢迎各国文艺界来宾

1952-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国文学艺术界联合会
    举行茶话会欢迎各国文艺界来宾
    【新华社三日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会在三日上午举行茶话会，欢迎应邀参加我国纪念世界四大文化名人（阿维森纳、达·芬奇、雨果、果戈理）纪念大会和出席我国“五一”节观礼的各国文艺界来宾。
    应邀出席的有：法国和平理事会主席法奇和夫人、文艺批评家罗阿和夫人，意大利哲学家班菲、物理学家潘齐尼、电影导演德·桑蒂，波兰诗人蒙达里昂，德意志民主共和国电影剧本作家斯德恩，瑞典新闻记者欧曼，丹麦著名剧作家阿伯尔和夫人、小说家乌尔夫、报纸编辑赫曼·任森。来我国访问的智利画家何塞·万徒勒里和夫人也应邀出席。
    出席作陪的有：中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾、周扬，常务委员沙可夫、赵树理、欧阳予倩、李伯钊、曹禺以及在京委员，和首都文艺界人士多人。
    会上茅盾代表中华全国文学艺术界联合会致欢迎辞。各国来宾相继致辞。他们转达了本国文艺界人士对中国文学艺术工作者的敬意，并表示愿为促进各国人民间的文化交流和了解而努力。接着来宾们分别和我国文艺界人士交换了关于文艺问题的意见和经验。会上全国文联并将“毛主席在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话”（外文本）和荣获斯大林奖金的作品“太阳照在桑干河上”、“暴风骤雨”、“白毛女”等分送给各国文艺界来宾。
