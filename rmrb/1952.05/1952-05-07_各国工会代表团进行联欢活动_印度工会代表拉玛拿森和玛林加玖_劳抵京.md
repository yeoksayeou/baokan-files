Title: Union Delegations from Various Countries Hold Joint Celebrations; Indian Union Representatives Ramanatham and Malingak J. Rao Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 6th—Union delegations from various countries who came to China to attend the May Day celebrations held joint activities in Beijing on the 6th. On the morning of the 6th, the Soviet Union Union delegation and all members of the Indian Union delegations who arrived in Beijing in batches held a joint luncheon with representatives of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Representatives of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions present at the luncheon included: Liu Ningyi, Chen Shaomin, Lai Ruoyu, Li Zaiwen, Yang Zhihua, and others. Councilor Kauribanov of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions also attended the luncheon. At the beginning of the luncheon, Liu Ningyi first raised a glass to congratulate the friendly unity of the working classes of the Soviet Union, India, and China. Shefchenko, the head of the Soviet Union Union delegation, raised his glass and said: In the short period since arriving in China, we have established a brotherly and close relationship. May our friendship be increasingly consolidated, and may the friendly unity of the three great nations of India, China, and the Soviet Union last forever. Then, Indian Union representative Malingak J. Rao, on behalf of the All-India Trade Union Congress, raised a glass to salute the workers of China and the Soviet Union. Indian Union representative Saxena said: The trade union representatives of the Soviet Union, China, and India present represent 1.1 billion people of the three great nations. If the three great nations of the Soviet Union, China, and India unite and resolutely safeguard peace, war will definitely not happen. Indian Union representative Mitra said: The Chinese working class has achieved great achievements in the three years since the victory of the Chinese revolution, and they will soon live a happier life like the Soviet working class. The Indian working class is currently struggling, and I believe that the Indian working class will definitely succeed in taking the path you have taken.
	On the afternoon of the 6th, the Bulgarian Union delegation and the Australian Union delegation also held a joint luncheon. During the banquet, representatives of the two countries frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the unity and friendship of the working people of the two countries and the working people of the world, and toasted the health of Stalin, Mao Zedong, Chervenkov, and Sharki. They also discussed issues related to the labor movement in the two countries during the banquet.
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 6th—Indian Union representatives Ramanatham (General Secretary of the Madras Committee of the All-India Trade Union Congress) and Malingak J. Rao (General Secretary of the Railway Workers' Union of Hyderabad Province), who were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to China to attend the May Day celebrations but were delayed for some reason, arrived in Beijing by car on the morning of the 6th. Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jin Ziming, Deputy Minister of the International Liaison Department, Qin Dayuan, Chief of the Secretariat of the General Office, and others went to the station to welcome them.

### 各国工会代表团进行联欢活动  印度工会代表拉玛拿森和玛林加玖·劳抵京

1952-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表团进行联欢活动
    印度工会代表拉玛拿森和玛林加玖·劳抵京
    【新华社六日讯】来我国参加“五一”节观礼的外国工会代表团六日在北京进行了联欢活动。六日晨，苏联工会代表团和先后到达北京的各批印度工会代表团的全体团员和中华全国总工会的代表举行了联欢餐会。代表中华全国总工会出席餐会的有：刘宁一、陈少敏、赖若愚、栗再温、杨之华等。世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员考里班诺夫也参加了餐会。餐会开始时，刘宁一首先举杯祝贺苏联、印度、中国三大国工人阶级的友好团结。苏联工会代表团团长谢甫钦科举杯说：在来到中国后的短短时期中，我们之间建立了兄弟般的亲密关系，愿我们的友谊日益巩固，愿印、中、苏三大民族的友好团结长存。接着，印度工会代表玛林加玖·劳代表印度统一工会大会向中国和苏联工人举杯致敬。印度工会代表萨克森纳说：在座的苏、中、印三国的工会代表代表着三大民族的十一亿人民，苏、中、印三大民族团结起来坚决维护和平，战争就一定不会发生。印度工会代表米特拉说：中国工人阶级在中国革命胜利后的三年中获得了伟大的成就，他们不久就会和苏联工人阶级一样过更幸福的生活。印度工人阶级目前正在奋斗中，相信印度工人阶级一定会成功地走向你们所曾经走过的道路。
    六日中午，保加利亚工会代表团和澳大利亚工会代表团也举行了联欢餐会。席间两国代表频频举杯，祝贺两国劳动人民和全世界劳动人民的团结友好，并为斯大林、毛泽东、契尔文科夫和夏基的健康干杯。他们并在席间畅谈了有关两国工人运动情况的问题。
    【新华社六日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼，因故迟到的印度工会代表拉玛拿森（印度统一工会大会马德拉斯委员会总书记）和玛林加玖·劳（海德拉巴省铁路员工工会总书记）于六日晨乘车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中华全国总工会组织部部长许之桢，国际联络部副部长金子明，办公厅秘书处处长秦达远等人。
