Title: France-China Friendship Association Established and Sends Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 18th: TASS reports that the France-China Friendship Association has been declared established. The inaugural meeting was presided over by Mr. Labbé, Honorary Director of the Bank of France. More than 100 people, including French artists, writers, scientists, and trade union representatives, attended the meeting.

The constitution of the France-China Friendship Association states that the purpose of the association is to "strengthen and develop friendly relations between the people of France and the people of the People's Republic of China."

The inaugural meeting elected an executive committee headed by Professor Déléage and sent a telegram of respect to the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong.

### 法中友好协会成立  并电毛主席致敬

1952-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    法中友好协会成立
    并电毛主席致敬
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社巴黎十八日讯：法中友好协会已宣告成立。成立大会由法兰西银行名誉董事拉贝里耶主持。参加大会的，有法国艺术家、作家、科学家和各工会代表共一百余人。
    法中友好协会的会章上说：法中友好协会的宗旨，是“加强并且发展法国人民和中华人民共和国人民间的友好关系”。
    成立大会选出了以德莱士教授为首的执行委员会，并且打电报向中国人民领袖毛泽东致敬。
