Title: Panchen Erdeni Meets the Dalai Lama; PLA Tibet Military Region Hosts Banquet for All Officials of Panchen's Retinue
Author:
Date: 1952-05-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 6th: Panchen Erdeni met with the Dalai Lama at the Potala Palace in Lhasa at 4:00 PM on April 28th. After the meeting ceremony, the Dalai Lama invited Panchen Erdeni to his bedroom for a conversation. When the Dalai Lama inquired about the construction of the motherland, Panchen Erdeni told him about the great achievements he had personally witnessed in the mainland after liberation in the areas of political, economic, and cultural construction, as well as the implementation of the central government's policies of ethnic equality and freedom of religious belief. They also held friendly talks on how to strengthen the unity between the Han and Tibetan nationalities and within the Tibetan nationality, and how to implement the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet.
    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 6th: The People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region hosted a banquet for all officials of Panchen's retinue on April 30th. Also attending the banquet were the Dalai Lama's four chief secretaries and senior secular and religious officials of the Tibetan local government. Commander Zhang Guohua of the Tibet Military Region and First Deputy Commander Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme (Tibetan) both delivered welcoming speeches at the banquet. In his speech, General Zhang Guohua extended cordial greetings to all members of Panchen's retinue who had gloriously returned to Tibet. He said: Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet was signed, and Panchen Erdeni was able to return to Tibet in glory. This is a great and joyful event for the people of Tibet to be pleased and inspired by. Today, at this banquet, let us drink a toast to the health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all our nationalities! Let us drink a toast to the close unity of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni! Lhamin Yixi Tsultrim Gyaltsen Jigme, Secretary-General of the Kanbu Khenpo Conference of Panchen's retinue, expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality extended by the leaders of the Tibet Military Region and the local governments of Tibet at all levels along the way and after entering Lhasa. He stated that in the future, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government and the direct leadership of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, they must strive to build a prosperous new Tibet. An entertainment evening was held after the banquet.

### 班禅额尔德尼会见达赖喇嘛  解放军西藏军区欢宴班禅行辕全体官员

1952-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼会见达赖喇嘛
    解放军西藏军区欢宴班禅行辕全体官员
    【新华社拉萨六日电】班禅额尔德尼在四月二十八日下午四时，在拉萨的布达拉宫会见达赖喇嘛。会面仪式完毕后，达赖喇嘛邀请班禅额尔德尼到卧室叙谈。当达赖喇嘛询问祖国的建设情况时，班禅额尔德尼便把自己在内地亲眼看到解放后的祖国在政治、经济、文化建设方面的伟大成就，及中央人民政府民族平等、宗教信仰自由等政策在内地实施的情况，都告诉了达赖喇嘛。他们并对如何加强汉藏民族和西藏民族内部团结，以及如何实现关于和平解放西藏办法的协议等问题，都作了友谊的交谈。
    【新华社拉萨六日电】人民解放军西藏军区在四月三十日欢宴班禅行辕全体官员。出席宴会的还有达赖喇嘛的四大秘书和西藏地方政府的高级僧俗官员。西藏军区张国华司令员和第一副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美（藏族），都在欢宴会上致欢迎词。张国华将军在致词中，向光荣返藏的班禅行辕全体人员，致以亲切的慰问。他说：在毛主席的英明领导下，和平解放西藏办法的协议得以签订，班禅额尔德尼得以光荣回藏，这是值得我们西藏人民欢欣鼓舞的大喜事。今天，在这个宴会上，让我们为我们各民族伟大领袖毛主席的健康而干杯！为达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的亲密团结而干杯！班禅行辕堪布会议厅秘书长拉敏益喜楚臣·札萨计晋美在讲话中，表示感谢西藏军区各首长与西藏地方各级政府在沿途及进入拉萨后对他们的热烈欢迎和招待，并表示今后一定要在中央人民政府毛主席的英明领导下，和达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼的直接领导下，为建设繁荣的新西藏而努力。宴会后并举行游艺晚会。
