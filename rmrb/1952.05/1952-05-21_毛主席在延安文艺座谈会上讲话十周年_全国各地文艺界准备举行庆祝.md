Title: Tenth Anniversary of Chairman Mao's Speech at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art: Literary and Art Circles Across the Country Prepare to Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1952-05-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th: This year, May 23rd marks the tenth anniversary of Chairman Mao's speech at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art. Literary and art circles across the country are preparing to hold celebrations on this day. Literary and art circles in various regions will hold commemorative forums or report meetings, combined with the current rectification movement in literature and art. According to Chairman Mao's literary and art principles, they will conduct in-depth reviews of literary and art work, criticize the many erroneous ideas existing in literary and art circles, and further study Chairman Mao's literary and art principles. Local newspapers and literary and art publications will publish commemorative articles.
    In addition to holding a forum, the Beijing literary and art circles, the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles is preparing to convene a meeting of literary and art cadres in Beijing participating in the literary and art rectification and study. Responsible comrades of the Federation will give a report on current literary and artistic thought and literary and art work issues.

### 毛主席在延安文艺座谈会上讲话十周年  全国各地文艺界准备举行庆祝

1952-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席在延安文艺座谈会上讲话十周年
    全国各地文艺界准备举行庆祝
    【新华社二十日讯】今年五月二十三日是毛主席在延安文艺座谈会上发表讲话的十周年，全国各地的文艺界都准备在这一天举行庆祝。各地的文艺界将举行纪念的座谈会或报告会，与当前的文艺整风相结合，根据毛主席的文艺方针，深入检查文艺工作，并批评文艺界中存在着的许多错误思想，进一步的学习毛主席文艺方针。各地报纸和文艺刊物都将发表纪念文章。
    北京文艺界除举行座谈会外，全国文联准备召集北京参加文艺整风学习的文艺干部会议，由文联负责同志作关于当前文艺思想和文艺工作问题的报告。
