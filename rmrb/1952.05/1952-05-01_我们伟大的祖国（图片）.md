Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: Yu Wengui
Date: 1952-05-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The great Chinese working class is enthusiastically launching a production competition to celebrate "May Day". Employees of the Tianjin Steel Plant have overfulfilled the April production increase and savings plan ahead of schedule. This is Pan Changyou, Deputy Director of the Steelmaking Department of Tianjin Steel Plant and National Model Worker, and Liu Changfu, worker of the wire rod section and Tianjin Special Model Worker. They are studying ways to further improve safety equipment and increase production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）
于文贵
1952-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    伟大的中国工人阶级正热烈地展开生产竞赛来庆祝“五一”节。天津钢厂的职工提前超额完成了四月份的增产节约计划。这是天津钢厂炼钢部副主任、全国劳动模范潘长有和线村部工人、天津市特等劳动模范刘长福，他们正在研究继续改进安全设备和提高产量的办法。
