    Our Great Motherland
    The Huachuan Water Conservancy Farm on the south bank of the Songhua River in Northeast China provides farmers participating in the farm with land, housing, and major agricultural machinery, greatly improving production efficiency. This is the tractor team of the Huachuan Water Conservancy Farm, pulling five-bottom plows to the fields for cultivation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国东北松花江南岸的桦川水利农场，供给参加这个农场的农民以土地、房屋和主要的农业机械，使生产效率大为提高。这是桦川水利农场的拖拉机队，它们正拉着五铧犁到田间去耕作。
