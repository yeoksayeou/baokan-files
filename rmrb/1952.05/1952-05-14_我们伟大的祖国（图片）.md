    Our Great Motherland
    Zhang Wenlan, a female worker and member of the Communist Youth League in the coarse spinning class of the Jilin Provincial Public-owned Flax Factory's spinning workshop, created a set of advanced operating methods that increased production efficiency by 12.57 percent, overcoming the imbalance between coarse spinning and fine spinning. Within three months, she increased the nation's wealth by the value of 113 tons of grain. This is Zhang Wenlan, and she has become a labor model respected by everyone.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    吉林省公营亚麻工厂纺织车间粗纺班的女工、青年团员张文兰，创造了一套先进的操作方法，提高生产效率百分之十二点五七，克服了粗纺和精纺不平衡的现象，三个月内为国家增产价值一百十三吨粮食的财富。这就是张文兰，她成了人人尊敬的劳动模范。
