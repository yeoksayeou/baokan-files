Title: Dedicated to You, Respected Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-25
Page: 4

Dedicated to You, Respected Chairman Mao
Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation
Respected Chairman Mao,
	We have seen you,
	It is truly boundless joy,
	Unspeakable happiness!
Respected Chairman Mao,
	We come from the heroic battlefields of Korea.
	In the smoke-filled trenches,
	On the snow-covered hills,
	In the rain-soaked forests…
	We are always longing for you.
Any violence,
	Cannot stop our fearless advance,
	Because there is the greatest leader—
	The wise Chairman Mao.
Because of you,
	There is glory, there is victory!
Respected Chairman Mao,
	Under your wise leadership,
	The motherland becomes younger and more beautiful.
	Workers show selfless labor and infinite wisdom,
	Constantly emerging new records,
	Emit brilliance.
	Farmers happily labor in the fields,
	Advancing along the road of "collectivism" and "mutual aid,"
	Striving for patriotic bumper harvests.
	Children are all:
	Always ready,
	To realize your great ideals,
	Study hard,
	Exercise.
	All ethnic groups across the country,
	Closely united under your banner,
	Living happily in the big family of the motherland.
	The "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" movements you initiated,
	Changed the bad habits of the old society,
	Defeated the rampant attacks of illegal bourgeois elements,
	Laid the foundation for the industrialization of the motherland.
	………………………………
	………………………………
From the peaks of the Kunlun Mountains,
	To the source of the Yellow River;
From the shores of the South China Sea,
	To the Yalu River,
Everywhere singing of victory,
Everywhere praising you,
—Most respected Chairman Mao!
Respected Chairman Mao,
	We are your good soldiers,
	We are the sons and daughters of a heroic motherland,
	We sincerely swear to you:
	For the honor of the motherland,
	For the Korean people,
	To defend world peace,
	We will surely achieve the final victory in the anti-aggression war!
Respected Chairman Mao,
	All the comrades of the Volunteer Army care about you.
	They asked us to bring a message:
	Greetings to dear Chairman Mao.
	The people of the whole country miss you,
	They asked us to bring a message:
	Greetings to dear Chairman Mao.
Every soldier,
Every person,
	Cordially wishes you,
	Boundless longevity!
	Eternal health!
We enthusiastically shout to you,
	Long live Chairman Mao!
	Long live! Long, long live!
May 23rd

### 献给您，敬爱的毛主席

1952-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    献给您，敬爱的毛主席
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团
    敬爱的毛主席，
    我们见到了您，
    真是无限的快乐，
    说不尽的欢欣！
    敬爱的毛主席，
    我们来自朝鲜的英雄阵地。
    在烟火弥漫的战壕里，
    在冰天雪地的山岗上，
    在大雨滂沱的树林里……
    我们时刻都在向往您。
    任何暴力，
    都不能阻止我们无畏前进，
    因为有一个最伟大的领袖——
    英明的毛主席。
    由于您，
    就是光荣，就是胜利！
    敬爱的毛主席，
    在您英明领导下，
    祖国变得更年青，更美丽。
    工人表现了忘我劳动和无穷智慧，
    不断涌现的新纪录，
    发射着光辉。
    农民愉快地劳动在田野上，
    沿着“集体”“互助”的道路前进，
    为爱国丰产而努力。
    少年儿童们，都在：
    时刻准备着，
    为实现您的伟大理想，
    好好学习，
    锻炼身体。
    全国各兄弟民族，
    亲密团结在您的旗帜下，
    快乐的生活在祖国的大家庭里。
    您提出的“三反”“五反”运动，
    改变了旧社会的恶习，
    打垮了不法资产阶级分子的猖狂进攻，
    奠定了祖国工业化的根基。
    ………………………………
    ………………………………
    从昆仑山巅，
    到黄河之源；
    从南海之滨，
    到鸭绿江畔，
    到处歌唱胜利，
    到处歌颂着您，
    ——最敬爱的毛主席！
    敬爱的毛主席，
    我们是您的好战士，
    我们是英雄祖国的儿女，
    我们赤诚的向您宣誓：
    为了祖国的荣誉，
    为了朝鲜人民，
    为保卫世界和平，
    我们一定取得反侵略战争的最后胜利！
    敬爱的毛主席，
    志愿军全体同志都关怀着您。
    他们托我们带来了一句话：
    问候亲爱的毛主席。
    全国的人民都想念您，
    他们托我们带来了一句话：
    问候亲爱的毛主席。
    每个战士，
    每一个人，
    都亲切地祝福您，
    万寿无疆！
    永远健康！
    我们热烈地向您高呼，
    毛主席万岁！
    万岁！万万岁！
    五月二十三日
