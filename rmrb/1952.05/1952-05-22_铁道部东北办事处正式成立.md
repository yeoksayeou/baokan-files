Title: Northeast Office of the Ministry of Railways Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 1

    The Northeast Office of the Ministry of Railways Officially Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] To meet the needs of railway business development and strengthen the unified management of railways nationwide, the Northeast Special Commissioner Office of the Ministry of Railways was reorganized on May 10th in accordance with the order of the Ministry of Railways, and the Northeast Office of the Ministry of Railways was formally established.
    The Northeast Office of the Ministry of Railways is an agency representing the Ministry of Railways in the Northeast region for supervising and inspecting transportation operations. After the establishment of the Northeast Office of the Ministry of Railways, the Qiqihar, Jilin, and Jinzhou Railway Administrations and the Andong Railway Branch Bureau will be directly and uniformly led by the Ministry of Railways.

### 铁道部东北办事处正式成立

1952-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    铁道部东北办事处正式成立
    【新华社二十日讯】为适应铁路业务发展的需要，加强全国铁路统一管理，铁道部驻东北特派员办事处遵照铁道部命令在五月十日改组，正式成立铁道部东北办事处。
    铁道部东北办事处是代表铁道部在东北区关于运输业务的督促监察机关。铁道部东北办事处成立以后，齐齐哈尔、吉林、锦州铁路管理局和安东铁路分局由铁道部直接统一领导。
