Title: Indian Trade Union Representative Nambiar Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1952-05-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 18th: Nambiar, a member of the Indian trade union delegation who came to our country at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations, arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai by train on the 17th. He left Guangzhou for Hong Kong via train this morning, the 18th, to return to his country. Li Jiebo, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, went to the station to see him off.

### 印度工会代表南比尔返国

1952-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工会代表南比尔返国
    【新华社广州十八日电】应中华全国总工会之邀，前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的印度工会代表团团员南比尔，于十七日自上海乘车到达广州。十八日晨离广州赴香港转道回国。前往车站欢送的有中华全国总工会代表李颉伯，广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云，广州市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟等。
