Title: "Former American POWs" Annual Meeting Delegates Oppose the U.S. Side's Unreasonable Claim of "Voluntary Repatriation"
Author: 
Date: 1952-05-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 23rd: According to the New York "Daily Worker" on the 16th, at the fifth annual meeting of "Former American POWs" recently held in Albuquerque, New Mexico, 400 delegates opposed the attitude of the U.S. State Department and Department of Defense in obstructing the Korean armistice negotiations with "voluntary repatriation," and demanded that the U.S. government agree to repatriate all prisoners of war by both sides after the signing of the Korean armistice agreement.
　　The "Former American POWs" annual meeting is organized by the backbone of veterans who participated in the Battle of Bataan in New Mexico. The annual meeting passed a resolution addressed to the U.S. Far East Command, stating their views. The resolution was proposed by Mrs. Genevieve Miller from Fan Petero, California, whose husband has gone missing in the Korean War.

### “美国前战俘”年会全体代表  反对美方“自愿遣返”无理主张

1952-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    “美国前战俘”年会全体代表
    反对美方“自愿遣返”无理主张
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】据纽约“工人日报”十六日讯，最近在美国新墨西哥州阿尔伯开克举行的第五次“美国前战俘”年会的全体到会代表四百人，反对美国国务院和国防部在朝鲜停战谈判中以“自愿遣返”来妨碍谈判的态度，要求美国政府同意在朝鲜停战协定签字之后，双方遣返全部俘虏。
    “美国前战俘”年会是新墨西哥州以前参加过巴丹战役的退伍军人的骨干分子所组织的。年会通过了致美国远东司令部的决议，表明他们的意见。这个决议是加利福尼亚州凡彼得罗地方的珍尼维甫·米勒夫人提出的，她的丈夫已在朝鲜战争中失纵。
