Title: Central South Institute for Nationalities Visiting Delegation Departs for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th:

    The Central South Institute for Nationalities visiting delegation, comprising over 170 members, departed from Shanghai on the morning of the 19th to continue their tour in Hangzhou. Song Richang and Jin Xuecheng, Deputy Secretaries-General of the East China Military and Administrative Committee; Lu Jin, Deputy Director of the General Office of the East China Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Li Qitao, Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Working Committee of the Youth League; Zhu Dezong, Vice Chairman of the East China Student Federation; and Song Xiheng, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Student Federation, and others went to the station to see them off.

    The visiting delegation arrived in Shanghai on May 12th and received a warm welcome from all sectors. They visited rubber, printing and dyeing, and cotton textile factories, visited Jiaotong University and other schools, and enthusiastically held get-togethers with workers and students. Before their departure, the East China Military and Administrative Committee and the Shanghai Municipal People's Government hosted a farewell banquet for them.

### 中南民族学院参观团赴杭州

1952-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中南民族学院参观团赴杭州
    【新华社上海十九日电】中南民族学院参观团一行一百七十多人，在十九日晨离开上海，前往杭州继续参观。华东军政委员会副秘书长宋日昌、金学成，中国新民主主义青年团华东工作委员会办公厅副主任陆进、青年团上海市工作委员会副书记李琦涛、华东学生联合会副主席朱德宗、上海市学生联合会副主席宋锡恒等亲往车站欢送。
    参观团在五月十二日到达上海，受到各界的热烈欢迎。他们参观了橡胶、印染、棉纺等工厂，访问了交通大学等学校，并热情地和工人及学生们举行联欢。临行前，华东军政委员会和上海市人民政府曾设宴欢送他们。
