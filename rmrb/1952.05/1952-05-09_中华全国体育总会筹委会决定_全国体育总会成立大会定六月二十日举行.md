Title: Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation Decides: Inaugural Meeting of the All-China Sports Federation Scheduled for June 20th
Author:
Date: 1952-05-09
Page: 3

    Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation Decides:
    Inaugural Meeting of the All-China Sports Federation Scheduled for June 20th
    The inaugural meeting of the All-China Sports Federation has been decided to be held in Beijing on June 20th. The Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation held a meeting on April 10th. The meeting was attended by Director Feng Wenbin, Deputy Directors Ma John, Xu Yingchao, Rong Gaotang, and Standing Committee members Jiang Ling, among a total of fifteen people. The meeting began with Director Feng Wenbin explaining various issues regarding the convening of the inaugural meeting of the All-China Sports Federation. The meeting then unanimously passed the resolution to convene the inaugural meeting of the All-China Sports Federation in Beijing on June 20th.
    The meeting discussed and approved the draft charter of the All-China Sports Federation, the draft trial regulations for the "Prepare for Labor and Defend the Motherland" sports system, and the outline of the 1952 sports work plan.
    The meeting also heard a report by Deputy Director and Secretary-General Rong Gaotang on recent work and international liaison work. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中华全国体育总会筹委会决定  全国体育总会成立大会定六月二十日举行

1952-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国体育总会筹委会决定
    全国体育总会成立大会定六月二十日举行
    中华全国体育总会成立大会已决定六月二十日在北京举行。中华全国体育总会筹备委员会常务委员会曾在四月十日举行会议，出席这次会议的有主任冯文彬、副主任马约翰、徐英超、荣高棠和常务委员江凌等共十五人。会议首由冯文彬主任作了关于召开中华全国体育总会成立大会的各项问题的说明。会议随即一致通过在六月二十日于北京召开中华全国体育总会成立大会。
    会议讨论并通过了中华全国体育总会章程草案、“准备劳动与保卫祖国”体育制度试行条例草案和一九五二年体育工作计划大纲。
    会议并听取了副主任兼秘书长荣高棠所作的关于最近工作情况及国际联络工作情况的报告。（新华社）
