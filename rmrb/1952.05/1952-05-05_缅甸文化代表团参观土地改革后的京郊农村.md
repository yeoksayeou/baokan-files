Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Visits Rural Suburbs of Beijing After Land Reform
Author:
Date: 1952-05-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 4th:

    The Burmese Cultural Delegation visited Taipingqiao in the southwestern suburbs of Beijing on the 4th to see the situation in the rural areas after land reform. The delegation visited farmers of various types. They met with model laborers of the village, Li Shanyuan, Pan Yongtai, etc., and visited the families of farmers Li Shancheng, Zhang Fu, etc. The farmers introduced the happy lives they have been living since the land reform to the delegation.
    In the evening, Liao Luyan, Director of the Counselor's Office of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government, held a discussion with the delegation on the issue of land reform. Liao Luyan introduced to the delegation the general situation of China's land reform, the Central People's Government's policies on land reform, and the new face of the countryside after land reform. He also discussed various issues related to land reform with the delegation.

### 缅甸文化代表团参观土地改革后的京郊农村

1952-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团参观土地改革后的京郊农村
    【新华社四日讯】缅甸文化代表团四日到北京西南郊的太平桥参观土地改革后农村的情况。代表团访问了各种类型的农民。他们会见了该村的劳动模范李善元、潘永泰等，并访问了农民李善成、张福等的家庭。农民们向代表团介绍了他们在土地改革后所过的幸福生活。
    晚上，中央人民政府政务院参事室主任廖鲁言和代表团举行关于土地改革问题的座谈会。廖鲁言向代表团介绍了中国土地改革的概况、中央人民政府对土地改革的政策，以及土地改革后农村的新面貌，并和代表团交谈了有关土地改革的各项问题。朝鲜谈判四日继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城四日电】四日朝鲜停战谈判代表团大会继续举行行政性会议。
