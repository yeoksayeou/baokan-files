Title: China Peace Committee Sends Congratulations to Canadian Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-05-12
Page: 4

    China Peace Committee Sends Congratulations to Canadian Peace Congress
    Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: The Canadian Peace Congress is being held on the 10th. The China People's Committee for World Peace has sent a telegram to congratulate it. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the Canadian Peace Congress:
    We extend our warm congratulations to your Congress. The American aggressors are rearming Japan, obstructing the Korean armistice negotiations, and recently, disregarding international law and human morality, are conducting bacteriological warfare in Korea and China, slaughtering peaceful residents. All this seriously threatens the security of the people of Asia and the Pacific region. We are willing to work with the peace-loving people of Canada to jointly stop the crimes of the American aggressors. We wish your Congress success.
    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Canadian people!
    China People's Committee for World Peace
    May 9, 1952

### 我保卫世界和平委会电贺加拿大和平大会

1952-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委会电贺加拿大和平大会
    【新华社十日讯】加拿大和平大会在十日举行，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致电祝贺。电文如下：加拿大和平大会：
    谨向你们的大会致以热烈的祝贺。美国侵略者重新武装日本，阻挠朝鲜停战谈判，最近更不顾国际公法与人类道义，在朝鲜和中国进行细菌战屠杀和平居民。这一切都严重地威胁亚洲及太平洋区域人民的安全。我们愿与加拿大爱好和平的人民在一道，共同制止美国侵略者这种罪行。谨祝你们的大会成功。
    中加人民友好万岁！中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    一九五二年五月九日
