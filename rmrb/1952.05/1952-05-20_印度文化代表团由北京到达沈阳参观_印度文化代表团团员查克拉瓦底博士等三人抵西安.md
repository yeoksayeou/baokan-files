Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Shenyang from Beijing for Visit; Three Members of the Indian Cultural Delegation, Dr. Chakravarti, Arrive in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1952-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 18th: The Indian Cultural Delegation, led by Mrs. Pandit, arrived in Shenyang on the 17th, accompanied by Chen Shuliang, Commissioner of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, for a visit. Those who greeted them at the station included: Liu Zhiming, Minister of Culture, and Luo Feng, Deputy Minister, of the Northeast People's Government; Che Xiangchen, Minister of Education; Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang, and Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor; Lu Xi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Northeast People's Government; Cao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Literary and Art Workers Association; Sai Ke, Director of the Northeast People's Art Theater; and many representatives from the cultural and educational circles of Shenyang. In the evening, the Ministry of Culture of the Northeast People's Government held a reception to welcome the delegation, followed by a film screening.
    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 18th: Dr. Chakravarti (Director of the Archaeological Survey of India), Professor Shi Jueyue (Dean of the Graduate School of Visva-Bharati University), and Professor Peng De (Professor of Painting at the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda), members of the Indian Cultural Delegation, arrived in Xi'an by train on May 18th. Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhu Maoqing, Director of the Xi'an Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education; Liu Shangda, Director of the Xi'an Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles; writer Zheng Boqi; historian Wu Bolun; painter Zhao Wangyun; and artist Chang Shuhong.
    The Xi'an Municipal People's Government hosted a banquet to welcome them at 6:00 PM that day.

### 印度文化代表团由北京到达沈阳参观  印度文化代表团团员查克拉瓦底博士等三人抵西安

1952-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团由北京到达沈阳参观
    印度文化代表团团员查克拉瓦底博士等三人抵西安
    【新华社沈阳十八日电】印度文化代表团在团长潘迪特夫人率领下，于十七日由我中央人民政府外交部专员陈叔亮陪同抵达沈阳参观。前往车站欢迎的有：东北人民政府文化部部长刘芝明、副部长罗烽，教育部部长车向忱，沈阳市市长朱其文、副市长焦若愚，东北人民政府外事局局长陆曦，东北文艺工作者协会副主席草明，东北人民艺术剧院院长塞克及沈阳市文化、教育界代表多人。晚上，东北人民政府文化部曾举行酒会欢迎代表团。会后并放映电影。
    【新华社西安十八日电】印度文化代表团团员查克拉瓦底博士（印度考古研究所所长）、师觉月教授（印度国际大学研究院院长）、彭德教授（印度巴洛达大学绘画教授）于五月十八日乘车抵西安。到车站欢迎的有西安市文教局局长朱茂青，西安市文学艺术界联合会主任刘尚达，作家郑伯奇，历史学家武伯伦，画家赵望云及艺术家常书鸿等。
    西安市人民政府于当日下午六时设宴欢迎。
