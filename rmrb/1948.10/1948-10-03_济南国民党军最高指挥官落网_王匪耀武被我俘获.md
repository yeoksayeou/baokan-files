Title: Top Kuomintang Military Commander in Jinan Captured: Bandit Wang Yaowu Captured by Our Forces
Author:
Date: 1948-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 1st: Wang Yaowu, the top Kuomintang military commander in Jinan, has been captured. After the fall of Jinan, Bandit Wang, disguised with four guards, fled eastward and was identified and arrested by our local armed forces in the Shouguang area north of Changle. He has now been escorted to the Political Department of the East China Military Region. Wang Yaowu is from Tai'an County, Shandong Province, a member of the Kuomintang Central Executive Committee, and formerly served as the commander of the Kuomintang 51st Division, commander of the 74th Army, commander-in-chief of the 24th Army Group, and commander of the 4th Army Corps. In early 1946, he served as the commander of the Kuomintang Second Pacification Zone, responsible for commanding the enemy troops in Shandong. In November of the same year, he concurrently served as the chairman of the Kuomintang Shandong Provincial Government.

### 济南国民党军最高指挥官落网　王匪耀武被我俘获

1948-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　济南国民党军最高指挥官落网
    王匪耀武被我俘获
    【新华社华东一日电】济南国民党军最高指挥官王耀武已被俘获，王匪于济南城破后，偕卫士四人化装向东潜逃，在昌乐以北寿光地带被我当地地方武装查明逮捕。现已押送至华东军区政治部。王耀武是山东泰安县人，国民党中央执行委员，曾任国民党军五十一师师长，七十四军军长，第二十四集团军总司令，第四方面军司令官，一九四六年初任国民党第二绥区司令员，负责指挥山东敌军，同年十一月，兼任国民党山东省政府主席。
