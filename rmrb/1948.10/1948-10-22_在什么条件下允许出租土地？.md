Author: 
Date: 1948-10-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency Mailbox

　　Under What Conditions is Land Leasing Permitted?
    Question: Article 11 of the Land Law Outline permits people who have been allocated land to "lease it out under specific conditions." What are these "specific conditions" referring to?
    Answer: The specific conditions referred to here are conditions regarding both the lessor and the rent amount. First, generally those who have been allocated land should not be allowed to lease it out. However, if the person allocated land is an orphan, widow, disabled person, or a family member lacking labor power of a revolutionary soldier or revolutionary staff member, then they should be allowed to lease it out. Second, for the land leased by such lessors, the local government should stipulate an appropriate rent amount to ensure the interests of both the lessor and the lessee.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　[Xinhua News Agency Northern Shaanxi, 15th]

### 在什么条件下允许出租土地？

1948-10-22
第1版()
专栏：新华社信箱

　　在什么条件下允许出租土地？
    问：土地法大纲第十一条允许分得土地的人“在特定条件下出租”。这里所说的“特定条件”是指什么条件？
    答：这里所说的特定条件是指出租者和租额两方面的条件。第一，对于一般分得土地的人应当不允许其出租，但是例如分得土地的人是孤寡废疾，或者是革命军人、革命职员的缺乏劳动力的家属等，那么就应当允许其出租。第二，对于这种出租者的土地应当由当地政府规定适当的租额，使出租者和承租者双方的利益都得到保障。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　【新华社陕北十五日电】
