Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Liberation of Jinan - This is One of the Most Serious Blows Dealt to the Enemy in the Development of More Than Two Years of Revolutionary War

Author:
Date: 1948-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party recently sent a telegram congratulating the East China People's Liberation Army on the great victory of liberating Jinan and annihilating 100,000 enemies. The original telegram is as follows:

Comrades Chen Yi, Rao Shushi, Zhang Yunyi, Su Yu, Tan Zhenlin, Xu Shiyou, Wang Jian'an, and all comrades of the East China People's Liberation Army:

We celebrate your great victory in liberating Jinan and annihilating 100,000 enemies. Your courageous, decisive, and swift action, and the winning over of General Wu Huawen's 96th Army to revolt, proves that the People's Liberation Army's ability to attack fortified positions has greatly improved, and the influence of the victory has shaken the interior of Chiang Kai-shek's reactionary army. This is one of the most serious blows dealt to the enemy in the development of more than two years of revolutionary war. We hope you will continue to work hard to annihilate more Chiang troops and liberate all the people of East China! The martyrs of the Jinan liberation campaign will be immortal!

　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 29, 1948

### 中共中央电贺解放济南  这是两年多革命战争发展中给予敌人的最严重打击之一

1948-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺解放济南
    这是两年多革命战争发展中给予敌人的最严重打击之一
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】中共中央顷电贺华东人民解放军解放济南、歼敌十万的伟大胜利。原电如下：
    陈毅、饶漱石、张云逸、粟裕、谭震林、许世友、王建安诸同志，并转华东人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们解放济南、歼敌十万的伟大胜利。你们这一勇猛果敢敏捷的行动，并争取了吴化文将军所率九十六军的起义，证明人民解放军的攻坚能力已大大提高，胜利影响已动摇了蒋介石反动军队的内部，这是两年多革命战争发展中给予敌人的最严重的打击之一。尚望继续努力，为歼灭更多蒋军、解放全华东人民而战！解放济南战役中的烈士们，永垂不朽！
    　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年九月二十九日
