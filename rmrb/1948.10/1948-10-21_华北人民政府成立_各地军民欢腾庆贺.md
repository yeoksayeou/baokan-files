Title: The Establishment of the North China People's Government: Jubilant Celebrations by Military and Civilians in Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1948-10-21
Page: 1

　　The Establishment of the North China People's Government
　　Jubilant Celebrations by Military and Civilians in Various Regions
　　Taihang Congratulatory Telegram
　　[Taihang Telegram] The administrative office, regional party committee, military region, and other organs here have jointly sent a telegram to congratulate the establishment of the North China People's Government. The original telegram reads: Chairman Dong of the North China People's Government, Vice Chairmen Bo, Lan, and Yang:
　　We are extremely excited to hear of the official establishment of the North China People's Government, and we offer our sincere congratulations. The birth of this new people's regime is truly the glorious fruit and the victorious symbol of the ten-year War of Resistance Against Japan and the more than two-year War of Liberation for the 45 million people of North China. Under your strong leadership, the five million people of Taihang will concentrate their will and their strength, carry forward the spirit of past arduous struggles, fully organize themselves, strive for production and construction, support the front lines, eliminate the Kuomintang bandits in North China, accelerate the final victory of the national liberation war, and struggle to establish a new democratic China.
　　Taihang Administrative Office, Regional Party Committee, Military Region, People's Armed Forces Department, Taihang Federation of Trade Unions.
　　Jinan Congratulatory Telegram
　　[Jinan Telegram] The Jinan Regional Party Committee and Administrative Office have sent separate telegrams to congratulate the establishment of the North China People's Government. The original telegrams are as follows: Chairman Dong, Vice Chairmen Bo, Lan, and Yang, and to the North China Government Committee:
　　The seven million people of Jinan are all rejoicing and celebrating the establishment of the North China People's Government. The North China People's Government is the result of the bloodshed, sacrifice, and arduous struggle of the millions of people of North China over the past decade. This achievement will bring a happy new democratic life to the people of North China. The North China People's Government also signifies the great convergence of the strength and will of the people of North China. This convergence will promote the demise of the Kuomintang bandit gang and accelerate the early arrival of national new democratic victory. We hereby send our most sincere telegram of congratulations.

### 华北人民政府成立　各地军民欢腾庆贺

1948-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府成立
  　各地军民欢腾庆贺
  　太行贺电
    【太行电】此间行署、区党委、军区等机关顷联电祝贺华北人民政府的成立。原电称：华北人民政府董主席、薄、蓝、杨副主席：
    欣闻华北人民政府的正式成立，我们以极度兴奋的心情敬贺，这一崭新的人民政权的诞生，实是全华北四千五百万人民十年抗日战争两年多解放战争光辉的果实，胜利的标志，太行五百万人民将在你们坚强的领导下，意志集中，力量集中，发扬过去艰苦斗争的精神，充分组织起来，为努力生产，建设，支援前线，扫清华北国民党匪徒，加速全国解放战争最后胜利的到来，建立新民主主义的新中国而奋斗。
    太行行署、区党委、军区、人民武装部、太行总工会。
    冀南贺电
    【冀南电】冀南区党委、行署顷分别电贺华北人民政府成立。原电如下：董主席、薄、蓝、杨副主席并转华北政府委员会：
    我们冀南七百万人民，都在欢欣鼓舞庆祝华北人民政府的成立，华北人民政府，是华北千百万人民十数年来流血牺牲，历尽艰辛奋斗的成果。这个成果，将给华北人民带来新民主主义的幸福生活。华北人民政府，也标帜着华北人民力量与意志的大汇合。这个汇合，将促进国民党匪帮的灭亡，加速全国新民主主义胜利的早日到来。我们谨以最大热诚致电祝贺。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共冀南区党委　十月十五日董主席、薄、蓝、杨副主席并转华北政府委员会：
    欣知华北人民政府正式成立，华北千百万群众从此开始了新民主主义建设的幸福生活，并为支援前线，早日彻底消灭国民党匪帮加强了基础。喜讯传来，万民腾欢，我们在首长们的领导下，一定要加强组织性、纪律性、服从统一集中、完成土改、提高生产，努力支援前线，在已有的基础上，进行可能与必要的诸般建设，以争取华北以至全中国的最后胜利。谨代表冀南人民驰电致贺。
    　　　　冀南行署主任王任重、副主任范若一、高元贵　十月十五日
