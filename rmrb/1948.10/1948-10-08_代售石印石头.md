Lithographic Stone for Sale
We are selling surplus lithographic stones, a special and valuable product of Lin County, Taihang Mountains. These stones have been tested extensively and praised by the Luxibei Printing Factory in our city, the Raoyang Industrial and Commercial Department Printing Section in Jizhong, the Tongqi Match Company Printing Section in the Jiluyu District, the newspaper office in the same district, and the Beihai Huimin Public Printing Factory. The results have been excellent. Their usage is exactly the same as German artificial stones, with no other differences. Each plate can print up to 10,000 copies.

Those who wish to purchase may first visit the various factories that have experimented with them to observe and inquire. After that, please purchase from our office.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Sales Office: Yangji Street, Linqing City
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Heji Lithographic Stone Bureau
