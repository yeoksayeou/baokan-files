Title: Hu bandits' heartland continues to be struck; 400 enemies annihilated west of the Luo River
Author:
Date: 1948-10-26
Page: 2
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 23rd. Hu Zongnan's bandit forces, who were severely beaten east of the Luo River, were subjected to continuous strikes west of the Luo River as he routinely propagandized so-called "great victories" after defeats. A unit of the People's Liberation Army, operating in the Weibei Plain, the heartland of the Hu bandits, after capturing Jincheng and Jiangyuan towns, belonging to Pucheng County, on the 15th and 16th, annihilated a battalion of the 93rd Regiment of the 31st Brigade of the Regular 27th Division of the enemy that had invaded from Tongguan and Pucheng in Xingshi Town, 20 li west of Pucheng, on the 17th. On the same day, another unit of the People's Liberation Army routed the Baishui Security Regiment and the Shaanxi Security 5th Regiment in Yaoshan, 20 li northwest of Pucheng, and Taochi Town, 10 li north of Pucheng. The day before, the People's Liberation Army attacked Liuqu Town, a stronghold of the Hu bandits, about 30 li northeast of Fuping. In the above battles, a total of more than 420 enemy soldiers were killed, wounded, or captured. Ninety-six long and short guns and other supplies were seized.

### 胡匪心脏地区续遭打击洛河以西歼敌四百

1948-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　胡匪心脏地区续遭打击洛河以西歼敌四百
    【新华社陕中前线二十三日电】在洛河以东被痛击的胡匪宗南，当他照例宣传战败时的所谓“大捷”的时候，又在洛河以西受到连续的打击。活动在胡匪心脏地区渭北平原的解放军一部，继十五、十六两日攻克蒲城县属的晋城、姜原两镇后，十七日在蒲城西二十里的兴市镇，歼灭自同官及蒲城出犯之敌整二十七师三十一旅九十三团的一个营。同日解放军另部在蒲城西北二十里的尧山及蒲城北十里的陶池镇，击溃白水保安团及陕保五团。在此前一日，解放军袭击富平东北约三十里的胡匪据点流曲镇。以上各次战斗，共毙伤俘敌四百二十余名。缴获长短枪九十六支及其他物资一部。
