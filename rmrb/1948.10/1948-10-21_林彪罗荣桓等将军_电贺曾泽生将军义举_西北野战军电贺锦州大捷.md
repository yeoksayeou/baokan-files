Title: Generals Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, et al. Cable Congratulations to General Zeng Zesheng on his Righteous Uprising; Northwest Field Army Cables Congratulations on the Great Victory at Jinzhou
Author:
Date: 1948-10-21
Page: 1

　　Generals Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, et al.
　　Cable Congratulations to General Zeng Zesheng on his Righteous Uprising
　　Northwest Field Army Cables Congratulations on the Great Victory at Jinzhou
    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 19th -- Lin Biao, Commander of the Northeast People's Liberation Army, Luo Ronghuan, Political Commissar, Liu Yalou, Chief of Staff, Tan Zheng, Director of the Political Department, and others jointly cabled congratulations to General Zeng Zesheng and all officers and soldiers of the 60th Army, saying: We are overjoyed to hear that your department, under your leadership, has staged an anti-Chiang uprising. This glorious and righteous action of your department is a heavy blow to the Chiang bandits in the Northeast and an important contribution to the people of the Northeast. We hereby send our congratulations.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 19th -- All commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army Northwest Field Army have sent a cable congratulating the great victory at Jinzhou. The cable stated: "Just as our Northwest Field Army is continuing its efforts to expand the autumn offensive, we are delighted to hear of your great victory in liberating Jinzhou City, a strategic stronghold in the western Liaoning corridor, and annihilating 100,000 enemy troops, which gives our army even greater excitement. We will continue to work hard, annihilate large numbers of enemies, and cooperate with your offensive operations."

### 林彪罗荣桓等将军　电贺曾泽生将军义举　西北野战军电贺锦州大捷

1948-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　林彪罗荣桓等将军
  　电贺曾泽生将军义举
  　西北野战军电贺锦州大捷
    【新华社东北前线十九日电】东北人民解放军司令员林彪、政治委员罗荣桓、参谋长刘亚楼、政治部主任谭政等联名电贺曾泽生将军及六十军全体官兵同志称：闻贵部在吾兄领导下举行反蒋起义，不胜欣慰。贵部此一光荣义举，对东北蒋匪实为一沉重打击，对东北人民则是一个重要的贡献，特电致贺。
    【新华社陕中前线十九日电】人民解放军西北野战军全体指战员顷电贺锦州大捷。电称：“正当我西北野战军再接再厉，扩张秋季攻势作战之际，欣闻你们一举解放辽西走廊战略重镇锦州城、歼敌十万人的伟大胜利，更予我军以莫大兴奋。我们当继续努力，大量歼灭敌人，配合你们的攻势作战。”
