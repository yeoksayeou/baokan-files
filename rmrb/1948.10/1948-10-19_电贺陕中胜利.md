Title: Telegram of Congratulations on the Victory in Central Shaanxi
Author:
Date: 1948-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations to the Northwest People's Liberation Army on its victory east of the Luo River. The telegram reads:

To Comrades Peng Dehuai, He Long, Lin Boqu, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Zongxun, Zhao Shoushan, and to all comrades of the Northwest People's Liberation Army:

We celebrate your great victory in annihilating the bulk of Hu bandit’s entire 17th Division, entire 38th Division, and part of the entire 65th Division east of the Luo River, and liberating the six county towns of Chaoyi, Pingmin, Baishui, Taiyang, Chengcheng, and Gouyi.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 15, 1948

### 电贺陕中胜利

1948-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　电贺陕中胜利
    【新华社陕北十七日电】中共中央电贺西北人民解放军在洛河以东的胜利。电称：
    彭德怀、贺龙、林伯渠、习仲勋，张宗逊、赵寿山诸同志并转西北人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们此次在洛河以东歼灭胡匪整十七师、整三十八师大部及整六十五师一部，并解放朝邑、平民、白水、邰阳、澄城、枸邑六座县城的巨大胜利。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会　
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十月十五日
