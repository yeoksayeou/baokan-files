Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Liaoxi Victory
Author:
Date: 1948-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has sent a telegram congratulating the Liaoxi Victory. The telegram reads: Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Gao Gang, Chen Yun, and all comrades of the Northeast People's Liberation Army: Congratulations on your great victory in annihilating five armies and twelve divisions of the main force of the enemy in Northeast China in the Liaoxi area. In two weeks, our Northeast Army has won three major victories in Jinzhou, Changchun, and Liaoxi, causing the enemy to lose 26 entire divisions, totaling approximately 300,000 troops, making a great contribution to the overall national war situation. It is hoped that the entire army will be encouraged to continue its efforts, fight to completely annihilate the Northeast bandit troops, and liberate Shenyang!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 28, 1948

### 中共中央电贺辽西大捷

1948-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺辽西大捷
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】中共中央顷致电祝贺辽西大捷。电称：林彪、罗荣桓、高岗、陈云诸同志及东北人民解放军全体同志们：庆祝你们此次在辽西地区歼灭东北敌军主力五个军十二个师的伟大胜利。东北我军在两星期内连获锦州、长春、辽西三次大捷，使敌人损失二十六个整师共约三十万人的兵力，对于全国战局贡献极大。尚望激励全军再接再厉，为全歼东北匪军，解放沈阳而战！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十月二十八日
