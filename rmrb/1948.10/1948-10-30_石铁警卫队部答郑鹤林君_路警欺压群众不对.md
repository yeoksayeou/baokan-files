Title:石家庄-铁道警卫队部答郑鹤林君: Railway Police Bullying the People is Wrong
Author:
Date: 1948-10-30
Page: 2

　　Shijiazhuang-Railway Guard Corps' Reply to Mr. Zheng Helin
  　Railway Police Bullying the People is Wrong
    To the Editor: Regarding the matter of railway guards bullying the people raised by Zheng Helin in the criticism column of the People's Daily on September 17th, we believe that the spirit of this suggestion is very good and will greatly help our future work. We have conducted a deep self-criticism and sincerely accept this suggestion. If similar incidents occur again in the future, it would be best to ascertain the insignia and names and submit them to our team for investigation. We hope that you will provide more suggestions in the future so that the guard team can truly serve the people.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Shijiazhuang-Railway Guard Corps Headquarters)

### 石铁警卫队部答郑鹤林君　路警欺压群众不对

1948-10-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　石铁警卫队部答郑鹤林君
  　路警欺压群众不对
    编辑同志：九月十七日人民日报批评栏内郑鹤林所提铁路警卫队员欺压群众一事，我们认为提的这个意见精神很好，对我们今后的工作帮助很大，我们已作了深刻的检讨，并诚恳的接受这个建议，如果再有以上类似事情发生，最好查明佩戴符号姓名，送交本队，便于追查，并希今后多提供意见，以便警卫队作到为人民服务。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（石铁警卫大队部）
