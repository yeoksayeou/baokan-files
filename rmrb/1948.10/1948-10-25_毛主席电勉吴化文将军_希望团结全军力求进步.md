Title: Chairman Mao Sends a Telegram to General Wu Huawen, Hoping to Unite the Entire Army and Strive for Progress
Author:
Date: 1948-10-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd. General Wu Huawen and others, who initiated the Jinan Uprising, have issued a circular telegram, as reported in yesterday's news. It is now learned that Chairman Mao of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations today. The telegram reads as follows:
    To General Wu, Brigade Commander Yang, Brigade Commander Zhao, Brigade Commander He, and all officers and soldiers:

    We are extremely pleased to learn that you, General, and others led your troops in an uprising, issued a circular telegram, and resolved to participate in the cause of people's liberation. The Chinese Communist Party, standing on the side of the people, warmly welcomes any officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang army, regardless of their past behavior, as long as they can, at this crucial moment in the People's Liberation War, awaken to the situation, break away from the reactionary leadership of the Kuomintang government, and join the ranks of the People's Liberation Army, resolutely opposing American imperialism and its running dogs, the Kuomintang reactionaries. Your righteous act is in line with the hopes of the people and is deeply gratifying. It is hoped that you will unite the entire army, strive for progress, improve relations between officers and soldiers, and between the army and the people, and fight for the victory of the revolutionary war throughout the country.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, October 22nd

### 毛主席电勉吴化文将军　希望团结全军力求进步

1948-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电勉吴化文将军
  　希望团结全军力求进步
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】济南起义的吴化文将军等发表通电，已志昨讯。兹悉，中共中央毛主席于今日致电祝贺。电文如下：吴军长、杨旅长、赵旅长、何旅长并转全体官兵均鉴：
    贵军长等率部起义，发表通电，决心参加人民解放事业，极为欣慰。中国共产党站在人民立场上，对于任何国民党军队的官兵们，不问其过去行为如何，只要他们能够在人民解放战争的紧要关头，幡然觉悟，脱离国民党政府的反动领导，加入人民解放军阵营，坚决反对美国帝国主义及其走狗国民党反动派，即表示热烈欢迎。贵军长等此次义举，符合人民的希望，深堪庆贺。尚望团结全军，力求进步，改善官兵关系、军民关系，为革命战争在全国的胜利而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东　十月二十二日
