Title: Italian Communist Party Holds Central Committee Plenary Session; Togliatti Attends After Recovering Health
Author: 
Date: 1948-10-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th. Electromessage News Agency reports: The Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party began holding a plenary session on the 23rd. Togliatti has recovered his health and is attending. The main reports at the meeting are one by the Deputy Secretary-General of the Italian Communist Party, Gian Carlo Pajetta, on "The Party's Current Political and Ideological Orientation," and one by the Secretary-General of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, Vittorio, on "The Communist Party's Struggle for Trade Union Unity." The Central Committee plenary session is scheduled to last for three days.

### 意共举行中央全会　托格里亚蒂已恢复健康出席参加

1948-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　意共举行中央全会
    托格里亚蒂已恢复健康出席参加
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】电通社讯：意大利共产党中央委员会于二十三日起举行全会，托格里亚蒂已恢复健康并出席参加。会上之主要报告，一为意共副总书记降哥关于“党当前的政治与思想意识方针”的报告，一为意大利总工会总书记维托里奥关于“共产党为职工会的统一而斗争”的报告。中央全会的会期预定为三天。
