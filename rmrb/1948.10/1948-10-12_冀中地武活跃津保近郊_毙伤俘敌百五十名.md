Title: Jizhong Local Armed Forces Active Near Tianjin-Baoding, Killing, Wounding, and Capturing 150 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1948-10-12
Page: 2

(Jizhong, 8th) Jizhong local armed forces are actively operating in the vicinity of Baoding and Tianjin. On the 28th of last month, a unit of the Eighth Military Sub-district's local armed forces raided Nantianmen, about 60 miles southeast of Tianjin, capturing four enemies. On the evening of the 29th, a unit of the same military sub-district's local armed forces captured the Goushi stronghold, more than 90 miles southwest of Tianjin, capturing 52 enemies. On the 30th, a battalion of the 284th Regiment of Chiang Kai-shek's 95th Division attacked Beilianzhuang, north of the Jianhe River in southern Tianjin, but was repelled by our army, which annihilated more than 60 enemies. On the evening of the 4th of this month, a unit of the local armed forces in southern Tianjin raided Datun, southeast of Jinghai, capturing 12 enemies. On October 2nd, the Ninth Military Sub-district's local armed forces ambushed the Chiang Kai-shek puppet "Save the People Vanguard Team" in Dongwuyao and Wozhuang, more than ten miles southwest of Baoding, killing, wounding, and capturing 24 enemies. In the above battles, a total of 152 enemies were killed, wounded, and captured, and one machine gun, 57 rifles and carbines, and two submachine guns were seized.

### 冀中地武活跃津保近郊  毙伤俘敌百五十名

1948-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀中地武活跃津保近郊
    毙伤俘敌百五十名
    【冀中八日电】冀中地方武装积极活动在保定天津近郊。上月二十八日八分区地武一部奔袭天津东南约六十里之南天门，俘敌四名。二十九日晚，该分区地武一部，攻克津西南九十余里之沟石据点，俘敌五十二名。三十日，蒋匪九十五师二八四团一个营，向津南碱河以北之北连庄进攻，被我军击退，歼敌六十余。本月四日晚，津南地武一部，奔袭静海东南之大屯，俘敌十二名。九分区地武于十月二日在保定西南十余里之东五窑、窝庄伏击保定蒋伪“救民先锋队”，毙伤俘敌二十四名。以上战斗共毙伤敌一百五十二名，缴机枪一挺、长短枪五十七支、冲锋枪二支。
