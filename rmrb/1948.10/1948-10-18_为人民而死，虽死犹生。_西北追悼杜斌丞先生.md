Title: "To die for the people is weightier than Mount Tai." Northwest Memorial for Mr. Du Bin-cheng

Author:
Date: 1948-10-18
Page: 2

　　"To die for the people is weightier than Mount Tai."
  　Northwest Memorial for Mr. Du Bin-cheng
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 17th] More than a thousand people from all walks of life in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region gathered on October 9th to commemorate Mr. Du Bin-cheng, the Northwest leader of the China Democratic League who was murdered by the Kuomintang a year ago. The mourning documents displayed at the meeting included Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China’s eulogy: "To die for the people is weightier than Mount Tai." Those who spoke at the meeting included Chairman Lin Boqu of the Border Region Government, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan, and Deputy Commander Zhao Shoushan.

### “为人民而死，虽死犹生。”　西北追悼杜斌丞先生

1948-10-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　“为人民而死，虽死犹生。”
  　西北追悼杜斌丞先生
    【新华社西北十七日电】陕甘宁边区各界千余人，于十月九日集会追悼一年前被国民党杀害的民盟西北负责人杜斌丞先生。会场上陈列的哀挽文件中，包括中共中央毛泽东主席的挽词：“为人民而死，虽死犹生”。在会上演说的，有边府林伯渠主席、杨明轩副主席、赵寿山副司令员等。
