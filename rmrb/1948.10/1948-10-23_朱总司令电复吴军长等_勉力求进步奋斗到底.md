Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu Telegraphed a Reply to Military Commander Wu and Others: Striving for Progress and Fighting to the End
Author:
Date: 1948-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st. Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the People's Liberation Army recently telegraphed a reply to Military Commander Wu Huawen and others' circular telegram, saying:

To Military Commander Wu, Brigade Commander Yang, Brigade Commander Zhao, Brigade Commander He, and relayed to all officers and soldiers: I was extremely gratified to read your circular telegram of September 25th. Your determination to stand on the side of the people, to fight for the expulsion of the aggressive forces of American imperialism, and to overthrow the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang deserves the warm welcome of the people of the whole country. It is hoped that you will adhere to this policy, strive for progress, and fight to the end. Your future is bound to be bright.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhu De, October 13th

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 21st. Generals Chen Yi, Rao Shushi, Zhang Yunyi, Su Yu, and Tan Zhenlin of the East China People's Liberation Army replied to Military Commander Wu Huawen and others' circular telegram of September 25th, saying:

To Military Commander Wu, Brigade Commander Yang, Brigade Commander Zhao, Brigade Commander He, and relayed to all commanders and combatants: I was extremely gratified to read your circular telegram of September 25th. Your righteous action on the Jinan front not only showed all your commanders a bright road, but also pointed out a bright road for officers and soldiers of the National Government army throughout the country. Now the People's Liberation War is developing victoriously throughout the country. It is hoped that you will unite the entire army and fight alongside the People's Liberation Army of the whole country to fight to the end for the expulsion of the aggressive forces of American imperialism and to overthrow the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang.

　　　　　　　Chen Yi, Rao Shushi, Zhang Yunyi, Su Yu, Tan Zhenlin, October 13th

### 朱总司令电复吴军长等　勉力求进步奋斗到底

1948-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱总司令电复吴军长等
  　勉力求进步奋斗到底
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】人民解放军朱总司令顷电复吴化文军长等通电称：
    吴军长、杨旅长、赵旅长、何旅长、并转全体官兵均鉴：接读九月二十五日通电，极为欣慰。你们决心站在人民立场上，为驱逐美国帝国主义的侵略势力，为打倒国民党反动统治而奋斗，这个决心，值得全国人民的热烈欢迎。希望你们本此方针，力求进步，奋斗到底，你们的前途，必然是光明的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　朱德十月十三日
    【新华社华东二十一日电】华东人民解放军陈毅、饶漱石、张云逸、粟裕、谭震林诸将军复吴化文军长等九月二十五日通电称：
    吴军长、杨旅长、赵旅长、何旅长、并转全体指挥员战斗员同志们：接读九月二十五日通电，极为欣慰。贵军在济南前线的义举，不但贵军全体指挥员找到了一条光明大道，而且给全国国民党军队官兵指出了一条光明大道。现在人民解放战争正在全国各地胜利地发展，尚望诸兄团结全军，与全国人民解放军并肩作战，为驱逐美国帝国主义的侵略势力为打倒国民党反动统治而奋斗到底。
    　　　　　　　陈毅、饶漱石、张云逸、粟裕、谭震林十月十三日
