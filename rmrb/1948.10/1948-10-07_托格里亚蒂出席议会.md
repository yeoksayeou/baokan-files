Title: Togliatti Attends Parliament
Author:
Date: 1948-10-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: TASS, Rome: After recovering his health, Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti attended a parliamentary session for the first time on September 30th. When Togliatti appeared in the Chamber of Deputies, members of all parties stood up and gave him a long ovation. After Togliatti took his seat among the communist members, Speaker Gronchi congratulated Togliatti on his recovery on behalf of the entire parliamentary group. In a brief reply, Togliatti said, "I would like to take this opportunity to extend my greetings to all Italians who expressed indignation at the disgraceful assassination attempt on July 14th, including those who were arrested and imprisoned for participating in protest movements."
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: TASS: Nenni, the leader of the left wing of the Italian Socialist Party, delivered a speech at the Bologna Socialist Party newspaper commemorative meeting on September 26th, denouncing the propagandists and organizers of the Third World War. Nenni pointed out the Soviet Union's desire for peace and insisted that "the war party exists in the United States and Western European capitalist countries." Nenni sharply criticized the De Gasperi government's foreign policy, declaring, "If the current government continues its policy of helping foreigners enslave Italy, we are prepared to resume the resistance movement against German Nazis and Italian fascists from 1940 to 1945."

### 托格里亚蒂出席议会

1948-10-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　托格里亚蒂出席议会
    【新华社陕北四日电】塔斯社罗马讯：意共总书记托格里亚蒂恢复健康后，于九月三十日首次出席议会会议。当托格里亚蒂在众院出现时，各党派议员都起立并向托氏致以长时间的欢呼。托氏在共产党议员席间就座后，议长格朗基即代表全体议会党团祝贺托氏恢复健康。托氏于简短答词中称，“我愿趁此说话的机会，向一切对七月十四日可耻谋害表示愤慨的意大利人，包括那些因参加抗议运动而被逮捕和监禁的人们致意。”
    【新华社陕北四日电】塔斯社讯：意大利社会党左翼领袖南尼，于九月廿六日在波伦亚社会党报纸纪念会上发表演说，斥责第三次世界大战的宣传者与组织者。南尼指出苏联的和平愿望，并力称“战争党存在于美国和西欧资本主义国家里”。南尼尖锐抨击加斯贝利政府的外交政策，宣称，“如果现政府继续其帮助外国人奴役意大利的政策，则我们准备重新进行一九四○至一九四五年间反对德国纳粹与意大利法西斯的抵抗运动。”
