　　A Ten-Day Market Report
    (September 19th - 29th)
    In the past ten days, in our area and neighboring liberated areas, because the autumn harvest is nearing completion and people are busy sowing wheat, transportation has decreased, and the exchange of goods in various places has been delayed, resulting in uneven price fluctuations. In the Kuomintang-controlled areas, since the "currency reform," prices have soared daily, but due to the violent suppression by the Chiang bandits, open trading has turned to the black market. The specific situation of various goods in various places is reported as follows:
    Grain
    Recently, wheat from the Hebei-Shandong-Henan area has flowed south to the Yellow River, and the price has increased slightly. The price of other grains has also increased due to the reduction in market supply. The price of rice and wheat in Puyang and Liaocheng has increased by 5% compared to fifteen days ago. The situation in southern Hebei is similar to that in Hebei-Shandong-Henan. In Handan, the price of millet and wheat has increased by about 10% compared to the previous ten days. In Linqing, the price of rice and wheat has increased by 10-12%. Hengshui has also seen an increase due to the rising grain prices in Xinji and Shizhuang in the west, with rice and wheat prices rising by 11-15%. In central Hebei, grain prices have been affected by crop failures due to floods and pests in some areas. On September 29th, the price of millet in Xinji rose sharply by 25%, while wheat and miscellaneous grains rose by 4-10%. In Bozhen and Duancun, the price of rice and wheat rose by 4-10%. In Cangxian, the price has remained stable due to its proximity to the Bohai grain-producing area. Shizhuang has also seen an increase recently due to the high grain prices in the east, with the price of millet rising by 15% compared to the previous ten days, and wheat and other grains rising by 4-10%. In addition to the above-mentioned areas in our region where grain prices have risen significantly, the following areas have seen prices decline: In the North Yue area, the price of millet in Hunyuan fell by 14.5% on the 28th, while corn and sorghum fell by 3-4%. In Yangquan, the price of rice and wheat fell by 200 yuan per斗, and corn fell by 500 yuan. In Jinzhong, the price of grain in Yuci fell by 10-14% compared to the previous ten days. In Taihang, the price of rice and wheat in Changzhi and Xingtai fell by an average of about 8%. In Taiyue, the price of millet in Guodao fell by 11%, and corn fell by 4-5%.
    In the neighboring Dezhou area, grain prices have generally fallen, with each type of grain falling by 0.5%. In the Jin-Sui area, the autumn harvest this year has been generally good, and grain prices have fallen sharply in some places. In Daixian, the price of sorghum fell by 41.2% on the 20th, and black beans fell by 10%.
    In the Kuomintang-controlled area of Tianjin, the black market is prevalent, and grain prices are fluctuating again. On the 17th, the black market price of a bag of flour was 10,660, while the "official price" was 10,500. The black market price of rice (per斤) was 0.226, while the "official price" was 0.212. The black market price of corn flour (per斤) was 0.133, while the "official price" was 0.090. The average black market price of the above three items exceeded the official price by 3-4% (this disparity is still developing). If compared with the price limit set by the Chiang bandit authorities on August 19th, the black market price of grain has risen by about 45% as of now (mid-September). This is not only the case in Tianjin, but also higher in other cities. On the 15th, the price of millet in Xinxiang has exceeded the level of August 19th by one time (originally 1,500, now 3,000).
    Cotton
    The trend of cotton prices in the inland areas this ten-day period is the same as the situation of grain. The areas with high grain prices also have high cotton prices, and vice versa. In the neighboring Bohai and Jin-Sui areas, due to the arrival of the season, the masses are preparing clothing and bedding, and the cotton market is active and prices are rising. The rise and fall of prices in various places are listed below: In the Hebei-Shandong-Henan area, cotton is flowing towards Jiaodong, and the price in Puyang and Liaocheng has increased by 5%. In southern Hebei, cotton in Handan is flowing to Shizhuang, and the price has increased by 15%. In central Hebei, cotton prices have risen in various places: Xinji increased by 11.5%, Duancun and Bozhen increased by 4%, and Wurenqiao increased by 8%. In Yangquan, North Yue area, the price has increased by 200 yuan per斤, but in Laiyuan and Hunyuan in the north, the price has fallen by 7.5-10%. In Yuci, Jinzhong, the price has fallen by 5%. Prices in various places in Taihang are stable, but the price in Xingtai has fallen by 100 yuan per斤. In Taiyue, the price in Jincheng is stable, but the price in Guodao has fallen by 80 yuan per斤. In Yicheng, the price of cotton has increased by 8% due to the flow of cotton from southern Shanxi to Luoyang.
    In Dezhou, the price increased by 8% from the 22nd to the 26th, and in Daixian, the price increased by 0.3%.
    There is a severe shortage of cotton in the Kuomintang-controlled areas. The bandit authorities in Tianjin strictly control the price, stipulating that the price of cotton shall not exceed 4.8 million (Chiang currency) per斤, but the China Textile Corporation absorbs it at 5-5.2 million, and the black market price has reached 5.8 million yuan. Recently, the bandit authorities have established a cotton exchange in Nankai, which is exclusively absorbed by the China Textile Corporation, prohibiting private trading, and using unscrupulous merchants to seize cotton from our area. All localities should pay attention!
    Cloth, Oil, Salt, and Foreign Goods
    The prices of native cloth, edible oil, edible salt, and foreign goods have generally been stable in the past ten days, and the situation is roughly the same as in the previous ten days. However, the price of native cloth in central Hebei has also slightly increased due to the rising prices of grain and cotton, but the increase is not large. ### 一旬商情
华北贸易总公司资料室
1948-10-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　一旬商情
    （九月十九日——廿九日）
    旬日来本区及友邻解放区，由于秋收将毕又赶忙于播种小麦，以致运输减少，各地物资交流迟滞，物价表现升降参差不齐。国民党统治区自“币改”以来，物价日益飞腾，但由于蒋匪暴力镇压，公开交易已转向黑市。兹将各地各物具体情况分别报导于次：
    粮食
    冀鲁豫近来小麦向黄河南流，价稍见提，其他各种粮食，因上市量缩减价亦显昂，濮阳、聊城二地米麦比十五日前升百分之五。冀南与冀鲁豫相仿。邯郸小米小麦比上一旬升百分之十左右。临清市米、麦升百分之十——十二。衡水因西边辛集石庄粮价上升的影响，亦逞上升，米麦升百分之十一—十五。冀中因部分地区遭受水灾虫灾，农产歉收，影响粮价上升。辛集二十九日小米猛涨百分之二十五，小麦及杂粮升百分之四至百分之十。泊镇端村米麦升百分之四——十。沧县因毗连渤海产粮区价持平稳。石庄近来因东面粮价高的影响亦随之上升，比上一旬小米升百分之十五，小麦及其他粮食升百分之四——十。本区除上述各地粮价显升外，以下各地均趋下跌：北岳区浑源二十八日小米下跌百分之十四·五，玉米红粮下跌百分之三——四，阳泉市米、麦每斗下跌二○○元，玉米跌五○○元。晋中榆次粮食比上一旬下跌百分之十——十四。太行长治邢台二地米麦平均下跌百分之八左右。太岳郭道小米下跌百分之十一，玉米跌百分之四——五。
    友邻区德（州）市粮食平落，各粮下跌百分之○·五。晋绥区本年秋收一般都好，有的地方粮价猛跌，代县二十日红粮下跌百分之四一·二，黑豆跌百分之十。
    国民党统治区天津黑市流行，粮价又在波动中。十七日兵船粉（袋）暗市一○·六六○，“官价”为一○·五○○。大米（斤）暗市○·二二六，“官价”为○·二一二。玉米面（斤）暗市○·一三三，“官价”为○·○九○，以上三种暗价平均超过官价百分之三——四（这种悬殊尚在继续发展中）。如果以蒋匪当局八月十九日限价来比较，粮食截至现在（九月中旬）为止，暗市价已涨到百分之四十五左右，不惟津市如此，其他城市尤高，新乡十五日小米价格已超过八月十九日的水平一倍（原为一·五○○今则为三·○○○）。
    棉花
    本旬内地棉价趋势，与粮食情况相同，粮食高的地区棉亦高，反之粮低棉亦跌，友邻渤海及晋绥二区，由于季节来临，群众备置衣被棉市活跃价格上提。各地升降情况列下：冀鲁豫棉花近走胶东，濮阳聊城升百分之五。冀南邯郸棉花走石庄价升百分之十五。冀中各地棉价均升：辛集升百分之十一点五，端村泊镇升百分之四，伍仁桥升百分之八。北岳阳泉市每斤升二○○元，北部涞源浑源却下跌百分之七·五——百分之十。晋中榆次下跌百分之五。太行各地平稳，惟邢台每斤下跌一○○元。太岳晋城价平郭道每斤下跌八○元，翼城因晋南棉向洛阳流影响棉价升百分之八。
    德（州）市二十二——二十六日升百分之八，代县升百分之○·三。
    国民党统治区棉花奇缺，津市匪当局严行统制，压价规定棉花每斤不得超过四八○万（蒋币），而中纺吸收却以五○○——五二○万来吸收，暗市已达五八○万元。近来匪当局在南开设立棉花交易所，专由中纺吸收，禁止私人买卖，并利用奸商四出向我区攫取棉花，各地应提起注意！
    布、油、盐、外货
