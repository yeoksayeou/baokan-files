Title: The North China Government, the North China Bureau, and the Military Region Telegraph Congratulations on the Great Victory at Jinzhou
Author:
Date: 1948-10-20
Page: 1

　　The North China Government, the North China Bureau, and the Military Region
　　Telegraph Congratulations on the Great Victory at Jinzhou
　　[Reported by this newspaper] The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and the North China Military Region have sent a telegram to congratulate our Northeast Army on its great victory in annihilating 100,000 enemies and liberating Jinzhou. The original telegram is as follows:
　　Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Gao Gang, Chen Yun, and all comrades of the Northeast People's Liberation Army:
　　We are thrilled to hear of your great victory in annihilating 100,000 enemies and liberating Jinzhou. The military and civilians of North China are very excited.
　　We will strengthen production and strive to fight, to eliminate the isolated strongholds of the bandit troops in North China, in order to cooperate with your victory. We hereby send a telegram of congratulations.
　　The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the North China Military Region
　　October 18th

### 华北政府、华北局暨军区　电贺锦州大捷

1948-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北政府、华北局暨军区
  　电贺锦州大捷
    【本报讯】华北人民政府、中共中央华北局、华北军区顷电贺东北我军歼敌十万解放锦州的伟大胜利。原电如下：
    林彪、罗荣桓、高岗、陈云诸同志并东北人民解放军全体同志们：
    欣闻你们歼敌十万、解放锦州的伟大胜利，华北军民甚为兴奋。
    我们将加强生产、努力作战，肃清华北匪军狐立据点，以配合你们的胜利，特电致贺。　　华北人民政府、中共中央华北局、华北军区　十月十八日
