Title: Our Forces Attack Yingcheng on the Periphery of Wuhan
Author: 
Date: 1948-10-12
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 10th. The People's Liberation Army, operating on the periphery of Wuhan, attacked Yingcheng County, an important stronghold of the Kuomintang in central Hubei, on the 29th of last month, wiping out a part of the 80th Brigade of the 28th Division of the defending enemy. More than 140 enemies were killed, wounded, or captured, including Major Battalion Commander Wu Bingzhang (captured). A portion of weapons were seized. The People's Liberation Army returned to their defenses victoriously after completing their mission.

### 武汉外围我袭应城

1948-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　武汉外围我袭应城
    【新华社中原十日电】活动在武汉外围的解放军，上月二十九日袭击汉口西北国民党鄂中重要据点应城县城，歼灭守敌二十八师八十旅一部，毙伤俘敌少校营长吴炳章（俘）以下一百四十余人，缴获武器一部。解放军于完成任务后胜利返防。
