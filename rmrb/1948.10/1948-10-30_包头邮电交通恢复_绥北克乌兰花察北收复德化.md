Title: Baotou Post and Telecommunications Traffic Restored; Northern Suiyuan's Ke Ulanhot, Northern Chahar's Recovered Dehua

Author:

Date: 1948-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sui-Meng Front, October 28th: When the People's Liberation Army, with its strict discipline and respect for the people, entered Baotou, the entire city's population lined the streets to welcome them, and order was well-maintained. Employees of the post office, telephone bureau, and other institutions continued to work, and all equipment was intact. Shops have gradually reopened for business. Among the captured supplies were two locomotives and forty carriages, and railway traffic was quickly restored. The city's radio station resumed broadcasting on the 24th.

Xinhua News Agency, North China, October 28th: Delayed news from the Sui-Meng Front: Ulanhot, a commercial center in northern Suiyuan, directly north of Gui Sui, was captured by the People's Liberation Army on the 19th, and a large amount of grain was seized.

Xinhua News Agency, Ping-Sui Front, October 28th: Delayed news: Our troops in northern Chahar captured Dehua County, northeast of Shangdu, on the 14th, capturing more than 30 enemies and seizing more than 30 rifles and horses, and more than 300 warhorses.

### 包头邮电交通恢复　绥北克乌兰花察北收复德化

1948-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　包头邮电交通恢复
  　绥北克乌兰花察北收复德化
    【新华社绥蒙前线二十八日电】纪律严明秋毫无犯的人民解放军开进包头时，全城人民夹道欢迎，秩序良好。邮局电话局等机关职工均继续工作，所有设备完好无损。商店已陆续开业。徼获物资中有火车头二个，车皮四十节，铁路交通迅即恢复。市内广播电台已于二十四日恢复广播。
    【新华社华北二十八日电】绥蒙前线迟到消息：归绥正北的绥（远）北商业重镇乌兰花已于十九日为解放军攻占，缴获粮食甚多。
    【新华社平绥前线二十八日电】迟到消息：察北我军于十四日攻占商都东北的德化县城，俘敌三十余，缴步马枪三十余支，战马三百余匹。
