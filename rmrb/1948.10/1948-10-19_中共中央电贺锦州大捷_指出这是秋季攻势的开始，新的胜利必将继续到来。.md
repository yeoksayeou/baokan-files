Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Great Victory at Jinzhou: Pointing out that this is the Beginning of the Autumn Offensive, New Victories Will Surely Continue to Arrive.
Author:
Date: 1948-10-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th. The CCP Central Committee recently sent a telegram congratulating the Northeast People's Liberation Army on the great victory of liberating Jinzhou and annihilating 100,000 enemies. The telegram stated: To Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Gao Gang, Chen Yun and to all comrades of the Northeast People's Liberation Army:
　　Congratulations on your great victory in annihilating 100,000 enemies and liberating Jinzhou this time. This victory appeared at the beginning stage of your autumn offensive this year, and new victories will surely continue to arrive. We hope that you will continue to work hard and fight for the complete annihilation of the Chiang bandit troops in the Northeast and the complete liberation of the people of the Northeast.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, October 17, 1948

### 中共中央电贺锦州大捷　指出这是秋季攻势的开始，新的胜利必将继续到来。

1948-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺锦州大捷
  　指出这是秋季攻势的开始，新的胜利必将继续到来。
    【新华社陕北十七日电】中共中央顷电贺东北人民解放军解放锦州，歼敌十万的伟大胜利。电称：林彪、罗荣桓、高岗、陈云诸同志并转东北人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们此次歼敌十万，解放锦州的伟大胜利。这一胜利出现于你们今年秋季攻势的开始阶段，新的胜利必将继续到来。望你们继续努力，为全歼东北蒋匪军队，完全解放东北人民而战。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会　一九四八年十月十七日
