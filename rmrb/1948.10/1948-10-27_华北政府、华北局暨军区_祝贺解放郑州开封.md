Title: North China Government, North China Bureau and Military Region Congratulate the Liberation of Zhengzhou and Kaifeng
Author:
Date: 1948-10-27
Page: 1

　　North China Government, North China Bureau and Military Region
  　Congratulate the Liberation of Zhengzhou and Kaifeng
    [From Our Newspaper] The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the North China Military Region have sent a telegram to congratulate the great victory of the liberation of Zhengzhou and the recovery of Kaifeng. The original telegram is as follows:
    Comrades Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, and all comrades of the Central Plains People's Liberation Army:
    We are delighted to hear that the strategically important town of Zhengzhou has been liberated and Kaifeng has been recovered. Wherever our mighty army goes, the enemy flees in terror. The soldiers and civilians of North China are extremely excited and will make every effort to support you in continuing your victorious advance towards the KMT-controlled areas!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　North China People's Government
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　North China Military Region
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 25th

### 华北政府、华北局暨军区　祝贺解放郑州开封

1948-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北政府、华北局暨军区
  　祝贺解放郑州开封
    【本报讯】华北人民政府、中共中央华北局、华北军区特电贺解放郑州克复开封的伟大胜利。原电如下：
    刘伯承、邓小平诸同志及中原人民解放军全体同志们：
    欣闻解放军事重镇郑州，光复开封，大军所至，守敌望风披靡，华北军民极为振奋，将以一切努力支援你们继续向蒋区胜利前进！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北人民政府
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央华北局
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北军区
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十月二十五日
