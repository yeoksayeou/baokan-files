Title: CCP Central Committee Congratulates on the Liberation of Changchun
Author:
Date: 1948-10-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th: The CCP Central Committee sent a special telegram to congratulate on the liberation of Changchun. The telegram is as follows:

　　To Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Gao Gang, Chen Yun, and all comrades of the Northeast People's Liberation Army:

　　Following the liberation of Jinzhou and the annihilation of 100,000 enemy troops, Changchun has now been liberated. General Zeng Zesheng led his troops in uprising, and General Zheng Dongguo led his troops in surrendering. The famous city has been restored, order is maintained, and the people rejoice in their rebirth, with cheers resounding like thunder. This is all the result of the heroic fighting of our People's Liberation Army and the concerted efforts of the front and rear personnel and the broad masses of the people. We hereby send a telegram of congratulations.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 20, 1948

### 中共中央祝贺长春解放

1948-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央祝贺长春解放
    【新华社陕北二十日电】长春解放，中共中央特电祝贺。电文如下：
    林彪、罗荣桓、高岗、陈云诸同志及东北人民解放军全体同志们：
    锦州解放，歼敌十万之后，长春即告解放，曾泽生将军率部起义，郑洞国将军率部投诚，名城光复，秩序井然，人庆更生，欢声雷动。此皆我人民解放军英勇善战，前后方工作人员与广大民众协力奋斗的结果。特电祝贺。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十月二十日
