Title: Comrade Zhu Rui, Commander of the Northeast Liberation Army Artillery, Dies a Heroic Death; The CPC Central Committee Sends a Special Telegram of Condolence
Author:
Date: 1948-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th. Comrade Zhu Rui, Commander of the Artillery of the Northeast People's Liberation Army and concurrently Principal of the Artillery School, heroically sacrificed his life in the battle to capture Yixian on the 1st. The CPC Central Committee sent a special telegram to the Northeast People's Liberation Army to express condolences. The telegram reads:

"Commander Lin, Political Commissar Luo, and to all comrades of the artillery units and artillery school of the Northeast People's Liberation Army: Just as our army is victoriously advancing on the Beining Line, Comrade Zhu Rui, Commander of the Artillery of our Northeast People's Liberation Army, heroically sacrificed his life in the battle to capture Yixian. Comrade Zhu Rui made outstanding contributions to the construction of the artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. His sacrifice today is a great loss to the cause of the Chinese people's liberation. The Central Committee expresses its deep condolences. We hope that you will convey this to the entire army to continue to struggle for the complete victory of the revolutionary war, in memory of Comrade Zhu Rui's eternal glory!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　CPC Central Committee
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1948"

### 东北解放军炮兵司令员　朱瑞同志光荣殉职  中共中央特电哀悼

1948-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北解放军炮兵司令员
    朱瑞同志光荣殉职  中共中央特电哀悼
    【新华社陕北四日电】东北人民解放军炮兵司令员兼炮兵学校校长朱瑞同志，在一日攻克义县作战中光荣牺牲。中共中央特致电东北人民解放军哀悼。电文称：
    “林司令员，罗政治委员，并转东北人民解放军炮兵部队炮兵学校全体同志们：正当我军在北宁线上胜利进攻之际，我东北人民解放军炮兵司令员朱瑞同志于义县攻城战中光荣牺牲。朱瑞同志在中国人民解放军的炮兵建设中功绩卓著，今日牺牲，实为中国人民解放事业中之巨大损失。中央特致深切的悼念。望转令全军，继续为革命战争的彻底胜利而奋斗，以纪念朱瑞同志永垂不朽！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十月三日”
