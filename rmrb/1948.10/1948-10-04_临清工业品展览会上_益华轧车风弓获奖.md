Title: Yihua Rolling Mill's Bellows Win Award at Linqing Industrial Products Exhibition

Author:

Date: 1948-10-04
Page: 2

Linqing Industrial Products Exhibition

　　Yihua Rolling Mill's Bellows Win Award
　　[Jinan News] The rolling mill and bellows produced by the Yihua Iron Factory in Linqing City were rated as special-grade industrial products at the "September 1st" Industrial Products Exhibition and received a pennant, a certificate, and six medals. The products are characterized by: high quality, wide sales, good sand casting, durability, and the bellows' cylinder and small wheel projectiles are both fast and ripe.

### 临清工业品展览会上　益华轧车风弓获奖

1948-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　临清工业品展览会上
    益华轧车风弓获奖
    【冀南讯】临清市益华铁工厂出产的轧车、风弓，在“九一”工业品展览大会上被评为特等工业产品，得锦标一面，奖状一具，奖章六枚。产品的特色是：质量高，销路广，翻沙好，坚韧耐久，风弓的圆筒和小轮弹的穰子又快又熟。
