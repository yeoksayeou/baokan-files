Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulations on the Liberation of Zhengzhou and Kaifeng; Over 10,000 Enemies Annihilated Between Zhengzhou and the Yellow River

Author:
Date: 1948-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has sent a telegram congratulating the Central Plains People's Liberation Army on the victories in liberating Zhengzhou and Kaifeng. The original telegram is as follows:

Comrades Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, and all comrades of the Central Plains People's Liberation Army:

The liberation of Zhengzhou, the annihilation of over 10,000 enemies, and the enemy's abandonment of Kaifeng and fleeing eastward, resulting in the second liberation of Kaifeng. From now on, the three major cities of the Central Plains, Luoyang, Zhengzhou, and Kaifeng, are all under the control of the People's Liberation Army, which is extremely beneficial to the future war situation. We hereby send our congratulations via telegram.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 25, 1948

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 26th: The number of enemy troops from the 40th Army annihilated by our Central Plains People's Liberation Army north of Zhengzhou and south of the Yellow River Iron Bridge is not over 8,000, but over 10,000. Among them, over 9,000 were captured alive. Another enemy force was also annihilated near the south bank of the Yellow River, and the results of the battle are pending investigation.

### 中共中央电贺郑州开封解放　郑州黄河间歼敌万余

1948-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺郑州开封解放
  　郑州黄河间歼敌万余
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】中共中央委员会顷电贺中原人民解放军解放郑州、开封的胜利。原电如下：
    刘伯承、邓小平两同志及中原人民解放军全体同志们：
    郑州解放、歼敌万余，开封守敌弃城东窜，开封遂告第二次光复。从此中原三大名城洛阳、郑州、开封均入人民解放军掌握，对于今后战局，极为有利。特电祝贺。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十月二十五日
    【新华社郑州二十六日电】我中原人民解放军在郑州以北黄河铁桥以南歼敌四十军部队，不是八千余，而是一万余。其中生俘者即达九千余人。另一部敌人在黄河南岸附近亦被歼灭，战果待查。
