Title: The North China Government, the North China Bureau, and the Military Region Telegraph Congratulations on the Victory in Central Shaanxi
Author:
Date: 1948-10-20
Page: 1

　　The North China Government, the North China Bureau, and the North China Military Region
  　Telegraph Congratulations on the Victory in Central Shaanxi
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and the North China Military Region have telegraphed congratulations to our Northwest Army on its great victory east of the Luo River. The original telegram is as follows:
    To Peng Dehuai, He Long, Lin Boqu, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Zongxun, Zhao Shoushan, and all comrades of the Northwest People's Liberation Army:
    We celebrate your great victory east of the Luo River, where you annihilated nearly two divisions of the bandit troops of Hu [Zongnan] and liberated six county towns. We will strengthen production and strive to fight to support you.
    　　　　　The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and the North China Military Region, October 18th

### 华北政府、华北局暨军区　电贺陕中胜利

1948-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北政府、华北局暨军区
  　电贺陕中胜利
    【本报讯】华北人民政府、中共中央华北局、华北军区顷电贺西北我军在洛河以东的巨大胜利。原电如下：
    彭德怀、贺龙、林伯渠、习仲勋、张宗逊、赵寿山并西北人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们在洛河以东，歼灭胡匪近两个师、解放县城六座的巨大胜利，我们将加强生产，努力作战，支援你们。
    　　　　　华北人民政府、中共中央华北局、华北军区　十月十八日
