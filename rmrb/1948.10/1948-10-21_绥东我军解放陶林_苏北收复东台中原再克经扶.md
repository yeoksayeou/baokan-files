Title: Our Army Liberates Taolin in Eastern Sui; Subei Recovers Dongtai; Central Plains Recaptures Jingfu
Author:
Date: 1948-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sui-Mongolian Front, 19th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army captured Taolin County, an important town northwest of Jining in eastern Suiyuan, on the morning of the 16th, killing, wounding, and capturing 500 enemy troops, and seizing more than 300 war horses and over 10,000 dan of grain. With this victory, the five counties in eastern Suiyuan, namely Taolin, Xinghe, Fengzhen, Jining, and Liangcheng, have all been liberated.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 19th: The Subei Liberation Army recovered Dongtai County on the Tongyu Highway south of Yeting (Yancheng) on the 11th of this month. The Liberation Army withdrew from the city on October 26, 1946, almost two years ago. Currently, we hold eight county towns in Central China: Yeting (Yancheng), Funing, Lianshui, Shuyang, Siyang, Suqian, Sixian, and Dongtai.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 19th: A unit of the Central Plains Liberation Army captured Xinjji (Jingfu) County in southeastern Henan Province on the 8th of this month, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 250 enemy troops. They seized two mortars, two 60mm mortars, seven light and heavy machine guns, and 187 rifles.

### 绥东我军解放陶林　苏北收复东台中原再克经扶

1948-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　绥东我军解放陶林
  　苏北收复东台中原再克经扶
    【新华社绥蒙前线十九日电】解放军一部，十六日晨攻克集甯西北绥东重镇的陶林县城，毙伤俘敌达五百名，并俘获战马三百余匹，粮食一万余担。至此，绥远东部五县即陶林、兴和、丰镇、集甯、凉城已全告解放。
    【新华社华东十九日电】苏北解放军本月十一日收复叶挺（盐城）以南通榆公路上的东台县城。解放军于一九四六年十月二十六日撤离该城，至今已近两年。现华中已有叶挺（盐城）、阜宁、涟水、沭阳、泗阳、宿迁、泗县、东台等八座县城在我手中。
    【新华社中原十九日电】中原解放军一部本月八日攻克河南省东南部的新集（经扶）县城，毙伤俘敌二百五十余名。缴获迫击炮二门，六零炮二门，轻重机枪七挺，步枪一百八十七支。
