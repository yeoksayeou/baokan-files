Title: People from All Circles in Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Shanxi-Suiyuan Send Telegrams to Congratulate on the Two Great Victories in Northern Liquan

Author:
Date: 1948-10-23
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st—People from all circles in Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Shanxi-Suiyuan sent telegrams to congratulate the Northwest People's Liberation Army on the two great victories in northern Liquan. They expressed their admiration and congratulations for the Northwest People's Liberation Army's brilliant victory of annihilating more than 23,000 enemy troops, liberating six county towns, and winning over the uprising of more than 1,000 members of the Chaoyi security police team shortly after the Huanglong victory annihilated the 36th Division of the bandit Hu. The telegram stated: "Inspired by your victory, we will work harder, strengthen our studies, and successfully complete production tasks in order to cooperate with and support the victorious advance of the front line, and strive to accelerate the liberation of the Great Northwest!"

### 陕甘宁、晋绥各界　电贺荔北两次大捷

1948-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁、晋绥各界
  　电贺荔北两次大捷
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】陕甘宁、晋绥党政军民学各界电贺西北人民解放军荔北两次大捷。对西北人民解放军继黄龙大捷歼灭胡匪三十六师之后不久，又获此歼敌二万三千余名、解放六座县城并争取朝邑保警队千余人起义的辉煌胜利，同申赞贺。电称：“在你们胜利鼓舞下，我们将更加努力工作、加强学习、胜利完成生产任务，以配合与支援前线的胜利进军，为加速解放大西北而奋斗！”
