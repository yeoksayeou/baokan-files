Title: Escaping Human Hell and Seeking Refuge in the Liberated Zone: Over a Thousand Faculty and Students from Changchun University Arrive at Northeast University
Author:
Date: 1948-10-05
Page: 2

　　Escaping Human Hell and Seeking Refuge in the Liberated Zone
    Over a Thousand Faculty and Students from Changchun University Arrive at Northeast University
    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 3rd. When the Kuomintang turned Changchun into a human hell, faculty and students from Changchun University flocked to the liberated areas. According to the person in charge of Northeast University, 1,005 faculty, staff, and students from Changchun University (including 176 family members) have arrived at the university; including Wang Meng, acting dean of the School of Engineering, Jia Zhizhong, acting dean of the School of Law, as well as department heads and professors from Civil Engineering, Chemistry, Veterinary Medicine, and other departments. They have all been properly settled. Upon hearing this news, the Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the Education Department of the Northeast Administrative Committee jointly sent a telegram of welcome on September 18, encouraging them to concentrate on teaching and work together to build a new Northeast and a new China.

### 逃出人间地狱投奔解放区　长春大学教员学生千余人到东北大学

1948-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　逃出人间地狱投奔解放区
    长春大学教员学生千余人到东北大学
    【新华社东北三日电】当国民党把长春变为一座人间地狱的时候，长春大学师生纷纷投奔解放区。据东北大学负责人称：抵达该校的长大教职员、学生等已达一千零五名（内家属一百七十六名）；其中包括工学院代理院长王孟，法学院代理院长贾执中，以及土木工程、化学、兽医等系主任、教授多人，现该校均已妥为安置。中共中央东北局宣传部及东北行政委员会教育部闻讯后，特于九月十八日联衔致电欢迎，勉以安心教学，共同为建设新东北和新中国而努力。
