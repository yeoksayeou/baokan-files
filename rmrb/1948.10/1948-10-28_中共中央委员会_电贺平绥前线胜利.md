Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Telegram of Congratulations on Victory on the Ping-Sui Front
Author:
Date: 1948-10-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has sent a telegram congratulating the People's Liberation Army on the victory on the Ping-Sui Railway front. The original telegram is as follows:

　　Comrades Yang Dezhi, Luo Ruiqing, Yang Chengwu, Li Jingquan, and all comrades of the North China, Jin-Sui, and Ji-Re-Cha People's Liberation Army:

　　Congratulations on your great victory in annihilating over 10,000 enemies, liberating Baotou and 16 other cities, and controlling most of the Ping-Sui Railway in the month since the start of the autumn offensive.

　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, October 25, 1948

### 中共中央委员会　电贺平绥前线胜利

1948-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会
  　电贺平绥前线胜利
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】中共中央委员会顷电贺平绥路前线人民解放军的胜利，原电如下：
    杨德志、罗瑞卿、杨成武、李井泉诸同志及华北、晋绥、冀热察人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆贺你们秋季攻势开始后一个月内歼敌万余、解放包头及其他城市十六座、控制平绥路大部的巨大胜利。
    　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会　一九四八年十月二十五日
