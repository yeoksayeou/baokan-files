Title: The Establishment of the North China People's Government: Jubilation and Encouragement Among Soldiers and Civilians in Various Regions

Author:
Date: 1948-10-19
Page: 1

　　The Establishment of the North China People's Government
  　Jubilation and Encouragement Among Soldiers and Civilians in Various Regions
  　Jinzhong Congratulatory Telegram
    [Jinzhong Telegram] The Jinzhong District Party Committee, Administrative Office, and Military Region telegraphed congratulations on the establishment of the North China People's Government. The original telegram is as follows:
    To Chairman Dong, Vice Chairmen Bo, Lan, and Yang of the North China People's Government:
    We are extremely excited to hear of the establishment of our North China People's Government and extend our warmest congratulations on behalf of the 2.5 million people of Jinzhong. Currently, the Yan bandit in Shanxi is trapped in the isolated city of Taiyuan. We are mobilizing all forces to support the front lines and striving to restore and develop production, aiming to liberate Taiyuan as soon as possible. The establishment of the North China People's Government has further boosted the confidence of our soldiers and civilians in victory. In the future, under the unified leadership of the North China People's Government, we will work together to fight for the liberation of Taiyuan, the construction of Jinzhong, and the liberation of all of China!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　Jinzhong District Party Committee, Administrative Office, Military Region
    Jicha Rehe Congratulatory Telegram
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Jicha Rehe District Party Committee, Administrative Office, and Military Region telegraphed congratulations on the establishment of the North China People's Government. The original telegram is as follows:
    To the North China Central Bureau and Comrade Dong Biwu, forwarded to the North China People's Government Committee:
    The North China Provisional People's Representative Conference has successfully concluded and the North China People's Government has been formally established. Our soldiers and civilians throughout the region are extremely excited and encouraged. We hereby send a telegram of congratulations.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Jicha Rehe District Party Committee, Administrative Office, Military Region

### 华北人民政府成立　各地军民欢欣鼓舞

1948-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府成立
  　各地军民欢欣鼓舞
  　晋中贺电
    【晋中电】晋中区党委、行署、军区电贺华北人民政府成立。原电如下：
    华北人民政府董主席、薄、蓝、杨副主席钧鉴：
    欣闻我华北人民政府组成，不胜兴奋，谨代表我晋中二百五十万人民致以热烈的庆贺。现在山西阎匪困守太原孤城，我们正动员一切力量支援前线，并努力恢复和发展生产，争取早日解放太原。华北人民政府的成立，更加鼓舞了我军民胜利的信心，今后在华北人民政府统一领导下，齐心协力，为解放太原，建设晋中，解放全中国而斗争！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　晋中区党委、行署、军区
    冀察热贺电
    【本报讯】冀察热区党委、行署、军区电贺华北人民政府成立，原电如下：
    华北中央局并董必武同志转华北人民政府委员会：
    华北临时人民代表大会胜利闭幕，正式成立华北人民政府，我全区军民莫不兴奋鼓舞。谨电祝贺。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　冀察热区党委、行署、军区
