　　Correspondence
　　No. 12
To responsible comrades of local prefectural, special, and county offices:

    We thank you for sending us various situation materials, which have enriched the content of the newspaper. In particular, the work reports sent by the Taihang Fifth and Sixth Prefectural Committees and several counties have enabled us to understand the overall situation of a region, which is of great help to the editors. In addition to regularly sending us work plans, summaries, and various situation materials for each period, we hope that you will also regularly send a copy of your reports to higher levels and any problems that arise to this section.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Editorial Department's Liaison Section

    Comrades Zhan Wu, Han Degong, Su Sanxi, Guo Huiyao, Du Sheng, Liang Qishun, Qie Minglu, Yang Xiaoying, Guo Shimin, Chen Dian, Ban Zhicheng, Liu Jiezhi, Jie Bing, Cao Shuanglu, Li Haide, Zhao Zhibin, Hao Jimin, Zhang Wan, Yue Peizhong, Hai Yan, Jinghua, Sun ＠, Fan Shengqing, Mei Wu, Yang Shi, Xiao Ning, Zhao Qiong, and Chong Bennuo: Please provide your mailing addresses so that we can send you your manuscript fees.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Editorial Department's Liaison Section

### 通讯往来　第十二号

1948-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
  　第十二号各地委、专、县办公室负责同志：
    本报承你们惠寄各种情况材料，丰富了报纸内容。尤其太行五、六地委与若干县份寄来向上级的工作报告，使我们了解了一个地区的整个情况，对编辑帮助甚大。希望各地除照常惠寄每个时期的工作计划、总结及各种情况材料外，并将向上级的报告和随时发生的问题也经常惠寄本科一份。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
    詹武、韩德功、苏三喜、郭辉耀、杜省、梁启顺、郄明禄、杨效英、郭世民、陈殿、班志诚、刘介之、捷兵、曹双禄、李海德、赵质彬、郝济民、张万、岳培忠、海燕、菁华、孙＠、范生庆、枚吾、杨时、萧宁、赵琼、冲奔诺同志：请告诉通讯地址，以便寄奉稿费。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
