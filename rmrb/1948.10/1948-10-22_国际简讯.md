    International Briefs

    ▲ Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov notified the Italian Ambassador to the Soviet Union on October 6th that the Italian government's delay in fulfilling its treaty obligations to transfer 33 ships to the Soviet Union is unacceptable. The Italian government should immediately take measures to implement the treaty provisions.
    ▲ The Iranian People's Party recently held a mass rally of 5,000 people to commemorate the seventh anniversary of the party's founding. Speakers at the meeting emphasized the role of the People's Party in the struggle for Iran's peace and independence, against Anglo-American imperialism and the Iranian reactionaries. Dr. Radmanesh, Secretary-General of the People's Party, delivered a lengthy speech.
    ▲ The British Commonwealth Conference opened in London on the 11th. This two-week conference will discuss the economic and military problems of the British Empire. Representatives from Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, India, Pakistan, Ceylon, and Southern Rhodesia are participating in this conference. The conference is chaired by British Prime Minister Attlee.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1948-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际简讯
    ▲苏外长莫洛托夫于十月六日通知意驻苏大使称，意政府拖延履行根据和约应予移交苏联之三十三艘舰只是不能容许的，意政府应立即采取措施实现和约的规定。
    ▲伊朗人民党最近召开五千人的群众大会，纪念该党建党七周年。会上讲演者着重指出人民党在为伊朗和平独立、反对美英帝国主义及伊朗反动派斗争中所起的作用。人民党总书记拉德马涅施博士曾发表长篇演说。
    ▲英帝国自治领会议于十一日在伦敦开幕。这一历时两周的会议，将讨论英帝国的经济及军事问题。参加此一会议者，计有加拿大、澳洲、纽西兰、南非、印度、巴基斯坦、锡兰及南罗得西亚之代表。会议由英首相艾德礼主持。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
