Title: Our Ambassadors to India, Burma, Pakistan, Indonesia, Sweden, and Other Countries Hold Grand Receptions to Celebrate the Third National Day of Our Country

Author: 

Date: 1952-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Our Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, the Embassy in the Republic of Indonesia, Ambassador to Pakistan, Han Nianlong, the Embassy in Sweden, the Legation in Denmark, Minister to Switzerland, Feng Xuan, and the Legation in Finland held grand receptions or banquets on October 1 to celebrate the third National Day of our country.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, included: Indian Government Minister of Defense Ayyangar, as well as senior officials from various ministries of the Indian government. Also present were Madam Pandit, head of the Indian cultural delegation visiting our country, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India, including: the Ambassadors of the Soviet Union, Burma, Indonesia, the Netherlands, and Afghanistan; the High Commissioners of Pakistan and Ceylon; the Ministers of Denmark, Switzerland, and Finland; and the Chargé d'affaires of Czechoslovakia and Hungary; Also invited were prominent figures from Indian cultural circles, the press, and society, as well as representatives of overseas Chinese from various parts of India.

Those invited to attend the banquet held by our Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, included: Prime Minister of the Union of Burma U Nu, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi, many senior officials of the Union Government of Burma, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma, totaling more than six hundred people.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Embassy in the Republic of Indonesia included: Indonesian Prime Minister Wilopo, Foreign Minister Mukarto, Minister of Health Leimena, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Darmasetiawan, Director of the Policy Department Rashid, Deputy Mayor of Jakarta and Director of the Ethnic Minorities Department Suwiryo, and National Police Chief Sukanto, etc. Also: diplomatic envoys from India, Burma, Pakistan, Switzerland, Ceylon, and the United Kingdom to Indonesia, the Dutch Commissioner, and the Hungarian trade delegation. A total of more than two hundred and fifty people attended the reception.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Ambassador to Pakistan, Han Nianlong, included: Speaker of the Pakistan Parliament Tamizuddin Khan, Minister of Industry Nishter, Minister of the Interior Gurmani, Minister of Labor, Health and Engineering Malik, Minister of Information and Broadcasting Qureshi, Minister of Kashmir Affairs Hussein, and Governor of Sindh Province Muhammad. Also invited were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Pakistan, Pakistani writers, poets, freelancers, as well as prominent figures from education, business, religious circles, and leaders of mass organizations, totaling more than four hundred and fifty people.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Embassy in Sweden included: Secretary-General of the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs Lundberg, Director of the Protocol Department Lamm, and many other senior officials from other departments of the Swedish government, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries that have established diplomatic relations with our country and other guests, totaling more than two hundred and fifty people.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Legation in Denmark included: Danish Government Minister of Fisheries Rye, Director-General of Customs and Taxation Kost, Chief of Staff of the Ministry of Defense Quistgaard, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Eickhoff, etc., as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from countries that have established diplomatic relations with us and people from all walks of life in Denmark, totaling approximately three hundred people.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Minister to Switzerland, Feng Xuan, included: Member of the Swiss Federal Council and Minister of Economic Affairs Rubattel, as well as other senior officials from various departments of the Swiss Federal Government, in addition to diplomatic envoys from various countries that have established diplomatic relations with our country, famous Swiss scholars, professors, bankers, industrialists, journalists, and overseas Chinese residing in Switzerland, totaling two hundred and sixty people. In the reception hall, many photos and statistical charts were displayed, illustrating China's peaceful construction and all the achievements of the past three years. The film of our country's "May Day" celebrations was also screened at the meeting.

Those invited to attend the reception held by our Legation in Finland included: Finnish Minister of Foreign Affairs Tuomioja, Minister of the Interior Sukselainen, Minister of Agriculture Luukka, Minister of Education Oittinen, Minister of Transport and Public Works Kekomäki, Minister of Justice Högström, Commander-in-Chief of the Finnish Armed Forces Sihvo, Deputy Mayor of Helsinki Floren, the wife of Prime Minister Kekkonen, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to Finland and well-known figures from all walks of life in Finland, totaling more than three hundred people.

### 我驻印度缅甸巴基斯坦印尼瑞典等国使节  举行盛大招待会庆祝我国第三届国庆节

1952-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻印度缅甸巴基斯坦印尼瑞典等国使节
    举行盛大招待会庆祝我国第三届国庆节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：我国驻印度大使袁仲贤、驻缅甸大使姚仲明、驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆、驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙、驻瑞典大使馆、驻丹麦公使馆、驻瑞士公使冯铉、驻芬兰公使馆于十月一日举行盛大招待会或宴会，庆祝我国第三届国庆节。
    应邀出席我国驻印度大使袁仲贤举行的招待会的有：印度政府国防部长阿扬加尔以及印度政府各部高级官员。还有前来我国访问的印度文化代表团团长潘迪特夫人，各国驻印度外交使节有：苏联、缅甸、印尼、荷兰、阿富汗的大使，巴基斯坦、锡兰的高级专员，丹麦、瑞士、芬兰的公使，捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利的代办，英国代理高级专员。应邀到会的还有印度文化界、新闻界与社会知名人士以及印度各地华侨代表。
    应邀出席我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明举行的宴会的有：缅甸联邦政府总理吴努，外交部长藻昆卓，缅甸联邦政府许多高级官员和各国驻缅甸使节等共六百多人。
    应邀出席我国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆举行的招待会的有：印尼总理韦洛坡，外交部长慕加多，卫生部长雷墨拿，外交部秘书长达马斯蒂亚宛，政策司司长拉锡，雅加达副市长兼少数民族司司长苏外约，国家警察长苏甘多等。还有：印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、瑞士、锡兰、英国诸国驻印尼外交使节，荷兰专员，匈牙利商业代表团。出席招待会的共计二百五十多人。
    应邀出席我国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙举行的招待会的有巴基斯坦议会议长达米祖丁·汗，工业部部长尼希塔，内政部部长顾尔马尼，劳工、卫生和工程部部长马力克，情报和广播部部长古瑞西，克什米尔事务部部长胡森，信德省省长穆罕默德。应邀到会的还有各国驻巴基斯坦的外交使节，巴基斯坦作家、诗人、自由职业者以及教育界、工商界、宗教界知名人士和群众团体领袖等共四百五十多人。
    应邀出席我国驻瑞典大使馆的招待会的，有瑞典外交部秘书长龙德贝、交际司司长拉摩及瑞典政府其他部门高级官员多人，此外还有已经和我国建立外交关系的各国驻瑞典外交使节及其他来宾等共二百五十余人。
    应邀出席我国驻丹麦公使馆的招待会的，有丹麦政府渔业大臣芮义、海关兼税务局局长考思特、国防部参谋长奎伏斯高德、外交部交际司副司长艾可夫等，此外还有与我建交国家的驻丹麦外交使节及外交官员和丹麦各界人士共约三百人。
    应邀出席我国驻瑞士公使冯铉举行的招待会的，有瑞士联邦政务委员会委员兼经济部部长陆巴特尔以及瑞士联邦政府各部门的其他高级官员多人，此外还有已与我国建立外交关系的各国的外交使节，瑞士著名学者、教授、银行家、工商业家、新闻记者以及居留瑞士的华侨等共二百六十人。在接待室大厅上，陈列着许多照片和统计图表，说明中国的和平建设以及过去三年来的一切成就。会上还放映了我国“五一”节影片。
    应邀出席我国驻芬兰公使馆的招待会的，有芬兰外交部长杜米奥雅、内政部长苏克西莱宁、农业部长卢卡、教育部长奥伊梯宁、交通及公共工程部长克里莫拉、司法部长贺格斯特朗、芬兰武装部队总司令西赫伏、赫尔辛基副市长佛伦凯、总理吉科宁的夫人以及各国驻芬兰外交使节和外交官员与芬兰各界知名人士共三百余人。
