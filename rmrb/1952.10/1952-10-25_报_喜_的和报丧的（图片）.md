Author: Fang Cheng
Date: 1952-10-25
Page: 4

    Announcing "Good News" and Announcing Bad News
    By Fang Cheng
    The U.S. Air Force authorities are constantly boasting about their so-called "remarkable achievements," but recently, U.S. Marine Corps Commandant Shepherd had to admit: "The U.S. Air Force's intense bombing in the rear of North Korea to destroy the communist army's supply lines has failed." He also pointed out that the "remarkable achievements" published by the U.S. Air Force authorities are exaggerated.
