Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This year, Liucunfan Village, Longmen Town, Fuyang County, Zhejiang, has achieved a bumper harvest of rice throughout the village, exceeding the highest level before the War of Resistance Against Japan. The average yield of dried rice per mu in the whole village was 800 jin and 2 liang, an increase of 48% compared with before the War of Resistance Against Japan. An old farmer said to his daughter: "I have never seen such good rice in my life. This is what Chairman Mao brought us."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江富阳县龙门镇六村畈今年全村的水稻都获得高额丰收，超过抗日战争以前的最高水平。全村平均每亩收干谷八百斤零二两，比抗日战争前增产四成八。这位老农民对他的女儿说：“我这辈子可没见过这么好的稻子，这是毛主席带给我们的。”
