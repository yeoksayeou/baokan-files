Title: International Telecommunication Union Holds Plenipotentiary Conference; Soviet Delegation Supports Statement by Our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai; Suggests Expelling Kuomintang Representatives and Inviting Our Country's Representatives to Attend the Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, October 21st: TASS, New York, October 20th: The International Telecommunication Union is holding a Plenipotentiary Conference in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The conference will discuss the report on the Union's work over the past five years and adopt new telecommunications conventions on adjusting international radio, telephone, and telegraph services. Representatives from sixty-eight countries are participating in this conference, including the Soviet Union, the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, and the People's Democratic countries.

At the first plenary session, the Soviet delegation supported the statement made by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, to the conference regarding the issue of China's representation in the International Telecommunication Union. The Soviet delegation proposed expelling the representatives of the Kuomintang clique from the conference and inviting representatives of the People's Republic of China—the only legitimate representatives of China—to attend the meeting. The Soviet representative pointed out that without the participation of the People's Republic of China, the conference could not resolve issues in the field of international telecommunications, as the People's Republic of China has extensive radio, telephone, and telegraph networks. Delegations from Ukraine, Belarus, the People's Democratic countries, India, Pakistan, and other countries supported the Soviet proposal.

The United States representative opposed the Soviet proposal and requested an indefinite postponement of the discussion on this issue.

The Chairman of the meeting, the representative from Argentina, disregarding the Soviet representative's protest, first put the United States' proposal to a vote as a "procedural" motion. The United States' proposal was eventually passed by a majority vote of the United States and its satellite countries. However, representatives from twenty-five countries did not support the United States' proposal, some voting against it and others abstaining. In addition to the Soviet Union, Ukraine, Belarus, and the People's Democratic countries, major Asian countries such as India, Pakistan, and Indonesia also voted against the United States' proposal.

Under pressure from the United States delegation, the conference, with a mechanical majority, rejected the Soviet proposal to accept the legitimate demands of the German Democratic Republic and the Mongolian People's Republic regarding inviting their representatives to attend the conference.

The delegations of the Soviet Union, Ukraine, Belarus, and the People's Democratic countries issued statements pointing out that the above-mentioned resolutions of the conference were erroneous and illegitimate.

### 国际电信联盟举行全权代表大会  苏联代表团支持我周恩来外长的声明  建议驱逐国民党代表、邀请我国代表参加会议

1952-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    国际电信联盟举行全权代表大会
    苏联代表团支持我周恩来外长的声明
    建议驱逐国民党代表、邀请我国代表参加会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约二十日讯：国际电信联盟正在阿根廷的布宜诺斯艾利斯举行全权代表大会。大会将讨论关于联盟在过去五年中的工作的报告，并通过关于调整国际无线电、电话和电报事业的新电讯公约。这次大会有六十八个国家的代表参加，其中有苏联、乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国、白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国和人民民主国家。
    在第一次全体大会上，苏联代表团支持中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来致大会的关于中国在国际电信联盟代表权问题的声明。苏联代表团建议把国民党集团的代表从大会驱逐出去，并邀请中华人民共和国代表——中国的唯一合法代表参加会议。苏联代表指出：没有中华人民共和国参加，大会就不能解决国际电讯事业方面的问题，因为中华人民共和国有广泛的无线电、电话和电报网。乌克兰、白俄罗斯、人民民主国家、印度、巴基斯坦以及其他国家的代表团都支持苏联的建议。
    美国代表反对苏联的建议，并要求无限期延缓关于这个问题的讨论。
    会议主席阿根廷代表不顾苏联代表的抗议，首先把美国的建议当作一个“关于程序的”建议付表决，美国的建议终于以美国及其附庸国家的多数票获得通过。但是，有二十五个国家的代表没有支持美国的建议，它们有的投票反对，有的弃权。在苏联、乌克兰、白俄罗斯、人民民主国家以外，印度、巴基斯坦、印尼等主要亚洲国家也投票反对美国的建议。
    在美国代表团的压力下，大会以机械多数否决了苏联提出的接受德意志民主共和国和蒙古人民共和国关于邀请它们的代表出席大会的合法要求的建议。
    苏联、乌克兰、白俄罗斯以及人民民主国家的代表团发表声明，指出大会的上述决议是错误的、不合法的。
