Title: Peace Will Surely Triumph: Celebrating the Success of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-13
Page: 6

At the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference on the second day, Han Xueye, head of the Korean delegation, gave a report on "Peaceful Settlement of the Korean Issue on a Reasonable Basis."
(Xinhua News Agency Draft · Qi Guanshan Photo)

During a break in the conference, female representatives from various countries chatted happily together. (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

The Lao delegation presented a flag to the conference. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Ding Yi Photo)

The Vietnamese delegation presented gifts to the conference. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Chen Bo Photo)

Female representatives from various countries had a cordial conversation. From right: Cao Mengjun, delegate of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and guest of the conference, Ms. Rohini Bhadre from India, Santi from the Women's International Democratic Federation, Uribe from Chile, and Mrs. Mohammad Tam from Indonesia.
(Xinhua News Agency Draft · Qi Guanshan Photo)

The Indian delegation presented gifts to the Korean delegation. Han Xueye, head of the Korean delegation, and Dr. Kitchlew, head of the Indian delegation, embraced warmly. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Liu Dong’ao Photo)

Han Xueye, head of the Korean delegation's report on "Peaceful Settlement of the Korean Issue on a Reasonable Basis" was warmly welcomed by all the delegates. This is the scene of Louis Wheaton, head of the U.S. delegation, shaking hands with Han Xueye. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Liu Dong’ao Photo)

Deng Yingchao, head of the Chinese delegation (first from the left), chatting with U Chit Thein, representative of Burma (second from the right). (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Zheng Jingkang Photo)

The Vietnamese delegation held a get-together with the French youth delegation. Le Dinh Tham, head of the Vietnamese delegation, and Robert Shelbé, head of the French youth delegation, exchanged gifts. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Yue Guofang Photo)

Guo Moruo, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, talking with Japanese representatives. Second from the right is Nambo, deputy head of the Japanese delegation; third from the right is Nakamura Kanjuro, acting head of the delegation; fourth from the right is Kameda Togo, representative. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Cai Shangxiong Photo)

Kim Young-soo (first from the left) and Moon Ye-bong (second from the right) from the Korean delegation, and Doramjav (first from the right) and Tserindava (second from the left) from the Mongolian delegation, chatted happily together.
(Xinhua News Agency Draft · Jia Huamin Photo)

The Indian delegation and the Pakistani delegation held a get-together. The delegates unanimously applauded to enhance the friendship between the two peoples. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Yuan Hao Photo)

Representatives from the Soviet Union, China, Lebanon and Syria, Algeria, Turkey and Iraq, and other countries together. (Xinhua News Agency Draft · Qi Guanshan Photo)

### 和平一定胜利  庆祝亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议成功（图片）

1952-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    和平一定胜利
    庆祝亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议成功
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议第二日，朝鲜代表团团长韩雪野作“关于在合理的基础上和平解决朝鲜问题”的报告。
    （新华社稿·齐观山摄）
    会议休会时，各国女代表们在一起欢谈。（新华社稿）
    寮国代表团向大会献旗。 （新华社稿·丁一摄）
    越南代表团向大会献礼。 （新华社稿·陈勃摄）
    各国女代表在一起亲切晤谈。右起：大会来宾中华全国民主妇女联合会代表曹孟君、印度代表萝希妮·巴德女士、国际民主妇女联合会代表桑蒂、智利代表乌里贝、印度尼西亚代表摩罕默德·谭夫人。
    （新华社稿·齐观山摄）
    印度代表团向朝鲜代表团献礼，朝鲜代表团团长韩雪野和印度代表团团长克其鲁博士热烈拥抱。（新华社稿·刘东鳌摄）
    朝鲜代表团团长韩雪野“关于在合理的基础上和平解决朝鲜问题”的报告受到全体代表们的热烈欢迎。这是美国代表团团长路易士·惠吞和韩雪野亲切握手时的情形。（新华社稿·刘东鳌摄）
    中国代表邓颖超（左第一人）和缅甸代表都钦七（右第二人）晤谈。     （新华社稿·郑景康摄）
    越南代表团与法国青年代表团联欢。越南代表团团长黎廷探和法国青年代表团团长劳贝·谢尔培相互赠礼。（新华社稿·岳国芳摄）
    中国代表团副团长郭沫若与日本代表们在一起交谈。右第二人为日本代表团副团长南博、右第三人为代理团长中村翫右卫门、右第四人为代表龟田东伍。        （新华社稿·蔡尚雄摄）
    朝鲜代表金英秀（左第一人）、文艺峰（右第二人）和蒙古代表都拉姆扎夫（右第一人）、切林达娃（左第二人）在一起欢谈。      
    （新华社稿·贾化民摄）
    印度代表团和巴基斯坦代表团联欢。代表们一致为增进两国人民的友谊而鼓掌。     （新华社稿·袁浩摄）
    苏联、中国、黎巴嫩和叙利亚、阿尔及利亚、土耳其和伊拉克等国的代表们在一起。     （新华社稿·齐观山摄）
