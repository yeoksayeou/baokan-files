Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee and All-China Youth Federation Host Banquet for French Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation held a banquet on the evening of the 1st to welcome the French Youth Delegation led by Robert Charvin, Secretary of the National Committee of the Republican Youth Union of France. The banquet was attended by Hu Yaobang, Li Chang, Rong Gaotang, alternate secretaries Qu Tangliang, Gao Yangwen, and Yang Shu from the Central Committee of the League, as well as the directors and deputy directors of various departments of the Central Committee of the League, and Qian Weichang and Ding Cong, deputy secretaries-general of the All-China Youth Federation. Invited to accompany the guests were Cao Mengjun, deputy secretary-general of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and Liao Mengxing, deputy director of the International Work Department, Tian Demin, chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Li Zaiwen, director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Liu Ziju, director of the Cultural and Educational Department, Huang Zhong, acting secretary-general of the All-China Sports Federation and Xu Yingchao, member, Li Chao, deputy secretary of the North China Working Committee of the Youth League, and Yang Bozhen, secretary of the Beijing Committee of the Youth League.
    At the banquet, Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Central Committee of the League, delivered a speech warmly welcoming the French Youth Delegation. All participants at the banquet repeatedly toasted for lasting world peace, for the friendship between the Chinese and French youth, and for Thorez, Chairman Mao, and Stalin.
    The banquet proceeded in a joyful and friendly atmosphere.

### 青年团中央和全国青联  欢宴法国青年代表团

1952-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央和全国青联
    欢宴法国青年代表团
    【新华社二日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合总会在一日晚上举行宴会，欢宴以法国共和青年联盟全国委员会书记劳贝·谢尔培为首的法国青年代表团。出席宴会的有团中央书记胡耀邦、李昌、荣高棠、候补书记区棠亮、高杨文、杨述及团中央各部正副部长，中华全国民主青年联合总会副秘书长钱伟长、丁聪。应邀作陪的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君、国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，中华全国总工会办公室主任栗再温、文教部部长刘子久，中华全国体育总会代理秘书长黄中、委员徐英超，青年团华北工作委员会副书记李超，青年团北京市委员会书记杨伯箴等。
    席上，团中央书记胡耀邦致词热烈欢迎法国青年代表团。全体参加宴会人员不断为世界持久和平干杯，为中法两国青年的友谊干杯，为多列士、毛主席、斯大林干杯。
    宴会在愉快友好的气氛中进行。
