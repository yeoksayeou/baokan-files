Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 156th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th—The 156th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 24th. At the meeting, Guo Moruo, Deputy Premier and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, made a report on cultural and educational work in 1952. The report included: the ideological remolding movement of intellectuals, the adjustment of departments in universities, the patriotic health campaign, the work of eliminating illiteracy, and the development of people's film and drama. The report pointed out that in 1952, New China's cultural and educational undertakings had made significant achievements in all aspects of restoration, rectification, transformation, and development, and put forward the direction of future efforts for various tasks.
In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal proposals and submitted appointment and removal proposals for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第一五六次会议

1952-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五六次会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十六次政务会议在二十四日下午举行。会上，郭沫若副总理兼政务院文化教育委员会主任作了关于一九五二年几项文化教育工作的报告。报告内容包括有：知识分子的思想改造运动、高等学校的院系调整、爱国卫生运动、扫除文盲工作、人民电影和戏剧事业的发展，报告指出在一九五二年内，新中国的文化教育事业无论在恢复、整顿、改造和发展的各方面都有了重大的成就，并就各项工作，提出了今后努力的方向。
    此外，会议通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
