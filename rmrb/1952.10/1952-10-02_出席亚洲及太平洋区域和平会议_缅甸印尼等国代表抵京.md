Title: Representatives from Burma, Indonesia, and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Representatives and observers from Burma, India, Indonesia, and Pakistan, who will be attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 30th.  Greeting them at the airport were Jin Cheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and others. Also welcoming them at the airport were representatives from India, Indonesia, and Pakistan, who had arrived earlier, and Wu La, Burmese Secretary of the Peace Conference Preparatory Committee. Thang Tumao, Second Secretary, and Sumatao, Third Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome the Indonesian representatives.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Ten representatives and observers from Chile, Costa Rica, and Indonesia, who will be attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 1st. Greeting them at the airport were Hu Wenyao, a representative of the Chinese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and staff members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, among others. Also welcoming them at the airport were representatives from Costa Rica, El Salvador, and Nicaragua, who had arrived earlier, and were attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  缅甸印尼等国代表抵京

1952-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    缅甸印尼等国代表抵京
    【新华社一日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的缅甸、印度、印度尼西亚和巴基斯坦的一部分代表和列席代表，于九月三十日乘飞机抵达北京。到机场欢迎的有：中国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表团副秘书长金城，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒等人。到机场欢迎的还有前已到京的印度、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦代表及和平会议筹备委员会缅甸秘书吴拉。印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆二等秘书汤杜茂和三等秘书苏玛陶也前往机场欢迎印度尼西亚代表。
