Title: The First Batch of Rice Sold by China to Ceylon Has Been Exported from Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-10-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The contract signed in Beijing on October 4th between the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the government of Ceylon for the sale of 80,000 tons of rice has begun to be fulfilled. The first batch of over 9,800 tons of rice shipped from China to Ceylon was loaded onto the Xiangli Wheel in Shanghai on the 25th and exported.

### 我国售给锡兰的大米第一批已从上海出口

1952-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国售给锡兰的大米第一批已从上海出口
    【新华社二十八日讯】十月四日在北京签订的中华人民共和国中央人民政府售给锡兰政府八万吨大米的合同已经开始履行。第一批从中国运往锡兰的大米九千八百多吨，已在二十五日由祥利轮从上海载运出口。
