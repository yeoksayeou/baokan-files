Title: Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Holds Meeting; Resolution to Further Expand and Strengthen Cooperation Between Chinese and Soviet Scientists
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th -- TASS reports: The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a meeting on the 3rd to discuss further expanding and strengthening scientific ties between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China. Speakers at the meeting included A.N. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, V.A. Topchiyev, Academic Secretary of the Academy, Academicians A.I. Oparin, N.P. Dubinin, E. Varga, and other scientists, who unanimously praised the achievements of Chinese science and fully supported the proposal to establish closer ties between the scientists of the two great powers.

The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences passed a resolution on creative cooperation with Chinese scientists. The resolution stipulates that the most interesting works of Chinese scientists will be translated into Russian and published. The Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences will be responsible for translating and annotating the works of Chinese scientists. Journals published by the Academy will dedicate special columns to report on Chinese scientific life and related articles and reviews about China. In addition, the Central Library of the Soviet Academy of Sciences will establish a long-term exhibition of the latest publications from the People's Republic of China.

### 苏联科学院主席团举行会议  决议进一步扩大和加强中苏科学家的合作

1952-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院主席团举行会议
    决议进一步扩大和加强中苏科学家的合作
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院主席团在三日举行会议，讨论进一步扩大和加强苏联和中华人民共和国在科学方面的联系问题。在会上发言的有苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫、科学院秘书陶普契夫、科学院院士奥巴林、杜比宁、瓦尔加和其他科学家，他们一致赞扬中国科学的成就并完全赞成两个大国的科学家建立更密切的联系的建议。
    苏联科学院主席团通过了关于和中国科学家实行创造性的合作的决议。决议规定把中国科学家的最饶有兴趣的著作翻成俄文出版。苏联科学院所属的东方学研究所将负责翻译和注释中国科学家的著作。科学院所出的期刊将辟专栏登载中国的科学生活及有关中国的文章和评论。此外，苏联科学院中央图书馆还将设立中华人民共和国最新出版的书籍的长期展览会。
