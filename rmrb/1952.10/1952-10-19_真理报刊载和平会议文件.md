Title: Pravda Publishes Documents of the Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 18th: Pravda has been publishing documents from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in prominent positions for several days: On the 15th, it published the letter to the United Nations; on the 16th, it published the letter to the people of the world and resolutions on the Korean question and on intensifying the movement to strive for a Five-Power Peace Pact; on the 17th, it published resolutions on supporting the convocation of the World People's Peace Congress, on the Japanese question, and on cultural exchange; on the 18th, it published resolutions on economic exchange, on national independence, and on the establishment of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee.

### 真理报刊载和平会议文件

1952-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    真理报刊载和平会议文件
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】真理报连日以显著地位刊载亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议文件：十五日刊载致联合国书，十六日刊载告世界人民书和关于朝鲜问题、关于加紧争取五大国缔结和平公约运动等决议，十七日刊载关于拥护召开世界人民和平大会、关于日本问题、关于文化交流等决议，十八日刊载关于经济交流问题、关于民族独立问题、关于建立亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会等决议。
