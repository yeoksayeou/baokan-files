Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Entire Command and Fighters Send a Telegram of Congratulations to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 2nd. The entire commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The telegram is as follows: To Secretary-General Liu Ningyi of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, please forward to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference: We warmly congratulate the victorious opening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. This is a great action in which the broad masses of people in Asian and Pacific countries unite to further oppose war and defend peace. We will resolutely support the resolutions of this conference and respond to the call of this conference. In the past two years, in order to defend our motherland and defend peace in Asia and the world, we have fought side by side with the Korean People's Army and defeated the invading army headed by US imperialism, demonstrating with practical actions our determination to defend peace in Asia. In the future, we will continue to work tirelessly to achieve a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue and to defend the peace of the 1.6 billion people in Asia and the Pacific region! The entire commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army

### 中国人民志愿军全体指战员  致电祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议

1952-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军全体指战员
    致电祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    【新华社朝鲜前线二日电】中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员致电祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。电文如下：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会刘宁一秘书长请转亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议：我们热诚地祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的胜利开幕。这是亚洲及太平洋区域各国广大人民团结起来进一步反对战争、保卫和平的伟大行动。我们将坚决支持这个会议的决议和响应这个会议的号召。近两年来，我们为了保卫自己的祖国，保卫亚洲及全世界的和平，与朝鲜人民军并肩作战，打败了以美帝国主义为首的侵略军队，以实际行动表现了我们保卫亚洲和平的决心。今后我们仍将继续为实现和平解决朝鲜问题、为保卫亚洲及太平洋区域十六亿人民的和平而奋斗不懈！中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员
