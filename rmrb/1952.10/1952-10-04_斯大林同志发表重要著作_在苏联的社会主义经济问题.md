Title: Comrade Stalin Publishes Important Work – "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR"
Author:
Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 1

     Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd Dispatch from Moscow: "Pravda" published the news of the publication of the eighteenth issue of the theoretical and political organ "Bolshevik" of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks). This issue contains an article written by J. Stalin: "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR." Among them are: "Comments on Economic Issues Related to the November 1951 Discussion," "Reply to Comrade Alexander Ilyich Notkin," "On the Errors of Comrade L. D. Yaroshenko," "Reply to Comrades A. V. Sanina and V. G. Venzher." This is a new and major contribution to Marxist-Leninist science about society, about the science of communist construction.
     Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd Dispatch from Moscow: The State Political Books Publishing Bureau has printed J.V. Stalin's work "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" as a separate edition. 1.5 million copies of the separate edition have been published.

### 斯大林同志发表重要著作——“在苏联的社会主义经济问题”

1952-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

     斯大林同志发表重要著作——“在苏联的社会主义经济问题”
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：真理报登载了苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会理论与政治性机关刊物“布尔什维克”第十八期出版的消息。这一期刊载了约·斯大林所写的文章：“在苏联的社会主义经济问题”。其中有：“对于与一九五一年十一月讨论有关的经济问题的意见”，“给亚历山大·伊里奇·诺特金同志的答复”，“关于鲁·德·雅罗申科同志的错误”，“给阿·伏·萨宁纳和伏·格·文热尔同志的答复”。这是对马克思主义—列宁主义关于社会的科学、关于共产主义建设的科学之新的、最大的贡献。
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：国家政治书籍出版局已把约·维·斯大林的著作“在苏联的社会主义经济问题”印成单行本。单行本已出版一百五十万册。
