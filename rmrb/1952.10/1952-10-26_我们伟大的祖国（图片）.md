Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Many new-style schools are being built in our country. This is the Northeast Agricultural College under construction. The school buildings can accommodate 2,500 people attending classes at the same time, and there is a large auditorium that can accommodate 3,000 people. The entire project can be completed in mid-November. This is the scene of the workers building the gate.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    许多新式的学校正在我国建设起来。这是正在建筑中的东北农学院，校舍可供二千五百人同时上课，并有一个容纳三千人的大礼堂，全部工程在十一月中旬可以完工。这是工人正在修建大门的情形。
