Title: U.S. Plane Again Intrudes into Meeting Area Airspace
Author:
Date: 1952-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 24th -- On October 22nd, U.S. military aircraft violated the Korean Armistice Negotiation Meeting Area Agreement by intruding into the airspace over the Panmunjom meeting area. On the 24th, our chief liaison officer protested this to the other side, demanding that the other side earnestly abide by the Meeting Area Agreement, strictly restrain its military personnel, and prevent similar incidents from happening again. At the liaison officers' meeting on the same day, the other side admitted to the shelling of the meeting area on October 19th, expressed regret, and claimed that it would take necessary measures to prevent the occurrence of similar incidents.

### 美机又侵入会场区上空

1952-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    美机又侵入会场区上空
    【新华社开城二十四日电】十月二十二日，美方军用飞机违反朝鲜停战谈判会场区协议，侵入板门店会场区上空。二十四日我方首席联络官对此向对方提出抗议，要求对方切实遵守会场区协议，严格约束对方军事人员，使类似事件不再发生。同日双方联络官会议上，对方承认了十月十九日炮击会场区的事件，表示遗憾，并声称：将采取必要措施防止类似事件的发生。
