Title: Hope for More Good Propaganda Posters
Author: Dong Xiuzheng
Date: 1952-10-21
Page: 2

Reader's Letter:

    Hope for More Good Propaganda Posters
    Comrade Editor: The propaganda poster "Peace Representatives, Welcome You!" created by Comrades Yu Feng and Feng Zhen has been praised by many comrades at the Renmin University of China. We believe that this painting vividly and prominently expresses the Chinese people's happy life and their sincere desire for peace. This is a work with considerable ideological and artistic merit. We hope that our artists will create more such works.
    Dong Xiuzheng

### 希望多创作好的宣传画
董修正
1952-10-21
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    希望多创作好的宣传画
    编辑同志：郁风、冯真二同志合作的“和平代表，欢迎你！”这幅宣传画，在中国人民大学受到了许多同志的赞赏，我们认为这幅画鲜明而突出地表现了中国人民幸福的生活和衷心热爱和平的心愿。这是一幅具有相当思想性和艺术性的作品。希望我们的艺术家多多创作这样的作品。    董修正
