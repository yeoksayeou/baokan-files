Our Great Motherland
This year, cotton has also had a bumper harvest in various parts of Southwest China. This is the cotton field of the Zhang Sizhou Mutual Aid Team, a cotton planting model in Wazi Township, Fourth District, Jianyang County, Sichuan Province. In this cotton field, the members of the mutual aid team are stepping up their cotton picking efforts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年西南各地棉花也丰收了。这是四川省的产棉区——简阳县第四区瓦子乡植棉模范张泗洲互助组的棉田。在这棉田里，互助组员们正在加紧采摘棉花。
