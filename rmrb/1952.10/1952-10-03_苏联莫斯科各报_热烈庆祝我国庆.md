Title: Soviet Moscow Newspapers Warmly Celebrate Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st Dispatch: According to TASS, Moscow newspapers widely celebrated the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China on the 1st. Newspapers prominently published congratulatory telegrams from Joseph Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Andrei Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs. Pravda and Izvestia both published editorials celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China. Newspapers carried many reports and articles about the National Day of the great Chinese people. Trud also published letters from Chinese workers.

### 苏联莫斯科各报  热烈庆祝我国庆

1952-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

     苏联莫斯科各报
    热烈庆祝我国庆
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：一日莫斯科各报普遍庆祝中华人民共和国成立三周年。各报以显著地位刊载了苏联部长会议主席斯大林和苏联外交部部长维辛斯基分别打给中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东和总理兼外交部部长周恩来的贺电。真理报和消息报都发表了庆贺中华人民共和国国庆的社论。各报刊载了许多关于伟大中国人民的国庆的报道和文章。劳动报并刊载了中国工人的信函。
