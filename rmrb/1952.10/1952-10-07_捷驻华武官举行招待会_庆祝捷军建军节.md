Title: Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The military attaché of the Czechoslovak Republic's embassy in China, Colonel Procházka, held a reception in Beijing on the 6th to celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day.

Those invited to attend included: Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission聂荣臻 (Nie Rongzhen), Deputy Director of the General Political Department萧华 (Xiao Hua), Commander of the Air Defense 周士第 (Zhou Shidi), Commander of the Artillery 陈锡联 (Chen Xilian), Director of the General Office of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission 萧向荣 (Xiao Xiangrong), Deputy Commander of the Navy 王宏坤 (Wang Hongkun), Chief of Staff of the Air Force 王秉璋 (Wang Bingzhang), and senior officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army from the Army, Air Force, and Navy.

Also invited were Vice Premiers of the Central People's Government 郭沫若 (Guo Moruo) and 黄炎培 (Huang Yanpei), President of the Supreme People's Court 沈钧儒 (Shen Junru), Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs 伍修权 (Wu Xiuquan), heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, and Vice Mayor of Beijing 吴晗 (Wu Han).

Also invited to attend were representatives from the Chinese People's Volunteers' delegation to the National Day celebrations.

Also invited were diplomatic envoys of various countries to China, diplomatic personnel of various embassies in China, and military attachés from the Soviet Union, Poland, and Indonesia.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. During the event, guests frequently toasted to the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Czechoslovakia, to the President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Commander-in-Chief of the Czechoslovak Army Gottwald, to the great leader of the Chinese people, the creator and leader of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the leader of the world's working people, the brilliant commander of the Soviet Army, Marshal Stalin.

The Czechoslovak Vit Nejedlý Army Art Troupe, which was invited to our country, performed wonderful programs during the reception.

### 捷驻华武官举行招待会  庆祝捷军建军节

1952-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    捷驻华武官举行招待会
    庆祝捷军建军节
    【新华社六日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆武官普罗查上校于六日在北京举行招待会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国军队建军节。
    应邀到会的有：中国人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、总政治部副主任萧华、防空司令员周士第、炮兵司令员陈锡联、中国人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣、海军副司令员王宏坤、空军参谋长王秉璋以及中国人民解放军陆、空、海军高级军官等。
    应邀到会的尚有中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、外交部副部长伍修权及中央人民政府各部门首长和各民主党派、人民团体负责人，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    应邀到会的还有中国人民志愿军国庆节观礼代表团代表们。
    应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节、各国驻华使馆外交人员及苏联、波兰、印度尼西亚驻华武官等。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。席间宾主频频为中、捷两国人民和两国军队的友谊，为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统兼捷克斯洛伐克军队总司令哥特瓦尔德，为中国人民的伟大领袖、中国人民解放军的创造者和领导者毛泽东主席，为世界劳动人民的领袖、苏联军队的天才统帅斯大林大元帅的健康干杯。
    应邀来我国的捷克斯洛伐克维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团在招待会间演出精彩节目。
