Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Seventh Anniversary of the World Federation of Trade Unions
Author: Liu Ningyi
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Seventh Anniversary of the World Federation of Trade Unions

    [Dispatch from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram of congratulations on the seventh anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Victorio, Chairman, and Comrade Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions:
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Trade Unions, I extend my respects on behalf of the Chinese working class to the World Federation of Trade Unions, the leader of the world trade union movement. In seven years of unremitting work, the World Federation of Trade Unions has faithfully defended the interests of the working class around the world with heroic actions. The Chinese working class is closely united under the fighting banner of the World Federation of Trade Unions, struggling to consolidate and expand the unity of the working class worldwide, improve their living standards, oppose the war preparations and aggression policies led by American imperialism, and the conspiracy to divide the unity of the working class, and striving for lasting world peace.
    Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 世界工联成立七周年  刘宁一致电祝贺
刘宁一
1952-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联成立七周年
    刘宁一致电祝贺
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一致电祝贺世界工会联合会成立七周年。电文如下：世界工会联合会主席维多里奥总书记赛扬同志：
    欣逢世界工会联合会成立七周年，谨代表中国工人阶级向世界工会运动的领导者世界工会联合会致敬。世界工会联合会在七年坚持不懈的工作中，以英勇的行动忠实地捍卫着全世界工人阶级的利益。中国工人阶级紧紧团结在世界工会联合会的战斗旗帜下，为巩固与扩大全世界工人阶级的团结、提高他们的生活水平、反对美帝国主义带头推行的备战侵略政策和分裂工人阶级团结的阴谋而斗争，为争取世界持久和平而斗争。
    中华全国总工会副主席  刘宁一
