Title: Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Appoints Hoang Van Hoan as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1952-10-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, has appointed Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador-level representative of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China.

### 越南民主共和国政府任命  黄文欢为驻华大使

1952-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府任命
    黄文欢为驻华大使
    【新华社九日讯】越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明已任命越南民主共和国驻华代表团大使衔代表黄文欢为越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权大使。
