Title: Hungarian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 30th—The Hungarian People's Republic Trade Delegation, consisting of eleven members (of whom seven had previously arrived in Beijing), led by Mr. Szarvasy István, Director of the Department of Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing on the 30th.  Greeting them at the airport were Lin Haiyun, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau; Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau; and officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    Wang Hanmin, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in the Hungarian People's Republic, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

    Nagy, Commercial Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and Secretary Czator Dolai also went to the airport to greet them.

### 匈商务代表团抵京

1952-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    匈商务代表团抵京
    【新华社三十日讯】匈牙利人民共和国商务代表团一行十一人（内七人已先在京），由团长匈牙利人民共和国政府对外贸易部司长沙鲁辛斯基·伊斯特万率领，三十日抵达北京。前往机场迎接的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部办公厅主任林海云，进口局局长张化东，第二局副局长贾石以及外交部官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有我驻匈牙利人民共和国大使馆商务参赞王汉民。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆商务参赞那吉，秘书察托尔道伊等亦前往机场迎接。
