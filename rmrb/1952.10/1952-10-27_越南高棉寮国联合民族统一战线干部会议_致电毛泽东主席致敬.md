Title: Cadre Conference of the United National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos Sends Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1952-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch

    The Cadre Conference of the United National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos convened on September 14th. The conference adopted a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

    The Cadre Conference of the United National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos opened on September 14, 1952. The conference extends to you its highest and most sincere respects.

    We are honored to invite you to be the honorary chairman of the conference. We are pleased to see that the world's democratic and peace camp, under the wise leadership of Marshal Stalin, is growing stronger day by day, becoming a powerful and invincible force; at the same time, we are also pleased to see that the anti-aggression struggle of the people of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos has achieved many victories with the support of the people of the world.

    We extend our most excited and joyful congratulations on the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and congratulate the Chinese people on the great achievements they have made under your leadership and the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party in the cause of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea and building a new China. We deeply believe that the new China is a strong fortress of the peace movement in the Asian and Pacific region, and also an important force in the world's democratic and peace camp.

    We sincerely wish you good health and longevity. We are willing to learn various valuable experiences from the Chinese people, and to vigorously promote the war of resistance against the French colonialists and the American imperialist interventionists and their puppets, in order to strive for national independence, safeguard the security of the Asian nations, and defend the cause of lasting world peace.

    Cadre Conference of the United National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos

### 越南高棉寮国联合民族统一战线干部会议  致电毛泽东主席致敬

1952-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    越南高棉寮国联合民族统一战线干部会议
    致电毛泽东主席致敬
    【新华社讯】越南、高棉、寮国三国联合民族统一战线九月十四日召开了干部会议。会议通过了致我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席的致敬电。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    越南、高棉、寮国三国联合民族统一战线的干部会议，于一九五二年九月十四日开幕。会议向您致以崇高的、诚恳的敬礼。
    我们很荣幸地敦请您为会议的名誉主席。我们愉快地看到：世界民主和平阵营在斯大林大元帅的英明领导下日益壮大起来，成为一个雄厚的无敌的力量；同时我们也愉快地看到：越南、高棉、寮国三国人民的反侵略斗争，因有全世界人民的支持而获得了许多胜利。
    我们以最兴奋和愉快的心情来祝贺中国第三届国庆节，祝贺中国人民在您和中国共产党的领导之下、在抗美援朝和建设新中国等事业中所获得的伟大成就。我们深深地相信：新中国是亚洲及太平洋区域和平运动的坚强堡垒，也是世界民主和平阵营中的重要力量。
    我们谨以至诚，敬祝您身体健康、万寿无疆。我们愿向中国人民学习各种宝贵的经验，为争取国家的独立、保卫亚洲各民族的安全、保卫世界持久和平等事业、而把反对法殖民主义者和美帝国主义干涉者及其傀儡们的抗战强有力地向前推进。
    越南、高棉、寮国三国联合民族统一战线干部会议
