Title: Composition of Soviet, Belorussian, and Ukrainian Delegations to the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: According to TASS, the Council of Ministers of the USSR, the Government of the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, and the Government of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic have respectively appointed the delegations to attend the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly, which will be convened in New York on October 14, 1952. The head of the Soviet delegation is Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR; the members of the delegation are: Gromyko, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Zorin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR and Soviet Representative to the Security Council, Zarubin, Soviet Ambassador to the United States, and Sobolev, Soviet Ambassador to Poland. Alternate members are: Kuchkarov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, Yulans, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Latvian Soviet Socialist Republic, Arkadyev, Director of the American Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Morozov, Deputy Chief of the Treaty Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Spirunskaya, Member of the Presidium of the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the USSR.

The head of the delegation of the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic is Kiselev, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic. The members of the delegation include Astapenko, Novikov, Lenkov, and Poviedev.

The head of the delegation of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic is Baranovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic. The members of the delegation are: Balamarzuk, Kriven, Khokhlov, and Bashan, and the alternate members are Shtokalo and Demchenko.

### 苏联白俄罗斯乌克兰出席联大代表团组成

1952-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联白俄罗斯乌克兰出席联大代表团组成
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社讯：苏联部长会议、白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国政府和乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国政府已分别任命出席一九五二年十月十四日在纽约召开的联合国大会第七届会议的代表团。苏联代表团团长为苏联外交部部长维辛斯基；代表团团员为：苏联驻英国大使葛罗米柯、苏联外交部副部长兼苏联驻安全理事会代表佐林、苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾、苏联驻波兰大使索波列夫。候补团员为：乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国外交部长库赤卡罗夫、拉脱维亚苏维埃社会主义共和国外交部副部长尤兰斯、苏联外交部美国司司长阿尔卡捷夫、苏联外交部条约科副科长莫罗佐夫和苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会主席团委员斯彼伦斯卡雅。
    白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团团长为白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长基塞列夫。代表团团员有阿斯塔平科、诺维科夫、连可夫、波维杰夫等人。
    乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团团长为乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长巴拉诺夫斯基。代表团团员为：巴拉马尔朱克、克利文、霍赫夫和巴尚，候补团员为什托卡洛和杰姆琴科。
