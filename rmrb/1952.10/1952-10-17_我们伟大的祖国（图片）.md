Author:
Date: 1952-10-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Laiyang pears, famous from Shandong, are estimated to have a harvest of over 2.4 million catties this year, an increase of about 900,000 catties compared to last year. This is Wang Zhitang, an old farmer from Xinge Village in Laiyang County, picking pears under the tree. He said, "This year is even better than last year."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山东著名的莱阳梨，今年收获估计约在二百四十万斤以上，可比去年增产九十万斤左右。这位正在树下摘梨的是莱阳县辛格庄的老农民王志堂。他说：“今年的光景比去年更好了。”
