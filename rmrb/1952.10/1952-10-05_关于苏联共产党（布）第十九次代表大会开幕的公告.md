Title: Announcement on the Opening of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th. TASS Moscow, 4th: The following announcement is published here: Announcement on the Opening of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks). The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) informs the delegates attending the 19th Congress that the Congress will open at 19:00 on October 5th in the Great Hall of the Kremlin.

### 关于苏联共产党（布）第十九次代表大会开幕的公告

1952-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    关于苏联共产党（布）第十九次代表大会开幕的公告
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科四日电：此间发表公告如下：关于苏联共产党（布尔什维克）第十九次代表大会开幕的公告苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会通知出席第十九次代表大会的代表，本次代表大会将于十月五日十九时在克里姆林宫大厅中开幕。
