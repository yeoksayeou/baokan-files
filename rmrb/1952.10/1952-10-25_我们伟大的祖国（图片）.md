    Our Great Motherland
    It has been two years since the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers went abroad to fight. This is a picture of the Chinese People's Volunteers guarding the Kaesong area. They are closely monitoring the enemy and striking at the unreasonable provocations of the American aggressors.

    我们伟大的祖国
