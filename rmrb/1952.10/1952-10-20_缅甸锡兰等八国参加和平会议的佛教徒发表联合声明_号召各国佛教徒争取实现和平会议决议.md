Title: Buddhists from Burma, Ceylon, and Eight Other Countries Participating in the Peace Conference Issue Joint Statement Calling on Buddhists of All Countries to Strive to Implement Resolutions of Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Buddhists from Burma, Ceylon, China, Japan, Laos, Malaya, Thailand, and Vietnam, participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, issued a joint statement on the 16th as follows:

    We, the representatives of various countries who believe in Buddhism and are participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, would like to extend our brotherly concern and sincere wishes to Buddhists in this region and all countries of the world.

    In this conference, we have been deeply encouraged by the sincere and selfless friendship between the people of various countries. This friendship is the most precious human quality in Buddhism, the source of people's higher life and great strength, and the hope of the world.

    Today, the suffering of people in many countries is unprecedentedly deep. Economic plunder, political oppression, military aggression, inhumane persecution and killing, and the propaganda of evil and hatred are being carried out madly, and large-scale and destructive war preparations are seriously threatening all mankind around the world.

    Stopping aggression and defending peace is the urgent task of everyone at present, and it is also the urgent task of us Buddhists.

    This task must be completed and can be completed.

    Hundreds of millions of Buddhists who worship Buddha's peaceful teachings have a major responsibility in today's peace movement.

    Therefore, we call on Buddhists of all countries to unite and make every effort to implement the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. These resolutions, generated by common compassion and wisdom, are the necessary path to achieve human peace and the standard by which we distinguish right from wrong and truth from falsehood in our peace work. We must be vigilant against any conspiracy to use religion as a guise to carry out aggression and undermine peace. Defending true peace is also defending the purity of religion.

    May the light of Buddha's peace shine on the whole world!

    Signatories:

    China: Venerable Yuan Ying, Venerable Xirao Jiacuo, Aqier Yehuhuletu, Zhao Puchu.

### 缅甸锡兰等八国参加和平会议的佛教徒发表联合声明  号召各国佛教徒争取实现和平会议决议

1952-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸锡兰等八国参加和平会议的佛教徒发表联合声明
    号召各国佛教徒争取实现和平会议决议
    【新华社十九日讯】参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的缅甸、锡兰、中国、日本、寮国、马来亚、泰国和越南八个国家的佛教徒，十六日发表联合声明如下：
    我们参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各国信仰佛教的代表们，愿向这个区域内以及全世界各国佛教徒致以兄弟的关怀和诚恳的祝愿。
    在这次会议中，我们深深地被各国人民之间的真诚无私的友爱所鼓舞，这种友爱正是佛教所最宝贵的人类的品性，是人们的更高生命和伟大力量的泉源，是世界的希望。
    今天许多国土上的人民所遭受的苦难，是空前深重的。经济的掠夺，政治的压迫，军事的侵略，违反人道的迫害与残杀，邪恶与仇恨的宣传正在疯狂地进行，而大规模的具有毁灭性的战争准备更在严重地威胁着全世界的人类。
    制止侵略，保卫和平，是当前每一个人的迫切任务，也是我们佛教徒的迫切任务。
    这任务是必须完成，而且能够完成的。
    数以亿万计的崇奉佛陀和平教义的佛教徒在今天的和平运动中有着重大责任。
    因此，我们号召各国佛教徒团结起来，为实现亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的决议而作一切努力。由共同慈悲和智慧所产生的这些决议，是实现人类和平的必要的道路，也是我们在和平工作中鉴别是非真假的标准。我们必须警惕任何企图利用宗教的名义以进行侵略破坏和平的阴谋活动。保卫真正的和平，也就是保卫宗教的纯洁。
    愿佛陀和平之光照耀于全世界！
