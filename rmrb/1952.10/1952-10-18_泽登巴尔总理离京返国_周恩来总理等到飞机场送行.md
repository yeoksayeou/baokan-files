Title: Tsedendambaal Departs Beijing for Home; Premier Zhou Enlai and Others See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: Tsedendambaal, Head of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic and Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic, departed Beijing by plane on the 17th to return home. Accompanying him were: Ramsuren, member of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Shirindib, member of the delegation, Minister of Education; Purbuzhal, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Dulburjin, Deputy Director of the Department of Eastern Affairs; and Secretary Uljibayar.

Those who saw him off at the airport included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Vice Premiers Guo Moruo and Deng Xiaoping; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff; Bo Yibo, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council and Minister of Finance; Ye Jizhuang, Vice Chairman and Minister of Foreign Trade; Ma Xulun, Vice Chairman of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council and Minister of Education; Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Protocol of the Central People's Government; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Bingzhang, Chief of Staff of the Air Force; Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Xu Yixin, Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Department of Asian Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Reception Department; Shen Ping, Deputy Director; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zhou Libo, head of the Chinese literary and art delegation recently returned from Mongolia, and others.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Jalkasaihan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and staff of the Mongolian Embassy in China; Rudenko, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China; Saffronko, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China; Petkov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Bulgarian People's Republic to China; U Hla Maung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to China; Kwon O-jik, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China; Kirilok, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to China; Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with the title of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary; Gudev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in China; Jabroski, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in China; Zhou Liang, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China; Saifani, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Republic of India in China; Thandumaung, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China; and Iftikhar Ali, Second Secretary of the Embassy of Pakistan in China.

Also at the airport to see them off was Bebilev, head of the Commercial Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

An honor guard was lined up at the airport, and the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic were played.

The flags of the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China were hung at the airport.

### 泽登巴尔总理离京返国  周恩来总理等到飞机场送行

1952-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔总理离京返国
    周恩来总理等到飞机场送行
    【新华社十七日讯】蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔于十七日乘飞机离北京返国。同行的有：蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团员、副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，代表团团员、教育部部长锡林迪布，外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒，东方司副司长都勒布尔金，秘书乌勒吉巴雅尔。
    到机场送行的有：中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，副总理郭沫若、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长张宗逊，政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼财政部部长薄一波，副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮，政务院文化教育委员会副主任兼教育部部长马叙伦，副主任习仲勋，中国共产党中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中央人民政府公安部部长罗瑞卿，邮电部部长朱学范，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长丁西林，中国科学院副院长李四光，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，空军参谋长王秉璋，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航，苏联东欧司司长徐以新，亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处处长王倬如、副处长沈平，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，和最近由蒙古回国的中国文艺代表团团长周立波等。
    到机场送行的还有：以蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗为首的蒙古驻华大使馆人员，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使鲁登科，匈牙利人民共和国驻华特命全权大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使彼得科夫，缅甸联邦驻华特命全权大使吴拉茂，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华特命全权大使权五稷，波兰人民共和国驻华特命全权大使基里洛克，德意志民主共和国特命全权大使衔外交使团团长柯尼希，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基，越南民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书周亮，印度共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书赛发尼，印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书汤杜茂，巴基斯坦驻华大使馆二等秘书伊夫提加·艾里。
    到机场送行的还有：保加利亚人民共和国商务代表团团长别比列夫。
    飞机场上排列着仪仗队，并奏中华人民共和国国歌和蒙古人民共和国国歌。
    飞机场上挂着蒙古人民共和国和中华人民共和国国旗。
