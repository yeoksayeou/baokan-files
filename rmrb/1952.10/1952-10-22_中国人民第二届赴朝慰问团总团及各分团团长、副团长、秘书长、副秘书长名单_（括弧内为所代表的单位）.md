Title: List of Leaders of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, Including the Heads, Deputy Heads, Secretaries-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General of the Main Delegation and Various Sub-Delegations (Units Represented in Parentheses)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency
    List of Leaders of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, Including the Heads, Deputy Heads, Secretaries-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General of the Main Delegation and Various Sub-Delegations (Units Represented in Parentheses)
    Head of the Main Delegation: Liu Jingfan (Communist Party of China); Deputy Heads: Chen Yi (Chinese People's Liberation Army), Li Minghao (Non-Party Democrat), Hu Juewen (China Democratic National Construction Association), Zhou Qinyue (Non-Party Democrat); Secretary-General: Li Jiebo (All-China Railway Workers' Union); Deputy Secretaries-General: Yan Rengeng (Professor, Peking University), Ma Yi (New Democratic Youth League of China).
    First Sub-Delegation: Head: Wu Juenong (China Democratic National Construction Association); Deputy Heads: Shen Zijiu (All-China Democratic Women's Federation), Zhang Xiuzhu (All-China Federation of Trade Unions), Ye Duyi (China Democratic League), Yu Zhenying (Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang), Yu Zhou (Shanghai Regional Committee of the China Railway Workers' Union); Deputy Head and Secretary-General: Wu Zhaotong (Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Deputy Secretaries-General: Luo Ziwei (China Democratic League), Jiang Han (Directly Subordinate Organs of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China).
    Second Sub-Delegation: Head: Xin Shuzhi (President of Northwest Agricultural College, Non-Party Democrat); Deputy Heads: Yusuf (Uyghur, Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army), Cao Xinghua (Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army), Zhou Weixin (Hui, Imam of the South Gate Mosque in Linxia, Gansu); Deputy Head and Secretary-General: Qin Chuan (Propaganda Department of the Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China); Deputy Secretaries-General: Feng Yihang (Cultural and Educational Circles of Shaanxi Province), Guo Yucai (Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China).
    Third Sub-Delegation: Head: Wang Wenbin (Southwest Office of the China Democratic National Construction Association); Deputy Heads: Zhu Dannan (Preparatory Committee of the Southwest Federation of Literary and Art Circles), Yin Chaofan (Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army).
    Fourth Sub-Delegation: Head: Hu Juewen (concurrent); Deputy Heads: Chen Tongsheng (East China Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea), Zhu Chunhe (East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army), Fang Lingru (female, Professor at Fudan University, Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation); Secretary-General: Zhang Jinyi (Professor at Fudan University, Shanghai Branch of the All-China Federation of Literary Workers); Deputy Secretary-General: Li Yuxuan (East China Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea).
    Fifth Sub-Delegation: Head: Zhang Zhen (Central South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Deputy Heads: Lu Wenxin (Central South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army), Sun Yaohua (China Democratic National Construction Association), Tang Wuyuan (Hubei Provincial Working Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party); Secretary-General: Wang Daojin (Central South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Deputy Secretary-General: Ji Guanqing (Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions).
    Sixth Sub-Delegation: Head: Zhang Minghe (Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China); Deputy Head: Tan Zhiqing (Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce); Deputy Head and Secretary-General: Wang Bingyan (North China Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Deputy Secretary-General: Tian Ye (Tianjin Federation of Literary and Art Circles).
    Seventh Sub-Delegation: Head: Chen Xianzou (Northeast General Branch Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League); Deputy Heads: Chen Yuanzhi (Songjiang Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea), Xie Fengshan (Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Secretary-General: Guan Mengjue (Northeast General Branch Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League); Deputy Secretaries-General: Shao Zhengtian (Public Security Forces of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army), Gong Tianmin (Preparatory Committee of the Shenyang Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association), Qiao Chuanjue (Preparatory Committee of the Dalian Branch of the China Democratic League).
    Eighth Sub-Delegation: Head: Wang Xiangli (Central South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Deputy Head: Wang Lihua (Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea).
    Ninth Sub-Delegation: Head: Ning Wu (Northeast Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang); Deputy Heads: Yi Meihou (Preparatory Committee of the Returned Overseas Chinese Association), Li Xiangjiu (Xi'an Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association); Secretary-General: Cui Mingshan (Logistics Department of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army). (Xinhua News Agency)
