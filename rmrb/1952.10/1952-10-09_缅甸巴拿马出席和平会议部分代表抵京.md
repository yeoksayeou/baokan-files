Title: Burmese and Panamanian Representatives to Peace Conference Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—Two Burmese representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Venerable U Binya Yawnta (formerly translated as U Pingyewen Da) and Venerable U Kuna Yawnta (formerly translated as U Gan Na Wenda), arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 8th. Chinese representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Venerable Yuan Ying, President of the China-Burma Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, editor of "Modern Buddhism" Venerable Ju Zan, Buddhist layman Zhao Puchu, and representatives from religious circles went to the station to welcome them. Also at the station to welcome them were the head of the Burmese delegation, Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Secretary-General U Ba Yin, and other members of the delegation. The head of the Ceylon delegation, Venerable Dhammaratana, also went to welcome them.

Venerable U Binya Yawnta, who arrived today, is the abbot of the Binya Yameiga Monastery in Yangon, and Venerable U Kuna Yawnta is a standing committee member of the Yangon Young Monks Association in Burma.

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—Part of the Panamanian delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 4th. Deputy Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee Wu Maosun, committee member Feng Binfu and others went to the airport to welcome them. Also there to welcome them was the Panamanian representative Cleto Manuel Sousa, who had already arrived in Beijing.

List of Panamanian representatives who arrived in Beijing:

Head of Delegation: Carlos Francisco Changmarín, winner of the National Literary Prize
Member: Carlo Di Pirro Bertelsky, dentist, Standing Committee Member of the Panama Peace Committee

### 缅甸巴拿马出席和平会议部分代表抵京

1952-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸巴拿马出席和平会议部分代表抵京
    【新华社八日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的缅甸代表宇彬养温达（前译宇平野文达）法师和宇古那温达（前译宇干纳文达）法师二人于八日下午乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表圆瑛法师，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，“现代佛学”编辑巨赞法师，佛教居士赵朴初及宗教界代表等多人。到车站欢迎的还有缅甸代表团团长德钦哥都迈、秘书长宇巴迎及团员多人。锡兰代表团团长达马拉塔纳法师亦前往欢迎。
    今天到达的缅甸代表宇彬养温达法师是仰光彬耶也美加佛寺的住持，宇古那温达法师是缅甸仰光青年和尚协会常务委员。
    【新华社八日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的巴拿马代表团一部分代表在四日下午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪，委员冯宾符等多人。前往欢迎的还有前已抵京的巴拿马代表克莱托·马努爱尔·萨乌沙。
    到达北京的巴拿马代表名单
    团长：卡罗斯·弗兰西斯科·钱玛林  国家文学奖金得奖人
    团员：卡罗·第·皮尔奥·柏特里斯基  牙科医生，巴拿马和平委员会常务委员
