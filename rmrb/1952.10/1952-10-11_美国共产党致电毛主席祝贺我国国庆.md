Title: Communist Party of the United States Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Congratulate China's National Day
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 10th: The New York "Daily Worker" published on October 3rd the congratulatory telegram from the Communist Party of the United States to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party on the National Day of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

    "On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our warmest congratulations. The Chinese revolution is one of the greatest and most decisive events in the history of all mankind. It marks the beginning of the end of imperialism in Asia and gives great encouragement to the forces of peace, democracy and socialism throughout the world.
    "The peace-loving people of the world can and are determined to stop the Wall Street monopolists from launching a third world war."

### 美国共产党致电毛主席祝贺我国国庆

1952-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    美国共产党致电毛主席祝贺我国国庆
    【新华社布拉格十日电】十月三日纽约“工人日报”刊载了美国共产党在中华人民共和国国庆日给中国共产党毛泽东主席的贺电。电文如下：
    “值此中华人民共和国建国三周年之际，我们谨致以最热烈的祝贺。中国的革命是全体人类历史上最伟大、最有决定性的事件之一。它标志着帝国主义在亚洲终结的开始，并给予全世界各地的和平、民主与社会主义的力量以极大的鼓励。
    “全世界爱好和平的人民群众，能够而且决心制止华尔街的垄断资本家发动第三次世界大战。”
