Title: Tsendenbal, Prime Minister, Arrives in Nanjing; Visits Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and Lays Wreath to Pay Respects
Author:
Date: 1952-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 7th. Tsendenbal, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic and Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Nanjing on the evening of the 6th. Accompanying him were members of the delegation, including Ramsuren, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; Jalkhasaikhan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and his wife; Purbuzhal, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; and Duleburjin, Deputy Director of the Department of the Orient. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, accompanied them. Ke Qingshi, Mayor of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, and Vice Mayor Jin Shanbao, welcomed them at the station.

On the 7th, the distinguished guests from the Mongolian People's Republic visited the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and laid a wreath to pay their respects. In the evening, the Nanjing Municipal People's Government hosted a banquet to warmly welcome them.

### 泽登巴尔总理抵南京  曾谒中山陵并献花致敬

1952-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔总理抵南京
    曾谒中山陵并献花致敬
    【新华社南京七日电】蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔六日晚到达南京。同来的有代表团团员、蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民共和国驻华特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗及其夫人，蒙古人民共和国外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒及东方司副司长都勒布尔金等。我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如陪行。南京市人民政府市长柯庆施，副市长金善宝等到车站欢迎。
    七日，蒙古人民共和国贵宾谒中山陵，献花致敬。晚间，南京市人民政府设宴热情款待。
