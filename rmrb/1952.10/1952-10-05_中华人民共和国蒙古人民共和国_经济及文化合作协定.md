Title: Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic
Author:
Date: 1952-10-05
Page: 1

The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, in view of the fact that the establishment of close economic and cultural cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic is entirely in the interests of the two peoples, and is also of great significance in further developing and consolidating the friendly relations between the two countries, have decided to conclude this Agreement, and have each appointed plenipotentiary representatives as follows:

The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of China; The Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic has appointed Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic. The plenipotentiary representatives of both parties, having examined each other's full powers and found them to be in good and due form, have agreed upon the following articles:

Article 1 The Contracting Parties agree to establish, develop and consolidate cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic in the fields of economy, culture and education.

Article 2 In accordance with this Agreement and for the purpose of implementing this Agreement, the relevant economic, trade, cultural and educational departments of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic shall conclude specific agreements separately.

Article 3 This Agreement shall be ratified as soon as possible and shall enter into force from the date of ratification, with a term of validity of ten years. The instruments of ratification shall be exchanged in Ulaanbaatar.

If neither Contracting Party notifies the other of its intention to terminate this Agreement one year before the expiration date, it shall be automatically extended for another ten years.

Done in Beijing on the fourth day of October, 1952, in duplicate, each in the Chinese and Mongolian languages, both texts being equally authentic.

    Plenipotentiary Representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
    Plenipotentiary Representative of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic Tsedenbal

### 中华人民共和国蒙古人民共和国  经济及文化合作协定

1952-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国蒙古人民共和国
    经济及文化合作协定
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府与蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团鉴于建立中华人民共和国与蒙古人民共和国间密切的经济及文化合作完全符合两国人民的利益，并在进一步发展及巩固两国间的友好关系上亦具有重大意义，兹决定订立本协定，并各派全权代表如左：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府特派中国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来；蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团特派蒙古人民共和国总理尤睦佳·泽登巴尔。双方全权代表互相校阅全权证书认为妥善后，议定下述各条：
    第一条  缔约双方同意在经济、文化、教育方面，建立、发展及巩固中华人民共和国与蒙古人民共和国间的合作。
    第二条  根据本协定并为实现本协定计，中华人民共和国及蒙古人民共和国有关经济、贸易及文化教育部门之间将分别缔结具体协定。
    第三条  本协定应尽速批准，并自批准之日起生效，其有效期为十年。批准书在乌兰巴托互换。
    本协定如在期满前一年未经缔约任何一方通知废止时，则将自动延长十年。
    一九五二年十月四日订于北京，共两份，每份均以中文及蒙文书就，两种文字的条文具有同等效力。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府全权代表  周恩来
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔全权代表  泽登巴尔
