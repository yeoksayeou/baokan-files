Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu Sends Congratulations on Romanian Army Day
Author: Zhu De
Date: 1952-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st - On October 2nd, Romanian People's Republic Army Day, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a congratulatory telegram to Comrade Bodnaras, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

Comrade Bodnaras, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic:

On the occasion of the Romanian People's Republic Army Day, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend my warm congratulations to you. For the sake of peace and security, may the Romanian army achieve even greater successes.

Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De
October 1, 1952, in Beijing

### 朱总司令电贺罗马尼亚建军节
朱德
1952-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    朱总司令电贺罗马尼亚建军节
    【新华社一日讯】十月二日是罗马尼亚人民共和国建军节，中国人民解放军朱德总司令致电罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部部长波德纳拉斯，表示祝贺。电文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部部长波德纳拉斯同志：值此罗马尼亚人民共和国建军节之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员向您致热烈的祝贺。为了和平与安全，愿罗马尼亚军队获得更进一步的成就。
    中国人民解放军总司令  朱德
    一九五二年十月一日  于北京
