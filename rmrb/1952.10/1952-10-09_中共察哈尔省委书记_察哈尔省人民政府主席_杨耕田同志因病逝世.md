    Comrade Yang Gengtian, Secretary of the CPC Chahar Provincial Committee and Chairman of the Chahar Provincial People's Government, Passes Away Due to Illness

    [This newspaper] Comrade Yang Gengtian, Secretary of the Chahar Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the Chahar Provincial People's Government, and Political Commissar of the Chahar Provincial Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, passed away on October 8th due to a prolonged illness. In order to handle the funeral arrangements for Comrade Yang Gengtian, the North China Bureau established a funeral committee on October 8th, with the following members: Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao, Wang Congwu, Li Baohua, Liu Xiufeng, Lin Tie, Zhang Su, Hu Xikui, Tang Yanjie, Wang Ping, Liu Jie, Zhang Linchi, Li Gengtao, Chen Shouchong, Xun Changwu, Xu Shiping, Tian Xiujuan, Wang Wenxing, Jiang Zhanchun, Yang Shijie, Sun Jingwen, Li Jihuan, Lü Fu, Shi Zhiben, Hu Kaiming, Li Jiesan, Sun Ming, Wang Mengling, Liang Zhengzhong, Zhang Xianjin, Zhu Weihua, Xing Yuhong, Zhao Ziqian, Zheng Jiqiao.
    On October 9th, the funeral committee for Comrade Yang Gengtian issued the following obituary:

    Obituary

    Comrade Yang Gengtian, Secretary of the Chahar Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the Chahar Provincial People's Government, and Political Commissar of the Chahar Provincial Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, passed away in Beijing at 8:20 AM on October 8, 1952, due to cirrhosis of the liver, which had been treated for a long time without success. He was 39 years old. The memorial service will be held at the Jiaxing Temple Funeral Home in Beijing at 9:30 AM on October 10th. Friends of Comrade Gengtian who wish to send elegiac couplets or wreaths are requested to deliver them to the secretariat of the committee (the office is located at Jiaxing Temple Funeral Home, address: Bei Huangchenggen, telephone number: Second Bureau 0104) before 6:00 PM on October 9th.
                 The Funeral Committee for Comrade Yang Gengtian
                 October 9, 1952

### 中共察哈尔省委书记  察哈尔省人民政府主席  杨耕田同志因病逝世

1952-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    中共察哈尔省委书记
    察哈尔省人民政府主席
    杨耕田同志因病逝世
    【本报讯】中国共产党察哈尔省委员会书记、察哈尔省人民政府主席、中国人民解放军察哈尔省军区政治委员杨耕田同志因病久治不愈，于十月八日逝世。为主持杨耕田同志治丧事宜，华北区于十月八日成立治丧委员会，名单如下：薄一波、聂荣臻、刘澜涛、王从吾、李葆华、刘秀峰、林铁、张苏、胡锡奎、唐延杰、王平、刘杰、张林池、李耕涛、陈守中、荀昌五、许世平、田秀涓、王文兴、姜占春、杨士杰、孙敬文、李济寰、吕复、石志本、胡开明、李杰三、孙明、王梦龄、梁正中、张献金、朱卫华、邢予洪、赵子谦、郑季翘。
    十月九日，杨耕田同志治丧委员会发出讣告如下：
    讣闻
    中国共产党察哈尔省委员会书记、察哈尔省人民政府主席、中国人民解放军察哈尔省军区政治委员杨耕田同志，因患肝脏硬化症，久治不愈，不幸于一九五二年十月八日八时二十分在北京逝世。享年三十九岁。兹订于十月十日上午九时半在北京嘉兴寺殡仪馆举行追悼会。如蒙耕田同志生前友好赠送挽联、花圈，请于十月九日十八时前交委员会秘书处（办公处设嘉兴寺殡仪馆，地址北皇城根，电话二局○一○四）。
                 杨耕田同志治丧委员会
                 一九五二年十月九日
