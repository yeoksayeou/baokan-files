Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country produces a large quantity of fruits in all regions. These are apples produced in Jinzhou, Lüda District. Members of the Wang Hongyuan Fruit Tree Production Cooperative, an agricultural labor model in Jinzhou, are packing apples into wooden boxes, ready to be transported to the local products company for sale.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各地都出产大量水果。这是旅大区金州出产的苹果。金州农业劳动模范王洪元果树生产合作社的社员，正把苹果装在木箱里，准备运到土产公司去出售。
