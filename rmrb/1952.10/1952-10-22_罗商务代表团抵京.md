Title: Romanian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-22
Page: 1

    Romanian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] The Romanian People's Republic Trade Delegation, consisting of ten members, led by Stanțiu Emil, Chairman of the Food Supply Committee of the Romanian People's Republic Government, and Deputy Leader Bobescu Marcel, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing on October 21st. They were greeted by Lei Renmin and Xu Xuehan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Lin Haiyun, Director of the General Office, Jia Shi and Yi Qintang, Deputy Directors of the Second Bureau, Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau, Kang Li, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, Wen Ning, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.
    Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation was Rudenko, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China.

### 罗商务代表团抵京

1952-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    罗商务代表团抵京
    【新华社二十一日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国商务代表团一行十人由团长罗马尼亚人民共和国政府粮食供应委员会主席斯坦裘·艾米尔，副团长罗马尼亚人民共和国对外贸易部第一副部长包别斯库·马尔契尔率领，在十月二十一日抵京。前往迎接的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民、徐雪寒，办公厅主任林海云，第二局副局长贾石、依钦堂，进口局局长张化东，出口局副局长康利，以及外交部苏联东欧司副司长温宁，交际处副处长沈平等。
    前往机场欢迎的还有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使鲁登科等。
