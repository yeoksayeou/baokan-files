Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. Delegation and Song and Dance Troupe Visit Marshal Choibalsan's Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 29th. The Chinese Art Troupe, participating in the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" sponsored by the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Ulaanbaatar on the 27th. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Sharav; Secretary of the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party, Susorbaatar; Chairman of the Ulaanbaatar Laborers' Khural, Bag; Chairman of the Central Council of the Mongolian National Women's Congress, Udval; Secretary-General of the Central Council of Mongolian Trade Unions, Ganbold; Deputy Minister of Education, Zamsuren; President of the Academy of Sciences, Damdin Süren; President of the Academy of Arts, Bolod; Director of the Department of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ochirbat; Director of the Department of the Orient, Sanj Mito; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, Idam Süren; Director of the Mongolian National Theater, Tsegmid; National Meritorious Artist, Choijilsuren; and representatives from mass organizations and cultural and artistic circles of Ulaanbaatar. The Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Ji Yatai, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport. Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Sharav, delivered a welcoming speech, saying: "The Mongolian people welcome the establishment of the People's Republic of China with great joy and celebrate the achievements made by the great Chinese people in the past three years." The purpose of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" is to broadly understand the achievements made by the great Chinese people under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong in the large-scale construction work that is currently booming throughout the new China. In order to commemorate this Friendship Week, our workers, herdsmen, and intellectuals have undertaken new competitive tasks and achieved many great results in their work. The Mongolia-China Friendship Week will surely further deepen and consolidate the fraternal friendship between our two peoples, which is of great significance in the cause of safeguarding peace and security in Asia and the world. Zhou Libo, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, also made a speech at the airport.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 2nd. After arriving in the Mongolian People's Republic on the 27th, the People's Republic of China Art Troupe was warmly welcomed and received by the Mongolian government and people from all walks of life.
    On the afternoon of the 27th, the delegation, accompanied by Zamsuren, Deputy Minister of Education of Mongolia, toured the city of Ulaanbaatar and visited the Mongolian Revolutionary History Museum. In the evening, the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" Working Committee held a party at the National Theater, performing the Soviet play "In the Jungle" by Korneychuk, to entertain the delegation and all members of the song and dance troupe. On the 29th, the delegation, accompanied by Sharav, Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zamsuren, Deputy Minister of Education, and Sanj Mito, Director of the Department of the Orient of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, visited the Mongolian National Choibalsan University.
    In front of the delegation's residence, the Altai Hotel, Mongolian students and Young Pioneers run up every day to warmly shake hands with the members of the delegation and the song and dance troupe. The Mongolian cultural and artistic circles have prepared to hold various academic seminars and thematic visits with the Chinese Art Troupe during the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week."
    【Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 2nd. On the afternoon of September 30th, Zhou Libo, head of the People's Republic of China Art Troupe, led the delegation and all members of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe to pay respects to the mausoleum of Marshal Choibalsan, leader of the Mongolian People's Revolution, and laid a wreath. Ji Yatai, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and embassy staff accompanied the delegation to the mausoleum.

### 我国文艺代表团抵达蒙古首都乌兰巴托  代表团及歌舞团全体团员晋谒乔巴山元帅陵墓

1952-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文艺代表团抵达蒙古首都乌兰巴托
    代表团及歌舞团全体团员晋谒乔巴山元帅陵墓
    【新华社乌兰巴托二十九日电】参加蒙古人民共和国政府主办的“蒙中友好旬”的我国文艺代表团已于二十七日到达乌兰巴托。到机场欢迎的有：蒙古人民共和国副总理沙拉普，人民革命党中央委员会书记苏索巴马，乌兰巴托劳动者小呼拉尔主席巴格，蒙古全国劳动妇女大会中央理事会主席乌达瓦尔，蒙古工会中央理事会秘书长冈波卢伦，教育部副部长扎木苏荣，科学院院长丹姆丁索龙，艺术院院长宝洛德，外交部交际司司长奥齐尔巴图，东方司司长桑几米托，蒙古革命青年团中央书记义达木苏伦，蒙古国家剧院院长策格米德，国家功勋演员超格吉勒玛以及乌兰巴托市各群众团体和文化艺术界人士等，中国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰及使馆人员也到机场欢迎。蒙古人民共和国副总理沙拉普致欢迎词说：我们蒙古人民怀着极高兴的心情迎接了中华人民共和国的成立并欢欣祝贺伟大中国人民在三年来所获得的成就。“蒙中友好旬”的目的就是为了广泛地了解伟大中国人民在伟大领袖毛泽东主席领导之下，目前正在整个新中国蓬勃展开的大规模的建设性的工作中所取得的成就。为了纪念这一友好旬，我国的工人、牧民和知识分子都担负起了新的竞赛任务，在工作中获得许多巨大的成绩，蒙中友好旬一定能使我们两国人民之间的兄弟般的友谊进一步地亲密和巩固，这在保卫亚洲及世界和平与安全的事业中有着非常巨大的意义。我文艺代表团团长周立波在机场也发表了谈话。
    【新华社乌兰巴托二日电】中华人民共和国文艺代表团于二十七日抵达蒙古人民共和国以后，受到蒙古政府和各界人民热情的欢迎和接待。
    二十七日下午，代表团由蒙古教育部副部长扎木苏荣陪同环游乌兰巴托市区，并参观了蒙古革命历史博物馆。当晚，“蒙中友好旬”工作委员会在国家剧院举行晚会，演出苏联考涅楚克的话剧“在丛林中”，招待代表团及歌舞团全体团员。二十九日，代表团由蒙古人民共和国副总理沙拉普、教育部副部长扎木苏荣、外交部东方司司长桑几米托等陪同参观了蒙古国立乔巴山大学。
    在代表团的住所阿尔泰旅馆门前，每天都有蒙古的青年学生和少年儿童队员跑来和代表团及歌舞团团员们亲切地握手。蒙古的文化艺术界已准备在“蒙中友好旬”期间和中国文艺代表团举行各种学术座谈会，进行专题的访问。
    【新华社乌兰巴托二日电】中华人民共和国文艺代表团团长周立波于九月三十日下午率领代表团及中国歌舞团全体团员晋谒蒙古人民革命领袖乔巴山元帅陵墓，并献花圈。陪同代表团前往谒陵的有我国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰及使馆人员。
