Title: Tsendenbal Returns to Beijing After Visiting Nanjing and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1952-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsendenbal, returned to Beijing on the morning of the 13th after visiting cities such as Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. Returning with him were delegation members, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Ramsuren, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Jalkhasaikhan and his wife, the Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Purbuzhal, and the Deputy Director of the Eastern Department, Dulburjin. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, also accompanied them back to Beijing.

Prime Minister Tsendenbal and his party left Beijing on the 5th of this month to visit cities such as Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. The delegation visited factories and historical sites in various places and was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life.

### 泽登巴尔总理访南京等地后返京

1952-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔总理访南京等地后返京
    【新华社十三日讯】蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔在访问了南京、上海、杭州等城市后，已于十三日上午返抵北京。同返的有代表团团员、蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民共和国驻华特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗及其夫人，蒙古人民共和国外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒和东方司副司长都勒布尔金等。我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如亦陪同返京。
    泽登巴尔总理等一行于本月五日离开北京，前往南京、上海、杭州等城市访问。代表团在各地参观了工厂和名胜古迹，受到各界人民的热烈欢迎。
