Title: The U.S. Side Violates the Agreement Again by Bombarding the Meeting Area; Our Liaison Officer Lodges a Protest with the U.S. Side
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 20th – On the morning of October 19th, at approximately 11:00 AM, the U.S. side violated the agreement regarding the Kaesong armistice negotiation meeting area by bombarding the Panmunjom meeting area. The shells exploded within the meeting area. Security officers from both sides' meeting areas conducted a joint investigation, and irrefutable evidence, both witnesses and physical evidence, confirmed the U.S. side's responsibility. On the 20th, our chief liaison officer lodged a protest with the opposing side, pointing out that the incident on the 19th was the second violation of the agreement by the U.S. side's bombardment of the meeting area this month. Our chief liaison officer demanded that the opposing side earnestly abide by the agreement, strictly restrain their military personnel, and prevent similar incidents from occurring again.

### 美方又违反协议炮击会场区  我方联络官向美方提出抗议

1952-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又违反协议炮击会场区
    我方联络官向美方提出抗议
    【新华社开城二十日电】十月十九日上午十一时左右，美方破坏朝鲜停战谈判会场区协议，炮击板门店会场区，炮弹在会场区内爆炸。双方会场区安全军官进行了共同调查，人证和物证都无可争辩地确定了美方的责任。二十日我方首席联络官向对方提出抗议，指出十九日的事件已是本月份内美方第二次炮击会场区的违反协议的事件。我方首席联络官要求对方切实遵守协议，严格约束对方军事人员，使类似事件不再发生。
