Title: Chinese Art Troupe Pays Respects to Chairman Bumtsend
Author:
Date: 1952-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 8th: The People's Republic of China Art Troupe, accompanied by Ji Yaotai, our country's ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic, paid respects to Bumtsend, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, on the 7th.

Present were: Suranzhab, Vice Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Jamsorong-Sambuu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Zhou Libo, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the Mongolian government and people, and presented commemorative gifts to Bumtsend and Suranzhab.

### 我国文艺代表团  谒见布曼增迪主席

1952-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我国文艺代表团
    谒见布曼增迪主席
    【新华社乌兰巴托八日电】中华人民共和国文艺代表团七日由我国驻蒙古人民共和国大使吉雅泰陪同，谒见蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席布曼增迪。
    在座的有：蒙古人民共和国副总理苏兰扎布和外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布。
    中国文艺代表团团长周立波在对蒙古政府与人民的热情接待表示谢意后，向布曼增迪和苏兰扎布赠送纪念礼品。
