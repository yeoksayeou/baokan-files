Title: Speech by Japanese Representative Professor Minami Hiroshi

Author: 
Date: 1952-10-14
Page: 1

We, the Japanese delegation, like all the representatives attending this conference, would like to express our gratitude once again to our Chinese friends for such a successful outcome of this conference.

Although the conference has successfully concluded today, we believe that today is the first step for the Asian peace movement to embark on a new journey, a day of beginning. Because the end of the conference is also the beginning of our great work.

At this conference, the peace movements of all participating countries have transcended national boundaries and united closely.

Our task is to bring this spirit of unity back to our respective countries and put it into practice. In this way, the peace movements in our respective countries can mutually benefit each other, cooperate, and accelerate their development into a greater force.

At this conference, the representatives of all participating countries unanimously agreed that Japan's conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty is a prerequisite for establishing peace in the Asian and Pacific region; they expressed deep understanding of this and gave us strong support. We, the Japanese delegation, are greatly encouraged by this!

We will bring this result back to Japan. With the cooperation of all of you, we will make Japan a peaceful country as soon as possible, in order to strive for a happy common life together with the nations of Asia and the Pacific region.

Finally, we once again pay our heartfelt respect to the success of this conference, which symbolizes the friendship and unity of peace activists in Asia, the Pacific region, and countries around the world.

Long live the people of Beijing!

Long live the Chinese people!

Long live the people of the world!

Long live world peace!

### 日本代表南博教授演词

1952-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    日本代表南博教授演词
    我们日本代表团，对于这次大会得到如此圆满的结果，和所有参加大会的各位代表一样，要再次地向中国的朋友们表示感谢。
    会议虽然在今天胜利地结束了；但是我们认为：今天才是亚洲和平运动踏上从新出发的第一步，是一个开始的日子。因为，会议的结束，同时就是我们的伟大工作的开始。
    在这一次大会上，所有参加的各个国家的和平运动，都超越了国家的境界紧密地团结在一起了。
    我们的任务，就是要把这种团结的精神，带回到我们各个国家里去，表现在实践上。这样，我们各个国家的和平运动，便能够相互有利的配合起来，加速的发展为更大的力量。
    在这次大会上，参加会议的各国代表，一致认为日本的缔结全面性和约，是确立亚洲及太平洋区域和平的前提条件；对此表示了深刻的理解，并对我们给予了有力的支持。我们日本代表团，为此感到莫大的振奋！
    我们要把这个结果带回日本去。由于有诸位先生的合作，我们要尽速地使日本成为和平的国家，以求得和亚洲、太平洋区域的各民族在一起，努力于幸福的共同生活。
    最后，我们对于象征着亚洲、太平洋区域以及世界各国和平运动者的友爱与团结的这次大会的成功，再一次地致以衷心的敬意。
    北京市民万岁！
    中国人民万岁！
    世界人民万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
