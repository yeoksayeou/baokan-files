Title: Mongolian Deputy Prime Minister Suranjab Receives Chinese Art and Literature Delegation; "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" Preparatory Committee Holds New China Film Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 5th: Mongolian People's Republic Deputy Prime Minister Suranjab received the head of the Chinese Art and Literature Delegation, Zhou Libo, and delegation members Ding Yi, Wang Chaowen, Bu He, and Zhang Yunxi on the 4th. The delegation was accompanied by Ji Yatai, our country's ambassador to Mongolia.

Those present during Suranjab's reception of the delegation also included Mongolian Deputy Prime Minister Sharap and Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee Secretary Susobama.

Deputy Prime Minister Suranjab extended a warm welcome to the Chinese Art and Literature Delegation for visiting Mongolia and participating in "Mongolia-China Friendship Week," and introduced the general overview of the construction achievements of the Mongolian People's Republic to the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 6th: The New China Film Exhibition, organized by the Preparatory Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic's "Mongolia-China Friendship Week," held its opening ceremony on October 5th at the Nirjeb Ochib Central Cinema in Ulaanbaatar.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Baoluo, the Dean of the Mongolian Academy of Arts. In his opening remarks, he said: The People's Republic of China has sent excellent films during the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week." Through these films, the Mongolian people will have a deeper understanding of the great achievements of the great and heroic Chinese people in the economic and cultural fields. The New China Film Exhibition will have a more significant role in further developing and strengthening the friendship and cultural exchange between the Mongolian and Chinese people.

Urong, a member of the Mongolian Academy of Arts, People's Actor, and recipient of the Choibalsan Prize, introduced the development and achievements of the New China film industry at the meeting. She said: The achievements of the New China film industry will be a good reference for the further development of the Mongolian film industry.

Zhou Libo, the head of the Chinese Art and Literature Delegation, was invited to speak. He briefly introduced the themes and contents of the six Chinese films participating in the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" performances. These six Chinese films are: "The Victory of the Chinese People," "The White-Haired Girl," "Resist America, Aid Korea," "Red Flag on Cui Gang," "Steel Warrior," and "Sons and Daughters of China."

These six films have been screened simultaneously at the Nirjeb Ochib Central Cinema and the Aralt Cinema in Ulaanbaatar since October 1st, and are also being screened simultaneously in the five cities of Choibalsan, Ondorkhaan, Sainshand, Choir and Khovd. The Chinese films have been warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Mongolia. At the Nirjeb Ochib Central Cinema and the Aralt Cinema, twenty-eight screenings were held in just three days, each one fully booked, with more than 12,000 viewers. The two cinemas originally scheduled two screenings per day, but due to the enthusiastic booking of tickets by the audience, extra screenings were added every day. Along Stalin Avenue, workers, students, staff, soldiers, and residents lined up every day to watch the movies. In front of the cinema, primary school students and young pioneers wearing red scarves sang "The East is Red" and "Singing Mao Zedong" composed by Mongolian composer Morjdorj. Whenever Chairman Mao appeared on the screen, the audience burst into warm applause and cheers that lasted for a long time.

### 蒙古副总理苏兰扎布接见我国文艺代表团  “蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会举办新中国电影展览

1952-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古副总理苏兰扎布接见我国文艺代表团
    “蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会举办新中国电影展览
    【新华社乌兰巴托五日电】蒙古人民共和国副总理苏兰扎布四日接见中国文艺代表团团长周立波及代表团团员：丁毅、王朝闻、布赫、张云溪。代表团由我国驻蒙大使吉雅泰陪同前往。
    苏兰扎布接见代表团时在座的还有：蒙古副总理沙拉普和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记苏索巴马。
    苏兰扎布副总理对中国文艺代表团访问蒙古参加“蒙中友好旬”表示热烈欢迎，并向代表团介绍了蒙古人民共和国建设成就的一般概况。
    【新华社乌兰巴托六日电】蒙古人民共和国“蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会主办的新中国电影展览，于十月五日在乌兰巴托的尼尔杰布·奥奇布中央电影院举行揭幕仪式。
    揭幕仪式由蒙古艺术院院长宝洛德主持。他在致开幕词时说：中华人民共和国在“蒙中友好旬”期间送来了优秀的影片。通过这些影片，蒙古人民将更深刻地了解伟大英勇的中国人民在经济上、文化上的伟大成就。新中国电影展览对于进一步发展与加强蒙中两国人民的友谊与文化的交流，将会有更显著的作用。
    蒙古艺术院委员、人民演员、乔巴山奖金获得者乌荣在会上介绍了新中国电影事业的发展与成就。她说：新中国电影事业的成就，对蒙古电影事业的进一步发展将是很好的借镜。
    中国文艺代表团团长周立波应邀讲话。他扼要地介绍了参加“蒙中友好旬”演出的六部中国影片的主题与内容。这六部中国影片是：“中国人民的胜利”、“白毛女”、“抗美援朝”、“翠岗红旗”、“钢铁战士”、“中华女儿”。
    这六部影片已经从十月一日起在乌兰巴托的尼尔杰布·奥奇布中央电影院和阿勒特电影院同时放映，并在乔巴山、翁都尔汉、三音尚达、齐齐尔里克和科布多五个城市也同时放映。中国影片受到了蒙古各阶层人民热烈的欢迎。在尼尔杰布·奥奇布中央电影院和阿勒特电影院，仅在三天中，就放映了二十八场，场场满座，观众有一万二千多人。这两家电影院原定每天放映二场，由于观众预定电影票非常踊跃，以致每天都加场。沿着斯大林大街，每天都有工人、学生、职员、战士和居民们排着长长的行列去看电影。电影院门前，小学生和结着红领巾的少年儿童队员，高唱着“东方红”和蒙古作曲家莫日道尔吉作的“歌唱毛泽东”。每当毛主席在银幕上出现时，观众们便响起了热烈的掌声和欢呼声，经久不息。
