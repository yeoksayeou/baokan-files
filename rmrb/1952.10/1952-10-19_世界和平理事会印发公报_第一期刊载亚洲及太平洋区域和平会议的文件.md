Title: World Peace Council Publishes Bulletin: First Issue Contains Documents from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, October 17th: The World Peace Council's press office has published a bulletin in Vienna, the purpose of which is to keep people around the world informed of the progress of the work of various countries in preparing for the World People's Peace Congress.

The first issue of the bulletin contains documents from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The bulletin emphasizes that 1.6 billion people in Asian and Pacific countries demand peace and international peaceful cooperation.

The bulletin also reports on the preparations for the World People's Peace Congress in Britain, France, and Arab countries.

### 世界和平理事会印发公报  第一期刊载亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的文件

1952-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会印发公报
    第一期刊载亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的文件
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社维也纳十七日讯：世界和平理事会新闻处已在维也纳印发公报，其目的是使全世界的人民都随时知道各国筹备世界人民和平大会的工作的进展情况。
    公报的第一期刊载了亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的文件。公报着重指出：亚洲及太平洋各国十六亿人民要求和平与国际和平合作。
    公报并且报道了英国、法国和阿拉伯国家筹备世界人民和平大会的情况。
