Title: Nation Celebrates Second Anniversary of Resisting America and Aiding Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-10-27
Page: 1

    Nation Celebrates Second Anniversary of Resisting America and Aiding Korea
    [This News] Major cities such as Shenyang, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, Guangzhou, and Dihua, with excitement, warmly celebrated the second anniversary of Resisting America and Aiding Korea, congratulating the brilliant victories achieved by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in fighting abroad for the past two years.
    Various places held grand celebrations. Shenyang held a celebration meeting on the 25th with more than 2,000 people participating. Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast General Branch of Resisting America and Aiding Korea, called on the city and the people of Northeast China to continue to heighten vigilance, strengthen the work of Resisting America and Aiding Korea, and more forcefully strike at the American aggressors. Kim Kui-nam, representative of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China stationed in Shenyang, presented a flag to the Volunteer Army convalescents on behalf of the Korean expatriates in the Northeast. Major cities such as Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, Guangzhou, and Dihua held celebrations. The heads of the general branches and branches of Resisting America and Aiding Korea in various places praised the great victories achieved by the Volunteer Army in fighting abroad for two years at the meeting, and unanimously pointed out the American aggressors' recent delaying of the armistice negotiations in an attempt to expand the war. They called on people from all walks of life to further carry out the movement to increase production and practice economy, and use greater strength to support the just struggle of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and defend world peace. The celebrations in various places all passed congratulatory telegrams to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army.
    Factories, organs, schools, and troops in various cities carried out celebration activities: holding discussion meetings, report meetings, revising patriotic pacts, etc. All the staff and workers of the Hengda Flour Mill General Branch in Tianjin, in order to celebrate the second anniversary of the Volunteer Army's participation in the war, completed the administrative production task for the entire year of 1952 at 12:00 noon on the 25th, sixty-seven days ahead of schedule, with extremely excitement. The staff and workers also expressed their determination to maintain their existing glory, never be arrogant, continue to tap potential and find tricks, improve technology, promote advanced experience, and do a good job in key assessment work to further strive to complete the task of increasing production and practicing economy ahead of schedule. Various organs in Wuhan held small discussion meetings to talk about the heroic achievements of the Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. Students of all ethnic groups at Yunnan Nationalities Institute in Kunming guaranteed at the commemoration meeting to repay the Volunteer Army by studying hard. People from all walks of life in the first district of Xi'an held an evening party on the 25th to entertain the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers. The Lu Agou team of the state-owned Shanghai No. 2 Textile Machinery Factory inspected and revised the patriotic pact before the second anniversary of Resisting America and Aiding Korea. All team members put forward specific guarantees, determined to further improve quality, complete this year's winter production task ahead of schedule, and support the Korean front. In Lhasa, on the frontier of the motherland, the soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet held a commemorative evening party in front of their tents. They were all excited about the great victory of the Volunteer Army over the past two years. The soldiers guaranteed to intensify the land reclamation competition. They said: "Volunteer Army brothers use their blood to defend the motherland and defend peace! We must sweat more to build the frontier of the motherland."
    People from all over the country warmly comforted the wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army who were recuperating and the families of the Volunteer Army. On the 24th, people from all walks of life in Shenyang sent thirty representatives to the Volunteer Army convalescent hospital to express their condolences and showed films such as "Resisting America and Aiding Korea" and "Return to the Front" for the wounded and sick soldiers. The Andong Anti-American Aid Korea Branch organized a delegation to warmly comfort the wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army and the families of the Volunteer Army. More than 200 people from various democratic parties, people's organizations, labor models, and women models in Tianjin formed a delegation and carried a letter of condolence from the mayor of Tianjin, Huang Jing, and the deputy mayors, Wu De and Zhou Shutao, to various rear hospitals on the 25th to comfort the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army who were recuperating in Tianjin. The representatives of the delegation conveyed the deep concern of the people of Tianjin to them, encouraged them to recuperate with peace of mind, and strive to recover their health as soon as possible. Li Xiannian, the mayor of Wuhan, personally consoled the wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army who were recuperating in the city. After receiving the condolences from people from all walks of life, the wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army were determined to strive to recover their health as soon as possible, return to the front, and strike at the American aggressors.
    People's organizations and the masses of people throughout the country wrote letters or sent telegrams to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. The telegram from the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions described the efforts of all Shanghai workers in the past two years to produce and support the Korean front. The Northwest Federation of Trade Unions, the Northwest Democratic Women's Federation, and other organizations jointly wrote to Commander Peng Dehuai of the Volunteer Army, praising the heroic deeds of the Volunteer Army. The workers of the Second Printing Factory of the Hankou Yangtze River Daily said in their letter about the encouragement the victory of the Volunteer Army gave them: "When our typesetting workers typeset your good news, we typeset it quickly and well, and we felt unspeakably happy. We are determined to speed up production and work hard to build the motherland." Before the second anniversary of Resisting America and Aiding Korea, people from all walks of life in Shenyang wrote nearly ten thousand letters of condolences to the Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. The letters were full of enthusiasm and unanimously put forward guarantees: to use greater strength to support the heroes on the Korean front.

### 全国庆贺抗美援朝两周年

1952-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国庆贺抗美援朝两周年
    【本报讯】沈阳、天津、上海、武汉、重庆、西安、广州、迪化等大城市人民，都以兴奋的心情，热烈庆祝抗美援朝两周年，祝贺中国人民志愿军出国作战两年来所获得的辉煌胜利。
    各地都举行了盛大的庆祝会。沈阳市在二十五日举行了二千余人参加的庆祝大会。东北抗美援朝总分会主席高崇民在会上号召全市和全东北人民继续提高警惕，加强抗美援朝工作，更有力地打击美国侵略者。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆驻沈阳代表金贵南，代表东北区的朝鲜侨民在会上向志愿军休养员献旗。上海、武汉、重庆、西安、广州、迪化等各大城市都举行了庆祝会。各地抗美援朝总分会和分会的负责人，在会上都颂扬了志愿军出国作战两周年所获得的伟大胜利，并一致指出美国侵略者最近拖延停战谈判，企图扩大战争的阴谋诡计，号召各界人民进一步开展增产节约运动，用更大的力量来支援中国人民志愿军的正义斗争，保卫世界和平。各地的庆祝会都通过了给中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军致敬电。
    各城市的工厂、机关、学校、部队都进行了庆祝活动：举行座谈会、报告会，修订爱国公约等。天津市恒大面粉厂总分厂全体职工，为庆祝志愿军出国作战两周年，以极兴奋的心情，于二十五日十二时，提前六十七天完成了一九五二年全年行政生产任务。职工们并表示决心保持已有的光荣，绝不骄傲，继续挖潜力找窍门，改进技术，推广先进经验，搞好重点查定工作，进一步争取提前完成增产节约任务。武汉市各机关举行了小型座谈会，讲述志愿军和朝鲜人民军的丰功伟绩。昆明市云南民族学院的各族学员们，在纪念会上保证以搞好学习来报答志愿军。西安市第一区各界人民在二十五日举行了晚会，招待革命烈士家属和革命军人家属。国营上海第二纺织机械厂陆阿狗小组在抗美援朝两周年前夕检查和修订了爱国公约，全体组员提出了具体保证，决心进一步提高质量，提前完成今年冬季生产任务，支援朝鲜前线。在祖国边疆的拉萨市，人民解放军入藏部队的战士们在帐篷前举行了纪念晚会，他们都为志愿军两年来的伟大胜利而感到兴奋。战士们保证加紧展开开荒竞赛。他们说：“志愿军弟兄们用鲜血保卫祖国，保卫和平！我们要多流汗，建设祖国的边疆。”
    各地人民热烈地慰问了正在休养的志愿军伤病员和志愿军的家属。沈阳市各界人民在二十四日推派了三十个代表到志愿军休养院进行慰问，并为伤病员放映“抗美援朝”、“重返前线”等影片。安东市的抗美援朝分会组织了慰问团，热烈慰问志愿军伤病员和志愿军家属。天津市各民主党派、各人民团体及劳动模范、妇女模范等二百余人组成慰问团，于二十五日携带天津市人民政府黄敬市长，吴德、周叔弢副市长的慰问信，分赴各后方医院，慰问中国人民志愿军与中国人民解放军在天津休养的伤病员。慰问团的代表们传达了天津市各界人民对他们的深切关怀，鼓励他们安心休养，争取早日恢复健康。武汉市人民政府市长李先念亲自慰问了在该市休养的志愿军伤病员。志愿军伤病员受到各界人民的慰问后，决心争取早日恢复健康，重返前线，打击美国侵略者。
    全国各地的人民团体和人民群众都写信或打电报给中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。上海总工会的电报上叙述了全上海工人两年来努力生产、支援朝鲜前线的情况。西北总工会、西北民主妇女联合会等团体联名写信给志愿军彭德怀司令员，歌颂志愿军的英雄事迹。汉口长江日报第二印刷厂工人，在信中谈到志愿军的胜利对他们的鼓舞时说：“我们排字工人，当排到你们的捷报时，排得又快又好，心里说不出的高兴。我们决定要加紧生产，为建设祖国而努力。”沈阳市各界人民在抗美援朝两周年前夕，给志愿军和朝鲜人民军写了近万封的慰问信，信上充满了热情，一致提出保证：要用更大的力量来支援朝鲜前线的英雄们。
