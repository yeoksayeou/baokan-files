Title: Soong Ching-ling and Guo Moruo Send Telegrams to Joliot-Curie Reporting on the Establishment of the Peace Liaison Committee and Expressing Hope for His Guidance on the Committee's Future Work
Author:
Date: 1952-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Soong Ching-ling, Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, sent a telegram to Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, on October 21st. The telegram reads as follows:

Respected Chairman of the World Peace Council, Mr. Joliot-Curie:

I, on behalf of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, which was formed by the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, extend the warmest respect to you, our respected chairman and friend.

The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference has completed its mission and has now concluded victoriously. Throughout our work, we constantly felt the encouragement and support given to us by the World Peace Council and you. Please allow me, on behalf of this Peace Conference and the Regional Peace Liaison Committee it established, to express our deep gratitude for your congratulatory telegram and valuable instructions to the conference. At the same time, we earnestly hope that you will provide constant guidance and support for the future work of our Liaison Committee.

We believe that the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference is an important step in welcoming the upcoming World People's Peace Congress. At the Regional Peace Conference, we also passed a resolution supporting the convening of the World People's Peace Congress, calling on the people of all countries in this region, as well as all political, social, and religious groups, to make the greatest efforts to prepare for the upcoming World People's Peace Congress and to send broadly representative delegations to attend.

I wish the World People's Peace Congress success!

Wishing you good health!

Soong Ching-ling
Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
October 21, 1952, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram to Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, on October 21st. The telegram reads as follows:

To Mr. Joliot-Curie, Prague World Peace Council, Dear Chairman and Friend:

Following your correct and valuable instructions, the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference concluded with great success. All delegates unanimously agreed and supported the convening of the World People's Peace Congress in Vienna. Our Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference decided to organize a Liaison Committee and elected Mr. Soong Ching-ling as Chairman.

I sincerely thank you for your great efforts in organizing the International Scientific Committee. The report of this committee is of great significance for striving for world peace. Your recent righteous statement at the Pleyel Hall marks a step forward in the peace movement. The Chinese people express their high respect to you. Please convey our gratitude to all the members of the International Scientific Committee who continue their heroic struggle after returning to their countries.

With brotherly greetings,

Guo Moruo, October 21st

### 宋庆龄郭沫若分电约里奥——居里  报告和平联络委员会成立  盼望他对联络委员会今后的工作给以指导

1952-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄郭沫若分电约里奥——居里
    报告和平联络委员会成立
    盼望他对联络委员会今后的工作给以指导
    【新华社三十日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会主席宋庆龄于十月二十一日致电世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里，电文如下：敬爱的世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里先生：
    我代表由亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议所产生的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会向您，敬爱的主席与朋友，致最热烈的敬意。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议完成了它的任务，现已胜利地闭幕了。在我们的工作里经常感觉到世界和平理事会和您，给予我们的鼓舞和支持。请允许我代表我们这次的和平会议和由它所产生的区域和平联络委员会对您给大会的贺电和珍贵的指示，表示深切的谢忱。同时，恳切地盼望您对于我们联络委员会今后的工作给以经常的指导和支持。
    我们认为，亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议是迎接即将召开的世界人民和平大会的一个重要步骤。我们在区域和平会议上并且通过了一项拥护召开世界人民和平大会的决议，号召这个区域的各国人民，以及一切政治、社会和宗教的团体尽最大的努力准备即将到来的世界人民和平大会，并派遣具有广泛代表性的代表团前往出席。
    预祝世界人民和平大会的成功！
    祝您健康！
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会主席  宋庆龄
     一九五二年十月二十一日于北京
    【新华社三十日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若于十月二十一日致电世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里，电文如下：布拉格世界和平理事会　约里奥—居里先生亲爱的主席和朋友：
    遵照您正确和宝贵的指示，亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议获得很大的成就后闭幕了。全体代表一致赞成并拥护在维也纳召开世界人民和平大会。我们的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议作出决定，组织了一个联络委员会，并推选宋庆龄先生为主席。
    我衷心地感谢您对于组织国际科学委员会所作的很大努力。这个委员会的报告书对于争取世界和平具有非常重要的意义。最近您在普列叶大厅所发表的正义声明，标志着和平运动已推进了一步，中国人民向您表示崇高的敬意。请您把我们的谢忱转达回国后继续英勇斗争的国际科学委员会的各位委员们。  此致兄弟的敬礼
    郭沫若  十月二十一日
