Title: Korean Ambassador to China, Kwon O-jik, Presents Gifts to Chairman Mao on Behalf of General Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1952-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th—Korean Ambassador to China, Kwon O-jik, on behalf of General Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, presented gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to celebrate the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, expressing the Korean people's gratitude to the Chinese people and their great leader. The gifts included famous Korean specialties such as ginseng, apples, and rice.

### 朝鲜驻我国大使权五稷  代表金日成将军向毛主席赠礼

1952-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻我国大使权五稷
    代表金日成将军向毛主席赠礼
    【新华社二十四日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成将军为庆祝中国人民志愿军参加抗美援朝战争两周年，特向中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席赠送礼品，表示朝鲜人民对中国人民及其伟大领袖的感谢。礼品有朝鲜著名的特产人参、苹果及大米等，由朝鲜驻我国大使权五稷代表金日成将军赠送。
