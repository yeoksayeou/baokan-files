Title: Marshal Stalin's Telegram to Chairman Mao
Author: Yō·Sidalín (Yo. Stalin)
Date: 1952-10-01
Page: 1

    Marshal Stalin's Telegram to Chairman Mao
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China: On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept my heartfelt congratulations, Comrade Chairman. I wish the Chinese people, the government of the People's Republic of China, and yourself new achievements in the construction of a strong people's democratic Chinese state. I wish the great friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics—the strong pillar of peace and security in the Far East and the world—further consolidation and development.
    Yo. Stalin
    Moscow, September 29, 1952

### 斯大林大元帅致毛主席电
约·斯大林
1952-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅致毛主席电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：值此中华人民共和国成立三周年之际，主席同志，请您接受我衷心的祝贺。祝中国人民，中华人民共和国政府及您本人在强大的人民民主的中国国家之建设中获得新的成就。祝中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的伟大友谊——远东及全世界和平与安全的坚强支柱更加巩固和发展。
    约·斯大林
    一九五二年九月二十九日于莫斯科
