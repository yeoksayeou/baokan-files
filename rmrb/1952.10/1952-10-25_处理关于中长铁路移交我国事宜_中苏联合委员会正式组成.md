Title: Sino-Soviet Joint Committee Formally Established to Handle Matters Concerning the Transfer of the Chinese Changchun Railway to China

Author:

Date: 1952-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, October 24th: According to the Sino-Soviet announcement of September 15, 1952, regarding the transfer of the Chinese Changchun Railway to the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese and Soviet governments have appointed members of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee. The first meeting of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee was held in Harbin on October 20th. The meeting was conducted in a friendly atmosphere. The meeting began with Soviet Committee representative M.S. Yelogov and Chinese Committee representative Yu Guangsheng reporting the list of committee members appointed by the Soviet government and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. After mutual introductions and greetings, the formal establishment of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee regarding the transfer of the Chinese Changchun Railway to the Government of the People's Republic of China was announced. The Soviet members appointed by the Soviet government were: M.S. Yelogov, Vice-Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Chinese Changchun Railway Company; N.G. Vasilyev, Chairman of the Supervisory Board of the Chinese Changchun Railway Company; G.S. Dobashin, Soviet Consul General in Harbin; N.A. Zotov, representative of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade; I.D. Imiryanov, representative of the Soviet Ministry of Finance. The Chinese representatives appointed by the Government of the People's Republic of China were: Yu Guangsheng, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Chinese Changchun Railway Company; Li Mingzhe, Vice-Chairman of the Supervisory Board of the Chinese Changchun Railway Company; Lu Xi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Northeast People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Huang Da, Deputy Minister of Trade of the Northeast People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Wang Xueming, Deputy Minister of Finance of the Northeast People's Government of the People's Republic of China. In order to smoothly carry out the work of the committee, the meeting determined that the workplace would be the Chinese Changchun Railway Board of Directors in Harbin and decided on the organizational structure of the meeting, working methods, and candidates for secretaries and translators. The meeting decided to entrust Comrades Yelogov and Yu Guangsheng to prepare the committee's work plan and submit it for discussion at the next meeting. Finally, Yu Guangsheng, on behalf of the Chinese committee members, expressed his gratitude to the Soviet committee members for their great efforts and assistance in preparing for the meeting, and wished the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee success in fulfilling the glorious task entrusted by the two governments. Yelogov, on behalf of the Soviet committee members, stated that the successful conduct of this meeting was based on the friendly cooperation in the Sino-Soviet co-management of the Chinese Changchun Railway, and he firmly believed that the work of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee would be successfully completed.

### 处理关于中长铁路移交我国事宜  中苏联合委员会正式组成

1952-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    处理关于中长铁路移交我国事宜
    中苏联合委员会正式组成
    【新华社哈尔滨二十四日电】根据一九五二年九月十五日中苏关于中国长春铁路移交中华人民共和国政府的公告，中苏两国政府已经任命了中苏联合委员会的委员。十月二十日在哈尔滨市举行了中苏联合委员会的第一次会议。会议在友好气氛中进行。会议首先由苏方委员代表叶洛果夫和我方委员代表余光生报告了苏联政府与中华人民共和国中央人民政府所任命的委员名单，在互相介绍并致敬意后，宣布关于中国长春铁路移交中华人民共和国政府的中苏联合委员会正式组成。委员会经苏联政府任命的苏方委员为：中国长春铁路公司理事会副主席姆·斯·叶洛果夫、中国长春铁路公司监事会主席恩·格·华西里也夫、苏联驻哈尔滨总领事哥·斯·多巴申、苏联对外贸易部代表恩·阿·左多夫、苏联财政部代表依·德·依米里亚诺夫；经中华人民共和国政府任命的我方代表为：中国长春铁路公司理事会主席余光生、中国长春铁路公司监事会副主席李明哲、中华人民共和国东北人民政府外事局长陆曦、中华人民共和国东北人民政府贸易部副部长黄达、中华人民共和国东北人民政府财政部副部长王学明。为了顺利地进行委员会的工作，会议确定了工作地点为哈尔滨中国长春铁路理事会会所，并确定会议组织、工作方法及秘书、翻译的人选。会议决定委托叶洛果夫与余光生二委员编拟委员会的工作计划，提交下次会议讨论。最后余光生代表我方委员，对于苏方委员关于会议准备工作的巨大努力和帮助致以谢意，并预祝中苏联合委员会胜利地完成中苏两国政府所给予的光荣任务。叶洛果夫代表苏方委员表示此次会议的胜利进行，是建筑在中苏共管中国长春铁路的友好合作的基础之上，他深信中苏联合委员会的工作，一定能胜利地完成。
