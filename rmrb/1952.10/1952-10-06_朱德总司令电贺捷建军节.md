Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends Congratulatory Message on Czechoslovak Army Day
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. On October 6th, the founding anniversary of the Czechoslovak People's Democratic Army, Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sent a telegram to Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic, to express his congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
Comrade Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic:

  　On the occasion of the founding anniversary of the Czechoslovak People's Democratic Army, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to you and all the personnel of the People's Democratic Army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and myself. May the friendship between the people and armies of China and Czechoslovakia become increasingly strong.

                    Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
                     October 5, 1952, in Beijing

### 朱德总司令电贺捷建军节

1952-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德总司令电贺捷建军节
  　【新华社五日讯】十月六日是捷克斯洛伐克共和国人民民主军建军节，中国人民解放军朱德总司令致电捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡，表示祝贺。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡同志：
  　值此捷克斯洛伐克人民民主军建军节之际，谨代表中国人民解放军及我本人，向您及全体人民民主军人员致以最热烈的祝贺。谨祝中捷两国人民及军队友谊日臻强固。
                    中国人民解放军总司令　朱德
                     一九五二年十月五日于北京
