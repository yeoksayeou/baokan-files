Title: The Chinese People's Second Delegation to Korea to Extend Regards, General Delegation Leaves Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1952-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 30th: The General Delegation of the Chinese People's Second Delegation to Korea to Extend Regards, led by Head of Delegation Liu Jingfan and Deputy Head of Delegation Hu Juewen, left Pyongyang on the 29th. Those who saw them off included: Park Tae-joon, Vice Chairman of the People's Inspection Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Park Hyo-sam, Director of the Organization and Mobilization Bureau of the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army; Li Zhongyi, representative of the General Political Bureau; Zhao Fuli, Vice Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League; Kim Jeong-chen, Minister of International Affairs; and representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang.

Head of Delegation Liu Jingfan said in a farewell speech: "Our delegation came to Korea this time and received sincere and warm welcome and hospitality from General Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people, the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, all circles in Pyongyang, and the people of various places in Korea. As we are about to leave, please allow me, on behalf of the delegation, to express my heartfelt gratitude to General Kim Il-sung and all comrades and friends.

Under the warm assistance of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Korean people, our delegation to Korea has successfully completed the glorious task entrusted to us by the Chinese people. This is to further consolidate the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and encourage the Chinese and Korean troops to strive for greater victories.

After we return to China, we will definitely convey the great victories achieved by the Korean people and the Chinese and Korean troops, as well as the Korean people's infinite love and deep friendship for the Chinese people, to all the Chinese people, so as to achieve what General Kim Il-sung said: the Chinese people will hold the banner of resisting America and aiding Korea even higher.

Finally, on behalf of the Chinese people, I wish General Kim Il-sung good health forever and the Korean people's just struggle to achieve more glorious victories."

The General Delegation of the Chinese People's Second Delegation to Korea to Extend Regards was warmly welcomed by all circles in Pyongyang after arriving in Pyongyang on October 21. Representatives of the delegation successively visited and extended regards to various agencies and organizations in Pyongyang, and held discussions with workers, farmers, women, youth, people's army heroes and other people from all walks of life in the Pyongyang area. The representatives personally witnessed the actual situation of the bloody atrocities of American aerial bandits bombing and massacring peaceful residents of Pyongyang, and the heroic Pyongyang people's unyielding and tenacious fighting spirit.

The Chinese People's Second Delegation to Korea to Extend Regards left an extremely profound and good impression on the people of Pyongyang, the temporary capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The art troupe of the delegation performed dance dramas such as "Luo Shengjiao" at the Pyongyang Underground Theater, which was warmly welcomed and highly praised by all circles in Pyongyang.

### 中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团离平壤

1952-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团离平壤
    【新华社平壤三十日电】中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团在团长刘景范、副团长胡厥文率领下，已于二十九日离开平壤。欢送他们的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府人民检阅委员会副委员长朴泰俊、朝鲜人民军最高司令部组织动员局局长朴孝三、总政治局代表李忠翼、朝鲜民主妇女同盟副委员长赵福礼、国际部长金情臣和平壤市各界代表等。
    刘景范团长在临行前发表谈话说：“我们慰问团此次来到朝鲜，受到朝鲜人民领袖金日成将军、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府、平壤市各界及朝鲜各地人民诚挚的热烈的欢迎与招待。当我们临行之时，请让我代表本团向金日成将军及各位同志们、朋友们表示衷心的感谢。
    我们赴朝慰问团在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府及朝鲜人民热情的协助下，胜利地完成了中国人民赋与我们的光荣任务。这就是进一步巩固朝中人民的友谊和鼓舞朝中人民部队争取更大的胜利。
    我们回国之后，一定把朝鲜人民及朝中部队所取得的伟大胜利以及朝鲜人民对中国人民无限的热爱与深厚的友情传达给全中国人民，以达成如金日成将军所说的：中国人民把抗美援朝的旗帜打得更高。
    最后，我谨代表中国人民祝金日成将军永远健康，祝朝鲜人民的正义斗争获得更光辉的胜利。”
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团自十月二十一日抵达平壤后，受到平壤市各界的热烈欢迎。慰问团代表们曾先后访问与慰问了平壤市各机关各团体，并与平壤地区的工人、农民、妇女、青年、人民军英雄等各界人士举行座谈。代表们亲眼看到了美国空中强盗狂轰滥炸、屠杀平壤和平居民的血腥暴行的实际情况，和英雄的平壤市人民不屈不挠、顽强斗争的坚毅精神。
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时首都平壤市人民留下了极其深刻的良好印象。慰问团的文艺工作团在平壤地下剧场演出舞剧“罗盛教”等节目，受到平壤各界热烈的欢迎和很高的评价。
