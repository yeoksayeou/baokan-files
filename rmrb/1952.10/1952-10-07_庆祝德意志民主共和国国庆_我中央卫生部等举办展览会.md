Title: Celebrating the National Day of the German Democratic Republic: Exhibition Held by Our Central Ministry of Health and Others

Author:

Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. To celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government and the Bureau of External Cultural Relations have organized an exhibition to display the glass anatomical model presented to our government by the government of the German Democratic Republic, as well as photographs introducing Germany. The opening ceremony of the exhibition was held at the waterside pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing, at 10:00 a.m. on the 6th. Mr. Koenig, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic, attended the opening ceremony. Also present were more than 70 guests from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Education, the Academy of Sciences, and the medical community of our Central People's Government.

Fu Lianzhang, Vice Minister of Health of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony, expressing his gratitude on behalf of the Chinese people, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, and the Chinese medical community to the government of the German Democratic Republic for presenting the precious glass anatomical model to the Chinese people during our National Day last year. He expressed his respect for the spirit of the scientists and working class of the German Democratic Republic who are dedicated to scientific research and manufacturing to seek happiness for mankind. Mr. Koenig, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic, delivered a reply. He congratulated the Chinese people and the scientific community on the great victories they have achieved in the struggle against the bacteriological warfare of American imperialism and in improving the health of the people.

The exhibits are arranged in four exhibition rooms. The glass anatomical model is displayed in the first exhibition room, with each part of the human model equipped with lighting to aid explanation. In addition, there is a detailed Chinese-language recording explaining the functions of each part of the human body.

The photos on display include: photos of workers in the German Democratic Republic engaged in peaceful socialist construction, including the construction of the huge "East Steel Plant" and the reconstruction of Berlin; photos illustrating the happy lives of workers and peasants; photos documenting the struggle of the people of West Germany demanding peace and unity and opposing the "General Treaty"; and various posters.

The exhibition also showcased some Chinese books that have been translated into German.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国国庆  我中央卫生部等举办展览会

1952-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国国庆
    我中央卫生部等举办展览会
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府卫生部及对外文化联络事务局为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年，特举办展览会，展览德意志民主共和国政府赠送给我国政府的玻璃人体模型及介绍德国的图片。展览会于六日上午十时在北京中山公园水榭举行揭幕典礼。德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希参加了揭幕典礼。到会并有我国中央人民政府外交部，文化部，教育部，科学院及医学界来宾共七十余人。
    中央人民政府卫生部副部长傅连暲在揭幕典礼上致词，他代表中国人民和中央人民政府卫生部以及中国医学界对于德意志民主共和国政府在去年我国国庆时赠送给我国人民以珍贵的玻璃人体模型，表示感谢。他对于德意志民主共和国的科学家和工人阶级致力于科学上的研究制造来为人类谋求幸福的精神，表示崇敬。德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希致答词。他祝贺中国人民及科学界在反对美帝国主义的细菌战和提高人民健康水平的斗争中所取得的伟大胜利。
    展览品分列在四个展览室中。玻璃人体模型陈列在第一展览室中，人体模型中的各部分都配备有帮助说明的灯光设置；此外，并附有详细讲解人体各部分作用的中文录音。
    展出的图片有：德意志民主共和国工人从事和平的社会主义建设的照片，包括建造巨大的“东方炼钢厂”及重建柏林的照片等；说明工人与农民的幸福生活的照片；纪录西德人民要求和平与统一、反对“一般性条约”斗争的照片；以及各种招贴画。
    展览会上并展出了一部分已翻译成德文的中国书籍。
