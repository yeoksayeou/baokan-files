Title: Sino-Chilean Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-24
Page: 1

    Sino-Chilean Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd) To promote the friendship between the people of China and Chile and to develop trade relations between the two countries, representatives of the China National Import and Export Corporation and Mr. Casanova, representative of the Chilean Sino-Chilean Trade Company, recently held talks in Beijing. The negotiations were conducted in a very friendly atmosphere, and the Sino-Chilean Trade Agreement was signed in Beijing on October 23rd. This is the first trade agreement signed between China and a South American country after the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference passed the "Resolution on Economic Exchange Issues." Mr. Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Mr. Casanova, the Chilean representative to the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference and representative of the Chilean Sino-Chilean Trade Company, signed on behalf of the Chilean side.
    Those attending the signing ceremony included Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department of the Council, and Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others.

### 中智贸易协议在北京签订

1952-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中智贸易协议在北京签订
    【新华社二十三日讯】为促进中国与智利两国人民的友谊及发展两国间的贸易关系，中国进出口公司代表与智利中智贸易公司代表卡萨诺伐最近在北京进行了商谈。谈判在极友好的空气中进行，并于十月二十三日在北京签订了中智贸易协议。这是亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议通过“关于经济交流问题的决议”案后，中国与南美洲国家签订的第一个贸易协议。中国方面签字的为中国进出口公司经理卢绪章，智利方面签字的为参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的智利代表、智利中智贸易公司代表卡萨诺伐。
    参加签字仪式的有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎和该会联络部部长谢筱乃，中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢等。
