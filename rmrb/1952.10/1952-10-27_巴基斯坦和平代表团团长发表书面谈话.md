Title: Head of Pakistani Peace Delegation Issues Written Statement
Author:
Date: 1952-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 25th: The head of the Pakistani delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Pir Shahab Manki Sharif, issued the following written statement before leaving China:

    The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference that we came here to attend was a great success, and we will take the news of the conference back to our country, hoping to spread this news to every remote corner. We will also take back the pleasant memories of the grand reception and sincere welcome from our Chinese hosts. We express our greatest gratitude, love, and blessings to the people of the new China. Long live the great leader Comrade Mao Zedong! Long live world peace!

### 巴基斯坦和平代表团团长发表书面谈话

1952-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦和平代表团团长发表书面谈话
    【新华社广州二十五日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的巴基斯坦代表团团长毕尔·沙哈布·曼基·沙里夫，在离开中国前发表书面谈话如下：
    我们来此参加的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议是一个伟大的成功，我们将把会议的消息带回我国，希望把这消息在一切穷乡僻壤都传播开来。我们还将带回关于我们的中国主人的盛大招待和诚挚欢迎的愉快的回忆。我们对于新中国的人民表示最大的感谢、热爱和祝福。伟大的领袖毛泽东同志万岁！世界和平万岁！
