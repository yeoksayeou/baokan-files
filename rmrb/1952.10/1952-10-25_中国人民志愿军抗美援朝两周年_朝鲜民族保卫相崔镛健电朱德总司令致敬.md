Title: Two Years of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea: Greetings from Choi Yong-kun, Minister of National Defence of Korea, to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De
Author: Cui Yongjian
Date: 1952-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: On the eve of the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, Choi Yong-kun, Minister of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, expressing his respect. The telegram is as follows:
General Zhu De:

	On the occasion of the second anniversary of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Korean People's Army and in my own name, extend my deep respect to you and all the commanders and fighters of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	The Korean people and the Korean People's Army, who are fighting a just war of liberation of the fatherland against American armed aggression, are greatly encouraged by the fraternal assistance of the great Chinese people and the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers, and are even more determined in their confidence in final victory.

	I wish you even greater achievements in your noble cause of defending and consolidating human peace and happiness!

	Choi Yong-kun
	Minister of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
	October 23, 1952, in Pyongyang

### 中国人民志愿军抗美援朝两周年  朝鲜民族保卫相崔镛健电朱德总司令致敬
崔镛健
1952-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军抗美援朝两周年
    朝鲜民族保卫相崔镛健电朱德总司令致敬
    【新华社二十四日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相崔镛健，在中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战两周年前夕，致电我中国人民解放军朱德总司令表示敬意。电文如下：朱德将军：
    兹值英雄的中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战两周年之际，我以全体朝鲜人民军指挥员、战斗员和我个人的名义，谨向您和英雄的中国人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员，致以深切的敬意。
    反对美国武装侵略、正在进行着正义的祖国解放战争的朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军，大大地被伟大的中国人民和英勇的中国人民志愿军的兄弟的援助所鼓舞，更加坚定了最后胜利的信心。
    谨祝在为保卫和巩固人类的和平与幸福的您的高贵的事业中，将有更大的成就！
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相  崔镛健
    一九五二年十月二十三日于平壤
