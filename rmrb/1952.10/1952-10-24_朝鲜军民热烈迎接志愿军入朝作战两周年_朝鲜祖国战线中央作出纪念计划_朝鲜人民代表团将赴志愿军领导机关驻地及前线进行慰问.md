Title: Korean People and Army Warmly Welcome the Second Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers Entering Korea to Fight; Korean Fatherland Front Central Committee Makes Commemoration Plan; Korean People's Delegation to Visit the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Front Lines to Express Condolences

Author:
Date: 1952-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd. The Korean people are warmly welcoming the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight. The Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front made a commemoration plan on the 11th.

The Korean People's Delegation headed by Hong Myong-hui and a cultural troupe of 70 people will bring banners, a large number of gifts, and letters of condolences to visit the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers. The delegation will also represent the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea to award Republic Medals and Orders to the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers who have made outstanding contributions. At the same time, two other delegations will go to the front lines to visit the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting in their posts.

In Pyongyang, photo exhibitions held in five locations were inaugurated on the 21st. These photos introduced to the citizens of Pyongyang the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteers who have fought side by side with the Korean People's Army for the past two years and achieved brilliant victories, as well as the great achievements of the Chinese people's movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. Workers, peasants, youth, and women are widely holding commemorative report meetings and seminars. The streets of Pyongyang are covered with slogans such as "Long live the militant solidarity between the Korean and Chinese peoples," "Long live General Kim Il-sung," and "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong," as well as propaganda posters depicting the Korean and Chinese People's Armies fighting side by side on the front lines.

Similar report meetings, seminars, and photo exhibitions are being held in the northern provinces, cities, and counties of Korea. Many delegations and cultural troupes organized by people from all walks of life in various places will visit the stationed areas and hospitals of the Volunteers around this anniversary.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd. The Korean People's Army units are enthusiastically carrying out various commemorative activities to welcome the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight.

According to the "Democratic Korea" newspaper, various lectures and seminars are being widely held in the Korean People's Army units on topics such as "The Great Achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the Past Two Years," "The Chinese People's Movement to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea is a Brilliant Example of Great Internationalist Assistance and Friendship," and "The Fraternal Solidarity Between the Korean and Chinese Peoples." The wall newspapers of the Korean People's Army units generally publish a large number of articles, materials, and photos praising the heroic achievements of the Chinese comrades and the Chinese people's movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea.

The Korean People's Army units are organizing delegations to congratulate and visit the neighboring friendly units of the Volunteers around October 25th, and to have get-togethers with the Chinese comrades. They are also preparing to visit the wounded and sick of the Volunteers in the hospitals near their stationed areas, and to sweep the tombs and offer flowers to the martyrs of the Volunteers who heroically sacrificed themselves for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.

While welcoming the festival of their close Chinese comrades, the commanders and fighters of the Korean People's Army units unanimously stated that they will further consolidate the unbreakable friendship and solidarity between the Korean and Chinese peoples and strive for greater victories as a gift to commemorate the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea to fight.

### 朝鲜军民热烈迎接志愿军入朝作战两周年  朝鲜祖国战线中央作出纪念计划  朝鲜人民代表团将赴志愿军领导机关驻地及前线进行慰问

1952-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军民热烈迎接志愿军入朝作战两周年
    朝鲜祖国战线中央作出纪念计划
    朝鲜人民代表团将赴志愿军领导机关驻地及前线进行慰问
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】朝鲜人民热烈迎接中国人民志愿军入朝作战两周年。朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会在十一日就作出了纪念的计划。
    以洪命熹为团长的朝鲜人民代表团和由七十人组成的文工团，将携带锦旗和大批慰问品、慰问信前往中国人民志愿军领导机关驻地慰问。代表团并将代表朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会，向功勋卓著的中国人民志愿军指挥员和战斗员授予共和国勋章和奖章。同时，另外还有两个代表团将分赴前线各地慰问在战斗岗位上的中国人民志愿军战士。
    在平壤，在五个地方分别举行的照片展览会，已经在二十一日揭幕。这些照片向平壤市民介绍了中国人民志愿军两年来和朝鲜人民军并肩作战取得辉煌胜利的英雄事迹和中国人民抗美援朝运动的伟大成就。工人、农民、青年、妇女广泛地举行着纪念报告会和座谈会。平壤街头已遍贴着“朝中两国人民的战斗团结万岁”、“金日成将军万岁”、“毛泽东主席万岁”等标语和描写朝中人民军队在前线并肩作战的宣传画。
    同样的报告会、座谈会、照片展览会，正在朝鲜北部各道、市、郡举行。各地各阶层人民组织的许多代表团和文工团，将在这一纪念日前后分别前往志愿军驻地和医院进行慰问。
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】朝鲜人民军各部队为了迎接中国人民志愿军入朝作战两周年，正热烈进行各种纪念活动。
    据“民主朝鲜”报报道：朝鲜人民军各部队中，正广泛地举行着各种讲演会和座谈会，主题有“中国人民志愿军两年来的伟大功勋”、“中国人民的抗美援朝运动是伟大的国际主义援助和友谊的光辉榜样”、“朝中人民的兄弟团结”等。朝鲜人民军各部队的墙报普遍地大量刊载着颂扬中国战友的英雄业绩和中国人民抗美援朝运动的文章、资料和照片。
    朝鲜人民军各部队正纷纷组织代表团，准备在十月二十五日前后到志愿军友邻部队去祝贺与慰问，并和中国战友们联欢。他们还准备到他们驻地附近的医院里去慰问志愿军的伤病员，到为抗美援朝而英勇牺牲的志愿军烈士墓地去扫墓献花。
    在迎接着自己亲密的中国战友的节日的同时，朝鲜人民军各部队的指挥员和战斗员们一致表示要进一步巩固朝中人民牢不可破的友谊团结，并争取更大的胜利，作为纪念中国人民志愿军入朝作战两周年的献礼。
