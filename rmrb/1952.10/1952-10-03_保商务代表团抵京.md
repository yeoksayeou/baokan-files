Title: Bulgarian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st—The Bulgarian People's Republic Trade Delegation, a group of five led by Beberev, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Bulgarian People's Republic Government, arrived in Beijing at 4:00 PM on the 1st. Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Lin Haiyun, Director of the General Office, Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Su Dan, Section Chief of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, were present to greet them.

    Also present at the airport to welcome them was Petkov, Ambassador of the Bulgarian People's Republic to China, and others.

### 保商务代表团抵京

1952-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    保商务代表团抵京
    【新华社一日讯】保加利亚人民共和国商务代表团一行五人，由团长保加利亚人民共和国政府对外贸易部副部长别比列夫率领，于一日午后四时抵京。前往迎接的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，办公厅主任林海云，外交部交际处副处长沈平及苏联东欧司科长苏丹等。
    前往机场欢迎的还有保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使彼得科夫等。
