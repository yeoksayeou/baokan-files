Title: Some Delegates to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Depart Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 13th:
After the victorious conclusion of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, some of the delegates left Beijing for Europe on the morning of the 13th. They were Canadian delegates John Boyd and attending delegate Gerald Filiol, New Zealand delegate Willis Airey, World Peace Council Vice-President D'Astier de la Vigerie and Secretary Montagu, who were invited to participate in the Peace Conference, and Algerian attending delegates Gouchi Mohammed and Bentoumi Amak.
Those who went to the airport to see them off included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Secretary-Generals Liu Ningyi and Liu Guanyi, Chinese delegates to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Liao Chengzhi, Li Dequan, Zhu Xuefan, Burhan, Xiao San, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and many others. Also going to the airport to see them off were Malaysian delegate Zeng Xuehong and International delegate of the Teachers' Union, Sal Jim Keble. Prior to this, those who had left Beijing successively included: British attending delegate John Burns, Mexican delegate Luis Rivera Terrazas, special guest Professor Brandweiner of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Indian delegate Gopalan and Secretary Hardev Singh, special guest Mario Fabio from Brazil, and Pakistani delegates Mahmud Ali and Mian Iftikharuddin, and others.

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议部分代表离京

1952-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议部分代表离京
    【新华社十三日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的一部分代表在和平会议胜利闭幕后，于十三日上午离开北京赴欧洲。他们是加拿大代表约翰·波依德和列席代表哲拉德·费利翁，新西兰代表威利斯·艾雷，应邀参加和平会议的世界和平理事会副主席达波赛、书记蒙塔古，阿尔及利亚列席代表顾奇·摩罕默德和本都米·阿玛克。
    前往机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，秘书长刘宁一、刘贯一，中国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表廖承志、李德全、朱学范、鲍尔汉、萧三及中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等多人。前往机场欢送的还有出席和平会议的马来亚代表曾雪虹和教育工作者工会国际代表萨尔·吉姆·凯布列尔。在此之前，先后离开北京的还有：英国列席代表约翰·伯恩斯，墨西哥代表路易斯·里维拉·泰拉萨斯，特邀代表、国际民主法律工作者协会的布兰德魏纳教授，印度代表高普兰及秘书哈狄夫·辛格，特邀代表、巴西的马里奥·费比奥，巴基斯坦代表马姆德·阿里和米安·伊夫提卡鲁丁等人。
