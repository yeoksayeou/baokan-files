Title: Peace Representatives from Various Countries Return Home or Visit Different Places
Author:
Date: 1952-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Korean delegation attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, led by head of delegation Han Xueye, returned to Pyongyang on the 18th and was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Pyongyang. The delegation will soon report the achievements of the Peace Conference to the entire Korean people.

Ninety-seven representatives from Australia, Burma, India, Indonesia, New Zealand, and Pakistan, who attended the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, left Beijing on the 20th to visit the Northeast and other parts of China, and will then return home via Guangzhou. Those who came to the station to see them off included Chinese representatives attending the Peace Conference, Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, Li Siguang, Liu Ningyi, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, Xiraojiacuo, Chen Hansheng, Jin Zhonghua, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, and approximately one thousand workers, citizens, students, and Young Pioneers from Beijing. The crowd shouted "Long live peace" and bid farewell to the representatives with enthusiasm. The platform was filled with a joyful and friendly atmosphere.

On the same day, Canadian representatives Nelson, Nish, Ecuadorian representatives Juan Morales Eloy, Augusto Sesa Soldos, Mexican representatives Javier Berak, Ismael Cosio Viriges, Lebanese and Syrian representative Ahmed Kasar, and observers from the World Federation of Trade Unions Grassi, the International Democratic Women's Federation Santi, and the International Union of Students Echevarria, a group of ten people, left Beijing by plane for Europe.

Indian representatives Abdul Alem, Mrs. Monjushri Chattopadhyaya, Brij Raj Kishore, Wu Badeya, Mrs. Banjaji Acharya, Jokindra Kumar Banerjee, Magolal Barik, and Dr. Arvind Mehta, a group of eight people, arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 17th, and left Guangzhou by train that evening via Shenzhen and Hong Kong to return home.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference have been visiting various parts of our country in recent days. The head of the Soviet delegation Anisimov, representatives Kozhevnikov, Tursun-Zade, Hedejev, Fomina, Yefimov, and the famous Turkish poet Hikmet, along with staff, a total of seventeen people, arrived in Wuhan by train on the afternoon of the 17th. The representatives toured Wuhan city accompanied by welcomers and visited the Central South District Land Reform Exhibition. That evening, the representatives took a train to Guangzhou and arrived in Guangzhou on the morning of the 19th for a visit.

The head of the New Zealand delegation, Alan Courtney Montash, and four members of the delegation, visited Tianjin on the 17th.

Special invitees to the Peace Conference, Brazil's Abe Chermont and Fuad Saad, arrived in Hangzhou for a visit on the 17th. The head of the Pakistani delegation, Peer Shahab Manki Sharif, and eight members of the delegation, and Indian representative Govind Sahai and four others, arrived in Hangzhou for a visit on the 19th.

Sixty-five representatives from sixteen countries, including Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Cyprus, Ecuador, Guatemala, Honduras, Iran, Iraq, Israel, Lebanon and Syria, Mexico, Nicaragua, Panama, and Peru, left Beijing on the 20th to visit Shanghai and other places.

### 各国和平代表分别返国或赴各地参观

1952-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    各国和平代表分别返国或赴各地参观
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的朝鲜代表团在韩雪野团长率领下，已在十八日返抵平壤，受到平壤各界人士的热烈欢迎。代表团即将向全朝鲜人民报告和平会议的成就。
    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的澳大利亚、缅甸、印度、印度尼西亚、新西兰和巴基斯坦六个国家的一部分代表九十七人，二十日离北京前往东北及我国其他各地参观，然后即取道广州回国。到车站欢送的有出席和平会议的中国代表陈叔通、张奚若、李四光、刘宁一、李德全、廖承志、喜饶嘉措、陈翰笙、金仲华，中印友好协会会长丁西林，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，以及北京市的工人、市民、学生和少年儿童队员约一千人。欢送的人群高呼着“和平万岁”，并热情地和代表们话别。月台上洋溢着欢腾友爱的气氛。
    同日，出席和平会议的加拿大代表聂尔森、尼什、厄瓜多尔代表胡安·摩拉雷斯·埃洛伊、阿古斯多·塞沙·索尔多斯、墨西哥代表查维尔·贝拉克、伊斯玛尔·科西奥·维里盖斯、黎巴嫩和叙利亚代表阿姆德·喀萨尔和列席会议的世界工联代表葛拉西、国际民主妇联代表桑蒂、国际学联代表艾切伐里亚一行十人，乘飞机离北京赴欧洲。
    出席和平会议的印度代表阿卜杜勒·阿勒姆、蒙朱斯利·查托巴达雅夫人、布立志·拉志·吉索尔、吴巴代亚、班佳紫·阿查里亚夫人、卓金德拉·库玛·班那吉、马高拉尔·巴立克、阿尔文德·梅达博士一行八人，于十七日下午由上海到达广州，并于当晚乘车离广州经深圳、香港返国。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表，连日分别赴我国各地参观访问。苏联代表团团长阿尼西莫夫、代表科热夫尼科夫、图尔逊—萨德、贺捷耶夫、佛米纳、叶菲莫夫及土耳其著名诗人希克梅特和工作人员共十七人，于十七日下午乘火车抵达武汉。代表们曾在欢迎者陪同下游览武汉市区，并参观了中南区土地改革展览会。当晚，代表们即乘车赴广州，并于十九日晨到达广州参观访问。
    新西兰代表团团长艾伦·柯特尼·蒙太施和团员等一行四人，十七日到天津参观。
    参加和平会议的特邀列席代表、巴西的阿培·彻蒙特和符艾德·萨德二人，于十七日到达杭州访问。巴基斯坦代表团团长毕尔·沙哈布·曼基·沙里夫及团员八人、印度代表高文德·萨海等四人在十九日到达杭州访问。
    智利、哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、塞浦路斯、厄瓜多尔、危地马拉、洪都拉斯、伊朗、伊拉克、以色列、黎巴嫩和叙利亚、墨西哥、尼加拉瓜、巴拿马、秘鲁十六个国家的部分代表六十五人，在二十日离京去上海等地参观访问。
