Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-24

    Our Great Motherland
    After the contract was signed between our Central People's Government and the Ceylonese government for the sale of 80,000 tons of rice, the first batch of rice shipped from our country to Ceylon departed from Shanghai on October 20th. This is the scene of the loading before departure.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我中央人民政府售给锡兰政府八万吨大米的合同签订后，我国运往锡兰的第一批大米已于十月二十日从上海起运。这是起运前装船的情形。
