    Our Great Motherland
    The capital city on National Day night, colorful fireworks and searchlights illuminate the night sky. Overlooking the city that never sleeps, intertwined with sparks and songs, from the gate tower of Tiananmen Square, fills people with boundless confidence and strength in building the motherland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国庆节的首都之夜，彩色的焰火和探照灯照亮了夜空。从天安门的门楼上俯瞰被火花和歌声交织的不夜之城，给人们以建设祖国的无比信心和力量。
