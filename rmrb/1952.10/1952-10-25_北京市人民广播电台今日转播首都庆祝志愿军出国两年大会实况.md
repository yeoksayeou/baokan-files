Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Today's Capital Celebration of the Second Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Departure to Korea

Author:
Date: 1952-10-25
Page: 4

    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Today's Capital Celebration of the Second Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Departure to Korea
    [This newspaper's dispatch] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast live the celebration meeting of all circles in the capital commemorating the second anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's departure to Korea at 1:45 pm today. Frequencies are 850 kilocycles, 1080 kilocycles, 1320 kilocycles, and 750 kilocycles.

### 北京市人民广播电台今日转播首都庆祝志愿军出国两年大会实况

1952-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    北京市人民广播电台今日转播首都庆祝志愿军出国两年大会实况
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台今日下午一时四十五分转播首都各界庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战两周年大会实况。周率为八五○千周、一○八○千周、一三二○千周、七五○千周。
