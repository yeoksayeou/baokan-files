Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Congratulations on "Mongolia-China Friendship Week"; Premier Tsedenbal Replies with Thanks
Author:
Date: 1952-10-02
Page: 1

    Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Congratulations on "Mongolia-China Friendship Week"
    Premier Tsedenbal Replies with Thanks

    [Report from this newspaper] Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government sent a telegram on September 29th to Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, congratulating the government of the Mongolian People's Republic on holding "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" from October 1st to 10th. Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on October 1st, expressing his gratitude.

    Premier Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram

    To Comrade Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic: On the occasion of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" being held in the Mongolian People's Republic, I extend warm, fraternal congratulations to you and to the government and people of the Mongolian People's Republic, and wish Mongolia and China greater achievements in economic cooperation, cultural exchange, and the common cause of defending peace and democracy. Long live the unbreakable friendship between Mongolia and China!

    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
    September 29, 1952, in Beijing

    Premier Tsedenbal's Reply Telegram

    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China: Comrade Premier, I sincerely thank you for the warm and fraternal congratulations and good wishes expressed on the opening of "Mongolia-China Friendship Week." The Mongolian people are learning about the outstanding achievements of the great brotherly Chinese people with great interest and joy. Under the leadership of their great leader, Comrade Mao Zedong, a sincere friend of the Mongolian people, the Chinese people have made brilliant achievements in the construction of great People's China, with each passing day bringing something new. The close cooperation between our two peoples in the economic and cultural fields has further consolidated the unbreakable, brotherly friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples, and is conducive to defending and consolidating the cause of peace. May the unbreakable, brotherly friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples be increasingly consolidated! Long live the powerful camp of peace, democracy, and socialism, headed by our common, sincere friend, the great Soviet Union!

    Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic Tsedenbal
    October 1, 1952

### 周恩来总理电贺“蒙中友好旬”泽登巴尔总理复电表示谢意

1952-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理电贺“蒙中友好旬”
    泽登巴尔总理复电表示谢意
    【本报讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理于九月二十九日致电蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，对蒙古人民共和国政府在十月一日至十日举办“蒙中友好旬”表示祝贺。蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔于十月一日致电周恩来总理表示谢意。
    周恩来总理的贺电
    蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔同志：值此“蒙中友好旬”在蒙古人民共和国举行之际，我谨向您及蒙古人民共和国政府和人民致以热烈的兄弟般的庆贺，并祝蒙中两国在经济合作、文化交流以及保卫和平民主的共同事业上获得更大的成就。蒙中牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    中华人民共和国政务院总理  周恩来
    一九五二年九月二十九日于北京
    泽登巴尔总理的复电
    中华人民共和国政务院总理周恩来同志：总理同志，我衷心地感谢你在“蒙中友好旬”开幕时所表示的热烈的和兄弟般的祝贺与良好的愿望。蒙古人民以极高的兴趣和愉快的心情，来认识伟大兄弟的中国人民的卓越成就。中国人民在自己伟大的领袖，蒙古人民真诚的朋友毛泽东同志的领导下，在伟大的人民中国的建设事业中取得了日新月异的辉煌成就。我们两国人民在经济及文化上的紧密合作，进一步巩固了蒙中两国人民间牢不可破的兄弟般的友谊，并有利于保卫与巩固和平的事业。祝蒙中两国人民间牢不可破的兄弟般的友谊日益巩固起来！以我们共同的真挚的良友，伟大的苏联为首的强大的和平、民主与社会主义阵营万岁！
    蒙古人民共和国总理  泽登巴尔
    一九五二年十月一日
