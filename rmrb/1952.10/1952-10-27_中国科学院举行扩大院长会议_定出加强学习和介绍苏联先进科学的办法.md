Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Holds Expanded Meeting of Presidents, Sets Out Methods to Strengthen Learning and Introducing Advanced Soviet Science
Author:
Date: 1952-10-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 25th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences held an expanded meeting of presidents in Beijing on the 24th to discuss the issue of strengthening learning and introducing advanced Soviet science. Those attending the meeting included Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Vice Presidents Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, the heads of the Chinese Academy of Sciences' Planning Bureau, Compilation Bureau, and General Office, as well as the heads of the thirteen research units of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing, totaling twenty-six people. The meeting was presided over by President Guo Moruo. Guo Moruo first expressed his welcome and gratitude for the resolution passed by the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on October 3rd regarding creative cooperation with Chinese scientists. Guo Moruo pointed out that the advanced experience of the Soviet Union has played a huge guiding role in the various tasks of the new China over the past three years. He said: "Science occupies a very important position in national construction work. In the upcoming large-scale economic construction, strengthening cooperation with the Soviet scientific community and striving to learn from and absorb the advanced experience of the Soviet Union will have even greater significance." Dr. Qian Chongshu, Director of the Institute of Plant Taxonomy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, said: "I very much need to learn advanced Soviet science, so even though I am seventy years old, I am still actively learning Russian. I sincerely welcome the resolution passed by the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences." Fan Wenlan, Director of the Institute of Modern History and historian, said: "The Soviet Union is willing to introduce their advanced science to us without reservation, which embodies the great internationalist spirit of Soviet scientists." Tao Menghe, Vice President, Dr. Peng Huanwu, Deputy Director of the Institute of Modern Physics, Dr. Yuan Hanqing, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and others also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their welcome to the creative cooperation between Soviet and Chinese scientists.
    The meeting decided on four measures to strengthen learning and introducing advanced Soviet science: (1) The research units affiliated to the Chinese Academy of Sciences should strengthen the study of Soviet scientific achievements, hold seminars on Soviet science, and be responsible for proposing specific suggestions to relevant state administrative departments for adopting Soviet scientific achievements in practical work. (2) The Chinese Academy of Sciences will immediately begin publishing the "Soviet Science Series," translating scientific works that have won Stalin Prizes and other excellent Soviet scientific works. The Chinese Academy of Sciences' publication "Science Bulletin" will systematically introduce the latest achievements in Soviet science. (3) In collaboration with the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and various social science societies, exhibitions and lectures introducing Soviet science and scientists will be held in central cities, starting in November of this year – during the Sino-Soviet Friendship Month. (4) A delegation will be organized to visit the Soviet Academy of Sciences to learn from the advanced experience of Soviet scientific work and to discuss specific ways to further strengthen cooperation in scientific work between China and the Soviet Union.

### 中国科学院举行扩大院长会议  定出加强学习和介绍苏联先进科学的办法

1952-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院举行扩大院长会议
    定出加强学习和介绍苏联先进科学的办法
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国科学院二十四日在北京举行扩大院长会议，讨论加强学习和介绍苏联先进科学问题。出席会议的有中国科学院院长郭沫若，副院长陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，中国科学院计划局、编译局、办公厅的负责人以及中国科学院在北京的十三个研究单位的负责人等共二十六人。会议由郭沫若院长主持。郭沫若首先对十月三日苏联科学院主席团会议通过关于和中国科学家实行创造性的合作的决议表示欢迎和感谢。郭沫若指出苏联的先进经验对新中国三年来的各项工作都起了极大的指导作用。他说：“科学在国家建设工作中占很重要的地位，在即将到来的大规模经济建设中，加强和苏联科学界的合作、努力学习和吸取苏联的先进经验将有更重要的意义。”中国科学院植物分类研究所所长钱崇澍博士说：“我十分需要学习苏联的先进科学，所以我虽然已经七十岁，还在积极学习俄文。我衷心欢迎苏联科学院主席团会议通过的决议。”近代史研究所所长、历史学家范文澜说：“苏联愿意无保留地将他们的先进科学介绍给我们，这体现了苏联科学家的伟大的国际主义精神。”在会上发言的还有陶孟和副院长，近代物理研究所副所长彭桓武博士，中华全国科学技术普及协会副秘书长袁翰青博士等。他们一致表示欢迎苏联科学家和中国科学家实行创造性的合作。
    会议决定了加强学习和介绍苏联先进科学的四项办法：（一）中国科学院所属各研究单位应加强研究苏联科学成果，举行关于苏联科学的讨论会，并负责向有关的国家行政部门提出在实际工作中采用苏联科学成果的具体建议。（二）中国科学院即行着手出版“苏联科学丛书”，翻译获得斯大林奖金的科学著作和其他优秀的苏联科学著作。中国科学院刊物“科学通报”有系统地介绍苏联科学的最新成就。（三）协同中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会及社会科学各学会，在中心城市举办介绍苏联科学和科学家的展览会和演讲会，并在本年十一月份——中苏友好月中开始。（四）组织代表团访问苏联科学院，学习苏联科学工作的先进经验，并商讨进一步加强中苏两国科学工作合作的具体办法。
