Title: Some Delegates of Thai Delegation to Peace Conference Arrive in Beijing; One Australian Observer Also Arrives Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: A portion of the Thai delegation to the Peace Conference for Asian and Pacific Regions arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 5th. An Australian observer also arrived in Beijing on the same plane. Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Tu Chang-wang, National Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

Also present to welcome them were H.E. Prasong Sunthorn, head of the already arrived Thai delegation to the Peace Conference for Asian and Pacific Regions, Deputy Head H.E. Charoon Tullalok, and member Ms. Lamchoon Sunthorn.

List of Thai Delegates Arriving in Beijing Today:
	Surin Tongsawanich, Committee Member of the Thai Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Committee Member of the Thai Press Association, and Editor of "Thai Voice Newspaper";
	Ravi Pengchai, Reporter for "Bonchun Newspaper";
	Athorn Pholthesomburn, Committee Member of the Thai Peace Committee, Representative of Thai Youth.

Australian Observer Arriving in Beijing Today:
	Douglas Bruce Hart, Carpenter.

### 泰国出席和平会议部分代表抵达北京  澳大利亚列席代表一人亦于昨日同时到达

1952-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国出席和平会议部分代表抵达北京
    澳大利亚列席代表一人亦于昨日同时到达
    【新华社五日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的泰国代表团一部分代表于五日下午乘飞机到达北京。同机抵京的还有澳大利亚列席代表一人。前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会全国委员涂长望等人。
