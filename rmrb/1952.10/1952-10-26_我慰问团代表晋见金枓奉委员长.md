Title: My慰问团 Representatives Meet with Chairman Kim Tu-bong
Author:
Date: 1952-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, October 25th: A delegation of nineteen representatives from the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, led by General Director Liu Jingfan, paid a courtesy call to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, at 10:30 a.m. on October 23rd.

Also present were Hong Ki-ju, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Kang Yang-uk, Secretary-General, and Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the Republic Government.

General Director Liu Jingfan, on behalf of the Chinese people, extended the highest respect and cordial greetings to Chairman Kim Tu-bong and the heroic Korean people who have made brilliant contributions to the cause of defending world peace. Liu Jingfan said: Under the leadership of General Kim Il-sung and yourself, the heroic Korean people and army have become stronger in battle and will continue to inflict more severe blows on the enemy. We firmly believe that with the close unity of the Chinese and Korean people and the righteous support of the Soviet Union and the people of the world, all the conspiracies of the American aggressors will be shattered, and the Korean people will win the final victory.

Following this, the delegation presented Chairman Kim Tu-bong with a flag and gifts. The purple-red velvet banner with gold edges embroidered in gold thread the words: "The great power of the close unity of the Chinese and Korean people will surely defeat the American aggressors." The gifts included a set of Xiang embroidery portraits of General Kim Il-sung, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Marshal Stalin, two 16mm projectors self-made by the August 1st Film Studio of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and a set of photos of the Chinese People's Volunteers at the forefront of the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea.

Finally, Chairman Kim Tu-bong delivered a response, thanking the delegation for their kind visit and asking the delegation to convey the sincere gratitude of the Korean people to the people of our country and Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 我慰问团代表晋见金枓奉委员长

1952-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我慰问团代表晋见金枓奉委员长
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十五日电】中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团以刘景范总团长为首的十九位代表于十月二十三日上午十时半晋见朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会金枓奉委员长。
    在座的还有最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长洪箕畴、书记长康良煜、共和国政府文化宣传相许贞琡。
    慰问团刘景范总团长代表中国人民向金枓奉委员长和在保卫世界和平的事业中作了辉煌贡献的英勇的朝鲜人民致以崇高的敬礼和亲切的慰问。刘景范说：英雄的朝鲜军民在金日成将军和您的领导下，已经越战越强，并将再接再厉地给敌人以更严重的打击。我们坚信在中朝两国人民的紧密团结和苏联以及全世界人民的正义支持下，美国侵略者的一切阴谋必将被粉碎，朝鲜人民必将赢得最后的胜利。
    接着慰问团代表向金枓奉委员长献旗献礼，在湘绣的紫红金边的丝绒锦旗上，用金色丝线绣着“中朝人民亲密团结的伟大力量，一定能够打败美国侵略者”的字样。礼品中包括湘绣金日成将军、毛泽东主席、斯大林大元帅像一套，中国人民解放军八一电影机制造厂自制的十六糎放映机二部，在抗美援朝最前线的中国人民志愿军照片一套。
    最后金枓奉委员长致答词，他感谢慰问团慰问的盛意，并请慰问团转达朝鲜人民对我国人民及毛泽东主席的衷心感谢。
