Title: Dutch Soldiers Refuse to Fight Against the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1952-10-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 17th: The Dutch bourgeois newspapers, in commemorating the second anniversary of the Dutch battalion's participation in the American aggressors' war against the Korean people, admit that the Dutch are unwilling to fight in Korea. According to the "Maasbode" [Nieuwe Rotterdamsche Courant], a large number of Dutch soldiers recently refused to fight against the peace-loving Korean people and have already departed from Korea to return to the Netherlands.

### 荷兰士兵拒绝对朝鲜人民作战

1952-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    荷兰士兵拒绝对朝鲜人民作战
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社海牙十七日讯：荷兰资产阶级报纸在纪念荷兰营参加美国侵略者进攻朝鲜人民的战争二周年之际，承认荷兰人不愿在朝鲜作战。据“卖士河消息报”报道：最近有一大批荷兰士兵拒绝再对爱好和平的朝鲜人民作战，并已从朝鲜启程返回荷兰。
