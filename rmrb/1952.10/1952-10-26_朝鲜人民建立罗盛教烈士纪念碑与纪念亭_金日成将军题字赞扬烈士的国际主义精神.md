Title: Korean People Build the Luo Shengjiao Martyr Monument and Memorial Pavilion; General Kim Il-sung Inscribes Words Praising the Martyr's Internationalist Spirit
Author:
Date: 1952-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, October 23rd: The Korean people, in memory of the great internationalist fighter Luo Shengjiao, have built a solemn and magnificent monument and memorial pavilion on "Luo Shengjiao Mountain," and solemnly held the inauguration ceremony on October 3rd. More than 700 people attended the ceremony, including Yao Junhao, Vice Chairman of the Korean Workers' Party Ping An South Dao Committee, Song Changren, Chairman of the Ping An South Dao People's Committee of Korea, representatives of the Korean People's Army, representatives of the local people, Cui ?, the boy rescued by the martyr, representatives of the leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and comrades-in-arms of Luo Shengjiao.

    On the monument, which is one zhang and five chi high, are the golden characters inscribed by General Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people: "The great internationalist spirit of Martyr Luo Shengjiao will coexist with the Korean people!" There is a eulogy from the leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army: "The glorious internationalist fighter Martyr Luo Shengjiao will be immortal!" The monument is surrounded by flowers, and many elegiac couplets hang in the memorial pavilion, which is a classical-style building.

    After the ceremony began, representatives of the Korean Workers' Party, the government, the People's Army, the local people, and the Korean boy Cui ? spoke one after another. They all said that the great internationalist fighter Luo Shengjiao will always live in the hearts of the Korean people, and his great internationalist spirit will always inspire the Korean people to forge ahead bravely. The representative of the leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army said: "Martyr Luo Shengjiao's heroic act of sacrificing himself to save others embodies the internationalist spirit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's love for the Korean people. All commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army will unite more closely with the Korean people and fight the enemy more courageously." Luo Shengjiao's former comrades-in-arms reported on their deeds of learning the spirit of internationalism from Martyr Luo Shengjiao. Finally, all participants in the ceremony swore before the monument: In the future, we must unite more closely, learn from Luo Shengjiao's patriotism and internationalism, transform grief and anger into strength, and resolutely use practical actions of heroic fighting, active work, and diligent production to realize Martyr Luo Shengjiao's great will to "avenge the Korean people."

### 朝鲜人民建立罗盛教烈士纪念碑与纪念亭  金日成将军题字赞扬烈士的国际主义精神

1952-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民建立罗盛教烈士纪念碑与纪念亭
    金日成将军题字赞扬烈士的国际主义精神
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十三日电】朝鲜人民为纪念伟大的国际主义战士罗盛教烈士，特在“罗盛教山”上建筑了庄严雄伟的纪念碑与纪念亭，并在十月三日隆重举行落成典礼。参加典礼的有朝鲜劳动党平安南道委员会副委员长姚俊镐、朝鲜平安南道人民委员会委员长宋昌稔，朝鲜人民军代表、当地人民代表、被烈士营救出来的少年崔?及中国人民志愿军领导机关代表和罗盛教烈士生前战友等七百多人。
    在一丈五尺高的纪念碑上，有朝鲜人民的领袖金日成将军亲笔所题的金色大字：“罗盛教烈士伟大的国际主义精神与朝鲜人民共存！”有中国人民志愿军领导机关的挽词：“光辉的国际主义战士罗盛教烈士永垂不朽！”在纪念碑周围堆满了鲜花，在一座古典式建筑的纪念亭里挂着许多挽联。
    典礼开始后，朝鲜劳动党、政府、人民军、当地人民的代表和朝鲜少年崔?都相继讲话，他们都说：伟大的国际主义战士罗盛教烈士将永远活在朝鲜人民的心里，他的伟大的国际主义精神将永远鼓舞着朝鲜人民奋勇前进。中国人民志愿军领导机关代表说：“罗盛教烈士舍己救人的英勇行为体现了中国人民志愿军热爱朝鲜人民的国际主义精神。中国人民志愿军全体指挥员战斗员将更密切地与朝鲜人民团结一致，更加奋勇地打击敌人。”罗盛教生前的战友们报告了他们向罗盛教烈士学习国际主义精神的事迹。最后，全体参加典礼者在纪念碑前宣誓：今后要更加亲密地团结一致，学习罗盛教爱国主义与国际主义精神，化悲愤为力量，决以英勇战斗、积极工作和努力生产的实际行动来实现罗盛教烈士
    “为朝鲜人民报仇”的伟大遗志。
