Title: Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Held a Reception in Beijing; The Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the People’s Government Held a Celebration Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th of this month: To celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, Mr. Koenig, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, held a reception in Beijing at 7:00 p.m. on the 7th. Among those invited to attend the reception were: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Li Jishen, Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, leaders of various democratic parties, heads of various people's organizations, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han, totaling about 300 people.

Also invited to attend the reception were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials of various countries to China.

Also invited to attend the reception were the head of the Commercial Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Bulgaria, Mr. Bebelev, and members of the delegation.

Also invited to attend the reception were representatives of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, and representatives of industrial labor models.

Deputy heads of the German Democratic Republic Government Commercial Delegation, Mr. Barkov and Mr. Norbert, and members of the delegation also attended the reception.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

[Our Newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a celebration meeting at the "Capital Cinema" in Beijing at 9:00 a.m. on the 7th to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Mr. Koenig, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic, and members of the mission, Mr. Barkov and Mr. Norbert, deputy heads of the German Democratic Republic Government Commercial Delegation, and members of the delegation attended the meeting.

Also participating in the celebration meeting were representatives from government agencies and various circles in Beijing, totaling more than 1,200 people.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing first delivered a speech at the meeting to congratulate the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Mr. Koenig then gave a speech, expressing his gratitude for the celebration meeting held to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. Then there was a performance of literary and artistic programs. Finally, the famous German film "Reunion of Gods," dubbed in Chinese, which exposes the criminal activities of the German monopoly capital group, was shown.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年  德外交使团团长在京举行招待会  我对外文化联络事务局昨举行庆祝大会

1952-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年
    德外交使团团长在京举行招待会
    我对外文化联络事务局昨举行庆祝大会
    【新华社七日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团团长柯尼希，为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年，特于七日下午七时在北京举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部副部长伍修权，中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派领袖、各人民团体负责人和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等约三百人。
    应邀出席招待会的还有各国驻我国外交使节和外交官员。
    应邀出席招待会的还有保加利亚人民共和国商务代表团团长、保加利亚对外贸易部副部长别比列夫及代表团团员。
    应邀出席招待会的还有中国人民解放军及中国人民志愿军的战斗英雄代表及工业劳动模范代表。
    德意志民主共和国政府商务代表团副团长巴尔柯夫和诺贝尔特以及代表团团员也出席了招待会。
    招待会自始至终在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年，于七日上午九时在北京“首都电影院”举行庆祝大会。
    德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希及使团人员，德意志民主共和国政府商务代表团副团长巴尔柯夫、诺贝尔特以及代表团团员均到会。
    参加庆祝大会的还有我政府机关及北京市各界代表共一千二百余人。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在会上首先致词祝贺德意志民主共和国成立三周年。
    柯尼希继起致词，他对于为了庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年而举行的庆祝大会，表示谢意。接着举行文艺节目表演。最后并放映了揭露德国垄断资本集团罪恶活动的华语对白的德国著名影片“群神会”。
