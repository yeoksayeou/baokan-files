Title: Indian and Pakistani Delegations Issue Joint Statement Calling for Peaceful Resolution of Outstanding Issues Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1952-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Indian and Pakistani delegations attending the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference issued the following joint statement at the morning session of the Peace Conference on the 9th:

　　The delegations of India and Pakistan attending the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, with a common desire to safeguard world peace, have consulted on some common issues in a spirit of goodwill and friendship.

　　We declare: We firmly believe that all outstanding issues between India and Pakistan, without exception, can and must be resolved peacefully.

　　We believe that the constant friction between the two countries is a serious crisis for peace in Asia and the world, and gives imperialism the opportunity to interfere in our internal affairs, which is detrimental to both of our countries. The result of the constant tension between the two countries is huge military expenditures accounting for more than half of the national budget, which the people cannot afford. If peace can be ensured, these funds can be used to develop India and Pakistan and improve the living standards of the people.

　　Therefore, we declare that we will continue to strive to promote goodwill and friendship between the two countries and nations by all possible means—including multifaceted cultural exchanges, development of trade and economic cooperation.

　　The so-called efforts of the committees and representatives sent by the United Nations to resolve the Kashmir issue have deepened the divisions between our two countries, and our two delegations are deeply concerned about their approach. Regarding the Kashmir dispute, the aim of Britain and the United States is clearly to aggravate the conflict between India and Pakistan in order to make these two countries submit to them.

　　This situation endangers the sovereignty of the two countries and makes them victims of imperialism's acquisition of military bases and cannon fodder.

　　We believe that only the entire people of Jammu and Kashmir have the right to decide their own destiny and future, and they should be guaranteed the opportunity to freely exercise this right. We call on the people of India and Pakistan to take immediate practical measures to ensure that all the people of Jammu and Kashmir can freely decide their future destiny on an equal and just basis, without hindrance, fear, or prejudice.

　　The delegations of India and Pakistan are deeply convinced that peace-loving people in our two countries, despite differing opinions in other areas, will unanimously recognize this as the best way to resolve the Kashmir issue.

　　Our two delegations recommend that representatives of the peace movements of India and Pakistan hold a joint meeting to exchange views and pave the way for a fair resolution of Kashmir and other outstanding issues.

　　The statement was signed on behalf of the Indian delegation by: Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew (Chairman of the All-India Peace Council), Dr. Gyan Chand (Economist), Ravi Shankar Shivlal Vyas (Social Worker), Ramesh Chandra (Secretary-General of the All-India Peace Council).

　　The statement was signed on behalf of the Pakistani delegation by: Pir Shahab Manki Sharif (Islamic Leader), Sardar Shaukat Hayat (Businessman), Ataur Rahman (Lawyer), Mir Abdul Qayoom (Lawyer).

### 印度和巴基斯坦代表团发表联合声明  呼吁和平解决两国间未决问题

1952-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    印度和巴基斯坦代表团发表联合声明
    呼吁和平解决两国间未决问题
    【新华社九日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度和巴基斯坦两国代表团在和平会议九日上午会议上发表联合声明如下：
    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度与巴基斯坦两国的代表团，为了保障世界和平的一致的愿望，曾在善意和友好的气氛中，会商了一些共同的问题。
    我们声明：我们坚定地相信，印度和巴基斯坦间一切悬而未决的问题，毫无例外地，都能够而且必须用和平方式来解决。
    我们认为，两国间不断的摩擦，对于亚洲和世界和平是一个严重的危机，而使帝国主义有机会来干涉我们的内政，这对于我们两国都是不利的。两国间经常的紧张局势的结果，就是占国家预算半数以上的巨额军事费用，这是人民负担不起的。如果和平能够确保，这些款项就可以用来开发印度和巴基斯坦，和提高人民的生活水准。
    因此我们声明，我们要不断地努力，用一切可能的方法——包括多方面的文化交流、发展贸易和经济合作——来促进两个国家与民族间的善意与友好。
    联合国派来的委员会和代表，在解决克什米尔问题方面的所谓努力，加深了我们两国间的隔阂，我们这两个代表团对于他们这种作法，感到切身的忧虑。对于克什米尔争端，英、美的目的很明显的是要使印度和巴基斯坦间的冲突更加严重，以便使这两个国家向它们屈服。
    这种情况危害两国的主权，同时使两国变成帝国主义取得军事基地和炮灰的牺牲品。
    我们认为，只有查谟和克什米尔邦的全体人民，才有权决定他们自己的命运和前途，而且应该保证他们有自由运用这种权利的机会。我们呼吁印度和巴基斯坦两国人民，立刻采取实际措施，保证查谟和克什米尔邦的全体人民在平等公正的基础上，不受妨碍，无所恐惧，没有偏袒的情况下，自由决定他们未来的命运。
    印度和巴基斯坦两国的代表团深信，我们两国爱好和平的人士，尽管在其他方面有不同的意见，都会一致承认这是解决克什米尔问题的最好的办法。
    我们这两个代表团建议，印巴两国和平运动的代表们举行联席会议，以便交换意见，铺平道路，使克什米尔和其他悬而未决的问题，获得公正的解决。
    代表印度代表团在声明上签字的是：赛福丁·克其鲁博士（全印和平理事会主席）、杰安·昌德博士（经济学家）、拉维·山加尔·史佛兰姆·维亚斯（社会工作者）、罗米西·钱德拉（全印和平理事会秘书长）。
    代表巴基斯坦代表团签字的是：毕尔·沙哈布·曼基·沙里夫（回教领袖）、萨达尔·修格特·哈雅特（商业家）、阿陶尔·拉赫曼（律师）、米尔·阿勃都尔·加尧姆（律师）。
