Title: Beijing "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Office Established
Author:
Date: 1952-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Beijing "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Office has been established to specifically organize and lead the various activities of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" in Beijing. Wu Han, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, writer Lao She, Liao Mosha, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, will serve as Director and Deputy Directors of the office, respectively.
    In order to meet the needs of all parties for publicity and education, the Beijing "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Office is compiling a large number of materials related to "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," including texts, pictures, comic strips, slides, and cultural entertainment materials, and will strive to distribute them to various units starting on October 25. At the same time, the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Office plans to organize several large-scale report meetings before November 7, inviting Ai Siqi and others to give special reports on "The October Revolution and China" to train publicity backbones in order to carry out extensive publicity and education work during the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month."

### 北京“中苏友好月”办公室成立

1952-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    北京“中苏友好月”办公室成立
    【新华社讯】北京市成立“中苏友好月”办公室，具体组织和领导北京市“中苏友好月”的各项活动。北京市中苏友好协会副会长、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、作家老舍、中共北京市委宣传部副部长廖沫沙、北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫等，分别担任办公室主任、副主任。
    北京“中苏友好月”办公室为了满足各方宣传教育的需要，正大量编选有关“中苏友好月”的文字、图片、连环画、幻灯、文化娱乐的材料，争取在十月二十五日开始分发给各个单位。同时，“中苏友好月”办公室准备在十一月七日前组织几次大规模的报告会，邀请艾思奇等作关于“十月革命与中国”等专题报告，培养宣传骨干，以便在“中苏友好月”里展开广泛的宣传教育工作。
