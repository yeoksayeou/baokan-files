Title: General Nam Il Protests US Side for Again Wounding Our Captured Personnel

Author:

Date: 1952-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 15th - On the afternoon of October 11th, the US side wounded three more of our captured personnel on Geoje Island. General Nam Il, chief representative of our delegation to the Korean Armistice Talks, lodged a protest with the other side on the 15th, pointing out that the US side bears full responsibility for the continuous war crimes of abusing and killing prisoners of war. On the 15th, the chief liaison officer of the other side's delegation admitted to the incident of the US side shelling the Panmunjom conference area on October 8th, expressed regret, and stated that they would strive to prevent similar incidents from happening again.

### 南日将军抗议美方又打伤我方被俘人员

1952-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    南日将军抗议美方又打伤我方被俘人员
    【新华社开城十五日电】十月十一日下午，美方在巨济岛上又打伤我方被俘人员三名。朝鲜停战谈判我方代表团首席代表南日将军在十五日向对方提出抗议，指出美方对于连续发生的虐杀战俘的战争罪行负有完全责任。十五日对方代表团首席联络官承认十月八日美方炮击板门店会场区的事件，并表示遗憾，声称将努力使类似事件不再发生。
