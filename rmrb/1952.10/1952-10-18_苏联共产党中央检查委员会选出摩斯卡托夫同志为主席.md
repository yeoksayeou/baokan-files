Title: Central Auditing Commission of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Elects Comrade Moskatov as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 17th: TASS Moscow, October 17th—The Central Auditing Commission of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a meeting on October 16, 1952.

    The Central Auditing Commission elected P. G. Moskatov as its chairman.

### 苏联共产党中央检查委员会选出摩斯卡托夫同志为主席

1952-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央检查委员会选出摩斯卡托夫同志为主席
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：苏联共产党中央检查委员会于一九五二年十月十六日举行会议。
    中央检查委员会选出波·格·摩斯卡托夫为其主席。
