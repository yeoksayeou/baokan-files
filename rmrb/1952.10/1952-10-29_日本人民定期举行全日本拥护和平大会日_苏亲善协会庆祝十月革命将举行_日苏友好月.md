Title: Japanese People Regularly Hold All-Japan Congress for Peace; Japan-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold "Japan-Soviet Friendship Month" to Celebrate the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1952-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 28th: TASS Tokyo reported: According to the "Akahata" newspaper, the Japan Peace Protection Committee and the Japan Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference have decided to hold the All-Japan Congress for Peace on October 31st and November 1st. The agenda of the conference includes: a report on the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference and how to implement the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference in Japan, as well as issues related to participating in the World People's Peace Congress.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: TASS Tokyo, 28th: "World Peace" newspaper published an appeal to the Japanese people by Ikuo Oyama and Yoshitaro Hirano regarding the upcoming World People's Peace Congress. They called on the Japanese people to take action, together with all peace-loving people of all countries around the world, to oppose the rearmament of Japan for the purpose of aggression, to demand an end to the Korean War, to demand that the Japanese government and the US government ratify the 1925 Geneva Protocol, to prohibit atomic weapons, to reduce armaments, and to conclude a five-power peace treaty with the participation of the government of the People's Republic of China.

The appeal said that the people of all countries around the world, including the American people, are eager for peace. In the current international situation, the forces of peace can win. If the peace-loving people of all countries unite, peace can be maintained. All people who sincerely hope for peace must support the World People's Peace Congress to be held in Vienna. We must send representatives to the congress.

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: According to TASS Tokyo, 24th: The Japan-Soviet Friendship Association is preparing to hold "Japan-Soviet Friendship Month" from October to November to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

During the Friendship Month, the various levels of organizations of the Japan-Soviet Friendship Association will report to the Japanese people on the lives of the Soviet people and its peaceful policies. Special emphasis will be placed on disseminating Stalin's work "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" and Malenkov's report at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

The organizations of the Japan-Soviet Friendship Association are also preparing to more widely launch a campaign to support Japan-Soviet trade and disseminate the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, and to work hard to send Japanese representatives to the World People's Peace Congress in Vienna.

### 日本人民定期举行全日本拥护和平大会日  苏亲善协会庆祝十月革命将举行“日苏友好月”

1952-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民定期举行全日本拥护和平大会日
    苏亲善协会庆祝十月革命将举行“日苏友好月”
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社东京讯：据“赤旗报”报道，日本拥护和平委员会与亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议日本筹备委员会决定在十月三十一日与十一月一日举行全日本拥护和平大会。大会的议程有：关于亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议决议的报告和如何在日本实现亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的决议，以及关于参加世界人民和平大会的问题。
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社东京二十八日讯：“世界和平”报刊载了大山郁夫与平野义太郎联名向日本人民发表的有关未来的世界人民和平大会的一篇呼吁。他们号召日本人民采取行动，与全世界一切爱好和平的各国人民一道，反对为了进行侵略而重新武装日本，要求结束朝鲜战争，要求日本政府和美国政府批准一九二五年日内瓦议定书。禁止原子武器，裁减军备以及缔结有中华人民共和国政府参加的五大国和平公约。
    呼吁说，全世界各国人民，包括美国人民在内，都渴望和平。在目前国际局势下，和平力量能够赢得胜利。如果渴望和平的各国人民联合一致，则和平就能够保持。一切诚挚地希望和平的人民必须支持将在维也纳举行的世界人民和平大会。我们必须派遣代表出席大会。
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社东京二十四日讯：日苏亲善协会为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年，正在布置于十月到十一月间举行“日苏友好月”。
    在友好月期间，日苏亲善协会的各级组织将向日本人民报道苏联人民的生活和它的和平政策。特别着重的是要向人民传播斯大林的著作“苏联社会主义经济问题”及马林科夫在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会中所作的报告。
    日苏亲善协会的各组织并准备更广泛地展开拥护日苏贸易与传播亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的决议的运动，并为派遣日本代表出席维也纳世界人民和平大会而努力。
