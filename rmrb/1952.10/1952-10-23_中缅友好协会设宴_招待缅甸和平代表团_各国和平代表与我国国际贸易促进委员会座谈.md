Title: Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Burmese Peace Delegation; Peace Representatives from Various Countries Hold Discussions with China Council for the Promotion of International Trade
Author:
Date: 1952-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Zheng Zhenduo, and Vice President, Qian Weichang, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the 17th to entertain the Burmese delegation attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Invited guests included the head of the Burmese delegation, Tekin Ko Tu Maung, the deputy head, Mann Tun Yin, and more than twenty members of the delegation.

Also invited were the Third Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Toe, Charlie Pe Roe, and attendant U Tin Dun.

Also invited were directors of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association and representatives from China's education, art and literature, and science circles, totaling several dozen people.

President Zheng Zhenduo delivered a speech congratulating the Burmese delegation for its contribution to the great creation of the Eastern peace movement at the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. President Zheng Zhenduo said that the vast Chinese people and the Burmese people today share a common and urgent aspiration in the cause of defending peace. This will further strengthen the unity between our two peoples and make greater contributions to the Far Eastern peace movement.

In his reply speech, delegation head Tekin Ko Tu Maung emphasized the enormous contribution that the unity between the Burmese and Chinese peoples can make to the cause of defending world peace, and expressed the hope that the Burmese and Chinese peoples will unite to strive for world peace, and that the people of Asian countries will unite to strive for world peace.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

In other news, approximately seventy representatives from various countries attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held a symposium with the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade on the 17th to exchange information on the foreign trade situations of their respective countries. General Bertie, the French representative attending the Peace Conference, and Daudé-Yves, representative of the International Organization of Journalists, also participated in the symposium.

### 中缅友好协会设宴  招待缅甸和平代表团  各国和平代表与我国国际贸易促进委员会座谈

1952-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅友好协会设宴
    招待缅甸和平代表团
    各国和平代表与我国国际贸易促进委员会座谈
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长于十七日在北京设宴招待出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的缅甸代表团。应邀出席的有缅甸代表团团长德钦哥都迈、副团长曼吞印及团员二十余人。
    应邀出席的还有缅甸驻我国大使馆三等秘书吴陶、查理辟鲁，随员吴廷栋。
    应邀出席的还有中缅友好协会的理事及我国教育界、文艺界、科学界代表等数十人。
    郑振铎会长致词祝贺缅甸代表团在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议这一东方和平运动的伟大创举中所作的贡献。郑振铎会长说，广大中国人民与缅甸人民今天在保卫和平的事业中，有着共同的迫切愿望，这将使我们两国人民的团结继续加强，对远东和平运动作出更大的贡献。
    德钦哥都迈团长在致答词中，强调缅甸和中国人民的团结对于保卫世界和平事业所能作的巨大贡献，希望缅、中两国人民团结起来争取世界和平，亚洲各国人民团结起来争取世界和平。
    宴会自始至终在友好的空气中进行。
    【又讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各国代表约七十人，十七日和中国国际贸易促进委员会举行座谈会，交换各国对外贸易情况。列席和平会议的法国代表柏蒂将军和国际新闻工作者协会代表道莱依西也参加了座谈会。
