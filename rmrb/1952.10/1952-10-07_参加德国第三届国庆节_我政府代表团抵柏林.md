Title: Our Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Attend the Third German National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 4

    To Attend the Third German National Day
    Our Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th] Wei Que and Peng Kang, members of our government delegation traveling to participate in the celebration of the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Berlin by train from Warsaw on the 5th.
    Those who greeted them at the station included: Minister of Internal Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Stanhoff; Minister of Machine Industry, Zille; Minister of Health, Steidel; and Director of the Press Office, Eisler. Ji Pengfei, head of our diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic, also greeted them at the station.
    Ji Pengfei is the head of the delegation appointed by the Government of the People's Republic of China to participate in the celebration of the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

### 参加德国第三届国庆节  我政府代表团抵柏林

1952-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    参加德国第三届国庆节
    我政府代表团抵柏林
    【新华社柏林六日电】前往参加庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年的我国政府代表团团员韦悫和彭康于五日从华沙乘火车抵达柏林。
    到车站欢迎他们的有：德意志民主共和国内务部部长斯丹霍夫、机器工业部部长齐勒、卫生部部长斯泰德尔和新闻处处长艾斯勒。我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团团长姬鹏飞也到车站欢迎。
    姬鹏飞是中华人民共和国政府指派参加庆祝德意志民主共和国成立三周年的代表团的团长。
