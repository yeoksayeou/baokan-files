Title: Standing Committee of the World Peace Council Issues Communiqué on the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 17th: TASS, Vienna, 17th: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, meeting in Vienna to discuss preparations for the World People's Peace Congress to be held in Vienna in December, issued the following communiqué:

The World Peace Council's call for the convening of the World People's Peace Congress has aroused great interest in various countries and has promoted the implementation of many plans.

The policy of strength in international relations has resulted in tensions, the consequences and dangers of which are becoming increasingly apparent. In this situation, people from all walks of life, specifically political, trade union, religious, and anti-war groups, believe that a way out should be found.

How to stop the current wars? How to stop the "Cold War"? How to guarantee national independence? How to guarantee the security of all countries? The answers to these questions can only be found in the discussions of those who truly embody the people's desire for peace.

The World People's Peace Congress, initiated by the World Peace Council, will be an international gathering of men and women representing various nations and different ways of life. Delegates, guests, or observers attending the congress do not necessarily have to be involved in the peace movement. In a free and effective discussion, the World People's Peace Congress will strive to find reasonable solutions to the problems that currently divide the world. Even if an agreement is reached on one issue, it will facilitate the resolution of other issues and will ease the international situation.

The people of all countries will find the path to peace through joint action.

### 世界和平理事会常务委员会  发表关于世界和平大会的公报

1952-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常务委员会
    发表关于世界和平大会的公报
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社维也纳十七日讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会在维也纳开会讨论了将于十二月在维也纳召开的世界人民和平大会的筹备工作后，发表下述公报：
    世界和平理事会建议召开世界人民和平大会的号召在各国引起了很大的兴趣，并已推动了许多计划的进行。
    在国际关系上使用实力政策的结果造成了紧张关系，这种紧张关系的后果和危险性愈来愈明显了。在这种情形下，各界人士，具体地说，政治、工会、宗教以及非战团体都认为应当寻出一条出路来。
    如何制止目前的战争？如何制止“冷战”？如何保证民族独立？如何保证各国安全？这些问题的答复，只能由真正体现人民要求和平的意愿的人们的讨论中得到。
    世界和平理事会所发起的世界人民和平大会，将是代表各个民族与各种不同生活方式的男女的一个国际性集会。参加大会的代表、来宾或观察员不一定是参加了和平运动的人。在自由、有效的讨论中，世界人民和平大会将努力求得目前使世界分裂的问题有一个合理的解决办法。即使在一个问题上有了协议，也就便利其他问题求得解决，并且将使国际形势有所缓和。
    各国人民将从共同行动中找到走向和平的道路。
