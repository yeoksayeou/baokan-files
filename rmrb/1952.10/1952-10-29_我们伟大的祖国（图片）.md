Our Great Motherland

The Chen Shulan Advanced Production Group of the private Shashi Yarn Factory in Chongqing improved the fine yarn doffing method by learning the Hao Jianxiu Work Method, shortening the machine stopping time from 45 seconds to 15 seconds, creating a national record. This is the scene of the female workers of the Chen Shulan Group stopping the machine to prepare for doffing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    重庆市私营沙市纱厂陈树兰先进生产小组，在学习郝建秀工作法中，改进了细纱落纱工作法，关车时间从四十五秒缩短到十五秒，创造了全国最高纪录。这是陈树兰小组的女工正在关车准备落纱的情形。
