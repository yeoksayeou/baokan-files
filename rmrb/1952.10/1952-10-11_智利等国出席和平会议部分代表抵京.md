Title: Partial Representatives from Chile and Other Countries Attending Peace Conference Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-11
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, October 10th—Representatives from Chile, Mexico, and the United States attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, as well as Wojciech Dolejsi (editor of Czech Labor Daily), representative of the International Organization of Journalists invited to participate in the conference, and Sal Gem Keble, representative of the General Secretary of the International Federation of Teachers' Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th. Cai Tingkai, member of the China Peace Committee, Hu Wenyao, Chinese representative attending the Peace Conference, Cao Yu and Zhao Zhongyao, observers, Huang Caoliang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the New China News Agency, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Jabroski, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Hedlicka, Cultural Commissioner, and others also went to the airport to welcome Wojciech Dolejsi.
    The list of Chilean representatives arriving in Beijing today:
    Maria Isabella Holguera Romero, female textile worker, representative of the Peace Committee Trade Union
    The list of Mexican representatives arriving in Beijing today:
    Javier Guerrero, painter
    Felipe Sanchez Acevedo, textile worker, trade union worker
    The list of American representatives arriving in Beijing today:
    Kuzi Whitton, staff member

### 智利等国出席和平会议部分代表抵京

1952-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    智利等国出席和平会议部分代表抵京
    【新华社十日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的智利、墨西哥和美国的代表，以及应邀参加会议的国际新闻工作者协会代表渥伊吉赫·道莱依西
    （捷克斯洛伐克劳动报编辑），和教育工作者工会国际总书记德拉努的代表萨尔·吉姆·凯布列尔于十日下午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会委员蔡廷锴，出席和平会议中国代表胡文耀，列席代表曹禺和赵忠尧，新华通讯社副总编辑黄操良等。捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基，文化专员赫德利奇卡等亦前往机场欢迎渥伊吉赫·道莱依西。

    玛丽亚·伊萨贝拉·霍尔盖拉·罗美洛  纺织女工，和平委员会工会代表
    今日到达北京的墨西哥代表名单：
