Title: Korean People's Army Supreme Headquarters Holds Grand Meeting to Welcome Our慰问团 (Comfort Mission)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 29th: The Supreme Headquarters of the Korean People's Army held a cadre meeting at 11:00 AM on October 25th to warmly welcome Liu Jingfan, head of the Second Chinese People's Comfort Mission to Korea, and representatives of the Comfort Mission.

    After General Park Chang-won of the People's Army Supreme Headquarters delivered the opening remarks, General Yu Chengzhe of the People's Army Supreme Headquarters delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the People's Army. General Yu Chengzhe expressed warm welcome and heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese people for sending comfort missions to Korea twice and presenting a large number of valuable gifts. He said that in the war against the common enemy alongside the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army will repay the strong support and solidarity of the peace-loving people of the world, headed by the great Soviet Union, and the 475 million Chinese people with new victories and achievements. Finally, he said, in order to further consolidate the friendship and unity between the Korean and Chinese people, and for the peace and security of Asia, he wished the brotherly Chinese people even more brilliant achievements and victories in the future under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong.

    Then, Liu Jingfan, head of the Comfort Mission, delivered a message of condolence, first extending his high respect and cordial greetings on behalf of the entire mission and the entire Chinese people to General Kim Il-sung, leader of the Korean people, the heroic Korean People's Army, and the fighting Korean people. After describing the enthusiastic Resist-America, Aid-Korea movement vigorously carried out by the Chinese people and the great achievements of the Chinese people in the past three years, he quoted the words of Chairman Mao Zedong, leader of the Chinese people, in his telegram congratulating the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea: "In order to resist the common enemy, for the peace of Korea and the security of China, the Chinese people will always stand firmly with the Korean people and fight to completely defeat the American imperialist aggressors." Liu, in closing, said: The entire Chinese people, in accordance with the great will of Chairman Mao Zedong, will permanently and firmly stand behind you, supporting you in carrying out the just war until the victorious end, until the attainment of true peace.

    Zhang Zhen, Deputy Commander of the Hubei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and representative of the Comfort Mission, warmly congratulated the Korean People's Army on its great victory in the war against America.

    Subsequently, the Comfort Mission presented a flag and gifts to the People's Army. The banner was inscribed with the firm vow of the Chinese people: "The Chinese people are determined to give their full support to your struggle to completely defeat the common enemy, the American aggressors."

    The meeting ended after reading a letter of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong.

    After the meeting, Kim Jae-wook, Director of the General Political Bureau of the People's Army, accompanied Liu Jingfan, head of the delegation, and 19 representatives of the Chinese People's Comfort Mission to visit a large number of American weapons seized by the People's Army. Director Kim also hosted a banquet for the Comfort Mission.

### 朝鲜人民军最高司令部盛会欢迎我慰问团

1952-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军最高司令部盛会欢迎我慰问团
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】朝鲜人民军最高司令部于十月二十五日上午十一时举行干部大会，热烈欢迎中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团刘景范总团长及慰问团代表。
    大会在人民军最高司令部朴昌源将军致开会词后，由人民军最高司令部俞成哲将军代表人民军全体将士致欢迎词。俞成哲将军对中国人民两次派遣慰问团来朝慰问并赠送大批珍贵礼品，表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的感谢。他说，朝鲜人民军全体将士在与中国人民志愿军并肩反对共同仇敌的战争中，必将以新的胜利和成就来报答以伟大苏联为首的全世界爱好和平的人民以及四亿七千五百万中国人民对我们朝鲜人民强有力的支持和声援。他最后说，为了更加巩固朝中两国人民的友谊和团结，为了亚洲的和平与安全，谨祝兄弟般的中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下今后有更光辉的成就和胜利。
    接着由慰问团刘景范总团长致慰问词，他首先代表全团和全中国人民，向朝鲜人民领袖金日成将军、英勇的朝鲜人民军和战斗中的朝鲜人民致以崇高的敬意和亲切的慰问。他在叙述中国人民热烈开展的抗美援朝运动和中国人民三年来的伟大成就之后，引述了中国人民领袖毛泽东主席在祝贺朝鲜解放七周年的电文中的话：“为了抗击共同的敌人，为了朝鲜的和平及中国的安全，中国人民将永远坚定地与朝鲜人民在一起，为彻底战胜美帝国主义侵略者而奋斗。”刘总团长最后说：全中国人民遵照毛泽东主席的伟大意志，将永久地坚定地作你们的后盾，支援你们进行正义的战争一直到胜利结束，一直到获得真正的和平。
    慰问团代表、中国人民解放军湖北军区张轸副司令员，在讲话中热烈地祝贺朝鲜人民军获得了抗美战争的伟大胜利。
    随后，慰问团向人民军献旗、献礼。锦旗上写着中国人民坚定的誓言：“中国人民决以全力支援你们为完全击败共同的敌人美国侵略者而奋斗”。
    大会在宣读了给毛泽东主席的致敬信后结束。
    会后人民军总政治局金载郁局长陪同刘景范总团长及十九位中国人民慰问团的代表，参观了人民军缴获的大批美国武器。金局长并设宴招待慰问团。
