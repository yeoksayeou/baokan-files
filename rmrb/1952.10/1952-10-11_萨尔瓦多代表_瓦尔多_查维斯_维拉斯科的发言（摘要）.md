Title: Speech by Valdo Chaves Velasco, Representative of El Salvador (Summary)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-11
Page: 2

    Speech by Valdo Chaves Velasco, Representative of El Salvador (Summary)
    The Salvadoran delegation wishes to bring to the attention of the General Assembly one of the most important issues—an issue that causes suffering to millions of people everywhere, and whose existence creates a hateful obstacle that prevents people from achieving their own economic, cultural, and political development.
    We are referring to the colonial system. In mentioning this, we should speak of those Latin American countries that have been economically, culturally, and politically colonized, and of the threat of militarism they are subjected to, as our delegation has already mentioned. We wish to clearly point out this danger, not only to the dependent nations of Latin America and other continents, but also to the peace of the entire world.
    The Salvadoran delegation suggests that the following recommendation should be included in the declaration of this conference:
    “Condemning the colonial system, considering it a form of imperialism, which hinders the independence and normal historical development of other countries, making them merely human resources for wars of aggression.”
    The Salvadoran delegation in Beijing received a telegram informing us that: the government of El Salvador, using the convening of the Peace Conference of the Asian and Pacific Regions as a pretext, and actually intending to preempt any possible impact this conference might have on people's minds, has declared a state of martial law throughout the country, sending 1,200 of our compatriots to prison, because they committed the most terrible crime—the crime of fighting for peace.
    These 1,200 people imprisoned in our country may be our brothers and relatives; they are all our brothers in the glorious struggle for peace. This incident shows beyond any doubt what the war-mongering governments in Latin America, subsidized by the US State Department's dollars, are capable of.
    The people love peace, and they know the criminals who launch wars. History will expose the criminals and punish them.
    The Salvadoran delegation, on behalf of all of us nations suffering from imperialist aggression, requests those attending this conference to protest and support us, which will be a valuable support for all the people suffering in the struggle for peace. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 萨尔瓦多代表  瓦尔多·查维斯·维拉斯科的发言（摘要）

1952-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    萨尔瓦多代表
    瓦尔多·查维斯·维拉斯科的发言（摘要）
    萨尔瓦多代表团希望向大会提出至为重要的问题之一——这个问题使到处千百万人民遭受苦难，并且由于它的存在而产生了一个可恨的障碍，使人民不能获得他们自己经济、文化和政治的发展。
    我们指的是殖民制度。提起这个，我们就应该说到那些在经济上、文化上和政治上殖民地化了的拉丁美洲各国，说到我们的代表团已经提到过的、它们所受到的军国主义的威胁。我们愿意明确指出这种危险性，不只是对拉丁美洲和其他大陆的附属国家的危险，而且是对整个世界和平的危险。
    萨尔瓦多代表团建议这个会议的宣言中应该包括下列建议：
    “谴责殖民制度，认为它是帝国主义的一种形式，它妨害其他国家的独立和正常历史发展，使他们仅仅成为侵略战争的人力资源。”
    在北京的萨尔瓦多代表团接到了一封电报，得知：萨尔瓦多政府以亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的召开为藉口，而实际上是想事先防止这次会议对人民思想上可能发生的影响，宣布全国戒严状态，把我国一千二百位同胞送入监狱，因为他们犯了最可怕的罪——为和平而斗争的罪。
    这一千二百位被囚在我国监狱里的人，可能是我们的兄弟、亲属，他们都是我们在争取和平的光荣斗争中的兄弟。这件事使人无可怀疑地看到：拉丁美洲得到美国国务院美元津贴而扶植起来的煽动战争的政府可以干出什么勾当来。
    人民是爱好和平的，他们是知道发动战争的罪犯的。历史将会揭露罪犯们并给他们以惩罚。
    萨尔瓦多代表团代表我们全体同受帝国主义侵略的国家，要求出席本次大会的人士提出抗议并支持我们，这样对一切在争取和平的斗争中受到痛苦的人民将是一种可贵的支援。     （新华社）
