Title: South Korean People's Guerrilla Attacks Railway Between Seoul and Busan
Author:
Date: 1952-10-30
Page: 4

    South Korean People's Guerrilla

    Attacks Railway Between Seoul and Busan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang] The people's guerrillas in southern Korea continue their heroic struggle against the U.S.-Syngman Rhee bandit gang. According to the South Korean "Haptong News Agency," a unit of the people's guerrillas operating in the Gyeongsangnam-do area attacked a key point on the Seoul-Busan railway line between Gupo Station and Mulgeum Station on the night of the 16th, at one point cutting off the enemy's supply line between Seoul and Busan. The guerrillas returned to their base after a successful attack.

### 朝鲜南部人民游击队  袭击汉城釜山间铁路

1952-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜南部人民游击队
    袭击汉城釜山间铁路
    【新华社平壤电】朝鲜南部的人民游击队继续为反对美李匪帮而进行着英勇的斗争。据南朝鲜合同通讯社透露，在庆尚南道地方活动的人民游击队一支部队，在十六日晚袭击汉城—釜山铁路线上的要冲龟浦站和勿禁站的中间地点，一度切断了汉城到釜山间的敌军需物资的运输线。游击队在胜利出击后返回了基地。
