Title: Allotting American Gifts (Picture)
Author: Hua Junwu
Date: 1952-10-17
Page: 4

Allotting American Gifts
Hua Junwu

    The American conspiracy to expand the war of aggression forces them to try their best to use the casualties of soldiers from other countries to replace American soldiers' casualties, in order to ease the dissatisfaction of the broad American people. The US government has chosen the United Nations General Assembly as the place for him to spur these countries to provide cannon fodder. United Nations Secretary-General Trygve Lie has dutifully arranged the next step for Washington. In his "World Situation" report to the United Nations General Assembly, he said that all countries must "more fairly share the burden in Korea."

### 摊派美国礼品（图片）
华君武
1952-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    摊派美国礼品
    华君武  作
    美国阴谋扩大侵略战争，不得不想尽量用其它国家士兵的伤亡来代替美国兵的伤亡，以缓和美国广大人民的不满。美国政府选择了联合国大会，作为他鞭策这些国家出炮灰的场所，联合国秘书长赖伊已经忠顺地替华盛顿布置了下一步，在他给联合国大会的“世界形势”报告中，他说各国必须“更公平地分任在朝鲜的负担”。
