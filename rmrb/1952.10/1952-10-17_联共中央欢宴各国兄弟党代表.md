Title: CPSU Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1952-10-17
Page: 1

    CPSU Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th] According to TASS, Moscow: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a banquet in the Kremlin on the 15th, the day after the conclusion of the 19th Party Congress, to entertain the representatives of the Communist Parties and other fraternal parties from various countries who had attended the congress.
    All the representatives of the fraternal parties from various countries, as well as members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, attended the banquet.
    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. A grand concert was held at the end.

### 联共中央欢宴各国兄弟党代表

1952-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    联共中央欢宴各国兄弟党代表
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会在第十九次党代表大会闭幕的次日—十五日，在克里姆林宫设宴招待曾列席大会的各国共产党和其他兄弟党的代表们。
    出席宴会的有各国兄弟党的全体代表，还有苏联共产党中央委员会的委员和候补委员们。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。最后举行了盛大的音乐会。
