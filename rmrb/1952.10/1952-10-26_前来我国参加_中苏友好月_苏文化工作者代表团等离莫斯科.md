Title: Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation and Others Depart Moscow to Attend "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1952-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 25th: The Soviet cultural workers delegation, the performing arts troupe, and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, which are going to our country to participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," left here by train for Beijing on the evening of the 23rd. The Soviet cultural workers delegation consists of five people, led by Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee and famous poet. The members are Belousov, Dean of the Agronomy Department of the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy; Grusenko, Academic Secretary of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Zakharov, a famous composer; and Yefimov (already in China), a professor at Leningrad University and author of a modern Chinese history. The performing arts troupe is led by Churaki, Deputy Chairman of the Arts Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and includes 27 people, including the famous ballet dancer Ulanova. Among them, ballet dancers Ulanova and Kondratov, and pianist Zaitseva, will fly to Beijing at the end of the month. The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble consists of 260 people, led by Alexandrov. Those who came to the station to see them off included Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Soviet Union, and Counselor Ge Baoquan; Tverdokhlebov, Deputy Chairman of the Arts Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers; Kalishyan, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Kotov, Executive Secretary of the Soviet Peace Committee; and Pastoyev, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 前来我国参加“中苏友好月”  苏文化工作者代表团等离莫斯科

1952-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    前来我国参加“中苏友好月”
    苏文化工作者代表团等离莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】前往我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联文化工作者代表团、演员队和苏军红旗歌舞团于二十三日晚乘火车离此赴北京。苏联文化工作者代表团由五人组成，团长为苏联拥护和平委员会主席、名诗人吉洪诺夫，团员为蒂米利亚泽夫农业学院农学系主任别洛沙普卡，苏联科学院学术秘书格鲁森科，名作曲家查哈罗夫，列宁格勒大学教授、中国近代史作者叶菲莫夫（已在中国）。演员队由苏联部长会议艺术委员会副主席楚拉基带领，包括名巴蕾舞演员乌兰诺娃等二十七人，其中巴蕾舞演员乌兰诺娃、康德拉托夫和钢琴家查伊采娃将于月底乘飞机前往北京。苏军红旗歌舞团二百六十人由亚历山大罗夫带领。到车站送行者有：我驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天和参赞戈宝权，苏联部长会议艺术委员会副主席特维尔多赫列波夫，苏联对外文化协会副主席卡利什扬，苏联拥护和平委员会执行书记科托夫，苏联外交部交际司副司长巴斯托耶夫。
