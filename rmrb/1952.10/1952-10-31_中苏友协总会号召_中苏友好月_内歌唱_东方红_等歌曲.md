Title: All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Calls for Singing "The East is Red" and Other Songs During "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1952-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has called on people throughout the country to sing the following songs during "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month": "The East is Red," "Ode to Stalin," "Moscow-Beijing," "March of the Motherland," "The People of the World are of One Heart," and "Sino-Soviet Friendship Song" (to be announced soon). The Central People's Broadcasting Station and local radio stations will broadcast lessons on "Sino-Soviet Friendship Song" starting from November 1st.

### 中苏友协总会号召“中苏友好月”内歌唱“东方红”等歌曲

1952-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会号召“中苏友好月”内歌唱“东方红”等歌曲
    【新华社三十日讯】中苏友好协会总会号召全国人民在“中苏友好月”期间内歌唱下列歌曲：“东方红”、“斯大林颂”、“莫斯科—北京”、“祖国进行曲”、“全世界人民心一条”、“中苏友好歌”（不日公布）。中央人民广播电台及各地广播电台自十一月一日起将广播教授“中苏友好歌”。
