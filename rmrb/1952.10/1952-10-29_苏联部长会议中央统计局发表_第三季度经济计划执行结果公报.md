Title: Communiqué of the Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on the Results of the Implementation of the Economic Plan for the Third Quarter
Author:
Date: 1952-10-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 28th (TASS): The Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union issued the following communiqué on the results of the implementation of the plan for the development of the national economy in the third quarter of 1952:
　　The development of industry, agriculture, and trade in the Soviet Union in the third quarter of 1952 can be seen from the following materials:
　　I. Industry
　　The total industrial output for the quarter fulfilled 100.7 percent of the original plan. The percentages of fulfillment of the total industrial output plan for the quarter by various industrial departments are as follows: Ministry of Ferrous Metallurgy 103, Ministry of Non-Ferrous Metallurgy 99.9, Ministry of Coal Industry 100.1, Ministry of Petroleum Industry 100.8, Ministry of Power Stations 100.1, Ministry of Chemical Industry 101, Ministry of Electrical Industry 102, Ministry of Communications Equipment Industry 101, Ministry of Heavy Machine Building 99.2, Ministry of Automobile and Tractor Industry 100.8, Ministry of Machine Tool Building 100, Ministry of Machine and Instrument Manufacturing 100.1, Ministry of Construction and Road-Building Machinery Manufacturing 104, Ministry of Transport Machine Building 93, Ministry of Agricultural Machine Building 94, Ministry of Building Materials Industry of the Soviet Union 99.7, Ministry of Timber Industry of the Soviet Union 92, Ministry of Paper and Woodworking Industry 104, Ministry of Light Industry of the Soviet Union 101, Ministry of Fisheries of the Soviet Union 95, Ministry of Meat and Dairy Industry of the Soviet Union 100.2, Ministry of Food Industry of the Soviet Union 105, Industrial Enterprises of the Ministry of Cotton Growing of the Soviet Union 83, Industrial Enterprises of the Ministry of Railways 102, Industrial Enterprises of the Ministry of Health of the Soviet Union 103, Industrial Enterprises of the Ministry of Cinematography of the Soviet Union 107, Local Industrial Departments and Local Fuel Industrial Departments of various Union Republics 107, Production Cooperatives 103.
　　The total industrial output of the Soviet Union in the third quarter of 1952 increased by 11 percent compared to the third quarter of 1951.
　　In the third quarter of 1952, the labor productivity of industrial workers in the Soviet Union increased by 6 percent compared to the third quarter of 1951.
　　The target set for reducing the production cost of industrial products in the third quarter of 1952 has been exceeded.
　　II. Agriculture
　　The harvesting work of collective farms, machine tractor stations, and state farms is about to be successfully completed. The total grain output in 1952 is 8 billion poods, an increase of 600 million poods compared to last year. According to statistics from the Central State Survey Committee on Grain Harvest Volume under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, this year's grain harvest in Ukraine, North Caucasus, and Crimea has been abundant.
　　The grain harvest volume in other regions of the country is also greater than last year.
　　The total output of flax fiber, sunflowers, and potatoes has also increased compared to 1951. The total harvest of raw cotton and sugar beets this year is greater than in 1951.
　　The material and technical foundation of agriculture has been further strengthened this year, and the level of mechanization of agricultural work in collective farms and state farms has been improved. In the past period of 1952, the agricultural work done by machine tractor stations for collective farms increased by 8 percent compared to the same period in 1951. The grain harvested by combines increased by 15 percent compared to last year.
　　III. Expansion of Trade
　　In the third quarter of 1952, Soviet trade continued to expand. The retail sales of state-owned stores and cooperative stores increased by 10 percent compared to the third quarter of 1951. The sales volume of various goods increased as follows: meat 17 percent, fish products 20 percent, butter and other oils 25 percent, cheese 21 percent, eggs 24 percent, sugar 31 percent, confectionery 15 percent, cotton fabrics 5 percent, silk fabrics 27 percent, clothing 19 percent, knitwear 25 percent, hosiery 19 percent, leather shoes 24 percent, household soap 14 percent, toilet soap 31 percent, bicycles 12 percent, cameras 33 percent, sewing machines 11 percent, watches and clocks 5 percent.
　　In the third quarter of 1952, the retail sales of agricultural products in collective farm markets, especially butter, eggs, poultry, vegetables, flour, and grain, all increased compared to the third quarter of 1951. The prices of flour and flour products in collective farm markets have decreased.
　　Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union (Signed)

### 苏联部长会议中央统计局发表  第三季度经济计划执行结果公报

1952-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议中央统计局发表
    第三季度经济计划执行结果公报
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日电：苏联部长会议中央统计局发表一九五二年第三季发展国民经济计划执行结果的公报如下：
    一九五二年第三季工业、农业和贸易的发展情况，可由下列材料看出来：
    一、工业方面
    本季工业总产量完成原定计划的百分之一百点七。各工业部完成本季工业总产量计划的百分比如下：钢铁工业部一○三，有色金属工业部九九·九，煤矿工业部一○○·一，石油工业部一○○·八，电站部一○○·一，化学工业部一○一，电气工业部一○二，通讯器材工业部一○一，重型机器制造部九九·二，汽车和拖拉机工业部一○○·八，母机制造部一○○，机器和器械制造部一○○·一，建造和筑路机器制造部一○四，运输机器制造部九三，农业机器制造部九四，苏联建筑材料工业部九九·七，苏联木材工业部九二，造纸和木工工业部一○四，苏联轻工业部一○一，苏联渔业部九五，苏联肉乳工业部一○○·二，苏联食品工业部一○五，苏联植棉部工业企业八三，铁道部工业企业一○二，苏联卫生部工业企业一○三，苏联电影事业部工业企业一○七，各加盟共和国的地方工业部和地方燃料工业部一○七，生产合作社一○三。
    苏联一九五二年第三季工业总产量比一九五一年第三季增加百分之十一。
    一九五二年第三季苏联产业工人的劳动生产率比一九五一年第三季提高了百分之六。
    为一九五二年第三季减低工业产品生产成本所规定的目标已经超过。
    二、农业方面
    集体农庄、机器拖拉机站和国营农场的收割工作即将顺利完成。一九五二年谷物总产量为八十亿普特，较去年增加六亿普特。根据苏联部长会议所属谷物收获量中央国家调查委员会的统计数字，今年乌克兰、北高加索和克里米亚谷物都已丰收。
    国内其他区域的谷物收获量也较去年为多。
    亚麻纤维、向日葵和马铃薯的总产量也较一九五一年增加。今年原棉和糖萝卜的总收成较一九五一年为多。
    今年农业的物质基础和技术基础已进一步加强，集体农庄和国营农场农业工作的机械化水平已经提高。在一九五二年过去的时期中，机器拖拉机站为集体农庄所做的农业工作较一九五一年同时期增加百分之八。联合收割机收割的谷物较去年增加百分之十五。
    三、扩充贸易方面
    在一九五二年第三季，苏联贸易继续扩充。国营商店和合作社商店的零售额较一九五一年第三季增加百分之十。各种货物的销售量增加如下：肉类百分之十七，鱼品百分之二十，牛油和其他油类百分之二十五，干酪百分之二十一，蛋类百分之二十四，糖百分之三十一，糖果百分之十五，棉织品百分之五，丝织品百分之二十七，衣服百分之十九，针织品百分之二十五，袜类百分之十九，皮鞋百分之二十四，家用肥皂百分之十四，香皂百分之三十一，脚踏车百分之十二，照相机百分之三十三，缝纫机百分之十一，钟表百分之五。
    一九五二年第三季集体农庄市场上的农产品零售额，特别是牛油、蛋类、家禽、蔬菜、面粉和谷物，都比一九五一年第三季增加。集体农庄市场上的面粉和面粉制造品价格有了降低。
    苏联部长会议中央统计局（签字）
