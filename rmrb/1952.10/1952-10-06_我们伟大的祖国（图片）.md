Author:
Date: 1952-10-06

    Our Great Motherland
    Tobacco leaves are one of China's main cash crops and have a large market abroad. This is a large batch of tobacco leaves that have been re-baked at the Xuchang Tobacco Re-drying Factory in Henan Province. They are being packaged and are ready to be shipped abroad.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    烟叶是我国的主要经济作物之一，在国外有广大市场。这是河南省许昌烟叶复烤厂经过焙制的大批烟叶，它们被包装起来，正准备外运。
