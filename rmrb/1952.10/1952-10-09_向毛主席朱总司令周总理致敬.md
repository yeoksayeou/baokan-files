Title: Paying Respects to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1952-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th:

    Delegations of various regions and ethnic groups attending the National Day celebrations and paying respects presented flags to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council on the afternoon of the 8th.

    The delegations presenting flags represented the following 46 ethnic groups: Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Uyghur, Miao, Yi, Zhuang, Buyi, Korean, Kazakh, Tatar, Kyrgyz, Uzbek, Russian, Xibe, Solon, Tajik, Dongxiang, Tu, Salar, Dai, Mosuo, Lahu, Akha, Dong, Yao, Li, Gaoshan, Shantou, Gelao, Aini, Sani, Sha, She, Oroqen, Daur, Kyrgyz, Hezhen, Vietnamese, Manchu, Tuli, Achang, Bili, Maonan, Jewish, and Shemin.

    Present at the flag presentation were Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Huang Yanpei and Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan, and Vice Chairman Liu Geping.

    Tibetan Delegation and Representatives from the Qamdo Region Present Flags and Gifts

    Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council received flags and gifts from the Tibetan Delegation and representatives of monks and laypeople from the Qamdo region on the afternoon of the 8th, and met with representatives of the Tibetan Delegation and representatives from Qamdo.

    Present were: Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Huang Yanpei and Deng Xiaoping, Deputy Director of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee Xi Zhongxun, Chairman of the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan, and Vice Chairman Liu Geping.

    Overseas Chinese Delegations from Korea, India, Indonesia, Burma, and Others Present Flags

    Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: Overseas Chinese delegations returning to China for sightseeing from Korea, India, Indonesia, Burma, and representatives of returned Malayan refugees who suffered from imperialist persecution presented flags to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council on the afternoon of the 8th.

    Present were: Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Huang Yanpei and Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government He Xiangning, and Vice Chairman Liao Chengzhi.

    Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe Presents Gifts

    Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council received gifts from the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe on the afternoon of the 8th, and met with the head and deputy head of the troupe.

    Present was Romanian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Rudenko.

### 向毛主席朱总司令周总理致敬

1952-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    向毛主席朱总司令周总理致敬
    各地区各民族国庆节观礼团和致敬团献旗
    【新华社八日讯】各地区各民族国庆节观礼团和致敬团，在八日下午向中央人民政府毛泽东主席、和中国人民解放军朱德总司令、政务院周恩来总理献旗。
    献旗的观礼团和致敬团，代表的是：蒙族、回族、藏族、维吾尔族、苗族、彝族、僮族、布依族、朝鲜族、哈萨克族、塔塔尔族、柯尔克兹族、乌兹别克族、俄罗斯族、锡伯族、索伦族、塔吉克族、东乡族、土族、撒拉族、傣族、么些族、倮?族、阿佧族、侗族、瑶族、黎族、高山族、山头族、仡佬族、爱尼族、撒尼族、沙族、畲族、鄂伦春族、达呼尔族、吉尔吉斯族、赫哲族、越南族、满族、土里族、阿车族、侎俐族、毛难族、犹太人、?民等共四十六个民族。
    献旗时，在场的有中央人民政府副主席李济深，秘书长林伯渠，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，政务院副总理黄炎培、邓小平，中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，副主任委员刘格平。
    西藏致敬团和昌都地区人民代表献旗献礼
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军朱德总司令、政务院周恩来总理，八日下午接受西藏致敬团和昌都地区僧俗人民代表的献旗、献礼，并接见了西藏致敬团的代表和昌都致敬代表。
    在场的，有：中央人民政府副主席李济深，秘书长林伯渠，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，政务院副总理黄炎培、邓小平，政务院文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋，中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，副主任委员刘格平。
    朝鲜印度印尼缅甸华侨回国观光团等献旗
    【新华社八日讯】朝鲜、印度、印尼、缅甸华侨回国观光团以及遭受帝国主义迫害的马来亚归国难侨的代表，在八日下午向中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军朱德总司令、政务院周恩来总理献旗。
    在场的，有：中央人民政府副主席李济深，秘书长林伯渠，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，政务院副总理黄炎培、邓小平，中央人民政府华侨事务委员会主任委员何香凝，副主任委员廖承志。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团献礼
    【新华社八日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军朱德总司令、政务院周恩来总理，在八日下午接受罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团的献礼，并接见了该团的正副团长。
    在场的，有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使鲁登科。
