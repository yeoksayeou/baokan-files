Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 155th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch—The 155th session of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 17th. The meeting heard a report by Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, on the ideological remoulding movement of teachers at all levels of schools across the country, and approved a number of appointment and removal cases and appointment and removal cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一五五次会议

1952-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五五次会议
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十五次政务会议在十七日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府教育部钱俊瑞副部长所作的关于全国各级学校教师思想改造运动的报告，并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
