Title: Trade Union Workers from Various Countries Attending the Peace Conference Protest Against the Atrocities of the Pinay Government
Author:
Date: 1952-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th:

    Trade union workers from various countries attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and Thornton, a member of the Liaison Bureau for Asia and Australia of the World Federation of Trade Unions, jointly sent a telegram to French Prime Minister Pinay on the 14th, protesting against the French government's unreasonable arrest of French peace fighter and labor leader Le Léap. The original telegram is as follows:
    We, the trade union representatives participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, strongly protest against your government's unreasonable arrest of Le Léap, Secretary-General of the French General Confederation of Labor and Vice-President of the World Federation of Trade Unions. It is very clear that the only charge you have brought against Le Léap is that he is a supporter of peace. Striving for peace is not a crime, therefore we demand that you immediately order the release of this outstanding trade union leader and stop persecuting the peace movement.

### 出席和平会议各国工会工作者  抗议比内政府暴行

1952-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    出席和平会议各国工会工作者
    抗议比内政府暴行
    【新华社十九日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各国工会工作者和世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员桑顿，于十四日联名致电法国总理比内，抗议法国政府无理逮捕法国和平战士、工人领袖勒里普。原电如下：
    我们，参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的工会代表，强烈抗议你的政府逮捕法国总工会总书记、世界工会联合会副主席勒里普的无理行为。非常明显，你所提出的勒里普的唯一罪状，就是他是一个和平拥护者。争取和平并不犯罪，因此我们要求你立即下令释放这位杰出的工会领袖并停止迫害和平运动。
