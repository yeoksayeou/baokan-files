Title: Zhou Libo and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    Zhou Libo, head of the People's Republic of China Art Troupe, Dynasty Wen, member, and some members of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe, returned to Beijing by plane from Ulaanbaatar, capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, on the 13th.

    Those who went to Ulaanbaatar Airport to see them off included: Mongolian Deputy Prime Ministers Suranzhab, Maidar, Lubsang, Shalap; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Susobama; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian Government, Zhamtsolron-Sambuu; Chairman of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, Tugsulong; Chairman of the Mongolian Women's Federation, Udaval; Deputy Minister of Education of the Mongolian Government, Zhamtsurong; President of the Mongolian Academy of Sciences, Damdin Soron; President of the Academy of Arts, Baolod; Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ochirbatu; Director of the Department of Oriental Affairs, Sanjimitoob, and more than 100 people from Ulaanbaatar's cultural and artistic circles. Ji Yaotai, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Novikov, and personnel from the Chinese, Soviet, and Korean Embassies also went to the airport to see them off.

    Zhou Libo, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, issued a written statement before his departure, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to the Mongolian government and people for their care and hospitality towards the delegation.

    When Zhou Libo and others returned to Beijing, those who greeted them at the airport included: Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mongolian Ambassador to China, Jalkhasaikhan, and many embassy staff members.

    In addition: Ding Yi, member of the Chinese Art Troupe and head of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe; Bu He, member of the delegation and deputy head of the Song and Dance Troupe, and Zhang Yunxi led a part of the Song and Dance Troupe on the 13th to Sukhbaatar city and other cities along the railway in Mongolia to perform.

### 周立波等返抵北京

1952-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周立波等返抵北京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华人民共和国文艺代表团团长周立波、团员王朝闻及中国歌舞团部分团员，于十三日乘飞机从蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托返抵北京。
    到乌兰巴托机场欢送的有：蒙古政府副总理苏兰扎布、迈达尔、鲁布桑，沙拉普，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记苏索巴马，蒙古政府外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布，蒙苏文化协会主席图格苏龙，蒙古劳动妇女联合会主席乌达瓦尔，蒙古政府教育部副部长札木苏荣，蒙古科学院院长丹姆丁索龙，艺术院院长宝洛德，外交部交际司司长奥齐尔巴图、东方司司长桑几米托布，以及乌兰巴托文化艺术界人士等共一百余人。我国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰、苏联临时代办诺维克夫，以及中国、苏联、朝鲜大使馆人员亦到机场送行。
    中国文艺代表团团长周立波在临行前发表书面谈话，对蒙古政府和人民对代表团的关怀和招待表示衷心的感谢。
    周立波等返抵北京时，到机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，及蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗及使馆人员等多人。
    又讯：中国文艺代表团团员兼中国歌舞团团长丁毅、代表团团员兼歌舞团副团长布赫、张云溪带领歌舞团一部分团员，于十三日离乌兰巴托赴蒙古苏赫—巴托尔城及铁路沿线城市演出。
