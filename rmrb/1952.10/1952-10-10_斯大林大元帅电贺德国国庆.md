Title: Marshal Stalin Sends Congratulations on German National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-10-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 8th: The German News Agency, Berlin, reported on the 7th that Chairman Stalin of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating the German Democratic Republic on its third anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:
    On the occasion of the third National Day of the German Democratic Republic, I extend my congratulations to the German people, the government, and to you personally, Comrade Prime Minister, and wish you further achievements in the great work of building a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

### 斯大林大元帅电贺德国国庆

1952-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅电贺德国国庆
    【新华社八日讯】德意志通讯社柏林七日讯：苏联部长会议主席斯大林致电德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，祝贺德意志民主共和国成立三周年，电文如下：
    值此德意志民主共和国第三届国庆日，我仅向德国人民、政府和您本人，总理同志致以祝贺，并祝在建立统一、独立、民主和爱好和平的德国的伟大工作中取得进一步的成就。
