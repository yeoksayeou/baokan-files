Title: Long Live Peace - Celebrating the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 8

Long Live Peace
Celebrating the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference

The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference opened in Beijing on October 2nd. This is the "Long Live Peace" slogan written in Chinese, Russian, English, and French at the entrance of the conference hall. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Jiang Qisheng)

October 3rd - On the second day of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Guo Moruo, Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation, delivered a general report entitled "Unite as One to Defend Peace". (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Chen Bo)

When the conference opened, members of the Beijing Municipal Children's Team presented flowers to the presidium of the conference, and applause and cheers filled the hall. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Qi Guanshan)

October 3rd, the Korean delegation presented a flag to the presidium of the conference. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Liu Dong'ao)

Soong Ching Ling, Head of the Chinese Delegation, served as the Executive Chairman of the opening ceremony and delivered the opening speech. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Qi Guanshan)

The heads and deputy heads of the delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and India gathered to talk happily. From left to right: I.I. Anisimov, Head of the Soviet Delegation; Saifuddin Kitchlew, Head of the Indian Delegation; and Peng Zhen, Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Liu Dong'ao)

October 2nd, during a break in the conference, Soong Ching Ling, Head of the Chinese Delegation, talked with Du Chin Chee of Myanmar. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Liu Dong'ao)

The Chinese delegation representative, Venerable Yuan Ying (right), spoke with the head of the Myanmar delegation, Thakin Kodaw Hmaing (left), during a break in the conference. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Yuan Ling)

Raymonda Dien (right), a French peace fighter and National Committee Secretary of the French Women's Union, who once lay across train tracks in France to prevent the passage of a train loaded with American munitions for the invasion of Vietnam, met with Nguyen Thi Than, a Vietnamese representative and female guerrilla hero of the Vietnamese People's Army. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Ding Yi)

Li Dequan, a representative of the Chinese delegation, and Korean representatives Mun Ye-bong and Kim Young-soo held talks. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Yuan Hao)

Japanese representative Toogo Kameda (left) and Australian representative Victor James (right), who gave a supplementary report on the Japanese issue, shook hands warmly. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, photo by Qi Guanshan)

### 和平万岁  庆祝亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议（图片）

1952-10-07
第8版()
专栏：

    和平万岁
    庆祝亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议已于十月二日在北京开幕。这是在大会进口处用中、俄、英、法四国文字书写的“和平万岁”标语。（新华社稿·蒋齐生摄）
    十月三日——亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议第二日，中国代表团副团长郭沫若作了“团结一心，保卫和平”的总报告。　（新华社稿·陈勃摄）
    大会开幕时，北京市少年儿童队队员向大会主席团献花，会场内掌声四起，洋溢着一片欢呼。　（新华社稿·齐观山摄）
    十月三日，朝鲜代表团向大会主席团献旗。（新华社稿·刘东鳌摄）
    中国代表团团长宋庆龄任大会开幕式执行主席，并致开幕词。
    （新华社稿·齐观山摄）苏联、
    中国、印度三国代表团的团长和副团长欢聚畅谈。自左起为苏联代表团团长伊·伊·阿尼西莫夫、印度代表团团长赛福丁·克其鲁、中国代表团副团长彭真。　（新华社稿·刘东鳌摄）
    十月二日大会休息时，中国代表团团长宋庆龄与缅甸代表都钦七交谈。　（新华社稿·刘东鳌摄）
    中国代表团代表圆瑛和尚（右）与缅甸代表团团长德钦哥都迈（左）在会议休息时谈话。　（新华社稿·袁　苓摄）
    曾在法国横卧火车轨道，阻止侵略越南的装有美国军火列车通过的法国和平战士、法国女青年联盟全国委员会书记雷蒙达·第安（右）和越南代表、越南人民军游击队女英雄阮氏羶等晤谈。（新华社稿·丁一摄）
    中国代表团代表李德全和朝鲜代表文艺峰、金英秀会谈。（新华社稿·袁浩摄）
    日本代表龟田东伍（左）和作关于日本问题的补充报告的澳大利亚代表维克多·詹姆斯（右）热烈握手。（新华社稿·齐观山摄）
