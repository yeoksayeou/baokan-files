Title: China and Bulgaria Exchange Instruments of Ratification for Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1952-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th—The ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification for the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the governments of China and Bulgaria was held in Beijing on October 25th. Vice Minister Wu Xiuquan of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government and Ambassador Petkov of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China signed the exchange of ratification certificates on behalf of their respective governments. On the Chinese side, those who attended the ceremony included: Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Shen Yanbing; Ma Yinchu, Plenipotentiary Representative of China to Bulgaria for the signing of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement; Chen Hansheng, member of the delegation; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Culture and Education; Dong Xibai, Chief Secretary of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhao Feng, Counselor of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Culture and Education; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. On the Bulgarian side: Counselor Tsarvulkov and Commercial Counselor Konstantinov of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China, and others attended. The Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Sofia on July 14, 1952, and was ratified by the Central People's Government of China and the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria on August 7 and September 25, 1952, respectively.

### 中保两国政府互换文化合作协定批准书

1952-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中保两国政府互换文化合作协定批准书
    【新华社二十五日讯】中保两国政府互换中保文化合作协定批准书仪式于十月二十五日在北京举行，由我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权与保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使彼得科夫分别代表中保两国政府在互换批准证书上签字。参加仪式的我方有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、我赴保签订中保文化合作协定全权代表马寅初、代表团团员陈翰笙、外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、外交部条约委员会主任秘书董希白、政务院文化教育委员会参事赵沨、外交部交际处副处长沈平等；保方有：保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞察尔伏拉诺夫、商务参赞康士坦丁诺夫等。中保文化合作协定于一九五二年七月十四日在索非亚签订，并先后于一九五二年八月七日及九月二十五日分别经我中央人民政府及保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团批准。
