    Our Great Motherland
    The forest area of the Lesser Khingan Mountains in Northeast China produces a large quantity of red pine, which is an indispensable and important timber for China's economic construction. Here, forestry workers are cleaning the forest farm after logging, preparing to continue planting trees.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-14

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国东北小兴安岭的林区，出产大量的红松，是我国经济建设中不可缺少的重要木材。这里的林业工人，在伐木后正进行打扫林场的工作，准备继续种植树木。
