Title: Great Writer Lu Xun's Works Published Over 400,000 Copies After Liberation
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The works of the great writer Lu Xun have been actively prepared for publication by the People's Literature Publishing House since the national liberation. Since their initial publication in September of last year, a total of 31 titles have been published, totaling over 417,000 copies. This greatly exceeds the total number of Lu Xun's works printed from the time of his death until liberation. In order to enable more readers to fully understand the great value of Lu Xun's works, the Lu Xun Works Editorial Department of the People's Literature Publishing House is currently carrying out annotation work. The annotated editions will be published successively next year.

### 伟大作家鲁迅著作解放后印行四十多万册

1952-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大作家鲁迅著作解放后印行四十多万册
    【新华社十八日讯】伟大作家鲁迅的著作，在全国解放后即由人民文学出版社积极筹备出版，自去年九月间开始印行，到现在共出书三十一种，约四十一万七千余册，较鲁迅先生逝世至解放前其著作的全部印行数量大大地增加。人民文学出版社鲁迅著作编刊部，为了使更多的读者群众能充分地领会鲁迅著作的伟大价值，现正进行注释工作，注释本将在明年陆续出版。
