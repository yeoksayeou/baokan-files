Title: "Sino-Soviet Friendship News" Launched

Author: 
Date: 1952-10-08
Page: 1

    "Sino-Soviet Friendship News" Launched
     [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] To meet the needs of the broad masses, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has decided to change its official publication "Sino-Soviet Friendship" magazine (semi-monthly) into a popular newspaper "Sino-Soviet Friendship News". The newspaper was launched on October 5th - the third anniversary of the association's founding. "Sino-Soviet Friendship News" is published every ten days, on the 5th, 15th and 25th of each month, in a four-page, four-sheet format. Its mission is: to publicize the friendly relations between the Chinese and Soviet people, so that the broad masses of our people understand the great significance of the Sino-Soviet friendly alliance in the cause of national construction and safeguarding world peace; to publicize the brilliant achievements of socialist and communist construction in the Soviet Union; to introduce the advanced production experience and scientific inventions of the Soviet Union, as well as the communist morality and exemplary deeds of the Soviet people. In order to make it easier for workers and peasants to read, "Sino-Soviet Friendship News" will strive to select materials that meet the actual needs of our country's construction, with short, popular texts, and will also publish photographs and illustrations.

### “中苏友好报”创刊

1952-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    “中苏友好报”创刊
     【新华社七日讯】中苏友好协会总会为了适合广大群众的需要，决定把该会机关刊“中苏友好”杂志（半月刊）改为通俗报纸“中苏友好报”。该报已在十月五日——该会成立三周年纪念日创刊。“中苏友好报”为十日刊，每月五日、十五日、二十五日出版，四开四版一张。它的任务是：宣传中苏两国人民的友好关系，使我国广大人民认识中苏两国友好同盟在祖国建设和保卫世界和平事业中的伟大意义；宣传苏联社会主义和共产主义建设的辉煌成就；介绍苏联先进生产经验和科学发明，以及苏联人民的共产主义道德与模范事迹。为了使工农群众便于阅读，“中苏友好报”在选材方面将力求适合我国建设的实际需要，文字短小、通俗，并且登载照片和图画。
