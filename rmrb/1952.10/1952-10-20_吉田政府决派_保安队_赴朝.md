Title: Yoshida Government Decides to Send "Security Force" to Korea

Author:

Date: 1952-10-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, October 18th (TASS): According to Kyodo News, Ichiro Hatoyama—one of the leaders of the Liberal Party—said in a speech to his group of parliamentarians that officers of the National "Security Force" had told him, "It has been decided to send personnel from the force to Korea."

### 吉田政府决派“保安队”赴朝

1952-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    吉田政府决派“保安队”赴朝
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社东京十八日讯：据共同社报道：鸠山一郎——自由党党魁之一——对他的国会议员团的议员讲演时说，国家“保安队”的军官曾告诉他说：“已决定派该队的人员到朝鲜去。”
