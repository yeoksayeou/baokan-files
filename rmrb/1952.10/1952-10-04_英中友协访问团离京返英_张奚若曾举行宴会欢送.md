Title: Sino-British Friendship Association Delegation Departs Beijing for Return to Britain; Zhang Xiruo Hosted Farewell Banquet

Author:

Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd -- Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 2nd to bid farewell to the visiting delegation of the Britain-China Friendship Association.

The delegation left Beijing by plane for their return to Britain on the morning of the 3rd. Among those seeing them off at the airport were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, as well as representatives from Chinese trade, education, and women's circles.

The visiting delegation of the Britain-China Friendship Association arrived in Beijing on September 4th. From the 11th, they visited Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and Hankou. In various locations, they held multiple discussions with Chinese workers, farmers, social workers, scientists, literary and art circles, business and industry circles, and religious circles. They returned to Beijing on the 28th to attend the National Day celebrations. The delegation spent one month in China.

### 英中友协访问团离京返英  张奚若曾举行宴会欢送

1952-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    英中友协访问团离京返英
    张奚若曾举行宴会欢送
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若于二日晚举行宴会欢送英中友好协会访问团。
    该团于三日晨乘飞机离京返英，到机场欢送的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若暨中国贸易、教育、妇女界人士。
    英中友好协会访问团自九月四日抵京，十一日起前往南京、上海、杭州、广州、汉口各地参观，在各地与我国工人、农民、社会工作者、科学界、文学艺术界、工商界、宗教界等人士举行多次座谈，二十八日返北京出席我国国庆节观礼。该团在中国历时一月。
