Title: Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Korea and the Soviet Union: Kim Il-sung Sends a Telegram of Respect to Marshal Stalin
Author:
Date: 1952-10-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: TASS Moscow, 12th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. The telegram reads as follows: Dear Comrade Stalin,

　　On the fourth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, I would like, on behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and all the Korean people, to express to you, dear Joseph Vissarionovich, the liberator and best friend of the Korean people, our warm admiration and gratitude, and ask you to convey this to the Soviet government and all the Soviet people.

　　Four years ago today, the Soviet government recognized the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; the Soviet army liberated the Korean people from the shackles of Japanese imperialism, thus enabling the Korean people to establish an independent and democratic country. Since then, the great Soviet Union has always provided the Democratic People's Republic of Korea with selfless assistance and friendly support. The friendly relations between our countries have been further strengthened in the fierce struggle of the Korean people against American interventionists and for the freedom and independence of their country.

　　Dear Joseph Vissarionovich, the Korean people express their willingness to continue to develop and strengthen the friendly relations between our two peoples in every possible way.

　　The Korean people deeply recognize the full significance of strengthening Korean-Soviet friendship and understand that only with the fraternal and selfless assistance of the great Soviet Union is it possible for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to exist peacefully and independently and for it to prosper.

　　Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!

　　Glory to the great Soviet Union—the indestructible fortress of world peace, democracy, and socialism!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Il-sung (signed)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Pyongyang, October 10, 1952

### 庆祝朝苏两国建交四周年  金日成电斯大林大元帅致敬

1952-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝苏两国建交四周年
    金日成电斯大林大元帅致敬
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成致电苏联部长会议主席约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林，电文如下：亲爱的斯大林同志：
    在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间建立外交关系第四周年纪念日，我谨代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和全体朝鲜人民，向您，亲爱的约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇，朝鲜人民的解放者和最好的朋友，致热烈的敬佩与感激之意，并请您将此意转达苏联政府和全体苏联人民。
    四年前的今天，苏联政府承认了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府；苏联军队把朝鲜人民从日本帝国主义者的枷锁下解放出来，从而使朝鲜人民能够建立一个独立、民主的国家。从那时候以来，伟大的苏联始终给予朝鲜民主主义人民共和国以无私的援助和友好的支持。我们国家之间的友好关系，在朝鲜人民反对美国干涉者、争取他们国家的自由与独立的激烈斗争中，得到进一步的加强。
    亲爱的约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇，朝鲜人民表示他们愿意继续以各种可能的方法来发展与加强我们两国人民之间的友好关系。
    朝鲜人民深切认识到加强朝苏友好的全部意义，并且了解到：只有得到伟大的苏联的兄弟般和无私的援助，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和平、独立地存在下去和它的繁荣才是可能的。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间的永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    光荣归于伟大的苏联——不可摧毁的世界和平、民主与社会主义的堡垒！                                                    
               朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成（签字）
                     一九五二年十月十日于平壤
