Title: "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" Successfully Concludes; Deputy Prime Minister Suranzabu Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Chinese Art Troupe

Author: 
Date: 1952-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 14th: The "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" was victoriously concluded on the 10th in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, and in the provincial capitals of all eighteen provinces.

The closing ceremony of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" in Ulaanbaatar was held at the State Theatre. The attendees included: Mongolian Deputy Prime Ministers Suranzabu, Maidar, and Sharap; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Susobaama; heads of various departments of the Mongolian government; heads of mass organizations; the chairman of the Ulaanbaatar City Labor Small Hural, Bag; and figures from the Mongolian cultural and artistic circles, totaling over 700 people. Ji Yatai, the Chinese Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic; Kim Yong-jin, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Novikov, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim; personnel from various embassies; Zhou Libo, head of the People's Republic of China Art Troupe; members of the Chinese Art Troupe; members of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe; and all members of the Chinese Industrial Exhibition Working Group also attended the closing ceremony.

Mongolian Deputy Prime Minister and Chairman of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" Preparatory Committee, Sharap, delivered a speech at the meeting, pointing out: The "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" was held on the occasion of the great festival of the Chinese people—the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. During the Friendship Fortnight, the 19th Congress of the Communist Party (Bolsheviks) of the Soviet Union was convened, and the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference was held in Beijing; at the same time, Mongolia and China signed an economic and cultural cooperation agreement. He said: The Mongolian people warmly welcome this agreement because our people know that this is of great significance for strengthening economic and cultural cooperation between Mongolia and China, consolidating and developing the friendship between the two countries, and safeguarding the cause of world peace.

Sharap then described the great achievements of the Friendship Fortnight, and pointed out: Through the work of the Friendship Fortnight, the working people of our country have further recognized the brilliant achievements of the great neighboring Chinese people. This is an important step in further strengthening economic and cultural exchanges between Mongolia and China. The work of the Friendship Fortnight has not only strengthened the profound friendship and permanent cooperation between Mongolia and China, but also strengthened the common cause of the two peoples and strengthened the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union against imperialism.

Ji Yatai, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, then spoke. He emphasized that the great achievements of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight," the visit to Beijing by the delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic government headed by Prime Minister Tsedenbal, and the signing of the economic and cultural cooperation agreement between China and Mongolia "all demonstrate that the close friendly and cooperative relationship between China and Mongolia has entered a new era."

Finally, Zhou Libo, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, spoke. He described the lofty friendship shown by the Mongolian people to the Chinese people during the Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight, and praised the great achievements made by the Mongolian people in the political, economic, and cultural fields under the correct leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and its leaders Sukhbaatar, Choibalsan, and Tsedenbal, and with the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union.

The closing ceremony ended victoriously in cheers of "Long live the unbreakable friendship between Mongolia and China" and warm applause. Afterwards, a gala evening was held, with the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe and the Mongolian State Theatre jointly performing wonderful programs.

[Report from this newspaper] According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 14th: The Preparatory Committee for the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Mongolia-Soviet Cultural Association held a reception on the evening of the 11th to celebrate the successful conclusion of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight." Mongolian Deputy Prime Minister and Chairman of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" Preparatory Committee, Sharap, spoke at the reception. He described the achievements of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight" and expressed his gratitude to all members of the Chinese Art Troupe, the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe, and the Chinese Industrial Exhibition Working Group who participated in the "Mongolia-China Friendship Fortnight." The reception proceeded in an atmosphere full of internationalism and warm friendship. The guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the unbreakable friendship between Mongolia and China, and to the health of Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Tsedenbal.

Also reported: Mongolian Deputy Prime Minister Suranzabu hosted a banquet on the evening of the 12th to bid farewell to the Art Troupe of the People's Republic of China. Before the banquet began, Mongolian Deputy Prime Minister Suranzabu, on behalf of the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, presented commemorative gifts to Zhou Libo, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and all members of the delegation. The commemorative gifts included esteemed Mongolian national costumes, embroidered portraits of Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao Zedong, a statue of Sukhbaatar, the founder of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Mongolian national arts and crafts. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### “蒙中友好旬”胜利闭幕  苏兰扎布副总理设宴欢送我文艺代表团

1952-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    “蒙中友好旬”胜利闭幕
    苏兰扎布副总理设宴欢送我文艺代表团
    【新华社乌兰巴托十四日电】“蒙中友好旬”于十日在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托和全国十八个省的省会宣告胜利闭幕。
    乌兰巴托的“蒙中友好旬”闭幕典礼在国家剧院举行。到会的有：蒙古副总理苏兰扎布、迈达尔、沙拉普，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记苏索巴马及蒙古政府各部门首长、群众团体负责人和乌兰巴托市劳动者小呼拉尔主席巴格以及蒙古文化艺术界人士等共七百余人。我国驻蒙古人民共和国大使吉雅泰、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使金龙镇、苏联临时代办诺维克夫及各使馆人员，以及中华人民共和国文艺代表团团长周立波、中国文艺代表团、中国歌舞团团员、中国工业展览会工作团全体人员也参加了闭幕典礼。
    蒙古副总理、“蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会主任沙拉普在会上致词指出：“蒙中友好旬”是在中国人民的伟大节日——中华人民共和国成立三周年纪念之际举行的，在友好旬期间，正值苏联共产党（布）召开第十九次代表大会、在北京召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议；与此同时，蒙中两国签订了经济及文化合作协定。他说：蒙古人民热烈欢迎这个协定，因为我国人民知道，这对于加强蒙中两国经济及文化的合作，巩固与发展两国间的友谊和保卫世界和平事业，都有重大意义。
    沙拉普接着叙述了友好旬的伟大收获，并指出：通过友好旬的工作，已使我国劳动人民进一步认识了伟大的邻邦中国人民的光辉成就。这是蒙中两国进一步加强经济及文化交流的重要步骤。友好旬的工作，不仅加强了蒙中两国的深厚友谊与永久的合作，而且也加强了两国人民的共同事业，加强了以苏联为首的反对帝国主义的和平民主阵营。
    我国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰继起致词。着重指出：“蒙中友好旬”的巨大成就、以泽登巴尔总理为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团的访问北京，以及中蒙两国签订了经济及文化合作协定“都说明了中蒙两国之间的亲密的友好合作关系，已进入一个新的时期”。
    最后由中国文艺代表团团长周立波讲话。他叙述了蒙中友好旬期间，蒙古人民对中国人民所表现的崇高的友谊，并盛赞蒙古人民在蒙古人民革命党及其领袖苏赫—巴托尔、乔巴山及泽登巴尔的正确领导以及苏联无私的帮助之下，在政治、经济及文化方面所获得的巨大成就。
    闭幕典礼在欢呼“蒙中两国牢不可破的友谊万岁”和热烈的掌声中胜利结束。会后举行了游艺晚会，由中国歌舞团与蒙古国家剧院联合演出精彩节目。
    【本报讯】据新华社乌兰巴托十四日电：蒙古人民共和国“蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会和蒙苏文化协会，于十一日晚举行招待会，庆祝“蒙中友好旬”胜利闭幕。蒙古人民共和国副总理、“蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会主任沙拉普在招待会上讲话。他叙述了“蒙中友好旬”的工作成就，并向参加“蒙中友好旬”的中国文艺代表团、中国歌舞团和中国工业展览会工作团的全体人员表示感谢。招待会在充满国际主义精神和热烈友好的气氛中进行。宾主频频举杯为蒙中两国牢不可破的友谊干杯，为斯大林、毛泽东、泽登巴尔的健康干杯。
    又讯：蒙古人民共和国副总理苏兰扎布于十二日晚设宴欢送中华人民共和国文艺代表团。宴会开始前，蒙古副总理苏兰扎布代表蒙古人民共和国政府向中国文艺代表团团长周立波及代表团全体团员赠送纪念礼品。纪念礼品有蒙古尊贵的民族服装，斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席绣像，蒙古人民共和国的缔造者苏赫—巴托尔塑像以及蒙古民族美术工艺品等。宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
