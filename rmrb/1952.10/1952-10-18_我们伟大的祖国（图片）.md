Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The new Tanggu Port was opened yesterday, two and a half months ahead of schedule. Four ships, ranging from over three thousand tons to over ten thousand tons, including the Changchun, Hai'an, Beiguang, and Bellap, successively sailed into the port amidst the enthusiastic cheers of over four thousand employees of the new port. This is a scene of the waist drum team and the Yangko team of the new port employees welcoming the ships entering the port.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    塘沽新港已提前两个半月于昨日开港。从三千多吨到万吨以上的长春、海安、北光、贝拉普号等四艘轮船，当日在新港四千多职工的热烈欢呼声中，陆续驶入港口。这是新港职工的腰鼓队和秧歌队正在欢迎轮船进港的情形。
