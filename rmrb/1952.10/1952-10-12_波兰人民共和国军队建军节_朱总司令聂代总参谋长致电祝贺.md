Title: Army Day of the Polish People's Republic: Commander-in-Chief Zhu and Acting Chief of General Staff Nie Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-12
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 11th) October 12th is Army Day of the Polish People's Republic. Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission of China, sent telegrams to Konstantin Rokossovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, and Colonel General Jerzy Kirchmajer, Chief of the General Staff of the Polish Army and Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, respectively, to express their congratulations. The telegrams are as follows:

To Comrade Konstantin Rokossovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic:

	On the occasion of Army Day of the Polish Army, I, on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and myself, extend my most sincere congratulations to you and all members of the Polish Army.

	I wish the Polish Army further achievements in the cause of safeguarding the peaceful construction of your motherland.

					Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
					October 11, 1952, in Beijing

To Comrade Jerzy Kirchmajer, Chief of the General Staff of the Polish Army and Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic:

	On the occasion of Army Day of the Polish Army, I, on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend my warmest congratulations to you, and wish the friendship between the armies of China and Poland will become even closer day by day.

	Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission of the People's Republic of China

					October 11, 1952, in Beijing

### 波兰人民共和国军队建军节  朱总司令聂代总参谋长致电祝贺

1952-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰人民共和国军队建军节
    朱总司令聂代总参谋长致电祝贺
    【新华社十一日讯】十月十二日为波兰军队建军节，中国人民解放军朱德总司令与我国中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会聂荣臻代总参谋长分别致电波兰人民共和国国防部部长罗科索夫斯基及波兰军队总参谋长、波兰人民共和国国防部副部长柯里奇茨，表示祝贺。电文如下：波兰人民共和国国防部部长罗科索夫斯基同志：
    值此波兰军队建军节之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军及我本人，向您及波兰军队全体人员致最衷心的祝贺。
    谨祝波兰军队在为保卫你们祖国和平建设的事业中获得进一步的成就。
                             中国人民解放军总司令　朱　德   
                             一九五二年十月十一日于北京波兰军队总参谋长、波兰人民共和国国防部副部长柯里奇茨同志：
    值此波兰军队建军节之际，谨代表中国人民解放军向您致最热烈的祝贺，并愿中波两国军队友谊日臻紧密。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长
                                 聂荣臻                                                            
                        一九五二年十月十一日于北京
