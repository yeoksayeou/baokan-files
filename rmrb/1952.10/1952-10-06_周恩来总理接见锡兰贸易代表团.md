Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Ceylon Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th.
Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, received the head of the Ceylon Trade Delegation, Mr. Seneratne Gunewardene, on the 4th.

Also present at the reception were members of the Ceylon Trade Delegation: Fonseka, Kumaraswamy, Jayanetti, Silva, Martenstyn, and Duraiswamy.

Also present at the reception were: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji Zhuang, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

### 周恩来总理接见锡兰贸易代表团

1952-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见锡兰贸易代表团
  　【新华社五日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于四日接见锡兰贸易代表团团长沈纳那亚克。　同时受接见的还有：锡兰贸易代表团团员方席卡、柯马拉赛密、贾雅诺特尼、席尔瓦、马廷斯亭、杜锐斯瓦密。　接见时在座的有：政务院副总理陈云，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫。
