Title: A Tribute to China
Author: Kaidelong
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 3

    A Tribute to China
    Kaidelong, Head of the Icelandic Delegation to China

    Imir [Note 1] pays tribute to Pangu,
    Saga Island [Note 2] pays tribute to China,
    The smallest poet of the smallest country pays tribute to the greatest poet of the greatest country—Mao Zedong, who infuses new life into the great heart of the East!
    For centuries the sun mocked the poor,
    They toiled diligently and enduringly in the fields of the oppressors,
    Sweat and tears flowing into the yellow earth.
    For centuries the moon mocked the poor in their thatched huts,
    They lost all hope and dreams.
    The earth finally burned in agony,
    Pioneers emerged like radiant willow shoots from the red mountains,
    Brilliant dawn shone on Jinggang Mountain,
    —The miraculous Long March
    Leading the way to liberation;
    How precious the dew on the hair of the martyrs,
    How radiant the eyes of those who continue forward.
    Mao Zedong, carrying the harp of spring, walks at the forefront,
    With tea on his tongue,
    Mulberries in one hand,
    Grains of rice in the other,
    Saying:
    This is the truth of the world.
    Now, the heroes, the great victors, stand tall on the red imperial city,
    Shouting liberation to the world, shouting peace to the world, broadcasting the song of life to the world, with Mao Zedong as the lead singer. Bless those who rest in the earth! But thunder and lightning come from afar: the West remains barbaric, the shepherd of death still gallops across the azure sky, casting flames upon the heads of the people—even deceiving our blue island into his murderous machine. Punish those who trample upon the hearts of men! The poor people of Saga Island, who love grass and stones, who love stories and poems, who are lovers of freedom and peace, extend fraternal greetings to the comrades of the new China:
    May your shining example, your new life, awaken the peoples of the world,
    May the song of Mao Zedong triumph throughout the world,
    May your grains and our pastures become the truth of the world,
    May the sun laugh with us all, the moon smile upon us all, may a brand new golden age arrive!

    [Note 1] Ymir is a giant in Icelandic mythology, said to have created the world with his blood, flesh, and bones.
    [Note 2] "Saga" is an ancient Icelandic epic praising the lives of heroes; later, people called Iceland "Saga Island."
    —Translator's Note

### 向中国致敬
凯德隆
1952-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

    向中国致敬
    冰岛访华代表团团长  凯德隆
    伊米尔〔注一〕向盘古致敬，
    萨嘎岛〔注二〕向中华致敬，
    最小国的最渺小的诗人向最大国的最伟大的诗人——将新生命注入东方伟大的心脏的毛泽东致敬！
    千百年来太阳嘲笑着穷人，
    他们辛勤地坚忍地劳动在压迫者的田地里，
    汗水和眼泪流入黄色的泥土。
    千百年来月亮嘲笑着茅舍里的穷人，
    他们丧失了一切希望和梦想。
    大地终于痛苦得燃烧起来，
    先驱者好像光辉的柳芽从红色的山岩中冒出来，
    灿烂的曙光照射在井冈山上，
    ——奇迹般的长征
    导向解放的途径；
    烈士们发上的露珠多么宝贵，
    继续前进的人们的眼睛多么光辉。
    毛泽东怀抱着春天的竖琴，走在最前面，
    舌头上放着茶叶，
    一手拿着桑葚，
    一手握着谷粒，
    说道：
    这就是世界的真理。
    现在，英雄们，伟大的胜利者们，正挺立在红色的皇城上，
    向全世界高呼解放，向全世界高呼和平，向全世界播送着生命之歌，那领唱的歌手就是毛泽东。祝福那些安息在地下的人们！可是远方却传来鸣雷和闪电：西方仍旧是野蛮，死亡的牧人还在蔚蓝的天际奔驰，在人们头上投掷着火焰。——甚至把我们蓝色的岛屿也骗进他那杀人的机关。惩办那些践踏人心的家伙们！萨嘎岛的穷苦的人们，是喜爱草和山石的人，是喜爱故事和诗的人，是自由与和平的热爱者，谨向新中国的战友们致兄弟的问候：
    愿你们光辉的榜样，你们的新生能把世界各民族唤醒，
    愿毛泽东的歌声在全世界获胜，
    愿你们的谷子和我们的牧草成为世界的真理，
    愿太阳对我们一齐欢笑，月亮对我们大家微
    笑，愿崭新的黄金时代来到！
    〔注一〕伊米尔是冰岛神话中的巨人，传说用他的
    血、肉和骨骼创造了世界。
    〔注二〕“萨嘎”是冰岛古代歌颂英雄生活的史
    诗，后来人们称冰岛为“萨嘎岛”。
    ——译者注
