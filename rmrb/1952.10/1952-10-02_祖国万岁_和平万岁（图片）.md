Title: Long Live the Motherland, Long Live Peace (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-02
Page: 4

    Long Live the Motherland, Long Live Peace
    On October 1st, the people of the capital warmly celebrated the third National Day. Chairman Mao reviewed the advancing troops from the rostrum at Tiananmen Square.
    (Photo by Hou Bo)
    The Air Force and Tank Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed.
    After the parade began, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De reviewed the troops by car, passing in front of the infantry formation of the People's Liberation Army.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Sheng Jirun)
    The motorcycle unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Qi Guanshan)
    The Naval Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed.
    The Cavalry Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed.
    The athletes marching in the parade.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Chen Bo)
    The rocket artillery unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed.
    The armored unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army being reviewed.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Qi Guanshan)
    The masses participating in the parade.
    (The photos in this issue are taken by our reporter Zhu Bo, unless otherwise noted.)
    At the end of the celebration, the Young Pioneers and cadres from various institutions rushed towards the rostrum, cheering: Long Live the People's Republic of China! Long Live Chairman Mao!

    祖国万岁  和平万岁

    （新华社稿·盛继润摄）
    受检阅的中国人民解放军摩托车部队。
    （新华社稿·齐观山摄）
    受检阅的中国人民解放军海军部队。
