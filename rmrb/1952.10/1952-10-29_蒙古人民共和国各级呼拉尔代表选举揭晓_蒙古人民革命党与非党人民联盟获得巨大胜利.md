Title: Election Results of People's Hural Representatives at All Levels in the Mongolian People's Republic Announced: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Non-Party People's Alliance Achieve Great Victory
Author:
Date: 1952-10-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 28th: TASS reports: The Mongolian People's Republic held elections for representatives to local organs of state power on October 19. According to the final official materials, the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance won the election. In this election, the Mongolian people once again demonstrated their unity around the People's Revolutionary Party and their loyalty to the socialist cause. All 32,705 representative candidates from the leagues, cities, districts, and villages Hural (i.e., People's Congress) nominated by the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance were unanimously elected as representatives of the working people. Voters participating in the election accounted for 99.91 percent of the total number of voters, of whom 99.89 percent voted for candidates of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance. Among the elected representatives, members and alternate members of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party accounted for 27.5 percent, non-Party masses accounted for 72.5 percent, and women accounted for 23.1 percent.

### 蒙古人民共和国各级呼拉尔代表选举揭晓  蒙古人民革命党与非党人民联盟获得巨大胜利

1952-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国各级呼拉尔代表选举揭晓
    蒙古人民革命党与非党人民联盟获得巨大胜利
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国在十月十九日举行了国家政权地方机构的代表选举。根据官方最后的材料，蒙古人民革命党员与非党人民联盟在选举中获得胜利。蒙古人民在这次选举中又一次表现出他们团结在人民革命党的周围以及对社会主义事业的忠诚。由蒙古人民革命党员与非党人民联盟提出的各盟、各市、各区、各乡村呼拉尔（按即人民代表大会）的三万二千七百零五名代表候选人一致被选为劳动人民代表。参加投票的选民占选民总数的百分之九十九点九一，其中百分之九十九点八九的投票人投票选举蒙古人民革命党员与非党人民联盟的候选人。选出的代表中，蒙古人民革命党党员和候补党员占百分之二十七点五，非党群众占百分之七十二点五，妇女占百分之二十三点一。
