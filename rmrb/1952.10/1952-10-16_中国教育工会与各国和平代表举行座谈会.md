Title: Chinese Educational Workers' Union Holds Symposium with Peace Delegates from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1952-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The Chinese Educational Workers' Union held a symposium on the afternoon of the 14th with representatives from various countries attending the Asian and Pacific regions' peace conference, concerning the situation of the educational workers' union in New China. Sal Gim Kebleier, the representative of Delanou, the Secretary-General of the Educational Workers' International, who was attending the peace conference, also attended the symposium. The symposium began with a welcoming speech by Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union. This was followed by Liu Ziju, Vice-Chairman, who introduced the situation of the Educational Workers' Union in New China. He spoke about the organization of educational workers in New China, the welfare of teachers, the struggle of educational workers to serve the working people and defend world peace, and so on. In addition, he also spoke about the deeds of model teachers in New China and the issue of ideological study. The delegates from various countries were very interested in Liu Ziju's speech and raised many questions about the actual situation of the Educational Workers' Union in New China, which were answered by Wu Yuzhang and Liu Ziju. Sal Gim Kebleier, the representative of Delanou, the Secretary-General of the Educational Workers' International, also spoke at the meeting, introducing the situation of the Educational Workers' International and its efforts for the cause of peace. The symposium concluded with those present shouting "Long Live Peace" in Chinese.

Furthermore, on the 14th, the delegates from various countries attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference also visited the Palace Museum, the Yonghe Temple, the National Beijing Library, the Normal University, and the Beijing Municipal First Girls' Middle School.

### 中国教育工会与各国和平代表举行座谈会

1952-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中国教育工会与各国和平代表举行座谈会
    【新华社十四日讯】中国教育工会在十四日下午和出席亚洲及太平洋区域的各国代表举行关于新中国的教育工会情况的座谈会。列席和平会议的教育工会国际总书记德拉努的代表萨尔·吉姆·凯布列尔也出席了座谈会。座谈会由中国教育工会全国委员会主席吴玉章致欢迎辞。接着由副主席刘子久介绍了新中国教育工会的情况。他谈到了新中国教育工作者的组织、教师的福利事业、教育工作者为劳动人民服务和保卫世界和平的斗争等。此外他还谈到了新中国模范教师的事迹和思想学习问题。各国代表对于刘子久的讲话感到很大的兴趣，并就新中国教育工会方面的实际情况提出许多问题，当由吴玉章、刘子久作答。教育工会国际总书记德拉努的代表萨尔·吉姆·凯布列尔也在会上讲话，他介绍教育工会国际的情况及其为和平事业而作的努力。座谈会最后在到会者用中国话高呼的“和平万岁”声中结束。
    又讯：出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各国代表十四日还分别到故宫、雍和宫、国立北京图书馆、师范大学和北京市立第一女子中学等处参观。
