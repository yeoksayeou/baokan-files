Hikmet's Three Recent Works from Beijing

    The Stone Boat in Kunming Lake
    In Kunming Lake there is a boat,
    Its hull is carved from stone.
    All of China's sails
              are filled with wind,
    Only this boat feels lonely—
              It cannot move.

    Beijing's Pigeons
    My pigeons are whiter than mother's milk,
    For you to nest,
    Beijing gives you the best place:
    On the bright red pillars.

    Pigeons and Thirty-Seven Branches
    At the assembly hall there are thirty-seven national flags,
    These are thirty-seven branches of a large tree.
    White pigeons freely flap their wings and fly among these thirty-seven branches.
                                                (Translated by Xiao San)

### 希克梅特北京近作三首

1952-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    希克梅特北京近作三首
    昆明湖中的石船
    昆明湖中有一只船，
    船身是石头所凿成。
    中国所有的风帆
              都充满了风，
    只有这只船感觉得孤凄—
              它走不动。
    北京的鸽子
    我的鸽子比母亲的奶汁还白，
    为了你作巢，
    北京给你最好的地方：
    在鲜红的大柱上。
    鸽子和三十七个树枝
    在大会场有三十七树国旗，
    这是一棵大树的三十七个树枝。
    白鸽在这三十七个树枝中间自由地振翼而飞翔。
                                                （萧三译）
