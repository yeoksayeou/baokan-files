Title: Commander He Long Receives Head of Czech Army Performing Arts Troupe
Author:
Date: 1952-10-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 19th. Commander He Long of the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army received Jaroslav Veselý, head of the Czech Vit Nejedlý Army Performing Arts Troupe, who came to the Southwest for visits and performances, on the 17th. Mr. Reigler, First Secretary of the Czech Embassy in China, who accompanied the troupe to the Southwest, was also present. Also present were Li Fuke, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Southwest Military Region, Hu Chi, Secretary-General of the Political Department, Chen Peiqin, Minister of the Cultural Department of the Political Department, and Ren Jianqing, Deputy Minister. Commander He warmly received them and, at their request, told the story of the "August 1st" Nanchang Uprising and the liberation of Tibet, in which Commander He personally participated and led.
　　
　　At 3 p.m., Li Da, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region, hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the troupe. More than 300 senior cadres of the Southwest Military Region, literary and art workers of the troops, combat heroes, and model workers were invited to attend.
　　
　　In the evening, the troupe gave its first performance at the Chongqing Dockworkers' Building. The audience warmly welcomed it.

### 贺龙司令员接见捷军队文工团团长

1952-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙司令员接见捷军队文工团团长
    【新华社重庆十九日电】中国人民解放军西南军区贺龙司令员于十七日接见前来西南访问及演出的捷克斯洛伐克维特·尼耶德利军队文工团团长雅罗斯拉夫·维赛利。陪该团前来西南的捷驻华大使馆一等秘书赖格勒陪同前往。在座的有西南军区副参谋长李夫克、政治部秘书长胡痴、政治部文化部部长陈裴琴、副部长任剑青等人。贺司令员亲切热情地接待了他们，并应他们的请求，讲述了贺司令员亲身参加领导的“八一”南昌起义以及西藏解放的经过。
    下午三时，西南军区李达副司令员设宴欢迎该团全体团员。应邀作陪的有西南军区高级干部及部队文艺工作者、战斗英雄、模范工作者等三百多人。
    晚间，该团在重庆市搬运工人大厦作首次公演。观众热烈欢迎。
