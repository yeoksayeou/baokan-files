Title: Mongolian "Truth Daily" Publishes Editorial Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1952-10-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th dispatch from Ulan Bator: The "Truth Daily" of the Mongolian People's Republic published an editorial on October 8th entitled "Agreement of Peace and Friendship," celebrating the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The editorial stated: The Mongolian people excitedly welcome the good news of the signing of the agreement. Celebrations are being held in factories, institutions, and schools, in labor production associations and state-owned farms, and among herders in the centers of sums (counties) and baghs (districts). Speakers at the meetings expressed joy for the signing of this agreement and expressed their determination to do their best to consolidate the friendship between the two countries.

The editorial pointed out: The Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement is in line with the wishes of the people of both countries and in line with the interests of Mongolia and China, which are in the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism headed by the great Soviet Union and Comrade Stalin, a good friend of the people of Mongolia and China. This agreement will help consolidate Mongolia's economic strength and further develop its culture.

The editorial stated: The conclusion of the economic and cultural cooperation agreement between our two countries is a practical manifestation of our two countries' desire for peace. It once again shows that neither the Mongolian people nor the Chinese people want war. What we need is peace.

The editorial continued: The conclusion of this agreement and the warm response it has received among workers, farmers, and intellectuals prove that the Mongolian people, under the leadership of the People's Revolutionary Party, are loyal to the idea of friendship between all nations and the idea of internationalism, and they will continue to develop this friendship and wholeheartedly defend peace in the Far East and the world.

The editorial emphasized: This agreement has great significance in consolidating the cause of friendship between China and Mongolia and the Soviet Union, and in absorbing the rich experience of Soviet socialist construction.

### 蒙古“真理报”发表社论  庆祝中蒙经济及文化合作协定的签订

1952-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古“真理报”发表社论
    庆祝中蒙经济及文化合作协定的签订
    【新华社二十八日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国“真理报”于十月八日发表题为“和平与友谊的协定”的社论，庆祝中蒙经济及文化合作协定的签订。社论说：蒙古人民兴奋地迎接签订协定的喜讯。在工厂、机关和学校里，在劳动生产组合和国营农场中，在苏木（县）和巴戈（区）中心的牧人中间纷纷举行庆祝会。在会上讲演的人表示为这个协定的签订而欢欣，并表示决心要竭力巩固两国的友谊。
    社论指出：蒙中两国经济及文化合作协定是符合两国人民的愿望的，符合处在以伟大的苏联和蒙中两国人民的良友斯大林同志为首的和平、民主和社会主义阵营中的蒙中两国的利益的。这个协定将帮助蒙古经济力量的巩固和文化的更大发展。
    社论说：两国缔结经济及文化合作协定是我们两国和平愿望的行动表现。它再次表明：无论是蒙古人民或中国人民都不要战争。我们需要的是和平。
    社论接着说：这个协定的缔结及其在工人、农民和知识分子中间得到的热烈反应证明：在人民革命党的领导下的蒙古人民是忠于各国人民友好的思想和国际主义思想的，他们将继续发展这种友谊，全力捍卫远东与世界和平。
    社论强调说：这次缔结的协定在巩固中国和蒙古与苏联的友好事业中，在吸取丰富的苏联社会主义建设工作经验中都具有重大的意义。
