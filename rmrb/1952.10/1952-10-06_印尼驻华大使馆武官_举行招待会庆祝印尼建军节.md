Title: Indonesian Military Attaché to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Indonesian Armed Forces Day
Author:
Date: 1952-10-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 5th—The military attaché of the Republic of Indonesia to China, Lieutenant Colonel Salim, held a reception in Beijing on the 5th to celebrate Indonesian Armed Forces Day.

Those invited to attend included: Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office; Li Zhaobing, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Huang Yuan, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department; Liu Xiyuan, Deputy Director of the Youth League; and Zhu Fengxi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Department, and others.

Also invited to attend were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Protocol Department; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, as well as military attachés and deputy military attachés from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and Poland to China.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 印尼驻华大使馆武官  举行招待会庆祝印尼建军节

1952-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼驻华大使馆武官
    举行招待会庆祝印尼建军节
    【新华社五日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻中国大使馆武官沙林中校于五日在北京举行招待会，庆祝印度尼西亚建军节。
    应邀到会的有：中国人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、总政治部副主任萧华、办公厅主任萧向荣、文化部副部长李兆炳、宣传部副部长黄远、青年部副部长刘西元、对外联络处副处长朱凤熙等。
    应邀到会的尚有外交部副部长章汉夫、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、文化部副部长丁西林、典礼局局长余心清、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、亚洲司副司长何英、交际处副处长沈平及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰驻华正副武官等。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
