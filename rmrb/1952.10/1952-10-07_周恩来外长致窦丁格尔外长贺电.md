Title: Zhou Enlai's Message of Congratulations to Foreign Minister Doutinguer

Author: 
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 1

Zhou Enlai's Message of Congratulations to Foreign Minister Doutinguer

To Comrade Doutinguer, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic:

    On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, please accept my sincere congratulations, and I wish the forces of peace and progress in Germany greater victory in the struggle to conclude a peace treaty and restore German unity.

                     Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                           October 5, 1952, Beijing

### 周恩来外长致窦丁格尔外长贺电

1952-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长致窦丁格尔外长贺电德意志民主共和国外交部部长窦丁格尔同志：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立三周年之际，请接受我诚挚的祝贺，并祝德国和平与进步的力量在争取缔结和约与恢复德国统一的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
                     中华人民共和国外交部部长　周恩来
                           一九五二年十月五日北京
