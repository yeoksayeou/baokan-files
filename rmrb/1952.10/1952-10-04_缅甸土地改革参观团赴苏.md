Title: Burmese Land Reform Delegation Departs for Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: The Burmese Land Reform Delegation, after a month-long visit to China, departed from Beijing by plane at 7:00 AM on October 3rd for Moscow.

    Among those who saw them off at the airport were: Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government; Li Shijun, Director of the Pest and Disease Control Department; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department; Chen Ying, Commissioner; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; Qian Weichang, Vice President, and others.

    Also at the airport to see them off were Wu La Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, and members of the embassy staff.

### 缅甸土地改革参观团赴苏

1952-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸土地改革参观团赴苏
    【新华社三日讯】缅甸土地改革参观团来我国参观为时一个月，已于十月三日晨七时乘飞机离北京赴莫斯科。
    到机场欢送的有：中央人民政府农业部副部长杨显东、病虫害防治司司长李世俊，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、亚洲司副司长何英、专员陈应、交际处处长王倬如，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长等。
    到机场欢送的还有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂及大使馆馆员。
