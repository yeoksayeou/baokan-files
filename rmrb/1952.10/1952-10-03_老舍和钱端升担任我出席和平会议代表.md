Title: Lao She and Qian Duansheng to Serve as Representatives to the Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Due to illness, Nan Hanchen and Ding Ling, the Chinese representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, will be replaced by Lao She and Qian Duansheng.

Original: 


### 老舍和钱端升担任我出席和平会议代表

1952-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    老舍和钱端升担任我出席和平会议代表
    【新华社二日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表南汉宸和丁玲因病不能参加会议，已改由老舍和钱端升担任。
