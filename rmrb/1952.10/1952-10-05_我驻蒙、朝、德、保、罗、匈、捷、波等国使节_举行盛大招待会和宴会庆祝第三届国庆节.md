Title: Our Ambassadors to Mongolia, North Korea, Germany, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and Other Countries Hold Grand Receptions and Banquets to Celebrate the Third National Day

Author:
Date: 1952-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Our ambassadors to Mongolia, Ji Yatai; Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Gan Yetao; Head of the Diplomatic Mission to the German Democratic Republic with the rank of Ambassador, Ji Pengfei; the Embassy in Bulgaria; the Embassy in the Romanian People's Republic; Ambassador to the Hungarian People's Republic, Huang Zhen; Ambassador to the Czechoslovak Republic, Tan Xilin; and Ambassador to the Polish People's Republic, Zeng Yongquan, respectively held receptions and banquets on the 1st to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Among those invited to attend the reception held by our Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic, Ji Yatai, were: Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Suranzhab, and heads of various departments of the Mongolian government, heads of mass organizations, and others, totaling more than 250 people. Ambassador Ji Yatai introduced the achievements of the new China in various aspects of construction over the past three years at the reception. An entertainment evening was held after the reception.

    More than 350 people attended the grand banquet held by our Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Gan Yetao, in Pyongyang. The Korean Democratic People's Republic was represented by: Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Kim Tu-bong; Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Pak Hon-yong; Deputy Prime Ministers Hong Myong-hui and Ho Ka-i; senior generals of the People's Army; combat heroes; and heads of various democratic parties and social organizations. Diplomatic officials from various countries to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea also attended the banquet.

    Among those invited to attend the banquet held by Ji Pengfei, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of our country to the German Democratic Republic with the rank of Ambassador, were: President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck; Chairman of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann; Acting Prime Minister Heinrich Rau; Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke; Foreign Minister Doutingge; and representatives of various agencies and people's organizations, labor heroes, and celebrities, totaling more than 600 people. Chairman of the German Communist Party, Lehmann, was also invited to the banquet. Heads and members of the diplomatic missions of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries to Germany were also invited to the banquet. General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Saillant, and representatives of textile unions from the Soviet Union and other countries attending the Second International Congress of Textile and Garment Workers' Unions were also invited to the banquet. The delegation from our country attending the International Congress of Textile and Garment Workers' Unions, the delegation participating in the Leipzig Fair, and representatives of cooperatives visiting Germany also attended.

    Among those who attended the reception held by our embassy in Bulgaria were personnel of the Bulgarian government headed by Bulgarian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Raiko Damyanov, combat heroes, and labor models, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Bulgaria, totaling more than 500 people.

    Among those invited to attend the banquet held by our embassy in the Romanian People's Republic were: Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, Groza, as well as other senior officials and heads of the Romanian government, the Romanian Workers' Party, and people's organizations. Also present were heads of Romanian scientific, cultural, and artistic organizations, writers, and many worker representatives and labor heroes. Envoys and Chargés d'affaires from various countries to Romania were also invited. At the beginning of the banquet, our "May Day" film was shown, and a dance party was held after the banquet.

    Among the guests invited to attend the reception held by our Ambassador to the Hungarian People's Republic, Huang Zhen, at the Budapest Park Club were: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, Rákosi, and Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, Dobi, and more than 600 others.

    Among the guests invited to attend the reception held by our Ambassador to the Czechoslovak Republic, Tan Xilin, were: Deputy Prime Ministers of the Czechoslovak Republic, Ďuriš, Fierlinger, and Kiseľý, secretaries of the World Peace Council, and diplomatic representatives of various countries that have diplomatic relations with our country, totaling more than 1,000 people. A documentary about the May Day celebration in Beijing was shown after the reception.

    Among those invited to attend the grand reception held by our Ambassador to the Polish People's Republic, Zeng Yongquan, at the Warsaw National Theatre were: Prime Minister of the Polish People's Republic, Cyrankiewicz, and diplomatic representatives of various countries that have diplomatic relations with our country, totaling more than 700 people.

### 我驻蒙、朝、德、保、罗、匈、捷、波等国使节  举行盛大招待会和宴会庆祝第三届国庆节

1952-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻蒙、朝、德、保、罗、匈、捷、波等国使节
    举行盛大招待会和宴会庆祝第三届国庆节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：我国驻蒙古人民共和国大使吉雅泰、驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆代办甘野陶、驻德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞、驻保加利亚大使馆、驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆、驻匈牙利人民共和国大使黄镇、驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使谭希林、驻波兰人民共和国大使曾涌泉分别于一日举行招待会和宴会，庆祝中华人民共和国建国三周年。
    应邀参加我国驻蒙古人民共和国大使吉雅泰举行的招待会的有：蒙古人民共和国副总理苏兰扎布及蒙古政府各部门首长、各群众团体负责人等二百五十余人。吉雅泰大使在招待会上介绍了新中国三年来各方面建设的成就。会后举行了游艺晚会。
    出席我国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆代办甘野陶在平壤举行的盛大宴会的共三百五十多人。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国方面出席的有：最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相兼外务相朴宪永，内阁副首相洪命熹、许嘉谊，人民军高级将领，战斗英雄和各民主政党、社会团体负责人等。出席宴会的还有各国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的外交官。
    应邀出席我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞举行的宴会的有：德意志民主共和国总统皮克，人民议会主席狄克曼，代总理亨利·劳，副总理努舍克，外交部长窦丁格尔及各机关、各人民团体的代表，劳动英雄与社会名流共计六百多人。德国共产党主席雷曼也应邀赴宴。应邀赴宴的还有苏联及人民民主国家驻德外交使团的团长与团员。应邀赴宴的还有世界工会联合会总书记赛扬以及出席纺织服装工人工会国际第二次代表大会的苏联和其他国家纺织工会代表。我国出席纺织服装工人工会国际大会的代表团和参加来比锡博览会的代表以及访德的合作社代表也都到会。
    出席我国驻保加利亚大使馆举行的招待会的有以保加利亚部长会议副主席拉一科·达姆扬诺夫为首的保加利亚政府人员，战斗英雄和劳动模范，以及各国驻保加利亚外交使节，共计五百多人。
    应邀出席我国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆举行的宴会的有：罗马尼亚国民会议主席团主席格罗查以及罗马尼亚政府、罗马尼亚工人党、人民团体的其他高级官员和负责人。到会的还有罗马尼亚科学文化艺术团体的负责人、作家以及工人代表和劳动英雄多人。应邀到会的还有各国驻罗马尼亚使节和代办。宴会开始时放映了我国“五一”节影片，宴会后并举行舞会。
    应邀参加我国驻匈牙利人民共和国大使黄镇在布达佩斯公园俱乐部举行的招待会的来宾有：匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席拉科西和匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比等六百多人。
    应邀参加我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使谭希林举行的招待会的来宾有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理杜兰斯基、费林格和基斯里，世界和平理事会书记处书记以及与我国建交的各国外交代表等一千多人。会后放映了关于北京五一节的纪录片。
    应邀参加我国驻波兰人民共和国大使曾涌泉在华沙国家剧院举行的盛大的招待会的有：波兰人民共和国总理西伦凯维兹以及和我国有外交关系的各国外交代表等七百多人。
