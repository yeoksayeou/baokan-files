Title: Stalin's Speech Printed in 5 Million Copies in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-10-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 26th: TASS, Moscow, October 25th: J.V. Stalin's speech at the closing session of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has been printed by the Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House into pamphlets, with 5 million copies distributed in Moscow.

### 斯大林同志的演说苏联印行五百万册

1952-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林同志的演说苏联印行五百万册
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：约·维·斯大林在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会闭幕会议上的演说，已由苏联国立政治书籍出版局印成小册子，在莫斯科发行五百万册。
