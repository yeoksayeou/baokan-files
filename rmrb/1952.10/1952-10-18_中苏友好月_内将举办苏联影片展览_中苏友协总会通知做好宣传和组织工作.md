Title: Soviet Film Exhibition to Be Held During "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month"; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters Issues Notice to Do a Good Job of Publicity and Organization
Author:
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: During the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" to be held nationwide from November 7th to December 6th this year, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters and the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government will jointly host the "Soviet Film Exhibition." The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has issued a notice to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association organizations at all levels for this purpose.

The notice said: The "Soviet Film Exhibition" will be held from November 7th to December 6th simultaneously in fifty-seven cities across the country, including Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, Shijiazhuang, Xinxiang, Taiyuan, Tangshan, Zhangjiakou, Guihua, Shenyang, Andong, Fushun, Anshan, Harbin, Lushun-Dalian, Jilin, Changchun, Qiqihar, Mudanjiang, Jinzhou, Jiamusi, Ulanhot, Benxi, Shanghai, Nanjing, Yangzhou, Wuxi, Suzhou, Hangzhou, Hefei, Xuzhou, Qingdao, Jinan, Fuzhou, Xiamen, Hankou, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Changsha, Hengyang, Nanchang, Guangzhou, Shantou, Nanning, Liuzhou, Guilin, Zhanjiang, Chongqing, Chengdu, Kunming, Guiyang, Luzhou, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Dihua, Yinchuan, and Xining. The latest Soviet color films will be shown, such as "The Unforgettable Year 1919" and "Gold Star Hero," and opening ceremonies for the Soviet Film Exhibition will be held separately in each exhibition city. All localities should widely mobilize mobile film projection teams to widely screen Soviet films or additionally screen "Today's Soviet Union" Nos. 1, 2, 7, and 8, "Agricultural News" Nos. 1 to 4, and "Be Friends with Science" during the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." All cinemas in cities that do not hold exhibitions should schedule Soviet films. The film projection teams of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, cultural and educational, trade union, and military systems will also screen Soviet films and the latest Soviet color films. The China Film Distribution and Exhibition Corporation is responsible for scheduling and supplying films and promotional materials. All general and provincial branches should actively cooperate with local film distribution agencies at all levels to vigorously promote the work and organize the audience so that the broad membership of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and people from all walks of life can more widely and deeply understand Soviet films in this exhibition, thereby further consolidating the profound friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

The notice requires that the preparations, progress, and summaries of this work by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association organizations in various localities be included in the work of "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" and reported to the National Headquarters at any time.

### “中苏友好月”内将举办苏联影片展览  中苏友协总会通知做好宣传和组织工作

1952-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    “中苏友好月”内将举办苏联影片展览
    中苏友协总会通知做好宣传和组织工作
    【新华社十六日讯】在今年十一月七日到十二月六日全国举行的“中苏友好月”中，中苏友好协会总会和中央人民政府文化部将联合主办“苏联影片展览”。中苏友好协会已为此向各级中苏友协组织发出通知。
    通知说：“苏联影片展览”自十一月七日起至十二月六日止，在全国五十七个城市北京、天津、保定、石家庄、新乡、太原、唐山、张家口、归绥、沈阳、安东、抚顺、鞍山、哈尔滨、旅大、吉林、长春、齐齐哈尔、牡丹江、锦州、佳木斯、乌兰浩特、本溪、上海、南京、扬州、无锡、苏州、杭州、合肥、徐州、青岛、济南、福州、厦门、汉口、郑州、开封、长沙、衡阳、南昌、广州、汕头、南宁、柳州、桂林、湛江、重庆、成都、昆明、贵阳、泸州、西安、兰州、迪化、银川、西宁同时举行，映出最新的苏联彩色影片，如：“难忘的一九一九年”、“金星英雄”等片，并在展览各城市分别举行苏联影片展览开幕式。各地应广泛动员流动放映队，在“中苏友好月”内，广泛放映苏联影片或加映一九五○年“今日苏联”第一、二、七、八号，“农业新闻”第一到四期和“与科学为友”。凡不举行展览的各城市的影院，一律排映苏联影片。中苏友好协会、文教、工会、部队系统的放映队，也将一律放映苏联影片和最新的苏联彩色影片。关于影片及宣传品，由中国影片经理公司负责调度及供应。各总分会、省市分会应主动地协同各地各级影片发行机构，大力做好宣传工作和组织观众的工作，使中苏友好协会的广大会员和各阶层群众能够在这次展览中更广泛、深入地理解苏联电影，从而进一步巩固中苏两国人民之间的深厚友谊。
    通知要求各地中苏友好协会组织关于这一工作的准备、进行及总结，列入“中苏友好月”工作中，随时报告总会。
