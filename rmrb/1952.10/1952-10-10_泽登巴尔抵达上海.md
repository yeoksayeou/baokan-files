Title: Tsedenbal Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th. Tsedenbal, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic and Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Shanghai on the 8th. Accompanying him were Lhamsuren, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; Jalkhasaikhan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and his wife; Purbuzhal, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; and Dulebjin, Deputy Director of the Department of the Orient. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, accompanied them. Welcoming them at the station were Tan Zhenlin, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee; Wu Kejian, Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee; Shu Tong, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee; Liao Rongbiao, Deputy Commander of the Songhu Garrison; Fang Yi, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee; Wang Fan, Deputy Minister of Public Security of the East China Military and Political Committee; Pan Hannian and Sheng Pihua, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; Xia Yan, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and others. In the evening, Tan Zhenlin, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, hosted a special banquet to welcome them.

On the 9th, Prime Minister Tsedenbal and members of the delegation visited factories, nurseries, and the Caoyang New Village, the first batch of worker housing built in Shanghai, among other places.

### 泽登巴尔抵达上海

1952-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔抵达上海
    【新华社上海九日电】蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔于八日抵达上海。同来的有蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民共和国驻华特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗及其夫人，蒙古人民共和国外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒及东方司副司长都勒布尔金。我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如陪行。到车站欢迎的有华东军政委员会副主席谭震林，华东军政委员会秘书长吴克坚，华东军政委员会文化教育委员会主任舒同，淞沪警备司令部副司令员廖容标，华东军政委员会财政经济委员会副主任方毅，华东军政委员会公安部副部长王范，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、盛丕华，上海市人民政府文化教育委员会主任夏衍等。晚间，华东军政委员会副主席谭震林特设宴欢迎。
    九日，泽登巴尔总理和代表团团员参观了工厂、托儿所和上海第一批建筑的工人住宅曹杨新村等。
