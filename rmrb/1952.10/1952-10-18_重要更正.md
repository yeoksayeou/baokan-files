    Important Correction
    In the third paragraph of Comrade Gao Gang's article "Welcoming a New Period of Economic Construction" published on page two of this newspaper on October 1st, the following sentence, starting from the third line, "...it is a hundred times superior to the decadent capitalist social system and internal social reforms. ...Even in the serious struggle of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, China's economy has still been able to recover and develop rapidly,...", should be "...it is a hundred times superior to the decadent capitalist social system. ...Even in the serious struggle of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea and internal social reforms, China's economy has still been able to recover and develop rapidly,...".

### 重要更正

1952-10-18
第2版()
专栏：

    重要更正
    十月一日本报二版所载高岗同志“迎接经济建设的新时期”一文之第三段从第三行起的数句“……它比腐朽的资本主义的社会制度和内部社会改革优越百倍。……虽在抗美援朝的严重斗争中，我国经济仍能迅速恢复和发展，……”，应为“……它比腐朽的资本主义的社会制度要优越百倍。……虽在抗美援朝和内部社会改革的严重斗争中，我国经济仍能迅速恢复和发展，……”。
