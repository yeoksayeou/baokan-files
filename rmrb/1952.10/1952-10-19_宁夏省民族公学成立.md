Title: Ningxia Provincial Nationalities Public School Established
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 3
Cultural Briefs:

    Ningxia Provincial Nationalities Public School Established
    In order to actively prepare cadre conditions for the implementation of national regional autonomy, the Ningxia Provincial People's Government recently established the Ningxia Provincial Nationalities Public School in Yinchuan City. The Ningxia Provincial Nationalities Public School follows the New Democratic education policy stipulated in the Common Program and the spirit of the First Nationalities Education Conference, with the main tasks of cultivating young people of all ethnic groups to become national construction talents and improving the political and cultural level of existing minority cadres. The school opened in late September and currently has 250 students, including Hui, Mongolian, and Han ethnic groups, with Hui and Mongolian youths accounting for 80% of the total student population.

### 宁夏省民族公学成立

1952-10-19
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    宁夏省民族公学成立
    宁夏省人民政府为积极给推行民族区域自治准备干部条件，最近在银川市创办了宁夏省民族公学。宁夏省民族公学遵循共同纲领规定的新民主主义的教育方针和全国第一次民族教育会议的精神，以培养各民族青年成为国家建设人才，提高现有的少数民族干部的政治文化水平为主要任务。该校已在九月下旬开学，现有学生共二百五十人，包括回、蒙、汉三个民族成份，其中回族和蒙族青年占全校学生百分之八十。
