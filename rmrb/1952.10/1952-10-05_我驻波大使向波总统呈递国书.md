Title: Our Ambassador to Poland Presents Credentials to the Polish President
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-05
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 28th) (Delayed) Zeng Yongquan, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Polish People's Republic, accompanied by all the diplomatic officials of our embassy, presented his credentials to the President of the Polish People's Republic, Bolesław Bierut, on September 27th. Those from the Polish side who attended the ceremony included Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski, Deputy Minister Wierblowski, Head of the Presidential Chancellery Rybicki, Director of the Presidential Office Gurska, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Bartol.

### 我驻波大使向波总统呈递国书

1952-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻波大使向波总统呈递国书
    【新华社华沙九月二十八日电】（迟到）我国新任驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使曾涌泉，率领我大使馆全体外交官员于九月二十七日向波兰人民共和国总统贝鲁特呈递国书。波方参加典礼者，尚有外交部部长斯克列杰夫斯基、副部长威尔勃洛夫斯基、总统府文官长雷比茨基、总统府办公厅主任古尔斯卡及外交部交际司司长巴托尔。
