Title: Some Peace Delegates from Mongolia and Other Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch: The Mongolian delegates who participated in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Chadravalin Lodeidanba, Lubusanggen Dulamuzafu, Duogelun Qielindaowa, Manjialin Dugeersulun, and the Iranian delegate Monteleza Gasai, all left Beijing by plane on the 17th to return home. Mrs. Bufiyu, treasurer of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, and Sal Jim Keibriel, representative of the Education Workers' International, also left Beijing on the same plane.
    Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Bao Erhan, the Chinese delegate to the Peace Conference, Mei Ru'ao, member of the World Peace Council, and many others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th Dispatch: The Pakistani delegates who attended the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Ataur Rahman, Mazha Ali Khan, Mrs. Tahira Mazha, and Tafajur Hussein, arrived in Guangzhou on the morning of the 16th and left Guangzhou on the same day, returning home via Shenzhen and Hong Kong. Liao Siguang and Xiao Junying, members of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and many others, went to the station to welcome and see them off.

### 蒙古等国部分和平代表返国

1952-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古等国部分和平代表返国
    【新华社十七日讯】参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的蒙古代表查德拉瓦林·洛代丹巴，卢布桑根·都拉姆扎夫，多格伦·切林达娃，满加林·都格尔苏伦和伊朗代表蒙特勒扎·加赛米，均于十七日乘飞机离北京返国。同机离京的还有国际民主法律工作者协会司库布非郁夫人和教育工会国际代表萨尔·吉姆·凯布列尔。
    前往机场欢送的有出席和平会议中国代表鲍尔汉，世界和平理事会理事梅汝璈等多人。
    【新华社广州十六日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的巴基斯坦代表阿陶尔·拉赫曼，马兹哈·阿里汗，塔希拉·马兹哈夫人和塔法朱尔·胡塞因四人于十六日晨到达广州，并于当日离广州，经深圳、香港返国。前往车站欢迎、欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会委员廖似光、萧隽英等多人。
