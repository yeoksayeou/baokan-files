Title: Our Cultural and Educational Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1952-10-12
Page: 4

    Our Cultural and Educational Delegation Arrives in Berlin
    [This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: The People's Republic of China Cultural and Educational Delegation, headed by Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, left Warsaw on the 4th and arrived in Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, on the 6th.
    The delegation arrived in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, on September 11th. During this period, the delegation visited the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, the Ministry of Science and Higher Education, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Health, the Central Bureau of Technical Education, and other departments of the Polish People's Republic government in charge of cultural and educational affairs. It also visited cities such as Gdansk, Katowice, and Krakow, and visited famous Polish universities, specialized schools, primary and secondary schools, hospitals, museums, etc., and learned about the various experiences of the Polish people in cultural construction. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the Polish government and people everywhere it went.

### 我文化教育考察团抵达柏林

1952-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化教育考察团抵达柏林
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：以我中央人民政府教育部副部长韦悫为首的中华人民共和国文化教育考察团已于四日离华沙，并于六日抵达德意志民主共和国首都柏林。
    考察团是在九月十一日到达波兰首都华沙的。在此期间，考察团访问了波兰文化艺术部、科学与高等教育部、教育部、卫生部、中央技术教育局以及波兰人民共和国政府主管文化教育事宜的其他部门，并访问了格但斯克、卡托维兹、克拉科夫等城市，参观了波兰著名的各大学、专科学校、中小学校和医院、博物馆等，了解了波兰人民在文化建设方面的各项经验。考察团到处受到波兰政府与人民的热烈欢迎。
