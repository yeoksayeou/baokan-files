Title: US Planes Continue to Violate Our Airspace
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 22nd. From October 8th to 17th, American aggressors continued to dispatch 92 sorties of planes, totaling 571 aircraft, to intrude into the airspace over Andong, Kuandian, Fengcheng, Ji'an, Huanren, Changbai in Liaodong Province, and Helong and other cities and counties in Jilin Province.

    On October 8th, two sorties of American planes, totaling three aircraft, intruded into areas southeast of Kuandian, Dajiucai Gou, and west of Yongdian River Estuary and Anping River.
    On October 9th, twelve sorties of American planes, totaling eighty aircraft, intruded into Langtou, Jiuliancheng, Maokui Mountain, Kuandian, Changdian River Estuary, Anping River, Lagu Post, south of Taiping Post, Fengcheng, Ji'an, Huangbaidianzi, Tumenzi, Buda Yuan and other places.
    On October 11th, sixteen sorties of American planes, totaling ninety-six aircraft, intruded into Gaolichengzi, Fuyou Street, Buda Yuan, Shizhu, Dahuanggou, Xiaopushi River, Lagu Post, east of Kuandian, Changdian River Estuary, Stone City, Laogugou, Jiuliancheng, Andong, Yangzipao, Langtou, Qianyang, north of Dadonggou and other places.
    On October 12th, twenty-two sorties of American planes, totaling one hundred and forty aircraft, intruded into Yongdian River Estuary, Andong, Stone City, Lagu Post, Langtou, Laogugou, Ji'an, Fuyou Street, Kuandian, Buda Yuan, Shanghuolonggai, Huanren, Yangzipao, Dadonggou, Xiaopushi River, Shizhu, Taiping Post, Jiguanshan and other places.
    On October 13th, fifteen sorties of American planes, totaling seventy-four aircraft, intruded into Andong, Changdian River Estuary, Lagu Post, Yongdian River Estuary, Xiaopushi River, Shizhu, north of Changbai, Sandaogou, Dahuanggou, Helong, Ji'an, Laogugou, Maokui Mountain and other places.
    On October 14th, nine sorties of American planes, totaling fifty-eight aircraft, intruded into areas north of Shizhu, southeast of Jianchang, Stone City, Andong, west of Changdian River Estuary, Dadonggou, Anping River, Langtou, north of Kuandian, Ji'an, Shanghuolonggai and other places.
    On October 16th, six sorties of American planes, totaling forty-four aircraft, intruded into Langtou, north of Changdian River Estuary, east of Kuandian, Shizhu, Fuyou Street, Shajianzi, Shanghuolonggai, east of Huanren, Tongtiangou, Stone City, north of Pushi River and other places.
    On October 17th, ten sorties of American planes, totaling seventy-six aircraft, intruded into Yangzipao, Jiuliancheng, Maokui Mountain, Andong, Langtou, Lagu Post, Anping River, Laogugou, Shanghuolonggai, Changdian River Estuary, Buda Yuan and other places.
    Our people express great indignation at the unreasonable provocative actions of the American aggressors.

### 美机续犯我领空

1952-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    美机续犯我领空
    【新华社沈阳二十二日电】自十月八日至十七日的一旬间，美国侵略者继续出动飞机九十二批、五百七十一架次，侵扰我辽东省之安东、宽甸、凤城、辑安、桓仁、长白以及吉林省之和龙等市、县上空。
    十月八日，美机二批三架次，侵入宽甸东南、大韭菜沟以及永甸河口、安平河以西等处。
    十月九日，美机十二批八十架次，侵入浪头、九连城、帽盔山、宽甸、长甸河口、安平河、拉古哨、太平哨以南、凤城、辑安、黄柏甸子、土门子、步达远等地。
    十月十一日，美机十六批九十六架次，侵入高丽城子、富有街、步达远、石柱子、大荒沟、小蒲石河、拉古哨、宽甸以东、长甸河口、石头城、老古沟、九连城、安东、扬子泡、浪头、前阳、大东沟以北等处。
    十月十二日，美机二十二批一百四十架次，侵入永甸河口、安东、石头城、拉古哨、浪头、老古沟、辑安、富有街、宽甸、步达远、上活龙盖、桓仁、扬子泡、大东沟、小蒲石河、石柱子、太平哨、鸡冠山等地。
    十月十三日，美机十五批七十四架次，侵入安东、长甸河口、拉古哨、永甸河口、小蒲石河、石柱子、长白以北、三道沟、大荒沟、和龙、辑安、老古沟、帽盔山等地。
    十月十四日，美机九批五十八架次，侵入石柱子以北、碱厂东南、石头城、安东、长甸河口以西、大东沟、安平河、浪头、宽甸以北、辑安、上活龙盖等地。
    十月十六日，美机六批四十四架次，侵入浪头、长甸河口以北、宽甸以东、石柱子、富有街、沙尖子、上活龙盖、桓仁以东、通天沟、石头城、蒲石河以北等处。
    十月十七日，美机十批七十六架次，侵入扬子泡、九连城、帽盔山、安东、浪头、拉古哨、安平河、老古沟、上活龙盖、长甸河口、步达远等地。
    我国人民对美国侵略者的无理挑衅行为表示极大愤怒。
