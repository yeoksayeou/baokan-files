Title: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Issues a Notice: Celebrating the 35th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union, Mobilizing All Sectors of the Country to Participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities
Author:
Date: 1952-10-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its 42nd enlarged meeting on the afternoon of the 27th. After listening to the report on "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" by Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the meeting decided to issue: "Notice to all levels of Consultative Committees regarding the celebration of the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union and mobilizing people from all walks of life across the country to participate in the 'Sino-Soviet Friendship Month' activities."

    Those attending the meeting included Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and Standing Committee members Li Weihan, Wang Kunlun, Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Lan, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Zhang Xiruo, Zhu De, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, as well as members of the National Committee in Beijing, members of the Central People's Government Committee, and heads of various commissions, ministries, associations, academies, bureaus, and banks, totaling more than 80 people.

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference issued a "Notice to all levels of Consultative Committees regarding the celebration of the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union and mobilizing people from all walks of life across the country to participate in the 'Sino-Soviet Friendship Month' activities." The full text is as follows:

    November 7th this year is the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to hold a "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" nationwide from November 7th to December 6th to warmly celebrate this great festival that laid the foundation for the victory of people all over the world, and to use the great achievements of Soviet socialist construction to conduct a widespread and in-depth education on the future of socialism and communism among the masses of people across the country; to further develop and consolidate the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union on the basis of the Sino-Soviet friendship alliance. The consultative committees of all provinces and cities and the standing committees of counties should do their best to assist the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in carrying out the various tasks of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month." For this reason, the following is hereby notified:

    1. During the aforementioned period and before and after it, the consultative committees of all provinces and cities and the standing committees of counties should assist the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in vigorously mobilizing and organizing people from all walks of life to participate in the various activities and work of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month."

    2. The study committees of the consultative committees of all provinces and cities should, in conjunction with the patriotic increase production and practice economy movement and the ideological remolding movement, and based on the specific circumstances of people from all walks of life, promote the study of various advanced experiences of the Soviet Union, report and discuss the great significance of Sino-Soviet friendship to world peace; organize various symposiums, report meetings, and evening parties of local democratic parties and people's organizations, and may appropriately organize symposiums on the achievements of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to carry out such study.

    3. In places where Soviet delegations and Soviet song and dance troupes arrive, the consultative committees of all provinces and cities should invite responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as famous people from all walks of life, to have cordial talks with the Soviet representatives and express their welcome and gratitude. Appropriate joint lectures may be organized, inviting Soviet representatives to give special reports on various topics, in order to gain direct benefit.

### 中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会发出通知  庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命卅五周年动员全国各界参加“中苏友好月”活动

1952-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会发出通知
    庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命卅五周年动员全国各界参加“中苏友好月”活动
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在二十七日下午扩大举行第四十二次会议。会议在听取了中苏友好协会总会钱俊瑞总干事所作的关于“中苏友好月”的报告后，决定发出：“关于庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念、动员全国各界人士参加‘中苏友好月’活动给各级协商委员会的通知”。
    出席这次会议的，有人民政协全国委员会副主席周恩来、李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通，常务委员李维汉、王昆仑、蒋光鼐、张澜、章伯钧、章乃器、张奚若、朱德、沈雁冰、邵力子以及全国委员会在京委员、中央人民政府委员会委员、各委、部、会、院、署、行首长等共八十余人。
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会发出“关于庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念、动员全国各界人士参加‘中苏友好月’活动给各级协商委员会的通知”，全文如下：
    今年十一月七日是苏联十月社会主义革命三十五周年纪念日。中苏友好协会总会决定从十一月七日起至十二月六日在全国范围内举行“中苏友好月”，以热烈庆祝这个奠定全世界人民胜利局面的伟大节日，并用苏联社会主义建设的伟大成就，在全国人民群众中普遍和深入地进行一次社会主义和共产主义前途的教育；在中苏两国友好同盟的基础上，进一步发展与巩固中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。各省市协商委员会和县常务委员会，应尽力协助中苏友好协会做好“中苏友好月”的各项工作。为此，特通知如下：
    一、各省市协商委员会和县常务委员会在上述期间及其前后，应协助当地中苏友好协会大力动员和组织各界人士参加“中苏友好月”的各项活动与工作。
    二、各省市协商委员会学习委员会，应结合爱国增产节约运动和思想改造运动，并根据各界人士中的具体情况，推动学习苏联各项先进经验，报告和座谈中苏友好对世界和平的伟大意义；组织当地各民主党派、各人民团体的各种座谈会、报告会及晚会等，并可适当地组织关于苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的成就的座谈会，以进行这种学习。
    三、在苏联代表团和苏联歌舞团到达的地方，各省市协商委员会应约集各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人及各界著名人士和苏联代表亲切地晤谈，并表达欢迎感谢之意。并可适当地联合组织讲演会，请苏联代表作各项专题报告，以期获得直接的教益。
