Title: China's Islamic Hajj Delegation Returns to Beijing, Warmly Welcomed by Local People in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1952-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Hajj delegation sent by the preparatory meeting of the China Islamic Association to Mecca, the Islamic holy land, was unable to reach Mecca for some reason and returned to Beijing from Pakistan on October 5.

The Hajj delegation consisted of sixteen well-known figures from China's Islamic community, with Da Pao Sheng Da Ahong and Yiming Ma He Su Mu Ahong as the head and deputy head of the delegation, respectively. During the delegation's stay in Karachi, the capital of Pakistan, it was warmly welcomed by the local people. Newspapers in Karachi gave prominent coverage to the delegation's arrival, introducing the facts of China's ethnic equality, freedom of religious belief, and the significant improvements in the lives of various ethnic groups who believe in Islam. During a tour of the city, the delegation received flowers from local people four times. The delegation attended a welcoming reception held by Hatim Alavi, Chairman of the Pakistan-China Friendship Association, and a reception held by Qadri Alin, the general director of the Pakistan "Association of Islamic Scholars." Imam Hussaini, a close friend of Jinnah during his lifetime, presented a gift to Head of Delegation Da Pao Sheng, expressing the friendship between the people of China and Pakistan. The delegation also attended banquets hosted by the Egyptian Ambassador and the Saudi Arabian Minister to Pakistan.

During their stay in Karachi, Head of Delegation Da Pao Sheng and Deputy Head Yiming Ma He Su Mu visited prominent Islamic figures in Pakistan and hosted a banquet for Islamic scholars and religious figures in Karachi. During their stay in Lahore, the delegation attended a reception hosted by Mian Iftikharuddin, a member of the Pakistan Constituent Assembly and a council member of the World Peace Council, and went to the Wazir Khan Mosque for "Juma" prayers. Head of Delegation Da Pao Sheng was invited to lead the prayers and was warmly welcomed by the Muslims who attended the "Juma" prayers.

### 我国伊斯兰教朝觐团已返京  在巴基斯坦受到当地人民热烈欢迎

1952-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国伊斯兰教朝觐团已返京
    在巴基斯坦受到当地人民热烈欢迎
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：由中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议所派遣前往伊斯兰教圣地麦加的朝觐团，因故未能到达麦加，于十月五日由巴基斯坦返抵北京。
    朝觐团是由我国伊斯兰教界的知名人士十六人组成，以达浦生大阿訇和依明马合苏木阿訇为正、副团长。朝觐团在巴基斯坦首都喀喇蚩期间，受到当地人民的热烈欢迎。喀喇蚩的各报对朝觐团的到达曾着重地加以报道，并介绍我国民族平等信教自由及信仰伊斯兰教各民族生活获得显著改善的事实。在参观该市时，朝觐团曾接受当地人民四次献花。朝觐团曾出席巴中友好协会主席哈提姆·阿拉维的欢迎会及巴基斯坦“伊斯兰学者协会”总指导卡德瑞阿林的招待会。真纳生前好友伊玛目侯赛尼曾向达浦生团长赠送礼品，表达中、巴两国人民的友好。朝觐团并曾出席驻巴基斯坦的埃及大使及沙特阿拉伯公使的宴会。
    朝觐团在喀喇蚩逗留期间，达浦生团长、依明马合苏木副团长曾拜访了巴基斯坦的伊斯兰教知名人士，并曾设宴招待喀喇蚩市的伊斯兰教学者与宗教家。在拉合尔期间，朝觐团曾出席巴基斯坦制宪议会议员和世界和平理事会理事米安·伊夫提卡鲁丁的招待会，并赴卧兹尔汗大礼拜寺作“主麻”，达浦生团长被邀领导礼拜，受到同做“主麻”之穆士林的热烈欢迎。
