Title: Government Trade Delegation from the German Democratic Republic Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: An eighteen-member government trade delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Head of Delegation Balkow, arrived in Beijing at 4:00 PM on the 1st. Those who went to the airport to greet them included: Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Lin Haiyun, Director of the General Office, Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Su Dan, Section Chief of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department.
    Zhang Guangdou, Commercial Counselor of our diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    Grutner, Counselor of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic in China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 德意志民主共和国  政府商务代表团抵京

1952-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国
    政府商务代表团抵京
    【新华社一日讯】德意志民主共和国政府商务代表团一行十八人，由副团长巴尔柯夫率领，于一日午后四时抵京。前往机场迎接的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，办公厅主任林海云，外交部交际处副处长沈平及苏联东欧司科长苏丹等。
    赴机场欢迎的还有我驻德意志民主共和国外交使团商务参赞张光斗。
    德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团参赞葛鲁特纳等亦前往机场迎接。
