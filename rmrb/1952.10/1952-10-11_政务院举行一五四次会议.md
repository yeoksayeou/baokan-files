Title: Government Administration Council Holds 154th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The 154th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 10th. The meeting listened to a report on the financial and economic work situation in the first half of 1952, given by Bo Yibo, Vice-Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行一五四次会议

1952-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一五四次会议
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十四次政务会议在十日下午举行。会议听取了政务院财政经济委员会薄一波副主任所作的关于一九五二年上半年财经工作情况的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案。
