Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Fetes the Mongolian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-10-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet in the Qin Zheng Hall of Zhongnanhai on October 4th in honor of the Mongolian People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by the Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal.

    The Mongolian side attending the banquet included: Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Lhamsuren, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Shirindyb, Minister of Education; Jalkhjavyn Jamiyan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China; Purbuzhav, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; Dulevgun, Deputy Director of the Eastern Department; Uljii, Secretary; Shirindorj, Counselor of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China; Nyam, First Secretary; Ariun, Second Secretary; Gursed, Third Secretary.

    The Chinese side attending as guests included: Zhu De and Li Jishen, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Yun, Huang Yanpei and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of General Staff; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Bo Yibo, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council and Minister of Finance; Jia Tuofu, Deputy Director; Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director and Minister of Foreign Trade; Ma Xulun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council and Minister of Education; Lu Dingyi, Deputy Director; Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director and Minister of Culture; Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Director; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee; Luo Ruiqing, Zhang Naiqi, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Bojun, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Li Lisan, Li Dequan, and Lai Chuanzhu, Ministers of the Central People's Government; Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Zhou Yang, and Qian Junrui, Vice Ministers; Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Bingzhang, Chief of Staff of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

    The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of warmth and friendship.

### 毛泽东主席欢宴蒙古政府代表团

1952-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席欢宴蒙古政府代表团
    【新华社四日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席于十月四日在中南海勤政殿欢宴以蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。
    蒙古方面出席宴会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，教育部部长锡林迪布，蒙古人民共和国驻华特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗，蒙古人民共和国外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒，东方司副司长都勒布尔金，秘书乌勒吉巴雅尔，蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞斯林道尔吉，一等秘书尼玛，二等秘书艾林清苏诺木，三等秘书古尔斯德。
    中国方面出席作陪的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，副总理陈云、黄炎培、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，副总参谋长粟裕，总政治部副主任萧华，政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼财政部部长薄一波，副主任贾拓夫，副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮，政务院文化教育委员会副主任兼教育部部长马叙伦，副主任陆定一，副主任兼文化部部长沈雁冰，副主任习仲勋，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中央人民政府部长罗瑞卿、章乃器、滕代远、朱学范、章伯钧、李书城、梁希、李立三、李德全、赖传珠，副部长章汉夫、伍修权、周扬、钱俊瑞，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军空军参谋长王秉璋，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，副主任阎宝航，亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
