Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Burmese Land Reform Delegation; Minister Li Shucheng of Our Ministry of Agriculture Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Burmese Land Reform Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd—Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, received the Burmese Land Reform Delegation headed by Tekin Zin at 3:00 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd. Those present at the reception included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the General Office Yan Baohang, Director of the Asian Department Chen Jiakang, and Burmese Ambassador to China U La Maung.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd—Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, Li Shucheng, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 2nd to bid farewell to the Burmese Land Reform Delegation. Those present as hosts included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Agriculture Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Water Resources Zhang Hanying, Vice Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives Meng Yongqian, Director of the Central People's Government Bureau of Ceremonies Yu Xinqing, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice President Qian Weichang, and President of Beijing Agricultural University Sun Xiaocun, etc. Burmese Ambassador to China U La Maung and diplomatic personnel from the Burmese Embassy were also invited to attend the banquet. The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周恩来总理接见缅甸土地改革参观团  我农业部李书城部长设宴欢送缅甸土地改革参观团

1952-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见缅甸土地改革参观团
    我农业部李书城部长设宴欢送缅甸土地改革参观团
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于二日下午三时接见以德钦阵为首的缅甸土地改革参观团。接见时，在座者有：外交部副部长章汉夫、办公厅副主任阎宝航、亚洲司司长陈家康及缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂。
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府农业部部长李书城在二日晚设宴欢送缅甸土地改革参观团。出席作陪的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，农业部副部长杨显东，水利部副部长张含英，中华全国合作社联合总社副主任孟用潜，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，副会长钱伟长，北京农业大学校长孙晓村等。缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和缅甸大使馆外交人员也应邀参加宴会。宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
