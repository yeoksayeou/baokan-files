Title: Ceylon Representative Hector Sironis Fernando Signs Supplementary Statement Condemning the U.S. Government for Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-10-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency:
    Ceylon Representative Hector Sironis Fernando Signs Supplementary Statement Condemning the U.S. Government for Germ Warfare
    [Xinhua News Agency] Twenty-seven scientists and doctors from Brazil, Canada, Chile, China, Colombia, India, Japan, North Korea, Mexico, Mongolia, Nicaragua, the Soviet Union, and the United States who attended the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference issued a statement condemning the U.S. government for conducting germ warfare. On the 15th, Hector Sironis Fernando, a doctor and representative of Ceylon attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, also signed the statement in supplement.

### 锡兰代表费兰多补行签名  谴责美国政府进行细菌战

1952-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰代表费兰多补行签名
    谴责美国政府进行细菌战
    【新华社讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的巴西、加拿大、智利、中国、哥伦比亚、印度、日本、朝鲜、墨西哥、蒙古、尼加拉瓜、苏联和美国的科学家和医生二十七人，曾发表谴责美国政府进行细菌战的声明。十五日锡兰出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表、医生赫克多·希罗尼斯·费兰多也在声明上补行签名。
