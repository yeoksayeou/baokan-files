Title: 703 Returned Overseas Chinese Students Warmly Congratulate the Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-21
Page: 2

Reader's Letter:

    703 Returned Overseas Chinese Students Warmly Congratulate the Peace Conference

    Editor: The Peace Conference of the Asian and Pacific Regions not only represents the will of 1.6 billion people in the Asian and Pacific regions, but also reflects the common hope of peace-loving people all over the world.

    We are all overseas Chinese students from Southeast Asian countries. The terror and disaster brought to us by World War II are still fresh in our memories. The Japanese fascists' "Death March" in the Philippines, the "Great Purge" (massacre) in Malaya, the enslavement of millions of young people as "laborers" in Indonesia, the driving of hundreds of thousands of prisoners of war and people in Siam and Burma to build the "Death Railway"... all of this will forever be etched in our hearts. We are determined to prevent the recurrence of such terror and disaster.

    However, the aggressive bloc headed by US imperialism launched insane wars of aggression in Korea, Vietnam, and Malaya, and continue to sabotage the Korean armistice negotiations, use inhuman bacteriological weapons, and arm Japan and West Germany, in an attempt to expand the war of aggression and launch another world war. This seriously threatens the peaceful life of the people of the world. We are ardent lovers of peace and oppose wars of aggression, so we firmly support all the resolutions of the Peace Conference of the Asian and Pacific Regions. We firmly believe that through these resolutions, the peace-loving people of the Asian and Pacific regions will be encouraged to struggle for lasting world peace.

    703 Returned Overseas Chinese Students

### 七百零三名归国华侨学生热烈祝贺和平会议

1952-10-21
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    七百零三名归国华侨学生热烈祝贺和平会议
    编辑同志：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，不仅代表着亚洲及太平洋区域十六亿人民的意志，也反映了全世界爱好和平人民的共同希望。
    我们都是来自东南亚各国的华侨学生。第二次世界大战带给我们的恐怖和灾难至今记忆犹新。日本法西斯在菲律宾的“死亡进军”，在马来亚的“大检举”（大屠杀），在印度尼西亚把千百万的青年当作奴隶——“劳务者”，在暹罗，在缅甸，驱使数十万战俘和人民为其铺建“死亡铁路”……，这一切，将永远记在我们的心里，我们决心要阻止这种恐怖与灾难的重演。
    但是，以美帝国主义为首的侵略集团，他们在朝鲜，在越南，在马来亚，发动了疯狂的侵略战争，并在继续破坏朝鲜停战谈判，使用灭绝人性的细菌武器，武装日本和西德，企图扩大侵略战争，发动另一次世界大战。这严重地威胁了世界人民的和平生活。我们酷爱和平，反对侵略战争，因此坚决拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各项决议。我们深信，通过这些决议，将鼓舞亚洲及太平洋区域的和平人民为争取世界持久和平的斗争。  七百零三名归国华侨学生
