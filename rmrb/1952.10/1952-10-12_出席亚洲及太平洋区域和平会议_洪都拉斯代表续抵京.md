Title: Honduran Representative Continues to Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 11th: A Honduran representative, Elia Baccasaga de Mejia, has arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th to attend the Peace Conference of the Asian and Pacific Regions.
    Elia Baccasaga de Mejia is a social worker and a member of the Honduras Peace Committee.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  洪都拉斯代表续抵京

1952-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    洪都拉斯代表续抵京
    【新华社十一日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的洪都拉斯代表艾利亚·巴哥阿加·第·梅希亚已于十日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    艾利亚·巴哥阿加·第·梅希亚是社会工作者、洪都拉斯和平委员会委员。
