Title: Slogans of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters Regarding "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1952-10-29
Page: 1

Slogans of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters Regarding "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month"

	I. Celebrate the 35th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution!
	II. Pay tribute to the great Soviet people who are victoriously building communism! The friendly alliance between the Chinese and Soviet people is a powerful guarantee against the threat of new wars and for the defense of peace in Asia and the the world. Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people!
	III. Thank Marshal Stalin, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people for their selfless assistance in China's construction!
	IV. Welcome the Soviet cultural workers delegation, the acting troupe, and the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble!
	V. Pay tribute to the Soviet experts who have worked tirelessly and selflessly in China for the past three years to help with our country's construction!
	VI. The people of the whole country should work together to learn from the Soviet Union's advanced national construction experience, to welcome the upcoming large-scale economic construction and cultural construction, and to strive for the industrialization of the motherland!
	VII. Commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army! Strive to learn from the Soviet Union's advanced military science and technology, and strive to further build a modern national defense army!
	VIII. Workers and staff in state-owned enterprises! Learn from the Soviet Union's advanced production experience and enterprise management experience, create your own advanced working methods, strengthen labor protection work, improve labor productivity, improve product quality, save raw materials, and reduce costs, and strive to carry out the patriotic production increase and economy practice movement and complete and exceed the state production plan!
	IX. Male and female peasants! Learn from the Soviet Union's experience in agricultural collectivization and advanced farming techniques, develop mutual aid and cooperation organizations, launch patriotic bumper harvest competitions, increase yield per unit area, and strive to further improve agricultural production levels and improve your material and cultural lives!
	X. Male and female herders! Learn from the Soviet Union's advanced livestock breeding methods, improve breeds, prevent and control animal diseases, and strive to increase livestock!
	XI. State trade workers and cooperative workers! Learn from the Soviet Union's experience in state trade and cooperative work, universally develop supply and marketing cooperative organizations, and strive to carry out material exchanges between urban and rural areas!
	XII. State organ staff! Learn from the Soviet Union's state organ staff's spirit of actively studying their work, study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, abide by state discipline, pay close attention to the demands of the people, and strive to improve work efficiency!
	XIII. Young men and women! Strive to learn from the Soviet Union's advanced science and technology, emulate the patriotic heroism of the Soviet people, pay attention to physical exercise, and prepare to serve the motherland!
	XIV. Women! Look to Soviet women as an example! Strive to learn politics and culture, and actively participate in national construction work!
	XV. Educators! Learn from the Soviet Union's advanced educational theories and teaching methods, educate the younger generation with the spirit of patriotism and internationalism, and struggle to develop cultural and educational undertakings, cultivate construction talents, and improve the people's cultural level!
	XVI. Scientific and technological workers! Learn from the Soviet Union's advanced science and technology, closely integrate scientific research work with the actual needs of national construction, and serve the cause of national construction!
	XVII. Medical and health workers! Learn from the Soviet Union's advanced medical knowledge and health work experience, develop health undertakings, continue to carry out the patriotic health movement, and strive to improve the people's health!
	XVIII. Literary and art workers! Learn from the Soviet Union's advanced literary and artistic theories and excellent works, inherit the fine traditions of our country's literature and art, grasp the literary and artistic direction of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers, go deep into reality, improve artistic accomplishment, and strive to create literary and artistic works with high ideological content and artistic level!
	XIX. Industrialists and businessmen! Actively participate in Sino-Soviet friendly activities, and under the leadership of the working class and the state-owned economy, follow the path of the Common Program, and strive to develop economic undertakings that are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood!
	XX. Long live the great People's Republic of China!
	XXI. Long live the great Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, the bastion of world peace, democracy, and socialism!
	XXII. Long live the permanent and strong friendly alliance between China and the Soviet Union!
	XXIII. Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people!
	XXIV. Long live Marshal Stalin, the faithful friend of the Chinese people, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the whole world!

### 中苏友好协会总会关于“中苏友好月”的口号

1952-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会关于“中苏友好月”的口号
    一、庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十五周年！
    二、向正在胜利地建设共产主义的伟大的苏联人民致敬！中苏两国人民的友好同盟是反对新战争威胁、保卫亚洲和世界和平的有力保证。中国和苏联两国人民的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    三、感谢斯大林大元帅、苏联政府和苏联人民对于中国建设的大公无私的援助！
    四、欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团、演员队和苏军红旗歌舞团！
    五、向三年来在中国不辞艰苦、以忘我的精神帮助我国建设的苏联专家们致敬！
    六、全国人民一致努力学习苏联的先进的建国经验，迎接即将到来的大规模经济建设和文化建设，为祖国的工业化而奋斗！
    七、人民解放军的指战员们！努力学习苏联先进军事科学，为进一步建设现代化国防军而奋斗！
    八、男女工人们和国营企业中的工作人员们！学习苏联先进生产经验与企业管理的经验，创造自己的先进工作方法，加强劳动保护工作，提高劳动生产率，提高产品质量，节约原料，减低成本，为努力开展爱国增产节约运动、完成和超过国家生产计划而奋斗！
    九、男女农民们！学习苏联农业集体化的经验和先进耕作技术，发展互助合作组织，开展爱国丰产竞赛，提高单位面积的产量，为更加提高农业生产水平、提高自己的物质文化生活而奋斗！
    十、男女牧民们！学习苏联先进牲畜饲养方法，改良品种，防治畜疫，为增殖牲畜而努力！
    十一、国家贸易工作者和合作社工作者们！学习苏联国家贸易和合作社的工作经验，普遍发展供销合作社的组织，努力开展城乡物资交流！
    十二、国家机关工作人员们！学习苏联国家机关工作人员积极钻研工作的精神，学习马克思列宁主义和毛泽东思想，遵守国家纪律，密切关心人民群众的要求，努力提高工作效率！
    十三、男女青年们！努力学习苏联先进的科学和技术，效法苏联人民爱国主义的英雄榜样，注意锻炼身体，准备为祖国服务！
    十四、妇女们！向苏联妇女看齐！努力学习政治和文化，积极参加国家建设工作！
    十五、教育工作者们！学习苏联先进的教育理论和教学法，以爱国主义和国际主义的精神教育青年一代，为发展文化教育事业、培养建设人才和提高人民的文化水平而斗争！
    十六、科学技术工作者们！学习苏联先进的科学技术，使科学研究工作与国家建设的实际需要密切结合，为国家建设事业服务！
    十七、医药卫生工作者们！学习苏联先进医学知识和卫生工作经验，发展卫生事业，继续开展爱国卫生运动，为增进人民健康而努力！
    十八、文学艺术工作者们！学习苏联文学艺术的先进理论和优秀作品，继承我国文学艺术的优良传统，掌握为工农兵服务的文艺方向，深入实际，提高艺术修养，为创造有高度思想内容和艺术水平的文艺作品而努力！
    十九、工商业家们！积极参加中苏友好的活动，在工人阶级和国营经济的领导下，遵循共同纲领的轨道，努力发展有利于国计民生的经济事业！
    二十、伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    二十一、世界和平、民主、社会主义的堡垒——伟大的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟万岁！
    二十二、中苏两国永久的强大的友好同盟万岁！
    二十三、中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席万岁！
    二十四、中国人民的忠实朋友、全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师斯大林大元帅万岁！
