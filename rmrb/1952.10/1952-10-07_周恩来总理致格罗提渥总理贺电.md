Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 1

    Premier Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Grotewohl
    To Comrade Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic:
    On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, please accept my warm congratulations, and allow me to express my best wishes for the increasing consolidation and development of the friendship between China and Germany.
            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                      October 5, 1952, Beijing

### 周恩来总理致格罗提渥总理贺电

1952-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致格罗提渥总理贺电德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥同志：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立三周年之际，请接受我热烈的祝贺，并对中德两国友谊的日益巩固与发展表示最好的愿望。
            中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理　周恩来
                      一九五二年十月五日北京
