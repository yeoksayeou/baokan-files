Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Representatives of Women from Various Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1952-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation hosted a banquet on the 13th for women representatives of various fraternal ethnic groups who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. Invited to attend were Tsering Dolma, the sister of the Dalai Lama of the Tibetan ethnic group, and Jamyang Bum, the wife of the Dege Tusi of Xikang and deputy director of the Qamdo Regional Liberation Committee; Mayinur, the wife of the Uyghur martyr Ahmatjan and vice chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial Democratic Women's Federation; Wen Yuancheng, a model revolutionary soldier's family member of Korean ethnicity from Songjiang Province; Jin Yu, a Mongolian learning model; and thirty women representatives of the Li, Miao, Yi, Yao, Dong, Zhuang, Hui, Vietnamese, Maonan, and Tatar ethnic groups.
    Attending the banquet were Deng Yingchao and Xu Guangping, vice chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, as well as members of the Standing Committee, the secretary-general, and heads of various departments. Deng Yingchao spoke at the meeting, warmly welcoming the sisters from various fraternal ethnic groups, and expressed the hope that the sisters of all ethnic groups would further unite as one to build a great motherland. The atmosphere was filled with friendly solidarity, and the representatives frequently raised their glasses to wish Chairman Mao Zedong, the savior of the people of all ethnic groups, good health, and to wish the sisters of all ethnic groups unity, progress, and health.
    After the meeting, the Beijing Youth Art Theater performed wonderful programs.

### 全国民主妇女联合会欢宴各民族妇女代表

1952-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国民主妇女联合会欢宴各民族妇女代表
    【新华社十五日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会在十三日欢宴各兄弟民族来京参加国庆节观礼的妇女代表。应邀参加的有藏族达赖喇嘛的姐姐泽仁卓玛和西康德格土司、昌都地区解放委员会副主任甲央伯母，维吾尔族烈士阿哈买提江的夫人、新疆省民主妇女联合会副主席马依努尔，松江省朝鲜族烈军属模范文源成，蒙族学习模范金玉及黎族、苗族、彝族、瑶族、侗族、僮族、回族、越南族、毛难族、塔塔尔族等妇女代表三十人。
    出席宴会的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、许广平及常委、秘书长、各部负责人等。邓颖超在会上讲话，热烈欢迎各兄弟民族的姊妹，并希望各族姊妹更进一步团结一心，建设伟大的祖国。席间，充满着友爱团结的欢乐气氛，各代表频频举杯祝福各族人民的救星毛泽东主席的健康，祝福各族姊妹的团结、进步和健康。
    会后，有北京青年艺术剧院演出精彩的节目。
