Title: In a Bright Mansion
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1952-10-09
Page: 3

    In a Bright Mansion
    Yuan Shuipai
    Here is a bright mansion, embracing people from two nations at war. On the soft carpet, there is no barbed wire, only a row of blooming irises. In Korea, American aggressors rage like beasts, burning the green grass and pines everywhere. But in this bright mansion, the American people offer an evergreen tree to their Korean brothers. The atrocities of the American aggressors are so bloody, clamoring to bomb seventy-eight peaceful cities flat. But the American people bless this tree to live forever in Korea, using its shade to protect the sons and daughters of this heroic nation. Race and race are not divided, people and people are close, only war criminals and peace lovers—the line is clear. The common enemy of the people of all countries, the chief culprit, is only the fascist leader who uses poison gas and bacteria to kill people. Here is a bright mansion, flags like flowers blooming side by side, America and Korea, France and Vietnam, Britain and Malaya… Friendship between peoples cannot be separated!

### 在一座光明的大厦里
袁水拍
1952-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    在一座光明的大厦里
    袁水拍这里是一座光明的大厦，拥抱着的人，来自两个战争中的国家。柔软的地毯上，没有铁丝网，只有一列盛开着的鸢尾花。在朝鲜，美国侵略者野兽般发疯，焚烧着到处的绿草和青松。但在这一座光明的大厦里，美国人民把一棵长绿树献给了朝鲜弟兄。美国侵略者的暴行是那样血腥，它叫嚣着要把七十八个和平城市炸平。但美国人民祝福这棵树永远活在朝鲜，用它的绿荫佑护这英勇民族的子孙。种族和种族不分，人民和人民相亲，只有战争罪犯与和平爱好者——界线分明。各国人民的共同敌人、那首恶元凶只有一个，那就是用毒气、细菌来杀人的法西斯头领。这里是一座光明的大厦，一面一面国旗好像一朵一朵并开着的鲜花，美国和朝鲜，法国和越南，英国和马来亚……人民之间的友谊不能分家！
