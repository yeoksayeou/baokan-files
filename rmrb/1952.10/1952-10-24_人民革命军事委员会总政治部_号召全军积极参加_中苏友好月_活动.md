Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Calls on the Entire Army to Actively Participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities
Author:
Date: 1952-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd:

    The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission
    Calls on the Entire Army to Actively Participate in "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Activities
    Following the notification issued by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters regarding the holding of "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," the General Political Department of the Military Commission specifically issued instructions, calling on the entire army to actively participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" work.
    The instructions first pointed out: The "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" decided to be held nationwide by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters is extremely necessary for the troops. All departments should combine the current central tasks and specific conditions of the troops, and mobilize all propaganda forces of the troops to do this work well.
    The instructions then requested: Through "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," an education on the prospects of socialism and communism and an education on internationalism should be conducted in the troops, so that all commanders and fighters deeply recognize that the Soviet Union is our good teacher and helpful friend, and that the Sino-Soviet friendly alliance and the Soviet Union's selfless assistance to our country are of decisive significance for accelerating our country's various construction undertakings and defending Eastern and world peace, so as to enhance the patriotic and internationalist spirit of the troops; and the propaganda of "learning from the Soviet army" should be emphasized, promoting the powerful and invincible Soviet army as the best example for our army's construction, in order to encourage the troops to accelerate the modernization of construction. It is required that this propaganda should be widespread and in-depth, so that every commander and fighter can be educated, laying the foundation for the regular Sino-Soviet friendship work of the troops in the future.
    Regarding the propaganda methods for "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," the instructions stipulated: All troops must hold grand celebration meetings on the anniversary of the October Revolution; all newspapers and periodicals of the troops should publish special issues on Sino-Soviet friendship and learning from the Soviet Union, publishing papers, speaking materials or articles and reports from responsible comrades; the "Troop Program" of the Central People's Broadcasting Station and the wired broadcasting in the troops must organize programs related to Sino-Soviet friendship for propaganda; all troops should widely hold film evenings, exhibitions, lectures and symposiums of different scales according to the specific conditions of the troops; all cultural troupes and work teams should prepare propaganda programs related to Sino-Soviet friendship, make tour performances in the troops, and universally teach Sino-Soviet friendship songs.
    Finally, in order to more forcefully carry out the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" work, the instructions also emphasized: All troops should keep in touch with the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at any time, and appropriately cooperate with and participate in various activities of the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 人民革命军事委员会总政治部  号召全军积极参加“中苏友好月”活动

1952-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部
    号召全军积极参加“中苏友好月”活动
    【新华社二十二日讯】在中苏友好协会总会发出关于举行“中苏友好月”的通知后，军委总政治部特发布指示，号召全军积极参加“中苏友好月”工作。
    指示首先指出：中苏友好协会总会决定在全国范围内举行的“中苏友好月”，对部队极为需要，各部应结合部队当前中心任务和具体情况，动员部队所有宣传力量作好这个工作。
    指示中继即要求：通过“中苏友好月”，要在部队中进行一次社会主义与共产主义前途的教育和国际主义的教育，务使全体指战员深刻认识苏联是我们的良师益友，中苏友好同盟和苏联对我国的大公无私的援助，对加速我国的各种建设事业、保卫东方与世界和平有决定的意义，以提高部队爱国主义与国际主义的精神；并应着重“向苏联军队学习”的宣传，宣传强大无敌的苏联军队，就是我们军队建设的最好榜样，以鼓舞部队加速进行现代化的建设。要求这次宣传应普遍深入，使每个指战员都受到教育，为今后部队经常的中苏友好工作打下基础。
    在“中苏友好月”的宣传办法上，指示中规定：在十月革命节各部队须隆重举行庆祝会；各部队所有报纸刊物都应出版关于中苏友好与学习苏联的专号，发表论文、讲话材料或负责同志的文章报告；中央人民广播电台“部队节目”和部队中的有线广播，须组织有关中苏友好的节目进行宣传；各部队应根据部队具体情况，广泛地举行不同规模的电影晚会、展览会、讲演会和座谈会；一切文工团、文工队应准备有关中苏友好的宣传节目，在部队中作巡回演出，并普遍教唱中苏友好的歌曲。
    最后，为更有力地开展“中苏友好月”工作，指示中还强调指出：各部队应随时与当地中苏友协取得联系，并适当配合和参加驻地中苏友协的各种活动。
