Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Army Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1952-10-08
Page: 1

    People's Revolutionary Military Commission General Political Department
    Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Army Art Troupe

   [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet on the evening of the 7th for all members of the Czechoslovak Vit Nejedlý Army Art Troupe. Deputy Director Xiao Hua presided over the banquet. Invited to attend were Minister of the General Logistics Department of the Military Commission Yang Li-san, Deputy Political Commissar of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wu Fa-xian, Deputy Political Commissar of the Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Li Tian-huan, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Zhi-xiang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government Zhu Zao-guan, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department Li Zhao-bing, and more than 400 senior military officers and literary and art workers from the Army, Air Force, and Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Also invited to attend were Chen Qi-han, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army National Day observation delegation, and deputy heads Zhang Kai, Li Zuo-peng, Ding Qiu-sheng, Yin Xian-bing, Gao Wei-song, and Wang Hui-qiu, as well as Gan Si-qi, head of the Chinese People's Volunteers National Day returning observation delegation, and others.

    Also invited to attend were Yu Xin-qing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

    Also invited to attend were Jaroslav Jablonski, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic's Embassy in China, and Military Attaché Procha. Mrs. Ambassador Konsala of the Czechoslovak Republic in China was also invited to attend.

    During the banquet, guests and hosts frequently toasted the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Czechoslovakia, the cultural exchange between the two countries, and the health of President Gottwald, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Marshal Stalin.

    The banquet took place in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 人民革命军事委员会总政治部  欢宴捷军队文艺工作团

1952-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部
    欢宴捷军队文艺工作团
   【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部于七日晚欢宴捷克斯洛伐克维特·尼耶德利军队文工团的全体人员。宴会由萧华副主任主持，应邀作陪的有军委总后勤部杨立三部长，中国人民解放军空军副政委吴法宪，中国人民解放军公安部队副政委李天焕，中国人民解放军华北军区政治部副主任张致祥，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳，中国人民解放军陆、空、海军高级军官及部队文艺工作者四百余人。
    应邀作陪的还有中国人民解放军国庆观礼代表团团长、副团长陈奇涵、张凯、李作鹏、丁秋生、尹先炳、高维嵩、王辉球和中国人民志愿军国庆归国观礼代表团团长甘泗淇等。
    应邀作陪的还有中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗。
    应邀作陪的还有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基及武官普罗查。捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使康萨拉夫人亦应邀作陪。
    席间宾主频频为中捷两国人民及其军队的友谊，为中捷两国文化的交流，为哥特瓦尔德总统、毛泽东主席、斯大林大元帅的健康而干杯。
    宴会在友好热烈的气氛中进行。
