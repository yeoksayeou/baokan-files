Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-10-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    All nationalities in our country are closely united in one big family and enjoy full democratic rights. Now, many areas where ethnic minorities live in compact communities have implemented ethnic regional autonomy. This is the people of the Miao nationality in Xiangxi, Hunan Province. They are joyfully singing and dancing to celebrate the establishment of the autonomous region's people's government.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各民族亲密团结在一个大家庭里，享有充分的民主权利。现在许多少数民族聚居的地区都已经实行了民族区域自治。这是湖南省湘西苗族的人民。他们正为庆祝自治区人民政府的成立而欢欣歌舞。
