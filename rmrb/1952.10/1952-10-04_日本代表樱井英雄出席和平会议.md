Title: Japanese Representative Hideo Sakurai Attends Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd.

    Japanese representative Hideo Sakurai attends peace conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] Hideo Sakurai, Chairman of the Japan Bussan Co., Ltd., the Japanese representative who came to Beijing at the end of September to discuss Sino-Japanese trade, has been confirmed by the Japanese Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference as a formal representative of the Japanese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and attended the opening ceremony of the conference on the 2nd.

### 日本代表樱井英雄出席和平会议

1952-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    日本代表樱井英雄出席和平会议
    【新华社二日讯】九月底来北京商谈中日贸易的日本代表、日本巴桑事株式会社董事长樱井英雄，已经亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议日本筹备委员会确定为出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的日本代表团的正式代表，并已于二日出席会议的开幕式。
