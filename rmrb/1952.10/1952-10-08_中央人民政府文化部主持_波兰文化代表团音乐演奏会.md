Title: Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government Presents: Concert by the Polish Cultural Delegation

Author:
Date: 1952-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government held a music concert by the Polish Cultural Delegation here at 3:00 PM today. All six members of the Polish Cultural Delegation were invited to attend. The concert was personally presided over by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government. Representatives from Chinese government departments and people's organizations attending the concert included: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Sha Kefu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee; Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Arts Bureau of the Ministry of Culture; Huang Yaomian, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Ai Qing, a National Committee member; and Wu Qing, a specialist from the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Also attending the concert were over a hundred Chinese musicians including Li Ling, Jin Ziguang, Ma Ke, Sun Shen, He Shide, Bian Jun, Guo Lanying, Lao Zhicheng, Li Yuanqing, Wang Yunjie, Qu Wei, Zhou Guangren, and Yang Bingsun.

The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo and his wife, along with all the staff of the embassy, were also invited to attend the concert.

Members of the Polish Cultural Delegation - pianist Shimanovitz and vocalist Kaviecka - played and sang several works by famous Polish composers Chopin, Mazurka, Moniuszko, and famous Soviet composers Rachmaninoff and Gliere. Each program garnered enduring applause from the audience.

After the performance, a reception was held, where members of the Polish Cultural Delegation and Chinese musicians exchanged experiences in artistic work. The reception proceeded in a joyful and harmonious atmosphere, ending happily at 6:00 PM.

### 中央人民政府文化部主持  波兰文化代表团音乐演奏会

1952-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府文化部主持
    波兰文化代表团音乐演奏会
   【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府文化部今日下午三时在此间举行波兰文化代表团音乐演奏会。波兰文化代表团全体团员六人应邀出席。演奏会由中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林亲自主持。出席演奏会的我国政府部门和人民团体代表有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、文化部办公厅主任沙可夫、文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、文化部艺术局副局长周巍峙、全国文学艺术界联合会副秘书长黄药眠、全国委员艾青、外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青等。出席演奏会的还有我国音乐工作者李凌、金紫光、马可、孙慎、何士德、边军、郭兰英、老志诚、李元庆、王云阶、瞿维、周广仁、杨秉荪等百余人。
    波兰驻我国大使基里洛克和他的夫人以及使馆全体人员也应邀出席了演奏会。
    波兰文化代表团团员——钢琴家史曼诺维契及歌唱家卡维茨卡在会上奏唱了波兰名作曲家萧邦、玛佐尔卡、莫纽什柯及苏联名作曲家拉赫玛尼诺夫、格里埃尔等作品多首。每个节目都博得听众历久不息的掌声。
    演奏完毕后并举行酒会，波兰文化代表团团员和我国音乐工作者在会上交流了两国艺术工作的经验。酒会在欢乐融洽气氛中进行，到六时尽欢而散。
