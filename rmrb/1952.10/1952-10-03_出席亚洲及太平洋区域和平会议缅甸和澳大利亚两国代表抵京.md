Title: Burmese and Australian Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Some representatives from Australia and Burma, attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd. Two Indian observers also arrived at the same time. Greeting them at the airport were Hu Yuzhi, representative of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Shao Zonghan, director of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and others. Representatives from Australia, Burma, and India who had previously arrived in Beijing to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference also welcomed them at the airport.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议缅甸和澳大利亚两国代表抵京

1952-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    缅甸和澳大利亚两国代表抵京
    【新华社二日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的澳大利亚和缅甸两国的部分代表于二日乘飞机到达北京，同时到达的还有印度列席代表二人。前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会代表胡愈之，中缅友好协会理事邵宗汉等人。到机场欢迎的还有前已到京的澳大利亚、缅甸和印度出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表。
