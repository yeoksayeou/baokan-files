Title: National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Holds Banquet to Welcome Tibetan Delegation and Ethnic Representatives from Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1952-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th to welcome the Tibetan Delegation and all representatives of forty-six ethnic groups from various regions.

Those attending the reception included: Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee; Standing Committee members Li Weihan, Wang Kunlun, Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Ma Xulun, Ulanhu, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi; and members Yu Xinqing, Mei Gongbin, Zhou Xinmin, Shi Fuliang, Xu Deheng, Xiao Hua, Liu Zijiu, Yi Lirong, Shi Liang, Wu Han, Zheng Zhenduo, Zhu Zaoguan, Fei Zhendong, Zhao Puchu, Dong Luan, Qian Changzhao, Li Shucheng, Chen Qiyuan, and others, totaling more than forty people.

At the banquet, Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, delivered a welcoming speech. Vice-Chairman Li Jishen said: The People's Republic of China is a big family of fraternal cooperation among all ethnic groups in China. In the past three years, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, our big family has achieved great successes and victories in opposing aggression, defending peace, and national construction. Through three years of fraternal cooperation, all fraternal ethnic groups across the country have also achieved unprecedented great unity. These victories and achievements, especially the unprecedented great unity achieved by our fraternal ethnic groups, are inseparable from the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao. Without the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, we could not have achieved these great victories and successes, nor would we have such a big family of fraternal cooperation as we have today.

During the banquet, the attendees frequently raised their glasses to wish good health to the representatives of the fraternal ethnic groups and good health to Chairman Mao, the great leader of all ethnic groups in China.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of intimacy and friendship.

### 人民政协全国委员会举行宴会  招待西藏致敬团及各地民族代表

1952-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会举行宴会
    招待西藏致敬团及各地民族代表
    【新华社十八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在十八日晚设宴招待西藏致敬团及各地区四十六个民族的全体代表。
    出席招待会的有：人民政协全国委员会副主席李济深、沈钧儒，常务委员李维汉、王昆仑、蒋光鼐、章伯钧、章乃器、马叙伦、乌兰夫、梁希、邵力子，委员余心清、梅龚彬、周新民、施复亮、许德珩、萧华、刘子久、易礼容、史良、吴晗、郑振铎、朱早观、费振东、赵朴初、董鲁安、钱昌照、李书城、陈其瑗等四十余人。
    宴会上，人民政协全国委员会李济深副主席致词欢迎。李济深副主席说：中华人民共和国是中国各民族人民友爱合作的大家庭。三年来，我们这个大家庭在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下，在反对侵略、保卫和平和国家建设各方面都得到了伟大的成绩和胜利。经过三年来的友爱合作，全国各兄弟民族也达到了空前大团结。这些胜利和成绩，特别是我们各兄弟民族能够得到空前大团结，是和中国共产党、毛主席的领导分不开的。没有中国共产党、没有毛主席，我们就不可能有这些伟大的胜利和成绩，也不会出现像今天这样友爱合作的大家庭。
    席间，赴宴者频频举杯祝各兄弟民族代表们的健康、祝中国各族人民伟大领袖毛主席的健康。
    宴会在亲密友爱的空气中进行。
