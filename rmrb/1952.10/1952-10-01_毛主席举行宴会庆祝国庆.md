Title: Chairman Mao Holds Banquet to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th September: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government held a banquet on the evening of September 30th, the eve of the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, to celebrate National Day.

Distinguished guests invited to attend this grand banquet included: the head of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Prime Minister Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the members of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic he led: Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic Lhamsuren, Minister of Education of the Mongolian People's Republic Shirindib, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Jalkhasaikhan.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China who were invited to attend the celebration banquet included: Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Rudenko; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Saffronko; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Bulgarian People's Republic to China, Petkov; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to China, U Myat Tun; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Pakistan to China, Rochar; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Kwon O-jik; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirilok; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to China, Raghavan; Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the title of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, Koenig; Representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China with the title of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, Hoang Van Hoan; Minister of the Kingdom of Denmark to China with the title of Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary, Munk; Minister of the Republic of Finland to China with the title of Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary, Cronvall; Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China, Isa Matari; Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Kingdom of Sweden in China, Bevin; Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in China, Godev; Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Legation of the Swiss Confederation in China, Stiner; Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in China, Jabrosky, and over one hundred others.

The following government delegations currently in Beijing were invited to attend the celebration banquet: the Czechoslovak "Vit Nejedlý Army Art Troupe," the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Ensemble, the Burmese Land Reform Visiting Group, the Ceylon Trade Delegation, and more than twenty others.

Foreign guests invited to the banquet included representatives of international organizations attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference: Montague, Secretary of the World Peace Council; Grassi, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions; Sundaram, Member of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions; Santi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation; Mrs. Buffy, Treasurer of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers; Echevarria, Vice-President of the International Union of Students, specially invited guests of the aforementioned Peace Conference: D'Arbousse, Vice-President of the World Peace Council; Mrs. Felton, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize; Turkish famous poet Hikmet; Brandweiner, Member of the World Peace Council; Chermont, Chairman of the Brazilian Committee for the Defense of Peace; Fabio, Member of the World Peace Council, delegations and representatives of various countries of the aforementioned Peace Conference: Australia, Burma, Canada, Ceylon, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Ecuador, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Laos, Malaya, Mexico, Mongolia, New Zealand, Nicaragua, Pakistan, Panama, Peru, Philippines, Thailand, the United States, the Soviet Union, Vietnam, as well as the delegations and representatives of the following countries attending the aforementioned Peace Conference as observers: Algeria, Brazil, Britain, Cyprus, France, Iran, Iraq, Israel, Lebanon, Syria, Turkey. More than three hundred people in total.

Foreign guests invited to the banquet also included: people's visiting delegations from various countries currently in Beijing: the Britain-China Friendship Association Visiting Group, the Icelandic Visiting Delegation, the French Youth Delegation, and more than forty others.

Foreign guests invited to the banquet also included: Soviet experts, the President and doctors of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, and more than sixty others.

Invited to attend this grand banquet were: representatives and combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, the Tibetan Delegation for Greetings and representatives of various regions and ethnic groups, representatives of overseas Chinese returning to the country, industrial labor models, agricultural labor models, water conservancy project and railway engineering models, presidents and professor representatives of universities in Beijing, representatives of literary and art workers and opera workers, and over one thousand two hundred others.

Invited to accompany the guests were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Presidents Wu Gaizhi, Zhang Zhiran, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Lan Gongwu, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Su Yu, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, as well as members of the Central People's Government Committee, members of the Government Administration Council, heads of the commissions, ministries, departments, academies, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, senior officers of the PLA Army, Navy, and Air Force, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and more than one hundred and ninety others.

At the banquet, Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a speech saying:

"Dear friends:

The third anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China has arrived!

In the past year, we have done a lot of work for the happiness of the people and international peace; I hope we can do more work in the coming year.

Long live the People's Republic of China!

Long live the great unity of the people of all nationalities in the China and overseas Chinese!

Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers!

Long live the Sino-Soviet friendship and unity!

Long live the Sino-Mongolian friendship and unity!

Long live the friendship and unity between China and all new democratic countries!

Long live the friendship and unity of the people of all Asian countries!

Wish the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference success!

Long live the friendly unity of the people of the world!" Chairman Mao Zedong immediately raised his glass to all those present and said:

"To everyone's health, Cheers!" The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of unity, friendship, and joy and ended at nine o'clock.

### 毛主席举行宴会庆祝国庆

1952-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席举行宴会庆祝国庆
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席在    中华人民共和国成立三周年前夕——九月三十日晚七时举行宴会，欢庆国庆节。
    应邀出席这一盛大宴会的贵宾，有：蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔及他率领的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团的团员：蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦、蒙古人民共和国教育部部长锡林迪布、蒙古人民共和国驻华特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗。
    应邀出席庆祝宴会的各国驻华使节和各国使馆外交人员，有：罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使鲁登科、匈牙利人民共和国驻华特命全权大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使彼得科夫、缅甸联邦驻华特命全权大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦驻华特命全权大使罗查、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华特命全权大使权五稷、波兰人民共和国驻华特命全权大使基里洛克、印度共和国驻华特命全权大使赖嘉文、德意志民主共和国驻华特命全权大使衔外交使团团长柯尼希、越南民主共和国驻华特命全权大使衔代表黄文欢、丹麦王国驻华特命全权公使穆克、芬兰共和国驻华特命全权公使克诺林、印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、瑞典王国驻华大使馆临时代办贝凡、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫、瑞士联邦驻华公使馆临时代办施禔纳、捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基等共一百余人。

    应邀赴宴的外宾，有出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各国际组织代表：世界和平理事会书记蒙塔古、世界工会联合会书记葛拉西、世界工联亚澳联络局委员桑顿、国际民主妇女联合会书记处副总书记桑蒂、国际民主法律工作者协会司库布非郁夫人、国际学生联合会副主席艾切伐里亚，上述和平会议的特邀人士：世界和平理事会副主席达波赛、斯大林国际和平奖金获得者费尔顿夫人、土耳其名诗人希克梅特、世界和平理事会理事布兰德魏纳、巴西保卫和平委员会主席彻蒙特、世界和平理事会理事费比奥，上述和平会议的各国代表团和代表：澳大利亚、缅甸、加拿大、锡兰、智利、哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、厄瓜多尔、萨尔瓦多、危地马拉、洪都拉斯、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜、寮国、马来亚、墨西哥、蒙古、新西兰、尼加拉瓜、巴基斯坦、巴拿马、秘鲁、菲律宾、泰国、美国、苏联、越南，以及列席上述和平会议的下列各国的代表团和代表：阿尔及利亚、巴西、英国、塞浦路斯、法国、伊朗、伊拉克、以色列、黎巴嫩、叙利亚、土耳其。共三百余人。

    宴会上毛泽东主席致词说：
    “亲爱的朋友们：
    伟大的中华人民共和国成立三周年纪念日到来了！
    过去的一年，为了人民的幸福和国际的和平，我们做了不少的工作；我希望在今后的一年我们能做更多的工作。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国各民族人民及海外华侨大团结万岁！
    中国人民解放军及中国人民志愿军万岁！
    中苏友好团结万岁！
    中蒙友好团结万岁！
    中国与各新民主主义国家友好团结万岁！
    亚洲各国人民友好团结万岁！
    祝亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的成功！
    全世界人民的友好团结万岁！”毛泽东主席旋即向全体到会者举杯说：
    “为了各位的健康，干杯！”宴会在团结友好的欢乐空气中进行，至九时结束。
