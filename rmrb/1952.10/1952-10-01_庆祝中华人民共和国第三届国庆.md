Title: Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1952-10-01
Page: 2

Chairman Kim Tu-bong of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Great Leader of the Chinese People:

On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China, I extend my warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the fraternal Chinese people on behalf of all the Korean people and myself.

Under your wise leadership, the respected and beloved, the fraternal Chinese people have created a free and powerful People's Republic of China and continue to achieve great victories in the cause of people's democracy. These achievements of the fraternal Chinese people have become a guarantee of the victory of the just struggle of the Korean and Chinese people against the US imperialist aggressors and have made a great contribution to upholding the cause of peace in Asia and the world.

In the flames of the just struggle against US imperialism, the Korean people, for the freedom, independence, and honor of Korea, and for the security of China and world peace, will, I believe, resolutely fight to the end with the Chinese People's Volunteers for the complete victory over the enemy.

Welcoming this glorious festival, I wish you and the fraternal Chinese people even greater achievements in the cause of peaceful construction, for the security of the peoples of Asia and lasting world peace.

Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
Pyongyang, September 30, 1952

Premier Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Zhou:

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

Respected Premier Zhou Enlai, on the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China, I extend my warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the Chinese people on behalf of all the Korean people and the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and myself. The victories won by the great Chinese people in the great cause of building a new China and in the common struggle against the US imperialist aggressors have further enhanced the firm confidence and infinite encouragement of the people of Asia and the world who are fighting for independence, freedom, and peace. In the struggle against the US imperialist aggressors, the Korean people, in order to further consolidate the friendship and solidarity between the Korean and Chinese people that have been solidified with blood, must wage a more resolute struggle against the enemy until the final victory is won.

I wish you brilliant achievements in the cause of the prosperity of the great People's Republic of China and lasting world peace.

Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
Pyongyang, September 30, 1952

Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao:

To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I extend to you, the government of the People's Republic of China, and the people my sincere and warm congratulations on behalf of the Vietnamese government and people and in my own name.

Under your leadership, the Communist Party of China, and the Central People's Government, the Chinese people have achieved many great successes in building a people's democratic system and in the movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea.

Learning from the glorious example of the Chinese people, and with the support of the Soviet Union, China, and various people's democratic countries, the Vietnamese people are determined to defeat the French colonialists and US imperialist interventionists, so that the war of resistance will win complete victory. I wish the Chinese people and government more new achievements in building a great China and resisting US aggression and aiding Korea.

I wish you good health.

Long live the brotherly solidarity and friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people.

Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

Chairman Groza of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania's Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Chairman, I extend to you my warmest congratulations on behalf of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic and myself, and wish you further victories in leading the Chinese people in the great struggle to build a people's China and win world peace.

Dr. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic
Bucharest, September 27, 1952

Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic
Bucharest, September 27, 1952

### 庆祝中华人民共和国第三届国庆

1952-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国第三届国庆
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长
    金枓奉给毛主席的贺电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东同志：    
    欣逢伟大的中华人民共和国成立三周年纪念，我谨以全体朝鲜人民和我个人的名义，向您并经过您向兄弟的中国人民致最热烈的祝贺。
    在尊敬的您的英明领导下，兄弟的中国人民创建了自由强大的中华人民共和国，并在人民民主的事业上继续争取了巨大的胜利。兄弟的中国人民所争取的这些成就，已成为朝中两国人民反对美帝国主义侵略者的正义斗争的胜利的保证，并对拥护亚洲及全世界的和平事业有巨大的贡献。
    在反对美帝国主义的正义斗争的火焰中，相信朝鲜人民为朝鲜的自由、独立与荣誉，为了中国的安全和世界的和平，与中国人民志愿军为彻底战胜敌人坚决奋斗到底。
    迎接光荣的节日，我谨祝您和兄弟的中国人民在和平建设事业上，为亚洲各国人民的安全和世界的持久和平，将有更巨大的成就。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民
    会议常任委员会委员长  金枓奉
    一九五二年九月三十日平壤市
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相
    金日成给周总理的贺电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来同志：
    尊敬的总理周恩来同志，欣逢伟大的中华人民共和国成立三周年纪念，我谨代表全朝鲜人民及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和以我个人的名义，向您并经过您向中国人民致最热烈的祝贺。伟大的中国人民在建设新中国的伟大事业上，在反对美帝国主义侵略者的共同斗争中所获得的胜利，更提高了为独立和自由和平而斗争的亚洲及全世界人民对胜利的坚强信心和无限鼓舞。朝鲜人民在反对美帝国主义侵略者的斗争中，为更加巩固以血凝固成的朝中两国人民的友谊和团结，对敌人一定要展开更坚决的斗争，直到取得最后胜利为止。
    祝您在为了伟大的中华人民共和国的繁荣和世界的持久和平的事业上获得辉煌的成就。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相  金日成
    一九五二年九月三十日平壤
    越南民主共和国政府主席

    中国人民在您、中国共产党和中央人民政府的领导下，在建设人民民主制度和抗美援朝运动中已获得许多伟大的成就。
    学习中国人民光辉的榜样，在苏联、中国和各人民民主国家的支持下，越南人民坚决击败法殖民主义者和美帝国主义干涉者，使抗战赢得完全胜利。敬祝中国人民和政府在建设伟大的中国与抗美援朝过程中获得更多新的成就。
    敬祝您健康。
    中越两国人民兄弟般的团结友谊万岁。
