Title: Communiqué on the Conclusion of the Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation Between the People's Republic ofChina and the Mongolian People's Republic
Author:
Date: 1952-10-05
Page: 1

    Communiqué on the Conclusionof the Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation Between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, in order to consolidate and develop the profound friendly relations that have long existed between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples, signed the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Beijing on October 4 after friendly and harmonious negotiations. The signatories to this agreement were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the Chinese side, and Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister, on the Mongolian side.
    Those who attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side included: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government; Li Jishen, Vice Chairman; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Chen Yun, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office; and Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department. On the Mongolian side were: Lhamsuren, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Shirindib, Minister of Education; Jalkhajaamts, Ambassador to China; Purbuzal, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Dulburjin, Deputy Director of the Oriental Department.

### 中华人民共和国与蒙古人民共和国  关于缔结经济及文化合作协定的公报

1952-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国与蒙古人民共和国
    关于缔结经济及文化合作协定的公报
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔为了巩固与发展中蒙人民之间早已存在的深厚的友好关系，经过友好与融洽的谈判，已于十月四日在北京签订了中蒙经济及文化合作协定。签订此协定者，中国方面为中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，蒙古方面为总理泽登巴尔。
    参加签字典礼者，中国方面有：中央人民政府主席毛泽东，副主席李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理陈云、黄炎培、邓小平，外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅主任王炳南，亚洲司司长陈家康；蒙古方面有：副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，教育部部长锡林迪布，驻中国大使贾尔卡赛汗，外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒，东方司副司长都勒布尔金。
