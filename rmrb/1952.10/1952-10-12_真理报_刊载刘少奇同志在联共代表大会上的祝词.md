Title: "Pravda" Publishes Comrade Liu Shaoqi's Congratulatory Speech at the CPSU Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-10-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 11th: TASS, Moscow, October 9th: "Pravda" published the congratulatory speech made by Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) on the 8th.

### “真理报”刊载刘少奇同志在联共代表大会上的祝词

1952-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    “真理报”刊载刘少奇同志在联共代表大会上的祝词
