Title: Decision on Disciplining Marty and Dijon: French Communist Party Central Committee Issues Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1952-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. "L'Humanité" published a communiqué from the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party on September 17, stating: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party, at its meeting on September 3rd and 4th, after listening to and discussing the report of the Political Bureau of the Party Central Committee, unanimously decided to remove Marty from his position as Secretary of the Party, while retaining his position as a member of the Political Bureau; to remove Dijon from his position as a member of the Political Bureau, while retaining his position as a member of the Central Committee. The communiqué pointed out: The Party Central Committee unanimously decided to give Comrades Marty and Dijon the necessary disciplinary measures because they politically disagreed with the Party's views and engaged in factional activities that endangered Party unity.

### 关于处分马尔梯和狄戎的决定  法共中央发表公报

1952-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    关于处分马尔梯和狄戎的决定
    法共中央发表公报
    【新华社八日讯】“人道报”在九月十七日发表了法国共产党中央委员会书记处的公报说：法国共产党中央委员会在九月三日和四日的会议上听取并且讨论了党中央委员会政治局的报告后，一致决定解除马尔梯所任党的书记的职务，而保留其政治局委员的职务；解除狄戎所任政治局委员的职务，而保留其中央委员会委员的职务。公报指出：党中央委员会一致通过予马尔梯同志和狄戎同志以必要的处分，是因为他们在政治上和党的意见不一致，并且进行派系活动而使党内团结有受损害的危险。
