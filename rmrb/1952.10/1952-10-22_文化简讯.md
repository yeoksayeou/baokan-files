    All-China Democratic Women's Federation Holds Reception to Welcome Female Performers of National Opera Performance Review

    On the 16th, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a reception to welcome the female performers of the First National Opera Performance Review. Over 200 female performers from delegations and review groups from major administrative regions, provinces, and cities across the country, who came to Beijing to participate in the performance review, were invited to attend the reception. Those present at the reception included Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, leading comrades from various departments, as well as Tian Xiujuan, director of the North China Regional Work Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Zhang Xiaomei, director of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation. Deng Yingchao spoke at the meeting, extending a warm welcome to all the female performers on behalf of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. She encouraged the female performers to strengthen their political studies, improve their performing skills, and implement Chairman Mao's literary and art policy of serving the workers, peasants, and soldiers. She also expressed her hope that they would take care of their health and serve the people for a long time. Subsequently, more than ten performers from various regions, including Chang Xiangyu, Fan Ruijuan, Xiao Wenyan, Qin Youmei, Wang Xiulan, Wang Yingqiu, and Hu Shufang, spoke or performed on stage. They all expressed their heartfelt gratitude for the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. They said that with Chairman Mao's leadership, they were able to break free from the heavy oppression they had suffered in the past, receive the care and assistance of the People's Government, and live such free and happy lives today.

    Agricultural Science Team of the Tibet Work Team of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government Arrives in Lhasa

    The Agricultural Science Team of the Tibet Work Team of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government arrived in Lhasa on October 14th. The main purpose of this team's visit to the Kangzang area is to investigate and study the agricultural, forestry, and animal husbandry conditions in the Kangzang area, and to assist the People's Liberation Army units stationed in Tibet in researching agricultural production technology. Before arriving in Lhasa, they conducted various investigations and research work in various parts of Xikang, and invited local farmers and herdsmen to hold several production symposiums, which were warmly welcomed by the people of all ethnic groups. The team consists of eleven experts in animal husbandry, veterinary medicine, botany, vegetables, soil, and water conservancy, led by Li Lianjie, a professor in the Soil Science Department of Beijing Agricultural University.

    文化简讯
    全国民主妇联举行欢迎会欢迎全国戏曲观摩大会女演员
    中华全国民主妇女联合会在十六日举行欢迎会，欢迎第一届全国戏曲观摩演出大会的女演员。应邀出席欢迎会的，有全国各大行政区、省、市来京参加观摩演出大会的代表团、观摩团的女演员共二百余人。出席欢迎会的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、李德全和各部门负责同志以及中华全国民主妇女联合会华北区工作委员会主任田秀涓，北京市民主妇女联合会主任张晓梅等。邓颖超在会上讲话，她代表全国民主妇联向全体女演员表示热烈的欢迎。她勉励女演员加强政治学习，提高演技，贯彻毛主席为工农兵服务的文艺方针；并希望她们保重身体，长期为人民服务。接着有各地区演员常香玉、范瑞娟、筱文艳、秦友梅、王秀兰、王盈秋、胡漱芳等十余人上台讲话或演唱。她们一致衷心地感谢毛泽东主席的领导。她们说有了毛主席的领导，她们才能解脱了过去所受到的重重压迫，得到人民政府的关怀和帮助，过着今天这样自由幸福的生活。
    中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会西藏工作队农业科学组抵拉萨
    中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会西藏工作队农业科学组于十月十四日抵达拉萨。该组这次来到康藏地区，主要是调查研究康藏地区农林、畜牧业情况，并协助人民解放军进藏部队研究农业生产技术。他们在到达拉萨前曾在西康各地进行了各种调查研究工作，邀请当地农民、牧民开了多次生产座谈会，受到各族人民的热烈欢迎。该组共有畜牧、兽医、植物、蔬菜、土壤、水利等十一位专家，由北京农业大学土壤系教授李连捷领队。
