Title: Petrov, Soviet Ambassador, Visits US Ambassador

Author: 
Date: 1946-09-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 20th: Nanjing dispatch: Soviet Ambassador Petrov visited US Ambassador Stuart on the afternoon of the 19th at 5 o'clock.
