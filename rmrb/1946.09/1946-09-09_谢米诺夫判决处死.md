Title: Semenov Sentenced to Death

Author:

Date: 1946-09-09
Page: 1

　　Semenov Sentenced to Death
    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 6th. Moscow dispatch: Semenov, a notorious White Russian counter-revolutionary leader, and four others were sentenced to death by the Supreme Military Tribunal here on the 31st of last month. Note: Semenov and others were captured by the Soviet Union when they defeated the Japanese invaders in Northeast China. Between the two world wars, this criminal and others organized White bandits in the Far East, mainly Siberia, to sabotage the Soviet Union. Later, in Northeast China, they assisted the Japanese invaders in creating the September 18th Incident, and had close ties with the Japanese fascist organization Black Dragon Society and White Russian anti-Soviet organizations in Europe and the United States.

### 谢米诺夫判决处死

1946-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　谢米诺夫判决处死
    【新华社延安六日电】莫斯科讯：著名白俄反革命头子谢米诺夫等五人，已被此间最高军事法庭于上月三十一日判决处死。按：谢米诺夫等系苏联在东北击溃日寇时捕获，在两次大战之间，该犯等曾在远东主要是西伯利亚组织白匪，破坏苏联，后在东北协助日寇制造成九一八事变，与日本法西斯组织黑龙会及欧美白俄反苏组织俱有密切联系。
