    Good Medicine for Weeding

    The staff of the Huangken Sales Office of the Jianyang County Commercial Bureau in Fujian Province recently invented a weeding powder. This powder can kill weeds in the field without harming rice seedlings, solving the problem of weeding after close planting.

    The formula for this powder is: 20% soda ash, 5% sulfur, 5% copperas, 5% 0.6% 666 powder, 5% copper sulfate, and 60% lime. When this powder is sprinkled on the weeds in the field, the weeds rot within a few hours (the rice seedlings are unharmed). According to repeated experiments, if used more often, the weeds can be completely eradicated. This liberates a large amount of labor and saves a lot of time, and the rotten grass becomes fertilizer.

### 除草良药

1958-09-03
第3版()
专栏：

    除草良药
    福建省建阳县商业局黄坑营业所的职工最近发明了一种除草药粉。这种药粉可以毒死田中杂草而不伤害稻苗，解决了密植后的中耕除草问题。
    这种药粉的配方是：土碱20％、硫磺5％、青矾5％、0.6％六六六粉5％、硫酸铜5％、石灰60％配成。这种药粉撒在田里的杂草上，几小时后杂草就腐烂了（稻苗无损）。据多次实验，如果运用次数多些，便能将杂草彻底斩绝。这样便解放了大批劳力和节省了很多时间，并且腐烂后的草还成了肥料。
