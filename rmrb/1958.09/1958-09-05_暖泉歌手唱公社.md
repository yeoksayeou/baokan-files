Title: Warm Spring Singers Sing of the Commune
Author: Xu Chi
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 8

　　Warm Spring Singers Sing of the Commune
　　Compiled by Xu Chi
    The Warm Spring Township in Huailai County, Hebei Province, has joined the Shacheng People's Commune. The singers of this township have created many folk songs. Among them, Wang An's song was recited in public at the commune's founding meeting.
　　My Heart is Happy to Establish a Commune
　　My heart is happy to establish a commune,
　　I wake up laughing several times a night.
　　My enthusiasm is like a fierce tiger leaping,
　　Soaring ten thousand feet to chase rockets.
　　I have already smashed superstition,
　　Stomping my feet, the earth spins.
　　Fighting at night to accumulate millions of catties of fertilizer,
　　The crops in Jiahe Village will change.
　　Warm Spring is building a hydroelectric power station,
　　Electricity will soon be everywhere.
　　Ten thousand acres of land are closely linked,
　　Mechanization is complete in every way.
　　The footsteps of communism,
　　Resound in our big family.
　　Commune members flood into the club,
　　Children flood into the kindergarten.
　　The steamed buns in the public canteen are fragrant,
　　Wearing fashionable Beijing floral jackets.
　　The beautiful and abundant life is just beginning,
　　Encouraging everyone to move forward forever.
　　(Former Jiahe Agricultural Cooperative Chairman Wang An)
　　The Great Commune is Realized Early
　　The great commune is realized early,
　　Life is getting more and more beautiful.
　　Asphalt roads are everywhere,
　　Tall pines and cypresses are planted on both sides.
　　The trees grow lush and green,
　　Bright electric lights shine,
　　Illuminating the cars moving forward,
　　Illuminating the farmers in the fields.
　　Young people are operating machines,
　　The loud noise is really energetic,
　　Increasing production and saving labor,
　　The saved people go to the factory,
　　To produce various industrial products,
　　Our happiness is endless.
　　  (Former Jiahe Cooperative Member Guo Quanbao)

### 暖泉歌手唱公社
徐迟
1958-09-05
第8版()
专栏：

　　暖泉歌手唱公社
　　徐迟辑
    河北省怀来县的暖泉乡，已经参加了沙城人民公社。这个乡的歌手创作了许多首民歌。其中王安的一首曾在公社成立大会上当众朗诵。
　　建立公社心喜欢
　　建立公社心喜欢，
　　一夜笑醒好几遍。
　　干劲好比猛虎跳，
　　高纵万丈追火箭。
　　早把迷信粉碎了，
　　把脚一跺地飞转。
　　夜战积肥千万斤，
　　夹河庄稼要改变。
　　暖泉修建水电站，
　　电气就要到处传。
　　万亩土地紧相连，
　　闹机械化样样全。
　　共产主义脚步声，
　　响到咱们大家庭。
　　社员涌进俱乐部，
　　小孩涌进幼儿园。
　　公共食堂馒头香，
　　北京花袄穿时鲜。
　　美第二版满生活初到来，
　　鼓舞大伙永向前。
　　（原夹河农业社社长　王安）
　　大公社来早实现
　　大公社来早实现，
　　生活越过越美满。
　　柏油公路到处有，
　　高高松柏栽两旁。
　　树上长得绿盈盈，
　　盏盏电灯放光明，
　　照见汽车往前行，
　　照见园里庄稼人。
　　青年正在开机器，
　　实实响的怪起劲，
　　又增产来又省人，
　　省出人来上工厂，
　　多造各样工业品，
　　咱们的幸福说不尽。
　　  （原夹河社社员　郭全宝）
