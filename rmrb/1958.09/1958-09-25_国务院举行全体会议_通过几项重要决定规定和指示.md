Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session, Adopts Several Important Decisions, Regulations, and Instructions
Author:
Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The 80th plenary session of the State Council was held on the afternoon of the 24th.

The meeting discussed and adopted the following important decisions, regulations, and instructions: 1. The Decision of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council on Cadres Participating in Physical Labor; 2. The Regulations of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council on the Graded Management of Market Prices; 3. The Regulations of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council on Improving the Planning Management System; 4. The Regulations of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council on Improving the Method of Approving Design Task Books for Basic Construction Projects Above Quota; 5. Several Regulations of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council on Improving the Materials Allocation System; 6. The Instructions of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council on Vigorously Developing the Copper and Aluminum Industries; 7. The Decision of the State Council on the Retirement of Military Cadres.

The meeting approved the State Council's several draft regulations on further improving the financial management system and improving the bank credit management system, and decided to submit this draft to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

The meeting also approved the State Council's decision to abolish the four counties of Hekou, Pingbian, Lijiang, and Cangyuan in Yunnan Province, and to establish the four autonomous counties of Hekou Yao, Pingbian Miao, Lijiang Naxi, and Cangyuan Va.

The meeting approved the State Council's proposal to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the establishment of the Basic Construction Commission.

Finally, the meeting also approved the list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  通过几项重要决定规定和指示

1958-09-25
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    通过几项重要决定规定和指示
    新华社24日讯  国务院全体会议第八十次会议，在24日下午举行。
    会议讨论和通过了下列一些重要的决定、规定和指示：一、中共中央、国务院关于干部参加体力劳动的决定；二、中共中央、国务院关于市场物价分级管理的规定；三、中共中央、国务院关于改进计划管理体制的规定；四、中共中央、国务院关于改进限额以上基本建设项目设计任务书审批办法的规定；五、中共中央、国务院关于改进物资分配制度的几项规定；六、中共中央、国务院关于大力发展铜、铝工业的指示；七、国务院关于军队干部退出现役问题的决定。
    会议通过了国务院关于进一步改进财政管理体制和改进银行信贷管理体制的几项规定（草案），并且决定将这个草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还通过了国务院关于撤销云南省河口、屏边、丽江、沧源四个县，设立河口瑶族、屏边苗族、丽江纳西族、沧源佧佤族四个自治县的决定。
    会议通过了国务院就设立基本建设委员会向全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出的议案。
    会议最后还通过了任免名单。
