Title: U.S. Aircraft Again Intrudes into the Airspace of Xiamen, Fujian Province; We Issue the Twelfth Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: On September 24th, two U.S. F4D fighter planes intruded into the airspace of the Xiamen area of Fujian Province. A spokesman for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to state: The Chinese government issues its twelfth serious warning regarding this military provocation by the United States.

### 美机又侵入我福建厦门地区领空  我第十二次提严重警告

1958-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又侵入我福建厦门地区领空
    我第十二次提严重警告
    新华社24日讯  9月24日美国F4D型战斗机两架侵入我国福建厦门地区的领空，我外交部发言人奉命声明：对于美国这种军事挑衅，中国政府第十二次提出严重警告。
