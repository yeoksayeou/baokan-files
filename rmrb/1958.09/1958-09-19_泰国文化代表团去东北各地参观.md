Title: Thai Cultural Delegation Visits Various Places in Northeast China
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Thai Cultural Exchange Promotion Delegation, a group of twelve, led by head of delegation Gula Shippalit, left Beijing on the 18th to visit Changchun, Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, and other places. After completing their visit to the Northeast, the delegation will return to Beijing to attend China's National Day celebrations.

### 泰国文化代表团去东北各地参观

1958-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国文化代表团去东北各地参观
    新华社18日讯　泰国促进文化交流团一行十二人，在古腊·柿巴立团长率领下，于18日离开北京到长春、沈阳、鞍山、抚顺等地参观访问。代表团将在结束东北的访问后回北京参加我国国庆节观礼。
