Title: A Glimpse of Steel Tapping
Author: Cheng Hui
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 8

　　A Glimpse of Steel Tapping (and another poem)
    Cheng Hui
　　A wisp of cloud, a stretch of sky,
　　Crimson clouds hang on the horizon high,
　　Sparks fly, golden light gleams,
　　In an instant, molten steel turns to flowing streams.

　　The Blast Furnace Opens Its Mouth in Laughter
　　The blast furnace opens its mouth in laughter,
　　Crimson light flashes, soaring to the sky after,
　　Steelworkers stand before the furnace with pride,
　　Scattering rainbows, far and wide.

### 出钢小景
承诲
1958-09-04
第8版()
专栏：

　　出钢小景（外一首）
    承诲
　　一脉云烟一脉天，
　　朵朵红云挂天边，
　　火花飞溅金光闪，
　　转眼钢水变河川。
　　高炉张开大嘴笑
　　高炉张开大嘴笑，
　　红光闪闪冲云霄，
　　钢铁工人炉前站，
　　撒下长虹千万道。
