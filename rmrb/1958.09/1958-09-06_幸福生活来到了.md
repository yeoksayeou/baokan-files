Happy Life Has Arrived (Clapper Talk)
　　Shen Pengnian

　　The Party Central Committee, Chairman Mao,
　　Point the way with the great banner, making decisions.
　　This decision is priceless treasure,
　　Five hundred million farmers all say it's good.
　　Priceless treasure, treasure priceless,
　　Its name is called collectivization.
　　Agricultural cooperatives, small merge with big,
　　From now on, we fear nothing.
　　Fear no floods, fear no droughts,
　　The Jade Emperor and Dragon King are turned around at will,
　　The five grains are abundant, we can't finish eating,
　　One mu of wheat yields ten thousand jin.
　　Fear no poverty, fear no blankness,
　　Two great revolutions come together,
　　Technical and cultural revolutions,
　　Causing the earth to shake and the heavens to move,
　　The countryside starts big factories,
　　Entering the village is like entering the city.
　　Both farmer and worker,
　　Doing business and being a soldier,
　　Running middle schools and universities,
　　Farmers are also college students.
　　Everyone is a jack-of-all-trades,
　　Eight-armed Nezha dares not boast,
　　Organized militarily,
　　Acting like a battle,
　　Living life, collectivized,
　　The benefits of the three “izations” are extremely great.
　　Collectivization becomes a giant,
　　Moving mountains and overturning seas is not too heavy,
　　Can overturn the seas, can move mountains,
　　A punch raised touches the sky.
　　The Party Central Committee, Chairman Mao,
　　Point the way with the great banner, making decisions,
　　This decision is priceless treasure,
　　Farmers cheer for the goodness of the commune,
　　The commune is good, the commune is good,
　　Happy life has arrived!

### 幸福生活来到了
沈彭年
1958-09-06
第8版()
专栏：

　　幸福生活来到了（快板）
    沈彭年
　　党中央，毛主席，
　　大旗一指拿主意。
　　这个主意无价宝，
　　五亿农民都说好。
　　无价宝，宝无价，
　　名子就叫公社化。
　　农业社、小并大，
　　从今以后天不怕。
　　不怕涝，不怕旱，
　　玉皇龙王随手转，
　　五谷丰登吃不了，
　　一亩小麦打十万。
　　不怕穷，不怕白，
　　两大革命一齐来，
　　技术、文化闹革命，
　　闹得地动天也动，
　　农村干起大工厂，
　　进村好像进了城。
　　又是农，又是工，
　　又做买卖又当兵，
　　办了中学办大学，
　　农民又是大学生。
　　人人都是多面手，
　　八臂哪吒难夸口，
　　组织起来军事化，
　　行动起来战斗化，
　　过日子，集体化，
　　三化好处非常大。
　　公社化成一巨人，
　　移山倒海不嫌沉，
　　能倒海，能搬山，
　　拳头一举碰着天。
　　党中央，毛主席，
　　大旗一指拿主意，
　　这个主意无价宝，
　　农民欢呼公社好，
　　公社好，公社好，
　　幸福生活来到了！
　　（附图片）
　　（人民美术出版社供稿）
