Title: Untie the Ropes Binding Japan to the American War Chariot: Japanese Socialist Party Demands Abolition of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty"
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 13th: The Japanese people's calls for revising the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" are growing louder. Several influential political parties and social organizations in Japan have made such demands.

According to Tokyo Radio broadcasts, the Japanese Socialist Party issued a special statement on the 12th, demanding the abolition of this treaty that "ties Japan to the American war chariot."

The statement emphasized that although Dulles outwardly made a gesture of concession to Japan, in reality, he "wants Japan to play a major role in realizing the United States' strategic plans in the Far East."

The statement concluded: "The Socialist Party demands that the Kishi Nobusuke government hold talks with the U.S. government and strive to abolish the Security Treaty."

### 解开把日本绑在美国战车上的绳索  日社会党要求废除日美“安全条约”

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    解开把日本绑在美国战车上的绳索
    日社会党要求废除日美“安全条约”
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社东京13日讯：日本人民要求修改美日“安全条约”的呼声日益响亮。日本一些有影响的政党和社会团体都提出了这种要求。
    据东京电台广播，日本社会党12日发表了特别声明，要求取消这个“把日本缚在美国战车上的”条约。
    声明强调指出，虽然杜勒斯在外表上也做出一种向日本让步的姿态，实际上“却是要日本在实现美国在远东的战略计划中起重大的作用”。
    声明最后说：“社会党要求岸信介政府同美国政府举行会谈，并且争取废除安全条约。”
