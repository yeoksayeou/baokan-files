Title: U.S. Warships Continue to Intrude into Our Territorial Waters, Provoking; I Lodge the Ninth Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - On September 21st, three U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Fujian region of our country three times in succession. A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated upon instruction: The American aggressors, disregarding the repeated warnings of the Chinese people and the ongoing Sino-American ambassadorial talks, continue to carry out serious military provocations. The Chinese government lodges its ninth serious warning regarding this.

### 美舰继续侵入我领海挑衅  我第九次提出严重警告

1958-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰继续侵入我领海挑衅
    我第九次提出严重警告
    新华社21日讯　9月21日，美国军舰三艘先后三次侵入我国福建地区的领海。我外交部发言人奉命声明：美国侵略者竟然不顾中国人民的一再警告和中美大使级会谈的进行，继续进行严重地军事挑衅。对此，中国政府第九次提出严重警告。
