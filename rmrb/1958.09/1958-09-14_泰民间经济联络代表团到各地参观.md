Title: Thai Private Economic Liaison Delegation Visits Various Places

Author: 
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. The Thai Private Economic Liaison Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, has left Beijing by train tonight for Changchun, Shenyang, Anshan, Dalian, Tianjin, Shanghai and other places to visit after completing their visit in Beijing. They will return to Beijing at the end of September to attend the National Day celebrations.

### 泰民间经济联络代表团到各地参观

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    泰民间经济联络代表团到各地参观
    新华社13日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请的泰国民间经济联络代表团在北京参观访问后，已于今晚乘车前往长春、沈阳、鞍山、大连、天津、上海等地参观。他们将于9月底返京参加国庆观礼。
