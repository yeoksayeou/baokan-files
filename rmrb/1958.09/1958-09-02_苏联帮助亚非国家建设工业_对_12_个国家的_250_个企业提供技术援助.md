Title: Soviet Union Helps Asian and African Countries Build Industry, Provides Technical Assistance to 250 Enterprises in 12 Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 1st: The Soviet Union is currently providing technical assistance to 250 enterprises and other construction projects in twelve Asian and African countries. These countries include India, the United Arab Republic, Indonesia, Afghanistan, Cambodia, and Ceylon.

    The Soviet Union is assisting the Egyptian region of the United Arab Republic in building dozens of industrial enterprises. Among them are four chemical plants, eight ferrous and non-ferrous metal plants, eleven machine-building plants, six oil processing plants, and nine food processing and light industry enterprises. In addition, the Soviet Union is also helping the Egyptian region with geological exploration and mining work, and is assisting in the establishment of twenty scientific research institutions. In the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic, the Soviet Union will help build thirty factories, help develop the Euphrates River, and build highways and railways.

### 苏联帮助亚非国家建设工业  对12个国家的250个企业提供技术援助

1958-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助亚非国家建设工业
    对12个国家的250个企业提供技术援助
    新华社莫斯科1日电　苏联正在对亚非十二个国家的二百五十个企业和其他建设工程提供技术援助。这些国家包括印度、阿拉伯联合共和国、印度尼西亚、阿富汗、柬埔寨、锡兰等国。
    苏联正在帮助阿拉伯联合共和国埃及地区建设几十个工业企业。其中，有四个化学工厂，八个黑色和有色金属工厂，十一个机器制造工厂，六个石油加工工厂和九个食品加工和轻工业企业。此外，苏联还帮助埃及地区进行地质勘探和采矿工作，并且在帮助建立二十个科学研究机构。在阿拉伯联合共和国的叙利亚地区，苏联将帮助建设三十个工厂，帮助开发幼发拉底河以及建设公路和铁路等。
