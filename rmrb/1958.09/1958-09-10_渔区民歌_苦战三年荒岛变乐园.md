Title: Fishermen's Folk Song: Three Years of Hard Work Transform Barren Island into Paradise

Author:

Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 7

Fishermen's Folk Song
Three Years of Hard Work Transform Barren Island into Paradise

　　Full ocean of motorized sailing boats,
　　Kelp carpets the harbor.
　　Chimneys like forests,
　　Islands sparkle with electric light.
　　Per-mu yield of two thousand catties,
　　Every household's grain like mountains.
　　Rocks bloom red flowers, (note)
　　Seawater dries into white salt.
　　Cattle and sheep run all over the mountains,
　　Barren island turns into paradise.
　　（Putuo County, Zhejiang Province）
    Note: Referring to growing laver on the rocks of the beach.

### 渔区民歌  苦战三年荒岛变乐园

1958-09-10
第7版()
专栏：

　　渔区民歌
    苦战三年荒岛变乐园
    满洋机帆船，
    海带铺港湾。
    烟囱像森林，
    岛岛电光闪。
    亩产双千斤，
    户户谷如山。
    岩石开红花，（注）
    海水晒白盐。
    牛羊满山跑，
    荒岛变乐园。
        （浙江省普陀县）
    注：指在海滩的岩石上养紫菜。
