Title: Attempting to Drive Israel to Provoke War: US-UK Plotting New Anti-Arab Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 11th: According to Amman's "Masses Daily", US-UK imperialists, in collaboration with the Israeli authorities and Jordanian traitors, are plotting a new conspiracy against the Jordanian people.

The newspaper writes that the conspiracy stipulates that Israeli troops will occupy the West Bank of the Jordan River. Once the Arab countries rise up against Israel, the US and UK troops stationed in the territories of Jordan and Lebanon will immediately intervene to occupy the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic and crush the union of Egypt and Syria. At the same time, in order to make it impossible for the Jordanian army to resist the Israeli offensive, King Hussein has ordered the recall of ammunition from the Jordanian troops stationed on the West Bank.

According to the newspaper, during Israeli Foreign Minister Golda Meir's visit to Europe, the "Baghdad" Pact member states - Turkey, Iran and Pakistan - have agreed to implement this plan against the Jordanian people.

### 企图驱使以色列挑起战争  美英策划反阿拉伯新阴谋

1958-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    企图驱使以色列挑起战争
    美英策划反阿拉伯新阴谋
    新华社12日讯　塔斯社大马士革11日讯：据“群众报”安曼消息，美英帝国主义者在以色列当局和约旦卖国贼的合作下正在策划反对约旦人民的新阴谋。
    报纸写道，这个阴谋规定以色列军队占领约旦河西岸。一旦阿拉伯国家起来反对以色列，驻在约旦和黎巴嫩领土上的美英军队马上插手干涉，来占领阿拉伯联合共和国的叙利亚地区，粉碎埃及和叙利亚的联合。同时，为了使约旦军队没有可能抵抗以色列的进攻，侯赛因国王已经下令收回驻在约旦河西岸的约旦军队的弹药。
    据这家报纸报道，在以色列外交部长梅厄夫人访问欧洲期间，“巴格达”条约成员国——土耳其、伊朗和巴基斯坦已经同意实现这项对付约旦人民的计划。
