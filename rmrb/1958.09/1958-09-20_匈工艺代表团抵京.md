Title: Hungarian Handicraft Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th -- A four-member Hungarian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation, led by Erdesz, Deputy Minister of Light Industry of Hungary and Chairman of the Central Federation of Hungarian Handicraft Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 18th. Ma Yuzhang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 匈工艺代表团抵京

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    匈工艺代表团抵京
    新华社19日讯　由匈牙利轻工业部副部长、匈牙利工艺合作社中央联社主席艾尔德什率领的匈牙利工艺合作社访华代表团一行四人，18日下午乘飞机抵京。前往机场迎接的有中华全国手工业合作总社副主任马豫章等。
