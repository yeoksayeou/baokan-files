Title: Peng Zhen and Chervenkov Speak at Banquet, Condemning US War Provocations in Unison
Author:
Date: 1958-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Vice Chairman Peng Zhen and Chairman Chervenkov both condemned the military provocations of US imperialism against the Chinese people at a banquet tonight welcoming the Bulgarian guests, unanimously pointing out that the shameless conspiracy of the American aggressors will be thoroughly defeated.

Tonight's banquet was held by Vice Chairman Peng Zhen to welcome all members of the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation and the Sofia City People's Council Delegation. In his speech at the banquet (full text on page four), Vice Chairman Peng Zhen said: The determination and confidence of the Chinese people to liberate their own territory, Taiwan and the Penghu Islands, are unshakable, and no war provocation by the United States will intimidate the Chinese people.

In his speech (full text on page four), Chairman Chervenkov pointed out that the entire Bulgarian people unanimously support the just struggle of the Chinese people and strongly condemn the shameless provocations of the aggressors, whose provocations will surely meet with a shameful defeat.

The solemn statements of Vice Chairman Peng Zhen and Chairman Chervenkov won long and enthusiastic applause from all Chinese and foreign guests attending the banquet. Also present at tonight's banquet were Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, and Bulgarian Ambassador to China Panchevski, among others.

The head of the Sofia City People's Council Delegation, Kostov, also said in his speech at the banquet that he fully agreed with Chairman Chervenkov's speech and warmly supported the just struggle of the Chinese people.

### 彭真、契尔文科夫在宴会上讲话  同声谴责美国战争挑衅

1958-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真、契尔文科夫在宴会上讲话
    同声谴责美国战争挑衅
    据新华社23日讯　彭真副委员长和契尔文科夫团长今晚在欢迎保加利亚贵宾的宴会上同声谴责美帝国主义对中国人民的军事挑衅，一致指出美国侵略者的无耻阴谋一定会遭到彻底失败。
    今晚的宴会是彭真副委员长为欢迎保加利亚国民议会代表团和索非亚市人民会议代表团全体人员而举行的。彭真副委员长在宴会上的讲话（全文见第四版）中说：中国人民解放自己的领土台湾和澎湖列岛的决心和信心是不可动摇的，美国的任何战争挑衅都不会吓倒中国人民。
    契尔文科夫团长在讲话（全文见第四版）中指出，保加利亚全体人民一致支持中国人民的正义斗争，并且强烈地谴责侵略者的无耻挑衅，它们的挑衅一定会遭到可耻的失败。
    彭真副委员长和契尔文科夫团长的严正表示，博得了参加宴会的所有中外客人长时间的热烈掌声。出席今晚宴会的，还有李济深、陈叔通副委员长，聂荣臻副总理，外交部姬鹏飞副部长，北京市副市长冯基平，保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基等。
    索非亚市人民会议代表团团长科斯托夫在宴会上的讲话中也说他完全同意契尔文科夫团长的讲话，热烈支持中国人民的正义斗争。
