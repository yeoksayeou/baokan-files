Title: Denouncing the American Warmongers
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 8

　　Denouncing the American Warmongers
    Guo Moruo
    Warmongers are detestable,
    Rampant and without measure!
    Just flexing muscles in West Asia,
    Now they want to make war on us.
    Who doesn't know Taiwan,
    Is Chinese territory?
    This historical fact,
    Is older than your America.
    Hateful handful of scum,
    Selling out their country into slavery.
    Willing to be American puppets,
    Openly recognizing the thief as their father.
    Therefore Taiwan and Penghu,
    Including Jinmen and Matsu,
    Are still occupied by the madmen,
    Nine years not returned to their homeland.
    The Chinese people are patient,
    Treating it as a pending case;
    Waiting for the appropriate time,
    To liberate all the islands.
    Who knew the American madmen,
    Would become even more presumptuous!
    妄想发动战争，
    继承轴心步武。
    妄想发动战争，
    继承轴心步武。
    妄想发动战争，
    继承轴心步武。
    Deluding themselves into starting a war,
    Inheriting the Axis' footsteps.
    Bringing out gunboat policy,
    You are an anachronism.
    May I ask where are Hiranuma and Tojo now?
　　而今竟在何处？
　　侵朝战争何如？
　　侵黎罪行受阻！
　　狂人总是健忘，
　　失掉全部脑髓！
　　What was the Korean War like?
　　The invasion of Lebanon's crimes were thwarted!
　　Madmen are always forgetful,
　　Losing all their brains!
　　Today's People's China,
　　Cannot be underestimated by madmen!
　　Today's Chinese people,
　　Cannot be bullied!
　　The spirit of the Chinese people,
　　Dare to think, dare to speak, dare to do;
　　Any great threat,
　　Fearless, without worry, without regard!
　　We have the strength to defend ourselves,
　　Protecting our sacred territory.
　　Six hundred million people are soldiers,
　　We will never retreat half a step!
　　You have nuclear submarines,
　　They can only scare away stingrays!
　　You have atomic weapons,
　　They can only frighten mice!
　　Dulles and Eisenhower,
　　Even ten thousand cannot help!
　　What "brink of war,"
　　You can shout until your dog's belly bursts!
　　You are launching an unjust war,
　　I am gaining abundant support.
　　Comrades-in-arms are all over the world,
　　Who is afraid of paper tigers?
　　The Soviet Union and Eastern European brothers,
　　Are all peaceful stalwarts.
　　Asia, Africa, and Latin America,
　　Are all anti-war companions.
　　Haven't you heard that being pointed at by ten fingers,
　　Makes one cry out in sickness even without disease?
　　Moreover, your madness is boundless,
　　Violating the public anger of humanity?
　　I tell you madmen to sober up,
　　Stop committing suicide!
　　The Taiwan Strait is not wide,
　　It will be your grave!
　　Having eyes but not recognizing Mount Tai,
　　Dare to come and disturb us;
　　I will make you go and never return,
　　Imperialism will end!
　　I advise the people of Europe and America,
　　To take on the attitude of masters!
　　Stop the warmongers,
　　Don't let them hesitate any longer!
　　For the sake of human happiness,
　　We must coexist peacefully.
　　In order to prevent the plague,
　　We must eliminate the virus!
　　Stopping the warmongers,
　　Is a sacred mission.
　　We dare to take on this responsibility,
　　Willing to be humanity's vanguard.
　　We want to make humanity equal,
　　Eternally loving and helping each other.
　　We want to make the world's affairs,
　　Eternally dominated by peace!
　　September 7, 1958

### 斥美国战争狂人
郭沫若
1958-09-08
第8版()
专栏：

　　斥美国战争狂人
    郭沫若
    战争狂人可恶，
    横行霸道无谱！
    才在西亚弄兵，
    又想对我动武。
    谁不知道台湾，
    它是中国领土？
    这个历史事实，
    比你美国还古。
    可恨一撮败类，
    卖国卖身为虏。
    甘做美国傀儡，
    公然认贼作父。
    因此台湾澎湖，
    连及金门马祖，
    尚被狂人霸占，
    九年未归故土。
    中国人民忍耐，
    作为悬案待处；
    等待适当时期，
    解放全部岛屿。
    谁知美国狂人，
    更加喧宾夺主！
    妄想发动战争，
    继承轴心步武。
    搬出炮舰政策，
    你是时代错误。
    请问希莫东条，
　　而今竟在何处？
　　侵朝战争何如？
　　侵黎罪行受阻！
　　狂人总是健忘，
　　失掉全部脑髓！
　　今日人民中国，
　　不容狂人低估！
　　今日中国人民，
　　不容受人欺侮！
　　中国人民精神，
　　敢想敢说敢做；
　　任何天大威胁，
　　无畏无忧无睹！
　　我们有力自卫，
　　保护神圣领土。
　　六亿人民皆兵，
　　决不后退半步！
　　你有热核潜艇，
　　只能哄走魟鱼！
　　你有原子武器，
　　只能骇倒老鼠！
　　杜勒斯和艾克，
　　有一万个何补！
　　什么“战争边缘”，
　　任你叫破狗肚！
　　你是师出无名，
　　我是得道多助。
　　战友遍满世界，
　　谁怕纸糊老虎？
　　苏联东欧兄弟，
　　都是和平干卤。
　　亚非拉丁美洲，
　　都是反战伴侣。
　　不闻十手所指，
　　叫人无病鸣呼？
　　何况疯狂无边，
　　干犯人类众怒？
　　叫你狂人清醒，
　　莫再操刀自屠！
　　台湾海峡不宽，
　　会是你的坟墓！
　　有眼不识泰山，
　　敢来头上动土；
　　叫你有去无回，
　　帝国主义结束！
　　奉告欧美人民，
　　拿出主人态度！
　　制止战争狂人，
　　莫再放任踌蹰！
　　为了人类幸福，
　　必须和平共处。
　　为了防止瘟疫，
　　必须消灭病毒！
　　制止战争狂人，
　　这是神圣任务。
　　我们敢于担当，
　　愿作人类前驱。
　　要使人类平等，
　　永恒友爱互助。
　　要使世界风云，
　　永由和平作主！
　　1958年9月7日
