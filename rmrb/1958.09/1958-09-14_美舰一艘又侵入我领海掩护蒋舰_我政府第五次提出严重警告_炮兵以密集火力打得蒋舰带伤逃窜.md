Title: U.S. Warship Again Intrudes into Our Territorial Waters to Cover Chiang Ships; Our Government Issues Fifth Stern Warning; Artillery Fires Intense Barrage, Driving Chiang Ships Away Wounded
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: At 3:55 AM on September 13th, a U.S. warship escorting Chiang Kai-shek's military transport ships again intruded into our territorial waters in the Kinmen and Xiamen areas. A spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare that the Chinese government is issuing its fifth stern warning regarding this.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 13th: At 3:48 AM and 4:20 PM on September 13th, Chiang Kai-shek's military transport ships twice attempted to reinforce Kinmen under the cover of U.S. warships. Our Fujian front artillery units bombarded them with intense fire. The Chiang Kai-shek military transport ships, after being hit, fled in panic without unloading their cargo.

### 美舰一艘又侵入我领海掩护蒋舰  我政府第五次提出严重警告  炮兵以密集火力打得蒋舰带伤逃窜

1958-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰一艘又侵入我领海掩护蒋舰
    我政府第五次提出严重警告
    炮兵以密集火力打得蒋舰带伤逃窜
    新华社13日讯　9月13日上午三时五十五分，为蒋军运输舰护航的美国军舰一艘又侵入我国金门、厦门地区的领海，我外交部发言人奉命声明，中国政府对此第五次提出严重警告。
    新华社福建前线13日电　9月13日上午三时四十八分和下午四时二十分，蒋军运输舰只两次企图在美国军舰的掩护下增援金门。我福建前线炮兵部队以密集炮火进行轰击。蒋军运输舰只在受创后，未及卸载即仓惶逃遁。
