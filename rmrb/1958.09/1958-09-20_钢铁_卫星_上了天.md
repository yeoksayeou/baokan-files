Title: Steel "Satellite" Ascends to the Sky
Author: Zhang Shihui
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 8

    Steel "Satellite" Ascends to the Sky
    Zhang Shihui, Worker at Wuhan Iron and Steel
    The blast furnace roars, flames leaping high,
    Red light pierces the nine layers of heaven,
    Molten iron rushes forth, surpassing the Yangtze's flow,
    Red warriors guard the furnace side.
    Labor songs resound in the clouds,
    Fluttering in the wind, spreading everywhere,
    Construction speed is like lightning,
    A steel "satellite" ascends to the sky.

### 钢铁“卫星”上了天
张世怀
1958-09-20
第8版()
专栏：

    钢铁“卫星”上了天
    武钢工人　张世怀
    高炉熊熊冒火焰，
    红光冲破九重天，
    铁水奔腾赛江水，
    红色的战士守炉边。
    劳动歌声响云间，
    随风飘扬到处传，
    建设速度似闪电，
    钢铁“卫星”上了天。
