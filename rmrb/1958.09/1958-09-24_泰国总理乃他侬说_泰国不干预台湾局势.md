Title: Thai Prime Minister Thanom Says Thailand Will Not Interfere in the Taiwan Situation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 23, Bangkok: According to newspaper reports, Thai Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn told reporters on the 19th, reiterating Thailand's position of non-intervention in the Taiwan Strait situation. He stated that Thailand would not provide the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan with spiritual or material support and assistance.

Thanom said that Thailand is unwilling to interfere in other people's affairs. Thailand is a small country, and the government's pressing task at present is to solve the various problems it encounters at home.

When a reporter asked about Chiang's gang's announcement that Thailand would support Taiwan, Thanom said that he did not know where Taiwan learned of the Thai government's attitude. He said that he had not expressed support for Taiwan in writing or in speech.

### 泰国总理乃他侬说  泰国不干预台湾局势

1958-09-24
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国总理乃他侬说
    泰国不干预台湾局势
    新华社23日讯　曼谷消息：据报纸报道，泰国总理乃他侬·吉滴卡宗19日向报界发表谈话，重申泰国对台湾海峡局势采取不介入立场。他表示泰国不会给台湾蒋介石集团以精神上和物质上的支持和援助。
    乃他侬说，泰国不愿意干预别人的事，泰国是一个小国，目前政府的迫切任务是解决国内所遇到的各项问题。
    有记者问到蒋帮曾宣布泰国将支持台湾的问题时，乃他侬说，他不知道台湾从哪里知道泰国政府的态度。他说，他没有在书信上或言论上表示过要支持台湾。
