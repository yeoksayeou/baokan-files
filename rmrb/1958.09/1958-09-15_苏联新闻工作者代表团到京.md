Title: Soviet Journalists' Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: A twelve-member Soviet journalists' delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 14th, led by the head of the delegation, Moscowsky, Director of the Russian Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the CPSU. The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by leading members of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the CPC, the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the All-China Journalists Association, and the capital's press circles.
The eleven members of the delegation are: Obiedkov, member of the editorial board of "Pravda"; Vavilina, deputy editor-in-chief of "Women Workers"; Velichko, editor-in-chief of "Young Generation"; Delopyshevsky, editor-in-chief of the Chelyabinsk Oblast Party newspaper; Kudyshev, editor-in-chief of the Pavlodar Oblast Party newspaper of Kazakhstan; Lazabonik, editor-in-chief of the Ukrainian "Worker's Newspaper"; Salemjagi, editor-in-chief of "Soviet Estonia"; Troyanovsky, feature reporter of "Soviet Russia"; Tais, feature reporter of "Izvestia"; Glitsch, counselor of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and translator Larin.

### 苏联新闻工作者代表团到京

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会和外交部新闻司邀请来我国访问的苏联新闻工作者代表团一行十二人，由代表团团长、苏共中央俄罗斯宣传部部长莫斯科伏斯基率领，在14日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团在飞机场受到了中共中央宣传部、外交部新闻司、中华全国新闻工作者协会和首都新闻界负责人的热烈欢迎。
    代表团的十一位团员是：“真理报”编委俄比埃德科夫，“女工”副主编瓦维林娜，“青年一代”主编维里赤科，车里雅宾斯克州党报主编得洛彼雪夫斯基，哈萨克巴夫洛达尔州党报主编库堆雪夫，乌克兰“工人报”主编拉扎波尼克，“苏维埃爱沙尼亚”主编萨列姆雅吉，“苏维埃俄罗斯报”特写记者特洛扬诺夫斯基，“消息报”特写记者台斯，外交部新闻司参赞葛利赤，翻译拉林。
