Title: Song of the Minfeng Furnace Workers
Author: Zhang Tingrong
Date: 1958-09-01
Page: 2

Song of the Minfeng Furnace Workers

　　Don't look down on this small, earthen furnace,
　　Everyone calls it the vanguard model.
　　It produces 700 jin of iron a day,
　　Accumulating to a mountain height in a year.

　　The blower roars and rumbles,
　　The smelting furnace blazes brightly,
　　Visitors throng in crowds,
　　The good news team drums loudly,
　　Fire dragons leap from the furnace.

　　The workers' energy is like Wu Song's,
　　Substituting white coal for coke is a success,
　　Costs are reduced and output is high,
　　The small, earthen blast furnace shows its might.
                (Zhang Tingrong)
　　(Attached picture)
Xinhua News Agency draft: The No. 0.18 earthen blast furnace of the Minfeng Boiler Factory in Suzhou City, Jiangsu Province, has been operating normally since it was ignited on the morning of July 26. Currently, Suzhou City is vigorously promoting this "Minfeng-style" earthen blast furnace. Gu Dongsheng (New China News Agency draft)

### 民丰炉炉工之歌
张听荣
1958-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　民丰炉炉工之歌
　　别看此炉土和小，
　　人人称它先锋号，
　　一天出铁七百斤，
　　一年下来像山高。
　　鼓风机声音隆隆，
　　炼铁炉火光熊熊，
　　参观者人头涌涌，
　　报喜队锣鼓咚咚，
　　炉膛内窜出火龙。
　　工人干劲如武松，
　　白煤代焦试成功，
　　成本降低产量高，
　　小土高炉显威风。
                （张听荣）
　　（附图片）
    江苏省苏州市民丰锅厂的○·一八土高炉，从7月26日清晨点火以来，生产一直正常。目前，苏州市正在大力推广这种“民丰式”土高炉。　　　　　　顾东升摄（新华社稿）
