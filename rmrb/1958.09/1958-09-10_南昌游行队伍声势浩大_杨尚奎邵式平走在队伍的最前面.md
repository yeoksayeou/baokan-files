Title: The Nanchang Parade is Mighty; Yang Shangkui and Shao Shiping Walked at the Front of the Procession
Author:
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang Telegram: On the afternoon of the 7th, 180,000 people in Nanchang City held a mass rally to support the Chinese government's statement and oppose the military provocations of U.S. imperialism. A mighty parade was held after the meeting.

Yang Shangkui, First Secretary of the Jiangxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Shao Shiping, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Governor, all secretaries of the Secretariat of the Provincial Committee, members of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Committee, heads of various departments, and the first secretaries of local and county committees participating in the ongoing Fifth Enlarged Plenary Session of the Provincial Committee also attended the meeting.

Guo Guangzhou, First Secretary of the Nanchang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, called on the city's people at the meeting to heighten their vigilance a hundredfold, strengthen preparations, arm themselves ideologically, and act organizationally. Workers, farmers, residents, intellectuals, industrialists, business people, students, and all the people of the city should immediately launch a new upsurge in production to support the Chinese People's Liberation Army in liberating Jinmen and Mazu, and liberating Taiwan. The meeting also unanimously adopted a resolution by people from all walks of life in the province and city supporting the Chinese government's statement and opposing the military threats of U.S. imperialism.

After the meeting, a parade was held. Yang Shangkui, Shao Shiping, provincial and municipal party and government leaders, and the first secretaries of local and county committees walked at the front of the procession.

On the morning of the same day, the Fifth Enlarged Plenary Session of the Jiangxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, which is currently being held, adopted a resolution supporting Premier Zhou's statement, calling on the people of the province to produce more steel and more grain, actively develop the great people's commune movement, enhance the strength to defend the motherland, and smash the aggressive conspiracies of U.S. imperialism.

### 南昌游行队伍声势浩大  杨尚奎邵式平走在队伍的最前面

1958-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　南昌游行队伍声势浩大
　　杨尚奎邵式平走在队伍的最前面
    据新华社南昌电　7日下午，南昌市十八万人举行了拥护我国政府声明、反对美帝国主义的军事挑衅的群众大会。会后举行了声势浩大的游行。
    中共江西省委第一书记杨尚奎和省委书记、省长邵式平及全部省委书记处书记、省委常务委员和各部负责人，以及参加正在举行的省委第五次扩大会议的各地委、县委第一书记也参加了大会。
    中共南昌市委第一书记郭光洲在会上号召全市人民百倍地提高警惕，加强准备，从思想上武装起来，从组织上行动起来，全市工人、农民、居民、知识分子、工商业者、学生和全体人民，目前要立即掀起一个新的生产高潮，支援中国人民解放军解放金门、马祖，解放台湾。会上还一致通过了省、市各界人民拥护我国政府声明，反对美帝国主义的军事威胁的决议。
    会后进行游行，走在队伍最前面的是杨尚奎、邵式平和省市党政领导人及各地委、县委第一书记。
    当日上午，正在举行的中共江西省委第五次扩大会议，通过了拥护周总理声明的决议，号召全省人民多产钢铁、多产粮食，积极开展伟大的人民公社运动，增强保卫祖国的力量，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋。
