Title: Greek "Dawn Daily" Points Out that the United States is an Aggressor in Both the Middle East and the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. "Dawn Daily" writes that the provocative actions launched by the United States and the Chiang Kai-shek elements against the People's Republic of China have created a hotbed of new tensions.

The newspaper pointed out that the United States' acts of aggression in the Far East are linked to its acts of aggression in the Middle East. The aggressors are attempting to refuse to implement the United Nations' decisions on the withdrawal of foreign troops from Lebanon and Jordan. "Dawn Daily" concluded by writing that the people know that the American aggressors are threatening peace in the Middle East and the Far East.

### 希腊“黎明日报”指出  美国在中东和远东都是侵略者

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    希腊“黎明日报”指出
　　美国在中东和远东都是侵略者
    新华社2日讯　“黎明日报”写道，美国和蒋介石分子对中华人民共和国发动的挑衅行为造成了新的紧张局势的温床。
    报纸指出，美国在远东的侵略行为同它在中东的侵略行为是有联系的，侵略者企图拒绝执行联合国关于外国军队撤出黎巴嫩和约旦的决定。“黎明日报”最后写道，人民知道，美国侵略者威胁着中东和远东和平。
