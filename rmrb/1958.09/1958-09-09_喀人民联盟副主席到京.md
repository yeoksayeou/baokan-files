Title: Cameroon People's Union Vice-Chairman Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th - Ernest Ouandié, Vice-Chairman of the Cameroon People's Union, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 喀人民联盟副主席到京

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　喀人民联盟副主席到京
    据新华社8日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请的喀麦隆人民联盟副主席爱尔耐斯特·翁第于今日下午到达北京。
