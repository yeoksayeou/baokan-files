Title: Thanking Support for the Finnish Art Exhibition: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram to President Kekkonen
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: The "Finnish Art Exhibition" has been deeply welcomed by the general public since its opening on September 1st at the Wenhua Hall of the Palace Museum. This exhibition was held with the direct support of the President of the Republic of Finland, Kekkonen, and his wife. The Finnish President and his wife sent a telegram to the organizers of the exhibition on the eve of its opening, congratulating them on its success.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to President Kekkonen and his wife on September 6th, expressing his gratitude for their support and sponsorship. The telegram stated: "Please accept my gratitude for Your Excellency the President's support and sponsorship. I hope that the friendly relations and cultural exchanges between our two countries will be further developed." Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also sent a telegram to President Kekkonen and his wife on the same day to express his gratitude.

### 答谢对芬兰艺术展览会的支持  周总理致电吉科宁总统

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    答谢对芬兰艺术展览会的支持
    周总理致电吉科宁总统
    新华社13日讯　“芬兰艺术展览会”自9月1日在故宫博物院文华殿开幕以来，深受广大观众的欢迎。这个展览会是在芬兰共和国总统吉科宁及其夫人直接赞助下举办的，芬兰总统及其夫人在展览会开幕前夕致电展览会组织者，祝贺展览会成功。
    国务院总理周恩来在9月6日致电吉科宁总统及其夫人，对他们的支持和赞助表示感谢。电文说：对总统阁下的赞助支持，谨致谢意，并愿贵我两国的友好关系和文化交流进一步发展。中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南也在同日致电吉科宁总统及其夫人表示感谢。
