Title: CCP Central Committee Instruction on Deep Plowing and Soil Improvement
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 6

Deep plowing is the central technical measure for increasing agricultural production. It is necessary to put politics in command, mobilize the entire Party, and involve all the people. From now on, before the autumn planting this year and in the coming winter and spring, we must launch a high tide of deep plowing and soil improvement.
               —CCP Central Committee Instruction on Deep Plowing and Soil Improvement

### 中共中央关于深耕和改良土壤的指示

1958-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    深耕是农业增产技术措施的中心。必须政治挂帅，全党动员，全民动手，从现在起，在今年秋种以前和今冬明春，掀起一个深耕和改良土壤运动的高潮。
               ——中共中央关于深耕和改良土壤的指示
