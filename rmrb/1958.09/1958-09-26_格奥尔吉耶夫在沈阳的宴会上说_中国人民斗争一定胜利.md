Title: Georgiev Says at Shenyang Banquet: The Chinese People's Struggle Will Surely Be Victorious
Author:
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 24th:  Liaoning Province Vice Governor Huang Da hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Bulgarian National Assembly delegation headed by Chervenkov and the Sofia City People's Assembly delegation headed by Kostov. The distinguished guests arrived in Shenyang this afternoon from Beijing for a visit.

    At the banquet, Vice Governor Huang Da warmly welcomed the distinguished guests on behalf of the people of Liaoning Province. He said that the delegation's visit to Liaoning Province is a great encouragement to the people of Liaoning Province who are engaged in the great leap forward in industrial and agricultural production and the struggle against American imperialist aggression. The delegation's visit to our country will further strengthen and consolidate the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Bulgarian people.

    Georgiev, a member of the Bulgarian National Assembly delegation and Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, and Kostov, head of the Sofia City People's Assembly delegation, both spoke at the banquet. Georgiev said that the unity and cooperation between Bulgaria and China and the countries of the socialist camp are the source of the rapid growth of the power of the socialist camp. After denouncing the shameful military provocations of American imperialism in the Taiwan Strait of our country, he said that the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan will surely be victorious. In his speech, Kostov also expressed his warm support for the Chinese people's just struggle against aggression.

    Also present at the banquet today were Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Major General He Qingji, Head of the Liaoning Garrison, Liu Baotian, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Shenyang, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City. Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of North Korea in Shenyang, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    The distinguished guests visited factories and toured the Beiling scenic spots in Shenyang this afternoon.

### 格奥尔吉耶夫在沈阳的宴会上说  中国人民斗争一定胜利

1958-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    格奥尔吉耶夫在沈阳的宴会上说
    中国人民斗争一定胜利
    新华社沈阳24日电  辽宁省副省长黄达今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团和以科斯托夫为首的索非亚市人民会议代表团。贵宾们是在今天下午由北京来到沈阳参观访问的。
    黄达副省长在宴会上代表辽宁省人民热烈欢迎贵宾。他说，代表团来辽宁省参观访问对正在进行工农业生产大跃进和反对美帝国主义侵略斗争的辽宁省人民是莫大的鼓舞。代表团这次来我国访问必将更进一步加强和巩固中保两国人民的友谊和团结。
    保加利亚国民议会代表团团员、国民议会主席团副主席格奥尔吉耶夫，索非亚市人民会议代表团团长科斯托夫都在宴会上讲了话。格奥尔吉耶夫说，保中两国和社会主义阵营国家间的团结合作是社会主义阵营的威力迅速增长的源泉。他在痛斥美帝国主义在我国台湾海峡进行可耻的军事挑衅后说，中国人民解放台湾的斗争一定胜利。科斯托夫在讲话中也对中国人民反侵略的正义斗争表示了热烈的支持。
    今天参加宴会的还有中共辽宁省委书记李荒，辽宁驻军首长贺庆积少将，中共沈阳市委书记、沈阳市市长刘宝田，以及辽宁省和沈阳市各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人。朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也应邀参加宴会。
    贵宾们今天下午在沈阳参观了工厂，游览了北陵名胜。
