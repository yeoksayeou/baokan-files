Title: Foreign Ministry Information Department Receives Chinese and Foreign Journalists, Announces Evidence of Chiang Kai-shek Planes Using US-Made Missiles
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs held a press conference this evening to expose to Chinese and foreign journalists in Beijing the new military provocations and crimes of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force, under the direct instigation of the United States, using missiles to attack our mainland air force. At the meeting, the head of the Information Department distributed to the journalists the "Statement of the Spokesman of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China on the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Using Missile Weapons to Attack Our Mainland Air Force under the Direct Instigation of the United States."

The physical objects of important parts of the US-made "Sidewinder" missile recovered in the Wenzhou area, such as the infrared receiving part (head), the guidance part, the unexploded warhead part, and the solid rocket part, were all exhibited at tonight's Chinese and foreign press conference. From the exhibited missile entities, journalists could see that the missile was more than two meters long, five inches in diameter, and the warhead was loaded with high explosives. On the aluminum alloy shell of the missile's power unit and solid rocket part, the words "five-inch rocket" and "Bureau of Ordnance" were written in English.

This crime of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force under the instigation of the American imperialist aggressors aroused great indignation among the journalists.

### 外交部新闻司招待中外记者  公布蒋机使用美制导弹罪证

1958-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    外交部新闻司招待中外记者
    公布蒋机使用美制导弹罪证
    新华社29日讯  外交部新闻司今晚举行记者招待会，向在北京的中外记者揭露了蒋介石空军在美国直接指使下，竟然使用导弹向我大陆空军进攻的新的军事挑衅和罪行。会上，新闻司负责人向记者们分发了“中华人民共和国国防部发言人关于蒋介石空军在美国直接指使下使用导弹武器向我大陆空军进攻的声明”。
    在温州地区捡获的美国制造的“响尾蛇”导弹的红外线接受部分（头部）、导向部分、未爆炸的弹头部分和固体火箭部分等导弹的重要部分的实体，都在今晚的中外记者招待会上展出。从展出的导弹实体上，记者们可以看到导弹长约二公尺多，直径五英寸，弹头部分装有烈性炸药。在导弹的动力装置及固体火箭部分的铝合金外壳上面，用英文写有“五英寸火箭”和“军械局”的字样。
    在美帝国主义侵略者指使下的蒋介石空军的这种罪行，引起了记者们的极大愤慨。
