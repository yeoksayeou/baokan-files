Title: American Aggressors Direct Chiang Kai-shek Planes to Use "Sidewinder" Missiles, Spokesperson of Our Ministry of National Defense Solemnly Declares: Our Army Will Inflict Punitive Strikes

Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Statement by the spokesperson of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China regarding the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force's use of missile weapons to attack our mainland air force under the direct instigation of the United States.

On September 24th, the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force dispatched a total of 143 aircraft sorties to intrude into the airspace over the provinces of Fujian, Zhejiang, and Guangdong to carry out military provocations. In the aerial combat, Chiang Kai-shek's aircraft even used American-made "Sidewinder" missiles, shooting down one of our aircraft. The Chiang Kai-shek Air Force launched a total of five "Sidewinder" missiles this time, between 9:30 AM and 9:40 AM on September 24th. The airspace for launching the missiles was over the Wenzhou, Rui'an, and Yueqing areas of Zhejiang. We have now recovered the infrared receiving part (warhead), guidance part, unexploded warhead part, and solid rocket part of the missile in the Wenzhou area, and have already transported the important parts to Beijing, preparing for public exhibition.

It must be pointed out that this new military provocation by the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force occurred after the United States equipped the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force with modern weapons such as F-86F and F-100 aircraft and "Sidewinder" missiles, and it occurred while the US-Chiang military leaders were holding talks in Taipei. Therefore, this is an extremely serious event.

The spokesperson of the Ministry of National Defense is ordered to solemnly declare: The Chinese People's Liberation Army will take punitive strikes against such crimes by the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force.

### 美国侵略者指使蒋机使用“响尾蛇”导弹  我国防部发言人郑重宣布  我军必将给予惩罚性打击

1958-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美国侵略者指使蒋机使用“响尾蛇”导弹
    我国防部发言人郑重宣布
    我军必将给予惩罚性打击
    新华社29日讯  中华人民共和国国防部发言人关于蒋介石空军在美国直接指使下使用导弹武器向我大陆空军进攻的声明。
    9月24日，蒋介石空军先后出动飞机一百四十三架次，窜入我福建、浙江、广东三省上空进行军事挑衅。在空战中，蒋介石飞机竟然使用了美国制造的“响尾蛇”导弹，击落我机一架。蒋介石空军这次共发射“响尾蛇”导弹五枚，时间为9月24日上午9时30分至9时40分。发射导弹的空域是浙江的温州、瑞安、乐清地区的上空。现在，我们已经在温州地区分别捡获导弹的红外线接受部分（头部）、导向部分、未爆炸的弹头部分和固体火箭部分，并且已经将其重要部分运来北京，准备公开展出。
    必须指出，蒋介石空军这一新的军事挑衅，是发生在美国用F—86F型、F—100型的飞机和“响尾蛇”导弹等现代武器装备了蒋介石空军以后，是发生在美蒋军事首脑正在台北举行会谈的时候，因此，这是一个极其严重的事件。
    国防部发言人奉命郑重宣布：中国人民解放军对于蒋介石空军的这种罪行，必将采取惩罚性的打击。
