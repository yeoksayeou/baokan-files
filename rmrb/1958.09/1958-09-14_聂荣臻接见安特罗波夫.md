Title: Nie Rongzhen Receives Antropov
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen received Antropov, the head of the Soviet delegation to the First Mineral Conference in China and the Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection of the Soviet Union, as well as all members of the delegation, on the morning of September 13th. He Changgong and Xu Jie, Vice Ministers of Geology, were present at the reception.

### 聂荣臻接见安特罗波夫

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻接见安特罗波夫
    新华社13日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻13日上午接见了应邀参加我国第一届矿产会议的苏联地质和矿藏保护部代表团团长、苏联地质和矿藏保护部部长安特罗波夫，以及代表团全体团员。接见时在座的有地质部副部长何长工、许杰。
