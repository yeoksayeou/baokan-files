Title: Zhejiang's Early Rice Output Triples Last Year's

Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou Electric Dispatch According to statistics from the Zhejiang Provincial Statistics Bureau, the total output of early rice in Zhejiang Province this year reached more than 11.5 billion jin, an increase of more than two times compared to the 3.8 billion jin last year; the average yield per mu was 861 jin, more than double that of last year.

This year, the early rice planting area in Zhejiang Province was 13.36 million mu, an increase of 2.91 million mu compared to last year, of which the double-cropping early rice planting area was 11.06 million mu, an increase of 1.67 million mu compared to last year. There are fourteen counties in the province with an average yield of more than 1,000 jin per mu of early rice, and twenty-five counties with an average yield of 800 jin to 1,000 jin per mu. There are 719 townships in the province with more than 900 jin, 1,856 co-ops with 1,200 jin, 169,000 mu of high-yield fields with more than 1,500 jin, and 562 mu of high-yield fields with more than 3,000 jin.

The large-scale bumper harvest of early rice in Zhejiang is the result of the hard work and solid efforts of the people of the province under the leadership of the Party, and their heroic struggle against nature. At present, one million cadres throughout the province have set up camp in the fields, carrying out rectification at the field head while participating in production, and are working with the masses to strive for a greater bumper harvest of late rice.

### 浙江早稻比去年增加两倍

1958-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    浙江早稻比去年增加两倍
    新华社杭州电　据浙江省统计局统计，浙江省今年早稻总产量达到一百一十五亿多斤，比去年的三十八亿斤增加两倍多；平均每亩产量八百六十一斤，比去年提高一倍多。
    今年浙江省早稻种植面积为一千三百三十六万亩，比去年增加二百九十一万亩，其中双季早稻种植面积一千一百零六万亩，比去年增加一百六十七万亩。全省有十四个县早稻平均每亩产量达到千斤以上，有二十五个县平均每亩产量达到八百斤至一千斤。全省九百斤以上的乡有七百一十九个，一千二百斤的社有一千八百五十六个，一千五百斤以上的高产田有十六万九千亩，三千斤以上的高产田有五百六十二亩。
    浙江早稻大面积丰收，是全省人民在党的领导下苦干实干，向自然界英勇斗争的结果。目前，全省一百万干部已经扎营田间，一面在田头开展整风，一面参加生产，正在同群众一起争取晚稻更大的丰收。
