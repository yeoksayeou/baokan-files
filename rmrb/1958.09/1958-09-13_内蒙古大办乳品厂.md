Title: Inner Mongolia Vigorously Establishes Dairy Factories
Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, September 12th: This year, rural and pastoral areas of Inner Mongolia have newly built 704 medium and small dairy factories. Together with the original factories, the entire autonomous region has an average of ten dairy factories per banner and county. It is estimated that the total output of milk powder, butter, lactose, casein, etc. produced in Inner Mongolia this year will reach 8,000 tons, an increase of two and a half times compared with last year.

### 内蒙古大办乳品厂

1958-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    内蒙古大办乳品厂
    据新华社呼和浩特12日电  内蒙古各地农村、牧区今年已新建了中、小型乳品工厂七百零四座，连同原有的一批，全自治区平均每一个旗、县拥有乳品厂十座。预计今年内蒙古生产的奶粉、奶油、乳糖、酪胶等的总产量将达八千吨，比去年增加两倍半。
