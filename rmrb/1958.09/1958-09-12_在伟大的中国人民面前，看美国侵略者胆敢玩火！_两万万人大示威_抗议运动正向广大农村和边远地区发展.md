Title: Before the Great Chinese People, See How the American Aggressors Dare to Play with Fire! Two Hundred Million People Demonstrate, Protest Movement Spreads to Vast Rural and Remote Areas
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The rampant armed provocations of American imperialism in the Taiwan Strait area have ignited a powerful anti-aggression movement across the country, like a raging storm. From the 7th to the 11th, in just five days, 212 million people across the country participated in unprecedented large-scale demonstrations.

This is the climax of a great patriotic movement of national scale. The universal and rapid action of the people demonstrates that a situation of national mobilization to counter American aggression has taken shape throughout the country.

In recent days, the tense situation in the Taiwan Strait has continued to develop. The American warmongers, ignoring the repeated serious warnings issued by our government, continue to carry out arrogant military provocations. Following the intrusion of American warships into our territorial waters twice, and the joint large-scale provocative military exercises with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, American military aircraft blatantly intruded into the airspace of Fujian, Jiangxi, and Guangdong provinces on the morning of the 10th for high-altitude reconnaissance. The peaceful residents of Xiamen and Xiamen University were bombarded by American-made artillery fire from the enemy's positions. This afternoon, four American warships, disregarding the repeated warnings of the Chinese people, continued to intrude into China's territorial waters, carrying out extremely dangerous provocative activities.

The continuous and frantic provocations of the American aggressors have aroused the boundless anger of the people across the country. In particular, Chairman Mao Zedong's analysis of the domestic and international situation at the Supreme State Conference has greatly encouraged the people's determination to oppose American aggression and accelerate the construction of the motherland. In recent days, militia teams across the country are rapidly forming, and the development of people's communes has also greatly accelerated. On the iron and steel and agricultural production fronts, people are fighting day and night with soaring enthusiasm. The loud slogan of the people across the country is: "We have the people, we have the steel, we have the grain!" Determined to be ready to fight back against any enemy who dares to invade.

The magnificent socialist scenery displayed since the Great Leap Forward has taught the Chinese people how to defend their happiness. The people's power to defend their happiness is invincible. But countless historical facts prove that the imperialist elements are always so foolish and ignorant in estimating the great power of the people. They simply cannot imagine why the Chinese people can rise up at the slightest hint of trouble overnight, why in a few dozen hours, everyone can be a soldier in cities and villages. They also simply cannot imagine that, for the struggle against aggression, tens of millions of people in a province in China can hold demonstrations and parades; in a county in Liaoning Province, on the same night, torches of demonstration can be lit simultaneously within a radius of more than two hundred miles. These facts powerfully demonstrate that, in order to defend the motherland and defend peace, the Chinese people do not hesitate to shed their blood.

Now, this protest movement is developing in depth into the vast rural and remote areas. The people of the whole country are closely watching the movements of American imperialism, guarding against new war provocations by the aggressors. If the aggressors do not repent and stop, then the Chinese people are not to be trifled with. Before the great Chinese people, see what the American aggressors dare to do!

### 在伟大的中国人民面前，看美国侵略者胆敢玩火！ 两万万人大示威  抗议运动正向广大农村和边远地区发展

1958-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    在伟大的中国人民面前，看美国侵略者胆敢玩火！
    两万万人大示威
    抗议运动正向广大农村和边远地区发展
    新华社11日讯　美帝国主义在台湾海峡地区猖狂的武装挑衅，已经在全国范围内激起了一个急如风暴的强大的反侵略运动。从7日到11日，仅仅五天时间，全国各地就有两亿一千二百万人参加了规模空前的大示威。
    这是一个全民规模的伟大的爱国运动的高潮，人民普遍的迅速的行动，表明了一个反击美国侵略的全民动员的形势，已经在全国范围内形成。
    这几天，台湾海峡的紧张局势仍在发展。美国战争贩子们竟然无视我国政府一再发出的严重警告，继续不断地进行嚣张的军事挑衅活动。继美国军舰两次侵入我国领海，并且同蒋介石卖国集团联合进行了大规模的挑衅性的军事演习以后，美国军用飞机在10日上午竟又公然侵入我国福建、江西、广东省地区的上空，进行高空侦察。厦门的和平居民和厦门大学受到了来自敌军阵地上的美制炮火的轰击。今天的下午，美国四艘军舰竟又不顾中国人民的一再警告，继续侵入了中国的领海，进行极端危险的挑衅活动。
    美国侵略者不断的疯狂挑衅，激起了全国人民的无比愤怒。特别是毛泽东主席在最高国务会议上对国内外形势的分析发表以后，更大大地鼓舞了全国人民反对美国侵略的斗志和加速建设祖国的决心。这几天，全国各地的民兵队伍正在迅速地组成，人民公社的发展也大大加快了速度。在钢铁和农业生产战线上，人们正以冲天的干劲而日夜紧张的战斗。全国人民当前响亮的口号是：“要人有人，要钢有钢，要粮有粮！”决心以战斗的姿态，随时反击敢于进犯的敌人。
    从大跃进以来展现出来的社会主义壮丽的美景，教育了中国人民懂得应该怎样去保卫自己的幸福。人民保卫自己幸福的力量是无敌的。但是无数历史事实证明：帝国主义分子在估计人民群众的伟大力量时，总是那样愚蠢和无知，他们根本不会想到，为什么中国人民在一个晚上就能闻风而起，为什么在几十个小时以内就能够城城乡乡全民皆兵。他们也根本不会想到，为了反侵略的斗争，在中国的一个省就有成千万人举行示威游行；在辽宁省的一个县，在同一天的晚上就在方圆二百多里以内一齐燃起了示威的火把。这些事实都有力地说明，为了保卫祖国、保卫和平，中国人民是不吝惜自己的鲜血的。
    现在，这一抗议运动正在向广大农村和边远地区深入发展，全国人民正密切注视着美帝国主义的动向，戒备着侵略者的新的战争挑衅，如果侵略者再不悔悟住手，那么中国人民不是好惹的。在伟大的中国人民面前，看美国侵略者敢做些什么！
