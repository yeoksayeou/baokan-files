Title: Ceylonese Minister of Agriculture and Food and Acting Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association Arrive in Beijing

Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency:  Mr. Gunawardena, the Ceylonese Minister of Agriculture and Food, and his party of four, who were invited to visit China by the Ministry of Agriculture, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th.  Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.
    Mr. Amarasingam, Acting Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and his party of four, who were invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th.

### 锡兰农粮部长和锡中友协代理主席等到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰农粮部长和锡中友协代理主席等到京
    据新华社讯  应农业部邀请来我国访问的锡兰农业和粮食部长古纳瓦德纳及随行人员四人，29日下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有农业部副部长杨显东，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长卢绪章等人。
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的锡兰锡中友好协会代主席阿马拉辛甘姆等一行四人，26日下午乘飞机到达北京。
