Title: Congratulating China's National Day: Matsumoto to Lead Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 8th, Tokyo dispatch: The Japan-China Friendship Association held a meeting of its standing directors on the 6th, deciding to accept the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and dispatch a delegation led by Chairman Jiichiro Matsumoto to China to celebrate China's National Day.
　　The directors participating in the meeting unanimously believed that at a time when Japan-China relations are still tense and the situation in the Taiwan Strait is increasingly tense, the Japan-China Friendship Association's dispatch of a delegation to China is of great significance. It will encourage the Japanese people to further launch a movement to break the tense situation in Japan-China relations and restore normal diplomatic relations.
　　The delegation will consist of nine people, including Jiichiro Matsumoto, Yoshitaro Hirano, Kanichi Kawakami, Noriharu Yoshida, and Okinoshin Tanaka.

### 祝贺我国国庆  松本将率代表团来我国

1958-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    祝贺我国国庆
　　松本将率代表团来我国
    新华社8日讯　东京消息：日中友好协会在6日举行常任理事会会议，决定接受中国人民对外文化协会的邀请，派遣由会长松本治一郎率领的祝贺中国国庆节代表团访问中国。
    参加会议的理事们一致认为，在目前日中关系仍然存在紧张局面和台湾海峡局势日益紧张的时候，日中友好协会派遣代表团访问中国具有重大意义，它将鼓舞日本人民进一步展开打开日中关系紧张局面和恢复正常邦交的运动。
    代表团将由松本治一郎、平野义太郎、川上贯一、吉田法晴和田中织之进等九人组成。
