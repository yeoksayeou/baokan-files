Title: Nine-tenths of Taiyuan City's Population Engulfed in the Torrent of Opposition to American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 7th. One hundred thousand people in Taiyuan City gathered at Xinghualing Stadium on the 7th to hold a demonstration, resolutely opposing the military provocations of American imperialism. Workers, farmers, students, government cadres, and People's Liberation Army soldiers submitted letters of determination to the meeting, expressing their commitment to intensify production and work hard, ready to respond to the call of the motherland at any time, and severely strike the American imperialists who dare to invade our territory. The activities in Taiyuan City against the military provocations of American imperialism have rapidly spread to every corner of the city. As of 10:00 PM on the 7th, 1,110,000 people in this city with a population of 1,220,000 had participated in various activities. The Shanxi Daily, which published Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait and related news reports, increased its circulation from the usual 150,000 copies to 260,000 copies. Nearly 2,000 employees of the Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory held a meeting during their lunch break, resolutely supporting Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait. The workers at the meeting proposed that they would provide manpower and steel to fully support the struggle to liberate Jinmen and Matsu, and to liberate Taiwan.
    Today, more than ninety cities and counties and the vast rural areas throughout Shanxi Province held rallies and parades from morning to night. Workers at the Yangquan Coal Mine stated that if American imperialism dares to provoke the 600 million Chinese people, the coal miners will respond to the call of the motherland at any time, put down their coal shovels, pick up their weapons, and fiercely strike this paper tiger. National famous agricultural labor models Li Shunda, Guo Yu'en, Shen Jilan, and Wu Houli all participated in a gathering of more than 3,000 people in Pingshun County today. They said that we farmers will provide manpower, grain, and iron and steel.

### 太原市十分之九的人口  卷入反对美国侵略的巨流

1958-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    太原市十分之九的人口
　　卷入反对美国侵略的巨流
    新华社太原电　太原市7日有十万人聚集在杏花岭体育场举行示威大会，坚决反对美帝国主义的军事挑衅。工人、农民、学生、机关干部和解放军战士纷纷向大会递决心书，表示加紧生产和努力工作，准备随时响应祖国号召，狠狠打击敢于侵犯我国领土的美帝国主义者。太原市的反对美帝国主义军事挑衅的活动，迅速发展到全市每个角落，截至7日晚十时止，这个拥有一百二十二万人口的城市已经有一百十一万人参加了各种活动。刊登周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势声明和有关新闻报道的山西日报，由平时的十五万份增加到二十六万份。太原矿山机器厂近两千名职工在午饭后休息时间举行集会，坚决拥护周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，工人们在会上提出要人有人，要钢有钢，全力支援解放金门、马祖，支援解放台湾的斗争。
    今天，山西省全省九十多个市县和广大农村，从早到晚纷纷举行集会游行，阳泉煤矿工人提出，美帝国主义胆敢向六亿中国人民挑衅，煤矿工人将随时响应祖国的召唤，放下铲煤工具，拿起武器，狠狠的打击这只纸老虎。全国著名农业劳动模范李顺达、郭玉恩、申纪兰和武侯犁，今天都参加了平顺县城三千多人的集会，他们说，我们农民要人有人，要粮有粮，要钢铁有钢铁。
