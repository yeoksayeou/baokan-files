Title: Welcoming Distinguished Guests on the Eve of National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 4

The Head of the Vietnamese Water Conservancy Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Chen Dengke, head of the Water Conservancy Planning Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th at the invitation of our country. Feng Zhongyun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, and others greeted him at the airport.
Some members of the delegation arrived in our country as early as the beginning of July. After inspecting Henan, Anhui, Jiangsu, and Guangdong, they returned to Beijing on September 24. Another group of delegation members arrived in Beijing from Hanoi on September 21. The delegation will hold talks with relevant departments in our country to introduce the management of the Red River and planning opinions in both countries, and exchange opinions on future scientific and technological cooperation in water conservancy between China and Vietnam.
Bulgarian Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
The Bulgarian agricultural delegation of seven people, led by Buchvarov, Minister of the Rural Work Department of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th at the invitation of Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture. Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, Zhou Junming, Vice Minister of Forestry, Yang Yu, Director of the Office of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and others greeted them at the airport. Telenoglasski, Chargé d'Affaires of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, also greeted them at the airport.
Indonesian Veterans Delegation Arrives in Beijing
The Indonesian Veterans Delegation, invited by the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association for a friendly visit to China, arrived in Beijing from Moscow by plane on the morning of the 26th. The delegation is composed of seven people and is led by Lieutenant Colonel Suhario. This delegation previously visited the Soviet Union.
Wang Renshu, Director of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association, and others greeted them at the airport.
Prince Kainu and Baba Arrive in Beijing
Prince Sise Zakaria Ibn Kainu of Mauritania and Sharif Daria Baba, representative of Senegal to the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 国庆前夕迎佳宾

1958-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    国庆前夕迎佳宾
    越南水利考察团团长抵京
    应邀来我国访问的越南民主共和国水利规划考察团团长、越南民主共和国水利部部长陈登科，25日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有水利电力部副部长冯仲云等多人。
    考察团部分团员早在7月初就已到达我国，他们在河南、安徽、江苏和广东等地考察后，在9月24日回到北京。另外一批团员在9月21日由河内到达北京。考察团将同我国有关部门举行会谈，相互介绍两国境内红河的治理情况和规划意见，并交换今后中越两国在水利方面的科学技术合作意见。
    保加利亚农业代表团抵京
    应农业部部长廖鲁言邀请来我国访问的保加利亚农业代表团一行七人，由保共中央农村工作部部长布契瓦洛夫率领，26日下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有农业部副部长刘瑞龙、林业部副部长周骏鸣、中共中央农村工作部办公室主任杨煜等人。保加利亚驻华大使馆代办捷列诺格拉斯基也到机场欢迎。
    印度尼西亚退伍军人代表团抵京
    应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的印度尼西亚退伍军人访华代表团一行七人，由团长苏哈里约中校率领，在26日上午乘飞机由莫斯科到达北京。这个代表团在以前曾访问了苏联。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事王任叔等到机场欢迎他们。
    卡伊努亲王和巴巴抵京
    应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的毛里塔尼亚西塞·扎卡里亚·伊本·卡伊努亲王及塞内加尔驻亚非人民团结理事会代表谢里夫·达里亚·巴巴于25日乘机抵京。
                  （据新华社）
