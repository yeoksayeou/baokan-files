Title: Pyongyang Rally Celebrates the 10th Anniversary of National Foundation; Kim Il-sung's Speech Demands the Immediate Withdrawal of U.S. Troops from South Korea
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 8th: A grand rally of 4,000 representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang was held tonight at the Pyongyang People's Army Gymnasium to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

When the Korean Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Choi Yong-kun, and other state leaders, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea, ascended the rostrum, warm applause resounded throughout the spacious hall. Also attending the meeting were Korean Deputy Premiers Kim Il, Hong Myong-hui, Chung Il-ryong, Nam Il, and Chung Joon-taek, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, and Kim Chang-man, ministers of the cabinet, heads of various departments of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and social organizations, and senior generals of the People's Army.

Invited to attend the meeting was the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation headed by General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar.

Premier Kim Il-sung delivered a lengthy speech amidst warm applause. In his speech, he recounted the glorious achievements made in the ten years since the founding of the Republic, the policy of peaceful reunification of the fatherland, and the efforts made to achieve peaceful reunification. He also pointed out the prospects for the Republic's prosperity and development, the basic tasks of current socialist construction, and the grand leap forward goals for the next few years.

Premier Kim Il-sung expressed his firm support for Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Straits and condemned the United States' acts of aggression in the Taiwan Straits. He also demanded that U.S. troops immediately get out of South Korea.

Subsequently, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, China, and other fraternal countries to Korea delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

After the meeting, more than 2,000 artists and amateur artists from factories and villages gave wonderful performances.

### 平壤集会庆祝建国十周年  金日成演说要求美军立即滚出南朝鲜

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　平壤集会庆祝建国十周年
　　金日成演说要求美军立即滚出南朝鲜
    新华社平壤8日电　平壤市各界代表四千人今天晚上在朝鲜人民军体育馆举行盛大集会，庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十周年。
    当朝鲜内阁首相金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健等国家领导人和各国驻朝鲜的外交使节登上主席台时，宽敞的大厅里响起了热烈的掌声。参加大会的还有朝鲜内阁副首相金一、洪命熹、郑一龙、南日、郑准泽，劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满，内阁各相，劳动党中央各部部长和各民主政党、社会团体的负责人，人民军高级将领等。
    应邀参加大会的还有以中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将和政治委员王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团。
    金日成首相在热烈的掌声中发表了长篇演说。他在演说中叙述了共和国成立十年来取得的光辉成就、和平统一祖国的政策以及为实现和平统一所作的努力。他还指出了共和国繁荣发展的远景、当前社会主义建设的基本任务和最近几年的宏伟跃进目标。
    金日成首相表示坚决支持中国总理周恩来关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，谴责美国在台湾海峡的侵略行为。他还要求美军立即滚出南朝鲜。
    接着，苏联、中国和各兄弟国家驻朝鲜的外交使节相继在大会上致祝词。
    会后，两千多名艺术家和来自工厂农村的业余艺术家们作了精彩的演出。
