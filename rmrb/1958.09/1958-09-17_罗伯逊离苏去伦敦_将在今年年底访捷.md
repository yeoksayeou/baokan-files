Title: Robeson Leaves Soviet Union for London; To Visit Czechoslovakia at the End of This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 15th: The famous American black singer Paul Robeson said in Prague today that he might make a short visit to Czechoslovakia in December. He will also participate in the 1959 "Prague Spring" Music Festival.

Robeson and his wife arrived in Prague at noon today from the Soviet Union en route to London.

Robeson stated that he plans to live in London for the next few years.

### 罗伯逊离苏去伦敦  将在今年年底访捷

1958-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    罗伯逊离苏去伦敦
    将在今年年底访捷
    据新华社布拉格15日电　美国著名黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊今天在布拉格说，他在12月份可能在捷克斯洛伐克进行一次简短的访问。他还将参加1959年“布拉格之春”音乐节。
    罗伯逊和他的夫人是在今天中午由苏联去伦敦途中到达布拉格的。
    罗伯逊表示，在今后几年内他准备住在伦敦。
