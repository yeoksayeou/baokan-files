Title: First Batch of US Missiles Arrives in Britain
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 10th: The British Air Ministry announced on the 9th that the first batch of "Thor" missiles had been shipped from the United States to Britain.

According to the American "New York Times", the first batch of sixty "Thor" intermediate-range missiles shipped to Britain were shipped from the United States last week.

### 美国首批导弹运抵英伦

1958-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国首批导弹运抵英伦
    据新华社10日讯　伦敦消息：英国空军部9日宣布，第一批“雷神”式导弹已经从美国运到英国。
    据美国“纽约时报”透露，第一批运往英国的六十枚“雷神”式中程导弹是在上周从美国起运的。
