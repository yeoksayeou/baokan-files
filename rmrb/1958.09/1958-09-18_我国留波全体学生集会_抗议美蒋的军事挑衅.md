Title: Chinese Students in Poland Hold Gathering to Protest US-Chiang Kai-shek Military Provocations
Author:
Date: 1958-09-18
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 15th. All the Chinese students in Poland held a gathering at the Warsaw University of Technology gymnasium tonight with immense anger, protesting the provocative actions of US imperialism in the Taiwan Strait area.

    At the meeting, the students expressed their firm support for Premier Zhou Enlai's statement, resolutely forbidding American pirates from infringing upon China's sacred territory.

    At the meeting, the students unanimously passed a letter of salute to the heroic soldiers on the Fujian front. All the students unanimously assured the Party that they are ready at any time to respond to the call of the motherland and go to the forefront of the struggle. They also decided to donate a cannon to the motherland to support the struggle against the enemy.

### 我国留波全体学生集会  抗议美蒋的军事挑衅

1958-09-18
第2版()
专栏：

    我国留波全体学生集会
    抗议美蒋的军事挑衅
    新华社华沙15日电　我国在波兰的全体留学生今晚以无比愤怒的心情在华沙工业大学体育馆举行集会，抗议美帝国主义在台湾海峡地区的挑衅行为。
    会上，同学们纷纷表示坚决拥护周恩来总理的声明，绝不允许美国海盗侵犯我国神圣的领土。
    会上，同学们一致通过给福建前线英勇的战士们写的致敬信。全体同学一致向党保证随时准备好，响应祖国的号召到斗争的最前线去。他们并且决定向祖国捐献一门大炮来支援对敌斗争。
