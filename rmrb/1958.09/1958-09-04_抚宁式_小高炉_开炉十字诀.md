　　"Funing Style" Small Blast Furnace
　　Ten-Character Opening Formula
　　Baking the furnace needs five days, the furnace temperature rises slowly.
　　Raw materials should be prepared sufficiently, and the specifications should meet the requirements.
　　Equipment should be inspected, and test runs performed for two days.
　　Pipes should be pressure tested to prevent water and air leaks.
　　Spare parts must be available to ensure trouble-free production.
　　Ingredients must be well proportioned; avoid haphazard mixing.
　　Coke should be burned red before sending in hot air.
　　First heat the hot blast stove, then send the gas in sections.
　　Blast iron slag notch to raise the temperature of the hearth.
　　Use appropriate amount of cooling water to avoid the furnace cooling down.
　　Add more empty coke to ensure high furnace temperature.
　　Ore should be carefully selected for good permeability and reduction.
　　Don't change materials abruptly; gradually increase the load.
　　Use more coke powder to block the mud, making it easy to open the taphole.

    管道试压好　防止漏水风

    配料要配好　切忌胡乱搞
    焦炭要烧红　再把热风送
    先烧热风炉　煤气分段送
    喷吹铁渣口　炉缸温上升
    冷却水适量　避免炉子凉
    空焦宜多加　保证炉高温
    矿石要净选　透气还原好
    变料不要猛　负荷逐渐增
    堵泥多焦粉　便于开铁口
