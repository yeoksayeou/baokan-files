Title: Gale and Frost Alert
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 6

    Gale and Frost Alert
    (Reported by this newspaper) At 8:00 PM on September 26, 1958, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale and frost alert. It is expected that in the next thirty-six hours, eastern Gansu, Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, western and central Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Shaanxi, Shanxi, and northwestern Hebei will experience northerly winds of level six or seven. Northern Sichuan, the Hebei Plain, Henan, Shandong, and the Bohai Sea will experience northerly winds of level five or six. The area between the Huai River and the Yangtze River will experience northerly winds of around level five. Southern Sichuan will experience northerly winds of level four or five. In the next forty-eight hours, Heilongjiang, Jilin, and Liaoning will experience gales of level six to eight, with the wind direction shifting from southerly to northerly. After the cold air front passes, the temperature in areas north of the Great Wall will drop by about 15 degrees Celsius; the temperature in areas north of the Yellow River will drop by 10 to 12 degrees Celsius; and the temperature in the area between the Yellow River and the Yangtze River will drop by 8 to 10 degrees Celsius. On the morning of the 27th, the minimum temperature in western Gansu will drop below zero degrees Celsius. On the mornings of the 28th and 29th, after the wind stops and the sky clears, eastern Gansu, Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, northern and central Shaanxi, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, northern and central Shanxi, northwestern Hebei, and all provinces of the Northeast, except for coastal areas, will experience frost.

### 大风和霜冻警报

1958-09-27
第6版()
专栏：气象台

    大风和霜冻警报
    本报讯  1958年9月26日下午八时中央气象科学研究所发布大风和霜冻警报。预计未来三十六小时内，甘肃东部、宁夏回族自治区、内蒙古自治区西部和中部、陕西、山西、河北西北部将有六、七级的偏北风。四川北部、河北平原、河南、山东和渤海将有五、六级的偏北风。淮河到长江之间的地区将有五级左右的偏北风。四川南部将有四、五级的偏北风。未来四十八小时内，黑龙江、吉林和辽宁将有六—八级的大风，风向偏南转偏北。在冷空气前锋过境以后，长城以北地区气温将下降摄氏十五度左右；黄河以北地区气温将下降摄氏十到十二度；黄河到长江之间的地区气温将下降摄氏八到十度。27日早晨，甘肃西部最低气温将下降到零度以下。28日和29日早晨，风停天晴以后，甘肃东部、宁夏回族自治区、陕西北部和中部、内蒙古自治区、山西北部和中部、河北西北部和东北各省除沿海以外的地区将有霜冻出现。
