Title: Great Leap Forward of the Sea Cavalry
Author: Yu Guangbi
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 8

    Great Leap Forward of the Sea Cavalry,
    Torpedo boats roar with their engines.
    In the daytime the sun shines brightly,
    At night, stars and moon overhead.
    Fearing neither hardship nor fatigue,
    We bravely breast the giant waves.
    Should enemy ships dare to invade,
    A torpedo will send them to see the Dragon King.
          From a certain unit, Yu Guangbi

### 海上轻骑大跃进
于光璧
1958-09-12
第8版()
专栏：

    海上轻骑大跃进
    海上轻骑大跃进，
    鱼雷快艇马达响。
    白天太阳当空照，
    夜晚头顶星月光。
    不怕苦，不怕累，
    挺起胸膛破巨浪。
    敌舰胆敢来侵犯，
    一颗鱼雷让它开花见龙王。
          某部　于光璧
