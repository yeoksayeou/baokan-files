Brief News

　　A Bulgarian National Assembly delegation headed by Dimov, an alternate member of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and a member of the Presidium of the National Assembly, arrived in Tirana by plane on August 31 at the invitation of the Albanian People's Assembly. A Cambodian economic delegation headed by Bu Lie-fang, advisor to the Cambodian Ministry of Planning, arrived in Moscow on August 30.
    The delegation will conduct economic negotiations with relevant Soviet parties. Nearly 15,000 new university students newly enrolled in the German Democratic Republic this year will begin their university life on September 1.
    It is announced that about 5,000 of these university students will study engineering and natural sciences. The Lee Seung-man clique's Public Information Office closed down another twenty-two South Korean periodicals on August 29, including "International Opinion" and "Economic Circles."
    Since the beginning of this year, the Lee Seung-man clique has closed down sixty-eight periodicals. A U.S. Navy jet fighter based in Atsugi crashed in flames into a residential area of Yokohama on the morning of August 31, shortly after taking off, destroying six houses.
    According to Japanese police authorities, two people died, two are missing, and eleven were injured, all of whom are Japanese. The American pilot on the plane also died.

### 简讯

1958-09-01
第5版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
　　以保共中央政治局候补委员、国民议会主席团委员迪莫夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团，应阿尔巴尼亚人民议会的邀请，8月31日乘飞机抵达地拉那。以柬埔寨计划部顾问蒲烈芳为首的柬埔寨经济代表团8月30日抵达莫斯科。
    代表团将同苏联有关方面进行经济谈判。民主德国今年新招考的将近一万五千名大学生将在9月1日开始他们的大学生活。
    据宣布，在这些大学生中有大约五千名是要学工程和自然科学的。李承晚集团的公报室8月29日又封闭了二十二种南朝鲜期刊，其中包括“国际舆论”和“经济界”等。
    今年以来，李承晚集团已经封闭了六十八种期刊。驻日美国海军的一架喷气式飞机，8月31日上午从厚木基地起飞后不久，就烈焰腾腾地坠毁在横滨的一个居民区里，毁坏了六幢房子。
    据日本警察当局说，有两人死亡，两人失踪，十一人受伤，他们都是日本人。飞机上的美国驾驶员也死掉了。
