Title: American Diplomat Acts Suspiciously; Cairo Police Arrest Him
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 10th. According to a report in today's "People's News," an official from the American Embassy in Cairo was arrested early on the morning of the 9th by police guarding the residence of the former Syrian ambassador to Cairo.

The official was discovered in the former ambassador's garden. He refused to give his name or a legitimate reason for being there. He also assaulted the police and verbally abused Egyptians.

The American Embassy was notified. The First Secretary of the American Embassy identified the official as Harold Holtz and revealed that he was a member of the American diplomatic mission. He requested that Holtz be handed over to him, stating that the American Ambassador wanted to know if Holtz had been attacked.

The case has been submitted to the Public Prosecutor's Office. An investigation is underway.

### 美外交官贼头贼脑  开罗警察予以逮捕

1958-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美外交官贼头贼脑
    开罗警察予以逮捕
    新华社开罗10日电　据“人民报”今天报道，美国驻开罗大使馆的一个官员在9日清早被守卫前叙利亚驻开罗大使住宅的警察逮捕。
    这个官员是在这位前大使的花园内被发现的，他拒绝说出他的名字，也说不出他呆在那里的正当理由。同时，他还动手殴打警察和辱骂埃及人。
    美国大使馆得到了通知，美国大使馆的一等秘书说出这个官员的名字是哈罗德·霍耳茨，并且透露他是美国外交使团的人员，他要求把霍耳茨交给他，并且说美国大使希望知道霍耳茨是否遭到了攻击。
    这个案件已经提交给检察署。调查工作正在进行。
