Title: Kuwait is Considering Joining the Arab League
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 25th: Kuwait's Deputy Emir Abdullah Mubarak Al-Sabah, who arrived here on the 24th, told reporters today that Kuwait is seriously considering joining the Arab League in order to contribute to the Arabs and their major cause.

Sabah said that Kuwait's leaders are very concerned about the Arab League and believe that it is a regional organization that could become a strong fortress for the Arabs.

### 科威特正在考虑参加阿拉伯联盟

1958-09-27
第5版()
专栏：

    科威特正在考虑参加阿拉伯联盟
    据新华社开罗25日电  在24日到达这里的科威特副酋长阿卜杜拉·穆巴拉克·萨巴赫今天对新闻记者说，科威特正在认真地考虑参加阿拉伯联盟，以便能够为阿拉伯人和他们的主要事业贡献一份力量。
    萨巴赫说，科威特领袖们很关心阿拉伯联盟，认为它是一个可能成为阿拉伯人的一个强大堡垒的地区性组织。
