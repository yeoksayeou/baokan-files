Title: Iraq Announces Removal of Arif from Position as Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces Due to Public Interest Needs
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, September 12th. Radio Iraq announced today an order from the Iraqi Republic, stating that Iraqi Republic Vice Premier Arif has been relieved of his concurrent post as Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces.
    The order, signed on September 11th by the Chairman and members of the Iraqi Republic Sovereignty Council, the Prime Minister, and the Minister of Defense, stated that the decision was made "in consideration of the needs of the public interest" and based on the recommendation of Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Qassem. The order will be implemented by the Minister of Defense.
    Arif also serves concurrently as the Minister of Interior in the Iraqi Republic government.

### 伊拉克宣布鉴于公共利益需要  解除阿里夫的武装部队副总司令兼职

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克宣布鉴于公共利益需要
    解除阿里夫的武装部队副总司令兼职
    新华社巴格达12日电　伊拉克共和国电台今天广播了伊拉克共和国的一项命令，宣布解除伊拉克共和国副总理阿里夫兼任的武装部队副总司令的职务。
    由伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席、委员，总理和国防部长在11日签署的这项命令说，这项决定是“鉴于公共利益的需要”，根据总理兼国防部长卡塞姆的建议作出的。命令将由国防部长执行。
    阿里夫还兼任伊拉克共和国政府的内政部长。
