Title: Four U.S. Warships Invade China's Territorial Waters; Chinese Government Issues Stern Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th - On September 7th, from 2:45 PM to 7:00 PM, four U.S. warships intruded into China's territorial waters within twelve nautical miles of the Jinmen and Xiamen areas to conduct activities. A spokesperson for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, acting on instructions, stated: This act of violating China's sovereignty is dangerous, and the Chinese government issues a stern warning regarding this.

### 四艘美舰侵入我国领海  我国政府提出严重警告

1958-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　四艘美舰侵入我国领海  我国政府提出严重警告
    新华社7日讯　九月七日下午二时四十五分到七时，有美国军舰四艘侵入我国金门、厦门地区的领海十二海里以内，进行活动，我外交部发言人奉命声明：这种侵犯我国主权的行为是危险的，中国政府对此提出严重警告。
