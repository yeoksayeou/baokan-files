Title: Kim Il-sung Receives Our Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 29th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of North Korea, and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government received all members of the Chinese People's Delegation headed by Guo Moruo this evening.

Those who received the delegation included Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Jung Il-yong, Nam Il, and Jung Joon-taek, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, and Kim Chang-man.

The delegation was accompanied by Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea.

Premier Kim Il-sung had warm and friendly talks with Head of Delegation Guo Moruo.

### 金日成接见我代表团

1958-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

    金日成接见我代表团
    新华社平壤29日电  以郭沫若团长为首的中国人民代表团全体团员今天晚上访问了金日成首相等朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的领导人。
    接见代表团的有朝鲜内阁首相金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，内阁副首相金一、洪命熹、郑一龙、南日、郑准泽，劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满等。
    代表团由中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光陪同前往访问。
    金日成首相同郭沫若团长进行了热烈友好的谈话。
