Title: DPRK Recognizes Algerian Provisional Government; Premier Kim Il-sung Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Abbas
Author:
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 25th. Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, today sent a telegram to Ferhat Abbas, Premier of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, announcing the recognition of the Algerian Provisional Government.

In the telegram, Kim Il-sung first warmly congratulated the establishment of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria. Kim Il-sung said that the establishment of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria is a brilliant victory achieved by the Algerian people in their long and arduous struggle against colonialism and for freedom and national independence, and marks a new stage in the Algerian people's liberation struggle.

The telegram stated that the Korean people, who are struggling for the peaceful reunification of their fatherland and against the aggression of the American imperialists, fully support the just struggle of the Algerian people.

Kim Il-sung expressed his belief that the friendship and cooperative relations between the Korean people and the Algerian people will be further strengthened and developed in the common struggle against foreign aggressors and for peace and national independence.

### 朝鲜承认阿临时政府  金日成首相电贺阿巴斯总理

1958-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜承认阿临时政府
    金日成首相电贺阿巴斯总理
    新华社平壤25日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成今天发电报给阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯，宣布承认阿尔及利亚临时政府。
    金日成在电报中首先热烈祝贺阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的成立。金日成说，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的成立，是阿尔及利亚人民反对殖民主义和争取自由和民族独立的长期艰苦斗争所取得的辉煌胜利，标志着阿尔及利亚人民解放斗争的一个新的阶段。
    电报说，为和平统一祖国和反对美帝国主义者的侵略而斗争的朝鲜人民完全支持阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争。
    金日成表示相信，朝鲜人民同阿尔及利亚人民的友谊和合作关系，在反对外国侵略者和争取和平和民族独立的共同斗争中将进一步加强和发展。
