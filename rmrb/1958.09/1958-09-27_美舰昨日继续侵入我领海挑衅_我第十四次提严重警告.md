Title: U.S. Warships Continue Provocative Intrusion into Our Territorial Waters; We Issue Our Fourteenth Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: On September 26th, six U.S. warships intruded into our territorial waters in the Fujian area. Two of them intruded into the inner sea of our Matsu (Mazu) area. A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare: The Chinese government lodges its fourteenth serious warning against this grave military provocation by the United States.

### 美舰昨日继续侵入我领海挑衅  我第十四次提严重警告

1958-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰昨日继续侵入我领海挑衅
    我第十四次提严重警告
    新华社26日讯  9月26日美国军舰六艘侵入我国福建地区的领海，其中两艘侵入到我国马祖地区的内海，我外交部发言人奉命声明：对美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第十四次提出严重警告。
