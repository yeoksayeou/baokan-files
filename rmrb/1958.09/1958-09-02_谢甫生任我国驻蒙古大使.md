Title: Xie Fusheng Appointed as China's Ambassador to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Xie Fusheng has been appointed by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Mongolia.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed He Ying from the post of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Mongolia.

### 谢甫生任我国驻蒙古大使

1958-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　谢甫生任我国驻蒙古大使
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命谢甫生为中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去何英中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
