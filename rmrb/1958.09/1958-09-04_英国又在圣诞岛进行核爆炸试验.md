Title: Britain Conducts Another Nuclear Explosion Test at Christmas Island
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 5

    Britain Conducts Another Nuclear Explosion Test at Christmas Island
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. London dispatch: According to British official announcements, on the 2nd Britain conducted another nuclear explosion test over Christmas Island in the Pacific.
  This is the second test in a series of nuclear explosion tests that the British government began on Christmas Island after announcing its willingness to negotiate with the Soviet Union on the issue of halting nuclear tests.

### 英国又在圣诞岛进行核爆炸试验

1958-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    英国又在圣诞岛进行核爆炸试验
    新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：据英国官方宣布，英国2日又在太平洋的圣诞岛上空进行了一次核爆炸试验。
  这是英国政府宣布愿意同苏联谈判停止核试验问题以后，在圣诞岛开始的一系列核爆炸试验中的第二次试验。
