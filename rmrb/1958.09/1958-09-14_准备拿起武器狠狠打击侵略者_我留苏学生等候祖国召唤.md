Title: Preparing to Take Up Arms and Severely Strike the Aggressors - Chinese Students in the Soviet Union Await the Call of the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Ready to Take Up Arms and Severely Strike the Aggressors
Chinese Students in the Soviet Union Await the Call of the Motherland
    This newspaper reports from Moscow on the 12th: Chinese students in the Soviet Union unanimously support Premier Zhou’s statement and firmly protest the aggressive acts of the U.S. imperialists in interfering in our country's internal affairs. On the 11th, Chinese students from Moscow University, Moscow Institute of Non-Ferrous Metals, Moscow Power Engineering Institute, and other schools held rallies to protest the military provocations of U.S. imperialism. They said, the Chinese people must liberate Taiwan! We must liberate Jinmen and Mazu! If U.S. imperialism dares to continue its armed interference in our internal affairs, we will certainly wipe out the aggressors completely!
    At the rally protesting U.S. imperialist aggression, Chinese students at Moscow University unanimously expressed their determination to strengthen their physical training and be ready at any time to heed the call of the motherland, take up arms, and defend the motherland. They also put forward a proposal at the meeting, calling on all Chinese students in the Soviet Union to jointly donate an airplane before the National Day on October 1st to severely strike U.S. imperialism. In addition, they also passed a letter of respect to the soldiers on the Fujian front and a letter of condolence to the students of Xiamen University.

### 准备拿起武器狠狠打击侵略者  我留苏学生等候祖国召唤

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    准备拿起武器狠狠打击侵略者
    我留苏学生等候祖国召唤
    本报莫斯科12日电　在苏联的中国留学生一致拥护周总理的声明，坚决抗议美帝国主义干涉我国内政的侵略行为。11日，莫斯科大学、莫斯科有色金属学院、莫斯科动力学院等校的中国留学生，都纷纷举行集会，抗议美帝国主义的军事挑衅行为。他们说，中国人民一定要解放台湾！一定要解放金门、马祖！如果美帝国主义敢于继续武装干涉我国内政，我们就一定把侵略者消灭干净！
    莫斯科大学的中国留学生在抗议美帝侵略的大会上，一致表示要加强锻炼身体，随时听候祖国的号召，拿起武器，保卫祖国。他们还在会上提出一项倡议，号召在苏联的全体中国留学生在十月一日国庆节前，共同捐献一架飞机，以狠狠地打击美帝国主义。此外，他们还通过了致福建前线战士的致敬信和致厦门大学同学的慰问信。
