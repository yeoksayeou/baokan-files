Title: The Friendship Between the Chinese and Iranian People Cannot Be Severed: Zhang Xiruo Hosts a Banquet for the Iranian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Iraqi Republic Cultural Delegation, a group of fifteen led by Abed Zarzara, Chief Inspector of the Iraqi Ministry of Education, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 29th.

This delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The delegation members include representatives from Iraqi education, medicine, and cultural circles.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a luncheon today to welcome all members of the Iraqi Republic Cultural Delegation headed by Director Zarzara.

All members of the Iraqi People's Friendship Delegation to China, headed by Mahmoud, and representatives of Iraqi students in Beijing were also invited to attend the luncheon.

In his speech at the luncheon, Director Zarzara spoke of the friendship between the Iraqi and Chinese people. After warmly expressing his congratulations on the ninth anniversary of China's National Day, he sincerely hoped that China would also send a delegation to Baghdad in the future to participate in the celebrations of the founding of the Iraqi Republic.

In his speech, Director Zarzara once again expressed the Iraqi people's determination to further consolidate their national independence and defend peace.

Zhang Xiruo delivered a speech at the beginning of the luncheon, paying tribute to all Iraqi friends. In his speech, he warmly praised the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Iraqi peoples, and especially expressed his gratitude to the Iraqi people for their sympathy and support for the Chinese people in their struggle against American armed provocations and the liberation of Taiwan.

Liu Ningyi, President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, also addressed the luncheon, congratulating the growing friendship between the two peoples and the increasing victory of the common struggle against colonialism.

Also present at the luncheon were Burhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Director of the China Islamic Association, and all the directors of the China-Iraq Friendship Association in Beijing.

### 中伊人民友谊割不断  张奚若欢宴伊文化代表团

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    中伊人民友谊割不断
    张奚若欢宴伊文化代表团
    新华社29日讯  伊拉克共和国文化代表团一行十五人，由伊拉克教育部总督学阿布德·扎尔扎拉率领，在29日晨乘飞机到达北京。
    这个代表团是应我国对外文化联络委员会的邀请来中国访问的。代表团团员包括伊拉克教育界、医学界和文化界等方面的代表人士。
    对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若今天中午举行酒会，欢迎以扎尔扎拉团长为首的伊拉克共和国文化代表团全体人员。
    以马哈茂德团长为首的伊拉克人民友好访华团全体人员和在北京的伊拉克学生代表，也应邀出席酒会。
    扎尔扎拉团长在酒会上的讲话谈到了伊拉克人民和中国人民的友谊。他热情地表示了对中国国庆九周年的祝贺以后，衷心地希望中国将来也能够派代表团到巴格达去参加伊拉克共和国成立的庆祝日。
    扎尔扎拉团长在讲话中再一次表达了伊拉克人民进一步巩固祖国独立和保卫和平的决心。
    张奚若在酒会开始时致词，向全体伊拉克朋友们致敬。他在讲话中热情地歌颂了中国伊拉克两国人民的战斗友谊，并且特别表示感谢伊拉克人民对中国人民在反对美国武装挑衅和解放台湾的斗争中所给的同情支持。
    中国伊拉克友好协会会长刘宁一也在酒会上致词，祝贺两国人民的友谊日益发展，祝贺反殖民主义的共同斗争日益胜利。
    出席酒会作陪的，还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉和中国伊拉克友好协会的全体在京理事。
