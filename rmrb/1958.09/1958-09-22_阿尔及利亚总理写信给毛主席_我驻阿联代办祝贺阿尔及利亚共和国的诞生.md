Title: Algerian Prime Minister Writes Letter to Chairman Mao; Our Chargé d'Affaires in the United Arab Republic Congratulates the Birth of the Algerian Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 20th – Zhang Yue, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cairo, received Ahmed Francis, Minister of Finance and Economy, Yousuf Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Affairs, of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic today. They handed Zhang Yue a letter to Chairman Mao Zedong signed by Ferhat Abbas, Prime Minister of Algeria.
　　Zhang Yue congratulated the birth of the Algerian Republic and promised to forward the letter to Chairman Mao immediately.

### 阿尔及利亚总理写信给毛主席  我驻阿联代办祝贺阿尔及利亚共和国的诞生

1958-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚总理写信给毛主席
    我驻阿联代办祝贺阿尔及利亚共和国的诞生
    新华社开罗20日电　中国驻开罗大使馆临时代办张越，今天接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府财政经济部长艾哈迈德·弗朗西斯、社会事务部长优素福·本·赫达，他们把阿尔及利亚总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯签字的一封给毛泽东主席的信交给了张越。
    张越祝贺阿尔及利亚共和国的诞生，并且答应立即把这封信转交毛泽东主席。
