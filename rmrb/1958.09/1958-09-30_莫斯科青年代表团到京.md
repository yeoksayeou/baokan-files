Title: Moscow Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. A thirteen-member Moscow Youth Delegation, invited by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and led by A.A. Soshin, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th.

### 莫斯科青年代表团到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科青年代表团到京
    新华社29日讯  应共青团北京市委员会邀请来北京访问的莫斯科市青年代表团一行十三人，由苏联列宁共产主义青年团莫斯科市委书记阿·阿·索辛率领，29日乘飞机到达北京。
