Title: Khrushchev Receives Ambassador Liu Xiao
Author:
Date: 1958-09-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Yalta, September 17th: According to TASS, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, in Yalta on the 16th and had a cordial conversation with him.

After the conversation, Liu Xiao and his wife had dinner with Khrushchev and his family.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见刘晓大使

1958-09-18
第3版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见刘晓大使
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社雅尔塔讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫16日在雅尔塔接见了中国驻苏联大使刘晓，并且同他进行了亲切的谈话。
    谈话以后，刘晓和他的夫人同赫鲁晓夫一家人共进了晚餐。
