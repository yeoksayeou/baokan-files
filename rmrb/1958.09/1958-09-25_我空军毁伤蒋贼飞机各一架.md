Title: Our Air Force Destroys and Damages One Chiang Kai-shek Plane Each

Author:
Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 24th. On September 24th, Chiang Kai-shek's bandit planes sneaked into the airspace over our Fujian mainland to carry out harassment activities. One F86 type Chiang plane was shot down and one was damaged by our heroic air force.

### 我空军毁伤蒋贼飞机各一架

1958-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军毁伤蒋贼飞机各一架
    新华社福建前线24日电  9月24日蒋贼军飞机窜入我福建大陆上空进行袭扰活动，被我英勇空军击落击伤F86型蒋机各一架。
