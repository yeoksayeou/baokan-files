Title: San Shenggong Yellow River Bridge Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot Dispatch: The main bridge of the San Shenggong Bridge, the largest Yellow River railway bridge on the Baotou-Lanzhou Railway, was completed and officially opened to traffic on the 9th. This project was completed 116 days ahead of the original plan.

The San Shenggong Bridge is the longest of the three Yellow River bridges on the Baotou-Lanzhou line, with a total length of 673 meters and a total of 12 spans. The foundation engineering adopted the Soviet Union's advanced "water jet pile sinking method," driving 454 pipe piles into the ground, which, when linked together, are nearly 30 li long, and pouring more than 80,000 cubic meters of concrete.

### 三盛公黄河大桥通车

1958-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    三盛公黄河大桥通车
    新华社呼和浩特电　包兰铁路上最大的一座黄河大铁桥——三盛公大桥的正桥，在9日建成并正式通车。这项工程比原计划提前一百一十六天完成。
    三盛公大桥是包兰路上三座黄河大桥中最长的一座，全长六百七十三公尺，共十二孔。基础工程采用苏联先进的“射水沉桩法”，打入地下的管桩有四百五十四根，联起来有近三十里长，浇灌的混凝土有八万多立方公尺。
