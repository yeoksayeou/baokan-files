Title: Making Mental Preparations, Waiting for the Motherland's Call: Young People Determined to Go to the Forefront of the Battle

Author:

Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 3

Making Mental Preparations, Waiting for the Motherland's Call
Young People Determined to Go to the Forefront of the Battle
　　We heard Dulles' "statement" on September 4th, which aroused our unparalleled anger and unbearable fury. We express our strongest protest against the U.S. imperialists' brutal interference in our internal affairs!
　　Now, we, a group of young people of eligible age, have made full mental preparations and are ready to respond to the call of the motherland at any time and go to the forefront of the battle. If the United States dares to launch an armed invasion, we will give them a devastating blow and let them be buried on the bottom of the Pacific Ocean, with no way back!
　　　　　　The Entire Locomotive Class of the Cadre School of the Railway Ministry Administration Bureau, Changxindian Factory

### 做好精神准备　等候祖国召唤  青年人决心走向战斗最前哨

1958-09-10
第3版()
专栏：

    做好精神准备　等候祖国召唤
    青年人决心走向战斗最前哨
    我们听了杜勒斯在9月4日的“声明”，激起了我们无比的愤怒和难以忍耐的怒火，对美帝国主义这种粗暴地干涉我国内政的强盗行动，表示最强烈的抗议！
    现在，我们这一群适龄的年青人，已做好充分精神准备，随时响应祖国召唤，走向战斗的最前哨。美国胆敢发动武装侵略，我们就给他以毁灭性的打击，让他葬身在太平洋海底，永无回去之路！
              长辛店铁道部工厂管理局干部学校机车班全体
