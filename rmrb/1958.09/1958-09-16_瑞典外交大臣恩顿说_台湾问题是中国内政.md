Title: Swedish Foreign Minister Undén Says the Taiwan Issue is China's Internal Affair
Author:
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th, Stockholm News: Swedish Foreign Minister Undén said in a speech in Sollefteå, northern Sweden, on September 14th that the Taiwan issue is China's internal affair and has nothing to do with other countries. Undén said: "To call the avoidance of interference in the internal affairs of other countries as an act of surrender like Munich is not a reasonable statement." A United Press International reporter said that Undén's sentence clearly referred to Eisenhower's televised broadcast on September 11th.

Undén also said that treating the Chiang Kai-shek clique as the appropriate representative of the largest country in Asia and Africa is "becoming increasingly absurd and ridiculous."

### 瑞典外交大臣恩顿说  台湾问题是中国内政

1958-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    瑞典外交大臣恩顿说
    台湾问题是中国内政
    新华社15日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：瑞典外交大臣恩顿14日在瑞典北部的索勒夫提亚发表演说时说，台湾问题是中国的内政问题，同其他国家无关。恩顿说：“把避免干涉其他一些国家的内政，称之为是同慕尼黑一样的投降行为，这种说法不能说是合理的。”合众国际社记者说，恩顿这一句话显然指的是艾森豪威尔9月11日发表的电视广播演说。
    恩顿还说，把蒋介石集团当作亚非最大的国家的适当代表，是“越来越荒唐可笑了”。
