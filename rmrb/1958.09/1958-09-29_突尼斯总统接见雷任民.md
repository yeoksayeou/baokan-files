Title: Tunisian President Receives Lei Renmin
Author:
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaiwan, September 27th: Tunisian President Bourguiba today received Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation, and all members of the delegation in Kaiwan, a Muslim holy city about 180 kilometers south of Tunis.

　　Bourguiba received Lei Renmin during his annual public reception for foreign diplomats and Tunisian civil leaders who gathered in Kaiwan to celebrate the Muslim festival of Muhammad's birthday. Lei Renmin wished the President a happy holiday and Tunisia prosperity. In his reply, the President wished China prosperity and that the relations between Tunisia and China would become closer.

### 突尼斯总统接见雷任民

1958-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯总统接见雷任民
    据新华社凯万27日电  突尼斯总统布尔吉巴今天在突尼斯以南大约一百八十公里的穆斯林圣城凯万接见了正在这里访问的中国政府贸易代表团团长雷任民和全体团员。
    布尔吉巴是在每年公开接见聚集在凯万庆祝穆斯林节日——穆罕默德诞辰的外国外交人员和突尼斯民间领袖的时候接见雷任民的。雷任民祝总统在节日中愉快，祝突尼斯繁荣。总统在致答辞时祝中国繁荣，祝突尼斯和中国的关系日趋密切。
