Title: The Philosophy Institute of the Academy of Sciences Determines Research Directions and Tasks
Author:
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 7
Academic Trends

    The Philosophy Institute of the Academy of Sciences has recently further clarified the directions and tasks of its research work, determining that philosophical research must step out of the study and go deep into practice, serving politics and serving production. In order to achieve this, researchers must go into practical life to implement the "four combinations," namely the combination of theory and practice, the combination of learning and teaching, the combination of scientific research and production activities, and the combination of mental and physical labor. Currently, the vast majority of researchers are about to go down to factories or people's communes. The institute plans to complete four types of works before National Day next year: (1) "Ten Years of Chinese Philosophy." (2) "Textbook of Historical Materialism" - as a reader for communist theory education. (3) "Philosophy Readers for Workers and Peasants" (Historical Materialism and Dialectical Materialism written separately). (4) "Collection of Critical Essays on Bourgeois Academic Thought" (Chinese Philosophy History, Western Philosophy History, and Logic written separately).

### 科学院哲学研究所确定研究的方针和任务

1958-09-26
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    科学院哲学研究所确定研究的方针和任务
    哲学研究所最近进一步明确了研究工作的方针和任务，确定了哲学研究工作必须走出书斋，深入实际，为政治服务，为生产服务。为了做到这一点，研究人员要到实际生活中去实行“四结合”，即理论与实际结合、学与教结合、科学研究与生产活动结合、脑力劳动与体力劳动结合。目前绝大部分研究人员，即将下到工厂或人民公社。该所准备在明年国庆前完成四类著作：（1）十年来的中国哲学。（2）历史唯物主义教科书——作为共产主义理论教育的读本。（3）工农哲学读物（历史唯物主义、辩证唯物主义分开撰写）。（4）资产阶级学术批判论文集（中国哲学史、西方哲学史和逻辑分开编写）。
