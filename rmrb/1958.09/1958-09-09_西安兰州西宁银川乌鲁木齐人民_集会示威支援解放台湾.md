Title: Xi'an, Lanzhou, Xining, Yinchuan, and Urumqi People Hold Rallies and Demonstrations to Support the Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, September 7th: Over 600,000 people in Xi'an participated in rallies and parades today, supporting Premier Zhou's statement and denouncing U.S. imperialist military provocations. Liu Geng, the Mayor of Xi'an and Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, stated at the rally that, on behalf of the city's more than one million people, they were determined to support the liberation of Jinmen and Matsu, and the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions of overfulfilling this year's production targets. Representatives of workers, farmers, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations also spoke at the rally.

The People's Liberation Army and Air Force units stationed in Xi'an also held separate rallies to protest the aggressive actions of U.S. imperialism.

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, September 7th: 400,000 people in Lanzhou held rallies and parades today, warmly supporting Premier Zhou Enlai's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait area and resolutely opposing U.S. imperialist military provocations and war threats. Starting at 7:00 AM, slogans such as "U.S. troops get out of the Taiwan area" resounded throughout the urban and suburban areas of Lanzhou, and countless protest letters and resolutions were plastered on many streets and walls. Employees of steelmaking units participating in today's parade pledged to complete the task of refining 100,000 tons of steel this year to support the liberation of Jinmen and Matsu, and the liberation of Taiwan.

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, September 7th: More than 300,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xining City held demonstrations and parades overnight starting on the evening of the 6th. While parading, the masses conducted propaganda, with hundreds of costumed propaganda teams and propaganda vehicles mobilized on the streets.

At 3:00 PM today, more than 60,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life gathered in the square at the foot of Beishan Mountain to hold the "Qinghai People of All Nationalities Rally Supporting Our Government's Statement and Opposing U.S. Imperialist Military Threats." Yuan Rengyuan, Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Qinghai Province, spoke at the meeting, calling on the people of all ethnic groups in the province to step up production, and at the same time to arm all the people to defend socialist construction and consolidate our national defense.

Combat heroes, workers, youth, representatives from business, cultural, and educational circles also spoke at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, September 7th: More than 30,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Yinchuan City held a demonstration and parade today, resolutely supporting Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait area and opposing U.S. imperialist military provocations in the Taiwan Strait.

At 3:00 PM, the parade converged from various streets to the South Gate Square of Yinchuan City to hold a demonstration rally. At the meeting, Ma Yuhuai, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in the entire autonomous region, strongly condemned the criminal actions of American imperialism. He also called on the people of the entire region of Ningxia to support the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions.

Xu Liqing, Director of the Minle Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Yinchuan City, said at the meeting: We vow to support the liberation of Jinmen and Matsu, and the liberation of Taiwan, with all our strength.

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 7th: 70,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Urumqi City held a large-scale rally and demonstration parade today. Countless slogans were posted on the streets, and many broadcasting propaganda vehicles were dispatched. Saifuddin, Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lieutenant General Guo Peng, Deputy Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and Ren Gebai, First Secretary of the Urumqi Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, all participated in today's mass rally. Personnel from the Soviet Consulate General in Urumqi also attended the meeting.

Saifuddin called on the people of the autonomous region to push industrial and agricultural production to a greater leap forward climax and to build and defend the socialist motherland with practical actions.

A grand demonstration parade was held after the meeting.

### 西安兰州西宁银川乌鲁木齐人民  集会示威支援解放台湾

1958-09-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　西安兰州西宁银川乌鲁木齐人民
　　集会示威支援解放台湾
    据新华社西安7日电　西安市今天有六十多万人参加了集会和游行，拥护周总理的声明，怒斥美帝的军事挑衅。西安市市长、中共西安市委书记刘庚在大会上讲话时，代表全市一百多万人民决心以超额完成今年生产任务的实际行动，支援解放金门、马祖，支援解放台湾。工人代表、农民代表，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人等都在大会上讲了话。
    驻西安的人民解放军和空军部队也分别集会，抗议美帝国主义的侵略行为。
    据新华社兰州7日电　兰州市四十万人今天集会游行，热烈拥护周恩来总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，坚决反对美帝国主义军事挑衅和战争威胁。早晨七点起，“美国军队从台湾地区滚出去”等口号声即响彻整个兰州市城区和郊区，数不尽的抗议书和决心书贴满了许多街道和墙壁。今天参加游行的炼钢单位的职工，纷纷提出保证完成今年炼钢十万吨的任务，以支援解放金门、马祖，解放台湾。
    新华社西宁7日电　西宁市三十多万各族各界人民从6日晚上开始通宵达旦举行示威游行，群众一面游行一面进行宣传，几百个化装宣传队和宣传车一齐出动在街头。
    今天下午三时，各族各界人民六万多人齐集在北山脚下的广场，举行了“青海各族各界人民拥护我国政府声明反对美帝国主义军事威胁大会”。中共青海省委书记、青海省省长袁任远在会上讲话，号召全省各族人民加紧生产，同时要把全体人民武装起来，保卫社会主义建设和巩固我们的国防。
    在会上讲话的还有战斗英雄、工人、青年、工商界、文教界的代表。
    据新华社银川电　银川市各族各界三万多人今天举行示威游行，坚决拥护周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，反对美帝国主义在台湾海峡的军事挑衅。
    下午三时，游行队伍从各个街道汇集到银川市南门广场，举行示威大会。会上，宁夏回族自治区筹备委员会副主任马玉槐代表全自治区的各族人民，强烈谴责美国帝国主义的罪恶行动。他并号召宁夏全区人民以实际行动支援解放台湾。
    银川市郊区民乐农业社主任徐立清在会上说：我们誓以全力支援解放金门、马祖，支援解放台湾。
    据新华社乌鲁木齐7日电　乌鲁木齐市各族各界七万人今天举行大规模的集会和示威游行。街道上贴着无数标语，出动了许多广播宣传车。中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会书记、新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎，政协新疆维吾尔自治区委员会主席包尔汉、中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂，人民解放军新疆军区副司令员郭鹏中将和中共乌鲁木齐市委员会第一书记任戈白等都参加今天的群众大会。苏联驻乌鲁木齐总领事馆的人员也参加了大会。
    赛福鼎号召自治区人民把工农业生产推向更大的跃进高潮，以实际行动建设和保卫社会主义祖国。
    会后举行了盛大游行示威。
