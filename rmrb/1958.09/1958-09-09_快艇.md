Title: Speedboat
Author: Huang Guodong
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 8

　　Speedboat

　　Little speedboat,
　　Head held high, chest out.
　　Flying across the sea,
　　Fast as a shooting star.
　　When enemy boats meet,
　　They're sent to a watery grave.

　　某部 Huang Guodong
　　(Unit unknown)
