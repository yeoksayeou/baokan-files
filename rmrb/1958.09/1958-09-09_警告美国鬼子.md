Title: Warning to the American Devils
Author: Xiao San
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 8

　　Warning to the American Devils
    Xiao San
　　You stubbornly cling to that filthy carpet (note)
　　——A small bunch of fleeing, vile traitorous groups;
　　You stubbornly embrace that political corpse
　　——Chiang Kai-shek, the bandit rejected by six hundred million people.
    (Ah! I really don't want to write these three words: they stain my pen and paper! The folk song sings: Chiang Kai-shek—Chiang should die!);
　　You have occupied our Taiwan for many years,
　　Interfering in our internal affairs, infringing on our sovereignty;
　　The new accounts of your aggression in the Middle East have not yet been settled,
　　And you come to the Far East to carry out provocations;
　　We have truly reached the end of our tolerance,
　　Today we issue a call of incomparable anger:
　　Whether it's Taiwan or Penghu,
　　Whether it's Kinmen or Matsu,
　　——All are our sacred territory!
　　We want to liberate these places,
　　To administer our border and coastal defense,
　　——No one has the right or power to stop us!
　　The Chinese people who have stood up have become giants,
　　Nothing can deceive them, nothing can frighten them!
　　Your paper tiger is already full of holes,
　　And you still dare to brandish your claws and bare your fangs at our doorstep!
　　If you have forgotten the lessons you have repeatedly received,
　　If you want to have another go with us,
　　Then come and taste the giant's club again,
　　We'll make you lie down like dead dogs and dead snakes!
　　You must know:
　　On our right shoulder—
　　Stands the great Soviet Union
　　And the entire socialist camp,
　　In our left hand—
　　Stand the national independence comrades-in-arms
　　And the people of the world who love peace.
　　We must liberate Kinmen and Matsu!
　　We must liberate Taiwan and Penghu!
　　If the American devils don't get out quickly,
　　You will all be buried in the belly of the fish.
    (Note) Chilean poet Pablo Neruda described the Chiang Kai-shek government as "a government as filthy as a carpet."

### 警告美国鬼子
萧三
1958-09-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　警告美国鬼子
    萧三
　　你死啃着那块龌龊的地毯（注）
　　——一小撮逃跑的下流卖国集团；
　　你死抱住那具政治僵尸
　　——被六亿人所唾弃的蒋贼介石。
    （哎！我真不愿写这三个字：它们沾污我的笔和纸！民歌唱的是：蒋介石——蒋该死！）；
　　你霸占我们的台湾已经多年，
　　干涉我国内政，侵犯我国主权；
　　你侵略中东的新账还没算清，
　　又来到远东进行挑衅；
　　我们实在已经忍无可忍，
　　今天发出无比愤怒的呼声：
　　不论台湾、澎湖，
　　不论金门、马祖，
　　——都是我国神圣的领土！
　　我们要解放这些地方，
　　管辖我们的边疆海防，
　　——谁也无权无力来阻挡！
　　站起来了的中国人民成了巨人，
　　任什么也骗他不了，吓他不倒！
　　你这纸老虎已是窟窿满身，
　　还敢在我们门口舞爪张牙！
　　你若是忘了多次受过的教训，
　　若是再要和我们较量较量，
　　你就再来尝尝巨人的一棍，
　　管叫你躺下像死狗死蛇一样！
　　你要知道：
　　在我们的右肩——
　　站着伟大的苏联
　　和整个社会主义阵营，
　　在我们左手——
　　立着民族独立的战友
　　和世界爱好和平的人民。
　　我们一定要解放金门、马祖！
　　我们一定要解放台湾、澎湖！
　　美国鬼子如不赶快滚出，
　　就将你全部葬身鱼腹。
    （注）智利诗人聂鲁达形容蒋介石政府是“龌龊得像地毯一样的政府”
