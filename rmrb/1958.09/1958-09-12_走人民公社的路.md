Title: Taking the Road of the People's Commune
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 8

Taking the Road of the People's Commune
　　The commune is like a boat
　　The commune is like a boat,
　　The east wind fills the sails,
　　Our helmsman is Chairman Mao,
　　Six hundred million people steer the boat.
　　　　(Dong Wei Bi, Funing County, Hebei)
　　Crossing the Yangtze River in a Day
　　Since we had the advanced cooperative,
　　The sandy land has undergone great changes,
　　Now joining the great commune,
　　We cross the Yangtze River in a day.
　　　　(Chen Bingzhong, Huailai County, Hebei)
　　The People's Commune Belongs to the People
　　The people's commune belongs to the people,
　　The people are the masters;
　　Members are united like brothers,
　　Townships and communes are all one family.
　　　　(Zhang Zemin, Shuguang Commune, Shanghai)
　　Industry and Agriculture Together
　　The People's Commune is so good,
　　Industry and agriculture are pursued together,
　　The crops in the fields are stronger than oxen,
　　The factory chimneys are taller than the sky.
  　　(Qiao Fuyu, Dongfeng Commune, Huailai, Hebei)
      Militia Are Not Lacking
    The People's Commune is good,
    Militia are not lacking,
    The commune has brigades,
    To protect production and construction,
    Actions are militarized,
    Everywhere are heroes.
    (Hu Shide, Dali County, Shaanxi)
    The Blueprint is Already Designed
  Break the old bricks and tiles,
  Smash the cooking stove to make fertilizer;
  Gather firewood to bake new bricks,
  And build tall buildings together.
  The blueprint is already designed,
  Tree-lined avenues in all directions,
  Small rivers flow around the commune,
  Large bridges span the rivers.
  Cars like meteors on the bridge,
  Members ride everywhere,
  Going to factories and fields,
  And taking a stroll in the park.
　　　　　　(Gu Gong)
　　The People's Commune Has Four Abundances
　　More accumulated production funds,
　　More manpower for mutual cooperation,
　　More agricultural implements for vigorous innovation,
　　More experience in unified leadership,
　　Everywhere are living Zhuge Liangs.
　　(Zhang Zemin, Shuguang Commune, Shanghai)
　　People's Commune Power Plant
　　Brick walls, red tiled roofs,
　　The chimney stands tall in the blue sky;
　　A white painted sign hangs at the entrance:
　　"People's Commune Power Plant".
　　The power plant, so beautiful,
　　The motor hums at night,
　　All the lights in the commune shine,
　　Like a cluster of stars falling on the ground.
　　　　(Shen Yan, Huaiyin County, Jiangsu)
　　Thousands of Branches Share One Root
　　Thousands of branches share one root,
　　Branches connected, green and shady,
　　Millions of hands with one heart,
　　With united hearts, yellow earth turns to gold,
　　The people's commune is truly good,
　　Together we walk the happy spring of socialism.
　　(Luo Shigang, Guanting People's Commune)

### 走人民公社的路

1958-09-12
第8版()
专栏：

    走人民公社的路
    公社好比一只船
    公社好比一只船，
    东风鼓起船上帆，
    舵手是咱毛主席，
    赶船的人六万万。
    （河北抚宁县董为璧）
    一天就把长江跨
    自打有了高级社，
    沙地起了大变化，
    如今参加大公社，
    一天就把长江跨。
        （河北怀来县陈秉忠）
    人民公社属人民
    人民公社属人民，
    人民当家作主人；
    社员团结似兄弟，
    乡乡社社一家人。
        （上海曙光社张泽民）
    工农业一齐搞
    人民公社就是好，
    工业农业一齐搞，
    地里庄稼比牛壮，
    工厂烟囱比天高。
  （河北怀来东风公社乔福钰）
      民兵不缺少
    人民公社好，
    民兵不缺少，
    社内有大队，
    生产建设保，
    行动军事化，
    到处是英豪。
    （陕西大荔县扈世德）
    图纸已经设计好
  破砖烂瓦全拆掉，
  砸碎锅台做肥料；
  拾把柴火烧新砖，
  齐把楼房来建造。
  图纸已经设计好，
  东南西北林荫道，
  公社四周流小河，
  小河上面横大桥。
  桥上汽车似流星，
  社员乘着满处跑，
  又去工厂又下地，
  还去公园绕三绕。
              （顾工）
  人民公社有四多
  生产资金积累多，
  互相协作人力多，
  大力革新农具多，
  统一领导经验多，
  到处都是活诸葛。
  （上海曙光社　张泽民）
  人民公社发电厂
  青砖墙，红瓦房，
  烟囱竖到蓝天上；
  门口挂上白漆牌：
  “人民公社发电厂”。
  发电厂，真漂亮，
  晚来电机突突响，
  全社电灯一齐亮，
  好似星群落地上。
      （江苏淮阴县  沈彦）
  千条树枝一股根
  千条树枝一股根，
  枝连枝儿绿荫荫，
  千万只手一颗心，
  心齐黄土变成金，
  人民公社真正好，
  同走社会主义幸福春。
  （官厅人民公社  罗士钢）
