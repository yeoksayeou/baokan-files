Title: Soviet Government Note to Our Government: Complete Respect for Our Decision on Territorial Seas
Author:
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Soviet Charge d'Affaires ad interim to China, Antonov, delivered the Soviet government's note to the Chinese government to Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country on the 9th. The note expressed complete respect for our government's declaration on territorial seas. The full text of the note is as follows:

The Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics presents its respects to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China and, on behalf of the Soviet Government, has the honor to notify the following regarding the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' note of September 4 this year, which was attached to the Declaration of the Government of the People's Republic of China on China's territorial sea:

The Soviet Government has learned of and fully respects the decision in the Declaration of the Government of the People's Republic of China, namely: that the breadth of the territorial sea of the People's Republic of China is set at twelve nautical miles, and that all foreign military vessels and foreign aircraft may not enter the territorial sea and the airspace above the territorial sea of the People's Republic of China without the permission of the Government of the People's Republic of China. It has also learned of the method of calculating the territorial sea of the People's Republic of China as stipulated in the declaration.

The Soviet Government has learned that the decision of the Government of the People's Republic of China applies to the entirety of the territory of the People's Republic of China, including the Chinese mainland, Taiwan, and the islands surrounding it, the Penghu Islands, and other islands belonging to the People's Republic of China.

The Embassy has the honor to inform the Ministry of Foreign Affairs that the relevant Soviet authorities have been instructed to strictly observe the twelve-nautical-mile boundary line of the territorial sea of the People's Republic of China as stipulated by the Government of the People's Republic of China.

### 苏联政府照会我国政府  完全尊重我国关于领海的决定

1958-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联政府照会我国政府
    完全尊重我国关于领海的决定
    新华社10日讯　苏联驻中华人民共和国临时代办安东诺夫9日把苏联政府给中国政府的照会交给了我国外交部副部长章汉夫。照会表示完全尊重我国政府关于领海的声明。这份照会全文如下：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆谨向中华人民共和国外交部表示敬意，并受苏联政府的委托荣幸地就今年9月4日外交部的附有中华人民共和国政府关于中国领海的声明的照会作如下的通知：
    苏联政府已经获悉并且完全尊重中华人民共和国政府声明中的决定，即：中华人民共和国的领海宽度规定为十二海里、一切外国军用船舶和外国飞机。未经中华人民共和国政府的许可不得进入中华人民共和国的领海和领海上空。同时也获悉声明所规定的计算中华人民共和国领海的方法。
    苏联政府获悉，中华人民共和国政府的决定适用于中华人民共和国的全部领土，包括中国大陆、台湾及其周围的岛屿、澎湖列岛以及其他属于中华人民共和国的岛屿。
    大使馆荣幸地通知外交部，苏联有关的机构已经得到指示严格遵守中华人民共和国政府所规定的十二海里的中华人民共和国领海的界线。
