Title: Hungarian Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Hungarian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Hungarian People's Republic Military Attaché to China, Mo Xi Ferenc and his wife, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian People's Army.

Invited to the reception were Senior General Huang Kecheng, Senior General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Naval Senior General Wang Hongkun, Air Force Senior General Liu Zhen and other high-ranking generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and representatives of officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force, as well as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei.

General Baluku, head of the Albanian Military Delegation visiting China, General Salajan, head of the Romanian Military Delegation visiting China, and Lieutenant General Bat Doorj, head of the Mongolian People's Republic Military Delegation visiting China, were also invited to attend the reception.

Also present at today's reception were Hungarian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Nogradi, as well as diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China.

### 庆祝匈人民军建军节  匈驻华武官举行招待会

1958-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈人民军建军节
    匈驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社29日讯  匈牙利人民共和国驻华武官莫西·费伦茨及夫人今天下午举行招待会，庆祝匈牙利人民军建军节。
    应邀出席招待会的有黄克诚大将、王树声大将、萧华上将、张宗逊上将、彭绍辉上将、王宏坤海军上将、刘震空军上将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆、海、空军军官代表，还有我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团团长巴卢库上将、罗马尼亚访华军事代表团团长萨拉扬上将、蒙古人民共和国访华军事代表团团长巴特·道尔吉中将等也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席今天招待会的还有匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使诺格拉第以及各国驻华使节和武官。
