Title: East German Agriculture Reaps a Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1958-09-19
Page: 4

    East German Agriculture Reaps a Bumper Harvest
    This year, the German Democratic Republic has achieved a bumper grain harvest across the board. The average yield per hectare for various grains has increased by 180 kilograms compared to last year, when the average yield per hectare was 2,330 kilograms.

### 民主德国农业丰收

1958-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国农业丰收
    今年，德意志民主共和国的谷物普遍获得了丰收。各种谷物平均单位面积产量每公顷比去年增产一百八十公斤，去年每公顷平均产量是二千三百三十公斤。
