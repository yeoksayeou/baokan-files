Title: Bulgarian Newspapers Say: China is an Invincible Force
Author:
Date: 1958-09-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 5th - Bulgarian newspapers "Otechestven Front" and "Zemedelsko Zname" today published editorials and articles strongly condemning the military provocations of the American imperialists against China.

    "Otechestven Front" in its editorial exposed the evil schemes of the American aggressors against China. It emphasized that all conspiracies against China will be defeated because the Chinese people are strong and determined to be masters of their own territory. The Chinese people consider it their sacred duty to liberate their territory from the Chiang Kai-shek bandits who were driven out of the mainland to complete the unification of their motherland. They are determined to liberate these territories. The Chinese people are no longer weak and helpless in the face of aggressors, and the people of all countries in the powerful socialist camp and all peace-loving and democratic people in the world stand with the Chinese people.
    "Zemedelsko Zname" in a short commentary emphasized that the peace-loving people of all countries regard the new aggressive conspiracy of the American imperialists as a provocation against world peace, so they firmly stand on the side of the People's Republic of China. Military adventurers should remember that the struggle of the Chinese people to recover Taiwan and the coastal islands is sacred, and the Soviet Union, the socialist camp, and all progressive humanity support this struggle. It is indeed arrogant to provoke such an invincible force.

### 保加利亚报纸说：中国是不可战胜的力量

1958-09-07
第2版()
专栏：

    保加利亚报纸说：中国是不可战胜的力量
    据新华社索非亚5日电　保加利亚“祖国阵线报”和“农业旗帜报”今天发表社论和文章，严厉谴责美帝国主义者对中国的军事挑衅。
    “祖国阵线报”在社论中揭露了美国侵略者对中国的罪恶阴谋。它着重指出，一切反对中国的阴谋都将遭到失败，因为中国人民是强大的，他们决心成为本国领土的主人。中国人民认为从被驱逐出大陆的蒋介石强盗手中解放本国领土以完成祖国的统一，是自己神圣的职责。他们决心要解放这些领土。中国人民在侵略者面前不是软弱无力的了，强大的社会主义阵营各国人民和世界上一切爱好和平民主的人们都和中国人民站在一起。
    “农业旗帜报”在短评中着重指出，爱好和平的各国人民把美国帝国主义者的新的侵略阴谋看成是对世界和平的挑衅，所以他们坚决站在中华人民共和国的一边。军事冒险狂人应该记住，中国人民收复台湾及沿海岛屿的斗争是神圣的，苏联、社会主义阵营和全体进步人类都支持这一斗争。向这种不可战胜的力量挑衅，的的确确是狂妄的。
