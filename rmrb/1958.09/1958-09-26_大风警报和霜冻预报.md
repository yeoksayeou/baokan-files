    Gale Warning and Frost Forecast
    (Reported by this newspaper) At 7:00 PM on September 25, 1958, the Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale warning and frost forecast: It is expected that the Hexi Corridor will have northerly winds of level 6 or 7 in the next 24 hours. In the next 24 to 48 hours, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Heilongjiang Province will experience northerly winds of level 6 or 7 successively from west to east. Eastern Gansu, northern Shaanxi, northern Shanxi, and northern Hebei will have northerly winds of level 5 or 6. In the next 48 to 72 hours, Jilin, Liaoning, the Bohai Sea, southern Hebei, southern Shanxi, and Shandong will have northerly winds of level 5 or 6. In the aforementioned areas, the temperature will drop by 8 to 10 degrees Celsius after the cold air front passes. It is expected that after the wind stops and the sky clears on the mornings of the 27th and 28th, the Hexi Corridor and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will experience frost successively. On the morning of the 29th, in the northeastern provinces excluding coastal areas, as well as in northern Shanxi and northwestern Hebei, frost will also occur.

### 大风警报和霜冻预报

1958-09-26
第6版()
专栏：

    大风警报和霜冻预报
    本报讯  1958年9月25日下午七时中央气象科学研究所发布大风警报和霜冻预报：预计未来二十四小时内河西走廊将有六、七级的偏北风，未来二十四到四十八小时内，内蒙古自治区和黑龙江省自西向东将先后有六、七级的偏北风，甘肃东部、陕西北部、山西北部、和河北北部将有五、六级的偏北风。未来四十八至七十二小时内，吉林、辽宁、渤海、河北南部、山西南部和山东将有五、六级偏北风。以上地区在冷空气前锋过境后，气温将下降摄氏八到十度。预计27日和28日晨河西走廊和内蒙古自治区风停天晴以后，将先后有霜冻。29日晨东北各省除沿海以外的地区，山西北部和河北西北部也将有霜冻出现。
