Title: Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th -- The Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Ensemble, invited to visit China under the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by jet on the afternoon of the 27th, led by the head of the delegation, Sayit Zade, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Azerbaijan Republic.

    The Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Ensemble is one of the Soviet Union's large national song and dance troupes and has a history of twenty-two years. The ensemble will be performing in China for two months.

### 苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团抵京

1958-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团抵京
    据新华社27日讯  根据中苏文化合作协定1958年执行计划应邀来我国访问的苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团，在团长、阿塞拜疆共和国文化部副部长萨伊特·扎德率领下，于27日下午乘喷气式客机到达北京。
    苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团是苏联大型的民族歌舞团之一，现已有二十二年的历史。这个歌舞团将在我国进行两个月的访问演出。
