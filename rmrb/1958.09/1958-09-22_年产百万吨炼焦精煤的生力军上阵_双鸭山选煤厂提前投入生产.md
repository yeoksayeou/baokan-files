Title: A New Force with an Annual Output of One Million Tons of Washed Coking Coal Enters the Scene: Shuangyashan Coal Preparation Plant Put into Production Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 21st. A modern coal preparation plant - the Central Coal Preparation Plant of the Shuangyashan Mining Bureau, was officially put into production ahead of schedule yesterday. From now on, it will produce a large amount of "food" for the rapidly developing steel marshal.

The Central Coal Preparation Plant of the Shuangyashan Mining Bureau is one of the large-scale modern factories built with the help of the Soviet Union, equipped with world-class technology and equipment. Its main product is washed coking coal, which is indispensable to the steel industry. According to its design capacity, it can wash 1.5 million tons of raw coal annually and recover more than 1.16 million tons of washed coal.

At the handover ceremony held yesterday, Lu Zemin, Chairman of the State Acceptance Committee and First Secretary of the Shuangyashan Municipal Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Volodin, head of the Soviet delegation who was invited to China to participate in the handover ceremony of this factory, delivered speeches.

### 年产百万吨炼焦精煤的生力军上阵  双鸭山选煤厂提前投入生产

1958-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    年产百万吨炼焦精煤的生力军上阵
    双鸭山选煤厂提前投入生产
    据新华社哈尔滨21日电　现代化的选煤厂——双鸭山矿务局中央选煤厂提前在昨天正式投入生产。从现在起，它将为飞跃发展的钢铁元帅大量生产“粮食”。
    双鸭山矿务局中央选煤厂是苏联帮助我国建设的、用世界第一流技术设备装置起来的大型现代化工厂之一，它的主要产品是钢铁工业不可缺少的炼焦精煤。按照设计能力，它每年能选原煤一百五十万吨，可收回精煤一百一十六万多吨。
    在昨天举行的移交生产典礼大会上，国家验收委员会主任委员、中共双鸭山市委第一书记卢泽民、应邀来我国参加这个厂移交生产典礼大会的苏联代表团团长沃洛基金等讲了话。
