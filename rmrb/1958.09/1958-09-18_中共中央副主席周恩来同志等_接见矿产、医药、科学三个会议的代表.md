Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Others Receive Representatives of Three Conferences on Minerals, Medicine, and Science

Author:

Date: 1958-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Comrades Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, He Long, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Chen Boda, alternate member, Wu Yuzhang, Nie Rongzhen, members of the Central Committee, and Zhou Yang, alternate member, and Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received this afternoon all the geological scientists attending the first National Mineral Conference, all the representatives attending the National Conference on Exchange of Experiences in the Technological Revolution in Medicine and Health, and all the representatives attending the National Congress of the National Federation of Science and Technology and the National Congress of Popular Science.

### 中共中央副主席周恩来同志等  接见矿产、医药、科学三个会议的代表

1958-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央副主席周恩来同志等
    接见矿产、医药、科学三个会议的代表
    新华社17日讯　中共中央副主席周恩来，中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、彭真、贺龙和候补委员陈伯达，中央委员会委员吴玉章，聂荣臻和候补委员周扬，中国科学院院长郭沫若，在今天下午先后接见了参加全国第一届矿产会议的全体地质科学工作者、参加全国医药卫生技术革命经验交流会议的全体代表和参加全国科联全国科普全国代表大会的全体代表。
