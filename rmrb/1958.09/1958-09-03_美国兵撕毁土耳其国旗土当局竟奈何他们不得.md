Title: American Soldiers Tear Up Turkish Flag, Turkish Authorities Helpless
Author:
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Istanbul, September 1st (TASS): According to "Freedom Daily," on Turkish Victory Day, two sailors from the American Sixth Fleet tore up the Turkish flag in Izmir. The newspaper pointed out that the Americans were not arrested because special permission is required to arrest them.

### 美国兵撕毁土耳其国旗土当局竟奈何他们不得

1958-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国兵撕毁土耳其国旗土当局竟奈何他们不得
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社伊斯坦布尔1日讯：据“自由报”报道，在土耳其胜利日那天，在伊兹密尔，美国第六舰队的两名水兵撕毁了土耳其国旗。报纸指出，美国人没有被逮捕，因为要逮捕他们必须得到特别允许才行。
