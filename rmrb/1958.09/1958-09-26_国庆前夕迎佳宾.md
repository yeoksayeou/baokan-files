Title: Welcoming Guests on the Eve of National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 4

    American Woman Writer Strong Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency: American woman writer Anna Louise Strong, who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Writers Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the 18th. Zhu Bocheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Tang Mingzhao, a council member, were among those who welcomed her at the airport.
    Czechoslovak National Social Security Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The Czechoslovak National Social Security Workers Delegation, led by Erban, Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Security Bureau, arrived in Beijing on the 22nd. Xie Juezai, Minister of Internal Affairs, Vice Ministers Wang Ziyi, Wang Yifu, and Guo Bingkun, and Li Xian, Director of the Social Insurance Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.
    Ceylonese Minister of Health Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency: Mrs. Vimala Wijewardene, the Ceylonese Minister of Health, and another person arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou on the 24th.
    They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Health and will participate in the celebrations during China's National Day. Qian Xinzhong, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Health, and several experts, as well as Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Goonewardene, and his wife, and embassy staff, welcomed them at the station.

### 国庆前夕迎佳宾

1958-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    国庆前夕迎佳宾
    美国女作家斯特朗到京
    新华社讯  应中国人民对外文化协会和中国作家协会邀请来我国访问的美国女作家安娜·路易·斯特朗已于18日抵京。前往机场迎接的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯琛，理事唐明照等。
    捷国家社会保障工作者代表团到京
    新华社22日讯  捷克斯洛伐克国家社会保障工作者代表团在团长、捷克斯洛伐克国家保障局主席埃尔班率领下于22日到京。到机场欢迎的有内务部部长谢觉哉，副部长王子宜、王一夫、郭炳坤，中华全国总工会社会保险部部长李仙等。
    锡兰卫生部长到京
    新华社讯  锡兰卫生部部长维玛拉·维杰瓦德尼夫人等二人24日由广州乘火车抵达北京。
    他们应我国卫生部的邀请前来我国进行友好访问，并将参加我国国庆节期间的庆祝活动。到车站欢迎的有我国卫生部副部长钱信忠和专家多人，还有锡兰驻我国大使高伯瓦拉和他的夫人，以及使馆人员。
