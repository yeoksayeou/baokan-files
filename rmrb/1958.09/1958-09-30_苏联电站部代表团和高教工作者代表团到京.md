Title: Soviet Ministry of Power Stations Delegation and Higher Education Workers Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Soviet Ministry of Power Stations Delegation of five people, headed by the Soviet Minister of Power Stations, Pavlenko, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 29th at the invitation of the Minister of Water Conservancy and Electric Power of our country. Li Baohua, Acting Minister of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, and Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister, and others welcomed them at the airport. Fumin, Economic Cooperation Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Soviet Higher Education Workers Delegation of ten people, headed by Rumiantsev, Deputy Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by Tu-104 on September 29th. Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Education, and presidents of relevant universities in Beijing went to the airport to greet them.

### 苏联电站部代表团和高教工作者代表团到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联电站部代表团和高教工作者代表团到京
    新华社29日讯  应我国水利电力部部长邀请来我国访问的、以苏联电站部部长巴甫连科为首的苏联电站部代表团五人，已于29日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有水利电力部代理部长李葆华、副部长刘澜波等人。苏联驻中国大使馆经济合作参赞符明也到机场欢迎。
    新华社29日讯  以苏联高等教育部副部长鲁明采夫为首的苏联高等教育工作者代表团一行十人，已于29日乘图一○四抵京。教育部副部长刘子载以及北京有关高等院校校院长等曾前往机场迎接。
