Open Hearth Furnace Hearth Utilization Coefficient

The open hearth furnace hearth utilization coefficient is a major indicator of the production capacity of an open hearth furnace. It refers to the amount of steel produced per square meter of hearth area per day and night. The larger this coefficient, the higher the efficiency of the furnace.

### 平炉炉底利用系数

1958-09-11
第6版()
专栏：小辞典

    平炉炉底利用系数
    平炉炉底利用系数是衡量平炉生产能力的主要指标。它是指每一平方公尺炉底面积一昼夜所生产的钢的数量。这个系数越大，说明炉子的效率越高。
