Title: U.S. Aggression Fails to Gain Support: SEATO Nations and Australia Unwilling to Follow

Author:

Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 10th, Bangkok Dispatch: Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Secretary-General Pote Sarasin told reporters on the evening of the 8th, at a reception commemorating the fourth anniversary of the establishment of this military bloc, that "there is no need now to expand the organization's territory to include the Taiwan Strait."

　　As previously reported, U.S. Army Secretary Brucker, who is conducting war planning in the Far East, arrived in Bangkok on the 8th to attend the commemoration of the fourth anniversary of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

　　According to Bangkok newspapers, the United States has coerced the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization to "provide Chiang Kai-shek with military assistance."

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 10th, Canberra Dispatch: Australian Prime Minister Menzies said today that if a war breaks out in the Taiwan Strait, Australia has no obligation to join.

　　Menzies said that the scope of the ANZUS "security" treaty does not extend to Taiwan.

### 美国侵略行动得不到支持  东南亚集团国家和澳大利亚都不愿追随

1958-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    美国侵略行动得不到支持
    东南亚集团国家和澳大利亚都不愿追随
    新华社10日讯　曼谷消息：东南亚条约组织秘书长沙拉信8日晚上在纪念这个军事集团成立四周年的招待会上向记者们说，“现在无需把这个组织的地域扩大到包括台湾海峡。”
    前已报道，在远东进行战争策划的美国陆军部长布鲁克曾经在8日赶到曼谷，参加东南亚条约组织成立四周年的纪念会。
    据曼谷报纸报道，美国曾经胁迫东南亚条约组织“给予蒋介石军事援助”。
    新华社10日讯　堪培拉消息：澳大利亚总理孟席斯今天说，如果在台湾海峡爆发一场战争，澳大利亚没有义务加入。
    孟席斯说，美澳新“安全”条约的范围并没有扩展到台湾。
