Title: Unremitting Actions Loyal to the Cause of Peace: The Third Batch of the Chinese People's Volunteers is About to Begin Withdrawal

Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 3

    Unremitting Actions Loyal to the Cause of Peace
    The Third Batch of the Chinese People's Volunteers is About to Begin Withdrawal
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. The headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers issued a communique on the withdrawal of troops: The third batch of Chinese People's Volunteers will begin to withdraw from Korea on September 25, 1958.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong: Major General Jiang Shanghao, chief member of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, sent a letter to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on the 7th regarding the withdrawal of the third batch of the Chinese People's Volunteers from Korea.
    The letter stated that the third batch of the Chinese People's Volunteers will begin to withdraw from Korea via Sinuiju on September 25, 1958. Jiang Shanghao said that if the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission sends inspection teams to conduct inspections, the Korean-Chinese side will take appropriate steps.

### 忠于和平事业的不懈行动  志愿军第三批撤军即将开始

1958-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    忠于和平事业的不懈行动
　　志愿军第三批撤军即将开始
    新华社平壤7日电　中国人民志愿军总部撤军公报：中国人民志愿军第三批部队将于1958年9月25日开始撤离朝鲜。
    新华社平壤8日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员姜尚昊少将7日就中国人民志愿军第三批部队撤出朝鲜问题致函中立国监察委员会。
    这封信说，中国人民志愿军第三批部队将于1958年9月25日开始经由新义州撤离朝鲜。姜尚昊说，如果中立国监察委员会派出视察小组进行视察，朝中方面将采取适当步骤。
