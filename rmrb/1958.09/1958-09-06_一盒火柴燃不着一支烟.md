Title: A Box of Matches Can't Light a Single Cigarette
Author: Yang Dehan
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 2

From the Masses

    A Box of Matches Can't Light a Single Cigarette
    In August, I bought a package of "Hengzi" brand red-tip matches produced by the Tianjin Zhonghua Match Factory. The quality of these matches is so poor that a whole box of matches can't even light a single cigarette!
                (Hubei Yang Dehan)

### 一盒火柴燃不着一支烟
杨德汉
1958-09-06
第2版()
专栏：从群众中来

    一盒火柴燃不着一支烟
    8月份，我买了一包天津市中华火柴厂生产的“恒字”牌红头火柴。这种火柴质量太差，一盒火柴竟燃不着一支纸烟！
                （湖北  杨德汉）
