Title: Pakistan Appoints A.M. Malik as Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Government of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan has appointed Dr. A.M. Malik as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China.

### 巴基斯坦任命阿·姆·马立克为驻我国大使

1958-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦任命阿·姆·马立克为驻我国大使
    新华社15日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国政府已经任命阿·姆·马立克博士为驻华特命全权大使。
