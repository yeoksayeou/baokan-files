Title: Coastal Live Firing Practice
Author: Li Shihong
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 8

    Coastal Live Firing Practice
    A Unit of the Jinan Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Li Shihong
    Violent rain and wind howl,
    Coastal artillery is practicing firing.
    Rainwater soaks through military uniforms,
    Aiming at targets in the sea.
    Aim accurately,
    Point towards the target.
    Once the order is given, fire the cannons,
    Guarantee the "enemy ships" cannot escape.
    Determination cannot be dampened,
    Willpower cannot be blown down.
    Our common oath:
    Improve our skills to protect the country.

### 海岸练射击
李士宏
1958-09-11
第8版()
专栏：

    海岸练射击
    解放军济南军区某部  李士宏
    暴雨狂风呼呼叫，
    海防炮兵在练炮。
    雨水湿透了军衣，
    瞄准在海中的目标。
    正确瞄准，
    指向目标。
    命令一下就开炮，
    保证“敌舰”跑不掉。
    决心淋不动，
    意志刮不倒。
    我们共同的誓言：
    提高本领把国保。
