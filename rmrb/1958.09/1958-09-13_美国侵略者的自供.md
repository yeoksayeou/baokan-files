Confessions of the American Aggressors

    The heads of the American ruling clique, in order to conceal the crimes of American expansion and aggression around the world, have recently disguised themselves as protectors of national independence for various countries, and have slandered China, the Soviet Union, and other countries as aggressors. But black is black, and the ugly face of American imperialism cannot be covered up. When the American "Life" magazine, in its August 18th issue, boasted of the military exploits of the American invading army, it published some pictures of American aggression against other countries in history. Although this is extremely incomplete, it is enough to expose American imperialism as the most ferocious enemy of the people of the world.

    (Attached pictures)

    1801-1805, the United States launched a war against the North African Muslim countries, and the US Marines landed in Tripoli (now a port in Libya).

    In 1847, the United States launched a war against Mexico. The US Marines invaded Mexico City, the capital of Mexico.

    In 1871, the United States, on the pretext that Korea had burned an American ship that had run aground, sent troops to land at the mouth of the Han River. The landing site at that time was near the Incheon landing point of the US troops invading Korea in 1950.

    In 1900, the United States sent troops to participate in the Eight-Nation Alliance's invasion of Beijing, China.

    In 1912 and 1925, the United States twice sent troops to support Nicaraguan dictator Diaz in the bloody suppression of popular uprisings.

    In 1915, when the Haitian people's uprising overthrew the reactionary regime, the United States brazenly sent troops to suppress it.

### 美国侵略者的自供

1958-09-13
第3版()
专栏：

    美国侵略者的自供
    美国统治集团的头子，最近为了掩饰美国在世界各地进行扩张和侵略的罪行，把自己粉饰为各国民族独立的维护者，并且诬蔑中苏等国是侵略者。但是黑的就是黑的，美帝国主义的凶恶面目是掩盖不了的。美国“生活”杂志8月18日的一期在夸耀美国侵略军队的武功的时候，就登出了一些美国在历史上对其他国家进行侵略的图片，这虽然是极不完全的，但也足以暴露出美帝国主义是全世界人民最凶恶的敌人。
    （附图片）
    1801—1805年，美国对北非回教国发动战争，美国海军陆战队登陆的黎波里（今利比亚一港口）。
    1847年美国发动对墨西哥的战争。美国海军陆战队侵入墨西哥首都墨西哥市。
    1871年美国借口朝鲜烧毁美国一艘搁浅的船只，派兵登陆汉江口，当时登陆地点即在1950年侵朝美军在仁川登陆点附近。
    1900年美国派兵参加八国联军侵入我国北京。
    1912年和1925年美国两度出兵支持尼加拉瓜独裁统治者迪亚斯对人民起义的血腥镇压。
    1915年美国在海地人民起义推翻反动政权时，悍然出兵镇压。
