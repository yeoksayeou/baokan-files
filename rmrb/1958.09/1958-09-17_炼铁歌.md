Title: Iron Smelting Song
Author:
Date: 1958-09-17
Page: 1

    Iron Smelting Song

    (I)
    Industry blossoms red flowers everywhere, iron smelting adopts indigenous methods,
    Studying diligently and working hard to catch up with the advanced, steel piled up like pagodas.

    (II)
    Communes build large factories everywhere, vigorously engage in iron and steel smelting;
    Blast furnaces lined up row upon row, molten iron gushing out like a river.

    (III)
    Iron furnaces built in the Temple of the Fire God, bellows pulled with a whooshing sound,
    Molten iron flows gurgling out, the Fire God smiles with narrowed eyes upon seeing it.

### 炼铁歌

1958-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    炼铁歌
    （一）
    工业遍地开红花，炼铁采用土办法，
    苦钻苦学赶先进，钢铁堆起像宝塔。
    （二）
    社社兴建大工厂，大闹炼铁和炼钢；
    高炉排成一行行，铁水滚出像条江。
    （三）
    铁炉砌在火神庙，风箱扯起呼呼叫，
    炼成铁水汩汩流，火神见了眯眯笑。
