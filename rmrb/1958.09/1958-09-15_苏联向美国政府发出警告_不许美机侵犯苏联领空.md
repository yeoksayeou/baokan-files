Title: The Soviet Union Warns the U.S. Government Against U.S. Aircraft Violating Soviet Airspace
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 13th (TASS): The Soviet government, in a note to the U.S. Embassy on September 12th, firmly demanded that the U.S. government take effective measures to prevent U.S. aircraft from violating Soviet airspace in the future.

In response to the U.S. government's request for any possible information regarding a U.S. Air Force aircraft that crashed over Turkey on September 2nd, the Soviet government replied that an investigation conducted in response to the aforementioned U.S. note revealed the wreckage of a crashed aircraft and the remains of approximately six aircrew members fifty-five kilometers north of Yerevan (Armenia).

This aircraft belonged to the U.S. Air Force and had deeply intruded into Soviet airspace, crashing on Soviet territory. The Soviet note emphasized that the aircraft deliberately violated the Soviet border, for which the Soviet government lodged a firm protest with the U.S. government.

The note stated that repeated violations of Soviet airspace by U.S. aircraft and the U.S. authorities' failure to take measures to prevent similar violations can only be regarded as a policy that leads to the sharpening of Soviet-U.S. relations.

### 苏联向美国政府发出警告  不许美机侵犯苏联领空

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联向美国政府发出警告
    不许美机侵犯苏联领空
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：苏联政府在9月12日给美国大使馆的一份照会中坚决要求美国政府采取有效措施，不许美国飞机今后再侵犯苏联领空。
    鉴于美国政府请求向它提供一架美国空军飞机于9月2日在土耳其上空飞行时失事的任何可能的情报，苏联政府回答说：据针对上述美国照会所进行的调查表明，在距埃里温（亚美尼亚）北五十五公里的地方发现了一架坠毁的飞机残骸和大约六个飞行人员的尸体残骸。
    这架飞机是属于美国空军的，它侵入了苏联领空很远，并且坠落在苏联领土上。苏联照会强调指出，这架飞机是蓄意侵犯苏联国境的，为此苏联政府向美国政府提出坚决抗议。
    照会说，美国飞机一再侵犯苏联领空和美国当局不采取措施防止类似的侵犯，这只能看作是导致苏美两国关系尖锐化的一种政策。
