Title: "Pakistan Times" Says Armed Intervention is Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-09-01
Page: 4

　　"Pakistan Times" Says
　　Armed Intervention is Aggression
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 30th. The "Pakistan Times" published an editorial on the 29th regarding the tense situation created by the United States in the Taiwan Strait, stating that "these American conspiracies are highly provocative, and American armed intervention in this region will constitute an act of aggressive war."
　　The editorial said that the Chiang Kai-shek group, driven out of mainland China, has been raiding mainland China with direct military assistance and protection from the United States, sending spies to mainland China to carry out sabotage activities, and obstructing and disrupting Chinese and foreign merchant ships traveling to and from the Taiwan Strait.

### “巴基斯坦时报”说  武装干涉就是侵略

1958-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    “巴基斯坦时报”说
　　武装干涉就是侵略
    新华社卡拉奇30日电　“巴基斯坦时报”29日就美国在台湾海峡制造紧张局势一事发表社论说，“美国这些阴谋活动具有很大的挑衅性，美国对这个地区的武装干涉将构成侵略战争的行为”。
    社论说，被赶出中国大陆的蒋介石集团在美国直接的军事援助和庇护下，一直在偷袭中国大陆，派遣间谍去中国大陆进行破坏活动，而且在台湾海峡阻挠和破坏中国同外国的商船来往。
