Title: Prince Sihanouk Says the Chinese People Have the Right to Liberate Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 3

    Prince Sihanouk Says
    Chinese People Have the Right to Liberate Taiwan
    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 11th - Prince Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, made a written statement to a reporter from the Hong Kong Ta Kung Pao today on the current situation in the Taiwan Strait area, saying: "The opinion of most Asian countries is that Jinmen and Mazu are part of Chinese territory, and whether politically or geographically, the People's China's recovery of these islands is justified." He also said: "In my opinion, if a peaceful arrangement cannot be reached, the People's China is determined to use all means to restore its sovereignty over these coastal islands."
    Prince Sihanouk pointed out that "the best way to ease international tensions is for countries and heads of government to hold summit meetings to engage in frank discussions."
    Prince Sihanouk reiterated Cambodia's position in advocating for the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations. He said: "The People's China, representing more than 600 million people, should be accepted to participate in important international gatherings and conferences. I feel that if it is excluded, then finding satisfactory solutions to world peace, especially Asian peace issues, will be very difficult, if not impossible."

### 西哈努克亲王说  中国人民有权解放台湾

1958-09-13
第3版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王说
    中国人民有权解放台湾
    据新华社香港11日电　柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克亲王今天就目前台湾海峡地区局势问题向香港大公报记者发表书面谈话说：“亚洲大部分国家的意见都认为，金门和马祖是中国领土的一部分，无论从政治上和地理上来说，人民中国收回这些岛屿都是有根据的”。他又说：“在我看来，如果不能有和平安排的话，人民中国抱有决心用一切方法来恢复对这些沿海岛屿的主权”。
    西哈努克亲王指出，“缓和国际紧张局势的最好办法就是由各国和各国政府首脑们举行最高级会议，从事坦率的讨论。”
    西哈努克亲王重申柬埔寨主张恢复中国在联合国中的合法席位的意见。他说：“代表六亿多人民的人民中国应该被接受参加国际重大的集会和会议。我觉得，如果它被排斥在外，那么，找寻有关世界和平特别是亚洲和平问题的满意的解决办法时，即使不是不可能，也将是非常困难的。”
