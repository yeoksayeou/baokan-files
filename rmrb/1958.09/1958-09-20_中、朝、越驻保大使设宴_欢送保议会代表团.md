Title: Chinese, North Korean, and Vietnamese Ambassadors to Bulgaria Host Banquet to Bid Farewell to Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 18th: The ambassadors of China, North Korea, and Vietnam to Bulgaria, Zhu Qiwenz, Kim Hak In, and Hoang Van Loi, hosted a luncheon on September 18th to bid farewell to the entire delegation of the Bulgarian National Assembly, which is about to pay a friendly visit to China, North Korea, and Vietnam. The delegation will be led by Vulko Chervenkov, member of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Minister, and member of the National Assembly.

Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhu Qiwenz spoke at the banquet on behalf of the embassies of China, North Korea, and Vietnam in Bulgaria. He expressed a heartfelt welcome to the delegation for their friendly visit to the three countries.

The Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation and the Sofia City People's Council Delegation will depart for China on September 20.

### 中、朝、越驻保大使设宴  欢送保议会代表团

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    中、朝、越驻保大使设宴
    欢送保议会代表团
    据新华社索非亚18日电　中国、朝鲜、越南三国驻保加利亚大使朱其文、金学仁和黄文利9月18日设午宴欢送即将到中、朝、越三国进行友好访问的保加利亚国民议会代表团全体人员。代表团将由保共中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席、国民议会议员契尔文科夫率领。
    中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文在宴会上代表中、朝、越三国驻保加利亚使馆讲了话。他表示衷心欢迎代表团到这三个国家进行友好访问。
    保加利亚议会代表团和索非亚市人民会议代表团将于9月20日启程到中国。
