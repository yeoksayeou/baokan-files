Author: Tan Renrong

Eggs and Stones
		Tan Renrong
		Premier's statement is so loud and clear,
		Warning the U.S. imperialists to consider the consequences,
		Eggs stubbornly striking against stones,
		Capitalism is destined to be buried.

### 鸡蛋和石头
谈仁荣
1958-09-08
第7版()
专栏：

    鸡蛋和石头
    谈仁荣
    总理声明多响亮，
    警告美帝算算账，
    鸡蛋硬往石头碰，
    资本主义定埋葬。
