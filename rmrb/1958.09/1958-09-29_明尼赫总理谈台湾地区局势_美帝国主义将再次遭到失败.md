Title: Premier Münnich Talks About the Situation in the Taiwan Area; U.S. Imperialism Will Be Defeated Again
Author:
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 26th: Premier Münnich of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, in his report to the final session of the current National Assembly on the 26th, pointed out that U.S. imperialism is accustomed to creating international tensions. After carrying out aggression in the Middle East, they then engaged in military provocations in the Taiwan Strait area. However, this time they encountered such a great power as China. He said that the Soviet Union and other socialist countries unanimously support China, and there is no doubt that U.S. imperialism will be defeated again.

### 明尼赫总理谈台湾地区局势  美帝国主义将再次遭到失败

1958-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    明尼赫总理谈台湾地区局势
    美帝国主义将再次遭到失败
    据新华社布达佩斯26日电  匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫26日在本届国民议会最后一次会议上作报告时指出，美帝国主义者惯于制造国际紧张局势，他们在中东进行了侵略以后，接着又在台湾海峡地区进行军事挑衅。但是，他们这次遇到的是中国这样一个巨大的力量。他说，苏联和其他社会主义国家都一致支持中国，毫无疑问，美帝国主义将再次遭到失败。
