Title: Orders to Steel Marshal
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 8

Orders to Steel Marshal
Worker of State-owned Factory 541, Li Xue'ao
    Quick! Quick! Quick!
    Not delaying a second,
    Not delaying a minute,
    Steel Marshal! Please don your helmet and armor,
    Quickly mount your horse and go into battle!
    Six hundred million people are moving to clear the way,
    It all depends on your might!
    Quick! Quick! Quick!
    Not delaying a second,
    Not delaying a minute,
    Steel Marshal! Please raise your long sword high,
    Gallop across the land of China,
    And fiercely chop down any difficulties you encounter!
    You must be the hero of heroes!
    Quick! Quick! Quick!
    Not delaying a second,
    Not delaying a minute,
    Steel Marshal! Please quickly execute the orders of the class!

专栏：

　　给钢元帅的命令
　　国营五四一厂工人　李学鳌

    不迟延一秒，
    不迟延一分，

    不迟延一秒，
    不迟延一分，

    在中国的大地上驰骋，
    碰到困难就猛烈砍掉！
    你要当英雄中的英雄！
    快！快！快！
    不迟延一秒，
    不迟延一分，
    钢元帅！请你快执行阶级的命令！
