Title: General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada Says: The U.S. Commits Arson but Demands China "Cease Fire"; The Invader's Method is Truly Shameless
Author:
Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Ottawa Dispatch: Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, published an article in the "Canadian Tribune" strongly condemning the United States' war-provoking and adventurist policy in the Taiwan Strait against China.

The author pointed out that the main goal of the U.S. imperialists in the Taiwan Strait area is to maintain U.S. naval and army control over the Taiwan Strait. Therefore, their repeated demands that the People's Republic of China abandon the use of force are hypocritical and shameless. Buck pointed out that the use of force was caused precisely by the United States' aggression against China.

Buck sternly refuted Eisenhower's "reason" for the United States' aggression against China, which is that if the United States loses control over the Taiwan Strait, it will lose control over the entire Western Pacific. He pointed out that the Eisenhower administration's untenable "reason" only proves that the United States is trying to establish itself as the imperialist police of the entire Pacific region and the Asian continent on the other side of the Pacific coast, to prevent the people of various countries from establishing forms of government not accepted by the interests of U.S. monopoly capital.

### 加拿大劳工进步党总书记说  美国放火倒要中国“停火”  侵略者这种手段实在无耻

1958-09-25
第3版()
专栏：

    加拿大劳工进步党总书记说
    美国放火倒要中国“停火”
    侵略者这种手段实在无耻
    据新华社24日讯  渥太华消息：加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克在“加拿大论坛报”上发表文章，强烈谴责美国在台湾海峡对中国的战争挑衅冒险政策。
    作者指出，美国帝国主义者在台湾海峡地区的主要目标是要保持美国海军和陆军对台湾海峡的控制。因此他们一再要求人民中国政府放弃使用武力乃是虚伪的无耻手段。布克指出，正是由于美国对中国进行侵略，才引起了使用武力。
    布克严正驳斥了艾森豪威尔为美国对中国的侵略所制造的“理由”，就是，如果美国失去对台湾海峡的控制，它就会失去对整个西太平洋的控制。他指出，艾森豪威尔政府这种经不起一驳的“理由”，只不过是证明了美国正在设法确立自己作为整个太平洋地区和太平洋岸那边的亚洲大陆上的帝国主义警察的地位，来阻止各国人民建立不为美国垄断资本利益所接受的政府形式。
