Title: Korean Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Korean Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, the Korean Chairman, and Vice Minister of Light Industry, An Han-sik, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 28th to attend the first meeting of the Sino-Korean Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee. Korean committee member, Shen Jinglong, Director of the First Bureau of the Korean State Technology Committee, and Secretary Park Kyung-dong arrived earlier on September 21.

### 朝鲜科技合作代表团到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜科技合作代表团到京
    新华社28日讯  前来我国出席中朝科技合作委员会第一届会议的朝鲜科技合作代表团团长、朝方主席、轻工业省副相安汗式于28日上午抵达北京。朝方委员、朝鲜国家技术委员会第一局局长沈敬龙和秘书朴景东已于9月21日先期到达。
