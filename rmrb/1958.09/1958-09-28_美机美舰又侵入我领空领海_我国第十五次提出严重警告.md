Title: U.S. Planes and Warships Again Invade Our Airspace and Territorial Waters; Our Country Issues 15th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: On September 27th, fourteen U.S. fighter planes successively intruded into the airspace over the territorial waters of the Xiamen area of Fujian Province, while three U.S. warships also intruded into the territorial waters of the Fujian area of our country. A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare: The Chinese government issues its fifteenth serious warning against this grave military provocation by the United States.

### 美机美舰又侵入我领空领海  我国第十五次提出严重警告

1958-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    美机美舰又侵入我领空领海
    我国第十五次提出严重警告
    新华社27日讯  9月27日美国战斗飞机十四架先后侵入我国福建厦门地区的领海上空，与此同时，美国军舰三艘亦侵入我国福建地区的领海，我外交部发言人奉命声明：对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第十五次提出严重警告。
