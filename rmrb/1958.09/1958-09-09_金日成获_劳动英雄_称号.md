Title: Kim Il-sung Awarded the Title of "Labor Hero"
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th - On the eve of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea issued a decree conferring the title of "Labor Hero" on Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet, and awarding him the Order of the National Flag, First Class, and the Gold Star Medal.

The decree pointed out that Comrade Kim Il-sung led the Korean people to victory in the struggle for the freedom, independence, and peaceful reunification of the fatherland. After the war, he brilliantly led the people in rapidly restoring the people's economy, which had been severely damaged, and carrying out socialist construction in the northern part of the Republic. He has made outstanding contributions in leading the leap forward in all sectors of the people's economy and rapidly improving the people's living standards.

The Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea also issued another decree commending other leaders of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Republic's government, and social organizations who have made outstanding contributions to the peaceful reunification of the fatherland and the socialist construction in the northern part of the Republic, and awarding them medals. Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Kim Il, Hong Myung-hui, Jung Il-yong, Nam Il, Jung Joon-taek, and Lee Joo-yeon, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jung-ae, Park Kim-chul, Kim Chang-man, Standing Committee members Lim Hae, Lee Hyo-soon, Kim Kwang-hyup, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Party Hong Ki-hwang, and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party Kim Dal-hyun were all awarded the Order of the National Flag, First Class.

Minister of Light Industry Moon Man-wook was awarded the title of "Labor Hero" and the Order of the National Flag, First Class, for his special contributions to the construction of light industry.

### 金日成获“劳动英雄”称号

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　金日成获“劳动英雄”称号
    新华社平壤8日电　朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国创建十周年纪念日的前夕发布政令，授予内阁首相金日成同志以“劳动英雄”的称号，并授予一级国旗勋章和金星奖章。
    政令指出，金日成同志领导朝鲜人民在为祖国的自由独立和和平统一的斗争中走向胜利，在战后他英明地领导人民迅速地恢复了曾遭到惨重破坏的人民经济，进行共和国北部的社会主义建设。在领导一切人民经济部门的飞跃发展和迅速提高人民生活的事业中建立了卓越的功勋。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会另外还发布政令表扬在为祖国的和平统一和共和国北部的社会主义建设中建立了卓越功勋的朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府和社会团体的其他领导人，并授予他们勋章。朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健、内阁副首相金一、洪命熹、郑一龙、南日、郑准泽、李周渊，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满，常务委员林海、李孝淳、金光侠，朝鲜民主党中央委员会委员长洪箕璜，朝鲜天道教青友党中央委员会委员长金达铉都荣获一级国旗勋章。
    朝鲜轻工业相文万郁因他在建设轻工业方面的特殊功勋获得“劳动英雄”称号和一级国旗勋章。
