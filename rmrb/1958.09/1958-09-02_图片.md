Title: Gu Yuan
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 8

　　In previous years, girls didn't go up the mountains,
  　Now girls don't leave the mountains,
　　Girls on the mountain look forward to going down,
  　Girls at the foot of the mountain marry up the mountain.
　　Gu Yuan's Painting
　　Wang Shuhua (Fourteen years old, fourth grade)
