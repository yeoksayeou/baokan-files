Title: What excuse do the invaders have to remain in Lebanon? Shehab wants the United States to begin withdrawing troops

Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, September 2nd: According to a TASS report from Beirut on September 2nd: According to the "Telegraph," Lebanon's newly elected President Shehab recently met with the U.S. Ambassador to Lebanon, McClintock, and the commander of the U.S. invading forces in Lebanon, Adams, and requested that U.S. troops begin withdrawing from Lebanon.

### 侵略者还有什么借口留在黎巴嫩  谢哈布要美国开始撤军

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    侵略者还有什么借口留在黎巴嫩
　　谢哈布要美国开始撤军
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特2日讯：据“电讯报”报道，黎巴嫩新当选的总统谢哈布最近接见了美国驻黎巴嫩大使麦克林托克和侵黎美军司令亚当斯，要求美军开始从黎巴嫩撤退。
