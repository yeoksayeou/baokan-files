Title: NPC Standing Committee Approves the Chinese Government's Declaration on Territorial Seas
Author:
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress decided at its 100th meeting today to approve the "Declaration of the Government of the People's Republic of China on Territorial Seas."

### 我人大常委会批准我政府关于领海的声明

1958-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我人大常委会批准我政府关于领海的声明
　　新华社4日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在本日举行的第一百次会议上决定批准“中华人民共和国政府关于领海的声明”。
