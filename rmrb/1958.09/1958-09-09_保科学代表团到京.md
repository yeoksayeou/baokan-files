Title: Bulgarian Scientific Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th — A three-member scientific delegation from the Ministry of Education and Culture of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, led by Gero Boychev Grozev, First Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

### 保科学代表团到京

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    保科学代表团到京
    据新华社8日讯　保加利亚人民共和国教育和文化部科学工作者代表团一行三人，在保加利亚人民共和国教育和文化部第一副部长盖罗·包依切夫·格罗再夫率领下，今天下午到达北京。
