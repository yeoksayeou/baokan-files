Title: Hungary to Celebrate Three Major Events
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 17th: In order to celebrate the founding of the Hungarian Communist Party, the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, and the 40th anniversary of the Hungarian bourgeois democratic revolution of October 1918, a preparatory committee of 52 people, including representatives from all walks of life, has been formed upon the suggestion of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government.

    The chairman of the committee is Munnich Ferenc, Prime Minister of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government.

### 匈牙利将庆祝三件大事

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利将庆祝三件大事
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　为了庆祝匈牙利共产党成立、匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立和匈牙利1918年十月资产阶级民主革命四十周年，在匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会和匈牙利工农革命政府的建议下组成了包括各界代表五十二人的筹备委员会。
    委员会主席是匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫。
