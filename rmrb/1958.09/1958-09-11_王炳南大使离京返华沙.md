Title: Ambassador Wang Bingnan Leaves Beijing to Return to Warsaw
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Our ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, who was summoned to Beijing five days ago, left Beijing today to return to his post in Warsaw.

### 王炳南大使离京返华沙

1958-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    王炳南大使离京返华沙
    新华社10日讯　我国驻波兰大使王炳南五日前奉召来京，今日离京返回华沙任所。
