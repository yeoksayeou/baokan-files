Title: Algerian Prime Minister Abbas Says China's Recognition is a Great Encouragement
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 23rd. Algerian Prime Minister Abbas said this morning to Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic: "We believe that the recognition of the provisional government by the 600 million Chinese people is a great encouragement to our country, and we believe that this is just the beginning of friendly cooperation between the two countries."

This morning, Ambassador Chen Jiakang visited Prime Minister Abbas and presented him with the telegrams from Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai congratulating the establishment of the Algerian Republic. Ambassador Chen Jiakang also presented Algerian Foreign Minister Debaghine, who was present at the time, with the telegram of Chinese Foreign Minister Chen Yi announcing China's official recognition of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria.

Also present on the Algerian side were Minister of Social Affairs Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister of North African Affairs Abdelhamid Mehri, and Minister of Information Mohamed Yazid. On the Chinese side was Zhang Yue, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the United Arab Republic.

During the conversation, Ambassador Chen Jiakang also congratulated the establishment of the Algerian Provisional Government and expressed his belief that victory belonged to the Algerian people and the Arab people. Chen Jiakang also stated that the Chinese people support the desire of all Arab people to strive for national independence.

### 阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯说  中国的承认是巨大鼓舞

1958-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯说
    中国的承认是巨大鼓舞
    新华社开罗23日电　阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯今天早晨对中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康说：“我们认为，六亿中国人民承认临时政府，是对我国极大的鼓舞，我们相信这只是两国之间友好合作关系的一个开端。”
    陈家康大使今天早晨访问了阿巴斯总理，并且把毛泽东主席和周恩来总理祝贺阿尔及利亚共和国成立的电报递交给他。陈家康大使还把中国外交部长陈毅的宣布中国正式承认阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的电报递交给当时在场的阿尔及利亚外交部长德巴金。
    同时在场的，阿尔及利亚方面有社会事务部长优素福·本·赫达、北非事务部长阿卜杜勒·哈米德·迈赫里和宣传部长穆罕默德·雅齐德。中国方面有中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使馆参赞张越。
    陈家康大使在交谈中还祝贺阿尔及利亚临时政府的成立并且表示相信，胜利是属于阿尔及利亚人民和阿拉伯人民的。陈家康还表示中国人民支持一切阿拉伯人民争取民族独立的愿望。
