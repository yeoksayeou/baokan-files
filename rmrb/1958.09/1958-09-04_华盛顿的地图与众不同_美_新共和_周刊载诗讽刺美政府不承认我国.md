Title: Washington's Maps Are Different: US "New Republic" Magazine Publishes Poem Satirizing US Government's Non-Recognition of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

Editor's Note: The September 1st issue of "The New Republic" published a poem by Sagittarius, satirizing the US government's disregard for the existence of our country. It is now translated and published below:

China is an Island

“The State Department intends to continue non-recognition of Communist China”

——Memorandum to US Embassies, August 12th.

Here is a map, Taiwan is discernible,
A coastal island of China.
This area is dark in color,
Indicating the mainland has sunk.
The former territory of China, vast and unsearchable.
Remember the old map, China had a coastline,
Fujian and Taiwan separated by a strait.
Obsolete projection,
Now to be revised,
Tokyo to Manchuria, landmarks without a trace. (Note)
The outer periphery of Asia, still discernible,
South Korea and Vietnam clearly distinguished at a glance.
Political erosion fierce,
China sinks to the seabed,
Asia's water boundary, from now on, extends.
Waves surging and rolling,
Directly to the Mongolian border,
Four million square miles of land no longer seen.
Now, with the naked eye,
Shanghai is nowhere to be found,
Let alone Beijing, Hankou, Guangzhou, and Nanjing.
Taiwan is located here, originally part of China,
At a time when China was reduced to nothing.
Strict non-recognition,
Changes the situation,
The coast having disappeared, where does the island belong?
Here is supposed to be China, located next to Taiwan,
If not transformed into a boundless chasm.
Modern cartography,
Changes geography, open the map to see, mainland China is dead.

Note: Tokyo refers to the Gulf of Tonkin in Vietnam.

### 华盛顿的地图与众不同  美“新共和”周刊载诗讽刺美政府不承认我国

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　华盛顿的地图与众不同
　　美“新共和”周刊载诗讽刺美政府不承认我国
    编者按：9月1日的“新共和”周刊刊载萨季特里厄斯的一首诗，讽刺美国政府无视我国的存在。现译载于下：
    中国是一岛
　　“国务院打算继续不承认共产党中国”
    ——8月12日致美国各使馆备忘录。
　　此处有地图，台湾可辨认，
　　本是中国沿海一岛屿。
    这一地区颜色深，
    表明本土已沉沦。
　　昔日中国域，茫茫不可寻。
　　记得旧地图，中国有海滨，
    福建台湾相隔一海峡。
    过时投影图，
    现在要修正，
　　东京到满洲，界标无踪影。（注）
　　亚洲外围地，依然可辨认，
    南韩越南一眼看分明。
    政治浸蚀烈，
    中国海底沉，
　　亚洲水界线，从此有展伸。
　　海浪汹涌卷，
　　直达蒙古边，
    四百万里土地不复见。
    现在有肉眼，
    上海无处寻，
　　遑论北京、汉口、广州和南京。
　　台湾位于此，原划中国境，
    时在中国化为乌有前。
    严格不承认，
    情况遂不同，
　　海岸既消失，岛屿何所从。
　　此处本中国，位于台湾旁，
    若未化为鸿沟茫无际。
    现代制图术，
    改变地理学，打开地图看，大陆中国亡。
    注：东京是指越南东京湾
