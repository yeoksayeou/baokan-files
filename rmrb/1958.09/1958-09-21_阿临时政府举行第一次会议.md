Title: Algerian Provisional Government Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-09-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 19th: The Algerian Provisional Government held its first meeting this afternoon at its temporary government headquarters in Cairo.

　　It is understood that Ben Bella and the four ministers of state were previously held in French prisons, and three secretaries of state are now in Algeria. The other cabinet ministers are: Krim Belkacem and Mahmoud Cherif, who are currently in Tunisia, and Debaghine and Boussouf, who are currently in Morocco.

　　The Algerian government has already notified all foreign envoys stationed in Cairo about the establishment of the government.

### 阿临时政府举行第一次会议

1958-09-21
第3版()
专栏：

    阿临时政府举行第一次会议
    新华社开罗19日电　阿尔及利亚临时政府今天下午在开罗的政府临时所在地召开了第一次会议。
    据悉，贝拉和四个国务部长过去被关在法国监牢里，三个国务秘书现在在阿尔及利亚。其他内阁部长是：克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆和马哈茂德·谢里现在在突尼斯，德巴金和布苏夫现在在摩洛哥。
    阿尔及利亚政府已经把它成立政府这件事情通知驻开罗的所有外国使节。
