　　Gas Alarm

　　China's first gas alarm was born in the Shanxi Provincial State-Operated Yinying Coal Mine. With this instrument, coal mines will be able to fundamentally eliminate gas explosions in the future.

　　Production in coal mines is often threatened by gas, and in severe cases, gas explosions can occur. Currently, coal mines have taken many measures, such as ventilation or sending gas inspectors to check everywhere, but these still cannot completely solve the problem. With a gas alarm, gas explosions can be fundamentally eliminated. This instrument can replace manual inspection and continuously and actively monitor the enemy – the dynamics of gas, and ventilation conditions. When the gas content reaches a certain level, it will automatically sound an alarm, enabling production operators to handle and prevent it in a timely manner.

### 瓦斯警报器

1958-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　瓦斯警报器
    我国第一部瓦斯警报器在山西省地方国营荫营煤矿诞生了。有了这种仪器，今后的煤矿将可以从根本上杜绝瓦斯爆炸。
    在煤矿中生产，经常会受到瓦斯的威胁，严重的地方甚至会引起瓦斯爆炸。现在煤矿里采取了很多措施，如通风，或者派瓦斯检查员到处检查，但是仍然不能彻底解决。有了瓦斯警报器，就可以从根本上杜绝瓦斯爆炸。这种仪器可以代替人工检查，并且不间断的主动的监视着敌人——瓦斯的动态，和通风情况，在瓦斯含量达到一定程度时，它就会自动发出警报，使生产操作人员及时进行处理和防范。
