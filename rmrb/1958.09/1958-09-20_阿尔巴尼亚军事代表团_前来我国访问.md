Title: Albanian Military Delegation Arrives in Our Country for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 19th - An Albanian military delegation to China, at the invitation of Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, departed for China on the morning of the 19th. The head of the delegation is Comrade Beqir Balluku, Member of the Political Bureau of the Albanian Labour Party Central Committee, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defence.

On the eve of the delegation's departure, Luo Shigao, China's ambassador to Albania, hosted a special banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团  前来我国访问

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团
    前来我国访问
    新华社地拉那19日电　阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团应中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅的邀请，在19日上午启程前往中国。代表团团长是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库上将。
    在代表团临行前夕，我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高特设宴为代表团饯行。
