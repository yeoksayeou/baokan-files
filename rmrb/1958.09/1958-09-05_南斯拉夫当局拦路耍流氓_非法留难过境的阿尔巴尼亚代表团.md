Title: Yugoslav Authorities Act as Thugs, Illegally Detaining Albanian Delegation in Transit
Author:
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 4th: The Albanian News Agency reported on September 3rd that the Albanian Chargé d'Affaires in Belgrade lodged a protest with the Yugoslav State Secretariat for Foreign Affairs on August 30th regarding the illegal detention by Yugoslav authorities of the Albanian delegation en route through Yugoslavia to Damascus to attend the exposition. He demanded assurances that similar incidents would not occur again.

On August 22nd, the Albanian delegation personnel bound for Damascus flew from Tirana to Belgrade airport. As they disembarked to transfer to a plane about to depart for Cairo, Yugoslav authorities unreasonably detained the Albanian delegation under the pretext that they must first undergo inspection by the Yugoslav People's Militia. It is understood that inspection measures are not applicable to passengers transiting by air, especially to passengers holding diplomatic passports like the Albanian delegation. However, the Yugoslav authorities, in violation of conventions and international rules, opened the Albanian delegation's luggage and forcibly seized a box containing silver handicrafts prepared for exhibition at the Damascus exposition. At the same time, the Yugoslav authorities also prevented the Third Secretary of the Albanian Embassy in Belgrade from meeting with the delegation.

Only after strong representations by representatives of the Albanian Embassy did the Yugoslav authorities allow the Albanian delegation to continue their journey to Damascus.

### 南斯拉夫当局拦路耍流氓  非法留难过境的阿尔巴尼亚代表团

1958-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫当局拦路耍流氓
　　非法留难过境的阿尔巴尼亚代表团
    新华社地拉那4日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社3日报道：阿尔巴尼亚驻贝尔格莱德临时代办8月30日就南斯拉夫机关非法留难路过南斯拉夫前往大马士革参加博览会的阿尔巴尼亚代表团一事，向南斯拉夫外交国务秘书处提出了抗议，要求保证不再发生类似的事情。
    8月22日，前往大马士革的阿尔巴尼亚代表团人员乘搭一架飞机从地拉那飞到贝尔格莱德机场，当他们走下飞机准备换搭一架马上就要飞往开罗的飞机时，南斯拉夫机关以他们首先必须经过南斯拉夫民警检查为借口来无理留难阿尔巴尼亚代表团。大家知道，检查措施对于乘飞机过境的旅客是不适用的，特别是对于像阿尔巴尼亚代表团这样持有外交护照的旅客。但是，南斯拉夫机关竟违反惯例和国际规则，打开了阿尔巴尼亚代表团的箱子，并且强行扣下了一只装有准备在大马士革博览会上展出的银制手工业品的箱子。同时，南斯拉夫机关还不让阿尔巴尼亚驻贝尔格莱德公使馆的三等秘书会见代表团。
    只是在阿尔巴尼亚公使馆的代表大力交涉后，南斯拉夫机关才让阿尔巴尼亚代表团人员继续他们前往大马士革的旅行。
