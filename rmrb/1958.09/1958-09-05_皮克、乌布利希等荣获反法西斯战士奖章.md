Title: Pieck, Ulbricht, and Others Awarded Medals for Anti-Fascist Fighters
Author:
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 3rd: The government of the German Democratic Republic awarded the "Medal for Anti-Fascist Fighters 1933-1945" to two hundred anti-fascist fighters on September 3rd. Leaders of the East German worker-peasant regime Wilhelm Pieck, Walter Ulbricht, and Otto Grotewohl, as well as members and alternate members of the Politburo of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, were awarded this medal.
    The award ceremony, presided over by Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau, was held in the Council of Ministers building on the afternoon of the 3rd.

### 皮克、乌布利希等荣获反法西斯战士奖章

1958-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　皮克、乌布利希等荣获反法西斯战士奖章
    据新华社柏林3日电　德意志民主共和国政府3日以“1933—1945年间反法西斯战士奖章”授予二百名反法西斯战士。民主德国工农政权领导人皮克、乌布利希和格罗提渥，德国统一社会党政治局委员和候补委员们，都荣获了这种奖章。
    由代理总理海因里希·劳主持的颁奖仪式，3日下午在部长会议大厦举行。
