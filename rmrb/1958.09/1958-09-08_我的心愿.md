Title: My Wish
Author: Zhang Zuncheng
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 7

　　Sacred territorial waters are not to be infringed upon!
　　The voice of six hundred million people is as firm and unyielding as steel.
　　Recover Jinmen and Mazu, liberate Taiwan!
　　The will of six hundred million people,
　　The call of the motherland.
　　Tighten your military uniform, grip your gun tightly,
　　Soldiers face the sea and swear:
　　Blood can flow, heads can be severed,
　　But we will not rest until Taiwan is liberated!

### 我的心愿
张遵程
1958-09-08
第7版()
专栏：

　　我的心愿
    张遵程神
　　圣领海不容侵犯！
　　六亿人民的声音铁硬钢坚。
　　收复金门、马祖解放台湾！
　　六亿人民的意志，
　　祖国的召唤。
　　束好戎装握紧枪，
　　战士面对大海立誓言：
　　血可流头可断，
　　不解放台湾心不甘！
