Title: Chinese Student Delegation Attends Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chinese student delegation led by Hu Qili, chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, attended the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students today.

    The Chinese student delegation consists of five representatives and eleven observers. These representatives and observers come from universities and colleges in cities and autonomous regions such as Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Xi'an, Xinjiang, and Inner Mongolia in our country.

### 中国学生代表团出席代表大会

1958-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    中国学生代表团出席代表大会
    新华社4日讯　由中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立率领的中国学生代表团今天出席了国际学联第五届代表大会。
    中国学生代表团由代表五人和观察员十一人组成。这些代表和观察员来自我国的北京、上海、天津、南京、武汉、广州、成都、西安、新疆、内蒙古等市和自治区的大学和学院。
