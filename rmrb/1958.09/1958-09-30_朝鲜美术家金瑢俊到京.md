Title: Korean Artist Kim Yong-jun Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. Korean artist Kim Yong-jun arrived in Beijing on September 28th for a visit to China under the 1958-1959 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 朝鲜美术家金瑢俊到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜美术家金瑢俊到京
    新华社28日讯  根据中朝文化合作协定1958—1959年执行计划来我国访问的朝鲜美术家金瑢俊，28日到达北京。
