Title: My Ambassador to Iraq Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1958-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, September 22nd: Chen Zhifang, China's ambassador to the Republic of Iraq, presented his credentials to the Supreme Power Committee of the Republic of Iraq today. Ambassador Chen Zhifang was received by Najib al-Ruba'i, Chairman of the Supreme Power Committee of the Republic of Iraq, and the committee members.

    In his speech, Ambassador Chen Zhifang said that the Iraqi people had carried out a heroic national revolutionary struggle and won a great victory, overthrowing the reactionary rule of the imperialist agent Faisal and establishing an independent and free republic. The Chinese people express their heartfelt congratulations and admiration to the Iraqi people. The Chinese government and people are willing to continue to strengthen friendly cooperation between China and Iraq on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, in the spirit of the Bandung Conference. The Chinese people will do their utmost to support the Iraqi government and people in their heroic struggle to defend national independence and sovereignty and prevent imperialist aggression. Ambassador Chen Zhifang wished the Iraqi people continued victories in their struggle to consolidate national independence and build a democratic and happy life.

    In his reply, Chairman Ruba'i said that the People's Republic of China and Iraq are two countries with a long history of culture. They had established friendly relations in the past, and now they have restored this friendly relationship. Ruba'i assured that the friendship between the two countries will develop with time. Ruba'i concluded by saying, "We will cooperate in social and economic fields."

    Ambassador Chen Zhifang had a friendly conversation with the Chairman and members of the Supreme Power Committee and conveyed the respect and best wishes of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese people. Chairman Ruba'i asked Ambassador Chen Zhifang to convey the same respect to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese people. Ambassador Chen Zhifang presented gifts from Chairman Mao Zedong to the Supreme Power Committee of Iraq.

### 我驻伊拉克大使递交国书

1958-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻伊拉克大使递交国书
    据新华社巴格达22日电　中国驻伊拉克共和国大使陈志方今天向伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会递交了国书。陈志方大使受到伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·鲁巴伊和委员们的接见。
    陈志方大使在致词中说，伊拉克人民曾经进行了英勇的民族革命斗争，并且赢得了伟大的胜利，推翻了帝国主义代理人费萨尔的反动统治，建立了独立和自由的共和国。中国人民对伊拉克人民表示衷心的祝贺和钦佩。中国政府和人民愿意根据万隆会议的精神在和平共处五项原则的基础上，继续努力加强中国和伊拉克两国之间的友好合作。中国人民将尽最大的力量来支持伊拉克政府和人民为了保卫民族独立和主权、防止帝国主义侵略而进行的英勇斗争。陈志方大使预祝伊拉克人民在巩固民族独立和建设民主幸福生活的斗争中继续取得胜利。
    鲁巴伊主席在致答辞中说，中华人民共和国和伊拉克是两个文化悠久的国家，它们过去曾经建立过友好关系，现在恢复了这种友好关系。鲁巴伊保证两国的友谊将随着时间而发展。鲁巴伊最后说，“我们将在社会和经济方面进行合作。”
    陈志方大使同最高权力委员会主席和委员们进行了友好会谈，并且转达了毛泽东主席和中国人民的敬意和祝愿。鲁巴伊主席要求陈志方大使向毛泽东主席和中国人民转达同样的敬意。陈志方大使向伊拉克最高权力委员会转交了毛泽东主席赠送的礼品。
