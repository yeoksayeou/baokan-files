Title: Iraqi People's Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Iraqi Mechanical Repair Workers' Union Head Saleh Arrives on Same Flight

Author:
Date: 1958-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The Iraqi People's Friendship Delegation, led by Mahmoud, Chairman of the Iraqi National Bar Association, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou today. Saleh, the head of the Iraqi Mechanical Repair Workers' Union, who was invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit China, also arrived in Beijing on the same flight.

This Friendship Delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. The delegation consists of fifteen people, including former ministers, well-known legal professionals, professors, peace activists, trade union workers, journalists, and business people.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President, and heads of relevant organizations. Young women of the capital presented the guests with flowers.

### 伊人民友好访华团到京  伊机械修理工会负责人萨莱赫也同机到达

1958-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    伊人民友好访华团到京
    伊机械修理工会负责人萨莱赫也同机到达
    新华社22日讯　由伊拉克全国律师协会主席马哈茂德率领的伊拉克人民友好访华团，今天由广州乘飞机到达北京。应中华全国总工会邀请来华访问的伊拉克机械修理工会负责人萨莱赫也同机到京。
    这个友好访华团是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。访华团由十五人组成，其中有前部长，知名的法律工作者、教授、和平人士，工会工作者、新闻工作者和商界人士。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长周鲠生，以及各有关团体的负责人。首都的女青年向客人们献了鲜花。
