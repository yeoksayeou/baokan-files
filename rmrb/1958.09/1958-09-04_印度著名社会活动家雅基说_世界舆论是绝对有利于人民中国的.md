Title: Famous Indian Social Activist Yaqi Says World Opinion Is Absolutely in Favor of People's China
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 2nd - Prominent Indian social figure, author of the booklet "The True Face of American Monopoly Democracy," and Indian National Congress MP, Shri Nidra Yaqi, issued a statement on the 1st, strongly condemning the new adventurism of American imperialism in the Far East.

Yaqi pointed out that the adventurist behavior of American imperialists, like in West Asia, is doomed to failure. He added, "World opinion is absolutely in favor of People's China," and the United States "should heed the voice of justice, ordinary common sense, and the legitimate status of People's China in the United Nations General Assembly," and not obstruct China from unifying its own territory.

### 印度著名社会活动家雅基说  世界舆论是绝对有利于人民中国的

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印度著名社会活动家雅基说
　　世界舆论是绝对有利于人民中国的
    据新华社新德里2日电　印度著名社会人士、“美国垄断民主的真面目”的小册子的作者、印度国会国大党议员希尔·尼德拉·雅基在1日发表声明，严厉地谴责了美帝国主义在远东的新的冒险行为。
    雅基指出，美帝国主义者的冒险行为正像在西亚那样，是注定要失败的。他又说，“世界舆论是绝对有利于人民中国的”，美国“应该注意到正义的呼声、普通的常识和人民中国在联合国大会的合法地位”，不要阻挠中国统一它自己的领土。
