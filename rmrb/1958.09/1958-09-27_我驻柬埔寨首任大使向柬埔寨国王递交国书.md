Title: First Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Presents Credentials to Cambodian King
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, September 26th: Wang Youping, the first Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, presented his credentials to Cambodian King Norodom Suramarit on the 25th.

　　The Cambodian and Chinese flags were hung in the Royal Palace. Ambassador Wang Youping reviewed the royal guard of honor lined up outside the Royal Palace amidst the playing of the national anthems of both countries. Cambodian Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mau Say, introduced Ambassador Wang Youping to the King of Cambodia.

　　In his congratulatory remarks, Ambassador Wang Youping pointed out that there is a traditional and deep friendship between Cambodia and China, and this friendship has been strengthened by the mutual visits of Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Sihanouk, as well as Prince Sihanouk's recent visit to China. He added that the development of this friendly relationship proves the vitality of Panchsheel and the Bandung spirit. The Ambassador expressed his willingness to do his best through his position to develop this friendship.

　　In his reply, the King of Cambodia said that the arrival of the Ambassador opened a new stage in the relations between the two countries. The King emphasized that by recognizing China, "Cambodia is willing to show its firm determination to maintain absolute neutrality." He said, "Cambodia hopes to express its sincerity and true friendship to the great Chinese people through this action." After expressing his hope that the cooperative efforts of the two countries would help maintain peace and security in Asia, the King extended his blessings to Chairman Mao Zedong and congratulated the Chinese people on their prosperity.

　　After the presentation of credentials ceremony, the King and the Ambassador had a sincere conversation.

### 我驻柬埔寨首任大使向柬埔寨国王递交国书

1958-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻柬埔寨首任大使向柬埔寨国王递交国书
    新华社金边26日电  中国驻柬埔寨首任大使王幼平25日向柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特递交国书。
    在王宫里悬挂着柬中两国国旗，王幼平大使在两国国歌声中检阅了排列在王宫外的王室仪仗队。柬埔寨代理外交大臣毛赛把王幼平大使介绍给柬埔寨国王。
    王幼平大使在致颂词中指出，柬埔寨和中国之间存在着传统的深厚友谊，这种友谊由于周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王的互相访问以及西哈努克亲王最近访问中国而加强。他又说，这种友好关系的发展证明了潘查希拉和万隆精神的生命力。大使表示愿意尽力通过他的职务来发展这种友谊，
    柬埔寨国王在答词中说，大使的到来为两国的关系开辟了新的阶段。国王强调指出，通过对中国的承认，“柬埔寨愿意表明保持绝对中立的坚定决心”。他说，“柬埔寨希望通过这个行动向伟大的中国人民表示它的诚意和真正的友谊。”国王在希望两国合作的努力将有助于维护亚洲的和平和安全之后，向毛泽东主席表示祝福和祝贺中国人民繁荣昌盛。
    递交国书仪式完毕以后，国王和大使进行了诚挚的交谈。
