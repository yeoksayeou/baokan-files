Title: Guangzhou and Nanjing Universities Extend Warm Greetings to Xiamen University Faculty and Students
Author:
Date: 1958-09-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The crime of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops entrenched in Jinmen, who frantically shelled Xiamen University, has aroused boundless anger among faculty and students in universities in Guangzhou, Nanjing, and other places. They have written letters to Xiamen University expressing their warm greetings and firmly stating their commitment to supporting the front lines with practical actions and avenging the wounded.

    The letter from all the students of Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou stated: We firmly stand with you, transforming anger into strength, and shattering America's war provocations! The letter from all the students of the South China Institute of Technology stated that they have formed militia units and are conducting military training, ready to respond to the call of the motherland at any time.

    Students at Nanjing University held rallies to denounce the crimes of the US-Chiang clique, vying to sign protest letters. The student union of this university sent a telegram of condolence to the students of Xiamen University.

### 广州南京高等学校亲切慰问厦大师生

1958-09-17
第2版()
专栏：

    广州南京高等学校亲切慰问厦大师生
    新华社15日讯　盘踞金门的蒋贼军疯狂炮击厦门大学的罪行，激起了广州、南京等地高等学校师生的无比愤怒，他们纷纷写信给厦门大学表示亲切的慰问，并坚决表示以实际行动支援前线，为伤者复仇。
    广州市中山大学全体同学写的信里说：我们坚决与你们站在一起，化愤怒为力量，粉碎美国的战争挑衅！华南化工学院全体同学的信上说，他们组成了民兵队伍，正在进行军事训练，时刻准备响应祖国的召唤。
    南京大学的学生纷纷集会声讨美蒋罪行，争先恐后在抗议书上签名，这个学校的学生会发出慰问电，慰问厦门大学的学生。
