Title: Britain Detonates Another Nuclear Bomb
Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 4

    Britain Detonates Another Nuclear Bomb

    Xinhua News Agency, London, September 11th: The British government announced tonight that Britain conducted another nuclear bomb test on Christmas Island in the Pacific today.

    This nuclear explosion took place just as the British government expressed its agreement to the Soviet Union's proposal to hold negotiations on the cessation of nuclear testing in Geneva next month.

### 英国又爆炸核弹

1958-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    英国又爆炸核弹
    据新华社伦敦11日电　英国政府今晚宣布，英国今天在太平洋圣诞岛进行了另一次核弹试验。
    正当英国政府表示同意苏联提出的下月在日内瓦举行关于停止核试验问题的谈判的时候，英国又进行了这次核爆炸。
