Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Two Brazilian Journalists
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd -- Chairman Mao Zedong met with Brazilian journalists Murilo Marroquim and Dutra in northern China on the evening of September 2nd and had a conversation with them for approximately two hours.

### 毛主席接见巴西两记者

1958-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见巴西两记者
    新华社2日讯　毛泽东主席2日晚间在我国北方接见了巴西记者穆列洛·马罗金和杜特列，进行了约两小时的谈话。
