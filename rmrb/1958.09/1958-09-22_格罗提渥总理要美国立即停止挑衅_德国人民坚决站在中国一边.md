Title: Grotewohl Demands the United States Immediately Cease Provocations; The German People Firmly Stand with China

Author:

Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 20th – Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Repubic and Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, recently said at the Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany: As long as the United States does not withdraw from the Taiwan Strait and other coastal islands of China that it occupies, peace in the Far East will be seriously endangered. For the sake of peace, we resolutely demand that the United States government immediately cease its provocations, abandon its occupation of Chinese territory, abandon its behavior that constantly violates the United Nations Charter, and allow the People's Republic of China to obtain the seat it deserves in the United Nations.

Grotewohl said that in the struggle against aggression, the German people firmly stand on the side of the Chinese people, and the people of Germany and China are most closely linked together through a treaty of friendship. Peace-loving people of all countries can trust that Democratic Germany is a loyal friend.

In the report made by Central Politburo member Materrn on behalf of the Politburo, he also severely condemned the military provocations of U.S. imperialism against the People's Republic of China, and expressed that Democratic Germany will firmly stand on the side of the Chinese people, who possess political, moral, and military superiority.

Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, 21st – Premier Grotewohl of Democratic Germany said here on the 20th that the provocative actions of the imperialist war-mongers against the People's Republic of China have brought a new and serious threat to world peace. Peace-loving humanity looks hopefully to China, the Soviet Union, and other socialist countries, which, together with other peace-loving countries, are unanimously fighting back against this intimidation and are determined to take all necessary measures to defend peace.

Grotewohl made these remarks at a reception he held for the distinguished guests and representatives of various countries attending the First Friendship Army Games.

### 格罗提渥总理要美国立即停止挑衅  德国人民坚决站在中国一边

1958-09-22
第3版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理要美国立即停止挑衅
    德国人民坚决站在中国一边
    新华社柏林20日电　德意志民主共和国总理、德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员格罗提渥最近在德国统一社会党第二次中央全会上说：只要美国不从台湾海峡和它所占领的中国其它沿海岛屿撤退，在远东的和平就会遭到严重的危害。为了和平的利益，我们坚决要求美国政府立即停止挑衅，放弃它对中国领土的占领，放弃它的经常违反联合国宪章的行为，而使中华人民共和国取得它在联合国所应得的席位。
    格罗提渥说，在反侵略的斗争中，德国人民坚定地站在中国人民一边，德中两国人民是通过友好条约而最紧密地联系在一起的。爱好和平的各国人民可以信赖民主德国是一个忠实的朋友。
    德国统一社会党中央政治局委员马特恩代表政治局所作的报告中，也严厉地谴责了美帝国主义对中华人民共和国的军事挑衅，并表示民主德国将坚定地站在拥有政治、道义和军事优势的中国人民一边。
    据新华社来比锡21日电　民主德国总理格罗提渥20日在这里说，帝国主义战争贩子对中华人民共和国的挑衅行动，给世界和平带来了新的严重的威胁。爱好和平的人类满怀希望地把目光投向中国、苏联和其他社会主义国家，它们正在同其他爱好和平的国家共同一致地反击这种恫吓，并决心为保卫和平采取一切必要的措施。
    格罗提渥这些话是在他为参加第一届友军运动大会的贵宾和各国代表团代表举行的招待会上说的。
