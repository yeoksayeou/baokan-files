Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Grand Reception to Welcome Student Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a grand reception this evening to warmly welcome student representatives, observers, and conference staff from various countries who have come to our country to participate in the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students. This congress will open in Beijing on the 4th.

The banquet hall of the Beijing Hotel, surrounded by evergreen palms, transformed into a "Palace of Joy" filled with peace and friendship this evening. Representatives and observers from sixty-one countries and regions in Asia, Africa, Latin America, Europe, and Australia sat around tables with renowned Chinese scientists, professors, writers, artists, and young people from all walks of life, laughing and chatting to their hearts' content, and constantly raising their glasses to celebrate world peace, the friendship of people from all countries, and the great unity of students worldwide.

The grand reception began at 7 p.m. As young friends from all over the world entered the banquet hall in a continuous stream, Mayor Peng Zhen, Vice Mayors Wan Li, Feng Jiping, Wu Han, Jia Tingsan, Le Songsheng, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Hu Yaobang, President of the All-China Students' Federation Hu Qili, and others greeted the guests at the entrance of the hall. Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, also attended the reception tonight.

After a burst of warm applause subsided, Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a speech to welcome his guests. He said, "The Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students is being held in Beijing, the capital of our People's Republic of China. All the people of Beijing are very happy about this and extend a warm welcome to you." In his enthusiastic speech, Mayor Peng Zhen also expressed his hope that friends from all countries would feel at home during their stay in Beijing, have a pleasant life, and work smoothly. Mayor Peng Zhen concluded by inviting everyone to raise their glasses together to celebrate world peace and the successful victory of the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students.

President Belyakov of the International Union of Students thanked Mayor Peng Zhen for his warm hospitality on behalf of the student representatives from various countries attending the conference. He said that after several months of preparation, the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students will officially open on September 4th. The delegates attending this conference come from various countries, with different traditions and beliefs, but we are all willing to discuss common issues together. It is hoped that through this discussion, international student cooperation can be further promoted, and more people can be attracted to participate in the struggle to maintain peace, strive for national independence, carry out educational reforms, and improve the living conditions of students. Belyakov said that the Chinese student organization is one of the initiators of the International Union of Students and is its most active supporter. On behalf of the student organizations of various countries participating in the International Union of Students, he expressed his gratitude to the Chinese students.

Finally, Belyakov excitedly told everyone that the heads of the various delegations attending the conference proposed at a meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd that the representatives should participate in a voluntary labor activity during the conference to express their respect for the Chinese people.

Roae El-Kadasi, the head of the Iraqi student delegation, spoke at the meeting. He said that we are very happy to have the opportunity to see the achievements of the great Chinese people with our own eyes during the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students. These achievements have shaken the people of Asia and Africa and changed the course of history. This is beneficial to the world's peace forces. We are proud that your hundreds of millions of heroic people effectively support our liberation cause and our just struggle against US-British imperialism. Your great victories and achievements, and your unwavering stance on our liberation cause, have inspired the fighting spirit and strengthened the confidence of the Iraqi people and the entire Arab nation.

In the middle of the reception, singers and magicians from the capital performed wonderful programs. These performances won rounds of thunderous applause. After each performance, representatives from many countries ran over to present flowers to the performers, shake hands warmly with them, and some representatives even asked the artists to sign autographs as souvenirs.

The reception was immersed in the joy of friendship from beginning to end, and people were tightly embraced by the noble aspirations of friendship, unity, and peace.

### 欢迎各国学生代表  彭真市长举行盛大招待会

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎各国学生代表
　　彭真市长举行盛大招待会
    新华社3日讯　北京市彭真市长在今天晚间举行盛大招待会，热烈欢迎前来我国参加国际学联第五届代表大会的各国学生代表、观察员和大会工作人员。这个大会将于4日在北京开幕。
    环绕着常青棕榈的北京饭店宴会大厅，今天晚间变成了充满和平和友谊的“欢乐之宫”。来自亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、欧洲和澳洲的六十一个国家和地区的代表和观察员，同我国著名的科学家、教授、作家、艺术家和各界青年朋友们围桌而坐，尽情欢笑叙谈，并且不断举杯，用各种语言祝贺世界和平，祝贺各国人民的友谊，祝贺全世界学生的大团结。
    盛大的招待会是在晚间七时开始的。当来自世界各地的青年朋友们鱼贯地步入宴会大厅的时候，彭真市长和副市长万里、冯基平、吴晗、贾庭三、乐松生，共青团中央书记处第一书记胡耀邦，全国学联主席胡启立等，都在大厅入口处迎接来宾们。今晚，政协北京市委员会主席刘仁、全国青联主席刘西元也出席了招待会。
    在一阵热烈的掌声平息以后，彭真市长致词欢迎他的客人。他说：“国际学联第五届代表大会在我们中华人民共和国的首都北京召开，我们北京市全体人民都为此十分高兴，都对你们表示热烈的欢迎。”彭真市长还在他的热情的讲话中，希望各国的朋友们在北京逗留期间，能像在自己家里一样，生活愉快，工作顺利。彭真市长最后请大家举杯，一起来祝贺世界和平，祝贺国际学联第五届代表大会的胜利成功。
    国际学联主席贝利康代表参加大会的各国学生代表感谢彭真市长的热忱款待。他说，经过好几个月的准备以后，国际学联第五届代表大会就将在9月4日正式开幕了。参加这次大会的代表来自各个不同的国家，有着不同的传统和信仰，但是我们都愿意在一起讨论共同的问题。希望通过这次讨论能够进一步促进国际学生间的合作，吸引更多的人参加维护和平、争取民族独立、进行教育改革和改善学生生活条件的斗争。贝利康说，中国学生组织是国际学联的发起者之一，是国际学联的最积极的支持者。他代表参加国际学联的各国学生组织对中国学生表示感谢。
    贝利康最后兴奋地告诉大家，参加这次大会的各国代表团团长在3日下午的一次会议上，提议代表们在大会期间要参加一次义务劳动，以表示对中国人民的敬意。
    伊拉克学生代表团团长罗埃·埃尔·卡德希在会上讲了话。他说，我们十分高兴能趁国际学联第五届代表大会的机会亲眼看到伟大的中国人民的成就。这些成就震动了亚非各国人民，改变了历史的发展趣向。这对全世界和平力量是有利的。使我们感到骄傲的是，你们亿万的英勇人民有效地支持我们的解放事业，支持我们反对美英帝国主义的正义斗争。你们的伟大胜利和成就，你们对我们的解放事业的坚定不移的立场，已经鼓舞了我们伊拉克人民和整个阿拉伯民族的斗志和坚定了我们的信心。
    招待会进行中间，首都的歌唱家和魔术家们表演了精采的节目。这些表演博得了一阵阵雷鸣般的掌声。每次表演结束时都有许多国家的代表跑过去给表演者献花，同他们热烈握手，有的代表还请艺术家们签名留念。
    招待会自始至终都沉浸在友谊的欢乐中，人们被友谊、团结和和平的崇高的愿望紧紧拥抱在一起。
