Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Thai Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet on the 7th to welcome the Thai Cultural Exchange Promotion Delegation headed by the famous Thai writer, Kularb Saipradit.

　　At the banquet, Chu Tunan delivered a speech, expressing his heartfelt admiration for the friendly spirit of the Thai Cultural Exchange Promotion Delegation, which overcame many difficulties and gladly accepted the invitation to visit China in order to enhance the friendship and cultural exchange between the peoples of China and Thailand.

　　In his speech, Mr. Kularb Saipradit traced the historical friendly relations between the peoples of China and Thailand, and pointed out that this long-standing friendly relationship was interrupted for a time due to some obstacles, but this interruption was not the wish of the people. The Thai Cultural Exchange Promotion Delegation will play a bridging role in promoting friendship between the two peoples.

　　Among those attending the banquet were Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and more than 20 Chinese writers, poets, and journalists, including Xu Guangping, Xiao San, Xie Bingxin, Wang Yafan, Lu Minggu, and Wu Jiang.

　　The Thai Cultural Exchange Promotion Delegation arrived in Beijing on the 6th. The delegation is composed of Thai writers, journalists, and artists, totaling twelve people.

### 楚图南欢宴泰文化代表团

1958-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　楚图南欢宴泰文化代表团
    据新华社讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南7日举行宴会欢迎以泰国著名作家古腊·柿巴立为首的泰国促进文化交流团。
    宴会上，楚图南致词，对泰国促进文化交流团为了增进中泰两国人民的友谊和文化交流，克服许多困难欣然应邀前来中国访问的那种友好精神，表示衷心的钦佩。
    古腊·柿巴立先生在讲话中，追溯了中泰两国人民的历史友好关系，同时指出，这种悠久的友好关系由于一些障碍一度中断，但这种中断不是人民的愿望。泰国促进文化交流团在促进两国人民友好方面将起桥梁的作用。
    参加宴会的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林以及我国作家、诗人和新闻界人士许广平、萧三、谢冰心、王亚凡、卢鸣谷和吴江等二十多人。
    泰国促进文化交流团是在6日抵达北京的。这个代表团由泰国的作家、新闻工作者和艺术工作者组成，共十二人。
