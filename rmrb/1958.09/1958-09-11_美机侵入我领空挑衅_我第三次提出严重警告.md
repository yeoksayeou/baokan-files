Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into Our Airspace for Provocation; We Issue the Third Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- At 7:00 AM on September 10th, a U.S. Navy PSM-1 patrol plane intruded into the airspace over the Quanzhou and Meizhou Island area of Fujian Province and over Meizhou Island. At approximately 9:00 AM on the same day, a U.S. U-2 strategic reconnaissance plane intruded into the airspace over Fujian, Jiangxi, and Guangdong provinces for high-altitude reconnaissance.

    The spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare: Following the intrusion of U.S. warships into our territorial waters on September 7th and 8th, U.S. military aircraft have now intruded into our airspace. This is a serious infringement upon our sovereignty and a heinous act of war provocation. The Chinese government has repeatedly issued serious warnings regarding such reckless behavior. However, the U.S. aggressors are determined to put the noose around their own necks. The Chinese people will never be intimidated by any show of force. The Chinese government hereby issues the third serious warning regarding this matter.

### 美机侵入我领空挑衅  我第三次提出严重警告

1958-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我领空挑衅
    我第三次提出严重警告
    新华社10日讯　9月10日七时，美国海军PSM—1型巡逻机一架侵入我国福建省泉州、湄州岛地区领海上空和湄州岛上空，同日九时许，美国u—2型战略侦察机一架侵入我国福建、江西、广东省地区上空，进行高空侦察。
    我外交部发言人奉命声明：继美国军舰于9月7日、8日两次侵入我国领海以后，美国军用飞机竟又侵入我国领空，这是严重侵犯我国主权、蓄意进行战争挑衅的罪恶行为。中国政府曾经一再对这种冒险的行为提出严重警告。但是，美国侵略者却硬要把绞索套在自己的脖子上。中国人民决不是任何炫耀武力所能吓倒的。中国政府对此第三次提出严重警告。
