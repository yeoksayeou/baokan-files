Title: Planned Passenger Transportation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 2

Criticism and Response

　　Planned Passenger Transportation
    On July 18th, the People's Daily published Comrade Wang Yingju's letter, criticizing our station for failing to properly organize passenger transportation on June 28th, resulting in overcrowding and disorder on the 11th train to Sankeshut. We accept the criticism and guarantee to organize passenger transportation in a planned manner in the future.
    Fengtai Station Passenger Office, Beijing Railway Administration

### 有计划地运送旅客

1958-09-04
第2版()
专栏：批评反应

　　有计划地运送旅客
    7月18日人民日报刊登王英举同志的来信，批评我站在6月28日没有把运送旅客工作组织好，以致开往三棵树方面去的十一次列车乘客拥挤，车内秩序混乱。我们接受批评，保证今后有计划地运送旅客。
    北京铁路管理局丰台站客运室
