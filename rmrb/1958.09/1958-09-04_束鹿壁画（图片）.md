Title: Shulu Murals (Picture)
Author: Zuo Hai
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 8

　　Shulu Murals, Poem by Zuo Hai
　　Shulu is rumored to be covered in gold,
　　Millennial exploitation brings bitter groans.
　　Since the revolution turned the world upside down,
　　Today's bumper harvest fills hearts with joy.
