Title: Mukhiddinov Says at Cairo University: American Troops Must Withdraw from the Taiwan Area
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 25th. Mukhiddinov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Nationalities Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, stated during a visit to Cairo University today that all peace-loving countries denounce the actions of the American imperialists, demand the withdrawal of American troops from the Taiwan area, demand the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations, and support its struggle to unify its territory.

Mukhiddinov expressed his heartfelt hope for the success of the Chinese people's just struggle to liberate their territory and strive for stability and security in the Far East and the world.

He said, "There is only one China, not two Chinas. Taiwan is an indivisible part of China. The Soviet Union and the United Arab Republic, as well as all peace-loving people, stand with the Chinese people in their struggle against American imperialists and their agents to liberate Chinese territory."

Three thousand students listened to Mukhiddinov's more than one-hour speech at Cairo University, applauding and cheering enthusiastically for the friendship between Arab countries and the Soviet Union. They shouted slogans: "We support China against America!" "Support Lebanon against America!", "We support Algeria against France!", "We support Jordan against America!"

Mukhiddinov continued that American imperialists are plotting new conspiracies against China, but the 600 million Chinese people will not be intimidated.

The Soviet guest arrived at Cairo University this morning in the company of the Minister of Education of the United Arab Republic, Hussein, and was warmly welcomed by professors and students.

Mukhiddinov then stated that the Arab people will always be able to see that the Soviet government and leaders are true friends who sincerely care about the national aspirations of all peoples. The Soviet government and people welcome all countries that have freed themselves from foreign shackles and have begun to build their new lives. They sincerely and earnestly support the Arab people's stance in defending freedom and national independence.

### 穆希金诺夫在开罗大学说  美国军队必须撤出台湾地区

1958-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    穆希金诺夫在开罗大学说
    美国军队必须撤出台湾地区
    据新华社开罗25日电  苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席穆希金诺夫在今天参观开罗大学时发表演说指出，所有爱好和平的国家都斥责美帝国主义者的行动，要求美国军队撤出台湾地区，要求恢复中国在联合国中的合法席位和支持它争取统一它的领土的斗争。
    穆希金诺夫表示衷心希望中国人民为解放它的领土以及争取远东和全世界的稳定和安全而进行的正义斗争获得成功。
    他说，“只有一个中国，没有两个中国。台湾是中国不可分割的一部分。苏联和阿拉伯联合共和国以及所有爱好和平的人民在中国人民反对美帝国主义者和他们的代理人、争取解放中国领土的斗争中，是同中国人民站在一边的。”
    有三千名学生听取了穆希金诺夫在开罗大学发表的一个多小时的演说，他们热烈鼓掌，欢呼阿拉伯国家和苏联的友谊。他们高喊口号：“我们支持中国反对美国！”“支持黎巴嫩反对美国！”，“我们支持阿尔及利亚反对法国！”“我们支持约旦反对美国！”
    穆希金诺夫继续说道，美帝国主义者正在对中国策划新的阴谋，但是六亿中国人民是不会被吓倒的。
    这位苏联贵宾是在阿拉伯联合共和国教育部长侯赛尼的陪同下，在今天上午到达开罗大学的，他受到了教授和学生们的热烈欢迎。
    穆希金诺夫接着说道，阿拉伯人民将始终能看到苏联政府和领袖们是对各国人民的民族愿望怀着真诚的心情的真正朋友。苏联政府和人民欢迎所有摆脱了外国的枷锁、开始建立它们的新生活的国家。他们真挚和诚恳地支持阿拉伯人民保卫自由和民族独立的立场。
