Title: Yankee, Get Out of Taiwan!
Author: Ālièkèsī Qiàqí
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 8

    Yankee, Get Out of Taiwan!
    Albanian Poet Alex Chaçi

    Yankee, get out of Taiwan! If you don't leave on your own, we will drive you out. Do you know? I am Albanian, and I have a deep friendship with the Chinese people. Soviets, Chinese, Arabs, Bulgarians... we are all united like one person. Yankee, don't forget, doomsday is waiting for you! You have come to Taiwan from a very far place! What are you doing on this land that belongs to China? The people, the land, and the sea are all roaring: Yankee, get out! Chinese people, clench your fists, we will always stand with you. We are as numerous as grains of sand in the sea, our power is as vast as the depth of the seawater. The sun that shines on the world belongs to us, there is no share for you, because the whole world belongs to us, Yankee, there is no place for you here! (Translated by Li Ninglai)

### 美国佬，从台湾滚出去！
阿列克斯·恰奇
1958-09-13
第8版()
专栏：

    美国佬，从台湾滚出去！
    阿尔巴尼亚诗人　阿列克斯·恰奇美国佬，从台湾滚出去！如果你自己不走，我们就要把你赶走。你知道吗？我是阿尔巴尼亚人，我和中国人民有着深厚的友谊。苏联人、中国人、阿拉伯人、保加利亚人……我们大家团结得像一个人。美国佬，你不要忘记，末日正在等待着你！你从很远的地方来到了台湾！你到这块属于中国的土地上来干什么？人民、土地和大海一起都在怒吼：美国佬，滚出去！中国人，紧握起你的拳头，我们永远和你们站在一起。我们像大海中的沙粒一样多，我们的力量洪大得像海水一样深。照耀世界的阳光属于我们，没有你一点份，因为整个世界就属于我们，美国佬，这里没有你的位子！　（李宁来译）
