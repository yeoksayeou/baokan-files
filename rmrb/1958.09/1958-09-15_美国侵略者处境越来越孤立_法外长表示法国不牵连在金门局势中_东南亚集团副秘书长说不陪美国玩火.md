Title: American Aggressors Increasingly Isolated: French Foreign Minister States France Will Not Be Involved in the Quemoy Situation; Southeast Asia Group Deputy Secretary-General Says He Will Not Play with Fire with the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th - Paris dispatch: French Foreign Minister Couve de Murville said during a televised speech on the evening of the 12th that France is not directly involved in the Quemoy Strait situation.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 13th: "Dawn" reported today that William Worth, Deputy Secretary-General of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, said in Lahore on the 12th that he opposed the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization being drawn into a possible war in the Taiwan Strait. Worth told reporters that the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization cannot be required to participate in activities outside the treaty area.

### 美国侵略者处境越来越孤立  法外长表示法国不牵连在金门局势中  东南亚集团副秘书长说不陪美国玩火

1958-09-15
第3版()
专栏：

    美国侵略者处境越来越孤立
    法外长表示法国不牵连在金门局势中
    东南亚集团副秘书长说不陪美国玩火
    新华社14日讯　巴黎消息：法国外交部长德姆维尔12日晚在发表电视谈话的时候说，法国并不直接牵连在金门海峡局势中。
    新华社卡拉奇13日电　“黎明报”今天报道，东南亚条约组织副秘书长威廉·沃思12日在拉合尔表示反对东南亚条约组织被卷入台湾海峡万一发生的战争。沃思对记者们发表谈话说，东南亚条约组织不能被要求参加条约地区以外的活动。
