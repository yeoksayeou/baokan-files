Title: We Must Liberate Taiwan
Author: Xu Chi
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 8

    We Must Liberate Taiwan
    Xu Chi
    Nine years is too long,
    Nine years without seeing the sun,
    Nine years of suffering too deep,
    Nine years without hearing laughter.
    We must liberate Taiwan,
    Our loved ones have waited too long,
    Their eyes have already looked through,
    Their blood is almost drained by the vampires!
    We must liberate Taiwan,
    Let Taiwan return to the embrace of the motherland,
    Let ships sail from Keelung back to the motherland,
    Let the hot springs of Taipei wash away its humiliation.
    We must liberate Taiwan,
    Let the forests of Alishan regain their verdant green,
    Let the suffering of Taiwan Sugar become sweet again,
    Let our loved ones also come to establish communes.
    Together we will try the traitor Chiang,
    And then celebrate National Day,
    The time is now,
    We must liberate Taiwan!

### 一定要解放台湾
徐迟
1958-09-11
第8版()
专栏：

    一定要解放台湾
    徐迟
    九年的时间太长，
    九年没有见太阳，
    九年的苦难太深，
    九年不闻欢笑声。
    一定要解放台湾，
    亲人们已等得太久，
    眼睛已经望穿，
    血也快叫吸血鬼吸干！
    一定要解放台湾，
    让台湾回入祖国胸怀，
    让船只从基隆驶回祖国，
    让台北温泉洗净自己的屈辱。
    一定要解放台湾，
    让阿里山森林恢复青翠，
    让苦痛的台糖重新发甜，
    让我们的亲人也来建立公社。
    共同来审判蒋贼，
    然后来庆祝国庆，
    这已经是时候了，
    一定要解放台湾！
