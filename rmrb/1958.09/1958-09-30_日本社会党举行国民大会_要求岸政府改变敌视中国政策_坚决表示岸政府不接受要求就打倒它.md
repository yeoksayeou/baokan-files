Title: Japanese Socialist Party Holds National Convention Demanding the Kishi Government Change Its Hostile Policy Toward China; Vows to Overthrow the Kishi Government if It Refuses to Accept Demands

Author: 
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 29th: The Japanese Socialist Party held a national convention in Tokyo this afternoon, demanding that the Nobusuke Kishi government change its hostile policy towards the Chinese people. The convention unanimously adopted a resolution on immediately establishing diplomatic relations and trade with China.

The resolution, passed at the convention attended by more than one thousand Tokyo citizens, stated that in light of the current tense situation in the Taiwan Strait, and for the sake of Japan's peace, security, and economic independence, the convention, in the name of all the people, demands that the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet take the following measures:

	I. Stop all acts of creating "two Chinas" and realize the restoration of diplomatic relations with China;

	II. The Taiwan issue is China's internal affair, and the international tensions related to the Taiwan issue should be peacefully resolved by the countries concerned;

	III. Do not participate in alliances such as the "Northeast Asia Military Alliance" that are aimed at China;

	IV. Do not bring nuclear weapons into Japan;

	V. Support China's restoration of its legitimate seat in the United Nations;

	VI. Express apology to China for the Nagasaki insult to the Chinese national flag incident, prevent similar incidents from happening again, and guarantee the dignity of the Chinese national flag;

	VII. Do not hinder efforts to normalize Japan-China diplomatic relations, and provide active assistance and support to cultural, economic, and other exchanges;

	VIII. The American troops occupying Taiwan and "supporting" the Chiang gang are the root cause of the tense situation in Taiwan. Therefore, the Japanese government must take the following attitude: A. Oppose the US military's armed intervention in the Taiwan issue; B. The Chiang gang must withdraw from Jinmen and Mazu; C. Oppose the use of bases in Japan by the US military; D. Place hopes on the Sino-US ambassadorial talks for a peaceful resolution of the Taiwan, Jinmen and Mazu issues.

The resolution stated that, in order to achieve the above goals, all participants will concentrate the strength of all the people to launch a long-term and vigorous movement.

The convention finally passed a declaration resolutely stating that if the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet does not accept these demands, all the Japanese people will rise up and overthrow the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet in order to establish a peaceful and democratic government.

### 日本社会党举行国民大会  要求岸政府改变敌视中国政策  坚决表示岸政府不接受要求就打倒它

1958-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党举行国民大会
    要求岸政府改变敌视中国政策
    坚决表示岸政府不接受要求就打倒它
    据新华社29日讯  东京消息：日本社会党今天下午在东京举行国民大会，要求岸信介政府改变敌视中国人民的政策。大会一致通过了关于立即打通日中邦交和贸易的决议。
    有一千多东京市民参加的这次大会的决议说，在目前台湾海峡形势紧张的情况之下，大会为了日本的和平、安全和经济自立，以全体人民的名义要求岸信介内阁采取下列措施：
    一、停止制造“两个中国”的一切行为，实现恢复同中国的邦交；
    二、台湾问题是中国的内政问题，同台湾问题有关系的国际紧张局势应由有关国家来和平解决；
    三、不要参加以中国为对象的类似“东北亚军事同盟”等的同盟；
    四、不把核武器运进日本；
    五、在联合国支持中国恢复它的合法席位；
    六、就长崎侮辱中国国旗事件对中国表示歉意，防止今后再发生类似事件，并且保障中国国旗的尊严；
    七、不要阻碍日中邦交正常化的努力，并且对文化、经济等的交流给予积极协助和支持；
    八、占据台湾的美国军队“支援”蒋帮是台湾局势紧张化的根源，因此日本政府必须采取下列态度：甲、反对美军武装干涉台湾问题；乙、蒋帮必须撤出金门、马祖；丙、反对美军使用在日本的基地；丁、为和平解决台湾、金门和马祖问题，对中美大使会谈寄于期望。
    决议表示，全体与会者为了达到上述目标，将集中全体人民的力量展开长期的有力的运动。
    大会最后通过一项宣言坚决表示，如果岸信介内阁不接受这些要求。全体日本人民将起来打倒岸信介内阁，以便建立和平、民主的政府。
