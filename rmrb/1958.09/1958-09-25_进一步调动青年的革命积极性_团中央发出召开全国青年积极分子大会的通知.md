Title: Further Arouse the Revolutionary Enthusiasm of the Youth: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Issues a Notice on Convening a National Conference of Youth Activists

Author: 
Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League issued a notice today regarding the convening of the Second National Conference of Youth Socialist Construction Activists. The notice stated:

    "The Second National Conference of Youth Socialist Construction Activists is scheduled to open on November 5, 1958, in our country's capital—Beijing.

    "This conference will commend youth advanced collectives and youth advanced individuals who have emerged on various fronts since the Great Leap Forward in our country's socialist construction, disseminate their exemplary deeds, exchange advanced experiences, and focus on discussing how to further mobilize the greater revolutionary enthusiasm of the nation's youth to realize the Party's general line for socialist construction and to struggle for the realization of the great tasks proposed by the Beidaihe Enlarged Meeting of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee.

    "Representatives of youth advanced individuals and youth advanced collectives attending this conference will be selected by the Communist Youth League committees of provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, or elected through youth activists conferences in provinces, cities, and autonomous regions. They must possess a high degree of communist spirit on all fronts of socialist construction, have made outstanding achievements, and have created labor results that reach or approach the advanced national or international level. The quota of representatives is set at 5,000.

    "During the conference, a National Youth Socialist Construction Achievements Exhibition will be held in Beijing to reflect the glorious achievements and communist spirit of the youth across the country on all fronts of socialist construction, exchange and promote their advanced experiences, and educate and encourage the broad masses of youth to greet the Great Leap Forward in 1959 with greater revolutionary vigor and to launch a new high tide of learning from the advanced, comparing with the advanced, catching up with the advanced, and surpassing the advanced. It is hoped that the youth across the country will use their labor achievements to enrich and expand this exhibition.

    "The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League calls on all members of the Communist Youth League and the youth across the country to take immediate action and launch a wave to welcome the Second National Conference of Youth Socialist Construction Activists. Under the leadership of the Party, under the brilliant illumination of the general line, muster greater efforts, continue to carry forward the communist style of daring to think, daring to speak, and daring to do, seize the time to work hard and go all out, vigorously engage in steel production, actively take the lead in participating in people's communes, vigorously engage in deep plowing of land, autumn harvesting and planting, and water conservancy construction, technological revolution, and cultural revolution, as well as other socialist construction, actively participate in national armament and military training, and use practical actions to welcome the convening of the Second National Conference of Youth Socialist Construction Activists and make greater achievements for the socialist construction of the motherland."

### 进一步调动青年的革命积极性  团中央发出召开全国青年积极分子大会的通知

1958-09-25
第7版()
专栏：

    进一步调动青年的革命积极性
    团中央发出召开全国青年积极分子大会的通知
    新华社23日讯  共青团中央委员会今天发出关于召开第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的通知，通知中说：
    “第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会定于1958年11月5日在我国的首都——北京开幕。
    “这次大会，将表彰我国社会主义建设大跃进以来各个战线上出现的青年先进集体和青年先进人物，传播他们的模范事迹，交流先进经验，并着重讨论如何进一步调动起全国青年更大的革命积极性，为实现党的社会主义建设总路线，为实现党中央政治局北戴河扩大会议提出的伟大任务而斗争。
    “出席这次大会的青年先进人物和青年先进集体的代表，由省、市、自治区团委负责选拔，或经省、市、自治区的青年积极分子大会选举产生。他们必须是在社会主义建设各个战线上有高度的共产主义精神，做出了突出的成绩，创造了达到或接近全国或国际先进水平的劳动成果。代表名额定为五千名。
    “大会期间，将在北京举办一个全国青年社会主义建设成就的展览会，反映全国各地青年在社会主义建设各个战线上的辉煌战果和共产主义的精神面貌，交流和推广他们的先进经验，以教育和鼓舞广大青年，用更大的革命干劲来迎接1959年的大跃进，掀起一个学先进、比先进、赶先进、超先进的新高潮。希望全国各地青年，用自己的劳动成果来充实和丰富这个展览会。
    “共青团中央号召全体团员和全国青年，立即行动起来，掀起一个迎接第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的热潮。在党的领导下，在总路线的光辉照耀下，鼓足更大的干劲，继续发扬敢想敢说敢做的共产主义风格，抓紧时间大干特干，大闹钢铁生产，积极带头参加人民公社，大搞深翻土地、秋收秋种和水利建设、技术革命和文化革命，以及其它各项社会主义建设，积极投入全民武装和练兵活动，用实际的行动来迎接第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的召开，为祖国的社会主义建设作出更大的成就。”
