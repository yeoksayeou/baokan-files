Title: Larger Scale, Lower Cost
Author:
Date: 1958-09-17
Page: 5

    Larger Scale
    Lower Cost
    In 1958, the installed capacity of basic construction projects in the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power system was 1.4 million kilowatts, 2.2 times that of 1957.
    The capacity of power stations started this year is 2.2 million kilowatts, five times the capacity of power stations started during the First Five-Year Plan.

### 规模越来越大  造价越来越低

1958-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    规模越来越大
    造价越来越低
    水利电力工业部系统1958年基本建设装机容量140万瓩，为1957年的2.2倍。
    今年开工建设的电站容量2.200万瓩，为第一个五年计划期间开工电站容量的五倍。
