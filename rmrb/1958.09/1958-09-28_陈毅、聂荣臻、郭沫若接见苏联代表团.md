Title: Chen Yi, Nie Rongzhen, and Guo Moruo Receive Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen, and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo received the Soviet delegation and Soviet atomic energy experts in China this morning. The delegation came to China to attend the handover ceremony of the experimental atomic reactor and cyclotron. The delegation was led by Yefremov, Deputy Director of the General Administration for the Utilization of Atomic Energy of the Soviet Council of Ministers. Members of the delegation were Academician Alikhanov and Academician Vinogradov. Also present at the reception were Zhang Jingfu, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Atomic Energy.

### 陈毅、聂荣臻、郭沫若接见苏联代表团

1958-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅、聂荣臻、郭沫若接见苏联代表团
    新华社27日讯  陈毅副总理、聂荣臻副总理和中国科学院院长郭沫若今天上午接见了来我国参加实验性原子反应堆和回旋加速器移交生产典礼的苏联代表团和在华苏联原子能专家。代表团由苏联部长会议原子能利用总局副局长叶夫列莫夫率领，团员是阿里哈诺夫院士和维诺格拉多夫院士。接见时在座的有中国科学院副院长张劲夫和原子能研究所所长钱三强等人。
