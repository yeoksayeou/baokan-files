Title: Cambodian "Free People's News" Urges the US Not to Fight for Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh. The Cambodian newspaper "Free People's News" wrote on the 28th, commenting on the situation in the Taiwan Strait, that "a panic-stricken America is sending warships and planes to aid Chiang Kai-shek." It asked, "Can steel and armaments defeat people?" The newspaper said, "We hope the United States will not send Americans to die for the island of Taiwan and cause American families to mourn."

### 柬埔寨“自由人民报”  要美国不为台湾作战

1958-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨“自由人民报”
    要美国不为台湾作战
    据新华社金边电　柬埔寨“自由人民报”28日在评论台湾海峡局势时写道，“惊惶失措的美国派遣军舰和飞机去援助蒋介石”。它问道，“难道钢铁和军火能胜过人么？”报纸说，“我们希望美国不要为台湾岛而叫美国人去送死，并且使美国家庭哭丧。”
