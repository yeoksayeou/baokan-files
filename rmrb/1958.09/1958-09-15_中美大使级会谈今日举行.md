Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks to be Held Today
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 14th. The following communiqué on the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was released here today:

Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Communiqué – Warsaw, September 14, 1958.

After consultation, the representatives of the People's Republic of China and the representatives of the United States of America have decided that the next meeting of the ambassadorial talks will be held at 3:00 p.m. on Monday, September 15, 1958, at the Miechilewicki Palace in Warsaw, at a venue provided by the Polish government.

### 中美大使级会谈今日举行

1958-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈今日举行
    新华社华沙14日电　今天在这里发表了中美大使级会谈公报如下：
    中美大使级会谈公报——1958年9月14日于华沙。
    中华人民共和国代表和美利坚合众国代表协商以后决定，大使级会谈下次会议于1958年9月15日（星期一）下午三时在波兰政府所提供的会场华沙梅希里维茨基宫举行。
