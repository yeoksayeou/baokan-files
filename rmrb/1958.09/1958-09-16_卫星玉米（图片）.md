Title: Satellite Corn (Picture)
Author: Ye Qianyu
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 8

    Satellite Corn
    Ye Qianyu Poem and Painting
    Entering the king corn experimental field,
    Eighteen Arhats shake on one cob;
    Red flags flutter on top of the crops,
    One suspects sparrows are being besieged.

### 卫星玉米（图片）
叶浅予
1958-09-16
第8版()
专栏：

    卫星玉米
    叶浅予　诗并画
    走进王米试验田，
    十八罗汉一穗摇；
    庄稼顶上红旗飘，
    疑是麻雀遭围剿。
