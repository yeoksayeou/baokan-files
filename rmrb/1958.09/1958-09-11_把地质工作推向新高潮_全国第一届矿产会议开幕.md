Title: Push Geological Work to a New Climax: The First National Mineral Conference Opens

Author:

Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 6

Push Geological Work to a New Climax
The First National Mineral Conference Opens

(Our Dispatch) The first National Mineral Conference of our country opened in Beijing on the 10th. This conference was jointly initiated by the Ministry of Geology of the People's Republic of China, the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Geological Society of China, and various geological colleges, and was convened after a long period of preparation. More than 500 representatives attended the conference, including administrative leaders and engineering technicians from various production units, technical cadres and experts from scientific research departments, and professors and teachers from various geological colleges.

The Soviet delegation, headed by Comrade Antropov, Minister of Geology and Mineral Protection of the Soviet Union, was invited to participate in the conference. The delegation included Shatbayev, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic and Academician of the All-Union Academy of Sciences, Abdullayev, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic and Academician of the All-Union Academy of Sciences, and nine others, who arrived in Beijing at 4:10 p.m. on September 8. Many Soviet experts who are helping our country with socialist construction also attended the meeting.

The purpose of this conference is to summarize the experience of prospecting and mineral exploration since liberation, and to further implement the general line of going all out, aiming high, and building socialism with greater, faster, better, and more economical results, in order to push geological work to a new climax.

At the conference, outstanding Soviet geologists will also introduce advanced Soviet geological science theories and experience.

### 把地质工作推向新高潮  全国第一届矿产会议开幕

1958-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    把地质工作推向新高潮
    全国第一届矿产会议开幕
    本报讯　我国第一届矿产会议10日在北京开幕。这次会议是在中华人民共和国地质部、中华人民共和国冶金部、中国科学院、中国地质学会、各地质院校等共同倡议下又经较长时间的筹备召开的。到会的代表有来自各生产单位的行政领导和工程技术人员，有科学研究部门的技术干部和专家，有各地质院校的教授和教师等共五百余人。
    以苏联地质保矿部部长安特洛波夫同志为首的苏联代表团应邀参加了这次会议。代表团团员有哈萨克加盟共和国科学院院长、全苏科学院院士沙特巴也夫，乌兹别克加盟共和国科学院院长、全苏科学院院士阿布杜拉也夫等一行九人，他们已在9月8日下午四时十分到达北京。出席会议的还有许多帮助我国进行社会主义建设的苏联专家。
    这次会议的目的，是总结解放以来普查找矿的经验，进一步贯彻鼓足干劲，力争上游，多快好省地建设社会主义的总路线，以便把地质工作推向新的高潮。
    在会议上还将有卓越的苏联地质学者介绍先进的苏联地质科学理论和经验。
