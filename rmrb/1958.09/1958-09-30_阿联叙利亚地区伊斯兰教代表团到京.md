Title: Islamic Delegation from the Syrian Region of the United Arab Republic Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Islamic delegation from the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic, consisting of six members led by Grand Mufti Dr. Abidin, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th at the invitation of the China Islamic Association for a visit to our country.

### 阿联叙利亚地区伊斯兰教代表团到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联叙利亚地区伊斯兰教代表团到京
    新华社28日讯  应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请来我国访问的阿联叙利亚地区伊斯兰教代表团一行六人，由大木夫提·阿比丁博士率领，29日下午乘飞机到达北京。
