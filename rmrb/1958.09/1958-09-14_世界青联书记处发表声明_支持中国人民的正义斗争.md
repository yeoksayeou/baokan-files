Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretariat Issues Statement Supporting the Just Struggle of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 12th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a statement regarding the war provocations carried out by the United States government against the Chinese people in the Taiwan Strait area. The statement expressed that the World Federation of Democratic Youth will give full support to the just struggle of the Chinese people and Chinese youth to liberate their own territory of Taiwan and other coastal islands.
    The statement said that Taiwan and other coastal islands of China have been an inseparable part of Chinese territory since ancient times, and the issue of liberating Taiwan and other islands is China's internal affair. The solution to this problem can only be decided by the Chinese people themselves.
    The statement called on the youth and youth organizations of the world to support the just struggle of the Chinese people and Chinese youth to liberate Taiwan and other coastal islands, and demanded that the US armed forces immediately withdraw from the Taiwan Strait area.
    On September 12, the Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth also sent a telegram to Eisenhower, protesting the US government's interference in China's internal affairs. The telegram said that the US government's war provocations in the Taiwan Strait area have intensified international tensions and threatened peace in the Far East and the world.
    The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth also sent a telegram to the All-China Youth Federation, expressing full support for the just struggle of the Chinese people and Chinese youth.

### 世界青联书记处发表声明  支持中国人民的正义斗争

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    世界青联书记处发表声明
    支持中国人民的正义斗争
    新华社布达佩斯12日电　世界民主青年联盟书记处就美国政府在台湾海峡地区对中国人民所进行的战争挑衅发表声明。声明表示，世界民主青年联盟在中国人民和中国青年为解放自己的领土台湾和其他沿海岛屿所进行的正义斗争中，将给予全力的支持。
    声明说，台湾和中国的其他沿海岛屿自古以来就是中国领土的不可分割的一部分，解放台湾和其他岛屿的问题是中国人民的内政。这个问题的解决只能由中国人民自己决定。
    声明号召全世界的青年和青年组织支持中国人民和中国青年为解放台湾和其他沿海岛屿的正义斗争，并且要求美国武装力量立即从台湾海峡地区撤出去。
    世界民主青年联盟书记处12日还给艾森豪威尔打电报，抗议美国政府对中国内政的干涉。电报说，美国政府在台湾海峡地区的战争挑衅加剧了国际紧张局势，并且威胁着远东和全世界的和平。
    世界民主青年联盟书记处还致电中华全国青年联合会，表示全力支持中国人民和中国青年的正义斗争。
