Title: Promoting Common Prosperity in Socialist Construction: China and North Korea Sign Long-Term Trade Agreement; Agreement on China Providing Loans to North Korea Signed Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1958-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: During the friendly visit of the North Korean government delegation headed by Vice Premier Li Jooyun of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to our country, it held talks with our government on economic cooperation between the two countries. The talks were conducted in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere and have now been successfully concluded. Today, the two sides signed in Beijing the "Agreement between China and North Korea on the Mutual Supply of Major Goods from 1959 to 1962" and the "Agreement on the Government of the People's Republic of China Providing Two Loans to the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea."

According to the provisions of the long-term trade agreement, China will supply North Korea with major materials such as coal, cotton, cotton yarn, tires, refined tin, rolled steel, ferromanganese, sulfur, paraffin, and gypsum; North Korea will supply China with major materials such as iron ore, copper, lead, zinc, high-speed steel, carbon tool steel, calcium carbide, ginseng, and seafood products.

In order to meet the needs of socialist construction in both countries, the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea have decided to jointly invest in the construction of the Yunfeng Hydropower Station. Regarding half of the Yunfeng Hydropower Station construction costs borne by the North Korean side, at request of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Government of the People's Repubic of China has agreed to provide a long-term loan to the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The North Korean government will repay the loan with goods over ten years starting in 1963.

At the request of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Government of the People’s Republic of China has agreed to provide a long-term loan to the Government of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea. According to the loan agreement, North Korea will use this loan to purchase the machinery and equipment needed for a textile factory and two cement paper bag factories from China. The North Korean government will repay the loan with goods over ten years starting in 1961.

Vice Premier Bo Yibo of the State Council of the People's Republic of China signed the above three agreements on behalf of the Chinese side; Vice Premier Li Jooyun of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, head of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed on behalf of the North Korean side.

At the same time, the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Commerce of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea also signed the "Protocol on the Supply of Complete Sets of Equipment and Machinery Equipment from the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" today in accordance with the provisions of the two countries' loan agreements. Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China Ye Jizhuang and Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Li Jong-ok signed the protocol.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony. The Chinese side attending the signing ceremony also included Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and Li Fanru, our Commercial Counselor to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and others.

The North Korean side attending the signing ceremony included: Li Yongho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of China, and Kim Choi-seon, Deputy Minister of Domestic and Foreign Commerce of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 促进社会主义建设事业共同繁荣  中朝签订长期贸易协定  我国向朝鲜提供贷款的协定同时签字

1958-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    促进社会主义建设事业共同繁荣
    中朝签订长期贸易协定
    我国向朝鲜提供贷款的协定同时签字
    新华社27日讯  以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊为首的朝鲜政府代表团在我国进行友好访问期间，同我国政府就两国经济合作问题举行了会谈，会谈是在友好而融洽的气氛中进行的，现在已经圆满结束。双方今天在北京签订了中朝关于1959年到1962年相互供应主要货物的协定和关于中华人民共和国政府向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府提供两项贷款的协定。
    根据长期贸易协定的规定，中国方面将供应朝鲜煤炭、棉花、棉纱、轮胎、炼锡、压延钢材、锰铁、硫磺、石腊、石膏等主要物资；朝鲜方面将供应中国铁矿石、铜、铅、锌、高速度钢、炭素工具钢、电石、人参、海产品等主要物资。
    为了满足两国社会主义建设的需要，中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府决定共同投资兴建云峰水电站。关于朝鲜方面所分担云峰水电站建设费用之半数，根据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的要求，中华人民共和国政府同意向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府提供长期贷款。朝鲜政府将自1963年起分十年以货物偿还。
    根据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的要求，中华人民共和国政府同意向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府提供一项长期贷款。按照贷款协定规定，朝鲜将用此款向中国购买一个纺织厂和两个水泥纸袋厂所需的机器设备。朝鲜政府将自1961年起分十年以货物偿还。
    代表中国方面在上述三个协定上签字的是中华人民共和国国务院副总理薄一波；代表朝鲜方面签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊。
    同时，中华人民共和国对外贸易部和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国对内外商业省根据两国贷款协定的规定，也在今天签订了“关于中华人民共和国供应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成套设备及机器设备的议定书”，在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家计划委员会委员长李钟玉。
    周恩来总理参加了签字仪式。中国方面参加签字仪式的还有国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长林海云和我驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国商务参赞李范如等。
    朝鲜方面参加签字仪式的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中华人民共和国大使李永镐，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国对内外商业省副相金最善。
