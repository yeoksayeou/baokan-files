Title: Burmese Local Administrative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th September. The Burmese Local Administrative Delegation, led by U Than Maung, consisting of eight members, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by plane from Moscow at the invitation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

    Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and others welcomed them at the airport. U Ba Maung, First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, was also present at the airport to welcome them.

    In the evening, Wan Li, Vice Mayor of Beijing, received and hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation.

### 缅甸地方行政代表团到京

1958-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸地方行政代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　应北京市人民委员会邀请来我国访问的以吴丹茂为首的缅甸地方行政代表团一行八人，今天下午从莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。
    北京市副市长吴晗等到机场欢迎。前往机场欢迎的还有缅甸驻中国大使馆一等秘书吴巴茂。
    今天晚上，北京市副市长万里接见并设宴欢迎代表团全体人员。
