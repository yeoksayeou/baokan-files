    What are the Autumn-Sown Crops?

    Autumn sowing is generally called winter sowing or winter planting in southern regions. The autumn-sown crops referred to here are all overwintering crops. In northern regions, the main crop is winter wheat. In southern regions, the main crops are: winter wheat, winter rapeseed, broad beans, peas, barley, and winter green manure (milk vetch, vetch, etc.).

    The autumn-sown crop with the largest area is winter wheat, covering about 350 million mu (in 1957, the same below). Next is barley, covering about 70 million mu. Last year, the winter rapeseed planting area was more than 30 million mu, while broad beans and peas each covered 20 to 30 million mu, and winter green manure covered 50 to 60 million mu.

### 秋播作物有哪些？

1958-09-17
第6版()
专栏：小常识

    秋播作物有哪些？
    秋播在南方地区一般叫冬播或冬种。这里指的秋播作物都是越冬作物，北方地区主要是冬小麦，南方地区主要是：冬小麦、冬油菜、蚕豆、豌豆、大麦、冬季绿肥（紫云英、苕子等）。
    这些秋播作物面积最大的是冬小麦，约三亿五千多万亩（1957年，下同），其次是大麦约七千万亩左右。去年冬油菜播种面积是三千多万亩，蚕豆、豌豆各两、三千万亩，冬季绿肥五、六千多万亩。
