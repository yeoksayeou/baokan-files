Title: Dedicate All Strength According to the Needs of the Motherland; Resolutely Smash the War Provocations of the U.S. Imperialists

Author:

Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 3

    According to the Needs of the Motherland, Dedicate All Strength
    Resolutely Smash the War Provocations of the U.S. Imperialists
    After Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait area was published, our entire institute held a symposium of student representatives, unanimously expressing boundless anger at the war provocations of the American imperialists, and firmly expressing support for our government's statement, and a determination to respond to the call of the Supreme State Conference with concrete actions. If the American imperialists dare to obstruct our 600 million people from liberating their own territory of Taiwan and the coastal islands, everyone is determined to contribute everything to oppose aggression, defend the integrity of the motherland's territorial sovereignty, and shatter the war provocations of the American imperialists. We will go wherever the motherland needs us, and we will not hesitate to make any sacrifices. Everyone firmly believes that under the leadership of the great Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and with the common support of peace-loving countries and people all over the world, we will surely be able to defeat the American imperialists.
                     All members of the Socialist Academy (photo montage)

### 根据祖国需要　献出一切力量  坚决粉碎美帝国主义者的战争挑衅

1958-09-10
第3版()
专栏：

    根据祖国需要　献出一切力量
    坚决粉碎美帝国主义者的战争挑衅
    周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明发表以后，我们全院举行了学员代表座谈会，一致对美国帝国主义者的战争挑衅，表示无比的愤怒，并坚决表示拥护我国政府的声明，要以具体行动来响应最高国务会议的号召。如果美国帝国主义者胆敢阻挠我六亿人民解放自己领土台湾和沿海岛屿，大家决心贡献自己的一切，为反对侵略，保卫祖国的领土主权的完整，粉碎美国帝国主义者的战争挑衅。祖国要我们到那里我们就到那里，任何牺牲在所不惜。大家都坚信，在伟大的中国共产党和毛主席领导之下，全世界爱好和平的国家和人民的共同支持，我们一定能够打败美国帝国主义者。
                     社会主义学院全体人员（照片剪贴画）
