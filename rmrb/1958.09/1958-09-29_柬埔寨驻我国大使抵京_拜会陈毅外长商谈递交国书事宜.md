Title: Cambodian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing; Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Chen Yi to Discuss Presentation of Credentials
Author:
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, Mr. Leng Ngeth, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today. Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department Li Chen were at the airport to welcome him.

The Deputy Head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, Mr. Nay Valentin, and members of the delegation were also present to welcome him.

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, Mr. Leng Ngeth, paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Chen Yi this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 柬埔寨驻我国大使抵京  拜会陈毅外长商谈递交国书事宜

1958-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨驻我国大使抵京
    拜会陈毅外长商谈递交国书事宜
    新华社28日讯  柬埔寨王国首任驻华特命全权大使兰·涅特今天中午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有外交部副部长章汉夫，礼宾司司长俞沛文，亚洲司副司长李晨。
    柬埔寨王国驻华经济代表团副团长乃瓦朗丹和代表团人员，也前往迎接。
    新华社28日讯  柬埔寨王国首任驻华特命全权大使兰·涅特今天下午拜会我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。拜会时在座的有外交部亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司司长俞沛文。
