Title: Arab Students in Damascus Express to Ambassador Chen Jiakang: Arab Students Support Chinese People's Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 3rd: A group of students from various Arab countries at Damascus University visited the Consulate General of the People's Republic of China here this afternoon, expressing their support for the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan and the coastal islands.

Ambassador Chen Jiakang and Consul General Shi Gu, who are here to attend the opening ceremony of the Damascus International Fair, received more than thirty university students. The students requested Ambassador Chen Jiakang to convey their respects to the Chinese people and Chairman Mao Zedong. They stated that Arab students are prepared to support the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan and will do their utmost to ensure that the American and British occupying forces immediately withdraw from Lebanon and Jordan.

The students also told Ambassador Chen Jiakang that they are grateful to the Chinese people for their help to the people of Arab countries and expressed their belief in the strength of the Chinese people. They said that the Chinese people will certainly be able to defeat the American imperialists and their running dogs.

Ambassador Chen Jiakang thanked the students for their solidarity with the Chinese people. He pointed out that the current world situation is characterized by the East wind prevailing over the West wind. The struggle of the Chinese people and the Arab people against a common enemy will surely achieve final victory. He said: "The friendship and cooperation between the Arab people and the Chinese people are the best guarantee for maintaining our peace, independence, freedom and prosperity."

### 大马士革的阿拉伯国家学生向陈家康大使表示  阿拉伯学生支援中国人民解放台湾

1958-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    大马士革的阿拉伯国家学生向陈家康大使表示
　　阿拉伯学生支援中国人民解放台湾
    新华社大马士革3日电　大马士革大学的许多阿拉伯国家的学生今天下午访问了这里的中华人民共和国总领事馆，表示支持中国人民为解放台湾和沿海岛屿而进行的斗争。
    前来这里参加大马士革国际博览会开幕典礼的陈家康大使和施谷总领事接见了三十多名大学生。学生们要求陈家康大使向中国人民和毛泽东主席转达他们的敬意。他们表示阿拉伯国家的学生准备支援中国人民解放台湾的斗争，并且将尽他们的力量使美英占领军立即撤出黎巴嫩和约旦。
    学生们还告诉陈家康大使，他们感谢中国人民对阿拉伯国家人民的帮助，并且表示他们相信中国人民的力量。他们说，中国人民肯定能够击溃美帝国主义者和他们的走狗。
    陈家康大使感谢学生们对中国人民的声援。他指出，目前世界局势的特点是东风压倒西风。中国人民和阿拉伯国家人民为反对共同的敌人而进行的斗争必将获得最后胜利。他说：“阿拉伯人民和中国人民之间的友谊和合作是维护我们的和平、独立、自由和繁荣的最好保证。”
