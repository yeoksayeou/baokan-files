Title: Mongolian and Arab United Republic Ambassadors to China Say Progressive Humanity Supports the Chinese People

Author:

Date: 1958-09-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Lubusan, and the Ambassador of the Arab United Republic to China, Rajab, at a reception held this evening at the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, jointly condemned the military provocations carried out by US imperialism in the Taiwan Strait region of our country. The two ambassadors pointed out that all progressive humanity and all peace-loving countries support the just struggle of the Chinese people, Taiwan and all coastal islands have always been Chinese territory, and the People's Republic of China will surely win the final victory.

This reception was held by Ambassador Lubusan to thank the government of the Arab United Republic for its donation to help the earthquake victims of the Mongolian People's Republic. At the reception, Ambassador Lubusan handed a letter of thanks from Zedanbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, to Ambassador Rajab, asking him to forward it to President Nasser.

After their speeches, the two ambassadors also raised their glasses to congratulate the People's Republic of China on its prosperity and victory, and to congratulate the unity of the people of Asian and African countries.

### 蒙古阿联驻我国大使说  进步人类都支持中国人民

1958-09-21
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古阿联驻我国大使说
    进步人类都支持中国人民
    新华社20日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻华大使鲁布桑、阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使拉加卜，今天晚间在蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆举行的一个招待会上，一致谴责美帝国主义在我国台湾海峡地区进行的军事挑衅。两位大使指出，全体进步人类和一切爱好和平的国家都支持中国人民的正义斗争，台湾和所有沿海岛屿一向是中国的土地，中华人民共和国必将获得最后胜利。
    这个招待会是鲁布桑大使为答谢阿拉伯联合共和国政府曾赠款帮助蒙古人民共和国的地震受难者一事而举行的。在招待会上，鲁布桑大使把蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔的一封感谢信递交给拉加卜大使，请他转交纳赛尔总统。
    两位大使在致词后还举杯祝贺中华人民共和国富强和胜利，祝贺亚非各国人民的团结。
