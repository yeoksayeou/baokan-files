Title: List of Delegates from the Sofia City People's Council

Author: 
Date: 1958-09-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. List of delegates from the Sofia City People's Council, capital of Bulgaria:
    Head of Delegation:
    Georgi Kostov - Member of the Executive Committee of the Sofia City People's Council, First Secretary of the Sofia City Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party.
    Members:
    Volko Sivalov - Vice-Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Sofia City People's Council.
    Todor Kochev - Vice-Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Sofia City People's Council.
    Stoil Aleksiev - Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Levski District People's Council in Sofia.
