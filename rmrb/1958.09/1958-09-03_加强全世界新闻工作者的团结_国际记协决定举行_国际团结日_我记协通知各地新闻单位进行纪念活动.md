Title: Strengthen the Unity of Journalists Worldwide: International Association of Journalists Decides to Hold "International Day of Solidarity"; China Journalists Association Notifies News Organizations in Various Regions to Conduct Commemorative Activities
Author:
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd:

　　Strengthen the Unity of Journalists Worldwide
　　International Association of Journalists Decides to Hold "International Day of Solidarity"
    China Journalists Association Notifies News Organizations in Various Regions to Conduct Commemorative Activities
    Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: The All-China Journalists Association issued a notice on September 2nd regarding the holding of International Day of Solidarity as follows:
    The Fourth Congress of the International Organization of Journalists, held in Bucharest in May this year, passed a resolution calling on journalists around the world to hold an "International Day of Solidarity." The resolution pointed out: "To express sympathy for journalists persecuted for their work in the cause of peace and mutual understanding among peoples, the International Organization of Journalists calls on journalists around the world to set aside a day of solidarity and donate all or part of that day's wages to the International Solidarity Fund." The Secretariat of the International Organization of Journalists issued a notice on June 26th, deciding that September 8th of this year would be Solidarity Day. This day is the anniversary of the death of Comrade Julius Fucik.
    The Standing Council of the All-China Journalists Association recently held a meeting to discuss activities and fundraising for International Day of Solidarity. The association believes that this activity initiated by the International Organization of Journalists is of great significance for strengthening the unity of peace-loving journalists of all countries and for supporting the struggle of journalists against colonialism and for national independence. The All-China Journalists Association hopes that newspapers, news agencies, and radio stations at the central level and in various provinces (autonomous regions), municipalities, and province-administered cities will publish and broadcast news, articles, and materials related to International Day of Solidarity before September 8th to publicize the significance of Solidarity Day; it hopes that journalists' associations in various provinces (autonomous regions), municipalities, and province-administered cities will organize commemorative activities according to circumstances and call on news workers to donate all or part of their day's wages to the "International Solidarity Fund." The above-mentioned commemorative activities will mainly be carried out in news organizations in various provinces (autonomous regions), municipalities, and province-administered cities. In places where no journalists' association has been established, the local provincial (autonomous region), municipal, and province-administered city party newspaper should consult with various news organizations to handle the matter.

### 加强全世界新闻工作者的团结  国际记协决定举行“国际团结日”  我记协通知各地新闻单位进行纪念活动

1958-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　加强全世界新闻工作者的团结
　　国际记协决定举行“国际团结日”
    我记协通知各地新闻单位进行纪念活动
    新华社2日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会2日发出关于举行国际团结日的通知如下：
    今年5月间在布加勒斯特举行的国际新闻工作者协会第四届代表大会，通过了号召全世界的新闻工作者举行“国际团结日”的决议。决议指出：“为表示对因从事争取和平和人民的相互了解而受迫害的新闻工作者的同情，国际新闻工作者协会号召全世界新闻工作者定出一个团结日，把这一天的工资的全部或一部分捐作国际团结基金。”国际新闻工作者协会秘书处在6月26日发出通知，决定今年9月8日为团结日。这一天是尤利乌斯·伏契克同志遇难的纪念日。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会常务理事会最近举行会议，讨论了关于国际团结日的活动和募捐问题。协会认为国际新闻工作者协会倡议的这次活动，对加强各国爱好和平的新闻工作者的团结，对支持反对殖民主义、争取民族独立的新闻工作者的斗争，有很重大的意义。中华全国新闻工作者协会希望中央级和各省（自治区）、市及省辖市的报纸、通讯社和广播电台，在9月8日以前登载和广播有关国际团结日的消息、文章和资料，宣传团结日的意义；希望各省（自治区）、市新闻工作者协会根据情况组织纪念活动，并号召新闻工作人员为“国际团结基金”捐出他一天的工资的全部或一部分。上述纪念活动主要是在各省（自治区）、市及省辖市的新闻单位中进行。没有成立新闻工作者协会的地方，由当地的省（自治区）、市及省辖市的党报同各新闻单位商量办理。
