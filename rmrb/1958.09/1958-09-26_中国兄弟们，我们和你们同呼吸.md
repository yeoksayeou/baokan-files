Title: Chinese Brothers, We Breathe With You
Author: Ai'erwa Fabuleijiasai (爱尔娃·法布莱加斯)
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 8

    Chinese Brothers, We Breathe With You
    Argentine Poet Ai'erwa Fabuleijiasai

    The whole world is listening to the voice of the Chinese people: Liberate Taiwan, "This is the sacred and inviolable right of the Chinese people."① On the mainland of China, the Five-Star Red Flag flutters in the wind. The angry tide of the Chinese people will surely destroy the American aggressors' intimidating fortresses and shatter the paper tiger that is strong in appearance but weak in reality. These shameless robbers occupy your islands.

    The people want peace! Peace, I sing praises to you! Tranquil peace! You have been ravaged! Taiwan, laden with golden bananas, is like a silver pearl, and your brother islands: Jinmen, Mazu, and the Penghu Islands. Crows② invade your airspace and muddy your seawater. Stormy islands, a vast white mist shrouds your deep bays, and red flying birds soar on those steep mountains. Taiwan, with its hot summers, has vast primeval forests, a fertile land, growing rice, tea, sugar cane, and delicious fresh fruits; rich in gold and oil.

    Taiwan, you have more than nine million people, dear maritime province, you are a part of the People's China, and we wish you liberation soon. The masses of the people defend you, advancing by trampling on the weapons of the invaders; you will always be the people's island, your airspace and territorial waters belong to the people. Asia, Africa, Latin America—the whole world—unite and utter a united and just roar: Liberate Taiwan, "This is the sacred and inviolable right of the Chinese people."

    Taiwan, in your bosom, a heart beats for the free people, it is the heart of the motherland, the people of the motherland. "The Chinese, who account for one-quarter of humanity, have stood up from now on."③ The heroic people are masters of their own house. Our people's voice, the voice of liberating Taiwan, shakes the heavens and the earth.

    Long live People's China! Victory belongs to truth! The sacred and inviolable right of the Chinese people: Liberating Taiwan will surely succeed!

    September 22, 1958, Beijing

    [Note] ① Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait area
    ② Refers to the US imperialist planes
    ③ Mao Zedong's words in the opening speech of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (Translated by Tang Bosheng and Wang Huaizu)

### 中国兄弟们，我们和你们同呼吸
爱尔娃·法布莱加斯
1958-09-26
第8版()
专栏：

    中国兄弟们，我们和你们同呼吸
    阿根廷诗人  爱尔娃·法布莱加斯全世界都在倾听中国人民的呼声：解放台湾，“这是中国人民的神圣不可侵犯的权利。”①在中国的大陆上，五星红旗迎风招展。中国人民的怒潮一定要击毁美国侵略者唬人的堡垒，粉碎外强中干的纸老虎。这些无耻强盗盘据着你们的海岛。人民要和平！和平，我歌颂你！恬静的和平啊！你遭到了蹂躏！金色香蕉累累的台湾像一颗银色的珍珠，还有你的兄弟海岛：金门、马祖、澎湖列岛，乌鸦②侵犯着你们的领空，搅浑你的海水。暴风骤雨的海岛，茫茫白雾笼罩着你深深的海湾，在那些峻峭的高山上，红色的飞鸟翱翔。夏日炎炎的台湾，有着辽阔的原始森林，一片肥沃的土地，生长着稻米、茶叶、甘蔗，和美味可口的鲜果；蕴藏着丰富的黄金和石油。台湾，你有九百多万人口，亲爱的海上之省，你是人民中国的一部分，我们愿你早日解放。人民群众保卫你，要践踏着侵略者的武器前进；你永远是人民的海岛，你的领空、领海属于人民。亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲——全世界——都团结起来，同声齐发正义的吼声：解放台湾，“这是中国人民的神圣不可侵犯的权利。”台湾，在你的胸膛里，一颗心为自由的人民而跳动，这是祖国的心，祖国的人民。“占人类总数四分之一的中国人从此站立起来了。”英雄的人民自己当家作主。我们人民的呼声，解放台湾的呼声，惊天动地。人民中国万岁！胜利属于真理！中国人民的神圣不可侵犯的权利：解放台湾，一定成功！
    1958年9月22日  北京
    〔注〕①周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明
    ②指美帝的飞机
    ③毛主席在中国人民政协第一届全体会议的开幕词中语（汤柏生、王怀祖译）
