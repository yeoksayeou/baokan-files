Title: New Folk Song: Singing Makes the Sheep Cover the Hills
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 4

New Folk Song
Sing songs, just sing songs,
Sing thousands of flowers;
Sing milk like flowing water,
Sing sheep covering the hills.
              —Xinjiang

### 新民歌  唱得羊儿满山坡

1958-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    新民歌
    唱得羊儿满山坡说唱歌，就唱歌，唱得花儿千万朵；唱得牛奶似流水，唱得羊儿满山坡。
              —新疆
