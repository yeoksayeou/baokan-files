Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the nine-member delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by its head, Comrade Cherednichenko, a member of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by jet airliner on the afternoon of the 27th to attend the National Day celebrations in our country and pay a visit.

The delegation includes painters, writers, poets, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, heroes of socialist labor, academicians of the Academy of Sciences, agronomists, and female workers. The members also include heads of local organizations of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and the editor-in-chief of the "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" magazine.

Gao Baoquan and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Jinghua, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Wang Zhijie, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 苏中友好协会代表团抵京

1958-09-28
第3版()
专栏：

    苏中友好协会代表团抵京
    新华社27日讯  应中苏友好协会总会邀请前来参加我国国庆观礼和进行访问的苏中友好协会代表团一行九人，由团长、苏中友好协会理事会理事、苏联铁路工会中央委员会主席契列德尼钦科率领，27日下午乘喷气式客机到达北京。
    代表团的成员中有画家、作家、诗人、苏联最高苏维埃代表、社会主义劳动英雄、科学院院士、农艺师和女工，成员中还有苏中友好协会地方组织的负责人和“苏中友好”杂志的总编辑。
    中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权、李沾吾，北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华，中国铁路工会全国委员会主席王志杰等前往机场欢迎。
