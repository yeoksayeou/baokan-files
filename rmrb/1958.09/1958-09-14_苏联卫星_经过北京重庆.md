Title: Soviet Satellite Passes Over Beijing and Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 2

    Soviet Satellite
    Passes Over Beijing and Chongqing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: According to TASS, the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite and its carrier rocket will be visible from Chongqing and Beijing on the 15th and 16th, respectively.
    On the 15th, at 6:27 AM Beijing time, the satellite will pass over Rangoon, at 6:30 AM it will pass over the southwestern sky of Chongqing, and at 6:35 AM it will pass over Beijing. At 5:15 AM, the carrier rocket will pass over the southwestern sky of Beijing. On the 16th, at 4:31 AM, the rocket will pass over Beijing again, appearing in the due south direction. Areas near the satellite's orbit south of 29 degrees north latitude in our country, and areas near the rocket's orbit between 33 and 45 degrees north latitude, will be able to see it.

### 苏联卫星  经过北京重庆

1958-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联卫星
    经过北京重庆
    据新华社13日讯　据塔斯社消息：重庆和北京15日、16日可分别看到苏联第三个人造卫星和它的运载火箭。
    15日北京时间六点二十七分卫星将经过仰光，六点三十分经过重庆西南方上空，六点三十五分经过北京。五点十五分运载火箭将经过北京西南方上空。16日四点三十一分火箭再次经过北京，在正南方向出现。我国北纬二十九度以南卫星运行路线附近地区，北纬三十三度至四十五度间火箭运行路线附近地区都可看到。
