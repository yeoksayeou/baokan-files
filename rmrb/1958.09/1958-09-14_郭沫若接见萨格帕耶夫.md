Title: Guo Moruo Receives Sagatpayev
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Sagatpayev, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic, Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and member of the Soviet Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection Delegation, on the afternoon of September 13th.

### 郭沫若接见萨格帕耶夫

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见萨格帕耶夫
    新华社13日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若13日下午接见了苏联哈萨克共和国科学院院长、苏联科学院院士、苏联地质和矿藏保护部代表团团员萨格帕耶夫。
