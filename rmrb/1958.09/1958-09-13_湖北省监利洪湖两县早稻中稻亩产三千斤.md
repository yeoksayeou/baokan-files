Title: Hubei's Jianli and Honghu Counties Achieve 3,000 Jin per Mu Yield of Early and Mid-Season Rice
Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 11th: After being awarded the title of "Thousand Jin County" for early rice, Jianli and Honghu counties in Hubei Province have achieved a large-scale bumper harvest of over 2,000 jin per mu on their 480,000+ mu of mid-season rice.

According to the assessment, Jianli County's 329,473 mu of mid-season rice yielded an average of 2,316 jin per mu, an increase of more than five times compared to last year's yield of 336 jin per mu. Honghu County's 160,049 mu of mid-season rice yielded an average of 2,077 jin per mu, an increase of more than three times compared to last year's yield.

### 湖北省监利洪湖两县早稻中稻亩产三千斤

1958-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    湖北省监利洪湖两县早稻中稻亩产三千斤
    据新华社武汉11日电　湖北省监利、洪湖两县在获得早稻千斤县的称号后，两县的四十八万多亩中稻又获得平均亩产二千多斤的大面积丰收。
    根据验收：监利县三十二万九千四百七十三亩中稻，平均亩产二千三百一十六斤，比去年的亩产三百三十六斤增加五倍多。洪湖县十六万零四十九亩中稻，平均亩产二千零七十七斤，比去年亩产量增加三倍多。
