Title: Indian Defense Minister Menon Says It Is Necessary to Accelerate the Return of Taiwan to China
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch from London: Indian Defense Minister Menon said in London on the 12th that the unification of China and Taiwan must be accelerated. He pointed out that "a part of China is still not under the legitimate control of China"; the Chinese shelling of Jinmen is one of the results of "the division of China."

Menon was in London en route to New York to attend the 13th session of the United Nations General Assembly. After arriving in London, he said in response to questions about the situation in the Far East that "the danger of war certainly exists."

Before arriving in London, Menon said while passing through Rome that India has always believed that Taiwan is an indivisible part of China and that the Chinese government represents all the Chinese people.

Menon welcomed the resumption of Sino-US ambassadorial talks to improve the situation. He also said, "But we believe that this improvement can only be lasting after an agreement is reached between the directly concerned parties (China and the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan) through direct negotiations."

Menon expressed the hope that China would regain its legitimate seat in the United Nations.

### 印度国防部长梅农说  必须加速使台湾归还中国

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    印度国防部长梅农说
    必须加速使台湾归还中国
    新华社13日讯　伦敦消息：印度国防部长梅农12日在伦敦说，必须加速中国和台湾的统一。他指出，“中国的一部分还不在中国的合法控制之下”；中国炮击金门一事是使“中国分裂的结果之一”。
    梅农在前往纽约参加联合国大会第十三届会议途中路过伦敦。他在到达伦敦以后回答有人提出的有关远东局势的问题时说，“战争的危险肯定是存在的”。
    梅农在到达伦敦以前路过罗马时说：印度一向认为，台湾是中国的不可分割的一部分，同时中国政府代表着全部中国人民。
    梅农表示欢迎恢复中美大使级会谈，来改善局势。他还说，“但是我们认为，只有直接有关的双方（中国和台湾蒋介石集团）之间用直接谈判的方法达成协议以后，这种改善才可以长久。”
    梅农表示希望恢复中国在联合国中的合法席位。
