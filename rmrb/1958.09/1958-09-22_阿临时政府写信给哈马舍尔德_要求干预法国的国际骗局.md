Title: Algerian Provisional Government Writes to Hammarskjöld Demanding Intervention in France's International Swindle
Author:
Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, September 21st, New York Dispatch: The representative of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria to the United States, Chanderli, announced that the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria has written to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, protesting France's imposition of a referendum on the de Gaulle dictatorial constitution on the Algerian people.
    The letter once again drew the attention of the United Nations Secretary-General to this unilateral and arbitrary decision by the French government, stating: The Algerian people and government condemn this international deception, and they will oppose it with all their strength.
    The letter pointed out: "The United Nations cannot remain indifferent to this act, which is about to be staged in Algeria and constitutes a blatant attack on the most basic democratic principles."
    The letter concluded, "If the planned 'referendum' really takes place in Algeria, it will cause serious consequences, for all of which France should bear full responsibility."

### 阿临时政府写信给哈马舍尔德  要求干预法国的国际骗局

1958-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    阿临时政府写信给哈马舍尔德
    要求干预法国的国际骗局
    新华社21日讯　纽约消息：阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府驻美国代表香德利宣布，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府已经写信给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，抗议法国把关于戴高乐独裁宪法的公民投票强加在阿尔及利亚人民身上。
    这封信再次提请联合国秘书长注意法国政府这个单方面和独断独行的决定，信中说：阿尔及利亚人民和政府谴责这种国际欺骗，他们将用他们所有的一切力量来反对。
    信件指出：“联合国不能对这个将要在阿尔及利亚上演的、构成对最基本的民主原则的粗暴进攻的行为毫不关心”。
    这封信最后说，“如果计划中的‘公民投票’真的在阿尔及利亚进行，就会引起严重的后果，所有这些后果应该由法国负全部责任”。
