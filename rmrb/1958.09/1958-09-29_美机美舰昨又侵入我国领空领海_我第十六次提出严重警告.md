Title: U.S. Planes and Warships Again Invade Our Country's Airspace and Territorial Waters Yesterday; Our 16th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- On September 28th, twenty-two U.S. fighter planes successively intruded into the airspace over the territorial waters of the Xiamen region of Fujian Province, and five U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Fujian region of our country. A spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare: The Chinese government lodges its sixteenth serious warning against this grave military provocation by the United States.

### 美机美舰昨又侵入我国领空领海  我第十六次提出严重警告

1958-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    美机美舰昨又侵入我国领空领海
    我第十六次提出严重警告
    新华社28日讯  9月28日美国战斗飞机二十二架先后侵入我国福建厦门地区的领海上空，美国军舰五艘侵入我国福建地区的领海，我外交部发言人奉命声明：对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第十六次提出严重警告。
