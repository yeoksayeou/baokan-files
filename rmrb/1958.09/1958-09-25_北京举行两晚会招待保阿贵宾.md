Title: Beijing Holds Two Evening Parties to Entertain Bulgarian and Albanian Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress hosted a Peking Opera performance tonight to entertain the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation headed by Comrade Chervenkov and the Sofia City People's Council Delegation headed by Comrade Kostov.

Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, among others, accompanied the distinguished guests to watch the opera. Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, was also invited to attend the evening party.

At the party, the distinguished guests watched "Hunting the Tiger," performed by famous Peking Opera actors from the capital. At the end of the performance, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen and others accompanied the distinguished guests onto the stage, shook hands with the Chinese actors one by one, and took a group photo as a memento.

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Marshal Peng Dehuai said at tonight's banquet welcoming the Albanian Military Delegation to China: The people and armed forces of China and Albania are closely united under the glorious banner of Marxism-Leninism and, together with the peace-loving people of the world, will fight together and surely defeat imperialism.

The entire Albanian Military Delegation, headed by First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, General Baluku, attended tonight's banquet. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Comrades-in-Arms Cultural Troupe gave a wonderful performance for the distinguished guests.

After the performance, General Baluku, on behalf of the Albanian People's Army, presented flags and gifts to the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

After accepting the gifts, Marshal Peng Dehuai delivered a speech in which he expressed the highest respect on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the heroic Albanian People's Army.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to the evening party were also General Xiao Hua, General Zhang Zongxun, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, Rear Admiral Yuan Ye, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, among others.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, and his wife were also invited to attend the evening party.

### 北京举行两晚会招待保阿贵宾

1958-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    北京举行两晚会招待保阿贵宾
    据新华社23日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会今晚举办京剧晚会，招待以契尔文科夫团长为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团和以科斯托夫为首的索非亚市人民会议代表团。
    李济深、彭真、陈叔通副委员长等人陪同贵宾们观剧。保加利亚驻华大使潘切夫斯基也应邀出席晚会。
    晚会上，贵宾们观看了首都著名京剧演员演出的“猎虎记”。演出结束时，贵宾们由彭真副委员长等陪同走上舞台，同中国演员一一握手，合影留念。
    新华社24日讯  彭德怀元帅在今天欢迎阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团的晚会上说：中阿两国人民及其武装力量紧密地团结在马列主义的光辉旗帜下，同世界爱好和平的人民一道，共同奋斗，一定能打败帝国主义。
    以部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库上将为首的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团全体人员，参加了今天的晚会。中国人民解放军战友文工团为贵宾们作了精彩演出。
    在演出结束后，巴卢库上将代表阿尔巴尼亚人民军向中国人民解放军赠旗和赠礼。
    彭德怀元帅在接受礼品后讲了话，他代表中国人民解放军全体官兵向英勇的阿尔巴尼亚人民军致以崇高的敬意。
    陪同贵宾们参加晚会的，还有萧华上将，张宗逊上将，萧向荣中将，常乾坤空军中将，杨秀山中将，袁也烈海军少将和外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里及夫人也应邀参加了晚会。
