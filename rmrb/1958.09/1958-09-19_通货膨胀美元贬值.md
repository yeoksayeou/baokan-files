Inflation Devalues the Dollar

The "U.S. News & World Report" in its August 22nd issue stated that the U.S. government, in order to compensate for deficits, is issuing large amounts of banknotes, resulting in serious inflation and the declining purchasing power of the dollar. The magazine said: As a result of the massive devaluation of the dollar, the current purchasing power of the dollar is one-third less than after World War II, and does not even reach half its 1939 value. (Reported by our newspaper)

### 通货膨胀美元贬值

1958-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    通货膨胀美元贬值
    8月22日一期的“美国新闻与世界报道”说，美国政府为了弥补亏空，大量发行钞票，结果引起严重的通货膨胀，美元购买力日益下降。这家杂志说：美元大量贬值的结果，目前美元的购买力，比第二次世界大战以后减少了三分之一，还达不到1939年的一半价值。（本报讯）
