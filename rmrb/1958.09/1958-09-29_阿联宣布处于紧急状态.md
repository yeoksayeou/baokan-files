Title: The UAR Declares a State of Emergency
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 27th: President Nasser of the United Arab Republic announced tonight that the UAR is in a state of emergency.
    Earlier this evening, the President promulgated a decree declaring a state of emergency in the UAR, replacing the emergency decrees separately issued by Syria and Egypt in 1949.

### 阿联宣布处于紧急状态

1958-09-29
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联宣布处于紧急状态
    新华社开罗27日电  阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔今天晚上宣布阿联处于紧急状态。
    总统在今晚早些时候颁布了关于宣布阿联处于紧急状态的法令，用来代替叙利亚和埃及在1949年分别发布的紧急法令。
