Title: Vice Chairman Lin Receives Bulgarian Guests
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Vice Chairman Lin Boqu received this evening the entire Bulgarian National Assembly delegation headed by Comrade Chervenkov and the Sofia City People's Assembly delegation headed by Comrade Kostov.

    Present at the reception were Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, and others.

    Also present at the reception was Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevsky.

### 林副委员长接见保加利亚贵宾

1958-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    林副委员长接见保加利亚贵宾
    新华社22日讯　林伯渠副委员长今天晚间接见了以契尔文科夫团长为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团和以科斯托夫团长为首的索非亚市人民会议代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有李济深、黄炎培副委员长，外交部姬鹏飞副部长，北京市冯基平副市长等人。
    接见时，保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基也在座。
