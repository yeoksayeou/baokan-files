Title: The CPC Central Committee and the Council of Ministers Made a Decision: Gas Production to Increase Fourfold in Seven Years; Construction of an Iron Mine with an Annual Output of 12 Million Tons of Ore is Being Accelerated
Author:
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 1st: Newspapers here published the resolution of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on further developing the Soviet gas industry. The resolution stipulates that by 1965, the end of the Seven-Year Plan (1959-1965), the Soviet gas extraction and production volume should increase from 31 billion cubic meters this year to 150 billion cubic meters, an increase of more than four times.

    In order to ensure the high-speed development of the gas industry, the CPC Central Committee and the Soviet Council of Ministers decided to strengthen the exploration of natural gas producing areas. The resolution stipulates that in the next seven years, the amount of deep-well drilling in this area will reach 15 million meters, so that the Soviet Union's industrial gas reserves (recoverable reserves) will increase by 304 billion cubic meters in seven years.

    The resolution stipulates that an additional 26,000 kilometers of gas pipelines will be built during the Seven-Year Plan to ensure gas supply to cities, settlements, and industrial enterprises throughout the Soviet Union.

    The resolution emphasized that further accelerating the development of the gas industry is an important condition for further developing Soviet productivity and improving the material and cultural living standards of the Soviet people.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 2nd: The Soviet Union is building the country's largest iron mine in Krivoy Rog, with an annual output of 12 million tons of iron ore.

    This mine includes two inclined shafts for transporting ore, eight vertical shafts, and two entrances for miners to go up and down. The construction of the vertical shafts has already begun. In addition, concentrators, mechanical plants, and workers' living areas will be built above the mine.

    This large mine will be fully mechanized. The mine's ventilation system can deliver 1,000 cubic meters of fresh air into the mine every minute.

### 苏共中央和部长会议作出决定  七年内煤气产量增加四倍  年产矿砂一千二百万吨的铁矿正在加速建设

1958-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和部长会议作出决定
　　七年内煤气产量增加四倍
    年产矿砂一千二百万吨的铁矿正在加速建设
    新华社莫斯科1日电　此间报纸发表了苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议关于进一步发展苏联瓦斯工业的决议。决议规定，在七年计划（1959年—1965年）结束时的1965年，苏联瓦斯开采量和生产量要从今年的三百一十亿立方公尺增加到一千五百亿立方公尺，即增加为四倍以上。
    为了保证瓦斯工业能够高速度发展，苏共中央和苏联部长会议决定加强对天然瓦斯产地的勘探工作。决议规定在今后七年间使这方面的深井钻探工作量达到一千五百万公尺，以便使得苏联瓦斯的工业储藏量（即可开采量）在七年中增加三万零四百亿立方公尺。
    决议规定在七年计划期间再建设两万六千公里长的瓦斯输送管，以便保证对苏联各地城市、居民点和工业企业的瓦斯供应。
    决议强调指出，进一步加速发展瓦斯工业，是进一步发展苏联生产力和提高苏联人民的物质和文化生活水平的一个重要条件。
    新华社莫斯科2日电　苏联正在克里沃罗格建立一个全国最大的年产一千二百万吨铁矿砂的铁矿。
    这个矿井包括两个运矿砂的斜井、八个竖井和两个供矿工上下用的井口。竖井的建设工程已经开始。此外，在矿井上面还要建立选矿厂、机械厂以及工人区等建筑。
    这个大矿井将是完全机械化的。矿井的通风装置每分钟可向井内输送一千立方公尺新鲜空气。
