Title: Premier Zhou Meets Canadian Legal Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the head of the Canadian legal workers delegation, Edward B. Joliffe, and the delegation's secretary, Norman A. Endicott.

Present at the meeting was Wu Defeng, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association.

### 周总理接见加法律代表团

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见加法律代表团
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了加拿大法律工作者代表团团长爱德华·B·乔里费和代表团秘书诺尔曼·A·恩地柯特。
    接见时在座的有中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰。
