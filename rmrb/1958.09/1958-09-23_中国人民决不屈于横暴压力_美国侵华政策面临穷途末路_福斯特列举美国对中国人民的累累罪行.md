Title: The Chinese People Will Never Yield to Brutal Pressure; American Aggression Policy Against China Faces a Dead End; Foster Lists the Numerous Crimes of the United States Against the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1958-09-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 20th: American Communist Party Honorary Chairman Foster published an article in the "Worker's Weekly" saying, "America's self-appointed role as a gendarme suppressing the People's China is rapidly approaching its end." "This act of aggression is the ugliest act of one country invading another."

Foster continued, saying that American aggression against China is carried out in several aspects, each of which is a "serious infringement by American imperialism on the dignity and happiness of the Chinese people."

Foster listed the following criminal acts of the United States against the People's Republic of China:

First, the United States presumptuously delimits the boundaries of the Chinese revolution, which is an insult to the Chinese people. Wall Street arrogantly declared to the Chinese people that the doomed Chiang Kai-shek is the legitimate ruler of the Chinese people and prevents the Chinese people from driving this robber out of Taiwan.

Second, the United States sent its Seventh Fleet to the Taiwan Strait in order to maintain an armed fortress directly in the harbors of Chinese cities.

Third, the United States does not recognize the legitimate government of China and instigates some puppet governments that it keeps to carry out this political nonsense.

Fourth, the United States provokes an economic boycott against the People's China.

Fifth, the United States unreasonably disregards the twelve-nautical-mile territorial water limit stipulated by the People's China and sends warships to invade China's territorial waters.

Sixth, the United States obstructs the People's China from entering the United Nations, so that a country accounting for more than a quarter of the world's population has no representation in this world organization.

Seventh, the United States threatens China with atomic war.

Foster said, but "the despicable system of economic, political, and military resistance that the United States has established against China is bound to collapse. The Chinese people have long since torn this system to pieces."

Foster pointed out that the People's Republic of China has achieved unprecedented achievements in the economy and other aspects. He said, "It would be most foolish to imagine that a people who have recognized their own strength will succumb to the current brutal pressure of American imperialism. More and more people around the world recognize the actual situation in the New China. The Eisenhower-Dulles policy of trying to force a free China back into a state of slavery is unlikely to gain sympathy anywhere." Many prominent politicians in the United States are now also condemning Eisenhower and Dulles' policy toward Jinmen and Mazu.

### 中国人民决不屈于横暴压力  美国侵华政策面临穷途末路  福斯特列举美国对中国人民的累累罪行

1958-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民决不屈于横暴压力
    美国侵华政策面临穷途末路
    福斯特列举美国对中国人民的累累罪行
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社纽约20日讯：美国共产党名誉主席福斯特在“工人周报”上发表文章说，“美国自封为镇压人民中国的宪兵角色很快就接近他们的末日”。“这种侵犯行为是一个国家侵略另外一个国家的最丑恶的行为。”
    福斯特接着说，美国对中国的侵略是沿着几个方面进行的，每一个方面都是“美帝国主义对中国人民的尊严和幸福的严重侵犯”。
    福斯特列举了美国对中华人民共和国的下列罪恶行动：
    一，美国妄自划定中国革命的界限，这是对中国人民的侮辱。华尔街狂妄地擅自向中国人民宣布，注定灭亡的蒋介石是中国人民的合法执政者，并且阻止中国人民把这个强盗赶出台湾。
    二，美国把它的第七舰队派到台湾海峡，为的是直接在中国城市的港湾中保持武装堡垒。
    三，美国不承认中国的合法政府，并且唆使受它豢养的一些傀儡政府来进行这种政治上的胡作非为。
    四，美国挑唆对人民中国实行经济抵制。
    五，美国无理地无视人民中国所规定的十二海里的领水界限，并且派遣军舰侵犯中国的领海。
    六，美国阻挠人民中国进入联合国，使得占地球上四分之一以上的人口的国家在这个世界性的组织中没有代表。
    七，美国用原子战争威胁中国。
    福斯特说，但是“美国对中国所建立的经济、政治和军事抵制的卑鄙的体系必然崩溃。中国人民早已把这个体系摧毁得四分五裂”。
    福斯特指出，中华人民共和国在经济和其他方面取得了空前的成就，他说，“如果妄以为认识了自己的力量的人民会屈服于美帝国主义目前的横暴压力，那就是最愚蠢不过的了。全世界越来越多的人认识到新中国的实际情况。企图迫使自由的中国重新陷入奴役地位的艾森豪威尔—杜勒斯政策很难在什么地方得到同情”。美国现在也有很多著名的政治家谴责艾森豪威尔和杜勒斯对金门和马祖的政策。
