Title: CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session Decides to Convene Party Congress in January Next Year
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 7th, via TASS: On September 5th, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a plenary session, which decided to convene the 21st (Extraordinary) Congress of the CPSU on January 27, 1959.

The plenary session removed Nikolai Bulganin from his position as a member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee.

The resolution of the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on convening the 21st (Extraordinary) Congress of the CPSU is as follows:

I. To convene the 21st (Extraordinary) Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on January 27, 1959, with the agenda being: "Control Figures for the Development of the Soviet National Economy for 1959-1965."

To designate Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, as the speaker.

To publish in the newspapers the outline of the report on the control figures for the development of the Soviet national economy for 1959-1965.

II. To determine the number of delegates to the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union as follows: one delegate with voting rights for every six thousand members, and one delegate with speaking rights for every six thousand probationary members.

III. According to the Party Charter, delegates to the Party Congress shall be elected by secret ballot (anonymous voting). Delegates from Party organizations in the states, territories, and autonomous republics of the Russian Federation shall be elected at the Party conferences of the respective states and territories. Delegates from other union republics to the Party Congress shall be elected by the Central Committees of the Communist Parties of the respective union republics at the state party conferences or at the congresses of the Communist Parties of the union republics.

Members of the Party organizations in the Soviet Army and Navy, the Soviet Public Security Guard, and the Border Troops shall, together with other Party organizations, elect delegates to the 21st Party Congress at the state, territorial, or union republic Party conferences.

Members of the Party organizations in the Soviet Army and Navy units stationed abroad shall elect delegates to the 21st Party Congress at the Party conferences of their respective army corps.

IV. In December 1958 and the first half of January 1959, state and territorial Party conferences and congresses of the Communist Parties of the union republics shall be held to elect delegates to the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and to discuss the outline of the report on the control figures for the development of the Soviet national economy for 1959-1965.

### 苏共中央全会决定  明年一月召开党代表大会

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏共中央全会决定
　　明年一月召开党代表大会
    据新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：9月5日苏联共产党中央委员会举行了全体会议，会议决定于1959年1月27日召开苏共第二十一次（非常）代表大会。
    全会取消了尼古拉·布尔加宁苏共中央主席团委员的资格。
    苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议关于召开苏共第二十一次（非常）代表大会的决议如下：
    一、定于1959年1月27日召开苏联共产党第二十一次（非常）代表大会，议程是：“1959年—1965年发展苏联国民经济的控制数字”。
    确定苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志为报告人。
    在报上公布关于1959年—1965年发展苏联国民经济控制数字的报告提纲。
    二、确定出席苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的代表名额如下：每六千名党员产生一名有表决权的代表，每六千名预备党员产生一名有发言权的代表。
    三、根据党章，出席党代表大会的代表用不记名投票（秘密投票）方式选出。俄罗斯联邦各州、边疆区和自治共和国党组织的代表在各州、边疆区党代表会议上选出。其他加盟共和国出席党代表大会的代表，由各加盟共和国共产党中央委员会自行决定在州的党代表会议上或是在加盟共和国共产党代表大会上选出。
    苏联陆海军、苏联公安卫护部队和边防部队党组织中的党员同其余党组织一起在州、边疆区党代表会议或加盟共和国共产党代表大会上选举出席第二十一次党代表大会的代表。
    驻在国外的苏联陆海军部队党组织中的党员在各该军团的党代表会议上选举出席第二十一次党代表大会的代表。
    四、1958年12月和1959年1月上半月举行各州、边疆区党代表会议和加盟共和国共产党代表大会来选举出席苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的代表，并讨论关于1959年—1965年发展苏联国民经济的控制数字的报告提纲。
