Title: Hailar to Build Iron and Steel Complex
Author:
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 30th: The Hailar Iron and Steel Complex broke ground on the 26th. When fully completed, it will be able to produce 150,000 to 190,000 tons of iron and 100,000 tons of steel annually.

　　It will be built in two phases. The first phase includes two 55-cubic-meter blast furnaces, which will be completed and put into production in December this year. The second phase includes two 100-cubic-meter blast furnaces, a steelmaking converter, and mechanical and rolling workshops, which will be completed next year.

　　Most of the machinery and equipment needed for the Hailar Iron and Steel Complex will be manufactured and installed by local factories.

### 海拉尔兴建钢铁联合企业

1958-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　海拉尔兴建钢铁联合企业
    新华社呼和浩特30日电　海拉尔钢铁联合企业26日开工兴建。它全部建成以后，每年能产铁十五到十九万吨，钢十万吨。
    它将分两期建成。第一期工程有两座五十五立方公尺的高炉，在今年12月建成并投入生产；第二期工程包括两个一百立方公尺的高炉、一个炼钢转炉和机械、轧钢车间等，将在明年建成。
    海拉尔钢铁联合企业所需要的绝大部分机械设备，都由当地有关工厂承制安装。
