Title: Hebei Accelerates Mass Armed Movement; Tianjin Citizens Resolve to Contribute All Their Strength to Liberating Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, September 7th: Over 1.5 million people in Tianjin held a grand rally and demonstration today, supporting our government's statement and opposing the war threats made by U.S. imperialism against our people.

　　Today, the main streets of the city and the rally venue were transformed into an ocean of anger, with people shouting slogans such as "Support Premier Zhou's Statement," "We Must Liberate Taiwan," and "We Must Liberate Jinmen and Matsu."

　　Lin Tie, First Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke first at the rally. He said: The 45 million people of Hebei Province firmly support and endorse Premier Zhou Enlai's statement, resolutely oppose the military threats and acts of aggression by U.S. imperialism, and we are determined to mobilize all forces to resolutely fight U.S. imperialism's acts of aggression to the end.

　　Lin Tie called on the people of the province to immediately mobilize, launch a massive mass movement to support our government's statement and oppose U.S. military threats, further initiate a new upsurge in industrial and agricultural production and construction, resolutely strive for the great victory on the steel front and the grain production front, fully complete the first step of establishing people's communes, and rapidly launch a mass armed movement to contribute all our strength to the great task of liberating our territory of Taiwan and the Penghu Islands.

　　Li Gengtao, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Tianjin, Yu Zhiyuan, Chairman of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, Yang Yizhou, Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Peng Qing, Director of the Hebei Provincial Women's Federation, and others unanimously stated in their speeches: The city is determined to continue its efforts on the basis of the victory of the Great Leap Forward, and is determined to actively participate in the just struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and for the liberation of the motherland's territory of Taiwan with practical actions.

　　Representatives of workers, peasants, and students unanimously expressed their determination to support the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions in their speeches. Qiu Guanghe, a worker at Tianjin Steel Plant and a municipal-level model worker, said: If U.S. imperialism dares to invade our territory, we will use our iron fists to severely strike them and turn their warships into the invaders' own coffins.

　　Over 1.5 million people in the city held a grand demonstration parade after the rally.

### 河北加速开展全民武装运动  天津市民决心为解放台湾贡献一切力量

1958-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　河北加速开展全民武装运动
    天津市民决心为解放台湾贡献一切力量
    据新华社天津7日电　天津市一百五十多万人7日举行了空前盛大的集会和游行示威，拥护我国政府声明，反对美帝国主义向我国人民进行战争威胁。
    今天，全市主要街道和大会会场被人流汇成愤怒的海洋，人们高呼着“拥护周总理声明”、“一定要解放台湾”、“一定要解放金门、马祖”等口号。
    中共河北省委第一书记林铁在大会上首先讲话，他说：河北省的四千五百万人民坚决拥护和支持周恩来总理的声明，坚决反对美帝国主义的军事威胁和侵略行为，我们决心动员一切力量，和美帝国主义的侵略行为坚决斗争到底。
    林铁号召全省人民立即动员起来，展开声势浩大的拥护我国政府声明、反对美帝军事威胁的群众运动，进一步掀起工农业生产建设的新高潮，坚决争取钢铁战线和粮食生产战线的伟大胜利，要全部完成建立人民公社的第一步工作，要迅速地开展全民武装运动，为解放我国领土台湾和澎湖列岛的伟大任务贡献出一切力量。
    中共天津市委书记处书记，天津市市长李耕涛，河北省工会联合会主席于志远，中国国民党革命委员会天津市委员会主任委员杨亦周、河北省妇联主任彭青等在讲话中一致表示：全市决心在大跃进胜利的基础上，再接再励，决心以实际行动积极参加反对美帝国主义侵略和解放祖国领土台湾的正义斗争。
    工人、农民、学生代表在发言中一致表示要以实际行动支援解放台湾的决心。天津钢厂工人、市级劳动模范丘光和说：如果美帝国主义胆敢侵犯我国的领土，我们就用钢铁的拳头狠狠地打击他们，要他们的军舰变成侵略者自己的棺材。
    全市一百五十多万人在会后举行了盛大的示威游行。
