Title: Baotou Steel Sings Praises to Wuhan Steel
Author: Ruǎn Zhāngjìng
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 4

　　Baotou Steel Sings Praises to Wuhan Steel
　　Ruǎn Zhāngjìng

　　From Baiyun Ebo to the Yellow River bank,
　　A thousand miles of joyful songs fill the grassland.
　　Pushing aside the yellow wind, gazing south,
　　Red auspicious clouds rise in the southern sky.
　　The Yangtze dances, the Yellow River sings,
　　At the Baotou Steel base,
　　We sing songs of triumph to congratulate Wuhan Steel!
　　Wuhan Steel's blast furnace is like a celestial being,
　　Standing tall and firm on the banks of the Yangtze,
　　One Great Leap Forward soars three thousand zhang,
　　Two Great Leaps Forward reach the heavens.
　　Fast as light, fierce as lightning.
　　Our name is very loud and clear,
　　A gentle tap, and it's steel, steel, steel,
　　Steel everywhere, steel, steel, steel,
　　Watching us shine brightly!
　　The brothers of Wuhan Steel are all heroes,
　　Leaping up before National Day,
　　Scattering ten thousand rays of red light across the sky!
　　The Yangtze River rolls out of the Three Gorges,
　　Molten iron roars out of Wuhan Steel.
　　Iron troops, iron courage,
　　One furnace can hold a thousand mountains,
　　Melting ten thousand mountains into molten slurry,
　　We will spew out, beside the great river,
　　A new Yangtze River of flowing steel!
　　Congratulations, congratulations, triple congratulations,
　　Baotou Steel sings praises to Wuhan Steel.
　　Songs of triumph rise over the Ulan Mountain,
　　Songs of triumph lift up the ancient Yellow River!
　　A thousand miles of grassland take flight,
　　Untie our belts to weave a strand,
　　A golden, glittering steel Yellow River!
　　The Yangtze dances, the Yellow River sings,
　　Put our backs into it, raise our voices,
　　The eyes of the motherland are watching us,
　　The Yellow River and Yangtze must sing louder!
　　The motherland's sky stretches ten thousand miles,
　　Strings of steel satellites must be launched,
　　Competing and resounding in the sky!
　　Sing songs of triumph, congratulate Wuhan Steel,
　　Work hard, work smart, strive to produce steel.
　　Pillars of socialism,
　　We are born to be the backbone.
　　Forge a ten-thousand-ton iron fist,
　　And smash those American imperialists,
　　Who tie nooses around their own necks,
　　Into a complete and utter mess!

　　September 13, 1958

### 包钢给武钢唱凯歌
阮章竞
1958-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    包钢给武钢唱凯歌
    阮章竞白云鄂博到黄河边，千里欢歌漫草原。推开黄风朝南望，南天升起红彩云。长江起舞黄河唱，包头钢铁基地上，唱起凯歌祝武钢！武钢高炉赛天神，顶天立地在长江滨，一声跃进三千丈，两声跃进接天云。一冲冲过三峡水，一蹬蹬过几十年，快如光，猛如电。我们的名字很响亮，轻轻敲敲就钢，钢，钢，到处都在钢，钢，钢，望着我们大放光！武钢哥哥们尽是好汉，飞身跃起在国庆前，把万道红光撒天上！江水滚滚出三峡，铁水熊熊出武钢。钢铁军，钢铁胆，一炉能装千座山，高山万座化熔浆，要在大江旁边吐一条，钢水奔流的新长江！祝贺祝贺三祝贺，包钢给武钢唱凯歌。凯歌漂起乌拉山，凯歌抬起古黄河！千里草原身飞起，解下腰带要甩一条，金光闪闪的钢黄河！长江起舞黄河唱，鼓足干劲放大嗓，祖国的眼睛望着我们，黄河长江要比声亮！祖国天空万万里，钢铁卫星要连串放，在天空比武响叮当！唱凯歌，祝武钢，苦干、巧干争出钢。社会主义的擎天柱，天生我们来当骨干。铸个万万吨的铁拳头，把自套绞索的美国佬，捣成个稀巴烂！
        1958年9月13日
