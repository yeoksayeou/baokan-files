Title: World Famous Athletes, the Zátopek Couple, to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5

    World Famous Athletes
    The Zátopek Couple to Visit Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 19th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the world-renowned long-distance runner Emil Zátopek and his wife Dana Zátopková will visit China for a month in early October.
    They will visit various sports colleges and sports centers, and Zátopková may also participate in some competitions.

### 世界著名运动家  查托皮克夫妇将访我国

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    世界著名运动家
    查托皮克夫妇将访我国
    新华社布拉格19日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社消息：世界著名长跑家查托皮克和他的夫人查托普科娃将在10月初前往中国作为时一个月的访问。
    他们将访问各体育学院和各体育中心，查托普科娃还可能参加一些比赛。
