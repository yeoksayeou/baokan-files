Title: Chen Zhifang Appointed as China's Ambassador to Iraq; Our Chargé d'Affaires in Iraq Pays Courtesy Call on Iraqi Acting Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1958-09-01
Page: 1

　　Chen Zhifang Appointed as China's Ambassador to Iraq
　　Our Chargé d'Affaires in Iraq Pays Courtesy Call on Iraqi Acting Foreign Minister
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed Chen Zhifang as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Iraq.
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 30th - Chen Jiakang, the Ambassador of China to the United Arab Republic, today introduced Guo Dakai, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Iraq, to Shangshal, the Iraqi Minister of National Guidance and Acting Foreign Minister. Shangshal welcomed the formal establishment of the Chinese Embassy in Iraq. Guo Dakai arrived in Baghdad on the 29th. Chen Jiakang left Baghdad at noon today for Damascus. He and his entourage visited the ancient city of Ur in southern Iraq.

### 陈志方任我国驻伊拉克大使  我驻伊临时代办拜会伊代理外长

1958-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈志方任我国驻伊拉克大使
    我驻伊临时代办拜会伊代理外长
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东，任命陈志方为中华人民共和国驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使。
    新华社巴格达30日电　中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康今天上午介绍中国驻伊拉克大使馆临时代办郭达凯同伊拉克国家指导部长兼代理外交部长尚沙尔会见。尚沙尔对于中国在伊拉克正式建立大使馆表示欢迎。郭达凯是在29日到达巴格达的。陈家康在今天中午离开巴格达前往大马士革。他曾经和他的随行人员到伊拉克南部的古城乌尔访问。
