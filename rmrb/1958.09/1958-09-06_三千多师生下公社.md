Title: Over 3,000 Teachers and Students Go to Communes
Author:
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. With the goal of tempering themselves as ordinary laborers in the high tide of the people's commune movement, over 3,500 teachers and students from universities and colleges in the capital have, starting from the 2nd and 3rd, gone to the rural areas in the suburbs to participate in the work of establishing people's communes.

The majority of those going to the countryside this time are teachers and students from various departments of social sciences and linguistics from schools such as Peking University, Renmin University of China, Beijing Normal University, and Beijing University of Political Science and Law.

The teachers and students going to the countryside this time will first labor together with the peasants, participate in the construction work of the communes as ordinary laborers, and join the peasants in the great blooming, great contending, and great debate about the establishment of people's communes. Some teachers and senior students will also conduct scientific research in conjunction with their specialties.

### 三千多师生下公社

1958-09-06
第7版()
专栏：

　　三千多师生下公社
    新华社3日讯作为一个普通劳动者到人民公社化的高潮中去锻炼自己，首都三千五百多名高等学校的教师和学生在这个目标下，从2、3日开始陆续到郊区农村参加办人民公社的工作。
    这次下乡的多数是北京大学、中国人民大学、北京师范大学、北京政法学院等校的社会科学、语言科学各系的师生。
    各校师生这次下乡将首先和农民在一起劳动，以普通劳动者的身份参加公社的建设工作，并且和农民一起参加关于举办人民公社的大鸣大放大辩论。其中部分教师和高年级学生将结合专业进行科学研究。
