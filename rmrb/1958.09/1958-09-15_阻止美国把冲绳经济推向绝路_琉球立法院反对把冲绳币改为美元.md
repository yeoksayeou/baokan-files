Title: Prevent the US from pushing Okinawa's economy to ruin: Ryukyu Legislature opposes changing Okinawan currency to US dollars
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Naha, September 13th: According to Kyodo News, the Ryukyu Legislature today opposed the US occupation authorities' plan to change the Okinawan currency to US dollars.

The United States, in order to strengthen its economic control over Okinawa, announced not long ago that it would change the Okinawan currency to US dollars. The Ryukyu Legislature then established a Special Committee on Currency Countermeasures. This committee has submitted a request to the US High Commissioner to Okinawa, Booth, hoping that the United States will not change Okinawa's currency system.

The Special Committee on Currency Countermeasures believes that the US occupation authorities' measure to change the Okinawan currency to US dollars will lead Okinawa's economy to ruin and will bring anxiety to the lives of the Okinawan people.

### 阻止美国把冲绳经济推向绝路  琉球立法院反对把冲绳币改为美元

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阻止美国把冲绳经济推向绝路
    琉球立法院反对把冲绳币改为美元
    新华社13日讯　那霸消息：据共同社报道，今天琉球立法院反对美国占领当局把冲绳币改为美元的计划。
    美国为了加强对冲绳的经济控制，曾经在不久以前宣布要把冲绳币改为美元。琉球立法院随即成立了通货对策特别委员会。这个委员会已经向美国驻冲绳高级专员布思提出要求书，希望美国不要改变冲绳的币制。
    通货对策特别委员会认为，美国占领当局把冲绳币改为美元的措施，将使冲绳的经济走上绝路，将会给冲绳人民的生活带来不安。
