Title: Asian and African People Will Not Tolerate the US "Gunboat Policy": Burmese "Vanguard" Condemns US Interference in China's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 1st. The Burmese "Vanguard" stated in an editorial today entitled "The Gunboat Policy of the American Imperialists" that the American imperialists' dispatch of naval forces to Taiwan is an interference in China's internal affairs and is intended to create another world tension.

The "Vanguard" condemned the recent aggressive activities of the United States in the Taiwan Strait, its aggressive activities in South Korea and South Vietnam, and its supply of arms to Indonesian rebels. The newspaper's editorial said that these actions clearly show that the United States is continuing to create tensions in one part or another of the world.

The editorial emphasized that the people of Asia and the peace-loving people of the world should not allow American imperialists to interfere in the Quemoy issue. Nor should American troops be allowed to continue to stay in Taiwan and other parts of Asia. Now is the time to unanimously demand the immediate withdrawal of the US Army and Navy from Asia.

The editorial said: "In today's world, the people of Asian and African countries will not tolerate the outdated gunboat policy of the United States." "Now is the time for the United States to realize that the people of these countries will never allow it to carry out infiltration and subversion in their countries."

### 亚非人民不会容忍美国的“炮舰政策”  缅甸“先锋报”谴责美国干涉中国内政

1958-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非人民不会容忍美国的“炮舰政策”
    缅甸“先锋报”谴责美国干涉中国内政
    新华社仰光1日电　缅甸“先锋报”今天在题为“美帝国主义者的炮舰政策”的一篇社论中指出，美帝国主义者派遣海军到台湾，是干涉中国的内政，并且是为了制造另一次世界紧张局势。
    “先锋报”谴责美国最近在台湾海峡进行的侵略活动、它在南朝鲜和南越的侵略活动以及用军火接济印度尼西亚叛乱分子的行为。这家报纸的社论说，这些行动极其清楚地表明，美国正在继续不断地在世界的这一部分或那一部分制造紧张局势。
    社论强调说，亚洲人民和世界上爱好和平的人民不应该容许美帝国主义者在金门问题上进行干涉。也不应该允许美国军队在台湾和亚洲其它地方继续驻留下去。现在是一致要求美国的陆军和海军立即撤出亚洲的时候了。
    社论说：“在今天的世界上，亚非各国人民都不会容忍美国的过了时的炮舰政策”，“现在是美国应该认识到这些国家的人民绝不容许它在他们的国家里进行渗透和颠复活动的时候了”。
