Title: Factory Address Should Be on Trademarks
Author: Mao Yong'en
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 2
Column: From the Masses

　　Factory Address Should Be on Trademarks
　　Some commodity trademarks do not have addresses, making it inconvenient to contact these enterprises or offer suggestions. Could all factories add their factory address to their product trademarks? (Mao Yong'en)

### 商标上应有厂址
毛永恩
1958-09-06
第2版()
专栏：从群众中来

    商标上应有厂址
    有些商品的商标上没有地址，想跟这些企业联系或者提意见，很不方便。各厂是否可以在产品商标上都加注厂址呢？　（毛永恩）
