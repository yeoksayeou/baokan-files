Title: Sino-US Talks Continued Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1958-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 18th. The 75th meeting of the ambassadorial talks between the People's Republic of China and the United States of America was held at the Myśliwiecki Palace in Warsaw at 10:00 am today.

The meeting lasted one hour and fifty-five minutes. Afterwards, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on the 21st at 10:00 am.

### 中美会谈昨日继续举行

1958-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中美会谈昨日继续举行
    新华社华沙18日电　中华人民共和国和美利坚合众国大使级会谈第七十五次会议，今天上午十时在华沙梅希里维茨基宫举行。
    本次会议历时一小时五十五分。会后，双方宣布，下次会议将在21日上午十时继续举行。
