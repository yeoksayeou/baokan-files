　　Writing After Editing
　　The Logic of Aggressors
　　　　U.S. State Department's mouthpiece, White, said that the "aggressor" in the Taiwan area is China, not the United States. If China is the "aggressor," then the United States is naturally the "victim of aggression." A "victim of aggression" being invaded on the territory and territorial waters of the "aggressor" can only be justified by the logic of aggressors.
　　Recognizing in Order to Subvert
　　　　The U.S. Embassy in Lebanon has an organization specifically engaged in subversive activities. The United States recognized the Iraqi Republic government after its plan of armed intervention in Iraq failed, harboring such a despicable intention.

### 编完写起

1958-09-13
第3版()
专栏：编完写起

    编完写起
    侵略者的逻辑
    美国国务院的传声筒怀特说，在台湾地区的“侵略者”是中国而不是美国。如果中国是“侵略者”，那末美国就自然是“被侵略者”了。“被侵略者”在“侵略者”的领土和领海上受侵略，这只有侵略者的逻辑才能够说得通。
    承认为了颠复
    美国驻黎巴嫩大使馆设有一个专门从事颠复活动的机构。美国在武装干涉伊拉克的计划失败后承认了伊拉克共和国政府，原来怀的是这样一个卑劣的用心。
