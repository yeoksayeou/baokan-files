Title: Prime Minister Nehru Leaves Tibet for Bhutan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Xi Xia Si Ma, Tibet, September 19th: Prime Minister Nehru, who is visiting Bhutan, arrived at Xi Xia Si Ma in Tibet, China on the afternoon of the 18th, and left Xi Xia Si Ma in Tibet, China for Bhutan at noon today.

General Tan Guansan, the special representative of Lieutenant General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, Suokang Wangqinggele, the representative of the Dalai Lama, Laming Yixi Chuchen, the representative of the Panchen Erdeni, Yang Gongsu, the director of the Tibetan Foreign Affairs Office, and others, went to Hongling in the Xiayadong area to see Prime Minister Nehru off, wishing Prime Minister Nehru a safe journey, and sent people to escort Prime Minister Nehru to Asang Bridge.

### 尼赫鲁总理  离西藏赴不丹

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理
    离西藏赴不丹
    新华社西藏下司马19日电　前往不丹访问的尼赫鲁总理，18日下午到达我国西藏的下司马，今天中午离开我国西藏下司马前往不丹。
    中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武中将的特派代表谭冠三将军和达赖喇嘛代表索康·旺清格勒，班禅额尔德尼代表拉敏·益喜楚臣，西藏外事处处长杨公素等，都到下亚东地区的红岭，为尼赫鲁总理送行，祝贺尼赫鲁总理旅途平安，并派人护送尼赫鲁总理至阿桑桥。
