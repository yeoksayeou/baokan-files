Title: Albanian Government Supports Premier Zhou's Statement, Points Out It Is Time for the U.S. to Stop Playing with Fire
Author:
Date: 1958-09-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 15th: The Albanian Council of Ministers issued a statement on September 15th, expressing full support for Premier Zhou Enlai's statement regarding the situation in the Taiwan Strait, and supporting the Chinese people's just action to defend their territorial integrity.

The statement said that the Albanian government and people are deeply angered by the U.S. aggression in the Taiwan Strait, which more clearly demonstrates the U.S.'s hostile attitude towards China and constitutes a serious threat to world peace.

The statement pointed out that the Albanian government and people express their strong condemnation of the U.S.'s continuous interference in China's internal affairs, and fully support the Chinese government's measures to defend its territorial integrity and legitimate rights. They protest the U.S.'s provocations against China, and believe that this is an act of aggression and intimidation against the entire socialist camp, all peace-loving countries, and all progressive humanity.

The statement concluded that it is now time for the U.S. government to abandon its policy of aggression and interference in other countries' internal affairs and to put a definitive end to its dangerous games of playing with fire. The Albanian people, like the entire socialist camp and other peace-loving people, will always support the great Chinese people's just struggle to safeguard territorial integrity and defend world peace.

### 阿政府支持周总理声明  指出现在是美国结束玩火的时候了

1958-09-17
第3版()
专栏：

    阿政府支持周总理声明
    指出现在是美国结束玩火的时候了
    新华社地拉那15日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议15日发表声明，表示完全支持周恩来总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，支持中国人民保卫领土完整的正义行动。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚政府和人民对美国在台湾海峡地区的侵略行为感到深切的愤怒，这种侵略行为更加清楚地表明了美国对中国的敌对态度，它构成了对全世界和平的严重威胁。
    声明指出，阿尔巴尼亚政府和人民表示严厉谴责美国不断干涉中国内政的行为，完全支持中国政府所采取的保卫领土完整和合法权利的措施。他们抗议美国对中国的挑衅，并且认为这是对整个社会主义阵营、对所有爱好和平的国家以及全体进步人类的侵略和恫吓行为。
    声明最后说，现在已经是美国政府放弃侵略和干涉其他国家内政的政策和永远结束危险的玩火行动的时候了。阿尔巴尼亚人民和整个社会主义阵营以及其他爱好和平的人民一样，将始终支持伟大的中国人民为维护领土完整、保卫世界和平而进行的正义斗争。
