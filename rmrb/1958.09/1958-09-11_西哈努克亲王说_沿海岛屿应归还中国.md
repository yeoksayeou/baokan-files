Title: Prince Sihanouk Says Coastal Islands Should Be Returned to China
Author:
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, September 9th: Prince Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, passed through Hong Kong on his way to New York to attend the 13th session of the United Nations General Assembly. Today, he told reporters here, when discussing the issue of China liberating its coastal islands, that returning the coastal islands to China is "reasonable and beneficial to peace."

Sihanouk passed through here from Phnom Penh en route to New York to attend the 13th session of the United Nations General Assembly scheduled for the 16th.

Sihanouk said, "The United States cannot continue to ignore the existence of China, such a great power."

He pointed out that China's seat in the United Nations should be restored.

When talking about Sino-Cambodian relations, Sihanouk said: The relationship between China and Cambodia has always been very harmonious. China has not interfered in Cambodia's affairs, nor has it carried out any subversive activities at all.

### 西哈努克亲王说  沿海岛屿应归还中国

1958-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王说
    沿海岛屿应归还中国
    据新华社香港9日电　柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克亲王前往纽约参加联合国大会第十三届会议的途中经过香港。他今天在这里对记者谈到中国解放沿海岛屿的问题时认为，把沿海岛屿归还给中国是“合理的，对和平有利的”。
    西哈努克是从金边路过这里，前往纽约出席预定在16日举行的联合国大会第十三届会议的。
    西哈努克说，“美国不能够继续无视中国这样一个大国的存在”。
    他指出，应该恢复中国在联合国的席位。
    西哈努克在谈到中柬关系问题时说：中国同柬埔寨的关系一向是很融洽的。中国没有干涉柬埔寨的事务，也根本没有进行什么颠复活动。
