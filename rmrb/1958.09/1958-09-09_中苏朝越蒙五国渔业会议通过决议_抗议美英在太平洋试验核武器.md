Title: China, Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia Fishery Conference Passes Resolution Protesting US and British Nuclear Weapons Tests in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 7th: The commissioners and experts from China, the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia who participated in the Third Plenary Meeting of the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee in Pyongyang, passed a letter of protest against the nuclear weapons tests conducted by the United States and the United Kingdom in the Pacific at their final meeting on September 4th.

The letter of protest pointed out that large amounts of radioactivity in the air and water, especially in the western Pacific, have caused fish, invertebrates, and algae, which are used as food by the broad masses of people in East Asia, to be infected with large amounts of radioactivity. The letter of protest emphasized that it must not be allowed that the vast aquatic resources of the Pacific bring not benefits, but disease and death to humanity.

### 中苏朝越蒙五国渔业会议通过决议  抗议美英在太平洋试验核武器

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏朝越蒙五国渔业会议通过决议
　　抗议美英在太平洋试验核武器
    据新华社平壤7日电　参加在平壤举行的太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第三次全体会议的中国、苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古五国的委员和专家，在9月4日的最后一次会议上通过了反对美国和英国在太平洋进行核武器试验的抗议书。
    抗议书指出，空气和水中大量的放射能，特别是在太平洋西部，使东亚广大人民用作食物的鱼类、无脊椎动物和藻类感染了大量的放射能。抗议书强调指出，绝不能容许使太平洋广大水产资源给人类带来的不是利益，而是疾病和死亡。
