Title: Declaration Reaches the Workshop
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 8

　　Declaration Reaches the Workshop
    Worker of State-Owned Factory 541
    Li Xue'ao
　　The solemn declaration reaches the workshop,
　　Everyone raises their arms and shouts:
　　Resolutely support the Premier's declaration,
　　No force can stop the determination to liberate Taiwan!
　　Whose crops, they harvest,
　　Chinese affairs are managed by the Chinese,
　　Dulles, stop dreaming,
　　American soldiers, get out of Taiwan quickly!
　　One voice, hundreds of millions of iron fists,
　　Hundreds of millions of iron fists, hundreds of millions of bombs.
　　Want to dig out Taiwan from the map of China,
　　Unless the sun rises in the west and the earth turns upside down!

### 声明传到车间
李学鳌
1958-09-08
第8版()
专栏：

　　声明传到车间
    国营五四一工厂工人
    李学鳌
　　庄严的声明传到车间，
　　人人振臂高喊：
　　坚决拥护总理的声明，
　　任何力量都不能把解放台
    湾的决心阻拦！
　　谁的庄稼谁收割，
　　中国的事情中国人管，
　　杜勒斯不要再作梦，
　　美国兵从台湾快滚蛋！
　　一个声音，亿万铁拳，
　　亿万铁拳，亿万颗炸弹。
　　想从中国地图上把台湾挖掉，
　　除非叫太阳西出，地球倒转！
