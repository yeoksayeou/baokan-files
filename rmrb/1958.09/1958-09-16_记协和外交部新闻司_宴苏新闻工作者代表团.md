Title: ACJA and Ministry of Foreign Affairs Information Department Host Banquet for Soviet Journalists Delegation

Author:
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The All-China Journalists Association and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the entire Soviet Journalists Delegation headed by Moscowsky, Director of the Russian Propaganda Department of the CPSU Central Committee.

Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Sudarikov, and the Beijing-based reporters from Pravda and TASS were also invited to attend the banquet. Responsible persons from the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, the All-China Journalists Association, and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as representatives from the capital's press circles, were present to accompany the guests.

### 记协和外交部新闻司  宴苏新闻工作者代表团

1958-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    记协和外交部新闻司
    宴苏新闻工作者代表团
    新华社15日讯  中华全国新闻工作者协会和外交部新闻司今晚举行宴会，欢迎以苏共中央俄罗斯宣传部部长莫斯科伏斯基为首的苏联新闻工作者代表团全体人员。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利科夫和真理报、塔斯社驻北京的记者也应邀出席了宴会。出席作陪的有中共中央宣传部、中华全国新闻工作者协会、外交部新闻司的负责人和首都新闻界代表人士。
