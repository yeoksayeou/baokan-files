Title: Thai Economic Liaison Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, 8th—The Thai non-governmental Economic Liaison Delegation, consisting of five Thai members of parliament, arrived in Beijing today after visiting Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other places at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

　　The head of the delegation is Nai Sa-ing Marangon, and the deputy head is Nai Wulana Zhanbapan.

### 泰经济联络代表团到京

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　泰经济联络代表团到京
    据新华社8日讯　由五位泰国国会议员组成的泰国民间经济联络代表团应中国人民外交学会邀请，在广州、武汉等地参观访问后，已于今日到达北京。
    代表团团长乃沙茵·玛兰恭，副团长乃武拉纳·占巴攀。
