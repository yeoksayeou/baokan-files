Title: A Whistle Blows, Ten Thousand Households Open
Author: Gu Gong
Date: 1958-09-24
Page: 8

　　A Whistle Blows, Ten Thousand Households Open
　　Gu Gong
　　A whistle blows, ten thousand households open,
　　Men, women, old and young rush out;
　　Three commands, a thousand hoes raised,
　　Battalion and platoon in formation to the fields.
　　"Satellite" platoons rush west,
　　"Rocket" companies advance east,
　　Surrounding and annihilating pests and weeds,
　　In the blink of an eye, they are all wiped out.
　　The sun sets in the west, the moon rises in the east,
　　Putting down hoes, sharpening knife edges,
　　Bayonets gleam, figures move,
　　The fields echo with the sounds of martial practice.

### 号声一响万户开
顾工
1958-09-24
第8版()
专栏：

    号声一响万户开
    顾工
  号声一响万户开，
  男女老少奔出来；
  口令三声千锄举，
  连营班排赴田野。
  卫星班排往西冲，
  火箭营连向东进，
  围歼害虫和野草，
  转眼已经消灭尽。
  太阳遍西月东升，
  放下锄头磨刀锋，
  刺刀闪光人影动，
  四野一片练武声。
