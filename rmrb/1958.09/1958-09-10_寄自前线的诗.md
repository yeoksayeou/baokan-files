Title: Poems from the Front Lines
Author: Bai Ren
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 8

　　Poems from the Front Lines
　　Bai Ren
　　The cannon speaks
　　The cannon speaks,
　　Singing heroic songs;
　　This is a symphony of defending peace,
　　This is the highest note in stopping aggression.
　　August 23rd afternoon,
　　The setting sun shines on suffering Kinmen;
　　Warships coming from Taiwan,
　　Are fully loaded with the menacing Chiang troops.
　　The artillerymen held back their full of hatred,
　　Barrage of guns aimed at both Greater and Lesser Kinmen;
　　Like a fierce storm,
　　Scaring the enemy out of their wits!
　　The Chiang troops are in a panic,
　　Enemy ships flee in haste;
　　Thick smoke billows on the islands,
　　The suffering people long for the People's Liberation Army!
　　Forward! Torpedo boats
　　The sunset transforms into a spectacle,
　　White clouds drift over the island;
　　Forward! Torpedo boats!
　　Don't waste this beautiful dusk.
　　Listen! The artillerymen on the coast,
　　Are using shells to "welcome" enemy ships;
　　Hurry up and dash forward!
　　Also use torpedoes to "send them off."
　　White waves are split on the sea,
　　The speedboat sped forward;
　　Blocked two "Zhong" class landing crafts,
　　Caught the frightened fleeing enemies.
　　The captain issued the order to attack!
　　Torpedoes fly on the water;
　　The "osprey" pecks at the eyes of the "great shark,"
　　A violent explosion rang out on the enemy ship!
　　One enemy ship fled wounded,
　　One enemy ship slowly sinks;
　　The sea is ablaze with fire,
　　Black smoke turns into red clouds in an instant.
　　Ode to the Eagle
　　Sparrows are afraid of eagles,
　　Doves love to eat locusts;
　　When the F86 sees our fighter jets,
　　It immediately flees without a trace.
　　The war results are brilliant over the past month,
　　Air Force warriors have repeatedly made extraordinary achievements;
　　The first battle in the Shantou area was a victory,
　　And heroes were once again displayed in the skies over Fujian.
　　Five enemy planes flew away smoking,
　　Five enemy planes crashed into the sea;
　　"Crows" dare not come to harass again,
　　"Eagles" control the skies of the motherland.
　　Condolences to the People's Liberation Army
　　The army, navy, and air force have achieved great victories,
　　My heart is full of joy when I hear the news;
　　Men, women, old and young go to the front line,
　　Competing to offer condolences to the PLA soldiers.
　　Sending eggs and salted duck eggs,
　　Carrying bananas and big pineapples;
　　The gifts are small, but the intention is great,
　　Comrades, eat more to kill the enemy.
　　You work hard to protect national defense,
　　We keep building without rest;
　　I hope Taiwan will be liberated soon,
　　Completely eliminate Chiang Kai-shek!
　　(August 28, 1958, on the Xiamen front line)

### 寄自前线的诗
白刃
1958-09-10
第8版()
专栏：

　　寄自前线的诗
　　白刃
　　大炮开口了
　　大炮开口了，
　　唱出豪壮的歌声；
　　这是保卫和平的交响曲，
　　这是制止侵略的最高音。
　　八月二十三日下午，
　　夕阳照着苦难的金门；
　　从台湾开来的兵舰，
　　满载着张牙舞爪的蒋军。
　　炮兵忍住了满腔仇恨，
　　排炮对准大小金门；
　　像一阵猛烈的风暴，
　　打的敌人吓掉了魂！
　　蒋军慌了爪子，
　　敌舰仓惶逃奔；
　　岛屿上浓烟滚滚，
　　受苦的百姓盼着解放军！
　  前进！鱼雷快艇
　　晚霞变幻着奇景，
　　岛上飘过了白云；
　　前进！鱼雷快艇！
　　莫辜负这美丽的黄昏。
　　听！海岸上的炮手，
　　正用炮弹向敌舰“欢迎”；
　　你们赶快飞奔吧！
　　也用鱼雷为他们“送行”。
　　海上劈起一道道白浪，
　　快艇风驰电掣地往前奔；
　　堵住了两只“中”字号登陆艇，
　　抓住了惊慌逃窜的敌人。
　　艇队长发出攻击的号令！
　　一条条鱼雷在水上飞行；
    “渔鹰”啄住“大鲨鱼”的眼睛，
　　敌舰上响起剧烈的爆炸声！
　　一艘敌舰负伤逃跑，
　　一艘敌舰慢慢下沉；
　　海面上火光冲天，
　　黑烟刹那间变成红云。
    鹰之歌
　　麻雀怕见老雕，
　　鹁鸽爱吃蝗虫；
　　F86见到我歼击机，
　　立刻逃得无影无踪。
　　一个月来战果辉煌，
　　空军勇士屡建奇功；
　　汕头地区初战告捷，
　　福建上空再逞英雄。
　　五架敌机冒烟飞逃，
　　五架敌机坠入海中；
　  “乌鸦”不敢再来骚扰，
　　“神鹰”控制着祖国的天空。
    慰问解放军
　　海陆空军大胜利，
　　消息传来心欢喜；
　　男女老少上前线，
　　争着慰问解放军战士。
　　送来鸡蛋咸鸭子，
　　挑来香蕉大凤梨；
　　礼物不多心意重，
　　同志们吃了多杀敌。
　　你们努力保国防，
　　我们建设不休息；
　　但愿台湾早解放，
　　彻底消灭蒋介石！
      （1958年8月28日于厦门前线）
