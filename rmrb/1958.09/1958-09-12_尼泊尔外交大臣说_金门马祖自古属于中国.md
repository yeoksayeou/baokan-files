Title: Nepalese Foreign Minister Says Jinmen and Mazu Have Belonged to China Since Ancient Times
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 11th: According to the Indian Press Trust, Nepalese Foreign Minister Purna Bahadur Vikram Shah stated in Calcutta on the 10th to the news agency's reporter that it is a historical fact that the coastal islands of Jinmen (Quemoy) and Mazu belong to China.

Shah welcomed the resumption of the Sino-American ambassadorial talks.

Shah, en route from Kathmandu to New York to attend the 13th session of the United Nations General Assembly scheduled for the 16th, said that Nepal would support the restoration of China's seat in the United Nations.

Shah said that Nepal's view on nuclear weapons testing is consistent with that of other countries, especially Asian and African countries. He said: "Our position on this is clear and definite: the testing of these weapons should be banned immediately."

### 尼泊尔外交大臣说  金门马祖自古属于中国

1958-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔外交大臣说
    金门马祖自古属于中国
    新华社新德里11日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，尼泊尔外交大臣普兰德拉·维克拉姆·沙阿10日在加尔各答对这家通讯社的记者说，金门和马祖等沿海岛屿属于中国是历史的事实。
    沙阿表示欢迎恢复中美大使级会谈。
    沙阿从加德满都路过加尔各答，前往纽约参加预定在16日举行的联合国大会第十三届会议。他说，尼泊尔将支持恢复中国在联合国中的席位。
    沙阿说，尼泊尔对核武器试验的看法同其他国家，特别是亚非国家的看法是一致的。他说：“我们在这一点上的立场是明确和肯定的：应该立即禁止试验这些武器。”
