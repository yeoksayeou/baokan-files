Title: Guo Moruo and Liao Chengzhi Receive Iraqi Guests

Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, jointly received all members of the Iraqi People's Friendship Delegation to China this morning. The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation.

### 郭沫若  廖承志接见伊拉克贵宾

1958-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若  廖承志接见伊拉克贵宾
    新华社26日讯  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，今天上午共同接见了伊拉克人民友好访华团全体人员。宾主双方进行了亲切的谈话。
