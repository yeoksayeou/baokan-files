Title: Smelting Iron from Wuhan Steel No. 1 Blast Furnace
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 4

    Smelting Iron from Wuhan Steel No. 1 Blast Furnace
    (Tune: "West River Moon")
      Guo Moruo
      Completing the task ahead of schedule,
      Wuhan Steel's No. 1 blast furnace.
      Wuhan is now again a steel capital,
      Iron waves surge and pour forth.
      The steel commander ascends the throne,
      All the world cheers.
      The Yangtze River Bridge is not isolated,
      Red flags flutter everywhere.

郭沫若
1958-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    庆武钢一号高炉出铁
    （调寄“西江月”）
      郭沫若
      提早完成任务，
      武钢一号高炉。
      武昌今又成钢都
      铁浪滔天奔注。
      钢帅新升宝座，
      普天四海欢呼。
      长江大桥德不孤，
      面面红旗飞舞。
