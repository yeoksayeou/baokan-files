Title: U.S. Warships Invade China's Territorial Waters Five Times Yesterday; I Issue Seventh Stern Warning

Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. On September 19th, five U.S. warships intruded into China's territorial waters off the coast of Fujian five times in succession, penetrating deep into the waters near the inland islands of Jinmen (Quemoy) and Baiquan (White Dog) Islands. A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated under instruction that U.S. warships intruded into China's territorial waters and inland seas five times within fifteen hours, which is a dangerous development of increasingly serious military provocations by the United States since the resumption of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks. The Chinese government issues its seventh stern warning regarding this.

### 美国军舰昨天五次侵犯我国领海  我第七次提严重警告

1958-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰昨天五次侵犯我国领海
    我第七次提严重警告
    新华社19日讯　9月19日，美国军舰五艘先后五次侵入我国福建地区的领海，并且深入我国内海岛屿金门和白犬列岛的附近海域。我外交部发言人奉命声明，美国军舰今日在十五小时以内，竟五次侵入我国领海和内海，乃是美国在中美大使级会谈恢复以来日益严重的军事挑衅的危险发展，对此，中国政府第七次提出严重警告。
