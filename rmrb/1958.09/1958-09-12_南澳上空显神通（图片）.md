Title: Divine Power Manifested Over Nan'ao (Picture)
Author: Cai Zhongzhi
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 1

    Nan'ao Airspace Displays Divine Power
    On September 8th, at around 9:00 AM, our Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force fighter jet unit shot down two F86 U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit army aircraft over the Nan'ao Island area of Guangdong Province. The picture shows Comrade Zhang Yilin, who shot down the U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit army aircraft. Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi photographed.

    南澳上空显神通
