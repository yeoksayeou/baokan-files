Title: Swearing an Oath to the Party
Author: Chen Kecheng
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 7

Swearing an Oath to the Party
    Chen Kecheng
    Protest against the American wolfish ambition!
    Support our country's good proposition!
    Taiwan is Chinese territory,
    It is right and proper to liberate our territory.
    I swear to the Party committee,
    To turn anger into strength,
    Strive for Redness and expertise in peacetime,
    Join the army in wartime!

### 向党宣誓
陈克成
1958-09-08
第7版()
专栏：

　　向党宣誓
    陈克成
    抗议美国野心狼！
    拥护我国好主张！
    台湾本是中国土，
    解放领土理应当。
    我向党委来宣誓，
    要把愤怒化力量，
    平时奔向红与专，
    战时投笔上战场！
