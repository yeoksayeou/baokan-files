    Northern Vietnam Advances Towards Socialism
    Industry: From 1955 to 1958, 32 old enterprises were restored. 53 new enterprises were built. Compared with 1955, in 1957, the industry producing means of production increased fourfold, and the industry producing means of livelihood increased twenty-fourfold.
    Agriculture: Historically, rice production was insufficient for consumption. In 1956, the rice output exceeded the pre-war level of 1939—2,407,000 tons, reaching 4,132,000 tons.

### 向社会主义迈进的越南北方

1958-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    向社会主义迈进的越南北方
    工业　1955年至1958年，修复旧企业三十二个。建设新企业五十三个。1957年与1955年比较，生产生产资料的工业增加了四倍，生产生活资料的工业增加了二十四倍。
    农业　历史上大米生产不足食用。1956年稻谷产量超过战前1939年的水平——二百四十万七千吨，达到四百一十三万二千吨。
