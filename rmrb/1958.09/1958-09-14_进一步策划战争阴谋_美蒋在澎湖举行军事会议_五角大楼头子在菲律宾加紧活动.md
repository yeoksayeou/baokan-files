Title: Further Plotting War Conspiracies: US-Chiang Hold Military Conference in Penghu; Pentagon Chief Intensifies Activities in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. According to foreign news agencies, US Commander in Taiwan, Smoot, and US Seventh Fleet Taiwan Strait Patrol Fleet Commander, Blackburn, convened a military conference on Magong Island, Penghu, on the 12th. Chiang Kai-shek and a handful of Chiang's bandit generals attended. They plotted the US's conspiracy to intensify war provocations in the Taiwan Strait region and drive the Chiang troops to carry out new harassment and sabotage activities.

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th. Manila News: US Secretary of the Army Bruck held a "secret meeting" with Philippine President Garcia today.

According to US news agencies, Bruck discussed with Garcia in the meeting the "possible impact" of the US's provocative actions in the Taiwan area on the Southeast Asia aggressive bloc, the implementation of the US-Philippine military assistance agreement, and the US's acceleration of "aid" to the Philippines.

US Pacific Area Forces Commander Felt also met with Garcia last night.

According to the "Philippine Herald" reported on the 10th, after US Air Force Vice Chief of Staff LeMay arrived in the Philippines on the 9th, US 13th Air Force Commander in Clark, Moorman, convened a meeting overnight with representatives from both the US and the Philippines, and exchanged views on the situation in the Taiwan Strait.

It is reported that those present included US Ambassador to the Philippines Bohlen, Philippine Defense Minister Vargas, and Philippine Air Force Commander Molina. Moorman also reported on the activities of the US 13th Air Force at the meeting.

It is reported that the Philippine Minister of Defense reported to members of Congress on the 9th about the situation of the Philippine military forces. Newspapers said that members of Congress clamored for the United States to equip the Philippines with "the most modern weapons" and to step up military assistance to the Philippines.

### 进一步策划战争阴谋  美蒋在澎湖举行军事会议  五角大楼头子在菲律宾加紧活动

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    进一步策划战争阴谋
    美蒋在澎湖举行军事会议
    五角大楼头子在菲律宾加紧活动
    新华社13日讯　据外国通讯社消息，侵台美军司令斯摩特和美国第七舰队台湾海峡巡逻舰队司令布莱克本，12日在澎湖的马公岛上，召集蒋介石和一小撮蒋贼军的将领举行了一次军事会议，策划美国在台湾海峡地区加紧战争挑衅、驱赶蒋军进行新的骚扰破坏活动的阴谋。
    新华社12日讯　马尼拉消息：美国陆军部长布鲁克今天同菲律宾总统加西亚进行了“秘密会谈”。
    据美国的通讯社透露，布鲁克在会谈中同加西亚讨论了美国在台湾地区的挑衅行动对东南亚侵略集团“所可能产生的影响”，履行美菲军事援助协定，以及美国加速对菲律宾的“援助”等问题。
    美国太平洋地区部队总司令费耳特昨天晚上也同加西亚进行了会谈。
    据“菲律宾先驱报”10日报道，在美国空军副参谋长李梅9日到达菲律宾后，美国驻克拉克第十三航空队司令穆尔曼连夜为他召集了有美菲双方代表参加的会议，并且就台湾海峡局势交换了看法。
    据报道，到会的有美国驻菲律宾大使波伦、菲律宾国防部长瓦加斯以及菲律宾空军司令摩林纳等。穆尔曼还在会上报告了美国第十三航空队的活动情况。
    据报道，菲律宾国防部长9日向国会议员报告了菲律宾军事力量的情况。报纸说，国会议员们曾叫嚣要美国以“最现代化的武器”来装备菲律宾，并且加紧对菲律宾进行军事援助。
