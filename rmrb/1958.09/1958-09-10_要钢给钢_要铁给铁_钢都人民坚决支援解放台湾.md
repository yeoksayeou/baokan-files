Title: Give Steel if Steel is Needed, Give Iron if Iron is Needed: The People of the Steel City Resolutely Support the Liberation of Taiwan

Author:
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 8th: Today in the steel city of Anshan, 300,000 people held a demonstration parade in pouring rain, supporting Premier Zhou's statement and opposing the war threats of US imperialism.

At a grand mass rally, Zhao Min, Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, said: The 800,000 people of Anshan guarantee to the people of the whole country that we will definitely complete this year's leap forward plan for steel production. If the front needs steel, we will give steel; if they need iron, we will give iron; if they need people, we will give people; whatever they need, we will give it, and fully support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan. Representatives from the military, workers, farmers, and various mass organizations who spoke at the meeting unanimously condemned the aggressive crimes of US imperialism and firmly expressed their determination to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan and the coastal islands with practical actions. Liu Mingsheng, a steelworker from the No. 1 Steelmaking Plant of Anshan Iron and Steel, said: In order to oppose the aggressive behavior of US imperialism, the workers of our plant are determined to shorten the smelting time of each furnace of steel from the previous eight minutes and twenty seconds to seven minutes and fifteen seconds, guaranteeing an increase of more than 8,000 tons of steel per furnace per year.

### 要钢给钢　要铁给铁  钢都人民坚决支援解放台湾

1958-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　要钢给钢　要铁给铁
    钢都人民坚决支援解放台湾
    新华社鞍山8日电　钢都鞍山今天有三十万人在大雨滂沱中举行示威游行，拥护周总理声明，反对美帝国主义进行战争威胁。
    在盛大的群众集会上，中共鞍山市委书记赵敏讲话说：鞍山市八十万人民向全国人民保证，我们一定完成今年钢铁生产跃进计划，前线要钢就给钢，要铁就给铁，要人就给人，要什么就给什么，全力支援人民解放军解放台湾。在会上讲话的部队、工人、农民和各群众团体代表，一致痛斥美帝国主义的侵略罪行，坚决表示要以实际行动支援人民解放军解放台湾和沿海岛屿。鞍钢第一炼钢厂炼钢工刘明生说：我们厂的职工为了反对美帝国主义的侵略行为，决心把每炉钢的冶炼时间从以前的八点二十分缩短到七点十五分，保证每个炉子一年增产八千多吨钢。
