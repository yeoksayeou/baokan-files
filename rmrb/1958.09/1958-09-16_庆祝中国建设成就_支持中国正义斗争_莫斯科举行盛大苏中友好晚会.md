Title: Moscow Holds Grand Soviet-Chinese Friendship Soiree to Celebrate China's Construction Achievements and Support China's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th. TASS Moscow, 14th: On September 14th, several thousand Moscow residents, along with Chinese students studying in Moscow and Chinese technicians interning in factories, participated in a Soviet-Chinese friendship soiree held at the Sokolniki Culture and Rest Park.

After visiting a photo exhibition on the achievements of fraternal China and an exhibition showcasing Chinese books and newspapers, they attended a grand Soviet-Chinese friendship soiree. Dubrovinina, Vice-Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, who presided over the soiree, emphasized in her speech that unbreakable friendship will forever link the peoples of China and the Soviet Union together.

Professor Vyatkin, one of the leaders of the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, introduced to the attendees the historically significant achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction.

The participants of the soiree unanimously expressed their solidarity with the Chinese people and firmly opposed the American aggressors.

Zhang Yingwu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, delivered a speech that was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

### 庆祝中国建设成就　支持中国正义斗争  莫斯科举行盛大苏中友好晚会

1958-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国建设成就　支持中国正义斗争
    莫斯科举行盛大苏中友好晚会
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：9月14日几千个莫斯科居民以及在莫斯科学习的中国学生和在工厂实习的中国技术人员参加了在索科尼基文化与休息公园举行的苏中友好晚会。
    他们参观了关于兄弟中国成就的图片展览会和介绍中国书报的展览会之后，出席了盛大的苏中友好晚会。主持晚会的苏中友好协会理事会副主席杜伯洛维娜在会上强调指出，牢不可破的友谊永远把中苏两国人民联结在一起。
    苏联科学院汉学研究所领导人之一维亚特金教授向到会者介绍了中国人民在社会主义建设中所取得的具有历史意义的成就。
    晚会参加者一致表示声援中国人民，坚决反击美国侵略者。
    中国驻苏联大使馆参赞张映五的演说受到了到会者热烈的欢迎。
