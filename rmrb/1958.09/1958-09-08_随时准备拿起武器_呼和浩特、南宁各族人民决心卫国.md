Title: Ready to Take Up Arms at Any Moment: People of All Nationalities in Hohhot and Nanning Determined to Defend the Country
Author:
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 4

　　Ready to Take Up Arms at Any Moment
　　People of All Nationalities in Hohhot and Nanning Determined to Defend the Country
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 7th: Over 120,000 people of all nationalities in Hohhot City, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, held a massive demonstration this afternoon, resolutely supporting Premier Zhou's statement and unanimously passing a letter of protest against U.S. imperialism.

　　Wang Duo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, said at the meeting: The more than nine million people of all nationalities in our autonomous region, together with the people of the whole country, resolutely support and support Premier Zhou's statement with practical actions. We must build various steelmaking and ironmaking furnaces faster and in greater numbers, and strive to complete the autonomous region's great leap forward targets for steel and other industrial, agricultural, and animal husbandry production this year; we must vigorously establish people's communes, carry out the people's commune movement, strengthen socialist and communist education, accelerate socialist construction, and actively carry out people's armed construction, and carry out the people's arming movement. Representatives of workers and farmers of all nationalities in Hohhot City stated at the meeting that they would step up production, be ready at any time, await the call of the motherland, take up arms, and resolutely, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely drive the American aggressors out of Chinese territory.

　　This afternoon, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Committee and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a joint (expanded) meeting to discuss Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait region. Ulanhu called on the people of all nationalities in the region to unite and fight back against the military provocations of U.S. imperialism.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 7th: At dawn today, Nanning City, the capital of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, was filled with angry slogans such as "We must liberate Jinmen and Mazu!" "We must liberate Taiwan!" "American troops get out of Taiwan!" More than 120,000 people participated in the demonstration.

　　At 4:00 p.m., the angry crowd gathered at Minsheng Road for a meeting, which finally passed a resolution supporting our government's statement and resolutely opposing the military threat of U.S. imperialism. After the meeting, the demonstrations proceeded in different directions.

### 随时准备拿起武器  呼和浩特、南宁各族人民决心卫国

1958-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　随时准备拿起武器
　　呼和浩特、南宁各族人民决心卫国
    据新华社呼和浩特7日电　内蒙古自治区首府——呼和浩特市各族人民十二万多人，今天下午举行声势浩大的游行示威大会，坚决拥护周总理的声明，一致通过了对美帝国主义的抗议书。
    中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记处书记王铎在会上说：我们自治区九百多万各民族人民和全国人民一起，坚决用实际行动拥护和支持周总理的声明。我们要更快地更多地建立各种炼钢炼铁炉，保证今年为完成自治区钢铁和其他各项工农牧业生产大跃进指标而奋斗；要大办人民公社，开展人民公社运动，加强社会主义和共产主义教育，加速社会主义建设，要积极开展人民武装建设，开展全民武装运动。呼和浩特市各族工人和农民代表在会上表示，加紧生产，随时准备，等待祖国的召唤，拿起武器，把美国侵略者坚决、彻底、干净、全部地从中国领土上赶出去。
    今天下午，内蒙古自治区人民委员会和政协内蒙古自治区委员会举行联席（扩大）会议，讨论周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明。乌兰夫号召全区各民族人民团结一致，回击美帝国主义的军事挑衅。
    据新华社南宁7日电　今天拂晓，广西僮族自治区首府南宁市就开始响遍了“一定要解放金门、马祖！”“一定要解放台湾！”“美国军队从台湾滚出去”等愤怒的口号声，参加示威游行的有十二万多人。
    午后四时，愤怒的人群汇集在民生路口举行大会，大会最后通过拥护我国政府声明坚决反对美帝国主义军事威胁的决心书。会后，示威游行分头进行。
