Title: Khrushchev Receives Kaoru Yasui
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Professor Kaoru Yasui, Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, on the 13th and held talks with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见安井郁

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见安井郁
    据新华社14日讯　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫13日接见了正在苏联访问的日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长、列宁国际和平奖金得奖人安井郁教授，并同他谈了话。
