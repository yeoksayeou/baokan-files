Title: Blast Furnace Utilization Coefficient Calculated Proportionally
Author:
Date: 1958-09-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The Ministry of Metallurgical Industry issued a notice on the 21st to change the calculation method of blast furnace utilization coefficient. The notice instructs all regions to use the proportional calculation method to calculate the blast furnace utilization coefficient starting in September. That is: the production of pig iron per cubic meter of blast furnace effective volume per day and night is the blast furnace utilization coefficient.

The notice says: The blast furnace utilization coefficient is currently calculated using the inverse proportional method. The higher the production efficiency of the blast furnace, the smaller the number representing the utilization coefficient. The calculation methods for the utilization coefficients of other metallurgical equipment, such as open-hearth furnaces, electric furnaces, and converters, are the opposite of this. Although this calculation method for blast furnace utilization coefficient has been used for a long time, there is no particular reason for it, and it is inconvenient to apply and can create the opposite concept. Now, provinces, cities, special districts, and counties are building thousands of blast furnaces, and the blast furnace utilization coefficient has become a figure of constant concern for leading comrades and the vast number of cadres and workers at all levels. Therefore, for ease of use and ease of understanding, it has been decided to change the calculation method of the utilization coefficient. The new method is calculated proportionally. An example is given below: A 100-cubic-meter blast furnace produces 120 tons of pig iron per day and night. The calculation of the utilization coefficient:

    1. According to the old calculation method:
    Utilization coefficient = 100/120 = 0.833.

    2. According to the new calculation method:
    Utilization coefficient = 120/100 = 1.2.

### 高炉利用系数按正比例计算

1958-09-25
第5版()
专栏：

    高炉利用系数按正比例计算
    新华社23日讯  冶金工业部在21日发出改变高炉利用系数计算方法的通知，通知各地从9月份起用正比例的计算方法来计算高炉利用系数。即：高炉有效容积每立方公尺每昼夜的生铁产量即为高炉利用系数。
    通知说：高炉利用系数目前是采用反比例的计算方法。高炉的生产效率越高，表示利用系数的数字则越小。其他冶金设备利用系数，如平炉利用系数、电炉利用系数、转炉利用系数等的计算方法，都与此相反。高炉利用系数采用这种计算方法虽然沿用已久，但是也并没有什么特别的道理，而且在应用上不方便，还会使人产生相反的概念。现在各省、市、专区、县正在建设成千上万座高炉，高炉利用系数已成为各级领导同志和广大干部、工人经常关心的数字。因此，为着使用方便，容易为大家所了解，决定把利用系数的计算方法予以改变。新的方法是按正比例计算。举例说明如下：一座一百立方公尺的高炉每昼夜生产一百二十吨生铁，利用系数的计算：
    1、按旧的计算方法
    利用系数＝120分之100＝0.833。
    2、按新的计算方法
    利用系数＝100分之120＝1.2。
