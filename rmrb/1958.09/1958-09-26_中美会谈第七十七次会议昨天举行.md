Title: Sino-American Talks Seventy-Seventh Meeting Held Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 25th. The seventy-seventh meeting of the Sino-American ambassadorial talks was held at the Mysliewicki Palace in Warsaw at 3:00 PM today.

    This meeting lasted one hour and forty-two minutes.

    After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held at 10:00 AM on September 30th (Tuesday).

### 中美会谈第七十七次会议昨天举行

1958-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中美会谈第七十七次会议昨天举行
    新华社华沙25日电  中美两国大使级会谈第七十七次会议，今日下午三时在华沙梅希里维茨基宫举行。
    本次会议历时一小时四十二分。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议在9月30日（星期二）上午十时举行。
