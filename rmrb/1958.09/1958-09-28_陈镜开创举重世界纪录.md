Title: Chen Jingkai Sets World Weightlifting Record
Author:
Date: 1958-09-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, 26th -- Chinese weightlifter Chen Jingkai broke the world record for the clean and jerk in the flyweight division with a lift of 140.5 kilograms at the first Friendship Army Games held in Leipzig tonight. The previous world record was 139.5 kilograms, which he himself set in Moscow last August. The new record exceeds the previous one by one kilogram.

    Chen Jingkai's total score in this competition was 332.5 kilograms (97.5 kilograms in the press, 95 kilograms in the snatch). This score is 10 kilograms higher than his previous national record.

### 陈镜开创举重世界纪录

1958-09-28
第3版()
专栏：

    陈镜开创举重世界纪录
    据新华社来比锡26日电  我国举重运动健将陈镜开今天晚间在来比锡举行的第一届友军运动大会的举重赛中以一四○点五公斤的成绩打破了最轻量级挺举世界纪录。原来的世界纪录是他自己去年8月在莫斯科创造的一三九点五公斤，这次新纪录比上次多一公斤。
    陈镜开在这次比赛中的总成绩是三三二点五公斤（推举九十七点五公斤，抓举九十五公斤），这个成绩比他原来保持的全国纪录多了十公斤。
