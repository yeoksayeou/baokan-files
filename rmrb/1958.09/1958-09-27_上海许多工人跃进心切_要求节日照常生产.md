Title: Many Shanghai Workers Eager for Great Leap Forward, Requesting Normal Production During the Holiday
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 26th: Many workers in Shanghai's factories, construction sites, and transportation departments have been actively requesting to continue production as usual during the National Day holiday, aiming to celebrate the 9th anniversary of the founding of the nation with increased steel production and exceeding production targets.

Steelworkers like Ji Shengxiang and Zou Renyi from Shanghai Steel Plant No. 1 proposed to achieve a "red banner" performance on October 1st as a gift to National Day. The No. 1 converter in the new converter workshop of Shanghai Steel Plant No. 3 has just been put into production, and the steelworkers have proposed to make use of all holidays to quickly master production technology, work hard for three months, and strive to complete this year's steel production tasks. Workers in various workshops of the Qiuxin Shipyard, who are rushing to produce steel smelting equipment, have put up many big-character posters these days, expressing their determination to work hard during the holidays.

### 上海许多工人跃进心切  要求节日照常生产

1958-09-27
第2版()
专栏：

    上海许多工人跃进心切
    要求节日照常生产
    据新华社上海26日电  上海市许多工厂、建筑工地和交通运输部门的职工纷纷要求国庆节照常生产，用增产钢铁和超额完成生产任务来庆祝国庆九周年。
    上钢一厂炼钢工人吉生祥、邹仁一等提出要在10月1日创造满堂红的成绩，来向国庆献礼。上钢三厂新转炉车间第一只转炉刚刚投入生产，炼钢工人们就提出要利用一切假日，迅速掌握生产技术，苦战三个月，为完成今年钢的生产任务而奋斗。正在赶制钢铁冶炼设备的求新造船厂各个车间的工人这几天贴出了很多大字报，表示假日也要大干一场。
