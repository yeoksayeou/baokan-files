Title: Dulles's "Eye Disease"
Author: Damadian
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 4

    Dulles's "Eye Disease"
    Ophthalmologist: Can't you even see the People's Republic of China with its 600 million people?
    Dulles: I can't see it. I can only see Chiang Kai-shek.
                Damadian (Originally published in the Romanian newspaper "Spark")

### 杜勒斯的“眼疾”（图片）
达马迪安
1958-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯的“眼疾”
    眼科医生：难道你连六亿人口的中华人民共和国也看不见吗？
    杜勒斯：我看不见。我只能看见蒋介石。
                达马迪安（原载罗马尼亚“火花报”）
