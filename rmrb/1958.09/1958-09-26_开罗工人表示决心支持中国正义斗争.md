Title: Cairo Workers Express Determination to Support China's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 24th: Cairo workers continue to send telegrams and letters to the Chinese Embassy here, supporting the Chinese people's right to liberate Taiwan and the coastal islands.

    A letter signed by one hundred and twenty workers from the Cairo Gaohella Textile Factory condemned the United States for interfering in China's internal affairs and guaranteed to actively support the Chinese people's just struggle against U.S. imperialism.

    Eighty workers from the Cairo Glass Factory said in a letter that they warmly supported China's liberation of the coastal islands and Taiwan.

    The staff of the Cairo Hotel Syndicate also wrote a similar letter of support.

### 开罗工人表示决心支持中国正义斗争

1958-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

    开罗工人表示决心支持中国正义斗争
    新华社开罗24日电  开罗工人继续给这里的中国大使馆电报和信件，支持中国人民解放台湾和沿海岛屿的权利。
    开罗高赫拉纺织厂一百二十名工人签名的一封信，谴责美国干涉中国内政，并且保证积极声援中国人民反对美帝国主义的正义斗争。
    开罗玻璃工厂八十名工人在一封信中说，他们热诚地支持中国解放沿海岛屿和台湾。
    开罗旅馆辛迪加的职工们也写了一封类似的支持信。
