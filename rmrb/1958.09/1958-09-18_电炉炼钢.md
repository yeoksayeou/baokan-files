Title: Electric Furnace Steelmaking
Author:
Date: 1958-09-18
Page: 6

Electric furnace steelmaking is a relatively new smelting method that converts electrical energy into heat energy to smelt alloy steel and ferroalloys.

The advantages of electric furnace steelmaking are: high furnace temperature and easy to control, can effectively remove harmful impurities from steel, and can smelt any type of steel; convenient and flexible operation; less loss of alloying elements, and more complete deoxidation of the steel. Because it uses electricity as fuel, the cost of steel is higher.

### 电炉炼钢

1958-09-18
第6版()
专栏：工业常识

    电炉炼钢
    电炉炼钢法是把电能变为热能，用来冶炼合金钢和铁合金的一种最新的冶炼方法。
    电炉炼钢的优点是：炉温高，并易于控制，能有效地除去钢的有害杂质，炼成任何钢种；操作方便、灵活；合金元素损失少，钢的脱氧更完全。因为以电力为燃料，钢的成本较高。
