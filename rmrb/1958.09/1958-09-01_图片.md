Title: Photo
Author: Li Yuan
Date: 1958-09-01
Page: 3

    Qingpu County, Jiangsu Province, is vigorously carrying out agricultural tool reform. Seven townships in the county have already realized the mechanization of irrigation windmills. The irrigation efficiency of a single windmill has increased from 40 mu to 80 mu.

　　Qingpu Newspaper Office, Photo by Li Yuan
