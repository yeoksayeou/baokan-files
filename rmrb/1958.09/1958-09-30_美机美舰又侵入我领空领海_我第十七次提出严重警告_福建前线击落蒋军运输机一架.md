Title: U.S. Planes and Warships Again Invade Our Airspace and Territorial Waters; We Issue the Seventeenth Serious Warning; Front Line in Fujian Shoots Down a Chiang Kai-shek Transport Plane
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: On September 29th, five U.S. fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Xiamen region of Fujian, China, and three U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of Fujian, China. A spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated under instruction: The Chinese government issues its seventeenth serious warning regarding this serious military provocation by the United States.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, September 29th: The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units on the Fujian front shot down a Chiang Kai-shek transport plane today in Zhenhai, Xiamen region. The downed Chiang Kai-shek plane crashed into the sea five nautical miles southeast of Zhenhai.

### 美机美舰又侵入我领空领海  我第十七次提出严重警告  福建前线击落蒋军运输机一架

1958-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美机美舰又侵入我领空领海
    我第十七次提出严重警告
    福建前线击落蒋军运输机一架
    新华社29日讯  9月29日美国战斗机五架侵入我国福建厦门地区的领海上空，美国军舰三艘侵入我国福建地区的领海，我外交部发言人奉命声明：对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第十七次提出严重警告。
    新华社福建前线29日电  中国人民解放军福建前线高射炮部队，今天在厦门地区的镇海，击落蒋军运输机一架。被击落的蒋机，坠落在镇海东南五浬的大海中。
