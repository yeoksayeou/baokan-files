Title: Soviet Northern Fleet to Conduct Exercises with Warships and Aircraft Using Modern Weapons
Author:
Date: 1958-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 12th, TASS reported from Moscow on September 12th: "Izvestia" published the following notice from the Soviet Ministry of Defense today regarding naval exercises:

    "According to the combat training plan, the Northern Fleet will conduct exercises in the Barents Sea and the Kara Sea from September to October this year, in which warships and aircraft using various modern weapons will participate in live exercises.

    For this reason, the following areas in the Barents Sea and the Kara Sea are declared dangerous for navigation of Soviet and foreign ships and for the flight of aircraft from September 20 to October 25 this year.

    This area is: from longitude 42 degrees 00 minutes west, latitude 77 degrees 30 minutes north, latitude 70 degrees 30 minutes south, and longitude east to a line connecting the points: latitude 70 degrees 30 minutes, longitude 57 degrees 30 minutes ; latitude seventy-two degrees twenty minutes, longitude sixty-five degrees zero minutes; latitude seventy-seven degrees thirty minutes, longitude seventy-four degrees zero minutes.

    The Soviet Ministry of Defense warns all owners of Soviet and foreign ships and aircraft that the Soviet Ministry of Defense will not be responsible for any material losses suffered by their ships and aircraft as a result of entering the danger zone."

### 苏联北方舰队将演习  有使用现代化武器的军舰飞机参加

1958-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联北方舰队将演习
    有使用现代化武器的军舰飞机参加
    新华社12日讯　塔斯社莫斯科12日讯：“消息报”今天刊登了苏联国防部有关举行海军演习的下列通告：
    “北方舰队根据战斗训练计划，今年9月至10月将在巴伦支海和喀拉海举行演习，届时将有实际使用各种现代化武器的军舰和飞机参加。
    为此，宣布巴伦支海和喀拉海的下列地区，从今年9月20日到10月25日对苏联和外国的船舰的航行和对飞机的飞行都是危险区。
    这个地区是：西起经线四十二度○○分，北起纬线七十七度三十分，南起纬线七十度三十分和东起与下列各点连结起来的一条线：纬度七十度三十分，经度五十七度三十分；纬度七十二度二十分，经度六十五度○○分；纬度七十七度三十分，经度七十四度○○分。
    苏联国防部预告苏联和外国船舰和飞机的所有者，如果它们的船舰和飞机因为进入危险区而遭到任何物质损失时，苏联国防部将不担负责任”。
