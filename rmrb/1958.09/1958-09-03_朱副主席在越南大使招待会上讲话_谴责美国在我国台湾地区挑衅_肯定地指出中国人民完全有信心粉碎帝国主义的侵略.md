Title: Vice Chairman Zhu's Speech at Vietnamese Ambassador's Reception: Condemning U.S. Provocations in China's Taiwan Region, Affirmatively Pointing Out that the Chinese People Have Full Confidence in Crushing Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: Vice Chairman Zhu De tonight strongly condemned the U.S. imperialists' provocative acts in China's Taiwan region at the National Day reception hosted by the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, and pointed out that the victorious Chinese people have full confidence in crushing imperialist provocations.

Vice Chairman Zhu De said: "The United States and Britain, not only disregarding the resolutions of the United Nations Emergency Special Session and continuing to delay the withdrawal of their troops from Lebanon and Jordan, but also carrying out wanton provocations in China's Taiwan region, such behavior that violates the peace aspirations of the world's people and is hostile to the Chinese people has aroused strong condemnation from many peace-loving countries and people." Vice Chairman Zhu De affirmatively pointed out: "The Chinese people, who have won victory after a long struggle against imperialism, have full confidence in crushing imperialist provocations, and firmly believe that in the face of the powerful forces of peace and justice, any aggressive conspiracy of imperialism is bound to be thoroughly defeated."

The hundreds of Chinese and foreign guests present at the reception welcomed Vice Chairman Zhu De's remarks with prolonged and enthusiastic applause.

### 朱副主席在越南大使招待会上讲话  谴责美国在我国台湾地区挑衅  肯定地指出中国人民完全有信心粉碎帝国主义的侵略

1958-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席在越南大使招待会上讲话
　　谴责美国在我国台湾地区挑衅
    肯定地指出中国人民完全有信心粉碎帝国主义的侵略
    新华社2日讯　朱德副主席今晚在越南驻华大使举行的国庆招待会上强烈地谴责了美帝国主义在我国台湾地区的挑衅行为，并且指出胜利了的中国人民完全有信心粉碎帝国主义的挑衅。
    朱德副主席说：“美国和英国不仅无视联合国紧急特别会议的决议，继续拖延从黎巴嫩、约旦撤出他们的军队，同时，美国还在我国的台湾地区进行放肆的挑衅，这种违反世界人民和平愿望和敌视中国人民的行为，已经引起许多爱好和平的国家和人民强烈的谴责。”朱德副主席肯定地指出：“经过长期反对帝国主义斗争而取得了胜利的中国人民，完全有信心粉碎帝国主义的挑衅，并且坚信在强大的和平正义力量面前，帝国主义的任何侵略阴谋都必然遭到彻底的失败。”
    出席招待会的几百位中外来宾，长时间地热烈鼓掌欢迎朱德副主席的这一番话。
