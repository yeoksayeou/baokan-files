Title: China and Vietnam are as Close as Lips and Teeth, Fighting Aggression Side by Side; Anti-American Demonstration Movement Rises in Vietnam

Author: 
Date: 1958-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 22nd: A vigorous movement against U.S. imperialism's interference in China's internal affairs and in support of the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan and the coastal islands is underway in various parts of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　In Haiphong City, a rally and demonstration march with more than 30,000 people participating was held on the evening of the 21st, demanding that U.S. imperialism get out of Taiwan and South Vietnam.

　　Nguyen Van But, head of the Haiphong City Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, spoke at the meeting, introducing the Chinese people's struggle to liberate their own territory of Taiwan and the coastal islands. Nguyen Van But said that U.S. imperialism's aggression against China is also aggression against Vietnam. We firmly stand with the Chinese people and demand that U.S. imperialism get out of Taiwan, South Vietnam, and South Korea.

　　More than two hundred cadres of the Vietnam Labor Youth League in Hai Duong Province held an anti-American aid-China rally on the 19th. The meeting passed a resolution expressing their determination to support the Chinese people's struggle to liberate their territory with practical actions by mobilizing young people to actively participate in production and construction.

　　The Vinh Linh area, which is close to the 17th parallel, also held an anti-American rally on the 21st. The masses present unanimously expressed their support for the statement by Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai on the situation in the Taiwan Strait, opposed U.S. imperialism's interference in China's internal affairs, and demanded that U.S. imperialism immediately get out of China's territory of Taiwan and the coastal islands.

### 中越唇齿相依　并肩反抗侵略  越南人民反美示威运动高涨

1958-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中越唇齿相依　并肩反抗侵略
    越南人民反美示威运动高涨
    新华社河内22日电　越南民主共和国各地正在热烈开展反对美帝国主义干涉中国内政、支持中国人民解放台湾和沿海岛屿的运动。
    海防市在21日晚上举行了有三万多人参加的集会和示威游行，要求美帝国主义滚出台湾和南越。
    越南祖国战线海防市委员会负责人阮文笔在会上讲话时，介绍了中国人民为解放自己的领土台湾和沿海岛屿而斗争的情况。阮文笔说，美帝国主义侵略中国，也就是侵略越南，我们坚决同中国人民一起，要求美帝国主义滚出台湾、南越和南朝鲜。
    海阳省二百多个越南劳动青年团的干部在19日举行了反美援华集会。会议通过决议，表示决心以发动青年积极参加生产建设的实际行动来支援中国人民解放自己的领土的斗争。
    紧靠十七度线的永灵区也在21日举行了反美集会。到会群众一致表示支持中国周恩来总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，反对美帝国主义干涉中国内政，要求美帝国主义立即从中国的领土台湾和沿海岛屿滚出去。
