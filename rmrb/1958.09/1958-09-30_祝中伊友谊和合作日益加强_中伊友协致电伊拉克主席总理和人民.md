Title: Wishing Sino-Iraqi Friendship and Cooperation to Grow Stronger Daily: Sino-Iraqi Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Iraqi President, Prime Minister, and People
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The China-Iraq Friendship Association sent a telegram of respect to the President, Prime Minister, and people of Iraq:

To the esteemed President Najib ar-Ruba'i,
To the esteemed Prime Minister Abdul Karim Qassem,
And to all the fraternal brothers and sisters of the Republic of Iraq:

The China-Iraq Friendship Association was officially established today at a grand rally in Beijing. On behalf of the conference, I extend our highest respects to Your Excellency the President, Your Excellency the Prime Minister, and all the people of Iraq.

The establishment of the China-Iraq Friendship Association demonstrates the desire of the Chinese people to further develop the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Iraq. We sincerely hope that the friendship and mutually beneficial cooperation between China and Iraq will be strengthened and consolidated daily.

At present, world peace is seriously threatened due to the aggression and military provocations of the colonialists in West Asia and the Far East. Peace-loving people all over the world express their utmost anger at this. The just struggle of the people of China and Iraq to safeguard national sovereignty and world peace is receiving increasing sympathy and support from peace-loving and righteous people all over the world. It can be believed that if the colonialists persist in their war conspiracy, they will inevitably meet with a shameful defeat.

The people of China and Iraq, together with peace-loving people all over the world, will hold high the banner of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and make due efforts to safeguard peace in Asia, Africa, and the world.

The Chinese people warmly congratulate the revolutionary victory of the Iraqi people and wish the Republic of Iraq prosperity and its people happiness!

May the fraternal friendship and friendly cooperation between China and Iraq be further developed!

Liu Ningyi, President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association
September 29, 1958
Beijing

### 祝中伊友谊和合作日益加强  中伊友协致电伊拉克主席总理和人民

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    祝中伊友谊和合作日益加强
    中伊友协致电伊拉克主席总理和人民
    新华社29日讯  中国伊拉克友好协会向伊拉克主席、总理和人民致敬电文敬爱的纳吉布·鲁巴伊主席：阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆总理：并致伊拉克共和国全体兄弟姊妹们：
    中国—伊拉克友好协会，今天在北京举行的盛大的集会上正式成立了。我谨代表大会向主席阁下、总理阁下和伊拉克全体人民致以崇高的敬礼。
    中国—伊拉克友好协会的成立，表明了中国人民进一步发展中、伊两国人民牢不可破的友谊的意愿。我们热诚地希望中、伊两国的友谊和互助合作关系日益加强和巩固。
    目前由于殖民主义者对西亚和远东的侵略和军事挑衅，世界和平受到严重的威胁。全世界爱好和平的人民对此表示无比的愤怒。中、伊两国人民为维护国家主权和世界和平而进行的正义斗争，正在得到全世界和平正义人士的日益增长的同情和支持，可以相信，殖民主义者如果坚持它的战争阴谋，必将遭到可耻的失败。
    中、伊两国人民，将同全世界爱好和平人士一起，更加高举和平共处五项原则的旗帜，为维护亚、非和世界和平而作出应有的努力。
    中国人民热烈地祝贺伊拉克人民的革命胜利，并祝伊拉克共和国繁荣和人民幸福！
    祝中、伊两国兄弟般的友谊和友好合作进一步发展！
    中国—伊拉克友好协会  会长刘宁一
                        一九五八年九月二十九日
                                      北京
