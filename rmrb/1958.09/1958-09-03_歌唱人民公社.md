Title: Singing the People's Commune
Author:
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 3

　　Singing the People's Commune

　　Entering the Commune is Like Ascending to Heaven
　　Entering the commune is like ascending to heaven,
　　One night surpasses thousands of years,
　　Sharp knives sever the roots of private ownership,
　　Opening a new era in history.
　　Chairman Mao, speak a word,
　　And happiness will descend from the sky,
　　The people's commune has many benefits,
　　Everyone is vying to join.
　　When the people hear about setting up communes,
　　The cheers shake the world like thunder,
　　Millions of women walk out of their homes,
　　The elderly smile until tears flow.
　　　　　——Zhengzhou Lu Ke

　　The Commune Can Turn the Earth
　　Small cooperatives can only manage gullies,
　　Merging into large cooperatives to fight the mountains,
　　Once the commune is established,
　　It can turn the earth.
　　　　　——Dengfeng Xu Changxin

### 歌唱人民公社

1958-09-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　歌唱人民公社
　　入了公社如上天
　　入了公社如上天，
　　一夜赛过几千年，
　　利刀斩断私有根，
　　开辟历史新纪元。
　　毛主席，说句话，
　　幸福就从天上下，
　　人民公社好处多，
　　谁都争着来参加。
    人民一听办公社，
    欢声如雷震天下，
    千万妇女走出家，
    老人笑得掉泪花。
          ——郑州卢苛
　　公社就能翻地球
    小社只能治山沟，
    合成大社战山头，
    公社一建立，
    就能翻地球。
        ——登封徐长欣
