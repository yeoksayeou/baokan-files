Title: International Trade Union Conference in Cairo Supports the Algerian People's Struggle for Independence; Chinese Representative Points Out that US Imperialism is the Most Ferocious Enemy of Oppressed Nations
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 12th. An international trade union conference in support of the Algerian workers and people opened here this morning. The conference was held at the suggestion of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions.

Representatives from the World Federation of Trade Unions, the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, and trade unions from the Soviet Union, Algeria, Sudan, the United Arab Republic, China, the German Democratic Republic, India, Indonesia, Italy, Aden, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Morocco, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Cyprus, and others attended the meeting. The purpose of this conference is to discuss taking concerted action to assist the Algerian people in their struggle for independence.

Anwar Sadat, Secretary of the Egyptian National Union, attended the opening meeting.

Fawzi Kamel, Secretary-General of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, presided over the opening ceremony.

At the meeting this morning, after hearing the opening speech by Luigi Grassi, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the representatives suggested that Kamel, Secretary-General of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, serve as the chairman of the conference, and established a secretariat composed of representatives from China, Morocco, Italy, India, and the World Federation of Trade Unions.

Algerian representative Mashu Abdul Qadir spoke, calling for material and spiritual support for the Algerian people and emphasizing that the situation requires swift action to provide such support. He condemned De Gaulle for continuing to adopt a policy of suppression. He assured the representatives attending the meeting that the Algerian people will continue to fight to completely eradicate the forces of imperialism in Algeria. He demanded that workers around the world force the withdrawal of French troops from Algeria, just as they demanded the withdrawal of American troops from Lebanon and British troops from Jordan.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 13th. The international trade union conference in support of the Algerian workers and people continued its meeting this afternoon.

Kang Yonghe, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke at the meeting, expressing support for the Algerian struggle.

Speaking about the American conspiracy on the Algerian issue, he said that the United States sometimes pretends to sympathize with the Algerian national movement, attempting to deceive the Algerian people and replace France's position; on the other hand, it helps French imperialism suppress the Algerian people. Therefore, we must recognize that US imperialism is the most ferocious enemy of oppressed nations.

### 支援阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争  国际工会会议在开罗举行  中国代表指出美帝国主义是被压迫民族最凶恶的敌人

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    支援阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争
    国际工会会议在开罗举行
    中国代表指出美帝国主义是被压迫民族最凶恶的敌人
    新华社开罗12日电　支持阿尔及利亚工人和人民国际工会会议今天上午在这里开幕。这个会议是根据世界工会联合会和阿拉伯工会国际联合会的建议举行的。
    世界工会联合会、阿拉伯工会国际联合会的代表们，以及苏联、阿尔及利亚、苏丹、阿拉伯联合共和国、中国、德意志民主共和国、印度、印度尼西亚、意大利、亚丁、越南民主共和国、摩洛哥、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、塞浦路斯等的工会代表们出席了会议，举行这次会议的目的是要讨论采取一致的行动，来援助阿尔及利亚人民进行的争取独立的斗争。
    埃及民族联盟秘书长安瓦尔·萨达特出席了开幕会议。
    阿拉伯工会国际联合会总书记法齐·卡迈勒主持会议开幕式。
    在今天上午的会议上，听取了世界工会联合会书记路易吉·葛拉西的开幕词后，代表们建议由阿拉伯工会国际联合会总书记卡迈勒担任大会主席，并且成立了由中国、摩洛哥、意大利、印度和世界工会联合会的代表组成的秘书处。
    阿尔及利亚代表马舒·阿卜杜勒·卡德尔发言，他呼吁给予阿尔及利亚人民物质和精神上的支持，并强调局势要求迅速采取行动以提供这种支持。他谴责了戴高乐继续采取镇压的政策。他对参加会议的代表们保证，阿尔及利亚人民将继续进行斗争，完全根除帝国主义在阿尔及利亚的势力。他要求全世界工人迫使在阿尔及利亚的法国军队撤退，正如他们要求美国军队从黎巴嫩撤退，英国军队从约旦撤退一样。
    据新华社开罗13日电　支持阿尔及利亚工人和人民国际工会会议今天下午继续举行会议。
    中华全国总工会代表康永和在会上发言，表示支持阿尔及利亚的斗争。
    他谈到美国在阿尔及利亚问题上的阴谋时说，美国有的时候假装同情阿尔及利亚民族运动，企图欺骗阿尔及利亚人民，并且取代法国的地位；另一方面，它帮助法帝国主义镇压阿尔及利亚人民。因此，我们应当认识到美帝国主义是被压迫民族的最凶恶的敌人。
