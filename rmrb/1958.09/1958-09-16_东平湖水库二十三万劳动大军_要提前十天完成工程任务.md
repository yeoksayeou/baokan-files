Title: Twenty-three Thousand Strong Labor Army at Dongping Lake Reservoir to Complete Project Task Ten Days Ahead of Schedule
Author: Chen Liye
Date: 1958-09-16
Page: 2

    Dongping Lake Reservoir's Twenty-three Thousand Strong Labor Army
    To Complete Project Task Ten Days Ahead of Schedule
    We, the twenty-three thousand strong labor army on the Dongping Lake Reservoir construction site in Shandong Province, held rallies around the 10th to support Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait, and conducted grand demonstrations on the construction site. We wrote tens of thousands of big-character posters, pledges, oaths, and applications, determined to support the liberation of Taiwan, Jinmen, and Matsu Islands and defend the security of the motherland and world peace with the practical action of completing the reservoir project ten days ahead of schedule. Everyone also expressed: once the motherland needs it, we will put down our carts and shovels, pick up weapons, and rush to the front lines.
    Shandong Laiwu Chen Liye

### 东平湖水库二十三万劳动大军  要提前十天完成工程任务
陈立业
1958-09-16
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    东平湖水库二十三万劳动大军
    要提前十天完成工程任务
    我们，山东省东平湖水库工地上的二十三万劳动大军，在10日前后，分别召开了拥护周总理关于台湾海峡局势的声明大会，并在工地上进行了盛大的示威游行。大家写了数万张大字报、决心书、誓词和申请书，决心以提前十天完成水库工程的实际行动，支援解放台湾、金门和马祖等岛屿，保卫祖国安全和世界和平。大家还表示：一旦祖国需要，就放下小车和铁锨，拿起武器，奔赴前线。　山东莱芜　陈立业
