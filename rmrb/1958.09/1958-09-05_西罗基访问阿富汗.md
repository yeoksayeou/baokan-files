Title: Široký Visits Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 3rd: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký arrived in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan, by plane on September 2nd. Afghan Prime Minister Daud, Deputy Prime Ministers Ali Mohammed and Naim, as well as many senior government officials, welcomed him at the airport.

A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the airport. Premier Široký inspected the guard of honor. Široký and Daud exchanged greetings.

Today, Kabul newspapers published editorials warmly welcoming Široký's visit.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency today, Afghan Prime Minister Daud held a reception on the 2nd to entertain Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký, who arrived in Kabul that day for a visit.

### 西罗基访问阿富汗

1958-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    西罗基访问阿富汗
    据新华社布拉格3日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基9月2日乘飞机到达阿富汗首都喀布尔。阿富汗首相达乌德、副首相阿里·穆罕默德和纳伊姆以及许多政府高级官员到机场欢迎。
    在机场上举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。西罗基总理检阅了仪仗队。西罗基和达乌德相互致了词。
    今天，喀布尔的报纸都发表社论，表示热烈欢迎西罗基的访问。
    新华社布拉格4日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社今天报道，阿富汗首相达乌德在2日举行招待会，招待在当天到达喀布尔进行访问的捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基。
