Title: Beating the American Imperialists Until Their Legs Go Weak
Author: Yuan Shuibai
Date: 1958-09-05
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting Pairing

　　Beating the American Imperialists Until Their Legs Go Weak
　　Yuan Shuibai
    In April and May, Nixon visited South America and was badly beaten in Venezuela and other countries. Recently, the American magazine "Life" published a photo of Nixon's car, its safety glass shattered by the crowd.
　　I
　　The shouts of abuse shake the sky,
　　Stones, rotten eggs, steel fists fly.
　　Safety glass is not safe, it's true,
　　Shattered and pierced right through.
　　The president panics, what a fright,
　　Dares not show his face in public light.
　　"Friendly" visit turns the other way,
　　A widespread hatred on display.
　　II
　　All because they’ve done evil deeds galore,
　　Sucking up resources from every shore,
　　Interfering in internal affairs, called American aid,
　　Subverting democratic regimes, a dirty trade.
　　American dollars can't buy people's hearts, it's plain,
　　Intimidation efforts are all in vain.
　　Behold the heroes of South America, so grand,
　　Beating the American imperialists until their legs can't stand!

### 打得美帝腿软
袁水拍
1958-09-05
第8版()
专栏：诗画配

　　打得美帝腿软
　　袁水拍
    4、5月间尼克松访问南美，在委内瑞拉等国大挨其揍。最近，美国“生活”杂志登了一张照片，那是尼克松坐的汽车，它的保险玻璃窗被群众打得粉碎。
　　一
　　一路骂声震天，
　　石头、臭蛋、钢拳。
　　保险玻璃不保险，
　　打得粉碎洞穿。
　　总统慌作一团，
　　人前不敢露脸。
　　“友好”访问成反面，
　　引起仇恨普遍。
　　二
　　皆因作恶多端，
　　吸尽各国资源，
　　干涉内政是美援，
　　颠覆民主政权。
　　人心美金难换，
　　恫吓也是枉然。
　　请看南美英雄汉，
　　打得美帝腿软！

　　    （漫画照片　赵志方作）
