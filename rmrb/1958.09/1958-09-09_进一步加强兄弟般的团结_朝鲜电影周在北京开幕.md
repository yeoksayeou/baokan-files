Title: Further Strengthening Fraternal Solidarity: Korean Film Week Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th — The Film Week of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea opened in Beijing tonight. This Film Week will be held successively in ten cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Harbin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Xi'an, and Urumqi starting today.

More than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing attended the opening ceremony of the Film Week.

Li Yongho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Xuqiu, head of the Korean Art Troupe who was passing through Beijing on his way to visit the Mongolian People's Republic, and members of the art troupe, as well as diplomatic envoys of various countries to China, also attended the opening ceremony.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, congratulated on the success of the Film Week in his speech and wished the Korean people greater victories in building their motherland and in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of their country.

Li Yongho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, introduced the great achievements of the Korean film industry since liberation in his speech.

### 进一步加强兄弟般的团结  朝鲜电影周在北京开幕

1958-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强兄弟般的团结
　　朝鲜电影周在北京开幕
    据新华社8日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影周，今天晚上在北京举行开幕式。这次电影周从今天起将在北京、上海、天津、沈阳、哈尔滨、武汉、广州、成都、西安、乌鲁木齐等十个城市陆续举行。
    出席电影周开幕式的有北京各界人士一千多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐、访问蒙古人民共和国途经北京的朝鲜艺术团团长李诩求和艺术团团员以及各国驻华使节也出席了开幕式。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在讲话中祝电影周的成功，并祝贺朝鲜人民在建设祖国，在为祖国的和平统一的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐在讲话中介绍了解放以后朝鲜电影事业的巨大成就。
