Title: Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing Today; Sofia City People's Council Delegation Arrives Together
Author:
Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: The Bulgarian People's Republic National Assembly Delegation, led by Vulko Chervenkov, member of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, will arrive in our country tomorrow for a friendly visit at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi. The Sofia City People's Council Delegation, headed by Georgi Kostov, member of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Sofia City Committee, will also arrive in Beijing on the same day.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 20th: The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation left Sofia by special plane for Beijing on the morning of the 20th. The delegation is visiting China, North Korea, and Vietnam at the invitation of these countries. Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included: members of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Gavrilov and Taskov, Central Committee Secretary Grigorov, alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee Dimov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly Kulishev, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly Kozovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Sofia City People's Council Popov, and others. Also present at the airport were the ambassadors of China, North Korea, and Vietnam to Bulgaria, Zhu Qiwu, Kim Hak-in, and Hoang Van Loi, as well as Soviet Ambassador Prikhodov.

The Sofia City People's Council Delegation also traveled to Beijing on the same plane for a friendly visit.

### 保议会代表团今天到北京  索非亚市人民会议代表团同来

1958-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    保议会代表团今天到北京
    索非亚市人民会议代表团同来
    新华社21日讯　由保加利亚人民共和国国民议会代表、保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席伏尔科·契尔文科夫率领的保加利亚人民共和国国民议会代表团，应刘少奇委员长的邀请，将在明天到达我国进行友好访问。以保共中央委员、保共索非亚市委第一书记格奥尔基·科斯托夫为首的索非亚市人民会议代表团，也将在同一天到达北京。
    据新华社索非亚20日电　保加利亚国民议会代表团20日早晨乘专机离索非亚飞往北京。代表团是应邀到中、朝、越三国进行友好访问的。到机场送行的有：保共中央政治局委员加?夫、塔斯科夫，中央书记格里戈罗夫，中央政治局候补委员迪莫夫，国民议会主席团副主席库利歇夫，国民议会执行委员会主席科佐夫斯基，索非亚市人民会议执行委员会主席波波夫等。到机场送行的还有中、朝、越三国驻保加利亚大使朱其文、金学仁和黄文利，苏联大使普里霍多夫。
    同机到北京进行友好访问的还有索非亚市人民会议代表团。
