Title: Fearing Dulles's War Policy Will Bring Disaster, The US Ruling Group Feels Uneasy

Author:

Date: 1958-09-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 15th: More and more people in the American ruling group are expressing unease about the expansion of American aggression in the Taiwan Strait region.

Senator Mansfield, the Democratic Party's main foreign policy spokesman in the Senate, advised Eisenhower to proceed "cautiously" while on a campaign speech tour in Montana on the 14th. He said: "We should not underestimate 'Pravda' or Khrushchev's statements."

Senator Monroney, in a statement issued on the 14th, also worried that the US's Far East policy might lead to a tragic and costly nuclear war. He pointed out that interfering in China not only takes risks, but also invites nuclear retaliation.

On September 9, the "New York Times" published a letter to the editor from former US Air Force Secretary Finletter, in which Finletter accused the United States of dangerously and unnecessarily playing with war on the issue of Jinmen and Mazu. Finletter said that the imminent catastrophic danger of war requires the US president to take immediate action to choose the path to peace, rather than rushing down the dangerous slope towards war as it is now, in which the United States will face two major powers alone without the support of its allies and world opinion.

Former US diplomat Van Slande also wrote to the "New York Times" saying that the current US Far East policy completely disregards the basic interests of the United States and the opinions of its allies. Van Slande asked: How much longer are we going to cling to the illusion that Chiang Kai-shek's existence is permanent?

### 害怕杜勒斯的战争政策带来灾难  美国统治集团内部惴惴不安

1958-09-17
第3版()
专栏：

    害怕杜勒斯的战争政策带来灾难
    美国统治集团内部惴惴不安
    据新华社15日讯　华盛顿消息：有越来越多的美国统治集团人士对美国在台湾海峡地区扩大侵略的行动表示不安。
    参议院民主党主要外交政策发言人曼斯菲尔德14日在蒙大拿州作竞选演说旅行时，劝告艾森豪威尔“谨慎”从事。他说：“我们不应该低估‘真理报’或赫鲁晓夫的声明”。
    参议员门罗尼在14日发表的一篇声明中，也担心美国的远东政策可能导致一场悲惨而代价巨大的核战争。他指出，干涉中国不仅冒风险，而且会招致核报复。
    9月9日“纽约时报”曾在“读者来信”栏中刊载了美国前空军部长芬勒特写给该报的信，芬勒特在信中指责美国目前在金门和马祖问题上危险而不必要地玩弄战争。芬勒特说，迫在眉睫的灾难性的战争危险使美国总统必须立即采取行动来选择通往和平的道路，而不像目前那样沿着危险的下坡路冲向战争，在这个战争中，美国将得不到盟国和世界舆论的支持而独自对付两个大国。
    前美国外交官范宣德也写信给“纽约时报”说，美国现在的远东政策完全不顾美国的基本利益和盟国的意见。范宣德问道；我们到底还有多久要抱着这样的幻想：蒋介石的存在是永久的。
