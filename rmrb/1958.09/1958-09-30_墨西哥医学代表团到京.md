Title: Mexican Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. At the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, the Mexican medical delegation of seventeen members, led by Professor E. Cabez, arrived in Beijing on the 28th.

### 墨西哥医学代表团到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥医学代表团到京
    新华社28日讯  应中华医学会邀请来我国访问的墨西哥医学代表团一行十七人，在团长依·卡维兹教授率领下，28日到达北京。
