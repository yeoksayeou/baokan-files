Title: The Torpedo's Song
Author: Sui Yupei
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 8

　　The Torpedo's Song
　　Torpedo Platoon,
　　Torpedo Platoon,
　　Responsible for guns, cannons, and torpedo tubes,
　　Today is a large-scale naval exercise,
　　Raise vigilance and don't be careless.
　　Light weapons,
　　Submachine guns,
　　Bullets loaded in magazines,
　　Once enemy bandit ships are discovered,
　　It will be difficult to escape the law even with wings.
　　My cannon,
　　Great use,
　　Can hit both air and sea,
　　If enemy planes come to disturb,
　　One shot will bring them down to the sea.
　　Torpedo tube,
　　Loaded with torpedoes,
　　Ready to greet the enemy fleet,
　　As long as it arrives,
　　Guarantee it will take a bath in the sea.
　　A certain unit, Sui Yupei

### 鱼雷的歌
隋育培
1958-09-10
第8版()
专栏：

　　鱼雷的歌
    鱼雷班，
　　鱼雷班，
　　负责枪支火炮发射管，
　　今日海上大演习，
　　提高警惕莫麻痹。
　　轻武器，
　　冲锋枪，
　　子弹装在弹夹上，
　　一旦发现敌匪船，
　　插翅也难逃法网。
　　我的炮，
　　用处大，
　　对空对海都能打，
　　如果敌机来扰乱，
　　一炮打下向海扎。
　　发射管，
　　装鱼雷，
　　准备迎接敌舰队，
　　只要它来到，
　　保证让它洗海澡。
　　某部队　隋育培
