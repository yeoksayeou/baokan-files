Title: Resolution on Responding to the Party Central Committee's Call to Strive for the Realization of the Twelve-Year Science and Technology Plan Five Years Ahead of Schedule
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-26
Page: 6

    The Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council, put forward a solemn call to the conference: "Work hard for three years to fundamentally change the face of science and technology in our country, strive to complete the Twelve-Year Science and Technology Plan in 1962, and catch up with the world's advanced scientific and technological levels." All the representatives unanimously and enthusiastically supported this call and are willing to contribute all their strength to complete this great task.
    The conference requires the various levels of the Science and Technology Association, under the leadership of the Party and the government, to launch a large-scale mass movement of technological revolution, and actively assist the relevant departments to take various specific measures to promote the spirit of communist cooperation; strive to complete the Twelve-Year Science and Technology Plan in 1962 or in a shorter period of time; and strive for the early realization of local science plans; and struggle to build a socialist motherland with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture, and to realize the great ideal of communism sooner.
    September 25, 1958

### 关于响应党中央号召为提前五年实现十二年科学技术规划而斗争的决议

1958-09-26
第6版()
专栏：

    关于响应党中央号召为提前五年实现十二年科学技术规划而斗争的决议
    聂荣臻副总理代表中国共产党中央和国务院向大会提出了“苦战三年，基本改变我国科学技术面貌，争取在1962年完成十二年科学技术规划，赶上世界先进的科学技术水平”的庄严号召，全体代表一致热烈拥护，并愿为完成这一伟大任务贡献出一切力量。
    大会要求科协各级组织，在党和政府领导下，发动大规模的技术革命的群众运动，并积极协助各有关部门采取各种具体措施，发扬共产主义的协作精神；争取在1962年或更短的时间内，完成十二年科学技术规划；并争取各地方科学规划的提前实现；为建成现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的社会主义祖国，更早地实现共产主义的伟大理想而奋斗。    1958年9月25日
