Ode to the New Flying Apsaras - Written to Celebrate the Average Wheat Yield of 1,000 Jin Per Mu in Dunhuang
Tao Jie

　　Although I am not a painter,
　　I also want to paint a new Dunhuang mural,
　　Without red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, or purple,
　　Using only the flowers blooming in the hearts of the people of Dunhuang!
　　I won’t paint Sakyamuni or Guanyin,
　　I’ll paint a group of flying apsaras laughing happily,
　　Their dance is healthy, beautiful, and full of spirit,
　　Holding ears of wheat in their hands, no longer embracing the pipa.
　　The background is the sunrise in the east, filling the sky with rosy clouds,
　　Sixty thousand mu of wheat shines brighter than the five-colored sand,
　　The good news from the "Thousand Jin County" flies to the whole country,
　　Like a rainbow hanging in the sky.
　　I pause my brush and add a few more strokes -
　　The people of Dunhuang entrust the flying apsaras to send a message,
　　Inviting Shennong to come down to earth and take a look,
　　Dunhuang's yield per mu will increase even more next year!
　　Although I am not a painter,
　　Today I painted this new Dunhuang mural,
　　The people of Dunhuang go to the Mogao Grottoes to ask Chang Shuhong,
　　Is this a copy or a sketch?

### 新飞天赞  为敦煌小麦平均亩产一千斤而作
陶捷
1958-09-02
第7版()
专栏：

　　新飞天赞  为敦煌小麦平均亩产一千斤而作
　　陶捷
　　我虽然不是一个画家，
　　也要画一幅新的敦煌壁画，
　　不用赤橙黄绿青蓝紫，
　　单用敦煌人心里怒放的花！
　　我不画释迦牟尼、观世音，
　　画一群飞天小神笑哈哈，
　　他们的舞姿健美神气大，
　　双手托麦穗，不再抱琵琶。
　　背景是日出东方满天霞，
　　六万亩小麦光彩胜过五色沙，
　　千斤县的喜报向全国飞，
　　好像一道彩虹当空挂。
　　我停笔沉思又添几笔——
　　敦煌人嘱咐飞天小神捎个话，
　　邀请神农氏下界来看看，
　　敦煌的亩产明年还要加大！
　　我虽然不是一个画家，
　　今天画出了这幅敦煌新壁画，
　　敦煌人到莫高窟问问常书鸿，
　　这是临摹还是写生画？
