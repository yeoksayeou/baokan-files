Title: Li Dequan Departs for Czechoslovakia to Attend Health Conference
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The People's Republic of China's Ministry of Health delegation, led by Minister of Health Li Dequan and Vice Minister Cui Yitian, to attend the Third Conference of Ministers of Health of Socialist Countries held in the Czechoslovak Republic, left Beijing on the 9th.

### 李德全赴捷出席保健会议

1958-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    李德全赴捷出席保健会议
    新华社11日讯　出席在捷克斯洛伐克共和国召开的第三届社会主义国家保健部长会议的中华人民共和国卫生部代表团，在卫生部部长李德全和副部长崔义田率领下，已在9日离开北京。
