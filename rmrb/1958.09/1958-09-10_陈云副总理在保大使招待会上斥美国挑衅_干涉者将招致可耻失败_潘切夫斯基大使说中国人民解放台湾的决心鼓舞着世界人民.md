Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Condemns US Provocations at Bulgarian Ambassador's Reception; Interventionists Will Meet a Disgraceful Defeat; Ambassador Panchevski Says the Chinese People's Determination to Liberate Taiwan Inspires the People of the World
Author:
Date: 1958-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Vice Premier Chen Yun today sternly condemned the US imperialists for escalating tensions in the Taiwan Strait area and blatantly threatening to expand their aggression to the coastal islands of mainland China. He said, "This brutal act of interference in our internal affairs by US imperialism will result in nothing but a shameful defeat for themselves."

Vice Premier Chen Yun said: "We Chinese people, like the people of all countries in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, are engaged in the great cause of socialist construction with soaring revolutionary zeal, but we have never forgotten that imperialist aggressive blocs still exist in the world. They stubbornly pursue the long-outdated colonialist policy, constantly threatening world peace and tranquility. The US and British imperialists, in defiance of the peace aspirations of the people of the world, not only disregard the resolutions of the United Nations Emergency Special Session and fail to withdraw their troops from Lebanon and Jordan, but are intensifying new provocations in Lebanon and other parts of the Middle East."

Vice Premier Chen Yun said: "Recently, US imperialism has again intensified tensions in the Taiwan Strait area, blatantly threatening to expand its aggression to the coastal islands of mainland China. This provocative act by the United States has not only aroused the utmost indignation of the Chinese people, but has also been unanimously condemned by peace-loving people all over the world. This brutal act of interference in our internal affairs by US imperialism will result in nothing but a shameful defeat for themselves."

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, held a grand reception at the New Qiao Hotel tonight to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, President Dong Biwu of the Supreme People's Court, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo attended the reception.

Vice Premier Chen Yun spoke at the reception. He said: "The Chinese people are excitedly watching the glorious achievements made by the Bulgarian people under the leadership of the long-tested Bulgarian Communist Party and the Bulgarian People's Government in the great cause of building socialism and safeguarding peace in the Balkans and the world."

Vice Premier Chen Yun said: "Today, Bulgaria stands tall on the Balkan Peninsula as a socialist country with a developed industry and large-scale cooperative and mechanized agriculture. The Bulgarian people are confidently implementing the Third Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy, marching heroically towards the great goal of building socialism. The Bulgarian people have once again vividly demonstrated the infinite vitality of Marxism-Leninism and the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system with their practical actions."

Vice Premier Chen Yun said: "The Bulgarian Communist Party has made outstanding contributions in the struggle to defend Marxism-Leninism and combat modern revisionism. The Chinese people are delighted and encouraged by the achievements and contributions of the fraternal Bulgarian people."

Ambassador Panchevski spoke at the beginning of the reception. He said: "The Bulgarian people have traversed an arduous and heroic path in fourteen years of free life. Under the leadership of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the People's Government, with the continuous and comprehensive assistance of the Soviet Union and the closest cooperation with the People's Republic of China and other people's democracies, the Bulgarian people have achieved significant accomplishments in all aspects of socialist construction in a short period of time."

The ambassador said: "Bulgaria steadfastly makes efforts in the struggle of all peace-loving people against imperialist aggression, especially in the cause of safeguarding peace in the Balkan region. Bulgaria also makes unreserved contributions to the cause of consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union."

The ambassador said: "The great achievements of socialist construction in the People's Republic of China, the achievements of the Chinese government's peace-loving foreign policy, and the Chinese people's determination to liberate Taiwan expressed in the statement by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, inspire the creative labor and firm will to defend peace of the Bulgarian people and the people of other countries."

More than five hundred people attended this grand reception, which lasted for one and a half hours. Among the ambassador's guests were people from all walks of life in China and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 陈云副总理在保大使招待会上斥美国挑衅  干涉者将招致可耻失败  潘切夫斯基大使说中国人民解放台湾的决心鼓舞着世界人民

1958-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈云副总理在保大使招待会上斥美国挑衅
    干涉者将招致可耻失败
    潘切夫斯基大使说中国人民解放台湾的决心鼓舞着世界人民
    据新华社9日讯　陈云副总理今天严厉谴责美帝国主义在台湾海峡地区加剧紧张局势，公然威胁要把侵略范围扩大到中国大陆的沿海岛屿的罪恶行为。他说：“美帝国主义这一粗暴干涉我国内政的行为，除了给自己招来可耻的失败以外，不会有其他结果。”
    陈云副总理说：“我们中国人民，同以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民一样，正以冲天的革命干劲从事于伟大的社会主义建设，但是我们从来没有忘记世界上还存在着帝国主义侵略集团。他们顽固地奉行早已过时的殖民主义政策，时刻威胁着世界的和平和安宁，美英帝国主义违反全世界人民的和平愿望，不但无视联合国紧急特别会议的决议，不从黎巴嫩和约旦撤出他们的军队，反而变本加厉地在黎巴嫩和中东其他地区进行新的挑衅。”
    陈云副总理说：“最近，美帝国主义又在台湾海峡地区加剧紧张局势，公然威胁要把侵略范围扩大到中国大陆的沿海岛屿。美国的这种挑衅行为，不但激起了中国人民的无比愤慨，而且受到了全世界爱好和平的人民的一致谴责。美帝国主义这一粗暴干涉我国内政的行为：除了给自己招来可耻的失败以外，不会有其他结果。”
    据新华社9日讯　保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基今晚在新侨饭店举行盛大招待会，庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆日。
    周恩来总理，陈云、聂荣臻副总理，林伯渠、李济深、黄炎培、陈叔通副委员长，董必武院长和外交部罗贵波副部长，都出席了招待会。
    陈云副总理在招待会上讲了话。他说：“中国人民兴奋地注视着保加利亚人民在久经考验的保加利亚共产党和保加利亚人民政府的领导下，在建设社会主义和维护巴尔干和世界和平的伟大事业中逐日取得的光辉成就。”
    陈云副总理说：“今天，保加利亚以一个具有发达的工业和大规模合作化和机械化的农业的社会主义国家巍然地屹立在巴尔干半岛上。保加利亚人民正在满怀信心地执行着发展国民经济的第三个五年计划，向建成社会主义的伟大目标豪迈地前进。保加利亚人民以自己的实际行动再一次生动地证明了马克思列宁主义的无限生命力和社会主义制度的无比优越性。”
    陈云副总理说：“在捍卫马克思列宁主义、反击现代修正主义的斗争中，保加利亚共产党作出了卓越贡献。中国人民对兄弟的保加利亚人民的成就和贡献感到欢欣鼓舞。”
    潘切夫斯基大使在招待会开始的时候讲话。他说：“保加利亚人民在十四年的自由生活中经历了艰辛而英勇的道路。保加利亚人民在保加利亚共产党和人民政府的领导下，在苏联不断的全面的援助和同中华人民共和国以及其他人民民主国家最密切的合作中，在不长的时期里，在社会主义建设各个方面取得了重大的成就。”
    大使说：“保加利亚在一切爱好和平人民反对帝国主义侵略的斗争中，特别在保卫巴尔干地区和平的事业中，坚定不移地作出努力。在巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营团结的事业中，保加利亚也毫无保留地作出贡献。”
    大使说：“中华人民共和国社会主义建设的巨大成就、中国政府热爱和平的外交政策的成就和国务院总理周恩来同志声明中所表示的中国人民解放台湾的决心，鼓舞着保加利亚人民和其他国家人民的创造性劳动和保卫和平的坚决意志。”
    有五百多人参加的这个盛大招待会共举行了一个半小时。在大使的客人中还有中国各方面的人士和各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
