Title: Prosperous Harvest
Author: Sheng Guo
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 7

    Ningjin County, Hebei Province, held a "Three-in-One" (integrating politics, culture, and technology) school to further improve culture and technical studies. This is a "Three-in-One" school student transplanting cotton seedlings in an intercropping field of cotton and wheat. The lower right picture shows commune members studying in the "Three-in-One" school.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Sheng Guo photographed
