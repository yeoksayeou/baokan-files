Author: Zhao Guangsheng
Date: 1958-09-16

Reader's Letters

    A Mongolian Youth's Determination
    I am a Mongolian youth, living on the grasslands of Inner Mongolia in the motherland, and I grew up under the nurturing of New China. The motherland is my mother.
    American imperialism is a vicious wolf, biting and harming peaceful and happy people everywhere. Not long ago, it ran to the Middle East to harm the people of Lebanon; it attempted to interfere in the revolution of the people of Iraq. Now it wants to bite my loving mother—threatening our country with force to liberate Jinmen, Mazu, and Taiwan.
    I am a son of the motherland. I have the responsibility to protect my mother—the safety of the motherland. To safeguard the security of the motherland, I will not hesitate to give my life and strength. When the motherland needs me, I will put down my pen and pick up my gun and rush to the front lines.
    In addition, I want to respond to the call of the motherland with practical actions and act actively:
    1. Strengthen political and current affairs study, raise revolutionary vigilance, and expose the aggressive conspiracies of American imperialism.
    2. Work hard to ensure the early and over-fulfillment of my work tasks.
    3. Actively publicize current affairs policies to the people, so that the people can further recognize the aggressive face of American imperialism.
    4. Strengthen physical exercise, actively participate in military training, and improve the ability to defend the motherland.
    Grain Section, Kezuohou Banner, Zhemeng League, Inner Mongolia
        Zhao Guangsheng

### 一个蒙族青年的决心
赵光升
1958-09-16
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    一个蒙族青年的决心
    我是个蒙族青年，生活在祖国的内蒙古草原上，是在新中国的抚育下长大的。祖国就是我的母亲。
    美帝国主义是一条恶狼，到处咬害和平幸福的人民。不久前跑到中东危害黎巴嫩人民；企图干涉伊拉克人民的革命。现在它又想来咬我慈爱的母亲——以武力威胁我国解放金门、马祖和台湾。
    我是祖国的儿子。有责保护母亲——祖国的安全。为了保卫祖国的安全不惜我的生命和力量，当祖国需要我的时候，我就放下笔杆拿起枪杆，奔赴前线。
    另外，我要以实际行动响应祖国的召唤，积极地行动起来：
    一、加强政治和时事学习，提高革命警惕，揭穿美帝国主义的侵略阴谋。
    二、努力工作，保证提前和超额完成自己的工作任务。
    三、积极向人民群众宣传时事政策，使人民进一步认清美帝国主义的侵略面目。
    四、加强锻炼身体，积极参加兵役训练，提高保卫祖国的本领。
    内蒙古哲盟科左后旗粮食科
        赵光升
