Title: People in Various Parts of Vietnam Denounce US War Provocations, Ready to Fight Alongside the Chinese People; Hanoi City Council Sends Telegram Supporting Our Country's Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th - The Chinese Embassy in Vietnam continues to receive numerous letters and resolutions from mass rallies held by people from all walks of life and overseas Chinese in Vietnam, unanimously expressing enthusiastic support for Premier Zhou Enlai's statement, supporting China's liberation of Taiwan and the coastal islands, and opposing the provocative actions of US imperialism.

    A letter from all the staff of the Vietnam Handicraft Company stated that US imperialism is plotting to push mankind into the abyss of war, but the people of the world will not allow their conspiracy to succeed. The Chinese and Vietnamese people are two brothers as close as lips and teeth, and we firmly support the Chinese people in liberating Taiwan and the coastal islands. A letter from all the staff of the Vietnam Sundries Import and Export Company stated that we will always be vigilant, deepen our hatred for US imperialism, and be ready to work with our Chinese brothers to completely eliminate the US aggressors who dare to risk launching a war of aggression.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th - The third session of the Hanoi City People's Council today passed a telegram to the Beijing Municipal People's Congress, expressing firm support for the Chinese people's struggle against US imperialism's interference in China's internal affairs and for the liberation of Taiwan and the coastal islands. The telegram said that the Chinese people's struggle is a contribution to defending Southeast Asia and world peace. 50,000 people in Hanoi held a demonstration to oppose US imperialism's interference in China's internal affairs and support the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan.

### 越南各地人民怒斥美国战争挑衅  随时准备同中国人民并肩作战  河内市议会致电支持我国解放台湾

1958-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越南各地人民怒斥美国战争挑衅
    随时准备同中国人民并肩作战
    河内市议会致电支持我国解放台湾
    据新华社河内27日电  中国驻越南大使馆继续不断地收到越南各界人民和华侨寄来的许多信件和他们举行的群众集会的决议，一致热烈表示支持周恩来总理的声明，支持中国解放台湾和沿海岛屿，反对美帝国主义的挑衅行为。
    越南工艺品公司全体工作人员的来信说，美帝国主义阴谋想把人类推进战争的深渊，但世界人民不会允许他们的阴谋的实现。中越人民是唇齿相依的两兄弟，我们坚决支持中国人民解放台湾和沿海岛屿。越南杂品进出口公司全体工作人员的来信说，我们将随时提高警惕、加深对美帝国主义的同仇敌忾，随时准备同中国兄弟人民在一起来彻底消灭胆敢冒险发动侵略战争的美国侵略者。
    据新华社河内27日电  河内市人民议会第三次会议今天通过了一封致北京市人民代表大会的电报，表示坚决支持中国人民反对美帝国主义干涉中国内政和解放台湾和沿海岛屿的斗争。电报说，中国人民的这一斗争对保卫东南亚和世界和平是一种贡献。河内五万群众举行示威游行，反对美帝国主义干涉中国内政，支持中国人民解放台湾。
