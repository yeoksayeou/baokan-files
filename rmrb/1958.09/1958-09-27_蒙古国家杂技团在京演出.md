Title: Mongolian National Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, met with Ba. Arrigi, head of the Mongolian People's Republic National Acrobatic Troupe, and others at the People's Theater this evening, unanimously congratulating the ever-growing friendship between the fraternal Chinese and Mongolian peoples and the continuous strengthening of cultural cooperation between the two countries.

Also present at the People's Theater this evening to watch the performance of the Mongolian People's Republic acrobatic artists were diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

During the meeting between Vice Chairman Li Jishen and Ba. Arrigi, those who were also received included the acrobatic troupe's artistic director, meritorious actor Damdinsuren, meritorious actor Danzhan, acrobatic troupe secretary general Dabgedorj, and actor Chendayuwus. Lubusang, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, was also present at the meeting.

Today's performance by the acrobatic artists of the Mongolian People's Republic was their second performance since arriving in Beijing. The martial arts, soft acrobatics, comedy, trapeze, strongman, and high-altitude trapeze acts with strong national characteristics that they performed in the two performances repeatedly won warm applause and cheers of "bravo" from the audience. At the welcome of the audience, the artists took curtain calls again and again, some as many as six times.

After the performance ended tonight, Li Jishen, Zhang Zhixiang, Zheng Zhenduo, Ji Pengfei and others, accompanied by Ambassador Lubusang, went to the stage, shook hands warmly with the acrobatic artists one by one, and presented them with flower baskets.

The Mongolian Acrobatic Troupe already held its first performance in Beijing last night. Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing watched the performance.

The National Acrobatic Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic is visiting China at the invitation of the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. They arrived in Beijing on the 22nd. Before this, they had already visited and performed in Shenyang, Anshan, Lushun-Dalian, Changchun, Fushun and other places in Northeast China.

### 蒙古国家杂技团在京演出

1958-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古国家杂技团在京演出
    据新华社26日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、文化部副部长郑振铎和外交部副部长姬鹏飞，今天晚间在人民剧场接见蒙古人民共和国国家杂技团团长巴·阿尔里吉等的时候，一致祝贺兄弟般的中蒙两国人民的友谊日益发展，祝贺两国文化合作不断加强。
    今天晚间在人民剧场观看蒙古人民共和国杂技艺术家演出的，还有各国驻华使节。
    在李济深副委员长接见巴·阿尔里吉的时候，接受接见的还有杂技团的艺术指导、功勋演员达姆丁苏伦，功勋演员丹占，杂技团秘书长达布格道尔吉和演员成达尤乌斯。接见时，蒙古人民共和国驻华大使鲁布桑也在座。
    蒙古人民共和国杂技艺术家今天的演出是他们来到北京后的第二次演出。他们在两次演出中所演的富有强烈民族风格的武术、柔术、滑稽、空中飞人、大力士和高架飞人等精采节目，不时博得全场观众的热烈掌声和叫“好”声，在观众的欢迎下，艺术家们一再谢幕，有的谢幕达六次之多。
    今晚的演出全部结束后，李济深、张致祥、郑振铎、姬鹏飞等由鲁布桑大使陪同走上舞台，同杂技艺术家们一一亲切握手，并向他们赠送了花篮。
    昨天晚间蒙古杂技团已在北京举行了首次演出。文化部部长沈雁冰观看了演出。
    蒙古人民共和国国家杂技团是根据中蒙文化合作协定1958年执行计划应邀来我国访问的。他们于22日来到北京，在这以前，他们已经在东北的沈阳、鞍山、旅大、长春、抚顺等地作了访问演出。
