Title: Sand Ripples
Author: Yang Zhongjian
Date: 1958-09-24
Page: 8

    Sand Ripples
    Yang Zhongjian supports Foreign Minister Chen Yi's statement.
  Confusing right and wrong in haste,
  Spouting nonsense.
  Daring to be rampant in our territory!
  If we don't wipe them out completely,
  How can we punish the thugs!
  The taste of paper tigers has long been sampled,
  Losing face on the battlefield,
  They actually jump over the wall like desperate dogs again.
  A thousand fingers point at them from people all over the world,
  Dying without disease.

### 浪淘沙
杨钟健
1958-09-24
第8版()
专栏：

    浪淘沙
    杨钟健拥护陈毅外长声明
  黑白倒置忙，
  信口雌黄。
  我领土内敢猖狂！
  如不尽情歼灭之，
  怎惩强梁！
  纸虎味早尝，
  丢丑疆场，
  居然狗急又跳墙。
  全世人民千夫指，
  无疾而亡。
