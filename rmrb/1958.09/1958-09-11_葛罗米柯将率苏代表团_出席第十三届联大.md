Title: Gromyko to Head Soviet Delegation to the 13th UN General Assembly
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. TASS, 9th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed a Soviet delegation headed by Foreign Minister Gromyko to attend the 13th session of the United Nations General Assembly, which will open in New York on September 16.

    The delegation members also include: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin (Deputy Head of the Delegation), Russian Federation Minister of Social Security Muravyeva, Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations Sobolev, and Soviet Foreign Ministry Collegium Member Chaplygin.
    The government of the Belorussian Republic has also appointed a delegation headed by the Republic's Foreign Minister Kiselev to attend this session of the General Assembly.

### 葛罗米柯将率苏代表团  出席第十三届联大

1958-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯将率苏代表团
    出席第十三届联大
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社9日讯：苏联部长会议已经任命以外交部长葛罗米柯为首的苏联代表团，去出席将于9月16日在纽约开幕的联合国大会第十三届会议。
    代表团成员还包括：苏联外交部副部长佐林（代表团副团长）、俄罗斯联邦社会赡养部长穆拉维耶娃、苏联驻联合国常驻代表索波列夫、苏联外交部部务委员会委员查拉普金。
    白俄罗斯共和国政府也已经任命了以共和国外交部长基谢廖夫为首的出席联大这次会议的代表团。
