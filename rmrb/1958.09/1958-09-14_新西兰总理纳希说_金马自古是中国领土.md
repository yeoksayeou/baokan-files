Title: New Zealand Prime Minister Nash Says Kinmen and Matsu Have Been Chinese Territory Since Ancient Times
Author:
Date: 1958-09-14
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wellington, September 12th: New Zealand Prime Minister Nash, when discussing the situation in the Taiwan Strait in Parliament on the evening of the 11th, explicitly stated that Kinmen and Matsu have historically been part of mainland China. He believes that the Chiang Kai-shek group's occupation of these two islands constitutes a threat to mainland China.

### 新西兰总理纳希说  金马自古是中国领土

1958-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    新西兰总理纳希说
    金马自古是中国领土
    新华社12日讯　惠灵吞消息：新西兰总理纳希11日晚间在议会上谈到台湾海峡局势时，明确表示金门和马祖在历史上就是中国大陆的一部分，他认为蒋介石集团盘据在这两个岛屿上构成了对中国大陆的威胁。
