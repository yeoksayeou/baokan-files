Title: Singing the People's Commune
Author:
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 3

　　Singing the People's Commune
　　(I) The happiness of the commune (ha) is endless
　　Welfare undertakings (ha) are all complete
　　All the land (ha) is connected into one piece
　　All kinds of machines (ha) are turning
    (II)
　　The Communist Party's kindness is like the sea
　　Leading the people
　　To the road of happiness
　　The people's minds are greatly liberated
　　Establishing a commune
　　Stepping into paradise in one step
    (III)
　　The benefits of the commune are endless
　　Happy things are done completely
　　Good life becomes more and more beautiful
　　The elderly become young again
    (IV)
　　A great leap forward in industry, agriculture, and animal husbandry
　　Establishing a commune meets the people's hearts
　　The Communist Party is planting a root of wealth for us
　　Eliminating thousands of years of poverty
    (V)
　　Sunflowers follow the sun
　　Water red flowers bloom by the roadside
　　The People's Commune is to be built
　　Male and female commune members like it

    (Folk songs of various ethnic groups in Minhe County. Originally published in Qinghai Daily)

### 歌唱人民公社

1958-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　歌唱人民公社
    （一）公社的幸福（哈）说不完
　　福利的事业（哈）俱全
　　全部的土地（哈）连成片
　　各样的机器（哈）都转
    （二）
　　恩情似海的共产党
　　把人民
　　引到幸福的路上
　　人民的思想大解放
　　办公社
　　一步儿登上了天堂
    （三）
　　公社的好处说不完
　　幸福事办了个齐全
　　好生活愈过愈美满
　　老年人变成了少年
    （四）
　　工农牧业大跃进
　　办公社应了人心
　　共产党为我们扎富根
　　消灭了千万年贫困
    （五）
　　向日葵跟着太阳转
　　水红花开在个路边
　　人民公社要兴建
　　男女社员们喜欢
    （民和县各族人民民歌。原载青海日报）
