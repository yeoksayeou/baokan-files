Culture Delivered to the Doorstep

　　Books Go to the Fields
    Recently, the Party branch of Jianshe Township in Tieling County, Liaoning Province, mobilized librarians to organize "mobile book stations" to deliver books directly to the fields to serve production. For example, when planting sweet potatoes, librarians proactively delivered two copies of "Sweet Potato Cultivation Methods" to the technicians of each team for reference; when planting potatoes, they delivered the book "Horticultural Cultivation Methods." After learning the "potato vernalization method," commune members used this method to plant potatoes, ensuring the germination rate and reducing the waste of potato seedlings. When swine fever occurred in the village, librarians promptly delivered the book "Swine Fever Prevention and Treatment Methods" to commune members. Commune members used this method to treat swine fever, achieving a cure rate of 80 percent.

### 图书下田

1958-09-04
第7版()
专栏：文化送上门

　　图书下田
    最近，辽宁省铁岭县建设乡的党支部发动图书管理员组织了“流动图书站”，把图书送到地里直接为生产服务。如栽地瓜时，图书管理员就主动地把两本“红薯栽培法”送给各队技术员作参考；在栽土豆时，他们又送上“园艺栽培法”一书，社员们学到了“土豆春化法”就用这种方法栽土豆，保证了出芽率，减少了土豆栽子的浪费。当村里发生猪瘟时，图书管理员就及时把“猪瘟防治法”的书籍送给社员们看，社员们运用这个方法治猪瘟，治愈率达到百分之八十。
