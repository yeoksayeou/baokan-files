Title: A Tribute to the Soldiers on the Front Lines
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 8

    A Tribute to the Soldiers on the Front Lines
    Worker Li Xue'ao, State-owned Factory 541
    Salute! Salute!
    To the heroic soldiers on the Fujian Front Lines!
    It is you who, with unparalleled vigilance,
    Guard the gate of our motherland,
    Guard the thriving of thousands of blast furnaces,
    Guard the birth of thousands of people's communes!
    Salute! Salute!
    To the heroic soldiers on the Fujian Front Lines!
    It is you who, with outstanding tactics,
    Defeated the enemy's planes and ships,
    Smashed the skulls of the pirates,
    Knocked down the "prestige" of the traitors!
    Salute! Salute!
    To the heroic soldiers on the Fujian Front Lines!
    We give you—
    More ore,
    More steel ingots,
    More machines,
    More flour!
    Salute! Salute!
    To the heroic soldiers on the Fujian Front Lines!
    Heroes—
    Raise your deep green cannons even higher!
    Boom! Boom! Boom! With your massive muzzles,
    Unleash the roar of 600 million people:
    We will surely liberate Matsu and Kinmen!!
    We will surely liberate Taiwan and Penghu!!

### 向前线将士致敬
李学鳌
1958-09-11
第8版()
专栏：

    向前线将士致敬
    国营541厂工人　李学鳌
    致敬！致敬！
    向英勇的福建前线将士致敬！
    是你们以无比的机警，
    看守着祖国的大门，
    看守着千万个高炉茁长，
    看守着千万个人民公社诞生！
    致敬！致敬！
    向英勇的福建前线将士致敬！
    是你们以卓越的战术，
    击败了敌人的飞机、舰艇，
    打碎了海盗的脑骨，
    打掉了卖国贼的“威风”！
    致敬！致敬！
    向英勇的福建前线将士致敬！
    我们以——
    更多的矿石，
    更多的钢锭，
    更多的机器，
    更多的面粉！
    致敬！致敬！
    向英勇的福建前线将士致敬！
    英雄们——
    更高地支起深绿色的炮身！
    轰！轰！轰！用粗大的炮口，
    喷出六万万人民的吼声：
    我们一定解放马祖、金门！！
    我们一定解放台湾、澎湖！！
