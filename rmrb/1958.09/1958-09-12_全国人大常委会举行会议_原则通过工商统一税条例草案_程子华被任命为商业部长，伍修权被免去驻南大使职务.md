Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Principles Adopted for Draft of Consolidated Industrial and Commercial Tax Regulations; Cheng Zihua Appointed Minister of Commerce, Wu Xiuquan Removed from Post of Ambassador to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 101st meeting this afternoon and principally adopted the "Draft of the People's Republic of China Consolidated Industrial and Commercial Tax Regulations."

    The meeting also passed resolutions approving the organizational regulations of the people's congresses and people's committees at all levels of the Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture of Gansu Province; approving the organizational regulations of the people's congresses and people's committees of the Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Gansu Province. Chairman Mao has ordered the promulgation of these two organizational regulations based on the resolutions of the meeting.

    The meeting decided to merge the First Ministry of Commerce and the Second Ministry of Commerce into the Ministry of Commerce.

    The meeting also approved a list of appointments and removals.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th—Chairman Mao Zedong, based on the decision of the 101st meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Cheng Zihua as Minister of Commerce.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th—Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong removed Wu Xiuquan from the post of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Federative People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

### 全国人大常委会举行会议  原则通过工商统一税条例草案  程子华被任命为商业部长，伍修权被免去驻南大使职务

1958-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人大常委会举行会议
    原则通过工商统一税条例草案
    程子华被任命为商业部长，伍修权被免去驻南大使职务
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天下午举行第一百零一次会议，原则通过了“中华人民共和国工商统一税条例（草案）”。
    会议还通过决议批准甘肃省临夏回族自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例；批准甘肃省甘南藏族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例。毛主席已根据会议的决议，命令公布这两个组织条例。
    会议决定将第一商业部和第二商业部合并为商业部。
    会议还通过了任免名单。
    新华社11日讯　毛泽东主席根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第一百零一次会议的决定，任命程子华为商业部部长。
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去伍修权中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
