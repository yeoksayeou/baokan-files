Title: Sweat
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 5

    Sweat soaks the clothes,
    Sweat drips down from the corners of the garments;
    Every drop of sweat is for steel,
    Steel brings happiness for ten thousand years.

### 汗

1958-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    汗
    汗水淋湿了衣裳，
    汗顺着衣角往下淌；
    滴滴汗水为了钢铁，
    钢铁换来幸福万年长。
