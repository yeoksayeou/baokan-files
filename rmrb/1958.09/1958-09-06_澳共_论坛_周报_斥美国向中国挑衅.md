Title: Australian Communist Party "Forum" Weekly Denounces US Provocations Against China
Author:
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney: The Australian "Forum" weekly, in its latest issue, condemned the American aggressors for provoking China and demanded that the American provocateurs get out of Taiwan.

The "Forum" weekly also demanded that the Australian government abolish the Southeast Asia Treaty and the ANZUS "Security" Treaty, and recognize the Chinese government as the government of all of China, including Taiwan. The weekly pointed out that the United States is attempting, through these two aggressive treaties, to drag Australia into a military adventure against China.

### 澳共“论坛”周报  斥美国向中国挑衅

1958-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    澳共“论坛”周报
    斥美国向中国挑衅
    据新华社讯　悉尼消息：澳大利亚“论坛”周报在最近出版的一期中谴责美国侵略者向中国进行挑衅，并且要求美国挑衅者滚出台湾。
    “论坛”周报还要求澳大利亚政府废除东南亚条约和美澳新“安全”条约，并且承认中国政府为包括台湾在内的全中国的政府。这家周报指出，美国企图通过上述两个侵略性条约，把澳大利亚拖进对中国进行的军事冒险。
