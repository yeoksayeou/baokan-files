Title: Algerian People's Struggle for Independence Enters a New Stage: Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria Established
Author:
Date: 1958-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th, Cairo Dispatch: The "Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria" was declared in Cairo today and officially took office at 1:00 PM Algerian time.

After more than three years of guerrilla warfare against the French colonial army, the declaration of the establishment of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria is a glorious victory for the Algerian national independence movement, marking a new stage in the country's struggle for independence.

The establishment of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria was announced simultaneously in Algiers, Cairo, Tunis, and Rabat (Morocco). The Prime Minister of the Provisional Government is Ferhat Abbas, leader of the Algerian National Liberation Front.

After the establishment of the Provisional Government, Abbas read the following declaration:

"I am honored to issue this declaration, which has been issued in Algeria and has been conveyed to all residents on Algerian soil.

"The Algerian National Revolutionary Committee, in the name of the Algerian people, has decided to establish a provisional government representing the Republic of Algeria."

The cabinet list of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria is as follows:

Prime Minister: Ferhat Abbas
First Deputy Prime Minister: Ahmed Ben Bella
Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense: Krim Belkacem
Minister of State: Hussein Ait Ahmed, Rabah Bitat, Mohamed Boudiaf, and Mohamed Tedel
Minister of Foreign Affairs: Mohamed Lamine Debaghine
Minister of Armaments and Supply: Mahmoud Cherif
Minister of Interior: Ahdar Ben Tobbal
Minister of Transport and Communications: Abdelhafid Boussouf
Minister of North African Affairs: Abdelhamid Mehri
Minister of Information: Mohamed Yazid
Minister of Social Affairs: Youssef Ben Khedda
Minister of Culture: Ahmed Tewfik El Madani
Undersecretaries of State: Lamine Khane, Omar Oussedik, and Mustapha Stambouli.

The Provisional Government will be responsible to the Algerian National Revolutionary Committee. After the Provisional Government was announced, the flag of the Republic of Algeria, half green and half white, with a red crescent and star, was raised on the building of the Cairo headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Front.

A spokesperson for the Algerian National Liberation Front office in Cairo said, "We will inform the governments of all countries of the establishment of the Algerian government and request that they recognize it."

Abbas said after reading the declaration that the Provisional Government would issue a policy statement next week. He said the location of the Provisional Government had not yet been determined.

### 阿人民争取独立斗争进入新阶段  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府成立

1958-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿人民争取独立斗争进入新阶段
    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府成立
    新华社19日讯　开罗消息：“阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府”今天在开罗宣告成立，并且在阿尔及利亚时间下午一时正式就职。
    在对法国殖民军进行了三年多的游击战争以后，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的宣告成立，是阿尔及利亚民族独立运动的一个光辉的胜利，这标志着这个国家争取独立的斗争进入一个新的阶段。
    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的成立，是在阿尔及利亚宣布后，又在开罗、突尼斯和拉巴特（摩洛哥）三地同时宣布的。临时政府的总理是阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线领导人费尔哈特·阿巴斯。
    阿巴斯在临时政府宣布成立后宣读了如下的宣言：
    “我荣幸地发表这项宣言，这项宣言已经在阿尔及利亚发表，并且已经传达给阿尔及利亚土地上的全体居民。
    “阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会以阿尔及利亚人民的名义，决定成立一个代表阿尔及利亚共和国的临时政府。”
    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府内阁名单如下：
    总理：费尔哈特·阿巴斯
    第一副总理：艾哈迈德·本·贝拉
    副总理兼国防部长：克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆
    国务部长：侯赛因·埃特·艾哈迈德、拉巴·比塔特、穆罕默德·布伊迪亚和穆罕默德·特德尔
    外交部长：穆罕默德·拉明·德巴金
    军备和供应部长：马哈茂德·谢里
    内政部长：阿赫达尔·本·陶巴勒
    交通联络部长：阿卜杜勒·哈菲兹·布苏夫
    北非事务部长：阿卜杜勒·哈米德·迈赫里
    宣传部长：穆罕默德·雅齐德
    社会事务部长：优素福·本·赫达
    文化部长：艾哈迈德·陶菲克·迈达尼
    国务次长：拉明·哈恩、奥马尔·乌西迪克和穆斯塔法·伊斯坦布利。
    临时政府将对阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会负责。临时政府宣布成立后在阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线开罗总部的大厦上即升起了阿尔及利亚共和国国旗，国旗为半绿色半白色，并有一个红新月和星。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线驻开罗办事处的发言人说，“我们将把阿尔及利亚政府成立的消息通知各国政府，并且要求它们予以承认。”
    阿巴斯在宣读宣言后还说，临时政府将在下星期发表政策声明。他说临时政府的所在地尚未确定。
