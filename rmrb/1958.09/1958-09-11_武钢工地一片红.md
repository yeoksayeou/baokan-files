Title: The Wugang Construction Site is a Sea of Red
Author: Yu Zhou, Wang Guizhou
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 6

    Wugang Workers' Poem, Wugang Workers' Painting
    The Wugang construction site is a sea of red,
    No. 1 blast furnace is completely completed.
    The steel army is eager to fight,
    Ladle cars are arranged in a long line.
    Waiting for the motherland to give an order,
    We will charge on horseback.
    Let the whistle speak for us,
    Let the molten iron dye the sky red,
    The good news of the blast furnace tapping iron spreads throughout the world,
    The American imperialists are beaten black and blue by iron fists.

### 武钢工地一片红
宇宙  王贵州
1958-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    武钢工人　宇宙诗　武钢工人　王贵州画
    武钢工地一片红，
    一号高炉全部竣工。
    钢铁的大军磨拳擦掌，
    铁水罐排成一道长龙。
    单等祖国一声令下，
    我们就要跨马冲锋。
    让汽笛为我们发言，
    让铁水把天空染红，
    高炉出铁的喜讯传遍世界，
    铁拳揍的美国佬鼻青脸肿。
