Title: Famine in Eastern Uttar Pradesh, India, Many Legislators Demand Immediate Action from the Government

Author:

Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 1st. Forty-three members of both houses of the Indian Parliament today issued a joint statement demanding that the government accept Professor Shibanlal Saksena's thirteen-point demands on resolving the famine in eastern Uttar Pradesh. According to Uttar Pradesh's Food Minister, Zahir, the central government has agreed to urgently supply 75,000 tons of wheat to the region in September, which will be sold through low-priced food stores.

### 印度北方邦东部粮荒  许多议员要求政府立即采取措施

1958-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度北方邦东部粮荒
    许多议员要求政府立即采取措施
    据新华社新德里1日电　印度国会两院的四十三位议员今天在这里发表联合声明，要求政府接受议员希班拉尔·萨克森纳教授提出的关于解决北方邦东部粮荒问题的十三点要求。据北方邦粮食部长查希尔说，中央政府同意在9月份赶紧把七万五千吨小麦供应这个地区，这些小麦将通过廉价粮食店出售。
