Title: Eliminate the Four Pests
Author: Dengfeng
Date: 1958-09-11
Page: 7

    Eliminate the Four Pests
    The four pests, the four pests, our hatred runs deep as the sea,
    They cause illness, weakening the body,
    Preventing production, and costing us money,
    Wasting grain, a truly great harm,
    If we want to eliminate them, let's all come together.
            —Dengfeng

### 歼灭四害
登封
1958-09-11
第7版()
专栏：

    歼灭四害
    四害四害，仇深似海，
    叫人生病，身弱体衰，
    不能生产，还得破财，
    糟踏粮食，真是大害，
    要想消灭，大家齐来。
            ——登封
