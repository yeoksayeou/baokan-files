Date: 1958-09-03

From the Readers

    An Eggplant is One Foot and Two Inches Long
    This year, the Yong'an County Farm in Fujian Province has had a bumper harvest of eggplants. Among them is a rare, unusually large eggplant of the Yong'an variety, measuring one foot and two inches in length, seven inches and eight fen in circumference, and weighing one catty and thirteen liang net.

### 一只茄子身长一尺二

1958-09-03
第3版()
专栏：读者中来

    一只茄子身长一尺二
    福建永安县农场今年茄子大丰收，其中有一条永安品种的肥大罕见的茄子，身长一尺二寸，体粗七寸八分，净重一斤十三两。
