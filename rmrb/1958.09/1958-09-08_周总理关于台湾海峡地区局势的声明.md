Title: Premier Zhou's Statement on the Situation in the Taiwan Strait Area
Author:
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 4

The Chinese government is fully entitled to resolutely strike at the Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on the coastal islands and take necessary military actions. Any foreign interference is a criminal act of infringing upon Chinese sovereignty.
                       —— Premier Zhou's Statement on the Situation in the Taiwan Strait Area

### 周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明

1958-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中国政府完全有权对盘据在沿海岛屿的蒋介石部队给予坚决的打击和采取必要的军事行动，任何外来的干涉，都是侵犯中国主权的罪恶行为。
                       ——周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明
