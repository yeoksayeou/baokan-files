Title: Qiu Caikang Is Recovering and Will Soon Be Discharged From the Hospital
Author:
Date: 1958-09-19
Page: 7

Qiu Caikang, a worker at the Shanghai Iron and Steel Plant No. 3 who was severely burned by molten iron, is gradually recovering his health and will be discharged from the hospital before National Day after three months of treatment by the medical staff of Shanghai Guangci Hospital with a spirit of communism and with the care of all sectors of society.

Currently, Qiu Caikang is in the hospital and can already read newspapers and listen to the radio every day, and is in good spirits.

### 丘财康伤愈即将出院

1958-09-19
第7版()
专栏：

    丘财康伤愈即将出院
    本报讯　被铁水严重烫伤的上海钢铁厂第三厂工人丘财康在上海广慈医院医务人员以共产主义精神抢救和社会上各方面的关怀下，经过三个月的治疗，现已逐渐恢复健康，并将于国庆节前出院。
    目前，丘财康在医院里，每天已可以读报和听收音机，精神很好。
