　　Private (Gun Poetry)
　　A silver star is not big,
　　Adorned on the two corners of the collar,
　　Walking on the street, everyone praises,
　　The star gives me great encouragement.
　　The stars of the world gather in the Milky Way,
　　There are many heroes in the People's Liberation Army,
　　If you compare the silver star to a hero,
　　I am the brightest one.
(A soldier)

### 列兵

1958-09-01
第8版()
专栏：

　　列兵（枪杆诗）
　　一颗银星不算大，
　　缀在衣领两角下，
　　走在街上人人夸，
　　星星给我鼓舞大。
　　天下星星集银河，
　　解放军里英雄多，
　　若将银星比英雄，
　　我是最亮那一颗。
                （一战士）
