Title: GDR Party and Government Delegation to Visit Bulgaria
Author: 
Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 21st: According to newspapers here today, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, a party and government delegation of the German Democratic Republic will pay a friendly visit to Bulgaria in October. The delegation will be led by Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

The delegation members include GDR Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, Chairman of the States Chamber Parliament O. Bach, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz, and Deputy Prime Minister Scholz, among others.

### 民主德国党政代表团将访问保加利亚

1958-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国党政代表团将访问保加利亚
    新华社索非亚21日电　据这里的报纸今天报道，应保加利亚共产党中央委员会和保加利亚部长会议的邀请，德意志民主共和国党和政府代表团将在10月间到保加利亚进行友好访问。代表团将由德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希率领。
    代表团成员包括民主德国总理格罗提渥，州联议院主席奥·巴赫，副总理兼外交部长博尔茨和副总理绍尔茨等。
