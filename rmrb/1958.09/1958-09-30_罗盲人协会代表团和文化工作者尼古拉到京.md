Title: Romanian Blind Association Delegation and Cultural Worker Nicolae Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Romanian Blind Association delegation, led by Naya Filip, chairman of the Romanian Central Blind Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China Blind Welfare Association.

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Nistorescu Nicolae, a Romanian mass culture worker who came to China for a visit according to the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian cultural cooperation agreement, arrived in Beijing on the 28th.

### 罗盲人协会代表团和文化工作者尼古拉到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    罗盲人协会代表团和文化工作者尼古拉到京
    新华社29日讯  由罗马尼亚中央盲人协会主席纳亚·非力浦率领的罗马尼亚盲协代表团，应中国盲人福利会邀请，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    新华社29日讯  根据中罗文化合作协定1958年执行计划来我国访问的罗马尼亚群众文化工作者尼斯道尔·尼古拉28日到达北京。
