Title: Harvest Rhymes
Author: Peng Dacheng, Tang Zhixue, Wu Changya
Date: 1958-09-03
Page: 8
　　Harvest Rhymes

　　A pumpkin takes three people to hug,
　　A sweet potato fills half a basket,
　　Half a tomato is too much to eat,
　　Half an ear of corn is enough for you to taste.
      (Danleng, Peng Dacheng)
　　Corn stalks as tall as the sky,
　　Children carried on their backs amidst the white clouds,
　　Little brother smiles with joy,
　　Sitting on a plane to transport it away.
      (Nanchong, Tang Zhixue)
　　A grain mountain ten thousand feet high,
　　White clouds entwined around the mountainside;
　　The sun is so tired it sweats profusely,
　　Unable to climb over the ridge in half a day.
　　  (Suining, Sichuan, Wu Changya)

### 丰收谣
彭大成　唐治学　吴昌亚
1958-09-03
第8版()
专栏：丰收谣

　　丰收谣
　　一个南瓜三人抱，
　　一个红苕装半筐，
　　半边蕃茄吃不了，
　　半截包谷够你尝。
      （丹棱　彭大成）
　　包谷秆儿高齐天，
　　娃娃背在白云间，
　　弟弟眉开眼又笑，
　　坐上飞机把它搬。
      （南充　唐治学）
　　一座粮山高万丈，
　　白云缠在山腰上；
　　太阳累得汗长淌，
　　半天爬不上山岗。
　　  （四川遂宁　吴昌亚）
