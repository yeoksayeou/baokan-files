Title: Stepping Up the Creation of Tension in the Taiwan Area: U.S. Again Transfers an Air Force Squadron to the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-09-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, September 1st: According to an announcement by the U.S. Tactical Air Command, a squadron of U.S. F-100 jet fighters has been transferred from George Air Force Base in California to Clark U.S. Air Force Base in the Philippines to reinforce the Seventh Fleet, which is creating tension in the Taiwan area. These aircraft landed in Hawaii on August 31st en route to the Far East. The squadron's equipment and logistics personnel are also being transported to the Philippines by transport aircraft. The report said that a U.S. Air Force squadron typically has eighteen aircraft.

### 加紧在台湾地区制造紧张局势  美又调一空军中队到远东

1958-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    加紧在台湾地区制造紧张局势
    美又调一空军中队到远东
    新华社1日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国战术空军司令部宣布，美国F—100型喷气机的一个中队，已经由加利福尼亚州乔治空军基地调往菲律宾的克拉克美国空军基地，增援正在台湾地区制造紧张局势的第七舰队。这队飞机8月31日飞往远东途中，在夏威夷着陆。这个中队的装备和后勤人员，也正在由运输机载运前往菲律宾。消息说，美国空军一个中队通常有十八架飞机。
