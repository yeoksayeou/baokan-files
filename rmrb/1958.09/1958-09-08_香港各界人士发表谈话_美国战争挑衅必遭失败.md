Title: People from All Walks of Life in Hong Kong Issue Statements: U.S. War Provocations Are Bound to Fail
Author:
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, September 7th: People from all walks of life in Hong Kong issued statements to Xinhua reporters today, expressing their firm support for Premier Zhou Enlai's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait and condemning the United States for its war provocations against China.

    Li Sheng, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, said: "Premier Zhou's statement on the situation in the Taiwan Strait reiterates our country's attitude and determination to liberate Taiwan, and issues a serious warning to the US imperialists' aggressive conspiracies in Taiwan and along our country's coast. Workers in all industries in Hong Kong and Kowloon unanimously express their firm support."
    Feng Yong, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Planting General Association, said: "If the United States dares to disregard the repeated warnings of the Chinese people, it is destined to suffer a miserable defeat. We warmly support Premier Zhou's statement."
    Gao Zhuoxiong, President of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, pointed out: The United States' occupation of China's territory of Taiwan and its current war provocations against China are a serious threat to peace in the Far East and the world. He said: "Premier Zhou's statement expresses the solemn position of the 600 million people of our country, and I express my firm support."
    Chen Pishi, Chairman of the Hong Kong Chinese Reform Association, said: "We firmly condemn the US imperialists for occupying our sacred territory of Taiwan."
    Liu Fang, Chairman of the Hong Kong South China Film Workers Union, said: "Taiwan and the Penghu Islands are an inseparable part of Chinese territory, and the Chinese people's liberation of their own territory is not allowed to be interfered with by any foreign country."
    Zhang Zihong, Director of the Hong Kong Commercial Press, said: "We must liberate Taiwan, the Penghu Islands, and the coastal islands such as Jinmen and Mazhu. This is our internal affair, and no US war provocation can scare us."
    Huang Binghuan, Supervisor of the Chung Hwa Book Company, Chen Zulin, Manager of New Democracy Publishing House, Lan Zhen, Manager of the Hong Kong branch of Joint Publishing (Sanlian Books), Zhu Shilin, film director, Gong Qiuxia, actress, Huang Zufen, Principal of Chung Wah Middle School, and Zhao Kechi, Principal of Yukun Middle School, all well-known figures in Hong Kong's publishing, film, and education circles, also unanimously expressed their warm support for Premier Zhou's solemn statement. They believe that with the strength of the powerful Chinese people and peace-loving people all over the world today, any war provocation by US imperialism is bound to suffer a shameful defeat.

### 香港各界人士发表谈话  美国战争挑衅必遭失败

1958-09-08
第3版()
专栏：

    香港各界人士发表谈话
　　美国战争挑衅必遭失败
    据新华社香港7日电　香港各界人士今天对新华社记者发表谈话，表示坚决拥护周恩来总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，谴责美国对我国进行战争挑衅。
    港九工会联合会理事长李生说：“周总理关于台湾海峡地区局势的声明，再三申明我国一定解放台湾的态度和决心，对美帝国主义在台湾和我国沿海的侵略阴谋活动提出了严重的警告，港九各业工人一致表示坚决拥护和支持。”
    港九种植总公会理事长冯镛说：“如果美国胆敢悍然不顾中国人民再三的警告，它必然注定要惨败的。我们热烈拥护周总理的声明。”
    香港中华总商会会长高卓雄指出：美国侵占我国领土台湾和目前向我国进行战争挑衅，是对远东和世界和平的严重威胁。他说：“周总理的声明表示了我国六亿人民严正的立场，我表示坚决拥护。”
    香港华人革新协会主席陈丕士说：“我们坚决谴责美帝国主义侵占我国神圣的领土台湾。”
    香港华南电影工作者联合会理事长刘芳说：“台湾和澎湖列岛是中国领土不可分割的一部分，中国人民解放自己的领土是不容许任何外国干涉的。
    香港商务印书馆厂长张子宏说：“我们一定要解放台湾、澎湖列岛及金门、马祖等沿海岛屿，这是我们的内政，美国任何战争挑衅是绝对吓不倒我们的。”
    香港出版界知名人士中华书局监理黄秉桓、新民主出版社经理陈祖霖、三联书店香港分店经理蓝真，电影界人士导演朱石麟、演员龚秋霞，教育界人士中华中学校长黄祖芬和育群中学校长赵克墀等，也一致表示热烈拥护周总理的严正声明，他们认为在今天强大的中国人民和全世界爱好和平人民的力量下，美帝国主义任何战争挑衅，必然遭到可耻的失败。
