Title: Iraq Bans Assemblies and Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1958-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, September 14th: The Iraqi military governor issued a statement on the evening of the 13th, reiterating the order to ban assemblies, demonstrations, or the distribution of illegal leaflets. All violators will be subject to military and other legal trials.

The statement reminded Iraqi citizens not to be deceived by these individuals. The statement said, "We believe that assemblies, demonstrations, and the distribution of illegal leaflets are detrimental to national interests at a time when we urgently need to consolidate our unity and defend our republic. These actions will undermine our unity and disrupt peace."

### 伊拉克禁止集会游行

1958-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克禁止集会游行
    新华社巴格达14日电　伊拉克军事长官13日晚间发表声明，重申禁止举行集会、示威游行或者散发非法传单的命令，一切违法分子将要受到军事和其他法律的审讯。
    声明提醒伊拉克公民不要受这些分子的欺骗。声明说，我们认为，当我们极需巩固我们的团结和保卫我们的共和国的时候，集会、游行示威和散发非法的传单对民族利益都是有害的。这些行动会使得我们的团结遭到破坏，并且会破坏和平。
