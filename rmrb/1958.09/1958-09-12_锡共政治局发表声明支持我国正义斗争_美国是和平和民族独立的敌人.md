Title: Ceylon Communist Party Politburo Issues Statement Supporting Our Country's Just Struggle: The United States is the Enemy of Peace and National Independence
Author:
Date: 1958-09-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 10th: TASS reports that the General Secretary of the Ceylon Communist Party, Keuneman, on behalf of the party's Politburo, issued a statement condemning the United States' adventurous actions in the Far East.

The statement said that the U.S. imperialism has been interfering in China's internal affairs, which has created a dangerous situation for universal peace. The U.S. imperialism's efforts to provide armed assistance to the Chiang Kai-shek clique once again demonstrate that it is an irreconcilable enemy of world peace and an enemy of the national independence of Asian countries. Kinmen Island and other coastal islands, as well as Taiwan, are undeniably integral parts of Chinese territory. No other country can infringe upon the rights of the Chinese government and the Chinese people to control their own territory.

The statement said that the Ceylon Communist Party and the people of Ceylon fully support the People's Republic of China's position in defending its national territorial integrity.

### 锡共政治局发表声明支持我国正义斗争  美国是和平和民族独立的敌人

1958-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    锡共政治局发表声明支持我国正义斗争
    美国是和平和民族独立的敌人
    据新华社11日讯　塔斯社科伦坡10日讯：锡兰共产党总书记克尼曼以党的政治局的名义发表了一项声明，谴责美国在远东进行的冒险行动。
    声明说，由于美国帝国主义一直干涉中国内政，这就为普遍和平造成了危险的局面。美国帝国主义竭力向蒋介石集团提供武装援助的这种行为，再次表明它是世界和平不共戴天的敌人，是亚洲国家民族独立的敌人。金门岛和其他沿海岛屿以及台湾，无容争辩都是中国领土的组成部分。任何其他国家都不能侵犯中国政府及中国人民控制其本国领土的权利。
    声明说，锡兰共产党和锡兰人民完全支持中华人民共和国在保卫国家领土完整方面所采取的立场。
