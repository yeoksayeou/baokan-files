Title: Enescu Music Competition Awards Ceremony; Chivu Stoica Hosts Reception for Participants

Author:
Date: 1958-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 16th. The awards ceremony for the winners of the first George Enescu International Music Competition was grandly held in Bucharest tonight.

Romanian Workers' Party and state leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica and others attended the awards ceremony. Also attending the awards ceremony were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania and some internationally renowned musicians.

Li Mingqiang, the young Chinese pianist who won the first prize in the piano competition, was awarded a gold medal and a prize of 30,000 lei.

After the concert, the Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, held a reception for the participants of this International Music Competition. Some leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the state, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania attended the reception.

### 埃奈斯库音乐比赛授奖  基伏·斯托伊卡为比赛参加者举行招待会

1958-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    埃奈斯库音乐比赛授奖
    基伏·斯托伊卡为比赛参加者举行招待会
    据新华社布加勒斯特16日电　乔治·埃奈斯库第一届国际音乐比赛会获奖者的授奖大会，今晚在布加勒斯特隆重举行。
    罗马尼亚工人党和国家的领导人乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡等出席了授奖大会。出席授奖大会的还有各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节和一些国际上著名的音乐家。
    获得钢琴比赛一等奖的中国青年钢琴家李名强，荣获金质奖章一枚和奖金三万列伊。
    演奏会结束以后，罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡为这届国际音乐比赛会参加者举行了招待会。罗马尼亚工人党和国家的一些领导人、各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节参加了招待会。
