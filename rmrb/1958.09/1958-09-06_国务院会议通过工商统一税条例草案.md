Title: State Council Meeting Approves Draft Regulations on Consolidated Industrial and Commercial Tax

Author:
Date: 1958-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th - The State Council held its 79th plenary meeting on the morning of the 5th, discussing and approving the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Consolidated Industrial and Commercial Tax (Draft)". This draft regulation will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and decision.

The meeting discussed and approved the State Council's proposal to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to merge the First and Second Commercial Ministries into a Ministry of Commerce.

The meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) for People's Congresses and People's Committees at All Levels in the Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture," "Organizational Regulations (Draft) for People's Congresses and People's Committees in the Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture," and "Organizational Regulations (Draft) for People's Congresses and People's Committees in the Yongjian Hui Autonomous County, Yunnan Province." These draft regulations will all be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and decision.

The meeting also approved some decisions on changes in administrative divisions.

Finally, the meeting approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院会议通过工商统一税条例草案

1958-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　国务院会议通过工商统一税条例草案
    新华社5日讯　国务院在5日上午举行第七十九次全体会议，讨论和通过了“中华人民共和国工商统一税条例（草案）”。这个条例草案将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议决定。
    会议讨论通过了国务院关于将第一、第二商业部合并为商业部向人大常委会提出的议案。
    会议通过了“临夏回族自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例（草案）”、“甘南藏族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）”、“云南省永建回族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）”。这些条例草案都将提请人大常委会审议决定。
    会议还通过了行政区划变动的一些决定。
    最后，会议通过了任免名单。
