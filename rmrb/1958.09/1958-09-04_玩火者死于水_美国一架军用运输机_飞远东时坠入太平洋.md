Title: Those Who Play with Fire Will Die by Water: US Military Transport Plane Crashes into Pacific Ocean While Flying to the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-09-04
Page: 4

　　Those Who Play with Fire Will Die by Water
    U.S. Military Transport Plane
    Crashes into Pacific Ocean While Flying to the Far East
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. A U.S. news agency reports that a U.S. C-124 military transport plane crashed into the Pacific Ocean near Guam on the 2nd while flying to the Far East. All nineteen people on board perished. The report did not reveal the identities of these people.
    This military transport plane took off from an air force base in California.

### 玩火者死于水  美国一架军用运输机  飞远东时坠入太平洋

1958-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　玩火者死于水
    美国一架军用运输机
    飞远东时坠入太平洋
    新华社3日讯　据美国通讯社报道，美国一架C—124型军用运输机2日在飞来远东途中在关岛附近失事，坠入太平洋中。机上十九个人全都葬身鱼腹。消息没有透露这些人的身份。
    这架军用运输机是从加利福尼亚州的空军基地起飞的。
