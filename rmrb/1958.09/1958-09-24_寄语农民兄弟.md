    A Message to Our Farmer Brothers
    When I opened the September 3rd People's Daily to the third page, the five characters "Miracles Emerge from the Great Leap Forward" appeared before my eyes. In this "Miracles" column were published: two extraordinary pumpkins and watermelons, jointly yielding 1,320 jin of melons; a chili pepper plant producing nine jin; a yam weighing over two hundred jin; a great bumper harvest of Shandong peaches, the lightest weighing half a jin, the heaviest over one jin, and so on. Like the miracles in grain production, these news items are so inspiring! I carefully read them once, deeply moved. A message to our farmer brothers: Thank you for creating so many miracles in the Great Leap Forward, and I hope you will create even more miracles!
    Worker of Qiqihar Jianhua Machinery Factory, Wang Liantai

### 寄语农民兄弟
王连泰
1958-09-24
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    寄语农民兄弟
    翻开9月3日的人民日报第三版，“跃进出奇迹”五个大字呈现在我的眼前。在这个“奇迹”栏里登载的有：两棵奇异的南瓜西瓜，共结瓜一千三百二十斤；一棵产九斤的辣椒；两百多斤重的芋头；山东肥桃大丰收，轻的一个有半斤，重的一斤多，等等。同粮食生产上的奇迹一样，这些消息多么振奋人心啊！我仔细地看了一遍，深受感动。寄语农民兄弟，感谢你们在大跃进中创造了这么多奇迹，希望你们创造更多的奇迹！齐齐哈尔建华机械厂工人　王连泰
