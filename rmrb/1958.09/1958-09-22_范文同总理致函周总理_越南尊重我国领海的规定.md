Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Sends Letter to Premier Zhou: Vietnam Respects China's Regulations on Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1958-09-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a letter to Premier Zhou Enlai of our country, representing the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in expressing its recognition and support for the Chinese government's statement on territorial waters. The letter states: "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam respects this decision and will instruct the relevant state organs to strictly respect China's regulation that its territorial sea has a width of twelve nautical miles whenever it has dealings with the People's Republic of China on the sea." This letter was delivered to Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, today by Nguyen Khang, the Vietnamese Ambassador to China.

### 范文同总理致函周总理  越南尊重我国领海的规定

1958-09-22
第3版()
专栏：

    范文同总理致函周总理
    越南尊重我国领海的规定
    新华社21日讯　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同致函我国周恩来总理，代表越南民主共和国政府表示承认和赞成中国政府关于领海的声明。信中说：“越南民主共和国政府尊重这项决定，并将指示负有职责的国家机关，凡在海面上和中华人民共和国发生关系时要严格尊重中国领海宽度为十二海里的规定。”这封信是由越南驻中国大使阮康今天交给我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞的。
