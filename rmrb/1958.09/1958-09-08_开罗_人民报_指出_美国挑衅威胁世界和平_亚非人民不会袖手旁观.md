Title: Cairo "People's News" Points Out: U.S. Provocations Threaten World Peace; Asian and African People Will Not Stand Idly By
Author:
Date: 1958-09-08
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 7th:  A commentator in Cairo's "People's News," commenting on U.S. Senator Smith's remarks on the 5th after meeting with Dulles, in which he clamored that the United States would use force to prevent China from liberating Jinmen and Mazu, pointed out that Smith's clamor "means that the United States openly disregards the legitimate rights of 600 million free and independent people.  The role and importance of these 600 million people in international affairs cannot be ignored."

    The commentator said that in the face of U.S. imperialism, which threatens world peace, China is not isolated, because the socialist camp will help China correct the mistakes of the United States and will support China in liberating its territory. The commentator also said that the people of Asia and Africa will not stand idly by regarding the U.S. policy towards China, and the people of all countries in the world will never allow the United States to ignite the sparks of world war.

### 开罗“人民报”指出  美国挑衅威胁世界和平  亚非人民不会袖手旁观

1958-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    开罗“人民报”指出
　　美国挑衅威胁世界和平
    亚非人民不会袖手旁观
    据新华社开罗7日电　开罗“人民报”评论员在就美国参议员史密斯5日同杜勒斯会谈后扬言美国将以武力阻止中国解放金门、马祖的消息时指出，史密斯的这种叫嚣“意味着美国公然无视六亿自由和自主的人民的正当权利。而这六亿人民在国际事务中的作用和所占的重要地位是不能忽视的。”
    评论员说，在面对着威胁世界和平的美帝国主义的情况下，中国并不是孤立的，因为社会主义阵营将帮助中国纠正美国的错误，并且将支持中国解放它的领土。评论员又说，对于美国对中国的政策，亚非各国人民是不会袖手旁观的，全世界各国人民绝不会容许美国燃起世界大战的火星。
