Title: Vietnamese Catholic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The delegation of the Liaison Committee of Patriotic and Peace-Loving Catholics of Vietnam, led by Father Nguyen The Vinh, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of Pi Shushi, Chairman of the Chinese Catholic Patriotic Association, for a visit to our country.

### 越天主教徒代表团到京

1958-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    越天主教徒代表团到京
    新华社29日讯  应中国天主教友爱国会主席皮漱石邀请来我国访问的越南爱祖国爱和平天主教徒联络委员会代表团，由阮世咏神父率领，在今天乘火车到达北京。
