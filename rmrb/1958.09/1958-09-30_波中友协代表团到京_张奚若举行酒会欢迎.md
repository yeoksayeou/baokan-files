Title: Polish-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Zhang Xiruo Hosts Reception to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: A five-member delegation from the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, led by its head, Bulkin, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the National Women's Federation, and others.  The Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Furato, also welcomed them at the airport.

That evening, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a welcome reception for the delegation.  Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing and representatives from various circles in the capital attended as guests. At the reception, Director Zhang Xiruo expressed his gratitude to the Polish people for the assistance they have given to China's socialist cause, and thanked them for the support they have given to the Chinese people in their struggle against the military provocations of U.S. imperialism. Delegation head Bulkin enthusiastically said that he brought the deep friendship of the entire Polish people to the Chinese people, and pointed out that in the Chinese people's struggle against American aggression, the Polish people will always stand with the Chinese people.

### 波中友协代表团到京  张奚若举行酒会欢迎

1958-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

    波中友协代表团到京
    张奚若举行酒会欢迎
    新华社29日讯  波中友好协会代表团一行五人，由团长布尔金率领，在今天下午到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹，全国妇联书记处书记曹孟君等人。波兰驻中国临时代办傅拉托也到机场欢迎。
    当晚，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若为代表团举行欢迎酒会。文化部沈雁冰部长和首都各界代表出席作陪。酒会上，张奚若主任感谢波兰人民在中国社会主义事业中所给予的援助，感谢他们在中国人民反对美帝国主义军事挑衅的斗争中所给予的支持。布尔金团长热情地说他带来了全体波兰人民对中国人民的深厚友谊，并且指出在中国人民反对美国侵略的斗争中，波兰人民永远同中国人民站在一起。
