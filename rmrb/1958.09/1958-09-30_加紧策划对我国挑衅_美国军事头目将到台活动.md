Title: Stepping Up Planning of Provocations Against Our Country: US Military Head to Conduct Activities in Taiwan

Author:

Date: 1958-09-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Washington dispatch: Following the recent visits to Taiwan by numerous high-ranking American military personnel, US Secretary of Defense McElroy will soon travel to Taiwan to plot military conspiracies.

McElroy already arrived in Honolulu on the 28th, beginning his five-week trip to "inspect" US military bases in the Far East and Middle East regions. Accompanying him is US Vice Chief of Naval Operations, Admiral James Russell. One of McElroy's main targets of his visit is Taiwan and the US Seventh Fleet, which is currently conducting aggression against the Chinese people in the Taiwan Strait. In Taiwan, he will meet with Chiang Kai-shek.

### 加紧策划对我国挑衅  美国军事头目将到台活动

1958-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

    加紧策划对我国挑衅
    美国军事头目将到台活动
    据新华社29日讯  华盛顿消息：继美国许多高级军人最近访问台湾之后，美国国防部长麦克耳罗伊最近又要到台湾策划军事阴谋。
    麦克耳罗伊已经在28日到达檀香山，开始他“视察”远东和中东地区美军基地的为期五周的旅行。同行的有美国海军作战部队副司令詹姆斯·鲁塞尔海军上将。麦克耳罗伊主要访问目标之一，是台湾和正在台湾海峡内对中国人民进行侵略活动的美国第七舰队。在台湾，他将同蒋介石会谈。
