Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Donates to the Chinese Red Cross

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy yesterday donated RMB 13,718 to the Chinese Red Cross. This donation was personally given by Prime Minister Suhrawardy to Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross, at a banquet held before his departure from Beijing. President Li Dequan expressed his gratitude for his friendly donation.

### 苏拉瓦底总理捐款给中国红十字会

1956-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理捐款给中国红十字会
    新华社24日讯　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底昨天向中国红十字会捐赠了人民币一万三千七百一十八元。这项捐款是苏拉瓦底总理在他离开北京前举行的宴会上亲自交给中国红十字会会长李德全的，李德全会长对他的友好捐赠表示了感谢。
