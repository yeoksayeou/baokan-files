Title: Rural Joint Enterprise Established in Xiangxi
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency: A joint enterprise encompassing power generation, mechanized oil pressing, flour milling, food processing, and farm tool repair is being established in the deep mountains of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture in Huayuan County. This enterprise will be put into production in the first quarter of next year.

Before liberation, there were no factories or mines in the autonomous prefecture. Now, there are already fifty-four factories and mines in this remote mountainous area.

### 湘西建立农村联合企业

1956-10-22
第2版()
专栏：

    湘西建立农村联合企业
    新华社讯　在湘西苗族自治州花垣县深山里，正在建立一座包括发电、机器榨油、磨粉、食品加工和农具修配等多种生产业务的联合企业。这个企业将在明年第一季度投入生产。
    解放前，自治州没有一座工厂和矿山。现在，在这个深山里已经拥有五十四个工厂和矿山了。
