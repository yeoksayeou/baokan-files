Title: Sudanese Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: The Sudanese women's delegation, led by Ms. Doulat Muhammad Hassan, left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow. On the evening of the 14th, the All-China Women's Federation held a farewell banquet for the guests. Before leaving Beijing, the delegation visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Changchun, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.

### 苏丹妇女代表团赴苏

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹妇女代表团赴苏
    据新华社15日讯　以道拉特·穆罕默德·哈桑女士为首的苏丹妇女代表团今天早晨乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。14日晚，全国民主妇联为客人们举行了欢送宴会。离北京前，代表团曾到沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、长春、上海、杭州等地参观访问。
