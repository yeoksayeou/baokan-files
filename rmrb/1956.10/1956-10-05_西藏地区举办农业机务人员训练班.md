Title: Tibet Region Holds Agricultural Machinery Personnel Training Class
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa. An agricultural machinery and junior technician training class opened here on September 15th. This training class is organized by the Agricultural and Forestry Department of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region.

　　Among the sixty-five male and female students in the training class, sixty are Tibetans. They are all children of farmers and herdsmen from all over Tibet. Some of them have just put down their wooden plows and are about to begin learning to drive tractors and combine harvesters.

### 西藏地区举办农业机务人员训练班

1956-10-05
第3版()
专栏：

    西藏地区举办农业机务人员训练班
    据新华社拉萨讯　一个农业机务和初级技术人员训练班9月15日在这里开学。这个训练班是西藏自治区筹备委员会农林处举办的。
    训练班的六十五名男女学员中，有六十名是藏族，他们都是从西藏各地来的农牧民子女。其中有些人刚抛下木犁，马上就要开始学习驾驶拖拉机和康拜因收割机。
