Title: Resolution on Convening the Fourth World Trade Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 4th TASS: The resolution on convening the Fourth World Trade Union Congress passed by the Eighth Session of the World Federation of Trade Unions Council states: The Council approves the report made by the Executive Committee on convening the Fourth World Trade Union Congress and entrusts the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions to submit a detailed draft plan for preparing and holding the 1957 Congress to the next Executive Committee meeting.

### 关于召开世界工会第四次代表大会的决议

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    关于召开世界工会第四次代表大会的决议
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社索非亚4日讯：世界工会联合会理事会第八次会议通过的关于召开世界工会第四次代表大会的决议说：理事会赞同执行委员会作的关于召开世界工会第四次代表大会的报告，并且委托世界工联书记处向下次执行委员会会议提出筹备和举行1957年代表大会的详细计划草案。
