Title: Suhrawardy Pays Courtesy Call on Premier Zhou Enlai, Premier Zhou Enlai Invites Distinguished Guest to Dinner
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Prime Minister Suhrawardy of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and all the personnel who accompanied him on his visit to China paid a courtesy call on Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China this evening.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, He Long, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Water Resources Fu Zuoyi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, and Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Geng Biao and his wife.

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Prime Minister Huseyn Shaheed Suhrawardy was invited to dinner with Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife at 7:00 PM this evening.

Also invited were Prime Minister Suhrawardy's daughter, Mrs. Akhtar Suleiman, Mrs. Noon, wife of the Pakistani Minister of Foreign Affairs and Commonwealth Relations, Pakistani Ambassador to China Ahmed and his wife, Princess Abida Sultan, and Foreign Secretary M.S.A. Baig and his wife.

Accompanying them for dinner were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Geng Biao and his wife.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy paid a courtesy call on Premier Zhou Enlai on October 18. (Attached picture)

### 苏拉瓦底总理拜会周恩来总理  周恩来总理邀请贵宾共进晚餐

1956-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理拜会周恩来总理
    周恩来总理邀请贵宾共进晚餐
    新华社18日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理苏拉瓦底和同他一起来中国访问的全体人员今晚拜会了中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来。
    拜会时在座的，有国务院副总理彭德怀、贺龙、乌兰夫、李富春，秘书长习仲勋，水利部部长傅作义，外交部副部长张闻天，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人。
    新华社18日讯　侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底总理今晚七时应邀同周恩来总理和夫人共进晚餐。
    苏拉瓦底总理的女儿阿赫塔·苏来曼夫人，巴基斯坦外交和联邦关系部长努恩的夫人，巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德和夫人，阿比达·苏尔唐公主，外交秘书米·西·阿·贝格和夫人也应邀出席。
    陪同进餐的，有国务院副总理贺龙，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人。
    苏拉瓦底总理在10月18日拜会周恩来总理。（附图片）
