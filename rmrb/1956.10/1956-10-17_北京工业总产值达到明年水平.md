Title: Beijing's Total Industrial Output Value Reaches Next Year's Level
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: According to statistics from the Beijing Municipal Statistics Bureau, as of the end of September, the total output value of state-owned, locally state-owned, and joint state-private industrial enterprises in Beijing had already reached the level of the final year of the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. Among the city's 177 state-owned, locally state-owned, and joint state-private industrial enterprises, only 55 had not yet reached the 1957 output value level.

Of the 16 major products of 57 central state-owned and joint state-private industrial enterprises, the output of eight products, including electricity, pig iron, metallurgical coke, cast iron pipes, metal cutting machine tools, milling machines, planers, and double-wheel double-share plows, has already reached the 1957 level. It is expected that by the end of the year, the output of four products, namely cement, cotton yarn, cotton cloth, and paper, will also reach the 1957 level.

The production level of Beijing's local industry has improved significantly in recent years. The total output value in 1956 is expected to increase by 127% compared to 1952, with an average annual increase of 22.8% over four years; while the Five-Year Plan stipulated an annual increase of 14.6%.

### 北京工业总产值达到明年水平

1956-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

    北京工业总产值达到明年水平
    新华社15日讯　据北京市统计局统计：到9月底为止，北京国营、地方国营和公私合营工业企业的生产总值，已经提前达到第一个五年计划最后一年的水平。在全市一百七十七个国营、地方国营、公私合营工业企业中，只有五十五个还没有达到1957年产值水平。
    五十七个中央国营、公私合营的工业企业的十六种主要产品中，目前有电力、生铁、冶金焦、铁铸管、金属切削机床、铣床、刨床、双轮双铧犁等八种产品产量，已经达到1957年水平。预计到年底水泥、棉纱、棉布、纸四种产品产量，也将达到1957年水平。
    北京市地方工业生产水平几年来有了显著的提高，1956年生产总值预计将比1952年提高127％，四年平均每年增长22.8％；而五年计划规定每年增长14.6％。
