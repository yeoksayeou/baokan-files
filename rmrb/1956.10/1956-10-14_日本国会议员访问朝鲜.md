Title: Japanese Diet Members Visit North Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 13th: Japanese Liberal Democratic Party Senator Shoichi Aoyama and Labor-Farmer Party Representative Hisao Ishino arrived in Pyongyang by train on the evening of October 12th. They are visiting North Korea at the invitation of the Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea.

### 日本国会议员访问朝鲜

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本国会议员访问朝鲜
    据新华社平壤13日电　日本自由民主党参议院议员青山正一和劳农党众议院议员石野久男在10月12日晚上乘火车到达平壤。他们是应朝鲜最高人民会议议长的邀请前来朝鲜访问的。
