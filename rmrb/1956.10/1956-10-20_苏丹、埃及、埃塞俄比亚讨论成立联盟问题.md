Title: Sudan, Egypt, Ethiopia Discuss Formation of Union
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 19th: Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil said in Cairo on October 18th that Sudan, Egypt, and Ethiopia are currently discussing the formation of a "Tripartite African Union."

The Prime Minister said that initially, this union would be a good-neighborly and friendly pact. In due course, it could be expanded and become a political alliance. Other African countries, such as Nigeria, Libya, and the Gold Coast, would be welcome to join this organization.

### 苏丹、埃及、埃塞俄比亚讨论成立联盟问题

1956-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹、埃及、埃塞俄比亚讨论成立联盟问题
    据新华社19日讯　开罗消息：苏丹总理哈利勒10月18日在开罗说，苏丹、埃及和埃塞俄比亚最近正在讨论成立“三国非洲联盟”问题。
    这位总理说，在开始时，这个联盟将是一个友好睦邻公约。在相当的时候，它可以扩大，并且成为一个政治联盟。其它非洲国家，例如，尼日利亚、利比亚和黄金海岸都将被欢迎参加这个组织。
