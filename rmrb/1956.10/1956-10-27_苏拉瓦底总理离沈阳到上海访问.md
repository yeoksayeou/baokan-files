Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Leaves Shenyang for Shanghai Visit
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Shenyang: Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage left Shenyang by special plane this morning to visit Shanghai.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister Suhrawardy warmly shook the hand of Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, and repeatedly expressed his gratitude for the warm reception he received in Shenyang.

Before departing, Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, and Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, presented gifts to Prime Minister Suhrawardy, respectively. Prime Minister Suhrawardy also presented gifts to the Governor, Deputy Mayor, and leaders of the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Tianjin, October 26th: Hussein Shahid Suhrawardy, Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, and his entourage, accompanied by Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Tianjin by special plane at 10:23 this morning.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy was en route from Shenyang to Shanghai, passing through here. After having lunch in Tianjin, he departed for Shanghai again at 11:47.

Those who went to the airport to welcome and see off the distinguished guests today included Huang Huoqing, Mayor of Tianjin, and other responsible persons.

The wife of the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed, the Embassy Counselor Kaiser and his wife, and others also came from Beijing to bid farewell to Prime Minister Suhrawardy.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Shanghai, October 26th: Pakistani Prime Minister Hussein Shahid Suhrawardy and his entourage arrived in Shanghai by plane this afternoon. Accompanying them were Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy's special plane landed at Shanghai's Longhua Airport at 3:05 p.m. Today, more than 7,000 people gathered at the airport to welcome the distinguished Pakistani guests. Xu Jianguo, Liu Jiping, Song Richang, and Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayors of Shanghai, Major General Hu Darong, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Shen Zhiyuan and Li Zhaohuan, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, greeted the distinguished guests at the airport. Foreign diplomatic officials stationed in Shanghai also went to the airport to welcome them.

After a brief rest at the guesthouse, Prime Minister Suhrawardy arrived at the Children's Palace. Young Pioneers lined up and saluted the esteemed guests and accompanied them to visit the various activities being held in the Children's Palace. Prime Minister Suhrawardy watched the puppet show "Wang Xiaoer Fights the Tiger" performed on a small stage with scenery in the puppet group's activity room. When he saw Wang Xiaoer and his son finally kill the cunning tiger, he laughed heartily. The girls in the embroidery group gave Prime Minister Suhrawardy a doll and two cushion covers they had made. The white satin cushion covers were embroidered with flowers and the words "Long Live China-Pakistan Friendship!" The children in the nautical model group presented the Prime Minister with a small sailboat they had made called "Friendship."

Wearing the red scarf presented to him by the Young Pioneers, Prime Minister Suhrawardy spoke to the children in the Young Pioneer Hall. He said: I am very happy to come to the beautiful and magnificent Children's Palace founded by Madame Soong Ching Ling and see your happy faces and your various activities. I really feel that you Chinese children are happy because your leaders have created very good conditions for you. You are the future of China, and I hope everything is wonderful for you. The Prime Minister also said: I bring with me the friendship of the children of Pakistan. The children of Pakistan have sent a small elephant to the children of China this time. If you go to Beijing, please go and see it—before it grows up. The children expressed their gratitude to the Prime Minister with warm applause.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy also donated £500 to the Children's Palace for activity expenses. Chen Weibo, Director of the Palace, thanked him on behalf of the children of Shanghai.

In the evening, Xu Jianguo, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, held a grand banquet in the hall of the Municipal People's Committee to welcome Prime Minister Suhrawardy and other Pakistani guests. At the banquet, after Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo delivered a speech expressing a warm welcome to Prime Minister Suhrawardy, Prime Minister Suhrawardy spoke. He said that he was very pleased to know that Shanghai had hosted guests from Pakistan more than once. He said: I have extremely warm friendship for the Chinese people. He hoped that China and Pakistan could continue to cooperate. He said that the friendship between China and Pakistan is conducive to world peace. The Prime Minister also said, I want to mention your Premier Zhou Enlai. I am very impressed by his vision, foresight, imagination, and his love for the people. When I talk to him, I often feel that he is thinking of the people. Then, Prime Minister Suhrawardy proposed a toast to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai. During the banquet, Prime Minister Suhrawardy stood in front of the loudspeaker again and said that the fish he ate at the banquet reminded him of water, and then of rivers and dockworkers. He said that when our country was very short of food, the dockworkers on Shanghai's Huangpu River worked tirelessly to ensure that Chinese rice could be shipped from Shanghai to Pakistan in time. The Prime Minister proposed a toast to the workers, to the laborers, especially the dockworkers of the Huangpu River. He also announced a donation of £500 to the Huangpu River dockworkers' union as a fund.

### 苏拉瓦底总理离沈阳到上海访问

1956-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理离沈阳到上海访问
    据新华社电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员，今天早晨乘专机离开沈阳去上海访问。
    苏拉瓦底总理上飞机以前，紧紧握着辽宁省省长杜者蘅的手，对在沈阳受到的热诚招待一再表示感谢。
    临别以前，辽宁省省长杜者蘅和沈阳市副市长张力克，分别向苏拉瓦底总理赠送了礼物。苏拉瓦底总理也向省长、副市长和辽宁省人民委员会的领导人分别赠送了礼物。
    据新华社天津26日电　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员，由中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和外交部礼宾司司长王倬如陪同，在今天上午十时二十三分乘专机到达天津。
    苏拉瓦底总理是由沈阳前往上海路过这里的，在天津进过午餐以后，十一时四十七分又启程飞往上海。
    今天前往机场迎送贵宾的，有天津市市长黄火青等负责人。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德的夫人、大使馆参赞凯瑟和夫人等，也特地由北京赶来欢送苏拉瓦底总理。
    新华社上海26日电　巴基斯坦总理侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员，今天下午乘飞机到达上海。陪同前来的有中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长王倬如。
    苏拉瓦底总理的专机在下午三时零五分降落在上海的龙华机场。今天聚集在机场上欢迎巴基斯坦贵宾的有七千多人。上海市副市长许建国、刘季平、宋日昌、刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员胡大荣少将，政协上海市委员会副主席沈志远、黎照寰等在机场上迎接贵宾。各国驻上海的外交官员们也到机场欢迎。
    苏拉瓦底总理在宾馆稍事休息后来到了少年宫。少先队员们列队用队礼欢迎尊贵的客人，并且陪他们参观正在少年宫里进行的各项活动，苏拉瓦底总理在木偶小组的活动室里观看了在一个有布景的小舞台上演出的木偶戏“王小二打虎”。当他看到王小二和他的儿子最后终于把狡猾的老虎打死的时候，高兴地哈哈大笑起来。刺绣小组的女孩子们把自己作的一个洋娃娃和两个靠垫套子送给了苏拉瓦底总理。用白缎子作的靠垫套子上绣着花和“中国巴基斯坦友好万岁！”的字样。航海模型小组的孩子们送给总理一只自己制造的名字叫“友好”号的小帆船。
    苏拉瓦底总理带着少先队员献给他的红领巾在少年厅里向少年儿童们讲话。他说：来到宋庆龄夫人创办的美丽而辉煌的少年宫，看到你们欢乐的面孔和你们的各种活动，我感到很愉快。我确实感到你们中国儿童是幸福的，因为你们的领袖为你们创造了很好的条件。你们是中国的未来，我希望你们一切美好。总理还说：随着我带来的是巴基斯坦儿童的友谊。巴基斯坦儿童们这次送给中国儿童一只小象，如果你们到北京，请去看看它——在它长大以前。孩子们用热烈的掌声来表示对总理的感谢。
    苏拉瓦底总理还捐给少年宫五百英镑作为活动经费。宫主任陈维博代表上海少年儿童向他致谢。
    晚上，上海市副市长许建国在市人民委员会大厅里举行盛大宴会，欢迎苏拉瓦底总理和巴基斯坦的其他客人们。宴会上，在许建国副市长致词向苏拉瓦底总理表示热烈欢迎之后，苏拉瓦底总理讲了话。他表示很高兴知道上海已经不止一次地招待了来自巴基斯坦的客人。他说：我对中国人民有极其热烈的友谊。他希望中国和巴基斯坦能够继续合作。他说，中国和巴基斯坦两国的友谊有助于世界和平。总理还说，我想提到你们的总理周恩来。我对他的眼光、远见、想像力，他对人民的爱，感到非常钦佩。我和他谈话时，往往感到他是在为人民着想。接着，苏拉瓦底总理建议为周恩来总理的健康干杯。在宴会进行中，苏拉瓦底总理又站到扩音器前讲话说，宴席上吃的鱼使他想到了水，又想到了江河和码头工人。他说，当我们国家十分缺乏粮食的时候，上海黄浦江的码头工人们为了使中国的大米能及时从上海口岸运到巴基斯坦而不辞劳苦地工作着。总理建议为工人、为劳动者、特别是黄浦江的码头工人干杯。他并且宣布捐款五百英镑给黄浦江码头工人的工会作为基金。
