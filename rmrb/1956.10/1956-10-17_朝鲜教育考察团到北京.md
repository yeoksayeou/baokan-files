Title: Korean Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: A fifty-member education delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Jeong Jin-seok, President of Songdo University of Politics and Economics, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 15th.
    The education delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is visiting China in accordance with the provisions of the 1956 Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Plan. They will comprehensively examine higher education and general education in our country and study some fundamental issues in education. The delegation includes university professors, educational administrative cadres, primary and secondary school principals, and teachers.
    Lin Li-ju, Vice Minister of Education; Ding Hua, Assistant Minister of Higher Education; Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Education Trade Union; Weng Dujian, Director of the Beijing Municipal Education Bureau; and principals and academic deans of universities and secondary schools in Beijing were at the station to welcome them.
    Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, was also at the station to welcome them.

### 朝鲜教育考察团到北京

1956-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜教育考察团到北京
    新华社15日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育考察团五十人，在团长、松都政治经济大学校长郑镇石率领下，15日下午乘火车到达北京。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育考察团是根据中朝1956年文化合作计划的规定来中国访问的。他们将全面地考察我国高等教育和普通教育，研究教育工作中一些带根本性的问题。代表团中有大学教授、教育行政干部、中小学校长和教师等。
    到车站欢迎的有教育部副部长林砺儒、高等教育部部长助理丁华、中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明、北京市教育局局长翁独健，以及北京市的高等学校和中小学校长、教务长等人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南也到车站欢迎。
