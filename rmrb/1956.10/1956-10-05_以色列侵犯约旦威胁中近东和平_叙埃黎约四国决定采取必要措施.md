Title: Israel's Invasion of Jordan Threatens Peace in the Near East; Syria, Egypt, Lebanon, and Jordan Decide to Take Necessary Measures
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 3rd - TASS reports: The conference of representatives from the four Arab nations of Syria, Egypt, Lebanon, and Jordan, issued a communique following its meeting on October 2nd, stating that the participants discussed the situation created by the recent invasion of Jordanian territory by Israeli forces. The communique indicated that the participants reached an agreement to take necessary measures regarding this event.

The Lebanese representative, Fadl Amoun, who attended the meeting, stated to reporters that the four countries were in complete solidarity in discussing all issues, particularly in their unified condemnation of Israel. A letter of condemnation is soon to be submitted to the Security Council.

According to "Al-Nahar" reports, the letter of condemnation states that Israel's continuous acts of aggression are a threat to peace in the Near East and Middle East. The newspaper stated that the four Arab nations are calling on the Security Council to take severe and impactful measures against Israel.

### 以色列侵犯约旦威胁中近东和平  叙埃黎约四国决定采取必要措施

1956-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列侵犯约旦威胁中近东和平
    叙埃黎约四国决定采取必要措施
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特3日讯：叙利亚、埃及、黎巴嫩、约旦这四个阿拉伯国家的代表会议在10月2日会议结束后发表公报说，与会者讨论了不久以前以色列军队侵犯约旦领土所造成的局势。公报指出，与会者达成协议，要对这个事件采取必要的措施。
    参加会议的黎巴嫩代表法德·阿蒙在对记者发表谈话的时候指出四国在讨论所有问题上的充分团结，特别是在共同控诉以色列问题上的一致。控诉书即将提交安全理事会。
    据“日报”报道，控诉书说，以色列方面不断的侵略行为是对近东和中东和平的威胁。这家报纸说，四个阿拉伯国家呼吁安全理事会对以色列采取严厉的有影响的措施。
