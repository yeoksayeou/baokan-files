Title: Czechoslovakia Achieves Full Employment
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th - According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 3rd, Czechoslovakia has eliminated unemployment and underemployment and has achieved full employment. By the end of 1955, the number of employees nationwide had reached 4.4 million, accounting for approximately one-third of the country's total population.

### 捷克斯洛伐克实现充分就业

1956-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克实现充分就业
    新华社布拉格5日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社3日报道，捷克斯洛伐克已经消灭失业和半失业现象，实现了充分就业。到1955年年底，全国职工人数已经达到四百四十万人，约占全国总人口的三分之一。
