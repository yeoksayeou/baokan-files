Title: President Prasad says in Nepal that the fates of Asian countries are closely linked; India advocates peaceful resolution of disputes and opposes interference in other countries' internal affairs
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 23rd: Kathmandu Dispatch: Indian President Rajendra Prasad, who is on a state visit to Nepal, said at a reception held for him by the Nepal Asian Countries and International Affairs Association in Kathmandu on October 22nd that in today's world, no country can live in isolation. The fates of all Asian countries are interconnected.
    He said: Today's Asia has a new awakening, "We find that our aims and ideals are more or less the same. We are fighting for national freedom, human dignity, and peace. I believe that if we unite our efforts, our success will be greater."
    Prasad expressed "India's sympathy and best wishes" for Nepal's first five-year plan.
    Prasad said that since the dawn of history, many aspects of contact, such as geographical proximity, cultural heritage, and natural common interests, have bound India and Nepal together.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 23rd: Nepalese King Mahendra held a state banquet in Kathmandu on October 22nd to welcome Indian President Prasad.
    President Prasad said at the banquet that India does not believe in military alliances and military blocs. "We believe in the method of peaceful consultation to resolve international conflicts."
    He said that India has no territorial ambitions and no one needs to worry about its aggression. He added: "We do not want to interfere in the internal affairs of other countries."
    The Indian President said that any threat to Nepal's peace and security is also a threat to India's peace and security. "Your friends are our friends, and our friends are your friends."

### 普拉沙德总统在尼泊尔说  亚洲国家的命运息息相关  印度主张和平解决争端反对干涉别国内政

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    普拉沙德总统在尼泊尔说
    亚洲国家的命运息息相关
    印度主张和平解决争端反对干涉别国内政
    据新华社新德里23日电　加德满都消息：正在尼泊尔作国事访问的印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德10月22日在加德满都尼泊尔亚洲国家和国际事务协会为他举行的招待会上说，在今天的世界里，没有一个国家能够孤立地生活。亚洲所有国家的命运都是彼此相联的。
    他说：今日的亚洲有着一种新的觉醒，“我们发现我们的目的和理想或多或少是相同的。我们为民族自由、人类尊严和和平而奋斗。我相信，假如把我们的努力联合起来，我们的成功就会更大一些。”
    普拉沙德向尼泊尔第一个五年计划表达了“印度的同情和最好愿望”。
    普拉沙德说，从历史的黎明时期起，地理上的邻接、文化遗产和天然的共同利益等许多方面的联系就把印度和尼泊尔维系在一起。
    新华社新德里23日电　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉10月22日在加德满都举行国宴，招待印度总统普拉沙德。
    普拉沙德总统在宴会上说，印度不相信军事同盟和军事集团。“我们相信和平协商解决国际冲突的方法。”
    他说，印度没有领土野心，谁也用不着担心它会进行侵略。他又说：“我们不希望干涉别国内政。”
    这位印度总统说，对尼泊尔的和平和安全的任何威胁也就是对印度的和平和安全的威胁。“你们的朋友也就是我们的朋友，我们的朋友也就是你们的朋友。”
