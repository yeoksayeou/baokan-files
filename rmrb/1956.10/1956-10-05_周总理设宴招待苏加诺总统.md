Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held a grand banquet tonight in honor of the President of the Republic of Indonesia, Sukarno.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, thanked President Sukarno for his friendly visit, and asked President Sukarno to convey the fraternal friendship of the six hundred million Chinese people to the Indonesian people. The Premier said that the friendship between the peoples of China and Indonesia has grown in the common struggle to safeguard world peace and oppose colonialism, and will continue to advance hand in hand towards this common goal in the future. Since we can understand each other and sympathize with each other, we should support and help each other without any conditions attached. We should also learn from each other, and be friends forever, like our own relatives, like our own brothers.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai said that the growing friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries will greatly strengthen the power of the people of the Asian and African regions to strive for peace, independence, and freedom.

　　At the end of his speech, Premier Zhou Enlai shouted "Merdeka" three times and hailed "Bung Karno."

　　President Sukarno expressed his gratitude in his speech at the banquet. He said, "I have been in Beijing for five days, and I have seen the Chinese nation and the spirit of the nation. I have found a friendly, kind, hardworking, freedom-loving, and united nation. Such a nation will never die and will live forever. Indonesia is proud to have such a nation as a friend, comrade, and comrade-in-arms. Our friendship is the friendship of comrades in battle, and I hope it will last forever." Finally, President Sukarno proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Chinese people, to the prosperity of the Indonesian people, to the friendship between the Chinese and Indonesian people, to world peace and prosperity, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

　　At the banquet, President Sukarno sat together with Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Chairman Li Jishen, drinking and talking, and the other distinguished guests also freely gathered with the Chinese hosts for conversation. The hall was filled with laughter and conversation.

　　Pleasant music was constantly played in the hall. Cai Huanzhen, a member of the chorus of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, sang the Indonesian song "Bengawan Solo" to the accompaniment of the orchestra, which won warm applause from the Indonesian guests and all the guests. Cai Huanzhen was invited to the seats of the distinguished guests to sing the song again, and also sang the Indonesian song "Halo, Halo Bandung." After the banquet, Beijing artists performed Chinese folk songs and dances for the distinguished guests.

　　Among those present at the banquet were Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Vice Chairmen Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Bao Erhan, as well as heads of various departments of Chinese state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations.

　　The heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China who attended tonight's banquet included the ambassadors of Burma, Poland, Mongolia, the Soviet Union, Finland, Pakistan, Yugoslavia, India, the German Democratic Republic, Norway, Afghanistan, Denmark, Sweden, Romania, Indonesia, Albania, and Egypt, the chargés d'affaires of the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, the chargés d'affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Bulgaria, and Switzerland, the head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, and the counselors of Hungary and Czechoslovakia.

### 周总理设宴招待苏加诺总统

1956-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待苏加诺总统
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今晚举行盛大宴会，招待印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统。
    周恩来总理代表中国政府和人民感谢苏加诺总统的友好访问，并且请苏加诺总统把六亿中国人民的兄弟友谊转达给印度尼西亚人民。总理说，中国和印度尼西亚两国人民的友谊是在维护世界和平和反对殖民主义的共同斗争中成长起来的，今后也将为着这一共同目标而携手前进。既然能够互相了解、互相同情，我们就应该互相支援，互相帮助，而不要附带任何条件。我们还应该互相学习，永远友好，像自己的亲人一样，像自己的兄弟一样。
    周恩来总理说，我们两国友好合作关系的日益发展，将大大地加强亚非地区人民争取和平、独立和自由的力量。
    周总理在他的讲话结束时，三呼“默地加”和高呼“朋加诺”。
    苏加诺总统在会上致词表示感谢。他说，我在北京已经五天，我看到了中国民族和民族的精神，我发现了一个友好的、仁慈的、勤劳的、爱好自由的和团结的民族。这样的民族永远也不会死亡，将永远生存下去。印度尼西亚以有这样的民族作为朋友、作为同志和战友而感到自豪。我们的友谊是战斗中的同志的友谊，我希望它永世长存。最后，苏加诺总统为中国人民的繁荣，为印度尼西亚人民的繁荣，为中国人民和印度尼西亚人民的友谊，为世界的和平和繁荣，为毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
    宴会上，苏加诺总统同朱德副主席、周恩来总理、李济深副委员长围坐在一起饮酒交谈，其他的贵宾们也同中国主人自由地聚谈。大厅内一片谈笑声。
    大厅里不断奏着悦耳的音乐。中央乐团的合唱队员蔡焕贞在管弦乐队的伴奏下演唱的印度尼西亚歌曲“梭罗河”，博得了印度尼西亚贵宾们和全体来宾的热烈掌声。蔡焕贞又被邀到贵宾们的席前把这支歌重唱了一遍，并且又唱了印度尼西亚歌曲“哈啰，哈啰，万隆”。在宴会以后，北京的文艺工作者为贵宾们表演了中国的民间歌舞。
    出席宴会作陪的，有副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春，副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席叶剑英、程潜、张治中、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉，以及中国国家机关各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人。
    出席今晚宴会的各国驻华使节和外交官员，有缅甸、波兰、蒙古、苏联、芬兰、巴基斯坦、南斯拉夫、印度、德意志民主共和国、挪威、阿富汗、丹麦、瑞典、罗马尼亚、印度尼西亚、阿尔巴尼亚和埃及的大使，荷兰和英国的代办，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、保加利亚和端士的临时代办，柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长，匈牙利和捷克斯洛伐克的参赞。
