Title: French National Assembly to Hold Vote of Confidence in Government
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 20th - TASS reports: The French National Assembly will conclude its debate on the general policies of the Mollet government on October 23rd and will hold a vote of confidence in the government on the 25th. A special committee composed of socialists has already been appointed to draft a resolution expressing approval of the government's general policies, which will be submitted to the National Assembly after the debate.

The National Assembly's debate on the government's policies in Algeria concluded on the 18th.

### 法国国民议会将对政府举行信任投票

1956-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    法国国民议会将对政府举行信任投票
    据新华社20日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：法国国民议会将在10月23日结束对摩勒政府一般政策的辩论，并且将在25日对政府举行信任投票。目前已经指定由社会党人组成的专门委员会草拟表示赞同政府的一般性政策的决议草案，准备在辩论结束后提交国民议会。
    国民议会关于政府在阿尔及利亚的政策的辩论已经在18日结束。
