Title: The United States and South Vietnam Repeatedly Violate the Geneva Accords: International Commission for Vietnam Concludes Six Cases
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 13th. According to the Vietnam News Agency today, the International Commission has concluded six cases of violations of the Geneva Accords by the United States and South Vietnamese authorities. These conclusions were mentioned in a letter sent by the Commission to the Liaison Delegation of the High Command of the Vietnam People's Army on October 9th.

In these six cases, five are violations of Article 16 of the Geneva Accords on Vietnam, which stipulates that all reinforcements of troops and new military personnel are prohibited from entering Vietnam. These cases concern five U.S. military aircraft transporting U.S. military personnel to Tan Son Nhut Airport near Saigon on November 10th, 12th, 24th, and 27th, and December 2nd, 1955, respectively.

The other case concerns a violation of Article 17 of the Accords, which stipulates that all reinforcements of weapons, ammunition, and other war materials of all types are prohibited from entering Vietnam. This case occurred on November 8th, 1955, when a U.S. military aircraft transported numerous aircraft parts to Tan Son Nhut Airport.

The Liaison Delegation of the High Command of the Vietnam People's Army has condemned these violations of the Accords in two protest notes submitted to the International Commission earlier this year.

### 美国南越屡次违反日内瓦协定  越南国际委员会对六个案件作出结论

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    美国南越屡次违反日内瓦协定
    越南国际委员会对六个案件作出结论
    新华社河内13日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，国际委员会已经对美国和南越当局违反日内瓦协定的六个案件作出了结论。这些结论是在10月9日委员会送交越南人民军总司令部联络代表团的一封信件中提到的。
    在这六个案件中，五件是违反关于越南的日内瓦协定第十六条的，这一条规定，禁止一切增援部队和新增的军事人员进入越南。这些案件是关于五架美国军用飞机分别在1955年11月10日、12日、24日和27日以及12月2日把美国军事人员运到西贡附近的新山机场的案件。
    另一个案件是关于违反协定第十七条的案件，这一条规定，禁止各种类型的武器、弹药和其他作战物资的一切增援进入越南。这个案件发生在1955年11月8日，当时有一架美国军用飞机把许多飞机零件运到了新山机场。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团曾经在今年年初送交国际委员会的两封抗议书中谴责了这些违反协定的行动。
