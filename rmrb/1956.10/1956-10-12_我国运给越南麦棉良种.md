Title: China Sends Good Wheat and Cotton Seeds to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: China's aid of seventy tons of excellent wheat seeds to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has been completely transferred and shipped; and another three hundred and twenty tons of "Guannong No. 1" excellent cotton seeds are being inspected in preparation for shipment.

The batch of wheat seeds transferred to Vietnam is an excellent farmer's variety from Beiliu County, Guangxi Province. The "Guannong No. 1" cotton seed is also a famous variety in China.

### 我国运给越南麦棉良种

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我国运给越南麦棉良种
    据新华社10日讯　我国支援越南民主共和国的七十吨优良麦种已经全部调运完毕；还有三百二十吨“关农一号”优良棉种正在检验，准备起运。
    调运到越南去的这批麦种是广西省北流县的优良农家品种。“关农一号”，棉种也是我国的著名品种。
