Title: Representatives of Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Meeting of the Heads of Hydrometeorological Bureaus and Representatives of Postal and Telecommunications Departments of Five Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. The delegations from Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union, who are here to attend the meeting of the heads of hydrometeorological bureaus and representatives of postal and telecommunications departments of Vietnam, China, North Korea, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union, which is about to be held in Beijing, have arrived in Beijing by train and plane between the 18th and 22nd.

The heads of the delegations arriving in Beijing are all the heads, directors, or chiefs of hydrometeorological bureaus/departments, and the directors or section chiefs of postal and telecommunications departments of their respective countries. The members of the delegations are all experts or consultants from hydrometeorological bureaus and postal and telecommunications departments of their respective countries.

When the delegations from various countries arrived in Beijing, Tu Changwang, Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau, Wang Gonggui, Deputy Director, and Wang Zigang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, as well as personnel from the embassies of Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union in China, went to the railway station and airport to welcome them.

### 参加五国水文气象局局长和邮电部代表会议  越、朝、蒙、苏四国代表团到京

1956-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    参加五国水文气象局局长和邮电部代表会议
    越、朝、蒙、苏四国代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　前来参加即将在北京举行的越、中、朝、蒙、苏五国水文气象局局长和邮电部代表会议的越、朝、蒙、苏四国代表团，已经在18日到22日先后乘火车和飞机到达北京。
    到达北京的各国代表团团长都是本国的水文气象局局长、署长、台长和邮电部电政局的局长或司长。团员都是各国水文气象局和邮电部门的专家或顾问。
    各国代表团到达北京的时候，中央气象局局长凃长望、副局长王功贵和邮电部副部长王子纲以及越、朝、蒙、苏等国驻我国大使馆的人员，都曾分别前往车站和机场欢迎。
