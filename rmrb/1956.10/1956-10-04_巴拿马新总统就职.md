Title: Panama's New President Takes Office
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 3rd: TASS reported: Panama's new President, Ernesto de la Guardia Jr., was sworn into office on October 1st.

    In his inaugural address, de la Guardia said that differences exist between Panama and the United States in interpreting the new 1955 Panama Canal Agreement, particularly regarding the labor compensation of Panamanians working for the canal and the annual payments that the United States should pay for using the Canal Zone territory.
    According to newspaper reports, Panama's former president, Arias Espinosa, who is about to take office as Panama's ambassador to Washington, said that the United States has not fulfilled its obligations under this agreement.

### 巴拿马新总统就职

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    巴拿马新总统就职
    据新华社3日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：巴拿马新总统德拉古阿迪亚10月1日宣誓就职。
    德拉古阿迪亚在发表就职演说的时候说，巴拿马同美国之间在解释1955年新巴拿马运河协定方面，特别是在为运河工作的巴拿马人的劳动报酬，美国使用运河区领土所应支付的年金上，存在着分歧。
    据报纸报道，巴拿马前总统即将就任巴拿马驻华盛顿大使的阿里亚斯·埃斯皮诺萨说，美国没有执行自己根据这项协定所承担的义务。
