Title: Ho Chi Minh Receives Representatives of the Chinese Exhibition Group and Others; Chinese Railway Repair Team Awarded Vietnamese First-Class Labor Medal
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 16th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received representatives of the Chinese Economic Exhibition Group, the Chinese Ministry of Railways Railway Repair Team, and three Chinese writers and painters who are currently visiting Vietnam on October 13th and 16th.

The Chinese Railway Repair Team was awarded the First-Class Labor Medal by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on October 15th for its assistance in repairing the Hanoi-Friendship Pass railway. In his speech at the award ceremony on that day, Nguyen Van Tran, Minister of Transportation and Postal Affairs of Vietnam, thanked China for sending a railway repair team to help strengthen the Hanoi-Friendship Pass railway after helping the Vietnamese people quickly repair it, and at the same time helping Vietnam train and establish a railway repair team.

On the evening of the award ceremony, the Vietnamese Ministry of Transportation and Postal Affairs hosted a banquet for more than 50 representatives of the Chinese Railway Repair Team.

The Chinese Railway Repair Team, which has been in Vietnam for more than seven months, will leave Vietnam and return to China on October 17th.

### 胡志明接见中国展览团等代表  我国铁路整修队荣获越南一级劳动勋章

1956-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明接见中国展览团等代表
    我国铁路整修队荣获越南一级劳动勋章
    新华社河内16日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在10月13日和16日先后接见了中国经济展览团、中国铁道部铁路整修队的代表以及正在越南访问的三位中国作家和画家。
    中国铁路整修队由于帮助了越南整修河内—睦南关铁路，在10月15日获得了越南民主共和国政府授给的一级劳动勋章。越南交通邮电部部长阮文珍在这一天的授勋仪式上的致词中，感谢中国在帮助越南人民迅速修复河内—睦南关铁路之后，又派一个铁路整修队来帮助加固它，同时帮助越南培养和建立了一个铁路整修队。
    在授勋的当天晚上，越南交通邮电部设宴招待了中国铁路整修队的五十多个代表。
    在越南七个多月的中国铁路整修队将在10月17日离越南回国。
