Title: Pakistani Prime Minister to Visit Our Country Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. At the invitation of Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese government, Prime Minister Hussein Shaheed Suhrawardy of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and Foreign Minister Malik Firoz Khan Noon will pay an official visit to China from October 18th to 29th. They will depart from Karachi on October 16th, and their stay in China will be twelve days.

### 巴基斯坦总理即将来我国访问

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理即将来我国访问
    新华社7日讯　应中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来和中国政府的邀请，巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底和外交部长马利克·菲罗兹·汗·努恩将自10月18日到29日来中国作正式访问。他们将在10月16日自喀喇蚩动身，在中国的停留期限是十二天。
