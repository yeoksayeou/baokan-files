Title: Northwest Steel Base Under Exploration (Picture)
Author: Hu Yue
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Yue photographed:

    Northwest Steel Base Under Exploration
    An exploration team is searching for iron ore in the Jingtieshan area of ​​Gansu. In more than half a year, they have found more than ten ore bodies. Now the exploration team continues to search for mineral deposits in the Jingtieshan area.

    Explorers have settled down in the Jingtieshan area.
    Exploration team members take mineral samples on cliffs.

### 西北钢铁基地在勘探中（图片）
胡越
1956-10-27
第2版()
专栏：

    西北钢铁基地在勘探中
    一支勘探人员，正在甘肃镜铁山区寻找铁矿，半年多来，已找到了十多处矿体。现在勘探人员继续在镜铁山一带寻找矿藏。
                          新华社记者　胡越摄
    勘探人员在镜铁山区安下了家。
    勘探队员在悬崖峭壁间，采取矿样。
