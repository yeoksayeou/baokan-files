Title: Britain Increases Troops in the Persian Gulf Region
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Cairo dispatch: According to a report in "Al-Ahram" on October 1st, three thousand fully armed British troops landed in Bahrain in the Persian Gulf, and two other British warships carrying troops were anchored eighty miles off the coast of Bahrain.
    The newspaper said that the action of dispatching these troops to the Persian Gulf region was carried out after the summit meeting of the three major Arab countries held by Saudi Arabian King Saud, President Nasser, and Syrian President Kuwatly.

### 英国在波斯湾地区增兵

1956-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    英国在波斯湾地区增兵
    新华社5日讯　开罗消息：据“金字塔报”10月1日报道，三千名全副武装的英国军队在波斯湾的巴林登陆，另外还有两艘载着军队的英国军舰停泊在离巴林海岸八十英里的地方。
    这家报纸说，把这些军队派遣到波斯湾地区去的行动是在沙特阿拉伯沙特国王、纳赛尔总统和叙利亚总统库阿特利举行了阿拉伯三大国最高级会议以后进行的。
