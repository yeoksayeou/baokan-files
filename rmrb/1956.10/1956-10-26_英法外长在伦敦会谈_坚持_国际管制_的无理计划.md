Title: British and French Foreign Ministers Meet in London, Insisting on the Unreasonable Plan of "International Control"
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 24th - It is reported from London that French Foreign Minister Pineau arrived in London from Paris on October 23 and held talks with British Prime Minister Eden and Foreign Secretary Lloyd. Pineau said after the meeting that Britain and France still insist on the "Eighteen-Nation Proposal" to "internationalize" the Suez Canal.

### 英法外长在伦敦会谈  坚持“国际管制”的无理计划

1956-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    英法外长在伦敦会谈
    坚持“国际管制”的无理计划
    新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：法国外交部长比诺10月23日从巴黎到伦敦，同英国首相艾登和外交大臣劳埃德举行了会谈。比诺在会谈后说，英法仍然坚持使苏伊士运河“国际化”的“十八国建议”。
