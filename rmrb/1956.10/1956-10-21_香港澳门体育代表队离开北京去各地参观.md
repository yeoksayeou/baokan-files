Title: Hong Kong and Macao Sports Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Places
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: The Hong Kong and Macao sports delegation, which participated in the selection competition for the Chinese Olympic team, left Beijing today to visit various places.

Cai Shufan, Director of the Preparatory Committee for China's Participation in the 16th Olympic Games and Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, hosted a farewell banquet for them on the evening of the 19th.

The Hong Kong and Macao sports delegation will visit Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou successively, and then return to Hong Kong and Macao respectively.

### 香港澳门体育代表队离开北京去各地参观

1956-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    香港澳门体育代表队离开北京去各地参观
    新华社20日讯　参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的香港和澳门的体育代表队今天离开北京到各地参观游览。
    中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会主任、体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩19日晚曾经设宴欢送他们。
    香港和澳门的体育代表队将先后到天津、南京、上海、杭州、广州参观游览，然后分别回香港和澳门。
