Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 29th—Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Huseyn Shaheed Suhrawardy, and his entourage, left Guangzhou for home by special train this morning. Geng Biao, our country's ambassador to Pakistan, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied them to Shenzhen.

At Guangzhou Railway Station, Prime Minister Suhrawardy told those who came to see him off that he had held free and friendly talks with Chinese leaders, in which they discussed matters relating to the interests of the world and the mutual interests of China and Pakistan. The Prime Minister said that these talks, conducted in an atmosphere of friendship and warmth, had greatly helped to better understand our common problems and to strengthen relations between our two countries. We should develop closer relations between China and Pakistan through more such contacts to promote understanding and growing cooperation between the two countries.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage arrived at Guangzhou Railway Station at 6:30 this morning. Accompanied by Governor Tao Zhu, Mayor Zhu Guang, and Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison, Prime Minister Suhrawardy reviewed the guard of honor. Then the distinguished guests shook hands with the heads of various departments, organizations, and groups in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, representatives from all walks of life, as well as the Polish Consul in Guangzhou and the Acting Consul of the Soviet Union in Guangzhou who came to see them off.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy stepped up to the loudspeaker and bid farewell to those who came to see him off. The citizens of Guangzhou responded to Prime Minister Suhrawardy's enthusiastic speech with warm applause and cheers of friendship. The Prime Minister smiled and walked in front of the farewell crowd to shake hands with them.

Subsequently, Prime Minister Suhrawardy walked through the crowd waving goodbye and entered the carriage. After the train started, people continued to wave to the distinguished guests.

### 苏拉瓦底总理离广州回国

1956-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理离广州回国
    新华社广州29日电　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员，今天早晨乘专车离开广州回国。我国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和外交部礼宾司司长王倬如陪送他们到深圳。
    苏拉瓦底总理在广州车站上对欢送他的人们说，他和中国的领袖们进行了自由而友好的会谈，在会谈中讨论了有关世界利益和中巴两国相互利益的事项。总理说，这些在友好热诚气氛中进行的会谈已经大大地有助于更好地了解我们共同的问题，和加强我们两国间的关系，我们应该通过更多的这样的接触来发展中国和巴基斯坦之间的更紧密的关系，促进两国之间的了解和日益增长的合作。
    苏拉瓦底总理和他的随行人员在早晨六时三十分来到广州车站。在陶铸省长、朱光市长和广州驻军首长文年生中将陪同下，苏拉瓦底总理检阅了仪仗队。然后贵宾们同广东省、广州市各机关团体负责人，社会各界代表以及前往欢送的波兰驻广州领事和苏联驻广州的代领事握手告别。
    苏拉瓦底总理走到扩音机前，向欢送他的人们话别。广州市民们以热烈的掌声和友谊的欢呼声，回报苏拉瓦底总理的热情洋溢的讲话。总理含着微笑走到欢送人群前面同他们握手。
    随后，苏拉瓦底总理穿过夹道欢送的人群走进车厢。列车开动后，人们还不断地向贵宾们招手。
