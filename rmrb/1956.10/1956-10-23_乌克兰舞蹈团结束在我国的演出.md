Title: Ukrainian Dance Troupe Concludes Performances in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 22nd. The Soviet Ukrainian National Dance Troupe held its closing ceremony today in Tianjin, marking the end of its performance tour in our country.

    Yang Yizhou, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, expressed welcome and gratitude to the Ukrainian artists from the Dnieper River on behalf of the people of Tianjin at the closing ceremony.

    Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, said in his speech: You have already performed forty times in eight cities in our country, including Wuhan, Guangzhou, and Beijing, and more than 200,000 audience members have enjoyed your wonderful artistic performances. Xia Yan, on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, expressed his heartfelt gratitude to all the members of the Soviet Ukrainian National Dance Troupe. He concluded by saying: We believe that you will bring back to your country the profound friendship of the Chinese people towards the Soviet people.

    The artistic director of the dance troupe, P. P. Virsky, said in his reply: Today we are very happy to hold the last performance of our dance troupe in the People's Republic of China here. He concluded by saying, I would like to say here a word that is very dear to both the Soviet people and the Chinese people: Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people! He also shouted "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!" in Chinese! At this time, the whole audience burst into warm applause.

    After the closing ceremony, the artists performed fifteen wonderful programs, including the Ukrainian folk dances "Embroidered Girl" and "Gopak," for more than 2,400 audience members in Tianjin. The beautiful and enthusiastic dances were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 乌克兰舞蹈团结束在我国的演出

1956-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    乌克兰舞蹈团结束在我国的演出
    新华社天津22日电　苏联乌克兰国家舞蹈团今天在天津举行了来我国访问演出的闭幕式。
    天津市副市长杨亦周在闭幕式上代表天津市人民向来自第聂伯河的乌克兰艺术家们表示欢迎和感谢。
    文化部副部长夏衍在致词中说：你们已经在我国武汉、广州、北京等八个城市作了四十场访问演出，有二十多万观众欣赏了你们精彩的艺术表演。夏衍代表中华人民共和国文化部对苏联乌克兰国家舞蹈团的全体人员表示衷心的感谢。他最后说：我们相信你们将把中国人民对苏联人民的深厚友谊带回你们祖国去。
    舞蹈团艺术指导巴·巴·维尔斯基在答词中说：今天我们在这里举行我们舞蹈团在中华人民共和国的最后一次表演会，我们感到非常高兴。他最后说，我想在这里说一句对于苏联人民和中国人民都是非常亲切的话：中苏两国人民永恒的、牢不可破的友谊万岁！他并且用中国话高呼“中苏友好万岁！”这时，全场暴发出热烈的掌声。
    闭幕式以后，艺术家们为天津市二千四百多名观众表演了乌克兰民间舞蹈“绣花姑娘”、“哥巴克”等十五个精彩节目。优美而热情的舞蹈受到了观众们的热烈欢迎。
