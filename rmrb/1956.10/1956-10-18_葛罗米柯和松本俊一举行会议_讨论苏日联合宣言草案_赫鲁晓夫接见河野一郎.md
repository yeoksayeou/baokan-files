Title: Gromyko and Matsumoto Shunichi Hold Meeting to Discuss Draft of Soviet-Japanese Joint Declaration, Khrushchev Receives Kono Ichiro
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 5

    Gromyko and Matsumoto Shunichi Hold Meeting
    Discuss Draft of Soviet-Japanese Joint Declaration
    Khrushchev Receives Kono Ichiro
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. TASS Moscow dispatch: Soviet Plenipotentiary Representative Gromyko and Japanese Plenipotentiary Representative Matsumoto Shunichi held a meeting on the 16th in the conference hall of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs. They were entrusted with conducting preliminary discussions on the draft documents and would submit the agreed-upon drafts to the delegations of both governments for review.
    The meeting discussed the draft of the joint declaration and the draft protocol on developing trade and granting each other most-favored-nation treatment.
    The meeting was conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.
    Both parties agreed that the representatives would continue to study the aforementioned drafts in order to submit mutually agreed-upon recommendations to the Soviet and Japanese government delegations for review.
    The next meeting of the representatives is scheduled for October 17th.
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. TASS Moscow dispatch: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received Kono Ichiro, a member of the Japanese government delegation currently in Moscow for negotiations on the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations and the Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, on the 16th, and held talks with him.

### 葛罗米柯和松本俊一举行会议  讨论苏日联合宣言草案  赫鲁晓夫接见河野一郎

1956-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯和松本俊一举行会议
    讨论苏日联合宣言草案
    赫鲁晓夫接见河野一郎
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联全权代表葛罗米柯和日本全权代表松本俊一16日在苏联外交部会议厅举行会议。他们被委托对各项文件草案进行初步讨论，并且将把取得协议的草案提交双方政府代表团审查。
    会上讨论了联合宣言草案以及关于发展贸易和互相给予最惠国待遇的议定书草案。
    会议是在诚恳和友好的气氛中进行的。
    双方议定，代表们继续研究上述草案，以便把双方取得一致的建议提交苏日两国政府代表团审查。
    下次代表会议定于10月17日举行。
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫16日接见了目前在莫斯科进行苏日关系正常化谈判的日本政府代表团团员、农林相河野一郎，并且和他进行了会谈。
