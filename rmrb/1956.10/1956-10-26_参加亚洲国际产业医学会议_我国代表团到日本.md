Title: Our Delegation Arrives in Japan to Attend Asian International Industrial Medicine Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th: The delegation from the Chinese Medical Association, invited to attend the Asian International Industrial Medicine Conference, arrived in Tokyo by plane on October 22nd.

The Chinese delegation, headed by Liu Yunzhong, brought several papers and will present them at the conference.

### 参加亚洲国际产业医学会议  我国代表团到日本

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲国际产业医学会议
    我国代表团到日本
    据新华社25日讯　东京消息：中华医学会应邀派出参加亚洲国际产业医学会议的代表团已经在10月22日乘飞机到东京。
    以刘允中为首的中国代表团携带论文数篇，将在会上发表。
