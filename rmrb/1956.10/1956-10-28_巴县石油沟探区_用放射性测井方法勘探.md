Title: Exploration of the Shiyougou Exploration Area in Ba County Using Radioactive Logging Methods
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 2

    Shiyougou Exploration Area in Ba County
    Explored Using Radioactive Logging Methods
    (Xinhua News Agency) Starting from the 24th, the Shiyougou exploration area in Ba County, Sichuan Province, is using radioactive logging methods to search for oil and natural gas.
    Prior to this, the Sichuan Provincial Petroleum Exploration Department had already adopted various logging methods such as electrical logging and gas logging here. However, because this area has a limestone formation, the logging was not very effective. Now, using radioactive logging methods, it may be easier to find fractured oil, gas, and water permeable zones in limestone formations.

### 巴县石油沟探区  用放射性测井方法勘探

1956-10-28
第2版()
专栏：

    巴县石油沟探区
    用放射性测井方法勘探
    据新华社讯　四川省巴县石油沟探区，从24日起使用放射性测井方法寻找石油和天然气。
    在此以前，四川省石油勘探部门已在这里采用了电测、气测等各种测井办法。但是因为这个地区是石灰岩地层，所以测井的收效不大。现在使用放射性测井方法，就有可能比较容易地在石灰岩地层中找到有裂缝的油、气、水渗透地带。
