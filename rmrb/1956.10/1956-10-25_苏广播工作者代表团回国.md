Title: Soviet Broadcasting Workers Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 5

    Soviet Broadcasting Workers Delegation Returns Home
    (Our newspaper dispatch) The Soviet Broadcasting Workers Delegation of seven people, headed by N.V. Shomin, Director of the Soviet Central Broadcasting Administration, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 24th to return to their country.

### 苏广播工作者代表团回国

1956-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏广播工作者代表团回国
    本报讯　以苏联中央广播事业局局长尼·弗·萧明为首的苏联广播工作者代表团一行七人，在24日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
