Title: Shanghai Establishes Halal Dining Hall
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The state-run halal dining hall in Shanghai began operating on the 15th.

    This dining hall has a large dining room and a snack section, which can accommodate three hundred people at the same time. The dining hall serves more than three hundred kinds of halal dishes, snacks, and marinated foods with both southern and northern flavors every day. According to Islamic regulations, the cattle, sheep, chickens, and ducks used in the dining hall are slaughtered by an Ahong.

### 上海设立清真食堂

1956-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    上海设立清真食堂
    据新华社讯　上海的国营清真食堂于15日开始营业。
    这个食堂有很大的餐厅和一个小吃部，可以同时容纳三百人用膳。食堂每天供应三百多种南、北口味的清真菜肴、点心和卤味。根据伊斯兰教的教规，食堂所用的牛、羊、鸡、鸭都由阿訇来宰。
