Title: National People's Congress Delegate Liu Hongsheng Passes Away Due to Illness
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd. Mr. Liu Hongsheng, a delegate to the National People's Congress, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Standing Committee member and Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and a well-known figure in China's industrial and commercial circles, passed away at his residence in Shanghai on the evening of October 1st due to a severe heart condition that could not be cured after prolonged treatment. He was 69 years old.

Mr. Liu Hongsheng was a native of Dinghai, Zhejiang. He engaged in national industry and commerce for several decades before his death. After liberation, he actively participated in various patriotic movements and enthusiastically supported and implemented the state's policy of socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce.

Yesterday, those who went to offer condolences upon hearing the news included Sheng Pihua, Vice Mayor of Shanghai and Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce, as well as representatives from the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, the United Front Work Department of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, and people from industrial and commercial circles.

### 全国人民代表大会代表刘鸿生病逝

1956-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表刘鸿生病逝
    新华社上海2日电　全国人民代表大会代表、政协全国委员会委员、中国民主建国会中央委员会常务委员兼上海市委员会副主任委员、我国工商界知名人士刘鸿生先生，因患严重心脏病，长期诊治无效，10月1日晚在上海寓所病逝。享年六十九岁。
    刘鸿生先生是浙江定海人。生前从事民族工商业数十年；解放后积极参加各项爱国运动，热诚拥护并贯彻国家对资本主义工商业的社会主义改造政策。
    昨日闻讯前往吊唁的有上海市副市长、上海市工商业联合会主任委员盛丕华以及上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会、中共上海市委员会统一战线工作部的代表和工商界人士等。
