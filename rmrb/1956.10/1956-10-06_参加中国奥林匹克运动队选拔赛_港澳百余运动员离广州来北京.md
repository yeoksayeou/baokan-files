Title: More Than 100 Athletes from Hong Kong and Macau Leave Guangzhou for Beijing to Participate in the Chinese Olympic Team Trials
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 5th. One hundred and twenty-six athletes from Hong Kong and Macau, participating in the Chinese Olympic Team trials, left Guangzhou for Beijing on the morning of the 4th.

The Hong Kong team included football (two teams), basketball (two teams), swimming, track and field, weightlifting, and gymnastics athletes, totaling one hundred and two people, led by Zhang Guoxuan, director of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce of Hong Kong and vice chairman of the Hong Kong Chinese Importers' and Exporters' Association. The Macau team consisted of twenty-four basketball and swimming athletes, led by Cui Deqi, vice chairman of the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce.

During their stay in Guangzhou, the Hong Kong and Macau athletes visited and toured the city, visited relatives and friends, and held several exciting exhibition matches, which were watched by more than 100,000 spectators.

The Guangdong Provincial People's Committee and the Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation held banquets and tea parties to receive them.

### 参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛  港澳百余运动员离广州来北京

1956-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛
    港澳百余运动员离广州来北京
    新华社广州5日电　参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的香港、澳门运动员一百二十六人，4日上午离开广州前往北京。
    香港运动队包括足球（两队）篮球（两队）、游泳、田径、举重、体操运动员共一百零二人，由香港中华总商会会董、香港中华出入口商会副理事长张国璇担任总领队。澳门运动队有篮球、游泳运动员二十四人，总领队是澳门中华总商会副理事长崔德祺。
    港澳运动员在广州期间，除参观游览和探访亲友外，并曾举行了多次精采的表演赛，有十多万观众观看了他们的表演。
    广东省人民委员会和中华全国体育总会广东省分会曾分别举行宴会和茶会招待他们。
