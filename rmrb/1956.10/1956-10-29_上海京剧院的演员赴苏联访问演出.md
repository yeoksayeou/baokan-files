Title: Shanghai Peking Opera Company Actors Visit Soviet Union for Performances
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: A group of over fifty actors and staff from the Shanghai Peking Opera Company, led by the company's director Zhou Xinfang, departed from Beijing today for Moscow to conduct a two-month performance tour in the Soviet Union. Another group of over ten actors and staff from the Shanghai Peking Opera Company had already traveled to Moscow in advance.

The main actors of this company include the director, renowned Peking Opera performing artist Zhou Xinfang, and famous Peking Opera actors Li Yuru, Wang Jinlu, Zhang Meijuan, Zhao Xiaolan, and others. They will perform eighteen plays, including "Fifteen Strings of Cash," "Four Scholars," "Fisherman's Revenge," "The Drunken Beauty," "Stealing the Magic Herb," and "Mount Yandang."

### 上海京剧院的演员赴苏联访问演出

1956-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    上海京剧院的演员赴苏联访问演出
    据新华社28日讯　中国上海京剧院的演员等五十多人在院长周信芳率领下，今天从北京启程赴莫斯科，将在苏联作两个月的访问演出。中国上海京剧院的另一部分演员和工作人员十多人已先期前往莫斯科。
    这个剧院的主要演员有担任院长的著名京剧表演艺术家周信芳，著名京剧演员李玉茹、王金璐、张美娟、赵晓岚等人。他们将演出“十五贯”、“四进士”、“打渔杀家”、“贵妃醉酒”、“盗仙草”、“雁荡山”等十八个剧目。
