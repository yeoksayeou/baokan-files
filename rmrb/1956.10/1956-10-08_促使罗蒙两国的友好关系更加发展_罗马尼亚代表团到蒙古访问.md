Title: Promoting Further Development of Friendly Relations Between Romania and Mongolia: Romanian Delegation Visits Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 7th - A delegation from the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane on the 6th for a visit at the invitation of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Gheorghiu-Dej stated at the airport that the Romanian and Mongolian peoples have a very good friendship, and that this visit by the delegation will further develop the friendly relations between the two countries. Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, including Tsedenbal and Sambu, welcomed the Romanian delegation at the airport.

    Tsedenbal also spoke at the airport. He welcomed the distinguished Romanian guests to Mongolia on behalf of the Mongolian people.

    The citizens of Ulaanbaatar, in the warm sunshine, stood on both sides of the road to warmly welcome the distinguished guests.

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 7th - The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic hosted a banquet on the evening of October 6, with a Mongolian noble whole sheep and mare's milk wine, to welcome the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic.

    Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, and Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party, raised their glasses at the banquet, wishing for the further development of the friendly relations between the Mongolian and Romanian peoples, and wishing both countries greater achievements in socialist construction.

    Leaders of the Mongolian Party and government, as well as ministers of various departments, attended the banquet. Ambassadors of various countries to Mongolia were also invited to attend the banquet.

    On the afternoon of October 6, the Romanian delegation paid a visit to the leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, and the Council of Ministers. The delegation also visited Choibalsan University and the Industrial United Factory.

### 促使罗蒙两国的友好关系更加发展  罗马尼亚代表团到蒙古访问

1956-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    促使罗蒙两国的友好关系更加发展
    罗马尼亚代表团到蒙古访问
    新华社乌兰巴托7日电　以罗马尼亚工人党第一书记乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团，应蒙古人民共和国的邀请，6日乘飞机到达乌兰巴托访问。
    乔治乌—德治在机场上发表讲话说，罗蒙两国人民有着很好的友谊，代表团这次访问，将使两国的友好关系更加发展。蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人泽登巴尔、桑布等在机场欢迎罗马尼亚代表团。
    泽登巴尔也在机场讲了话。他代表蒙古人民欢迎罗马尼亚贵宾访问蒙古。
    乌兰巴托的市民们在温暖的阳光下，站在马路两旁热烈地欢迎贵宾们。
    新华社乌兰巴托7日电　蒙古人民革命党和蒙古人民共和国部长会议10月6日晚上以蒙古尊贵的全羊和马奶酒欢宴罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团。
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔、罗马尼亚工人党第一书记乔治乌—德治先后在宴会上举杯，祝蒙罗两国人民的友好关系更加发展，祝两国在社会主义建设中取得更大成就。
    蒙古党和政府的领导人以及各部部长等出席了宴会。各国驻蒙古使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    罗马尼亚代表团在10月6日下午拜会了蒙古人民革命党、大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议的负责人。代表团还参观了乔巴山大学和工业联合工厂。
