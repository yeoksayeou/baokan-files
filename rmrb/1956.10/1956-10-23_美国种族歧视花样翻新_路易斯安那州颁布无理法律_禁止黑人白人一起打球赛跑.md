Title: American Racial Discrimination Takes on New Forms: Louisiana Enacts Absurd Law Prohibiting Black and White People from Playing Ball Games and Racing Together
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd, New York Dispatch: American Louisiana State began enforcing a racial discrimination law from October 15th. This law stipulates that Black people must not participate in any sports activity with White people at the same time. Violators can be sentenced to one year in prison and a fine of one thousand dollars.

### 美国种族歧视花样翻新  路易斯安那州颁布无理法律  禁止黑人白人一起打球赛跑

1956-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    美国种族歧视花样翻新
    路易斯安那州颁布无理法律
    禁止黑人白人一起打球赛跑
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：美国路易斯安那州从10月15日起执行一项种族歧视的法律。这项法律规定，黑人不得同白人同时参加任何一项体育活动，违禁者可以处一年徒刑和一千美元罚款。
