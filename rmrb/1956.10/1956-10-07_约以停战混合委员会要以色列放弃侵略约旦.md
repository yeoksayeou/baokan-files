Title: Jordanian-Israeli Mixed Armistice Commission Demands Israel Cease Aggression Against Jordan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th—The Jordan-Israel Mixed Armistice Commission held a meeting on October 4th. The commission demanded that the Israeli authorities cease their acts of aggression against Jordan.

### 约以停战混合委员会要以色列放弃侵略约旦

1956-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    约以停战混合委员会要以色列放弃侵略约旦
    据新华社6日讯　约旦—以色列停战混合委员会10月4日举行了会议。委员会要求以色列当局放弃对约旦的侵略行动。
