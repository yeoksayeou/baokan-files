Title: Casablanca Dockworkers Prevent French Troops from Landing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 20th: According to newspaper reports, dockworkers in Casablanca, Morocco, prevented 163 French soldiers arriving from Marseille from landing on the 19th. They also blocked the moving of unloaded military supplies into warehouses.
    The movement of the Moroccan working people demanding the withdrawal of French troops from Morocco has been growing recently. They also oppose the French army using Morocco as a base for suppressing the Algerian national liberation movement.

### 卡萨布兰卡码头工人阻止法军登陆

1956-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    卡萨布兰卡码头工人阻止法军登陆
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：据报纸报道，摩洛哥卡萨布兰卡港的码头工人19日没有让从马赛来的一百六十三个法国士兵登陆。他们还阻挡把已经卸下的军需品搬入仓库。
    摩洛哥劳动人民要求法国军队撤出摩洛哥的运动最近已经愈来愈蓬勃。他们还反对法军利用摩洛哥作为镇压阿尔及利亚民族解放运动的根据地。
