Title: Fifteen-Nation Conference Disagreements: No Agreement Reached on Organizational Structure and Other Issues of the "Suez Canal Users Association"
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, October 5th: The fifteen-nation ambassadorial-level conference in London passed a resolution on October 4th regarding the organization of the "Suez Canal Users Association." It stipulates that the "Association" shall have a council composed of all member states, an executive committee composed of seven countries elected from the council's member states, and a chief executive officer called the administrator.
The conference failed to reach an agreement on issues such as the composition of the executive committee, the selection of the administrator, and the location of the "Association's" headquarters due to disagreements among member states.

### 十五国会议意见分歧  在“运河使用国协会”机构组成等问题上没有达成协议

1956-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    十五国会议意见分歧
    在“运河使用国协会”机构组成等问题上没有达成协议
    据新华社5日讯　伦敦消息：十五国伦敦大使级会议10月4日通过了一项关于“苏伊士运河使用国协会”组织的决议。它规定“协会”有一个由全体会员国组成的理事会，有一个由理事会会员国中选出的七个国家组成的执行委员会，还有一个叫作管理人的首席执行官员。
    会议在执行委员会的组成、管理人人选和“协会”总部的地点等问题上因为会员国意见分歧没有达成协议。
