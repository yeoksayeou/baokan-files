Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai Visit Japanese Commodity Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, visited the Japanese Commodity Exhibition this morning, accompanied by Shukuya Eiichi, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, and Kataoka Takashi, deputy head of the group.
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and ministers of various ministries, visited the Japanese Commodity Exhibition this afternoon, accompanied by Murata Shozo, Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Ikeda Masanosuke, representative of the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, and Shukuya Eiichi, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group.

### 朱德副主席、周恩来总理参观日本商品展览会

1956-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席、周恩来总理参观日本商品展览会
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国朱德副主席今天上午在日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一、副团长片冈峻的陪同下，参观了日本商品展览会。
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理和各部部长，今天下午在日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏、日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表池田正之辅和日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一的陪同下，参观了日本商品展览会。
