Title: Deng Zihui Hosts Banquet to See Off Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui hosted a banquet at 1:00 p.m. today to bid farewell to the Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation, which is about to return home. Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

At the banquet, Deng Zihui said that through the delegation's extensive and in-depth inspections and visits in China, mutual understanding between China and Yugoslavia in the field of agriculture has been greatly enhanced. Deng Zihui also expressed his hope that agricultural workers from the two countries will have more exchanges in the future in order to further exchange experience in agricultural production and promote the development of agricultural production in both countries. Delegation leader Jo. Veselinov and Ambassador Popovic also spoke. They expressed their deep gratitude to the Chinese people for the brotherly and warm reception the delegation received during its visit and stated that they would bring the Chinese people's warm and friendly feelings to the Yugoslav people.

Since arriving in Beijing on September 5, the Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation has visited Beijing and the northeastern cities of Shenyang, Dalian, and Anshan. Before returning home, the delegation will also make short visits to Nanjing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou.

### 邓子恢设宴欢送南农业代表团

1956-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    邓子恢设宴欢送南农业代表团
    据新华社3日讯　国务院副总理邓子恢今日下午一时设宴欢送即将回国的南斯拉夫农业代表团。南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和使馆人员也应邀参加了宴会。
    邓子恢在宴会上说，经过代表团这次在中国广泛深入的考察和访问，已经大大增进了中、南两国之间在农业方面的相互了解。邓子恢还希望今后两国农业工作者有更多的交往，以便进一步交流农业生产的经验，促进两国农业生产的发展。代表团团长约·维塞利诺夫、大使波波维奇也都讲了话。他们对中国人民给予代表团在访问过程中兄弟般的热情接待表示深切谢意；并且表示要将中国人民对代表团的热烈友好感情带回去告诉南斯拉夫人民。
    南斯拉夫农业代表团自9月5日到达北京后，曾在北京和去东北沈阳、大连、鞍山等地参观访问。代表团在归国以前，还将在南京、上海和广州等地作短时期的参观访问。
