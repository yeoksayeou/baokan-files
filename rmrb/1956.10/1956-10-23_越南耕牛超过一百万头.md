Title: Vietnam's Draft Cattle Exceed One Million Head
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 22nd -- According to the Vietnamese Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, the number of draft cattle in North Vietnam has increased by more than 254,000 head in the first nine months of this year, exceeding the state's original annual draft cattle increase plan by more than 15,000 head. The total number of draft cattle in Vietnam now exceeds one million head.

During the war, Vietnam's draft cattle suffered heavy slaughter by the colonial army. To ensure the practical realization of the draft cattle number development plan, the Vietnamese government has organized farmers and made great efforts in feeding work.

### 越南耕牛超过一百万头

1956-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南耕牛超过一百万头
    据新华社河内22日电　据越南农林部消息，越南北部的耕牛在今年头9个月里已经增加了二十五万四千多头，比国家原定的今年全年增加耕牛计划超过一万五千多头。现在越南耕牛总数已超过一百万头。
    越南的耕牛在战争期间曾经遭到殖民军队的大量屠杀。为了保证耕牛数目的发展计划能够切实实现，越南政府曾经组织农民，对喂养工作做了很大努力。
