Title: Chairman Mao's Inscription for the Japanese Commodity Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong wrote an inscription for the Japanese Commodity Exhibition. The full text of the inscription is as follows:

　　Having seen the Japanese Exhibition, I think it is very good. Congratulations to the Japanese people on their success.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong

### 毛主席为日本商品展览会题词

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席为日本商品展览会题词
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席为日本商品展览会题词。题词全文如下：
      看了日本展览会，觉得很好。祝贺日本人民的成功。
                                     毛泽东
