Title: Syria, Egypt, and Jordan to Confer on Joint Defense Issues; Saudi Arabian King Expresses Firm Support for Jordan

Author:

Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, October 23rd: Syrian Army Chief of Staff Nizam al-Din announced on October 22nd that he would leave for Amman within forty-eight hours to discuss "joint defense measures for Syria, Egypt, and Jordan to deal with the threat from Israel."

Nizam al-Din told reporters that in Amman he would meet with Egyptian Minister of Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Egyptian-Syrian Joint Command, General Abdul Hakim Amer, and Jordanian Army Chief of Staff Abu Nuwar.

Nizam al-Din said: They will "decide on the joint defense measures to be taken by these three countries in the event of a new Israeli aggression against Jordan." These three countries have formed an alliance through border defense agreements to deal with the issue of Israel.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 23rd: The Middle East News Agency reported from Jeddah that Jordanian Foreign Minister Hadi left Saudi Arabia on October 21st to return home after holding four days of talks with Saudi Arabian authorities on providing military and financial assistance to Jordan.

Hadi said that he carried a letter from Saudi Arabian King Saud to Jordanian King Hussein, promising "firm support for Jordan and providing all the personnel and weapons it needs for defense."

### 叙埃约三国将会商联防问题  沙特阿拉伯国王表示坚决援助约旦

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    叙埃约三国将会商联防问题
    沙特阿拉伯国王表示坚决援助约旦
    据新华社23日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚陆军参谋长尼扎姆丁10月22日宣布，他将在四十八小时内动身前往安曼，讨论“叙利亚、埃及和约旦对付以色列的威胁的联合防御措施”。
    尼扎姆丁对记者们说，他在安曼将同埃及国防部长兼埃及—叙利亚联合司令部总司令阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔将军和约旦陆军参谋长阿布·诺瓦尔将军进行会谈。
    尼扎姆丁说：他们将“决定在一旦以色列对约旦发动新的侵略时这三国所采取的联合防御措施”。这三个国家在对付以色列问题上已经通过边界防御协定结成了联盟。
    新华社23日讯　开罗消息：中东通讯社记者从吉达报道，约旦外交大臣哈迪同沙特阿拉伯当局举行了关于给予约旦军事和财政援助的四天会谈以后，已经在10月21日离开沙特阿拉伯回国。
    哈迪说，他带有沙特阿拉伯国王沙特给约旦国王侯赛尼的一封信，信中答应“坚决支持约旦，并且提供所有它在防御上需要的人员和武器”。
