Title: Hungarian Population Nears Ten Million
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 15th: A Budapest dispatch states: The Hungarian Central Bureau of Statistics published an annual report stating that the Hungarian population was 9,860,000 on January 1st of this year. Among them, the Hungarian capital, Budapest, has 1,900,000 people.

### 匈牙利人口接近千万

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人口接近千万
    新华社布拉格15日电　布达佩斯消息：匈牙利中央统计局出版的年鉴说，匈牙利的人口在今年1月1日有九百八十六万人。其中匈牙利首都布达佩斯有一百九十万人。
