Title: Polish First Deputy Prime Minister Minc Resigns Due to Illness
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 10th: Newspapers here published a communique from the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party on the 10th. The communique stated that Comrade Hilary Minc, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and First Deputy Prime Minister of the Polish Council of Ministers, had requested the Political Bureau to relieve him of his duties as a member of the Political Bureau and to resign from his position as First Deputy Prime Minister of the Polish Council of Ministers due to a long-term illness that prevented him from performing his duties. The Political Bureau has accepted Comrade Minc's request for resignation and will submit suggestions to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish Council of State, respectively, to accept his resignation as a member of the Political Bureau and First Deputy Prime Minister of the Council of Ministers.

### 波兰部长会议第一副主席  明兹因病辞职

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰部长会议第一副主席
    明兹因病辞职
    新华社华沙10日电　此间报纸在10日刊载了波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局的公报。公报说，波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员、波兰部长会议第一副主席希拉里·明兹，因长期患病不能执行他担负的职务曾经提请政治局解除他的政治局委员职务和辞去波兰部长会议第一副主席职务。政治局已经接受了明兹同志的辞职请求，并且将分别向波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰国务委员会提出建议，接受他辞去政治局委员和部长会议第一副主席职务的请求。
