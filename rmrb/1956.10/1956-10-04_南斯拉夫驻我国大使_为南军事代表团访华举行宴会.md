Title: Yugoslav Ambassador to China Holds Banquet for Visit of Yugoslav Military Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, held a banquet this evening for the visit of the Yugoslav Military Delegation to China.

Invited to attend were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Ye Jianying, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Xiao Ke, General Deng Hua, General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Yugoslavia.

On the morning of October 3rd, General Kosta Nadj, on behalf of the Yugoslav Military Delegation, presented a gift to Marshal Peng Dehuai of the People's Republic of China. The gifts were an exquisite bronze sculpture, an oil painting, and a hunting rifle.

### 南斯拉夫驻我国大使  为南军事代表团访华举行宴会

1956-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫驻我国大使
    为南军事代表团访华举行宴会
    新华社3日讯　南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇为南斯拉夫军事代表团访问中国，在今天晚上举行宴会。
    应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，叶剑英元帅，中国人民解放军高级将领萧克上将、邓华上将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、萧向荣中将等人。
    在宴会上，宾主们频频为中、南两国人民和军队的兄弟友谊干杯。
    三日上午，科斯塔·纳季上将代表南斯拉夫军事代表团，向中华人民共和国彭德怀元帅赠礼。礼品是一座精致的青铜雕刻、一幅油画和一枝猎枪。
