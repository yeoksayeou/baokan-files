Title: Vietnamese Parliamentary Delegation Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th - The Vietnamese National Assembly delegation led by Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, left Hanoi today for the Soviet Union. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    The delegation will attend the Soviet Union's October Revolution Day celebrations.

### 越议会代表团赴苏访问

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　越议会代表团赴苏访问
    新华社河内8日电　由越南国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜率领的越南国民大会代表团今天离开河内前往苏联。这个代表团是应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请去苏联作友好访问的。
    代表团将参加苏联的十月革命节庆祝典礼。
