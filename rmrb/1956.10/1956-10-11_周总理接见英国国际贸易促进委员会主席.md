Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Chairman of the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Lord Boyd-Orr, Chairman of the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and his wife this evening.

Present at the reception were Nan Han-chen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Ji Chao-ding, Vice-Chairman, and Huang Hua, Director of the West European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见英国国际贸易促进委员会主席

1956-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英国国际贸易促进委员会主席
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见英国国际贸易促进委员会主席波伊德—奥尔勋爵和夫人。
    接见时在座的有中国国际贸易促进委会员主席南汉宸、副主席冀朝鼎和外交部西欧司司长黄华。
