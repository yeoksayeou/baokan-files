Title: Czechoslovakian Musicians Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Czechoslovakian musicians delegation, led by head Zhdaněk Mikula, left Beijing on the 14th to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.
    Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.
    During their forty-three-day visit to China, the delegation performed twenty-four concerts, playing the masterpieces of great Czech musicians Smetana and Dvořák, as well as works by European classical masters Haydn and Beethoven, for Chinese audiences and music lovers.

### 捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团离京

1956-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团离京
    据新华社讯　捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团由团长日丹涅克·米库拉率领，已在14日离京赴蒙古人民共和国访问。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎曾举行宴会欢送代表团。
    代表团在中国四十三天的访问中，共演出了二十四场，为中国听众和音乐爱好者演奏了捷克伟大音乐家斯麦塔那、德沃夏克的名作，以及欧洲古典音乐大家海顿、贝多芬的作品。
