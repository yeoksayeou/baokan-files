Title: Chairman U Nu Arrives in Kunming at Premier Zhou's Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 22nd. At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of the Union of Burma arrived in Kunming by special plane at 2:38 pm today for a visit to China.

Accompanying Chairman U Nu were his wife, U Min Thein, Judge of the High Court of the Union of Burma, and his wife, Bo Thin Maung Kyi, Executive Secretary of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of the Union of Burma and Member of Parliament, U Tun Shein, Executive Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Khin Nyo, Director of the Research Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Yao Zhongming, China's ambassador to Burma, accompanied Chairman U Nu to Kunming.

Today, Kunming was sunny and bright, and more than 3,000 people of all ethnic groups gathered at the airport to welcome Chairman U Nu. The national flags of China and Burma fluttered in the wind at the airport.

At 2:38 pm, a silver special plane landed slowly at Kunming Airport. Chairman U Nu stepped off the plane amidst cheers of "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship," smiling and shaking hands with Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Pan Shoduan, Mayor of Kunming, and heads of various departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations of Yunnan Province, and leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming. Buddhists from Kunming also greeted Chairman U Nu with palms together. A group of Young Pioneers and youths rushed forward to present flowers to Chairman U Nu, and shouts of "Welcome Chairman U Nu" and "Long live peace" were constantly heard in the crowd. Chairman U Nu also responded to the cheers with "Long live" in Chinese.

As U Nu walked through the welcoming crowd, Dai youths danced the peacock dance to the rhythm of elephant-foot drums. U Nu watched the dance with great interest.

Afterwards, Chairman U Nu delivered a speech. He said that this was his second visit to Kunming. In 1940, he visited as a member of the Burmese friendship delegation. He said that this time, friends gave him such a grand welcome, as if he had arrived at the home of his relatives. I don't feel like I am in a foreign country, but as if I am in my own country, it's so cordial. Friends are so friendly and welcome us like relatives, which makes me very moved.

Today, U Maw Maw Doke, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and members of the Consulate General, and representatives of the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also went to the airport to welcome U Nu.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 22nd. Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of the Union of Burma left Yangon for China on a special Chinese plane at 8:00 am today. He is making a friendly visit to China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai.

Yao Zhongming, China's Ambassador to Burma, accompanied U Nu to China. U Nu's wife and entourage also went to China with him.

Those who saw him off at the airport were: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister Sao Khun Cho, Minister of Information Thakin Chit Maung, Minister of Relief and Resettlement Sao Htun Aye, Minister of Labor U Tin Nyunt, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ba Swe, Prime Minister's Secretary U Thant, Aung San's wife, and Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, Dr. Sin Po.

Lei Yang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, embassy staff, and diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in Burma also went to the airport to see him off.

Before U Nu boarded the plane, Chinese and Indian expatriates in Yangon presented him with wreaths and bouquets.

### 应周总理的邀请来我国访问  吴努主席到达昆明

1956-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应周总理的邀请来我国访问
    吴努主席到达昆明
    新华社昆明22日电　应国务院周恩来总理的邀请来中国访问的缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努，今天下午二时三十八分乘专机到达昆明。
    随同吴努到达昆明的，有吴努主席的夫人，缅甸联邦高级法院法官吴敏登和夫人，缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟执行秘书，国会议员波丁貌基，外交部执行秘书吴吞辛，外交部研究司司长吴钦纽等人。
    我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同吴努主席到达昆明。
    今天，昆明市阳光明媚，三千多各民族人民聚集在机场上，欢迎吴努主席，中缅两国国旗在机场上迎风飘扬。
    二时三十八分，一架银色的专机在昆明机场上缓缓着陆，吴努主席在“中缅友谊万岁”的欢呼声中走下飞机，微笑着和云南省副省长刘明辉、昆明市市长潘朔端以及云南省各机关、民主党派、人民团体负责人和中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长握手相见。昆明市佛教界人士也合掌迎接吴努主席。一群少先队员和青年涌上前来向吴努主席献花，人群里不断发出“欢迎吴努主席”“和平万岁”的欢呼声。吴努主席也不断用中国话“万岁”来答复人们的欢呼。
    当吴努走过欢迎的人群时，傣族青年人和着象脚鼓的节奏声跳起了孔雀舞，吴努很有兴趣地站着观看了舞蹈。
    接着，吴努主席发表了讲话。他说，这是他第二次来到昆明访问，在1940年的时候，他曾以缅甸友好代表团团员之一的身份来访问过。他说，这次我来访问，朋友们对我作了如此盛大的欢迎，就像到了自己亲戚家里一般。我不像到了外国，而好像到了自己的国家一样地亲切。朋友们是如此的友好，是如此亲戚般地欢迎我们，使我非常感动。
    今天，缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎和总领事馆人员，越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆的代表，都到机场上欢迎吴努。
    新华社仰光22日电　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努在今天上午八时搭乘一架中国专机离开仰光前往中国。他应周恩来总理邀请到中国进行友好访问。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同吴努前往中国。同吴努一起去中国的还有努夫人和随行人员。
    到机场送行的有：缅甸副总理藻昆卓，宣传部长德钦漆貌，救济和安置部长绍吞埃，劳工部长吴丁纽，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，总理秘书吴丹，昂山夫人，缅中友好协会主席信波博士。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和使馆人员，以及各国驻缅甸外交使节也到机场欢送。
    在吴努登机前，仰光的中国侨民和印度侨民向他献了花环和花束。
