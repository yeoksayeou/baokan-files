Title: Suhrawardy Expresses Gratitude to Premier Zhou Enlai in a Telegram Before Leaving Our Country

Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai to express his gratitude before leaving Chinese territory. The telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    As I leave your vast and beautiful land, I wish to once again express my own and my companions' deep gratitude to your great Chairman, Your Excellency, and the Chinese Government and people for the warm welcome and gracious hospitality we have received everywhere. If my visit helps to clear up misunderstandings and strengthen the friendly relations between China and Pakistan, I will have better accomplished my task. I sincerely hope that this friendship will grow and flourish in the years to come.

    I am deeply moved by the achievements you have made for your people in such a short period of time. I wish you even greater achievements and wish the Chinese people eternal prosperity and progress. Please accept, Your Excellency, my deep admiration and regards, and thank you once again for your hospitality. I will leave with the most pleasant memories. I look forward to your visit to my country.
                                      
                                      H.S. Suhrawardy
                        October 29, Bao'an County

### 苏拉瓦底总理在离开我国国境前  致电周恩来总理表示感谢

1956-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理在离开我国国境前
    致电周恩来总理表示感谢
    新华社29日讯　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底在离开中国国境前，致电周恩来总理表示感谢。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生阁下：
    在我离开你们广阔而美丽的国土的时候，我愿再一次向你们的伟大的主席，阁下以及中国政府和人民对我们到处受到的热烈欢迎和殷勤招待表示我个人以及我的同伴们的深切的谢意。如果我的访问有助于澄清误解，加强中国和巴基斯坦的友好关系，我就更好地完成了我的任务。我衷心希望这一友谊将在今后的年代里成长和蓬勃发展。
    我深深地为你们在这样短的时间内为你们的人民作出的成绩而感动。祝你们取得更大的成就，祝中国人民永远兴旺、进步。阁下，请接受我的深切的钦佩和致意，并再一次感谢你们对我们的招待。我将带着最愉快的回忆离开。我期待着你到我的国家来访问。
                                      苏拉瓦底
                        10月29日于宝安县
