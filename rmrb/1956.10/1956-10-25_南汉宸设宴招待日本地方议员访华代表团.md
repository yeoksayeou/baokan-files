Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for Japanese Local Assembly Members Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the entire Japanese Local Assembly Members Delegation to China, which arrived in Beijing yesterday.

    Among those present at the banquet were Lei Renmin and Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China. Mr. Suketani Eiichi, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, and others were also invited to the banquet.

### 南汉宸设宴招待日本地方议员访华代表团

1956-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    南汉宸设宴招待日本地方议员访华代表团
    新华社24日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚设宴欢迎昨天到京的日本地方议员访华代表团全体人员。
    出席宴会作陪的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中国红十字会会长李德全等人。应邀参加宴会的还有日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一等人。
