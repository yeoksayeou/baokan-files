Title: Gandhi's Eighty-Seventh Birthday Anniversary: Commemorations Held Across India
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 3rd. Assemblies and various forms of social activities were held across India on October 2nd to commemorate the eighty-seventh birthday anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, the father of the Indian nation. The commemorative activities will continue for a week.
    Indian President Prasad issued a message regarding the commemoration of Mahatma Gandhi's birthday.

### 甘地诞生八十七周年  印度各地举行纪念

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    甘地诞生八十七周年
    印度各地举行纪念
    据新华社新德里3日电　印度各地10月2日举行集会和各种形式的社会活动，纪念印度国父圣雄甘地诞生八十七周年。纪念活动将继续举行一个星期。
    印度总统普拉沙德发表了关于圣雄甘地诞生纪念的文告。
