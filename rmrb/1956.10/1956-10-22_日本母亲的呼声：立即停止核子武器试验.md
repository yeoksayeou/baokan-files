Title: A Call from Japanese Mothers: Immediately Stop Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th: Approximately five hundred mother representatives from all over Japan held a conference in Tokyo today. The conference passed a resolution demanding the immediate prohibition of all nuclear weapon testing.

The conference decided to send Kubo Suzuko, the wife of Aikichi Kuboyama, the first victim of the American hydrogen bomb, to New York to appeal to the United Nations General Assembly, which will convene in November, to stop nuclear weapon testing and to submit the conference's resolution to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld.

### 日本母亲的呼声：立即停止核子武器试验

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日本母亲的呼声：
    立即停止核子武器试验
    新华社20日讯　东京消息：来自日本全国各地的约五百个母亲代表今天在东京举行大会。大会通过决议，要求立即禁止核子武器的一切试验。
    大会决定派遣美国氢弹的第一个牺牲者久保山爱吉的妻子久保山铃到纽约去，向将于11月召开的联合国大会呼吁停止试验核子武器，并且把这个大会的决议提交给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
