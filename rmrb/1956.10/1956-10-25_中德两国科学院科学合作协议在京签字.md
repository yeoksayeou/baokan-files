Title: Sino-German Academies of Sciences Sign Scientific Cooperation Agreement in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. The scientific cooperation agreement between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the German Academy of Sciences for 1957-1958 was signed in Beijing tonight.

    The delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the delegation of the German Academy of Sciences, in accordance with the cultural cooperation agreement signed by the two governments, reached this agreement in October this year in order to further develop and strengthen the scientific cooperation between the two academies of sciences in the future and to strengthen the friendship between the two peoples.

    The agreement stipulates the specific tasks of cooperation between the two academies of sciences in 1957-1958. According to this agreement, the two academies of sciences will exchange and train young scientific workers; send senior scientific workers to conduct scientific research, lecture, and participate in relevant conferences; jointly conduct scientific research and exchange scientific information and materials.

    Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, signed on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Steinmitz, head of the delegation of the German Academy of Sciences, signed on behalf of the German Academy of Sciences.

    Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Chinese scientists, and all members of the delegation of the German Academy of Sciences attended the signing ceremony.

    Deikes, First Secretary of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, attended the signing ceremony.

    After the signing ceremony of the agreement, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, presented gifts to the German Academy of Sciences and the members of the delegation.

    After the signing ceremony, Guo Moruo held a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the delegation of the German Academy of Sciences who were about to leave China.

### 中德两国科学院科学合作协议在京签字

1956-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中德两国科学院科学合作协议在京签字
    新华社24日讯　中国科学院和德国科学院关于1957年—1958年科学合作协议今天晚上在北京签字。
    中国科学院的代表团和德国科学院代表团，根据两国政府签定的文化合作协定，为进一步发展和加强今后两国科学院之间的科学合作事业和加强两国人民的友谊，在今年10月间，达成了这个协议。
    协议规定了1957年—1958年度两国科学院合作的具体任务。根据这个协议，两国科学院将交换培养青年科学工作人员；互派高级科学工作人员进行科学研究、讲学和相互参加有关会议；共同进行科学研究以及交换科学情报和资料等。
    代表中国科学院签字的是中国科学院副院长陶孟和，代表德国科学院签字的是德国科学院代表团团长斯坦因尼茨。
    参加签字仪式的有中国科学院院长郭沫若和中国的科学家，以及德国科学院代表团全体人员。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书戴克斯参加了签字仪式。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若在协议书签字仪式结束后，向德国科学院及代表团人员赠送了礼品。
    签字仪式结束后，郭沫若举行宴会，为即将离开中国的德国科学院代表团全体人员饯行。
