Title: Chinese and Japanese Railway Union Representatives Sign Agreement to Strengthen Friendship Among Railway Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Representatives from the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China and the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation's China Delegation signed four agreements in Beijing on the 23rd to strengthen friendship between Chinese and Japanese railway workers.

Wang Zhijie, Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China, and Yasuhisa Ryōichi, leader of the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation's China Delegation, signed the agreements on behalf of their respective railway unions.

Attending today's signing ceremony were Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat; Chen Yu and Ma Chungu, Executive Committee Members; Xiao Hongqi and Liang Yongfu, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China; and Itakawa Seigo and Kitamura Tomizo, Deputy Leaders of the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation's China Delegation, as well as all members of the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The full text of the agreement signed in Beijing by representatives of the Chinese and Japanese railway unions on strengthening friendship between Chinese and Japanese railway workers is as follows:

The National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China and the People's Republic of China Investigation Delegation of the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation, based on the agreement reached between Mr. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Mr. Fujita Totaro, Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, on November 27, 1955, have fully consulted on specific measures to further strengthen exchanges and friendship between the two sides in the future. After full consultation, the following items have reached complete consensus and the following agreement:

	1. Mutually dispatch and exchange delegations;

	2. Exchange newspapers, magazines, materials, photos, and paintings to exchange information on labor movement, sports activities, and cultural activities;

	3. Deepen the friendship and understanding between Chinese and Japanese railway workers, consolidate unity, and bravely strive to promote the normalization of diplomatic relations, so as to achieve coexistence and co-prosperity;

	4. Strengthen the solidarity and cooperation of Asian workers, and contribute to the efforts to achieve Asian peace and independence, as well as world peace.

	National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China
	Chairman Wang Zhijie

	People's Republic of China Investigation Delegation of the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation
	Leader Yasuhisa Ryōichi
	October 23, 1956

### 中日两国铁路工会代表  签订加强铁路工人友好的协议

1956-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中日两国铁路工会代表
    签订加强铁路工人友好的协议
    新华社23日讯　中国铁路工会全国委员会和日本私营铁路工会总联合会中国考察团，23日在北京签订了加强中日两国铁路工人友好的四项协议。
    中国铁路工会全国委员会主席王志杰，日本私营铁路工会总联合会中国考察团团长安恒良一分别代表两国铁路工会在协议上签字。
    参加今天的签字仪式的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜，书记处书记董昕，执行委员陈宇、马纯古，中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启、梁永福，日本私营铁路工会总联合会中国考察团副团长板川正五、北村富藏和全体团员。
    新华社23日讯　中日两国铁路工会代表在北京签订的关于加强中日两国铁路工人友好的协议，全文如下：
    中国铁路工会全国委员会和日本私营铁路工会总联合会中华人民共和国考察代表团，根据1955年11月27日中华全国总工会主席赖若愚先生与日本工会总评议会议长藤田藤太郎先生之间所订的协议事项，就今后双方进一步加强交流和友好的具体措施，充分地进行商谈的结果，对下列各项获得完全一致的意见，达成如下协议：
    一、互相派遣和交换代表团；
    二、为互相交流工运、体育活动和文化活动等情况，进行交换报纸、杂志、资料、照片和绘画；
    三、加深中、日铁路工人的友谊和了解，巩固团结，勇敢地为促进邦交正常化而努力，以求共存共荣；
    四、加强亚洲工人的团结和合作，为努力实现亚洲和平与独立以及世界和平而作出贡献。
    中国铁路工会全国委员会
    主席王志杰
    日本私营铁路工会总联合会中华人民共和国考察团
    团长安恒良一
                1956年10月23日
