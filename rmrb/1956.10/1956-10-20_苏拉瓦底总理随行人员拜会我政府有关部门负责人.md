Title: Pakistan Prime Minister Suhrawardy's Entourage Visits Responsible Persons of Relevant Departments of Our Government
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Members of Pakistan Prime Minister Suhrawardy's entourage today visited the responsible persons of relevant departments of the Chinese government.
    Ghulam Faruque, Chairman of the Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation, visited Bo Yibo, Director of the State Economic Commission; S.M. Yusuf, Joint Secretary of the Pakistan Ministry of Commerce, visited Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Khan M. Azam Khan, Pakistani water conservancy expert and Chief Engineer of the Warsak Hydropower Project, visited Qian Zhengying, Vice Minister of Water Resources; and Colonel Hamid Nawaz Khan, Director of Protocol of the Pakistan Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Commonwealth Relations, visited Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached photo)
    Prime Minister Suhrawardy visited Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai on the 19th. The photo shows the group photo during the meeting. Front row from right to left: Huang Yanpei, Mrs. Begum, Shen Junru, Princess Abida Sultana, Li Jishen, Ahmed, Liu Shaoqi, Mrs. Akhta Suleiman, Mao Zedong, Suhrawardy, Zhu De, Mrs. Noon, Zhou Enlai, Mrs. Ahmed, Chen Yun, Mian Sayed A. Beg.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo, Lü Houmin.

### 苏拉瓦底总理随行人员拜会我政府有关部门负责人

1956-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理随行人员拜会我政府有关部门负责人
    新华社19日讯　巴基斯坦苏拉瓦底总理的随行人员今天上午分别拜会了中国政府有关部门的负责人。
    巴基斯坦工业开发公司主席吴拉姆·法鲁克拜会了国家经济委员会主任薄一波，巴基斯坦商务部联合秘书斯·穆·优素福拜会了对外贸易部副部长雷任民，巴基斯坦水利专家、瓦尔萨克水利工程总工程师汗·穆·阿查姆汗拜会了水利部副部长钱正英，巴基斯坦外交和联邦关系部交际司长哈米德·纳瓦兹汗上校拜会了外交部礼宾司司长王倬如。（附图片）
