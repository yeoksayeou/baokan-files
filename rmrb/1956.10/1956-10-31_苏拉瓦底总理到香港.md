Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Arrives in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 30th. Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy arrived in Hong Kong by train on October 29th from Guangzhou.

When Suhrawardy answered questions from reporters at the station, he said that he considered his visit to China this time a success.

When Suhrawardy spoke about his talks with Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai in Beijing, he said, "We talked about everything." He also said, "The whole atmosphere was very harmonious—this was not superficial, but actually like that."

### 苏拉瓦底总理到香港

1956-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理到香港
    新华社香港30日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底10月29日从广州乘火车到达香港。
    苏拉瓦底在车站回答报界的问题的时候说，他认为他这次对中国的访问是成功的。
    苏拉瓦底在谈到在北京同毛泽东主席和周恩来总理会谈的时候说：“我们无所不谈”。他还说：“整个气氛都是非常融洽的——这不是表面上的，而是实际上是这样的。”
