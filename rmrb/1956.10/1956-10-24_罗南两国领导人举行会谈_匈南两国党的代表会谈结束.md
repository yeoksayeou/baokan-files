Title: Romanian and Yugoslav Leaders Hold Talks; Meeting of Representatives of Hungarian and Yugoslav Parties Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: A communique issued in Belgrade on the evening of October 22nd stated that the Romanian government and the delegation of the Romanian Workers' Party had begun talks with the Yugoslav delegation on the morning of October 22nd.

    During the talks, the two sides reviewed the implementation of the Romanian-Yugoslav Bucharest Agreement and expressed satisfaction with the results achieved, which led to the drafting of the Romanian-Yugoslav trade and payment agreement, the 1956-1960 exchange of goods protocol, and the scientific and technological cooperation agreement. These agreements and protocols will be signed during the Romanian delegation's visit to Yugoslavia.

    A cultural cooperation agreement is being drafted.

    The communique also stated that during the talks, the two sides also reviewed measures related to further developing broad cooperation between the two countries.

    On the Yugoslav side, those participating in the talks included Tito, Pijade, Kardelj, and others; on the Romanian side, those participating included Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Stoica, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: The meeting between the delegation of the Hungarian Working People's Party and the delegation of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia concluded on the evening of October 22nd in Belije, Croatia.

    A joint communique signed during the meeting will be published simultaneously in the capitals of both countries.

    After the meeting concluded, Ranković, Secretary of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, hosted a banquet in honor of the Hungarian guests who were about to leave Yugoslavia to return to their country.

### 罗南两国领导人举行会谈  匈南两国党的代表会谈结束

1956-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    罗南两国领导人举行会谈
    匈南两国党的代表会谈结束
    新华社23日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：10月22日晚间在贝尔格莱德发表的一份公报说，罗马尼亚政府和罗马尼亚工人党代表团同南斯拉夫代表团已经在10月22日上午开始举行会谈。
    在会谈中，双方检查了罗南布加勒斯特协议的执行情况，并且对所获得的成果表示满意，这些成果导致了拟定罗南贸易和支付协定，1956—1960年换货议定书，以及科学技术合作协定。这些协定和议定书将在罗马尼亚代表团访问南斯拉夫期间签订。
    文化合作协定正在拟定中。
    公报又说，会谈中，双方还审议了有关进一步发展两国间广泛合作的措施。
    参加会谈的，南斯拉夫方面有铁托、皮雅杰、卡德尔等人；罗马尼亚方面有乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡等人。
    新华社23日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：匈牙利劳动人民党代表团同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟代表团的会谈10月22日晚间在克罗地亚共和国的贝利埃结束。
    会谈中签署的联合公报即将在两国首都同时发表。
    会谈结束以后，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会书记兰科维奇设宴招待即将离开南斯拉夫回国的匈牙利客人。
