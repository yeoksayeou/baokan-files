Title: Korean Ministry of Light Industry Exceeds Planned Targets for First Three Quarters

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th -- It has been learned from relevant departments in Korea that by the end of September, the Korean light industry sector had exceeded its total output value plan for the first three quarters of this year by 16%. The sector's 1954-1956 three-year total output value plan was completed as early as the beginning of August. It is estimated that the total output value of light industry this year may exceed 91% of that in 1949.

The cotton textile sector put a large number of new equipment into production at the end of last year and the beginning of this year. Employees increased the utilization rate of the equipment and completed the three-year plan in August this year. The production of silk fabrics also exceeded the three-year production plan by 80% at the end of August.

### 朝鲜轻工业部  超额完成头三个季度计划

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜轻工业部
    超额完成头三个季度计划
    据新华社平壤16日电　从朝鲜有关部门获悉，到9月底为止，朝鲜轻工业部门已经超额16％完成了今年头三个季度的总产值计划。这个部门的1954—1956年三年总产值计划早在8月初就已完成。预计今年轻工业的总产值可以超过1949年的91％。
    棉纺织部门在去年年底和今年年初有大量新设备投入生产，职工们提高了设备的利用率，在今年8月就完成了三年计划。丝织品的生产也在8月底超额80％完成三年生产计划。
