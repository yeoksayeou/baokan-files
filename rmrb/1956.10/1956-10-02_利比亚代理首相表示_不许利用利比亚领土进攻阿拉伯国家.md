Title: Libyan Acting Prime Minister States: Libya Will Not Allow Its Territory to be Used to Attack Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, 30th -- TASS reported from Tripoli on the 29th: Libyan Acting Prime Minister Kalal stated that Libya will not allow its territory and the military bases on its territory to be used to attack any Arab country.

    Kalal made these remarks when answering questions from a reporter from the Egyptian radio station "Voice of the Arabs." He believes that Egypt's proposal to establish a negotiating body to peacefully resolve the Suez Canal issue proves Egypt's good will in striving for a peaceful resolution to the dispute. This solution will guarantee Egypt's sovereignty and the freedom of navigation on the Suez Canal.

　　He said: The Western countries' proposal to establish a "Suez Canal Users Association" is vague, and the countries participating in this plan themselves are also divided.

　　Kalal also expressed the hope that the Security Council will discuss the opinions of both sides in accordance with the principles of the United Nations Charter in order to peacefully resolve the dispute.

### 利比亚代理首相表示  不许利用利比亚领土进攻阿拉伯国家

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    利比亚代理首相表示
    不许利用利比亚领土进攻阿拉伯国家　　
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社的黎波里29日讯：利比亚代理首相卡拉勒说，利比亚不允许把它的领土和在它领土上的军事基地用来攻击任何一个阿拉伯国家。　　
    卡拉勒是在回答埃及“阿拉伯人之声”电台记者的问题的时候说这番话的。他认为埃及关于成立一个和平解决苏伊士运河问题的谈判机构的建议证明了埃及争取和平解决争端的良好愿望这种解决办法将保证埃及的主权和苏伊士运河的通航自由。
　　他说：西方国家提出的成立“运河使用国协会”的方案是含混不清的，而且参与这个方案的国家本身也发生了分歧。　　卡拉勒还表示希望，安全理事会将根据联合国宪章的原则为和平解决争端而讨论双方的意见。
