Title: Two Philippine Senators Call for Trade with China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Manila, October 21st: Philippine Senator Edmundo Cea, Chairman of the Senate Committee on Commerce and Industry, reiterated his past proposal to trade with China in the Senate on October 20th. This senator from the Nacionalista Party (the ruling party) pointed out that even the three major Western powers are consciously or unconsciously doing business with China.
　　Another Nacionalista Party Senator, Jose Locsin, also stated in the Senate on the same day that the Philippines should seriously consider trading with China.

### 菲律宾两参议员要求同中国贸易

1956-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    菲律宾两参议员要求同中国贸易
    新华社21日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾参议院工商委员会主席埃德蒙多·塞阿10月20日在参议院重申他过去提出的要同中国贸易的主张。这个国民党（执政党）的参议员指出，甚至西方三大国也正在有意识地或者无意识地同中国做生意。
    国民党的另一个参议员何塞·洛克辛同一天也在参议院表示，菲律宾应该认真地考虑同中国进行贸易。
