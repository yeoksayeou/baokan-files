Title: Indian Military Attaché Hosts Reception for Visiting Indian Military Delegation

Author:

Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Indian Military Attaché to China, Brigadier Malik, hosted a reception this evening for the visiting Indian Military Friendship Delegation to China.

Among those invited to the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China; Long Yun, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Chen Geng, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Gan Siqi, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Navy Vice Admiral Fang Qiang, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, as well as representatives from various military branches and arms of service.

Also invited to the reception were Ding Xilin and Chen Hansheng, President and Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries were also invited to the reception.

At the reception, Brigadier Malik, Marshal Peng Dehuai, Ambassador R.K. Nehru, and Lieutenant General Thorat Nath Chaudhuri, head of the Indian Military Friendship Delegation, delivered speeches successively. At the conclusion of their speeches, they repeatedly proposed toasts to the health of the leaders of both countries, to the growing friendship between the peoples and their armed forces, and to the maintenance of the common cause of peace and the Five Principles.

### 印度驻华武官为印度军事代表团访华举行酒会

1956-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印度驻华武官为印度军事代表团访华举行酒会
    新华社15日讯　印度驻华武官马立克准将今天晚上为印度军事友好访华代表团访问中国举行酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的，有中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，国防委员会副主席龙云，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、李克农上将、李达上将、甘泗淇上将、刘亚楼空军上将、方强海军中将、肖向荣中将以及各军种、兵种部队的代表。
    应邀出席酒会的，还有中、印友好协会正副会长丁西林、陈翰笙等人。
    各国使节和驻华武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    在酒会上，马立克准将、彭德怀元帅、拉·库·尼赫鲁大使和印度军事友好访华代表团团长卓荣铎·纳特·乔杜里中将先后讲了话。在讲话结束时，他们一再提议为两国领袖的健康，为两国人民及其武装部队之间日益增长的友谊，为维护共同的和平事业和五项原则干杯。
