Title: Japanese Youth and Women's Delegation to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Japanese Youth and Women's Delegation to China, consisting of twenty-six members, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

　　The delegation's temporary leader, Eichi Hirasawa, and deputy leader, Yoshiko Nakajima, as well as the members of the delegation, were warmly welcomed at the station by Liu Xiyuan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation and Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Luo Yi, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the youth of Beijing.

　　This delegation is composed of representatives from the All-Japan Youth Women's Assembly, the Youth Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and the Women's Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan.

### 日本青年与妇女访华代表团到北京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    日本青年与妇女访华代表团到北京
    新华社30日讯　日本青年与妇女访华代表团二十六人今天上午乘火车到达北京。代表团是应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来中国访问的。
    代表团临时团长平泽荣一和副团长中岛芳子以及团员们在车站上，受到中华全国民主青年联合会副主席、中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记刘西元，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记罗毅，以及北京市青年们的热烈欢迎。
    这个代表团由全日本青年妇女会议、日本工会总评议会青年协议会和日本工会总评议会妇女协议会的代表们组成。
