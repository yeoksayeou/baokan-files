Title: Develop International Economic Cooperation, Improve People's Material Conditions: Poland Prepares to Compile Long-Term Economic Development Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 11th. According to the Polish Press Agency, the Polish State Economic Planning Commission has begun preparing a long-term plan for national economic development for the next fifteen to twenty years.

The work of collecting data will be completed in 1957. The first draft of the long-term plan will be drawn up in 1958. A crucial feature of this plan is that, unlike before, it will not develop all sectors of the national economy, but will instead more extensively develop international economic cooperation, developing certain sectors and producing certain products based on its own most advantageous conditions.

The main goal of the plan is to improve the material conditions of the people. Investments and other projects in the plan will be adapted to this main principle. This principle has already been adopted as the basis for compiling the five-year plan from 1956 to 1960.

One of the problems to be solved in the long-term plan is how much impact the use of atomic energy in industry will have on coal mining during the period of fifteen to twenty years.

### 发展国际经济合作，改善人民物质条件  波兰准备编制长期经济发展计划

1956-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    发展国际经济合作，改善人民物质条件
    波兰准备编制长期经济发展计划
    新华社华沙11日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰国家经济计划委员会已经开始了编制今后十五年到二十年的国民经济发展的长期计划的准备工作。
    1957年将完成收集资料的工作。长期计划的第一个草案将在1958年拟订出来。这个计划的极为重要的特点是：不像以前那样发展国民经济的一切部门，而是更广泛地发展国际经济合作，根据自己最有利的条件来发展某些部门和生产某些产品。
    计划的主要目的是改善人民的物质条件。计划中的投资和其他项目将适应这个主要原则。这个原则已经被采用作为编制从1956年到1960年的五年计划的基础。
    长期计划中尚待解决的问题之一是，在十五年到二十年的期间内，在工业上使用原子能究竟对于采煤将有多大影响。
