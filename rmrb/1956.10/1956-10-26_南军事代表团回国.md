Title: Yugoslav Military Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 25th: The military delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia concluded its visit to China and left Guangzhou by train today to return home. Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, the head of the Guangzhou garrison, accompanied the distinguished guests to Shenzhen.

### 南军事代表团回国

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    南军事代表团回国
    据新华社广州25日电　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国军事代表团结束了在中国的访问，今天乘火车离开广州回国。广州驻军首长文年生中将陪送贵宾们到深圳。
