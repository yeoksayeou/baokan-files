Title: Nuclear Physics Institute Under Construction Near Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 29th: According to a TASS report from Prague on the 28th, the largest scientific research institution in the country, the Nuclear Physics Institute, is under construction near Prague.

    The four-story main building of the institute is being built rapidly. It is reported that an atomic reactor will be installed in one of the main halls of the building, and experimental equipment will be installed in the remaining halls. At present, the concrete foundation of the atomic reactor has been completed, and installation workers have begun to replace the concrete workers here. The foundation for the cyclotron (the institute's second major machine) has also been laid.

### 布拉格近郊正建设核子物理研究所

1956-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    布拉格近郊正建设核子物理研究所
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社布拉格28日报道：布拉格近郊正在建设全国最大的科学研究机构之一——核子物理研究所。
    这个研究所的四层主楼正在迅速建筑中。据悉，主楼的一个大厅将安装原子反应堆，其余大厅将安装实验用的设备等。目前，原子反应堆的混凝土地基已经完工，安装工人已经开始代替混凝土工人来这里工作。安装回旋加速器（研究所的第二个主要机器）的地基也打好了。
