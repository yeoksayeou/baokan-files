Title: Indian Vice President Visits Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. News: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan arrived in Tokyo by plane last night. He will pay a nine-day friendly visit to Japan as a state guest.

    Radhakrishnan visited Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama this morning. According to reports, during the meeting, the Indian Vice President requested the Japanese Prime Minister to cooperate in maintaining peace in Asia and the world. Radhakrishnan also emphasized the need to strengthen cultural and economic exchanges between India and Japan.
    This afternoon, Radhakrishnan visited Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu. In the evening, Ichiro Hatoyama held a banquet to welcome the distinguished Indian guest.

### 印副总统访问日本

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    印副总统访问日本
    新华社2日讯　东京消息：印度副总统拉德哈克里希南在昨天晚上乘飞机到达东京。他将作为日本的国宾在日本进行九天的友好访问。
    拉德哈克里希南今天上午访问了日本首相鸠山一郎。据消息报道，在会见时，印度副总统要求日本首相在维护亚洲和世界和平方面进行合作。拉德哈克里希南并且强调需要加强印度和日本之间的文化和经济交流。
    今天下午，拉德哈克里希南访问了日本外务相重光葵。晚间，鸠山一郎举行宴会欢迎印度贵宾。
