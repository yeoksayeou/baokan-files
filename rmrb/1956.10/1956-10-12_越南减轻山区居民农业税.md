Title: Vietnam Reduces Agricultural Tax for Mountainous Residents
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th - According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Prime Minister's Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently announced an order to reduce the agricultural tax burden on residents in mountainous areas. The highest tax rates in these areas will be reduced by more than 6% compared to the Red River Delta region. In order to encourage fixed cultivation, the government has also decided to reduce taxes by 50% on mountainous land that has been continuously cultivated for three years.

### 越南减轻山区居民农业税

1956-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    越南减轻山区居民农业税
    新华社河内11日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南民主共和国总理府最近宣布了一项减轻山区居民的农业税负担的命令。在这些地区的最高税率将比红河三角洲地区减少6％以上。为了鼓励固定耕作，政府还决定对连续耕作三年的山区土地减少50％的税收。
