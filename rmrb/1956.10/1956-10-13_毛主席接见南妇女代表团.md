Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Yugoslav Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Yugoslav Women's Delegation headed by Lidija Šentjurc this evening.

    Present at the meeting were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Vice-Chairman Deng Yingchao, and Secretary of the Secretariat Cao Mengjun.

    Also present at the meeting were the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, and his wife.

### 毛主席接见南妇女代表团

1956-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见南妇女代表团
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席在今晚接见了以里第亚·申特尤尔茨为首的南斯拉夫妇女代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超、书记处书记曹孟君。
    接见的时候，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇和夫人也在座。
