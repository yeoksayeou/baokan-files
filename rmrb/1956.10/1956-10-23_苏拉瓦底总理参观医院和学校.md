Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Visits Hospitals and Schools
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage visited the Jishuitan Hospital, Tsinghua University, and the Central Institute for Nationalities in Beijing this morning.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy, accompanied by Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and Director of Jishuitan Hospital Xu Huamin, visited Jishuitan Hospital's laboratory, pharmacy, traditional Chinese medicine clinic, X-ray room, nursery, and operating room.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy, on behalf of the Government of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, presented five sets of medical equipment to Jishuitan Hospital. Director Xu Huamin presented Prime Minister Suhrawardy with an album containing photographs of the interior and exterior of Jishuitan Hospital as a souvenir.

At Tsinghua University, Prime Minister Suhrawardy and other distinguished guests accompanying him visited the university's hydraulic laboratory, hydroelectric power hub, the architecture department's laboratory, precision machine tool laboratory, and library. In the architecture department's laboratory, Prime Minister Suhrawardy carefully viewed many architectural models. He also viewed student work in the architecture department building.

At the Central Institute for Nationalities, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by many minority students. Prime Minister Suhrawardy happily took pictures with them at the school gate. (Attached pictures)

### 苏拉瓦底总理参观医院和学校

1956-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理参观医院和学校
    新华社22日讯　苏拉瓦底总理和他的随行人员，今天上午参观了北京市积水潭医院、清华大学、中央民族学院。
    苏拉瓦底总理由卫生部长李德全、北京市副市长吴晗和积水潭医院院长徐化民等陪同，参观了积水潭医院的化验室、药房、中医诊疗室、X光室、婴儿室和手术室等。
    苏拉瓦底总理代表巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国政府，赠给积水潭医院五套医疗器械。徐化民院长将一本贴有积水潭医院内外景照片的像册，赠送给苏拉瓦底总理留作纪念。
    在清华大学，苏拉瓦底总理和随行的其他贵宾们参观了这个大学的水力实验馆、水力发电枢纽、建筑系的实验室、精密机床实验室和图书馆等。在建筑系的实验室里，苏拉瓦底总理仔细地观看了许多建筑模型。在建筑系的大楼里，他还观看了学生的作业。
    在中央民族学院，贵宾们受到了许多少数民族学生的热情欢迎。苏拉瓦底总理愉快地和他们一起在学校门口留影。（附图片）
    积水潭医院接受苏拉瓦底总理赠送的医疗器材后，卫生部部长李德全同苏拉瓦底总理握手致谢。　本报记者　高粮摄
    苏拉瓦底总理在中央民族学院参观时，把穿着各种民族服装的学生们摄入镜头。　本报记者　高粮摄
