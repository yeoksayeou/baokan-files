Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Prominent Mexican Painter Siqueiros
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the famous Mexican painter Alfaro Siqueiros at 2:00 PM today.

Present at the meeting were Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Zuoren, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, and Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Siqueiros and his wife arrived in Beijing on October 11th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association.

### 周恩来总理接见墨西哥名画家西盖罗斯

1956-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见墨西哥名画家西盖罗斯
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理今天下午二时接见了墨西哥著名画家阿尔法罗·西盖罗斯。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，中国美术家协会副主席吴作人，外交部新闻司司长龚澎。
    西盖罗斯和他的夫人是应中国人民对外文化协会和中国美术家协会的邀请，在10月11日到达北京的。
