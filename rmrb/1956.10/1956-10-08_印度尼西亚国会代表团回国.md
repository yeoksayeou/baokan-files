Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation returned home by plane from Beijing this morning.

Those who saw them off at the airport were: Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia. They shook hands with the members of the Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation beside the plane to say goodbye. The young women of Beijing presented flowers to the members of the delegation before they boarded the plane.

The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation consisted of twenty-three members of parliament and two secretaries. After arriving in Beijing on September 13, they participated in the Chinese National Day celebrations, visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Changchun, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities, and held discussions with relevant Chinese parties on political, economic, social, and religious issues.

Also present at the airport to bid farewell today were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, and other heads of people's organizations, and Xue Zizheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

Officials from the Indonesian Embassy in China were also at the airport to see them off.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团回国

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团回国
    新华社6日讯　印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团今天早晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    到机场送行的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。他们在飞机旁边同印度尼西亚国会代表团团员们握手道别。北京的女青年们在代表团团员们登机前向他们献花。
    印度尼西亚国会代表团是由二十三位议员和两位秘书组成的。他们在9月13日到达北京后，曾经参加了中国国庆典礼，访问了北京、沈阳、鞍山、长春、南京、上海、杭州等城市，并且同中国各有关方面就政治、经济、社会、宗教等各项问题进行了座谈。
    今天到机场欢送的还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会的委员们，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之和其他各人民团体负责人，北京市副市长薛子正等。
    到机场送行的还有印度尼西亚驻华大使馆官员。
