Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Czechoslovakia for Romania

Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 22nd. The Indonesian parliamentary delegation, headed by Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party parliamentary group, left Czechoslovakia on the 21st.

    At a press conference before leaving Czechoslovakia, they said that the valuable experience and materials they obtained during their visit would be used in the construction of their country.

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. According to a report by the Romanian News Agency: The Indonesian parliamentary delegation, invited to visit Romania, arrived in Bucharest by plane on October 21st.

    Pavalache, Chairman of the Romanian National Assembly, who welcomed the guests at the airport, delivered a welcome speech, and Hadi delivered a reply.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团离捷赴罗访问

1956-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团离捷赴罗访问
    新华社布拉格22日电　以印度尼西亚国民党议会党团主席哈迪为首的印度尼西亚议会代表团，21日离开了捷克斯洛伐克。
    他们在离捷前的记者招待会上说，他们在访问期间所得到的有价值的经验和材料，将运用在他们国家的建设中。
    据新华社22日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：应邀到罗马尼亚访问的印度尼西亚国会代表团10月21日乘飞机抵达布加勒斯特。
    到机场欢迎的罗马尼亚国民议会主席帕伏列斯库向来宾们致欢迎词，哈迪致了答词。
