Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet for Japanese Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The All-China Journalists Association held a banquet this evening to entertain journalists from the Japanese newspapers "Asahi Shimbun," "Mainichi Shimbun," "Yomiuri Shimbun," "Trade Daily News," and "Jiritsu Keizai News Service," who are visiting Beijing with the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group.

Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, and Kunishin Shimazu, a reporter from the Japanese "Mainichi Shimbun," spoke at the banquet. They both expressed the hope that news reporting would promote the development of friendly relations between China and Japan and enhance the friendship between the two peoples.

### 我新闻工作者联谊会设宴招待日本记者

1956-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者联谊会设宴招待日本记者
    新华社10日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会今晚举行宴会，招待随日本商品展览团来北京访问的日本“朝日新闻”、“每日新闻”、“读卖新闻”、“贸易日日通信”和“自立经济特信”等报纸的记者。
    中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长王芸生和日本“每日新闻”报记者岛津国臣先后在宴会上讲了话，他们一致希望能通过新闻报道来促进中日两国友好关系的发展和增进两国人民的友谊。
