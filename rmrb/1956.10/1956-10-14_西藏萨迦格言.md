Title: Sakya Proverbs of Tibet
Author: Wang Yao
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 8

    Sakya Proverbs of Tibet
    A wise man bravely corrects his mistakes,
    A fool doesn't even dare to admit his shortcomings;
    An eagle can peck to death a venomous snake,
    A crow doesn't even dare to offend a small snake.
                ＊
    Around truly learned people,
    People naturally rush to consult them;
    In a fragrant flower cluster,
    Bees naturally gather like clouds.
             Translated by Wang Yao
   [Note] The Sakya sect is one of the four major sects of Lamaism.

### 西藏萨迦格言
王尧选
1956-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

    西藏萨迦格言
    聪明人能勇敢地改正他的错误，
    傻瓜连缺点都不敢承认；
    雄鹰能啄死有毒的大蛇，
    乌鸦连小蛇都不敢得罪。
                ＊
    在真正有学问的人的周围，
    自然有人争先恐后地去请教；
    在香气四溢的花丛里，
    自然有蜂儿像云朵一样聚集。
             王尧选译
   〔注〕萨迦派是喇嘛教四大教派之一。
