Title: Our Film Delegation Goes to Berlin to Participate in the Film Week of People's Democratic Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 6

　　Participating in the Film Week of People's Democratic Countries
　　Our Film Delegation Goes to Berlin
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. According to the provisions of the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement, our country was invited to send a film worker delegation to participate in the "Film Week of People's Democratic Countries" to be held by the German Democratic Republic starting from October 12.
    During the film week, the German Democratic Republic will screen the feature film "The Caravan Bell Rings in the Mountains" produced by the Shanghai Film Studio of our country.
    The head of the Chinese film worker delegation is film screenwriter Ke Ling, and the members include film actress Shu Xiuwen, and Ma Shouqing, deputy director of the Technical Department of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture. They left Beijing for Berlin on the 9th.

### 参加人民民主国家电影周  我电影代表团去柏林

1956-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　参加人民民主国家电影周
　　我电影代表团去柏林
    新华社9日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1956年执行计划的规定，我国应邀派遣电影工作者代表团前往参加德意志民主共和国自10月12日起举办的“人民民主国家电影周”。
    电影周期间，德意志民主共和国将放映我国上海电影制片厂出品的故事片“山间铃响马帮来”。
    中国电影工作者代表团团长是电影剧作家柯灵，团员有电影女演员舒绣文，文化部电影事业管理局技术处副处长马守清。他们已在9日离北京前往柏林。
