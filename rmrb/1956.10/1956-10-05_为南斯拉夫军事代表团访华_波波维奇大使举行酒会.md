Title: Ambassador Popovic Hosts Reception for Visit of Yugoslav Military Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, hosted a grand reception this afternoon for the visit of the Yugoslav Military Delegation to China.

    Those invited to attend included Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China; Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Chen Yi; Minister of Health Li Dequan; Director of the Chinese Language Reform Committee Wu Yuzhang; Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia Wu Xiuquan; Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Marshal Ye Jianying; and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Xiao Ke, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Deng Hua, Admiral Wang Hongkun, Senior General Chen Shiju, and others. Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in China were also invited to the reception.

    The hosts and guests engaged in cordial conversations at the reception, repeatedly raising their glasses to toast each other, and the hall was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere.

    Tonight, the head of the Yugoslav Military Delegation, Senior General Kosta Nadj, presented Marshal Zhu De with gifts, which included a bronze bas-relief and a finely printed album.

### 为南斯拉夫军事代表团访华  波波维奇大使举行酒会

1956-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    为南斯拉夫军事代表团访华
    波波维奇大使举行酒会
    新华社4日讯　南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇今天下午为南斯拉夫军事代表团访华举行盛大酒会。
    应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国总理周恩来，副总理陈云、陈毅，卫生部长李德全，中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章，中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，中国人民解放军高级将领萧克上将、李克农上将、邓华上将、王宏坤海军上将、陈士榘上将等人。各国驻中国使节和武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    宾主们在酒会上亲切地交谈，并一再举杯互相敬酒，大厅里充满了友好、热烈的气氛。
    今天晚上，南斯拉夫军事代表团团长科斯塔·纳季上将向朱德元帅赠礼，礼品是一座青铜浮雕和一本精印的画册。
