Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indonesian Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received Indonesian journalists who accompanied President Sukarno on his visit to China this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, Mei Yi and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 周总理接见印度尼西亚记者

1956-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度尼西亚记者
    新华社5日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了随同苏加诺总统来中国访问的印度尼西亚记者。
    接见的时候在座的，有外交部新闻司司长龚澎，亚洲司副司长陈叔亮，中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长梅益、王芸生。
