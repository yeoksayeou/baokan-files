Title: International Buddhist Delegation Departs Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The international Buddhist delegation left Beijing by plane this morning, returning to India via Kunming.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Ce, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council; Xirao Gyatso, President of the Chinese Buddhist Association; Venerable Neng Hai, Vice President; Zhao Puchu, layman, as well as many Buddhist monks and believers from the capital.
    Personnel from the Burmese and Indian Embassies, and Yang An, head of the Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 国际佛教僧侣代表团离北京

1956-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    国际佛教僧侣代表团离北京
    新华社9日讯　国际佛教僧侣代表团今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，经昆明返印度。
    前往机场送行的，有国务院副秘书长张策，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉措、副会长能海法师和赵朴初居士，以及首都佛教僧侣信众多人。
    缅甸大使馆和印度大使馆人员，柬埔寨王国驻华经济代表团团长杨安也到机场送行。
