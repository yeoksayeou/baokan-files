Title: Preliminary Survey of the Railway to the Karamay-Urho Oil Area Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 20th - The preliminary survey of the railway to the Karamay-Urho oil area has been completed. This line is an important branch of the Lanzhou-Urumqi-Aktogay Railway. Its starting point will be Shihezi New City or Dushanzi in the Manas reclamation area, passing through the Karamay-Urho oil area, and extending all the way to the Altay region. After the railway surveyors recently completed the preliminary survey to Karamay, they have separately started the preliminary survey from Karamay to Hoboksar Mongol Autonomous County and the reconnaissance work from Hoboksar Mongol Autonomous County to Altay.

### 通往克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区的铁路草测完毕

1956-10-22
第2版()
专栏：

    通往克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区的铁路草测完毕
    新华社乌鲁木齐20日电　通住克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区的铁路已经草测完毕。这条线路是兰州—乌鲁木齐—阿克斗卡铁路上的一条重要支线，它的起点将是玛纳斯垦区的石河子新城或独山子，经过克拉玛依—乌尔禾油区，一直延伸到阿勒泰地区。铁路勘测人员在最近草测到了克拉玛依后，已经分头展开从克拉玛依到和布克赛尔蒙古族自治县的草测和从和布克赛尔蒙古族自治县到阿勒泰的踏勘工作。
