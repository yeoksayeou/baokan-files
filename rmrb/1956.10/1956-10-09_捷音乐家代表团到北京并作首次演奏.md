Title: Czech Musicians Delegation Arrives in Beijing and Performs First Concert
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – A Czechoslovakian musicians delegation of twelve, led by head Ždan K. Mikula, arrived in Beijing yesterday and today.

    The delegation came to China at the end of August. Before coming to Beijing, they visited Shenyang, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and Hangzhou. The twenty musical performances given by the delegation in various places were warmly welcomed by more than 28,000 Chinese audience members.

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th – The Czechoslovakian musicians delegation gave its first concert in Beijing tonight.

    The Czechoslovakian musicians' concert tonight was warmly welcomed by the audience. Due to audience requests, the concert program was increased from the planned ten pieces to twenty.

    The musicians performed classical and folk music from Czechoslovakia, as well as famous pieces by Haydn and Beethoven.

    After the concert, Vice Ministers of Culture Zheng Zhenduo and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and others went on stage to shake hands with the Czechoslovakian musicians to express their gratitude.

### 捷音乐家代表团到北京并作首次演奏

1956-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    捷音乐家代表团到北京并作首次演奏
    据新华社7日讯　捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团一行十二人由团长日丹?克·米库拉率领，在昨天和今天先后到达北京。
    代表团在8月底就来到中国。他们在来北京以前，曾经访问沈阳、天津、上海、广州、武汉和杭州。代表团在各地举行的二十场音乐演奏会受到二万八千多名中国听众的热烈欢迎。
    据新华社8日讯　捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团今天晚上在北京举行首次演奏会。
    捷克斯洛伐克音乐家今晚的演奏会，受到听众的热烈欢迎。由于听众的请求，演奏会的节目由预定的十个增加到二十个。
    音乐家们今晚演奏了捷克斯洛伐克的古典音乐和民间音乐，也演奏了海顿和贝多芬的名曲。
    在演奏会结束以后，文化部副部长郑振铎、张致祥，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等登上舞台同捷克斯洛伐克音乐家们握手表示谢意。
