Title: Britain Threatens to Intervene in Israeli-Jordanian Conflict
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: London dispatch: A British Foreign Office spokesman stated on the 17th that the British government is prepared to intervene in the Jordanian-Israeli border conflict under certain circumstances.

The spokesman said: "In the event of a war of aggression against Jordan, the British government is fully prepared to fulfill its obligations under the Anglo-Jordanian treaty to assist Jordan."

However, the British "Daily Mail" pointed out on the same day that, worse still, Britain also bears the obligation of making the same promise to Israel.

### 英国扬言要干预以约冲突

1956-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    英国扬言要干预以约冲突
    新华社18日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人17日表示，英国政府在一定情况下要对约旦和以色列边境冲突事件加以干预。
    这位发言人说：“万一发生侵犯约旦的战争，英国政府完全准备履行英约（旦）条约所规定的义务去援助约旦。”
    但是英国“每日邮报”在同一天指出，事情更糟的是，英国也担负着对以色列作下的同样诺言的义务。
