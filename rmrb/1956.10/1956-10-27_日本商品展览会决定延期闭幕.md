Title: Japanese Product Exhibition Decides to Postpone Closing

Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. To meet the demands of the audience, the Japanese Product Exhibition has decided to postpone its closing for three days from the 27th.

The exhibition has been on display for three weeks, and the number of visitors has reached more than 1.1 million.

### 日本商品展览会决定延期闭幕

1956-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本商品展览会决定延期闭幕
    新华社26日讯　日本商品展览会为了满足观众的要求，决定从27日起延期三天闭幕。
    展览会展出三个星期以来，观众已达一百一十多万人。
