Title: Yugoslavia's Industrial Production to Reach Planned Targets This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency on the 8th, based on materials published by the Yugoslav Federal Bureau of Statistics, Yugoslav industrial production in the first eight months of this year increased by 7% compared to the same period last year.

The largest increase was in the paper industry (an increase of 44%), followed by the chemical industry (an increase of 19%). Rubber industry and electricity production increased by 15% and 14% respectively. According to estimates by the Federal Bureau of Statistics, the production targets of eighteen major industrial sectors will reach the goals stipulated in this year's social plan - an increase of 10% compared to last year.

### 南斯拉夫今年工业生产将达到规定指标

1956-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫今年工业生产将达到规定指标
    据新华社9日讯　南斯拉夫通讯社8日报道，根据南斯拉夫联邦统计局发表的材料，南斯拉夫工业生产在今年头八个月中比去年同期增加了7％。
    增加得最多的是造纸工业（增加44％），其次是化学工业（增加19％）。橡胶工业和电力的生产分别增加了15％和14％。据联邦统计局的估计，十八个主要工业部门的生产指标将达到今年社会计划中所规定的目标——比去年增加10％。
