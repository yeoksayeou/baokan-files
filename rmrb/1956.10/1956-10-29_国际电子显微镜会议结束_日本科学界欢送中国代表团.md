Title: International Electron Microscope Conference Concludes, Japanese Scientific Community Bids Farewell to Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 28th – The International Electron Microscope Conference for the Asia and Oceania region held in Tokyo concluded on the 27th. The Chinese delegation attending the conference, including Fang Xin-fang, attended a farewell reception held by the Japanese Society of Electron Microscopy that evening.

### 国际电子显微镜会议结束  日本科学界欢送中国代表团

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    国际电子显微镜会议结束
    日本科学界欢送中国代表团
    新华社28日讯　东京消息：在东京举行的亚洲和大洋洲地区国际电子显微镜会议已经在27日结束。出席会议的中国代表团方心芳等人当天晚上应邀出席了日本电子显微镜学会举行的送别酒会。
