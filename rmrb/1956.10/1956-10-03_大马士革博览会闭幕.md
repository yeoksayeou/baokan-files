Title: Damascus Fair Closes

Author:

Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 2nd: TASS, Damascus: The Third Damascus International Fair closed on September 30th. According to preliminary statistics, 1.5 million people visited the fair. Approximately 5,000 companies and various industrial and commercial groups from 31 countries participated in the fair.

    The Soviet Pavilion was very popular. Visitors to this pavilion were very enthusiastic, and many people wrote words of praise in the guestbook.

    The exhibition halls of the People's Republic of China, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and Arab countries were all very popular.

### 大马士革博览会闭幕

1956-10-03
第5版()
专栏：

    大马士革博览会闭幕
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社大马士革讯：大马士革第三次国际博览会在9月30日闭幕。据初步统计，有一百五十万人参观了博览会。参加博览会的有三十一个国家的约五千个公司和各种工商团体。
    苏联馆很受欢迎。参观这个馆的观众非常踊跃，许多人在意见簿上写下了称赞的词句。
    中华人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚和阿拉伯国家的展览馆都很受欢迎。
