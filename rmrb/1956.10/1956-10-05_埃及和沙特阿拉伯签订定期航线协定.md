Title: Egypt and Saudi Arabia Sign Regular Air Route Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 6

    Egypt and Saudi Arabia Sign Regular Air Route Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd, TASS Cairo, October 1st: According to newspaper reports, Egypt and Saudi Arabia signed an agreement in Cairo on September 30th regarding the establishment of regular air routes.

### 埃及和沙特阿拉伯签订定期航线协定

1956-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及和沙特阿拉伯签订定期航线协定
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社开罗1日讯：据报纸消息，埃及和沙特阿拉伯9月30日在开罗签订了关于建立定期航空线的协定。
