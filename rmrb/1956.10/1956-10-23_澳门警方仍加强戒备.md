Title: Macao Police Still on High Alert
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 22nd—Macao News: Macao is still under a state of alert today.

　　Macao authorities' police dispatched patrol cars to patrol some main streets last night.

　　Sources say that in recent nights, groups of suspicious individuals have been seen gathering in Macao on several occasions. A small number of residents are planning to move their families to Hong Kong.

　　People from all walks of life in Macao are raising their vigilance, demanding that the Portuguese authorities in Macao earnestly take responsibility for protecting the safety of people's lives and property, and prevent the Kowloon bloodshed from being repeated in Macao.

### 澳门警方仍加强戒备

1956-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    澳门警方仍加强戒备
    新华社广州22日电　澳门消息：澳门今天还处在戒备状态中。
    澳门当局警方昨晚出动警车在部分主要街道巡逻。
    有消息说，澳门近日夜间数次发现有成批可疑人物结集。有少数居民计划将家眷搬往香港。
    澳门各界人士都提高警惕，要求澳门葡萄牙当局切实负起保障人民生命财产安全的责任，不使九龙流血事件在澳门重演。
