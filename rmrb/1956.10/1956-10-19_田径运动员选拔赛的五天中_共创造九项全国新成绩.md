Title: Nine New National Records Created in Five Days of Track and Field Athlete Selection Trials
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The track and field athlete selection trials for the Chinese Olympic team concluded today with the final eleven events. The track and field athlete selection trials, which began on October 14th, lasted five days, during which nine male and female athletes created nine new national track and field records.

The nine athletes are:

Men: Yu Jinan in javelin (68.78 meters), Cai Yishu in pole vault (4.15 meters), Bi Hongfu in hammer throw (48.74 meters), Fu Shenghai in the 5,000-meter race (15 minutes 13.8 seconds), Tian Xiudong in the 3,000-meter steeplechase (9 minutes 53.6 seconds), and Hao Jianren in the decathlon (5,135 points).

Women: Zhu Yuanyu in the 800-meter race (2 minutes 19.5 seconds), Wang Jianxia in the pentathlon (3,710 points), and Tang Rongqing in grenade throwing (48.13 meters, a non-Olympic event).

Due to strong winds and cold weather, today's competition results were affected. In the men's 110-meter hurdles final, Beijing athlete Zhou Lianli achieved a time of 14.8 seconds, surpassing his national record of 14.9 seconds set in August of this year. However, because the wind speed (tailwind) exceeded the prescribed standard of two meters per second, the record could not be recognized.

### 田径运动员选拔赛的五天中  共创造九项全国新成绩

1956-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    田径运动员选拔赛的五天中
    共创造九项全国新成绩
    新华社18日讯　中国奥林匹克运动队田径运动员选拔赛今天进行了最后的十一项决赛。至此，田径运动员选拔赛已经全部结束。
    这次田径运动员选拔赛从10月14日开始，共举行了五天，有九名男、女运动员创造了九项全国田径比赛新成绩。
    这九名运动员是：
    男子：于金安的标枪掷远（六十八公尺七十八公分）、蔡艺墅的撑竿跳高（四公尺十五公分）、毕鸿福的链球掷远（四十八公尺七十四公分）、傅生海的五千公尺赛跑（十五分十三秒八）、田秀东的三千公尺障碍赛跑（九分五十三秒六）、郝健仁的十项全能运动（五千一百三十五分）。
    女子：朱元昱的八百公尺赛跑（二分十九秒五）、王剑侠的五项全能运动（三千七百一十分）、唐荣清的手榴弹掷远（四十八公尺十三公分，非奥林匹克项目）。
    由于风很大，天气也比较冷，今天的各项比赛成绩都受到影响。在男子一百一十公尺高栏决赛中，北京的运动员周连立的成绩达到了十四秒八，超过了他今年8月间创造的十四秒九的全国最高成绩，但是，因为赛跑的时候风速（顺风）超过了每秒两公尺的规定标准，所以这个纪录不能被承认。
