Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th. The delegation of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam arrived in Moscow by plane on the 17th at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The head of the delegation is Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly.
    Those who greeted the guests at the airport included Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Lacis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    Shortly after the delegation arrived, the chairmen of the two houses of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union received Ton Duc Thang and all the members of the delegation in the Kremlin and had cordial talks with them.

### 越南国民大会代表团访苏

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会代表团访苏
    新华社莫斯科18日电　越南民主共和国国民大会代表团应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请在17日乘飞机到达莫斯科。代表团团长是国民大会常务委员会主席孙德胜。
    到机场欢迎客人的有苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫、苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯等。
    在代表团到达后不久，苏联最高苏维埃两院主席在克里姆林宫接待了孙德胜和全体团员，并且和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
