Title: "Wenhui Bao" Resumes Publication in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: "Wenhui Bao," which is popular among intellectuals and young students, resumed publication in Shanghai on October 1st. The October 1st issue of "Wenhui Bao" featured supplements and columns familiar to readers, such as "Bi Hui" (Pen Meeting), "Social University," and "News Window." It also published congratulatory poems and messages from Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Ma Xulun, and others.

### “文汇报”在上海复刊

1956-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    “文汇报”在上海复刊
    据新华社讯　受到知识分子和青年学生欢迎的“文汇报”10月1日起在上海复刊。一日的文汇报上，出现了读者熟悉的“笔会”、“社会大学”、“新闻窗”等副刊和专栏。还登载了郭沫若、陈叔通、马叙伦等人的贺诗、贺词。
