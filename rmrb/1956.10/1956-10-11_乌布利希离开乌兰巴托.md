Title: Ulbricht Departs from Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 10th - After a two-day visit to the Mongolian capital, Ulaanbaatar, Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Politburo member, Hilde Benjamin, departed by plane today.

Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Zhamsrangiin Sambuu, and Sükhbaataryn Yanjmaa, were at the airport to see them off.

During their visit to Mongolia, Ulbricht and others laid wreaths at the mausoleums of Sükhbaatar and Khorloogiin Choibalsan.

Ulbricht also presented the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, on behalf of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, with a bust of Ernst Thälmann and an album documenting the revolutionary struggles of the German people.

### 乌布利希离开乌兰巴托

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    乌布利希离开乌兰巴托
    据新华社乌兰巴托10日电　德国统一社会党第一书记乌布利希、政治局委员希德万等，在蒙古首都访问了两天后，已经在今天乘飞机离开乌兰巴托。
    蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、锡林迪布等到机场欢送。
    乌布利希等在访问蒙古期间曾经向苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山陵墓献花圈。
    乌布利希还代表德国统一社会党向蒙古人民革命党赠送了台尔曼的半身铜像和纪录德国人民革命斗争的像册。
