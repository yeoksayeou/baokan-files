Title: Ukrainian National Dance Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The Soviet Ukrainian National Dance Troupe left Beijing this morning to return home.
    Among those at the station to see them off were Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and dozens of people from the Beijing art circles.
    Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others also came to the station to see them off.

### 乌克兰国家舞蹈团回国

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    乌克兰国家舞蹈团回国
    据新华社25日讯　苏联乌克兰国家舞蹈团今日上午离北京回国。
    到车站欢送的有文化部副部长夏衍、中苏友好协会总会理事会副秘书长戈宝权和数十位北京艺术界人士。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞李海秋等人也到车站送行。
