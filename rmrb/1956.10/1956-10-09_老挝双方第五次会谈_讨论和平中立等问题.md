Title: Lao Sides Hold Fifth Meeting, Discussing Issues Such as Peace and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th - According to a Vientiane Radio broadcast on the 7th, the political delegation of the Kingdom of Laos and the political delegation of the Lao Fighting Units held their fifth meeting at the Prime Minister's Office on the afternoon of the 5th. At this meeting, issues such as peace and neutrality, the democratic and free rights of the people, the civil rights of the Lao side, supplementary general elections, a coalition government, and the regimes in Phong Saly and Sam Neua provinces were discussed.

The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of intimacy and mutual understanding.

### 老挝双方第五次会谈  讨论和平中立等问题

1956-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　老挝双方第五次会谈
    讨论和平中立等问题
    新华社河内8日电　据万象电台7日广播：老挝王国政治代表团和寮国战斗部队政治代表团5日下午在首相府举行第五次会议。在这次会议上，讨论了和平中立、人民的民主自由权利、寮国方面的公民权利、补充普选、联合政府、丰沙里和桑怒两省的政权等问题。
    会议是在亲密和互相了解的气氛中进行的。
