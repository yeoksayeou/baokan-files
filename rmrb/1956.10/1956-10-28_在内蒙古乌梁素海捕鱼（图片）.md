Title: Fishing in W梁素海, Inner Mongolia (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 3

    Fishing in W梁素海, Inner Mongolia
    W梁素海 in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is a natural lake with an area of 1.2 million mu, mainly producing carp, catfish, and whitebait. There are already eleven fishing teams organized locally, and in the late autumn season, they can catch more than 70,000 jin of fish every day.
    The delicious carp is about to be transported to Beijing, Baotou and other regions.
    Fishing boats of advanced fishery production cooperatives are fishing.
                                     Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ruxun photo

### 在内蒙古乌梁素海捕鱼（图片）

1956-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    在内蒙古乌梁素海捕鱼
    内蒙古自治区的乌梁素海是一个天然湖泊，面积有一百二十万亩，主要产鲤鱼、鲇鱼、白条鱼等。当地已有十一个渔业队组织起来，在深秋季节里，每天可以捕鱼七万多斤。
    鲜美的鲤鱼，即将运往北京、包头等地区。
    先进渔业生产合作社的鱼船在捕鱼。
                                     新华社记者　袁汝逊摄
