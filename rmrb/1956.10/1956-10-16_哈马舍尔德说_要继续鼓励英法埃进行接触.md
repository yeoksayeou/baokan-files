Title: Hammarskjöld Says Continued Contact Between Britain, France and Egypt Should Be Encouraged
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 6

    Hammarskjöld Says
    Continued Contact Between Britain, France and Egypt Should Be Encouraged
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 15th - United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld issued a statement to the press on the 14th, saying that he will continue to encourage the governments of Britain, France, and Egypt to make contact to find a solution to the Suez problem.
    Reports say that Hammarskjöld discussed the general conditions for negotiations with the foreign ministers of Britain, France, and Egypt on the 14th. He will later submit his working plan for negotiations to these countries.

### 哈马舍尔德说  要继续鼓励英法埃进行接触

1956-10-16
第6版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德说
    要继续鼓励英法埃进行接触
    据新华社15日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德14日向报界发表一项声明说，他将继续鼓励英、法和埃及政府进行接触来找出一个解决苏伊士问题的办法。
    有消息说，哈马舍尔德14日曾经同英、法和埃及外交部长讨论过谈判的一般条件。他以后将向这几个国家提出他对谈判的工作计划。
