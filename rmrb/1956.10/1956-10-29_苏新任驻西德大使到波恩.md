Title: New Soviet Ambassador to West Germany Arrives in Bonn
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, October 27th. The new Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Federal Republic of Germany (West Germany), Mr. Smirnov, arrived in Bonn on October 26th.

　　In a statement at the airport, Smirnov emphasized that the development of relations between the Soviet Union and West Germany is not only in the interests of the people of both countries, but also helps to maintain peace in Europe.

### 苏新任驻西德大使到波恩

1956-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    苏新任驻西德大使到波恩
    新华社波恩27日电　苏联新任驻德意志联邦共和国（西德）特命全权大使斯米尔诺夫10月26日到达波恩。
    斯米尔诺夫在飞机场发表谈话的时候强调指出，发展苏联和西德两国之间的关系不但符合两国人民的利益，而且有助于维护欧洲和平。
