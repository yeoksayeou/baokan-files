Title: Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China, Mass Organizations from Many Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, the Chinese People's Committee for World peace, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the All-China Democratic Women's federation, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters have continued to receive telegrams from mass organizations and prominent figures in many countries congratulating our country on its National Day.

    Chairman Mao Zedong received telegrams from the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, the Mexico-China Friendship Association, and Lebanese Archbishop Zahle Niemeh congratulating our country on National Day.
    Premier Zhou Enlai received National Day congratulatory telegrams jointly sent by the African National Congress, the South Africa Indian Congress, and the South African Colored People's Organisation.
    The congratulatory telegram signed by these three organizations said: "The continuous progress of the Chinese people under the current system is the most reliable guarantee of peace."
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace received National Day congratulatory telegrams from the Japan Peace Committee, the Tokyo Peace Association, and Walter Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Council. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries received National Day congratulatory telegrams from Pei Ji, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and the Albanian Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the France-China Friendship Association, and the Chile-China Friendship Association. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation received National Day congratulatory telegrams from the Polish Women's League and the Hungarian National Council of Women. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters received congratulatory telegrams celebrating our National Day from the Central Council of the Soviet-Polish Friendship Association, Academician Barhoon, Chairman of the Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association, K. Litvin, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Ukrainian Society for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Council of the Uzbek Writers' Association of the Soviet Union, and the Council of the Uzbek Society for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国国庆  许多国家群众团体来电祝贺

1956-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国国庆
    许多国家群众团体来电祝贺
    新华社4日讯　毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会总会继续收到许多国家的群众团体和著名人士祝贺我国国庆的电报。
    毛泽东主席收到了日中友好协会、锡中友好协会、墨中友好协会和黎巴嫩大主教扎赫勒·尼末祝贺我国国庆节的电报。
    周恩来总理收到了非洲人国民大会、南非印度人大会、南非有色人组织联合发来的国庆贺电。
    这一份由三个团体署名的贺电中说：“中国人民在现在制度下的不断进步，是对和平最可靠的保证。”
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会收到了日本和平委员会、日本东京都和平会和德国和平理事会主席华尔特·腓特列祝贺我国庆节的贺电。中国人民对外文化协会收到了越中友好协会会长裴纪和阿尔巴尼亚对外文化协会、法中友好协会、智中友好协会的国庆节贺电。中华全国民主妇女联合会收到了波兰妇女联盟、匈牙利妇女全国委员会的国庆贺电。中苏友好协会总会收到了波苏友好协会中央理事会、罗苏友好协会主席巴尔洪院士、苏联乌克兰对外文化协会理事会主席康·李特文、苏联乌兹别克作家协会理事会和乌兹别克对外文化协会理事会发来的祝贺我国庆节的贺电。
