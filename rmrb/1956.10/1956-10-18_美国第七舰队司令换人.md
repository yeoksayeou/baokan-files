Title: US Seventh Fleet Commander Replaced
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: U.S. President Eisenhower appointed Wallis Moore Beakley as the commander of the U.S. Seventh Fleet on the 13th. The current commander of the Seventh Fleet is Ingersoll, who is concurrently the commander of the U.S. military's "Defense" of Taiwan. Both the Seventh Fleet and the so-called Taiwan Defense Command are carrying out the task of armed obstruction of the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan.

### 美国第七舰队司令换人

1956-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

    美国第七舰队司令换人
    据新华社15日讯　美国总统艾森豪威尔13日任命沃利斯·莫里斯·比克利为美国第七舰队司令。目前第七舰队司令是由美军“协防”台湾司令英格索尔兼任的。第七舰队和所谓协防台湾司令部都在执行武装阻挠中国人民解放台湾的任务。
