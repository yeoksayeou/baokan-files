Title: A Group of Literary, Artistic, and Scientific Figures from Hungary and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th.  Reilincz György, President of the Hungarian National Ballet Institute; Birlo Sándor, Hungarian ballet expert; Bulgarian vocalist Djakovicz; Professor Gossling, band conductor from the German Democratic Republic; Hofte, choir conductor; Christelmann, clarinet expert; and Hoffmann, horn expert; and the Egyptian archaeologist, Emil, recently arrived in Beijing for visits and lectures in China under the cultural agreement.

Dr. Vaidya, an Indian medical scientist who was invited to visit China, also recently arrived in Beijing.

### 匈牙利等国一批文艺、科学界人士到京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利等国一批文艺、科学界人士到京
    新华社30日讯　匈牙利国家芭蕾舞学院院长赖林茨·久尔基，匈牙利芭蕾舞专家比尔洛斯·山道尔，保加利亚声乐家迪雅克维茨，德意志民主共和国的乐队指挥戈斯林教授、合唱指挥霍夫特、黑管专家克利斯特曼、圆号专家霍夫曼，埃及考古学家埃米尔，最近先后到达北京，他们是根据文化协定来中国访问和讲学的。
    应邀来中国访问的印度医学家维德医师也在最近来到北京。
