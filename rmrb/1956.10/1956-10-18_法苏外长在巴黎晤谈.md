Title: French and Soviet Foreign Ministers Meet in Paris
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th: According to TASS, Soviet Ambassador to France, Vinogradov, hosted a banquet in Paris on the 16th for the Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov, who is currently in Paris. French Foreign Minister Pineau and others attended the banquet.

　　After the banquet, talks were held, during which some issues in Franco-Soviet relations were mentioned.

### 法苏外长在巴黎晤谈

1956-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

    法苏外长在巴黎晤谈
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社巴黎16日讯：苏联驻法国大使维诺格拉多夫在16日设宴招待目前在巴黎的苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫。法国外交部长比诺等出席了宴会。
    宴会后进行了谈话，谈话中间提到法苏关系中的一些问题。
