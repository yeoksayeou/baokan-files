Title: Chairman Liu Visits Japanese Commodity Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Shaoqi, visited the Japanese Commodity Exhibition this afternoon, accompanied by the head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Delegation, Eiichi Yadoya, and the deputy head, Takashi Kataoka.

### 刘委员长参观日本商品展览会

1956-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    刘委员长参观日本商品展览会
    新华社17日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，今天下午在日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一、副团长片冈峻陪同下，参观了日本商品展览会。
