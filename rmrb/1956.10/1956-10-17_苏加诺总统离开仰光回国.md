Title: President Sukarno Leaves Rangoon to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 16th: Indonesian President Sukarno and his entourage left Rangoon at 7:30 this morning on two special planes from Indonesia to return home.

    Burmese Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister U Kyaw Nyein and other government ministers went to the airport to see them off.

    Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen, and staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Burma were all at the airport to see them off. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma were also at the airport to see them off.

### 苏加诺总统离开仰光回国

1956-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统离开仰光回国
    新华社仰光16日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和随行人员今天上午七时三十分分乘两架来自印度尼西亚的专用飞机离开仰光回国。
    缅甸副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓和其他政府部长多人到机场上欢送。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和中国驻缅甸大使馆的工作人员都在机场上欢送。在机场上送行的还有各国驻缅甸的外交使节。
