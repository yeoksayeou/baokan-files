Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Departs for Visit to China; Our Embassy in Pakistan Holds Reception to See Off Distinguished Guest
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 16th – Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy, at the invitation of the Chinese government, left here this morning by plane for China with his entourage.
    Among those seeing him off at the airport were senior Pakistani government officials and political leaders, as well as Chen Ying, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 16th – Chen Ying, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, held a grand reception on October 15th on the eve of Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage's departure for China, to bid farewell to the distinguished Chinese guests.
    That evening, the new building of the Chinese Embassy, located in the central district of Karachi, was specially decorated and brightly lit to welcome the first Pakistani Prime Minister to visit China.
    Prime Minister Suhrawardy arrived at the embassy in high spirits, warmly shaking hands with the host and hostess. He told Chargé d'affaires Chen Ying that he hoped to visit China to "study and learn some things." He also said, "China has been successful in transforming its huge agricultural economy. I hope to learn something from your experience, especially in agriculture."
    The vast green lawn, illuminated by colorful lights, was filled with guests, including Pakistani government officials, military officers, members of the National Assembly, prominent figures from all walks of life, and foreign diplomats. The theme of the guests' enthusiastic conversations in a friendly atmosphere was Prime Minister Suhrawardy's visit to China. Guests from all walks of life had their own pleasant expectations for this visit. Business people expect that trade between China and Pakistan will further develop. People in cultural circles expect more cultural exchanges between the two countries. The common feeling was that Prime Minister Suhrawardy was heading to China with the goodwill, friendship, and expectations of the entire Pakistani people.

### 巴基斯坦总理启程来我国访问  我驻巴大使馆举行招待会为贵宾饯行

1956-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理启程来我国访问
    我驻巴大使馆举行招待会为贵宾饯行
    新华社喀喇蚩16日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底应中国政府的邀请，在今天早晨和他的随行人员乘飞机离开这里前往中国。
    到机场送行的有巴基斯坦政府的高级官员和政界领袖，以及中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办陈应等。
    新华社喀喇蚩16日电　中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办陈应10月15日在苏拉瓦底总理和他的随行人员启程前往中国的前夕，举行了盛大的招待会为中国的贵宾们饯行。
    这天晚上，位于喀喇蚩市中心区的中国大使馆的新建筑物，为了欢迎这位访问中国的第一个巴基斯坦总理，特别装饰一新，灯火辉煌。
    苏拉瓦底总理十分愉快地到达大使馆，同主人和女主人热烈地握手。他告诉陈应代办说，他希望访问中国，以“研究和学习一些东西”。他还说：“中国在改造它的巨大的农业经济方面是成功的。我希望从你们的经验中、特别是农业方面学习一些东西。”
    在多彩的灯光照耀下的广阔的绿色草地上，挤满了客人，其中有巴基斯坦政府官员、军官、国民议会议员、各界著名人士以及各国外交使节。客人们在友好气氛中热烈交谈的主题是苏拉瓦底总理的访问中国。来自各界的客人们对这次访问都怀着自己的愉快的期待。商界人士期望巴中两国的贸易将进一步发展。文化界人士期望两国之间进行更多的文化接触。大家共同的感觉是：苏拉瓦底总理是带着整个巴基斯坦人民的亲善、友谊和期待前往中国的。
