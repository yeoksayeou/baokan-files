Title: Prime Minister Acharya Ends Visit to Our Country; Guangdong Governor Tao Zhu Holds Grand Banquet to Bid Farewell to Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 11th. Nepal's Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya and his wife, along with accompanying senior Nepalese officials, left Hangzhou this morning by special plane for Guangzhou.

　　Prime Minister Acharya and his wife received flowers from Young Pioneers at the airport and then shook hands with Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu, head of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations who came to see them off.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 11th. Nepalese Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya and his wife, accompanying senior Nepalese officials, and Badra Kali Mishra, Chairman of the Nepalese People's Party, who is also visiting China, attended a grand farewell luncheon held for them today by Guangdong Governor Tao Zhu. The distinguished guests arrived in Guangzhou by special plane at 11:15 this morning.

　　At the beginning of the banquet, Governor Tao Zhu said in his speech: "Your Excellency the Prime Minister's visit to our country this time, and the agreements signed between China and Nepal before Your Excellency the Prime Minister's visit, undoubtedly promote mutual understanding, trust and cooperation between the peoples of China and Nepal. Nepal has always been a neighbor with friendly relations with China for more than a thousand years. This friendly relationship has been further developed, which is not only conducive to the prosperity of the two countries, but also helps to maintain peace in Asia and the world."

　　Then, Prime Minister Acharya spoke, saying: Almost two weeks ago, I arrived in your great country from Guangzhou. Today, we are passing through this place again to return to our country. During this visit, we have seen the people of the neighboring country that shares a border with us, and we have gained a deep impression of the merits of the Chinese people.

　　Prime Minister Acharya also said that the hospitality of the Chinese people is world-renowned, and we are returning home after fully enjoying this hospitality. I sincerely thank the Chinese people and the Chinese government for the friendship and cooperation they have shown us.

　　The hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to the friendship between China and Nepal and to lasting peace in Asia and the world.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 11th. Nepal's Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya and his wife, along with accompanying senior Nepalese officials, concluded their visit to China and left Guangzhou by special train for Shenzhen at 1:56 pm today. They will visit Japan before returning home. Badra Kali Mishra, Chairman of the Nepalese People's Party, also accompanied them.

　　Prime Minister Acharya said to those who came to see him off at Guangzhou Railway Station: Goodbye! I hope we can meet again. We always wish you well. Asians are indeed deeply satisfied to see an Asian country that was once backward develop. We sincerely hope for world peace so that you can continue to progress.

　　The Prime Minister continued, "When I came to your country, I brought the best wishes of tens of millions of Nepalese people; when I return, I will bring the friendship of 600 million Chinese people to my people. I think this is the greatest achievement of my visit to China."

　　The distinguished guests arrived at Guangzhou Railway Station at 1:30 pm, where the national flags of Nepal and China were flying. About 3,000 Guangzhou citizens gathered here. They came to bid farewell to the distinguished Nepalese guests with flowers, slogans, and red flags.

　　The band played the national anthems of Nepal and China. Prime Minister Acharya, accompanied by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, head of the Guangzhou Garrison, reviewed the guard of honor. Then the distinguished guests walked to the other responsible persons of the Guangdong and Guangzhou municipal party and government organs, representatives from all walks of life, and the consuls of the Soviet Union and Poland stationed in Guangzhou, and shook hands with them to say goodbye. At this time, thunderous applause erupted from the crowd, and children came out of the crowd to present flowers to the Prime Minister and other distinguished guests. Later, the Prime Minister, holding the flowers, walked to the loudspeaker to say goodbye to the send-off party.

　　His words, filled with friendship for the Chinese people, once again aroused enthusiastic applause and cheers from the send-off crowd.

　　The Prime Minister walked enthusiastically to the send-off crowd who were applauding and waving bouquets to him and shook hands with them. The Prime Minister loudly told the send-off party that our two countries have a common border, so we are like one nation.

　　Amid applause, cheers, and the song "People of the World Are of One Heart," the Prime Minister smiled and walked through the crowd of well-wishers lining the street into the carriage.

　　The train started to move to the sound of farewell music, and the Prime Minister waved goodbye again at the door of the carriage.

　　Pan Zili, China's Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, as well as Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the distinguished Nepalese guests to Shenzhen.

### 阿查里雅首相结束在我国的访问  广东省省长陶铸曾举行盛大宴会欢送贵宾

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相结束在我国的访问
    广东省省长陶铸曾举行盛大宴会欢送贵宾
    新华社杭州11日电　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔高级官员，今天早上乘专机离杭州前往广州。
    阿查里雅首相和夫人在机场上接受了少先队员的献花，然后和前来欢送的浙江省省长沙文汉、杭州市市长吴宪、中国人民解放军浙江军区负责人周贯五中将和各民主党派各人民团体负责人一一握手告别。
    新华社广州11日电　尼泊尔首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人、随行的尼泊尔高级官员，以及同时访华的尼泊尔人民党主席巴德腊卡里·米斯腊，今天出席了广东省省长陶铸为他们举行的盛大欢送午宴。贵宾们是在今天上午十一时十五分乘专机到达广州的。
    宴会开始时，陶铸省长致词说：“首相阁下这次到我国访问，和在首相阁下访问我国前所签订的中尼两国间的协定，无疑地促进了中尼两国人民之间的互相了解、信任和合作，尼泊尔本来是同中国具有一千多年友好关系的邻邦，这种友好关系已得到了进一步的发展，这样不仅有利于两国的繁荣，而且有助于维护亚洲和世界的和平。”
    接着，阿查里雅首相讲话，他说：差不多两个礼拜以前，我到广州进入了你们伟大的国家，今天我们又经过这个地方回到我们的国家。在这次访问中，我们看到了和我们共一边境的邻国人民，我们对中国人民的优点得到了深刻的印象。
    阿查里雅首相还说，中国人民的好客是世界闻名的，我们是充分的享受了这种款待以后回国的。我衷心的感谢中国人民和中国政府对我们表示的友谊和合作。
    宾主间一再举杯为中尼两国的友谊，为亚洲和世界的持久和平干杯。
    新华社广州11日电　尼泊尔首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔高级官员，结束了在中国的访问，今天午后一时五十六分从广州乘专车前往深圳。他们将赴日本访问后回国。同行的还有尼泊尔人民党主席巴德腊卡里·米斯腊。
    阿查里雅首相在广州车站上对送行的人说：再见吧！希望我们能再次见面。我们永远希望你们好。亚洲人看到一个从前落后的亚洲国家发展起来，确实是深感满意的。我们衷心希望世界和平，让你们能继续不断地进步。
    首相接着说，当我到贵国时，我带着千万尼泊尔人民的最好的愿望；我回去时，我又带着六亿中国人民的友谊给我国人民，我认为这是我这次访华的最大成就。
    贵宾们是在一时三十分来到飘扬着尼泊尔、中国两国国旗的广州车站的。这里聚集着大约三千多广州市民。他们拿着鲜花、标语和红旗前来送别尼泊尔的贵宾们。
    乐队奏起尼泊尔、中国两国的国歌，阿查里雅首相由广东省省长陶铸、广州市市长朱光和广州驻军首长文年生中将陪同检阅了仪仗队。然后贵宾们走到广东省、广州市党政机关其他负责人、各界代表和苏联、波兰驻广州的领事人员面前，和他们一一握手告别。这时候，群众中发出如雷的掌声，儿童们从人群中走出来把鲜花献给首相和其他的贵宾们。后来，首相抱着鲜花走到扩音器前，向送行者话别。
    他的充满着对中国人民友谊的话，再次引起了欢送群众热烈的掌声和欢呼声。
    首相兴致勃勃地走到向他鼓掌和挥舞着花束的欢送人群前面，同他们握手。首相大声地对欢送者说，我们两国有共同的边界，因此我们就像一个民族一样。
    在掌声、欢呼声和“全世界人民心一条”的歌声中，首相微笑着穿过夹道欢送的人群，走进车厢。
    列车在欢送乐曲声中开动了，首相走到车厢门口再次招手告别。
    陪同尼泊尔贵宾们到深圳的，有中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人以及外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
