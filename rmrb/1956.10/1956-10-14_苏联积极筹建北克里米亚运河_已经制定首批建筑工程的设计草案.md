Title: The Soviet Union Actively Prepares for the Construction of the North Crimean Canal; The First Batch of Architectural Design Drafts Has Been Formulated
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th dispatch: TASS reports that the first design drafts for the construction of the North Crimean Canal, a major Soviet water conservancy project, have recently been completed. Experts have begun to prepare working drawings, and geological and topographical surveying teams have set out for the canal route.

This canal, with a total length of 425 kilometers, is scheduled to be built during the Soviet Union's Sixth Five-Year Plan (1956-1960). It will include four pumping stations, six reservoirs, and hundreds of other construction projects. Its route will first run along the Krasnoznamenka Canal near Kakhovka to Kalanchak in Kherson Oblast, then through the Perekop Isthmus to the Crimean Peninsula, traversing six districts, and finally reaching the Kerch Reservoir.

Water from the Kakhovka Reservoir will flow into the North Crimean Canal at a rate of 40 cubic meters per second through the Krasnoznamenka Canal. Currently, the main project for drawing water from the Kakhovka Reservoir has been completed, and the corresponding main project for the Krasnoznamenka Canal has also begun construction.

The design draft stipulates that during the construction of this canal, special attention will be paid to using local materials.

### 苏联积极筹建北克里米亚运河  已经制定首批建筑工程的设计草案

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联积极筹建北克里米亚运河
    已经制定首批建筑工程的设计草案
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联一个大水利工程——北克里米亚运河建筑工程的第一批设计草案最近已经制定完毕。专家们已经开始着手拟制工作图案，地质和地形勘探队已经出发到运河线上。
    这条运河全长四百二十五公里，是苏联第六个五年计划（1956—1960年）规定在这个时期内建筑的。它将包括四个抽水站、六个水库和其他数百个建筑工程。它的路线将首先沿着卡霍夫卡附近红旗区运河到达赫尔松州的卡兰恰克，然后经过皮列科普地峡通向克里米亚半岛，横贯六个区，最终到达刻赤水库。
    卡霍夫卡水库的水将以每秒钟四十立方公尺的水量通过红旗区运河，流入北克里米亚运河。目前，从卡霍夫卡水库取水的主要工程已经完成，红旗区运河相应的主要工程也已经开始建筑。
    设计草案规定，这条运河在建筑期间，将特别注意就地取材。
