Title: Mollet and Pineau Go to London to Confer with the British Government on the Middle East Situation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Paris dispatch: French Foreign Minister Pineau and Prime Minister Mollet left Paris by plane today one after the other for London. They will hold talks with British government leaders on the latest developments in the Middle East situation.

    The French National Assembly and the Council of the Republic decided to hold meetings separately tonight to listen to Mollet's statement on the joint British and French policy in the Middle East.

    Mendès-France, Vice-Chairman of the Radical Socialist Party, issued a special statement to the press, requesting the French government to convene a four-power conference of France, Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union to discuss the Middle East situation.

### 摩勒和比诺去伦敦同英国政府会商中东局势

1956-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    摩勒和比诺去伦敦同英国政府会商中东局势
    据新华社30日讯　巴黎消息：法国外交部长比诺和总理摩勒今天先后乘飞机离巴黎到伦敦，他们将同英国政府领袖就中东局势最近的发展举行会谈。
    法国国民议会和共和国参议院决定在今晚分别举行会议，听取摩勒关于英法在中东的共同政策的声明。
    激进社会党副主席孟戴斯—弗朗斯向报界发表特别声明，要求法国政府召开法、英、美、苏四大国会议讨论中东局势。
