Title: Kabul Mayor Dr. Sohel of Afghanistan Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 21st: Dr. Sohel, Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, who visited China at the invitation of Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, left Guangzhou by train this morning to return to his country.
    Dr. Sohel visited Huanghuagang, Yuexiu Mountain People's Park, and toured the city of Guangzhou.

### 阿富汗喀布尔市市长索海尔博士回国

1956-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗喀布尔市市长索海尔博士回国
    新华社广州21日电　应北京市市长彭真邀请来中国访问的阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士，今天早晨乘火车离开广州回国。
    索海尔博士在广州游览了黄花岗、越秀山人民公园，并参观了广州市容。
