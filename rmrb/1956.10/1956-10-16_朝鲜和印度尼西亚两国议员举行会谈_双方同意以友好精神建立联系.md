Title: DPRK and Indonesian Parliamentarians Hold Talks; Agree to Establish Ties in a Friendly Spirit
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th. Indonesian parliamentarians Soepardi and Ibnu Parna, who were invited to visit Korea, signed a joint communique with members of the DPRK Supreme People's Assembly on the 14th in Pyongyang. The communique stated that both sides believe that the issue of Korean reunification must be resolved through peaceful means, and that the issue of the Republic of Indonesia's recovery of West Irian must be resolved in accordance with the spirit of the Bandung Conference. Both sides believe that mutual support and cooperation to achieve the above goals is very beneficial to the people of both countries.

The communique stated that the establishment of mutual relations between the two countries in a spirit of understanding and friendship, and the joint struggle against colonialism, are in line with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and will help maintain and consolidate world peace and security.

The communique stated that the establishment of normal trade relations between the two countries is an urgent issue, and both sides will make efforts to hold meetings between trade representatives of the two countries.

The communique pointed out that the visit of Indonesian parliamentarians to Korea played an important role in strengthening and developing friendly relations between the two countries. In the future, both sides will strive to expand exchanges and contacts between the parliaments of the two countries and to develop cultural exchanges between the two countries. Representing the Korean side, Choi Won Taek, member of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Kwon Chyoo Chel, member of the Supreme People's Assembly, signed the communique.

### 朝鲜和印度尼西亚两国议员举行会谈  双方同意以友好精神建立联系

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和印度尼西亚两国议员举行会谈
    双方同意以友好精神建立联系
    新华社平壤15日电　应邀到朝鲜访问的印度尼西亚国会议员苏帕迪和伊卜努·帕尔纳同朝鲜最高人民会议议员14日在平壤签订了两国议员会谈的联合公报。公报说，双方认为，朝鲜统一问题必须以和平的方法来解决；印度尼西亚共和国收复西伊里安的问题必须遵照万隆会议的精神来解决。双方相信，为了达到上述目标而互相支持和合作是对两国人民非常有利的。
    公报说，两国之间本着谅解和友好的精神建立互相的关系，为反对殖民主义而共同进行斗争，是符合和平共处五项原则的，并且将有助于维护和巩固世界的和平和安全。
    公报说，两国建立正常贸易关系是个紧迫的问题，双方要进行努力使两国贸易代表举行会议。
    公报指出，印度尼西亚国会议员这次访问朝鲜，对加强和发展两国友好关系起了重要的作用。今后双方要为扩大两国国会间的往来和接触，为发展两国文化交流而努力。代表朝鲜方面在公报上签字的是朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员崔元泽，最高人民会议议员权昼哲。
