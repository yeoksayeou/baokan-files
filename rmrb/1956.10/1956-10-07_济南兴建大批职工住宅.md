Title: Jinan Builds Large Numbers of Employee Residences
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 2
Jinan is Building a Large Number of Employee Residences

    (Our newspaper report) In order to solve the housing difficulties of workers and staff, factories, enterprises, institutions, and schools in Jinan City have decided to build over 250,000 square meters of employee dormitories this year. The building area of these dormitories after completion will be more than five times the amount of employee dormitories built last year, exceeding the construction volume of any previous year.

    Currently, the dormitory area completed and under construction has reached more than 150,000 square meters. After the completion of the 7,200-plus square meters of employee dormitories in Jinan No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, it can solve the housing difficulties of 60% of the employees. Jinan Railway Bureau's newly built employee dormitories cover more than 15,000 square meters, which can provide housing for more than 30% of the employees with housing difficulties.

### 济南兴建大批职工住宅

1956-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

    济南兴建大批职工住宅
    本报讯　济南市各工厂企业和机关、学校等单位，为了解决职工住宅困难问题，决定今年兴建二十五万多平方公尺的职工宿舍。这些宿舍建成后，建筑面积相当于去年所建职工宿舍的五倍多，超过过去任何一年的建筑量。
    目前已经建成和正在建筑的宿舍面积达十五万多平方公尺。济南第一机床厂七千二百多平方公尺的职工宿舍建成后，可以解决60％职工的宿舍困难问题。济南铁路管理局新建职工宿舍一万五千多平方公尺，可使30％以上居住有困难的职工有房屋住。
