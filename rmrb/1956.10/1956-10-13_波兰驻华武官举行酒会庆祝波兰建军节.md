Title: Polish Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Polish Army Day
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Polish Military Attaché to China, Colonel Wanczeshi, held a reception tonight to celebrate Polish Army Day.

    Among those attending the reception were General Chen Geng, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as Senior Generals Li Da, Li Kenong, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and representatives from various military branches and arms of the service.

    Military attachés from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend today's reception.

    All members of the visiting Indian Military Friendship Delegation, currently on a tour in Beijing, were also invited to attend the reception.

    At the reception, Colonel Wanczeshi and General Chen Geng delivered speeches respectively. In their speeches, they both emphasized the tremendous role of the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Polish armies in safeguarding world peace.

### 波兰驻华武官举行酒会庆祝波兰建军节

1956-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰驻华武官举行酒会庆祝波兰建军节
    新华社12日讯　波兰驻华武官万采施上校今天晚上为庆祝波兰建军节举行酒会。
    出席酒会的，有中国人民解放军代总参谋长陈赓大将和李达上将、李克农上将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、萧向荣中将等高级将领以及各军种、兵种部队的代表。
    各国驻华武官也应邀出席了今天的酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的，还有正在北京参观访问的印度军事友好访华代表团全体人员。
    酒会上，万采施上校和陈赓大将先后讲了话。他们在讲话中都着重提到中、波两国军队之间的兄弟友谊对于保卫世界和平的巨大作用。
