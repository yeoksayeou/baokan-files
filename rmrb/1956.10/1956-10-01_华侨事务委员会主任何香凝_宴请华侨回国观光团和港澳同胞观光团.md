Title: He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Hosts a Banquet for Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriot Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, hosted a banquet this afternoon for Overseas Chinese visiting groups and Hong Kong and Macao compatriot visiting groups. More than one thousand people attended the banquet.
    He Xiangning spoke at the banquet, expressing a warm welcome and regards to the members of the visiting groups who had come from afar. She hoped that after they returned, they would convey what they had seen in various parts of the motherland to overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, uniting them to work together for the socialist construction of the motherland and to enhance the friendship between overseas Chinese and the people of their countries of residence.
    Among those present were Liao Chengzhi, Fang Fang, and Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Directors of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and members Cai Tingkai, Chen Jiageng, Zhuang Mingli, and others.

### 华侨事务委员会主任何香凝  宴请华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    华侨事务委员会主任何香凝
    宴请华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团
    新华社30日讯　华侨事务委员会主任何香凝今天下午设宴招待华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团。参加宴会的有一千多人。
    何香凝在宴会上讲话，对远道回来观光的团员表示热烈欢迎和慰问。她希望他们回去之后，能把在祖国各地所看到的情况转告国外侨胞和港澳同胞，团结他们共同为祖国的社会主义建设、为增进国外侨胞与侨居国人民的友谊而努力。
    出席作陪的有华侨事务委员会副主任廖承志、方方、庄希泉，委员蔡廷锴、陈嘉庚、庄明理等。
