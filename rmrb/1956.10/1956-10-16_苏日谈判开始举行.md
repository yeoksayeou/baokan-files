Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Begin

Author:

Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: Negotiations between delegations of the Soviet and Japanese governments began at 12:00 noon (Moscow time) on October 15th in the Kremlin. The Soviet government delegation is headed by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin. The Japanese government delegation is headed by the Prime Minister of Japan, Ichiro Hatoyama.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, hosted a banquet in the Grand Kremlin on October 15th in honor of the Prime Minister of Japan, Ichiro Hatoyama.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th, Moscow Dispatch from TASS, October 14th: The Prime Minister of Japan, Ichiro Hatoyama, who is currently visiting Moscow, and his wife, visited the Gorky Lenin Museum on October 14th.

### 苏日谈判开始举行

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判开始举行
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社莫斯科15日讯：苏联和日本两国政府代表团10月15日中午12时（莫斯科时间）在克里姆林宫开始谈判。率领苏联政府代表团的是苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁。率领日本政府代表团的是日本首相鸠山一郎。
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社莫斯科15日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁10月15日在大克里姆林宫设宴招待日本首相鸠山一郎。
    据新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科14日电：正在莫斯科访问的日本首相鸠山一郎和他的夫人10月14日参观了戈尔基的列宁博物馆。
