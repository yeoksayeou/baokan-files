Title: Lao Sides Discuss Ceasefire Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th. According to a Vientiane radio broadcast on the 3rd, the military delegations of the Kingdom of Laos and the Lao Fighting Forces continued their meeting on October 2nd to discuss the effective implementation of the cessation of hostilities.
The report said that the meeting was conducted in an atmosphere "full of fraternal friendship and mutual understanding."

### 老挝双方讨论停战问题

1956-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝双方讨论停战问题
    新华社河内4日电　据万象电台3日广播：老挝王国和寮国战斗部队的军事代表团10月2日继续开会，讨论了有效地实现停止敌对行动的问题。
    消息说，会议是在“充满兄弟友情和互相谅解的气氛”中进行的。
