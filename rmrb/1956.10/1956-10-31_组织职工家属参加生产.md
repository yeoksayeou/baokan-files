Title: Organizing Family Members of Workers to Participate in Production
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 2

    Organizing Family Members of Workers to Participate in Production

    (Report from our newspaper) Many enterprises in Jinan City are paying attention to organizing family members of workers with difficult living conditions to participate in auxiliary labor or sideline production in the factories. According to a survey of twenty-nine enterprises in four industries, including light industry and machinery, twenty-eight enterprises have already organized more than 1,500 family members of workers to participate in work such as washing clothes, making clothes, making shoe soles, pasting paper boxes, packaging products, and cleaning up waste materials.

### 组织职工家属参加生产

1956-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    组织职工家属参加生产
    本报讯　济南市许多企业注意组织生活困难的职工家属参加厂里的辅助劳动或副业生产。据轻工、机械等四个产业的二十九个企业调查，已有二十八个企业组织了一千五百多名职工家属参加洗衣、做衣、纳鞋底、糊纸盒或包扎产品、清理废料等工作。
