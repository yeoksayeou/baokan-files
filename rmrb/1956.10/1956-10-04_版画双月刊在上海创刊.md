Title: " 版画" Bi-monthly Magazine Launched in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 3rd. The inaugural issue of the "Ban Hua" (Print) magazine was published in Shanghai on the 1st. "Ban Hua" is a bi-monthly publication dedicated to publishing prints and guiding printmaking creation. Its main content is modern Chinese printmaking creation. At the same time, it publishes ancient, foreign, and folk print works, and introduces creative experiences, printmaking historical materials, etc. The inaugural issue is a special issue published to commemorate the twentieth anniversary of the death of Mr. Lu Xun, the advocate of woodcut in China.

### 版画双月刊在上海创刊

1956-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    版画双月刊在上海创刊
    据新华社上海3日电　“版画”画刊创刊号1日在上海出版，“版画”画刊是一个专门发表版画作品和指导版画创作的双月刊物，以现代中国版画创作为主要内容，同时刊登古代、外国及民间版画作品，并介绍创作经验、版画史料等。创刊号为纪念我国木刻提倡者鲁迅先生逝世二十周年出版特辑。
