Title: Demanding a Ban on Nuclear Weapons: Japanese People to Launch Nationwide Movement; Atomic and Hydrogen Bomb Victims Call for Ban on Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th: The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held an expanded meeting of its executive committee on the 23rd, deciding that December 4th will be "Petition Day for Banning Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Rescuing Atomic and Hydrogen Bomb Victims." On this day, a nationwide movement demanding a ban on nuclear weapons will be launched.

The executive committee decided that the central demands of this movement are: 1. Urge the Japanese government to request countries possessing nuclear weapons to conclude an agreement banning atomic and hydrogen bomb tests, and request the United Nations to pass a resolution banning atomic and hydrogen bomb tests; 2. Request the Japanese Diet to pass a resolution opposing atomic and hydrogen bomb bases and opposing the import of nuclear weapons into Japan; 3. Request the Japanese government to be responsible for establishing research institutions for preventing radiation hazards, and request nuclear weapon-possessing countries to establish international radiation medical research institutions.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th: The Japan Confederation of A-Bomb and H-Bomb Sufferers Organizations sent letters on the 24th to US President Eisenhower, US presidential candidate Stevenson, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin, British Prime Minister Eden, and the Pope, calling for a ban on atomic and hydrogen bomb tests before Christmas (December 25).

### 要求禁止核子武器  日本人民将展开全国性运动  原子弹氢弹受害者呼吁禁止试验核子武器

1956-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    要求禁止核子武器
    日本人民将展开全国性运动
    原子弹氢弹受害者呼吁禁止试验核子武器
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会23日举行干事会扩大会议，决定12月4日为“禁止原子弹、氢弹和救援原子弹、氢弹受害者的请愿日”。在这一天将展开全国性要求禁止核子武器的运动。
    干事会决定这次运动的中心要求是：一、敦促日本政府要求保有核子武器的国家缔结禁止原子弹和氢弹试验的协定，并且要求联合国通过禁止原子弹和氢弹试验的决议；二、要求日本国会通过反对原子弹和氢弹基地和反对向日本运入核子武器的决议；三、要求日本政府负责设立防止放射线灾害的研究机关，并且要求核子武器保有国设立国际性的放射线医学研究机关。
    据新华社25日讯　东京消息：日本原子弹氢弹受害者团体协议会在24日寄信给美国总统艾森豪威尔、美国总统候选人史蒂文森、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、英国首相艾登和罗马教皇，呼吁在圣诞节（12月25日）以前禁止试验原子弹和氢弹。
