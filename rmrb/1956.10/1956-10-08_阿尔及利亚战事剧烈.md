Title: Fierce Fighting in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: According to a TASS dispatch from Paris on the 7th: According to "France-Soir," as a result of clashes between French troops and Algerian insurgents near Tébessa-Kasserine on the Algerian-Tunisian border, both sides suffered heavy losses. The French side suffered eight deaths, including two officers. A second fierce battle took place in the Oum-el-Araies area on the Algerian-Tunisian border. Dozens of people were killed and injured on both sides, French troops and Algerian insurgents.

### 阿尔及利亚战事剧烈

1956-10-08
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚战事剧烈
    据新华社7日讯　据塔斯社巴黎7日讯：据“法兰西晚报”报道，法国军队和阿尔及利亚起义军在阿尔及利亚和突尼斯边界附近的特赫拉—卡赛林发生冲突的结果，双方都遭受很大损失。法军方面计有八人被击毙，其中包括两名军官。第二次激烈战斗发生在阿尔及利亚和突尼斯边界上的埃姆—德拉哈姆区。法军和阿尔及利亚起义军双方死伤数十人。
