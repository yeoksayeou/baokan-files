Title: Phouma Visits Eden
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 5th: Laotian Prime Minister Prince Phouma, who is currently visiting Britain, visited British Prime Minister Eden on October 4th.

Phouma arrived in Britain on the morning of October 3rd for a two-day visit. On the afternoon of the 3rd, Phouma held talks with British Deputy Foreign Minister Nutting. According to Agence France-Presse, quoting authoritative sources, Nutting assured Phouma that Laos could count on Britain's "full and friendly support" in the diplomatic relations between the two countries.

### 富马访问艾登

1956-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    富马访问艾登
    新华社5日讯　伦敦消息：正在英国访问的老挝首相富马亲王10月4日访问了英国首相艾登。
    富马是在10月3日早晨到英国作为期两天的访问的。3日下午，富马同英国外交副大臣纳丁举行了会谈。据法新社引权威人士的话说，纳丁曾经向富马保证，在英国和老挝两国的外交关系中，老挝可以指望得到英国“充分和友好的支持”。
