Title: Jiangsu Province Establishes Agricultural Cooperation Cadre Schools
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 21st. Eighty agricultural cooperation cadre schools have been established in eight special districts and various counties of Jiangsu Province. As of mid-October, more than 60,000 agricultural cooperative directors, deputy directors, accountants, animal husbandry and veterinary technicians, and agronomy technicians have been rotated into the schools for study. This winter and throughout next year, another 260,000 people will be rotated into the schools.

The curriculum of these schools includes the operation and management of agricultural cooperatives, basic agricultural knowledge, as well as specialized courses in accounting, animal husbandry, and cultivation. The study period is thirty to forty days.

### 江苏省举办农业合作干部学校

1956-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    江苏省举办农业合作干部学校
    新华社南京21日电　江苏省八个专区和各县举办了八十所农业合作干部学校，到10月中旬为止，已有六万多名农业社的主任、副主任、会计员、畜牧兽医技术员、农艺技术员等轮流入校学习。今年冬季和明年全年，还将有二十六万人轮流入学。
    这些学校的学习内容有农业合作社的经营管理、农业基础知识以及会计、家畜饲养、栽培等专业课程。学习时间为三十到四十天。
