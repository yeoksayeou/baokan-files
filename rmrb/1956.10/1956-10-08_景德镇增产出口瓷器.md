Title: Jingdezhen Increases Production of Export Porcelain

Author:

Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang -- Jingdezhen supplied more than 50,000 cases of porcelain for export in the first three quarters of this year, more than double the total for the entire year of last year. These porcelains include vases, flower pots, tableware, and other items. These porcelains have been shipped to more than 20 countries in Europe, the Americas, Africa, and Asia for sale.

In light of the increase in foreign sales, Jingdezhen will further increase the production of export porcelain in the future.

### 景德镇增产出口瓷器

1956-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    景德镇增产出口瓷器
    据新华社南昌电　景德镇今年前三个季度供应出口的瓷器有五万多箱，比去年全年还多一倍以上。这些瓷器中有花瓶、花缽、餐具等。这些瓷器曾运到了欧洲、美洲、非洲和亚洲等二十多个国家销售。
    根据外销增加的情况，景德镇今后还要增产出口瓷器。
