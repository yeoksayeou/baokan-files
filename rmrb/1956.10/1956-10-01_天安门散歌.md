Date: 1956-10-01

    Tiananmen Rhapsody
    Fang Yin
        I　　Here, millions of hearts are closely tied, here, millions of eyes are drawn; here is the eastern branch of the world peace radio station, here, the sounds of peace are broadcast every moment.

        II　　During festivals, this place is an ocean—
  	     an ocean of people,
  	     an ocean of lights,
  	     an ocean of fireworks,
  	     an ocean of red flags,
  	     an ocean of laughter… Yet, which day here isn't a festival? You, visitor from afar, please feel free to take a seat on the reviewing stand for sightseeing at any time. …

        III　　Who says the red walls here are as red as fire? Fire, however, cannot emit such warmth and
  	      light—
  	     "Long Live the People's Republic of China"
  	     "Long Live the Great Unity of the People of the World" These slogans are not embedded in the wall,
  	      but engraved in people's hearts,
  	      shining in the sky that all mankind can
  	      see…

          Written on the eve of the 1956 National Day

### 天安门散歌
方殷
1956-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    天安门散歌
    方殷
        一　　这儿紧系着千万人的心，这儿吸引着千万人的眼睛；这儿是世界和平电台的东方分
  站，这儿每时每刻都播送着和平的
  声音。
        二　　节日里，这儿是一片海洋——
  人的海洋，
  灯的海洋，
  焰火的海洋，
  红旗的海洋，
  欢笑的海洋……然而，这儿又哪天不是节日呢？你远方的来客，请随时坐上观礼台观光。……
        三　　谁说这儿的红墙红似火？火，却不能发出这样的热和
  光——
  “中华人民共和国万岁”
  “世界人民大团结万岁”标语不是嵌在墙上，
  是刻在人们的心上，
  辉映在全人类都能看见的天
  上……
          1956年国庆节前夕作
