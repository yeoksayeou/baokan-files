Title: Foreign Minister of Italy States Opposition to Using Force to Resolve Suez Issue in Foreign Policy Report to the Upper House
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, October 3rd: According to TASS, Italian Foreign Minister Martino, when delivering a report on foreign policy in the Senate on October 2nd, said that after the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, the Italian government's actions "started from condemning Egypt's decision." He assured that "Western unity is the cornerstone of Italian policy." However, Martino said, "From the beginning of the crisis, the Italian government has ruled out the possibility of using force because it believes that it is not easy to limit any possible conflict to this region." Martino pointed out that the Italian representative had expressed doubts about the basic tasks that the United States, Britain, and France initially proposed to assign to the so-called Suez Canal Users' Association plan, which was to establish such an association. Martino then pointed out that Italy is concerned about paying the Suez Canal passage fees to Egypt according to the bookkeeping procedures. According to him, Italy "unreservedly participates in the Canal Users' Association, but with one unspoken reservation, which is to consider our arguments, our decisions, our opinions..."
    Martino reiterated in answering questions that Italy "hopes to assist in finding a solution sufficient to guarantee peace."

### 在上院作外交报告  意外长表示反对使用武力解决苏伊士问题

1956-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    在上院作外交报告
    意外长表示反对使用武力解决苏伊士问题
    据新华社4日讯　据塔斯社罗马3日讯：意大利外交部长马蒂诺10月2日在上议院作关于外交政策的报告的时候说，在苏伊士运河公司国有化之后，意大利政府的行动是“以指责埃及的决定为出发点的”。他保证说：“西方团结是意大利政策的基石。”然而，马蒂诺说，
  “意大利政府从危机一开始就排斥使用武力的可能性，因为它认为，要把可能发生的冲突局限在这个地区是不容易的。”马蒂诺指出，意大利代表对美英法三国最初提出建立所谓苏伊士运河使用国协会计划时企图使这一协会担负的基本任务曾经表示怀疑。马蒂诺接着指出，意大利关心的是，根据划账的手续把船只通过苏伊士运河的通行费付给埃及。据他说，意大利“无保留地参加运河使用国协会，但是有一点不明确表示的保留，这就是要考虑我们的论据，我们的决定，我们的意见……”
    马蒂诺在答复问题的时候再次保证，意大利“希望协助寻找足以保障和平的解决办法”。
