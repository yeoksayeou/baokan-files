Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Leaves Beijing to Visit Various Places

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: After a six-day visit to Beijing, the Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Huseyn Shaheed Suhrawardy, and his entourage, left Beijing this morning by special plane to visit various parts of China.

In a speech before his departure, Prime Minister Suhrawardy said that he had benefited from the talks and consultations with the great leaders of China, and that it was gratifying that China and Pakistan had many common goals and purposes. The Pakistani Prime Minister also said that these discussions were conducted in the most harmonious and friendly spirit. "I hope that any misunderstandings that may have existed have been cleared up, and that there is now a broader prospect for closer and more harmonious contact between us in promoting world peace."

Premier Zhou Enlai went to the airport today to see off the distinguished Pakistani guests. Also present at the airport to see them off were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and his wife, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Burhan and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, as well as heads of various departments of the Chinese government, democratic parties and people's organizations, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, directors of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Heads of diplomatic missions, diplomatic officials and economic delegations from various countries to China also went to the airport to see them off today.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived at the airport by car at 8:45 a.m. Here, he shook hands with the Chinese state leaders who came to see him off.

The band played the national anthems of Pakistan and China, Prime Minister Suhrawardy reviewed the guard of honor, and then shook hands with the well-wishers to say goodbye.

After Prime Minister Suhrawardy accepted the flowers presented to him by young women from Beijing, he once again shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai in a cordial farewell. After boarding the plane, he waved frequently to the well-wishers at the cabin door.

The special plane took off from Beijing Airport at 9:10 a.m.

Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the distinguished guests to visit various places on the same plane.

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, October 24th: The Prime Minister of Pakistan, Huseyn Shaheed Suhrawardy, and his entourage, accompanied by Geng Biao, our Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Changchun City from Beijing by special plane today.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by the Governor of Jilin Province, Li Youwen, and his wife, Major General He Jian, Commander of the Jilin Military Region, Zhou Guang, Mayor of Changchun, and his wife, as well as heads of democratic parties and mass organizations.

After arriving in Changchun, the distinguished guests first visited the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, which had only been in operation for ten days. Guo Li, Deputy Director and Chief Engineer, guided the distinguished guests on a detailed tour of the production process in the casting, engine, final assembly, and auxiliary workshops. As the distinguished guests moved from one workshop to another, the assembled workers warmly applauded their welcome. Prime Minister Suhrawardy was very interested in all the new machines and the good ventilation and transportation equipment in the workshops, and he often stopped to observe carefully. Next to the final assembly line, the Prime Minister watched with great interest as two young workers skillfully operated electric machines to deftly turn the heavy chassis over and then install other parts of the car. The Prime Minister took a picture of this scene with his camera. As he walked out of the workshop, the Prime Minister shook hands warmly with Guo Li, Deputy Director and Chief Engineer, and said, "This factory is really excellent!"

Prime Minister Suhrawardy also visited the Changchun Film Studio. The distinguished guests watched the studio's dubbing of the feature film "Malan Blossom" specifically for the Korean ethnic group in the mixing studio. In a studio, a scene from the rural feature film "Wedding of a Commune Member" was being filmed. After watching it, the Prime Minister took a group photo with the actors. The distinguished guests also visited the studio's film dubbing and watched clips from several completed films. Before leaving, Prime Minister Suhrawardy accepted a gift from the Deputy Director of the factory on behalf of the staff and wrote an inscription for the factory.

In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a banquet held by the Governor of Jilin Province, Li Youwen. (Attached picture)

### 苏拉瓦底总理离北京到各地参观

1956-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理离北京到各地参观
    新华社24日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底总理和他的随行人员在北京进行了六天的访问以后，今天早晨乘专机前往中国各地参观。
    苏拉瓦底总理在临行前发表的讲话中说，同中国的伟大领袖们的会谈和协商使他得到了益处，令人高兴的是中巴两国有很多目标和目的是相同的。这位巴基斯坦总理又说，这些商谈是以最融洽友好的精神进行的。“我希望，任何可能存在过的误解都已经消除了，而在促进世界和平方面，现在在我们之间有了进行更紧密、更融洽的接触的更广阔的前景。”
    周恩来总理今天到机场欢送巴基斯坦贵宾。到机场欢送的还有国务院副总理贺龙和夫人，李富春、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席傅作义，全国人民政协副主席包尔汉和夫人，外交部副部长张闻天，以及中国政府各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们，中巴友好协会的理事们和首都社会各界人士。
    各国驻华使节、外交官员和经济代表团团长今天也到机场欢送。
    苏拉瓦底总理由周恩来总理陪同在八时四十五分乘汽车来到飞机场。在这里，他同前来欢送的中国国家领导人握手。
    乐队奏巴、中两国国歌，苏拉瓦底总理检阅了仪仗队，然后同欢送者握手告别。
    苏拉瓦底总理接受了北京女青年献给他的鲜花以后，再一次同周恩来总理亲切地握手告别。在登上飞机以后，他在机舱门口向欢送者频频挥手致意。
    专机在九时十分起飞离开北京机场。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长王倬如同机陪同贵宾们到各地参观。
    新华社长春24日电　巴基斯坦侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底总理和他的随行人员，今天由我国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人以及外交部礼宾司司长王倬如陪同，从北京乘专机来到长春市访问。
    贵宾们在机场上受到吉林省省长栗又文和夫人、吉林军区司令员贺健少将、长春市市长周光和夫人以及各民主党派、群众团体负责人等的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们来到长春后，首先参观了开工生产才十天的第一汽车制造厂。副厂长兼总工程师郭力引导贵宾们详细参观了铸工、发动机、总装配、辅助等车间的生产过程。贵宾们从一个车间到另一个车间时，聚集的工人都热烈鼓掌欢迎。苏拉瓦底总理对各车间内全部崭新的机器和良好的通风、输送等设备很感兴趣，他不时停留下来仔细观察。在总装配线旁，总理怀着很大兴趣观看两个青年工人熟练地操纵电动机器，把沉重的车架轻巧地翻过身来，然后安装汽车其它零件。总理用摄影机拍下了这个镜头。总理在走出车间时，和副厂长兼总工程师郭力热烈握手说：“这个工厂真是好极了！”
    苏拉瓦底总理还参观了长春电影制片厂。贵宾们在混合录音室观看这个厂专门为朝鲜族译制故事影片“马兰花开”的情形。在一个摄影棚中，正在拍摄农村故事影片“社员的婚事”的一个镜头。总理看完后和演员们集体摄影留念。贵宾们在这个厂还参观了译制影片的情形，并且看了几部已摄制完成的影片的片段。苏拉瓦底总理在离开前，接受了这个厂的副厂长代表职工赠送的礼品，并且为这个厂题了祝词。
    晚上，贵宾们出席了吉林省省长栗又文举行的宴会。（附图片）
