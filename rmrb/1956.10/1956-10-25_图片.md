Title: You Yunggu
Author: You Yunggu
Date: 1956-10-25

    This year, Sichuan Province has achieved its seventh bumper harvest since liberation, and the surplus grain in areas with surplus grain for transfer has become even more abundant. Large quantities of surplus grain are being successively transferred to support the people in disaster areas.
                              Xinhua News Agency reporter You Yunggu photo
