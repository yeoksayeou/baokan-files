Title: Franco-Vietnamese Sign Telecommunications Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 6th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 6th, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the government of the French Republic signed a telecommunications agreement in Hanoi on the afternoon of October 5.

    According to this agreement, the two sides will exchange various telegrams from October 15, including official telegrams, private telegrams, news telegrams, letter telegrams, commercial telegrams, and intelligence.

    The signing of this agreement will promote communication between Vietnam and France and will further create favorable conditions for strengthening economic and cultural exchanges between the two countries.

### 法越签订电讯协定

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    法越签订电讯协定
    新华社河内6日电　据越南通讯社6日讯：越南民主共和国政府和法兰西共和国政府10月5日下午在河内签订了一项电讯协定。
    根据这项协定，双方将从10月15日起交换各种电报，包括公文电报、私人电报、新闻电报、书信电报、商业电报和情报。
    这一协定的签订，将促进越法两国间的联络，并且将进一步为加强两国间的经济和文化交流创造有利的条件。
