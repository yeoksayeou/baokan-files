Title: Indonesian Trade Union Federation Issues Statement Supporting Egypt's Struggle to Maintain Sovereignty over the Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th - TASS reported from Jakarta on the 29th: The National Committee of the Indonesian Trade Union Federation and the Central Council of Trade Unions discussed the issue of launching a movement by Indonesian workers to support the Egyptian people. Representatives of the trade unions issued a statement pointing out that the Suez issue must be resolved by peaceful means and armed intervention must be stopped.

The statement emphasized that the struggle of the Egyptian people to win their sovereignty and rights over the Suez Canal is consistent with the struggle of the Indonesian people to abolish the Round Table Conference Agreement and nationalize Dutch companies in Indonesia.

### 印度尼西亚总工会发表声明  支援埃及维护运河主权的斗争

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总工会发表声明
    支援埃及维护运河主权的斗争
    据新华社30日讯　塔斯社雅加达29日讯：印度尼西亚总工会全国委员会和工会中央理事会在联席会议上讨论了展开印度尼西亚工人支援埃及人民运动的问题。工会的代表们发表声明指出必须用和平方法解决苏伊士问题和制止武装干涉。
    声明着重指出，埃及人民为了争取他们对苏伊士运河的主权和权利而进行的斗争同印度尼西亚人民为了废除圆桌会议协定和把印度尼西亚境内的荷兰公司收归国有而进行的斗争是一致的。
