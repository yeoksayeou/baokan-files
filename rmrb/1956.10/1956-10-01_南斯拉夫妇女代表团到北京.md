Title: Yugoslav Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Yugoslav Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation was led by Lydia Sentjurc, Vice-President of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia.

Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zeng Xianzhi, Secretary of the Secretariat, Lei Jieqiong, Standing Committee member, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and his wife also came to welcome the delegation.

### 南斯拉夫妇女代表团到北京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫妇女代表团到北京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来中国访问的南斯拉夫妇女代表团一行四人，在团长、南斯拉夫联邦人民议会副主席里第亚·申特尤尔茨率领下，在今天下午坐飞机到达北京。
    到飞机场欢迎代表团的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、书记处书记曾宪植、常务委员雷洁琼等人。
    南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇和他的夫人也来欢迎代表团。
