Title: Chairman U Nu Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, U Nu, and others visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory this morning.

Chairman U Nu, accompanied by Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of Textile Industry, Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, visited various workshops of the factory. He carefully observed the operation of processes such as drawing, spinning, warping, and sizing, and watched the newly woven khaki drill in the finishing workshop. Chairman U Nu praised the cleanliness of the workshop to the factory director, Song Ting.

Afterwards, Chairman U Nu and others visited the workers' residential area and the nursery. Chairman U Nu and his wife held two children in the nursery and invited two nursery workers to take pictures with them and their son and daughter.

This morning, Chairman U Nu and others also visited the department store on Wangfujing.

### 吴努主席在北京参观

1956-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努主席在北京参观
    新华社25日讯　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努等今天上午参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂。
    吴努主席由纺织工业部副部长陈维稷、中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和外交部礼宾司副司长王雨田等陪同参观了这个工厂的各个车间。他仔细地参观了并条、细纱、整经、浆纱等工序的操作情况，在整理车间观看了新织成的卡叽布。吴努主席向厂长宋汀称赞车间的整洁。
    随后，吴努主席等参观了工人住宅区和托儿所。吴努主席和夫人在托儿所里抱起两个小孩，并且邀请两位保育员同他们以及他们的公子和女儿在一起照了像。
    今天上午，吴努主席等还参观了王府井的百货大楼。
