Title: Most Jordanian Parties Demand Abolition of Anglo-Jordanian Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 6
    
    Most Jordanian Parties
    Demand Abolition of Anglo-Jordanian Treaty
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 13th—According to TASS, parliamentary elections are scheduled to be held in Jordan on October 21st. According to "Al-Jiran" newspaper, the majority of parties participating in the election are demanding the abolition of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty of 1948 (according to which Britain obtained the right to station troops on Jordanian territory), the abolition of military alliances and military treaties, and cooperation with Egypt and Syria, which pursue independent foreign policies.

### 约旦大多数政党  要求废除英约条约

1956-10-16
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦大多数政党
    要求废除英约条约
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特13日讯：约旦订于10月21日举行议会选举。据“邻人报”报道，参加竞选的大多数政党要求废除1948年的英—约条约（根据这个条约英国获得了在约旦领土上驻扎军队的权利），要求废除军事同盟和军事条约，并且要求同实行独立外交政策的埃及和叙利亚合作。
