Title: Football Selection Match: United Team White defeats Shanghai Team 2-1
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-15
Page: 4

Football Selection Match
	United Team White defeats Shanghai Team 2-1
    (Our newspaper reports) In a football selection match held yesterday afternoon at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium, United Team White defeated the Shanghai Team by a score of 2-1.

### 足球选拔赛  联队白队二比一胜上海队

1956-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    足球选拔赛
    联队白队二比一胜上海队
    本报讯　昨天下午在先农坛体育场举行的一场足球选拔赛，联队白队以二比一战胜了上海市队。
