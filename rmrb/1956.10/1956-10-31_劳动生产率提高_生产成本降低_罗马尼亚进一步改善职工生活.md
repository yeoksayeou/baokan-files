Title: Increased Labor Productivity and Reduced Production Costs: Romania Further Improves Workers' Lives
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 30th: Bucharest News: Today, the newspapers here published the decision of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Council of the Romanian Trade Unions on improving the current wage system and preferential treatment system.

The decision stated that, because the industrial production plan for the first nine months of this year was completed at 104%, labor productivity increased, and production costs decreased, it is possible to further improve the lives of workers and staff.

The decision stipulates that the wages of low-wage workers will be increased starting from November 1st of this year.

Starting from December 1st, 1956, the amount of preferential treatment for some recipients of preferential benefits will be increased.

The decision also stipulates that the research on improving the wage system should be completed before December 31, 1956, so that this improvement can promote material incentives for workers, increase labor productivity, and increase enterprise profits.

### 劳动生产率提高　生产成本降低  罗马尼亚进一步改善职工生活

1956-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    劳动生产率提高　生产成本降低
    罗马尼亚进一步改善职工生活
    新华社布拉格30日电　布加勒斯特消息：今天此间各报刊载了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会、部长会议和罗马尼亚工会中央理事会关于改善现行的工资制度和优抚金制度的决定。
    决定说，由于今年头九个月的工业生产计划完成了104％，劳动生产率提高了，生产成本减低了，有可能进一步来改善职工的生活。
    决定规定低工资工人的工资将自今年11月1日起提高。
    自1956年12月1日起，有几种优抚金领取者的优抚金额将予增加。
    决定还规定，在1956年12月31日以前结束对改善工资制度问题的研究，使这种改善能够促进对工人的物质刺激，提高劳动生产率和增加企业赢利。
