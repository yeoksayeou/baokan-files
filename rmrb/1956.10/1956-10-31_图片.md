Title: Pictures
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 4

    Top picture: After the Wuchang Uprising, Sun Yat-sen returned to China from overseas and was elected as the Provisional President of the Republic of China. The picture shows Sun Yat-sen when he took office as the Provisional President.
    Top right picture: In 1914, Sun Yat-sen re-organized the Chinese Revolutionary Party in Japan to continue the revolutionary struggle. The picture shows a group photo of party members at the founding of the Chinese Revolutionary Party. Sun Yat-sen is in the center of the front row.
    Right picture: In 1924, during the First National Congress of the Chinese Kuomintang, news of Lenin's death arrived, and the meeting was immediately suspended for three days and the flag was flown at half-mast to express condolences. The picture shows the scene of the memorial service for Lenin's death. The person standing in the center is Sun Yat-sen.
                            (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 图片

1956-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

    上图：武昌起义后，孙中山由海外兼程回国，被举为中华民国临时大总统。图为就任临时大总统时的孙中山。
    右上图：1914年，孙中山在日本重新组织中华革命党，继续进行革命斗争。图为中华革命党成立时党员合影。前排中是孙中山。
    右图：1924年，中国国民党第一次全国代表大会会议期间，列宁逝世消息传到，当即停会三日并下半旗致哀。图为列宁逝世追悼会开会情形。中立者是孙中山。
                            （新华社稿）
