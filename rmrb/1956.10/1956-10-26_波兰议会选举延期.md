Title: Polish Parliamentary Elections Postponed
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 25th. The Polish Parliament decided at its meeting on the 24th to postpone the parliamentary elections from December 16th this year to January 20th, 1957.

### 波兰议会选举延期

1956-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰议会选举延期
    新华社华沙25日电　波兰议会24日会议决定把议会选举的日期从今年12月16日延期到1957年1月20日。
