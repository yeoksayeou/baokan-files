Title: Khrushchev Receives Kono Again
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: TASS, Moscow: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received Ichiro Kono, the Japanese Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, who is in Moscow as a member of the Japanese government delegation participating in the negotiations for the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations, on October 17 and held talks with him.
    Present at the meeting was Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Fedin.

### 赫鲁晓夫再接见河野

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫再接见河野
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫10月17日接见了以日本政府代表团团员身份在莫斯科参加苏日关系正常化谈判的日本农林相河野一郎，并且同他进行了会谈。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部副部长费德林。
