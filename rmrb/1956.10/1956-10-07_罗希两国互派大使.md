Title: Romania and Greece Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. According to a report by the Romanian News Agency on the 5th: Based on the joint statement signed in Athens in August regarding the restoration of diplomatic relations between the two countries, the Romanian government and the Greek government have recently appointed diplomatic envoys at the ambassadorial level.
    The Romanian government's appointed ambassador to Greece is Delinceanu. The Greek government's appointed ambassador to Romania is Pembras.

### 罗希两国互派大使

1956-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    罗希两国互派大使
    新华社6日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社5日报道：罗马尼亚政府和希腊政府根据8月间在雅典签署的关于恢复两国间外交关系的联合声明，最近已经分别任命了大使级的外交使节。
    罗马尼亚政府任命的驻希腊大使是德林奇亚努。希腊政府任命的驻罗马尼亚大使是彭布腊斯。
