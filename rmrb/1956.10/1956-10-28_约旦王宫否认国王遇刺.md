Title: Jordanian Royal Palace Denies King's Assassination
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 27th—The Jordanian Royal Palace denied reports of King Hussein's assassination on the 26th.

Royal Palace officials pointed out that the rumors circulating in Paris on the morning of the 26th regarding the assassination of the King of Jordan were completely untrue.

That evening, King Hussein met with some foreign journalists at the Royal Palace. He said that he believed there were political motives behind this rumor.

### 约旦王宫否认国王遇刺

1956-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦王宫否认国王遇刺
    新华社27日讯　安曼消息：约旦王宫26日否认国王侯赛尼被刺的消息。
    王宫官员指出，26日上午在巴黎流传的关于约旦国王遇刺的消息是完全不真实的。
    当天晚间，侯赛尼国王在王宫同一些外国新闻记者见面。他说，他认为这一谣言背后是藏有政治动机的。
