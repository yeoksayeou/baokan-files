Title: Lhasa, Xigaze, and Qamdo Rejoice
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 1st. The cities of Lhasa, Xigaze, and Qamdo in Tibet held grand gatherings today to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    The ancient city of Lhasa was decorated with colorful banners, and every household throughout the city was covered with national flags, while the streets and alleys were plastered with colorful slogans. At noon, more than 30,000 people held a celebration meeting on the People's Square in front of the Potala Palace. Responsible cadres of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Tibet Military Region, as well as personnel from the Central Youth Delegation, major officials of the Tibetan local government, and Gandan Tri Rinpoche, the successor to the Yellow Sect master Tsongkhapa, all attended the meeting today. Also invited to observe were the Indian Consul General in Lhasa, B.N. Memon, and the Nepalese Yu Ta Gong Bixing in Lhasa.

    When the chairman of the meeting, Xianga Juimei Dorje, announced the opening, to the sound of ceremonial music and salutes, colored balloons carrying huge slogans "Long Live the Chinese Communist Party" and "Long Live the Great Unity of All Nationalities" flew from the venue into the sky above Lhasa. Ape Aawang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, in their speeches at the meeting, warmly congratulated our country on the great achievements made in all aspects of socialist construction and socialist transformation, and the vigorous development of various construction undertakings in Tibet since the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. Ape Aawang Jinmei pointed out in his speech that the Tibetan people should continue to carry forward the spirit of anti-imperialism, patriotism, unity, and progress, and create conditions for the establishment of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region and reform.

    Today, workers, students, lamas, and thousands of newly employed Tibetan cadres in Lhasa joyfully celebrated the national holiday. The male and female Tibetan workers who built the Lhasa Hydropower Plant worked overnight to generate electricity today, and today they came to participate in the meeting. After the meeting, the square began a festive carnival. The Dalai Lama's "Gaba" song and dance troupe and the famous Jormolong Tibetan Opera Troupe performed classical Tibetan songs and dances and Tibetan opera. Officials of the Tibetan local government performed archery. More than 700 young men and women from all over Tibet who participated in the first Tibetan Youth Congress performed the most beautiful songs and dances from all over Tibet.

    Xigaze held a mass rally of more than 8,000 people today, with many farmers and herdsmen coming from dozens of miles away to participate. National flags were planted on the rooftops of various organs, shops, and residents' homes in the city, and the streets and alleys were swept very clean. In order to celebrate the National Day, folk artists from all over Xigaze also came to the urban area. They will perform Tibetan opera and songs and dances for the masses for three consecutive days starting today. Before the National Day, a upsurge of studying the documents of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China was set off in Xigaze. The Xigaze wired broadcasting station will continuously broadcast various reports and documents of the "Eighth National Congress" in the first week of October.

    More than 5,000 people from all walks of life in Qamdo also held a gathering today to celebrate the National Day. The city was decorated with lanterns and colored streamers everywhere. Tibetan and Han workers, citizens, suburban farmers, and lamas in Qamdo all wore new clothes today. Early in the morning, they went to the celebration meeting place on the banks of the Lancang River in groups, waving colorful flags and beating gongs and drums. After the celebration meeting, the joyous masses performed more than 20 cultural programs on the square and carried out various sports activities. Today, the citizens also set up more than 300 tents on the grassland and foothills near the celebration meeting place. The whole family drank and sang in the tents, and many people gathered around the tents to sing and dance to their heart's content.

### 拉萨、日喀则、昌都在欢腾中

1956-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    拉萨、日喀则、昌都在欢腾中
    新华社拉萨1日电　西藏拉萨、日喀则、昌都等城市今天都举行盛大集会，庆祝建国七周年。
    古城拉萨装饰得五彩缤纷，全市家家户户遍悬国旗，大街小巷贴满了彩色标语。中午，三万多人在布达拉宫前的人民广场上举行庆祝大会，西藏自治区筹备委员会、中共西藏工委和西藏军区的负责干部、中央青年代表团的人员以及西藏地方政府的主要官员和黄教大师宗喀巴的继承人甘丹赤巴，今天都参加了大会。应邀观礼的还有印度在拉萨的总领事毕·纳·梅农和尼泊尔在拉萨的宇大公必兴。
    当大会主席先喀·居美多吉宣布开会以后，在礼乐和礼炮声中，彩色气球带着“中国共产党万岁”“各民族大团结万岁”的巨幅标语从会场飞上拉萨上空。西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美和中共西藏工委副书记范明在大会上的讲话中，都热烈地祝贺我国在社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业各方面取得的巨大成就和西藏自治区筹委会成立后西藏各项建设事业的蓬勃发展。阿沛·阿旺晋美在讲话中指出，西藏人民应该继续发扬反帝爱国、团结进步的精神，为成立统一的西藏自治区和改革创造条件。
    今天，拉萨市的工人、学生、喇嘛和新参加工作的上千名的藏族干部都欢乐地庆祝国家节日。修建拉萨水电厂的男女藏族工人，为了争取在今天发电，昨夜有的连夜工作，今天他们又来参加大会。大会结束后，广场里开始节日的狂欢。达赖喇嘛的“噶巴”歌舞团和有名的觉木隆藏剧团表演了西藏的古典歌舞和藏剧。西藏地方政府的官员们表演了射箭。参加西藏第一届青年代表大会的七百多名西藏各地男女青年，表演了西藏各地最优美的歌舞。
    日喀则今天举行了八千多人的群众大会，许多农民牧民从数十里外赶来参加大会。市内各机关、商店和市民的房顶上遍插国旗，大街小巷打扫得十分清洁。为了庆祝国庆，日喀则各地的民间艺人也来到市区，他们将从今天起连续为群众演出三天藏戏和歌舞。在国庆节前，日喀则市掀起了一个学习中国共产党第八次全国代表大会各种文件的热潮。日喀则有线广播站将在10月的第一周内连续广播“八大”的各种报告和文件。
    昌都各界人民五千多人今天也举行了庆祝国庆的集会。市内到处张灯结彩。昌都市藏、汉族工人、市民、郊区农民和喇嘛今天都穿着新衣，他们一早就成群结队地打着彩旗，敲锣打鼓地赶到澜沧江边的庆祝大会会场。在庆祝大会结束后，欢乐的群众在广场上表演了二十多个文娱节目，并进行了各种体育活动。市民们今天还在庆祝大会会场附近的草原上和山坡下搭起了三百多顶帐篷，他们全家大小在帐篷里饮酒高歌，许多人围在帐篷旁边纵情歌舞。
