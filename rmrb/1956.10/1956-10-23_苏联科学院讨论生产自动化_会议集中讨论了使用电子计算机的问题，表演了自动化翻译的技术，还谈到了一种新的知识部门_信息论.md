Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Discusses Production Automation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. According to TASS news: The Soviet Academy of Sciences recently held a conference in Moscow to discuss production automation. The main focus of the meeting was the use of electronic computers.

　　More than a hundred reports were heard at the meeting. The reports emphasized the importance of calculating machines for production automation and discussed the specific tasks of automation in the power, metallurgy, chemical, and other industrial sectors. One report stated that the time was ripe for using mathematical machines in the power sector. In the near future, a unified power system will be established first in the European part of the Soviet Union and then in Siberia. The characteristics of this huge power system are that the conditions in each region are complex and diverse, and the charts and load characteristics of each day and night are also different. Managing such a power system can only be achieved with automatic computers. A report on the automation of the chemical industry pointed out that dispatchers using mathematical management machines could increase labor productivity by more than double, greatly reduce equipment wear and tear, and reduce product costs.

　　One report said that at present, it is entirely possible to manufacture a computer that performs hundreds of thousands of operations per second, and this is not the limit. The application of new methods in physics, especially nanosecond technology, electromagnetic waves, and superconductivity phenomena, is of great significance. Connecting many machines with a common supplementary control device to work simultaneously is one way to increase calculation speed.

　　At this meeting, the technology of automated translation was demonstrated. An English technical document was transmitted from the conference hall to the research institute where the fast electronic computer was installed via teletypewriter. After a very short period, it was accurately translated into another language.

　　The meeting also discussed a new branch of knowledge—information theory. Information theory is of great help to the work of calculating fast computers, the work of calculating control equipment, and the work of automatic telecommunications systems. Moreover, information theory also creates new possibilities for studying the theory of writing and certain other non-technical sciences.

　　In addition to experts in automation, physics, and mathematics, many representatives from the industrial sector also participated in this meeting.

### 苏联科学院讨论生产自动化  会议集中讨论了使用电子计算机的问题，表演了自动化翻译的技术，还谈到了一种新的知识部门——信息论

1956-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院讨论生产自动化
    会议集中讨论了使用电子计算机的问题，表演了自动化翻译的技术，还谈到了一种新的知识部门——信息论
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联科学院最近在莫斯科举行了讨论生产自动化的会议。会议主要注意的中心是使用电子计算机的问题。
    会议共听取了一百多个报告。会上的报告强调指出了计算机器对生产自动化的重要意义，并且谈到了动力、冶金、化学等工业部门自动化的具体任务。有一个报告说，在动力方面使用数学机器的时机已经成熟了。在不久的将来，将首先在苏联的欧洲部分、随后在西伯利亚建立统一的动力系统。这种巨大的动力系统的特点是：各个地区的条件是复杂多样的，图表和每一昼夜的负荷性质也各不相同。管理这种动力系统，只有用自动计算机才能实现。关于化学工业自动化的报告指出，调度员利用数学管理机器，可能把劳动生产率提高一倍以上，大大减少设备的磨损和减低产品的成本。
    有一个报告说，在目前，制造一种每秒钟作几十万次运算的计算机是完全可能的，而这还不是极限。对物理学上的新的方法、特别是对毫微秒技术、电磁波、超导电性现象的运用有着重大的意义。用一个共同的补充操纵装置把许多机器联结起来同时进行工作，是提高计算速度的一种方法。
    在这个会议上，曾表演了自动化翻译的技术。一篇英文的技术文件通过电传打字机从会议的大厅传送到装置快速电子计算机的研究所，经过一个很短时间，它就被确切地译成了别的文字。
    会上还谈到了一种新的知识部门——信息论。信息论对计算快速计算机的工作、计算操纵设备的工作以及自动电讯系统的工作等大有帮助。而且，信息论还为研究文字和其他某些非技术科学的理论创造了新的可能性。
    参加这次会议的不但有自动化、物理、数学等方面的专家，而且还有工业界的许多代表。
