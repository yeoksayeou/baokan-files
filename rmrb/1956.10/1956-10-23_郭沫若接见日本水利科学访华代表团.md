Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Hydraulic Science Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received the Japanese Hydraulic Science Delegation this morning. The delegation was invited to visit China and was led by Head of the Delegation Saburo Taniguchi, and members Soichiro Hayami, Takeshi Ito, Yuzuru Koyanagi, Shigeru Ichinura, and Secretary Tasuke Ishimura.

   During the meeting, the Japanese hydraulic scientists presented the Chinese Academy of Sciences with gifts such as hydraulic science materials.

   Present at the reception were Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yan Jici, Director of the Department of Technical Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Mao Yisheng, Deputy Director, among others.

### 郭沫若接见日本水利科学访华代表团

1956-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本水利科学访华代表团
    新华社22日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天上午接见了应邀来中国访问的日本水利科学访华代表团团长谷口三郎，团员速水颂一郎、伊藤刚、小柳弥、市浦繁和秘书石村太助。
    在接见的时候，日本水利学家向中国科学院赠送了水利科学资料等礼品。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院秘书长裴丽生，中国科学院技术科学学部主任严济慈、副主任茅以升等人。
