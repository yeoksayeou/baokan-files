Title: Fifteen Asian and African Nations in the United Nations Request Inclusion of the Algerian Question on the UN General Assembly Agenda
Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 2nd dispatch: Fifteen Asian and African nations formally requested on October 1st to include the Algerian issue on the agenda of the United Nations General Assembly. These fifteen nations are: Afghanistan, Burma, Ceylon, Egypt, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Pakistan, the Philippines, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, and Syria.

The Chairman of the Asian-African Group for October at the United Nations, Indonesian Ambassador Zondro Negoro, has submitted the letter making this request to Secretary-General Hammarskjöld.

### 联合国十五个亚非国家  要求把阿尔及利亚问题列入联大议程

1956-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国十五个亚非国家
    要求把阿尔及利亚问题列入联大议程
    新华社2日讯　纽约消息：十五个亚非国家在10月1日正式要求把阿尔及利亚案件列入联合国大会议程，这十五个国家是：阿富汗、缅甸、锡兰、埃及、印度尼西亚、伊朗、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、巴基斯坦、菲律宾、沙特阿拉伯、也门和叙利亚。
    联合国亚非集团10月份主席、印度尼西亚大使宗德罗尼哥罗已经把提出这个要求的信件交给秘书长哈马舍尔德。
