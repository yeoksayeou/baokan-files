Title: All-China Sports Federation Holds First Plenary Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. The All-China Sports Federation held its first plenary committee meeting today.

　　The meeting elected Ma John, Rong Gaotang, Wu Yunrui, Dong Shouyi, Huang Zhong, Zhang Lianhua, Zhu Debao, Li Qingan, Luo Aihua, Hu Xiaofeng, Zhao Junyi, Xia Xiang, Li Shubin, Yuan Dunli, Zhang Zhen, Zhang Zhihuai, Zhang Huilan, Zhong Shitong, and Han Fudong as nineteen members of the Standing Committee, forming the Standing Committee of the All-China Sports Federation. The meeting elected Ma John as Chairman of the Standing Committee, Rong Gaotang, Wu Yunrui, Dong Shouyi, and Huang Zhong as Vice-Chairmen, and Zhang Lianhua as Secretary-General.

### 中华全国体育总会举行第一次全体委员会议

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    中华全国体育总会举行第一次全体委员会议
    新华社24日讯　中华全国体育总会今天举行第一次全体委员会议。
    这次会议选出了马约翰、荣高棠、吴蕴瑞、董守义、黄中、张联华、朱德宝、李清安、罗爱华、胡晓凤、赵均一、夏翔、栗树彬、袁敦礼、张轸、张之槐、张汇兰、钟师统、韩复东等十九人为常务委员，组成中华全国体育总会常务委员会；会议推选了马约翰为常务委员会主席，荣高棠、吴蕴瑞、董守义、黄中为副主席，张联华为秘书长。
