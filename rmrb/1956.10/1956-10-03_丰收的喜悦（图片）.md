The Joy of Harvest
The highest-yielding corn in Yongfeng Agricultural Cooperative, Sancha Kou Township, Ji County, Hebei, can produce 800 to 900 catties. Cooperative members Zhao Suzhen and Yang Xiuyun feel unspeakably happy looking at the high-yielding corn. Look! How happy they are!
　　Qianjin Newspaper Agency Liu Ming Photo
