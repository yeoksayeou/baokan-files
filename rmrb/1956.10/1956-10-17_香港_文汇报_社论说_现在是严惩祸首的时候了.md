Title: Hong Kong "Wen Wei Po" Editorial Says Now Is the Time to Severely Punish the Culprits

Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 16th: Hong Kong News: Hong Kong's "Wen Wei Po" published an editorial today entitled "Severely Punish the Culprits of the Tsuen Wan Tragedy." The editorial stated: "The main culprits behind the Kowloon's worst tragedy in a century caused by rioters in Tsuen Wan are Kuomintang secret agents, which is absolutely true."

The editorial said: "Now is the time for the Hong Kong government to take action, assume responsibility, and severely punish the culprits of the Tsuen Wan tragedy."

The editorial also said: "In order to take responsibility for ensuring the safety of citizens' lives and property, we hope that the Hong Kong government will have a concrete and satisfactory solution for this Kowloon riot." (Attached picture)
The Zhou Shengsheng Gold Shop on Cheung Sha Wan Road, Kowloon, looted and destroyed by Kuomintang secret agents.

### 香港“文汇报”社论说  现在是严惩祸首的时候了

1956-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    香港“文汇报”社论说  现在是严惩祸首的时候了
    据新华社广州16日电　香港消息：香港“文汇报”今天发表题为“严惩荃湾大惨案的祸首”的社论。社论写道：暴徒这次在荃湾造成九龙百年来最大的惨剧，主凶是国民党特务分子，是绝对千真万确。
    社论说：“现在已是香港政府坐言起行，负起责任严惩荃湾大惨案的祸首的时候了”。
    社论还说：“为了负起责任保障市民生命财产安全，我们希望香港政府对于这次九龙大暴乱，应该有一个具体收场的善后办法。（附图片）
    被国民党特务分子抢劫捣毁的九龙长沙湾道周生生金铺。
