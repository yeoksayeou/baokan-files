Title: The People's Party of Malaya Demands the Authorities Resume Peace Talks with the Malayan Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Singapore Dispatch: According to the "Newspaper", the People's Party of Malaya believes that only by resuming peace talks with the Malayan Communist Party can some major issues be resolved and peace in Malaya be restored.

The People's Party emphasized the importance of peace for Malaya in its publication "Guidance Weekly." It stated that Malaya is now stepping onto the threshold of independence, but what is the significance of an independent Malaya without peace?

### 马来亚人民党要求当局同马共恢复和谈

1956-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    马来亚人民党要求当局同马共恢复和谈
    新华社19日讯　新加坡消息：据“新报”报道，马来亚人民党认为，只有恢复同马来亚共产党进行和平谈判，一些主要的问题才有可能解决，马来亚和平才有可能恢复。
    人民党在它的刊物“指导周刊”中强调和平对于马来亚的重要意义，它说，现在马来亚正在踏上独立的门槛，但是，如果没有和平的话，那么独立的马来亚有什么意义呢？
