Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the German News Agency: President of the German News Agency Holds Reception in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Deba Weiland, president of the German News Agency, who is visiting China at invitation, held a reception in Beijing tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the German News Agency.
    Deba Weiland said in a speech at the meeting that celebrating the 10th anniversary of the German News Agency in China today, she is willing to cooperate with the press circles of various countries to work together for world peace.
    Among those present at the reception today were Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Wu Lengxi, President of the Xinhua News Agency, as well as people from the Beijing press circles and foreign journalists in Beijing.
    The heads of news agencies from Albania, Vietnam, Czechoslovakia and other countries who are visiting Beijing also attended today's reception.

### 庆祝德意志通讯社成立十周年  德意志通讯社社长在北京举行招待会

1956-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志通讯社成立十周年
    德意志通讯社社长在北京举行招待会
    据新华社8日讯　应邀来中国访问的德意志通讯社社长德芭·魏兰，今晚在北京举行招待会，庆祝德意志通讯社成立十周年。
    德芭·魏兰在会上致词，她说，今天在中国庆祝德意志通讯社成立十周年，愿和世界各国的新闻界合作，共同为世界和平而奋斗。
    出席今天招待会的，有人民日报总编辑邓拓、新华通讯社社长吴冷西以及北京新闻界人士、在京的外国记者等。
    正在北京访问的阿尔巴尼亚、越南、捷克斯洛伐克等国通讯社的负责人也出席了今天的招待会。
