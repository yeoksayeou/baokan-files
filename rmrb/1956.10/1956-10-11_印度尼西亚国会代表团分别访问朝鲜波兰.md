Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegations Visit North Korea and Poland Separately
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 9th: Over a thousand people from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a welcoming rally tonight to welcome Indonesian parliamentarians Supardi and Ibnu Parna on their visit to North Korea.

    Kim Ung-gi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, said in his welcoming speech that the visit of the two parliamentarians to North Korea would strengthen the friendship between the people of North Korea and Indonesia. He hoped that the two countries would conduct cultural exchanges in the future, and that artists and writers from both countries would visit each other.

    Supardi said in his speech that the Indonesian and North Korean people are both struggling for the unification of their motherlands. The Indonesian people want to strengthen their friendship with the North Korean people.

    Kim Doo-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, received Supardi and Ibnu Parna on the 8th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 10th: An Indonesian parliamentary delegation arrived in Warsaw from the Soviet Union on October 9th at the invitation of the Polish Parliament. (The Indonesian parliamentary delegation visited the Soviet Union from September 18th at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.)

    The head of the delegation is Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian National Party Parliamentary Group and member of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee. The delegation members include representatives from various political parties in the Indonesian Parliament.

    The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Polish Deputy Speaker of Parliament, Ozga-Michalski, and others.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团分别访问朝鲜波兰

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团分别访问朝鲜波兰
    据新华社平壤9日电　平壤市各界一千多人今天晚上举行欢迎大会，欢迎印度尼西亚国会议员苏帕迪和伊卜努·帕尔纳到朝鲜访问。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长金应基在会上致欢迎词说，两位议员的访问朝鲜将加强朝鲜和印度尼西亚两国人民间的友谊。他希望两国今后进行文化交流，两国的艺术家和作家互相访问。
    苏帕迪在会上讲话说，印度尼西亚人民和朝鲜人民都在为统一祖国而斗争。印度尼西亚人民要加强同朝鲜人民的友谊。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉在8日接见了苏帕迪和伊卜努·帕尔纳。
    据新华社沙华10日电　印度尼西亚国会代表团应波兰议会的邀请，10月9日由苏联来到华沙。（印度尼西亚国会代表团是应苏联最高苏维埃邀请，在9月18日到苏联访问的。）
    代表团团长是印度尼西亚国民党国会党团主席、国会外交委员会委员哈迪。代表团团员包括印度尼西亚国会中各个政党的代表。
    代表团在机场受到波兰议会副议长奥兹加—密哈尔斯基等人的欢迎。
