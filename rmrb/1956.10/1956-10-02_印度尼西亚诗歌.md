Indonesian Poetry (Three Poems)

Youth March

Blood, ah, blood, ah, blood!
On this bright red battle flag,
White, white stars are scattered in its center.
Red signifies: the motherland is in blood and flames!
White signifies: we have a pure conscience!
Stars signify: a just war is being waged,
The darkness of Sumatra, the sorrow of Ambon, are coming to an end.
We will defend this flag—
A new, happy hour
Will be born in our country burning with war!

Red Flower

Red flowers illuminate the road for us,
Their petals light up the East.
We know—the red light of happiness
Will dispel melancholy and sorrow.

	Everywhere, at all times, ruthless poverty
	Burns and tramples us.
	The soul is brave,
	The body is black,
	We live in darkness,
	Living in blackened shacks.
	In the eyes of the white man,
	We are less than dogs.
	Darkness makes us uncomfortable... in the night there is not even a glimmer of light:
	The executioner hands us over to the executioner again!
	Gold yuan rule the ancient Anru City,
	We cry and moan in pain…

	No, you foreigners, get out of here!
	We don't need you!
	The soul is brave,
	The body is black,
	Against the vicious enemy,
	We protested! …
	The strength in battle is often disproportionate,
	And the heart feels deeply pained -
	But sometimes as brave as a mountain eagle,
	A small bird is also fighting a peacock.
	Although this bird does not look pretty -
	The heart is shining!
	The soul is brave,
	The body is black,
	For the cause of justice
	We are fighting heroically!
	The giant stood up -
	The enemy is trembling with fear!
	Light pierced through the darkness!
Friends, do not be melancholy and sad!
Let waves of joy surge around,
Let them run to the left and right,
Like lava erupting from a volcano!

	Red flowers illuminate the road for us!
	What a bright red flower!
	The soul is brave,
	The body is black,
	We want to be free people,
	Equal to the white man!
	We will be victorious!
	The enemy will perish!
	Brothers! That is how it is!

Pearl Mountain

Comrades, look back at the battlefield—
Everywhere blood is flowing,
Countless rivers of blood…
My motherland!
I am with you wholeheartedly,
I have grown up,
I have become more severe…
	You are like the mother of soldiers, the sisters of soldiers,
	Gulong Sha Li - Pearl Mountain!
Blood soaked all my clothes,
But for you,
I will never spare my life,
My sacred motherland,
The birthplace of hope—
No country in the world
	Is more lovely than you!
	You will be free - the time will come -
	Gulong Sha Li - Pearl Mountain!

[Note] "Youth March" was written in 1946 when the Indonesian people's war against colonialism was in full swing. It is the battle song of the "People's Youth League," an organization that unites all progressive Indonesian youth. Ambon City is the capital of the Moluccas Islands located on Ambon Island.
"Red Flower" - This is a song against the rule of the Dutch colonialists in Indonesia. The red flower symbolizes the flag of the Indonesian national liberation struggle.
"Red Flower," "Youth March," and "Pearl Mountain" were all works sung by the Indonesian Youth Delegation at the Berlin Youth Festival in 1951.
These three poems are selected from "Song of Ordinary People" published by the Moscow State Publishing House.
(Translated by Zou Jiang)

### 印度尼西亚诗歌

1956-10-02
第8版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚诗歌　（三首）青年进行曲血啊，血啊，血啊！在这鲜红的战旗上，白色的，白色的星星满布在它的中央。鲜红表示着：祖国在血和火焰中！白色表示着：我们有纯洁的良心！星星表示着：正义的战争在进行，苏门答腊的昏暗，安汶城的悲痛在告终。我们要保卫这旗帜——新的，幸福的时辰啊，就要在燃烧着战火的我们的国土上诞生！
      红　花红花为我们照耀着道路，它的花瓣照亮了东方。我们知道——幸福的红光将要驱散忧郁和悲伤。
    随时随地，狠心的穷困
    将我们燃烧和蹂躏。
    灵魂是勇敢的，
    身体是黑色的，
    我们住在阴暗中，
    住着熏黑了的窝棚。
    在白种人眼里，
    我们比狗还不如。
    黑暗使我们难受……黑夜里连一线光明也没有：刽子手把我们又交给刽子手！金元统治着古老的安汝城，我们痛苦地哭泣和呻吟……
    不，外国佬，从这里滚开吧！
    我们不需要你们！
    灵魂是勇敢的，
    身体是黑色的，
    向着凶狠的敌人，
    我们提出了抗议！……战斗中力量往往很悬殊，心里啊深深地感到痛苦——但有时像山鹰一样勇敢，小鸟儿也在和孔雀交战。
    虽然这鸟儿看起来不漂亮——
    心儿却在发着光！
    灵魂是勇敢的，
    身体是黑色的，
    为了正义的事业
    我们在英勇地战斗！
    巨人站起来了——
    敌人吓得发颤！
    光明射穿了黑暗！朋友，不要忧郁和悲伤！让周围涌起欢乐的波浪，让它们向左右两边奔跑，就像火山口喷出的熔浆！
    红花给我们照耀着道路！
    多么鲜红的花！
    灵魂是勇敢的，
    身体是黑色的，
    我们要作自由人，
    和白人一样平等！
    我们将要胜利！
    敌人将要灭亡！
    兄弟们！就是这样！
        珍珠山
    同志啊，回头看一看战场
        吧——
    到处都在流着血，
    无数的血的河流……
    我的祖国啊！
    我全心全意地和你在一起，
    我长大成人了，
    我变得更严峻……
      你就像战士的母亲，战士的
          姊妹，
      古隆格·沙利——珍珠山！
    血液浸透了我所有的衣服，
    但是为了你，
    我决不吝惜生命，
    我的神圣的祖国啊，
    希望的诞生地——
    世界上没有什么国家
      比你更可爱！
      你将要自由——时间将会到
        来，——
      古隆格·沙利——珍珠山！
    【注】“青年进行曲”作于1946年印度尼西亚人民反殖民主义的战争正激烈的时候。这是团结一切进步的印度尼西亚青年的组织“人民青年团”的战歌。安汶城是位于安汶岛上的摩鹿加群岛的首府。
    “红花”——这是一首反对荷兰殖民主义者在印度尼西亚的统治的歌曲。红花象征着印度尼西亚民族解放斗争的旗子。
    “红花”“青年进行曲”和“珍珠山”都是印度尼西亚青年代表团1951年在柏林青年联欢节上演唱的作品。
    这三首诗选自莫斯科国家出版社印行的“平凡人之歌”。
        （邹　绛译）
