Title: Jordan National Socialist Party Leader Tasked with Forming Cabinet; Announces Party's Foreign Policy is Completely Aligned with Egypt

Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th dispatch from Amman: The caretaker cabinet of Jordan, headed by Ibrahim Hashem, resigned on October 27th. King Hussein of Jordan entrusted Suleiman Nabulsi, the General Secretary of the National Socialist Party, which won the most seats in the recent parliamentary elections, to form a new government that same day.

Nabulsi said that the task his party has put forward in terms of foreign policy is to be completely aligned with the foreign policy of Egypt.

### 约旦国家社会党领袖受命组阁  宣布该党外交政策同埃及完全一致

1956-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦国家社会党领袖受命组阁
    宣布该党外交政策同埃及完全一致
    新华社28日讯　安曼消息：以易卜拉欣·哈希姆为首的约旦看守内阁已经在10月27日辞职。约旦国王侯赛尼当天委托在最近议会选举中获得席位最多的国家社会党的总书记苏莱曼·纳布西组织新政府。
    纳布西说，他的党在外交政策方面所提出的任务，是同埃及的外交政策完全一致的。
