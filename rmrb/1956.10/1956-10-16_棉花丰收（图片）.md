Title: Bumper Cotton Harvest (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 3

    Cotton Harvest
    The cotton grown by the Wuxing Cooperative in Turpan, Xinjiang, has reached the target for the international competition. This is a scene of the cooperative members working hard to pick cotton. Photo by Huang Binying (Xinjiang Daily)

    The "Dai" cotton planted by the October Agricultural Cooperative in Xishui County, Hubei Province, has doubled its yield compared to last year due to various technical measures. The lint is long and the color is pure white, truly top quality. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning

    Jianyang County, Sichuan Province, promoted the experience of square grid seedling transplanting for cotton this year, resulting in a good cotton harvest. This is a scene of members of the Changzheng Agricultural Cooperative in Dongxi Township picking transplanted cotton. Xinhua News Agency reporter You Yungu

    A large amount of new cotton from Hubei Province has been put on the market. From late August to the end of September, over 300,000 dan of cotton have been put on the market in the province. This is new cotton being transported to Wuhan. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yan Zhennan

### 棉花丰收（图片）

1956-10-16
第3版()
专栏：

    棉花丰收
    新疆吐鲁番五星社种植的棉花，产量达到了参加国际竞赛指标。这是社员正在紧张的进行收花工作。　黄斌英摄（新疆日报社稿）
    湖北省浠水县十月农业社，今年种的“岱”字棉，经过各种技术措施，棉花比去年增产一倍，绒头长，色洁白，真是上品。　新华社记者　刘馨宁摄
    四川省简阳县今年推广了棉花方格育苗移栽的经验，棉花收成良好。这是东溪乡长征农业社的社员在采摘移栽的棉花。
                    新华社记者　游云谷摄
    湖北省新棉已大量上市。8月下旬到9月底止，全省上市棉花已达三十多万担。这是运到武汉市的新棉。　新华社记者　严震南摄
