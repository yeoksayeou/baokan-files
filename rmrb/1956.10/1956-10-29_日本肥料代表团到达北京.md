Title: Japanese Fertilizer Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th.

    The Japanese fertilizer delegation, consisting of 22 members led by head of delegation Shoji Seijiro, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China National Import and Export Corporation.
    Shoji Seijiro said upon arrival in Beijing that the delegation he leads represents all manufacturers in the Japanese fertilizer industry. Their mission is to introduce the general situation of the Japanese fertilizer industry to all sectors in China, and they hope to cooperate with China's actively developing agriculture in order to consolidate the friendly relations between China and Japan.
    Cao Zhongshu, manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and many others welcomed them at the airport.

### 日本肥料代表团到达北京

1956-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    日本肥料代表团到达北京
    新华社28日讯　应中国进出口公司邀请来中国访问的日本肥料代表团一行二十二人，由团长庄野精二郎率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    庄野精二郎在到达北京时说，他所率领的代表团是代表日本肥料业全部的厂商。他们的任务是为了向中国各方面介绍日本肥料工业的一般情况，并且希望与积极发展着的中国的农业合作，以期巩固中日两国友好关系。
    中国进出口公司经理曹中枢等多人到机场欢迎。
