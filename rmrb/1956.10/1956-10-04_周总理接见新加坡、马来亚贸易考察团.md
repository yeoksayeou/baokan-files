Title: Premier Zhou Receives Singapore, Malaya Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd October. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received the entire Singapore Chamber of Commerce Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Chamber of Commerce Trade Delegation this morning.

Present at the reception were Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Li Zhishen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. (Attached photo)

The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai talking with Mr. Ko Teck Kin (center), head of the Singapore Chamber of Commerce Trade Delegation, and Mr. Lee Yan Lian (left), head of the Federation of Malaya Chamber of Commerce Trade Delegation.

### 周总理接见新加坡、马来亚贸易考察团

1956-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见新加坡、马来亚贸易考察团
    新华社3日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天上午接见了新加坡工商业贸易考察团和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团全体人员。
    接见的时候在座的有中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘等。（附图片）
    图为周恩来总理和新加坡工商业贸易考察团团长高德根（中）、马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团团长李延年（左）交谈。
                                       新华社记者　吕厚民摄
