Title: National Industrial Sports Association Holds Football League

Author:

Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 2nd. The 1956 National Industrial Sports Association Football League began this afternoon in Wuhan.

Seventeen industrial sports associations' representative teams are participating in this league, with a total of 321 athletes. The representative teams of the chemical, metallurgical, construction, and commercial sports associations, which did not participate in the football competition at the First National Workers' Games last year, are participating in this league.

In the first preliminary match held today, the First Machinery representative team, which won first place in the football competition at the First National Workers' Games last year, drew with the coal mine team, whose technical level was not very high last year, with a score of 1-1.

The preliminary round is divided into three groups for round-robin matches, and then the top two teams from each group will participate in the finals. All forty preliminary matches are scheduled to end on October 10.

### 全国产业体育协会举行足球联赛

1956-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    全国产业体育协会举行足球联赛
    新华社武汉2日电　1956年全国产业体育协会足球联赛今天下午在武汉开始举行。
    参加这次联赛的有十七个产业体育协会的代表队，运动员共三百二十一人。去年在第一届全国工人体育运动大会上没有参加足球比赛的化学、冶金、建筑、商业等体育协会的代表队，这次参加了联赛。
    在今天举行的第一场预赛中，去年在第一届全国工人体育运动大会上获得足球比赛第一名的第一机械代表队，以一比一同去年技术水平还不高的煤矿队踢成平局。
    这次预赛分三组进行循环赛，然后每组各取前二名参加决赛。全部四十场预赛预定在10月10日结束。
