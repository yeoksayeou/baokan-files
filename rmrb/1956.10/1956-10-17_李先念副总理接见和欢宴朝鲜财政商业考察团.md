Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives and Hosts Korean Financial and Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Deputy Prime Minister Li Xiannian of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Korean Financial and Trade Delegation, headed by Chon Se-ung and deputy head Han Dae-young, and all members of the delegation this afternoon.

　　This evening, Deputy Prime Minister Li Xiannian held a banquet to welcome the Korean Financial and Trade Delegation.

　　The delegation of twelve arrived in Beijing on the 15th and will be visiting China for more than a month. The head of the delegation, Chon Se-ung, is the Vice Minister of Finance of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the deputy head, Han Dae-young, is the Deputy Director of the Commercial Finance and Cooperation Group Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party. The nine members of the delegation include responsible cadres from the fields of finance, banking, and commerce in Korea.

### 李先念副总理接见和欢宴朝鲜财政商业考察团

1956-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见和欢宴朝鲜财政商业考察团
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，16日下午接见了朝鲜财政商业考察团团长全世雄、副团长韩大英和全体团员。
    今天晚间，李先念副总理举行宴会，欢迎朝鲜财政商业考察团。
    考察团一行十二人是在15日到达北京的，将在中国作为期一个多月的访问。考察团团长全世雄是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国财政省副相，副团长韩大英是朝鲜劳动党中央委员会商业财政合作团体部副部长，团员九人包括朝鲜的财政、金融、商业等方面的负责干部。
