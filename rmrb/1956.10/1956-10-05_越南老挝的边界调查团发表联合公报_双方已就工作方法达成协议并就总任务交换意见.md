Title: Vietnam-Laos Boundary Survey Team Issues Joint Communiqué: Both Sides Reach Agreement on Working Methods and Exchange Views on General Tasks
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd. The Vietnam Democratic Republic and the Kingdom of Laos boundary survey delegations recently issued a joint communiqué stating that the two delegations have reached an agreement on future working methods and exchanged views on the overall tasks. Both sides believe that there are many similarities in their respective viewpoints.

These preliminary results were achieved at the first meeting between the two delegations held on September 29th in Nong Khang, on the Vietnam-Laos border. The joint communiqué said that the meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of sincerity, friendship, and mutual understanding. Both sides pledged to make every effort to fulfill the task entrusted to them by the two governments, which aims to promote the inherent good neighborly relations between Vietnam and Laos.

### 越南老挝的边界调查团发表联合公报  双方已就工作方法达成协议并就总任务交换意见

1956-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    越南老挝的边界调查团发表联合公报
    双方已就工作方法达成协议并就总任务交换意见
    新华社河内3日电　越南民主共和国和老挝王国的边界调查代表团最近发表联合公报说，两国代表团已经就今后的工作方法问题达成了协议，并且就总的任务交换了各自的观点。双方都认为，在彼此的观点中有着许多相同之处。
    这些初步的成果是9月29日两国代表团在越、老边界的农核举行的首次会议上取得的。联合公报说，这次会议是在真诚、友好和相互了解的气氛中进行的。双方都作了保证，要作出一切努力来完成两国政府所交给的目的在于促进越南和老挝之间固有的睦邻关系的任务。
