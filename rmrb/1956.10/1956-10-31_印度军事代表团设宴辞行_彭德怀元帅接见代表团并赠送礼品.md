Title: Indian Military Delegation Hosts Farewell Banquet; Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives the Delegation and Presents Gifts
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th:

    The Indian Military Goodwill Delegation to China, led by General Cho Na Choudhuri, held a farewell banquet at 7:00 PM tonight. In his speech at the reception, Choudhuri said: "We will be leaving China in three days, and we will return to our country with goodwill, friendship, and warm feelings." He particularly mentioned the special symposium held for them by senior Chinese military generals, saying: "Some of your views will be of great benefit to the continued development of our country's defense forces." He expressed great welcome for the upcoming visit of a Chinese military delegation to India. He said, "Allow me to quote an idiom from your country, 'First time strangers, second time friends'—we are friends at our first meeting, and we will be even closer at our second meeting." Defense Minister Marshal Peng Dehuai also spoke at the banquet. He said, "I thank the Indian Military Delegation for visiting China. Your visit has left a very good impression on the Chinese people." He said that a Chinese military delegation will visit India soon, which will make the friendship between the people and armies of the two countries even closer.
    Marshal Chen Yi, Senior General Chen Geng, and Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, also spoke at the banquet. They all spoke of the significant role of Sino-Indian friendship.
    Invited to the banquet tonight were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Marshal Chen Yi, Senior General Chen Geng, Senior General Xu Guangda, Senior General Li Da, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Chen Shiqu, Senior General Xiao Hua, and representatives from various military services.
    President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and Vice President Chen Hansheng, also attended tonight's banquet.
    Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, also attended today's banquet.
    Military attachés and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China also attended the banquet.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Peng Dehuai, received the Indian Military Goodwill Delegation to China again at 4:00 PM today and presented gifts. During the reception, Marshal Peng Dehuai had a cordial conversation with General Cho Na Choudhuri and the delegation members for one hour, exchanging views on many issues related to the armed forces of the two countries.
    Also present at the reception were Senior General Chen Geng, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Gan Siqi, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin.
    After the reception, Marshal Peng Dehuai presented gifts to all members of the Indian Military Goodwill Delegation, including Beijing cloisonné vases, marble stationery, Xiang embroidery, and picture albums of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, pictorials of the Liberation Army, and pictures of the delegation's activities in China. General Choudhuri and the delegation members expressed their gratitude to Marshal Peng Dehuai as they received the gifts one by one. Then, General Choudhuri presented Marshal Peng Dehuai with a gift in return, which was a handcrafted Indian cigarette box and a fruit plate. Choudhuri happily said to Marshal Peng Dehuai: "I hope the Marshal will often use these gifts when eating fruit and smoking cigarettes."

### 印度军事代表团设宴辞行  彭德怀元帅接见代表团并赠送礼品

1956-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    印度军事代表团设宴辞行
    彭德怀元帅接见代表团并赠送礼品
    新华社30日讯　印度军事友好访华代表团团长卓·纳·乔杜里将军今晚七时举行辞行宴会。乔杜里在酒会上致词说：三天以后我们就要离开中国了，我们将带着善意友好的热烈的感情回到我们的国家。他特别谈到中国高级军事将领为他们所举行的专门座谈会，他说：你们的有些看法对我国国防力量的继续发展将有重大好处。他对中国军事代表团即将访问印度表示极大的欢迎。他说，允许我引用你们国家的成语“一回生，两回熟”——我们初次见面是朋友，再次见面就更亲密。国防部长彭德怀元帅也在宴会上讲了话。他说，我感谢印度军事代表团访问中国，你们的访问对中国人民留下了很好的印象。他说不久中国军事代表团将到印度访问，这将使两国人民和军队间的友情更紧密的连结起来。
    陈毅元帅、陈赓大将和印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也在宴会上讲了话。他们都谈到了中印友好的重大作用。
    应邀出席今晚宴会的有彭德怀元帅、陈毅元帅、陈赓大将、许光达大将、李达上将、刘亚楼空军上将、甘泗淇上将、陈士榘上将、萧华上将以及各军种兵种的代表。
    中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙也出席了今晚的宴会。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也出席了今天的宴会。
    出席宴会的还有各国驻华武官和使馆人员。
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天下午四时再次接见印度军事友好访华代表团，并赠送礼品。接见时彭德怀元帅同卓·纳·乔杜里将军及团员们进行了一小时的亲切交谈；就两国军队方面的许多问题交换了意见。
    接见时在座的有陈赓大将、李达上将、甘泗淇上将、刘亚楼空军上将、萧向荣中将和外事处长朱开印大校。
    接见完毕后，彭德怀元帅向印度军事友好访华代表团全体团员赠送了礼品，礼品中有北京景泰蓝花瓶、大理石文具、湘绣和中国人民解放军画册、解放军画报及代表团在中国活动的图片等。乔杜里将军和团员们接受礼品时一一向彭德怀元帅致谢意。接着乔杜里将军回赠给彭德怀元帅一份礼品，礼品是印度手工艺品香烟盒和一个水果盘。乔杜里高兴地向彭德怀元帅说：“希望元帅在吃水果吸香烟的时候常常使用这些礼品。”
