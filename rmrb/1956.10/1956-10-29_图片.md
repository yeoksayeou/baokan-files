Date: 1956-10-29

    The Ganzhou Xihe Bridge, connecting the north and south highways of Jiangxi Province, is currently the largest newly built bridge in Jiangxi Province, with eight piers and seven spans, and a total length of 256.2 meters. The construction of this bridge is now nearing completion and is expected to be open to traffic by the end of the year.
        Photo by Jiang Tao (Manuscript from Ganzhou Dazhong Newspaper)
