Title: Part of the Delegation of the French and Far East Cultural Exchange Returned Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Serge Lafitte-Dupond, head of the French and Far East Cultural Exchange National Center Delegation, and some members of the delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Other members of the delegation will continue to visit various places.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on August 20 and visited Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Xi'an, Kunming, Anshan, Changchun and other places.

The delegation also participated in the National Day celebrations in Beijing.

### 法国和远东文化交流代表团部分人员回国

1956-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    法国和远东文化交流代表团部分人员回国
    新华社3日讯　法国和远东文化交流全国中心代表团团长塞日·拉斐特—杜邦和部分团员今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。其他的团员将继续在各地进行参观访问。
    代表团是在8月20日到达北京的，他们曾经在北京、上海、广州、杭州、南京、西安、昆明、鞍山、长春等地参观访问。
    代表团还曾经在北京参加了国庆节观礼。
