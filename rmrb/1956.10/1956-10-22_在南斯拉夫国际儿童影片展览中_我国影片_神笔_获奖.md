Title: Chinese Film "Magic Brush" Wins Award at Yugoslav International Children's Film Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5

    At the Yugoslav International Children's Film Exhibition
    Chinese Film "Magic Brush" Wins Award
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 21st: Belgrade dispatch: The first Yugoslav International Children's Film Exhibition closed on October 19th.
    The Chinese puppet film "Magic Brush" won the award for Outstanding Children's Film. Films from Yugoslavia, the Soviet Union, France, Czechoslovakia and other countries also won awards.

### 在南斯拉夫国际儿童影片展览中  我国影片“神笔”获奖

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    在南斯拉夫国际儿童影片展览中
    我国影片“神笔”获奖
    据新华社布拉格21日电　贝尔格莱德讯：南斯拉夫举办的第一届国际儿童影片展览已经在10月19日闭幕。
    中国木偶影片“神笔”获得优秀儿童电影奖。获奖的还有南斯拉夫、苏联、法国、捷克斯洛伐克等国的影片。
