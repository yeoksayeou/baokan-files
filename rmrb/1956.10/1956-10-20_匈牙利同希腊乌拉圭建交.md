Title: Hungary Establishes Diplomatic Relations with Greece and Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 17th. According to TASS Budapest, October 16th, Hungary, after successful negotiations with Greece and Uruguay, has reached an agreement on the establishment of diplomatic relations. Hungary will soon establish embassies in Athens and Montevideo.

### 匈牙利同希腊乌拉圭建交

1956-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利同希腊乌拉圭建交
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯16日讯，匈牙利同希腊、乌拉圭经过顺利的谈判后，已经达成了建立外交关系的协议。匈牙利最近就要在雅典和蒙得维的亚设立使馆。
