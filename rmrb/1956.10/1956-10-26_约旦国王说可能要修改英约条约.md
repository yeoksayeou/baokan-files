Title: Jordanian King Says Anglo-Jordanian Treaty May Be Revised
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, October 25th: King Hussein of Jordan said at a press conference on October 24th that Jordan may revise the Anglo-Jordanian treaty to make it more in line with the spirit of the times.

The Anglo-Jordanian Treaty of Alliance, signed in 1948 for a period of twenty years, grants Britain many privileges in Jordan. According to this treaty, Britain can station troops and use military bases on Jordanian territory. In the recent Jordanian parliamentary elections, many parties and non-party individuals have proposed to abolish or revise this treaty, and they hold a dominant position in the new parliament.

Hussein also said that Jordan believes that the Arab world is an inseparable whole, and its aim is to strive for complete independence and to serve humanity together with other free countries.

### 约旦国王说可能要修改英约条约

1956-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦国王说可能要修改英约条约
    据新华社25日讯　安曼消息：约旦国王侯赛尼10月24日在记者招待会上说，约旦很可能要修改英国—约旦条约，使它更符合时代精神。
    1948年签订的英国—约旦二十年同盟条约使英国在约旦享有很多特权，根据这个条约，英国可以在约旦领土上驻扎军队，使用军事基地。在最近举行的约旦议会选举中，很多党派和无党派人士都提出要废除或修改这个条约，他们在新议会中占优势。
    侯赛尼还说，约旦认为阿拉伯世界是一个不可分开的整体，它的目的是争取完全的独立并且同其他自由国家一起为人类服务。
