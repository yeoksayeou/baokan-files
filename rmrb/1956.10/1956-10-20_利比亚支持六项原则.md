Title: Libya Supports Six Principles
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 19th: Libyan Representative to the United Nations, Abidiayn, wrote to the Security Council on October 19th, clarifying the Libyan government's position on the Suez issue.

    The letter stated that Libya supports the Security Council's resolution of October 13th on the six principles for resolving the Suez dispute.

    Abidiayn's letter condemned the deployment of troops by Britain and France in the Eastern Mediterranean and the imposition of economic pressure on Egypt, and pointed out that the measures taken by Britain and France are a threat to peace in the Middle East and the world.

### 利比亚支持六项原则

1956-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    利比亚支持六项原则
    据新华社19日讯　纽约消息：利比亚驻联合国代表阿比迪扬10月19日写信给安全理事会，阐明利比亚政府对苏伊士问题的立场。
    这封信表示，利比亚支持安全理事会10月13日通过的关于解决苏伊士争端的六项原则的决议。
    阿比迪扬的信谴责英法两国在东地中海集结军队和对埃及施行经济压力，并且指出英法两国采取的措施是对中东和世界和平的威胁。
