Title: “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe Concludes Performances in China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 11th. The Romanian “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe held its closing ceremony for its performance tour in China on the evening of the 10th in Nanning, Guangxi Province.

	Guangxi Province Vice Governor Qin Yingji delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People’s Republic of China and the Guangxi Provincial People’s Committee at the closing ceremony, congratulating the “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe on its successful performances in China.

	The head of the “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe, Yani Nicolae, then delivered a speech. He said that during the four months of their stay in China, the "Ciocîrlia" Folk Dance and Music Troupe had been warmly received and fraternally cared for by your great people, which would be engraved in their hearts and never forgotten. Our visit and performances have provided a good opportunity for artists from China and Romania to exchange experiences. Finally, Yani Nicolae wished the cultural relations between China and Romania would become increasingly prosperous.

	Before the closing ceremony, the “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe held a farewell performance, and 1,600 spectators enjoyed their last performance in China. During its four-month stay in China, the “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe visited and performed in seventeen cities, including Beijing, Shenyang, Xi'an, Chongqing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Nanning, giving sixty-two performances to an audience of 247,000 people.

	After concluding its visit and performances in China, the “Ciocîrlia” Folk Dance and Music Troupe will travel to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for a performance tour.

### “云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团结束在我国演出

1956-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    “云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团结束在我国演出
    据新华社南宁11日电　罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团，10日晚上在广西省南宁市举行在中国访问演出的闭幕式。
    广西省副省长覃应机代表中华人民共和国文化部和广西省人民委员会在闭幕式上致词，对“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团在中国演出的成功表示祝贺。
    “云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团团长亚尼·尼古拉接着致词。他说，在中国停留的四个月中，“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团蒙受到你们伟大人民的热情接待和兄弟般的关怀，这将铭刻在我们心中，永志不忘。我们的访问演出，给予了中罗两国艺术家以交流经验的良好机会。亚尼·尼古拉团长最后祝中罗两国的文化关系日益繁荣昌盛。
    在闭幕式举行前，“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团举行了告别演出，一千六百名观众欣赏了他们在中国最后的一场演出。“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团在中国四个月期间，曾先后在北京、沈阳、西安、重庆、上海、广州和南宁等十七个城市访问演出六十二场，观众达二十四万七千人。
    “云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团在结束中国访问演出后，将前往越南民主共和国进行访问演出。
