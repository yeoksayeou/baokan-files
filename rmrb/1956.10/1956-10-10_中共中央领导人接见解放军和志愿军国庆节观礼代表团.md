Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CPC Receive National Day Observance Delegation of the PLA and the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De; and Peng Dehuai, member of the Central Political Bureau and Minister of National Defense, received the National Day Observance Delegation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the National Day Observance Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: General Chen Geng, General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Xiao Ke, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Liu Yalou, and Senior General Yang Chengwu.

### 中共中央领导人接见解放军和志愿军国庆节观礼代表团

1956-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见解放军和志愿军国庆节观礼代表团
    新华社9日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，中央政治局委员、国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天下午接见了中国人民解放军国庆节观礼代表团和中国人民志愿军国庆节观礼代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、萧克上将、萧华上将、刘亚楼上将、杨成武上将。
