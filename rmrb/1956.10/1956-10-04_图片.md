Title: Pictures

Author: 

Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

People from all walks of life in Beijing held a rally at the Beijing Xianongtan Stadium on October 3 to welcome President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia. The picture shows the venue of the meeting. Our newspaper Wang Zhiyuan photographed.

President Sukarno at the meeting. Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.

Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing, presents a flag to President Sukarno on behalf of the people of Beijing. Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.

Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling held a family banquet on the evening of October 3 to entertain Indonesian President Sukarno. The picture shows Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling (right) accompanying President Sukarno through the garden towards the living room. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue photographed.

The youths of Beijing who participated in the meeting cheered warmly for President Sukarno. Our newspaper Wang Zhiyuan photographed.

President Sukarno, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited the Forbidden City on October 3. The picture shows President Sukarno looking at the Qing Dynasty emperor's toy "automatic magic trick." Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.

Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception on October 3 after the performance of the Indonesian Bali Art Friendship Delegation to entertain Indonesian artists. Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.
