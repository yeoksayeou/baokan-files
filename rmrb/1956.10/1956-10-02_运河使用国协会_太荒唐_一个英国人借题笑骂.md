Title: "The 'Canal Users Association' is Absurd": A Briton Mocks the Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 28th: The "Manchester Guardian" published a letter from a Briton named Pilkington. Pilkington said in his letter that he and several of his friends intend to establish the Kennet-Avon "Canal Users Association." This canal was nationalized by the British government in 1948.

This short canal connects the River Thames near Reading to the River Avon south of Stratford, and is a place for suburban residents to enjoy boating.

Pilkington's letter says, "In the past, when the British government nationalized this canal, it did not obtain my permission in advance, and did not even seek my opinion, which made me deeply indignant, because I am a user of the Kennet-Avon Canal."

Because Western countries unreasonably demand "international control" over the Suez Canal, this British-style joke is particularly pungent and interesting.

### “运河使用国协会”太荒唐  一个英国人借题笑骂

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    “运河使用国协会”太荒唐
     一个英国人借题笑骂
    据新华社28日讯　塔斯社伦敦28日讯：“曼彻斯特卫报”刊登了英国人皮尔金顿的一封信。皮尔金顿在信中说，他和他的几个朋友打算建立肯内特—亚冯“运河使用者协会”。这条运河在1948年被英国政府收归国有。
　　这条不长的运河把里丁附近的泰晤士河和斯特拉福以南的亚冯河连接起来，是郊区居民划船闲游的地方。　　皮尔金顿的信说，“过去英国政府把这条运河收归国有的时候，事先没有获得我的许可，甚至没有征求我的意见，这使我深感愤慨，因为我是肯内特—亚冯运河的使用者。”　　
    由于西方国家无理地要求对苏伊士运河建立“国际管制”，这个英国风味的笑话就特别辛辣有趣。
