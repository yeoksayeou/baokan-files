Title: German Academy of Sciences Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The entire delegation of the German Academy of Sciences left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chinese scientists went to the airport to see the delegation off.
    Daecks, First Secretary of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 德国科学院代表团回国

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    德国科学院代表团回国
    据新华社25日讯　德国科学院代表团全体人员今天早晨坐飞机离开北京回国。
    中国科学院副院长陶孟和，以及中国的科学家们到机场欢送代表团。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书戴克斯也到机场送行。
