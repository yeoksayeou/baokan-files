Title: Soviet Water Conservancy Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The Soviet Water Conservancy Delegation, led by Academician Askochensky, Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 26th to return home after participating in the scientific and technological exchange conference on the new construction and renovation of irrigation systems.
    The Water Conservancy Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will also leave Beijing in the coming days to visit Anhui, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places before returning home.

### 苏联水利代表团回国

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联水利代表团回国
    据新华社讯　由苏联农业部副部长阿斯科琴斯基院士率领的苏联水利代表团，在参加灌溉系统新建改建科学技术交流会议以后，26日上午乘飞机离北京回国。
    越南民主共和国水利代表团也将在日内离北京，前往安徽、上海、杭州等地参观后回国。
