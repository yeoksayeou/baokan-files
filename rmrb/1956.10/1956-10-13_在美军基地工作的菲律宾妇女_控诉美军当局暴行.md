Title: Philippine Women Working at US Military Base Accuse US Military Authorities of Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 12th, TASS, New York, October 11th: Forty-eight Philippine women working at the U.S. Naval Base in Olongapo, Zambales Province, said in a joint letter to President Magsaysay that Filipinos working at U.S. bases "are often subjected to various illegal treatments."

The letter, published in the "Manila Chronicle," pointed out that U.S. military authorities carry out illegal arrests and searches of Filipinos, and also use violence.

### 在美军基地工作的菲律宾妇女  控诉美军当局暴行

1956-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    在美军基地工作的菲律宾妇女
    控诉美军当局暴行
    据新华社12日讯　塔斯社纽约11日讯：在三描礼示省乌朗牙坡美国海军基地工作的四十八名菲律宾妇女在致麦格赛赛总统的联名信上说，在美国基地工作的菲律宾人“经常受到各种非法的待遇”。
    这封刊载在“马尼拉纪事报”上的信指出，美军当局对菲律宾人进行非法的逮捕和搜查，并且还使用暴力。
