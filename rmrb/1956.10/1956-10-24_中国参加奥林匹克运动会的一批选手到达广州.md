Title: A Group of Chinese Athletes Participating in the Olympic Games Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 23rd: A group of Chinese athletes participating in this Olympic Games, including members of the gymnastics team, weightlifting team, and basketball team, have arrived in Guangzhou in batches on the 16th, 21st, and 22nd of this month.
　　When these athletes arrived in Guangzhou, they were warmly welcomed by responsible persons from the Guangzhou Municipal Sports Commission, responsible persons from the Guangzhou Sports Club, and sports workers from Guangzhou.

### 中国参加奥林匹克运动会的一批选手到达广州

1956-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国参加奥林匹克运动会的一批选手到达广州
    据新华社广州23日电　中国参加本届奥林匹克运动会的一批选手，包括体操队、举重队和篮球队队员已在本月16日、21日和22日分批到达广州。
    这批选手到达广州时，受到了广州市体育运动委员会负责人、广州市体育俱乐部负责人以及广州市体育工作者的热烈欢迎。
