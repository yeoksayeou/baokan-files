Title: Ambassador Ragab Hosts Banquet for the Exhibition of Egyptian Art in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: Egyptian Ambassador to China, Hassan Ragab, hosted a banquet tonight to celebrate the opening of the Egyptian Art Exhibition in Beijing.

    Ambassador Ragab said at the banquet, "I hope that the cultural exchange between our two friendly peoples will continue to develop and flourish, which will have the greatest impact on consolidating the relations between the two countries in all aspects."

    Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing said in his speech, "The artistic talent and wisdom of the great Egyptian people, as well as the sincere desire for freedom, a happy life, and world peace expressed in their works, deeply attract us. Undoubtedly, we will learn a lot from this exhibition."

    Among those attending the banquet tonight were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations, and more than 200 artists from the capital.

    After the banquet, a documentary film about the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company and the Egyptian port of Alexandria was shown.

    Before the banquet tonight, Mr. Mustafa and Mr. Sherif, Egyptian artists in charge of the Egyptian Art Exhibition, held a press conference to introduce the development of Egyptian art to the press in the capital.

### 拉加卜大使为埃及艺术展览会在北京展出举行宴会

1956-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    拉加卜大使为埃及艺术展览会在北京展出举行宴会
    据新华社17日讯　埃及驻华大使哈桑·拉加卜今晚为埃及艺术展览会在北京展出举行宴会。
    拉加卜大使在宴会上说，我希望两个友好人民之间的文化交流继续发展，日益繁荣，这对于巩固两国之间在各方面的关系是会有最大的影响的。
    文化部部长沈雁冰致词说，伟大的埃及人民的艺术天才和智慧以及表现在作品中的热烈要求自由，幸福生活和世界和平的善良愿望深深地吸引了我们。无疑地我们将从这次展览会学到很多东西。
    出席今晚宴会的有国务院副总理贺龙，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长章汉夫，各部部长、副部长，各民主党派和人民团体负责人以及首都艺术家等二百多人。
    宴会结束后，放映了关于苏伊士运河公司国有化和埃及亚历山大港的纪录影片。
    今晚的宴会开始以前，负责埃及艺术展览会的埃及艺术家穆斯塔法先生和舍里夫先生曾举行记者招待会，向首都新闻界介绍埃及艺术事业发展的情况。
