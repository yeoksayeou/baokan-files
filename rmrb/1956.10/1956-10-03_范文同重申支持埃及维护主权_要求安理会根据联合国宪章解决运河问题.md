Title: Phạm Văn Đồng Reaffirms Support for Egypt in Maintaining Sovereignty, Demands Security Council Resolve Canal Issue According to UN Charter
Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 2nd. Vietnamese Democratic Republic Minister of Foreign Affairs Phạm Văn Đồng issued a statement this afternoon on the Suez Canal issue, demanding that the United Nations Security Council take appropriate measures on the Suez issue, in accordance with the spirit of the United Nations Charter, that are in line with the interests of the Egyptian people and international peace and cooperation.

The statement reiterated that the Government of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's position on the Suez issue is: fully support Egypt's decision to nationalize the Suez Canal Company, and believes that this issue must be resolved through peaceful negotiation on the basis of respecting Egyptian national sovereignty, while ensuring the freedom of navigation of the Suez Canal.

The Government of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic believes that military preparations aimed at attacking Egypt must be stopped, as these military preparations threaten world peace.

In the statement, Phạm Văn Đồng also said that the Government of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic warmly welcomes the proposals for a peaceful resolution of the Suez issue put forward by the Soviet Union, China, India, Indonesia and other Asian and African countries. At the same time, the Government of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic warmly supports the proposal made by the Government of the Republic of Egypt on September 9th and 10th, 1956 to establish a negotiating body and convene an international conference.

### 范文同重申支持埃及维护主权  要求安理会根据联合国宪章解决运河问题

1956-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    范文同重申支持埃及维护主权
    要求安理会根据联合国宪章解决运河问题
    新华社河内2日电　越南民主共和国外交部长范文同今天下午就苏伊士运河问题发表声明，要求联合国安全理事会根据联合国宪章的精神，对苏伊士问题采取符合埃及人民和国际和平与合作的利益的适当措施。
    声明重申越南民主共和国政府对苏伊士问题所采取的立场是：完全支持埃及把苏伊士运河公司收归国有的决定，并且认为必须在尊重埃及国家主权的基础上，通过和平协商的方式解决这个问题，同时保证苏伊士运河通航的自由。
    越南民主共和国政府认为，目的在于进攻埃及的军事准备必须停止，因为这些军事准备威胁世界和平。
    范文同在声明中又说，越南民主共和国政府热烈欢迎苏联、中国、印度、印度尼西亚和亚非其他国家所提出的和平解决苏伊士问题的建议。同时，越南民主共和国政府热烈支持埃及共和国政府在1956年9日10日提出的建立谈判机构和召开国际会议的建议。
