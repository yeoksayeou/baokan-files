Title: Most of the Oil Used by European Countries is Transported Through Suez
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 9th: A large portion of the petroleum products used by European countries is transported through the Suez Canal.

According to the statistical bulletin released by the Egyptian Customs on October 8th: In 1955-56, 56% of the petroleum products used by the United Kingdom were transported through the Suez Canal. For France, it even reached 84%. For Italy, it was 41%, Belgium 30%, the Netherlands 35%, and West Germany 13% through the Suez Canal.

### 欧洲国家使用的石油大部分经过苏伊士运输

1956-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    欧洲国家使用的石油大部分经过苏伊士运输
    新华社9日讯　开罗消息：欧洲国家使用的石油产品有很大一部分是通过苏伊士运河运输的。
    据埃及海关的统计公报10月8日公布：在1955—56年，英国使用的石油产品有56％是通过苏伊士运河运输的。法国的甚至达到84％。意大利有41％，比利时有30％，荷兰有35％，西德有13％是通过苏伊士运河的。
