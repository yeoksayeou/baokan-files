Title: "Suez Canal Users Association" Sets Up Fund to Facilitate Illegal Activities
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th, London Dispatch: The "Suez Canal Users Association" has decided to establish a fund of 50,000 pounds for administrative expenses, in order to facilitate the "Association" to begin operations. It was announced on the 26th that the United States and Britain will each bear 20% of the "Association's" expenses in the future, and France will bear 15%. Other countries will pay the "Association" according to their "interests and ability to pay."

### “运河使用国协会”设置便于非法活动的基金

1956-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    “运河使用国协会”设置便于非法活动的基金
    新华社28日讯　伦敦消息：“苏伊士运河使用国协会”决定设置一笔五万英镑的基金作为行政费用，以便于“协会”开始行动。据26日宣布，在今后“协会”的费用中，美国和英国各负担20％，法国负担15％。其他国家按照它们的“利益和支付能力”向“协会”付费。
