Title: State Council Holds 38th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The State Council held its 38th plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 10th.

　　After listening to the explanation by Wu Yuzhang, Director of the Chinese Language Reform Committee, the meeting decided to establish a Committee for the Review and Approval of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, and adopted the list of members of this committee.

　　After listening to the explanation by Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture, the meeting approved the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. After listening to the explanation by Burhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art delegation, the meeting approved the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and Government of the Syrian Republic. The above two agreements will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

　　After listening to the explanation by Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister of National Defense, the meeting adopted the State Council's order on conscription for 1956.

　　After listening to the explanation by Zhou Xing, Vice Minister of Public Security, the meeting adopted the Regulations of the People's Police of the People's Republic of China (Draft) and the Regulations on Police Ranks of the People's Republic of China (Draft), and submitted these two regulations (Draft) to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval.

　　The meeting adopted a proposal to amend certain provisions of the Marriage Law of the People's Republic of China and submitted it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for decision.

　　The meeting adopted the State Council's decision to abolish the Dorbod Banner in Heilongjiang Province and establish the Dorbod Mongolian Autonomous County; the State Council's decision to abolish Chengbu County in Hunan Province and establish the Chengbu Miao Autonomous County; and the State Council's decision to abolish Gongshan County in Yunnan Province and establish the Gongshan Dulong and Nu Autonomous County.

　　The meeting also approved several appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第三十八次会议

1956-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第三十八次会议
    新华社10日讯　国务院全体会议在十日下午举行第三十八次会议。
    会议在听取了中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章的说明后，决定成立汉语拼音方案审订委员会，并且通过了这个委员会的名单。
    会议在听取了文化部副部长钱俊瑞的说明后，核准了中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟文化合作协定。在听取了中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉的说明后，核准了中华人民共和国政府、叙利亚共和国政府文化合作协定。以上两个协定将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审核批准。
    会议在听取了国防部副部长黄克诚的说明后，通过了国务院关于1956年度征兵的命令。
    会议在听取了公安部副部长周兴的说明后，通过了中华人民共和国人民警察条例（草案）和中华人民共和国人民警察警衔条例（草案），并将这两个条例（草案）提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准。
    会议通过了关于修改中华人民共和国婚姻法部分条文的议案，并提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定。
    会议通过了国务院关于撤销黑龙江省杜尔伯特旗、设置杜尔伯特蒙古族自治县的决定，国务院关于撤销湖南省城步县、设置城步苗族自治县的决定，国务院关于撤销云南省贡山县、设置贡山独龙族怒族自治县的决定。
    会议还通过任免事项多起。
