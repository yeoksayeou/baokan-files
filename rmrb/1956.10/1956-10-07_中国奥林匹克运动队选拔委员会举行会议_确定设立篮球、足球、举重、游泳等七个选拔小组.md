Title: Chinese Olympic Team Selection Committee Holds Meeting, Determines Establishment of Seven Selection Groups for Basketball, Football, Weightlifting, Swimming, etc.
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Chinese Olympic Team Selection Committee held its first meeting today.

    Rong Gaotang, Director of the Selection Committee, briefed the committee members present on the preparatory work of the Selection Committee. The meeting also decided to establish seven selection groups under the Selection Committee for basketball, football, weightlifting, swimming, gymnastics, track and field, and shooting.

    This Selection Committee is composed of thirty-two representatives from the Sports Commission, the All-China Sports Federation , the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Central National Defense Sports Club, Hong Kong, Macao, and well-known figures in the sports field. They will begin various specific selection tasks from the 7th.

    The Selection Committee has one director, six deputy directors, and twenty-five committee members. Among the twenty-five committee members are veteran basketball player Mu Zuoyun, a participant in the 11th Olympic Games held in Berlin in 1936, sprint expert Liu Changchun, and basketball international referee Shu Hong, among others.

    Some committee members who are on their way to Beijing did not attend today's meeting.

### 中国奥林匹克运动队选拔委员会举行会议  确定设立篮球、足球、举重、游泳等七个选拔小组

1956-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中国奥林匹克运动队选拔委员会举行会议
    确定设立篮球、足球、举重、游泳等七个选拔小组
    新华社6日讯　中国奥林匹克运动队选拔委员会今天召开了第一次会议。
    选拔委员会主任荣高棠向到会的委员们介绍了选拔委员会的筹备工作情况。会上还确定在选拔委员会下设立篮球、足球、举重、游泳、体操、田径和射击七个选拔小组。
    这个选拔委员会是由体育运动委员会、中华全国体育总会、中国人民解放军、中华全国总工会、青年团中央委员会、中央国防体育俱乐部、香港和澳门等单位的代表，以及体育界的知名人士共三十二人组成的。他们将从7日开始进行各项具体选拔工作。
    选拔委员会设主任一人、副主任六人和二十五名委员。在二十五名委员当中，包括曾经参加过1936年在柏林举行的第十一届奥林匹克运动会的篮球老将牟作云、短跑名手刘长春和篮球国际裁判舒鸿等人。
    部分正在来北京途中的委员没有参加今天的会议。
