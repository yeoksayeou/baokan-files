Title: Zhu Kezhen Hosts Banquet for Pakistani Scientific Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Pakistani scientific delegation.

The delegation, led by Dr. Bashir Ahmad, was invited to attend the banquet.

Kaiser, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

Zhu Kezhen said in his speech at the banquet: The Chinese scientific community is very pleased to welcome the arrival of the Pakistani scientific delegation. Zhu Kezhen hoped that Pakistani friends in the scientific community could offer criticism of the shortcomings in China's science, culture, and education. He said: We will regard this criticism as sincere support for the Chinese people.

In his speech at the banquet, Dr. Bashir Ahmad stated that the delegation brought with them the sincere friendship of three Pakistani scientific organizations towards the Chinese people and scientists. Dr. Ahmad said that Pakistani scientists are very eager to see the great construction of New China. Your construction reflects the aspirations and strength of the Asian people. It is necessary for Pakistan and China to help and cooperate with each other to build their countries and safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

After Dr. Bashir Ahmad's speech, he presented gifts to the Chinese Academy of Sciences on behalf of the Pakistani scientific community.

Kaiser also spoke at the banquet.

Among those present tonight were Luo Jingyi, Secretary-General of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, Ma Jian, a member of the China Islamic Association, as well as many Chinese scientists and professors.

### 竺可桢欢宴巴基斯坦科学代表团

1956-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    竺可桢欢宴巴基斯坦科学代表团
    新华社30日讯　中国科学院副院长竺可桢今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎巴基斯坦科学代表团。
    以巴希尔·艾哈迈德博士为首的代表团人员应邀出席了宴会。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使馆临时代办凯瑟和他的夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    竺可桢在宴会上致词说：中国科学界十分愉快地欢迎巴基斯坦科学代表团的到来。竺可桢希望巴基斯坦科学界的朋友，能对中国科学和文化教育等工作中的缺点提出批评。他说：我们将把这种批评看作是对中国人民真诚的支持。
    巴希尔·艾哈迈德博士在宴会上致词时表示，代表团这次带来了巴基斯坦三个科学组织对中国人民和科学家的诚挚友谊。艾哈迈德博士说，巴基斯坦的科学家们很希望看到新中国的伟大建设，你们的建设反映了亚洲人民的愿望和力量，巴、中两国有必要互助合作，以建设国家，并保卫亚洲和世界和平。
    巴希尔·艾哈迈德博士致词以后，代表巴基斯坦科学界向中国科学院赠送了礼品。
    凯瑟在宴会上也讲了话。
    今天晚上出席作陪的有中国巴基斯坦友好协会总干事罗静宜，中国伊斯兰教协会委员马坚，以及中国的科学家、教授多人。
