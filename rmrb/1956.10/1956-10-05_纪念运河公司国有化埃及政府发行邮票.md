Title: Egyptian Government Issues Stamps to Commemorate Nationalization of Suez Canal Company
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 4th. The Egyptian government has issued one million stamps commemorating the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company. Egyptian authorities say they have received requests from philatelists around the world, including Britain, to purchase these stamps.

### 纪念运河公司国有化埃及政府发行邮票

1956-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念运河公司国有化埃及政府发行邮票
    新华社4日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府发行的纪念苏伊士运河公司国有化的邮票，已经发行了一百万张。埃及当局说，他们收到了包括英国在内的全世界各国的许多集邮家所提出的购买这种邮票的要求。
