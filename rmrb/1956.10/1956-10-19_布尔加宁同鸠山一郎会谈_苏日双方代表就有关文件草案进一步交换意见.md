Title: Bulganin Meets with Hatoyama Ichiro; Soviet and Japanese Representatives Further Exchange Views on Draft Documents
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: TASS, Moscow - Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin and Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan met with Japanese Prime Minister Hatoyama Ichiro and Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Kono Ichiro on October 17th.

The meeting discussed issues related to the restoration of normal diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and Japan.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: TASS, Moscow - Soviet Plenipotentiary Gromyko and Japanese Plenipotentiary Matsumoto Shunichi continued their meeting on October 17th in the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs conference hall. At the meeting, they further exchanged views on draft documents.

Soviet and Japanese advisors and experts also attended the meeting.

### 布尔加宁同鸠山一郎会谈  苏日双方代表就有关文件草案进一步交换意见

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁同鸠山一郎会谈
    苏日双方代表就有关文件草案进一步交换意见
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在10月17日同日本首相鸠山一郎和农林相河野一郎举行会谈。
    会谈讨论了有关恢复苏联和日本之间的正常外交关系的问题。
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联全权代表葛罗米柯和日本全权代表松本俊一在10月17日继续在苏联外交部会议厅举行会议。会上就有关文件的草案进一步交换了意见。
    苏日双方的顾问和专家也出席了会议。
