Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Cultural Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received the entire Japanese Cultural Delegation to China this morning.

    A short reception was held at the time of the meeting.

    Those present at the reception included Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and heads of various literary and art groups.

### 郭沫若接见日本文化人士访华团

1956-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本文化人士访华团
    新华社9日讯　中国文学艺术界联合会主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，今天上午接见了日本文化人士访华团全体人员。
    接见的时候举行了简短的酒会。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙和各文艺团体负责人。
