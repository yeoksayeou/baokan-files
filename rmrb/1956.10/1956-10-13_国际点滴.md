　　International Snippets

　　Unsuitable "Aid"
　　Some time ago, the Philippines accepted U.S. military equipment as aid, but this military equipment remains untouched in the Philippine Department of National Defense warehouse. Why is this? People in the Philippine Department of National Defense said that the U.S. military equipment sent under the Mutual Defense Assistance Treaty is not the type needed for Philippine defense.

　　The Application of the Term "Defense"
　　Britain and some Western European countries selling arms to Latin America has aroused dissatisfaction in Washington.
　　U.S. Congressman Porter Hardy, who once inspected Latin America, said in Washington that such purchases are "detrimental to the defense of the Americas."

　　"Consultants" Who Instigate Crime
　　Recently, American television has come up with a novel so-called "love consultant" program, which is performed by two or three grotesque men and women. This performance is not only pornographic and boring but also teaches people to commit crimes. For example, a woman asks: "I have a lover who loves me very much, but I don't want to embarrass my husband, what should I do?" Then the "consultant" answers: "Find a gun" to solve it.

### 国际点滴

1956-10-13
第6版()
专栏：国际点滴

    国际点滴
    不合需要的“援助”
    不久以前，菲律宾接受了美国军事装备的援助，但是这些军事装备至今仍原封不动地放在菲律宾国防部仓库里。这是为什么呢？菲律宾国防部人士说，根据共同防御援助条约送来的美国军事装备都不合乎菲律宾防务所需要的那种类型。
    “防务”一词的应用
    英国和部分西欧国家把军火卖给拉丁美洲的事，引起了华盛顿的不满。
    曾经在拉丁美洲视察的美国国会议员波特·哈丁在华盛顿说，这类购买“有害于美洲的防务”。
    教唆犯罪的“顾问”
    近来，美国电视又别出心裁搞了个所谓“恋爱顾问”的新节目，这个节目是由两三个妖形怪状的男女来表演。这种表演不仅色情无聊，而且教人犯罪。例如一个女的问：“我有一个情人，他很爱我，但，我又不想使我的丈夫难堪，应该怎么办？”接着那个“顾问”就回答说：“找柄手枪”来解决。
