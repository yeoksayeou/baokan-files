Title: Belgian Peace Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Belgian Peace Delegation left Beijing for home on the 3rd. During their one-month stay in China, they visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and other places. They also participated in the National Day celebrations in Beijing.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Xu Baoju, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and people from all walks of life, including Qian Duansheng, Yue Songsheng, Wu Zuoren, and many others.

### 比利时和平代表团离京返国

1956-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时和平代表团离京返国
    新华社3日讯　比利时和平代表团在3日离京返国。他们在我国逗留了一个月，先后访问了北京、沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、上海、杭州、广州等地，并在北京参加了国庆观礼。
    今天在机场欢送他们的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员许宝驹，和各界人士钱端升、乐松生、吴作人等多人。
