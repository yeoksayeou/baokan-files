Title: Xinjiang Agricultural Production Corps Sets New Record for High Cotton Yield
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi -- The Xue Zhanchun group of the Third Company of a certain unit of the Xinjiang Military Region Agricultural Production and Construction Corps has created a new national record for high cotton yield. The three mu and eight fen four li of high-yield cotton fields managed by this group yielded an average of 1,677.16 jin of seed cotton per mu, which is 284.3 jin higher than the national cotton high-yield record created last year by the cotton planting platoon led by Liu Xuefo, a cotton planting expert of the Xinjiang Military Region.

Xue Zhanchun and his team members Yang Laisheng, Xie Fu'en, and Wang Shixing planted 100 mu of Soviet "611-Bo" cotton variety this year at Anjihai Farm in the Shihezi Reclamation Area, with an average actual yield of more than 644 jin of seed cotton per mu. The three mu of high-yield cotton fields had an average of 7,274 cotton plants per mu, with an average of 25.6 cotton bolls per plant, and the average weight of cotton lint in each boll reached 4.5 grams.

### 新疆农业生产部队创棉花高额丰产新纪录

1956-10-30
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆农业生产部队创棉花高额丰产新纪录
    据新华社乌鲁木齐电　中国人民解放军新疆军区农业生产建设部队某部三连薛占春小组，创造了棉花高额丰产的全国新纪录。这个小组所管理的三亩八分四厘丰产棉田，平均每亩实收籽棉一千六百七十七点一六斤，比新疆军区植棉能手刘学佛领导的植棉排去年创造的全国棉花丰产纪录高出二百八十四点三斤。
    薛占春和他的组员杨来生、谢富恩、王世兴，今年在石河子垦区安集海农场种植苏联“六一一波”种棉花一百亩，平均每亩实收籽棉六百四十四斤多。三亩多高额丰产棉田，平均每亩有棉株七千二百七十四株，平均每株结棉铃二十五点六个，每个棉铃中的棉丝平均重达四点五公分。
