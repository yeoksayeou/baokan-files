Title: Romanian Delegation Leaves Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 8th. The delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, concluded its visit to Mongolia and left Ulaanbaatar by plane this morning.
　　Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Shirendyb, and Dugersuren, went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.
　　Diplomatic envoys of various countries to Mongolia also went to the airport to see them off.

### 罗代表团离开乌兰巴托

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗代表团离开乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团结束了在蒙古的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开乌兰巴托。
    蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、锡林迪布和杜格苏伦等到机场欢送贵宾。
    各国驻蒙古的使节也到机场送行。
