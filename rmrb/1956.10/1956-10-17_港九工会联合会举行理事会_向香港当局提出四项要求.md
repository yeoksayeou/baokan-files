Title: Hong Kong and Kowloon Trades Union Council Holds Council Meeting, Presents Four Demands to Hong Kong Authorities
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th - Hong Kong News: The Hong Kong and Kowloon Trades Union Council held an emergency council meeting on the evening of the 14th regarding the Kowloon riots.

The meeting unanimously decided to launch a donation campaign among workers of all trades to express condolences to the families of the deceased workers and the injured workers. It also unanimously passed a statement protesting the Hong Kong British authorities' failure to take prompt and effective measures to stop the riots by Kuomintang secret agents, which resulted in serious deaths and injuries to workers.

The statement put forward four demands to the Hong Kong British authorities and earnestly hoped that workers of all trades and compatriots from all walks of life would work together to realize these four demands. These demands are:

	One, Severely punish the masterminds and perpetrators of this riot to appease public anger.

	Two, The Hong Kong side should immediately take effective measures to effectively stop the violence and ensure the safety of Hong Kong and Kowloon society.

	Three, Guarantee the safety of life and property of all residents in Hong Kong.

	Four, Immediately rescue and provide medical treatment to the beaten and injured workers and residents. Reasonable solutions should be considered immediately for the aftermath of the damaged trade unions, factories, shops, schools, markets, etc.

### 港九工会联合会举行理事会  向香港当局提出四项要求

1956-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    港九工会联合会举行理事会
    向香港当局提出四项要求
    新华社广州16日电　香港消息：港九工会联合会为九龙大骚乱事件在14日晚举行紧急理事会。
    会议一致决定发动各业工人捐款慰问死难工人家属与受伤工人，并一致通过一项声明，对香港英国当局没有采取迅速有效措施制止国民党特务分子的骚乱，使工人受到严重死伤提出抗议。
    声明中向香港英国当局提出四项要求，并且恳切希望各业工人和各界同胞共同为实现这四项要求而努力。这些要求是：
    一、严厉惩办此次策划暴乱的主事者及肇事者，以平民愤。
    二、港方应立即采取有效措施，切实制止暴行，确保港九社会安全。
    三、保障全港居民生命财产的安全。
    四、对被殴打受伤的工人和居民，立即进行抢救并负责医理。对已受破坏的工会、工厂、商店、学校市场等，应即考虑合理的善后办法。
