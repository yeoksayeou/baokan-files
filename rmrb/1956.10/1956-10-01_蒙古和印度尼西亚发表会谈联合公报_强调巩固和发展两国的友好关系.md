Title: Mongolia and Indonesia Issue Joint Communiqué on Talks, Emphasizing Consolidation and Development of Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 30th. Mongolian newspapers today published a joint communiqué on talks between the Mongolian People's Republic and the Republic of Indonesia, stating that both sides believe President Sukarno's friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic will further consolidate and develop the friendly relations between the two countries.

The communiqué stated that the talks between Mongolia and Indonesia on strengthening friendly relations between the two countries were held by President Sukarno during his visit to Mongolia and with Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia. Silindiibu, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, and Abdulgani, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia, also participated in the talks.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 30th. President of the Republic of Indonesia, Sukarno, and his entourage, left here by plane for Beijing this morning. Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, who came from Beijing to welcome Sukarno, accompanied him.

Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zedenbal, Dugersuren, Silindiibu, and other leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, as well as citizens of Ulaanbaatar, went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and the envoys of the Soviet Union, North Korea, and Czechoslovakia to Mongolia also went to the airport to see them off.

Sukarno also visited the Central Museum of the Mongolian People's Republic in Ulaanbaatar this morning.

### 蒙古和印度尼西亚发表会谈联合公报  强调巩固和发展两国的友好关系

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古和印度尼西亚发表会谈联合公报
    强调巩固和发展两国的友好关系
    新华社乌兰巴托30日电　蒙古报纸今天刊载的蒙古人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国的会谈联合公报说，会谈双方相信苏加诺总统对蒙古人民共和国的友好访问将更加巩固和发展两国的友好关系。
    公报说，蒙古和印度尼西亚两国就加强两国友好关系问题的会谈，是苏加诺总统在访问蒙古期间和蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布举行的。蒙古部长会议第一副主席锡林迪布，印度尼西亚共和国外交部长阿卜杜加尼也参加了会谈。
    新华社乌兰巴托30日电　印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和他的随行人员今天早晨从这里乘飞机去北京。从北京来迎接苏加诺的中国外交部副部长姬鹏飞陪同前往。
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、蒙古人民革命党和政府领导人泽登巴尔、杜格苏伦、锡林迪布等和乌兰巴托的市民都到机场欢送贵宾。
    中国驻蒙古大使何英和苏联、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克驻蒙古的使节也到机场送行。
    苏加诺今天早晨还在乌兰巴托参观了蒙古人民共和国中央博物馆。
