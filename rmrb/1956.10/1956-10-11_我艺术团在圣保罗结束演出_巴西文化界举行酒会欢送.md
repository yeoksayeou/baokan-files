Title: Our Art Troupe Concludes Performances in São Paulo; Brazilian Cultural Circles Hold Reception in Farewell

Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, São Paulo, 9th: The Chinese Art Troupe held its final performance in São Paulo, Brazil, on the evening of October 7th at the Municipal Theatre. At the end of the performance, the audience applauded warmly. All the performers came on stage to bid farewell to the São Paulo audience.

The art troupe held a total of eleven performances in Brazil. During the last performance, São Paulo Television broadcast the entire program, allowing one million people to watch the performance on television.

On the evening of October 8th, the São Paulo Writers Association, the Film and Drama Actors Association, the Architectural Society, the Art Gallery, and the Brazil-China Cultural Association and other organizations held a reception to bid farewell to the art troupe. Two hundred people from Brazilian cultural circles and society attended the reception. They congratulated the art troupe on its significant success in Brazil and pointed out that every Brazilian loved the dramas performed by the art troupe.

### 我艺术团在圣保罗结束演出  巴西文化界举行酒会欢送

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    我艺术团在圣保罗结束演出  巴西文化界举行酒会欢送
    新华社9日讯　圣保罗消息：中国艺术团10月7日晚间在市立剧院举行了在巴西圣保罗的最后一次演出。在演出结束时，观众热烈鼓掌。全体演员都上台向圣保罗观众告别。
    艺术团在巴西一共举行了十一次演出。在最后一次演出的时候，圣保罗电视公司播送了全部节目，使一百万人都在电视中看到了表演。
    10月8日晚上，圣保罗作家协会、电影和戏剧演员协会、建筑学会、艺术馆、和巴西—中国文化协会等组织举行了酒会，欢送艺术团。巴西文化界人士和社会人士二百人参加了这次酒会。他们祝贺艺术团在巴西的重大成功，并且指出，每一个巴西人都喜爱艺术团演出的戏剧。
