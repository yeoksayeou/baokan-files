Title: A New Antarctic Expedition Will Set Off Again Early Next Month
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

    Moscow, October 13 (Report from our newspaper) A new Soviet Antarctic comprehensive expedition will depart from Kaliningrad in early November to replace the current expedition working in Antarctica.
    The "Ob," "Lena," and "Kapitan Bilanov," which recently returned from Antarctica, will carry eight thousand tons of cargo and one hundred and sixty Soviet scientists back to the coast of "Mirny."
    This new Antarctic comprehensive expedition is divided into two research groups: a continental group and an oceanographic group. The continental scientific research group will not only expand the scientific research work at the "Mirny" and "Pioneer" scientific stations, but will also build two new scientific research stations in the Antarctic interior: "Vostok" and "Sovetskaya." The "Vostok" scientific station will be built in the South Geomagnetic Pole region, 1,500 kilometers from "Mirny." The "Sovetskaya" scientific station will be built approximately between 82 degrees south latitude and 50 to 60 degrees east longitude, near the "pole of inaccessibility," 2,000 kilometers from "Mirny." A transition station, the "Komsomolskaya" scientific station, will be built between "Mirny," the "Vostok" scientific station, and the "Sovetskaya" scientific station in the Antarctic interior. These scientific stations will comprehensively observe all natural phenomena in Antarctica. A team of continental research group led by Professor Sumsky will conduct a 5,000-kilometer scientific research along the way from "Mirny" to the "Sovetskaya" scientific station in 1957.

### 一支新的南极考察队下月初又将出发

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    一支新的南极考察队下月初又将出发
    本报莫斯科13日电　苏联一支新的南极综合考察队将在11月初从加里宁格勒出发去南极海岸替换现在在南极工作的考察队。
    不久前从南极返回的“鄂毕号”、“勒拿号”和“卡毕拉采号”，将载运八千吨货物和一百六十个苏联科学工作者再度开往“和平村”边岸。
    这支新的南极综合考察队分为大队和海洋两个研究组。南极大队科学研究组不仅要扩大“和平村”和“少年先锋队”科学站的科学研究工作，还将在南极的腹地建起两个新的科学研究站：“东方”和“苏维埃”。“东方”科学站将建立在离“和平村”一千五百公里的南地磁极区。“苏维埃”科学站大约将建立在南纬八十二度东经五十度到六十度之间，离“和平村”有两千公里的“难于到达的极地”附近。在“和平村”、“东方”科学站和南极洲腹地的“苏维埃”科学站中间，将建起一个过渡站“共青团”科学站。这些科学站将全面观察南极的一切自然现象。苏姆斯基教授领导的一队南极大队研究组，将在1957年从“和平村”到“苏维埃”科学站作五千公里的沿途科学研究工作。
