Title: Bulgaria Builds a Giant Hydroelectric Complex Hub
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th: A giant hydraulic project, the "Studen Kladenetz" hydroelectric complex hub, is under construction on the Arda River in southern Bulgaria.
    This hydroelectric complex includes a dam, reservoir, and hydroelectric power station. The dam under construction is 336 meters long and 76 meters high. The hydroelectric power station being built near the dam will be one of the largest in the country, generating 216 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually. The reservoir belonging to the complex, once completed, will be able to store 500 million cubic meters of water, which will be used to irrigate 900,000 decares (each decare equals 1.5 Chinese mu) of land in the Arda River basin.
    It is estimated that the "Studen Kladenetz" hydroelectric complex will be completed by the end of 1957.

### 保加利亚兴建一个巨型水力综合工程枢纽

1956-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚兴建一个巨型水力综合工程枢纽
    新华社布拉格20日电　索非亚消息：保加利亚南部阿尔达河上正在建设一个巨型的水力工程——“斯图坚克拉杰涅次”水力综合工程枢纽。
    这个水力综合工程包括拦河坝、水库和水电站等。正在施工中的拦河坝长三百三十六公尺、高七十六公尺。在拦河坝附近建设的水力发电站将是全国最大的水电站之一，它每年将可以发出二亿一千六百万度的电力。综合工程所属的水库在落成后，将可以蓄水五亿立方公尺，这些水将可以用来灌溉阿尔达河流域九十万狄卡尔（每狄卡尔合一市亩半）的土地。
    预计，“斯图坚克拉杰涅次”水力综合工程将在1957年年底建成。
