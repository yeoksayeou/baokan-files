Title: Huainan Coal Mine Improves Coal Mining Efficiency
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: From January to September this year, the coal produced by each worker in the Huainan Coal Mine increased by 6% compared to the state plan, with the efficiency of coal miners increasing by 20% compared to the plan. Compared with the same period last year, each coal miner produced more than 150 tons of coal for the country. The Xin Zhuang Zi Coal Mine, which saw the fastest increase in efficiency, had each coal miner produce 293 tons more coal than in the same period last year.
    Through the advanced producers movement, Huainan Coal Mine employees promoted many advanced experiences, greatly improving labor productivity. For example, after the Xin Zhuang Zi Coal Mine employees fully promoted the advanced experience of "blasting coal," the efficiency of coal miners increased by 32% compared to using pneumatic picks. After the fourth coal mining team of the Caijiagang Mine adjusted its labor organization and strengthened cooperation, it produced more than 1,700 tons of coal for the country in July alone.

### 淮南煤矿采煤效率提高

1956-10-28
第2版()
专栏：

    淮南煤矿采煤效率提高
    据新华社讯　今年1月至9月，淮南煤矿每个工人生产的煤比国家计划增加了6％，其中采煤工的效率比计划提高了20％。与去年同期比较，每个采煤工人为国家多生产了一百五十多吨煤炭。效率提高最快的新庄孜煤矿，每个采煤工人比去年同期多生产了二百九十三吨煤。
    淮南煤矿职工在先进生产者运动中推广了许多先进经验，使劳动生产率大大提高。如新庄孜煤矿职工全面推广“放炮落煤”的先进经验以后，采煤工的效率比用风镐采煤提高了32％。蔡家岗矿采煤四队调整劳动组织和加强协作以后，只是7月份就为国家多生产了一千七百多吨煤。
