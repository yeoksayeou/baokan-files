Title: French Foreign Ministry Spokesman Says They Do Not Accept the Suggestions of Egypt and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 9th: The spokesman for French Foreign Minister Pineau said on the 8th, when commenting on the speeches made by Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi and Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov at the United Nations Security Council meeting that day: "The suggestions put forward by (the Egyptian and Soviet Foreign Ministers) are unlikely to be accepted by France. The Soviet request to withdraw the Anglo-French proposal is one that I think we will not accept."

The spokesman attempted to sow discord between Egypt and the Soviet Union, saying, "Some of the things Egypt said today point in the direction of negotiations. However, the proposals put forward by the Soviet Union point in a different direction." But at the same time, he said that the Soviet Union fully supported Egypt.

When asked by reporters whether the Egyptian representative's speech provided a solid basis for negotiations, he did not give a clear answer. Regarding whether France would accept the Soviet representative's proposal to establish a negotiating committee, he only vaguely said: "We have always said that we would accept negotiations if there is a basis for negotiation. We disagree with discussing procedural issues first, but this is a problem that can be resolved in the next few days." He also said: "Let us first hold secret meetings and see how things go."

### 法国外交部发言人表示  不接受埃及和苏联的建议

1956-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　法国外交部发言人表示
　　不接受埃及和苏联的建议
    据新华社9日讯　纽约消息：法国外交部长比诺的发言人8日谈到埃及外交部长法齐和苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫当天在联合国安全理事会会议上的发言的时候说：“（埃及和苏联外交部长）所提出来的建议很少可能为法国所接受。苏联关于撤回英法提案的要求就是我认为我们不会接受的一个建议。”
    这个发言人企图在埃及和苏联之间进行挑拨，他说：“今天埃及说的某些话指向谈判的方向。然而苏联提出来的建议却指向一个不同的方向。”但是同时他又说，苏联完全支持埃及。
    对于记者提出的关于埃及代表的发言是否提供了谈判的一个坚固的基础这个问题，他不作明确的答复。对于法国会不会接受苏联代表关于设立谈判委员会的方案这个问题，他也只是闪烁其词地说：“我们一直说我们会接受谈判，假如有一个谈判的基础的话。我们不同意先讨论程序问题，但是，这是可以在以后几天解决的问题。”他还说：“让我们先举行秘密会议，再看情况怎样罢。”
