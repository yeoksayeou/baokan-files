Title: "Ta Kung Pao" Begins Publication in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. "Ta Kung Pao" will continue to be published in Beijing starting today. The "Ta Kung Pao" newspaper office has moved from Tianjin to its new location on Yong'an Road in Beijing.

### “大公报”开始在北京出版

1956-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    “大公报”开始在北京出版
    新华社1日讯　“大公报”从今天起在北京继续出版。“大公报”报社已经由天津迁移到北京永安路新址。
