Title: Tito Receives Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the Bulgarian National Assembly delegation, headed by Zhivkov, a member of the Presidium of the National Assembly and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party, returned to Belgrade on October 5 after visiting several republics in Yugoslavia for more than ten days.
    The delegation visited Zagreb, Ljubljana, Split and other cities.
    On October 7, Yugoslav President Tito received the delegation and had an hour-long conversation with them.

### 铁托接见保议会代表团

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托接见保议会代表团
    新华社8日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，以国民议会主席团委员、共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团在南斯拉夫的一些共和国访问了十多天后，10月5日回到贝尔格莱德。
    代表团曾经到萨格勒布、卢布尔雅那、斯普利特等城市参观。
    10月7日，南斯拉夫总统铁托接见了代表团人员，和他们进行了一小时的交谈。
