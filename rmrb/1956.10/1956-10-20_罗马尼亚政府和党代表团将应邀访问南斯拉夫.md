Title: Romanian Government and Party Delegation to Visit Yugoslavia on Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Belgrade, 18th (Xinhua) -- It was announced in Belgrade that a Romanian government and Romanian Workers' Party delegation is scheduled to arrive in Yugoslavia on October 20th for a nine-day official visit. The delegation is visiting Yugoslavia at the invitation of Yugoslav President Tito and the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

The delegation will include Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, among others.

### 罗马尼亚政府和党代表团将应邀访问南斯拉夫

1956-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府和党代表团将应邀访问南斯拉夫
    新华社18日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社18日报道：贝尔格莱德宣布，罗马尼亚政府和罗马尼亚工人党代表团定于10月20日到南斯拉夫作为时九天的正式访问。代表团是应南斯拉夫总统铁托和南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会的邀请到南斯拉夫访问的。
    代表团人员将包括罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治和部长会议主席斯托伊卡等人。
