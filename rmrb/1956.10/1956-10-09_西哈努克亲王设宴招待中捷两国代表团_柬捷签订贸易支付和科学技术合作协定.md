Title: Prince Sihanouk Hosts Banquet for Chinese and Czechoslovakian Delegations; Cambodia and Czechoslovakia Sign Trade Payment and Scientific-Technological Cooperation Agreements
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th Dispatch: Phnom Penh News: Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk hosted a banquet at the Royal Palace on October 6th to entertain the Chinese Economic Delegation to Phnom Penh and the Czechoslovakian Delegation.

　　Many high-ranking Cambodian government officials attended the banquet.

　　During the banquet, Prince Sihanouk toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and the President of Czechoslovakia, and to the prosperity and happiness of the peoples of China, Czechoslovakia, and all nations.

　　After the banquet, the Cambodian Royal Dance Troupe performed national dances.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch: TASS, Hanoi, 8th Dispatch: According to a Phnom Penh radio broadcast, the Kingdom of Cambodia and the Czechoslovak Republic, after negotiations in Phnom Penh, signed an agreement on trade payments and a scientific and technological cooperation agreement between the Kingdom of Cambodia and the Czechoslovak Republic.

　　The trade agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two parties will reach 450 million Riels (Cambodian currency). Cambodia will exchange agricultural products for Czechoslovakian industrial goods.

　　According to the scientific and technological cooperation agreement, the two parties will exchange students and experts. Czechoslovakia has agreed to Cambodia's request to sell scientific experimental equipment and industrial equipment to Cambodia.

### 西哈努克亲王设宴招待中捷两国代表团  柬捷签订贸易支付和科学技术合作协定

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克亲王设宴招待中捷两国代表团
    柬捷签订贸易支付和科学技术合作协定
    新华社河内8日电　金边消息：柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王10月6日在皇宫设宴招待中国驻金边经济代表团和捷克斯洛伐克代表团。
    柬埔寨政府的许多高级官员参加了这次宴会。
    席间，西哈努克亲王为毛泽东主席和捷克斯洛伐克总统的健康、为中国、捷克斯洛伐克和各国人民的繁荣与幸福而干杯。
    宴会后，柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团表演了民族舞蹈。
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社河内8日讯：据金边电台广播，柬埔寨王国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国在金边举行谈判后，签订了关于柬埔寨王国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国之间的贸易支付协定和科学技术合作协定。
    贸易协定规定双方的贸易额达四亿五千万瑞尔（柬埔寨币）。柬埔寨将以农产品换取捷克斯洛伐克的工业品。
    根据科学技术合作协定，双方将互派学生和专家。捷克斯洛伐克答应柬埔寨的要求，将售给柬埔寨科学实验设备和工业设备。
