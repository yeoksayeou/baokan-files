Title: Ministry of Higher Education Welcomes Delegation from the Central Finance and Economics School of the Vietnam People's University
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Xu Jianren, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education, hosted a luncheon today to welcome Đoàn Trọng Chuẩn, the head of the delegation from the Central Finance and Economics School of the Vietnam Democratic Republic's People's University, and all members of the delegation. In the evening, over 800 faculty and students from the China People's University held a welcome meeting. Hu Xikui, Vice President of the China People's University, delivered a speech at the meeting, warmly welcoming the delegation's arrival on behalf of all the faculty and students.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 25th. They are visiting the China People's University specifically. During their visit, they will exchange teaching experience with the China People's University.

### 高教部欢迎越南人民大学中央财政经济学校代表团

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    高教部欢迎越南人民大学中央财政经济学校代表团
    新华社27日讯　高等教育部副部长徐建人今天中午欢宴越南民主共和国人民大学中央财政经济学校代表团团长段仲瑑和全体团员。晚间，中国人民大学师生八百多人举行了欢迎大会。中国人民大学副校长胡锡奎在大会上致词，代表全校师生热烈欢迎代表团的到来。
    这个代表团在25日抵达北京。他们是专门访问中国人民大学的。在访问期间将和中国人民大学交流教学工作经验。
