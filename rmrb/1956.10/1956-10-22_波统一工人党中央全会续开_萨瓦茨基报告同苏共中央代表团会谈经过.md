Title: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Plenary Session Continues: Zawadzki Reports on Talks with CPSU Central Committee Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 21st. According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party continued on October 20th.

　　Zawadzki reported to the meeting on the course of the talks between the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

　　Following this, the plenary session began to discuss the draft resolutions proposed by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee.

　　Gomułka was the first to speak, expressing his views on fundamental political and economic issues in Poland.

　　After lunch, Jędrychowski reported to the plenary session on the changes made to the government's Five-Year Plan bill compared to the resolutions of the previous Central Committee plenary session.

　　Jaroszewicz, Wozki, Krupiński, and Rumiński also spoke during the discussion.

　　The plenary session elected a committee to draft motions and amendments to the draft resolutions proposed by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee.

### 波统一工人党中央全会续开  萨瓦茨基报告同苏共中央代表团会谈经过

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    波统一工人党中央全会续开
    萨瓦茨基报告同苏共中央代表团会谈经过
    新华社华沙21日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰统一工人党中央委员会第八次全体会议10月20日继续举行。
    萨瓦茨基向会议报告了波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局同苏联共产党中央委员会代表团举行会谈的经过情况。
    接着，全体会议开始讨论中央委员会政治局提出的各项决议草案。
    首先发言的是哥穆尔卡，他发表了对于波兰根本的政治和经济问题的意见。
    在午餐后，英德里霍夫斯基向全体会议报告了政府关于五年计划的法案同上一次中央全会的决议相较有了哪些变动。
    在讨论中发言的还有雅罗谢维奇、伏兹基、克鲁平斯基和鲁明斯基。
    全体会议选出了委员会来草拟对中央委员会政治局提出的各项决议草案的动议和修正案。
