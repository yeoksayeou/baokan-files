Title: Bangkok "Zu Guo" Weekly Publishes Editorial Condemning Hong Kong Authorities for Conniving with Kuomintang Secret Agents, Demands Thai Government Expel Vicious Kuomintang Elements
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th, Bangkok Dispatch: The Bangkok-based "Zu Guo" (Motherland) Weekly condemned the Kowloon riots instigated by Kuomintang secret agents with the connivance of the British authorities in Hong Kong on the 22nd.

The magazine stated in its editorial: "Why would these people dare to launch such riots? It is because the Hong Kong authorities have consistently adopted an attitude of indifference towards the despicable actions of the Kuomintang elements who create disturbances. And this attitude is tantamount to directly helping and encouraging these rioters' despicable actions."

The editorial pointed out that everyone knows Hong Kong is a place used by the Kuomintang and the United States to establish espionage organizations and engage in hostile activities against the People's Republic of China. However, the Hong Kong authorities have not taken any measures to prevent serious incidents that may occur, nor have they learned a lesson from the Kuomintang secret agents' bombing of the "Kashmir Princess" airliner. This Kowloon riot has caused serious damage to the local people.

The editorial also exposed the fact that Kuomintang elements are openly and semi-openly carrying out hostile acts against the People's Republic of China in Thailand and oppressing local overseas Chinese, and demanded that the Thai government must resolutely expel those vicious Kuomintang elements from the country.

### 曼谷“祖国”周刊发表社论  谴责香港当局纵容国民党特务  要求泰政府驱逐凶恶的国民党分子

1956-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    曼谷“祖国”周刊发表社论
    谴责香港当局纵容国民党特务
    要求泰政府驱逐凶恶的国民党分子
    新华社28日讯　曼谷消息：这里的“祖国”周刊22日谴责香港英国当局纵容国民党特务分子发动的九龙暴乱。
    这家杂志在社论中说：“这班人为什么敢于发动那样的暴乱呢？那是因为香港当局一直对制造骚乱的国民党分子的卑劣行径，采取不闻不问的态度。而这种态度又等于是直接帮助鼓励了这些骚乱分子的卑劣行径”。
    社论指出，大家知道，香港是被国民党和美国用以建立间谍组织同中华人民共和国实行敌对的地方。但是香港当局并没有怎样采取措施来防止可能发生的严重事件，同时也并没有从国民党特务分子爆炸“克什米尔公主号”客机事件中吸取教训。这次九龙暴乱事件，已使得当地人民遭受严重损害。
    社论还揭露了国民党分子公开和半公开地在泰国对中华人民共和国进行的敌对行为和压迫当地华侨的事实，并且要求泰国政府必须坚决将那些凶恶的国民党分子驱逐出境。
