Title: Inaugural Meeting of the Tibetan Branch of the Buddhist Association of China Concludes; Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini Elected Honorary Presidents
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 11th -- The inaugural meeting of the Tibetan branch of the Buddhist Association of China concluded successfully yesterday under the auspices of the Dalai Lama. At noon, when the Dalai Lama arrived at the venue, the square in front of the Lhasa Auditorium was filled with welcoming crowds.

As the Dalai Lama ascended to the Buddhist throne, the Dalai Lama's "Kapa" band played Buddhist drums and music from the gallery of the venue. Also on the stage were Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, Gadan Tri Rinpoche, the successor of Tsongkhapa, the Dalai Lama's main and deputy tutors, representatives of various sects of Tibetan Buddhism, Apao A-pei Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, and senior officials from the Tibetan local government and the Bainqen Kanpo Lija. Gadan Tri Rinpoche, wearing a yellow pointed Dharma hat and a yellow Dharma robe, presided over the ceremony of offering "Mandala" to the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini.

After representatives of various Tibetan sects spoke and a representative of Islam delivered a congratulatory speech, the meeting, amidst warm applause from all representatives, adopted the articles of association for the Tibetan branch of the Buddhist Association of China and the list of branch leaders. The Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini were elected honorary presidents, Gadan Tri Rinpoche Tubdain Gunga was elected president of the council, and the meeting also elected vice presidents, secretaries-general and deputy secretaries-general, 118 council members, and 27 standing council members.

The Dalai Lama spoke at the closing ceremony of the meeting. He congratulated the establishment of the Tibetan branch of the Buddhist Association of China. He said: Such a large-scale meeting has never been held in Tibet before. I firmly support the resolutions of the conference. We should unite as one, regardless of religious sect, like a fusion of water and milk, to strengthen the unity between the Tibetan and Han nationalities and within the Tibetan nationality, so as to achieve the long-term existence of Buddhism. Gadan Tri Rinpoche, the successor of the Yellow Sect founder Tsongkhapa and President of the Tibetan Branch of the Buddhist Association of China, delivered the closing speech. He said: "For the sake of patriotism, unity, progress, and the promotion of Buddhism, we should widely and deeply convey the spirit of this meeting to all Tibetan Buddhists, continue to work hard to study the scriptures, actively participate in patriotic studies, and strive to achieve the goals stipulated in the articles of association of this association."

A congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong and a congratulatory telegram from Bainqen Erdini were read out at the meeting. After the closing ceremony of the meeting, the Lhasa Auditorium, as the venue, was immediately opened to monks, nuns, and the general public of Lhasa. A constant stream of people came to the venue to pay homage to the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini's Buddhist thrones and the huge Buddha statues set up on the stage.

### 中国佛教协会西藏分会成立会议闭幕  达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼被选为名誉会长

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中国佛教协会西藏分会成立会议闭幕
    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼被选为名誉会长
    新华社拉萨11日电　中国佛教协会西藏分会成立会议昨天在达赖喇嘛的主持下圆满闭幕。中午，当达赖喇嘛来到会场的时候，拉萨大礼堂前站满了欢迎的人群。
    当达赖喇嘛登上佛座时，会场的楼座上，达赖喇嘛的“噶巴”乐队奏起了佛鼓佛乐。在主席台上的还有中共西藏工委副书记范明、宗喀巴继承人噶丹赤巴，达赖喇嘛的正经师和副经师、西藏佛教各教派的代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美以及西藏地方政府和班禅堪布会议厅委员会的高级官员们。头戴黄色尖顶法帽、身披黄色法衣的噶丹赤巴主持了向达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼供献“曼陀罗”的仪式。
    会议在西藏各教派代表发言和伊斯兰教代表致祝词以后，全体代表热烈鼓掌通过中国佛教协会西藏分会组织简则和分会领导人的名单。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼被选为名誉会长，噶丹赤巴·土登滚噶为理事会会长，会议还选出理事会副会长，正副秘书长和理事一百一十八人，常务理事二十七人。
    达赖喇嘛在会议的闭幕仪式上讲了话。他祝贺中国佛教协会西藏分会的成立。他说：像这样大规模的会议，在西藏过去还从未有过。我坚决拥护大会的决议。我们应该不分宗教派别，像水乳溶和一样的团结一致，加强藏汉民族间和西藏民族内部的团结，以达到佛教常住久存。黄教创始人宗喀巴的继承人、中国佛教协会西藏分会会长噶丹赤巴致闭幕词。他说：“为了爱国、团结、进步和佛教的宏扬，我们应该把这次会议的精神广泛而深入地向全体西藏佛教徒们进行传达，继续努力钻研经典，积极参加爱国主义学习，为实现本会简则所规定的宗旨而奋斗。”
    会上宣读了给毛泽东主席的致敬电和班禅额尔德尼的贺电。　　会议闭幕式结束以后，作为会场的拉萨大礼堂立即为拉萨市的僧俗群众开放，参观的人群不断来到会场里，人们向设在主席台上的达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的佛座及巨大的佛像顶礼朝拜。
