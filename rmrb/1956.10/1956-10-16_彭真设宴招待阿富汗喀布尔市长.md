Title: Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Kabul Mayor of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: Dr. Soheyl, the Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, for a visit to China.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor, the Secretary-General and Directors of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and the Director of the Foreign Affairs Office.

Also at the airport to welcome him was Samad, the Afghan Ambassador to China.

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: At 10 o'clock this evening, Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, hosted a banquet in honor of Dr. Soheyl, the Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

At the banquet, Peng Zhen said: "We wholeheartedly welcome our close neighbor, Dr. Soheyl, the Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. Mr. Soheyl brings with him the profound friendship of the people of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and the people of Kabul, as well as the construction experience of Kabul. Because Dr. Soheyl has visited cities in many countries around the world, he also brings with him the construction experience of many cities around the world. We welcome him to visit all aspects of our capital and hope that he will offer criticisms and corrections of the shortcomings in our work."

Dr. Soheyl spoke at the banquet. He said: "From the ancient books of our country, we can find records of the friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan, and from ancient pictures, we can see the situation of China at that time. The deep friendship between our two countries has developed from ancient times to today. We hope that this friendship can contribute to the great goal of peace in the world today."

Samad, the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, also spoke at the banquet.

Before the banquet began, Mayor Peng Zhen met with Dr. Soheyl.

Those present at the banquet tonight included Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Wang Kunlun, and Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ma Hanbing, head of the Chinese cultural and artistic delegation that visited Afghanistan in August and September this year, and the Secretary-General and Directors of the Municipal People's Committee, and the Director of the Foreign Affairs Office.

### 彭真设宴招待阿富汗喀布尔市长

1956-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真设宴招待阿富汗喀布尔市长
    新华社15日讯　应北京市市长彭真邀请来中国访问的阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有北京市市长彭真、副市长乐松生、北京市人民委员会秘书长、局长、外事处长等人。
    到机场欢迎的还有阿富汗驻我国大使萨马德。
    新华社15日讯　今天晚上十时北京市市长彭真设宴招待阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士。
    彭真在宴会上说：我们衷心地欢迎我们的近邻、阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士，索海尔先生带来了阿富汗王国人民、喀布尔市人民的深厚友谊，带来了喀布尔市建设经验。因为索海尔博士到过世界许多国家的城市，所以他也带来了世界许多城市的建设经验。我们欢迎他到我们首都各方面参观，并希望对我们工作中的缺点提出批评、指正。
    在宴会上索海尔博士讲了话。他说：从我们国家古老的书籍中，可以找到有关中阿两国人民友谊的记载，从古老图画中可以看到那时中国的情况。我们两国深厚的友谊从古代一直发展到今天。我们希望这种友谊能对今天世界伟大的目标——和平有所贡献。
    阿富汗王国驻我国大使萨马德也在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会开始前，彭真市长接见了索海尔博士。
    今天晚上出席宴会的，有北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗、王昆仑、乐松生，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁，曾经在今年八、九月间到阿富汗访问过的中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰以及市人民委员会秘书长、局长、外事处长等人。
