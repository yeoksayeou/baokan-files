Title: Eighth Session of the World Federation of Trade Unions Council Concludes, Adopts Resolutions on the Saillant Report and Appeals for Disarmament, Among Other Documents
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 4th - According to TASS: The eighth session of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) Council concluded here on October 3rd.

At the closing session, the meeting unanimously adopted the following documents: A resolution on the report of WFTU General Secretary Saillant, a resolution on the report of WFTU Vice-President Diallo, a memorandum to the United Nations General Assembly, an appeal to the working people and trade unions of the world on the issue of atomic and thermonuclear weapons and disarmament, a resolution on convening the Fourth World Trade Union Congress, a resolution on the Cyprus issue, a resolution on the situation in Okinawa, a resolution on the Cameroon issue, and others.

The meeting approved the applications of the Algerian Trade Union Federation and the Madagascar Labor Union Federation to join the World Federation of Trade Unions.

Later that day, a grand rally celebrating the closing of the meeting was held in Sofia.

### 世界工联理事会第八次会议闭幕  通过关于赛扬报告决议和裁军呼吁书等文件

1956-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联理事会第八次会议闭幕
    通过关于赛扬报告决议和裁军呼吁书等文件
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社索非亚讯：世界工会联合会理事会第八次会议10月3日在这里结束。
    在闭幕会议上，会议一致通过了下列文件：关于世界工会联合会总书记赛扬的报告的决议，关于世界工会联合会副主席迪亚洛的报告的决议，致联合国大会的备忘录，就原子武器和热核子武器问题以及裁军问题致全世界劳动人民和工会的呼吁书，关于召开第四次世界工会代表大会的决议，关于塞浦路斯问题的决议，关于冲绳岛局势问题的决议，关于喀麦隆问题的决议等。
    会议批准了阿尔及利亚工会联合会和马达加斯加工人工会联合会提出的加入世界工会联合会的申请。
    当天晚些时候，在索非亚举行了庆祝会议闭幕的盛大集会。
