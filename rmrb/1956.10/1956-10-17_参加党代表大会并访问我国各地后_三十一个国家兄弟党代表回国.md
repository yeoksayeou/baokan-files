Title: Representatives of Brotherly Parties from Thirty-One Countries Return Home After Attending the Party Congress and Visiting Various Parts of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th - By today, twenty delegations from Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries, invited to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, have visited various parts of China after the congress and have returned home one after another. In addition, some members of the delegations from eleven fraternal parties have also returned.
    The fraternal party delegations that have completely returned home are: Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Romanian Workers' Party, French Communist Party, Italian Communist Party, Albanian Party of Labour, Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Bulgarian Communist Party, Hungarian Working People's Party, Korean Workers' Party, Vietnam Workers' Party, Communist Party of Finland, Norwegian Communist Party, Swedish Communist Party, Austrian Communist Party, Swiss Labour Party, Syrian-Lebanese Communist Party, Israeli Communist Party, Icelandic Socialist Unity Party, and the Greek Communist Party.
    The fraternal party delegations that have had some members return are: Polish United Workers' Party, Czechoslovak Communist Party, Communist Party of India, Communist Party of Great Britain, Spanish Communist Party, Communist Party of Germany, Communist Party of Argentina, Communist Party of Australia, Communist Party of Ecuador, Communist Party of Morocco, and the Cuban Popular Socialist Party.
    Before returning to their countries, the delegations and representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries visited Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Harbin, Fushun, Anshan, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Wuxi and other places.
    Other delegations and representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries are continuing their visits in various parts of our country.

### 参加党代表大会并访问我国各地后  三十一个国家兄弟党代表回国

1956-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    参加党代表大会并访问我国各地后
    三十一个国家兄弟党代表回国
    新华社16日讯　到今天为止，已经有二十个应邀参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各国共产党和工人党的代表团，在大会结束后访问了中国各地，并先后回国。另外，有十一个国家兄弟党的代表团的部分人员也已经返国。
    已经全部回国的兄弟党代表团是：苏联共产党、德国统一社会党、罗马尼亚工人党、法国共产党、意大利共产党、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、蒙古人民革命党、保加利亚共产党、匈牙利劳动人民党、朝鲜劳动党、越南劳动党、芬兰共产党、挪威共产党、瑞典共产党、奥地利共产党、瑞士劳动党、叙利亚—黎巴嫩共产党、以色列共产党、冰岛统一社会党、希腊共产党的代表团。
    有部分人员返国的兄弟党代表团是：波兰统一工人党、捷克斯洛伐克共产党、印度共产党、英国共产党、西班牙共产党、德国共产党、阿根廷共产党、澳大利亚共产党、厄瓜多尔共产党、摩洛哥共产党、古巴人民社会党。
    各国共产党、工人党的代表团和代表在返国前曾在北京、上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、广州、西安、哈尔滨、抚顺、鞍山、南京、杭州、无锡等地进行参观访问。
    其他各国共产党、工人党的代表团和代表们正继续在我国各地进行参观访问。
