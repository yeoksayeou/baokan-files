Title: Meeting Between Soviet Communist Party Leaders and Swiss Party of Labour Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 9th: According to TASS, Soviet Communist Party leaders and the Swiss Party of Labour delegation held talks in Moscow, discussing issues of concern to both parties.

Participating in the talks were: P.N. Pospelov, Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, B.N. Ponomarev, Member of the Central Committee, and from the Swiss Party of Labour, Edgar Woog, General Secretary, Vincent, Secretary of the Central Committee, and Straub, Member of the Central Committee.

### 苏共领导人和瑞士劳动党代表团会谈

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共领导人和瑞士劳动党代表团会谈
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联共产党领导人和瑞士劳动党代表团在莫斯科举行会谈，讨论了两党所关心的问题。
    参加会谈的有：苏共中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫、中央委员波诺马烈夫，瑞士劳动党总书记沃克、中央委员会书记温森和中央委员斯特布勒。
