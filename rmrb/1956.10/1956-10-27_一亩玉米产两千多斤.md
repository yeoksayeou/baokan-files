Title: One Mu of Corn Yields Over Two Thousand Jin
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: In Chengcxian County, Gansu Province, a mu of "Golden Queen" corn in the Le Lou Agricultural Production Cooperative yielded as much as 2,295 jin.

    This cooperative planted 77.5 mu of improved corn varieties this year, with an average yield of over 800 jin per mu, an increase of over 112% compared to last year. Among them, 17 mu had yields exceeding 1,000 jin per mu.

### 一亩玉米产两千多斤

1956-10-27
第3版()
专栏：

    一亩玉米产两千多斤
    据新华社讯　甘肃省成县乐耧农业生产合作社的一亩金皇后玉米丰产地，产量高达二千二百九十五斤。
    这个社今年播种的七十七点五亩优良品种玉米，平均每亩产八百多斤，比去年增产112％多。其中有十七亩每亩产量都在一千斤以上。
