Title: Chairman Mao Receives Yugoslav Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the Yugoslav military delegation headed by General Kosta Nadj on the evening of the 7th.

Present at the reception were Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense; Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Chen Geng, Senior General Xiao Ke, Senior General Xiao Hua, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

Also present at the reception was the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic.

### 毛主席接见南军事代表团

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见南军事代表团
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席在7日晚间接见了以科斯塔·纳季上将为首的南斯拉夫军事代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将，萧克上将，萧华上将，萧向荣中将。
    接见的时候，南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也在座。
