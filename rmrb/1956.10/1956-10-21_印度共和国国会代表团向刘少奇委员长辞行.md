Title: Indian Republic Parliamentary Delegation Bids Farewell to Chairman Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The entire Indian Republic Parliamentary Delegation, headed by Speaker A. A. Ayyangar, bid farewell to Chairman Liu Shaoqi this evening. The hosts and guests had a friendly and warm conversation.

Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Deputy Secretaries-General Zhang Su and Qu Wu.

### 印度共和国国会代表团向刘少奇委员长辞行

1956-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    印度共和国国会代表团向刘少奇委员长辞行
    新华社20日讯　以阿·阿延加尔议长为首的印度共和国国会代表团全体人员，今晚向刘少奇委员长辞行。宾主进行了友好热诚的谈话。
    交谈时在座的，有李济深、郭沫若、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通副委员长，张苏、屈武副秘书长。
