Title: David Marshall Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 15th: David Marshall, Chairman of the Singapore Labour Front and former Chief Minister of Singapore, concluded his visit to China and left Guangzhou yesterday to return home.

David Marshall was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country. During his stay in China, he visited various places in Northeast China, as well as Kunming, Shanghai, and other cities.

### 戴维·马歇尔离广州回国

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    戴维·马歇尔离广州回国
    新华社广州15日电　新加坡劳工阵线主席、前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔结束了在中国的访问，昨天离广州回国。
    戴维·马歇尔是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的。他在中国期间曾访问了东北各地和昆明、上海等城市。
