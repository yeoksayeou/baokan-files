Title: Czechoslovak President Zápotocký Receives Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 21st. According to the Czechoslovak News Agency: Czechoslovak President Zápotocký received the Indonesian parliamentary delegation headed by Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party parliamentary group, on October 20th.

Present at the reception were Fierlinger, Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, and David, Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 捷总统萨波托斯基接见印度尼西亚议会代表团

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    捷总统萨波托斯基接见印度尼西亚议会代表团
    新华社布拉格21日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社消息：捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基10月20日接见了以印度尼西亚国民党议会党团主席哈迪为首的印度尼西亚议会代表团。
    接见时在座的有捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格和外交部长戴维。
