Title: Snippets from the Eve of the Olympic Games
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 6

　　Snippets from the Eve of the Olympic Games
　　　　Melbourne Adorned and Ready
　　Melbourne's main decoration for the Olympic Games – a torch over eighteen meters high – has been hung at the intersection of Flinders Street and Swanston Street. This torch, weighing three and a half tons, is suspended in the air by steel cables, and will be lit before the opening of the Games.
　　The World’s Center for Sports and Social Life
　　During the Olympic Games, Melbourne will not only be the center of world sports activities, but also the center of social activities. In addition to more than five thousand athletes and more than one thousand two hundred staff members from more than seventy units, more than ten thousand people of all kinds will appear in various occasions in Melbourne. Among them are princes, princesses, nobles, and former national presidents. From November 15 to December 9, the schedule is already full of cocktail parties, garden parties, water concerts and other events.
　　Three Hundred and Thirty Female Drivers
　　When the Olympic Games are held, Melbourne residents will see female drivers wearing green and gold Olympic uniforms. They will be driving more than one hundred brand-new cars for the staff of the Games, speeding on the roads day and night. There are a total of three hundred and thirty of them, all of whom were recruited after strict examinations by the organizing committee.
　　Olympic Torch to be Lit on November 2
　　The relay team to welcome the Olympic torch has left Melbourne. The torch will be lit on Mount Olympia in Greece on November 2. On November 9, the torch will be transported by plane from Athens to Cairns, Australia, and then relayed to Melbourne by two thousand seven hundred and fifty young Australian athletes.
                  (Xinhua News Agency report)

### 奥林匹克运动会前夕花絮

1956-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    奥林匹克运动会前夕花絮
        墨尔本盛装以待
    墨尔本在奥林匹克运动会期间的主要装饰物——一个十八公尺多高的火炬，已经在费林德斯路和斯温顿路交叉的十字路悬挂起来。这个重达三吨半的火炬是用钢缆吊在空中的，大会开幕前这个火炬就要点燃起来。
    全世界的体育和社交中心
    在奥林匹克运动会期间，墨尔本不但将是世界体育活动的中心，而且也将成为社交活动的中心。除了来自七十多个单位的五千多名选手和一千二百多名工作人员外，墨尔本的各种场合将出现一万多名各式各样的人物。他们中间有王子、公主，也有贵族和以前的国家总统。从11月15日起一直到12月9日，日程表上已经排满了鸡尾酒会、游园会和水上音乐会等项目。
    三百三十名女汽车司机
    在奥林匹克运动会举行的时候，墨尔本的居民将可以看见身穿绿色和金黄色奥林匹克制服的女子司机。她们将驾着一百多辆崭新的大会工作人员的汽车，日夜在马路上奔驰。她们一共有三百三十人，都是经过大会严格的考试后被录取的。
  奥林匹克火炬11月2日点火
    迎接奥林匹克火炬的接力队已经离开墨尔本。火炬将在11月2日在希腊的奥林匹亚山点燃。11月9日，火炬将由飞机从雅典运到澳大利亚的凯恩斯，然后由二千七百五十名澳大利亚的青年运动员接力奔向墨尔本。
                  （据新华社讯）
