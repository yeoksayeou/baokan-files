Title: Japan Socialist Party Central Executive Committee Passes Resolution Demanding All Countries in the World Recognize China, Proposes "Ten Principles of Peace" Advocating Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 12th. The Japan Socialist Party decided yesterday that at the Asian Socialist Conference to be held in early November, it will demand that all countries in the world recognize the People's Republic of China.

    In a resolution passed by its Central Executive Committee meeting, the Japan Socialist Party pointed out that the government of the People's Republic of China has established a very solid power on the Chinese mainland, and therefore the Chinese government should be recognized by all countries in the world. At the same time, the Chinese government's right to representation in the United Nations should also be recognized. The Socialist Party also hopes in the resolution that relevant countries will resolve the tensions in the Taiwan region peacefully.

    The Socialist Party also passed a resolution on the Okinawa issue. The resolution pointed out that the current situation of the United States establishing military bases in Okinawa has exceeded the scope of the San Francisco Peace Treaty, seriously infringed upon Japan's sovereignty, and become a factor of international tension. The Socialist Party hopes to issue a warning to the United States on this issue and hold negotiations with the US government to ensure that Okinawa is completely returned to Japan.

    The Central Executive Committee meeting of the Socialist Party also passed new "Ten Principles of Peace" to be submitted to the Asian Socialist Conference. The contents of these ten principles are as follows:

    I. Respect the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and the United Nations Charter.

    II. All races are equal, all countries are equal.

    III. Respect the right to self-determination of all nations and the independence of countries.

    IV. Respect the sovereignty and territory of all countries and non-interference in the internal affairs of other countries.

    V. Peaceful settlement of international disputes, elimination of threats of aggression against other countries.

    VI. Do not participate in any military alliances, establish a collective security system including both Eastern and Western camps, and withdraw foreign troops.

    VII. Promote the movement for disarmament, prohibition of atomic weapons, and peaceful use of atomic energy on a worldwide scale centered on major powers.

    VIII. Eliminate all forms of colonialism.

    IX. Promote trade in accordance with the principle of equality and mutual benefit.

    X. In accordance with the comprehensive economic plan centered on the United Nations, develop undeveloped areas and improve living standards.

    The Socialist Party also decided yesterday on the list of 14 delegates who will attend the Asian Socialist Conference. The delegation will be led by the Secretary-General of the Socialist Party, Inejiro Asanuma.

### 日本社会党中央执行委员会通过决议  要求世界所有国家承认中国  提出“和平十项原则”主张和平共处

1956-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党中央执行委员会通过决议
    要求世界所有国家承认中国
    提出“和平十项原则”主张和平共处
    新华社12日讯　东京消息：日本社会党昨天决定，在11月初举行的亚洲社会党会议上，它将要求世界所有国家承认中华人民共和国。
    日本社会党在它的中央执行委员会会议通过的决议中指出，中华人民共和国政府已经在中国大陆建立了非常稳固的权力，因此中国政府应该得到世界上所有国家的承认。同时，中国政府在联合国的代表权也应该得到承认。社会党在决议中还希望有关国家用和平方式解决台湾地区的紧张局势问题。
    社会党还通过了一项关于冲绳岛问题的决议。决议指出，美国目前在冲绳岛建立军事基地的情况已经超过了旧金山和约的范围，严重地侵犯了日本的主权，成为国际局势紧张的因素。社会党希望就这个问题向美国发出警告，并且同美国政府举行谈判，以使冲绳岛完全归还日本。
    社会党中央执行委员会会议还通过了准备提交亚洲社会党会议的新的“和平十项原则”。这十项原则的内容如下：
    一、尊重世界人权宣言和联合国宪章。
    二、所有的种族平等，所有的国家平等。
    三、尊重所有的民族的自决权利和国家的独立。
    四、尊重所有国家的主权和领土，不干涉他国的内政。
    五、和平解决国际争端，消除侵略他国的威胁。
    六、不参加一切军事同盟，建立包括东西两个阵营在内的集体安全体制，撤退外国军队。
    七、推进以大国为中心的全世界范围的裁军、禁止原子武器、和平利用原子能的运动。
    八、清除一切形态的殖民主义。
    九、按照平等互惠的原则促进贸易。
    十、按照以联合国为中心的综合经济计划，开发未开发地区，提高生活水平。
    社会党在昨天还决定了出席亚洲社会党会议的十四人代表团的人选。代表团将由社会党书记长浅沼稻次郎率领。
