Title: Berlin People's Police Orchestra Donates Funds to Aid Disaster Victims in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 1

    Berlin People's Police Orchestra Donates Funds to Aid Disaster Victims in Our Country
    (Our Newspaper News) To celebrate the 7th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army hosted a banquet at noon on the 6th for the Berlin People's Police Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic, which is visiting China for performances. At the banquet, the orchestra donated 3,000 yuan, the labor remuneration obtained from recordings at our country's radio stations, to the victims of the recent typhoons and floods in our country. Major General Chen Yi, Minister of Culture of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, accepted the donation on behalf of the disaster victims in our country and expressed his gratitude on behalf of the disaster-stricken people of our country.
    At this banquet, Major General Chen Yi also presented gifts to the orchestra on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 柏林人民警察乐团捐款救济我国灾民

1956-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    柏林人民警察乐团捐款救济我国灾民
    本报讯　为庆贺德意志民主共和国七周年国庆，6日中午，中华人民共和国文化部、中国人民解放军总政治部，特为来华访问演出的德意志民主共和国柏林人民警察乐团举行了宴会。在宴会上，乐团将在我国电台录音所得劳动报酬三千元，捐献给我最近遭受台风和水灾的灾民。中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂少将代表我国灾民接受捐款，并代表我国受灾人民表示谢意。
    在这个宴会上，陈沂少将还代表中华人民共和国文化部和中国人民解放军总政治部向乐团赠送了礼品。
