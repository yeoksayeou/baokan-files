Title: Calling on the People to Unite Against the Conspiracy of the Iraq Petroleum Company. Lebanese Political Parties and Groups Issue Statements. Syria Expresses Eternal Support for Lebanon's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th. Tass, Beirut, October 13th: "Al-Telgraph" reports: Lebanese political parties and groups issued a joint statement saying that the Lebanese people hope to consolidate their sovereignty, but the Iraq Petroleum Company is attempting to dominate the country and its government. The statement calls on the people to "unite to fight against the conspiracy of the Iraq Petroleum Company and support the Lebanese government in defending the national interests of the country."
    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th. Tass, Beirut, October 13th: According to a report in the Lebanese "Al-Telgraph," Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar, when talking about the dispute between Lebanon and the Iraq Petroleum Company, said, "Syria will always stand on the side of Lebanon. We oppose the Iraq Petroleum Company's request to lay an oil pipeline to Baniyas."
    Bitar pointed out that this dispute is a link in the whole set of conspiracies of imperialism against Arab countries.

### 号召人民团结对付伊拉克石油公司的阴谋  黎巴嫩各政党团体发表声明  叙利亚表示永远支持黎巴嫩的斗争

1956-10-16
第6版()
专栏：

    号召人民团结对付伊拉克石油公司的阴谋
    黎巴嫩各政党团体发表声明
    叙利亚表示永远支持黎巴嫩的斗争
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特13日讯：“电讯报”报道：黎巴嫩各政党和团体发表了一个联合声明说，黎巴嫩人民希望巩固他们的主权，而伊拉克石油公司却企图驾凌于这个国家和政府之上。声明要求人民“团结起来对付伊拉克石油公司的阴谋，支持黎巴嫩政府保卫这个国家的民族利益”。
    据新华社15日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特13日讯：据黎巴嫩“电讯报”报道，叙利亚外交部长比塔尔在谈到黎巴嫩同伊拉克石油公司的纠纷的时候说，“叙利亚将永远站在黎巴嫩这一边。我们反对伊拉克石油公司提出的敷设一条通到班尼亚斯的输油管的要求。”
    比塔尔指出，这次纠纷是帝国主义对付阿拉伯国家的整套阴谋中的一个环节。
