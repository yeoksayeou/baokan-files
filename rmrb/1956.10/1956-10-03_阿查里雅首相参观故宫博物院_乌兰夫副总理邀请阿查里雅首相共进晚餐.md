Title: Prime Minister Acharya Visits the Palace Museum; Vice Premier Ulanhu Invites Prime Minister Acharya to Dinner

Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd -- Nepalese Prime Minister Acharya, his wife, and some accompanying senior officials visited the Palace Museum this morning. Chen Qiao, the deputy director of the museum, briefed the Prime Minister on the museum's restoration and renovation over the past few years. Chen Qiao also presented the Prime Minister with a five-volume set of reproduced Song Dynasty paintings. The Prime Minister said he would donate this set of famous Chinese Song Dynasty paintings to the National Library of Nepal for preservation. The museum also gave each of the other accompanying officials a set of pictures of the Palace Museum's architecture.
    The Nepalese Prime Minister and others also visited the ancient Chinese cultural relics preserved in the three main halls of the Palace Museum.
    Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the Prime Minister and others on the visit today.
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd -- Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu invited the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Acharya, and his wife to dinner this evening.
    Also invited from the Kingdom of Nepal was B. Misra, Chairman of the Nepal Praja Parishad party.
    Among those accompanying the dinner were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and his wife, and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife.

### 阿查里雅首相参观故宫博物院  乌兰夫副总理邀请阿查里雅首相共进晚餐

1956-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相参观故宫博物院
    乌兰夫副总理邀请阿查里雅首相共进晚餐
    据新华社2日讯　尼泊尔首相阿查里雅和他的夫人以及部分随行高级官员今天上午参观了故宫博物院。博物院副院长陈乔向首相介绍了这个博物院几年来恢复和整修的情况。陈乔并且送给首相一套五大本复制的宋人画册。首相说他将把这套中国宋代名画送给尼泊尔国家图书馆珍藏。博物院还送给其他随行官员每人一套故宫建筑图片。
    尼泊尔首相等还参观了保存在故宫三大殿的中国古代文物。
    今天陪同首相等参观的有外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
  新华社2日讯　国务院副总理乌兰夫在今天晚间邀请尼泊尔王国首相阿查里雅和夫人共进晚餐。
    尼泊尔王国方面应邀出席的还有尼泊尔人民党主席巴·米斯腊。
    陪同进餐的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人。
