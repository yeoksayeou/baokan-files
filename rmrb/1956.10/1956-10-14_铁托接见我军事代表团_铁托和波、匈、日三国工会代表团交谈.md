Title: Tito Receives Chinese Military Delegation; Tito Talks with Trade Union Delegations from Poland, Hungary, and Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 13th: Belgrade News: Yugoslav President and Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces, Tito, received the Chinese military delegation headed by General Deng Hua in Belgrade on the morning of October 13th.

　　General Deng Hua presented a gift from the Chinese People's Liberation Army to President Tito. President Tito expressed his gratitude to the Chinese guests and had a cordial conversation with them.

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, Yugoslav President Tito received the Polish Trade Union Delegation, the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation, and the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan Delegation in Belgrade on the 12th, and had talks with the delegation members.

　　These three delegations were invited by the Central Council of the Federation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia to visit Yugoslavia.

### 铁托接见我军事代表团  铁托和波、匈、日三国工会代表团交谈

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托接见我军事代表团
    铁托和波、匈、日三国工会代表团交谈
    据新华社布拉格13日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫总统、武装部队最高统帅铁托10月13日上午在贝尔格莱德接见了以邓华上将为首的中国军事代表团。
    邓华上将代表中国人民解放军向铁托总统赠礼。铁托总统对中国来宾表示感谢，并且和他们进行了亲切的交谈。
    据新华社13日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫总统铁托12日在贝尔格莱德接见了波兰工会代表团、匈牙利工会代表团和日本工会总评议会代表团，并且和代表团人员进行了交谈。
    这三个代表团是应南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会的邀请到南斯拉夫来访问的。
