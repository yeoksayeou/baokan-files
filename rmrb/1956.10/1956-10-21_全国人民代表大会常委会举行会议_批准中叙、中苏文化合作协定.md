Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting, Approves Sino-Syrian and Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreements
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 49th meeting on the afternoon of the 20th.

　　After hearing the explanation by Burhan, the head of the Chinese cultural and art delegation that visited Syria and other countries, the meeting decided to approve the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Syrian Republic. After hearing the explanation by Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture, the meeting decided to approve the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

　　The meeting also passed appointments.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  批准中叙、中苏文化合作协定

1956-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    批准中叙、中苏文化合作协定
    新华社20日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在20日下午举行第四十九次会议。
    会议在听取前访问叙利亚等国的中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉的说明后，决定批准中华人民共和国政府、叙利亚共和国政府文化合作协定；在听取文化部副部长钱俊瑞的说明后，决定批准中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟文化合作协定。
    会议还通过了任命事项。
