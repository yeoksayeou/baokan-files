Title: Shen Yanbing Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to Ukrainian National Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: The National Dance Ensemble of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union, which has performed in ten cities in our country for nearly four months, will leave Beijing on the 25th to return home.

Tonight, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing and Vice Minister Xia Yan held a farewell banquet at the Xinqiao Hotel in Beijing.

At the beginning of the banquet, Shen Yanbing, on behalf of the Ministry of Culture, congratulated the dance ensemble on the great success it had achieved in its performances in various places. Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, also spoke at the banquet. He said that Chinese dance workers had learned many excellent programs from them, and they should also take some Chinese dance programs back with them.

The dance ensemble's artistic director, Virsky, thanked the hosts on behalf of all the members of the group, saying that this visit and performance had become a major event in the lives of all the members and would never be forgotten.

### 沈雁冰举行宴会送别乌克兰国家舞蹈团

1956-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰举行宴会送别乌克兰国家舞蹈团
    据新华社24日讯　在我国十个城市作了近四个月访问演出的苏联乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国国家舞蹈团将在25日离京返国。
    今晚，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长夏衍在北京新侨饭店举行送别宴会。
    宴会开始，沈雁冰代表文化部祝贺舞蹈团在各地访问演出所获得的巨大成功。中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权也在席间讲话。他说，中国舞蹈工作者向你们学了许多优秀节目，你们也要带一些中国的舞蹈节目回去。
    舞蹈团艺术指导维尔斯基代表全团人员向主人道谢，他说，这次访问演出已经成为全团人员生活中的重大事件，永远不能忘怀。
