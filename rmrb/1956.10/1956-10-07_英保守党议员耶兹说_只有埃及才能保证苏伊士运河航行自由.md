Title: British Conservative MP Yates Says Only Egypt Can Guarantee Freedom of Navigation on the Suez Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 3rd (TASS): According to the Arab News Agency, British Conservative MP Yates, who is in Cairo, told reporters that only Egypt can guarantee freedom of navigation on the Suez Canal. He said that no other country could do this because the canal passes through Egyptian territory.
    Yates expressed his belief that the Suez issue could be resolved at an international conference to re-examine the 1888 Convention. When talking about the attitude of Western countries towards Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal, Yates said, "Any form of hysteria is completely incompatible with good diplomacy when dealing with Egypt or any other country in Africa and Asia."
    The British MP said that he would support Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal after returning to London.
    President Nasser received Yates on October 2nd.

### 英保守党议员耶兹说  只有埃及才能保证苏伊士运河航行自由

1956-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    英保守党议员耶兹说
    只有埃及才能保证苏伊士运河航行自由
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社开罗3日讯：据阿拉伯通讯社报道，在开罗的英国保守党议员耶兹向报界说，只有埃及才能够保证苏伊士运河的航行自由。他说，任何其他国家都不能做到这一点，因为运河流过埃及的领土。
    耶兹表示相信苏伊士问题能够在重新审议1888年公约的国际会议上解决。耶兹在谈到西方国家对埃及把苏伊士运河收归国有的态度的时候说：“同埃及或者非洲和亚洲的其他任何国家打交道的时候，任何形式的歇斯特里都是同良好的外交完全不相容的。”
    这位英国议员说，他回到伦敦以后将支持埃及把苏伊士运河收归国有。
    纳赛尔总统在10月2日接见了耶兹。
