Title: Soviet and Hungarian Party Leaders Hold Talks; Italian Communist Party Delegation Holds Talks with Yugoslav Communist Party Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th - TASS Moscow dispatch: Members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan and Suslov, held talks on October 6th at the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union with the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Gerő, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Kádár and Szántó, and member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Hildász.
    The talks were held in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and complete understanding.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th - According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, talks between the Italian Communist Party delegation and representatives of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia began on the morning of the 6th. Both sides have already held an initial exchange of views. The talks will continue on the afternoon of the 6th.
    All members of the Italian delegation, headed by Deputy General Secretary Longo, participated in the talks. The Yugoslav side was represented by members of the Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Vukmanović, and members of the Central Committee, Kraigher, Marković, Tomšič, Vlahović, Popović, Begović, and Sekulić.

### 苏匈两国党的领导人会谈  意共代表团同南共代表会谈

1956-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏匈两国党的领导人会谈
    意共代表团同南共代表会谈
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员米高扬和苏斯洛夫10月6日在苏联共产党中央委员会同匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记格罗、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会政治局委员卡达尔、希达什，匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会委员桑托进行了会谈。
    这次会谈是在真诚友好和完全谅解的气氛中进行的。
    新华社7日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，意大利共产党代表团和南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟代表们之间的会谈在6日上午开始。双方已经举行了第一次的意见交换。会谈将在6日下午继续进行。
    以副总书记隆哥为首的意大利代表团全体团员都参加了会谈。南斯拉夫方面的代表有南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟执行委员会委员伏克曼诺维奇和中央委员会委员克拉伊格尔、马尔科维奇、汤姆斯克、弗拉霍维奇、波波维奇、贝戈维奇和塞库利奇。
