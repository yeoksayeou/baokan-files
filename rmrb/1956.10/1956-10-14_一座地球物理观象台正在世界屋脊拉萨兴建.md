Title: A Geophysical Observatory is Being Built in Lhasa, the Roof of the World
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report: Construction of a geophysical observatory has begun in the ancient city of Lhasa, on the roof of the world. The Institute of Geophysics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences has already transported some special building materials to Lhasa for this observatory.

The main construction of the observatory is expected to be completed by the end of this year, and observations can begin in early next year.

The establishment of this geophysical observatory is mainly for observing geophysical phenomena such as geomagnetism and earthquakes on the plateau. It will provide relevant research data for the upcoming International Geophysical Year from 1957 to 1958. The complete set of instruments and equipment for the observatory is being assembled, and two of the staff members are Tibetan youths.

### 一座地球物理观象台正在世界屋脊拉萨兴建

1956-10-14
第7版()
专栏：

    一座地球物理观象台正在世界屋脊拉萨兴建
    新华社讯　一座地球物理观象台开始在世界屋脊上的拉萨古城动工修建。中国科学院地球物理研究所为这个观象台准备的一部分特殊建筑材料，已经运到拉萨。
    观象台的主要工程预计今年年底可以建成，明年初就可以开始观测。
    这座地球物理观象台的建立，主要是观测高原上的地磁、地震等地球物理观象，它将为即将举行的1957年到1958年国际地球物理年提供有关研究资料。观象台的全套仪器设备正在装配，工作人员中有两名是藏族青年。
