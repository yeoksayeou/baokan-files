Title: Soong Ching-ling Hosts Banquet for President Sukarno
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 11th - Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, hosted a banquet at her residence in Shanghai tonight to welcome President Sukarno.

President Sukarno inscribed a message here for the magazine "China Reconstructs" founded by Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling.

After the banquet, Soong Ching-ling and others accompanied President Sukarno for a walk in the garden, and Soong Ching-ling presented the President with a pair of white doves symbolizing peace.

Also invited to the banquet tonight were Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani and Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopranto.

Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai; Ke Qingshi, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Vice-Mayors Xu Jianguo and Cao Diqiu; Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also attended as guests.

### 宋庆龄宴请苏加诺总统

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄宴请苏加诺总统
    新华社上海11日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今晚在上海的住所里设宴欢迎苏加诺总统。
    苏加诺总统在这里为宋庆龄副委员长创办的杂志“中国建设”题了词。
    宴会后，宋庆龄等陪同苏加诺总统在花园里散步，宋庆龄并将一对象征和平的白鸽送给总统。
    今晚应邀出席宴会的，还有印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼，印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托。
    上海市市长陈毅，政协上海市委员会主席柯庆施，副市长许建国、曹荻秋，中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇，外交部礼宾司司长王倬如等都出席作陪。
