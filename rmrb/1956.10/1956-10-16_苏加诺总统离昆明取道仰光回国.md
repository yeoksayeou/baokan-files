Title: President Sukarno Leaves Kunming for Home via Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 15th - Indonesian President Sukarno and his accompanying senior officials left Kunming by special plane at 10:05 this morning, heading home via Rangoon.

Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen accompanied President Sukarno to Rangoon today. The Indonesian women's delegation visiting China also returned to Indonesia with President Sukarno.

Today, the Indonesian and Chinese national flags fluttered at Kunming Airport, and more than 10,000 men and women of all ethnic groups gathered at the airport to bid farewell to President Sukarno. As the President entered the airport amidst enthusiastic cheers, the sounds of elephant-foot drums, Lusheng pipes, Tibetan hand drums, and A'xi's large three-stringed instruments rang out. Young men and women of ethnic minorities danced the Aini dance, A'xi Jumping Moon dance, and Tibetan hand drum dance. President Sukarno walked up to the Tibetan girls, took a hand drum, and played together with the dancing Tibetan youths. At this moment, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani danced joyfully to the accompaniment of the President's hand drum. Later, the President also danced with his two friends.

Next, President Sukarno shook hands with Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Xie Fuzhi, Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, Major General Jin Rubo, Head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, and the heads of various departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations of Yunnan Province and Kunming City, to bid farewell.

Before boarding the plane, President Sukarno repeatedly shook hands with Vice Premier Chen Yi to say goodbye. Vice Premier Chen Yi said to the President: "The success of the President's visit to China this time is an honor for the Chinese people." President Sukarno replied: "I am very touched by the warm reception I received from the Chinese government and the Chinese people during my visit to China. Please convey my greetings to the Chinese people." He also said to Vice Premier Chen Yi: "I wish the Marshal good health and hope to see you again in Indonesia!" President Sukarno accepted the red scarf presented by the Young Pioneers and boarded the plane, waving goodbye to the well-wishers.

Also at the airport to see him off today were Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijayapranoto, and diplomatic officials from the Union of Burma and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam stationed in Kunming.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 15th - Indonesian President Sukarno and his entourage arrived in Rangoon this afternoon from Kunming on a special Chinese plane. Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen also arrived with President Sukarno.

Those who greeted him at the airport included Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe, Deputy Prime Ministers Sao Hkun Hkio and U Kyaw Nyein, and various ministers. Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and diplomatic envoys from various countries also welcomed him at the airport.

During his stay in Rangoon, President Sukarno will be the guest of Burmese President Ba U. He is scheduled to leave Rangoon on October 16th.

### 苏加诺总统离昆明取道仰光回国

1956-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统离昆明取道仰光回国
    新华社昆明15日电　印度尼西亚苏加诺总统和随行的高级官员今天上午十时零五分乘专机离开昆明，取道仰光回国。
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇今天陪送苏加诺总统前往仰光。在中国进行访问的印度尼西亚妇女代表团也随同苏加诺总统返回印度尼西亚。
    今天，昆明机场上飘扬着印度尼西亚和中国国旗，一万多各民族男女在机场上欢送苏加诺总统。当总统在热烈的欢呼声中进入机场时，机场上响起了象脚鼓声、芦笙声、藏族的手鼓声和阿细人的大三弦声，少数民族青年男女们跳起了爱尼舞、阿细跳月和藏族手鼓舞。苏加诺总统走到藏族姑娘们的面前，接过手鼓，和正在起舞的藏族青年们一起合奏，这时，陈毅副总理、印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼在总统手鼓的伴奏下跳起了愉快的舞蹈。后来，总统也和他的两位朋友一起起舞。
    接着，苏加诺总统和云南省副省长刘明辉，政协云南省委员会主席谢富治，昆明市市长潘朔端，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长金如柏少将以及云南省、昆明市各机关、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人一一握手告别。
    苏加诺总统在上机前一再和陈毅副总理握手道别。陈毅副总理对总统说：“总统这次访问中国的成功，是中国人民的荣幸”，苏加诺总统回答说：“这次在中国访问，受到中国政府和中国人民的热情接待，使我非常感动，向中国人民致意。”他还向陈毅副总理说：“祝元帅身体健康，愿在印度尼西亚再见！”苏加诺总统在接受了少年先锋队员们献的红领巾后走上飞机，并向欢送者频频招手告别。
    今天到机场欢送的，还有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如，印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托和缅甸联邦、越南民主共和国驻昆明的外交官员。
    新华社仰光15日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和他的随行人员今天下午乘中国的专机从昆明到达仰光。陪同苏加诺总统一起到达的还有中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
    到机场迎接的有缅甸总理吴巴瑞、副总理藻昆卓和吴觉迎以及各部部长。中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和各国使节也到机场欢迎。
    苏加诺总统在仰光停留期间将作为缅甸总统巴宇的贵宾。他订于10月16日离开仰光。
