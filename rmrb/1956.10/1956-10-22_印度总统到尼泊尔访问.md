Title: Indian President Visits Nepal
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 21st: According to a Kathmandu dispatch from the Indian Press Trust: Indian President Prasad and his wife, along with accompanying personnel, arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by plane today. President Prasad will be on a four-day visit to Nepal. King Mahendra of Nepal welcomed him at the airport.

### 印度总统到尼泊尔访问

1956-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    印度总统到尼泊尔访问
    据新华社新德里21日电　据印度报业托辣斯加德满都消息：印度总统普拉沙德和夫人以及随行人员在今天乘飞机到达尼泊尔首都加德满都。普拉沙德总统将在尼泊尔进行为期四天的访问。尼泊尔国王马亨德拉到机场欢迎。
