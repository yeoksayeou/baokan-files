Title: Tanggangzi Sanatorium Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Tanggangzi Sanatorium for Chinese Coal Miners, located very close to Anshan, has recently been completed.

This sanatorium was built by the Ministry of Coal Industry and the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union and has two hundred to two hundred and fifty beds. By mid-October, about one hundred coal miners will be admitted for recuperation.

Coal miners are most susceptible to rheumatoid arthritis. This hot spring sanatorium is specifically for the treatment of arthritis in the coal mining system's employees.

### 汤岗子疗养院落成

1956-10-13
第2版()
专栏：

    汤岗子疗养院落成
    据新华社讯　离鞍山很近的中国煤矿工人汤岗子疗养院最近落成。
    这个疗养院是由煤炭工业部、中国煤矿工会全国委员会建设的，有床位二百到二百五十张。到10月中旬，将有一百名左右煤矿工人入院疗养。
    煤矿工人最容易患风湿性关节炎。这座温泉疗养院，就是专门治疗煤矿系统职工的关节炎的。
