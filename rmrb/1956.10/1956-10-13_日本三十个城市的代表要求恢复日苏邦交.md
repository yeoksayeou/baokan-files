Title: Representatives of Thirty Japanese Cities Demand Restoration of Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th—News: Representatives of residents and business circles from thirty cities in the Sea of Japan area held a mass meeting on the 10th in Nanao City, Ishikawa Prefecture.
    Those attending the meeting passed a resolution demanding the speedy restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union, the development of trade relations with China, Korea, and other neighboring countries, and the abolition of the ban prohibiting the export of Japanese goods to these countries.

### 日本三十个城市的代表要求恢复日苏邦交

1956-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    日本三十个城市的代表要求恢复日苏邦交
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：日本海一带的三十个城市的居民代表和商界代表10日在石川县七尾市举行群众大会。
    出席大会的人们通过决议，要求迅速恢复日本同苏联的外交关系，发展和中国、朝鲜及其他邻国的贸易关系，并且废除禁止日本商品向这些国家输出的禁令。
