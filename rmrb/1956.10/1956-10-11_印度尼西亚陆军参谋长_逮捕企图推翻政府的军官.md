Title: Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Arrests Officers Attempting to Overthrow the Government
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 9th: The Indonesian news agency reported today that Army Chief of Staff Major General Nasution ordered the arrest on October 4th of several army officers accused of attempting to alter or overthrow the legitimate government.

Army Intelligence Officer Lieutenant Colonel Piengadie confirmed this news today, saying that there have recently been indications that a small number of army officers are actively participating in activities with definite political aims. He said that participating in political activities means "attempting to alter or overthrow the legitimate government."

### 印度尼西亚陆军参谋长  逮捕企图推翻政府的军官

1956-10-11
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚陆军参谋长
    逮捕企图推翻政府的军官
    据新华社雅加达9日电　印度尼西亚新闻社今天报道，陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安少将已经在10月4日下令逮捕一些被控企图改变或者推翻合法政府的陆军军官。
    陆军情报军官皮恩加迪中校今天证实了这个消息，他说，近来有迹象表明，少数陆军军官正在积极参加有肯定的政治目的的活动。他说，参加政治活动即是指“企图改变或者推翻合法政府”。
