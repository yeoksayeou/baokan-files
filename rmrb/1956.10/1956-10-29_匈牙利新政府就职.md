Title: Hungarian New Government Takes Office
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 28th: According to the Hungarian Telegraph Agency, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, based on the recommendation of the National Committee of the Patriotic People's Front, approved the list of members of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic on the 27th. The new government has taken office.

The list of government personnel is as follows: Chairman of the Council of Ministers: Imre Nagy; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers: Antal Apró; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers: József Bognár; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers: Ferenc Erdei; Minister of State: Zoltán Tildy; Minister of Foreign Affairs: Imre Horváth; Minister of Interior: Ferenc Münnich; Minister of Defense: Károly Janza; Minister of Agriculture : Béla Kovács; Minister of Finance: István Kossa; Minister of Justice: Erik Molnár; Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Manufacturing Industry: János Csergő; Minister of Health: Antal Babics; Minister of Municipal Management: Ferenc Nezvál; Minister of Food Industry: Rezső Nyers; Minister of Domestic Trade: János Tausz; Minister of Culture: György Lukács; Chairman of the National Planning Bureau: Árpád Kiss; Minister of Light Industry: Józsefné Nagy; Minister of State Farms: Miklós Ribyánszki; Minister of Construction: Antal Apró; Minister of Posts and Telecommunications and Transportation: Lajos Bebrits; Minister of Education: Albert Kónya; Minister of Foreign Trade: József Bognár; Minister of Power and Coal Mining Industry: Sándor Czottner; Minister of Chemical Industry: Gergely Szabó.

The new government issued its first decree, signed by Imre Nagy, establishing the Government Social Supply Committee, headed by Zoltán Vas, to ensure the food supply to residents.

### 匈牙利新政府就职

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利新政府就职
    新华社布拉格28日电　据匈牙利通讯社报道，匈牙利人民共和国主席团根据爱国人民阵线全国委员会的建议，在27日批准了匈牙利人民共和国政府组成人员的名单。新政府已经就职。
    政府人员名单如下：部长会议主席伊姆雷·纳吉；部长会议副主席安托尔·奥普罗；部长会议副主席约瑟夫·波格纳尔；部长会议副主席费伦茨·埃尔德；国务部长佐尔坦·蒂尔迪；外交部长伊姆雷·霍瓦特；内政部长费伦茨·明尼奇；国防部长卡罗伊·扬查；农业部长伯拉·科瓦奇；财政部长伊斯特万·科沙；司法部长埃里克·莫尔纳尔；冶金和机器制造工业部长亚诺什·策尔戈；卫生部长安托尔·巴比奇；市镇管理部长费伦茨·内兹瓦尔；食品工业部长雷热·涅尔什；国内贸易部长亚诺什·塔乌斯；文化部长捷尔吉·卢卡奇；国家计划局局长阿帕德·基什；轻工业部长约瑟夫妮·纳吉；国营农场部长米克洛什·里比安斯基；建造部长安托尔·奥普罗；邮电和交通部长拉约什·贝布里奇；教育部长艾伯特·科尼亚；对外贸易部长约瑟夫·波格纳尔；动力和煤矿工业部长山多尔·卓特纳；化学工业部长格尔格伊·萨波。
    新政府颁布了由伊姆雷·纳吉签署的第一条法令，成立了由佐尔坦·瓦什领导的政府社会供应委员会，以保证对居民的粮食供应。
