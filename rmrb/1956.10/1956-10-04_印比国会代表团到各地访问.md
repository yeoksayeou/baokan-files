Title: Indian and Belgian Parliamentary Delegations Visit Various Locations

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. After attending China's National Day celebrations, the parliamentary delegations from the Republic of India and the Kingdom of Belgium left Beijing this morning to visit other cities in China.
    The Indian Parliamentary Delegation will first go to Shenyang to visit Northeast China, and then to Nanjing, Shanghai and other places.
    The Belgian Parliamentary Delegation will first visit Nanjing, and then Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places.

### 印比国会代表团到各地访问

1956-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印比国会代表团到各地访问
    新华社3日讯　印度共和国国会代表团和比利时王国国会代表团参加中国国庆典礼后，在今天上午离开北京，前往中国其他城市参观访问。
    印度国会代表团将先到沈阳，在中国东北地区参观，然后前往南京、上海等地访问。
    比利时国会代表团先到南京访问，以后再访问上海、广州、武汉等地。
