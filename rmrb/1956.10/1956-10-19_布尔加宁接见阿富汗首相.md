Title: Bulganin Receives Afghan Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: TASS reported: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin and Soviet Supreme Soviet Presidium Deputy Chairman Tarasov received Afghan Prime Minister Daoud separately in the Kremlin today.
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: Afghan Prime Minister Mohammad Daoud arrived in Moscow by plane on the 17th at the invitation of the Soviet government.
　　At the Central Airport, the Afghan Prime Minister and his entourage were welcomed by Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin, Soviet Supreme Soviet Presidium member Khrushchev, Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairmen Mikoyan and Pervukhin, and Soviet Defense Minister Zhukov, among others.

### 布尔加宁接见阿富汗首相

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见阿富汗首相
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫，今天在克里姆林宫分别接见了阿富汗首相达乌德。
    新华社莫斯科18日电　阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德应苏联政府的邀请，在17日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    在中央飞机场上，阿富汗首相和他的随行人员受到了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和别尔乌辛、苏联国防部长朱可夫等人的欢迎。
