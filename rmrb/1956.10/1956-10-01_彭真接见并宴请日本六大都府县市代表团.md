Title: Peng Zhen Receives and Hosts Delegation from Six Major Metropolitan Prefectures and Cities of Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, received the entire delegation from the six major metropolitan prefectures and cities of Japan visiting China this morning.

Present at the reception were Vice Mayors of Beijing, Wang Kunlun, Cheng Hongyi, and Le Songsheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, Chai Zemin, and Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, Xin Yi.

During the reception, the head of the delegation, Shoichiro Ando, presented gifts to Mayor Peng Zhen.

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, hosted a banquet at noon today in honor of the delegation from the six major metropolitan prefectures and cities of Japan visiting China.

The banquet was presided over by Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wang Kunlun, on behalf of Mayor Peng Zhen.

In his speech at the banquet, Wang Kunlun extended a warm welcome to the delegation. He said that this visit will further deepen mutual understanding and friendly relations between the cities of the two countries, and will help promote the normalization of relations between the two countries.

In his speech, the head of the delegation, Shoichiro Ando, thanked Mayor Peng Zhen for the invitation. He said that we feel regret that diplomatic relations between China and Japan have not yet been restored, and we are still in a state where we cannot fully invite many Chinese friends to visit Japan. However, we believe that diplomatic relations between China and Japan will not be severed forever, and that a day will come when we can freely visit each other.

Accompanying the guests at the banquet were Vice Mayors of Beijing, Cheng Hongyi and Le Songsheng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ling Qijun, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, Wang Jiong, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers, Lao She, heads of various departments of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

Japanese person in Beijing, Togo Kameda, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 彭真接见并宴请日本六大都府县市代表团

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    彭真接见并宴请日本六大都府县市代表团
    新华社30日讯　北京市市长彭真今天上午接见了日本六大都、府、县、市访华代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有北京市副市长王昆仑、程宏毅、乐松生，北京市人民委员会秘书长柴泽民和外事处处长辛毅。
    接见的时候，代表团团长安藤章一郎向彭真市长赠送了礼品。
    新华社30日讯　北京市市长彭真今天中午设宴招待日本六大都、府、县、市访华代表团。
    宴会由北京市副市长王昆仑代表彭真市长主持。
    王昆仑在宴会上的致词中，对代表团表示热烈欢迎。他说，你们这次访问，将进一步加深两国城市之间的互相了解和友好关系，有助于促进两国关系的正常化。
    代表团团长安藤章一郎在讲话中，感谢北京市长彭真的邀请。他说，我们觉得遗憾的是，中日两国之间还没有恢复邦交，我们还处在不能够充分地邀请许多中国朋友到日本访问的状态中。但是，我们相信，中日两国的邦交是不会永远断绝的，总有一天会有完全自由地彼此来往的日子到来。
    出席宴会作陪的，有北京市副市长程宏毅、乐松生，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会副主席凌其峻，北京市工会联合会主席王炯，北京市文学艺术工作者联合会主席老舍，北京市人民委员会各部门负责人和北京市各界人士。
    在京的日本人士龟田东伍也应邀出席了宴会。
