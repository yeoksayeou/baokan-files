Title: David Marshall Leaves Beijing for Singapore
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th—David Marshall, Chairman of the Labor Front of Singapore and former Chief Minister of Singapore, left Beijing by plane this morning and will return to Singapore via Guangzhou.

At the airport, Marshall told reporters that he welcomed Premier Zhou Enlai's views on the nationality of Chinese in Singapore when he met with him on October 9. He said that this very important clarification regarding the policy of the Chinese People's Government would greatly help the growing friendship between Singapore and China. He also hoped that this policy would encourage Singaporean Chinese to work with people of all ethnic groups to build a stable and independent Singapore. Marshall said, "I thank the Premier for his friendly understanding and his generous assistance."

Those who went to the airport to see David Marshall off today included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Liao Chengzhi, member of the Standing Committee; Chen Hansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Luo Longji, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General.

### 戴维·马歇尔离北京回新加坡

1956-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    戴维·马歇尔离北京回新加坡
    新华社13日讯　新加坡劳工阵线主席、前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，将经广州回新加坡。
    马歇尔在机场对记者说，他欢迎周恩来总理10月9日接见他的时候所表示的关于新加坡中国人的国籍问题的意见。他说这个有关中国人民政府政策的非常重要的阐明将大大有助于新加坡同中国之间日益发展的友谊。他并希望这个政策将鼓励新加坡中国人同其他各族人民一道为建立稳定、独立的新加坡而努力。马歇尔说：“我感谢总理的友善的谅解和他的慷慨的协助。”
    今天到机场欢送戴维·马歇尔的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，常务委员廖承志，中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、乔冠华、罗隆基、秘书长吴茂荪等人。
