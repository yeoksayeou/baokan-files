Title: Qinghai Youth Gather to Commemorate Lu Xun
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: More than 800 youths from all walks of life in Xining City gathered on October 11 to commemorate Lu Xun. Cheng Xiushan, Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles, gave a report on Lu Xun's life and deeds at the meeting. Li Shaling, Editor-in-Chief of Qinghai Youth Daily, delivered a speech entitled "What Should Young People Learn from Lu Xun?"

Recently, some schools and organizations in Xining City have held commemorative Lu Xun forums, exhibitions of his works and pictures, poetry recitations, and wall newspapers. In the coming days, all schools, organizations, factories, and mines will universally hold activities to commemorate Lu Xun.

### 西宁青年集会纪念鲁迅

1956-10-16
第7版()
专栏：

    西宁青年集会纪念鲁迅
    据新华社讯　西宁市各界青年八百多人在10月11日集会纪念鲁迅。青海省文联副主席程秀山在会上作了关于鲁迅生平事迹的报告。青海青年报总编辑李沙铃作了“青年应该向鲁迅学习什么”的演说。
    最近，西宁市部分学校、机关举办了纪念鲁迅的座谈会、著作和图片展览、作品朗诵会、壁报等，今后数日内，各学校、机关、厂矿都要普遍举行纪念鲁迅的活动。
