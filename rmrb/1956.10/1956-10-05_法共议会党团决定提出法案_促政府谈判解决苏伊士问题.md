Title: French Communist Parliamentary Group Decides to Propose a Bill to Urge the Government to Negotiate a Solution to the Suez Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 3rd: According to TASS, the French Communist Party's parliamentary group held a meeting on October 2nd and decided to submit a number of bills to the recently reconvened National Assembly. It requested that the National Assembly discuss these bills with particular speed. These bills include proposals advocating that the government immediately hold negotiations with the plenipotentiary representatives of the Algerian people regarding a cessation of hostilities and a peaceful resolution of the Algerian question, as well as proposals regarding a negotiated solution to the Suez Canal issue. In addition, the Communist parliamentary group intends to put forward a number of proposals concerning the improvement of the material conditions of the French working people.

The parliamentary group's resolution emphasized that the Communist parliamentarians will resolutely defend the program they put forward to the voters, and will, in accordance with the resolutions of the Fourteenth Congress of the French Communist Party, do their utmost to implement the policy of unified action of the working class and unite all democratic forces in the country.

### 法共议会党团决定提出法案  促政府谈判解决苏伊士问题

1956-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    法共议会党团决定提出法案
    促政府谈判解决苏伊士问题
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社巴黎3日讯：法国共产党议会党团在10月2日举行会议，决定向最近复会的国民议会提出一些法案，并且要求国民议会特别迅速地讨论这些法案，这些法案中包括主张政府立刻同阿尔及利亚人民的全权代表举行关于停止战争和和平解决阿尔及利亚问题的谈判的建议，关于通过谈判解决苏伊士运河问题的建议。此外，共产党议会党团还打算提出一些关于改善法国劳动人民的物质状况的建议。
    议会党团的决议着重指出，共产党议员将坚决保卫他们向选民提出的纲领，并且将根据法国共产党第十四次代表大会的决议，竭尽全力执行工人阶级行动一致的政策，把国内的一切民主力量团结起来。
