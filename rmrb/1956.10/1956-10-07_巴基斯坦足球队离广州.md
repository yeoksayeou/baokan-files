Title: The Pakistan Football Team Leaves Guangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th. The Pakistan National Football Team, a group of nineteen, left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return to their country. Liang Kehan, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, and others went to the station to see them off. (In the news about the Pakistan football team's return to their country published on page 4 of this newspaper on the 6th, the team's departure from Beijing by train on the 9th was a mistake for the 3rd.)

### 巴基斯坦足球队离广州

1956-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦足球队离广州
    新华社广州6日电　巴基斯坦国家足球队一行十九人，今天上午乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。中华全国体育总会广东省分会副主席梁克寒等多人曾往车站欢送。（6日本报第四版刊登巴基斯坦足球队回国的消息中，该队在9日乘火车离京，是3日之误。）
