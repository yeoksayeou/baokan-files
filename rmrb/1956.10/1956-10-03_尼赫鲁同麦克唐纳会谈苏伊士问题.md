Title: Nehru Meets with MacDonald to Discuss Suez Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 2nd. Indian Prime Minister Nehru received British High Commissioner to India MacDonald on October 1st and held a 45-minute meeting with him.

    A British spokesperson said that this was a "routine discussion on the Suez issue."

    Indian Minister Without Portfolio Menon, who recently held talks with Egyptian and British authorities on the Suez issue, returned to New Delhi on September 30.

### 尼赫鲁同麦克唐纳会谈苏伊士问题

1956-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁同麦克唐纳会谈苏伊士问题
    新华社新德里2日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在10月1日接见英国驻印度高级专员麦克唐纳，并且同他作了四十五分钟的会谈。
    一位英国发言人说，这是一次“例行的关于苏伊士问题的讨论”。
    最近曾经先后同埃及和英国当局就苏伊士问题举行了会谈的印度不管部长梅农，已经在9月30日回到新德里。
