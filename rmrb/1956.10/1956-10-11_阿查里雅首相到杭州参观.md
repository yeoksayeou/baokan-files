Title: Prime Minister Acharya Visits Hangzhou

Author:

Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th – The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, and his wife, along with accompanying senior Nepalese officials, concluded their visit to Shanghai and flew to Hangzhou by special plane this morning.

Xu Jianguo, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and his wife, Deputy Mayor Liu Shuzhou, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Shen Zhiyuan and Li Zhaohuan, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Shanghai went to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 10th – The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, his wife, and accompanying senior officials, accompanied by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou, and others, visited Hangzhou today.

When the distinguished guests arrived at the Huafeng Paper Mill in the morning, male and female workers gathered at the entrance, beating gongs and drums to welcome them. The distinguished guests carefully visited the factory's pulping and papermaking workshops and inquired in detail about raw materials, product types, and the production process.

Prime Minister Acharya told Xia Xianli, the director of the paper mill, "Nepal is also preparing to start construction of a paper mill soon."

At Lingyin Temple, the distinguished guests visited the Hall of Great Strength (Mahavira Hall) which was under construction. Dharma Master Xingkong told Prime Minister Acharya that it was rebuilt by the People's Government, as the old Hall of Great Strength had collapsed. The distinguished guests carefully viewed the completed statue of Sakyamuni meditating. When Prime Minister Acharya left Lingyin Temple, he also invited Dharma Master Xingkong to come to Nepal to worship the birthplace of Sakyamuni.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests braved the drizzle to tour West Lake, admiring its beautiful scenery.

In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a banquet held by Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, to welcome them. Prime Minister Acharya said at the banquet that he felt like he was in his hometown of Kathmandu when he arrived in Hangzhou. He expressed his gratitude for the deep friendship and warm hospitality of the people of Hangzhou. After the banquet, the distinguished guests also watched the film "The Butterfly Lovers."

Prime Minister Acharya arrived in Hangzhou by special plane this morning from Shanghai. Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu,负责人 of the Zhejiang Military Region, and负责人 of various democratic parties and people's organizations went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿查里雅首相到杭州参观

1956-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相到杭州参观
    新华社上海10日电　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔高级官员结束了在上海的访问，今天上午乘专机前往杭州。
    上海市副市长许建国和夫人，副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会副主席沈志远、黎照寰以及上海各界著名人士都到飞机场欢送。
    新华社杭州10日电　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和他的夫人以及随行的高级官员在中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人、外交部礼宾司副司长马振武、杭州市副市长顾春林等陪同下，今天在杭州参观游览。
    当贵宾们在上午抵达华丰造纸厂的时候，男女职工敲锣打鼓地齐集在门口迎接。贵宾们仔细参观了这个工厂的制浆、造纸等车间，又详细地询问原料、生产品种和生产过程。
    阿查里雅首相告诉造纸厂厂长夏宪礼说：“不久，尼泊尔也准备动工兴建一个造纸厂”。
    在灵隐寺，贵宾们参观了正在兴建的大雄宝殿。性空法师向阿查里雅首相介绍它是人民政府重修的，过去的大雄宝殿已经倒坍。贵宾们在这里仔细地观看了已经修成的释迦牟尼修道的佛像。阿查里雅首相在离开灵隐寺的时候，还邀请性空法师将来到尼泊尔去朝拜释迦牟尼诞生地。
    下午，贵宾们冒着蒙蒙细雨游览了西湖。贵宾们对西湖的湖光山色赞美不已。
    晚上，贵宾们出席了浙江省省长沙文汉为欢迎他们而举行的宴会。阿查里雅首相在宴会上讲话说，他到了杭州就像到了自己的家乡加德满都一样。他对杭州人民的深厚友谊和盛情款待表示感谢。宴会后，贵宾们还看了电影“梁山伯与祝英台”。
    阿查里雅首相是今天上午乘专机从上海来到杭州的。浙江省省长沙文汉、杭州市市长吴宪、浙江军区负责人周贯五中将和各民主党派、各人民团体负责人等都前往机场欢迎。
