Title: General Secretary of the All-India United Church, Reverend Williams, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- Reverend Williams, General Secretary of the All-India United Church, arrived in Beijing on the 29th. He is visiting China at the invitation of Wu Yaozong, Chairman of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of the Chinese Christian Church.

Reverend Wang Zizhong, Chairman of the Beijing Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee, and others were at the airport to welcome him.

### 全印自主教会联合会总干部威廉士牧师到京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    全印自主教会联合会总干部
    威廉士牧师到京
    新华社30日讯　全印自主教会联合会总干部威廉士牧师在29日到北京。他是应中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席吴耀宗的邀请来我国访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的有北京市基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席王梓仲牧师等人。
