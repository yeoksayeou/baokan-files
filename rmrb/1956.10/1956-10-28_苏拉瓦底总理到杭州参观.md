Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Visits Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 27th. Pakistan's Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage flew from Shanghai to Hangzhou by special plane this afternoon.

Xu Jianguo, Liu Jiping, Song Richang, and Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayors of Shanghai, along with heads of various government departments and representatives from all walks of life, were at the airport to see them off.

Before leaving Shanghai, the distinguished guests also took advantage of the morning to visit several places. In front of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building, Prime Minister Suhrawardy was surprised to learn that it only took nine months to build this magnificent building. Then, they came to People's Avenue in the city center and took photos. Afterward, they visited the Jiangnan Shipyard and toured the various workshops and docks. When the distinguished guests visited the East China Branch of the Central Conservatory of Music, they saw a university student orchestra rehearsing in the auditorium. At the Prime Minister's request, the students played Beethoven's Fifth Symphony for them.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, October 27th. Pakistan's Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage, accompanied by Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, as well as Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai by special plane at 2:45 this afternoon.

Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Yang Siyi, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, and Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, were at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests. Also welcoming them at the airport were heads of various people's organizations and government agencies.

After arriving in the city, Prime Minister Suhrawardy, accompanied by Governor Sha Wenhan, toured the West Lake. Prime Minister Suhrawardy was very interested in the captivating scenery of the West Lake. At Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, he used a small movie camera to capture the beautiful lake and mountain scenery. In the Fish Viewing at Flower Pond park, groups of workers and students spending the weekend applauded and cheered to welcome the distinguished guests who came to see the fish. A group of Young Pioneers rushed forward to greet the distinguished guests, and Prime Minister Suhrawardy replied in Chinese, "Hello, children!" The Prime Minister also visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory today.

In the evening, Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage attended a banquet held by Governor Sha Wenhan. At the end of the banquet, Governor Sha Wenhan presented Prime Minister Suhrawardy with famous Zhejiang specialties such as silk-woven landscape paintings of West Lake, Qingtian stone carvings, boxwood carvings, and Longjing tea. Prime Minister Suhrawardy presented the Mayor of Hangzhou with gifts such as silver cigarette cases and tea sets.

### 苏拉瓦底总理到杭州参观

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理到杭州参观
    新华社上海27日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员今天下午乘专机由上海飞往杭州。
    上海市副市长许建国、刘季平、宋日昌、刘述周和政府各部门负责人、各界代表人士到机场欢送。
    贵宾们离开上海以前，还利用上午的时间参观了许多地方。在中苏友好大厦前，苏拉瓦底总理听说建筑这座宏伟的大厦只用了九个月的时间，感到惊奇。接着，他们来到了市中心的人民大道，并且摄影留念，以后又到江南造船厂参观了各个车间和船坞。在贵宾们参观中央音乐学院华东分院时，他们在礼堂里看到大学生们的管弦乐队正在练习。学生们应总理的要求为他们演奏了贝多芬的第五交响乐。
    新华社杭州27日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底和随行人员在中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人以及外交部礼宾司司长王倬如等陪同下，今天下午两点四十五分乘专机从上海到达杭州。
    浙江省省长沙文汉、浙江省副省长杨思一、杭州市市长吴宪等到机场欢迎贵宾。在机场欢迎的还有各人民团体、各机关负责人。
    苏拉瓦底总理到市区后由沙文汉省长陪同游览了西湖，苏拉瓦底总理对引人入胜的西湖风景很感兴趣，他在三潭印月，用小型电影摄影机把这秀丽的湖光山色摄入镜头。在花港观鱼公园，一群群正在渡周末的职工、学生向前来观鱼的贵宾们鼓掌欢呼。一批少先队员赶上前来向贵宾问好，苏拉瓦底总理用中国话回答说：“孩子们好！”总理今天还参观了都锦生丝织厂。
    晚上，苏拉瓦底总理和随行人员出席了沙文汉省长举行的宴会。宴会结束时，沙文汉省长向苏拉瓦底总理赠送了西湖丝织的风景画、青田石刻、黄杨木刻、龙井茶叶等浙江著名特产。苏拉瓦底总理向杭州市长赠送了银质的烟盒、茶具等礼物。
