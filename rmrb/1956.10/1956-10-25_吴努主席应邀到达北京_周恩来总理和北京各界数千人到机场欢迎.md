Title: Chairman U Nu Arrives in Beijing at Invitation; Premier Zhou Enlai and Thousands of People from All Walks of Life in Beijing Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, his wife, and the Burmese guests accompanying Chairman U Nu on his visit to China were warmly welcomed by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and thousands of people from all walks of life in Beijing when they arrived in Beijing today.

Chairman U Nu delivered a speech at the airport:

Your Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai, Ladies and Gentlemen:

First of all, we are delighted by such a warm welcome.

I would like to express my greetings to you on behalf of the people of Burma and in my own name.

I am grateful to Premier Zhou Enlai for his sincere invitation. I am pleased to accept this invitation and come to Beijing once again to meet with Premier Zhou Enlai and other dear friends. We will naturally take this opportunity to discuss issues of close concern to everyone, such as how we can best contribute to maintaining peace. In addition, we also hope to exchange views on issues related to our two countries. If there are differences of opinion between the two sides, we expect that it will not be difficult to reach understanding and consensus, because we are both acting in accordance with the Five Principles that we agree with and abide by.

The traditional friendship between China and Burma remains solid. Through frequent friendly and familial exchanges of views, and through the realization of the common aspirations of the two countries, the friendship between our two countries will surely develop and consolidate day by day.

May China and Burma prosper day by day!

May the people of China and Burma be happy!

The special plane carrying Chairman U Nu landed at the Beijing West Suburb Airport at 4:00 PM. Here, the national flags of China and Burma and countless colored flags fluttered in the wind, and a huge archway was inscribed in both Chinese and Burmese: "Welcome Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma."

When Chairman U Nu stepped off the plane amidst enthusiastic cheers and applause, Premier Zhou Enlai was the first to step forward to welcome Chairman U Nu, and they shook hands warmly. Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai to welcome Chairman U Nu were Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Gao Kelin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Xu Guangping.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and embassy staff also welcomed him at the airport.

After Chairman U Nu, his wife, and the Burmese guests accompanying Chairman U Nu on his visit to China accepted flowers from Beijing's children, they met and shook hands with the heads of various departments of Chinese state organs, various democratic parties, people's organizations, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, people from all walks of life, and foreign envoys, diplomatic officials, and heads of economic delegations stationed in China who welcomed him at the airport.

Finally, Chairman U Nu, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, walked side by side towards the welcoming crowd lined up at the airport, meeting with the welcoming masses. At this time, the airport was jubilant, and people kept shouting: "Welcome Chairman U Nu!" "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!" "Long live the unity of Asian and African countries!" "Long live peace!" Many female students ran out of the welcoming line to present bouquets of flowers to Chairman U Nu. Chairman U Nu walked from one end of the welcoming line to the other, smiling and constantly waving to the welcoming masses.

At 4:25 PM, Chairman U Nu, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, went to the guesthouse to rest.

Today, Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, accompanied Chairman U Nu to Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 24th. Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, his wife, son, and the Burmese Federal High Court Judge U Myint Thein and his wife, and other accompanying personnel who came to visit our country, left Kunming by special plane at 8:00 this morning to fly to Beijing.

Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, accompanied U Nu on the same plane to Beijing.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, and the heads of various agencies, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Yunnan Province and Kunming City, as well as the leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming.

U Maung Maung Toe, the Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, also went to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, October 24th. Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, his wife, and accompanying senior officials, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, arrived in Xi'an by special plane at 10:25 AM on October 24.

Zhao Boping, Acting Governor of Shaanxi Province, Fang Zhongru, Mayor of Xi'an, and the heads of various agencies, democratic parties, people's organizations, and the leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Xi'an went to the airport to welcome them.

Chairman U Nu, his wife, and accompanying senior officials rested briefly in the airport's lounge before having lunch, accompanied by Zhao Boping, Acting Governor of Shaanxi Province, Fang Zhongru, Mayor of Xi'an, and others.

At 1:15 PM, Chairman U Nu and others boarded the special plane and left Xi'an. (Attached picture)

### 吴努主席应邀到达北京  周恩来总理和北京各界数千人到机场欢迎

1956-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努主席应邀到达北京
    周恩来总理和北京各界数千人到机场欢迎
    新华社24日讯　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和他的夫人以及随吴努主席来我国访问的缅甸客人们今天到达北京时，受到中华人民共和国国务院总理、中共中央副主席周恩来和北京各界人民数千人的热烈欢迎。
    吴努主席在机场上发表谈话说：
    周恩来总理阁下，女士们，先生们：
    首先对大家这样热烈的欢迎我们感到欢喜。
    我谨代表缅甸人民并以我个人的名义向你们表示问候。
    我感谢周恩来总理对我的诚恳的邀请。我高兴地接受了这一邀请，又一次来到了北京，同周恩来总理和其他亲爱的朋友会面。我们自然将利用这一机会商谈对大家有密切关系的问题，例如我们怎样能够尽自己的一份力量最好地对维护和平作出贡献的问题。此外，我们也希望就有关两国的问题交换意见。如果双方在意见上有分歧，我们料想不难取得谅解和一致意见，因为我们都是本着我们赞同和遵守的五项原则行事的。
    中缅两国的传统友谊仍然是巩固的，通过经常的朋友般和亲戚般的交换意见，通过实现两国共同的主张，我们两国的友谊必将日益发展和巩固。
    祝中缅两国日益繁荣！
    祝中缅两国人民幸福！
    吴努主席乘坐的专机下午四时在北京西郊机场降落。这里，中缅两国国旗和无数面彩旗迎风飘扬，一座巨大的牌坊上用中缅两国文字写着：“欢迎缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟吴努主席”。
    当吴努主席在一片热烈的欢呼声和鼓掌声中走下飞机的时候，周恩来总理第一个上前欢迎吴努主席，他们亲切地紧紧握手。同周恩来总理一起欢迎吴努主席的，有副总理陈云、陈毅，副委员长郭沫若、彭真，全国人民政协副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席张治中，最高人民法院副院长高克林，外交部副部长章汉夫，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平。
    缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    吴努主席和他的夫人以及随吴努主席来我国访问的缅甸客人们接受了北京市少年儿童的献花后，同在机场上欢迎的中国国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们和社会各界人士，以及各国驻中国的使节、外交官员和经济代表团团长会见握手。
    最后，吴努主席同周恩来总理并肩向排列在机场上的欢迎人群走去，同欢迎群众会见。这时，机场上一片欢腾，人们不停地高呼：“欢迎吴努主席！”“中缅友好万岁！”“亚非国家团结万岁！”“和平万岁！”许多女学生从欢迎队伍中跑出来把一束束鲜花送给吴努主席。吴努主席从欢迎队伍的这一头走到另一头，他微笑着，不断向欢迎群众招手答礼。
    四时二十五分，吴努主席由周恩来总理陪同赴宾馆休息。
    今天，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同吴努主席到北京。
    新华社昆明24日电　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和他的夫人、公子及随同来我国访问的缅甸联邦高级法院法官吴敏登和夫人以及其他的随行人员，已在今晨八时乘专机离开昆明飞往北京。
    我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同吴努同机飞往北京。
    到机场欢送的有云南省副省长刘明辉、昆明市市长潘朔端及云南省、昆明市各机关、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长等。
    到机场欢送的还有缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎。
    新华社西安24日电　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和夫人及随行高级官员，在我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同下，于10月24日上午十时二十五分乘专机到达西安。
    前往机场欢迎的有陕西省代省长赵伯平、西安市市长方仲如以及陕西省、西安市各机关、民主党派、人民团体负责人和中国人民解放军驻西安部队的首长等。
    吴努主席和夫人及随行高级官员在机场的休息室稍事休息后，在陕西省代省长赵伯平、西安市市长方仲如等陪同下进了午餐。
    下午一时十五分，吴努主席等乘上专机离开西安。（附图片）
