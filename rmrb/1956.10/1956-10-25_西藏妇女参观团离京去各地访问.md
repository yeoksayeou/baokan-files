Title: Tibetan Women's Visiting Delegation Leaves Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd. The Tibetan Women's Visiting Delegation left Beijing this afternoon to visit places such as Hohhot.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Central Institute for Nationalities, Beihai Kindergarten, Women's Embroidery Production Cooperative, Children's Hospital, Guanting Reservoir, and toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Summer Palace and the Forbidden City.

### 西藏妇女参观团离京去各地访问

1956-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏妇女参观团离京去各地访问
    新华社23日讯　西藏妇女参观团今天下午离开北京去呼和浩特等地参观访问。
    参观团在京期间，曾经参观了中央民族学院、北海幼儿园、妇女挑花生产合作社、儿童医院、官厅水库等，游览了颐和园、故宫等名胜古迹。
