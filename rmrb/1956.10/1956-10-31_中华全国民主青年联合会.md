Title: All-China Youth Federation

Author:

Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The All-China Youth Federation protests the illegal kidnapping of Algerian national leaders by the French authorities. The full text of the protest is as follows:

    The recent act of the French authorities in illegally kidnapping Algerian national leaders has aroused great indignation among all Chinese youth. This fact once again shows that the French colonialists, disregarding the universal desire for peace of the people of the world and the Algerian people's demand for national independence, are attempting to continue their policy of armed suppression, undermining efforts to peacefully resolve the Algerian issue. However, in this era of unprecedented strength in national independence movements and peace movements, the colonialists' attempts are bound to fail completely.
    We, the Chinese youth, together with the Chinese people, warmly support the Algerian people's struggle for national independence and efforts to peacefully resolve the Algerian issue. We solemnly protest the French authorities' betrayal of morality in illegally kidnapping Algerian national leaders and demand their immediate release.

### 中华全国民主青年联合会

1956-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主青年联合会
    新华社30日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会抗议法国当局非法绑架阿尔及利亚民族领袖。抗议全文如下：
    法国当局最近用非法手段绑架阿尔及利亚民族领袖的行径，引起了我们全体中国青年的极大愤怒。这一事实又一次表明法国殖民主义者不顾世界人民的普遍和平愿望和阿尔及利亚人民的民族独立要求，而企图继续采取武力镇压的政策，破坏和平解决阿尔及利亚问题的努力。但是在民族独立运动和和平运动空前强大的我们这个时代，殖民主义者的这种企图是一定要彻底失败的。
    我们中国青年和中国人民一道，热烈支持阿尔及利亚人民的民族独立斗争和和平解决阿尔及利亚问题的努力。我们严正地抗议法国当局非法绑架阿尔及利亚民族领袖的背弃道义的行为，并要求立即予以释放。
