Title: South's Secretary of State for National Defense Hosts Chinese Military Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 12th – According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the Secretary of State for National Defense of Yugoslavia, General Gosnjak, received the Chinese military delegation on the morning of the 11th, which had been invited to visit Yugoslavia.

At 1:00 PM, Gosnjak hosted a luncheon in honor of the Chinese military delegation headed by General Deng Hua.

General Vukicevic, Chief of the General Staff of the Yugoslav People's Army, held a reception on the evening of the 11th to welcome the Chinese military delegation. Among those attending the reception were Vukmanovic, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, and General Gosnjak, Secretary of State for National Defense. Wu Xiuquan, Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, also attended the reception.

### 南国防国务秘书长招待我军事代表团

1956-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    南国防国务秘书长招待我军事代表团
    新华社12日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫国防国务秘书长戈什尼亚克大将11日上午接见了应邀到南斯拉夫访问的中国军事代表团。
    下午一点钟，戈什尼亚克设午宴招待了以邓华上将为首的中国军事代表团。
    南斯拉夫人民军总参谋长伏奇科维奇上将在11日晚上举行招待会，欢迎中国军事代表团。出席招待会的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇，国防国务秘书长戈什尼亚克大将等。中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权也出席了招待会。
