Title: Pakistan Football Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Pakistan national football team has ended its visit to China and will leave Beijing by train on the 9th via Hong Kong to return home.
    Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 2nd to bid them farewell.
    Since arriving in China on September 17th, the Pakistan national football team has played four friendly matches in Guangzhou, Shanghai, and Beijing with four Chinese football teams. They drew in two matches and lost in two. They also participated in the National Day celebrations in Beijing.

### 巴基斯坦足球队回国

1956-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦足球队回国
    新华社4日讯　巴基斯坦国家足球队结束了在中国的访问，9日坐火车离开北京经香港回国。
    体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩曾经在2日晚设宴欢送他们。
    巴基斯坦国家足球队从9月17日到中国以后，先后在广州、上海、北京同中国的四个足球队举行了四场友谊比赛，他们有两场踢成平局，有两场失败。他们还在北京参加了国庆节观礼。
