Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Bids Farewell to the Balinese Art Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Indonesian Balinese Art Friendship Delegation, which is about to leave Beijing for visits and performances in various locations.

	Peng Zhen spoke first at the banquet. He expressed his gratitude to the art troupe for bringing beautiful art to the people of Beijing.

	Peng Zhen said: We welcome the sisters and brothers of the art troupe as if we were welcoming our own family members, because we share a common situation and common ideals.

	The head of the art troupe, Saléh Umar, then delivered a speech. He said that the Chinese people are building a Great Wall of peace and friendship that is many times longer than the Great Wall of the past, and the art troupe's performance in China this time is to put some small bricks into this Great Wall of peace and friendship. Umar also recited a newly composed poem impromptu and presented the poem to Mayor Peng Zhen.

	During the banquet, Chinese and Indonesian artists performed wonderful entertainment programs.

	Those present tonight to accompany the guests included: Vice Ministers of Culture Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Bao Erhan, Vice President Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Yang Hansheng, as well as heads of relevant units in Beijing and well-known figures from the literary and art circles.

	Anwar Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 彭真市长欢送峇厘艺术团

1956-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真市长欢送峇厘艺术团
    新华社7日讯　北京市市长彭真今天晚间设宴欢送即将离开北京前往各地访问演出的印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团。
    彭真在宴会上首先讲话。他对艺术团给北京人民带来优美的艺术表示感谢。
    彭真说：我们欢迎艺术团的各位姊妹、兄弟，就像欢迎自己家里人一样，因为我们有着共同的处境和共同的理想。
    艺术团团长萨勒·乌玛尔接着致词。他说，中国人民正在建筑比过去的万里长城不知长多少倍的和平、友谊的长城，艺术团这次来中国演出，就是为了能在这座和平、友谊的长城中放进一些小小的砖石。乌玛尔还即席朗诵了一首他新作的诗，并且把这首诗赠给彭真市长。
    在宴会进行中，中国和印度尼西亚的艺术家们表演了精采的文娱节目。
    今天晚上出席作陪的有：文化部副部长郑振铎、夏衍，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉、副会长胡愈之，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，以及北京市各有关单位的负责人和文艺界著名人士。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼和他的夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
