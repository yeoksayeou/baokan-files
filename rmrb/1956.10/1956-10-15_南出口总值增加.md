Title: Increase in Total Value of Southern Exports
Author:
Date: 1956-10-15
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency on the 13th: The total value of Yugoslavia's exports in the past nine months reached over 67.2 billion dinars (Yugoslav currency), an increase of 30% compared to the same period last year.
    September was one of the months with the highest total export value in the past four years, reaching 8.332 billion dinars. The countries that purchased the most Yugoslav goods in this month were West Germany and the Soviet Union, followed by Italy, the United States, the United Kingdom, Greece, and Poland.
    Among the exported goods, industrial manufactured products accounted for the main part, followed by raw materials, fruits, vegetables, and dairy products.

### 南出口总值增加

1956-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    南出口总值增加
    新华社14日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社13日报道：南斯拉夫在过去九个月中的出口总值共达六百七十二亿多第纳尔（南币），比去年同期增加了30％。
    9月份是四年来出口总值最高的月份之一，达到了八十三亿三千二百万第纳尔。这一月购买南斯拉夫货物最多的国家是西德和苏联，次多的是意大利、美国、英国、希腊和波兰等国。
    在出口的货物中，工业制成品占主要部分，其次是原料、水果、蔬菜和奶制品等。
