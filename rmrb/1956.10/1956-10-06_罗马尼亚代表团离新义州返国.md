Title: Romanian Delegation Leaves Sinuiju for Home

Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 5th: The delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, which was invited to visit Korea, left Sinuiju by plane this morning to return home after a three-day visit to Korea.

Premier Kim Il-sung, who accompanied the delegation on a tour of various parts of Korea, went to the airport to see them off.

Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej delivered a speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude for the warm reception given to the delegation by the Korean people. He wished the Korean people brilliant victories in their struggle for the peaceful reunification of their country and socialist construction. He emphasized that the delegation's visit to Korea would strengthen the friendship and solidarity between the peoples of the two countries.

### 罗马尼亚代表团离新义州返国

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚代表团离新义州返国
    据新华社平壤5日电　应邀到朝鲜访问的以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团在朝鲜进行了三天访问以后，已经在今天上午从新义州乘飞机回国。
    陪同代表团到朝鲜各地参观访问的金日成首相曾经到机场欢送。
    乔治乌—德治在机场上致词，他对于朝鲜人民给予代表团的热情接待表示感谢。他祝朝鲜人民在和平统一祖国和社会主义建设的斗争中取得光辉的胜利。他强调说，代表团的访问朝鲜将加强两国人民之间的友谊和团结。
