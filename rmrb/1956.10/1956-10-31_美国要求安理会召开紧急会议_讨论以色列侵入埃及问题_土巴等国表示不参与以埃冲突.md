Title: U.S. Demands Security Council Emergency Meeting to Discuss Israeli Invasion of Egypt; Turkey, Pakistan and Other Countries Express Non-Participation in Egypt-Israel Conflict
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th, dispatch from New York: U.S. Chief Representative to the United Nations, Lodge, requested the Security Council on the 29th to convene an emergency meeting to discuss the Israeli armed invasion of Egypt. The United Nations headquarters announced late last night that the Security Council is scheduled to meet today at 11:00 AM local time.

Lodge's request was made after U.S. President Eisenhower convened a special meeting at the White House regarding the issue of Israel's military actions, attended by senior U.S. officials responsible for military, diplomatic, and intelligence matters.

In Washington and at the United Nations headquarters in New York, diplomatic officials from the United States, Britain, and France, as well as diplomatic officials from other relevant countries, are conducting frequent talks on the situation in the Middle East. After meeting with the British and French ambassadors, Dulles requested that the two countries support U.S. actions in the Security Council.

The diplomatic envoys to Washington from the four member states of the Baghdad Pact—Pakistan, Iraq, Iran, and Turkey—met with Dulles and expressed their non-participation in the Egypt-Israel conflict.

### 美国要求安理会召开紧急会议  讨论以色列侵入埃及问题  土巴等国表示不参与以埃冲突

1956-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    美国要求安理会召开紧急会议
    讨论以色列侵入埃及问题
    土巴等国表示不参与以埃冲突
    新华社30日讯　纽约消息：美国驻联合国首席代表洛奇29日要求安全理事会召开紧急会议讨论以色列对埃及的武装入侵。联合国总部在昨晚深夜宣布，安理会定在今天上午当地时间十一时举行会议。
    洛奇的要求是在美国总统艾森豪威尔在白宫召开了一次关于以色列的军事行动问题的特别会议以后提出的，出席这次会议的有美国军事、外交和情报方面的高级负责官员。
    在华盛顿和纽约联合国总部，美、英、法三国的外交官员以及各有关国家的外交官员正在就中东局势进行频繁的会谈。杜勒斯在同英、法两国使节会谈后要求两国支持美国在安理会的行动。
    巴格达条约的四个成员国——巴基斯坦、伊拉克、伊朗、土耳其——驻华盛顿的外交使节会见杜勒斯，表示不参与以埃冲突。
