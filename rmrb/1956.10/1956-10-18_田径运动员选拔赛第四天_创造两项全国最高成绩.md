Author:
Date: 1956-10-18

Athletics Athlete Selection Competition Day 4
Two National Best Records Created

(Xinhua News Agency, October 17th) The athletics athlete selection competition for the Chinese Olympic team entered its fourth day today, and two more athletes created two national best records.

Bi Hongfu from Beijing created a national best record of 48.74 meters in the men's hammer throw preliminary and final rounds today. Before this, the national best record for this event was 45.22 meters.

In the women's grenade throw (non-Olympic event) preliminary and final rounds, Sichuan athlete Tang Rongqing created a national best record of 48.13 meters, breaking the national record of 47.75 meters set by Liu Fu in 1954.

Today also held the finals of the men's 800 meters, men's long jump, men's 200 meters, women's 200 meters, women's 80 meters hurdles, as well as the semi-finals of the men's 400 meters hurdles, and the preliminary rounds of the men's and women's 4x400 meters relay.

Li Zhonglin from Beijing won first place in the men's 800 meters final with a time of 1 minute 54.7 seconds. This result broke his own national record for this event in 1955 (1 minute 56 seconds), but failed to catch up with the national best record he created in July this year (1 minute 51.6 seconds).

The first place in the men's 200 meters final was Liu Jingren from Beijing, with a time of 22.6 seconds. Liu Jingren set a national best record of 21.7 seconds in this event in July this year.

Liu Xingyu from Sichuan and Zeng Shaode from Sichuan won first and second place respectively in the women's 200 meters final. Their results were both 25.8 seconds, which is equal to the national record for this event in 1955.

Wen Yunzhen from Guangxi broke the 1955 national record for the women's 80 meters hurdles (11.9 seconds) with a time of 11.8 seconds today, winning first place in this event. Liu Yuying from Liaoning took second place in this event with a time of 11.9 seconds. In July this year, Wen Yunzhen's national best record in this event reached 11.5 seconds. Zhang Qishan from the Chinese People's Liberation Army broke the 1955 national record for the men's long jump (6.93 meters) with a jump of 7.10 meters in the men's long jump final, but this result also did not exceed the national best record he created in this event in July this year (7.23 meters).

The two-day men's decathlon competition concluded today. Hao Jianren from Shandong won first place with a total score of 5,135 points. (Attached picture)

### 田径运动员选拔赛第四天  创造两项全国最高成绩

1956-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    田径运动员选拔赛第四天
    创造两项全国最高成绩
    据新华社17日讯　中国奥林匹克运动队田径运动员选拔赛今天进入第四天，又有两名运动员创造了两项全国最高成绩。
    北京的毕鸿福今天在男子链球预决赛中创造了四十八公尺七十四公分的全国最高成绩，在这以前这项比赛的全国最好成绩是四十五公尺二十二公分。
    在女子掷手榴弹（非奥林匹克运动项目）预决赛中，四川省的运动员唐荣清创造了四十八公尺十三公分的全国最高成绩，打破了刘馥在1954年创造的四十七公尺七十五公分全国纪录。
    今天还举行了男子八百公尺、男子跳远、男子二百公尺、女子二百公尺、女子八十公尺低栏决赛，以及男子四百公尺中栏复赛、男子和女子四百公尺接力预赛等。
    北京的李中林以一分五十四秒七的成绩取得了男子八百公尺决赛的第一名，这个成绩打破了他自己保持的这项比赛1955年的全国纪录（一分五十六秒），但是没能赶上他自己今年7月间创造的这项比赛的全国最高成绩（一分五十一秒六）。
    男子二百公尺决赛的第一名是北京的刘敬仁，他的成绩是二十二秒六。刘敬仁今年7月间曾经创造了这项比赛的二十一秒七的全国最高成绩。
    四川的刘兴玉和四川的曾绍德分别取得了女子二百公尺决赛的第一名和第二名。她们的成绩都是二十五秒八，这个成绩同这项比赛1955年的全国纪录相等。
    广西的文蕴珍今天以十一秒八的成绩打破了女子八十公尺低栏1955年的全国纪录（十一秒九），取得了这项比赛的第一名。辽宁的刘玉英以十一秒九的成绩，取得了这项比赛的第二名。今年7月间文蕴珍在这项比赛中创造的全国最高成绩曾经达到十一秒五。中国人民解放军的张启山在男子跳远决赛中，以七公尺十公分的成绩打破了男子跳远1955年全国纪录（六公尺九十三公分），但是这个成绩也没有超过他今年7月间创造的这项比赛的全国最高成绩（七公尺二十三公分）。
    进行了两天的男子十项运动全能比赛已经在今天结束。获得第一名的是山东的郝健仁，他的总成绩是五千一百三十五分。（附图片）
