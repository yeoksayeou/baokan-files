Title: Soviet Communist Party Leaders Meet with Norwegian Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th: According to TASS, responsible members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union met on September 29th with a delegation from the Norwegian Communist Party that was passing through Moscow on its way home from China.

Those participating in the meeting included Suslov and Pospelov, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, and Løvlien, Chairman of the Norwegian Communist Party, and Lippe, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Norwegian Communist Party, and others.

The meeting between the representatives of the two fraternal parties took place in an atmosphere of friendship and mutual understanding.

### 苏共负责人和挪共代表团会谈

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共负责人和挪共代表团会谈　　
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会负责人9月29日同从中国回国路过莫斯科的挪威共产党代表团举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的有苏共中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫和挪威共产党主席洛夫林、挪威共产党中央委员会书记利佩等人。　　
    两个兄弟党代表的会谈是在友好和相互谅解的气氛中进行。
