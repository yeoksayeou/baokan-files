Title: Dinosaur Fossil Discovered in Xinjiang
Author: Tian Zaiyi
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 7

    Dinosaur Fossil Discovered in Xinjiang
    (Our dispatch) A fossil of a dinosaur that lived approximately 150 million years ago during the Mesozoic Era has been discovered on the southern foot of the Karamaili Mountain, north of Qitai, Xinjiang. Dinosaurs were giant reptiles of the geological Mesozoic Era, resembling lizards in the desert. Preliminary measurements indicate that this dinosaur fossil is about ten meters long, with forty-four caudal and vertebral bones visible on the surface. The discovery of this fossil is of great value for paleontological research. At the same time, it also provides some clues for geological departments to divide stratigraphic ages and for petroleum exploration personnel to find oil. (Tian Zaiyi)

### 新疆发现恐龙化石
田在艺
1956-10-25
第7版()
专栏：

    新疆发现恐龙化石
    本报讯　一个生存在距今约一亿五千万年前的恐龙的化石，在新疆奇台以北的克拉美丽山南麓被发现了。恐龙是地质时期中生代的巨型生虫，形状像沙漠中的壁虎。这个恐龙化石据初步测量长十公尺左右，在地面能够看见尾骨和脊椎骨四十四节。这个化石的发现，对于古生物学的研究具有很大价值。同时，对于地质部门划分地层年代、石油勘探人员寻找石油也提供了一些线索。　（田在艺）
