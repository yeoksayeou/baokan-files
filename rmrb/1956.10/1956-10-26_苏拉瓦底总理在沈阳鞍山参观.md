Title: Prime Minister Suhrawardy Visits Shenyang and Anshan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 25th: Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage arrived in Shenyang by special plane from Changchun this morning for a visit. Geng Biao, China's Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, along with Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied them.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by thousands of Shenyang citizens at Dongta Airport. Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Zhao Chengjin, Deputy Commander of the Liaoning Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, leading members of various Liaoning and Shenyang municipal organizations, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as the Soviet and North Korean Consuls General and Acting Consuls General in Shenyang, greeted the distinguished guests at the airport.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy visited the First Machine Tool Plant in Shenyang. The Prime Minister carefully observed the process of the sand mold falling off the castings on the sand removing machine in the casting workshop, after the castings were transferred from the cooling room. In the assembly workshop, he watched the skilled turner Liu Rude use the Kolesov tool method to process spline shafts. During the visit, Ye Xuanping, the first deputy director of the factory, answered in detail the questions raised by Prime Minister Suhrawardy regarding production.

At 12:50 PM, Qiu Youwen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, accompanied the distinguished guests on a special train to Anshan for a visit, returning to Shenyang that evening.

In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a banquet hosted by Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province.

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, October 25th: Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy and his entourage arrived in Anshan by special train this afternoon.

After getting off the train, Prime Minister Suhrawardy went directly to the Anshan Iron and Steel Works (Ansteel) factory area and visited the Large Rolling Mill, the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and the Iron Smelting Plant. Workers in all three factories beat drums and gongs to welcome the distinguished guests. Prime Minister Suhrawardy carefully observed every production process of the automated machines in these factories. At the Large Rolling Mill, he even took several photos of the magnetic crane transporting heavy steel rails. When leaving the finishing section of the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, Prime Minister Suhrawardy warmly shook hands with the workers. At the No. 8 blast furnace, he watched the smelting process through the blue lenses of the iron smelting workers.

After visiting Ansteel, Yuan Zhen, the manager of Ansteel, on behalf of all the staff and workers, presented Prime Minister Suhrawardy with a model of Tiananmen Square made of Ansteel's steel. After accepting the gift, Prime Minister Suhrawardy said, "I am very happy to come here to visit. Your great achievements are beyond our imagination." Then, the Prime Minister presented silver cigarette cases and other gifts to Li Weimin, the Mayor of Anshan, and Yuan Zhen, the manager of Ansteel, saying, "I hope you will remember that I came here to visit."

Accompanying Prime Minister Suhrawardy on his visit to Anshan were Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Geng Biao, China's Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, and Qiu Youwen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy left Anshan at 5:30 PM and returned to Shenyang.

### 苏拉瓦底总理在沈阳鞍山参观

1956-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏拉瓦底总理在沈阳鞍山参观
    新华社沈阳25日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员今天上午从长春乘专机到达沈阳参观。我国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人以及外交部礼宾司司长王倬如陪同前来。
    贵宾们在东塔机场受到几千名沈阳市民的热烈欢迎。辽宁省省长杜者蘅、沈阳市副市长张力克、中国人民解放军辽宁军区副司令员赵承金少将和辽宁省、沈阳市各机关首长、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人以及苏联、朝鲜驻沈阳总领事、代总领事等在机场上迎接贵宾。
    苏拉瓦底总理在沈阳参观了第一机床厂。总理在铸造车间的落砂机前仔细地观看了刚从冷却室传送出来的铸件在这个机器上脱落砂模的情形。在装配车间里，他观看了优秀车工刘汝德用科列索夫车刀法加工花键轴的操作情况。在参观时，这个工厂的第一副厂长叶选平对苏拉瓦底总理提出的有关生产的一些问题详细地作了回答。
    十二时五十分，辽宁省副省长仇友文陪同贵宾乘专车去鞍山参观，当日晚回到沈阳。
    晚上，贵宾们出席了辽宁省省长杜者蘅举行的宴会。
    新华社鞍山25日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底和他的随行人员今天午后乘专车到达鞍山。
    苏拉瓦底总理下车后，直接去鞍钢工厂区参观了大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂、炼铁厂。三个工厂的工人都敲锣打鼓欢迎贵宾。苏拉瓦底总理很细致地观看了这些工厂的自动化机器的每一个生产过程。在大型轧钢厂，他还拍摄了几张磁力吊车搬运重型钢轨的照片。离开无缝钢管厂精整工段时，苏拉瓦底总理和工人们热情地握手。在八号高炉上他拿着炼铁工人的蓝色镜片观看了冶炼的情况。
    参观鞍钢以后，鞍钢经理袁振代表全体职工把一个用鞍钢的钢材制成的天安门模型送给苏拉瓦底总理。苏拉瓦底总理在受礼后说：“我很高兴来到你们这里参观，你们这些重大的成就，超出我们的想像。”接着，总理把用银制的烟盒等赠送给鞍山市市长李维民和鞍钢经理袁振。并且说：“我希望你们能够记得我曾来到这里参观。”
    陪同苏拉瓦底总理来鞍山参观的有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如，我国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人，辽宁省副省长仇友文。
    苏拉瓦底总理在下午五时三十分离开鞍山返沈阳。
