Title: Mongolia Awards Order to Johnson
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, October 5th—According to TASS, the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic awarded the Order of the "Polar Star" of Mongolia to the Archbishop of Canterbury of England, and member of the World Peace Council, Johnson, on October 4th, in recognition of his outstanding achievements in the cause of defending peace and consolidating the friendship of the peoples of all countries.

    When accepting the medal, Johnson said that the high appraisal of my achievements expressed by the Mongolian government in the form of awarding medals is a pleasant event in my life. I will tell the people in the West about your country's sincere welcome to me, the achievements of your country and your people in defending peace, developing cultural and scientific undertakings, and in the cause of prospering your motherland.

### 蒙古授给约翰逊勋章

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古授给约翰逊勋章
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团10月4日授给英国坎特伯雷教长、世界和平理事会理事约翰逊一枚蒙古的“北极星”勋章，奖励他在保卫和平和巩固各国人民友谊的事业中的卓越功迹。
    约翰逊在接受勋章的时候说，蒙古政府以授勋形式对我的功迹所表示的崇高评价，是我有生以来的一件愉快的事。我要把贵国对我的真诚的欢迎、贵国和贵国人民在保卫和平、发展文化和科学事业中以及在繁荣自己祖国的事业中所取得的成就告诉西方的人们。
