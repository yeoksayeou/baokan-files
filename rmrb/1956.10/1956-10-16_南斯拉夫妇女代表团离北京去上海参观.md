Title: Yugoslav Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Shanghai Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The Yugoslav Women's Delegation, led by Lidia Sentjurc, and members Vera Popović, Irina Prijelić, Zamira Kolonomos, and Jovanka Janković, accompanied by Zou Dexin, Deputy Director of the International Propaganda Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, left Beijing by train this afternoon to visit Shanghai and other places. Before the delegation left Beijing, Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, held a banquet to bid farewell to the guests.

### 南斯拉夫妇女代表团离北京去上海参观

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫妇女代表团离北京去上海参观
    据新华社14日讯　南斯拉夫妇女代表团团长里第亚·申特尤尔茨和团员维拉波波维奇、伊莉娜·皮耶利奇、扎米拉·科洛诺莫斯、约凡卡·扬科维奇等一行五人，在中华全国民主妇女联合会国际宣传部副部长邹得心陪同下，今天下午乘火车离开北京去上海等地参观访问。代表团离京前中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超举行宴会为客人们饯行。
