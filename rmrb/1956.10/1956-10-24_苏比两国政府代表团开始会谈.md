Title: Soviet-Belgian Government Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 23rd: Belgian Prime Minister Van Acker, Foreign Minister Spaak, and their entourage arrived in Moscow by plane on October 22nd at the invitation of the Soviet government.

This morning, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Bulganin and Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Tarasov separately received Belgian Prime Minister Van Acker.

This morning, talks between the Soviet and Belgian government delegations began in the Kremlin. The head of the Soviet delegation was Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Bulganin; the head of the Belgian delegation was Belgian Prime Minister Van Acker.

### 苏比两国政府代表团开始会谈

1956-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    苏比两国政府代表团开始会谈
    据新华社莫斯科23日电　比利时首相范阿克、外交大臣斯巴克和他们的随行人员应苏联政府的邀请在10月22日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    今天上午，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫分别接见了比利时首相范阿克。
    今天上午，苏联和比利时两国政府代表团的会谈在克里姆林宫开始举行。苏联代表团团长是苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁；比利时代表团团长是比利时首相范阿克。
