Title: Khrushchev Meets with Kono

Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: TASS reports: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Ichiro Kono, member of the Japanese government delegation currently in Moscow for negotiations on the normalization of Soviet-Japanese relations and Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of Japan, on October 18 and held talks with him.

Present at the meeting was Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Fedrin.

### 赫鲁晓夫同河野会谈

1956-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同河野会谈
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫10月18日接见了目前在莫斯科进行苏日关系正常化谈判的日本政府代表团团员、日本农林相河野一郎，并且同他进行了会谈。
    会谈时在座的有苏联外交部副部长费德林。
