Title: Indonesia Building Large Hydropower Project in West Java
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 21st: Indonesia is building a giant dam and a hydropower project with a power generation capacity of 100,000 to 125,000 kilowatts in West Java Province. The Jatiluhur Hydropower Project, located six miles southwest of Purwakarta, began construction in 1953 and is one of the four giant projects being built according to the provisions of Indonesia's first five-year plan. The project is scheduled for completion in 1961.

### 印度尼西亚在西爪哇建筑一个大水电工程

1956-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚在西爪哇建筑一个大水电工程
    据新华社雅加达21日电　印度尼西亚正在西爪哇省建筑一座巨型水坝和一项发电能力为十万瓩到十二万五千瓩的水力发电工程。这个在普哇加打西南六英里的耶蒂卢胡尔水力发电工程，在1953年开始建筑，是根据印度尼西亚第一个五年计划的规定正在建造的四项巨大工程之一，这项工程预定在1961年完工。
