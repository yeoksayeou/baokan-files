Title: Peng Zemin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 19th - Peng Zemin, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, member of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China, and Honorary President of the Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine, passed away in Beijing at 9:50 PM on October 18, 1956, due to arteriosclerotic heart disease, heart failure, and pneumonia, despite medical treatment.
    
    Comrade Peng Zemin was a native of Sihui County, Guangdong Province, and was 81 years old.

### 农工民主党副主席彭泽民逝世

1956-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    农工民主党副主席彭泽民逝世
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员、中国农工民主党副主席、中国民主同盟中央常务委员、中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席、中国红十字会副会长、中医研究院名誉院长彭泽民，患动脉硬化性心脏病、心力衰竭并有肺炎等症，经医生诊治无效，于1956年10月18日下午九时五十分在北京逝世。
    彭泽民委员是广东省四会县人，享年八十一岁。
