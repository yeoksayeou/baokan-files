Title: Egypt Plans to Vigorously Expand the Suez Canal to Enable Passage of Supertankers and the Largest Passenger Ships
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 3rd: Egyptian authorities are drafting a long-term maintenance and development plan for the Suez Canal. Officials from the Egyptian Suez Canal Authority said that a new large-scale development project worth 20 million Egyptian pounds would be announced soon. The purpose of this project is to enable the canal to accommodate supertankers and huge ocean liners.

Agents of the British Transportation Company in Port Said also admitted on September 30th that, in the past two weeks, Egypt had not only demonstrated that it could manage the Suez Canal, but also managed it extremely effectively.

A pilot training program is being implemented at the fastest possible speed. According to a report in the Cairo "Al-Akhbar" on October 1st, there are now 205 pilots working on the Suez Canal. Among them, 113 pilots can guide ships through the canal completely independently.

According to official Egyptian materials, 106 ships from 18 countries, including British, French, and American ships, passed through the Suez Canal on September 27th, 28th, and 29th.

"Al-Akhbar" reported that from July 1st to September 30th this year, a total of 3,888 ships passed through the canal, while the number of ships passing through the canal during the same period in 1954 and 1955 was 3,278 and 3,642, respectively.

### 埃及计划大力扩建苏伊士运河  要使得超级油轮和最大邮船能够通航

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及计划大力扩建苏伊士运河
    要使得超级油轮和最大邮船能够通航
    新华社3日讯　开罗消息：埃及当局正在拟订对苏伊士运河的长期保养和发展计划。埃及苏伊士运河管理局的负责人员说，一个新的大规模的价值二千万埃镑的发展工程将在最近宣布。这个工程的目的是要使运河能够供超级油轮和巨大的海洋邮船通航。
    塞得港的英国运输公司的代理人员9月30日也承认，在过去两个星期中，埃及不仅表明它可以管理苏伊士运河，而且把它管理得极为有效。
    训练引水员的计划正在以最快的速度执行。据10月1日开罗“消息报”报道，现在苏伊士运河上已经有二百零五名引水员工作。其中有一百一十三名引水员可以完全独立地引导船只通过运河。
    据埃及官方的材料宣布，9月27日、28日、29日这三天通过苏伊士运河的有十八个国家的一百零六艘船，其中有英国船、法国船和美国船。
      “消息报”报道，从今年7月1日到9月30日，运河通行船只共有三千八百八十八艘，而1954年和1955年同一时期通过运河的船只数是三千二百七十八艘和三千六百四十二艘。
