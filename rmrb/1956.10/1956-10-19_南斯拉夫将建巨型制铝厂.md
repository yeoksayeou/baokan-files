Title: Yugoslavia to Build Giant Aluminum Plant
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Yugoslavia has decided to build its first aluminum complex with an annual output of 100,000 tons of aluminum, located between Nikšić and Titograd in the Republic of Montenegro. The first phase of the project is to build a plant with an annual output of 50,000 tons of aluminum.

According to the agreement signed in Moscow on August 2 this year, the construction of this factory will be carried out in cooperation with the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic. The factory will undergo trial production in mid-1961 and officially commence production on January 1, 1962.

### 南斯拉夫将建巨型制铝厂

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫将建巨型制铝厂
    据新华社18日讯　南斯拉夫决定在门的内哥罗共和国的尼克西兹和铁托格勒之间的地方建立第一个年产十万吨铝的制铝联合企业。第一阶段工程是建立一个年产五万吨铝的工厂。
    根据今年8月2日在莫斯科签订的协定，这个工厂的建设工作将在同苏联和德意志民主共和国合作下进行。这个工厂将在1961年年中进行试生产，在1962年1月1日正式生产。
