Title: Premier Zhou Receives International Buddhist Monks Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received this morning the head of the International Buddhist Monks Delegation, Indian Bhadanta Ananda Kaushalyayana, the deputy head, Sri Lankan Niruwi Jinaratana, and all members of the delegation.

### 周总理接见国际佛教僧侣代表团

1956-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见国际佛教僧侣代表团
    据新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天上午接见了国际佛教僧侣代表团团长印度巴丹达·阿难陀·柯萨尔雅雅那法师，副团长锡兰尼鲁威·吉那拉塔那法师和代表团全体团员。
