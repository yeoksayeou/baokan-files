Title: International Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The international youth delegation, composed of youth from four international youth organizations and sixteen countries, left Beijing by train tonight to visit various places.
    During their stay in Beijing, the delegation members visited factories, schools, temples, hospitals, and prisons, toured scenic spots and historical sites, and watched Chinese Peking Opera and dance performances.

### 国际青年代表团离京

1956-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    国际青年代表团离京
    据新华社29日讯　由四个国际青年组织和十六个国家的青年组成的国际青年代表团，今天晚上乘火车离开北京去各地参观。
    在北京期间，代表团人员曾经分别参观了工厂、学校、寺院、医院、监狱，游览了名胜古迹，并且观看了中国的京剧歌舞。
