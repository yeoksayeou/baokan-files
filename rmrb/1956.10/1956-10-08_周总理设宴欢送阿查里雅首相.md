Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Farewell Banquet for Prime Minister Acharya
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held a banquet at Wucheng Hall, Zhongnanhai, on the evening of the 7th, to bid farewell to the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, his wife, and accompanying senior officials.

Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his reluctance to part with Prime Minister Acharya and other distinguished Nepalese guests. He said that Prime Minister Acharya and other distinguished Nepalese guests had been in China for ten days. Through Prime Minister Acharya's visit, China and Nepal have not only restored their traditional friendship, but also further developed this friendly relationship. Premier Zhou proposed a toast to the friendship between China and Nepal, to the happiness of the people of China and Nepal, and to the friendship between China, Nepal, and India.

Prime Minister Acharya said in his speech at the banquet that he would never forget the warm hospitality of the Chinese people, and he would bring back the friendship of the Chinese government and the Chinese people to the Nepalese government and people. The Prime Minister also said that Nepal is situated between two great nations, China and India, and Nepal will always be a reliable friend of China and India. He proposed a toast to the lasting friendship of China, India, and Nepal.

Those present at the banquet were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and his wife, Ulanhu, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Yuan Zhongxian and his wife, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade Li Zheren, Lei Renmin, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, and Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China Zhao Puchu.

Mrs. R.K. Nehru, wife of the Indian Ambassador to China, and Counselor Singh were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 周总理设宴欢送阿查里雅首相

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴欢送阿查里雅首相
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来7日晚在中南海武成殿举行宴会，为尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和他的夫人及随行的高级官员饯行。
    周恩来总理向阿查里雅首相和其他的尼泊尔贵宾们表示惜别。他说，阿查里雅首相和其他的尼泊尔贵宾来到中国已经有十天了。通过阿查里雅首相的访问，中国和尼泊尔不仅恢复了传统的友谊，而且进一步发展了这种友好关系。周总理建议为中国尼泊尔友好、为中国和尼泊尔两国人民的幸福，为中国、尼泊尔、印度三国友好干杯。
    阿查里雅首相在宴会上致词说，中国人民对我的热情招待使我永志不忘，我将把中国政府和中国人民对尼泊尔政府和人民的友谊带回去。首相又说，尼泊尔处在中国和印度两个伟大的国家之间，尼泊尔将永远是中国和印度的可靠的朋友，他提议为中国、印度、尼泊尔三国的持久友谊干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的，有国务院副总理贺龙和夫人、乌兰夫，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫、袁仲贤和夫人，对外贸易部副部长李哲人、雷任民，中国人民解放军代总参谋长陈赓大将，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中国尼泊尔友好协会会长周建人，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁的夫人和参赞辛格也应邀出席了宴会。
