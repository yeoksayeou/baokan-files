Title: Henan Has Planted 30 Million Mu of Wheat
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 9th: Recently, Henan Province has experienced sunny weather, and agricultural cooperatives in various regions have been actively planting winter wheat. As of the 8th, the province had planted approximately 30 million mu of winter wheat.
Many local leaders have gone to the countryside to conduct thorough inspections of the wheat planting situation in agricultural cooperatives.
Henan Province is one of the largest wheat-producing provinces in the country, and this year plans to plant 73 million mu of wheat, maintaining last year's level.

### 河南已种麦三千万亩

1956-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

    河南已种麦三千万亩
    新华社郑州9日电　近来河南省天晴日和，各地农业社纷纷播种冬小麦。到8日为止，全省已经种下冬小麦约三千万亩。
    许多地区的领导干部都下乡去，深入检查农业社的种麦情况。
    河南省是全国产麦最多的一个省份，今年计划种麦七千三百万亩，保持去年的水平。
