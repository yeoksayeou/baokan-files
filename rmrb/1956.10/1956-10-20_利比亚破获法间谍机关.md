Title: Libya Smashes French Spy Organization
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, 18th (TASS dispatch) -- "Al-Bashir" reported that Libyan authorities have smashed a spy organization in Libya. The head of this spy organization was the Second Secretary of the French Embassy in Tripoli, Pousse (who died in an accident in September).

### 利比亚破获法间谍机关

1956-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    利比亚破获法间谍机关
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社的黎波里18日讯：“预言家报”报道，利比亚当局在利比亚破获了一个间谍组织。这个间谍组织的头目是法国驻的黎波里公使馆二等秘书普斯（普斯已经在9月间一次意外事故中死亡）。
