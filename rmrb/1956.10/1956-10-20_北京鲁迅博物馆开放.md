Title: Beijing Lu Xun Museum Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report: The newly built Beijing Lu Xun Museum held a preview on the 18th and opened to the public on the 19th. The preview hosted foreign writers and poets who came to China to attend the 20th anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's death.

The foreign guests showed great interest in Lu Xun's glorious life and immortal works. Among the Japanese friends was Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama, an old friend of Lu Xun's. He recalled many past events here. Standing in front of Lu Xun's remains, he was tearful and reluctant to leave for a long time. Writers and representatives from Mongolia, Yugoslavia, Indonesia, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the United Kingdom, Burma, Bulgaria, Romania, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Albania also came to visit. After visiting the Lu Xun Museum, the foreign guests also visited the nearby former residence of Lu Xun.

Before the opening of the museum, Guo Moruo, Shen Junru, Wu Yuzhang, Mao Dun, Hu Qiaomu, Zhou Yang, Zheng Zhenduo, Shao Lizi, Zhang Bojun, Hu Yuzhi, and Xia Yan visited. (Photo attached)

The Lu Xun Museum, located next to Lu Xun's former residence in Beijing, opened on the 19th. The museum displays Mr. Lu Xun's manuscripts, letters, and other relics, as well as books, newspapers, periodicals, woodcuts, and oil paintings related to his fighting life.

### 北京鲁迅博物馆开放

1956-10-20
第7版()
专栏：

    北京鲁迅博物馆开放
    据新华社讯　新落成的北京鲁迅博物馆18日举行预展后，已在19日开放。预展时接待了来我国参加鲁迅先生逝世二十周年纪念大会的外国作家和诗人。
    外宾们在这里看到鲁迅一生的光辉事迹和不朽的著作，都感到极大的兴趣。在日本友人中，有鲁迅的生前老朋友——内山完造。他在这里回忆起许多往事，他站在鲁迅遗容前面，热泪盈眶，久久不肯离去。蒙古、南斯拉夫、印度尼西亚、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、英国、缅甸、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、越南民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚等国的作家和代表也都来参观。外宾们看完了鲁迅博物馆，又到了邻近的鲁迅故居参观。
    在博物馆开幕前，郭沫若、沈钧儒、吴玉章、茅盾、胡乔木、周扬、郑振铎、邵力子、章伯钧、胡愈之和夏衍等都前往参观。（附图片）
    位在北京鲁迅故居旁边的鲁迅博物馆19日开放了。博物馆里陈列着鲁迅先生的手稿、信札和其他遗物，也陈列着和他战斗的一生有关的书报、刊物、木刻、油画等。　　本报记者　袁毅平、王志渊摄
