Title: Eden Cables Bulganin to Thank Ballet Troupe for Performance in England
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th TASS dispatch: Soviet Minister of Council Chairman Bulganin received a cable from British Prime Minister Eden thanking the Soviet Union for sending the Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Troupe to perform in England.

Eden's cable said: "My wife and I were very pleased to watch the Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Troupe's first performance last night – "Romeo and Juliet." The actors received a very warm welcome, and we are very grateful to you for sending this outstanding troupe to London."

### 艾登致电布尔加宁  感谢巴蕾舞剧团去英演出

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　艾登致电布尔加宁
    感谢巴蕾舞剧团去英演出
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁收到英国首相艾登感谢苏联派遣大剧院巴蕾舞剧团去英演出的电报。
    艾登的电报说：“我的妻子和我十分满意地观看了大剧院巴蕾舞剧团昨天晚上第一次演出的剧目——“罗米欧与朱丽叶”。演员们受到了极热烈的欢迎，我们十分感谢你派这个卓越的剧团来伦敦。”
