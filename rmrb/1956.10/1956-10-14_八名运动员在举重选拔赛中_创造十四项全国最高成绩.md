Title: Eight Athletes Create Fourteen National Records in Weightlifting Trials
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Eight weightlifters created fourteen national records today in the Chinese Olympic Team weightlifting trials, including total scores in six weight classes.

The famous lightweight weightlifter Huang Qianghui achieved a total score of 355 kg today, exceeding the 350 kg achieved by the second-place finisher in the same class at the 15th Olympic Games in 1952, and only 7.5 kg less than the first-place finisher.

The flyweight weightlifter Chen Jingkai achieved a total score of 307.5 kg today, equal to the second-place finisher in the same class at the previous Olympic Games. Chen Jingkai's snatch today was 130 kg, failing to break his own world record of 133 kg. Although he failed twice to lift 135 kg today, many spectators believe he still has great potential.

The featherweight weightlifter Yin Guohua achieved a total score of 315 kg today, exceeding the 312.5 kg achieved by the fifth-place finisher in the same class at the previous Olympic Games. The previous best national record for featherweight was 307.5 kg.

Middleweight weightlifter Zhu Hongquan, light heavyweight weightlifter Li Baiyu, and heavyweight weightlifter Chang Guanqun all achieved total scores today that exceeded the previous national best.

Zhu Hongquan's total score was 362.5 kg (the previous best was 360 kg). Li Baiyu's total score was 382.5 kg (the previous best was 377.5 kg). Chang Guanqun's total score was 377.5 kg (the previous best was 365 kg).

A total of twenty weightlifters participated in the weightlifting trials, including three weightlifters from Hong Kong, who participated in the flyweight, featherweight, lightweight, middleweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight competitions (no light middleweight).

Flyweight weightlifter Xue Deming broke the only remaining unbroken national record for flyweight snatch of 92.5 kg today with a score of 93 kg. As of now, all twenty-eight national weightlifting records announced in January of this year have been broken.

The other national records created today are as follows:

　　Flyweight press: Lin Zhongying, 95.5 kg (fourth attempt).

　　Featherweight press: Xu Honglin, 100 kg; snatch: Yin Guohua, 97.5 kg.

　　Lightweight snatch: Huang Qianghui, 107.5 kg.

　　Middleweight press: Zhu Hongquan, 117.5 kg.

　　Heavyweight press: Chang Guanqun, 132.5 kg; clean and jerk: Chang Guanqun, 142.5 kg (fourth attempt). (Photo attached)

### 八名运动员在举重选拔赛中  创造十四项全国最高成绩

1956-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    八名运动员在举重选拔赛中
    创造十四项全国最高成绩
    新华社13日讯　八名举重运动员今天在中国奥林匹克运动队举重选拔赛中创造了十四项全国最高成绩，其中包括六个等级的举重总成绩。
    著名的轻量级举重运动员黄强辉今天的举重总成绩达到了三五五公斤，已经超过1952年第十五届奥林匹克运动会同级第二名三五零公斤的成绩，并且比第一名的成绩只少七点五公斤。
    最轻量级举重运动员陈镜开今天的举重总成绩是三○七·五公斤，同上届奥林匹克运动会同级第二名的成绩相等。陈镜开今天双手挺举的成绩是一三○公斤，没能打破他自己保持的一三三公斤的世界纪录。他今天虽然两次试举一三五公斤的重量没有成功，但是许多观众都认为他还有着很大的潜在力量。
    次轻量级举重运动员殷国华今天取得了三一五公斤的总成绩，超过了上届奥林匹克运动会同级第五名三一二·五公斤的成绩。过去次轻量级全国最好的成绩是三○七·五公斤。
    中量级举重运动员朱鸿全、轻重量级举重运动员李白玉和重量级举重运动员常冠群今天的总成绩都超过了过去全国最好的成绩。
    朱鸿全的总成绩是三六二·五公斤（过去最好的是三六○公斤）。李白玉的总成绩是三八二·五公斤（过去最好的是三七七·五公斤）。常冠群的总成绩是三七七·五公斤（过去最好的是三六五公斤）。
    参加这次举重选拔赛的共有二十名举重运动员，其中包括三名香港的举重运动员，他们分别参加了最轻量级、次轻量级、轻量级、中量级、轻重量级和重量级的比赛（无次重量级）。
    最轻量级举重运动员薛德明今天以九十三公斤的成绩，打破了在今天以前唯一没有被打破的最轻量级双手抓举九二·五公斤的全国纪录。至此，今年1月公布的二十八项举重全国纪录已经全部被打破。
    今天创造的其他几项全国最高成绩如下：
    最轻量级双手推举：林仲英，九五·五公斤（第四次举起）。
    次轻量级双手推举：徐鸿林，一○○公斤；双手抓举：殷国华，九七·五公斤。
    轻量级双手抓举：黄强辉，一○七·五公斤。
    中量级双手推举：朱鸿全，一一七·五公斤。
    重量级双手推举：常冠群，一三二·五公斤；双手挺举：常冠群，一四二·五公斤（第四次举起）。（附图片）
