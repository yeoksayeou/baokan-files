Title: Tokyo Police Chief Should Be Held Responsible for Sunagawa-machi Bloodshed; Japan Socialist Party and Student Federation File Lawsuit with the Court
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th: The Japan Socialist Party and the All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations (Zengakuren) today separately filed lawsuits with the Tokyo District Court, accusing the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department Superintendent General Mitoru Eguchi of being responsible for the police's acts of violence in Sunagawa-machi.
    In order to expand the U.S. Tachikawa Air Base, the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department police created a bloody incident in Sunagawa-machi on October 12th and 13th. The lawsuits filed by these two groups stated that a total of 2,300 police officers used batons to beat workers, students, and farmers defending their land, resulting in injuries to approximately 1,000 people. (Picture attached)
    On October 3rd, the Japanese public held a rally in Sunagawa-machi, expressing their determination to fight to the end against the U.S. military's expansion of the Tachikawa base and to defend their land. (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 东京警察头子应对砂川町血案负责  日本社会党和学联向法庭提出控告

1956-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    东京警察头子应对砂川町血案负责
    日本社会党和学联向法庭提出控告
    新华社18日讯　东京消息：日本社会党和全日本学生自治会总联合会今天分别向东京地方裁判所控告东京警视厅警视总监江口见登留应对警察在砂川町犯下的暴行负责。
    为了扩大美国立川空军基地，东京警视厅的警察于10月12日和13日在砂川町制造了流血事件。这两个团体提出的控告说，共二千三百名警察用警棒殴打保卫土地的工人、学生和农民，结果使约一千人受伤。（附图片）
    10月3日，日本公众在砂川町举行集会，表示为反对美军扩大立川基地和保卫他们土地而斗争到底。　　　　　　　（新华社稿）
