Title: Film Commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen Begins Production; Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications to Issue Stamps Commemorating the Ninetieth Anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio is now undertaking the production of a film commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

　　This film will provide a general introduction to the life and deeds of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. It will include historically significant major events such as Mr. Sun Yat-sen's swearing-in ceremony for the Northern Expeditionary Army in Guangzhou and his journey north via Hong Kong, as well as the scene of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's coffin being transported from Biyun Temple in Beijing to Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum in Nanjing after his death. These historical shots are spliced together using film materials taken on the spot more than thirty years ago. The film studio's photographers stationed in various locations are also rushing to film Mr. Sun Yat-sen's former residences and memorial halls in Cuiheng Village, Zhongshan County, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Beijing, and other places.

　　This documentary will be completed on November 12th, the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio also plans to shoot a film of commemorative activities held in various places to mark the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications is about to issue stamps commemorating the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth.

　　The center of the commemorative stamp is a portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, with Mr. Sun Yat-sen's handwritten words "Future revolution will have no success without taking Russia as a teacher" printed on the right. Above are the words "Ninetieth Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth" and "1866-1956".

　　This set of commemorative stamps comes in two denominations: four fen and eight fen. The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has decided to first issue them in the four cities of Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Guangzhou in November.

### 纪念孙中山先生的影片开始摄制  邮电部即将发行纪念孙中山先生诞生九十周年的邮票

1956-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山先生的影片开始摄制
    邮电部即将发行纪念孙中山先生诞生九十周年的邮票
    新华社26日讯　纪念孙中山先生的影片现由中央新闻纪录电影制片厂着手摄制。
    这部影片将概括地介绍孙中山先生的生平事迹。其中有孙中山先生在广州举行北伐军誓师大会和经香港北上等富有历史意义的重大活动，以及孙中山先生逝世后灵柩由北京碧云寺运往南京中山陵的情景。这些历史镜头，都是利用三十多年前当场拍摄的影片资料剪接的。这个电影制片厂驻各地的摄影师也正分别赶拍中山县翠亨村、上海、广州、北京等地的孙中山先生故居、纪念馆。
    这部纪录影片将在孙中山先生诞生九十周年纪念日——11月12日制成。中央新闻纪录电影制片厂还计划拍摄一部各地举行孙中山先生诞生九十周年纪念活动的影片。
    新华社26日讯　邮电部即将发行纪念孙中山先生诞生九十周年的邮票。
    纪念邮票的中央是孙中山先生的肖像，右旁印着孙中山先生亲笔写的“今后之革命非以俄为师断无成就”，上方是“孙中山诞生九十周年”和“1866—1956”等字样。
    这套纪念邮票有四分、八分两种。邮电部决定于11月先在北京、上海、南京、广州四个城市发行。
