Title: Overseas Chinese in Japan Enthusiastically Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd: More than 1,500 overseas Chinese residing in Tokyo held a National Day celebration meeting at the Toyoshima Public Hall on the afternoon of October 1st.

    Kang Mingqiu, President of the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting.

    The assembly unanimously passed a declaration stating that the overseas Chinese in Japan are united and vowed to strive for the liberation of Taiwan with patriotic enthusiasm.

    Kanzo Uchiyama, Vice President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Kenzo Nakajima, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Yoshitaro Hirano, Secretary-General of the Japanese Peace Committee, representatives of Japanese war criminals recently released from China, and representatives of Korean residents in Japan delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

    The assembly received congratulatory telegrams from overseas Chinese organizations, Tikhvinsky, head of the Soviet delegation in Tokyo, many Japanese organizations, and Korean residents organizations in Japan.

    After the meeting, wonderful cultural performances were presented and Chinese films were shown.

    The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association also held a National Day celebration reception on the afternoon of September 30th. Representatives from all sectors of Japan, including peace, friendship, labor, and culture, as well as representatives of overseas Asian residents in Japan, were invited to attend the reception.

    Overseas Chinese residing in other parts of Japan also enthusiastically celebrated National Day on the 1st. Overseas Chinese in Yokohama held a celebration meeting at the Yokohama Overseas Chinese Women's Hall, followed by a celebration banquet.

### 旅日华侨热烈庆祝国庆

1956-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    旅日华侨热烈庆祝国庆
    新华社2日讯　东京消息：旅居东京的华侨一千五百多人，1日下午在丰岛公会堂举行庆祝国庆大会。
    东京华侨总会会长康鸣球在会上致祝词。
    大会全体一致通过一项宣言，表示旅日华侨团结一致，以爱国的热情誓为解放台湾而努力。
    日中友好协会副会长内山完造、日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏、日本和平委员会书记长平野义太郎、最近从中国被释放回国的日本战争犯罪分子代表及旅日朝鲜侨民代表等相继在会上致词祝贺。
    大会接到了华侨团体、苏联驻东京代表团团长季契温斯基、日本很多团体和朝鲜旅日侨民团体的贺电。
    会后表演了精彩的文娱节目和放映了中国影片。
    东京华侨总会还曾在9月30日下午举行了庆祝国庆的酒会。日本的和平、友好、劳动、文化等各界代表以及旅日亚洲各国侨民代表应邀出席了酒会。
    旅居日本其他各地的华侨在1日也都热烈庆祝国庆节。横滨华侨在横滨华侨妇女会馆举行了庆祝大会，会后还举行了庆祝宴会。
