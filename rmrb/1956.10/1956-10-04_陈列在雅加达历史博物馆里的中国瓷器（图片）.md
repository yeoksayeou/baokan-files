Title: Chinese Porcelain on Display at the Jakarta History Museum (Picture)
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 8

    Chinese Porcelain on Display at the Jakarta History Museum (Pencil Sketch)
    We visited the Jakarta History Museum,
    And saw Chinese porcelain and ancient coins;
    The history of the Chinese and Indonesian people is a history of friendship,
    The lives and cultures of the Chinese and Indonesian people are intertwined.
                                            Shao Yu
