Title: Greece to Support Egypt and Arab Countries in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Alexandria, 2nd. Greek Consul Agiropoulos, speaking to Greek students at Alexandria University on September 30th, said that in the Suez Canal dispute, Greece would stand with Egypt and support it and other Arab countries in the United Nations.

Agiropoulos expressed Greece's regret at the attempts by some Western countries to use force against Egypt. He also stated that Greece is grateful for Egypt's attitude on the Cyprus issue.

### 希腊将在联合国支持埃及和阿拉伯国家

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊将在联合国支持埃及和阿拉伯国家
    新华社2日讯　埃及亚历山大港消息：希腊领事阿居罗波洛9月30日在亚历山大港大学向希腊学生们讲话的时候说，在苏伊士运河纠纷中，希腊将同埃及站在一边，并且将在联合国支持它和其他阿拉伯国家。
    阿居罗波洛表示，希腊对于某些西方国家企图对埃及使用武力感到遗憾。他还表示，希腊感谢埃及在塞浦路斯问题上采取的态度。
