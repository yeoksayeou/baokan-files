Title: Soviet Experts Assisting in the Identification of Dam Sites in the Yangtze River Basin Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 1

    Soviet experts, including Semenov, Shantsel, Sokolov, and Yakusheva, who came to China to assist in identifying geological work at dam sites in the Yangtze River Basin, returned to their country by train on the morning of the 16th, according to Xinhua News Agency on the 16th. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Vice Minister of Water Resources Feng Zhongyun and others.
    Another Soviet geological expert, Popov, returned to his country by plane on the 17th.

### 帮助我国鉴定长江流域坝址工作的苏联专家回国

1956-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    帮助我国鉴定长江流域坝址工作的苏联专家回国
    据新华社16日讯　来我国协助鉴定长江流域坝址地段地质工作的苏联地质专家谢苗诺夫、商采尔、索科洛夫、雅库舍娃等一行四人，已经在16日早晨乘火车回国。前往车站欢送的有水利部副部长冯仲云等多人。
    另外一位苏联地质专家波波夫在17日乘飞机回国。
