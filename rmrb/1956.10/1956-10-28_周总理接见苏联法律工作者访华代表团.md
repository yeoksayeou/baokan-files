Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Soviet Legal Workers Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this evening with the entire Soviet legal workers delegation headed by Soviet Deputy Procurator General Mishutin.

Present at the meeting were Gao Kelin, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Huang Huoxing, Deputy Procurator General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Minister of Justice Shi Liang, and Vice Minister Zheng Shaowen.

### 周总理接见苏联法律工作者访华代表团

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏联法律工作者访华代表团
    新华社27日讯　周恩来总理今日晚上接见了以苏联副总检察长米舒金为首的苏联法律工作者访华代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有最高人民法院副院长高克林，最高人民检察院副检察长黄火星，司法部长史良、副部长郑绍文等。
