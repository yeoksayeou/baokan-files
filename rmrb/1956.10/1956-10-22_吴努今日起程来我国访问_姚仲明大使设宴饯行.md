Title: U Nu Departs Today for Visit to Our Country; Ambassador Yao Zhongming Hosts Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 21st. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, held a banquet last night to bid farewell to U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League. U Nu, at the invitation of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai, will leave Rangoon on October 22 for a friendly visit to China.
    Chairman U Nu, Madam Nu, and their son attended the banquet.
    Also attending the banquet were: Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe and his wife, Minister of Transport U Win Maung, Minister of Information Tekin Chi Maung, Minister of Land Nationalization Tekin Shan Mi, Aung San's wife, Supreme Court Judge U Min Dun, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ba Lin Dun, as well as other senior Burmese government officials, prominent figures in Rangoon society, and representatives of various diplomatic missions stationed in Rangoon.
    During the banquet, U Nu, U Ba Swe, and Ambassador Yao Zhongming engaged in friendly and pleasant conversations.

### 吴努今日起程来我国访问  姚仲明大使设宴饯行

1956-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努今日起程来我国访问  姚仲明大使设宴饯行
    新华社仰光21日电　中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明昨晚举行宴会，为反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努饯行。吴努应中国总理周恩来的邀请，将在10月22日离开仰光去中国进行友好访问。
    吴努主席、努夫人以及他们的儿子出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有：缅甸总理吴巴瑞及夫人，交通部长吴温貌，宣传部长德钦漆貌，土地国有化部长德钦山密，昂山夫人，最高法院法官吴敏顿，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，以及缅甸政府其他高级官员，仰光的社会名流，各国驻仰光外交使团代表。
    在宴会中，吴努、吴巴瑞和姚仲明大使进行了友好的愉快的交谈。
