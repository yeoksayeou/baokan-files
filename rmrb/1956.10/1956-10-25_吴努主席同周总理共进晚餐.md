Title: Chairman U Nu Dines with Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th -- The Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, dined this evening with the Premier of the State Council and Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhou Enlai, and his wife.

    Judge U Min Thein of the Supreme Court of Burma and his wife, and the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife, were also invited to dine together.

    Accompanying them at the dinner were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and his wife, and the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming.

### 吴努主席同周总理共进晚餐

1956-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努主席同周总理共进晚餐
    新华社24日讯　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和夫人今晚同国务院总理、中国共产党中央委员会副主席周恩来和夫人共进晚餐。
    缅甸联邦高级法院法官吴敏登和夫人，缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和夫人，也应邀一起进餐。
    陪同进餐的，有国务院副总理陈毅和夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
