Title: Soviet-Belgian Cultural Cooperation Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: TASS reports: On October 25th, in the Kremlin, the Soviet and Belgian government delegations signed the Soviet-Belgian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and Belgian Foreign Minister Spaak signed the agreement.

### 苏比签订文化合作协定

1956-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏比签订文化合作协定
    据新华社26日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联和比利时两国政府代表团10月25日在克里姆林宫签订了苏比文化合作协定。苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫和比利时外交部长斯巴克分别在协定上签了字。
