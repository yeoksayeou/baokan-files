Title: Picture
Author: Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 2

    A new, modern cement factory has appeared in the southwest corner of Datong City. The equipment for this factory was supplied by the German Democratic Republic, and the main machinery of the factory is controlled by electrical equipment. The above picture shows a corner of the Datong Cement Factory.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua
