Title: Japanese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: A four-member Japanese trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday. The head of the delegation is Fukubei Ichikawa, Chairman of the All Japan Congress of Industrial Unions.
    Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others welcomed them at the airport. Kenta Kaneko, the Japanese representative of the World Federation of Trade Unions Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau, also welcomed them at the airport.
    The three-member Japanese Metalworkers' Union delegation and the eight-member Japanese Electrical Communication Workers' Union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the 22nd. The head of the Japanese Metalworkers' Union delegation is Yonematsu Nakamura, Deputy Chairman of the Mie Branch of the All Japan Metalworkers' Union, and the head of the Japanese Electrical Communication Workers' Union delegation is Tomio Watanabe, Director of the Finance Bureau of the Central Headquarters of the All Japan Telecommunication Workers' Union.

### 日本工会代表团到达北京

1956-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本工会代表团到达北京
    新华社25日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的日本工会代表团一行四人，昨天乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是全日本产业别工会会议主席市川福平。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、书记处书记董昕等。到机场欢迎的还有世界工联亚澳联络局日本代表金子健太。
    应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的日本五金工会代表团一行三人和应中国邮电工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的日本全国电气通讯工会代表团一行八人，已经在22日到达北京。日本五金工会代表团负责人是全日本金属工会三重支部副委员长中村米松，日本全国电气通讯工会代表团负责人是日本全国电气通讯工会中央本部财政局长渡部富雄。
