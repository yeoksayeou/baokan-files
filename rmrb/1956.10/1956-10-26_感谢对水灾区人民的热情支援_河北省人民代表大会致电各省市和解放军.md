Title: Expressing Gratitude for the Enthusiastic Support for the People in the Flood-stricken Areas: Hebei Provincial People's Congress Sends Telegrams to Various Provinces, Cities, and the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 25th: The fifth session of the First Hebei Provincial People's Congress, held in Baoding, unanimously passed a telegram of thanks on the 24th to the People's Committees of Beijing, Tianjin, Shanxi, Henan, Shandong, Liaoning, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, thanking them for their support to the people of Hebei Province who suffered from the floods.

The telegram stated that since Hebei Province suffered from severe flooding, you have not only given us great assistance in the fight against flood prevention, rescue, and saving disaster victims, but also in the supply of production and living materials and the promotion of sideline products in the disaster areas. Recently, you have also been collecting winter clothing for the disaster victims in Hebei Province. Your enthusiasm and enormous support have greatly encouraged the courage and confidence of the people in the disaster areas of Hebei Province to overcome the disaster. Here, we would like to express our deepest gratitude to you on behalf of the people of Hebei Province and through you to the relevant departments and the broad masses of workers, cadres, and people.

The telegram stated that at present, the people in the disaster areas of Hebei Province are extensively and deeply carrying out production for self-relief.

Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 25th: The fifth session of the First Hebei Provincial People's Congress, held in Baoding, unanimously passed a telegram of thanks on the 24th to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, thanking the troops for their active rescue and enthusiastic support to the people of Hebei Province who suffered from the floods.

The telegram stated that Hebei Province suffered severe flooding this year. When we were fighting the floods, you sent planes to airdrop a large number of life-saving equipment and food to the people surrounded by water, and sent tens of thousands of officers and soldiers to actively participate in the struggle to protect the dikes, rush to rescue, and save the victims. Then, you organized comfort groups and medical teams to go deep into the disaster areas of Hebei Province for cordial condolences and free treatment. These enormous human, material, and spiritual assistance have greatly encouraged the people in the disaster areas to overcome the courage of the disaster, and made the people of Hebei Province further realize the close relationship between the people's army and the people. We would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to you on behalf of the people of Hebei Province.

### 感谢对水灾区人民的热情支援  河北省人民代表大会致电各省市和解放军

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    感谢对水灾区人民的热情支援
    河北省人民代表大会致电各省市和解放军
    据新华社保定25日电　在保定举行的河北省第一届人民代表大会第五次会议24日一致通过一项致北京市、天津市、山西省、河南省、山东省、辽宁省和内蒙古自治区人民委员会的感谢电，感谢他们对河北省遭受水灾人民的支援。
    这份电报说，自河北省遭受严重水灾以来，你们不仅在防汛、抢险和抢救灾民的斗争中给了我们大力的援助，并且在灾区的生产、生活资料供应和副业产品的推销等方面也都给了我们很大的帮助。最近你们还在为河北省灾民募集寒衣。你们这种热情和巨大的支援，大大地鼓舞了河北省灾区人民战胜灾荒的勇气和信心。在这里，我们谨代表河北省全省人民特向你们并通过你们向各有关部门和广大职工干部、人民群众表示深切的感谢。
    电报说，现在，河北省灾区人民正在广泛深入地开展生产自救。
    据新华社保定25日电　在保定举行的河北省第一届人民代表大会第五次会议24日一致通过一项致中国人民解放军的感谢电，感谢各部队对河北省遭受水灾人民的积极抢救和热情支援。
    这份电报说，今年河北省遭受了严重的水灾。当我们同洪水搏斗的时候，你们派出飞机向被水围困的人民空投了大量的救生器材和食物，并派出数以万计的官兵积极参加了护堤抢险和抢救灾民的斗争。接着，你们又组织了慰问团和医疗队，深入河北省灾区进行亲切的慰问和免费治疗，以上这些人力、物力和精神上的巨大援助，大大地鼓舞了灾区人民战胜灾荒的勇气，并且使河北省人民进一步体会到人民军队和人民群众的密切关系。我们谨代表河北省全省人民向你们致以衷心的感谢。
