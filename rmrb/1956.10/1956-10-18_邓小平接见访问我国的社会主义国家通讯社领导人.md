Title: Deng Xiaoping Receives Leaders of News Agencies from Socialist Countries Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and Hu Qiaomu, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, received the leaders of the news agencies of various socialist countries who were visiting China at the invitation of Xinhua News Agency on the morning of the 17th. They were Nano, Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency; Gavrilova, Director of the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency; Balczi, Director of the Hungarian Telegraph Agency; Hoang Tuấn, Director of the Vietnam News Agency; Wieland, Director of the German News Agency; Park Moo, Director of the Korean Central News Agency; Dumitrescu, Director of the Romanian News Agency; Mecher, Deputy Director of the Czechoslovak News Agency; Stainduhar, Deputy Director of the Yugoslav Telegraph Agency; and Hoffman, Director of the International Department of the Polish Press Agency.

Present at the reception were Wu Lengxi, Director of the Xinhua News Agency, and Zhu Muzhi, Miao Haileng, and Deng Gang, Deputy Directors.

Pargunov, Director of TASS, who was also invited to visit China, left Beijing for the Soviet Union on October 12 after visiting Beijing and Shanghai.

### 邓小平接见访问我国的社会主义国家通讯社领导人

1956-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平接见访问我国的社会主义国家通讯社领导人
    新华社17日讯　中共中央总书记邓小平和书记处候补书记胡乔木17日上午接见了应新华通讯社的邀请来中国访问的各社会主义国家通讯社的领导人，他们是阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长纳诺、保加利亚通讯社社长加夫里洛娃、匈牙利通讯社社长巴尔奇、越南通讯社社长黄俊、德意志通讯社社长韦兰德、朝鲜中央通讯社社长朴茂、罗马尼亚通讯社社长杜米特列斯库、捷克斯洛伐克通讯社副社长梅切尔、南斯拉夫通讯社副社长斯塔因杜哈尔和波兰通讯社国际部主任霍夫曼。
    接见时在座的，有新华通讯社社长吴冷西，副社长朱穆之、缪海棱、邓岗。
    应邀来华访问的塔斯社社长帕尔古诺夫，在访问了北京和上海之后，已于10月12日离北京回苏联。
