Title: Taiwan Commodity Prices Continue to Rise
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Taiwan's commodity prices continued to rise in the first half of October. According to market information published in Taiwan newspapers, the wholesale prices of rice, flour, and sugar in Taipei City on October 13th increased compared to October 1st as follows: Taiwan's special grade sugar increased from 460 yuan (Taiwan dollars, same below) to 538 yuan per 100 kilograms; Taiwan's central Penglai brown rice increased from 179 yuan to 186 yuan per 60 kilograms, and central Penglai white rice increased from 197 yuan to 205 yuan; a certain brand of flour increased from 76 yuan to 79 yuan per bag.

### 台湾物价继续上涨

1956-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    台湾物价继续上涨
    新华社23日讯　台湾物价在10月上半月继续上涨。据台湾报纸刊载的商情，10月13日台北市米、面、糖的批发价比10月1日上涨如下：台湾特级砂糖每百公斤由四百六十元（台币，下同）涨到五百三十八元；台湾中部蓬来糙米每六十公斤由一百七十九元涨到一百八十六元，中部蓬来白米由一百九十七元涨到二百零五元；一品牌面粉每袋由七十六元涨到七十九元。
