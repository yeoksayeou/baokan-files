Title: Syria, Lebanon, Egypt, Jordan Decide to Accuse Israel of Invading Jordan
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, October 3rd: TASS Beirut, October 2nd: According to Lebanese newspaper reports, the four Arab countries of Syria, Lebanon, Egypt, and Jordan have decided to jointly file a complaint with the United Nations Security Council regarding the recent invasion of Jordanian territory by Israeli troops. The first meeting to draft the indictment was held on October 1st.

The newspaper also reported that a meeting of military representatives from these four Arab countries will be held in Damascus or Amman in the near future.

### 叙利亚、黎巴嫩、埃及、约旦  决定向安理会控诉以色列侵犯约旦

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚、黎巴嫩、埃及、约旦
    决定向安理会控诉以色列侵犯约旦
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特10月2日讯：据黎巴嫩报纸消息，叙利亚、黎巴嫩、埃及和约旦四个阿拉伯国家已经决定就以色列军队不久以前侵犯约旦领土一事共同向联合国安全理事会提出控诉。10月1日举行了拟定控诉书的第一次会议。
    报纸还报道，这四个阿拉伯国家的军事代表会议将于最近在大马士革或安曼举行。
