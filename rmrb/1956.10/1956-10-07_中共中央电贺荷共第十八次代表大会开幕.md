Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to the Opening of the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 1

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, to the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands:
    On the occasion of the convening of the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all party members and the people of the whole country, extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the Congress.
    We wish the 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands complete success, and wish the Communist Party of the Netherlands new achievements in strengthening the unity of the working class, striving for democratic renewal and social progress, and wish the Dutch people greater progress in safeguarding national interests, opposing participation in military blocs, striving for world peace, and developing friendly relations among the people of all countries.
                                    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                             October 2, 1956

### 中共中央电贺荷共第十八次代表大会开幕

1956-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺荷共第十八次代表大会开幕荷兰共产党中央委员会转荷兰共产党第十八次代表大会：
    欣逢荷兰共产党召开第十八次代表大会，中国共产党中央委员会谨代表全体党员和全国人民，向大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    祝荷兰共产党第十八次代表大会获得圆满的成功，祝荷兰共产党在增强工人阶级的团结、争取民主更新和社会进步的斗争中取得新的成就，祝荷兰人民在维护民族利益、反对参加军事集团、争取世界和平和发展各国人民友好关系的努力中取得更大的进展。
                                    中国共产党中央委员会
                                             1956年10月2日
