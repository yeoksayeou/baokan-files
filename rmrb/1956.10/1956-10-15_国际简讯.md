　　·International Briefs·
　　Soviet Union Appoints Smirnov as Ambassador to West Germany
　　The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR appointed A.A. Smirnov as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Federal Republic of Germany, relieving him of his previous post as the Soviet Ambassador to Austria.
　　Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance Troupe Visits Vietnam
　　The Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance and Music Troupe arrived in Hanoi on the afternoon of October 13th. All members of the troupe were warmly welcomed at the railway station by a delegation of over one hundred representatives from Vietnamese cultural and artistic circles, headed by Qu Huy Can, Deputy Minister of Culture of Vietnam. They will be visiting and performing in Vietnam for about a month.
　　Yugoslav "Kolo" Dance Ensemble Performs in the US
　　The Yugoslav "Kolo" National Folk Dance Ensemble gave its first performance in the United States at Carnegie Hall in New York on the evening of October 11th. The performance received favorable reviews from various New York newspapers.
　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 国际简讯

1956-10-15
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    苏联任命斯米尔诺夫为驻西德大使
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命安·安·斯米尔诺夫为苏联驻德意志联邦共和国特命全权大使，免除他原先担任的苏联驻奥地利大使的职务。
    罗“云雀”民间舞蹈团访问越南
    罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团13日下午到达河内。全体团员在火车站受到以越南文化部副部长瞿辉近为首的越南文化艺术界代表一百多人的热烈欢迎。他们这次将在越南访问演出约一个月左右。
    南“科罗”歌舞团在美演出
    南斯拉夫“科罗”国家民间歌舞团10月11日晚上在纽约卡乃基大厅作访问美国的第一次演出。演出获得了纽约各报纸的好评。
          （据新华社讯）
