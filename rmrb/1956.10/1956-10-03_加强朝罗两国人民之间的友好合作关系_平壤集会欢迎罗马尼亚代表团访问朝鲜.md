Title: Strengthen the Friendly Cooperative Relations Between the Chinese and Romanian Peoples: Pyongyang Rally Welcomes Romanian Delegation's Visit to Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd - A delegation from the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party, arrived in Pyongyang by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Korean government.
After disembarking, the delegation, accompanied by Premier Kim Il-sung, who came to welcome them, traveled by car to Kim Il-sung Square, where a grand mass rally was held. A total of 250,000 people gathered in the square and along the streets the delegation passed to welcome them. The cities of Pyongyang and the airport were decorated with the national flags of both Korea and Romania.
At the welcoming rally, Kim Il-sung delivered a welcoming speech. He first expressed a warm welcome to the delegation and thanked Romania for its assistance to Korea. He then said that this visit by the delegation to Korea would make a great contribution to strengthening the friendly cooperative relations between the peoples of Korea and Romania, and to the common cause of the two countries in building socialism and striving for world peace. He also said that, relying on the united efforts of all the Korean people and the support of peace-loving forces around the world, the peaceful reunification of Korea will surely be realized.
Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej said in his speech at the welcoming rally that the economic and cultural ties between Romania and Korea are expanding and strengthening, and we are confident that this relationship will be further strengthened. He said that the Romanian People's Republic believes that the efforts of the Korean people to peacefully reunify their country on a democratic basis are an important factor in strengthening peace in the Far East and the world, and gives active support to these efforts.
Other members of the Romanian delegation were: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Mogioroş; Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Workers' Party, Rauţu; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, Vladimir; and Romanian Ambassador to Korea, Jianu.
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd - Korean Premier Kim Il-sung held a banquet this evening to welcome the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej.
More than 350 people attended the banquet, including Korean state and Labor Party leaders, heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, senior generals of the People's Army, and diplomatic envoys of various fraternal countries stationed in Korea.
This afternoon, the Romanian delegation visited the leaders of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, the Korean Cabinet, and the Central Committee of the Labor Party.

### 加强朝罗两国人民之间的友好合作关系  平壤集会欢迎罗马尼亚代表团访问朝鲜

1956-10-03
第5版()
专栏：

    加强朝罗两国人民之间的友好合作关系
    平壤集会欢迎罗马尼亚代表团访问朝鲜
    新华社平壤2日电　应邀到朝鲜访问的以罗马尼亚工人党第一书记乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团今天下午乘飞机到达平壤。
    代表团下飞机后，在前来欢迎的金日成首相陪同下，乘汽车前往金日成广场。在那里，举行了盛大的群众欢迎大会。聚集在广场和代表团经过的街道两旁的欢迎群众共有二十五万人。平壤全城和飞机场上到处飘扬着朝罗两国国旗。
    在欢迎大会上，金日成致欢迎辞。他首先对代表团表示热烈的欢迎，对罗马尼亚给予朝鲜的援助表示感谢。他接着说，代表团这次来朝鲜访问将对加强朝罗两国人民之间的友好合作关系、对两国建设社会主义和争取世界和平的共同事业作出巨大的贡献。他还说，依靠全体朝鲜人民的团结努力和全世界爱好和平的力量的支持，朝鲜的和平统一必能实现。
    乔治乌—德治在欢迎大会上讲话说，罗朝两国之间的经济文化联系正在扩大和加强着，我们确信，这个关系将会进一步加强。他说，罗马尼亚人民共和国认为，朝鲜人民在民主的基础上和平统一祖国的努力是加强远东和世界和平的重要因素，并且对这种努力给以积极的支持。
    罗马尼亚代表团的其他成员是：罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席莫吉奥罗希、工人党政治局候补委员拉乌图、工人党中央书记处书记弗拉基米尔、罗马尼亚驻朝鲜大使日亚努。
    新华社平壤2日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团。
    朝鲜国家和劳动党领导人，各民主党派和社会团体负责人，人民军高级将领，各兄弟国家驻朝鲜外交使节等共三百五十多人，出席了宴会。
    今天下午，罗马尼亚代表团访问了朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会、朝鲜内阁和劳动党中央委员会的领导人。
