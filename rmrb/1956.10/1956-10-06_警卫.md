Title: Guard
Author: Li Changda
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 8

    Guard

    The sea breeze sends the fleet out of the harbor,
    At the bow stands a fishing expert,
    Each one blows a conch shell.
    The loud sound of the conch urges the fishing boats to move quickly,
    The gunners raised their arms,
    Blessing the fishermen a smooth journey.
    Fishermen, quickly raise the sails full,
    Helmsman, be bold and go forward!
    Our cannons raise their heads high,
    Faithfully guarding the motherland's ocean.
    Wind, quickly send the fleet to the fishing grounds,
    And send the gunners' regards along the way,
    We will wait until the sun sets in the west,
    We will guard until the east dawns again,
    Hoping that the returning fleet,
    Each one is so heavy that it lowers the waves.

### 警卫
李昌达
1956-10-06
第8版()
专栏：

    警卫
    李昌达
    海风把船队送出港口，
    船头上站着打渔的能手，
    一个个把海螺吹奏。
    海螺宏亮的声音催促着渔船快行，
    炮手们扬起了手臂，
    祝福渔人们一帆风顺。
    渔人呵，快拉足风蓬，
    舵手呵，你大胆向前！
    我们的大炮昂起了头，
    忠实地警卫着祖国的海洋。
    风呵，你快把船队送到渔场，
    顺便把炮手的心意捎上，
    我们会等到夕阳西下，
    我们将守到东方再亮，
    但愿归来的船队，
    只只沉重得压低了浪花。
