Title: Security Council Discusses Jordan-Israel Dispute; Israeli Representative Threatens to Use Force Against Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, October 27th: The United Nations Security Council resumed its discussion on October 25th of Jordan's complaint against Israeli acts of aggression. The Council also discussed Israel's complaint against Jordan.

    Israeli representative Eban, in his speech, defended the military actions taken by Israeli armed forces against Jordan. He tried every means to uphold Israel's so-called "retaliation policy" against Arab countries. He explained that Israeli authorities refused to cooperate with the United Nations Truce Supervision Organization (UNTSO) because this organization was ineffective. He said that Israel would use force against Arab countries as "retaliation" in the future according to its own will. In this regard, he cited the statements of U.S. President Eisenhower, Australian Prime Minister Menzies, and British Foreign Secretary Lloyd threatening to use force in resolving the Suez Canal issue.

    Jordanian representative Rifai made a brief speech. He thanked other countries for their support to Jordan and refuted the arguments of the Israeli representative.

    Iranian representative Ardalan suggested that the Security Council seek the opinion of UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld before continuing the debate.

    Soviet representative Sobolev requested that the meeting now select the date for the next meeting, rather than delaying the resolution of the issue.

    The President of the Security Council, France's Goulne-Janty, then requested careful study of the statements of both Jordan and Israel. He pointed out the seriousness of the Israeli violation of Jordan and expressed his hope that peace could be restored and maintained.

### 安理会讨论约旦以色列争端  以色列代表扬言要对阿拉伯国家使用武力

1956-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    安理会讨论约旦以色列争端
    以色列代表扬言要对阿拉伯国家使用武力
    据新华社27日讯　纽约消息：联合国安全理事会10月25日重新开始讨论约旦对以色列侵略行动的控诉案。理事会同时讨论以色列对约旦的控诉案。
    以色列代表伊班在发言中为以色列武装部队对约旦所采取的军事行动辩护。他千方百计地维护以色列对阿拉伯国家执行的所谓“报复政策”。他解释说，以色列当局拒绝同联合国监督执行停战协定的机构合作，是因为这个机构没有效力。他说，以色列今后将根据自己的意愿，对阿拉伯国家使用武力作为“报复”。在这方面，他援引了美国总统艾森豪威尔、澳大利亚总理孟席斯和英国外交大臣劳埃德在解决苏伊士运河问题方面威胁要使用武力的言论。
    约旦代表里法伊作了简短的发言。他感谢其他国家对约旦的支持，并且驳斥了以色列代表的论点。
    伊朗代表安迪让建议安全理事会征求联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的意见，然后再继续进行辩论。
    苏联代表索波列夫要求会议现在就选定下一次会议的日期，而不要拖延问题的解决。
    安全理事会主席法国的戈尔尼—让蒂接着要求仔细研究约旦和以色列双方的发言。他指出以色列侵犯约旦的事件的严重性，并且表示希望能够恢复和维持和平。
