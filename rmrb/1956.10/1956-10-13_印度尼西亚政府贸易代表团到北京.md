Title: Indonesian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- The Indonesian government trade delegation, a group of seven, led by the Deputy Head of the delegation, Taher Ibrahim, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The head of the delegation is Wiwoho Purbohadidjojo, the Indonesian Ambassador to China.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Yang Haolu, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, Wang Zhaoxun, Commissioner of the Fourth Bureau, and Wu Jingfang, Acting Director of the Protocol Department.

Anwar Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and others also welcomed them at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚政府贸易代表团到北京

1956-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府贸易代表团到北京
    新华社12日讯　印度尼西亚政府贸易代表团一行七人，在副团长、印度尼西亚经济部对外贸易署署长塔赫尔·易卜拉欣率领下今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长由印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托担任。
    到飞机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部部长助理杨浩庐、第四局专员王兆勋和交际处代处长巫竞放等人。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼等也到机场欢迎。
