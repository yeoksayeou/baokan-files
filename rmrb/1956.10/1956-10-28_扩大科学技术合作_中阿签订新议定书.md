Title: China and Albania Sign New Protocol to Expand Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 26th: Tirana News: The Chinese Technical Cooperation and Agricultural Observation Delegation and the Albanian government representatives recently held the third meeting of the Sino-Albanian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee in Tirana, and signed the corresponding protocol on October 22nd.

According to the signed protocol, the People's Republic of China will provide Albania with technical data on the utilization of rice by-products, prevention of alkalization of rice fields, gypsum production, rock wood carving, etc., provide seeds and saplings, and accept Albanian experts for internship in straw weaving production, etc. Albania will provide the People's Republic of China with information on social insurance, healthcare, construction of small hydropower stations in mountainous areas, cultivation of tobacco and olives, as well as seeds of various southern crops and grains for experimentation.

The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of friendship and sincere desire to further expand scientific and technological research between the two countries.

The Chinese delegation visited major institutions in various parts of Albania and toured factories and urban and rural cooperatives.

### 扩大科学技术合作  中阿签订新议定书

1956-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    扩大科学技术合作
    中阿签订新议定书
    新华社布拉格26日电　地拉那消息：中国技术合作和农业考察代表团同阿尔巴尼亚政府代表，最近在地拉那举行了中阿科学技术合作委员会第三届会议，并且在10月22日签订了相应的议定书。
    根据已经签定的议定书，中华人民共和国将供给阿尔巴尼亚有关利用水稻副产品，防止稻田碱化、石膏生产、岩石木材雕刻等技术资料，供给种子和树苗，接受阿尔巴尼亚专家实习草编品生产等。阿尔巴尼亚将供给中华人民共和国关于社会保险、保健、建造山区小型水电站、培植烟草、橄榄等有关资料，以及作试验用的各种南方作物和谷物的种子等。
    会议是在双方友好和为了进一步扩大两国间科学技术研究的真诚愿望的气氛中进行的。
    中国代表团访问了阿尔巴尼亚各地比较主要的机构，参观了工厂和城乡合作社。
