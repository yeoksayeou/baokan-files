Title: Chinese Ambassadors in Various Countries Hold Receptions
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 5

    The Chinese Embassy in Egypt held a National Day reception at 7:00 PM on October 1st.
    Invited to the reception were many Egyptian government ministers and senior officials, the Secretary-General of the Presidential Palace, the Governor of the Liberation Province, the Mayor of Cairo, as well as people from Egyptian financial, economic, cultural, and educational circles, and labor union leaders. The Secretary-General of the Islamic Congress, Sadat, and the Secretary-General of the Arab League, Hassouna, were also invited to attend the reception.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Egypt, as well as the entire Soviet agricultural delegation and the Czechoslovak trade delegation currently visiting Egypt, also attended the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency reported that Feng Xuan, the Chinese Ambassador to Switzerland, held a National Day reception at the embassy on the evening of October 1st. More than 400 people attended the reception, including the Swiss Federal Political Minister, Petitpierre, the Speaker of the Federal Assembly, leaders of the Swiss Farmers' Party, the Liberal Party, and the Social Democratic Party, and well-known figures from all walks of life.

### 我驻各国使节举行招待会

1956-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻各国使节举行招待会
    中华人民共和国驻埃及大使馆在10月1日晚上七时举行了庆祝国庆招待会。
    应邀到会的有埃及政府许多部长和高级官员、总统府秘书长、解放省省长、开罗市长以及埃及财政、经济、文化、教育等各界人士和工会领袖。伊斯兰大会秘书长萨达特和阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳也应邀出席招待会。
    各国驻埃及的外交使节以及正在访问埃及的苏联农业代表团和捷克斯洛伐克贸易代表团全体人员也出席了招待会。
    中国驻瑞士大使冯铉1日晚在大使馆举行国庆招待会。出席招待会的有瑞士联邦政治部长彼蒂彼爱，联邦议会议长，瑞士农民党、自由党、社会民主党的领导人和各界知名人士等四百多人。（据新华社讯）
