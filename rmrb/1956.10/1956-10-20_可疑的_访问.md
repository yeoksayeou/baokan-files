Title: Suspicious "Visit"
Author: Fang Da
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 6

    Suspicious "Visit"
    An article in the "Tribune Weekly," published in Sydney, Australia, on October 3, discussed the "visit" of U.S. special agent Allen Dulles to Australia at the end of September: he quietly slipped into Canberra, the Australian capital, stayed for a short time, and then slipped away without any fanfare. The newspaper asked, "Why did the U.S. special agent sneak in?"
    Indeed, when a person visits someone's home, it should be open and aboveboard. Why was Allen Dulles acting so furtively?
    "Tribune Weekly" revealed a piece of information: the Spry-Ricardo Security Organization in Canberra is plotting to provoke unfriendly actions between athletes from countries with different social systems during the Olympic Games to sabotage the Olympic Games. At the same time, they are also planning to install special interpreters in the Olympic Village where athletes from socialist countries reside, or to carry out other activities. The newspaper believes that Allen Dulles' "visit" is related to these sinister plans.
    On November 22nd, the Olympic Games will open in Australia. Such sporting events can enable people from different countries to understand each other, enhance friendship, learn from each other, and work together for world peace. The reactionary group in the United States actually opposes the Olympic Games and everything that peace-loving people cherish. However, the Olympic torch is a torch of peace, and it will be passed down from generation to generation by peace-loving people, which cannot be extinguished by the likes of Allen Dulles.
             (Fang Da)

### 可疑的“访问”
方达
1956-10-20
第6版()
专栏：国际札记

    可疑的“访问”
    10月3日，在澳大利亚悉尼市出版的“论坛周报”上，有一篇文章谈到美国特务头子艾伦·杜勒斯在9月底“访问”澳大利亚的情况：他悄悄地溜进了澳大利亚首都堪培拉，停留一个短时间，然后又毫不声张地溜走了。这家报纸问道：“美国特务头子偷偷进来了，为什么？”
    的确，一个人到别人家里去做客，应该是光明正大的，艾伦·杜勒斯为什么这样鬼头鬼脑的呢？
    “论坛周报”透露一个消息说：堪培拉的斯普来—里查德保安机构正策划在奥林匹克运动会期间在社会制度不同的国家的运动员之间挑拨起不友好的举动，来破坏奥林匹克运动会。同时，他们还计划在奥林匹克村中社会主义国家的运动员所住的地方安插特别的译员，或者进行其他勾当。这个报纸认为，艾伦·杜勒斯的“访问”是同这些阴谋计划有关的。
    11月22日，奥林匹克运动会就要在澳大利亚开幕了。这种体育活动可以使不同国家的人们能够彼此了解、增进友谊、相互学习、共同为世界和平而努力。美国的反动集团竟敌视奥林匹克运动会，敌视和平人民所爱好的一切。可是，奥林匹克的火炬是和平的火炬，它将由爱好和平的人们一代一代传下去，那是艾伦·杜勒斯之流扑灭不了的。
             （方达）
