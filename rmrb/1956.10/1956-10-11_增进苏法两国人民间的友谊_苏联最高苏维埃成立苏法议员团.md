Title: To Enhance Friendship Between the Peoples of the Soviet Union and France: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Establishes Soviet-French Parliamentary Group
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5

    To Enhance Friendship Between the Peoples of the Soviet Union and France
    The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Establishes Soviet-French Parliamentary Group
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: TASS reported from Moscow: A group of representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union held a meeting in Moscow on the 8th and decided to establish a Soviet-French parliamentary group under the Soviet parliamentary group in order to expand parliamentary ties between representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and members of the French Parliament, which would be conducive to the development of relations between the two countries.
    The meeting elected Kairov, President of the Academy of Educational Sciences of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, as Chairman of the Soviet-French Parliamentary Group.
    The meeting expressed satisfaction with the establishment of the French-Soviet Parliamentary Group under the National Assembly and the Senate of the Republic of France, and expressed the hope of meeting with representatives of the French-Soviet Parliamentary Group in Moscow or Paris this year in order to understand each other's parliamentary activities and enhance friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

### 增进苏法两国人民间的友谊  苏联最高苏维埃成立苏法议员团

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    增进苏法两国人民间的友谊
    苏联最高苏维埃成立苏法议员团
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃的一批代表8日在莫斯科举行会议，决定在苏联议员团下成立苏法议员团，以扩大苏联最高苏维埃代表和法国议会议员之间有利于发展两国关系的议会联系。
    会议选举俄罗斯联邦共和国教育科学院院长凯罗夫为苏法议员团主席。
    会议对法国议会在国民会议和共和国参议院之下设立法苏议员团表示满意，并且表示希望今年在莫斯科或巴黎同法苏议员团的代表会见，以便彼此了解两国议会的活动以及增进两国人民之间的友谊。
