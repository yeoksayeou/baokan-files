Title: Prime Minister Acharya Visits Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Nepal's Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya and his wife, along with members of his delegation to China, flew from Beijing to Northeast China this morning to visit various locations. They were accompanied by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, as well as Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 3rd. The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Acharya, arrived in Shenyang this morning on a special plane, accompanied by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, as well as Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

As soon as the special plane carrying Prime Minister Acharya, his wife, and accompanying officials landed, the thousands of people welcoming them at the airport immediately erupted in warm applause and cheers. Then, Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, and his wife, Zhang Like, Vice Mayor of Shenyang, and Zhao Chengjin, Deputy Commander of the Liaoning Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, stepped forward to greet them. Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

The distinguished guests shook hands with the heads of various agencies, democratic parties, and people's organizations of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City who had come to welcome them. Du Zheheng also introduced the Consuls General of the Soviet Union and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea stationed in Shenyang, who had come to greet them, to the distinguished guests.

Prime Minister Acharya delivered a short speech at the airport, thanking the people of Shenyang for their warm welcome.

The distinguished guests then toured under the accompaniment of Governor Du Zheheng and Vice Mayor Zhang Like. At the No. 1 Machine Tool Plant in Shenyang, the distinguished guests watched the operation of a linked boring machine in the 1-A62 workshop and carefully examined the finished products produced in the workshop. Prime Minister Acharya later visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall.

In the afternoon, Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, and Zhang Panxin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, accompanied the distinguished guests to Anshan to visit factories and parks, returning to Shenyang in the evening.

In the evening, Du Zheheng hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests. After the banquet, the distinguished guests watched an acrobatic performance.

### 阿查里雅首相去东北参观

1956-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相去东北参观
    新华社3日讯　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人以及随同来华访问的人员今天早晨从北京乘飞机前往东北各地参观。陪同前往的有中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
    到机场送行的，有外交部副部长章汉夫和礼宾司副司长王雨田。
    新华社沈阳3日电　尼泊尔王国阿查里雅首相今天上午在我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，外交部礼宾司副司长马振武等陪同下乘专机到达沈阳。
    阿查里雅首相和夫人及随行官员们乘坐的专机刚刚着陆，在机场上数千人的欢迎人群立即发出热烈的掌声和欢呼声。接着，辽宁省省长杜者蘅和夫人、沈阳市副市长张力克、中国人民解放军辽宁军区副司令员赵承金少将等上前迎接。少先队员向贵宾们敬献了鲜花。
    贵宾们与前来欢迎的辽宁省、沈阳市各机关各民主党派各人民团体负责人一一握手。杜者蘅又把到机场迎接的苏联、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻沈阳总领事介绍给贵宾们。
    阿查里雅首相在机场上发表了简短的讲话，感谢沈阳市人民对他的热诚欢迎。
    贵宾们随即在省长杜者蘅、副市长张力克陪同下进行参观。贵宾们在沈阳第一机床厂一A六二车间观看了联动镗床的操作表演，并仔细地看了这个车间生产的成品。阿查里雅首相后来又参观了东北工业陈列馆。
    下午，辽宁省省长杜者蘅和外事处处长张盘新又陪同贵宾们到鞍山参观了工厂，游览了公园，当晚回到沈阳。
    晚间，杜者蘅设宴招待了贵宾。宴会后，贵宾们观看了杂技表演。
