Title: President Sukarno's Speech
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 2

My brother Zhou Enlai, honorable guests, ladies and gentlemen, brothers and sisters, comrades:

    I thank you, my brother, for your praise, and I thank you for inviting me to visit your beautiful country, your strong country. You also overpraise me. I am just a citizen; I am the President of the Republic of Indonesia because of my people. Without my people, I would just be an ordinary person. Without my people, I am completely insignificant. But together with my people, I hope to see Indonesia attain complete freedom. Together with my people and the people of all nations, I hope to see the Chinese people attain complete freedom.

    I have been in Beijing for five days. Before I set out for Beijing, people told me that in Beijing I would see beautiful and grand buildings, I would see Tiananmen, I would see the beautiful Forbidden City, I would see the beautiful Summer Palace. Yes, I have seen these things, my brother Zhou Enlai.

    But in Beijing, I have also seen a nation and a spirit. For me, the real Beijing is not in the beautiful architecture of Tiananmen, not in the beautiful palaces, not in the beautiful Summer Palace. The real Beijing, the real China, is in your bustling streets, in the eyes of the people, in the smiles of your children, in the spirit of the people.

    The nation I have seen is a friendly, kind, hardworking, freedom-loving, and united nation. Such a nation will never perish. Such a nation will endure forever.

    The Indonesian people are proud to have the people of such a country as their friends. The Indonesian people are proud to have the people of such a country as their comrades, as their fellow fighters.

    The friendship between us is the friendship between two fellow fighters. I hope that this friendship will continue forever.

    Honorable guests, ladies and gentlemen, brothers and sisters:

    Now I would like to ask you to stand up with me and

    To the prosperity of the Chinese people,

    To the prosperity of the Indonesian people,

    To the friendship between the Chinese and Indonesian people,

    To world peace and prosperity,

    To the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, cheers!

### 苏加诺总统的讲话

1956-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统的讲话我的兄弟周恩来，诸位阁下，女士们和先生们，兄弟姊妹们，同志们：
    我感谢你，我的兄弟，对我的称赞，我感谢你们邀请我来访问你们的美丽的国家，强盛的国家。你对我也是过奖了。我只是一个国民，我是由于我的人民才当了印度尼西亚共和国的总统。没有我的人民。我只不过是一个普通人罢了。没有我的人民，我是完全不足道的。但是同我的人民一起，我希望看到印度尼西亚得到完全的自由。同我的人民和各国人民一起，我希望看到中国人民得到完全的自由。
    我到北京已经五天了。在我动身来北京以前，人们对我说，你在北京将会见到美丽、伟大的建筑物，你将会见到天安门，你将会见到美丽的故宫，你将会见到美丽的颐和园。是的，我见到了这些东西，我的兄弟周恩来。
    但是我在北京也看到了一个民族和一种精神。对我来说，真正的北京不是在美丽的天安门的建筑中，不是在美丽的宫殿中，不是在美丽的颐和园。真正的北京、真正的中国是在你们的熙熙攘攘的街道上，在人们的眼睛里，在你们儿童的微笑中，在人民的精神中。
    我所见到的民族是一个友好、和善、勤劳、爱好自由和团结一致的民族。这样一个民族永远不会灭亡。这样一个民族将永世长存。
    印度尼西亚人民因为有这样一个国家的人民做它的朋友而感到骄傲。印度尼西亚人民因为有这样一个国家的人民做它的同志，做它的战友而感到骄傲。
    我们之间的友谊是两个战友之间的友谊。我希望这种友谊将能永久继续下去。
    诸位阁下，女士们，先生们，兄弟们，姊妹们：
    现在我想请你们站起来同我一起
    为中国人民的繁荣，
    为印度尼西亚人民的繁荣，
    为中国人民和印度尼西亚人民之间的友谊，
    为世界和平和繁荣，
    为毛泽东主席的健康，干杯。
