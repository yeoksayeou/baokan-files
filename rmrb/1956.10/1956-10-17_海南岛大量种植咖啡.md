Title: Hainan Island Plants Coffee on a Large Scale
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Haikou, 15th. Hainan Island is planting coffee on a large scale. State-owned tropical economic crop farms have already planted more than 3,000 mu of coffee this year, and are also cultivating more than 2 million coffee seedlings for development next year.

Hainan Island has more than 50 years of coffee planting history. This year, the island's coffee planting area will be more than six times larger than it was at the time of liberation in 1950.

### 海南岛大量种植咖啡

1956-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

    海南岛大量种植咖啡
    新华社海口15日电　海南岛大量种植咖啡。国营热带经济作物农场今年已经种植的咖啡有三千多亩，同时还为明年发展咖啡育苗二百多万株。
    海南岛有五十多年种植咖啡的历史。今年岛上的咖啡种植面积将比1950年解放时扩大六倍以上。
