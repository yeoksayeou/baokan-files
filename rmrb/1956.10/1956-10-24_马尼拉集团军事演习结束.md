Title: Manila Group Military Exercise Ends
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6

    Manila Group Military Exercise Ends
    Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 23rd: The "Albatross" military exercise held by member states of the Manila Treaty Organization in the Java Sea and the South China Sea ended on October 21st.
    The exercise began on September 23rd in the Java Sea and went through three stages. Participating in the exercise were aircraft carriers, destroyers, transport ships, patrol ships, and tactical air force.
    According to United Press International, the final stage of the exercise, which took place in the South China Sea from October 17th, focused on coordinated naval and air force operations. The main core of the "combat force" was the American aircraft carrier, submarines, and carrier-based aircraft.
    Associated Press said that this exercise is the first in a series of tests of the "combat capabilities" and "cooperation capabilities" of the various military units of the Manila Treaty Organization.
    France, one of the member states of the Manila Treaty Organization, did not send warships to participate in this exercise.

### 马尼拉集团军事演习结束

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    马尼拉集团军事演习结束
    新华社23日讯　马尼拉消息：马尼拉条约组织成员国在爪哇海和南海一带举行的“信天翁”军事演习已经在10月21日结束。
    这次演习从9月23日起在爪哇海开始，前后经过了三个阶段。参加演习的有航空母舰、驱击舰、运输舰、警备舰以及战术空军。
    据合众社报道，从10月17日起在南中国海所进行的演习的最后阶段着重在海军和空军的协同动作方面。“作战力量”的主要核心是美国的航空母舰，潜水艇和舰载机。
    美联社说，这次演习是对马尼拉条约组织各军事单位的“作战能力”和“合作能力”的一系列考验的第一次。
    马尼拉条约组织成员国之一的法国，没有派军舰参加这次演习。
