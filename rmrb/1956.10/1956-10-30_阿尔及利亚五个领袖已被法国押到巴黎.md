Title: Five Algerian Leaders Have Been Escorted to Paris by France
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, October 29th: News from Paris: The five leaders of the Algerian National Liberation Front, who were kidnapped by France, were escorted from Algiers to Paris by the French government by plane on October 28th.

　　Upon arrival in Paris, they were immediately imprisoned in Santé Prison.

### 阿尔及利亚五个领袖已被法国押到巴黎

1956-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚五个领袖已被法国押到巴黎
    新华社29日讯　巴黎消息：被法国绑架的阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的五个领导人已经在10月28日被法国政府用飞机从阿尔及尔押解到巴黎。
    他们到达巴黎后，马上被关进桑台监狱。
