Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Burmese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 29th — The All-China Democratic Women's Federation hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Burmese Women's Delegation.

    The banquet was held in a bright hall. Laughter of friendship filled the entire hall. The hosts and guests chatted cordially.

    Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to the Burmese sisters, headed by Delegation Leader Daw Sein Pu.

    Daw Sein Pu said that she would never forget the warm hospitality of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and that she would bring the precious friendship of the Chinese sisters back to Burma to convey it to the Burmese women.

    Women leaders from all walks of life in Beijing also attended the banquet this evening.

    After the banquet, the hosts and guests sang and danced together with great enthusiasm, and took group photos. At the time of parting, they said "Goodbye!" and "Kyay zu tin ba de" (meaning friendship) with reluctance!

### 全国妇联欢宴缅甸妇女代表团

1956-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联欢宴缅甸妇女代表团
    新华社29日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今晚设宴欢迎缅甸妇女代表团。
    宴会是在一个明亮的大厅里举行的。友谊的欢笑声充满了整个大厅。主人和客人们亲切地交谈着。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全对以杜盛布团长为首的缅甸姐妹们来中国访问表示热烈欢迎。
    杜盛布说，中华全国民主妇女联合会对我们的盛情招待是永远忘不了的，她要把中国姐妹们的珍贵的友情带回去转达给缅甸妇女们。
    北京市各界妇女负责人也出席了今晚的宴会。
    宴会结束后，主人和客人们兴致勃勃地在一起唱歌、跳舞，并且合影留念。临别时。彼此恋恋不舍地说着“再见”！“漆及也”（友谊的意思）！
