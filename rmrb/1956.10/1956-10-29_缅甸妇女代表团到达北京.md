Title: Burmese Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th -- A seven-member Burmese women's delegation arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
    After getting off the plane, the head of the Burmese Women's Delegation, Du Shengbu, and others warmly shook hands with Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Liu Wang Liming, President of the National Association of Chinese Women's Temperance Society, and Wang Chunping, Deputy Director of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, who came to welcome them.
    Delegation leader Du Shengbu expressed her gratitude for the invitation and warm welcome from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. She said that the leaders of China and Burma have paved the way for our friendship, and she hopes that we will do our work well, standing at our common posts.
    Personnel from the Burmese Embassy in China also came to the airport to welcome them.

### 缅甸妇女代表团到达北京

1956-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸妇女代表团到达北京
    新华社28日讯　缅甸妇女代表团一行七人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。她们是应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来中国访问的。
    缅甸妇女代表团团长杜盛布等下飞机后，同前来欢迎的中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君，中华妇女节制会全国总会会长刘王立明和北京市民主妇女联合会副主任王春平等热烈握手。
    杜盛布团长对中华全国民主妇女联合会的邀请和热烈欢迎，表示感谢。她说，中缅两国的领导人已为我们铺好友谊的道路，希望我们站在共同的工作岗位上作好我们的工作。
    缅甸驻华大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
