	On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to express to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency, to the people of the People's Republic of China, the most sincere congratulations and best wishes of the people of the Republic of Indonesia and myself.

Nasser, President of Egypt, Sends Congratulatory Telegram
To His Excellency Mao Zedong, President of the People's Republic of China:
	On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I express, with particular pleasure, in my own name and on behalf of the Egyptian government and people, my most heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency, and my sincere wishes for your health and happiness, and for the glory and prosperity of the noble Chinese people.

Polish National Leaders Send Congratulatory Telegram
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing
To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Beijing
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
	On the occasion of the 7th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, the Government of the Polish People's Republic, and the entire Polish people, extend to you, dear comrades, and to the great Chinese people, our most sincere fraternal congratulations.
	The great achievements of the People's Republic of China in strengthening the power of the people's state, in the cause of socialist transformation of the country, and in continuously improving the material and cultural living standards of the residents fill the Polish people and all friends of China with joy and admiration. The peaceful foreign policy of the People's Republic of China and the close friendship between the Chinese people and the people of the countries of the socialist camp undoubtedly benefit peace in Asia and the world.
	The Polish people strive to continuously strengthen their fraternal cooperation with the Chinese people in political, economic, and cultural fields. The Polish people firmly support the restoration of the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations, which rightfully belong to it, and support the just demands of the Chinese people to return those lands that have been illegally taken from the motherland.
	On this joyous occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, we warmly wish the fraternal Chinese people further victories in the struggle to achieve the consolidation and comprehensive prosperity of their great motherland.
        Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman, State Council of the Polish People's Republic
        Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman, Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic
        Adam Rapacki, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish People's Republic
                                    Warsaw, September 29, 1956

Czechoslovak National Leaders Send Congratulatory Telegram
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing
To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Beijing
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing: Dear Comrades:
	On the great festival of the Chinese people—the 7th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please allow us to express our most sincere congratulations on behalf of the people of all nationalities of Czechoslovakia, and in our own names. The Czechoslovak people follow with great joy the achievements of the Chinese people in the economic and cultural development of their country, and, together with the progressive forces of the world, feel heartfelt joy at the great results achieved by the policies of the Chinese Party and government, which were once again confirmed at the recently concluded Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. This historic congress has put forward new tasks for the victorious construction of socialism in the People's Republic of China. There is no doubt that the results of this congress will be a powerful factor in enabling China to rapidly become an industrially developed socialist country. The Czechoslovak people and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic highly appreciate the great efforts made by the People's Republic of China in resolving various international issues and in developing friendly relations with all countries based on the principles of peaceful coexistence. This unwavering peaceful policy has won the Chinese people ever greater prestige and respect throughout the world. The Czechoslovak people and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic will continue to resolutely support the just demands of the Chinese people and their government to realize the recognition of the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations.
	Dear Comrades, we wish you, and through you, the Chinese people, further achievements in building socialism in your country and in pursuing a peaceful foreign policy.
                Antonin Zapotocky, President of the Czechoslovak Republic
              Viliam Široký, Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic
              Zdeněk Fierlinger, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic
                                  Prague, September 29, 1956

### 庆贺中华人民共和国成立七周年

1956-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    庆贺中华人民共和国成立七周年
    印度尼西亚副总统哈达的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆佳节，我非常愉快地向阁下，并通过阁下，向中华人民共和国人民，表达印度尼西亚共和国人民和我本人的最恳切的庆贺和最良好的愿望。
                            印度尼西亚共和国副总统　哈达
                                        10月1日于雅加达
        埃及总统纳赛尔的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆之际，我怀着特别愉快的心情以我个人的名义并代表埃及政府和埃及人民，向阁下表达最衷心的祝贺，并为阁下的健康和幸福以及高贵的中国人民的光荣和繁荣表示真挚的愿望。
                                加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
                                          1956年9月30日
        波兰国家领导人的贺电北京中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    当中华人民共和国成立七周年纪念之际，我们谨代表波兰人民共和国国务委员会、波兰人民共和国政府和全体波兰人民向你们，敬爱的同志们，并向伟大的中国人民致最衷心的兄弟的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国在加强人民国家的威力、在国家的社会主义改造事业和不断提高居民的物质和文化生活水平方面取得的伟大成就使波兰人民和中国的所有的朋友们感到欣喜和钦佩。中华人民共和国的和平外交政策以及中国人民同社会主义阵营各国人民间的亲密无间的友谊毫无疑义是有利于亚洲和世界和平的。
    波兰人民力求不断加强他们同中国人民之间在政治、经济和文化等方面所进行的兄弟合作。波兰人民坚决支持中华人民共和国恢复理应属于她的在联合国的合法权利，并支持中国人民力求使那些被非法夺走的土地归还祖国的正义要求。
    在中华人民共和国国庆的愉快节日里，我们热烈地预祝兄弟的中国人民在争取自己伟大的祖国的巩固和全面繁荣的斗争中取得进一步的胜利。
        波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席亚历山大·萨瓦茨基
        波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹
        波兰人民共和国外交部长阿达姆·拉帕茨基
                                    1956年9月29日于华沙
      捷克斯洛伐克国家领导人的贺电北京中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志，中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：尊敬的同志们：
    在中国人民的伟大节日——中华人民共和国建国七周年的时候，请允许我们代表捷克斯洛伐克各族人民，并且用我们自己的名义，向你们表示最衷心的祝贺。捷克斯洛伐克人民以万分欢欣的心情关注着中国人民在本国的经济和文化发展中所获得的成就，并且同全世界的进步力量一道对中国党和政府的政策所取得的，而且在日前结束的中国共产党第八次全国代表大会上又一次得到证实的那些巨大成果，感到由衷的喜悦。这次具有历史意义的代表大会为中华人民共和国胜利地建设社会主义的道路，提出了新的任务，毫无疑问，这次代表大会的成果将是一个有力的因素，能使中国迅速成为一个工业发达的社会主义的国家。捷克斯洛伐克人民和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府对中华人民共和国在解决各项国际问题和根据和平共处各项原则发展同各国之间的友好关系方面所作的巨大努力，给予很高的评价。这个始终不渝的和平政策使中国人民在全世界博得越来越大的威望和尊敬。捷克斯洛伐克人民和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府今后仍将继续坚决支持中国人民及其政府实现其关于承认中华人民共和国在联合国里的合法权利的正义要求。
    亲爱的同志们，我们预祝你们，并且通过你们祝中国人民在本国建设社会主义、以及在实现和平外交政策方面获得进一步的成就。
                捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基
              捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威廉·西罗基
              捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席兹·费林格
