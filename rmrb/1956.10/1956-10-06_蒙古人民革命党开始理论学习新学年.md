Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Begins New Academic Year of Theoretical Study
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 4th - The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party's theoretical study year for 1956-1957 began on October 1st.

    According to the decision of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Party Central Committee, the study tasks for the new academic year are: to study philosophy, political economics, and the history of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party in a more systematic manner.

    The Party Central Committee's newspaper "Unen" published an editorial on the 2nd, calling on party members to strengthen their studies in order to master Marxist-Leninist theory. The editorial criticized dogmatic study methods and demanded that the study of Marxist-Leninist theory be combined with practical work and practical life.

### 蒙古人民革命党开始理论学习新学年

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党开始理论学习新学年
    新华社乌兰巴托4日电　蒙古人民革命党组织的1956—1957年的理论学习年度，在10月1日开始。
    根据党的四中全会的决定，新学年的学习任务是：比较系统的学习哲学、政治经济学和蒙古人民革命党党史。
    党中央机关报“真理报”2日发表社论，号召党员要为掌握马克思列宁主义的理论而加强学习。社论批评了教条主义的学习方法，要求在学习马克思列宁主义理论时，和实际工作、实际生活结合起来。
