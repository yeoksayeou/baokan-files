Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Indonesian Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The All-China Journalists Association President Deng Tuo held a reception this afternoon to welcome Indonesian journalists who were visiting China as part of President Sukarno's delegation.

   When Deng Tuo gave a speech at the reception, he warmly welcomed the arrival of the Indonesian journalists. He said that with President Sukarno's visit to China, the friendship between our two countries' journalists has further developed. In recent days, the Chinese press has been moved by President Sukarno's very vivid and cordial speeches. In particular, President Sukarno's words about fighting for the ideals of independence, the ideals of human happiness, the ideals of a new world, and the ideals of world peace have left a deep impression on the Chinese press. Deng Tuo said, may the press of our two countries work together for the ideals that President Sukarno spoke of.
   Sukrisno of "Antara News Agency" spoke on behalf of the visiting Indonesian journalists. He expressed his gratitude to the Chinese press for its support of the Indonesian people in their struggle for independence. He said that our two countries share the common ideals of opposing colonialism and striving for world peace. He hoped that the press of both countries would work together for these ideals.
   At the reception, Darmosugondo of the Indonesian Broadcasting Corporation also asked Deng Tuo to make a recorded speech.
   At today's reception, Indonesian journalists extensively met with members of the Chinese press. Among them were Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association Wu Lengxi, Wang Yunsheng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gong Peng, and heads of newspapers, periodicals, and radio stations in Beijing.

### 我新闻工作者联谊会  招待印度尼西亚记者

1956-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者联谊会
    招待印度尼西亚记者
    新华社4日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓今天下午举行酒会，招待随同苏加诺总统来中国访问的印度尼西亚新闻记者。
    邓拓在酒会上致词的时候，对印度尼西亚记者的到来表示热烈欢迎。他说，随着苏加诺总统的来中国访问，我们两国新闻工作者的友谊更加发展了。这几天来，中国新闻界都为苏加诺总统的非常生动和亲切的演说所感动。特别是苏加诺总统的为独立的理想、人类幸福的理想、新世界的理想和世界和平的理想而斗争的话，对中国新闻界留下了深刻的印象。邓拓说，愿我们两国新闻界为苏加诺总统所说的这种理想而共同努力。
       “安塔拉通讯社”的苏克里斯诺代表来访的印度尼西亚记者讲话。他对中国新闻界对印度尼西亚人民在争取独立的斗争的支持表示感谢。他说，我们两国都有反对殖民主义和争取世界和平的共同理想。他希望两国新闻界都为这种理想共同努力。
    在酒会上，印度尼西亚广播事业局的达尔莫苏贡多还请邓拓作了录音讲话。
    在今天的酒会上，印度尼西亚记者们同中国新闻界广泛地见了面。其中有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长吴冷西、王芸生，外交部新闻司司长龚澎和北京各报刊、广播电台的负责人。
