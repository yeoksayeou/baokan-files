Title: Arab League Political Committee Passes Resolution Demanding a Halt to the Algerian War
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd, Cairo Dispatch: The Arab League Political Committee, currently meeting in Cairo, passed a resolution on October 21st demanding that all peace-loving countries take action within the framework of the United Nations to halt the “destructive war” being waged in Algeria.

The Chairman of the Political Committee, Jordanian Ambassador to Egypt, Fazi Mulki, said that the resolution reaffirms the Arab people's support for the Algerian nationalist struggle.

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st, TASS Cairo Dispatch, October 21st: According to Cairo Radio, the Arab League Political Committee held a meeting in Cairo on October 20th.

The meeting adopted a resolution on establishing diplomatic relations between Arab countries and Burma. Currently, only Egypt and Iraq have diplomatic representation in Burma among Arab countries. On the issue of relations between Arab countries and Scandinavian countries, the Political Committee recommended increasing diplomatic representation between Arab and Scandinavian countries.

The meeting decided to provide necessary assistance to Arab refugees in Cyprus.

Attendees decided to have the Arab countries' representatives to the United Nations raise the issue of the persecution of Arab minorities in Israel at the United Nations General Assembly.

### 阿拉伯联盟政委会通过决议  要求制止阿尔及利亚战争

1956-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟政委会通过决议
    要求制止阿尔及利亚战争
    新华社22日讯　开罗消息：正在开罗开会的阿拉伯联盟政治委员会在10月21日通过了一项决议，要求一切爱好和平的国家在联合国的范围内行动起来制止在阿尔及利亚进行的“毁灭性的战争”。
    政治委员会的主席、约旦驻埃及大使法齐·穆尔基说，这个决议重申了阿拉伯人民支持阿尔及利亚的民族主义的斗争。
    新华社21日讯　塔斯社开罗21日讯：据开罗电台广播，阿拉伯联盟政治委员会10月20日在开罗举行会议。
    会议通过了一项关于在阿拉伯国家和缅甸之间建立外交关系的决议。目前在阿拉伯国家中只有埃及和伊拉克在缅甸有外交代表机构。在阿拉伯国家同斯堪的纳维亚国家的关系问题上，政治委员会建议增设阿拉伯和斯堪的纳维亚国家之间的外交代表机构。
    会议决定给予在塞浦路斯的阿拉伯难民以必要的帮助。
    到会者决定，让阿拉伯国家驻联合国代表向联合国大会提出关于少数阿拉伯人在以色列遭到迫害的问题。
