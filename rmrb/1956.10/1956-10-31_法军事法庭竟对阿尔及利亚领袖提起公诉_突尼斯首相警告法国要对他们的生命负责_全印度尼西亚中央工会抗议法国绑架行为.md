Title: French Military Tribunal Indicts Algerian Leaders; Tunisian Prime Minister Warns France to Take Responsibility for Their Lives; All-Indonesia Central Labor Union Protests French Kidnapping

Author:

Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th, Paris: The French military tribunal's investigating judge on the 29th formally charged the five Algerian national leaders who were abducted to Paris, accusing them of the so-called "engaging in depressing the morale of the French army and undermining popular sentiment." According to Article 76 of the French Penal Code, this charge can be punished by death.

The date for the formal trial has not yet been determined.

On the same day, Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba warned the French government that Tunisia bears responsibility for the lives of the five Algerian national leaders.

Bourguiba published an article in "Action" newspaper saying, "If any of them die, Tunisia will break off relations with France forever."

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 30th: According to a report by Antara News Agency today, the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, the largest labor union organization in Indonesia, has protested to the French government regarding the French kidnapping of five Algerian national leaders.

The National Committee of this labor union, on behalf of its 2.3 million members, sent a telegram to the French government demanding the immediate release of the five Algerian leaders.

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th, Tripoli: The "Tarabulus al-Gharb" newspaper, in an editorial published on the 29th, demanded that the United Nations Security Council discuss the Algerian question.

The newspaper also called on all Arab countries to strengthen the struggle against the French colonialists' repressive actions in Algeria.

### 法军事法庭竟对阿尔及利亚领袖提起公诉  突尼斯首相警告法国要对他们的生命负责  全印度尼西亚中央工会抗议法国绑架行为

1956-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    法军事法庭竟对阿尔及利亚领袖提起公诉
    突尼斯首相警告法国要对他们的生命负责
    全印度尼西亚中央工会抗议法国绑架行为
    新华社30日讯　巴黎消息：法国军事法庭预审推事29日对被劫持到巴黎的五个阿尔及利亚民族领袖提起公诉，加给他们的罪名是所谓
  “从事沮丧法军士气和败坏民心”。根据法国刑法第七十六条，这个罪名可以被判处死刑。
    正式审讯的日期还没有确定。
    同一天，突尼斯首相布尔吉巴警告法国政府说，突尼斯对五个阿尔及利亚民族领袖的生命负有责任。
    布尔吉巴在“行动报”上发表文章说：“如果他们当中有谁死了，突尼斯就要永远同法国绝交。”
    据新华社雅加达30日电　据安塔拉通讯社今天报道，印度尼西亚最大的工会组织全印度尼西亚中央工会就法国绑架五个阿尔及利亚民族领袖事件向法国政府提出了抗议。
    这个工会的全国委员会代表自己的二百三十万会员打电报给法国政府，要求立即释放阿尔及利亚的五个领袖。
    据新华社30日讯　的黎波里消息：“塔拉勃卢加布报”29日在它发表的一篇社论中，要求联合国安全理事会讨论阿尔及利亚问题。
    这家报纸还号召所有阿拉伯国家加强反对法国殖民者在阿尔及利亚的镇压行为的斗争。
