Title: Our Art Troupe Visits and Performs in New Zealand
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Christchurch, 10th. News: The Chinese Art Troupe, which is visiting and performing in New Zealand and Australia, arrived in Christchurch on the east coast of New Zealand on October 9th and will hold a performance here on October 11th.

When the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Auckland, a large city in northern New Zealand, on October 3rd, it was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life. Auckland Mayor Luxford held a reception on October 4th to welcome the Chinese Art Troupe.

Before arriving in Christchurch, the art troupe also visited Hamilton and Rotorua.

### 我艺术团到新西兰进行访问和演出

1956-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    我艺术团到新西兰进行访问和演出
    新华社10日讯　新西兰基督堂市消息：到新西兰和澳大利亚进行访问演出的中国艺术团在10月9日到达了新西兰东海岸的基督堂市，将在10月11日在这里举行演出。
    中国艺术团在10月3日到达新西兰北部的大城市奥克兰的时候，受到当地各界人士的热烈欢迎。奥克兰市长路克斯福德在10月4日举行了招待会招待中国艺术团。
    艺术团在到达基督堂市以前还访问过哈密尔顿市和罗托鲁等地。
