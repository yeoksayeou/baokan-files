Title: Lebanese President Visits Iran
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, October 17th, TASS reported from Beirut on the 17th: The President of the Lebanese Republic, Camille Chamoun, flew to Tehran today for an official visit. It is reported that Chamoun will stay in Iran for seven days.

### 黎巴嫩总统访问伊朗

1956-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总统访问伊朗
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特17日讯：黎巴嫩共和国总统夏蒙今日乘飞机前往德黑兰作正式访问。据悉，夏蒙将在伊朗逗留七天。
