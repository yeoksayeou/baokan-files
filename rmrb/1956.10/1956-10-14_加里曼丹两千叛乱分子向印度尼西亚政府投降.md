Title: Two Thousand Kalimantan Rebels Surrender to Indonesian Government
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 12th – According to Antara News Agency from Balabai, East Kalimantan, the leader of the "League of Oppressed People," a rebel organization in South Kalimantan, and two thousand of his men formally surrendered to the Indonesian military authorities on October 10th.

The rebel leader, Haja, signed a guarantee in the presence of Lieutenant Colonel Basri, the local army commander, and Milono, the Governor of Kalimantan Province. In the guarantee, he stated that he and his men, responding to the government's call, unconditionally abandoned the fight.

### 加里曼丹两千叛乱分子向印度尼西亚政府投降

1956-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    加里曼丹两千叛乱分子向印度尼西亚政府投降
    据新华社雅加达12日电　据安塔拉通讯社东加里曼丹巴拉巴伊消消，南加里曼丹的叛乱分子组织——“被压迫人民联盟”的领袖和他的部下二千人在10月10日正式向印度尼西亚军事当局投降。
    叛乱分子领袖哈贾当着当地陆军司令巴斯里中校和加里曼丹省长米洛诺的面在保证书上签了字。他在保证书中说，他和他的部下响应政府的号召，无条件地放弃战斗。
