Title: Chairman U Nu Tours Kunming
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 23rd: Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, his wife, son, and the Burmese guests accompanying Chairman U Nu on his visit to our country, today, accompanied by Yunnan Province Vice Governor Liu Minghui and others, paid homage to the famous temples of Kunming and toured the scenic spots in the suburbs of Kunming.

    In the morning, Chairman U Nu and others braved the drizzle to climb the Western Hills and paid homage to Huating Temple, one of China's famous Buddhist temples.

    Following this, Chairman U Nu and others also paid respects at Taihua Temple. In the afternoon, Chairman U Nu and others paid homage to the statues of the Five Hundred Arhats at Qiongzhu Temple.

    This morning, Chairman U Nu and others also toured the hot springs and visited the sanatorium there.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 23rd: Yunnan Province Vice Governor Liu Minghui hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Chairman U Nu of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma. Chairman U Nu's wife, son, Judge U Min Din of the Supreme Court of Burma and his wife, as well as other accompanying personnel, attended the banquet.

### 吴努主席在昆明游览

1956-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努主席在昆明游览
    据新华社昆明23日电　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和他的夫人、公子及随吴努主席来我国访问的缅甸客人们，今天由云南省副省长刘明辉等陪同朝拜了昆明的著名寺院，游览了昆明市郊的风景区。
    上午，吴努主席等冒着细雨，登临西山，朝拜了我国著名佛寺之一的华亭寺。
    接着，吴努主席等又瞻仰了太华寺。下午，吴努主席等又在邛竹寺瞻仰了五百罗汉的塑像。
    今天早晨，吴努主席等还游览了温泉，访问了那里的疗养院。
    据新华社昆明23日电　云南省副省长刘明辉今晚设宴招待缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努。吴努主席的夫人、公子和缅甸联邦高级法院法官吴敏登和夫人以及其他随行人员都出席了宴会。
