Title: Nagy Appointed University Professor
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 19th. The Hungarian News Agency reported that the Hungarian Council of Ministers reappointed Imre Nagy as Professor of Agricultural Economics at the Karl Marx University of Economics in Budapest. Nagy had previously served as a professor at this university.

### 纳吉被任命为大学教授

1956-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    纳吉被任命为大学教授
    新华社布拉格19日电　据匈牙利通讯社报道，匈牙利部长会议重新任命伊姆雷·纳吉为布达佩斯卡尔·马克思经济大学农业经济学教授。纳吉以前曾经担任过这个大学的教授。
