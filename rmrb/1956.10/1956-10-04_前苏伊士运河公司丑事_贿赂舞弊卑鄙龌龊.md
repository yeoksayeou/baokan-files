Title: Former Suez Canal Company Scandal: Bribery, Fraud, Despicable and Filthy
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd dispatch from Cairo: The "Liberation News" of Egypt reported the despicable and filthy affairs of the former Suez Canal Company. It quoted the words of Ahmed Bakouri, the Egyptian Minister of Religious Foundations, saying that, according to the files of the former Suez Canal Company, this company spent five million Egyptian pounds every year to bribe government officials and the press.

The newspaper also stated that, according to the inspection of the former Suez Canal Company's files, 90% of the French warships passing through the Suez Canal between 1950 and 1954 did not pay fees.

### 前苏伊士运河公司丑事  贿赂舞弊卑鄙龌龊

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    前苏伊士运河公司丑事
    贿赂舞弊卑鄙龌龊
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社开罗2日讯：埃及“解放报”报道了前苏伊士运河公司的卑鄙龌龊的事情。它引用埃及宗教基金部长艾哈迈德·巴库里的谈话说，根据前苏伊士运河公司的档案文件证明，这家公司每年花费五百万埃镑用来贿赂政府官吏和新闻界。
    这家报纸又说，检查前苏伊士运河公司档案发现，在1950年到1954年间通过苏伊士运河的法国军舰有90％没有付费。
