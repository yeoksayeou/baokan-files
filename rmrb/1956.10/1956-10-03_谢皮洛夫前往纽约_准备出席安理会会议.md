Title: Shepilov Travels to New York to Attend Security Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-03
Page: 6

    Shepilov Travels to New York
    To Attend Security Council Meeting
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to TASS, Moscow: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov departed Moscow by plane on October 2nd for New York, to attend the Security Council meeting to discuss the Suez Canal issue.

### 谢皮洛夫前往纽约  准备出席安理会会议

1956-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫前往纽约
    准备出席安理会会议
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫在10月2日乘飞机离莫斯科前往纽约，准备参加安全理事会讨论苏伊士运河问题的会议。
