Title: Membership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Increases to Over 37,000
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 23rd (TASS): The Mongolian "Truth" newspaper reported that since the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the number of members and probationary members of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party has increased by 18%. At the time of the 12th Party Congress in November 1954, there were a total of 31,740 party members, and now the number of party members has increased to more than 37,450.

### 蒙古人民革命党党员增加到三万七千多人

1956-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党党员增加到三万七千多人
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古“真理报”报道，自从蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会召开以后，蒙古人民革命党党员和预备党员的数目已经增加了18％。1954年11月十二次党代表大会开会的时候，共有党员三万一千七百四十人，现在党员的数目已经增加到三万七千四百五十多人。
