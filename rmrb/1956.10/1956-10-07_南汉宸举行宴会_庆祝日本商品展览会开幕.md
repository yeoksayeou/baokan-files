Title: Nan Hanchen Holds Banquet to Celebrate Opening of Japanese Commodity Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to celebrate the opening of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition.

    In his speech, Nan Hanchen said that the successful opening of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition marks the development of trade between China and Japan year by year and progress step by step, which is in line with the common aspirations of the people of both countries. He said that the cooperation between China and Japan, through the joint efforts of the people of the two countries, cannot be stopped by any external oppression or artificial obstacles.

    President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and President of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition, Shozo Murata, said that the successful opening of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition is entirely the result of the strong assistance of the relevant Chinese authorities and organizations. He went on to say that before the opening ceremony today, Chairman Mao Zedong visited the exhibition, and Vice Premier Chen Yun and more than 5,000 Chinese and foreign people in Beijing attended the opening ceremony, which exceeded our expectations. He expressed his deep gratitude for this.

    Invited to attend the banquet were Masanosuke Ikeda, representative of the League of Diet Members for Promoting Japan-China Trade, Eiichi Shukutani, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Delegation and all members of the delegation, more than 200 Japanese people currently visiting China, and Togo Kameda, a Japanese person in Beijing.

    Mr. and Mrs. Boyd-Orr, Chairman of the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and representatives of the Korean International Trade Promotion Committee were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Among the more than 500 people accompanying the guests at today's banquet were Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen and Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang.

### 南汉宸举行宴会  庆祝日本商品展览会开幕

1956-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    南汉宸举行宴会
    庆祝日本商品展览会开幕
    据新华社6日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今天晚上在北京饭店举行宴会，庆祝日本商品展览会开幕。
    南汉宸在致词中说，日本商品展览会的胜利开幕，标志着中日两国间的贸易一年比一年发展，一次比一次进步，这是符合两国人民的共同愿望的。他说，中日两国之间的合作，经过两国人民的共同努力，是任何外来压迫、人为障碍阻挡不住的。
    日本国际贸易促进协会会长、日本商品展览会总裁村田省藏讲话说，日本商品展览会的胜利开幕，完全是中国各有关机关、团体大力协助的结果。他接着说，今天在举行开幕典礼以前，蒙毛泽东主席来参观，陈云副总理和北京的中外人士五千多人参加开幕典礼，这些都是超过我们意料之外的。对此，表示深切的谢意。
    应邀参加宴会的，有日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表池田正之辅，日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一和全体团员，正在访问中国的二百多位日本人士，以及在京的日本人士龟田东伍。
    英国国际贸易促进委员会主席波伊德—奥尔夫妇和朝鲜国际贸易促进委员会代表也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席今天宴会作陪的，有北京市市长彭真，对外贸易部部长叶季壮等五百多人。
