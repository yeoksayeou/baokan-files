Title: Striving for Unified Action of National Democratic Forces, National Independence, and World Peace: Dutch Communist Party Congress Passes Resolution and Program
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amsterdam, 8th (TASS dispatch) – The 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands concluded in Amsterdam on October 7th.

The congress passed a resolution and a political program, and elected a Central Committee of thirty-one members.

A spirit of great firmness and unity marked the entire course of the congress. The resolution unanimously adopted by the congress proves that the Dutch Communists are loyal to the immortal ideas of Marxism-Leninism. The resolution stipulates the path of striving for unified action of national democratic forces, for national independence, and for world peace.

The resolution analyzes the international situation and the domestic political situation. The congress pointed out the dangers posed to the Netherlands by the fact that Dutch foreign policy is subordinate to the United States, that the Netherlands participates in Western military blocs, and that West German militarism is being revived. The congress decided to "consistently implement the party's policy of easing international tensions, striving for peace, eliminating American military and economic domination, and restoring national independence."

In order to restore national independence, the Communist Party of the Netherlands put forward the following tasks: to strive to eliminate the unilateral nature of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (i.e., an organization mainly dominated by the United States) and to transform the North Atlantic Treaty Organization into a universal organization guaranteeing European security on the basis of the autonomous equality of all European countries; to strive to abolish all American military and economic privileges, especially the American air force base at Soesterberg; to strive to expand trade with Eastern European countries and China, and to expand economic ties with underdeveloped countries (including India and Indonesia); and to strengthen comprehensive contact with the peoples of socialist countries.

The congress then declared: "National independence can only be restored by establishing a domestic democratic system, a system that will be able to influence government policy and ultimately guide government policy."

In this regard, the congress called on Dutch Communists to strive for unified action by the working class, and to strive to absorb the broad masses of peasants, middle class, intellectuals, and all those who desire national independence to participate in this struggle. The congress resolution pointed out many specific ways that could promote unified action, and emphasized that the primary task is to unite workers, not separate them.

The congress finally formulated a government program, which the Communist Party of the Netherlands will strive to realize. The program stipulates: reducing military expenditures for the coming year; the Dutch government advocates banning atomic weapons and stopping their testing; resuming negotiations with the Republic of Indonesia on future economic relations and the status of West Irian; and in addition, it stipulates many social measures related to social security, housing construction, school construction, agriculture, etc.

### 为荷兰全国民主力量统一行动、为国家独立、为世界和平而奋斗  荷共代表大会通过决议和纲领

1956-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

    为荷兰全国民主力量统一行动、为国家独立、为世界和平而奋斗
　　荷共代表大会通过决议和纲领
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社阿姆斯特丹8日讯：荷兰共产党第十八次代表大会10月7日在阿姆斯特丹闭幕。
    大会通过了决议和政纲，并且选举了一个三十一人的中央委员会。
    一种非常坚定和一致的精神标志着大会的整个过程。代表大会所一致通过的决议证明荷兰共产党员是忠于不朽的马克思—列宁主义思想的。决议规定了为全国民主力量统一行动、为国家独立、为世界和平而奋斗的道路。
    决议中对国际形势及国内政治状况作了分析。代表大会指出荷兰外交政策的从属于美国、荷兰的参加西方军事集团以及西德军国主义化的这样一些事实对荷兰产生的危险。代表大会决定“始终如一地执行党的政策，即缓和国际紧张局势、争取和平、消灭美国在军事和经济上的统治以及恢复国家独立的政策”。
    为了恢复国家的独立，荷兰共产党提出了下列任务：努力消除北大西洋公约组织（即美国占主要统治地位的组织）的片面性质和把北大西洋公约组织改造成在所有欧洲国家自主和平等的基础上保障欧洲安全的普遍组织；努力取消美国在军事和经济方面的一切特权，特别是要取消美国在苏斯特堡的空军基地；努力扩大同东欧国家和中国贸易，扩大同不发达的国家（包括印度和印度尼西亚）的经济联系；加强同社会主义国家人民全面的接触。
    代表大会接着宣布：“只有建立国内的民主制度这种办法，才能恢复国家独立，这种制度将能够影响政府的政策，并且最终将能够指导政府的政策。”
    在这方面代表大会号召荷兰共产党员努力争取工人阶级的统一行动，努力争取吸收广大农民、中产阶级、知识分子和一切渴望国家独立的人参加这一斗争。代表大会决议指出许多能够促进统一行动的具体道路，并且强调指出，首要的任务是团结工人，而不是分离他们。
    代表大会最后拟定一项政府施政纲领，荷兰共产党将为实现这个纲领而奋斗。这个纲领规定：减少下一年度的军事支出；荷兰政府主张禁止原子武器和停止对它的试验；同印度尼西亚共和国重新举行关于今后的经济关系和西伊里安的地位的谈判；此外还规定了许多有关社会赡养、住房建设、学校建设、农业等方面的社会性的措施。
