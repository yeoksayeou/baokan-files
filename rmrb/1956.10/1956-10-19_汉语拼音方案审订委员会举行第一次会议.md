Title: The Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch—The Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee, established by the State Council, held its first meeting today.

    The meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo, Director of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee.

    The meeting determined the task of the Review Committee is to discuss and review the various suggestions on the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme put forward by the Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Committee of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee, and to organize symposiums to listen to the opinions of all parties. Then, a revised draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme will be decided and submitted to the State Council for approval and promulgation. After discussing and approving the work plan of the Review Committee at today's meeting, the committee listened to Wang Li, a member of the Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Committee, explain his preliminary opinions on revising the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme.

### 汉语拼音方案审订委员会举行第一次会议

1956-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    汉语拼音方案审订委员会举行第一次会议
    新华社17日讯　国务院决定设立的汉语拼音方案审订委员会在今天召开了第一次会议。
    会议由汉语拼音方案审订委员会主任郭沫若主持。
    会议确定审订委员会的任务是对中国文字改革委员会拼音方案委员会提出的有关汉语拼音方案的各种建议加以讨论审查，并组织座谈，听取各方人士意见，然后决定一个汉语拼音方案修正草案，提请国务院批准公布。今天会议在讨论通过了审订委员会的工作计划之后，听取了拼音方案委员会委员王力关于修正汉语拼音方案草案初步意见的说明。
