Title: Soviet "Pravda" Publishes Resolutions of the Polish Party's Eighth Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th. Today, "Pravda" published the main parts of the Polish United Workers' Party's Eighth Plenary Session's resolution regarding the party's current political and economic tasks on page five, occupying two-thirds of the page.

### 苏联“真理报”刊载波兰党的八中全会决议

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联“真理报”刊载波兰党的八中全会决议
    新华社莫斯科28日讯　“真理报”今天在第五版以三分之二的地位登载了波兰统一工人党八中全会关于党在目前的政治和经济任务的决议的主要部分。
