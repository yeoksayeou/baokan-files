Title: Hoping to Introduce Advanced Experiences in Commercial Work
Author: Shen Hongmei
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 8

Column: Reader, Author, Editor

    Hoping to Introduce Advanced Experiences in Commercial Work
    I often read criticisms of commercial work by consumers in the People's Daily, which is very helpful to us. But we also hope to read articles and news that introduce advanced experiences in commercial work and commend commercial workers.
    Zhejiang Provincial Cultural Goods Company, Shen Hongmei

### 希望介绍商业工作的先进经验
沈宏美
1956-10-26
第8版()
专栏：读者  作者  编者

    希望介绍商业工作的先进经验
    在人民日报上常读到消费者批评商业工作的意见，这对我们很有帮助。但是我们也很希望能够读到介绍商业工作的先进经验和表扬商业工作者的文章和新闻。
    浙江省文化用品公司　沈宏美
