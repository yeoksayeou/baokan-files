Title: Short Quotations
Author: Qu Qiubai
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 8

(1899-1935)
　　Only when "I" and "non-I" combine can there be something in common.

　　When advancing work and the brain and strength are stirring, expanding, and developing, people feel positive and happy - that is life; when tired, lazy, and slackening, people feel passive and resting - that is death.

　　The era also has its masters: for some people, this is the end of the century; for others, this may be the beginning of the century - the beginning of a golden age.

　　The light radiating from underground, although still very weak for the time being, its source is inexhaustible, and its future is to completely change the scene on the ground.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Compiled by Jiefu)

### 小语录
瞿秋白
1956-10-10
第8版()
专栏：

    小语录
    瞿秋白
    （1899——1935）
    “我”与“非我”相合，方有共同之处可言。
    进取工作，脑血筋力鼓动膨胀发展时，人觉积极的乐意，——是生活；疲惫怠荡弛缓时，人觉消极的休息，——是死灭。
    时代也是有主人的：对于有些人这是世纪末；对于另外一些人这也许是世纪初——黄金时代的开始呢。
  ＊地底下放射出来的光明，暂时虽然还很微弱，然而它的来源是没有穷尽的，它的将来是要完全改变地面上的景象的。
              （洁夫辑）
