Title: Iran to Join "Users' Association" But Undertake No Obligations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, October 1st: Iranian Foreign Minister Ardalan issued a statement to the press on September 30, announcing the Iranian government's decision to join the "Suez Canal Users' Association."

　　He said: Iran's participation in this association "does not entail any obligations, and we still oppose the use of force against Egypt, or even the implementation of economic sanctions."

　　Ardalan said in the statement that the Iranian government agrees with the view that the association aims to peacefully resolve the Suez Canal issue through the United Nations.

### 伊朗将参加“使用国协会”  但不承担任何义务

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    伊朗将参加“使用国协会”
    但不承担任何义务
  　新华社1日讯　德黑兰消息：伊朗外交大臣阿达兰9月30日向报界发表声明，宣布伊朗政府决定参加“苏伊士运河使用国协会”。　　
    他说：伊朗参加这个协会“并不承担任何义务，我们仍旧反对对埃及使用武力，或者那怕是实行经济制裁”。　　
    阿达兰在声明中说，伊朗政府同意这个看法：协会以通过联合国来和平解决苏伊士运河问题作为它的目的。
