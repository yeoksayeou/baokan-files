Title: Soong Ching-ling Hosts Prime Minister Suhrawardy for Lunch
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 27th. Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, invited Pakistan's Prime Minister Suhrawardy to lunch at her Shanghai residence today.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy's daughter, Mrs. Akhtar Suleiman, the wife of Pakistan's Minister of Foreign Affairs and Commonwealth Relations, Mr. Noon, and the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Mr. Ahmed, were also invited.

Also invited were Xu Jianguo, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and his wife, Ke Qingshi, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling had met Prime Minister Suhrawardy during her visit to Pakistan. Today, they were very happy to meet again in Shanghai. The Prime Minister presented Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling with a silver-framed photograph of himself, as well as exquisite sarongs, small handbags, and a silver cigarette case. Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling presented Prime Minister Suhrawardy with a complete set of Fujian lacquer tea sets and the finest varieties of Chinese tea. The tea set was green. Soong Ching-ling said to Suhrawardy: I think you will like its color. I know that green is known as the color of Pakistan. I hope you will use this tea set often and think of China.

Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling also asked Prime Minister Suhrawardy to hand over some brocade fabrics to the wife of Pakistani President Mirza, which were intended as gifts for the President's wife.

### 宋庆龄邀苏拉瓦底总理进午餐

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄邀苏拉瓦底总理进午餐
    新华社上海27日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，今天中午在上海寓所里邀请巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底共进午餐。
    苏拉瓦底总理的女儿阿赫塔·苏来曼夫人，巴基斯坦外交和联邦关系部长努恩的夫人，巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德也应邀出席。
    应邀出席的有上海市副市长许建国和夫人，政协上海市委员会主席柯庆施，中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长王倬如等人。
    宋庆龄副委员长访问巴基斯坦的时候，曾和苏拉瓦底总理见过面。今天他们很高兴地在上海又一次会见。总理把他本人的一张镶着银质相架的照片以及精美的沙笼、小手袋和银制烟匣，送给宋庆龄副委员长。宋庆龄副委员长赠送给苏拉瓦底总理的是一整套福建漆制的茶具和中国出产的最佳品种的茶叶。茶具是绿色的。宋庆龄对苏拉瓦底说：我想你会喜欢它的颜色。我知道，绿色被称为巴基斯坦的颜色。希望你经常使用这套茶具，想到中国。
    宋庆龄副委员长还把送给巴基斯坦总统米尔扎的夫人的一些织锦缎衣料请苏拉瓦底总理转交给总统夫人。
