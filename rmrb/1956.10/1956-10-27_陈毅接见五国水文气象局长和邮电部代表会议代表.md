Title: Chen Yi Receives the Heads of Five National Hydrological and Meteorological Bureaus and Representatives to the Postal and Telecommunications Conference

Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th – Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received this morning all members of the delegations from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union who are participating in the meeting of the heads of the five national hydrological and meteorological bureaus and representatives to the Postal and Telecommunications Conference.

Present at the reception were Tu Changwang, Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau, Wang Zigang, Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and several Soviet advisors working at the Central Meteorological Bureau and the Central Meteorological Research Institute.

### 陈毅接见五国水文气象局长和邮电部代表会议代表

1956-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见五国水文气象局长和邮电部代表会议代表
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今日上午接见了参加五国水文气象局局长和邮电部代表会议的越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古、苏联等国的代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有中央气象局长涂长望和邮电部副部长王子纲以及在中央气象局和中央气象研究所工作的苏联顾问等多人。
