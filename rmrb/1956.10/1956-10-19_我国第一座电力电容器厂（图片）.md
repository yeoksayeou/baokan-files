Title: China's First Power Capacitor Factory (Photo)
Author: Yao Zongyi
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 2

    China's First Power Capacitor Factory
    China's first power capacitor factory, designed according to the latest international technical standards, is under intense construction in Xi'an. Once completed, this factory will produce various capacitors that reduce power loss during transmission line transport and improve power utilization.
    Top picture: High-altitude work during the hoisting of the factory building.
    Left picture: The installation of the factory's steel roof trusses. Due to the adoption of the comprehensive hoisting method, it takes one-third less time than ordinary installation.
            Xinhua News Agency reporter Yao Zongyi

### 我国第一座电力电容器厂（图片）
姚宗仪
1956-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

    我国第一座电力电容器厂
    我国按照国际最新技术标准设计的第一座电力电容器厂正在西安紧张施工。这座工厂建成后，将生产各种减少输电线路途中的电力损耗和提高电力利用率的电容器。
    上图：在厂房吊装中的高空作业。
    左图：工厂的钢屋架的安装，由于采取了综合吊装法，比一般的安装少用三分之一的时间。
            新华社记者　姚宗仪摄
