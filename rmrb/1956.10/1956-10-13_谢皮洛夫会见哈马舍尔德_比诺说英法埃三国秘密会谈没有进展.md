Title: Shepilov Meets Hammarskjöld; Pineau Says Secret Talks Between Britain, France, and Egypt Have Made No Progress
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 12th Dispatch from TASS: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov met with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the 11th and held a long conversation with him.

On the same day, Shepilov also attended a banquet hosted by French Foreign Minister Pineau.

On the 10th, Shepilov separately received Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi and Yugoslav Foreign Secretary Popović, and held long talks with them.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 12th Dispatch: The United Nations Security Council held a brief closed meeting on the afternoon of October 11. It was revealed that the meeting decided to postpone expressing an attitude toward the requests of Israel and seven Arab countries to participate in the meeting discussions. Based on France's suggestion, the Security Council will hold another closed meeting on the 12th to hear a report on the secret negotiations between the foreign ministers of Britain, France, and Egypt.

On the mornings and evenings of the 11th, the foreign ministers of Britain, France, and Egypt and UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld held two secret talks. French Foreign Minister Pineau stated after both talks that there had been no progress.

### 谢皮洛夫会见哈马舍尔德  比诺说英法埃三国秘密会谈没有进展

1956-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫会见哈马舍尔德
    比诺说英法埃三国秘密会谈没有进展
    新华社12日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫11日会见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，并且同他进行了长时间的会谈。
    同一天，谢皮洛夫还出席了法国外交部长比诺的宴会。
    10日，谢皮洛夫曾经分别接见了埃及外交部长法齐和南斯拉夫外交秘书长波波维奇，并且同他们进行了长时间的谈话。
    据新华社12日讯　纽约消息：联合国安全理事会10月11日下午举行了一次简短的秘密会议。据透露，会上决定延期对以色列和七个阿拉伯国家参加会议讨论的要求表示态度。根据法国的建议，安全理事会在12日还要举行秘密会议来听取关于英国、法国和埃及的外交部长秘密谈判情况的报告。
    11日上午和晚上，英、法、埃及三国的外交部长和联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德先后举行了两次秘密会谈。法国外交部长比诺在两次会谈后都表示会谈没有什么进展。
