Title: Indian Vice President Postpones Visit to China Due to Wife's Illness
Author:
Date: 1956-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, Vice President of the Republic of India, was originally scheduled to visit China in mid-October at the invitation of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China. Due to his wife's illness, it has been decided to cancel the current visit and reschedule it for the future.

### 印度副总统因夫人患病暂不来我国访问

1956-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    印度副总统因夫人患病暂不来我国访问
    新华社14日讯　印度共和国副总统萨·拉达克里希南博士应中华人民共和国毛泽东主席的邀请原定于10月中旬前来中国访问。现因他的夫人患病，决定取消目前的这一访问，留待将来再作安排。
