Title: Jordan Asks the Security Council to Impose Sanctions on Israel
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th, New York Dispatch: Jordan's representative to the United Nations, Rifai, requested the Security Council on the 16th to impose economic and diplomatic sanctions on Israel for its acts of aggression against the Jordanian border. The Security Council is scheduled to hold a meeting on October 19th to hear the arguments of Jordan and Israel.

On the same day, Rifai conducted extensive contacts with representatives of several countries. He met twice with the President of the Security Council for October, French representative Cornut-Gentille, and also met with the representatives of the United States and Britain, as well as the Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi. He also met with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld. He said that he had contacted the Soviet representative Sobolev and would discuss the matter with him again soon.

### 约旦要求安理会制裁以色列

1956-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦要求安理会制裁以色列
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：约旦驻联合国代表里法伊16日要求安全理事会对以色列侵犯约旦边界的行为实行经济和外交的制裁。安全理事会预定在10月19日举行会议听取约旦和以色列的辩论。
    同一天，里法伊同一些国家的代表进行了广泛的接触。他曾经两次会见10月份的安全理事会主席、法国代表戈尔尼—让蒂，并且会见了美国和英国的代表以及埃及外交部长法齐。他还会见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。他说，他已经同苏联代表索波列夫接触过，不久还要再同他商谈。
