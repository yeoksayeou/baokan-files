Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Balinese Art Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception this evening to welcome the Indonesian Balinese Art Friendship Delegation. Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto, was invited to attend the reception. Premier Zhou cordially conversed with the Balinese artists at the reception. Premier Zhou praised the beautiful Balinese dances and expressed hope that Chinese dance practitioners would diligently study Balinese dance art.

Prior to the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai watched a performance by the Indonesian artists and, during the intermission, met with the delegation leader, Saléh Umar, deputy leader Air Eslach Moel Boedjocharsono, and Secretary-General I Gedé Boeger and others.

When the performance concluded, Premier Zhou and others went on stage to shake hands with all the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance. Beijing literary and art circles presented flower baskets to the Balinese artists.

Those who attended the reception and watched the performance tonight included Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Deng Zihui, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei; Vice Ministers of Culture Qian Junrui, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, and Chen Kehan; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Dong Biwu; President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Burhan Shahidi; Vice Presidents Hu Yuzhi and Ma Yu Huai, as well as well-known figures from all circles in the capital.

### 周总理招待峇厘艺术访问团

1956-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理招待峇厘艺术访问团
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间举行酒会，招待印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团。印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托应邀出席了酒会。周总理在酒会上同峇厘艺术家们亲切地交谈着。周总理赞扬优美的峇厘舞蹈，并且希望中国的舞蹈工作者认真学习峇厘舞蹈艺术。
    在酒会以前，周恩来总理观看了印度尼西亚艺术家们的演出，并且在休息时接见了访问团团长萨勒·乌玛尔、副团长爱尔·埃斯拉赫末·布佐哈尔梭纳、秘书长伊·格德·布格尔等人。
    当演出结束的时候，周总理等走上舞台同全体演员们握手，祝贺他们演出成功。北京文艺界向峇厘艺术家们赠送了花篮。
    今晚出席酒会和观看演出的，有副总理陈云、邓子恢、李富春、李先念，副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞，文化部副部长钱俊瑞、郑振铎、夏衍、陈克寒，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席董必武，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉、副会长胡愈之、马玉槐，以及首都各界著名人士。
