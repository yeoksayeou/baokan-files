    New Slavic Studies Journal Published in Denmark
    Denmark has published the first issue of a new Slavic studies journal, "Scandinavica Slavica." The editor-in-chief of the journal is Professor A. Stender-Petersen, a Danish Slavicist. The journal is published in five languages: Russian, English, French, German, and Italian. In addition to many articles on the history of Russian literature, it also features a lengthy article by Knut Raben-Schmidt entitled "The Anglo-Russian Treaty of 1766 – A Study of Count Panin's Northern System Development." The main work of publishing "Scandinavica Slavica" is undertaken by the Slavic Institute of Aarhus University in Denmark.

### 丹麦出版斯拉夫学新杂志

1956-10-23
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    丹麦出版斯拉夫学新杂志
    丹麦出版了斯拉夫学新杂志“斯堪的那维亚斯拉夫学”第一期。杂志的总编辑是丹麦的斯拉夫学家阿·斯坦德·派特森教授。这家杂志用俄、英、法、德、意五种文字刊行。除了许多关于俄国文学史的文章以外，还刊载了克努特·拉宾·施米特的长篇文章“英俄两国1766年的条约—关于巴宁伯爵的北方制度发展的探讨”。“斯堪的那维亚斯拉夫学”杂志出版的主要工作是由丹麦奥尔胡斯大学斯拉夫研究所负责的。
