Title: Hong Kong and Kowloon Silk Weaving Industry Union and others send representatives to demand that the British Hong Kong authorities severely punish the murderers and handle the aftermath.
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 19th: Hong Kong message: Representatives from the Hong Kong and Kowloon Silk Weaving Industry Union and the Hong Kong and Kowloon Enamelware Industry Union visited the Hong Kong British Commissioner of Police on the afternoon of the 18th regarding the sabotage by Kuomintang secret agents and rioters against their respective associations, and the murder of workers. They submitted letters requesting punishment of the murderers and reasonable handling of the aftermath, demanding that the Hong Kong authorities severely punish the murderers and handle the aftermath properly.
　　The British Superintendent of Police, who received the representatives of the two trade unions on behalf of the Commissioner of Police, immediately replied that regarding the punishment of the murderers, many "troublemakers" have already been arrested, and the hunt for the murderers will continue; regarding the protection of residents' lives and property, he said that such things would not happen again; as for the Tsuen Wan police's failure to prevent in advance and failure to dispatch police when the incident occurred, he said that the police would investigate this matter; regarding the aftermath, he would forward it to the British Hong Kong authorities for handling.

### 港九丝织业总工会等派出代表  要求香港英国当局严惩凶手办理善后

1956-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    港九丝织业总工会等派出代表
    要求香港英国当局严惩凶手办理善后
    据新华社广州19日电　香港消息：港九丝织业总工会和港九搪瓷业总工会的代表于18日下午为各该会所受国民党特务分子及暴徒捣毁和工人被杀害事件，分别往见香港英国警务处长，并递交要求惩凶和合理办理善后的信件。要求香港当局严惩凶手办理善后。　　代表警务处长分别接见两个工会代表的英籍警司当即答复说，关于惩凶，现在已经捉了许多“搅事”的人，并将继续进行缉凶；对于保障居民生命财产，他则说不会再有这种事发生了；至于荃湾警方预先不防御、事发时不派出警察，他说这事将由警方进行调查；关于善后问题，他将转呈香港英国当局处理。
