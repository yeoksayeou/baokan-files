Title: Volunteer Army's Sixth Anniversary of Resisting America and Aiding Korea: Commemorative Activities Begin in Pyongyang

Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 21st: Commemorative activities for the sixth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army resisting America and aiding Korea have begun in Pyongyang.

    Recently, factories, enterprises, and rural areas in the suburbs have held discussion meetings and lectures. Photo exhibitions have been held in the streets to introduce the scenes of the Volunteer Army fighting side-by-side with the Korean people during the war and helping the Korean people restore construction after the war.

    All cinemas will screen the large-scale Korean-produced documentary "The Chinese People's Volunteer Army" and other films on October 25th.

    The art troupes of Pyongyang No. 1 Girls' High School and No. 4 High School have prepared artistic programs and will conduct comfort performances in the Volunteer Army units around October 25th. People from all walks of life in the city have been writing letters of appreciation to the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army.

### 志愿军抗美援朝六周年  平壤市各界开始纪念活动

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    志愿军抗美援朝六周年
    平壤市各界开始纪念活动
    新华社平壤21日电　纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝六周年的活动已经在平壤市开始了。
    市内各工厂、企业和郊区农村最近举行了座谈会和讲演会。各街头举办了图片展览，介绍有关志愿军在战争中与朝鲜人民并肩作战和在战后帮助朝鲜人民恢复建设的情景。
    各电影院将在10月25日放映朝鲜摄制的大型纪录片“中国人民志愿军”等影片。
    平壤市第一女子高中和第四高中的文艺小组准备了文艺节目，将在10月25日前后到志愿军部队中进行慰问演出。市内各界人民纷纷给志愿军官兵写慰问信。
