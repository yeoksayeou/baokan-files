Title: Shandong Huang County Achieves Another Bumper Corn Harvest
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report: Huang County in Shandong Province, renowned for its consecutive years of large-scale corn bumper harvests, has achieved another widespread bumper corn harvest this year. The county's 310,000 mu of corn fields have yielded an average of over 500 jin per mu.

The county has also seen the emergence of many agricultural production cooperatives that have achieved high yields across large areas of corn. The corn yield of most agricultural cooperatives in Zhongcun District averaged over 700 jin per mu. Among them, the two mu of high-yield corn fields of the agricultural cooperative in Zhongjia Township achieved an average yield of 1,300 jin per mu.

### 山东黄县玉米又丰产

1956-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

    山东黄县玉米又丰产
    据新华社讯　以玉米连年大面积丰产著称的山东省黄县，今年玉米又普遍丰收。全县三十一万多亩玉米，每亩平均产量在五百斤以上。
    全县还涌现出很多获得玉米大面积高额产量的农业生产合作社。中村区大部分农业社的玉米，每亩平均产量都在七百斤以上。其中仲家乡农业社的两亩玉米高额丰产田，每亩平均产量达到一千三百斤。
