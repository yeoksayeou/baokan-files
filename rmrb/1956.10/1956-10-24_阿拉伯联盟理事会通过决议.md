Title: Arab League Council Passes Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd, Cairo: The Arab League Council decided on October 22nd that its member states would support the People's Republic of Mongolia's entry into the United Nations and support Indonesia's position on the West Irian issue.

    The Council also decided that when the issue of Cyprus' demand for self-determination is raised at the United Nations General Assembly, the Arab League countries will support this demand.

    The member states of the Arab League advocate increasing the number of member states of the major committees of the United Nations to include a certain number of countries that have not yet been able to participate in these committees.

### 阿拉伯联盟理事会通过决议

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟理事会通过决议
    将支持蒙古进入联合国，支持印度尼西亚在西伊里安问题上的立场，支持塞浦路斯对自决的要求
    新华社23日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟理事会10月22日决定，它的成员国将支持蒙古人民共和国进入联合国，支持印度尼西亚在西伊里安问题上的立场。
    理事会还决定，当塞浦路斯要求自决的问题在联合国大会上提出来的时候，阿拉伯联盟国家将支持这种要求。
    阿拉伯联盟的成员国主张增加联合国中大的委员会的委员国数目，以便包括进一定数量的直到目前还没能参加这些委员会的国家。
