Title: Protest Against France's Kidnapping of Algerian Leaders

Author:

Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th—The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace protests against the French authorities' illegal kidnapping of Algerian national leaders. The full text of the protest is as follows:

    Recently, the French authorities, violating the ardent desire of people all over the world to end the Algerian war through peaceful negotiations, openly resorted to illegal means to kidnap the Algerian national liberation leaders. This unjust act of the French authorities, which violates international morality and international codes of conduct, has aroused condemnation and anger from all peace-loving people in the world. We, the Chinese people, lodge a serious protest against the French authorities for taking such illegal measures to undermine the peaceful resolution of the Algerian issue and exacerbate the tense situation in North Africa, and resolutely demand the immediate release of the arrested Algerian national leaders.

    The peace-loving people of China, Asia, Africa, and the world sympathize with and support the Algerian people's struggle for freedom and national independence and firmly believe that they will surely achieve final victory. We hope that the French authorities will abandon the policy of armed suppression and peacefully resolve the Algerian issue on the basis of respecting national independence.

### 抗议法国绑架阿尔及利亚领袖  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会

1956-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议法国绑架阿尔及利亚领袖
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    新华社30日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会抗议法国当局非法绑架阿尔及利亚民族领袖。抗议全文如下：
    最近法国当局违背全世界人民迫切要求用和平谈判方式结束阿尔及利亚战争的愿望，公然采取了非法手段绑架阿尔及利亚的民族解放领袖。法国当局这种违反国际道义和国际行为准则的不义行径，已经引起了世界一切爱好和平人民的谴责和愤怒。我们中国人民对法国当局采取这种非法手段以破坏和平解决阿尔及利亚问题和加剧北非紧张局势的行动，提出严正的抗议，并坚决要求立即释放被捕的阿尔及利亚民族领袖。
    中国、亚非和全世界爱好和平的人民是同情和支持阿尔及利亚人民争取自由和民族独立的斗争的，并深信他们一定能获得最后的胜利，我们希望法国当局放弃武力镇压的政策而在尊重民族独立的基础上，和平解决阿尔及利亚的问题。
