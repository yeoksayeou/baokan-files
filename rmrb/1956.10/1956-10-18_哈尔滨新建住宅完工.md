Title: Newly Built Housing Completed in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 14th: More than 480,000 square meters of new housing built in Harbin this year for workers are being completed one after another. Nearly 20,000 workers and their families will move into new homes before the ground freezes. The employee housing built by the Northeast Third Engineering Company, which can accommodate more than 2,000 households, will see many young men and women preparing for marriage moving in this month.

This year, the Harbin Municipal Real Estate Management Bureau also built a well-equipped building for more than 30 senior intellectuals.

### 哈尔滨新建住宅完工

1956-10-18
第2版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨新建住宅完工
    新华社哈尔滨14日电　哈尔滨市今年为职工新建的四十八万多平方公尺的住宅，正在陆续完工。在结冻以前将有近两万户职工和家属搬进新居。东北第三工程公司新建的、可以居住两千多户的职工住宅，这个月内将有许多准备结婚的青年男女搬进去住。
    今年，哈尔滨市房地产管理局还为三十多户高级知识分子新建了一座设备较好的楼房。
