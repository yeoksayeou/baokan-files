Title: Our Military Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visit to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Chinese military delegation, invited by Yugoslav Federal People's Republic Secretary of National Defense General Ivan Gošnjak to visit Yugoslavia, left Beijing by special plane on the morning of the 6th.

　　The delegation consists of eight people, including: Head of the delegation General Deng Hua, members Admiral Wang Hongkun, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, Major General Chen Ruiting, Senior Colonel Zhang Ting, Senior Colonel Hua Nan, and our military attache to Yugoslavia Senior Colonel Ding Ganru.

　　Yugoslav Embassy in China Military Attache Colonel Bruno Vuletic accompanied the delegation to Yugoslavia.

　　Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popovic saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 我军事代表团离京去南斯拉夫访问

1956-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团离京去南斯拉夫访问
    据新华社6日讯　应南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国防秘书长伊·哥斯尼亚克大将邀请，去南斯拉夫访问的中国军事代表团，在六日早晨乘专机离开北京。
    代表团由八人组成，包括：团长邓华上将、团员王宏坤海军上将、王秉璋空军中将、杨秀山中将、陈锐霆少将、张挺大校、华楠大校和我国驻南斯拉夫武官丁甘如大校。
    南斯拉夫驻我国大使馆武官布鲁诺·武累提奇上校陪同代表团前往南斯拉夫。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇到机场送行。
