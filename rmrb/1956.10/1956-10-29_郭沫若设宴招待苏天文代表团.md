Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Soviet Astronomical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 27th to welcome the Soviet astronomical delegation invited to China for observation. The delegation was led by A.A. Mikhailov, Corresponding Academician and Chairman of the Astronomical Council of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and the deputy leader was Professor B.V. Kukarkin, Vice-Chairman of the Astronomical Council of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, as well as delegation members Professor Zayets and Professor Shcheglov.
Those present to assist included Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhang Yuzhe, Director of the Nanjing Purple Mountain Observatory, and several other Chinese astronomers.
Mikhailov arrived in Beijing on the 21st.

### 郭沫若设宴招待苏天文代表团

1956-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴招待苏天文代表团
    新华社28日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若27日晚上设宴招待应邀来中国考察的苏联天文代表团团长、苏联科学院天文委员会主席亚·亚·米哈依洛夫通讯院士，副团长苏联科学院天文委员会副主席波·华·库加金教授，以及团员节依奇教授和谢格洛夫教授。
    作陪的有中国科学院秘书长裴丽生，南京紫金山天文台台长张钰哲及中国天文学家多人。
    米哈依洛夫是21日到达北京的。
