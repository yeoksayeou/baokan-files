Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet for All Members of the Korean National Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing held a banquet on the evening of the 23rd to welcome all members of the Korean Democratic People's Republic National Art Troupe, which arrived in Beijing on the 22nd. Troupe leader Yun Du-heon, Deputy Troupe Leader Lee Won-bae, and all troupe members attended the banquet. Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, and embassy staff were also invited to attend.

Deputy Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Chen Yi, and more than 50 of our country's playwrights and musicians attended as guests.

Some of the Korean artists attending the banquet had been to China before, and some of the Chinese artists had been to Korea. Some of the artists from the two countries were already well acquainted. They conversed very happily at the banquet. The artists spoke of the lofty friendship between the people of the two countries in the common struggle against American imperialist aggression, the new developments in the national arts of the two countries, and the desire of the artists of the two countries to exchange experiences. Minister Shen Yanbing and our country's artists repeatedly raised their glasses to wish the Korean artists success in their performances of the Korean Changgeuk "Chunhyangjeon" and "Simcheongjeon" in our country.

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The National Art Troupe of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, including more than one hundred artists, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 22nd at the invitation of our Ministry of Culture.

### 沈雁冰欢宴朝鲜民族艺术剧团全体人员

1956-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰欢宴朝鲜民族艺术剧团全体人员
    新华社23日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰23日晚举行宴会，欢迎22日到达北京的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族艺术剧团全体人员。剧团团长尹斗宪、副团长李源培和全体团员都出席了宴会。应邀出席宴会的还有朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南和使馆人员。
    出席作陪的有文化部副部长丁西林、中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂和我国的戏剧家、音乐家共五十多人。
    出席宴会的朝鲜艺术家，有的人是来过中国的，中国艺术家有的人是去过朝鲜的，两国艺术家中间有些人过去就很熟悉。他们在宴会上叙谈甚为欢愉。艺术家们谈到了两国人民在共同反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争中的崇高友谊，谈到两国发展民族艺术的新情况和两国艺术家交流经验的愿望。沈雁冰部长和我国艺术家曾一再举杯预祝朝鲜艺术家在我国演出朝鲜唱剧“春香传”和“沈清传”获得成功。
    据新华社22日讯　包括一百多位艺术家的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国立民族艺术剧团，应我国文化部的邀请，在22日下午到达北京。
