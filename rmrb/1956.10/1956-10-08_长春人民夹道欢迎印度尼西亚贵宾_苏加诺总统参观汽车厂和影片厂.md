Title: Changchun People Welcome Indonesian Guests; President Sukarno Visits Automobile Factory and Film Studio
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 7th - President Sukarno and his entourage, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited the Changchun No. 1 Automobile Manufacturing Plant this morning. The male and female workers of this automobile factory, which is about to fully commence production, warmly applauded and cheered as the President arrived, showering the distinguished guests with colorful paper flowers.

President Sukarno, guided by the factory director and chief engineer of the automobile factory, walked from the foundry workshop at the beginning of the automobile assembly line to the final assembly workshop at the end of automobile production. Workers warmly applauded to welcome the distinguished guests. A female worker, Zhang Shufang, requested President Sukarno to sign his name, and the President gladly obliged. The distinguished guests arrived at the final assembly line in the chassis workshop. Workers were busy assembling cars. President Sukarno asked a female assembler, Zou Guizhen, about her start date at the factory and her job responsibilities. The President then asked another female assembler, Li Lingdi, to demonstrate the assembly of a gearbox. After she skillfully completed the assembly, he expressed his satisfaction. President Sukarno walked to the end of the final assembly line, where a new "Liberation" brand car was being completed. Amidst warm applause, President Sukarno sat in the driver's seat, and the adjustment worker, Wang Shangzhe, drove the car out of the workshop. After getting out of the car, the President warmly shook hands with factory director Rao Bin and deputy factory director Meng Shaonong, and said to them: "I congratulate you, your cars are manufactured very well!" This car was the three hundred and thirty-fifth completed by the factory since it began trial production in July.

As the distinguished guests walked out of the final assembly workshop, President Sukarno happily said to Vice Premier Chen Yi, "I had a very good and satisfying visit today." At this moment, many workers crowded around to applaud and cheer the President. The President took photos with them, arm in arm.

After the visit, President Sukarno went to the city center. On the tree-lined road from the automobile factory to the city center, there was a large banner with the slogan "Pay Tribute to President Sukarno." Tens of thousands of people in Changchun lined both sides of the wide road, cheering for President Sukarno. President Sukarno sometimes stepped out of the car, stood on the hood of the engine, and waved to the people who were warmly welcoming him.

President Sukarno and his entourage arrived in Changchun by plane from Shenyang at 11:00 am. Welcoming President Sukarno at the airport were Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, Major General He Jian, Commander of the Jilin Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Chengkun, Deputy Mayor of Changchun, and responsible persons from democratic parties and people's organizations in Jilin Province and Changchun City, as well as more than 5,000 people from all walks of life.

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 7th - President Sukarno and his entourage visited the Changchun Film Studio in Changchun City this afternoon.

The distinguished guests first visited the mixing recording studio. Here, music recording for the film "National Day at Ten O'Clock" was in progress. President Sukarno and Vice Premier Chen Yi sat in front of the orchestra and listened quietly to two beautiful pieces of music. President Sukarno tapped his hand lightly to the rhythm of the music, repeatedly nodding in praise.

President Sukarno came to an automated soundstage. Here, a Pingju opera stage documentary "Liu Qiao'er" was being filmed. When the President arrived, the famous Chinese Pingju opera actress Xin Fengxia, playing the rural girl "Liu Qiao'er," was carrying a small basket, walking slowly along a small stream, expressing her pleasant mood of overcoming the feudal marriage system and finding a new in-law family. After this shot was completed, President Sukarno shook hands with the director Yi Lin, actress Xin Fengxia, and others, and took photos with them. The President praised Xin Fengxia for acting very well and said, "Welcome to visit Indonesia." Xin Fengxia said, "I am very happy to go to Indonesia."

In another soundstage, President Sukarno also visited a scene being filmed for the film "National Day at Ten O'Clock." During the visit, the President inquired about the filming and recording process of the film. President Sukarno watched clips from the films "Liu Qiao'er," "National Day at Ten O'Clock," "Eagle in the Storm," and "Older Brother and Younger Sister" in the screening room. President Sukarno also talked with the actors.

President Sukarno visited the Changchun Film Studio for more than two hours. When the President arrived and left, the film actors and staff welcomed him with gongs and drums on both sides of the road.

Tonight, Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, held an informal banquet to welcome President Sukarno.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th - President Sukarno left Shenyang for Changchun by special plane at 9:50 this morning.

Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the CPC, Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Lieutenant General Wu Xinquan, head of the Shenyang garrison, and deputy governors, deputy mayors, and responsible persons of democratic parties and people's organizations in the province and city, more than 100 people, went to the airport to see him off.

The Consuls General of the Soviet Union and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Shenyang also went to the airport to see him off.

Before boarding the plane, President Sukarno shook hands with Du Zheheng to say goodbye and thanked him for his kind hospitality.

### 长春人民夹道欢迎印度尼西亚贵宾  苏加诺总统参观汽车厂和影片厂

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    长春人民夹道欢迎印度尼西亚贵宾
    苏加诺总统参观汽车厂和影片厂
    新华社长春7日电　苏加诺总统和他的随从人员今天上午由陈毅副总理陪同，到长春第一汽车制造厂参观。这个即将全面开工生产的汽车厂的男女工人，在总统到来时，夹道热烈鼓掌欢呼，并且把五色纸花撒在贵宾们身上。
    苏加诺总统由汽车厂厂长和总工程师引导，从汽车流水生产线的源头铸工车间走到汽车生产的终点总装配车间，工人们都热烈鼓掌欢迎贵宾。女工张淑芳请求苏加诺总统签字，总统欣然为她签上了自己的名字。贵宾们来到底盘车间的总装配线旁参观。工人们正在忙着装配汽车。苏加诺总统问一位女装配工邹桂珍的入厂时间和担负的工作职务。总统又请另一位女装配工李玲娣表演装配变速箱，当她熟练地装配完后，他表示很满意。苏加诺总统走到总装配线的尽头，正好一辆新“解放牌”汽车装配完成。苏加诺总统在热烈的掌声中坐进驾驶室，由调整工王尚哲驾驶汽车出车间，总统走下汽车以后，同厂长饶斌、副厂长孟少农热烈握手，并对他们说：“我向你们祝贺，你们的汽车制造得很好！”这辆汽车是这个工厂自7月份开始试生产后完成的第三百三十五辆。
    贵宾们走出总装配车间时，苏加诺总统高兴地对陈毅副总理说：“我今天参观得很好，很满意。”这时许多工人拥上来向总统鼓掌欢呼。总统和他们一起手挽手摄影留念。
    参观结束以后，苏加诺总统即到市区去。在由汽车厂进入市区的林荫马路上，竖着“向苏加诺总统致敬”的大幅标语。长春市数万人民列队在广阔的马路两侧向苏加诺总统夹道欢呼。苏加诺总统有时走出汽车，站在汽车发动机的顶盖上向热情欢迎他的人挥手致意。
    苏加诺总统和他的随行人员是上午十一时由沈阳乘飞机到达长春的。在机场上欢迎苏加诺总统的，有吉林省省长栗又文，中国人民解放军吉林军区司令员贺健少将，长春市副市长李承锟和吉林省、长春市的各民主党派和人民团体负责人和各界人民五千多人。
    新华社长春7日电　苏加诺总统和他的随行人员今天下午在长春市参观了长春电影制片厂。
    贵宾们首先参观了混合录音室。这里正在录制影片“国庆十点钟”的音乐录音。苏加诺总统和陈毅副总理坐在乐队的面前静听了两段优美的乐曲。苏加诺总统随着音乐的节奏用手轻轻地打着拍子，连连点头称赞。
    苏加诺总统来到一座自动化的摄影棚里。这里正在摄制评剧舞台纪录片“刘巧儿”。总统到达时，中国著名评剧演员新凤霞扮演的农村姑娘“刘巧儿”正提着一只小篮，沿着一条小溪慢行，倾述着自己战胜封建婚姻制度寻找新婆家的愉快心情。这个镜头摄完后，苏加诺总统同导演伊琳、演员新凤霞等握手，并且摄影留念。总统称赞新凤霞演得很好，并说：“欢迎你到印度尼西亚去看看。”新凤霞说：“我很高兴到印度尼西亚去。”
    在另一个摄影棚中，苏加诺总统又参观了正在拍摄影片“国庆十点钟”中的一个镜头。总统在参观时，询问了影片的拍摄和录音等过程。苏加诺总统在放映室看了“刘巧儿”、“国庆十点钟”、“暴风中的雄鹰”、“哥哥和妹妹”四个影片的片断。苏加诺总统还同演员们谈了话。
    苏加诺总统在长春电影制片厂参观了两个多小时。在总统来到和离开的时候，电影演员和职工们都敲着锣鼓夹道欢迎。
    今天晚间，吉林省长栗又文举行便宴，欢迎苏加诺总统。
    新华社沈阳7日电　苏加诺总统今日上午九点五十分乘专机离开沈阳去长春。
    辽宁省省长杜者蘅，中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚、沈阳市副市长张力克、沈阳驻军首长吴信泉中将和副省长、副市长及省市各民主党派、各人民团体负责人一百多人到机场欢送。
    苏联和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻沈阳总领事也到机场欢送。
    苏加诺总统在上飞机前和杜者蘅握手告别，并感谢对他的亲切招待。
