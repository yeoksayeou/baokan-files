Title: Tito Meets with Gerő in Yalta
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yalta, 1st. TASS reports: President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia arrived at the villa where First Secretary Khrushchev of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was vacationing on the morning of September 30. First Secretary Gerő of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, who was also vacationing in the Crimea, also came to the villa. They all went for a walk by the sea, where they met vacationers recuperating at the Livadia Sanatorium. They talked with these people and took pictures with them.

　　Subsequently, Khrushchev, Tito, and Gerő went for a walk in the mountains of the Yalta district. There, Chairman Bulganin of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union also joined them; Bulganin had come to the Crimea from Sochi for a vacation.

　　Vice President Ranković of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia and others also joined the walk.

　　Members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Kirichenko, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU Brezhnev, and Furtseva also joined the walk.

　　After the walk, they had lunch together in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 铁托在雅尔塔同格罗会晤

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托在雅尔塔同格罗会晤　　
    新华社1日讯　 据塔斯社雅尔塔讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托9月30日上午来到了苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫正在那里休假的别墅。正在克里米亚休假的匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记格罗也来到这个别墅。他们全都到海边去散步，在海边他们碰到了在利瓦吉亚疗养院休养的休假者，他们和这些人一同谈话并且还和他们在一起照相。　　
    随后，赫鲁晓夫、铁托和格罗到雅尔塔区的山地去散步。在那里，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁也同他们一道散步，布尔加宁是从索契到克里米亚来休假的。　　
    在一起散步的还有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇等。
    苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员基里钦科、苏共中央书记勃列日涅夫和福尔采娃等也加入了散步的行列。　　
    在散步之后，他们在友好热烈的气氛中共进了午餐。
