Title: East German Civil Aviation Opens Three International Routes
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th - According to a report by the German News Agency: On October 4th, a passenger plane of the East German Lufthansa Airlines made its first flight on the newly opened route between Berlin and Moscow. The planes flying on this route daily land in Warsaw and Vilnius en route.
    In addition, Lufthansa Airlines has also opened the Berlin-Sofia line and the Berlin-Bucharest line. The planes flying on these two routes all pass through Prague and Budapest.

### 民主德国民航机开辟三条国际航线

1956-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国民航机开辟三条国际航线
    新华社柏林5日电　据德意志通讯社报道：民主德国汉莎航空公司的客机10月4日在柏林和莫斯科之间新开辟的航线上作了第一次飞行。在这条航线上每天飞行的班机，中途都在华沙和维尔纽斯着陆。
    此外汉莎航空公司还开辟了柏林——索非亚线和柏林——布加勒斯特线。在这两条航线上飞行的班机都经过布拉格和布达佩斯。
