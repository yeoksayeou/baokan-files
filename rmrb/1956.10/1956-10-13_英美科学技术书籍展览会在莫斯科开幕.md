Title: Anglo-American Scientific and Technological Book Exhibition Opens in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: According to TASS, the Anglo-American Scientific and Technological Book Exhibition, organized by the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, opened on the 10th at the House of Scientific Workers in Moscow.

The British bookseller "Collet's Holdings Limited" enthusiastically displayed many of the latest scientific and technological books. Over a thousand new books and magazines in fields such as chemistry, optics, geography, biology, and medicine were displayed on the shelves.

In his opening speech, Academician Ostrovityanov, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, said: Soviet scientists and engineers are very interested in the Anglo-American Scientific and Technological Book Exhibition. We highly value the contributions of British and American scientists in science and technology, and we welcome everything that helps to strengthen the exchange of international scientific and technological achievements.

### 英美科学技术书籍展览会在莫斯科开幕

1956-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    英美科学技术书籍展览会在莫斯科开幕
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院主席团举办的英美科学技术书籍展览会，10日在莫斯科科学工作者之家开幕。
    英国书商公司“柯列兹·霍尔汀斯有限公司”热心地展出了许多最新科学技术书籍。陈列台上陈列着化学、光学、地理学、生物学、医学等方面的新书和杂志共一千多种。
    苏联科学院副院长奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫在致开幕词时说：苏联科学家和工程师对英美科学技术书籍展览会很感兴趣。我们十分珍视英国和美国的科学家在科学和技术方面的贡献，并且对凡是有助于加强国际科学技术成就的交流的一切事情都表示欢迎。
