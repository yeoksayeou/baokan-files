Title: Converting Sloping Land to Terraces Increases Grain Production
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 3

    Converting Sloping Land to Terraces Increases Grain Production
    (Xinhua News Agency) The "July 1st" Agricultural Production Cooperative in Suihua County, Heilongjiang Province, converted more than 110 hectares of sloping land into terraced fields. This year, grain production has increased by at least 200,000 jin. It is estimated that the yield of corn per hectare can reach 5,500 jin.

### 坡地改梯田增产粮食

1956-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

    坡地改梯田增产粮食
    据新华社讯　黑龙江省绥化县“七一”农业生产合作社把一百一十多顷山坡地改成梯田后，今年至少增产了二十万斤粮食。据估计：玉米每公顷的产量可以达到五千五百斤。
