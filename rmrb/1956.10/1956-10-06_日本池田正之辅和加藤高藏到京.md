Title: Japan's Ikeda Masanosuke and Kato Takazo Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th — Ikeda Masanosuke, head of the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade Delegation, and Kato Takazo, the deputy head, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Other members of the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade Delegation arrived in Beijing earlier on September 29th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them from the Chinese side included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, and Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, among others. From the Japanese side were Sukuya Eiichi, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, Morii Shonai, deputy head, Japanese personnel in Beijing, Kameda Tougo, and members of the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade Delegation who had arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 日本池田正之辅和加藤高藏到京

1956-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日本池田正之辅和加藤高藏到京
    新华社5日讯　日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团团长池田正之辅和副团长加藤高藏今天下午乘飞机到达北京。日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团其他人员早于9月29日到达北京。前往机场欢迎的，中国方面有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长乔冠华和中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民等多人。日本方面有日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一、副团长森井庄内，在京日本人士龟田东伍以及早日到京的日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团的人员。
