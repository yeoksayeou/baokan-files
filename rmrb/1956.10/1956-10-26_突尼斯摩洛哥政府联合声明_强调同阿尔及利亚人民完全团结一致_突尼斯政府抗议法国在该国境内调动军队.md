Title: Joint Statement by the Tunisian and Moroccan Governments Emphasizes Complete Solidarity with the Algerian People; Tunisian Government Protests French Troop Movements in the Country
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 25th, Tunis: The Tunisian and Moroccan governments issued a joint declaration on October 24th stating that the two governments “agree” on the Algerian issue and express “complete solidarity with the Algerian people.”

    The declaration also stated that Tunisia and Morocco will soon sign a treaty of cooperation and alliance and will maintain regular contact on cooperation in matters of common concern to the two countries.

    Following the release of the joint declaration, the Sultan of Morocco, who arrived in Tunis on October 22nd seeking a peaceful resolution to the Algerian issue, left Tunis for Rabat aboard an Italian plane.
    
      Xinhua News Agency, October 25th, Tunis: The Tunisian government issued a communique on the evening of October 24th protesting the concentration of French troops by France in some areas within Tunisia, particularly in areas along the border guarded by sentries.

    The communique stated that the Tunisian government's protest has been delivered to the French ambassador to Tunisia.

### 突尼斯摩洛哥政府联合声明  强调同阿尔及利亚人民完全团结一致  突尼斯政府抗议法国在该国境内调动军队

1956-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯摩洛哥政府联合声明
    强调同阿尔及利亚人民完全团结一致
    突尼斯政府抗议法国在该国境内调动军队
    新华社25日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯和摩洛哥政府10月24日发表联合宣言说，两国政府在阿尔及利亚问题上“意见一致”，并且表示“同阿尔及利亚人民完全团结一致”。
    宣言还说，突尼斯同摩洛哥不久将签订合作同盟条约，而且将在两国共同问题的合作方面保持经常的接触。
    联合宣言发表以后，在为谋求阿尔及利亚问题的和平解决而在10月22日到突尼斯的摩洛哥苏丹，就乘坐意大利飞机离突尼斯回拉巴特。
      新华社25日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯政府10月24日晚间发表公报，抗议法国在突尼斯境内一些地区，特别是沿着边境哨兵警戒地区集结法国军队。
    公报说，突尼斯政府的抗议已经送交驻突尼斯法国大使。
