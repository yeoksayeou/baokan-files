Title: Soviet Union Publishes Documents of the Eighth National Congress of the CPC
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 13th: The Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House has published separate editions of Comrade Mao Zedong's and Comrade Liu Shaoqi's opening address and political report at the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

The publishing house is also preparing to publish a collection of documents from the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. The collection will include reports, speeches by delegates to the congress, and resolutions adopted by the congress.

### 苏联出版中共八大文件

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联出版中共八大文件
    新华社莫斯科13日电　苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版了毛泽东同志和刘少奇同志在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会上所作的开幕词和政治报告的单行本。
    这个出版局还准备出版中国共产党第八次全国代表大会文集。文集将包括报告、大会代表的发言和大会通过的决议。
