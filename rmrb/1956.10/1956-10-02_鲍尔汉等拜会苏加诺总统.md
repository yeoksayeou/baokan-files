Title: Burhan and Others Visit President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Vice Presidents Hu Yuzhi and Ma Yuhuai, visited President Sukarno at Qinzheng Hall, Zhongnanhai at 5:30 p.m. today.

Also present at the visit were Da Punsheng and Dai Ailian, directors of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, and Ren Bosheng, Secretary-General.

During the visit, Burhan, on behalf of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, presented President Sukarno with a pair of large vases. These vases are famous Fuzhou lacquerware.

### 鲍尔汉等拜会苏加诺总统

1956-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉等拜会苏加诺总统
    新华社1日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉、副会长胡愈之、马玉槐今天下午五时半，前往中南海勤政殿拜会苏加诺总统。
    一起前往拜会的，还有中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事达浦生、戴爱莲，总干事任泊生。
    拜会的时候，鲍尔汉代表中国印度尼西亚友好协会赠送给苏加诺总统一对大型花瓶。这对花瓶是著名的福建脱胎漆器。
