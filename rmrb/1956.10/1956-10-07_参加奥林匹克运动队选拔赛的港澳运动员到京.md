Title: Hong Kong and Macau Athletes Arrive in Beijing for Olympic Team Trials
Author: Guo An
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 4

    Hong Kong and Macau Athletes Arrive in Beijing for Olympic Team Trials
    (Our newspaper reports) A group of one hundred and twenty-six athletes from Hong Kong and Macau, participating in the Chinese Olympic Team Trials, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou on the evening of the 6th.
    Upon disembarking, the Hong Kong and Macau athletes were warmly welcomed by Huang Zhong, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Sixteenth Olympic Games of China, and seventy male and female athletes from various regions, who had come to the station to greet them. Cheung Kwok-shuen, Director of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce of Hong Kong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Import and Export Association of Hong Kong, and General Leader of the Hong Kong Team, and Chui Tak-kei, Vice Chairman of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce of Macau and General Leader of the Macau Team, shook hands with those who came to welcome them, received flowers, and were accompanied out of the station by Huang Zhong and others. The athletes from various regions who came to welcome them enthusiastically competed to carry luggage for the Hong Kong and Macau athletes. Athletes who had known each other in the past or participated in competitions together began friendly conversations as they walked.
    The Hong Kong and Macau athletes will watch a friendly football match between the Chinese team and the Korean team on the afternoon of the 7th, and attend the opening ceremony of the Chinese Olympic Team Trials in the evening. On the afternoons of the 8th and 9th, the Hong Kong football team will play against the Shanghai team and the "August 1st" team respectively. (Guo An)

### 参加奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的港澳运动员到京
国安
1956-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    参加奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的港澳运动员到京
    本报讯　参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的香港、澳门运动员一行一百二十六人，6日晚上从广州乘火车到达北京。
    港、澳运动员下车后，受到前往车站迎接的中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会秘书长黄中和七十名各地男女运动员等的热烈欢迎。香港中华总商会会董、香港中华出入口商会副理事长、香港队总领队张国璇和澳门中华总商会副理事长、澳门队总领队崔德祺和前往欢迎的人们一一握手后，接受了献花，并由黄中等陪同步出车站。前往欢迎的各地运动员热情地争着替港、澳运动员提行李。他们中间过去相识的和一起参加过比赛的运动员，一边走一边开始了亲切的交谈。
    港、澳运动员将在7日下午观看中国队和朝鲜队的足球友谊比赛，晚上参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的开幕式。8日和9日下午，香港足球队将分别与上海队和“八一”队进行比赛。　（国安）
