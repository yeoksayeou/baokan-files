Title: Prime Minister Široký Receives Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th. The Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, Mr. Široký, received the Indonesian parliamentary delegation, which was visiting Prague at his invitation, on the 16th.

During the meeting, the two sides held talks. Prime Minister Široký answered questions raised by the guests regarding the economic, cultural, and social life of Czechoslovakia.

On the same day, the Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, Mr. Fierlinger, hosted a luncheon for the delegation members. The day before, Mr. Fierlinger had also received the Indonesian guests.

In the afternoon of the 16th, the guests visited the Škoda Stalingrad Machine Manufacturing Factory in Prague.

### 西罗基总理接见印度尼西亚国会代表

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    西罗基总理接见印度尼西亚国会代表
    新华社布拉格17日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基16日接见了应邀到布拉格访问的印度尼西亚国会代表团。
    在接见的时候，宾主双方进行了交谈。西罗基总理回答了客人们提出的有关捷克斯洛伐克的经济、文化和社会生活的问题。
    同日，捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格设午宴招待代表团人员。在前一天，费林格还接见了印度尼西亚客人。
    16日下午，客人们在布拉格参观了斯克德斯大林格勒机器制造工厂。
