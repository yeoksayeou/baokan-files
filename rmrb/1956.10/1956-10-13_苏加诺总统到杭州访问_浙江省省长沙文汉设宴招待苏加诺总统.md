Title: President Sukarno Visits Hangzhou; Zhejiang Governor Sha Wenhan Hosts a Banquet for President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: President Sukarno and accompanying high-ranking Indonesian officials concluded their visit to Shanghai and flew to Hangzhou on a special plane on the morning of the 2nd. Accompanying them were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Zhuoru, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen, and Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijopranoto.

Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who was in Shanghai, went to the airport to see them off.

President Sukarno's special plane took off at 9:30 AM and arrived in Hangzhou at 10:15 AM. Zhejiang Governor Sha Wenhan, First Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Jiang Hua, Hangzhou Mayor Wu Xian, Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region Lieutenant General Lin Weixian, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Zhejiang Province welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

Over 100,000 people in Hangzhou greeted the distinguished guests today. Women were dressed in festive attire, and colorful arches were erected along the streets. The people of Hangzhou were all jubilant at the arrival of the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 12th: President Sukarno toured West Lake this afternoon for nearly two hours.

Accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Zhejiang Governor Sha Wenhan, the President boarded a tour boat from Pinghu Qiuyue (Autumn Moon over the Calm Lake) and toured Santanyinyue (Three Pools Mirroring the Moon) and Huagangguanyu (Viewing Fish at Flower Harbor).

At Santanyinyue, a nine-turn bridge aroused the President's keen interest. He walked back and forth on the bridge, carefully observing and studying it, praising the bridge's architecture as very poetic.

At Huagangguanyu, the President stood on a stone bridge to admire the goldfish and learned about the methods of cultivating goldfish.

President Sukarno was warmly welcomed by tourists everywhere in West Lake. When leaving Santanyinyue, a group of touring students surrounded him and enthusiastically cheered and paid tribute to the President. The President asked the students to sing a song, and the students immediately sang "People of the World are of One Mind." The President welcomed the students to sing another, and the students sang "The East is Red." At Huagangguanyu, President Sukarno was surrounded by a group of kindergarten children, and the President sat on the lawn with the children and took pictures.

Before touring West Lake, President Sukarno and his entourage also visited the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory. The workers of this factory presented President Sukarno with one hundred portraits of the President woven by themselves.

President Sukarno visited the entire production process of the factory and asked many questions about production. He expressed his happiness when he saw workers in a production workshop embroidering a large portrait of him, and he smiled and applauded with the workers.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 12th: Zhejiang Governor Sha Wenhan hosted a banquet tonight in honor of President Sukarno and the accompanying high-ranking Indonesian officials. Vice Premier Chen Yi and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen, who accompanied President Sukarno on his visit to Hangzhou, also attended.

Also present were First Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Jiang Hua, Hangzhou Mayor Wu Xian, Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region Lieutenant General Lin Weixian, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

At the end of the banquet, Governor Sha Wenhan presented President Sukarno with Zhejiang's famous specialties: boxwood carvings, Qingtian stone carvings, brocade satin, silk woven West Lake scenery, and Longjing tea.

After the banquet, President Sukarno watched the Zhejiang Yue Opera "Xiao Gu Xian," the Shao Opera "Havoc in Heaven," and the Wu Opera "Broken Bridge."

### 苏加诺总统到杭州访问  浙江省省长沙文汉设宴招待苏加诺总统

1956-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统到杭州访问
    浙江省省长沙文汉设宴招待苏加诺总统
    据新华社电　苏加诺总统和随行的印度尼西亚高级官员结束了在上海的访问，二日上午乘专机前往杭州。陪同的有国务院副总理陈毅、外交部礼宾司司长王倬如、中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托。
    正在上海的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄到机场欢送。
    苏加诺总统乘坐的专机在九时三十分起飞。在十点十五分到达杭州。浙江省省长沙文汉、中共浙江省委员会第一书记、中国人民政治协商会议浙江省委员会主席江华、杭州市市长吴宪、浙江军区司令员林维先中将和浙江省各民主党派各人民团体负责人都到机场上欢迎贵宾。
    今天杭州市有十多万群众迎接贵宾，妇女们都穿上了节日的盛装，街道上搭起了一座座彩色牌楼，全杭州市的人民都为贵宾们的到来而欢欣鼓舞。
    新华社杭州12日电　苏加诺总统今天下午在西湖上游览了将近两个小时。
    总统由陈毅副总理和浙江省省长沙文汉陪同，由平湖秋月乘上游船，到三潭印月和花港观鱼游览。
    在三潭印月，一座九曲桥引起了总统浓厚的兴趣。他在这座桥上走来走去，仔细观看研究，称赞这座桥的建筑很有诗意。
    在花港观鱼，总统站在一座石桥上欣赏了这里的金鱼，并了解了培养金鱼的方法。
    苏加诺总统在西湖各处，受到游人的热情欢迎。在离开三潭印月的时候，一群游览的学生围上来热情地向总统欢呼致敬。总统要学生们唱一支歌，学生们马上唱了“全世界人民心一条”。总统欢迎学生们再来一个，学生们又唱了一个“东方红”。在花港观鱼，苏加诺总统被一群幼儿园的孩子们包围住，总统和孩子们一起坐在草坪上照了像。
    在游西湖以前，苏加诺总统和他的随行人员还参观了杭州都锦生丝织厂。这个工厂的工人们把自己织成的一百幅总统像送给了苏加诺总统。
    苏加诺总统参观了这个工厂的全部生产过程，询问了许多有关生产的问题。他在一个生产车间里看到工人们正在绣制他的巨幅像的时候表示很高兴，他微笑着同工人们一起鼓掌。
    新华社杭州12日电　浙江省省长沙文汉今天晚上设宴招待苏加诺总统和随行的印度尼西亚高级官员。陪同苏加诺总统来杭州参观的陈毅副总理、中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇也出席作陪。
    出席作陪的还有中共浙江省委第一书记、中国人民政治协商会议浙江省委员会主席江华，杭州市市长吴宪，浙江军区负责人林维先中将和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人。
    宴会结束时，沙文汉省长向苏加诺总统赠送了浙江著名特产品黄杨木雕、青田石刻、织锦缎、丝织西湖风景、龙井茶叶等。
    宴会后，苏加诺总统观看了浙江越剧“小姑贤”，绍剧“闹天宫”和婺剧“断桥”。
