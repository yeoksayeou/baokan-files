Title: The New Face of Uzbekistan: Rapid Industrial Development, Complete Elimination of Illiteracy, and the Hungry Steppe Will Become the Largest Cotton Producing Area
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tashkent, 13th dispatch from TASS: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Uzbekistan Communist Party, Mukhitdinov, said: Now Uzbekistan has dozens of giant enterprises capable of producing metals, agricultural machinery, and textile machinery, etc. In agriculture, Uzbekistan's cotton production is now equal to the total production of Brazil, Pakistan, Turkey, and Iran. The production of silkworm cocoons is more than one-half greater than the combined production of Brazil, France, Turkey, Iran, Greece, Spain, Lebanon, Indochina, and the Belgian Congo. The republic's bast fiber crop production is second only to India, ranking second in the world.

Mukhitdinov made these remarks in a report at the Uzbekistan Intellectuals' Congress held in Tashkent. He said that illiteracy has been eliminated forever in Uzbekistan. Uzbekistan has now implemented seven years of universal education, established many primary and secondary schools, and opened thirty-four universities and many secondary technical schools. There are several thousand cultural and educational institutions. Uzbekistan's intellectuals are a large force. This force includes more than 25,000 engineers and technicians, 10,000 agronomists, breeders, and water conservancy workers, 24,000 doctors and medical workers, 60,000 teachers, and about 6,000 scientific workers. The proportion of doctors to the total population in Uzbekistan is higher than in major capitalist countries such as Britain, France, and Japan.

Mukhitdinov's report mainly discussed the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the tasks of Uzbekistan's intellectuals. When talking about the tasks of the Sixth Five-Year Plan, he said that during these five years, Uzbekistan's total industrial output value will increase by about 1.6 times. Various agricultural sectors, especially cotton planting, must be developed rapidly. The total cotton output in 1960 should reach 3.7 million tons. The time is not far off when the Hungry Steppe will be transformed into the largest cotton producing area.

He emphasized that intellectuals play a significant role in solving all these tasks. Then he talked about the specific tasks of the intellectuals.

Mukhitdinov talked about historical research. While affirming some valuable work, he also pointed out that many historical issues of Uzbekistan have not been adequately clarified from a Marxist-Leninist standpoint. There are serious shortcomings in the study of the modern situation in the East. He requested that the Uzbekistan Academy of Sciences establish scientific ties with the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Academies of Sciences of the various republics, and the Academies of Sciences of China, India, Pakistan, Iran, and other Asian countries. Mukhitdinov analyzed the achievements and shortcomings of ideological work in the republic and the importance of conducting internationalist education for the working people.

### 乌兹别克的新面貌  工业迅速发展文盲已全部消灭  饥饿草原将变成最大的棉产区

1956-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    乌兹别克的新面貌
    工业迅速发展文盲已全部消灭
    饥饿草原将变成最大的棉产区
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社塔什干讯：乌兹别克共产党中央委员会第一书记穆希金诺夫说：现在乌兹别克拥有数十座巨型企业，能够生产金属、农业机器和纺织机器等等。农业方面，现在乌兹别克的棉花产量，等于巴西、巴基斯坦、土耳其和伊朗产量的总和。蚕茧的产量比巴西、法国、土耳其、伊朗、希腊、西班牙、黎巴嫩、印度支那、比属刚果的产量总和还要多二分之一以上。共和国韧皮纤维作物的产量仅次于印度，居世界第二位。
    穆希金诺夫是在塔什干举行的乌兹别克知识分子代表大会上作报告时说这些话的。他说，乌兹别克的文盲永远消灭了。现在乌兹别克已经实施了七年普及教育，建立了许多中小学校，开办了三十四个高等学校和许多中等技术学校。文教机关共有好几千个。乌兹别克的知识分子是一支庞大的队伍。这支队伍里面有二万五千多名工程师和技术人员，一万名农学家、饲养员和水利工作者，二万四千名医师和医务工作者，六万名教师和六千名左右科学工作人员。乌兹别克的医生在总人口中的比例比英、法、日等大资本主义国家还高。
    穆希金诺夫的报告主要谈苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议和乌兹别克知识分子的任务。他在谈到第六个五年计划的任务时说，在这五年中，乌兹别克工业总产额将增加到一点六倍左右。各个农业部门，尤其是植棉业要迅速地发展。1960年的棉花总产量要达到三百七十万吨。把饥饿草原变成为最大的棉产区已经为时不远了。
    他着重指出，知识分子在解决这一切任务方面，具有很大作用。接着他谈到了知识分子的具体任务。
    穆希金诺夫谈到了历史研究问题。他在肯定一些有价值的工作的同时，还指出有许多乌兹别克的历史问题还没有从马克思列宁主义的立场来加以应有的阐明。在研究现代的东方情况方面有着严重的缺点，他要求乌兹别克科学院同苏联科学院，各加盟共和国科学院以及同中国、印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗和亚洲其他国家的科学院之间建立科学联系。穆希金诺夫分析了共和国中的思想工作的成绩和缺点以及对劳动人民进行国际主义教育的重要。
