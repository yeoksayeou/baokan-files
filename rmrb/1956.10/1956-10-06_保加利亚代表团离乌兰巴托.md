Title: Bulgarian Delegation Leaves Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 5th: The Bulgarian delegation headed by Vulko Chervenkov, after a two-day friendly visit to Mongolia, left Ulaanbaatar by plane this morning.

　　Mongolian Party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Sambu, and others, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia, went to the airport to see them off.

### 保加利亚代表团离乌兰巴托

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚代表团离乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托5日电　以于哥夫为首的保加利亚代表团在蒙古进行了两天的友好访问以后，已经在今天上午乘飞机离开乌兰巴托。
    蒙古党和政府的领导人泽登巴尔、桑布等，以及各国驻蒙古的使节都到机场欢送。
