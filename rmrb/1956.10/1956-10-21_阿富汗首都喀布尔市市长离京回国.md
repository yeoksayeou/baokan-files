Title: Mayor of Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan, Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Dr. Sohel, the Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, who was visiting China at the invitation of the Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, took a plane back to his country this morning.
    Dr. Sohel arrived in Beijing on the 15th and visited hospitals, factories, and schools. He also toured the Summer Palace, Beihai Park, Badaling, the Ming Tombs and other scenic spots, and held discussions with the heads of many departments of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

### 阿富汗首都喀布尔市市长离京回国

1956-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首都喀布尔市市长离京回国
    据新华社20日讯　应北京市市长彭真的邀请来中国访问的阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士，今天清晨乘飞机回国。
    索海尔博士在15日到达北京，先后参观了医院、工厂、学校，游览了颐和园、北海、八达岭、明十三陵等名胜古迹，并且和北京市人民委员会许多部门负责人进行了座谈。
