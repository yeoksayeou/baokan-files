Title: Ministry of Agriculture Awards 1955 Agricultural Production Increase Models: Second Batch of 113 Awarded Units and Individuals
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Ministry of Agriculture recently issued a notice to award the second batch of 113 units and individuals who were models of agricultural production increase in 1955 with flags, certificates, medals, and bonuses.

Among the recipients of this batch, there are the famous agricultural labor model Geng Changsuo's Wugong Township Agricultural Production Cooperative (Raoyang County, Hebei Province) and other older cooperatives, as well as the Wang Yukun Agricultural Production Cooperative (Anping County, Hebei Province) formed by three poor peasant households mentioned by Chairman Mao in his report "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation," and other new cooperatives. There is also the Yunguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jieyang County, Guangdong Province (director Lin Yancheng), which achieved large-scale high-yield rice and created the highest national record for rice yield per unit area. In addition, there is the Second Platoon of the 2nd Company of the 19th Regiment of the Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army (platoon leader Liu Xuefo), which achieved large-scale high-yield cotton and created the highest national record for cotton yield per unit area. The Yunguang Agricultural Production Cooperative planted 102.9 mu of double-cropping rice, with an average yield of 1277.58 jin per mu; including 2 mu of high-yield fields, with an average yield of 2012.6 jin per mu. Liu Xuefo's platoon planted 491.58 mu of cultivated land entirely with cotton, with an average yield of 550 jin of seed cotton per mu; including 1.05 mu of high-yield land, with an average yield of 1392.86 jin of seed cotton per mu. The achievements of Dong Xin, Chen Gengtao, and others from the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute in researching 666 insecticide have made important contributions to agricultural production increase and have also been rewarded.

This batch of awarded units and individuals was mainly reported by the agricultural departments of fourteen provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities, including Liaoning, Hebei, Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, Xinjiang, Jiangsu, Fujian, Henan, Hubei, Guangdong, Guizhou, Yunnan, and Beijing, and approved by the Ministry of Agriculture. In addition, there are units additionally reported by the agricultural departments of Anhui, Zhejiang, and Jiangxi provinces, units directly under the Ministry of Agriculture and their staff, as well as units affiliated with the Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army. These units and individuals include state-owned farms, sheep breeding farms, agricultural machinery and tractor stations, agricultural technology extension stations, agricultural experiment station breeding groups, agricultural college practice farms, military reclamation farms, high-yield counties, townships, villages, agricultural, animal husbandry, special product, and fishery production cooperatives and mutual aid groups, traditional Chinese veterinary medicine groups, and individual farmers.

### 农业部奖励1955年度农业增产模范  第二批受奖单位和个人共一百十三个

1956-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    农业部奖励1955年度农业增产模范
    第二批受奖单位和个人共一百十三个
    新华社18日讯　农业部最近发出通知，对1955年度农业增产模范第二批一百一十三个单位和个人，分别给予奖旗、奖状、奖章和奖金。
    在这一批受奖者中，有著名的农业劳动模范耿长锁领导的五公乡农业生产合作社（河北省饶阳县）和其他老社，还有毛主席在“关于农业合作化问题”报告中提到的三户贫农组织的王玉坤农业生产合作社（河北省安平县）等新社，有获得水稻大面积丰产并创造全国水稻单位面积产量最高纪录的广东省揭阳县云光农业生产合作社（社主任林炎城），还有获得棉花大面积丰产并创造全国棉花单位面积产量最高纪录的人民解放军新疆军区生产建设兵团十九团二连二排（排长刘学佛）。云光农业生产合作社种植双季连作水稻一百零二点九亩，平均每亩产一千二百七十七点五八斤；内有丰产田二亩，平均每亩产二千零一十二点六斤。刘学佛排四百九十一点五八亩耕地全部种植棉花，平均每亩产籽棉五百五十斤；内有丰产地一点零五亩，平均每亩产籽棉一千三百九十二点八六斤。华北农业科学研究所董新、陈耕陶等八人研究六六六杀虫药剂的成就，对农业增产有重要贡献，也受到了奖励。
    这一批受奖的单位和个人，主要是由辽宁、河北、陕西、甘肃、青海、新疆、江苏、福建、河南、湖北、广东、贵州、云南、北京等十四个省、自治区和市的农业部门报请农业部核准的。此外，还有安徽、浙江、江西三省农业部门补报的单位，农业部的直属单位和其中的工作人员，以及人民解放军新疆军区生产建设兵团所属的单位。这些单位和个人包括国营农场、种羊场、农业机器拖拉机站、农业技术推广站、农业试验站育种组，农业院校的实习农场，军垦农场，丰产县、乡、村、农业、畜牧、特产、渔业等生产合作社和互助组，中兽医组和个体农民。
