Title: Tianjin Pingju Opera Troupe Performs in Kaesong, Hosts Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th. According to the Korean Central News Agency from Kaesong: The Tianjin Pingju Opera Troupe from China, which is currently visiting Korea for performances, performed for the members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Kaesong on October 2nd.
    Personnel from the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission also attended the performance.

### 天津评剧团在开城演出  招待中立国监察委员会

1956-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    天津评剧团在开城演出
    招待中立国监察委员会
    据新华社平壤4日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：正在朝鲜作访问演出的中国天津市评剧团，10月2日在开城为中立国监察委员会的委员们表演。
    军事停战委员会朝中方面人员也出席观看了这次演出。
