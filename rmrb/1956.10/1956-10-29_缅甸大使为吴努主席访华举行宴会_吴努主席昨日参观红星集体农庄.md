Title: Burmese Ambassador Holds Banquet for Chairman U Nu's Visit to China; Chairman U Nu Visits Red Star Collective Farm Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th — The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife, held a banquet at noon today for the visit to China of U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and his wife.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Association Zhao Puchu, and others attended the banquet.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the banquet.

Children from Burma, China, and various embassies also attended the banquet.

The banquet took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

At the end of the banquet, Chairman U Nu, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other guests went to the garden to watch the children play happily.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th — U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and other Burmese guests, visited the Red Star Collective Farm in the suburbs of Beijing today.

After listening to a comprehensive introduction to the Red Star Collective Farm, Chairman U Nu said that he was very satisfied that the houses built by the farm for the workers were not only for the workers to live in, but also for the workers' parents and the parents of their wives. He said that such a situation did not exist in Western countries.

Chairman U Nu, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Zhang Qingtai, Director of the Cooperative Business Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture, carefully visited the Red Star Collective Farm's seed selection machine, seeder, dairy farm, vegetable greenhouses, staff dormitories, Red Star Township Clinic, Red Star Township Middle School classes, and the Tenth Central Primary School in Nanyuan District of Beijing, among other places.

In the evening, Chairman U Nu and other Burmese guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, watched the fairy tale play "Malan Flower" performed by the China Children's Art Theater.

When Chairman U Nu and Premier Zhou Enlai entered the theater, more than a thousand young audience members in the theater stood up, cheering and applauding warmly to welcome them. During the intermission, representatives of the young audience presented red scarves and toys to Chairman U Nu's son and daughter, and gave gifts to other Burmese children attending the evening event. The China Children's Art Theater presented Chairman U Nu with a set of performance stills from "Malan Flower."

At the end of the performance, Chairman U Nu presented flower baskets to the young actors, and his son and daughter also presented small flower baskets to the young actors. Finally, Chairman U Nu and others, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, went on stage to meet with the young actors and took a group photo together.

### 缅甸大使为吴努主席访华举行宴会  吴努主席昨日参观红星集体农庄

1956-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸大使为吴努主席访华举行宴会
    吴努主席昨日参观红星集体农庄
    新华社28日讯　缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和夫人，今天中午为缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和夫人访问中国举行宴会。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理、外交部副部长章汉夫、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初等人出席了宴会。
    各国驻华使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    参加宴会的还有缅甸、中国和各国使馆的儿童。
    宴会在亲切友好的气氛中进行。
    宴会结束时，吴努主席、周恩来总理和其他客人们走到花园里看孩子们欢乐游戏。
    新华社28日讯　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和缅甸其他客人，今天参观了北京郊区红星集体农庄。
    吴努主席听了关于红星集体农庄情况的全面介绍后说，农庄给工人修建的房屋，不仅给工人住，连工人的父母和他妻子的父母也给他们住，这一点使他非常满意。他说，像这样的情况在西方国家是没有的。
    吴努主席由中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和农业部合作事业管理局局长张庆泰陪同，仔细参观了红星集体农庄的选种机、播种机、奶牛场，种植蔬菜的温室，职工宿舍，红星乡门诊部，红星乡中学班和北京市南苑区第十中心小学等。
    晚上，吴努主席和缅甸其他客人，由周恩来总理陪同，观看了中国儿童剧院演出的童话剧“马兰花”。
    当吴努主席同周恩来总理等步入剧场的时候，在剧场内的一千多名小观众都站起来，欢呼着热烈鼓掌欢迎。在休息的时候，小观众的代表向吴努主席的儿子和女儿赠送了红领巾和玩具，并向出席晚会的缅甸其他小朋友赠送了礼品。中国儿童剧院把一套“马兰花”的演出剧照送给吴努主席。
    演出结束的时候，吴努主席向小演员们赠送了花篮，他的儿子和女儿也分别向小演员们赠送了小花篮。吴努主席等最后由周恩来总理陪同，到台上同小演员们会见，并在一起合影。
