Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Mayor of Kabul, the Capital of Afghanistan; Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Reception to Host Distinguished Guests
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Premier Zhou Enlai received Dr. Sohel, the Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, on the evening of the 18th.

Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor, and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present at the reception was Samad, the Afghan Ambassador to China.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a reception at noon today to host Dr. Sohel, the Mayor of Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

Among those attending the reception were Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayors of Beijing; Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture; Fu Lianzhang, Deputy Minister of Health; and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

Samad, the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, also attended today's reception.

### 周总理接见阿富汗首都喀布尔市市长  北京市彭真市长举行酒会招待贵宾

1956-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿富汗首都喀布尔市市长
    北京市彭真市长举行酒会招待贵宾
    新华社19日讯　国务院总理周恩来在18日晚接见了阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士。
    接见时在座的有北京市市长彭真，副市长吴晗，外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋。
    接见时在座的还有阿富汗驻我国大使萨马德。
    新华社19日讯　北京市市长彭真今天中午举行酒会招待阿富汗王国首都喀布尔市市长索海尔博士。
    出席酒会的有北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁，文化部副部长郑振铎，卫生部副部长傅连暲以及北京市各界人士。
    阿富汗王国驻我国大使萨马德也出席了今天的酒会。
