Title: Menon and Nasser Hold Fifth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 20th - Indian Minister Without Portfolio, Menon, arrived in Cairo on October 19th from London and held talks with Egyptian President Nasser that day. This is their fifth meeting since Egypt nationalized the Suez Canal Company on July 26th.

Indian sources in Cairo say that Menon will inform Nasser of the results of his contacts with the British, American, and French diplomatic authorities, and the views of these three countries on resolving the Suez Canal issue.

### 梅农和纳赛尔进行第五次会谈

1956-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    梅农和纳赛尔进行第五次会谈
    据新华社20日讯　开罗消息：印度不管部部长梅农10月19日从伦敦到了开罗，当天同埃及总统纳赛尔进行了会谈。这是从埃及在7月26日把苏伊士运河公司收归国有以来他们进行的第五次会谈。
    开罗印度人士说，梅农将会把他同英、美、法外交当局接触的结果和这三个国家对解决苏伊士运河问题的看法告诉纳赛尔。
