Title: Delegation of Japanese Diet Members Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 21st - The delegation of the Japanese Diet Members League for the Promotion of Sino-Japanese Trade, led by Masanosuke Ikeda, a group of twelve people, left Guangzhou by train this morning to return home. Two other members of the delegation had already returned home yesterday.
    The delegation of the Japanese Diet Members League for the Promotion of Sino-Japanese Trade arrived in our country on September 28th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团回国

1956-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团回国
    新华社广州21日电　以池田正之辅为首的日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团一行十二人，今天早晨乘火车离开广州回国。另有团员二人已经在昨天先期回国。
    日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团是应中国人民外交学会邀请在9月28日到达我国访问的。
