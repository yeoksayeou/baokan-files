Title: Bulgaria and Israel Sign Barter Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- TASS reports: Bulgaria and Israel signed a one-year barter protocol in Sofia yesterday.

According to the protocol, Bulgaria will supply Israel with chemical industrial products, pharmaceuticals, timber, agricultural products, tobacco, and building materials, etc.; Israel will supply Bulgaria with citrus fruits, automobile tires, steel pipes, chemical industrial products, and pharmaceuticals, etc.

### 保以签订换货议定书

1956-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    保以签订换货议定书
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社报道：保加利亚和以色列昨天在索非亚签订了一项为期一年的换货议定书。
    根据议定书，保加利亚将供给以色列化学工业产品、药品、木材、农产品、烟草和建筑材料等；以色列将供给保加利亚柑类、汽车外胎、钢管、化学工业产品和药品等。
