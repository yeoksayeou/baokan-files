Date: 1956-10-05

Shaanxi Province's Bumper Cotton Harvest

    Shaanxi Province's farmers are busily picking new cotton. According to the current situation, it is estimated that the province's more than 4.7 million mu of cotton fields will produce an average of more than 60 jin of unginned cotton per mu, an increase of more than 16 jin over the original plan.

    Female members of the Dengta No. 1 Agricultural Cooperative in Dali, Shaanxi Province, are harvesting cotton in the fields.

    Members of the Dengta No. 1 Agricultural Cooperative are drying cotton in order to sell it to the state.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Jiang Guoxian photographed.

### 陕西省棉花丰收（图片）

1956-10-05
第3版()
专栏：

    陕西省棉花丰收
    陕西省农民正在紧张地采摘新棉。根据目前情况估计，全省四百七十多万亩棉田，平均每亩可产皮棉六十斤以上，比原计划增产十六斤以上。
    陕西省大荔灯塔第一农业社的女社员正在田间收棉花。
    灯塔第一农业社的社员正在把棉花晒干，以便卖给国家。
                                        新华社记者　姜国宪摄
