Title: French "Combat" Says: U.S. Policy in Asia and Africa Aims to Replace the Position of Britain and France
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 6

    French "Combat" Says:
    U.S. Policy in Asia and Africa Aims to Replace the Position of Britain and France
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th Dispatch from Paris, 10th: French newspapers believe that the attempts by some American officials to make people feel that the US policy is not of a colonialist nature is just propaganda, the purpose of which is only to cover up the rampant activities of American colonialists.
    "Combat" published today an article by its New York correspondent, which pointed out that in South Vietnam, the Middle East and many other regions, "American policy is equally subject to selfish American economic interests." American monopolists are trying to become the "heirs" of Britain and France in Asia and Africa.
    "Combat" wrote that one of the most important objectives of this policy is to ensure that the United States "seizes huge raw material resources in these new territories (and later occupies the market) to satisfy the huge appetite of American industry." In addition, the United States is also attempting to occupy new military strategic positions in these territories.

### 法国“战斗报”说：  美国在亚非地区的政策  目的在取代英法的地位

1956-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    法国“战斗报”说：
    美国在亚非地区的政策  目的在取代英法的地位
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社巴黎10日讯：法国报纸认为，某些美国官方人士企图使人感到美国的政策并没有殖民主义性质的做法只是一种宣传，其目的只不过是为了掩饰美国殖民者的猖狂活动。
    “战斗报”今天刊登了它驻纽约记者的一篇文章，文章指出，在南越、中东和其他许多地区，“美国的政策都同样是服从于美国自私的经济利益”。美国垄断资本家企图成为英法在亚洲和非洲的“继承人”。
    “战斗报”写道，这种政策的最重要的目的之一就是保证美国“在这些新领土上攫取巨大的原料资源（而以后便是占有市场），以便满足美国工业的大胃口”。此外，美国还企图占据这些领土上的新的军事战略阵地。
