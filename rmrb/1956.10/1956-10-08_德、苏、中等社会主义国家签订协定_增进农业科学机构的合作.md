Title: Germany, the Soviet Union, China, and Other Socialist Countries Sign Agreement to Enhance Cooperation Among Agricultural Science Institutions
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th – According to a report by the German News Agency, scientists from the German Democratic Republic, the Soviet Union, China, and other People's Democratic countries held a meeting in Berlin from October 3rd to 5th to discuss the coordination of scientific work in agriculture and forestry, and signed an agreement on further cooperation among agricultural science institutions in socialist countries.

    The agreement stipulates cooperation on the following issues: reviewing the qualifications of scientific cadres; exchanging research methods in various fields of agricultural science; mutually informing each other of new instruments, tools, and equipment, and providing support in obtaining these items; expanding the exchange of publications and information, and exchanging materials conducive to the development of scientific research. The meeting agreed on a calendar of meetings for 1957-1958 to avoid overlapping of meetings among friendly countries and to ensure good preparation for international conferences in friendly countries. The meeting also decided to convene a coordination meeting every two years.

    The agreement also stipulates inviting agricultural science institutions from other friendly countries to participate in this agreement.

### 德、苏、中等社会主义国家签订协定  增进农业科学机构的合作

1956-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    德、苏、中等社会主义国家签订协定
    增进农业科学机构的合作
    新华社柏林7日电　据德意志通讯社报道：民主德国、苏联、中国和其他人民民主国家的科学家从10月3日到5日在柏林举行了会议，讨论农业和林业方面科学工作的配合的问题，并且签订了一项社会主义国家农业科学机构进一步合作的协定。
    协定规定在下列问题上进行合作：审定科学干部的资格；交换在农业科学各个不同领域中的研究方法；互相通知新的仪器、工具、设备的情况，并且在获得这些物品方面给予支持；扩大出版物和情报的交换以及交换有助于科学研究工作发展的材料。会议商妥了一个1957年—1958年的会议日历，以避免各个友好国家之间会议的重叠，并使友好国家的各个国际会议能够有很好的准备。会议还决定每两年召开一次配合会议。
    这个协定还规定邀请其它友好国家的农业科学机构来参加这个协定。
