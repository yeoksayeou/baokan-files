Title: All-China Youth Federation and Communist Youth League Central Committee Host Banquet to Welcome Japanese Youth and Women Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The All-China Youth Federation (ACYF) and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League (CNDYL) held a banquet at noon today to welcome the Japanese Youth and Women Delegation visiting China.
    Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the ACYF, on behalf of the ACYF and the CNDYL Central Committee, extended a warm welcome to the Japanese youth and women delegation who had traveled thousands of miles to strengthen Sino-Japanese youth friendship.
    Ida Tamotsu, the head of the delegation, expressed his gratitude to the hosts for their warm reception on behalf of all the members. He expressed his hope that through the delegation's visit, the friendly relations between the people and youth of China and Japan would be further enhanced, and the early restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Japan would be promoted.
    Before the banquet, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the ACYF, Vice Chairman Liu Xiyuan, and Secretaries of the CNDYL Central Committee Hu Yaobang and Hu Keshi received all the members of the delegation.

### 青联和团中央举行宴会欢迎日本青年和妇女代表团

1956-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    青联和团中央举行宴会欢迎日本青年和妇女代表团
    据新华社6日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会今天中午举行宴会，欢迎日本青年和妇女访华代表团。
    全国青联主席廖承志代表全国青联和青年团中央委员会向不远千里而来、为加强中日青年友好的日本青年与妇女访华代表团表示热烈的欢迎。
    代表团团长井田保代表全体团员向主人的热情招待表示感谢，他希望通过代表团的这次访问，进一步增进中日两国人民和青年的友好关系，促进中日邦交的早日恢复。
    宴会前，全国青联主席廖承志、副主席刘西元、青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、胡克实曾接见了代表团全体团员。
