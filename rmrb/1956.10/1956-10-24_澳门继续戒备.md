Title: Macao Remains on Alert
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 23rd: Macao News: Macao's security authorities continue to step up security measures.

Since relevant parties have confirmed that undesirable elements have infiltrated Macao, attempting to follow the example of the Kowloon riots and launch riots in Macao, people from all walks of life here continue to be vigilant. After nightfall, many people are unwilling to go out.

### 澳门继续戒备

1956-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    澳门继续戒备
    据新华社广州23日电　澳门消息：澳门治安当局继续加紧戒备。
    由于有关方面已证实有不良分子潜入澳门，企图步九龙暴乱事件后尘，在澳门发动骚乱，此间各方人士仍继续提高警惕。入夜后，许多人不愿出门。
