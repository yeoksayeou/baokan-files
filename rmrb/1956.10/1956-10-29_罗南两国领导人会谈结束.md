Title: Romanian-Yugoslav Leaders' Talks Conclude
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, the talks between the Yugoslav leaders and the Romanian leaders concluded on October 27th in Brioni.

    The Brioni talks were held on the 27th, following the Belgrade talks. After the conclusion of this meeting, Josip Broz Tito, General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, signed a declaration on relations between the two parties on the same evening.

    On the same evening, a communiqué was also issued regarding the visit of the Romanian government delegation to Yugoslavia. In addition, some economic agreements and cultural cooperation agreements between Romania and Yugoslavia were also signed on the same evening.

### 罗南两国领导人会谈结束

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    罗南两国领导人会谈结束
    新华社28日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫领导人同罗马尼亚领导人之间的会谈10月27日在布里俄尼结束。
    布里俄尼的会谈是继贝尔格莱德会谈之后在27日举行的。这次会谈结束以后，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托和罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治在当天晚上签署了两党关系的宣言。
    在这一天晚上，还发表了关于罗马尼亚政府代表团访问南斯拉夫的公报。此外，有关南罗两国的一些经济协定和文化合作协定也在这天晚间签字。
