Title: Socialists and Communists Serve as Heads of State in San Marino
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 3rd: TASS San Marino dispatch: The newly appointed Regents (i.e., heads of state) of the Republic of San Marino – Socialist Ceccoli and Communist Belluzzi – held their inauguration ceremony in San Marino on October 1st. Their term of office is six months, from October 1, 1956, to March 31, 1957. Diplomatic representatives of various countries to the Republic of San Marino attended the inauguration ceremony.

### 社会党人和共产党人担任圣马力诺国家首脑

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    社会党人和共产党人担任圣马力诺国家首脑
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社罗马讯：圣马力诺消息：圣马力诺共和国新任摄政者（即国家首脑）——社会党人切科耳和共产党人贝尔汭迪尼10月1日在圣马力诺举行了就职仪式。他们的任期为半年，即从1956年10月1日到1957年3月31日为止。各国驻圣马力诺共和国的外交代表参加了就职仪式。
