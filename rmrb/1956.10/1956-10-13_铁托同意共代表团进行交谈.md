Title: Tito Agrees to Talks with Communist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Tito, received the Italian Communist Party delegation headed by Longo in Belgrade on the 10th and held talks with the representatives.

    Rankovic and Vukmanović, members of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, also participated in the talks. Tito also had lunch with the delegation.

    On the 11th, the delegation arrived in Zagreb, the capital of the Republic of Croatia, from Belgrade. The delegation will hold talks with members of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Croatia.

### 铁托同意共代表团进行交谈

1956-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托同意共代表团进行交谈
    新华社12日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托10日在贝尔格莱德接见了以隆哥为首的意大利共产党代表团，并且同代表们进行了交谈。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员兰科维奇和伏克曼诺维奇等也参加了这次谈话。铁托并同代表团人员共进午餐。
    代表团11日从贝尔格莱德到达克罗地亚共和国首都萨格勒布。代表团人员将同克罗地亚共产主义者联盟中央委员会委员们进行会谈。
