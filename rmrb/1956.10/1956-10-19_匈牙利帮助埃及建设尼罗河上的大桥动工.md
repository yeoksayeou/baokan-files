Title: Hungary Helps Egypt Build Bridge Over Nile Begins Construction
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th. A Hungarian bridge construction company is building a bridge for the Egyptian government in Helwan on the Nile River. This bridge will be one of the largest bridges in the world, and it will be able to carry both trains and cars.

　　The complex geological and hydrological conditions of the Nile River make bridge construction extremely difficult. Therefore, when the Egyptian government decided to build a bridge in Helwan, it announced an open international tender. Bridge construction companies from fifteen countries participated in the bidding. A design by a Hungarian company won first place. As a result, the Egyptian government concluded a bridge construction contract with this company.

### 匈牙利帮助埃及建设尼罗河上的大桥动工

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利帮助埃及建设尼罗河上的大桥动工
    据新华社布拉格17日电　匈牙利一家桥梁建筑公司正在尼罗河上赫卢安地方为埃及政府建筑一座大桥。这座桥是世界上最大的桥梁之一，桥上将可以通行火车和汽车。
    尼罗河复杂的地质和水文条件使得在这条河上建筑桥梁的工作极为困难。因此，当埃及政府决定在赫卢安建筑一座桥梁时，它宣布进行公开的国际投标。十五个国家的桥梁建筑公司参加了投标。匈牙利的一个公司的设计图获得了第一。结果，埃及政府就同这家公司缔结了桥梁建筑工程合同。
