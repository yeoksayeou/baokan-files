Title: Bulganin Meets with Afghan Prime Minister to Exchange Views on Soviet-Afghan Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - TASS reports from Moscow on the 19th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shepilov, met today in the Kremlin with the Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Mohammad Daoud, and the First Deputy Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Ali Mohammad.

The meeting took place in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. During the meeting, the two sides exchanged views on issues related to Soviet-Afghan relations.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - TASS reports from Moscow: On October 18, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, held a banquet in the Kremlin to entertain the Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Mohammad Daoud. At the banquet, the hosts and guests raised their glasses to congratulate the enhanced friendship between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan.

Ali Mohammad, the First Deputy Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Abdul Hakim, the Minister of Interior, and others who accompanied Mohammad Daoud on his visit to the Soviet Union also attended the banquet.

Leaders of the Communist Party and the government of the Soviet Union also attended the banquet.

### 布尔加宁同阿富汗首相会谈  就苏阿两国关系问题交换意见

1956-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁同阿富汗首相会谈
    就苏阿两国关系问题交换意见
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫今天在克里姆林宫同阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德、阿富汗第一副首相阿里·穆罕默德会谈。
    会谈在友好和恳切的气氛中进行。在会晤时双方就有关苏联和阿富汗的两国关系问题交换了意见。
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：10月18日，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德。在宴会上，宾主双方举杯祝贺苏联和阿富汗两国增进友好。
    随同穆罕默德·达乌德到苏联访问的阿富汗第一副首相阿里·穆罕默德、内政大臣阿卜杜勒·哈基姆等也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有苏联共产党和政府的领导人。
