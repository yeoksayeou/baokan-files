Title: National Cycling Competition Concludes; Three National Records Broken
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The National Cycling Competition, which began on October 14th in Beijing, concluded today. The final results for each event are as follows:

    Men: 1,500 meters, first place: Sun Shihai (PLA), with a time of 2 minutes 26 seconds; 5,000 meters, first place: Qian Huaiyu (PLA), with a time of 8 minutes 27.9 seconds (breaking the 1955 national record of 8 minutes 30 seconds); 10,000 meters, first place: Qian Huaiyu, with a time of 18 minutes 35.7 seconds.

    Women: 1,500 meters, first place: Wu Shuhua (Liaoning), with a time of 2 minutes 48.1 seconds (breaking the 1955 national record of 2 minutes 59.6 seconds); 3,000 meters, first place: Zhang Shengzhu (Beijing), with a time of 5 minutes 56.3 seconds (breaking the 1955 national record of 6 minutes 14.4 seconds); 5,000 meters, first place: Cui Shufang (Hebei), with a time of 10 minutes 21 seconds.

### 全国自行车比赛结束  三项成绩破全国纪录

1956-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    全国自行车比赛结束
    三项成绩破全国纪录
    新华社18日讯　从10月14日开始在北京举行的全国自行车比赛今天全部结束，各项决赛成绩如下：
    男子：一千五百公尺第一名孙世海（解放军），成绩二分二十六秒；五千公尺第一名钱怀玉（解放军），成绩八分二十七秒九（打破1955年八分三十秒的全国纪录）；一万公尺第一名钱怀玉，成绩十八分三十五秒七。
    女子：一千五百公尺第一名吴淑华（辽宁），成绩二分四十八秒一（打破1955年二分五十九秒六的全国纪录）；三千公尺第一名张胜竹（北京），成绩五分五十六秒三（打破1955年六分十四秒四的全国纪录）；五千公尺第一名崔淑芳（河北），成绩十分二十一秒。
