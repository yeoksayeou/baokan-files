Title: Poles Love Our Acrobatics
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 4th—The Shanghai Acrobatic Troupe of China left Poland on the evening of October 2nd for Hungary after performing in Poland for two months.

The Shanghai Acrobatic Troupe performed sixty-six shows in Poland, with a total audience of over 174,000 people.

The performances in Warsaw were the most outstanding. The acrobatic troupe performed a total of forty-eight shows in Warsaw for over a month, with an audience of over 130,000 people.

### 波兰人热爱我国杂技

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰人热爱我国杂技
    据新华社华沙4日电　中国上海杂技团在波兰作了两个月演出后，已经在10月2日晚上离开波兰前往匈牙利。
    上海杂技团在波兰演出六十六场，观众共达十七万四千多人。
    在华沙的演出成绩最为特出。杂技团在华沙共演出了四十八场，为时一个多月，观众达十三万人以上。
