Title: All-China Journalists Association Welcomes Pakistani Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: The All-China Journalists Association held a reception this afternoon to welcome the Pakistani journalists who are accompanying Prime Minister Suhrawardy on his visit to China.

    Pakistani Public Relations Officer Abdul Gayoom, Chief Photographer Merchant, Pakistan United Press representative Safdar Qureshi, and Pakistan Broadcasting Corporation representative Ahmed Hassan Shah were invited to attend the reception.

    Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, extended a heartfelt welcome to the Pakistani journalists.

    Abdul Gayoom expressed his hope that Chinese journalists would visit Pakistan in the near future. He said that through personal contact, this relationship could develop into friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

    Heads of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as of Beijing newspapers and radio stations, also attended the reception.

### 中国新闻工作者联谊会欢迎巴基斯坦记者

1956-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中国新闻工作者联谊会欢迎巴基斯坦记者
    据新华社20日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会今天下午举行招待会，欢迎随苏拉瓦底总理访问中国的巴基斯坦记者。
    巴基斯坦公共关系官阿卜杜勒·加尧姆，主任摄影师麦钱特，巴基斯坦联合通讯社代表萨弗达尔·库赖希和巴基斯坦广播电台代表艾哈迈德·哈桑·夏赫应邀出席了招待会。
    联谊会副会长王芸生对巴基斯坦新闻工作者表示衷心的欢迎。
    阿卜杜勒·加尧姆希望中国记者不久的将来到巴基斯坦去访问。他说，通过个人间的接触，可以使这种关系发展成为两国人民的友谊。
    外交部新闻司和北京各报刊以及广播电台负责人也出席了招待会。
