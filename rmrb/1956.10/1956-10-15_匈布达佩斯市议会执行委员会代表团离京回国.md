Title: Hungarian Budapest City Council Executive Committee Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Hungarian Budapest City Council Executive Committee delegation left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. The delegation was visiting China at the invitation of Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen. Those who went to the airport to see them off today included Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu, Jia Tingsan and others.

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Skradan, was also at the airport to see them off.

After arriving in Beijing on September 29, the delegation participated in China's National Day celebrations and visited the Yongding River Water Diversion Project, Peking University, Beijing Children's Hospital, Qinghe Woolen Mill and other places, and toured the Ming Tombs, the Great Wall, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven and other places of interest.

On October 6, the delegation left Beijing to visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan and other places, and visited Guangzhou at the invitation of Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang.

### 匈布达佩斯市议会执行委员会代表团离京回国

1956-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    匈布达佩斯市议会执行委员会代表团离京回国
    新华社14日讯　匈牙利布达佩斯市议会执行委员会代表团今天上午乘飞机离北京回国。这个代表团是应北京市市长彭真邀请来中国访问的。今天到机场欢送的有北京市市长彭真，副市长张友渔、贾庭三等人。
    到机场欢送的还有匈牙利驻我国大使斯克拉丹。
    代表团在9月29日来北京后，曾参加了我国国庆典礼，并先后参观了永定河引水工程，北京大学，北京儿童医院，清河制呢厂等地方，游览了明十三陵、长城、故宫、天坛等名胜古迹。
    10月6日代表团离开北京到上海、杭州、武汉等地参观，并且应广州市市长朱光的邀请访问了广州。
