Title: What Exactly Are Britain and France Up To? Egyptian Newspapers Condemn the Two Countries for Delaying Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Cairo news: The "Republic" newspaper condemned Britain and France on October 22nd for deliberately delaying direct talks with Egypt by demanding that Egypt propose new solutions to the Suez Canal issue.

The newspaper said: "We may ask, what exactly is the intention of Britain and France in insisting that Egypt put forward so-called concrete proposals, instead of immediately sitting down with Egyptian representatives at a round table to seek a satisfactory solution to this problem in a sincere manner?"

### 英法究竟居心何在？  埃及报纸谴责两国拖延谈判

1956-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    英法究竟居心何在？
    埃及报纸谴责两国拖延谈判
    据新华社23日讯　开罗消息：“共和国报”10月22日谴责英国和法国以要求埃及提出新的解决苏伊士运河问题的建议这种办法来故意拖延不同埃及举行直接的会谈。
    这家报纸说：“我们也许要问，英法不立即同埃及的代表们坐在一张圆桌旁边来以诚恳的态度设法使这个问题得到满意的解决，而坚持要埃及提出所谓具体的建议，这究竟是用意何在呢？”
