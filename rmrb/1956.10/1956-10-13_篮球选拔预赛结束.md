Title: Basketball Selection Preliminary Round Ends
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The preliminary round of the basketball selection competition for the Chinese Olympic team has concluded. The men's basketball teams participating in this selection competition will compete in the finals on the 14th and 15th.

In the final two group stage round-robin matches held tonight, the men's basketball team of the PLA stationed in Shenyang defeated their last opponent, the Hong Kong men's basketball team, with a score of 107-57, securing first place in their group. The Shenyang men's basketball team defeated the Beijing men's basketball team with a score of 86-73, taking second place in their group.

On the 14th and 15th, these four teams will compete against teams with the same ranking from the other group.

### 篮球选拔预赛结束

1956-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    篮球选拔预赛结束
    据新华社12日讯　中国奥林匹克运动队篮球选拔赛预赛全部结束，参加这次选拔赛的男子篮球队将在14和15两日进行决赛。
    在今晚举行的最后两场分组循环赛中，解放军驻沈阳部队男子篮球队以一百零七比五十七战胜了最后一个对手——香港男子篮球队，获得了分组的第一名；沈阳男子篮球队以八十六比七十三赢了北京男子篮球队，获得了分组的第二名。
    14日和15日，这四个队将要和另一个组中同名次的队进行比赛。
