Title: The CPC Central Committee Issues Slogans for Celebrating the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 24th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has issued slogans for celebrating the 39th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The CPC Central Committee first hailed in its slogans, "Long live the 39th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!" and extended fraternal greetings to the people of all countries struggling for peace, democracy, and socialism!

The CPC Central Committee said in its slogans, "Working people of all countries, hold high the banner against the danger of war and for peace and cooperation among all nations! Strive for the reduction of armaments and armed forces!"

Demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction! May atomic energy serve the peaceful progress of humanity!

Wish the working people strengthen their unity, and the Communists, Socialists, and all progressive forces strengthen their cooperation and struggle for peace, democracy, and national independence!

Constantly strengthen the great and friendly alliance of socialist countries, and develop and consolidate the fraternal friendship between the peoples of these countries!

Extend fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who are victoriously building socialism in their own country!

Long live the People's Republic of China!

Wish the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China—a powerful factor for world peace—grow and prosper day by day!

Extend fraternal greetings to the working people of the Polish People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, the Romanian People's Republic, the Bulgarian People's Republic, the Albanian People's Republic, and the Mongolian People's Republic, who are selflessly striving for the further development of their national economies and cultures, for the continuous improvement of the people's living standards, and for peace and the construction of socialism! Extend fraternal greetings to the people of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, who are building socialism! Extend fraternal greetings to the heroic people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam,who are striving to restore and further develop their national economies, for peace, and for the realization of the national unification of their countries on a democratic basis!

Long live the German Democratic Republic—the stronghold of all peace-loving forces in Germany struggling against the revival of militarism, for the rapprochement of the two Germanys, and for the unification of their homeland on a peaceful and democratic basis!

The CPC Central Committee then extended greetings in its slogans to the great Indian people who are striving for the progress of their motherland and for peace in Asia and the world, to the Indonesian and Burmese people who are striving for peace and the consolidation of their national independence, and to the Egyptian people who are heroically defending the sovereignty and independence of their motherland!

The CPC Central Committee said in its slogans, "Long live the friendship and cooperation of the people of Great Britain, the United States, France, and the Soviet Union in the cause of easing international tensions, peaceful coexistence among nations, and ensuring lasting world peace!"

Wish the friendly cooperation between the Soviet people and the people of Italy, Finland, and Austria consolidate and develop, and wish the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and Iceland grow increasingly solid and develop.

The CPC Central Committee also paid tribute to the Japanese people who are bravely opposing the revival of militarism and striving for the peaceful, democratic, and independent development of their country, to the people of Asia and Africa who have broken the chains of colonial oppression and are striving for peace, freedom, and the strengthening of their national independence, and to the people of colonial and dependent countries who are resisting imperialist oppression and fighting for their freedom and national independence!

The CPC Central Committee said, "Long live the Leninist foreign policy pursued by the Soviet Union—the policy of peaceful coexistence among countries with different social systems, the policy of maintaining and consolidating peace, and the policy of developing economic and cultural ties with all countries!"

The CPC Central Committee called on the soldiers of the Soviet Army and Navy to tirelessly improve their military and political knowledge, master modern combat equipment and weapons, and enhance vigilance and combat readiness, and called on the workers in all sectors of the Soviet national economy, Soviet women, young men and women, Communist Party members, and Communist Youth League members to strive for the victorious completion of the Sixth Five-Year Plan!

### 苏共中央发布庆祝十月革命节口号

1956-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央发布庆祝十月革命节口号
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会发布了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年的口号。
    苏共中央在口号中首先欢呼伟大的十月社会主义革命三十九周年万岁，并且向各国为和平、民主和社会主义而奋斗的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    苏共中央在口号中说，各国的劳动人民，更高地举起反对战争危险和争取各国人民之间的和平和合作的旗帜！争取实现裁减军备和武装部队！
    要求禁止原子武器、氢武器和其它大规模毁灭性武器！使原子能为人类的和平进步服务！
    祝劳动人民加强团结，共产党人、社会党人以及所有进步力量加强合作，为和平、民主和民族独立而奋斗！
    不断加强社会主义阵营各国伟大的友好联盟，发展和巩固这些国家人民之间的兄弟友谊！
    向正在自己国家胜利地建设社会主义的伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    祝世界和平的强大因素——苏、中两国人民牢不可破的友谊和合作日益繁荣！
    向忘我地为进一步发展国民经济和文化、为不断提高人民生活水平、为和平和建设社会主义而奋斗的波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！向正在建设社会主义的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的人民致兄弟的敬礼！向正在为恢复和进一步发展国民经济、为和平、为在民主基础上实现自己国家的全国统一而奋斗的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国的英雄人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    为反对军国主义复活、为两个德国的接近、为在和平和民主基础上统一自己祖国而奋斗的全德国爱好和平的力量的堡垒——德意志民主共和国万岁！
    苏共中央在口号中接着向正在为自己祖国的进步、为亚洲和全世界的和平而奋斗的伟大的印度人民，向正在为和平和巩固本国民族独立而奋斗的印度尼西亚人民和缅甸人民，向英勇保卫自己祖国的主权和独立的埃及人民致以敬礼！
    苏共中央在口号中说，英国、美国、法国和苏联的人民在争取缓和国际紧张局势和各国和平共处、争取保障世界持久和平的事业中的友谊和合作万岁！
    祝苏联人民同意大利、芬兰和奥地利人民的友好合作巩固和发展，祝苏联同瑞典、挪威、丹麦、冰岛之间的友好关系日益巩固和发展。
    苏共中央还向英勇反对军国主义复活、为自己国家的和平的、民主的和独立的发展而奋斗的日本人民致敬，向打碎了殖民主义压迫的锁链、正在为和平、自由和加强自己民族独立而奋斗的亚洲和非洲的人民，向正在反抗帝国主义压迫、为自己的自由和民族独立而奋斗的殖民地和附属国的人民致敬！
    苏共中央说，苏联执行的列宁的外交政策——社会制度不同的各国和平共处的政策、维护和巩固和平的政策、发展同各国的经济联系和文化联系的政策万岁！
    苏共中央号召苏联陆海军战士坚持不懈地增进自己的军事知识和政治知识，掌握现代的战斗装备和武器，提高警惕性和战斗准备，号召苏联国民经济各部门的劳动者，苏联妇女、男女青年、共产党员和共青团员为胜利完成第六个五年计划而奋斗！
