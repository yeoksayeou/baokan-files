Date: 1956-10-28

Work Plan for Commemorating the 90th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
(Passed by the First Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth on October 26, 1956)
I. Holding a Commemorative Meeting:
  (I) The National Committee of the CPPCC and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC will jointly hold a commemorative meeting in the CPPCC Auditorium on November 11. Scope of participation:
    (1) Members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing, representatives of the National People's Congress in Beijing; responsible persons of the central committees of various democratic parties and the national federations of people's organizations; members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Beijing.
    (2) Invite ambassadors and their wives of various countries to China, Chinese and foreign journalists, and other relevant people to participate in the commemorative meeting.
    (3) Invite foreign guests from Asia, Africa, Europe, the Americas, and Australia to Beijing to participate in the commemorative meeting.
  (II) The CPPCC committees of various provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities will hold commemorative meetings on November 12.
  (III) Notify overseas Chinese to hold commemorative meetings locally.
  (IV) Invite overseas Chinese and Kuomintang members abroad to return to China to participate in commemorative meetings in various places.
II. Paying Respects at Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, Sun Yat-sen Hall, and Sun Yat-sen's Former Residence:
  (I) The National Committee of the CPPCC will hold a ceremony of paying respects at the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Biyun Temple at 3:00 PM on November 12. Participants will include members of the preparatory committee, members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing, members of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and representatives of other democratic parties and people's organizations.
    The National Committee of the CPPCC will organize a delegation (composed of representatives from the government, parties, and organizations) to visit the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum in Nanjing on November 12.
    Visit Mr. Sun Yat-sen's residence when he passed away at Tieshizi Hutong, Beijing.
  (II) The Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC will announce that people from all walks of life in Beijing can freely visit the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Biyun Temple on November 11.
  (III) The arrangements for paying respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, Sun Yat-sen Hall, and Sun Yat-sen's Former Residence in Nanjing, Guangzhou, Shanghai, and other places will be planned by the respective local authorities.
  (IV) Broadcast to Kuomintang members in Taiwan and scattered abroad, calling on them to return and pay respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum.
III. Publish a selection of Sun Yat-sen's works and collect Sun Yat-sen's documents and cultural relics.
IV. Various newspapers will successively publish commemorative articles, publish commemorative special editions on November 12, and select some of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's works for publication in the newspapers. Various magazines will publish commemorative articles.
V. Hold an exhibition, based on the original materials of the Beijing Library and the calligraphy of Mr. Sun Yat-sen sent by Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, plus other exhibits, to be exhibited in the Sun Yat-sen Hall of the Sun Yat-sen Park in Beijing. The recording of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's speech will be reproduced as a new film and played at the exhibition.
VI. Produce newsreels and commemorative films.
VII. Mint commemorative medals.
VIII. Issue commemorative stamps.
IX. Various democratic parties, people's organizations, universities in Beijing, and local organizations of democratic parties and people's organizations in Tianjin, Shenyang, Xi'an, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu and other eight cities, as well as institutions of higher learning, may organize articles and hold speeches, report meetings, symposiums, etc., according to their specific circumstances.
X. Write a brief biography of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
XI. Publish Mr. Sun Yat-sen's calligraphy, and print Mr. Sun Yat-sen's handwritten Outline of National Reconstruction before November 11.

### 纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年工作计划

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年工作计划
    （1956年10月26日孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会第一次会议通过）
    一、举行纪念大会：
      （一）政协全国委员会和政协北京市委员会于11月11日在政协礼堂联合举行纪念大会。参加范围：
      （1）政协全国委员会在京委员、全国人民代表大会在京代表；各民主党派中央、各人民团体总会负责人；政协北京市委员会委员、北京市各民主党派、各人民团体负责人。
      （2）邀请各国驻华大使及夫人、中外记者和其他有关人士参加纪念大会。
      （3）邀请亚非、欧洲、美洲、澳洲各国的外宾来京参加纪念大会。
      （二）政协各省、自治区、市委员会于11月12日举行纪念大会。
      （三）通知国外华侨就地分别举行纪念会。
      （四）邀请华侨和在国外的国民党人回国分别参加各地纪念大会。
    二、参谒中山陵、中山堂和中山故居：
      （一）政协全国委员会于11月12日下午三时，在碧云寺中山堂举行参谒仪式，参加者为筹备委员会委员、政协全国委员会在京委员、民革中央委员以及其他民主党派和人民团体的代表。
    政协全国委员会组织代表团（由政府、党派和团体的代表组成）于11月12日到南京参谒中山陵。
    参谒北京铁狮子胡同中山先生逝世时的住所。
      （二）政协北京市委员会公告北京市各界人士于11月11日到碧云寺中山堂自由参谒。
      （三）南京、广州、上海等地参谒中山陵、中山堂和中山故居的办法，由各地自行计划。
      （四）向台湾和散居国外的国民党人广播，号召他们回来参谒中山陵。
    三、出版孙中山选集，征集中山先生的文献、文物。
    四、各报陆续发表纪念文章，11月12日出纪念专刊，并选择一些中山先生的著作在报上发表。各杂志刊载纪念文章。
    五、举办展览会，以北京图书馆原有资料和宋庆龄副主席送来的中山先生遗墨为基础，再加其他展品，于北京中山公园中山堂展出。并将中山先生讲话录音，翻印新片，在展览会上播放。
    六、摄制新闻纪录片和纪念影片。
    七、铸制纪念章。
    八、发行纪念邮票。
    九、各民主党派、各人民团体、北京市各大学和天津、沈阳、西安、南京、上海、武汉、广州、成都等八个城市的民主党派、人民团体地方组织及高等院校，可根据各自的具体情况，分别组织文章，举行演讲会、报告会、座谈会等。
    十、编写中山先生传略。
    十一、编印中山先生遗墨，并在11月11日以前印出中山先生手书的建国大纲。
