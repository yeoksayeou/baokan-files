Title: Egypt, Turkey, and South Vietnam Withdraw from Olympic Football Tournament
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, October 26th - The Olympic Committee of the International Football Federation officially announced on the 24th that, following Hungary, Egypt, Turkey, and South Vietnam have also decided not to participate in the Olympic Games football tournament.

### 埃及土耳其和南越不参加奥运会足球赛

1956-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及土耳其和南越不参加奥运会足球赛
    据新华社墨尔本26日电　国际足球联合会的奥林匹克委员会在24日正式宣布：继匈牙利之后又有埃及、土耳其、南越三单位决定不参加奥林匹克运动会的足球夺标赛。
