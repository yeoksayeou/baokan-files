Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The following is the full text of the statement issued by the Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation:

The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation paid a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. As the delegation departs from the capital of the People's Republic of China on October 6, 1956, to return to Indonesia, the following statement is issued:

I. The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation would like to express its gratitude to:

1. The Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China for inviting the Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation to pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China;

2. The Chairman of the People's Republic of China, the central and local governments of the People's Republic of China—they gave the delegation opportunities to hold discussions and exchange views with leaders in administrative, military, various parties, mass organizations, state-owned enterprises, joint state-private enterprises, and other fields;

3. The Chinese people who welcomed the delegation with warm and fraternal feelings.

II. The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation believes that:

1. The Government of the People's Republic of China is working hard for the happiness of its people;

2. The Government of the People's Republic of China is carrying out large-scale industrial construction;

3. The people of the People's Republic of China are working hard to build their country.

III. The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation hopes that the friendly relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia will be closer than ever before. This is in line with the resolutions and spirit of the Asian-African Conference held in Bandung and will promote lasting world peace.

  The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation
    Head of Delegation: Kusnan
    Deputy Head of Delegation: Hasan Basri
    Deputy Head of Delegation: Saran Ridwan
    Deputy Head of Delegation: Yusuf Ajitorop
      October 6, 1956, Beijing

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团发表声明

1956-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团发表声明
    新华社6日讯　印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团的声明，全文如下：
    印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团应中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长阁下的邀请，到中华人民共和国进行了友好访问。代表团在1956年10月6日离开中华人民共和国首都返回印度尼西亚之际，发表声明如下：
    一，印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团谨向：
    1，邀请印度尼西亚共和国国会派遣友好代表团访问中华人民共和国的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长表示感谢；
    2，中华人民共和国主席、中华人民共和国中央及地方政府——他们给予代表团机会和行政方面的、军事方面的领导人、各党派、群众团体的领导人、国营企业的领导人、公私合营企业的经理以及其他方面的领导人进行了座谈和交换了意见——表示感谢；
    3，向以热烈的和兄弟般的感情欢迎代表团到来的中国人民表示感谢。
    二，印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团认为：
    1，中华人民共和国政府正在为其人民的幸福而努力工作；
    2，中华人民共和国政府正在进行着大规模的工业建设；
    3，中华人民共和国的人民正在努力建设着他们的国家。
    三，印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团希望：中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国之间的友好关系将比以往更加紧密，这是符合在万隆召开的亚非会议的决议和精神的，并会促进世界的持久和平。
  印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团
    团长：库斯南
    副团长：哈桑·巴斯里
    副团长：萨兰·里德旺
    副团长：优素福·阿吉托罗普
      1956年10月6日于北京
