Title: Soviet Ukrainian Dance Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Soviet Ukrainian National Dance Troupe, a group of eighty people, returned to Beijing by train tonight.

The dance troupe returned to Beijing after visiting and performing in various locations. Over the past two months, the dance troupe performed thirty-two shows in Guangzhou, Shanghai, Wuxi, Wuhan, Xi'an, Nanning, Zhengzhou, and Hangzhou, including eight shows specifically for workers and farmers, with a total audience of more than 184,000 people.

The dance troupe will give several public performances in Beijing this time and hold a closing ceremony for its visit and performances in China.

Vice Minister of Culture Zheng Zhenduo, representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China were all at the station tonight to welcome the dance troupe.

### 苏联乌克兰舞蹈团回到北京

1956-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联乌克兰舞蹈团回到北京
    新华社9日讯　苏联乌克兰国家舞蹈团一行八十人今天晚上坐火车回到北京。
    这个舞蹈团是在各地访问演出以后回到北京的。两个多月以来，舞蹈团在广州、上海、无锡、武汉、西安、南宁、郑州和杭州演出了三十二场，其中专门为工人和农民演出了八场，观众共有十八万四千多人。
    舞蹈团这次将在北京公演几场，并且举行在中国访问演出的闭幕式。
    文化部副部长郑振铎，中苏友好协会的代表，以及苏联驻华大使馆人员等今天晚上都到车站欢迎舞蹈团。
