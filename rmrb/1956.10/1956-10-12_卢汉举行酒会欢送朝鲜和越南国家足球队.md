Title: Lu Han Holds Reception to Bid Farewell to North Korean and Vietnamese National Football Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th — Lu Han, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, held a reception in Beijing today to bid farewell to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea National Football Team and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam National Football Team.

    Lu Han said in his speech at the reception: Through this friendly football tournament between the three countries, the football players of our China, Korea, and Vietnam have exchanged experiences and techniques, and further consolidated and developed our fraternal friendship.

    Lu Han said: The courageous and tenacious fighting spirit of the Korean National Football Team and the Vietnamese National Football Team, who are not arrogant in victory and not discouraged in defeat, is worthy of learning by every member of the Chinese football team.

    The leader of the Korean National Football Team, Zheng Yihong, and the leader of the Vietnamese National Football Team, Song Yueqing, also gave speeches respectively.

    After the reception, the football teams of China, Korea, and Vietnam exchanged gifts and showed a film.

    The Korean National Football Team and the Vietnamese National Football Team will leave Beijing for home on the 12th.

### 卢汉举行酒会欢送朝鲜和越南国家足球队

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    卢汉举行酒会欢送朝鲜和越南国家足球队
    新华社11日讯　体育运动委员会副主任卢汉，今日在北京举行酒会欢送朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家足球队和越南民主共和国国家足球队。
    卢汉在酒会上致词，他说：经过这次三国足球友谊比赛，我们中、朝、越三个国家的足球队员相互交流了经验和技术，并且进一步巩固和发展了兄弟般的友谊。
    卢汉说：朝鲜国家足球队和越南国家足球队的胜不骄、败不馁的勇敢、顽强的战斗精神，是值得中国足球队的每一个队员学习的。
    朝鲜国家足球队领队郑义鸿和越南国家足球队领队宋曰庆也分别讲了话。
    酒会后，中国、朝鲜和越南三国足球队相互赠送了礼物，并且放映了电影。
    朝鲜国家足球队和越南国家足球队将在12日分别离京回国。
