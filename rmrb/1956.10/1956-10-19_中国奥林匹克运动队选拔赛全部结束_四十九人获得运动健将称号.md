Title: Chinese Olympic Team Trials Conclude; Forty-Nine Athletes Awarded Title of Master of Sports
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 4

The Chinese Olympic team trials concluded after twelve days of intense competition, with a closing ceremony held yesterday afternoon at the Xianongtan Stadium in Beijing.

At the closing ceremony, the names of forty-nine Masters of Sports were announced. The performances of these athletes have reached the standard for the Master of Sports level in the "Athlete Grade System Regulations (Draft)" published by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.

Gymnasts: Bao Naijian, Wang Huize, Yang Jianmin, Qiao Zhenying, Wu Shude, Han Yi, Lu Enchun, Xu Renjie, Chen Xiaozhang (female), Qi Yufang (female), Lan Yalan (female), Guo Keyu (female), Zhang Feizi (female), Zheng Fuson (female), Jiang Xiuzhi (female), Sun Xiaozhen (female).

Weightlifters: Chen Jingkai, Lin Zhongying, Yin Guohua, Huang Qianghui, Zhu Hongquan, Zhao Qingkui, Li Baiyu.

Swimmers: Lin Jinzhu, Mu Xiangxiong, Qi Lieyun, Chen Yunpeng, Wang Qiangli, Xu Zhixiang.

Track and Field Athletes: Liu Jingren, Li Zhonglin, Zhou Lianli, Li Dapei, Ma Xianglong, Cai Yishu, Xu Tiquan, Yu Jinan, Ma Changlu, Wen Yunzhen (female), Zheng Fengrong (female), Shi Baozhu (female), Fu Xueyan (female).

Shooting Athletes: Zhao Shanjun, Zhang Hong, Li Jie, Dong Ruiguang, Zhang Zhiming, Cao Jingfen (female), Fu Junjie (female). (The organizing committee of the Chinese Olympic Team Shooting Trials and the 1956 National Shooting Championships announced on the 17th that the shooting results of Pan Guilin, Xu Yuhua, Chen Jipeng, Hu Zhi, and Li Suping, which had also reached the Master of Sports level, were invalid because their shooting results reached the Master of Sports level before the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission published the Athlete Grade System Regulations (Draft) and cannot be retroactively counted.)

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the closing ceremony and personally awarded the Masters of Sports badges to the athletes who received this glorious title.

Ma Yuehan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, delivered a speech at the closing ceremony, encouraging all athletes not to be arrogant, to learn with humility, to consolidate their existing achievements, and to strive for even higher achievements.

After the closing ceremony, the final match of the football trials was held, with the United Team Red winning against the United Team White with a score of two to one. (Photos attached)

### 中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛全部结束  四十九人获得运动健将称号

1956-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛全部结束
    四十九人获得运动健将称号
    本报讯　中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛，经过十二天的紧张比赛后，已经全部结束，昨日下午在北京先农坛体育场举行了闭幕式。
    在闭幕式上公布了四十九名运动健将的名单，这些运动员的成绩已经达到了体育运动委员会公布的运动员等级制度条例（草案）中运动健将级的标准。
    体操运动员：鲍乃健、王辉泽、杨健民、乔振英、吴树德、韩毅、陆恩淳、徐仁杰、陈孝彰（女）、戚玉芳（女）、蓝亚兰（女）、郭可愚（女）、张飞子（女）、郑馥荪（女）、姜秀芝（女）、孙孝贞（女）。
    举重运动员：陈镜开、林仲英、殷国华、黄强辉、朱鸿全、赵庆奎、李白玉。
    游泳运动员：林锦珠、穆祥雄、戚烈云、陈运鹏、王强立、徐致祥。
    田径运动员：刘敬仁、李中林、周连立、李大培、马翔龙、蔡艺墅、徐体权、于金安、马长路、文蕴珍
  （女）、郑凤荣（女）、石宝珠（女）、傅雪雁（女）。
    射击运动员：赵善俊、张鈜、李杰、董瑞光、张志明、曹靖芬
  （女）、傅钧洁（女）。（中国奥林匹克运动队射击选拔赛和1956年全国射击运动大会组织委员会17日宣布的潘桂林、徐雨华、陈纪彭、胡智、李素萍的射击成绩也达到运动健将级标准无效，因为他们的射击成绩是在体育运动委员会公布运动员等级制度条例（草案）以前达到运动健将级标准的，不能追算。）
    周恩来总理出席了闭幕式，并且亲自把运动健将的证章颁发给获得这个光荣称号的运动员。
    中华全国体育总会副主席马约翰在闭幕式上致辞，他勉励全体运动员不要骄傲，虚心学习，巩固现有的成绩，并且努力取得更高的成绩。
    闭幕式后举行足球选拔赛的最后一场比赛，联队红队以二比一胜联队白队。（附图片）
