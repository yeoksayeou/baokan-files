Title: The Ministry of Culture Holds Peking Opera and Song & Dance Evening to Welcome Prime Minister Suhrawardi
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. Prime Minister Suhrawardi, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, attended the Peking Opera and Song & Dance Evening held by the Ministry of Culture this evening to welcome Prime Minister Suhrawardi.

The distinguished guests accompanying Prime Minister Suhrawardi on his visit to China also attended the evening.

Also accompanying the distinguished guests in watching the Peking Opera and Song & Dance performance were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Shutong, and responsible persons from various departments of the state organs.

After the performance, Prime Minister Suhrawardi presented flower baskets to the Chinese artists. Prime Minister Suhrawardi, accompanied by Premier Zhou, went on stage to express his gratitude to the Chinese artists.

### 文化部举办京剧歌舞晚会招待苏拉瓦底总理

1956-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部举办京剧歌舞晚会招待苏拉瓦底总理
    新华社21日讯　苏拉瓦底总理由周恩来总理陪同，在今天晚上出席了文化部为欢迎苏拉瓦底总理举行的京剧歌舞晚会。
    随同苏拉瓦底总理访华的贵宾们也出席了晚会。
    陪同贵宾们观看京剧歌舞的，还有国务院副总理贺龙，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通和国家机关各部门负责人。
    演出结束后，苏拉瓦底总理向中国艺术家赠送了花篮。苏拉瓦底总理由周总理陪同登上舞台，向中国艺术家们表示感谢。
