Title: Five Thai Opposition Parties Demand Government Withdraw from Manila Group
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, October 26th: According to TASS, Bangkok, October 26th: Five Thai opposition parties—the Socialist Party, the Economist Party, the Labour Party, the Social Democratic Party, and the Hyde Park Movement Party—held press conferences in recent days, expressing unanimous opposition to the Manila Treaty Organization.

    According to the "Siam Masses" newspaper, the leader of the "Economist" Party, Chote Nuchit, said at a press conference that if the recent statements issued by these five opposition parties are not enough to convince the Thai government that it must withdraw from the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, then these five parties will organize a national referendum on this issue.

### 泰国五个反对党要求政府退出马尼拉集团

1956-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国五个反对党要求政府退出马尼拉集团
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社曼谷26日讯：泰国五个反对党——社会党、经济人党、工党、社会民主党和海德公园运动党在最近几天举行了记者招待会，表示一致反对马尼拉条约组织。
    据“暹罗群众报”报道，“经济人”党的领袖触的努七在记者招待会上发言说，如果这五个反对党在最近所发表的声明还不能说服泰国政府必须退出东南亚条约组织的话，那么这五个政党将就这个问题组织全民投票。
