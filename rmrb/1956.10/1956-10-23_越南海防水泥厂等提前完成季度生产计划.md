Title: Vietnam's Haiphong Cement Plant and Others Complete Quarterly Production Plans Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 21st: According to the Vietnam News Agency: The Mining Bureau of the Vietnamese Ministry of Industry recently announced at a conference that the Haiphong Cement Plant, the Guan Chao Coal Mine in Thai Nguyen Province, and the Nam Phat Phosphate Plant in Thanh Hoa Province have completed their third-quarter plans seven to nineteen days ahead of schedule.
	Over the past three months, some measures have also been taken to improve the lives of workers. The wages of workers at the Haiphong Cement Plant have increased by more than 26%.

### 越南海防水泥厂等提前完成季度生产计划

1956-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南海防水泥厂等提前完成季度生产计划
    据新华社河内21日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南工业部矿务局最近在一次会议上宣布，海防水泥厂、太原省的关潮煤矿和清化省的南发磷酸厂已经提前七天到十九天完成第三季度计划。
    过去三个月来，在改善工人生活方面也采取了一些措施。海防水泥厂工人的工资增加了26％以上。
