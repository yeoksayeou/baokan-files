Title: 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands Opens: Discussions to Focus on Summaries of the CPSU Congress and Party Work
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Amsterdam, 6th dispatch from TASS: The 18th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands opened in Amsterdam on October 5th. According to the agenda, the congress will discuss two issues: the summary of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the work of the Communist Party of the Netherlands.

At the morning session of the congress, the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, Ger Groot, delivered a report on the first item on the agenda.

At the afternoon session, the Organizational Secretary of the Central Committee, Hoekstra, delivered a report on the second item on the agenda.

A discussion then began, with ten delegates speaking.

Before the end of the afternoon session, the chairman of the meeting, Reitte, read out a congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the congress. The message wished the Communist Party of the Netherlands success in uniting its ranks based on the principles of Marxism-Leninism, and wished the Communist Party of the Netherlands further expansion of its ties with the masses of people in the struggle to win the united action of the working class and to strive for peace, democracy, and socialism.

The evening session continued the discussion of the reports by Groot and Hoekstra.

The congress will last for three days.

### 荷兰共产党第十八次代表大会开幕  将讨论苏共代表大会的总结和党的工作

1956-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    荷兰共产党第十八次代表大会开幕
    将讨论苏共代表大会的总结和党的工作
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社阿姆斯特丹讯：荷兰共产党第十八次代表大会10月5日在阿姆斯特丹开幕。根据议程，这次代表大会将讨论两个问题：苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的总结和荷兰共产党的工作。
    荷兰共产党中央委员会总书记格鲁特在代表大会的上午会议上，就第一项议程作了报告。
    在下午会议上，中央委员会组织书记胡克斯特拉就第二项议程作了报告。
    接着开始讨论，有十个代表发了言。
    在下午会议结束前，会议主席雷特宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会给代表大会的贺词。贺词祝荷兰共产党在根据马克思列宁主义的原则团结自己的队伍方面获得成就，并且祝荷兰共产党在争取工人阶级行动一致、争取和平民主和社会主义的斗争中进一步扩大同人民群众的联系。
    晚间会议继续讨论格鲁特和胡克斯特拉的报告。
    代表大会将举行三天。
