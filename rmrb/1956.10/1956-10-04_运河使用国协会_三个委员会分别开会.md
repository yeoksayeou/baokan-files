Title: Three Committees of the "Suez Canal Users Association" Hold Separate Meetings
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 3rd. The organizational committee, the shipping committee, and the finance committee of the "Suez Canal Users Association," which was established on the 1st, held separate meetings on the 2nd to study details. According to Reuters, quoting "authoritative sources at the meeting," the organizational committee's recommendations, which may be submitted to the eighteen-nation plenary meeting for discussion, include the establishment of an executive body and the appointment of a manager. The manager may be Swedish.

### “运河使用国协会”三个委员会分别开会

1956-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    “运河使用国协会”三个委员会分别开会
    据新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：1日成立的“苏伊士运河使用国协会”的组织委员会、航运委员会和财务委员会2日分别开会研究细节问题。据路透社引“会议权威人士”说，可能提交十八国全体会议讨论的组织委员会的建议包括建立一个执行机构和任命一位管理人的建议。管理人可能是瑞典人士。
