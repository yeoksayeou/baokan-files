Title: Minister Fu Zuoyi Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese Water Conservancy Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th—The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Water Conservancy Delegation, headed by the Minister of Water Conservancy and Construction, Tran Dang Khoa, arrived in Beijing by train and plane successively on the 8th and 10th.

This delegation consists of six people, who are here to participate in the scientific and technological exchange conference on the new construction and renovation of irrigation systems to be held in Beijing. After the meeting, they will visit water conservancy projects in various places.

Xinhua News Agency, October 11th—Minister of Water Conservancy Fu Zuoyi hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Vietnamese Water Conservancy Delegation headed by Tran Dang Khoa, Minister of Water Conservancy and Construction of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

At the banquet, Fu Zuoyi and Delegation Leader Tran Dang Khoa spoke successively, and jointly toasted to the development of water conservancy and irrigation in China and Vietnam, to the eternal friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam, and to the prosperity of the peoples of China and Vietnam.

Among those present at the banquet tonight were Vice Minister of Water Conservancy Feng Zhongyun and others.

### 傅作义部长设宴招待越南水利代表团

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    傅作义部长设宴招待越南水利代表团
    据新华社10日讯　以越南民主共和国水利和建筑部部长陈登科为首的水利代表团，已经在8日到10日先后乘火车和飞机到达北京。
    这个代表团一共有六人，他们是来参加即将在北京召开的灌溉系统新建改建科学技术交流会议的。会后并将到各地参观水利工程。
    新华社11日讯　水利部长傅作义今晚举行宴会，欢迎以越南民主共和国水利和建筑部部长陈登科为首的越南水利代表团。越南民主共和国驻中国临时代办周亮也应邀参加了宴会。
    在宴会上，傅作义和代表团团长陈登科等都先后讲了话，并且共同为中、越两国水利灌溉事业的发展，为中、越两国人民永恒的友谊和中、越两国人民的繁荣干杯。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，还有水利部副部长冯仲云等多人。
