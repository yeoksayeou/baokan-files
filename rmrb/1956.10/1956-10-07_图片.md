    The Japanese commodity exhibition opened at the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing on October 6. Speaking on the stage is Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.
    Shozo Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, is speaking.
    Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, cuts the ribbon.
    Guests attending the opening ceremony visit agricultural machinery. (Photographed by our reporter)
