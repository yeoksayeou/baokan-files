Title: Premier Zhou and Chairman U Nu Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th — Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, U Nu, continued their talks this afternoon.
