Title: Chen Shutong and Li Zhucheng Host a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Singapore-Malaya Trade Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Li Zhucheng, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation.

At the banquet, the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation presented silk banners and gifts to the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade respectively.

Among those present at the banquet tonight were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and well-known overseas Chinese figure Chen Jiageng. David Marshall, advisor to the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and former Chief Minister of Singapore, also attended the banquet.

### 陈叔通、李烛尘设宴欢送新加坡马来亚贸易考察团

1956-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    陈叔通、李烛尘设宴欢送新加坡马来亚贸易考察团
    新华社3日讯　中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘今晚举行宴会，欢送新加坡工商业贸易考察团、马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团。
    在宴会上，新加坡工商业贸易考察团和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团，分别向中华全国工商业联合会和中国国际贸易促进委员会献锦旗和赠送礼品。
    出席今晚宴会的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、华侨事务委员会主任何香凝和华侨著名人士陈嘉庚等。新加坡工商业贸易考察团顾问前新加坡首席部长戴维·马歇尔也出席了宴会。
