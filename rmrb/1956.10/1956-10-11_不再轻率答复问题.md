Title: No Longer Responding to Questions Lightly

Author:

Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 2

Response to Criticism

    No Longer Responding to Questions Lightly

    The Design Bureau of the Ministry of Railways writes: The People's Daily published an article entitled "At a Loss" on September 8, criticizing the Technical Section of the Design Bureau of the Ministry of Railways for giving inconsistent answers to the Jilin Railway Bureau regarding the mortar grade used for the foundation engineering of the cultural palace. This criticism is correct. The comrade in charge of replying from our bureau's Technical Section provided a general answer to the question without understanding the actual conditions of the engineering site. After understanding the actual situation, they then provided a different answer, leaving the on-site staff at a loss. Our bureau has now instructed the relevant comrades to pay attention to preventing this shortcoming in the future.

### 不再轻率答复问题

1956-10-11
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    不再轻率答复问题
    铁道部设计总局来信说：9月8日人民日报发表“何所适从”一文，批评铁道部设计总局技术处答复吉林铁路管理局关于文化宫基础工程采用砂浆标号问题，前后不一致。这个批评是正确的。我局技术处负责答复的同志，在未了解工程现场的实际情况之前，即一般地答复问题，等到了解实际情况以后，又提出不同的答复，使现场工作人员无所适从。现在我局已令有关同志今后注意防止这种缺点。
