Title: Soviet Legal Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. A twelve-member Soviet legal workers delegation headed by Mishutin, Deputy Prosecutor General of the Supreme Prosecutor's Office of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.
    Gao Kelin, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Liang Guobin, Deputy Prosecutor General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Shi Liang, Minister of Justice, and Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, were among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    Sudalikov, Cultural Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 苏联法律工作者访华代表团到北京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联法律工作者访华代表团到北京
    新华社30日讯　以苏联最高检察署副总检察长米舒金为首的苏联法律工作者访华代表团一行十二人今日下午到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有最高人民法院副院长高克林、最高人民检察院副检察长梁国斌、司法部部长史良、中苏友好协会副会长邵力子等。
    苏联大使馆文化参赞苏达利柯夫也到机场欢迎。
