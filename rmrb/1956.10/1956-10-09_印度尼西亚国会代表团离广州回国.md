Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th. The twenty-three member Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation, led by Chairman Kusnan, left Guangzhou by train today via Hong Kong to return home. Guangdong Province Governor Tao Zhu, Vice Governor He Ximing, Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang and others went to the station to see them off.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团离广州回国

1956-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团离广州回国
    新华社广州8日电　印度尼西亚国会代表团一行二十三人由团长库斯南率领，今天乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。广东省省长陶铸、副省长贺希明，广州市市长朱光等到车站欢送。
