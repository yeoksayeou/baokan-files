Title: Nasser Condemns Israeli Attack on Jordan, Points Out This Is a New Plan Against Arab Nationalism
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 30th: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser has accused the recent Israeli attacks on Jordan as part of a new plan against Arab nationalism.
　　In a letter from the Egyptian President to the Syrian President, published in Cairo on September 29th, it was also stated that providing weapons to Israel is "tantamount to a hostile act against the entire Arab nation."
　　Cairo's "Al Akhbar" wrote on September 29th that the British Prime Minister and the French Premier agreed to use Israel to implement their plans against Egypt, and they have made arrangements to supply Israel with a large number of weapons.

### 纳赛尔谴责以色列进攻约旦  指出这是反对阿拉伯民族主义的新计划

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔谴责以色列进攻约旦
    指出这是反对阿拉伯民族主义的新计划
    新华社30日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔指责最近以色列对约旦的攻击是一个反对阿拉伯民族主义的新计划的一部分。
    9月29日在开罗发表的一封埃及总统致叙利亚总统的信中还说：对以色列提供武器“无异是对整个阿拉伯国家的敌对行动”。
    开罗“今日消息”9月29日写道，英国首相和法国总理商定要利用以色列来实行他们的反对埃及的计划，他们已经作好供给以色列大量武器的安排。
