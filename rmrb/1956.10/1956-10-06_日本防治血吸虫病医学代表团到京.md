Title: Japanese Medical Delegation for Schistosomiasis Prevention Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th — The Japanese Medical Delegation for Schistosomiasis Prevention arrived in Beijing by train this evening. They are here at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association to exchange medical experiences, according to the friendly agreement signed between the Chinese Medical Association and the Japanese Medical Delegation. The head of the delegation is Yoshitaka Komiya, Director of the Parasitology Department of the National Institute of Preventive Health. The members are: Yoshio Yoshizumi, Professor of Kurume University; Hirohiro Okabe, Professor of Kurume Medical University; Jiro Ito, Professor of Shizuoka University; and Kazuo Ayaragi, staff member of the National Institute of Preventive Health.

### 日本防治血吸虫病医学代表团到京

1956-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日本防治血吸虫病医学代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　日本防治血吸虫病医学代表团今晚乘火车到达北京。他们是根据中华医学会和日本医学代表团签订的友好协议，应中华医学会邀请来中国进行交流医学经验的。代表团团长是日本国立预防卫生研究所寄生虫部部长小宫义孝，团员有：久留米大学教授吉住好夫，久留米医科大学教授冈部浩洋，静冈大学教授伊藤二郎，日本国立预防卫生研究所所员安罗冈一男。
