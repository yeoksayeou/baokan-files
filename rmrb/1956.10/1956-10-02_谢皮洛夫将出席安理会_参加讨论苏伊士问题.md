Title: Shepilov to Attend Security Council to Discuss Suez Issue

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

    Shepilov to Attend Security Council
    to Discuss Suez Issue

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th - TASS Moscow, 30th: In view of the fact that the United Nations Security Council will discuss the Suez issue, the Soviet government has appointed Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov as the Soviet representative to participate in the discussion of this issue. Shepilov will depart for New York in the near future.

### 谢皮洛夫将出席安理会　参加讨论苏伊士问题　　

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫将出席安理会　
    参加讨论苏伊士问题　　
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科30日讯：由于联合国安全理事会将讨论苏伊士问题，苏联政府指派苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫为参加讨论这个问题的苏联代表。谢皮洛夫在最近将动身前往纽约。
