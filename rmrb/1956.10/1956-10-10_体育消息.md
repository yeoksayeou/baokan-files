	Sports News

	Japan Sends 147-Member Delegation to the 16th Olympic Games
	Tokyo Dispatch: The Japanese delegation to the 16th Olympic Games was formed on October 8th. The delegation has a total of one hundred and forty-seven people, with the swimming team having the largest number, accounting for thirty-two people.
	Japan will participate in the Olympic Games in eleven events including swimming, track and field, gymnastics, weightlifting, wrestling, soccer, basketball, rowing, boxing, shooting, and cycling.

	Hungarian Soccer Team Wins Consecutive Victories
	Paris Dispatch: The Hungarian soccer team defeated the French soccer team in Paris on October 7th with a score of two to one. The Hungarian soccer team has recently achieved consecutive victories in several international competitions. On September 16th, the Hungarian national mixed team defeated the Yugoslavian national mixed soccer team in Belgrade. On September 23rd, Hungary's first and second soccer mixed teams defeated the Soviet Union's first and second soccer mixed teams in Moscow and Budapest, respectively. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 体育消息

1956-10-10
第6版()
专栏：体育消息

    体育消息
    日本组成147人的代表团参加十六届奥林匹克运动会
    东京消息：日本参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会的代表团已经在10月8日组成。代表团一共有一百四十七人，其中游泳队人数最多，占三十二人。
    日本将参加奥林匹克运动会的游泳、田径、体操、举重、摔交、足球、篮球、划船、拳击、射击和自行车等十一个项目的比赛。
    匈牙利足球队连战连胜
    巴黎消息：匈牙利足球队10月7日在巴黎以二比一击败法国足球队。匈牙利足球队最近在一些国际比赛中连续取得了胜利。9月16日，匈牙利国家混合队在贝尔格莱德击败南斯拉夫国家混合足球队。9月23日匈牙利的第一足球混合队和第二足球混合队又分别在莫斯科和布达佩斯击败了苏联的第一和第二足球混合队。　（据新华社讯）
