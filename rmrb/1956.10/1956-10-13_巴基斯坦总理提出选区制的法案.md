Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Proposes Constituency System Bill
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 11th October: Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy presented a bill on the constituency system to the National Assembly on October 10th. The bill stipulates that in future general elections, East Pakistan will adopt a joint electorate system, while West Pakistan will adopt a separate electorate system.

    This bill was proposed based on a compromise reached between the ruling People's League and the Republican Party on the evening of October 9th. This compromise reconciled the opinions of the two provincial assemblies. Originally, the East Pakistan Provincial Assembly advocated for the adoption of a joint electorate system throughout the country, while the West Pakistan Provincial Assembly insisted on adopting a separate electorate system nationwide.

    The Muslim League and other elements opposed to the joint electorate system are trying to obstruct the passage of this bill.

### 巴基斯坦总理提出选区制的法案

1956-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理提出选区制的法案
    据新华社喀喇蚩11日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底10月10日在国民议会就选区制问题提出了一项法案，这项法案规定，在未来的大选中，东巴基斯坦采用联合选区制，西巴基斯坦采用单独选区制。
    这个法案是根据执政的人民联盟和共和党在10月9日晚间达成的一项折衷方案提出来的。这个折衷方案调和了两个省议会的意见。原来，东巴基斯坦省议会主张在全国都采用联合选区制，而西巴基斯坦省议会则坚持要在全国范围内采用单独选区制。
    穆斯林联盟和其他反对联合选区制的分子正在设法阻挠这个法案获得通过。
