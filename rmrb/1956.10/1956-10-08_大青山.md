Title: Daqing Mountain
Author: Feng Zi
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 7

    Mountain spring water flows shallowly through the rocky stream,
    Down the mountain, the rocky stream ripples with clear spring water,
    On the mountain peaks, green pines beckon white clouds,
    Those white clouds—netting the blue sky.

    Spring water is the blood of Daqing Mountain,
    Daqing Mountain is the heart of the local anti-Japanese people!
    During the day, the devils looted the villages at the foot of the mountain,
    In the evening, the corpses of the invaders are sacrificed on the ruins.

    People are spreading the stories of the political commissar and his orderly,
    The past of that mythical guerrilla team:
    "Today, the political commissar is our Vice Chairman Yang, (Note)
    His orderly—that unyielding grandpa
    is still managing the horses in the cooperative!"

    The mountain spring water sings its own song,
    The clear spring water below echoes lightly,
    Spring water, sing!
    Your song, with the spring water flowing through the rocky stream,
    Nourishes the land,
    Nourishes the people.

Note: Comrade Yang Zhilin, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, led the people in Daqing Mountain to persist in guerrilla warfare during the Anti-Japanese War.

### 大青山
凤子
1956-10-08
第7版()
专栏：

    大青山
    凤子
    山上泉水浅浅流过石涧，
    山下石涧潺潺荡漾着清泉，
    山峰上青松招唤着白云，
    那白云——网住了蓝蓝的天。
    泉水是大青山的血管，
    大青山是地方抗日人民的心脏！
    白天鬼子洗劫了山下的村庄，
    晚间侵略者的尸身祭奠在废墟上。
    人们争传着政委和他的通讯员的故事，
    那神话般的游击队的往日：
    “今天政委就是我们的杨副主席，（注）
    他的通讯员——那不服老的爷爷
    还在合作社里经管着马哩！”
    山上泉水唱着他自己的歌，
    山下清泉轻快地应和，
    泉水呵！唱吧！
    你的歌声随着流过石涧的泉水
    滋润着土地，
    滋润着人。
    注：内蒙古自治区副主席杨植霖同志，抗日战争时期，领导人民在大青山坚持游击斗争。
