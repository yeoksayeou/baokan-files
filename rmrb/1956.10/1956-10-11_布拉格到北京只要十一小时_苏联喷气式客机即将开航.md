Title: Prague to Beijing in Just Eleven Hours: Soviet Jet Airliner to Begin Service Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. The Soviet TU-104 jet airliner will soon begin service on the Prague-Moscow-Beijing route. The total distance of this route is 8,220 kilometers, and the plane can reach Beijing eleven hours after taking off from Prague. The Prague-Moscow section of this international route is already officially open.

The TU-104 jet airliner is also continuing to open new routes within the Soviet Union. In addition to the Moscow-Irkutsk line, which already has three scheduled flights per week, the Moscow-Tashkent route has been test flown and will officially open on October 17th. Within this month, the Moscow-Tbilisi line and the Moscow-Khabarovsk line will also open.

### 布拉格到北京只要十一小时  苏联喷气式客机即将开航

1956-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    布拉格到北京只要十一小时
    苏联喷气式客机即将开航
    新华社莫斯科10日电　苏联ＴＹ—104喷气式客机不久将在布拉格—莫斯科—北京线上开航。这一航程总共有八千二百二十公里，飞机从布拉格起飞十一小时以后就可以到达北京。在这一国际航线上，布拉格到莫斯科一段已经正式开航。
    ＴＹ—104喷气式客机在苏联国内也在继续开辟新航线。除了莫斯科到伊尔库次克线已经定期每周有三次班机以外，在莫斯科和塔什干之间已经试航并且将在10月17日正式开航。在本月以内，莫斯科—第比利斯线和莫斯科—伯力线也将开航。
