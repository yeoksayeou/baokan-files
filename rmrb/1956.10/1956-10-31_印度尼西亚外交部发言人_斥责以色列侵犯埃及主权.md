Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Condemns Israeli Infringement on Egyptian Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 30th. Indonesian Foreign Ministry spokesman Kusumowidado told Antara news agency reporters today that the Israeli army's invasion of the Egyptian border seriously infringed on Egypt's territorial sovereignty.

The spokesman said that Israel's action has worsened the situation and increasingly endangers peace in the Middle East.

Kusumowidado said that the actions of the Israeli army cannot be agreed to in any way, and that such actions can be described as open aggression.

He called on the Security Council to take action to prevent greater danger, and for the Israeli army to withdraw from Egyptian territory. He said the most important thing is to localize the problem.

### 印度尼西亚外交部发言人  斥责以色列侵犯埃及主权

1956-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部发言人
    斥责以色列侵犯埃及主权
    新华社雅加达30日电　印度尼西亚外交部发言人库苏莫维达多今天对安塔拉通讯社记者说，以色列军队侵入埃及边境严重地侵犯了埃及的领土主权。
    这位发言人说，以色列的这一行动已经使局势恶化并且越来越危及中东的和平。
    库苏莫维达多说，以色列军队的这种行动是怎么说也不能使人同意的，这种行动可以说是公开的侵略。
    他要求安全理事会采取行动来防止更大的危险，并且要以色列军队撤出埃及领土。他说，最重要的是使问题局部化。
