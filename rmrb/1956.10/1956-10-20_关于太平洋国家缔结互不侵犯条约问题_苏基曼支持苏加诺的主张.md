Title: Sukiman Supports Sukarno's Proposal on the Issue of Pacific Nations Concluding a Non-Aggression Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 19th. "Life Daily" reported today that Sukiman, Vice Chairman of the Masjumi Party, expressed his support for President Sukarno's proposal that Pacific region nations conclude a non-aggression treaty.

Sukiman told a "Life Daily" reporter that when the President put forward this proposal in his speech in Shanghai, "I applauded enthusiastically because it is completely in line with my opinion." According to this Masjumi Party leader, this non-aggression treaty should have a broad foundation, including the United States, China, Japan, Indonesia, Australia, the Philippines, and other countries. He said that in this way, if any country dares to launch a war, it will surely be condemned by other countries.

Sukiman said that the people of the world, including the people of the United States, the Soviet Union, China, India, and Indonesia, all love peace. He also said that this does not include those with imperialist and colonialist ideas.

Sukiman's impression of Chairman Mao Zedong is: kind attitude, simple clothing, and concise speech.

He has a deep impression of the selfless spirit and firm confidence of the Chinese people in construction work. He said that construction work is being carried out earnestly in the Soviet Union and China.

Sukiman believes that among the countries visited, China gave the warmest welcome to Sukarno and his entourage.

### 关于太平洋国家缔结互不侵犯条约问题  苏基曼支持苏加诺的主张

1956-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    关于太平洋国家缔结互不侵犯条约问题
    苏基曼支持苏加诺的主张
    新华社雅加达19日电　今天的“生活报”报道，马斯友美党副主席苏基曼表示支持苏加诺总统提出的主张太平洋地区国家缔结互不侵犯条约的建议。
    苏基曼对“生活报”记者说，当总统在上海发表的演说中提出这个建议的时候，“我热烈地鼓了掌因为这是完全符合我的意见的”。据这位马斯友美党领袖说，这种互不侵犯条约应当具有广泛的基础，包括美国、中国、日本、印度尼西亚、澳大利亚、菲律宾和其他国家。他说，这样，一旦任何国家胆敢发动战争，它一定会受到其他国家的谴责。
    苏基曼说，全世界人民，包括美国、苏联、中国、印度和印度尼西亚人民，都热爱和平。他又说，这不包括那些具有帝国主义思想和殖民主义思想的人。
    苏基曼对毛泽东主席的印象是：态度和蔼，衣着朴素，谈吐简洁。
    他对中国人民在建设工作中的忘我精神和坚定信心有深刻的印象。他说，在苏联和中国，建设工作正在认真地进行。
    苏基曼认为，在所访问的国家中，中国对苏加诺和他的随行人员的欢迎最为热烈。
