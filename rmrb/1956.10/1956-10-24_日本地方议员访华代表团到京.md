Title: Japanese Local Assembly Members' Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Japanese Local Assembly Members' Delegation, a group of twenty-four people led by delegation head Fumio Kawabata, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.
    Greeting them at the station were Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and Cao Zhongshu, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, among others.
    Eiichi Yadoya, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group; Shozo Morii and Takashi Kataoka, deputy heads; and Kanzo Uchiyama, Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, also greeted them at the station.

### 日本地方议员访华代表团到京

1956-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    日本地方议员访华代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　日本地方议员访华代表团一行二十四人在团长川端文夫率领下，今天晚上乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘，北京市副市长乐松生，以及中国进出口公司经理曹中枢等人。
    日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一，副团长森井庄内、片冈峻，日本日中友好协会理事长内山完造等也到车站迎接。
