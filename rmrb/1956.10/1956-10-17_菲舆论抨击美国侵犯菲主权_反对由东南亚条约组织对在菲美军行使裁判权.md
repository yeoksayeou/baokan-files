Title: Philippine Public Opinion Criticizes US Infringement on Philippine Sovereignty: Opposes the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Exercising Jurisdiction over US Troops in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1956-10-17
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Manila, October 14th: Philippine parliamentarians, political figures, and public opinion oppose the Washington authorities' proposal to place US military personnel on US bases in the Philippines under the legal jurisdiction of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

　　Foreign policy expert and Senator Claro Recto criticized this proposal as if it were placing the Philippines under trusteeship.

　　Recto said: If the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization is allowed to exercise jurisdiction over American military personnel, it would mean that the Philippines must cede a portion of its sovereignty to this eight-nation group.

　　Philippine newspapers also criticized the US authorities' proposal as a blatant attempt to force the Philippines to abandon its national sovereignty.

　　Due to the US refusal to abolish its extraterritorial rights over US military bases in the Philippines, negotiations between the US and the Philippines on revising the military base agreement have reached a standstill. The US chief representative, Benderzen, returned to the United States on September 25th to consult with the Washington authorities. Benderzen returned to Manila from Washington on October 12th. Negotiations will resume on October 17th. The US request that the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization exercise jurisdiction over US military bases was revealed in a Western news agency dispatch from Washington on October 13th.

### 菲舆论抨击美国侵犯菲主权  反对由东南亚条约组织对在菲美军行使裁判权

1956-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    菲舆论抨击美国侵犯菲主权
    反对由东南亚条约组织对在菲美军行使裁判权
    据新华社14日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾议员政界人士和舆论反对华盛顿当局提出的把菲律宾美国基地上的美军人员置于东南亚条约组织的法律管辖之下的建议。
    外交政策专家、参议员克拉罗·雷克托指责这种建议就好像是要把菲律宾置于托管之下一样。
    雷克托说：如果允许东南亚条约组织对美国军事人员行使裁判权，那就意味着菲律宾必须将它的主权的一部分让予这一个八国集团。
    菲律宾报纸也抨击美国当局的这个建议，是一个要迫使菲律宾放弃国家主权的无耻企图。
    由于美国拒绝取消对它在菲律宾的美国军事基地的治外法权，美菲关于修改军事基地协定的谈判陷入了僵局，美国首席代表本德岑并且在9月25日回美国去向华盛顿当局请示。本德岑已经在10月12日从华盛顿回到马尼拉。谈判将于10月17日恢复。美国要求由东南亚条约组织来行使对美国军事基地的裁判权的建议，是10月13日西方通讯社从华盛顿发出的一条消息中透露出来的。
