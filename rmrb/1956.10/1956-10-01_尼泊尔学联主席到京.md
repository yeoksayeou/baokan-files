Title: Chairman of the Nepalese Student Union Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. At the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, the Chairman of the Nepalese Student Union, Bharat Raj Joshi, arrived in Beijing on the 29th for a visit to China.

### 尼泊尔学联主席到京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔学联主席到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来中国访问的尼泊尔学生联合会主席巴拉特·拉吉·乔希在29日到达北京。
