Title: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC Holds Enlarged Meeting, Decides to Solemnly Commemorate the Ninetieth Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd. The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its thirtieth meeting (enlarged) today to discuss preparations for commemorating the ninetieth anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

    The meeting was presided over by Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

    The meeting decided to solemnly commemorate the ninetieth anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. The meeting approved the establishment of a Preparatory Committee for the Ninetieth Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth, composed of eighty-seven members (list to be issued separately).

    The meeting discussed the methods of commemoration. It was decided that:

    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will host a commemorative meeting in Beijing on November 11th. The provincial, autonomous region, and municipal committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will hold commemorative meetings on November 12th, inviting relevant individuals from the Communist Party of China, various democratic parties, non-party democratic personages, and people's organizations to participate.

    Representatives of democratic parties and people's organizations in Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places will visit the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Biyun Temple in Beijing, the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum in Nanjing, the Sun Yat-sen Former Residence in Shanghai, and the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Guangzhou on November 11th.

    The Beijing People's Daily and Guangming Daily will publish commemorative special issues. Newspapers in various regions will publish speeches from the commemorative meeting in Beijing.

    In addition, it was decided to publish Selected Works of Sun Yat-sen, issue commemorative stamps, film a news documentary commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen, hold photo exhibitions, and cast commemorative medals.

    The meeting also decided to invite friends of Mr. Sun Yat-sen from abroad to attend the Sun Yat-sen Commemorative Meeting.

### 政协全国委员会常务委员会举行扩大会议  决定隆重纪念孙中山诞辰九十周年

1956-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常务委员会举行扩大会议
    决定隆重纪念孙中山诞辰九十周年
    新华社22日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天举行第三十次会议（扩大），讨论纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年筹备事宜。
    会议由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来主持。
    会议决定隆重纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十周年。会上通过成立孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会，筹备委员会由八十七位委员组成（名单另发）。
    会议讨论了纪念的办法。决定：
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于11月11日在北京主持举行纪念大会。中国人民政治协商会议各省、自治区、市委员会在11月12日举行纪念大会，邀请中国共产党、各民主党派、无党派民主人士、各人民团体的有关人士参加。
    北京、南京、上海、广州等地的民主党派和人民团体的代表，在11月11日分别到北京的碧云寺中山堂、南京的中山陵、上海的中山故居及广州的中山纪念堂等处去参谒。
    北京人民日报和光明日报出纪念专刊。各地报纸发表北京的纪念会上的演讲。
    另外，还决定出版孙中山选集，发行纪念邮票，拍摄纪念孙中山先生的新闻纪录影片，举办图片展览，铸制纪念章等。
    会议还决定邀请国外孙中山先生生前友好前来参加孙中山先生纪念大会。
