Title: Piratical Act Violating International Law: South Korean Navy Abducts Crew of Our Fishing Vessel in International Waters
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: The South Korean Syngman Rhee group's naval vessels blatantly created another incident of kidnapping nine crew members of our fishing vessel on the high seas.

At 21:30 on the evening of October 15th, the "Yu 125" fishing vessel of the Shanghai Aquatic Products Company was fishing in international waters at 123 degrees 58 minutes East longitude and 33 degrees 36 minutes North latitude. The Syngman Rhee group's naval vessels fired shots and forcibly boarded our "Yu 125" fishing vessel, abducting nine of our fishing vessel's crew members, including Captain Li Hongxiu, Chief Mate Li Hongsong, Chief Engineer Yu Shenliang, Second Engineer Li Wenshan, Oiler Ding Fugen, Assistant Chief Mate Yan Zhenglun, Cook Sun Guangmao, Fishermen Pan Yunsheng, and Huang Xingkuan. After kidnapping our crew members, the Syngman Rhee naval vessels sailed due east.

This incident is another illegal act by the Syngman Rhee group following its armed provocation against our fishing vessels on the high seas on December 25, 1955. This piratical behavior of the Syngman Rhee group, which blatantly violates international law, has aroused strong indignation among the Chinese people.

### 破坏国际法的海盗行为  南朝鲜海军在公海劫走我渔轮船员

1956-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    破坏国际法的海盗行为
    南朝鲜海军在公海劫走我渔轮船员
    新华社17日讯　南朝鲜李承晚集团的海军舰艇公然在公海上再一次制造绑架我国渔轮船员九人的事件。
    10月15日晚二十一时三十分，我国上海水产公司“渔125号”渔轮在东经123度58分，北纬33度36分的公海上捕鱼，李承晚集团的海军舰艇竟鸣枪强行登上我“渔125号”渔轮，绑走我国渔轮上的船长李宏秀、大副李鸿嵩、轮机长余申良、副司机李文珊、加油工丁富根、助理大副严正伦、炊事员孙光懋、渔捞员潘云盛、黄杏宽等九人。李承晚的海军舰艇绑走我船员后，即向正东方驶去。
    这次事件是李承晚集团继1955年12月25日在公海上对我国渔轮实行武力挑衅后的又一次不法事件，李承晚集团这种公然破坏国际法的海盗行为已激起了中国人民的强烈的愤慨。
