Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation from the Parliament of the Republic of India
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Chairman Mao Zedong received the delegation from the Parliament of the Republic of India, headed by Speaker Ananthasayanam Ayyangar, tonight.

Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen; Zhang Su, Qu Wu, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General; Zhang Hanfu, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to India.

Also present at the reception was the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru.

### 毛主席接见印度共和国国会代表团

1956-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见印度共和国国会代表团
    新华社19日讯　毛泽东主席今天夜里接见了以阿南塔萨亚纳姆·阿延加尔议长为首的印度共和国国会代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、副委员长李济深、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，副秘书长张苏、屈武、余心清，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻印度大使潘自力。
    接见的时候，印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也在座。
