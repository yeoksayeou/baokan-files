Title: Indian Trial Flight Aircraft Lands Safely in Tibet
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, October 24th: An Indian aircraft, conducting a trial flight to Chinese Tibet, took off from Jorhat Airport in northeastern India at 9:35 this morning and safely landed at Damshung Airport near Lhasa in Chinese Tibet at 11:58 this morning, after crossing the Himalayas.

On October 21st, the Chinese Civil Aviation aircraft "Beijing" first opened the route from Damshung in Chinese Tibet, across the Himalayas, to Bagdogra in northern India. Thus far, two new routes between Chinese Tibet and India have been successfully tested.

These trial flights by China and India are being conducted in accordance with the temporary trial flight agreement between the two governments.

After the Indian trial flight aircraft landed at Damshung Airport, it was welcomed by representatives from the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Civil Aviation Administration of China. The welcoming party also held a special welcome tea party and banquet at the airport for the Indian trial flight crew.

### 印度试航飞机在西藏安全着陆

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度试航飞机在西藏安全着陆
    据新华社拉萨24日电　今天上午九时三十五分由印度东北部卓哈特机场起飞的向中国西藏试航的一架印度飞机，在上午十一时五十八分越过喜马拉雅山在中国西藏拉萨附近的当雄机场安全着陆。
    10月21日，中国民航飞机“北京号”首先开辟了从中国西藏当雄越过喜马拉雅山飞往印度北部巴格多格拉之间的航线。至此，中国西藏和印度之间已有两条新航线试航成功。
    中印两国这次试航，是按照两国政府临时试航协议进行的。
    印度的试航飞机在当雄机场着陆后，受到西藏自治区筹备委员会和中国民用航空局代表们的欢迎。欢迎的人们在机场上还特地为印度的试航飞行人员举行了欢迎茶会和宴会。
