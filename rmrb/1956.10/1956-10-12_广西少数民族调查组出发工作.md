Title: Guangxi Minority Investigation Teams Start Work
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 8th. Two investigation teams specializing in examining the social and historical circumstances of the Zhuang, Yao, Dong, and Miao minorities have successively departed to work in Chongzuo County of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture and the Dayao Mountains Yao Autonomous County in Guangxi Province. The investigation teams will be working in Guangxi Province for a long period, extensively collecting data on the historical development, customs, habits, politics, culture, education, religion, art, and philosophy of the various ethnic minorities within the province, and conduct systematic and comprehensive research on the history of these ethnic groups.

### 广西少数民族调查组出发工作

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    广西少数民族调查组出发工作
    据新华社南宁8日电　两个专门检查僮、瑶、侗、苗等少数民族的社会历史情况的调查组，先后出发去广西省的桂西僮族自治州崇左县和大瑶山瑶族自治县工作。调查组将在广西省长期进行工作，大量搜集省内各少数民族的历史发展、风俗习惯、政治、文教、宗教、艺术、哲学等各方面的资料，并对这些民族的历史作系统全面的研究。
