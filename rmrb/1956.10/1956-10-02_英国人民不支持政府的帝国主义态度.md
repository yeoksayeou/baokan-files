Title: British People Do Not Support the Government's Imperialist Attitude
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

——Letter from London——

    On August 14th, our newspaper published a letter from a Shanghai family supporting the just struggle of the Egyptian people. Recently, this family received a letter from a student in London, England.

    This letter shows that the British people are firmly opposed to the British government's armed intervention in Egypt. This family asked us to publish the letter below:

    Dear Friends,

    I have seen the translated version of your letter supporting the Egyptian people. I hope you believe that there are many people in Britain who do not support Eden and his government's imperialist attitude. Please do not misunderstand the British people because of the British government's extremely unpopular mistakes and unjust actions.

    I hope to visit China one day, and perhaps we will meet happily.

    Finally, I wish you and all the Chinese people peace, happiness, and prosperity.

                              A London Student, Sam Woolf
                                                 August 19th

### 英国人民不支持政府的帝国主义态度

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    英国人民不支持政府的帝国主义态度
    ——伦敦来信——　　　
    本报在8月14日刊登了上海一个家庭支持埃及人民正义斗争的信。最近这个家庭收到了英国伦敦一个学生的来信。　　
    这封信说明了英国人民是坚决反对英国政府对埃及进行武装　　
    干涉的。这个家庭要求我们把信发表在下面：亲爱的朋友们：　
    我看到了你们支持埃及人民的信的译文。我希望你们相信，在英国有许多人是不支持艾登及其政府的帝国主义态度的。不要因为英国政府的极其不得人心的错误和不正义的行为，而对英国人民有所误解。
  　我希望有一天能够访问中国，或许我们将会愉快地会见。　　
    最后，祝你们和全体中国人民安乐、富强。　　
                              一个伦敦学生　赛姆·伍尔夫　　
                                                 8月19日　　（附图片）
    伦敦工人游行示威反对英国政府企图武装干涉埃及主权的阴谋活动。示威者胸前挂着“不许在苏伊士发动战争”的标语牌。
