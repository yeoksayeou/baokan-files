Title: Berlin People's Police Orchestra Holds Closing Ceremony in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1956-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, October 23rd: The Berlin People's Police Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic, which has been touring China for more than two months, held its closing ceremony for its visit to China in Harbin last night.

　　The closing ceremony was held in the auditorium of the Northeast Institute of Agronomy. Li Wei, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army of China, on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army of China, congratulated the Berlin People's Police Orchestra on its successful performances during its visit to China. Willi Kaufmann, head of the Berlin People's Police Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic, gave a farewell speech.

　　After the closing ceremony, the Berlin People's Police Orchestra played thirteen wonderful programs for the audience, receiving enthusiastic applause again and again.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, October 24th: The Berlin People's Police Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic left Harbin on the morning of the 24th to return to their country.

### 柏林人民警察乐团在哈尔滨举行演出闭幕式

1956-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    柏林人民警察乐团在哈尔滨举行演出闭幕式
    据新华社哈尔滨23日电　在我国进行了为期两个多月访问演出的德意志民主共和国柏林人民警察乐团，昨天晚上在哈尔滨举行访问中国演出的闭幕式。
    闭幕式是在东北农学院大礼堂举行的。中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长李伟大校代表中华人民共和国文化部、中国人民解放军总政治部祝贺柏林人民警察乐团在我国访问期间成功的演出。德意志民主共和国柏林人民警察乐团团长维利·考夫曼作了告别讲话。
    闭幕仪式结束后，柏林人民警察乐团为听众们演奏了十三个精采节目，受到一次又一次热烈掌声的欢迎。
    据新华社哈尔滨24日电　德意志民主共和国柏林人民警察乐团在24日上午离哈尔滨回国。
