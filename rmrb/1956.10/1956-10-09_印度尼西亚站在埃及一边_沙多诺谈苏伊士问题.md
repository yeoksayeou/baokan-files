Title: Indonesia Stands with Egypt, Sadono Discusses the Suez Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 8th. Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sadono stated at a parliamentary session today: On the Suez Canal issue, we "stand with Egypt, which is natural and based on the point of view of justice."

He said at the meeting: "It is precisely for this reason that the attitude of the Indonesian delegation at the first London conference in August this year could only be: fully supporting Egypt's right to nationalize the canal company, and supporting Egypt's interests in the Suez Canal dispute."

When talking about domestic issues, Speaker Sadono said: "We are still facing the task of perfecting our country's sovereignty. We have abolished the Round Table Conference Agreement, and we are currently making efforts to deal with the consequences of the abolition of the Round Table Conference." He emphasized that concrete steps must be taken to strive for the recovery of West Irian.

When talking about President Sukarno's visits abroad, Sadono said that President Sukarno's visits to the United States, Canada, Western Europe, Eastern European countries, and People's China are of great significance in promoting peace among nations, strengthening friendship between Indonesia and other countries, and introducing the ideals of the Indonesian people to foreign peoples.

The parliament resumed today. The third stage of this session will discuss a number of important bills, including the first Five-Year Plan, the treaty on dual nationality with China, the bill on provincial and regional divisions, and other bills.

### 印度尼西亚站在埃及一边  沙多诺谈苏伊士问题

1956-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚站在埃及一边
    沙多诺谈苏伊士问题
    新华社雅加达8日电　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺今天在国会会议上说：我们在苏伊士运河问题上“站在埃及一边，这是自然的而且也是从正义观点出发的”。
    他在这次会议上说：“正是由于这种缘故，印度尼西亚代表团在今年8月间第一次伦敦会议上的态度就只能是：完全支持埃及把运河公司收归国有的权利，支持埃及在苏伊士运河争端中的利益。”
    沙多诺议长在谈到内政问题的时候说：“我们目前仍然面临着使我国的主权趋于完备的任务。我们已经废除了圆桌会议协定，目前我们正在进行努力，处理由于废除圆桌会议而引起的后果。”他强调说，必须采取具体步骤，争取收复西伊里安。
    沙多诺在谈到苏加诺总统在国外所进行的访问的时候说，在促进各国之间的和平、加强印度尼西亚和其他国家之间的友谊并且向外国人民介绍印度尼西亚人民的理想这些方面，苏加诺总统在美国、加拿大、西欧、东欧国家和人民中国所进行的访问，是具有十分重大的意义的。
    国会是在今天复会的。本届会议的第三阶段将要讨论一些重要的法案，其中包括第一个五年计划、关于同中国缔结的双重国籍问题的条约、关于省区划分的法案以及其他的法案。
