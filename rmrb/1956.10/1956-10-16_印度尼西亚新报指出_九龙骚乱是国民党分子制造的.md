Title: Indonesian "Sin Po" Points Out: Kowloon Riots Were Created by Kuomintang Elements
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 15th -- The Indonesian newspaper "Sin Po," in a report on the third day after the Kowloon riots, stated: "The Kowloon riots are entirely understandable because, according to reports, this incident was created by Kuomintang elements." The newspaper pointed out: "Everyone knows that Hong Kong and Kowloon are a stronghold for Kuomintang elements."

### 印度尼西亚新报指出  九龙骚乱是国民党分子制造的

1956-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新报指出
    九龙骚乱是国民党分子制造的
    新华社雅加达15日电　印度尼西亚报纸“新报”在九龙骚乱事件发生后的第三天报道说：“九龙的骚乱是完全可以理解的，因为，据报道，这次事件是国民党分子制造的。”这家报纸指出：“人人都知道，香港和九龙是国民党分子的一个据点。”
