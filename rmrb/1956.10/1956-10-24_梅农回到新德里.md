Title: Menon Returns to New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 23rd. Indian Minister Without Portfolio, Menon, stated in New Delhi on October 22nd, in response to questions from reporters, that he knew that a meeting would likely be held in Geneva in the coming days to continue discussions on the Suez Canal issue that had already taken place in New York.

Menon said that he did not have a specific plan to resolve the Suez Canal issue. However, he had made suggestions at different stages during the various talks. Menon returned to New Delhi on October 22nd after holding talks with the governments of the countries concerned in New York, London, and Cairo.

### 梅农回到新德里

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    梅农回到新德里
    新华社新德里23日电　印度不管部部长梅农10月22日在新德里回答记者问题时说，他知道，大概会在日内瓦举行一个会议来继续进行已经在纽约进行过的关于苏伊士运河问题的讨论。
    梅农说，他并没有解决苏伊士运河问题的具体计划。但是，他在各次会谈中曾经在不同的阶段提出过建议。梅农在纽约、伦敦、开罗同各有关国家政府会谈后，在10月22日回到了新德里。
