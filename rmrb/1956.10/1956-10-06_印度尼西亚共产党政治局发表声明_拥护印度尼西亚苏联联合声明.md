Title: Indonesian Communist Party Politburo Issues Statement Endorsing the Soviet-Indonesian Joint Statement
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th: The Politburo of the Indonesian Communist Party issued a statement on October 3rd saying that the Soviet-Indonesian Joint Statement is fully in line with the thoughts, feelings, and national interests of the Indonesian people. It has made Indonesia's independent foreign policy more complete. This matter has found a new way out for Indonesia to get rid of its current economic difficulties.

The statement of the Politburo of the Indonesian Communist Party, published in the Indonesian "People's Daily," said that the joint statement issued by influential countries like the Soviet Union and Indonesia expressing support for the Ten Principles of the Bandung Conference is a victory for Indonesian foreign policy.

### 印度尼西亚共产党政治局发表声明  拥护印度尼西亚苏联联合声明

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党政治局发表声明
    拥护印度尼西亚苏联联合声明
    新华社雅加达4日电　印度尼西亚共产党政治局10月3日发表声明说，苏联—印度尼西亚联合声明是完全符合印度尼西亚人民的思想感情和民族利益的。它已经使印度尼西亚所奉行的独立的外交政策更加完备了。这件事情已经为印度尼西亚摆脱目前的经济困难找到了新的出路。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”刊载的印度尼西亚共产党政治局的这个声明说，像苏联和印度尼西亚这样有影响的国家共同发表声明表示支持万隆会议的十项原则，这是印度尼西亚外交政策的胜利。
