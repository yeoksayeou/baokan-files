Title: South Republican and Bulgarian Communist Representatives Hold Talks, Decide to Strengthen Cooperation Between the Two Parties on the Basis of Mutual Respect
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, during the visit of the Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation to Yugoslavia, representatives of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and representatives of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held talks in Belgrade on October 6th and 7th.

The communique issued on the evening of the 7th stated that the candid and comradely exchange of views during the talks showed that both sides had the conditions, common aspirations, and consensus to continue the contact established between the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and the Bulgarian Communist Party during this meeting, and to further strengthen the ties and cooperation between the two parties through personal contact - mutual dispatch of delegations, visits by party workers, and exchange of party documents, journals and newspapers, or other appropriate means.

These contacts and cooperation, based on equality, mutual respect, and non-interference in internal affairs, will be conducted through the free exchange of opinions on matters of common concern and the exchange of experiences in socialist construction, in order to further strengthen the ties and cooperation between the peoples of the two countries in their socialist construction and their activities to consolidate peace.

The representatives of the two parties firmly believe that these ties and cooperation will help to further develop mutual understanding and trust between the peoples of the two countries and will have a positive impact on the comprehensive development of relations between the two countries.

The Yugoslav side participating in the talks included: Rankovic and Pijade, members of the Executive Committee of the League of Communists, and others.

The Bulgarian side participating in the talks included: Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party, Jankov, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, and others.

### 南共和保共代表举行会谈  决定在互相尊重的基础上加强两党合作

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　南共和保共代表举行会谈
    决定在互相尊重的基础上加强两党合作
    新华社8日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，在保加利亚议会代表团访问南斯拉夫期间，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会的代表和保加利亚共产党中央委员会的代表10月6日和7日在贝尔格莱德举行了会谈。
    7日晚上发表的会谈公报说，会谈中坦率地和同志式地交换意见表明，双方既有条件也具有共同愿望和一致意见来继续保持南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟和保加利亚共产党之间在这次会谈中所建立的接触，并且通过个人接触——互派代表团，党的工作人员的访问，以及交换党内文件、期刊和报纸或其他适当办法来进一步加强两党之间的联系和合作。
    这些以平等、互相尊重和不干涉内部事务为基础的接触和合作，将以自由交换关于共同有关的问题的意见和交流社会主义建设的经验的方式进行，以进一步加强两国人民在两国社会主义建设和争取巩固和平的活动中的联系和合作。
    两党代表深信，这些联系和合作将有助于进一步发展两国人民之间的相互了解和信任，并且会对两国关系的全面发展产生有利的影响。
    南斯拉夫方面参加会谈的有：共产主义者联盟执行委员会委员兰科维奇和皮雅杰等。
    保加利亚方面参加会谈的有：共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫，中央委员会政治局委员詹科夫等。
