Title: A Batch of Small Hydropower Stations Built in Old Revolutionary Base Areas of Jiangxi
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, October 10th: Fifteen small hydroelectric power stations are under construction in rural areas of Ruijin, Xingguo, Yujiang and other counties in the old revolutionary base areas of Jiangxi Province. Four of them were completed by the end of September, and the rest will be completed before the end of the year.

After these power stations are completed, the electricity generated will be used for irrigation by agricultural cooperatives, as well as for oil pressing, timber processing, grain processing, and lighting for the homes of agricultural cooperative members.

These small hydroelectric power stations located in the old revolutionary base areas are all built with state investment.

### 江西革命老根据地兴建一批小型水电站

1956-10-13
第2版()
专栏：

    江西革命老根据地兴建一批小型水电站
    新华社南昌10日电　有十五个小型水力发电站，正在江西省革命老根据地瑞金、兴国、余江等县的农村中建设着；其中的四个，在9月底已经完工，其余的，在年底以前也都可以完工。
    这些电站建成以后，发出的电力将供给农业社抽水灌溉、榨油、木材、粮食加工，以及农业社社员家庭照明用。
    这些设在革命老根据地的小型水力发电站，都是由国家投资兴建的。
