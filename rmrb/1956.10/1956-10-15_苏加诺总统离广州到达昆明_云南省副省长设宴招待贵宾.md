Title: President Sukarno Leaves Guangzhou and Arrives in Kunming; Yunnan Vice Governor Hosts Banquet for Distinguished Guests
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 14th October: President Sukarno, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, left Guangzhou by special plane at 11:35 AM today for Kunming.

    From early this morning, over 10,000 Guangzhou citizens, holding Chinese and Indonesian national flags, pictures of President Sukarno, and flowers, gathered at Baiyun Airport in the northern suburbs of Guangzhou to bid farewell to President Sukarno.

    President Sukarno arrived at the airport at 11:00 AM. Accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Governor Tao Zhu, Mayor Zhu Guang, and Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, the President reviewed the guard of honor. After the review, the President returned to the front of the guard of honor and greeted the soldiers. The soldiers stood at attention and returned the greeting with a salute.

    As the President passed through the crowds lining the streets to see him off, people presented him with bouquets of flowers, and a large group of children reluctantly followed the President to the plane, where the President warmly held their hands.

    After shaking hands firmly with his hosts once again, the President boarded the plane. At the plane's door, the President turned back to wave goodbye to the crowd, and Governor Tao Zhu wished the President a safe journey.

    The plane carrying the President took off at 11:35 AM, circled once over the crowd, and then flew westward.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 14th October: President Sukarno and the accompanying senior Indonesian officials, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, arrived in Kunming by special plane at 3:25 PM this afternoon.

    President Sukarno stepped off the plane to cheers and applause from more than 6,000 people. Yunnan Vice Governor Liu Minghui, Yunnan Provincial CPPCC Chairman Xie Fuzhi, Kunming Mayor Pan Shuoduan, and Commander of the PLA stationed in Kunming, Major General Jin Rubai, stepped forward to welcome the distinguished guests at the cabin door. At this moment, men and women of various ethnic groups wearing brightly colored national costumes continuously shouted "Welcome President Sukarno!" "Long live peace!" Dai youths from the Yunnan border played elephant-foot drums, and Miao farmers played reed pipes. Young Pioneers presented bouquets of flowers to the President. As the President, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Yunnan Vice Governor Liu Minghui, rode in an open-top car to the city, Kunming city was filled with jubilation, and more than 50,000 men and women of various ethnic groups waved flowers and cheered the President along the streets.

    Also present at the airport to welcome President Sukarno today were the heads of various Yunnan Province and Kunming City organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 14th October: Yunnan Vice Governor Liu Minghui hosted a banquet tonight in honor of President Sukarno. Vice Premier Chen Yi, who accompanied President Sukarno to Kunming, and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen also attended.

    Also present were Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others.

    After the banquet, President Sukarno watched a performance of songs and dances by various ethnic groups in Yunnan, specially organized for him at his residence. President Sukarno was very interested in the folk songs sung by the famous singer Huang Hong, who had visited Indonesia for performances, as well as the Dai harvest dance and the Miao flower dress dance.

### 苏加诺总统离广州到达昆明  云南省副省长设宴招待贵宾

1956-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统离广州到达昆明
    云南省副省长设宴招待贵宾
    新华社广州14日电　苏加诺总统今天十一时三十五分在陈毅副总理陪同下乘专机离开广州前往昆明。
    从今天早晨起，一万多广州市民就举着中国和印度尼西亚国旗，拿着苏加诺总统的画像，携着鲜花来到广州北郊的白云机场欢送苏加诺总统。
    苏加诺总统在十一时正来到了机场。在陈毅副总理、陶铸省长、朱光市长和广州驻军首长文年生中将陪同下，总统检阅了仪仗队。检阅完毕后，总统又回到仪仗队伍前面，向战士们问好。战士们全体肃立，向总统举行注目礼答谢。
    当总统穿过夹道欢送的人群时，人们把一束束的鲜花献给他，一大群儿童依依不舍地跟随着总统一齐走到飞机跟前，总统亲切地拉着他们的手。
    再一次和主人们紧紧握手之后，总统走上了飞机。在飞机舱门前，总统回转身来向欢送人群挥手告别，陶铸省长祝总统一路平安。
    载着总统的飞机在十一时三十五分起飞，飞机在人群上空旋转了一周，然后便向西飞去。
    新华社昆明14日电　苏加诺总统和随行的印度尼西亚高级官员在国务院陈毅副总理陪同下，今天下午三时二十五分乘专机到达昆明。
    苏加诺总统在六千多人的欢呼和掌声中走下飞机。云南省副省长刘明辉、政协云南省主席谢富治、昆明市市长潘朔端、中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长金如柏少将走到机舱门口欢迎贵宾。这时，穿着鲜艳民族服装的各民族男女们不断高呼“欢迎苏加诺总统！”“和平万岁！”来自云南边疆的傣族青年们打起了象脚鼓，苗族农民们吹起了芦笙。少年先锋队员们把一束束的鲜花献给总统。当总统在陈毅副总理和云南省副省长刘明辉的陪同下，乘敞篷汽车驶向市区时，昆明市区万众欢腾，五万多各民族男女手摇鲜花夹道向总统欢呼。
    今天到机场欢迎苏加诺总统的还有云南省和昆明市各机关、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人。
    新华社昆明14日电　云南省副省长刘明辉今天晚上设宴招待苏加诺总统。陪同苏加诺总统来昆明的陈毅副总理和中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇也出席作陪。
    出席作陪的还有中共云南省委第一书记、中国人民政治协商会议云南省委员会主席谢富治等人。
    宴会以后，苏加诺总统在他的住所观看了特地为他组织的云南各民族歌舞表演。苏加诺总统对曾到印度尼西亚作过访问演出的著名歌唱家黄虹演唱的民歌、傣族的丰收舞和苗族的花衣舞等节目很感兴趣。
