Title: Premier Zhou and Chairman U Nu Continue Talks
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, U Nu, continued their talks on the afternoon of the 27th.

### 周总理同吴努主席继续会谈

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同吴努主席继续会谈
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努在27日下午继续进行了会谈。
