Title: Promoting Mutual Understanding Between the People of China and the Federal Republic of Germany: Our Art Troupe's First Visit and Performance in West Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 2nd. The Chinese Art Troupe, which arrived in West Berlin from Switzerland on September 30th, will perform at the Titania Palast Theater in West Berlin for seven days starting from October 3rd. After that, they will tour five major cities in the Federal Republic of Germany: Munich, Hanover, Dusseldorf, Stuttgart, and Hamburg for about a month and a half. This is the first time that a Chinese art troupe has visited and performed in the Federal Republic of Germany.

The art troupe held a press conference in West Berlin on October 1st, introducing the composition of the art troupe, the schedule for visiting and performing in the Federal Republic of Germany, and the program, etc., to the press.

Xu Pingyu, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, delivered a speech at the press conference, expressing his belief that this visit and performance will help promote mutual understanding between the people of China and the Federal Republic of Germany, as well as develop friendly relations between the cultural and artistic circles of the two countries.

Dozens of German journalists attending the press conference watched a short preview of the Chinese Peking opera actors with great interest and raised some questions to the Chinese art troupe. These questions were answered by Xu Pingyu, the head of the art troupe.

### 促进中国和德意志联邦共和国人民的相互了解  我艺术团首次到西德访问演出

1956-10-04
第5版()
专栏：

    促进中国和德意志联邦共和国人民的相互了解
    我艺术团首次到西德访问演出
    新华社柏林2日电　9月30日从瑞士到达西柏林的中国艺术团将从10月3日起在西柏林的提塔尼亚宫剧院作七天的演出，然后到德意志联邦共和国的慕尼黑、汉诺威、杜塞尔多夫、斯图加特、汉堡五个大城市巡回演出约一个半月。这是中国的艺术团体第一次在德意志联邦共和国进行访问演出。
    艺术团10月1日在西柏林举行了记者招待会，向新闻界介绍艺术团的组成，预定在德意志联邦共和国进行访问演出的日程和节目等。
    中国艺术团团长徐平羽在记者招待会上致词，表示相信通过这次访问演出将有助于促进中国同德意志联邦共和国两国人民之间的相互了解以及发展两国文化艺术界之间的友好关系。
    出席记者招待会的数十位德国新闻界人士很感兴趣地观看了中国京剧演员的简短预演，并且向中国艺术团提出了一些问题。这些问题都由艺术团团长徐平羽做了解答。
