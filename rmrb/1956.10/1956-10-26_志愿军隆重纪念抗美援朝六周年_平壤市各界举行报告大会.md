Title: Volunteer Army Solemnly Commemorates the Sixth Anniversary of the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; People from All Walks of Life in Pyongyang Hold Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army units solemnly commemorated the sixth anniversary of the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea today.

A memorial meeting was held by a certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed in the Shangganling area. Representatives from the Party and government organs of Jiangyuan Province in Korea, representatives from a certain unit of the Korean People's Army who fought side by side to defend the position, the famous "Volunteer Army Mother" Xian Zaifu, the model of supporting the front line Ju Fuli, and the family members of the great internationalist martyr Park Zai-gen, came from afar to offer their congratulations. They brought with them a large number of letters of comfort and gifts. Xian Zaifu and others are highly respected and loved by the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army. Their deeds of loving the Volunteer Army once again gave the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army a profound internationalist education and encouragement.

A certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed on the west coast, which had helped the Korean people build the "South Pyongan Irrigation Project," held a photo exhibition to commemorate the sixth anniversary of the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. They were also warmly congratulated by representatives of the Party and government organs of North Pyongan Province and the local people.

Volunteer Army units stationed in other areas of Korea also held various commemorative activities. They were all equally congratulated by representatives of local Korean Party and government organs, Korean People's Army units, and the people.

The personnel of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission also held commemorative activities.

Today, representatives of Party and government organs, People's Army units, and the masses of people in various parts of Korea also, accompanied by representatives of the Volunteer Army, swept the tombs of Volunteer Army martyrs in various places.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 24th: People from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a report meeting tonight to commemorate the sixth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea.

More than 800 people attended the meeting, including Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Hong Myung-hee, Kim Il, and other party and government leaders, heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, senior generals of the Korean People's Army, and representatives of the working people of Pyongyang.

The Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation of more than 100 people, headed by General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Li Zhimin, Political Commissar, were invited to attend the meeting.

Cao Keqiang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea attended the meeting.

Lieutenant General Li Quanwu of the Korean People's Army gave a report at the meeting.

After Li Quanwu's report, Vice Premier Kim Il of the Korean Cabinet presented a banner to the Volunteer Army on behalf of the Supreme People's Assembly and the Cabinet of Korea. Yang Yong and Li Zhimin received the banner.

Then, General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, spoke.

After the meeting, the Tianjin Pingju Opera Troupe of China and Korean art troupes jointly performed programs.

### 志愿军隆重纪念抗美援朝六周年  平壤市各界举行报告大会

1956-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    志愿军隆重纪念抗美援朝六周年
    平壤市各界举行报告大会
    据新华社平壤25日电　中国人民志愿军各部队今天隆重纪念抗美援朝六周年。
    驻守在上甘岭地区的志愿军某部举行了纪念大会。远道前来祝贺的有朝鲜江原道党政机关的代表，并肩守卫阵地的朝鲜人民军某部代表，有名的“志愿军妈妈”咸在福，支前模范具富力，伟大的国际主义烈士朴在根的家属等。他们携带着大批慰问信和礼品。咸在福等在部队中受到极大的尊敬与爱戴，她们热爱志愿军的事迹再一次给了志愿军官兵以深刻的国际主义教育与鼓舞。
    曾经帮助朝鲜人民修建“平南灌溉工程”的驻西海岸志愿军某部，举行了抗美援朝六周年照片展览。他们也受到了平安北道党政机关代表和当地人民的热烈祝贺。
    驻朝鲜其他地区的志愿军部队也举行了各种纪念活动。他们都同样地受到当地朝鲜党政机关、人民军部队和人民群众代表的祝贺。
    军事停战委员会朝中方面代表团的人员，也举行了纪念活动。
    今天，朝鲜各地党政机关、人民军部队和人民群众的代表，还在志愿军代表的陪同下祭扫了在各地的志愿军烈士陵园。
    据新华社平壤24日电　平壤市各界今天晚上举行报告大会，纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战六周年。
    参加大会的有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相崔庸健、洪命熹、金一等党和政府的领导人，各民主政党、社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军高级将领，平壤市劳动人民代表等八百多人。
    以中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇将军、政治委员李志民将军为首的中国人民志愿军代表团一百多人应邀参加了大会。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办曹克强和各国驻朝使节都参加了大会。
    朝鲜人民军中将李权武在大会上作报告。
    李权武报告后，朝鲜内阁副首相金一代表朝鲜最高人民会议和内阁向志愿军献旗。杨勇和李志民受旗。
    接着，中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇将军讲话。
    会后，中国天津市评剧团和朝鲜的艺术团体联合演出了节目。
