Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for Delegation from the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, held a banquet today to entertain the entire delegation from the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade.

Also invited to the banquet were Japanese House of Councillors member Takashi Tomi and Japanese personage in Beijing, Kameda Togo.

### 南汉宸宴请日国会议员促进日中贸易联盟访华团

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    南汉宸宴请日国会议员促进日中贸易联盟访华团
    新华社11日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今天举行宴会，招待日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟访华代表团全体人员。
    应邀出席宴会的还有日本参议院议员高良富、在京日本人士龟田东伍。
