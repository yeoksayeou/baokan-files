Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Holds Tea Party to Host Indonesian Deputy Speaker and Parliamentarians
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, Liu Shaoqi, held a tea party this afternoon to host the First Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Zainul Arifin, Dr. Yo Leimena, and Sutarto Hadisudibyo. Deputy Speaker Arifin and the two parliamentarians are visiting China with President Sukarno.

Also attending the tea party were Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Deputy Secretary-General Zhang Su.

The hosts and guests had a cordial and pleasant conversation at the tea party.

### 刘少奇委员长举行茶会  招待印度尼西亚副议长和议员

1956-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长举行茶会
    招待印度尼西亚副议长和议员
    新华社4日讯　中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，在今天下午举行茶会，招待印度尼西亚国会第一副议长蔡努尔·阿里芬、议员约·来梅纳博士和苏塔托·哈迪苏迪比约。阿里芬副议长和两位议员都是随苏加诺总统来中国访问的。
    参加茶会的还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，副秘书长张苏。
    宾主在茶会上作了亲切愉快的交谈。
