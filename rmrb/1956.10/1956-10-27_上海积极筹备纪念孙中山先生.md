Title: Shanghai Actively Prepares to Commemorate Mr. Sun Yat-sen
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 26th. The Shanghai Municipality yesterday established the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth. Ke Qingshi, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, serves as the Director of the Preparatory Committee.
    Various activities commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen have begun preparation. The Preparatory Committee decided to hold a commemorative meeting on November 12, inviting representatives from the Chinese Communist Party, various democratic parties, non-party democratic人士, and people's organizations to participate; collecting materials from all aspects to organize a photo exhibition of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's life and deeds. People from all walks of life will visit the Former Residence of Sun Yat-sen from the 11th to the 18th. Shanghai's newspapers and magazines will also successively publish commemorative articles about Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Around the anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth, the city will universally hold lectures or symposiums.
    The Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a poetry recital on the 22nd to commemorate the ninetieth anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. Currently, more than forty poems and songs have been received from old members of the Tongmenghui and veterans who participated in the revolutionary activities led by Mr. Sun Yat-sen residing in Shanghai.

### 上海积极筹备纪念孙中山先生

1956-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    上海积极筹备纪念孙中山先生
    新华社上海26日电　上海市昨天成立了孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会。中国人民政协上海市委员会主席、中共上海市委书记处第一书记柯庆施担任筹备委员会主任。
    纪念孙中山先生的各项活动已在开始筹备。筹备委员会决定在11月12日举行纪念大会，邀请中国共产党、各民主党派、无党派民主人士、各人民团体代表参加；向各方面搜集材料，筹组孙中山先生生平事迹图片展览会。各界人士在11日至18日将参谒中山故居。上海的报刊杂志上也将陆续发表关于中山先生的纪念文章。在孙中山先生诞辰纪念日前后，全市将普遍举行演讲会或座谈会。
    中国国民党革命委员会上海市委员会在22日举办了纪念孙中山先生九十诞辰的诗会。目前已经收到居住在上海的老同盟会会员和曾经参加过孙中山先生领导的革命活动的老人们写的诗词共四十多首。
