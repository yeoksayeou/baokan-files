Title: Mongolian People's Republic Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 16th. A three-member trade delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Avirmed, Deputy Minister of Commerce of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Three more members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing tomorrow.
    Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and others went to the airport today to welcome the guests.
    Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 蒙古人民共和国贸易代表团到达北京

1956-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国贸易代表团到达北京
    新华社16日讯　蒙古人民共和国贸易代表团一行三人，在团长、蒙古人民共和国商业部副部长阿维尔米德率领下今天乘飞机到达北京。另外三名团员将在明天到达北京。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长林海云等今天前往机场欢迎客人。
    前往机场欢迎的还有蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使奥其尔巴特和使馆人员。
