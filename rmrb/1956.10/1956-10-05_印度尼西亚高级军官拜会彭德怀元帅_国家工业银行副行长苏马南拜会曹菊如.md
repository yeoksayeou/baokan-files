Title: Indonesian Senior Military Officers Visit Marshal Peng Dehuai; Vice President of the National Industrial Bank, Sumanan, Visits Cao Juru
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Dr. Sumanan, Vice President of the Indonesian National Industrial Bank, visited Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, this afternoon.

On the same afternoon, Colonel Yu. F. Warouw, a senior officer of the Indonesian Army, Brigadier General Aburachmat, a senior officer of the Air Force, Lieutenant Commander S. Piengadi, a senior officer of the Navy, and Major Prayogo, an army officer, visited Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense.

### 印度尼西亚高级军官拜会彭德怀元帅  国家工业银行副行长苏马南拜会曹菊如

1956-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚高级军官拜会彭德怀元帅
    国家工业银行副行长苏马南拜会曹菊如
    新华社4日讯　印度尼西亚国家工业银行副行长苏马南博士，今天下午拜会了中国人民银行行长曹菊如。
    同日下午，印度尼西亚陆军高级军官尤·弗·瓦罗伍上校、空军高级军官阿布腊赫马特准将、海军高级军官斯·皮恩加迪中校和陆军军官普拉约戈中校，前往拜会了国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅。
