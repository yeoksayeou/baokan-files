Title: Kim Il-sung Hosts Banquet for Mongolian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 5

    Kim Il-sung Hosts Banquet for Mongolian Government Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th — Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, held a banquet tonight to welcome the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Tsedenbal, which is visiting North Korea.
    Among those present as guests were: Korean state leaders Kim Tu-bong, Choi Yong-kun, Park Jung-ai, and more than 300 people from all walks of life.
    Diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in North Korea were also invited to the banquet.
    Today, the Mongolian government delegation laid a wreath at the Liberation Tower and visited the city of Pyongyang.

### 金日成欢宴蒙古政府代表团

1956-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    金日成欢宴蒙古政府代表团
    新华社平壤26日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎到朝鲜访问的以泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。
    出席作陪的有：朝鲜国家领导人金枓奉、崔庸健、朴正爱等和各界人士共三百多人。
    各国驻朝鲜外交使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    今天，蒙古政府代表团向解放塔献花圈，并且参观了平壤市市容。
