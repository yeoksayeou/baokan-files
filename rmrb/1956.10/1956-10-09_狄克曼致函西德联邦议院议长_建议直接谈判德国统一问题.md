Title: Dieckmann Writes to Speaker of the West German Federal Assembly Suggesting Direct Negotiations on German Reunification
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th - Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, wrote to Gerstenmaier, Speaker of the Federal Assembly of the Federal Republic of Germany, suggesting that parliamentary representatives from both parts of Germany begin direct negotiations on the issue of German reunification.

In his letter, Dieckmann expressed his willingness to meet with Gerstenmaier to discuss the preliminary issues concerning negotiations between representatives of the People's Chamber and the Federal Assembly.

### 狄克曼致函西德联邦议院议长  建议直接谈判德国统一问题

1956-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    狄克曼致函西德联邦议院议长
    建议直接谈判德国统一问题
    据新华社柏林7日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼写信给德意志联邦共和国联邦议院议长格斯登美尔，建议德国两部分的议会派代表就德国统一问题开始进行直接谈判。
    狄克曼在信中表示愿意同格斯登美尔会晤，讨论有关人民议院和联邦议院代表进行谈判的先决问题。
