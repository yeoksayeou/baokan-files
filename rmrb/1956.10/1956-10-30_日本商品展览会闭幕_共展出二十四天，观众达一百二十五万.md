Title: Japanese Product Exhibition Closes; 1.25 Million Visitors in 24 Days
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Japanese Product Exhibition closed in Beijing today. Japanese Product Exhibition delegation members and Chinese staff assisting with the Japanese Product Exhibition held a closing celebration this evening.

    Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, attended the closing celebration and delivered a speech. Eiichi Shukutani, head of the Japanese Product Exhibition Delegation, Xie Qin, Director of the Foreign Exhibition Department of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Fumio Hashikawa, head of the Japanese Local Assembly Members League for Promoting International Trade delegation to China, also spoke at the meeting. They all congratulated the successful conclusion of the Japanese Product Exhibition. In his speech, Shukutani Eiichi expressed heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the Japanese Product Exhibition delegation and the Japanese people to the Chinese government, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and all relevant departments for their assistance with the exhibition. In his speech, Hashikawa Fumio stated that the Japanese Local Assembly Members League for Promoting International Trade will strive to break down the current artificial obstacles in the future and work to develop friendly relations between Japan and China and expand trade between the two countries.

    The Japanese Product Exhibition was held in Beijing for twenty-four days, and was visited by a total of more than 1.25 million people.

### 日本商品展览会闭幕  共展出二十四天，观众达一百二十五万

1956-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    日本商品展览会闭幕
    共展出二十四天，观众达一百二十五万
    新华社29日讯　日本商品展览会今天在北京闭幕。日本商品展览团人员和协助日本商品展览会工作的中国工作人员，今晚举行了闭幕联欢晚会。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民参加了联欢晚会并讲了话。日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一、中国国际贸易促进委员会国外来华展览工作部部长谢钦和日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟访华代表团团长端川文夫也先后在会上讲了话。他们一致祝贺日本商品展览会胜利闭幕。宿谷荣一在讲话中，代表日本商品展览团人员和日本人民，对中国政府、中国国际贸易促进委员会和各有关部门对展览会的帮助，表示衷心的感谢。端川文夫在讲话中表示：日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟今后将努力打破目前人为的障碍，为发展日中友好关系和扩大两国的贸易而努力。
    日本商品展览会在北京展出了二十四天，前往参观的观众共有一百二十五万多人。
