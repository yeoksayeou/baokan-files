Title: "Three-Nation African Alliance" Approaching Maturity; Sudanese Prime Minister Invites Egyptian and Ethiopian Leaders to Visit

Author:

Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd dispatch from Cairo: The "Al-Ahram" newspaper reported on October 22nd that Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil has invited Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie and Egyptian President Nasser to visit Sudan.

	The "Al-Ahram" correspondent in Khartoum reported that the Sudanese Prime Minister hopes that during the visit of the Ethiopian Emperor and the Egyptian President to Sudan, Sudan, Egypt, and Ethiopia will sign an agreement on the "Three-Nation African Alliance." The issue of establishing the "Three-Nation African Alliance" is currently under discussion.

	Khalil told the "Al-Ahram" reporter that the proposed "Three-Nation African Alliance" is defensive and will become the core of the anti-colonialist movement of African countries.

	"Al-Ahram" also quoted Sudanese Foreign Minister Mahgoub as saying that the Ethiopian Emperor will visit Khartoum after his state visit to Egypt in February next year.

### “三国非洲联盟”接近成熟  苏丹总理邀请埃及和埃塞俄比亚元首前往访问

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    “三国非洲联盟”接近成熟
    苏丹总理邀请埃及和埃塞俄比亚元首前往访问
    新华社23日讯　开罗消息：“金字塔报”10月22日报道，苏丹总理哈利勒已经邀请埃塞俄比亚国王塞拉西和埃及总统纳赛尔访问苏丹。
      “金字塔报”驻喀士穆记者报道说，苏丹总理希望，在埃塞俄比亚国王和埃及总统访问苏丹的时候，苏丹、埃及、埃塞俄比亚将签订一项关于“三国非洲联盟”的协定。成立“三国非洲联盟”的问题目前正在讨论中。
    哈利勒对“金字塔报”记者说，拟议中的“三国非洲联盟”是防御性的，并且将成为非洲国家反殖民主义运动的核心。
      “金字塔报”同时援引了苏丹外交部长马哈古卜的话说，埃塞俄比亚国王明年2月在埃及进行国事访问后将访问喀士穆。
