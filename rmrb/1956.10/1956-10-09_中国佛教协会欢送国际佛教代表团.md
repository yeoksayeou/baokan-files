Title: Chinese Buddhist Association Fetes International Buddhist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: The Chinese Buddhist Association held a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to the International Buddhist Monastic Delegation.

President Xirao Gyatso said in his speech at the banquet that the International Buddhist Monastic Delegation had brought the pure, sincere, and precious friendship of Buddhists and people from seven Buddhist countries to the Chinese Buddhist community and people. He wished that the consolidated friendship between all Buddhist countries would be as eternal as Mount Sumeru, and hoped that Buddhist circles of all countries would make new contributions to the common cause of stopping war and maintaining lasting world peace.

The head of the delegation, Indian Bhadanta Ananda Kausalayana, said that during the delegation's visit to China, it had achieved the purpose of strengthening the friendship between Buddhist countries in Asia and the people of all countries. He said: "When we came, we were strangers, but when we leave, we have become close friends."

The Indian monk also said, "We are very happy to see the good influence of the religious policy of the People's Republic of China on religion."

Vice-Presidents of the Chinese Buddhist Association, Dharma Master Neng Hai, Layman Zhao Puchu, and Living Buddha Gara Zang, as well as Vice-President of the Chinese Buddhist Academy, Dharma Master Fazun, attended the banquet.

Invited to the banquet were Zhang Ce, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council; Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; and Ma Songting, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Cultural Association.

Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Zhou Liang, Counselor of the Indian Embassy, Singh, and First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy, U Ba Maung, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 中国佛教协会欢送国际佛教代表团

1956-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中国佛教协会欢送国际佛教代表团
    据新华社8日讯　中国佛教协会今天中午举行宴会，欢送国际佛教僧侣代表团。
    喜饶嘉措会长在宴会上致词说，国际佛教僧侣代表团带来了七个佛教国家的佛教徒和人民对中国佛教界及人民的清净真挚的珍贵友谊。他祝愿各佛教国家之间的巩固友谊像须弥山一样永固，并希望各国佛教界为制止战争和维护世界持久和平的共同事业努力作出新的贡献。
    代表团团长印度巴丹达·阿难陀·柯萨尔雅雅那法师说，代表团在中国的访问期间，已经达到了加强亚洲各佛教国家和各国人民之间的友谊这个目的。他说：“我们来的时候还是陌生人，但是走的时候却成了亲密的朋友。”
    这位印度法师又说，“我们很高兴能够看到中华人民共和国的宗教政策对宗教所起的良好影响。”
    中国佛教协会副会长能海法师、赵朴初居士和噶喇藏活佛以及中国佛学院副院长法尊法师出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的有国务院副秘书长张策，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋，华侨事务委员会副主任庄希泉，中国回民文化协进会副主任马松亭。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮，印度大使馆参赞辛格和缅甸大使馆一等秘书吴巴茂也应邀出席了宴会。
