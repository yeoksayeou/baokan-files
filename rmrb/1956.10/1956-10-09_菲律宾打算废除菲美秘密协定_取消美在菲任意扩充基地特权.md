Title: Philippines Plans to Abolish Secret Philippine-U.S. Agreement, Cancel U.S. Privilege of Expanding Bases at Will in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 7th dispatch from TASS: During the negotiations in Manila between the Philippines and the United States regarding U.S. military bases in the Philippines, the Philippine side intends to abolish a right secretly granted to the United States when the Philippine-U.S. Military Bases Agreement was signed in 1947.
    In 1947, at the same time as signing the basic agreement on U.S. military bases in the Philippines, the two sides also exchanged secret notes. According to these notes, the United States obtained the right to expand its military bases in the Philippines and increase its troop deployments without seeking the consent of the Philippine government.
    The Philippine side now wants to abolish this secret "agreement" and has proposed a plan to establish a Philippine-U.S. Joint Committee to handle "common defense" issues. American newspapers point out that if this committee is established, the Americans will have to consult with the Philippine government on issues related to U.S. military bases.

### 菲律宾打算废除菲美秘密协定  取消美在菲任意扩充基地特权

1956-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    菲律宾打算废除菲美秘密协定  取消美在菲任意扩充基地特权
    据新华社7日讯　塔斯社纽约消息，在菲律宾同美国在马尼拉举行的关于美国在菲军事基地的谈判中，菲律宾方面打算取消1947年签订美菲军事基地协定时秘密给予美国的一项权利。
    1947年，在签订关于美国在菲律宾的军事基地的基本协定的同时，双方还交换了秘密照会，根据这项照会美国获得了这样的权利：美国不征求菲律宾政府的同意就可以在菲律宾扩充自己的军事基地和增加自己的驻军。
    菲律宾方面现在想废除这一秘密“协定”，它提出了建立一个处理“共同防御”问题的菲美联合委员会的计划。美国报纸指出，如果这一委员会建立了起来，美国人就不得不同菲律宾政府商量有关美国军事基地的问题。
