Title: Chinese and Pakistani Prime Ministers Continue Talks
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and the Prime Minister of Pakistan, Hussein Shaheed Suhrawardy, continued their talks this afternoon.
    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Geng Biao, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin.
    The Pakistani side participating in the talks included Pakistani Ambassador to China Ahmed, Foreign Secretary M.S.A. Baig, and Joint Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Commonwealth Relations S.K. Dehlavi.

### 中巴两国总理继续会谈

1956-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中巴两国总理继续会谈
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理和巴基斯坦侯赛恩·沙希德·苏拉瓦底总理今天下午继续进行会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理贺龙，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚和外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋。
    巴基斯坦方面参加会谈的，有巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德，外交秘书米·西·阿·贝格，外交和联邦关系部联合秘书斯·克·德拉维。
