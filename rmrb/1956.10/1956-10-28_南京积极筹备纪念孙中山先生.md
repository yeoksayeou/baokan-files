Title: Nanjing Actively Prepares to Commemorate Mr. Sun Yat-sen
Author:
Date: 1956-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 27th: The Standing Committee of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the Standing Committee of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC held enlarged meetings today to discuss the preparations for the 90th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth and established the Preparatory Committee for the 90th Anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth.
    Nanjing is the location of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's mausoleum and where Mr. Sun Yat-sen took office as Provisional President. People from all walks of life in Nanjing will hold a memorial meeting on November 12.
    On the anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth, people from all walks of life in Nanjing will visit the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to pay respects and lay wreaths; and visit Mr. Sun Yat-sen's office when he took office as Provisional President.

### 南京积极筹备纪念孙中山先生

1956-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    南京积极筹备纪念孙中山先生
    新华社南京27日电　政协江苏省委员会常委会和政协南京市委员会常委会今天举行扩大会议，讨论孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念的筹备工作，并且成立了孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念筹备委员会。
    南京是孙中山先生陵墓和孙中山先生就任临时大总统的所在地，南京市各界人民将在11月12日举行纪念大会。
    在中山先生诞辰的纪念日子里，南京市各界人民将去中山陵谒陵、献花圈；瞻仰孙中山先生就任临时大总统时的办公室。
