Title: Changsha Overseas Chinese Federation Established
Author:
Date: 1956-10-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, October 30th: The Changsha Overseas Chinese Federation has been formally established. At the Changsha Overseas Chinese Representatives Conference, which closed on the 29th, Yang Wenyuan, Li Ding and twenty-five others were elected to serve as Chairman, Vice-Chairman, and members of the Changsha Overseas Chinese Federation, respectively.

This four-day conference adopted the charter of the Changsha Overseas Chinese Federation, and defined the Federation's working principles and basic tasks.

The conference also unanimously adopted a letter of protest against the Hong Kong British authorities for taking actions to shield and condone Kuomintang secret agents' persecution of compatriots in Hong Kong and Kowloon.

### 长沙归国华侨联合会成立

1956-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

    长沙归国华侨联合会成立
    据新华社长沙30日电　长沙归国华侨联合会正式成立。在29日闭幕的长沙归国华侨代表会议上，选出杨文苑、李定等二十七人分别担任长沙归国华侨联合会的主席、副主席和委员。
    这次历时四天的会议，通过了长沙归国华侨联合会的章程，确定了联合会的工作方针和基本任务。
    会议还一致通过了抗议香港英国当局采取包庇和纵容国民党特务分子残害港九同胞的行为的抗议书。
