Title: Delegation from the All-Russia Association of the Blind of the Soviet Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. A four-member delegation from the All-Russia Association of the Blind of the Soviet Union arrived in Beijing this afternoon.
    The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Xie Juezai, Chairman of the Chinese Blind Welfare Association, and Vice Chairmen Wu Yunfu, Huang Nai, and others.
    After participating in our National Day celebrations, the delegation will visit various places.

### 苏联全俄盲人协会代表团到北京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联全俄盲人协会代表团到北京
    新华社30日讯　苏联全俄罗斯盲人协会代表团一行四人今天下午到达北京。
    代表团人员在飞机场上受到中国盲人福利会主任委员谢觉哉、副主任委员伍云甫、黄乃等人的欢迎。
    这个代表团在参加我国国庆节典礼后，将到各地参观访问。
