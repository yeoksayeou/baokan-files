Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives Former Indonesian Minister of Defense
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, received Iwa Koesoemasoemantri, former Minister of Defense of Indonesia, on the evening of the 8th.

### 彭德怀元帅接见印度尼西亚前国防部长

1956-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅接见印度尼西亚前国防部长
    据新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，在8日晚间接见了印度尼西亚前国防部长伊哇·库素玛素曼特里。
