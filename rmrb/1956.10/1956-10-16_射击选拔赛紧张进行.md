Title: Shooting Trials Proceeding Intensely
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: In today's shooting trials for the Chinese Olympic team, Zhang Hong, once known as a sharpshooter, achieved a score of 289 rings (on-site score) in the first stage of the rapid-fire pistol silhouette target event, surpassing the first-place record of the previous Olympic Games in the same event. Another athlete, Li Jie, achieved a score of 287 rings (on-site score) in the same event today, matching the first-place score of the previous Olympic Games.

In today's men's 50-meter (60 shots) prone position shooting selection with a small-bore rifle, Zhao Shanjun achieved a score of 595 rings (on-site score), earning first place. This score has reached the standard of a Master of Sports in our country's shooting sport.

The National Shooting Games held the women's 50-meter and 100-meter prone position small-bore sport rifle shooting competition today. According to the on-site score statistics of the Organizing Committee of the Games, the Liaoning Provincial Shooting Team won the team first place with a total score of 1647 rings. The best individual score was achieved by Peng Qianhua from Hunan Province (556 rings, with a perfect score of 600 rings).

### 射击选拔赛紧张进行

1956-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    射击选拔赛紧张进行
    据新华社15日讯　在今天的中国奥林匹克运动队射击选拔赛中，曾经被称为神枪手的张鈜，手枪人像靶速射第一阶段的成绩（现场成绩）达到二八九环，超过了上一届奥林匹克运动会这项比赛的第一名的纪录。另一名运动员李杰今天同一项的射击成绩（现场成绩）达到二八七环，和上届奥林匹克这项射击第一名的成绩相同。
    今天男子自选小口径步枪五十公尺（六十发）卧姿射击选拔中，赵善俊以五九五环的成绩（现场成绩）获得了第一名，这个成绩已经达到了我国射击运动健将级的标准。
    全国射击运动大会今天进行了女子小口径运动步枪五十公尺和一百公尺卧姿射击比赛。根据大会组织委员会的现场成绩统计，辽宁省射击代表队以一六四七环的总成绩获得团体第一名。个人成绩最好的是湖南省的彭前华（五五六环，满环是六○○环）。
