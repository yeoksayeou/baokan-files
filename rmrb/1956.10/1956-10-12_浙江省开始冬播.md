Title: Zhejiang Province Begins Winter Planting
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 9th. Various regions of Zhejiang Province have successively begun sowing barley, wheat, rapeseed, and grass seeds.

This year, Zhejiang Province plans to reach a winter planting area of more than 27 million mu, an increase of more than one-tenth compared to last year. Among them, the rapeseed planting area is required to reach 3.57 million mu, an increase of 740,000 mu compared to last year.

### 浙江省开始冬播

1956-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

    浙江省开始冬播
    新华社杭州9日电　浙江省各地已先后开始播种大麦、小麦、油菜和草籽。
    浙江省今年冬播面积计划达到二千七百多万亩，比去年增加十分之一以上。其中油菜播种面积要求达到三百五十七万亩，比去年增加七十四万亩。
