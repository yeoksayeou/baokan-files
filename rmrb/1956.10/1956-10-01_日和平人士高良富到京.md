Title: Japanese Peace Advocate Tomi Kora Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Japanese peace advocate and member of the Japanese House of Councillors, Tomi Kora, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Those who went to the airport to welcome her included leaders from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

### 日和平人士高良富到京

1956-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    日和平人士高良富到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来中国访问的日本和平人士、日本参议院议员高良富在今日到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中华全国民主妇女联合会负责人。
