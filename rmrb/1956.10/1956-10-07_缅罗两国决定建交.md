Title: Myanmar and Romania Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 6th. The Myanmar Foreign Ministry announced on the afternoon of the 5th that the Government of the Union of Myanmar and the Government of the Romanian People's Republic have unanimously agreed to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys at the level of ministers.

### 缅罗两国决定建交

1956-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    缅罗两国决定建交
    新华社仰光6日电　缅甸外交部5日下午宣布，缅甸联邦政府和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府一致同意建立外交关系和互派公使级的外交使节。
