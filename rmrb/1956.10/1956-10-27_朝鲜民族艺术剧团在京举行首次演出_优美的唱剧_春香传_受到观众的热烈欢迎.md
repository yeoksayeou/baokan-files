Title: Korean National Art Troupe Holds First Performance in Beijing; Beautiful Changgeuk "The Story of Chun香" Receives Warm Welcome from Audience
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: The National Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea gave its first performance in China tonight. Korean artists met with the capital's audience on the stage of the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing with their brilliant national art. The performed repertoire was the Changgeuk "The Story of Chun香." The performance of classical works originating from the Korean nation by Korean artists in a strong national style aroused greater interest among the audience.

More than 1,500 people attended the performance tonight, including Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing.

Before the official performance, Ding Xilin delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and the Chinese cultural and artistic circles, welcoming the Korean artists' visit and performance in China, believing that this will further strengthen the great friendship between the people of China and Korea. Yun Du-heon, head of the Korean National Art Troupe, also spoke. He said: "The Korean people fervently hope that we will perform Korean art for the fraternal Chinese people. Please accept the gift presented to you by the Korean people, the flowers of Korean art."

The performance of the Korean artists tonight was warmly welcomed by the audience in the capital. At the end of each act, the audience expressed their gratitude to the artists with prolonged applause. Vice Premier He Long and Minister Shen Yanbing and others met with the leaders of the troupe during the intermission. More than one hundred young literary and art workers from the capital also presented flowers to each Korean artist.

### 朝鲜民族艺术剧团在京举行首次演出  优美的唱剧“春香传”受到观众的热烈欢迎

1956-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜民族艺术剧团在京举行首次演出
    优美的唱剧“春香传”受到观众的热烈欢迎
    据新华社26日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国立民族艺术剧团今晚举行访华首次演出，朝鲜艺术家们以他们的光采绚丽的民族艺术在北京天桥剧场的舞台上和首都观众见了面。演的剧目是唱剧“春香传”。朝鲜的艺术家们以浓厚的民族风格演出的产生自朝鲜民族的古典作品，引起了观众更大的兴趣。
    今晚出席观看的有国务院副总理贺龙，文化部部长沈雁冰等一千五百多人。
    正式演出前，丁西林代表文化部和我国文化艺术界致词，欢迎朝鲜艺术家们在中国的访问演出，认为这将进一步加强中朝两国人民的伟大友谊。朝鲜国立民族艺术剧团团长尹斗宪也讲了话。他说：“朝鲜人民热烈地希望我们把朝鲜艺术演出给兄弟般的中国人民看，请你们接受朝鲜人民向你们献出的礼物，朝鲜的艺术花朵。”
    今晚朝鲜艺术家们的演出，获得了首都观众们热烈的欢迎。每一场戏结束，观众们都用历久的鼓掌来向艺术家们表示感谢。贺龙副总理和沈雁冰部长等在幕间休息时曾接见了剧团的领导人。首都的一百多个青年文艺工作者还向每一个朝鲜艺术家献了鲜花。
