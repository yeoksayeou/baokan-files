Title: U Nu to Visit Our Country Soon

Author:

Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th. At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of the Union of Burma, and his wife will come to our country on October 22, 1956, for a friendly visit.

### 吴努即将来我国访问

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努即将来我国访问
    新华社11日讯　缅甸联邦反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请将于1956年10月22日偕夫人前来我国进行友好访问。
