Title: Reception Held by Delegation of the Belgian Kingdom's Parliament

Author:

Date: 1956-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The delegation of the Belgian Kingdom's Parliament held a reception on the evening of the 14th to bid farewell to people from all walks of life in China.

The head of the delegation, Mr. Huysmans, spoke at the reception. He expressed gratitude for the warm hospitality they had received during their time in China. He said, "We have visited many countries recently, and your country has made the deepest impression on us." He said, "You have received us very kindly, and we will never forget it. Your country is a country with vast land, a large population, and the most ancient culture. After a long struggle, you have gained independence and freedom." He said, "We wish you success in overcoming all obstacles and hope that you can solve many international problems."

Huysmans added, "Your achievements in the past seven years have been great and have given us great encouragement. We hope that our friendly relations will become closer in the future, and we should become the closest of friends."

Chairman Liu Shaoqi then spoke. He said, "The delegation has conducted a friendly visit to various parts of China for more than twenty days, extensively contacted people from all walks of life, and frankly exchanged opinions. You have already become friends well-known to the Chinese people."

Chairman Liu Shaoqi said, "The Chinese people attach great importance to the development of friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Belgian peoples. We deeply welcome the delegation's friendly wishes to promote the establishment of normal relations between China and Belgium, develop trade, and exchange culture. We are convinced that the development of normal relations and friendly cooperation between China and Belgium fully conforms to the vital interests of the people of both countries and will be realized sooner or later."

Liu Shaoqi said, "Let us work together to develop normal relations and friendly cooperation between China and Belgium, and for world peace."

During the reception, the hosts and guests engaged in cordial and frank conversations. Also invited to attend the reception tonight were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, vice-chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as some members of the Standing Committee, Zhang Bojun, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and some members of the Standing Committee and members, Zhang Hanfu, vice minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and heads of various people's organizations.

The Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Sman, was also invited to attend.

### 比利时王国国会代表团举行招待会

1956-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    比利时王国国会代表团举行招待会
    新华社14日讯　比利时王国国会代表团在14日晚举行招待会，向中国各界人士辞别。
    代表团团长胡斯曼在会上讲话。他对他们在中国期间受到的盛情招待表示感谢。他说，我们最近访问了许多国家，你们给我们的印象最深刻。他说，你们非常友好地接待了我们，我们永远也不会忘记。你们的国家是土地辽阔、人口众多和文化最悠久的国家，经过长期的斗争以后，你们获得了独立和自由。他说，我们祝福你们克服一切障碍，并且希望通过你们来解决国际上许多的问题。
    胡斯曼又说，七年来，你们的事业是很伟大的，给了我们很大的鼓舞，希望在将来我们的友好关系更加密切，我们彼此应该成为最亲密的朋友。
    刘少奇委员长接着讲话。他说，代表团在中国各地进行了二十多天的友好访问，同各界人士广泛进行了接触，坦率地交换了意见，已经成为中国人很熟悉的朋友了。
    刘少奇委员长说，中国人民十分重视发展中比两国人民的友好合作，对于代表团诸位先生促进中比两国建立正常关系、发展贸易、交流文化的友好愿望，深表欢迎，我们确信，发展中比两国的正常关系和友好合作，完全符合中比两国人民的切身利益，迟早一定会实现的。
    刘少奇说，让我们为发展中比两国的正常关系和友好合作，为世界和平而共同努力。
    席间，宾主们亲切坦率地进行交谈。应邀出席今晚招待会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通和部分委员，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧和部分常务委员、委员，外交部副部长章汉夫和各人民团体负责人。
    荷兰驻中国代办司曼也应邀出席。
