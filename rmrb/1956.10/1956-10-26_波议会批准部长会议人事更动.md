Title: Polish Parliament Approves Personnel Changes in the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 25th. The Polish Parliament approved the list of personnel changes in the Polish Council of Ministers proposed by Council of Ministers Chairman Cyrankiewicz at its evening session on October 24th.

The session relieved the following five people of their duties as Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers: T. Gede, S. Jędrychowski (who remains Chairman of the State Economic Planning Commission), S. Wapot, O. Stawiński (who remains Minister of Light Industry), and F. Jóźwiak.

The session relieved H. Minc and Z. Nowak of their duties as First Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers. The session approved S. Ignar and Z. Nowak as Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers.

In view of R. Zambrowski's election as Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, he was relieved of his duties as Minister of State Supervision.

### 波议会批准部长会议人事更动

1956-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    波议会批准部长会议人事更动
    新华社华沙25日电　波兰议会在10月24日晚间会议上批准了部长会议主席西伦凯维兹提出的波兰部长会议人事更动的名单。
    会议解除了下列五人部长会议副主席的职务：塔·盖德，斯·英德里霍夫斯基（仍然留任国家经济计划委员会主席），斯·瓦波特，欧·斯塔文斯基（仍然留任轻工业部长），弗·约兹维亚克。
    会议解除了希·明兹和泽·诺瓦克部长会议第一副主席的职务。会议批准斯·伊格纳尔和泽·诺瓦克为部长会议副主席。
    鉴于罗·萨姆布罗夫斯基当选为波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记，解除他国家监察部长的职务。
