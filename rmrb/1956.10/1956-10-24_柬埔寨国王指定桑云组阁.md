Title: Cambodian King Appoints Sangkum to Form Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 23rd -- According to a broadcast by Phnom Penh Radio today, King Suramarit of Cambodia announced that he has designated Sangkum to form a new cabinet. He said that this decision was made after consulting with the Speaker of the National Assembly and the Chairman of the Royal Council following the resignation of Sihanouk.

Sangkum had previously expressed difficulties in forming a cabinet and serving as prime minister, but was willing to work hard to complete the task during the cabinet crisis.

Sangkum once served as the mayor of Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia, and was a commissioner in the Prime Minister's Office before forming the cabinet.

### 柬埔寨国王指定桑云组阁

1956-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王指定桑云组阁
    新华社河内23日电　据金边电台今天广播：柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特宣布，他已经指定桑云出来组织新的内阁。他说，这是他在接受西哈努克辞职后同国民议会议长和皇廷会议主席商量后决定的。
    桑云曾经表示他在组阁和担任首相职务方面有困难，但是愿意在内阁危机期间努力去完成任务。
    桑云曾经任柬埔寨首都金边市市长，在组阁前是首相府的一个专员。
