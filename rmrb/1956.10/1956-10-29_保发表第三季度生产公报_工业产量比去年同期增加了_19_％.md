Title: Bulgaria Announces Third Quarter Production Bulletin, Industrial Output Increased by 19% Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, October 28th: According to TASS, the Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria published a communique on October 27th regarding the implementation of the national economic development plan for the third quarter of 1956.

The communique pointed out that the industrial production plan for the third quarter was fulfilled by 102.4%. Industrial output increased by 19% compared to the same period last year. Among them, electricity increased by 16%, lignite increased by 11%, petroleum increased by 39%, zinc increased more than fivefold, and cotton, wool, and silk fabrics increased by 9% to 32%; sugar increased by 48%.

In agriculture, the output of rice, sugar beets, long-fiber flax, apples, and other agricultural products all increased compared to last year. The output of grain products, sunflowers, certain vegetables, and fodder crops was lower than last year. In the third quarter, agriculture received 1,952 tractors (each calculated at 15 horsepower) and 269 combine harvesters. Great achievements were also made in the development of animal husbandry.

The turnover of commodities in this quarter increased by 15% compared to the same period last year. Sales of rice, meat, sugar, various fabrics, shoes, and many other commodities increased.

### 保发表第三季度生产公报  工业产量比去年同期增加了19％

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    保发表第三季度生产公报
    工业产量比去年同期增加了19％
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社索菲亚讯：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议中央统计局10月27日发表了关于1956年第三季度发展国民经济国家计划执行情况的公报。
    公报指出，第三季度的工业生产计划完成了102.4％。工业产量比去年同时期增加了19％。其中电力增加了16％，褐煤增加11％，石油增加39％，锌增加了五倍多，棉织品、毛织品和丝织品增加了9％到32％；糖增加了48％。
    在农业方面，稻子、甜菜、长纤维亚麻、苹果和其他农产品的产量都比去年提高了。谷物产品、向日葵、某些蔬菜和饲料作物的产量比去年为低。在第三季度里，农业获得了一千九百五十二台拖拉机（每台以十五匹马力计算）和二百六十九台联合机。在发展畜牧业方面也获得了很大成就。
    这一季度的商品周转额比去年同期提高了15％。大米、肉类、糖、各种织品、鞋类和其他许多商品的销售量都增加了。
