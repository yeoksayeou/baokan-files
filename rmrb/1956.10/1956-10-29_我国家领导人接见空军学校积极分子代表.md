Title: National Leaders Receive Representatives of Activists from Air Force Schools
Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lin Boqu, Peng Dehuai, and He Long, received representatives to the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force School Activists' Congress this morning. Marshal Ye Jianying and General Huang Kecheng also participated in the reception.

### 我国家领导人接见空军学校积极分子代表

1956-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人接见空军学校积极分子代表
    新华社28日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席和中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、彭德怀、贺龙，今天上午接见了中国人民解放军空军学校积极分子代表大会的代表。叶剑英元帅、黄克诚大将也参加了接见。
