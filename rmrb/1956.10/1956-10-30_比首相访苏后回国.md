Title: Belgian Prime Minister Returns Home After Visiting the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th dispatch from Brussels: Belgian Prime Minister Achille Van Acker returned to Brussels on the evening of October 26th from Moscow.

In summing up the visit, Van Acker mentioned the signing of a cultural agreement between the Soviet Union and Belgium and added that the development of trade relations between the two countries was foreseeable. A commercial agreement on shipping has been drafted, and this agreement will probably be signed before Foreign Minister Spaak returns to Belgium.

Van Acker said that such visits could promote better mutual understanding and ensure better international relations and peaceful coexistence.

### 比首相访苏后回国

1956-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    比首相访苏后回国
    据新华社29日讯　据塔斯社布鲁塞尔讯：比利时首相阿希尔·范阿克10月26日晚从莫斯科回到布鲁塞尔。
    范阿克在总结这次访问的时候提到苏联—比利时签订了文化问题协定，并且补充说，两国间贸易关系的发展是可以预见的。已经拟定了一项关于航运的商业协定，这一协定大概能在外交部长斯巴克回比利时以前签字。
    范阿克说，这样的访问能促进更好地相互了解，保证国际间保持更好的关系和和平共处。
