Title: Hungarian-Yugoslav Party Delegations to Hold Talks in Belgrade
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 6th. TASS, Budapest, 6th: An official communiqué was released here stating:

    The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party and the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia have reached an agreement through correspondence to hold talks between delegations from both parties. The talks will be held in Belgrade on October 15th of this year.

    The Hungarian Working People's Party delegation will include Gerő (head of the delegation), Apró, Hegedüs, Kádár, and Kovács.

### 匈南两国党的代表团将在贝尔格莱德会谈

1956-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    匈南两国党的代表团将在贝尔格莱德会谈
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社布达佩斯6日讯：这里发表一份正式公报说：
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会和南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会通过通讯联系已经达成协议，决定两党代表举行会谈。会谈将于今年10月15日在贝尔格莱德举行。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会方面参加会谈的将有格罗（代表团团长）、阿普罗、赫格居斯、卡达尔和科瓦奇等人。
