Title: Pakistan Ambassador's Wife Holds Tea Party
Author:
Date: 1956-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The wife of the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Mr. Ahmed, held a tea party this afternoon. The tea party was held for the visit to China by Prime Minister Suhrawardy's daughter, Mrs. Suleiman, Mrs. Noon, and Princess Sultan.

Invited to attend the tea party were Li Dequan and Shi Liang, vice-chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the wife of Vice-Chairman Li Jishen, the wife of Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, the wife of the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Geng Biao, the wife of the director of the China Islamic Association, Bao Erhan, the wife of the president of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, Liu Geping, and the wives of diplomatic officials from various countries to China, totaling more than 100 people.

The tea party was held in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere for an hour and a half.

### 巴驻华大使夫人举行茶会

1956-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    巴驻华大使夫人举行茶会
    新华社23日讯　巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德的夫人今天下午举行茶会。这次茶会是为苏拉瓦底总理的女儿苏来曼夫人、努恩夫人和苏尔唐公主等访问中国而举行的。
    应邀出席茶会的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、史良、李济深副委员长的夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫的夫人，中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚的夫人，中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉的夫人，中巴友好协会会长刘格平的夫人，各国驻华外交官员的夫人们等共一百多人。
    茶会在愉快友好的气氛中进行了一个半小时。
