Title: Soviet Communist Party Central Delegation Returns to Moscow from Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st: According to TASS, the delegation of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, consisting of Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Molotov, and Khrushchev, members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, returned to Moscow from Warsaw on October 20.
    When they left Warsaw on the same day, those who saw them off at the airport included members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party: Cyrankiewicz, Dworakowski, Jóźwiak-Witold, R. Nowak, Z. Nowak, Ochab, Rapacki, Rokossowski, Zambrowski, Zawadzki, and others. Gomulka, a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏共中央代表团从华沙回到莫斯科

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央代表团从华沙回到莫斯科
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、莫洛托夫和赫鲁晓夫组成的苏共中央主席团代表团，已经于10月20日由华沙回到莫斯科。
    他们在当天离开华沙的时候，到机场欢送的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员西伦凯维兹、德沃拉科夫斯基、约兹维亚克—韦托德、罗·诺瓦克、泽·诺瓦克、奥哈布、拉帕茨基、罗科索夫斯基、萨姆布罗夫斯基、萨瓦茨基等。波兰统一工人党中央委员会委员哥穆尔卡也曾经到机场欢送。
