Title: Chairman Mao Visits Japanese Commodity Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Mr. Shozo Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Mr. Eiichi Yadoya, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Delegation, this noon, meticulously toured the exhibits in all sections of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition. The visit lasted for one hour and thirty minutes.

### 毛主席参观日本商品展览会

1956-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席参观日本商品展览会
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席今天中午在日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏、日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一的陪同下，详细地参观了日本商品展览会各个部分的展品。参观共历时一小时三十分。
