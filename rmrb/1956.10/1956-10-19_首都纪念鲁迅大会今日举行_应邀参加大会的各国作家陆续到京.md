Title: Capital Commemoration of Lu Xun Conference Held Today; Writers from Various Countries Invited to Attend the Conference Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The capital's "20th Anniversary Commemoration of Mr. Lu Xun's Death" is scheduled to be held at 2:00 PM on the 19th at the auditorium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing. The conference is organized by seven units including the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; participants include people from all walks of life in the capital and 1,600 writers from more than 20 countries who have been invited to China to attend the commemoration. The conference will be presided over by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, will deliver a report entitled "Lu Xun - From Revolutionary Democratism to Communism." Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and foreign writers attending the conference will also speak at the meeting.

The conference will also hold academic report meetings on the 20th and 22nd to commemorate the 20th anniversary of Lu Xun's death, presided over by Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Lao She, Zheng Zhenduo, and Feng Xuefeng, with writers and scholars presenting academic research reports.

A film evening will be held on the evening of the 20th, screening the documentary "The Life of Lu Xun" and the feature film "Blessing" adapted from Lu Xun's original work.

Various commemorative activities will also be held in various places on and around the anniversary.

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Writers from various countries who have been invited to China to attend the capital's "20th Anniversary Commemoration of Mr. Lu Xun's Death" have arrived in Beijing one after another.

They are Albanian writer Steljo Spasse, Bulgarian writer Nikola Marinov, Burmese writers U Thein Pe Myint and U Tin Maung, Czechoslovakian writers Běluša Křilebsová and Šarlota Stiepanová, British writer John Sommerfield, Hungarian writer Szamiuo Georgy, Indonesian writer Pramoedya Ananta Toer, Italian writer Malaparte, Japanese writers Kanzo Uchimaya, Kyōya Satomi, Shizuo Uchimaya, Yoshiro Nagayo, Mochiko Nagayo, and Yonnosuke Natori, Korean writer Han Sorya, Mongolian writers Choiralsuren and Erhembayar, Pakistani writer Ahmed Ali, Polish writer Jerzy Putrament, Romanian writer Auel Mihal, Soviet writers Polevoi, Galin, and Zalygin, Vietnamese writers Phan Khoi and Tran Te Xuong, Yugoslav writers Ivo Andrić and Filip Kljajić, and others.

German Academy of Sciences Vice President and Sinologist Steinitz, Czechoslovakian Sinologist Průšek and well-known cultural figures Stoll, and Australian writer Cusack, who are currently in China, will also be invited to attend the conference.

### 首都纪念鲁迅大会今日举行  应邀参加大会的各国作家陆续到京

1956-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    首都纪念鲁迅大会今日举行
    应邀参加大会的各国作家陆续到京
    新华社18日讯　首都的“鲁迅先生逝世二十周年纪念大会”定19日下午二时在北京中国人民政治协商会议礼堂举行。大会是中国文学艺术界联合会等七个单位负责筹备的；参加大会的有首都各界人士和应邀来我国参加纪念会的二十多个国家的作家等一千六百人。大会将由中国科学院院长、中国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若主持，中国文学艺术界联合会副主席、中国作家协会主席茅盾作题为“鲁迅——从革命民主主义到共产主义”的报告，中共中央宣传部部长陆定一和参加大会的外国作家也将在会上讲话。
    大会还将在20日和22日举办鲁迅逝世二十周年纪念的学术报告会，由郭沫若、茅盾、周扬、老舍、郑振铎、冯雪峰主持，有作家和学者作学术研究报告。
    纪念会还将在20日晚上举行电影晚会，放映文献纪录片“鲁迅生平”和根据鲁迅原著改编的故事片“祝福”。
    各地也将在纪念日和纪念日前后分别举行各项纪念活动。
    新华社18日讯　应邀来我国参加首都“鲁迅先生逝世二十周年纪念大会”的各国作家陆续到达北京。
    他们是阿尔巴尼亚作家斯捷利奥·斯巴赛，保加利亚作家尼古拉·马里诺夫，缅甸作家吴登佩密、吴廷发，捷克斯洛伐克作家别尔查·克列布索娃、沙利·斯蒂芬，英国作家约翰·索麦费尔德，匈牙利作家萨米奥·乔治，印度尼西亚作家普拉姆迪雅·阿南达·杜尔，意大利作家马拉帕脱，日本作家内山完造、里见弴、内山静夫、长与善郎、长与茂子、名取洋之助，朝鲜作家韩雪野，蒙古作家乔然尔查文，尔哈木苏伦，巴基斯坦作家艾阿默德·阿里，波兰作家魏得志，罗马尼亚作家阿乌埃勒·米哈尔，苏联作家波列沃伊、加林、扎雷金，越南作家潘魁、陈济亨，南斯拉夫作家伊伏·安得利奇和菲里浦·孔巴多维奇等人。
    正在中国的德国科学院副院长、汉学家施太尼次，捷克斯洛伐克汉学家普实克和文化界著名人士斯托尔，澳大利亚作家古沙克等人也将应邀参加大会。
