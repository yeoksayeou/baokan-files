Title: Japanese Hydrological Science Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - The Japanese Hydrological Science Delegation, invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by its head, Saburo Taniguchi, and consisted of six members.

Representatives from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the preparatory committee of the Chinese Hydraulic Engineering Society were at the airport to welcome them.

### 日本水利科学访华代表团到京

1956-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    日本水利科学访华代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国科学院邀请来中国访问的日本水利科学访华代表团，在团长谷口三郎率领下一行六人，今天乘飞机到北京。
    中国科学院、中国水利学会筹备委员会代表多人到飞机场欢迎。
