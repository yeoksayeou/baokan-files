Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Delegation Leaves for Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1956-10-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 15th: According to TASS, Budapest, 15th, the delegation of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party has departed for Belgrade. The delegation will hold talks with the delegation of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

The head of the delegation is Gerő, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party. Members of the delegation include Apró, Hegedüs, Kádár, and Kovács, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party.

### 匈劳动人民党代表团动身去贝尔格莱德

1956-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党代表团动身去贝尔格莱德
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯15日讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会代表团已经动身前往贝尔格莱德。代表团将同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会代表团会谈。
    代表团团长是匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记格罗。代表团团员有匈牙利劳动人民党中央政治局委员阿普罗、赫格居斯、卡达尔和科瓦奇。
