    Warsaw University Emphasizes Papyrology Research

    Warsaw University has a Papyrological Institute, which is the center for papyrological research in Poland. In the past decade, the institute has edited and published more than seventy scientific works both domestically and internationally. Of particular note is Professor Taubenschlag's book, "Greco-Roman Law in Egyptian Papyri," which comprises eight hundred pages. This is the only work in world literature that studies this issue. The institute maintains extensive contacts with scientific institutions worldwide. Polish papyrologists are members of the International Association of Papyrologists, regularly corresponding with papyrologists from all foreign institutes, exchanging publications, and publishing works by papyrologists from various countries in their journal, the "Journal of Juristic Papyrology."

    To promote international cooperation, the institute, with the support of the Polish Academy of Sciences, convened a conference on ancient legal issues last December. Scholars from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries also participated in this conference.

    Note: Papyrology is an auxiliary historical discipline that studies ancient documents written on papyrus from ancient Egypt and other countries. It is of significant importance for studying the laws of ancient Egypt and other countries.

### 华沙大学重视纸草学的研究

1956-10-23
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    华沙大学重视纸草学的研究
    华沙大学有一个纸草学研究所，这是波兰研究纸草学的中心。这个研究所在十年来的活动中，编辑了在国内外出版的七十多种科学著作。其中特别值得提及的是塔乌宾施拉格教授所著的计达八百页的“埃及用纸草纸写成的希腊—罗马法”一书。这是世界文献中研究这个问题的唯一著作。研究所同全世界的科学机关保持着广泛的联系。波兰纸草学家是国际纸草学者联合会的会员，他们经常同所有外国研究所的纸草学者通信，交换出版物，并在自己的刊物“法律纸草学年志”上发表各国纸草学者的著作。
    为了发扬国际间的合作，研究所在波兰科学院的赞助下曾于去年12月召开过研究古代法律问题的会议。苏联和各人民民主国家的学者也参加了这个会议。
    按：纸草学是一门辅助的历史学科，以古埃及等国家用纸草纸写成的古文献为研究对象，对于研究古代埃及等国家的法律有重要的意义。
