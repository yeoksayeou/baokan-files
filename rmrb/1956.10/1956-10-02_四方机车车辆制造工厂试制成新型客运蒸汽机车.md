Title: Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory Successfully Trial-Manufactures New Passenger Steam Locomotive
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, October 1st. The first new ㄇT6-type locomotive successfully trial-manufactured by the Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory rolled off the production line on the eve of National Day. This locomotive is a passenger steam locomotive with 1,500 horsepower. When running on level ground, it can pull 800 tons at a speed of no less than 85 kilometers per hour. Compared with the existing ㄇT6-type locomotives designed by the Japanese in China, its horsepower is increased by 4.4%, and fuel can be saved by 7.6%.

This new product has undergone technical inspection and test runs before leaving the factory, proving that the quality fully meets the requirements, and is scheduled to be officially put into production in October.

### 四方机车车辆制造工厂试制成新型客运蒸汽机车

1956-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    四方机车车辆制造工厂试制成新型客运蒸汽机车
    据新华社青岛1日电　四方机车车辆制造工厂试制成功的第一台新的攵T6型机车在国庆节前夕出厂。这种机车是具有一千五百马力的客运蒸汽机车，运行在平道上的时候，能以不低于每小时八十五公里的速度牵引八百吨。它比中国现有的过去日本人设计的攵T6型机车，马力提高4.4％，燃料能节省7.6％。
    这一新产品经出厂前进行技术检验和试车证明，质量完全合乎要求，预定在10月份正式生产。
