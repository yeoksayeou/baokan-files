Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Reorganizes Leadership: Kadar Elected First Secretary, Apro and Others Form Politburo; Presidium of the Republic Appoints Nagy as Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1956-10-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 26th - Budapest Dispatch: At its meeting on October 25th, the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party elected János Kádár as First Secretary of the Central Committee, replacing Ernő Gerő.

Prior to this, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, upon the recommendation of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, appointed Imre Nagy as Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, with the former Chairman, Hegedüs, becoming First Deputy Chairman.

On the evening of October 23rd, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party held an emergency meeting to discuss the situation that had arisen in Hungary and reorganized the party's leadership. Ferenc Donáth, Géza Losonczy, György Lukács, Ferenc Münnich, and Imre Nagy were elected to the Central Committee.

The Central Committee re-elected the Politburo and Secretariat. The following eleven comrades were elected as members of the Politburo: Antal Apró, Sándor Gáspár, Ernő Gerő, András Hegedüs, Gyula Kállai, János Kádár, Károly Kiss, György Marosán, Zoltán Szántó, Imre Nagy, and József Köböl. The following were elected as Secretaries of the Central Committee: First Secretary Gerő, Donáth, Kádár, and Kállai.

Nagy served as Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers from July 1953 to April 1955. His successor was Hegedüs.

Gerő replaced Rákosi as First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Working People's Party in July of this year.

Kádár was elected to the Central Committee and Politburo of the Working People's Party in July of this year.

### 匈劳动人民党中央改组领导机构  卡达尔当选第一书记　奥普罗等组成政治局  共和国主席团任命纳吉为部长会议主席

1956-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党中央改组领导机构
    卡达尔当选第一书记　奥普罗等组成政治局
    共和国主席团任命纳吉为部长会议主席
    新华社布拉格26日电　布达佩斯消息：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会政治局在10月25日的会议上选举了亚诺什·卡达尔代替埃诺·格罗为中央委员会第一书记。
    在此以前，匈牙利人民共和国主席团根据匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会政治局的建议，任命了伊姆雷·纳吉为匈牙利部长会议主席，原任主席赫格居斯为第一副主席。
    10月23日晚上，匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会举行了紧急会议，讨论了匈牙利已经产生的情况，并且改组了党的领导机构。费伦茨·多纳特，格佐·洛松齐，捷尔吉·卢卡奇，费伦茨·明尼奇，伊姆雷·纳吉等五位同志被选入中央委员会。
    中央委员会改选了政治局和书记处。当选为政治局委员的有：安托尔·奥普罗，山多尔·加什帕尔，埃诺·格罗，安德拉什·赫格居斯，久洛·科洛伊，亚诺什·卡达尔，卡罗利·基什，捷·马罗山，佐尔坦·桑托，伊姆雷·纳吉，约瑟夫·克贝耳等十一位同志。当选为中央委员会书记的有：第一书记格罗，多纳特，卡达尔和科洛伊。
    纳吉在1953年7月到1955年4月期间曾经担任过匈牙利部长会议主席。他后来的继任者是赫格居斯。
    格罗曾经在今年7月间接替拉科西担任劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记。
    卡达尔是在今年7月间被选入劳动人民党中央委员会和政治局的。
