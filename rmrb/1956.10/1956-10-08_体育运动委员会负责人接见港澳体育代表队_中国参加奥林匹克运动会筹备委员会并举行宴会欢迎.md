Title: Sports Commission Leaders Receive Hong Kong and Macao Sports Delegations; China Olympic Games Preparatory Committee Hosts Banquet to Welcome Them

Author:

Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Leaders of the Sports Commission met with the entire Hong Kong and Macao sports delegations this morning.

    Cai Tingkai, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, spoke during the meeting, extending a warm welcome to the Hong Kong and Macao sports delegations' visit to Beijing. Cai Tingkai briefed them on the vigorous development of sports in China in recent years. He said that we must mobilize all the strength of the sports community to bring all Chinese sports to international levels.

    Cai Shufan, Director of the China Olympic Games Preparatory Committee for the 16th Olympic Games, and Huang Qixiang, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, also spoke, expressing their hope that athletes from Hong Kong and Macao would perform well in the trials and strive to represent China at the 16th Olympic Games in Melbourne.

    During the meeting, the entire Hong Kong and Macao sports delegations also met with Lu Han, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, and Rong Gaotang and Dong Shouyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Sports Federation.

    After the meeting, the entire Hong Kong and Macao sports delegations visited the competition hall, practice hall, and swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium.

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The China Olympic Games Preparatory Committee for the 16th Olympic Games hosted a banquet at noon today to welcome the entire Hong Kong and Macao sports delegations.

    He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Sports Commission, attended the banquet and delivered a speech. He Long said: On behalf of the government, I warmly welcome the athletes from Hong Kong and Macao to Beijing to participate in the selection trials for the Chinese Olympic team. I hope that after participating in the trials, you will visit and tour various places. He Long's speech was repeatedly interrupted by warm applause.

    After his speech, He Long toasted each member of the Hong Kong and Macao sports delegations attending the banquet, wishing them good results in the trials.

    Cai Shufan, Director of the China Olympic Games Preparatory Committee for the 16th Olympic Games, and Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, also spoke at the banquet. In their speeches, they both welcomed the athletes from Hong Kong and Macao to Beijing and expressed their hope that Chinese athletes from Hong Kong and Macao would participate in more national sports competitions in the future.

    Also attending today's banquet were Cai Tingkai and Huang Qixiang, Deputy Directors of the Sports Commission, Dong Shouyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and members of the Chinese Olympic Games selection committee.

### 体育运动委员会负责人接见港澳体育代表队  中国参加奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会并举行宴会欢迎

1956-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    体育运动委员会负责人接见港澳体育代表队
    中国参加奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会并举行宴会欢迎
    新华社7日讯　体育运动委员会的负责人今天上午接见了香港和澳门体育代表队的全体人员。
    体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴在接见的时候讲了话，他对香港和澳门体育代表队来北京表示热烈欢迎。蔡廷锴向他们介绍了几年来中国体育运动蓬勃开展的情况。他说，我们要发动体育界的全部力量，使中国的各项体育运动都达到国际水平。
    中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会主任蔡树藩和体育运动委员会副主任黄琪翔也讲了话，他们希望香港和澳门的运动员在选拔赛中表现好的成绩，争取代表中国去墨尔本参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会。
    接见的时候，香港和澳门体育代表队全体人员还同体育运动委员会副主任卢汉，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、董守义等见了面。
    接见以后，香港和澳门体育代表队全体人员参观了北京体育馆的比赛馆、练习馆和游泳馆。
    新华社7日讯　中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会今天中午举行宴会，欢迎香港和澳门体育代表队的全体人员。
    国务院副总理兼体育运动委员会主任贺龙出席了宴会，并且在宴会上讲了话。贺龙说：我代表政府热烈欢迎香港和澳门的运动员来北京参加中国奥林匹克运动队的选拔赛。希望你们参加了选拔赛以后，到各地参观游览。贺龙的讲话一再被热烈的掌声所打断。
    贺龙在讲话以后，同参加宴会的香港和澳门体育代表队的全体人员一一碰杯，预祝他们在选拔赛中获得良好的成绩。
    中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会主任蔡树藩、中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠先后在宴会上讲了话。他们在讲话中都对香港和澳门的运动员来北京表示欢迎，并且希望香港和澳门的中国运动员以后能多来参加全国性的体育比赛。
    出席今天宴会的还有体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、黄琪翔，中华全国体育总会副主席董守义，中国奥林匹克运动队选拔委员会的委员们。
