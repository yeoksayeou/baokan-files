Title: Exchanging Party Work Experience: Delegation of CPSU Party Workers Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 21st. A delegation of CPSU party workers, headed by Aristov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Prague on the 20th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia.

The delegation includes many secretaries of local CPSU committees and other party workers.

The delegation is here to learn about Czechoslovak life and the work of the CPC, and to exchange experiences with each other.

Welcoming them at the airport were Novotný, Barák, Hendrych, Köhler and other responsible persons from the CPC Central Committee.

### 交换党的工作经验  苏共党的工作者代表团赴捷

1956-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    交换党的工作经验
    苏共党的工作者代表团赴捷
    新华社布拉格21日电　以苏联共产党中央委员会书记处书记阿里斯托夫为首的苏联共产党党的工作者代表团，应捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会的邀请于20日到达布拉格。
    代表团包括许多苏共地方党委书记和其他党的工作人员。
    代表团来这里是为了熟悉捷克斯洛伐克生活和捷共的工作，并且互相交换经验。
    在机场欢迎的有捷共中央负责人诺沃提尼、巴拉克、亨德里赫、科列尔等。
