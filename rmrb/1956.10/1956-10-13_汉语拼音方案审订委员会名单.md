Title: List of Members of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The following is the list of members of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee, approved by the 38th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on October 10th:

　　Director: Guo Moruo
　　Deputy Directors: Zhang Xiruo, Hu Qiaomu
　　Members: Ye Gongchuo, Ye Shengtao, Zhu Xuefan, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji
　　　　Zhou Jianren, Hu Sheng, Lu Dingyi, Xu Deheng, Xu Guangping, Huang Shaohong
　　　　Shu Sheyu, Tao Menghe, Ma Xulun, Qian Junrui
