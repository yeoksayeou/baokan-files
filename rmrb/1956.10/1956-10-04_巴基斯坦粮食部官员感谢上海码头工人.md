Title: Pakistani Ministry of Food Officials Thank Shanghai Dockworkers
Author:
Date: 1956-10-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 3rd - According to the "Pakistan Times", Pakistani Ministry of Food officials thanked the Chinese dockworkers in Shanghai for loading rice destined for East Pakistan with exceptional enthusiasm.

    The officials in Karachi said that the Shanghai dockworkers loaded 10,000 tons of rice in approximately twenty-four hours. It is said that loading the same amount of rice in Karachi port would take at least four days. The Chinese workers decided to work enthusiastically in order to contribute to alleviating the disaster of the people in East Pakistan.

### 巴基斯坦粮食部官员感谢上海码头工人

1956-10-04
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦粮食部官员感谢上海码头工人
    新华社喀喇蚩3日电　据“巴基斯坦时报”报道：巴基斯坦粮食部官员们感谢上海的中国码头工人以特别的热情装运运往东巴基斯坦去的大米。
    这些官员们在喀喇蚩说，上海码头工人在大约二十四小时内装运了一万吨大米。据说，在喀喇蚩港装运同样数量的大米至少需要四天。中国工人决定热情地工作以便为减轻东巴基斯坦人民的灾难而出一把力。
