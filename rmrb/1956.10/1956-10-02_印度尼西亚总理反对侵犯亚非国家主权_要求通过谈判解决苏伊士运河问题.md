Title: Indonesian Prime Minister Opposes Infringement Upon Sovereignty of Asian-African Nations; Demands Resolution of Suez Canal Issue Through Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 1st. Sastroamidjojo said on the evening of September 30th: "We strongly oppose the use of force as a means of resolving the Suez Canal conflict."

He said: "We believe that the only solution that satisfies all parties must be achieved through negotiations. I think it is not beyond human capability to find such a solution. As long as the parties concerned do not overemphasize the loss of prestige, a peaceful solution can certainly be found."

The Indonesian Prime Minister made these remarks at a banquet in honor of Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan. The Indian Vice President left here for Japan on September 30th after a four-day visit to Indonesia.

The Prime Minister further said: "We in Asia give the West the same prestige and respect that we give ourselves. We do not consider others to be superior people whose behavior will not be wrong."

He said: Indonesia has done its part in resolving the Suez issue. It has presented its views in London, Washington, and Cairo. "We do this based on a strong desire: to give Egypt what it must retain, to ensure the freedom of navigation of the canal, and last but not least, to ensure world peace."

He also said: The Suez Canal issue constitutes a challenge to the liberation of the Asian and African people. Any action that infringes upon the sovereignty of Asian and African countries will be strongly opposed, because it will be seen as an attempt to stop this process of liberation.

### 印度尼西亚总理反对侵犯亚非国家主权  要求通过谈判解决苏伊士运河问题　　

1956-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总理反对侵犯亚非国家主权
    要求通过谈判解决苏伊士运河问题　　
    新华社雅加达1日电　沙斯特罗阿米佐约9月30日晚上说：“我们强烈反对用武力作为解决苏伊士运河冲突的手段。”　　
    他说：“我们相信，唯一的使各方面都满意的解决办法必须通过谈判来取得。我认为，找到这样一个解决办法并不是人力所不能及的。只要有关方面不过于强调有失威望，那末和平解决的办法就一定能够找到。”　　
    这位印度尼西亚总理是在招待印度副总统拉德哈克里希南的宴会上说这些话的。印度副总统在印度尼西亚访问四天后已经在9月30日离开这里前往日本。　　
    这位总理进一步说：“我们在亚洲的人给予西方的威望和尊重是同我们给予我们自己的一样的。我们并不认为别人是行为不会错误的优越的人。”　　
    他说：印度尼西亚在解决苏伊士问题上已经尽了他的本分。它不论在伦敦、华盛顿和开罗都提出了它的意见。“我们这样做是根据一种强烈的愿望，这就是：给埃及以它所必须保有的东西，保障运河的通行自由，最后但是并不是最不重要的是，保障世界和平。”　　
    他还说：苏伊士运河问题构成了对亚非人民解放的挑战。任何侵犯亚非国家主权的行动都将受到强烈的反对，因为这将被看作是企图阻挡这种解放的进程。
