Title: Further Anglo-French-Egyptian Negotiations to Be Held in Ten Days; Sabri Reiterates Egypt's Opposition to Any International Rule
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th: Ali Sabri, director of the Office of the President of the Egyptian Republic and a member of the Egyptian delegation attending the United Nations Security Council meeting, told reporters at the airport upon his return to Cairo on the 17th that further negotiations between Egypt, Britain, and France on the Suez issue would be held within ten days. The meeting place would most likely be in Geneva.
    He said that Egypt is ready to implement international cooperation, "but we firmly oppose any international rule."

### 英法埃进一步谈判将在十天内举行  萨布里重申埃及反对任何国际统治

1956-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    英法埃进一步谈判将在十天内举行
    萨布里重申埃及反对任何国际统治
    新华社18日讯　开罗消息：参加联合国安全理事会会议的埃及代表团团员、埃及共和国总统办公厅主任阿里·萨布里17日返回开罗时在机场上对新闻记者说，埃及同英国、法国关于苏伊士问题的进一步的谈判将在十天内举行。会谈地点很可能将在日内瓦。
    他说，埃及准备实行国际合作，“但是我们坚决反对任何国际统治。”
