Title: Ambassador Gipner Holds Grand Reception to Celebrate National Day of the German Democratic Republic
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, held a grand reception tonight to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai were invited to attend the reception.

Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, who is currently visiting China, and the personnel accompanying him on the visit to China, were also invited to attend the reception.

First Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, First Deputy Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Walter Ulbricht, and people from all walks of life of the German Democratic Republic in Beijing attended tonight's reception.

At the beginning of the reception, the band played the national anthems of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China. Ambassador Gipner spoke first, expressing his gratitude to all the guests for attending the reception to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. Ambassador Gipner said:

	"We are particularly honored by the presence of His Excellency Prime Minister Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal and his wife, as well as members of the Nepalese government delegation. I extend a particularly warm welcome to them and congratulate them on the successful signing of the Treaty of Friendship between the Kingdom of Nepal and the People's Republic of China."

Ambassador Gipner said: The international relations of the German Democratic Republic have greatly expanded over the past year, which is also reflected in the fact that, in addition to the ambassadors and diplomats of socialist countries, the ambassadors and charge d'affaires of countries in the Asian-African peace region, namely India, Burma, Indonesia, Egypt, and Nepal, are also attending today's reception. I would also like to express my special respect to them.

He said: The friendly relations and cooperation between the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China have been greatly deepened since the conclusion of the treaty last December.

He proposed a toast to the friendship between China and Germany,

	to the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people,

	to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China.

First Deputy Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Walter Ulbricht, delivered a speech at the reception (full text on page four).

Premier Zhou Enlai spoke at the meeting, expressing warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people to the government and people of the German Democratic Republic. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to the health of President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic. (Full text on page four)

Prime Minister Acharya of Nepal spoke at the reception, expressing his gratitude for being invited to attend today's reception. He said that the German nation is a great nation, and if the German nation is still divided, it cannot contribute to the cause of maintaining world peace. He expressed the hope that Germany can be unified and play a role in international affairs. Those who oppose the unification of Germany do not want peace in the world. He also thanked Ambassador Gipner for his kind words about the friendly relations between China and Nepal.

Prime Minister Acharya finally proposed a toast to the peaceful unification of Germany and to world peace.

At the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhu De stood up to propose a toast. He toasted to the health of Prime Minister Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz.

Vice Chairman Li Jishen also stood up to propose a toast to the health of Chairman Dieckmann.

At the reception, Ambassador Gipner toasted to the health of Prime Minister Acharya and his wife, who are about to leave Beijing, and wished them a safe journey home and the Kingdom of Nepal prosperity and strength.

During the reception, the German People's Police Band performed a wonderful musical program.

Invited to attend the reception were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Ulanhu, Li Xiannian, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Zeng Yongquan, and heads of various departments of Chinese state organs, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

### 纪普纳大使举行盛大招待会  庆祝德意志民主共和国国庆

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    纪普纳大使举行盛大招待会
    庆祝德意志民主共和国国庆
    据新华社7日讯　德意志民主共和国驻中国大使纪普纳今晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝德意志民主共和国成立七周年。
    朱德副主席、周恩来总理应邀出席了招待会。
    正在中国访问的尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和同他一起来中国访问的人员也应邀出席了这个招待会。
    德国统一社会党第一书记、德意志民主共和国第一副总理乌布利希和在京的德意志民主共和国各界人士都出席了今晚的招待会。
    招待会开始时，乐队奏德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。纪普纳大使首先讲话，他对全体来宾出席招待会庆祝德意志民主共和国成立七周年表示感谢。纪普纳大使说：
      “使我们感到特别荣幸的是尼泊尔王国首相阿查里雅阁下和夫人以及尼泊尔政府代表团团员的出席。我对他们表示特别热烈的欢迎，并向他们祝贺尼泊尔王国和中华人民共和国友好条约的胜利签订。”
    纪普纳大使说：德意志民主共和国的国际关系一年来有了很大的开展，这也表现在出席今天招待会的除了社会主义国家的大使和外交官以外，还有亚非和平地区的国家即印度、缅甸、印度尼西亚、埃及、尼泊尔等国的大使和临时代办。我也特别向他们表示敬意。
    他说：德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国之间的友好关系和合作在去年12月缔结条约以后获得了大大的加深。
    他建议为德中友谊，
    为中国人民的幸福和繁荣，
    为中华人民共和国毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理的健康干杯。
    德意志民主共和国第一副总理瓦·乌布利希在酒会上致词（全文见第四版）。
    周恩来总理在会上讲话，他代表中国政府和中国人民向德意志民主共和国政府和人民表示热烈的祝贺。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克的身体健康。（全文见第四版）
    尼泊尔首相阿查里雅在招待会上讲话，对受邀参加今天的招待会表示感谢。他说，德意志民族是一个伟大的民族，如果德意志民族还分裂的话，它就不能对维护世界和平的事业作出贡献。他表示希望德意志能得到统一，并在国际事务中发挥作用。反对德意志统一的人，就是不希望世界上能够得到和平的人。他并且感谢纪普纳大使刚才所说对中国和尼泊尔两国友好关系的好话。
    阿查里雅首相最后提议为德意志的和平统一，为世界的和平而干杯。
    在会上，朱德副主席起立祝酒。他为格罗提渥总理、乌布利希副总理和博尔茨副总理兼外交部长的健康干杯。
    李济深副委员长也起立祝酒，为狄克曼议长的健康干杯。
    纪普纳大使在会上为行将离开北京的阿查里雅首相和他的夫人健康干杯，并且祝他们归途一路平安，祝尼泊尔王国繁荣和富强。
    在招待会进行中，德意志人民警察乐团演奏了精采的音乐节目。
    应邀出席招待会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通、国务院副总理贺龙、乌兰夫、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞，中国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉和中国国家机关各部门负责人，北京各界人士。
    各国驻中国外交使节和外交官员也应邀出席。
