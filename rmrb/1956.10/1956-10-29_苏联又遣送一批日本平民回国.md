Title: The Soviet Union Sends Another Group of Japanese Civilians Home

Author:

Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. TASS reported on the 27th that recently, another group of Japanese civilians was repatriated with the assistance of the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the USSR. This repatriation is based on the communique signed in November 1953 by the representatives of the Executive Committee of the Union and the representatives of the Japanese Red Cross regarding the repatriation of Japanese prisoners of war and Japanese civilians who have completed their sentences or were released early.
　　
This group of forty-seven repatriated Japanese civilians has been handed over to the representatives of the Japanese Red Cross who arrived at the Soviet port of Nakhodka on the Japanese ship "Shinyo Maru".

All those repatriated have safely returned to their homeland.

### 苏联又遣送一批日本平民回国

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联又遣送一批日本平民回国
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社27日报道：不久前，又有一批日本平民在苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会的协助下，被遣送回国。这是根据1953年11月这个联合会执委会代表和日本红十字会代表签订的关于把服刑期满的和提前释放的日本战俘以及日本平民遣送回国的公报遣返的。
    这一批遣返的日本平民四十七人已经被交给乘日本船“信阳丸”来到苏联纳霍德卡港口的日本红十字会代表。
    所有被遣返的人已经平安地回到他们的祖国。
