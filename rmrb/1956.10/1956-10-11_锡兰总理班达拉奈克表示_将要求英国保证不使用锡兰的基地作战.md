Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike Says He Will Ask Britain to Guarantee Not to Use Ceylon's Bases for Combat
Author:
Date: 1956-10-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 10th: Colombo dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said on October 9th that he will ask British Prime Minister Eden to guarantee that Britain will not use its military bases in Ceylon for war.

Bandaranaike will leave for New York in early November to attend the United Nations General Assembly. He said he intends to discuss the Suez Canal issue with Eden when he passes through London.

Bandaranaike also said that he will first visit Cairo for two days en route, and discuss the Suez Canal issue with Egyptian President Nasser.

### 锡兰总理班达拉奈克表示  将要求英国保证不使用锡兰的基地作战

1956-10-11
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理班达拉奈克表示
    将要求英国保证不使用锡兰的基地作战
    新华社新德里10日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克10月9日说，他将要求英国首相艾登保证英国不使用它在锡兰的军事基地来进行战争。
    班达拉奈克将在11月初动身去纽约参加联合国大会。他说，他打算在路过伦敦的时候同艾登讨论苏伊士运河问题。
    班达拉奈克还说，他将在途中首先到开罗访问两天，并且同埃及总统纳赛尔商讨苏伊士运河问题。
