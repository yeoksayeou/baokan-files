Title: Longo Discusses Yugoslav Socialist Construction, Believes Yugoslav Experience Will Aid Italian Communist Party's Struggle for Socialism
Author:
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - According to a Yugoslav news agency report, Italian Communist Party Deputy General Secretary Longo said that the Italian Communist Party delegation currently visiting Yugoslavia has achieved the basic goal of this visit - to understand as fully as possible the experience of the Yugoslav socialist movement.

In a conversation published in the Yugoslav newspaper "Politika" on the 11th, Longo emphasized that, in general, the experience of socialist construction in socialist Yugoslavia is of great significance, and these experiences are helpful to the Italian Communists' struggle for socialism.

The head of the Italian Communist Party delegation said that the delegation was most interested in the experience and achievements gained in direct democracy regarding the realization of the working people themselves solving the problems of management and production in enterprises, and in the self-management of workers regarding issues that directly affect the lives of workers.

Longo said that the fact that workers in power are able to involve such a wide range of people in the management of the country and the economy can only be helpful to our struggle in Italy—the struggle to enable the Italian working class to gain the position of managing the country.

### 隆哥谈南斯拉夫社会主义建设  认为南斯拉夫的经验有助于意共争取社会主义的斗争

1956-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    隆哥谈南斯拉夫社会主义建设
    认为南斯拉夫的经验有助于意共争取社会主义的斗争
    新华社12日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，意大利共产党副总书记隆哥说，目前在南斯拉夫访问的意大利共产党代表团已实现了这次访问的基本目的——尽可能地充分了解南斯拉夫社会主义运动的经验。
    隆哥在发表于11日的南斯拉夫“政治报”上的一篇谈话中强调指出，总的来说，社会主义的南斯拉夫建设社会主义的经验是具有重大意义的，而这些经验，对意大利共产党人争取社会主义的斗争是有帮助的。
    这位意大利共产党代表团团长说，代表团人员最感兴趣的是关于实现劳动人民自己解决企业中的管理和生产问题的直接民主以及关于直接影响工人生活的问题的工人自己管理方面所获得的经验和成就。
    隆哥说，当权的工人能够使得这样广泛的人参加管理国家和经济的事实，对我们在意大利的斗争——使意大利工人阶级获得管理国家的地位的斗争，只能是有帮助的。
