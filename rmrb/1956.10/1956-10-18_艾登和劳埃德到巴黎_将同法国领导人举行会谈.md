Title: Eden and Lloyd Arrive in Paris to Hold Talks with French Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-10-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 17th. British Prime Minister Eden and Foreign Secretary Lloyd arrived in Paris by plane on the evening of the 16th to hold talks with French Prime Minister Mollet and Foreign Minister Pineau.

Upon arrival, Eden said that they had come to Paris at the invitation of French Prime Minister Mollet to discuss the joint policy of the British and French "governments on the Suez issue after the Security Council meeting" in order to "maintain full Anglo-French unity."

According to British official announcements, Eden and Lloyd will return to London on the morning of the 17th.

It is reported that in addition to the Suez issue, the leaders of the British and French governments will also discuss the dispute between Israel and Arab countries.

### 艾登和劳埃德到巴黎  将同法国领导人举行会谈

1956-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

    艾登和劳埃德到巴黎
    将同法国领导人举行会谈
    新华社17日讯　巴黎消息：英国首相艾登和外交大臣劳埃德在16日晚上乘飞机到达巴黎，以便同法国总理摩勒和外交部长比诺举行会谈。
    艾登在到达后说，他们是应法国总理摩勒的邀请到巴黎来讨论英法“两国政府在安全理事会会议之后对苏伊士问题的共同政策”，以便“保持英—法的充分团结”的。
    据英国官方公报，艾登和劳埃德将在17日上午返回伦敦。
    有消息说，英法两国政府领导人除了要谈苏伊士问题以外，还要讨论以色列和阿拉伯国家之间的纠纷的问题。
