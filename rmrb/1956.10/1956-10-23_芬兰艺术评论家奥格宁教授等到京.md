Title: Finnish Art Critic Professor Aulanko Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-23
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. Professor Aulanko, a well-known Finnish art critic and academician of the Academy of Sciences, and his wife, along with Professor Tuhkanen, a printmaker, and two others, arrived in Beijing on the 19th at the invitation of the Chinese Artists Association for a visit to our country. Representatives from the Chinese Artists Association were at the airport to welcome them.

### 芬兰艺术评论家奥格宁教授等到京

1956-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    芬兰艺术评论家奥格宁教授等到京
    新华社20日讯　应中国美术家协会邀请来我国访问的芬兰著名艺术评论家、科学院院士奥格宁教授夫妇和版画家杜赫格教授等四人，已经在19日到达北京。中国美术家协会代表曾到机场迎接。
