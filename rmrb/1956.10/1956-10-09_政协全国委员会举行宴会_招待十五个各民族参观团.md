Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Banquet to Welcome Visiting Delegations from Fifteen Ethnic Groups
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a banquet this evening to welcome fifteen ethnic group visiting delegations to Beijing and other parts of the country.
    Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, delivered a speech at the banquet, extending a warm welcome on behalf of the CPPCC National Committee to the visiting delegations from various ethnic groups. He said that in order to build our country into an advanced industrial nation, minority ethnic regions must also achieve a high level of economic and cultural development, especially industrial construction in minority ethnic regions. He said that all of these points have been clearly stated in the report and resolutions of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. Most of you present here today are representatives and leading figures from various ethnic groups, and it is hoped that after your visits to the capital and other parts of the country, you will further unite the people of all ethnic groups in your respective areas and work together to build our motherland into a great socialist country.
    Present at the banquet this evening were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, and Burhan, Vice Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee; Xing Xiping, Secretary-General; Liu Geping, Chairman of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, and Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren, Vice Chairmen of the Ethnic Affairs Commission, and many others.

### 政协全国委员会举行宴会  招待十五个各民族参观团

1956-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行宴会
    招待十五个各民族参观团
    据新华社6日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今天晚间举行宴会，招待到首都北京和祖国各地参观的十五个各民族参观团。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李维汉在宴会上致词的时候，代表中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会对各民族参观团表示热烈欢迎。他说，为了把我们的国家建设成为一个先进的工业国，各少数民族地区也要在经济文化上得到高度的发展，特别是在少数民族地区进行工业的建设。他说，所有这些都在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的报告和决议中有了明确的说明。今天在座的各位，大都是各民族中的代表人士和领袖人物，希望大家在首都和祖国各地参观回去以后，进一步团结好当地各族人民，为把我们的祖国建设成为一个伟大的社会主义国家而共同奋斗。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、沈钧儒、章伯钧、鲍尔汉，秘书长邢西萍；全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平和民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁等多人。
