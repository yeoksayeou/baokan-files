Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe Receives Ma Hanbing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 30th - Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe received Ma Hanbing, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and members of the delegation at his residence on the afternoon of September 29th.

Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, was also present at the reception.

Ma Hanbing thanked the Burmese government for the warm hospitality extended to the delegation.

U Ba Swe said that the delegation's visit to Burma would help promote friendship between the governments and peoples of China and Burma. He hoped that cultural exchanges between the two countries would be further developed to promote friendly cooperation between the two countries.

Prime Minister U Ba Swe and his wife invited the guests to have tea and refreshments.

Ma Hanbing presented gifts to Prime Minister U Ba Swe and his wife on behalf of the delegation.

That morning, Ma Hanbing, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador Yao Zhongming, paid a visit to First Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio. During the meeting, Sao Khun Hkio praised Chinese art and expressed his hope to strengthen cultural exchanges between the two countries.

On the same morning, Ma Hanbing and some members of the delegation also laid a wreath at the tomb of Burmese national hero Aung San.

### 缅甸总理吴巴瑞接见马寒冰

1956-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴巴瑞接见马寒冰
    新华社仰光30日电　缅甸总理吴巴瑞9月29日下午在他的住宅接见了中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰和团员们。
    接见的时候，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也在座。
    马寒冰感谢缅甸政府给予代表团的热诚招待。
    吴巴瑞说，代表团这次访问缅甸，将有助于促进中缅两国政府和人民之间的友谊。他希望两国之间的文化交流获得进一步的发展，以便促进两国之间的友好合作。
    吴巴瑞总理和夫人请客人们共进茶点。
    马寒冰代表代表团向吴巴瑞总理和夫人赠送了礼物。
    当天上午，马寒冰在中国大使姚仲明的陪同下拜会了第一副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓。藻昆卓在会见时称赞中国的艺术，并且表示希望加强两国之间的文化交流。
    马寒冰和一部分团员在当天早上还到缅甸民族英雄昂山的墓前献了花圈。
