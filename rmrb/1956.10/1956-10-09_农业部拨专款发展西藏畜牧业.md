Title: Ministry of Agriculture Allocates Special Funds to Develop Animal Husbandry in Tibet
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa. The Central Ministry of Agriculture recently allocated 180,000 yuan in special funds to support the development of animal husbandry and veterinary work in the Tibetan region. The Animal Husbandry Department of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region has used part of the allocation for the purchase of medicines and equipment, and the majority of the funds are being used to develop animal husbandry and veterinary services in the Qamdo region of eastern Tibet.

### 农业部拨专款发展西藏畜牧业

1956-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    农业部拨专款发展西藏畜牧业
    新华社拉萨电　中央农业部最近拨了十八万元专款供给西藏地区发展畜牧兽医工作。西藏自治区筹备委员会畜牧处已将拨款的一部分作为药械的购置费，并把大部分拨款用来发展西藏东部昌都地区的畜牧兽医事业。
