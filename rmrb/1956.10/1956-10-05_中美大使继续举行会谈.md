Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-10-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 4th - The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their 59th meeting from 10:00 AM to 12:07 PM on October 4th. The next meeting will be held on October 18th.

### 中美大使继续举行会谈

1956-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    中美大使继续举行会谈
    新华社日内瓦4日电　中美两国大使级会谈在10月4日上午10时到12时零7分举行了第五十九次会议。下次会议将在10月18日举行。
