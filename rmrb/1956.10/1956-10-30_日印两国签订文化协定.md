Title: Japan and India Sign Cultural Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 6
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 29th—Japan and India today signed a ten-year cultural agreement in Tokyo.
　　According to this agreement, the two countries will exchange books, magazines and other publications, hold lectures, concerts, art performances and art exhibitions with each other, and show scientific educational films or cultural films to each other. The agreement also stipulates that the two countries will send professors, scholars, students and personnel from academic institutions to each other.

### 日印两国签订文化协定

1956-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    日印两国签订文化协定
    新华社29日讯　东京消息：日本和印度今天在东京签订了一项为期十年的文化协定。
    根据这个协定，两国将交换书籍、杂志和其他出版物，互相举行演讲会、音乐会、艺术演出、艺术展览，并且互相放映科学的教育影片或者文化影片。协定还规定，两国将互派教授、学者、学生和学术机构的人员。
