Title: Mao Dun Hosts Indian, Guatemalan, and German Writers
Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, hosted a banquet tonight for Indian writer Kumar, Guatemalan writer Asturias and his wife, and German writers Maul and Munzdoch. They were invited to Beijing to participate in the commemoration of the 20th anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's death, but arrived after the memorial meeting due to transportation difficulties.
    Mao Dun said in his speech that he hoped to enhance the friendship between the people of all countries through contact between writers.
    Kumar said at the banquet, "I am very grateful to the hosts for giving me such a good opportunity to come to China and commemorate Lu Xun with everyone."
    Guests tonight included writers Yang Hansheng, Wu Zuxiang, Xiao San, Ai Qing, Feng Zhi, Chen Baichen, Yan Wenjing, Xiao Qian, Han Zi and others.

### 茅盾欢宴印度、危地马拉和德国作家

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    茅盾欢宴印度、危地马拉和德国作家
    据新华社25日讯　中国文学艺术界联合会副主席、中国作家协会主席茅盾今晚欢宴印度作家库玛尔、危地马拉作家阿斯德里亚斯和他的夫人、德国作家玛乌尔和蒙兹多克。他们是应邀来北京参加鲁迅先生逝世二十周年纪念活动的，由于交通不便而在纪念大会举行以后才到达。
    茅盾在席间致词，他希望通过作家之间的接触，增进各国人民的友谊。
    库玛尔在宴会上讲话，他说，我非常感谢主人们给我这样好的机会，让我能够来中国和大家一起纪念鲁迅。
    今晚的陪客有作家阳翰笙、吴组缃、萧三、艾青、冯至、陈白尘、严文井、萧乾、菡子等人。
