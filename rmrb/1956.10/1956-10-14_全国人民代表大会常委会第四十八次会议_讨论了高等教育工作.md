Title: The 48th Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Discussed Higher Education Work
Author:
Date: 1956-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 48th session on the afternoon of the 13th.

The meeting listened to the report by Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, on the 1956 enrollment situation and issues such as overcoming the excessive learning burden of students, strengthening the cultivation of students' independent thinking and independent working abilities, and improving the quality of cadre training. The meeting discussed higher education work and put forward some criticisms and suggestions.

The meeting approved the appointment of Liu Guanyi as Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会第四十八次会议  讨论了高等教育工作

1956-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会第四十八次会议
    讨论了高等教育工作
    新华社13日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在13日下午举行第四十八次会议。
    会议听取了高等教育部部长杨秀峰关于1956年招生情况和克服学生学习负担过重现象、加强培养学生的独立思考和独立工作能力、提高培养干部质量等问题的报告。会议对高等教育工作进行了讨论，提出了一些批评和建议。
    会议通过任命刘贯一为全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长。
