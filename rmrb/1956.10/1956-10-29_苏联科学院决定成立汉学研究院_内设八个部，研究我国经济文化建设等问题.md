Title: The Soviet Academy of Sciences Decides to Establish the Institute of Chinese Studies: Eight Departments Established to Study China's Economic and Cultural Development

Author:
Date: 1956-10-29
Page: 5

Moscow Dispatch from Our Newspaper, 28th: The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences passed a resolution on the 26th to establish the Institute of Chinese Studies. The task of the Institute of Chinese Studies is to study issues related to the People's Republic of China's government, economy, culture, language, history, literature, and international relations, and to write scientific works on these aspects. The institute will translate and publish Chinese historical and literary documents, as well as modern historical, economic, and socio-political works. It will establish broad cooperation with Chinese scientific institutions and Chinese scholars. The Institute of Chinese Studies will be based on the Chinese Studies Department of the former Oriental Institute, and it will be divided into eight departments: history, economics, national construction of the People's Republic of China, languages of Chinese ethnic groups, literature and art, cultural and socio-political works, scientific information, and publishing.

### 苏联科学院决定成立汉学研究院  内设八个部，研究我国经济文化建设等问题

1956-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院决定成立汉学研究院
    内设八个部，研究我国经济文化建设等问题
    本报莫斯科28日电　苏联科学院主席团在26日通过了成立汉学研究院的决议。汉学研究院的任务是研究中华人民共和国国家、经济、文化、语言、历史、文学和国际关系诸方面的问题，并撰写这些方面的科学论著。研究院将翻译和出版中国历史和文学的文献以及现代历史、经济和社会政治各方面的著作。它将和中国科学机关、中国学者取得广泛合作。汉学研究院将以原东方学院的汉学部为基础，它分为历史、经济、中华人民共和国国家建设、中国各民族的语言、文学艺术、文化和社会政治著作、科学情报和出版事业等八个部分。
