Title: Prime Minister Acharya Returns to Beijing; Visited Beijing Municipal Prison Yesterday Afternoon
Author:
Date: 1956-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, and his wife, along with accompanying senior officials, returned to Beijing by special plane on the morning of the 6th after visiting various places in Northeast China.

Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests from Nepal.

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, October 6th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Acharya, and his wife, along with accompanying senior Nepalese officials, left Dalian for Beijing by special plane at a little after 8:00 this morning.

Song Li, Mayor of Dalian, and his wife, Vice Mayors Qiao Chuanjue and Yu Guying, and the heads of people's organizations and local organizations of various democratic parties in Dalian were at the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Also at the airport to see them off was Soviet Consul General to Dalian, Belyanov.

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, visited Beijing Municipal Prison this afternoon. After the visit, the Prime Minister said that the prison was like a factory.

Accompanied by the prison warden, Li Yiping, Prime Minister Acharya and his wife visited various parts of the prison. In the prison's attached weaving factory and hosiery factory, they saw the inmates working diligently in clean workshops, with the names and deeds of some inmates with excellent work performance posted on the walls.

The prison warden told the Prime Minister that the purpose of labor reform for prisoners is to enable them to become self-reliant citizens.

Prime Minister Acharya visited the inmates' cells, the prison's medical clinic with X-ray equipment, bathrooms, kitchens, and sports grounds.

On the same afternoon, the Chairman of the Nepalese People's Party, Ba Misra, and senior officials accompanying the Nepalese Prime Minister on his visit to China, toured Beihai Park and visited the Beihai Kindergarten.

### 阿查里雅首相回到北京  昨天下午参观了北京市监狱

1956-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相回到北京
    昨天下午参观了北京市监狱
    新华社6日讯　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人，以及随行的高级官员等一行在到东北各地进行参观后，在6日上午乘专机回到北京。
    到机场迎接尼泊尔贵宾的，有外交部副部长章汉夫和礼宾司副司长王雨田。
    新华社旅大6日电　尼泊尔王国首相阿查里雅和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔高级官员今天上午八点多钟乘专机离开旅大去北京。
    到机场欢送贵宾的，有旅大市市长宋黎和夫人，副市长乔传珏、于谷莺，旅大市各人民团体和各民主党派旅大地方组织的负责人等。
    到机场欢送的，还有苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫。
    新华社6日讯　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅今天下午参观了北京市监狱。首相参观后说，这个监狱就像工厂一样。
    阿查里雅首相和他的夫人在监狱长李一平陪同下，参观了这个监狱的各个部分。在监狱附设的织布厂和织袜厂中，他们看到犯人们在清洁的车间里认真地工作着，墙上张贴着工作成绩优良的一些犯人的名单和事迹。
    监狱长对首相说，对犯人进行劳动改造，是为了使他们能成为自食其力的公民。
    阿查里雅首相参观了犯人居住的狱室和监狱中有爱克斯光设备的医务所、浴室、厨房和运动场。
    同一天下午，尼泊尔人民党主席巴·米斯腊和随同尼泊尔首相来中国访问的高级官员们游览了北海，参观了北海幼儿园。
