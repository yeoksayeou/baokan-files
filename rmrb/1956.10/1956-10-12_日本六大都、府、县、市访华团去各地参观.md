Title: Japanese Delegation from Six Major Metropolitan Areas, Prefectures, Counties, and Cities Visits Various Locations in China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Japanese delegation from six major metropolitan areas, prefectures, counties, and cities, which came to China at the invitation of Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, left Beijing tonight to visit the Northeast, East China, and other regions.

On the 9th at noon, the delegation held a banquet. Invited guests included Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu, Wang Kunlun, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, Le Songsheng, President of the Red Cross Society of China Li Dequan, and others.

### 日本六大都、府、县、市访华团去各地参观

1956-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本六大都、府、县、市访华团去各地参观
    据新华社10日讯　应北京市市长彭真邀请来中国访问的日本六大都、府、县、市访华代表团，今天晚上离开北京去东北、华东等地参观。
    9日中午代表团曾举行了宴会。应邀参加的有北京市市长彭真，副市长张友渔、王昆仑、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，中国红十字会总会会长李德全等人。
