Title: Our Art Troupe Arrives in Hanover; Munich Audiences Highly Praise Chinese Art
Author:
Date: 1956-10-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanover, 28th. After eight performances in Munich, the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Hanover, the capital of Lower Saxony, Federal Republic of Germany, on the evening of the 27th. The art troupe will perform three times in Hanover.
The art troupe's performances in Munich have won great welcome and widespread praise from the audience. Many famous figures in the literary and art circles have published articles in newspapers praising the performances.
Munich's largest daily newspaper, the "Süddeutsche Zeitung," praised the art troupe's performance, saying, "This kind of art is unparalleled in Europe." The "Merkur" also wrote: "Whoever sees Peking Opera experiences the luckiest moment of his life."

### 我艺术团到汉诺威  慕尼黑观众盛赞我国艺术

1956-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我艺术团到汉诺威
    慕尼黑观众盛赞我国艺术
    据新华社汉诺威28日电　中国艺术团在慕尼黑演出八场以后在27日晚上到达德意志联邦共和国下萨克森州首府汉诺威。艺术团在汉诺威将演出三场。
    艺术团在慕尼黑的演出博得了观众们的巨大欢迎及普遍好评。许多著名的文艺界人士都在报上发表文章盛赞这次演出。
    慕尼黑最大的日报“南德日报”赞扬艺术团的表演说：“这种艺术在欧洲是无与伦比的”。“水星报”也写道：“谁要是看了京剧，谁就经历了他一生中最幸运的时刻。”
