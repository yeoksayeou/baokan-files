Title: New Corn Yield Record Set in Manas Reclamation Area, Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi — Two groups in the Manas Reclamation Area of the Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps have set new national records for corn yield. According to the threshed output: the 5.1 mu of corn in贺天宣 (He Tianxuan)丰产 (high yield) group averaged 1,925 jin per mu, and the 2.52 mu of corn in 党存玉 (Dang Cunyu)丰产 (high yield) group averaged 1,830 jin per mu. Both exceeded the national highest yield record of 1,544 jin of corn per mu set by the 潘大勇 (Pan Dayong) group of the Production and Construction Corps in 1954 on 2.7 mu of land.

Both the 贺天宣 (He Tianxuan) and 党存玉 (Dang Cunyu) high-yield groups are production teams of the Ula Usu Farm in the Manas Reclamation Area. This year, the four members of the 贺天宣 (He Tianxuan) group managed 632 mu of corn, averaging 618 jin per mu, and the three members of the 党存玉 (Dang Cunyu) group managed 200 mu of corn, averaging 700 jin per mu. These two groups used the Golden Queen corn variety in the high-yield fields, and also carried out an additional cultivation, a top dressing, and an artificial pollination.

This year, the corn crops in the Manas Reclamation Area are generally growing well. The Ula Usu Farm's more than 5,000 mu of corn sown this year have all achieved yields of more than 500 jin.

### 新疆玛纳斯垦区  创玉米丰产新纪录

1956-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆玛纳斯垦区
    创玉米丰产新纪录
    新华社乌鲁木齐电　新疆军区生产建设部队玛纳斯垦区有两个小组创造了全国玉米丰产新纪录。根据脱粒后的产量：贺天宣丰产组的五点一亩玉米平均每亩产一千九百二十五斤，党存玉丰产组的二点五二亩玉米平均每亩产一千八百三十斤，都超过了生产建设部队潘大勇小组1954年在二点七亩土地上创造的每亩产一千五百四十四斤玉米的全国最高丰产纪录。
    贺天宣和党存玉两个丰产组都是玛纳斯垦区乌拉乌苏农场的生产小组。贺天宣小组四个人今年管理的六百三十二亩玉米平均每亩产六百一十八斤，党存玉小组三个人管理的二百亩玉米平均每亩产七百斤。这两个小组在丰产田采用金皇后玉米良种，还多进行了一次中耕、一次追肥和一次人工授粉。
    今年玛纳斯垦区玉米作物普遍生长良好。乌拉乌苏农场今年播种的五千多亩玉米都获得五百斤以上的产量。
