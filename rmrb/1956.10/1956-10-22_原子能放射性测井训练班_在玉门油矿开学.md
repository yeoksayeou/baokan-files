Title: Atomic Energy Radioactive Logging Training Class Opens at Yumen Oilfield
Author:
Date: 1956-10-22
Page: 2

    Atomic Energy Radioactive Logging Training Class
    Opens at Yumen Oilfield
    (Xinhua News Agency) The first atomic energy radioactive logging training class in China opened at the Yumen Oilfield on the 18th. More than 30 students from five units, including the Ministry of Geology, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, the Xinjiang Petroleum Administration Bureau, the Qinghai Petroleum Exploration Bureau, and the Yumen Oilfield, are participating in the training class. The training class will last for two months, and the learning method will combine theory and practice. Mukher, a well-known Soviet atomic energy radioactive logging expert, will teach technical theory courses and guide students in practice in the training class.
    Atomic energy prospecting uses gamma rays, neutron rays, and isotope elements and radioactive instruments. The use of this new technology can not only promptly and accurately ascertain underground conditions and obtain geological data, but also quickly solve major difficult problems existing in petroleum geology and oil extraction work.

### 原子能放射性测井训练班  在玉门油矿开学

1956-10-22
第2版()
专栏：

    原子能放射性测井训练班
    在玉门油矿开学
    据新华社讯　我国开办的第一个原子能放射性测井训练班，18日在玉门油矿开学。参加这个训练班学习的学员有来自地质部、石油工业部、新疆石油管理局、青海石油勘探局、玉门油矿等五个单位的三十多人。训练班共进行两个月，学习方法是理论和实际相结合。苏联著名的原子能放射性测井专家穆赫尔，将在训练班里担任讲授技术理论的课程和指导学员实习。
    原子能探矿是利用珈玛射线、中子射线和同位素三种元素和放射性仪器来进行的。运用这种新的技术，不但可以及时准确地探明地下情况，取得地质资料，而且能够迅速解决石油地质和采油工种中存在的重大疑难问题。
