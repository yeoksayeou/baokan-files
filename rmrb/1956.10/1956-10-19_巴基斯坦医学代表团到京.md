Title: Pakistani Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Pakistani medical delegation, led by Munawar Ali, Vice Chairman of the Pakistan Medical Association, consisting of fifteen members, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association.
    The Pakistani medical delegation will visit various parts of China and exchange medical experiences.

### 巴基斯坦医学代表团到京

1956-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦医学代表团到京
    新华社18日讯　应中华医学会邀请，前来我国访问的以巴基斯坦医学会副主席穆纳瓦尔·阿里为首的巴基斯坦医学代表团一行十五人，已经在今天下午到达北京。
    巴基斯坦医学代表团将在我国各地进行参观访问和交流医学经验。
