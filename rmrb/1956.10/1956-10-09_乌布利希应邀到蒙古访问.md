Title: Ulbricht Invited to Visit Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1956-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 8th. Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane from Beijing on the 8th, having been invited to visit Mongolia.

Ulbricht delivered a speech at the airport, thanking those who welcomed him. He said that our visit to Mongolia will further strengthen the friendly relations between the people of Germany and Mongolia.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural Sambuu, and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party of Mongolia Shirindib and others welcomed the distinguished guest at the airport.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia were also at the airport to welcome him.

That evening, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers held a banquet in the Government Building to entertain the distinguished guest.

### 乌布利希应邀到蒙古访问

1956-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　乌布利希应邀到蒙古访问
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　应邀到蒙古访问的德国统一社会党第一书记乌布利希，8日从北京乘飞机到达乌兰巴托。
    乌布利希在机场上发表讲话，向欢迎者致谢。他说，我们访问蒙古将进一步加强德蒙两国人民的友好关系。
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔、大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员锡林迪布等到机场欢迎贵宾。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻蒙古的使节。
    当天晚上，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会和部长会议在政府大厦设宴招待贵宾。
