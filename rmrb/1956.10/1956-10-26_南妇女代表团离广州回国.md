Title: Yugoslav Women's Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:
Date: 1956-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 25th. The Yugoslav Women's Delegation, led by its head Lydia Sentjurc, concluded its visit to China and left Guangzhou by train today to return home.

### 南妇女代表团离广州回国

1956-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    南妇女代表团离广州回国
    据新华社广州25日电　由团长里第亚·申特尤尔茨率领的南斯拉夫妇女代表团结束了在中国的访问，今天乘火车离开广州回国。
