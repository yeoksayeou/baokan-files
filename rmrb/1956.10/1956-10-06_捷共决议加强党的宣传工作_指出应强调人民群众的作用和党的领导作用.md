Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Resolution Strengthens Party Propaganda Work, Emphasizing the Role of the People and the Leading Role of the Party
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th. According to TASS, "Red Right Daily" published on the 4th the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee's resolution on the tasks of the party's propaganda work adopted at the opening of the party school.

    The resolution discussed the tasks of the party's propaganda work, the political education of party members, and pointed out the necessity to raise the ideological level of propaganda work and conscientiously study economic and international issues.

    The resolution stated that in the entire education work, the decisive role of the masses of the people, the leading role of the Communist Party, and the issue of class struggle in the current stage of socialist construction in Czechoslovakia should be emphasized.

    The resolution emphatically pointed out the international significance of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the necessity of studying the experience of the CPSU and the history of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

    The resolution concluded by saying that party classes will be conducted in the current policy training courses, in the national economic problem research groups, district Marxist-Leninist night schools, political economy and economics night schools, in Marxist-Leninist seminars, and in Marxist-Leninist night universities, using the method of independent study of Marxist-Leninist theory.

### 捷共决议加强党的宣传工作  指出应强调人民群众的作用和党的领导作用

1956-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    捷共决议加强党的宣传工作
    指出应强调人民群众的作用和党的领导作用
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社布拉格讯：“红色权利报”4日刊载了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会就党校开学所通过的关于党的宣传工作的任务的决议。
    决议谈到了党的宣传工作的任务，对党员的政治教育，并且指出必需提高宣传工作的思想水平和认真研究经济和国际问题。
    决议说，在整个教育工作中，应当强调人民大众的决定性作用、共产党的领导作用和在捷克斯洛伐克社会主义建设现阶段中的阶级斗争问题。
    决议着重指出了苏联共产党的国际意义和研究苏共的经验以及苏联共产党历史的必要性。
    决议最后说，党课将在党当前政策训练班、在国民经济问题研究小组、区马克思列宁主义夜校、政治经济学和经济学夜校、在马克思列宁主义讲习班、在马克思列宁主义夜大学中以独立研究马克思列宁主义理论的方法进行。
