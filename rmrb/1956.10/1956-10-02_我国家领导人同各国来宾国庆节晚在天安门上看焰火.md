Title: Our National Leaders Watched Fireworks with Foreign Guests on National Day Evening at Tiananmen

Author:

Date: 1956-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st -- On the evening of National Day, Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other national leaders, together with President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia, Prime Minister Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal, and their entourages, watched the fireworks and the mass celebration in Tiananmen Square from Tiananmen.

Also watching the fireworks and mass celebration on Tiananmen were representatives of communist parties, workers' parties, labor parties, labor people's parties, and people's revolutionary parties from various countries who were attending the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, heads of delegations and prominent figures from more than fifty countries, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai received the heads of delegations and prominent figures from various countries.

### 我国家领导人同各国来宾国庆节晚在天安门上看焰火

1956-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人同各国来宾国庆节晚在天安门上看焰火
    新华社1日讯　国庆节的夜晚，毛泽东主席和朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理以及国家其他领导人员，同印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统、尼泊尔王国阿查里雅首相和他们的随行人员，在天安门上观看了焰火和天安门广场上的群众大联欢。
    在天安门上观看焰火和群众大联欢的，还有参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各国共产党、工人党、劳动党、劳动人民党和人民革命党的代表，来自五十多个国家的各代表团负责人和著名人士，以及各国驻中国的使节。
    毛泽东主席、周恩来总理接见了各国代表团负责人和著名人士。
