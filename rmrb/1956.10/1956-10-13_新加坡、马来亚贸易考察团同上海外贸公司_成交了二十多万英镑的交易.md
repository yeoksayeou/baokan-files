Title: Singapore and Malayan Trade Delegation Concludes Transactions Worth Over £200,000 with Shanghai Foreign Trade Companies
Author: 
Date: 1956-10-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 12th. The Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, during their stay in Shanghai, concluded transactions worth over £200,000 with Shanghai's foreign trade companies. They purchased tung oil, mixed beans, cotton cloth, tiles, matches, records, as well as knitwear, small commodities, and other goods.

The two delegations arrived in Shanghai on October 7th and left for Hangzhou on the 10th. During this period, they visited the Shanghai State-owned Foreign Trade Corporation and the Joint State-Private International Trade Corporation, toured factories producing rubber, printing and dyeing, textiles, film production, and sewing machines, and visited the city center. Some members of the delegations also visited Shanghai business figures Rong Yiren, Wu Zhichao, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, October 12th. The Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, a group of seventy-five people, left here this afternoon by special train for Fujian.

The two trade delegations arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai on the afternoon of October 10th. During their stay in Hangzhou, they visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory and toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake.

### 新加坡、马来亚贸易考察团同上海外贸公司  成交了二十多万英镑的交易

1956-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡、马来亚贸易考察团同上海外贸公司
    成交了二十多万英镑的交易
    新华社上海12日电　新加坡工商业贸易考察团和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团，在上海期间同上海的对外贸易公司成交了为数达二十多万英镑的交易。他们购买了桐油、杂豆、棉布、瓷砖、火柴、唱片以及针棉织品、小百货等商品。
    两个考察团在10月7日到上海，10日离上海去杭州，在这期间，访问了上海国营对外贸易公司和公私合营的国际贸易公司，参观了橡胶、印染、纺织、电影制片、缝纫机等工厂，游览了市区；部分团员并且访问了上海工商界人士荣毅仁、吴志超等人。
    据新华社杭州12日电　新加坡工商业贸易考察团和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团一行七十五人，今天下午从这里乘专车赴福建。
    这两个贸易考察团是10日下午从上海来杭州的。他们在杭期间参观了都锦生丝织厂，游览了西湖名胜古迹。
