Title: Two French Communist Party Deputies Elected as Vice Presidents of the National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1956-10-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 5th, TASS, Paris: The French National Assembly elected its secretariat at the meeting on the 3rd. The secretariat consists of six vice presidents, fourteen secretaries, and three treasurers. The secretariat is elected according to the proportion of seats occupied by each parliamentary group. The communist Roger Garaudy was elected as the first vice president. Another communist, Marie-Claude Vaillant-Couturier, was also elected as vice president. Among the fourteen secretaries, there are three communists.

### 法共两个议员当选国民议会副议长

1956-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    法共两个议员当选国民议会副议长
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：法国国民议会在3日会议上选出了秘书处，秘书处由六个副议长、十四个秘书和三个司库组成。秘书处是按各议会党团所占议席比例选出的。共产党人罗歇·加罗迪当选为第一副议长。另一个共产党人玛丽—克洛德·瓦扬—古久里也当选为副议长。在十四个秘书中有三个共产党人。
