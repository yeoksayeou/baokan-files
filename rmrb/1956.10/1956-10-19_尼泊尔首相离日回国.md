Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Leaves Japan for Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-10-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th: Nepalese Prime Minister Acharya concluded his five-day visit to Japan and flew back home on the evening of the 16th.

Before leaving Japan, Prime Minister Acharya told Japanese reporters that Nepal would welcome the assistance of Japanese technical personnel. However, there are no plans to discuss economic cooperation between Japan and Nepal at this time.

He said that the People's Republic of China has decided to provide Nepal with unconditional assistance of 60 million rupees; India will provide assistance of 100 million rupees. He hopes to receive assistance of 80 million rupees from the United States.

### 尼泊尔首相离日回国

1956-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相离日回国
    据新华社18日讯　东京消息：尼泊尔首相阿查里雅已经结束了他在日本为期五天的访问，在16日晚乘飞机回国。
    阿查里雅首相在离开日本以前曾经对日本记者说，尼泊尔将欢迎日本技术人员的帮助。但是目前还没有计划要讨论日本—尼泊尔之间的经济合作的问题。
    他说，中华人民共和国已经决定对尼泊尔提供不附带条件的六千万卢比的援助；印度将提供一亿卢比的援助。他希望能从美国得到八千万卢比的援助。
