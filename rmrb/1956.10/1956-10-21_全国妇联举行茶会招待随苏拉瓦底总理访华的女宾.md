Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Tea Party to Greet Female Guests Accompanying Prime Minister Suhrawardy on Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1956-10-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. The All-China Women's Federation held a tea party this afternoon to welcome Mrs. Akhtar Suleiman, daughter of the Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Suhrawardy, as well as Mrs. Noon, Mrs. Ahmed, Princess Sultan, Mrs. Begg, and other wives of distinguished guests accompanying the Prime Minister on his visit to China.

The host of the tea party, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to the sisters from Pakistan.

Mrs. Akhtar Suleiman repeatedly expressed her gratitude for the warm hospitality of the hosts. She said, "We have finally come to China, and we will never forget the warm welcome we have received here." She expressed her hope that Chinese and Pakistani women will help each other, exchange ideas, and advance together in the future.

The guests and hosts engaged in pleasant conversation during the hour-and-a-half tea party.

### 全国妇联举行茶会招待随苏拉瓦底总理访华的女宾

1956-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联举行茶会招待随苏拉瓦底总理访华的女宾
    据新华社20日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天下午举行茶会招待巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理苏拉瓦底的女儿阿赫塔·苏来曼夫人和努恩夫人、阿哈默德夫人、苏尔唐公主、贝格夫人以及其他随同总理访华的贵宾的夫人们。
    茶会的主人、全国妇联副主席李德全向来自巴基斯坦的姊妹们表示热烈欢迎。
    阿赫塔·苏来曼夫人对主人们的热情招待一再表示感谢。她说，我们终于来到中国了，我们在这里所受到的热情欢迎是永远不会忘记的。她希望中巴两国妇女今后互相帮助，交流思想，共同前进。
    在历时一个半小时的茶会上，宾主们进行了愉快的交谈。
