Title: Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day
Author:
Date: 1956-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Tonight, Czechoslovak Military Attaché to China, Colonel Dvorak, held a reception to celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day.

Among those attending the reception were Xiao Ke, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, as well as Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army including Li Kenong, Chen Shiju, and Xiao Xiangrong.

Ambassadors and military attachés from various countries to China also attended tonight's reception.

At the reception, Colonel Dvorak and General Xiao Ke delivered speeches successively. In their speeches, they both emphasized the growing friendship between the people and armies of China and Czechoslovakia, and the great role of the two armies in defending world peace.

### 捷驻华武官举行酒会庆祝捷建军节

1956-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    捷驻华武官举行酒会庆祝捷建军节
    新华社6日讯　今天晚上，捷克斯洛伐克驻华武官杜瑞上校为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克建军节举行酒会。
    出席酒会的，有中华人民共和国国防部副部长萧克上将以及李克农上将、陈士矩上将、萧向荣中将等中国人民解放军高级将领多人。
    各国驻华大使和武官也出席了今晚的酒会。
    酒会上，杜瑞上校和萧克上将先后讲了话。他们在讲话中都强调指出中、捷两国人民及其军队之间的友谊日益增长和两国军队保卫世界和平的巨大作用。
