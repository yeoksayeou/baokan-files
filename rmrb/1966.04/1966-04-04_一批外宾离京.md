Title: A Batch of Foreign Guests Depart from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    △ The Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation, invited by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Scientific and Technological Commission to come to China to sign the 1966 Executive Plan for the Sino-Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Cooperation Agreement and to pay a friendly visit, left Beijing by plane on the 2nd to visit various parts of southern China before returning home.
    Shi Huaibi, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Heng, Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission, and others were at the airport to see them off.
    Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.
    △ Habibullah, former Vice Chairman of the Karachi Municipal Committee of Pakistan, who was invited to visit China, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 2nd, heading home via Guangzhou.
    △ Ian Alistair Ballantyne, Chairman of the Dunedin Branch of the New Zealand-China Society, and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, concluded their visit to Beijing and left for Guangzhou on the 2nd before returning home.

### 一批外宾离京

1966-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    一批外宾离京
    △　应对外文化联络委员会和科学技术委员会邀请来我国签订中柬文化科学合作协定一九六六年执行计划并进行友好访问的柬埔寨文化科学代表团，二日乘飞机离京到我国南方各地访问，然后返国。
    到机场欢送的有对外文委副主任史怀璧、科委副主任武衡等。
    柬埔寨驻华大使张岗和使馆人员也前往机场送行。
    △　应邀来我国访问的巴基斯坦前卡拉奇市政委员会副主席哈比布拉，二日上午乘飞机离北京，取道广州回国。
    △　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的新西兰—中国协会达尼丁分会主席伊恩·阿利斯特·巴兰坦及夫人，结束了在北京的访问，二日离京去广州，然后回国。
