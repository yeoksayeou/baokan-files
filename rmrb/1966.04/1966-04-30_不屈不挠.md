Title: Unyielding

Author:

Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 5

    Currently, the people of the world are engaged in a fierce struggle with U.S. imperialism. In this great struggle, the Dominican people are a tenacious force. On the first anniversary of U.S. imperialism's armed intervention in the Dominican Republic, they have risen up courageously again, holding continuous and powerful demonstrations to oppose the aggression of U.S. imperialism.
    U.S. imperialism's armed intervention in the Dominican Republic has resulted in tying a noose around its own neck. The longer U.S. imperialism stays in the Dominican Republic, the deeper the Dominican people's hatred for U.S. imperialism becomes, and the higher their struggle against U.S. imperialism rises. The resistance struggle of the Dominican people will not stop until U.S. imperialism rolls out of the Dominican Republic. U.S. imperialism is carrying out aggression everywhere in the world, and it cannot but fall into one quagmire after another in various places.
    U.S. imperialism and its running dogs attempt to deal with the demonstrating masses in the Dominican Republic with cruel suppression. However, doing so will never extinguish the Dominican people's anti-American anger. Isn't the rising tide of the Dominican people's anti-American struggle precisely aroused by the armed intervention of the United States? The barbaric suppression by U.S. imperialism can only prove that it is not the Dominican people who fear U.S. imperialism, but U.S. imperialism that fears the Dominican people.

### 不屈不挠

1966-04-30
第5版()
专栏：短评

    不屈不挠
    目前，全世界人民同美帝国主义正在进行着一场剧烈的大搏斗。在这场大搏斗中，多米尼加人民是一支顽强的方面军。在美帝国主义武装干涉多米尼加一周年的时候，他们又奋勇而起，连续举行强大的示威，反对美帝国主义的侵略。
    美帝国主义武装干涉多米尼加，结果在自己的脖子上拴上了一条绞索。美帝国主义赖在多米尼加越久，多米尼加人民对美帝国主义的仇恨就越加深，他们反对美帝国主义的斗争就越高涨。美帝国主义一天不从多米尼加滚出去，多米尼加人民的反抗斗争就一天不会停止。美帝国主义在全世界到处进行侵略，它就不能不在各地陷入一个又一个泥潭之中。
    美帝国主义及其走狗，企图用残酷的镇压手段来对付多米尼加的示威群众。但是，他们这样做决扑灭不了多米尼加人民反美的怒火。多米尼加人民日益高涨的反美斗争浪潮，不正是美国的武装干涉所激起的吗？美帝国主义的野蛮镇压，只能证明，不是多米尼加人民害怕美帝国主义，而是美帝国主义害怕多米尼加人民。
