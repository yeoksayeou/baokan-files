Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 401st Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd—On April 22nd, from 7:14 AM to 10:25 AM, a U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters of Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 7:54 AM to 7:58 AM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace south of Huangliu in Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 8:50 AM to 9:04 AM and from 3:41 PM to 4:07 PM, two more U.S. military aircraft successively intruded into the airspace of Bei Island, Yongxing Island, and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesman of our Foreign Ministry was instructed to issue the 401st serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百零一次严重警告

1966-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百零一次严重警告
    新华社二十二日讯　四月二十二日七时十四分至十时二十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领海。同日七时五十四分至七时五十八分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛的黄流以南地区领空。同日八时五十分至九时零四分和十五时四十一分至十六时零七分，又有美国军用飞机各一架，先后侵入我广东省西沙群岛的北岛、永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零一次严重警告。
