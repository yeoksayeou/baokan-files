Title: Afghan Newspapers Praise China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué: China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué is a New Symbol of Strengthened Friendship
Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, April 11th. The Afghan newspaper *Kabul Times* published an editorial on the 10th, praising the China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué. The editorial stated that the China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué is another symbol of the friendly relations between the two countries, and it "demonstrates the long-standing traditional friendship, good-neighborly relations, and cooperative feelings between two countries with different social systems." The communiqué embodies the desire of both countries to uphold the principles of mutual respect, equality, and non-interference in each other's internal affairs.
    The editorial expressed appreciation for the following points mentioned in the Joint Communiqué: "Both countries expressed their support for the struggle of Asian and African countries to strive for and safeguard national independence and eliminate old and new colonialism." "Afghanistan once again reiterated: It is convinced that the (legitimate) rights of the People's Republic of China must be restored in the United Nations."
    The editorial also said: "We believe that Prime Minister Mohammad Hashim Maiwandwal's visit to China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai will further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries."
    The local newspapers have all prominently published the *People's Daily* editorial of April 9th, titled "A New Symbol of the Development of China-Afghanistan Friendship," on their front pages. *Friendship News* also published an editorial titled "The Relations Between Afghanistan and China are Based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

### 阿富汗报纸赞扬中阿联合公报  中阿联合公报是友谊加强的新标志

1966-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    阿富汗报纸赞扬中阿联合公报
    中阿联合公报是友谊加强的新标志
    新华社喀布尔十一日电　阿富汗《喀布尔时报》十日发表社论，赞扬中国—阿富汗联合公报。社论说，中国—阿富汗联合公报是两国友好关系的又一标志，它“表明了两个不同社会制度国家的悠久的传统友谊、睦邻关系和合作感情”。公报体现了两国维护相互尊重、平等和互不干涉内政的原则的愿望。
    社论对联合公报提到的下列问题表示赞扬：“两国表示支持亚非国家争取和维护民族独立、消灭新老殖民主义的斗争。”“阿富汗再一次重申：它确信必须恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的（合法）权利。”
    社论还说：“我们相信，穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔首相应周恩来总理的邀请对中国的访问，将进一步加强两国之间的友好关系。”
    这里的报纸都在头版以显著地位刊登了北京《人民日报》九日题为《中国阿富汗友谊发展的新标志》的社论。《友谊报》还以《阿富汗和中国的关系是建立在和平共处五项原则的基础上的》为题发表了社论。
