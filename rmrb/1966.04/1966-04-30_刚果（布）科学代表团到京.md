Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Scientific Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th - The Congolese (Brazzaville) scientific delegation, invited by the China Association for Science and Technology to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and pay a visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Makani Levy, Director-General of Education of the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Ministry of Education, and consisted of two members.

### 刚果（布）科学代表团到京

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）科学代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应中国科学技术协会邀请来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动和访问的刚果（布）科学代表团一行二人，在团长刚果（布）国民教育部教育总局长马卡尼·列维率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
