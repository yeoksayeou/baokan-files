Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to Syria Arrives in Damascus
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 6th – Chen Tan, the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Syria, arrived in Damascus, the Syrian capital, on the 6th.
    He was welcomed at the airport by Samouhi Foklad, Director of the Protocol Department of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Li Huanong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Syria, and other officials of the embassy also greeted him at the airport.

### 我新任驻叙利亚大使到大马士革

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻叙利亚大使到大马士革
    新华社大马士革六日电　新任命的中国驻叙利亚大使陈坦六日到达叙利亚首都大马士革。
    他在机场受叙利亚外交部礼宾司司长萨穆希·福克拉德的欢迎。中国驻叙利亚大使馆临时代办李华农和大使馆的其他官员也到机场迎接。
