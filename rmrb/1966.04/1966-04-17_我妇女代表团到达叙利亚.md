Title: My Women's Delegation Arrives in Syria
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 15th. The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by Guo Jian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, arrived here on the morning of the 15th at the invitation of the Syrian Women's Federation for a friendly visit to Syria.
    Ms. Jazeri, a member of the Syrian Women's Federation Committee, and other heads of the Federation welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    Chen Tan, the Chinese Ambassador to Syria, and his wife also greeted the delegation at the airport.

### 我妇女代表团到达叙利亚

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团到达叙利亚
    新华社大马士革十五日电  由中华人民共和国妇女联合会书记处书记、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员郭建率领的中国妇女代表团，应叙利亚妇女联合会的邀请，十五日上午到达这里，对叙利亚进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：叙利亚妇女联合会委员会委员贾泽里女士和联合会的其他负责人。
    中国驻叙利亚大使陈坦和夫人也到机场迎接。
