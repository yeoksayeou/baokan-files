Title: Johnson Praises Sato for Peddling Plan to Invade Southeast Asia
Author:
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 14th dispatch: Japanese Prime Minister Eisaku Sato, acting on American directives, held talks with U.S. Ambassador to Japan, Reischauer, on the 13th, following the "Ministerial Conference for Southeast Asian Development." According to Kyodo News, Sato and Reischauer, during their meeting, exchanged views on "current problems such as the Vietnam situation, the status quo in Indonesia, and the results of the 'Ministerial Conference for Southeast Asian Development'," in addition to discussing the holding of a joint Japan-U.S. Trade and Economic Committee meeting in July. It is reported that Sato, through Reischauer, reported to the United States on the "achievements" of the "Ministerial Conference for Southeast Asian Development," where Japan took the lead in peddling Johnson's plan to invade Southeast Asia. Reischauer conveyed Johnson's praise for the Sato government, saying that "the U.S. President gives a high evaluation to this conference."
    According to the *Sankei Shimbun*, a meeting held by the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 8th decided to convey the situation of the "Ministerial Conference for Southeast Asian Development" to some countries concerned about it. These countries and regions, in addition to the United States, include the Soviet Union, India, Yugoslavia, the Park Chung-hee puppet clique, and the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang in Taiwan.

### 约翰逊夸奖佐藤兜售侵略东南亚计划

1966-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    约翰逊夸奖佐藤兜售侵略东南亚计划
    新华社十四日讯  东京消息：日本首相佐藤荣作在秉承美国旨意召开“东南亚开发部长会议”之后，十三日同美国驻日本大使赖肖尔举行了会谈。据共同社报道，佐藤和赖肖尔在会谈中，除了讨论在七月间召开日美贸易经济联合委员会会议问题外，还就“越南局势、印度尼西亚现状、‘东南亚开发部长会议’结果等当前面临的问题交换了意见”。据报道，佐藤通过赖肖尔向美国汇报了由日本出面兜售约翰逊侵略东南亚计划的“东南亚开发部长会议”所取得的“成果”。赖肖尔传达了约翰逊对佐藤政府的称赞，说“美国总统对这次会议给以很高评价”。
    据《产经新闻》报道，日本外务省八日举行的一次会议决定，将向关心“东南亚开发部长会议”的一些国家传达这次会议的情况。这些国家和地区除了美国之外，还有苏联、印度、南斯拉夫、朴正熙傀儡集团和台湾蒋介石匪帮等。
