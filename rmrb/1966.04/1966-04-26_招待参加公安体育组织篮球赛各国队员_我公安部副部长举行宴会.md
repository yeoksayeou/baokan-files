Title: Deputy Minister of Public Security Holds Banquet for Players from Various Countries Participating in the Public Security Sports Organization Basketball Tournament
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Hosting a Banquet for Players from Various Countries Participating in the Public Security Sports Organization Basketball Tournament
    Deputy Minister of Public Security Hosts Banquet
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 25th) Yang Qiqing, Deputy Minister of Public Security and Director of the National Committee of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome members of the basketball teams from various countries participating in the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations. Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Ling Yun, Vice Minister of Public Security, and the Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations, attended tonight's banquet.

### 招待参加公安体育组织篮球赛各国队员  我公安部副部长举行宴会

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    招待参加公安体育组织篮球赛各国队员
    我公安部副部长举行宴会
    据新华社二十五日讯　公安部副部长兼中国“前卫”体育协会全国委员会主任杨奇清，今晚设宴，招待参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会的各国篮球代表队的成员。国家体委副主任荣高棠，公安部副部长凌云和社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会组织委员会主任委员等，出席了今晚的宴会。
