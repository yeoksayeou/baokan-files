Title: Our Civil Aviation Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Our Civil Aviation Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. The Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics held negotiations in Moscow from February 17th regarding the revision of the 1954 Aviation Transportation Agreement between the two governments and have reached an agreement. The two sides signed a new Aviation Transportation Agreement in Moscow on April 4th. The Chinese Civil Aviation Delegation, a group of four people led by Deputy Head Shen Tu, returned to Beijing this afternoon. Liu Jinping, Chen Boqing, Zhang Xisan, Li Ming, and other heads of relevant departments went to the airport to greet them. Mo Chuli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others also went to the airport to greet them.

### 我民用航空代表团回到北京

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我民用航空代表团回到北京
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国政府民用航空代表团和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府民用航空代表团，于二月十七日开始在莫斯科就修改两国政府一九五四年航空交通协定进行了谈判，并已达成协议。双方已于四月四日在莫斯科签订了新的航空交通协定。中国民用航空代表团一行四人，由副团长沈图率领今天下午回到北京。前往机场迎接的，有各有关部门负责人刘锦平、陈伯清、张西三、黎明等。苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办莫初黎等也到机场迎接。
