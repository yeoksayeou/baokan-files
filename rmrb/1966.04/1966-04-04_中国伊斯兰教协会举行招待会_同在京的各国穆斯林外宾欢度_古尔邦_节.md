Title: China Islamic Association Holds Reception to Celebrate "Eid al-Adha" with Foreign Muslim Guests in Beijing

Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    China Islamic Association Holds Reception
    Celebrating "Eid al-Adha" with Foreign Muslim Guests in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd) The capital's Muslims gathered this afternoon at a reception held by the China Islamic Association with Muslim envoys and their spouses from Asian and African countries stationed in China, Muslim experts helping with China's construction, and Muslim foreign guests visiting Beijing to celebrate "Eid al-Adha."
    Sha Mengbi, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, spoke at the reception, extending festive greetings to Muslim brothers from various countries. He introduced the happy lives of Chinese Muslims and praised the deep historical friendship between the Chinese people and Muslims and the peoples and Muslims of various Asian and African countries.
    Sha Mengbi said that Chinese Muslims, together with the entire Chinese people, resolutely support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against U.S. aggression and for national salvation, and resolutely support the just struggle of the peoples of Asia, Africa, Latin America, and other countries against the U.S.
    Sha Mengbi condemned U.S. and British imperialism for plotting to establish the so-called "Islamic Pact" group in the Middle East.
    UAE Ambassador to China, Imam, also spoke at the reception. He condemned the colonialists for attempting to use the name of Islam to achieve their goals of aggression and suppression of national liberation movements. He said that Muslims of all countries should support the just struggles of the Vietnamese and Palestinian peoples.
    Also present at the reception were Liu Pinyi, Li Shu, and Bai Shouyi, Deputy Directors of the China Islamic Association, and Ding Xilin, Zhang Tiesheng, Wang Chuanbin, Liu Ying and other people from various circles in the capital.

### 中国伊斯兰教协会举行招待会  同在京的各国穆斯林外宾欢度“古尔邦”节

1966-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会举行招待会
    同在京的各国穆斯林外宾欢度“古尔邦”节
    据新华社二日讯　首都穆斯林今天下午在中国伊斯兰教协会举行的招待会上，同亚非各国驻中国的穆斯林使节和夫人、帮助我国建设的穆斯林专家、正在北京访问的穆斯林外宾聚集一堂，欢度“古尔邦”节。
    中国伊斯兰教协会副主任沙梦弼在招待会上讲话，向各国穆斯林兄弟致以节日的祝贺。他介绍了中国穆斯林的幸福生活，并且赞扬了中国人民和穆斯林同亚非各国人民和穆斯林的深厚历史友谊。
    沙梦弼说，中国穆斯林同全中国人民一起，坚决支持越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争，坚决支持亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和其他各国人民的反美正义斗争。
    沙梦弼谴责美英帝国主义正在中东策划建立所谓“伊斯兰条约”集团。
    阿联驻中国大使伊马姆也在招待会上致词。他谴责殖民主义者企图用伊斯兰教的名义来达到他们进行侵略和镇压民族解放运动的目的。他说，各国穆斯林应该支持越南人民和巴勒斯坦人民的正义斗争。
    出席招待会的，还有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任刘品一、李恕、白寿彝，首都各界人士丁西林、张铁生、王传斌、刘郢等。
