Title: Iraqi President Killed in Plane Crash
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 14th (Dispatch) – According to a report from Baghdad Radio on the morning of April 14th, Iraqi President Abdul Salam Muhammad Arif was killed on the evening of April 13th when the plane he was traveling in crashed during a severe storm en route from Qurna to Basra.

    The message stated that Minister of Interior Daraji, Minister of Industry Mustafa Abdullah, and eight others were also killed in the accident.

    According to a statement issued by the Iraqi government, in accordance with the provisions of the Iraqi Interim Constitution, Iraqi Prime Minister Abdul Rahman al-Bazzaz will serve as acting president until a new president is elected within one week.

### 伊拉克总统因飞机失事遇难

1966-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克总统因飞机失事遇难
    新华社巴格达十四日电　据巴格达电台十四日上午报道，伊拉克总统阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·穆罕默德·阿里夫十三日晚上在从库尔纳前往巴士拉途中，因所乘飞机在猛烈风暴中失事遇难。
    消息说，同机遇难的还有内政部长达拉吉、工业部长穆斯塔法·阿卜杜拉，以及其他八人。
    据伊拉克政府发表的一项声明宣布，根据伊拉克临时宪法的规定，由伊拉克总理阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·巴扎兹代行总统职务，直到在一周内选出新总统为止。
