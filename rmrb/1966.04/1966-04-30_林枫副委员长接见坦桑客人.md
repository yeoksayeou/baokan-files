Title: Vice Chairman Lin Feng Receives Tanzanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th – Vice Chairman Lin Feng received all members of the Tanzanian education delegation led by delegation head A. Makame and deputy head Masrur Sad this morning, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Guiru, Fang Nanjun, and others.
    Tanzanian Ambassador to China Wazir Juma was also present.

### 林枫副委员长接见坦桑客人

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长接见坦桑客人
    新华社二十九日讯　林枫副委员长今天上午接见了由团长阿·马卡梅、副团长马斯鲁尔·萨德率领的坦桑尼亚教育代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有张桂如、方南君等。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也在座。
