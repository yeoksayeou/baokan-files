Title: Sino-Canadian Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: A nine-member delegation from the Canada-China Friendship Association, led by the delegation head Andrew Chow, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit our country.
    Ren Yinglun, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others were present at the airport to welcome them.

### 加中友协代表团到京

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    加中友协代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请，来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动和访问的加拿大—中国友好协会代表团一行九人，在团长安德鲁·周的率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外友协副会长任映仑等。
