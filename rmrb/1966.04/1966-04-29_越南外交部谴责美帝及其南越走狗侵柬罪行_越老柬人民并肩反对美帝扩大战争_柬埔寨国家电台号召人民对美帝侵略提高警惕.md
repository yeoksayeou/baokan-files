Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Condemns U.S. Imperialism and its South Vietnamese Puppets' Crimes of Aggression Against Cambodia; Vietnamese, Lao, and Cambodian People Stand Shoulder to Shoulder Against U.S. Imperialism's Expansion of the War; Cambodian National Radio Calls on the People to Heighten Vigilance Against U.S. Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th – The Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 27th, strongly condemning the crimes of U.S. imperialism and its South Vietnamese puppets for repeatedly sending troops to invade the territory of the Kingdom of Cambodia on April 15th and 18th, killing and wounding many Cambodian soldiers and civilians. The statement pointed out that this act of aggression by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys against the Kingdom of Cambodia once again exposed U.S. imperialism's ambition to recklessly expand the war of aggression to the countries of Indochina.
    The statement said that U.S. imperialists and their running dogs must bear full responsibility for the consequences arising from their rampant actions.
    The statement expressed full support for the correct position in the statement of the Royal Government of Cambodia on April 23, 1966. The statement said that the Vietnamese people are determined to stand shoulder to shoulder with the Cambodian and Lao people to jointly struggle to defend the independence, sovereignty, and territorial integrity of their countries and oppose U.S. imperialism's conspiracy to expand its war of aggression.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh – An article broadcast by Cambodian National Radio on the 23rd pointed out that because of the intensified aggression against Cambodia by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys recently, "our vigilance cannot be relaxed for even a day."
    The article said: "Our patience is limited, and this intolerable rampant aggression, especially the renewed aggression against our Takeo Province, will have the most serious consequences. Therefore, international public opinion needs to understand more than ever that the imperialists' constant aggression against our borders also increases the new threat to world peace."

### 越南外交部谴责美帝及其南越走狗侵柬罪行  越老柬人民并肩反对美帝扩大战争  柬埔寨国家电台号召人民对美帝侵略提高警惕

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    越南外交部谴责美帝及其南越走狗侵柬罪行
    越老柬人民并肩反对美帝扩大战争
    柬埔寨国家电台号召人民对美帝侵略提高警惕
    新华社河内二十八日电　越南民主共和国外交部二十七日发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义及其南越走狗四月十五日和十八日连续派遣军队侵犯柬埔寨王国领土，打死打伤柬埔寨军民多人的罪行。声明指出，美帝国主义及其走狗对柬埔寨王国的这一侵略行动，再一次暴露了美帝国主义企图冒险地把侵略战争扩大到印度支那各国的野心。
    声明说，美帝国主义者及其走狗必须对由于它们的猖狂行动所引起的后果承担全部责任。
    声明表示完全支持柬埔寨王国政府一九六六年四月二十三日声明中的正确立场。声明说，越南人民坚决与柬埔寨人民和老挝人民并肩一起，为保卫自己国家的独立、主权和领土完整和反对美帝国主义扩大侵略战争的阴谋而共同斗争。
    据新华社金边电　柬埔寨国家电台二十三日广播的一篇文章指出，由于美帝国主义及其走狗最近对柬埔寨进行变本加厉的侵略，“我们的警惕一天也不能放松”。
    文章说：“我们的忍耐是有限度的，这种不可容忍的猖狂侵略特别是对我们茶胶省的再次侵略，将产生最严重的后果。因此，国际舆论比以往任何时候都需要了解，帝国主义者对我们边界的不断侵略，也即增加了对世界和平的新的威胁。”
