Title: Boston Masses Demonstrate Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam; Four Youths Publicly Burn Draft Cards in Protest
Author:
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Boston: On March 31, a group of Boston residents held a demonstration in front of the courthouse. Four youths participating in the demonstration publicly burned their draft cards to express their protest against Johnson's war of aggression in Vietnam.
    These demonstrators had previously held an anti-war demonstration outside the Boston Army Base on March 25 and were subsequently summoned to court. However, the demonstrators were not intimidated by the authorities' persecution and held another demonstration before appearing in court.
    The demonstrators were beaten by dozens of thugs in front of the courthouse, and some were injured.

### 波士顿群众示威反对美国侵略越南  四名青年当众烧毁征兵证以示抗议

1966-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    波士顿群众示威反对美国侵略越南
    四名青年当众烧毁征兵证以示抗议
    新华社讯　波士顿消息：波士顿一批居民三月三十一日在法院门前举行示威，参加示威的四名青年当众烧毁了他们的征兵证，以表示他们对约翰逊侵越战争的抗议。
    这些示威者曾经在三月二十五日在波士顿陆军基地外面举行反战示威，并因而受到法院的传讯。但是示威者并没有被当局的迫害吓倒，他们在出庭前再次举行了示威。
    示威者在法院门前受到数十名暴徒的殴打，一些人被打伤。
