Title: Dhaka Women's Groups Host Tea Party to Welcome Madam Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, 16th. Dhaka women's groups in East Pakistan jointly held a tea party on the afternoon of the 16th to welcome Madam Wang Guangmei, wife of Chairman Liu Shaoqi.
    Madam Zhang Qian, wife of Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Madam Chang Naizhi, wife of the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Ding Guoyu, also attended the tea party.
    In her speech at the tea party, Mrs. Salima Ahmed, President of the East Pakistan Branch of the All Pakistan Women's Association, expressed a warm welcome to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, and Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, for their visit to Pakistan.
    She said that Pakistani women are grateful to the Chinese people for their support of Pakistan, and they have deep feelings for Chinese women and the Chinese people. She expressed her hope that the visit of Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife would further develop the friendship between the women and people of the two countries.
    Wang Guangmei said in her speech: "During our visit to Pakistan, we have had extensive contact with Pakistani sisters and friends, which has enabled us to further understand your lives, your activities, and your aspirations." She said, "We are pleased to see that Pakistani sisters have an increasingly broad field to display their intelligence and talents in safeguarding national independence and participating in social life. The great people and women of Pakistan love their motherland and have lofty aspirations; they are admirable."
    She said: "In recent years, the friendship between our two peoples has developed anew and has withstood the test of difficulties. During our visit, whether in West Pakistan or East Pakistan, we heard people everywhere cheering and praising the friendship between China and Pakistan. The Chinese people and women cherish this friendship very much. We firmly believe that the friendship between our two peoples and women will play a greater role in the future struggle against imperialism, against foreign aggression, and in safeguarding national independence."
    Among the more than 400 women who attended the tea party were: the wife of Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto, leading members of East Pakistan women's organizations, and well-known figures in women's circles. Earlier today, Chairman Liu Shaoqi's wife and Vice Premier Chen Yi's wife, accompanied by Mrs. Bhutto, visited the East Pakistan College of Arts and Crafts here.
    The Chinese guests, led by Zainul Abedin, president of the college, visited an exhibition of paintings, pottery, and other artwork by the college's students.

### 达卡妇女团体茶会欢迎刘主席夫人

1966-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    达卡妇女团体茶会欢迎刘主席夫人
    新华社达卡十六日电　东巴基斯坦达卡各妇女团体十六日下午联合举行茶会，欢迎刘少奇主席的夫人王光美。
    陈毅副总理的夫人张茜和中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰的夫人常乃志也出席了茶会。
    全巴基斯坦妇女协会东巴基斯坦分会主席萨利马·艾哈迈德夫人在茶会上的讲话中，表示热烈欢迎刘少奇主席和夫人、陈毅副总理和夫人访问巴基斯坦。
    她说，巴基斯坦妇女感谢中国人民对巴基斯坦的支持，她们对中国妇女和人民怀有深情厚谊。她表示希望，刘少奇主席和夫人的访问将使两国妇女和人民的友谊继续发展。
    王光美在讲话中说：“在我们这次访问巴基斯坦期间，我们同巴基斯坦的姊妹们和朋友们进行了广泛的接触，使我们进一步了解了你们的生活，你们的活动和愿望。”她说：“我们高兴地看到，巴基斯坦姊妹们在维护民族独立和参加社会生活方面已经有了愈来愈广阔的天地来发挥她们的聪明和才智。巴基斯坦伟大的人民和妇女热爱祖国，有崇高的志向，他们是令人钦佩的。”
    她说：“最近几年来，我们两国人民的友谊有了新的发展，并且经受了困难的考验。在我们这次访问期间，不论在西巴基斯坦还是在东巴基斯坦，我们到处听到人们欢呼称赞中国和巴基斯坦之间的友谊。中国人民和妇女十分珍视这种友谊。我们深信，我们两国人民和妇女之间的友谊，在今后反对帝国主义，反对外国侵略和维护民族独立的斗争中将会起更大的作用。”
    参加茶会的四百多名妇女中有：巴基斯坦外交部长布托的夫人，东巴基斯坦各妇女组织的领导成员和妇女界知名人士。今天早些时候，刘少奇主席的夫人和陈毅副总理的夫人由布托夫人陪同，参观了这里的东巴基斯坦工艺美术学院。
    中国客人由学院院长载努尔·阿贝丁带领参观了学院学生的绘画、陶器和其他艺术作品展览会。
