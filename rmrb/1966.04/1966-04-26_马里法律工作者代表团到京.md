Title: Malian Legal Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th – The Malian legal workers delegation, consisting of Youssouf Kouate and Mamadou Yatassaye, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society.  Wang Jiren, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Qin Ping, Secretary of the Secretariat, were present at the airport to welcome them.
    Malian Ambassador to China, Birama Traoré, was also at the airport to greet them.

### 马里法律工作者代表团到京

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    马里法律工作者代表团到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的马里法律工作者代表团尤苏夫·库阿特和马马杜·雅塔萨耶二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国政治法律学会副会长王吉仁、书记处书记秦平等。
    马里驻华大使比拉马·特拉奥雷也到机场迎接。
