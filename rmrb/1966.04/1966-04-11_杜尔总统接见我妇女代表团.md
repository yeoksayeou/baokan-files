Title: President Touré Receives Chinese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, April 9th – Guinean President Sékou Touré received the Chinese Women's Delegation, led by Guo Jian, and all members of the delegation at the Presidential Palace on the morning of April 9th. President Touré had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Chai Zemin, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, and his wife.

### 杜尔总统接见我妇女代表团

1966-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统接见我妇女代表团
    新华社科纳克里九日电　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔九日上午在总统府接见了中国妇女代表团团长郭建和代表团的全体团员。杜尔总统同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民和他的夫人。
