Title: Kenyan President and Many Foreign Groups and Individuals Send Messages of Condolence to People in Our Earthquake-stricken Areas

Author:
Date: 1966-04-10
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 9th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai recently received messages of condolence from Kenyan President Kenyatta and Yugoslav Federal Executive Council President Stambolic regarding the earthquake in Hebei Province. In their telegrams, they expressed condolences to the people affected by the disaster in the earthquake-stricken areas of Hebei Province. In addition, some Chinese people's organizations have also received messages of condolence or letters of sympathy from trade unions, women's federations, Red Cross societies, friendship associations with China, and individuals from Albania, Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Japan, Britain, Yugoslavia, Turkey, and other countries. Representatives of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, some democratic parties, and people's organizations in Vietnam also visited the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam to express their condolences.

### 肯尼亚总统等和许多外国团体及个人  来电慰问我地震灾区人民

1966-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    肯尼亚总统等和许多外国团体及个人
    来电慰问我地震灾区人民
    新华社九日讯　刘少奇主席和周恩来总理，最近分别收到了肯尼亚总统肯雅塔和南斯拉夫联邦执委会主席斯坦鲍利奇就我河北省发生地震发来的慰问电。他们在电报中对河北省地震地区受到灾害的人民表示慰问。另外，我国一些人民团体也收到了阿尔巴尼亚、越南、朝鲜、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、日本、英国、南斯拉夫、土耳其等国的工会、妇联、红十字会、对华友好协会等团体和个人发来的慰问电或慰问信。越南祖国战线、越南的一些民主党派和人民团体的代表，还到中国驻越南大使馆表示慰问。
