Title: China-Cambodia Friendship Association Decides to Appoint Zhang Hanfu as President
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th -- In order to strengthen the work of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, the Standing Council of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association has decided to adjust and enrich the organizational structure and leadership members of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, with Zhang Hanfu serving as the President.

**Named Entities**

中国柬埔寨友好协会	Zhongguo Jianpuzhai Youhao Xiehui (China-Cambodia Friendship Association)
章汉夫	Zhang Hanfu
新华社	Xinhua News Agency

### 中柬友协决定由章汉夫任会长

1966-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中柬友协决定由章汉夫任会长
    新华社二十七日讯　为加强中国柬埔寨友好协会的工作，经中柬友协常务理事会决定调整、充实中柬友协的组织机构和领导成员，由章汉夫担任会长。
