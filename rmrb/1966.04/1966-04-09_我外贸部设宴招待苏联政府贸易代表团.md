Title: The Ministry of Foreign Trade Hosts a Banquet for the Soviet Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Our Ministry of Foreign Trade Hosts a Banquet for the Soviet Government Trade Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th.  Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, hosted a banquet this evening in honor of the Soviet Government Trade Delegation led by Grishin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union. Mo Chuli, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also invited to attend.
    This morning, Lin Haiyun received all members of the Soviet Government Trade Delegation.

### 我外贸部设宴招待苏联政府贸易代表团

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我外贸部设宴招待苏联政府贸易代表团
    新华社八日讯　对外贸易部代理部长林海云今天晚上设宴招待由苏联对外贸易部副部长格里申率领的苏联政府贸易代表团。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办莫初黎也应邀出席。
    今天上午，林海云接见了苏联政府贸易代表团全体成员。
