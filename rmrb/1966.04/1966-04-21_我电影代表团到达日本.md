Title: Chinese Film Delegation Arrives in Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Our Film Delegation Arrives in Japan
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th. The Chinese film delegation of seven people, led by Yue Lin, director of the Changchun Film Studio, arrived in Tokyo on the 19th.
    More than thirty people went to the airport to welcome them, including famous film directors Tomu Uchida and Satsuo Yamamoto, actors Izumi Ashikawa and Shiro Osaka, film screenwriter Masato Ide, and film critics Akira Iwasaki and Tadao Uryu.
    Young Japanese women from the film industry presented flowers to all members of the Chinese film delegation.
    Tomu Uchida delivered a speech on behalf of the Japanese film industry and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, warmly welcoming the Chinese film delegation. Head of Delegation Yue Lin also delivered a speech.

### 我电影代表团到达日本

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    我电影代表团到达日本
    新华社东京二十日电　由长春电影制片厂厂长岳林率领的中国电影代表团一行七人十九日到达东京。
    前往机场欢迎的有著名电影导演内田吐梦、山本萨夫，演员芦川泉、大坂志郎，电影剧作家井手雅人，电影评论家岩崎昶、瓜生忠夫等三十多人。
    日本电影界女青年向中国电影代表团全体人员献了花。
    内田吐梦代表日本电影界和日中文化交流协会致词，表示热烈欢迎中国电影代表团。岳林团长也讲了话。
