Title: Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Hosts a Banquet for the Japan-China Friendship Association Youth Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 1st – Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, hosted a banquet this evening to entertain the Japan-China Friendship Association Youth Women's Delegation led by Sugita Hideko.
    Responsible individuals from relevant departments who attended the banquet included Luo Shuzhang, Xia Zhixu, Cao Mengjun, Shen Zijiu, Zeng Xianzhi, Yang Yunyu, and Wu Quanheng.
    Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing, also attended the banquet.

### 我妇联副主席宴请日中友协青年妇女代表团

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇联副主席宴请日中友协青年妇女代表团
    新华社一日讯　全国妇联副主席许广平今晚设宴招待由杉田日出子率领的日中友协青年妇女代表团。
    出席宴会的有罗叔章、夏之栩、曹孟君、沈兹九、曾宪植、杨蕴玉、吴全衡等有关方面的负责人。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也出席了宴会。
