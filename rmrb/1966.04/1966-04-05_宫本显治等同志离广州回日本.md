Title: Kenji Miyamoto and Comrades Leave Guangzhou for Japan
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 4th – Kenji Miyamoto, head of the delegation of the Japanese Communist Party and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, along with some members and staff of the delegation, left Guangzhou by train today to return to their country. Those who saw them off at the station included Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC Central-South Bureau; Zhao Ziyang, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC; and Lin Liming, Qu Mengjue, Yin Linping, and Li Jianzhen, Secretaries of the Secretariat; as well as Zeng Sheng, Third Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, and others. Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.
    Masayoshi Oka, Deputy Head of the Delegation, Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Secretary of the Secretariat, returned to his country a few days ago.

### 宫本显治等同志离广州回日本

1966-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    宫本显治等同志离广州回日本
    新华社广州四日电　日本共产党代表团团长、日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治同志和代表团部分团员、随员今天乘火车离开广州回国。到车站欢送的有中共中央中南局第一书记陶铸，中共广东省委第一书记赵紫阳和书记处书记林李明、区梦觉、尹林平、李坚真，以及中共广州市委第三书记曾生等。少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    代表团副团长、日本共产党中央政治局常务委员、书记处书记冈正芳同志已于日前回国。
