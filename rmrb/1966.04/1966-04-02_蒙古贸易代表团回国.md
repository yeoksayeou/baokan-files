Title: Mongolian Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Mongolian government trade delegation, led by Z. Khishig, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Mongolian People's Republic and head of the delegation, left Beijing by train this afternoon to return home.

### 蒙古贸易代表团回国

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古贸易代表团回国
    据新华社三十日讯　蒙古政府贸易代表团在团长、蒙古人民共和国对外贸易部副部长札·赫希格率领下，今天下午乘火车离开北京回国。
