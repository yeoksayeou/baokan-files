Title: Another Group of Foreign Guests Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-27
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: A delegation from the Ceylon Independent Industrial and Commercial Workers' Union, led by K.M. Punchi Banda, a member of the Ceylon Independent Industrial and Commercial Workers' Union Committee, H.P. Liyanage, General Secretary of the Ceylon National Machine and General Workers' Union, and a delegation from the Zanzibar Afro-Shirazi Party Workers' Department, led by Ali Mdahoma, a member of the Central Committee of the Zanzibar Afro-Shirazi Party Workers' Department, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. These foreign trade union delegations and trade union representatives were invited to our country to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to pay friendly visits.

### 又一批外国客人到京

1966-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    又一批外国客人到京
    新华社二十六日讯　由锡兰独立工商工会委员会委员克·莫·庞奇·班达拉率领的锡兰独立工商工会代表团，锡兰全国机器和普通工人工会总书记赫·普·李亚纳格，由桑给巴尔非洲设拉子党工人部中央委员会委员阿里·姆达霍马率领的桑给巴尔非洲设拉子党工人部代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。这些外国工会代表团和工会代表是应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和进行友好访问的。
