Title: U.S. Planes Brazenly Bomb Suburbs of Hanoi; Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army High Command Lodges Strong Protest

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 17th -- Senior Colonel Ha Van Lau, head of the Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army High Command, sent an urgent telegram to the Chairman of the International Commission for Vietnam on the 17th, strongly protesting the brazen bombing of suburban areas of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, by U.S. bandit planes at 3:00 PM on the 17th.

    The telegram stated that at the same time, U.S. planes also bombed the city of Phu Ly, the capital of Nam Ha province, and a residential area in Hai Duong province.

    The telegram pointed out that this is a new step taken by U.S. imperialism in its destructive war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and a blatant provocation against world peace-loving and justice-upholding public opinion. This barbarous bombing and strafing action by U.S. imperialism proves that it is deliberately intensifying and expanding its war of aggression in order to salvage its current predicament in South Vietnam.

    The telegram stated that the above-mentioned war actions of U.S. imperialism will not allow it to escape defeat in South Vietnam, but will only further expose its extremely bellicose and aggressive nature, and further arouse the strong condemnation of world opinion. This action by the United States also proves that U.S. President Johnson's peace rhetoric is nothing but a deception used to cover up the dangerous war "escalation" conspiracy being carried out by the U.S. government in Vietnam.

    The telegram warned the U.S. government that it must bear full responsibility for all the consequences arising therefrom.

### 美国飞机悍然轰炸河内市郊  越人民军总司令部联络代表团提出强烈抗议

1966-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美国飞机悍然轰炸河内市郊
    越人民军总司令部联络代表团提出强烈抗议
    新华社河内十七日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长何文楼大校十七日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，强烈抗议美国强盗飞机十七日下午三时悍然轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内市郊区。
    电报说，与此同时，美国飞机还轰炸了南河省府里市和海阳省的一个居民区。
    电报指出，这是美帝国主义在对越南民主共和国进行破坏性战争中的一个新的步骤，是对世界爱好和平和主持正义的舆论的一个明目张胆的挑衅。美帝国主义的这一蛮横的轰炸和扫射行动证明，它正在蓄意加强和扩大侵略战争，以挽救它目前在越南南方的困境。
    电报说，美帝国主义的上述战争行动，决不会使它逃脱在越南南方的失败，只会更加暴露出它的极其好战的侵略本质，更加激起世界舆论的强烈谴责。美国的这一行动还证明，美国总统约翰逊的和平论调，只不过是一种欺骗，以便用来掩盖美国政府在越南进行的危险的战争“升级”阴谋。
    电报警告美国政府：它必须对由此而造成的一切后果承担全部责任。
