Title: Sino-Latin American Friendship Association Holds Reception to Welcome Chilean Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 1st, the China-Latin America Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to warmly welcome all members of the Chilean Women's Delegation led by Mrs. Carmen Lasso Carrera, a member of the Chilean House of Representatives. Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, and leading figures from various related fields, including Luo Shuzhang, Cao Mengjun, Fang Ming, and Wu Jieping, attended the reception. At the reception, filled with warm friendship, Chu Tunan and Mrs. Carrera gave toasts successively. They proposed: a toast to the friendship between the people of China and the people of Chile and other Latin American countries in the struggle against imperialism!

### 中拉友协举行招待会欢迎智利客人

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中拉友协举行招待会欢迎智利客人
    据新华社一日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今天下午举行招待会，热烈欢迎由智利众议院议员卡门·拉索·卡雷拉夫人率领的智利妇女代表团全体成员。中拉友协会长楚图南和各有关方面负责人罗叔章、曹孟君、方明、吴阶平等出席了招待会。在洋溢着热情友好的招待会上，楚图南和卡雷拉夫人先后祝酒。他们提议：为中国人民同智利人民和拉丁美洲各国人民在反对帝国主义斗争中的友谊干杯。
