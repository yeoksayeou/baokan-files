Title: My Photography Delegation Visits Albania

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Our Photography Delegation Visits Albania
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 20th) According to the 1966 executive plan of the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, a three-member Chinese photography delegation, led by Gao Fan, editor-in-chief of "China Photography," left by plane this morning for a friendly visit to Albania.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Wu Qun, Secretary-General of the Chinese Photographic Society, and Liu Chengxing, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Liberation Army Pictorial."

### 我摄影代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚访问

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    我摄影代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚访问
    新华社二十日讯　根据中阿文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划，中国摄影代表团一行三人，在团长、《中国摄影》主编高帆的率领下，今天早晨乘飞机前往阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问。
    前往机场送行的有中国摄影学会秘书长吴群，《解放军画报》副总编辑柳成行等。
