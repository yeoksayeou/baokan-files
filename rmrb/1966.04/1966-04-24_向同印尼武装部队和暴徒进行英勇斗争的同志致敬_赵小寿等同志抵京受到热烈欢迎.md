Title: Saluting Comrades Who Fought Heroically Against Indonesian Armed Forces and Rioters; Comrades Zhao Xiaoshou and Others Arrive in Beijing and Receive a Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd – Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou, a staff member of our embassy in Indonesia who was gloriously wounded while heroically protecting our national flag from being insulted by the rioters organized by the Indonesian right-wing military regime, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou was seriously wounded in the chest by gunfire on the morning of April 15th when personnel of the Indonesian armed forces colluded with rioters to brutally attack our embassy in Indonesia. He was shot while trying to protect the solemn five-star national flag of the People's Republic of China from being insulted by the rioters.
    Also returning to Beijing on the same plane were Xing Lu, the economic counselor of our embassy in Indonesia, as well as embassy staff and a group of Chinese economic construction experts in Indonesia. They also participated in the heroic struggle to defend our embassy in Indonesia on April 15th.
    Today, Zhou Rongxin, Secretary-General of the State Council, Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Fang Fang, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission, Shi Huaibi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Relations Commission, and other responsible persons, Wang Ping, Du Yu-yun, Li Wu, Shen Zijiu, Shao Naifen, Zhang Dehua, He Lin, as well as the staff of the Xinhua branch office temporarily withdrawn from Jakarta, went to the airport to welcome Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou and others.
    Yao Zhongming, our ambassador to Indonesia, who is currently in Beijing, also went to the airport to greet and express his regards.
    After Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou was carried off the plane on a stretcher, Zhou Rongxin and others expressed their warm regards to Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou beside the stretcher. Young women presented flowers to Zhao Xiaoshou. Zhou Rongxin and other responsible persons also expressed their warm regards to the other comrades who returned to Beijing at the same time. At this time, hundreds of staff members of the organizations who came to the airport to welcome them warmly applauded, paying tribute to all the comrades who had waged a heroic struggle against the Indonesian armed forces personnel and rioters.
    On their way back to China, Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou and others arrived in Phnom Penh on the morning of the 22nd. Zhao Xiaoshou was welcomed and cared for at the airport by Chen Shuliang, the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, and the embassy staff. Overseas Chinese compatriots and medical personnel living in Cambodia also came to the airport to welcome and take care of Zhao Xiaoshou. The staff of the Royal Air Cambodge and the personnel of Pochentong Airport, as well as Cambodian friends, were very concerned about Zhao Xiaoshou and gave him warm help in returning to his motherland.
    When Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou and others returned to Guangzhou by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd, Wang Lanxi, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Ji Jinzhang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and responsible persons from relevant departments went to the airport to welcome and express their regards. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou and others to pay their respects.
    Huang Yuping, a representative of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs who made a special trip from Beijing to Guangzhou to greet them, also went to the airport to welcome and express his regards. (Attached pictures)

### 向同印尼武装部队和暴徒进行英勇斗争的同志致敬  赵小寿等同志抵京受到热烈欢迎

1966-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    向同印尼武装部队和暴徒进行英勇斗争的同志致敬
    赵小寿等同志抵京受到热烈欢迎
    新华社二十三日讯　英勇保护我国国旗不受印度尼西亚右派军人政权组织的暴徒侮辱而光荣负伤的我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆工作人员赵小寿同志，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    赵小寿同志是在四月十五日上午，印度尼西亚武装部队人员伙同暴徒野蛮袭击我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆时，为了保护中华人民共和国庄严的五星国旗不受暴徒侮辱，而被枪击胸部受重伤的。
    同机回到北京的，还有我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆经济参赞邢路，以及大使馆工作人员和我国在印度尼西亚的一批经济建设专家。他们也在四月十五日，参加了保卫我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆的英勇斗争。
    今天前往机场欢迎赵小寿等同志的，有国务院秘书长周荣鑫，外交部副部长韩念龙，华侨事务委员会副主任方方，对外经委副主任杨琳，对外文委副主任史怀璧，其他方面的负责人王屏、杜毓沄、李伍、沈兹九、邵乃奋、张德华、何林，以及暂时撤离雅加达回国的新华分社的工作人员。
    现在北京的我国驻印度尼西亚大使姚仲明也到机场迎接和慰问。
    赵小寿同志用担架从飞机上抬下来以后，周荣鑫等在担架旁向赵小寿同志表示亲切慰问。女青年向赵小寿献了花。周荣鑫等负责人还向同时回到北京的其他同志，表示亲切慰问。这时，到机场欢迎的数百名机关工作人员热烈鼓掌，向同印度尼西亚武装部队人员和暴徒进行了英勇斗争的全体同志致敬。
    赵小寿等同志在返国途中于二十二日上午到达金边。赵小寿在机场受到中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮和使馆工作人员的欢迎和照顾。到机场欢迎和照顾赵小寿的还有居住在柬埔寨的华侨同胞和医务人员。柬埔寨王家航空公司的工作人员和坡成东机场的人员以及柬埔寨朋友，都非常关心赵小寿，对他返回祖国给予了热情的帮助。
    赵小寿等同志二十二日下午乘飞机回到广州时，广东省副省长王阑西，广州市副市长纪锦章，以及有关方面负责人，前往机场欢迎和慰问。一群少先队员向赵小寿等同志献花致敬。
    专程从北京前往广州迎接的外交部代表黄玉平也到机场欢迎和慰问。（附图片）
