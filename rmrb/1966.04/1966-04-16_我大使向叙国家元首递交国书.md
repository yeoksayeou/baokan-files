Title: My Ambassador Presents Credentials to Syrian Head of State
Author:
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 14th: Chen Tan, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of China to the Arab Syrian Republic, presented his credentials to the Syrian Head of State, Nur al-Din al-Atassi, here at noon on the 14th.

    When presenting his credentials, Ambassador Chen Tan conveyed Chairman Liu Shaoqi's greetings to Head of State al-Atassi and the Syrian people.

    In his eulogy, Ambassador Chen Tan expressed his heartfelt congratulations to the Syrian people on the achievements they have made in safeguarding national independence and developing the national economy.

    The Ambassador said that there is a deep friendship between China and Syria. The governments and peoples of the two countries have consistently sympathized with and supported each other in the common struggle against imperialism, to defend national independence, and to build their countries. He said that the development of friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries not only conforms to the interests of the two peoples but also benefits the common cause of Asian and African peoples in uniting against imperialism and defending world peace.

    In his reply, Head of State al-Atassi conveyed his greetings to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the Chinese people. He said that the deep friendship between the peoples of China and Syria has been consolidated and developed in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism. The revolutionary struggle of the Asian and African peoples for liberation, independence, and unity is a powerful force that will shatter the invaders and exploiters. He said that the great Chinese people's brilliant victories in their own country have made outstanding contributions to the struggles of the people of all countries in the world.

### 我大使向叙国家元首递交国书

1966-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    我大使向叙国家元首递交国书
    据新华社大马士革十四日电　新任中国驻阿拉伯叙利亚共和国特命全权大使陈坦十四日中午在这里向叙利亚国家元首努尔丁·阿塔西递交国书。
    陈坦大使在递交国书时向国家元首阿塔西转达了刘少奇主席对他和叙利亚人民的问候。
    陈坦大使在致颂词时对叙利亚人民在维护民族独立和发展民族经济方面所取得的成就表示衷心的祝贺。
    大使说，中国和叙利亚之间存在着深厚的友谊。两国政府和人民在反对帝国主义、保卫民族独立和建设国家的共同斗争中一贯相互同情和支持。他说，两国之间友好合作关系的发展，不仅符合两国人民的利益，而且也有利于亚非人民团结反帝和保卫世界和平的共同事业。
    国家元首阿塔西在致答词时向刘少奇主席和中国人民问候。他说，中国和叙利亚两国人民的深厚友谊在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中得到了巩固和发展。亚非人民争取解放、独立和统一的革命斗争是一支强大的力量，这支力量将粉碎侵略者和剥削者。他说，伟大的中国人民在自己的国家里取得的辉煌胜利，对世界各国人民的斗争作出了卓越的贡献。
