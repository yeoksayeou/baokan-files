Title: US Gives a Batch of Grain to the Ghana Coup Authorities; West German Government to Give Ghana a Sum of "Development Aid" Funds
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 2nd – The Ghana coup authorities and the United States signed an agreement on the morning of the 1st, under which the United States will give the Ghana coup authorities “surplus” agricultural products worth more than 2.6 million pounds.
    According to a press release issued by the Ghana coup authorities, the agricultural products provided by the United States include 25,000 metric tons of flour, 20,000 metric tons of rice, 5,000 metric tons of corn, as well as cotton, vegetable oil, tobacco, etc. Most of the agricultural products will be shipped to Ghana within the next six months.
    In addition, two U.S. Air Force planes transported the first batch of 25 tons out of 500 tons of condensed milk given by the United States to the Ghana coup authorities to Accra on March 30.

    Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, 1st – According to a report by the West German German News Agency, the West German government decided to provide the Ghana coup authorities with 40 million West German marks in "development aid" and send a government delegation to visit Ghana.

### 美国给加纳政变当局一批粮食  西德政府将给加纳一笔“发展援助”款项

1966-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    美国给加纳政变当局一批粮食
    西德政府将给加纳一笔“发展援助”款项
    新华社二日讯　阿克拉消息：加纳政变当局和美国一日上午签订一项协定，美国将给加纳政变当局价值两百六十多万英镑的“剩余”农产品。
    据加纳政变当局发表的一项新闻公报，在美国给的这批农产品中，包括面粉二万五千公吨、大米二万吨、玉米五千吨以及棉花、植物油、烟草等，大部分农产品将在最近六个月内运到加纳。
    此外，美国空军飞机两架三月三十日把美国给加纳政变当局的五百吨炼乳中的第一批二十五吨运到阿克拉。
    新华社波恩一日电　据西德德意志新闻社报道，西德政府决定向加纳政变当局提供四千万西德马克的“发展援助”，并且派遣一个政府代表团去加纳访问。
