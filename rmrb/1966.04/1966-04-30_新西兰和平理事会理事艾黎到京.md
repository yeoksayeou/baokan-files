Title: New Zealand Peace Council Member Alley Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th – Mr. Philip Alley, a member of the New Zealand Peace Council and a renowned soil mechanic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 27th at the invitation of the China Peace Committee. Zhu Ziqi, a standing committee member of the China Peace Committee, and Mr. and Mrs. Lake, New Zealand friends in Beijing, were at the airport to welcome him.

### 新西兰和平理事会理事艾黎到京

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰和平理事会理事艾黎到京
    新华社二十九日讯　新西兰和平理事会理事、著名土壤力学家菲力浦·艾黎，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问，二十七日晚乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国和委会常委朱子奇和在北京的新西兰朋友雷克夫妇等人。
