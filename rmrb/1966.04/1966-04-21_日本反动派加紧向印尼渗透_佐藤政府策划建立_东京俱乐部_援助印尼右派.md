Title: Japanese Reactionaries Intensify Infiltration into Indonesia; Sato Government Plans to Establish "Tokyo Club" to Aid Indonesian Right-Wingers
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th -- Tokyo newspapers reported on the 19th that Japanese Prime Minister Sato announced on the 18th that the Japanese government will "actively cooperate in all aspects" with the Indonesian government in the future. On the issue of "aid" to Indonesia, the Japanese government will "coordinate" with the United States.
    Sato made this decision after listening to a report on the 18th from the Japanese Ambassador to Indonesia, Saito Shizuo, who had recently returned to Japan. Sato said: The Japanese government "has a full understanding of the Indonesian situation and is willing to actively assist."
    According to the *Tokyo Shimbun*, the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs revealed that the Sato government has proposed to relevant countries to establish a "Tokyo Club" to create an "international cooperation system" to clarify the things that Japan and the United States and other countries need to do, in order to intensify "aid" to the Indonesian right-wing military regime.
    The Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs also revealed that the Sato government is actively working to enable Indonesia to return to the United Nations when the United Nations General Assembly convenes in September this year. The Sato government will also mediate the dispute between Indonesia and "Malaysia".

### 日本反动派加紧向印尼渗透  佐藤政府策划建立“东京俱乐部”援助印尼右派

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    日本反动派加紧向印尼渗透
    佐藤政府策划建立“东京俱乐部”援助印尼右派
    新华社东京十九日电　据东京报纸十九日报道，日本首相佐藤十八日宣布，日本政府今后将同印度尼西亚政府“在一切方面积极合作”，在“援助”印度尼西亚问题上，日本政府将和美国“协调”一致。
    佐藤是在十八日听取了最近回国的日本驻印度尼西亚大使斋藤镇男的报告后，作出这个决定的。佐藤说：日本政府“对于印度尼西亚形势，因为有充分的理解，愿意积极地加以协助”。
    据《东京新闻》报道，日本外务省（外交部）透露，佐藤政府向有关国家提议成立“东京俱乐部”，建立一个“国际合作体制”，把日本和美国等国需要作的事情都明确起来，以便加紧进行对印度尼西亚右派军人政权的“援助”。
    日本外务省还透露，佐藤政府为使印度尼西亚能够在今年九月联合国大会开会时回到联合国，正在积极活动。佐藤政府还要出面调停印度尼西亚同“马来西亚”的纠纷。
