Title: Wang Huaizhi
Author: Wang Huaizhi
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 3

    Since January of this year, a large number of cadres and youths in Albania have voluntarily gone to the forefront of agricultural production. Cherishing the lofty aspiration of building a new socialist countryside, they appear everywhere as ordinary laborers, greatly promoting production, strengthening ties with the masses, and receiving a warm welcome from the peasants. The picture shows the cadres who have been sent to the countryside in the Tirana region participating in labor. Photo by Wang Huaizhi (Xinhua News Agency draft)

专栏：

    今年一月以来，阿尔巴尼亚大批干部和青年，自愿奔赴农业生产第一线。他们胸怀建设社会主义新农村的雄心壮志，处处以普通劳动者的姿态出现，大大促进了生产，密切了群众关系，受到了农民的热烈欢迎。图为地拉那区的下放干部在参加劳动的情形。　王怀智摄（新华社稿）
