Title: Deputy Director of the State Science and Technology Commission Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 14th. Zhao Jichang, Deputy Director of the Scientific and Technological Commission, hosted a banquet at noon today to warmly welcome the scientific and technological delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Nguyen Xuan Lam, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry of Vietnam.
    Zhao Jichang and Nguyen Xuan Lam spoke at the banquet. They wished the fighting friendship between the people of China and Vietnam and the scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries would be further developed.
    Among those present at the banquet were Lu Xianlin, Pan Shuren, Zhang Xianwen and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Su An, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, was invited to attend the banquet. The Vietnamese government's science and technology delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon.

### 我科委副主任宴请越南客人

1966-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    我科委副主任宴请越南客人
    新华社十四日讯　科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌今天中午举行宴会，热烈欢迎由越南重工业部副部长阮春林率领的越南民主共和国政府科学技术代表团。
    赵继昌和阮春林在宴会上先后讲话。他们祝愿中越两国人民的战斗友谊和两国科学技术合作进一步发展。
    出席宴会的，有陆献琳、潘树人、张宪文等有关方面负责人。
    越南驻中国大使馆参赞苏安应邀出席了宴会。越南政府科学技术代表团是在昨天下午到达北京的。
