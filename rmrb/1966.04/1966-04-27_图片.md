    On April 26, Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, Djawoto, and the representatives of various countries attending the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association. He also received the Lao Patriotic Forces news delegation and the students of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Training Class. The picture shows a group photo during the meeting.
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong photo
