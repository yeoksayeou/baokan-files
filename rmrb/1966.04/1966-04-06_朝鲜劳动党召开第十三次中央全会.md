Title: The Korean Workers' Party Convenes the Thirteenth Plenary Session of the Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 5th (via dispatch) – According to reports in the local newspapers today, April 5th, the Fourth Central Committee of the Korean Workers’ Party held its thirteenth plenary session from March 28th to April 4th, presided over by Comrade Kim Il-sung.
    The plenary session discussed the following agenda:
    1. Summary of the implementation of the decisions made at the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Fourth Central Committee of the Party;
    2. On convening the Party Representative Conference.
    The plenary session proposed that in order to manage national life thriftily, promote socialist construction, and further improve people’s lives, the decisions of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Fourth Central Committee of the Party will be implemented more thoroughly.
    The plenary session decided that the Party Representative Conference will be held in September or October of 1966.

### 朝鲜劳动党召开第十三次中央全会

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党召开第十三次中央全会
    新华社平壤五日电　据这里的报纸五日报道，从三月二十八日到四月四日，朝鲜劳动党第四届中央委员会在金日成同志主持下举行了第十三次全体会议。
    全体会议讨论了下列议程：
    1、关于第四届党中央委员会第十二次会议决定执行情况的总结；
    2、关于召开党代表会议。
    全体会议提出，为了勤俭地管理好国家生活，促进社会主义建设，为了进一步提高人民生活，将更加彻底地贯彻第四届党中央委员会第十二次全体会议的决定。
    全体会议决定，党代表会议将于一九六六年九月或十月召开。
