Title: Two Journalists from Congo (Brazzaville) Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 3

    Two Journalists from Congo (Brazzaville) Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. Mbassi-Moba, news commissioner of the youth organization of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville) and editor of "Voice of the Congolese Revolution" radio station, and Henri Bongui, broadcaster of "Voice of the Congolese Revolution" radio station, arrived in Beijing by plane today at our invitation to visit China.

1966-04-11

    刚果（布）两位新闻工作者到京
