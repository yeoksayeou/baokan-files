Title: U.S. Imperialism's War Actions Will Escalate Another Level If They Fail, and Will Escalate Again: The U.S. Brazenly Sends B-52 Aircraft to Indiscriminately Bomb North Vietnam; The Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Solemnly Declares Resolute Crushing of U.S. New Adventure
Author:

    U.S. Imperialism's War Actions Will Escalate Another Level If They Fail, and Will Escalate Again
    The U.S. Brazenly Sends B-52 Aircraft to Indiscriminately Bomb North Vietnam
    The Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Solemnly Declares Resolute Crushing of U.S. New Adventure
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 13th (Electric Dispatch) A spokesman for the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 13th, severely condemning U.S. imperialism for using B-52 strategic bombers to bomb the territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on April 12th.
    The statement pointed out that the use of B-52 strategic bombers by the U.S. aggressors to bomb North Vietnam is an extremely serious new "escalation" in the U.S.'s destructive war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam using air power.
    The statement said that at present, U.S. imperialism is suffering heavy defeats and is in a very difficult situation in the war of aggression against Vietnam, both in the South and the North. Faced with this situation, U.S. imperialism, in order to get out of this predicament, has blatantly used B-52 strategic bombers to intensify the bombing of North Vietnam, brazenly taking another step forward in its military adventures. This act of aggression infringes upon the sovereignty, territory, and security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and tramples on the Geneva Accords of 1954 on the Vietnam issue and the most basic norms of international law. It is also an unforgivable provocation against world public opinion which loves peace and upholds justice. It is clear that U.S. imperialism still intends to continue down the road of intensifying and expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam. The statement pointed out that no matter how rampant the U.S. aggressors are, they cannot threaten the spirit of the Vietnamese people, but will only make the Vietnamese people hate them more and be determined to defeat them. The Vietnamese people earnestly appeal to the people and governments of all countries in the world who love peace to persist in the struggle, and promptly stop the actions of the U.S. war criminals, so as to prevent them from committing new crimes by using B-52 strategic bombers to bomb North Vietnam again.
    The statement said that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam severely condemns and denounces the above-mentioned war actions of the United States, and resolutely demands that the U.S. government must permanently and unconditionally stop the bombing and all other war actions against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, stop the war of aggression against South Vietnam, withdraw all U.S. and its followers' troops and weapons from South Vietnam, and respect and solemnly implement the 1954 Geneva Accords on the Vietnam issue. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sternly warns the U.S. government that it must bear full responsibility for all the consequences arising therefrom.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 13th (Electric Dispatch) According to the Vietnam News Agency, Colonel Ha Van Lau, head of the liaison delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, sent an urgent telegram to the chairman of the International Commission for Vietnam on the 12th, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism for using B-52 aircraft to bomb the territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The telegram said that this is an extremely serious act of aggression and a new escalation of U.S. imperialism's gradual expansion of the destructive war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This shows that U.S. imperialism is deliberately going down the road of strengthening and expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam. The telegram said that the U.S. government must bear full responsibility for all the serious consequences arising from this new war action.

### 美帝国主义战争行动再升一级失败也将再升一级  美国悍然出动B—52型飞机滥炸越南北方  越南外交部严正表示坚决粉碎美国新冒险

1966-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义战争行动再升一级失败也将再升一级
    美国悍然出动B—52型飞机滥炸越南北方
    越南外交部严正表示坚决粉碎美国新冒险
    新华社河内十三日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人十三日发表声明，严厉谴责美帝国主义四月十二日用B—52型战略轰炸机轰炸越南民主共和国领土。
    声明指出，美国侵略者使用B—52型战略轰炸机轰炸越南北方，是美国在用空军对越南民主共和国进行破坏性战争中的一次极其严重的新的“升级”。
    声明说，目前，美帝国主义在侵略越南的战争中，正在越南南方和北方遭到惨重失败，并且处境十分危困。面对这种情况，美帝国主义为了摆脱这一困境，竟然明目张胆地使用B—52型战略轰炸机，加强对越南北方的轰炸，悍然在它的军事冒险中又前进了一步。这一侵略行动，侵犯了越南民主共和国的主权、领土和安全，践踏了一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议和国际法的最起码准则。它也是对世界上爱好和平和主持公理的舆论的不可饶恕的挑衅。很显然，美帝国主义仍然蓄意在强化和扩大侵略越南的战争道路上走下去。声明指出，不论美国侵略者如何猖狂，它也威胁不了越南人民的精神，只会使越南人民更加仇恨它，并且决心打败它。越南人民恳切呼吁世界爱好和平的各国人民和政府，坚持斗争，及时制止美国战争罪犯的行动，不让它再用B—52型战略轰炸机轰炸越南北方而犯下新的罪行。
    声明说，越南民主共和国外交部严厉谴责和控诉美国的上述战争行动，坚决要求美国政府必须永远和无条件地停止对越南民主共和国的轰炸和其他一切战争行动，停止对越南南方的侵略战争，把美国及其仆从的军队和武器全部从越南南方撤走，尊重和严正履行一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议。越南民主共和国外交部严厉警告美国政府，它必须对为此而引起的一切后果承担全部责任。
  据新华社河内十三日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长何文楼大校，十二日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，强烈谴责美帝国主义使用B—52型飞机轰炸越南民主共和国领土。
    电报说，这是一种极其严重的侵略行动，是美帝国主义逐步扩大对越南民主共和国的破坏性战争的新的升级。这表明，美帝国主义蓄意要沿着加强和扩大侵越战争的道路走下去。电报说，美国政府必须对这一新的战争行动所产生的一切严重后果负全部责任。
