Title: Romanian Military Officers Arrive in Beijing for Vacation in China
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: A group of sixteen Romanian People's Army officers and their wives, led by Lieutenant General Niculescu Marin, Commander of Artillery of the Romanian People's Army, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for vacation in China.

### 来我国休假的罗马尼亚军官到京

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    来我国休假的罗马尼亚军官到京
    据新华社二十九日讯　由罗马尼亚人民军炮兵司令尼考列斯库·马林中将和夫人率领来中国休假的罗马尼亚人民军军官和他们的夫人一行十六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
