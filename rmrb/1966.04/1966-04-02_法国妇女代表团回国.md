Title: French Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency: The French Women's Delegation, led by Madame Hélène de Lipkowski, President of the International Liaison Committee of French Women's Organizations, left Beijing by plane on the morning of March 25 to return home after concluding its visit to our country. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Yun, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation, as well as Luo Shuzhang, Ding Xilin, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 法国妇女代表团回国

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    法国妇女代表团回国
    据新华社讯　由法国妇女团体国际联络委员会主席伊兰·德·李普考斯基夫人率领的法国妇女代表团一行五人，结束在我国的访问后，三月二十五日上午乘飞机离京回国。到机场欢送的，有全国妇联副主席章蕴，以及有关方面负责人罗叔章、丁西林等。
