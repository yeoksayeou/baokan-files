Title: Albanian “Dinamo” Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: The Albanian Ministry of Interior "Dinamo" basketball delegation, consisting of sixteen people led by Lieutenant Colonel Neshtet Take, has arrived in Beijing to participate in the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations, which will soon be held in Beijing.
    Upon the arrival of the Albanian "Dinamo" basketball team, they were warmly welcomed by Ma Xingwu, Director of the Organizing Committee of the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations and Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Public Security Forces, and Mu Zuoyun, Deputy Director of the Ball Games Department of the State Sports Commission.
    Meleqi Babani, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Albanian Embassy in China, and Colonel Feliq Hatu, Military Attaché, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 阿“狄纳莫”男子篮球代表队到京

1966-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    阿“狄纳莫”男子篮球代表队到京
    新华社十日讯  参加即将在北京举行的社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛的阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”篮球代表队一行十六人，在内斯德特·塔凯中校率领下已经到达北京。
    阿尔巴尼亚“狄纳莫”篮球队到达时，受到社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会组织委员会主任、中国人民公安部队政治部副主任马星五，国家体委球类司副司长牟作云等的热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼、武官费利克·哈图上校也到机场迎接。
