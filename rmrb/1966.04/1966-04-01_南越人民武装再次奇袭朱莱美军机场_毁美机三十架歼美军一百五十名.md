Title: South Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Again Raid Chu Lai US Airbase, Destroying 30 US Aircraft and Eliminating 150 US Troops
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 31st - According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Armed Forces of Quang Nam Province, South Vietnam, raided the Chu Lai US Airbase on the night of March 23rd. Preliminary reports indicate that the Liberation Armed Forces eliminated 150 US aggressor troops, destroyed or burned 30 enemy jet aircraft, and also destroyed many enemy bunkers.
    This is the second raid by the Liberation Armed Forces on the US military lair in Chu Lai. On October 27th of last year, the Liberation Armed Forces eliminated more than 300 US aggressor troops and destroyed 57 US aircraft during a raid on this airbase.
    Xinhua News Agency, Saigon, March 30th - Saigon news: The South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces used bombs to blow up a US Air Force officers' dormitory near the Tan Son Nhut Airport on the early morning of March 30th.
    According to preliminary reports from the Associated Press, some US aggressors were sleeping in this two-story dormitory when the bomb exploded, and four or five of them were injured.
    On the same day one year ago, the Liberation Armed Forces blew up the US "Embassy" in Saigon.

### 南越人民武装再次奇袭朱莱美军机场  毁美机三十架歼美军一百五十名

1966-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    南越人民武装再次奇袭朱莱美军机场
    毁美机三十架歼美军一百五十名
    新华社河内三十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南省解放武装力量三月二十三日夜晚奇袭朱莱美军机场。据初步消息，解放武装力量歼灭美国侵略军一百五十名，击毁或烧毁敌喷气式飞机三十架，还炸毁敌人许多碉堡。
    这是解放武装力量对朱莱的美军巢穴的第二次奇袭。去年十月二十七日，解放武装力量在奇袭这个机场时，曾歼灭美国侵略军三百多名，击毁美机五十七架。
    新华社三十日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量三十日清晨用炸弹爆炸了新山一机场附近的一所美国空军军官宿舍。
    据美联社初步透露，炸弹爆炸时，一些美国侵略者正在这所二层楼的宿舍中睡觉，其中有四、五人被炸伤。
    在一年前的同一天，解放武装力量爆炸了美国驻西贡“大使馆”。
