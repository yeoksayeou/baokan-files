Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Leaves for Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. A seven-member delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, led by Vice President Pei Lisheng, left Beijing by plane today for Albania. The delegation will represent the Chinese Academy of Sciences in discussing and signing the 1966-1967 Executive Plan for Scientific Cooperation between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Albanian State University of Tirana with the Albanian State University of Tirana. Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Zhu Kezhen, Shi Huaibi, Zhao Jichang, and Qin Lisheng, were present at the airport to see them off.
    Meleqi Babani, Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 我科学院代表团去阿尔巴尼亚

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    我科学院代表团去阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社二十日讯　由中国科学院副院长裴丽生率领的中国科学院代表团一行七人，今天乘飞机离开北京赴阿尔巴尼亚。代表团将代表中国科学院同阿尔巴尼亚国立地拉那大学商量签订中国科学院和阿尔巴尼亚国立地拉那大学科学合作一九六六至一九六七年执行计划。前往机场送行的有有关部门负责人竺可桢、史怀璧、赵继昌、秦力生等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆参赞梅莱契·巴巴尼也到机场送行。
