Title: Director of the Cultural Affairs Department of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Erling Kristoffersen, Director of the Cultural Affairs Department of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of Song Yiping, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 挪威外交部文化司司长到京

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威外交部文化司司长到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应对外文委副主任宋一平邀请来我国访问的挪威外交部文化司司长尔林·克里斯托费生，今天乘飞机到达北京。
