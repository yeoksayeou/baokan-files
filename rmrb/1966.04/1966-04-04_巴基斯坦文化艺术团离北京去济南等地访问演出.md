Title: Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for Visits and Performances in Jinan and Other Places
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st -- The Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Beijing and left Beijing by train this morning for visits and performances in Jinan, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places.
    Those who went to the station to see off the Pakistani guests included Yang Chun, Vice-Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhou Weizhi, Xu Jingwu, Zhang Mingyang, Dai Ailian, Wang Yuanfang, Wang Xiaowei, and other responsible persons from relevant departments; as well as figures from the capital's literary and art circles. Pakistani Chargé d'affaires ad interim to China, Durrani, and his wife also saw them off at the station.

### 巴基斯坦文化艺术团离北京去济南等地访问演出

1966-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦文化艺术团离北京去济南等地访问演出
    新华社一日讯　巴基斯坦文化艺术团结束了在北京的访问演出，今天上午乘火车离开北京，前往济南、上海、杭州、广州等地访问演出。
    到车站欢送巴基斯坦客人的，有对外文委副主任杨纯，有关方面负责人周巍峙、徐净武、张明养、戴爱莲、王元方、汪小为等，以及首都文艺界人士。巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办杜拉尼和夫人也到车站送行。
