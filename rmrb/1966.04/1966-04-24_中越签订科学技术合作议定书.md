Title: China and Vietnam Sign Protocol on Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: A protocol of the sixth meeting of the executive bodies for scientific and technological cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam was signed this afternoon.
    Vice Premier Bo Yibo attended the signing ceremony. Representing the two sides in signing were Zhao Jichang, Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission on the Chinese side, and Nguyen Xuan Lam, head of the Vietnamese government's scientific and technological delegation and Vice Minister of Heavy Industry of Vietnam, on the Vietnamese side.
    Attending the signing ceremony were Fan Changjiang, Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission, Dong Xuelin, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. Tran Tu Binh, Vietnamese Ambassador to China, and all members of the Vietnamese government's scientific and technological delegation also attended the signing ceremony.
    After the signing, Zhao Jichang hosted a banquet for the Vietnamese comrades.

### 中越签订科学技术合作议定书

1966-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中越签订科学技术合作议定书
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国科学技术合作执行机构第六次会议议定书，今天下午签字。
    薄一波副总理出席了签字仪式。代表双方签字的，中国方面是科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌；越南方面是越南政府科学技术代表团团长、越南重工业部副部长阮春林。
    参加签字仪式的，有科学技术委员会副主任范长江、外交部第二亚洲司副司长董学林等。越南驻中国大使陈子平和越南政府科学技术代表团全体成员，也参加了签字仪式。
    签字以后，赵继昌设宴招待越南同志们。
