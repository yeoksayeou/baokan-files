Title: Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations, Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament Continues

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 16th: The Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Public Security Sports Organizations of Socialist Countries continued this evening at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium.
    The Albanian team and the Romanian team defeated the Czechoslovakian team and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea team with scores of 68-56 and 72-68 respectively.
    The first match this evening, Albania vs. Czechoslovakia, was very exciting. The Albanian team won 68-56.
    The match between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Romania was also closely contested this evening. Finally, the Romanian team won 72-68.
    The lineup for the matches continuing at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium on the afternoon and evening of the 17th is: Bulgaria vs. China; Czechoslovakia vs. Romania; Poland vs. Albania.

### 社会主义国家公安体育组织  第七届男子篮球比赛继续进行

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家公安体育组织
    第七届男子篮球比赛继续进行
    据新华社十六日讯　社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛，今晚在北京工人体育馆继续进行。
    阿尔巴尼亚队和罗马尼亚队分别以六十八比五十六、七十二比六十八胜捷克斯洛伐克队和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队。
    今晚的第一场阿尔巴尼亚队对捷克斯洛伐克队的比赛，打得十分精采。阿尔巴尼亚队以六十八比五十六取胜。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队同罗马尼亚队的比赛，今晚也打得难解难分。最后罗马尼亚队以七十二比六十八获胜。
    十七日下午和晚上将在北京工人体育馆继续进行比赛的阵容是：保加利亚队对中国队；捷克斯洛伐克队对罗马尼亚队；波兰队对阿尔巴尼亚队。
