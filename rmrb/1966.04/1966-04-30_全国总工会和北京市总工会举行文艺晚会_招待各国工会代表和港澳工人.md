Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Hold a Cultural Evening to Entertain Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries and Hong Kong and Macao Workers
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions jointly held a cultural evening tonight to entertain trade union delegations and representatives from more than 30 countries and regions in Asia, Africa, Latin America, Europe, and Oceania, as well as Hong Kong and Macao workers who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations. The large-scale ballet "The Red Detachment of Women" was performed at the evening.
    Among those who accompanied the guests were Zhu Xuefan, Ma Chungu, and Kang Yonghe, vice chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Wang Yingpu, vice chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. Model workers from the industrial front in the capital also watched the performance with international friends.

### 全国总工会和北京市总工会举行文艺晚会  招待各国工会代表和港澳工人

1966-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会和北京市总工会举行文艺晚会
    招待各国工会代表和港澳工人
    新华社二十九日讯　中华全国总工会和北京市总工会，今晚联合举行文艺晚会，招待来自亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、欧洲、大洋洲的三十多个国家和地区的工会代表团和代表，以及来北京参加“五一”国际劳动节的港澳工人。晚会上演出了大型芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
    陪同观看演出的有，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、马纯古、康永和，以及北京市总工会副主席王瑛璞等。首都工业战线上的劳动模范也同国际友人一起观看了演出。
