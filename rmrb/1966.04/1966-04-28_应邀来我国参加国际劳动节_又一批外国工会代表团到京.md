Title: Another Group of Foreign Trade Union Delegations Arrives in Beijing to Attend International Labor Day Celebrations in China

Author:

Date: 1966-04-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. A Romanian trade union delegation led by Benescu Mihail, member of the Presidium of the Central Council of Romanian Trade Unions and Chairman of the Romanian Construction and Building Materials Industry Trade Union; a Revolutionary Cuban Workers' Central Trade Union delegation led by Agapito Figueroa, General Secretary of the Cuban Metallurgical Trade Union; a Pakistani trade union delegation led by Sonnamat, Chairman of the East Pakistan Regional Trade Union of the Pakistan Workers' Federation; a delegation of the Palestinian Trade Union Federation led by Fa. Fida, Executive Committee member of the Palestinian Trade Union Federation and Secretary of the Kuwait Branch of the Palestinian Trade Union Federation; a delegation of the National Workers' Union of Mali led by Mamadou Keita, member of the Council of the National Workers' Union of Mali and Deputy Secretary-General of the National Metallurgical Trade Union of Mali; an Argentinian Peronist Revolutionary Movement trade union delegation led by Abdala Barruchi, trade union secretary of the Central Committee of the Argentinian Peronist Revolutionary Movement; a delegation of the Chilean Private Enterprise Employees Federation led by Ernesto Lenon, Chairman of the Chilean Private Enterprise Employees Federation; a delegation of the United Workers' Central Union of Venezuela led by Horacio Scott Bovell, Chairman of the United Workers' Central Union of Venezuela; and a delegation of the Italian General Confederation of Labor led by Zini Domenico, Administrative Minister of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, arrived in Beijing by plane today. These foreign trade union delegations are invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to pay friendly visits. Zhu Xuefan and Kang Yonghe, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 应邀来我国参加国际劳动节  又一批外国工会代表团到京

1966-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加国际劳动节
    又一批外国工会代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　由罗马尼亚工会中央理事会主席团委员、罗马尼亚建筑和建筑材料工业工会主席贝内斯库·米哈伊尔率领的罗马尼亚工会代表团，由古巴冶金工会总书记阿加比多·费盖罗阿率领的革命古巴工人中央工会代表团，由巴基斯坦工人联合会东巴基斯坦地区工会主席宋纳玛特率领的巴基斯坦工会代表团，由巴勒斯坦工会联合会执行委员、巴勒斯坦工会联合会科威特分会书记法·费达率领的巴勒斯坦工会联合会代表团，由马里全国工人联合会理事、马里全国冶金工会副总书记马马杜·凯塔率领的马里全国工人联合会代表团，由阿根廷庇隆主义革命运动中央委员会工会书记阿布达拉·巴鲁契率领的阿根廷庇隆主义革命运动工会代表团，由智利全国私营企业雇员联合会主席厄尔内斯多·莱农率领的智利私营企业雇员联合会代表团，由委内瑞拉工人统一中央工会主席奥拉西奥·斯科特·鲍维尔率领的委内瑞拉工人统一中央工会代表团，由意大利总工会行政部长齐尼·多米尼科率领的意大利总工会代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。这些外国工会代表团是应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和进行友好访问的。到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、康永和等。
