Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Our "August 1st" Shooting Team Visits Romania for Competition
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 25) At the invitation of the "Star" Club of the Romanian Armed Forces Department, the "August 1st" Shooting Team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a group of twenty people, led by Ren Dacheng of the Military Training Department of the General Staff Department, flew to Bucharest today for a friendly visit and competition. Peng Shilu, head of the Military Training Department of the General Staff Department, and Tian Tao, head of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, saw them off at the airport.
    Colonel Solescu, Military Attaché of the Romanian Embassy in China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 我“八一”射击队去罗马尼亚访问比赛

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我“八一”射击队去罗马尼亚访问比赛
    新华社二十五日讯　应罗马尼亚武装部队部“星”俱乐部的邀请，中国人民解放军“八一”射击队一行二十人，在总参谋部军训部任大成率领下，今天乘飞机前往布加勒斯特进行友好访问比赛。到机场送行的有总参谋部军训部负责人彭施鲁，国防部外事局负责人田涛等。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使馆武官索列斯库上校也到机场送行。
