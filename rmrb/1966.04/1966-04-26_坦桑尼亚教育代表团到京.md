Title: Tanzanian Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th - A Tanzanian education delegation of twelve people, led by head A. Makame and deputy head Masrur Sad, arrived in Beijing by plane from Shanghai on the 24th. Minister of Education He Wei held a welcome banquet for the guests tonight. Tanzanian Ambassador to China Waziri Juma was also invited to attend.
    The delegation arrived in Shanghai by plane on the 20th.

### 坦桑尼亚教育代表团到京

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚教育代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　由团长阿·马卡梅、副团长马斯鲁尔·萨德率领的坦桑尼亚教育代表团一行十二人，二十四日从上海乘飞机到达北京。教育部部长何伟今晚为客人举行了欢迎宴会。坦桑尼亚驻华大使瓦齐里·朱马也应邀出席。
    代表团是在二十日乘飞机到达上海的。
