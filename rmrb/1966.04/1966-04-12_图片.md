Title: Liu Zuowen
Author: Liu Zuowen
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 4

    Thousands of workers in Bamako, the capital of Mali, demonstrated to protest against the US imperialist aggression against Vietnam and the Congo (Leopoldville). They raised their arms and shouted "Down with US imperialism!" The sign in the picture reads: "No to US imperialism!"
      Photo by Liu Zuowen (Xinhua News Agency draft)
