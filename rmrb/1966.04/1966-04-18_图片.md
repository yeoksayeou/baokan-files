Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and His Wife Arrive in Burma for Friendly Visit
Author: Dù Xiūxián
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 3

    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife arrived in Burma on the 17th for a friendly visit. The picture shows the citizens of Rangoon waving Chinese and Burmese flags, warmly welcoming Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife at the airport.
                      Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian (Fax photo)
