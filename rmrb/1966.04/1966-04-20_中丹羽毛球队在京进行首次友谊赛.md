Title: China-Denmark Badminton Teams Hold First Friendly Match in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-20
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency on the 19th, when the Chinese men's and women's badminton team No. 1 met the Danish men's and women's badminton team at the Beijing Gymnasium tonight, they won all five games of men's and women's singles, men's and women's doubles, and mixed doubles.

    Approximately 6,000 people watched the brilliant performances of the badminton stars from both countries.

    The Chinese badminton players' unique style of "fast, accurate, ruthless, and agile" and the Danish players' powerful backhand smash techniques repeatedly won warm applause and praise from the audience.

    In the five games, the Chinese players won four games with a score of 2-0, and only lost one game in the mixed doubles.

    The Danish men's and women's badminton teams will play a friendly match with the Chinese men's and women's badminton team No. 2 on the evening of the 20th.

### 中丹羽毛球队在京进行首次友谊赛

1966-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    中丹羽毛球队在京进行首次友谊赛
    据新华社十九日讯　中国男女羽毛球一队今晚在北京体育馆迎战丹麦男女羽毛球队时，在男女单打、男女双打和混合双打共五盘比赛中，获得全胜。
    约有六千人观看了两国羽毛球名将们的精采表演。
    我国羽毛球选手“快、狠、准、活”的独特风格和丹麦选手强有力的反手扣球技巧，一再博得观众的热烈掌声和赞扬。
    在五盘比赛中，我国选手有四盘以二比○获胜，只有混合双打曾经输了一局。
    丹麦男女羽毛球队二十日晚上将同中国男女羽毛球二队进行友谊比赛。
