Title: Delegation of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of China Leaves Japan for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Our Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee Delegation Leaves Japan for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo) The delegation of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of China, led by Yang Shuo, concluded its friendly visit to Japan and left Tokyo by plane on the 6th to return home, carrying with them the profound friendship of the Japanese people.
    At noon on the 5th, Hakamada Satomi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Nishizawa Tomio, alternate member of the Political Bureau, and Ii Yashiro, Okamoto Hiroyuki, and Kaneko Mitsuhiro, deputy director of the United Front Work Department, members of the Secretariat, received all members of the delegation and had a cordial conversation.

### 我亚非团结委员会代表团离日回国

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会代表团离日回国
    据新华社东京电　由杨朔率领的中国亚非团结委员会代表团结束了在日本的友好访问后，六日满载着日本人民的深情厚谊乘飞机离开东京回国。
    五日中午，日共中央政治局委员袴田里见，政治局候补委员西泽富夫，书记处书记伊井弥四郎、冈本博之和统战部副部长金子满广接见了代表团全体成员，并进行了亲切交谈。
