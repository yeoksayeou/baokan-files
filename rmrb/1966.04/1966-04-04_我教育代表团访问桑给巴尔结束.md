Title: My Education Delegation Concludes Visit to Zanzibar
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, 2nd. The Chinese Education Delegation, led by Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, left Zanzibar by plane on the morning of the 2nd for Dar es Salaam, the capital of Tanzania, after a two-day friendly visit to the island of Zanzibar.
    Among those who went to the airport to see off the delegation were Ali Sultan Issa, Minister of Education of Zanzibar, and other officials. Liu Gan, Chinese Consul in Zanzibar, also went to see them off. Karume, First Vice President of the United Republic of Tanzania, received the delegation on March 31 and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    The delegation visited six primary and secondary schools, attended the opening ceremony of a newly completed primary school, and visited the Zanzibar Youth Working Team at the Kama Farm, ten miles north of Zanzibar City.
    Members of the delegation held talks with representatives of the Zanzibar education community and visited Tawakkali Khamis, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Zanzibar, and Mtororo Rehani Kingo, Vice Chairman of the Afro-Shirazi Party and Mayor of Zanzibar City.
    The Minister of Education of Zanzibar held a banquet at noon on the 1st to entertain the members of the Chinese delegation.
    Liu Gan, Chinese Consul in Zanzibar, also held a reception on the evening of the 1st for the delegation's visit.

### 我教育代表团访问桑给巴尔结束

1966-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我教育代表团访问桑给巴尔结束
    新华社桑给巴尔二日电  由教育部副部长刘皑风率领的中国教育代表团在桑给巴尔岛进行了两天友好访问后，二日上午乘飞机离开这里前往坦桑尼亚首都达累斯萨拉姆。
    前往机场欢送代表团的有：桑给巴尔教育部长阿里·苏丹·伊萨和其他官员。中国驻桑给巴尔领事刘干也前往送行。坦桑尼亚联合共和国第一副总统卡鲁姆三月三十一日接见了代表团，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    代表团曾经参观了六所小学和中学，出席了一所新落成的小学开学仪式，访问了桑给巴尔市以北十英里的卡马农场的桑给巴尔青年工作队。
    代表团团员同桑给巴尔教育界代表进行了交谈，拜访了桑给巴尔外交部副部长塔瓦卡利·哈米斯和非洲—设拉子党副主席、桑给巴尔市长姆托罗·雷哈尼·金戈。
    桑给巴尔教育部长一日中午举行宴会，招待中国代表团团员。
    中国驻桑给巴尔领事刘干一日晚上也为代表团的访问举行了招待会。
