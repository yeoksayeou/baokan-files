Title: China-Africa Friendship Association Holds Tea Party to Celebrate the National Day of the United Republic of Tanzania
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The China-Africa People's Friendship Association held a tea party this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the United Republic of Tanzania.
    Ding Xilin, Vice President of the China-Africa Friendship Association, and Waziri Juma, Tanzanian Ambassador to China, spoke at the tea party. Ding Xilin said: The imperialists, headed by the United States, are by no means willing to give up their colonial rule in Africa. They are doing everything possible to carry out aggression, interference, infiltration and subversion in Africa. However, the awakened African people dare to wage a resolute struggle against imperialism and neo-colonialism.
    Ambassador Waziri Juma said: Imperialism and neo-colonialism want to overthrow Tanzania and subvert Africa, but they cannot eliminate our goal, which is the independence of all of Africa. In order to achieve this goal, we will fight until all colonialism is eliminated.
    The Tanzanian Education Delegation headed by A. Makame and Tanzanian experts and students studying in Beijing were invited to attend the tea party.
    Also present at the tea party were Song Changhua, Shen Zijiu, Zhang Xiaomei, Qian Dawei, Xu Lijin, Gong Dafei, Cao Shengong and other responsible persons from relevant departments. After the tea party, Tanzanian and Chinese films were shown.

### 庆祝坦桑尼亚联合共和国国庆  我中非友协举行茶会

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝坦桑尼亚联合共和国国庆
    我中非友协举行茶会
    新华社二十五日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会今天下午举行茶会，庆祝坦桑尼亚联合共和国国庆。
    中非友协副会长丁西林和坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马在茶会上讲话。丁西林说：以美国为首的帝国主义决不甘心放弃它在非洲的殖民统治，它们千方百计地对非洲进行侵略、干涉、渗透和颠覆活动。但是，觉醒了的非洲人民是敢于同帝国主义和新老殖民主义进行坚决斗争的。
    瓦齐里·朱马大使说：帝国主义和新老殖民主义想推翻坦桑尼亚和颠覆非洲，但是它们不能消除我们的目标，这个目标就是全非洲的独立。我们为实现这个目标，将斗争到把殖民主义全部清除掉。
    以阿·马卡梅为首的坦桑尼亚教育代表团和在京的坦桑尼亚专家、留学生应邀出席了茶会。
    出席茶会的还有宋常华、沈兹九、张晓梅、钱大卫、许力进、宫达非、曹胜功等有关方面负责人。茶会结束后，放映了坦桑尼亚和中国的影片。
