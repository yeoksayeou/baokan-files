Title: The Electric Welder's Wish
Author: Hao Xingwei
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 6

    The Electric Welder's Wish
    Hao Xingwei, Dalian Chemical Industrial Company
    Hands grasping the ladder to heaven,
    Feet treading on clouds,
    In the azure sky,
    Let those splendid flowers bloom.
    This flower I planted,
    Cannot be withered by the wind, cannot be defeated by the frost,
    The wilder the storms,
    The brighter the radiance shines beyond the horizon.
    Look, colorful streamers flutter everywhere in the motherland,
    How fast the revolutionary edifice grows,
    I am determined to let this welding torch of mine,
    Not weld a single steel beam crooked.
    [Selected from "Dahua Worker's News"]

### 电焊工的心愿
郝兴卫
1966-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    电焊工的心愿
    大连化学  工业公司  郝兴卫
    手攀着天梯，
    脚踏上云彩，
    在碧蓝的天空上，
    让那繁花盛开。
    我栽的这朵花呀，
    风吹不凋，霜打不败，
    越是风狂雨暴，
    越是光彩射天外。
    看，祖国到处飘彩絮，
    革命大厦长得有多快，
    我决心让我这焊把，
    不把一根钢梁焊歪。
    〔选自《大化职工报》〕
