Title: A Batch of Foreign Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, some foreign guests invited by relevant units in our country to participate in the "May 1st" celebration activities and to visit China have arrived in Beijing in recent days. They are: the Mexico-China Friendship Association delegation led by Juan Larios Tolentino; the Ceylon Freedom Party delegation composed of Loni de Mel and his wife; the Peruvian journalists delegation composed of Antonio Fernandez Arce, Carlos Ortega, Alberto Arrarte Villanueva, and Eduardo Justo; the Japanese local autonomy friendship delegation led by Daisuke Kawaguchi; the Seine Provincial Architects Association of France travel group led by Henri Desgouttes; the French National Student Union delegation; French lawyer Abbé Albert Mayard; Canadian Senator, Executive Director of HEC Montréal Cameron and his wife; and Hamilton, Director of the Canadian Wheat Board; Morapeli Gobeila, editor of the Basutoland "Makatile" weekly and representative of the Basutoland Congress Party in London; Alfonso Ospina Echeverri, a famous Colombian lawyer; and Miguel Tovar Silva, a member of the National Congress.

### 一批外国客人到京

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    一批外国客人到京
    据新华社讯　应我国各有关单位邀请来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动和访问的一些外国客人，于近日内先后到达北京。他们是：由胡安·拉里奥斯·托伦蒂诺率领的墨中友协代表团，由洛尼·德麦尔和夫人组成的锡兰自由党代表团，由安东尼奥·费尔南德斯·阿尔塞，卡洛斯·奥尔特加，阿尔贝托·阿拉约·比利亚努埃瓦和爱德华多·胡斯托组成的秘鲁新闻工作者代表团，由川口大助率领的日本地方自治友好代表团，由亨利·德赛戈内率领的法国塞纳省建筑师协会旅行团，法国全国学生联合会代表团，法国律师阿贝特·马雅尔，加拿大参议员、本夫高等经营管理学校执行董事喀麦隆和夫人以及加拿大谷物局局长汉弥尔顿，巴苏陀兰《马卡托勒》周刊编辑、巴苏陀兰大会党驻伦敦代表莫拉波·戈贝拉，哥伦比亚著名律师阿方索·奥斯皮纳·埃切维利奥和国会议员米格尔·托瓦尔·席尔瓦。
