Title: Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization: Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament Continues

Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Comprehensive report from our newspaper based on Xinhua News Agency: The basketball players from seven countries participating in the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Public Security Sports Organizations of Socialist Countries continued their competition on the 19th, with six countries participating. As a result of the competition, the Bulgarian "Spartak" team defeated the Albanian Ministry of Interior "Dinamo" team seventy-seven to sixty-three after forty minutes of fierce fighting.

    The Chinese "Qianwei" basketball team defeated the basketball team of the Sports Guidance Committee of the Ministry of Social Security of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea one hundred and twelve to ninety; the Romanian "Dinamo" basketball team defeated the Polish "Guards" basketball team seventy-three to sixty-six.

    On the 20th, the competition entered its fifth day, and the Romanian Bucharest "Dinamo" team defeated the Albanian Ministry of Interior "Dinamo" team eighty-four to sixty-seven that afternoon.

    On the evening of the 20th, the Chinese "Qianwei" basketball team and the Bulgarian "Spartak" basketball team defeated the Polish "Guards" basketball team and the Czechoslovak "Red Star" basketball team, respectively. The scores were ninety-two to seventy-four and seventy-five to sixty-nine, respectively.

    By the evening of the 20th, twelve of the twenty-one games in this men's basketball tournament had been completed. Except for the Romanian team, which has won all four games, the Chinese team has also maintained its undefeated record with three wins and three games. The Bulgarian team's record is three wins and one loss; the Polish team is one win and two losses; the Albanian team is one win and three losses; the Czechoslovak team and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea team are three losses in three games.

### 社会主义国家公安体育组织  第七届男子篮球赛继续举行

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家公安体育组织
    第七届男子篮球赛继续举行
    本报综合报道据新华社讯参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会的七个国家的篮球选手，十九日有六个国家继续参加了比赛。比赛结果，保加利亚“斯巴达克”队经过四十分钟的激战，以七十七比六十三赢了阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”队。
    中国“前卫”篮球队以一百一十二比九十战胜了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国社会安全省体育指导委员会篮球队；罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”篮球队以七十三比六十六赢了波兰“近卫军”篮球队。
    二十日，比赛进入第五天，罗马尼亚布加勒斯特“狄纳莫”队在当天下午以八十四比六十七战胜了阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”队。
    二十日晚，中国“前卫”篮球队和保加利亚“斯巴达克”篮球队，分别战胜了波兰“近卫军”篮球队和捷克斯洛伐克“红星”篮球队。比数分别是九十二比七十四和七十五比六十九。
    这届男子篮球赛到二十日晚为止，全部二十一场比赛已经进行了十二场。除罗马尼亚队已经四战四胜外，中国队也已三战三胜保持不败。保加利亚队的战绩是三胜一负；波兰队是一胜两负；阿尔巴尼亚队是一胜三负；捷克斯洛伐克队和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队是三战三负。
