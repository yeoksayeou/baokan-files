Title: Miyamoto Secretary-General Leads JCP Delegation Back to Tokyo After Concluding Visits to China, Vietnam, North Korea, etc.
Author:
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th: The delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by Miyamoto Kenji, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, returned to Tokyo by plane on the evening of the 4th after concluding its visits to the People's Republic of China, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included: Nosaka Sanzō, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and members of the Politburo Hakamada Satomi, Oka Masayoshi, and Kasuga Shoichi.
    Yang Shuo, head of the delegation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity currently visiting Japan, Chen Kang, representative of the Liaison Office of the Liao Chengzhi Office in Tokyo, and representatives of many Japanese labor unions and democratic organizations also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 结束对中越朝等国的访问  宫本总书记率日共代表团回到东京

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    结束对中越朝等国的访问
    宫本总书记率日共代表团回到东京
    新华社五日讯　东京消息：由日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治率领的日本共产党代表团结束了在中华人民共和国、越南民主共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问之后，四日晚上乘飞机回到东京。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有：日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三，政治局委员袴田里见、冈正芳和春日正一等。
    正在日本访问的中国亚非团结委员会代表团团长杨朔，廖承志办事处驻东京联络事务所代表陈抗以及日本许多工会和民主团体的代表们也到机场欢迎。
