Title: A Group of Foreign Guests Arrive in Beijing to Attend International Labor Day at the Invitation of Our Country

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    At the invitation of relevant units in our country, a group of foreign guests have arrived in our country to participate in "May Day" International Labor Day and to conduct friendly visits. Harry Lall, Chairman of the Guyana Agricultural Workers' Union, and Cyril Burke, Secretary of the Wharf Branch of the Guyana Sawmill and Forestry Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing on the 21st. A delegation of the General Union of Algerian Workers, headed by Mejiahel Abdelkader, Secretary-General of the Asnan Province Union of Algeria, and a delegation of the General Federation of Syrian Workers, headed by Marouan Sala, member of the General Federation of Syrian Workers' Unions and Chairman of the Homs Refinery Union, arrived in Beijing on the 23rd. Mamadou Sissoko, a teacher of natural sciences at the Mali Higher Normal School, arrived in Beijing on the 24th.
                      (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 应邀来我国参加国际劳动节  一批外国客人到京

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加国际劳动节
    一批外国客人到京
    应我各有关单位邀请，一批外国客人前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节，并进行友好访问活动。英属圭亚那农业工人工会主席哈里·拉尔和英属圭亚那锯木及林业工会码头分会书记西里尔·伯克已于二十一日到京。阿尔及利亚阿斯南省工会总书记梅贾海尔·阿卜代尔卡达尔为首的阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表团，以叙利亚工人工会总联合会成员、霍姆斯炼油厂工会主席马鲁昂·萨拉为首的叙利亚工会总联合会代表团，已于二十三日到京。马里高等师范学校自然科学教员玛玛杜·西斯，于二十四日到京。
                      （据新华社）
