Title: Chairman Ne Win Receives Ambassador Geng Biao
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 4th—Chairman Ne Win of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma received Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

### 奈温主席接见耿飚大使

1966-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温主席接见耿飚大使
    新华社仰光四日电　缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温四日下午接见了中国驻缅甸大使耿飚，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
