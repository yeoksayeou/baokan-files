Title: Foreign Friends in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 5

    Foreign Albanian comrades visited the "Rent Collection Courtyard" clay sculpture exhibition and were moved by the lifelike peasant sculptures. This set of clay sculptures depicts the cruel exploitation of the landlord class and the painful hatred of the peasants before liberation. It is a product of China's socialist cultural revolution and a great revolution in the field of sculpture.　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong photo

    At the Lugouqiao People's Commune Supply and Marketing Cooperative, a young Japanese woman was fascinated by the floral fabrics sold there. The prosperous scene of the market in Beijing's urban and rural areas left a deep impression on the Japanese friends.
            Xinhua News Agency reporter Guo Zhanying photo

    Tanzanian guests warmly congratulated the militia on their excellent results after watching the shooting performance of the Jiaozhuanghu militia. The Jiaozhuanghu militia has a glorious revolutionary tradition. During the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation, they opened up tunnels as long as 23 *li*, coordinated with the main force in combat, and won the honorary title of "People's First Fortress."
            Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Jiguo photo

    Chilean Congresswoman Carmen Lasso Carrera visited the Beijing Steel Wire Factory. She picked up an extremely thin wire, half the thickness of a hair, and said happily: This is simply like magic. The Beijing Steel Wire Factory was originally a small straw rope factory. Under the leadership of the Party, all the workers and staff dared to think and act, and produced iron-chromium-aluminum resistance elements that are currently only produced by a few countries in the world.		Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Guiyu photo

### 外国朋友在北京（图片）

1966-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    外国朋友在北京
    阿尔巴尼亚同志参观《收租院》泥塑群象展览，被栩栩如生的农民塑象所感动。这组泥塑作品表现了解放前地主阶级的残酷压榨和农民的痛苦仇恨。它是我国社会主义文化革命的产物，也是雕塑界的一次大革命。　　新华社记者　刘长忠摄
    在芦沟桥人民公社供销社，日本女青年对这里出售的花布爱不释手。北京城乡市场一片繁荣景象，给了日本朋友深刻的印象。
            新华社记者　郭占英摄
    坦桑尼亚客人参观焦庄户民兵射击表演后，热烈祝贺民兵们取得优秀成绩。焦庄户民兵具有光荣的革命传统，他们在抗日战争和解放战争时期曾开辟了长达二十三华里的地道，配合主力部队作战，曾获得“人民第一堡垒”的光荣称号。
            新华社记者　武基国摄
    智利众议员卡门·拉索·卡雷拉夫人在北京钢丝厂参观。她拿起一根相当于头发二分之一的极细丝，高兴地说：这简直和变魔术一样。北京钢丝厂原是一个打草绳的小厂。全厂职工在党的领导下，敢想敢干，生产出目前世界上只有少数国家能造的铁铬铝电阻元件。		新华社记者　张桂玉摄
