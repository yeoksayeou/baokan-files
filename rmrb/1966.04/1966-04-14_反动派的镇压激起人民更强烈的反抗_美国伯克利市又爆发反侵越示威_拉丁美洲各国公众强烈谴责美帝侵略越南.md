Title: Reactionary Suppression Provokes Stronger Resistance from the People; Anti-Vietnam War Demonstrations Erupt Again in Berkeley, USA; Public in Latin American Countries Strongly Condemns US Imperialist Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 13th -- Berkeley News: Approximately two thousand Americans held a rally in Berkeley, near San Francisco, on the evening of the 12th to protest against American aggression in Vietnam. At the rally site, paper models of US President Johnson and Vice President Humphrey were hung as if being executed, and another paper model of a uniformed soldier, symbolizing the US invading army in South Vietnam, was also hung. These scenes reflect the American people's deep hatred for the Johnson administration's insistence on the war of aggression in Vietnam and their firm opposition to this unjust war.
    This rally was organized by the Berkeley "Vietnam Day Committee." Three days earlier, the committee's headquarters had been damaged by a powerful bomb on the 9th by reactionaries. American reactionaries thought that by relying on terrorist methods, they could intimidate the American people who persisted in opposing the war of aggression against Vietnam. But the demonstration on the 12th shows that the situation developed in the opposite direction.
    The Berkeley residents' protest rally was held near the University of California campus. They held or carried thousands of placards and used loudspeakers to loudly promote their opposition to American aggression against Vietnam. When they hung up the paper models symbolizing the culprits and masterminds of the war of aggression against Vietnam, the crowd erupted in cheers.
    The Berkeley authorities dispatched more than one hundred military police to the scene, and these military police frantically wielded batons to beat the demonstrators. This aroused even greater anger from the demonstrators, who bravely engaged in hand-to-hand combat with the military police.
    Approximately one thousand demonstrators then marched to the city hall and prepared to enter it. In front of the city hall, they were blocked by a large number of police. The demonstrators then shouted slogans, expressing their firm determination to fight against the war of aggression against Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 13th -- Public opinion in some Latin American countries continues to oppose American aggression against Vietnam.
    Three hundred farmers from Rio Bravo, Tamaulipas, Mexico, and representatives from three neighboring regions held a demonstration march on the 11th, angrily protesting against American aggression against Vietnam. During the march, the demonstrators burned a puppet effigy of Uncle Sam. This demonstration was organized by the Central Union of Independent Farmers of Mexico. At a rally held before the march, people strongly condemned the US imperialist interventionists and demanded that they get out of Vietnam.
    Prior to this, a leader of the Revolutionary Workers' Union of Mexico published a statement in a newspaper saying that the American troops must get out of Vietnam and the Dominican Republic so that the people of these countries can freely choose their own future.
    The youth organization of the Costa Rican Socialist Party unanimously passed a resolution at its recent national congress, demanding that the Johnson administration withdraw its invading troops from Vietnam and expressing its full support for the heroic patriotic struggle of Vietnamese youth, workers, and students. The resolution said that the Vietnamese people provide an example of patriotism and heroism for youth around the world.
    In El Salvador, some university student organizations also recently held mass rallies in support of the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against American aggression. Some political figures and leaders of trade unions also attended the meeting. A leader of the April-May Revolutionary Party introduced the current heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people at the meeting. He emphasized that US imperialism must bear serious military, political, and moral responsibility for its aggression against the Vietnamese people who desire freedom.

### 反动派的镇压激起人民更强烈的反抗  美国伯克利市又爆发反侵越示威  拉丁美洲各国公众强烈谴责美帝侵略越南

1966-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

    反动派的镇压激起人民更强烈的反抗
    美国伯克利市又爆发反侵越示威
    拉丁美洲各国公众强烈谴责美帝侵略越南
    新华社十三日讯　伯克利消息：大约两千名美国人十二日晚上在旧金山附近的伯克利举行抗议美国侵略越南的大会。在集会的地点，美国总统约翰逊和副总统汉弗莱的纸制模型象处绞刑一样被吊在空中，另外一个象征美国在南越的侵略军的穿军服的纸制模型也被吊在空中。这些情景反映了美国人民对坚持侵越战争的约翰逊政府的深恶痛绝，对这场不义战争的坚决反对。
    这个集会是由伯克利的“越南日委员会”组织的。在三天前，这个委员会的总部曾在九日被反动分子用烈性炸弹炸坏。美国反动派以为凭借恐怖手段，就能够吓倒坚持反对侵越战争的美国人民。但是十二日的示威说明，事态的发展适得其反。
    伯克利居民的抗议集会是在加利福尼亚大学校园附近举行的。他们举着或拿着成千的标语牌，并且用扩音器大声宣传反对美国侵略越南的主张。当他们把象征侵越战争的元凶祸首的纸制模型吊起来的时候，群众发出一片欢呼声。
    伯克利当局出动了一百多名军警赶到现场，这些军警疯狂地挥舞警棍殴打示威者。这激起了示威者更大的愤怒，他们英勇地同军警展开了搏斗。
    大约一千名示威群众接着游行到市政厅，并且准备开到市政厅里面去。在市政厅面前，他们遭到大批警察的阻拦。示威者这时高呼口号，表示坚决要为反对侵越战争而斗争。
    新华社十三日讯　一些拉丁美洲国家公众继续反对美国侵略越南。
    墨西哥塔毛利帕斯州里奥布拉沃镇三百名农民以及来自三个邻近地区的代表十一日举行示威游行，愤怒抗议美国侵略越南。示威者在游行过程中烧毁了一个山姆大叔的傀儡象。这次示威是由墨西哥独立农民中央联盟组织的。在游行前举行的集会上，人们强烈谴责美帝国主义干涉者，并且要求他们从越南滚出去。
    在这以前，墨西哥革命工人联合会一位领导人在一家报纸上发表谈话说，美国军队必须从越南和多米尼加滚出去，以便这些国家的人民自由地选择他们自己的前途。
    哥斯达黎加社会党青年组织在最近举行的全国代表大会上一致通过一项决议，要求约翰逊政府从越南撤出它的侵略部队，并且表示完全支持越南青年、工人和学生的英勇爱国斗争。决议说，越南人民为全世界青年提供了爱国主义和英勇的榜样。
    在萨尔瓦多，一些大学生组织最近也举行群众集会，声援越南人民反对美国侵略的正义斗争。参加这次会议的还有一些政界人士和工会组织的领导人。四月五月革命党的一位领导人在会上介绍了越南人民目前的英勇斗争。他强调指出，美帝国主义必须对它向渴望自由的越南人民发动的侵略承担军事上、政治上和道义上的严重责任。
