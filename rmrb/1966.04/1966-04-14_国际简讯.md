　　International Briefs
　　Lebanese Prime Minister Forms New Cabinet
　　Beirut News: Lebanon formed a new cabinet on the 9th with Abdallah Yafi as Prime Minister.

　　In the new cabinet composed of ten members, Yafi concurrently serves as Minister of Finance and Minister of Information. Fadl Boutros is Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Education and National Defense, Philip Takla is Minister of Foreign Affairs, Pierre Gemayel is Minister of Interior, and Kamal Joumblatt is Minister of Public Works and Minister of Posts and Telecommunications.

　　The previous Karami cabinet resigned at the end of March. The Lebanese President appointed Yafi to organize a new cabinet on April 5th. Yafi had served as Prime Minister of Lebanon multiple times before 1956.
　　Costa Rica Extends Territorial Sea
　　San Jose News: The Costa Rican government recently requested the parliament to approve its accession to the "Santiago Declaration of Chile," thereby formally declaring the extension of its territorial sea to a range of 200 nautical miles from the coast. This measure was taken to prevent the plunder of marine resources off the coast of Costa Rica by American fishing boats.
　　U.S. Recognizes New Government of Ecuador
　　Quito News: The Johnson administration formally notified the new government of Ecuador on the 12th that "relations between the United States and Ecuador will continue," thereby formally recognizing the Yerovi Indaburu regime.
　　Yerovi Indaburu was recommended by the chiefs of the three armed forces of Ecuador and assumed the post of interim president on March 30th after the downfall of the pro-American dictatorship of Castro Jijón. On the 4th, he formed a nine-member cabinet, eight of whom represent the interests of industrial, commercial, and financial oligarchs.

### 国际简讯

1966-04-14
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    黎巴嫩总理组成新的内阁
    贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩九日组成以阿卜杜拉·雅非为总理的新内阁。
    在由十人组成的新内阁中，雅非兼任财政和新闻部长。法德·布特罗斯任副总理兼教育和国防部长，菲利普·塔克拉任外交部长，皮埃尔·杰马耶勒任内政部长，卡迈勒·琼卜拉特任公共工程和邮电部长。
    上届卡拉米内阁在三月底辞职。黎巴嫩总统在四月五日任命雅非组织新阁。雅非在一九五六年以前曾多次担任黎巴嫩的总理。
    哥斯达黎加扩大领海范围
    圣约瑟消息：哥斯达黎加政府最近要求议会批准它加入“智利圣地亚哥宣言”，从而正式宣布把本国的领海扩大到距海岸二百海里的范围。这一措施是为了防止美国渔船对哥斯达黎加沿岸海洋资源的掠夺而采取的。
    美国承认厄瓜多尔新政府
    基多消息：约翰逊政府十二日正式通知厄瓜多尔新政府：“美国和厄瓜多尔的关系将继续下去”，从而正式承认了因达武鲁政权。
    耶罗维·因达武鲁是在卡斯特罗·希洪亲美独裁政权倒台后，由厄瓜多尔三军首脑推举，在三月三十日担任临时总统的。四日，他组成了一个九人内阁，其中八个成员代表工商界和金融界寡头的利益。
