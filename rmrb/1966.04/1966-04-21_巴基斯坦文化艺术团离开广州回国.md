Title: Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 20th — The Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe, led by Head of the Troupe Rahman and Deputy Head of the Troupe Madame Aziz, concluded its visit and performances in our country and left Guangzhou by plane this afternoon to return home.

    Li Xueguang, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Cultural Bureau, Zheng Shaokang, Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chen Yimin, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Cultural Bureau, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments, literary and art figures, and hundreds of people, warmly saw them off at the airport.

    This noon, Zeng Sheng, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou, and his wife, Ruan Qunying, held a banquet to warmly bid farewell to the Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe.

### 巴基斯坦文化艺术团离开广州回国

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦文化艺术团离开广州回国
    据新华社广州二十日电　由团长拉赫曼、副团长阿奇兹夫人率领的巴基斯坦文化艺术团结束了在我国的访问演出，今天下午乘飞机离开广州回国。
    广东省文化局局长李雪光、对外文协广州分会副会长郑少康、广州市文化局局长陈一民，以及有关方面负责人、文艺界人士和数百群众，到机场热烈欢送。
    今天中午，广东省副省长、广州市市长曾生和夫人阮群英举行宴会，热烈欢送巴基斯坦文化艺术团。
