Title: Spring Rain Welcomed in Parts of Northern China
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. From the 5th to the 7th, a spring rain fell in the eastern part of the Hetao area of Inner Mongolia, northern Shaanxi, northern and central Shanxi, northern and central Hebei, northwestern Shandong and the Jiaodong area, central and western Liaoning, and Beijing.
    According to statistics from the central meteorological department, the rainfall in the northern rainfall area was uneven. In some areas of northern and central Shanxi, central Hebei, northwestern Shandong, and central Liaoning, the general rainfall was more than 10 millimeters. Among them, Mount Wutai in Shanxi had the largest rainfall, reaching 27 millimeters; other areas generally had less than 10 millimeters. The rainfall in Beijing was six or seven millimeters. This spring rain is beneficial to the upcoming spring plowing and sowing in the aforementioned areas. However, areas with less rain should not relax their work on drought prevention and resistance.

### 北方部分地区喜降春雨

1966-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

    北方部分地区喜降春雨
    新华社七日讯　五日到七日，我国北方的内蒙古河套东部地区，陕西北部，山西北部和中部地区，河北北部和中部地区，山东西北部和胶东地区，辽宁中部和西部地区以及北京市，下了一场春雨。
    据中央气象部门统计，这次北方降雨区的雨量不均匀，山西北部和中部部分地区、河北中部、山东西北部和辽宁中部，一般降雨量在十毫米以上，其中，山西五台山下雨最大，降雨量达二十七毫米；其它地区下雨一般不到十毫米。北京市区雨量为六七毫米。这场春雨对上述地区即将开始的春耕春种有好处。但是，雨少地区仍不能放松防旱、抗旱工作。
