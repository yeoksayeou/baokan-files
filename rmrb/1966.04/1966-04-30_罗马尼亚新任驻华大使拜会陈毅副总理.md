Title: The New Romanian Ambassador to China Pays a Courtesy Call on Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th - The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian Socialist Republic to the People's Republic of China, Aurel Duma, paid a courtesy call on Vice Premier and concurrently Foreign Minister Chen Yi this evening to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Vice Premier Chen Yi held friendly talks with him.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th - The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian Socialist Republic to the People's Republic of China, Aurel Duma, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 罗马尼亚新任驻华大使拜会陈毅副总理

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新任驻华大使拜会陈毅副总理
    新华社二十九日讯　罗马尼亚社会主义共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奥列尔·杜马，今天晚上拜会陈毅副总理兼外交部长，商谈递交国书事宜。陈毅副总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
    新华社二十九日讯　罗马尼亚社会主义共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奥列尔·杜马，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
