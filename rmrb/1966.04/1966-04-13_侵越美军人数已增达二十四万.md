Title: U.S. Troops in Vietnam Increased to 240,000

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th dispatch: Saigon news: A U.S. military spokesman in Vietnam announced on the 11th that, as of April 7th, the number of American aggressor troops in South Vietnam had reached 240,000. This includes 146,600 army personnel, 48,600 Marine Corps personnel, 32,200 air force personnel, and 12,600 navy personnel.
    According to other reports, a South Korean mercenary regiment of 5,000 men sailed from Busan, South Korea, on a U.S. Navy transport ship on the 10th to serve as cannon fodder in the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam. This regiment is the first batch of approximately 20,000 mercenary troops sent by the South Korean puppet clique to South Vietnam at the behest of the United States. The remaining 15,000 men of a division are scheduled to go to South Vietnam in July of this year.

### 侵越美军人数已增达二十四万

1966-04-13
第3版()
专栏：

    侵越美军人数已增达二十四万
    新华社十二日讯　西贡消息：侵越美军发言人十一日宣布，到四月七日为止，在南越的美国侵略军已达二十四万人。其中包括陆军十四万六千六百人，海军陆战队四万八千六百人，空军三万二千二百人，海军一万二千六百人。
    另据报道，南朝鲜帮凶军一个团五千人，已在十日从南朝鲜的釜山乘一艘美国海军运输舰去南越，充当美国侵越战争的炮灰。这个团是南朝鲜傀儡集团奉美国之命增派去南越的约二万人的帮凶部队的第一批。其余一万五千人的一个师预定在今年七月前往南越。
