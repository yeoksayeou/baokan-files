Title: Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Lodges Strongest Protest Against Attack on Our Embassy by Indonesian Armed Forces in Collusion with Rioters; Great China's Diplomats Will Never Be Intimidated by Any Violence

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 1

    Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Lodges Strongest Protest Against Attack on Our Embassy by Indonesian Armed Forces in Collusion with Rioters
    Great China's Diplomats Will Never Be Intimidated by Any Violence
    Solemnly Points Out: The Indonesian government's direct organization and direction of the attack on the Chinese Embassy is a barbaric fascist atrocity, and it must bear all consequences of this incident.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 16th—According to news from Jakarta: Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, met with Suwito, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Indonesia, on the afternoon of the 15th.
    On the afternoon of the 15th, Chargé d'affaires Yao Dengshan went to the official residence of Adam Malik, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Indonesian government, requesting to meet with Malik to protest and negotiate with the Indonesian government regarding the extremely serious incident of the barbaric attack on the Chinese Embassy. However, Malik declined to meet, citing excuses, and summoned Suwito from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to receive Chargé d'affaires Yao Dengshan.
    Chargé d'affaires Yao solemnly pointed out to Suwito that the Indonesian government's direct organization and direction of the armed forces and rioters' barbaric attack on the Chinese Embassy, injuring Chinese diplomats and shooting embassy staff, is an extremely serious and barbaric fascist atrocity. This action by the Indonesian government completely destroys the norms of international relations and tramples on international law. This is extremely rare in the history of international relations.
    Chargé d'affaires Yao lodged the strongest oral protest to the Indonesian government regarding this anti-China atrocity, demanding that the Indonesian government bear all consequences of this incident. Chargé d'affaires Yao solemnly pointed out: "The diplomats of the great People's Republic of China will never be intimidated by any violence."

### 姚登山临时代办就印尼武装部队伙同暴徒袭击我使馆提出最强烈抗议  伟大中国的外交人员绝不会被任何暴力吓倒

1966-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    姚登山临时代办就印尼武装部队伙同暴徒袭击我使馆提出最强烈抗议
    伟大中国的外交人员绝不会被任何暴力吓倒
    严正指出：印度尼西亚政府直接组织和指挥袭击中国大使馆，是非常野蛮的法西斯暴行，必须对这一事件承担一切后果的责任。
    新华社十六日讯　雅加达消息：中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办姚登山，十五日下午会见印度尼西亚外交部副部长苏维托。
    姚登山临时代办在十五日下午前往印度尼西亚政府外交部长阿达姆·马利克的官邸，要求会见马利克，就中国大使馆遭到野蛮袭击的极端严重事件向印度尼西亚政府提出抗议和交涉。但是，马利克托词不见，并从外交部把苏维托召来接待姚登山代办。
    姚代办向苏维托严正地指出，印度尼西亚政府直接组织和指挥武装部队和暴徒野蛮袭击中国大使馆，打伤中国外交官，枪伤大使馆的工作人员的事件是十分严重的，是非常野蛮的法西斯暴行。印度尼西亚政府的这一行径使国际关系准则完全被破坏，国际公法完全被践踏，这是国际关系史上非常罕见的。
    姚代办就这次反华暴行，向印度尼西亚政府提出最强烈的口头抗议，要印度尼西亚政府对这一事件承担一切后果的责任。姚代办严正指出：“伟大的中华人民共和国的外交人员绝不会被任何暴力所吓倒”。
