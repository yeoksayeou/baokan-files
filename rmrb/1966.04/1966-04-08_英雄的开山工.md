Title: Heroic Road Opener
Author: Li Xuanwu
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    Heroic road opener,
    Everyone praises you,
    Carrying a sledgehammer, you travel the world.
    You tread on dangerous peaks,
    You smash ten-thousand-zhang cliffs!
    With one swing, the hot sweat, oh,
    Washes away the ice and snow on the cliff.
    With two swings, the stubborn rocks, oh,
    Glare and burst with anger!
    With three swings, that smiling face, oh,
    Watches the tiger's mouth swallow tons of dynamite.
    Hammer falls, the cannon roars,
    The western sky, oh, darkens;
    Earthquakes crack, mountains collapse;
    The train runs through the clouds,
    Who doesn't say the plateau scenery is the best!
    Road opener, everyone praises you,
    Carrying a sledgehammer, you travel the world.
    Holding high the red flag of revolution,
    Standing on cliffs, looking out at Asia, Africa, and Latin America,
    Looking at Vietnam, where the gangsters are burning and killing,
    A belly full of fiery hatred!
    Swinging the hammer, you're not just drilling holes, oh,
    You're hitting Johnson's head!
    Good, road opener,
    Beat them hard, hit them hard!
    With your eight-pound hammer,
    Smash the American imperialists into bean curd dregs!
    With your road-opening cannon,
    Blast the old world into flowing water and falling flowers!
    Road opener, everyone praises you,
    Carrying a sledgehammer, you travel the world.
    The motherland calls to expel "poverty and blankness,"
    Wherever it's hard, you go to settle down.
    On steep cliffs, you build your bed,
    Steel drills as pillows,
    Never tired.
    On holidays, climb to the top of the peak,
    Looking towards Tiananmen—setting off fireworks.
    Truly "infinite scenery is at the dangerous peak,"
    Road opening is extremely beautiful!

### 英雄的开山工
李选吾
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    英雄的开山工
    铁路工人　李选吾
    开山工，谁不夸，
    扛起铁锤走天下。
    重重险峰任你踩，
    万丈山崖任你砸！
    甩一锤，那热汗呵，
    把崖上的冰雪冲刷。
    甩两锤，那顽石呵，
    瞪着眼来肺气炸！
    甩三锤，那笑脸呵，
    看虎口把万吨炸药吞下。
    锤落炮声响，
    西天呀，黑压压；
    地震裂，山崩垮；
    火车云中走，
    谁不说高原风光最佳！
    开山工，谁不夸，
    扛起铁锤走天下。
    革命红旗高高举，
    脚踏山崖，放眼亚非拉，
    望越南，强盗在烧杀，
    满腔仇恨火辣辣！
    抡起锤，你哪是在打眼呵，
    你是在打约翰逊的脑瓜！
    好哇，开山工，
    狠狠揍，狠狠打！
    用你八磅锤，
    把美国佬砸成豆腐渣！
    用你开山炮，
    把旧世界轰个流水落花！
    开山工，谁不夸，
    扛起铁锤走天下。
    祖国叫声驱“穷白”，
    哪儿艰苦哪儿去安家。
    峭壁上，把床架，
    钢钎当睡枕，
    永远不疲乏。
    节日，登上峰顶，
    遥望那天安门——放礼花
    真是“无限风光在险峰”，
    开山美极啦！
