Title: African Representatives from Four Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Enlarged Meeting of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 20th—Bela Joudjou and Benazzouz from Algeria; Sebastien Ramazhah, Joseph Kisuli, and Leon Lukongo from Congo (L); Bakary Traore, Lassina Kane, and Modibo Alfred Keita from Mali; and Valentim Sissol and Alfredo Chembene from Mozambique, who are here to attend the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Li Morison, Sugiyama Shihei, and A. L. Abukus, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association; Mei Yi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association; and Li Wu, Secretary of the Secretariat.

### 前来参加亚非记协书记处扩大会议  非洲四国代表到达北京

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    前来参加亚非记协书记处扩大会议
    非洲四国代表到达北京
    新华社二十日讯　前来参加亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）的阿尔及利亚的贝拉朱格、贝纳佐兹，刚果（利）的塞巴斯蒂安·拉马扎赫、约瑟夫·基苏利、列昂·卢孔戈，马里的巴卡里·特拉奥雷、拉西纳·卡内、莫迪博·艾尔弗雷德·凯塔，莫三鼻给的瓦朗蒂姆·西索尔、阿尔弗雷多·切姆贝尼，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有亚非新闻工作者协会书记处书记里·摩里逊、杉山市平、阿·勒·阿布库斯，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益，书记处书记李伍等。
