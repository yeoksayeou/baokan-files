Title: Strange Agenda

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 3

　　Strange Agenda
　　The Foreign Affairs Committee of the Japanese House of Representatives openly held a so-called "round table" forum to discuss the "Chinese national character and tendency toward aggression."
　　To fabricate a discussion about China's "tendency toward aggression" out of thin air reflects the increasingly strong "tendency toward aggression" of the Japanese reactionaries. Haven't they already described the war of aggression against China twenty years ago as an anti-aggression "national liberation war"?
　　However, China's "national character" is worth the Japanese militarists carefully re-examining. If they have truly forgotten the results of having competed with the Chinese people, it would be beneficial to study it again, which might restrain their "tendency toward aggression" a bit.

### 奇怪议题

1966-04-08
第3版()
专栏：三言两语

    奇怪议题
    日本众议院外交委员会公然举行所谓“圆桌”座谈会，讨论“中国的民族性和侵略倾向”的问题。
    无中生有地讨论什么中国的“侵略倾向”，这种举动正是反映出了日本反动派日益增强的“侵略倾向”。他们不是已经把二十年前的侵华战争说成是反侵略的“民族解放战争”了吗？
    不过，中国的“民族性”，倒是值得日本的军国主义者很好地重新研究一下的。如果他们对于曾经同中国人民较量过的结果，真的忘记了的话，研究一下更有好处，这样可以使他们的“侵略倾向”收敛一点。
