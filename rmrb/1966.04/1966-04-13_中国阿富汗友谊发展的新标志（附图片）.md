Author: Du Xiuxian
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 6

    New Symbol of the Development of Chinese-Afghan Friendship
    At the invitation of His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his wife paid a friendly visit to Afghanistan from April 4 to 8, 1966. Accompanying the visit were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and his wife, Zhang Qian, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others. During the visit, Chairman Liu and his entourage received a very warm welcome and grand reception. This demonstrated the sincere friendship of the Afghan people towards the Chinese people.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and King Mohammed Zahir Shah shake hands warmly.
    Citizens of Kabul braved the rain to warmly welcome Chairman Liu Shaoqi along the streets.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his entourage visited the Friday Mosque in Herat, the third largest city in Afghanistan, which can accommodate 80,000 people.
    In Kabul, Wang Guangmei, wife of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, accompanied by Zhang Qian, wife of Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited the Afghan Women's Association and received a warm welcome from Afghan women and children.
    At the welcoming rally of Kabul citizens, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Prince Sultan, President of the Afghan-Chinese Friendship Association, were together.
    Citizens of Kabul warmly cheered the distinguished Chinese guests.
    The citizens of Kabul welcomed the Chinese guests by holding a goat-grabbing competition - an activity that the Afghan people usually hold only during grand ceremonies and solemn festivals.
                    All photos in this edition are taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 中国阿富汗友谊发展的新标志（附图片）
杜修贤
1966-04-13
第6版()
专栏：

    中国阿富汗友谊发展的新标志
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人应阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下的邀请，于一九六六年四月四日至八日对阿富汗进行了友好访问。陪同访问的有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人、外交部副部长章汉夫等。在访问期间，刘主席和他的随行人员受到了十分热烈的欢迎和隆重的接待。这表现了阿富汗人民对中国人民的真挚的友谊。
    刘少奇主席和穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿国王热烈握手。
    喀布尔市民冒雨夹道热烈欢迎刘少奇主席。
    刘少奇主席和他的随行人员在阿富汗第三大城市赫拉特市，参观可以容纳八万人的礼拜五清真寺。
    在喀布尔，刘少奇主席的夫人王光美在陈毅副总理的夫人张茜陪同下访问阿富汗妇女协会，受到阿富汗妇女和儿童的热烈欢迎。
    在喀布尔市民欢迎大会上，陈毅副总理和阿中友好协会会长苏尔坦亲王在一起。
    喀布尔市民向中国贵宾热烈欢呼。
    喀布尔市民以举行抢羊比赛——阿富汗人民通常在盛大的仪式和隆重的节日才举行的一种活动，来欢迎中国客人。
                    本版照片均系新华社记者　杜修贤摄
