Title: UAR Prime Minister Receives Ambassador Huang Hua
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th -- UAR Prime Minister Zakaria Mohieddin received Chinese Ambassador to the UAR Huang Hua here today and had a friendly conversation with him.

### 阿联总理接见黄华大使

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联总理接见黄华大使
    新华社开罗十九日电　阿联总理查卡里亚·毛希丁十九日在这里接见了中国驻阿联大使黄华，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
