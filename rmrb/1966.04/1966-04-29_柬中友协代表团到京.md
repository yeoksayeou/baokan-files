Title: Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: A six-member delegation from the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by its chairman, Lang Nette, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association. When Lang Nette and other distinguished guests stepped off the plane, they were warmly welcomed by hundreds of people. They beat drums and gongs, waved bouquets, and shouted "Long live the friendship between China and Cambodia!" Zhang Hanfu, President of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association, warmly embraced Lang Nette. Young women from the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport also included Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Ren Yinglun and Zhou Yiping, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wang Demao, Vice-President of the Supreme People's Court; and Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation.
    Li Jun, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Cambodian Embassy in China, his wife, and diplomatic officials from the embassy also greeted the delegation at the airport.

### 柬中友协代表团到京

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    柬中友协代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　由柬中友好协会主席兰·涅特率领的柬中友协代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中柬友协的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。兰·涅特等贵宾走下飞机时，受到数百群众的热烈欢迎。他们敲锣打鼓，挥动手中的花束，高呼“中柬友谊万岁！”中柬友协会长章汉夫同兰·涅特热烈拥抱。首都女青年向贵宾们献花。
    到机场欢迎的，还有对外文委副主任丁西林，人大常委会副秘书长连贯，对外友协副会长任映仑、周一萍，最高人民法院副院长王德茂，全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九等。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办李军和夫人，以及大使馆外交官员也到机场迎接。
