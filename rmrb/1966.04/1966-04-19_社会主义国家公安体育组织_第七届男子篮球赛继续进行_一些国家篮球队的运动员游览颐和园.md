Title: Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament Continues; Athletes from Some National Basketball Teams Tour the Summer Palace
Author:
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization continued this afternoon at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium. The Chinese team defeated the Bulgarian team with a score of 83 to 54.
    In the two games held this evening, the Romanian team defeated the Czechoslovakian team with a score of 91 to 77; the Polish team defeated the Albanian team with a score of 79 to 73.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: Today is the first rest day during the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization. On this day, athletes from some of the countries participating in this tournament toured the Summer Palace.
    Ma Xingwu, Director of the Organizing Committee of this tournament and Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Public Security Forces, and members of the Chinese "Pioneer" Men's Basketball Team accompanied the guests on their tour.
    Tonight, athletes from the various countries participating in this tournament watched the Chinese ballet "The Red Detachment of Women."

### 社会主义国家公安体育组织  第七届男子篮球赛继续进行  一些国家篮球队的运动员游览颐和园

1966-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家公安体育组织
    第七届男子篮球赛继续进行
    一些国家篮球队的运动员游览颐和园
    据新华社十七日讯　社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球赛今天下午在北京工人体育馆继续进行。中国队以八十三比五十四战胜了保加利亚队。
    今晚进行的两场比赛，罗马尼亚队以九十一比七十七胜捷克斯洛伐克队；波兰队以七十九比七十三胜阿尔巴尼亚队。
    新华社十八日讯　今天是社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球赛比赛期间的第一个休息日。这一天，参加这届比赛的一些国家的球队的运动员游览了颐和园。
    这届比赛大会组织委员会主任委员、中国人民公安部队政治部副主任马星五和中国“前卫”男子篮球队的队员们，陪同客人们一起游园。
    今天晚上，参加这届比赛的各国球队的运动员，观看了中国芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
