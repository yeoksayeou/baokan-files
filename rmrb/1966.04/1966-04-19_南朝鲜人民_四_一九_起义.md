Title: South Korean People's "April 19th" Uprising
Author:
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 4

    The 19th of April, 1960, marked a high tide in the struggle of the South Korean people who rose up against US imperialism and its running dogs. This struggle overthrew Syngman Rhee, a running dog fostered by US imperialism for many years, and achieved a great victory in the South Korean people's anti-American patriotic struggle.
    The Syngman Rhee clique, the running dog of US imperialism, carried out a long period of fascist rule in South Korea. In order to continue to maintain his position, Syngman Rhee conducted a sham presidential "election" on March 15, 1960.
    The South Korean people rose up against the sham presidential "election".
    On April 19, 1960, hundreds of thousands of people in South Korean cities such as Seoul, Busan, Gwangju, Daegu, and Incheon held massive demonstrations. Under the heavy blows of the South Korean people's fighting forces, the Syngman Rhee puppet government was forced to announce its collective resignation on April 21. On the 27th, Syngman Rhee himself was forced to step down and slipped off to the United States.
                (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 南朝鲜人民“四·一九”起义

1966-04-19
第4版()
专栏：资料

    南朝鲜人民“四·一九”起义
    一九六○年四月十九日，是当时掀起的南朝鲜人民奋起反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争的一个高潮。这次斗争把美帝国主义豢养多年的走狗李承晚赶下了台，在南朝鲜人民的反美爱国斗争中，取得了一次巨大的胜利。
    美帝国主义的走狗李承晚集团在南朝鲜进行了长期的法西斯统治。为了继续维持他的地位，在一九六○年三月十五日，李承晚又进行伪总统“选举”。
    南朝鲜人民奋起反对伪总统“选举”。
    一九六○年四月十九日，南朝鲜几十万群众在汉城、釜山、光州、大丘、仁川等许多城市举行了声势浩大的示威。在南朝鲜人民斗争力量的沉重打击下，李承晚伪政府被迫在四月二十一日宣告集体辞职。二十七日，李承晚本人也被迫下台，溜到美国去了。
                （据新华社）
