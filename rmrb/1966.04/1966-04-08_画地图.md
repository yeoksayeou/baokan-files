Title: Drawing a Map
Author: Cheng Xinghan
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    Drawing a Map
    Squad Leader Cheng Xinghan
    Drawing on the world map,
    First draw Asia, Africa, and Latin America.
    Mark good and bad with red stars and black circles,
    Record the daily news.
    Imperialism—rotten roots,
    Communism—new shoots.
    American imperialism is like a drowning dog,
    The people of the world beat it with sticks.
    Drawing on the world map,
    The South Vietnamese people have great victories,
    Congo and Latin America are marked with red stars,
    Volcanoes erupt in America’s backyard.
    The people of the world want revolution,
    The red glow rises in the East.
    Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the globe,
    Red flags flutter all over the world.

### 画地图
程兴汉
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    画地图
    班长  程兴汉
    世界地图本上画，
    首先画上亚、非、拉。
    红星、黑圈标好坏，
    每天消息都记下。
    帝国主义——烂草根，
    共产主义——新树芽。
    美帝好象落水狗，
    世界人民拿棍打。
    世界地图本上画，
    南越人民战绩大，
    刚果、拉美标红星，
    美国后院火山发。
    世界人民要革命，
    东方升起红彩霞。
    毛泽东思想照全球，
    红旗飘飘满天下。
