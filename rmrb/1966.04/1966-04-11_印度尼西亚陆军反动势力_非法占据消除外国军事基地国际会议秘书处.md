Title: Indonesian Army Reactionary Forces Illegally Occupy Secretariat of the International Conference for the Elimination of Foreign Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 10th -- News from Jakarta: The right-wing reactionary forces of the Indonesian Army have illegally occupied the building of the Secretariat of the International Conference for the Elimination of Foreign Military Bases in Jakarta.
    According to reports, before the occupation by the right-wing reactionary forces of the Army, hundreds of thugs attacked the building on the afternoon of the 8th, and looted relevant documents.
    This secretariat was established by Mrs. Suriadarma, chairperson of the organizing committee of the International Conference for the Elimination of Foreign Military Bases held in Jakarta in October 1965, and a well-known Indonesian social activist.

### 印度尼西亚陆军反动势力  非法占据消除外国军事基地国际会议秘书处

1966-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚陆军反动势力
    非法占据消除外国军事基地国际会议秘书处
    新华社十日讯　雅加达消息：印度尼西亚陆军右派反动势力在雅加达非法占据了消除外国军事基地国际会议秘书处的建筑物。
    据报道，在陆军右派反动势力占据以前，数以百计的暴徒曾在八日下午袭击了这个建筑物，劫走了有关文件。
    这个秘书处是一九六五年十月在雅加达举行消除外国军事基地国际会议以后，由这个会议的组织委员会主席、印度尼西亚著名社会活动家苏里亚达马夫人负责建立的。
