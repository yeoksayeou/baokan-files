Title: Chairman Liu Holds Talks with King Zahir Shah. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Prime Minister Hashim Maiwandwal and Others Participated in the Talks
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 5th -- Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi held talks with King of Afghanistan Mohammed Zahir Shah at the Dilkusha Palace here this morning.
    The Chinese side also attending the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Public Security Wang Dongxing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Feng Xuan, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Wang Daohan, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Lai Yali, and Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Tong.
    The Afghan side also attending the talks included Prime Minister Mohammed Hashim Maiwandwal, Foreign Minister Noor Ahmad Etemadi, Court Minister Ali Mohammed, Finance Minister Abdullah Yaftali, President of the Afghanistan-China Friendship Association Prince Sultan Mahmud Ghazi, and Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Abdul Ghafoor Rawan Farhadi.
    Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Chen Feng and Afghan Ambassador to China Mohammed Aushaf Sohel also attended the talks.
    The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 刘主席同查希尔·沙阿国王举行会谈  陈毅副总理兼外长和哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔首相等参加了会谈

1966-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席同查希尔·沙阿国王举行会谈
    陈毅副总理兼外长和哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔首相等参加了会谈
    新华社喀布尔五日电　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天上午在这里的迪尔库沙宫同阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿举行了会谈。
    中国方面出席会谈的还有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，公安部副部长汪东兴，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长冯铉，对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵，外交部礼宾司司长赖亚力和外交部第一亚洲司司长张彤。
    阿富汗方面出席会谈的还有首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔，外交大臣努尔·艾哈迈德·埃特马迪，宫廷大臣阿里·穆罕默德，财政大臣阿卜杜拉·雅夫塔利，阿富汗—中国友好协会会长苏尔坦·马哈茂德·加齐亲王和外交部政治司司长阿卜杜勒·加福尔·拉万·法哈迪。
    中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫和阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔也出席了会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
