Title: National Chess Tournament Opens This Year
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 2

　　This Year's National Chess Tournament Opens
　　(Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 10th) The 1966 National Chess Tournament opened in Zhengzhou today.
　　This is the ninth national chess tournament since our country's liberation. The competition events include Go, Chinese Chess, and International Chess. More than 150 outstanding players from 20 provinces, cities, and autonomous regions are participating in this competition.
　　The competition method is as follows: Chinese Chess and International Chess adopt a group round-robin system; Go is divided into adult and youth groups. The adult group adopts a group round-robin in the first stage and a scoring round-robin in the second stage, while the youth group adopts a grand round-robin competition method. Many outstanding young chess players have voluntarily signed up to participate in the adult group's competition in order to quickly improve their chess skills.
　　This chess competition will end on May 10th.

### 今年全国棋艺锦标赛开幕

1966-04-12
第2版()
专栏：多种经营喜讯

    今年全国棋艺锦标赛开幕
    据新华社郑州十日电　一九六六年全国棋艺锦标赛今天在郑州开幕。
    这是我国解放后的第九次全国棋艺锦标赛。比赛的项目有围棋、中国象棋和国际象棋。参加这次比赛的有来自二十个省、市、自治区的优秀选手一百五十多人。
    这次比赛的办法是：中国象棋和国际象棋采取分组循环制；围棋分为成年和少年两个组，成年组第一阶段分组循环，第二阶段记分循环，少年组采取大循环比赛的方法。有不少优秀的少年棋手，为了迅速提高棋艺水平，自愿报名参加成年组的比赛。
    这次棋艺比赛将在五月十日结束。
