Title: Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch Personnel Forced to Evacuate and Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 1st: Jakarta news: Reporters and staff of the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch, which was unreasonably declared "temporarily closed" by the Indonesian government, temporarily evacuated Jakarta and returned to China on April 1st, led by the branch chief, Zhang Haitao.
    On the eve of the evacuation, Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, hosted a farewell banquet for the branch staff. Early this morning, Ambassador Yao Zhongming and all the staff of the embassy gathered in front of the embassy to express their warm farewell.
    Those who went to Jakarta Airport to see them off included: Lin Feng, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, and other diplomatic officials, diplomatic officials from the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Indonesia, and reporters from the Korean Central News Agency stationed in Jakarta.
    Before the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch personnel evacuated today, Branch Chief Zhang Haitao wrote a letter to Alatas, Director of the Press Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs: Due to the Indonesian government's closure of the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch and the cessation of all its activities, the Xinhua News Agency Branch personnel were forced to temporarily evacuate Jakarta.
    Before evacuating, the Xinhua Branch distributed a letter to Indonesian readers.

### 新华社雅加达分社人员被迫撤离回国

1966-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    新华社雅加达分社人员被迫撤离回国
    新华社一日讯　雅加达消息：被印度尼西亚政府无理宣布“暂时封闭”的新华社雅加达分社记者和工作人员，在分社社长张海涛率领下于四月一日乘飞机暂时撤离雅加达回国。
    撤离前夕，中国驻印度尼西亚大使姚仲明曾设便宴为分社工作人员饯行。今天凌晨，姚仲明大使和全馆工作人员聚集在大使馆门前表示热烈欢送。
    前往雅加达机场送行的有：中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆文化参赞林风和其他外交官员，越南民主共和国驻印度尼西亚大使馆外交官和朝鲜中央通讯社驻雅加达记者。
    新华社雅加达分社人员在今天撤离前，分社社长张海涛写信通知印度尼西亚外交部新闻司司长阿拉塔斯：由于印度尼西亚政府封闭新华社雅加达分社，停止其一切活动，新华社分社人员被迫暂时撤离雅加达。
    新华分社在撤离时，散发了告印度尼西亚读者书。
