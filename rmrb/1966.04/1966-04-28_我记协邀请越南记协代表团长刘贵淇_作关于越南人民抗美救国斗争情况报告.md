Title: All-China Journalists Association Invites Head of the Vietnamese Journalists Association Delegation, Lưu Quý Kỳ, to Give a Report on the Situation of the Vietnamese People's Struggle Against American Aggression and for National Salvation

Author:
Date: 1966-04-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th—The All-China Journalists Association held a report meeting tonight, inviting Lưu Quý Kỳ, head of the Vietnamese Journalists Association Delegation, to give a report on "The Situation of the Vietnamese People and Vietnamese Journalists' Struggle Against American Aggression and for National Salvation."
    The responsible persons of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association and people from the capital's journalistic circles attended the report meeting.
    Hoàng Vạn Lý, head of the South Vietnam Patriotic and Democratic Journalists Association Delegation, was also invited to attend the report meeting.
    Lưu Quý Kỳ and Hoàng Vạn Lý presented souvenirs to the All-China Journalists Association.

### 我记协邀请越南记协代表团长刘贵淇  作关于越南人民抗美救国斗争情况报告

1966-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协邀请越南记协代表团长刘贵淇
    作关于越南人民抗美救国斗争情况报告
    新华社二十七日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行报告会，邀请越南新闻工作者协会代表团团长刘贵淇作了“关于越南人民和越南新闻工作者抗美救国斗争情况”的报告。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处负责人和首都新闻界人士出席了报告会。
    越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会代表团团长黄万里也应邀出席了报告会。
    刘贵淇和黄万里向中国记协赠送了纪念品。
