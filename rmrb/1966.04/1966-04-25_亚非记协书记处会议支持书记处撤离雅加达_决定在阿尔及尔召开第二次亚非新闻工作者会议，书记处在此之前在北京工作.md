Title: Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Secretariat Meeting Supports Secretariat's Withdrawal from Jakarta, Decides to Hold Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference in Algiers, Secretariat to Work in Beijing Before That

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association's "Resolution on the Temporary Withdrawal of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association from Jakarta" is as follows:

    The Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association was held in Beijing from April 20th to 24th, 1966.

    After listening to the Secretary-General's report on April 21st, 1966, regarding the temporary withdrawal of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association from Jakarta,

    Noting the changes in the Indonesian situation since October 1965,

    Having fully studied the serious difficulties and interference that the Secretariat had to encounter under the above circumstances from October to December 1965, which undoubtedly made it impossible for the Secretariat to continue its normal activities in Jakarta,

    Unanimously approved the report made by the Secretary-General on the temporary withdrawal of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association from Jakarta,

    Unanimously supports the decision made by the Resident Secretary of the Secretariat in December 1965 regarding the temporary withdrawal, and expresses gratitude for the outstanding efforts made by the Resident Secretary in continuing to work in accordance with the guiding principles of the Jakarta Declaration and the Charter of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association's "Resolution on the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference" is as follows:

    The Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, held in Beijing from April 20th to 24th, 1966, unanimously decided that the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference will be held in Algiers as soon as possible within one year from now. The meeting learned with joy and gratitude that Algerian colleagues are prepared to make efforts to realize the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference. It entrusts the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association with full responsibility for preparing and convening the meeting, and taking the necessary steps to ensure the success of the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association's "Resolution on the Working Location of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Before the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference is Held" is as follows:

    The Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association was held in Beijing from April 20th to 24th, 1966.

    Considering that the Secretariat found that it could not uphold the principles of the 1963 Jakarta Declaration and could not continue to work in Jakarta;

    Noting that the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference will be held within one year, at which the location of the headquarters of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association will be discussed and decided in accordance with the Charter of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association;

    Unanimously decided that, before the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference is held, the Secretariat will work in Beijing.

### 亚非记协书记处会议支持书记处撤离雅加达  决定在阿尔及尔召开第二次亚非新闻工作者会议，书记处在此之前在北京工作

1966-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处会议支持书记处撤离雅加达
    决定在阿尔及尔召开第二次亚非新闻工作者会议，书记处在此之前在北京工作
    新华社二十四日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）“关于亚非新闻工作者协会书记处暂时撤离雅加达的决议”，全文如下：
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议
  （扩大）一九六六年四月二十日至二十四日在北京举行。
    在听取了总书记一九六六年四月二十一日所作的关于亚非新闻工作者协会书记处暂时撤离雅加达的报告之后，
    注意到自从一九六五年十月以来印度尼西亚局势发生了变化，
    充分研究了书记处从一九六五年十月到十二月在上述情况下不得不遇到的严重困难和干涉，而这些严重困难和干涉无疑使得书记处无法继续在雅加达进行它的正常活动，
    一致通过了总书记所作的关于亚非新闻工作者协会书记处暂时撤离雅加达的报告，
    一致支持书记处常驻书记一九六五年十二月份所作的关于暂时撤离的决定，表示感谢常驻书记在根据雅加达宣言和亚非新闻工作者协会章程的指导原则继续工作方面所作出的杰出努力。
    新华社二十四日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）“关于第二次亚非新闻工作者会议的决议”，全文如下：
    一九六六年四月二十日到二十四日在北京举行的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大），一致决定第二次亚非新闻工作者会议在从现在起的一年内尽早在阿尔及尔召开。会议以高兴而感激的心情得知，阿尔及利亚同事准备为实现第二次亚非新闻工作者会议而作出的努力。它委托亚非新闻工作者协会书记处完全负责筹备和召开会议，并采取必要的步骤保证会议的成功。
    新华社二十四日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）“关于亚非新闻工作者协会书记处在举行第二次亚非新闻工作者会议之前的工作地点的决议”，全文如下：
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）于一九六六年四月二十日到二十四日在北京举行。
    考虑到书记处发现它无法维护一九六三年雅加达宣言的原则，无法继续在雅加达进行工作；
    注意到第二次亚非新闻工作者会议将在一年之内举行，在那次会议上将根据亚非新闻工作者协会的章程来讨论并决定亚非新闻工作者协会总部的所在地；
    一致决定，在举行第二次亚非新闻工作者会议之前，书记处在北京工作。
