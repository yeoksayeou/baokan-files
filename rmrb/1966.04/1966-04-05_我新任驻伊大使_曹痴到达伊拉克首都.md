Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to Iraq, Cao Chi, Arrives in the Iraqi Capital
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 3rd—The newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, Cao Chi, arrived here on the morning of the 3rd.
    Among those who welcomed him at the airport were Abdul Wadud Sheikhly, Director of the Protocol Department of the Iraqi Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and diplomatic envoys from some countries to Iraq.
    Diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy and Chinese students also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 我新任驻伊大使  曹痴到达伊拉克首都

1966-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻伊大使
    曹痴到达伊拉克首都
    新华社巴格达三日电  新任命的中国驻伊拉克大使曹痴三日上午到达这里。
    到机场欢迎他的有伊拉克外交部礼宾司司长阿卜杜勒·瓦杜德·谢赫利和一些国家驻伊拉克外交使节。
    中国大使馆外交官员和中国留学生也到机场欢迎。
