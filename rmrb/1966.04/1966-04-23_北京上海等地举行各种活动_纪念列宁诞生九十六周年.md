Title: Beijing, Shanghai, and Other Places Hold Various Activities to Commemorate the Ninety-Sixth Anniversary of Lenin's Birth
Author:
Date: 1966-04-23
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd. In recent days, Beijing, Shanghai, and other places have held various activities to commemorate the ninety-sixth anniversary of the birth of the great proletarian revolutionary teacher, Lenin.
    Beijing and more than twenty cities across the country have decorated street display windows, showcasing the photo exhibition "The Great Revolutionary Teacher Lenin." Newspapers in the capital have published photos and information about Lenin's life. The Central People's Broadcasting Station and Beijing Television broadcast literary and artistic programs praising Lenin. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a film evening at the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall on the 22nd, screening the film "Lenin in October."

### 北京上海等地举行各种活动  纪念列宁诞生九十六周年

1966-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    北京上海等地举行各种活动
    纪念列宁诞生九十六周年
    新华社二十二日讯　北京、上海等地近日来举行各种活动，纪念伟大的无产阶级革命导师列宁诞生九十六周年。
    北京以及全国二十多个城市布置街头橱窗，展出了《伟大的革命导师列宁》的图片。首都的报纸刊登了有关列宁生平的图片和资料；中央人民广播电台和北京电视台播送了有关歌颂列宁的文艺节目。中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会，二十二日在北京市中苏友好馆举行电影晚会，放映了影片《列宁在十月》。
