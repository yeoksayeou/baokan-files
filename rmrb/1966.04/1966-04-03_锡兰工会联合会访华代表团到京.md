Title: Ceylon Trade Union Federation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. A seven-member delegation of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation, led by D. Henry, General Secretary of the Ceylon Engineering Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. Tang Zhang, Cao Xianbo, and others were at the airport to welcome them.




**Named Entities**

锡兰工程工人联合会  Ceylon Engineering Workers' Union

北京  Beijing

### 锡兰工会联合会访华代表团到京

1966-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰工会联合会访华代表团到京
    新华社二日讯　由锡兰工程工人联合会总书记迪·亨利率领的锡兰工会联合会访华代表团一行七人今晚乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有唐章、曹宪波等。
