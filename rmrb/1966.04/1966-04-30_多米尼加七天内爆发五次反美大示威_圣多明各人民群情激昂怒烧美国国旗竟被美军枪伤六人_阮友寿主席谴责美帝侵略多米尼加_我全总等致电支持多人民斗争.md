Title: Five Anti-American Demonstrations Erupt in the Dominican Republic in Seven Days; People of Santo Domingo Are Enraged, Burned the American Flag, and Six People Were Shot by U.S. Troops; Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Condemns U.S. Imperialist Aggression Against the Dominican Republic; All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Others Send Telegrams to Support the Struggle of the Dominican People

Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Santo Domingo, 29th: News from Santo Domingo: The people of the Dominican capital held another large-scale and fierce anti-American demonstration on the 28th, angrily protesting the first anniversary of the Johnson administration's dispatch of troops to invade the Dominican Republic. This is the fifth large-scale anti-American demonstration held by the Dominican people in the past week.
    According to reports, on this day, several thousand Dominicans, regardless of the strict guard of the American invaders and reactionary military police, held a fierce anti-American demonstration in front of the American Embassy for an hour and a half. Demonstrators held up signs demanding the immediate withdrawal of American invading troops from the Dominican Republic, shouted anti-American slogans with excitement, and burned three American flags, expressing their incomparable hatred for the American invaders.
    After this, the masses participating in the demonstration held a march protesting the American aggression.
    Throughout the demonstration and march by the Dominican people, the Dominican puppet army fostered by the United States maintained a strict guard in various parts of the city. At the same time, two American helicopters circled in the air, showing the fear and panic of the American invaders and their lackeys in the face of the people's power.
    On the evening of the 28th, another group of Dominican people held a demonstration near an observation post of the American invading army in the suburbs of Santo Domingo. They shouted anti-American slogans and burned an American flag. The American invading army brazenly opened fire on the demonstrating masses, wounding six people. The angry masses retaliated against these American murderers with stones.
    On this day, the people of the Dominican capital also held scattered strikes, protesting the occupation of the Dominican Republic by the American invading army. Residents of Santo Domingo hung Dominican flags with black紗(gauze) on their houses, expressing their mourning for the compatriots killed by the American invaders, and expressing their determination to fight to drive out the American invaders and restore national sovereignty.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th: According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the people and the National Front for the Liberation of South Vietnam fully support the just struggle of the fraternal Dominican people and strongly condemn the illegal military intervention of U.S. imperialism in the Dominican Republic.
    This was stated in a letter from Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the National Front for the Liberation of South Vietnam, to the various revolutionary political parties, trade unions, youth, student, and women's organizations and people of the Dominican Republic on April 20, for the "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People" and the first anniversary of the Dominican "Constitutionalist faction" uprising (April 24).
    The letter said: "South Vietnam and the Dominican Republic are geographically far apart, but we stand on the same front against the common enemy of the people of our two countries and all progressive mankind—U.S. imperialism."
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation, as well as the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, have recently sent telegrams or letters to relevant organizations in the Dominican Republic, expressing their firm support for the Dominican people's just struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and for independence and the maintenance of sovereignty.
    In a telegram to the Dominican Workers' Union, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions praised the Dominican workers and people for their struggle over the past year against the aggression and intervention of U.S. imperialism and its servants, marking a new upsurge in the national democratic movement in the Dominican Republic and Latin America. The telegram pointed out that the Dominican people's dauntless spirit of struggle is a great encouragement to the anti-American struggle of the people of Latin America and the world.

### 多米尼加七天内爆发五次反美大示威  圣多明各人民群情激昂怒烧美国国旗竟被美军枪伤六人  阮友寿主席谴责美帝侵略多米尼加　我全总等致电支持多人民斗争

1966-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    多米尼加七天内爆发五次反美大示威
    圣多明各人民群情激昂怒烧美国国旗竟被美军枪伤六人
    阮友寿主席谴责美帝侵略多米尼加　我全总等致电支持多人民斗争
    新华社二十九日讯　圣多明各消息：多米尼加首都人民二十八日再度举行大规模的和激烈的反美示威，愤怒抗议美国约翰逊政府派遣军队侵略多米尼加一周年。这是多米尼加人民在过去一星期当中举行的第五次大规模反美示威。
    据报道，这一天好几千多米尼加人不顾美国侵略者和反动军警的严密警戒，在美国大使馆门前举行了长达一个半小时的激烈的反美示威。示威者高举着要求美国侵略军立即撤出多米尼加的标语牌，情绪激昂地不断高呼反美口号，并且烧掉三面美国国旗，表示他们对美国侵略者的无比痛恨。
    在这之后，参加示威集会的群众举行了抗议美国侵略的游行。
    在多米尼加人民举行示威游行的整个过程中，美国扶植的多米尼加傀儡军在市区各处严密警戒。同时，两架美国直升飞机在空中盘旋，显示出美国侵略者及其走狗在人民力量面前的恐惧和惊慌。
    二十八日晚上，另一批多米尼加群众在圣多明各郊区美国侵略军的一个观察哨附近举行示威。他们高呼反美口号，并且烧掉一面美国国旗。美国侵略军竟悍然向示威群众开枪，打伤了六人。愤怒的群众用石块回击了这些美国凶手。
    在这一天，多米尼加首都人民还举行了分散的罢工活动，抗议美国侵略军占领多米尼加。圣多明各居民在他们的住房上悬挂着系有黑纱的多米尼加国旗，表示他们对于被美国侵略者杀害的同胞的哀悼，并且表示他们为赶走美国侵略者和恢复国家主权而斗争的决心。
    据新华社河内二十七日电 据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方人民和民族解放阵线完全支持兄弟的多米尼加人民的正义斗争，并强烈谴责美帝国主义对多米尼加共和国进行的非法军事干涉。
    这是越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿为“声援多米尼加人民周”和多米尼加“宪法派”起义一周年（四月二十四日），于四月二十日写给多米尼加各革命政党、工会、青年、学生和妇女组织和人民的一封贺信中说的。
    贺信说：“越南南方和多米尼加在地理上相隔很远，但是我们站在同一条战线上反对我们两国人民和全体进步人类的共同敌人——美帝国主义。”
    据新华社二十七日讯　中华全国总工会、中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会以及中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会，最近分别打电报或写信给多米尼加的有关团体，对多米尼加人民反对美帝国主义侵略、争取独立和维护主权的正义斗争表示坚决支持。
    中华全国总工会在致多米尼加工人联合会的电报中，赞扬多米尼加工人和人民一年来为反对美帝国主义及其仆从的侵略和干涉而进行的斗争，标志着多米尼加和拉丁美洲民族民主运动的新高涨。电报指出，多米尼加人民大无畏的斗争精神，对拉丁美洲人民和全世界人民的反美斗争是很大的鼓舞。
