Title: Executioners Cannot Escape the Punishment of Revolutionary People: South Vietnamese Youth Armed Self-Defense Forces Execute the Murderer Who Persecuted Martyr Nguyen Van Troi
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th – According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Saigon-Gia Dinh area youth armed self-defense forces in South Vietnam executed Nguyen Van Trao, the "chief" of the Chi Hoa Prison of the US-puppet clique, on April 1st. He interrogated and tortured the South Vietnamese hero Nguyen Van Troi.

    The punishment of this heinous executioner has greatly pleased the people of Saigon. After executing Nguyen Van Trao, the Saigon-Gia Dinh area youth armed self-defense forces specifically wrote a letter to Nguyen Van Troi's wife, Phan Thi Quyen, reporting this good news. The letter stated: "We – the sons of heroic Saigon – are continuing to promote Nguyen Van Troi's indomitable fighting spirit, determined to punish the American aggressors and their running dogs, and avenge the patriotic compatriots who were killed!"

### 刽子手逃不脱革命人民的惩罚  南越青年武装自卫队处决迫害阮文追烈士的凶手

1966-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    刽子手逃不脱革命人民的惩罚
    南越青年武装自卫队处决迫害阮文追烈士的凶手
    新华社河内十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方西贡—嘉定地区青年武装自卫队，在四月一日枪决了曾经审讯和拷打南越英雄阮文追的刽子手——美伪集团志和监狱“总管”阮文招。
    这个罪大恶极的刽子手被惩办，使西贡市人心大快。西贡—嘉定地区青年武装自卫队在枪决阮文招后，特地写信向阮文追的妻子潘氏娟报告这一喜讯。信中说：“我们——英雄西贡的儿子，正在继续发扬阮文追不屈不挠的斗争精神，决心惩办美国侵略者和他们的走狗，为被杀害的爱国同胞报仇！”
