Title: Chairman Zhu Watches Splendid Performance by Lao Artists, Receives Leaders and Principal Performers of the Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe
Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, watched a splendid performance by the Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe tonight.

    The Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe, born and grown in the flames of battle under the care and leadership of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, is known as the "revolutionary arts troupe" by the Lao people. The songs and dances they performed tonight, such as "Red Lantern Dance," "Triumphal Dance," and "Friendship Dance," enthusiastically praised the wise leadership of the Lao Patriotic Front, introduced the brave spirit of the Lao people's anti-American patriotic struggle, and praised the close friendship between the people of Laos and China. The audience gave warm applause to their performances of Lao and Chinese songs and dances.

    After the performance, to the sound of "Unity is Strength," Chairman Zhu De walked onto the stage to meet the Lao artists cordially and congratulated them on their successful performance.

    Also present at the performance tonight were Lian Guan, President of the Sino-Lao Friendship Association, Yan Jinsheng, Vice Minister of Culture, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Lü Ji and Ding Bo.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Kanma Phoukhong, head of the Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe, deputy heads Paseuth Sisalon and Channong Inthavong, and principal performers this evening, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

    Present at the reception were Lian Guan, Yan Jinsheng, and others. (Attached picture)

### 朱委员长观看老挝艺术家精采演出  接见老挝爱国战线党文工团负责人和主要演员

1966-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长观看老挝艺术家精采演出
    接见老挝爱国战线党文工团负责人和主要演员
    新华社十日讯　中共中央副主席、人大常委会委员长朱德，今天晚上观看了老挝爱国战线党文工团的精采演出。
    老挝爱国战线党文工团是在老挝爱国战线党中央的关怀和领导下，在战斗的烈火中诞生和成长的，被老挝人民称为“革命的文工团”。今晚他们演出的“红灯舞”、“凯旋舞”、“友谊舞”等歌舞，热情地歌颂老挝爱国战线党的英明领导，介绍了老挝人民反美爱国斗争的勇敢精神，赞扬了老中两国人民的亲密友谊。全场观众对他们演出的老、中两国的歌舞节目，报以热烈的掌声。
    演出结束后，在《团结就是力量》的歌声中，朱德委员长走上舞台和全体老挝艺术家亲切见面，对他们的成功演出表示祝贺。
    观看今晚演出的，还有中老友协会长连贯，文化部副部长颜金生，以及有关方面负责人吕骥、丁波等。
    新华社十日讯  中共中央副主席、人大常委会委员长朱德，今晚接见了老挝爱国战线党文工团团长坎马·富贡，副团长巴色·西沙隆和占农·因塔冯以及主要演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有连贯、颜金生等。（附图片）
