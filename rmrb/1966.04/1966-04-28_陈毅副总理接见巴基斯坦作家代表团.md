Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Pakistani Writers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-28
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th - Vice Premier Chen Yi received Ibrahim Khan and the entire Pakistani Writers Delegation led by him this afternoon.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were leading members of the Chinese Writers Association and writers Liu Baiyu, Yang Shuo, and others.



**Named Entities**

陈毅	Chen Yi
易卜拉欣·汗	Yibulaixin Han (Ibrahim Khan)
巴基斯坦作家代表团	Pakistan Writers Delegation
刘白羽	Liu Baiyu
杨朔	Yang Shuo
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
中国作家协会	Chinese Writers Association

### 陈毅副总理接见巴基斯坦作家代表团

1966-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见巴基斯坦作家代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见易卜拉欣·汗和由他率领的巴基斯坦作家代表团全体团员。
    陈毅副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中国作家协会的负责人和作家刘白羽、杨朔等。
