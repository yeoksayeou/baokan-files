Title: Supporting the Dominican People's Anti-American Patriotic Struggle: Chinese Organizations to Hold "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People"
Author:
Date: 1966-04-20
Edition: 4

    Supporting the Dominican People's Anti-American Patriotic Struggle
    Chinese Organizations to Hold "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People"
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th – In order to support the Dominican people’s just, patriotic, and anti-American struggle, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and other Chinese people's organizations will hold a "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People" from April 24th to 30th. During the week of solidarity, mass rallies, report meetings, and other activities will be held.
    The holding of the "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People" was decided upon at a resolution made at the Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference held this January.

### 支持多米尼加人民反美爱国斗争  我各团体将举行“声援多米尼加人民周”

1966-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    支持多米尼加人民反美爱国斗争
    我各团体将举行“声援多米尼加人民周”
    新华社十九日讯　为了支持多米尼加人民反美爱国正义斗争，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会和中国其他人民团体，将在四月二十四日至三十日期间举行“声援多米尼加人民周”。声援周期间将举行群众集会和报告会等各项活动。
    举行“声援多米尼加人民周”，是今年一月举行的亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议的一项决议中决定的。
