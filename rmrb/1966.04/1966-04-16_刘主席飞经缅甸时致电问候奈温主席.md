Title: Chairman Liu Sends Greetings to Chairman Ne Win While Flying Over Burma

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, April 15th (via telegram) Chairman Liu Shaoqi, on the morning of April 15th, while flying to Pakistan for a visit and passing over Burmese airspace, sent a telegram to Chairman Ne Win of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma to express his greetings. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency, the esteemed Chairman Ne Win of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, Rangoon:
    As my colleagues and I are en route to Pakistan and flying over your country, I would like to extend my sincere greetings to Your Excellency. May the "Pauk-Phaw" friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people grow stronger every day. I look forward to meeting with Your Excellency soon.
          Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                  April 15, 1966

### 刘主席飞经缅甸时致电问候奈温主席

1966-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席飞经缅甸时致电问候奈温主席
    新华社达卡十五日电　刘少奇主席十五日上午乘飞机前往巴基斯坦访问飞经缅甸领空时，打电报问候缅甸联邦革命委员会奈温主席。电文如下：仰光缅甸联邦革命委员会尊敬的奈温主席阁下：
    我和我的同事们在前往巴基斯坦途中飞经贵国国境，谨向阁下致以诚挚的问候。祝中缅两国人民的“胞波”友谊与日俱增。我期待着和阁下很快会晤。
          中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                  一九六六年四月十五日
