Title: Vice Premier Chen Receives Djawoto and Friends from the Asian and African Press

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-27
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received Djawoto, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, and representatives from various countries attending the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association this evening.

    Also received were the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation currently visiting Beijing and students of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Training Class.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi held cordial and friendly conversations with them. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Mei Yi, Fan Jin, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 陈副总理接见查禾多和亚非新闻界朋友

1966-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    陈副总理接见查禾多和亚非新闻界朋友
    新华社二十六日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今晚接见亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多和参加亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）的各国代表。
    同时被接见的，还有正在北京访问的老挝爱国力量新闻代表团和亚非新闻工作者进修班的学员。
    陈毅副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有廖承志、梅益、范瑾等有关方面负责人。
