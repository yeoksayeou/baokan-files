Title: Syrian Cotton Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. A three-member Syrian Cotton Trade Delegation led by Malak Azmah, Director-General of the Syrian Cotton Promotion Organization, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday. Li Yusheng, Wang Mingjun, and other responsible persons from relevant departments were present to welcome them.
    Zahiri Farah, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Syrian Embassy in China, also came to greet them.

### 叙棉花贸易代表团到京

1966-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    叙棉花贸易代表团到京
    新华社三日讯  由叙利亚棉花推销总局局长马拉克·阿兹迈率领的叙利亚棉花贸易代表团一行三人昨天乘飞机到达北京。前往欢迎的有李郁生、王明俊等有关方面的负责人。
    叙利亚驻华大使馆临时代办查希里·法拉赫也前往迎接。
