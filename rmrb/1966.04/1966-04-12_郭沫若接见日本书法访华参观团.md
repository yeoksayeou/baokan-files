Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Calligraphy Visiting Group

Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 3

    Guo Moruo, Honorary President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, met this afternoon with all members of the Japanese calligraphy visiting group headed by Kagawa Kyo. They held a cordial and friendly conversation.

    Present at the meeting were Lin Lin, Guo Laowei, and others.

    Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, was also present.



**Named Entities**


郭沫若   Guo Moruo
香川京   Kagawa Kyo
林林   Lin Lin
郭劳为   Guo Laowei
西园寺公一  Saionji Kinkazu

### 郭沫若接见日本书法访华参观团

1966-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本书法访华参观团
    新华社十一日讯　中日友好协会名誉会长郭沫若今天下午接见了以香川京为首的日本书法访华参观团全体人员。同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有林林、郭劳为等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
