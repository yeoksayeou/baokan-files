Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Arrives in Guinea
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, April 2nd – The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by Guo Jian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, arrived in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, on the morning of April 2nd.

    The Chinese Women's Delegation was welcomed at the airport by a delegation headed by Mrs. Bangoura Fatou, Chairwoman of the Women's Committee of the First District of Conakry, and a delegation headed by Mrs. Goundé Fatou, Administrative Secretary of the Women's Committee of the Second District of Conakry. Li Youfeng, wife of Chai Zemin, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, was also at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我妇女代表团到几内亚

1966-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团到几内亚
    新华社科纳克里二日电　由中华人民共和国妇女联合会书记处书记、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员郭建率领的中国妇女代表团，二日上午到达几内亚首都科纳克里。
    中国妇女代表团在机场受到以科纳克里第一区妇女委员会主席邦古拉·法图夫人为首的代表团和以科纳克里第二区妇女委员会行政书记贡德·法图夫人为首的代表团的欢迎。中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民的夫人李友锋也到机场欢迎。
