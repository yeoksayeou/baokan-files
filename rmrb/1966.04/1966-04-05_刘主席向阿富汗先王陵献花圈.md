Title: Chairman Liu Presents Wreath at Tomb of Former King of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, April 4th -- Chairman Liu Shaoqi laid a wreath at the tomb of former King of Afghanistan, Mohammed Nadir Shah, here this afternoon.

    Accompanying Chairman Liu were Prince Sultan, President of the Afghanistan-China Friendship Association, and Mohammed Azaf Sohel, the Afghan Ambassador to China.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, other members of Chairman Liu's entourage, and Chen Feng, the Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, were also present at the wreath-laying ceremony.

    The tomb of the former King is located on a mountaintop near Kabul. After the ceremony, Chairman Liu and other Chinese guests stood by the tomb and overlooked the historical site of Bala Hissar Fortress, the battlefield of the second war against British colonialists, which began in 1878.

### 刘主席向阿富汗先王陵献花圈

1966-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席向阿富汗先王陵献花圈
    新华社喀布尔四日电　刘少奇主席今天下午在这里向阿富汗先王穆罕默德·纳迪尔·沙阿的陵前献了花圈。
    陪同刘主席的有阿富汗—中国友好协会会长苏尔坦亲王和阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔。
    献花圈时，陈毅副总理、外交部副部长章汉夫和刘主席的其他随行人员以及中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫也在场。
    先王陵位于喀布尔市附近的一座山顶上。在仪式结束后，刘主席和其他中国客人站在陵墓旁俯瞰了历史遗迹巴拉·希萨尔城堡，它是从一八七八年开始的第二次反抗英国殖民主义者战争的战场。
