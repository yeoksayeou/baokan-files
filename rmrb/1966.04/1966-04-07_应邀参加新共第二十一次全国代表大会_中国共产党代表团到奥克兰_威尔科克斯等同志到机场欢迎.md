Title: Invited to Attend the 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand, Chinese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Auckland, Comrade Wilcox and Others Welcome at Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Auckland, 6th: A Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the CPC, arrived in Auckland, New Zealand on the afternoon of the 6th.
    The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Communist Party of New Zealand to attend the 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand.
    The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, and members of the Political Committee, Nunez, Austerle, and MacLeod.
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: A Chinese Communist Party delegation, invited by the Communist Party of New Zealand to attend the 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand, led by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the CPC, left Beijing by plane on the 4th.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee, and Xu Li and Zhang Xiangshan, responsible personnel of the central organs of the CPC.

### 应邀参加新共第二十一次全国代表大会  中国共产党代表团到奥克兰  威尔科克斯等同志到机场欢迎

1966-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加新共第二十一次全国代表大会
    中国共产党代表团到奥克兰
    威尔科克斯等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社六日讯　奥克兰消息：由中共中央委员刘宁一率领的中国共产党代表团六日下午到达新西兰的奥克兰。
    代表团是应新西兰共产党的邀请，前来参加新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会的。
    代表团在机场受到新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯和政治委员会委员努涅斯、奥斯特勒和麦克劳德等同志的欢迎。
    新华社六日讯　应新西兰共产党邀请，前往新西兰参加新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会的中国共产党代表团，在中共中央委员刘宁一率领下，已在四日乘飞机离开北京。
    前往机场送行的有：中共中央政治局候补委员、中央书记处书记康生，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，中共中央机关负责工作人员许立、张香山等。
