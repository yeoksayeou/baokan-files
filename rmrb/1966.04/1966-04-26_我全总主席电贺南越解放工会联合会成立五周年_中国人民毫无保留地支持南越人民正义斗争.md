Title: ACFTU Chairman Sends Telegram Congratulating South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions on its Fifth Anniversary; Chinese People Unreservedly Support the Just Struggle of the South Vietnamese People

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram yesterday to Pham Xuan Thai, Chairman of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions, warmly congratulating the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions on its fifth anniversary.
    In the telegram, Liu Ningyi praised the South Vietnamese workers, together with the people of other strata in South Vietnam, under the glorious banner of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, for carrying out heroic, tenacious, and moving struggles to drive out the American imperialist aggressor bandits and overthrow the heinous rule of their dog cohorts, and for achieving extremely brilliant victories. In the telegram, Liu Ningyi reiterated that the Chinese workers and all the Chinese people resolutely and unreservedly support the just struggle of the South Vietnamese workers and people, and firmly believe that the sacred cause of the Vietnamese people to liberate the South, defend the North, and reunify the motherland will surely achieve final victory.

### 我全总主席电贺南越解放工会联合会成立五周年  中国人民毫无保留地支持南越人民正义斗争

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我全总主席电贺南越解放工会联合会成立五周年
    中国人民毫无保留地支持南越人民正义斗争
    新华社二十五日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一昨天打电报给越南南方解放工会联合会主席范春泰，热烈祝贺越南南方解放工会联合会成立五周年。
    刘宁一在电报中赞扬越南南方工人和越南南方其他各阶层人民一起，在越南南方民族解放阵线的光辉旗帜下，为赶走美帝国主义侵略强盗、打倒它的走狗集团的罪恶统治而进行了英勇顽强、可歌可泣的斗争，取得了极其辉煌的胜利。刘宁一在电报中重申，中国工人和全体中国人民坚决地、毫无保留地支持越南南方工人和人民的正义斗争，并且坚信越南人民解放南方、保卫北方、统一祖国的神圣事业必将取得最后胜利。
