Title: Afghan Queen Hosts Banquet for Chairman Liu’s Wife and Vice Premier Chen’s Wife; Chairman Liu Shaoqi’s Wife Visits Afghan Women’s Association
Author:
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 5th. Afghan Queen Homeira held a luncheon at the Gul Khana Palace on the 5th to host Chairman Liu Shaoqi’s wife and Vice Premier Chen Yi’s wife.

    Before the luncheon, they had a cordial and friendly conversation.

    Those present at the banquet included: Princess Bilqis; Princess Maryam; Hatula, wife of Crown Prince Ahmad Shah; Liluma Hosseini, wife of the King's second son; and the wife of Prince Sultan Mahmoud Ghazi, etc.

    The wife of Prime Minister Maiwandwal also attended the luncheon.

    Also present at the luncheon was the wife of Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, Chen Feng.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 5th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi's wife, Wang Guangmei, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi's wife, Zhang Qian, visited the Afghan Women's Association here on the morning of the 5th, and were warmly welcomed by 1,700 Afghan women, girls and children.

    The wife of Prince Sultan Mahmoud Ghazi, President of the Afghanistan-China Friendship Association, accompanied them on the visit.

    Afghan women and female students stood on both sides of the road to the association, cheering the Chinese guests, waving the Chinese and Afghan flags, showering them with flowers, and shouting slogans such as "Long Live Afghanistan-China Friendship."

    The Afghan Women's Association held a tea party to entertain the Chinese guests.

    Saliha Farouk Etemadi, head of the Afghan women's delegation who had just returned from a visit to China four days ago and president of the Afghan Women's Association, spoke at the tea party. She warmly welcomed the Chinese sisters to visit Afghanistan on behalf of the Afghan women. She said that the great achievements made by the hardworking, proactive, and intelligent Chinese people in all aspects over the past sixteen years had left her with an indelible impression. She wished the friendship between the women and people of the two countries would last forever.

    In her reply, Chairman Liu's wife said that the Chinese people have deep friendship for the hardworking and brave Afghan people. She said: "Afghan women have made contributions in the struggle to defend their motherland. Your heroine Malalai's heroic deeds of fearlessly taking the lead in defeating the invaders have left a glorious page in the history of the Afghan people's struggle to safeguard national independence."

    She said that we are pleased to see that more and more Afghan women are participating in the cause of developing the national economy and national culture, and are playing an active role in the prosperity and strength of their motherland. "We sincerely hope that Afghan sisters will continue to make new achievements in safeguarding national sovereignty and building their motherland."

    Chairman Liu's wife said that under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, Chinese women are participating in the socialist construction of their motherland with great enthusiasm. Chinese women are determined to study hard, strive for progress, overcome all difficulties, and give full play to their potential. Chinese women are willing to learn from each other and advance together with Afghan sisters. She also said that women from China and Afghanistan have increased their understanding of each other and established close sisterhood through mutual visits. She said: "We believe that the friendship between the two countries will be as continuous and towering as the Hindu Kush Mountains!"

    Afterwards, the Chinese guests visited the association's embroidery classes, weaving classes, typing classes, and other training classes, as well as nurseries and kindergartens. A group of Afghan children dressed in brightly colored national costumes said to the guests in Chinese: "Hello, Aunties!" and performed the Afghan national dance, the Attan.

    Accompanying Chairman Liu's wife were also the wife of the Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, Chen Feng, and the wives of other diplomatic personnel from the embassy. (With pictures)

### 阿富汗王后宴请刘主席夫人陈副总理夫人  刘少奇主席夫人访问阿富汗妇女协会

1966-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王后宴请刘主席夫人陈副总理夫人
    刘少奇主席夫人访问阿富汗妇女协会
    新华社喀布尔五日电  阿富汗王后霍梅拉五日在古尔罕纳宫举行午宴，招待刘少奇夫人和陈毅夫人。
    午宴之前，她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    出席这次宴会的有：贝尔格丝公主；玛丽亚姆公主；艾哈迈德·沙阿王储的夫人哈图拉；国王次子的夫人利卢马·侯赛尼和苏尔坦·马哈茂德·加齐亲王的夫人等。
    迈万德瓦尔首相的夫人也出席了午宴。
    出席午宴的还有中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫的夫人。
    新华社喀布尔五日电刘少奇主席的夫人王光美在陈毅副总理的夫人张茜陪同下，五日上午在这里访问了阿富汗妇女协会，受到一千七百名阿富汗妇女、姑娘和儿童的热烈欢迎。
    阿富汗—中国友好协会会长苏尔坦·马哈茂德·加齐亲王的夫人陪同她们访问。
    阿富汗妇女和女学生站在协会的道路两旁向中国客人欢呼，挥动中国和阿富汗的国旗，向她们抛撒鲜花，高呼“阿中友好万岁”的口号。
    阿富汗妇女协会举行茶会招待中国客人。
    四日刚从中国访问回国的阿富汗妇女代表团团长、阿富汗妇女协会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪夫人在茶会上讲了话。她代表阿富汗妇女热烈欢迎中国姐妹访问阿富汗。她说，勤劳、积极和智慧的中国人民十六年来在各方面取得的伟大成就给了她不可磨灭的印象。她祝两国妇女和人民的友谊万古长青。
    刘少奇夫人在致答词中说，中国人民对勤劳勇敢的阿富汗人民，怀有深厚的友情。她说：“阿富汗妇女在保卫祖国的斗争中曾作出贡献。你们的巾帼英雄玛拉拉伊奋不顾身，身先士卒，打败侵略者的英勇事迹，在阿富汗人民维护民族独立的斗争史上留下了光辉的一页。”
    她说，我们高兴地看到，越来越多的阿富汗妇女参加了发展民族经济和民族文化的事业，为祖国的繁荣富强发挥着积极的作用。“我们衷心地希望，阿富汗姐妹们在维护国家主权和建设祖国的事业中，不断取得新的成就。”
    刘少奇夫人说，中国妇女在毛泽东主席的领导下，正在以高度的热情参加祖国的社会主义建设。中国妇女决心努力学习，奋发图强，克服一切困难，充分发挥她们的潜力。中国妇女愿意同阿富汗姐妹们互相学习，共同前进。她还说，中阿两国妇女通过相互访问，增加了彼此的了解，建立了亲密的姐妹情谊。她说：“我们相信，两国的友谊将象兴都库什山一样，绵亘不断，巍峨长存！”
    接着，中国客人参观了协会举办的刺绣班、编织班、打字班和其他训练班以及托儿所和幼儿园。一群穿着艳丽多采的民族服装的阿富汗儿童用中国话对客人们说：“阿姨好！”并且表演了阿富汗民族舞蹈阿丹舞。
    陪同刘少奇夫人的还有中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫的夫人和大使馆其他外交人员的夫人。（附图片）
