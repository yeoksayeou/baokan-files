Title: Pakistani Journalist Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Ijaz Hussain, a journalist from the Pakistani newspaper "Dawn", arrived in Beijing on the 6th for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association. Chang Zhiqing, Executive Director of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Director and Editor-in-Chief of the Ta Kung Pao, hosted a banquet for the guest tonight.

### 巴基斯坦一记者到京

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦一记者到京
    新华社七日讯　巴基斯坦《黎明报》记者伊查兹·侯赛因，应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问，于六日到达北京。中华全国新闻工作者协会常务理事、大公报副社长兼总编辑常芝青今晚设宴招待客人。
