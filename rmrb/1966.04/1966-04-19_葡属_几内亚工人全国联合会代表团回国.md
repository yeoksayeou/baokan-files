Title: "Portuguese" Guinea Workers' National Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, 18th, the "Portuguese" Guinea Workers' National Union delegation led by Luís Almeida Cabral, General Secretary of the "Portuguese" Guinea Workers' National Union, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home. Kang Yonghe, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the airport to see them off.

### “葡属”几内亚工人全国联合会代表团回国

1966-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    “葡属”几内亚工人全国联合会代表团回国
    据新华社十八日讯　由“葡属”几内亚工人全国联合会总书记路易斯·阿尔美达·卡布拉尔率领的“葡属”几内亚工人全国联合会代表团，结束在我国的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会副主席康永和等。
