Title: Upon the invitation of General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife will visit Burma. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and his wife will accompany them on the visit.
Author:
Date: 1966-04-10
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 9th　At the invitation of General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, Wang Guangmei, will pay a friendly visit to Burma. Accompanying the visit will be Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, as well as other personnel.

### 应缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军的邀请  刘少奇主席和夫人将访问缅甸  陈毅副总理兼外长和夫人陪同前往访问

1966-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    应缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军的邀请
    刘少奇主席和夫人将访问缅甸
    陈毅副总理兼外长和夫人陪同前往访问
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美应缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军的邀请，将前往缅甸进行友好访问。陪同访问的有中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人以及其他人员。
