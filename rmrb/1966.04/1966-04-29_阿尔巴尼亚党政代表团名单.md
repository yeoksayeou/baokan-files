Title: List of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th - List of the Party and Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Albania:
    Head of Delegation: Mehmet Shehu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania
    Members of Delegation: Hysni Kapo, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party
    Abdyl Këllezi, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association
    Nesti Nase, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania
    Vasil Nathanaili, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China
    Attendants: Piro Bita, Director of the International Department of the Party Central Committee
    Agim Popa, Secretary of the Delegation
    Fikri Vogli, Editor-in-Chief of "Unity" Newspaper
    Vasili Skorovoti, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
    Rambi Pecini, Head of the Delegation's Guards
    Nesti Saraçi, Director of the Bureau of the Council of Ministers
    Rambi Qiqishti, Doctor
    Niko Mitrushi, Chinese Interpreter
    Kleanthis Antoni, Guard
    Gani Kodra, Guard
    Nafrus Çanaj, Guard
    Nexhip Pajo, Guard
    Peci Kalluçi, Deputy Secretary of the Delegation
    Aneta Frëto (female), Stenographer
    Andrei Kote, Nurse
    Enver Çakaj, Attendant
    Dhimitër Lala, Film Cameraman
