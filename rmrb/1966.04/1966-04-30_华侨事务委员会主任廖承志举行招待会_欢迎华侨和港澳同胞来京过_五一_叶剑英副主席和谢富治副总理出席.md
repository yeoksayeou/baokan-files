Title: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Holds Reception Welcoming Overseas Chinese and Compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao to Beijing for May Day; Vice Chairman Ye Jianying and Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi Attend

Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, held a reception at noon today to welcome more than 600 overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who have come to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

    Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, and Xie Fuzhi, Vice Premier of the State Council, attended the reception.

    Liao Chengzhi proposed a toast at the reception, extending warm festive greetings to the overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who came to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. He extended cordial greetings and respect to the staff of our embassy in Indonesia and the overseas Chinese in Indonesia who returned to the motherland after bravely struggling against the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces in the most difficult and arduous circumstances. He asked the overseas Chinese present to convey the cordial greetings of the people of the motherland to the overseas Chinese all over the world who support the motherland, love the motherland, and fight against imperialism and reactionaries.

    Ye Jianying also proposed a toast at the reception. He said that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, our great motherland is becoming stronger year by year. The people who support us and sympathize with us are all over the world.

    He said that the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces have recently frantically massacred and persecuted the Indonesian people and overseas Chinese in Indonesia, but this is only a temporary cloud in the sky. He said that imperialism and reactionaries are bound to perish, the revolutionary struggle of the world's people is bound to win, and the future of the world is bright.

    The reception was filled with an atmosphere of close unity. Overseas Chinese from five continents gathered with people from all walks of life in the capital. They frequently raised their glasses and toasted to the increasing prosperity of the great motherland, to Chairman Mao's health, to the patriotic unity of the overseas Chinese, and to the progressive cause of the people of the world and the development of the world revolution.

    Also present at the reception were Ping Jiesan, Secretary General of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Liu Shuzhou, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Fang Fang, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Zhuang Xiquan, Acting Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese; and Lian Guan, Shao Lizi, Chen Qiyou, Chen Qiyuan, Wang Kunlun and other relevant responsible persons.

    Yao Zhongming, our ambassador to Indonesia who is currently in Beijing, and the staff of the embassy, the staff of our representative offices in Indonesia who were temporarily forced to withdraw from Indonesia, and the staff of the Xinhua News Agency's Jakarta branch, also attended the reception.

### 华侨事务委员会主任廖承志举行招待会  欢迎华侨和港澳同胞来京过“五一”  叶剑英副主席和谢富治副总理出席

1966-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨事务委员会主任廖承志举行招待会
    欢迎华侨和港澳同胞来京过“五一”
    叶剑英副主席和谢富治副总理出席
    新华社二十九日讯　华侨事务委员会主任廖承志今天中午举行招待会，欢迎前来北京参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的六百多名华侨和港澳同胞。
    政协全国委员会副主席、国防委员会副主席叶剑英，国务院副总理谢富治出席了招待会。
    廖承志在招待会上祝酒，向前来欢度“五一”国际劳动节的华侨和港澳同胞致热烈的节日祝贺。他向在最困难、最艰苦环境下同印度尼西亚右派反动势力进行英勇斗争后回到祖国的我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆工作人员和印度尼西亚华侨，表示亲切的问候和敬意。他请在座的侨胞向全世界支持祖国、爱护祖国，同帝国主义和反动派进行斗争的侨胞，转达祖国人民的亲切问候。
    叶剑英也在招待会上祝酒。他说，我们伟大祖国在毛主席和中国共产党的英明领导下，一年比一年强盛。拥护我们、同情我们的人民遍于全世界。
    他说，印度尼西亚右派反动势力最近疯狂屠杀和迫害印度尼西亚人民和印度尼西亚华侨，这只不过是天空上暂时出现的一片乌云。他说，帝国主义和反动派是一定要灭亡的，世界人民的革命斗争是一定要胜利的，世界前途是光明的。
    招待会上洋溢着亲密团结的气氛。来自五大洲的侨胞同首都各界人士欢聚一堂，他们频频举杯，为伟大祖国的日益昌盛，为毛主席的健康，为侨胞们的爱国大团结，为全世界人民的进步事业和世界革命的发展而干杯。
    出席招待会的，还有政协全国委员会秘书长平杰三，中共中央统一战线工作部副部长刘述周，华侨事务委员会副主任方方，侨联代主席庄希泉，以及有关方面负责人连贯、邵力子、陈其尤、陈其瑗、王昆仑等。
    正在北京的我国驻印度尼西亚大使姚仲明和使馆工作人员，暂时被迫从印度尼西亚撤回的我国驻印度尼西亚代表机构的工作人员和新华社驻雅加达分社的工作人员等，也出席了招待会。
