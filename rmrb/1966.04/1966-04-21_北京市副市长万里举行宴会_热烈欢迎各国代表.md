Title: Beijing Vice Mayor Wan Li Hosts Banquet, Warmly Welcoming Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. Beijing Vice Mayor Wan Li hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome representatives from various countries attending the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association.

    Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association Djawoto, as well as members of the Secretariat and representatives from Azania (i.e., South Africa), Cambodia, Ceylon, China, Guinea, Indonesia, Japan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Palestine, Syria, Tanzania, the United Arab Republic, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, South Vietnam, and Zimbabwe, were invited to attend the banquet.

    Vice Mayor Wan Li proposed a toast at the banquet. He warmly congratulated the opening of the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association in Beijing and wished the meeting a success. He proposed a toast to the victory of the Afro-Asian people's unity against imperialism, and to the friendship of the vast number of Afro-Asian journalists and Afro-Asian people.

    In his toast, Djawoto, on behalf of the representatives from various countries, expressed his deep gratitude to the Chinese hosts. He wished success to the cause of the Afro-Asian people's struggle and victory to the people fighting in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

    Beijing Vice Mayor Cui Yueli, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Journalists Association Wang Yunsheng and Fan Jin, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Qin Jialin, and heads of news organizations in the capital also attended the banquet.

### 北京市副市长万里举行宴会  热烈欢迎各国代表

1966-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市副市长万里举行宴会
    热烈欢迎各国代表
    新华社二十日讯　北京市副市长万里今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎参加亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）的各国代表。
    亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多，以及书记处成员和阿扎尼亚（即南非）、柬埔寨、锡兰、中国、几内亚、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、巴勒斯坦、叙利亚、坦桑尼亚、阿联、越南民主共和国、越南南方、津巴布韦等国家和地区的代表，应邀出席了宴会。
    万里副市长在宴会上祝酒。他热烈祝贺亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）在北京开幕，预祝会议取得成功。他提议，为亚非人民团结反帝的胜利，为广大亚非新闻工作者和亚非人民的友谊干杯。
    查禾多在祝酒中代表各国代表向中国主人表示深切的谢意。他祝愿亚非人民的斗争事业获得成功，祝愿亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲正在战斗的人民取得胜利。
    北京市副市长崔月犁，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生、范瑾，外交部新闻司司长秦加林，以及首都新闻单位的负责人也出席了宴会。
