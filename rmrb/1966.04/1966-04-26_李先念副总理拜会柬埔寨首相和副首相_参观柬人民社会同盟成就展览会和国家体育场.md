Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Pays Visits to Cambodian Prime Minister and Deputy Prime Minister; Visits Exhibition of Achievements of the Cambodian People's Socialist Community and National Stadium
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 25th: Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, paid separate visits this morning to Prince Norodom Kantol, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Cambodia, and His Excellency Son Sann, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Economic Affairs, Finance, and Planning Coordination.

    Wang Daohan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, accompanied him on the visits.

    Today, during the meeting with Prince Kantol, Vice Premier Li Xiannian conveyed the greetings of the Chinese state leaders to Prince Kantol. Prince Kantol asked Vice Premier Li Xiannian to convey his thanks to the Chinese state leaders. They also had a very cordial conversation on the friendly relations between China and Cambodia.

    During the meeting with Deputy Prime Minister Son Sann, the Chinese guests were warmly welcomed by the heads and staff of the National Bank of Cambodia.

    Zhang Gang, Cambodian Ambassador to China, and Chen Shuliang, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, were present at both meetings.

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 24th: Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of China, accompanied by Son Sann, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Economic Affairs, Finance, and Planning Coordination of Cambodia, visited the Exhibition of Achievements of the Cambodian People's Socialist Community this afternoon. Wang Daohan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, accompanied him on the visit.

    During the visit, Deputy Prime Minister Son Sann personally explained things to the Chinese guests. Vice Premier Li Xiannian praised the exhibition, saying that it was a rich and colorful exhibition.

    After the visit, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, accompanied by Phnom Penh Mayor Tioulong Khun, toured the city of Phnom Penh in an open-top car and was warmly welcomed by the general public along the way. When the Chinese guests' car arrived at Mao Zedong Avenue, symbolizing Sino-Cambodian friendship, the masses standing on both sides of the avenue applauded and cheered enthusiastically, expressing their friendship towards the Chinese people to Vice Premier Li Xiannian. Subsequently, Vice Premier Li Xiannian visited the Cambodian National Stadium. It was already dark at this time, and the stadium was brightly lit. Mayor Tioulong Khun told the Chinese guests that the construction of this stadium was completed by Cambodia itself. Vice Premier Li Xiannian expressed his appreciation for the construction and facilities of this stadium.

### 李先念副总理拜会柬埔寨首相和副首相  参观柬人民社会同盟成就展览会和国家体育场

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理拜会柬埔寨首相和副首相
    参观柬人民社会同盟成就展览会和国家体育场
    新华社金边二十五日电中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念二十五日上午分别拜会了柬埔寨首相兼外交大臣康托尔亲王和副首相兼经济事务、财政和计划统筹大臣宋双阁下。
    陪同拜会的有中国对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵。
    今天，在会见康托尔亲王时，李先念副总理转达了中国国家领导人对康托尔亲王的问候。康托尔亲王请李先念副总理向中国国家领导人转达他的谢意。他们还就中柬两国之间的友好关系进行了十分融洽的交谈。
    在会见宋双副首相时，中国贵宾还受到了柬埔寨国家银行负责人和工作人员的热烈欢迎。
    两次会见时，柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗和中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮都在座。
    新华社金边二十四日电中国国务院副总理李先念二十四日下午由柬埔寨副首相兼经济事务、财政和计划统筹大臣宋双陪同，参观了柬埔寨人民社会同盟成就展览会。随同参观的有中国对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵。
    在参观时，宋双副首相亲自给中国客人作了讲解。李先念副总理赞扬这个展览会说，这是一个丰富多采的展览会。
    参观之后，李先念副总理由金边市长狄潘陪同，乘敞篷汽车参观了金边市容，一路上受到了广大群众的热烈欢迎。当中国客人的汽车来到象征中柬友谊的毛泽东大道的时候，站在大道两旁的群众热烈鼓掌欢呼，向李先念副总理表达了他们对中国人民的友谊。接着，李先念副总理参观了柬埔寨国家体育场。这时候天已黑了，体育场上灯火通明。狄潘市长告诉中国客人说，这座体育场的工程是柬埔寨自己完成的。李先念副总理对这座体育场的建筑和设备表示赞扬。
