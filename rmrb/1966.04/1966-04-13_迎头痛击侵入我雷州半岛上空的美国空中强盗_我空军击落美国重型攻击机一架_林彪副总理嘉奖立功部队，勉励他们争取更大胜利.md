Title: Deal a Head-On Blow to the American Air Pirates Invading the Airspace Over Leizhou Peninsula; Our Air Force Shoots Down an American Heavy Attack Aircraft; Vice Premier Lin Biao Commends the Meritorious Unit, Encouraging Them to Strive for Greater Victories
Author:
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th – The Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army shot down an American imperialist A-3B heavy attack aircraft in the airspace over the Leizhou Peninsula in Guangdong Province this afternoon.

    This American aircraft intruded into our country's airspace from the southeast of Hainan Island, Guangdong Province, at around 13:00 today. Later, it crossed the Qiongzhou Strait and penetrated deep into the airspace over the Leizhou Peninsula, carrying out military provocations. Our aircraft immediately took off to monitor and repeatedly approached the enemy aircraft to issue warnings. The American aircraft ignored our aircraft's warnings and continued to penetrate deep into our airspace. To safeguard our country's sovereignty, our aircraft decisively shot down the enemy aircraft.

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th – Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao issued a commendation order today, praising the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for shooting down an American imperialist A-3B heavy attack aircraft in the airspace over the Leizhou Peninsula in Guangdong Province.

    The commendation order pointed out that the dispatch of heavy attack aircraft by American imperialism deep into the airspace over our mainland for military provocations is a blatant violation of our country's airspace sovereignty and a serious step by American imperialism to impose war on the Chinese people while stepping up its expansion of the war of aggression against Vietnam. You fought well in this battle, dealing a head-on blow to the rampant war provocations of aggressive American imperialism.

    The commendation order hopes that the broad masses of commanders and fighters of the Air Force will continue to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, give prominence to politics, be on strict guard, resolutely eliminate all enemy aircraft that dare to invade, and strive for greater victories in the struggle to defend the motherland's airspace and smash the war provocations of American imperialism.

### 迎头痛击侵入我雷州半岛上空的美国空中强盗  我空军击落美国重型攻击机一架  林彪副总理嘉奖立功部队，勉励他们争取更大胜利

1966-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    迎头痛击侵入我雷州半岛上空的美国空中强盗
    我空军击落美国重型攻击机一架
    林彪副总理嘉奖立功部队，勉励他们争取更大胜利
    新华社广州十二日电　中国人民解放军空军部队，今天下午在广东省雷州半岛地区上空击落美帝国主义A—3B重型攻击机一架。
    这架美机于今天十三时许从我广东省海南岛东南侵入我国领空，以后又越过琼州海峡，深入到我雷州半岛地区上空，进行军事挑衅活动。我机当即起飞监视，并多次逼近敌机，以示警告。美机竟然不顾我机警告，继续深入我领空。我机为维护我国主权，乃一举将这架敌机击落。
    新华社十二日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪，今天颁发嘉奖令，表扬在广东省雷州半岛地区上空击落美帝国主义A—3B重型攻击机一架的中国人民解放军空军部队。
    嘉奖令指出，美帝国主义派重型攻击机深入到我大陆上空进行军事挑衅活动，是对我国领空主权的公然侵犯，也是美帝国主义在加紧扩大侵略越南战争的同时，妄图把战争强加在中国人民头上的一个严重步骤。你们这一仗打得好，给了侵略成性的美帝国主义的猖狂战争挑衅以迎头痛击。
    嘉奖令希望空军部队广大指战员，继续高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出政治，严阵以待，坚决消灭敢于来犯的一切敌机，在保卫祖国领空和粉碎美帝国主义的战争挑衅的斗争中，争取更大的胜利。
