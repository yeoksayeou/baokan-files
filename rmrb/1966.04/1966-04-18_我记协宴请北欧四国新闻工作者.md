Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet for Journalists from Four Nordic Countries
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 17th – The All-China Journalists Association hosted a banquet tonight to welcome journalists from four Nordic countries: Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden.

    Fan Jin, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, spoke at the banquet. Sleiten, Chairman of the Norwegian Press Association, also spoke on behalf of the journalists from the four Nordic countries. They wished the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of the four Nordic countries would grow stronger day by day.

    Present at the banquet were Zhou Xinwu, Chen Weifan, and other responsible individuals from relevant departments, as well as figures from the capital's press circles. Toivo Kala, the Finnish Ambassador to China; Acre, the Norwegian Ambassador to China; Wahlquist, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Swedish Embassy in China; and Bering, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Danish Embassy in China, attended the banquet.

### 我记协宴请北欧四国新闻工作者

1966-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    我记协宴请北欧四国新闻工作者
    新华社十七日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行宴会，欢迎来自丹麦、芬兰、挪威、瑞典等北欧四个国家的新闻工作者。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席范瑾在宴会上讲了话。挪威新闻协会主席斯莱顿代表北欧四国新闻工作者也讲了话。他们祝愿中国人民和北欧四国人民的友谊日益增强。
    出席宴会的，有周新武、陈维帆等有关方面负责人，以及首都新闻界人士。芬兰驻中国大使托依伏拉，挪威驻中国大使阿克雷，瑞典驻中国大使馆临时代办沃尔奎斯特，丹麦驻中国大使馆临时代办培林等出席了宴会。
