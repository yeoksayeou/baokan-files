Title: Angolan Patriots Attack Colonial Military Convoy, Annihilating Twenty-Seven Enemy Soldiers in the Last Week of March
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, 9th. The General Staff of the Angolan National Liberation Army issued a communique on the 4th, announcing that Angolan nationalist armed forces killed a total of twenty-seven Portuguese colonial soldiers in the fighting during the last week of March.
    The communique stated that on March 26th, a commando unit of the Angolan National Liberation Army attacked a Portuguese colonial military convoy near Brito Godins, killing five enemy soldiers. On the same day, another unit of the National Liberation Army engaged in a fierce battle with a Portuguese patrol near Kakuso, killing nine enemy soldiers.
    On March 28th, Angolan guerrillas destroyed a bridge, killing all five enemy soldiers on a Portuguese military vehicle that was crossing the bridge.
    The communique stated that on March 30th, near Santo Antonio do Zaire, guerrillas killed three enemy soldiers sitting in a jeep. In the Kimbele area, a Portuguese patrol was lured by guerrillas disguised as peasants onto a road and ambushed, resulting in five enemy soldiers being killed.

### 安哥拉爱国者袭击殖民军车队  在三月最后一周中歼敌二十七名

1966-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

    安哥拉爱国者袭击殖民军车队
    在三月最后一周中歼敌二十七名
    新华社阿尔及尔九日电  安哥拉民族解放军参谋部四日发表公报宣布，安哥拉民族主义者武装力量在三月最后一周的战斗中，共打死葡萄牙殖民军二十七名。
    公报说，三月二十六日，安哥拉民族解放军的一支突击队在布里托戈丁斯附近袭击了葡萄牙殖民军的军用车队，打死敌军五名。同一天，民族解放军的另一支部队同葡萄牙巡逻队在卡库索附近展开了一场激战，打死敌军九名。
    三月二十八日，安哥拉游击队炸毁一座桥梁，使正在过桥的一辆葡萄牙军用车上的敌军五名全部丧命。
    公报说，三月三十日，在圣安东尼奥·多扎伊雷附近，游击队又打死了坐在一辆吉普车上的三名敌军。在金贝勒地区，一支葡萄牙巡逻队被化装成农民的游击队引到一条路上遭到伏击，五名敌军被打死。
