Title: International Strike News

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    International Strike News
    Three Million Italian Workers Hold General Strike
    Rome Dispatch: Currently in Italy, over three million workers are engaged in a struggle to improve their living and working conditions, either through nationwide strikes or through intermittent strikes in a specific location.
    The striking workers include 1.2 million metalworkers and machine manufacturing workers, 1 million construction workers, 500,000 food workers, as well as workers from other industries.
    Zambian Anglo-American Copper Mine African Workers Strike
    Lusaka Dispatch: The strike initiated on the 28th of last month by African miners at the copper mines operated by the Zambian Anglo American Corporation has, as of the 4th, expanded to all seven mining areas controlled by these companies.
    These seven mining areas employ more than 30,000 African workers. Due to the strike by African miners, copper production at these Anglo American Corporation mines has come to a standstill.
    240,000 Argentine Transportation Workers Strike
    Buenos Aires Dispatch: Approximately 240,000 workers and staff members belonging to the Argentine Transportation Workers Union held a 24-hour general strike on the 5th in support of the strike struggle of Argentine aviation personnel.
    This large-scale strike has paralyzed the operation of trains, ships, and airplanes nationwide, and urban passenger transport has also partially ceased.
    At the same time, non-teaching staff at eight Argentine universities also held a 24-hour strike on the 5th, demanding reasonable wages and benefits.

### 国际罢工要闻

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：国际罢工要闻

    国际罢工要闻
    意大利三百万工人举行大罢工
    罗马消息：目前意大利有三百多万工人在进行争取改善生活条件和劳动条件的斗争，他们或者是举行全国规模的罢工，或者是在一个地方举行时断时续的罢工。
    罢工工人包括一百二十万五金工人和机器制造工人，一百万建筑工人，五十万食品工人，以及其他行业的工人。
    赞比亚英美铜矿非洲工人罢工
    卢萨卡消息：赞比亚英美矿业公司经营的铜矿的非洲矿工上月二十八日开始举行的罢工，四日已经扩展到这些公司所控制的全部七个矿区。
    这七个矿区雇佣着三万多名非洲工人。由于非洲矿工的罢工，这些英美矿业公司的铜的生产已陷于停顿状态。
    阿根廷二十四万运输工人罢工
    布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：大约二十四万名参加阿根廷运输工会的工人和职员五日举行二十四小时大罢工，声援阿根廷航空人员的罢工斗争。
    这次大规模的罢工，使全国的火车、船只和飞机的行驶陷于瘫痪，城市客运也部分停顿。
    与此同时，阿根廷八所大学的非教学职工五日也举行了二十四小时罢工，要求合理的工资待遇。
