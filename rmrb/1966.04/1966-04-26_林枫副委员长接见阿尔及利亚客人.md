Title: Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Algerian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Algerian journalists delegation led by Semmiche Abdelhamid this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Li Wu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, and Qin Jialin, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 林枫副委员长接见阿尔及利亚客人

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长接见阿尔及利亚客人
    新华社二十五日讯　林枫副委员长今天上午接见了由塞米什·阿布德拉哈米德率领的阿尔及利亚新闻工作者代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李伍、外交部新闻司司长秦加林。
