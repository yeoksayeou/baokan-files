Title: Afghan Guests Leave Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    Afghan Guests Leave Guangzhou for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th) The Afghan women's delegation of five people, headed by Saliha Farouk Etemadi, President of the Afghan Women's Association, concluded its friendly visit to our country and left Guangzhou by train this morning to return home.

### 阿富汗客人离穗回国

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗客人离穗回国
    据新华社广州三十日电　以阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪为首的阿富汗妇女代表团一行五人，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天上午乘火车离开广州回国。
