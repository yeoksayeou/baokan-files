Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, Receives Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received this afternoon the entire delegation of the Japanese Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, headed by delegation leader Yamaguchi Keiichi, and held cordial and friendly talks with them. After the reception, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo hosted a banquet to welcome the Japanese guests.
    Participating in the reception and banquet were leading members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and relevant organizations.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社四日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，今天下午接见了以团长山口圭一为首的日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会友好代表团全体团员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见后，郭沫若副委员长设宴欢迎日本客人。
    参加接见和宴会的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会和有关团体的负责人。
