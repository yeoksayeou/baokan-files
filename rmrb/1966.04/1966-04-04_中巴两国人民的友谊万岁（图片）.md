Title: Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and Pakistani People (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 6

    Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and Pakistani People
    At the invitation of Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People’s Republic of China, and his wife Wang Guangmei, paid a friendly visit to Pakistan from March 26 to March 31. They were accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, as well as other accompanying personnel.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei arrived in Rawalpindi, the temporary capital of Pakistan, and were warmly welcomed by Pakistani President Ayub Khan, government officials, and local people. The picture shows Chairman Liu shaking hands warmly with President Ayub Khan.
            Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian Photo
    Hundreds of thousands of people in Rawalpindi lined the streets to warmly welcome Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other distinguished Chinese guests visiting Pakistan.
            Xinhua News Agency Reporter Ge Chunjiang Photo
    Pakistani President Ayub Khan paid a return visit to Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, at the Presidential Guest House in Rawalpindi. They held talks in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
                                      Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian Photo
    The Pakistan-China Friendship Association in Lahore, the capital of West Pakistan Province, held a grand luncheon to entertain Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi attended the banquet and delivered a speech. The slogan in the picture is: "We are very grateful to China for supporting us against Indian aggression!" Xinhua News Agency Reporter Ge Chunjiang Photo
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife visited Islamabad and listened to a briefing on the construction of Islamabad, the new capital of Pakistan. The second from the left in the front row is President Ayub Khan.
                         Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian Photo
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi, accompanied by President Ayub Khan, visited Islamabad and planted a Chinese tallow tree on the mountain. Pakistani friends said that the evergreen tallow tree symbolizes the lasting friendship between China and Pakistan.
                            Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian Photo
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife visited the Taxila Museum. Local residents sang and danced to welcome the Chinese guests.
                        Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian Photo

### 中巴两国人民的友谊万岁（图片）

1966-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    中巴两国人民的友谊万岁
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美，应巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅的邀请，从三月二十六日到三月三十一日对巴基斯坦进行了友好访问。陪同访问的有陈毅副总理和夫人以及其他随行人员。
    刘少奇主席和夫人王光美到达巴基斯坦临时首都拉瓦尔品第，受到巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗和政府官员以及当地人民的盛大欢迎。图为刘主席同阿尤布·汗总统热烈握手。
            新华社记者　杜修贤摄
    拉瓦尔品第数十万群众夹道热烈欢迎刘少奇主席等中国贵宾访问巴基斯坦。
            新华社记者　戈春江摄
    巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗在拉瓦尔品第的总统迎宾馆回拜中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，他们在亲切友好的气氛中举行会谈。
                                      新华社记者　杜修贤摄
    西巴基斯坦省首府拉合尔的巴基斯坦—中国友好协会举行盛大午宴，招待刘少奇主席。陈毅副总理兼外交部长出席宴会并发表讲话。图中标语是：“我们非常感谢中国支持我们反对印度侵略！”　新华社记者　戈春江摄
    刘少奇主席和夫人参观伊斯兰堡，听取巴基斯坦新首都伊斯兰堡建设情况的介绍。前左二是阿尤布·汗总统。
                         新华社记者　杜修贤摄
    刘少奇主席在阿尤布·汗总统陪同下参观伊斯兰堡，在山上种下一棵中国乌臼树。巴基斯坦朋友们说，常青的乌臼树象征着中巴两国的持久友谊。
                            新华社记者　杜修贤摄
    刘少奇主席和夫人参观泰格西拉（城）的博物馆，当地居民载歌载舞迎候中国客人。
                        新华社记者　杜修贤摄
