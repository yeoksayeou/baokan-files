Title: The People of China and Myanmar Will Always Live Together in Friendship (Pictures)
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 6

　　The Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, and his wife paid a friendly visit to the Union of Burma from April 17 to 19 at the invitation of General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma. Accompanying Chairman Liu on the visit were Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and others.
　　General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, and his wife held a grand state banquet to entertain Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife. The picture shows Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Chairman Ne Win toasting each other warmly at the banquet.
　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife paid a courtesy call on Chairman Ne Win and his wife. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife were also present during the call.
　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and other Chinese guests, accompanied by Chairman Ne Win and his wife, watched a cultural performance. After the performance, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, accompanied by Chairman Ne Win and his wife, went on stage and took a group photo with the performers.
　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice Premier Chen Yi laid a wreath at the tomb of Burmese national hero General Aung San.
　　At the State Guest House in Yangon, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu admired the scenery in the company of Chairman Ne Win.
　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi, accompanied by Chairman Ne Win, waved to the crowd at the Mingaladon Airport in Yangon.
　　Citizens of Yangon waved the national flags of China and Myanmar and cheered enthusiastically for Chairman Liu and others.
　　(Photos on this page are taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian, except where credited)

### 中缅两国人民永远友好相处（图片）

1966-04-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中缅两国人民永远友好相处
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人，应缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军的邀请，四月十七日到十九日对缅甸联邦进行了友好访问。陪同刘主席访问的有：国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫等。
    缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军和夫人举行盛大国宴招待刘少奇主席和夫人。图为刘少奇主席和奈温主席在宴会上亲切碰杯。
    刘少奇主席和夫人拜会奈温主席和夫人。拜会时，陈毅副总理和夫人也在座。
    刘少奇主席和夫人、陈毅副总理和夫人及其他中国客人，在奈温主席和夫人等陪同下观看了文艺表演。演出结束后，刘少奇主席和夫人等在奈温主席和夫人陪同下，登上舞台，和演员合影。
    刘少奇主席和陈毅副总理向缅甸民族英雄昂山将军陵墓献花圈。
    在仰光国宾馆，陈毅副总理、章汉夫副部长在奈温主席陪同下观赏风景。
    刘少奇主席在奈温主席的陪同下，在仰光明加拉顿机场上向群众挥手致意。
    仰光市民挥舞中缅两国国旗，向刘主席等热烈欢呼。
      （本版照片除署名者外，均为新华社记者杜修贤摄）
