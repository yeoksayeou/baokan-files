Title: Chinese Table Tennis Association Chairman to Attend ITTF Meeting in London
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 4

    The Asian Vice-President of the International Table Tennis Federation and Chairman of the Chinese Table Tennis Association, Chen Xian, took a flight to Britain this morning to attend the meeting of the International Table Tennis Federation Advisory Committee to be held in London from April 17 to 18. Fu Qifang, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Table Tennis Association, also traveled to London on the same flight to attend the meeting of the ITTF's Grading and Grouping Committee.

### 我乒协主席赴伦敦参加国际乒联会议

1966-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我乒协主席赴伦敦参加国际乒联会议
    新华社十三日讯　国际乒乓球联合会亚洲副主席、中国乒乓球协会主席陈先今天上午乘飞机前往英国，出席四月十七日到十八日在伦敦举行的国际乒乓球联合会谘询委员会会议。中国乒乓球协会副主席傅其芳也同机前往伦敦，参加国际乒乓球联合会分级分组委员会会议。
