Title: Vice Chairman Song Receives Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: Vice Chairman Song Qingling received Miyazaki Seimin, Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.
    Present at the meeting was Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present. (Attached photo)
    Vice Chairman Song Qingling talks with Miyazaki Seimin (second from right), Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association.
   　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen

### 宋副主席接见日中友协理事长

1966-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    宋副主席接见日中友协理事长
    新华社三日讯　宋庆龄副主席今天下午接见了日中友好协会理事长宫崎世民，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中日友协会长廖承志。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
    宋庆龄副主席同日中友好协会理事长宫崎世民（右二）谈话。
   　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
