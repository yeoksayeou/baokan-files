Title: Transforming Barren and Acidic Land into Fertile Fields: Romania Achieves Success in Soil Improvement

Author:
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 3

    Transforming Barren and Acidic Land into Fertile Fields
    Romania Achieves Success in Soil Improvement
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest. In recent years, Romania has achieved some results in improving barren and acidic land.
    The states of Oltenia, Galați, Dobrogea, Crișana, etc. in Romania have a total of nearly 372,000 hectares of barren land. In order to transform these lands, Romanian scientists have adopted methods of solidification and fertilization, planted a large number of windbreaks, used diluted asphalt and ethylene acid to fix the soil, and applied nitrogen and phosphate fertilizers. As a result, the grapes and fruit trees planted on these lands have yielded good harvests. For example, the grape yield per hectare of such land in Oltenia has reached 6,000-17,000 kilograms.
    In addition, they also conducted experiments on 200,000 hectares of acidic land. Due to the application of lime powder, the yield of wheat and corn per hectare increased by 200-500 kilograms.

### 把贫瘠和酸性土地改造为良田  罗马尼亚改良土壤工作获成绩

1966-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    把贫瘠和酸性土地改造为良田
    罗马尼亚改良土壤工作获成绩
    新华社布加勒斯特电　罗马尼亚近几年来在改良贫瘠土地和酸性土地方面取得了一些成果。
    罗马尼亚奥尔登尼亚、加拉茨、多布罗加、克里沙纳等州共有将近三十七万二千公顷的贫瘠土地。为了改造这些土地，罗马尼亚科学工作者采用了加固化和施肥化的办法，大量种植了防风林，利用稀沥青、乙稀酸来固定土壤，并施上氮、磷肥。结果，使在这些土地上种植的葡萄和果树取得了好收成，如奥尔登尼亚州这类土地每公顷的葡萄产量达到了六千——一万七千公斤。
    另外，他们还在二十万公顷的酸性地上进行试验，由于施了石灰粉，使每公顷的小麦、玉米产量增加了二百——五百公斤。
