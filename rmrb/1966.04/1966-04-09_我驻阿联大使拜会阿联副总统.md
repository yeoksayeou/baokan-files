Title: Chinese Ambassador to the UAR Pays Courtesy Call on UAR Vice President
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 7th. Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, Huang Hua, paid a courtesy call on the UAR Vice President, Ali Sabri, here on the 7th. They held friendly talks.


**Named Entities**

中国	China
阿拉伯联合共和国	Arab Republic of Egypt (UAR)
黄华	Huang Hua
阿里·萨布里	Ali Sabri
开罗	Cairo
