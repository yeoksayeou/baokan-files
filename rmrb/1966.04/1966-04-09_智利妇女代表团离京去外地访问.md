Chilean Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Other Places
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. The Chilean Women's Delegation, led by Chilean Congresswoman Mrs. Carmen Lasso Carrera, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation, left Beijing by train today for visits to other places. Those who went to the station to see them off included Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and responsible persons from relevant departments, Luo Shuzhang, Cao Mengjun, Lin Ping, and others.
