Title: Revolution Forever Forward
Author: Tao Shimian
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 6

    Revolution Forever Forward
    Worker Tao Shimian
    How far is the road?
    Ask our iron feet.
    How many buildings are built?
    Look at the calluses on our hands.
    Another year of hard battle,
    Good news fills the sky.
    Projects spread across the wild hills,
    Power lines enter the deep mountains.
    Revolution carries a heavy burden,
    A surge of heroism fills our hearts.
    "Selected Works of Mao" in our bosom,
    The world is in our sights.
    The golden book guides the way,
    A wide road stretches out,
    Not afraid of wind and rain,
    Revolution forever forward!
  〔Selected from Metallurgical "Battle Report"〕

### 革命永向前
陶世绵
1966-04-26
第6版()
专栏：

    革命永向前	工人　陶世绵
    路走几多远？
    问咱铁脚板。
    楼盖几多座？
    看咱手上茧。
    鏖战又一年，
    捷报红满天。
    工程遍野岭，
    电线进深山。
    革命挑重担，
    豪情一腔满。
    “毛选”怀里揣，
    世界放眼看。
    金书把路引，
    一条大路宽，
    不怕风和雨，
    革命永向前！
  〔选自冶金《战报》〕
