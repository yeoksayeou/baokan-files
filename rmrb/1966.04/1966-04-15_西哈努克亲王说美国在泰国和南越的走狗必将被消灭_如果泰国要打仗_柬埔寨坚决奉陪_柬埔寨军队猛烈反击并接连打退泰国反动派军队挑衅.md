Title: Prince Sihanouk says the American running dogs in Thailand and South Vietnam will be eliminated. If Thailand wants to fight, Cambodia will resolutely accompany them. Cambodian troops fiercely counterattack and repeatedly repel provocations by the Thai reactionary army.
Author:
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 14th (report) According to the Cambodian News Agency on April 14th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk, speaking at a mass meeting in a village in the province of Prey Veng on the 12th, regarding the incident of Thai troops occupying the Preah Vihear temple, said: "Although we face the Thais who have been highly armed by their American masters, we six million Cambodians are determined to resist the enemy's attempts to conquer us, and are determined to make sacrifices if necessary, to safeguard the honor of our motherland."
    Prince Sihanouk said: "If the Thais want to fight, we will accompany them. If the Thais receive the support of the Americans, we also have the support of friends, especially our Chinese friends."
    He emphasized: "We are not afraid of these American running dogs, the Thais. We will never give up the Preah Vihear temple or any other piece of our territory." Prince Sihanouk pointed out: "They will inevitably be defeated, just like the American running dogs—the Vietnamese in Saigon and the American imperialists themselves." He said: "All these traitors in Saigon and Bangkok will be eliminated."
    He said: "As for us, we continue to move forward along the path we have already taken, maintain close unity, and victory will surely belong to us."
    He emphasized: "This is my firm belief, and it is also the firm belief of all Cambodian people."
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 13th (report) According to the Cambodian News Agency on April 13th, on April 6th, Thai troops once again used heavy weapons and automatic weapons to fire at the Cheam Krasaing outpost in Koh Kong province, Cambodia. Under the fierce counterattack of the Cambodian troops, the Thai invaders were forced to stop firing.
    On the same day, in the morning, two Thai jet fighters repeatedly flew over the military facilities in Cham Yeam area of Koh Kong province, Cambodia. In the evening, a group of Thai troops, under the cover of heavy weapons, frantically attacked a construction site of a Cambodian army outpost in Oddar Meanchey province. They engaged in a half-hour exchange of fire with the Cambodian troops before being forced to retreat into Thai territory.
    On the 5th, the Thai armed forces at the Khla Khlok outpost once again fired rifles at the Cambodian soldiers guarding the Cheam Krasaing forward outpost. The Cambodian soldiers launched a fierce counterattack.
    On the 11th, Thai troops, under the cover of naval gunfire, fired at the Kiri Vong outpost in Koh Kong province, Cambodia. The Cambodian outpost soldiers returned fire.

### 西哈努克亲王说美国在泰国和南越的走狗必将被消灭  如果泰国要打仗　柬埔寨坚决奉陪  柬埔寨军队猛烈反击并接连打退泰国反动派军队挑衅

1966-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王说美国在泰国和南越的走狗必将被消灭
    如果泰国要打仗　柬埔寨坚决奉陪
    柬埔寨军队猛烈反击并接连打退泰国反动派军队挑衅
    新华社金边十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王十二日在波萝勉省的一个村庄中举行的群众大会上谈到泰国军队侵占隆端古寺事件的时候说：“尽管我们面前是已经被他们的美国主子高度武装起来的泰国人，我们六百万柬埔寨人决心抗击敌人征服我们的企图，并且在必要时决心作出牺牲，以维护祖国的荣誉。”
    西哈努克亲王说：“如果泰国人要打仗，我们就奉陪。如果泰国人得到美国人的支持，我们也有朋友的支援，特别是我们的中国朋友。”
    他强调指出：“我们不怕这些美国的走狗泰国人。我们根本不会放弃隆端古寺或者我国的其他一块领土。”西哈努克亲王指出：“他们必将遭到失败，如同美国人的走狗——西贡的越南人和美帝国主义者自身一样。”他说：“西贡和曼谷的所有这些卖国贼必将被消灭”。
    他说：“至于我们，我们继续沿着已经走过的道路前进，保持紧密团结，胜利必将属于我们。”
    他强调说：“这就是我的坚定信念，也是全体柬埔寨人民的坚定信念。”
    据新华社金边十三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十三日报道，四月六日，泰国军队再次用重武器和自动武器向柬埔寨戈公省奇内萨哨所射击。在柬埔寨军队猛烈的回击下，泰国侵略者被迫停止射击。
    同一天早晨，两架泰国喷气式飞机多次飞越柬埔寨戈公省昌扬地方的军事设施上空。到了晚上，一批泰国军队在重武器的掩护下，疯狂进攻奥多棉藏省柬埔寨军队的一个哨所建筑工地。他们同柬埔寨军队进行了半小时的交火后，被迫退回泰国境内。
    五日，哈勒哨所的泰国武装部队用步枪再次射击守卫奇内萨前沿哨所的柬埔寨士兵。柬埔寨士兵进行了猛烈回击。
    十一日，泰国军队在军舰炮火的掩护下，向柬埔寨戈公省吉里翁哨所射击。柬埔寨哨所士兵给予了回击。
