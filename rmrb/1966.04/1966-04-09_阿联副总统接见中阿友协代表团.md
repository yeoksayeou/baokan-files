Title: UAR Vice President Receives Delegation from the Chinese-Arab Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo - UAR Vice President and Secretary-General of the Arab Socialist Union, Ali Sabri, and Vice President and Prime Minister Zakaria Mohieddin, separately received here on the 6th the delegation from the Chinese-Arab Friendship Association led by Zhang Jie, and held friendly talks with them.
    During the meetings, both Sabri and Mohieddin expressed their hope to strengthen the long-standing friendship and economic and cultural relations between the two countries.
    Present at the meetings was Hassan Ashmawi, Secretary-General of the UAR-China Friendship Association. Huang Hua, Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, was also present.
    Earlier, Ahmed Hassan al-Baquri, President of Al-Azhar University, hosted a luncheon in honor of the delegation. Present at the luncheon were UAR Deputy Prime Minister Sharabassi and Chinese Ambassador Huang Hua. The Supreme Council for Islamic Affairs of the UAR also hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation. The delegation has visited Cairo, Port Said, Aswan, Luxor and other cities in the past two weeks.

### 阿联副总统接见中阿友协代表团

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联副总统接见中阿友协代表团
    新华社开罗电　阿联副总统、阿拉伯社会主义联盟总书记阿里·萨布里和副总统兼总理查卡里亚·毛希丁六日分别在这里接见由张杰率领的中国阿联友好协会代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时，萨布里和毛希丁都表示希望加强两国之间悠久的友谊以及经济和文化关系。
    接见时在座的有：阿联—中国友好协会秘书长哈桑·阿什马维。中国驻阿联大使黄华也在座。
    早些时候，爱资哈尔大学校长艾哈迈德·哈桑·巴库里举行午宴招待代表团。出席午宴的有：阿联副总理沙拉巴西和中国大使黄华。阿联伊斯兰事务最高委员会也曾经设宴欢迎代表团。代表团在过去两周中访问了开罗、塞得港、阿斯旺、路克苏尔和其他城市。
