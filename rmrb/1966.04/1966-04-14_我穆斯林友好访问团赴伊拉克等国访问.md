Title: My Muslim Friendly Delegation Visits Iraq and Other Countries

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- The Chinese Muslim Friendly Delegation, led by Deputy Head Shen Xiaxi, left Beijing by plane today for friendly visits to countries including Iraq, Kuwait, Sudan, and Pakistan.
    The head of the delegation, Zhang Jie, had previously left Beijing.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included responsible individuals from relevant departments, such as Sha Mengbi, Liu Pinyi, and Liu Ying.

### 我穆斯林友好访问团赴伊拉克等国访问

1966-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林友好访问团赴伊拉克等国访问
    新华社十二日讯　中国穆斯林友好访问团在副团长沈遐熙率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往伊拉克、科威特、苏丹、巴基斯坦等国家进行友好访问。
    访问团团长张杰已先期离开北京。
    到机场送行的，有沙梦弼、刘品一、刘郢等有关方面负责人。
