Title: Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau Issues Statement, Decides to Convene Emergency Meeting of Afro-Asian Writers in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 17th. The Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau issued a statement here on the 16th, saying that the Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau has decided to convene an emergency meeting of Afro-Asian writers in Beijing this June to discuss issues such as supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.
    The statement, signed by Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau Secretary-General Senanayake, said: "After soliciting the opinions of member states, the Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau has decided to convene an emergency meeting of Afro-Asian writers in Beijing this June to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national liberation. This meeting will also oppose the cultural aggression carried out by imperialism and neo-colonialism on these two continents." The statement also said that preparations for the meeting will begin in Beijing on April 20.

### 亚非作家会议常设局发表声明  决定在北京召开亚非作家紧急会议

1966-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非作家会议常设局发表声明
    决定在北京召开亚非作家紧急会议
    新华社科伦坡十七日电  亚非作家会议常设局十六日在这里发表声明说，亚非作家会议常设局决定今年六月在北京召开亚非作家紧急会议，讨论支持越南人民抗美救国斗争等问题。
    由亚非作家会议常设局秘书长森纳那亚克签署的这项声明说：“亚非作家会议常设局在征求各成员国意见以后，决定今年六月在北京召开亚非作家紧急会议，支持越南人民反对美国侵略和争取民族解放的斗争。这次会议还将反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义对这两大洲进行的文化侵略。”声明还说，这次会议的筹备工作将在四月二十日在北京开始进行。
