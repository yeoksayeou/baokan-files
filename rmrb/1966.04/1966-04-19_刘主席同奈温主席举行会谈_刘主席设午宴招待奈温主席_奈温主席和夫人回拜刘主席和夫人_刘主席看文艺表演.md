Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Holds Talks with Chairman Ne Win; Chairman Liu Hosts Luncheon for Chairman Ne Win; Chairman Ne Win and Wife Return Visit to Chairman Liu and Wife; Chairman Liu Watches Cultural Performance
Author:
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th – Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, held talks here this morning.

    The talks proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

    Present on the Chinese side at the talks were: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Wang Daohan, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Geng Biao. Present on the Burmese side at the talks were: Minister of Foreign Affairs U Thi Han, Minister of Education and Health Colonel La Han, Minister of Industry Colonel Maung Shwe, Secretary General of the Revolutionary Council Colonel Ko Ko, and Secretary of the Ministry of Industry Maung Maung Kha.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th – Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, hosted a luncheon here at the State Guest House on the 18th in honor of General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma.

    The luncheon proceeded in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere. During the meal, the hosts and guests conversed happily and frequently toasted the further development of the profound friendship between Burma and China.

    Attending the luncheon were: Burmese Minister of Foreign Affairs U Thi Han, as well as Colonel La Han, Colonel Maung Shwe, Colonel Ko Ko, and Maung Maung Kha. Also attending the luncheon were: Chinese Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Wang Daohan, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Geng Biao.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th – General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, and his wife paid a return visit to Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his wife at the State Guest House here on the morning of the 18th.

    Accompanying Chairman Ne Win on the return visit were: Minister of Foreign Affairs U Thi Han and his wife, Minister of Education and Health Colonel La Han, Minister of Industry Colonel Maung Shwe, Secretary General of the Revolutionary Council Colonel Ko Ko, and Secretary of the Ministry of Industry Maung Maung Kha. Present during the return visit were: Chinese Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Wang Daohan, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Geng Biao and his wife.

    The hosts and guests held talks in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 17th – Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, accompanied by Chairman Ne Win and his wife, watched a cultural performance at the open-air theater in the gardens of the State Palace here on the evening of the 17th.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other Chinese guests also watched the performance.

    Accompanying the Chinese guests to watch the performance were: members of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma and their wives, Minister of Foreign Affairs U Thi Han and his wife.

    The beautiful national music and dance performed this evening – mainly classical programs – demonstrated the characteristics of the traditional Burmese dance art with a long history.

    Today, countless sparkling colored light bulbs were hung on every tree in the gardens of the State Palace, some of the light bulbs resembling strings of bright and colorful grapes. Rows of colorful lanterns were hung on both sides of the open-air theater, adding a charming color to the beautiful scenery.

    After the performance, Chairman Liu and his wife, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, accompanied by Chairman Ne Win and his wife, went on stage, shook hands with the artists one by one, and presented them with flower baskets. (Picture attached)

### 刘主席同奈温主席举行会谈  刘主席设午宴招待奈温主席  奈温主席和夫人回拜刘主席和夫人　刘主席看文艺表演

1966-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席同奈温主席举行会谈
    刘主席设午宴招待奈温主席
    奈温主席和夫人回拜刘主席和夫人　刘主席看文艺表演
    新华社仰光十八日电　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温今天上午在这里举行会谈。
    会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    会谈时中国方面在座的有：副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵和中国驻缅甸大使耿飚。会谈时缅甸方面在座的有：外交部长吴蒂汉，教育和卫生部长拉汉上校，工业部长貌瑞上校，革命委员会秘书长郭哥上校和工业部秘书貌貌卡上校。
    新华社仰光十八日电　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇十八日在这里的国宾馆设午宴招待缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军。
    宴会是在非常亲切友好的气氛中进行的。席间，宾主愉快地交谈，并为进一步发展缅中两国的深厚友谊频频祝酒。
    出席宴会的有：缅甸外交部长吴蒂汉以及拉汉上校、貌瑞上校、郭哥上校和貌貌卡上校。出席宴会的还有：中国副总理陈毅、外交部副部长章汉夫、对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵和中国驻缅甸大使耿飚。
    新华社仰光十八日电　缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军和夫人十八日上午到这里的国宾馆回拜了中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人。
    陪同奈温主席回拜的有：外交部长吴蒂汉和夫人，教育和卫生部长拉汉上校，工业部长貌瑞上校，革命委员会秘书长郭哥上校和工业部秘书貌貌卡上校。回拜时在座的有：中国副总理陈毅和夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵和中国驻缅甸大使耿飚和夫人。
    宾主在亲切友好的气氛中进行了谈话。
    新华社仰光十七日电　刘少奇主席和夫人由奈温主席和夫人陪同，十七日晚上在这里的国家宫花园里的露天剧场观看了文艺表演。
    陈毅副总理和夫人、外交部副部长章汉夫和其他中国客人也观看了表演。
    陪同中国客人观看演出的有：缅甸联邦革命委员会委员们和他们的夫人、外交部长吴蒂汉和夫人。
    今晚演出的优美的民族音乐和舞蹈——主要是古典的节目——表现了具有悠久历史的缅甸传统舞蹈艺术的特点。
    今天，国家宫花园里的每棵树上都挂着无数闪闪发光的彩色灯泡，有些灯泡象一串串亮晶晶的多彩的葡萄。露天剧场两旁挂着一行行的彩色灯笼，这种布置给美丽的景色增添了迷人的色彩。
    表演结束后，刘主席和夫人，陈毅副总理和夫人在奈温主席和夫人陪同下登上舞台，同艺术家们一一握手，并且向他们赠送了花篮。（附图片）
