Title: Soviet Government Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Soviet Government Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The Soviet Government Trade Delegation led by Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Grishin, returned home by plane on the 27th. Those who saw them off at the airport included Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others. Soviet Ambassador to China, Lapin, and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 苏联政府贸易代表团离京回国

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府贸易代表团离京回国
    据新华社讯　由苏联对外贸易部副部长格里申率领的苏联政府贸易代表团，二十七日乘飞机回国。到机场送行的，有对外贸易部副部长李强等。苏联驻华大使拉宾等也到机场送行。
