Title: Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament Opens in Beijing; Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi Receives Leaders of Participating Teams

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 3

    Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization
    Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament Opens in Beijing
    Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi Receives Leaders of Participating Teams
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 15) The Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization opened tonight at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium.
    Tonight, behind the rostrum in the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium, stood the national flags of the seven socialist countries participating in the competition. At 7:30 PM, as the teams from the Albanian Ministry of Internal Affairs "Dinamo," the Bulgarian "Spartak," the Sports Guidance Committee of the Ministry of Public Security of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Polish "Guard," the Bucharest "Dinamo" Sports Club of Romania, the Czechoslovak "Red Star," and the Chinese "Qianwei" successively entered the competition hall of the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium with the tournament flag leading the way amidst music, rhythmic applause erupted from the audience. Afterwards, Young Pioneers presented flowers to the participating teams.
    Yang Qiqing, Vice Minister of Public Security and Director of the Chinese "Qianwei" Sports Association, delivered the opening speech. He extended a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese "Qianwei" Sports Association to the participating teams and wished the tournament a complete success.
    The first match tonight was between Bulgaria and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The Korean team lost with a score of 58 to 70.
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 15) Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Public Security Xie Fuzhi received the leaders of the basketball teams from various countries participating in the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization tonight. They were: Lieutenant Colonel Nesdet Take of Albania, Colonel Mircho Langlov of Bulgaria, Major General Han Qianqiu of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Major General Nicolae Kiriac of Romania, Major Frantisek Vostry of Czechoslovakia, and Long Qian of China. Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi had friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Yang Qiqing, Vice Minister of Public Security and Director of the Chinese "Qianwei" Sports Association; Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; and Ma Xingwu, Director of the Organizing Committee of the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization.
    Also present at the reception were the envoys of the relevant countries participating in this competition to China. (With Picture)
    The Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization opened at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium on April 15. Our reporter Jiang Duo.

### 社会主义国家公安体育组织  第七届男子篮球比赛在京开幕  谢富治副总理接见各国代表队领队

1966-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家公安体育组织
    第七届男子篮球比赛在京开幕
    谢富治副总理接见各国代表队领队
    据新华社十五日讯　社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会，今晚在北京工人体育馆开幕。
    今晚，在北京工人体育馆的大会主席台后面，树立着参加这次比赛的七个社会主义国家的国旗。七时半，当阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”代表队、保加利亚“斯巴达克”代表队、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国社会安全省体育指导委员会代表队、波兰“近卫军”代表队代表、罗马尼亚布加勒斯特“狄纳莫”体育俱乐部代表队、捷克斯洛伐克“红星”代表队和中国“前卫”代表队，各以会旗为先导，在乐曲声中先后走进北京工人体育馆比赛馆时，观众席上响起了有节奏的掌声。之后，少先队员们向各国代表队献了花。
    公安部副部长、中国“前卫”体育协会主任杨奇清致开幕词。他代表中国“前卫”体育协会向参加这次比赛的各国代表队，表示热烈的欢迎，并预祝这次比赛圆满成功。
    今晚出战首场的是保加利亚队同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队。朝鲜队以五十八比七十失利。
    新华社十五日讯　国务院副总理兼公安部部长谢富治，今晚接见了参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛的各国篮球代表队的领队。他们是：阿尔巴尼亚的内斯德特·塔凯中校，保加利亚的米尔乔·朗格洛夫上校，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的韩千秋少将，罗马尼亚的尼古拉·基里亚克少将，捷克斯洛伐克的弗朗齐歇克·沃斯特里少校，和中国的龙潜。谢富治副总理同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有公安部副部长、中国“前卫”体育协会主任杨奇清，国家体委副主任荣高棠，社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会组织委员会主任马星五等。
    接见时，参加这次比赛的各有关国家的驻华使节等也在座。（附图片）
    四月十五日，社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛在北京工人体育馆开幕。　本报记者　蒋铎摄
