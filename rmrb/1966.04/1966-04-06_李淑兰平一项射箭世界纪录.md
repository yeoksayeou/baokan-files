Title: Li Shulan Ties Archery World Record
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 5th - Li Shulan, a female archery athlete from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, achieved an excellent score of 329 rings in the women's 30-meter single round (total of 36 arrows) competition during the friendly archery match between the People's Liberation Army, Qinghai, and Inner Mongolia this afternoon, tying the world record held by American Vonderhide. This score also broke Li Shulan's own national record of 328 rings.
    
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou - On the morning of the 2nd, Li Shulan, an archery athlete from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, broke the national record of 297 rings in the women's 60-meter single round, previously held by Sun Chunlan of Shanghai, with a score of 299 rings in the women's 60-meter single round competition held in Guangzhou as part of the friendly archery match between the People's Liberation Army, Qinghai, and Inner Mongolia.

### 李淑兰平一项射箭世界纪录

1966-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    李淑兰平一项射箭世界纪录
    新华社广州五日电　中国人民解放军女子射箭选手李淑兰今天下午在解放军、青海、内蒙古三单位射箭友谊赛的女子三十米单轮（共三十六箭）比赛中，以三百二十九环的优异成绩平由美国冯德海德所保持的这项世界纪录。这个成绩同时打破了李淑兰本人创造的三百二十八环的全国纪录。
    据新华社广州电　中国人民解放军射箭选手李淑兰二日上午在广州举行的解放军、青海、内蒙古三单位射箭友谊赛的女子六十米单轮比赛中，以二百九十九环的成绩，打破了上海选手孙春兰保持的女子六十米单轮二百九十七环全国纪录。
