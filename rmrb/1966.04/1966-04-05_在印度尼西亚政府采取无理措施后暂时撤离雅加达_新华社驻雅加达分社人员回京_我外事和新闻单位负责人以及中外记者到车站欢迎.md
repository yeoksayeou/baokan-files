Title: After the Indonesian Government Took Unreasonable Measures, the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch Personnel Temporarily Evacuated Jakarta and Returned to Beijing. Responsible Persons from Our Foreign Affairs and News Units, as well as Chinese and Foreign Journalists, Welcomed Them at the Station.
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th - The reporters and staff of the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch, who temporarily evacuated Jakarta and returned to China, returned to Beijing this morning, led by Zhang Haitao, the director of the branch.

    The reporters and staff of the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch evacuated Jakarta by plane on April 1st, after the Indonesian government unreasonably announced the "temporary closure" of the Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch.

    Those who came to the station to welcome them today included Hao Deqing, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shi Huaibi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Affairs Commission, Wu Lengxi, President of Xinhua News Agency, Vice Presidents Miao Haileng, Deng Gang, Wang Minzhao, Fan Jin, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Vice Mayor of Beijing, Chen Jun and Wang Yi, Deputy Editors-in-Chief of the *People's Daily*, Gu Wenhua, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Bureau, and the heads of other news units in the capital, Chang Zhiqing, Mu Xin, Shi Mai, Lü Liang, Ke Zaishuo, as well as journalists from the capital.

    After Comrade Zhang Haitao and others got off the train, responsible persons from various departments and journalists from the capital expressed their warm welcome and cordial greetings to them, and presented them with flowers.

    Abu Kus (Syria), Chen Quanbi (China), Manuvila (Ceylon), Morrison (South Africa), and Sugiyama Ichibei (Japan), the resident secretaries of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association who came to Beijing for a temporary meeting, came to the station to welcome them.

    Also present to welcome them were journalists from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency stationed in China, as well as some journalists from other countries in Beijing.

    Comrade Zhang Haitao and others returned to Guangzhou by plane on April 1st. They were welcomed by Zeng Sheng and Wang Lanxi, Vice Governors of Guangdong Province, Luo Peiyuan, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and responsible persons of news units in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City.

    When Comrade Zhang Haitao and others passed through Phnom Penh on April 1st, the cultural counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia and the Xinhua News Agency reporter in Phnom Penh welcomed and saw them off at the airport.

### 在印度尼西亚政府采取无理措施后暂时撤离雅加达  新华社驻雅加达分社人员回京  我外事和新闻单位负责人以及中外记者到车站欢迎

1966-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    在印度尼西亚政府采取无理措施后暂时撤离雅加达
    新华社驻雅加达分社人员回京
    我外事和新闻单位负责人以及中外记者到车站欢迎
    新华社四日讯  暂时撤离雅加达回国的新华社驻雅加达分社记者和工作人员，在分社社长张海涛率领下，今天上午回到北京。
    新华社驻雅加达分社记者和工作人员，是在印度尼西亚政府无理宣布“暂时封闭”新华社驻雅加达分社以后，于四月一日乘飞机撤离雅加达的。
    今天到车站欢迎的，有国务院外事办公室副主任郝德青，外交部副部长韩念龙，对外文委副主任史怀璧，新华社社长吴冷西，副社长缪海棱、邓岗、王敏昭，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席、北京市副市长范瑾，《人民日报》副总编辑陈浚、王揖，广播事业局副局长顾文华，首都其他新闻单位的负责人常芝青、穆欣、史迈、吕梁、柯在铄，以及首都新闻工作者。
    张海涛等同志走下火车以后，各方面的负责人和首都新闻工作者向他们表示热烈欢迎和亲切慰问，并向他们献了花。
    来北京暂时会合的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处驻会书记阿布库斯（叙利亚）、陈泉璧（中国）、曼努维拉（锡兰）、摩里逊（南非）、杉山市平（日本），到车站欢迎。
    前往欢迎的，还有朝鲜、越南民主共和国、越南南方解放通讯社驻中国的记者，以及在北京的其他国家的一些新闻工作者。
    张海涛等同志是在四月一日乘飞机回到广州的。他们受到广东省副省长曾生、王阑西，广州市副市长罗培元，以及广东省和广州市各新闻单位负责人的欢迎。
    张海涛等同志四月一日路过金边的时候，中国驻柬埔寨大使馆文化参赞和新华社驻金边记者，到机场欢迎和欢送他们。
