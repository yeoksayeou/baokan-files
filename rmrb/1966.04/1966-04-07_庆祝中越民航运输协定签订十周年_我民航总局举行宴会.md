Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Air Transport Agreement, Civil Aviation Administration of China Hosts Banquet
Author:
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 5

    To Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Air Transport Agreement
    Civil Aviation Administration of China Hosts Banquet
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th — Zhang Xisan, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, held a banquet this evening to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Air Transport Agreement.
    Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, along with embassy officials, and Le Tung Son, acting head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation to China, were invited to attend the banquet. Zhang Xisan and Ambassador Tran Tu Binh delivered speeches at the banquet. They warmly praised the great fighting friendship between the people of China and Vietnam, which is built on the foundation of Marxism-Leninism, and wished for the growing development of cooperative relations between the civil aviation industries of the two countries.
    Among those attending the banquet were Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as Yu Lijin, Peng Deqing, Li Ming, Sun Xiaocun, Pan Zhenwu, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 庆祝中越民航运输协定签订十周年  我民航总局举行宴会

1966-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中越民航运输协定签订十周年
    我民航总局举行宴会
    新华社六日讯　中国民用航空总局副局长张西三今晚举行宴会，热烈庆祝中越民用航空运输协定签订十周年。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平和使馆官员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山，应邀出席了宴会。张西三和陈子平大使在宴会上致词。他们热情称颂建立在马克思列宁主义基础上的中越两国人民的伟大战斗友谊，并祝两国民航事业的合作关系日益发展。
    出席宴会的，有外交部副部长韩念龙，以及余立金、彭德清、黎明、孙晓村、潘振武等有关方面负责人。
