Title: Muslims in Ürümqi and Yining Celebrate Corban Festival
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Ürümqi, 2nd—Muslims of various nationalities who believe in Islam in Ürümqi and Yining City, Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, joyfully celebrated the annual traditional festival - the Corban Festival on the 1st.
    On the morning of the 1st, Muslims and Ahongs of various nationalities in Ürümqi held congregational prayer ceremonies in various mosque neighborhoods. In order to allow cadres, workers and peasants of various nationalities who believe in Islam to celebrate the festival, the various agencies, factories and suburban people's communes in Ürümqi City were given a three-day holiday. On the 1st, people of all nationalities wearing festive costumes visited each other to celebrate the festival. Han cadres, workers and residents also visited the homes of fraternal nationalities who believe in Islam to pay their respects for the festival, and the atmosphere of national unity was everywhere.
    On the morning of the 1st, tens of thousands of Muslims in Yining City held religious ceremonies in the famous mosques. After the congregational prayer, people wearing festive costumes of various nationalities visited each other to celebrate the festival, greeting each other with "Salam" (peace). On this day, Yining City was full of songs and joy. Muslims all went to their favorite places to visit and tour. All of the city's forty-plus cultural rooms were open. Many people gathered here to participate in various cultural and recreational activities.
    In order for the Muslims in Ürümqi and Yining to celebrate the festival, the commercial departments provided more ethnic specialty commodities than in previous years.

### 乌鲁木齐、伊宁市穆斯林欢度古尔邦节

1966-04-05
第2版()
专栏：

    乌鲁木齐、伊宁市穆斯林欢度古尔邦节
    新华社乌鲁木齐二日电　新疆维吾尔自治区乌鲁木齐和伊宁市信仰伊斯兰教的各民族的穆斯林们，一日欢欢乐乐地度过了一年一度的传统节日——古尔邦节。
    乌鲁木齐各民族的穆斯林和阿訇们，在一日清晨到各清真寺坊举行了聚礼仪式。为了让各民族信仰伊斯兰教的干部、工人和农民欢度节日，乌鲁木齐市各机关、工厂和郊区人民公社，放假三天。一日，穿着节日盛装的各民族人民，互相走访贺节。汉族干部、工人和居民也纷纷到信仰伊斯兰教各兄弟民族家中拜节，到处洋溢着民族团结的气氛。
    一日清晨，伊宁市有数万名穆斯林分别在著名的清真寺举行了宗教仪式。聚礼完毕，穿着各民族节日盛装的人们，互相走访贺节，问候“萨拉姆”（平安）。这天，伊宁市到处歌声嘹亮，喜气洋洋。穆斯林们都到自己喜爱的地方去参观和游览。全市的四十多个文化室全部开放。很多人都在这里欢聚，参加各种文化娱乐活动。
    乌鲁木齐和伊宁的商业部门为了穆斯林们欢度节日，供应了比往年更多的民族特需商品。
