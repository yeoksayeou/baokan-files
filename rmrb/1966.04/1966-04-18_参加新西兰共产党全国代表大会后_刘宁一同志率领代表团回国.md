Title: Comrade Liu Ningyi Leads Delegation Back to China After Attending the National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Auckland, 17th: The delegation of the Chinese Communist Party, led by Comrade Liu Ningyi, which attended the 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand, left New Zealand on the 16th to return to China.
    Among those who went to see them off were Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, and other responsible comrades, including Nownes, Austerley, and MacLeod. After attending the 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand, Liu Ningyi and others visited Rotorua and Christchurch, where they were warmly received by local comrades of the Communist Party of New Zealand and friends from all walks of life.

### 参加新西兰共产党全国代表大会后  刘宁一同志率领代表团回国

1966-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    参加新西兰共产党全国代表大会后
    刘宁一同志率领代表团回国
    新华社十七日讯　奥克兰消息：参加新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会的中国共产党代表团在团长刘宁一率领下十六日离开新西兰回国。
    前往欢送的有新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯和其他负责同志纽恩斯、奥斯特勒、麦克劳德等。刘宁一等在参加新共第二十一次全国代表大会以后，参观访问了罗托鲁阿和克赖斯特彻奇等城市，受到当地新共同志们和各方面朋友们的热情招待。
