Title: Chairman Liu Sends Telegram to President Ayub Khan, Thanking the Pakistani Government and People for Their Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, April 17th (Electric Dispatch) Chairman Liu Shaoqi sent a telegram to Pakistani President Ayub Khan on April 17th, upon concluding his friendly visit to Pakistan and departing from the country, expressing his gratitude. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Dhaka:
    As we conclude our visit and leave the territory of Pakistan, my wife and I, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and all accompanying personnel once again extend our heartfelt gratitude to Your Excellency the President and Madame, and to the government and people of Pakistan. The grand reception and warm welcome given to us by the Pakistani government and people embodies the sincere friendship of the Pakistani people towards the Chinese people. We are deeply moved. May the friendship between the Chinese and Pakistani peoples grow stronger day by day. We wish Pakistan continued new achievements on the road of independent development.
                  Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                        April 17, 1966

### 刘主席致电阿尤布·汗总统  感谢巴基斯坦政府和人民的隆重接待

1966-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席致电阿尤布·汗总统
    感谢巴基斯坦政府和人民的隆重接待
    新华社达卡十七日电　刘少奇主席十七日在结束对巴基斯坦的友好访问离开巴基斯坦的时候，打电报给巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗，表示感谢。电报全文如下：达卡巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅阁下：
    在我们结束访问离开巴基斯坦国境的时候，我和我的夫人、陈毅副总理和夫人以及全体随行人员再一次向总统阁下和夫人、向巴基斯坦政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。巴基斯坦政府和人民给予我们的隆重的接待和热烈的欢迎，体现了巴基斯坦人民对中国人民的真挚友谊。我们深为感动。祝中巴两国人民的友谊日益增强。祝巴基斯坦在独立发展的道路上不断取得新的成就。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                        一九六六年四月十七日
