Title: International Strike News

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 4

    International Strike News
    Strike of Agricultural Workers in California, USA
    San Francisco Dispatch: The strike of more than five thousand vineyard workers in the Delano area of central California, USA, entered its eighth month on the 9th. The demonstration march to the state capital Sacramento, initiated by the striking workers, also successfully reached its destination on the 10th, lasting twenty-five days and covering approximately four hundred miles.
    This is the largest agricultural strike in California since the 1930s. The workers are demanding that monopoly capitalist enterprises and vineyard owners increase wages, improve working and living conditions, and recognize union rights. They have received enthusiastic support and solidarity from people and groups throughout California.
    French Airline Flight Personnel Hold Strike
    Paris Dispatch: Two thousand five hundred aircraft pilots and other flight personnel of Air France began a two-day strike on the 8th, paralyzing air traffic in Paris.
    This is the second two-day strike by flight personnel in two weeks. They are demanding a 5% wage increase and negotiations with the government to sign a new contract.
    Workers at US-owned Enterprise in British Guiana Go-Slow
    Georgetown Dispatch: Workers at the US-owned Reynolds Metals Company in Everton and Kwakwani, British Guiana, disregarding the capitalists' threats to close the mines, continue to persist in their go-slow, opposing the capitalists' policy of cruel oppression and racial discrimination.
    After three weeks of go-slow by workers at the bauxite mines and factories in these two locations, the company authorities brazenly closed all mines and factories on the 2nd, threatening them to stop the go-slow struggle.
    South Korean Workers Employed by US Military Hold General Strike
    Seoul Dispatch: Twenty-five thousand Korean workers employed by US military organizations in Seoul and twelve other locations in South Korea held a 24-hour general strike on the 6th, demanding increased wages and improved living conditions. These striking workers have been waging a struggle against the US military authorities since last year. Now they are demanding that the US military authorities honor their promise made on March 2 to increase workers' wages by 15 to 20 percent.

### 国际罢工要闻

1966-04-13
第4版()
专栏：国际罢工要闻

    国际罢工要闻
    美国加利福尼亚州农业工人罢工
    旧金山消息：美国加利福尼亚州中部德兰诺地区五千多名葡萄园工人的大罢工，在九日进入第八个月。由罢工工人们发起的向州首府萨克拉门托进军的示威队伍，也在十日胜利到达目的地，前后历时二十五天，路程约四百英里。
    这是加利福尼亚州三十年代以来最大的农业罢工。工人们要求垄断资本企业和葡萄园主增加工资、改善劳动条件和居住条件，并承认工会权利。他们得到了加利福尼亚州各地人民和团体的热烈支持和声援。
    法国航空公司飞行人员举行罢工
    巴黎消息：法国航空公司的二千五百名飞机驾驶员和其他空勤人员八日开始举行两天的罢工，使巴黎的空中交通陷于瘫痪。
    这是空勤人员在两周中举行的第二次为期两天的罢工，他们要求增加工资百分之五，要求同政府举行签订新合同的谈判。
    英属圭亚那美资企业工人怠工
    乔治敦消息：英属圭亚那美资雷诺斯金属公司在埃佛尔敦和克瓦克瓦尼两地的工人，不顾资方以关闭厂矿相恫吓，继续坚持怠工，反对资方实行残酷压迫和种族歧视的政策。
    在这两个地方的铁矾土矿场和工厂的工人怠工了三个星期之后，公司当局二日悍然封闭全部矿场和工厂，要挟他们停止怠工斗争。
    南朝鲜美军雇佣工人举行总罢工
    汉城消息：在汉城和南朝鲜其他十二个地方的美军组织雇佣的二万五千名朝鲜工人六日举行二十四小时总罢工，要求增加工资和改善生活待遇。这些罢工工人从去年以来就向美国军事当局展开了斗争。现在他们要求美军当局信守在三月二日作出的把工人工资增加百分之十五到二十的诺言。
