Title: Prince Sihanouk Says He Rejects the "Mekong River Committee" Aid Plan: Cambodia Would Rather Live in Poverty to Maintain Independence
Author:
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 22nd (Dispatch) According to the Cambodian News Agency on April 22nd, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk said in Phnom Penh on the 19th: “The United Nations Organization and some major powers hope that we will participate with Thailand, Laos, and South Vietnam in their plan to develop the lower Mekong River basin,” but “we ask it (the United Nations) to stop mentioning this Mekong River dam project to us. We neither want to be ‘Americanized,’ nor do we want to be bound by anyone.”
    Prince Sihanouk made this speech in Phnom Penh when awarding diplomas to new administrators of agriculture, water conservancy, forestry, and fisheries. He pointed out that on the issue of the Mekong River dam project, the fox tail of American imperialism has been revealed. We have seen the harmful role the Americans play in India, and how they demand that India, in fact, abandon its sovereignty to obtain US aid.
    Prince Sihanouk said: "The Americans control the 'Mekong River Committee,' and without their permission, this committee simply cannot do anything. Assuming the committee's plan is implemented, we will lose our independence like India. American imperialists will come back and act like overlords here."
    He said: "In this case, our answer is: we are not interested in this trickery." "Cambodia does not want to follow in India's footsteps. It wants to maintain its independence and would rather live in poverty."
    He also said that Cambodia will build ten small dams itself, which is enough for our farmers. If someone wants us to do this and do that, we will tell them: don't bother, thank you!
    Prince Sihanouk said that immediately after rejecting US aid, Cambodia had a surplus in foreign trade in 1964 and 1965, but this year, due to reduced agricultural production, foreign trade will have a deficit. He said that Cambodia must limit imports to reduce the deficit. "We must meet our country's consumption with our own products, instead of products we have to import from abroad."

### 西哈努克亲王表示拒绝“湄公河委员会”援助计划  柬埔寨宁愿过穷日子也要保持独立

1966-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王表示拒绝“湄公河委员会”援助计划
    柬埔寨宁愿过穷日子也要保持独立
    新华社金边二十二日电据柬埔寨新闻社二十二日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王十九日在金边说：“联合国组织和一些大国希望我们同泰国、老挝和南越一起参加他们的开发湄公河下游盆地的计划”，但是“我们要求它（联合国）别再跟我们提这个湄公河水坝计划了。我们既不愿意把我们‘美国化’，也不愿意受任何人的束缚。”
    西哈努克亲王是在金边给农业、水利、林业和渔业的新的管理人员颁发文凭时发表这讲话的。他指出，在湄公河水坝计划的问题上，美帝国主义的狐狸尾巴已经露出来了。我们看到了美国人在印度扮演的害人角色，看到了他们是怎么样要求印度在事实上放弃主权而取得美援的。
    西哈努克亲王说：“美国人控制着‘湄公河委员会’，不得到他们的许可，这个委员会就根本不能办任何事情。我们假设这个委员会的计划付诸实施，那我们就会象印度一样丧失独立。美帝国主义者就会卷土重来，要在我们这里作威作福了。”
    他说：“在这种情况下，我们的回答是：我们对这套玩艺儿不感兴趣。”“柬埔寨不愿蹈印度的覆辙。它要保持独立，宁愿过穷日子。”
    他还说，柬埔寨将自己建设十个小型水坝，这对我国的农民来说已经够用了。如果有人要我们这么办那么办，我们就告诉他们：别费心了，谢谢！
    西哈努克亲王说，紧接着拒绝美援之后，柬埔寨一九六四年和一九六五年的对外贸易都有盈余，但是今年由于农业减产，对外贸易将会有赤字。他说，柬埔寨一定要限制进口来减少赤字。“我们一定要以本国产品来满足我国的消费，而不用我们要从国外进口的产品。”
