Title: Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi Receives Executive Vice Chairman of Pakistan International Airlines / Pakistan International Airlines Holds Reception to Celebrate Second Anniversary of Air Service Between Pakistan and China
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi received Enver Jamal, Executive Vice Chairman of Pakistan International Airlines, and his wife this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Shen Tu and others were present at the reception.
    Saman, the Pakistan International Airlines Manager in China, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: Pakistan International Airlines held a reception this evening to celebrate the second anniversary of air service between Pakistan and China.
    Enver Jamal, Executive Vice Chairman of Pakistan International Airlines, and Shen Tu, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, spoke at the reception. They praised the Sino-Pakistan air route for promoting the friendship between the people of China and Pakistan over the past two years, and jointly wished for the further development of the friendly cooperation between the people of China and Pakistan and the civil aviation undertakings of the two countries.
    Among those invited to attend the reception were Fang Shishan, He Tingyi, Xu Jingwu, Le Songsheng, Sha Mengbi, Wu Jingfang and other relevant personnel.
    The wife of Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Roza, Chargé d'Affaires Durrani, and Saman, the Pakistan International Airlines Manager in China, also attended the reception.
    This noon, Shen Tu, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, hosted a banquet to welcome Mr. and Mrs. Jamal.

### 谢富治副总理接见巴国际航空公司执行副主席  巴国际航空公司举行招待会庆祝巴中通航两周年

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    谢富治副总理接见巴国际航空公司执行副主席
    巴国际航空公司举行招待会庆祝巴中通航两周年
    新华社二十八日讯　谢富治副总理今天下午接见巴基斯坦国际航空公司执行副主席恩维·加马尔和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有沈图等。
    巴基斯坦国际航空公司驻中国经理萨曼也在座。
    新华社二十八日讯　巴基斯坦国际航空公司今晚举行招待会，庆祝巴中两国通航两周年。
    巴基斯坦国际航空公司执行副主席恩维·加马尔和中国民航总局副局长沈图先后在招待会上讲话，他们赞扬了两年来中巴航线促进了中巴两国人民的友谊，共祝中巴两国人民和两国民航事业的友好合作进一步发展。
    应邀出席招待会的，有方石珊、何廷一、徐净武、乐松生、沙梦弼、巫竞放等有关方面人士。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使罗查的夫人，使馆临时代办杜拉尼，巴基斯坦国际航空公司驻中国经理萨曼也出席了招待会。
    今天中午，中国民航总局副局长沈图曾设宴欢迎加马尔夫妇。
