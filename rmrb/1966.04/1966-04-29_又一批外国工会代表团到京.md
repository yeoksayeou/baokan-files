Title: Another Group of Foreign Trade Union Delegations Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. The Ceylon Trade Union Federation Delegation led by T.N. Nadongge, Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation; the Ceylon Independent Trade Union Federation Delegation led by Soesha, Member of the Action Committee of the Ceylon Independent Trade Union Federation and Chairman of the Ceylon Pottery Employees' Union; the Guinea National Workers' Union Delegation led by Louis Behanzan, Member of the Education Union of the Guinea National Workers' Union and Inspector General of Education; the All-Tanganyika Workers' Union Delegation led by Joseph Clemens Lavigxila, Minister of Economy of the All-Tanganyika Workers' Union; and Bernard Kinini Gumedi, Deputy Secretary of the Textile Workers' Union of the Zimbabwe African Trade Union Congress, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. These foreign trade union delegations and trade union representatives were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to pay friendly visits to our country. The guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Di Zicai, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others.

### 又一批外国工会代表团到京

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    又一批外国工会代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　由锡兰工会联合会副主席蒂·恩·纳东格率领的锡兰工会联合会代表团，由锡兰独立工会联合会行动委员会委员、锡兰陶业雇员工会主席索埃沙率领的锡兰独立工会联合会代表团，由几内亚全国工人联合会教育工会委员、教育总监察长路易·贝昂赞率领的几内亚全国工人联合会代表团，由坦噶尼喀全国工人联合会经济部长约瑟夫·克莱门斯·拉威格西拉率领的全坦噶尼喀工人联合会代表团，津巴布韦非洲人工会大会纺织工人工会副书记贝纳德·基尼尼·古麦迪，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。这些外国工会代表团和工会代表是应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和进行友好访问的。客人们在机场受到了中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、书记处书记狄子才等人的热烈欢迎。
