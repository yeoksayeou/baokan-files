Title: Abacus
Author: Peng Delin
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    Abacus (Clapper Talk)
    Peng Delin, Hubei Gaofei Brigade No. 1

    Abacus, one foot three,
    I keep it by my side day and night,
    I use the abacus to recall the past,
    The old and new societies are worlds apart.
    The old society, so dark,
    The evil landlord controlled the abacus,
    The abacus clicks and clacks,
    Calculating the blood and sweat of the poor.
    In forty-nine, a new sky dawned,
    The poor regained the abacus,
    I took the abacus to school,
    And learned culture and technology completely.
    Collectivization, days are sweet,
    The son of a poor peasant controls the abacus,
    Every year the harvest is calculated,
    Enough food and clothing, smiling faces.
    Abacus, one foot three,
    I keep it by my side day and night,
    Grasping the abacus tightly, following the Party,
    The two characters "Revolution" are kept in mind.

### 铁算盘
彭德林
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    铁算盘（快板）
    湖北高飞一大队  彭德林
    铁算盘，一尺三，
    日夜不离我身边，
    我打算盘忆往事，
    新旧社会两重天。
    旧社会，真黑暗，
    万恶地主掌算盘，
    算盘一打滴嗒响，
    算尽穷人血和汗。
    四九年，换新天，
    穷人夺回铁算盘，
    我拿算盘上学校，
    文化技术学得全。
    集体化，日子甜，
    贫农的儿子掌算盘，
    年年丰收把账算，
    吃穿有余笑开颜。
    铁算盘，一尺三，
    日夜不离我身边，
    紧握算盘跟党走，
    革命二字记心间。
