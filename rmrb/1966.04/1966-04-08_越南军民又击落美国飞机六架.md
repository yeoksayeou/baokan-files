Title: Vietnamese People and Army Shoot Down Six More American Planes

Author:

Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 3

    Hanoi, April 6 (Xinhua) – It was announced here on the 6th that the army and people of Vinh City in Nghe An Province, North Vietnam, shot down an invading American bandit plane on the 4th.
    Hanoi, April 3 (Xinhua) – It was announced here on the evening of the 3rd that the army and people of Quang Binh, Ha Tinh, and Thanh Hoa provinces in North Vietnam shot down a total of five American bandit planes and captured American pilots alive in the three days from the 1st to the 3rd. On the morning of the 1st, the army and people of Quang Binh Province shot down an American jet fighter.
    On the afternoon of the 2nd, the army and people of Quang Binh Province shot down two more American jet fighters and captured an American pilot alive.
    On the afternoon of the 2nd, the army and people of Ha Tinh Province also shot down an American jet fighter.
    On the afternoon of the 3rd, the army and people of Thanh Hoa Province shot down an American jet fighter.

### 越南军民又击落美国飞机六架

1966-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美国飞机六架
    新华社河内六日电　据这里六日公布，越南北方义安省荣市军民四日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内三日电　据这里三日晚上公布，越南北方广平、河静、清化等省军民在一日到三日的三天中，共击落美国强盗飞机五架，并活捉了美国飞行员。一日早晨，广平省军民打落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    二日下午，广平省军民又打落美国喷气式飞机两架，并活捉了美国飞行员。
    二日下午，河静省军民也打落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    三日下午，清化省军民打落美国喷气式飞机一架。
