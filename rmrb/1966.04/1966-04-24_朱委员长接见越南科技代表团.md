Title: Chairman Zhu Receives Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation
Author: Zheng Xiaozhen
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 1

    Chairman Zhu Receives Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. Chairman Zhu De received the Vietnamese government science and technology delegation led by Nguyen Xuan Lam, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry of Vietnam, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Zhao Jichang, Lu Xianlin and others were present at the reception.
    Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, was also present. (Attached picture)
    Right picture: Chairman Zhu De receives the Vietnamese government science and technology delegation led by Nguyen Xuan Lam (second from left in the front row), Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry of Vietnam. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 朱委员长接见越南科技代表团
郑小箴
1966-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见越南科技代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　朱德委员长今天下午接见了由越南重工业部副部长阮春林率领的越南政府科学技术代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有赵继昌、陆献琳等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平也在座。（附图片）
    右图：朱德委员长接见由越南重工业部副部长阮春林（前左二）率领的越南政府科学技术代表团。		  新华社记者　郑小箴摄
