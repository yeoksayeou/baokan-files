Title: The Heroic Land of Eagles Advances Victoriously in the Struggle Against Imperialism and Revisionism: Documentary "Albania on the Road of Revolutionization" Released

Author:

Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 2

    The Heroic Land of Eagles Advances Victoriously in the Struggle Against Imperialism and Revisionism
    Documentary "Albania on the Road of Revolutionization" Released
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The documentary "Albania on the Road of Revolutionization," produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, will be released in the capital and throughout the country starting today.
    This film reports on the recent situation of the heroic Land of Eagles – Albania – breaking through the blockade and sabotage of imperialism and modern revisionism, and advancing victoriously under the correct leadership of the Albanian Party of Labor and Comrade Enver Hoxha. The film records the scene of the Albanian Party of Labor Central Committee issuing an open letter to the people of the whole country this March, calling on the people to strive for the realization of revolutionary measures. Comrade Enver Hoxha pointed out at a symposium held by the Party Central Committee that these major measures are: fundamentally eliminating bureaucracy, dispatching leading Party and government cadres to work at the grassroots level, reducing the salaries of senior cadres, re-establishing political commissars in the armed forces, and abolishing the military rank system. The film reflects the masses of people implementing revolutionary measures with revolutionary actions. Workers are responding to the Party's call with increased production and thrift; members of agricultural cooperatives are determined to rely on their own efforts to achieve self-sufficiency in grain as soon as possible; officers and soldiers of the People's Army are determined to strive for the revolutionization of the army; and hundreds of thousands of cadres from government offices go to the mountains and the countryside to work side-by-side with the masses on the front lines of production.
    The film also reports on the situation of Xu Jianguo, China's ambassador to Albania, and the staff of the embassy working in the countryside with the fraternal Albanian people.
    The film shows that these revolutionary measures of the Albanian Party of Labor are an example of combining the universal truth of Marxism-Leninism with the practice of their own country, and it is forming an unstoppable revolutionary upsurge among the masses of the people, guiding Albania to advance rapidly on the road of socialism.

### 英雄的山鹰之国在反帝反修斗争中胜利前进  纪录片《阿尔巴尼亚在革命化大道上》上映

1966-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    英雄的山鹰之国在反帝反修斗争中胜利前进
    纪录片《阿尔巴尼亚在革命化大道上》上映
    新华社二十九日讯　中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的纪录片《阿尔巴尼亚在革命化大道上》，今天起陆续在首都和全国各地上映。
    这部影片报道了英雄的山鹰之国——阿尔巴尼亚冲破帝国主义和现代修正主义的封锁和破坏，在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和恩维尔·霍查同志的正确领导下胜利前进的近况。影片纪录了今年三月阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会发表致全国人民的公开信，号召全国人民为实现革命化措施而奋斗的情景。恩维尔·霍查同志在党中央举行的座谈会上指出这些重大措施是：从根本上清除官僚主义，派遣党政领导干部到基层工作，降低高级干部的工资，在武装部队中重新设立政治委员和取消军衔制。影片反映了人民群众以革命化行动来贯彻执行革命措施。工人们要用增产节约来回答党的号召；农业合作社的社员们决心依靠自己力量，争取尽快做到粮食自给自足；人民军官兵坚决要为军队的革命化而奋斗；成千上万的机关干部上山下乡，下放生产第一线和群众并肩劳动。
    影片还报道了我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国和使馆工作人员来到农村和兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民一起劳动的情况。
    影片表明，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党这些革命措施是把马克思列宁主义的普遍真理和本国实践相结合的范例，它正在人民群众中形成一个不可阻挡的革命化热潮，引导着阿尔巴尼亚在社会主义大道上飞速前进。
