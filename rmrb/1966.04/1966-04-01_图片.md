Title: Liu Shaoqi Attends Reception in Karachi

Author: Dù Xiūxián

Date: 1966-04-01
Edition: 3

    On March 30th, the Karachi Municipal Corporation, the largest city in Pakistan, held a civic reception at Jinnah Park to warmly welcome Chairman Liu Shaoqi. The picture shows Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice Premier Chen Yi at the Karachi civic reception.
　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian (Fax Photo)
