Title: Japanese Delegation of Activists Expanding "People's China" Readers Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—Invited by the "People's China" magazine, the Japanese Delegation of Activists Expanding "People's China" Readers, led by Tanikawa Toshizo, Managing Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Li Zemin and Kang Dachuan, Deputy Editors-in-Chief of "People's China," and Luo Jun, Director of the Foreign Languages Publishing and Distribution Administration, welcomed them at the airport.

### 日扩大《人民中国》读者积极分子访华团到京

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    日扩大《人民中国》读者积极分子访华团到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应《人民中国》杂志社邀请，由日中友协常务理事长谷川敏三率领的日本扩大《人民中国》读者积极分子访华代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    《人民中国》副总编辑李泽民、康大川，外文出版发行事业局局长罗俊等到机场欢迎。
