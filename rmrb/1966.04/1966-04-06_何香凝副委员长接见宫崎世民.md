Title: He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Miyazaki Seimin
Author:
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Miyazaki Seimin, Director-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.
    Present at the reception was Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association.
    Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 何香凝副委员长接见宫崎世民

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    何香凝副委员长接见宫崎世民
    新华社五日讯　何香凝副委员长今天下午接见日中友协理事长宫崎世民，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中日友协会长廖承志。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
