Title: Prince Sihanouk Writes to Premier Zhou Enlai, Thanking Him for Warm Reception of Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-23
Edition: 4

    Prince Sihanouk Writes to Premier Zhou Enlai
    Thanking Him for Warm Reception of Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd – Premier Zhou Enlai recently received a letter of thanks from Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk. In the letter, he expressed his gratitude to Premier Zhou Enlai and the Chinese government for the warm reception given to the Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation during their visit to China. The full text of the letter of thanks is as follows:
    To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai, State Council of the People’s Republic of China, Beijing,
    Mr. Premier:
    Our country's Cultural and Scientific Delegation, currently in Beijing, has reported to me the extremely warm reception accorded to them by Your Excellency and the Chinese government. Please allow me to express to you my heartfelt gratitude for the great friendship that China has once again shown us.
    I would especially like to express my deep gratitude for your kind sentiments conveyed to me through the head of our government delegation and for reaffirming China's support for Cambodia. Because we have unbreakable solidarity with our Chinese brothers, we are filled with confidence in the outcome of our struggle against the aggression, harassment, and threats of U.S. imperialism and its servants.
    I would like to take this opportunity to point out that the fighting solidarity that unites our two countries is of great significance to the future of Asian nations who refuse to submit to American rule and refuse to abandon their independence and dignity.
    Please accept my highest and most cordial regards.
              Norodom Sihanouk
      Phnom Penh, April 7, 1966

### 西哈努克亲王写信给周恩来总理  感谢我热情接待柬文化科学代表团

1966-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王写信给周恩来总理
    感谢我热情接待柬文化科学代表团
    新华社二十二日讯　周恩来总理最近收到了柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的一封感谢信。信中向周恩来总理和中国政府对柬埔寨文化科学代表团在访问中国期间所给予的热情接待表示谢意。感谢信全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下总理先生：
    目前正在北京的我国文化科学代表团已向我报告阁下和中国政府对他们十分热情的接待。请允许我为中国又一次对我们表示的伟大友谊，向你致以最衷心的谢意。
    承蒙你通过我国政府代表团团长向我转达你的友好情谊并重申中国对柬埔寨的支持，我要特别向你表示深切的谢意。由于我们同我们中国兄弟有着无懈可击的团结，因此我们对我们为反对美帝国主义及其仆从的侵略、骚扰和威胁而进行的斗争的结局充满着信心。
    我愿借此机会指出，把我们两国联结在一起的战斗团结，对拒绝屈服于美国统治和拒绝放弃自己独立和尊严的亚洲各国人民的前途，具有伟大的意义。
    顺致最崇高的、最亲切的敬意。
              诺罗敦·西哈努克
      一九六六年四月七日于金边
