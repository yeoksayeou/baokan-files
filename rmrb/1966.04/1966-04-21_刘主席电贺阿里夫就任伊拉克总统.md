Title: Liu Chairman Sends Telegram to Congratulate Arif on Assuming Office as President of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th April—Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on April 20th to Abd al-Rahman Muhammad Arif, President of the Republic of Iraq, congratulating him on his election as President. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Major General Abd al-Rahman Muhammad Arif, President of the Republic of Iraq, Baghdad:
    On the occasion of Your Excellency's election as President of the Republic of Iraq, I extend to Your Excellency my heartfelt congratulations. I wish the people of your country happiness. May the friendship between the people of China and Iraq be increasingly consolidated and developed.
            Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                    April 20, 1966

### 刘主席电贺阿里夫就任伊拉克总统

1966-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺阿里夫就任伊拉克总统
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇二十日打电报给伊拉克共和国总统阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德·阿里夫，祝贺他当选为总统。电文如下：巴格达伊拉克共和国总统阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德·阿里夫少将阁下：
    在阁下当选为伊拉克共和国总统的时候，谨向阁下致以衷心的祝贺。祝贵国人民幸福。祝中伊两国人民的友谊日益巩固和发展。
            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    一九六六年四月二十日
