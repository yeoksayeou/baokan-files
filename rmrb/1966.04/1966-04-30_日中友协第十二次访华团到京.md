Title: Japan-China Friendship Association's 12th Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th – The Japan-China Friendship Association's 12th Delegation to China, invited by the China-Japan Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation of ten people was led by Saito Norie, Director of the General Affairs Department of the Japan-China Friendship Association. Responsible persons from relevant departments who greeted them at the airport included Zhao Anbo, Yang Yunyu, Lin Lin, Wang Xiaoyun, and others.

### 日中友协第十二次访华团到京

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协第十二次访华团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　 应中日友协邀请来我国访问的日中友协第十二次访华代表团一行十人，在团长、日中友协总务部长斋藤纪江率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场迎接的有关方面负责人有赵安博、杨蕴玉、林林、王晓云等。
