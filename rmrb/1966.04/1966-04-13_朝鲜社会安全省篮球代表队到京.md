Title: Korean Ministry of Social Security Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 4

    Korean Ministry of Social Security basketball team arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, 10th) The 15-member Korean Ministry of Social Security basketball team, led by Major General Han Qianqiu, arrived in Beijing by train this morning.
    The visiting team is invited to participate in the 7th Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization Men's Basketball Tournament to be held soon in Beijing. They were warmly welcomed upon arrival by Ma Xingwu, Director of the Organizing Committee of the 7th Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organization Men's Basketball Tournament and Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Public Security Forces, and Mou Zuoyun, Deputy Director of the Ball Games Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.
    Korean Ambassador to China, Park Se-chang, and Military Attaché Colonel Choe Je-ryong of the Embassy also greeted them at the station.

### 朝鲜社会安全省篮球代表队到京

1966-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜社会安全省篮球代表队到京
    新华社十日讯　朝鲜社会安全省篮球代表队一行十五人，在领队韩千秋少将率领下今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    客队是应邀参加即将在北京举行的社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛的，他们到达时受到社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会组织委员会主任、中国人民公安部队政治部副主任马星五，国家体委球类司副司长牟作云等的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜驻华大使朴世昌，大使馆武官崔济荣上校等也到车站迎接。
