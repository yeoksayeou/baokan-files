Title: Production News Flying Everywhere
Author: Huo Richi
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 6

    Production News Flying Everywhere
    Shanghai Engineering Machinery Factory Fitter, Huo Richi
    The factory held a worker representative meeting,
    A whirlwind of production battles swept through:
    Boosting drive, you chase me, I catch up with you;
    Making innovations, wisdom is given full play!
    Look at the hammer in the hands of our workers,
    Striking and making golden stars fall.
    Waves of hammer sounds,
    Waves of thunderclaps!
    Even stubborn steel,
    Must be smashed to pieces…
    Road rollers rumble in formation,
    Production news flying everywhere.

### 生产喜报满天飞
霍日炽
1966-04-09
第6版()
专栏：车间黑板报

    生产喜报满天飞
    上海工程机械厂钳工　霍日炽
    厂里开了职工代表会，
    生产战线掀风雷：
    鼓干劲，你追我赶；
    闹革新，智慧大发挥！
    你看咱工人手中锤，
    打得满天金星坠。
    阵阵锤声，
    阵阵惊雷！
    是顽钢，
    也要把它砸碎……
    压路机隆隆排成队，
    生产喜报呀满天飞。
