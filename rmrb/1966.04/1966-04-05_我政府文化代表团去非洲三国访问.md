Title: Chinese Government Cultural Delegation Visits Three African Countries
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: A six-member Chinese Government Cultural Delegation headed by Li Qi, Vice Minister of Culture, left by plane today for Congo (Brazzaville), Guinea, and Mali to discuss the 1966 executive plans for cultural cooperation agreements and to pay friendly visits. Song Yiping, Zhao Xinchu, and other responsible persons from relevant departments were at the airport to see them off. Birama Traoré, Ambassador of Mali to China, Bakoula Daniel, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, and Keita Atanaz, Commercial Commissioner of the Guinean Embassy in China, were also at the airport to see them off.

### 我政府文化代表团去非洲三国访问

1966-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府文化代表团去非洲三国访问
    新华社四日讯　由文化部副部长李琦率领的中国政府文化代表团一行六人，今天乘飞机赴刚果（布）、几内亚、马里商谈文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划并进行友好访问。前往机场送行的有宋一平、赵辛初等有关方面负责人。马里驻华大使比拉马·特拉奥雷，刚果（布）驻华大使馆临时代办巴库拉·丹尼尔，几内亚驻华大使馆商务专员凯塔·阿塔纳兹也到机场送行。
