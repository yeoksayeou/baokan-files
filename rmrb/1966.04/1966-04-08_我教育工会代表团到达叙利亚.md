Title: Chinese Education Union Delegation Arrives in Syria
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 5th. A Chinese Education Union delegation, led by Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Union, arrived in Damascus, the capital of Syria, on the evening of the 4th at the invitation of the Syrian Teachers' Union.
    Ahmed Khatib, Chairman of the Syrian Teachers' Union, and Beshra Heze, Vice Chairman, were at the airport to welcome the delegation. Li Huanong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Syria, and Yang Keyi, Cultural Counselor, also went to the airport to meet them.

### 我教育工会代表团到达叙利亚

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我教育工会代表团到达叙利亚
    新华社大马士革五日电　由中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明率领的中国教育工会代表团，应叙利亚教师联合会的邀请，四日晚上到达叙利亚首都大马士革。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有叙利亚教师联合会主席艾哈迈德·哈提卜和副主席贝什拉·赫泽。中国驻叙利亚大使馆临时代办李华农和文化参赞杨克毅也到机场迎接。
