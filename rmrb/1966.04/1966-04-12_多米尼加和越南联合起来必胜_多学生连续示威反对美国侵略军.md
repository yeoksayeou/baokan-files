Title: Dominican Republic and Vietnam United Will Surely Win; Many Students Continuously Demonstrate Against American Aggressor Troops
Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 4

    Dominican Republic and Vietnam United Will Surely Win
    Many Students Continuously Demonstrate Against American Aggressor Troops
    (Xinhua News Agency, Santo Domingo, 7th) Students in San Juan de la Maguana city, west of the Dominican capital, Santo Domingo, held continuous demonstrations and rallies on the 5th and 6th to protest against the occupation of Dominican territory by American troops.
    According to reports, on the evening of the 5th, when students in San Juan de la Maguana city held an anti-American rally and demonstration, the authorities dispatched police to brutally suppress the students and arrested one demonstrator. The police's suppression aroused great anger among the demonstrating students, and they launched a heroic fight with the police. At this time, the police brazenly opened fire on the demonstrating students, killing one student and seriously injuring two others.
    (Xinhua News Agency, Santo Domingo, 10th) Patriotic youths in Santo Domingo, the capital of the Dominican Republic, drove a group of American aggressor troops and Brazilian accomplice troops who were bathing at the beach out of the beach resort last weekend, and shouted slogans such as "Yankees go home" and "Dominican Republic and Vietnam united will surely win."
    According to reports, this anti-American incident occurred at Boca Chica Beach, about thirty kilometers from Santo Domingo. Dominican patriotic youths came to the beach in several groups and issued an ultimatum to the American soldiers and Brazilian accomplice troops there, demanding that they leave within an hour. These aggressor troops were panic-stricken and then slipped away under the protection of fully armed American soldiers who came to the rescue.

### 多米尼加和越南联合起来必胜  多学生连续示威反对美国侵略军

1966-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    多米尼加和越南联合起来必胜
    多学生连续示威反对美国侵略军
    据新华社七日讯　圣多明各消息：多米尼加首都西部圣胡安·德拉马瓜纳城的学生五日和六日连续举行示威游行和集会，抗议美国军队侵占多米尼加国土。
    据报道，五日晚间，当圣胡安·德拉马瓜纳城的学生举行反美集会和示威游行时，当局出动警察对学生进行野蛮的镇压，并且逮捕了一名示威者。警察的镇压行动激起了示威学生的极大愤怒，他们同警察展开了英勇的搏斗。这时，警察悍然向示威学生开枪，打死一名学生，另外两名学生受重伤。
    新华社十日讯　圣多明各消息：多米尼加首都圣多明各爱国青年上星期末把正在海滨沐浴的一批美国侵略军和巴西帮凶军赶出海滨浴场，并且高呼“美国佬滚出去”、“多米尼加和越南联合起来必胜”等口号。
    据报道，这一反美事件发生在离圣多明各大约三十公里的博卡奇卡海滩。多米尼加爱国青年分成几批来到海滨，对在那里的美国兵和巴西帮凶军发出通牒，要他们在一小时内离开那里。这些侵略军惊慌失措，随后就在前来救命的全副武装的美国兵保护下溜走了。
