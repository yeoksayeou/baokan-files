Title: Comrade Liu Ningyi and Others Meet with Foreign Trade Union Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th – Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice-Chairmen Zhu Xuefan, Ma Chungu, Kang Yonghe, and Secretary of the Secretariat Chen Yu, today met separately with the Romanian Trade Union Delegation headed by Benescu Mihail; the Central Organization of Cuban Workers delegation headed by Agapito Figueroa; the Pakistan Trade Union Delegation headed by Sonnamat; the Palestine Trade Union Federation delegation headed by F. Feda; the National Workers Union of Mali delegation headed by Mamadou Keita; the Trade Union Delegation of the Peronist Revolutionary Movement of Argentina headed by Abdala Barruchi; the Federation of Private Enterprise Employees of Chile delegation headed by Ernesto Lennon; and the Italian General Confederation of Labour delegation headed by Zini Domenico.
    Prior to this, Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Wang Zhijie, Secretary of the Secretariat, also met separately with the New Zealand Industrial Union Delegation headed by P.A. Hansen, Harry Lall, Chairman of the British Guiana Agricultural Workers Union, and Cyril Burke, Secretary of the Dock Branch of the British Guiana Sawmill and Forest Workers Union.

### 刘宁一同志等会见外国工会代表团

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    刘宁一同志等会见外国工会代表团
    新华社二十八日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，副主席朱学范、马纯古、康永和，书记处书记陈宇今天分别会见了以贝内斯库·米哈伊尔为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团，以阿加比多·费盖罗阿为首的革命古巴工人中央工会代表团，以宋纳玛特为首的巴基斯坦工会代表团，以法·费达为首的巴勒斯坦工会联合会代表团，以马马杜·凯塔为首的马里全国工人联合会代表团，以阿布达拉·巴鲁契为首的阿根廷庇隆主义革命运动工会代表团，以厄尔内斯多·莱农为首的智利私营企业雇员联合会代表团，以齐尼·多米尼科为首的意大利总工会代表团。
    在这以前，中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，书记处书记王志杰还分别会见了以彼·艾·汉森为首的新西兰产业工会代表团，英属圭亚那农业工人工会主席哈里·拉尔和英属圭亚那锯木及林业工会码头分会书记西里尔·伯克。
