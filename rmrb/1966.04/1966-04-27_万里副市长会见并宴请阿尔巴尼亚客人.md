Title: Vice Mayor Wan Li Meets and Hosts Albanian Guests

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-27
Edition: 3

    Vice Mayor Wan Li Meets and Hosts Albanian Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: Wan Li, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Mayor of Beijing, met and hosted a banquet tonight for the Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation led by Saimir Kumbaro, Chairman of the Central Union of Handicraft Cooperatives.
    Present at the meeting and banquet were Chen Yifan, Cui Yueli, and others.  Keqo Kasapi, Commercial Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China, was also invited to attend.

### 万里副市长会见并宴请阿尔巴尼亚客人

1966-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    万里副市长会见并宴请阿尔巴尼亚客人
    新华社二十六日讯　中共北京市委书记处书记、北京市副市长万里，今晚会见并宴请阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社中央联合会主席赛梅尔科率领的手工业合作社代表团。
    出席会见和宴会的有陈一帆、崔月犁等。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆商务参赞凯乔·卡萨比等也应邀出席。
