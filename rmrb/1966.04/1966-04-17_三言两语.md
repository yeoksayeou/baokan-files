    Brief Comments
    The Noose Around the Neck
    The American aggressors are facing increasingly difficult circumstances in South Vietnam. The Johnson administration is妄想(wang xiang - fantasizing) to escape its predicament by intensifying its war blackmail against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Taylor, a defeated general of the Vietnamese people, openly advocated "exerting military pressure on North Vietnam," clamoring to "tighten the noose around Hanoi's neck."
    Facts have long proven that with each step forward on the path of "gradual escalation," the American aggressors move closer to their grave. The approach advocated by the likes of Taylor is, in reality, tightening the noose around the neck of the American imperialists themselves.
    Lying with Eyes Wide Open
    An article published in the American Washington Post wrote: "The Vietnamese people believe that Nguyen Cao Ky is an American puppet." "Not a single Vietnamese person is unaware that once American troops withdraw from Vietnam, this regime will collapse within days." The newspaper also pointed out that even "senior officials at the American embassy in Saigon admit this."
    However, high-ranking officials in Washington, such as American Deputy Secretary of State Ball, insist on making people believe that the Nguyen Cao Ky gang is not America's stooge. He said with a straight face, "We have absolutely no interest in having puppets." This is truly lying with eyes wide open.

### 三言两语

1966-04-17
第4版()
专栏：三言两语

    三言两语
    脖子上的绞索
    美国侵略者在南越的处境日益困难，约翰逊政府妄想用对越南民主共和国加强战争讹诈的手段来摆脱困境。越南人民手下败将泰勒公开鼓吹“对北越施加军事压力”，叫嚷什么“收紧套在河内脖子上的绳索”。
    事实早已证明，美国侵略者在“逐步升级”的道路上，每迈前一步，就是离坟墓近一步。泰勒之流鼓吹的做法，实际上是收紧套在美帝自己脖子上的绞索罢了。
    瞪着眼睛撒谎
    美国《华盛顿邮报》发表的一篇文章写道：“越南人认为阮高其是美国傀儡”。“没有一个越南人不知道，一旦美军撤出越南，这个政权就会在几天之内垮台”。这家报纸还指出，甚至“美国驻西贡大使馆的高级官员也承认这点”。
    但是，华盛顿的高级官员、美国副国务卿鲍尔之流，却硬要人相信，阮高其一伙并非美国的走狗。他一本正经地说，“我们对拥有傀儡这样的事情一点也不感兴趣”。这真是瞪着眼睛撒谎。
