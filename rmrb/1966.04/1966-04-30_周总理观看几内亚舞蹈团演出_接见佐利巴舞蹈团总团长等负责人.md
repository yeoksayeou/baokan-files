Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Watches Performance by Guinean Dance Troupe and Receives Leaders of the Zolibah Dance Troupe

Author:

Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 1

    Premier Zhou Enlai Watches Performance by Guinean Dance Troupe
    Receives Leaders of the Zolibah Dance Troupe

    (Xinhua News Agency, April 29th) Premier Zhou Enlai watched the performance of the Guinean National Zolibah Dance Troupe tonight.

    The program performed by the Guinean artists tonight was warmly welcomed by the audience. Also watching the performance tonight were responsible persons from relevant departments, including Chu Tunan, Song Yiping, Yang Chun, Zhao Xinchu, Zhou Weizhi, Sun Shen, Sheng Jie, Ding Bo, and figures from the capital's art circles.

    After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Ambassador Camara Sekou, went on stage, shook hands cordially with each of the Guinean artists, congratulated them on their successful performance, and took photos with them.

    (Xinhua News Agency, April 29th) Premier Zhou Enlai received tonight the Director of the Guinean National Zolibah Dance Troupe, Sissoko Amadou; Deputy Director Kouyate Sory Kandia; Administrative Head Sana Mamadou Laminah; Artistic Director Madame Sissoko Apsita; and Stage Supervisor N'Diaye Abdoulaye. Premier Zhou Enlai had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Guinean friends.

    Present at the reception were responsible persons from relevant departments, including Chu Tunan, Song Yiping, Yang Chun, Zhao Xinchu, Zhou Weizhi, Sun Shen, Sheng Jie, Ding Bo, and figures from art circles.

    Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Sekou, and his wife were also present.

### 周总理观看几内亚舞蹈团演出  接见佐利巴舞蹈团总团长等负责人

1966-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理观看几内亚舞蹈团演出
    接见佐利巴舞蹈团总团长等负责人
    新华社二十九日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上观看了几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团的演出。
    几内亚艺术家今晚演出的节目，受到全场观众极其热烈的欢迎。观看今晚演出的，还有楚图南、宋一平、杨纯、赵辛初、周巍峙、孙慎、盛婕、丁波等有关方面负责人和首都艺术界人士。
    演出结束后，周总理在卡马拉·塞古大使陪同下走上舞台，同几内亚艺术家一一亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功，并且同他们一起照相。
    新华社二十九日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团总团长西索柯·阿马杜，副总团长库雅特·索里·康迪亚，行政负责人萨纳·马马杜·拉米纳，艺术指导西索柯·阿普西塔夫人，舞台总监督恩·迪阿依·阿布杜拉依。周总理同几内亚朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有楚图南、宋一平、杨纯、赵辛初、周巍峙、孙慎、盛婕、丁波等有关方面负责人和艺术界人士。
    几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古和夫人也在座。
