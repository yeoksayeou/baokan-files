Title: Pakistani Writers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 21st, the Pakistani Writers Delegation, led by Ibrahim Khan, a famous Pakistani writer and head of the delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 20th. Members of the delegation include: Zhusa Muding·Laxidi, Yijiazhi·Data Lawei, Jiasimuding, Yinamule·Hake, Wa Xide·Kuleixi, and Yibune·Yingsha. Another member of the delegation, Professor Wajiale·Aximu, will come to China soon.
    On the evening of the 21st, Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, hosted a banquet to welcome the Pakistani guests. Those who went to the airport to greet them and attended the banquet included Cao Yu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Writers Association, Yang Shuo, Secretary-General of the Asian-African Writers Liaison Committee of China, and writers Feng Mu, Hu Ke, Lei Jia, Huang Gang, Chen Bingyi, Han Beiping, and Wang Daoqian.

### 巴基斯坦作家代表团到京

1966-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦作家代表团到京
    据新华社二十一日讯　由团长、巴基斯坦著名作家易卜拉欣·汗率领的巴基斯坦作家代表团二十日乘飞机到达北京。代表团成员中有：朱萨穆丁·拉希迪，伊贾兹·达塔拉维，贾西穆丁，伊纳穆勒·哈克，瓦希德·库雷希和伊卜内·英沙等。代表团另一名团员瓦加勒·阿希姆教授不久即将来华。
    二十一日晚，中国作家协会副主席刘白羽设宴欢迎巴基斯坦客人。前往机场迎接和出席宴会的，有作家协会书记处书记曹禺，亚非作家中国联络委员会秘书长杨朔和作家冯牧、胡可、雷加、黄钢、陈冰夷、韩北屏、王道乾等。
