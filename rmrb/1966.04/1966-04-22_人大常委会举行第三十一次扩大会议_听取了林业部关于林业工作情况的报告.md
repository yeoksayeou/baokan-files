Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds 31st Enlarged Meeting Listens to Report from Ministry of Forestry on Forestry Work

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 31st enlarged meeting today. The meeting heard a report by Xun Changwu, Vice Minister of Forestry, on the state of forestry work. Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行第三十一次扩大会议  听取了林业部关于林业工作情况的报告

1966-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行第三十一次扩大会议
    听取了林业部关于林业工作情况的报告
    新华社二十一日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第三十一次会议扩大会议。会议听取了林业部副部长荀昌五关于林业工作情况的报告。政协全国委员会常务委员列席了会议。
