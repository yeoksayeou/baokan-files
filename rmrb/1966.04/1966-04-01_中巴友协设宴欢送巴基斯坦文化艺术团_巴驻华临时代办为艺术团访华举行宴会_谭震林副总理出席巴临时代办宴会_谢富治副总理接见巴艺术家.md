Title: Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association Hosts Farewell Banquet for Pakistani Cultural Arts Troupe; Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China Holds Banquet for Arts Troupe's Visit to China; Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Attends Banquet Held by Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires ad interim; Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi Receives Pakistani Artists
Author:
Date: 1966-04-01
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st. The China-Pakistan Friendship Association held a banquet tonight to warmly bid farewell to the Pakistani Cultural Arts Troupe, led by head Rahman and deputy head Aziz.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuzhi attended the banquet.
    Fang Shishan, Vice President of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, spoke at the banquet. He said that the Pakistani Arts Troupe's performances in Beijing have been a great success. Your beautiful rhythms and harmoniously consistent music and dance have given us great enjoyment. The program you performed against foreign aggression reflects the strong and unyielding national spirit of Pakistan. This spirit was fully manifested in the struggle against Indian aggression last year. The Chinese songs performed by Pakistani artists expressed the profound friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan. This friendship will be further strengthened in the years to come, in the struggle against imperialism, old and new colonialism, and in defense of national independence and world peace. Fang Shishan wished the art troupe greater success in their visits and performances in other cities in our country.
    In his speech, Head Rahman said that the Beijing audience's enthusiastic and sincere welcome to Pakistani music and dance is a great encouragement to us. He said that the Chinese people are the most sincere and loyal friends of the Pakistani people. The support of the Chinese people has left an indelible impression in the hearts of one hundred million Pakistani people, and it will forever be engraved in the annals of Sino-Pakistani friendship.
    Also present at the banquet were responsible persons from relevant departments, including Ren Yinglun, Yang Chun, Zhao Xinchu, Xu Jingwu, Wang Yuanfang, Zhou Weizhi, Ding Bo, and Dai Ailian, as well as figures from the capital's literary and art circles.
    Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Durrani, and Ambassador Rocha's wife were also invited to attend the banquet.
    After the banquet, the Pakistani Cultural Arts Troupe attended a cultural evening held by the China-Pakistan Friendship Association for them and watched song and dance performances by literary and art workers from the capital.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st. Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuzhi received Rahman, head of the Pakistani Cultural Arts Troupe, and Aziz, deputy head of the troupe, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were responsible persons from relevant departments, including Ren Yinglun, Yang Chun, Zhao Xinchu, Fang Shishan, and Xu Jingwu.
    Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Durrani, and Ambassador Rocha's wife were also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st. Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Durrani, and his wife gave a banquet at noon today for the Pakistani Cultural Arts Troupe's visit to China.
    Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin was invited to attend the banquet.
    The banquet was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere. Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Durrani and Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Commission Yang Chun spoke successively. They both pointed out that the extremely warm welcome received by Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice Premier Chen Yi in Pakistan fully demonstrated the profound friendship between the two peoples.
    In his speech, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Durrani used the saying "A friend in need is a friend indeed" to praise the friendship between the peoples of Pakistan and China. He said that we will never forget that in September of last year, when our nation was in a critical moment of life and death, the great Chinese people stood up and, with their words and actions, proved that they are indeed our sincere friends. No matter what happens, Sino-Pakistani friendship is unbreakable. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm reception the Cultural Arts Troupe received in Beijing.
    In her speech, Yang Chun expressed her belief that the friendship established between the peoples of China and Pakistan in the struggle against imperialism and expansionism, and in defense of national independence and world peace, will be further strengthened and developed. She pointed out that the Pakistani artists' superb performances in Beijing have been a great success. She hoped that the Pakistani artists would convey the Chinese people's support for Pakistan's just cause to the Pakistani people.
    Also invited to attend the banquet were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments in our country Zhao Xinchu, Fang Shishan, Dai Ailian, Wang Yuanfang, and Ding Bo, among others.
    The wife of the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Rocha, as well as Rahman, head of the Pakistani Cultural Arts Troupe, Deputy Head Aziz's wife, and all members, attended the banquet.

### 中巴友协设宴欢送巴基斯坦文化艺术团  巴驻华临时代办为艺术团访华举行宴会  谭震林副总理出席巴临时代办宴会　谢富治副总理接见巴艺术家

1966-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    中巴友协设宴欢送巴基斯坦文化艺术团
    巴驻华临时代办为艺术团访华举行宴会
    谭震林副总理出席巴临时代办宴会　谢富治副总理接见巴艺术家
    新华社三十一日讯　中国巴基斯坦友好协会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢送由团长拉赫曼、副团长阿奇兹率领的巴基斯坦文化艺术团。
    国务院副总理谢富治出席了宴会。
    中巴友协副会长方石珊在宴会上讲话。他说，巴基斯坦艺术团在北京的演出获得了很大的成功。你们那些美好的节奏与旋律和谐一致的音乐舞蹈，给了我们很大的享受。你们演出的反抗外国侵略的节目，反映了巴基斯坦的坚强不屈的民族精神。这种精神在去年举国一致反抗印度侵略的斗争中，充分体现出来。巴基斯坦艺术家演出的中国歌曲，表达了中巴两国人民的深厚友谊。这种友谊在今后的岁月里，在反抗帝国主义、新老殖民主义和维护民族独立和世界和平的斗争中，必将获得进一步加强。方石珊祝愿艺术团在我国其他城市访问演出获得更大的成功。
    拉赫曼团长在讲话中说，北京观众对巴基斯坦的音乐和舞蹈表示十分热情和真诚的欢迎，对我们是极大的鼓舞。他说，中国人民是巴基斯坦人民最诚恳、最忠实的朋友。中国人民的支援在一亿巴基斯坦人民心中留下了不可磨灭的印象，它将永远铭刻在巴中友谊的史册上。
    出席宴会的，还有任映仑、杨纯、赵辛初、徐净武、王元方、周巍峙、丁波、戴爱莲等有关方面负责人和首都文艺界人士。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办杜拉尼和罗查大使夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会后，巴基斯坦文化艺术团出席了中巴友协为他们举行的文艺晚会，观看了首都文艺工作者演出的歌舞节目。
    新华社三十一日讯　国务院副总理谢富治今天下午接见巴基斯坦文化艺术团团长拉赫曼、副团长阿奇兹，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有任映仑、杨纯、赵辛初、方石珊、徐净武等有关方面负责人。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办杜拉尼和罗查大使夫人也在座。
    新华社三十一日讯　巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办杜拉尼和夫人，今天中午为巴基斯坦文化艺术团访华举行宴会。
    谭震林副总理应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会上洋溢着友好热烈的气氛。杜拉尼临时代办和对外文委副主任杨纯先后讲话。他们都指出，刘少奇主席和陈毅副总理在巴基斯坦受到的极其热烈的欢迎，充分表现了两国人民之间的深厚友谊。
    杜拉尼临时代办在讲话中用“患难朋友才是真朋友”这一谚语来赞扬巴中两国人民的友谊。他说，我们永远不能忘记在去年九月间我们民族陷于生死存亡的危急关头，伟大的中国人民站起来用他的言语和行动，证明他们确实是我们真诚的朋友。不管发生什么事情，巴中友谊是牢不可破的。他对文化艺术团在北京受到的热情接待，表示衷心的感谢。
    杨纯在讲话中表示相信，中巴两国人民在反对帝国主义和扩张主义、维护民族独立和世界和平的斗争中建立起来的友谊，一定会进一步加强和发展。她指出，巴基斯坦艺术家在北京的精湛的演出，获得了很大的成功。她希望巴基斯坦艺术家把中国人民对巴基斯坦正义事业的支持转达给巴基斯坦人民。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有外交部副部长韩念龙以及我国有关方面负责人赵辛初、方石珊、戴爱莲、王元方、丁波等。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使罗查的夫人，以及巴基斯坦文化艺术团团长拉赫曼、副团长阿奇兹夫人和全体成员，出席了宴会。
