Title: Kashmir People Demonstrate Against Oppression by Indian Authorities; Indian Police Cruelly Suppress Protests, Killing and Wounding Eighty

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, April 4th—According to a report in the *Pakistan Times* on the 4th, the people in Indian-occupied Kashmir held demonstrations on April 2nd to protest Indian oppression. Local police opened fire on them and beat them with iron bars, killing five and seriously injuring approximately seventy-five.
    The demonstrators also held rallies. Speakers at the rallies condemned the deteriorating economic situation, rampant corruption, cronyism, regionalism, and other social evils.

### 克什米尔人民示威抗议印当局压迫  印度警察进行残酷镇压打死打伤八十人

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    克什米尔人民示威抗议印当局压迫
    印度警察进行残酷镇压打死打伤八十人
    新华社拉瓦尔品第四日电　据《巴基斯坦时报》四日报道，克什米尔印占区人民四月二日举行抗议印度压迫的示威，当地警察向他们开火，并用铁皮棍子打他们，五人被打死，大约七十五人受重伤。
    示威者还举行了集会。在会上讲话的人谴责经济情况的恶化，贪污、徇私、地方主义和其他社会罪恶的猖獗。
