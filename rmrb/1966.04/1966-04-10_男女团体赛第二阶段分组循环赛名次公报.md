Title: Ranking Announcement of the Second Stage Group Round Robin of Men's and Women's Team Competitions
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-10
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 9th (electric dispatch) – Ranking announcement of the second stage group round robin of the 1966 National Table Tennis Championships men's and women's team competitions.

    Men's Team Competition
    Group One, Group A – Shanghai First Team, Hebei First Team, Guangdong First Team, Jiangxi Team, Sichuan Team.
    Group One, Group B – Hubei Team, Beijing First Team, Shanghai Second Team, Jiangsu First Team, Jilin Team.
    Group Two, Group A – Jiangsu Second Team, Shanghai Third Team, Henan Team, Guangxi Team, Shaanxi Team.
    Group Two, Group B – Liaoning Team, Anhui Team, Fujian First Team, Guangdong Second Team, Hunan Team.
    Group Three, Group A – Beijing Second Team, Guizhou Team, Yunnan Team, Gansu Team, Hebei Third Team.
    Group Three, Group B – Zhejiang First Team, Heilongjiang Team, Hebei Second Team, Shanghai Fourth Team, Shandong Team.
    According to the competition rules, in the third stage of the competition starting tomorrow, teams with the same ranking in Group A and Group B of the first stage will compete against each other to determine the rankings from first to tenth; teams with the same ranking in Group A and Group B of the second stage will compete against each other to determine the rankings from eleventh to twentieth; teams with the same ranking in Group A and Group B of the third stage will compete against each other to determine the rankings from twenty-first to thirtieth.
    Group Four only has three teams, and the rankings have been determined today as follows: Thirty-first place, Qinghai Team; Thirty-second place, Fujian Second Team; Thirty-third place, Zhejiang Second Team.
    Women's Team Competition
    Group One, Group A – Shanghai First Team, Liaoning First Team, Hubei Team, Shandong First Team, Beijing Second Team.
    Group One, Group B – Sichuan Team, Guangdong First Team, Shanghai Second Team, Shanxi Team, Beijing First Team.
    Group Two, Group A – Jilin Team, Guangdong Second Team, Guangxi Team, Yunnan Team, Hunan Team.
    Group Two, Group B – Hebei First Team, Fujian First Team, Shanghai Third Team, Zhejiang First Team, Jiangsu Team.
    Group Three, Group A – Shanghai Fourth Team, Henan Second Team, Heilongjiang Team, Fujian Second Team, Hebei Second Team.
    Group Three, Group B – Anhui Team, Shandong Second Team, Guizhou Team, Liaoning Second Team, Gansu Team.
    As with the men's team competition, teams with the same ranking in Group A and Group B of the first group, Group A and Group B of the second group, and Group A and Group B of the third group in the women's competition will also compete against each other in the third stage to determine the rankings from first to tenth, eleventh to twentieth, and twenty-first to thirtieth, respectively.
    Group Four only has five teams, and the rankings have been determined today as follows: Thirty-first place, Jiangxi Team; Thirty-second place, Henan First Team; Thirty-third place, Shaanxi Team; Thirty-fourth place, Zhejiang Second Team; Thirty-fifth place, Qinghai Team.

### 男女团体赛第二阶段分组循环赛名次公报

1966-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

    男女团体赛第二阶段分组循环赛名次公报
    新华社上海九日电　一九六六年全国乒乓球锦标赛男、女团体赛第二阶段分组循环赛名次公报。
    男子团体赛
    第一组甲组——上海一队、河北一队、广东一队、江西队、四川队。
    第一组乙组——湖北队、北京一队、上海二队、江苏一队、吉林队。
    第二组甲组——江苏二队、上海三队、河南队、广西队、陕西队。
    第二组乙组——辽宁队、安徽队、福建一队、广东二队、湖南队。
    第三组甲组——北京二队、贵州队、云南队、甘肃队、河北三队。
    第三组乙组——浙江一队、黑龙江队、河北二队、上海四队、山东队。
    根据竞赛规则规定，在明天开始的第三阶段的比赛中，第二阶段第一组甲组和乙组同名次的队将相互交锋，决定第一到第十名的名次；第二组甲组和乙组同名次的队相互交锋，决定第十一到二十名的名次；第三组甲组和乙组同名次的队将相互交锋，决定第二十一到第三十名的名次。
    第四组只有三个队，今天比赛已决出名次如下：第三十一名青海队、第三十二名福建二队、第三十三名浙江二队。
    女子团体赛
    第一组甲组——上海一队、辽宁一队、湖北队、山东一队、北京二队。
    第一组乙组——四川队、广东一队、上海二队、山西队、北京一队。
    第二组甲组——吉林队、广东二队、广西队、云南队、湖南队。
    第二组乙组——河北一队、福建一队、上海三队、浙江一队、江苏队。
    第三组甲组——上海四队、河南二队、黑龙江队、福建二队、河北二队。
    第三组乙组——安徽队、山东二队、贵州队、辽宁二队、甘肃队。
    同男子团体赛一样，女子第一组甲组和乙组、第二组甲组和乙组、第三组甲组和乙组同名次的队，在第三阶段比赛中也相互交锋，分别决定第一到十、第十一到二十、第二十一到三十名的名次。
    第四组只有五个队，今天比赛已决出名次如下：第三十一名江西队、第三十二名河南一队、第三十三名陕西队、第三十四名浙江二队、第三十五名青海队。
