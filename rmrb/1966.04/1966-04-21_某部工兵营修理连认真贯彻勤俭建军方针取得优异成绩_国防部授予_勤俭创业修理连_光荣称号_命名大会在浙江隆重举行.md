Title: A Certain Engineering Corps Repair Company Earnestly Implements the Principle of Building the Army Through Diligence and Thrift, Achieving Outstanding Results; Ministry of National Defense Confers the Honorary Title of “Diligent and Thrifty Entrepreneurial Repair Company”; Naming Ceremony Grandly Held in Zhejiang

Author:

Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 2

    A Certain Engineering Corps Repair Company Earnestly Implements the Principle of Building the Army Through Diligence and Thrift, Achieving Outstanding Results
    Ministry of National Defense Confers the Honorary Title of “Diligent and Thrifty Entrepreneurial Repair Company”
    Naming Ceremony Grandly Held in Zhejiang
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Ministry of National Defense recently issued an order conferring the honorary title of "Diligent and Thrifty Entrepreneurial Repair Company" on the repair company of a certain engineering corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    The order pointed out that this repair company, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, creatively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, adhering to the "four firsts," earnestly implementing the principle of building the army through diligence and thrift, and carrying forward the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle, mobilized the masses and relied on the masses, achieving outstanding results in technical innovation.
    The order hopes that all commanders and fighters of the repair company will hold even higher the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, always give prominence to politics, remember Chairman Mao's teaching that "modesty helps one to make progress, while conceit makes one lag behind," carry forward the glorious tradition of modesty, prudence, and hard struggle, truly implement the Central Military Commission's guidelines and policies in the company, practically improve preparedness from the spiritual to the material, and strive to make greater contributions to national defense construction.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 20th - According to the order of the Ministry of National Defense, the naming ceremony to confer the title of "Diligent and Thrifty Entrepreneurial Repair Company" on the repair company of a certain engineering corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was grandly held in a certain location in Zhejiang today.
    Xu Shiyou, Vice Minister of National Defense, Secretary of the Secretariat of the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and person in charge of the Nanjing Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Bao Xianzhi, person in charge of the Nanjing Military Region; Li Weixin, Standing Committee Member of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPC and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province; as well as responsible persons of the local Party, government, and army, and representatives of fraternal units from across the army attended the conference.
    Xu Shiyou read out the order of the Ministry of National Defense conferring the title of "Diligent and Thrifty Entrepreneurial Repair Company" on the repair company at the conference, and on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense, presented a silk banner embroidered with the golden characters "Diligent and Thrifty Entrepreneurial Repair Company" to the repair company. At this moment, the entire venue applauded warmly to congratulate all the commanders and fighters of the repair company.
    In his speech at the naming ceremony, Xu Shiyou pointed out that the reason the repair company became such an advanced unit is that they used the great Mao Zedong Thought to arm revolutionary soldiers and workers. He called on everyone to learn from the repair company's fundamental experience of holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, giving prominence to politics, and creatively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works. Like the repair company, establish a firm concept of war preparedness and link one's work with safeguarding socialist construction in the motherland and supporting the revolutionary struggles of the world's people; like the repair company, dare to practice, dare to struggle, and carry forward the proletarian spirit of hard bones; like the repair company, implement the principle of self-reliance, building the country with diligence and thrift, building the army with diligence and thrift, and running all undertakings with diligence and thrift; like the repair company, rely on the masses, mobilize the masses, and adhere to the working method of the mass line. Maintain a hundredfold vigilance, fully prepare for combat, and be ready at any time to fight to the end to eliminate US imperialism that dares to invade our country.
    Chen Shijue, political instructor of the repair company, on behalf of all the commanders and fighters of the repair company, expressed at the meeting that they would firmly remember the teachings of the Party and Chairman Mao, remain neither arrogant nor rash, and in the future, hold even higher the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, regard Chairman Mao's books as the highest instructions for all tasks and as the criteria for all words and actions, constantly transform their thinking, improve their work, adhere to the path of self-reliance, implement the principle of diligence and thrift in starting undertakings, and continue to face construction and face war preparedness, and do a good job in mechanical repair.

### 某部工兵营修理连认真贯彻勤俭建军方针取得优异成绩  国防部授予“勤俭创业修理连”光荣称号  命名大会在浙江隆重举行

1966-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

    某部工兵营修理连认真贯彻勤俭建军方针取得优异成绩
    国防部授予“勤俭创业修理连”光荣称号
    命名大会在浙江隆重举行
    新华社二十日讯　国防部最近发布命令，授予中国人民解放军某部工兵营修理连以“勤俭创业修理连”光荣称号。
    命令指出，这个修理连，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，坚持四个第一，认真贯彻勤俭建军的方针，发扬了自力更生、艰苦奋斗的革命精神，发动群众，依靠群众，在技术革新方面取得了优异成绩。
    命令希望修理连全体指战员，更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，永远突出政治，牢记毛主席“虚心使人进步，骄傲使人落后”的教导，发扬谦虚谨慎、艰苦奋斗的光荣传统，使中央军委的方针政策在连队真正落实，从精神上到物质上切实地搞好战备，为国防建设作出更大的贡献而努力。
    新华社杭州二十日电　根据国防部的命令，授予中国人民解放军某部工兵营修理连以“勤俭创业修理连”称号的命名大会，今天在浙江某地隆重举行。
    国防部副部长、中共中央华东局书记处书记、中国人民解放军南京部队负责人许世友，南京部队负责人鲍先志，中共浙江省委常委、浙江省副省长李维新，以及当地的党、政府和军队的负责人和全军各兄弟部队代表出席了大会。
    许世友在大会上宣读了国防部授予这个修理连以“勤俭创业修理连”称号的命令，并且代表国防部把一面绣着“勤俭创业修理连”金字的锦旗授给修理连。这时，全场热烈鼓掌，向修理连全体指战员表示祝贺。
    许世友在命名大会上讲话中指出：修理连所以成为这样一个先进单位，是他们用伟大的毛泽东思想武装革命战士和工人的结果。他号召大家学习修理连高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出政治，活学活用毛主席著作这一条根本经验。象修理连那样，树立牢固的战备观念，把自己的工作同保卫祖国社会主义建设和支援世界人民革命斗争联系起来；象修理连那样，敢于实践、敢于斗争、发扬无产阶级的硬骨头精神；象修理连那样，贯彻自力更生，勤俭建国，勤俭建军，勤俭办一切事业的方针；象修理连那样，依靠群众，发动群众，坚持走群众路线的工作方法。百倍警惕，充分做好战斗准备，随时准备为消灭敢于侵犯我国的美帝国主义战斗到底。
    修理连政治指导员陈式珏代表修理连全体指战员在会上表示，一定牢记党和毛主席的教导，不骄不躁，今后要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，把毛主席的书当作各项工作的最高指示，当作一切言论行动的准则，不断改造思想，改进工作，坚持走自力更生的道路，贯彻勤俭创业的方针，继续面向施工，面向战备，做好机械修理工作。
