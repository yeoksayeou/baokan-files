Title: Afro-Asian Solidarity Organization of China and the Chinese Peace Committee Send Telegram to the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of Japan Supporting the Japanese People's Struggle Against US Imperialism's Occupation of Okinawa

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 5

    The Afro-Asian Solidarity Organization of China and the Chinese Peace Committee Send Telegram to the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of Japan Supporting the Japanese People's Struggle Against US Imperialism's Occupation of Okinawa
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 24th) The Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of China and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace today sent a telegram to the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of Japan, stating that US imperialism's occupation of Okinawa seriously undermines Japan's independence, violates Japan's sovereignty, and also seriously undermines peace in Asia and the world. The telegram pointed out that the Chinese people have always firmly supported the Japanese people's struggle to demand the return of Okinawa and Ogasawara, and deeply believe that the struggle of the great Japanese people will surely achieve victory. The Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of China and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sent the telegram to the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of Japan for the Japanese "April 28th" Okinawa Liberation Joint Action Day and the Japanese people's maritime demonstration demanding the return of Okinawa and Ogasawara.

### 我亚非团结会与和委会致电日本亚非团结委员会  支持日本人民反对美帝霸占冲绳的斗争

1966-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结会与和委会致电日本亚非团结委员会
    支持日本人民反对美帝霸占冲绳的斗争
    据新华社二十四日讯　中国亚非团结委员会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会今天打电报给日本亚非团结委员会说，美帝国主义霸占冲绳严重地破坏了日本的独立、侵犯了日本的主权，也严重地破坏了亚洲和世界和平。电报指出，中国人民一贯坚决支持日本人民要求归还冲绳和小笠原的斗争，并深信伟大的日本人民的斗争必然取得胜利。中国亚非团结委员会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会是为日本“四·二八”冲绳解放共同行动日和日本人民举行要求归还冲绳和小笠原海上示威大会致电日本亚非团结委员会的。
