Title: Danish, Finnish, Norwegian, and Swedish Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Mr. Holmegaard, international political editor of the Danish *Dagbladet Information*; Mr. Paulsen, feature editor and foreign affairs writer for the Danish News Agency; Mr. Valtasaari, editor of the Finnish Broadcasting Company; Mr. Rautonen, editor of the Finnish magazine *Avun Maailma*, Mr. Slaatten, Chairman of the Norwegian Press Association and editor of *Verdens Gang*; Mr. Tollefvik, editor of the Norwegian Bergen *Arbeiderbladet*; Mr. Brandtell, foreign affairs editor of the Swedish *Dagens Nyheter*; and Mr. Andersson, foreign affairs reporter of the Swedish Malmö *Arbetet*, who are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Zhou Xinwu, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Chen Weifan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and representatives of news organizations in the capital were at the airport to greet them.
    Officials from the Danish, Finnish, Norwegian, and Swedish embassies in China were also at the airport to greet them.

### 丹麦芬兰挪威瑞典新闻工作者到京

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    丹麦芬兰挪威瑞典新闻工作者到京
    新华社十六日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的丹麦《消息报》国际政治版编辑霍尔姆高，丹麦通讯社特写部编辑兼外事撰稿人鲍尔森；芬兰广播公司编辑瓦尔塔沙里，芬兰《帮助》杂志编辑拉乌托宁；挪威新闻协会主席、《世界之路》报编辑斯莱顿，挪威卑尔根《工人报》编辑托勒维克；瑞典《每日新闻》外事编辑勃朗台尔，瑞典马尔摩《劳动报》外事记者安德逊等，于今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有广播事业局副局长周新武、外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆，以及首都新闻单位的代表。
    丹麦、芬兰、挪威、瑞典四国驻华大使馆的官员也到机场迎接。
