Title: Chairman Ne Win to Visit Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 17th – A press release issued here on the 16th by the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced that General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, will pay a friendly visit to Pakistan from May 7th to 11th.


**Named Entities**

奈温  Nài Wēn (Ne Win)
巴基斯坦 Bājīsītǎn (Pakistan)

### 奈温主席将访问巴基斯坦

1966-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    奈温主席将访问巴基斯坦
    新华社仰光十七日电　缅甸外交部十六日在这里发表的一项新闻公报中宣布，缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军，将于五月七日到十一日对巴基斯坦进行友好访问。
