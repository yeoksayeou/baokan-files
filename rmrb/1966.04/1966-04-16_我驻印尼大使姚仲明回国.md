Title: My Ambassador to Indonesia, Yao Zhongming, Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 1

    My Ambassador to Indonesia, Yao Zhongming, Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, April 15th—Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Indonesia, left Jakarta on April 7th to return home for consultations and arrived in Beijing on the 13th.  Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Fang Fang, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and other responsible persons from relevant departments were at the airport to greet him.

### 我驻印尼大使姚仲明回国

1966-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印尼大使姚仲明回国
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使姚仲明四月七日离开雅加达返国述职，已于十三日回到北京。到机场迎接的，有外交部副部长韩念龙、华侨事务委员会副主任方方以及其他有关方面负责人。
