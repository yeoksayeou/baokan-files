Title: Sino-African Friendship Association Tea Party Celebrates Sixth Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The Chinese-African People's Friendship Association held a tea party this afternoon to celebrate the sixth anniversary of its founding.
    African foreign guests, experts, and students in Beijing were invited to attend the tea party.
    Nan Hanchen, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, delivered a speech at the tea party, warmly praising the profound friendship forged between the Chinese and African people in their struggle against imperialism and neo-colonialism. He said that this fighting friendship cannot be destroyed by any imperialism or reactionaries. We believe that with the joint efforts of the Chinese people and the people of African countries, our friendship will be further developed. Joining the African friends at the tea party were Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, as well as Song Changhua, Shen Zijiu, Wang Rensan, Xin Yi, and other council members of the association in Beijing and responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 中非友协茶会庆祝成立六周年

1966-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中非友协茶会庆祝成立六周年
    新华社十二日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会今天下午举行茶会，庆祝中国非洲人民友好协会成立六周年。
    在北京的非洲外宾、专家和留学生应邀出席了茶会。
    中非友协副会长南汉宸在茶会上致词，热烈赞颂中国人民和非洲人民在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义斗争中结成的深厚友谊。他说，这种战斗友谊是任何帝国主义和反动派破坏不了的。我们相信，在中国人民和非洲各国人民的共同努力下，我们的友谊必将得到进一步的发展。同非洲朋友在茶会上欢聚的，还有中非友协副会长张铁生，以及在北京的中非友协理事和有关方面负责人宋常华、沈兹九、王人三、辛毅等。
