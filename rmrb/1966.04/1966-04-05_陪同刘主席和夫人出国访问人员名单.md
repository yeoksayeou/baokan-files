Title: List of Personnel Accompanying Chairman Liu and his Wife on a State Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th - In addition to Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qian, the following personnel are accompanying Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei on their visit to Afghanistan:
    Zhang Hanfu		Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
    Wang Dongxing		Vice Minister of Public Security
    Feng Xuan		Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    Wang Daohan		Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries
    Lai Yali		Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
    Zhang Tong		Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

    张彤　外交部第一亚洲司司长
