Title: Speech by Mr. Kagisa, Head of the Tanzanian Journalists Delegation: The Tanzanian People are Determined to Overthrow Imperialism and Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 3

　　新华社二十三日讯　坦桑尼亚新闻工作者代表团团长彼得·卡吉萨，二十二日在亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）上发言，强烈谴责美国“中央情报局”策划颠覆非洲国家的进步政府，破坏非洲统一组织和搞垮非洲有影响的进步领袖等阴谋活动。

　　卡吉萨说，已经证明，进行斗争的不是武器，而是只有人民的勇气和决心才能赢得胜利。我们代表团要求这次会议要通过决议，坚决支持津巴布韦人民、强烈谴责受到英国殖民主义者支持的史密斯政权，要求我们在亚非洲的弟兄们反对和揭露由英国所策划的史密斯单方面宣布独立的作法，并给津巴布韦的斗争以全面支持。津巴布韦人民通过他们的坚决斗争，终将粉碎这个移民政权和它的殖民主子。

　　他说，有一些国家假装着是非洲的朋友，然而却通过后门去帮助史密斯。不仅弟兄们应当强烈谴责这些国家，而且这次会议也应当通过一个决议来强烈谴责它们。

　　卡吉萨谴责殖民主义者对坦桑尼亚政府所进行的破坏和颠覆活动。他说，坦桑尼亚在尼雷尔总统的领导下，击败了帝国主义的一切努力。因为我们是团结一致的。团结是我们的武器，我们将继续利用它来把帝国主义和新老殖民主义永远打垮。他说，尼雷尔已向世界表明，如果亚非国家仍然受到殖民统治或受到帝国主义者的轰炸、干涉和骚扰的话，坦桑尼亚将不会对自己的自由感到自豪。他说，我们完全支持现在遭受反动派和帝国主义者的迫害，今天在监狱中受苦的我们在加纳、印度尼西亚和亚丁的新闻工作者弟兄们。

　　他说，我们正式表示，我们完全支持亚非新闻工作者协会总书记所作的总报告和关于书记处撤离雅加达的报告。

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. Peter Kagisa, head of the Tanzanian Journalists Delegation, spoke on the 22nd at the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Expanded) of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association, strongly condemning the U.S. "Central Intelligence Agency" for plotting to subvert the progressive governments of African countries, undermine the Organization of African Unity, and destroy influential progressive leaders in Africa, and other conspiratorial activities.

Kagisa said that it has been proven that it is not weapons that wage the struggle, but only the courage and determination of the people can win victory. Our delegation requests that this meeting adopt a resolution to firmly support the people of Zimbabwe, strongly condemn the Smith regime supported by British colonialists, and demand that our brothers in Asia and Africa oppose and expose the British-orchestrated Smith's unilateral declaration of independence and give full support to the struggle in Zimbabwe. The people of Zimbabwe, through their resolute struggle, will eventually crush this immigrant regime and its colonial masters.

He said that some countries are pretending to be friends of Africa, but are helping Smith through the back door. Not only should our brothers strongly condemn these countries, but this meeting should also pass a resolution to strongly condemn them.

Kagisa condemned the sabotage and subversive activities carried out by colonialists against the Tanzanian government. He said that Tanzania, under the leadership of President Nyerere, has defeated all the efforts of imperialism, because we are united. Unity is our weapon, and we will continue to use it to forever overthrow imperialism and neo-colonialism. He said that Nyerere has made it clear to the world that Tanzania will not be proud of its freedom if Asian and African countries are still under colonial rule or are bombed, interfered with, and harassed by imperialists. He said that we fully support our journalist brothers in Ghana, Indonesia, and Aden who are now suffering persecution by reactionaries and imperialists and are suffering in prison today.

He said that we formally express our full support for the general report made by the Secretary-General of the Asian-African Journalists Association and the report on the Secretariat's withdrawal from Jakarta.
