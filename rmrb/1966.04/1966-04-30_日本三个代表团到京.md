Title: Three Japanese Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: At the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the delegation from the Japan Local Assembly Members League for the Promotion of International Trade, led by Miura Hachiro, president of the League, consisting of eighteen members; and the Amagasaki Economic Friendship Delegation, led by Omori Motoichi, vice president of the Amagasaki Chamber of Commerce and Industry, consisting of twelve members, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Also arriving on the same flight was the Japanese Women Local Assembly Members Delegation, led by Goto Mitsuru, a member of the Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly, consisting of seven members, invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the All-China Women's Federation.
    Zhang Huadong, Yang Yunyu, Wang Xiaoyun, and others went to the airport to welcome the Japanese guests.
    Japanese friends from trade circles in Beijing also went to welcome them.

### 日本三个代表团到京

1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本三个代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请，以日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟会长三浦八郎为首的日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟访华代表团一行十八人，以尼崎市商工会议所副会长大森基一为首的尼崎市经济友好代表团一行十二人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    同机到达的还有应中国国际贸易促进委员会和全国妇联邀请的、由日本东京都议员后藤满率领的日本妇女地方议员代表团一行七人。
    前往机场欢迎日本客人的有张化东、杨蕴玉、王晓云等。
    在京的日本友好贸易界人士也前往欢迎。
