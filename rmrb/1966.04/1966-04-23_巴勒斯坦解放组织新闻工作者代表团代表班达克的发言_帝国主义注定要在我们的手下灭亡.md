Title: Speech by Mr. Bandak, Representative of the Palestinian Liberation Organization Journalist Delegation: Imperialism is Destined to Perish at Our Hands
Author:
Date: 1966-04-23
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd – Mazin Bandak, representative of the Palestinian Liberation Organization journalist delegation, pointed out in his speech at the Fourth Plenary (Enlarged) Meeting of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association on the morning of the 21st that "the Middle East is now facing a new great wave of revolutionary struggle." He said: "Imperialism is destined to perish, but it will perish at our hands. National liberation movements have no other way to go but to fight. We either fight or surrender, and we choose to fight."
    Bandak said that the Palestinian problem is the experience of a people whose country was driven out of their homes by the aggression of Zionism and imperialism, headed by the barbaric force of the United States. Today, our peoples in various countries are suffering from this kind of imperialist aggression everywhere. The so-called State of Israel is nothing more than a bridgehead of American imperialism aimed at the gateway to West Asia. We are fighting this imperialist fortress. It is entirely reasonable to say that this political germ is destined to perish. We live in an era where people decide their own destiny, and in an era where the fragile countries of imperialism and its puppets are being thrown into the dustbin of history.
    Bandak said: The United States is now openly coming forward to show the world that ample American military aid is flowing into Israel. Our nation is preparing to crush new aggression plans.
    He said: President Johnson, who is now waging war in Asia, announced last April his decision to ship American weapons to Israel. The first batch of two hundred Patton tanks has already been shipped, and more will be shipped. Our people are far from being intimidated and are now more determined than ever to fight. We firmly believe that steel and all modern military equipment cannot change the ultimate failure of imperialism. Man remains the master of his own destiny and battlefield.
    Bandak said: We admire with the utmost respect the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people against U.S. imperialism. It is this great struggle that has aroused the spirit of daring to think in the people of the world. It is irrefutable evidence that defeating imperialism is not impossible. Defeating imperialism is not impossible when the peoples of various countries are determined to fight and ready to sacrifice.
    Bandak said: After hearing the report of the Secretariat, our delegation believes that the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association can no longer stay in Jakarta after the recent bloody events in Indonesia. We believe that in order to realize the purpose of the Constitution of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, it is very necessary for the Afro-Asian Journalists Association to find a new venue. He said: The Palestinian delegation is very happy to attend this meeting in the great city of Beijing—the capital of the People's Republic of China, the center of revolutionary inspiration, the vanguard of anti-imperialist struggle and indomitable determination.

### 巴勒斯坦解放组织新闻工作者代表团代表班达克的发言  帝国主义注定要在我们的手下灭亡

1966-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦解放组织新闻工作者代表团代表班达克的发言
    帝国主义注定要在我们的手下灭亡
    新华社二十二日讯　巴勒斯坦解放组织新闻工作者代表团代表马津·班达克，二十一日上午在亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）上的发言中指出：“中东现在面临着新的伟大的革命斗争浪潮”。他说：“帝国主义注定要灭亡，但是是在我们的手下灭亡。民族解放运动除了战斗之外没有别的路可走。我们要么进行战斗，要么就是投降，我们选择的是战斗。”
    班达克说，巴勒斯坦问题是被以美国野蛮力量为首的犹太复国主义和帝国主义的侵略赶出他们的家园的一个国家的人民的经历。今天我们各国人民到处都遭到这种帝国主义侵略。所谓的以色列国家只不过是美国帝国主义针对亚洲西部大门的一个桥头堡而已。我们正在对这一帝国主义堡垒进行战斗。说这一政治细菌注定要灭亡是完全合情合理的。我们生活在人民决定自己命运的时代，生活在帝国主义及其傀儡的不堪一击的国家正在被抛进历史垃圾箱里的时代。
    班达克说：美国现在正在公开出面，向全世界表明，美国充足的军事援助正源源不断地流入以色列。我们的民族正在准备粉碎新的侵略计划。
    他说：现在正在亚洲进行战争的约翰逊总统去年四月宣布他决定运送美国武器去以色列。头一批二百辆巴顿式坦克已经运到，还要再运来。我们的人民远远没有被吓倒，现在比以往任何时候更坚决地进行斗争。我们坚信，钢和一切现代化的军事装备都改变不了帝国主义的最后失败。人仍然是自己的命运和战场的主人。
    班达克说：我们极为钦佩地注视着越南人民反对美帝国主义的英勇斗争。正是这场伟大的斗争激起了全世界人民敢想的精神。它是无可辩驳的证据证明打败帝国主义并不是不可能的。当各国人民决心战斗和准备牺牲时，打败帝国主义并不是不可能的。
    班达克说：听了书记处的报告以后，我们的代表团相信，在印度尼西亚发生了最近的血腥的事态以后，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处不能再呆在雅加达了。我们认为，为了实现亚非记协章程的宗旨，亚非记协寻找一个新的会址是非常必要的。他说：巴勒斯坦代表团在伟大的城市北京——中华人民共和国首都、革命鼓舞的中心、反帝斗争和不屈不挠的决心的先锋——出席这次会议，感到十分高兴。
