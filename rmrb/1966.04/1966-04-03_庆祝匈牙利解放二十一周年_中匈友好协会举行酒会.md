Title: China-Hungary Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate the 21st Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. The China-Hungary Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 21st anniversary of Hungary's liberation.
    Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General of the China-Hungary Friendship Association, and Halász József, the Hungarian Ambassador to China, toasted at the reception for the further development of the friendship between the people of China and Hungary.
    Chen Boqing, Jin Guangzu, Cui Baolin, and other responsible persons from relevant departments attended the reception.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 1st, the Huangtugang China-Hungary Friendship People's Commune in Beijing held a film soiree today to celebrate the 21st anniversary of Hungary's liberation. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Halász József, and his wife, along with other embassy officials, were invited to attend the soiree.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放二十一周年  中匈友好协会举行酒会

1966-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放二十一周年
    中匈友好协会举行酒会
    新华社二日讯　中国匈牙利友好协会今天下午举行酒会，庆祝匈牙利解放二十一周年。
    中匈友协秘书长张联华和匈牙利驻中国大使郝拉思·尤若夫在酒会上为中匈两国人民的友谊进一步发展干杯。
    出席酒会的有陈伯清、金光祖、崔保琳等有关方面负责人。
    据新华社一日讯　北京市黄土岗中匈友好人民公社今天举行电影晚会，庆祝匈牙利解放二十一周年。匈牙利驻华大使郝拉思·尤若夫和夫人及使馆其他官员应邀出席了晚会。
