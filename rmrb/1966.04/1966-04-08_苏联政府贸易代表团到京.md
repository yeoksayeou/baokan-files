Title: Soviet Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: The head of the Soviet Government Trade Delegation, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Grishin, and the deputy head, Director of the Oriental Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Shilyayev, arrived in Beijing by train this morning.
    Greeting them at the station were Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Fu Shenglin and others.
    Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Mochuli, and some members of the Soviet Government Trade Delegation who arrived in Beijing earlier also welcomed them at the station.

### 苏联政府贸易代表团到京

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社七日讯　苏联政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长格里申，副团长、对外贸易部东方局局长施利亚耶夫，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有对外贸易部副部长傅生麟等。
    苏联驻华大使馆临时代办莫初黎和先期到达北京的苏联政府贸易代表团部分团员也到车站迎接。
