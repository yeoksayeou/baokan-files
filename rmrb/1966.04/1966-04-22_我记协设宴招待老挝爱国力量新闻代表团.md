Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet for Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. Fan Jin, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet this evening in honor of the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation, headed by Zhalen Wengsangang, director of the "Voice of Laos" radio station.  Among those attending the banquet were Li Wu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Zhou Xinwu, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and Hu Chi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the *Liberation Army Daily*.

### 我记协设宴招待老挝爱国力量新闻代表团

1966-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协设宴招待老挝爱国力量新闻代表团
    新华社二十一日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席范瑾今晚设宴招待以“老挝之声”电台台长扎伦·翁桑昂为首的老挝爱国力量新闻代表团。出席宴会的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李伍、广播事业局副局长周新武和《解放军报》副总编辑胡痴等人。
