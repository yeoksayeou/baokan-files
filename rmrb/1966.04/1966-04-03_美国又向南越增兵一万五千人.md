Title: The United States Again Increases Troops in South Vietnam by 15,000

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-03
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 1st -- According to announcements by U.S. government officials on March 31, the United States increased its troop deployment in South Vietnam by 15,000 in March, bringing the total number of American troops invading Vietnam to 230,000.

    U.S. President Johnson stated at a press conference held at the White House on the same day that the United States would continue to send troops to South Vietnam in "the numbers deemed necessary" by the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff and the commander of U.S. forces invading Vietnam, Westmoreland.

### 美国又向南越增兵一万五千人

1966-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美国又向南越增兵一万五千人
    新华社一日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国政府官员三月三十一日宣布，美国在三月份又向南越增兵一万五千人，使侵越美军总数达到了二十三万人。
    美国总统约翰逊同一天在白宫举行的记者招待会上表示，美国还将按照美国参谋长联席会议和侵越美军司令威斯特摩兰“认为需要的数目”继续向南越增派军队。
