Title: A Guest from Nepal Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Mr. K.S. Thapa, Assistant Private Secretary to the Crown Prince of Nepal, arrived in Beijing on the 4th for a visit to China at the invitation of the China-Nepal Friendship Association.
    Zhou Shujia, Shen Qiyi and other responsible persons from relevant departments went to the airport to welcome him.
    Personnel from the Nepalese Embassy in China were also at the airport to greet him.

### 尼泊尔一客人到京

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔一客人到京
    新华社五日讯　应中国尼泊尔友好协会邀请来我国参观访问的尼泊尔王太子助理私人秘书克·斯·塔帕，于四日到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有周叔迦、沈其益等有关方面负责人。
    到机场迎接的还有尼泊尔驻华使馆人员。
