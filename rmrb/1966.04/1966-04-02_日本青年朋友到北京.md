Title: Japanese Youth Friends Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st—A delegation of nine members from the Japanese Socialist Youth League, led by the League's Chairman, Hajime Fukada, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation. The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Qian Dawei, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Yue Min, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, and others.



**Named Entities**


中华全国青年联合会	All-China Youth Federation
日本社会主义青年同盟	Japanese Socialist Youth League
深田肇	Fukada Hajime
钱大卫	Qian Dawei
中日友协	Sino-Japanese Friendship Association
岳民	Yue Min
北京	Beijing

### 日本青年朋友到北京

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本青年朋友到北京
    新华社一日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的日本社会主义青年同盟代表团一行九人，在这个同盟的委员长深田肇率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团在机场受到全国青联副主席钱大卫、中日友协副秘书长岳民等人的热烈欢迎。
