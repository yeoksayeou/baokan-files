Title: Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 15th April. Romanian newspapers published on the 14th a communique regarding the plenary session of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee.
    The communique stated that the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party held a plenary session from April 12th to 13th, 1966. The plenary session discussed the following issues:
    (1) On increasing party membership, the composition of the party, and its organizational structure.
    (2) On the situation of cadres in party and state organs and institutions in 1965.
    (3) Improving the organizational work of the Young Pioneers.
    (4) The situation of resolving various suggestions, opinions, and requests made by the working people to the party and state organs.
    (5) Reorganizing the Party History Institute.
    The communique stated that the plenary session unanimously adopted corresponding resolutions on all the issues discussed.

### 罗马尼亚共产党中央举行全会

1966-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚共产党中央举行全会
    新华社布加勒斯特十五日电　罗马尼亚报纸十四日发表了罗马尼亚共产党举行中央全会的公报。
    公报说，罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会于一九六六年四月十二日——十三日举行了全会。全会讨论了下列问题：
    （一）关于增加党员人数、党的成分和组织机构
    （二）关于一九六五年党和国家机关和机构的干部情况
    （三）改进少先队的组织工作
    （四）解决劳动人民向党和国家机关提出的各种建议、意见和要求的情况
    （五）改组党史研究所
    公报说，全会就所有讨论的问题，一致通过了相应的决议。
