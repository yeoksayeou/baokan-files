Title: Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and Pakistani People (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 6

    Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and Pakistani People
    At the invitation of Pakistani President Mohammad Ayub Khan, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, Wang Guangmei, continued their friendly visit to Pakistan and visited East Pakistan from April 15th to 17th. They were accompanied by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and others.
    Before the civic reception in Dhaka, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, President Ayub Khan, Governor of East Pakistan Abdul Monem Khan, and Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto and his wife, posed for a group photo.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, accompanied by President Ayub Khan and Governor of East Pakistan Abdul Monem Khan, watched a folk song and dance performance in Dhaka, and then applauded and thanked the actors who lined up to see them off.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other distinguished Chinese guests were greeted by hundreds of thousands of people along the way. The welcomers shouted, "Long live Pakistan-China friendship!"

### 中巴两国人民的友谊万岁（图片）

1966-04-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中巴两国人民的友谊万岁
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美，应巴基斯坦总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗的邀请，继续对巴基斯坦进行友好访问，于四月十五日至十七日访问了东巴基斯坦。陪同访问的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人、外交部副部长章汉夫等。
    在达卡的市民招待会前，刘少奇主席和夫人、陈毅副总理和夫人同阿尤布·汗总统、东巴基斯坦省督阿卜杜勒·穆奈姆·汗和巴基斯坦外交部长布托及夫人等一起合影留念。
    刘少奇主席和夫人由阿尤布·汗总统和东巴基斯坦省督阿卜杜勒·穆奈姆·汗陪同，在达卡观看了民间歌舞演出后，向列队欢送的演员们鼓掌致谢。
    刘少奇主席等中国贵宾受到数十万群众的夹道欢迎。欢迎者高呼：“巴中友好万岁！”　	　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　戈春江摄
