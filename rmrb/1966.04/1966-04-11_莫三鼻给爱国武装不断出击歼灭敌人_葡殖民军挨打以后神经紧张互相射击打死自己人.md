Title: Mozambican Patriotic Armed Forces Continuously Attack and Annihilate the Enemy; Portuguese Colonial Army, Beaten and Battered, Becomes Nervous and Shoots Each Other, Killing Their Own

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam dispatch: Mozambican patriotic armed forces have recently launched continuous attacks in the northern region, annihilating many Portuguese colonial soldiers.
    According to a communique issued here on March 30 by the headquarters of the Mozambique Liberation Front, Mozambican freedom fighters recently killed 28 Portuguese colonial soldiers in the northern provinces of Niassa and Cabo Delgado in the northeast corner, seizing a number of rifles, ammunition, grenades and heavy machine guns.
    On February 20, freedom fighters laid mines on the railway line between Nampula and Cuamba in Niassa Province, destroying a train carrying Portuguese soldiers and grain. Many Portuguese soldiers were killed. On February 17, freedom fighters killed 12 Portuguese soldiers in an attack launched in Chipande.
    The communique said that with the continuous development of the people's war in Mozambique, the Portuguese colonial army's fear has deepened, and they are extremely nervous, to the point of shooting each other, killing their own. For example, on March 8, two teams of Portuguese colonial soldiers in Mueda, Cabo Delgado Province, mistook each other for Mozambican people's guerrillas and shot at each other, resulting in five Portuguese soldiers dead and eight wounded.

### 莫三鼻给爱国武装不断出击歼灭敌人  葡殖民军挨打以后神经紧张互相射击打死自己人

1966-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

    莫三鼻给爱国武装不断出击歼灭敌人
    葡殖民军挨打以后神经紧张互相射击打死自己人
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆电  莫三鼻给爱国武装最近在北部地区不断出击，歼灭许多葡萄牙殖民军。
    据莫三鼻给解放阵线总部三月三十日在这里发表的一项公报说，莫三鼻给自由战士最近在北部的尼亚萨省和东北角的德尔加多角省打死葡萄牙殖民军二十八人，缴获不少步枪、弹药、手榴弹和重机枪。
    自由战士二月二十日在尼亚萨省的楠普拉和卡土尔之间的铁路线上埋设地雷，炸毁载运葡萄牙士兵和粮食的火车一列。许多葡萄牙士兵被炸死。自由战士二月十七日在契潘迪发动的一次袭击中，打死葡萄牙士兵十二人。
    公报说，在莫三鼻给人民战争不断发展的情况下，葡萄牙殖民军的恐惧心理日益加深，他们神经极为紧张，以致互相开枪，打死自己人。例如，三月八日，两队葡萄牙殖民军在德尔加多角省的穆安达，就错把对方当作莫三鼻给人民游击队，互相射击，结果葡萄牙士兵五名死亡，八名受伤。
