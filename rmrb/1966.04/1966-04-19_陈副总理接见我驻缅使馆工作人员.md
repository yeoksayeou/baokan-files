Title: Vice Premier Chen Receives Staff of Our Embassy in Burma
Author:
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th -- Vice Premier Chen Yi today received all the staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Burma and held talks with them.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi also received the Chinese experts working in Burma and the Chinese students studying in Rangoon.

### 陈副总理接见我驻缅使馆工作人员

1966-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    陈副总理接见我驻缅使馆工作人员
    新华社仰光十八日电　陈毅副总理今天接见了中国驻缅甸大使馆全体工作人员并同他们进行了谈话。
    陈毅副总理还接见了在缅甸工作的中国专家和在仰光学习的中国留学生。
