Title: Learn from the Revolutionary Spirit of the Martyrs and Carry the Revolution Through to the End. People Across the Country Commemorate Revolutionary Martyrs During the Qingming Festival.
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 5th. In the days leading up to the Qingming Festival, people from all over the country have been flocking to the tombs of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary martyrs' monuments to mourn the martyrs and resolve to learn from their revolutionary spirit and carry the revolution through to the end.
    In the past few days, people in the capital have been continuously visiting the Monument to the People's Heroes in Tiananmen Square and the Beijing Revolutionary Cemetery to mourn the revolutionary martyrs. Many wreaths were placed in front of the Monument to the People's Heroes in Tiananmen Square. Many ribbons on the wreaths were inscribed with words expressing people's remembrance of the martyrs and their determination to inherit their legacy. Many young people solemnly swore an oath in front of the monument, holding high the red flag, expressing their determination to inherit the will of the martyrs and to fight to the end for the socialist revolution and socialist construction of our country, and for the cause of human liberation.
    In recent days, many people have also gone to the Beijing Revolutionary Cemetery to mourn the martyrs. In front of the tombs or memorial halls of martyrs such as Qu Qiubai, Ren Bishi, and Lin Boqu, there were wreaths and bouquets offered by the people.
    Nearly 300,000 people from all walks of life in Shanghai have visited the revolutionary martyrs' cemetery in the past few days to hold commemorative activities. Workers, peasants, PLA soldiers, cadres from government offices, and young people studied the deeds of the revolutionary martyrs and read the poems written by them in front of the martyrs' tombs. Some factories, schools, and military units also held report meetings and symposiums in front of the martyrs' tombs to conduct class education and revolutionary tradition education for workers, students, and soldiers.
    In Nanjing, more than 80,000 people have come to the evergreen Yuhuatai in the past few days to mourn the revolutionary martyrs. More than 600 representatives from the Party and government organs of Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, people's organizations, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing held a memorial ceremony in front of the Monument to the Martyrs who died in Yuhuatai today. People who came to mourn the martyrs also visited the exhibition room of historical materials of revolutionary martyrs to see the portraits, letters, and relics of the martyrs.
    More than 3,500 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Urumqi went to the Yan'erwo Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in the southern suburbs this morning to mourn Comrade Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the Communist Party of China, and Comrade Mao Zemin and other martyrs, who were members of the CPC. Leaders of the Party, government, and army of the autonomous region and Urumqi City also attended the memorial ceremony and laid wreaths in front of the martyrs' tombs. After the memorial ceremony, people also visited the exhibition room of revolutionary martyrs' deeds.
    In the past few days, more than 17,000 people from Urumqi City have come to the Martyrs' Cemetery to mourn the revolutionary martyrs, and the monument was filled with wreaths from people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life. People of all ethnic groups and children also planted trees around the martyrs' tombs.
    The leaders of the Party and government of Guangzhou, representatives of various agencies, organizations, and local organizations of democratic parties in Guangzhou braved the rain today to hold memorial ceremonies in front of the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs' Cemetery and the Tomb of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang, and other martyrs' tombs.

### 学习先烈革命精神将革命进行到底  清明时节各地人民悼念革命先烈

1966-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    学习先烈革命精神将革命进行到底
    清明时节各地人民悼念革命先烈
    新华社五日讯　在临近清明节的这几天里，各地人民纷纷到革命烈士墓和革命烈士纪念碑前悼念先烈，决心学习先烈的革命精神，将革命进行到底。
    首都人民这几天络绎不绝地来到天安门广场人民英雄纪念碑前和北京市革命公墓悼念革命先烈。天安门广场人民英雄纪念碑前摆着许多花圈。不少花圈的飘带上写着人们怀念先烈和决心继承先烈遗志的词句。许多青少年在纪念碑前高举红旗庄严宣誓，表示要继承先烈遗志，为我国的社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业，为人类的解放事业奋斗到底。
    最近几天，还有许多人前往北京市革命公墓悼念先烈。在瞿秋白、任弼时、林伯渠等先烈的墓或灵堂前，摆着人们献的花圈和花束。
    上海市各界人民近三十万人这几天前往革命烈士墓地举行了纪念活动。工人、农民、解放军战士、机关干部和青少年，在烈士墓前学习革命烈士的斗争事迹，朗读革命烈士写下的诗篇。有些工厂、学校和部队还在烈士墓前举行报告会和座谈会，向工人、学生、战士进行阶级教育和革命传统教育。
    在南京市，这几天有八万多人来到松柏挺立的雨花台悼念革命先烈。江苏省和南京市的党政机关、人民团体以及中国人民解放军驻南京部队指战员代表六百多人，今天在雨花台主峰死难烈士纪念碑前举行了悼念仪式。前来悼念烈士的人们还纷纷到革命烈士史料陈列室参观了烈士们的遗象、书信和遗物。
    乌鲁木齐市各民族各界三千五百多人，今天上午到南郊燕儿窝革命烈士陵园，悼念中国共产党创始人之一的陈潭秋烈士和中共党员毛泽民等烈士。自治区和乌鲁木齐市党、政府、军队的负责人也参加了悼念仪式，并在烈士墓前献了花圈。悼念仪式结束后，人们还参观了革命烈士事迹陈列室。
    这几天乌鲁木齐市前来烈士陵园悼念革命先烈的有一万七千多人，在纪念碑前摆满了各民族各界人民献的花圈。各民族人民和少年儿童还在烈士墓四周栽植了树木。
    广州市党和政府的负责人，广州市各机关、团体、民主党派地方组织的代表，今天冒雨到广州起义烈士陵园和黄花岗七十二烈士墓等烈士墓前举行了纪念仪式。
