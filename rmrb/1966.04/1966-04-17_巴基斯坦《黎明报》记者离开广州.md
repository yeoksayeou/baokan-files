Title: Pakistani Dawn Reporter Leaves Guangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th — Ijaz Hussain, a reporter for the Pakistani newspaper *Dawn*, left Guangzhou on the 16th after completing his visit to China.
    During his time in China, he visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places.

### 巴基斯坦《黎明报》记者离开广州

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦《黎明报》记者离开广州
    新华社广州十六日电　巴基斯坦《黎明报》记者伊查兹·侯赛因于十六日结束在中国访问之后离开广州。
    他在中国期间，曾在北京、上海、杭州等地访问。
