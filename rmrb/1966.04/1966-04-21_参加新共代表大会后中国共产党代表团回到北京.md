Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending New Zealand Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 20th – The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the CPC, which went to New Zealand to attend the 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand, returned to Beijing by plane today.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee of the CPC; Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the CPC; and leading staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs including Xu Li, Zhang Xiangshan, Zhao Anbo, and Shen Jian.

### 参加新共代表大会后中国共产党代表团回到北京

1966-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    参加新共代表大会后
    中国共产党代表团回到北京
    新华社二十日讯　应新西兰共产党的邀请，前往新西兰参加新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会的中国共产党代表团，在中共中央委员刘宁一率领下，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有：中共中央委员伍修权，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，中共中央机关负责工作人员许立、张香山、赵安博、申健等。
