Title: Asian, African, and Latin American Public Opinion Supports the Struggle of the Dominican People; The Dominican People Will Surely Defeat the American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Asian, African, and Latin American Public Opinion Supports the Struggle of the Dominican People
    The Dominican People Will Surely Defeat the American Aggressors
    (Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, 25th) Youths and Dominican expatriates in Caracas, the capital of Venezuela, held a demonstration on the 24th to commemorate the first anniversary of the Dominican people’s armed struggle to resist American aggression and defend their country. The demonstrators carried banners with anti-American slogans and slogans supporting the Dominican people's struggle as they marched through the main streets of the city center, chanting slogans such as "Yankees get out of the Dominican Republic" and "Get out of Latin America."
    (Xinhua News Agency, San José, 22nd) Public opinion in Costa Rica has recently issued statements condemning the continued occupation of the Dominican Republic by American aggressor troops and actively supporting the just, patriotic, and anti-American struggle of the Dominican people.
    The National Committee in Solidarity with the Dominican Republic issued a statement strongly condemning the Johnson administration, calling on people's organizations, progressive groups, and all patriots to oppose the "hateful" and "illegal" interference of the United States in the internal affairs of the Dominican Republic, and firmly supporting the heroic struggle of the Dominican people. The statement also demanded that the Costa Rican government withdraw the twenty-one military police it sent to the Dominican Republic to act as accomplices of the American aggressors.
    (Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 24th) According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the Vietnam South and Latin American People’s Solidarity Committee recently sent a telegram to the Dominican people for the "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People," stating that the South Vietnamese people and the Dominican people will surely defeat the common enemy of the two peoples, the American aggressors.
    The telegram expressed sympathy and full support for the Dominican people who are bravely fighting against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs, and expressed hope that the fighting friendship between the Vietnamese and Dominican peoples would further develop.
    (Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th) The Korean "Rodong Sinmun" published an editorial on the 24th on the "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People," stating that the Korean people actively support the Dominican people's struggle.
    The editorial emphasized that the Dominican Republic belongs to the Dominican people, and the Dominican issue must be resolved by the Dominican people themselves. No one has the right to interfere in the internal affairs of the Dominican Republic. U.S. imperialism and the counter-revolutionary interventionist forces in Latin America must immediately stop their barbaric intervention in the Dominican Republic.
    (Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 21st) At a press conference held by the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization of the United Arab Republic on the 21st to support the Dominican Republic Week, it condemned U.S. imperialism’s armed intervention in the Dominican Republic and demanded the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops from the Dominican Republic.
    Secretary-General Sibai of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization of the United Arab Republic expressed full support for the heroic Dominican people's struggle for freedom and independence at the press conference. On April 24, more than 10,000 people in the Dominican capital marched in protest against American aggression. (Faxed photo by Xinhua News Agency)

### 亚非拉公众舆论声援多米尼加人民斗争  多米尼加人民定将打败美国侵略者

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非拉公众舆论声援多米尼加人民斗争
    多米尼加人民定将打败美国侵略者
    据新华社二十五日讯　加拉加斯消息：委内瑞拉首都加拉加斯青年和多米尼加侨民二十四日举行示威游行，纪念多米尼加人民抗美卫国武装斗争一周年。示威群众携带着写有反美口号和支持多米尼加人民斗争的标语牌穿过市中心大街，并高呼“美国佬从多米尼加滚出去”、“从拉丁美洲滚出去”等口号。
    据新华社圣约瑟二十二日电　哥斯达黎加公众近日来纷纷发表声明谴责美国侵略军继续占领多米尼加，积极声援多米尼加人民反美爱国的正义斗争。
    声援多米尼加全国委员会发表声明强烈谴责约翰逊政府，号召人民团体和进步组织以及所有爱国者反对美国对多米尼加共和国内政所进行的“可恨的”、“非法的”干涉，并且坚决支持多米尼加人民的英勇斗争。声明还要求哥斯达黎加政府撤回它派到多米尼加去充当美国侵略军帮凶的二十一名宪警。
    据新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方和拉丁美洲人民团结委员会最近就“声援多米尼加人民周”打电报给多米尼加人民说：越南南方人民和多米尼加人民定将打败两国人民的共同敌人美国侵略者。
    电报表示同情和完全支持同美帝国主义及其走狗进行英勇斗争的多米尼加人民，希望越南人民和多米尼加人民的战斗友谊进一步发展。
    据新华社平壤二十四日电　朝鲜《劳动新闻》二十四日就“声援多米尼加人民周”发表社论说，朝鲜人民积极支持多米尼加人民斗争。
    社论强调指出，多米尼加是多米尼加人民的，多米尼加问题必须由多米尼加人民自己解决，任何人都没有权利干涉多米尼加内政，美帝国主义和拉丁美洲的反革命干涉军队，必须立即停止对多米尼加的野蛮干涉。
    新华社开罗二十一日电　阿联亚非人民团结委员会二十一日为声援多米尼加周举行的记者招待会上谴责美帝国主义武装干涉多米尼加，并要求外国军队立即撤出多米尼加。
    阿联亚非人民团结委员会秘书长西巴伊在招待会上表示全力支持英雄的多米尼加人民争取自由和独立的斗争。四月二十四日，多米尼加首都一万多人游行示威反对美国侵略。　传真照片（新华社发）
