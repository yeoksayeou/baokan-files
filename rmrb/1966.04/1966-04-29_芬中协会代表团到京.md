Title: Finnish-Chinese Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, a three-member delegation from the Finnish-Chinese Association, led by the Chairman of the Finnish-Chinese Association, Toivo Tähkänen, arrived in Beijing by plane today.


**Named Entities**

中国    China
芬中协会  Finnish-Chinese Association
托依伏·台朗尼  Tuovo Tähkänen
中国人民对外文化友好协会 Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries
北京 Beijing
新华社  Xinhua News Agency

### 芬中协会代表团到京

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    芬中协会代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请来我国访问的芬中协会代表团一行三人，在芬中协会主席托依伏·台朗尼率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
