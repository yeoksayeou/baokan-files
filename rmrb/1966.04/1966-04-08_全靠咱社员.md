Title: All Depends on Our Commune Members
Author: Ding Dong
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    All Depends on Our Commune Members
    Nanzhang Village, Shulu 　Ding Dong
    One person and a pair of hands,
    Look at the sandy wasteland and worry.
    Ten thousand people and ten thousand pairs of hands,
    The sandy wasteland trembles when it sees them.
    One person and one shoulder,
    The spine is bent under the pressure.
    Ten thousand people and ten thousand shoulders,
    Can move Mount Tai.
    Seize a great harvest,
    All depends on the commune members.


**Named Entities**


束鹿   Shulu
南张村   Nanzhang Village
泰山   Mount Tai

### 全靠咱社员
丁东
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    全靠咱社员
    束鹿南张村　丁东
    一人一双手，
    看着沙荒干发愁。
    万人万双手，
    沙荒见了就发抖。
    一人一个肩，
    压的脊背弯。
    万人万个肩，
    能把泰山搬。
    夺取大丰收，
    全靠众社员。
