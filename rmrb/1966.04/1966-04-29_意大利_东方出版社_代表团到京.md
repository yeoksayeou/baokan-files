Title: Italian "Orient Publishing House" Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th—Invited by China International Bookstore to visit our country, the four-member delegation of the Italian "Orient Publishing House," led by the head of the delegation, Maria Regis, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Luo Jun, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Yan Baizhen, Deputy Director of the Foreign Languages ​​Publishing and Distribution Administration, and Cao Jianfei, Deputy Manager of China International Bookstore.

### 意大利“东方出版社”代表团到京

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    意大利“东方出版社”代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中国国际书店邀请，来我国进行访问的意大利“东方出版社”代表团一行四人，在团长玛丽亚·雷吉斯率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外友协副会长罗俊、外文出版发行事业局副局长阎百真、中国国际书店副经理曹健飞等。
