Title: Syrian Deputy Prime Minister Receives Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-10
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 7th. Syrian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Ibrahim Makhus received the Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation headed by Li Chang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and had a friendly conversation with them.
    During the meeting, Makhus expressed his gratitude for China's clear stance in supporting the struggle of the Palestinian people, and hoped to strengthen cultural exchanges between the two countries.
    On the 7th, Syrian Minister of Culture and National Guidance, Jamil Shaya, and Minister of Education, Mustafa Haddad, respectively received the delegation. In the evening, the Syrian Minister of Education hosted a banquet for the Chinese delegation.
    Li Huanong, Chargé d'affaires ad interim, and Yang Keyi, Cultural Counselor, of the Chinese Embassy in Syria also participated in the above-mentioned friendly activities.
    The delegation arrived here on the evening of the 6th at the invitation of the Syrian government.

### 叙利亚副总理接见我文化友好代表团

1966-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚副总理接见我文化友好代表团
    新华社大马士革七日电  叙利亚副总理兼外交部长易卜拉欣·马胡斯七日接见以中国对外文化联络委员会副主任李昌为首的中国政府文化友好代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    在接见时，马胡斯表示感谢中国支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争的明确立场，并且希望加强两国之间的文化交流。
    七日，叙利亚文化和国家指导部长加米勒·沙亚和教育部长穆斯塔法·哈达德分别接见了代表团。晚上，叙利亚教育部长设宴招待中国代表团。
    中国驻叙利亚大使馆临时代办李华农和文化参赞杨克毅也都参加了上述友好活动。
    代表团是应叙利亚政府的邀请，六日晚上到达这里的。
