Title: Northern Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four US Aircraft; People's Army Delegation Strongly Protests US Bombing of Wang Bi Power Plant and Incursion into Haiphong City
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th April – According to supplementary news released here at noon on the 20th, the military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down four more American aircraft on the 17th and 18th.
    On the 18th, the People's Army forces guarding Hon Go Island in the Vinh Linh area shot down two American aircraft, and the military and civilians in the northern region of Quang Binh province shot down one American aircraft.
    On the 17th, the military and civilians of Thai Binh province shot down one American aircraft.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th April – Colonel Ha Van Lau, head of the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army, sent an urgent telegram to the Chairman of the International Commission in Vietnam on the 19th, strongly protesting the bombing and strafing of the Wang Bi Power Plant in Quang Ninh Province and the incursion into Haiphong City by American bandit aircraft on the 19th.
    The telegram stated that multiple American aircraft bombed and strafed the Wang Bi Power Plant in Quang Ninh Province and some economic units and residential areas in the Cam Pha area on the 19th. The Wang Bi Power Plant alone has been bombed twice by American aircraft in two days. In addition, American aircraft also intruded into Haiphong City. The telegram pointed out that this proves that American imperialism is deliberately taking an extremely serious step in its destructive war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The telegram pointed out that these blatant acts of war expose the aggressive and bellicose nature of American imperialism. These bandit attacks by the United States infringe upon the sovereignty, territory, and security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and make the situation in Indochina and Southeast Asia even more dangerous.
    The telegram reiterated the warning to the US government that it must bear full responsibility for all consequences arising from its new war "escalation" actions.
    On the same day, in another telegram to the International Commission in Vietnam, the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army also protested the bombing and strafing of residential areas and economic facilities in the Vinh Linh area and the provinces of Ha Tinh, Nghe An, Lao Cai, and Quang Ninh by American aircraft on the 18th.

### 越南北方军民击落美机四架  人民军代表团强烈抗议美机轰炸汪秘发电厂和侵犯海防市

1966-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机四架
    人民军代表团强烈抗议美机轰炸汪秘发电厂和侵犯海防市
    新华社河内二十日电　据这里二十日中午公布的补充消息说，越南北方军民十七日和十八日又击落美国飞机四架。
    十八日，守卫永灵区昏果岛的人民军部队击落美国飞机两架，广平省北部地区的军民击落美国飞机一架。
    十七日，太平省军民击落美国飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长何文楼大校十九日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，强烈抗议美国强盗飞机十九日轰炸、扫射广宁省的汪秘发电厂和侵犯海防市。
    电报说，美国飞机多架十九日轰炸、扫射了广宁省的汪秘发电厂和锦普地区的一些经济单位和居民区。仅汪秘发电厂在两天内就遭到美国飞机两次轰炸。此外，美国飞机还侵犯了海防市。电报指出，这证明了美帝国主义蓄意在对越南民主共和国进行的破坏性战争中迈出极其严重的一步。
    电报指出，这些明目张胆的战争行动，暴露了美帝国主义的侵略和好战本质。美国的这些强盗进攻，侵犯了越南民主共和国的主权、领土和安全，使印度支那和东南亚的局势变得更加危险。
    电报再次警告美国政府，它必须对由它的新的战争“升级”行动所引起的一切后果承担全部责任。
    同一天，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团在打给越南国际委员会的另一份电报中，还抗议了美国飞机十八日轰炸、扫射永灵地区和河静、义安、老街、广宁等省的居民区和经济设施。
