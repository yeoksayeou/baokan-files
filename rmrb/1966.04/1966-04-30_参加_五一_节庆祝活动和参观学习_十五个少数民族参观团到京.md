Title: Fifteen Minority Nationality Visiting Groups Arrive in Beijing to Participate in "May Day" Celebrations and Study
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. On the eve of the "May Day" International Labor Day, fifteen minority nationality visiting groups from all over the country have come to Beijing to participate in celebration activities and study.
    These visiting groups come from provinces and autonomous regions including Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang, Tibet, Guangxi, Ningxia, Yunnan, Guizhou, Sichuan, Qinghai, Gansu, Guangdong, Hunan, Liaoning, Jilin, and Heilongjiang. There are more than 200 people in total, including more than 30 nationalities. The vast majority of the visiting group members are from communes and production teams in mountainous, pastoral, and border areas of various minority nationality regions. They are mostly advanced figures among grassroots cadres, militia, and laboring masses of minority nationalities who have made achievements in studying Chairman Mao's works and learning the revolutionary spirit of Dazhai. During the visit, they will exchange experiences in creatively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works and learn from the advanced deeds and experiences of people across the country in transforming nature and carrying out construction.

### 参加“五一”节庆祝活动和参观学习  十五个少数民族参观团到京

1966-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    参加“五一”节庆祝活动和参观学习
    十五个少数民族参观团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　“五一”国际劳动节前夕，全国各地有十五个少数民族参观团来北京参加庆祝活动和参观学习。
    这些参观团来自内蒙古、新疆、西藏、广西、宁夏、云南、贵州、四川、青海、甘肃、广东、湖南、辽宁、吉林、黑龙江等省和自治区，共二百多人，包括三十多个民族。参观团成员中绝大多数来自各少数民族地区的山区、牧区和边疆地区的公社和生产队，他们大都是在学习毛主席著作和学习大寨革命精神方面作出成绩的少数民族基层干部、民兵和劳动群众中的先进人物。在参观中，他们将要交流活学活用毛主席著作的经验，学习各地人民改造大自然进行建设的先进事迹和经验。
