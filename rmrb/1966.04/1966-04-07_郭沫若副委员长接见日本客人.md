Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 5

    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Receives Japanese Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, April 6th. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, received the entire delegation of the Japanese Socialist Youth League, led by Hajime Fukada, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Qian David and Wen Chi.
    Japanese peace figure in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 6th. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, received Masatsugu Wada, head of the Japanese Traditional Chinese Medicine Delegation, and delegation member Ryu Saikawa, this morning, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association, and Lu Zhijun, President of the Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社六日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中日友好协会名誉会长郭沫若今天下午接见了由深田肇率领的日本社会主义青年同盟代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有钱大卫、文迟等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
    新华社六日讯　郭沫若副委员长今天上午接见了日本中医代表团团长和田正系，代表团团员犀川龙，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华医学会会长傅连暲，中医研究院院长鲁之俊等。
