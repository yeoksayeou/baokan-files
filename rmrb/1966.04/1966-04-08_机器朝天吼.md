Title: The Machine Roars to the Sky
Author: Chen Yonghui
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    The crescent moon hangs on the willow tip,
    The machine roars to the sky!
    Green shoots dance with water droplets,
    A silver river flows through the fields.
    The moon smiles, curving its eyebrows,
    The willow tip smiles, lowering its head.
    Together we congratulate the land re-shapers,
    Daring to fight with the heavens!
    The flying spades are like dragons,
    Leading the silver river along.
    The land re-shapers,
    Sing out:
    "Socialism is good!"
    The loud song swims in the water.

**Named Entities**

### 机器朝天吼
陈永会
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    机器朝天吼
    束鹿杜合庄　陈永会
    弯月挂上柳梢头，
    机器朝天吼！
    青苗伴着水花舞，
    一条银河田里流。
    月亮笑弯眉，
    柳梢笑低头。
    同贺改畦手，
    敢与天公斗！
    飞锨象条龙，
    牵着银河走。
    改畦手，
    歌出口：
    “社会主义好！”
    响亮的歌声水里游。
