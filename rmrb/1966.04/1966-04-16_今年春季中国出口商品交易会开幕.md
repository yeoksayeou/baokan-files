Title: This Spring's China Export Commodities Fair Opens

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 15th: The 1966 Spring China Export Commodities Fair opened today in Guangzhou.
    Today, the hosts of the fair warmly welcomed the first group of guests, including merchants from distant regions such as Africa, Northern Europe, Western Europe, Latin America, Australia, and the Middle East, as well as merchants from our friendly neighboring countries such as Cambodia, Pakistan, and Ceylon. Also present were the Burmese trade delegation and the trade representatives from Congo (Brazzaville), who were invited to participate in the fair and were among the first to arrive in Guangzhou. Friendly trading companies from various parts of Japan also sent representatives. As of today, more than 2,200 merchants from various countries, overseas Chinese, and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau have arrived in Guangzhou.
    Among the nearly 30,000 commodities exhibited at this fair, there are more than 4,000 new varieties. The new varieties and designs of light industrial products have increased several times compared to the autumn fair last year.
    Rural people's communes are vigorously developing agriculture and diversified operations, providing the fair with a wider variety of agricultural, forestry, animal husbandry, sideline, and fishery products, as well as handicrafts and local specialties. New varieties of machinery, chemicals, hardware, minerals, and instruments are also on display.
    Tonight, the fair held a grand opening reception. Zeng Sheng, acting chairman of the China Export Commodities Fair and mayor of Guangzhou, spoke at the reception. Yang Haolu, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, attended tonight's reception and proposed a toast to the guests attending the fair.
    After the reception, the fair entertained the guests with a performance of the opera "Ayiguli" by the Central Opera and Dance Theater.

### 今年春季中国出口商品交易会开幕

1966-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

    今年春季中国出口商品交易会开幕
    据新华社广州十五日电  一九六六年春季中国出口商品交易会今天在广州开幕。
    今天，交易会的主人盛情地接待了第一批客人，他们有来自远道的非洲、北欧、西欧、拉丁美洲、澳洲、中东等地区的商人，也有我国友好邻邦的柬埔寨、巴基斯坦以及锡兰等国家的客商，还有应邀参加交易会首批到达广州的缅甸贸易代表团和刚果（布）的贸易代表。日本各地的友好商社也派来了代表。到今天为止，到达广州的各国商人和海外华侨、港澳同胞达二千二百多人。
    这次交易会展出的近三万种商品中，新品种有四千多种，其中轻工业品的新品种新花色比去年秋季交易会有了成倍的增长。
    农村人民公社大力发展农业和开展多种经营为交易会提供出口的农、林、牧、副、渔各业产品以及手工业产品、土特产，比以前显得更为丰富多彩。机械、化工、五金矿产、仪器也有新的品种展出。
    今天晚上，交易会举行盛大的开幕酒会。中国出口商品交易会代主任委员、广州市市长曾生在酒会上讲话。对外贸易部副部长杨浩庐出席了今晚的酒会，向参加交易会的来宾祝酒。
    酒会结束后，交易会招待来宾观看了中央歌剧舞剧院歌剧团演出的歌剧《阿依古丽》。
