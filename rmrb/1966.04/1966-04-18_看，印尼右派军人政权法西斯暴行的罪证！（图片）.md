Title: Look, Evidence of the Fascist Atrocities of the Indonesian Right-Wing Military Regime!
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 4

    Look, Evidence of the Fascist Atrocities of the Indonesian Right-Wing Military Regime!
    On April 15, the Indonesian government dispatched armed forces and thugs to beat up Chinese diplomats, and shot and seriously wounded Zhao Xiaoshou, a staff member of the embassy. Yao Dengshan (second from left), Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, and other diplomatic officials presented Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou's blood-stained clothes on the spot to express their strongest protest.
	传真照片（新华社发）[Facsimile photo (Xinhua News Agency)]
    The Indonesian government dispatched armed forces and thugs, broke into our embassy in Jakarta, frantically carried out sabotage, and set fire to the embassy's documents, furniture, and vehicles.
                                传真照片（新华社发）[Facsimile photo (Xinhua News Agency)]

### 看，印尼右派军人政权法西斯暴行的罪证！（图片）

1966-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    看，印尼右派军人政权法西斯暴行的罪证！
    四月十五日，印度尼西亚政府出动武装部队和暴徒，打伤中国外交官，开枪重伤大使馆工作人员赵小寿。中国大使馆临时代办姚登山（左二）和其他外交官员当场出示赵小寿同志的血衣，表示最强烈的抗议。　                传真照片（新华社发）
    印度尼西亚政府出动武装部队和暴徒，闯入我驻雅加达大使馆，疯狂地进行破坏，并且纵火烧毁大使馆的文件、家具和车辆。
                                传真照片（新华社发）
