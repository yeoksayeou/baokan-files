Title: Chairman Liu Holds Talks with President Ayub; Chairman Liu Dines with President Ayub
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Dacca, 16th - Chairman Liu Shaoqi and President Ayub Khan held talks at the Presidential Palace here this afternoon.
    Present at the talks on the Chinese side were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu; present on the Pakistani side were Foreign Minister Bhutto and President's Principal Secretary Yusuf.
    The talks proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, Dacca, 15th - Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife dined with Pakistani President Ayub Khan at the Presidential Palace here on the 15th.
    Also dining were Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife.
    Accompanying them were East Pakistan Governor Abdul Monem Khan, Foreign Minister Bhutto and his wife, and East Pakistan Finance Minister Huda.
    They engaged in friendly conversations.

### 刘主席同阿尤布总统举行会谈  刘主席同阿尤布总统共进晚餐

1966-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席同阿尤布总统举行会谈
    刘主席同阿尤布总统共进晚餐
    新华社达卡十六日电　刘少奇主席和阿尤布·汗总统十六日下午在这里的总统府举行了会谈。
    会谈时，中国方面在座的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅和外交部副部长章汉夫；巴基斯坦方面在座的有外交部长布托和总统首席秘书优素福。
    会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社达卡十五日电　刘少奇主席和夫人十五日在这里的总统府和巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗共进晚餐。
    一同进餐的有陈毅副总理和夫人。
    陪同他们的还有东巴基斯坦省督阿卜杜勒·穆奈姆·汗、外交部长布托和夫人、东巴基斯坦省财政部长胡达。
    他们进行了友好的谈话。
