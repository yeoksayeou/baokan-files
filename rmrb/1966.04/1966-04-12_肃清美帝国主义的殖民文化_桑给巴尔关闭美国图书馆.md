Title: Eradicating the Colonial Culture of American Imperialism: Zanzibar Closes American Library
Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 4

    Eradicating the Colonial Culture of American Imperialism
    Zanzibar Closes American Library
    Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, 7th—According to a report in the Tanganyika African National Union's newspaper *The Nationalist* on the 7th, the American Library in Zanzibar has been closed since April 5th. The Zanzibar Ministry of Foreign Affairs recently issued a notice to the consulates of various countries in Zanzibar, requiring them to cease operating public libraries and stop publicly screening films that have not been inspected and approved by the Zanzibar Ministry of Education and Culture. The American Library, operated by the American Consulate here, is the only public library affected by this notice.
    The people of Zanzibar have long opposed American cultural infiltration. The resolution passed at the Second Youth Lecture held in Zanzibar in December 1964 called on all citizens, especially young people, to develop national culture and demanded that American films and all other capitalist and colonialist films, books, and newspapers be banned in Zanzibar.

### 肃清美帝国主义的殖民文化  桑给巴尔关闭美国图书馆

1966-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    肃清美帝国主义的殖民文化
    桑给巴尔关闭美国图书馆
    新华社桑给巴尔七日电　据坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟机关报《民族主义者报》七日报道，在桑给巴尔的美国图书馆，从四月五日起关闭。桑给巴尔外交部最近向各国驻桑给巴尔的领事馆发出一项通知，要它们停办公共图书馆和停止公开放映未经桑给巴尔教育和文化部检查批准的电影。由这里的美国领事馆经办的美国图书馆是受到这项通知的影响的唯一的公共图书馆。
    桑给巴尔人民早就在反对美国的文化渗入。一九六四年十二月在桑给巴尔举行的第二次青年讲座通过的决议呼吁全体公民，特别是青年，发展民族文化，并要求在桑给巴尔禁止美国电影和其他一切资本主义和殖民主义的电影、书籍和报纸。
