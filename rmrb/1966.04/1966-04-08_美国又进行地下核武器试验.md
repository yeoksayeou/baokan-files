Title: US Conducts Another Underground Nuclear Weapon Test
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    The United States Conducts Another Underground Nuclear Weapon Test
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 7th—According to an announcement by the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission, the United States conducted another underground nuclear weapon test at the Nevada Test Site on the 6th.
    This is the eleventh nuclear weapon test officially announced by the United States this year, and the sixty-eighth nuclear weapon test conducted by the United States since the signing of the Moscow Three-Party Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty in August 1963.

### 美国又进行地下核武器试验

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美国又进行地下核武器试验
    新华社七日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国六日又在内华达试验场进行了一次地下核武器试验。
    这是今年美国官方宣布的第十一次核武器试验，也是一九六三年八月莫斯科三国部分禁止核试验条约签订以来美国的第六十八次核武器试验。
