Title: Winners of Five Individual Events

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th – The winning rankings of the five individual events in the 1966 National Table Tennis Championships:
    Men's Singles
    First Place: Zhuang Zedong (Beijing)
    Second Place: Li Furong (Shanghai)
    Third Place: Zhang Xielin (Shanghai), Yao Zhenxu (Shanghai) (Tie, no fourth place)
    Fifth Place: Yu Yize (Shanghai), Wang Jiasheng (Guizhou), Zhou Lansun (Shanxi), Lu Jufang (Guangdong) (Tie)
    Women's Singles
    First Place: Qiu Baoqin (Shanxi)
    Second Place: Liang Lizhen (Guangdong)
    Third Place: Li Henan (Shanghai), Zheng Minzhi (Shanghai)
    Fifth Place: Lin Huiqing (Shanghai), Li Li (Beijing), Shi Fengling (Beijing), Di Qianghua (Jiangsu)
    Men's Doubles
    First Place: Zhuang Zedong, Li Jingguang (Hebei)
    Second Place: Li Furong, Wang Jiasheng
    Third Place: Lu Jufang, Xu Shaofa (Jilin), Zhou Lansun, Yu Changchun (Shanghai)
    Fifth Place: Zhou Qian (Jiangsu), Gong Baohua (Jiangsu), Yu Yize, Wang Fucheng (Jiangsu), Li Deyang (Liaoning), Li Lianyi (Guangdong), Diao Wenyuan (Anhui), He Zubin (Hubei)
    Women's Doubles
    First Place: Li Henan, Li Li
    Second Place: Di Qianghua, Liu Yaqin (Beijing)
    Third Place: Liang Lizhen, Lin Xiuying (Shanghai), Zhou Yiling (Shanghai), Hu Xincheng (Shanghai)
    Fifth Place: Qiu Baoqin, Lin Ximeng (Fujian), Shi Fengling, Wang Biling (Beijing), Hu Yulan (Liaoning), Lin Meiqun (Guangdong), Yan Guifen (Sichuan), Li Yucheng (Sichuan)
    Mixed Doubles
    First Place: Wang Jiasheng, Li Henan
    Second Place: Zhuang Zedong, Liang Lizhen
    Third Place: Li Furong, Li Li, Li Deyang, Qiu Baoqin
    Fifth Place: Yao Zhenxu, Zheng Minzhi, Li Lianyi, Zheng Yushan (Guangdong), Su Guoxi (Guangdong), Lin Meiqun, Yu Yize, Di Qianghua.
