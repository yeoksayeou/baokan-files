Title: Celebrating the Successful Conclusion of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association Secretariat Meeting (Enlarged): All-China Journalists Association Holds Reception
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. Mei Yi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, held a reception tonight to celebrate "Afro-Asian Journalists Day" and warmly congratulate the successful conclusion of the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association.
    Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the reception.
    Djawarh, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, and representatives from various countries attending the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, were invited to attend the reception.
    The reception was filled with an atmosphere of militant solidarity and friendship. Mei Yi spoke at the reception. He said that the enlarged meeting of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association had achieved great success. The meeting was full of fighting spirit and solidarity. He emphasized that the success of the meeting powerfully struck at the conspiracies of imperialism, reactionaries in various countries, and modern revisionism attempting to undermine the anti-imperialist solidarity of Afro-Asian journalists.
    Mei Yi said that we sincerely welcome the decision of the Enlarged Secretariat Meeting to establish the Secretariat's working location in Beijing before the Second Afro-Asian Journalists Conference is held. We believe that this is a great trust in Chinese journalists. I would like to once again express my gratitude on behalf of the All-China Journalists Association and solemnly state that we will do our utmost to assist and support the work of the Secretariat.
    He said that since the establishment of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, our cause of anti-imperialist solidarity among Afro-Asian journalists has greatly developed. We firmly believe that in the future our cause will become even more prosperous and make greater contributions to the anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggles of the Afro-Asian people, especially against U.S. imperialism and its lackeys.
    Djawarh, speaking at the reception, expressed his gratitude to the All-China Journalists Association for its efforts in convening the enlarged meeting. He wished Afro-Asian journalists and Afro-Asian people even greater success in the anti-imperialist struggle.
    Le Tung Son, member of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation to China, Pha Rong Chulanon, Permanent Overseas Representative of the Patriotic Front of Thailand, Sharma, head of the Delegation of the Malayan National Liberation League to China, as well as press delegations from Asian and African countries currently visiting China, resident journalists from Asian and African countries in Beijing, and Asian and African experts working in Beijing news organizations, were also invited to attend the reception.
    Also present at the reception were Chu Tunan, Wu Defeng, Mao Yisheng, Yang Shuo, Cao Mengjun, Wang Zhijie, Fu Dexi, Zhang Mingqing, and responsible persons from the Chinese Journalists Association and the capital's press circles.

### 祝贺亚非记协书记处会议（扩大）胜利闭幕  我全国新闻工作者协会举行招待会

1966-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺亚非记协书记处会议（扩大）胜利闭幕
    我全国新闻工作者协会举行招待会
    新华社二十四日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益今晚举行招待会，庆祝“亚非新闻工作者日”，并热烈祝贺亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）胜利闭幕。
    人大常务委员会副委员长刘宁一出席了招待会。
    亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多和出席亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）的各国代表，应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上，充满了战斗团结的友谊气氛。梅益在招待会上讲话。他说，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处的这次扩大会议，取得了巨大的成就。会议充满了战斗的精神和团结的精神。他强调说，会议的成功，有力地打击了帝国主义、各国反动派和现代修正主义企图破坏亚非新闻工作者反帝团结的阴谋。
    梅益说，我们衷心欢迎书记处扩大会议决定在第二次亚非新闻工作者会议召开以前，把书记处的工作地点设在北京。我们认为这是对中国新闻工作者的极大信任。我愿再一次代表中华全国新闻工作者协会对此表示感谢，并且郑重表示，我们决将竭尽最大努力来协助和支持书记处的工作。
    他说，自从亚非新闻工作者协会成立以来，我们亚非新闻工作者团结反帝的事业有了很大的发展。我们深信，今后我们的事业必将更加蒸蒸日上，对亚非人民反帝反殖，特别是反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争，作出更大的贡献。
    查禾多在招待会上讲话时表示感谢中国记协为这次扩大会议的召开所作的努力。他祝愿亚非新闻工作者和亚非人民在反帝斗争中，进一步取得更大的成果。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团员黎松山，泰国爱国阵线常驻国外代表帕荣·朱拉暖，马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团团长沙尔玛，以及正在我国访问的亚非各国新闻工作者代表团，常驻北京的亚非国家的记者和在北京新闻单位工作的亚非国家专家，也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有楚图南、吴德峰、茅以升、杨朔、曹孟君、王志杰、傅德喜、张明卿以及中国新闻工作者协会和首都新闻界的负责人。
