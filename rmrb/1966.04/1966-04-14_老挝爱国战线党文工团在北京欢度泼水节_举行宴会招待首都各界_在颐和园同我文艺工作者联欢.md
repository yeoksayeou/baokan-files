Title: Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe Celebrates Water Splashing Festival in Beijing; Holds Banquet to Entertain People from All Walks of Life in the Capital; Joins Our Literary and Art Workers in a Get-Together at the Summer Palace
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, April 13th) The Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe held a banquet in Beijing tonight to entertain people from all walks of life in China and celebrate the Lao New Year - the "Water Splashing Festival."
    Lian Guan, President of the Sino-Lao Friendship Association, Ren Yinglun, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and responsible persons from relevant departments including Ding Bo, Guan Hetong, and Sheng Jie, were invited to attend the banquet. As they entered the banquet hall, Lao artists sprinkled water on the guests and presented them with flowers to express their welcome. The banquet hall was brightly decorated and filled with a joyful atmosphere.
    Kanma Phoukhoung, head of the Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe, spoke first at the banquet. He said that in the new year, the Lao people will further heighten their vigilance, smash the conspiracies of U.S. imperialism, and strive for new victories.
    Head Kanma Phoukhoung also warmly praised the fraternal friendship between the people and literary and art workers of Laos and China.
    Lian Guan extended warm festival greetings to the Lao friends in his speech. He emphasized that the Chinese people, the Lao people, and the people of the countries of Indochina support each other in the common struggle against U.S. imperialism and are close comrades-in-arms. If we unite and fight, we will surely achieve greater victories.
    Tran Van Khang, Cultural Attache of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Le Tung Son, representative of the Permanent Mission of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in China, also attended the banquet.
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 13th) Today is the Lao New Year - the "Water Splashing Festival." The entire group of artists from the Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe, currently visiting Beijing, toured the Summer Palace today to celebrate the festival.
    When the head of the art troupe, Kanma Phoukhoung, and the troupe members arrived at the Summer Palace, where magnolias were in full bloom, literary and art workers from the capital, who were waiting at the park entrance, sprinkled clear water on the guests according to Lao customs to wish them a happy festival. On Longevity Hill, Chinese and Lao friends held a get-together, dancing the "Nam Vong Dance" loved by the Lao people and the Chinese "Yangge Dance" together, and singing "Unity is Strength" and the Lao revolutionary song "Langda."
    The Lao artists said with deep feeling that they felt very warm among their Chinese friends, just like being at home. Ding Bo, Acting Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and Madame Quinim Pholsena, a Lao friend in Beijing, participated in today's garden party.

### 老挝爱国战线党文工团在北京欢度泼水节  举行宴会招待首都各界　在颐和园同我文艺工作者联欢

1966-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党文工团在北京欢度泼水节
    举行宴会招待首都各界　在颐和园同我文艺工作者联欢
    据新华社十三日讯　老挝爱国战线党文工团今晚在北京举行宴会，招待中国各界人士，欢庆老挝新年——“泼水节”。
    中老友协会长连贯，对外文协副会长任映仑，有关方面负责人丁波、关鹤童、盛婕等，应邀出席宴会。他们走进宴会厅的时候，老挝艺术家向客人们洒水、赠花，表示欢迎。宴会厅里悬灯结彩，喜气洋洋。
    老挝爱国战线党文工团团长坎马·富贡在宴会上首先讲话。他说，老挝人民在新的一年里，将更加提高警惕，粉碎美帝国主义的阴谋活动，争取新的胜利。
    坎马·富贡团长还热情赞扬了老中两国人民和两国文艺工作者的兄弟友谊。
    连贯在讲话中向老挝朋友致热烈的节日祝贺。他强调说，中国人民、老挝人民和印度支那各国人民，在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中互相支持，是亲密的战友。我们团结战斗，一定能够取得更大的胜利。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆文化专员陈文献，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黎松山，也出席了宴会。
    据新华社十三日讯　今天是老挝的新年——“泼水节”。正在北京访问的老挝爱国战线党文工团全体艺术家，今天畅游颐和园，欢度节日。
    当文工团团长坎马·富贡和团员们，乘车来到玉兰花盛开的颐和园的时候，迎候在公园门口的首都文艺工作者，按照老挝人民的风俗，把清水洒在客人们的身上，祝贺他们节日快乐。在万寿山上下，中老两国朋友还举行联欢，一起跳了老挝人民喜爱的“南旺舞”和中国的“秧歌舞”，齐唱《团结就是力量》和老挝革命歌曲《浪达》。
    老挝艺术家满怀深情地说，他们在中国朋友中间感到十分温暖，就象在自己家里一样。中国演出经理公司代经理丁波，在北京的老挝朋友贵宁·奔舍那夫人，参加了今天的游园联欢。
