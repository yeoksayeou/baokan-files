Title: Chairman Liao Chengzhi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 5

The China-Japan Friendship Association Chairman Liao Chengzhi held a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the delegation of the Japan Council against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs led by Yamaguchi Keiichi, the delegation of the Japan Socialist Youth League led by Fukaoka Haji, the delegation of the Japan Fisheries Association led by Ide Masataka, and the delegation of the Japan Traditional Medicine Association led by Wada Masayuki.

The hosts and guests toasted each other at the banquet. They raised their glasses to the friendship between the peoples of China and Japan.

Attending the banquet were relevant responsible persons. Before the banquet, Liao Chengzhi met with the members of the four delegations and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 廖承志会长宴请日本客人

1966-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长宴请日本客人
    新华社六日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以山口圭一为首的日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会友好代表团、以深田肇为首的日本社会主义青年同盟代表团、以井出正孝为首的日本水产代表团和以和田正系为首的日本中医代表团。宾主在宴会上先后祝酒。他们为中日两国人民的友谊干杯。
    出席宴会的有有关方面负责人。宴会前，廖承志接见了四个代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
