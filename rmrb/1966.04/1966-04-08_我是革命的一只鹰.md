Title: I am an Eagle of the Revolution
Author: Sun Shuliang
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    I am an Eagle of the Revolution
    Public Security Soldier Sun Shuliang
    The great wind cheers,
    The trees applaud,
    The telephone poles sway in the air.
    Telephone soldier,
    Ambitious and strong,
    Chairman Mao's teachings are remembered in my heart.
    Rubbing my hands,
    Tightening my belt,
    I quickly climb to the top of the pole.
    In an instant,
    Above the clouds,
    Every silver line is connected.
    Revolutionary heroism boils in my heart,
    A thousand catties of burden on my shoulders;
    The telephone soldier's mind,
    Is wider than the blue sky and wider than the ocean.
    Beloved motherland,
    I am an eagle of the revolution,
    Mao Zedong Thought has given me a pair of hard wings.
    I dare to fly through thousands of difficulties and dangers,
    I dare to brave mountains of knives and seas of fire.
    I'll set up dense lines,
    I will reach the hundred-foot pole,
    All just for:
    To let the fighting news of victory,
    Spread throughout Asia, Africa, and Latin America,
    Fly across the four oceans.



**Named Entities**

毛主席  Mao Zhuxi
毛泽东思想  Mao Zedong Thought
亚非拉  Asia, Africa, and Latin America

### 我是革命的一只鹰
孙书亮
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我是革命的一只鹰
    公安战士　孙书亮
    大风欢呼，
    树鼓掌，
    电线杆子在空中晃。
    电话兵，
    志气壮，
    毛主席教导记心上。
    搓搓手，
    紧紧腰，
    噌噌爬到杆顶上。
    霎时间，
    云头上，
    条条银线全系上。
    革命豪情心上沸，
    千斤重担肩上扛；
    电话兵的胸怀呀，
    比蓝天宽阔比海洋宽广。
    可爱的祖国啊，
    我是革命的一只鹰，
    毛主席思想给了我一双硬翅膀。
    千难万险我敢飞，
    刀山火海我敢闯。
    密密线条我来架，
    百尺竿头我来上，
    都只为：
    让那战斗的捷报，
    传遍亚非拉，
    飞遍四大洋。
