Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 399th Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-01
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st -- On March 30th, from 21:37 to 23:39, a U.S. warship invaded our territorial waters in the southeast area of Nanri Island, Fujian Province. On March 31st, from 02:17 to 10:01 and from 13:44 to 17:30, a U.S. warship twice invaded our territorial waters in the northeast of Pingtan Island and the southeast of Nanri Island, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 11:06 to 11:22, a U.S. military aircraft invaded the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 399th serious warning.

### 美国军舰飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第三百九十九次严重警告

1966-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    美国军舰飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第三百九十九次严重警告
    新华社三十一日讯　三月三十日二十一时三十七分至二十三时三十九分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省南日岛东南地区领海。三月三十一日二时十七分至十时零一分和十三时四十四分至十七时三十分，美国军舰一艘，两次侵入我福建省平潭岛东北、南日岛东南地区领海。同日十一时零六分至十一时二十二分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛和东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第三百九十九次严重警告。
