Title: Afghan Newspapers Publish China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué, Praising Sino-Afghan Friendship
Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 3

    Afghan Newspapers Publish China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué
    Praising Sino-Afghan Friendship

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, April 10th – Kabul newspapers on the 9th and 10th published the full text of the China-Afghanistan Joint Communiqué on Chairman Liu Shaoqi's state visit to Afghanistan.

    *Anis* newspaper published the communiqué with the subtitle: "The two countries declare their support for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Ten Principles of the Bandung Conference, and support the struggle of the peoples of all countries for freedom and the right to self-determination." *Islah* newspaper's subtitle was: "Friendly talks," "Peaceful coexistence," and "National independence and the eradication of colonialism."

    *Hiwad* newspaper published an editorial on the 9th, saying: "The communiqué reiterates the foundation of friendship between Afghanistan and China. It highly evaluates the Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression signed between the two countries." The editorial stated that the communiqué reiterated that Afghan-Chinese friendship is based on mutual respect, equality, and non-interference in internal affairs.

    The editorial stated that the two countries expressed their support for the struggles of Asian and African countries still under colonial enslavement.

### 阿富汗报纸刊登中阿联合公报  盛赞中阿两国友谊

1966-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

    阿富汗报纸刊登中阿联合公报
    盛赞中阿两国友谊
    新华社喀布尔十日电　喀布尔各报九日和十日全文刊载了中国和阿富汗就刘少奇主席在阿富汗进行国事访问的联合公报。
    《友谊报》刊载这个公报的副标题是：“两国申明支持和平共处五项原则和万隆会议十项原则，支持各国人民的自由和争取自决权利的斗争”。《革新报》的副标题是：“友好的会谈”，“和平共处”和“民族独立和肃清殖民主义”。
    《祖国报》九日发表社论说：“公报重申了阿富汗和中国之间的友谊的基础。它对两国签订的友好和互不侵犯条约作了高度的评价。”社论说，公报重申了阿富汗—中国的友谊是以互相尊重、平等相待、互不干涉内政为基础的。
    社论说，两国表示支持仍然处于殖民主义奴役下的亚非国家人民的斗争。
