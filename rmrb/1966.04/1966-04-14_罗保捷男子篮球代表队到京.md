Title: Romanian, Bulgarian, and Czechoslovakian Men's Basketball Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: The Romanian Bucharest "Dinamo" Sports Club men's basketball team, led by Major General Nicolae Chiriaque, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The Bulgarian "Spartak" men's basketball team, led by Colonel Mircho Ranghelov, and the Czechoslovakian "Red Star" men's basketball team, led by Major František Vostrý, also arrived in Beijing on the same plane.
    They were invited to participate in the 7th Men's Basketball Competition of Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations.
    The guests were welcomed at the airport by Ma Xingwu, Director of the Organizing Committee of the 7th Men's Basketball Competition of Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations and Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Public Security Forces, and Mu Zuoyun, Deputy Director of the Ball Games Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.
    Also present at the airport to welcome them were Petrescu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Colonel Solescu, Military Attaché; Major General Zaimov, Military Attaché of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, Counselor Dimitrov; Colonel Purgel, Military Attaché of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Juda, Cultural Attaché.

### 罗保捷男子篮球代表队到京

1966-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    罗保捷男子篮球代表队到京
    新华社十三日讯　罗马尼亚布加勒斯特“狄纳莫”体育俱乐部男子篮球代表队，由尼古拉·基里亚克少将率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。由米尔乔·朗格洛夫上校率领的保加利亚“斯巴达克”男子篮球代表队和由弗朗齐歇克·沃斯特里少校率领的捷克斯洛伐克“红星”男子篮球代表队也同机到达北京。
    他们是应邀参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛的。
    客人们在机场上受到了社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会组织委员会主任、中国人民公安部队政治部副主任马星五，国家体委球类司副司长牟作云等的欢迎。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办贝特勒斯库、武官索列斯库上校，保加利亚驻华大使馆武官扎伊莫夫少将、参赞迪米特洛夫，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆武官蒲尔格尔上校、文化专员朱达等也到机场迎接。
