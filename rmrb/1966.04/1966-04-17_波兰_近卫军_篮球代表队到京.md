Title: Polish "Guard" Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th April: The Polish "Guard" basketball team, a group of sixteen led by Colonel Czesław Woszczyński, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    The guest team is invited to participate in the Seventh Men's Basketball Competition of Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations held in Beijing.

### 波兰“近卫军”篮球代表队到京

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰“近卫军”篮球代表队到京
    新华社十六日讯　波兰“近卫军”篮球代表队一行十六人，在领队切斯瓦夫·伍什钦斯基上校的率领下今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    客队是应邀参加在北京举行的社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛的。
