Title: Guinean National Djoliba Dance Troupe's First Performance in Guangzhou; Dance Troupe's Performance of Folk-Themed Songs and Dances Warmly Welcomed; Vice Premier Tao Zhu Receives Dance Troupe Leaders and Principal Members
Author:
Date: 1966-04-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 4th. The Guinean National Djoliba Dance Troupe gave its first performance in China here tonight. The artists' folk-themed songs and dances, rich in the flavor of life, were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Tao Zhu and his wife Zeng Zhi, Acting Governor of Guangdong Province Lin Liming and his wife Yu Zhen, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou Zeng Sheng and his wife Ruan Qunying, watched the performance of the Guinean artists.
    The artists from the friendly West African country of Guinea sang the Chinese song "The East is Red" in Chinese tonight, as well as the performance song "Fighting Africa Praises Chairman Mao and His Great Cause," which they specially arranged for the Chinese audience after arriving in China. Their wonderful performances were repeated once again at the enthusiastic request of the audience.
    Tonight's performance began with the chorus "Djoliba." Singer Kouyate Sory Kandia deeply attracted the audience with his soaring and bright voice. Among the other five song and dance programs performed by the artists was the dance "Mother," which reflects the experience of a mother of a heroically sacrificed soldier in the struggle of the Guinean people against colonialism and in defense of their motherland. The artists' enthusiastic and unrestrained performances, accompanied by the characteristic African dunun drum sounds, often aroused warm applause from the audience.
    Before the performance, an opening ceremony was held. Zeng Sheng spoke at the opening ceremony, expressing the warmest welcome to the Guinean artists on behalf of the people of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City. He said that although China and Guinea are separated by thousands of miles, the people of our two countries have always sympathized with and encouraged each other in the struggle against imperialism and for national independence. He wished the Guinean National Djoliba Dance Troupe the greatest success in its performances in our country.
    Sissoko Amadou, Director-General of the dance troupe, said in his speech that he was honored and pleased to thank His Excellency Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, for the invitation extended to the dance troupe during his unforgettable visit to Guinea in 1965. He expressed his joy at the friendly relations between China and Guinea in culture, economy, and politics, and wished that this friendly relationship would grow day by day.
    Zheng Zhifu, representative of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Ding Bo, acting manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and local literary and art figures also attended the opening ceremony and watched the performance.
    Kouyate Bala, attaché of the Embassy of the Republic of Guinea in China, also attended the opening ceremony and watched the performance.
    After the performance, Vice Premier Tao Zhu and his wife and others went on stage, warmly shook hands with the actors, and congratulated them on their successful performance.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 4th. Vice Premier of the State Council Tao Zhu and his wife Zeng Zhi, met tonight with Sissoko Amadou, Director-General of the Guinean National Djoliba Dance Troupe, Deputy Director-General Kouyate Sory Kandia and his wife, as well as the principal members of the dance troupe. Vice Premier Tao Zhu had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Accompanying the meeting were Lin Liming, Acting Governor of Guangdong Province, and his wife Yu Zhen, Zeng Sheng, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou, and his wife Ruan Qunying, Zheng Zhifu, representative of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Ding Bo, acting manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and others.
    Kouyate Bala, attaché of the Guinean Embassy in China, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou. Zeng Sheng, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou, and his wife held a banquet on the evening of March 31st to warmly welcome the Guinean National Djoliba Dance Troupe, led by Director-General Sissoko Amadou and Deputy Director-General Kouyate Sory Kandia.
    When Zeng Sheng and Sissoko Amadou spoke at the banquet, they both pointed out that the common struggle against imperialism and colonialism has closely linked the people of China and Guinea, and the traditional friendship between the people of China and Guinea is profound. Zeng Sheng also expressed the warmest welcome to the Guinean artists who brought the friendship of the Guinean people, and wished them success in their visit and performances in China.
    The Guinean National Djoliba Dance Troupe, a group of sixty people, arrived in Guangzhou by plane on the evening of the 30th at the invitation of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee to visit and perform in our country.

### 几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团在穗首次演出  舞蹈团演出富有民族特色的歌舞节目受热烈欢迎  陶铸副总理接见舞蹈团领导人和主要成员

1966-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团在穗首次演出
    舞蹈团演出富有民族特色的歌舞节目受热烈欢迎
    陶铸副总理接见舞蹈团领导人和主要成员
    新华社广州四日电　几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团今晚在这里举行访问中国的首次演出。艺术家们演出的富有民族特色和浓厚生活气息的歌舞节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    国务院副总理陶铸和夫人曾志，广东省代省长林李明和夫人余珍，广东省副省长、广州市市长曾生和夫人阮群英，观看了几内亚艺术家们的演出。
    来自西非友好国家几内亚的艺术家们，今晚用中国话演唱了中国歌曲《东方红》，以及他们来中国后特地为中国观众赶排的表演唱《战斗的非洲歌颂毛主席和他的伟大事业》。他们演出的精采节目，在观众的热烈要求下又重演了一次。
    今晚的演出以合唱《佐利巴》开始。歌唱家库雅特·索里·康迪亚以他那高昂嘹亮的歌声，深深地吸引住了观众。艺术家们表演的其他五个歌舞节目中有舞蹈“母亲”，这个舞蹈反映了几内亚人民在反对殖民主义、保卫祖国的斗争中，一位英勇牺牲的战士的母亲的经历。艺术家们在富有非洲民族特色的咚咚鼓声中所作的热情奔放的演出，常常激起观众热烈的掌声。
    演出前，举行了开幕式。曾生在开幕式上讲话，他代表广东省、广州市人民向几内亚艺术家们表示最热烈的欢迎。他说，中几两国虽然远隔万里，但是我们两国人民在反对帝国主义、争取民族独立的斗争中，一向相互同情，相互鼓舞。他祝几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团在我国的演出获得最大的成功。
    舞蹈团总团长西索柯·阿马杜在讲话中说，他怀着荣幸和愉快的心情，在今天答谢中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅阁下，于一九六五年在几内亚难忘的访问期间对舞蹈团的邀请。他表示为中国和几内亚两国在文化、经济、政治上的友好关系而欢欣鼓舞，并祝这种友好关系与日俱增。
    出席开幕式和观看演出的，还有对外文委代表郑之辅、中国演出经理公司代经理丁波以及当地文艺界人士。
    几内亚共和国驻中国大使馆随员库亚特·巴拉也出席了开幕式并观看了演出。
    演出结束后，陶铸副总理和夫人等走上舞台，同演员们热烈握手，对他们的成功演出表示祝贺。
    新华社广州四日电　国务院副总理陶铸和夫人曾志，今晚接见了几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团总团长西索柯·阿马杜、副总团长库雅特·索里·康迪亚和夫人，以及舞蹈团的主要成员。陶铸副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有广东省代省长林李明和夫人余珍，广东省副省长、广州市市长曾生和夫人阮群英，对外文委代表郑之辅，中国演出经理公司代经理丁波等。
    几内亚驻中国大使馆随员库亚特·巴拉也在座。
    新华社广州电　广东省副省长、广州市市长曾生和夫人三月三十一日晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以总团长西索柯·阿马杜、副总团长库雅特·索里·康迪亚率领的几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团。
    曾生和西索柯·阿马杜在宴会上讲话的时候都指出，反对帝国主义和殖民主义的共同斗争，把中几两国人民紧密地联系在一起，中几两国人民的传统友谊是深厚的。曾生还向带来了几内亚人民友谊的几内亚艺术家们表示最热烈的欢迎，并祝他们在中国的访问演出获得成功。
    几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团一行六十人是应对外文委邀请来我国进行访问演出，在三十日晚乘飞机到达广州的。
