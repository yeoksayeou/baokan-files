Title: Liu Chairman and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on Syrian National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th April. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Nur al-Din al-Atassi, Head of State of the Syrian Arab Republic, on the 16th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Syrian Arab Republic. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Nur al-Din al-Atassi, Head of State of the Syrian Arab Republic, Damascus:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Syrian Arab Republic, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Syrian people.
    I wish the Syrian Arab Republic prosperity and strength, and its people happiness.
              Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                      Sixteenth of April, Nineteen Sixty-Six
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th April. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Yusuf Zuayyin, Prime Minister of the Syrian Arab Republic, on the 16th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Syrian Arab Republic. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Yusuf Zuayyin, Prime Minister of the Syrian Arab Republic, Damascus:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Syrian Arab Republic, I extend heartfelt congratulations and best wishes to Your Excellency, the government and the people of the Syrian Arab Republic.
    There exists a deep friendship between the people of China and Syria. The Chinese people have consistently supported the Syrian people's struggle against imperialism and for the maintenance of national independence. I sincerely hope that, through our joint efforts, the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Syria and the friendship between our two peoples will be increasingly consolidated and developed.
      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                      Sixteenth of April, Nineteen Sixty-Six

### 刘主席周总理电贺叙利亚国庆

1966-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理电贺叙利亚国庆
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇十六日打电报给阿拉伯叙利亚共和国国家元首努尔丁·阿塔西，祝贺阿拉伯叙利亚共和国国庆。电文如下：大马士革阿拉伯叙利亚共和国国家元首努尔丁·阿塔西阁下：
    在阿拉伯叙利亚共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和叙利亚人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝阿拉伯叙利亚共和国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。
              中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                      一九六六年四月十六日
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十六日打电报给阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理优素福·扎因，祝贺阿拉伯叙利亚共和国国庆。电文如下：大马士革阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理优素福·扎因阁下：
    在阿拉伯叙利亚共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下、阿拉伯叙利亚共和国政府和人民，表示衷心的祝贺和良好的祝愿。
    中叙两国人民之间存在着深厚的友谊。中国人民一贯支持叙利亚人民反对帝国主义、维护国家独立的斗争。我衷心地希望，在我们双方的共同努力下，中叙两国的友好合作关系和两国人民的友谊将日益巩固和发展。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      一九六六年四月十六日
