Title: Lumbering is Doing Revolution (Dialogue)
Author: Gu Xiangting
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 6

    Lumbering is Doing Revolution (Dialogue)
    Gu Xiangting, Tractor Driver, Sixth Farm, Dunhua Forestry Bureau
    A: The Changbai Mountains have countless ranges and peaks,
    B: The mountains have countless emerald cypresses and green pines.
    A: We are lumberjacks of the Changbai Mountains,
    B: We are the vanguards of the forestry front.
    A: Wearing willow helmets,
    B: Holding axes and saws,
    Both: We fight year-round in the high mountains and dense forests.
    A: When the axe rings,
    B: The mountains echo!
    A: Start the chainsaw,
    B: Like thunder!
    A: Look! The wood chips are like snowflakes,
    B: Look! The sawdust flies non-stop,
    A: The stirring and loud work songs,
    B: Awaken the echoes of the mountains.
    A: Thousand-year-old trees fall in response,
    B: Thump! Thump!
    A: Like the blast of an opening mountain cannon,
    B: Like the roar of spring thunder.
    A: The chainsaw flashing silver light to trim branches,
    B: The tractor gathers wood like a galloping steed;
    A: It's truly chasing after each other,
    B: Everyone strives to be a pioneer,
    A: Red flags wave on the mountains,
    B: Songs surge everywhere.
    A: The battle is so fierce!
    B: The competition is so fiery!
    A: What is scorching summer heat to us?
    B: What is freezing winter snow to us?
    A: Lumberjacks are like green pines,
    B: Unyielding to thunder, unshaken by wind!
    A: Chairman Mao's writings have given us infinite power,
    B: We can overcome any difficulty!
    A: Although we are in the forest,
    B: The great motherland is in our hearts—
    A: Our hearts beat for the motherland,
    B: Our blood boils for the revolution!
    A: Every construction project of the motherland,
    B: Has the labor of us lumberjacks.
    A: How can we not feel proud!
    B: How can we not feel honored!
    A: We have the wise leadership of the Party,
    B: We have the great Chairman Mao guiding our course!
    A: On the road of revolution and construction,
    B: Our forward steps will never stop!
    A: Go all out,
    B: Aim high,
    Both: Raise the revolutionary red flag high in our hands!
    A: Unite as one,
    B: Overcome all difficulties:
    Both: Strive to be heroes of revolution and construction!

### 伐木就是干革命（对口词）
顾香亭
1966-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    伐木就是干革命（对口词）
    敦化林业局六场拖拉机司机　顾香亭
    甲：长白山有无数丛山峻岭，
    乙：山岭上有无数翠柏青松。
    甲：我们是长白山的伐木工，
    乙：是林业战线的尖兵。
    甲：头戴着柳盔，
    乙：手握着斧锯，
    合：终年战斗在高山密林中。
    甲：斧子一响，
    乙：满山应；
    甲：开起油锯，
    乙：象雷鸣。
    甲：看！木楂有如雪片，
    乙：看！锯末飞舞不停，
    甲：激昂嘹亮的号子，
    乙：引起了群山的回鸣。
    甲：千年的古树应声倒下，
    乙：呼嗵！呼嗵！
    甲：好似开山炮响，
    乙：如同春雷轰鸣。
    甲：油锯打枝银光闪闪，
    乙：拖拉机集材似骏马奔腾；
    甲：真是你追我赶，
    乙：人人争当先锋，
    甲：山山红旗招展，
    乙：处处歌声沸腾。
    甲：战斗是这样激烈！
    乙：竞赛是这样火红！
    甲：说什么炎夏烈日如火，
    乙：管什么冬季大雪冰封，
    甲：伐木工人和青松一样，
    乙：雷打不倒，风吹不动！
    甲：毛主席著作给了我们无穷力量，
    乙：天大的困难也能战胜！
    甲：我们虽然身在林海，
    乙：伟大的祖国却在心中——
    甲：我们的心为祖国而欢跳，
    乙：我们的热血为革命而翻腾！
    甲：祖国的每一项建设，
    乙：都有我们伐木工的劳动。
    甲：我们怎能不感到自豪！
    乙：我们怎能不感到光荣！
    甲：我们有党的英明领导，
    乙：有伟大的毛主席指引航程！
    甲：在革命和建设的大路上，
    乙：我们前进的脚步永远不停！
    甲：鼓足干劲，
    乙：力争上游，
    合：革命红旗在手中高擎！
    甲：团结一心，
    乙：排除万难：
    合：争做革命和建设的英雄！
