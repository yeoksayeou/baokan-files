Title: Total Number of U.S. Troops Invading Vietnam Increases to 250,000
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Saigon, 29th dispatch: Approximately 5,000 American invading troops landed in Vung Tau, South Vietnam, on the 29th. This brings the total number of U.S. troops invading Vietnam to approximately 250,000.
    This unit is the main part of the First Brigade of the U.S. 25th Infantry Division. The Second and Third Brigades of this division arrived at the U.S. military bases in Cu Chi and Pleiku in January of this year and December of last year, respectively. Now, the U.S. 25th Infantry Division has basically all arrived in South Vietnam.
    According to reports from Washington, D.C., the testimony of U.S. Secretary of Defense McNamara, released on the 27th at a joint meeting of the U.S. Senate Armed Services Committee and the Military Appropriations Subcommittee, revealed that the United States will continue to increase troops to South Vietnam to meet the needs of expanding the war of aggression. McNamara said that the U.S. Department of Defense is prepared to "meet the continued expansion" of the requirements for U.S. troops in South Vietnam.

### 侵越美军总数增至二十五万

1966-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    侵越美军总数增至二十五万
    新华社二十九日讯　西贡消息：约五千名美国侵略军二十九日在南越头顿登陆。这使得侵越美军总数约达二十五万名。
    这支部队是美军第二十五步兵师第一旅的主要部分。这个师的第二旅和第三旅已经分别在今年一月和去年十二月到达南越的古芝和波来古美军基地。现在，美军第二十五步兵师已经基本上全部到达南越。
    另据来自华盛顿的消息报道，二十七日公布的美国国防部长麦克纳马拉在美国参议院军事委员会和军事拨款小组委员会联席会议上的证词透露，美国将继续增兵南越，以满足扩大侵略战争的需要。麦克纳马拉说，美国国防部准备“满足继续扩大”美国在南越的军队的要求。
