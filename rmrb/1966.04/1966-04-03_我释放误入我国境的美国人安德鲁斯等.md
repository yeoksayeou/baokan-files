Title: I Release Americans Andrews et al. Who Mistakenly Entered Our Territory

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 2nd. On March 24th, American William O. Andrews, along with Filipinos Ronnie Sentinar and Ted Alkisa, mistakenly entered the sea area of Lufeng County, Guangdong Province, in our country on a yacht, and were immediately detained by our local police. After our side pointed this out, they admitted their error in crossing the border. Today, our side has released them and allowed them to leave the country. During their detention, our relevant departments gave them the treatment they deserved.

### 我释放误入我国境的美国人安德鲁斯等

1966-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我释放误入我国境的美国人安德鲁斯等
    新华社广州二日电　三月二十四日，美国人威廉·欧·安德鲁斯，以及菲律宾人罗尼·森蒂纳尔和特德·阿尔基萨，乘坐游艇，误入我国广东省陆丰县海域，当即被我当地民警拘留。经我方指出后，他们已承认越境错误。今天，我方已将他们释放出境。在拘留期间，我有关部门给了他们应有的待遇。
