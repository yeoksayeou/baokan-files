Title: Artworks About Jiao Yulu Exhibited in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The art exhibition “Good Student of Chairman Mao, Jiao Yulu” opened today at the China Art Gallery in Beijing.
    The exhibition, organized by the Chinese Artists Association to satisfy the broad masses' desire to further learn from Comrade Jiao Yulu, displays more than 80 works of art in various forms, including sculptures, New Year pictures, serial pictures, prints, sketches, woodcuts, oil paintings, traditional Chinese paintings, color ink paintings, and propaganda posters. These works show the audience Comrade Jiao Yulu's brilliant deeds and noble ideological character of wholeheartedly serving the revolution and wholeheartedly serving the people from different perspectives. At the same time, through the scenes and images depicting Comrade Jiao Yulu studying and applying Chairman Mao’s works, people can prominently feel the brilliant light emitted by the great Mao Zedong Thought in this heroic figure.

### 关于焦裕禄的美术作品在京展出

1966-04-18
第2版()
专栏：

    关于焦裕禄的美术作品在京展出
    据新华社十七日讯　“毛主席的好学生焦裕禄”美术作品展览，今天在北京中国美术馆开幕。
    中国美术家协会为满足广大群众进一步向焦裕禄同志学习的愿望而举办的这次展览，陈列了雕塑、年画、连环画、版画、素描、木刻、油画、国画、彩墨画、宣传画等各种美术作品八十余件。这些作品从不同的方面，向观众显示了焦裕禄同志一心为革命、一心为人民的光辉事迹和崇高的思想品德。同时，通过表现焦裕禄同志学习、运用毛主席著作的画面、形象，使人们突出地感受到伟大的毛泽东思想在这一英雄人物身上发出的灿烂光芒。
