Title: Vietnamese Northern Army and People Shot Down Four U.S. Aircraft

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-20
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 19th - According to reports released here on the evening of the 19th: On the 19th, the army and people of Haiphong City, Quang Ninh Province, and Quang Binh Province in northern Vietnam shot down a total of four U.S. jet aircraft and damaged several others.
    On the 19th, U.S. bandit aircraft invaded the suburbs of Haiphong City and the airspace over northern Quang Binh Province, and bombed some residential areas in Quang Ninh Province. The local army and people fought heroically, using fierce artillery fire to severely punish the enemy aircraft. The army and people of Haiphong City and the army and people of Quang Ninh Province each shot down one enemy aircraft. The army and people of Quang Binh Province shot down two enemy aircraft, one of which was shot down by militiamen in Bu Trach district using light weapons.

### 越南北方军民击落美机四架

1966-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机四架
    新华社河内十九日电　据这里十九日晚上公布：越南北方海防市和广宁省、广平省军民十九日共击落美国喷气式飞机四架，击伤多架。
    十九日，美国强盗飞机侵入海防市郊区和广平省北部上空，并且轰炸广宁省的一些居民区。当地军民进行了英勇的战斗，以猛烈的炮火痛击敌机。海防市军民和广宁省军民各击落敌机一架。广平省军民击落敌机两架，其中布泽县民兵用轻武器击落一架。
