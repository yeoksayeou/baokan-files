Title: Argentine Vanguard Communist Organization's Newspaper "El Intransigente" Publishes Article: Mao Zedong Thought Guides the Revolutionary Transformation of Chinese Society; The Chinese Communist Party Educates the People to Serve the Chinese Revolution and the Revolutions of Asia, Africa, Latin America, and the World

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, Eighth Dispatch: The Argentine Vanguard Communist Organization's newspaper "El Intransigente" recently published an article stating: "Mao Zedong Thought guides the revolutionary transformation of Chinese society."
    The article, titled "This is How Socialism is Built," pointed out: "The Chinese Communist Party educates the people, workers, peasants, and soldiers to selflessly serve the cause of the Chinese revolution, the cause of the oppressed people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and the cause of the exploited of the world. The Chinese Communist Party has taken a major step in the history of the international communist movement by turning education into a fundamental tool of revolutionary struggle. Chinese comrades teach everyone that raising everyone's awareness through education helps to change reality, and they teach everyone that they must transform themselves and transform the world."
    The article said: China's "workers and peasants have created miracles in socialist construction. They understand that their work is not for personal gain, but for the benefit of all the Chinese people and the people of the world. Proletarian internationalism requires them to help the people of the world defeat U.S. imperialism."
    The article emphasized: "The Chinese People's Liberation Army is an army of workers and peasants. In this army, technical education is subordinate to political education. This army can defeat and crush U.S. imperialism because it has been educated with class hatred for the imperialist enemy and with love for the Chinese people and the exploited people of the world. The Chinese People's Liberation Army participates in socialist construction work and cooperates with workers and peasants in production. In these tasks, it demonstrates the finest qualities of a people's army."
    The article also said: Chinese soldiers showed heroism in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea. "They are preparing to confront U.S. imperialist aggression against China with courage and a spirit of sacrifice because they are people who are not afraid of hardship or death, because they love the people and serve the people."

### 阿根廷共产主义先锋组织机关报《不妥协报》发表文章  毛泽东思想指导着中国社会的革命变革  中国共产党教育人民为中国革命和亚非拉以及全世界革命服务

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    阿根廷共产主义先锋组织机关报《不妥协报》发表文章
    毛泽东思想指导着中国社会的革命变革
    中国共产党教育人民为中国革命和亚非拉以及全世界革命服务
    新华社八日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷共产主义先锋组织机关报《不妥协报》最近发表文章指出：“毛泽东思想指导着中国社会的革命变革”。
    这篇题为《社会主义是这样建设的》的文章指出：“中国共产党教育人民、工人、农民和士兵要无私地为中国革命的事业，为亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的被压迫人民和全世界的被剥削者的事业服务。中国共产党已在国际共产主义史上迈出了一大步，它把教育变成革命斗争的一个根本工具。中国同志们教导大家：通过教育来提高每个人的觉悟有助于改变现实，并且教导大家：必须改造自己和改造世界。”
    文章说：中国的“工人和农民创造了社会主义建设的奇迹，他们懂得，他们的工作不是追求个人利益，而是为了全中国人民的利益和世界各国人民的利益，无产阶级国际主义要求他们帮助世界各国人民击败美帝国主义。”
    文章强调说：“中国人民解放军是一支工农的军队，在这支军队里，技术教育从属于政治教育。这支军队能战胜和粉碎美帝国主义，因为它受了对帝国主义敌人的阶级仇恨和对中国人民和全世界被剥削人民的热爱的教育。中国人民解放军参加社会主义建设工作，并且在生产方面同工农合作。在这些工作中，它表现了人民军队的最优良的品质。”
    这篇文章还说：中国战士们在抗美援朝战争中表现出了英雄主义。“他们正准备以勇敢和牺牲的精神对付美帝国主义对中国的侵略，这是由于他们都是不怕苦、不怕死的人，因为他们热爱人民，为人民服务。”
