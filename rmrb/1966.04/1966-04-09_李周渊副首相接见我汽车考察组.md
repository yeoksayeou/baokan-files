Title: Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan Receives Chinese Automotive Inspection Group
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 8th -- Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan of the Korean Cabinet received the Chinese Automotive Inspection Group led by Wen Ge, Deputy Manager of the Changchun Automotive Branch of the China Automotive Industry Corporation, on the 5th, and held friendly talks with them.
    Present at the meeting were Huang Kuizhe, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Mechanical Industry of Korea, and Kong Zhentai, Deputy Director of the General Bureau of Foreign Economic Affairs. Jiao Ruoyu, Chinese Ambassador to Korea, was also present.
    The Chinese Automotive Inspection Group arrived in Korea on March 15th at the invitation of the Korean government.

### 李周渊副首相接见我汽车考察组

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    李周渊副首相接见我汽车考察组
    新华社平壤电　朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊五日接见了由中国汽车工业公司长春汽车分公司副经理文格率领的中国汽车考察组，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，朝鲜机械工业省副相黄奎喆和对外经济总局副局长孔振泰。中国驻朝鲜大使焦若愚也在座。
    中国汽车考察组是三月十五日应邀来朝访问的。
