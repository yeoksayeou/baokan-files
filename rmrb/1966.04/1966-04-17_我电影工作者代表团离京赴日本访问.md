Title: Our Film Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visit to Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th – At the invitation of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, a Chinese film workers delegation of seven people, led by Yue Lin, director of the Changchun Film Studio, left Beijing today for a friendly visit to Japan. Those who saw them off at the airport included Si Tu Huimin, Fu Bingyou, Tian Fang, Hong Zang, Guo Laowei and other relevant people.

### 我电影工作者代表团离京赴日本访问

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    我电影工作者代表团离京赴日本访问
    新华社十六日讯　应日中文化交流协会邀请，中国电影工作者代表团一行七人，在长春电影制片厂厂长岳林率领下，今日离京前往日本进行友好访问。到机场欢送的有司徒慧敏、傅兵友、田方、洪藏、郭劳为等有关方面人士。
