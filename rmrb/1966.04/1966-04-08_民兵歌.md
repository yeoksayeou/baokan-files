Title: Militia Song
Author: Wang Guilan
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    Militia Song
    Female Commune Member of Zongyang County Wang Guilan
    Hoe in hand by day, gun in hand by night,
    Production and training, busy day and night;
    Silver hoe tills a new world,
    Steel gun points sharply at the American wolf.

**Named Entities**

枞阳县 Zongyang County
汪桂兰 Wang Guilan
美国狼   American wolf
