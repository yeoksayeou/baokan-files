Title: Angry Protests Against U.S. Aggressors Occupying Dominican Territory: Anti-American Demonstrations Erupt Again in Dominican Republic's Second Largest City; Mexican General Jara Calls on People of All Countries to Strongly Protest U.S. Aggression

Author:

Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Santo Domingo, April 23rd: Thousands of young people in Santiago, the Dominican Republic's second-largest city, held a large-scale demonstration on the 22nd, demanding that U.S. aggressor troops get out of the Dominican Republic.

    The demonstrators, chests held high, strode through many streets of Santiago. They shouted anti-American slogans, angrily protesting the U.S. aggressor troops occupying Dominican territory under the banner of the "Inter-American Armed Forces" and trampling on Dominican sovereignty.

    It is reported that this demonstration is one of a series of activities held by the Dominican people to commemorate the first anniversary of the armed uprising that overthrew the pro-U.S. dictatorial regime of Cabral. As everyone knows, it was precisely to suppress this armed uprising and prevent the Dominican people from achieving independence and freedom that the U.S. government brazenly sent troops to invade the Dominican Republic a year ago.

    According to a report from Santo Domingo published in the U.S. weekly *National Guardian* on April 9th, the Dominican people harbor "unimaginable" deep hatred for U.S. imperialism. The author of the report wrote: "Here, I can feel the deep hatred accumulated against the United States from all sides... Dominicans are insulted every day, and they cannot forget their dead." "Since (last) May, Dominicans have never opened their doors to them (the U.S. aggressors)." The report also stated that in Santo Domingo, "silence is often broken by gunfire."

    The report discussed the various atrocities committed by the U.S. aggressor troops in the Dominican Republic. The report said that a Dominican student was killed immediately for refusing the insulting order from an American to sweep the streets. At the time, "the student said, 'I am not your dog,' and an American soldier shot him."

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, April 22nd: Mexican social figure General Heriberto Jara stated in a speech on the 21st that the U.S. aggression against the Dominican Republic is "the most lawless." He pointed out that "the people of all freedom-loving countries have a responsibility to strongly protest this crime that has affected all of Latin America."

    General Jara made these remarks while commemorating the 52nd anniversary of the Mexican people's opposition to U.S. aggression and defense of the Port of Veracruz.

    Earlier, the International Human Rights Front, based in Mexico City, called on people from all countries to actively support the "Week of Solidarity with the Dominican People" held from the 24th to the 30th of this month, and to organize rallies protesting the U.S. government's acts of aggression. The organization pointed out that the aggression launched by the U.S. authorities against the Dominican Republic undermined the right to self-determination, national sovereignty, and the principle of non-interference of all peoples.

### 愤怒抗议美国侵略者霸占  多米尼加国土尼加第二大城再度爆发反美示威  墨西哥哈拉将军呼吁各国人民强烈抗议美国侵略罪行

1966-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

    愤怒抗议美国侵略者霸占
    多米尼加国土尼加第二大城再度爆发反美示威
    墨西哥哈拉将军呼吁各国人民强烈抗议美国侵略罪行
    据新华社二十三日讯　圣多明各消息：多米尼加第二大城市圣地亚哥的数千名青年，二十二日举行大规模示威游行，要求美国侵略军滚出多米尼加。
    示威者挺着胸膛，在圣地亚哥的许多街道上阔步前进。他们高呼反美口号，愤怒抗议美国侵略军在“泛美武装部队”的招牌下霸占多米尼加领土，践踏多米尼加主权。
    据报道，这次示威是多米尼加人民为纪念推翻卡夫拉尔亲美独裁政权武装起义一周年而举行的一系列活动之一。大家知道，美国政府正是为了镇压这次武装起义，不让多米尼加人民取得独立自由，在一年前悍然派兵侵略多米尼加。
    另据美国《国民前卫》周报九日刊载一篇来自圣多明各的报道说，多米尼加人民对美帝国主义抱有“不可想象的”深刻仇恨。这篇报道的作者写道：“这里，我从各个方面都能感到对美国积蓄起来的深仇大恨。……多米尼加人每天都受到侮辱，他们忘不了他们的死者。”“从（去年）五月以来，多米尼加人从来不给他们（美国侵略者）打开自己的家门。”报道还说，在圣多明各，“寂静经常被枪声打破。”
    这篇报道谈到美国侵略军在多米尼加犯下的种种暴行。报道说，一个多米尼加学生由于拒绝美国佬要他打扫街道的侮辱性命令，立即遭到杀害。当时“这位学生说，‘我不是你们的狗’，一个美国兵就枪杀了他。”
    据新华社墨西哥城二十二日电　墨西哥社会名流埃里维尔托·哈拉将军二十一日发表谈话，谴责美国对多米尼加的侵略是“最无法无天的”。他指出，“爱好自由的各国人民有责任对这一影响了全拉丁美洲的罪行提出强烈的抗议”。
    哈拉将军是在纪念墨西哥人民反对美国侵略、保卫维拉克鲁斯港五十二周年时讲这番话的。
    早些时候，设在墨西哥城的国际人权阵线号召世界各国人士积极支持本月二十四日到三十日举行的“声援多米尼加人民周”，组织抗议美国政府侵略行为的集会。这个组织指出，美国当局对多米尼加发动的侵略破坏了各国人民的自决权、民族主权和不干涉原则。
