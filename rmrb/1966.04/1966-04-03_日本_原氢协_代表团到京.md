Title: Japanese "Gensuikyo" Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. A thirteen-member friendly delegation from the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs (Gensuikyo), led by Yamaguchi Keiichi, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. They are visiting our country at the invitation of the China Peace Committee.
    Wang Chuanbin, He Lin, and others welcomed them at the airport. Mr. Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese friend in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome them.



**Named Entities**

中国  China
北京  Beijing
日本  Japan
山口圭一  Yamaguchi Keiichi
王传斌  Wang Chuanbin
何林  He Lin
西园寺公一  Saionji Kinkazu
禁止原子弹氢弹协议会  Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs (translation)

### 日本“原氢协”代表团到京

1966-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本“原氢协”代表团到京
    新华社二日讯　由山口圭一率领的日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会友好代表团一行十三人，今晚乘飞机到达北京，他们是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有王传斌、何林等。在京的日本朋友西园寺公一先生，也前往机场欢迎。
