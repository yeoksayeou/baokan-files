Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Cables Thanks to King Zahir Shah; Sends Telegram of Greetings to President Ayub Khan While Flying Over Pakistan

Author:

Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 8th — Chairman Liu Shaoqi sent a telegram to King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan on the 8th, expressing his gratitude as he left Afghanistan to return home. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan, Kabul:
    As we are about to leave the beautiful land of Afghanistan, my wife and I, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and all accompanying personnel, would like to once again express our heartfelt gratitude to Your Majesty the King and Queen, to the Afghan government and people. Thank you for your warm and friendly reception and welcome. We wish Afghanistan prosperity and strength. We wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan will grow stronger day by day.
          Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People’s Republic of China
                    April 8, 1966

    Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 8th — When Chairman Liu Shaoqi left Afghanistan on the morning of the 8th to return home, flying over Pakistani airspace, he sent a telegram of greetings to Pakistani President Ayub Khan. The telegram reads:
    To His Excellency Field Marshal Mohammed Ayub Khan, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Rawalpindi:
    As I fly over your country's airspace, I extend friendly greetings to Your Excellency, to the Government and people of Pakistan. (Photo Attached)

    On April 8, Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei, fully loaded with the deep friendship of the Afghan people towards the Chinese people, left Kabul by special plane to return home. King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan (front row, right 1), Queen Homeira (front row, right 3) and the King's uncle Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi (front row, left 1) and others saw the Chinese guests off at the airport.
                          Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian (faxed photo)

    On April 7, Chairman Liu Shaoqi held a farewell banquet at the Dilkusha Palace in Kabul to entertain Afghan King Mohammed Zahir Shah and Queen Homeira. The picture shows the scene at the banquet. The first from the right is King Zahir Shah.
                            Xinhua News Agency Reporter Ge Chunjiang (faxed photo)

### 刘少奇主席电谢查希尔·沙阿国王  飞经巴基斯坦时致电问候阿尤布·汗总统

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电谢查希尔·沙阿国王
    飞经巴基斯坦时致电问候阿尤布·汗总统
    新华社喀布尔八日电　刘少奇主席八日在离开阿富汗回国的时候，打电报给阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿，表示感谢。电报全文如下：喀布尔阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下
    在即将离开美丽的阿富汗国土的时候，我和我的夫人、陈毅副总理和夫人以及全体随行人员，谨向国王和王后陛下，向阿富汗政府和人民再一次表示由衷的感谢。感谢你们对我们的热情友好的接待和欢迎。祝阿富汗繁荣昌盛。祝中阿两国人民的友谊日益发展。
          中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    一九六六年四月八日
    新华社乌鲁木齐八日电　刘少奇主席八日上午在离开阿富汗回国飞经巴基斯坦领空时，给巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗发去了问候电。电文说：拉瓦尔品第巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统
    穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅阁下：在飞经贵国领空的时候，我向阁下，向巴基斯坦政府和人民致以友好的问候。（附图片）
    四月八日，中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美，满载着阿富汗人民对中国人民的深厚友谊，乘专机离喀布尔回国。阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿（前右一）、王后霍梅拉（前右三）和国王的叔父沙阿·瓦利·汗·加齐元帅（前左一）等到机场欢送中国贵宾。
                          新华社记者　杜修贤摄（传真照片）
    四月七日，刘少奇主席在喀布尔的迪尔库沙宫举行告别宴会，招待阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿和王后霍梅拉。图为宴会时情形。右一是查希尔·沙阿国王。
