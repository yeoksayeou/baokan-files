Author:
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 3

    The Great Revolutionary Teacher -- Lenin
    This year, April 22nd marks the 96th anniversary of the birth of the great revolutionary teacher, Lenin. Lenin's life was the life of a great proletarian revolutionary, spent in fierce struggle against imperialism, all reactionaries, and opportunists.
    Russia is the birthplace of Leninism. After Stalin's death, the Soviet leadership, headed by Khrushchev, embarked on the path of betraying Lenin and Leninism, turning the Soviet leadership into the center of modern revisionism. However, more than ninety percent of the people in the world want revolution, and more than ninety percent of the communists want to advance on the Marxist-Leninist path. Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought will surely win, and imperialism, revisionism, and all reactionaries will surely be defeated. (Attached pictures)
    Right picture: Late at night on April 16, 1917, Lenin returned from Switzerland to Petrograd to directly lead the revolution. The picture shows Lenin delivering a speech on an armored car in front of the station, calling on the masses to fight for the victory of the socialist revolution. (Painting)
    Bottom picture: Lenin at work.
    Top picture: Lenin (third from the left in the front row) among the delegates to the Sixth Extraordinary Congress of All-Russia Soviets. In documents such as "Letter to American Workers" and the speech on the international situation at the Sixth Extraordinary Congress of All-Russia Soviets, Lenin pointed out that U.S. imperialism was "the executioner and gendarme who strangled Russian freedom" and played "the role of strangler and executioner of the world revolution."
    Left picture: On July 19, 1917, the Russian bourgeois Provisional Government ordered the arrest of Lenin. The Russian Communist Party (Bolsheviks) decided to have Lenin hide on the shores of Lake Razliv. Here, Lenin wrote the famous book "State and Revolution." (Painting) All pictures in this column are issued by Xinhua News Agency.

### 伟大的革命导师——列宁

1966-04-22
第3版()
专栏：

    伟大的革命导师——列宁
    今年四月二十二日是伟大的革命导师——列宁诞生九十六周年。列宁的一生，是伟大的无产阶级革命家的一生，是在同帝国主义、各种反动派和机会主义者的激烈斗争中度过的。
    俄国是列宁主义故乡。在斯大林逝世以后，以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏共领导，走上了背叛列宁、背叛列宁主义的道路，使苏共领导变成为现代修正主义的中心。但是，全世界百分之九十以上的人是要革命的，百分之九十以上的共产党人是要在马克思列宁主义轨道上前进的。马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想必胜，帝国主义、修正主义和一切反动派必败。（附图片）
    右图，一九一七年四月十六日深夜，列宁从瑞士回到彼得格勒直接领导革命。图为列宁在车站前的铁甲车上，向欢迎的人群发表演说，号召群众为社会主义革命的胜利而斗争。（画）
    下图，列宁在工作。
    上图，列宁（前左第三人）在全俄苏维埃第六次非常代表大会的代表中间。在《给美国工人的信》、全俄苏维埃第六次非常代表大会关于国际形势的演说等文件中，列宁曾指出，美帝国主义是“消灭俄国自由的刽子手和宪兵”，扮演了“世界革命的绞杀者和刽子手的角色”。
    左图，一九一七年七月十九日，俄国资产阶级临时政府下令逮捕列宁。俄共（布尔什维克）决定让列宁隐匿到拉兹里夫湖畔。在这里，列宁写成了著名的《国家与革命》一书。（画）		本栏图片均为新华社所发
