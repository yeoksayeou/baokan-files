Title: Intensifying Plans to Expand the War of Aggression Against Indochina: US Deputy Secretary of Defense in Bangkok
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, April 2nd: US Deputy Secretary of Defense Vance, leading a US government delegation, arrived in Bangkok on March 31st to discuss military deployments with the Thai puppet authorities to intensify the aggression against Indochina.

    According to Thai newspapers, Vance immediately went to the Thai Army Department after getting off the plane and discussed the "situation in Vietnam and Laos" and increasing US "military aid" to Thailand with Thai Army Commander-in-Chief Praphas Charusathien and other senior Thai military generals. After the meeting, Vance inspected the US naval base in Sattahip and nearby military facilities.

    It is reported that he will also hold talks with Thai Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn, who has just returned from activities in Vientiane.

    According to Thai newspapers, Thai Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn was in Laos for four days from March 28th to 31st. Thanom held "talks" with Prince Souvanna Phouma in Vientiane, discussing the "situation in Laos." He also held "talks" with Vientiane right-wing army commander Ouane Rattikone and others to discuss strengthening military collusion between Thailand and the Vientiane puppet clique and jointly suppressing the patriotic forces of Thailand and Laos in the Thai-Laotian border area.

### 加紧策划扩大侵略印度支那战争  美国防部副部长到曼谷活动

1966-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    加紧策划扩大侵略印度支那战争
    美国防部副部长到曼谷活动
    据新华社二日讯　曼谷消息：美国国防部副部长万斯率领美国政府代表团三月三十一日抵达曼谷，将同泰国傀儡当局讨论加紧侵略印度支那的军事部署问题。
    据泰国报纸报道，万斯一下飞机后，就立即前往泰国陆军署，同泰国陆军总司令巴博等泰国高级军事将领讨论“越南和老挝的局势”以及美国向泰国增加“军事援助”等问题。会谈后，万斯视察了美国在梭桃邑的海军基地和附近的军事设施。
    据报道，他还将同刚到万象活动回来的泰国总理他侬举行会谈。
    据泰国报纸报道，泰国总理他侬从三月二十八日到三十一日在老挝活动了四天。他侬在万象同富马亲王进行了“会谈”，讨论了“老挝局势”，还同万象右派军队总司令温·拉迪功等进行“会谈”，讨论加强泰国和万象傀儡集团的军事勾结和共同镇压泰、老边境的泰国和老挝爱国力量等问题。
