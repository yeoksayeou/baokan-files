Title: Syrian Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party Special Congress Announces Policy of Non-Alignment

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 5

    叙利亚阿拉伯复兴社会党特别代表大会
    宣布奉行不结盟对外政策
    新华社大马士革二日电　据大马士革电台二日广播，上星期闭幕的叙利亚阿拉伯复兴社会党特别代表大会发表一项声明，宣布奉行不结盟的对外政策。
    声明表示将同阿拉伯进步力量一起，反对新老殖民主义的统治，并且揭露它们的本质。
    声明要求撤除外国军事基地，撤出外国军队。
    声明指出，不结盟国家应当加强反对殖民主义和消除落后的斗争。声明谴责种族歧视，号召对世界犹太复国主义进行斗争。声明向正在英勇地进行反帝斗争的越南人民致敬。

Syrian Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party Special Congress
Announces Policy of Non-Alignment
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 2nd -- According to a broadcast by Damascus Radio on the 2nd, the Special Congress of the Syrian Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party, which closed last week, issued a statement announcing a policy of non-alignment in foreign affairs.
The statement expressed its intention to stand together with Arab progressive forces to oppose the rule of new and old colonialism and to expose their true nature.
The statement demanded the removal of foreign military bases and the withdrawal of foreign troops.
The statement pointed out that non-aligned countries should strengthen the struggle against colonialism and the elimination of backwardness. The statement condemned racial discrimination and called for a struggle against world Zionism. The statement paid tribute to the Vietnamese people who are heroically waging the anti-imperialist struggle.
