Title: Chairman Liu Receives Personnel of Our Embassy in Afghanistan, and Also Meets with Chinese Experts and Students in Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 7th — Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi met here this morning with all the personnel of the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan, as well as Chinese experts and students in Afghanistan, and took a group photo with them.
    Present at the reception were Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other personnel accompanying Chairman Liu on his visit to Afghanistan.

### 刘主席接见我驻阿使馆人员  同时接见我在阿专家和留学生

1966-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见我驻阿使馆人员
    同时接见我在阿专家和留学生
    新华社喀布尔七日电　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇七日上午在这里接见了中国驻阿富汗大使馆的全体人员以及在阿富汗的中国专家和留学生，并且同他们合影留念。
    接见时在座的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫和陪同刘主席访问阿富汗的其他人员。
