Title: Ambassador Natanaeli Pays Respects to Chairman Zhu and Vice Chairman Dong Separately
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Albania's newly appointed Ambassador to China, Vasil Nathanaili, paid a visit to Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon. Chairman Zhu De held a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Albania's newly appointed Ambassador to China, Vasil Nathanaili, paid a visit to Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, this morning. Vice Chairman Dong Biwu held a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

### 纳塔奈利大使分别拜会朱委员长董副主席

1966-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

    纳塔奈利大使分别拜会朱委员长董副主席
    新华社十六日讯　阿尔巴尼亚新任驻华大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利，今天下午拜会全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    新华社十六日讯　阿尔巴尼亚新任驻华大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利，今天上午拜会中华人民共和国副主席董必武。董必武副主席同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
