Title: Pakistan to Publish Chairman Liu's Works
Author:
Date: 1966-04-01
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 30th. According to a report in the "Pakistan Times" on the 29th, at this historically significant moment of Chairman Liu Shaoqi's visit to Pakistan, the Lahore People's Publishing House has decided to publish four of Chairman Liu Shaoqi's works in English, Urdu, and Bengali.

    The four books are "Nationalism and Internationalism," "On the Party," "Speech at the Meeting Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China," and "How to Be a Good Communist."

### 巴基斯坦将出版刘主席的著作

1966-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦将出版刘主席的著作
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三十日电　据《巴基斯坦时报》二十九日报道，在刘少奇主席访问巴基斯坦的具有历史意义的时刻，拉合尔人民出版社决定用英文、乌尔都文和孟加拉文出版刘少奇主席的四本著作。
    这四本书是《民族主义与国际主义》、《论党》、《在庆祝中国共产党成立四十周年大会上的讲话》以及《论共产党员的修养》。
