Title: Head of Chinese Government Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Bulgaria and East Germany
Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- Fu Shenglin, Head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation and Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade, returned to Beijing by train this afternoon after concluding trade negotiations for 1966 between our country and the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the German Democratic Republic, and signing related agreements.

    Jiang Ming, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade, and others went to the station to welcome him.

    Stoichev, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, and Liebermann, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic, also went to the station to welcome him.

### 我政府贸易代表团长访问保德后回京

1966-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团长访问保德后回京
    新华社六日讯　中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长傅生麟在结束我国同保加利亚人民共和国和德意志民主共和国一九六六年度贸易谈判并签订有关的协定后，于今天下午乘火车回到北京。
    前往车站迎接的有对外贸易部副部长江明等。
    保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使斯托伊切夫和德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办李伯曼等也到车站迎接。
