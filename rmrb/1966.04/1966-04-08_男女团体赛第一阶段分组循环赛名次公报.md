Title: First Stage Group Round Robin Rankings of Men's and Women's Team Competitions

Author:
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 6th – Public announcement of the rankings for the first stage group round robin competitions of the 1966 National Table Tennis Championships men's and women's team events.

    Men's Team Competition
    First Group – Shanghai Team 1, Shaanxi Team, Yunnan Team.
    Second Group – Beijing Team 1, Hunan Team, Shandong Team.
    Third Group – Hubei Team, Guangdong Team 2, Heilongjiang Team, Qinghai Team.
    Fourth Group – Jiangxi Team, Shanghai Team 3, Gansu Team, Fujian Team.
    Fifth Group – Hebei Team 1, Henan Team, Beijing Team 2, Zhejiang Team 2.
    Sixth Group – Jilin Team, Anhui Team, Hebei Team 2.
    Seventh Group – Shanghai Team 2, Liaoning Team, Zhejiang Team 1.
    Eighth Group – Sichuan Team, Jiangsu Team 2, Guizhou Team.
    Ninth Group – Guangdong Team 1, Guangxi Team, Hebei Team 3.
    Tenth Group – Jiangsu Team 1, Fujian Team 1, Shanghai Team 4.

    Women's Team Competition
    First Group – Shanghai Team 1, Hunan Team, Henan Team 2, Shaanxi Team.
    Second Group – Sichuan Team, Fujian Team 1, Shandong Team 2, Henan Team 1.
    Third Group – Guangdong Team 1, Zhejiang Team 1, Gansu Team.
    Fourth Group – Shandong Team 1, Guangxi Team, Hebei Team 2, Qinghai Team.
    Fifth Group – Beijing Team 2, Jilin Team, Shanghai Team 4, Jiangxi Team.
    Sixth Group – Beijing Team 1, Shanghai Team 3, Guizhou Team.
    Seventh Group – Shanghai Team 2, Jiangsu Team, Anhui Team.
    Eighth Group – Hubei Team, Guangdong Team 2, Heilongjiang Team.
    Ninth Group – Liaoning Team 1, Yunnan Team, Fujian Team 2, Zhejiang Team 2.
    Tenth Group – Shanxi Team, Hebei Team 1, Liaoning Team 2.

    The competition method for the second stage of this National Championship men's and women's team events is as follows: The teams from the first, fourth, fifth, eighth, and ninth groups in the first stage will be divided into Group A, and the teams from the second, third, sixth, seventh, and tenth groups in the first stage will be divided into Group B. Then, the teams with the same ranking in the five groups of the first stage within the large group will be divided into one group, and the single round robin competition method will be used to determine the ranking of the large group. For example, the first-place teams in the five groups of the first stage within the same large group will determine the rankings from first to fifth in the large group through a round robin competition; the second-place teams will determine the rankings from sixth to tenth, and so on.

### 男女团体赛第一阶段分组循环赛名次公报

1966-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

    男女团体赛第一阶段分组循环赛名次公报
    新华社上海六日电　一九六六年全国乒乓球锦标赛男、女团体赛第一阶段分组循环赛名次公报。
    男子团体赛
    第一组——上海一队、陕西队、云南队。
    第二组——北京一队、湖南队、山东队。
    第三组——湖北队、广东二队、黑龙江队、青海队。
    第四组——江西队、上海三队、甘肃队、福建队。
    第五组——河北一队、河南队、北京二队、浙江二队。
    第六组——吉林队、安徽队、河北二队
    第七组——上海二队、辽宁队、浙江一队。
    第八组——四川队、江苏二队、贵州队。
    第九组——广东一队、广西队、河北三队。
    第十组——江苏一队、福建一队、上海四队。
    女子团体赛
    第一组——上海一队、湖南队、河南二队、陕西队。
    第二组——四川队、福建一队、山东二队、河南一队。
    第三组——广东一队、浙江一队、甘肃队。
    第四组——山东一队、广西队、河北二队、青海队。
    第五组——北京二队、吉林队、上海四队、江西队。
    第六组——北京一队、上海三队、贵州队。
    第七组——上海二队、江苏队、安徽队。
    第八组——湖北队、广东二队、黑龙江队。
    第九组——辽宁一队、云南队、福建二队、浙江二队。
    第十组——山西队、河北一队、辽宁二队。
    这次锦标赛男、女团体赛第二阶段的比赛办法是：将第一阶段中第一、四、五、八、九小组的各队划为甲组，第二、三、六、七、十小组的各队划为乙组。然后将大组内第一阶段的五个小组同名次的队划为一组，分别采用单循环赛的办法决定大组的名次。如划在同一大组内的第一阶段五个小组的第一名，将经过循环赛决定大组的一至五名的名次；第二名的队决定六至十名的名次，其余依此类推。
