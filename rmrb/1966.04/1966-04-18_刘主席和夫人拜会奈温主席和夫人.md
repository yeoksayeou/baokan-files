Title: Chairman Liu and Madame Pay a Courtesy Call on Chairman Ne Win and Madame

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 17th (telegram) - Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and Madame paid a courtesy call on Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and Madame here this afternoon. They held cordial and friendly talks.
    Present on the Chinese side during the courtesy call were Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and Madame, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Geng Biao and Madame; present on the Burmese side were Minister of Foreign Affairs U Thi Han and Madame.

### 刘主席和夫人拜会奈温主席和夫人

1966-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席和夫人拜会奈温主席和夫人
    新华社仰光十七日电　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人十七日下午在这里拜会了缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军和夫人。他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    拜会时，中国方面在座的有，副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻缅甸大使耿飚和夫人；缅甸方面在座的有外交部长吴蒂汉和夫人。
