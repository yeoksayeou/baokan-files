Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Receives Chinese Transportation Delegation; Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Holds Banquet for Delegation's Visit

Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 3

    Premier Pham Van Dong Receives Chinese Transportation Delegation
    Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Holds Banquet for Delegation's Visit
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 11th – Pham Van Dong, Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received the Chinese transportation delegation led by Sun Daguang, Minister of Transportation, on the 9th and had a cordial and friendly conversation with the delegation.
    Present at the reception were Phan Trong Tue, Minister of Transportation and Communications of Vietnam, and Hong Thich Tam, Vice Minister.
    Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 11th – Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, held a banquet on the evening of the 10th for the visit of the Chinese transportation delegation led by Sun Daguang, Minister of Transportation.
    Invited to the banquet were Phan Trong Tue, Minister of Transportation and Communications of Vietnam, Hong Thich Tam, Nguyen Huu Mai, Vice Ministers, Hoang Quoc Viet, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Nguyen Co Thach, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Le Dong, Vice Minister of Commerce, and other responsible individuals from relevant departments.
    Ambassador Zhu Qiwu and Minister Phan Trong Tue spoke at the banquet. They jointly wished for the increasingly developed friendly, mutually supportive, and cooperative relationship between China and Vietnam. Ambassador Zhu Qiwu emphasized that the Chinese people firmly support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against American aggression and for national salvation. The fighting friendship and great unity between the Chinese and Vietnamese people cannot be destroyed by any force.
    On the evening of the 9th, the employees of the Vietnamese transportation and communications departments held a gathering to warmly welcome the Chinese transportation delegation.

### 范文同总理接见我交通代表团  我驻越大使为代表团访越举行宴会

1966-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    范文同总理接见我交通代表团
    我驻越大使为代表团访越举行宴会
    新华社河内十一日电　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同九日接见了由交通部部长孙大光率领的中国交通代表团，并同代表团进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，越南交通运输部部长潘仲慧和副部长洪赤心。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文也在座。
    新华社河内十一日电　中国驻越南大使朱其文十日晚上为由交通部部长孙大光率领的中国交通代表团访问越南举行宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的有，越南交通运输部部长潘仲慧、副部长洪赤心、阮友梅，越中友好协会会长黄国越，外交部副部长阮基石，商业部副部长黎东和其他有关方面的负责人。
    朱其文大使和潘仲慧部长在宴会上讲了话。他们共祝中越两国的友好互助合作关系日益发展。朱其文大使强调说，中国人民坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国正义斗争。中越两国人民的战斗友谊和伟大团结是任何力量也破坏不了的。
    九日晚上，越南交通运输部门的职工举行集会，热烈欢迎中国交通代表团。
