Title: Our Transportation Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-04-13
Edition: 4

    Our Transportation Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th—The Chinese Transportation Delegation, led by Minister of Communications Sun Dagang, returned to Beijing by plane today after a friendly visit to Vietnam.
    Responsible individuals from relevant departments who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Ma Yaoji, Peng Deqing, Ge Chen, and others. Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我交通代表团访越后回到北京

1966-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我交通代表团访越后回到北京
    新华社十二日讯　由交通部部长孙大光率领的中国交通代表团，在越南进行友好访问后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有关方面负责人有：马耀骥、彭德清、葛琛等。越南驻中国大使陈子平等也到机场迎接。
