Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    New Zealand Industrial Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 20th) At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the China Highway Transportation Trade Union, and the China Railway Trade Union, the New Zealand Industrial Union Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane today to participate in the “May Day” International Labor Day celebrations and pay a friendly visit to our country. They are Peter A. Hansen, National Secretary of the New Zealand Tramways Union; Luyier Su Sen, Chairman of the New Zealand Tramways Union; R. J. Doughty, Chairman of the New Zealand Railway Employees’ Union; Ian Smith, Branch Committee Member of the New Zealand Wellington Waterfront Workers' Union; and Ru Ti Smith, Organizer of the New Zealand North Island Drivers’ Union.
    Greeting them at the airport were Lu Zongying, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Zhi, Vice Chairman of the China Railway Trade Union, among others.

### 新西兰产业工会代表团到京

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    新西兰产业工会代表团到京
    新华社二十日讯　应中华全国总工会和中国公路运输工会、中国铁路工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动并进行友好访问的新西兰产业工会代表团今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是新西兰电车工会全国书记彼·艾·汉森，新西兰电车工会主席陆依尔·苏森，新西兰铁路从业人员工会主席尔·杰·道尔蒂，新西兰惠灵顿码头工会支委伊恩·史密斯，新西兰北岛司机工会组织员鲁·蒂·史密斯。
    到机场迎接的有中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长卢宗英、中国铁路工会副主席章智等。
