Title: Rahman Arif Assumes Office as New Iraqi President, Iraq Holds State Funeral for Late President
Author:
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 4

    Rahman Arif Assumes Office as New Iraqi President
    Iraq Holds State Funeral for Late President
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 17 (Dispatch) – According to Baghdad Radio's broadcast on the morning of April 17, Abdul Rahman Muhammad Arif was unanimously elected as President of the Republic of Iraq at a joint meeting of the Cabinet and the National Defense Council on the evening of the 16th and has been sworn into office.
    The new president is the elder brother of former President Abdul Salam Muhammad Arif, who died in a plane crash on the 13th. He previously served as Acting Chief of Staff of the Iraqi Army and Commander-in-Chief of Ground Forces.
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 16 (Dispatch) – A state funeral was held in Baghdad on the afternoon of April 16 for the late Iraqi President Abdul Salam Muhammad Arif.
    Among those attending the funeral were Acting President and Prime Minister of Iraq Abdul Rahman al-Bazzaz, Acting Chief of Staff Abdul Rahman Arif, members of the Iraqi cabinet, and other senior government officials and officers. Chinese Ambassador to Iraq Cao Chi, representatives from other countries, and diplomatic envoys stationed here also attended the funeral.

### 拉赫曼·阿里夫就任伊拉克新总统  伊拉克为故总统举行国葬

1966-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    拉赫曼·阿里夫就任伊拉克新总统
    伊拉克为故总统举行国葬
    新华社巴格达十七日电　据巴格达电台十七日上午广播，阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德·阿里夫在十六日晚上一次内阁和国防委员会的联席会议上，被一致选为伊拉克共和国总统，并已宣誓就职。
    新总统是在十三日因飞机失事遇难的前总统阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·穆罕默德·阿里夫的哥哥，他原任伊拉克代理陆军参谋长兼地面部队总司令。
    新华社巴格达十六日电　巴格达十六日下午为已故的伊拉克总统阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·穆罕默德·阿里夫举行国葬。
    参加送葬的有：伊拉克代理总统兼总理阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·巴扎兹，代理参谋长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·阿里夫，伊拉克内阁成员和其他高级政府官员及军官。中国驻伊拉克大使曹痴和其他国家的代表以及驻这里的外交使节也参加了葬礼。
