Title: Patriotic Armed Forces of the Congo (Léopoldville) Intensify Attacks, Fierce Fighting with White Mercenaries and Puppet Troops Near Stanleyville, Mobutu Puppet Clique Intensifies Troop Deployment to Suppress the People After Puppet Troops are Attacked
Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Léopoldville: Patriotic armed forces in northeastern Congo (Léopoldville) have recently intensified attacks in the areas surrounding Stanleyville.

    It is reported that patriotic armed forces engaged in fierce fighting with puppet troops and white mercenaries near Mokaria, northwest of Stanleyville. Patriotic armed forces also fought with puppet troops in the Bonteville area south of Stanleyville and in other areas.

    News reports say that patriotic armed forces attacked a puppet police post five miles from the center of Stanleyville last month. The Mobutu puppet clique hastily transported one hundred and five police officers from Luluabourg in Kasai Province to reinforce and guard against attacks by patriotic armed forces.

    Other news reports say that since the beginning of last month, after the patriotic armed forces of the Congo (Léopoldville) launched successive attacks on puppet troops in the east and northeast, the Mobutu clique has been carrying out frantic suppression of the people in these areas. They recently arrested many residents in Upper Congo Province (the capital of which is Stanleyville) who sympathize with the patriotic armed forces.

### 刚果（利）爱国武装加强袭击活动  在斯坦利维尔附近同白人雇佣军和伪军激战  蒙博托傀儡集团在伪军遭到进攻后加紧增兵镇压人民

1966-04-11
第3版()
专栏：

    刚果（利）爱国武装加强袭击活动
    在斯坦利维尔附近同白人雇佣军和伪军激战
    蒙博托傀儡集团在伪军遭到进攻后加紧增兵镇压人民
    新华社讯　利奥波德维尔消息：刚果（利）东北部爱国武装最近在斯坦利维尔周围地区加强了袭击活动。
    据报道，爱国武装在斯坦利维尔西北的莫卡里亚附近，同伪军和白人雇佣军进行了激战。在斯坦利维尔以南的邦泰维尔地区和其他一些地区，爱国武装也同伪军进行了战斗。
    消息说，爱国武装上月袭击了距斯坦利维尔市中心五英里的伪警察哨所。蒙博托傀儡集团急忙从开赛省的路路阿堡赶运了一百零五名警察去增援，以防备爱国武装的袭击。
    另有消息说，自从上月初刚果（利）爱国武装在东部和东北部对伪军接连进行出击后，蒙博托集团在这些地区对人民进行疯狂的镇压。他们最近在上刚果省（省会是斯坦利维尔）逮捕了许多同情爱国武装的居民。
