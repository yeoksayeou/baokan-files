Title: Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman, Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th – Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received this afternoon the delegation of young women from the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by Sugita Hidezuko, Vice Chairman of the Chiyoda Ward Branch of the New Japan Women's Association, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Luo Shuzhang, Xu Guangping, Yang Yunyu, and others.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见日本客人

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十五日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长刘宁一，今天下午接见了由新日本妇女会东京都千代田区分会副会长杉田日出子率领的日中友协青年妇女代表团，和她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有罗叔章、许广平、杨蕴玉等。
