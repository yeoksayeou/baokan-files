Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Congratulations to Obote on Assuming the Presidency of Uganda
Author:
Date: 1966-04-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 19th - Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on the 19th to the President of Uganda, Apollo Milton Obote, congratulating him on assuming the presidency. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Apollo Milton Obote, President of Uganda, Kampala:
    On the occasion of Your Excellency's assumption of the presidency of Uganda, we extend our sincere congratulations to Your Excellency. We wish the people of your country happiness. We wish the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Uganda and the friendship between the two peoples will develop day by day.
                    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of

### 刘少奇主席　周恩来总理  电贺奥博特任乌干达总统

1966-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席　周恩来总理
    电贺奥博特任乌干达总统
    新华社十九日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来十九日打电报给乌干达总统阿波洛·密尔顿·奥博特，祝贺他就任总统。电文如下：坎帕拉乌干达总统阿波洛·密尔顿·奥博特阁下：
    在阁下就任乌干达总统的时候，我们向阁下表示诚挚的祝贺。祝贵国人民幸福。祝中乌两国友好合作关系和两国人民的友谊日益发展。
                    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                          一九六六年四月十九日
