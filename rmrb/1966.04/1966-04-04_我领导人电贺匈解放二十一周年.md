Title: Our Leaders Send Telegrams to Congratulate Hungary on the 21st Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd -- Our Party and State leaders today sent telegrams to the Hungarian Party and State leaders, warmly congratulating them on the 21st anniversary of Hungary's liberation.
    The full text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade János Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Comrade István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Comrade Gyula Kállai, Premier of the Hungarian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the 21st anniversary of Hungary's liberation, we extend our warm congratulations to you and to the fraternal Hungarian people.
    We wish the Hungarian people new achievements on the road forward. We wish the friendship and friendly cooperative relations between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples will develop day by day.
    Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China  Mao Zedong
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China   Liu Shaoqi
    Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China   Zhu De
    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China  Zhou Enlai
                April 3, 1966
    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd -- Foreign Minister Chen Yi today sent a telegram to Hungarian Foreign Minister János Péter, warmly congratulating him on the 21st anniversary of Hungary's liberation. The full text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade János Péter, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic, Budapest:
    On the occasion of the 21st anniversary of Hungary's liberation, I extend my sincere congratulations to you. I wish the friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples will continue to develop.
    Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China  Chen Yi
                April 3, 1966

### 我领导人电贺匈解放二十一周年

1966-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我领导人电贺匈解放二十一周年
    新华社三日讯　我国党和国家领导人今天打电报给匈牙利党和国家领导人，热烈祝贺匈牙利解放二十一周年。
    电报全文如下：布达佩斯匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔·亚诺什同志，匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万同志，匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府总理卡拉伊·久洛同志：
    在匈牙利解放二十一周年的时候，我们向你们，向兄弟的匈牙利人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝匈牙利人民在前进的道路上取得新的成就。祝中匈两国人民的友谊和友好合作关系日益发展。中国共产党中央委员会主席　毛泽东中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  朱德中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                一九六六年四月三日
    新华社三日讯　陈毅外交部长今天打电报给匈牙利人民共和国外交部长彼得·亚诺什，热烈祝贺匈牙利解放二十一周年。电报全文如下：布达佩斯匈牙利人民共和国外交部长彼得·亚诺什同志：
    在匈牙利解放二十一周年的时候，谨向你致以衷心的祝贺。祝中匈两国人民的友谊不断发展。
    中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                一九六六年四月三日
