Title: Vietnamese Labor Party Delegation Departs Beijing for Home; Comrades Zhou Enlai and Deng Xiaoping Meet and Host Banquet for the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 15th – The Vietnamese Labor Party delegation led by Comrade Le Duan, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, stopped over in Beijing on the 12th on its way home, and flew to Hanoi by special plane on the 15th. Members of the delegation included Nguyen Duy Trinh, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and Hoang Tung, alternate member of the Central Committee.
    On the 13th, Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, met and hosted a banquet for Comrade Le Duan and all members of the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation.
    On the 14th, all members of the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation paid a friendly visit to the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship People's Commune and visited the Agricultural Exhibition Hall.
    When the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation arrived and departed Beijing, Comrades Deng Xiaoping, Li Xiannian, Kang Sheng, Zhao Yimin, and Wan Li and others greeted and saw them off at the airport.
    Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh, Tran Van Thanh, head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in China, and Nguyen Minh Phuong, deputy head, also greeted and saw them off at the airport.

### 越南劳动党代表团离京回国  周恩来邓小平同志会见并宴请代表团

1966-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党代表团离京回国
    周恩来邓小平同志会见并宴请代表团
    新华社十五日讯　越南劳动党中央委员会第一书记黎笋同志率领的越南劳动党代表团，在回国途中，于十二日到北京逗留，十五日乘专机飞往河内。同行的代表团成员有越南劳动党中央政治局委员阮维桢，中央候补委员黄松等。
    十三日，中共中央副主席周恩来、总书记邓小平会见并宴请了黎笋同志和越南劳动党代表团全体成员。
    十四日，越南劳动党代表团全体成员到中越友好人民公社进行友好访问，并参观了农业展览馆。
    越南劳动党代表团到达和离开北京时，邓小平、李先念、康生、赵毅敏、万里同志等到机场迎接和送行。
    越南驻华大使陈子平、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成和副团长阮明芳等，也到机场迎送。
