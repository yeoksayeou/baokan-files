Title: The 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand Opens; General Secretary Wilcox Delivers Report on Behalf of the National Committee
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-10
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Auckland, April 9th – The 21st National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand opened in Auckland on the evening of the 8th in an extremely warm atmosphere.

    Political Committee member W. McCallum delivered the opening address. He expressed regret that the party's National Chairman, M. H. Williams, was unable to attend due to illness. McCallum emphasized Williams' great contributions to the international working class and the New Zealand working class and wished him a speedy recovery.

    The congress welcomed representatives of fraternal parties with thunderous applause: Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; René Randaxhe, member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of Belgium, and Michel Grandorge, member of the Central Committee of the Party; E. F. Hill, Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), and Vice Chairman C. O'Shea.

    Because the New Zealand government refused to issue visas, the delegation of the Albanian Party of Labor was unable to attend, but the congratulatory message from the Albanian Party of Labor aroused prolonged applause.

    McCallum read out congratulatory messages from the Communist Party of Malaya, the Communist Party of Burma, the Communist Party of Japan, the Communist Party of Ceylon, the Communist Party of Thailand, and the Workers' Party of Korea. When the congratulatory message from the Communist Party of Indonesia was read, everyone stood up and applauded.

    V.J. Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, delivered a report on behalf of the National Committee.

    The report aroused enthusiastic and prolonged applause. After the report, the opening session was adjourned.

### 新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会开幕  威尔科克斯总书记代表全国委员会作报告

1966-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会开幕
    威尔科克斯总书记代表全国委员会作报告
    新华社奥克兰九日电　新西兰共产党第二十一次全国代表大会八日晚上在奥克兰在极热烈的气氛中开幕。
    政治委员会委员韦·麦卡拉致开幕词。他对党的全国主席默·哈·威廉斯因病不能出席表示遗憾。麦卡拉强调威廉斯对国际工人阶级和新西兰工人阶级作出的伟大贡献，并且祝他迅速恢复健康。
    大会以暴风雨般的掌声欢迎兄弟党的代表们——中国共产党中央委员会委员刘宁一，比利时共产党政治局委员雷纳·兰多尔夫和党中央委员会委员米歇尔·格兰多热，澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席爱·弗·希尔和副主席克·奥谢。
    由于新西兰政府拒绝发给签证，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团未能参加，但是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的贺词引起了经久不息的鼓掌。
    麦卡拉宣读了马来亚共产党、缅甸共产党、日本共产党、锡兰共产党、泰国共产党和朝鲜劳动党的贺电。当宣读印度尼西亚共产党的贺词时，全体起立鼓掌。
    新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯代表全国委员会作报告。
    这个报告引起了热烈的经久不息的掌声。报告后，开幕会议宣布结束。
