Title: Japanese Government Provides "Emergency Aid" to Indonesian Right-Wing Military Regime
Author:
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 5

    Tokyo, April 23 (Xinhua) - The Japanese Sato government officially decided on the 21st to provide 900 million yen in "emergency aid" to the Indonesian right-wing military regime.

    It is reported that this "emergency aid," announced shortly after the establishment of the Indonesian right-wing military regime, includes 10,000 tons of rice and 5,000 bales of cotton yarn. These supplies will be shipped to Indonesia before mid-May to solve the serious food and clothing difficulties currently faced by the Indonesian right-wing military regime.

    As previously reported, Japanese Prime Minister Sato instructed Japanese Ambassador to Indonesia Saito on the 18th to inform the Indonesian government that the Japanese government plans to provide Indonesia with "emergency and long-term economic assistance."

### 日本政府向印尼右派军人政权提供“紧急援助”

1966-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    日本政府向印尼右派军人政权提供“紧急援助”
    新华社二十三日讯　东京消息：日本佐藤政府二十一日正式作出了决定，要向印度尼西亚右派军人政权提供九亿日元的“紧急援助”。
    据报道，这项在印度尼西亚右派军人政权刚成立后就宣布了的“紧急援助”，包括一万吨大米和五千捆棉纱。这些物资将在五月中旬以前全部运到印度尼西亚，以便解决印度尼西亚右派军人政权当前面临的严重的粮食和衣着的困难。
    前已报道，日本首相佐藤曾在十八日指示日本驻印度尼西亚大使斋藤通知印度尼西亚政府，日本政府计划向印度尼西亚提供“紧急和长期经济援助”。
