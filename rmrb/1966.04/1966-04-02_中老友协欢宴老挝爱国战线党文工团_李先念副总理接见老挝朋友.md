Title: China-Laos Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Lao Patriotic Front Party Art Troupe; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Lao Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-04-02
Edition: 4

    China-Laos Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Lao Patriotic Front Party Art Troupe
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Lao Friends
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 1st) The China-Laos Friendship Association held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Lao Patriotic Front Party Art Troupe led by Khamma Phoukhong.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the banquet.
    Lian Guan, President of the China-Laos Friendship Association, said in his speech that the Lao Patriotic Front Party Art Troupe's visit to China again after 1964 will further strengthen the fighting solidarity and revolutionary friendship between the people of China and Laos.
    Lian Guan praised the heroic Lao people for achieving great victories in the struggle against U.S. armed intervention and aggression under the leadership of the Lao Patriotic Front Party and Prince Souphanouvong.
    Lian Guan said that the Chinese people will always stand with the Lao people and resolutely support the Lao people's just struggle.
    In his speech, the head of the delegation, Khamma Phoukhong, warmly praised the fighting friendship between the people and literary and art workers of the two countries, Laos and China. He said that the Lao Patriotic Front Party Art Troupe's visit to China again is a manifestation of the further development, closeness, and unbreakable nature of the friendship between the two peoples. He said that Lao revolutionary and national literature and art, under the leadership of the Lao Patriotic Front Party and Prince Souphanouvong, has played a great role in mobilizing the people to fight against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs.
    Khamma Phoukhong said that Laos is Laos for the Lao people. The American robbers must get out of Laos. In this great struggle, Lao literary and art workers will always be singers of victory.
    Also present at the banquet were Yan Jinsheng, Ren Yinglun, Jin Zhao, Wang Yuanfang, and people from the capital's literary and art circles.
    Mrs. Quinim Pholsena, a Lao friend in Beijing, also attended the banquet.
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 1st) Vice Premier Li Xiannian received Khamma Phoukhong, head of the Lao Patriotic Front Party Art Troupe, deputy heads Paseuth Sisanon and Chanthom Inthavong, and all the performers this evening, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Also present at the reception were Lian Guan, Yan Jinsheng, Ren Yinglun, Jin Zhao, and others.
    Mrs. Quinim Pholsena, a Lao friend in Beijing, was also present. (Attached photo)

### 中老友协欢宴老挝爱国战线党文工团  李先念副总理接见老挝朋友

1966-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中老友协欢宴老挝爱国战线党文工团
    李先念副总理接见老挝朋友
    据新华社一日讯　中国老挝友好协会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由坎马·富贡率领的老挝爱国战线党文工团。
    李先念副总理出席了宴会。
    中老友协会长连贯在讲话中说，老挝爱国战线党文工团继一九六四年之后，再次来中国访问，必将进一步加强中老两国人民的战斗团结和革命友谊。
    连贯赞扬说，英雄的老挝人民在老挝爱国战线党和苏发努冯亲王的领导下，在反对美国武装干涉和侵略的斗争中取得了巨大的胜利。
    连贯说，中国人民将永远同老挝人民在一起，坚决支持老挝人民的正义斗争。
    坎马·富贡团长在讲话中热情歌颂了老中两国人民和两国文艺工作者的战斗友谊。他说，老挝爱国战线党文工团再次访问中国，就是两国人民友谊更加发展、更加紧密和牢不可破的表现。他说，老挝革命的、民族的文艺，在老挝爱国战线党和苏发努冯亲王的领导下，在动员人民，反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争中，起了巨大的作用。
    坎马·富贡说，老挝是老挝人民的老挝。美国强盗必须从老挝滚出去。在这个伟大的斗争中，老挝文艺工作者永远是歌唱胜利的歌手。
    出席宴会作陪的，还有颜金生、任映仑、金照、王元方，以及首都文艺界人士。
    在北京的老挝朋友贵宁·奔舍那夫人也出席了宴会。
    新华社一日讯　李先念副总理今晚接见了老挝爱国战线党文工团团长坎马·富贡，副团长巴色·西沙隆和占农·因塔冯，以及全体演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有连贯、颜金生、任映仑、金照等。
    在北京的老挝朋友贵宁·奔舍那夫人也在座。（附图片）
