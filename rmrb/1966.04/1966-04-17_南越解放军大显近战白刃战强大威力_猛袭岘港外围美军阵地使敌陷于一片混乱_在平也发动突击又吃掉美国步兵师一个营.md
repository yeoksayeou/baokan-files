Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Displays Powerful Close-Quarters Combat and Bayonet Skills, Fiercely Assaults US Military Positions on the Outskirts of Da Nang, Throwing the Enemy into Chaos; Launches Surprise Attack in Bình Giã, Annihilating a US Infantry Division Battalion
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Saigon, 16th – Reports from Saigon: On the 16th at dawn, the South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces fiercely attacked several positions within the southwest defensive perimeter of Da Nang, a crucial US base in South Vietnam, inflicting casualties and losses on the enemy.
    According to Western news agencies, these positions, guarded by the US Marine Corps, are only fifteen kilometers from Da Nang.
    Before dawn on the 16th, the Liberation Army heavily bombarded the US military positions with mortars and recoilless rifles for approximately two hours. The intensity and scale of the bombardment was described as "the largest" in the South Vietnamese war. Amidst the enemy's chaos, the Liberation Army also charged into the enemy's positions, engaging them in bayonet combat, inflicting heavy casualties on the US military.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th – According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, on the 11th at noon, the Liberation Army of Bà Rịa Province, South Vietnam, suddenly attacked US-puppet troops and South Korean mercenary troops conducting "mopping-up" operations in the area of Xuân Sơn and Đất Đỏ, east of a highway north of Bình Giã, swiftly and cleanly wiping out a battalion of the US 1st Infantry Division, eliminating several hundred other enemy troops, and capturing a large quantity of weapons and military supplies.
    In this battle, the Liberation Army fought extremely bravely. The soldiers grappled with the enemy, engaging in fierce hand-to-hand combat, fully utilizing the power of close-quarters combat, rendering the US aircraft and artillery completely useless.
    Recently, to protect Strategic Highway 15 from Saigon to Vũng Tàu and to realize their "pacification" scheme, the American bandits dispatched two infantry brigades from the US 1st Infantry Division and the 173rd Airborne Brigade, as well as many battalions of Australian, New Zealand, and South Korean mercenary troops, and South Vietnamese puppet troops, to conduct large-scale "mopping-up" operations in Châu Thành, Long Điền, Đất Đỏ, and Xuyên Mộc counties of Bà Rịa Province. However, the military and civilians of Bà Rịa Province displayed the power of people's war, attacking the enemy everywhere day and night, inflicting significant casualties on them.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th – According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, on the night of April 9th, the Liberation Armed Forces of the Đồng Tháp Mười region, South Vietnam, fiercely attacked the US "Special Forces" camp in Thạnh Phước commune, Tuyên Nhơn district, eliminating most of two commando companies, seizing more than forty various weapons and a large quantity of military supplies.
    The next morning, the Liberation Armed Forces ambushed the enemy's reinforcements, killing more than one hundred more enemy troops, capturing five, and seizing many weapons and ammunition.
    According to another report, prior to this, from March 21st to 23rd, the Liberation Army and guerrillas in this area continuously counterattacked more than five thousand puppet troops "mopping-up" operations against Bình Hòa commune in Mộc Hóa district and Bình Phong Thạnh commune in Tuyên Nhơn district, killing or wounding nearly one hundred and fifty enemy troops, destroying and damaging eight enemy M-113 armored vehicles.

### 南越解放军大显近战白刃战强大威力  猛袭岘港外围美军阵地使敌陷于一片混乱  在平也发动突击又吃掉美国步兵师一个营

1966-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    南越解放军大显近战白刃战强大威力
    猛袭岘港外围美军阵地使敌陷于一片混乱
    在平也发动突击又吃掉美国步兵师一个营
    新华社十六日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量十六日凌晨猛烈袭击美国在南越的重要基地岘港西南防御圈内的一些阵地，使敌人遭到伤亡和损失。
    据西方通讯社报道，这些由美国海军陆战队守卫的阵地距离岘港只有十五公里。
    十六日黎明前，解放军用迫击炮和无后座力炮猛烈轰击美军阵地约两个小时。轰击的猛烈程度和规模是南越战争中“最大的一次”。在敌人一片混乱中，解放军还冲进敌军阵地，同敌人展开白刃战，大量杀伤美军。
    新华社河内十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方巴地省解放军十一日中午突然袭击正在平也北面和附近一条公路东面的春山和登波一带进行“扫荡”的美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军，迅速干净地全歼美国第一步兵师一个营，另外还消灭敌人数百名，并缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    解放军在这次战斗中，打得十分勇猛。战士们揪住敌人，猛打猛杀，充分发挥近战的威力，使美国的飞机大炮完全失去了作用。
    最近，美国强盗为了保卫从西贡到头顿的第十五号战略公路和实现其“绥靖”阴谋，曾经出动了美国第一步兵师的两个步兵旅和第一七三空降旅，以及澳大利亚、新西兰、南朝鲜帮凶军和南越伪军的许多个营，对巴地省的周城、隆田、红土和川木等县进行大规模的“扫荡”。但是巴地省军民发挥人民战争的威力，不分昼夜地到处打击敌人，使敌人遭到了重大伤亡。
    新华社河内十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方同塔梅地区的解放武装力量四月九日夜间猛烈进攻宣仁县盛福乡的美国“特种部队”兵营，消灭了两个别动连的大部分敌人，缴获各种武器四十多件和大批军用物资。
    第二天上午，解放武装力量又截击敌人的援兵，再次打死敌人一百多名，俘虏五名，并且缴获许多武器和弹药。
    另据报道，在这以前，这个地区的解放军和游击队曾在三月二十一日到二十三日的三天中，连续反击五千多名伪军对木化县平和乡和宣仁县平丰盛乡的“扫荡”，共打死打伤敌人近一百五十名，炸毁和击毁敌人M—113型装甲车八辆。
