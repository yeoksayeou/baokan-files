    The Organizing Committee of the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of Public Security Sports Organizations of Socialist Countries Holds Meeting
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 14) The organizing committee for the Seventh Men's Basketball Tournament of Public Security Sports Organizations of Socialist Countries held a meeting this morning to discuss and approve the relevant regulations for the tournament and to draw lots to determine the specific schedule.
    The meeting was chaired by Ma Xingwu, Director of the Tournament Organizing Committee and Deputy Director of the China "Qianwei" Sports Association.  Participants included Nesdet Take, leader of the Albanian Ministry of Internal Affairs "Dinamo" basketball team; Mircho Langlov, leader of the Bulgarian "Spartak" basketball team; Han Qianqiu, leader of the Korean Ministry of Social Security basketball team; Nicola Kiriac, leader of the Romanian Bucharest "Dinamo" Sports Club basketball team; Frantisek Vostri, leader of the Czechoslovakian "Red Star" basketball team; and Long Qian, leader of the Chinese "Qianwei" basketball team. The Polish "Guard" basketball team had not yet arrived, and Dan Gwonbinska, Cultural and Sports Commissioner of the Polish Embassy in China, attended the meeting on their behalf.
    Ma Xingwu, Director of the Organizing Committee, introduced the preparations for the tournament. The leaders and deputy leaders of the various teams unanimously expressed their satisfaction with the preparations.
    The meeting decided that the Organizing Committee would establish a Referee Committee, with Zhang Zhihuai as the Director, Mou Zuoyun and Zhang Changlu as Deputy Directors, and the referees accompanying each team as members.
    The meeting unanimously agreed that the tournament would be held from April 15 to April 25. The seven basketball teams participating in the tournament would adopt a single round-robin system through drawing lots. The top three teams would be determined based on their performance in the competition. At the end of the meeting, lots were drawn to determine the specific competition schedule.

### 社会主义国家公安体育组织  第七届男篮比赛大会组织委员会举行会议

1966-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家公安体育组织
    第七届男篮比赛大会组织委员会举行会议
    据新华社十四日讯　社会主义国家公安体育组织第七届男子篮球比赛大会组织委员会今天上午举行会议，讨论和通过了这次比赛大会的有关规定，并抽签确定了比赛的具体日程。
    会议由大会组织委员会主任委员、中国“前卫”体育协会副主任马星五主持。参加会议的有阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”篮球队领队内斯德特·塔凯，保加利亚“斯巴达克”篮球队领队米尔乔·朗格洛夫，朝鲜社会安全省篮球队领队韩千秋，罗马尼亚布加勒斯特“狄纳莫”体育俱乐部篮球队领队尼古拉·基里亚克，捷克斯洛伐克“红星”篮球队领队弗朗齐歇克·沃斯特里，中国“前卫”篮球队领队龙潜等人。波兰“近卫军”篮球队尚未到达，由波兰驻华大使馆文化体育专员丹·格翁宾斯卡代表出席了会议。
    组织委员会主任委员马星五介绍大会的筹备情况，各代表队领队、副领队对筹备情况一致表示满意。
    会议通过决定：组织委员会下设裁判委员会，主任委员由张之槐担任，副主任委员由牟作云和张长禄担任，委员由各随队裁判员担任。
    会议一致通过：这次比赛大会由四月十五日开始到二十五日结束。参加大会的七个篮球队采取抽签进行单循环比赛的办法。按比赛成绩取前三名。会议最后抽签确定了具体的比赛日程。
