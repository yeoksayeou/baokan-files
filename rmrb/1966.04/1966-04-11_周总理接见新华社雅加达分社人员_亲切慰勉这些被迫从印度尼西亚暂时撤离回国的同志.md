Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Xinhua News Agency Jakarta Branch Personnel, Cordially Comforting These Comrades Forced to Temporarily Withdraw from Indonesia

Author:
Date: 1966-04-11
Edition: 1

    Premier Zhou Enlai met this evening with Zhang Haitao, the head of the Xinhua News Agency's Jakarta branch, and all the reporters and staff who had temporarily withdrawn from Jakarta and returned to Beijing.

    The personnel of the Xinhua News Agency's Jakarta branch returned to China after the Indonesian government unreasonably announced the "temporary closure" of the Xinhua News Agency's Jakarta branch.

    During the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his cordial greetings and encouragement to Comrade Zhang Haitao and others.

    Accompanying the meeting were Hao Deqing, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council; Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Shi Huaibi, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wu Lengxi, President of Xinhua News Agency; Miao Haileng, Mu Qing, and Wang Minzhao, Vice Presidents of Xinhua News Agency; Chen Jun, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; Gu Wenhua, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau; Li Wu and Li Bingquan, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association; and Chang Zhiqing, Mu Xin, Lü Liang, Shi Mai, Ke Zaishuo, and other heads of news organizations in the capital. (Attached picture)

### 周总理接见新华社雅加达分社人员  亲切慰勉这些被迫从印度尼西亚暂时撤离回国的同志

1966-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见新华社雅加达分社人员
    亲切慰勉这些被迫从印度尼西亚暂时撤离回国的同志
    新华社十日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了暂时撤离雅加达回到北京的新华社驻雅加达分社社长张海涛以及全体记者和工作人员。
    新华社驻雅加达分社人员，是在印度尼西亚政府无理宣布“暂时封闭”新华社驻雅加达分社以后回国的。
    在接见的时候，周恩来总理向张海涛等同志表示亲切慰问和勉励。
    陪同接见的，有国务院外事办公室副主任郝德青，外交部副部长韩念龙，对外文委副主任史怀璧，新华社社长吴冷西，副社长缪海棱、穆青、王敏昭，《人民日报》副总编辑陈浚，广播事业局副局长顾文华，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李伍、李炳泉，首都其他各新闻单位的负责人常芝青、穆欣、吕梁、史迈、柯在铄等。（附图片）
    右图：周恩来总理和新华社驻雅加达分社社长张海涛和全体人员合影。                      　新华社记者　吴元柳摄
