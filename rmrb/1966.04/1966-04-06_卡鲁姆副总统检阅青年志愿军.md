Title: Vice President Karume Inspects Youth Volunteer Army
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, April 4th (electric dispatch) - Tanzanian First Vice President Karume inspected the Zanzibar Youth Volunteer Army here on the morning of the 4th.
    Karume, addressing over one hundred volunteers armed with rifles, said that the government's aim is for all people to receive military training. He said, "Tanzania is a sovereign nation and should be defended."
    Karume said that it was gratifying that some Zanzibar women were also receiving military training. He hoped that more people would join the volunteer army.
    In his speech, a leader of the volunteer army assured Karume that the youth volunteer army was determined to serve the motherland. He said, "We will not hesitate to deal with any foreign interference."

### 卡鲁姆副总统检阅青年志愿军

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    卡鲁姆副总统检阅青年志愿军
    新华社桑给巴尔四日电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆四日上午在这里检阅了桑给巴尔青年志愿军。
    卡鲁姆对一百多名手执步枪的志愿军发表讲话说，政府的目的是全体人民都应当受军事训练。他说：“坦桑尼亚是一个主权国家，应当加以保卫。”
    卡鲁姆说，令人高兴的是一些桑给巴尔妇女也受到了军事训练。他希望将有更多的人参加志愿军。
    一位志愿军领导人在讲话时，向卡鲁姆保证青年志愿军决心为祖国服务。他说：“我们将毫不犹疑地对付任何外来干涉。”
