Title: Indira Gandhi Again Uses American Tone to Vigorously Attack China and Pakistan; Has Left the United States to Conduct Activities in London and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1966-04-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from New York: Indian Prime Minister Indira Gandhi, having just concluded talks with U.S. President Johnson, made a series of speeches attacking China and Pakistan before leaving the United States.

    On March 31, speaking at a gathering of the Asia Society Council, she once again attacked "China's expansion" and "threat" to India and other countries.

    Regarding Indo-Pakistani relations, Indira Gandhi slandered Pakistan for "invasion" and reiterated India's intention to continue to霸占 (bàzhàn - forcibly occupy) Kashmir and oppose the self-determination of the Kashmiri people. She said, "It is too late to talk about a plebiscite now." She also said that Kashmir is now "extremely important for India's defense against China" in Ladakh.

    In a speech on the U.S. National Broadcasting Company's "Meet the Press" program on the 1st, she also attacked the friendly relations between China and Pakistan as a "threat" to India.

    Indira Gandhi expressed great sympathy for the Johnson administration's predicament in the war of aggression against Vietnam, saying, "The Americans are now in a difficult situation, and I can now understand their difficulties."

    Mrs. Gandhi left the United States by plane on the afternoon of the 1st for London and Moscow. On March 31, she said that she would meet with Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kosygin in Moscow. It is reported that before leaving the United States, she "discussed the Vietnam War and ways to end the conflict" with United Nations Secretary-General U Thant.

### 甘地夫人又用美国腔调大肆攻击中巴  已离美国前往伦敦和苏联进行活动

1966-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    甘地夫人又用美国腔调大肆攻击中巴
    已离美国前往伦敦和苏联进行活动
    新华社二日讯　纽约消息：刚结束同美国总统约翰逊的会谈的印度总理甘地夫人在离开美国前夕又连续发表谈话，攻击中国和巴基斯坦。
    她三月三十一日在亚洲协会理事会一个集会上讲话时，又一次攻击“中国进行扩张”、“威胁”印度和其他国家。
    谈到印巴关系，英迪拉·甘地诬蔑巴基斯坦“入侵”，并且重申印度要继续霸占克什米尔，反对克什米尔人民自决。她说什么“现在谈论公民投票是太晚了。”她还说，现在，克什米尔对于印度在拉达克“防御中国极为重要”。
    她一日对美国全国广播公司“会见报界”节目发表的讲话中还攻击中国和巴基斯坦的友好关系是对印度的“威胁”。
    英迪拉·甘地对约翰逊政府在侵越战争中的困境表示十分同情，她说：“美国人现在处在困难局面中，我现在能够理解他们的困难”。
    甘地夫人已在一日下午乘飞机离开美国前往伦敦和莫斯科。她在三月三十一日说，她到莫斯科后将会晤苏联部长会议主席柯西金。据报道，在离开美国之前，她同联合国秘书长吴丹“讨论了越南战争以及结束这场冲突的办法”。
