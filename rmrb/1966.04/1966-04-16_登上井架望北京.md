Title: Gazing at Beijing from the Derrick
Author: Qiao Ba
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 6

    Gazing at Beijing from the Derrick
    Qiao Ba, Worker, Yuanzhuang Coal Mine, Huaibei, Anhui

    Steel derrick towers into the clouds,
    Gazing at Beijing from the derrick,
    Thousands of miles of mountains and rivers,
    The more I look, the brighter it becomes.
    Gazing at Beijing in the morning,
    A round of sunrise ascends in the East,
    Red light brilliantly illuminates the mine,
    Above and below the well, all rejoice.
    Gazing at Beijing at night,
    The Big Dipper stars twinkle brightly,
    The radiance melts into the miner's lamp,
    A bright light atop, seizing black gold.
    Beijing, oh Beijing,
    In the city lives our guide;
    Chairman Mao waves and smiles at us,
    Our revolutionary zeal increases tenfold.
    Gazing at Beijing from the derrick,
    Beijing is the revolutionary beacon,
    We follow the Party wholeheartedly,
    Hold high the red flag and march forward!

### 登上井架望北京
乔巴
1966-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    登上井架望北京
    安徽淮北袁庄煤矿工人  乔巴
    钢铁井架耸入云，
    登上井架望北京，
    千里山川万里路，
    越看越光明。
    早晨望北京，
    一轮朝阳东方升，
    红光灿灿照矿山，
    井上井下齐欢腾。
    夜晚望北京，
    北斗星儿亮晶晶，
    光辉溶进矿灯里，
    头顶亮光夺乌金。
    北京城呵北京城，
    城里住着咱引路人；
    毛主席挥手朝咱笑，
    咱革命干劲增十分。
    登上井架望北京，
    北京是革命的航标灯，
    咱一心一意跟党走，
    高举红旗向前进！
