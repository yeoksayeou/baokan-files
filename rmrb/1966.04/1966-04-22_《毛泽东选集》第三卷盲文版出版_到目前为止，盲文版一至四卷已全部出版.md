Title: Braille Edition of Volume III of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published; Braille Editions of Volumes I-IV Now Complete
Author:
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 2

    Braille Edition of Volume III of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published
    To date, Braille editions of Volumes I to IV have all been published.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: The Braille edition of Volume III of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has recently been translated and published by the Braille Monthly Magazine of the China Association for the Blind and Deaf. This volume is divided into seven books. It is being distributed through Xinhua bookstores nationwide starting today. To date, Braille editions of Volumes I, II, III, and IV of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" have all been translated and published.

### 《毛泽东选集》第三卷盲文版出版  到目前为止，盲文版一至四卷已全部出版

1966-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

    《毛泽东选集》第三卷盲文版出版
    到目前为止，盲文版一至四卷已全部出版
    新华社二十一日讯　《毛泽东选集》第三卷盲文版最近已由中国盲人聋哑人协会盲人月刊社翻译出版。本卷共分七册。自即日起在全国各地新华书店发行。到目前为止，《毛泽东选集》盲文版一、二、三、四卷已经全部翻译出版。
