Title: Colombian Students Hold Anti-American Demonstration; Demonstrators Angrily Attack US Consulate with Rocks
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Bogotá, 22nd dispatch: Hundreds of university students in the Colombian capital held an anti-American demonstration in front of the US consulate on the 21st.
    The demonstrators shouted anti-American slogans and attacked the building of the US consulate with rocks.
    It is reported that the students demanded that the authorities release two students who were unreasonably arrested a few days ago and increase educational funding during the demonstration. Due to financial constraints, at least seven institutions of higher learning are currently facing serious economic difficulties.

### 哥伦比亚学生举行反美示威  示威者群情激愤用石块袭击美领事馆

1966-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚学生举行反美示威
    示威者群情激愤用石块袭击美领事馆
    新华社二十二日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚首都上百名大学生二十一日在美国领事馆前面举行反美示威。
    示威者高呼反美口号，用石块袭击了美国领事馆的建筑。
    据报道，学生们在示威中要求当局释放前几天被无理逮捕的两名学生和增加教育经费。由于经费拮据，目前至少有七个高等学校面临着严重的经济困难。
