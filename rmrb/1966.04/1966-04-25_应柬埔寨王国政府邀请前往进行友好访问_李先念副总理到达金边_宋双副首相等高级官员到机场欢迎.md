Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Arrives in Phnom Penh for a Friendly Visit at the Invitation of the Royal Government of Cambodia; Deputy Prime Minister Son Sann and Other Senior Officials Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 24th – At the invitation of the Royal Government of Cambodia, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian, arrived in Phnom Penh by special plane on the morning of the 24th for a friendly visit.  Accompanying him was Wang Daohan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Cambodian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Economic Affairs, Finance, and Planning Coordination, Son Sann, other senior Cambodian officials, representatives of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League, and representatives of overseas Chinese.
    Today, the Cambodian and Chinese national flags were flying at Pochentong Airport.  The special plane arrived at 10:30 a.m.  As Vice Premier Li Xiannian stepped out of the plane, Deputy Prime Minister Son Sann immediately stepped forward to greet him.  They warmly shook hands and embraced.  Three Cambodian girls presented flowers to Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Chairman Wang Daohan.
    Accompanied by Deputy Prime Minister Son Sann, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Chairman Wang Daohan reviewed the guard of honor and warmly shook hands with the Cambodian officials who had come to the airport to welcome them. Members of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League waved Cambodian and Chinese national flags and repeatedly chanted in unison, "Long live Cambodian-Chinese friendship!"
    Also present at the airport to welcome them were: Cambodian Minister of Industry, Phlek Chhat; Minister of Commerce, So Nem; Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Nouth Chhayly; Director General of the National Bank, Touch Kim; and Deputy Director General of the National Bank, Chea Thor.  Cambodian Ambassador to China, Truong Cang, also came to the airport to welcome them.
    Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Chen Shuliang, and his wife, staff members of the Chinese Embassy, and representatives of Chinese experts were also present to welcome them. Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kang Hak-mo, also came to welcome them.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th – Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Xiannian, left Beijing by special plane on the 21st to pay a friendly visit to Cambodia at the invitation of the Royal Government of Cambodia. Accompanying him was Wang Daohan, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Premiers Bo Yibo and Xie Fuzhi, as well as Lin Haiyun, Han Nianlong, Yang Lin, Shen Tu, and other responsible persons of relevant departments. Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Cambodian Embassy in China, Ly Khim, also went to the airport to see them off.

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 24th – Vice Premier Li Xiannian left Guangzhou by plane today for Phnom Penh.
    When Vice Premier Li Xiannian left Guangzhou, Chen Yu, Third Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC Central South Bureau, Zhao Ziyang, First Secretary of the CPC Guangdong Provincial Committee, and others, went to the airport to see him off. Kim Lun, General Manager of the Royal Air Cambodge, who had come specially from Cambodia to welcome Vice Premier Li Xiannian, accompanied Vice Premier Li Xiannian and others to Phnom Penh.

### 应柬埔寨王国政府邀请前往进行友好访问  李先念副总理到达金边  宋双副首相等高级官员到机场欢迎

1966-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应柬埔寨王国政府邀请前往进行友好访问
    李先念副总理到达金边
    宋双副首相等高级官员到机场欢迎
    新华社金边二十四日电　中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念应柬埔寨王国政府的邀请，二十四日上午乘专机到达金边，进行友好访问。随同访问的有中国对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵。他们在机场上受到柬埔寨副首相兼经济事务、财政和计划统筹大臣宋双、柬埔寨其他高级官员和高棉王家社会主义青年联盟以及华侨代表的热烈欢迎。
    今天，在坡成东机场上悬挂着柬、中两国国旗。专机于十点半到达，当李先念副总理走出机舱时，宋双副首相立即迎上前去，他们热烈握手和拥抱。三名柬埔寨少女向李先念副总理和汪道涵副主任献了鲜花。
    李先念副总理和汪道涵副主任在宋双副首相的陪同下检阅了仪仗队，并且同前来机场欢迎他们的柬埔寨官员亲切握手。高棉王家社会主义青年联盟盟员们挥舞着柬中两国国旗，不断地齐声欢呼：“柬中友谊万岁！”
    到机场欢迎的还有：柬埔寨工业大臣蒲烈恰，商业大臣苏南，外交部秘书长阮柴里，国家银行总监笃金，国家银行副行长谢托。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也到机场欢迎。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮和夫人，中国大使馆人员和中国专家代表也前往欢迎。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使姜卨模也前往欢迎。
    新华社二十四日讯　国务院副总理李先念，应柬埔寨王国政府邀请前往柬埔寨进行友好访问，于二十一日乘专机离开北京。随同访问的有对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵等。
    前往机场欢送的，有薄一波、谢富治副总理，以及林海云、韩念龙、杨琳、沈图等有关方面的负责人。柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办李军也到机场欢送。
    新华社广州二十四日电　李先念副总理今天乘飞机离开广州前往金边。
    李先念副总理在离开广州的时候，中共中央中南局第三书记陈郁，中共广东省委第一书记赵紫阳等，到机场欢送。专程从柬埔寨前来迎接李先念副总理的柬埔寨王家航空公司总经理金伦，陪同李先念副总理等一起前往金边。
