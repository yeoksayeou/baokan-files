Title: British Labour Party Forms New Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-07
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, London, April 5th, the British Labour Party, which returned to power in the general election on March 31st, formed a new cabinet on the 5th.
    According to Prime Minister Wilson's announcement, the main members of the new cabinet remain unchanged, including: First Secretary of State and Secretary of State for Economic Affairs Brown, Foreign Secretary Stewart, Defence Secretary Healey, Chancellor of the Exchequer Callaghan, President of the Board of Trade Jay, Secretary of State for Commonwealth Relations Bottomley. It is announced that the final result of the British general election on March 31st is: the Labour Party won 363 seats in the 630-seat House of Commons, the Conservative Party 253 seats, the Liberal Party 12 seats, the Northern Ireland Labour Party 1 seat, and another seat is held by the Speaker without voting rights.

### 英国工党组成新内阁

1966-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    英国工党组成新内阁
    据新华社伦敦五日电　在三月三十一日大选中再次上台的英国工党五日组成新内阁。
    据首相威尔逊宣布，新内阁的主要成员没有变动，其中包括：首席国务大臣兼经济事务大臣布朗，外交大臣斯图尔特，国防大臣希利，财政大臣卡拉汉，贸易大臣杰伊，联邦关系大臣博顿利。据宣布，英国三月三十一日大选的最后结果是：工党在下院六百三十个席位中获得三百六十三席，保守党二百五十三席，自由党十二席，北爱尔兰共和国工党一席，另一席为没有表决权的议长占有。
