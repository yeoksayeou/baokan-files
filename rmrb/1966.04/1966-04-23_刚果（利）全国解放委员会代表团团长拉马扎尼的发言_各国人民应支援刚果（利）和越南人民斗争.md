Title: Ramazani, Head of the Delegation of the National Liberation Council of the Congo (L): People of All Countries Should Support the Struggle of the People of the Congo (L) and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-04-23
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Sébastien Ramazani, head of the delegation of the National Liberation Council of the Congo (L), in his speech at the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association on the morning of the 21st, strongly condemned the monstrous crimes of U.S. imperialism in massacring unarmed residents in the Congo (L) and Vietnam. He also exposed the U.S. imperialism's attempt, under worldwide condemnation, to make public opinion believe that it has abandoned aggression, and even shamelessly promote the hypocrisy of the peace it envisions.
    He said: The massacre of our residents of Stanleyville by the American criminal army, with the assistance of their British and Belgian accomplices, is a brutal crime committed against these residents, which is enough for any observer to understand the nature of a government like the Johnson administration in the 20th century. After committing such shameful crimes in the Congo, the United States, far from stopping under the strong protests of the people of all countries in the world, has continued to send batches of additional troops and combat equipment to strengthen its military forces in the Congo. Kitona and Kamina have become important American bases in the Congo today.
    He pointed out that the sole aim of the United States is to exterminate the Congolese people in order to rule it. He said: The series of coups in Africa… especially the coup in Ghana, fully demonstrate the various crimes of the imperialist alliance led by the United States in Africa, and prove that imperialism is controlling this continent.
    Ramazani said: All the people of our country, who have taken up arms under the leadership of the National Liberation Council, are determined to frustrate all these evil and insane goals of the United States.
    He said: Generally speaking, the current serious situation is characterized by many provocations by U.S. imperialism against other Asian and African countries, especially the repeated provocations by U.S. imperialism against the People's Republic of China and Cambodia, armed aggression against Laos, conspiracy in Indonesia and Rwanda, subversion in the Congo (Brazzaville) and Yemen, and opposition to the Palestinian people.
    Ramazani condemned the crimes of U.S. imperialism in Vietnam, saying: How can people naively believe the slogans of peaceful settlement that the Washington government is publicizing? At the same time, in addition to escalating the war in North Vietnam and wantonly bombing hospitals, kindergartens, agricultural equipment, and other industries, the United States is also spreading death around the world and causing great suffering to the people of all countries.
    He said: Regarding giving moral support to the Vietnamese people, the delegation of the National Liberation Council of the Congo (L) hopes to see this meeting of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association strengthen its mission of conducting propaganda on these two continents. Regarding the issue of solidarity with this brotherly people, the delegation of the Congo (L) hopes that this solidarity should be manifested in organizing a series of demonstrations by the masses of the people of Asian and African countries.
    Ramazani finally said that regarding the current struggle of the Congolese people, the delegation of the National Liberation Council requests that this meeting prepare to carry out extensive and regular propaganda on the activities of the Congolese patriots, and requests the Afro-Asian Journalists Association to help distribute the appeal issued by the International Committee for Aid and Support to the Congo, calling on the people of Asian and African countries to support and provide moral and material support to the noble and just struggle of the Congolese people.

### 刚果（利）全国解放委员会代表团团长拉马扎尼的发言  各国人民应支援刚果（利）和越南人民斗争

1966-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    刚果（利）全国解放委员会代表团团长拉马扎尼的发言
    各国人民应支援刚果（利）和越南人民斗争
    新华社二十二日讯　刚果（利）全国解放委员会代表团团长塞巴斯蒂安·拉马扎尼，二十一日上午在亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）上的发言中，强烈谴责美帝国主义在刚果（利）和越南屠杀手无寸铁的居民的滔天罪行，并且揭露美帝国主义在受到全世界谴责的情况下，企图使舆论相信它已放弃侵略，甚至无耻地宣扬他们所设想的和平的虚伪态度。
    他说：美国罪恶军队在它们的英国和比利时帮凶的协助下屠杀我们的斯坦利维尔居民，这是对这些居民犯下的残酷的罪行，这足以使任何观察者都可以了解在二十世纪时代象约翰逊政府这样的一个政府的性质。在刚果犯下如此可耻的罪行之后，美国不但没有在全世界各国人民的强烈抗议下住手，反而继续派出一批批的另外的军队和作战装备，来加强它在刚果的军事部队。基托纳和加米纳在今天已成为美国在刚果的重要基地。
    他指出，美国的唯一目的是灭绝刚果人民以便统治它。他说：在非洲的一系列政变……特别是加纳的政变，充分表明在美国领导下的帝国主义联合在非洲的各种罪行，并证明帝国主义正在控制这个大陆。
    拉马扎尼说：在全国解放委员会领导下拿起武器的我国全体人民，坚定不移地决心使美国的所有这些罪恶和疯狂的目的无法得逞。
    他说：总的说来，目前的严重局势的特点是美帝国主义对其他亚非国家进行了许多挑衅，特别是美国帝国主义多次对中华人民共和国和柬埔寨进行了挑衅、武装侵略老挝、在印度尼西亚和卢旺达制造阴谋、在刚果（布）和也门进行颠覆活动以及反对巴勒斯坦人民。
    拉马扎尼谴责美帝国主义在越南的罪行时说：人们怎么能够天真地相信华盛顿政府大肆宣扬的和平解决的口号？就在此同时，除了在越南北方搞战争升级，大肆轰炸医院、幼儿园、农业设备和其他的工业之外，美国还在世界各地散布死亡，和使各国人民遭受巨大苦难。
    他说：关于给越南人民以道义支持，刚果（利）全国解放委员会代表团希望看到亚非新闻工作者协会的这次会议加强对这两大洲进行宣传的使命。关于声援这个兄弟人民的问题，刚果（利）代表团希望，这种声援应该表现为组织亚非国家人民群众的一系列的示威。
    拉马扎尼最后说，对于刚果人民目前的斗争的问题，全国解放委员会代表团请求现在这次会议准备对刚果爱国者的活动进行广泛的和经常性的宣传，并且请求亚非新闻工作者协会帮助散发援助和支持刚果国际委员会发出的呼吁书，号召亚非各国人民声援并从道义和物质上支援刚果人民的崇高和正义的斗争。
