Title: We Rivet Bridges for the Revolution
Author: Tang Zhaodi
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 6

    We Rivet Bridges for the Revolution 	Jinan Locomotive Factory Tang Zhaodi
    Across ten thousand rivers, over a thousand mountains,
    We wield rivet guns,
    Marching north and south,
    Riveting tens of thousands of bridges,
    Rainbows soaring into the sky!
    Hey! The more we rivet, the more energetic we become,
    A pat on the chest, a resounding thump:
    Motherland,
    How many bridges
    Will the Third Five-Year Plan need?
    Place the heavy burden of revolution on us,
    Rivet workers all have iron shoulders!
    We've riveted the pillars of the Yangtze River Bridge,
    The beams of the Yellow River Bridge!
    We've seen the dragon whirlwinds of the Qinling Mountains,
    The sky-high waves of Pukou!
    Standing at the bridgehead ten zhang high,
    Waves crashing—heart doesn't skip a beat,
    Wind shaking—feet don't wobble!
    The furnace fire tempers our steel will,
    We rivet bridges for the revolution.
    Rivet! Rivet tight the rows of red rivets,
    Rivet on each and every red heart!
    Rivet! Rivet out jade roads and golden bridges,
    Rivet firm iron walls and bronze ramparts!
    We rivet workers, ah,
    Our ambition lies beyond a thousand li,
    Our red hearts face the Party,
    A rivet gun makes revolution,
    Rain or shine, we won't leave our posts!
    Want to know why our energy is so fierce?
    —Fighting on the bridge,
    Seeing the flames of Vietnam...

**Named Entities**

济南机车工厂	Jinan Locomotive Factory
长江大桥	Yangtze River Bridge
黄河大桥	Yellow River Bridge
秦岭	Qinling Mountains
浦口	Pukou
第三个五年计划	Third Five-Year Plan
越南	Vietnam

### 咱为革命铆大桥
唐兆迪
1966-04-22
第6版()
专栏：

    咱为革命铆大桥 	济南机车工厂　唐兆迪
    跨万水，越千山，
    咱手舞铆枪，
    南征北战，
    铆起大桥千万座，
    道道彩虹飞天上！
    嘿！咱越铆越起劲，
    胸脯一拍咚咚响：
    祖国呀，
    第三个五年计划，
    要架多少桥梁？
    革命重担尽管放，
    铆工都是铁肩膀！
    咱铆过长江大桥的柱，
    黄河大桥的梁！
    咱见过秦岭的龙旋风，
    浦口的拍天浪！
    战在十丈桥头，
    浪打——心不跳，
    风摇——脚不晃！
    炉火炼红钢铁志，
    咱为革命铆桥梁。
    铆！把排排红钉铆紧，
    把颗颗红心铆上！
    铆！铆出玉路金桥，
    铆牢铁壁铜墙！
    咱铆工呵，
    志在千里外，
    红心向着党，
    一杆铆枪闹革命，
    风里雨里不下岗！
    要问劲头咋这猛？
    ——身在桥上战斗，
    望见越南火光……
