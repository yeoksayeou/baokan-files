Title: Chairman Liu Sends Condolences on the Death of Iraqi President Arif
Author:
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 15th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to Iraqi Acting President Bazzaz on the 15th, expressing deep condolences on the unfortunate death of Iraqi President Arif in a plane crash. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Abdul Rahman Bazzaz, Acting President of the Republic of Iraq, Baghdad:
    I was shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of His Excellency Abdul Salam Mohammed Arif, President of the Republic of Iraq

### 刘主席电唁伊拉克总统阿里夫逝世

1966-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电唁伊拉克总统阿里夫逝世
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇十五日打电报给伊拉克代总统巴扎兹，对伊拉克总统阿里夫因飞机失事不幸逝世表示深切的哀悼。电文如下：巴格达伊拉克共和国代总统阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·巴扎兹阁下：
    惊悉伊拉克共和国总统阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·穆罕默德·阿里夫阁下因飞机失事不幸逝世，谨向阁下表示深切的哀悼。
            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                      一九六六年四月十五日
