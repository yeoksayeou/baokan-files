Title: Burmese Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-29
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – The Burmese Trade Delegation, invited to attend the Guangzhou Chinese Export Commodities Fair and to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight from Guangzhou. The delegation was headed by Lieutenant Colonel U Maung Maung, Manager of the Burma Export-Import Corporation.
    Li Xinong and other responsible persons from relevant departments went to the airport to welcome them.
    The Burmese Ambassador to China, Thakin Saw Maung Dwah Sinwa Nawng, and other diplomatic officials also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 缅甸贸易代表团到京

1966-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸贸易代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应邀参加广州中国出口商品交易会和前来我国访问的缅甸贸易代表团在团长、缅玛出进口公司经理翁貌中校率领下，今天晚上由广州乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有李新农等有关部门负责人。
    缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙和其他外交官员也到机场迎接。
