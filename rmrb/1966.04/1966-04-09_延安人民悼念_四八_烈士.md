Title: Yan'an People Commemorate the "April 8th" Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, April 8th -- Today is the 20th anniversary of the martyrdom of Comrades Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Ye Ting, Deng Fa, and Mr. Huang Qisheng. Since April 5th, more than 5,000 people from all walks of life in Yan'an have gone to the Yan'an Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery to mourn the "April 8th" martyrs.
    Many people also went to the tomb of Comrade Zhang Side to mourn this Communist Party member who sacrificed himself for the interests of the people. Comrade Zhang Side's tomb was moved to the Yan'an Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery last year.

### 延安人民悼念“四八”烈士

1966-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

    延安人民悼念“四八”烈士
    据新华社延安八日电　今天是王若飞、秦邦宪、叶挺、邓发等同志和黄齐生先生殉难二十周年纪念日。从四月五日以来，延安各界已有五千多人到延安革命烈士陵园悼念“四八”烈士。
    许多人还到张思德同志墓前悼念这位为人民利益而牺牲的共产党员。张思德同志的墓是在去年迁入延安革命烈士陵园的。
