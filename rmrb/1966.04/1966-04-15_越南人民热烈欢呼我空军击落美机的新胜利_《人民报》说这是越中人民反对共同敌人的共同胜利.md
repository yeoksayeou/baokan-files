Title: Vietnamese People Warmly Hail the New Victory of Our Air Force in Shooting Down an American Plane; "People's Daily" Says This Is a Common Victory of the Vietnamese and Chinese People Against a Common Enemy

Author:
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 3

    The Vietnamese People Warmly Hail the New Victory of Our Air Force in Shooting Down an American Plane
    "People's Daily" Says This Is a Common Victory of the Vietnamese and Chinese People Against a Common Enemy
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 14th (Electric Dispatch) – The Vietnamese "People's Daily" published a commentary today, warmly hailing the new victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force units in shooting down an American imperialist A-3B heavy attack aircraft over the Leizhou Peninsula area of Guangdong Province on the 12th. The commentary said, "This is undeniable new evidence of the American imperialist policy of provocation against the People's Republic of China."
    The commentary said that in the past seven years, since the Chinese government issued its first serious warning to the United States, American imperialism has continuously dispatched aircraft and warships to violate China's airspace and territorial waters, and has repeatedly attacked Chinese fishing boats and merchant ships, trampling on international law. These increasingly rampant acts of robbery in terms of degree and scale are premeditated actions by American imperialism and its lackeys, and are part of their conspiracy to provoke, sabotage, encircle, and prepare for a war of aggression against China.
    The commentary said that the Vietnamese people warmly hail the revolutionary vigilance and readiness for combat of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, and warmly hail the new military achievements of the Chinese Air Force.
    The commentary said that this is also a common victory of the Vietnamese and Chinese peoples against the common enemy—the American imperialist aggressors.

### 越南人民热烈欢呼我空军击落美机的新胜利  《人民报》说这是越中人民反对共同敌人的共同胜利

1966-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    越南人民热烈欢呼我空军击落美机的新胜利
    《人民报》说这是越中人民反对共同敌人的共同胜利
    新华社河内十四日电  越南《人民报》今天发表评论，热烈欢呼中国人民解放军空军部队十二日在广东省雷州半岛地区上空击落美帝国主义A—3B重型攻击机一架的新胜利。评论说，“这是美帝国主义对中华人民共和国的挑衅政策的不可抵赖的新证据”。
    评论说，在过去的七年中，从中国政府向美国提出第一次严重警告之日起，美帝国主义不断派遣飞机和军舰侵犯中国的领空和领海，多次袭击中国的渔船和商船，践踏了国际法。这些在程度和规模上日益增加的强盗行动，是美帝国主义及其走狗的预谋行动，是它们对中国进行挑衅、破坏、包围和准备对中国进行侵略战争的阴谋的一部分。
    评论说，越南人民热烈欢呼英雄的中国人民解放军的革命警惕性和随时准备战斗的精神，热烈欢呼中国空军的新战功。
    评论说，这也是越中两国人民反对共同的敌人——美帝国主义侵略者的共同胜利。
