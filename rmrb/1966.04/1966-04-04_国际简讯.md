　　International Briefs

　　U.S. Conducts Another Nuclear Weapons Test
　　Washington dispatch: The U.S. Atomic Energy Commission announced that the United States conducted another underground nuclear weapons test at the Nevada Test Site on the 1st.
　　This is the tenth nuclear weapons test officially announced by the United States this year, and the sixty-seventh nuclear weapons test conducted by the United States since the signing of the Moscow Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty in August 1963.

　　South Korean Park Chung-hee Clique Expands Military
　　Seoul dispatch: South Korean puppet Minister of National Defense Kim Sung-eun announced on March 31 that South Korea will newly establish an army division and a marine brigade, and these puppet troops will be armed with equipment soon to be shipped from the United States.
　　In order to strengthen the South Korean puppet army, U.S. imperialism has handed over dozens of M-48 tanks and 105mm howitzers, one hundred heavy gun carriers and military vehicles, and one hundred and twenty river-crossing vessels to the Park Chung-hee clique in the past three months.

　　Turkish Parliament Elects New President
　　Ankara dispatch: The Turkish Grand National Assembly elected Cevdet Sunay, nominated by the ruling Justice Party, etc., as the new President of Turkey on the 28th, to replace Cevdet Sunay, who is seriously ill.
　　The position of Chief of General Staff of the Turkish Armed Forces, originally held by Sunay, has been taken over by Tural, Commander of the Ground Forces.

　　South African Authorities Create Election Farce
　　Pretoria dispatch: The South African racist regime staged a so-called House of Assembly "election" in South Africa on March 30. The vast majority of South Africa's population, 14 million Africans and other non-whites, were completely deprived of the right to vote.
　　According to the announced "election" results, the ruling National Party won 126 of the total 170 seats, and the remaining seats were also occupied by whites.

　　A Batch of British Troops Withdraw from Libya
　　Cairo dispatch: The last batch of British troops stationed in the western Tripoli region of Libya was forced to withdraw on the 31st. So far, according to the agreement reached between the Libyan and British governments in May last year, the 1,200 British troops to be withdrawn in the first phase have been completely withdrawn.

### 国际简讯

1966-04-04
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    美国又进行核武器试验
    华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国又于一日在内华达试验场进行了一次地下核武器试验。
    这是今年以来美国官方宣布的第十次核武器试验，也是一九六三年八月莫斯科部分禁止核试验条约签订以来美国的第六十七次核武器试验。
    南朝鲜朴正熙集团扩军
    汉城消息：南朝鲜伪国防部长官金圣恩三月三十一日宣布，南朝鲜将新成立一个陆军师和一个海军陆战队旅，这些伪军将用不久从美国运来的装备武装起来。
    为了加强南朝鲜伪军，美帝国主义在过去三个月里交给了朴正熙集团几十辆M—48型坦克和一○五毫米榴弹炮，一百辆重型炮车和军用车辆，以及一百二十艘渡河船只。
    土耳其议会选举新总统
    安卡拉消息：土耳其大国民议会二十八日选举由执政的正义党等提名的杰夫德特·苏奈为土耳其新总统，以代替患重病的古尔塞勒。
    苏奈原来担任的土耳其武装部队总参谋长职务已由地面部队总司令图拉勒担任。
    南非当局制造选举骗局
    比勒陀利亚消息：南非种族主义政权三月三十日在南非导演了所谓众议院“选举”。占南非人口绝大多数的一千四百万非洲人和其他非白人完全被剥夺了选举权利。
    据宣布的“选举”结果，执政党国民党获得全部一百七十席中的一百二十六席，其余议席也都为白人所占有。
    一批英军队撤出利比亚
    开罗消息：英国驻在利比亚的黎波里西部地区的最后一批军队在三十一日被迫撤走。至此，根据利比亚和英国政府去年五月达成的协议规定，在第一阶段要撤走的一千二百名英军已完全撤走。
