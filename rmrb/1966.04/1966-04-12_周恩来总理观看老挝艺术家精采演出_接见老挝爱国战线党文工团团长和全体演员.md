Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Watches Splendid Performance by Lao Artists; Receives Head and All Performers of the Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe
Author:
Date: 1966-04-12
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th.  Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, watched the performance of the Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe tonight.

    The Lao artists' performances of revolutionary and ethnic songs and dances such as the "Triumphal Dance," the mixed chorus "Khatun," and the performance of Chinese revolutionary songs, won warm applause from the audience.

    After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai went onto the stage, warmly shook hands with the Lao artists, congratulated them on their successful performance, and took a group photo with them.

    Also present at tonight's performance were Wu Yuzhang, Lian Guan, Ding Xilin, Song Yiping, Yang Chun, Xu Pingyu, Lü Ji, Ding Bo and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th.  Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, received this evening Khamman Phoukhong, head of the Lao Patriotic Front Arts Troupe, deputy heads Paset Sisalon and Chamnong Inthavong, and all the performers, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.

    Present at the reception were Lian Guan, Ding Xilin, Song Yiping, Yang Chun, Xu Pingyu, and others.

### 周恩来总理观看老挝艺术家精采演出  接见老挝爱国战线党文工团团长和全体演员

1966-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理观看老挝艺术家精采演出
    接见老挝爱国战线党文工团团长和全体演员
    新华社十一日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，今晚观看了老挝爱国战线党文工团的演出。
    老挝艺术家演出的具有革命内容和民族特色的“凯旋舞”、混声合唱《卡通》等歌舞节目，以及演唱的中国革命歌曲，博得了全场热烈的掌声。
    演出结束以后，周恩来总理走上舞台，和老挝艺术家亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功，并且合影留念。
    观看今晚演出的，还有吴玉章、连贯、丁西林、宋一平、杨纯、徐平羽、吕骥、丁波等有关方面负责人。
    新华社十一日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，今晚接见了老挝爱国战线党文工团团长坎马·富贡，副团长巴色·西沙隆、占农·因塔冯和全体演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在场的有连贯、丁西林、宋一平、杨纯、徐平羽等。
