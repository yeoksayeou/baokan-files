Title: Chinese Red Cross President Sends Condolences to Soviet Earthquake Victims
Author:
Date: 1966-04-28
Edition: 4

    Chinese Red Cross President Sends Condolences to Soviet Earthquake Victims
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 27th) Qian Xinzhong, President of the Chinese Red Cross, today sent a telegram to Professor Mityerev, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, expressing deep condolences for the recent earthquakes in the Greater Caucasus Mountains and Azerbaijan in the Soviet Union. The telegram stated, "We learned from news reports about the earthquakes in the Greater Caucasus Mountains and Azerbaijan. On behalf of the Chinese Red Cross and the Chinese people, I extend my deepest condolences to your association, and through your association, to the Soviet people. We wish the people in the earthquake-stricken areas a speedy return to normal life."
    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 26th) According to a TASS report, a strong earthquake occurred in Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan in the Soviet Union, at 5:23 am local time on April 26th, with an intensity of 7.5 degrees. This is the largest earthquake to occur in Tashkent since 1868.
    According to preliminary reports, the earthquake caused casualties and damaged many buildings.

### 我红十字会会长电慰苏联地震灾民

1966-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我红十字会会长电慰苏联地震灾民
    据新华社二十七日讯　中国红十字会会长钱信忠今天打电报给苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会主席米捷列夫教授，对最近在苏联大高加索山脉和阿塞拜疆发生地震表示深切的问候。电报说，“从新闻报道中获悉在大高加索山脉和阿塞拜疆发生地震。谨代表中国红十字会和中国人民向贵会，并通过贵会向苏联人民表示最深切的慰问。祝地震地区受灾人民早日恢复正常生活。”
    据新华社莫斯科二十六日电据塔斯社报道，苏联乌兹别克首都塔什干在四月二十六日当地时间五点二十三分发生了一次强烈地震，烈度为七度半。这是从一八六八年以来在塔什干发生的最大的一次地震。
    据初步报道，这次地震引起了人的伤亡，许多建筑物遭到破坏。
