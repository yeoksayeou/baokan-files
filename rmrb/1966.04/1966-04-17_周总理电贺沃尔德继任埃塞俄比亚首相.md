Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to Walda on Re-election as Prime Minister of Ethiopia
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 2

    Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to Walda on Re-election as Prime Minister of Ethiopia
    Xinhua News Agency, April 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a cable on April 16th to the Prime Minister of the Ethiopian Empire, Aklilu Habte-Wold, congratulating him on his re-appointment as Prime Minister of the Ethiopian Empire. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Excellency Aklilu Habte-Wold, Prime Minister of the Ethiopian Empire, Addis Ababa:

    On the occasion of Your Excellency's re-appointment as Prime Minister of the Ethiopian Empire, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency on behalf of the Chinese Government and in my own name. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness. May the friendship between the people of China and Ethiopia grow stronger day by day.

      Zhou Enlai
      Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                  April 16, 1966

### 周总理电贺沃尔德继任埃塞俄比亚首相

1966-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺沃尔德继任埃塞俄比亚首相
    新华社十六日讯　周恩来总理十六日打电报给埃塞俄比亚帝国首相阿克利卢·哈普特·沃尔德，祝贺他重新被任命为埃塞俄比亚帝国首相。电文如下：亚的斯亚贝巴埃塞俄比亚帝国首相阿克利卢·哈普特·沃尔德阁下：
    在阁下重新被任命为埃塞俄比亚帝国首相的时候，我代表中国政府，并以我个人的名义，向阁下表示热烈祝贺。祝贵国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。祝中埃两国人民的友谊日益发展。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                  一九六六年四月十六日
