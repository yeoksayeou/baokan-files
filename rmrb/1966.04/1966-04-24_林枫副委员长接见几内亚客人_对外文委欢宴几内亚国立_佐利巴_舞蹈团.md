Title: Vice Chairman Lin Feng Receives Guinean Guests; Foreign Cultural Commission Hosts Banquet for Guinean National "Djoliba" Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. Vice Chairman Lin Feng received Sissoko Amadou, the General Director of the Guinean National "Djoliba" Dance Ensemble, and his wife, Deputy Director Kouyate Sory Kandia, and his wife, as well as the main members of the dance ensemble this evening, and held cordial and friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Song Yiping, Xu Pingyu, Zhang Tiesheng, Hou Yefeng, Ding Bo, and others. Also present were the wife of Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Sekou, and embassy attaché Kouyate Balala.
    The Guinean National "Djoliba" Dance Ensemble arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 22nd.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. The Foreign Cultural Commission held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Guinean National "Djoliba" Dance Ensemble. Vice Chairman Lin Feng attended the banquet.
    Vice Director of the Foreign Cultural Commission, Song Yiping, and the General Director of the Guinean National "Djoliba" Dance Ensemble, Sissoko Amadou, spoke at the banquet successively. The wife of the Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Sekou, and embassy attaché Kouyate Balala, were invited to attend the banquet.

### 林枫副委员长接见几内亚客人  对外文委欢宴几内亚国立“佐利巴”舞蹈团

1966-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长接见几内亚客人
    对外文委欢宴几内亚国立“佐利巴”舞蹈团
    新华社二十三日讯　林枫副委员长今天晚上接见了几内亚国立“佐利巴”舞蹈团总团长西索柯·阿马杜和夫人，副总团长库雅特·索里·康迪亚和夫人，以及舞蹈团主要成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有宋一平、徐平羽、张铁生、侯野烽、丁波等。几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古的夫人和使馆随员库亚特·巴拉也在座。
    几内亚国立“佐利巴”舞蹈团是在二十二日下午到京的。
    据新华社二十三日讯　对外文委今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎几内亚国立“佐利巴”舞蹈团。林枫副委员长出席了宴会。
    对外文委副主任宋一平和几内亚国立“佐利巴”舞蹈团总团长西索柯·阿马杜先后在宴会上讲话。几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古的夫人和使馆随员库亚特·巴拉应邀出席了宴会。
