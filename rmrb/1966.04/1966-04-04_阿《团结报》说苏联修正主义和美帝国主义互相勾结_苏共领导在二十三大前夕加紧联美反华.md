Title: 阿《团结报》说苏联修正主义和美帝国主义互相勾结 苏共领导在二十三大前夕加紧联美反华 / Albanian "Bashkimi" Says Soviet Revisionism and U.S. Imperialism Collude; Soviet Leaders Intensify Alliance with U.S. Against China on Eve of 23rd CPSU Congress
Author:
Date: 1966-04-04
Edition: 4

    地拉那二日电　阿尔巴尼亚《团结报》二日发表题为《帝国主义和修正主义的“兄弟般团结”》的文章指出，苏联修正主义领导人最近经常谈论什么国际“团结”和各国人民的相互帮助和支持。但是修正主义者的这些话要从相反的意义去理解。他们通过国际“团结”同帝国主义者和世界上一切反动和反人民的集团联合在一起，在“相互帮助和支持”的幌子下达到同反动势力的相互帮助和支持的目的。
    新华社 Tirana, April 2nd (Xinhua) - The Albanian newspaper "Bashkimi" published an article on April 2nd entitled "The 'Fraternal Solidarity' of Imperialism and Revisionism," pointing out that Soviet revisionist leaders have recently frequently talked about international "unity" and the mutual help and support of the peoples of all countries. However, these words of the revisionists should be understood in the opposite sense. They unite with the imperialists and all reactionary and anti-people groups in the world through international "unity," and under the guise of "mutual help and support" achieve the goal of mutual help and support with reactionary forces.

    文章说，最近几年大量的历史事实充分地证明了这一无可否认的真理：每当美帝国主义者及其走狗处境困难的时候，他们就要求并立即得到现代修正主义者的帮助和支持；而每当现代修正主义者有需要的时候，他们又得到帝国主义者及其仆从的有力支持。文章接着列举大量的事实，揭露了苏联修正主义者同帝国主义者和各国反动派在越南、刚果（利）问题和印度尼西亚局势上相互勾结，狼狈为奸，共同反共、反人民和反对马克思列宁主义。文章说，美帝国主义者及其仆从也为苏联修正主义者帮了最好的忙。他们开动一切宣传机器，在反对马克思列宁主义和全世界革命力量的斗争中，支持修正主义领导人，疯狂地攻击中华人民共和国和中国共产党，攻击阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党以及坚持马克思列宁主义立场的其他兄弟党，把它们说成是“好战”，是“所有战争和今天世界上存在的不和的制造者”，甚至“是亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国人民饥饿和贫困的根源”。
    The article said that a large number of historical facts in recent years have fully proved this undeniable truth: whenever the U.S. imperialists and their running dogs are in difficulty, they demand and immediately receive help and support from modern revisionists; and whenever modern revisionists are in need, they receive strong support from the imperialists and their servants. The article then listed a large number of facts, exposing the Soviet revisionists' collusion with the imperialists and reactionaries of various countries on the issues of Vietnam, the Congo (Leopoldville), and the situation in Indonesia, acting as accomplices, jointly opposing communism, the people, and Marxism-Leninism. The article said that the U.S. imperialists and their servants have also done the best favors for the Soviet revisionists. They have started up all propaganda machines to support the revisionist leaders in the struggle against Marxism-Leninism and the revolutionary forces of the world, frantically attacking the People's Republic of China and the Chinese Communist Party, attacking the People's Republic of Albania and the Albanian Party of Labor, as well as other fraternal parties that adhere to the Marxist-Leninist stance, calling them "belligerent," "the creators of all wars and discord existing in the world today," and even "the root cause of hunger and poverty for the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America."

    文章说，“最近，在苏联共产党第二十三次代表大会召开的时候，美帝国主义者及其走狗进一步加紧同苏联的勾结，以便在苏联人民和其它国家人民中制造一种印象，似乎赫鲁晓夫分子如此努力奉行的和平共处政策，正在得到所谓‘减轻国际紧张局势’的具体效果。前些时候，苏美签订了新的文化合作协定。美国参加日内瓦十七国裁军会议的代表大肆宣扬美国关于不扩散核武器协议的草案有了一些‘变化’。英国政府在苏共二十三大召开前几天派出一位最老练的外交官去莫斯科，其目的在于进一步提高苏联领导人的威信。在美国主子的公开怂恿下，日本军国主义者也热烈欢迎苏联为扩大两国贸易和政治关系所作的努力。波恩复仇主义者在这方面也不落后。”
    The article said, "Recently, at the time of the convening of the 23rd Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the U.S. imperialists and their running dogs have further intensified their collusion with the Soviet Union in order to create an impression among the Soviet people and the people of other countries that the policy of peaceful coexistence, so assiduously pursued by the Khrushchev elements, is achieving concrete results in the so-called 'easing of international tensions.' Some time ago, the Soviet Union and the United States signed a new cultural cooperation agreement. The representative of the United States participating in the Geneva Seventeen-Nation Disarmament Conference vigorously publicized that the U.S. draft on the non-proliferation of nuclear weapons agreement had undergone some 'changes.' The British government sent one of its most seasoned diplomats to Moscow a few days before the opening of the 23rd CPSU Congress, with the aim of further enhancing the prestige of the Soviet leaders. Under the open instigation of their U.S. masters, the Japanese militarists also warmly welcomed the Soviet Union's efforts to expand trade and political relations between the two countries. The Bonn revanchists are not lagging behind in this regard either."

    文章说，帝国主义者和世界各国反动派在对苏联领导人表示“亲热”和“团结”的同时，对中华人民共和国和坚强地站在马克思列宁主义立场上的其它国家增加了敌对宣传，对它们制造各种各样荒谬绝伦的诬蔑。正是在苏共二十三大的前夕，西方报纸发表了苏共中央致许多国家共产党和工人党的“秘密”信件，这件事绝不是偶然的。这封信对中国共产党进行了极其恶毒的诬蔑，把中华人民共和国说成是“好战”的，是苏联和苏联人民的最野蛮的敌人。不难理解，这封如此“秘密”的信件为什么会落到资产阶级报纸的手里，也不难理解苏联修正主义领导人为什么至今对此还未加以否认。苏联修正主义领导人把自己打扮成是国际共产主义运动公开论战的坚决反对者，但是，却又经常委托帝国主义者和各国反动报纸作为自己在反对中华人民共和国和中国共产党的斗争中的代言人。
    The article said that while the imperialists and reactionaries of various countries in the world expressed "intimacy" and "unity" with the Soviet leaders, they increased hostile propaganda against the People's Republic of China and other countries that firmly stood on the Marxist-Leninist position, fabricating all kinds of absurd slanders against them. It is no accident that on the eve of the 23rd CPSU Congress, Western newspapers published "secret" letters from the CPSU Central Committee to the communist and workers' parties of many countries. This letter made extremely vicious slanders against the Chinese Communist Party, calling the People's Republic of China "belligerent" and the most barbaric enemy of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people. It is not difficult to understand why this so "secret" letter fell into the hands of the bourgeois newspapers, nor is it difficult to understand why the Soviet revisionist leaders have not denied it to this day. The Soviet revisionist leaders have disguised themselves as staunch opponents of open debate in the international communist movement, but they often entrust the imperialists and reactionary newspapers of various countries as their spokespersons in the struggle against the People's Republic of China and the Chinese Communist Party.

    文章说，这就是帝国主义和修正主义的“兄弟般团结”，这就是苏联修正主义者所理解的国际团结。文章最后强调指出，不管赫鲁晓夫修正主义者及其亲密的朋友美帝国主义者如何努力，他们是达不到他们欺骗各国人民的目的的。尽管他们彼此互相吹捧，但是他们不可能掩盖他们是社会主义和共产主义的凶恶敌人、是各国人民的凶恶敌人的真正嘴脸。
    The article said that this is the "fraternal solidarity" of imperialism and revisionism, and this is the international unity understood by the Soviet revisionists. The article finally emphasized that no matter how hard the Khrushchev revisionists and their close friends, the U.S. imperialists, try, they will not achieve their goal of deceiving the people of all countries. Although they praise each other, they cannot hide their true faces as vicious enemies of socialism and communism, and vicious enemies of the people of all countries.
