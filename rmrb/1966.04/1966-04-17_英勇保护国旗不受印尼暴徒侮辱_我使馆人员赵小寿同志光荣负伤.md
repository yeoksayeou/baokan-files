Title: Bravely Protecting the National Flag from Insults by Indonesian Thugs: Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou of Our Embassy Gloriously Wounded
Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 16th Dispatch: Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou, a staff member of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, who was shot and wounded during the barbaric attack on the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia by armed military police and thugs dispatched by the Indonesian government on the morning of the 15th, remains in critical condition after undergoing surgery in the hospital on the morning of the 16th due to severe blood loss.

    Zhao Xiaoshou was gloriously wounded while protecting the national flag of the People's Republic of China from being insulted by the thugs. At the time, he was brave and tenacious, fighting bare-handed to protect the five-star red flag. At this moment, armed soldiers who had scaled the wall and rushed into the embassy compound along with the thugs continuously fired at Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou and other comrades protecting the national flag. Zhao Xiaoshou was shot in the chest and fell to the ground, and was rescued by other comrades at the risk of their lives.

    On the afternoon of the 15th, Zhao Xiaoshou was sent to the hospital for emergency treatment. He was incoherent due to excessive blood loss and was first given plasma, and later transfused with 2000 CC of blood. From 5:00 AM to 8:00 AM today, Zhao Xiaoshou underwent surgery. He was seriously injured, with two ribs broken in his right chest and his right lung perforated by a bullet. After the surgery, Zhao Xiaoshou remained in the ward next to the operating room for treatment due to being in a critical condition, and is continuing to receive blood transfusions and oxygen. Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou has consistently shown tenacity in the hospital.

    The Chinese Embassy in Indonesia has been sending people to stay with and take care of Comrade Zhao Xiaoshou in the hospital. The responsible comrades of the embassy visited him in the hospital this morning.

### 英勇保护国旗不受印尼暴徒侮辱  我使馆人员赵小寿同志光荣负伤

1966-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    英勇保护国旗不受印尼暴徒侮辱
    我使馆人员赵小寿同志光荣负伤
    新华社十六日讯　雅加达消息：在印度尼西亚政府十五日上午出动武装军警和暴徒野蛮袭击中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆时被开枪打伤的大使馆工作人员赵小寿同志，因伤重流血过多，在十六日晨在医院动手术后仍处在危险期中。
    赵小寿是为了保护中华人民共和国国旗不受暴徒们的侮辱而光荣负伤的。当时，他英勇顽强，赤手空拳为保护五星红旗而斗争。这时，同暴徒们一起翻墙冲到使馆院内的武装军人向赵小寿等保护国旗的同志连续开枪，赵小寿胸部中弹倒地，经其他同志冒着生命危险把他抢救出来。
    十五日下午，赵小寿被送入医院急救，他因流血过多，神志不清，先输了血浆，后来又输血二千CC。今天上午五时到八时，为赵小寿作了手术，他伤势很重，右胸肋骨被打断两根，右肺被枪弹穿孔。赵小寿在手术后因处在危险期中，仍留在手术室旁边的病室治疗，正在继续输血和供氧气。赵小寿同志在医院中一直表现顽强。
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆一直派人留在医院陪同和照顾赵小寿同志，大使馆的负责同志今天上午到医院探望了他。
