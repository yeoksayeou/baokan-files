Title: I Love the Workshop
Author: Xiao Ping
Date: 1966-04-08
Edition: 6

    I Love the Workshop
    Xiao Ping, Beijing People's Printing Factory
    A steed loves the prairie;
    A dove loves the blue sky;
    A soldier loves his weapon;
    A worker loves the workshop.
    I love the workshop:
    The Secretary participates in labor,
    The Director follows the shift in production.
    Leaders and workers,
    Are intimately connected,
    The Party and the masses,
    Are linked by flesh and blood.
    I love the workshop:
    The workshop is the furnace of revolution,
    The workshop is the cradle of revolution.
    Pure steel,
    Can withstand a thousand hammer blows!
    Pine and cypress,
    Can withstand wind, snow, and bitter cold!
    The petrel,
    Dares to fight against storms!
    The eagle,
    Fears not the perilous mountains and rivers.
    I love the workshop:
    Comparing to the advanced,
    Find shortcomings fiercely;
    Learning from the advanced,
    Enthusiasm soars to the sky;
    Helping the backward,
    Class feeling weighs a thousand pounds,
    Surpassing the advanced,
    Scaling the peak never complacent.
    I love the workshop:
    Shift after shift submitting battle pledges,
    Opponents competing joyfully!
    Labor emulation surges like the spring tide,
    Comparing, learning, catching up, and helping, the waves are towering!
    Listen:
    The roar of motors,
    The spinning of machine wheels,
    Each and every paean of triumph,
    Stirring the heart...
    Look:
    Innovation red flowers,
    Blooming in competition,
    Abundant fruits,
    Gleaming with golden light...
    Red flowers,
    Abundant fruits,
    Triumphant news,
    Poems,
    Adorn the workshop so beautifully and solemnly.
    I love the workshop: For the people—
    Advance bravely,
    For the revolution—
    Loyal and devoted!
    Difficulties—fight!
    Victory—forward!
    This is: Our heroic words and bold declaration!



**Named Entities**

### 我爱车间
晓萍
1966-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我爱车间
    北京人民印刷厂　晓萍
    骏马，爱草原；
    白鸽，爱蓝天；
    战士，爱武器；
    工人，爱车间。
    我爱车间：
    书记参加劳动，
    主任跟班生产。
    领导和工人，
    亲密无间，
    党和群众，
    血肉相连。
    我爱车间：
    车间是革命的熔炉，
    车间是革命的摇篮。
    纯钢，
    经得起千锤百炼！
    松柏，
    顶得住风雪严寒！
    海燕，
    敢与狂风暴雨搏斗！
    雄鹰，
    不怕那险峻的万岭千山。
    我爱车间：
    比先进，
    狠找差距；
    学先进
    干劲冲天；
    帮后进，
    阶级感情千斤重，
    超先进，
    攀登高峰永不自满。
    我爱车间：
    班班组组递战表，
    对手对口赛得欢！
    劳动竞赛春潮涌，
    比学赶帮浪滔天！
    听：
    马达轰鸣，
    机轮飞转，
    曲曲凯歌，
    动人心弦……
    看：
    革新红花，
    争芳斗艳，
    硕果累累，
    金光闪闪……
    红花，
    硕果，
    捷报，
    诗篇，
    把车间打扮得多么美丽、庄严。
    我爱车间：为人民——
    勇往直前，
    为革命——
    忠心赤胆！
    困难——战斗！
    胜利——向前！
    这就是：我们的壮语、豪言！
