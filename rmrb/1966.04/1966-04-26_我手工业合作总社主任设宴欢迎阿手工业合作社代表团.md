Title: China Handicraft Industry Cooperative Director Hosts Banquet to Welcome Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-26
Edition: 4

    Director of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives Hosts Banquet to Welcome Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 24th) Chen Yifan, Director of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, hosted a banquet on the 23rd to welcome the Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation led by Seimerko, Chairman of the Central Union of Albanian Handicraft Cooperatives.  Present as guests were Deng Jie, Vice Minister of the Second Ministry of Light Industry, and Du Ganquan, member of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission. Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasil Nathanaili, and Commercial Counselor Keço Kasapi, were also invited to attend.
    The Albanian friends arrived in Beijing on the 22nd.

### 我手工业合作总社主任设宴欢迎阿手工业合作社代表团

1966-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我手工业合作总社主任
    设宴欢迎阿手工业合作社代表团
    据新华社二十四日讯中华全国手工业合作总社主任陈一帆二十三日设宴欢迎由阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社中央联合会主席赛梅尔科率领的阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社代表团。出席作陪的有第二轻工业部副部长邓洁、对外经委委员杜干全等。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利、商务参赞凯乔·卡萨比也应邀出席。
    阿尔巴尼亚朋友是在二十二日抵京的。
