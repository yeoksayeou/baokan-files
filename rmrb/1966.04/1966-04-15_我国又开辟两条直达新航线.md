Title: China Opens Two New Direct Air Routes

Author: 
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 2

    China Opens Two New Direct Air Routes
    (Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 14) China has opened two new direct air routes: one from Shenyang via Beijing and Taiyuan to Xi'an; the other from Shenyang via Beijing, Taiyuan, and Xi'an to Chengdu.
    These two direct air routes, traversing Northeast, Northwest, and Southwest China, were inaugurated on the 13th and 14th of this month, respectively.
    The opening of the new direct air routes will greatly promote the exchange of goods between Northeast and Northwest, Southwest China, and facilitate travel for passengers. The flight from Shenyang to Xi'an takes only seven and a half hours, and the flight from Shenyang to Chengdu takes only eight and a half hours.

### 我国又开辟两条直达新航线

1966-04-15
第2版()
专栏：

    我国又开辟两条直达新航线
    新华社沈阳十四日电　我国又开辟两条空中直达航线，一条是沈阳经北京、太原至西安；另一条是沈阳经北京、太原、西安至成都。
    这两条贯穿我国东北和西北、西南的直达航线，已经分别在本月十三和十四日开航。
    新的直达航线的开辟，将大大促进东北和西北、西南地区的物资交流，便利旅客的往来。从沈阳至西安只需飞行七个半小时，沈阳至成都只需飞行八个半小时。
