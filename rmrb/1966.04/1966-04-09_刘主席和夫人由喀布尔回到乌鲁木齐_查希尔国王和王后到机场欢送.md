Title: Chairman Liu and his Wife Return to Urumqi from Kabul; King Zahir and Queen See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, April 8th. After a four-day friendly visit to Afghanistan, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China and his wife Wang Guangmei left Kabul by special plane on the morning of April 8th, full of the Afghan people's deep friendship for the Chinese people, to return to their country.
    King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan and Queen Homeira saw off the distinguished Chinese guests at the airport.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qian, Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and other accompanying personnel who accompanied Chairman Liu on his visit returned on the same plane.
    Among those who saw the Chinese guests off at the airport were: the King's uncle, Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi; Prime Minister Mohammad Hashim Maiwandwal and his wife; President of the Afghanistan-China Friendship Association, Prince Sultan Mahmoud Ghazi and his wife; Speaker of the Senate Abdul Hadi Dawi; Speaker of the House of the People Abdul Zahir; Minister of the Royal Court Ali Mohammad; Foreign Minister Nur Ahmad Etemadi; Defense Minister General Khan Mohammad; Interior Minister Abdul Sattar Shalizi and his wife; Governor of Kabul Province Mohammad Omar Wardak; Mayor of Kabul Mohammad Asghar; Afghan Ambassador to China Mohammad Ashaf Sohail, other senior military and government officials, and diplomatic representatives of various countries stationed here.
    Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Chen Feng and his wife, all personnel of the Chinese Embassy, as well as Chinese experts and students in Afghanistan, also went to the airport to see them off.
    The King and Queen of Afghanistan arrived at the Forty Pillars Palace at 6:30 in the morning to accompany Chairman Liu and his wife from there to the airport by car. They arrived at the airport at 7:00 in the morning, and were greeted by cheers from the citizens of Kabul along the way.
    Chinese and Afghan national flags were hung at the airport, and more than 2,000 people gathered at the airport to see off the distinguished guests. As the Chinese guests circled the field to bid farewell to government officials, military officers, and the people, a storm of cheers resounded at the airport. The masses cheered, waved the flags of the two countries, and sprinkled countless flowers on the distinguished guests. They shouted: "Long live the friendship between Afghanistan and China." A grand farewell ceremony was held at the airport. The band played the national anthems of both countries. Chairman Liu, accompanied by the King, reviewed the guard of honor.
    Before boarding the plane, Chairman Liu and his wife and accompanying personnel cordially shook hands and said goodbye to the King and Queen, Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi, Prince Sultan Mahmoud Ghazi and his wife, and others.
    A group of Afghan children in national costumes presented them with flowers.
    Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, April 8th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei concluded their friendly visit to Afghanistan and returned to Urumqi by special plane at noon today.
    Returning on the same plane were Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qian, Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Public Security Wang Dongxing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Feng Xuan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Wang Daohan, and others who accompanied them on the visit abroad.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his party were welcomed at the airport by more than 5,000 people from all walks of life in Urumqi. Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Party Committee, and his wife Luo Lan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Party Committee, Chairman of the Autonomous Region Saifuddin and his wife Ayimu, as well as leaders of the Xinjiang Party, government, army, and Production and Construction Corps. Young Pioneers presented bouquets to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice Premier Chen Yi and others.

### 刘主席和夫人由喀布尔回到乌鲁木齐  查希尔国王和王后到机场欢送

1966-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席和夫人由喀布尔回到乌鲁木齐
    查希尔国王和王后到机场欢送
    新华社喀布尔八日电　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美在阿富汗进行了四天的友好访问后，八日上午满载着阿富汗人民对中国人民的深厚友谊乘专机离喀布尔回国。
    阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿和王后霍梅拉到机场欢送中国贵宾。
    陈毅副总理和夫人张茜，外交部副部长章汉夫和陪同刘主席访问的其他随行人员同机回国。
    到机场欢送中国客人的有：国王的叔父沙阿·瓦利·汗·加齐元帅，首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔和夫人，阿富汗—中国友好协会会长苏尔坦·马哈茂德·加齐亲王和夫人，长老院议长阿卜杜勒·哈迪·达威，人民院议长阿卜杜勒·查希尔，宫廷大臣阿里·穆罕默德，外交大臣努尔·艾哈迈德·埃特马迪，国防大臣汗·穆罕默德大将，内政大臣阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利奇和夫人，喀布尔省省长穆罕默德·奥马尔·瓦尔达克，喀布尔市市长穆罕默德·阿斯加尔，阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔、其他高级军政官员和各国驻这里的外交代表。
    中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫和夫人，中国大使馆全体人员以及在阿富汗的中国专家和留学生也到机场送行。
    阿富汗国王和王后在早晨六时三十分就到四十柱宫，陪同刘主席和夫人从那里乘车前往机场。他们在早晨七时到达机场，沿途受到喀布尔市民的欢呼。
    机场上挂起了中国和阿富汗的国旗，两千多人聚集在机场上为贵宾送行。当中国客人绕场向政府官员和军官以及人民群众告别时，机场上响起了暴风雨般的欢呼声。群众欢呼着，挥动着两国的国旗，把无数的花朵撒在贵宾身上。他们高呼：“阿富汗和中国的友谊万岁”。机场上举行盛大的欢送仪式。乐队高奏两国国歌。刘主席在国王陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    刘主席和夫人以及随行人员在登上飞机前，同国王和王后，沙阿·瓦利·汗·加齐元帅，苏尔坦·马哈茂德·加齐亲王和夫人等亲切握手告别。
    一群穿着民族服装的阿富汗儿童向他们献了花。
    新华社乌鲁木齐八日电刘少奇主席和夫人王光美结束了在阿富汗的友好访问，今天中午乘专机回到乌鲁木齐。
    同机回来的有陪同出国访问的国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人张茜，外交部副部长章汉夫，公安部副部长汪东兴，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长冯铉，对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵等。
    刘少奇主席等一行在机场上受到乌鲁木齐市各族各界五千多人的欢迎。到机场欢迎的有自治区党委第一书记王恩茂和夫人骆岚，自治区党委书记处书记、自治区主席赛福鼎和夫人阿衣木以及新疆党、政府、军队和生产建设兵团的负责人。少先队员们向刘少奇主席和陈毅副总理等献了花束。
