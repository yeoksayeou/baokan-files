Title: Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-20
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: The Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation, invited to visit China by the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing today, led by Chaloun Vongsamsang, Director of the "Voice of Laos" radio station.  Zhou Xinwu, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and others were at the airport to greet them.


**Named Entities**

中华全国新闻工作者协会	All-China Journalists Association
老挝爱国力量新闻代表团	Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation
扎伦·翁桑昂	Chaloun Vongsamsang
老挝之声	Voice of Laos
周新武	Zhou Xinwu
广播事业管理局	Broadcasting Affairs Bureau
北京  Beijing
新华社  Xinhua News Agency

### 老挝爱国力量新闻代表团到京

1966-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国力量新闻代表团到京
    新华社十九日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的老挝爱国力量新闻代表团，在“老挝之声”电台台长扎伦·翁桑昂率领下，今天到达北京。到机场迎接的有广播事业管理局副局长周新武等。
