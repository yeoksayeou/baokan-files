Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Thirtieth Enlarged Meeting Listened to the Report by the Ministry of Culture on Resolutely Carrying the Socialist Cultural Revolution Through to the End
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 14th  The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its thirtieth meeting (enlarged) this afternoon.
    The meeting listened to a report by Shi Ximin, Vice Minister of Culture, entitled "Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought and Resolutely Carry the Socialist Cultural Revolution Through to the End."
    Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

**Named Entities**

全国人民代表大会常务委员会	National People's Congress Standing Committee
文化部	Ministry of Culture
石西民	Shi Ximin
毛泽东思想	Mao Zedong Thought
政协全国委员会	National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference

### 人大常委会举行第三十次扩大会议  听取了文化部关于坚决把社会主义文化革命进行到底的报告

1966-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行第三十次扩大会议
    听取了文化部关于坚决把社会主义文化革命进行到底的报告
    新华社十四日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天下午举行第三十次会议扩大会议。
    会议听取了文化部副部长石西民作的关于高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚决把社会主义文化革命进行到底的报告。
    政协全国委员会常务委员列席了会议。
