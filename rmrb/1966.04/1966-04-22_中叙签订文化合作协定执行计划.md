Title: China and Syria Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-22
Edition: 4

    China and Syria Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 20th -- China and Syria signed here on the 20th the 1966 and 1967 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries.
    Li Chang, head of the Chinese government cultural delegation and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Yusuf Shaqra, Secretary-General of the Syrian Ministry of Culture and National Guidance, signed the plan on behalf of their respective governments.
    In their speeches, Li Chang and Shaqra both pointed out that the signing of the implementation plan will promote cultural exchanges between China and Syria, strengthen friendship, and promote the common anti-imperialist cause.
    Present at the signing ceremony were Chen Tan, Chinese Ambassador to Syria, members of the Chinese government cultural delegation, and senior officials from the Syrian Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 中叙签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中叙签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社大马士革二十日电　中国和叙利亚二十日在这里签订了两国文化合作协定一九六六年和一九六七年执行计划。
    中国政府文化代表团团长、对外文委副主任李昌和叙利亚文化和国家指导部秘书长优素福·沙克拉分别代表两国政府在计划上签字。
    李昌和沙克拉在讲话中都指出，执行计划的签署，将促进中叙两国的文化交流、加强友谊和共同的反帝事业。
    签字时在场的有中国驻叙利亚大使陈坦、中国政府文化代表团团员，以及叙利亚教育部和外交部的高级官员。
