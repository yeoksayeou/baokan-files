Title: Iraqi Foreign Ministry's Economic Affairs Director Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 3

    Iraqi Foreign Ministry's Economic Affairs Director Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 18th -- Mr. Mutawalli, Director of the Economic Affairs Department of the Iraqi Foreign Ministry, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of our country. Du Yun, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to greet them. Diplomatic officials from the Iraqi Embassy in China also greeted them at the airport.

### 伊拉克外交部经济司司长到京

1966-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克外交部经济司司长到京
    新华社十八日讯　应邀前来我国访问的伊拉克外交部经济司司长莫托瓦利和夫人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的，有对外贸易部部长助理杜毓澐等。伊拉克驻中国大使馆外交官员也到机场迎接。
