Title: US-Soviet-Japanese-Indian "Holy Alliance" Cannot Stop the Asian Revolution; Cambodian Newspaper Says China Stands at the Forefront of the Anti-US Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 4

    US-Soviet-Japanese-Indian "Holy Alliance" Cannot Stop the Asian Revolution
    Cambodian Newspaper Says China Stands at the Forefront of the Anti-US Struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 11th -- "Cambodia Telegram" published a commentary on the 11th stating: Asian countries such as China, Cambodia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are serious obstacles to US imperialism's attempt to dominate the world. And standing at the forefront of the struggle against US imperialism is China. "This explains why the warmongers in Washington and their various lackeys must take cruel, mad, and malicious actions against China and other places in Asia that persist in the anti-imperialist struggle."
    The commentary said, "US imperialism has reduced its 'pressure' on Europe in order to concentrate its greedy goals and aggressive forces in Asia." US imperialism has gathered its lackeys in this region of the world to establish a "ring of encirclement" that serves its aggressive purposes. And at the heart of this new strategy is the "Holy Alliance" that brings together the Soviet Union, Japan, and India around the United States.
    The commentary emphasized: "Asia has already been swept by a storm. National liberation movements are surging. Democratic forces and the people are more determined than ever to confront the American aggressors. No force can stop or hinder this surging tide. Even the notorious military 'ring of encirclement' of the United States cannot stop or hinder this tide."

### 美苏日印“神圣同盟”挡不住亚洲革命  柬报说中国站在反美斗争最前列

1966-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美苏日印“神圣同盟”挡不住亚洲革命
    柬报说中国站在反美斗争最前列
    新华社金边电　《柬埔寨电讯报》十一日发表评论说：中国、柬埔寨、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国等亚洲国家，都是美帝国主义企图独霸世界的严重障碍。而站在反对美帝国主义的斗争的最前面的是中国。“这就说明了华盛顿好战分子及其形形色色的走狗为什么要对中国以及亚洲坚持反帝斗争的其他地方竭力采取残酷的疯狂行动和恶意的行动。”
    评论说，“美帝国主义已经减轻了它对欧洲的‘压力’，以便把它的贪婪的目标和侵略势力集中到亚洲来。”美帝国主义纠集它的走狗在世界这个地区建立了一个服务于它的侵略目的的“包围圈”。而它的这个新战略的核心是把苏联、日本和印度纠集在美国周围的“神圣同盟”。
    评论强调指出：“亚洲已经掀起了风暴。民族解放运动风起云涌。民主力量和人民比以往更坚决地起来对付美国侵略者。任何势力都不能制止和阻挡这股汹涌澎湃的潮流。即使是美国这个臭名远扬的军事‘包围圈’也不能制止和阻挡这股潮流。”
