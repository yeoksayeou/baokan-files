Title: Chairman Liu Cables Thanks to Chairman Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1966-04-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 19th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi sent a cable to General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, on the 19th, upon leaving the Union of Burma to return home, expressing his gratitude. The full text of the cable is as follows:
    To His Excellency Chairman Ne Win of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, Rangoon:
    As we leave your country, filled with the profound friendship of the Burmese people towards the Chinese people, my wife and I, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and all accompanying personnel once again express our heartfelt gratitude to Your Excellency Chairman and Madame, and to the Burmese Government and people, for the warm and friendly reception you gave us during our visit to Burma. We wish the Union of Burma prosperity and its people happiness, and we wish the friendship between China and Burma to grow day by day.
    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
            April 19, 1966

**Named Entities**

仰光	Yǎngguāng (Rangoon)

### 刘主席电谢奈温主席

1966-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电谢奈温主席
    新华社仰光十九日电　刘少奇主席十九日在离开缅甸联邦回国的时候，打电报给缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军表示感谢。电报全文如下：仰光缅甸联邦革命委员会尊敬的奈温主席阁下：
    当我们满载缅甸人民对中国人民的深厚友谊离开贵国国境时，我和我的夫人、陈毅副总理和夫人以及全体随行人员再一次向主席阁下和夫人，向缅甸政府和人民表示衷心的感谢，感谢你们在我们访问缅甸期间所给予我们的热情友好的接待。祝缅甸联邦繁荣昌盛、人民幸福，祝中缅友谊日益发展。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
            一九六六年四月十九日
