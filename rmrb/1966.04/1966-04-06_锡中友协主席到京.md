Title: Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 5th -- At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Mr. Alardiya Wannakum, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and his wife arrived in Beijing on the 4th for a visit to our country.
    Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Zhang Zhizhiang, Lin Lin, Yang Huanan, and Shi Mingke, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 锡中友协主席到京

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    锡中友协主席到京
    新华社五日讯　应中国锡兰友好协会邀请来我国访问的锡兰中国友好协会主席阿拉季亚瓦纳和夫人，于四日到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有张志让、林林、杨化南、石鸣珂等有关方面负责人。
