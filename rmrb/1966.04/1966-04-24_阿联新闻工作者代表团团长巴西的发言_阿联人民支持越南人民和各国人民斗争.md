Title: Speech by Ibrahim Said Brazil, Head of the Arab League Journalists Delegation: The Arab League People Support the Struggle of the Vietnamese People and the People of All Countries

Author:
Date: 1966-04-24
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd - Ibrahim Said Brazil, head of the Arab League Journalists Delegation, spoke at the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association on the 22nd, condemning the aggression and plunder carried out by imperialism in Africa and Southeast Asia. He expressed his support for the struggle of the Vietnamese people and condemned American aggression against Vietnam.

    Ibrahim Said Brazil said that the Arab League delegation recognizes the seriousness of the current international situation. We emphasize that the cause of the current international tensions in Africa, and especially in Southeast Asia, is none other than the imperialist monopolists uniting to plunder the wealth of the people. These monopolists rely on the remnants of old colonialism and its influence, as well as imperialism that paves the way for Zionism and colonialism, and they also rely on aggressive treaties, military bases, and fleets.

    He said that the imperialists are doing this to maintain their control, expand their power, and eliminate the revolutionary spirit of the region. If it were not for imperialism disregarding the Arab people's human right to survival and forcibly imposing Israel on the Arab homeland of Palestine, and if it were not for the imperialist countries protecting and providing Israel with the means to prolong its life, the situation in the Middle East would never have reached such a dangerous state of tension. He said that in Africa, imperialism has united against the people of the Congo, the people of Ghana, and the people of Tanganyika, carrying out sabotage and massacres.

    Ibrahim Said Brazil, on behalf of the Arab League delegation, expressed his support for the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people and condemned American aggression against Vietnam. He said, we demand an immediate end to this aggression. We demand that the Vietnamese people be given full rights to decide its unification and complete freedom. He also expressed his support for the struggles of the peoples of Laos, Angola, Mozambique, so-called Portuguese Guinea, Southern Rhodesia, Nyasaland, Basutoland, and other parts of the world, supported the inherent right of the People's Republic of China to obtain its legitimate seat in the United Nations and the Security Council, supported the uninterrupted and fierce struggle of the Palestinian people to reclaim their stolen homeland, and supported the People's Revolution in Yemen.

### 阿联新闻工作者代表团团长巴西的发言  阿联人民支持越南人民和各国人民斗争

1966-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联新闻工作者代表团团长巴西的发言
    阿联人民支持越南人民和各国人民斗争
    新华社二十三日讯　阿联新闻工作者代表团团长易卜拉欣·赛义德·巴西二十二日在亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）上发言，谴责帝国主义在非洲和东南亚进行的侵略和掠夺。他表示支持越南人民的斗争，谴责美国对越南的侵略。
    易卜拉欣·赛义德·巴西说，阿联代表团认识到目前国际局势的严重性。我们强调指出，当前非洲、特别是东南亚存在的国际紧张局势的原因不是别的，而是帝国主义垄断家联合起来对人民的财富实行掠夺。这些垄断家们依赖老殖民主义及其影响的残余，还依赖为犹太复国主义和殖民主义铺平道路的帝国主义，他们还依赖侵略条约、军事基地和舰队。
    他说，帝国主义者这样做是为了维持他们的控制、扩大势力和消灭这个地区的革命精神。如果不是帝国主义不顾阿拉伯争取生存的人权、硬把以色列强加到巴勒斯坦的阿拉伯家园，如果不是帝国主义国家保护并向以色列提供苟延残喘的手段的话，中东的局势也永远不会达到这样危险的紧张状况。他说，在非洲，帝国主义联合起来反对刚果人民、加纳人民、坦噶尼喀人民，进行破坏和屠杀。
    易卜拉欣·赛义德·巴西代表阿联代表团表示支持越南人民的英勇斗争，谴责美国对越南的侵略。他说，我们要求立即停止这种侵略。我们要求给越南人民以充分的权利来决定它的统一和完全自由。他还表示支持老挝、安哥拉、莫三鼻给、所谓葡属几内亚、南罗得西亚、尼亚萨兰、巴苏陀兰以及世界其他地方的人民的斗争，支持中华人民共和国取得它在联合国和安理会的合法席位的固有权利，支持巴勒斯坦人民为夺回他们被偷偷夺走的国土而进行的不间断的激烈斗争，支持也门的人民革命。
