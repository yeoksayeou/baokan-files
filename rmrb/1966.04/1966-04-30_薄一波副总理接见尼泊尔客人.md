Title: Vice Premier Bo Yibo Receives Nepalese Guests
Author: Liu Changzhong
Date: 1966-04-30
Edition: 4

    Vice Premier Bo Yibo Receives Nepalese Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo received K.S. Thapa, Assistant Private Secretary to the Crown Prince of Nepal, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.
    Present at the reception were Zhou Shujia, Lin Lin, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Nepalese Ambassador to China, Randhir Subba, was also present. (Attached picture)
    April 29, Vice Premier Bo Yibo receives K. S. Thapa, Assistant Private Secretary to the Crown Prince of Nepal. Randhir Subba, Nepalese Ambassador to China, is on the right.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong

### 薄一波副总理接见尼泊尔客人
刘长忠
1966-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理接见尼泊尔客人
    新华社二十九日讯　国务院副总理薄一波今天下午接见尼泊尔王太子的助理私人秘书克·斯·塔帕，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有周叔迦、林林等有关方面负责人。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也在座。（附图片）
    四月二十九日，薄一波副总理接见尼泊尔王太子的助理私人秘书克·斯·塔帕。右一是尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴。
                                    新华社记者　刘长忠摄
