Title: South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-27
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and pay a friendly visit to our country, the delegation of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions, led by Tran Hoai Nam, Standing Committee Member of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Kang Yonghe, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Peng Siming, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, and hundreds of workers from the capital. Le Tung Son, member of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, and Hoang Bac, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 南越解放工会联合会代表团到京

1966-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    南越解放工会联合会代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和进行友好访问的越南南方解放工会联合会代表团，在团长、越南南方解放工会联合会常务委员陈怀南率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团在飞机场受到了中华全国总工会副主席康永和、北京市总工会主席彭思明以及首都数百工人的热烈欢迎。越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团员黎松山和越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞黄北也到机场欢迎。
