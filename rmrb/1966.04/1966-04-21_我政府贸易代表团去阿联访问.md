Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Visits the UAR
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 19th – A Chinese government trade delegation led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, left Beijing by plane this morning for a friendly visit to the United Arab Republic.
    Among those seeing the delegation off at the airport were Fu Shenglin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others.  Diplomatic officials such as Zakaria Adly Imam, Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, were also at the airport to see them off.

### 我政府贸易代表团去阿联访问

1966-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团去阿联访问
    新华社十九日讯　由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去阿拉伯联合共和国进行友好访问。
    前往机场送行的有对外贸易部副部长傅生麟等。阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使查卡里亚·阿德利·伊马姆等外交官员也到机场送行。
