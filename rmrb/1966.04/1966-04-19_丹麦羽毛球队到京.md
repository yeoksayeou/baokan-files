Title: Danish Badminton Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-04-19
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: A nine-member Danish men's and women's badminton team, led by Arne Bruun, Vice Chairman of the Danish Badminton Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning for a friendly visit and matches in our country. This is the first Danish sports team to visit our country.
    Guo Lei, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Badminton Association, and Chinese badminton players warmly welcomed them at the airport.
    That evening, Guo Lei held a reception to welcome all the Danish guests. On the evening of the 19th, the Danish men's and women's badminton teams will have their first friendly match with the Chinese men's and women's badminton teams.

### 丹麦羽毛球队到京

1966-04-19
第3版()
专栏：

    丹麦羽毛球队到京
    据新华社十八日讯　应邀来我国访问和进行友谊比赛的丹麦男女羽毛球队一行九人，由丹麦羽毛球协会副主席阿纳·布鲁恩率领，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。这是丹麦第一个来我国访问的体育队伍。
    中国羽毛球协会副主席郭雷和我国羽毛球运动员们到机场热烈欢迎他们。
    当天晚上，郭雷举行招待会，欢迎全体丹麦客人。十九日晚上，丹麦男女羽毛球队将同中国男女羽毛球一队进行首次友谊比赛。
