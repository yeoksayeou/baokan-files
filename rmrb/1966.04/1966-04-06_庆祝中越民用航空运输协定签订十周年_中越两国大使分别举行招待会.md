Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Aviation Transportation Agreement: Chinese and Vietnamese Ambassadors Hold Receptions Respectively
Author:
Date: 1966-04-06
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 5th, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Zhu Qiwen held a reception on the evening of the 5th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Aviation Transportation Agreement.

    Tran Huu Duc, Minister of the Premier's Office of Vietnam, Phan Trong Tue, Minister of Transport, Nguyen Co Thach, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nguyen Van Tien, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration, Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee, and others attended the reception.

    At the reception, Ambassador Zhu Qiwen and Minister Tran Huu Duc delivered speeches successively, jointly wishing that the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people would be increasingly consolidated and strengthened, and wishing the further development of the civil aviation undertakings of China and Vietnam.

    On the evening of the 3rd, Nguyen Van Tien, Director of the Vietnamese Civil Aviation Administration, also held a reception to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Aviation Transportation Agreement.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, April 5th, Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, held a reception tonight to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Civil Aviation Transportation Agreement between the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the People's Republic of China.

    At the reception, Ambassador Tran Tu Binh and Zhang Xisan, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, delivered speeches. They praised that the Sino-Vietnamese Civil Aviation Transportation Agreement has been fully implemented for ten years, making many achievements in developing the economies, cultures, and transportation undertakings of the two countries.

    Invited to attend the reception were Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as Yu Lijin, Li Qiang, Peng Deqing, Sun Xiaocun, Pan Zhenwu, and other relevant responsible persons.

    Le Tung Son, Acting Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation to China, also attended the reception.

### 庆祝中越民用航空运输协定签订十周年  中越两国大使分别举行招待会

1966-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中越民用航空运输协定签订十周年
    中越两国大使分别举行招待会
    据新华社河内五日电　中国驻越南大使朱其文五日晚上为中越民用航空运输协定签订十周年举行招待会。
    越南总理府部长陈友翼，交通运输部部长潘仲慧，外交部副部长阮基石，民用航空局局长阮文先，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴等出席招待会。
    招待会上，朱其文大使和陈友翼部长先后讲了话，共祝中越两国人民的战斗友谊日益巩固和加强，祝中越两国民航事业进一步发展。
    三日晚上，越南民用航空局局长阮文先也举行招待会，纪念中越民用航空运输协定签订十周年。
    据新华社五日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平今晚举行招待会，庆祝越南民主共和国政府和中华人民共和国政府民用航空运输协定签订十周年。
    在招待会上，陈子平大使和中国民用航空总局副局长张西三讲了话。他们赞扬越中两国民用航空运输协定十年来得到了完满的执行，为发展两国的经济、文化和交通事业，作出了很多成绩。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有外交部副部长韩念龙，以及余立金、李强、彭德清、孙晓村、潘振武等有关方面的负责人。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山也出席了招待会。
