Title: Yemeni Acting President Receives Chinese Experts
Author:
Date: 1966-04-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Sana, 7th. Yemeni Acting President Abdullah al-Eriani received the leaders of the Chinese textile expert team on the 6th and discussed with them the cotton textile printing and dyeing factory being built with Chinese assistance in Yemen.
    The Acting President expressed his deep gratitude for the great efforts made by the Chinese experts in helping Yemen build the cotton textile printing and dyeing factory. He said that this cotton textile printing and dyeing factory, built with Chinese assistance, is Yemen's first large textile factory. It will bring many benefits to the Yemeni people.

### 也门代理总统接见我国专家

1966-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    也门代理总统接见我国专家
    新华社萨那七日电　也门代理总统阿卜杜拉·阿里亚尼六日接见了中国纺织专家小组的领导人，同他们就中国帮助也门建设的棉织印染厂进行了讨论。
    代理总统对中国专家帮助也门建设棉织印染厂所作的巨大努力表示深切感谢。他说，由中国帮助建设的这个棉织印染厂是也门的第一个大纺织厂。它将给也门人民带来很多好处。
