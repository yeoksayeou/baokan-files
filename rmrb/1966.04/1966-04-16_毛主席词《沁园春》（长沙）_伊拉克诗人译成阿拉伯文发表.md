Title: Chairman Mao's Poem "Qinyuan Spring" (Changsha) Translated into Arabic and Published by Iraqi Poet
Author:
Date: 1966-04-16
Edition: 3

    Chairman Mao's Poem "Qinyuan Spring" (Changsha)
    Translated into Arabic and Published by Iraqi Poet
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 14th: The Iraqi magazine "Al-Aqlam" (The Pen) published the Arabic translation of Chairman Mao Zedong's poem "Qinyuan Spring" (Changsha) in its January issue.
  The translator of the poem is Iraqi poet Numan Mahir Kanani.

### 毛主席词《沁园春》（长沙）  伊拉克诗人译成阿拉伯文发表

1966-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    毛主席词《沁园春》（长沙）
    伊拉克诗人译成阿拉伯文发表
    新华社十四日讯　巴格达消息：伊拉克杂志《笔》一月号刊登了毛泽东主席的词《沁园春》（长沙）的阿拉伯文译文。
  这首词的译者是伊拉克诗人努曼·马希尔·卡纳尼。
