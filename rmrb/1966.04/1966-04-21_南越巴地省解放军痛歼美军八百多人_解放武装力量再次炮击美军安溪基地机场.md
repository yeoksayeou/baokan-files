Title: South Vietnamese Ba Ria Province Liberation Army Annihilates Over 800 American Soldiers; Liberation Armed Forces Again Shell American An Khe Base Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th – According to supplementary reports from the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Army of Ba Ria Province, South Vietnam, completely annihilated one battalion of the U.S. 1st Infantry Division and severely damaged another during counter-"sweep" operations on April 10th and 11th, killing and wounding over 800 American invading soldiers.
    On the 10th, the Liberation Army ambushed a battalion of American invading soldiers on the highway from Binh Gia to Dat Do, approximately 23 kilometers northeast of Ba Ria city, the capital of Ba Ria province. After an hour of fierce fighting, over 200 American soldiers were eliminated, and the remaining American soldiers fled in disarray.
    At 2:00 PM on the following day, the Liberation Army surrounded and attacked another battalion of American soldiers in the Lam Xuan commune, over 30 kilometers northeast of Ba Ria city. As soon as the battle commenced, the Liberation Army launched an assault and engaged the enemy in fierce hand-to-hand combat, completely and thoroughly eliminating the battalion, wiping out over 600 American invading soldiers, and seizing a large quantity of weapons and military supplies. After the battle, the enemy frantically dispatched a large number of helicopters to recover the bodies of the American soldiers, which took two full days to complete.
    Thus, from January 28th to April 11th this year, on the South Vietnam battlefield, the Liberation Armed Forces have completely annihilated sixteen battalions of American and puppet troops, including ten battalions of American invading soldiers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th – Saigon Dispatch: The South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces attacked the airport at the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division's An Khe base in the early hours of the 20th.
    According to Western news agencies, the Liberation Armed Forces bombarded the airport with mortars at 2:00 AM. Under the cover of artillery fire, Liberation Army fighters swiftly crossed the enemy's numerous defense facilities, entered the airport, and used explosive charges to blow up American aircraft parked on the airfield before safely withdrawing.
    This is the second attack by the Liberation Armed Forces on the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division's lair in An Khe since February this year. During the first attack on this American base in the early hours of February 20th, the Liberation Armed Forces wiped out 497 enemy soldiers and destroyed 97 aircraft of various types in one fell swoop.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 19th – According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces recently eliminated over 550 enemy soldiers in Ben Tre Province and the Dong Thap Muoi region.
    The report stated that in the late hours of April 12th, the Liberation Armed Forces attacked the puppet army's Kien Hoa military training center, located 1.5 kilometers east of Ben Tre city. With the assistance of the people, the Liberation Armed Forces launched a surprise attack, swiftly destroying the puppet army training center, killing over 200 enemy soldiers, capturing nearly 100, seizing over 200 weapons, six tons of ammunition, and a large quantity of military supplies, and burning down all the training center's warehouses and barracks.
    The news agency also reported that on the night of April 9th, the Liberation Armed Forces of the Dong Thap Muoi region attacked the puppet army stationed in Thạnh Phước commune, Tuyên Nhơn district, completely annihilating five enemy platoons, killing 150 soldiers, and seizing all weapons and military supplies. On the morning of the following day, the Liberation Armed Forces ambushed a unit of enemy troops sent to collect the bodies of the puppet soldiers, killing over 100 soldiers and shooting down one helicopter.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th – According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the soldiers and civilians of the provinces in the southern West Zone of South Vietnam, located at the southern end of the Mekong Delta, launched continuous attacks on enemy-occupied towns and cities and launched offensives against the defenders of "strategic hamlets" in the first two months of this year, achieving great victories.
    According to incomplete statistics, from the beginning of January to February 22nd, the Liberation Armed Forces launched twenty-seven attacks on cities such as Can Tho, Rach Gia, Di Thach, Tra Vinh, and Vinh Long, as well as many other towns, killing and wounding over 1,200 enemy soldiers, including 456 American invading soldiers, destroying and damaging nearly eighty enemy aircraft, destroying four ammunition depots, six oil depots, two radar stations, twenty U.S. military dormitories, and many military warehouses and puppet regime organs, destroying thirty enemy military vehicles and two 105 mm howitzers, and sinking and damaging one enemy warship each.
    During this period, the soldiers and civilians of the southern West Zone fought many brilliant surprise attacks. (Attached image)
    The heroic South Vietnam Liberation Army continues to annihilate the enemy, advancing from victory to victory.				Xinhua News Agency Reporter Guo Ji

### 南越巴地省解放军痛歼美军八百多人  解放武装力量再次炮击美军安溪基地机场

1966-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    南越巴地省解放军痛歼美军八百多人
    解放武装力量再次炮击美军安溪基地机场
    新华社河内二十日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，越南南方巴地省解放军四月十日和十一日在反“扫荡”战斗中，全歼美国第一步兵师一个营，重创另一个营，共打死打伤美国侵略军八百多名。
    十日，解放军在距巴地省城巴地市东北面约二十三公里的从平也到登波的公路上截击美国侵略军一个营。经过一小时的激烈战斗，歼灭美军二百多名，剩下的美军狼狈逃窜。
    第二天下午两时，解放军又在距离巴地市东北面三十多公里的林春乡包围和攻击了另一营美军。战斗一打响，解放军就发起冲锋，同敌人展开激烈的肉搏战，全部、干净地消灭了这个营，共消灭美国侵略军六百多名，缴获大批武器和军用物资。战斗结束后，敌人慌忙出动大批直升飞机前来抢运美军尸体，整整两天才把美军尸体运完。
    这样，从今年一月二十八日到四月十一日，在越南南方战场上，解放武装力量已经全歼美、伪军十六个营，其中有美国侵略军十个营。
    新华社二十日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量二十日凌晨袭击了美国第一骑兵师安溪基地的机场。
    据西方通讯社报道，解放武装力量在凌晨二时用迫击炮进行轰击。解放军战士在炮火的掩护下，迅速越过敌人的重重防御设施，进入机场，用炸药包爆炸了停放在机场上的美国飞机，然后安全撤退。
    这是解放武装力量今年二月份以来第二次袭击美国第一骑兵师在安溪的巢穴。二月二十日凌晨解放武装力量第一次袭击美军这个基地时，一举歼灭敌人四百九十七名，炸毁各种飞机九十七架。
    新华社河内十九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方解放武装力量最近在槟椥省和同塔梅地区歼灭敌人五百五十多名。
    报道说，四月十二日深夜，解放武装力量袭击了位于槟椥市东面一公里半的伪军建和军事训练中心。解放武装力量在人民的协助下，发起突然攻击，迅速地摧毁了这个伪军训练中心，打死敌人两百多名，俘虏近百名，缴获两百多件武器，六吨弹药和大批军用物资，并且烧毁这个训练中心的所有仓库和兵营。
    这家通讯社还报道，同塔梅地区的解放武装力量四月九日夜间，袭击了驻在宣仁县盛福乡的伪军，全歼敌人五个排，打死一百五十名，缴获全部武器和军用物资。第二天上午，解放武装力量又截击前来收拾伪军尸体的一支敌军，打死敌人一百多名，击落直升飞机一架。
    据新华社河内十八日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道，位于湄公河三角洲南端的越南南方南部西区各省军民在今年头两个月中，不断袭击敌占区的城镇和向“战略村”的守敌发动进攻，取得了很大胜利。
    据不完全统计，从一月初到二月二十二日，解放武装力量先后对芹苴、金瓯、迪石、茶荣、永隆等城市和其它许多市镇发动了二十七次进攻，共打死打伤敌人一千二百多名，其中有美国侵略军四百五十六名，击毁击伤敌机近八十架，炸毁军火库四座、油库六座、雷达站两个、美军宿舍二十所以及许多军用仓库和伪政权机关，炸毁敌人军车三十辆、一○五毫米口径大炮两门，击沉击伤敌舰各一艘。
    在这个期间，南部西区军民打了许多次漂亮的奇袭战。（附图片）
