Title: Chairman Zhu De and other leaders visit the Iraqi Embassy to offer condolences on the death of President Arif

Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 16th - Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuzhi, and others, went to the Iraqi Embassy in China this morning to offer condolences on the death of Abdul Salam Mohammed Arif, President of the Republic of Iraq.
    In the hall of the Iraqi Embassy in China was placed a portrait of President Arif, and in front of the portrait were wreaths sent by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi. Accompanying the state leaders to the Iraqi Embassy to offer condolences were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and other responsible persons from relevant departments including Fu Shenglin, Song Yiping, Yue Songsheng, Luo Jun, and Liu Pinyi.

### 朱德委员长等领导人  前往伊拉克使馆吊唁阿里夫总统逝世

1966-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长等领导人
    前往伊拉克使馆吊唁阿里夫总统逝世
    新华社十六日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，副委员长郭沫若，国务院副总理谢富治等，今天上午前往伊拉克驻中国大使馆，吊唁伊拉克共和国总统阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·穆罕默德·阿里夫逝世。
    伊拉克驻中国大使馆大厅里放着阿里夫总统的遗象，遗象前摆着刘少奇主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长送的花圈。同国家领导人一起前往伊拉克大使馆吊唁的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及其他有关方面的负责人傅生麟、宋一平、乐松生、罗俊、刘品一等。
