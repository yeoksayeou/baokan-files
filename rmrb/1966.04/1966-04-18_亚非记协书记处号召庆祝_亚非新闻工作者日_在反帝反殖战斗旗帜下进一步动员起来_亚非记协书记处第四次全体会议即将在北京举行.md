Title: AASJ Secretariat Calls for Celebration of “Asian-African Journalists' Day” Further Mobilize Under the Banner of Anti-Imperialist and Anti-Colonialist Struggle AASJ Secretariat to Hold Fourth Plenary Meeting in Beijing

Author:
Date: 1966-04-18
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. The Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association issued a call on April 17th to celebrate "Asian-African Journalists' Day." The full text is as follows:
    "Asian-African Journalists' Day" – April 24th is approaching. This is the anniversary of the historic First Asian-African Journalists Conference held in Jakarta in 1963 by journalists from forty-seven Asian and African countries and regions, and also the anniversary of the closing of the historic Bandung Conference in 1955 and the birth of the guiding principles of the Asian-African movement—the Bandung Spirit. The First Asian-African Journalists Conference made great contributions to promoting the struggle of Asian-African journalists against imperialism and neo-colonialism and their militant unity, and adopted the famous 1963 "Jakarta Declaration."
    This year, the struggle of the Asian-African people against imperialism headed by the United States has reached an unprecedented upsurge. We believe that widely carrying out celebrations of "Asian-African Journalists' Day" is of great significance for consolidating and expanding our ranks and inspiring the Asian-African people who are waging struggles against imperialist aggressors.
    The Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association will hold its (expanded) Fourth Plenary Meeting of the Secretariat in Beijing starting on April 20th, and will celebrate this "Asian-African Journalists' Day" at the close of the meeting.
  The Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association calls on all Asian-African journalist colleagues and organizations who are firmly united under the banner of the 1963 "Jakarta Declaration" to celebrate this unforgettable day through statements, rallies, seminars, articles, or other possible methods, and to further mobilize Asian-African journalists under the fighting banner of anti-imperialism and anti-colonialism. We would be very grateful if you could inform us of the celebrations of "Asian-African Journalists' Day" in your country, so that we can include these activities in our various publications.

### 亚非记协书记处号召庆祝“亚非新闻工作者日”  在反帝反殖战斗旗帜下进一步动员起来  亚非记协书记处第四次全体会议即将在北京举行

1966-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处号召庆祝“亚非新闻工作者日”
    在反帝反殖战斗旗帜下进一步动员起来
    亚非记协书记处第四次全体会议即将在北京举行
    新华社十七日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处四月十七日发出庆祝“亚非新闻工作者日”的号召，全文如下：
    “亚非新闻工作者日”——四月二十四日就要到来了。这是来自四十七个亚非国家和地区的新闻工作者于一九六三年在雅加达举行历史性的第一次亚非新闻工作者会议的日子，也是一九五五年历史性的万隆会议闭幕和亚非运动的指导原则——万隆精神诞生的日子。第一次亚非新闻工作者会议在促进亚非新闻工作者反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争和他们的战斗团结方面，作出了伟大的贡献，并通过了著名的一九六三年“雅加达宣言”。
    今年，亚非人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义的斗争已达到空前的高涨。我们认为，广泛开展庆祝“亚非新闻工作者日”对巩固和扩大我们的队伍、鼓舞正在进行反对帝国主义侵略者的斗争的亚非人民，是有重要意义的。
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处将在四月二十日开始在北京举行（扩大的）书记处第四次全体会议，在会议闭幕的时候将庆祝这个“亚非新闻工作者日”。
  亚非新闻工作者协会书记处号召所有坚决团结在一九六三年“雅加达宣言”的旗帜下的亚非新闻工作者同业和组织，通过声明、集会、座谈、文章或其他可能的方式方法，来庆祝这个难忘的日子，在反帝反殖的战斗旗帜下把亚非新闻工作者进一步动员起来。如果你们告诉我们在你们国家里“亚非新闻工作者日”的庆祝活动，使我们能够把这些活动写进我们的各种出版物里，我们将非常感激。
