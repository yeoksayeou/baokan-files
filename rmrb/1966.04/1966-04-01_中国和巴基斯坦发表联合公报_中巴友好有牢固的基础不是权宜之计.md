Title: China and Pakistan Issue Joint Communiqué: Sino-Pakistani Friendship Has a Solid Foundation and Is Not a Stopgap Measure
Author:
Date: 1966-04-01
Edition: 1

    China and Pakistan Issue Joint Communiqué
    Sino-Pakistani Friendship Has a Solid Foundation and Is Not a Stopgap Measure
    Further Consolidating and Developing Sino-Pakistani Friendly Cooperation Conforms to the Common Aspirations of the Two Peoples and Is Conducive to Asian-African Solidarity and World Peace. The Kashmiri People's Right to Self-Determination Must Be Respected, and the Kashmir Dispute Should Be Resolved According to the Wishes of the Kashmiri People.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st—Joint Communiqué between the People's Republic of China and the Islamic Republic of Pakistan on Chairman Liu Shaoqi's State Visit to the Islamic Republic of Pakistan
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China and his wife, at the invitation of Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, President of Pakistan, paid a friendly state visit to Pakistan. They were accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, as well as other senior officials of the Chinese government.
    During the visit, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his entourage received an extremely warm welcome and grand reception from the Pakistani government and people everywhere, demonstrating the sincere friendship of the Pakistani people towards the Chinese people. Chairman Liu Shaoqi expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Pakistani President, government, and people.
    The Chairman and the President frankly and cordially exchanged views on the current international situation and on issues of further developing friendly and cooperative relations between China and Pakistan. They expressed satisfaction with the results of the talks.
    The Chairman and the President emphasized that the Kashmiri people’s right to self-determination must be respected, and the Kashmir dispute should be resolved in accordance with the wishes of the Kashmiri people, as guaranteed by Pakistan and India to the Kashmiri people. The President reiterated that the Pakistani government will continue to support the Kashmiri people's struggle for the right to self-determination. The Chairman reiterated that the Chinese government and people firmly support the Pakistani government’s just stand on this dispute and the Kashmiri people’s just struggle for the right to self-determination.
    The President expressed the deep gratitude of the Pakistani government and people for the support given by the Chinese government and people during their resistance against aggression. The Chairman expressed admiration for the heroic spirit and patriotism demonstrated by the Pakistani people in their struggle against foreign aggression and interference. The Chairman reiterated that the Chinese government and people adhere to the principled stand of opposing aggression and supporting the victims of aggression. In the struggle of the Pakistani people to safeguard national independence and sovereignty and oppose aggression, the Chinese people unswervingly stand on the side of the Pakistani people.
    The President reiterated that the Pakistani government and people firmly believe that the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations should be restored, and any conspiracy to create "two Chinas" is bound to fail. The Chairman expressed his gratitude for this.
    The Chairman and the President expressed satisfaction with the development of friendly relations between the two countries. They emphasized that Sino-Pakistani friendship is not a stopgap measure but has a solid foundation. The peoples of the two countries have forged a deep friendship through mutual sympathy and support in the struggle against aggression. Both sides are determined to further consolidate and develop friendly and cooperative relations between China and Pakistan, and believe that doing so is in line with the common aspirations of the two peoples, conducive to Asian-African solidarity and world peace.
                              March 31, 1966

### 中国和巴基斯坦发表联合公报  中巴友好有牢固的基础不是权宜之计

1966-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国和巴基斯坦发表联合公报
    中巴友好有牢固的基础不是权宜之计
    进一步巩固和发展中巴友好合作，符合两国人民共同愿望，有利亚非团结和世界和平克什米尔人民自决权利必须受到尊重，克什米尔争端应当按照克什米尔人民愿望解决
    新华社三十一日讯　中华人民共和国和巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国就刘少奇主席在巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国进行国事访问的联合公报
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人，应巴基斯坦总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅的邀请，到巴基斯坦进行了友好的国事访问。陪同访问的有陈毅副总理和夫人以及中国政府的其他高级官员。
    刘少奇主席和他的随行人员，在访问期间，到处受到了巴基斯坦政府和人民十分热烈的欢迎和隆重的接待，这表现了巴基斯坦人民对中国人民的真挚的友谊。刘少奇主席向巴基斯坦总统、政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。
    主席和总统就当前的国际形势和进一步发展中巴两国友好合作关系的问题，坦率而友好地交换了意见。他们对会谈的结果表示满意。
    主席和总统强调，克什米尔人民的自决权利必须受到尊重，克什米尔争端应该同巴基斯坦和印度向克什米尔人民所保证的那样，按照克什米尔人民的愿望加以解决。总统重申，巴基斯坦政府将继续支持克什米尔人民争取自决权的斗争。主席重申，中国政府和人民坚决支持巴基斯坦政府对于这个争端的严正立场和克什米尔人民争取自决权的正义斗争。
    总统表达了巴基斯坦政府和人民的深切谢意，感谢中国政府和人民在他们反抗侵略的时候所给予的支持。主席对巴基斯坦人民在反对外来侵略和干涉的斗争中所表现的英勇气概和爱国主义精神，表示钦佩。主席重申，中国政府和人民坚持反对侵略、支援被侵略者的原则立场，在巴基斯坦人民维护民族独立和主权，反对侵略的斗争中，中国人民毫不动摇地站在巴基斯坦人民一边。
    总统重申，巴基斯坦政府和人民坚信，中华人民共和国在联合国的合法权利应当恢复，任何制造“两个中国”的阴谋都是一定要失败的。主席对此表示感谢。
    主席和总统对两国友好关系的发展表示满意。他们强调，中巴友好不是权宜之计，是有牢固基础的。两国人民在反对侵略的斗争中互相同情，互相支持，结成了深厚的友谊。双方决心进一步巩固和发展中巴友好合作关系，并且认为，这样做是符合两国人民的共同愿望、有利于亚非团结和世界和平的。
                              一九六六年三月三十一日
