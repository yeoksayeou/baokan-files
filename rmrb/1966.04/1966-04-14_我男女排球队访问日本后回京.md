Title: Our Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Return to Beijing After Visiting Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 13th. The Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams, a group of thirty people led by Zhang Zhihuai, Chairman of the Chinese Volleyball Association, returned to Beijing on the 11th after concluding their visit to Japan.

### 我男女排球队访问日本后回京

1966-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我男女排球队访问日本后回京
    新华社十三日讯　中国男、女排球队一行三十人结束了在日本的访问后，十一日在中国排球协会主席张之槐率领下回到北京。
