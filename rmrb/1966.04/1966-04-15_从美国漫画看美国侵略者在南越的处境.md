Title:  American Cartoons Reflect the Plight of American Aggressors in South Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-15
Edition: 3

    From American Cartoons Reflecting the Situation of American Aggressors in South Vietnam
    "A crisis with more political significance than military significance is causing the war here (South Vietnam) to suffer another setback. The war, in which the Americans are deeply mired, is developing towards one crisis after another. And each crisis is more serious than the previous one." This is how the pre-published April 18 issue of "U.S. News & World Report" depicts the escalating infighting among the South Vietnamese puppets and the predicament of American aggressors in South Vietnam. Some American cartoons also depict the increasingly difficult situation the United States is facing.
    Bottom left illustration: A cartoon published in the American "Nashville Tennessean." The title is "Fighting Against the Tide." The image is of a gun-toting "American" deeply mired in a quagmire. The words on the innermost vortex are "Shaky Government" (referring to the South Vietnamese puppet regime), the middle layer is "Demonstrators" (referring to demonstrating masses in the South Vietnamese enemy-occupied areas), and the outermost layer is "Viet Cong" (referring to the South Vietnamese People's Armed Forces).
    Bottom right illustration: A cartoon published in the American "New York Herald Tribune." American soldiers are blowing soap bubbles of "Military Victory." The words on the soap dish are "Vietnam," the words on the soap bubble are "Military Victory," and the words in the bursting bubble are "Domestic Confusion."
          (Fan)

### 从美国漫画看美国侵略者在南越的处境

1966-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    从美国漫画看美国侵略者在南越的处境
    “一个政治意义超过军事意义的危机，使这里（南越）的战争再一次遭受挫折。美国人深深地陷入的这场战争，朝着一个又一个的危机发展。而每一次危机都比前一次更为严重”。这是提前出版四月十八日一期的《美国新闻与世界报道》对当前南越傀儡内部争权夺利的内讧愈演愈烈的形势下、美国侵略者在南越处境的描绘。一些美国漫画也勾划了美国日益陷入困境的情况。
    下左图：美国《纳什维尔田纳西人报》刊登的一幅漫画。题目是《逆流而战》。图中的形象是一个持枪的“美国佬”深深地陷入泥淖。最内一层漩涡上的字样是“摇摇晃晃的政府”（指南越傀儡政权），中间一层是“示威者”（指南越敌占区示威群众），最外一层是“越共”（指南越人民武装）。
    下右图：美国《纽约先驱论坛报》发表的一幅漫画。美国军人正在吹“军事胜利”的肥皂泡。肥皂缸上的字样是“越南”，肥皂泡上的字样是“军事胜利”，破裂的泡沫里有“内政混乱”的字样。　　
          （凡）
