Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Receives British Journalists
Author:
Date: 1966-04-14
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 13th – Vice Chairman Dong Biwu received British freelance journalist Mr. and Mrs. Guilder this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with them. (Picture attached)
