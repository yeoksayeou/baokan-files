Title: Pakistani Minister of Information and Broadcasting Condemns Indian Expansionism: If India Attacks Pakistan Again, It Will Suffer a Miserable Defeat

Author:

Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 18th—According to a report in the "Pakistan Times" on the 18th, Pakistani Minister of Information and Broadcasting Khawaja Shahabuddin stated in Hyderabad on the 17th that India's expansionist policies will prove disastrous for itself.
    The minister said that India's recent attack on Pakistan exposed its wicked conspiracy of expansionism, demonstrating that its claims of peaceful coexistence are false. He said that the whole world generally recognizes that India is trying its best to seize the territory of its neighbors.
    The minister made these remarks to members of the Hyderabad Institute of International Relations on the afternoon of the 17th. He said that the whole world saw India invade Pakistan, using all the weapons it obtained from the world's two major blocs to attack us.
    He warned India that if India repeats its mistake and attacks Pakistan again, it will suffer an even more disastrous defeat at the hands of the resolute Pakistani people. The Pakistani people have clearly demonstrated that they consider no sacrifice too great in order to safeguard Pakistan's territorial integrity and sovereignty.

### 巴基斯坦新闻和广播部长谴责印度扩张主义  印度如再次进攻巴基斯坦，必遭惨败

1966-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新闻和广播部长谴责印度扩张主义
    印度如再次进攻巴基斯坦，必遭惨败
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十八日电　据《巴基斯坦时报》十八日报道，巴基斯坦新闻和广播部长哈瓦贾·夏哈布丁十七日在海得拉巴说，印度的扩张主义政策将证明对它自己是灾难性的。
    这位部长说，印度最近攻击巴基斯坦，暴露了它的扩张主义的邪恶阴谋，说明它的和平共处的说法是假的。他说，全世界普遍都认识到，印度竭力想夺取它的邻国的领土。
    这位部长是十七日下午在海得拉巴国际关系学会向会员们讲这番话的。他说，全世界都看到印度入侵巴基斯坦，利用从世界两大集团获得的所有武器来进攻我们。
    他警告印度说，如果印度重蹈覆辙，又来进攻巴基斯坦，它将在坚决的巴基斯坦人民手中遭到更惨重的失败。巴基斯坦人民已明确证明，他们为了维护巴基斯坦的领土完整和主权，认为任何牺牲都不算大。
