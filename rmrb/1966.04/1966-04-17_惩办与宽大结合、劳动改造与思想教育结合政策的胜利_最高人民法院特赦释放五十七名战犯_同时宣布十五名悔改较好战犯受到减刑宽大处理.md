Title: Victory of the Policy of Combining Punishment with Leniency and Combining Labor Reform with Ideological Education: The Supreme People's Court Grants Amnesty and Releases Fifty-Seven War Criminals; Simultaneously Announces Fifteen War Criminals Who Have Shown Relatively Good Remorse Receive Commutation and Leniency

Author:
Date: 1966-04-17
Edition: 2

    Victory of the Policy of Combining Punishment with Leniency and Combining Labor Reform with Ideological Education
    The Supreme People's Court Grants Amnesty and Releases Fifty-Seven War Criminals
    Simultaneously Announces Fifteen War Criminals Who Have Shown Relatively Good Remorse Receive Commutation and Leniency
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th – According to the amnesty order of the President of the People's Republic of China, the Supreme People's Court today granted amnesty and released fifty-seven war criminals who have indeed turned over a new leaf.
    This is the sixth group of war criminals to be released under amnesty. The fact that these war criminals have been able to turn over a new leaf demonstrates the victory of the policy implemented by the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government of combining punishment with leniency and combining labor reform with ideological education.
    Among the war criminals released under amnesty today, fifty-two belong to the Chiang Kai-shek Group, including: Fang Jing, Lieutenant General and Commander of the Nationalist 79th Army; Yang Guangyu, Lieutenant General and Deputy Commander of the Nationalist 3rd Army; Kong Qinggui, Lieutenant General and Commander of the Nationalist Jiangyin Fortress; Li Peiqing, Secretary-General of the Nationalist Henan Provincial Party Committee; Xu Zhongrui, Lieutenant General Senior Advisor of the Nationalist Sichuan-Hubei Border Pacification Headquarters; Yang Anming, Lieutenant General Staff Member of the Nationalist Ministry of National Defense; Mou Zhongheng, Lieutenant General Deputy Commander of the Nationalist Second Pacification Zone Headquarters; Pan Chengqing, Deputy Director of the Nationalist Secret Service Bureau's Guizhou Station, and others.
    There are four war criminals belonging to the puppet Manchuria: Ganzhuerzhabu, Lieutenant General Commander of the 9th Military District of the puppet Manchuria; Cao Bingsen, Lieutenant General Commander of the Jiangshang Army of the puppet Manchuria; Yu Jingyuan, Minister of Economy of the puppet Manchuria; Yu Jingtao, Minister of Labor of the puppet Manchuria.
    There is one war criminal from the puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government, namely Bao Guiting, Major General Commander of the Puppet Mongolian Army Headquarters.
    The amnesty of this group of war criminals was announced by the Supreme People's Court and the Higher People's Courts of relevant regions at amnesty and release conferences held today at war criminal management facilities in various locations. The list of war criminals released under amnesty this time was read out at the meeting, and amnesty notices were issued to them.
    At the amnesty and release conferences in various locations, the Supreme People's Court and the Higher People's Courts of relevant regions also announced the list of a group of war criminals who had shown relatively good remorse and whose sentences had been commuted. A total of fifteen war criminals received leniency through commutation of sentences this time.

### 惩办与宽大结合、劳动改造与思想教育结合政策的胜利  最高人民法院特赦释放五十七名战犯  同时宣布十五名悔改较好战犯受到减刑宽大处理

1966-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

    惩办与宽大结合、劳动改造与思想教育结合政策的胜利
    最高人民法院特赦释放五十七名战犯
    同时宣布十五名悔改较好战犯受到减刑宽大处理
    新华社十六日讯　最高人民法院根据中华人民共和国主席特赦令，今天特赦释放了五十七名已经确实改恶从善的战争罪犯。
    这是特赦释放的第六批战争罪犯。这些战争罪犯能够改恶从善，显示了中国共产党和人民政府实行的惩办与宽大相结合、劳动改造与思想教育相结合的政策的胜利。
    今天特赦释放的战争罪犯中，属于蒋介石集团的战争罪犯有五十二名，其中有：国民党第七十九军中将军长方靖、国民党第三军中将副军长杨光钰、国民党江阴要塞中将司令孔庆桂、国民党河南省党部书记长李佩青、国民党川鄂边区绥靖公署中将高参徐钟瑞、国民党国防部中将部员杨安铭、国民党第二绥靖区司令部中将副司令官牟中珩、国民党特务机关保密局贵州站副站长潘澄清等。
    属于伪满洲国的战争罪犯有四名：伪满洲国第九军管区中将司令官甘珠尔扎布、伪满洲国江上军中将司令官曹秉森、伪满洲国经济部大臣于静远、伪满洲国勤劳部大臣于镜涛。
    有一名是伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯，即伪蒙古军总司令部少将司令官宝贵廷。
    这一批战争罪犯的特赦，是最高人民法院和有关地区的高级人民法院今天分别在各地战犯管理所召开的特赦释放大会上宣布的。会上宣读了这次特赦释放的战争罪犯名单，并发给他们特赦通知书。
    在各地的特赦释放大会上，最高人民法院和有关地区高级人民法院还同时宣布一批悔改较好的被减刑的战争罪犯名单，这次受到减刑宽大处理的战争罪犯共有十五名。
