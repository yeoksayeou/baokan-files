Title: Students in Turkey Give War Plague a Head-On Blow, Demonstrations Oppose Rusk's Activities in Turkey
Author: 
Date: 1966-04-21
Edition: 4

    Giving the War Plague a Head-On Blow
    Turkish Students Demonstrate Against Rusk's Activities in Turkey
    (Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, 20th) When U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk arrived in Ankara, the Turkish capital, on the 19th, Turkish students staged an anti-American demonstration, giving Rusk a blow to the head. Students marched in demonstration carrying signs that read: "Turkey is not Johnson's ranch," "Yankees go home."
    After Rusk got off the plane, he avoided the area of student demonstrations and detoured into the city of Ankara. Subsequently, a group of demonstrators carrying signs went to the U.S. Embassy to demonstrate. Rusk came to Ankara to attend the Ministerial Council meeting of the Central Treaty Organization.

### 给战争瘟神当头棒喝  土耳其学生示威反对腊斯克到土活动

1966-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    给战争瘟神当头棒喝
    土耳其学生示威反对腊斯克到土活动
    据新华社二十日讯　安卡拉消息：当美国国务卿腊斯克十九日到达土耳其首都安卡拉的时候，土耳其学生举行了一次反美示威，给腊斯克当头一棒。学生们举着标语牌示威游行，标语牌上写着：“土耳其不是约翰逊的牧场”，“美国佬滚回去”。
    腊斯克下飞机后，避开了学生示威的地区，绕道进入安卡拉市。接着，一群示威者举着标语牌前往美国大使馆示威。腊斯克是来安卡拉参加中央条约组织部长理事会会议的。
