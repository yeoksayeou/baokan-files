Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Djawoto and Representatives of News Media from Various Countries
Author: Yang Baokun
Date: 1966-04-25
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received this afternoon Djawoto, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, and the representatives from nineteen countries and regions attending the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, including Algeria, Azania (South Africa), Cambodia, Ceylon, China, Congo (Léopoldville), Guinea, Indonesia, Japan, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mali, Mozambique, Palestine, Syria, Tanzania, United Arab Republic, Democratic Republic of Vietnam, South Vietnam, and Zimbabwe.

    Also received were students from Congo (Brazzaville) and Tanzania studying at the Afro-Asian Journalists' Training Class, as well as the Algerian Journalists' Delegation and the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation currently visiting China.

    Chairman Zhu De held cordial and friendly talks with them.

    Present at the reception were Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee; and leading members of relevant departments Mei Yi, Wang Yunsheng, Fan Jin, Chu Tunan, Wu Defeng, Yang Shuo, Liu Baiyu, Mao Yisheng, Cao Mengjun, Wang Zhijie, Fu Dexi, and Zhang Mingqing. ### 朱德委员长接见查禾多和各国新闻界代表
杨宝坤
1966-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长接见查禾多和各国新闻界代表
    新华社二十四日讯　人大常委会委员长朱德今天下午接见了亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多和出席亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）的阿尔及利亚、阿扎尼亚（南非）、柬埔寨、锡兰、中国、刚果（利）、几内亚、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、马里、莫三鼻给、巴勒斯坦、叙利亚、坦桑尼亚、阿联、越南民主共和国、越南南方、津巴布韦等十九个国家和地区的代表。
    同时被接见的，还有亚非新闻工作者进修班的刚果（布）、坦桑尼亚的学员，以及正在我国访问的阿尔及利亚新闻工作者代表团和老挝爱国力量新闻代表团。
    朱德委员长同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有人大常委会副委员长刘宁一，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，以及有关方面负责人梅益、王芸生、范瑾、楚图南、吴德峰、杨朔、刘白羽、茅以升、曹孟君、王志杰、傅德喜、张明卿等。（附图片）
