    Hansen
    Hansen, who has once again become the Prime Minister of Denmark, is 51 years old. He began engaging in youth movements early in his life. In 1929, he served as the secretary of the Danish Social Democratic Youth Organization, and from 1933 to 1937, he was the chairman of this organization. From 1938 to 1939, he also served as the chairman of the Youth Committee of the Danish Ministry of Social Affairs.
    From 1937 to 1939, he held important positions in the Danish trade union organizations and the Danish Social Democratic Party organization. From 1939 to 1941 and from 1945 to 1955, he was twice elected as the secretary of the Social Democratic Party.
    After the war, Hansen held important positions in the Danish government several times, serving as Minister of Finance, Minister of Trade, and Minister of Foreign Affairs. From 1955, he served as Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs. In March of last year, he led a Danish government delegation to visit the Soviet Union. The joint communique of the two countries stated that they would further develop the existing good-neighborly relations between the two countries.

### 汉森

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：人物介绍

    汉森
    再度出任丹麦首相的汉森现年五十一岁。他早年就开始从事青年运动。1929年担任丹麦社会民主青年组织的书记，1933—1937年任这个组织的主席。1938—1939年，他还做过丹麦社会事务部青年委员会主席。
    1937—1939年，他在丹麦的工会组织和丹麦社会民主党组织中担任要职。1939—1941年以及1945—1955年期间，他曾两度被选为社会民主党的书记。
    战后，汉森在丹麦政府内数度担任重要职务，历任财政大臣、贸易大臣和外交大臣等职。1955年起，他任首相兼外交大臣。去年3月，他曾经率领丹麦政府代表团访问苏联，两国会谈公报表示要进一步发展两国之间现有的善邻关系。
