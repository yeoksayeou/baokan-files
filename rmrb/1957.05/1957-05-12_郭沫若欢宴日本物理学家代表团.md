Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for the Japanese Physicists Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel this evening to welcome the visiting Japanese Physicists Delegation.

This afternoon, Guo Moruo received the delegation members at the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若欢宴日本物理学家代表团

1957-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴日本物理学家代表团
    据新华社11日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚在北京饭店举行宴会，欢迎日本物理学家访华代表团。
    今日下午郭沫若在中国科学院接待了代表团人员。
