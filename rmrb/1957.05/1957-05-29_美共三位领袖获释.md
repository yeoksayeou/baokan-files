Title: Three US Communist Leaders Released
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th, TASS, New York, May 27th: The "Daily Worker" reported that three American Communist leaders - Elizabeth Gurley Flynn, Alexander Bittelman, and Louis Weinstock have been released. They were sentenced to three years imprisonment in 1955 under the notorious Smith Act.
    Currently, three other imprisoned leaders of the American Communist Party - Gilbert Green, Robert Thompson, and Henry Winston remain in prison.

### 美共三位领袖获释

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美共三位领袖获释
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社纽约27日讯：美国“工人日报”报道，三位美国共产党领袖——伊丽沙白·葛莱·弗林、亚历山大·毕特尔曼和路易斯·温斯托克已经获释。他们是在1955年根据臭名远扬的史密斯法被判三年徒刑的。
    现在，美国共产党的另外三位被监禁的领袖——吉伯特·格林、罗伯特·汤姆逊和亨利·涅斯顿仍然在狱中。
