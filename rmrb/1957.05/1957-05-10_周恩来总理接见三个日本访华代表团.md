Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Three Japanese Visiting Delegations

Author:

Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese Archaeological Delegation, the Japanese Fukuoka Prefecture Friendship Delegation, and the Japanese Drama Delegation in Beijing today.

Those present at the reception included Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Hansheng, Vice President, and Xia Nai, Deputy Director of the Institute of Archaeology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others.

### 周恩来总理接见三个日本访华代表团

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见三个日本访华代表团
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理今天在北京接见日本考古代表团、日本福冈县友好访华代表团和日本戏剧访华团。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院院长郭沫若，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南和副会长阳翰笙，中国科学院考古研究所副所长夏鼐等人。
