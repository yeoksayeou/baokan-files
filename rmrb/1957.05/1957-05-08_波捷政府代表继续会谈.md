Title: Polish and Czechoslovak Government Representatives Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. The Polish government delegation headed by Cyrankiewicz returned to Prague on the 6th after visiting various parts of Czechoslovakia and continued talks with the Czechoslovak government delegation.

### 波捷政府代表继续会谈

1957-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    波捷政府代表继续会谈
    据新华社布拉格7日电　以西伦凯维兹为首的波兰政府代表团在捷克斯洛伐克各地访问后，在6日回到了布拉格，并继续和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团举行会谈。
