Title: Indian Parliament's Lok Sabha Unanimously Passes Resolution Calling on Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union to Halt Nuclear Weapons Testing; International Organization of Journalists Calls on Journalists to Protest British Hydrogen Bomb Test
Author:
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 23rd: The Indian Parliament's Lok Sabha unanimously passed a resolution on the evening of May 22nd, calling on Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union to immediately halt atomic and thermo-atomic weapons testing, thereby "easing tensions and paving the way for disarmament, international cooperation, and progress towards peace."

The resolution expressed opposition to suggestions regarding so-called limited testing and registered testing, as it "would not help to abolish such weapons of mass destruction." The resolution believes that any or all countries possessing atomic weapons proposing an initiative to halt such test explosions would "greatly help to rid the world of the psychosis that is causing the current arms race."

During the discussion in the Lok Sabha on May 22nd, members put forward nineteen amendments. Nearly half of the amendments called for convening an Asian-African conference and an international conference to oppose atomic testing.

The Lok Sabha's resolution reflects the urgent demands of the Indian people. Since Britain's test explosion on Christmas Island, many political parties and groups have issued urgent appeals. Peace leaders from all over the country are holding meetings in Bangalore, expressing their opposition to atomic testing.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 22nd: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Secretariat of the International Organization of Journalists issued an appeal on May 21st, calling on journalists around the world to rise up and protest against Britain's nuclear weapons testing.

The appeal calls on all upright journalists around the world to tell readers about the real dangers contained in atomic and hydrogen bomb tests, to issue voices protesting these tests, to condemn those who, like some irresponsible journalists, try to treat atomic bombs as some kind of safe and harmless toy, and to condemn those who paralyze the vigilance of the people of all countries.

### 印度国会人民院一致通过决议  呼吁英美苏停止核武器试验  国际记协号召新闻工作者抗议英国试验氢弹

1957-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    印度国会人民院一致通过决议
    呼吁英美苏停止核武器试验
    国际记协号召新闻工作者抗议英国试验氢弹
    据新华社新德里23日电　印度国会人民院5月22日晚间一致通过了一项决议，呼吁英国、美国和苏联立即停止原子核和热原子核试验，从而“为缓和紧张局势，为向裁军、国际合作与和平前进开辟道路”。
    决议表示反对关于所谓限制试验和登记试验的建议，因为它“无助于废弃这种大规模毁灭性武器”。决议认为，任何或者所有拥有原子核武器的国家提出关于停止这种试验爆炸的倡议，都将会“大大有助于使全世界摆脱那种引起目前的军备竞赛的恐惧心理”。
    人民院5月22日在讨论的过程中，议员们提出了十九项修正案。将近一半的修正案要求召开亚非国家会议和国际会议来反对原子核试验。
    人民院的决议反映了印度人民的迫切要求。自从英国在圣诞岛进行试验爆炸以来许多政党和团体都发出了紧急呼吁。来自全国各地的和平领袖正在班加罗尔举行会议，表示反对原子核试验。
    据新华社布拉格22日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，国际新闻工作者协会秘书处5月21日发表呼吁书，号召全世界的新闻工作者起来抗议英国进行原子核武器试验。
    呼吁书号召全世界所有正直的新闻工作者，把原子弹和氢弹试验所包含的真正危险告诉读者，发出抗议这些试验的呼声，谴责像某些不负责任的新闻工作者那样企图把原子弹当成某种安全无害的玩具的人，谴责麻痹各国人民警惕性的那些人。
