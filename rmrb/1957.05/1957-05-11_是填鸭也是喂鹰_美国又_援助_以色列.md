Title: Both Cramming and Feeding Hawks: The United States "Aids" Israel Again
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 6

    Both Cramming and Feeding Hawks
    The United States "Aids" Israel Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 10th: The United States government announced on the 8th that it would sell $12.5 million worth of wheat to Israel as part of its "aid" to Israel. The wheat will be paid for in Israeli currency, which will then be loaned back to Israel for "economic development" plans.

### 是填鸭也是喂鹰  美国又“援助”以色列

1957-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    是填鸭也是喂鹰
    美国又“援助”以色列
    据新华社10日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府8日宣布售予以色列一千二百五十万美元的小麦，作为美国对以色列“援助”的一部分。小麦的货款用以色列货币支付，这笔款子将再借给以色列作为“经济发展”计划之用。
