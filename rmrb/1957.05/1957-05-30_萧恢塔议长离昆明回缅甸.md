Title: Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik Leaves Kunming for Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, May 29th. Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma and former President Sao Shwe Thaik and his wife, along with a party of fifteen, who came to our country for a visit at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, left Kunming by special plane this morning to return to their country.

During his more than fifty days in China, Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik visited more than ten cities, including Chongqing, Wuhan, Hangzhou, Beijing, Shenyang, and Anshan, traveling approximately 25,000 li, and received a warm welcome from governments and people in various places.

### 萧恢塔议长离昆明回缅甸

1957-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    萧恢塔议长离昆明回缅甸
    据新华社昆明29日电　应全国人民代表大会常务委员会的邀请来我国访问的缅甸联邦民族院议长、前总统萧恢塔和夫人等一行十五人在今天上午乘专机由昆明返国。
    萧恢塔议长在中国的五十多天中，曾先后参观访问了重庆、武汉、杭州、北京、沈阳、鞍山等十多个城市，行程约两万五千华里，受到了各地政府和人民的热情欢迎。
