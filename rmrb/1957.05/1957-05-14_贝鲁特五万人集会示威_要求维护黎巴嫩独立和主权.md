Title: Beirut Rally of 50,000 Demands the Maintenance of Lebanese Independence and Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: A mass rally of 50,000 people was held in Beirut on May 12th by the "National Front," composed of Lebanese opposition parties. The meeting passed a resolution demanding the maintenance of Lebanese independence and sovereignty; re-implementation of a policy of close cooperation with independent Arab countries; declaration of a policy of active neutrality; rejection of foreign military bases on Lebanese territory; rejection of participation in the Baghdad Pact and other military blocs, and rejection of any foreign aid detrimental to Lebanese freedom, independence, and sovereignty; and the establishment of a representative new government for parliamentary elections.

After the meeting, a demonstration was held, with the masses chanting the following slogans: "Down with the Eisenhower Doctrine!" "Down with the Baghdad Pact!" "Long live close cooperation between all Arab countries!" "Long live the united National Front!"

### 贝鲁特五万人集会示威  要求维护黎巴嫩独立和主权

1957-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特五万人集会示威
    要求维护黎巴嫩独立和主权
    据新华社13日讯　由黎巴嫩反对党组成的“民族阵线”5月12日在贝鲁特举行了一个有五万人参加的群众集会，会上通过决议要求维护黎巴嫩的独立和主权；重新奉行同独立的阿拉伯国家紧密合作的政策；宣布执行积极中立政策；拒绝让外国在黎巴嫩领土上建立军事基地；拒绝参加巴格达条约和其他军事集团并且拒绝接受有损黎巴嫩自由、独立和主权的任何外援；建立一个有代表性的新政府以便进行议会选举。
    会后，举行了示威游行，群众高喊着下面的口号：“打倒艾森豪威尔主义！”“打倒巴格达条约！”“一切阿拉伯国家紧密合作万岁！”“统一民族阵线万岁！”
