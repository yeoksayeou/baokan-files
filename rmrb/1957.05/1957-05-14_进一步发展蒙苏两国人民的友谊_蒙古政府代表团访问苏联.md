Title: Further Development of the Friendship Between the Mongolian and Soviet Peoples: Mongolian Government Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 5

Comprehensive report from our newspaper: According to Xinhua News Agency, at the invitation of the Soviet government, a government delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsedenbal, departed Ulaanbaatar on the morning of May 10th aboard a "Tu-104" jet airliner for a friendly visit to Moscow. They arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of the 10th.

	Tsedenbal delivered a short speech at the airport. He said that the Mongolian and Soviet peoples have a deep friendship. The Mongolian people have always regarded the Soviet Union as a loyal friend and comrade-in-arms. We believe that developing Mongolian-Soviet friendship and strengthening the unity of the socialist camp is a sacred duty.

	On the 11th, Soviet government delegation and the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic began talks in the Kremlin.

	On the same day, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Bulganin, and Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet Korotchenko separately received the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic led by Tsedenbal. Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko also received the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Ajilbish, on this day.

	On the 11th, the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic left Moscow by special train to visit Leningrad, and returned to Moscow on the 13th.

### 进一步发展蒙苏两国人民的友谊  蒙古政府代表团访问苏联

1957-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步发展蒙苏两国人民的友谊
    蒙古政府代表团访问苏联
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：蒙古人民共和国政府代表团应苏联政府的邀请，在部长会议主席泽登巴尔的率领下，于10日上午乘“图—104”喷气式客机离乌兰巴托前往莫斯科进行友好访问。10日下午抵达莫斯科。
    泽登巴尔在机场上发表了简短演说。他说，蒙苏两国人民有着深厚的友谊。蒙古人民一向把苏联看成是忠实的朋友和战友。我们认为，发展蒙苏友谊和加强社会主义阵营的团结是神圣的责任。
    11日苏联政府代表团和蒙古人民共和国政府代表团在克里姆林宫开始举行会谈。
    同一天，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和最高苏维埃主席团副主席科罗特钦科分别接见了以泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。苏联外交部长葛罗米柯也在这天接见了蒙古人民共和国外交部长阿吉尔毕希。
    11日，蒙古人民共和国政府代表团乘专车离莫斯科到列宁格勒访问，并于13日回到了莫斯科。
