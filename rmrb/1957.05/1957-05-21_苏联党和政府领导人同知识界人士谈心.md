Title: Soviet Party and Government Leaders Talk Heart-to-Heart with Intellectuals
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 20th: According to TASS, many Soviet intellectuals spent Sunday, May 19th, at a government villa near Moscow with Soviet government and Communist Party leaders.

These included writers, painters, sculptors, composers, and theater professionals. They were invited by the Soviet government and the Party Central Committee.

Among those receiving the guests were Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Suslov, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Shvernik, and the secretaries of the CPSU Central Committee.

There was a lively conversation between hosts and guests. Shepilov, Khrushchev, Mikoyan, and writers Fedin, Surkov, Sobolev, Simonov, Isakovsky, Kochuk, artist Ioganson, composer Khrennikov, and others, fifteen people in total, spoke in turn.

### 苏联党和政府领导人同知识界人士谈心

1957-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联党和政府领导人同知识界人士谈心
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联的许多知识界人士5月19日在莫斯科附近的政府别墅里，同苏联政府和共产党的领导人一起消度了一个星期天。
    这些人中有作家、画家、雕刻家、作曲家和戏剧界人士。他们是应苏联政府和党中央的邀请而来的。
