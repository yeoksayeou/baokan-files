Title: Mongolian Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Mongolian People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by Ts. Sürenjav, left Beijing for home this morning after a two-day visit to Beijing.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Kui Bi, as well as Zhang Hanfu, Li Zheren, Yang Xiandong, Zhang Zhixiang, Xiao Xiangrong, Zhang Youyu and others. Tseden-Ishi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 蒙古政府代表团离京回国

1957-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府代表团离京回国
    新华社8日讯　以齐·苏伦扎布为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，在北京作了两天访问后，已在今天清晨离北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有国务院副总理邓小平、李先念，内蒙古自治区副主席奎璧，以及章汉夫、李哲人、杨显东、张致祥、萧向荣、张友渔等人。蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办策登依什也到机场欢送。
