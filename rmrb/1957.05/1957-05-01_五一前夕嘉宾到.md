Title: Guests Arrive on the Eve of May Day
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 5
　　
    Thai Art Troupe
    The Thai Art Troupe, consisting of 48 members and led by Nai Suwat Waradilok, a famous Thai playwright and director, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of April 30th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

    Tunisian Agricultural Technology Delegation
    The Tunisian Agricultural Technology Delegation, consisting of three members and led by Salah Eddine, Director of the Northern Land Office of the Tunisian Ministry of Agriculture, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon on April 30th.

    Cambodian Sports Delegation
    The Cambodian Sports Delegation, making its first visit to China and consisting of 57 members, arrived in Beijing on April 30th, led by Choeu, Chairman of the Cambodian Sports Commission.

    Yugoslav Legal Workers Delegation
    At the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society, the Yugoslav Legal Workers Delegation, headed by Comrade Lejo Geršković, Legislative Secretary-General of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, arrived in Beijing on April 30th.

    Japanese Drama Visiting Group
    The Japanese Drama Visiting Group, consisting of eight members and led by Uno Jukichi, a famous Japanese stage actor, arrived in Beijing on April 30th.

    Okayama City Industrial and Trade Observation Group of Japan
    The Okayama City Industrial and Trade Observation Group of Japan, consisting of fifteen members and led by Kinosaku Kiba, Chairman of the Okayama City Council, and Vice-Leader Takeshi Tomioka, Deputy Mayor of Okayama City, arrived in Beijing on the evening of April 28th.

    Indian Christian Church and YMCA Delegation
    The Indian Christian Church and YMCA Delegation, consisting of six members, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of April 29th.

    Mr. and Mrs. Purbodiningrat
    Mr. and Mrs. Purbodiningrat, Chairman of the Indonesian National Peace Committee and Chairman of the Yogyakarta Indonesia-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on April 30th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

    Australian Trade Union Delegation
    The Australian Trade Union Delegation, consisting of five members and led by Monk, Chairman of the Australian Council of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 30th.

    Assistant Secretary-General of the Australian Peace Council
    Smith, Assistant Secretary-General of the Australian Peace Council, arrived in Beijing on April 30th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

### 五一前夕嘉宾到

1957-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    五一前夕嘉宾到
    泰国艺术团
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的泰国艺术团一行四十八人，在团长、泰国著名剧作家和导演乃素滑特·哇拉里洛率领下于30日下午到达北京。
    突尼斯农业技术代表团
    突尼斯农业技术代表团一行三人，在团长、突尼斯农业部北部土地办事处主任斯拉赫丁率领下，于30日中午乘飞机到北京。
    柬埔寨体育代表团
    第一次访问中国的柬埔寨体育代表团五十七人，在团长、柬埔寨体委主席舒厄率领下于30日分别到达北京。
    南法律工作者代表团
    应中国政治法律学会邀请，以南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会立法秘书长莱·盖尔施科维奇同志为首的南斯拉夫法律工作者代表团于4月30日抵达北京。
    日本戏剧访华团
    日本戏剧访华团一行八人，在团长、日本著名话剧演员宇野重吉的率领下于30日到达北京。
    日本冈山市产业贸易考察团
    日本冈山市产业贸易考察团一行十五人，在团长、日本冈山市议会议长木庭清作和副团长、冈山市副市长富冈毅率领下于28日晚到达北京。
    印度基督教及青年会代表团
    印度基督教教会及青年会代表团一行六人于29日晚乘火车到达北京。
    普波迪宁格拉特夫妇
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的印度尼西亚全国和平委员会主席、日惹印度尼西亚—中国友好协会主席普波迪宁格拉特夫妇，30日乘飞机到达北京。
    澳大利亚工会代表团
    以澳大利亚工会理事会主席蒙克为首的澳大利亚工会代表团一行五人于30日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    澳和平理事会助理秘书长
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国参观访问的澳大利亚和平理事会助理秘书长史密斯于30日到达北京。
