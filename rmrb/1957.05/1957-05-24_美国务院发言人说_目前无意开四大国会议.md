Title: U.S. State Department Spokesman Says There Are No Plans for a Four-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 22nd: A U.S. State Department spokesman said on the 21st that the United States is currently not considering holding a conference of the United States, Britain, France, and the Soviet Union.

### 美国务院发言人说  目前无意开四大国会议

1957-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    美国务院发言人说
    目前无意开四大国会议
    新华社22日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院发言人21日说，美国目前并没有考虑举行美、英、法、苏四大国会议的问题。
