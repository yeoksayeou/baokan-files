Title: Guo Moruo Receives Indian Mathematician D.D. Kosambi
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo, received Indian mathematician Professor D.D. Kosambi at his home this evening. Those present at the reception included Qian Xuesen, Director of the Institute of Mechanics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others.

### 郭沫若接见印数学家高善必

1957-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见印数学家高善必
    新华社6日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚在他的家中接见了印度数学家高善必教授。接见时，在座的有中国科学院力学研究所所长钱学森等人。
