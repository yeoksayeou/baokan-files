Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 4

    Offal Canned Goods
    The Wanxian Food Cannery in Sichuan Province has used pork belly, liver, intestines, and head left over from the production of meat canned goods to trial-produce canned goods such as "Garlic Sprouts Twice-Cooked Pork" and Braised Large Intestines in Brown Sauce, which are very popular with consumers.

### 内脏罐头

1957-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    内脏罐头
    四川省万县食品罐头厂，把生产肉类罐头剩下的猪肚、肝、心肠和头，试制成“蒜苗回锅肉”、红烧大肠等罐头，很受消费者欢迎。
