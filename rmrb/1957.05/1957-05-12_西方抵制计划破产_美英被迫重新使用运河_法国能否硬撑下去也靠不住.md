Title: Western Boycott Plan Fails; US and Britain Forced to Reuse Canal; France's Ability to Hold Out Also Unreliable
Author:
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 6

According to a Reuters report on the 10th, the "Suez Canal Users Association's" public statement on the 9th essentially opened the door for Western ships to resume using the Suez Canal, and even Britain has decided to allow its ships to pay tolls to Egypt and use the canal. Reuters reported on the 10th that the British Cabinet has made a decision to allow British ships to use the Suez Canal, although the date of the announcement of this decision has not yet been determined.

Washington sources believe that the reason Western countries are resuming the use of the Suez Canal is due to competition among these countries' shipping companies, which makes it impossible to implement the canal boycott plan. A US State Department spokesperson said on the 10th that US shipowners must decide for themselves whether to use the canal. If they do, shipowners "should pay the tolls to Egypt under protest" because "although the physical dangers in safely passing through the Suez Canal no longer exist, the legal dangers still remain."

France is temporarily maintaining its instructions for French ships to boycott the Suez Canal. Government officials said, "The conditions forcibly imposed by Egypt make it impossible for anyone to advise shipowners to use this canal at present." However, Washington sources doubt whether French shipping companies' ships can detour around Africa for a long time, while allowing their competitors to navigate the short route through the Suez Canal.

An Egyptian spokesman at the United Nations said that the "Users Association's" decision to fully use the canal was "wise."

### 西方抵制计划破产  美英被迫重新使用运河  法国能否硬撑下去也靠不住

1957-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

    西方抵制计划破产
    美英被迫重新使用运河
    法国能否硬撑下去也靠不住
    本报综合报道“苏伊士运河使用国协会”9日的公报，实际上是为西方国家船只恢复使用苏伊士运河打开了方便之门，甚至英国也决定让它的船只向埃及缴纳通行费使用运河了。据路透社10日报道，英国内阁已经作出了允许英国船只使用苏伊士运河的决定，虽然这个决定公布的日期还没有确定。
    西方国家恢复使用苏伊士运河的原因，据华盛顿人士认为，是由于这些国家的航运公司之间的竞争，使抵制运河的计划无法执行。美国国务院发言人10日说，美国船主必须自己决定是否使用运河。如果通过，船主“应在提出抗议的情况下把通行费付在埃及”，因为“虽然在安全通过苏伊士运河方面的物质危险已经不存在，但是法律上的危险仍旧存在”。
    法国暂时仍然保持它对法国船只抵制苏伊士运河的指示。政府人士说，“埃及强行规定的条件，使他人在目前无法建议船只的主人使用这条运河”。但是，华盛顿人士怀疑法国航运公司的船只是否能够长期绕道非洲，而让它的竞争者航行通过苏伊士运河的短航线。
    埃及驻联合国发言人说，“使用国协会”决定全面使用运河的决定是“明智的”。
