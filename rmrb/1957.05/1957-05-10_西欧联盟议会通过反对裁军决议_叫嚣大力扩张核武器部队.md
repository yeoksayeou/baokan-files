Title: Parliamentary Assembly of the Western European Union Passes Resolution Against Disarmament, Calls for Vigorously Expanding Nuclear Weapons Forces
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Strasbourg, 9th Dispatch: The Parliamentary Assembly of the Western European Union passed a resolution on the 8th opposing the reduction of "allied ground forces in continental Western Europe," accusing such actions of "undermining the core of the Western European defense system." The resolution believes that the so-called "forward defense forces" of Western Europe require at least thirty divisions of ground troops equipped with modern tactical atomic weapons, in addition to a strike force equipped with so-called strategic nuclear weapons.

The resolution demands that all Western forces be equally equipped with tactical atomic weapons and missiles.

This resolution passed with thirty-nine votes in favor, seven against, and nineteen abstentions. Almost all British representatives abstained.

The previous day, a proposal demanding condemnation of the Western European Union's Ministerial Council for approving Britain's withdrawal of some troops stationed in West Germany failed to pass because it did not receive the required absolute majority (forty-five votes). French Popular Republican Party member Deixonne, during the discussion of this proposal, accused Britain, saying: "It is unacceptable for an individual country to make decisions ignoring the existence of the Western European Union."

### 西欧联盟议会通过反对裁军决议  叫嚣大力扩张核武器部队

1957-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    西欧联盟议会通过反对裁军决议
    叫嚣大力扩张核武器部队
    新华社9日讯　斯特拉斯堡消息：西欧联盟议会8日通过决议，反对裁减“西欧大陆上的盟国地面部队”，指责这种做法会“破坏西欧防御体系的主体”。决议认为，西欧的所谓“前沿防御力量”至少需要三十师装备有现代战术性原子武器的地面部队，此外还要有一支装备所谓战略性核武器的打击力量。
    决议要求所有西方的部队都必须同等地装备战术原子武器和导弹。
    这个决议是以三十九票对七票通过的，十九票弃权。几乎所有英国代表都弃了权。
    在前一天，一项要求谴责西欧联盟部长理事会批准英国从西德撤退部分驻军的提案，因为没有获得应有的绝对多数票（四十五票），而没有通过。法国人民共和党议员戴尚在讨论这个提案的时候指责英国，他说：“一个个别国家无视西欧联盟的存在就作出决定，这是不能接受的。”
