Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulatory Message to the Communist Party of Sweden on its 40th Anniversary

Author:

Date: 1957-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 16th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory telegram on the 14th to mark the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Sweden. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Sweden:

    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the Communist Party of Sweden, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the Communist Party of Sweden and the Swedish working class.

    The forty years of struggle of the Communist Party of Sweden have demonstrated that the Communist Party of Sweden is the true representative of the interests of the Swedish people. It has consistently waged unyielding struggles to safeguard Swedish peace, expand the democracy of the Swedish people, improve the living conditions of the Swedish people, and consolidate the solidarity of the international working class.

    We congratulate the Communist Party of Sweden on its new achievements in the future struggle to unite the Swedish working class and the people of the whole country for peace, democracy, and socialism.

                  Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, May 14, 1957

### 中国共产党中央委员会  电贺瑞典共产党建党四十周年

1957-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会
    电贺瑞典共产党建党四十周年
    新华社16日讯　今年是瑞典共产党建党四十周年，中共中央在14日致电祝贺。贺电全文如下：瑞典共产党中央委员会：
    欣逢瑞典共产党第四十周年纪念之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们，并通过你们，向瑞典共产党和瑞典工人阶级致以热烈的祝贺。
    瑞典共产党四十年来的斗争，表明了瑞典共产党是瑞典人民利益的真正代表者。它一贯为维护瑞典和平、扩大瑞典人民的民主、改善瑞典人民的生活条件以及巩固国际工人阶级的团结而进行不屈的斗争。
    我们祝贺瑞典共产党在今后团结瑞典工人阶级和全国人民为争取和平、民主和社会主义而进行的斗争中，获得新的成就。
                  中国共产党中央委员会　1957年5月14日
