    A Library with One Book
    In India, there is a library that houses only one volume of scriptures. This library is located in Amritsar, in northern India, and the building is constructed of valuable marble and is extremely lavishly decorated inside.

### 藏书一卷的图书馆

1957-05-26
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    藏书一卷的图书馆
    印度有一个图书馆，馆内只藏有一卷经书。这个图书馆设立在印度北部阿木利沙尔地方，建筑物由贵重的大理石筑成，内部装饰极其华丽。
