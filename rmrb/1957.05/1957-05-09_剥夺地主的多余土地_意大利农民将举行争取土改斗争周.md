Title: Deprive Landlords of Excess Land: Italian Farmers to Hold Struggle Week for Land Reform
Author:
Date: 1957-05-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, May 7th: According to TASS, the General Secretary of the Italian Farm Laborers' Union, Romagnoli, announced on the 5th that farmers throughout Italy would hold a struggle week for land reform from May 12th to 19th. At the same time, a signature campaign will soon be launched nationwide to force Parliament to review the bill requiring land reform in accordance with the Constitution.

Romagnoli announced the above decision, unanimously adopted by the National Land Reform Conference, at a mass rally in Florence celebrating the conference's closing. He also stated the following demands of Italian farmers: establish a maximum limit of stable land ownership; deprive landlords of excess land and distribute it to landless and land-poor farmers; transfer a portion of state-owned land to farmers' production cooperatives; and provide state support and funding for small agricultural cooperative production.

More than 10,000 workers participating in the conference unanimously supported the decisions of the National Land Reform Conference.

### 剥夺地主的多余土地  意大利农民将举行争取土改斗争周

1957-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    剥夺地主的多余土地
    意大利农民将举行争取土改斗争周
    据新华社7日讯　据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利雇农联合会总书记罗曼奥利5日宣布，意大利全国各地的农民将从5月12日到19日举行争取土地改革斗争周。同时，全国马上就要展开一个签名运动，以便迫使议会按宪法规定审议要求实行土地改革的法案。
    罗曼奥利在佛罗伦萨庆祝全国土地改革会议闭幕的群众大会上宣布了这个会议一致通过的上述决定。他还申述了意大利农民的下列要求：建立最大限度的稳固的土地所有权；剥夺地主的多余土地，并且把它分配给无地和少地的农民；把国有土地的一部分转让给农民生产合作社；国家支持和资助小农业生产合作制。
    参加大会的一万多名劳动者一致支持全国土地改革会议的决定。
