Title: Chinese and Ceylonese Tennis Players Hold Exhibition Matches to Entertain Foreign Envoys to China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Ceylonese tennis team held an exhibition match with the Chinese tennis team at the Beijing International Club this afternoon to entertain foreign envoys and diplomatic officials in China. The Chinese team won one match and lost two matches in two men's singles and one men's doubles.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai met with the tennis players from both countries before the start of the exhibition match and took pictures with them.

### 中国和锡兰网球运动员  举行表演赛招待各国驻华使节

1957-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

    中国和锡兰网球运动员
    举行表演赛招待各国驻华使节
    据新华社10日讯　锡兰网球队同中国网球队今天下午在北京国际俱乐部举行表演比赛，招待各国驻华使节和外交官员。中国队在两场男子单打和一场男子双打中胜一场，败两场。
    国务院总理周恩来在表演比赛开始以前，接见了两国的网球运动员，并且同他们一起照了相。
