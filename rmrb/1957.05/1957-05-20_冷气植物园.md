Title: Cold Storage Botanical Garden
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-20
Page: 3

    Cold Storage Botanical Garden
    Botanists at the University of Copenhagen in Denmark have established a botanical garden inside a large cold storage warehouse. The temperature in the garden is kept below zero in winter and does not exceed 13 to 16 degrees Celsius in summer. Seeds of plants from the Arctic, Canada, and Norway can thrive and multiply rapidly in this cold storage warehouse. To promote the growth of Greenland plants, fluorescent lights are installed to provide long hours of illumination, because the sun does not set in Greenland during the summer. In winter, the plants are covered with artificial snow and transplanted into a darkroom in the garden.

### 冷气植物园

1957-05-20
第3版()
专栏：花絮

    冷气植物园
    丹麦哥本哈根大学的植物学家在一座大冷藏库内设立了一个植物园。园中的温度，冬天保持在零下，夏天不超过摄氏十三至十六度。北极、加拿大、挪威各地所产的植物种子，在这种冷藏库内都能欣欣向荣，迅速繁殖。为了促使格陵兰植物生长，还安装了日光灯加以长时间照明，因为在格陵兰夏天太阳是不落的。冬天则在这些植物上铺盖人工雪，移植园中暗室中。
