Title: Disregarding the Condemnation of People of All Countries, the United States Repeatedly Tests Nuclear Weapons
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th Dispatch from Nevada: The United States today tested a nuclear device equivalent to 10,000 tons of gunpowder at the Yucca Flat in Nevada.

Today's test is the first in a series of nuclear weapon tests that the United States plans to conduct starting this May, and will continue to be tested continuously until September 1st.

### 不顾各国人民的谴责  美国一再试验核武器

1957-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    不顾各国人民的谴责
    美国一再试验核武器
    据新华社28日讯　内华达州消息：美国今天在内华达州的育加平原上试验了一颗相当于一万吨火药的核装置。
    今天的试验是美国预定要在今年5月起进行的一连串核武器试验中的第一次，以后还要连续试验到9月1日为止。
