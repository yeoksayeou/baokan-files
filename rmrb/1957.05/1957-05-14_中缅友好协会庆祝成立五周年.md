Title: China-Myanmar Friendship Association Celebrates Fifth Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: The China-Myanmar Friendship Association held a grand meeting tonight at the Beijing Theater to celebrate the fifth anniversary of its founding.

    Qian Weichang, Vice President of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting. He first extended a warm welcome to the delegation of the Myanmar-China Friendship Association who had come from afar to participate in the celebration, and then recounted the achievements of China-Myanmar friendly cooperation over the past few years. Qian Weichang said, we welcome more Burmese friends to come to China to visit relatives in the future; we also hope to have more opportunities to visit Myanmar to see our good friends.

    U Ba Maung, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Myanmar Embassy in China, spoke at the meeting. He praised the efforts made and the achievements achieved by the China-Myanmar Friendship Association in developing friendship between China and Myanmar.

    Dr. Dhala, head of the delegation of the Myanmar-China Friendship Association, read the congratulatory message from the Myanmar-China Friendship Association at the meeting.

    The congratulatory message stated that the cause of Myanmar-China friendship is very important not only for our two countries, but also for Asian unity and world peace. At this celebration, let us express our determination to further strengthen the work of Myanmar-China friendship.

    Dr. Dhala also presented gifts to the China-Myanmar Friendship Association on behalf of the Myanmar-China Friendship Association.

    Students majoring in Burmese at Peking University recited their own poem "Ode to China-Myanmar Friendship" at the meeting. The Chinese Art Troupe, which recently visited Myanmar for performances, performed songs and dances from both China and Myanmar. The memorial meeting concluded with a performance of the Peking Opera "Lion Tower" by Peking Opera actors.

    More than a thousand people attended the memorial meeting.

### 中缅友好协会庆祝成立五周年

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅友好协会庆祝成立五周年
    据新华社13日讯　中缅友好协会今晚在北京剧场举行庆祝协会成立五周年纪念大会。
    中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长在会上讲话。他首先向远道而来参加庆祝会的缅中友协代表团表示热烈欢迎，并历述几年来中缅友好合作事业的成就。钱伟长说，我们欢迎今后有更多的缅甸朋友到中国来走亲戚；我们也希望有更多的机会去缅甸看望我们的好朋友。
    缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴茂在会上讲话，他赞扬中缅友好协会在发展中缅两国友谊的工作上所作的努力和已取得的成绩。
    缅中友协代表团团长达拉博士在会上宣读缅中友好协会的祝词。
    祝词说，缅中友好事业不仅对于我们两国，而且对于亚洲的团结和世界和平都是非常重要的。在这庆祝会上，让我们表示要更进一步加强缅中友好的工作的决心。
    达拉博士并代表缅中友好协会向中缅友好协会赠礼。
    北京大学缅甸语专业学生在会上朗诵了他们自己创作的诗“中缅友谊颂”。不久前赴缅甸访问演出的中国艺术团演出了中、缅两国的歌舞。纪念会最后由京剧演员表演了“狮子楼”一剧。
    出席纪念会的有一千多人。
