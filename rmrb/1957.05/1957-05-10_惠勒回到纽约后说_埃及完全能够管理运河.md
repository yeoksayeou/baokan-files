Title: Wheeler Says After Returning to New York: Egypt is Fully Capable of Managing the Canal
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 8th—New York dispatch: General Wheeler, the United Nations special representative responsible for assisting Egypt in clearing the Suez Canal, said after returning to New York by plane on the 7th that the Suez Canal is "fully capable of meeting the requirements of normal navigation." He also said that Egypt is "competent to manage the canal." Wheeler stayed in Egypt for a total of five months.

### 惠勒回到纽约后说  埃及完全能够管理运河

1957-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    惠勒回到纽约后说
    埃及完全能够管理运河
    新华社8日讯　纽约消息：联合国负责协助埃及清理苏伊士运河的特别代表惠勒将军7日乘飞机回到纽约后发表谈话说，苏伊士运河“完全能够满足正常通航的要求”。他还说，埃及能够“胜任地管理运河”。惠勒在埃及总共逗留了五个月。
