Title: Egypt Permits British and Other Ships to Pay Suez Canal Tolls in Pounds
Author:
Date: 1957-05-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 15th: Egypt has permitted ships belonging to Britain and other Western European countries to pay Suez Canal tolls in pounds. These countries' ships can also use pounds to pay for grain and other supplies purchased at Egyptian ports when passing through the canal. The Egyptian Monetary Authority issued a notice to banks on the 14th regarding this decision.
    On the same day, the Egyptian Ministry of Finance ordered the Egyptian banks to resume business dealings with British banks.
    According to Cairo Radio, an Egyptian delegation will go to Italy to discuss the complete unfreezing of Egypt's pound deposits in Britain with British representatives. This delegation will be composed of the Deputy Minister of Finance, the Governor of the National Bank of Egypt, and the Governor of the Egyptian Monetary Authority.
    The British ship "Balsam Hill" entered the Suez Canal on the 14th. This is the first British ship to do so after the British government announced on the 13th that it would no longer prohibit British ships from using the canal.

### 埃及准许英国等船只用英镑付运河通行费

1957-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及准许英国等船只用英镑付运河通行费
    据新华社开罗15日电　埃及已经准许英国和其他西欧国家所属船只以英镑缴付苏伊士运河通行费。这些国家的船只在通过运河时在埃及各港口购买粮食等供应物资的费用也可以用英镑支付。埃及货币管理局14日向各银行发出了关于这个决定的通知。
    同一天，埃及财政部下令准许埃及银行同英国银行恢复业务往来。
    据开罗电台广播，一个埃及代表团将去意大利同英国代表讨论有关全部解冻埃及在英国的英镑存款问题。这个代表团将由埃及财政部副部长、埃及国家银行行长以及埃及货币管理局局长组成。
    英国船“白杨山号”已经在14日进入苏伊士运河。这是在英国政府13日宣布不再禁止英国船使用运河以后这样做的第一艘英国船。
