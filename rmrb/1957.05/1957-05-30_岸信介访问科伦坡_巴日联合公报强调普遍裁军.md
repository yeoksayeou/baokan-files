Title: Kishi Nobusuke Visits Colombo; Pakistan-Japan Joint Communiqué Emphasizes Universal Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 6

    Kishi Nobusuke Visits Colombo
    Pakistan-Japan Joint Communiqué Emphasizes Universal Disarmament

    Xinhua News Agency Report: Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi arrived in Colombo on the afternoon of May 28th from Karachi.

    Before leaving Karachi, Kishi Nobusuke issued a joint communiqué with Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy. In the communiqué, they emphasized the need for universal disarmament and called on all relevant countries to promptly stipulate, limit, control, and reduce all armed forces' armaments, including the prohibition of the manufacture, testing, and use of all weapons of mass destruction, while calling for the establishment of an effective international inspection and control system for this purpose.

    The communiqué stated that Suhrawardy and Kishi Nobusuke discussed ways to enhance trade and economic cooperation between the two countries.

### 岸信介访问科伦坡  巴日联合公报强调普遍裁军

1957-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    岸信介访问科伦坡
    巴日联合公报强调普遍裁军
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：日本首相岸信介在5月28日下午从卡拉奇到达科伦坡。
    岸信介在离开卡拉奇前曾和巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底发表联合公报。他们在公报中强调必须实行普遍裁军，并且呼吁所有有关国家迅速规定、限制、管制和裁减一切武装部队的军备，包括禁止一切大规模毁灭性武器的制造、试验和使用在内，同时呼吁为了这个目的而建立有效的国际视察和管制制度。
    公报说，苏拉瓦底和岸信介讨论了增进两国贸易和经济合作的方法。
