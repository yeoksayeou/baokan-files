Title: US Union Representative Says Best Way to Save West Coast Ports is to Restore Trade with China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Portland (Oregon), May 27th: A representative of the International Longshore and Warehouse Union (ILWU) in Oregon, Meehan, said on May 25th that restoring US trade with China "would be like saving" the US West Coast ports.

Meehan said that the ILWU will send a delegation to Washington on June 1st to demand that the government adopt a "more realistic trade policy toward China."

### 美国一工会代表说  为了挽救西海岸港口  最好恢复同中国贸易

1957-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    美国一工会代表说
    为了挽救西海岸港口
    最好恢复同中国贸易
    新华社27日讯　波特兰（俄勒冈）消息：美国国际码头与仓库工人工会俄勒冈州代表米汉5月25日说，如果恢复了美国同中国的贸易，“等于是救了”美国西海岸各港口。
    米汉说，国际码头与仓库工人工会将在6月1日派代表团前往华盛顿要求政府采取一项“比较现实的对华贸易政策”。
