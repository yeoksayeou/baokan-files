Title: Omani Masses Attack British Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 27th. According to a report in today's "Republic," the Omani office in Cairo, representing the Sultanate of Oman on the eastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, announced that nationalist masses had attacked the British airport in Oman.
　　The Omani statement also said that it is believed that this action is the beginning of a large-scale resistance movement launched by various tribes after reaching an agreement in Muscat on a unified nationalist struggle.

### 阿曼群众袭击英国机场

1957-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼群众袭击英国机场
    新华社开罗27日电　据“共和国报”今天报道，阿拉伯半岛东岸的酋长国阿曼驻开罗办事处宣布民族主义群众曾经袭击英国在阿曼的飞机场。
    阿曼的声明又说，人们认为，这个行动是各部落在马斯喀特达成统一民族主义斗争的协议以后展开大规模抵抗运动的开始。
