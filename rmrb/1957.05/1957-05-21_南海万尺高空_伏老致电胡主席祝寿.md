Title: In the South China Sea at 10,000 Feet: Voroshilov Cables Chairman Ho to Wish Him a Happy Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th May: Chairman Voroshilov, en route from Indonesia to Vietnam on May 19th, sent a telegram from his Tu-1104 jetliner over Sumatra to Chairman Ho Chi Minh to congratulate him on his sixty-seventh birthday, May 19th.

Voroshilov expressed his regret in the telegram that they could not arrive in time for Ho Chi Minh's birthday.

The telegram stated: "But regardless of the relentless passage of time, we can still, at an altitude of 10,200 meters and a speed of 800 kilometers per hour, wish you good health, a long life, and many more great victories in your work and in the construction of a new life."

### 南海万尺高空  伏老致电胡主席祝寿

1957-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    南海万尺高空
    伏老致电胡主席祝寿
    据新华社河内20日电　伏罗希洛夫主席5月19日在从印度尼西亚到越南途中，从苏门答腊上空的图一一○四喷气式专机上，打电报向胡志明主席祝贺，因为5月19日是胡志明主席六十七岁的生日。
    伏罗希洛夫在这封电报里对他们赶不及在胡志明的生日的时候到达表示遗憾。
    电报说：“但是不顾无情的时间，我们还是可以在一万零两百公尺的高空和每小时八百公里的速度中祝您健康、长寿，祝您在工作和建设新生活中取得许多更巨大的胜利。”
