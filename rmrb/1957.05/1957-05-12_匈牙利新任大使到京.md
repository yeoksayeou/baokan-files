Title: New Hungarian Ambassador Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Sándor Nógrádi, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome him.
    Sár, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, heads of diplomatic missions of various countries in China, and diplomatic officials were also present to greet him.

### 匈牙利新任大使到京

1957-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利新任大使到京
    新华社11日讯　匈牙利人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使诺格拉第·山道尔今天上午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办沙尔、各国驻华使节和外交官员也前往迎接。
