Title: Dulles Returns to the US After Meeting with Mollet
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 7th May: US Secretary of State Dulles held a one-hour meeting with French Prime Minister Mollet on May 6th.

French Foreign Minister Pineau, who participated in the meeting, said that they discussed the recently concluded North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council meeting, the situation in the Middle East, and the Soviet Union's recent note on West German atomic armament. During the meeting, Mollet briefed Dulles on the situation in Algeria, but Dulles did not express an opinion. Mollet also stated that France believes that the Suez Canal issue cannot be resolved according to Egyptian conditions, and the current situation of the canal is "particularly bad."

On the morning of the same day, Dulles briefed the US ambassadors to Western European countries, who were gathering in Paris, on the situation of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council meeting. US Chargé d'Affaires to the Soviet Union, Davis, and the newly appointed US Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Thompson, who were passing through Paris on their way to the Soviet Union, also attended the meeting.

Dulles already flew back to the United States on the evening of the 6th.

### 杜勒斯同摩勒会谈后回美

1957-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯同摩勒会谈后回美
    新华社7日讯　巴黎消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯和法国总理摩勒5月6日举行了一小时会谈。
    参加会谈的法国外交部长比诺说，他们讨论刚结束的北大西洋集团理事会会议、中东局势和苏联最近关于西德原子军备问题的照会。摩勒在会谈中向杜勒斯综述了阿尔及利亚的局势，但是杜勒斯没有发表意见。摩勒还表示，法国认为不能按照埃及条件解决苏伊士运河问题，目前运河局势是“特别坏的”。
    同一天上午，杜勒斯向正在巴黎集会的美国驻西欧各国大使报告了北大西洋集团理事会会议的情况。出席会议的还有美国驻苏代办戴维斯和途经巴黎赴苏的新任驻苏大使汤普森。
    杜勒斯已经在6日晚间乘飞机回美国。
