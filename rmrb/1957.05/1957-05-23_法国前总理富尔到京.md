Title: Former French Prime Minister Faure Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: Former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Guangzhou. He is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

	At the airport, Faure told reporters that since he had been away from France for some time, it would be better not to comment on the resignation of the Mollet government.

	Faure said that the current French cabinet crisis is not a strange thing. Because in French political life, this kind of cabinet crisis is not uncommon. He himself has experienced it twice.

	Welcoming him at the airport were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and his wife, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Qian Duansheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

### 法国前总理富尔到京

1957-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    法国前总理富尔到京
    据新华社22日讯　法国前总理富尔和他的夫人今天下午从广州乘飞机到达北京。他是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
    富尔在机场上对记者说，由于他已离开法国一个时期，还是不要评论摩勒政府辞职的问题为好。
    富尔说，这次法国内阁的危机并不是奇怪的事。因为在法国政治生活中，这种内阁危机是并不罕见的。他本人就经历过两次。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和夫人，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪，北京市副市长乐松生。
