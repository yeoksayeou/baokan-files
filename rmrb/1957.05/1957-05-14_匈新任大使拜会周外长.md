Title: Hungarian Newly Appointed Ambassador Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Nogradi Sandor, paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

    Present at the meeting were Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Liu Jingyu, Acting Director of the Department of Soviet Union and Eastern Europe.

### 匈新任大使拜会周外长

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    匈新任大使拜会周外长
    新华社13日讯　匈牙利人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使诺格拉第·山道尔今天下午拜会我外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。
    拜会时在座的，有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如和苏联东欧司代司长刘靖宇。
