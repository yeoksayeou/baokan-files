Title: Soviet People Enthusiastically Save Money

Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 5

    Soviet People Enthusiastically Save Money
    (Dispatch from our Moscow correspondent, May 24) The savings of the Soviet people in banks are constantly increasing. This is evidence of their thrift and increased income. Last year, cash savings increased by 10 billion rubles, reaching 63.7 billion rubles by the end of the year. In the past four years, workers' deposits have increased 1.7 times, employees 1.2 times, and farmers 3.6 times.

### 苏联人民踊跃储蓄

1957-05-26
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联人民踊跃储蓄
    本报莫斯科24日电　苏联人民在银行里的储蓄不断增长。这是他们节约和收入提高的一个证据。去年储蓄的现款增加了一百亿卢布，截至去年年底，达到六百三十七亿卢布。在最近四年中，工人的存款增加了一点七倍，职员一点二倍，农民三点六倍。
