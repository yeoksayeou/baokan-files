Title: Joint Statement of the Governments and Parties of Romania and Germany: Determined to Strengthen the Solidarity of the Socialist Camp

Author:

Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 30th - According to a report by the German News Agency, the delegation of the government and party of the Romanian People's Republic, which visited the German Democratic Republic from April 24th, returned to their country from Berlin on the morning of April 29th.

During the visit, the government delegation headed by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Chivu Stoica, and the party delegation headed by the First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, held talks separately with the government delegation headed by the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and the party delegation headed by the First Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Walter Ulbricht. On April 28th, a joint statement of the two governments and parties was signed in Berlin.

The joint statement of the two governments expressed satisfaction with the strengthening of German-Romanian friendship and cooperation. It emphasized that the foundation of friendly relations between the two countries is the principle of Leninism, the principle of equality and proletarian internationalism. For the cause of socialism and peace, the two governments will do their best to strengthen the fraternal friendship and unbreakable unity in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

The purpose of the foreign policies of Germany and Romania is to safeguard peace and strengthen international security. The two countries criticized US imperialism for planning a new colonial policy that threatens the independence of Arab countries and condemned the Western countries for preparing for atomic warfare.

Germany and Romania hope for extensive cooperation among countries in the peaceful use of atomic energy, support the proposal for the establishment of a European collective security system, and the Soviet Union's proposal to establish arms limitation zones.

The two governments firmly oppose the revival of German militarism and unanimously believe that the peaceful reunification of Germany is a matter for the German people themselves, requiring mutual understanding between the two Germanys.

The two governments announced their readiness to develop political, economic and cultural relations with all countries and reiterated their commitment to the principles of peaceful coexistence.

When the statement discussed the relations between the two countries, it pointed out that both sides agreed to sign a long-term trade treaty for the period 1958 to 1960. At the same time, agreements were reached on expanding economic cooperation, technical cooperation, and agricultural cooperation, etc.

The joint statement of the two parties condemned the "Eisenhower Doctrine", the domestic and foreign policies of the West German government, and the so-called "Common Market" and "European Atomic Energy Community" plans.

The statement pointed out that the normalization of relations between the German Democratic Republic and Yugoslavia is in the interest of the two countries, which share the common goal of building socialism, and helps consolidate peace.

The statement emphasized that the two parties are determined to strengthen the solidarity and unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and believe that their primary task is to consistently safeguard the purity of Marxist-Leninist doctrine and educate party members in the spirit of proletarian internationalism.

### 罗德两国政府和党联合声明  决心加强社会主义阵营团结一致

1957-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    罗德两国政府和党联合声明
    决心加强社会主义阵营团结一致
    新华社柏林30日电  据德意志通讯社报道，4月24日到民主德国访问的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和党的代表团已经在4月29日上午离柏林回国。
    在访问期间，以罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡为首的政府代表团和以罗马尼亚工人党第一书记乔治乌—德治为首的党的代表团，分别同以德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥为首的政府代表团和以德国统一社会党第一书记乌布利希为首的党的代表团举行了会谈。4月28日在柏林分别签署了两国政府和党的联合声明。
    两国政府的联合声明对德罗友谊和合作的加强表示满意。它着重指出，两国友好关系的基础是列宁主义的原则，是平等和无产阶级国际主义的原则。为了社会主义事业和和平，两国政府将尽力加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营中的兄弟之谊和牢不可破的团结。
    两国外交政策的宗旨是维护和平和加强国际安全。德罗两国指责美帝国主义策划那种威胁阿拉伯国家独立的新殖民主义政策，并且谴责西方国家准备原子战争。
    德罗两国希望各国在和平使用原子能方面进行广泛合作，支持建立欧洲集体安全体系的主张和苏联提出的关于建立限制军备区的建议。
    两国政府坚决反对复活德国军国主义，一致认为和平恢复德国的统一是德国人民自己的事情，需要两个德国相互谅解。
    两国政府宣布准备同各国发展政治、经济和文化关系，并且重申忠于和平共处原则。
    声明谈到两国关系的时候指出，双方同意签订1958年至1960年的长期贸易条约。同时，对于扩大经济合作、技术合作和发展农业方面的合作等等也达成了协议。
    两党的联合声明谴责了“艾森豪威尔主义”、西德政府的对内和对外政策和所谓“共同市场”和“欧洲原子能联营组织”计划。
    声明指出，民主德国和南斯拉夫关系的正常化符合以建设社会主义为共同目标的这两个国家的利益，并且有助于巩固和平。
    声明着重指出，两党决心加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结一致，并且认为自己的首要任务是始终一贯地维护马克思列宁主义学说的纯洁性，以无产阶级国际主义精神教育两党党员。
