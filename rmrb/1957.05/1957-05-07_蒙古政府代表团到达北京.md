Title: Mongolian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Ch. Surenjav, and accompanied by Kui Bi, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, arrived in Beijing by special plane this morning.

At the West Suburb Airport, which was decorated with the national flags of Mongolia and China and colored flags, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and Zhang Youyu, Zhang Hanfu, Li Zheren, Yang Xiandong, Zhang Zhixiang, Xiao Xiangrong and others. A group of Mongolian girls presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Tsedendamba, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 蒙古政府代表团到达北京

1957-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府代表团到达北京
    新华社6日讯　以齐·苏伦扎布为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团由内蒙古自治区副主席奎璧等陪同，今天上午乘专机到达北京。
    在悬挂着蒙、中两国国旗和彩旗的西郊机场上，贵宾们受到了邓小平副总理、李先念副总理和张友渔、章汉夫、李哲人、杨显东、张致祥、萧向荣等人的热情欢迎。一群蒙古族女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办策登依什也到机场欢迎。
