Title: Tunisian Agricultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: The Tunisian Agricultural Technical Delegation left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. After arriving in Beijing on April 30, the delegation participated in the "May Day" celebrations and then visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Xi'an. They held discussions with staff members of agricultural cooperatives, agricultural machine tractor stations, and fruit tree nurseries in the suburbs of these cities.

Before the delegation left Beijing, Premier Zhou Enlai received the head of the delegation, Salahdin, director of the Tunisian Northern Land Office, and all members of the delegation.

### 突尼斯农业代表团回国

1957-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    突尼斯农业代表团回国
    新华社29日讯　突尼斯农业技术代表团在今天早晨乘飞机离北京回国。代表团在4月30日到达北京后，曾参加了“五一”节观礼，以后又先后到上海、杭州和西安等地参观访问，同这些城市郊区的农业社、农业机器拖拉机站和果树苗圃的工作人员举行座谈。
    代表团离北京前，周恩来总理曾接见了代表团团长、突尼斯北部土地办事处主任斯拉赫丁和代表团全体成员。
