Title: Voroshilov Sends Telegram to Thank Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Chairman Voroshilov's telegram of thanks to Chairman Mao. The full text is as follows: To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    As I bid farewell to the great Chinese people and their most respected leaders, I would like to express my most heartfelt gratitude to you, our dear Comrade Mao Zedong, and through you to all the Chinese people, for your friendly invitation to visit China, and for the fraternal affection shown to me and my companions by the great Chinese people during our visit to the People's Republic of China.
    Comrade Mao Zedong, I sincerely wish you good health and success in all the undertakings you are doing for the Chinese people!
    I wish the great Chinese people further achievements in the cause of socialist construction!
    May the unbreakable friendship and brotherly unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples be further consolidated and developed!
        K. Voroshilov (signed), Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
                     May 26, 1957, on board Ilyushin-14 aircraft, number 2321

### 伏老电谢毛主席

1957-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    伏老电谢毛主席
    新华社26日讯　伏罗希洛夫主席给毛泽东主席的感谢电。全文如下：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    在同伟大的中国人民和他们最敬爱的领导者们离别的时候，我仅向您，我们亲爱的毛泽东同志，并通过您向全中国人民表示我最衷心的谢意，感谢你们友好地邀请我访问中国，并且感谢伟大的中国人民对我和我的同伴们在中华人民共和国访问期间所表示的兄弟感情。
    毛泽东同志，衷心地祝您身体健康，祝您在为中国人民所做的一切事业中获得成就！
    祝伟大的中国人民在社会主义建设事业中获得进一步的成就！
    愿中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊和兄弟般的团结日益巩固和发展！
        苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫（签字）
                     1957年5月26日于伊—14型2321号飞机
