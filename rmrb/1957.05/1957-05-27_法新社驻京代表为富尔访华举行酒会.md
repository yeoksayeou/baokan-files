Title: AFP Beijing Representative Holds Reception for Faure's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: The Agence France-Presse (AFP) representative in Beijing held a reception today in Beijing for former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife's visit to China.
    Among those attending the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, and foreign journalists in Beijing.

### 法新社驻京代表为富尔访华举行酒会

1957-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    法新社驻京代表为富尔访华举行酒会
    新华社26日讯　法新社驻北京的代表为前法国总理富尔和他的夫人来华访问，今天在北京举行了一个酒会。
    出席这个酒会的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，各国驻华使节和在北京的外国新闻记者。
