Title: Rapeseed Bumper Harvest in the Western Sichuan Plain (Picture)
Author: Gan Hongguang
Date: 1957-05-19
Page: 4

    Rapeseed Bumper Harvest in the Western Sichuan Plain
    The Western Sichuan Plain, a major rapeseed producing area in Sichuan Province, has achieved another bumper rapeseed harvest this year. This is a picture of members of the Second Agricultural Cooperative of Xipu Township, Pixian County, harvesting rapeseed. Gan Hongguang (Draft from Sichuan Daily)
