Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Ceylonese Ambassador and the Ceylon Sama Samaja Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 1

    Chairman Mao Receives the Ceylonese Ambassador
    And Receives the Ceylon Sama Samaja Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong received the first Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Mr. Perera, at 10:00 PM this evening.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department.
    During the reception, Ambassador Perera presented Chairman Mao Zedong with valuable gifts.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Ceylon Sama Samaja Party delegation headed by N.M. Perera at 11:00 PM this evening.
    Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席接见锡兰大使  并接见了锡兰新平等社会党代表团

1957-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见锡兰大使
    并接见了锡兰新平等社会党代表团
    新华社23日讯　毛泽东主席今晚十时接见了锡兰驻中国首任大使佩雷拉。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫，亚洲司司长章文晋。
    佩雷拉大使在接见时向毛泽东主席赠送了珍贵的礼品。
    新华社23日讯　毛泽东主席今晚十一时接见了以纳·马·佩雷拉为首的锡兰新平等社会党代表团。
    接见时在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪。
