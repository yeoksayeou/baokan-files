Title: Hansen Reconstitutes Danish Government
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, May 28th: The new Danish government was formed on the evening of May 27th, ending the twelve-day government crisis. The new government is a coalition of the Social Democratic Party, the Radical Party, and the Justice League, with former Prime Minister Hansen continuing to serve as Prime Minister and Foreign Minister. The government holds ninety-three seats in the one hundred and seventy-nine seat parliament, making it the first government in Denmark since World War II to hold a parliamentary majority.

### 汉森重组成丹麦政府

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    汉森重组成丹麦政府
    新华社28日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦新政府已经在5月27日晚间组成，结束了为期十二天的政府危机。新政府是由社会民主党、激进党和正义同盟联合组成的，前首相汉森仍然担任首相和外交大臣。这个政府在议会一百七十九个议席中共拥有九十三个席位，是第二次世界大战以后丹麦第一个拥有议会多数的政府。
