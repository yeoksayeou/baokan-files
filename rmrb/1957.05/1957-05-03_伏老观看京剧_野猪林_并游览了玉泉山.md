Title: Voroshilov Attends Peking Opera "Wild Boar Forest" and Visits Yuquan Mountain
Author:
Date: 1957-05-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a Peking Opera gala at the CPPCC Auditorium this evening to welcome Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

Chairman Voroshilov, along with Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, watched the full-length "Wild Boar Forest" performed by famous Peking Opera actors Yuan Shihai, Li Huiliang, and Du Jinfang.

Vice Chairman Rashidov and other accompanying personnel also attended the gala at invitation.

Those accompanying to watch the opera included Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Deng Zihui, Vice Chairmen Li Weihan and Chen Shutong, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang and Ji Pengfei, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Lin Boqu, Li Dequan, Shen Yanbing, Ma Yinchu, and Shao Lizi, and Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Yu Xinqing, Lao She, and Cao Jinghua, among others.

During the intermission, Lin Boqu and Zhang Xiruo, on behalf of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, presented Chairman Voroshilov with two thick volumes of the "Great Friendship" album to express their respect and wish him a long life. Chairman Voroshilov expressed his gratitude, saying that the great and eternal friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China will undoubtedly develop infinitely.

At the end of the performance, Chairman Voroshilov and Chairman Mao Zedong went on stage, presented flowers to the actors, and shook hands with them one by one to express their thanks.

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: Chairman Voroshilov and the distinguished Soviet guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De and Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited Yuquan Mountain at noon today.

### 伏老观看京剧“野猪林”  并游览了玉泉山

1957-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    伏老观看京剧“野猪林”
    并游览了玉泉山
    新华社2日讯  中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会今天晚上在政协礼堂举行京剧晚会，欢迎苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫。
    伏罗希洛夫主席同毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理一起，观看了著名京剧演员袁世海、厉慧良、杜近芳等演出的全本“野猪林”。
    拉希多夫副主席和其他的随行人员也应邀出席了晚会。
    陪同观剧的有副总理陈云、邓子恢、副委员长李维汉、陈叔通，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长王稼祥、姬鹏飞，中苏友好协会总会副会长林伯渠、李德全、沈雁冰、马寅初、邵力子和北京市中苏友好协会副会长张奚若、许德珩、余心清、老舍、曹靖华等人。
    在演出休息的时候，林伯渠和张奚若代表中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会向伏罗希洛夫主席献赠了“伟大的友谊”画册两厚册表示敬意，并且祝他万寿无疆。伏罗希洛夫主席表示感谢，说苏中两国人民的伟大的永恒的友谊必将无限发展。
    演出结束时，伏罗希洛夫主席同毛泽东主席登台，向演员们赠花，并一一握手表示感谢。
    新华社2日讯  伏罗希洛夫主席和苏联的贵宾们由朱德副主席和陈毅副总理陪同，今天中午游览了玉泉山。
