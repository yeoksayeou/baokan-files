Title: Egyptian Radio Warns Imperialism: Any Attempt at Aggression Will Meet New Failure; Thirteen Countries in "Canal Users Association" Agree to Accept Egyptian Conditions
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 9th May: Cairo Radio commentators said on the evening of the 8th that "Imperialism is preparing a new conspiracy against Egypt."

He said, "We cannot but warn the West once again that any attempt to undermine our neutral policy, to impose its policies on the Middle East, will meet with new failure."

He pointed out that many statements made by the West against Egyptian management of the Suez Canal revealed "imperialism wants Egypt to lose the canal and wants the Middle East situation to remain tense." The commentator said, "In fact, the canal issue is only one aspect of our differences with imperialism, and the main aspect of these differences lies in our firm determination to pursue a policy of active neutralism, in our opposition to aggressive military pacts, and in our hope to live peacefully with all countries in the world."

Xinhua News Agency, 9th May: London News: Among the fifteen member states of the "Suez Canal Users Association", representatives of thirteen countries have agreed to accept Egyptian conditions for using the Suez Canal "under protest". Only the representatives of France and Spain said they were still waiting for instructions from their governments.

This was the situation at the association's meeting on the 8th. As France and Spain object to taking immediate action on the canal issue, the association will hold another meeting on the 9th. Currently, the pressure from shipowners is increasing, and they often use the canal according to Egyptian conditions, regardless of the government's "advice."

### 埃及电台警告帝国主义  任何侵略尝试必将遭到新的失败  “运河使用国协会”中有十三国同意接受埃及条件

1957-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及电台警告帝国主义
    任何侵略尝试必将遭到新的失败
    “运河使用国协会”中有十三国同意接受埃及条件
    新华社开罗9日电　开罗电台评论员8日晚上说，“帝国主义正在准备对埃及进行新的阴谋”。
    他说，“我们不能不再一次警告西方，想破坏我国中立政策、想把它的政策强加于中东的任何尝试，都将遭到新的失败。”
    他指出，西方为了反对埃及管理苏伊士运河而发表的许多言论透露了“帝国主义要使埃及丧失运河，要使中东局势始终紧张”。这位评论员说：“其实，运河问题只是我们同帝国主义分歧所表现的一个方面，而这些分歧的主要方面在于我们有坚决的决心要奉行积极的中立主义政策，在于我们反对侵略性的军事公约，在于我们希望同世界上所有的国家和平共处。”
    新华社9日讯　伦敦消息：在“苏伊士运河使用国协会”的十五个会员国中，有十三国的代表都同意在“表示抗议”的情况下接受埃及的条件使用苏伊士运河，只有法国和西班牙的代表说，他们仍然要等待本国政府的指示。
    这是协会8日会议上的情况。由于法国和西班牙反对马上就使用运河问题采取行动，协会在9日还要再举行一次会议。目前，船主们的压力已经愈来愈大，他们往往不顾政府的“劝告”而按照埃及的条件使用运河。
