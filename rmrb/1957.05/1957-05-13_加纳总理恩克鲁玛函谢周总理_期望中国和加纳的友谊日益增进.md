Title: Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah Thanks Premier Zhou, Expressing Hope for Growing Friendship Between China and Ghana
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Premier Zhou Enlai recently received a letter from Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah, dated April 27th.

In the letter, Prime Minister Nkrumah stated: "I am very pleased to write to you on behalf of the government and people of Ghana, expressing my sincerest gratitude for your acceptance of the Ghanaian government's invitation and the dispatch of such an important delegation to attend our independence ceremonies. The presence of your representatives and the enthusiasm and joy they showed during our independence celebrations were a great honor to us.

As Ghana begins its new life of independence, it is greatly encouraged by the idea that countries all over the world stand with it. Therefore, the government and people of Ghana were very pleased to receive your congratulatory message. My government's policy is to live in peace with all nations; as I thank you now for your concern, I wish to assure you that my country looks forward to growing friendship and understanding with your country. I wish your people peace and continuous progress."

### 加纳总理恩克鲁玛函谢周总理  期望中国和加纳的友谊日益增进

1957-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    加纳总理恩克鲁玛函谢周总理
    期望中国和加纳的友谊日益增进
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理最近收到了加纳总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛4月27日的一封信。
    恩克鲁玛总理在信中说：“我非常高兴代表加纳政府和人民给你写信，向你表示最诚挚的感谢，感谢你接受了加纳政府的邀请并派遣了这样重要的一个代表团来参加我们的独立典礼。你的代表们的出席和他们在我们庆祝独立时所表现的热情和欢乐，使我们感到莫大的荣幸。
    在加纳开始独立的新生活的时候，它大大地受到了全世界的国家都和它站在一起这种思想的鼓舞。因此，加纳政府和人民收到你的贺电时感到非常满意。我国政府的政策是和所有的国家和平相处；在我现在感谢你的关心的时候，我愿向你保证，我国期望着日益增进和贵国的友谊和谅解。顺祝贵国人民安宁和不断进步。
