Title: Khrushchev Receives Delegation of Chinese Journalists

Author:
Date: 1957-05-09
Page: 5

    Khrushchev
    Receives Delegation of Chinese Journalists
    (People's Daily, Moscow, May 8th) Comrade Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received the Chinese journalists' delegation visiting the Soviet Union at 4 p.m. today. He answered in detail the questions raised by the delegation leader, Xu Zhucheng, regarding the domestic situation in the Soviet Union and international issues. Comrade Khrushchev had a cordial conversation with the delegation for more than an hour. Present at the reception were Satyukov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda," Ilichev, Director of the Information Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kharlamov, Deputy Director, as well as reporters from this newspaper and Xinhua News Agency stationed in the Soviet Union. After the conversation, Comrade Khrushchev took a photo with everyone in his office.

### 赫鲁晓夫  接见我新闻记者代表团

1957-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫
    接见我新闻记者代表团
    本报莫斯科8日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫同志今天下午4时接见了在苏联访问的中国新闻记者代表团，详尽地解答了代表团团长徐铸成所提的有关苏联国内情况和国际问题。赫鲁晓夫同志和代表团作了一个多小时的亲切的谈话。接见时，“真理报”总编辑萨丘科夫、苏联外交部新闻司司长伊利切夫和副司长哈尔拉莫夫以及本报和新华社驻苏联的记者也在座。谈话后，赫鲁晓夫同志和大家一起在他的办公室里拍了照。
