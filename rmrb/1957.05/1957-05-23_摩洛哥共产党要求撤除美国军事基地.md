Title: Moroccan Communist Party Demands Removal of American Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1957-05-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatches: The Moroccan government and a U.S. delegation are currently holding negotiations in Rabat regarding the status of the American military bases established in Morocco.

    The Moroccan Communist Party recently issued a statement demanding the removal of American military bases in Morocco. The statement emphasized that leasing military bases on Moroccan territory to the United States not only constitutes a direct threat to Morocco but also endangers Morocco’s national sovereignty.

    Recently, dockworkers in Casablanca, Morocco’s largest port, refused to unload cargo from a ship carrying military supplies for the American military base in Morocco. Prior to this, several strikes of the same nature had taken place.

    On May 18th, Hati, a leader of the Moroccan national movement, issued a statement in Cairo, accusing the Moroccan government of negotiating with the United States to establish atomic air force bases in Morocco.

### 摩洛哥共产党要求撤除美国军事基地

1957-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥共产党要求撤除美国军事基地
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：摩洛哥政府和美国代表团目前正在拉巴特就美国在摩洛哥建立的军事基地的地位问题举行谈判。
    摩洛哥共产党近日发表声明，要求撤除美国在摩洛哥的军事基地。声明强调指出，把摩洛哥领土上的军事基地租给美国，不但构成对摩洛哥的直接威胁，而且也危害摩洛哥的民族主权。
    最近，摩洛哥最大的港口卡萨布兰卡的码头工人拒绝替一艘装载军事物资供摩洛哥美军基地使用的轮船卸货。在这以前，曾举行过好几次同样性质的罢工。
    18日，摩洛哥民族运动领袖哈蒂在开罗发表声明，指责摩洛哥政府同美国谈判在摩洛哥建立原子空军基地。
