Title: Protest Against the South Vietnamese Government Forcing Overseas Chinese to Change Nationality; All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Supports the Statement of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese today issued a statement supporting the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China in protesting against the South Vietnamese government's forcing Overseas Chinese to change their nationality.

The statement said that the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese feels boundless indignation at the South Vietnamese government's recent crude acts of persecution against our country's Overseas Chinese in South Vietnam. The statement issued by the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China on May 20 regarding the South Vietnamese government's forced change of nationality for Overseas Chinese is solemn and just. The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese firmly supports this statement and will do its utmost to support all measures taken by our government to safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of Overseas Chinese abroad.

The statement also said that the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese expresses its cordial concern and sympathy to the broad masses of Overseas Chinese in South Vietnam who have been persecuted! And firmly supports the just struggle of Overseas Chinese in South Vietnam!

### 抗议南越政府强迫华侨改变国籍  归国华侨联合会拥护华侨事务委员会的声明

1957-05-28
第2版()
专栏：

    抗议南越政府强迫华侨改变国籍
    归国华侨联合会拥护华侨事务委员会的声明
    新华社27日讯　中华全国归国华侨联合会今天发表拥护中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会抗议南越政府强迫华侨改变国籍的声明。
    声明中说，最近南越政府迫害我国在越南南部的华侨的粗暴行为，中华全国归国华侨联合会感到无比的愤慨。5月20日中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会就南越政府强迫华侨改变国籍事件发表的声明，是严正的。中华全国归国华侨联合会坚决拥护这个声明，并且尽力支持我国政府对维护国外华侨正当权利和利益所采取的一切措施。
    声明还说，中华全国归国华侨联合会对被迫害的广大越南南部华侨表示亲切关怀和慰问！并坚决支持越南南部华侨的正义斗争！
