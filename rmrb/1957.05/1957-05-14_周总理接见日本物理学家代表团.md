Title: Premier Zhou Receives Japanese Physicists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese physicists delegation headed by Shinichiro Tomonaga in Beijing today.

Present at the reception were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhou Peiyuan, Chairman of the Chinese Physical Society, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 周总理接见日本物理学家代表团

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本物理学家代表团
    新华社13日讯　周恩来总理今天在北京接见以朝永振一郎为首的日本物理学家访华代表团。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院副院长吴有训，中国物理学会理事长周培源，以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志等人。
