Title: Our Football Team Loses Preliminary Match in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 12th: The Chinese football team played a preliminary match for the World Football Championship against the Indonesian national football team in Jakarta on May 12th. The Indonesian team won with a score of 2-0. The first half ended in a tie. However, in the second half, Indonesian center forward, Rahman, scored two consecutive goals, leading to their victory.

The two football teams will also hold a match in China. Whether the Indonesian team definitively wins the preliminary round will depend on the outcome of the next match. If the Chinese team wins, a third match will be considered.

### 我国足球队  在雅加达初赛失利

1957-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

    我国足球队
    在雅加达初赛失利
    新华社雅加达12日电　中国足球队同印度尼西亚国家足球队5月12日在雅加达举行了世界足球锦标赛的预赛。印度尼西亚队以二比零胜。上半场结束时，双方打成平局。但是，在下半场中，印度尼西亚队中锋拉曼连续踢进两球因而获胜。
    两国足球队还将在中国举行一次比赛。印度尼西亚队是否在预赛中确取胜利，将要看下次比赛的结果而定。如果是中国队获胜，将考虑进行第三场比赛。
