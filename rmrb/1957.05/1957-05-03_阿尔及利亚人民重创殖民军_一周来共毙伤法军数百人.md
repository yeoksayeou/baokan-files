Title: Algerian People Inflict Heavy Losses on Colonial Army: Hundreds of French Soldiers Killed and Wounded in One Week

Author:

Date: 1957-05-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 1st: The Algerian Liberation Front office in Cairo announced on April 30th that the Algerian National Liberation Army had heavily struck French imperialism in the past week.

On April 24th, the French army's losses were: 63 soldiers killed, 4 wounded, and 4 captured; on April 25th, 3 French soldiers were killed and 13 wounded; on April 26th, 62 French soldiers were killed and hundreds wounded. Algerians also seized a large number of weapons and ammunition from the French troops.

### 阿尔及利亚人民重创殖民军  一周来共毙伤法军数百人

1957-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚人民重创殖民军
    一周来共毙伤法军数百人
    新华社开罗1日电  驻在开罗的阿尔及利亚解放阵线办事处4月30日宣布，阿尔及利亚民族解放军过去一周中沉重地打击了法国帝国主义。
    4月24日，法军的损失是：六十三名士兵毙命，四名受伤，四名被俘；4月25日，法军有三人毙命，十三人受伤；4月26日，有六十二名法国士兵毙命，数百名受伤。阿尔及利亚人还从法国部队那里缴获了大量武器和弹药。
