Title: American Oil Companies Add Fuel to the Fire: Honduras and Nicaragua Open Fire
Author:
Date: 1957-05-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd—The border armed conflict between Honduras and Nicaragua, which erupted not long ago, has escalated, and both countries declared a state of emergency and national mobilization on May 2.

According to news from May 2, the Honduran and Nicaraguan armies and air forces engaged in combat near the city of Las Manos in the El Paraíso province of east-central Honduras. A large number of Nicaraguan troops are also concentrated near El Espino in the Choluteca province of southeastern Honduras, with the possibility of fighting breaking out at any time.

According to a communiqué issued by the Honduran government, on May 1, Honduran troops, after a fierce battle, recaptured Mocoron, a border town occupied by Nicaragua. However, Nicaraguan President Somoza strongly accused Honduras of invading Mocoron.

The Council of the Organization of American States, based in Washington, held a meeting on May 2 and decided to send a fact-finding mission to investigate the conflict between the two countries and convene a meeting of foreign ministers of the Americas to discuss the issue.

The U.S. State Department issued a statement on May 2, expressing "concern" about the conflict between the two countries.

Disputes between Honduras and Nicaragua over border territory are not new. Since American oil companies discovered oil fields near the disputed area, the situation has become even more complicated.

### 美国石油公司在火上加油  洪都拉斯和尼加拉瓜开火

1957-05-04
第6版()
专栏：

    美国石油公司在火上加油
    洪都拉斯和尼加拉瓜开火
    新华社3日讯　不久前爆发的洪都拉斯和尼加拉瓜两国之间的边境武装冲突已经扩大，两国在5月2日已经分别宣布紧急状态和全国总动员。
    据5月2日的消息说，洪都拉斯和尼加拉瓜的陆军和空军在洪都拉斯东中部的艾尔巴拉以索省的拉斯马诺斯城附近发生战斗。在洪都拉斯东南部雀罗德卡省的艾尔埃斯庇诺附近，也集中有大量的尼加拉瓜军队，有随时爆发战斗的可能。
    据洪都拉斯政府发表的一项公报说，5月1日洪都拉斯军队经过了一场激烈的战斗后，重新夺回了被尼加拉瓜占领的一个边境市镇莫科隆。但是，尼加拉瓜的总统索摩查强烈指责洪都拉斯侵略了莫科隆。
    设在华盛顿的美洲国家组织理事会5月2日举行会议，决定派一个调查团去现场调查两国冲突和召集美洲国家外长会议来讨论这个问题。
    美国国务院5月2日发表声明，对两国冲突表示“关注”。
    洪都拉斯和尼加拉瓜由于边界领土而引起的纠纷已经不是第一次了。自从美国石油公司在争执的地区附近发现石油产地以后，这种情况就更加复杂起来。
