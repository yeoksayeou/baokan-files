Title: Italian Communist Party Achieves Significant Victory in Local Elections
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, May 29th: In the local autonomous body elections held on May 26th in 142 cities and villages in Italy, the number of votes obtained by the Italian Communist Party greatly increased.

　　According to incomplete statistics from the election results of nineteen towns with a population of over 10,000, the number of votes obtained by the Communist Party candidates increased by 12% compared to the previous parliamentary elections in these towns.

　　The Communist Party members also achieved victory in the town council elections in towns with a population of less than 10,000.

　　The number of votes obtained by the Christian Democratic Party in the elections also increased. The neo-fascist party, the Italian Social Movement, and the National Monarchist Party both lost many votes. The votes of the Liberal Party, the Republican Party, the Social Democratic Party, and the Socialist Party either suffered losses or remained unchanged.

### 意共在地方选举获显著胜利

1957-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    意共在地方选举获显著胜利
    据新华社29日讯　罗马消息：在5月26日举行的意大利一百四十二个城市和村庄的地方自治机构的选举中，意大利共产党所得的票数大大增加了。
    据居民一万人以上的十九个市镇选举结果的不完全统计，共产党候选人所得票数同这些市镇前一届议会选举相比增加了12％。
    共产党人在居民一万人以下的市镇议会选举中也取得了胜利。
    天主教民主党在选举中得到的票数也有所增加，新法西斯政党意大利社会运动和国家保皇党都失去了许多选票。自由党、共和党、社会民主党和社会党的选票或有所损失，或保持不变。
