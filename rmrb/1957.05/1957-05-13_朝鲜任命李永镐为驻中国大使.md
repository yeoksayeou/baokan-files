Title: Korea Appoints Li Yonghao as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th - According to the Korean Central News Agency: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea announced on May 9th the appointment of Li Yonghao as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Korea to China, and at the same time relieved Cui Yi of his post as Ambassador to China.

### 朝鲜任命李永镐为驻中国大使

1957-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜任命李永镐为驻中国大使
    新华社平壤11日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会5月9日宣布，任命李永镐为朝鲜驻中国特命全权大使，同时免去崔一驻中国大使的职务。
