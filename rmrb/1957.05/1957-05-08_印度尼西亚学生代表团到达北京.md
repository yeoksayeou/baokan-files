Title: Indonesian Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. An eight-member Indonesian student delegation headed by Sahara Hutagalung arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 6th by train from Guangzhou.
　　The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation.
　　The delegation was warmly welcomed at the station by Hu Qili, chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others.

### 印度尼西亚学生代表团到达北京

1957-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚学生代表团到达北京
    新华社7日讯　以沙哈拉·胡塔加隆为首的印度尼西亚学生代表团八人，6日晚上乘火车从广州到北京。
    代表团是应中华全国学生联合会的邀请来中国进行参观访问。
    代表团在车站上受到了全国学联主席胡启立等人的热情欢迎。
