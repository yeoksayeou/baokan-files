Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Receives Albanian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 10th. Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen received the Albanian People's Republic People's Assembly delegation headed by Chairman Marko this evening.
    Those present at the meeting included Liu Ren, Zhang Youyu, Feng Jiping, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, Le Songsheng, Chai Zemin, Yang Shu, Fan Jin, Lü Zhan, and Xin Yi.
    Albanian Ambassador to China, Bali Li, was also present at the meeting.

### 彭真市长接见阿议会代表团

1957-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长接见阿议会代表团
    新华社10日讯　北京市市长彭真今天晚上接见了以马尔科主席为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有刘仁、张友渔、冯基平、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生、柴泽民、杨述、范瑾、吕展、辛毅。
    接见的时候，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里也在座。
