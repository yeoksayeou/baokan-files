Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1957-05-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 15th: The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their sixty-seventh meeting at the Palais des Nations here at ten o'clock this morning. The meeting adjourned at 11:35 a.m. The next meeting will be held on July 11th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1957-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦15日电　中美两国大使级会谈今天上午十点钟在这里国联大厦举行第六十七次会议。会议在十一点三十五分休会。下次会议将在7月11日举行。
