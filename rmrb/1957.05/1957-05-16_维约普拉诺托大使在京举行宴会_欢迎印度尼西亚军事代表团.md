Title: Ambassador Wiyopranoto Holds Banquet in Beijing to Welcome Indonesian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th. Indonesian Ambassador to China, Sukardjo Wiyopranoto, held a banquet at 8 p.m. tonight to welcome the entire Indonesian Military Friendship Delegation to China, headed by Major General Gatot Subroto.
    Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Chen Geng, General Li Kenong, General Li Da, General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, General Hong Xuezhi, General Yang Chengwu, General Chen Shiju, General Fu Qiutao, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, attended the banquet.
    Ambassador Wiyopranoto spoke first at the banquet, saying that this was the first time in history that Indonesia had sent such a large military delegation to visit a friendly country. He hoped that the visit of the Indonesian Military Friendship Delegation to China would further strengthen the bridge of friendship built during President Sukarno's visit to China last year.
    Marshal Peng Dehuai delivered a speech at the banquet, congratulating the Republic of Indonesia on its prosperity and strength. He proposed a toast to the health of President Sukarno, who will forever be remembered by the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 维约普拉诺托大使在京举行宴会  欢迎印度尼西亚军事代表团

1957-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    维约普拉诺托大使在京举行宴会
    欢迎印度尼西亚军事代表团
    新华社15日讯　印度尼西亚驻中国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托今晚八时设宴欢迎以加托特苏布罗托少将为首的印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团全体人员。
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅和中国人民解放军的高级将领陈赓大将、李克农上将、李达上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、洪学智上将、杨成武上将、陈士榘上将、傅秋涛上将、萧向荣中将等人出席了宴会。
    维约普拉诺托大使在宴会上首先讲话，他说印度尼西亚派出这样大的军事代表团到友好国家访问在历史上还是第一次。他希望印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团的访问能够进一步加强去年苏加诺总统访问中国时架起来的友谊桥梁。
    彭德怀元帅在宴会上致词祝贺印度尼西亚共和国繁荣强盛。并建议为中国人民和中国人民解放军永远难以忘记的苏加诺总统的健康干杯。
