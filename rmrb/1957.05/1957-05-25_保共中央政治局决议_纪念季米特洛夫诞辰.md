Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau Resolution on Commemorating Dimitrov's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-05-25
Page: 5

    Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau Resolution
    Commemorating Dimitrov's Birthday

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, May 21st. The Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party has made a resolution stipulating that activities commemorating the 75th anniversary of the birth of the Bulgarian people's leader and mentor, Dimitrov, will be held on June 18th this year. The resolution pointed out that the commemorative slogan from now on is: to further realize Dimitrov's testament, strive for the complete victory of socialism in Bulgaria, consolidate Bulgarian-Soviet friendship, further enhance the Marxist-Leninist unity of the Bulgarian Communist Party, strengthen the Party's ties with the working masses, strengthen the unity of socialist countries, and oppose any attempt to revise Marxism-Leninism.

### 保共中央政治局决议  纪念季米特洛夫诞辰

1957-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    保共中央政治局决议
    纪念季米特洛夫诞辰
    据新华社索非亚21日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局作出决议，规定在今年6月18日举行保加利亚人民领袖和导师季米特洛夫诞辰七十五周年的纪念活动。决议指出，今后纪念口号是：进一步实现季米特洛夫的遗训，争取社会主义在保加利亚的完全胜利，巩固保苏友谊，进一步增强保共的马克思列宁主义团结，密切党同劳动群众的联系，加强社会主义各国的团结，反对修正马克思列宁主义的任何企图。
