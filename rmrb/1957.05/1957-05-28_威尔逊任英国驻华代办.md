Title: Wilson Appointed British Chargé d’Affaires to China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The current British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, will soon be recalled home. The British government has obtained the Chinese government's agreement to appoint Archibald Duncan Wilson as the new British Chargé d'Affaires to China.

### 威尔逊任英国驻华代办

1957-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    威尔逊任英国驻华代办
    新华社27日讯　英国驻华代办欧念儒将于最近奉调回国。英国政府已取得中国政府的同意，将另派阿基波尔德·邓肯·威尔逊（Archibald Duncan Wilson）继任英国驻华代办。
