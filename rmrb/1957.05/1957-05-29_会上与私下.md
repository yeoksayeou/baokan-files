    At Meetings and in Private
    At a meeting
    Director ×: "Our cadres are now developing a 'contract system' mentality: the government provides dormitories and necessary furniture; and this and that, some even want the government to provide pots, bowls, ladles, and pans. What kind of thinking is this?..."
          At home
    Wife: "Then what do we do?"
    Director ×: "What do you mean, what do we do?"
    Wife: "Didn't you criticize the 'contract system' mentality? We already have a plush sofa in our room, and didn't you say last night to ask the administrative section for a set of leather sofas?"
    Director ×: "Fool!"　　Nan Yu

### 会上与私下
南雨
1957-05-29
第8版()
专栏：雨丝风片

    会上与私下
    在会上
    ×长：“我们的干部，现在滋长了一种包干制思想：公家给了宿舍，置办了必要的家具；还有这个那个，有的连锅、碗、瓢、盆也要公家给。这是一种什么思想呢？……”
          在家里
    妻：“那我们怎办呢？”
    ×长：“什么怎办？”
    妻：“不是你批判了包干制思想吗？我们房里已经有了绒沙发，你昨晚不是说还叫行政科给一套皮沙发吗？”
    ×长：“傻瓜！”　　南雨
