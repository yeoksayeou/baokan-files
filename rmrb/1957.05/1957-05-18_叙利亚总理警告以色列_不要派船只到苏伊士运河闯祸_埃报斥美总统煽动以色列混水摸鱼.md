Title: Syrian Prime Minister Warns Israel Not to Cause Trouble by Sending Ships to the Suez Canal; Egyptian Newspaper Accuses US President of Inciting Israel to Fish in Troubled Waters
Author:
Date: 1957-05-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Damascus: Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali told reporters on the 16th that if Israel attempts to send ships to forcibly pass through the Suez Canal, it "will ignite the flames of war in the Middle East." He said: He believes that "Israel and its allies are not prepared to suffer the consequences of such a war at present or even in the distant future."

Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Cairo, 17th: Egyptian newspapers on the 16th unanimously criticized US President Eisenhower's remarks supporting the passage of Israeli ships through the Suez Canal and the Gulf of Aqaba.

"The Republic" said: "This statement is clearly a provocation against Egypt and Saudi Arabia, and it can also be regarded as openly inciting Israel to fish in troubled waters."

"The People's Daily" said: "It is indeed regrettable that just as the situation is beginning to stabilize, the United States is encouraging Israel to take an action that could ignite a global war."

Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Jerusalem, Israeli area, 17th: Israeli Foreign Minister Golda Meir reiterated on the 16th that Israel has the right to use the Suez Canal and the Gulf of Aqaba. She threatened: "We will use all means to defend our rights."

An Israeli Foreign Ministry spokesman said on the 16th that Israel will postpone sending ships to attempt to pass through the Suez Canal pending a decision by the UN Security Council on the Suez Canal issue. France has submitted the canal issue to the Security Council.

### 叙利亚总理警告以色列  不要派船只到苏伊士运河闯祸  埃报斥美总统煽动以色列混水摸鱼

1957-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理警告以色列
    不要派船只到苏伊士运河闯祸
    埃报斥美总统煽动以色列混水摸鱼
    本报讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利16日对记者说：如果以色列企图派遣船只强行通过苏伊士运河，它就“会燃起中东战争的火焰”。他说：他相信“以色列和它的盟国都不准备在目前或者甚至在遥远的将来遭受这种战争的后果之苦。”
    新华社开罗17日电　埃及报纸16日一致抨击美国总统艾森豪威尔支持以色列船只在苏伊士运河和亚喀巴湾通航的谈话。
   “共和国报”说：“这种声明显然是对埃及和沙特阿拉伯的挑衅，同时它也可以被认为是公开煽动以色列混水摸鱼。”
   “人民报”说：“正当局势开始稳定下来的时候，美国却怂恿以色列采取一种可能会燃起全世界的战火的行动，这的确是令人遗憾的事。”
    据新华社17日讯　耶路撒冷以色列区消息：以色列外交部长梅厄夫人16日重申以色列有权使用苏伊士运河和亚喀巴湾，她威胁说：“我们将用一切手段来保卫我们的权利。”
    以色列外交部发言人16日说，以色列将推迟派遣船只尝试通过苏伊士运河以待联合国安全理事会在苏伊士运河问题上作出决定。法国已经把运河问题提交安全理事会。
