Title: Egyptian Minister of National Guidance Says U.S. Diplomacy is Murder for Money
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 6th - Egyptian Minister of National Guidance, Fathi Radwan, said in a talk published in the "Radio" weekly that "the foundation and driving force of American foreign policy lies in the atmosphere of murder and illegal profiteering that controls the social life of the American people."

Radwan made these remarks when commenting on an annual report published by the United States. He said that according to this report, there are an estimated thirty-four murders, four thousand illegal profiteering cases, and four thousand other crimes committed in the United States every day.

Radwan said that this "atmosphere" of murder and illegal profiteering is also reflected in American foreign policy, in "America's expanding arms movement, and in the actions of manufacturing atomic bombs and hydrogen bombs."

Radwan said that unless a solution is found to this "criminal atmosphere" in Western social life, humanity has no hope. He believes that the work of finding this solution should become the "primary responsibility of the United Nations."

### 埃及国家指导部长说  美国外交是谋财害命

1957-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及国家指导部长说
    美国外交是谋财害命
    新华社开罗6日电　埃及国家指导部长法西·拉德温在“广播”周刊发表的一篇谈话中说，“美国外交政策的基础和原动力在于控制着美国人民社会生活的那种杀人和非法图财的风气。”
    拉德温是在评论美国所发表的一份年度报告时这样说的。他说，据这份报告估计，美国每天发生谋杀案三十四起，非法图财案四千起，其他罪案四千起。
    拉德温说，这种杀人和非法图财的“风气”，也反映在美国的外交政策上，反映在“美国的扩张军备运动上，反映在制造原子弹和氢弹这种行动上”。
    拉德温说，除非找到解决西方社会生活中的这种“犯罪风气”的办法，否则人类就没有希望。他认为，寻找这种解决办法的工作应当成为“联合国的首要责任”。
