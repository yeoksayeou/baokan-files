Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Israeli Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: The Thirteenth Congress of the Communist Party of Israel is scheduled to convene on the 29th. The CPC Central Committee sent a congratulatory telegram. The telegram reads as follows: To all delegates of the Thirteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of Israel, Comrades:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal greetings to the Thirteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of Israel.

    At a time when the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt has failed, and American imperialism is taking the opportunity to intensify its enslavement of the peoples of the Middle East, your party is uniting the Israeli people, determined to defend peace in the Middle East, oppose the Eisenhower Doctrine, and continue to strengthen the anti-colonial unity of the peoples of the Middle East. The Communist Party of China congratulates you on achieving new successes in this struggle.
                                      The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                          May 28, 1957

### 中共中央电贺以共代表大会

1957-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺以共代表大会
    新华社29日讯　以色列共产党第十三次代表大会定29日召开。中共中央特致电祝贺。电文如下：以色列共产党第十三次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会向以色列共产党第十三次全国代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    当英国、法国、以色列侵略埃及失败，而美帝国主义正在乘机加紧对中近东各民族的奴役的时候，你们的党正在团结以色列人民，决心为保卫中近东和平、反对艾森豪威尔主义、并且为继续加强中近东各民族的反殖民主义的团结而进行斗争。中国共产党祝贺你们在这个斗争中取得新的成就。
                                      中国共产党中央委员会
                                          1957年5月28日
