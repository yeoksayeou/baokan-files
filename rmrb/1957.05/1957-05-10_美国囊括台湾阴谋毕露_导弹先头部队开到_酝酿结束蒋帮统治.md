Title: US Conspiracy to Annex Taiwan Fully Exposed: Missile Advance Units Arrive, Brewing the End of Chiang Gang's Rule
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 5

　　Comprehensive report from our newspaper. Xinhua News Agency reported: The US military "Taiwan Defense Command" commander Ingersoll, who occupies China's territory of Taiwan, announced in Taipei on the 8th that the advance units of the US "Matador" missile force have arrived in Taiwan, including personnel and missiles. Ingersoll reiterated the old tune of the so-called "deterrent effect" of missiles at a press conference on the 8th. Ingersoll said that this missile force will become part of the US military "Taiwan Defense Command" and will be commanded by Fred Dean, commander of the US Air Force Task Force stationed in Taiwan.

　　According to Washington news: US President Eisenhower appointed Vice Admiral Austin Doyle on the 8th to succeed Ingersoll as the commander of the so-called "Taiwan Defense Command." Doyle was previously the head of air force training for the Department of the Navy. Ingersoll will be transferred to be the principal of the Naval War College in Newport, Rhode Island.

　　According to New Delhi news: The "Hindustan Times" published a Washington dispatch on the 9th, saying that when the news that the United States was establishing missile launch sites with atomic warheads in Taiwan came out, the United States was engaging in behind-the-scenes conspiracies, attempting to place Taiwan under US trusteeship through the United Nations. The news also said that a group of the so-called "Citizens' Diplomacy Committee" of the wealthiest business corporations in the United States is promoting this conspiracy, which is "equivalent to the United States annexing Taiwan." The news said that at a banquet held in Baltimore a few days ago, former US Naval Intelligence Chief Admiral Ellis Zacharias, General Charles Willoughby, MacArthur's intelligence chief in the last war, and millionaire James Cromwell all vigorously advocated the idea of ​​"UN trusteeship" of Taiwan. The news said, "This will declare the end of Chiang Kai-shek's power there and make Taiwan a second Okinawa." According to John O'Donnell, Washington bureau chief of the "New York Daily News," the US Department of Defense "has long secretly discussed" a plan for direct US control of Taiwan, which stipulates that "the United States demands an end to Chiang Kai-shek's rule over Taiwan" and places Taiwan "under the trusteeship of the United Nations, and transfers administrative power to the United States." Commenting on the purpose of this plan, O'Donnell said that this plan "will make Taiwan another US Far East base like Okinawa."

　　The New York "Daily News" on the 8th described Taiwan as a "fortress on the US Far East Pacific defense line." It said that the United States sent an air force squadron equipped with missiles to Taiwan to "protect" this "fortress."

　　According to a report from the Washington correspondent of the British "Daily Express," at the same time that the United States established a missile launch site in Taiwan, a theory emerged in the United States advocating that the United Nations should allow the United States to administer Taiwan as a trust territory. The reporter said that if this trusteeship is implemented, Chiang Kai-shek's rule will end, and Taiwan will become "a second Okinawa."

### 美国囊括台湾阴谋毕露  导弹先头部队开到　酝酿结束蒋帮统治

1957-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国囊括台湾阴谋毕露
    导弹先头部队开到　酝酿结束蒋帮统治
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：霸占中国领土台湾的美军“协防台湾司令部”司令英格索尔8日在台北宣布，美国“斗牛士式”导弹部队的先头部队已经开到台湾，其中包括人员和导弹。英格索尔8日又在记者招待会上重弹了导弹的所谓“威慑作用”的老调。英格索尔说，这支导弹部队将成为美军“协防台湾司令部”的一部分，由美国空军驻台湾特遣队司令弗雷德·迪恩指挥。
    据华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔8日任命奥斯汀·道伊尔海军中将继英格索尔为所谓“协防台湾司令部”的司令官。道伊尔以前是海军部的空军训练负责人。英格索尔将调任美国罗德岛州新港的海军军事学校校长。
    据新德里消息：　“印度斯坦时报”9日发表的一则华盛顿消息说，当美国在台湾建立带原子弹头的导弹发射站的消息传出的时候，美国正在幕后进行阴谋活动，企图通过联合国将台湾交由美国托管。消息又说，美国最有钱的大商业公司的一个所谓“公民外交委员会”的集团正在推进这种“等于美国吞并台湾”的阴谋。消息说，几天前在巴尔的摩举行的一次宴会上，美国前海军情报首脑埃利斯·萨查里阿斯海军上将，上次大战中麦克阿瑟的谍报首脑查尔斯·威洛贝将军和富翁詹姆斯·克隆威尔都竭力鼓吹“联合国托管”台湾的主张。消息说，“这将宣布蒋介石在那里的势力的结束，而使台湾成为第二个冲绳岛。”而据“纽约每日新闻报”华盛顿办事处主任约翰·奥当纳报道，美国国防部“早就秘密讨论过”一个关于由美国直接控制台湾的计划，这个计划规定“美国要求停止蒋介石对台湾的统治”，而把台湾“置于联合国的托管之下，并且把管理权交给美国”。奥当纳在谈到这个计划的目的时说，这个计划“将要使台湾变成像冲绳一样的另一个美国远东基地”。
    纽约“每日新闻”8日把台湾说成是“美国的远东太平洋防线上的要塞”。它说，美国把装备导弹的一个空军中队派去台湾，就是在“保护”这个“要塞”。
    据英国“每日快报”驻华盛顿记者报道，在美国在台湾建立异弹发射站的同时，美国出现了一种论调，主张要求联合国让美国托管台湾。这个记者说，如果实行这种托管，蒋介石的统治就要完结，而台湾将成为“第二个冲绳”。
