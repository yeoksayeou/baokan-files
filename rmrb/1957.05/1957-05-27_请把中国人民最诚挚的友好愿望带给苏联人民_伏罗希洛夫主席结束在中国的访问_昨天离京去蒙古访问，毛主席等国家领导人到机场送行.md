Title: Please Convey the Chinese People's Most Sincere Friendly Wishes to the Soviet People Chairman Voroshilov Ends His Visit to China Yesterday, He Left Beijing for Mongolia, Chairman Mao and Other National Leaders Saw Him Off at the Airport

Author:

Date: 1957-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - Chairman Voroshilov and other distinguished Soviet guests concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, other national leaders, heads of government departments, various parties, organizations, and generals, all went to the airport to see them off.

The heads of the two great countries, China and the Soviet Union, spoke successively in the center of the airport, surrounded by 20,000 well-wishers.

In his farewell speech, Chairman Mao Zedong said that we are here to bid farewell to Comrade Voroshilov and his entourage with deep feelings of regret. Although we are temporarily separated, the hearts of the Chinese and Soviet people will always be closely linked together. Chairman Mao said that Comrade Voroshilov's visit to China has written a new and brilliant page in the glorious history of Sino-Soviet friendship. (The full text is on page 3 of today's newspaper)

Chairman Voroshilov then delivered a farewell speech. He said that as an old Bolshevik, I am very happy to see the victory of Marxism-Leninism in China. Chairman Voroshilov emphasized that we are pleased and proud that our country can have such a loyal friend and ally as the great People's Republic of China. No force in the world can shake the friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people, which is the solid foundation of the socialist family. (The full text is on page 3 of today's newspaper)

Before the speeches, Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by Chairman Mao Zedong, reviewed the guard of honor composed of soldiers from the army, navy, and air force. After the speeches, the two heads of state walked hand in hand past the cheering crowds surrounding the airport. At this time, applause and cheers resounded across the airport, and many young women ran up to present flowers to Chairman Voroshilov.

As the time for the plane to take off approached, national leaders led by Chairman Mao escorted Chairman Voroshilov to the front of the plane. Here, Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Voroshilov embraced warmly once again.

Then, Chairman Voroshilov warmly embraced Chairman Liu, Vice Chairman Zhu, Premier Zhou, and others one after another. At this time, the applause at the airport was even more enthusiastic, and the cheers were even louder.

Chairman Voroshilov then stood with Chairman Mao, allowing photographers to take pictures.

Chairman Voroshilov's plane left Beijing at 9:30 a.m., accompanied by Vice Chairman Maidar of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, who had come specifically to greet him.

Today, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China went to the airport to see him off. (Attached picture)

Top picture: Chairman Voroshilov waves his hat to bid farewell to Chairman Mao and others after boarding the plane.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 请把中国人民最诚挚的友好愿望带给苏联人民  伏罗希洛夫主席结束在中国的访问  昨天离京去蒙古访问，毛主席等国家领导人到机场送行

1957-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    请把中国人民最诚挚的友好愿望带给苏联人民
    伏罗希洛夫主席结束在中国的访问
    昨天离京去蒙古访问，毛主席等国家领导人到机场送行
    据新华社26日讯　伏罗希洛夫主席和苏联的其他贵宾们结束了在中国的访问，今天上午乘专机离开北京，到蒙古人民共和国进行访问。
    毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和国家的其他领导人员，政府各部门、各党派、各团体的负责人以及将军们，都到机场送行。
    中苏两个伟大国家的元首站在被两万欢送群众环绕起来的机场正中先后讲了话。
    毛泽东主席在致欢送词中说，我们怀着深深的惜别的心情来欢送伏罗希洛夫同志和他的随行人员，我们虽然暂时分别了，但是中苏两国人民的心将永远紧紧地联结在一起。毛主席说，伏罗希洛夫同志在中国的访问给中苏友好关系的光荣历史写下了新的、灿烂的一页。（全文见今日本报第三版）
    伏罗希洛夫主席接着发表了告别演说。他说，我作为一个老布尔什维克，看到马克思列宁主义的思想在中国的胜利感到非常高兴。伏罗希洛夫主席强调说，我们的国家能有像伟大的中华人民共和国这样的忠实朋友和同盟者，使我们感到欣慰和骄傲。世界上没有任何力量能够动摇苏中两国人民的友谊，这种友谊是社会主义国家大家庭的坚固基础。（全文见今日本报第三版）
    在讲话以前，伏罗希洛夫主席由毛泽东主席陪同检阅了陆海空三军战士组成的仪仗队。讲话以后两位元首手挽着手从环绕在机场上欢送的群众面前走过。这时，机场上掌声和欢呼声响成一片，许多女青年跑上前来向伏罗希洛夫主席献花。
    飞机起飞的时间就要到了，以毛主席为首的国家领导人员，一直送伏罗希洛夫主席到飞机前面。在这里，毛泽东主席和伏罗希洛夫主席又一次亲热地拥抱在一起了。
    接着，伏罗希洛夫主席又先后同刘委员长、朱副主席、周总理等热烈拥抱。这时候，机场上的掌声更加热烈，欢呼声也更加响亮。
    伏罗希洛夫主席又同毛主席站在一起，让摄影记者们照像。
    伏罗希洛夫主席乘坐的飞机在九时三十分离开北京，和他同机的还有专程前来迎接他的蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席迈达尔等。
    今天，苏联驻中国大使尤金和各国驻华使节都到机场送行。（附图片）
