Title: Indonesian Ambassador Holds Reception to Welcome Indonesian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, held a reception this evening to welcome the entire Indonesian military goodwill delegation to China, headed by Major General Gatot Soebroto.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Chen Geng, General Li Kenong, General Li Da, General Xiao Hua, and General Gan Siqi, were invited to attend the reception.

Also present at the reception were the ambassadors of India, Yugoslavia, and Sweden, and military attachés from various countries stationed in China.

After the reception, Indonesian military attaché to China, Colonel Warouw, hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the Indonesian military goodwill delegation to China.

### 印度尼西亚大使举行酒会  欢迎印度尼西亚军事代表团

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚大使举行酒会
    欢迎印度尼西亚军事代表团
    新华社13日讯　印度尼西亚驻中国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托今晚举行酒会，欢迎以加托特·苏布罗托少将为首的印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团全体人员。
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、李克农上将、李达上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将等人应邀出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的还有印度、南斯拉夫、瑞典大使和各国驻中国的武官。
    酒会结束后，印度尼西亚驻华武官瓦罗伍上校设宴欢迎印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团全体团员。
