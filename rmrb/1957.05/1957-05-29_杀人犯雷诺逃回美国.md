Title: Murderer Reynolds Flees Back to the United States
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, May 28th: Murderer Reynolds arrived at Travis Air Force Base northeast of San Francisco on the evening of the 26th.

    This murderer left Travis in an Air Force utility vehicle. The American authorities even gave him a thirty-day vacation after he returned home.

### 杀人犯雷诺逃回美国

1957-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    杀人犯雷诺逃回美国
    新华社28日讯　旧金山消息：杀人犯雷诺26日晚到达旧金山东北翠菲斯空军基地。
    这个杀人凶手乘空军公用汽车离开翠菲斯。美国当局还在他回国后给了他三十天的假期。
