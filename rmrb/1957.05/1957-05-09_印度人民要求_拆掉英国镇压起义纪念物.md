Title: Indian People Demand the Removal of Monuments Commemorating British Suppression of the Uprising
Author:
Date: 1957-05-09
Page: 6

    Indian People Demand
    Removal of Monuments Commemorating British Suppression of the Uprising
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th - As India enthusiastically prepares to commemorate the centenary of the 1857 uprising for independence, demands are growing across the country for the removal of monuments erected by the British to glorify the suppression of this struggle.
    In Delhi, there are several such monuments, including the "Mutiny Memorial" and a statue of British General Nicholson, who suppressed the uprising.

### 印度人民要求  拆掉英国镇压起义纪念物

1957-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    印度人民要求
    拆掉英国镇压起义纪念物
    新华社新德里8日电　印度各地在热烈准备纪念1857年争取独立起义一百周年的时候，都要求拆除英国人为了颂扬镇压这次斗争而建立的纪念建筑物。
    在德里有一些这样的纪念建筑物，其中包括“平乱纪念碑”、镇压这次起义的英国将军尼科耳森的纪念像等。
