Title: Chairman Mao Congratulates Prasad on Re-election as President
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: Chairman Mao sent a telegram on the 12th to congratulate Rajendra Prasad on his re-election as President of the Republic of India. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:

I am delighted to learn of Your Excellency's re-election as President of the Republic of India, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of India on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I believe that with Your Excellency's re-election as President, the friendly and cooperative relations between China and India will be further developed. I wish Your Excellency and the people of India new achievements in the cause of building the Republic of India and safeguarding peace.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, May 12, 1957, Beijing

### 毛主席贺普拉沙德连任总统

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席贺普拉沙德连任总统
    新华社13日讯　毛泽东主席12日电贺印度共和国总统普拉沙德连任总统。电文如下：印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德阁下：
    欣悉阁下连任印度共和国总统，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向阁下和印度人民致热烈的祝贺。我相信，随着阁下再度荣任总统，中印两国间的友好合作关系将会得到进一步的发展。祝阁下和印度人民在建设印度共和国和维护和平的事业中取得新的成就。
          中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1957年5月12日于北京
