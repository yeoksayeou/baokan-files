Title: CPUSA National Committee Discusses Party Policy, Calls on People to Launch Struggle to Defend Civil Rights
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 5

New York Dispatch: According to a report in the May 6th New York "Daily Worker," the Communist Party USA headquarters announced that the Party's National Committee held a plenary session on April 27th and 28th to discuss a series of policy issues and established a twenty-member executive committee to carry out tasks during the recess of the National Committee.

    The National Committee meeting discussed Dennis's report on the work of the committee and how to organize it. The report called for strengthening party unity and immediately ending factionalism and sectarianism.

    The main policy issues discussed by the National Committee members were the civil rights struggle and the situation of the domestic Marxist newspapers.

    The committee adopted Benjamin Davis' report on the civil rights struggle. Davis emphasized the dangerous situation facing civil rights and pointed out that the American people are carrying out a growing and multifaceted mass movement to oppose racist attacks on civil rights.

    The National Committee read a collective report from a subcommittee on the situation of the Marxist newspapers, especially the "Daily Worker." The committee adopted the report and unanimously and firmly believed that these Marxist newspapers must be maintained and that full support must be guaranteed to the editors and staff of these newspapers in their current campaign to expand sales and raise funds.

    The National Committee elected William Z. Foster as honorary chairman.

    The elected members of the full executive committee are: George Blake Charney, Benjamin Davis, Eugene Dennis, Earle Durham, Fred Fine, Elizabeth Gurley Flynn, William Z. Foster, John Gates, James Jackson, Sidney Stein, David Davis, Claude Lightfoot, Louis Lumer, George Meyers, Carl Ross, Michael Russo, Martha Stone, Gil Wintner, Dorothy Healy, and Michael Lima.

### 美共全国委员会讨论党的政策  号召人民展开斗争维护民权

1957-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    美共全国委员会讨论党的政策
    号召人民展开斗争维护民权
    纽约消息：据5月6日的纽约“工人日报”报道，美国共产党总部宣布，共产党全国委员会在4月27日和28日举行了全体会议，讨论了一系列政策问题，并且成立了一个二十人的执行委员会以便在全国委员会休会期间执行任务。
    全国委员会这次会议讨论了丹尼斯关于委员会的工作以及怎样组织委员会的工作的报告。报告号召加强党的团结，立即结束派系和宗派主义。
    全国委员会委员们所讨论的主要政策问题是民权斗争和国内马克思主义报纸的情况。
    委员会通过了本杰明·戴维斯所提出的关于民权斗争的报告。戴维斯着重指出了民权所面临的危险局面，并且指出美国人民正在进行日益壮大的、多方面的群众运动，来反对种族主义者对民权的进攻。
    全国委员会宣读了一个小组委员会所提出的关于马克思主义报纸的情况、特别是关于“工人日报”的一份集体报告。委员会通过了这个报告，并且一致地、坚决地认为，必须维持这些马克思主义报纸，并且保证全力支持这些报纸的主编和工作人员目前发动的扩大销售量和筹募经费的运动。
    全国委员会选举了威廉·泽·福斯特为名誉主席。
    当选的全体执行委员会委员是：乔治·布莱克·查尼、本杰明·戴维斯、尤金·丹尼斯、厄尔·达拉姆、弗雷德·法因、伊丽沙白·葛莱·弗林、威廉·泽·福斯特、约翰·盖茨、詹姆斯·杰克逊、悉尼·斯坦因、戴维·戴维斯、克劳德·莱特富特、路默、乔治·迈尔斯、卡尔·罗斯、迈克尔·鲁索、马尔萨·斯通、吉尔·温特、道乐赛·希利和迈克尔·利马。
