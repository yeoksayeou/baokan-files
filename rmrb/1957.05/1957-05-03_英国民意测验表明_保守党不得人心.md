Title: British Opinion Poll Shows Conservative Party is Unpopular
Author:
Date: 1957-05-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 2nd -- According to a report in the British Liberal Party's "News Chronicle," the results of a recent opinion poll show that if a general election were held in Britain now, 50% of voters would vote for the Labour Party; the Conservative Party would only get 41% of the votes.

### 英国民意测验表明  保守党不得人心

1957-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    英国民意测验表明
    保守党不得人心
    新华社2日讯  伦敦消息：据英国自由党的“新闻纪事报”报道，最近一次民意测验的结果表明，假如目前英国举行大选，50％的选民将投工党的票；保守党只能得到41％的选票。
