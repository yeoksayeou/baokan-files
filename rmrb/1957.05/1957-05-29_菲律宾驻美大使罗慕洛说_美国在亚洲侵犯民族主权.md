Title: Philippine Ambassador to the US Romulo Says US Infringes on National Sovereignty in Asia
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 28th: Philippine Ambassador to the United States, Romulo, said on the evening of the 26th that the recent anti-American incidents in Taiwan, Japan, and South Korea were not incited by communists. Their origin is the United States' infringement of local national sovereignty.
    He said: "The situation is very clear. These riots are not limited to one specific area, but are spreading to all places in the Far East where the United States maintains military bases."

### 菲律宾驻美大使罗慕洛说  美国在亚洲侵犯民族主权

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    菲律宾驻美大使罗慕洛说
    美国在亚洲侵犯民族主权
    据新华社28日讯　纽约消息：菲律宾驻美大使罗慕洛26日晚上说，最近在台湾、日本和南朝鲜发生的反美事件不是共产党煽动的。它们的起因是美国侵犯了当地的民族主权。
    他说：“情况很明显，这些骚乱并不限于一个特定地区，而是蔓延于美国在远东保有军事基地的一切地方”。
