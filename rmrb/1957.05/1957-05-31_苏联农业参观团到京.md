Title: Soviet Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, May 30th. The Soviet delegation to visit the Chinese Agricultural Exhibition, led by Soviet Deputy Minister of Agriculture Petrov, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

    This delegation is jointly composed of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture and the Ministry of State Farms.

### 苏联农业参观团到京

1957-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联农业参观团到京
    据新华社30日讯　由苏联农业部副部长彼得罗夫率领的苏联参观中国农业展览会代表团一行三十九人，在今日下午乘机到达北京。
    这个代表团是由苏联农业部和国营农场部联合组成的。
