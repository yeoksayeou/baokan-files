Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai says at the meeting of advanced ordnance workers' representatives: Stand firm in a period of major social changes
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, called on officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army at the Chinese People's Liberation Army Ordnance Advanced Workers' Representative Conference this morning to always maintain and carry forward their glorious traditions.
	He said that the reason why the people of the whole country trust and love the Chinese People's Liberation Army is because they stand on the side of the people, serve the people wholeheartedly, and endure hardship before enjoying comfort.
	Marshal Peng Dehuai asked the representatives to stand firm, distinguish right from wrong, and correctly treat and handle contradictions among the people during this period of major social changes in China.
	This morning, Marshal Peng Dehuai listened to the typical speeches of the representatives at the conference and had cordial conversations with the representatives.

### 彭德怀元帅在军械先进工作者代表会议上说  在社会大变动时期站稳立场

1957-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅在军械先进工作者代表会议上说
    在社会大变动时期站稳立场
    新华社29日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部部长彭德怀元帅今天上午在中国人民解放军军械先进工作者代表会议上号召解放军官兵永远保持和发扬光荣传统。
  他说，全国人民所以信任爱戴中国人民解放军，就是因为他们站在人民的立场，忠心耿耿地为人民服务，吃苦在前，享受在后。
    彭德怀元帅要求代表们在中国社会大变动时期站稳立场，明辨是非，正确地对待和处理人民内部的矛盾。
    彭德怀元帅今天上午听了代表的大会典型发言，并同代表们亲切地谈了话。
