Title: Chairman Mao Appoints Zhang Canming as Ambassador to Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1957-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed Zhang Canming as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Ceylon.
