    Preparing to Celebrate the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Revolution
    Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Calls for Commemorating the Great Festival with Practical Actions

    The Bulgarian "Workers' Cause" newspaper published on April 28th the resolution of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party on celebrating the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution, calling on the Bulgarian people to celebrate this great festival with practical actions to complete this year's national economic plan.

    The resolution stipulates that Bulgarian newspapers and radio stations will widely publicize the great significance of the October Revolution Festival, publicize the great achievements made by the Soviet Union and other socialist countries in socialist construction and defending world peace, and publicize Marxism-Leninism; relevant publishing institutions in Bulgaria will publish various books about the October Revolution; a Bulgarian-Soviet friendship month will be held domestically from October 7th to November 7th.

    The resolution also specifically stipulates the extensive activities across the country to celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution. These activities include holding mass rallies and parades, exhibitions, concerts, issuing commemorative stamps, etc.

### 筹备庆祝十月革命四十周年  保共中央号召用实际行动纪念伟大的节日

1957-05-05
第5版()
专栏：兄弟党的生活

    筹备庆祝十月革命四十周年
    保共中央号召用实际行动纪念伟大的节日
    保加利亚“工人事业报”4月28日登载了保加利亚共产党中央政治局关于庆祝十月革命四十周年的决议，号召保加利亚人民用完成今年国民经济计划的实际行动来庆祝这个伟大的节日。
    决议规定：保加利亚报刊和广播电台将广泛宣传十月革命节的伟大意义，宣传苏联和其他社会主义国家在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平方面取得的巨大成就，宣传马克思列宁主义；保加利亚有关出版机构将出版有关十月革命的各种书籍；从10月7日到11月7日国内将举行保苏友好月。
    决议还具体规定了全国各地广泛庆祝十月革命四十周年的活动。这些活动包括举行群众大会和游行、展览会、音乐会、发行纪念邮票等。
