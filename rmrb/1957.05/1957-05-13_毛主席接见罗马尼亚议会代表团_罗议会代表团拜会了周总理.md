Title: Chairman Mao Receives Romanian Parliamentary Delegation; Romanian Parliamentary Delegation Calls on Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th May: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Romanian Grand National Assembly and Bucharest City People's Council delegation led by Chairman Con Pavelescu this afternoon. The hosts and guests had a cordial and warm conversation on the current international situation and the current tasks of the Chinese people.
    Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and Qu Wu and Yu Xinqing were also present at the reception.
    Also present at the reception was Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica.
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th May: The Romanian Grand National Assembly and Bucharest City People's Council delegation, led by Chairman Con Pavelescu, paid a visit to Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon. Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, was also present at the meeting. (Attached picture)
    Left: Chairman Mao with the Romanian Grand National Assembly and Bucharest City People's Council delegation.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 毛主席接见罗马尼亚议会代表团  罗议会代表团拜会了周总理

1957-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见罗马尼亚议会代表团
    罗议会代表团拜会了周总理
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了以康·帕伏列斯库主席为首的罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团。宾主就目前国际局势和中国人民当前的任务等问题作了亲切而热诚的交谈。
    接见的时候，周恩来总理，彭真副委员长和屈武、余心清也在座。
    接见的时候，在座的还有罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤。
    新华社12日讯　以康·帕伏列斯库主席为首的罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团，在今天下午拜会了周恩来总理。拜会的时候，罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤也在座。（附图片）
    左：毛主席同罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团合影。
                                        新华社记者　邹健东摄
