Title: Voroshilov Visits President Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 20th: The Presidential Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a communique today stating that Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, visited President Ho Chi Minh at 4:00 PM today.

　　Present from the Soviet side were Rashidov, Yelyutin, Fedyurin, and the Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Zimyanin. Present from the Vietnamese side were Ton Quang Phiet, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly; Deputy Prime Minister Vo Nguyen Giap; Truong Chinh, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Pham Hung, members of the Political Bureau of the Vietnam Workers' Party; and other leaders.

　　President Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Voroshilov exchanged greetings. Then, they held cordial talks.

### 伏老拜访胡志明主席

1957-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    伏老拜访胡志明主席
    新华社河内20日电　越南民主共和国主席府今天发表公报说，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫今天下午四点钟访问了胡志明主席。
    苏联方面在场的有拉希多夫、叶留金、费德林和苏联驻越南民主共和国大使吉米亚宁。越南方面在场的有国会常务委员会副委员长孙光阀，副总理武元甲，越南劳动党政治局委员长征、阮维祯、范雄和其他领导人员。
    胡志明主席和伏罗希洛夫主席互致问候。接着进行了亲切的谈话。
