Title: Romanian Guests Visit Shenyang and Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 5

Combined reports from our newspaper:

The delegation from the Romanian Grand National Assembly and the Bucharest City People's Congress, headed by Chairman Con Pavelescu, left Beijing by plane on the morning of May 4 to visit Shenyang and other places.

Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, accompanied the delegation on the visit.

After arriving in Shenyang on the morning of May 4, the delegation visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory, the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, the city and the workers' village. The delegation visited Anshan on May 5.

When the delegation left Shenyang for Shanghai on May 6, Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Huang Oudong, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, and responsible persons from democratic parties and people's organizations in the province and city, people from the Catholic community, and Romanian experts in Shenyang, went to the airport to see them off.

### 罗马尼亚贵宾访问沈阳上海

1957-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚贵宾访问沈阳上海
    本报综合报道　以康·帕伏列斯库主席为首的罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团4日早晨乘飞机离开北京，去沈阳等地参观访问。
    中共中央政治局候补委员康生、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长屈武和罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤陪同代表团参观访问。
    代表团在4日上午到达沈阳后，曾参观了沈阳第一机床厂、东北工业陈列馆，参观了市容和工人村。代表团5日到鞍山参观。
    代表团于6日离开沈阳去上海时，辽宁省省长杜者蘅，中共辽宁省委员会第一书记黄欧东，沈阳市市长刘宝田和省市各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，天主教界人士以及在沈阳的罗马尼亚专家等多人，曾到机场欢送。
