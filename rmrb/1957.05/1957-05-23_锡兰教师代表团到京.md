Title: Ceylon Teachers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd -- The Ceylon University Teachers' Association delegation of seven, led by Dr. B. A. Baptiste, Chairman of the Ceylon University Teachers' Association, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 22nd at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China.

The delegation will learn about the development of university education and scientific work in our country and conduct academic exchanges with Chinese professors and scholars.

### 锡兰教师代表团到京

1957-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰教师代表团到京
    据新华社22日讯　应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来中国访问的锡兰大学教师协会代表团七人，由锡兰大学教师协会主席布·阿·巴普提斯特博士率领，在22日晚到达北京。
    代表团将在我国了解大学教育和科学工作的发展情况，同我国教授和学者进行学术交流。
