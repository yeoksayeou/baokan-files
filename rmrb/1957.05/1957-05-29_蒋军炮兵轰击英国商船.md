Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops Shell British Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 28th — At 21:10 on the evening of the 27th, the British merchant ship "Cheong Fong," en route from Hong Kong and sailing near the Wuyu Island sea area approaching Xiamen, was shelled by Chiang Kai-shek troops artillery from Dadan Island, with a total of more than twenty shells fired. Our army's coastal defense artillery immediately carried out suppression fire, covering the "Cheong Fong's" safe entry into the port. Subsequently, Chiang Kai-shek troops artillery on Dajinmen and Xiaojinmen Islands blindly shelled Qiancun and other areas on Xiamen Island with more than one hundred shells. Our army artillery again carried out suppression fire.

### 蒋军炮兵轰击英国商船

1957-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    蒋军炮兵轰击英国商船
    新华社福建前线28日电　27日晚上二十一时十分，由香港开来的英国商船“成丰号”经浯屿岛附近海面驶近厦门，遭到大担岛上的蒋军炮兵轰击，共发炮二十多发。我军海防炮兵随即进行了压制射击，掩护“成丰号”安全进港。接着，大、小金门岛上的蒋军炮兵又向厦门岛前村等地盲目轰击一百多发。我军炮兵再次进行了压制射击。
