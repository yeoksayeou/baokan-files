Title: Turkish Troops Prepare for War on Syrian Border
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 20th: According to the "Al-Jamahir" newspaper on May 18th, "Turkish troops massed on the Syrian border are digging trenches and building many small airports. American-made jet bombers and fighter planes are taking off from these airports."
    The "Al-Jamahir" newspaper said that the Syrian "Committee for the Defense of the Sanjak of Alexandretta" strongly protested this "threat to peace and security in the Middle East" in a telegram to the relevant authorities.

### 土军在叙边境备战

1957-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    土军在叙边境备战
    据新华社20日讯　大马士革消息：据“群众报”5月18日报道，“集结在叙利亚边境上的土耳其军队正在掘壕沟，建筑许多小型机场，美国制造的喷气轰炸机和战斗机正在从这些机场起飞。”
      “群众报”说，叙利亚的“保卫亚历山大勒达州委员会”在一份给有关当局的电报中强烈抗议这个
  “对中东和平和安全的威胁”。
