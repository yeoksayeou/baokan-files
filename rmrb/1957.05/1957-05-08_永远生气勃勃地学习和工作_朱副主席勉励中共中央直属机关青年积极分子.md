Title: Always Study and Work Vigorously: Vice Chairman Zhu Encourages Young Activists of the Organs Directly Under the CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Vice Chairman Zhu De attended the representative meeting of young activists from the organs directly under the CPC Central Committee this afternoon. At the meeting, he instructed the young activists to work hard together with the youth of the whole country, to diligently study their professions, and to assiduously learn political theory and culture; at the same time, they should love labor, exercise their bodies, and always be vigorous, always be modest and prudent, always maintain the fine tradition of hard work and plain living, conscientiously implement the policy of building the country through diligence and thrift, and actively participate in the cause of the country's economic and cultural construction.

Vice Chairman Zhu De said to the young activists: "We old Communist Party members share a common feeling, which is to love our young comrades very much and place infinite hope in them. We are greatly comforted to hear of your progress and to see you studying and working vigorously. Because, we see the future of the Party's cause in you. With such a good younger generation as you, there is a reliable guarantee for realizing the great ideals of the Chinese people."

This meeting was held in Beijing from May 4th to 7th.

The meeting commended and rewarded 337 young activists from the organs directly under the Central Committee who had been active in professional work, study, Communist Youth League work, and other social work. Among them, 14 people spoke at the meeting, introducing their advanced deeds in working hard and studying their professions in their respective positions.

### 永远生气勃勃地学习和工作  朱副主席勉励中共中央直属机关青年积极分子

1957-05-08
第2版()
专栏：

    永远生气勃勃地学习和工作
    朱副主席勉励中共中央直属机关青年积极分子
    新华社7日讯　朱德副主席今天下午出席了中共中央直属各机关青年积极分子代表会议。他在会上教导青年积极分子们要和全国青年一道，努力做好工作，认真地钻研业务，刻苦地学习政治理论和文化；同时要热爱劳动，锻炼身体，要永远生气勃勃，永远谦虚谨慎，永远保持艰苦朴素的优良作风，认真执行勤俭建国的方针，积极参加祖国的经济建设和文化建设的事业。
    朱德副主席向青年积极分子们说：“我们老共产党员抱有一个共同的心情，就是非常爱年轻的同志，对于年轻的同志寄予无限的希望。听说你们有了进步，看见你们生气勃勃地学习和工作，我们就得到了很大的安慰。因为，从你们身上我们看到了党的事业的未来，有了你们这样好的青年一代，实现中国人民的伟大理想，就有了可靠的保证。”
    这次会议是在5月4日到7日在北京举行的。
    会议表扬和奖励了中央直属机关青年中在业务工作、学习、青年团工作和其他社会工作等方面的积极分子三百三十七人。其中有十四人在会上发言，介绍了他们如何在各自工作岗位上努力学习，埋头钻研业务的先进事迹。
