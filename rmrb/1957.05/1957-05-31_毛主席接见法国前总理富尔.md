Title: Chairman Mao Receives Former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Mao Zedong received former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife tonight. Premier Zhou Enlai, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, were present at the reception. (Attached picture)

　　Chairman Mao Zedong talking with Mr. and Mrs. Faure. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong

### 毛主席接见法国前总理富尔

1957-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见法国前总理富尔
    新华社30日讯　毛泽东主席在今天夜里接见了法国前总理富尔和夫人。接见的时候在座的，有周恩来总理和中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席和富尔夫妇谈话　新华社记者　邹健东摄
