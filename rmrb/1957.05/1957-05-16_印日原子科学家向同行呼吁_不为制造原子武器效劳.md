Title: Indian and Japanese Atomic Scientists Call on Colleagues Not to Serve the Manufacture of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1957-05-16
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 15th: Bangalore News: Dr. C.V. Raman, the 1930 Nobel Prize winner and Indian physicist, appealed on May 13th to scientists around the world not to participate in the manufacture of weapons of mass destruction. Dr. Raman supported the recent statement by West German scientists refusing to help manufacture nuclear weapons. He said that scientists should not help manufacture nuclear weapons even if it means going hungry.

　　Dr. Raman made these remarks on May 13th at the Shakyamuni Nirvana Commemoration Ceremony held by the Maha Bodhi Society in Bangalore.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th: Twenty-five atomic physicists in Japan, including Hideki Yukawa and Shinichiro Tomonaga, issued a joint declaration yesterday calling for a ban on atomic weapons.

　　The Japanese physicists' declaration was issued in response to the "Göttingen Declaration" issued by West German atomic physicists last month. The "Göttingen Declaration" stated that these West German atomic physicists would not cooperate in any way in the manufacture, testing, and research of atomic and hydrogen bombs. The Japanese atomic physicists expressed their intention to adopt the same attitude.

### 印日原子科学家向同行呼吁  不为制造原子武器效劳

1957-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    印日原子科学家向同行呼吁
    不为制造原子武器效劳
    据新华社新德里15日电　班加罗尔消息：1930年诺贝尔奖金获得者、印度物理学家克·维·拉曼博士，在5月13日吁请全世界的科学家们不要参与制造大规模毁灭性武器。拉曼博士支持西德科学家最近发表的拒绝帮助制造原子核武器的声明。他说，科学家们应当即使挨饿也不帮助制造原子核武器。
    拉曼博士5月13日在班加罗尔的摩诃菩提会举行的释迦牟尼涅槃纪念仪式上说了这些话。
    据新华社15日讯　东京消息：日本的汤川秀树和朝永振一郎等二十五位原子物理学家昨天发表联合宣言，呼吁禁止原子武器。
    日本物理学家们的这个宣言是为了响应西德原子物理学家在上个月发表的“戈丁根宣言”而发表的。“戈丁根宣言”声明这些西德的原子物理学家们对于制造、试验和研究原子弹和氢弹完全不实行合作。日本原子物理学家们表示要采取同样态度。
