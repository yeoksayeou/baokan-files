Title: Soviet Union's National Bonds Oversubscribed This Year; Sales Halted
Author:
Date: 1957-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd Dispatch from TASS: As of May 20th, the Soviet Union's 1957 National Economic Development Bonds had sold 14,535,686,000 rubles. The original planned sales target was 12,000,000,000 rubles.

According to instructions from the Soviet Council of Ministers, the Soviet Ministry of Finance has ordered all regions to cease further sales of the bonds.

### 苏联今年国家公债  认购超额　停止推销

1957-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联今年国家公债
    认购超额　停止推销
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到5月20日为止，苏联发行的1957年度发展国民经济公债已经推销了一百四十五亿三千五百六十八万六千卢布。原定的推销总额是一百二十亿卢布。
    根据苏联部长会议的指示，苏联财政部已经下令各地停止继续推销公债。
