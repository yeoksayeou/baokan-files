Title: Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, Holds Reception Celebrating the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th. Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to celebrate the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

    Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, and Vice Premier He Long attended the reception.

    Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass at the reception, wishing prosperity to the Kingdom of Afghanistan and happiness to its people, wishing continuous development of the friendship between Afghanistan and China, and wishing good health to His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan and His Excellency Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

    Prior to this, the host of the reception, Ambassador Samad, invited Chinese figures from all walks of life and foreign envoys to China attending the reception to join him in a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai, to the happiness of the Chinese people, and to the lasting friendship between China and Afghanistan.

    At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai also held friendly conversations with the envoys of various countries.

### 庆祝阿富汗王国独立纪念日  阿驻华大使萨马德举行招待会

1957-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿富汗王国独立纪念日
    阿驻华大使萨马德举行招待会
    新华社27日讯　阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德今天下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝阿富汗王国独立纪念日。
    周恩来总理、彭德怀副总理和贺龙副总理出席了招待会。
    周恩来总理在招待会上举杯，祝阿富汗王国繁荣和人民幸福，祝阿富汗和中国的友谊不断发展，祝阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下和阿富汗王国首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗阁下的身体健康。
    在这以前，招待会的主人——萨马德大使请出席招待会的中国各方面人士和各国驻华使节同他一起，为毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的健康，为中国人民的幸福，为持久的中国和阿富汗的友谊干杯。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理还同各国使节们进行了友谊的谈话。
