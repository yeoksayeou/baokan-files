Title: Netherlands Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception in Beijing to Celebrate the Birthday of the Queen of the Kingdom of the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Netherlands Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. S. van Karnebeek, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of Queen Juliana of the Kingdom of the Netherlands.

    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception, where he proposed a toast to the birthday of Her Majesty Queen Juliana of the Netherlands, to the prosperity of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, and to the prosperity of the Dutch people.

    At the beginning of the reception, Chargé d'Affaires S. van Karnebeek proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and to the prosperity of the Chinese people.

    During the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Chargé d'Affaires S. van Karnebeek and his wife frequently raised their glasses to each other, wishing each other good health.

    Among those attending the reception were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China.

### 庆祝荷兰王国女王诞辰  荷兰代办在北京举行酒会

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝荷兰王国女王诞辰
    荷兰代办在北京举行酒会
    新华社30日讯  荷兰驻华代办司曼今天下午举行酒会，庆祝荷兰王国女王朱丽安娜诞辰。
    周恩来总理出席了酒会，他在会上提议为庆贺荷兰女王朱丽安娜陛下诞辰，为荷兰王国的兴旺，为荷兰人民的繁荣而干杯。
    司曼代办在酒会开始时，向参加酒会的客人提议为中华人民共和国主席毛泽东的健康，为中国人民的繁荣而干杯。
    酒会进行中，周恩来总理和代办司曼及夫人彼此频频举杯，互祝健康。
    参加酒会的有外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民以及各国驻华外交使节和官员。
