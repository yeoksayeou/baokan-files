Title: Indonesian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th — The Indonesian military goodwill delegation to China, headed by Lieutenant General Gatot Soebroto, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Army, arrived in Beijing by train at 8:11 PM today.

General Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Senior General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong went to the station to welcome them.

Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were Indonesian Ambassador to China Sukardjo Wirjopranoto and embassy staff, as well as military attaches from various countries stationed in China.

The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 7th. Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin and Colonel Mao Chen, military attache of our embassy in Indonesia, went to Shenzhen to greet them and accompanied them to Beijing. (Photo attached)

The members of the Indonesian military goodwill delegation to China, accompanied by General Chen Geng (center), are stepping out of the station. On the right is head of the delegation, Soebroto. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photo

### 印度尼西亚军事代表团到京

1957-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚军事代表团到京
    据新华社10日讯　以陆军副参谋长加托特·苏布罗托少将为首的印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团今天下午八时十一分乘火车到北京。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈庚大将、李克农上将，中华人民共和国国防部副部长李达上将，总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将和萧向荣中将等前往车站欢迎。
    到车站欢迎的还有印度尼西亚驻我国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和使馆人员以及各国驻华武官。
    代表团是在7日到达广州的，朱开印大校和我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆武官茅琛上校曾前往深圳迎接，并陪同他们来到北京。（附图片）
    印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团人员，在陈赓大将（中）陪同下步出车站。右是苏布罗托团长。　　　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
