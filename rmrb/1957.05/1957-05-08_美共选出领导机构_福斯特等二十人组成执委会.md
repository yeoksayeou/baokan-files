Title: CPUSA Elects Leading Body; Foster and 19 Others Form Executive Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 5

    CPUSA Elects Leading Body
    Foster and 19 Others Form Executive Committee
    The National Committee of the Communist Party USA held a meeting in New York from April 27th to 28th. The meeting elected an Executive Committee composed of twenty people. William Foster, Eugene Dennis, Elizabeth Gurley Flynn, Ben Davis, and other prominent party workers were elected to the Executive Committee. The Executive Committee will work during the adjournment of the National Committee meeting.
    According to reports, the main issues discussed by the National Committee were: "The struggle for civil rights and the status of Marxist publications in the United States." (Xinhua News Agency)

### 美共选出领导机构  福斯特等二十人组成执委会

1957-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    美共选出领导机构
    福斯特等二十人组成执委会
    美国共产党全国委员会4月27日到28日在纽约举行了会议，会议选出了由二十个人组成的执行委员会。威廉·福斯特，尤金·丹尼斯，伊丽沙白·弗林，本·戴维斯和其他著名的党的工作者都被选入了执行委员会。执行委员会将在全国委员会会议休会期间进行工作。
    据消息指出，全国委员会讨论的主要问题是：“为争取公民权利和马克思主义刊物在美国的地位而斗争”。　（新华社）
