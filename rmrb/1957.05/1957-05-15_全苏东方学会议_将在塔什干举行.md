Title: All-Union Conference of Oriental Studies to Be Held in Tashkent
Author:
Date: 1957-05-15
Page: 5

(From our correspondent in Moscow, May 13th) A comprehensive All-Union Conference of Oriental Studies will be held in Tashkent from May 27th to June 4th. The conference will hear approximately one hundred academic reports and speeches, covering the political, economic, and cultural aspects of the Soviet Oriental republics and Asian countries. Gafurov, Director of the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, will report on the situation and tasks of Soviet Oriental Studies, which will be discussed. Regarding research on China, there will be the following special reports: "The International Significance of the Victory of the Chinese People's Revolution," "The History of the People's Republic of China," "The Development and Path of Modern Chinese Literature," and "Language Issues of the People's Republic of China." At the plenary session, there will be a series of academic reports, such as: "The Collapse of Imperialist Colonial System," "The International Significance of the Bandung Conference," "The Strengthening of Economic and Cultural Ties Between the Soviet Union and Oriental Countries," and "The Development of the Soviet Socialist State System in Uzbekistan." In group meetings, academic views will be exchanged on the following aspects: the historical, economic, and cultural connections of the peoples of the Orient, their history of struggle, their ancient and medieval history, archaeology and ethnology, their literature, language, manuscripts, etc.

### 全苏东方学会议  将在塔什干举行

1957-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    全苏东方学会议
    将在塔什干举行
    本报莫斯科13日电　一个内容丰富的全苏东方学会议将于5月27日到6月4日在塔什干举行。会议将听取约一百个左右的学术报告和发言，包括苏联东方各共和国和亚洲国家的政治、经济、文化各个方面。将由苏联科学院东方学研究所所长加弗罗夫报告苏联东方学的情况和任务，并且将加以讨论。关于中国的研究，将有如下专题报告：“中国人民革命胜利的国际意义”，“中华人民共和国的历史学”，“现代中国文学发展的情况和道路”，“中华人民共和国的语言问题”。在全体会议上，将有一系列学术报告，如像：“帝国主义殖民制度的崩溃”，“万隆会议的国际意义”，“苏联和东方国家的经济和文化联系的加强”，“苏维埃社会主义国体在乌兹别克斯坦的发展”。在分组会议上，将分别就如下方面交换学术见解：东方各族人民的历史、经济和文化的联系，他们的斗争史，他们的古代和中世纪的历史、考古学和人种学，他们的文学、语言、遗稿等。
