Title: Sukarno Calls for People's Unity, Cabinet Decides to Establish National Council
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 6

    Sukarno Calls for People's Unity
    Cabinet Decides to Establish National Council
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th - President Sukarno delivered two speeches on April 29th in Cirebon, West Java province, and on the way from Jakarta to Cirebon in Karawang. In his speeches, he called on the people to unite. He hoped that some people would not incite separatism and only care about the interests of their political parties.
    Hundreds of thousands of people participated in the mass rally in Cirebon.
    In his speech, President Sukarno pointed out that Indonesia is an important country, and therefore it has attracted the attention of imperialism.
    He said that Indonesia insists on implementing an independent foreign policy. Indonesia loves peace, but loves independence even more. Whoever wants to invade us, we will oppose and resist him.
    President Sukarno mentioned that some people say that Indonesia is friendly with communist countries and has become a puppet of the communists. He said that Indonesia is willing to be friendly with all countries.
    President Sukarno also refuted the Western newspapers' claims that Indonesia has abandoned democracy. He said that when the West did not understand democracy, Indonesia had already implemented its own democracy.
    President Sukarno mentioned that Voroshilov will visit Indonesia soon. He said that any distinguished guests will be warmly welcomed.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th - According to the government gazette, the Indonesian cabinet decided at a meeting held on April 29th that it would quickly organize the National Council in accordance with the law.
    The gazette did not further explain whether the National Council would be established according to emergency decrees or ordinary laws. The establishment of the National Council is the first point of the new cabinet's program.

### 苏加诺号召人民团结  内阁决定成立民族委员会

1957-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺号召人民团结
    内阁决定成立民族委员会
    新华社雅加达30日电  苏加诺总统4月29日在西爪哇省的井里汶和从雅加达到井里汶途中的加拉横发表了两次演说。他在演说中号召人民团结。他希望某些人不要煽动分裂主义和只顾政党的利益。
    井里汶的群众大会有几十万人参加。
    苏加诺总统在讲话中指出，印度尼西亚是一个重要的国家，因此它受到了帝国主义的注意。
    他说，印度尼西亚坚持执行独立自主的外交政策。印度尼西亚爱和平，但是更爱独立。谁要侵犯我们，我们就要反对和抵抗他。
    苏加诺总统提到有人说，印度尼西亚同共产党国家友好，成为共产党的傀儡。他说，印度尼西亚愿意同一切国家友好。
    苏加诺总统还驳斥了西方报纸认为印度尼西亚抛弃民主的说法。他说，当西方还不懂得民主的时候，印度尼西亚已经实行了自己的民主。
    苏加诺总统谈到，伏罗希洛夫即将访问印度尼西亚。他说，任何贵宾来访都将受到热烈的欢迎。
    据新华社雅加达30日电  据政府公报，印度尼西亚内阁在4月29日举行的会议上决定：将迅速根据法令组织民族委员会。
    公报没有进一步说明将根据紧急法令还是根据普通法令来成立民族委员会。成立民族委员会是新内阁政纲的第一条。
