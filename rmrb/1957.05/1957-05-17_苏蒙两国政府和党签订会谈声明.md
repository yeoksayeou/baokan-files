Title: Sino-Mongolian Governments and Parties Sign Joint Communique
Author:
Date: 1957-05-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th May - The Government of the Mongolian People's Republic and the delegation of the People's Revolutionary Party signed a joint communique between the Soviet and Mongolian governments and a statement of talks between the two parties with the Soviet government and Communist Party delegation on 15th May in the Kremlin.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th May - The delegation of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Tsedenbal, returned home by plane today after a five-day friendly visit to the Soviet Union.

Among those who greeted the delegation at the airport were Bulganin, Mikoyan, Molotov, Suslov, Khrushchev, and others.

Tsedenbal gave a farewell speech before boarding the plane. He said that the Soviet-Mongolian talks and the signed documents were of great significance. He expressed his gratitude to the Soviet people for their warm reception.

### 苏蒙两国政府和党签订会谈声明

1957-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    苏蒙两国政府和党签订会谈声明
    新华社莫斯科16日电　蒙古人民共和国政府和人民革命党代表团同苏联政府和共产党代表团5月15日在克里姆林宫签定了苏蒙两国政府联合声明和两党会谈声明。
    新华社莫斯科16日电　以部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团在苏联进行了五天的友好访问后，已在今天乘飞机离开莫斯科回国。
    在机场欢送代表团的有布尔加宁、米高扬、莫洛托夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫等。
    泽登巴尔在登机前发表了告别演说。他说，苏蒙谈判和所签订的文件具有巨大的意义。他对苏联人民给予的热情接待表示感谢。
