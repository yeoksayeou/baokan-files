Title: Japanese Physicists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. A Japanese physicists delegation arrived in Beijing today.

The delegation is composed of twenty Japanese physicists. Those who arrived in Beijing today were the head of the delegation, Shinichiro Tomonaga, President of Tokyo University of Education, and the deputy head, Kanetaka Ariyama, Professor of Nagoya University. Members included Yasuhiko Yamanouchi, Professor of Tokyo University, Masashi Kikuchi, Director of the Institute for Nuclear Study, Tokyo University, Yoshio Suga, Professor of Tokyo University, Tokutaro Hironé, Professor of Tohoku University, Wataru Watase, Professor of Osaka City University, Mokichiro Nogami, Professor of Tokyo University, Kenji Fushimi, Professor of Osaka University, Yataro Sekido, Professor of Nagoya University, Koichi Kamata, Researcher at the Scientific Research Institute, Jiro Koba, Professor of the Yukawa Institute for Theoretical Physics, Kyoto University, Hiroshi Yoshinaga, Professor of Osaka University, Hideaki Tamaki, Professor of Tokyo University, Tasuku Morita, Associate Professor of Kyushu University, Eiji Yamada, Associate Professor of Nagoya University, Masata Suekane, Assistant at Osaka City University, and Shoichiro Kawasaki, Graduate Student at Tokyo University. In addition, two other members, Yoichi Fujimoto, Professor of the Institute for Nuclear Study, Tokyo University, and Osamu Ogawa, Lecturer at Hiroshima University, will arrive in Beijing on the 12th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport today included Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhou Peiyuan, Chairman of the Chinese Physical Society, and over forty scientific figures including Shi Ruwei, Peng Huanwu, Zhang Wenyu, Chu Shenglin, and Pei Lisheng.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. (Attached picture)

### 日本物理学家代表团到京

1957-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    日本物理学家代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　日本物理学家访华代表团今日抵达北京。
    代表团由日本二十位物理学家组成。今天到达北京的有团长、东京教育大学校长朝永振一郎，副团长、名古屋大学教授有山兼孝。团员有东京大学教授山内恭彦，东京大学原子核研究所所长菊池正士，东京大学教授菅义夫，东北大学教授广根德太郎，大阪市立大学教授渡濑让，东京大学教授野上茂吉郎，大阪大学教授伏见康治，名古屋大学教授关户弥太郎，科学研究所研究员镰田甲一，京都大学基础物理学研究所教授木庭二郎，大阪大学教授吉永弘，东京大学教授玉木英彦，九洲大学副教授森田右，名古屋大学副教授山田英二，大阪市立大学助教末包昌太和东京大学研究生川崎昭一郎。另外，还有二位团员东京大学原子核研究所教授藤本阳一和广岛大学讲师小川修三将在12日抵达北京。
  今天到机场欢迎的有中国科学院副院长吴有训，中国物理学会理事长周培源以及施汝为、彭桓武、张文裕、褚圣麟和裴丽生等科学界人士四十多人。
    代表团是应中国科学院邀请来我国访问的。（附图片）
