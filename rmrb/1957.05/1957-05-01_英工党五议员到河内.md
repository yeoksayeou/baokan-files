Title: Five British Labour Party MPs Arrive in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 30th – According to the Vietnam News Agency: A delegation of five British Labour Party Members of Parliament, headed by Harold Davies, arrived in Hanoi on the afternoon of April 30th. They are visiting Vietnam at the invitation of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front.

### 英工党五议员到河内

1957-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    英工党五议员到河内
    新华社河内30日电  据越南通讯社讯：以哈罗德·戴维斯为首的五名英国工党议员4月30日下午到达河内。他们是应越南祖国战线中央委员会秘书处的邀请访问越南的。
