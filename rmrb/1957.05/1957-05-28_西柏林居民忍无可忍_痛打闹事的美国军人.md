Title: West Berlin Residents, At the End of Their Patience, Beat Up Trouble-Making American Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 27th: According to the "Berliner Morgenpost", some unbridled American soldiers clashed with residents of the Neukölln district of West Berlin in a bar on the 25th. Enraged by the Americans' insults, the residents beat up six American soldiers.

The "Berliner Morgenpost" reported that West Berlin police, arriving at the scene, arrested six West Berlin citizens indiscriminately. The American hooliganism and the West Berlin police's protection of the American soldiers who caused the trouble aroused the anger of West Berlin residents.

### 西柏林居民忍无可忍  痛打闹事的美国军人

1957-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    西柏林居民忍无可忍
    痛打闹事的美国军人
    新华社柏林27日电　据“柏林日报”消息，一些横行无忌的美国军人25日在西柏林新科隆区一个酒吧间里同该区居民发生了冲突。为美国人的侮辱所激怒的居民们痛打了六个美国军人。
    “柏林日报”报道：赶到出事地点的西柏林警察竟然不分青红皂白地逮捕了六名西柏林市民。美国人的流氓行为以及西柏林警察袒护美国肇事军人的行动激起了西柏林居民的愤怒。
