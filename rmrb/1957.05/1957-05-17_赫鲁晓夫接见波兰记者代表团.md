Title: Khrushchev Receives Polish Journalists Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, May 16th, TASS reports: On May 15th, Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received the Polish journalist delegation currently visiting Moscow, as well as the Moscow-based journalists from "People's Tribune" and the Polish Press Agency.

During the conversation, Khrushchev answered questions raised by the Polish journalists regarding the Soviet Union's domestic and foreign policies, the international workers' movement since the 20th Congress of the CPSU, and further consolidating Soviet-Polish friendship.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见波兰记者代表团

1957-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见波兰记者代表团
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫15日接见了正在莫斯科访问的波兰新闻记者代表团，以及“人民论坛报”和波兰通讯社驻莫斯科的记者。
    赫鲁晓夫在谈话中回答了波兰记者提出的有关苏联的内外政策、苏共二十次代表大会以后的国际工人运动和进一步巩固苏波友谊的问题。
