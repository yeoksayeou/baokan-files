Title: Tree Branch Spices

Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 4

    Tree Branch Spices
    The Zhejiang Huangmen Haiyan Chemical Factory has recently found three new raw materials. In spring and summer, they use the pine branches and seedlings cut down by farmers to refine pine needle oil. In autumn, they use wild plant Litsea cubeba seeds to extract lemon essence, and in winter, they squeeze tangerine peels to produce tangerine peel oil.

### 树枝制香料

1957-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    树枝制香料
    浙江黄门海岩化工厂最近找到了三种新原料。春天和夏天，利用农民砍下来的松树枝苗，炼制松针油。秋季利用野生植物山苍树籽提炼柠檬香精，冬季利用桔皮榨桔皮油。
