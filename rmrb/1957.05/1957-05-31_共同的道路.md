Title: Common Road
Author: Qíní Sàkē
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 5

    Common Road
    Writer, Zini Sako, member of the Albanian People's Assembly Delegation
    I can't speak Chinese, never mind that, comrades, I want to sing in Albanian, a bright song that warms my heart. We are far apart, separated by thousands of mountains and rivers, yet we visit each other frequently, like old friends and brothers, like coming to our own home. Because the world has changed for you, because we are not the same as before, when the cruiser "Aurora" sent out thunderous roars, calling mankind to liberation. All roads are open to us, our lives are lit up, the working masses raise their swords and guns, the raging fire sweeps, the shells roar, the struggle for liberation is like a raging wave. In the struggle, we shed blood, blood stained our banner red. That deep red flag is as red as blood, we hold it high, in the smoke, in the storm. Marxism-Leninism teaches us, in a common language. We have suffered, we have triumphed, we cherish a common ideal, to build communism.
          May 1957, Beijing
                Translated by Fan Chengzhu

### 共同的道路
作家  齐尼·萨科
1957-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    共同的道路
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团团员、作家  齐尼·萨科中国话我不会讲，且不管它，同志，我希望用阿尔巴尼亚语言来歌唱，一支明朗的歌子，温暖着我的心房。我们遥远地相隔着，千山万水把我们阻挡，然而我们却频繁来往，像老朋友和亲兄弟，像来到了自己的家乡。因为世界对你们变了样，因为我们也不像以往，当“阿芙乐尔”巡洋舰发出雷鸣似的轰响，召唤人类走向解放。条条道路都为我们开放，我们的生活里燃起亮光，劳动大众举起刀枪，烈火席卷，炮弹轰鸣，解放的斗争有如怒涛骇浪。在斗争中我们抛洒了鲜血，鲜血染红了我们的大旗。那深红色的旗帜红得像血，我们把它高高地擎起，在硝烟中，在风暴里。马克思列宁主义教导我们，用共同的言语。我们受过苦，我们已经胜利，我们怀着共同的理想，为了建设共产主义。
          1957年5月于北京
                范承祚译
