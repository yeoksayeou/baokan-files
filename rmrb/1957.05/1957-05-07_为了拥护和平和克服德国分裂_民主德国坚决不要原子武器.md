Title: In order to uphold peace and overcome the division of Germany, the German Democratic Republic resolutely rejects atomic weapons

Author:

Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 5th: The German News Agency reported that the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, met with eight prominent scientists of the German Democratic Republic on April 29 to discuss scientific issues arising from the dangers of atomic armament and the peaceful utilization of atomic energy.

　　Grotewohl analyzed for the scientists the political situation arising from the atomic armament policy of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the announcement that atomic weapons would be stored on West German soil. He pointed out that this situation has aggravated international tensions and created new obstacles to the peaceful reunification of Germany.

　　Grotewohl emphasized that atomic weapons will not be stored or produced on the territory of the German Democratic Republic, and nothing will change this policy in the future. He requested the scientists to cooperate with the government and provide advice to promote its policy of maintaining peace and peacefully utilizing their work results.

　　The scientists present unanimously declared that they had full confidence in the government's policy of maintaining peace and overcoming the division of Germany.

### 为了拥护和平和克服德国分裂  民主德国坚决不要原子武器

1957-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    为了拥护和平和克服德国分裂
    民主德国坚决不要原子武器
    新华社柏林5日电　据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥在4月29日接见了民主德国的八位著名科学家，同他们讨论了由于原子军备的危险而产生的科学方面的问题以及和平利用原子能的问题。
    格罗提渥向科学家们分析了由于北大西洋公约组织的原子军备政策和宣布在西德的土地上存放原子武器而产生的政治局势。他指出，这种情况加剧了国际紧张局势和造成了德国和平统一的新障碍。
    格罗提渥强调说，在民主德国的领土上不存放或生产原子武器，而且在将来，任何东西都改变不了这一方针。他要求科学家们同政府合作，提供意见，以便促进它执行维护和平和和平利用他们的工作成果的政策。
    在场的科学家们一致宣布他们对政府维护和平和克服德国分裂的政策具有充分的信心。
