Title: Bulgarian Sports Commission Chairman Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Vladimir Stoichev, Chairman of the Supreme Committee for Physical Culture and Sports of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Cai Shufan, Vice Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China. Chairman Vladimir Stoichev and his wife are visiting China at the invitation of He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China.

### 保体育运动最高委员会主席到京

1957-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    保体育运动最高委员会主席到京
    据新华社11日讯　保加利亚人民共和国部长会议体育运动最高委员会主席弗拉吉米尔·斯托伊切夫及其夫人今天上午坐飞机到达北京。他们在机场上受到了中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩的亲切欢迎。弗拉吉米尔·斯托伊切夫主席及其夫人是应国务院副总理、中华人民共和国体育运动委员会主任贺龙的邀请来中国进行友好访问的。
