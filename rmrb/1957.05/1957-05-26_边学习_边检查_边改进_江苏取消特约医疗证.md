Title: Learning, Checking, and Improving Simultaneously: Jiangsu Cancels Special Medical Certificates

Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 2

Learning, Checking, and Improving Simultaneously
Jiangsu Cancels Special Medical Certificates
　　This dispatch from Nanjing: The Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, in accordance with the spirit of rectification—learning, checking, and improving simultaneously—has already corrected certain unreasonable systems that could be immediately corrected.
　　On May 20th, the Standing Committee of the Provincial Party Committee held a meeting. At the meeting, it was decided to cancel the special medical certificates for cadres at the thirteenth grade and above. Like ordinary cadres, they should follow hospital procedures for medical treatment in the future. At the same time, it was stipulated that the cars used by senior cadres for work needs are strictly prohibited from being used for personal matters from now on.

### 边学习　边检查　边改进  江苏取消特约医疗证

1957-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

    边学习　边检查　边改进
    江苏取消特约医疗证
    本报南京电　中共江苏省委根据整风中边学习、边检查、边改进的精神，对某些能立即改正的不合理制度已做了改正。
    5月20日，省委常务委员会举行了会议。会上，决定取消十三级以上干部的特约医疗证。和一般干部一样，他们以后就医应按医院制度办事。同时规定，高级干部因工作需要而使用的小汽车，今后一律不准作私事之用。
