Title: Academy of Sciences Academic Committee Plenary Session Sends Telegram in Support of the Anti-American Struggle of Taiwan Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 1

    Academy of Sciences Academic Committee Plenary Session
    Sends Telegram in Support of the Anti-American Struggle of Taiwan Compatriots
    (Our Newspaper) The Second Plenary Session of the Academic Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences passed a telegram on May 30th in support of the patriotic anti-American struggle of the Taiwan compatriots. The telegram said:
    We scientific workers feel boundless anger and hatred towards the wanton atrocities of the American aggressors in Taiwan. We feel infinitely deep sympathy for the plight of Taiwan compatriots in dire straits!
    Taiwan compatriots, like the people of the whole country, are inviolable. The recent outbreak of patriotic anti-American demonstrations by Taiwan compatriots is a stern warning to the American aggressors, and it has encouraged the just struggle of the people of all countries still under the oppression of American imperialism against the American aggressors. We express our greatest respect for this heroic patriotic action of the Taiwan compatriots!
    We call on the scientific workers in Taiwan and all Taiwan compatriots to unite, carry forward the patriotic tradition, and resolutely carry the great patriotic anti-American struggle through to the end. We scientific workers, together with the people of the whole country, resolutely support the just struggle of the Taiwan compatriots, and work together to drive the American aggressors out of Taiwan and peacefully unify the great motherland!

### 科学院学部委员会全体会议  通电声援台湾同胞反美斗争

1957-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    科学院学部委员会全体会议
    通电声援台湾同胞反美斗争
    本报讯　中国科学院学部委员会第二次全体会议在5月30日通过关于声援台湾同胞的爱国反美斗争的通电。通电说：
    我们科学工作者对美国侵略者在台湾的恣意暴行，感到无比的愤怒和痛恨。对于台湾同胞水深火热般的处境，怀着无限深切的同情！
    台湾同胞同全国人民一样，是不可侮的。最近爆发的台湾同胞的爱国反美大示威，是对美国侵略者的严厉警告，鼓舞了尚处在美帝国主义压迫下的各国人民反对美国侵略者的正义斗争。我们对台湾同胞这种英勇的爱国主义行动，表示最大的敬意！
    我们吁请在台湾的科学工作者和台湾的全体同胞团结起来，发扬爱国主义传统，把爱国反美的伟大斗争坚决进行到底。我们科学工作者和全国人民一道，坚决支援台湾同胞的正义斗争，为把美国侵略者赶出台湾、和平统一伟大祖国而共同努力！
