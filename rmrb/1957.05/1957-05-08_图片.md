On this year’s “May Day,” 500,000 working people in Tokyo held a celebration meeting at Meiji Jingu Gaien. On that day, 3.8 million working people across Japan gathered in various places.

In the Tokyo people's May Day celebration demonstration, farmers from Sunagawa-cho marched forward, holding high the battle flags inscribed with "Unity" and "Absolutely Oppose Military Bases!"

专栏：

    今年“五一”节，东京五十万劳动人民在明治神宫外苑举行庆祝大会。当天，日本全国有三百八十万劳动人民在各地集会。
    东京人民庆祝“五一”节示威大游行的队伍中，砂川町的农民高举着写有“团结”、“绝对反对军事基地”的斗争旗帜前进！
