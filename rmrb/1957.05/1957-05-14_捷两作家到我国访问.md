Title: Two Czechoslovak Writers Visit China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 5

Two Czechoslovak Writers Visit China

    (Our newspaper dispatch) According to the 1957 implementation plan of the Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the famous Czechoslovak writer Jan Drda, and the member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, representative of the National Assembly, member of the Presidium of the Writers Association, famous playwright and poet, Aleš, arrived in Beijing on May 3rd for a visit to China. During their week in Beijing, they met with Chinese writers Mao Dun, Lao She, Xiao San, Chen Baichen, Xing Ye, and others. They also spoke with Comrade Zhou Yang about the policy of "Letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend" and how to correctly handle contradictions among the people. Comrade He Changgong, who personally participated in the Long March, introduced them to his experiences during the Long March. They also visited the Si Ji Qing Agricultural Production Cooperative and the National Agricultural Exhibition; and attended a celebration evening held by workers of the Beijing Film Processing Factory to commemorate the 12th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. At a reception held by the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China on May 9th, Premier Zhou had contact and conversations with them. On May 11th, they set off for Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and the old revolutionary bases of Nanchang, Ruijin, and Jinggangshan to collect writing materials.

### 捷两作家到我国访问

1957-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    捷两作家到我国访问
    本报讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1957年执行计划来中国访问的捷克斯洛伐克著名作家扬·德尔达和捷共中央委员、国民议会代表、作家协会主席团委员、著名剧作家、诗人亚雷希5月3日抵北京。在北京的一个星期中，他们曾和我国作家茅盾、老舍、萧三、陈白尘、邢野等见过面，又和周扬同志交谈了关于“百花齐放，百家争鸣”方针和如何正确对待人民内部矛盾等问题。亲自参加了长征的何长工同志向他们介绍了他参加长征的经历。他们还参观了四季青农业生产合作社和全国农业展览会；参加了北影影片洗印厂工人们举行的纪念捷克斯洛伐克解放十二周年的庆祝晚会。5月9日在捷驻华大使举行的招待酒会上，周总理曾和他们接触交谈。5月11日启程赴汉口、广州、杭州、上海和革命老根据地南昌、瑞金、井岗山一带访问，搜集写作材料。
