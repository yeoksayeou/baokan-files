Title: Chairman Marko Visits Chairman Liu Shaoqi to Discuss Distinguishing Between Contradictions Among the People and Contradictions Between Ourselves and the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th. The Chairman of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, Rita Marko, and his wife, as well as the delegation of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania he led, visited Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife this evening.

The hosts and guests discussed how to distinguish between contradictions among the people and contradictions between ourselves and the enemy, how to correctly handle these two types of contradictions by distinguishing different situations, and how to strengthen the unity of the socialist camp.

Present at the meeting were Vice Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, as well as Zhang Bojun, Bao Erhan, Xu Guangping, Zhang Su, Hu Ziang, Ji Pengfei, Wu Kejian, and Yu Xinqing.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, and his wife were also present. (Photo attached)

### 马尔科主席拜会刘少奇委员长  交谈如何分清人民内部矛盾和敌我矛盾等问题

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    马尔科主席拜会刘少奇委员长
    交谈如何分清人民内部矛盾和敌我矛盾等问题
    新华社9日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席里塔·马尔科和夫人以及他所率领的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团，今天晚上拜会了刘少奇委员长和夫人。
    宾主谈到了如何分清人民内部矛盾和敌我矛盾，如何分别不同情况来正确地处理这两种矛盾，以及加强社会主义阵营的团结等问题。
    拜会时在座的，有林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通副委员长和章伯钧、包尔汉、许广平、张苏、胡子昂、姬鹏飞、吴克坚、余心清。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里和夫人也在座。（附图片）
