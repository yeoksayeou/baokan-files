Title: Peng Dehuai Hosts Banquet for Indonesian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 11th - Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Indonesian Military Goodwill Delegation to China.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Sukardjo Wirjopranoto and military attachés from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

This morning, Marshal Peng Dehuai received all members of the Indonesian Military Goodwill Delegation to China.

### 彭德怀欢宴印度尼西亚军事代表

1957-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀欢宴印度尼西亚军事代表团
    据新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅今晚设宴欢迎印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团。
    印度尼西亚驻我国大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和各国驻中国武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    今天上午，彭德怀元帅接见了印度尼西亚军事友好访华代表团全体团员。
