Title: Luo Shigao Appointed as China's Ambassador to Albania
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Luo Shigao has been appointed by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Albania.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has relieved Xu Yixin of his duty as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Albania.

### 罗士高任我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    罗士高任我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命罗士高为中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去徐以新的中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
