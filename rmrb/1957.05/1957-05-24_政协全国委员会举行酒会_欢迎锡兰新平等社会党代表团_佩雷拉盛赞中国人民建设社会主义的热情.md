Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Reception Welcoming Delegation of Ceylon Lanka Sama Samaja Party; Perera Praises the Chinese People's Enthusiasm for Building Socialism
Author:
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception this afternoon to welcome the delegation of the Ceylon Lanka Sama Samaja Party.

The head of the delegation, Dr. N.M. Perera, said at the reception that the loss was not China’s, but the world’s, that China had not regained its seat in the United Nations.

He said that Ceylon is willing to do its bit, together with India, to work to restore China's seat in the United Nations.

Dr. N.M. Perera praised the Chinese people's enthusiasm for building socialism, saying that a country's greatness lies in the determination of its people. He also said that the people of Ceylon have a deep affection for the people of China.

Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, made a speech at the beginning of the reception to welcome the distinguished guests. He said that the distinguished guests had brought us the friendship of the people of Ceylon, and hoped that after visiting our country, they would also be able to bring the friendship of the Chinese people to the people of Ceylon.

Li Jishen also hoped that the distinguished guests would offer more comments on the shortcomings in our work during their visits, so as to help us improve our work.

Among those present at the reception were Zhang Bojun, Vice-Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and more than 100 well-known figures from all walks of life.

The Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Perera, and his wife were also invited to the reception.

### 政协全国委员会举行酒会  欢迎锡兰新平等社会党代表团  佩雷拉盛赞中国人民建设社会主义的热情

1957-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行酒会
    欢迎锡兰新平等社会党代表团
    佩雷拉盛赞中国人民建设社会主义的热情
    新华社23日讯　政协全国委员会今天下午为欢迎锡兰新平等社会党代表团举行酒会。
    代表团团长纳·马·佩雷拉博士在酒会上讲话，他认为中国没有恢复在联合国的席位，损失的不是中国，损失的是全世界。
    他说，锡兰愿尽它的微薄力量，同印度一起为使中国恢复在联合国的席位而努力。
    纳·马·佩雷拉博士说，一个国家的伟大，就在于人民的决心。他盛赞中国人民建设社会主义的热情。他还说，锡兰人民对中国人民有很深厚的感情。
    政协全国委员会副主席李济深在酒会开始的时候致词欢迎贵宾。他说，贵宾们为我们带来了锡兰人民的友谊，希望他们在访问我国之后，也能够把中国人民的友谊带给锡兰人民。
    李济深还希望贵宾们在参观中，对我们工作中的缺点多提意见，以便帮助我们改进工作。
    出席酒会的，还有政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，以及各方面的著名人士一百多人。
    锡兰驻中国大使佩雷拉和夫人也应邀出席了酒会。
