Title: 86th Anniversary of the Suppression of the Paris Commune: French Laboring People March in Commemoration
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 28th - According to TASS, under the call of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party and the General Confederation of Labor, the working people of Paris held a mass demonstration on the 26th to commemorate the eighty-sixth anniversary of the suppression of the Paris Commune. The demonstration procession of the working people passed by the memorial wall where the heroes of the Paris Commune were murdered, lasting for several hours. The demonstrating masses demanded a peaceful solution to the Algerian question, the establishment of a left-wing government, and the defense of the achievements of democratic freedom and social welfare.

### 巴黎公社遭镇压86周年  法国劳动人民游行纪念

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    巴黎公社遭镇压86周年
    法国劳动人民游行纪念
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：在法国共产党中央委员会和总工会的号召下，巴黎劳动人民26日举行了群众性的示威游行，纪念巴黎公社遭受镇压的八十六周年。劳动人民的示威行列从巴黎公社英雄们遇害的纪念壁旁边通过，前后历时好几个钟点。示威的群众要求和平解决阿尔及利亚问题、建立左翼政府、保卫民主自由和社会福利的成果。
