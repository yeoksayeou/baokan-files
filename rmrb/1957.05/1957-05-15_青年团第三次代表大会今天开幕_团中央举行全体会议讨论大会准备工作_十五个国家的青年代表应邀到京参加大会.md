Title: Youth League's Third National Congress Opens Today; Central Committee Holds Plenary Session to Discuss Preparations; Youth Representatives from Fifteen Countries Invited to Beijing to Attend

Author:
Date: 1957-05-15
Page: 1

    Youth League's Third National Congress Opens Today
    Central Committee Holds Plenary Session to Discuss Preparations
    Youth Representatives from Fifteen Countries Invited to Beijing to Attend
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th — The Third National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League is scheduled to open tomorrow. The 1,492 delegates attending the congress (with 27 others excused and two still en route to Beijing) held a preparatory meeting from the 10th to the 14th.
    The delegates, organized into nine delegations according to their elected regions and units, conducted extensive democratic discussions in groups on the work report to be submitted to the congress by the Second Central Committee of the Youth League, the Constitution of the Chinese Communist Youth League (draft), and the report on the Constitution of the Chinese Communist Youth League (draft). They offered criticisms and suggestions on the work of the Central Committee and made some important amendments to the documents. The delegates also conducted preliminary consultations and preparations for the election of the new Central Committee.
    At the plenary session of the preparatory meeting held on the afternoon of the 14th, the presidium of the congress, consisting of 73 members as suggested by the Second Central Committee, the secretary-general and deputy secretaries-general of the congress, and the credentials committee were approved. The preparatory meeting also approved the agenda and rules of procedure for the congress, deciding that the National Congress would officially begin on the 15th.
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th — The Fifth Plenary Session of the Second Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League was held on the afternoon of May 8th and the morning of the 14th.
    The plenary session conducted detailed discussions on the various preparations for the upcoming Third National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League. The meeting discussed and approved the work report to be submitted to the Third National Congress by the Second Central Committee, the Constitution of the Chinese Communist Youth League (draft), and the report on the Constitution of the Chinese Communist Youth League (draft). The meeting also approved the draft list of the presidium to be submitted to the congress, the list of the secretary-general and deputy secretaries-general, the list of the credentials committee, and the agenda and procedural rules of the congress.
    A total of 106 central committee members and alternate central committee members attended and were present at the plenary session.
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th — Thirty-seven representatives of youth organizations from fifteen countries, invited to attend the Third National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League, have arrived in Beijing successively. In addition, two representatives from Indonesia will arrive in Beijing tomorrow.
    Those who have already arrived in Beijing are: the delegation of the Albanian Labor Youth League headed by Llubonja, First Secretary of the Albanian Labor Youth League; the delegation of the British Communist Youth League headed by Joe Mai Drahoi, Chairman of the National Committee of the British Communist Youth League; Alexander Dimitrov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Dimitrov People's Youth League; the delegation of the Czechoslovak Youth League headed by Vladimir Mesnar, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League; Robert Zilli, Member of the National Committee of the French Communist Youth League; Irene Georgette Luc, Organizing Secretary of the National Executive Bureau of the French Women's Youth League; the delegation of the Free German Youth League headed by Helmut Müller, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League; the delegation of the Hungarian Communist Youth League headed by Ebersi Laszlo, Member of the Organizing Committee of the Hungarian Communist Youth League; the delegation of the Italian Communist Youth League headed by Lauro, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Youth League; Kogura Kinkichi, Central Committee Member of the Japanese Democratic Youth League; the delegation of the Korean Democratic Youth League headed by Kim Ki-soo, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League; the delegation of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League headed by Gonggadaorji, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League; the delegation of the Romanian Labor Youth League headed by Trofin Virgil, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth League; the delegation of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League headed by V.E. Semichastny, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League; the delegation of the Vietnamese Labor Youth League headed by Nguyen Lam, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Youth League; and the delegation of the Yugoslav People's Youth League headed by Da. Milanović, Chairman of the Central Committee of the People's Youth League of Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Member of the Presidium of the Yugoslav People's Youth League.
    Tonight, the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League held a reception to welcome representatives from various countries.

### 青年团第三次代表大会今天开幕  团中央举行全体会议讨论大会准备工作  十五个国家的青年代表应邀到京参加大会

1957-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团第三次代表大会今天开幕
    团中央举行全体会议讨论大会准备工作
    十五个国家的青年代表应邀到京参加大会
    新华社14日讯　中国新民主主义青年团第三次全国代表大会定期在明天开幕。出席大会的代表一千四百九十二人（另有二十七人请假，二人尚在来京途中），从10日到14日举行了代表大会的预备会议。
    代表们按照选举的地区和单位，分别组成九个代表团，并且分组对青年团第二届中央委员会准备提交代表大会的工作报告、中国共产主义青年团章程（草案）和关于中国共产主义青年团章程（草案）的报告，进行了广泛的民主讨论，对中央委员会的工作提出了批评和建议，对各项文件提出了一些重要的修改意见。代表们还对新的中央委员会的选举工作，进行了初步酝酿和准备。
    在14日下午举行的预备会议全体大会上，通过了第二届中央委员会建议的由七十三人组成的大会主席团，大会秘书长、副秘书长以及代表资格审查委员会。预备会议还通过了代表大会的日程和会议规则，决定全国代表大会从15日正式开始。
    新华社14日讯　中国新民主主义青年团第二届中央委员会第五次全体会议，在5月8日下午和14日上午举行。
    全会对即将召开的中国新民主主义青年团第三次全国代表大会的各项准备工作，进行了详细的讨论。会议讨论和通过了第二届中央委员会提交第三次全国代表大会的工作报告、中国共产主义青年团章程（草案）和关于中国共产主义青年团章程（草案）的报告。会议还通过了准备提交代表大会的主席团名单、秘书长和副秘书长名单、代表资格审查委员会名单和大会日程及议事规定的各项草案。
    出席和列席这次全会的有中央委员和候补中央委员，共一百零六人。
    新华社14日讯　应邀参加中国新民主主义青年团第三次全国代表大会的十五个国家的青年组织的三十七个代表已经先后到达北京。另外，印度尼西亚的代表二人将在明天到达北京。
    已经到达北京的是：以阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟第一书记卢鲍尼亚为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团；以英国共产主义青年团全国委员会主席约·迈·德拉霍伊为首的英国共产主义青年团代表团；保加利亚季米特洛夫人民青年联盟中央委员会第一书记亚历山大·季米特洛夫；以捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟中央委员会书记弗拉吉米尔·梅斯纳尔为首的捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟代表团；法兰西共产主义青年联盟全国委员会委员罗伯·齐利；法兰西女青年联盟全国执行局组织书记艾琳·若尔热特·吕克；以自由德国青年联盟中央委员会书记赫耳穆特·米勒为首的自由德国青年联盟代表团；以匈牙利共产主义青年团组织委员会委员艾伯利西·拉斯洛为首的匈牙利共产主义青年团代表团；以意大利共产主义青年联盟书记处书记劳罗为首的意大利共产主义青年联盟代表团；日本民主青年同盟中央委员小仓金吉；以朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长金基洙为首的朝鲜民主青年同盟代表团；以蒙古革命青年团中央委员会书记贡嘎道尔吉为首的蒙古革命青年团代表团；以罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会第一书记特罗芬·维吉尔为首的罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团；以苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记符·叶·谢米恰内伊为首的苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团；以越南劳动青年团中央委员会第一书记阮蓝为首的越南劳动青年团代表团；以南斯拉夫波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那人民共和国人民青年联盟中央主席、南斯拉夫人民青年联盟主席团委员达·米拉诺维奇为首的南斯拉夫人民青年联盟代表团。
    今天晚间，青年团中央委员会书记处举行酒会欢迎各国代表。
