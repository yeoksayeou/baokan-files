Title: Morocco and the United States Hold Negotiations on the Status of US Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: Moroccan Foreign Minister Balafrej and U.S. Ambassador to Morocco Cavendish Cannon began negotiations in Rabat on the afternoon of May 9th on the status of U.S. air and naval bases in Morocco.

The United States possesses four large strategic air force bases and one naval base in Morocco. These bases were constructed under an agreement signed between the United States and France in 1951.

### 就美军基地地位问题  摩洛哥同美国举行谈判

1957-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

    就美军基地地位问题
    摩洛哥同美国举行谈判
    据新华社10日讯　摩洛哥外交大臣贝拉弗里杰和美国驻摩洛哥大使卡文迪希·坎农9日下午在拉巴特开始就美国在摩洛哥的海空军基地的地位问题举行谈判。
    美国在摩洛哥拥有四个巨大的战略空军基地和一个海军基地。这些基地是根据美国和法国在1951年签订的协定修建的。
