Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Indonesian National Football Team
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 27th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Long, along with Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation Dong Shouyi, and Chairman of the Chinese Football Association Huang Zhong, today received the entire Indonesian National Football Team, led by the head of the team and Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Football Association, Kosasih Poerwanegara.

Those present at the reception included Vice Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Cai Shufan, Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, Zhang Lianhua, and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Football Association, Li Fenglou, among others.

### 贺龙副总理接见印度尼西亚国家足球队

1957-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见印度尼西亚国家足球队
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理、体育运动委员会主任贺龙和中华全国体育总会副主席董守义、中国足球协会主席黄中，今天接见了以印度尼西亚国家足球队领队、印度尼西亚足球总会副主席科沙西·普瓦内加拉为首的印度尼西亚国家足球队全体人员。
    接见时在座的有体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩、中华全国体育总会秘书长张联华和中国足球协会副主席李凤楼等人。
