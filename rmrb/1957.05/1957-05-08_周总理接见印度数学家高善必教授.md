Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indian Mathematician Professor D.D. Kosambi
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th – Premier Zhou Enlai received Indian mathematician Professor D.D. Kosambi today, who is visiting China to lecture. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen, Director of the Institute of Mathematics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Hua Luogeng, and others.

### 周总理接见印度数学家高善必教授

1957-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度数学家高善必教授
    新华社7日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了前来我国访问讲学的印度数学家高善必教授。接见时在座的有国务院副总理聂荣臻，中国科学院数学研究所所长华罗庚等。
