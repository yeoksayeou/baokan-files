Title: Syngman Rhee's Troops Approach the Ceasefire Line and Hold Atomic Warfare Exercises
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 6

    Syngman Rhee's Troops Approach the Ceasefire Line
    Hold Atomic Warfare Exercises
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 20th: Seoul dispatch: According to South Korean army authorities, the Syngman Rhee army's 27th Division conducted "mobile atomic warfare exercises" near the western Korean ceasefire line on May 17th.

    A Syngman Rhee military spokesman said that the 27th Division has been undergoing experimental reorganization for the past three years to make it an "atomic age unit."

    In order to realize his atomic warfare plan, Syngman Rhee has been constantly asking the United States for atomic weapons. The US Department of Defense announced on May 14th that it would ship atomic weapons to South Korea.

### 李承晚军靠近停战线  举行原子战演习

1957-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    李承晚军靠近停战线
    举行原子战演习
    新华社平壤20日电　汉城消息：据南朝鲜陆军当局宣布，李承晚军队第二十七师5月17日曾经在朝鲜西部停战线附近举行了“机动原子战演习”。
    李承晚军方发言人说，第二十七师在过去三年中一直在进行试验性的改编，以便使它成为一支“原子时代的部队”。
    李承晚为了实现他的原子战争计划，曾经不断向美国要原子武器，美国国防部已经在5月14日宣布要把原子武器运到南朝鲜。
