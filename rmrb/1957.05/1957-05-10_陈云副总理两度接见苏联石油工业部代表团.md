Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Receives Soviet Petroleum Industry Ministry Delegation Twice
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yun, received the Soviet Petroleum Industry Ministry Delegation, headed by Deputy Minister of the Soviet Petroleum Industry Ministry, S.A. Orudzhev, on the afternoon of April 29th and the afternoon of May 8th.

    Chen Yun held cordial talks with Orudzhev and the Chief Geologist of the Soviet Petroleum Industry Ministry, M.F. Mirchink, regarding China's petroleum geological exploration work. Based on the results of their on-site inspections in various parts of China, the delegation members evaluated China's major oil and gas producing areas and exploration efforts, and offered valuable suggestions on the future deployment of petroleum geological exploration work.

    Minister of Petroleum Industry, Li Juikui, and Vice Ministers Li Fanyi, Li Renjun, and Kang Shien were present at both receptions.

    The Soviet Union's Chief Economic Advisor in China, Arkhipov, was also present.

### 陈云副总理两度接见苏联石油工业部代表团

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云副总理两度接见苏联石油工业部代表团
    新华社9日讯　国务院副总理陈云在4月29日下午和5月8日下午，先后两次接见了以苏联石油工业部副部长萨·阿·奥鲁杰夫为首的苏联石油工业部代表团。
    陈云就中国石油地质勘探工作同奥鲁杰夫和苏联石油工业部总地质师米·费·米尔钦科进行了亲切的交谈。代表团团员们根据在中国各地现场考察的结果，对中国各主要油、气产区和探取作了评价，并且对今后石油地质勘探工作的部署等问题提出了宝贵的建议。
    两次接见时，在座的有石油工业部部长李聚奎，副部长李范一、李人俊、康世恩等。
    苏联驻华经济总顾问阿尔希波夫也在座。
