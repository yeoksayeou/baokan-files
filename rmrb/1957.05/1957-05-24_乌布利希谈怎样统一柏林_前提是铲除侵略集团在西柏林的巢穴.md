Title: Ulbricht Discusses How to Unify Berlin, Premise Being the Eradication of the Aggressor Group's Nest in West Berlin
Author:
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 23rd: According to a report in "Neues Deutschland," the Greater Berlin City Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany held a meeting of Party activists on the evening of May 21st. Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, pointed out at the meeting that the precondition for the unification of Berlin is the eradication of the North Atlantic Group's base and special service organizations in West Berlin, and the dismissal of revanchist politicians and militarists from West Berlin's economic and administrative institutions.

Ulbricht also pointed out that the Socialist Unity Party, together with the Social Democratic Party, can win an absolute majority in Berlin with the support of the trade unions. He said: "We urgently appeal to the Social Democratic Party in West Berlin, asking it to set an example for the peaceful development of West Germany for the sake of the peaceful development of Berlin." He further pointed out: "The Social Democratic Party has stated that it opposes Adenauer's Christian Democratic Union's atomic warfare policy and advocates the abolition of the conscription law. If the Social Democratic Party wants to implement this policy, then it should sever its ties with the Christian Democratic Union in Berlin and do some work for the cause of peace, democracy, and progress."

Ulbricht said: "If peace-loving democratic forces can also gain an overwhelming advantage in West Berlin, then Greater Berlin will become a city of peace, a capital of an economically and politically progressive, unified Germany."

### 乌布利希谈怎样统一柏林  前提是铲除侵略集团在西柏林的巢穴

1957-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    乌布利希谈怎样统一柏林
    前提是铲除侵略集团在西柏林的巢穴
    新华社柏林23日电　据“新德意志报”报道：德国统一社会党大柏林市委员会在5月21日晚上召开了党的积极分子大会。德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希在会上指出：柏林统一的前提条件是铲除在西柏林的北大西洋集团的基地和特务机构，并且撤销西柏林经济和行政机构中的复仇主义政客和军国主义者的职务。
    乌布利希还指出，统一社会党和社会民主党一道可以在工会的支持下在柏林赢得绝对多数。他说：我们向西柏林的社会民主党发出迫切的号召，要求它为了柏林和平发展的利益，给西德的和平发展作出榜样。他又指出：社会民主党曾经声明它是反对阿登纳基督教民主联盟的原子战争政策和主张取消征兵法的。如果社会民主党要执行这种政策，那么它就应该在柏林割断它同基督教民主联盟的联系而为和平民主和进步的事业做些工作。
    乌布利希说：如果爱好和平的民主力量也能在西柏林获得压倒的优势的话，那么大柏林就会成为和平的城市，成为经济和政治上进步的、统一的德国的首都。
