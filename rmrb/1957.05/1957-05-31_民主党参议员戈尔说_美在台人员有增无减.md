Title: Democratic Senator Gore Says US Personnel in Taiwan Have Increased, Not Decreased
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 30th – Democratic Senator Gore refuted the State Department's claim that the United States has been considering reducing its personnel stationed in Taiwan on the 29th. Gore pointed out that the total number of American personnel stationed in Taiwan has been increasing.

Gore visited Taiwan last year. He said that the State Department recently told him that since his visit to Taiwan, the American institutions stationed in Taiwan have added "several hundred" more Americans.

### 民主党参议员戈尔说  美在台人员有增无减

1957-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    民主党参议员戈尔说
    美在台人员有增无减
    新华社30日讯　华盛顿消息：美国民主党参议员戈尔29日驳斥了国务院关于美国一直在考虑削减驻台湾人员的说法。戈尔指出，美国驻台湾人员的总数一直在增长。
    戈尔去年曾访问过台湾。他说，最近国务院告诉他说，自从他访问台湾之后，美国驻台湾的机构已经又增加了“几百个”美国人。
