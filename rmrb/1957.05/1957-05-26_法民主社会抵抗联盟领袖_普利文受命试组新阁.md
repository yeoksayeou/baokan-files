Title: Leader of the French Democratic Socialist Resistance Union, Pleven, Commissioned to Attempt to Form a New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Paris Dispatch: Former French Prime Minister and leader of the Democratic Socialist Resistance Union, René Pleven, announced on the 24th after meeting with French President Coty that he had accepted a "tentative mission" to pave the way for the organization of a new government.

It is reported that Pleven's mission is to try to formulate a "minimum program" that excludes the Communist Party and the Progressive Republican Union, but attracts so-called left, right, and center parties.

### 法民主社会抵抗联盟领袖  普利文受命试组新阁

1957-05-26
第6版()
专栏：

    法民主社会抵抗联盟领袖
    普利文受命试组新阁
    据新华社25日讯　巴黎消息：法国前总理、民主社会抵抗联盟领袖雷纳·普利文24日在同法国总统戈蒂会谈以后宣布，他已经接受了一个“试探性的任务”，来为组织新政府铺平道路。
    据报道，普利文的使命是设法拟订一项排斥共产党和进步共和联盟，但是是拉拢所谓左、右和中间派各政党的“最低纲领”。
