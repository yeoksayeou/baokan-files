Title: Rallies in Stockholm and Other Places Celebrate the Fortieth Anniversary of the Swedish Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1957-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm: According to TASS, a rally was held in Stockholm on May 15 to celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the Swedish Communist Party. Party Secretary Knut Tell delivered a speech at the meeting, explaining the struggle of the Swedish Communists together with people from all walks of life to improve the living conditions of the working people, to strive for easing international tensions, to oppose the threat of atomic war, and to ban atomic weapons. Celebrations have also been held recently in various parts of Sweden. The Swedish Communist Party has also absorbed a number of new members.
The Swedish Communist Party's central organ, "New Daily," published an editorial in its commemorative special issue on the 17th, stating that the Swedish Communist Party will continue to fight for the cause of the Swedish working people and for the cause of communism. The commemorative special issue also published congratulatory letters from nine fraternal parties, including the Soviet Communist Party, the Chinese Communist Party, the French Communist Party, and the Italian Communist Party.

### 斯德哥尔摩等地集会  庆祝瑞典共产党四十周年

1957-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    斯德哥尔摩等地集会
    庆祝瑞典共产党四十周年
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：斯德哥尔摩5月15日举行集会庆祝瑞典共产党四十周年纪念日。党的书记克努特·特尔在会上发表演说，说明了瑞典共产党人同各界人民一起为改善劳动人民生活状况、为争取缓和国际紧张局势、反对原子战争的威胁、禁止原子武器而进行的斗争。瑞典各地最近以来也纷纷举行庆祝活动。瑞典共产党也吸收了一批新党员。
    瑞典共产党中央机关报“新日报”在17日的纪念专刊上发表社论说，瑞典共产党今后仍将继续为瑞典劳动人民的事业，为共产主义的事业奋斗。这期纪念专刊还刊登了苏共、中共、法共、意共等九个兄弟党给瑞典共产党的贺信。
