Title: Manila Group Conducts Exercises in Thailand; Bangkok Citizens Rally in Protest
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, May 30th, Bangkok Dispatch: Bangkok citizens held a massive mass rally on May 29th to protest the Manila Treaty Organization holding military exercises in Thailand.
    At this rally, organized by various parties of the Thai Socialist United Front, speakers condemned the American colonialists' attempts to use the conspiracies of the Manila Treaty Organization, and also accused the Manila Treaty Organization's military exercises of being a challenge.
    The slogans at the meeting expressed the demands of the Thai people: "We want peace," "We want a neutral policy," "We oppose military treaties."
    The Manila Treaty Organization began an exercise in Thailand on May 29th called "Air Link". The United States, Britain, New Zealand, and Thailand will participate in this exercise, which will be held in Saraburi Province and Lopburi Province north of Bangkok.

### 马尼拉集团在泰国演习  曼谷市民集会抗议

1957-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    马尼拉集团在泰国演习
    曼谷市民集会抗议
    新华社30日讯  曼谷消息：曼谷市民5月29日举行了一个巨大的群众集会，抗议马尼拉条约组织在泰国举行军事演习。
    在这个由泰国社会主义联合阵线各党组织的集会上，发言者谴责美国殖民主义者企图利用马尼拉条约组织的阴谋，并且指责马尼拉条约组织的军事演习是一种挑战行动。
    会场上的标语写出了泰国人民的要求：“我们要和平”，“我们要中立政策”，“我们反对军事条约”。
    马尼拉条约组织5月29日开始在泰国举行一次称为“空中联系”的演习。美国、英国、新西兰和泰国将参加这次在曼谷以北的北标府和华富里府举行的演习。
