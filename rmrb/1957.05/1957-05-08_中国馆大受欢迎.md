Title: Chinese Pavilion Very Popular
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from New York: The "New York Times" reported from Casablanca that at the Casablanca International Exposition, which opened on May 4th with twenty-five countries participating, the Chinese pavilion, like the American pavilion, "attracted the most visitors and received the highest praise."

The newspaper described the Chinese pavilion as follows: "Three huge red纱lanterns hang at the entrance of the People's Republic of China exhibition hall. More than two thousand five hundred carefully designed different exhibits attract thousands of Moroccans to the Chinese pavilion. ... This is the first time that Communist China has had an exhibition hall at the Moroccan Exposition."

### 中国馆大受欢迎

1957-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    中国馆大受欢迎
    新华社7日讯　纽约消息：“纽约时报”从卡萨布兰卡发出的消息说，在有二十五个国家参加的卡萨布兰卡国际博览会5月4日开幕的时候，博览会的中国馆和美国馆一样，“吸引了最多的观众，并且获得了最高的赞扬”。
    这家报纸介绍中国馆的情况说：“三个巨大的红纱灯挂在中华人民共和国展览馆的进口处。包括二千五百多种精心设计的不同的展览品把成千的摩洛哥人吸引到中国馆去。……共产党中国在摩洛哥博览会上有一个展览馆还是第一次。”
