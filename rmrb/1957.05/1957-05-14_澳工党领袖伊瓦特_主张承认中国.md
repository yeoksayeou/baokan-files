Title: Australian Labor Leader Evatt Advocates for Recognition of China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, 13th: Australian Labor Party leader Dr. Evatt stated at a Labor Party rally in Melbourne on the 12th that Australia should recognize China. He said, "We cannot exclude six hundred million people without consulting them on world affairs."

The Australian opposition leader also suggested that the Soviet Union, the United States, and the United Kingdom reach a tripartite agreement to ban nuclear tests.

### 澳工党领袖伊瓦特  主张承认中国

1957-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    澳工党领袖伊瓦特
    主张承认中国
    据新华社13日讯　墨尔本消息：澳大利亚工党领袖伊瓦特12日在墨尔本举行的工党集会上说，澳大利亚应当承认中国。他说，“我们不能排斥六亿人而不同他们磋商世界事务”。
    这位澳大利亚反对党领袖并且建议苏联、美国和英国达成三边协议，禁止原子核试验。
