Title: Our Friends Are All Over the World: Foreign Guests from 46 Countries Attend China's "May Day" Celebrations

Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th – More than a thousand foreign guests from 46 countries in Asia, Africa, Latin America, Australia, and Europe have arrived in Beijing successively, invited to attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

Among the guests attending the celebrations are Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and his entourage, as well as people from various fields including government, parliament, military, trade unions, peace and friendship organizations, women, youth, literature and art, science, education, sports, health, agriculture, water conservancy, social welfare, law, trade, news, and religion. They come from Albania, Algeria, Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Britain, Bulgaria, Burma, Cambodia, Ceylon, Chile, Czechoslovakia, Egypt, Finland, France, the German Democratic Republic, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Iran, Israel, Italy, Japan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lebanon, Mongolia, Morocco, Nepal, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Pakistan, Poland, Romania, Singapore, Spain, Sudan, Sweden, Syria, Thailand, Tunisia, the Soviet Union, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Yugoslavia.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Most of the trade union representatives from various countries invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations have arrived in Beijing. Including the more than fifty trade union representatives from India, Australia, Italy, and other countries who arrived in Beijing today, there are already more than two hundred representatives from thirty-four countries in Asia, Africa, Latin America, Australia, and Europe who have arrived in Beijing, representing more than eighty trade union organizations. In addition, some trade union representatives from Black Africa, Britain, and other countries are on their way to Beijing.

The number of countries and trade union organizations represented by foreign trade unionists attending the "May Day" celebrations in Beijing this year exceeds that of previous years. Among them are many representatives from countries and trade union organizations that are sending representatives to visit China for the first time. This fact reflects the expansion and strengthening of friendly ties between Chinese workers and workers of all countries, especially those in Asia and Africa. Thailand, a close neighbor of China, has sent the "Sixteen Trade Union Organizations" delegation and the Thai Trade Union Delegation for the first time. Nepal and Singapore have also sent trade union representatives to visit China for the first time. From the Middle East and Africa, ten trade union organizations with nineteen representatives from Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, Sudan, Iran, Morocco, and Algeria have come to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations. Greece, an ancient civilization of Europe, has sent a trade union delegation for the first time, and some countries in Latin America and Australia have also sent trade union delegations to participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

### 我们的朋友遍天下  四十六国外宾参加我国“五一”节观礼

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    我们的朋友遍天下
    四十六国外宾参加我国“五一”节观礼
    新华社30日讯  来自亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、澳洲和欧洲的四十六个国家的一千多位外宾已先后到达北京，应邀参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼。
    参加观礼的来宾有苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和他的随行人员，以及各国政府、议会、军事、工会、和平友好、妇女、青年、文艺、科学、教育、体育、卫生、农业、水利、社会福利、法律、贸易、新闻、宗教等各方面人士。他们来自阿尔巴尼亚、阿尔及利亚、澳大利亚、奥地利、比利时、巴西、英国、保加利亚、缅甸、柬埔寨、锡兰、智利、捷克斯洛伐克、埃及、芬兰、法国、德意志民主共和国、德意志联邦共和国、希腊、匈牙利、印度、印度尼西亚、伊朗、以色列、意大利、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、黎巴嫩、蒙古、摩洛哥、尼泊尔、荷兰、新西兰、巴基斯坦、波兰、罗马尼亚、新加坡、西班牙、苏丹、瑞典、叙利亚、泰国、突尼斯、苏联、越南民主共和国和南斯拉夫。
    新华社30日讯  应邀来我国参加“五一”观礼的各国工会代表已大部分到达北京。连同今天到达北京的印度、澳大利亚和意大利及其他国家的五十多位工会代表在内，已经到达北京的有来自亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、澳洲和欧洲的三十四个国家的两百多位代表，他们代表了八十多个工会组织。此外，还有黑非洲、英国及其他国家的一些工会代表正在来北京途中。
    今年来北京参加“五一”观礼的外国工会人士所代表的国家和工会组织都超过了往年，其中有许多代表是来自一些首次派遣代表来中国访问的国家和工会组织。这个事实反映了中国工人同各国工人，特别是亚非各国工人的友好联系的扩大和加强。中国近邻泰国首次派来了“十六工会组织”代表团以及泰国工会代表团，尼泊尔、新加坡也首次派遣工会代表来中国访问。从中东和非洲的埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、苏丹、伊朗、摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚，有十个工会组织的十九位代表来北京参加“五一”观礼。欧洲的文明古国——希腊首次派来了一个工会代表团，拉丁美洲的一些国家和澳大利亚也有工会代表团来参加“五一”观礼。
