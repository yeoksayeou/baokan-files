Title: Polish Party's Ninth Plenary Session Concludes; Resolution on "The Party's Current Tasks" Adopted
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 19th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Ninth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party concluded here on the 18th. The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution on "The Party's Current Tasks."
    According to the meeting's communique, a total of forty-two Central Committee members spoke during the discussion of Gomulka's report. Gomulka made a concluding speech.
    After discussing the report presented by Roman Nowak, Chairman of the Central Committee's Control Commission, the meeting adopted a resolution on the Party's responsibility for the deviations of the former Ministry of Public Security's institutions. Roman Nowak presented the report on behalf of a committee established at the Eighth Plenary Session.
    Jerzy Morawski, a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the United Workers' Party, reported on the preparations for the Third Congress of the United Workers' Party at the meeting. The plenary session adopted a resolution to convene the Party's Third Congress in December 1957, and decided to establish two committees to draft the draft resolution of the Congress and the draft revision of the Party Constitution, respectively. These two drafts should be submitted to the Central Committee no later than September of this year, and will be submitted to the entire Party for discussion after being approved by the Central Committee.
    The Central Plenary Session decided to relieve Politburo member Ochab of his post as Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat due to his appointment as Minister of Agriculture. The plenary session elected Jerzy Morawski and Zenon Kliszko as Secretaries of the Central Committee Secretariat.
    According to the meeting's communique, the resolutions of this plenary session will be published in the coming days.

### 波兰党九中全会闭幕  通过了关于“党的目前任务”的决议

1957-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰党九中全会闭幕
    通过了关于“党的目前任务”的决议
    新华社华沙19日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰统一工人党中央委员会第九次全体会议18日在这里闭幕。会议一致通过了关于“党的目前任务”的决议。
    据会议公报指出，在讨论哥穆尔卡的报告时，发言的中央委员共有四十二人。哥穆尔卡作了总结发言。
    会议在讨论了党中央监察委员会主席罗曼·诺瓦克提出的报告以后，通过了关于党对前公安部机构的偏差应负的责任的决议。罗曼·诺瓦克是代表在八中全会上成立的一个委员会提出报告的。
    党中央政治局委员耶日·莫拉夫斯基在会上报告了统一工人党第三次代表大会的筹备工作。全会通过了一项决议，决定在1957年12月召开党的第三次代表大会，并且决定成立两个委员会，分别负责起草代表大会的决议草案和关于党章的修改草案。这两个草案至迟要在今年9月提交中央委员会，并且将在中央委员会通过后提交全党讨论。
    这次中央全会决定，由于政治局委员奥哈布担任了政府农业部长的职务，解除他的中央委员会书记处书记的职务。全会选举耶日·莫拉夫斯基和泽农·克利什科担任中央委员会书记处书记。
    据会议公报宣布，这次全会的各项决议将在日内公布。
