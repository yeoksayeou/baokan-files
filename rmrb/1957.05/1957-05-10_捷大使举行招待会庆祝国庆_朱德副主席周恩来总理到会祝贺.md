Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador Holds Reception to Celebrate National Day; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai Attend to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th May — Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Gregor, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Czechoslovak Republic.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception.

    At the beginning of the reception, the Ambassador delivered a speech, proposing a toast to the further powerful development of the People's Republic of China, to the greater achievements of the Chinese people in building socialism, and to the further enhancement of the friendship and fraternal cooperation between the people of Czechoslovakia and China.

    The Ambassador said that the recent visit to China by the Czechoslovak Republic government delegation headed by Prime Minister Široký laid a solid and far-reaching foundation for the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of Czechoslovakia and China. He wished the eternal friendship and cooperation between the people of Czechoslovakia and China would last forever and be increasingly strengthened.

    He proposed a toast to the great son of the Chinese people, the organizer of the great victory of the Chinese people, the wise teacher and leader of the Chinese people - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China.

    The Ambassador also proposed a toast to the health of Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai, to the friendly coexistence of all peoples, and to all the guests attending the reception.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai successively raised their glasses in a toast. Vice Chairman Zhu asked everyone to raise their glasses and wish the Czechoslovak Republic prosperity and strength and the people happiness, and to wish Comrade President Zápotocký of the Czechoslovak Republic good health. Premier Zhou proposed a toast to the great Czechoslovak people, to the friendship between the Chinese and Czechoslovak people, to the unity of the socialist camp's big family, to world peace, to the health of Prime Minister Široký, and to the health of the host, Ambassador Gregor and his wife.

    The reception was very warm from beginning to end. Vice Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou shook hands and talked with the heads of diplomatic missions in China and Czechoslovak friends.

    At the reception, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, and others, also drank and talked with friends from various countries.

    More than 500 people attended the reception.

### 捷大使举行招待会庆祝国庆  朱德副主席周恩来总理到会祝贺

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    捷大使举行招待会庆祝国庆
    朱德副主席周恩来总理到会祝贺
    新华社9日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使格里哥尔今天下午举行招待会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国国庆。
    朱德副主席和周恩来总理出席了招待会。
    大使在招待会开始的时候致词，他提议为中华人民共和国进一步强大的发展，为中国人民在建设社会主义事业中取得更多的成就，为捷中两国人民间的友谊和兄弟般的合作更加增强而干杯。
    大使说，不久前，以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团的访问中国，为捷、中两国人民的友谊和合作奠定了巩固的和深远的基础。他祝捷中两国人民永恒的友谊和合作长存并日益巩固。
    他提议为中国人民伟大的儿子，中国人民的伟大胜利的组织者，中国人民的英明导师和领袖——中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志而干杯。
    大使还提议为朱德副主席、周恩来总理的健康，为各国人民间的友好相处，为出席招待会的全体来宾干杯。
    朱德副主席和周恩来总理相继举杯祝酒。朱副主席请大家举杯，祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国的繁荣富强和人民的幸福，祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基同志的健康。周总理提议为伟大的捷克斯洛伐克人民，为中捷两国人民的友谊，为社会主义阵营大家庭的团结，为世界和平，为西罗基总理的健康，为招待会的主人格里哥尔大使和夫人的健康干杯。
    招待会自始至终都很热烈。朱副主席和周总理同各国驻华使节及捷克斯洛伐克友人一一握手谈话。
    在招待会上，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通，副总理李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武等，都同各国朋友在一起饮酒交谈。
    出席招待会的共有五百多人。
