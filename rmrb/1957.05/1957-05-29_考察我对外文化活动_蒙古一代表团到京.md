Title: Inspecting Our Foreign Cultural Activities: Mongolian Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. A delegation from Mongolia, consisting of Erdeni, Secretary General of the Mongolian People's Republic Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau, and Nebu, Secretary General of the Mongolia-Soviet Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the People's Republic of China to investigate the working methods and experiences of China's foreign cultural activities.

### 考察我对外文化活动  蒙古一代表团到京

1957-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    考察我对外文化活动
    蒙古一代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　由蒙古人民共和国对外文化联络局秘书长额尔德尼和蒙苏友好协会秘书长涅布组成的代表团在27日下午乘飞机抵京。
    代表团是应中华人民共和国对外文化联络局邀请，来中国考察中国对外文化活动方面的工作方法和经验的。
