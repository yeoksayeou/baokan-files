Title: The Fourth Session of the National People's Congress Scheduled to Convene on June 3rd
Author:
Date: 1957-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The 69th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held this morning, decided to convene the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress on June 3rd.

    The meeting adopted the resolution on convening this session of the National People's Congress.

    After the meeting, a discussion was held on the daily work of the Standing Committee.

The Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Convening the Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress

       (Adopted at the 69th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on May 18, 1957)

    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress resolved at its 69th meeting on May 18, 1957: The Fourth Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China will be convened in Beijing on June 3, 1957. The main proposed agenda items are to hear the work report of the State Council, decide on the 1957 national economic plan, and review and approve the 1956 state final accounts and the 1957 state budget. National People's Congress deputies should register before May 30th.

### 全国人民代表大会第四次会议定六月三日召开

1957-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第四次会议定六月三日召开
    新华社18日讯　今天上午举行的全国人民代表大会常务委员会第六十九次会议，决定在6月3日召开第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议。
    会议通过了关于召开这次全国人民代表大会会议的决议。
    会后就常务委员会的日常工作进行了座谈。全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于召集第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议的决议
            （1957年5月18日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第六十九次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1957年5月18日第六十九次会议决议：定于1957年6月3日在北京召集中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第四次会议，拟定主要的议案为听取国务院工作报告，决定1957年度国民经济计划，审查和批准1956年国家的决算和1957年国家的预算。全国人民代表大会代表应于5月30日以前报到。
