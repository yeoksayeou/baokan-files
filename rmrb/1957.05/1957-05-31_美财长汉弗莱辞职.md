Title: US Treasury Secretary Humphrey Resigns
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: US Treasury Secretary Humphrey resigned on the 29th. US President Eisenhower has appointed former Navy Secretary Robert Anderson to his position.

According to the White House, the US government will also remove the current Deputy Treasury Secretary Randolph Burgess from his post and reassign him.

American newspapers have constantly predicted that Humphrey would leave the government. Sources say that Humphrey's resignation is related to his opposition to Eisenhower's budget policy of implementing huge expenditures.

### 美财长汉弗莱辞职

1957-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    美财长汉弗莱辞职
    据新华社30日讯　美国财政部长汉弗莱29日辞职。美国总统艾森豪威尔已经任命前海军部长罗伯特·安德逊担任他的职位。
    据白宫透露，美国政府还将免去现在的财政部副部长伦道夫·伯吉斯的职务，另有任用。
    美国报刊曾经不断预料汉弗莱将要离开政府。有消息说，汉弗莱的辞职，同他反对艾森豪威尔实行庞大开支的预算政策一事有关。
