Title: President Dong Biwu Receives Yugoslav Legal Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court of the People's Republic of China, received today the Yugoslav legal workers delegation led by Leon Geršković, Secretary-General of the Secretariat for Legislation and Organization of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia. Present at the reception were Zhang Zhiran and Ma Xiwu, Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court, Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and Shi Liang, Minister of Justice.

The Yugoslav legal workers delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society.

### 董必武院长接见南斯拉夫法律工作者代表团

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武院长接见南斯拉夫法律工作者代表团
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国最高人民法院院长董必武今天接见了南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会立法和组织秘书处秘书长莱昂·格尔什科维奇率领的南斯拉夫法律工作者代表团。接见时在座的有最高人民法院副院长张志让、马锡五，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞和司法部部长史良等。
    南斯拉夫法律工作者代表团是应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的。
