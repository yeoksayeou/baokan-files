Title: Prasad Reelected as President of India
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 10th: It was announced today that Dr. Rajendra Prasad has been re-elected as the President of the Republic of India.

The election took place on May 6th in New Delhi and the state capitals. Participating in the election were members elected by both houses of Parliament and members elected by the state legislative assemblies. Prasad was elected with an absolute majority of votes, while the other two candidates, Narayan Das and Hari Ram, received very few votes.

Prasad was born on December 3, 1884. He has served as Chairman and General Secretary of the Congress Party, and Speaker of the Indian Constituent Assembly. He was first elected as President of India in January 1950 and was re-elected in 1952. After this election, he will serve another five-year term as President.

### 普拉沙德蝉联印总统

1957-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    普拉沙德蝉联印总统
    新华社新德里10日电　据今天宣布，拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士已经再次当选为印度共和国总统。
    这次选举于5月6日在新德里和各邦首府举行。参加选举的是国会两院选出的议员和各邦立法会议选出的议员。普拉沙德以获得绝大多数选票而当选，其他两个候选人纳拉扬·达斯、哈里·拉姆得到的票数很少。
    普拉沙德生于1884年12月3日，曾历任国大党主席、总书记和印度制宪议会议长等职务。他在1950年1月首次当选为印度总统，在1952年连选连任。在这次当选后，他将再连任总统五年。
