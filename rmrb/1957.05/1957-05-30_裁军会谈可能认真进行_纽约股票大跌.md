Title: Disarmament Talks May Be Taken Seriously; New York Stock Market Plummets

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: New York dispatch: The New York stock market experienced its most severe decline in three and a half months yesterday, with aircraft company stocks suffering the worst losses.

Stocks in many important industries fell by one to four dollars per share.

Reports say that the stock market crash was due to the possibility that disarmament talks might be taken seriously. Oil stocks, which had been rising steadily, also plummeted amidst the overall market decline.

### 裁军会谈可能认真进行  纽约股票大跌

1957-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    裁军会谈可能认真进行
    纽约股票大跌
    新华社28日讯　纽约消息：纽约股票市场昨天发生了三个半月来最厉害的下跌，飞机企业股票跌得最惨。
    许多重要行业的股票每股下跌一美元到四美元。
    消息说，这次股票大跌是由于裁军会谈可能会认真进行。一直在上涨的石油股票在市场全面下跌的时候也跌得一蹋胡涂。
