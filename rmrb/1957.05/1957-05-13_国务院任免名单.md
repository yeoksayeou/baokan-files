Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The State Council appointed and dismissed a number of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and universities on April 29th. The list is as follows:

    Appointed Wan Meizi, Bao Huiseng, Wu Zaoxi, Chen Jun, Zhou Yizhi, Guo Bingyi, Yang Yuqing, Chen Xiaoling, Luo Jiezi (listed in order of the number of strokes in their surnames) as State Council Counselors;
    Yang Keming as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic;
    Mo Yanzhong as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam;
    Gan Chunlei, Fei Xiaotong, and Xie Hechou as Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission;
    Zhang Guofan as President of Tianjin University;
    Yang Shixian as President of Nankai University;
    Li Lengzhai as Vice President of Beian Agricultural College;
    Tu Feng as Vice President of Shanghai Foreign Language Institute;
    Zheng Zhen as Vice President of East China Institute of Water Conservancy;
    Yang Dezhai as Vice President of Shandong Agricultural College;
    Feng Huanwen as President of Subei Agricultural College, Guo Shouchun, Cheng Kejian, and Xia Yongsheng as Vice Presidents.
    Removed Zhang Xiaozeng from the position of Deputy Director of the Bureau of Affairs Administration of the State Council;
    Kang Runmin from the position of Deputy Director of the State Council Bureau of Overseas Workers;
    An Ruoding and Zhang Zhihe from the position of State Council Counselor;
    Peng Hua from the position of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Swiss Confederation;
    Wei Guoqing from the position of Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission;
    Yang Shixian from the position of Vice President of Nankai University;
    Wu De from the position of President of Tianjin University, Zhang Guofan from the position of Vice President.

### 国务院任免名单

1957-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社11日讯　国务院在4月29日任免一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员。名单如下：
    任命万枚子、包惠僧、吴藻溪、陈钧、周一志、郭秉毅、杨玉清、谌小岭、骆介子（以上按姓名笔划为序）为国务院参事；
    杨克明为中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆参赞；
    莫燕忠为中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆参赞；
    甘春雷、费孝通、谢鹤筹为民族事务委员会副主任；
    张国藩为天津大学校长；
    杨石先为南开大学校长；
    李冷斋为北安农学院副院长；
    涂峰为上海外国语学院副院长；
    郑镇为华东水利学院副院长；
    杨德斋为山东农学院副院长；
    冯焕文为苏北农学院院长，郭守纯、成克坚、夏永生为副院长。
    免去张效曾的国务院机关事务管理局副局长职务；
    康润民的国务院出国工人管理局副局长职务；
    安若定、张志和的国务院参事职务；
    彭华的中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦大使馆参赞职务；
    韦国清的民族事务委员会副主任职务；
    杨石先的南开大学副校长职务；
    吴德的天津大学校长职务，张国藩的副校长职务。
