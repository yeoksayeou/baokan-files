Title: Chairman Mao Approves the Sino-Czechoslovak Treaty and Agreements
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 68th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved on May 12, 1957, the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic, the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, and the Health Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Government, all signed in Beijing on March 27, 1957.

### 毛主席批准中捷条约和协定

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席批准中捷条约和协定
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第六十八次会议的决定，在1957年5月12日批准了1957年3月27日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国友好合作条约、中华人民共和国政府和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府文化合作协定、中华人民共和国政府和捷克斯洛伐克政府保健合作协定。
