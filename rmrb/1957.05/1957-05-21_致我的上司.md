Title: To My Superior

Date: 1957-05-21

To My Superior
Wang Ping

My esteemed superior, please allow me to remind you: have you noticed that there are only three types of people surrounding you:

	One is the ball type,
	One is the sheep type,
	One is the soft noodle type.

### 致我的上司
王平
1957-05-21
第8版()
专栏：

    致我的上司
    王平我那威严的上司，请让我提醒你：你可注意到只有三种人，围在你身边：
  一种是皮球式的，
  一种是绵羊式的，
  一种是软面式的。
