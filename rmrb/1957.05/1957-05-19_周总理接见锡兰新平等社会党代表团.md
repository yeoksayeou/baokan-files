Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from the Ceylon Lanka Sama Samaja Party
Author:
Date: 1957-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th - Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council today received the delegation from the Ceylon Lanka Sama Samaja Party, headed by N.M. Perera, and had lunch with the guests.

Among those present at the lunch were Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, and Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon Zhang Canming.

Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Perera, and his wife were also invited to attend.

N.M. Perera presented a gift to Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 周总理接见锡兰新平等社会党代表团

1957-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰新平等社会党代表团
    新华社18日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天接见了以纳·马·佩雷拉为首的锡兰新平等社会党代表团，并且同客人共进午餐。
    陪同进餐的，有国务院副总理贺龙，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华，中国驻锡兰大使张灿明。
    锡兰驻中国大使佩雷拉和夫人也应邀出席。
    纳·马·佩雷拉向周恩来总理赠送了礼品。
