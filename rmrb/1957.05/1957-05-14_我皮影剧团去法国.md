Title: My Shadow Puppet Troupe Goes to France
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chinese Shadow Puppet Troupe, consisting of twenty-one members from the nationally renowned Fujian Glove Puppet and Hunan Shadow Puppet groups, led by troupe leader Chen Hong, departed in two groups by plane for Paris, France on the 5th and 9th to conduct a performance tour.

### 我皮影剧团去法国

1957-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我皮影剧团去法国
    据新华社讯　由全国著名的福建布袋木偶及湖南皮影所组成的中国皮影木偶剧团一行二十一人，在团长陈虹率领下，已在5日、9日分两批乘飞机赴法国巴黎作访问演出。
