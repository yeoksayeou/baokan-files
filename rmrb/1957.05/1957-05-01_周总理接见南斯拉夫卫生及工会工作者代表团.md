Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Yugoslavian Health and Trade Union Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Yugoslavian Health and Trade Union Workers Delegation, led by Marko Marković, President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia and Chairman of the Joint Social Welfare Committee, this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister Cui Yitian, and others. The Ambassador of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to China, Popović, was also present.

### 周总理接见南斯拉夫卫生及工会工作者代表团

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见南斯拉夫卫生及工会工作者代表团
    新华社30日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了以南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会兼联合社会福利委员会主席马尔科维奇为首的南斯拉夫卫生及工会工作者代表团。
    接见时在座的有中华人民共和国卫生部部长李德全，副部长崔义田等人。南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻华大使波波维奇也在座。
