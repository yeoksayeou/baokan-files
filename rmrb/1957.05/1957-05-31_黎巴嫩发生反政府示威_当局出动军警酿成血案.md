Title: Anti-Government Demonstrations Occur in Lebanon; Authorities Dispatch Military and Police, Resulting in Bloodshed
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 30th: Five thousand people in Beirut, the capital of Lebanon, held a demonstration this morning, defying the government's ban. At the same time, some areas of Beirut held strikes. The demonstration and strikes were organized by the "National Front," which is composed of opposition parties. The "National Front" had demanded that the Solh government resign within forty-eight hours on May 27.

    The government dispatched a large number of military police, tanks, and armored vehicles to suppress the demonstrators. The police opened fire and threw tear gas. The demonstrators fought back with stones. Former Prime Minister Saeb Salam, who was leading the march, was injured in the face by a soldier with a rifle butt, and former Speaker of Parliament, Ahmad al-As'ad, was also injured in the abdomen. According to Reuters, seven people were killed and thirty-five were injured.

### 黎巴嫩发生反政府示威  当局出动军警酿成血案

1957-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩发生反政府示威
    当局出动军警酿成血案
    据新华社30日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特五千人今天上午不顾政府的禁令举行了示威游行。同时贝鲁特的部分地区举行了罢工罢市。这次示威和罢工罢市是反对党所组成的“民族阵线”组织的。“民族阵线”曾经在5月27日要求索勒哈政府在四十八小时内辞职。
    政府出动了大批军警和坦克、装甲车镇压示威的人，警察曾经开枪并投掷催泪弹。示威的人用石块反击军警。率领游行队伍的前总理赛布·萨拉姆的脸部被一个士兵用枪托打伤，前议会议长希马迪的腹部也被打伤。据路透社报道，有七人被打死，三十五人受伤。
