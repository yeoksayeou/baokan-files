Title: Chairman Mao Receives Maghreb Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the entire Maghreb (Morocco) National Consultative Assembly Delegation to China, led by Haji Muhammad Buamrani, this evening.

Present at the meeting were Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, Qiao Guanhua, Wu Maosun, and Ke Hua.

During the meeting, the delegation presented gifts to Chairman Mao.

### 毛主席接见马格里勃议会代表团

1957-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见马格里勃议会代表团
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了以哈吉·穆罕默德·布阿姆拉尼为首的马格里勃（摩洛哥）国民协商议会访华代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有陈叔通、张奚若、乔冠华、吴茂荪、柯华。
    接见时，代表团向毛主席送了礼品。
