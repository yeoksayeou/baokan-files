Title: Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Participate in the Fifth Anniversary Celebration of the Chinese-Burmese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1957-05-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by Dr. Thala, Councilor of the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association and Professor at Yangon University, and Deputy Head Wu Jiao Yin, Professor at Yangon University, arrived in Beijing today.

    Greeting them at the airport were Qian Weichang, Vice President of the Chinese-Burmese Friendship Association, Councilor Jian Bozan, and female writer Zi Gang, among others.

    The delegation is visiting Beijing at the invitation of the Chinese-Burmese Friendship Association to participate in the Fifth Anniversary Celebration of the Chinese-Burmese Friendship Association.

### 参加中缅友协成立五周年庆祝活动  缅中友协代表团到京

1957-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中缅友协成立五周年庆祝活动
    缅中友协代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　缅中友协代表团今天在团长缅中友好协会理事、仰光大学教授达拉博士和副团长、仰光大学教授吴叫因率领下到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长，理事翦伯赞和女作家子冈等人。
    代表团是应中缅友好协会邀请，来北京参加中缅友好协会成立五周年庆祝活动的。
