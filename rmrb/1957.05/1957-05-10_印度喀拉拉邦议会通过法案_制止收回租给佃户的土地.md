Title: Kerala State Assembly in India Passes Bill to Stop Landlords from Reclaiming Land Leased to Tenants
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 9th - The Kerala State Assembly in India yesterday passed a bill to prevent anyone from reclaiming land leased to tenants.

This bill replaces an order with the same content issued one week after the formation of the Kerala State Communist Cabinet. Its purpose is to prevent tenants who actually occupy the land from being disturbed before the parliament has time to study the entire land issue.

The land problem in Kerala is acute. Due to the high population density, there is great pressure on land. Landlords dominate the central regions of the state, such as Travancore, Cochin, and Malabar.

During the parliament's discussion of the bill to prevent the recovery of tenant land, three opposition parties withdrew from the meeting, saying that the Communist cabinet was hastily passing the bill. Communist parliamentarians pointed out that the state cabinet's original order would expire within six weeks, so the bill could not be delayed. They said, "Even a day's delay in passing the bill would result in some tenanted land being reclaimed."

### 印度喀拉拉邦议会通过法案  制止收回租给佃户的土地

1957-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    印度喀拉拉邦议会通过法案
    制止收回租给佃户的土地
    新华社新德里9日电　印度喀拉拉邦议会昨天通过一项法案，制止任何人收回租给佃户的土地。
    这一法案代替了喀拉拉邦共产党内阁组成一星期以后所发布的内容相同的一项命令。它的目的是要使那些实际占有土地的佃户在议会有时间研究整个土地问题以前不致受到干扰。
    喀拉拉邦的土地问题是尖锐的。由于人口很稠密，对土地的压力很大。地主在这个邦的特拉凡哥尔、柯钦和马拉巴等中部地区占统治地位。
    在议会讨论制止收回佃户土地的法案的时候，三个反对党退出了会议，他们说，共产党内阁要草率地通过这项法案。共产党议员们指出，邦内阁原先发布的命令在六星期之内就要失效，因此法案不能拖延了。他们说，“在通过法案时即使只拖延一天，其结果也会是有些佃田被收回。”
