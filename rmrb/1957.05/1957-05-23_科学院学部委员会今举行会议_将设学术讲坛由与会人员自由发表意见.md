Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Academic Committee to Hold Meeting Today; Academic Forum to be Established for Participants to Freely Express Opinions
Author:
Date: 1957-05-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: The Second Plenary Session of the Academic Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences is scheduled to be held in Beijing on the 23rd. The first plenary session was held in June 1955 when the Academic Divisions were established.

This meeting is scheduled to last for eight days, preparing to review the work of the Chinese Academy of Sciences over the past two years; clarify the future research direction of the Academy; read academic papers; and hold the award ceremony for the 1956 Chinese Academy of Sciences Science Award (Natural Science Section).

During the meeting, an academic forum will be opened, and participants will freely express their opinions on the development of China's scientific endeavors at the academic forum.

In addition to the members of the Academic Divisions, those attending the meeting will also include heads of various research units of the Academy of Sciences, winners of the 1956 Chinese Academy of Sciences Science Award (Natural Science Section), scientists who will read academic papers, and Soviet and Polish advisors and experts from the Academy of Sciences. More than 500 people will participate in the meeting, coming from scientific research institutions, universities, and industrial departments across the country.

### 科学院学部委员会今举行会议  将设学术讲坛由与会人员自由发表意见

1957-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    科学院学部委员会今举行会议
    将设学术讲坛由与会人员自由发表意见
    新华社22日讯　中国科学院学部委员会第二次全体会议定23日在北京举行。第一次全体会议是在1955年6月学部成立时举行的。
    这次会议预定举行八天，准备检查中国科学院两年来的工作；明确今后科学院的研究方针；宣读学术论文；并且举行1956年度中国科学院科学奖金（自然科学部分）授奖仪式。
    会议期间，将要开设学术讲坛，与会人员将在学术讲坛上自由发表对于发展我国科学事业的一切意见。
    参加这次会议的除了学部委员以外，还有科学院各研究单位的负责人，1956年度中国科学院科学奖金（自然科学部分）获得者，宣读学术论文的科学家，以及科学院的苏联和波兰顾问、专家。参加会议的将有五百多人，他们来自全国各地的科学研究机构、高等学校和产业部门等单位。
