Title: Indonesian People's Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th. The delegation of the Indonesian People's Youth League, invited to attend the Third National Congress of the Communist Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The head of the delegation is Kuhammod Wahartan, First Deputy Secretary-General of the Indonesian People's Youth League.

### 印度尼西亚人民青年团代表团到京

1957-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚人民青年团代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　应邀参加青年团第三次全国代表大会的印度尼西亚人民青年团代表团今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是印度尼西亚人民青年团第一副总书记库哈默德·华特汉。
