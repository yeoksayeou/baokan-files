Title: Yugoslavia Loans Money to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-05-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 2nd: After friendly talks between the trade delegations of Hungary and Yugoslavia, an economic agreement was signed in Budapest on April 30 regarding Hungary's use of a two-year commodity credit of $2 million granted by Yugoslavia.

### 南斯拉夫贷款给匈牙利

1957-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫贷款给匈牙利
    据新华社布达佩斯2日电  匈牙利和南斯拉夫两国贸易代表团经过友好会谈后，4月30日在布达佩斯就匈牙利使用南斯拉夫给予的为期两年的二百万美元的商品信贷问题签订了一项经济协定。
