    Pankratova
    Anna Mikhailovna Pankratova is member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and a member of the Presidium, of Supreme Soviet of the USSR. She is also an academician of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR, the Academy of Sciences of the Belorussian Republic, and the Academy of Educational Sciences of the Russian Federation. She has been elected as an academician or corresponding academician of the Hungarian, German, and Romanian Academies of Sciences.
    Pankratova was born in Odessa in 1897. In 1917, she graduated from Odessa University. From 1919 to 1922, she held responsible party positions in Ukraine. In 1925, she completed the course of the History Department of the Institute of Red Professors.
    Pankratova has written more than 200 historical scientific works. She has received the Stalin Prize, the Order of Lenin, and the Order of the Red Banner of Labor.
    In October 1954, Pankratova visited China as part of a Soviet cultural delegation.
        (Xin Fang) (Attached picture)

### 潘克拉托娃
新方
1957-05-28
第5版()
专栏：人物介绍

    潘克拉托娃
    安·米·潘克拉托娃是苏共中央委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员。她还是苏联科学院、白俄罗斯共和国科学院和俄罗斯联邦教育科学院的院士。她曾经被选为匈牙利、德国和罗马尼亚科学院的院士或通讯院士。
    潘克拉托娃1897年生于敖德萨。1917年，她在敖德萨大学毕业，1919年到1922年在乌克兰担任党的负责工作。1925年，她读完了红色教授学院历史系的课程。
    潘克拉托娃共写过二百多种历史科学著作。她曾获得斯大林奖金、列宁勋章和劳动红旗勋章。
    1954年10月潘克拉托娃曾经随苏联文化代表团访问过中国。
