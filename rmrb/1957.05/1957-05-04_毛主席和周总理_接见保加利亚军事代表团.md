Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Receive Bulgarian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire Bulgarian military delegation headed by Lieutenant General Bozhilov at midnight tonight.

Present at the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Senior General Li Da, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, as well as the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Negarkov.

During the reception, the delegation presented gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Marshal Peng Dehuai.

### 毛主席和周总理  接见保加利亚军事代表团

1957-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理
    接见保加利亚军事代表团
    新华社3日讯　毛泽东主席和周恩来总理今天夜间十二时接见了以波奇瓦罗夫中将为首的保加利亚军事代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有彭德怀元帅、李达上将和萧向荣中将，以及保加利亚驻中国大使湼加尔科夫。
    接见时，代表团向毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和彭德怀元帅赠送礼品。
