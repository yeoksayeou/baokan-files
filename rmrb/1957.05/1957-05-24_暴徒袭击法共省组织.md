Title: Mobs Attack French Communist Party Provincial Organization
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd, TASS, Paris, May 22nd: According to "Paris Radical News," mobs attacked the office of the French Communist Party's Eure department committee in Montpellier on the morning of the 22nd. The mobs threw bombs at the Communist Party office, causing great damage.

### 暴徒袭击法共省组织

1957-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    暴徒袭击法共省组织
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社巴黎22日讯：据“巴黎激进新闻”报道，22日上午蒙拜利埃市有暴徒袭击了共产党爱罗尔省委员会办公处。暴徒向共产党办公处投掷炸弹，造成很大破坏。
