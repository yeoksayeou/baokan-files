Brothers Parties' Life

    Marxist Library Established in Paris
    On the initiative of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, a Marxist library has been established in Paris. French Communist Party General Secretary Maurice Thorez and members of the French Communist Party Central Committee attended the opening ceremony of the library. The library currently houses 10,000 volumes, mainly works on the history of the international workers' movement published in France, the Soviet Union, and other countries.

### 马克思主义图书馆在巴黎成立

1957-05-05
第5版()
专栏：兄弟党的生活

    马克思主义图书馆在巴黎成立
    根据法国共产党中央委员会的倡议，在巴黎成立了一座马克思主义图书馆，法共总书记多列士和法共中央委员们参加了这座图书馆的开幕典礼。这座图书馆现在藏书一万卷，主要是在法国、苏联和其他国家出版的有关国际工人运动历史的著作。
