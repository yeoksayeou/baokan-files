Title: Chen Shutong Receives Toncic-Sorinj
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd. Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Toncic-Sorinj, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Austrian Parliament and member of the People's Party, this morning. Wu Kejian and Wu Maosun were also present at the reception.

### 陈叔通接见汤契奇—索里尼

1957-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    陈叔通接见汤契奇—索里尼
    新华社23日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通今天上午接见了奥地利议会外交委员会主席、人民党议员汤契奇—索里尼。接见的时候，吴克坚、吴茂荪也在座。
