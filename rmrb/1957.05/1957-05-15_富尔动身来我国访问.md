　　Faure Departs to Visit Our Country

　　Comprehensive report from our newspaper: Former French Prime Minister and Radical Socialist Party member Edgar Faure left Paris by plane on May 13, traveling via Karachi and Hong Kong to visit China. Faure is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, accompanied by his wife.

　　Edgar Faure was born in Béziers, Hérault department, southern France in August 1808, and is currently 49 years old. He obtained a Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of Paris. After graduating from university, he worked as a lawyer at the Paris Court of Appeal and Deputy Chairman of the French Auditing Commission. In December 1945, he represented the French Provisional Government at the Nuremberg International Tribunal, prosecuting German Nazi war criminals. In November 1946, he was elected as a Radical Socialist Party member of parliament, and in 1948 he became the Deputy Dean of the High Court, later serving as Secretary-General of the National Assembly Finance Committee. Faure served as Minister of Finance or Minister of Budget in eight cabinets. He served as French Prime Minister twice, in 1952 and 1955.

### 富尔动身来我国访问

1957-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    富尔动身来我国访问
    本报综合报道　法国前总理、激进社会党人埃加·富尔5月13日乘飞机离巴黎取道卡拉奇和香港前来中国访问。富尔是应中国人民外交学会邀请访问中国的，随同他前来的有他的夫人。
    埃加·富尔于1808年8月生于法国南部艾厚鲁特省的贝基耶城，现年49岁。他曾获得巴黎大学法律学士的学位。大学毕业后，当过巴黎上诉法院的律师和法兰西审讯委员会副主席。1945年12月他代表法国临时政府出席纽伦堡国际法庭，对德国纳粹战犯提起诉讼。1946年11月，他被选为激进社会党议员，1948年任高等法院副院长，后任国民议会财经委员会秘书长。富尔曾经在八届内阁中先后担任过财政部长或预算部长。在1952年和1955年两次出任法国总理。
