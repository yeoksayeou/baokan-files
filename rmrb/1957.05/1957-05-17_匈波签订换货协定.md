Title: Hungary and Poland Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-05-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 16th -- According to the Polish Press Agency, on the 15th in Budapest, delegations from Hungary and Poland signed a trade and payment agreement for 1957.

    According to the agreement, Poland's exports to Hungary will double compared to last year. Polish exports will include coal, coke, zinc, chemicals, textiles, motorcycles, textile machinery, excavators, etc. The agreement also stipulates an increase in Polish exports of capital goods to Hungary.

    Hungarian exports to Poland include: alumina, bauxite, petroleum products, stearin, precision instruments, buses, turbo-generators and machinery. Poland is also purchasing daily consumer goods from Hungary such as televisions, radios, motorcycles, bicycles, etc.

### 匈波签订换货协定

1957-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    匈波签订换货协定
    新华社16日讯　据波兰通讯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利和波兰两国代表团15日在布达佩斯签订了1957年换货和支付协定。
    根据协定，波兰向匈牙利的出口价值将比去年增加一倍。在波兰的出口货物中包括煤、焦炭、锌、化学品、纺织品、摩托车、纺织机器、挖土机等。协定还规定增加波兰向匈牙利输出的资本货。
    匈牙利向波兰出口的货物有：氧化铝、铁矾土、石油产品、硬脂、精密仪器、公共汽车、涡轮联动机和机器。波兰还在匈牙利购买了电视机、无线电、机器脚踏车、自行车等日用消费品。
