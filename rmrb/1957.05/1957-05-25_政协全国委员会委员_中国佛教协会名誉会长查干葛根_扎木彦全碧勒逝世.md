Title: Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China, Chagangegen Zhamiyangquanbile, Passes Away

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, May 23rd. Chagangegen Zhamiyangquanbile, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China, Standing Committee Member of the CPPCC Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee, and Honorary President of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Branch of the Buddhist Association of China, passed away due to illness on May 21st at Chagan Naose Temple in Sonid Left Banner, Xilin Gol League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, at the age of sixty-nine. The People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the CPPCC Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Branch of the Buddhist Association of China, and the Religious Affairs Bureau of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region jointly dispatched representatives today to Chagan Naose Temple to attend the funeral.

### 政协全国委员会委员  中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根·扎木彦全碧勒逝世

1957-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会委员  中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根·扎木彦全碧勒逝世
    新华社呼和浩特23日电　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中国佛教协会名誉会长、政协内蒙古自治区委员会常务委员、中国佛教协会内蒙古自治区分会名誉会长查干葛根·扎木彦全碧勒，5月21日因病逝世于内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟苏尼特左旗查干脑色庙，享年六十九岁。内蒙古自治区人民委员会、政协内蒙古自治区委员会、中国佛教协会内蒙古自治区分会和内蒙古自治区人民委员会宗教事务局，今天联合派出代表前往查干脑色庙参加葬礼。
