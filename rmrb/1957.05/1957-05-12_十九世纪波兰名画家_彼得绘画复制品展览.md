Title: Exhibition of Painting Replicas by Nineteenth-Century Polish Painter Piotr

Author:

Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:
	Exhibition of Painting Replicas by Nineteenth-Century Polish Painter Piotr Michałowski
	An exhibition of painting replicas by the nineteenth-century Polish painter Piotr Michałowski opened on the 8th at the waterside pavilion in Zhongshan Park.
	The exhibition includes eighty-four works by the painter, including his most admired paintings of horses, as well as other oil paintings, portraits, watercolors, and sketches.
	The exhibited works were a gift from Poland to the Chinese cultural delegation to Poland last year. The exhibition is organized by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations and, after being exhibited in Beijing, will also be exhibited in other cities.

### 十九世纪波兰名画家   彼得绘画复制品展览

1957-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    十九世纪波兰名画家   彼得绘画复制品展览
    十九世纪波兰名画家彼得·米哈奥夫斯基绘画复制品，8日在中山公园水榭展出。
    这次展出的作品有画家画的最得意的马，以及其他油画、肖像画、水彩画和速写等八十四幅。
    这次展出的作品是波兰在去年赠送我国赴波兰文化代表团的。展览由对外文化联络局举办，在北京展出后还将到其他城市展出。
              （新华社）
