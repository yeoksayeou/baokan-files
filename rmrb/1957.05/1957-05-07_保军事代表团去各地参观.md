Title: Bulgarian Military Delegation Visits Various Places
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: The Bulgarian military delegation, after a six-day visit in Beijing, left Beijing by special plane on the 4th, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, to visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places.

### 保军事代表团去各地参观

1957-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    保军事代表团去各地参观
    据新华社讯　保加利亚军事代表团在北京作了六天的访问后，4日上午在杨秀山中将的陪同下乘专机离开北京，去南京、上海、杭州、广州等地参观访问。
