Title: Demanding Withdrawal from Military Blocs, Pakistani Hunger Strikers Persist in Struggle
Author:
Date: 1957-05-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 16th May: Eight staff members of the Karachi Civil Liberties Committee continued their hunger strike outside the Presidential Palace on May 15th, demanding that Pakistan withdraw from the Baghdad Pact and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, and cancel the Baghdad Pact Council meeting. Five of them have been on hunger strike for nine days.

### 要求退出军事集团  巴基斯坦绝食者坚持斗争

1957-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    要求退出军事集团
    巴基斯坦绝食者坚持斗争
    据新华社卡拉奇16日电　卡拉奇公民自由委员会的八个工作人员5月15日继续在总统府外面绝食，要求巴基斯坦退出巴格达条约和东南亚条约组织，并且取消巴格达条约理事会会议。其中五人已经绝食九天。
