Title: Peng Zhen Hosts Reception for Faure; Faure Visits Guo Moruo
Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing, and his wife, hosted a reception in Beijing today to welcome former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception.

As the setting sun cast its glow and a gentle breeze blew, the guests sat around tables on the lawn, engaging in lively conversation.

Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, and Ma Yinchu spoke with Faure about the construction of Beijing and the current political situation in France.

At the reception, Peng Zhen also introduced the guests to Zhang Bojun, Xi Zhongxun, the Vice-Mayors of Beijing, and prominent figures from all walks of life. Here, the guests met many people from various fields who had studied or visited France.

The reception concluded as twilight descended.

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife paid a visit to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, this afternoon.

Faure mentioned his interest in China's policy of "Letting a hundred flowers bloom, and a hundred schools of thought contend." Guo Moruo explained that this policy could promote the prosperity of literary creation and the development of scientific research. He also introduced the contention of a hundred schools of thought in the Spring and Autumn and Warring States periods of China and the current implementation of the "Letting a hundred flowers bloom, and a hundred schools of thought contend" policy in China.

The economist from France also inquired in detail about the current economic situation in China.

The guests also discussed issues such as "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision."

### 彭真举行酒会欢迎富尔  富尔拜会郭沫若

1957-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真举行酒会欢迎富尔
    富尔拜会郭沫若
    新华社25日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真和夫人今天在北京举行酒会，欢迎法国前总理富尔和夫人。
    周恩来总理出席了酒会。
    夕阳西斜，清风徐来。宾主们在草坪上，围桌而坐，开怀畅谈。
    周恩来、彭真、陈叔通、张奚若、马寅初同富尔谈到了北京市的建设情况和法国的当前政局。
    在酒会上，彭真还介绍了客人同章伯钧、习仲勋、北京市各位副市长以及各界著名人士一一见面。在这里，客人会见了许多在法国读过书和访问过法国的各方面人士。
    酒会在暮色苍茫中结束。
    新华社25日讯　法国前总理富尔和夫人今天下午拜会了中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若。
    富尔谈到了他对中国的“百花齐放、百家争鸣”这一方针感到兴趣。郭沫若介绍说：这个方针可以促使文学创作的繁荣和科学研究工作的发展。他还介绍了中国春秋战国时代百家争鸣和中国当前贯彻“百花齐放、百家争鸣”方针等情况。
    这位来自法国的经济学家还详细了解了中国目前的经济情况。
    宾主还交谈了关于“长期共存、互相监督”等问题。
