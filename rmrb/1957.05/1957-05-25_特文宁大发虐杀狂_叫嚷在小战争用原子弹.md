Title: Twining, the Murderous Maniac, Raves About Using Atomic Bombs in Small Wars
Author:
Date: 1957-05-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th, New York: Nathan Twining, who is about to become Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, said in a speech on May 22nd that the United States must be prepared to use atomic weapons in small wars or localized conflicts anywhere in the world whenever American security is threatened.

The current U.S. Air Force Chief of Staff said that he personally "cannot understand" the view that the use of atomic weapons in small wars would inevitably lead to a world war. Twining said that if the United States intends to use atomic weapons, then it will provide itself with "new deterrent power."

### 特文宁大发虐杀狂  叫嚷在小战争用原子弹

1957-05-25
第6版()
专栏：

    特文宁大发虐杀狂
    叫嚷在小战争用原子弹
    新华社24日讯　纽约消息：即将就任美国参谋长联席会议主席的特文宁5月22日在一篇演说里说，美国必须做好准备在全世界任何地方，只要当美国的安全受到威胁，就在小规模战争或者局部性冲突中使用原子武器。
    这位现任美国空军参谋长说，他本人“决不能理解”认为在小规模战争里使用原子武器必然会导致世界大战的这种看法。特文宁说，如果美国打算使用原子武器的话，那就是它为自己提供了“新的威慑力量”。
