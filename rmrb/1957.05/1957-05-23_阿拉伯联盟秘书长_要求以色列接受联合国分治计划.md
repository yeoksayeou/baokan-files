Title: Arab League Secretary-General Demands Israel Accept the UN Partition Plan

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd -- According to a United Press International report from Baghdad, Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna demanded on May 20th that Israel accept the UN's 1947 partition plan for Palestine as the first step towards a comprehensive solution to the Palestinian issue.

UPI pointed out that this is the first time an Arab leader has indicated that Arab countries are willing to negotiate with Israel and even recognize its existence. The Arab League, represented by Hassouna, includes nine countries: Egypt, Syria, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Libya, and Sudan.

Hassouna told UPI reporters that the time is ripe for Israel to accept the UN resolution, "the sooner the better for Israel's future." He also said, "I see no other way out for Israel than to face reality."

Hassouna said that solving this problem would pave the way for resolving other issues such as the passage of Israeli ships through the Suez Canal and the Gulf of Aqaba.

According to the UN's 1947 partition plan, Israel should return to Arab countries approximately one-third of its current territory taken from Arab countries. Arab countries could reclaim parts of the Negev and Southern Galilee, and Jerusalem would become an international city.

UPI estimates that implementing this partition plan would allow 70% of the one million Arab refugees to return to their homes.

When asked if accepting the 1947 partition plan meant abandoning the previous position of Arab countries not recognizing Israel, Hassouna said, "In the beginning, everyone opposed the state of Israel, which is natural. But Israel exists now and is a member of the United Nations."

Hassouna also said, "If it (referring to Israel) was imposed on this world by the United Nations, then it is entirely justified to force it to accept all (UN) resolutions."

### 阿拉伯联盟秘书长  要求以色列接受联合国分治计划

1957-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟秘书长
    要求以色列接受联合国分治计划
    新华社22日讯　据合众社从巴格达报道，阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳5月20日要求以色列接受联合国1947年通过的巴勒斯坦分治计划，作为总的解决巴勒斯坦问题的第一步。
    合众社指出，这是一个阿拉伯领袖第一次表示阿拉伯国家愿意同以色列进行谈判，甚至愿意承认以色列的存在。哈苏纳所代表的阿拉伯联盟包括九个国家：埃及、叙利亚、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、沙特阿拉伯、也门、利比亚和苏丹。
    哈苏纳对合众社记者说，以色列接受联合国决议的时机已经成熟了，“为以色列的将来着想，越快越好”。他还说，“我看不出以色列除了面对现实而外还有什么别的出路”。
    哈苏纳说，这个问题的解决将为以色列船只通过苏伊士运河和亚喀巴湾等其他问题的解决铺平道路。
    根据联合国1947年的分治计划，以色列应当把约相当于它目前疆土的三分之一的取自阿拉伯国家的土地交还阿拉伯国家，阿拉伯国家可以收回尼加夫和南加利利的一部分，耶路撒冷将成为一个国际城市。
    据合众社估计，执行这个分治计划，将使一百万阿拉伯难民中的70％可以回他们的老家。
    在被问到接受1947年分治计划是否就是放弃了从前阿拉伯国家不承认以色列的立场时，哈苏纳说，“在开始时，大家反对以色列这个国家，这是自然的。但是目前以色列存在着，并且是联合国的一个会员国”。
    哈苏纳还说，“如果说它（指以色列）是由联合国强加给这个世界的，那么强使接受所有的（联合国）决议就完全是正当的了”。
