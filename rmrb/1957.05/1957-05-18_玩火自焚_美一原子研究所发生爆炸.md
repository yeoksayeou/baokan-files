Title: Playing with Fire and Burning Oneself: Explosion at U.S. Atomic Research Institute

Author:

Date: 1957-05-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th: An explosion occurred at the Brookhaven National Atomic Research Institute in the United States, injuring four people, one of whom was seriously injured. In addition, six other people were affected by acid vapor. An atomic discussion meeting with the participation of many Latin American representatives was being held there at the time of the explosion.

The cause of the explosion is unknown.

### 玩火自焚  美一原子研究所发生爆炸

1957-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    玩火自焚
    美一原子研究所发生爆炸
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：美国布罗克海温国立原子研究所发生爆炸，有四人受伤，其中一人重伤，另外还有六个人受到酸蒸汽的影响。爆炸时正在那里举行着有许多拉丁美洲代表参加的一次原子讨论会。
    发生爆炸的原因未详。
