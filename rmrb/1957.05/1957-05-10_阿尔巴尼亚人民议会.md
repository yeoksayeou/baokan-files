Title: Albanian People's Assembly
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 5

Column: Data

    The Albanian People's Assembly is the highest organ of state power in Albania. It was established on January 11, 1946, initially named the Constitutional Assembly, and later renamed the People's Assembly based on the constitution passed in March 1946.

    The Albanian People's Assembly is formed by representatives elected by citizens at a rate of one representative per 20,000 residents in each electoral district. After liberation, the People's Assembly has held three elections, the most recent of which was held on May 30, 1954.

    The People's Assembly is unicameral, with a term of four years and regular sessions twice a year. At the beginning of each session, the People's Assembly elects a chairman, a vice-chairman, and a secretary-general. The standing body of the People's Assembly is the Presidium of the People's Assembly. The current People's Assembly is the third term, with a total of one hundred and thirty-four deputies.

    The current Chairman of the People's Assembly is Rita Marko, the Vice-Chairman is Fecor Shehu, and the Secretary is Sali Mborja.

    Since 1955, the Albanian People's Assembly has successively invited parliamentary delegations from the Soviet Union, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, and our country to visit Albania. The Albanian People's Assembly has also organized delegations to visit the Soviet Union, Poland, and the German Democratic Republic.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会

1957-05-10
第5版()
专栏：资料

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会是阿尔巴尼亚最高的国家政权机关，它成立于1946年1月11日，当时称为制宪会议，后来根据1946年3月通过的宪法才改称为人民议会。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会是由公民按选区每二万居民选出代表一人而产生的。解放后，人民议会共举行过三次选举，最近一次选举是在1954年5月30日举行的。
    人民议会是一院制，每届任期四年，每年召开例会两次。人民议会在每届会议开始时，选出主席、副主席和秘书长各一人，人民议会的常设机构是人民议会主席团。本届人民议会是第三届，共有议员一百三十四名。
    现任的人民议会主席是里塔·马尔科，副主席是费切列特·谢胡，秘书是萨利·姆波里亚。
    从1955年起，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会曾先后邀请了苏联、波兰、德意志民主共和国和我国的议会代表团去阿尔巴尼亚访问，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会也组织了代表团到苏联、波兰和德意志民主共和国进行了访问。
