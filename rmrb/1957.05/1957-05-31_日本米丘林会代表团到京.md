Title: Delegation from the Japan Michurin Society Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The delegation from the Japan Michurin Society, led by the head of the delegation, President of the Japan Michurin Society, Professor of Hokkaido University, and Doctor of Science, Hajime Matsuura, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 30th.

    The full members of the delegation are: Deputy Head Kyoichi Washimi; Members Minoru Tokuda, Osamu Hagiya, Saburo Ishizaki, Tomoharu Egawa, Kenzo Kamei, Yoshimune Yatomi, Takajiro Tanabe, and Hiroshi Shiga, among others.

### 日本米丘林会代表团到京

1957-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    日本米丘林会代表团到京
    据新华社讯　日本米丘林会访华代表团30日晚在团长、日本米丘林会会长、北海道大学教授、理学博士松浦一率领下到达北京。
    代表团全体人员是：副团长鹫美京一；团员德田御稔、萩屋董、石埼三郎、江川友治、龟井健三、矢富良宗、田边鹰次郎和志贺浩等人。
