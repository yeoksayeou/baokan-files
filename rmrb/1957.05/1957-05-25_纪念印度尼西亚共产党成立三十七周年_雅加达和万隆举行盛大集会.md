Title: Grand Rallies Held in Jakarta and Bandung to Commemorate the 37th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1957-05-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 24th: The Jakarta City Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia held a grand rally on the evening of May 23rd, attended by two thousand people, to commemorate the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia. Among the guests were Suwirjo, Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party; responsible persons from the central government, Jakarta municipal government, and the garrison command; members of Parliament and the Constitutional Assembly; and leaders of mass organizations.

    Lukman, First Deputy General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, explained at the meeting the current political and organizational status and tasks of the Communist Party of Indonesia, and clarified the policy of the Communist Party of Indonesia in achieving national unity. He said that correct policies and truth will always be strengthened by the events that continue to occur. He also said that the Sukarno Plan is the most correct summary for achieving Indonesian national unity.

    The Bandung City Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia also held a commemoration meeting on May 22nd for the 37th anniversary of the Party's founding. More than five thousand people attended the commemoration meeting. Aidit, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, spoke at the meeting. He urged everyone to study more in order to improve revolutionary practice.

### 纪念印度尼西亚共产党成立三十七周年  雅加达和万隆举行盛大集会

1957-05-25
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念印度尼西亚共产党成立三十七周年
    雅加达和万隆举行盛大集会
    新华社雅加达24日电　印度尼西亚共产党雅加达市委员会5月23日晚上举行了有两千人参加的盛大集会，纪念印度尼西亚共产党成立三十七周年。来宾中有印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约、中央政府、雅加达市政府和卫戍司令部负责人、国会和制宪议会议员、群众团体领袖等。
    印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼在会上说明了目前印度尼西亚共产党在政治上和组织上的地位和任务，并且说明了印度尼西亚共产党在实现民族团结方面的政策。他说，正确的政策和真理将永远会被继续发生的事情所加强。他又说，苏加诺方案是实现印度尼西亚民族团结的最正确的总结。
    印度尼西亚共产党万隆市委员会5月22日也为党成立三十七周年举行纪念会。出席纪念会的有五千多人。印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地在那个会上讲了话。他要求大家为改善革命的实践而更多地学习。
