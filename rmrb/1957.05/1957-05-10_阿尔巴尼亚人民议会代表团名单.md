Title: List of Delegates of the Albanian People's Assembly

Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1

    List of Delegates of the Albanian People's Assembly
    Head of Delegation:
    Rita Marko: Chairman of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Secretary of the Party Central Committee.
    Members:
    Muslim Peza: Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, Vice-Chairman of the Albanian Democratic Front.
    Tonin Jakova: Member of the People's Assembly, Minister of Transportation, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.
    Mihal Shtulla: Member of the People's Assembly, Vice-Chairman of the Albanian Democratic Front, Chairman of the Albanian National Committee for the Defense of Peace, President of the Tirana Medical College.
    Nexhmije Hoxha (Mrs. Hoxha): Member of the People's Assembly (female), Vice-Chairman of the Albanian-Soviet Friendship Association, Member of the Presidium of the Albanian Women's Union, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Minister of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Party Central Committee.
    Sadik Bocaj: Member of the People's Assembly, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, First Secretary of the Party Committee of Shkodër Province.
    Çini Sako: Member of the People's Assembly, Member of the Albanian Institute of Science, Writer.
    Fejzo Ismaili: Member of the People's Assembly, Member of the Legal Committee of the People's Assembly.
    Demir Lici: Member of the People's Assembly.
    Muzafer Salihaj: Member of the People's Assembly.
    Recep Arapi: Member of the People's Assembly.
