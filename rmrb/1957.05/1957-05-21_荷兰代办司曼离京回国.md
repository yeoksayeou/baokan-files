Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Sasse departs Beijing for home

Author:

Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 5

    Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Sasse departs Beijing for home
    Xinhua News Agency: The Chargé d'Affaires of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to China, Mr. Sasse, was recalled and left Beijing for home on the 18th.
    Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the airport to see him off.

### 荷兰代办司曼离京回国

1957-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    荷兰代办司曼离京回国
    新华社讯　荷兰王国驻我国代办司曼已奉调在18日离开北京返国。
    前往机场送行的有我国外交部礼宾司司长王倬如等人。
