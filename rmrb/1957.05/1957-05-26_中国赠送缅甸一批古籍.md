Title: China Presents a Batch of Ancient Books to Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, May 25th: Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, presented more than 760 volumes of ancient Chinese books to the Burmese Ministry of Education and Culture on May 24th on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Culture.

### 中国赠送缅甸一批古籍

1957-05-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中国赠送缅甸一批古籍
    据新华社仰光25日电　中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明5月24日代表中国文化部将中国古籍七百六十多册，赠送给缅甸教育和文化部。
