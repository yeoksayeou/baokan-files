Title: Central Leaders Receive Youth Representatives from Various Countries; Peng Dehuai, He Long, Ye Jianying Receive Youth Representatives from the Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th - Central Committee leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, He Long, Lu Dingyi, Bo Yibo, Wang Jiaxiang, and Hu Qiaomu, this afternoon received the delegations of youth organizations from various countries who were invited to attend the Third National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. The delegations received were from sixteen countries, including the Soviet Union, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Romania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, Italy, Hungary, Indonesia, Albania, France, Yugoslavia, Mongolia, the United Kingdom, and Japan.

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th - Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense, and Marshals He Long and Ye Jianying, this morning received the youth representatives from the armed forces who were attending the Third National Congress of the Youth League.

Marshal Peng Dehuai congratulated the representatives on the renaming of the League. He said that the renaming of the Youth League to the Communist Youth League has increased the League's responsibilities. The youth must strive to build socialism, to eliminate poverty for 600 million people, and to realize the most beautiful social system of mankind—communist society.

Also present at the reception were General Chen Geng, General Tan Zheng, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Gan Siqi, and Admiral Wang Hongkun of the Navy.

### 中央领导人接见各国青年代表  彭德怀、贺龙、叶剑英接见部队青年代表

1957-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见各国青年代表
    彭德怀、贺龙、叶剑英接见部队青年代表
    新华社25日讯　中共中央领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、邓小平、林伯渠、董必武、彭真、贺龙、陆定一、薄一波、王稼祥、胡乔木，今天下午接见了应邀参加中国新民主主义青年团第三次全国代表大会的各国青年组织的代表团。受接见的有苏联、越南民主共和国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、意大利、匈牙利、印度尼西亚、阿尔巴尼亚、法国、南斯拉夫、蒙古、英国、日本等十六个国家的青年代表团。
    新华社25日讯　国防部长彭德怀元帅和贺龙元帅、叶剑英元帅，在今天上午接见了出席青年团第三次全国代表大会的部队青年代表。
    彭德怀元帅向代表们祝贺团的改名。他说，青年团改名为共产主义青年团使团的任务加重了。青年们要为建设社会主义，要为六亿人民消灭贫困，实现人类理想的最美好的社会制度——共产主义社会而奋斗。
    接见时在座的，还有陈赓大将、谭政大将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将和王宏坤海军上将。
