Title: Chairman Mao Receives Albanian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Albanian People's Republic People's Assembly delegation headed by Chairman Marko this evening. Chairman Mao and the distinguished guests had a cordial conversation about the world situation.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, Zhang Su, and Yu Xinqing were present at the reception.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, and his wife were also present at the reception.
Top: Chairman Mao receives the Albanian People's Assembly Delegation. (Attached photo)
Second from the left is Rita Marko.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo photographed.

### 毛主席接见阿尔巴尼亚议会代表团

1957-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿尔巴尼亚议会代表团
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚上接见了以马尔科主席为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团。毛主席同贵宾们在友好的气氛中，畅谈了世界大势。
    接见的时候，在座的有周恩来总理，彭真副委员长和张苏、余心清。
    接见的时候，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里和夫人也在座。上：毛主席接见阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团。（附图片）
    左起第二人是里塔·马尔科。
                                 新华社记者　侯波摄
