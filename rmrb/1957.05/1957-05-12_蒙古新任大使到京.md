Title: New Mongolian Ambassador Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Sunom Lubsang, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome him.

Tsedendishi, Chargé d'affaires ad interim, and all members of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 蒙古新任大使到京

1957-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古新任大使到京
    新华社11日讯　蒙古人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使苏诺姆·鲁布桑今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长王倬如。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办策登依什和全体馆员，以及各国驻华使节、外交官员也到机场欢迎。
