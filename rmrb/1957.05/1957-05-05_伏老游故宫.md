Title:伏老游故宫 (Old Voroshilov Visits the Palace Museum)
Author:
Date: 1957-05-05
Page: 1
Voroshilov Visits the Palace Museum

    (Our dispatch) Yesterday afternoon, Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De, visited the Palace Museum. Old Voroshilov and Vice Chairman Zhu De toured the three main halls, the Hall of Mental Cultivation, Changchun Palace, and other places, side by side and hand in hand.

    Old Voroshilov praised the architecture of the 500-year-old imperial palace. He was particularly interested in the diverse works of art displayed in the palaces, such as bronzes, jade artifacts, and sculptures. Old Voroshilov said that these represent the wisdom and talent of the Chinese working people.

    After touring the Imperial Garden with Vice Chairman Zhu, Old Voroshilov took a brief rest at the Shufangzhai. Wu Zhongchao, the director of the Palace Museum, presented Chairman Voroshilov with six volumes of copperplate Song dynasty paintings.

### 伏老游故宫

1957-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    伏老游故宫
    本报讯　昨天下午，伏罗希洛夫主席在朱德副主席陪同下，参观了故宫博物院。伏老和朱德副主席并肩携手地游览了三大殿、养心殿、长春宫等处。
    伏老对于已有五百年历史的皇宫的建筑称赞不已，特别是对宫殿里陈列的青铜器、玉器、雕塑等多种多样的艺术品发生浓厚的兴趣。伏老说这是中国劳动人民的智慧和才华。
    伏老同朱副主席游览了御花园后，在漱芳斋稍憩。故宫博物院院长吴仲超向伏罗希洛夫主席赠送了六本铜版宋人画册。
