Title: Pakistan Government Decides to Commemorate the Centenary of the Independence Uprising
Author:
Date: 1957-05-09
Page: 6

    The Pakistan Government Decides
    to Commemorate the Centenary of the Independence Uprising
    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th – The Pakistan Embassy in China announced today that the Pakistan government has decided to commemorate the centenary of the 1857 uprising for independence throughout Pakistan. Commemorative activities will begin on May 10th and last for one week.
    To commemorate this anti-British uprising that broke out on the Indian-Pakistani subcontinent, mass rallies will be held throughout Pakistan, at which prayers will be offered for the martyrs of the independence struggle. Parades will also be held in various places. Public places and private residences will be decorated with lanterns and colored streamers on the evening of May 10th. Reviews will also be held in major cities.

### 巴基斯坦政府决定  纪念独立起义一百周年

1957-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府决定
    纪念独立起义一百周年
    新华社8日讯　巴基斯坦驻华大使馆今天发布消息说，巴基斯坦政府决定在全巴基斯坦纪念1857年争取独立起义一百周年。纪念活动将从5月10日开始进行一个星期。
    为了纪念这次在印度—巴基斯坦次大陆上爆发的反英起义，巴基斯坦全国各地将举行群众大会，在会上将为独立斗争中的先烈祈祷。各地并且将举行游行。公共场所和私人住宅将在5月10日晚上悬灯结彩。各大城市还将举行检阅。
