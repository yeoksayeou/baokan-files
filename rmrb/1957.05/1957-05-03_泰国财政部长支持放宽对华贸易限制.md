Title: Thai Finance Minister Supports Relaxing Trade Restrictions on China
Author:
Date: 1957-05-03
Page: 6

    Thai Finance Minister Supports Relaxing Trade Restrictions on China
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to the "Bangkok World" report, Thai Finance Minister Yutakit said that the Thai government supports the wishes of some Western countries to relax restrictions on trade with the People's Republic of China.

### 泰国财政部长支持放宽对华贸易限制

1957-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国财政部长支持放宽对华贸易限制
    据新华社2日讯  据“曼谷世界报”报道，泰国财政部长裕他吉说，泰国政府支持一些西方国家要求放宽对同中华人民共和国进行贸易的限制的愿望。
