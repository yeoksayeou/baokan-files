Title: Central Leaders Receive Representatives from All Circles

Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, this afternoon received representatives of twenty-four fraternal nationalities who attended the "May Day" celebrations. At the same time, they received all the representatives of the Youth Activists Conference of the organs directly under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
    Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Li Jishen, Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan and others were present at the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, this afternoon received the graduates of the Central Commercial Cadre School.
    Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Li Jishen, Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan and others were present at the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong, and Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, this afternoon received representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers who participated in the "May Day" celebrations.

### 中央领导同志接见各界代表

1957-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见各界代表
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，今天下午接见了二十四个兄弟民族参加“五一”节观礼的代表。同时，接见了中共中央直属各机关青年积极分子代表会议的全体代表。
    接见时在座的，有邓小平、贺龙、李济深、陈叔通、包尔汉等人。
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理，今天下午接见了中央商业干部学校的毕业生。
    接见时在座的，有陈云、邓小平、贺龙、李济深、陈叔通、包尔汉等人。
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席和朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、邓小平、贺龙，在今天下午接见了中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军参加“五一”节观礼的代表。
