Title: Hungarian Education Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: The Hungarian education delegation, which came to China for a visit under the 1957 Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Plan, has concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing for home today.

### 匈教育代表团回国

1957-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    匈教育代表团回国
    据新华社27日讯　根据1957年中匈文化合作计划来中国访问的匈牙利教育代表团已经结束在我国的访问，今天离北京回国。
