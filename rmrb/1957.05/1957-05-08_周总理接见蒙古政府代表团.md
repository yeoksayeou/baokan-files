Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Mongolian Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Mongolian People's Republic government delegation headed by Qi. Su Renza Bu this morning and had a sincere conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Xiaoping and Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Kui Bi.

Also present at the reception were the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, Ce Deng Yishi, and his wife.

After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests. (Attached picture)

### 周总理接见蒙古政府代表团

1957-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见蒙古政府代表团
    新华社7日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见了以齐·苏伦扎布为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，并且同他们进行了恳切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有国务院副总理邓小平、李先念，外交部副部长章汉夫和内蒙古自治区副主席奎璧。
    接见时，蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办策登依什和夫人也在座。
    接见后，周恩来总理设宴招待贵宾。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和齐·苏伦扎布团长（中）谈话　新华社记者　吕厚民摄
