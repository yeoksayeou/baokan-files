Title: Premier Zhou at Reception for Thai Art Troupe Says: Welcome Thai Friends to China to Visit Relatives
Author:
Date: 1957-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception in Beijing this evening to welcome the Thai Art Troupe.

Among those attending the reception were Vice Premier He Long, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles such as Lao She and Mei Lanfang.

Premier Zhou Enlai spoke at the beginning of the reception, saying: "Head of the delegation, General Supervisor, and friends of the art troupe:

"We are very pleased to receive the Thai Art Troupe in Beijing and to see your valuable performances. Just as you sing in the song 'China and Thailand are as Close as One Family,' the people of China and Thailand are relatives. So we especially welcome you to China to visit relatives.

"Your beautiful performances are very similar to ours in tone and style, and there are many things that Chinese artists can learn from.

"I believe that this visit and performance will make the kinship between China and Thailand develop day by day.

"We hope that Thai art groups will often come to China to visit relatives, and we hope that one day Chinese art troupes will also be able to go to Thailand to visit relatives. I personally and my colleagues also hope to see you in Bangkok one day.

"For the development of kinship and cultural exchange between China and Thailand,

"For the success of your performance, and for the good health of all the artists, cheers!"

The head of the art troupe, Phra Chenduriyang, also spoke at the reception, and on behalf of the art troupe, presented Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou with gifts—smoking sets made entirely of tiger skull bones, which Premier Zhou accepted on their behalf.

Premier Zhou had cordial conversations with the Thai artists at the reception and introduced them to meet famous Chinese artists.

Chinese and Thai artists also performed wonderful programs at the reception to add to the fun.

Before the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, among others, watched the performance of the Thai Art Troupe. Premier Zhou Enlai also received the head of the art troupe, Phra Chenduriyang, General Supervisor Luang Sukhum Nayapradit, Manager Cham Sunthonwet, Thai film queen Suphan Buranaphim, and the main actors of the art troupe during the intermission of the performance.

After the performance, Premier Zhou and others went on stage to shake hands with the Thai artists one by one and took photos together.

### 周总理在招待泰国艺术团酒会上说  欢迎泰国朋友来中国走亲戚

1957-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在招待泰国艺术团酒会上说
    欢迎泰国朋友来中国走亲戚
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上在北京举行酒会，欢迎泰国艺术团。
    出席酒会的有副总理贺龙，国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，以及首都文艺界著名人士老舍、梅兰芳等。
    周恩来总理在酒会开始时讲话，他说：团长，总监督以及艺术团的朋友们：
    我们非常高兴能在北京接待泰国艺术团，并且看到你们珍贵的演出。确实像你们在“中泰一家亲”的歌中所唱的那样，中泰两国人民是亲戚。所以我们特别欢迎你们到中国来走亲戚。
    你们优美的演出在情调和风格上同我们的很相近，有许多地方值得中国的艺术家们学习。
    我相信，你们这次访问演出，将会使中泰两国的亲戚关系一天天地发展起来。
    希望泰国的艺术团体常常到中国来走亲戚，希望有一天中国的艺术团也能到泰国去走亲戚，我个人和我的同事也希望有一天能在曼谷见到你们。
    为中泰两国亲戚关系和文化交流的发展，
    为你们演出的成功，为各位艺术家们身体健康干杯！艺术团团长?素瓦·沃拉里洛在酒会上也讲了话，他并以艺术团名义向毛主席和周总理赠送了礼品——用全部虎头骨精制的烟具，由周总理代表受礼。
    周总理在酒会上同泰国艺术家们亲切交谈，并介绍中国著名艺术家同他们见面。
    中、泰艺术家们在酒会上还表演了精彩的节目助兴。
    在酒会以前，周恩来总理、贺龙副总理以及郭沫若副委员长等观看了泰国艺术团的演出，周恩来总理并且在演出休息时，接见了艺术团团长?素瓦·沃拉里洛，总监督?陆·陆速君猜，司理?山·山地那拉，泰国电影皇后素攀·武拉纳屏女士以及艺术团的主要演员。
    演出结束以后，周总理等上台同泰国艺术家们一一握手，并且在一起照了像。
