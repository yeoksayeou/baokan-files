Title: Voroshilov Visits President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1957-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 7th: Chairman Voroshilov paid a visit to President Sukarno at the Independence Palace this morning. The two heads of state had a cordial conversation. President Sukarno introduced Voroshilov to his collection of famous paintings and treasures one by one. They sat side by side in the study and took a photo.

    Those present from the Soviet side during the visit were: Vice-Chairman Rashidov, Minister Yelyutin, Deputy Minister Fedin, and Soviet Ambassador to Indonesia Zhukov.

    Those present from the Indonesian side were the Prime Minister, the First and Second Deputy Prime Ministers, the Speaker of the Parliament, the Speaker of the Constitutional Assembly, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    After visiting President Sukarno, Voroshilov, accompanied by Jakarta Mayor Sudiro and others, laid flowers at the Independence Monument. Afterwards, he laid a wreath at the Heroes' Cemetery in the Jakarta suburbs.

    The roadsides along which Voroshilov's motorcade passed were lined with welcoming crowds. They held Indonesian and Soviet flags, held bouquets of flowers, and cheered loudly to Voroshilov.

### 伏老拜会苏加诺总统

1957-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    伏老拜会苏加诺总统
    据新华社雅加达7日电　伏罗希洛夫主席今天早上在独立宫拜会苏加诺总统。两位元首进行了亲切的谈话。苏加诺总统把他收藏的名画和珍品一一向伏老介绍。他们在书房里并肩坐着照了相。
    拜会时在座的，苏联方面有：拉希多夫副主席、叶留金部长、费德林副部长和苏联驻印度尼西亚大使茹科夫。
    印度尼西亚方面在座的有内阁总理，第一、第二副总理，国会议长，制宪议会议长和外交部长。
    伏老在拜会苏加诺总统以后，在雅加达市长苏迪罗等陪同下向独立纪念碑献了花。这以后，又到雅加达郊区烈士墓献了花圈。
    伏老座车经过的道路两旁站满了欢迎的人群。他们手里拿着印度尼西亚和苏联国旗，捧着鲜花，向伏老高声欢呼。
