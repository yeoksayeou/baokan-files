Title: Premier Zhou Receives Several Japanese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1957-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th -- Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the head of the Japanese "Martyred Chinese Soldiers' Remains Escort Group," Takekazu Katsuo; member and Deputy Secretary-General of the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, Hiroshi Shinoda; and the head priest of the Japanese Denzuin Temple, Ryuko Okochi; Japanese Red Cross Society’s Iwa Kakegawa, Japan-China Friendship Association’s Masazumi Okumiya, Japan Peace Liaison Association’s Katsushige Matsui, and the head of the Japanese Kawasaki City Council Delegation to China, Katsuji Hirano, and 42 others.
    Present at the reception were President of the Red Cross Society of China, Li Dequan, advisor Liao Chengzhi, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wang Kunlun, and others.

### 周总理接见日本几个代表团

1957-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本几个代表团
    新华社18日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了日本“中国殉难烈士骨灰护送团”团长竹中胜男，团员、日本自由民主党副干事长筱田弘作和日本伝通院贯主大河内隆弘；日本红十字会挂川岩，日中友好协会奥宫正澄，日本和平联络会松井胜重以及日本川崎市议员访华代表团团长平野胜治等四十二人。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国红十字会总会会长李德全、顾问廖承志，北京市副市长王昆仑等。
