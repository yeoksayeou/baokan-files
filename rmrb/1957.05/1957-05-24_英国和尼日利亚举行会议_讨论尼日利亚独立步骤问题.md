Title: UK and Nigeria Hold Meeting to Discuss Steps for Nigerian Independence

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 23rd: A conference to discuss the steps for Nigeria, West Africa, to achieve self-government and then independence, is scheduled to be held today at Lancaster House here.

    This meeting has attracted great attention because it affects the future of the largest remaining British colony. This colony, which has been under British rule since 1900, has a population of more than 33 million, accounting for almost half of the entire British colonial population, and is equal to seven times the population of its neighbor, Ghana.

    Among the three important representatives attending this meeting—the three regional premiers—the Eastern Regional Premier Azikiwe and the Western Regional Premier Awolowo both demanded immediate self-government; that is to say, the regional premiers should first replace the British governors as administrative leaders. However, the Northern Regional Premier Ahmadu's party—the Northern People's Congress—does not demand that the North implement self-government now, but rather in 1959.

    Delegations from all regions (including the Northern Region) and political parties in Nigeria have now arrived in London, and they are now unanimously demanding that Nigeria achieve independence in 1959.

    The British side participating in this meeting includes Colonial Secretary Lennox-Boyd and the British Governor-General of the Federation of Nigeria, Robertson.

### 英国和尼日利亚举行会议  讨论尼日利亚独立步骤问题

1957-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    英国和尼日利亚举行会议
    讨论尼日利亚独立步骤问题
    据新华社伦敦23日电　讨论西非洲的尼日利亚先自治而后独立的步骤的会议，定于今天在这里兰开斯特大厦召开。
    这个会议引起了极大的注意，因为它影响残存的英国最大的殖民地的未来。这个从1900年起一直受英国统治的殖民地有人口三千三百多万，即几占英国全部殖民地人口的一半，等于它的邻国加纳的人口的七倍。
    在出席这次会议的三个重要代表——三个地方的总理中，东部地方总理阿齐克韦和西部地方总理阿沃卢瓦都要求立即实行自治；这就是说，首先由各地方总理代替英国总督成为行政领袖，但是北部地方总理艾哈迈德的党——北方人民大会并不要求现在就让北方实行自治，而是要求在1959年实行自治。
    尼日利亚所有各地区（包括北部地方在内）和各政党的代表团现在都已经来到伦敦，他们现在都一致要求尼日利亚在1959年实行独立。
    英国方面参加这次会议的，包括殖民地大臣伦诺克斯—波伊德和英国驻尼日利亚联邦总督罗伯逊。
