Title: Widespread Promotion of the World Youth Festival; China Organizes Long-Distance Relay with Korea, China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: A long-distance relay involving Korea, China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union is being organized to welcome the Sixth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival. The All-China Youth Federation, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Sports Federation, and the Physical Culture and Sports Commission have jointly organized the "China Headquarters for the Sixth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival Korean-Chinese-Mongolian-Soviet International Long-Distance Relay" to organize and direct this long-distance relay activity.

This international long-distance relay is organized according to the decision of the International Preparatory Committee of the Festival. At its Standing Committee meeting held in January this year, the International Preparatory Committee of the Festival decided to organize six international long-distance relays in Europe and Asia in order to widely publicize the Festival and its significance among young people.

After consultation with relevant parties in Korea and Mongolia, the Chinese delegation to the Festival Preparatory Committee has decided to organize the long-distance relay within China from June 5th to 18th. On June 5th, the baton will be received from the Korean side at the Dandong Sino-Korean border and passed through Liaoning and Hebei provinces, Beijing Municipality, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. On the 18th, it will be handed over to the Mongolian side at Zamyn-Üüd in the Mongolian People's Republic, near Erenhot. This relay will pass through thirty counties and cities, covering a total length of approximately 1,800 kilometers, and is planned to be carried out in various ways such as running, motorcycles, bicycles, and horse riding. Border youth meetings and other activities will be held in Sinuiju in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Dandong in China, and Zamyn-Üüd in the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 广泛宣传世界青年联欢节  我国组织朝中蒙苏长途接力

1957-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    广泛宣传世界青年联欢节
    我国组织朝中蒙苏长途接力
    新华社20日讯　一个为了迎接第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节而举办的朝、中、蒙、苏四国长途接力正在组织中。中华全国民主青年联合会、青年团中央、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国体育总会和体育运动委员会等单位已联合组织“第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节朝中蒙苏国际长途接力中国境内指挥部”，负责组织和指挥这一个长途接力活动。
    这个国际长途接力，是根据联欢节国际筹委会的决定而组织的。联欢节国际筹委会在今年1月间举行的常设委员会会议上决定在欧亚两洲组织六路国际长途接力，以便在青年中广泛宣传联欢节和它的意义。
    中国参加联欢节筹委会经过和朝鲜、蒙古有关方面磋商后，已经决定从6月5日至18日组织在中国境内的长途接力。6月5日在安东中朝边境从朝鲜方面接来接力棒，经过辽宁、河北两省和北京市、内蒙古自治区，于18日在靠近二连的蒙古人民共和国扎门乌德交给蒙古方面。这一段接力将经过三十个县市，全长约一千八百公里，准备用跑步、摩托车、自行车、跑马等各种方式进行。在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的新义州，我国的安东市和蒙古人民共和国的扎门乌德三地将举行边境青年会见等活动。
