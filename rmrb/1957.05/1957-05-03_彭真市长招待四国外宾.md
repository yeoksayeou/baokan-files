Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Foreign Guests

Author:

Date: 1957-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, hosted a reception this evening at the Zhongshan Park Cultural Club to entertain the Romanian Grand National Assembly and Bucharest City People's Council delegation headed by Chairman Constantin Pârvulescu, Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio, the Maghreb (Moroccan) National Consultative Assembly delegation to China, and the Japanese Tokai Local Government delegation to China.

Attending the reception as hosts were Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Zhang Youyu, Wang Kunlun, Feng Jiping, Cheng Hongyi, and Jia Tingsan, Vice Mayors of Beijing; heads of various departments of the Municipal People's Committee; and Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others.

After the reception, the guests participated in the garden party.

### 彭真市长招待四国外宾

1957-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长招待四国外宾
    新华社2日讯  北京市市长彭真今晚在中山公园文化俱乐部举行酒会，招待以康·帕伏列斯库主席为首的罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团、缅甸联邦民族院议长萧恢塔、马格里勃（摩洛哥）国民协商议会访华代表和日本东海地方自治体访华代表团。
    出席酒会作陪的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，中国红十字总会会长李德全，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑、冯基平、程宏毅、贾庭三，市人民委员会各部门负责人，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁等。
    酒会后客人们参加了游园会。
