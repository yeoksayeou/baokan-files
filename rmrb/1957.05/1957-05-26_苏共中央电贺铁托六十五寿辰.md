Title: CPSU Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Tito on His Sixty-Fifth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 24th (TASS): The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to Comrade Tito, General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, congratulating him on his sixty-fifth birthday. The telegram stated that the CPSU Central Committee believes that the great traditional friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia will continue to strengthen and develop, and that both sides will do their utmost to realize broad and comprehensive cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia, and to develop friendly relations between the two parties based on the principles of Marxism-Leninism, for the victory of universal peace and the cause of socialism.

### 苏共中央电贺铁托六十五寿辰

1957-05-26
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央电贺铁托六十五寿辰
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会打电报给南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托同志，祝贺他的六十五寿辰。电文说，苏共中央相信，南苏两国人民伟大的传统友谊将不断加强和发展，并且为了普遍和平和社会主义事业的胜利，双方将竭尽全力来实现南苏两国的广泛全面的合作，以及根据马克思列宁主义的原则来发展南苏两党之间的友好关系。
