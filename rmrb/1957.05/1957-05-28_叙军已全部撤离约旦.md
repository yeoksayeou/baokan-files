Title: Syrian Troops Completely Withdraw from Jordan
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: Amman Dispatch: The Jordanian government announced on the 26th that the last batch of Syrian troops stationed in Jordan had withdrawn from Jordan.
    On the same day, Jordanian authorities announced that police had arrested two more patriotic individuals accused of being communists, namely Asal and Salih. According to a Middle East News Agency report on the 26th, Jordanian authorities arrested a large number of Syrians in Jordan.
    Jordan's demand for the withdrawal of Syrian troops from Jordan has been criticized by some newspapers in Egypt, Syria, and Lebanon.

### 叙军已全部撤离约旦

1957-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    叙军已全部撤离约旦
    据新华社27日讯　安曼消息：约旦政府26日宣布，驻在约旦的最后一批叙利亚军队已经撤离约旦。
    约旦当局同一天宣布，警察又逮捕了两位被指为共产党人的爱国人士，他们是阿萨尔和萨勒。据中东通讯社26日报道，约旦当局逮捕了在约旦的大批叙利亚人。
    约旦要求叙利亚军队撤出约旦的行动，已经受到埃及、叙利亚和黎巴嫩一些报纸的抨击。
