Title: Soviet Minister of Higher Education Yelyutin Reports to Chinese Higher Education Workers on the Experience of Soviet Higher Education
Author:
Date: 1957-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Soviet Minister of Higher Education Yelyutin reported this afternoon to Chinese higher education workers in Beijing on the forty years of experience of Soviet higher education. He said that cultivating new, people's, socialist intellectuals is one of the most significant achievements of the Soviet Union in carrying out the cultural revolution under the leadership of the Communist Party and according to the Party's Leninist program. He comprehensively introduced the past and present of Soviet higher education, and also talked about the future of Soviet higher education.

Yelyutin said that the Soviet Union is extremely interested in China's culture, science and education, and this interest is growing. He hopes that this meeting will help further strengthen the links between the universities of the two countries.

### 苏联高等教育部部长叶留金向中国高教工作者  报告苏联高等教育经验

1957-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联高等教育部部长叶留金向中国高教工作者
    报告苏联高等教育经验
    据新华社4日讯　苏联高等教育部部长叶留金今天下午向在北京的中国高等教育工作者，报告苏联四十年高等教育的经验。他说，培养出新的、人民的、社会主义的知识分子，是苏联在共产党的领导下、根据党的列宁主义的纲领进行文化革命所取得的最重大的成果之一。他全面地介绍了苏联高等教育的过去和现在，也谈到了苏联高等教育的将来。
    叶留金说，苏联对中国的文化、科学和教育感到极大的兴趣，而且这种兴趣在日益增长。他希望这次会面将有助于两国高等学校之间联系的进一步加强。
