Title: Burhan Hosts Banquet for Albanian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: Burhan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and his wife hosted a banquet this evening in honor of the entire delegation of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Balleli, and his wife were also invited to the banquet.

Among those present were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Bojun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC; and Patikhan Sugurbayev, Vice-Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.

### 包尔汉宴请阿议会代表团

1957-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    包尔汉宴请阿议会代表团
    新华社29日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席包尔汉和夫人今天晚间设宴招待阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团全体人员。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里和夫人也应邀赴宴。
    作陪的有国务院秘书长习仲勋、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏、政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧、新疆维吾尔自治区副主席帕提汉·苏古尔巴也夫等人。
