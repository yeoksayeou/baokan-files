Title: Poland Makes First Jet Helicopter
Author:
Date: 1957-05-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 25th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency on the 25th, Poland has designed and manufactured its first jet helicopter.

The characteristics of this Polish aircraft are: very simple construction and low cost. Currently, the only countries in the world that mass-produce jet helicopters are the United States and the Netherlands.

### 波兰制成第一架喷气式直升飞机

1957-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰制成第一架喷气式直升飞机
    新华社华沙25日电　据波兰通讯社25日报道，波兰已经设计和制成了第一架喷气式直升飞机。
    波兰这种飞机的特点是：构造非常简单、成本低廉。目前，世界上大批生产喷气式直升飞机的只有美国和荷兰。
