Title: Czechoslovak and Polish Government Representatives Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-05-05
Page: 5

According to a Xinhua News Agency report: The Polish government delegation, headed by Cyrankiewicz, arrived in Prague on the 2nd, and began talks with the Czechoslovak government delegation, headed by Premier Široký, on the same day.

On the day the talks began, Premier Široký held a reception for the Polish government delegation. Široký and Cyrankiewicz spoke at the reception.

### 捷波两国政府代表开始会谈

1957-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    捷波两国政府代表开始会谈
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：以西伦凯维兹为首的波兰政府代表团，2日到达布拉格，当日便和以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团开始会谈。
    在开始会谈的这一天，西罗基总理曾经举行招待会，招待波兰政府代表团。西罗基和西伦凯维兹先后在招待会上讲了话。
