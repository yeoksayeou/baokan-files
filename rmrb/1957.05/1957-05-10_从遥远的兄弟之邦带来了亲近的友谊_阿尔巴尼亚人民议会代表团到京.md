Title: Bringing Close Friendship from a Distant Fraternal Country: Albanian People's Assembly Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The delegation of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, headed by Rita Marko, Chairman of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi spoke at the airport, welcoming the delegation to visit China. He said that the delegation's visit will strengthen the close unity and friendly cooperation between the people of China and Albania.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi said that the friendly cooperation and mutual exchanges between China and Albania profoundly embody the fraternal friendship and close unity in the socialist family.

Chairman Marko then spoke. He said that the long distance cannot become an obstacle to the closeness between the people of our two countries, because we are linked by a common road and common ideas, and we are linked by the lighthouse that illuminates the activities of our two parties and two governments—Marxism-Leninism.

Chairman Marko said that the Albanian people cherish the friendship with the Chinese people very much. He said: "We regard every achievement of yours as our own achievement, because it strengthens our common power."

The distinguished guests arrived in Beijing at 5:25 pm. After they got off the plane, they accepted flowers and shook hands cordially with the welcoming people.

After Chairman Marko spoke, accompanied by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, he went around the field to meet the welcoming masses. At this time, cheers erupted from the crowd, and warm applause rang out at the airport.

The national flags of China and Albania fluttered in the wind at the airport. More than 500 people were here to welcome the distinguished guests. Among them were Vice-Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, and Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and Bao Erhan. Among them were also members and deputy secretaries-general of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, heads of the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Beijing Municipal People's Committee and various people's organizations, as well as people from all walks of life who had visited Albania.

Albanian Ambassador to China Balili and his wife also came to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 9th: The Albanian People's Assembly delegation, at the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, left for China on the morning of the 8th for a visit.

The delegation consists of fourteen people, and the head of the delegation is Marko, Chairman of the Albanian People's Assembly.

### 从遥远的兄弟之邦带来了亲近的友谊  阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团到京

1957-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    从遥远的兄弟之邦带来了亲近的友谊
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　以阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席里塔·马尔科为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    刘少奇委员长在机场讲话，欢迎代表团来中国访问。他说，代表团的访问将加强中阿两国人民的紧密团结和友好合作。
    刘少奇委员长说，中阿两国间的友好合作和互相往来深刻地体现了社会主义大家庭中的兄弟友谊和亲密团结。
    马尔科主席接着讲话。他说，遥远的距离不能成为我们两国人民相亲近的障碍，因为联结着我们的有共同的道路、共同的思想，联结着我们的有照耀着我们两国党、两国政府的活动的灯塔——马克思列宁主义。
    马尔科主席说，阿尔巴尼亚人民非常珍视同中国人民的友谊。他说：“我们把你们的每一个成就也看作是我们自己的成就，因为它加强着我们共同的威力。”
    贵宾们是在下午五时二十五分到达北京的。他们走下飞机后，接受了献花，并同欢迎的人们亲切握手。
    马尔科主席讲话以后，由刘少奇委员长陪同，绕场一周，同欢迎的群众见面。这时，人群中发出了欢呼，机场上响起了热烈的掌声。
    机场上中阿两国国旗迎风招展。有五百多人在这里欢迎贵宾。他们中有林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通副委员长，李先念副总理、习仲勋秘书长，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧、包尔汉。他们中还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、副秘书长，最高人民法院、最高人民检察院、外交部、北京市人民委员会和各人民团体的负责人，以及访问过阿尔巴尼亚的各界人士。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里和夫人也到机场欢迎。
    新华社地拉那9日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团，应中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇的邀请，8日早晨动身前往中国访问。
    代表团由十四人组成，团长是阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席马尔科。（附图片）
