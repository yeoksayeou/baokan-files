Title: Soviet-German Consular Treaty Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic signed a consular treaty between the two countries in Moscow on May 10.

Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko signed the treaty on behalf of the Soviet Union, and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Dr. Bolz signed on behalf of the German Democratic Republic.

### 苏德签订领事条约

1957-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏德签订领事条约
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和民主德国5月10日在莫斯科签订了两国的领事条约。
    代表苏联方面在条约上签字的是苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，代表德意志民主共和国方面签字的是德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨博士。
