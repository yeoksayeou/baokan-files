Title: Albanian Parliamentary Delegation Visits Peking University
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Albanian People's Republic People's Assembly delegation, headed by Chairman Marko, visited Peking University this morning.

Currently, there are seven Albanian students studying Chinese at this university. President Ma Yinchu told Chairman Marko that these seven students are studying very well.

The guests visited the Biology Department, gymnasium, university history exhibition room, and library of Peking University. They also visited the dormitory of the Albanian students.

### 阿议会代表团参观北京大学

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    阿议会代表团参观北京大学
    据新华社13日讯　以马尔科主席为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团，今天上午参观了北京大学。
    在这个大学里，目前有七个阿尔巴尼亚的留学生在学习中文。马寅初校长告诉马尔科主席说，这七位留学生学习得很好。
    客人们参观了北京大学生物系、体育馆、校史展览室和图书馆，还参观了阿尔巴尼亚留学生的宿舍。
