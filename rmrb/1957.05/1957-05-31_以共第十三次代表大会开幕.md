Title: The Thirteenth Congress of the Israeli Communist Party Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-05-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, May 29th: According to TASS, the Thirteenth Congress of the Israeli Communist Party opened in Jaffa on May 29th. Three hundred and fifteen delegates are participating in this congress.

    Foreign guests who sent congratulatory messages to the congress included representatives from the Communist Parties of Britain, Bulgaria, and France. The congress also read out congratulatory telegrams from other fraternal Communist parties.

    The delegates warmly applauded the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the congress. The telegram from the CPSU Central Committee stated: "Guided by the great doctrine of Marxism-Leninism and the principles of proletarian internationalism, the Israeli Communist Party resolutely defends peace, unites all progressive forces in the country, smashes all conspiracies of the imperialist aggressors and their accomplices in the Near East, and strives for the unity of the working class and the vital interests of the people.
      "The CPSU Central Committee expresses its deep conviction that the Israeli Communist Party will continue to move forward unswervingly along this path and will achieve new successes in its work."

### 以共第十三次代表大会开幕

1957-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    以共第十三次代表大会开幕
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社特拉维夫29日讯：以色列共产党第十三次代表大会5月29日在雅法开幕。参加这次代表大会的有三百一十五位代表。
    向代表大会致贺词的外宾有英国、保加利亚和法国的共产党的代表。代表大会还宣读了其他兄弟共产党的贺电。
    大会代表们热烈鼓掌欢迎苏联共产党中央委员会给代表大会的贺电。苏共中央的贺电说：“以色列共产党以伟大的马克思列宁主义学说和无产阶级国际主义原则为指南，坚决捍卫和平，团结国内一切进步力量，粉碎帝国主义侵略者和他们在近东的帮凶们的各种阴谋，为争取工人阶级队伍的一致和人民的切身利益而斗争。
      “苏共中央表示深信，以色列共产党今后将依然一往直前地沿这条道路迈进，并且会在自己的工作中获得新的成就。”
