Title: South Korean Tragedy: Family of Seven Commit Suicide by Poison

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - Seoul News: South Korean police said today that in Busan, a 46-year-old unemployed man poisoned his wife and five children, and then committed suicide.

The only person left in this family that committed suicide by poisoning was the unemployed man's 69-year-old mother. The unemployed man wrote in a note to his mother: "I wanted you to leave this world with us, but I couldn't bring myself to do it."

### 南朝鲜的悲剧  一家七口服毒自杀

1957-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    南朝鲜的悲剧
    一家七口服毒自杀
    新华社平壤6日电　汉城消息：南朝鲜的警察今天说，在釜山有一个四十六岁的失业者用毒药杀死了他的妻子和五个孩子，并且自杀。
    在这个服毒自杀的家庭中，只剩下了这个失业者的六十九岁的老母。这个失业者在留给他母亲的一个纸条上写道：“我想要你和我们一起离开人间，但是我不忍下手。”
