Title: Anglo-Egyptian Talks First Stage Ends
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, May 29th: The Anglo-Egyptian financial and economic talks, which began in Rome on May 24, concluded their first stage of negotiations on the 28th. It is reported that during this stage, both sides presented their basic viewpoints without making any concessions. They will await new instructions from their respective governments before continuing the talks.

### 英埃会谈第一阶段结束

1957-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    英埃会谈第一阶段结束
    新华社29日讯　罗马消息：5月24日在罗马开始的英国和埃及的财政经济会谈，已经在28日结束了谈判的头一个阶段。据透露，在这一阶段里，双方都提出了它们的基本观点，而没有作一点让步。它们将等待各自的政府的新指示以后再继续举行会谈。
