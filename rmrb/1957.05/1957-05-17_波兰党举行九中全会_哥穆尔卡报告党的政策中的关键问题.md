Title: Polish Party Holds Ninth Plenary Session, Gomulka Reports on Key Issues in Party Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-05-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 16th - According to the Polish Press Agency on the 15th, the Ninth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party opened in Warsaw on the morning of May 15th.
    The plenary session adopted the following agenda: (1) "Key Issues in Party Policy" - Reporter: Gomulka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. (2) Report from the committee appointed by the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party - Reporter: Nowak, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Commission of the Party. (3) On the question of preparing for the Third National Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party. (4) Organizational matters.
    Subsequently, Gomulka gave a report on the first item on the agenda.

### 波兰党举行九中全会  哥穆尔卡报告党的政策中的关键问题

1957-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰党举行九中全会
    哥穆尔卡报告党的政策中的关键问题
    新华社华沙16日电　据波兰通讯社15日讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第九次全体会议5月15日上午在华沙开幕。
    全体会议通过议程如下：（一）“党的政策中的关键问题”——报告人：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡。（二）波兰统一工人党中央委员会第八次全体会议任命的委员会提出的报告——报告人：党中央监察委员会主席诺瓦克。（三）关于筹备召开波兰统一工人党第三次全国代表大会的问题。（四）组织问题。
    接着，哥穆尔卡就第一项议程作了报告。
