Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Advanced Meteorological Workers, Also Receive Graduates of the Academy of Educational Administration

Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairman Zhu De, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party received the representatives attending the National Conference of Advanced Meteorological Workers and the second graduating class of the Academy of Educational Administration this morning.

### 中共中央领导人接见气象先进工作者  同时接见教育行政学院毕业学生

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见气象先进工作者
    同时接见教育行政学院毕业学生
    新华社29日讯  中共中央主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、总书记邓小平今天上午先后接见了出席全国气象先进工作者代表会议的代表和教育行政学院第二期毕业学生。
