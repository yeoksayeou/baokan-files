Title: Albanian Parliamentary Delegation Visits Agricultural Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Albanian People's Republic People's Assembly delegation, headed by Chairman Marko, visited the Agricultural Exhibition today.

After the visit, all members of the delegation wrote inscriptions as souvenirs. In their inscriptions, the distinguished guests said that this agricultural exhibition is indeed a very good school for the people. They expressed their intention to convey the gains from this visit to the Albanian people, so that they can learn more from China.

The distinguished guests visited the exhibition for more than four hours.

Today, the distinguished guests also toured the Summer Palace.

### 阿议会代表团参观农业展览会

1957-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    阿议会代表团参观农业展览会
    据新华社11日讯　以马尔科主席为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团，今天参观了农业展览会。
    参观以后，代表团全体人员题词留念。贵宾们在题词中说，这个农业展览会确实是一座很好的人民的学校。他们表示要把这次参观的收获转达给阿尔巴尼亚人民，以便更多地向中国学习。
    贵宾们在这个展览会里共参观了四个多小时。
    今天，贵宾们还游览了颐和园。
