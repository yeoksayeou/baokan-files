Title: Soviet-Iranian Border Treaty Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-05-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th - TASS reports: The delegations of the Soviet Government and the Government of the Kingdom of Iran signed a treaty in Moscow on the 14th regarding the Soviet-Iranian border and the resolution of border conflicts and incidents.
    This treaty, aimed at preventing border incidents, stipulates regulations for the Soviet-Iranian border defense organizations, and also specifies the powers of the border authorities, their locations, and the areas of activity of the border plenipotentiaries.

### 苏伊签订边界条约

1957-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏伊签订边界条约
    新华社15日讯据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府和伊朗王国政府代表团14日在莫斯科签订了关于苏伊边界和解决边界冲突及事件的条约。
    这项目的在于防止发生边界事件的条约，规定了苏伊国境防卫组织条例，并且对边界当局的权限和它们驻在的地址以及边界全权代表的活动地区等问题都作了规定。
