Title: Grand Celebrations Held by the Peoples of Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-02
Page: 3

    North Korean, Vietnamese and Mongolian Peoples Hold Grand Celebrations
    This newspaper's comprehensive report  According to Xinhua News Agency reports, grand celebrations were held in Pyongyang, Hanoi, and Ulaanbaatar and other places on the "May Day" International Labor Day.
    On the 1st, more than 300,000 people in Pyongyang held a rally and parade to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. At 9:00 a.m., Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Kim Doo-bong and other leaders of the Korean Party and government ascended the rostrum amidst cheers from the masses. The mass parade began at 9:20 a.m.
    In Hanoi, 90,000 working people held a grand rally and parade in Ba Dinh Square on the 1st to celebrate "May Day" Labor Day. President Ho Chi Minh read out the May Day appeal to the cheers of the audience. He pointed out that the most important task for the people of Vietnam this year is to concentrate their efforts on completing the 1957 national plan.
    In Ulaanbaatar, more than 30,000 working people gathered in Sukhbaatar Square at 10:00 a.m. on the 1st to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. Damdin, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and First Secretary of the Ulaanbaatar Municipal Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, said at the meeting that the Mongolian people are welcoming this festival by completing the construction tasks of the Second Five-Year Plan.

### 朝越蒙三国人民举行盛会

1957-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    朝越蒙三国人民举行盛会
    本报综合报道  据新华社消息，在“五一”国际劳动节，平壤、河内和乌兰巴托等地举行了盛大的庆祝。
    1日平壤三十多万人举行集会游行，欢庆“五一”国际劳动节。上午九时，金日成首相、金枓奉委员长等朝鲜党和政府的领导人在群众的欢呼声中登上主席台。九时二十分开始群众游行。
    河内九万劳动人民1日在巴亭广场举行盛大集会和游行庆祝“五一”劳动节。胡志明主席在全场欢呼声中宣读五一号召书。他指出，今年越南全国人民最重要的任务是集中力量完成1957年国家计划。
    乌兰巴托市三万多劳动人民1日上午十时在苏赫—巴特尔广场集会庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员、乌兰巴托市委员会第一书记达姆丁在会上讲话说，蒙古人民正在以完成第二个五年计划的建设任务来迎接这个节日。
