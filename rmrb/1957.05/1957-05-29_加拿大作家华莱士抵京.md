Title: Canadian Writer Wallace Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Canadian progressive writer, Joe Wallace, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 加拿大作家华莱士抵京

1957-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    加拿大作家华莱士抵京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，来中国访问的加拿大进步作家乔埃·华莱士，在今日下午乘火车抵京。
