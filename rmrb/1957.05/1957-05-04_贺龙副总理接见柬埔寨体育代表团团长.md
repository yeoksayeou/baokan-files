Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Head of Cambodian Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd. Vice Premier of the State Council He Long received the leading members of the Cambodian sports delegation this morning, headed by Mr. Shu E, head of the Cambodian sports delegation and Chairman of the Cambodian Sports Commission.

### 贺龙副总理接见柬埔寨体育代表团团长

1957-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见柬埔寨体育代表团团长
    新华社3日讯　国务院副总理贺龙今天上午接见了以柬埔寨体育代表团团长、柬埔寨体委主席舒厄为首的柬埔寨体育代表团的领导人员。
