    A Surgeon Performs Appendectomy on Himself
    Dr. Sadao Omura, a surgeon at Shizuoka Hospital in Shizuoka City, Japan, removed his own appendix on May 8th. The entire operation took thirty-five minutes, and his recovery is progressing well. He claims to be the first surgeon in the world to perform an appendectomy on himself.

### 外科大夫自割盲肠

1957-05-25
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    外科大夫自割盲肠
    日本静冈市静冈医院的医生大村贞夫，5月8日自己割去了盲肠，手术整个过程花了三十五分钟，经过情况良好。他自称是世界上第一个自割盲肠的外科医生。
