Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, People of All Nationalities in Inner Mongolia Hold Celebration Rally; Li Xiannian Delivers Congratulatory Speech on Behalf of the CPC Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 30th. The celebration rally for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region was held in Hohhot this afternoon.

Among those attending the rally were Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and representatives of 19 nationalities from various provinces and autonomous regions across the country. Key leaders from the Party, government, military, and mass organizations of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region also attended the rally.

Also attending the celebration rally were the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic and all members of the art and sports delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, as well as all personnel of the Consulate General of the Mongolian People's Republic in Hohhot.

Soviet experts and Mongolian experts working in Inner Mongolia also attended the rally.

Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, delivered a speech first, extending a warm welcome and respect to guests from home and abroad on behalf of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the people of all nationalities in the autonomous region. Ulanhu said that the Mongolian nationality in Inner Mongolia is now transitioning from an economically and culturally backward nation to a socialist modern nation. In reviewing the path taken by the people of Inner Mongolia, he pointed out that the important conditions for the victory of the Mongolian people's revolution and the great achievements in socialist construction in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are: the correct leadership of the Party; the unity and mutual assistance of all nationalities inside and outside the region; the consolidation and development of the people's democratic united front; the formulation of specific policies and working methods based on the characteristics of the autonomous region and the region’s characteristics under the guidance of the state's unified guidelines and policies; and the training of Mongolian and other ethnic minority cadres.

Ulanhu concluded by saying that in the future, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region should earnestly study the instructions of the Central Government, learn from the advanced experience of socialist construction in the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries, learn from the advanced experience of fraternal provinces and regions, and combine these advanced experiences with the actual situation of the autonomous region.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian extended congratulations to the Mongolian nationality and the people of all nationalities in the autonomous region on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao. He said that the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is the earliest established, largest, and fastest-growing ethnic autonomous area in our country, and has played and will continue to play an important role in the cause of the unity of our great motherland and the unity of all nationalities. He expressed the hope that the people of all nationalities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will unite as one, be modest and prudent, carry forward the spirit of building the country through diligence and thrift, build a socialist Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and become a good example for the construction and development of ethnic autonomous areas in our country.

Chimiddorjyn Sürenjav, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, then spoke at the meeting. On behalf of the entire people and government of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, he extended fraternal congratulations to all participants in the celebration rally and all working people of Inner Mongolia. He said that the people of the Mongolian People's Republic and the people of Inner Mongolia have always been of the same nationality, and since ancient times, they have been brothers who share joys and sorrows and are flesh and blood. After outlining the great achievements made by the people of the Mongolian People's Republic in the development of the national economy and culture since liberation, he said that now we are living a life of freedom and equality in the socialist family, working together for socialist advancement, which gives the people of the Mongolian People's Republic infinite encouragement.

Following him, the following people spoke at the meeting: Zhou Jianren, Vice Minister of Higher Education; Iminov (Uyghur), Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region; Soikon Wangqing Geler (Tibetan), member of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region; Xirao Jiacuo (Tibetan), Vice Governor of Qinghai Province; Zhu Dehai (Korean), Vice Governor of Jilin Province; Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan), Vice Governor of Gansu Province; Ma Fuchen (Hui), Vice Governor of Qinghai Province; Tian Fuda (Gaoshan), member of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee; Xu Bukan, Deputy Minister of the Organization Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhao Lequn (Zhuang), Deputy Governor of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, Guangxi Province; Yang Daiti (Yi), Deputy Governor of the Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province; Zhaocunxin (Dai), Governor of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province; Chen Side (Miao), Deputy Governor of the Hainan Li and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, Guangdong Province; Hu Zhonghua (Kawa), Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Prefecture, Yunnan Province; Ayingga (Mongolian), Deputy Director of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of Liaoning Province; Wang Jiaxian (Buyi), Director of the Women's Federation of the Qian Southeast Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, Guizhou Province. In their speeches, they all congratulated the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region on the great achievements it has made in the past ten years.

After the speeches, the staff of the meeting read out congratulatory telegrams and messages from the Central Government and various autonomous regions and provinces.

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 30th. Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, held a banquet tonight to celebrate "May Day" and the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and others, and representatives of nineteen nationalities, all attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, all members of the art and sports delegation, Soviet experts and Mongolian experts working in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and their families, who were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 庆祝内蒙古自治区成立十周年  内蒙古各族人民举行庆祝大会  李先念代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席致词祝贺

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝内蒙古自治区成立十周年
    内蒙古各族人民举行庆祝大会
    李先念代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席致词祝贺
    新华社呼和浩特30日电  内蒙古自治区成立十周年庆祝大会今天下午在呼和浩特举行。
    参加大会的有国务院副总理李先念等人和来自全国各省、自治区十九个民族的代表。内蒙古自治区党政军群众团体主要负责人都参加了大会。
    参加庆祝大会的还有蒙古人民共和国政府代表团和蒙古人民共和国艺术体育团全体人员，蒙古人民共和国驻呼和浩特总领事馆全体人员。
    在内蒙古工作的苏联专家、蒙古专家们也参加了大会。
    内蒙古自治区主席乌兰夫首先致词，他代表内蒙古自治区人民委员会和自治区各族人民，向国内外来宾表示热烈的欢迎和敬意。乌兰夫说，内蒙古蒙古民族现在正由一个经济文化落后的民族向着社会主义的现代民族过渡。他在回顾内蒙古人民走过的道路时指出，蒙古人民革命取得胜利和内蒙古自治区社会主义建设取得伟大成就的重要条件是：党的正确领导；区内外各民族的团结互助；人民民主统一战线的巩固和发展；在国家统一的方针政策指导下，根据自治区的民族特点和地区特点规定具体政策和工作方法；培养蒙古族和各民族干部。
    乌兰夫最后说，今后内蒙古自治区要很好地学习中央的指示，学习苏联和其他兄弟国家社会主义建设的先进经验，学习兄弟省、区的先进经验，并且把这些先进经验与自治区实际结合起来。
    国务院副总理李先念代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛泽东主席向蒙古民族和自治区内各民族人民祝贺。他说，内蒙古自治区是我国建立最早、规模最大、发展很快的民族自治地方，在我们伟大祖国的统一和各民族团结的事业中曾经起过并将继续发挥重要的作用。他希望内蒙古自治区各族人民团结一致，谦虚谨慎，发扬勤俭建国的精神，建成社会主义的内蒙古自治区，成为我国各民族自治地方建设及发展的良好榜样。
    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长齐米德道尔吉·苏伦扎布接着在会上讲话。他代表蒙古人民共和国全体人民和政府及蒙古人民革命党中央委员会向参加这次庆祝大会的全体人员和内蒙古全体劳动人民致以兄弟般的祝贺。他说，蒙古人民共和国人民与内蒙古人民从来就是同一民族，并自古以来就是同甘共苦，骨肉相连的兄弟。他概述了蒙古人民共和国人民自解放以后在发展民族的经济、文化方面所取得的巨大成绩以后说，现在我们在社会主义大家庭中过着自由平等的生活，共同为建设社会主义前进，这给蒙古人民共和国人民以无限的鼓舞。
    接着在大会上讲话的有：高等教育部副部长周建人、新疆维吾尔自治区副主席伊敏诺夫（维吾尔族）、西藏自治区筹备委员会委员索康·旺清格来（藏族）、青海省副省长喜饶嘉错（藏族）、吉林省副省长朱德海（朝鲜族）、甘肃省副省长黄正清（藏族）、青海省副省长马辅臣（回族）、全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员田富达（高山族）、中华全国总工会组织部副部长徐步宽、广西省桂西僮族自治州副州长赵乐群（僮族）、四川省凉山彝族自治州副州长杨代蒂（彝族）、云南省西双版纳傣族自治州州长召存信（傣族）、广东省海南黎族苗族自治州副州长陈斯德（苗族）、云南省临沧专署副专员胡忠华（佧佤族）、辽宁省民族事务委员会副主任阿英嘎（蒙古族）、贵州省黔东南布依族苗族自治州妇联主任王家先（布依族）。他们在讲话中都祝贺内蒙古自治区十年来所取得的巨大成就。
    讲话完毕，大会工作人员宣读了中央和各自治区、省发来的贺电和贺词。
    新华社呼和浩特30日电  内蒙古自治区主席乌兰夫今天晚上举行宴会，庆祝“五一”和内蒙古自治区成立十周年。
    国务院副总理李先念等人和十九个民族的代表，都参加了宴会。
    参加宴会的还有蒙古人民共和国政府代表团、艺术体育团全体人员，在内蒙古自治区工作的苏联专家、蒙古专家和他们的家属，也应邀参加了宴会。
