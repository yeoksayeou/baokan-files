Title: 100,000 Italian Postal and Telegraph Workers Strike

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 6th. Italian national postal and telegraph workers, numbering 100,000, began a 72-hour strike from midnight on the 5th, demanding wage increases. This is the third strike by postal workers this year.
    The strike was jointly called for by three trade union organizations: the Italian General Confederation of Labor, the Christian Democratic "Italian Confederation of Workers' Trade Unions," and the Social Democratic "Italian Labor Union."

### 意大利十万邮电工人罢工

1957-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利十万邮电工人罢工
    新华社6日讯　罗马消息：意大利全国十万邮电工人从5日午夜起举行七十二小时大罢工，要求增加工资。这是今年以来邮政工人举行的第三次罢工。
    这次罢工是在意大利总工会、基督教民主党的“意大利劳动人民工会联合会”和社会民主党的“意大利劳工联盟”三个工会组织联合号召之下举行的。
