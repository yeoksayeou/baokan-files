Title: Zheng Zhenduo Hosts Reception to Bid Farewell to Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and Qian Weichang, Vice President, hosted a reception at the Beijing International Club tonight to bid farewell to the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation that came to Beijing to participate in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association.
　　At the reception, Zheng Zhenduo paid tribute to the distinguished guests who had come from afar. Dr. Dala, head of the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, also gave a speech to express his gratitude to the hosts.
　　Dr. Dala will return home by plane via Kunming on the 18th. Four members of the delegation will leave Beijing on the 19th for a visit to Northeast China.

### 郑振铎举行酒会欢送缅中友协代表团

1957-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    郑振铎举行酒会欢送缅中友协代表团
    据新华社17日讯　中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长今晚在北京国际俱乐部举行酒会，欢送来北京参加中缅友好协会成立五周年庆祝活动的缅中友协代表团。
    郑振铎在酒会上向远道而来的贵宾们致敬。缅中友协代表团团长达拉博士也致词向主人表示谢意。
    达拉博士将在18日乘飞机转道昆明返国。四位团员将在19日离北京赴东北参观访问。
