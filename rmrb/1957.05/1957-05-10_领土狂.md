Date: 1957-05-10

    Territory Mania
    An American science magazine recently advocated that a U.S. flag be carried to the moon by rocket, thereby declaring the planet to be U.S. territory.
    This American magazine said that this is the first step in realizing President Eisenhower's plan for the international control of outer space.

### 领土狂

1957-05-10
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    领土狂
    美国一家科学杂志最近主张由火箭携带一面美国国旗到月球上去，借此宣布这个星球为美国的领土。
    这家美国杂志说，这是实现艾森豪威尔总统国际管制宇宙空间计划的第一步。
