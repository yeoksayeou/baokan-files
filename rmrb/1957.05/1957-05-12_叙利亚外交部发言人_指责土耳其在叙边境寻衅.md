Title: Syrian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Accuses Turkey of Provocations on Syrian Border
Author:
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - According to a report in Damascus' "Victory Newspaper" on the 10th: A spokesman for the Syrian Foreign Ministry said that the Syrian government believes that the concentration of Turkish troops in the western section of the Syrian-Turkish border "is a hostile act against Syria, with the aim of worsening relations between the two countries."

The spokesman said: The concentration of Turkish troops is a confirmed fact. The Syrian government attaches great importance to this issue and has been in contact with the Turkish authorities.

### 叙利亚外交部发言人  指责土耳其在叙边境寻衅

1957-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚外交部发言人
    指责土耳其在叙边境寻衅
    新华社11日讯　据大马士革“胜利报”10日报道：叙利亚外交部发言人说，叙利亚政府认为，土耳其军队在叙土边境西段的结集“是对叙利亚的敌对行动，目的是想使两国的关系恶化”。
    发言人说：土耳其军队的结集是确实的实际情况。叙利亚政府十分重视这个问题，并且已同土耳其当局进行交涉。
