Title: Japanese Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-05-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 29th - At the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, the Japanese medical delegation of fifteen members (two have not yet arrived), led by the head of the delegation, Yoshio Domori, arrived in Beijing by plane today to carry out the friendly agreement between the medical communities of China and Japan for a visit to China.

### 日医学代表团到京

1957-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    日医学代表团到京
    据新华社29日讯　应中华医学会邀请，为执行中日两国医学界友好协议来中国访问的日本医学代表团一行十五人（二人尚未到）在团长堂森芳夫率领下，今天乘机抵京。
