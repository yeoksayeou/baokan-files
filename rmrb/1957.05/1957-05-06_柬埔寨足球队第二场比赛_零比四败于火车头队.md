Title: Cambodian Football Team Loses Second Match to Locomotive Team 0-4
Author:
Date: 1957-05-06
Page: 2
    Sports News

    Cambodian Football Team Loses Second Match to Locomotive Team 0-4

    This newspaper reports that the Cambodian national football team played a friendly match against the Chinese Locomotive Sports Association football team yesterday afternoon at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing. The result of the match was a 4-0 victory for the Locomotive team.

    The Locomotive team scored one goal in the first half and three goals in the second half, with the final goal being scored one minute before the end of the match. The visiting team's goalkeeper, Yang De, saved many dangerous shots during the match.

    The match was refereed by Mr. Ying Libing, Chairman of the Referee Committee of the Cambodian Football Federation and an international football referee. His correct and decisive judgments were well received by the audience.

### 柬埔寨足球队第二场比赛  零比四败于火车头队

1957-05-06
第2版()
专栏：体育新闻

    柬埔寨足球队第二场比赛
    零比四败于火车头队
    本报讯　柬埔寨王国足球队昨日下午在北京先农坛体育场，对中国火车头体育协会足球队举行了一场友谊比赛，比赛结果，火车头队以四比零胜。
    火车头队在上半场射进一球，下半场射进三个球，最后一球是在终场前一分钟射进的。客队的守门员杨德在比赛中曾扑救了不少险球。
    这场比赛是由柬埔寨足球联合会裁判委员会主席、国际足球裁判英利炳担任裁判，他正确、果断的判断得到了观众的好评。
