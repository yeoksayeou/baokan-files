Title: American Soldier Robs at Gunpoint in Osaka; Justice Ministry Trade Union Demands US Hand Over Murderer
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 6

    American Soldier Robs at Gunpoint in Osaka
    Justice Ministry Trade Union Demands US Hand Over Murderer
    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th, Tokyo Dispatch: The Osaka Prefectural Police Headquarters today accused an American soldier from the US military's Itami base of robbing a Japanese citizen and illegally possessing a weapon.
    According to police investigations, an American Air Force soldier named Bryant threatened a Japanese taxi driver in Osaka with a gun on March 22nd, robbing him of 1,300 yen.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 29th, Tokyo Dispatch: The Japan National Justice Ministry Employees' Union decided yesterday to submit a written protest to the US State Department regarding the Girard case, and demanded that the US authorities hand over the American soldier Girard who shot and killed a Japanese woman.

### 美国兵在大阪持枪行劫  日司法部职员工会要美国交出杀人犯

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美国兵在大阪持枪行劫
    日司法部职员工会要美国交出杀人犯
    据新华社28日讯　东京消息：大阪地方警察厅今天控诉美军伊丹基地的一个美国大兵抢劫日本人，并且非法持有武器。
    根据警察当局调查，这个名叫布里安特的美国空军士兵曾经在3月22日在大阪持枪威胁一个出租汽车的日本司机，劫走了这个司机的一千三百日元。
    据新华社29日讯　东京消息：日本全国司法部职员工会昨天决定就哲腊德事件向美国国务院提出书面抗议，并要求美国有关当局交出枪杀日本妇女的美国兵哲腊德。
