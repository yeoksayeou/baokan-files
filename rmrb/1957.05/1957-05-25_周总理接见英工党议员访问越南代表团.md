Title: Premier Zhou Receives British Labour Party Delegation Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the head of the British Labour Party delegation visiting Vietnam, Harold Davies, and members William Warbey, Mrs. Lena Jeger, and the delegation's secretary, Betty Buttrey. Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, and Luo Qing, Deputy Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 23rd after visiting Vietnam and will soon return to Britain.

### 周总理接见英工党议员访问越南代表团

1957-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英工党议员访问越南代表团
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了英国工党议员访问越南代表团团长哈罗德·戴维斯，团员威廉·华贝、莱娜·杰格夫人和代表团的秘书贝蒂·布特雷德。接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，外交部西欧司副司长罗清。
    代表团是在访问越南后在23日到达北京的，他们即将回到英国去。
