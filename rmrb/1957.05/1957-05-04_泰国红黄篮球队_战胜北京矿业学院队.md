Title: Thailand Red and Yellow Basketball Team Defeats Beijing Mining Institute Team
Author:
Date: 1957-05-04
Page: 2
Sports News

    Thailand Red and Yellow Basketball Team
    Defeats Beijing Mining Institute Team
    (Our Newspaper) News: On the evening of the 3rd, the Thailand Red and Yellow Men's Basketball Team defeated the Beijing Mining Institute Team with a score of 68 to 58 in their second match in Beijing.
    On the evening of the 2nd, in their first match in Beijing, the Thailand Red and Yellow Basketball Team lost to the Beijing team with a score of 49 to 158.

### 泰国红黄篮球队  战胜北京矿业学院队

1957-05-04
第2版()
专栏：体育新闻

    泰国红黄篮球队
    战胜北京矿业学院队
    本报讯　3日晚上，泰国红黄男子篮球队在北京的第二场比赛中，以六十八比五十八战胜了北京矿业学院队。
    2日晚，泰国红黄篮球队在到北京的第一场比赛中，以四十九比一百五十八败于北京队。
