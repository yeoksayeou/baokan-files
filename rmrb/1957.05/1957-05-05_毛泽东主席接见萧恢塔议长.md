Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Speaker Sao Shwe Thaik
Author:
Date: 1957-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th – Chairman Mao Zedong received Sao Shwe Thaik, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma, and his wife, along with their children, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairmen Lin Boqu and Li Jishen, as well as Zhang Su, Yu Xinqing, Liu Guanyi, and Cheng Zhiping. Wu Ba Mao, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, was also present.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th – Sao Shwe Thaik, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma, and his wife, along with their children, left Beijing by train this evening to visit various places in Northeast China. (See picture)

### 毛泽东主席接见萧恢塔议长

1957-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见萧恢塔议长
    新华社4日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了缅甸联邦民族院议长萧恢塔夫妇以及他们的子女等。
    接见的时候，在座的有林伯渠、李济深副委员长和张苏、余心清、刘贯一、程之平。接见的时候，缅甸驻中国大使馆临时代办吴巴茂也在座。
    据新华社4日讯　缅甸联邦民族院议长萧恢塔夫妇和他们的子女等在今晚乘火车离开北京，前往东北各地参观访问。（附图片）
