Title: Belgian Ministry of Economic Affairs Office Director Van Offelen Hosts Banquet for Chinese Economic Figures
Author:
Date: 1957-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd—Van Offelen, Director of the Office of the Belgian Ministry of Economic Affairs, and his wife, who are visiting our country, held a banquet in Beijing today to entertain well-known figures from Chinese international trade, commerce, finance, economic scholars, and other circles.

Baron Arnauld, Chairman of the Belgian International Trade Promotion Committee, who is also visiting Beijing, attended today's banquet.

Van Offelen is visiting China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, leading a delegation of Belgian economic scholars.

### 比经济部办公厅主任奥弗伦  设宴招待我经济界人士

1957-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    比经济部办公厅主任奥弗伦
    设宴招待我经济界人士
    据新华社3日讯　来我国访问的比利时经济部办公厅主任范·奥弗伦和夫人今天在北京举行宴会，招待我国国际贸易、商业、金融、经济学者等各界著名人士。
    正在北京访问的比利时国际贸易促进委员会主席阿纳尔男爵等出席了今天的宴会。
    范·奥弗伦是应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请，率领比利时经济学者访华代表团来我国访问的。
