Title: Albanian Parliamentary Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th - The People's Assembly Delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, headed by Chairman Marko, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon from Shenyang. The distinguished guests visited Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan and other places successively, and were warmly welcomed by the people of various places.

### 阿议会代表团回京

1957-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    阿议会代表团回京
    据新华社27日讯　由马尔科主席为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团今天下午从沈阳乘飞机返回北京。贵宾们先后访问了武汉、广州、杭州、上海、南京、天津、沈阳、鞍山等地，受到了各地人民热烈欢迎。
