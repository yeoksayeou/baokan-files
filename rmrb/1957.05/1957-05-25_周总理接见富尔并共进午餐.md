Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Edgar Faure and Has Lunch Together

Author:

Date: 1957-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with former French Prime Minister Edgar Faure and his wife this afternoon and had lunch with the guests. Accompanying the meeting and lunch were Zhang Xiruo and his wife, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Zhixiang, Qiao Guanhua, Gong Peng, Huang Hua, and Wu Maosun. (Attached picture)
Premier Zhou Enlai talks with Faure. From left: Zhang Xiruo, Mrs. Faure, Faure, Zhou Enlai. Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin photo

### 周总理接见富尔并共进午餐

1957-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见富尔并共进午餐
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了法国前总理富尔和夫人，并且同客人共进午餐。陪同接见和进餐的，有张奚若和夫人、叶季壮、张致祥、乔冠华、龚澎、黄华、吴茂荪。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和富尔谈话。左起：张奚若、富尔夫人、富尔、周恩来。　新华社记者　吕厚民摄
