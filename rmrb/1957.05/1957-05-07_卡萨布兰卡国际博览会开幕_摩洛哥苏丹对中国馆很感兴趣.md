Title: Casablanca International Exposition Opens; Moroccan Sultan Very Interested in Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th: A message from Casablanca states that the annual Casablanca International Exposition opened on the morning of the 4th, with twenty-five countries participating. Moroccan Sultan Yusuf cut the ribbon for the exposition.

Following the opening ceremony, the Moroccan Sultan, Prime Minister, ministers, and diplomatic envoys from many countries, accompanied by the chairman of the exposition, first visited the Chinese Pavilion. They were warmly welcomed by Wen Shizhen, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, and all the staff. The Sultan and others first visited the heavy industry section of the Chinese Pavilion, where they attentively watched the machine operation demonstrations. The Sultan expressed great interest in the finely printed Chinese Quran, tea, silk, and handicrafts, and asked several questions while viewing the models of water conservancy, transportation projects, and the blast furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Works.

On the same afternoon, the Moroccan Sultan received the heads of the exhibition delegations from various countries participating in the exposition at the palace. During the reception, Wen Shizhen, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, presented the Sultan with copies of the Chinese-published "Quran" and "Life of Chinese Muslims," as well as handicrafts.

### 卡萨布兰卡国际博览会开幕  摩洛哥苏丹对中国馆很感兴趣

1957-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    卡萨布兰卡国际博览会开幕
    摩洛哥苏丹对中国馆很感兴趣
    新华社布拉格6日电　卡萨布兰卡消息：一年一度的卡萨布兰卡国际博览会在4日上午开幕，参加这个博览会的有二十五个国家。摩洛哥苏丹优素福为博览会剪彩。
    举行开幕仪式以后，摩洛哥苏丹、首相、大臣们和许多国家的外交使节在博览会主席陪同下，首先参观中国馆，受到中国展览团团长文士桢和全体工作人员的热烈欢迎。苏丹等在中国馆首先参观了重工业馆，在那里注意地观看了机器操纵表演。苏丹对中国精印的可兰经、茶叶、丝绸和手工艺品表现了很大的兴趣，并且在观看关于水利、交通工程和鞍钢的高炉模型时，提出一些问题。
    在同一天下午，摩洛哥苏丹在皇宫接见了参加博览会的各国展览团的负责人。接见时，中国展览团团长文士桢向苏丹赠送了中国出版的“可兰经”和“中国穆斯林生活”各一册以及手工艺品等礼品。
