Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hosts a Peking Opera Performance for Distinguished Guests from Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held a Peking Opera soiree tonight to entertain the delegation from the Grand National Assembly of Romania and the Bucharest City People's Council, and the delegation from the People's Assembly of Albania.
    The distinguished guests enjoyed "Wild Boar Forest" performed by Yuan Shihai, Li Huiliang, and Du Jinfang.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会  请罗马尼亚贵宾看京剧

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会
    请罗马尼亚贵宾看京剧
    据新华社13日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天晚上举行京剧晚会，招待罗马尼亚大国民议会和布加勒斯特市人民会议代表团和阿尔巴尼亚人民议会代表团。
    贵宾们欣赏了由袁世海、厉慧良、杜近芳演出的“野猪林”。
