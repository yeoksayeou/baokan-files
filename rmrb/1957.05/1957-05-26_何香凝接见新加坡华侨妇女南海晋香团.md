Title: He Xiangning Receives Singapore Overseas Chinese Women's Pilgrimage Group to Nanhai

Author:

Date: 1957-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, received the Singapore Overseas Chinese Women's Pilgrimage Group to Nanhai at her home this morning. He Xiangning said to Wang Bilian, the head of the pilgrimage group, "If you need anything, we will do our best to assist you." He Xiangning repeatedly expressed her welcome for them to come again next time.

The Singapore Overseas Chinese Women's Pilgrimage Group to Nanhai arrived in Beijing on the 21st. In recent days, they have visited famous historical sites such as the Temple of Heaven, Beihai Park, and the Summer Palace. Yesterday morning, they went to Biyun Temple in the Western Hills to pay homage. This afternoon, the pilgrimage group visited the Chinese Buddhist Academy and worshipped Buddha in the various halls of Guangji Temple.

### 何香凝接见新加坡华侨妇女南海晋香团

1957-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    何香凝接见新加坡华侨妇女南海晋香团
    新华社25日讯　华侨事务委员会主任何香凝今天上午在她的家里接见了新加坡华侨妇女南海晋香团。何香凝对晋香团团长王碧莲说，你们需要什么，我们一定尽力协助。何香凝一再表示欢迎她们下次再来。
    新加坡华侨妇女南海晋香团是在21日到达北京的。连日来她们游览了天坛、北海、颐和园等名胜古迹。昨天上午，到西山碧云寺朝拜晋香。今天下午，晋香团参观了中国佛学院，并到广济寺各殿拜佛。
