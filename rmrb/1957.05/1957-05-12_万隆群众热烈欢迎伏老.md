Title: Bandung Masses Warmly Welcome Voroshilov
Author:
Date: 1957-05-12
Page: 5

    Bandung Masses Warmly Welcome Voroshilov
    (Comprehensive report from our newspaper) According to Xinhua News Agency: Chairman Voroshilov, accompanied by President Sukarno, arrived in Bandung on the 10th. The two heads of state rode in an open car through the city of Bandung to the West Java Provincial Commissioner's Office. Hundreds of thousands of people lined the streets, warmly cheering them. People constantly shouted, "Long live Indonesian-Soviet friendship!" "Long live peace!" Many people came from nearby areas. Chairman Voroshilov spoke briefly to the crowd gathered in front of the Provincial Commissioner's Office. He said that the Soviet people have long been familiar with Bandung. Because representatives of Asian and African countries had met here and made significant contributions to humanity.
    Sukarno also told the crowd that the people of Bandung love Chairman Voroshilov because Chairman Voroshilov is a representative of the Soviet people, and because the Soviet Union is a good friend of the Indonesian nation and people.
    At noon on the 11th, Chairman Voroshilov arrived in Yogyakarta, the capital of Indonesia during the post-war resistance against Dutch imperialism.
    Voroshilov laid flowers at the martyrs' cemetery and paid silent tribute at the tomb of General Sudirman, a national hero of Indonesia.

### 万隆群众热烈欢迎伏老

1957-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    万隆群众热烈欢迎伏老
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：伏罗希洛夫主席10日下午在苏加诺总统的陪同下到达万隆。两位元首坐着敞篷汽车经过万隆市区到西爪哇省公署。街道两旁有几十万人民向他们热烈欢呼。人们不断高呼：“印度尼西亚苏联友谊万岁！”“和平万岁！”许多人是从附近各地赶来的。伏罗希洛夫主席临时在省公署前向聚集在那里的群众讲话。他说，苏联人民早已熟知万隆这个地方了。因为亚非各国的代表曾经在这里开会，对人类作了重大的贡献。
    苏加诺也向群众说，万隆人民热爱伏罗希洛夫主席，因为伏罗希洛夫主席是苏联人民的代表，因为苏联是印度尼西亚国家和人民的好朋友。
    11日中午，伏罗希洛夫主席来到战后印度尼西亚反抗荷兰帝国主义侵略时期的首都日惹。
    伏罗希洛夫到烈士墓献了花，并且在印度尼西亚民族英雄苏基曼将军墓前默念致敬。
