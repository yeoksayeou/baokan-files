Title: Discussion on Further Improving Industrial Management: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Convenes Today
Author:
Date: 1957-05-07
Page: 5

Moscow Dispatch from our newspaper, May 6th – The seventh session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union will begin on the 7th in the Kremlin. This session has received special attention because it will hear Khrushchev’s report "On Further Improving the Organization of Management of Industry and Construction" and will discuss and decide on this major issue concerning the reform of the economic management system. This issue has been the subject of a nationwide discussion in the Soviet Union for more than a month. People participated in the discussion with unprecedented enthusiasm. In the Tatar Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic alone (with a population of approximately three million), 200,000 workers participated in the discussion, and more than 10,000 of them put forward constructive and specific suggestions. Now the nationwide discussion has ended, and the people's opinions are concentrated on the Soviet session. It is expected that there will be a lively discussion at this meeting, and then a decision will be made.

### 讨论进一步改进工业管理的问题  苏联最高苏维埃会议今天开幕

1957-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论进一步改进工业管理的问题
    苏联最高苏维埃会议今天开幕
    本报莫斯科6日电　苏联最高苏维埃第四届第七次会议将于7日在克里姆林宫开始举行。这次会议受到人们的特别重视，因为它将听取赫鲁晓夫“关于进一步改进管理工业和建设的组织工作”的报告，将讨论和决定这个关系经济管理制度的改革的重大问题。这个问题已经在苏联各地进行了一个多月的全民大讨论。人们空前热烈地参加了讨论。仅鞑靼自治共和国（它的人口约三百万人）就有二十万劳动者参加了讨论，其中一万多人提出了建设性的具体建议。现在全民讨论结束了，人民的意见集中到苏维埃会议上去。预计在这次会议上将进行热烈的讨论，然后作出决定。
