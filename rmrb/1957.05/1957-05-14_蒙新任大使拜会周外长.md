Title: Mongolian New Ambassador Visits Foreign Minister Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Sunom Lubsan, paid a visit to our Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 蒙新任大使拜会周外长

1957-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙新任大使拜会周外长
    新华社13日讯　蒙古人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使苏诺姆·鲁布桑今天下午拜会我外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。
    拜会时在座的，有外交部亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司司长王倬如。
