Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference

Author: 
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2

    Telegram of Congratulations from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, we extend our warm congratulations to you and the people of all ethnic groups in Inner Mongolia. Over the past ten years, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has implemented the ethnic policies advocated by the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong. Under the correct leadership of the Central People's Government, the autonomous region has achieved great success in its political, economic, and cultural work, playing a brilliant role in consolidating our country's people's democratic united front and the unity of the national family. We sincerely hope that you will further strengthen the fraternal unity of the people of all ethnic groups in Inner Mongolia and make greater contributions to our country's great socialist construction cause.
    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    April 27, 1957

### 政协全国委员会贺电

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会贺电
    内蒙古自治区人民委员会：
    当内蒙古自治区成立十周年的时候，我们谨向你们和内蒙古各民族人民致以热烈的祝贺。十年来，内蒙古自治区执行了中国共产党和毛泽东主席提倡的民族政策，在中央人民政府正确领导下，自治区的政治、经济和文化的各方面的工作都获得了巨大的成绩，对巩固我国人民民主统一战线和民族大家庭的团结，起了光辉的作用。热忱地希望你们更进一步地加强内蒙古各民族人民的兄弟般的团结，在我国伟大的社会主义建设事业中作出更大的贡献。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会  1957年4月27日
