Title: Syrian Radio Praises the Soviet Union for Upholding Arab National Sovereignty, Criticizes the United States for Using "Aid" to Infringe Upon the Independence of Middle Eastern Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-05-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 3rd, TASS dispatch: The well-known Syrian radio commentator, Nasib Ikhitiar, commenting on the Soviet Foreign Ministry's statement on the Jordanian issue, pointed out that the document clearly speaks of the Soviet Union's desire to safeguard the independence of Arab countries and consolidate their sovereignty.

He continued, the actions of Western countries are interfering in our internal affairs and are attempting to re-establish imperialist forces in the Middle East.

He sharply criticized the imperialist countries, especially the United States, for attempting to impose "aid" on Middle Eastern countries. He said that when providing "aid," the imperialists demand the establishment of a political system in our countries that conforms to their political objectives as a reward.

He concluded: "However, this kind of imperialist aid not only infringes upon our political independence but also infringes upon our economic independence, because the purpose of this aid is to keep our countries as agricultural countries so that large monopoly capital can completely control us. The purpose of this aid is to enslave us and seize strategic strongholds."

### 叙电台赞扬苏联维护阿拉伯国家主权  抨击美国用“援助”侵犯中东国家独立

1957-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    叙电台赞扬苏联维护阿拉伯国家主权
    抨击美国用“援助”侵犯中东国家独立
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社大马士革3日讯：著名的叙利亚电台评论员纳西卜·伊赫提尔在评论苏联外交部关于约旦问题的声明的时候指出，这项文件的内容清楚地谈到苏联要维护阿拉伯国家的独立和巩固它们的主权的愿望。
    他继续说，西方国家的行动是干涉我们的内政，而且是企图在中东国家重新建立帝国主义的势力。
    他尖锐地抨击帝国主义国家、特别是美国企图强加于中东国家的“援助”。他说，帝国主义者在提供“援助”的时候，却要求在我们这些国家建立一种符合他们的政治目的的政治制度，作为酬答。
    他最后说：“但是，这种帝国主义的援助不仅侵犯我们的政治独立，而且也侵犯我们的经济独立，因为这种援助的目的是要使我们的国家继续成为农业国，以便大垄断资本可以完全支配我们。这种援助的目的是为了要奴役我们并且攫取战略据点。”
