Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Elects Members of the Standing Committee and Secretariat Secretaries
Author:
Date: 1957-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th - The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League held its first plenary session on the evening of the 26th. One hundred and sixteen Central Committee members and thirty-five alternate members attended the meeting. The meeting elected members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee and secretaries of the Secretariat. The election results are as follows:

Members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League
(Listed in order of votes received, with those receiving the same number of votes listed in order of stroke count of their surnames)
Wang Wei, Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Wu Xueqian, Liu Xiyuan, Hu Keshi, Liang Buting, Gao Tianhui, Yang Haibo, Zhu Yujin, Wang Zhaohua, Chen Lian, Xiang Nan, Zhang Chao, Tong Ying, Zhang Shuguang, Zhang Liqun, Alatan Aoqi'er, Zeng Delin.

Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League
(Listed in order of votes received, with those receiving the same number of votes listed in order of stroke count of their surnames)
First Secretary: Hu Yaobang.
Secretaries: Liu Xiyuan, Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, Wang Wei, Liang Buting, Xiang Nan.

### 共青团中央举行全会  选出中央常委和书记处书记

1957-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    共青团中央举行全会
    选出中央常委和书记处书记
    新华社27日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会在26日晚间举行第一次全体会议。出席的有中央委员一百一十六人，候补委员三十五人。会议选举了中央委员会常务委员和书记处书记。选举结果如下：
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会常务委员
      （按得票多少排列，得票相同者按姓氏笔划排列）
    王伟、胡耀邦、罗毅、吴学谦、刘西元、胡克实、梁步庭、高天辉、杨海波、朱语今、王照华、陈琏、项南、张超、佟英、张曙光、张黎群、阿拉坦敖其尔、曾德林。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记
    （按得票多少排列，得票相同者按姓氏笔划排列）
    第一书记　胡耀邦。
    书记　刘西元、罗毅、胡克实、王伟、梁步庭、项南。
