    International Briefs
    Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation to Visit Korea
    Pyongyang Dispatch: At the invitation of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation will visit Korea in late May and early June.
  Soviet Peace Advocates Delegation Arrives in Japan
    Tokyo Dispatch: The Soviet Peace Advocates Delegation, headed by M. V. Nesterov, Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, arrived in Tokyo on the 17th at the invitation of the Japan Asia Solidarity Committee.
  Sihanouk Receives Chinese Buddhist Delegation
    Phnom Penh Dispatch: Cambodian Prime Minister Norodom Sihanouk received the Chinese Buddhist Delegation at the Royal Palace on the morning of the 18th. The Chinese Buddhist Delegation presented Prime Minister Sihanouk with gifts and thanked him for inviting the delegation to Cambodia to participate in the activities commemorating the 2,500th anniversary of the Buddhist Era.

### 国际简讯

1957-05-20
第3版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团将访朝鲜
    平壤讯：应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议的邀请，苏联最高苏维埃代表团将在5月底6月初来朝鲜访问。
  苏和平拥护者代表团到日本
    东京讯：以苏联商会主席米·维·?斯捷罗夫为首的苏联和平拥护者代表团应日本亚洲团结委员会的邀请已经在17日抵达东京。
  西哈努克接见中国佛教代表团
    金边讯：柬埔寨王国西哈努克首相18日上午在王宫里接见了中国佛教代表团。中国佛教代表团赠送给西哈努克首相一些礼物，并且感谢他邀请代表团到柬埔寨来参加纪念佛教纪元二千五百周年的活动。
