Xinhua News Agency Draft:

On May 1st, 500,000 Japanese people held a grand meeting in the outer garden of the Meiji Shrine in Tokyo and held a demonstration. A group of young people carried a paper model satirizing British Prime Minister Macmillan lighting the fuse of a hydrogen bomb, expressing their opposition to the British hydrogen bomb test on Christmas Island.
