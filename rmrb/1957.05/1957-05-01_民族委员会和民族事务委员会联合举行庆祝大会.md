Title: Nationalities Committee and National Affairs Commission Jointly Hold Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress and the National Affairs Commission jointly held a celebration meeting yesterday afternoon in the auditorium of the Central Institute for Nationalities, celebrating the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

Participating in the celebration meeting were students from the Central Institute for Nationalities, responsible persons and representatives from the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and the Inner Mongolia Office in Beijing, representatives from the Buddhist Association of China and the Chinese Islamic Cultural Association, as well as visiting delegations of various minority nationalities to Beijing, totaling more than 1,200 people.

Zhang Zhuyi, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, congratulated the people of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region for the great achievements they have made in the past ten years. He said that this is a great victory for the Chinese Communist Party's nationality policy and a great victory for the people of all nationalities in the country.

Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the National Affairs Commission, spoke at the meeting. He said that he felt particularly excited and happy as a member of the Mongolian nationality, celebrating the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. He briefly introduced the construction achievements of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region at the meeting. He said that the Mongolian nationality is a hardworking, brave, and simple nationality that has made glorious contributions to the founding of the great motherland in history, and will make even greater contributions in the future.

Su Keqin, Vice President of the Central Institute for Nationalities, Dunwang·Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Sun Gedunba, Head of the Tibet Song and Dance Troupe, spoke successively. Duoshuxiu, Deputy Director of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Office in Beijing, spoke at the meeting, thanking all fraternal nationalities for their help and support to the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

After the meeting, a joint celebration party and a film evening were held.

### 民族委员会和民族事务委员会联合举行庆祝大会

1957-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    民族委员会和民族事务委员会联合举行庆祝大会
    新华社30日讯  全国人民代表大会民族委员会、民族事务委员会联合庆祝内蒙古自治区成立十周年大会，昨天下午在中央民族学院礼堂举行。
    参加庆祝大会的有中央民族学院学生，达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处、内蒙古驻京办事处的负责人和代表，中国佛教协会和中国回民文化协进会的代表以及来京的各少数民族参观团等，共一千二百多人。
    全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员张执一在大会上祝贺内蒙古自治区各族人民十年来获得的伟大成就。他说，这是中国共产党的民族政策的伟大胜利，也是全国各族人民的伟大胜利。
    民族事务委员会副主任萨空了在会上讲话。他说，在庆祝内蒙古自治区成立十周年的时候，他作为蒙古民族成员之一，感到特别兴奋而愉快。他在会上概括地介绍了内蒙古自治区的建设成就。他说，蒙古民族是一个勤劳、勇敢、朴实的民族，在历史上对伟大祖国的缔造有过光荣的贡献，今后的贡献将会更大。
    中央民族学院副院长苏克勤、达赖驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴和西藏歌舞团团长孙格顿巴等相继讲话后，内蒙古自治区人民委员会驻京办事处副处长多淑秀在会上讲话，感谢各兄弟民族对内蒙古自治区的帮助和支援。
    会后举行了联欢晚会和电影晚会。
