Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Chairman of Austrian Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-05-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Chairman of the Austrian Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee and People's Party member of parliament Toncic-Sorinj and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, were at the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, 21st: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Toncic-Sorinj, Chairman of the Austrian Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee and People's Party member of parliament, and his wife.

### 张奚若欢宴奥地利议会外交委员会主席

1957-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    张奚若欢宴奥地利议会外交委员会主席
    新华社20日讯　奥地利议会外交委员会主席、人民党议员汤契奇—索里尼和夫人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、秘书长吴茂荪。
    新华社21日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎奥地利议会外交委员会主席、人民党议员汤契奇—索里尼和夫人。
