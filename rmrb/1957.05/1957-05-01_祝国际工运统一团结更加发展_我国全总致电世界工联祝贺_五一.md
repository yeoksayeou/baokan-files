Title: Wishing Greater Development in the Unity and Solidarity of the International Labor Movement - All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Cable to the World Federation of Trade Unions to Congratulate "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1957-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a cable to the World Federation of Trade Unions to congratulate them on the "May Day" International Labor Day. The text of the cable is as follows: Prague World Federation of Trade Unions:

    On the occasion of the "May Day" International Labor Day, a festival of solidarity for workers around the world, I would like to extend warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese workers to the World Federation of Trade Unions, the glorious banner of international solidarity for workers around the world.

    I wish the World Federation of Trade Unions even greater achievements in the struggle to improve the living standards of workers, to strive for trade union and democratic rights, and to strive for national independence and world peace. I wish for greater development in the unity and solidarity of the international trade union movement.

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions April 29th

### 祝国际工运统一团结更加发展  我国全总致电世界工联祝贺“五一”

1957-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    祝国际工运统一团结更加发展
    我国全总致电世界工联祝贺“五一”
    新华社28日讯  中华全国总工会致电世界工会联合会祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。电文如下：布拉格世界工会联合会：
    值此全世界工人团结的节日——“五一”国际劳动节之际，谨代表中国工人向世界工人国际团结的光辉旗帜——世界工会联合会致以热烈的祝贺。
    谨祝世界工联在争取提高工人的生活水平，争取工会与民主权利和争取民族独立与世界和平的斗争中获得更光辉的成就。祝国际工会运动的统一团结更加发展。
    中华全国总工会  4月29日
