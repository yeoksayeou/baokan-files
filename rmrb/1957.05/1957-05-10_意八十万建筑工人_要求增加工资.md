Title: 800,000 Italian Construction Workers Demand Wage Increase
Author:
Date: 1957-05-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, May 8th: According to TASS, eight hundred thousand construction workers in Italy are demanding a wage increase. To support this demand, the construction workers' union organizations have decided to hold a one-day strike in many regions. Construction workers in the provinces of Bologna, Modena, Parma, and Reggio Emilia have already held a strike on the 7th.

### 意八十万建筑工人  要求增加工资

1957-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    意八十万建筑工人
    要求增加工资
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利全国八十万建筑工人要求增加工资。建筑工人的工会组织为了支持这一要求，决定在许多地区举行一天罢工。波伦亚、摩登纳、巴马和勒佐尼尔艾米利亚等省的建筑工人已经在7日举行了罢工。
