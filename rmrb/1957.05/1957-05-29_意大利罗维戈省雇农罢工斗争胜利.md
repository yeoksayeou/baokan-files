Title: Victory of the Strike Struggle of Farmhands in Rovigo Province, Italy
Author:
Date: 1957-05-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 27th. According to TASS: The strike of 80,000 farmhands in Rovigo province, Italy, which has lasted for more than a month, is coming to a victorious end. 64,000 farmhands have already forced the landlords to make concessions and have increased their wages. At present, the strike is continuing in towns and villages where large landowners stubbornly refuse to meet the demands of the farmhands.

### 意大利罗维戈省雇农罢工斗争胜利

1957-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利罗维戈省雇农罢工斗争胜利
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社罗马27日讯：在意大利延续了一个多月的罗维戈省八万雇农要求增加工资的罢工正在胜利结束。六万四千个雇农已经使地主作了让步，并且提高了工资。现在，在大地主坚持拒绝满足雇农要求的村镇，罢工仍在继续着。
