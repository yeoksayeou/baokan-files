Title: Excerpts from the Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-12-11
Edition: 4

To oppose imperialism, one must oppose modern revisionism. In the struggle between Marxism-Leninism and modern revisionism, there is no middle ground. With regard to the modern revisionist group centered around the leadership of the CPSU, we must draw a clear line of demarcation, resolutely expose their true colors as renegades and scabs, and it is impossible to engage in any "joint actions" with them.

### 摘自党的八届十一中全会公报

1966-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    要反对帝国主义，就必须反对现代修正主义。在马克思列宁主义同现代修正主义的斗争中，决没有中间道路可走。对于以苏共领导为中心的现代修正主义集团，必须划清界限，坚决揭露他们工贼的真面目，不可能同他们搞什么“联合行动”。
    摘自党的八届十一中全会公报
