Title: U.S. Warplanes Brazenly Attack Hanoi City Center
Author:
Date: 1966-12-03
Edition: 1

Ignoring the strong condemnation of the people of the world, the U.S. imperialists have taken a new and serious step in escalating the war. On the afternoon of December 2, they brazenly dispatched multiple batches of dozens of warplanes to savagely attack two residential areas and the southern suburbs of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, committing heinous crimes. This is yet another serious escalation in the war by the aggressive U.S. aggressors against the Vietnamese people.

Today, the heroic military and civilian population of Hanoi, with great indignation, fought bravely in coordination with the People's Air Force, shooting down two U.S. planes and foiling the repeated arrogant attacks of the U.S. aggressor air force.

According to reports, on the same day, the military and civilian population of the northern provinces of Vietnam, including Bac Ninh, Bac Thai, Ninh Binh, Lang Son, Phu Tho, and Nghe An, also shot down nine U.S. aggressor planes.

Thus, in today's battle against the U.S. aggressor air force, the heroic military and civilian population of northern Vietnam shot down a total of eleven U.S. planes, damaged several more, and captured many U.S. pilots.

This new major victory has greatly boosted the morale of the heroic military and civilian population of Hanoi and all of northern Vietnam. The Vietnamese military and civilian population are maintaining high vigilance, standing ready to deal new and even heavier blows to the U.S. aggressors at any time.

### 美国强盗飞机悍然袭击河内市区

1966-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    无视世界人民强烈谴责，采取新的严重的战争“升级”行动
    美国强盗飞机悍然袭击河内市区
    河内军民英勇战斗揍下美国飞机两架
    越南北方各地同一天击落美机九架，活捉许多飞贼
    新华社河内二日电　据这里报道，美帝国主义无视世界人民的强烈谴责，在十二月二日下午，悍然出动多批数十架飞机，野蛮袭击越南民主共和国首都河内市区的两个居民区和南部郊区，犯下了滔天罪行。这是侵略成性的美国强盗对越南人民采取的又一次新的严重的战争“升级”行动。
    英雄的河内市军民，今天怀着极大的愤怒，在人民空军的配合下，进行了无比英勇的战斗，击落美机两架，粉碎了美国强盗空军的一次又一次的猖狂进攻。
    另据公布，同一天，越南北方河北、北太、宁平、谅山、宣光、永福等省军民，也击落美国强盗飞机九架。
    这样，在今天反击美国强盗空军的战斗中，英雄的越南北方军民共击落美国飞机十一架，击伤多架，活捉许多美国飞行员。
    这一新的重大胜利，极大地鼓舞着英雄的河内市军民和全越南北方的军民。越南军民正在保持高度警惕，严阵以待，随时准备给美国侵略强盗以新的更加沉重的打击。
