Title: Chairman Mao Cares for Victorious Anti-Revisionist Warriors Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1966-12-02
Edition: 1

	Chairman Mao cares for the victorious anti-revisionist warriors returning home. Comrade Chen Yi, on behalf of Chairman Mao, the Party Central Committee, and the State Council, extended greetings to the students who were unjustly ordered to return to China by the Hungarian government, praising them for their firm proletarian stance in the anti-revisionist struggle.

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report on December 1, Chen Yi, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, met with five Chinese students returning from Hungary this morning and had a cordial conversation with them.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi, on behalf of Chairman Mao, the Party Central Committee, and the State Council, extended warm greetings to the victorious returning anti-revisionist warriors. He praised the Chinese students in Hungary for their firm proletarian stance in the struggle against modern revisionism, saying they did the right thing and did it well.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi encouraged them to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to study and apply Chairman Mao's works actively, to participate in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, to temper themselves, and to carry out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution well.

### 毛主席关怀胜利回国的反修战士

1966-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席关怀胜利回国的反修战士
    陈毅同志代表毛主席、党中央和国务院，慰问被匈牙利政府无理勒令回国的留学生，赞扬他们在反修斗争中表现了坚定的无产阶级立场。
    新华社一日讯　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，今天上午接见被匈牙利政府无理勒令回国的我国五名留匈学生，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    陈毅副总理代表毛主席，代表党中央和国务院，向胜利归来的反修战士表示亲切的慰问。他赞扬留匈学生在反对现代修正主义的斗争中，表现了坚定的无产阶级立场，做得对，做得好。
    陈毅副总理勉励他们要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，积极投入到无产阶级文化大革命中去，锻炼自己，把无产阶级文化大革命搞好。
