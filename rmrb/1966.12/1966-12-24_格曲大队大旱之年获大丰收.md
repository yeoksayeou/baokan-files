Title: The Gecuo Production Brigade Achieves a Bumper Harvest in a Year of Severe Drought
Author:
Date: 1966-12-24
Edition: 2

The Gecuo Production Brigade in Jianza County, Huangnan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Qinghai Province, experienced an unprecedented severe drought this year. The Han and Tibetan community members, with the spirit of Yu Gong moving mountains, fought for 150 days to complete a seven-mile-long "Yu Gong Canal." This significantly helped them resist the drought and achieve a bumper harvest. The brigade's total grain output this year increased by 21 percent compared to the highest historical yield in 1965.

### 格曲大队大旱之年获大丰收

1966-12-24
第2版()
专栏：

    格曲大队大旱之年获大丰收
    青海省黄南藏族自治州尖扎县格曲大队今年遇上了百年不遇的大旱。回藏两族社员以愚公移山的精神，奋战一百五十天，修成了一条七里长的“愚公渠”，有力地抗御了旱灾，获得了大丰收。全队今年粮食总产量比历史上最高的一九六五年还增长百分之二十一。
