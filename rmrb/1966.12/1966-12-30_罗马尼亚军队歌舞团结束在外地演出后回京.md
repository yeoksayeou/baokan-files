Title: Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe Returns to Beijing After Performing in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1966-12-30
Edition: 4

The Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe, led by Colonel Apostol Emil, the head of the delegation, and Colonel Dinu Stelian, the troupe leader, concluded their friendly visit and performances in Shanghai. Accompanied by Vice Minister Yu Ji of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, they returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Colonel Sorlescu, the military attaché of the Romanian Embassy in China, who also traveled with the troupe to Shanghai, returned to Beijing on the same flight.

The Romanian artists received warm welcomes from local revolutionary masses, PLA soldiers, and Red Guards wherever they went.

### 罗马尼亚军队歌舞团结束在外地演出后回京

1966-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚军队歌舞团结束在外地演出后回京
    据新华社二十八日讯由领队阿波斯托尔·埃米尔上校和团长迪努·斯太利安上校率领的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国军队歌舞团，在上海进行友好访问演出后，由中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长虞棘陪同，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    随团到外地的罗马尼亚驻我国大使馆武官索列斯库上校也同机返回北京。
    罗马尼亚的艺术家们所到之处，受到了当地革命群众、解放军指战员和红卫兵小将们的欢迎。
