Title: Producing Diesel Engines Swiftly, Efficiently, and Economically
Author:
Date: 1966-12-08
Edition: 3

The workers of the Changzhou Diesel Engine Factory in Jiangsu have taken Chairman Mao's strategic directive on preparing for war, preparing for famine, and serving the people to heart. They have implemented it in their actions, producing small horsepower diesel engines swiftly, efficiently, and economically, and wholeheartedly serving agricultural mechanization. In the first three quarters of this year, they produced over 6,000 small horsepower diesel engines, which is more than 2.4 times the total production of last year. They have completed this year's state plan ahead of schedule and exceeded the quota.

### 多快好省地生产柴油机  常州柴油机厂今年前三个季度的产量比去年全年总产多一倍多

1966-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

    多快好省地生产柴油机
    常州柴油机厂今年前三个季度的产量比去年全年总产多一倍多
    据新华社南京电　江苏常州柴油机厂职工把毛主席关于备战、备荒、为人民的战略方针，刻在心上，贯彻到行动中，多快好省地生产小马力柴油机，全心全意为农业机械化服务，今年前三个季度，共造出小马力柴油机六千多台，相当于去年全年总产量的二点四倍多，提前超额完成了今年的国家计划。
