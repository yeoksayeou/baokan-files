Title: Several Sports Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Chinese government, the Guinean sports delegation, led by its head, Deputy Director of the National Sports Bureau of Guinea, Sanou Ali, arrived in Beijing last night by plane from Phnom Penh for a friendly visit to China.

### 几体育代表团到京

1966-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    几体育代表团到京
    新华社十三日讯　应邀来我国进行友好访问的几内亚体育代表团，由团长、几内亚全国体育处副处长桑供·阿里率领，于昨晚乘飞机由金边到京。
