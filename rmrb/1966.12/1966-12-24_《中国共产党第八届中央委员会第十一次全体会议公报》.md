Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-12-24
Edition: 1

We must have the great ambitions and aspirations of the proletariat. We must dare to tread paths where no one has gone before, and we must dare to scale peaks that no one has ever reached.
