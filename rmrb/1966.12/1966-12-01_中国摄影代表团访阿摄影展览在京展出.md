Title: Chinese Photography Delegation's Exhibition in Albania Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-01
Edition: 4

	Chinese Photography Delegation's Exhibition in Albania Opens in Beijing

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 29th, the "Chinese Photography Delegation's Visit to Albania Exhibition" officially opened today at the Beijing Exhibition Hall.

	Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Xiao, along with other relevant officials such as Chu Tuonan, Shi Huibin, and He Biao, attended the opening ceremony.

	The entire delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Pi·Duode, the Albanian Minister of Trade, the head of the Albanian Government Trade Delegation Qijiao·Enjila, and the Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasili·Nataneli, also attended the opening ceremony.

### 中国摄影代表团访阿摄影展览在京展出

1966-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中国摄影代表团访阿摄影展览在京展出
    据新华社二十九日讯　“中国摄影代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚摄影展览”今天在北京展览馆正式展出。
    外交部副部长刘晓，以及其他有关方面负责人楚图南、史怀璧、贺彪等出席了开幕式。
    由彼·多德率领的阿中友协代表团全体成员，阿尔巴尼亚贸易部长、阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团团长基乔·恩杰拉和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利等出席了开幕式。
