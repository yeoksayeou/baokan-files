Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1966-12-02
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai today received the delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association led by P. Doda, and the delegation of the Albanian government trade mission led by K. Nxhela.

Premier Zhou Enlai had a very cordial conversation with the Albanian comrades.

Present at the reception were Liu Xiao, Chu Tunan, He Biao, Li Qiang, and Jiang Ming.

Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli was also present.

### 周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚同志

1966-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚同志
    新华社一日讯　周恩来总理今天接见由彼·多德率领的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团，由基乔·恩杰拉率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团。
    周恩来总理同阿尔巴尼亚同志进行了十分亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有刘晓、楚图南、贺彪、李强、江明。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也在座。
