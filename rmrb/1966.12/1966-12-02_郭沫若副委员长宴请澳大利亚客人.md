Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Hosts Australian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-02
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo, along with Yu Liqun, received and hosted a banquet for Australian economist Willet and his wife today, engaging in warm and friendly conversations. Also present were Australian astronomer Christiansen and his wife, who are currently visiting our country.

### 郭沫若副委员长宴请澳大利亚客人

1966-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长宴请澳大利亚客人
    新华社一日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，以及于立群，今天接见并宴请了澳大利亚经济学家威尔莱特和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。在座的还有正在我国访问的澳大利亚天文学家克里斯琴森和夫人。
