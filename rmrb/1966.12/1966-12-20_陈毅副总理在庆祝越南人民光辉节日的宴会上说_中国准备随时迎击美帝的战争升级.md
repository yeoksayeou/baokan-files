Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Says China Ready to Confront U.S. Imperialism's Escalation
Author:
Date: 1966-12-20
Edition: 1

Vice Premier Chen Yi, at a banquet celebrating the glorious anniversary of the Vietnamese people, said that China is prepared to confront the U.S. imperialists' war escalation at any time.

	Chen Zi Ping, the Ambassador, said that Mao Zedong Thought is the true priceless treasure in China's aid to the Vietnamese people.
	Nguyen Minh Phuong, the Acting Head of the Delegation, said that the people of South Vietnam, regardless of the sacrifices they endure, are determined to annihilate the U.S. imperialists.

	New China News Agency, December 19th: Vice Premier Chen Yi, also a Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, hosted a banquet tonight to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the 22nd anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army.

	Ambassador Chen Zi Ping of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Acting Head of the Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and Colonel Chen Wen Peng, the military attaché of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, were invited to attend the banquet.

	At the banquet, Vice Premier Chen Yi delivered a speech. He warmly congratulated the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam on its sixth anniversary and its great achievements in the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation over the past six years. He warmly congratulated all the Vietnamese people, under the leadership of Chairman Ho Chi Minh, for their world-renowned great victory in the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. He said that this great victory proves that U.S. imperialism can be defeated. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation has created the most valuable experience in the 20th century for resisting imperialist aggression, which is the most brilliant example in history and worthy of our celebration. We celebrate the victory of the Vietnamese people. We learn from the Vietnamese people. This learning is for the Chinese people to be prepared for national defense and to be ready to confront the U.S. imperialists' war escalation at any time.

	Ambassador Chen Zi Ping, in his speech, highly praised the great Mao Zedong Thought. He said that providing material aid to the Vietnamese people is important, but Mao Zedong Thought is our true priceless treasure. He said that the 700 million Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are the closest comrades-in-arms of the Vietnamese people. Our combat friendship has been tested over a long period.

	Acting Head of the Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong, in his speech, expressed that regardless of the sacrifices and hardships endured, and regardless of whether the battle lasts for ten years, twenty years, or even longer, the people of South Vietnam are determined to annihilate U.S. imperialism and its lackeys. He said that in the arduous struggle, the people of South Vietnam have always received tremendous and valuable aid from the 700 million Chinese people. Every victory achieved by the military and civilians of South Vietnam on the battlefield is inseparable from the sincere, selfless, generous, and effective aid of the Chinese people and the Communist Party of China.

	Also present at the banquet tonight with the Vietnamese comrades were various responsible officials including Li Tianyou, Yuan Ziqin, Liu Shaowen, Pan Zhenwu, and Wang Zhi Fan.

### 陈毅副总理在庆祝越南人民光辉节日的宴会上说  中国准备随时迎击美帝的战争升级

1966-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在庆祝越南人民光辉节日的宴会上说
  中国准备随时迎击美帝的战争升级
    陈子平大使说，毛泽东思想是中国援助越南人民的真正无价之宝
  阮明芳代理团长说，南越人民不管经受多大牺牲，决心消灭美帝
    新华社十九日讯　国务院副总理、国防委员会副主席陈毅今晚举行宴会，热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年和越南人民军建军二十二周年。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳和越南驻中国大使馆武官陈文彭上校等，应邀出席了宴会。
    陈毅副总理在宴会上讲话。他热烈祝贺越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年，祝贺它六年来在抗美救国斗争中取得的伟大战绩。热烈祝贺全体越南人民在胡志明主席的领导下，在抗美救国斗争中取得的世界性的伟大胜利。他说，这个伟大胜利证明，美帝国主义是可以被打败的。陈毅副总理说，越南人民抗美救国斗争，为二十世纪六十年代抗击帝国主义侵略创造了最宝贵的经验，这是历史上最新最光辉的战例，是值得我们祝贺的。我们庆祝越南人民的胜利。我们向越南人民学习。这个学习，就是中国人民做好国防准备，随时迎击美帝国主义的战争升级。
    陈子平大使在讲话中热烈赞扬伟大的毛泽东思想。他说，给越南人民物质援助固然重要，但是，毛泽东思想才是我们真正的无价之宝。他说，用毛泽东思想武装起来的七亿中国人民和中国人民解放军是越南人民最亲近的战友。我们之间的战斗友谊是经过长期考验的。
    阮明芳代理团长在讲话中表示，不管经受多大牺牲和艰苦，不管战斗十年、二十年或者更长的时间，越南南方人民决心消灭美帝国主义及其走狗。他说，我们越南南方人民在艰苦的斗争中，始终得到七亿中国人民巨大和宝贵的援助。我们越南南方军民在战场上取得的每一个胜利，都是同中国人民和中国共产党的尽力、无私、慷慨、有效的援助分不开的。
    今晚同越南同志欢聚的，还有各方面负责人李天佑、袁子钦、刘少文、潘振武、王植范等。
