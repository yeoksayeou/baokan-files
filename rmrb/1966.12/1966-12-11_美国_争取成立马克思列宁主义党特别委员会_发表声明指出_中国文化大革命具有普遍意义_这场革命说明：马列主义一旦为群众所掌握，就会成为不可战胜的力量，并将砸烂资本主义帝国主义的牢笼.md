Title: The Special Committee for the Establishment of the Marxist-Leninist Party in the United States Issues a Statement Pointing Out that the Chinese Cultural Revolution Has Universal Significance
Author:
Date: 1966-12-11
Edition: 5

The Special Committee for the Establishment of the Marxist-Leninist Party in the United States issued a statement pointing out that the Chinese Cultural Revolution has universal significance. This revolution shows that once Marxism-Leninism is grasped by the masses, it becomes an invincible force and will smash the capitalist-imperialist prison.

	Xinhua News Agency reports: The Special Committee for the Establishment of the Marxist-Leninist Party in the United States issued a statement on the Chinese proletarian Cultural Revolution, pointing out that this Cultural Revolution has universal significance. It shows that once Marxism-Leninism is grasped by the masses, it becomes an invincible material force.

	The statement says, "The objective laws of class struggle show that after the victory of socialism, class struggle will still exist for a long time. This law applies to China and to all socialist countries until a classless society is established."

	"The concern of Chairman Mao and the Chinese people to prevent capitalist restoration and prevent revisionism is correct. This is a problem that all Marxist-Leninists must solve both in theory and in practice."

	The statement praises the Red Guards as the shock troops of this cultural revolution, saying they are the youth who study Chairman Mao's works and rebel against old ideas and old customs.

	The statement points out, "The universal significance of this revolution is evidence that once Marxism-Leninism is grasped by the masses, it becomes an invincible material force. The task of Marxist-Leninists is to teach revolutionary ideas to the masses. Once the masses grasp revolutionary ideas, their struggle will gain direction, and they will become invincible together with all workers, smashing the capitalist and imperialist prison that imprisons them."

### 美国“争取成立马克思列宁主义党特别委员会”发表声明指出  中国文化大革命具有普遍意义  这场革命说明：马列主义一旦为群众所掌握，就会成为不可战胜的力量，并将砸烂资本主义帝国主义的牢笼

1966-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    美国“争取成立马克思列宁主义党特别委员会”发表声明指出
    中国文化大革命具有普遍意义
    这场革命说明：马列主义一旦为群众所掌握，就会成为不可战胜的力量，并将砸烂资本主义帝国主义的牢笼
    新华社讯　美国“争取成立马克思列宁主义党特别委员会”就中国无产阶级文化大革命发表声明指出，这场文化大革命具有普遍的意义。它说明了，马克思列宁主义一旦为群众所掌握，就会成为不可战胜的物质力量。
    声明说，“阶级斗争的客观发展规律表明，在社会主义胜利之后，在长时间内仍旧存在阶级斗争。这个规律适用于中国，也适用于所有社会主义国家，直到建立没有阶级的社会为止。”
      “毛泽东主席和中国人民关心防止资本主义复辟和防止出现修正主义，这种关心是正确的。这是所有马克思列宁主义者都必须在理论上和实践上加以解决的问题。”
    声明赞扬“红卫兵是这次文化革命的突击力量，他们是学习毛主席著作和向旧思想、旧风俗造反的青年。”
    声明指出，“这个革命的普遍意义是这样一个证据：马克思列宁主义一旦为群众所掌握，就成为不可战胜的物质力量。我们马克思列宁主义者的任务是把革命的思想教给群众。一旦群众掌握革命思想，他们的斗争就会获得方向，他们就会同全体工人一起成为不可战胜的，并将砸烂资本主义、帝国主义囚禁他们的牢笼。”
