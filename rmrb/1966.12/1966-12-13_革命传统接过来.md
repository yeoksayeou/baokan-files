Title: Carrying Forward the Revolutionary Tradition
Author: Lü Línqīng
Date: 1966-12-13
Edition: 6

	The red flag leads the way,
	The songs are so bold and grand;
	Red armbands shine on the mountains and rivers,
	The "Long March" troops look magnificent!
	Every heart beats for Beijing,
	With broad chests and unyielding spirits!
	Marching through storms and thunder,
	Carrying forward the revolutionary tradition!
	No matter the wind, frost, rain, or snow,
	No matter the mountains of knives or seas of fire!
	In our hearts, we have the compass—
	The "Quotations from Chairman Mao" shine brightly!
	With boundless enthusiasm, we praise the sun,
	Everywhere is a stage for propaganda;
	Roll in the mud with the masses,
	Grow strong in the storms of struggle!
	Go and get to know society,
	Get to know the masses,
	Bring back the revolutionary "scriptures";
	Look forward to a future full of splendor,
	The red land will forever be springtime!
	The red flag leads the way,
	The songs are so bold and grand;
	The young saplings nurtured by Chairman Mao,
	Will be the pillars of the great edifice of communism!

### 革命传统接过来
吕林清
1966-12-13
第6版()
专栏：长征路上

    革命传统接过来	吕林清
    红旗把路开，
    歌声多豪迈；
    红彤彤袖章映山水，
    “长征”队伍好气派！
    颗颗红心向北京，
    胸怀开阔志不衰！
    踏着风雷走天下，
    革命传统接过来！
    哪怕什么风霜雨雪，
    哪怕什么刀山火海！
    胸中自有方向盘——
    《毛主席语录》放光彩！
    豪情满腔颂太阳，
    处处是宣传的舞台；
    到群众中滚一身泥土呵，
    在斗争的风浪中长成材！
    去熟悉社会，
    熟悉群众，
    把革命“真经”取回来；
    喜看前程多锦绣，
    红色江山春常在！
    红旗把路开，
    歌声多豪迈；
    毛主席栽培的小树苗呵，
    做共产主义大厦的栋梁材！
