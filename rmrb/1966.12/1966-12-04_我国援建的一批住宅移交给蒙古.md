Title: China Hands Over a Batch of Residential Buildings to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 5

	China has completed the construction of a batch of residential buildings for Mongolia through a loan agreement. The second phase of the project, totaling 220,000 square meters, has seen some of the buildings completed. At the request of the Mongolian side, the handover ceremony for these buildings was held on the construction site on December 30 by the handover teams from both China and Mongolia. The buildings handed over this time were constructed in accordance with the 1960 agreement between the governments of China and Mongolia on economic aid from the Chinese government to the Mongolian government.

### 我国援建的一批住宅移交给蒙古

1966-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我国援建的一批住宅移交给蒙古
    据新华社乌兰巴托一日电　中国以贷款方式援助蒙古建筑的二十二万平方米住宅第二期工程的部分建筑已建成。应蒙方要求，中蒙双方交接小组三十日在工地上举行了交接仪式。这次交付使用的部分建筑是根据中蒙两国政府一九六○年关于中国政府给予蒙古政府经济援助的协定兴建的。
