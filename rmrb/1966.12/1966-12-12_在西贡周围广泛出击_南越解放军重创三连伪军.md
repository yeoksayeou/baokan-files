Title: Liberation Army Strikes Widely Around Saigon, Inflicting Heavy Losses on Three Companies of Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-12-12
Edition: 5

Liberation forces in South Vietnam launched a series of attacks around Saigon on the night of the 9th, inflicting heavy losses on the puppet forces. According to reports from Western news agencies, the liberation forces attacked a large puppet military camp in Ba Ria province, about 65 kilometers southeast of Saigon, causing significant casualties and capturing numerous weapons. The liberation forces then successfully withdrew after the battle.

On the 10th, at noon, the liberation forces ambushed a company of puppet forces on a road about 35 kilometers northwest of Saigon, causing heavy casualties.

### 在西贡周围广泛出击  南越解放军重创三连伪军

1966-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    在西贡周围广泛出击
    南越解放军重创三连伪军
    新华社十一日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量九日晚上袭击了西贡东南六十五公里巴地省的一个巨大的伪军营地，使南越伪军遭受了惨重的损失。据西方通讯社报道，解放武装力量痛击了守卫这个营地的两连伪军，使敌人遭受了“非常重大的伤亡”，并且“损失了许多武器”。解放武装力量在歼敌后胜利地转移了。
    十日中午，解放武装力量又在西贡西北三十五公里的一条公路上伏击一连伪军，使敌人遭受重大伤亡。
