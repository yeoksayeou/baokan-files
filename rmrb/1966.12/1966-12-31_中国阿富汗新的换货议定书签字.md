Author:
Date: 1966-12-31
Edition: 5

New China-Afghanistan Barter Agreement Signed

	Peking, Dec. 28 (New China News Agency) - The China-Afghanistan Barter Agreement for 1966-1967 was signed here this morning.

	The agreement was signed after friendly and mutually beneficial negotiations between the two sides.

	Mohammed Akbar Mummar, Afghan Deputy Minister of Commerce, said at the signing ceremony that the signing of the agreement marked another step forward in promoting friendly relations between Afghanistan and China. He expressed his thanks for the interest-free loans provided by China to Afghanistan without any conditions. According to the agreement, the volume of trade between the two countries for the 1966-1967 fiscal year will be larger than in previous years.

### 中国阿富汗新的换货议定书签字

1966-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    中国阿富汗新的换货议定书签字
    新华社喀布尔二十八日电　中国—阿富汗一九六六——一九六七年换货议定书二十八日上午在这里签字。
    议定书是在双方本着友好互利的精神进行了会谈之后签订的。
    阿富汗商业副大臣穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·穆马尔在签字仪式上说，议定书的签订标志着在促进阿富汗和中国的友好关系方面又进了一步。他对中国向阿富汗提供的不附带任何条件的无息贷款表示感谢。根据议定书，两国之间一九六六——一九六七年度的贸易量比往年要大。
