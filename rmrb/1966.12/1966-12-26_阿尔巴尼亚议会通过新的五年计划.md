Title: Albanian Parliament Adopts New Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-12-26
Edition: 5

The sixth session of the second session of the Albanian People's Assembly, which lasted two days, concluded successfully in the afternoon of the 23rd. The representatives, based on the instructions of the fifth session of the Albanian Party of Labor, discussed and unanimously adopted important decrees such as the fourth five-year plan for the development of the economy and culture of the People's Republic of Albania (1966-1970).

The representatives, in their speeches on the afternoon of the 22nd and the morning of the 23rd, unanimously agreed with the reports made by the Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Kolega, and the Minister of Finance, Virli, to the Assembly. They pointed out that the Albanian people, under the correct leadership of the Albanian Party of Labor, have successfully defeated the imperialist and revisionist blockade schemes, completed the third five-year plan, and are smoothly completing the first year of the fourth five-year plan. Currently, they are confidently welcoming the new battle tasks of 1967.

The representatives, in their speeches, unanimously pledged that the Albanian people will unite more closely around the Albanian Party of Labor led by Comrade Enver Hoxha, resolutely complete and overfulfill the glorious tasks of the fourth five-year plan; consolidate the dictatorship of the proletariat; carry out the socialist revolution in the fields of ideology and culture; enhance revolutionary vigilance, strengthen national defense, and be ready at any time to deal a fatal blow to any enemy who dares to invade the motherland.

The decree on the fourth five-year plan for economic and cultural development adopted at this meeting stipulates: to continue the socialist industrialization of the country, increase industrial production by fully utilizing existing production capacities and new projects; to focus on further intensifying agriculture, especially grain production; to improve the material well-being and cultural level of the people on the basis of the growth of social production, enhance the national defense capabilities; to further perfect the socialist production relations on the correct revolutionary path, gradually narrow the gaps between urban and rural areas, between workers and peasants, between industry and agriculture, and between mental and manual labor, deepen the socialist revolution in the fields of ideology and culture, strengthen the dictatorship of the proletariat, so as to continuously advance the socialist revolution in all fields.

The decree also stipulates that during the fourth five-year plan period, the total industrial output value will increase by 50 to 54 percent, the total agricultural output value will increase by 71 to 76 percent, investment will increase by about 34 percent, and national income will increase by 45 to 50 percent.

The meeting also adopted the decree on the 1967 national plan for economic and cultural development, the decree on the 1967 national budget, and several other decrees.

### 阿尔巴尼亚议会通过新的五年计划

1966-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚议会通过新的五年计划
    进一步发展工农业生产，逐步缩小三大差别，深入开展思想文化革命，加强无产阶级专政
    新华社地拉那二十四日电　历时两天的阿尔巴尼亚第六届人民议会第二次会议于二十三日下午胜利闭幕。
    代表们在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会指示的基础上，讨论和一致通过了关于阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国发展经济和文化的第四个五年计划（一九六六——一九七○年）等重要法令。
    代表们在二十二日下午和二十三日上午的发言中一致同意国家计划委员会主席科列加和财政部长维尔利向大会所作的报告，并且指出：阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的正确领导下，胜利地击败了帝国主义和修正主义的封锁阴谋，完成了第三个五年计划，正在顺利地完成第四个五年计划第一年的计划。目前，他们正满怀信心地迎接着一九六七年的新的战斗任务。
    代表们在发言中一致保证：阿尔巴尼亚人民将更加紧密地团结在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的周围，坚决完成和超额完成第四个五年计划的光荣任务；巩固无产阶级专政；开展思想和文化领域内的社会主义革命；提高革命警惕，加强国防，随时准备给一切敢于侵犯祖国的敌人以致命的打击。
    这次会议通过的关于发展经济和文化的第四个五年计划的法令规定：继续国家的社会主义工业化，通过充分利用现有生产能力和新建项目增加工业生产；首先通过农业的进一步集约化，集中力量进一步发展农业生产，特别是粮食生产；在社会生产增长的基础上，提高人民的物质福利和文化水平，增强祖国的国防力量；在正确的革命道路上，进一步完善社会主义生产关系，逐渐缩小城乡之间、工农之间、工农业之间、脑力劳动和体力劳动之间的差别，深入开展思想和文化领域内的社会主义革命，加强无产阶级专政，以便把所有领域内的社会主义革命不断推向前进。
    法令还规定：在第四个五年计划期间，工业总产值将增长百分之五十至五十四，农业总产值将增长百分之七十一至七十六，投资将增长百分之三十四左右，国民收入将增长百分之四十五至五十。
    会议还通过了关于发展经济和文化的一九六七年国家计划的法令、关于一九六七年国家预算的法令以及其它的一些法令。
