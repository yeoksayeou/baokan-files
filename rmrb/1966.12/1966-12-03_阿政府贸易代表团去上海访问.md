Title: Albanian Government Trade Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1966-12-03
Edition: 5

An Albanian government trade delegation led by Kico Ngjela, Minister of Trade of Albania, arrived in Shanghai today for a visit. The delegation, accompanied by Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of China's Ministry of Foreign Trade, departed from Beijing by plane this morning. Those who saw them off at the airport included Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of China's Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others. The Albanian Ambassador to China, V. Nataneli, and Commercial Counselor Guo Ge Kema also went to the airport to see them off.

### 阿政府贸易代表团去上海访问

1966-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    阿政府贸易代表团去上海访问
    新华社二日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚贸易部部长基乔·恩杰拉率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团，在我国对外贸易部副部长江明陪同下，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去上海参观访问。到机场送行的，有我国对外贸易部代理部长林海云等。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦·纳塔奈利，商务参赞果戈·科兹马等也到机场送行。
