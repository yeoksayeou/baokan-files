Title: While the U.S. Empire Shouts "Stop Bombing," It Violates Hanoi's Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-12-26
Edition: 5

While the U.S. Empire and its accomplices are loudly proclaiming a halt to the bombing of North Vietnam, in the afternoon of the 25th, multiple batches of American aggressor aircraft brazenly violated the airspace over the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi, and other provinces.

The vigilant military and civilians of Hanoi responded with fierce artillery fire, shooting down one American aggressor aircraft on the spot.

The military and civilians in other parts of North Vietnam also fought bravely, delivering a crushing blow to the American air aggressors.

Regarding the various treacherous tricks played by U.S. imperialism, the heroic Vietnamese military and civilians expressed deep indignation. The people of Hanoi told reporters: "In the past two days, the American aggressors have been loudly shouting 'stop bombing,' 'peace talks,' while continuing to send aircraft to violate Hanoi and other regions. This further exposes the savage aggressive nature and cunning face of U.S. imperialism."

They declared: "We are not afraid of the American aggressors' bombs, nor do we believe their deceits. We are determined to carry out Chairman Ho Chi Minh's sacred call to resist America and save the nation. We will endure all hardships and sacrifices, and resolutely fight the U.S. imperialists to the end."

The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army Liaison Delegation strongly protested on the 25th against the American aggressor aircraft's continued violation of the airspace of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam over the past two days, conducting reconnaissance and harassment activities in Hanoi and many other places.

The Liaison Delegation pointed out that the U.S. imperialists' blatant aggressive actions over the past two days clearly show that the United States is still stubbornly preparing for a new "escalation" of the war, in order to savagely bomb the residential areas of North Vietnam.

### 美帝一面叫嚷“停炸”一面侵犯河内上空  河内军民击落美机一架  表示不怕美帝炸弹不受美帝欺骗决心战斗到底

1966-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝一面叫嚷“停炸”一面侵犯河内上空
    河内军民击落美机一架
    表示不怕美帝炸弹不受美帝欺骗决心战斗到底
    新华社河内二十五日电　本社记者报道：正当美帝国主义和它的帮凶大肆喧嚷对越南北方“停止轰炸”的时候，二十五日下午，又有多批美国强盗飞机猖狂地侵犯越南民主共和国首都河内市和其他一些省的上空。
    保持着高度警惕的河内市军民，以猛烈的炮火当场击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    越南北方其他地方的军民也进行了英勇的战斗，给美国空中强盗以迎头痛击。
    对于美帝国主义玩弄的种种阴险花招，英雄的越南军民表示无比愤怒。河内市人民向记者指出：两天来，美国强盗一方面大肆叫嚷“停炸”、“和谈”，一方面继续派遣飞机侵犯河内和其他地区，这就更加暴露了美帝国主义的凶恶的侵略本性和狡猾面目。
    他们表示：“我们绝不怕美国强盗的炸弹，也不听它的欺骗。我们决心实现胡志明主席的抗美救国的神圣号召，不怕一切艰苦、牺牲，坚决同美帝国主义战斗到底。”
    新华社河内二十五日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团二十五日强烈抗议美国强盗飞机在二十四日和二十五日两天中，继续侵犯越南民主共和国领空，对河内和其它许多地方进行侦察骚扰活动。
    联络代表团指出，美帝国主义这两天的明目张胆的侵略行动，清楚地表明美国仍然顽固地为新的战争“升级”作准备，以便野蛮地轰炸越南北方的居民区。
