Title: Hefei Develops Over 200 New Products
Author:
Date: 1966-12-07
Edition: 2

    The industrial workers of Hefei City, armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, have made a great leap in technological innovation during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. They have broken through the bourgeois technical "authorities" and successfully developed over 200 new products. Among these are a 100-ton single-column correcting press hydraulic machine, single-crystal photoresistors, large-scale mobile spray powder machines, invar alloy, seamless steel tubes of various shapes, high-frequency electric pulse machine tools, cycloidal pinwheel reducers, as well as lifting and transportation equipment, building materials, pesticides, and polyvinyl chloride artificial leather. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 合肥制成两百多种新产品

1966-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    合肥制成两百多种新产品
    合肥市工业战线广大职工，在无产阶级文化大革命中，以战无不胜的毛泽东思想为武器，大闹技术革新，大破资产阶级技术“权威”，试制成功了两百多种新产品。其中有一百吨单柱校正压装液压机、单晶体光敏电阻、大型机动喷雾喷粉机、因瓦合金、异形无缝钢管、高频电脉冲机床、摆线针轮减速电机以及起重运输设备、建筑材料、农药、聚氯乙烯人造革等。　（据新华社讯）
