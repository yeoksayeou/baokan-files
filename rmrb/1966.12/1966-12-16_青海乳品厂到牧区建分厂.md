Title: Qinghai Dairy Factory Establishes Branch in Pastoral Area
Author:
Date: 1966-12-16
Edition: 4

Qinghai Dairy Factory, in order to obtain raw materials nearby and further develop dairy production, recently established a branch factory on the grasslands under the Qilian Mountains in Hai Bei Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture. The spray drying equipment of this branch factory can process 20,000 jin (approximately 10,000 kg) of fresh milk daily and has officially started production.

This is the second branch factory established by Qinghai Dairy Factory. Last year, the first branch factory was set up in Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture.

### 青海乳品厂到牧区建分厂

1966-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    青海乳品厂到牧区建分厂
    青海乳品厂为了就近取得原料，进一步发展乳品生产，最近在祁连山下海北藏族自治州的草原上建立了一个分厂。这个分厂的喷雾设备，每天可以处理鲜奶两万斤，已经正式投入生产。
    这是青海乳品厂建立的第二个分厂。前一年，已经在果洛藏族自治州设立了第一个分厂。
                              （据新华社讯）
