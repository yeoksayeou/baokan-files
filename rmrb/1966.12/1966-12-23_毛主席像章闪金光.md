Title: Chairman Mao's Badge Shines with Golden Light
Author: Wú Shàoxùn
Date: 1966-12-23
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao's badge radiates a million rays of light. It brings the deep affection of the people of the entire nation, and it brings the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. Chairman Mao's badge, worn on the chest, warms the heart. The whole body is filled with a warm current of happiness, and Mao Zedong Thought is infused into the blood. Chairman Mao's badge, worn on the chest, shines with golden light. Border defense soldiers wearing it have revolutionary spirit soaring to the heavens. Clearing the way for revolution, the path forward is unobstructed; with a single-minded devotion to the people, willing to stand guard for the revolution for a lifetime.

### 毛主席像章闪金光
吴绍训
1966-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    毛主席像章闪金光
    边防战士　吴绍训毛主席像章，放射出万丈光芒。你带来了全国人民的深情厚意，带来了战无不胜的毛泽东思想。毛主席像章，戴在胸前，暖在心房，全身灌注着幸福的暖流，血液中溶化了毛泽东思想。毛主席像章，戴在胸前闪金光。边防战士戴上你，革命豪情高万丈。披荆斩棘干革命，前进道路无阻挡；一心一意为人民，愿意为了革命站一辈子岗。
