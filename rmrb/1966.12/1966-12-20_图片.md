Title: Congolese People Hold Mass Rally Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-12-20
Edition: 4

People of Congo (Brazzaville) are holding a grand mass rally, waving flags symbolizing the unity between Congo (Brazzaville) and Vietnam, to oppose U.S. imperialist aggression against Vietnam. The banner in front reads, "Oppose the war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam!"
