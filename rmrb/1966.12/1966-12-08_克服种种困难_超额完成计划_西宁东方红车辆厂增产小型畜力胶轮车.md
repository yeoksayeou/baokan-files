Title: Overcoming Various Difficulties to Exceed Production Plan: Xining Dongfanghong Vehicle Factory Increases Production of Small Animal-Drawn Rubber-Wheeled Cars
Author:
Date: 1966-12-08
Edition: 3

The workers of the Xining Dongfanghong Vehicle Factory, under the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, have been actively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works. They have overcome various difficulties and have already completed this year's production targets for quantity, quality, value, cost, and profit remittance ahead of schedule and beyond the plan. The monthly production of their main product, the small animal-drawn rubber-wheeled car, has increased from over 800 units in January to over 1,300 units in November. This year's plan was completed despite the fact that the workforce and equipment remained basically unchanged.

The workers of this factory have actively improved technology and equipment, achieving over 30 technological innovations, significantly increasing production efficiency.

Currently, the workers of this factory are further promoting the revolution and production. At the same time, they are repairing equipment and actively preparing for next year's production.

### 克服种种困难  超额完成计划  西宁东方红车辆厂增产小型畜力胶轮车

1966-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

    克服种种困难  超额完成计划
    西宁东方红车辆厂增产小型畜力胶轮车
    据新华社西宁七日电　西宁东方红车辆厂广大职工，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，克服种种困难，已经提前超额完成了今年的产量、质量、产值、成本和上缴利润计划。主要产品小型畜力胶轮车的月产量，由一月份的八百多辆增加到十一月份的一千三百多辆。今年的计划是在人员和设备基本上没有增加的情况下完成的。
    这个厂的职工们积极改进技术，改进设备，已经搞成了三十多项技术革新，使生产效率显著提高。
    目前，这个厂的职工正在进一步抓革命，促生产；同时，动手检修设备，积极做好明年的生产准备工作。
