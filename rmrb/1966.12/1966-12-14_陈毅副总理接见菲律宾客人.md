Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Filipino Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi received a delegation of nine young people and students from the Philippines this morning and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Wu Shaozu and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见菲律宾客人

1966-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见菲律宾客人
    新华社十二日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了菲律宾青年和学生代表九人，和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有伍绍祖等。
