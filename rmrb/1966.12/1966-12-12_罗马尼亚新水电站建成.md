Title: Romania's New Hydroelectric Power Station Completed
Author:
Date: 1966-12-12
Edition: 5

	Romania's largest hydroelectric power station, the Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Hydroelectric Power Station, began formal power generation on the 9th. Its installed capacity is 220,000 kilowatts, producing 400 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually, which is 100,000 kilowatts more than the capacity of Romania's previous largest hydroelectric power station.

	Romania's party and government leaders, including Nicolae Ceaușescu and Chivu Stoica, attended the completion ceremony of this large-scale hydroelectric power station on the 9th. Ceaușescu also delivered a speech at the ceremony.

### 罗马尼亚新水电站建成

1966-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新水电站建成
    据新华社布加勒斯特十日电　罗马尼亚最大的水电站乔治乌—德治水电站九日开始正式发电。它的设备能力为二十二万瓩，每年生产四亿度电，比罗马尼亚原来最大的水电站的设备能力还多十万瓩。
    罗马尼亚党政领导人齐奥塞斯库、毛雷尔等九日出席了这座大型水电站的落成典礼。齐奥塞斯库还在落成典礼上讲了话。
