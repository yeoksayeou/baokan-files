Title: Chairman Mao Has Made Immortal Contributions
Author: Abudujalimu
Date: 1966-12-10
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao has made immortal contributions. The great Chairman Mao Zedong is an unparalleled hero in China and a great leader of the world's people. Chairman Mao Zedong has made immortal contributions to humanity, and the world's people will forever remember him.

I have boundless love for the great Chairman Mao Zedong. My love for him is incomparable and indescribable. I wish Chairman Mao good health and longevity!

	Nigeria, Abudujalimu

### 毛主席作出了不朽的功业
阿卜杜加林姆
1966-12-10
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    毛主席作出了不朽的功业
    伟大的毛泽东主席是中国从未有过的伟人，也是世界人民的伟大领袖。毛泽东主席为人类作出了不朽的功业，世界人民永远记着他。
    我对伟大的毛泽东主席无限热爱，我对他热爱之深是无法比拟、无法形容的。祝毛主席健康、长寿！
    尼日利亚　阿卜杜加林姆
