Title: Studying the "Three Articles"
Author: Jiā Jīnshù
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 6

    After studying the "Three Articles," Dr. Norman Bethune is by my side,
    Selflessness and dedication to others, the spirit of communism is in my heart.
    After studying the "Three Articles," Comrade Zhang Side is by my side,
    Dying for the people is heavier than Mount Tai, even if my body is crushed, I am willing.
    After studying the "Three Articles," the Old Man Moving Mountains is by my side,
    The strength to move mountains is in my body, great difficulties are mere trifles.
    After studying the "Three Articles," the revolutionary spirit soars to the sky,
    Forever following Chairman Mao, even if the seas dry up and the stones crumble, my heart remains unchanged.

### 学了“老三篇”
贾金树
1966-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    学了“老三篇”
    贾金树学了“老三篇”，白求恩就在我身边，毫不利己专门利人，共产主义精神记心间。学了“老三篇”，张思德就在我身边，为人民去死重于泰山，粉身碎骨也情愿。学了“老三篇”，愚公就在我身边，搬山的劲头浑身有，天大的困难只等闲。学了“老三篇”，革命劲头冲破天，永远跟着毛主席，海枯石烂心不变。
