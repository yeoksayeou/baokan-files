Title: Swiss Ambassador to China Leaves Post
Author:
Date: 1966-12-30
Edition: 4

Swiss Ambassador to China Leaves Post

New China News Agency, December 29th: The Swiss Ambassador to China, Mr. Kaelin, has been recalled and will leave his post. Tonight, he departed Beijing by train.
