Title: Write Chairman Mao's Words Everywhere
Author: Pí Jīnzhōng
Date: 1966-12-25
Edition: 2

To propagate Chairman Mao Zedong's Thought on a larger scale and to ensure that everyone can see Chairman Mao's words frequently, I propose the following:

1. All publishing departments across the country should, when publishing or reprinting various books, diaries, picture albums, etc., print Chairman Mao's quotations on the inside front cover or other appropriate pages.

2. All relevant factories across the country should, when producing certain daily necessities such as teacups, also inscribe Chairman Mao's quotations on them.

Worker, Pi Jinzhong, Supply Station, Jiulonggang Coal Mine, Huainan, Anhui

### 处处写上毛主席的话
皮金忠
1966-12-25
第2版()
专栏：来信

    处处写上毛主席的话
    为了在更大的范围内宣传毛泽东思想，让大家经常看到毛主席的话，我建议：一，全国各地出版部门，出版或再版各种书籍、日记本、画片等时，在前扉内二页或其它适当地方，都印上毛主席语录。
    二，全国各地有关工厂，在生产某些日用品如茶杯等时，也写上毛主席语录。
    安徽淮南九龙岗煤矿供应站工人  皮金志
