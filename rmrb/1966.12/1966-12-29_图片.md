Title: Yunnan Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Laos
Author:
Date: 1966-12-29
Edition: 5

During their visit to the liberated areas of Laos, the Yunnan Acrobatic Troupe, undeterred by the bombing and strafing of American imperialist planes, went deep into the front lines to perform for the heroic soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army.
