Title: Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams
Author: Tao Shimian
Date: 1966-12-08
Edition: 6

	Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams
	Wuhan Metallurgical Construction Machinery Repair Factory

	Mao Zedong's portrait held high, language books on shoulders,
	The drums and gongs are beating loudly, revolutionary songs fill the sky,
	A golden flag with shining characters, we are:
	Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams!

	Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams,
	Propagating Mao Zedong Thought without fatigue.
	Team members stand on the streets, crowds gather around.
	We propagate Chairman Mao's words,
	Everyone listens with joy in their hearts,
	Actively learn and apply Chairman Mao's works,
	With red hearts and boundless energy!

	We sing of the heroes of the No. 321 Drilling Team,
	Everyone's face is wet with tears!
	Everyone learns from the heroes, the heroic spirit is most precious!
	...

	"Resolutely carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end!"
	One slogan, a thousand voices follow,
	A thousand voices like thunder!
	All sing together: "Navigating the seas relies on the helmsman..."
	Workers, peasants, and soldiers swear to defend Mao Zedong Thought to the death!
	Crowds on the streets, like tidal waves,
	Excited emotions, arms raised high,
	Ten thousand words, one sentence: Long live Chairman Mao!
	Ten thousand times, long live Chairman Mao!

### 毛泽东思想宣传队
陶世绵
1966-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东思想宣传队
    武汉冶金建筑机械修造厂　陶世绵毛主席像，手中举，语录板，肩上背，喜锣猛敲鼓猛擂，革命战歌满天飞，一杆大旗金字闪，咱们是：毛泽东思想宣传队！毛泽东思想宣传队，宣传毛泽东思想不知累。队员街头站，行人层层围。咱宣传毛主席的话，人人听得开心扉，活学活用毛主席著作，心红干劲足！咱歌唱三二一一一钻井队的英雄，人人脸挂两行泪！英雄榜样人人学，英雄精神最可贵！……“坚决把无产阶级文化大革命进行
  到底！”一声口号万声随，万声口号似春雷！齐高歌：“大海航行靠舵手……”工农兵誓死把毛泽东思想捍卫！街上人，似潮水，群情激，高举臂，万语千言合一句：毛主席万岁！万万岁！
