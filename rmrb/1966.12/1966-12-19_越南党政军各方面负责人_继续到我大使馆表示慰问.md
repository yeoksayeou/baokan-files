Title: Vietnamese Party, Government, and Military Leaders Continue to Visit Chinese Embassy to Express Condolences

Author:

Date: 1966-12-19

Edition: 6

Vietnamese Party, government, and military leaders continue to visit the Chinese Embassy to express condolences.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 18th — Nguyen Van Tho, Head of the Vietnam Workers' Party's International Liaison Department, Nguyen Van Tien, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nguyen Van Tho, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Nguyen Van Tho, Deputy Minister of Defense and Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, Nguyen Van Tho, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Army, and other leaders from various sectors in Vietnam have, in recent days, successively visited the Chinese Embassy, which was bombed by American aggressor planes. They expressed heartfelt condolences to Chinese Ambassador Zhu Qifeng and the entire embassy staff.

Many citizens of Hanoi, filled with immense anger towards American imperialism, have flocked to the Chinese Embassy and the Xinhua News Agency's Hanoi branch to inspect the scene of the bombing by American aggressor planes. They expressed to their Chinese comrades: The Vietnamese people will stand even more closely united with the Chinese people, fight together, and achieve victory together, thoroughly defeating the vicious American imperialism.

Leaders of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and representatives from various circles of overseas Chinese have also visited the Chinese Embassy and Xinhua branch in recent days to express their condolences.

Those who visited the Chinese Embassy to express condolences also included comrades from the Albanian Embassy in Vietnam and diplomatic officials from several other countries.

### 越南党政军各方面负责人  继续到我大使馆表示慰问

1966-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越南党政军各方面负责人
    继续到我大使馆表示慰问
    新华社河内十八日电　越南劳动党对外联络部部长春水，越南外交部副部长黄文进，越中友好协会副会长孙光阀，越南国防部副部长兼人民军副总参谋长陈参，人民军总政治局副主任范玉茂，以及越南其他方面的一些负责人，最近几天先后到遭受美国强盗飞机轰炸的中国大使馆，向中国大使朱其文和使馆全体人员表示亲切慰问。
    河内市许多市民，也怀着对美帝国主义无比愤怒的心情，纷纷前往中国大使馆和新华社河内分社察看被美国强盗飞机轰炸后的现场。他们向中国同志表示：越南人民将更加紧密地同中国人民团结在一起，战斗在一起，胜利在一起，彻底打败凶恶的美帝国主义。
    越南华侨联合总会的负责人和各界华侨代表，这几天也到中国大使馆和新华分社表示慰问。
    到中国大使馆慰问的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻越南大使馆的同志以及其他一些国家的外交官员。
