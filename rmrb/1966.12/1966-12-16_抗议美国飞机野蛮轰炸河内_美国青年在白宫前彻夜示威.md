Title: Protest Against U.S. Bombing of Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-12-16
Edition: 5

	On the evening of the 14th, dozens of American youth (mostly university students) held an all-night protest in front of the White House, condemning the serious crime of U.S. aircraft repeatedly and savagely bombing the residential areas of the capital city of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi.

### 抗议美国飞机野蛮轰炸河内  美国青年在白宫前彻夜示威

1966-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    抗议美国飞机野蛮轰炸河内
    美国青年在白宫前彻夜示威
    新华社十五日讯　华盛顿消息：数十名美国青年（其中主要是大学生）十四日晚上在白宫门前举行彻夜示威，抗议美国飞机连续野蛮轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内市区的严重罪行。
