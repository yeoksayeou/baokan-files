Title: Arm Yourself with Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Gong Ren Zhen Yang
Date: 1966-12-20
Edition: 6

    Arm yourself with Mao Zedong Thought,
    Worker Zhen Yang
    Be it mountains high as the sky,
    Or rocks hard as iron,
    A miner’s shout echoes through the mountains,
    The sound of cannon fire and gunpowder,
    Ten thousand feet of mountains turn into plains!
    Water from the well pours down from above,
    Mud splashes all over the body;
    The drill in the hand spins and turns,
    The heart swells with passion!
    To make revolution,
    A thousand hardships and ten thousand sufferings bring joy.
    Day and night without rest,
    I swing my shovel to make revolution,
    Passion splashes onto the rocks,
    Surges like the Yangtze River,
    The red heart forever faces the Party.
    Chairman Mao’s quotations shine with golden light,
    Every word is brighter than the red sun,
    The sunlight warms the heart.
    The red heart faces the Party to make revolution,
    Busy mining deep within the earth!
    Though in the mine shafts, one’s heart is with the world,
    Arm yourself with Mao Zedong Thought!

### 毛泽东思想来武装
振扬
1966-12-20
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东思想来武装	工人　振扬
    管你山高入云天，
    管你石硬比铁坚；
    矿山工人一声吼，
    炮声响处滚硝烟，
    万丈高山化平川！
    井水哗哗头上淋，
    泥浆飞迸身上溅；
    手下风钻突突转，
    胸中激情起波澜！
    干革命，
    千辛万苦心里欢。
    日不停，夜不停，
    我挥铁锹干革命，
    激情泼在矿石上，
    奔腾澎湃胜长江，
    红心永远向着党。
    毛主席语录放金光，
    句句胜过红太阳，
    阳光温暖照心房。
    红心向党干革命，
    地球深处采矿忙！
    身在井巷怀天下，
    毛泽东思想来武装！
