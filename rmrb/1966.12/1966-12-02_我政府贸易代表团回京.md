Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-02
Edition: 6

	Chinese Government Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing

	Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Led by Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, the Chinese Government Trade Delegation returned to Beijing by airplane this afternoon after signing the 1967 barter agreement between China and Ceylon in Ceylon.

	Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang and Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Ceylon Embassy in China, Korus, among others.

### 我政府贸易代表团回京

1966-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团回京
    新华社一日讯　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团，在锡兰签订了中锡一九六七年换货议定书后，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章和锡兰驻中国大使馆临时代办柯鲁斯等。
