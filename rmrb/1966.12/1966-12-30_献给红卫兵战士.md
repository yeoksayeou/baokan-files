Title: In Praise of the Red Guards
Author: Su Wan Tuan
Date: 1966-12-30
Edition: 5

Laotian writer Su Wan Tuan recently wrote this poem with great enthusiasm and personally transcribed it in Laotian script onto a red silk banner, requesting that the Chinese Economic and Cultural Mission in Laos convey it to the Chinese Red Guards.

	Hail, brothers and sisters,
	Red Guards advancing with the red flag of victory,
	The red flag of Mao Zedong Thought,
	Shaking the heavens and earth, inspiring the people,
	Imperialism trembles in fear, revolutionaries rejoice.
	Class struggle liberates the workers,
	Destroy the exploitative capitalist system.
	Marx exposed all the darkness,
	Showing us the path forward.
	Lenin enriched revolutionary theory,
	The working class takes the lead,
	United with the peasants in struggle,
	Overthrow the wicked capitalists.
	Today, you take it a step further,
	With Uncle Mao's theory, root out the weeds,
	Sweep away those who try to restore the old system,
	Hold high the red flag and march on!
	The blood of the warriors on the Steel Peak and Phou Kout Mountain,
	Dyes the revolutionary red flag,
	Laos and China, brothers forever in struggle,
	Let the red flag fly even brighter.

### 献给红卫兵战士
苏万吞
1966-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    献给红卫兵战士
    老挝　苏万吞
  老挝作家苏万吞最近满怀激情地写了这首诗，并亲笔将诗用老挝文抄写在一面红绸锦旗上，请我国驻老挝经济文化代表团转送给中国红卫兵。
    欢呼啊，兄弟姊妹们，
    高举胜利红旗前进的毛泽东思想红卫兵，
    声震天地，气壮山河，
    帝国主义恐慌万状，革命人民鼓舞欢欣。
    阶级斗争解放劳动者，
    摧毁吃人的资本主义制度。
    马克思揭露一切黑暗，
    为我们指明前进的道路。
    列宁丰富了革命的理论，
    工人阶级掌握领导，
    联合农民进行斗争，
    把万恶的资本家打倒。
    今天，你们更进一步，
    用毛伯伯的理论斩草除根，
    横扫企图复辟旧制度的狂人，
    高举红旗不断前进！
    钢峰富科特山上战士的鲜血，
    涂染着革命的红旗，
    老、中两国兄弟永远共同斗争，
    让红旗更加鲜艳。
