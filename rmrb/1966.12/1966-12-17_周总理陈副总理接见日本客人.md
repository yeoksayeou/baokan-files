Author:
Date: 1966-12-17
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Receive Japanese Guests

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th—Premier Zhou Enlai this evening received the leader of the Japanese Economic Delegation, Kawai Yoshio, and had a friendly conversation with him. Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi and others.

Xinhua News Agency, December 15th—Vice Premier Chen Yi this morning received the leader of the Japanese Economic Delegation, Kawai Yoshio, and had a friendly conversation with him.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi and others. (Accompanying photo)

December 16th, Premier Zhou Enlai received the leader of the Japanese Economic Delegation, Kawai Yoshio (third from the left). Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 周总理陈副总理接见日本客人

1966-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理陈副总理接见日本客人
    新华社十六日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见日本经济界代表团团长河合良成，同他进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有廖承志等。
    新华社十五日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了日本经济界代表团团长河合良成，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有廖承志等。（附图片）
