Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-02
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with members of the Burmese Film Delegation, led by team leader U Min So and deputy team leader U Tin Aye, this morning. He had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Culture Li Qi and others. U Sin Nao, the Burmese Ambassador to China, was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见缅甸客人

1966-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见缅甸客人
    据新华社一日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由团长吴敏索、副团长丁埃率领的缅甸电影考察团的成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有文化部副部长李琦等。缅甸驻中国大使信瓦瑙也在座。
