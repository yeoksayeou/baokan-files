Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three More U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-12-17
Edition: 6

According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam bravely shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on December 5 and 9. On December 11, the militia of Le Thuy County in Quang Binh Province also shot down one U.S. aircraft.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机三架

1966-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民又击落美机三架
    新华社河内十五日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南北方义安省军民在五日和九日英勇击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。广平省丽水县民兵十一日也击落美机一架。
