Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-12-18
Edition: 5

The Soviet revisionist leading clique pursues a policy of Soviet-American cooperation to dominate the world. In the international communist movement and the national liberation movement, it engages in activities of splitting, sabotage, and subversion, actively serving the interests of American imperialism.
