    Lebanon Forms New Government Led by Karami
    Lebanon formed a new cabinet on the 7th with Rashid Karami as the Prime Minister. The previous cabinet led by Yafi resigned on the 2nd. Lebanese President Charles Helou requested former Prime Minister Karami to form a new cabinet on the 3rd.

    Syria Seizes Foreign Oil Company Assets
    The Syrian government has decided to seize all movable and immovable property in Syria owned by the Iraq Petroleum Company, which is backed by British, American, French, and Dutch capital. This action is in response to the company's unreasonable delay in paying Syria's oil transit fees.

    The Syrian government also decided to increase the transit fees for foreign oil starting from January 1, 1966.

    US Imperialists Increase Troops in Vietnam to 363,000
    Another batch of 1,700 U.S. aggressor troops landed in Vung Tau, southeast of Saigon, on the 10th.

    According to Western news agencies, these 1,700 U.S. troops are the first contingent of the 199th Light Cavalry Brigade to arrive in South Vietnam.

    The arrival of this batch of troops has increased the total number of U.S. troops in Vietnam to over 363,000. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

1966-12-11
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    黎巴嫩组成以卡拉米为首的政府
    黎巴嫩在七日组成以拉希德·卡拉米为总理的新内阁。以雅非为首的前内阁在二日提出辞职。黎巴嫩总统夏尔·赫卢在三日要求前总理卡拉米组织新内阁。
    叙利亚没收外国石油公司的财产
    叙利亚政府决定没收由英国、美国、法国和荷兰资本所拥有的伊拉克石油公司在叙利亚的全部动产和不动产，作为这家石油公司无理拖欠叙利亚石油过境费的抵偿。
    叙利亚政府还决定自一九六六年一月一日起提高外国石油过境费。
    美帝侵越炮灰增至三十六万三千
    又一批美国侵略军一千七百名已于十日在西贡东南的头顿登陆。
    据西方通讯社报道，这一千七百名美军是第一九九轻骑兵旅到达南越的第一批侵略军。
    这批美军的到达使侵越美军总数增加到三十六万三千多人。（据新华社讯）
