Title: Chinese Ocean-going Vessel Arrives at Iraqi Port
Author:
Date: 1966-12-17
Edition: 5

	Chinese ocean-going cargo ship "Hangzhou" arrived at the Iraqi port of Basra on December 7th. This is the first time a Chinese ocean-going vessel has reached a port in the Arabian Gulf (Persian Gulf).

### 我国远洋轮船到伊拉克港口

1966-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    我国远洋轮船到伊拉克港口
    据新华社巴士拉十三日电　中国远洋货轮“杭州号”七日到达伊拉克港口巴士拉，这是中国远洋轮船第一次到达这个阿拉伯湾（波斯湾）的港口。
