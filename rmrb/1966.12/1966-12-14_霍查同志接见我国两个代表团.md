Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Two Chinese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 4

Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, received on December 12 the Chinese scientific and technological delegation led by Chen Weiji and the Chinese delegation of journalists led by Chai Yuren, who are visiting Albania. Comrade Enver Hoxha had a very cordial and friendly conversation with the Chinese guests.

Present at the reception were: Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Central Committee Secretary Ramiz Alia, Chairman of the Albanian side of the Sino-Albanian Joint Commission on Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Commission of Albania Petrit Muça, and Chairman of the Albanian Journalists' Association Fiqret Voga. Chinese Ambassador to Albania Xu Jian Guo was also present at the reception.

### 霍查同志接见我国两个代表团

1966-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见我国两个代表团
    新华社地拉那十三日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志十二日接见了正在阿尔巴尼亚访问的由陈维稷率领的中国科学技术代表团和由柴与人率领的中国新闻工作者代表团。霍查同志同中国客人们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记拉·阿利雅，中阿科学技术合作联合委员会阿方主席、阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普·穆拉，阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者协会主席菲·沃格利。接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国也在座。
