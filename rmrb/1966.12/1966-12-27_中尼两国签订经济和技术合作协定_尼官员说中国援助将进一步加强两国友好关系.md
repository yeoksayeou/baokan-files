Title: China and Nepal Sign Economic and Technological Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-12-27
Edition: 4

Nepalese official says Chinese aid will further strengthen friendly relations between the two countries

		Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu: The governments of Nepal and China signed an economic and technological cooperation agreement here on the evening of the 21st.

		The agreement stipulates that, to assist the Nepalese government in developing its economy, the Chinese government agrees to provide unconditional, interest-free aid to the Nepalese government from December 21, 1966, to December 31, 1971. The aforementioned aid will be provided in the form of complete sets of equipment, in installments, based on the capabilities of the Chinese government and the needs of the Nepalese government.

		Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Yang Gongsu, and Nepalese Secretary of the Ministry of Finance and Economic Planning, A.P. Panta, signed the agreement on behalf of the two governments.

		Nepalese Secretary of the Ministry of Finance and Economic Planning, A.P. Panta, said in his speech at the signing ceremony that this agreement is "a new, brilliant chapter in the history of Sino-Nepalese cooperation." He said that this aid will be of great help in achieving Nepal's planned targets. He also expressed gratitude to the Government of the People's Republic of China for providing Nepal with unconditional aid and for its concern for Nepal's economic development. He said that this aid will further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries. Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Yang Gongsu, in his speech, warmly praised the ever-growing Sino-Nepalese friendly relations.

### 中尼两国签订经济和技术合作协定  尼官员说中国援助将进一步加强两国友好关系

1966-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中尼两国签订经济和技术合作协定
    尼官员说中国援助将进一步加强两国友好关系
    新华社加德满都电　尼泊尔和中国两国政府二十一日晚在这里签订了经济和技术合作协定。
    协定规定，为了帮助尼泊尔政府发展经济，中国政府同意在一九六六年十二月二十一日到一九七一年十二月三十一日期间，向尼泊尔政府提供不附带任何条件的无偿援助。上述援助将根据中国政府的能力和尼泊尔政府的需要，以成套设备的方式分期提供。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素和尼泊尔政府财政和经济计划部秘书亚·普·潘特代表两国政府在协定上签字。
    尼泊尔财政和经济计划部秘书亚·普·潘特在签字仪式上讲话说，这项协定“是尼中合作史上新的光辉的一章”。他说：这项援助对于实现尼泊尔的计划指标有很大的帮助。他还说，尼泊尔政府对于中华人民共和国政府给予尼泊尔的不附带任何条件的援助以及中国对尼泊尔经济发展的关切表示感谢。这种援助将进一步加强两国的友好关系。中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素在讲话中热烈赞扬与日俱增的中尼友好关系。
