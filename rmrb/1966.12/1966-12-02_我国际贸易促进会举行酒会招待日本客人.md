Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Reception for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-02
Edition: 6

	China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Reception for Japanese Guests

	Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a reception tonight to welcome representatives from friendly Japanese trading companies.

	Vice Chairman Yong Longgui of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and Miyazaki Kichiro, representative of the friendly Japanese trading companies, both spoke at the reception. They jointly toasted to further development of Sino-Japanese friendship and trade.

	Representatives from China's trade sector and related fields, as well as Japanese peace activist Nishizonoe Koichi, attended the reception.

### 我国际贸易促进会举行酒会招待日本客人

1966-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    我国际贸易促进会举行酒会招待日本客人
    新华社一日讯　中国国际贸易促进会今晚举行酒会，招待日本友好商社的贸易界人士。
    中国国际贸易促进会副主任勇龙桂和日本友好商社代表宫崎幸一郎在酒会上讲了话，共祝中日友好和中日贸易获得进一步发展。
    中国贸易界和有关方面人士，日本和平人士西园寺公一，出席了酒会。
