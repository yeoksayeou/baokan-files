Title: Heartfelt Brotherly Thanks for the Warm Congratulations on the 22nd Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 1

	The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Chairman Mao Zedong received a telegram from the First Secretary of the Party of Labour of Albania, Comrade Enver Hoxha, on December 10. In the telegram, Hoxha expressed his heartfelt brotherly thanks to Mao Zedong for his warm congratulations on the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	Beijing
	Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Chairman Mao Zedong:

	On behalf of the Albanian people, the Party of Labour of Albania, and in my personal capacity, I extend my heartfelt brotherly thanks to you and, through you, to the fraternal Chinese people and the glorious Chinese Communist Party for your warm congratulations on the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

	Under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, headed by you, Comrade Mao Zedong, the great Chinese people have built their country into a powerful socialist state with a developed modern industry, collective socialist agriculture, advanced revolutionary culture and science, and an invincible defense force. Great People's China has become an unshakable bastion of revolution, socialism, and peace, and a reliable pillar for the peoples of all countries in their struggle for freedom and independence against imperialism and colonialism.

	The glorious Chinese Communist Party has consistently and unyieldingly held high the banner of Marxism-Leninism, waging a resolute and uncompromising struggle against imperialism headed by the United States and modern revisionism centered around the Soviet revisionist clique. It has made a contribution of world-historic significance to the cause of socialism and revolution.

	The fraternal Chinese people, with their surging revolutionary spirit and the power to shake all enemies of socialism, are successfully carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to eliminate any possibility of the emergence of revisionism and the restoration of capitalism in China. The Party of Labour of Albania and the entire Albanian people wholeheartedly congratulate the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and sincerely wish the glorious Chinese Communist Party and the great Chinese people greater achievements on the radiant road to socialism and communism.

	We sincerely wish that the unbreakable fighting friendship and brotherly cooperation between our two peoples and parties will be further consolidated and developed in the struggle against our common enemies—the American imperialists and the Khrushchev revisionists—and for the victory of our common cause—revolution, socialism, and communism.

	Long live the unbreakable friendship between our two parties and peoples!

	Enver Hoxha
	First Secretary, Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania
	December 10, 1966, Tirana

	The Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai, received a telegram from the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Comrade Mehmet Shehu, on December 10. In the telegram, Shehu expressed his heartfelt thanks to Zhou Enlai for his warm congratulations on the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	Beijing
	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai:

	On behalf of the Albanian people, the Government of the People's Republic of Albania, and in my personal capacity, I extend my heartfelt thanks to you and, through you, to the great Chinese people and the Government of the People's Republic of China for your warm congratulations on the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

	Under the far-sighted leadership of the glorious Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, the great Chinese people, numbering 700 million, have achieved brilliant successes in the building of socialism on all fronts, turning their country into a powerful socialist state with unquestionable international prestige and influence, into a bastion of socialism, Asia, and world peace that cannot be shaken. The Chinese people are successfully carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. This is a glorious page in the history of the Chinese people, aimed at thoroughly eliminating the danger of revisionism and the restoration of capitalism in China.

	We sincerely wish the heroic Chinese people, under the wise leadership of the glorious Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, even greater successes in the building of socialism in their own country. We sincerely wish that the unbreakable friendship and fighting unity between our two peoples and countries will be further consolidated in the common struggle against imperialism headed by American imperialism and the Khrushchev revisionists, and for the victory of socialism and communism.

	Mehmet Shehu
	Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania
	December 10, 1966, Tirana

	It is also reported that the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Chen Yi, also received a telegram from the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, Comrade Nesti Nashe, on December 10. In the telegram, Nashe expressed his thanks to Chen Yi for his congratulations on the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

### 最衷心地兄弟般地感谢对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的热烈祝贺  恩维尔·霍查同志致电毛泽东同志  穆罕默德·谢胡主席致电周恩来总理

1966-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    最衷心地兄弟般地感谢对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的热烈祝贺
    恩维尔·霍查同志致电毛泽东同志
    穆罕默德·谢胡主席致电周恩来总理
    新华社十四日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志收到了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一书记霍查同志十日打来的电报，霍查同志在电报中表示最衷心地兄弟般地感谢毛泽东同志对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的热烈祝贺，电报全文如下：北京中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志：
    我谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，并以我个人名义，向您并通过您向兄弟的中国人民和光荣的中国共产党表示最衷心的兄弟般的感谢，感谢您在阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的时候对我的热烈祝贺。
    伟大的中国人民，在以您毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的马克思列宁主义的英明领导下，把自己的祖国建设成为一个具有发达的现代化工业，集体化的社会主义农业，先进的革命文化和科学以及不可摧毁的国防力量的强大的社会主义国家。伟大的人民中国已经成了革命、社会主义与和平的坚不可摧的堡垒，成了争取自由和独立，反对帝国主义和殖民主义的各国人民的可靠支柱。
    光荣的中国共产党，一贯坚定不移地高举战无不胜的马克思列宁主义旗帜，同以美国为首的帝国主义和以苏共叛徒集团为中心的现代修正主义进行坚决的和毫不妥协的斗争，对社会主义和革命的命运作出了具有世界历史意义的贡献。
    兄弟的中国人民，以冲天的革命干劲和震撼社会主义一切敌人的威力，正胜利地开展着铲除在中国产生修正主义和复辟资本主义的任何可能的无产阶级文化大革命。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和全体阿尔巴尼亚人民衷心地祝贺无产阶级文化大革命，并全心全意地预祝光荣的中国共产党和伟大的中国人民在社会主义和共产主义的光辉道路上取得更大的成就。
    衷心祝愿我们两国人民和两党之间的牢不可破的战斗友谊和兄弟合作，在反对共同敌人——美帝国主义者和赫鲁晓夫修正主义者，争取我们的共同事业——革命、社会主义和共产主义胜利的斗争中日益巩固和发展。
    祝我们两党和两国兄弟人民之间的牢不可破的友谊与日俱增，万古长青！
              阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记
                    恩维尔·霍查
                          一九六六年十二月十日于地拉那
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志收到了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志十日打来的电报，谢胡同志在电报中表示衷心地感谢周恩来同志对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的亲切祝贺，电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    我谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，并以我个人的名义，向您，并通过您向伟大的中国人民和中华人民共和国政府对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的亲切祝贺表示衷心的感谢。
    伟大的七亿中国人民，在以毛泽东同志为首的光荣的中国共产党的卓有远见的领导下，在社会主义建设的各条战线上取得了辉煌的成就，把自己祖国建设成为一个享有不容置疑的国际声誉和威望的强大的社会主义国家，建设成为社会主义、亚洲和世界和平的坚不可摧的堡垒。中国人民正在胜利地开展无产阶级文化大革命。这是中国人民历史上光辉的一页，它的目的是彻底铲除在中国产生修正主义和复辟资本主义的危险。
    衷心地祝愿英雄的中国人民在以毛泽东同志为首的光荣的中国共产党的英明领导下，在自己祖国的社会主义建设中取得更加辉煌的成就。衷心地祝愿我们两国人民和两国之间的牢不可破的友谊和战斗团结，在反对以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义，反对赫鲁晓夫修正主义者，争取社会主义和共产主义胜利的共同斗争中日益巩固。
                阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席
                              穆罕默德·谢胡
                          一九六六年十二月十日于地拉那
    又讯：中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅同志也收到了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长奈斯蒂·纳赛同志十日打来的电报，纳赛同志在电报中感谢陈毅同志对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年的祝贺。
