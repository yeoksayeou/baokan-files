Title: In Support of the Panamanian People's Anti-Imperialist Patriotic Struggle

Author:

Date: 1966-12-15

Edition: 1

American imperialism is rampant everywhere, placing itself in a position of enmity against all the peoples of the world, making itself increasingly isolated. The tide of the world's peoples opposing American aggressors is irresistible. The struggle of the world's peoples against American imperialism and its lackeys will certainly achieve even greater victories.
