Title: Chinese Science and Technology Delegation Leaves Albania to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-12-23
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 21 - The Chinese delegation to the China-Albania Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee, led by the Chinese side's chairman and deputy minister of the Textile Industry Department, Chen Weiji, departed Tirana by airplane on the 20th and returned to China.

### 我科技代表团离阿回国

1966-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    我科技代表团离阿回国
    据新华社地拉那二十一日电　由中国阿尔巴尼亚科学技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、纺织工业部副部长陈维稷率领的中国科学技术代表团，二十日乘飞机离开地拉那回国。
