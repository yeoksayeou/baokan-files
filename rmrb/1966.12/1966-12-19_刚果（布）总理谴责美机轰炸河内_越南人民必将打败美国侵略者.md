Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Prime Minister Condemns U.S. Bombing of Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-12-19
Edition: 6

The Congolese (Brazzaville) Prime Minister Noumazalaye condemned the U.S. imperialists for sending aircraft to continuously bomb the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi, calling it a violent act. He said, "The Congolese (Brazzaville) people firmly believe that the Vietnamese people, whom we admire, will definitely defeat the U.S. aggressors and their puppets—the American lackeys in the region." He made these remarks at the closing ceremony of the National Assembly's budget meeting.

### 刚果（布）总理谴责美机轰炸河内  越南人民必将打败美国侵略者

1966-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）总理谴责美机轰炸河内
    越南人民必将打败美国侵略者
    据新华社布拉柴维尔十七日电　刚果（布）总理努马扎莱十七日在这里谴责美帝国主义者派遣飞机连续轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内的暴行说：刚果（布）
  “深信，我们所感激的越南人民必将打败美国侵略者及其傀儡—美国佬在当地的走狗”。他是在国民议会预算会议的闭幕式上这样说的。
