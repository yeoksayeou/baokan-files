Title: Red Guards from Shanghai's Nanshi District Schools Follow Chairman Mao's Long March
Author:
Date: 1966-12-01
Edition: 2

    A delegation called the "Follow Chairman Mao's Long March Delegation," composed of Red Guards from ten middle schools in Shanghai's Nanshi District, is propagating Mao Zedong Thought to commune members in the fields.
