Title: Study and Apply "Old Three Articles" Lecture Meeting
Author: Yang Yiqiu
Date: 1966-12-13
Edition: 3

In the mass movement to study Chairman Mao's works, the people of Jiaoqiao Commune in Hai'an County, Jiangsu, have resolved to achieve new heights in learning and applying what they have learned.
