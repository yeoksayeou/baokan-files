Title: The Publication and Distribution of the Single-Volume Edition of "Seizing New Victories"
Author:
Date: 1966-12-18
Edition: 1

The single-volume edition of the editorial "Seizing New Victories" from the 15th issue of *Red Flag* magazine has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from the 18th, it will be distributed in Beijing and will be gradually distributed nationwide.

### 《夺取新的胜利》单行本出版发行

1966-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    《夺取新的胜利》单行本出版发行
    新华社十七日讯　《红旗》杂志第十五期社论《夺取新的胜利》已经由人民出版社出版单行本，从十八日起在北京发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
