Title: Tribute to the Greatest Leader of Our Time
Author: Sha Yi Du
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 5

Dear comrades,

Although I am far from you, my heart is with you and with China. I request that you convey my highest respects to the greatest leader of our time, Chairman Mao. I love Chairman Mao. In my home, I have a photograph of Chairman Mao. Every morning before I go to work, I always look at Chairman Mao's portrait. I wish Chairman Mao good health and longevity.

I pay my highest respects to the great Chairman Mao!

Long live Chairman Mao!

Upper Volta, Sha Yi Du

### 向我们时代最伟大的领袖致敬
沙依杜
1966-12-04
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    向我们时代最伟大的领袖致敬
    亲爱的同志们，我离你们虽然很远，但我的心是和你们连在一起，和中国连在一起的。我请求你们代我向我们时代最伟大的领袖——毛主席致以最崇高的敬礼。我热爱毛主席，我家里有毛主席的照片，每天早上我上班前，总要看看毛主席的像，我祝愿毛主席身体健康、长寿。
    向伟大的毛主席致以最崇高的敬意！
    毛主席万岁！
                    上沃尔特　沙依杜
