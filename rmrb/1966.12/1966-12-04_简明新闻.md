Arab ships protest against U.S. warships

In support of Israel's aggression against Arab countries, U.S. imperialism has brazenly dispatched the Sixth Fleet into the eastern Mediterranean, threatening the Palestinian people and the Jordanian people who are engaged in revolutionary struggle. On November 30, when eight warships of this fleet sailed eastward through the Suez Canal, Arab ships on the canal expressed their protest in various ways.

These Arab ships lowered their flags and hung up placards with inverted images of the U.S. "Statue of Liberty."

14,000 Indian police stage hunger strike

On November 29, 14,000 Indian police officers staged a symbolic hunger strike in New Delhi to protest the Indian government's ban on their unionization. On November 5, 10,000 police officers in New Delhi had held a demonstration, chanting slogans demanding recognition of their union.

West German youths protest U.S. aggression in Vietnam

On the evening of November 28, more than a thousand West German youth students demonstrated in front of the U.S. Consulate in Munich to protest U.S. aggression in Vietnam. Despite the heavy obstruction by a large number of U.S. occupying troops and West German police armed with clubs, the demonstrators rushed to the front of the U.S. Consulate, shouting, "Hang Johnson!" "Yankees, get out!"

U.S. loses $100 million in gold in one week

According to an announcement by the U.S. Federal Reserve Bank on December 1, the U.S. lost another $100 million in gold in the week ending November 30, reducing its total gold reserves to $1,315,900,000, the lowest since August 1938. Including this week, the U.S. has lost a total of $152 million in gold in November.

### 简明新闻

1966-12-04
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    阿拉伯船只向美国军舰表示抗议
    美帝国主义为了支持以色列侵略阿拉伯国家，竟公然派出第六舰队进入东地中海，威胁巴勒斯坦人民和正在进行革命斗争的约旦人民。十一月三十日，当这支舰队的八艘军舰从地中海向东通过苏伊士运河时，在运河上的阿拉伯船只纷纷向它们表示抗议。
    这些阿拉伯船只降下了旗帜，并且挂起了标语牌，上面画着倒置的美国“自由女神像”。
    印度一万四千警察进行绝食斗争
    一万四千名印度警察十一月二十九日在新德里进行象征性绝食斗争，抗议印度当局禁止他们组织工会。十一月五日，新德里曾有一万名警察示威，高呼口号，要求承认他们成立的工会。
    西德青年示威抗议美国侵略越南
    一千多名西德青年学生十一月二十八日晚在慕尼黑美国领事馆前举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南。示威青年不顾大批手持棍棒的美国占领军和西德警察的重重阻拦，冲到美国领事馆跟前，高呼：“吊死约翰逊！”“美国佬滚出去！”
    美国黄金一周内又流失一亿美元
    据美国联邦储备银行一日宣布，在截至十一月三十日为止的一周内，美国又流失黄金一亿美元，使它的黄金储备总和只剩下一百三十一亿五千九百万美元，降到了一九三八年八月以来的最低点。连这次在内，美国在十一月份一共流失黄金达一亿五千二百万美元。
   （据新华社讯）
