Title: Delegation of the Afro-Chinese Friendship Association Visits Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 5

The delegation of the Afro-Chinese Friendship Association left Beijing today to visit and tour other regions. The president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Chu Tu-nan, accompanied them. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Sung I-p'ing, Ho Piao, and others.

### 阿中友协代表团去外地访问

1966-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    阿中友协代表团去外地访问
    新华社三日讯　阿中友协代表团今天离开北京赴外地参观访问。中国人民对外文化友好协会会长楚图南陪同前往。到机场送行的有宋一平、贺彪等。
