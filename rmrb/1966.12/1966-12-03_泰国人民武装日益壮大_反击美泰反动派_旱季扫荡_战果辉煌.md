Title: Thai People's Armed Forces Grow Stronger, Achieve Brilliant Results in Countering the "Dry Season Sweep" by U.S. and Thai Reactionaries
Author:
Date: 1966-12-03
Edition: 4

The central task and highest form of revolution is to seize political power through armed struggle. This Marxist-Leninist revolutionary principle is universally correct, whether in China or abroad. According to recent broadcasts by the "Voice of the Thai People" radio station, the Thai people's armed forces, which enjoy widespread support from the masses, have grown stronger in their struggle against U.S. imperialism and its lackey, the Thanom-Kittikachorn clique. They have smashed the enemy's repeated "encirclements" and achieved brilliant victories.

The radio station reported that, according to incomplete statistics, from the beginning of this year to mid-November, the people's armed forces in northeastern and southern Thailand engaged in 120 battles with the enemy, killing and wounding over 300 enemy personnel, including more than 20 officers and policemen.

The "Voice of the Thai People" radio station recently also reported that in early November, the U.S. and Thai reactionaries launched a "dry season sweep." The people's armed forces engaged in more than three battles with the military and police of the Thanom-Kittikachorn clique, killing and wounding more than 20 reactionary soldiers and police. The radio station commented on the victories of the Thai people's armed forces, saying that the victories achieved in this counterattack against the so-called "dry season sweep" once again thoroughly exposed the paper tiger nature of imperialism and the Thai reactionaries.

### 泰国人民武装日益壮大  反击美泰反动派“旱季扫荡”战果辉煌

1966-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国人民武装日益壮大
    反击美泰反动派“旱季扫荡”战果辉煌
    革命的中心任务和最高形式是武装夺取政权，是战争解决问题。这个马克思列宁主义的革命原则是普遍地对的，不论在中国在外国，一概都是对的。据“泰国人民之声”电台最近广播，获得广大人民支持的泰国人民武装在反对美帝国主义及其走狗他侬—巴博集团的斗争中越战越强，粉碎了敌人的多次“围剿”，取得了辉煌的胜利。
    电台说，据不完全的统计，从今年初到十一月中旬，泰国东北部和南部的人民武装同敌人进行了一百二十次战斗，打死打伤敌人三百多名，其中有军官和警官二十多名。
      “泰国人民之声”电台最近还报道，美、泰反动派在十一月初发动“旱季扫荡”，人民武装同他侬—巴博集团的军警进行了三次以上的战斗，共打死打伤二十多名反动军警。电台就泰国人民武装取得的胜利发表评论说，人民武装在这次反击所谓“旱季扫荡”中所取得的胜利，又一次彻底戳穿了帝国主义和泰国反动派的纸老虎本质。
