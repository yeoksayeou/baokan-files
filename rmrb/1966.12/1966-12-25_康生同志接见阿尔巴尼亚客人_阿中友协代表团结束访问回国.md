Title: Comrade Kang Sheng Receives Albanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-25
Edition: 5

	Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Mohammad Dhanaye, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "July 10th" magazine of the Albanian People's Army, and had a cordial talk with him today.

	Present at the reception were Hu Qizhi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and Chen Weifan, Deputy Director of the News Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated December 24, the delegation of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by P. Dode, a member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Korça District, concluded its visit to our country and departed from Beijing by plane this afternoon.

	Relevant officials Liu Xiao, Chu Tuonan, and He Biao, as well as over 300 Red Guards and people from various circles in the capital, went to the airport to warmly see off the Albanian comrades who stand on the front line of opposing revisionism.

	Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli also went to the airport to see them off.

### 康生同志接见阿尔巴尼亚客人  阿中友协代表团结束访问回国

1966-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

    康生同志接见阿尔巴尼亚客人
    阿中友协代表团结束访问回国
    新华社二十四日讯　中共中央政治局常委、人大常委会副委员长康生，今天接见阿尔巴尼亚人民军机关刊物《七月十日》杂志副总编辑穆罕默德·达纳耶，同他进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主任胡痴，外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆。
    据新华社二十四日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、科尔察区人民会议执行委员会主席彼·多德率领的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    有关方面负责人刘晓、楚图南、贺彪，以及首都红卫兵和各界群众三百多人，前往机场热烈欢送站在反修前线的阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也前往机场送行。
