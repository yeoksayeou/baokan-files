Title: The Liberation Army of South Vietnam Strikes Consecutively, Annihilating Over a Thousand Enemy Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 4

The armed forces of liberation in the provinces of Ba Dinh, Giong Trang, Can Tho, My Tho, Tien Giang, and Sa Dec in South Vietnam have recently launched consecutive attacks against the enemy, annihilating over 1,100 enemy soldiers.

According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the armed forces of liberation in Ba Dinh Province, on the night of December 9, attacked the enemy's military training school located two kilometers from Ba Dinh City. They completely annihilated two enemy battalions undergoing training and one battalion stationed there, killing and wounding over 300 enemy soldiers, capturing 132, and seizing a large quantity of weapons and military supplies.

The armed forces of liberation in Giong Trang Province, on November 23, bravely ambushed three enemy battalions conducting a "sweeping" operation in the village of Binh Xuan, Giong Trang County. They killed 230 enemy soldiers and wounded over 80.

The guerrilla forces and local troops in Chau Thanh County, Can Tho Province, on November 14, attacked the base of the enemy's "pacification" forces in the village of Long Soan. They killed and wounded 135 enemy soldiers.

The local troops in Chau Thanh County, My Tho Province, on the night of November 18, attacked the enemy's military academy in the town of Ben Tre. They killed, wounded, and captured 46 enemy soldiers.

The guerrilla forces and local troops in Phu Cat County, Tien Giang Province, on November 20, launched a fierce attack against a battalion of invading American troops that had just been air-dropped. They annihilated 60 American soldiers on the spot. On November 29, the guerrilla forces in the village of Dong My Thuan, De Nhiem County, attacked an enemy commando battalion at the Co Hoa outpost, killing and wounding 40 enemy soldiers.

The guerrilla forces and local troops in the villages of Dong Tan Nhon, Phu Long, Tan, and An Khe, Chau Thanh County, Sa Dec Province, over two days from November 29 to 30, killed and wounded over 100 enemy soldiers conducting a "sweeping" operation. They shot down one enemy aircraft, damaged four, sank six enemy landing boats, and destroyed and damaged one armored vehicle each.

According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the guerrilla forces and local troops in the provinces of Quang Ngai and Phu Yen in South Vietnam have recently achieved new victories in their anti-"sweeping" battles.

The guerrilla forces and local troops in Quang Ngai Province, from November 19 to 25, thoroughly smashed the large-scale "sweeping" operation launched by seven enemy battalions against the villages of Nghia Le, Nghia Thang, and Nghia Ky in Nghia Hiep County. They annihilated 284 enemy soldiers, shot down and damaged six enemy aircraft, and destroyed and damaged six enemy armored vehicles.

The local troops and guerrilla forces in the counties of Ham Thu, Le Hong Phong, and Bac Binh in Phu Yen Province, in late November, bravely attacked the enemy conducting "sweeping" operations and those inside the "strategic hamlets." They annihilated over 100 enemy soldiers, including completely wiping out one enemy platoon, and shot down one helicopter. According to a supplementary report, the armed forces of liberation in Binh Dinh Province, on December 2, ambushed the U.S. 11th Independent Armored Cavalry Regiment on Highway 1. They destroyed 10 American M-41 tanks, killed and wounded over 100 invading American soldiers, and shot down four enemy aircraft.

### 南越解放军连续出击歼敌一千多名  广义省解放武装力量彻底粉碎七个营伪军的“扫荡”

1966-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    南越解放军连续出击歼敌一千多名
    广义省解放武装力量彻底粉碎七个营伪军的“扫荡”
    新华社河内十二日电越南南方巴地、鹅贡、芹苴、美萩、堤岸、沙沥等省解放武装力量，最近又连续袭击敌军，歼敌一千一百多名。
    据越南南方解放通讯社报道，巴地省解放武装力量，十二月九日夜间袭击离巴地市两公里的万劫伪军事训练学校，全歼正在受训的敌军两个连和驻守的敌军一个连，打死打伤敌人三百多名，俘虏一百三十二名，缴获大量武器和军用物资。
    鹅贡省解放武装力量，十一月二十三日在鹅贡县平春乡英勇截击前来“扫荡”的三个营伪军，打死敌人二百三十名，打伤八十多名。
    芹苴省周城县游击队和地方部队，十一月十四日袭击隆旋乡伪“绥靖”人员的驻地，打死打伤敌人一百三十五名。
    美萩省周城县地方部队十一月十八日夜间袭击槟城镇伪军官学校，打死、打伤和俘虏敌人四十六名。
    堤岸省芹得县福云乡游击队和地方部队十一月二十日猛烈袭击刚刚空投下来的一个营美国侵略军，当场消灭美军六十名。十一月二十九日，这个省德惠县东美盛乡游击队，袭击了协和据点的伪军别动队一个连，打死、打伤敌人四十名。
    沙沥省周城县东新润、富隆、和新、安庆乡游击队和地方部队，在十一月二十九日和三十日两天内，打死打伤前来“扫荡”的敌军一百多名，击落敌机一架，击伤四架，击沉敌人的登陆艇六艘，击毁、击伤装甲车各一辆。
    新华社河内十三日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广义和平顺等省游击队和地方部队在最近的反“扫荡”战斗中，又取得了新胜利。
    广义省游击队和地方部队，从十一月十九日到二十五日，彻底粉碎了七个营伪军向思义县义林、义胜、义其等乡发动的大规模“扫荡”，歼灭敌人二百八十四名，击落击伤敌机六架，击毁击伤敌人装甲车六辆。
    平顺省咸新、黎鸿峰、北平等县地方部队和游击队，在十一月下旬接连英勇地袭击了正在进行“扫荡”的和“战略村”里的敌人，共消灭敌人一百多名，其中包括全歼敌人一个排，击落直升飞机一架。另据补充报道，边和省解放武装力量，十二月二日在第一号公路上袭击美国第十一独立装甲团，共击毁美国M—41型坦克十辆，打死打伤美国侵略军一百多名，击落敌机四架。
