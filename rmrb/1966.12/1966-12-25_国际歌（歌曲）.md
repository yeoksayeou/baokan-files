Title: The Internationale (Song)
Author:
Date: 1966-12-25
Edition: 6

    The Internationale (Song)
    Note: The term “Internationale” is a transliteration from French. In many countries around the world, the translation of “The Internationale” uses this phonetic transcription. “The Internationale will be humanities’” means that the ideal of international communism must be realized throughout the world.
    Editor's Note: As a new year approaches, we are republishing “The Internationale.” In this magnificent battle hymn, the proletariat expresses its revolutionary heroism for the first time: “To create the happiness of humanity, it depends on us alone.” Our great leader Chairman Mao has always believed in the masses, relied on the masses, and respected the masses’ pioneering spirit, allowing the masses to educate themselves and liberate themselves. The great struggle of the Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao, has proven this unshakable truth. Let us hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and with even more magnificent spirit, sing the revolutionary battle hymn in the Cultural Revolution to achieve new victories! ### 国际歌（歌曲）

1966-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    国际歌（歌曲）
    注：“英特纳雄耐尔”一词是按法文音译的，世界上许多国家翻译的《国际歌》，对这个词都用音译。“英特纳雄耐尔就一定要实现”，意思是说国际共产主义的理想一定要在全世界实现。
    编者附记：在新的一年即将来临的时候，我们重新发表了《国际歌》。在这首雄伟的战歌里，无产阶级第一次抒发了自己的革命英雄主义气概：“要创造人类的幸福，全靠我们自己。”我们伟大的领袖毛主席，从来就是相信群众，依靠群众，尊重群众的首创精神，让群众自己教育自己，自己解放自己的。毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命的伟大斗争实践，证明了这个颠扑不破的真理。让我们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在无产阶级文化大革命中，用更豪迈的气概高唱革命战歌，去夺取新的胜利！（附图片）
