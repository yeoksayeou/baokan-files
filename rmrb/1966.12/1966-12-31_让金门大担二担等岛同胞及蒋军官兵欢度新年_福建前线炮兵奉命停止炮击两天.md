Title: Allowing Fellow Countrymen and Nationalist Soldiers on Jinmen, Dadan, and Erdan Islands to Celebrate the New Year
Author:
Date: 1966-12-31
Edition: 3

The spokesman for the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Fujian Front announced today: In order to allow fellow countrymen and Nationalist soldiers on the islands of Jinmen, Dadan, and Erdan to celebrate the New Year, the artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Fujian Front have been ordered to cease firing on December 31, 1966, and January 1, 1967, for two days, as a gesture of concern.

### 让金门大担二担等岛同胞及蒋军官兵欢度新年  福建前线炮兵奉命停止炮击两天

1966-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    让金门大担二担等岛同胞及蒋军官兵欢度新年
    福建前线炮兵奉命停止炮击两天
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人今天宣布：为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞及国民党军官兵欢度新年，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队，奉命在一九六六年十二月三十一日和一九六七年一月一日两个单日停止炮击，以示关怀。
