Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-12-07
Edition: 5

A delegation of Chinese women, led by Yang Yunyu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, consisting of seven members, departed Beijing today for a visit to Japan.

The delegation is visiting Japan at the invitation of the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) and the China-Japan Friendship Association, in accordance with the "1966 Agreement on Friendly Exchanges Between the Peoples of China and Japan" signed by the two organizations.

### 我妇女访日代表团赴日本访问

1966-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我妇女访日代表团赴日本访问
    新华社五日讯　以全国妇联书记处书记杨蕴玉为首的中国妇女访日代表团一行七人，今天离开北京前往日本访问。
    代表团是根据日中友协（正统）总部和中日友协所签订的“一九六六年中日两国人民友好往来议定书”，应邀前往日本进行友好访问的。
