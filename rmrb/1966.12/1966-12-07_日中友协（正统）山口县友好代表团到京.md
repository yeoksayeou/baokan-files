Title: Delegation from Yamaguchi Prefecture of the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-07
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the China-Japan Friendship Association, a delegation of four members from the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association of Yamaguchi Prefecture, led by Chairman Kofuzo Ichiro, arrived in Beijing by plane in the evening of the 5th.

Secretary-General Zhao Anbo of the China-Japan Friendship Association and Japanese peace activists in Beijing, including Saionji Koichi, welcomed them at the airport.

### 日中友协（正统）山口县友好代表团到京

1966-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    日中友协（正统）山口县友好代表团到京
    新华社六日讯　应中日友协邀请来我国访问的日中友协（正统）山口县友好代表团一行四人，在团长古谷庄一郎率领下，五日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    中日友协秘书长赵安博和日本在京和平人士西园寺公一等到机场欢迎。
