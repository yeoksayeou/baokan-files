Title: Sakamoto Tokumatsu and Others Withdraw from the Afro-Asian Research Institute Controlled by Followers of Soviet Revisionism
Author:
Date: 1966-12-27
Edition: 4

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 24th, a message from Tokyo states: Sakamoto Tokumatsu and 14 others, who persist in opposing imperialism and revisionism and upholding Sino-Japanese friendship, jointly issued a statement on the 19th, announcing their withdrawal from the Afro-Asian Research Institute controlled by followers of Soviet modern revisionism.

Those who, along with Sakamoto Tokumatsu, announced their withdrawal from this research institute include: Asakawa Kenji, Ikegami Katsunori, Umehara Hiroshi, Otani Jun, Kato Nagao, Kai Shizuma, Sato Shigeo, Sugiyama Ichiro, Tanaka Shuji, Furukawara, Matsuoka Nobuo, Yamashita Ryuzou, and Rōzan Yoshirō.

### 坂本德松等退出苏修追随者把持的亚非研究所

1966-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    坂本德松等退出苏修追随者把持的亚非研究所
    据新华社二十四日讯　东京消息：坚持反对帝国主义、修正主义，坚持日中友好的坂本德松等十四人，十九日联合发表声明，宣布退出由苏联现代修正主义的追随者所把持的亚非研究所。
    和坂本德松同时宣布退出这个研究所的有：浅川谦次、池上干德、梅原宏之、大类纯、加藤长雄、甲斐静马、佐藤重雄、杉山市平、田中脩二郎、古川原、松冈信夫、山下龙三、蜡山芳郎。
