Title: Powerless
Author: Wen Ping
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 4

In the southern part of Vietnam, the reactionary clique of the Saigon puppet regime is facing a severe crisis. The people of South Vietnam are rising up in a great revolutionary storm, and the puppet regime is on the verge of collapse. The U.S. imperialists, who are the main culprits behind the war of aggression in Vietnam, are also in a state of panic. They are trying to find a way out of the quagmire they have created.

The puppet regime in Saigon is in a state of disarray. The internal strife and external pressures have weakened its grip on power. The people of South Vietnam are united in their struggle against the puppet regime and the U.S. imperialists. The revolutionary forces are gaining strength day by day, and the puppet regime is losing its support base.

The U.S. imperialists are also facing a difficult situation. Their war of aggression in Vietnam has not only failed to achieve their goals but has also led to a backlash from the international community. The people of the world are increasingly aware of the brutal nature of the U.S. imperialists and their war crimes in Vietnam. The U.S. imperialists are isolated and their actions are widely condemned.

The situation in South Vietnam is rapidly changing. The people of South Vietnam are determined to liberate their country from the puppet regime and the U.S. imperialists. The revolutionary forces are confident of their victory and are ready to fight to the end. The U.S. imperialists and the puppet regime are in a state of panic and are trying to find a way out of the crisis they have created.

The people of South Vietnam are united in their struggle for liberation. They are determined to fight against the puppet regime and the U.S. imperialists until they achieve their goal of national independence and reunification. The revolutionary forces are gaining strength and are ready to face any challenges that come their way. The U.S. imperialists and the puppet regime are powerless in the face of the great revolutionary storm that is sweeping across South Vietnam.
