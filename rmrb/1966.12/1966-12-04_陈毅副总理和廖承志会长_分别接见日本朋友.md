Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Chairman Liao Chengzhi Separately Receive Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Chairman Liao Chengzhi
Separately Receive Japanese Friends

	BEIJING, Dec. 3 (Xinhua) — Vice Premier Chen Yi this morning received a delegation led by Vice Chairman Yamaura Mitsuo of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) on their fourth study tour. He had a friendly and cordial talk with them.

	Present at the reception were Secretary-General Zhao Anbo of the China-Japan Friendship Association and others. Japanese peace activist Nishizono Kiichi, who is in Beijing, was also present.

	BEIJING, Dec. 3 (Xinhua) — Chairman Liao Chengzhi of the China-Japan Friendship Association hosted a reception and banquet in the evening for a delegation led by Vice Chairman Yamaura Mitsuo of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) on their fourth study tour. He had a friendly and cordial talk with them.

	Present at the reception were Secretary-General Zhao Anbo of the China-Japan Friendship Association and others. Japanese peace activist Nishizono Kiichi, who is in Beijing, was also present.

	The photo shows a group picture taken during the reception. Yamaura Mitsuo is the third from the right in the front row. (Photo by Xinhua reporter)
