Title: Advancing Under the Guidance of Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Mao Lai Lie
Date: 1966-12-10
Edition: 4

Latin America is boiling over. The working class and the peasantry have realized that the only way to change this rotten system is through violent revolution. True revolutionaries must advance under the principles of Marxism-Leninism and the great Mao Zedong Thought.

		Argentina, Mao Lai Lie

### 在毛泽东思想指导下前进
毛莱列
1966-12-10
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    在毛泽东思想指导下前进
    拉丁美洲已经沸腾起来，工人和农民阶级已经觉悟到唯一可以改变这一腐朽制度的途径是暴力革命。真正的革命者必须在马列主义的原则和伟大的毛泽东思想指导下前进。
                阿根廷　毛莱列
