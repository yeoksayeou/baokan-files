Title: U.S. Bandits Continue New War Adventures, Bomb Hanoi Again; Vietnamese Military and Civilians Bravely Defend Capital, Shoot Down Three U.S. Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-12-05
Edition: 1

The U.S. aggressors continue their new war adventures. On the afternoon of the 4th, dozens of U.S. bandit planes once again savagely bombed the northeastern suburbs of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The heroic military and civilians of Hanoi, with the spirit of "decisive battle and decisive victory," once again fiercely struck back with powerful artillery fire, shooting down three U.S. jet fighter-bombers, giving the U.S. air bandits a severe blow.

It was also reported that on the 4th, the military and civilians of Ninh Binh Province shot down one U.S. plane.

Facing the U.S. bandits' vicious "escalation" actions, the military and civilians of Hanoi have been fighting with high morale in recent days. While bravely fighting, they are also persevering in production, determined to display the mighty power of the people's war, defend their heroic capital, and give the U.S. air bandits who dare to invade even heavier blows.

The New China News Agency, Hanoi, December 4th: The Chief of the Liaison Mission of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, Major General Hoang Van Hau, strongly protested the U.S. bandit planes' savage bombing of Hanoi on the 4th.

In an urgent telegram to the Vietnam International Control Commission on the 4th, Hoang Van Hau emphasized that in just three days, from December 2nd to 4th, U.S. bandit planes had twice invaded Hanoi, savagely bombing and strafing residential areas, killing and injuring many civilians, including many women and children, and destroying numerous civilian homes. The telegram pointed out that this heinous crime by U.S. imperialism further proves that it continues to ignore the severe condemnation of world public opinion and stubbornly escalates the use of its air force against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This war action by U.S. imperialism once again exposes its deceitful talk of "peace" and "negotiations."

### 美国强盗继续进行新的战争冒险再次轰炸河内  越南军民英勇保卫首都击落美机三架  越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长痛斥美帝的战争“升级”罪行

1966-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    美国强盗继续进行新的战争冒险再次轰炸河内
    越南军民英勇保卫首都击落美机三架
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长痛斥美帝的战争“升级”罪行
    新华社河内四日电  美国侵略者继续进行新的战争冒险，四日下午，美国强盗飞机数十架次，又一次野蛮轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内市东北郊区。
    英雄的河内市军民，发扬“决战决胜”的精神，又以猛烈的炮火，击落美国喷气式战斗轰炸机三架，给了美国空中强盗以迎头痛击。
    另据公布，宁平省军民四日也击落美机一架。
    面对着美国强盗穷凶极恶的战争“升级”行动，连日来，河内市全体军民斗志昂扬，一面英勇战斗，一面坚持生产，决心发挥人民战争的强大威力，保卫自己的英雄首都，给胆敢侵犯的美国空中强盗以更沉重的打击。
    新华社河内四日电越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长何文楼大校，强烈抗议美国强盗飞机四日又一次野蛮轰炸河内市。
    何文楼四日在给越南国际委员会的紧急电报中强调指出，仅在十二月二日到四日的三天中，美国强盗飞机就两次侵犯河内市，野蛮地轰炸、扫射居民集中区，炸死炸伤和平居民多名，其中有不少妇女和儿童，并且炸毁了许多民房。电报指出，美帝国主义的这一令人发指的罪恶行动，更加证明了它仍然无视世界舆论的严厉谴责，顽固地加强使用空军对越南民主共和国进行战争“升级”。美帝国主义的这一战争行动，再一次揭穿了它的所谓“和平”、“谈判”的狡诈论调。
