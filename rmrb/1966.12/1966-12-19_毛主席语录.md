Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-12-19
Edition: 1

	The world's people should unite and defeat the U.S. aggressors and all their running dogs! The world's people should dare to fight, unafraid of difficulties and bravely marching forward. In that case, the world will surely belong to the people. All ghosts and monsters will be swept away.

		From the "Statement in Support of the People of the Congo (Leopoldville) in Their Struggle Against U.S. Aggression"

### 毛主席语录

1966-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    全世界人民团结起来，打败美国侵略者及其一切走狗！全世界人民要有勇气，敢于战斗，不怕困难，前赴后继，那末，全世界就一定是人民的。一切魔鬼通通都会被消灭。
            《支持刚果（利）人民反对美国侵略的声明》
