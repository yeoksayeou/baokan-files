Title: Delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association Arrives in Changsha
Author:
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 4

The delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association, led by Petrit Dod, a member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania and the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Korça District, arrived in Changsha by plane from Shanghai today after concluding their visit and tour in Shanghai.

During their stay in Shanghai, the Albanian comrades visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China and several factories in Shanghai.

### 阿中友协代表团到长沙

1966-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    阿中友协代表团到长沙
    据新华社十三日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、科尔察区人民会议执行委员会主席彼·多德率领的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团，结束了在上海的参观访问以后，今天乘飞机由上海到达长沙访问。
    阿尔巴尼亚同志在上海期间，参观访问了中国共产党第一次全国代表大会会址以及上海工厂等。
