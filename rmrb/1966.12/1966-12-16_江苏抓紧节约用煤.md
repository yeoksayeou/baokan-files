Title: Jiangsu Tightens Coal Conservation
Author:
Date: 1966-12-16
Edition: 4

Workers of the coal companies and the broad masses of coal-using units across Jiangsu have, under the impetus of the great situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, carried forward the spirit of wholeheartedly serving the people. Starting from reforming stoves, kilns, and boilers, they have been vigorously promoting coal conservation. Since this year, the entire province has saved 500,000 tons of coal.

### 江苏抓紧节约用煤

1966-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    江苏抓紧节约用煤
    江苏各地煤建公司职工和用煤单位的广大群众，在无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势推动下，发扬全心全意为人民服务的精神，从改革炉灶、砖窑和锅炉入手，狠抓节约用煤，今年以来，全省已经节约用煤五十万吨。
