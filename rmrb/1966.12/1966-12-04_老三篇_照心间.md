Title: "The Old Three Articles Shine in the Heart"
Author: Yànzi
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 6

	"The Old Three Articles" shine in the heart,
	The sea I dare to fill,
	The mountains I dare to move,
	With both hands I tame the black dragon,
	I manage both the earth and the sky!
	Sweat paints a colorful picture,
	The iron hoe writes a chapter of great ambition. (The above poem is selected from the Haihe construction site)

### “老三篇”照心间
燕子
1966-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    “老三篇”照心间
    燕子
  “老三篇”光辉照心间，
  大海我敢填，
  高山我敢搬，
  双手驯黑龙，
  管地也管天！
  汗水画出五彩图，
  铁锨写下壮志篇。（以上诗歌选自海河工地）
