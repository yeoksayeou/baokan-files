Title: Algerian People Hold Great Admiration for Our Great Leader Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-12-25
Edition: 4

The Algerian people hold boundless admiration for our great leader Chairman Mao. The picture shows two Algerian workers taking a commemorative photo in front of Chairman Mao's portrait at the Chinese Pavilion of the Algiers International Expo, expressing that Algerian workers will forever stand with Chairman Mao.
