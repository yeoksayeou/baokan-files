Title: The Albanian National Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-23
Edition: 5

The Albanian National Song and Dance Troupe, consisting of 61 members led by Director Ba·Peyo and Deputy Director Ro·Hado, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight as part of the 1966 execution plan of the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Relevant officials such as Zhou Yiping, Li Wei, and Yu Zhen, along with over 500 cultural workers from the capital, were at the airport to warmly welcome the Albanian comrades from the frontline of the anti-revisionist struggle. As the Albanian comrades stepped off the plane, the crowd enthusiastically shouted, "Mao Zedong—Enver!" "Enver—Mao Zedong!"

Those who welcomed them at the airport also included Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli and diplomatic staff from the embassy.

### 阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团到京  首都文艺工作者热烈欢迎来自反修斗争前线的战友

1966-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团到京
    首都文艺工作者热烈欢迎来自反修斗争前线的战友
    据新华社二十二日讯根据中阿文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划来我国访问演出的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国家歌舞团一行六十一人，在团长巴·佩约和副团长罗·哈多的率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。有关方面负责人周一萍、李伟、余湛等，以及首都文艺工作者五百多人到机场热烈欢迎来自反修斗争前线的阿尔巴尼亚战友。当阿尔巴尼亚战友们走下飞机时，人们齐声高呼“毛泽东—恩维尔！”“恩维尔—毛泽东！”
    到机场欢迎的，还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利和使馆外交官员。
