Title: Finnish Ambassador Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-12-07
Edition: 5

Finnish Ambassador to China, Jarl Toivo Lahti, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 49th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Finland.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo were among those invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Lahti delivered a speech. He praised the friendly relations between Finland and China and expressed his wish for further strengthening and development of friendly exchanges between the two countries and their peoples. Ambassador Lahti also commended the great achievements of the Chinese people in agriculture, industry, national defense, and scientific endeavors.

Vice Premier Chen Yi raised a toast at the reception.

He said, "The relations between China and Finland have always been friendly. Through our joint efforts, the friendly relations between our two countries and peoples will be continuously consolidated and developed."

In discussing China's current Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Vice Premier Chen Yi stated, "This unprecedented cultural revolution will enable our people to make greater contributions to the defense of world peace and will further strengthen and develop China's relations with friendly countries."

### 芬兰驻华大使举行国庆宴会  陈毅副总理等应邀出席

1966-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华大使举行国庆宴会
    陈毅副总理等应邀出席
    新华社六日讯　芬兰驻中国大使约爱尔·托依伏拉今晚举行宴会，庆祝芬兰共和国独立四十九周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民，外交部副部长罗贵波等，应邀出席宴会。
    托依伏拉大使在宴会上致词。他赞扬芬中两国的友好关系，并祝愿两国和两国人民之间的友好往来进一步加强和发展。托依伏拉大使还赞扬中国人民在农业、工业、国防以及科学事业等方面取得了巨大成绩。
    陈毅副总理在宴会上举杯祝酒。
    他说，中芬两国的关系一直是友好的。在我们双方共同努力下，存在于我们两国和两国人民之间的友好关系，将不断巩固和发展。
    陈毅副总理在谈到我国当前的无产阶级文化大革命时说，这场史无前例的文化大革命，也将使我国人民对保卫世界和平作出更大的贡献，将使中国同友好国家的关系进一步得到加强和发展。
