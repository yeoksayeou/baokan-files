Title: Prince Sihanouk Hosts Grand Reception for Delegations Attending the Asian Newly Emerging Forces Games
Author:
Date: 1966-12-08
Edition: 4

Prince Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia, the head of state, held a grand reception tonight at the Royal Palace to welcome the athletic delegations from various countries participating in the First Asian Newly Emerging Forces Games.

Also invited to the reception were representatives attending the Asian Council meeting of the Newly Emerging Forces Games, Chinese experts helping to build the "Sports City," the Chinese journalist delegation and film team covering the Asian Newly Emerging Forces Games, and the Chinese Wuhan Acrobatic Troupe currently visiting and performing in Phnom Penh.

When the guests arrived, Prince Sihanouk and his wife stood at the entrance of the Royal Palace to greet them one by one, warmly congratulating them on their significant contributions to the unity and anti-imperialist cause of the Asian people in the Asian Newly Emerging Forces Games.

The grand reception, attended by 3,500 people, was held in the beautiful gardens of the Royal Palace. Athletes from various countries and those who served the Asian Newly Emerging Forces Games gathered together to celebrate the splendid victories achieved in the First Asian Newly Emerging Forces Games. During the friendly and joyous reception, Cambodian artists performed pleasant music and beautiful dances. Chinese athletes sang songs composed after Prince Sihanouk's visit to China, such as "Missing China," and songs praising Chairman Mao, like "The Ship Needs a Helmsman." The Chinese Wuhan Acrobatic Troupe presented wonderful performances, which were warmly received. Attending the reception were leaders of the Royal Government of Cambodia and sports figures, as well as envoys from various countries to Cambodia.

### 招待参加亚新会的各国体育代表团  西  哈努克亲王举行盛大招待会

1966-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    招待参加亚新会的各国体育代表团
    西哈努克亲王举行盛大招待会
    新华社金边七日电　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天晚上在国家宫举行盛大招待会，招待参加第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的各国体育代表团。
    应邀参加招待会的还有出席新运会亚洲委员会会议的代表，帮助建设“体育城”的中国专家，参加亚洲新运会采访的中国记者团和电影代表团，以及正在金边访问演出的中国武汉杂技团。
    当来宾们到达的时候，西哈努克亲王和夫人站在国家宫前同他们一一握手，热烈祝贺他们在亚洲新运会上对亚洲人民团结反帝事业所作出的巨大贡献。
    有三千五百人参加的盛大招待会，是在国家宫美丽的庭园里举行的。各国体育健儿们和为亚洲新运会服务的人们相聚在一起，共同欢庆第一届亚洲新运会所取得的辉煌胜利。在友情洋溢的盛大招待会进行过程中，柬埔寨的艺术家们演奏了悦耳的音乐并表演了优美的歌舞。中国运动员们演唱了西哈努克亲王访问中国后创作的歌曲《怀念中国》，以及歌颂毛主席的歌曲《大海航行靠舵手》等。中国武汉杂技团表演了精彩节目，受到热烈欢迎。出席招待会的有柬埔寨王国政府的领导人和体育界人士，以及各国驻柬埔寨的使节。
