Title: Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-03
Edition: 5

The Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe, led by Colonel Emil Apostol and Colonel Dinu Stelian, arrived in Beijing today by train for a visit and performance, at the invitation of the Chinese government.

Among those who welcomed the Romanian guests at the station were Zhou Yiping, Jia Ruoyu, Li Zhongqi, Li Wei, Yu Ji, Chang Zhiqing, Wu Wei, Chen Bocheng, Wang Yongnian, and other relevant officials, as well as over 200 military and cultural workers from the People's Liberation Army and Beijing.

When the guests arrived, the welcoming crowd and the Red Guards enthusiastically beat drums and gongs, waved copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and sang revolutionary songs to express their warm welcome.

Colonel Sorlescu, the military attaché of the Romanian Embassy in China, and other embassy staff also greeted the guests at the station.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 2nd, the Chinese People's Republic's Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe, led by Colonel Emil Apostol and Colonel Dinu Stelian.

Attending the banquet were Zhang Sirou, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yuan Ziqin, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, and other relevant officials and military and cultural workers from Beijing.

Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Dumitru, and Colonel Sorlescu, the military attaché of the Romanian Embassy, were also invited to attend the banquet.

At the banquet, filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere, Zhang Sirou and Emil Apostol both spoke.

### 罗马尼亚军队歌舞团到京  我对外文委欢宴罗马尼亚客人

1966-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚军队歌舞团到京
    我对外文委欢宴罗马尼亚客人
    新华社二日讯　由领队阿波斯托尔·埃米尔上校和团长迪努·斯太利安上校率领的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国军队歌舞团一行二百人，应邀来我国访问演出，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎罗马尼亚客人的有周一萍、贾若瑜、李钟奇、李伟、虞棘、常芝青、吴伟、陈伯清、王永年等有关方面的负责人，以及中国人民解放军和首都的文艺工作者二百多人。
    当客人们到达时，欢迎的人群和红卫兵小将们，敲锣打鼓，挥动红光闪闪的《毛主席语录》，高唱革命歌曲，表示热烈欢迎。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆武官索列斯库上校和使馆人员也到车站迎接。
    据新华社二日讯　中国对外文化联络委员会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由领队阿波斯托尔·埃米尔上校和团长迪努·斯太利安上校率领的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国军队歌舞团。
    对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、中国人民解放军总政治部副主任袁子钦以及有关方面负责人和部队与首都的文艺工作者出席了宴会。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和大使馆武官索列斯库上校应邀出席宴会。
    在充满热烈友好气氛的宴会上，张奚若和阿波斯托尔·埃米尔先后讲了话。
