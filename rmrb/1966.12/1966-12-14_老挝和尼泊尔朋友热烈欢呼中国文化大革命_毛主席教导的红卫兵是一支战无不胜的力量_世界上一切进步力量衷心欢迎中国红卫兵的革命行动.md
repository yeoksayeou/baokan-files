Title: Friends from Laos and Nepal Warmly Hail China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 5

Friends from Laos and Nepal Warmly Hail China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Mao Zedong's Red Guards Are an Invincible Force
All Progressive Forces in the World Warmly Welcome the Revolutionary Actions of China's Red Guards

KANGOY, Dec. 13 (Xinhua) -- The head of the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation, Xalan·Wengsangang, recently spoke to overseas Chinese in the Khamkeut area about his impressions of his visit to China. He praised "Mao Zedong Thought as the pinnacle of contemporary Marxism-Leninism" and described the Red Guards as the vanguard of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Xalan·Wengsangang emphasized in his speech: "Mao Zedong Thought is the pinnacle of contemporary Marxism-Leninism, it is thorough revolution and wholeheartedly serving the people. Today, the Chinese people love and believe in Chairman Mao without limit, they regard Mao Zedong Thought as the brightest red sun in their hearts."

He said: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is personally led by Chairman Mao. Its purpose is to uproot capitalist and feudal ideology and to prevent revisionism. The Cultural Revolution is very suitable for China's socialist revolution, it will further consolidate the dictatorship of the proletariat and bring socialism to a new stage."

Xalan·Wengsangang highly praised the revolutionary actions of the Red Guards. He said: "The Red Guards are the vanguard of the Cultural Revolution, they are the vanguard of propagating Mao Zedong Thought. The Red Guards actively learn and propagate Mao Zedong Thought, they are ruthless in eliminating the old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits of the bourgeoisie."

He also specifically talked about how the people of Dazhai use Mao Zedong Thought, work hard, and rely on their own efforts for self-improvement.

He said: "The Dazhai spirit is not only for the Chinese people to learn, but also for the people of the world to learn."

KATHMANDU, Dec. 13 (Xinhua) -- Many friends from Nepal recently wrote letters, warmly praising China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the Red Guards. They wrote in their letters: "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!"

A friend named Sulandela wrote in his letter that Mao Zedong Thought is a powerful weapon against American imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries. All oppressed, exploited, and progressive forces in the world warmly welcome the revolutionary actions of the Chinese people and the Chinese Red Guards. No matter how much the imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries of all countries oppose it, Mao Zedong Thought will definitely triumph.

A friend named Batala wrote in his letter: "Under the guidance of Chairman Mao, the Red Guards are an invincible, steadfast, and heroic force that breaks through bourgeois ideology."

Another friend named Singh wrote in his letter: "Dear Chinese Red Guards, revolutionary students, and people: Your actions make us overjoyed. I believe you will definitely achieve victory. I salute the Red Guards friends!"

A friend named Balala wrote in his letter: Seeing your great achievements, we Nepalese youth are deeply moved. Your unprecedented achievements make us spiritually invigorated and greatly inspired.

Another friend named Tak wrote in his letter: "The Red Guards make the youth, farmers, workers, and everyone in Nepal feel joyous. The Red Guards point out the path of truth to the youth."

He said, the people of all countries in the world, advancing on the bright path indicated by Chairman Mao Zedong, will definitely be able to eliminate imperialism, modern revisionism, and all reactionary forces.

Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe and Capital Red Guards Celebrate Together. They, with boundless love for the great leader Chairman Mao, sang "The Boat Sails on Because of the Helmsman" with all the Red Guards. (Photo by Xinhua reporter)

### 老挝和尼泊尔朋友热烈欢呼中国文化大革命  毛主席教导的红卫兵是一支战无不胜的力量  世界上一切进步力量衷心欢迎中国红卫兵的革命行动

1966-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝和尼泊尔朋友热烈欢呼中国文化大革命
    毛主席教导的红卫兵是一支战无不胜的力量
    世界上一切进步力量衷心欢迎中国红卫兵的革命行动
    新华社康开电　老挝爱国力量新闻代表团团长扎伦·翁桑昂最近向川圹地区华侨谈访华观感时，赞扬“毛泽东思想是当代马列主义的顶峰”，“红卫兵是中国无产阶级文化大革命的先锋队”。
    扎伦·翁桑昂在讲话中强调说：“毛泽东思想就是当代马列主义的顶峰，就是彻底革命、全心全意为人民服务。今天，中国人民都无限热爱、无限信仰毛主席，中国人民都把毛泽东思想看成是自己心中最红最红的红太阳。”
    他说：“中国的无产阶级文化大革命是毛主席亲自领导的，它的目的就是挖掉资本主义、封建主义的思想，防止修正主义。”“文化革命是非常适合中国的社会主义革命的，它将进一步巩固无产阶级专政、把社会主义发展到一个新阶段。”
    扎伦·翁桑昂高度赞扬红卫兵的革命行动。他说：“红卫兵是文化大革命的先锋队，是宣传毛泽东思想的先锋队。”“红卫兵积极学习和宣传毛泽东思想，对资产阶级的旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗、旧习惯毫不留情，坚决清除。”
    他还着重谈了大寨人运用毛泽东思想、艰苦奋斗、自力更生的革命精神。
    他说：“大寨精神不但中国人民要学，世界人民也要学。”
    新华社加德满都电　许多尼泊尔朋友最近纷纷写信，热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命和红卫兵。他们在信中欢呼：“伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”
    一位名叫苏兰德拉的朋友在信中说，毛泽东思想是反对美帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派的强大武器。世界上一切被压迫、被剥削和进步的力量衷心欢迎中国人民和中国红卫兵的革命行动。不管帝国主义者、修正主义者和各国反动派怎样反对，毛泽东思想一定会取得胜利。
    一位名叫巴塔赖的朋友在信中写道：“在毛主席的教导下，红卫兵是一支破除资产阶级思想的战无不胜的、坚定的、英雄的力量。”
    另一位名叫辛格的朋友在信中写道：“亲爱的中国红卫兵、革命师生和人民：你们的行动使我们兴高采烈。
    我相信，你们一定会取得胜利。我向红卫兵朋友致敬！”
    一位名叫巴拉尔的朋友在信中写道：看到你们的伟大成就，我们尼泊尔青年一代深受感动。你们的空前的成就使我们精神振奋，使我们受到了很大的鼓舞。
    另一位名叫塔克的朋友在他的信中说：“红卫兵使尼泊尔青年、农民、工人和每一个人都感到欢欣鼓舞。红卫兵给青年指出了真理的道路。”
    他说，在毛泽东主席指引的光明大道上前进的世界各国人民，一定能够铲除帝国主义、现代修正主义和一切反动势力。（附图片）
    日本松山芭蕾舞团和首都红卫兵联欢。他们怀着对伟大领袖毛主席无限热爱的感情，和全体红卫兵一起高唱《大海航行靠舵手》。　新华社记者摄
