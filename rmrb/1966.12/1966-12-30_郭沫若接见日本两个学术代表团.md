Title: Guo Moruo Receives Two Japanese Academic Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-12-30
Edition: 4

    Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, on December 27th received the Japanese delegation of applied physics led by Professor Junji Kiyota of Osaka University and the Japanese delegation of experimental design led by Professor Genichi Taguchi of Seikei University. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若接见日本两个学术代表团

1966-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本两个学术代表团
    新华社二十八日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若，二十七日接见了以大阪大学教授筱田军治为首的日本应用物理学术代表团和以青山学院大学教授田口玄一为首的日本实验设计学术代表团，同他们进行友好谈话。
