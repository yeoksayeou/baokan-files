Title: Students from Asia, Africa, and Latin America Request Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1966-12-27
Edition: 5

On October 23rd, Chinese students studying in the Soviet Union held a farewell reception at the embassy for students from 29 countries in Asia, Africa, and Latin America. During the event, students from Asia, Africa, and Latin America eagerly requested copies of Chairman Mao's works.
