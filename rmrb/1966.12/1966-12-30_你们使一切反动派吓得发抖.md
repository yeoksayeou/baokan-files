Title: You Make All Reactionaries Tremble
Author: Ke La Wei Hao
Date: 1966-12-30
Edition: 5

To the Red Guards of China:

	Hail the glorious appearance of the Red Guards. Your brave revolutionary actions have shaken the entire world, making all reactionaries on earth tremble in fear. The young warriors of the Red Guards, nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought, have made the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution not only a guarantee of the sacred revolutionary cause in China but also in the whole world.

	Dear friends, people are learning from you with respect and following in your footsteps.

### 你们使一切反动派吓得发抖
克拉威豪
1966-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    你们使一切反动派吓得发抖中国的红卫兵朋友们：
    欢呼光荣的红卫兵的出现。你们勇敢的革命行动震撼了整个世界，使地球上一切反动派吓得发抖。用毛泽东思想哺育成长起来的红卫兵年青的战士们，使无产阶级文化大革命成为不仅在中国，而且在全世界继承神圣的革命事业的坚强保证。
    亲爱的朋友们，人们正以尊敬的心情在学习你们，并跟随着你们前进。
                              哥伦比亚　克拉威豪
