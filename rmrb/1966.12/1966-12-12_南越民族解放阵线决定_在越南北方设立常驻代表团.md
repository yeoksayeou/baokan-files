Title: The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Decides to Establish a Permanent Representative Office in North Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-12-12
Edition: 5

The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam has decided to establish a permanent representative office in North Vietnam.

According to a report by the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam decided at a recent enlarged meeting to establish a permanent representative office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in North Vietnam. The head of the representative office will be Nguyen Van Tien, a member of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front.

The enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam was held on December 4 and 5.

### 南越民族解放阵线决定  在越南北方设立常驻代表团

1966-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线决定  在越南北方设立常驻代表团
    新华社河内十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团在最近一次扩大会议上决定，在越南北方将建立越南南方民族解放阵线常驻代表团。代表团团长由民族解放阵线中央委员会委员阮文进担任。
    越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团的这次扩大会议，是在十二月四日和五日举行的。
