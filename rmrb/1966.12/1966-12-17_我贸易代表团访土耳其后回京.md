Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Turkey
Author:
Date: 1966-12-17
Edition: 5

The Chinese Council for the Promotion of International Trade delegation, led by Vice Chairman Xiao Fangzhou, was invited by the Turkish Union of Chambers and Commodity Exchanges and the Industrial Development Bank of Turkey. The delegation completed an eight-day friendly visit to Turkey and returned to Beijing on December 13.

During their stay in Turkey, the delegation made contact with Turkish economic and trade circles and exchanged extensive views on the development of civilian trade between China and Turkey.

### 我贸易代表团访土耳其后回京

1966-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团访土耳其后回京
    新华社十六日讯　由中国国际贸易促进委员会副主任萧方洲率领的中华人民共和国国际贸易促进委员会贸易代表团，应土耳其全国工、商、交易所联合会和实业投资银行的邀请，在土耳其进行了八天的友好访问后，已于十二月十三日回到北京。
    代表团访土期间，同土耳其经济、贸易界人士进行了接触，就发展中土两国民间贸易问题广泛地交换了意见。
