Title: People of the Liberated Areas of Laos Love Chairman Mao and Believe in Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-12-24
Edition: 4

The military and civilians in the liberated areas of Laos love Chairman Mao and believe in Mao Zedong Thought. The image shows a soldier of the Lao People's Liberation Army who, in the midst of intense combat, is reading Chairman Mao's works while taking notes.
