Title: Chairman of the Botswana People's Party Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-01
Edition: 4

Chairman of the Botswana People's Party, Seretse Khama, and the representative of the Botswana People's Party in Cairo, Bobbie Makgaba, arrived in Beijing today by airplane at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a visit to China.

### 贝专纳人民党主席到京

1966-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    贝专纳人民党主席到京
    新华社三十日讯　贝专纳人民党主席马坦特和贝专纳人民党驻开罗代表波比·迈克，应中国人民外交学会的邀请到中国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
