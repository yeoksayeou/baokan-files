Title: The Radiant Red Book - Praising "Quotations from Chairman Mao"
Author: Langbei'er·Aolaiyi
Date: 1966-12-28
Edition: 3

	The radiant red book of Mao Zedong,
	It is the book of truth, the book of radiant light.
	All proletarians of the world, all oppressed peoples of the world,
	From Mao Zedong's red book, you gather and draw
	The necessary and beneficial nourishment.
	This red book,
	Is the guiding light for those seeking light in the night.
	It illuminates the path of the world's peoples,
	It educates all oppressed peoples of the world.
	All proletarians of the world,
	Mao Zedong's red book is your battle manual.
	Study it, study it every day, study it diligently.
	It is the work of thinkers serving the cause of human liberation.
	Study it, study the red book of Mao Zedong!
	It is Mao Zedong who gave us this guiding light,
	May the name of Mao Zedong be praised for eternity.
	Mao Zedong's red book,
	It is the precious book of the 20th-century Marxist-Leninist warriors,
	It is a must-read book.

### 光芒普照的红书——歌颂《毛主席语录》
朗贝尔·奥拉伊
1966-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    光芒普照的红书
    ——歌颂《毛主席语录》
    刚果（布）小学教师  朗贝尔·奥拉伊毛泽东的这本红书呵，它是认识真理的书，光芒普照的书。全世界无产者，全世界被压迫的人们，从毛泽东的红书中，采集、汲取他们必需的有益的滋养料。这红书呵，是寻求光明的夜行者的指路明灯。它照亮着世界人民的路程，它教育着世界一切被压迫的人民。全世界的无产者呵，毛泽东的红书就是你们战斗的指南。学习吧，天天学，用心学。它是为人类解放事业而服务的思想家的著作。学习吧，学习毛泽东的红书吧！是毛泽东给我们这盏明灯，愿毛泽东的名字万代称颂。毛泽东的红书呵，它是二十世纪马克思列宁主义战士的珍贵的书，必读的书。
