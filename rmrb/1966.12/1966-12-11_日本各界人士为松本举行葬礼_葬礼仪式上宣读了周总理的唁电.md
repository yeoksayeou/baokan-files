Title: Japanese Figures from Various Circles Hold Funeral for Matsumoto
Author:
Date: 1966-12-11
Edition: 4

Japanese figures from various circles held a funeral for Matsumoto.

The funeral ceremony for Matsumoto Jiichiro, chairman of the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), chairman of the Japan Buraku Liberation League, and advisor to the Social Democratic Party, was held on the 10th in Tokyo's Aoyama Cemetery.

About 500 Japanese figures from various circles, including Social Democratic Party Chairman Sasakawa Kyuzo, chairman of the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) Miyazaki Seiken, and secretary-general of the Japan Buraku Liberation League Tanaka Orihiro, attended the funeral.

Liao Chengzhi's office chief representative in Tokyo, Sun Pinghua, head of the Chinese Economic and Trade Exhibition Team, Xiao Xiangqian, and head of the Chinese Women's Delegation to Japan, Yang Yunyu, also attended the funeral. Condolence messages from Premier Zhou Enlai and chairman of the China-Japan Friendship Association Liao Chengzhi, as well as various Chinese people's organizations, were read during the funeral ceremony.

### 日本各界人士为松本举行葬礼  葬礼仪式上宣读了周总理的唁电

1966-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各界人士为松本举行葬礼
    葬礼仪式上宣读了周总理的唁电
    据新华社十日讯东京消息：日中友好协会（正统）总部会长、日本部落解放同盟委员长、社会党顾问松本治一郎的葬礼十日在东京青山墓地举行。
    日本社会党委员长佐佐木更三、日中友好协会（正统）总部理事长宫崎世民、日本部落解放同盟书记长田中织之进等日本各界人士约五百人参加了葬礼。
    廖承志办事处驻东京联络事务所首席代表孙平化、中国经济贸易展览团团长萧向前、中国妇女访日代表团团长杨蕴玉等也出席了葬礼。葬礼仪式上宣读了周恩来总理和中日友好协会会长廖承志以及中国各人民团体的唁电。
