Title: Albanian Government Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-12-04
Edition: 5

The Albanian government trade delegation, led by Albanian Trade Minister Kico Ndreka, concluded its visit to Shanghai and departed for home by plane this evening. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Shanghai Vice Mayor Shi Ying and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Jiang Ming. The delegation arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 2nd. That evening, Shi Ying hosted a banquet to welcome the Albanian guests.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团回国

1966-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团回国
    新华社上海三日电　由阿尔巴尼亚贸易部部长基乔·恩杰拉率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团，结束了在上海的参观访问，今晚乘飞机离开上海回国。
    前往机场欢送的，有上海市副市长石英，对外贸易部副部长江明等。代表团是二日上午到达上海的。当晚，石英曾设宴欢迎阿尔巴尼亚客人。
