Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-12
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) Yamaguchi Prefecture Friendship Delegation led by Furutani Shoichiro and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhao Anbo, Secretary-General of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and others.

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received members of the Japanese Economic Delegation and had a friendly conversation with them.

Those participating in the reception included Liao Chengzhi, Lu Xuzhang, and Hou Tong, among others.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1966-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社十一日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见以古谷庄一郎为首的日中友协（正统）山口县友好代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中日友协秘书长赵安博等。
    新华社十一日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见日本经济代表团的成员，同他们进行友好的谈话。
    参加接见的有廖承志、卢绪章、侯桐等。
