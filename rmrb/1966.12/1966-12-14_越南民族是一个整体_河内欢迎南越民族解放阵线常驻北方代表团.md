Title: The Vietnamese People Are One
Author:
Date: 1966-12-14
Edition: 4

The Vietnamese people are one. Hanoi Welcomes the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to the North

New China News Agency, Hanoi, December 12 — The National Assembly, the Government, and the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a welcoming ceremony this afternoon in Hanoi to warmly welcome the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to the North, headed by Nguyen Van Tien, a member of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

Those who attended the welcoming ceremony included Nguyen Duy Trinh, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nguyen Van Hieu and Tran Dang Khoi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, Hoang Van Hoan, a member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front, Tran Huy Lieu, General Secretary of the Democratic Party of Vietnam, Nguyen Thoan, General Secretary of the Socialist Party of Vietnam, and Chen Wei Xing, Chairman of the Administrative Committee of Hanoi, among others.

Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Zhu Qiwen and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam were also invited to attend the welcoming ceremony.

Nguyen Van Tien, head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to the North, delivered a brief speech at the welcoming ceremony. On behalf of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the 14 million people of South Vietnam who are standing at the forefront of the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, determined to defeat the American aggressors, he extended the warmest greetings to Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the people of the North.

He said, "South Vietnam and North Vietnam are one, they are flesh and blood. The bond of flesh and blood between the Vietnamese people for thousands of years is unbreakable by any force."

### 越南民族是一个整体  河内欢迎南越民族解放阵线常驻北方代表团

1966-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民族是一个整体
    河内欢迎南越民族解放阵线常驻北方代表团
    新华社河内十二日电越南民主共和国国会、越南民主共和国政府和越南祖国战线中央委员会，今天下午在河内举行欢迎仪式，热烈欢迎以越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会委员阮文进为首的越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团。
    参加欢迎仪式的有：越南劳动党中央政治局委员、政府副总理兼外交部长阮维桢，越南国会常务委员会副主席阮文享、陈登科，越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员黄国越，越南民主党总书记严春菴，越南社会党总书记阮阐，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴等。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文和各国驻越南外交使节也应邀出席了欢迎仪式。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团团长阮文进在欢迎仪式上发表了简短的讲话。他代表越南南方民族解放阵线和屹立在抗美救国斗争最前线决心战胜美国侵略强盗的一千四百万越南南方人民，向胡志明主席和北方人民，致以最热烈的问候。
    他说，越南南方和北方是一个整体，是亲骨肉。越南民族千年万代的骨肉之情，是任何力量也分割不了的。
