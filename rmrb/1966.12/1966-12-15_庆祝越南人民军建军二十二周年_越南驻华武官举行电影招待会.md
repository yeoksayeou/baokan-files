Title: Celebrating the 22nd Anniversary of the Vietnam People's Army
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 5

Vietnamese military attaché to China, Colonel Chen Wenpeng and his wife held a movie screening reception this afternoon to enthusiastically celebrate the 22nd anniversary of the Vietnam People's Army.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Peng Shaohui, and relevant officials Jia Ruoyu, Pan Zhenwu, Chen Lei, Lai Yali, Cao Keqiang, and Wang Buyun attended the reception.

Military attachés from various countries' embassies in China also attended the reception.

The reception screened Vietnamese documentary films such as "Returning to the Embrace of the North."

### 庆祝越南人民军建军二十二周年  越南驻华武官举行电影招待会

1966-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南人民军建军二十二周年
    越南驻华武官举行电影招待会
    新华社十四日讯　越南民主共和国驻华大使馆武官陈文彭上校及夫人，今天下午举行电影招待会，热烈庆祝越南人民军建军二十二周年。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉和有关方面负责人贾若瑜、潘振武、陈雷、赖亚力、曹克强、王布云等出席了电影招待会。
    各国驻华大使馆武官也出席了电影招待会。
    招待会上放映了越南纪录影片《回到北方怀抱》等。
