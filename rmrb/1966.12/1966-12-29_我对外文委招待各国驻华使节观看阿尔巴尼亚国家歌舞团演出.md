Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Hosts Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries to Watch the Performance of the Albanian National Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1966-12-29
Edition: 2

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries hosted diplomatic envoys from various countries and diplomatic officials from embassies in China tonight to watch the performance of the Albanian People's Republic National Song and Dance Troupe, which is rich in fighting spirit and revolutionary atmosphere from the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist front. Relevant responsible persons such as Qiao Guanhua and Shi Huibin, as well as Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Natanaeli, accompanied the viewing of the performance.

### 我对外文委招待各国驻华使节观看阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团演出

1966-12-29
第2版()
专栏：

    我对外文委招待各国驻华使节观看阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团演出
    新华社二十八日讯　对外文化联络委员会今晚招待各国驻中国使节和大使馆外交官员，观看了来自反帝反修前线的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国家歌舞团富有战斗精神和革命气息的歌舞表演。有关方面负责人乔冠华、史怀璧等，以及阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利陪同观看了演出。
