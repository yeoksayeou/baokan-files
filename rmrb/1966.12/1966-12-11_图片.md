Title: Mao's Works Shine with Golden Light
Author:
Date: 1966-12-11
Edition: 6

Mao Zedong's works shine with golden light, every word and sentence radiates brilliance;
The people of Albania have mastered them, and have the strength to fight against imperialism and revisionism.

In South Vietnam, the fires of war rage, Mao Zedong Thought is the best weapon;
The heroic people diligently study revolutionary truths, vowing to carry on the war of resistance against American aggression to the end.

The East is red, the sun is rising, China has given birth to Mao Zedong.
The people of Vietnam love you, and sing "The East is Red" in unison.

Mao Zedong Thought shines brightly, revolutionary fighters in Laos praise it together;
The power of people's war is great, and in guerrilla warfare, they give the American wolves a painful beating.

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao, you are the red sun in the hearts of the world's people;
Friends from Guinea love you, and sing "The East is Red" loudly.

The precious book's words shine with golden light, the Palestinian Liberation Army strives to master Mao Zedong Thought;
Determined to learn it thoroughly, armed struggle for liberation.

The most beloved Chairman Mao, workers in Mali love you;
They are delighted to read the red book seriously, and remember every word and sentence in their hearts.

With arms raised high, they shout long live Chairman Mao, and sing loudly for the Communist Party of China;
The people of Pakistan welcome guests, because you come from the homeland of Mao Zedong.

(Photos in this edition are provided by Xinhua News Agency)
