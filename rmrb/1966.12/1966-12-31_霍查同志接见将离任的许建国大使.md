Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Ambassador Xu Jian Guo Who Is About to Leave His Post
Author:
Date: 1966-12-31
Edition: 5

The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, Comrade Enver Hoxha, received the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Xu Jian Guo, who is about to leave his post, on the morning of the 29th in this city.

Present at the reception were the Albanian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nasi.

The reception was held in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 霍查同志接见将离任的许建国大使

1966-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见将离任的许建国大使
    新华社地拉那三十日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查，于二十九日上午在这里接见了即将离任的中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国。
    接见时，在座的有阿尔巴尼亚外交部长纳赛。
    接见是在十分亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
