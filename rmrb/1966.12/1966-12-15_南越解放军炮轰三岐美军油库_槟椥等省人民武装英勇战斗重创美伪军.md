Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Shells Three Forks U.S. Military Oil Depot; People's Armed Forces in Binh Dinh and Other Provinces Bravely Fight, Inflicting Heavy Casualties on U.S. Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 5

	South Vietnamese Liberation Army Shells Three Forks U.S. Military Oil Depot

	Binh Dinh and Other Provinces' People's Armed Forces Bravely Fight, Inflicting Heavy Casualties on U.S. Puppet Forces

	Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Saigon News: On the morning of the 14th, the armed forces of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army shelled a U.S. military oil depot in Tam Ky, Quang Nam Province. According to news from Western news agencies, approximately 250,000 liters of various types of gasoline were destroyed.

	Reuters reported that after the oil depot was hit, it caught fire, and "the flames lit up the sky over Tam Ky."

	At the same time as the shelling of the oil depot, the liberation armed forces also attacked a puppet security force guarding a bridge five kilometers south of Tam Ky.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 14th: According to the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, on December 2nd and 3rd, the armed forces of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army in Binh Dinh Province captured enemy strongholds and outposts such as Long Hiep and Vung Mountain, and attacked New Life Airport. They killed and wounded a total of 122 enemy personnel (including the annihilation of an entire enemy company), destroyed one enemy aircraft, five M-113 amphibious armored vehicles, and seized a batch of weapons.

	The local forces and guerrillas of Tam Ky County, Quang Nam Province, on November 16th and 17th, bravely intercepted the U.S. puppet forces that were "sweeping" through Gia Tea Township and Gia Giao Township, killing and wounding 92 enemy personnel (including 32 U.S. invaders), shooting down three enemy aircraft, and damaging one enemy tank.

	On December 2nd, the local forces and guerrillas of Le Hoa Township and Hoa Long Township, Tan An County, Ninh Thuan Province, took advantage of the absence of part of the puppet security forces that had gone out for a "sweep," launching a sudden and fierce attack on the enemy's nest. They annihilated two platoons of the puppet security forces on the spot, killing and wounding more than 50 enemy personnel, and seized a batch of weapons and military supplies.

### 南越解放军炮轰三岐美军油库  槟椥等省人民武装英勇战斗重创美伪军

1966-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军炮轰三岐美军油库
    槟椥等省人民武装英勇战斗重创美伪军
    新华社十四日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量十四日清晨炮轰广南省三岐市的一个美军油库，据西方通讯社的消息透露，共毁掉各种汽油约二十五万公升。
    路透社说，这个油库被击中后起火，“熊熊烈火照红了三岐市上空”。
    在炮轰油库的同时，解放武装力量还袭击了在三岐市南面五公里处守卫一座桥梁的伪保安队。
    据新华社河内十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方槟椥省解放武装力量十二月二日和三日，在攻克隆兴、万丐山等敌军据点、哨所和袭击新成机场的战斗中，共打死打伤敌人一百二十二名（其中包括全歼敌人一个连），击毁敌机一架、M—113型两栖装甲车五辆，并缴获一批武器。
    广南省三岐县地方部队和游击队十一月十六日和十七日，在岐茶乡和岐姜乡英勇截击前往“扫荡”的美伪军，打死打伤敌人九十二名（其中包括美国侵略军三十二名），击落敌机三架，击伤敌坦克一辆。
    新安省新柱县乐晦乡和平廊乡地方部队和游击队，十二月二日乘伪保安队的一部外出“扫荡”的时候，突然向敌人的巢穴发起猛烈袭击，当场歼灭留守的伪保安队两个排，打死打伤敌人五十多名，缴获武器和军用物资一批。
