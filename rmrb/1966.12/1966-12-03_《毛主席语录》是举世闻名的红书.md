Title: The Little Red Book is Famous Around the World
Author:
Date: 1966-12-03
Edition: 1

	The Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists) organ, *The Vanguard*, today published an article praising *Quotations from Chairman Mao*:

	In "this world-famous little red book," there is "the most precious wealth in today's world, that is, Mao Zedong Thought." Therefore, the people praise and respect this book.

	The article states that in China, this book has already become the guide for the actions of 700 million Chinese people. Imperialism fears this book because "they fear the people, especially when the people are armed with the ideas of this book." Those modern revisionists centered around the Soviet Communist Party leadership "also harbor a strong hatred for this book" because "they are trying desperately to bury Marxism-Leninism, while this red book has handed Marxism-Leninism over to hundreds of millions of people."

	The article points out: "Mao Zedong Thought is the highest development of Marxism-Leninism. For the oppressed, it is life itself; for the oppressors, it means death."

	The article calls on the advanced workers of Australia to study Mao Zedong Thought. The article says: "This book will greatly help in learning the important principles of Marxism-Leninism and in studying Australian reality based on these principles."

### 《毛主席语录》是举世闻名的红书

1966-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    澳共《先锋报》发表文章赞扬说：
    《毛主席语录》是举世闻名的红书
    毛泽东思想是马列主义的最高发展，对被压迫者来说，它就是生命本身；对压迫者来说，它就意味着死亡。
    新华社墨尔本一日电　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）机关报《先锋报》今天发表文章赞扬《毛主席语录》说：在“这本举世闻名的红书”中，“有今天世界上最宝贵的财富，那就是毛泽东思想”。因此，人民颂扬和尊敬这本书。
    文章说，在中国，这本书已经是七亿中国人民行动的指南。帝国主义害怕这本书，因为“他们害怕人民，特别是当人民用这本书的思想武装起来的时候”。以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义者“对这本书也怀有强烈的仇恨”，因为
  “他们拚命地想埋葬马克思列宁主义，而这本红书把马克思列宁主义交给了亿万人民。”
    文章指出：“毛泽东思想是马克思列宁主义的最高发展”，“对被压迫者来说，它就是生命本身；而对压迫者来说，它就意味着死亡”。
    文章号召澳大利亚先进的工人学习毛泽东思想。文章说：“这本书将大大有助于学习马克思列宁主义的重要原理，并有助于根据这些原理来研究澳大利亚的现实。”
