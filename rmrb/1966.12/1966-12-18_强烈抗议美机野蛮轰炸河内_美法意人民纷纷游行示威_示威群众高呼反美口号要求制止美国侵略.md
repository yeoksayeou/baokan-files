Title: Strongly Protesting the Savage Bombing of Hanoi by U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-12-18
Edition: 4

A batch of American youths demonstrated in front of the White House on the afternoon of the 15th, protesting the savage bombing of the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi, by U.S. aircraft.

The demonstrators held up placards with slogans such as: “Murderer (Johnson)!” “Stop the bombing immediately!”

PARIS, Dec. 16 (Xinhua) — More than a thousand people gathered in the city center this evening to hold a large-scale demonstration and march, strongly protesting the new act of aggression by U.S. bandit aircraft in continuously bombing the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi.

The demonstrators carried flags of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and banners with slogans such as “Stop U.S. Aggression!” They gathered in the city center, broke through police barriers, and chanted slogans such as “U.S. murderers!” “Johnson is a butcher!” “The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam will surely win!” They then marched through the streets.

The demonstration and march lasted about two hours. During the demonstration, the police beat the demonstrators, injuring about fifteen people, and arrested several hundred.

ROME, Dec. 17 (Xinhua) — The Italian people are outraged by the continuous bombing of the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi, by the United States. In cities such as Rome, Bologna, Genoa, Livorno, Savona, Naples, Cagliari, and Bari, workers and people from all walks of life held strikes and street demonstrations to protest against the crimes of the United States. Many cities sent telegrams of protest to the U.S. Embassy in Rome. Some cities had walls with slogans condemning the new aggression by the United States.

In Rome, a procession composed of workers and students marched back and forth in front of the U.S. Embassy last night.

On the evening of December 16, more than a thousand people in Paris carried banners with slogans such as “U.S. murderers!” and marched through the streets.

### 强烈抗议美机野蛮轰炸河内  美法意人民纷纷游行示威  示威群众高呼反美口号要求制止美国侵略

1966-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    强烈抗议美机野蛮轰炸河内
    美法意人民纷纷游行示威
    示威群众高呼反美口号要求制止美国侵略
    新华社十六日讯　华盛顿消息：一批美国青年十五日下午在白宫门前举行示威，抗议美国飞机野蛮轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内的侵略罪行。
    示威者高举标语牌，上面写着：“（约翰逊）杀人犯！”“立即停止轰炸！”
    新华社巴黎十六日电巴黎一千多人今晚在市中心举行大规模示威游行，强烈抗议美国强盗飞机连续轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内市的新的侵略罪行。
    示威群众拿着越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜和写有“制止美国侵略！”的横幅标语在市中心集会，并冲破警察的阻挠，高呼着“美国杀人犯！”“约翰逊是刽子手！”“越南南方民族解放阵线必胜！”等口号，举行了游行。
    游行示威持续了约两小时。在示威过程中，警察殴打了示威群众，约有十五人受伤，并有数百人被捕。
    据新华社十七日讯　罗马消息：意大利人民愤怒抗议美国连续轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内的滔天罪行。在罗马、波伦亚、热那亚、里窝那、萨沃纳、那不勒斯、卡利亚里、巴里等城市，工人和各阶层人民都举行了罢工和街头示威，抗议美国的罪行。许多城市的人民向美国驻罗马大使馆发出抗议电报。一些城市的墙上出现了谴责美国新的侵略的标语。
    在罗马，由工人和学生组成的游行队伍昨晚在美国大使馆外面来回游行。（附图片）
    十二月十六日晚，巴黎一千多人举着写有“美国杀人犯！”的横幅标语在大街上游行示威。
                            传真照片（新华社发）
