Title: The Asian-African Journalists Association and the Asia-Pacific Peace Committee Condemn the New Crime of U.S. Bombing of Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-12-18
Edition: 4

The Asian-African Journalists Association and the Asia-Pacific Peace Committee condemn the new crime of U.S. bombing of Hanoi
Vietnamese people will surely smash all "peace talks" conspiracies

	According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 15th, the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association issued a statement, calling on all revolutionary and fighting journalists in Asia and Africa, and all progressive journalists around the world, to strongly protest and condemn the new crime of the U.S. bombing of the city of Hanoi.

	The statement pointed out that the new crime of U.S. imperialism has thoroughly exposed the deceptive nature of the "peace talks" fraud and ceasefire tricks plotted by it in collusion with Soviet revisionists and a handful of reactionaries. The statement said that the heroic Vietnamese people will surely smash all the "peace talks" conspiracies plotted by U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 17th, the Asia-Pacific Peace Committee issued a statement, strongly condemning the heinous crime of the U.S. imperialists' continuous bombing of Hanoi, and expressing firm support for the principled stance of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the government of China.

	The statement said that in the face of the U.S. imperialists' military threats and "peace" deception, the heroic Vietnamese people stand firm, fight to the end, and grow stronger with each battle. Such a people are invincible, cannot be deceived, and cannot be intimidated.

	The statement called on all peace-loving and justice-seeking people in the Asia-Pacific region and the world to strengthen their actions and make greater contributions to supporting the Vietnamese people in defeating the U.S. aggressors.

### 亚非记协和亚太和联谴责美机轰炸河内新罪行  越南人民必将粉碎一切“和谈”阴谋

1966-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非记协和亚太和联谴责美机轰炸河内新罪行
    越南人民必将粉碎一切“和谈”阴谋
    据新华社十五日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处今天发表声明，号召所有革命的和战斗的亚非新闻工作者和全世界进步新闻工作者，最强烈地抗议和谴责美国轰炸河内市区的新罪行。
    声明指出，美帝国主义的新罪行，彻底暴露了它同苏联修正主义者和一小撮反动派勾结起来策划的“和谈”骗局和停战诡计的欺骗性质。声明说，英雄的越南人民定将粉碎美帝国主义者和苏联修正主义者策划的一切“和谈”阴谋。
    据新华社十七日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会今天发表声明，严厉谴责美帝国主义连续轰炸河内的滔天罪行，表示坚决支持越南民主共和国政府声明和中国政府声明的严正立场。
    声明说，在美帝国主义武力威胁和“和平”欺骗面前，英雄的越南人民巍然屹立，决战决胜，越战越强，这样的人民是不可侮的，骗不了的，吓不倒的。
    声明呼吁亚洲及太平洋区域和世界一切爱好和平和正义的人民，加强行动，为支援越南人民打败美国侵略者作出更大的贡献。
