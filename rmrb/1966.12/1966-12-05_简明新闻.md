	The Sudan-China Friendship Association is Established

	The Sudan-China Friendship Association was recently established. On December 2, the executive committee of this friendship association held its first meeting and elected Abdel Wahab Zein Abdelin as chairman.

	The secretary-general of the association, Mustafa, said at the meeting that the purpose of establishing the Sudan-China Friendship Association is to strengthen the friendship between the Sudanese people and the Chinese people. He praised the great achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction and their support for the liberation struggles of the peoples of all countries.

	Asian-African Writers' Friendship Delegation Visits Syria

	The Asian-African Writers' Friendship Delegation, led by the secretary-general of the Asian-African Writers' Permanent Bureau, Senanayake, was warmly welcomed in Damascus, the capital of Syria. The Syrian people expressed their gratitude to the delegation for the support shown by the emergency meeting of the Asian-African Writers' Permanent Bureau held in Beijing for the anti-imperialist struggle of the Arab people, and expressed their support for the resolutions of the meeting.

	The delegation arrived in Damascus on November 28.

	Ecuadorian People Protest Against the Rise of Pro-American Elements

	Recently, people from all walks of life in Ecuador held demonstrations to protest against the election of the pro-American oligarch Otto Arosemena as the interim president by the Ecuadorian Constituent Assembly.

	In the capital Quito, more than a thousand people took to the streets, shouting slogans such as "Down with Arosemena!" and "Down with the Constituent Assembly!"

	Floods Strike the Industrial City of Cordoba in Argentina

	On December 2, the Argentine industrial city of Cordoba experienced its worst flood in 27 years.

	According to reports, the floodwaters submerged hundreds of houses, leaving hundreds of people homeless. Several bridges and sections of railway tracks were also washed away by the flood.

    苏丹—中国友好协会宣告成立
    苏丹—中国友好协会于最近成立。十二月二日，这个友好协会的执行委员会举行第一次会议，选举阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·宰因·阿卜丁为主席。
    这个协会的秘书长穆斯塔法在会上说，成立苏丹—中国友好协会的目的是为了增强苏丹人民和中国人民之间的友谊。他赞扬中国人民在社会主义建设方面所取得的巨大成就，赞扬中国人民支持世界各国人民的解放斗争。
    亚非作家友好代表团访问叙利亚
    由亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克率领的亚非作家友好代表团在叙利亚首都大马士革访问受到热烈欢迎。叙利亚人民向代表团表示感谢在北京举行的亚非作家常设局紧急会议对阿拉伯人民反帝斗争的支持，并表示拥护会议的决议。
    代表团是十一月二十八日到达大马士革的。
    厄瓜多尔人民抗议亲美分子上台
    厄瓜多尔各界人民群众最近举行示威游行，抗议厄瓜多尔立宪议会选举亲美寡头分子奥托·阿罗塞门纳为临时总统。
    在首都基多，一千多人举行示威，高呼打倒阿罗塞门纳和“打倒立宪议会”等口号。
    阿根廷的科尔多瓦城发生大水灾
    阿根廷工业城市科尔多瓦二日发生了二十七年来最大的一次水灾。
    据报道，洪水淹没了数百家住房，使数百人无家可归。几座桥梁和几段铁轨也被洪水冲毁。
                            （据新华社讯）
