Title: Revolutionary Art, a Weapon of Battle
Author:
Date: 1966-12-29
Edition: 5

The performances by the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe not only demonstrated superb and exquisite performing arts, but also presented a brand-new content on stage. They are not "art for art's sake," but through every song and every dance movement, they depict the brave fighting spirit and close unity of the revolutionary people of the 20th century.

	Pakistan, a spectator

The entire performance of our Chinese friends radiates the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought. Being able to enjoy the fruits of the Chinese Cultural Revolution across the sea in Japan makes us feel very fortunate.

	Japan, a spectator

We, the people of Yemen, love Chinese dance because it expresses patriotism, struggle, and revolution. It inspires us to move forward and enables us to look far ahead.

	Yemen, Muhammad

Chinese art reflects the struggle of the world's people for freedom, liberation, democracy, independence, and against imperialism. From this perspective, one can distinguish who is revolutionary and who is not.

	Palestine, Zahdi Hadidi

Under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, your art serves the workers, peasants, and soldiers, which is very correct. We are also exploring how to serve the current real struggle. The teachings of Mao Zedong inspire us to overcome difficulties and advance to victory.

	Algeria, a drama worker

The performances by Chinese artists indeed added revolutionary spirit to our courage, making us more eager to study Chairman Mao's works and thoughts. Mao Zedong Thought is the guide to carry the revolution through to the end. Mao Zedong Thought is the beacon for revolutionary people.

	Three African youth freedom fighters

There are no seductive tunes, no love stories, and no emperors, generals, scholars, or beauties in the programs of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe. Instead, they sing about workers, peasants, and soldiers, and about labor and struggle. From your performances, we can see that the Chinese Cultural Revolution has thoroughly smashed old ideas, and that's great!

	UAE, a spectator

The performances by Chinese artists reflect our struggle for freedom and have added to our strength.

	Zambia, a teacher

Chinese songs and dances reflect the strong desire of African and Asian peoples to strive for and defend national independence and build a new life. These performances have enhanced the anti-imperialist unity of African and Asian peoples.

	Uganda, a spectator

### 革命的艺术战斗的武器  各国人民热烈赞扬中国艺术家的演出

1966-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    革命的艺术战斗的武器
    各国人民热烈赞扬中国艺术家的演出
    中国歌舞团的演出，不仅显示了高超和精湛的表演艺术，而且以崭新的内容出现在舞台上。他们不是“为艺术而艺术”，而是通过每一支歌曲、每一个舞蹈动作，描绘了二十世纪革命人民的勇敢斗争精神和紧密的团结意志。
                            巴基斯坦　一位观众
    中国朋友的整个演出放射着毛泽东思想的光辉，在一海之隔的日本能享受到中国无产阶级文化大革命的成果，感到非常幸福。
                              日本　一位观众
    我们也门人民热爱中国的舞蹈。因为它们表现了爱国主义、战斗和革命；它们鼓舞我们前进，并使我们能够高瞻远瞩。
                              也门　穆罕默德
    中国的艺术反映了世界人民争取自由、解放、民主、独立和反对帝国主义的斗争。从这方面就能区别出谁是革命的，谁是不革命的。
                      巴勒斯坦　查赫迪·哈迪希
    在毛泽东主席领导下，你们的艺术为工农兵服务，这是很正确的。我们也正在探索怎样为当前的现实斗争服务。毛泽东的教导鼓舞着我们战胜困难，胜利前进。
                    阿尔及利亚　一位戏剧工作者
    中国艺术家的演出确实以革命精神增添了我们的勇气，促使我们更加迫切地想学毛主席著作和思想。毛泽东思想是把革命进行到底的指南。毛泽东思想是革命人民的明灯。
                          三位非洲青年自由战士
    中国歌舞团的节目里没有靡靡之音，没有谈情说爱，没有帝王将相、才子佳人；而是歌唱工农兵，歌唱劳动和斗争。从你们的演出中可以看到，中国文化大革命，大破旧思想，好得很！
                              阿联　一位观众
    中国艺术家的演出表现了我们争取自由的斗争，并且使我们增添了力量。　赞比亚　一位教师
    中国的歌舞反映了非洲和亚洲人民要争取和保卫民族独立、建设新生活的强烈愿望。这些演出增进了非洲和亚洲人民的反帝团结。
                              乌干达　一位观众
