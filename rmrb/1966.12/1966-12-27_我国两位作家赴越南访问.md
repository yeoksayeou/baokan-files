Title: Two Chinese Writers Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-12-27
Edition: 4

According to the 1966 execution plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Chinese writers Li Xueao and Wang Xingyuan departed from Beijing today to visit Vietnam.

### 我国两位作家赴越南访问

1966-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我国两位作家赴越南访问
    新华社二十六日讯　根据中越文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划我国作家李学鳌和王杏元，今天离开北京赴越南访问。
