Title: Shanghai Machine Tool Factory's Success in Designing a Chinese-Style Thread Grinder
Author:
Date: 1966-12-22
Edition: 2

Professional and amateur designers at the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, guided by Mao Zedong Thought, have successfully designed and tested a thread grinder with a distinct Chinese style. This grinder is easy to operate and achieves "zero-grade" precision. The image shows them using the Chinese-style thread grinder.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
