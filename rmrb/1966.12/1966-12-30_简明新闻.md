	Brief News

	North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three American Aggressor Aircraft

	On the afternoon of the 29th, the military and civilians of the North Vietnamese province of Ha Bac shot down an invading American aggressor unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft.

	According to a supplementary report, on the 19th, the military and civilians of the provinces of Ha Tinh and Nghe An shot down two invading American aggressor aircraft.

	Cambodia Cuts Off Consular and Trade Relations with South Korean Puppet Regime

	According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 27th, the Cambodian Ministry of Information issued a statement announcing that Cambodia has decided to sever consular and trade relations with the South Korean puppet regime.

	South Yemen Nationalists Inflict Heavy Losses on British Colonial Forces

	In the month from November 17 to December 17, the nationalist forces and commando units in the occupied South Yemen killed 68 British soldiers and 4 British officers, and wounded 88 others. During this period, they also shot down two British helicopters in the eastern Ladfan and eastern Hawashi areas.

	Italian People Protest Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

	On the 24th, dozens of anti-U.S. aggression in Vietnam and pro-Vietnamese people demonstrations took place in various parts of Italy.

	In Milan, several hundred young people gathered in the Piazza San Babila, chanting slogans to protest against the U.S. bombing of Hanoi and the expansion of aggression against the Vietnamese people.

	In Turin, people held an all-night vigil in the Modern Art Museum hall to support the Vietnamese people.

### 简明新闻

1966-12-30
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越北方军民击落美国强盗飞机三架
    越南北方河北省军民二十九日下午击落入侵的美国强盗无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。
    另据补充报道：河静省和义安省军民十九日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    柬埔寨同南朝鲜傀儡断绝领事关系
    据柬埔寨新闻社二十七日报道：柬埔寨新闻部发表公报宣布，柬埔寨决定同南朝鲜傀儡政权断绝领事和贸易关系。
    南也门民族主义者重创英国殖民军
    在十一月十七日至十二月十七日的一个月里，被占领的南也门民族主义者和突击队打死六十八名英国兵和四名英国军官，另有八十八名受伤。在此期间，还在东拉德凡和东哈瓦希卜两地共击落两架英国直升飞机。
    意大利人民示威抗议美国侵略越南
    意大利各地二十四日发生了几十起反对美国侵越战争、支持越南人民的示威活动。
    几百名青年人在米兰的圣巴比拉广场举行集会，高呼口号抗议美国轰炸河内和扩大对越南人民的侵略的罪行。
  　在都灵，人们在现代美术馆大厅里举行通宵守夜，声援越南人民。
                            （据新华社讯）
