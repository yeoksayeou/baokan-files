Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Returns Home After Visiting Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-12-11
Edition: 4

A delegation from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, led by Vice Chairman Kang Yonghe, concluded a 10-day visit to Japan at the invitation of the Japanese Trade Union Confederation. The delegation, consisting of five members, returned to Beijing on December 7.

### 我工会代表团访日后回国

1966-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团访日后回国
    新华社八日讯　以中华全国总工会副主任康永和为首的中国工会代表团一行五人，应日本工会总评议会的邀请到日本访问十天以后，已于十二月七日回京。
