    Brief News

    Algerians Protest at U.S. Embassy
    About five hundred people from various sectors of Algeria demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy in Algeria on the 20th, condemning U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam. The protesters handed a letter of protest to the U.S. Ambassador, demanding that the U.S. withdraw its invading troops from South Vietnam and dismantle all its military bases there.

    Iraq Halts Trade with Three U.S. Firms
    According to a report by the Iraqi News Agency on the 20th, the Iraqi government's high-level supply committee decided to halt all transactions with three U.S. companies—Ford Motor Company, American Radio Company, and Coca-Cola Beverage Company—as well as their subsidiaries. This decision was made because these companies do business with Israel, which is a tool of U.S. aggression against the Arab world.

    Italian Transport Workers Strike Nationwide
    On the 21st, 150,000 urban and intercity bus and tram workers across Italy went on a 48-hour strike, demanding wage increases. This is their second major strike this month, causing Italy's public transportation to come to a standstill.

### 简明新闻

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    阿尔及利亚人民在美使馆前示威
    阿尔及利亚各界人民约五百人，二十日在美国驻阿尔及利亚大使馆前示威游行，谴责美帝侵略越南的罪行。示威者向美国大使递交了抗议书，要求美国从南越撤走它的侵略军并撤除它在那里的一切军事基地。
    伊拉克停止同三家美国公司交易
    据伊拉克通讯社二十日报道，伊拉克政府高级供应委员会决定停止同福特汽车公司、美国无线电公司和可口可乐饮料公司等三家美国公司以及它们的分公司的一切交易，因为这些公司同美国侵略阿拉伯的工具以色列做生意。
    意大利全国城市交通工人总罢工
    意大利全国各地的市内和长途公共汽车、电车工人十五万人，二十一日举行四十八小时总罢工，要求增加工资。这是他们在这个月内的第二次大罢工；罢工已经使意大利的公共交通陷于停顿。　（据新华社讯）
