Title: Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1966-12-26
Edition: 1

Workers should focus on their work, but they should also learn military affairs, politics, and culture. They should also carry out socialist education movements and criticize the bourgeoisie.

Excerpted from the editorial "The Whole Country Should Become a Big School of Mao Zedong Thought" in the People's Daily.
