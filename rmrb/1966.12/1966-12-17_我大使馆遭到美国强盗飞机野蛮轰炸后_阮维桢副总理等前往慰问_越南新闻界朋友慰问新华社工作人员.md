Title: After the Savage Bombing of Our Embassy by American Bandit Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-12-17
Edition: 1

Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh and Others Pay Condolences
Vietnamese Journalists Pay Condolences to Xinhua News Agency Staff

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 15 — After learning that the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam had been savagely bombed by American bandit aircraft, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Duy Trinh, Deputy Foreign Ministers Hoang Van Ho and Nguyen Khac Tho, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee Tran Van Hien, and other responsible persons from various sectors of Vietnam visited the Chinese Embassy in the afternoon of December 14 to express their sincere condolences to Ambassador Zhu Qifeng and the entire embassy staff. They also inspected the bombed site.

The Director of the News Department of the Vietnamese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Vu Dinh, and friends from the Hanoi press also rushed to the Xinhua News Agency's Hanoi branch to express their condolences to the Xinhua staff.

### 我大使馆遭到美国强盗飞机野蛮轰炸后  阮维桢副总理等前往慰问  越南新闻界朋友慰问新华社工作人员

1966-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我大使馆遭到美国强盗飞机野蛮轰炸后
    阮维桢副总理等前往慰问
    越南新闻界朋友慰问新华社工作人员
    新华社河内十五日电　越南民主共和国政府副总理兼外交部长阮维桢、外交部副部长黄文利、阮基石，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴和越南其他方面的负责人，十四日下午在得悉中华人民共和国驻越南大使馆遭到美国强盗飞机的野蛮轰炸的消息后，先后到达中国大使馆，向朱其文大使和使馆全体人员表示亲切慰问，并且察看了被轰炸的现场。
    越南外交部新闻司司长吴田和河内新闻界的朋友，也赶到新华社驻河内分社，向新华社工作人员表示慰问。
