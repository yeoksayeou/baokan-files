Title: Holding the Quotations Book in Hand
Author: Zhou Shaowen, Ding Kunyuan
Date: 1966-12-17
Edition: 3

	Holding the Quotations Book in hand,
	The thoughts of Mao Zedong shine brightly;
	Border guards cherish it like a treasure,
	Revolution to the end relies on it.
	Holding the Quotations Book in hand,
	Learning it blooms in the heart;
	Strive hard in practice,
	Realize the revolutionization of thought.
	Holding the Quotations Book in hand,
	Learning it boosts training spirit;
	With red thoughts and strong skills,
	Dare to cross mountains of knives, dare to enter seas of fire!
