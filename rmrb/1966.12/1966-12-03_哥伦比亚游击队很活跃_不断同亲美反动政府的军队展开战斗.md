Title: Colombian Guerrillas Highly Active
Author:
Date: 1966-12-03
Edition: 4

The guerrilla forces active in southwestern Colombia have recently engaged in combat with the pro-American reactionary government's military.

After repelling a government offensive that began in late August, the guerrillas have once again clashed with government troops in the jurisdiction of Chaparral in the province of Tolima. According to official reports, the guerrillas killed and wounded one government soldier each. In the Yari Plains region of Caquetá, the guerrillas have also engaged in battles with government forces.

### 哥伦比亚游击队很活跃  不断同亲美反动政府的军队展开战斗

1966-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚游击队很活跃
    不断同亲美反动政府的军队展开战斗
    活跃在哥伦比亚西南部的游击队最近又同亲美反动政府的军队展开战斗。
    游击队在打退了政府军八月下旬开始的一次进攻后，最近又在托利马省查帕拉尔市的管辖区内同政府军作战。据官方报道，游击队打死打伤政府军各一人。在卡克塔州的雅里平原地区，游击队也同政府军展开了战斗。
