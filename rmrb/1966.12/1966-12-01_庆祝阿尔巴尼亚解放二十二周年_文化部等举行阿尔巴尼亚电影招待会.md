Title: Celebrating the 22nd Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-12-01
Edition: 4

The Ministry of Culture, the State Council for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the China-Albania Friendship Association jointly held a premiere screening of the Albanian film "Messengers of Light" today, enthusiastically celebrating the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

Members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, including Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Xiao, and responsible officials from relevant departments such as Chu Tu-nan, Li Qi, Song Yi-ping, and He Biao, as well as more than a thousand revolutionary masses from the capital, attended the screening.

The entire delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association, led by Petrit Doda, a member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Korça People's Assembly, Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Natanaili, and Albanian comrades in Beijing were invited to attend the screening.

Cultural officials from various countries' embassies in China were also invited.

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年  文化部等举行阿尔巴尼亚电影招待会

1966-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年
    文化部等举行阿尔巴尼亚电影招待会
    新华社三十日讯　文化部、对外文委和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会，今天联合举行阿尔巴尼亚影片《光明的使者》首映招待会，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年。
    中共中央委员、外交部副部长刘晓，各有关方面的负责人楚图南、李琦、宋一平、贺彪，以及首都革命群众一千多人，出席了招待会。
    由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、科尔察区人民会议执行委员会主席彼·多德率领的阿中友协代表团全体成员，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利，以及在京的阿尔巴尼亚同志应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国大使馆文化官员也应邀出席。
