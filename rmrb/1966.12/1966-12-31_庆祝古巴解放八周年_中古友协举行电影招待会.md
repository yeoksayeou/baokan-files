Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of Cuba's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-12-31
Edition: 5

The China-Cuba Friendship Association held a film screening event this afternoon to celebrate the eighth anniversary of Cuba's liberation. Leaders from the association and relevant departments attended the event.

Ambassador Pino Santos of Cuba to China, as well as the Cuban government's trade delegation, were also invited to attend the screening.

The film "The Young Generation" was shown during the event.

### 庆祝古巴解放八周年  中古友协举行电影招待会

1966-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝古巴解放八周年
    中古友协举行电影招待会
    新华社三十日讯中国古巴友好协会今天下午举行电影招待会，庆祝古巴解放八周年。中古友协会长申健等有关方面负责人出席了电影招待会。
    古巴驻中国大使皮诺·桑托斯以及古巴政府贸易代表团也应邀出席招待会。
    招待会上放映了中国故事片《年青的一代》。
