Title: Cuban Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 5

The Cuban government trade delegation, led by deputy head Ismael Bello, arrived in Beijing by airplane this afternoon.

The head of the delegation, Cuban Ambassador to China, Pino Santos, is already in Beijing.

### 古巴政府贸易代表团到京

1966-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社十四日讯　古巴政府贸易代表团在副团长伊斯迈尔·贝略率领下，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团长、古巴驻中国大使皮诺·桑托斯已在北京。
