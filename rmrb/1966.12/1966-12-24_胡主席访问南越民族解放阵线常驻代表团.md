Title: Chairman Hu Visits the Permanent Representative Office of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front
Author:
Date: 1966-12-24
Edition: 5

	Chairman Hu Visits the Permanent Representative Office of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: According to reports from Hanoi newspapers on the 23rd, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently visited the Permanent Representative Office of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in North Vietnam.

	Accompanying Chairman Hu on the visit were Vice Premier Pham Hung.

	During the visit, Chairman Hu inquired about the work and health of all members and staff of the representative office, and also inspected the living and working conditions of the office.

	Nguyen Van Tien, the head of the Permanent Representative Office of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in North Vietnam, on behalf of the people of South Vietnam, the entire staff and members of the representative office, expressed their deep gratitude to Chairman Ho Chi Minh for his visit and concern. He also, on behalf of the people of South Vietnam, wished Chairman Ho Chi Minh a long life. He further stated that the people of South Vietnam will resolutely fight and defeat the American aggressors.

### 胡主席访问南越民族解放阵线常驻代表团

1966-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    胡主席访问南越民族解放阵线常驻代表团
    新华社河内二十三日电　据河内报纸二十三日报道，越南民主共和国主席胡志明最近访问了越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团。
    陪同胡主席一起前往访问的有政府副总理范雄。
    胡志明主席在访问时，询问了代表团全体成员和工作人员的工作和健康情况，还看了代表团的食宿和工作的地方。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团团长阮文进代表越南南方人民、代表团全体成员和工作人员，对胡志明主席的访问和关怀表示无限感激，并且代表越南南方人民祝胡志明主席万寿无疆。他还表示，越南南方人民将坚决战斗，战胜美国侵略强盗。
