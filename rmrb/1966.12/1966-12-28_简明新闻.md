	Brief News
	Asia-Africa Journalists' Association Friendship Delegation Visits Gaza

	The Asia-Africa Journalists' Association, led by Secretary-General Sugiyama Shihei, arrived in Gaza on December 26 at the invitation of the Palestine Liberation Organization for a friendly visit.

	Before leaving Cairo, Chairman Shukairy of the Palestine Liberation Organization received the delegation and expressed full support for the revolutionary line of the Asia-Africa Journalists' Association.

	Tanzania Sends Over 10,000 Tons of Grain to Guinea

	Tanzania's Second Vice President Kawawa inspected the loading of 10,200 tons of corn and rice onto the Chinese ocean-going vessel "Friendship" at the port of Dar es Salaam on December 22. This grain is being sent to support the people of Guinea in their struggle against the United States.

	U.S. Rushes More Military Equipment to Jordan

	The U.S. State Department announced on December 22 that the U.S. government is accelerating the delivery of more military equipment to Jordan to bolster the faltering Hussein regime. A significant portion of this equipment will be airlifted.

	5,000 Dockworkers in Saigon Go on Strike

	Nearly 5,000 dockworkers at the port of Saigon in South Vietnam began a general strike on December 26 to protest the dismissal of 600 workers by the U.S. military authorities.

	According to Western news agencies, the strike has completely paralyzed this major port used by the invading U.S. forces. Ten ships carrying "most urgent war supplies" for the invading U.S. forces are stranded at the port, unable to unload their cargo.

	UK Unemployment Rises to Over 600,000

	The British Ministry of Labour announced on December 22 that the number of unemployed in the UK, including Northern Ireland, has risen to 601,771, the highest December figure since 1962.

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    亚非记协友好代表团到加沙访问
    由亚非新闻工作者协会书记处书记杉山市平率领的亚非新闻工作者协会友好代表团，应巴勒斯坦解放组织的邀请，二十六日到达加沙进行友好访问。
    在代表团离开罗之前，巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里曾接见了他们，表示完全支持亚非新闻工作者协会的革命路线。
    坦桑尼亚送几内亚一万多吨粮食
    坦桑尼亚第二副总统卡瓦瓦二十二日在达累斯萨拉姆港口，视察了中国远洋轮船“友好号”装运坦桑尼亚送给几内亚的一万零二百吨玉米和大米的工作。
    这批粮食，是为了支持几内亚人民的反美斗争而送去的。
    美国加紧向约旦运送更多的军火
    据美国国务院二十二日晚宣布，美国政府正加速运送更多的军事装备给约旦，来支撑摇摇欲坠的侯赛因政权，这批装备的很大部分将由飞机空运去。
    西贡五千名码头工人举行总罢工
    南越西贡港口的近五千名码头工人，为了抗议美军当局解雇六百名工人，在二十六日开始举行总罢工。
    据西方通讯社报道，罢工已经使这个供侵越美军使用的主要港口完全陷于瘫痪。十艘载运侵越美军“最紧迫的作战物资”的船只停在港口，无法卸货。
    英失业人数增至六十万一千多人
    英国劳工部二十二日公布的数字表明，包括北爱尔兰在内，英国的失业人数增加到了六十万零一千七百七十一人，这是一九六二年以来的十二月份的最高失业数字。
                          （据新华社讯）
