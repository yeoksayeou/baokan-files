Title: Nguyen Minh Huong Meets with All Crew Members of the "South China Sea Hero Ship"
Author:
Date: 1966-12-27
Edition: 4

Nguyen Minh Huong, deputy head of the Vietnam National Liberation Front's permanent delegation to China, met with all the heroic crew members and families of the martyrs of the "South China Sea Hero Ship" during his visit to Guangzhou on December 25.

During the meeting, Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Huong enthusiastically praised the crew members' heroic spirit in their struggle against American aggressor planes. He also, on behalf of the Vietnam National Liberation Front and the people of South Vietnam, extended warm greetings to the heroic crew members and the families of the martyrs.

Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Huong also presented a flag of the Vietnam National Liberation Front and other commemorative items to the "South China Sea Hero Ship." Captain Chen Daying of the "South China Sea Hero Ship" expressed gratitude on behalf of all crew members and the families of the martyrs for the warm greetings from Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Huong. He also stated their determination to forever stand together with the Vietnamese people, fighting unitedly to defeat the common enemy of the peoples of China, Vietnam, and the world—the American imperialists.

### 阮明芳会见我“南海英雄船”全体船员  代表南越民族解放阵线向船员和烈士家属表示慰问

1966-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    阮明芳会见我“南海英雄船”全体船员
    代表南越民族解放阵线向船员和烈士家属表示慰问
    新华社广州二十六日电　越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳二十五日在广州访问时，会见了“南海英雄船”全体英雄船员和烈士家属。
    会见时，阮明芳代理团长热情赞扬了船员们在同美国强盗飞机斗争时所表现的英勇精神，并代表越南南方民族解放阵线和越南南方人民向英雄船员和烈士家属表示亲切慰问。
    阮明芳团长还向“南海英雄船”赠送了一面越南南方民族解放阵线旗帜以及其它纪念品。“南海英雄船”指导船长陈大瑛代表全体船员和烈士家属对阮明芳代理团长的亲切慰问表示感谢，并表示决心永远同越南人民团结战斗在一起，打败中越两国人民和全世界人民的共同敌人美帝国主义。
