	Notice for the "Asian and African Peoples' Anti-Imperialist Cartoon Exhibition"

	In response to the broad masses' request, the "Asian and African Peoples' Anti-Imperialist Cartoon Exhibition" will be extended for one week, closing on December 22nd. Welcome to visit.
