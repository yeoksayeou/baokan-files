Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-12-30
Edition: 1

	The history of humanity is one of continually progressing from the realm of necessity to the realm of freedom. This history will never end. As long as there are classes in society, class struggle will never end. Even in a classless society, the struggle between the new and the old, between right and wrong, will never end. In the realms of production struggle and scientific experiment, humanity is always developing, and nature is always developing; it will never stop at a certain level. Therefore, humanity must constantly sum up experience, make discoveries, inventions, and creations, and make progress.

### 毛主席语录

1966-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    人类的历史，就是一个不断地从必然王国向自由王国发展的历史。这个历史永远不会完结。在有阶级存在的社会内，阶级斗争不会完结。在无阶级存在的社会内，新与旧、正确与错误之间的斗争永远不会完结。在生产斗争和科学实验范围内，人类总是不断发展的，自然界也总是不断发展的，永远不会停止在一个水平上。因此，人类总得不断地总结经验，有所发现，有所发明，有所创造，有所前进。
