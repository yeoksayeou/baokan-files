Title: Glory to the Great Mao Zedong
Author: 
Date: 1966-12-07
Edition: 5

Glory to the great Mao Zedong! Wishing you eternal life! You, the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, have creatively applied Marxism-Leninism, transforming a poor and backward China into a modern paradise on earth. Glory to the leadership of the Communist Party of China, glory to the great Mao Zedong and his comrades-in-arms, glory to the warriors who have sacrificed their lives! They have defeated the reactionaries and American imperialism. They have not only spread a new faith in China but also in all countries of the world, striving for one goal: to forever eliminate exploitation, oppression, poverty, and war, to let the sun shine forever over the earth!

### 光荣归于伟大的毛泽东
摩洛哥　贡拉德
1966-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    光荣归于伟大的毛泽东
    摩洛哥　贡拉德光荣归于伟大的毛泽东，祝您万寿无疆！您，当代最伟大的马列主义者，创造性地运用了马克思列宁的理论，把一个贫穷落后的中国，建设成为一个现代的人间天堂。光荣归于中国共产党的领导，光荣归于伟大的毛泽东和他的战友，光荣归于不惜牺牲自己的生命的战士，他们打败了反动派和美帝国主义。他们不仅在中国而且在世界上所有国家传播新的信仰，为着一个目标：永远消灭剥削、压迫、贫困和战争，为着让太阳永远普照大地！
