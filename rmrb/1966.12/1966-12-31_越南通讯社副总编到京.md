Title: Deputy Editor of the Vietnam News Agency Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-12-31
Edition: 5

According to the 1966 execution plan of the Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation agreement, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Vietnam News Agency, Chen Qingchun, arrived in Beijing today for a friendly visit to China.

### 越南通讯社副总编到京

1966-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社副总编到京
    新华社三十日讯　根据中越文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划到我国进行友好访问的越南通讯社副总编辑陈青春，今天到达北京。
