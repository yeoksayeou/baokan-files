Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-12-18
Edition: 4

The Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association held a film screening event today to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace Guo Moruo, and relevant leaders such as Yang Xiufeng, Luo Guibo, Peng Shaohui, and Song Yiping attended the event.

Deputy Head of the Permanent Mission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Nguyen Minh Phuong, and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Xuan Binh were invited to attend. The event screened documentaries: "Determined to Defeat the American Aggressors" and "Vietnam's Anti-American Struggle."

According to Xinhua News Agency on the 17th, members of the Beijing China-Vietnam Friendship Commune held a report meeting today to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. They unanimously strongly condemned the American imperialists' continuous bombing of Hanoi and firmly supported the Vietnamese people in their struggle to the end of the anti-American national salvation war. Representative of the Permanent Mission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Nguyen Thuan Ho, was invited to give a report, introducing the great victories achieved by the people of South Vietnam in their heroic struggle against American aggressors.

The commune's leaders, in their speeches, expressed their strong support for the statements issued by our government and firmly supported the statements issued by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, pledging to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and support the Vietnamese people with practical actions.

According to Xinhua News Agency on the 16th, to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, workers of the Beijing "June 7th" Locomotive and Rolling Stock Works held a report meeting this evening, warmly congratulating the Vietnamese military and people on their brilliant victories in the anti-American national salvation war, and strongly condemning the American imperialists' continuous bombing of Hanoi.

Representative of the Permanent Mission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Nguyen Thuan Ho, was invited to give a report.

Nguyen Thuan Ho said in his report that the Chinese people, led by Chairman Mao, are willing to make any sacrifice to support the Vietnamese people in their anti-American national salvation struggle, which has greatly encouraged the Vietnamese people.

The factory's leaders, in their speeches, expressed their complete support for the statement issued by our government on the 15th and firmly supported the statement issued by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the 14th, vowing to be a strong backing for the Vietnamese people.

### 热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年  我和委会、中越友协等举行电影招待会中越友好公社和“二七”机车厂分别集会

1966-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年
    我和委会、中越友协等举行电影招待会中越友好公社和“二七”机车厂分别集会
    据新华社十七日讯中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化友好协会、中越友好协会今天举行电影招待会，热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年。
    人大常委会副委员长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主任郭沫若，各有关方面负责人杨秀峰、罗贵波、彭绍辉、宋一平等出席了招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳、越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平应邀出席了招待会。招待会上放映纪录片：《决心击败美国侵略强盗》、《抗美斗争中的越南》。
    据新华社十七日讯北京中越友好人民公社社员在今天为庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年举行的报告会上，一致表示最强烈地谴责美帝国主义连续轰炸河内的滔天罪行，最坚决地支持越南人民把抗美救国战争进行到底。越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表阮顺和应邀在会上作报告，介绍了越南南方人民在英勇抗击美国侵略者的斗争中取得的伟大胜利。
    公社的负责人在讲话中表示热烈拥护我国政府的声明和坚决支持越南民主共和国政府的声明，表示要高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，以实际行动支持兄弟的越南人民。
    据新华社十六日讯　为庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立六周年，北京“二七”机车车辆工厂职工今晚举行报告会，热烈祝贺越南军民在抗美救国战争中所取得的辉煌胜利，并且强烈谴责美帝连续轰炸河内的滔天罪行。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表阮顺和应邀在会上作了报告。
    阮顺和在报告中说，以毛主席为首的中国共产党领导的七亿中国人民，不惜任何牺牲支持越南人民进行抗美救国斗争，给了越南人民极大的鼓舞。
    工厂的负责人在讲话中表示完全拥护我国政府十五日发表的声明，坚决支持越南民主共和国政府十四日发表的声明，誓作越南人民的坚强后盾。
