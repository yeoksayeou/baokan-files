Title: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-12-01
Edition: 4

The Chinese Academy of Sciences President Guo Moruo today received and hosted a dinner for a delegation of Japanese social scientists led by Bashita Keizan. The hosts and guests engaged in friendly and cordial conversation.

During the reception and dinner, Nishi Sonji, a Japanese peace activist residing in Beijing, was also present.

### 郭沫若院长接见日本客人

1966-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若院长接见日本客人
    新华社三十日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天接见并宴请以马场克三为首的日本社会科学家代表团，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见宴请时，在京日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
