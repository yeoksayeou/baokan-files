Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Vice Chairman Ton Duc Thang Reply to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho
Author:
Date: 1966-12-28
Edition: 4

Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Vice Chairman Ton Duc Thang of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on December 20, replied to a letter from Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. They expressed the determination of the people of North Vietnam to overcome all difficulties and hardships, and to support wholeheartedly the people of South Vietnam in their struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. The letter pointed out that the Vietnamese people's war of resistance is still a long and arduous one, but it will certainly achieve complete victory.

Nguyen Huu Tho had written to Ho Chi Minh and Ton Duc Thang on December 7, expressing the resolve of the military and civilian population of the South to endure hardships and sacrifices and to fight until final victory.

In their reply, Ho Chi Minh and Ton Duc Thang said: "Over the past six years, under the wise leadership of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, the people of the South have been united and fought bravely, achieving victory after victory in the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. The South is truly the bulwark of the nation and the pride of the people."

The letter further stated, "The people of the North will compete with the people of the South, determined to overcome all difficulties and hardships, to produce actively and fight bravely, and to support wholeheartedly the people of the South in their struggle for victory."

The letter pointed out: "The U.S. aggressors are suffering serious defeats. However, they will still resort to various brutal and cunning tactics. Therefore, our people's war of resistance is still a long and arduous one, but it will certainly achieve complete victory. We are determined to defend the North, liberate the South, and reunify the country." The letter concluded by wishing the people of South Vietnam greater and more brilliant victories in the new year.

### 胡志明主席和孙德胜副主席复信阮友寿主席  越南人民长期艰苦的抗战必定彻底胜利  南方不愧为祖国的铜墙　北方一定要全力支持

1966-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席和孙德胜副主席复信阮友寿主席
    越南人民长期艰苦的抗战必定彻底胜利
    南方不愧为祖国的铜墙　北方一定要全力支持
    新华社河内二十七日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明、副主席孙德胜二十日在给越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿的复信中，表示越南北方人民决心克服一切艰苦困难，全心全力支援越南南方人民夺取抗美救国斗争的胜利。信中指出，越南人民的抗战还是长期的、艰苦的，但是必定取得彻底胜利。
    阮友寿曾在十二月七日写信给胡志明和孙德胜，表示南方军民不怕艰苦牺牲，决心战斗到彻底胜利。
    胡志明和孙德胜在信里说：“六年来，在越南南方民族解放阵线的英明领导下，南方同胞团结一致，英勇战斗，在抗美救国事业中，从一个胜利走向另一个胜利。南方不愧为祖国的铜墙，不愧为民族的骄傲。”
    信里表示，“北方同胞要同南方同胞展开竞赛，决心克服一切困难、艰苦，边积极生产，边勇敢战斗，全心全力支援南方同胞夺取抗战的胜利。”
    信里指出：“美国侵略强盗正在遭到严重失败。然而，他们还会玩弄许多残暴和狡猾的手段。因此，我国全民的抗战还是长期的、艰苦的，但是必定取得彻底胜利。我们一定完成保卫北方，解放南方，统一祖国的事业。”信里祝贺越南南方人民在新的一年中取得更大更辉煌的新胜利。
