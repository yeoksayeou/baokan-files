Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Meets with the Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Resident in the North
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 5

Chairman Ho Chi Minh recently received the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam resident in the North, headed by Nguyen Van Tien and other members of the delegation. Chairman Ho Chi Minh told them, "With the unity of the entire people and the solidarity of the North and South, we will surely win, and the United States will surely be defeated. Our country will definitely be reunified."

During the meeting, Nguyen Van Tien conveyed the warmest greetings from the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the people of South Vietnam to Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the people of the North.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh warmly greeted all members of the delegation and extended warm greetings to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho and the members of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. Chairman Ho Chi Minh also enthusiastically praised the heroic struggles and brilliant victories of the military and people of the South.

Present at the meeting were some leaders from the Party and government of Vietnam and relevant departments.

### 胡志明主席接见南越民族解放阵线常驻北方代表团时说  全民团结　南北一心　越南必胜　美国必败  赞扬南方军民英勇战斗所取得的辉煌胜利

1966-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见南越民族解放阵线常驻北方代表团时说
    全民团结　南北一心　越南必胜　美国必败
    赞扬南方军民英勇战斗所取得的辉煌胜利
    新华社河内十四日电　据这里的报纸十四日报道：胡志明主席最近接见了越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团团长阮文进和代表团其他成员。胡志明主席对他们说，全民团结，南北一心，我们必胜，美国必败，我国一定统一。
    接见时，阮文进向胡志明主席表达了越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会和越南南方人民，对胡志明主席和北方同胞的最亲切的问候。
    胡志明主席亲切地向代表团全体成员问好，并向阮友寿主席和越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会委员们表示亲切问候。胡志明主席还热烈赞扬了正在英勇地战斗和取得辉煌胜利的南方军民。
    接见时在座的有越南党和政府以及有关方面的一些领导人。
