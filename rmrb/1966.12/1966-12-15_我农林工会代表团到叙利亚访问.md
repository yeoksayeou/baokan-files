Title: Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union Delegation Visits Syria
Author:
Date: 1966-12-15
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union, led by Song Chuan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union, arrived in Damascus on December 12 at the invitation of the Syrian National Peasants Union to participate in Syria's Farmers' Day activities.

Members of the Chinese delegation include Wang Guofan, Chairman of the Jianming Commune.

They were welcomed at the airport by Ahmad Hamdouni, Chairman of the Syrian National Peasants Union, and representatives from Syria's Ministry of Agriculture and Land Reform.

Chinese Ambassador to Syria Chen Tan and Counselor Li Hua Nong also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我农林工会代表团到叙利亚访问

1966-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    我农林工会代表团到叙利亚访问
    新华社大马士革十二日电　由中国农林工会副主任宋川率领的中国农林工会代表团，应叙利亚全国农民联合会的邀请，在十二日到达这里，参加叙利亚农民节活动。
    中国代表团的团员有建明公社社长王国藩。
    他们在机场受到叙利亚全国农民联合会主席艾哈迈德·哈姆杜尼等以及叙利亚农业和土地改革部代表的欢迎。
    中国驻叙利亚大使陈坦和参赞李华农也到机场欢迎。
