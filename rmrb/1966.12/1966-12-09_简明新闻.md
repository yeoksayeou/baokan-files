Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Shoot Down an Invading U.S. Plane

Patriotic military and civilians in the Mang Khon area of Laos' Luang Prabang Province shot down an invading U.S. aggressor plane on November 16. The two U.S. aggressors and nine U.S. lackeys on board were all killed.

Okinawan Farmers Hold Rally Against U.S. Military Land Seizure

On December 6, 1,500 villagers from Shikawachō in Okinawa Prefecture held a mass rally to firmly oppose the U.S. military's seizure of local farmland for the expansion of a military base. They also expressed strong indignation against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and its occupation of Okinawa.

For over two months, starting from October 7, 10 to 20 farmers have been staging sit-ins daily on the land designated for seizure by the U.S. military.

People in Several Western Jordanian Cities Stage Protests Again

Despite the unreasonable suppression by the Jordanian authorities, people in Jerusalem (Jordanian sector), Nablus, Hebron, and Ramallah in western Jordan held demonstrations on December 6. They demanded to be armed to resist Israeli aggression and condemned the Jordanian authorities' policy on the Palestinian issue.

Syrian People Hold Demonstrations in Support of Jordanian People's Struggle

Thousands of people in Damascus, the capital of Syria, held demonstrations on December 7 to support the revolutionary struggle of the Jordanian people. The demonstrators chanted, "People's liberation war is the guarantee to defeat the aggressors, people's liberation war is the guarantee to liberate Palestine."

Syrian Head of State Atassi, in a rally held after the demonstration, said: The Syrian people support the Jordanian people's struggle on both banks of the Jordan River and stand together with all fighters against imperialism, reactionaries, and Israel.

Nearly 40,000 Health Workers in Chile Go on Strike

Nearly 40,000 workers and employees in Chile's national health sector went on a 48-hour strike on December 7. They protested against the government's violent suppression of their demonstrations and demanded improved living conditions.

### 简明新闻

1966-12-09
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    老挝爱国军民击落入侵美机一架
    老挝琅勃拉邦省芒昆地区爱国军民，十一月十六日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，飞机上的两个美国强盗和九个美国走狗全部丧命。
  冲绳农民集会反对美军征用土地
    日本冲绳县志川村民一千五百人六日举行群众大会，坚决反对美军为扩建军事基地而征用这里的农民土地，并且对美帝国主义侵略越南和霸占冲绳表示极大的愤怒。
    在从十月七日起的两个多月里，每天都有十到二十名农民在美军预定征用的土地上坚持静坐斗争。
    约旦西部几个城市人民再次示威
    约旦西部的耶路撒冷（约旦区）、纳布卢斯、哈利勒、拉马拉等城市的人民，不顾约旦当局的无理镇压，在六日又举行了示威，要求让他们武装起来抵抗以色列侵略，并且谴责约旦当局对巴勒斯坦问题的政策。
    叙人民示威支持约旦人民的斗争
    叙利亚首都大马士革数以千计的群众，七日举行示威游行，支持约旦人民的革命斗争。示威者高呼：“人民解放战争是打败侵略者的保证，人民解放战争是解放巴勒斯坦的保证”。
    叙利亚国家元首阿塔西在游行示威结束后举行的集会上说：叙利亚人民支持约旦人民在约旦河两岸进行的斗争，并且同所有反对帝国主义、反动派和以色列的战士站在一起。
    智利近四万名卫生工作人员罢工
    智利全国卫生事业的工人和职员近四万人，七日举行四十八小时罢工，抗议政府镇压他们的示威游行的暴行，要求改善生活条件。
                          （据新华社讯）
