Nyerere lays foundation stone for Tanzania Bank building

President Nyerere presided over the foundation-laying ceremony for the Tanzania Bank building in Dar es Salaam on December 9.

The establishment of this national bank in Tanzania coincided with the issuance of the national currency in June this year. The issuance of the national currency and the establishment of the national bank are two important measures taken by the Tanzanian government to develop the national economy.

Iraq and Kuwait support the struggle of the Palestinian people

According to a broadcast by Baghdad Radio on December 6, Iraq and Kuwait issued a joint communiqué stating that all Arab forces must unite to confront imperialism and Zionism. The communiqué also expressed support for the Palestine Liberation Organization and the Palestinian people in their struggle to reclaim their usurped homeland.

West German students drive out South Vietnamese puppet "ambassador"

On the evening of December 7, the "ambassador" of the South Vietnamese puppet regime to West Germany, Nguyen Quoc Anh, was giving a speech at a university in West Berlin, defending the crimes of U.S. aggression in Vietnam. At this time, 600 students who were nearby participating in an anti-U.S. aggression in Vietnam rally rushed into the venue and held a demonstration. Amidst a chorus of protests, the South Vietnamese "ambassador" dared not continue his speech and had to slink away through the back door.

France rejects U.S. attempt to retain bases in France

France has firmly rejected the U.S. attempt to continue to retain its military bases in France.

France's representative to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's Standing Council, De Gaulle, announced at a Standing Council meeting on December 7 that negotiations between France and the U.S. on this issue had broken down.

### 简明新闻

1966-12-12

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    尼雷尔总统为坦桑银行大厦奠基
    尼雷尔总统九日在达累斯萨拉姆主持了坦桑尼亚银行大厦的奠基典礼。
    坦桑尼亚的这家国家银行是在今年六月发行坦桑尼亚国家货币时成立的。发行坦桑尼亚本国货币和成立国家银行，是坦桑尼亚政府发展民族经济的两项重要措施。
    伊科两国支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争
    据巴格达电台六日广播，伊拉克和科威特发表联合公报指出，所有阿拉伯力量必须团结起来，对付帝国主义和犹太复国主义。公报还表示支持巴勒斯坦解放组织和巴勒斯坦人民为收复被篡夺的家园而进行的斗争。
    西德大学生怒逐南越傀儡“大使”
    南越傀儡政权驻西德“大使”阮贵英七日晚上在西柏林一所大学讲话，为美帝侵越罪行辩解，这时，正在附近参加反对美国侵略越南的集会的六百名大学生冲进会场，举行示威。在一片抗议声中，这个南越“大使”不敢再讲下去，只好灰溜溜地从后门溜走。
    法国拒绝美保留在法基地的企图
    法国坚决拒绝美国想继续保留它在法国的军事基地的企图。
    法国驻北大西洋集团常设理事会代表德勒斯七日在常设理事会会议上宣布，法美两国关于这个问题的谈判已经破裂。
                          （据新华社讯）
