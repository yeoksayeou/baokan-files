Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Guests from Bechuanaland
Author:
Date: 1966-12-11
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with guests from Bechuanaland today in the afternoon. The guests were the Chairman of the Bechuanaland People's Party, Seretse Khama, and the representative of the Bechuanaland People's Party in Cairo, Bobo Keale. Chen Yi had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见贝专纳客人

1966-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见贝专纳客人
    新华社九日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见贝专纳人民党主席马坦特和贝专纳人民党驻开罗代表波比·迈克，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
