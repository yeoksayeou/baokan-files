Title: Learn and Apply
Author:
Date: 1966-12-23
Edition: 4

    Learn and Apply
    A Latin American friend visiting China went to the countryside to tour. On the plane, he took the initiative to help the flight attendants with their service work. The Chinese flight attendant expressed gratitude to him. This foreign friend said: "I have studied Chairman Mao's 'In Memory of Norman Bethune.' Learn and apply, I also want to serve the people."

### 学了就要用

1966-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    学了就要用
    一位到中国访问的拉丁美洲朋友到外地去参观。他在飞机上，主动帮助乘务员做服务工作。中国乘务员同志向他表示感谢。这位外国朋友说：“我学过毛主席的《为人民服务》。学了就要用，我也要为人民服务。”
