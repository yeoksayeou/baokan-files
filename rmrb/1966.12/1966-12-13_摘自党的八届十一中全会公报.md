Title: The Soviet Revisionist Leadership Clique's Policy of Soviet-American Cooperation to Dominate the World
Author:
Date: 1966-12-13
Edition: 4

The Soviet revisionist leadership clique pursues a policy of Soviet-American cooperation to dominate the world. In the international communist movement and the national liberation movement, they engage in activities of splitting, sabotage, and subversion, actively serving the interests of American imperialism.
