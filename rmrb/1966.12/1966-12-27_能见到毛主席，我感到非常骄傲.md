Title: Proud to Meet Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-12-27
Edition: 5

Recently, the Secretary-General of the Office of the Guinean Government, Ninihalé Koundé, who had visited China, spoke to a Chinese journalist in Conakry. He enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought.

Ninihalé said: "On the occasion of the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we were received by Chairman Mao, and we felt extremely proud. We saw that Chairman Mao is in very good health, and we are particularly happy about this. This is a great happiness for the revolutionary people of all countries."

He said: "Chairman Mao is the greatest leader of the Chinese people. His brilliant thoughts are inspiring and leading the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to continue advancing on the road to victory."

### 能见到毛主席，我感到非常骄傲

1966-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    能见到毛主席，我感到非常骄傲
    不久前访问过中国的几内亚政府办公厅秘书长尼尼哈莱·孔德特最近在科纳克里对中国记者发表谈话热烈颂扬毛主席和毛泽东思想。
    尼尼哈莱说：“在中华人民共和国成立十七周年的时候，我们受到毛主席的接见，感到非常骄傲。我们看到毛主席身体十分健康感到特别高兴。这是各国革命人民莫大的幸福”。
    他说：“毛主席是中国人民的最伟大的领袖。他的光辉思想正在鼓舞和领导中国的文化大革命在胜利的道路上继续前进。”
