Title: Soviet Art Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang to Celebrate "Korean-Soviet Friendship Decade"
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 3

　　Celebrating the "Korean-Soviet Friendship Decade"
  　Soviet Art Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd] The Soviet Art Delegation, consisting of ten members led by Director Markote (Director of the Moscow Theater), arrived here by plane at 1:00 PM on the 21st to perform in Korea to celebrate the "Korean-Soviet Friendship and Soviet Culture Decade." Starting from the 22nd, they will perform in Pyongyang for three days to entertain people from all walks of life, and then go to Chongjin, Hungnam, Hamhung, Wonsan, Sinuiju, Chinnampo and other places for performances and visits. They will also hold discussion meetings with people from the Korean cultural and artistic circles.

### 庆祝“朝苏友好旬间”　苏联艺术代表团抵平壤

1949-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝“朝苏友好旬间”
  　苏联艺术代表团抵平壤
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】为庆祝“朝苏友好和苏联文化旬间”来朝鲜公演的苏联艺术代表团一行十人，由团长马尔哥捷（莫斯科剧院院长）率领，已于二十一日午后一时飞抵此间。他们自二十二日起，在平壤表演三天招待各界，然后赴清津、兴南、咸兴、元山、新义州、镇南浦等地公演、参观，并与朝鲜文化艺术界人士举行座谈会。
