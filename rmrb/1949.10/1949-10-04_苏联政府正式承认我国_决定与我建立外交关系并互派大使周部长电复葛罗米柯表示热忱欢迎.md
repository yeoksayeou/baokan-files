Title: The Soviet Government Officially Recognizes Our Country: Decides to Establish Diplomatic Relations and Exchange Ambassadors; Minister Zhou Cables Gromyko to Express Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: On October 1st, after Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, sent Chairman Mao Zedong's announcement to the governments of all countries in the form of an official letter, the Soviet government received the announcement. On the 2nd, at 21:45, Gromyko sent a note to Minister Zhou Enlai, stating that the Soviet government had decided to establish diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and China and to exchange ambassadors. Gromyko's note is as follows:

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

The Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics has received the announcement of the Central People's Government of China of October 1 of this year, in which it proposes that the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union establish diplomatic relations. After studying the proposal of the Central People's Government of China, the Soviet government, in its unwavering desire to establish genuinely friendly relations with the Chinese people and convinced that the Central People's Government of China is the representative of the will of the overwhelming majority of the Chinese people, hereby notifies Your Excellency that the Soviet government has decided to establish diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China and to exchange ambassadors.

　　　　　　　　On Behalf of the Soviet Government, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Andrei Gromyko
　　　　　　　　　October 2, 1949, 21:45

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, replied to Andrei Gromyko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union in Moscow, expressing a warm welcome for the immediate establishment of diplomatic relations between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union and the exchange of ambassadors. Minister Zhou's reply reads:

To Mr. Andrei Gromyko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Moscow:

I, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, am honored to receive Your Excellency's telegram of October 2 of this year regarding the Soviet government's decision to establish diplomatic ties between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China. The Central People's Government of China is deeply convinced that the Soviet government has a profound friendship for the Chinese people and has become the first friendly nation to recognize the People's Republic of China. The Chinese government and the Chinese people feel boundless joy at this. I now inform Your Excellency that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China extends a warm welcome to the immediate establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union and the exchange of ambassadors.

　　　　　　Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1949, in Beijing

### 苏联政府正式承认我国  决定与我建立外交关系并互派大使周部长电复葛罗米柯表示热忱欢迎

1949-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府正式承认我国
    决定与我建立外交关系并互派大使周部长电复葛罗米柯表示热忱欢迎
    【新华社北京三日电】在本月一日下午中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，以公函向各国政府发出毛泽东主席的公告后，苏联政府业已收到此项公告。并于二日二十一时四十五分以葛罗米柯署名的照会电达周恩来部长，表示苏联政府决定建立苏中两国的外交关系，并互派大使。葛罗米柯的照会如下：
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来先生：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府业已收到中国中央人民政府本年十月一日之公告，其中建议中华人民共和国与苏联建立外交关系。苏联政府在研究了中国中央人民政府的建议之后，由于力求与中国人民建立真正友好关系的始终不渝的意愿，并确信中国中央人民政府是绝大多数中国人民意志的代表者，故特通知阁下：苏联政府决定建立苏联与中华人民共和国之间的外交关系，并互派大使。
    　　　　　　　　受苏联政府之委托　苏联外交部副部长
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　安德列·葛罗米柯
    　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月二日二十一时四十五分钟
    【新华社北京三日电】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来本日以照会电复莫斯科苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，表示热忱欢迎中苏两大国立即建立外交关系，并互派大使。周部长复电称：
    莫斯科苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部副部长安德列·葛罗米柯先生：
    我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府很荣幸地收到阁下本年十月二日来电关于苏联政府决定建立苏联与中华人民共和国之间的外交关系的通知。中华人民共和国中央人民政府深信苏联政府具有对中国人民的深厚友谊，今天又成为承认中华人民共和国的第一个友邦，中国政府和中国人民对此感到无限的欢欣。我现在通知阁下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府热忱欢迎立即建立中华人民共和国与苏联之间的外交关系，并互派大使。
    　　　　　　中华人民共和国中央人民政府　外交部部长　周恩来
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月三日于北京
