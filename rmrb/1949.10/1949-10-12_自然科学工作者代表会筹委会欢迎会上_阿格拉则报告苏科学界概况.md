Title: Preparatory Committee of the Natural Science Workers Representative Conference Holds Welcome Reception: Agra Reports on the State of Soviet Science

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 2

　　At the Welcome Reception of the Preparatory Committee of the Natural Science Workers Representative Conference
    Agra Reports on the State of Soviet Science
    [Report from this newspaper] The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Natural Science Workers Representative Conference welcomed L.E. Agra, Deputy Dean of the Georgian Academy of Sciences and Doctor of Technical Sciences from the Soviet delegation, at 2:00 PM on the 8th of this month to speak on the state of Soviet science. Representatives from various agencies and individuals from the scientific community, totaling over 900 people, attended. First, Chairman Wu Yuzhang delivered a welcome speech, stating that Soviet science has surpassed that of capitalist countries, and the construction of New China requires the help of the Soviet socialist country, especially in science and technology. In Dr. Agra's speech, he used the Georgian Republic as an example to illustrate the development of science in the Soviet Union. He said: Soviet scientists not only actively engage in academic research but also combine it with practice and politics, and every scientist considers serving the people as the greatest honor. Finally, he shouted "Long live Mao Zedong!" in Chinese.

### 自然科学工作者代表会筹委会欢迎会上　阿格拉则报告苏科学界概况

1949-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　自然科学工作者代表会筹委会欢迎会上
    阿格拉则报告苏科学界概况
    【本报讯】中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会于本月八日下午二时欢迎苏联代表团乔治亚科学院副院长技术科学博士Ｌ·Ｅ·阿格拉则讲苏联科学界的概况，到会的有各机关代表及科学界人士共九百余人。首先由主席吴玉章致欢迎词略谓：苏联的科学已超出于资本主义国家，新中国的建设需要苏联社会主义国家的帮助，尤其是科学技术方面。在阿格拉则博士的讲话中，他以乔治亚共和国为例说明苏联科学发展的情况。他说：苏联科学家不仅积极从事学术研究，而且与实际与政治相结合，每个科学家都以为人民服务为最大的光荣。最后他以中国话高呼毛泽东万岁！
