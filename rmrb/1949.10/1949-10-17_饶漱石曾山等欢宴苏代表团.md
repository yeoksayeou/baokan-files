Title: Rao Shushi, Zeng Shan, and Others Host Banquet for Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 2

　　Rao Shushi, Zeng Shan, and Others
  　Host Banquet for Soviet Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th: Rao Shushi, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zeng Shan and Pan Hannian, Vice Mayors of Shanghai, hosted a banquet at the International Hotel on the evening of the 15th to welcome the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers' delegation. Representatives from various circles of the Shanghai party, government, and military, as well as Soviet expatriates, totaling more than 200 people, were invited to attend. During the banquet, the orchestra led by Shanghai Soviet expatriates and the pianist Valterv from the Soviet art performance team performed wonderful songs. The hosts and guests were extremely convivial, frequently rising to toast the liberation of Guangzhou by the People's Liberation Army, Chairman Mao Zedong, Marshal Stalin, and the eternal friendship between China and the Soviet Union.

### 饶漱石曾山等欢宴苏代表团

1949-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　饶漱石曾山等
  　欢宴苏代表团
    【新华社上海十六日电】中共上海市委书记饶漱石及上海市副市长曾山、潘汉年十五日晚在国际饭店设宴欢迎苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团。应邀作陪的有沪市党政军各界代表和苏联侨民等共二百多人。席间有上海苏侨领导的乐队和苏联艺术演出队钢琴家瓦尔特尔夫演奏精彩歌曲，宾主极为欢洽，频频起立为人民解放军解放广州、为毛泽东主席、为斯大林大元帅、为中苏两国的永久友谊而干杯。
