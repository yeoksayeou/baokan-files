Title: Western Fujian Liberated: Wuping
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 28th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Wuping County in western Fujian on the 15th of this month, completely annihilating the defending enemy. In total, over 500 enemy soldiers were captured, and over 70 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded. Seized items included one 82mm mortar, nine heavy machine guns, sixteen light machine guns, over 300 rifles, and a portion of other military supplies.

### 福建西部解放武平

1949-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　福建西部解放武平
    【新华社福建前线二十八日电】人民解放军某部于本月十五日解放福建西部的武平县城，全歼守敌。计生俘匪五百余名，毙伤匪七十余名，缴获八二迫击炮一门、重机枪九挺、轻机枪十六挺、步枪三百余支及其他军用物资一部。
