Title: First Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday; Our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai and Others Personally Welcomed Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th: The Soviet Union's first ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin, arrived in Beijing by special train this afternoon. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, Mayor of Beijing Nie Rongzhen, our Ambassador to the Soviet Union Wang Jiaxiang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Deputy Director and Chief of Protocol Yan Baohang, Mr. Roshchin's Chinese friends Dong Biwu, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Zhang Bojun, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Li Dequan, Shi Liang, Wang Kunlun, Liao Chengzhi, Qian Junrui, and others, as well as Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy Chikhivinsky and embassy staff, members of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific delegation, and Soviet expatriates, totaling more than 50 people. More than 3,000 people gathered outside the station to welcome him, including workers, youth, women, and students. Chinese and Soviet national flags were hung at the entrance of the station's waiting room, with slogans in Chinese and Russian such as "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship." The special train arrived at 4:18 PM amidst welcoming military music. After Ambassador Roshchin alighted from the train, the band played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union. After the anthems, Chargé d'Affaires Chikhivinsky introduced the Ambassador to the welcoming party, who shook hands with him one by one. Ambassador Roshchin then delivered a speech on the platform, in which he, on behalf of the Soviet people and the Soviet government, congratulated the Chinese people on their victory in establishing the People's Republic of China; and expressed that he would make his utmost efforts to consolidate the close friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, in order to safeguard world peace and international security. Minister Zhou Enlai then delivered a reply, stating briefly: the diplomatic relations between China and the Soviet Union, and the deep friendship between the peoples of the two countries, will be further developed and consolidated in the future through the efforts of Ambassador Roshchin. After the speech, Ambassador Roshchin, accompanied by Minister Zhou and others, walked out of the station, receiving warm applause and cheers from the welcoming crowd. Ambassador Roshchin also happily shouted: "Long live the great Chinese people!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people!" and walked through the cheering crowds, nodding, applauding, and greeting everyone. Ambassador Roshchin then invited the welcomers to ride to the embassy to attend a cocktail party. At 4:55 PM, Ambassador Roshchin and others arrived at the embassy, and the Soviet flag was raised at the embassy. (Attached picture)

### 苏联驻我国第一任大使　罗申昨日抵京　我外长周恩来等亲赴车站欢迎

1949-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联驻我国第一任大使
    罗申昨日抵京
    我外长周恩来等亲赴车站欢迎
    【新华社北京十日电】苏联驻我国大使罗申今日下午专车抵京。前往车站欢迎的有外交部部长周恩来，北京市市长聂荣臻，我国驻苏大使王稼祥，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，副主任兼交际处长阎宝航，罗申大使的中国友人董必武、沈钧儒、郭沫若、章伯钧、滕代远、张治中、邵力子、李德全、史良、王昆仑、廖承志、钱俊瑞等，苏联大使馆代办齐赫文斯基和使领馆人员、苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团团员和苏联侨民等共五十余人。齐集车站外欢迎的群众有工人、青年、妇女、学生等三千余人。车站待客室门首悬挂中苏两国国旗，并贴有“中苏友好万岁”等中俄文标语。专车于四时十八分在欢迎的军乐声中抵站。罗申大使下车后，乐队即奏中苏两国国歌。奏毕，齐赫文斯基代办介绍大使与欢迎人员一一握手。罗申大使随即在月台上发表演说，在演说中罗申大使代表苏联人民和苏联政府对中国人民建立中华人民共和国的胜利，表示庆贺；并表示将以最大的努力来巩固苏联与中华人民共和国之间的亲切的友谊，以保卫世界和平与国际安全。周恩来部长继致答词，略称：中苏两国的邦交，中苏两国人民的深厚友谊，今后经过罗申大使的努力，将更加发展和巩固起来。致词毕，罗申大使即由周部长等陪同步出车站，受到欢迎群众的热烈鼓掌和欢呼。罗申大使也愉快地高呼：“伟大的中国人民万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席万岁！”并步行穿过欢呼着的群众行列，向大家颔首鼓掌和招呼。罗申大使继即邀请欢迎者乘车赴使馆参加鸡尾酒会。四时五十五分罗申大使等抵达使馆，大使馆即升起苏联国旗。（附图片）
