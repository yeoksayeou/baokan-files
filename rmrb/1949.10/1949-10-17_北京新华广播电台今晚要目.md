Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
　　Tonight's Program Highlights
    [From Our Newspaper] The Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's women's program at 18:15 tonight will introduce the life of women in Siam, one of the countries about to participate in the Asian Women's Representative Conference in Beijing. At 19:00, a lecture on natural science common knowledge, written by Zhang Jizhou of the Physics Research Institute of the National Peking Academy: "The Sun." At 18:30, the first literary program, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Association, will feature Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai performing the Pizi Opera "Yang Faguei Slams His Son." At 22:00, the second literary program will be broadcast by the Amateur New National Music Research Association, featuring Chinese music.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分妇女节目，介绍即将参加在北京召开的亚洲妇女代表会议的国家之一—暹逻妇女的生活情形。十九点自然科学常识讲座，国立北京研究院物理学研究所张济舟撰稿：“太阳”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办，顾荣甫、尹福来演唱牌子戏“杨发贵摔子”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，由业余新国乐研究社播送国乐。
