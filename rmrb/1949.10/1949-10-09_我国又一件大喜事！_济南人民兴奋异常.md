Title: Another Great Happy Event for Our Country! The People of Jinan are Extremely Excited

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 2

　　Another Great Happy Event for Our Country!
    Jinan People Extremely Excited
    The people of Jinan City were also very enthusiastic upon hearing the news. The chairman of the trade union of the Shandong Dazhong Daily Printing Factory said, "This is another great happy event following the founding of the People's Republic of China, demonstrating the position of the new China in the world." Yu Zengren, the director of the Fourth Factory of the Jinan Municipal Industry Bureau, said, "The great friendship of the Soviet Union towards the People's Republic of China is a guarantee of the success of our advancing cause."

### 我国又一件大喜事！　济南人民兴奋异常

1949-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　我国又一件大喜事！
    济南人民兴奋异常
    济南市人民闻讯后，情绪也十分热烈。山东大众日报印刷厂工会主任说：“这是继中华人民共和国成立后的再一件大喜事，显示出新中国在世界上的地位。”济南市工业局第四工厂厂长于增仁说：“苏联对中华人民共和国的伟大友谊，是我们前进事业的成功保证。”
