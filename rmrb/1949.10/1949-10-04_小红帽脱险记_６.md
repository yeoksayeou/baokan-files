Title: Little Red Riding Hood's Escape - 6
Author: Mei Zhi
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 8

　　Little Red Riding Hood's Escape
　　6
　　Oh no,
　　He's here!
　　Oh no,
　　His black demonic hands,
　　Are pushing open the wooden door!
　　Oh no!
　　………!
　
　　Just then,
　　The big white horse,
　　Jumped out
　　From behind the thatched cottage,
　　And aimed at the old mountain demon's spine,
　　Kicking him hard several times.
　
　　The old mountain demon,
　　Didn't even have time to see clearly,
　　Before being kicked to the ground,
　　Only able to cry out:
　　"Oh no,
　　I've been hit by a cannonball,
　　It hurts so much,
　　My stomach has exploded!
　　Hehe,
　　I can't eat milk and eggs anymore,
　　Hehe,
　　I can't hold any more fragrant and sweet white rice!"
　　The old mountain demon,
　　Began to cry loudly.
　
　　The big white horse
　　Was happy with his victory,
　　He wanted to sing a song,
　　To express his joy,
　　So he opened his mouth and raised his tail,
　　And neighed loudly.
　
　　"Damn it!
　　What the hell,
　　So it's you, you old nag,
　　That's just not right!
　　Think about it,
　　How good I've been to you,
　　Treated you like a guest,
　　I used you to drive the carriage,
　　Going back and forth together,
　　I used you to fight,
　　Charging into battle together,
　　Didn't I value you more than people?"
　
　　"You did value me highly,
　　And the effort I put in for you wasn't small,
　　And the money I earned for you wasn't little,
　　Yet you were still greedily whipping me,
　　Almost wanting me to give up my life.
　　I see that you've made it so,
　　That there's no one who doesn't hate you,
　　No one who doesn't resent you,
　　You're about to be finished,
　　I should have realized it sooner and run away sooner,
　　Who wants you, you wicked master!"
　
　　"Joke, joke,
　　This is really a joke!
　　Even loyal slaves
　　Are rebelling,
　　The world has really changed.
　　But I'm not afraid of you,
　　I understand you,
　　Tomorrow I'll come again,
　　Bringing a whip,
　　And also bringing a bundle of grass,
　　See which one you choose?
　
　　"Okay,
　　Little Red Riding Hood,
　　Old man's stomach hurts tonight,
　　Can't eat,
　　Tomorrow night I'll definitely come and eat you well."
　
　　The old mountain demon,
　　Tilting his head,
　　Frowning,
　　Holding his stomach with both hands,
　　Staggering down the mountain,
　　Step by step.
　　(End of Part Three)

### 小红帽脱险记  ６
梅志
1949-10-04
第8版()
专栏：

　　小红帽脱险记  
    ６
    不得了，
    他已经到了！
    不得了，
    他那黑色的魔手，
    正在推开柴门了！
    不得了！
    ………！
　
    这时候，
    大白马，
    从茅屋后面
    跳了出来，
    对准老山妖的背脊骨，
    狠命的踢了好几脚。
　
    老山妖，
    还来不及看清，
    就被踢倒在地上，
    只哎哟哎哟的叫：
    “不好了，
    我中了炮弹，
    我痛得不得了，
    我的肚子炸穿了！
    嗬嗬，
    我再也不能吃牛奶鸡蛋了，
    嗬嗬，
    我再也装不下又香又甜的白米饭了！
    ”
    老山妖，
    大声的哭了起来。
　
    大白马
    高兴着他的胜利，
    它想唱一个歌儿，
    表示表示它的欢喜，
    就张开口竖起了尾巴，
    吗哈哈的大叫了一声。
　
    “呔！
    活见鬼，
    原来是你这个死老马，
    那真太不该！
    想想看，
    我对你多么好，
    象客人似的看待，
    我用你驾车，
    一道来往，
    我用你打仗，
    一道冲锋，
    我是不是把你看得比人还高还重？”
　
    “你是把我看得高，
    我为你尽的力也不小，
    我为你赚的钱也不少，
    你还贪心不足的鞭打我，
    几乎要我顶好把性命也送掉。
    我看你，
    弄得没有一个人不恨，
    没有一个人不怨，
    马上就要完蛋了，
    我真该早觉悟，早跑掉，
    你这作恶的坏主人谁稀罕！”
　
    “笑话，笑话，
    这真是笑话！
    忠实的奴才
    也要反叛了，
    天下真是大变了。
    但是我不怕你，
    我懂得你，
    明天我再来，
    带来一根鞭子，
    还带来一把青草，
    看你选择那一样好？
　
    “好，
    小红帽，
    老子今晚肚子痛，
    吃不成，
    明晚一定来把你好好吃一顿。”
　
    老山妖，
    歪着头，
    皱着眉，
    双手按着肚子，
    一步一弯腰的
    下山去了。
    　　（第三节完）
