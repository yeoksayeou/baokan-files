Title: Little Red Riding Hood Escapes Danger ９
Author: Mei Zhi
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 6

Little Red Riding Hood Escapes Danger ９
　　　　　(V)

　　Tomorrow night, tomorrow night,
　　Even though tomorrow night hasn't arrived yet,
　　Little Red Riding Hood can't help but,
　　Be extremely anxious.
　　He thinks:
　　The dog is gnawing on a bone,
　　The horse is eating grass,
　　The bee is waiting for flowers to suck nectar,
　　No one will care about me, Little Red Riding Hood.
　　I am so poor,
　　I can't offer a bone,
　　I have no land to grow grass,
　　I have no garden to plant flowers,
　　Who would refuse the old mountain goblin's good things,
　　And help me for nothing, resist him?
　　Although I am young,
　　I already know,
　　In this world, it is hard to find
　　Someone who would sacrifice themselves for others,
　　Such people are really few,
　　Really few,
　　So few that they're gone!

　　Tonight, tonight,
　　I can no longer escape
　　His dark magic hands,
　　His bloodthirsty jaws!
　　Oh!
　　Mom!

　　Little Red Riding Hood,
　　The more he thinks, the more afraid he becomes,
　　The more afraid he becomes, the more helpless he is,
　　He quickly hides in the corner of the room,
　　Crying and thinking,
　　Thinking and shedding tears.

　　Ding ding ding,
　　Clang clang clang,
　　Blacksmithing, mending pots.
　　Ding ding ding,
　　Clang clang clang,
　　Blacksmithing, mending pots.
　　A little blacksmith,
　　Carrying a bellows,
　　Is passing by the thatched cottage.

　　He is so covered in coal smoke
　　That he looks like a little black person,
　　Only revealing the shiny
　　Pair of bright eyes.
　　Little Red Riding Hood peeks out through the crack in the door,
　　The little blacksmith is walking towards the thatched cottage,
　　Little Red Riding Hood sees him
　　And is so scared that he shivers.
　　He thinks:
　　This is really the end,
　　The old mountain goblin has already sent a little mountain goblin,
　　The time has come,
　　I can no longer escape,
　　No longer escape.

　　The little blacksmith,
　　Hears crying inside the door,
　　He puts down the bellows,
　　And gently knocks on the broken wood door:
　　"Which child is inside,
　　Crying so much?
　　If he is lost,
　　I can send him home,
　　If he is playing truant,
　　I don't have time to deal with him,
　　If he is just making noise wanting food and play and refusing to work,
　　Then I, the little blacksmith, will have to beat him!"

　　"Oh, it's you, little blacksmith,
　　Please don't hit me!
　　Please don't hit me!
　　I am not lost,
　　Nor am I playing truant,
　　Nor am I just making noise wanting food and play and refusing to work,
　　I am afraid of the old mountain goblin,
　　He says he will definitely come to eat me tonight.

　　"I am still so small,
　　If you eat me, I won't be able to grow tall
　　Like a tree,
　　If you eat me, I won't be able to grow strong
　　Like an ox.

　　"I am still so small,
　　I don't yet have the ability to go to the field,
　　To grow wheat and make steamed buns,
　　To grow rice and steam cakes.

　　"I am still so small,
　　I don't yet have the ability to go to the mountain,
　　To chop down big trees and build houses,
　　To dig stones and build big bridges.

　　"Speaking of building big bridges,
　　Little blacksmith,
　　I have a big secret,
　　Do you want to hear it or not?
　　You have to swear an oath,
　　Not to laugh at me,
　　And not to tell anyone."

　　"Okay!
　　I swear,
　　If I tell anyone,
　　May the fire burn my eyes when I am blacksmithing."

　　"I,
　　I had a dream:
　　I built a bridge,
　　As beautiful as a rainbow in the sky,
　　So long and so tall!
　　On the bridge are walking black-haired,
　　Red-haired,
　　Yellow-haired,
　　Gray-haired
　　Children,
　　They are holding hands,
　　Walking,
　　Jumping,
　　Singing and laughing.
　　(To be continued)

### 小红帽脱险记  ９
梅志
1949-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　小红帽脱险记  ９
    梅志
　　　　　（五）
　
    明晚，明晚，
    明晚虽还没有到，
    小红帽可已经忍不住，
    急得不得了。
    他想：
    狗在啃骨头，
    马在吃青草，
    蜜蜂在等着鲜花吸花汁，
    谁也不会管我小红帽。
    我是多么穷，
    拿不出骨头，
    没有土地长青草，
    没有花园种鲜花，
    有谁肯不要老山妖的好东西，
    白白的来帮助我，抵抗他？
    我虽然年纪小，
    但我已经知到了，
    世界上再也找不到
    肯牺牲自己为别人，
    这样的人实在少，
    实在少，
    少到没有了！
　
    今晚，今晚，
    我是再也逃不掉
    他那漆黑的魔手，
    他那血盆的大口！
    哎呀！
    妈呀！
　
    小红帽，
    越想越害怕，
    越害怕越没有办法，
    他赶快躲到屋角里，
    一面哭一面想，
    一面想一面流眼泪。
　
    钉钉钉，
    铛铛铛，
    打铁，补锅。
    钉钉钉，
    铛铛铛
    打铁，补锅。
    一个小铁匠，
    挑着风箱
    正从茅屋边走过。
　
    他被煤烟薰得
    象个小黑人，
    只露出光闪闪的
    两双亮眼睛。
    小红帽从门缝偷偷的向外看，
    小铁匠正走到了茅屋前，
    小红帽看到了他
    吓得只是打寒颤。
    他想：
    这下可真完了，
    老山妖已经派来了一个小山妖，
    时间到了，
    我是再也逃不掉，
    再也逃不掉。
　
    小铁匠，
    听见了门里面有哭声，
    他放下了风箱，
    轻轻敲着破柴门：
    “里面是哪个小孩子，
    这么哭哭啼啼的？
    如果是迷了路的，
    我可以送他回家去，
    如果是贪玩逃学的，
    我可没有功夫管他，
    如果是吵吃吵玩不肯工作的，
    那我小铁匠可还要打他！”
　
    “呵，原来是你呀，小铁匠，
    请你不要打！
    请你不要打！
    我不是迷了路，
    也不是逃学不上课，
    更不是吵吃吵玩不肯工作，
    我是害怕那老山妖，
    他说他今晚一定来吃我。
　
    “我还这么小，
    吃掉了就不能长得高高的
    象一棵树，
    吃掉了就不能长得壮壮的
    象一条牛。
　
    “我还这么小，
    我还没有本事到田里去，
    种出麦子做馍馍，
    种出大米来蒸糕。
　
    “我还这么小，
    我还没有本事到山上去，
    斫大树，盖房子，
    挖石头，造大桥。
　
    “说到造大桥，
    小铁匠，
    我有一个大秘密，
    你愿听不愿听？
    你要赌一个咒，
    不准笑我，
    也不准告诉人。”
　
    “行！
    我赌咒，
    如果我告诉人，
    打铁的时候火烧我的眼睛。”
　
    我呀，
    我做过一个梦：
    我造了一座桥，
    象半天上的彩虹那么美，
    那么长来那么高！
    桥上面走着黑头发的，
    红头发的
    黄头发的
    灰头发的
    小朋友，
    他们手牵着手，
    一面走，
    一面跳，
    一面唱又一面笑。
    （未完）
