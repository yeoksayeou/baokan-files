Title: List of Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and Members of the People's Supervisory Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th -- The third meeting of the Central People's Government Committee approved the following candidates for the People's Supervisory Committee of the Government Administration Council:

　　Chairman:
　　Tan Pingshan (Central Standing Committee Member of the San Min Chu I Comrades Association);

　　Vice-Chairmen (2):
　　Liu Jingfan (Chinese Communist Party, former Acting Chairman of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government),
　　Pan Zhenya (Lawyer, Standing Committee Member of the Fudan University Board of Trustees and Dean of the Law School).

　　Members (15):
　　Zhang Xiuyan (Chinese Communist Party, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation),
　　Zhang Muyao (Chinese Communist Party, former member of the Supervisory Committee of the North China People's Government),
　　Zhu Yunshan (Central Standing Committee Member of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang),
　　Han Zhao'e (China Democratic League, Counselor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government),
　　Dong Luan (Non-Party Democrat, former member of the North China People's Government and Deputy Dean of the People's Supervisory Procuratorate),
　　Zhang Nanxian (Democrat, Senior Counselor of the Wuhan Military Control Commission),
　　He @shi (Non-Party Democrat, Director of the Hangzhou Municipal People's Government Relief Committee),
　　Ning Wu (Kuomintang Democrat, Member of the Northeast People's Government),
　　Guo Renzhi (Non-Party Democrat, former Deputy Minister of Justice of the North China People's Government),
　　An Ziwen (Deputy Minister of the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party),
　　Xu Liqun (Central Committee Member of the China New Democratic Youth League),
　　Liu Dachao (Executive Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions),
　　Qiu Jin (Executive Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions),
　　Shuai Mengqi (Minister of the Organization Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation),
　　Xiao Ming (Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions).
