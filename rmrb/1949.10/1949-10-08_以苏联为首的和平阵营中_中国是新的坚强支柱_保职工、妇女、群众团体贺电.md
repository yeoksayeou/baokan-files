Title: China, a New Strong Pillar in the Peace Camp Headed by the Soviet Union - Congratulatory Telegrams from Bulgarian Workers, Women, and Mass Organizations
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 6th: The Council of the Bulgarian People's Women's Union sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, stating:

"To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

On the occasion of the election of the Central People's Government and the proclamation of the People's Republic of China, the women organized in the Bulgarian People's Women's Union extend our heartfelt respect to you and the working people of China. The victory of the Chinese people has filled all progressive humanity with joy. We, the women and mothers organized in the People's Women's Union, wholeheartedly hope for the most rapid construction of socialism within the People's Republic of China.

Long live the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and its leader, Mao Zedong!

The Council of the Bulgarian People's Women's Union, October 5th."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 6th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reported on the 4th that the Bulgarian National Agrarian Union, with over 1,300,000 farmers, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong to congratulate him on the occasion of the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The Bulgarian Anti-Fascist Fighters Alliance also sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong to congratulate him.

### 以苏联为首的和平阵营中　中国是新的坚强支柱  保职工、妇女、群众团体贺电

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　以苏联为首的和平阵营中
    中国是新的坚强支柱
    保职工、妇女、群众团体贺电
    【新华社北京六日电】保加利亚人民妇女联合会理事会电贺毛主席称：
    “北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    当此中央人民政府选出和中华人民共和国宣布成立之际，组织在保加利亚人民妇联的妇女，向你和中国劳动人民致以衷心的敬礼。中国人民的胜利，使全进步人类欢欣鼓舞。我们组织在人民妇联的妇女和母亲，全心全意地希望在中华人民共和国内最迅速地建成社会主义。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府及其领袖毛泽东万岁！
    　　　　　　　保加利亚人民妇女联合会理事会十月五日。”
    【新华社布拉格六日电】保加利亚通讯社四日报道称：值此中华人民共和国中央人民政府宣告建立之际，拥有一百三十万余农民的保加利亚全国农民联盟顷致电毛泽东主席祝贺。保加利亚反法西斯战士联盟也致电毛泽东主席祝贺。
