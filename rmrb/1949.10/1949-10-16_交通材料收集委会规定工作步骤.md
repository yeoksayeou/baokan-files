Title: Regulations on Work Steps of the Transportation Material Collection Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 4

　　Transportation Material Collection Committee
  　Specifies Work Steps
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Transportation Material Collection Committee was formally established yesterday (the 15th) and decided to launch the collection of transportation materials from the 15th of this month to the 15th of November. Collection sub-committees and collection stations will be established in each district of Beijing and in the East Railway Station and stations around the city under the jurisdiction of the Beijing Office of the Beijing-Tianjin Railway Bureau. The collection work will be carried out in accordance with the "Provisional Measures for Collecting and Protecting Railway Transportation Equipment" promulgated by the Railway Ministry of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission on August 31. The work steps stipulate that from the 15th to the 20th of this month, various district sub-committees and collection stations will be established. From the 21st to the 31st, propaganda, investigation, registration, and mobilization of donations will be carried out. The municipal government, the Beijing Office of the Beijing-Tianjin Railway Bureau, the Suburban Committee, the Municipal Public Security Bureau, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Federation of Industry, the Chamber of Commerce, and the district governments will separately communicate to their respective departments and citizens to earnestly implement the measures and enthusiastically donate various railway materials. From November 1st to 15th, the work of collection, transportation, counting, distribution of bonuses, and payment of transportation fees will commence.

### 交通材料收集委会规定工作步骤

1949-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　交通材料收集委会
  　规定工作步骤
    【北京讯】本市交通材料收集委员会已于昨（十五）日正式成立，并决定从本月十五日至十一月十五日展开收集交通材料工作。在京市各区及京津铁路局北京办事处所属东车站和环城各站，分别建立交通材料收集委员会各区分会和收集站，按中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部八月三十一日所颁发的“搜集与保护铁路交通器材暂行办法”，进行收集工作。工作步骤，规定本月十五日至二十日建立各区分会和各收集站，二十一日至三十一日进行宣传、调查、登记及动员献交等工作。由市府、京津铁路局北京办事处、郊委会、市公安总局、市总公会、市工业会、商会及各区政府分别进行传达所属各部门和市民切实执行，踊跃献交各种铁路材料。十一月一日至十五日开始收集、运输、点验、发放奖金及发给运费等工作。
