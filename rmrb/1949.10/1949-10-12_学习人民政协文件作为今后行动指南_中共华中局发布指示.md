Title: Study People's Political Consultative Conference Documents as a Guide for Future Actions - CCP Central China Bureau Issues Instructions
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, October 10th. The Propaganda Department of the CCP Central China Bureau issued instructions on studying the documents of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, calling on all Communist Party members and cadres, Communist Youth League members, intellectuals, members of government organs, public and private schools, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in the Central China region to earnestly study the important documents of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, understand the spirit and essence of these documents, and take them as a guide for future actions. It also requires all departments to study various relevant specific policies in conjunction with their own work. The study time is set as: one month for organs and schools, and two months for counties, districts, and general rural areas.

### 学习人民政协文件作为今后行动指南　中共华中局发布指示

1949-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　学习人民政协文件作为今后行动指南
    中共华中局发布指示
    【新华社汉口十日电】中共中央华中局宣传部发布关于学习人民政协文件的指示，号召华中地区所有的共产党员和干部、青年团员、知识分子，各地政府机关、公私学校、人民团体的成员和社会各界人士认真学习中国人民政治协商会议的重要文件，领会这些文件的精神和实质，把它作为今后行动的指南。并要求各部门结合自己的工作研究各种有关的具体政策。学习时间规定为：机关、学校一个月，县、区和一般农村两个月。
