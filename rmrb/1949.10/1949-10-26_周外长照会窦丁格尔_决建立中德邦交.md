Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Note to Dertinger: Decides to Establish Diplomatic Relations Between China and Germany
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 25th

　　Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Note to Dertinger
　　Decides to Establish Diplomatic Relations Between China and Germany

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 25th: The People's Republic of China has decided to establish diplomatic relations with the German Democratic Republic. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai has today notified Mr. Dertinger, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, of this decision by note. Minister Zhou's note to Dertinger is as follows:

Mr. Dertinger, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic:

As the German Democratic Republic is proclaimed and its provisional government is formed, I would like to express my heartfelt congratulations to you. The establishment of the German Democratic Republic not only conforms to the urgent wishes of the entire German people, but is also warmly welcomed by peace-loving people all over the world.

I now inform you that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China decides to establish diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic and to send diplomatic representatives.

Zhou Enlai
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
October 25, 1949, in Beijing

### 周外长照会窦丁格尔  决建立中德邦交

1949-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长照会窦丁格尔　
    决建立中德邦交
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中华人民共和国决定与德意志民主共和国建立外交关系。外交部周部长本日已将这一决定以照会通知德意志民主共和国外交部部长窦丁格尔。周部长给窦丁格尔的照会如下：德意志民主共和国临时政府外交部部长窦丁格尔先生：
    当德意志民主共和国宣告诞生及其临时政府组成之际，我谨向阁下表示衷心祝贺。德意志民主共和国之成立，不仅符合于全德国人民的迫切愿望，亦且为全世界爱好持久和平的人民所热烈欢迎。
    我现在通知阁下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府决定建立中华人民共和国与德意志民主共和国之间的外交关系，并派遣外交代表。
    中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来
    一九四九年十月二十五日　于北京
