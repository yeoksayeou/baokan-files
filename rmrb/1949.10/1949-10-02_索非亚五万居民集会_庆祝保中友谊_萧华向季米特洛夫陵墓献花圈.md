Title: 50,000 Residents of Sofia Gather to Celebrate Sino-Bulgarian Friendship; Xiao Hua Lays Wreath at Dimitrov Mausoleum
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st. On the evening of the 28th, a mass rally celebrating Sino-Bulgarian friendship was held in Freedom Square in Sofia, in front of the mausoleum of Georgi Dimitrov, the immortal teacher and leader of the Bulgarian people and the famous hero of the Leipzig Trial. More than 50,000 residents of Sofia, including workers, youths, and leaders and representatives from Bulgarian political, economic, and cultural fields, attended the rally, including representatives of the Council of Ministers such as Chervenkov, members of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party, representatives of the Fatherland Front, and the Central Committee of the People's Youth Union of the Bulgarian youth organization.

At 6:00 p.m., the Chinese youth delegation, currently visiting Bulgaria and led by General Xiao Hua, laid a wreath in front of the Dimitrov Mausoleum to the sound of the national anthem and bowed in mourning to this great son of the Bulgarian people and outstanding worker of the international labor movement. Xiao Hua delivered a moving speech to the assembled masses, describing the efforts of the Chinese people in their struggle to liberate themselves from the oppression of American imperialism and its Kuomintang puppet servants, and said: The Chinese people will never lay down their arms until they see the complete liberation of the country. His speech was greeted with enthusiastic applause and repeatedly interrupted by thunderous shouts of "Long Live Great Stalin," "Long Live Mao Zedong," and "Long Live the Friendship between Bulgaria and the Heroic Chinese People." City Soviet Chairman Zhivkov welcomed the Chinese delegation on behalf of the citizens of Sofia. The conference unanimously adopted a telegram to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Chinese Communist Party and leader of the Chinese people, stating: All the Bulgarian people stand with fighting China and congratulate China on its great victory.

### 索非亚五万居民集会　 庆祝保中友谊  萧华向季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈

1949-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　索非亚五万居民集会
    庆祝保中友谊
    萧华向季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈
    【新华社布拉格一日电】二十八日晚，在索非亚的自由广场上，在保加利亚人民不朽的导师和领袖、莱比锡审讯上著名英雄乔治·季米特洛夫的陵墓前面，举行了一个庆祝保中友谊的群众大会。索非亚五万余居民，工人、青年与保加利亚政治、经济和文化方面的领袖和代表均参加了这次集会，其中有部长会议代表契尔文科夫等，保加利亚共产党政治局委员，祖国阵线和保加利亚青年组织人民青年联盟中央委员会的代表等。
    下午六时，由萧华将军率领现正访问保加利亚的中国青年代表团，在国歌声中向季米特洛夫陵墓前献花圈，并向这一保加利亚人民的伟大儿子和国际工人运动的杰出工作者鞠躬悼念。萧华向与会群众作动人的演说，描述中国人民在为从美帝国主义及其国民党顺从奴仆的压迫下求得解放而斗争中的努力，并说：中国人民在看到全国解放之前，决不放下他们的武器。他的演说受到热烈的喝采并一再为雷鸣般“伟大的斯大林万岁”、“毛泽东万岁”、“保加利亚与英雄的中国人民的友谊万岁”声所阻断。市苏维埃主席齐夫科夫代表索非亚市民欢迎中国代表团。大会一致通过致中国共产党主席和中国人民领袖毛泽东的电文，内称：全保加利亚人民与战斗的中国站在一起，并祝贺中国的伟大胜利。
