Title: Soviet Delegation to Depart for Shanghai Today; Premier Zhou and Mayor Nie Hosted Farewell Banquet Last Night
Author:
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th: The Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation is scheduled to depart for Shanghai tomorrow. Premier Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Mayor Nie Rongzhen of Beijing Municipality hosted a cocktail party tonight to bid them farewell. The delegation, led by head Alexander Fadeyev and deputy head Konstantin Simonov, attended in full. Over four hundred guests were invited, including Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Gao Gang, Chen Yi, He Xiangning, Guo Moruo, and Chargé d'affaires Tikhvinsky of the Soviet Embassy. At the end of the reception, the People's Art Troupe, the North China University Art Troupe, dancer Dai Ailian, violinist Ma Sicong, and others performed, and the film "The Million-Strong Army Crosses the Yangtze River" was screened.

This morning, the delegation visited the National Art College Art Exhibition and the National Beijing Library, and paid respects at the former residence of Lu Xun. When the delegation visited the Beijing Library, the Higher Education Committee of the North China People's Government entrusted head Fadeyev with six copies of "Panoramic View of the Wenyuan Pavilion Collection" to be presented to major Soviet libraries. In the afternoon, representatives of the delegation held discussions with Chinese literary, educational, and theatrical groups.

### 苏代表团今日赴沪　周外长聂市长昨晚饯行

1949-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏代表团今日赴沪
    周外长聂市长昨晚饯行
    【新华社北京十日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团定明日去沪。外交部部长周恩来和北京市市长聂荣臻特于今晚举行鸡尾酒会送行。代表团由团长法捷耶夫、副团长西蒙诺夫率领全体出席。应邀赴会者有刘少奇、李济深、高岗、陈毅、何香凝、郭沫若和苏联大使馆代办齐赫文斯基等四百余人。酒会终了时由人民文工团、华北大学文工团和舞蹈家戴爱莲、提琴家马思聪等表演，并放映电影“百万雄师下江南”。
    代表团今天上午曾赴国立艺术专科学校美术展览会、国立北京图书馆参观，并拜访鲁迅故居。当代表团参观北京图书馆时，华北人民政府高等教育委员会以“文渊阁藏书全景”六份托法耶捷夫团长携赠苏联各大图书馆。下午，代表团的代表分别与中国的文学、教育、戏剧等团体举行座谈。
