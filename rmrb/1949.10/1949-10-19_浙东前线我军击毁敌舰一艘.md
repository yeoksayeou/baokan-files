Title: Our Army on the Zhejiang Eastern Front Destroys an Enemy Warship
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1
　　Our Army on the Zhejiang Eastern Front
    Destroys an Enemy Warship
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 17th] People's Liberation Army artillery units on the Zhejiang Eastern Front destroyed a bandit warship in the waters off Zhejiang Eastern Front on the 9th. That afternoon, three bandit warships sailed from the direction of the Zhoushan Islands, attempting to attack the People's Liberation Army's coastal positions. In the waters off Baiyadong, the largest of the bandit warships was hit by artillery fire from the People's Liberation Army. Thick smoke immediately billowed from the bandit warship, and the other two smaller bandit warships fled in a panic.

### 浙东前线我军击毁敌舰一艘

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东前线我军
    击毁敌舰一艘
    【新华社南京十七日电】浙东前线人民解放军炮兵部队于九日在浙东海面击毁匪军舰一艘。当日下午，匪舰三艘自舟山群岛方向驶来，企图袭击解放军沿海阵地，在白鸭洞海面被解放军炮兵发炮击中其最大的一艘，匪舰上立即升起浓烟，其余两艘较小的匪舰狼狈窜去。
