Title: North China People's Government and others dispatch medical teams to help compatriots in flood-stricken areas receive medical treatment.
Author:
Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 2

North China Government Health Department
　　【Our Newspaper Dispatches】Tangshan, Tongxian, Cangxian, Baoding, Tianjin and other special districts of Hebei Province have suffered from floods. The masses are living in difficult circumstances and suffering from malnutrition, which has led to many gastrointestinal diseases. The Health Department of the North China People's Government specially dispatched more than 100 epidemic prevention and medical personnel to Zhuoxian and Tongxian areas on September 27. The Tianjin Municipal Health Bureau and various municipal hospitals jointly formed three medical teams to Jinghai County, and the Hebei Provincial Health Department sent medical teams to Tangshan and Cangxian special districts to carry out treatment. The masses expressed warm welcome to the government's action. After the first medical team in Tianjin City treated more than 1,500 people in a week, when they were about to leave Jinghai County, more than 10,000 people in the city formed a team to see them off. This work has greatly encouraged the masses to fight against the disaster. The work will continue to be carried out to support the people in the disaster areas.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Reported by the North China Government Health Department)

### 华北人民政府等组派医疗队　帮助水灾区同胞治病

1949-10-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府等组派医疗队
  　帮助水灾区同胞治病
    【本报讯】河北省唐山、通县、沧县、保定、天津等专区，受水灾地区，群众生活困难，营养不良，并引起不少胃肠性疾病。华北人民政府卫生部特于九月二十七日派防疫医疗队一百余人前往涿县、通县地区，天津市卫生局暨各市立医院，合组三个医疗队前往静海县一带，河北省卫生厅派医疗队到唐山、沧县两专区进行救治。群众对政府这一行动表示热烈欢迎，天津市第一医疗队工作一周治愈一千五百余人后，当他们要离开静海县城时，城内群众万余人结队欢送。这一工作大大的鼓舞了群众向灾荒作斗争的情绪，今后仍将继续开展，以支援灾区人民。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（华府卫生部）
