Title: Congratulatory Telegrams from Various Parties on the Establishment of the Central People's Government
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd:

The Headquarters of the China Democratic League

　　The Headquarters of the China Democratic League sent a telegram to the Central People's Government to congratulate it. The telegram is as follows:

　　To Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and All Members of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China: Today is the date of the official proclamation of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and also the date of the official establishment of the Central People's Government. This signifies that the old China has ended, the new China has been born, the feudal, comprador, and fascist regime has been completely eliminated, and the people's democratic dictatorship has been established. From today onwards, the Chinese people have become the masters of the new China, and it is even more worthy of our enthusiastic celebration that the government is personally led by the people's leader, Chairman Mao!

　　From now on, our League will wholeheartedly and fully support the government, resolutely implement the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, in order to complete the construction of a new democratic new China!

　　Headquarters of the China Democratic League

　　October 1, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 1st:

　　The Northeast People's Government recently sent a telegram to congratulate the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government. The telegram is as follows: To Chairman Mao, Vice-Chairmen Zhu, Liu, Song, Li, Zhang, and Gao, and to all members of the Central People's Government Committee:

　　We are delighted to hear that the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference has successfully closed, and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has been proclaimed. We, on behalf of the 43 million people of the Northeast, express our firm support and warm congratulations!

　　The founding of the People's Republic of China is a victory of the long-term struggle of the Chinese people and a victory of the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao. From now on, the Chinese people have begun a new era in history, the final victory of the National Liberation War will be accelerated, and the great construction work in the whole country will be started uniformly and systematically. Our Northeast People's Government and governments at all levels guarantee that under the leadership of the Central People's Government, we will adhere to the general policy of the people's democratic dictatorship and unity with international friends, work diligently and conscientiously, fully restore and develop the economic construction of the Northeast, resolutely implement the Common Program and all resolutions passed by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, resolutely implement all orders and instructions of the Central People's Government, and strive to build an independent, democratic, unified, and prosperous new China!

　　Northeast People's Government

　　Chairman Gao Gang

　　Vice-Chairmen Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, Gao Chongmin

　　October 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 1st:

　　All commanders and fighters of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army recently sent a telegram to congratulate the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government. The telegram is as follows:

　　To Chairman Mao and all members of the Central People's Government:

　　We are very pleased and excited to warmly celebrate the successful closing of the People's Political Consultative Conference and the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government! This is the first truly national government belonging to the people in Chinese history. It will lead the people of the whole country to carry the revolutionary war through to the end and build a new democratic new China. The entire Second Field Army resolutely supports the Central People's Government, resolutely implements the program and all policies and decrees of the Central People's Government, listens to the Central People's Government's call at any time, is always a hundred times more vigilant against the sabotage conspiracies of all counter-revolutionary remnants and the US imperialist reactionaries, intensifies the construction and consolidation of ourselves, and always guards the forefront of national defense, defends the people's motherland, and safeguards world peace!

　　Long live the great unity of the Chinese people!

　　Long live the Central People's Government!

　　Long live Chairman Mao!

　　Long live the People's Republic of China!

　　Respectful congratulations from all commanders and fighters of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army.

　　October 1

### 各方电贺中央人民政府成立

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　各方电贺中央人民政府成立
    中国民主同盟总部
    【新华社北京二日电】中国民主同盟总部电中央人民政府祝贺，电文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席、副主席暨全体委员：今天是中华人民共和国正式宣告成立的日期，也是中央人民政府正式宣告成立的日期。这表示旧中国已经结束，新中国已经诞生，封建买办法西斯的政权已经完全消灭，人民民主专政已经建立起来。从今天起，中国人民做了新中国的主人，由人民领袖毛主席亲自领导政府，更值得我们热烈庆祝！
    本盟今后当竭诚地以全力拥护政府，坚决执行中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领，以完成新民主主义新中国的建设！
    中国民主同盟总部
    一九四九年十月一日
    　东北人民政府
    【新华社沈阳一日电】东北人民政府顷电贺中华人民共和国和中央人民政府的成立。电文如下：毛主席、朱、刘、宋、李、张、高副主席并转中央人民政府委员会全体委员钧鉴：
    欣闻中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议胜利闭幕，中华人民共和国中央人民政府宣告成立，谨代表东北四千三百万人民表示坚决的拥护和热烈的庆贺！
    中华人民共和国的成立，是中国人民长期斗争的胜利，是中国共产党和毛主席英明领导的胜利；从此，中国人民开始了历史的新纪元，全国解放战争的最后胜利将加速实现，全国范围内的伟大建设工作将统一地有系统地开始。我们东北人民政府及各级人民政府，保证在中央人民政府领导之下，坚持人民民主专政和团结国际友人的总方针，克勤克俭，兢兢业业，全力恢复和发展东北经济建设，坚决贯彻执行中国人民政治协商会议通过的共同纲领及一切决议，坚决执行中央人民政府的一切命令和指示，为建立独立、民主、统一、富强的新中国而奋斗！
    东北人民政府
    主席　高岗
    副主席　李富春、林枫、高崇民
    十月一日
    第二野战军
    【新华社南京一日电】人民解放军第二野战军全体指战员顷电贺中华人民共和国和中央人民政府的成立。电文如下：
    中央人民政府毛主席暨全体委员：
    我们以非常愉快和兴奋的心情热烈的庆祝人民政治协商会议的胜利闭幕，庆祝中华人民共和国和中央人民政府的成立！这是中国有史以来第一个真正属于人民的全国性的政府，它将领导全国人民将革命战争进行到底，建设新民主主义的新中国。第二野战军全军坚决拥护中央人民政府，坚决执行中央人民政府的纲领及其一切政策法令，听候中央人民政府的随时调用，时刻百倍地警惕一切反革命残余分子和美帝国主义反动派的破坏阴谋，加紧建设和巩固自己，永远警卫在国防前哨，捍卫人民祖国和保卫世界和平！
    中国人民大团结万岁！
    中央人民政府万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    人民解放军第二野战军全体指战员敬贺。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十月一日
