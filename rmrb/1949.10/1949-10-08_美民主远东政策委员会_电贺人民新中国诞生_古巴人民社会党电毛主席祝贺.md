Title: US Democratic Far East Policy Committee Cables Congratulations on the Birth of New China; Cuban People's Socialist Party Cables Chairman Mao to Congratulate Him
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: The American Democratic Far East Policy Committee sent a cable from New York on the 4th to congratulate Chairman Mao Zedong. The cable stated: "Chairman Mao Zedong, People's Republic of China, Beijing: Democratic Americans united in the struggle for friendship between our two nations celebrate the establishment of the new government representing the Chinese people. Democratic Far East Policy Committee."
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: The Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, Juan Marinello, and the General Secretary, Blas Roca, sent a cable to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 3rd of this month, congratulating him. The cable reads: Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing, China:
In the name of the People's Socialist Party, reflecting the national sentiments of the free people of Cuba, we joyfully celebrate your election as the Chairman of the great People's Republic of China, the strong pillar of the Chinese liberation struggle, progress, democracy, peace, and socialism. We are deeply convinced that the people of Cuba will soon create the conditions to sever relations between the Cuban Republic and the corrupt Kuomintang bandits serving American imperialism, and establish relations with the People's Republic of China. We respectfully extend our fraternal greetings to Your Excellency, the great Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese people.

　　　　　　　　　　Juan Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Blas Roca, General Secretary

### 美民主远东政策委员会　电贺人民新中国诞生  古巴人民社会党电毛主席祝贺

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　美民主远东政策委员会
    电贺人民新中国诞生
    古巴人民社会党电毛主席祝贺
    【新华社北京七日电】美国民主远东政策委员会四日自纽约电贺毛泽东主席。电称：“北京中华人民共和国毛泽东主席：联合起来为我们两个民族的友谊而奋斗的民主的美国人，庆祝代表中国人民的新政府的成立。民主远东政策委员会。”
    【新华社北京五日电】古巴人民社会党主席马里奈罗、总书记洛加在本月三日电贺毛泽东主席，电文称：中国北京毛泽东主席：
    以人民社会党的名义，反映着古巴自由人民的民族感情，我们欢欣地庆贺你被选为作为中国解放斗争、进步、民主、和平和社会主义的坚强柱石的，伟大的中华人民共和国的主席。我们深信古巴的人民不久就要造成条件，使古巴共和国和那为美国帝国主义服务的国民党腐败的党匪断绝关系，而和中华人民共和国缔结关系。敬向阁下，敬向伟大的中国共产党和中国人民，致我们的兄弟般的敬礼。
    　　　　　　　　　　古巴人民社会党主席　胡安·马里奈罗
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　总书记　勃拉斯·洛加
