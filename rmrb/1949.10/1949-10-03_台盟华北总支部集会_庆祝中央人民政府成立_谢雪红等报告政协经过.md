Title: Taiwan Democratic League, North China General Branch, Holds Meeting to Celebrate the Founding of the Central People's Government; Xie Xuehong and Others Report on the CPPCC Process
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3

　　Taiwan Democratic League, North China General Branch, Holds Meeting
  　Celebrating the Founding of the Central People's Government
  　Xie Xuehong and Others Report on the CPPCC Process
    [Report from this newspaper] The Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, North China General Branch, held a celebration meeting for all league members and Taiwanese compatriots in Beijing at 4:00 PM on the 2nd to celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China. First, Lin Jiansheng, Chairman of the General Branch, delivered an opening speech, followed by Xie Xuehong, Yang Xiaohuang, Li Weiguang, and Wang Tianqi, representatives of the Taiwan Democratic League attending the CPPCC, who successively reported on the progress of the CPPCC and encouraged all league members to quickly study the spirit of the Common Program of the CPPCC and work hard. All league members expressed warm celebrations for the emergence of the new China.

### 台盟华北总支部集会  庆祝中央人民政府成立  谢雪红等报告政协经过

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　台盟华北总支部集会
  　庆祝中央人民政府成立
  　谢雪红等报告政协经过
    【本报讯】台湾民主自治同盟华北总支部，为庆祝中华人民共和国的成立，于二日下午四时举行全体盟员及在京台湾同胞庆祝大会。先由该总支部林鉴生主委致开会辞，继由台盟出席政协的代表谢雪红、杨晓煌、李伟光、王天强相继报告政协的经过，并勉励各盟员应尽速学习政协共同纲领的精神，努力工作。各盟员对新中国的出现，皆表示热烈的欢庆。
