Title: German-Hungarian Trade Agreement Signed; Romania and Germany to Exchange Diplomatic Missions
Author:
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 3

　　German-Hungarian Trade Agreement Signed
  　Romania and Germany to Exchange Diplomatic Missions
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 22nd - TASS reports: The Minister of Foreign Trade and Materials Supply of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, and the Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Hungary, Rónai Sándor, signed the Hungarian-German barter and payment agreement in Budapest on the 19th. The government gazette emphasizes that this agreement marks a major step forward in the development of economic relations between the two countries. The amount of goods exchanged has increased fourfold compared to the previous agreement.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd - Dentsu News Agency, Bucharest, 20th: The Romanian government today proposed to the German Democratic Republic the exchange of diplomatic missions.

### 德匈缔贸易协定　罗德将交换外交使团

1949-10-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　德匈缔贸易协定
  　罗德将交换外交使团
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：德意志民主共和国对外贸易与物资供应部长韩克与匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部长罗奈，于十九日在布达佩斯签定匈德换货与支付协定。政府公报着重指出这个协定使两国间经济关系之发展向前迈进了一大步。双方换货数量，已增至上次协定的四倍。
    【新华社北京二十二日电】电通社布加勒斯特二十日讯：罗马尼亚政府今日向德意志民主共和国建议交换外交使团。
