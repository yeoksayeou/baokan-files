Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-10-18
Page: 4

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
　　Adjusted Starting Yesterday
　　[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has revised the exchange rate to be 1 RMB equals 62 Northeast Yuan, effective October 17th.

### 山海关货币牌价昨起调整

1949-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处，自十月十七日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券六十二元。
