Title: List of Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 1st: The full list of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation and art performance team invited to Beijing to participate in the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the China Peace Congress is as follows:

(A) Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation (total of thirteen people):
	A.A. Fadeev – Head of the delegation, General Secretary of the Soviet Writers' Union, Chairman of the Stalin Prize Committee, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, Standing Committee Member of the Soviet Peace Congress.
	K.M. Simonov – Deputy Head of the delegation, Deputy General Secretary of the Soviet Writers' Union, Standing Committee Member of the World Peace Congress, Standing Committee Member of the Soviet Peace Congress.
	S.A. Gerasimov – Professor, film director (director of the film "The Young Guard"), People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Standing Committee Member of the World Peace Congress.
	V.M. Stoletov – Doctor of Biological Sciences, President of the Soviet Timiryazev Agricultural Academy.
	L.E. Aglazé – Doctor of Technical Sciences, Vice President of the Georgian Academy of Sciences.
	L.Y. Dubrovin (female) – Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic.
	M.E. Deodorov – Chairman of the International Book Association (i.e., the International Bookstore).
	L.V. Varlamov – Film director.
	K.E. Fenognov – Painter, Vice Chairman of the Moscow Artists Association, Stalin Prize winner.
	E.S. Malkov – Deputy Minister of the Far East Department of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association.
	E.M. Vozni – Propaganda Minister of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Association, Secretary of the delegation.
	K.M. Chikarev – Deputy Minister of the Records Department of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, Administrative Officer of the delegation.
	K.M. Kurochkina (female) – Typist for the delegation.

(B) Art Performance Team (total of twenty-one people, including thirteen members of the Red Army Choir Youth Team):
	N.D. Karlmonov – Director, Deputy Director of the Music Theater Management Committee of the Art Affairs Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Leader of the Art Performance Team.
	G.S. Balinova (female) – Violinist, Stalin Prize winner, two-time first-class winner of the All-Soviet Music Competition.
	N.M. Dukinskaya (female) – Dancer, three-time Stalin Prize winner, Honored Artist of the Russian Republic, actress of the Leningrad Opera and Ballet Theater.
	K.M. Serkiev – Dancer, three-time Stalin Prize winner, Honored Artist of the Russian Republic, actor of the Leningrad Opera and Ballet Theater.
	T.P. Kravchenko (female) – Pianist, winner of the Russian Music Competition.
	H.A. Kazanmiva (female) – Singer, People's Artist of the Russian Republic.
	E.D. Smelev – Singer, winner of the All-Soviet Competition, soloist of the Moscow Central Broadcasting Station.
	N.G. Valter – Pianist, performer of the Moscow Central Broadcasting Station.

The following are the thirteen members of the Red Army Choir Youth Team, who won the highest award in the 1949 World Drama Competition in Hungary:
	G.A. Korzhenkov – Dancer, Honored Artist of the Russian Republic.
	T.L. Grodnizki
	V.E. Zhukov
	A.F. Zoev
	V.F. Loktev
	N.M. Romanov
	V.G. Medvedev
	S.V. Minayev
	E.S. Osmolovsky
	A.F. Sergeev
	A.E. Solokin
	L.E. Vominitz
	V.E. Shashin

### 苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团名单

1949-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团名单
    【新华社北京一日电】应邀来北京参加中苏友好协会总会与中国保卫世界和平大会的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团与艺术演出队全体名单如下：
    （甲）文化艺术科学工作者代表团（共十三人）：Ａ·Ａ·法捷耶夫——代表团团长，苏联作家协会总书记，斯大林奖金委员会主席，世界拥护和平大会常委会副主席，苏联拥护世界和平大会常委。
    Ｋ·Ｍ·西蒙诺夫——代表团副团长，苏联作家协会副总书记，世界拥护和平大会常委，苏联拥护和平大会常委。
    Ｓ·Ａ·格拉西莫夫——教授，电影导演（名片青年近卫军导演），苏联人民艺术家，世界拥护和平大会常委。
    Ｖ·Ｍ·斯托列托夫——生物科学博士，苏联契姆里也则夫农业科学院院长。
    Ｌ·Ｅ·阿格拉则——技术科学博士，乔治亚科学院副院长。
    Ｌ·Ｙ·杜伯洛维娜（女）——俄罗斯联邦共和国人民教育部副部长。
    Ｍ·Ｅ·德奥来多夫——国际书籍协会（即万国书店）主席。
    Ｌ·Ｖ·瓦尔拉莫夫——电影导演。
    Ｋ·Ｅ·费诺格诺夫——画家，莫斯科美术协会副主席，斯大林奖金得奖人。
    Ｅ·Ｓ·马尔权夫——苏联对外文化协会远东部副部长。
    Ｅ·Ｍ·伏兹尼——苏联青年反法西斯协会宣传部长，代表团秘书。
    Ｋ·Ｍ·齐卡列夫——苏联对外文化协会记录部副部长，代表团行政干事。
    Ｋ·Ｍ·古罗契基娜（女）——代表团打字员。
    （乙）艺术演出队（共二十一人，包括红军歌舞团青年队十三人）：
    Ｎ·Ｄ·卡尔蒙诺夫——导演，苏联部长会议艺术工作委员会音乐戏院管理委员会副主任，艺术演出队领队。
    Ｇ·Ｓ·巴利诺瓦（女）——提琴家，斯大林奖金得奖人，全苏音乐比赛两次优等。
    Ｎ·Ｍ·杜金斯卡娅（女）——舞蹈家，三次得斯大林奖，俄罗斯共和国功勋艺术家，列宁格勒歌舞戏院演员。
    Ｋ·Ｍ·谢尔基耶夫——舞蹈家，三次得斯大林奖，俄罗斯共和国功勋艺术家，列宁格勒歌舞戏院演员。
    Ｔ·Ｐ·克拉夫青柯（女）——钢琴家，俄罗斯音乐比赛获胜者。
    Ｈ·Ａ·卡赞米瓦（女）——歌唱家，俄罗斯共和国人民艺术家。
    Ｅ·Ｄ·史灭略夫——歌唱家，全苏比赛优胜者，莫斯科中央广播电台独唱家。
    Ｎ·Ｇ·瓦尔特尔——钢琴家，莫斯科中央广播电台演奏家。
