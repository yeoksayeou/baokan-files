Title: List of Chairman, Vice Chairmen and Members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th -- The complete list of the Vice Chairmen, Members, Chief of General Staff, and Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of China, appointed by the Third Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, together with the Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission appointed by the First Meeting of the Government Committee on October 1, is as follows:

Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission:
　　Mao Zedong (Former Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission);
Vice Chairmen (5 persons):
　　Zhu De (Former Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission),
　　Liu Shaoqi (Former Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission),
　　Zhou Enlai (Former Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission),
　　Peng Dehuai (Former Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission),
　　Cheng Qian (General who revolted).
Members (22 persons):
　　He Long (Commander of the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Liu Bocheng (Commander of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Chen Yi (Commander and concurrently Commander of the East China Military Region of the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Lin Biao (Commander and concurrently Commander of the Central China Military Region of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Xu Xiangqian (Former Commander and concurrently Political Commissar of the 18th Army Group of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Ye Jianying (Commander and concurrently Political Commissar of the South China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Nie Rongzhen (Commander of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Gao Gang (Commander and concurrently Political Commissar of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Su Yu (Deputy Commander of the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Zhang Yunyi (Deputy Commander of the South China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Deng Xiaoping (Political Commissar of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Li Xiannian (Commander and concurrently Political Commissar of the Hubei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Rao Shushi (Political Commissar and concurrently Political Commissar of the East China Military Region of the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Deng Zihui (Second Political Commissar and concurrently Second Political Commissar of the Central China Military Region of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Xi Zhongxun (Political Commissar of the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
　　Luo Ruiqing (Minister of Public Security),
　　Sa Zhenbing (Elderly statesman of the Navy),
　　Zhang Zhizhong (Democrat of the Kuomintang),
　　Fu Zuoyi (General who revolted),
　　Cai Tingkai (China Kuomintang Democratic Promotion Association),
　　Long Yun (Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang),
　　Liu Fei (Democrat of the Kuomintang).

Chief of General Staff: Xu Xiangqian;
Deputy Chief of General Staff: Nie Rongzhen.
