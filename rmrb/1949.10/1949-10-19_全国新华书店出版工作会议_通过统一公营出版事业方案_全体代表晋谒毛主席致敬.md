Title: National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference Passes Plan for Unified Publicly Owned Publishing Undertaking; All Representatives Pay Respects to Chairman Mao

Author:

Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 18th: The National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference continued its thirteenth and fourteenth plenary sessions on October 18th, with Huang Luofeng, Hu Sheng, and Xu Boxin, respectively, serving as executive chairmen of the presidium. The conference discussed and approved four major plans regarding the unified national publicly owned publishing undertaking, including editorial and publishing, printing, distribution, and enterprise management. All representatives of the conference, led by Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited Chairman Mao at 6:30 this afternoon to pay their respects. Chairman Mao, in his busy schedule, received all the representatives, shook hands with each of them, offered sincere encouragement, and wished the conference success.

### 全国新华书店出版工作会议　通过统一公营出版事业方案  全体代表晋谒毛主席致敬

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国新华书店出版工作会议
  　通过统一公营出版事业方案
    全体代表晋谒毛主席致敬
    【新华社北京十八日电】全国新华书店出版工作会议，于十月十八日继续举行第十三、十四次大会，由大会主席团黄洛峰、胡绳、徐伯昕分别担任执行主席。大会讨论并通过了关于统一全国公营出版事业的编审出版、印刷、发行、企业化管理等四大方案。大会全体代表曾于今日下午六时半由中共中央宣传部部长陆定一率领访问毛主席致敬。毛主席在百忙中，接见全体代表，一一握手，亲加慰勉，预祝会议成功。
