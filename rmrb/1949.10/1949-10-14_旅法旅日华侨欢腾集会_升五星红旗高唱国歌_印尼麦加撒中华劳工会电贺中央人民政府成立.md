Title: Overseas Chinese in France and Japan Joyfully Hold Rallies: Raising the Five-Star Red Flag and Singing the National Anthem; Chinese Labor Association in Makassar, Indonesia Cables Congratulations on the Establishment of the Central People's Government

Author:

Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 13th: Paris dispatch: On the afternoon of the 9th, the General Association of Chinese Laborers Who Participated in the War in France and various overseas Chinese groups in France held a celebration meeting at the General Association of Chinese Laborers to celebrate the establishment of the People's Republic of China. Hundreds of overseas Chinese from all walks of life in France, including industry, commerce, and academia, as well as all personnel from the former reactionary Kuomintang government's embassies and consulates in France, attended. A flag-raising ceremony was held at the meeting, and the national flag of the People's Republic of China, the five-star red flag, appeared in the sky above Paris for the first time. After the flag-raising ceremony, the participants solemnly and enthusiastically sang the national anthem of the People's Republic of China. Subsequently, overseas Chinese from all walks of life spoke one after another, unanimously expressing their love for the new China led by Chairman Mao. The meeting passed a congratulatory telegram to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, expressing sincere congratulations on the birth of the People's Republic of China.
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: According to an Agence France-Presse dispatch from Tokyo: The Overseas Chinese Association in Japan, uniting 30,000 overseas Chinese in Japan, gathered on the 10th to celebrate the establishment of the People's Republic of China. The celebration meeting officially announced its support for the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. On this day, the motherland's national flag, the five-star red flag, fluttered in the wind above the association's location in the center of Tokyo. A huge portrait of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu was hung at the meeting. Thousands of overseas Chinese representatives and Japanese progressives listened to revolutionary songs and speeches at the meeting. Japanese Communist Party leader Nozaka Sanzo (Okano Susumu) attended and gave a speech.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: The Chinese Labor Association in Makassar, Indonesia (on the southwest coast of Sulawesi Island) sent a telegram congratulating the establishment of the Central People's Government of China, stating: To the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, please forward to the Chairman and all government members of the Central People's Government in Beijing: On the occasion of the establishment of the new democratic government of China, we, like our compatriots at home, are rejoicing in unison. Please accept our sincere congratulations. Chinese Labor Association.

### 旅法旅日华侨欢腾集会　升五星红旗高唱国歌  印尼麦加撒中华劳工会电贺中央人民政府成立

1949-10-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　旅法旅日华侨欢腾集会
    升五星红旗高唱国歌
    印尼麦加撒中华劳工会电贺中央人民政府成立
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】巴黎讯：九日午后，旅法参战华工总会及旅法各华侨团体在华工总会举行庆贺中华人民共和国成立大会，到有旅法工商学各界侨胞及前国民党反动政府驻法使领馆全体人员数百人。会上曾举行升旗礼，中华人民共和国的国旗五星红旗第一次出现于巴黎上空。升旗礼后，与会者严肃而热烈地高唱中华人民共和国国歌。接着，各界侨胞纷纷发言，一致表示对毛主席所领导的新中国的热爱。大会通过致中华人民共和国中央人民政府贺电，表示竭诚庆贺中华人民共和国的诞生。
    【新华社北京十三日电】据法新社东京讯：团结着三万旅日华侨的旅日华侨协会，于十日集会庆祝中华人民共和国成立。庆祝大会正式宣布拥护中华人民共和国中央人民政府。这一天，祖国的国旗五星红旗在东京市中心的协会所在地上空迎风飘扬。大会上挂着毛主席和朱总司令的巨幅肖像。数千华侨代表和日本进步人士在会上听取了革命歌曲和演说。日共领袖野坂参三（冈野进）曾出席演讲。
    【新华社北京十三日电】印度尼西亚麦加撒（在西里伯斯岛西南岸）中华劳工会电贺中国中央人民政府成立称：上海市人民政府请转北京中央人民政府主席暨各政府委员：值此中华新民主政府成立之际，我们和国内同胞一样，同声欢跃。请接受我们衷诚的庆贺。中华劳工会。
