Title: Beijing-Guangzhou Telegraph Lines Open
Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 1

　　Beijing-Guangzhou Telegraph Lines Open
    [Beijing News] After the liberation of Guangzhou, wireless telegraph communications between Beijing and Guangzhou were restored at 2:00 PM the day before yesterday (the 15th).
