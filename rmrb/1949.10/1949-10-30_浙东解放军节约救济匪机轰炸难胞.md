Title: Zhejiang East Liberation Army Saves and Relieves Bombing Victims of Bandit Planes

Author:

Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 29th. The People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang East front has universally launched a saving and disaster relief campaign to help the victims of the Kuomintang bandit planes' indiscriminate bombing along the coast of Zhejiang East. Recently, various units have contributed a total of 18,635 jin of rice saved from their rations.

### 浙东解放军节约救济匪机轰炸难胞

1949-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东解放军节约救济匪机轰炸难胞
    【新华社杭州二十九日电】浙东前线人民解放军普遍发起节约救灾运动，救济浙东沿海遭受国民党匪机滥肆轰炸的灾民。日前各部已献出节约食米共一万八千六百三十五斤。
