Title: Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association to Open Second Plenary Session Tomorrow
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 4

　　Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association
    To Open Second Plenary Session Tomorrow
    [Beijing News] The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association is scheduled to hold its second plenary meeting at 9:00 AM on the 7th of this month in Beijing. The agenda includes work reports from various departments of the Standing Committee and local preparatory committees, as well as discussions on the association's future preparatory work and issues related to propaganda, organization, and operations in journalistic work. In addition to notifying all preparatory committee members to attend, the meeting will also invite journalists from various regions who are in Beijing to cover the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to attend as observers. Furthermore, the association has moved to a new address at 26 Guohui Street, with the telephone number being 2-3307.

### 全国新闻工作者协会筹备会　明开二届全体会

1949-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国新闻工作者协会筹备会
    明开二届全体会
    【北京讯】中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会，定本月七日上午九时，在京召开第二次全体会议。议程有常委会各部门和各地筹委的工作报告，及讨论该会今后的筹备工作和新闻工作中的宣传、组织、经营等问题。该会除已通知各筹委出席外，并将邀请此次来京采访人民政协新闻的各地记者列席。又该会已迁移至国会街二十六号新址，电话为二局三三○七。
