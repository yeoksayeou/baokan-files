Title: Launching the Communist Youth League Work in Beijing: Municipal Committee of the Youth League Established, Xu Liqun, Yang Bozhen, and Zhang Dazhong Appointed as Secretary and Deputy Secretaries

Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 21st: In order to launch the work of establishing the Communist Youth League in Beijing, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League has established the Beijing Municipal Working Committee of the Youth League. Xu Liqun, Yang Bozhen, Zhang Dazhong, Hou Junyan, Xiang Ziming, Zhang Jinlin, Ji Zhen, Du Ping, Yao Yisha, Xue Chengye, Zhang Shuowen, Sun Zhongming, Peng Peiyun, Leng Lin, Hu Juchang, Pan Liang, and Shi Luyi have been appointed as members. Xu Liqun is appointed as Secretary, Yang Bozhen as First Deputy Secretary, and Zhang Dazhong as Second Deputy Secretary. Xu Liqun, Yang Bozhen, Zhang Dazhong, Hou Junyan, Xiang Ziming, Zhang Jinlin, Ji Zhen, Du Ping, and Yao Yisha are appointed as Standing Committee members.

### 开展北京市建团工作　青年团市工委成立  许立群杨伯箴张大中分任正副书记

1949-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　开展北京市建团工作
  　青年团市工委成立
    许立群杨伯箴张大中分任正副书记
    【新华社北京二十一日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为开展北京市建团工作，特成立青年团北京市工作委员会，任命许立群、杨伯箴、张大中、侯俊岩、项子明、张进霖、冀真、杜平、姚亦沙、薛成业、张硕文、孙仲鸣、、彭佩云、冷林、胡聚长、潘梁、施璐怡等十八人为委员，并以许立群任书记，杨伯箴任第一副书记，张大中任第二副书记，以许立群、杨伯箴、张大中、侯俊岩、项子明、张进霖、冀真、杜平、姚亦沙任常务委员。
