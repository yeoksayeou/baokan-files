Title: Political Leaders and People's Organizations from Various Countries Congratulate New China, Chairman Mao Sends Return Telegrams of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th - Since the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, political leaders and people's organizations from various countries belonging to the people's democratic camp have sent congratulatory telegrams. Chairman Mao Zedong has sent return telegrams, expressing his deep gratitude.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th - Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a return telegram to the five Members of Parliament from the Independent Labour Group of the British House of Commons, which read: To Messrs. Pritt, Hodgkinsson, Mills, Solley, and Zilliacus:

I, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, thank you for your congratulations. May your righteous efforts and those of the broad British people to enhance the friendship between the Chinese and British peoples be successful.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, October 12, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th - Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a return telegram to the American Council for a Democratic Far Eastern Policy, which read:

Thank you deeply for your congratulations on the founding of the People's Republic of China, and for your efforts to enhance friendship between the Chinese and American peoples.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, October 12, 1949

### 各国政治领袖和人民团体庆贺新中国  毛主席先后复电致谢

1949-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国政治领袖和人民团体庆贺新中国
    毛主席先后复电致谢
    【新华社北京十二日电】中国人民政治协商会议开幕和中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立以来，属于人民民主阵营的各国政治领袖和人民团体纷纷来电祝贺。毛泽东主席已先后电复，表示深切的感谢。
    【新华社北京十二日电】毛泽东主席本日电复英国下院工党独立集团议员五人的贺电称：普里特、霍金逊、米尔斯、索莱、齐里亚库斯五位先生：
    我代表中国人民和中华人民共和国中央人民政府感谢你们的祝贺。愿你们和广大英国人民关于增进中英人民友谊的正当努力得到成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东　一九四九年十月十二日
    【新华社北京十二日电】毛泽东主席本日电复美国远东民主政策委员会的贺电称：
    深深地感谢你们对中华人民共和国成立的祝贺，和你们对增进中美人民友谊的努力。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东　一九四九年十月十二日
