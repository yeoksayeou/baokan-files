Title: Natural Science Lecture Program on Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd—The natural science common knowledge lecture program of Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station for the week starting October 23rd is as follows: October 23rd, the great Soviet biologist Timiryazev; October 24th, how to take a bus (Yang Guangzhong, Institute of Atomic Energy, Beijing Research Institute); October 25th, Gold (Gong Jiesheng, Department of Geology, Peking University); Silver (Zhai Yusheng, Department of Geology, Peking University); October 26th, Aluminum (Lin Moyin, Department of Geology, Peking University); October 27th, From Copper to China's Copper Mines (Zhai Yusheng); October 28th, From Lead to China's Lead-Zinc Mines (Zhao Pengda, Department of Geology, Peking University); October 29th, From Antimony to China's Antimony Mines (Gong Jiesheng).

### 北京新华广播电台自然科学讲座节目

1949-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台自然科学讲座节目
    【新华社二十二日电】北京新华广播电台自十月二十三日起一星期的自然科学常识讲座节目如下：十月二十三日，伟大的苏联生物学家季米里亚席夫；十月二十四日，怎样乘车（北京研究院原子研究所杨光中）；十月二十五日，金（北京大学地质系巩杰生）；银（北京大学地质系翟裕生）；十月二十六日，铝（北京大学地质系林墨荫）；十月二十七日，从铜谈到我国的铜矿（翟裕生）；十月二十八日，从铅谈到我国的铅锌矿（北京大学地质系赵鹏大）；十月二十九日，从锑谈到我国的锑矿（巩杰生）。
