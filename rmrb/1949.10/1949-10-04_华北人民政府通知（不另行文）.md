Title: Notice from the North China People's Government (Not to be issued separately)
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 1

　　Notice from the North China People's Government (Not to be issued separately)
    In order to celebrate the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, it is hereby decided that all personnel under the supply system or contract system of the Party, government, military and mass organizations in the North China region will be subsidized with one jin of pork per person for a celebratory meal, which will be issued based on the assessed value in October. It is hoped that this will be implemented immediately upon publication. This notice is hereby issued.
    October 2nd

### 华北人民政府通知（不另行文）

1949-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府通知（不另行文）
    为庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立，特决定华北区党政军及群众团体所有供给制或包干制之干杂人员，每人补助会餐费猪肉一斤，按十月份评价折款发给，希见报后即按照执行，特此通知。
    十月二日
