Title: Victory Meeting of the Northern Guangdong People's Liberation Army and People's Guerrilla Forces Celebrate the Birth of New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 3

　　Northern Guangdong People's Liberation Army and People's Guerrilla Forces Victoriously Meet
    Celebrating the Birth of New China

    [Xinhua News Agency, Southern China Front, 5th] The People's Liberation Army that liberated Nanxiong and Shixing has victoriously joined forces with the People's Guerrilla forces that persisted in the guerrilla war in the Five Ridges Mountains. During the victory meeting of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army welcoming a certain unit of the Guangdong-Jiangxi-Hunan Border Column on the 1st, the two brotherly forces gathered together to warmly celebrate the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Chairman Mao's election as the Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The cultural troupe of a certain unit also held an evening party on the evening of the 1st to express sincere condolences to the People's Guerrilla forces who have persisted in the guerrilla war in the Five Ridges Mountains for more than ten years.

### 粤北人民解放军、人民游击队胜利会师　共庆新中国诞生

1949-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　粤北人民解放军、人民游击队胜利会师
    共庆新中国诞生
    【新华社华南前线五日电】解放南雄、始兴的人民解放军已与坚持五岭山游击战争的人民游击队胜利会师。在一日解放军某部欢迎粤赣湘边纵队某部的胜利会师中，两支兄弟部队欢聚一堂，热烈庆贺中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立，和毛主席当选中华人民共和国中央人民政府的主席。某部文工团并于一日晚举行晚会，向坚持五岭山游击战争历十余年的人民游击队表示衷心的慰问。
