Title: Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace, Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to Our National Committee for the Defense of World Peace
Author: Nikolai Tikhonov
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace,
　　Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to Our National Committee for the Defense of World Peace
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th] A congratulatory telegram from Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace, to the Chinese National Committee for the Defense of World Peace has been received here. The telegram reads as follows:
　　Dear friends of the Chinese National Committee for the Defense of World Peace:
　　I extend my warmest greetings to the Chinese National Committee for the Defense of World Peace. I deeply regret that I cannot personally attend this conference! This conference of the great and outstanding people of China—a conference of peace advocates—is to open on October 2nd, International Peace Day. At the same time, millions of people in countries around the world will also hold grand marches and demonstrations on this day to demonstrate international unity, express their will to strive for peace, and affirm their belief that the democratic camp is far stronger than the war camp.
　　The Chinese people have waged many heroic struggles for the cause of peace, striving for freedom and independence, and have achieved success. All these achievements enable the people of the entire Soviet Union and all peace-loving people around the world to love and thank the Chinese people.
　　The people of the Soviet state—the pillar of world peace—are joyfully celebrating the fact that China, led by the Communist Party and the heroic people's leader Mao Zedong, has begun the construction of a new China of people's democracy, and deeply believe that the Chinese people can finally and completely defeat the reactionary remnants of aggression.
　　The Soviet people, the loyal old friends of the Chinese people, know that no force in the world can undermine this work of creating peace and happiness for all mankind. The Chinese National Committee for the Defense of World Peace is a clear event in the struggle for peace, a clear encouragement to peace-loving friends around the world, and a great blow to all instigators of new wars.
　　Long live the great Chinese people!
　　Long live Mao Zedong, the great, supremely glorious leader of the Chinese people!
　　Long live the unbreakable friendship between the two great peoples of China and the Soviet Union!
　　Long live the great Stalin, the inspirer of the struggle for peace, the wise leader and standard-bearer of the people of the world!

### 苏拥护世界和平大会主席吉洪诺夫　电贺我保卫世界和平大会
尼克莱·吉洪诺夫
1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏拥护世界和平大会主席吉洪诺夫
  　电贺我保卫世界和平大会
    【新华社北京四日电】此间收到苏联拥护世界和平大会主席吉洪诺夫致中国保卫世界和平大会的贺电，电文如下：
    中国保卫世界和平大会亲爱的朋友们：
    我深深地向中国保卫世界和平大会致热烈的敬礼。我不能亲身来参加这个大会，很觉遗憾！伟大的中国优秀人们的这个大会—拥护和平者大会，要在十月二日国际和平日这一天开幕，同时全世界各国内的千百万民众，也要在这一天进行为国际大团结，表现为争取和平的意志及坚信世界民主阵营远强于战争阵营的大游行示威。
    中国人民为了和平事业，争取自由与独立，进行了很多英勇的斗争，并获得了成就。所有这些成就，足使全苏联人民及全世界爱好和平的人民对中国人民予以爱戴及感谢。
    苏维埃国家的人民——就是世界和平的支柱，他们在兴高采烈地庆祝着以共产党及英勇的人民领袖毛泽东为首的中国，开始了人民民主的新中国的建设，并深深相信中国人民能最后全部战胜反动派的侵略残余力量。
    中国人民的忠实的老朋友——苏联人民，知道世界上任何力量也不能破坏这种创造全人类的和平幸福事业的工作。中国保卫世界和平大会就是争取和平的显明事迹，就是对全世界爱好和平的朋友们的显明鼓舞，也就是对所有新战争的挑拨者的大大打击。
    伟大中国人民万岁！
    中国人民的伟大的无上光荣的领袖毛泽东万岁！
    中苏两大人民坚不可破的友谊万岁！
    争取和平的鼓舞者、英明的世界人民领袖与旗手伟大的斯大林万岁！
