Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its Third Meeting; Approves the Organizational Regulations for Subordinate Agencies; Appoints Secretaries-General of Four Committees and Directors-General of Various Bureaus; Submits the List of the Suiyuan Provincial Government to the Chairman for Approval.
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 28th: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its third government affairs meeting today. Those present were Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Tan Pingshan, Xie Juezai, Bo Yibo, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Bojun, Li Lisan, Ma Xulun, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shao＠. Premier Zhou presided over the meeting.

　　The first item on the agenda was reporting matters. First, Vice Premier Dong reported on the emergency epidemic prevention meeting. The Government Administration Council decided to additionally appoint Government Affairs Commissioner Huang Shao＠ to participate in the Central Epidemic Prevention Committee. Next, Premier Zhou reported on the issue of foreign language translations of the names of government agencies. The meeting decided to assign Commissioners Li Lisan, Luo Longji, and Shao Lizi, as well as Zhang Xiruo, Qian Duansheng, and Qiao Guanhua, to conduct research.

　　The second item on the agenda was discussion matters. The "Organizational Regulations for Agencies Subordinate to the Government Administration Council" were approved, and it was decided to order all subordinate agencies to formulate draft organizational regulations based on these regulations and submit them to the Government Administration Council before November 5th, which will then be submitted to the Legislative Affairs Committee for preliminary review. Regarding the issue of taking over the central agencies, personnel, archives, books, property, and materials of the former Kuomintang government, the meeting approved the "Working Regulations of the Government Administration Council's Guiding Committee for Takeover Work," ordering the committee to be established and begin work as soon as possible, and decided that the committee would work within the Government Administration Council.

　　The third item on the agenda was appointments. The meeting unanimously approved the list of the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government and submitted it to the Chairman of the Central People's Government for approval. Finally, the appointment of thirty-two Government Administration Councilors was approved.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 28th: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government has announced the following appointments: Tao Xijin as Secretary-General of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council; Xue Muqiao as Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, with Hu Ziying and Zhou Rongxin as Deputy Secretaries-General; Hu Qiaomu as Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, with Yang Hansheng and Feng Naichao as Deputy Secretaries-General; Li Shizhang as Secretary-General of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council; Song Shaowen as Director-General of the Central Financial and Economic Planning Bureau; Cao Juru, Qian Changzhao, and Sun Xiaocun as Deputy Directors-General; Fan Ziwen as Director-General of the Central Financial and Economic Personnel Bureau; Zhong Lin as Director-General of the Central Technological Administration Bureau, with Chen Xiuhe as Deputy Director-General; Xue Muqiao as Director-General of the Central Private Enterprise Bureau, with Qian Jiaju and Wu Gengmei as Deputy Directors-General; Meng Yongqian as Director-General of the Central Cooperative Affairs Administration Bureau, with Liang Yao and Yu Shude as Deputy Directors-General; Ji Chaoding as Director-General of the Central Foreign Investment Enterprise Bureau; Wang Yinsheng as Director of the Compilation Office of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, with Li Guojun as Deputy Director.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 28th: The list of the thirty-two Government Administration Councilors approved at today's third government affairs meeting of the Government Administration Council is as follows: Lu Jiyi (Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang), Zheng Kunlian (Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang), Luo Ziwei (China Democratic League), Zhang Yuanshan (China National Democratic Construction Association), Sheng Kangnian (China National Democratic Construction Association), Zhang Yunchuan (Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party), Yan Xinmin (Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party), Wang Shenlin (Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party), Cao Mengjun (China National Salvation Association), Xu Baoju (San Min Principles Comrades Association), Wu Maosun (San Min Principles Comrades Association), Yu Zhenying (San Min Principles Comrades Association), Tan Dongjing (China Kuomintang Democratic Promotion Association), Lin Yiyuan (China Kuomintang Democratic Promotion Association), Yan Xichun (China Zhi Gong Party), Yuan Hanqing (Jiusan Society), Wu Zaoxi (Jiusan Society), Zhou Shiguan (Areas to be Liberated), Li Xigong (Areas to be Liberated), Sun Sunquan (Freelancer), Zhuang Mingli (Overseas Chinese), Lu Gandao (Specially Invited), Li Zheng (Specially Invited), Lu Yuwen (Specially Invited), Wang Shiming (Specially Invited), Xu Wentian (Specially Invited), Deng Haoming (Specially Invited), Cheng Xingling (Specially Invited), Liu Chenglie, Fan Puqi, Liu Zhonghua, Li Junlong.

### 政务院开第三次会议　通过所属各机构组织通则  任命四委员会秘书长及各局局长呈请主席批准任命绥远省政府名单

1949-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院开第三次会议　
    通过所属各机构组织通则            
    任命四委员会秘书长及各局局长呈请主席批准任命绥远省政府名单
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中央人民政府政务院今日举行第三次政务会议，出席者周恩来、董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培、谭平山、谢觉哉、薄一波、滕代远、章伯钧、李立三、马叙伦、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、黄绍＠。会议由周总理主持。议程第一项为报告事项。首由董副总理报告紧急防疫会议情形。政务会议决定加派政务委员黄绍＠参加中央防疫委员会。继由周总理报告政府各机关外国文字译名问题，会议决定指派李立三、罗隆基、邵力子三委员及张奚若、钱端升、乔冠华等进行研究。会议第二项议程为讨论事项。通过“政务院所属各机关组织通则”，并决定限令所属各机关根据该通则制定组织条例草案，于十一月五日以前呈送政务院，由政务院交法制委员会先行审议。关于接收前国民党政府中央机关、人员、档案、图书、财产、物资问题，会议通过“政务院指导接收工作委员会工作条例”，限令该委员会从速成立，开始工作，并决定该委员会在政务院内办公。议程第三项为任命事项。会议一致通过绥远省人民政府名单，呈请中央人民政府主席批准任命。最后，通过任命政务院参事三十二人。
