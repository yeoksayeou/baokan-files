Title: China Youth Lecture Series: Jiang Nanxiang's Speech
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 4

　　China Youth Lecture Series
    Jiang Nanxiang's Speech
    [Beijing News] The 12th "China Youth Lecture Series," sponsored by the "China Youth Society" of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, will be held tomorrow (the 16th) at 8:30 AM at the Taimiao Temple (if it rains, it will be moved to the auditorium of the Normal University outside Hepingmen). Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, will speak on "How to Study the Three Major Documents of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference." Cadres, teachers, students, workers, and citizens are welcome to attend.

### 中国青年讲演会　蒋南翔讲演

1949-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国青年讲演会
    蒋南翔讲演
    【北京讯】青年团中央宣传部“中国青年社”主办的第十二次“中国青年讲演会”，订于明日（十六）上午八点半钟在太庙举行（天雨改在和平门外师范大学礼堂）。请中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔讲“怎样学习人民政协三大文件”。欢迎干部、教师、学生、工人和市民，前往听讲。
