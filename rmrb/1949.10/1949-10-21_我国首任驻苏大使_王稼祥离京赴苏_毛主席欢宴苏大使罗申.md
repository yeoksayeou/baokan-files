Title: China's First Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Wang Jiaxiang, Departs Beijing for the Soviet Union; Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Soviet Ambassador Roshchin
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 20th:
　　China's First Ambassador to the Soviet Union
　　Wang Jiaxiang Departs Beijing for the Soviet Union
　　Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Soviet Ambassador Roshchin
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 20th】Wang Jiaxiang, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, accompanied by Counselor Zeng Yongquan and eight others, left Beijing by special train at 10 PM today for the Soviet Union. Those who went to the station to see him off included Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Committee; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; and Peng Zhen, Li Lisan, Cai Chang, Liu Ningyi, Shao Lizi, Li Dequan, Qian Junrui, and other representatives of the Beijing Municipal Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and workers, women, youth, students, and teachers of Beijing, totaling more than one hundred people. Soviet Ambassador to New China, Roshchin, and personnel from the embassy also went to the station to see him off.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 20th】Chairman Mao Zedong hosted a banquet for Soviet Ambassador Roshchin at 7 PM today. Those invited to the banquet included Ambassador Roshchin, Counselor Tikhvinskiy, and Consul General Seregin. Those accompanying Chairman Mao were Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, and Gao Gang, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Committee; Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Chief of the General Staff; Wang Jiaxiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs and Ambassador to the Soviet Union; and Li Kenong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs. ### 我国首任驻苏大使　王稼祥离京赴苏　毛主席欢宴苏大使罗申

1949-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国首任驻苏大使
  　王稼祥离京赴苏
  　毛主席欢宴苏大使罗申
    【新华社北京二十日电】中华人民共和国驻苏联大使王稼祥率公使衔参赞曾涌泉等九人于今日下午十时乘专车离京赴苏。前往车站送行的有中央人民政府委员会秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、副总理郭沫若，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒和彭真、李立三、蔡畅、刘宁一、邵力子、李德全、钱俊瑞等与北京市政府、中共北京市委会、北京市工人、妇女、青年、学生、教职员等代表一百余人。苏联驻新中国大使罗申和大使馆人员也前往车站送行。
    【新华社北京二十日电】毛泽东主席在今天下午七时欢宴苏联大使罗申。应邀赴宴者有罗申大使，齐赫文斯基参赞，谢列金总领事。作陪者有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜、高岗，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，中央人民政府委员会秘书长林伯渠，人民革命军事委员会副主席程潜、副总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部副部长兼驻苏大使王稼祥、外交部副部长李克农。（附图片）
