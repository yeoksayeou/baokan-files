Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Shanghai Yesterday; Rao Shushi, Zeng Shan, and Others Greet Them at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th—The Soviet delegation of cultural, artistic, and scientific workers, long awaited by the people of Shanghai, arrived in Shanghai from Nanjing at 4:00 AM this morning. The Chinese Performing Arts Troupe that attended the World Youth Festival also arrived in Shanghai on the same train. More than a thousand representatives from all walks of life in Shanghai gathered at the station before 2:00 AM this morning to welcome them. Among those welcoming were Rao Shushi, Secretary of the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee; Zeng Shan, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; Pan Hannian, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shanghai Branch; Chen Wangdao, Xia Yan, General Secretary; and representatives from all circles, including Shu Tong, Feng Ding, Song Shilun, Guo Huaruo, Liu Changsheng, Zhao Yuhua, and Li Chang. Vladimir, a Soviet diplomatic staff member stationed in Shanghai, and ten representatives of the Soviet expatriate community also went to the station to welcome them. The special train entered the station amidst the welcoming music of the band. Fadeyev, the head of the Soviet delegation, Simonov, the deputy head, and others, accompanied by Xiao San and others, got off the train one after another and shook hands with Rao Shushi and others. A brief welcome ceremony was then held on the platform. Deputy Mayor Zeng Shan said in his welcoming speech: The people of Shanghai feel extremely honored by the arrival of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific delegation in Shanghai. The Soviet government's first establishment of diplomatic relations with our Central People's Government demonstrates the profound and unwavering friendship of the great Soviet people towards the Chinese people. The great construction of the Soviet Union over the past thirty years has yielded brilliant achievements and rich experience. Today, as we are about to transform Shanghai into a new city for the people, we especially need to learn from our friendly neighbor, the Soviet Union. The famous Soviet director Gerasimov, in his reply, congratulated the citizens and workers of Shanghai, who have a revolutionary tradition, on their victory, and said: "We are very happy to contribute all our experience and knowledge to you, to our beloved, closest friends and comrades, to develop a new democratic culture!" After the speech, the Soviet delegation accepted flowers, and then, accompanied by Rao Shushi, Zeng Shan, Pan Hannian, Liu Changsheng, and others, walked out of the platform. As they passed through the welcoming crowd, the masses cheered enthusiastically and presented large bouquets of flowers. Fadeyev and Simonov frequently waved their hats in return. On the station square, the mercury lamps and torches shone brightly, red flags waved along the road, and the slogans and cheers of "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship!" "Long Live Stalin!" "Long Live Chairman Mao Zedong!" merged into one.

### 苏代表团昨抵沪　饶漱石曾山等赴站欢迎

1949-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏代表团昨抵沪
  　饶漱石曾山等赴站欢迎
    【新华社上海十五日电】上海人民所渴望的苏联文化、艺术、科学工作者代表团于今晨四时由宁抵沪。我国出席世界青年节的文工团亦同车来沪。上海各界代表千余人于今晨二时前即齐集车站迎接。欢迎者中有中共中央华东局书记兼上海市委会书记饶漱石、上海市人民政府副市长曾山、中苏友好协会上海分会筹备委员会副主任潘汉年、陈望道，总干事夏衍，及各界代表舒同、冯定、宋时轮、郭化若、刘长胜、赵毓华、李昌等。苏联留沪外交工作人员符拉基米洛夫及苏联侨民代表共十人亦到车站欢迎。专车在乐队的欢迎曲中进站。苏联代表团团长法捷耶夫、副团长西蒙诺夫等，由萧三等陪同相继下车，与饶漱石等一一握手。简短的欢迎会随即在月台上举行。曾山副市长致欢迎词时略称：上海人民对苏联文化艺术科学代表团的来到上海，感到极大的光荣。苏联政府之首先与我中央人民政府建立邦交，这表明伟大苏联人民对中国人民的深厚友谊，是始终不渝的。苏联三十余年来的伟大建设有了辉煌的成就和丰富的经验。今天正当我们要将上海改造为人民的新城市时，我们特别要向友邦苏联学习。苏联名导演格拉西莫夫致答词时向有革命传统的上海市民和工人祝贺胜利，并说：“我们很高兴把我们的全部经验和知识，贡献给你们，贡献给我们所敬爱的最亲密的朋友们和同志们，来发展新民主主义的文化！”致词毕，苏联代表团接受献花，继即由饶漱石、曾山、潘汉年、刘长胜等陪同步出月台。当他们经过欢迎的行列时，群众热烈欢呼，纷纷投献大束鲜花，法捷耶夫与西蒙诺夫等则频频挥帽答礼。车站广场上水银灯与火炬交相辉映，红旗夹道挥舞，“中苏友好万岁！”“斯大林万岁！”“毛泽东主席万岁！”的口号声和欢呼声融成一片。
