Title: PLA Unit to Conduct Explosion Exercise Today
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 4

　　The People's Liberation Army, a unit of the North China Military Region, will conduct explosion exercises and live ammunition artillery firing at the Huangsi Barracks in Beijing City today (26th). Citizens should not be alarmed by the sound.

### 解放军某部今演习爆炸

1949-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　解放军某部今演习爆炸
    【本报讯】人民解放军华北军区某部定今（二十六）日在京市黄寺营房演习爆炸及炮兵实弹射击。市民闻声勿惊。
