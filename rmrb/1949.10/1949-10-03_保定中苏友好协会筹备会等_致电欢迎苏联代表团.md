Title: Baoding China-Soviet Friendship Association Preparatory Committee, etc., Sends Telegram to Welcome Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 2

　　【Baoding, 1st Dispatch】The Preparatory Committee of the China-Soviet Friendship Association Baoding Branch and the Preparatory Committee of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Workers today sent telegrams to welcome the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation. The telegrams stated: We extend our warmest welcome to you, and pay our highest respects to the great revolutionary leader Stalin, the banner of world democracy and peace! We will never forget the friendship of the Soviet people towards the Chinese people, although this friendship was damaged and insulted by the Chinese counter-revolutionary Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is growing closer and closer. Now that the People's Republic of China has been proclaimed, there are no obstacles to the friendship and cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union. Let us shake hands more warmly! We and the people of Baoding are planning to learn from the Soviet Union's various construction experiences and great internationalist spirit. May we work together to smash the war schemes of the imperialist warmongers, defend world peace, and create a high level of human culture!

### 保定中苏友好协会筹备会等　致电欢迎苏联代表团

1949-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　保定中苏友好协会筹备会等
  　致电欢迎苏联代表团
    【保定一日电】中苏友好协会保定分会筹备会、河北省文学艺术工作者联合会筹委会于今日分别致电欢迎苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团。电文中略称：我们谨以无限热诚表示对你们的欢迎，并向世界民主和平的旗帜伟大的革命领袖斯大林致以崇高的敬意！我们永远不会忘记苏联人民对中国人民的友谊，虽然这友谊曾遭到中国的反革命蒋介石匪帮所破坏和污辱，但中国人民与苏联人民的友谊却是越来越密切。现在中华人民共和国已宣告成立，中苏两国人民的友谊合作已无任何障碍，让我们更加热烈的握手吧！我们与保市人民正在有计划的设法学习苏联的各种建设经验和伟大的国际主义精神，愿我们共同为粉碎帝国主义战争贩子的战争阴谋，保卫世界和平，创造人类高度的文化而努力！
