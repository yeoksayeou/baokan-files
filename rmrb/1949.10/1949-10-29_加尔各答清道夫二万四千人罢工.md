Title: 24,000 Calcutta Cleaners on Strike
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 27th: Calcutta, October 25th: Following the strike of 24,000 municipal employees on the 21st, Calcutta cleaners also went on strike at midnight on the same day in response. The city is now piled high with garbage, estimated to be over 10,000 tons. Foul odors and filth are causing serious epidemics. According to statistics, the number of cholera cases on the 24th was more than double that of the previous two days.

### 加尔各答清道夫二万四千人罢工

1949-10-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　加尔各答清道夫二万四千人罢工
    【新华社北京二十七日电】加尔各答二十五日讯：继二十一日市政机关二万四千雇员罢工之后，加尔各答清道夫也于当日午夜起罢工响应。现加城垃圾堆积成山，估计达一万余吨。脏气秽物招致严重的流行病，据统计，二十四日患霍乱病的人数，已超过前两天的一倍。
