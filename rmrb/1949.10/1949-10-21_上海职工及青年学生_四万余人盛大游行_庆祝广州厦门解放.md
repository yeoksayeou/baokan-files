Title: Shanghai Workers and Students Hold Grand Parade of Over 40,000 to Celebrate the Liberation of Guangzhou and Xiamen
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Workers and Students
  　Hold Grand Parade of Over 40,000
  　to Celebrate the Liberation of Guangzhou and Xiamen
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th] More than 20,000 workers from over 60 units in Shanghai, including railways, seamen, and industries, held a grand parade on the afternoon of the 18th to welcome the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation and to celebrate the liberation of Guangzhou. Starting from 2:00 PM, worker contingents from Pudong, Puxi, Pubei, and the central district converged at Tilanqiao from Yangshupu, Huishan Road, and Changzhi Road in Pudong. The parade proceeded majestically along Dongdaming Road towards the Bund, with drums beating and cheers ringing out. Workers held aloft giant portraits of the four great revolutionary figures: Mao Zedong, Sun Yat-sen, Lenin, and Stalin, demonstrating the deep friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. The "China-Soviet Cooperation Great Iron Bridge" made by the Shanghai Repair Factory and the railway workers' masterpiece, the "Guangzhou" train, were also particularly eye-catching. Yangko teams and waist drum teams from various units performed a variety of wonderful programs, which were warmly welcomed by the audience. In the evening, the marching队伍队伍队伍lit torches, illuminating the streets.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th] More than 20,000 students in Shanghai held a grand parade on the 19th to celebrate the liberation of Guangzhou and Xiamen and to bid farewell to the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation as they departed Shanghai. The marching crowds shouted slogans such as "Celebrate the Liberation of Guangzhou and Xiamen" and "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship" along the way.

### 上海职工及青年学生　四万余人盛大游行　庆祝广州厦门解放

1949-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海职工及青年学生
  　四万余人盛大游行
  　庆祝广州厦门解放
    【新华社上海二十日电】沪市铁路、海员、产业等六十多个单位二万余职工为欢迎苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团及庆祝广州解放，曾于十八日下午举行盛大游行。自下午二时起，沪东、沪西、沪北和中区工人队伍，即分别由杨树浦、汇山路、浦东长治路三路会师于提篮桥，浩浩荡荡地沿东大名路，向外滩进发，一路敲锣打鼓，欢呼歌唱，工人们高举毛泽东、孙中山、宁列、斯大林四个革命伟人的巨像，表示中苏两国人民深厚的友谊。上海修缮厂所制的“中苏合作大铁桥”和铁路工人的杰作“广州号火车”，也很引人注目。各单位的秧歌队、腰鼓队表演各种精彩的节目，获得观众的热烈欢迎。入晚，游行的队伍燃起火炬照得市街通明。
    【新华社上海二十日电】上海市青年学生二万余人，于十九日举行盛大游行，庆祝广州、厦门解放，并欢送苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团离沪。游行队伍沿途高呼“庆祝广州、厦门解放”、“中苏友谊万岁”等口号。
