Title: The Congress Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao to Congratulate Him

Author:

Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th. The Czechoslovakian Party, government, and people's organizations of all circles held a celebration on the 12th to celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China, and unanimously passed a telegram to Chairman Mao to congratulate him. The telegram stated: "The people of Czechoslovakia, who have gathered to express their warm enthusiasm for the founding of the People's Republic of China, respectfully salute you, the great representative of the Chinese people, and wish you and the entire Chinese people a most happy future. For us, the victory of the Chinese people is a guarantee of the common victory of peace and progress in the world. Moreover, we firmly believe that our two nations will advance towards this goal in the closest friendship with the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries."

### 大会电毛主席祝贺

1949-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　大会电毛主席祝贺
    【新华社布拉格十四日电】十二日捷克党政和各界人民团体庆祝中华人民共和国成立大会，一致通过致电毛主席祝贺。电文称：以集会来表示自己对中华人民共和国成立之热忱的捷克斯洛伐克人民，谨向你——中国人民伟大的代表致敬，并祝你和全体中国人民有一个最幸福的将来。对我们说来，中国人民的胜利，是全世界和平进步的共同胜利的保证。而且，我们坚信，我们两个民族决将在与苏联及其他人民民主国家最亲密的友谊中，向这个目标前进。
