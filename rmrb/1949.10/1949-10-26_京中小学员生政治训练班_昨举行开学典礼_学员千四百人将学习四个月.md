Title: Beijing Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Students Political Training Class Holds Opening Ceremony Yesterday; 1,400 Trainees Will Study for Four Months
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 4

　　Beijing Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Students Political Training Class
    Holds Opening Ceremony Yesterday
    1,400 Trainees Will Study for Four Months
    [Beijing News] The primary and secondary school teachers and students political training class, sponsored by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, held its opening ceremony at the auditorium of Fu Jen Catholic University at 7 pm yesterday (the 25th). There are more than 1,400 trainees (including 600 primary and secondary school teachers and staff and 800 secondary school students, of whom more than 100 are party members). First, Xiang Ziming, the director of the class, reported on the significance of opening the training class. The training period for the class is set for four months, ending at the end of February next year. The main learning content and documents are the Constitution of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi's "Report on the Revision of the Party Constitution" and "On the Self-Cultivation of Communists." The learning method emphasizes both lectures and self-study discussions. Then, Nie Zhen began to lecture on the first lesson: the position, viewpoint and method of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought.

### 京中小学员生政治训练班  昨举行开学典礼  学员千四百人将学习四个月

1949-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　京中小学员生政治训练班　
    昨举行开学典礼            
    学员千四百人将学习四个月
    【北京讯】中共北京市委会主办的中小学教员学生政治训练班，于昨（二十五）日下午七时在辅仁大学礼堂开学，学员共一千四百余人（包括中小学教职员六百人及中学生八百人，其中党员一百余人）。首先由该班主任项子明报告开设训练班的意义，训练班学习期间定为四个月，到明年二月底结束。学习内容和文件主要的是中国共产党党章，刘少奇同志“修改党章的报告”和“论共产党员的修养”。学习方法是讲授与自学讨论并重。继由聂真开始讲授第一课：马列主义毛泽东思想的立场观点与方法。
