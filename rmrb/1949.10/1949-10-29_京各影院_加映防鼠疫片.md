Title: Beijing Cinemas Add Anti-Plague Film Screenings
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 4

　　Beijing Cinemas
    Add Anti-Plague Film Screenings
    [This newspaper's report] To respond to the call of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council and the CCP Central Committee North China Bureau for the urgent prevention of pneumonic plague, the North China Film Manager Company has, from today (the 29th), added screenings of the educational short film "How to Prevent Pneumonic Plague," produced by the Northeast Film Studio, to every showing in the city's cinemas to enrich citizens' knowledge of epidemic prevention.

### 京各影院  加映防鼠疫片

1949-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　京各影院　
    加映防鼠疫片
    【本报讯】华北影片经理公司为响应中央人民政府政务院及中共中央华北局紧急预防鼠疫的号召，特自今（二十九）日起在本市各电影院每场加映东北电影制片厂出品之教育短片“怎样预防鼠疫”，以充实市民防疫知识。
