Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 4

    [This Newspaper's Report] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's employee program at 18:15 tonight will introduce the labor movement of Japan, one of the member countries of the Asian and Australasian Trade Union Conference to be held in Beijing soon. At 19:00, a lecture on common sense of natural science, given by Xue Qin, Associate Professor of Physics Department of the Faculty of Science of National Peking University: "Where does the electricity in the air come from?". At 18:30, the first cultural program, sponsored by the Chinese Improvement Society: Liu Shuhui sings the Meihua Dagu "Earning the City Plan". At 22:00, Soviet music will be broadcast.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    北京新华广播电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分职工节目，介绍即将在北京召开的亚、澳洲职工会代表会议会员国之一——日本的职工运动。十九点自然科学常识讲座，国立北京大学理学院物理系副教授薛琴访讲：“空中的电是哪里来的？”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国改进会主办：刘淑慧演唱梅花大鼓“赚城计”。二十二点播送苏联音乐。
