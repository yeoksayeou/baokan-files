Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Nanjing; Representatives from All Circles and Soviet Diplomatic Personnel Go to the Station to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1
    
　　Soviet Delegation Arrives in Nanjing
    Representatives from All Circles and Soviet Diplomatic Personnel Go to the Station to Welcome Them

    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 13th] The Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers' delegation, led by the famous writers Fadeyev and Simonov, consisting of 44 people, arrived in Nanjing at 5:05 AM on the 13th by special train from Beijing. Leading figures from the Nanjing Municipal Party, government, and military, cultural and scientific figures, representatives from various people's organizations numbering over a hundred, and Soviet diplomatic personnel from the Liuning office went to the station to give them a warm welcome. On the evening of the 12th, at 11:00 PM, representatives from the Nanjing Municipal Party, government, military and all walks of life gathered at the station to greet them, and especially dispatched Yuan Zhongxian, Commander of the Nanjing Garrison, Chen Xiuliang, Director of the Organization Department of the Municipal Party Committee, and Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, to cross the river to welcome them. When the train arrived at Xiaguan Station, military music played in unison, and Zhang Jichun, Deputy Political Commissar of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, Li Da, Chief of Staff, Zhou Junming, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Third Field Army, Zhang Kai, Deputy Director of the Political Department, Tang Liang, Deputy Director of the Military Control Commission, Ke Qingshi, Deputy Mayor, and scientists Yin Zanhsun, Jin Baoshan and others went forward to greet them. Fadeyev, Simonov and the other 40-plus members warmly shook hands with the welcomers one by one. At this moment, slogans such as "The Soviet Union is the true friend of the Chinese people" and "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship" resounded through the clouds. Soviet diplomatic personnel from the Liuning office and student and women representatives from Nanjing presented flowers. The Soviet friends were tightly surrounded by the welcoming crowd, and the road was blocked. When the delegation members walked out of the platform, the military music played loudly, and the honor guard paid tribute to the friends. The delegation members waved back frequently.

### 苏联代表团抵南京　各界代表及苏外交人员赴站欢迎

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联代表团抵南京
    各界代表及苏外交人员赴站欢迎
    【新华社南京十三日电】由著名作家法捷耶夫、西蒙诺夫率领的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团一行四十四人，已于十三日晨五时零五分由北京乘专车抵宁，南京市党政军首长、文化科学界人士及各人民团体代表百余人和苏联留宁外交工作人员均到车站热烈欢迎。十二日晚十一时，宁市党政军及各界代表即齐集车站迎接，并特派宁市警备司令袁仲贤，中共市委组织部长陈修良、外侨办事处处长黄华等渡江迎接。列车抵达下关车站，军乐齐奏，人民解放军第二野战军张际春副政委、李达参谋长、第三野战军周骏鸣副参谋长、张凯政治部副主任、军管会唐亮副主任、柯庆施副市长、科学界尹赞勋、金宝善等首先前往迎迓。法捷耶夫、西蒙诺夫等四十多人热情与欢迎者一一握手。此时，“苏联是中国人民真正的朋友”，“中苏友好万岁”等口号声，响澈云霄。苏联留宁外交工作人员与宁市学生、妇女代表前往献花。苏联的友人们被欢迎的人群紧紧包围，道路为之阻塞。当代表走出月台时，军乐大作，仪仗队齐向友人致敬。代表团人员频频挥手答礼。
