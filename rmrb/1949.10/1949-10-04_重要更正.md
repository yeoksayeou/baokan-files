　　Important Correction
    In yesterday's newspaper, page eight, in the article "A Great Day," in the third and sixth sections, "200,000" should be "300,000"; in the fourth section, "24 salvos" should be "28 salvos." We hereby issue this correction.

### 重要更正

1949-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    昨日本报八版所刊“伟大的一日”稿中，第三、六两节“二十万”应是“三十万”；第四节“二十四响”应是“二十八响”，特此更正。
