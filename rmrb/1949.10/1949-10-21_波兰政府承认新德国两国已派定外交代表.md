Title: Polish Government Recognizes New Germany, Both Countries Have Appointed Diplomatic Representatives
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th. Warsaw, 18th, Polish News Agency: The Polish government has decided to recognize the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic and dispatch diplomatic representatives to Germany. The Republic of Poland has appointed Karol Teokoz as the diplomatic representative to the government of the German Democratic Republic.
　　Also, Warsaw, 19th, Polish News Agency: The Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic has appointed the famous anti-fascist writer Friedrich Wolf as the diplomatic representative to the Polish government, and the Polish government has expressed its consent.

### 波兰政府承认新德国两国已派定外交代表

1949-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　波兰政府承认新德国两国已派定外交代表
    【新华社北京二十日电】波兰通讯社华沙十八日讯：波兰政府已决定承认德意志民主共和国临时政府，并派遣驻德外交代表。波兰共和国业已任命卡罗尔·特科兹为驻德意志民主共和国政府的外交代表。
    又波兰通讯社华沙十九日讯：德意志民主共和国临时政府已任命著名反法西斯作家腓特烈·吴尔夫为驻波兰政府外交代表，波兰政府业已表示同意。
