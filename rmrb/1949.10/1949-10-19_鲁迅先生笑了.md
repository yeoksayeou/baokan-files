Title: Mr. Lu Xun Smiled
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 5

Mr. Lu Xun Smiled

　　Mr. Lu Xun, people say you have left us for thirteen years,
　　But, I see you everywhere, you are so healthy,
　　Your face is no longer as bitter as usual,
　　But warm as spring, open and with depth, smiling.

　　On March 25th, at the Xiyuan Airport,
　　Chairman Mao and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China first arrived in the newly liberated
　　cultural capital – today’s people’s capital, Beijing.
　　All armed comrades, people's organizations, and leaders of democratic parties,
　　were on the field to welcome him, shouting "Long live Chairman Mao!"
　　At that time, I saw you, saw you smiling.

　　On April 23rd, in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia,
　　Representatives from seventy-two countries were simultaneously with Paris
　　holding the World Peace Congress in the parliament building,
　　The news of the liberation of Nanjing, the lair of the Chiang bandits, reached Europe,
　　All the representatives stood up and cheered: "Long live New China, long live Mao Zedong!"
　　At that time, I saw you, saw you smiling.

　　On the evening of July 1st, at the Xianongtan Public Stadium,
　　a rally was held to celebrate the 28th anniversary of the Communist Party of China.
　　Many young comrades enthusiastically, under the sudden heavy rain,
　　beat waist drums, danced the Yangko, and sang "The East is Red,"
　　actually conquered the haze, and after a rainbow appeared,
　　our leader appeared, and the whole audience cheered, "Long live Chairman Mao!"
　　At that time, I saw you, saw you smiling.

　　On the evening of July 6th, at the Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai,
　　on the fifth day of the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers,
　　at 7:20 PM, Chairman Mao suddenly arrived at the venue,
　　and said to everyone: "I am here to welcome you,
　　the people need you, therefore there is reason to welcome you."
　　The whole audience went wild, continuously cheering: "Long live Chairman Mao!"
　　At that time, I saw you, saw you smiling.

　　On September 21st, also in Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai,
　　the first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference opened,
　　Fifty-four salvos of artillery fire shattered all the shackles of China,
　　Chairman Mao delivered the opening speech, declaring "The era in which Chinese people were considered uncivilized has passed,
　　we will appear in the world as a nation with a high degree of culture."

　　On October 1st, in the large square in front of Tiananmen,
　　the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China was held.
　　Chairman Mao declared the establishment of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government
　　to the 300,000 people at Tiananmen,
　　The cheers of the audience were thunderous, and the sound of "Long live Chairman Mao" shook the heavens and the earth.
　　At that time, I saw you, saw you smiling.

　　Mr. Lu Xun, you will never leave us,
　　I almost always see you, see you smiling.
　　I believe this is by no means my personal illusion,
　　but the real feeling of thousands upon thousands of people.
　　I seem to hear you saying: "We should smile,
　　under the leadership of Chairman Mao, we should use our whole life,
　　to protect our smile, and smile until the emergence of the world of great harmony."
　　　　　　　　　　　　(October 17th)

### 鲁迅先生笑了

1949-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　鲁迅先生笑了
    鲁迅先生，人们说你离开我们十三年了，
    但，我却在四处都看见了你，你是那么健康，
    你的脸色已经再不象平常的那样苦涩，
    而是和暖如春地豁朗而有内函地在笑。
　
    三月二十五日，在西苑的飞机场上，
    毛主席和中共中央首先来到新解放的
    文化故都——今天的人民首都北京，
    全体武装同志，各人民团体，各民主党派的领袖，
    都在场上欢迎，高呼毛主席万岁！
    那时候我看见了你，看见你笑了。
　
    四月二十三日，在捷克首都布拉格，
    七十二个国家的代表正和巴黎同时
    在国会议事堂进行着世界拥护和平大会的时候，
    蒋匪的巢穴南京克复的消息传到了欧洲，
    全体代表一致起立欢呼：新中国万岁，毛泽东万岁！
    那时候我看见了你，看见你笑了。
　
    七月一日的晚上，在先农坛的公共体育场，
    举行中国共产党二十八周年纪念大会，
    许多年青的同志们热烈地在骤然袭来的大雨之下，
    打着腰鼓，扭着秧歌，唱着“东方红”，
    竟把阴霾征服了，在一道长虹出现之后，
    出现了我们的领袖，全场欢呼毛主席万岁！
    那时候我看见了你，看见你笑了。
　
    七月六日的晚上，在中南海的怀仁堂，
    中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会的第五天，
    下午七时二十分，毛主席突然到了会场，
    向大家说：“我是来欢迎你们的，
    人民需要你们，因此就有理由来欢迎你们。”
    全场热狂了，连续不断地欢呼：毛主席万岁！
    那时候我看见了你，看见你笑了。
　
    九月二十一日，也是在中南海的怀仁堂，
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议开幕，
    五十四声礼炮齐鸣震破了中国的一切枷锁，
    毛主席致开幕词，宣说“中国人被认为不文明的时代已经过去了，
    我们将以一个具有高度文化的民族出现于世界。”
　
    十月一日，在天安门前的大广场上，
    举行中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立典礼，
    毛主席在天安门上当着三十万的群众，
    宣告中华人民共和国成立，中央人民政府成立，
    全场欢声雷动，高呼毛主席万岁的声音震撼天地。
    那时候我看见了你，看见你笑了。
　
    鲁迅先生，你是永远不会离开我们的，
    我差不多随时随地都看见了你，看见你在笑。
    我相信这决不是我一个人的幻想，
    而是千千万万人民大众的实感。
    我仿佛听见你在说：“我们应该笑了，
    在毛主席的领导之下，应该用全生命来，
    保障着我们的笑，笑到大同世界的出现。”
    　　　　　　　　　　　　（十月十七日）
