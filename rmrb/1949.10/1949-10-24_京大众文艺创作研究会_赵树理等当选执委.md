Title: Beijing University Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association: Zhao Shuli and Others Elected as Executive Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 4

Beijing News: The inaugural meeting of the Beijing Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association, which lasted one day on the 15th, continued yesterday morning (23rd) at the Taimiao Temple, with over ninety members present. The meeting first passed the association's charter. Zhao Shuli, on behalf of the presidium, explained the draft charter article by article, and everyone offered suggestions for revisions and supplements, which were ultimately passed unanimously. Subsequently, elections were held, and Zhao Shuli, Wang Yaping, Miao Peishi, Xin Daming, Wang Xiezhu, and fifteen others were elected as executive committee members. The chairman and vice-chairman of the executive committee will be elected by the executive committee members at a meeting.

### 京大众文艺创作研究会　赵树理等当选执委

1949-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　京大众文艺创作研究会
  　赵树理等当选执委
    【北京讯】京市大众文艺创作研究会成立大会，于十五日进行一天后，昨（二十三）日上午在太庙继续开会，到有会员九十余人。大会首先通过会章。由主席团赵树理对会章草案逐条加以解释，大家又堤供了修改及补充的意见，最后经一致通过。接着进行选学，共选出赵树理、王亚平、苗培时、辛大明、王颉竹等十五人为执行委员。执行委员会主席及副主席将由执行委员开会互选。
