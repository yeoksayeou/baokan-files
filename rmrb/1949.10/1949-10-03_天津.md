Title: Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3

　　Tianjin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 2nd] People from all walks of life in Tianjin warmly support the Central People's Government headed by Chairman Mao, elected by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Last night at 7 o'clock, when Xinhua Radio broadcast the good news, the people of the city were very excited. The People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Tianjin immediately dispatched a propaganda team of more than a thousand people and a propaganda truck team to spread the good news to the general public. The cheers of "Long live Chairman Mao" and the sounds of Yangko dances and gongs and drums resounded through the night sky. Today, the whole city is filled with red flags and colorful signs, and the sounds of gongs, drums, and cheers reverberate through the clouds. The parade continued throughout the day.

### 天津

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　天津
    【新华社天津二日电】天津市各界人民热烈拥护人民政治协商会议所选出的以毛主席为首的中央人民政府。昨夜七时，当新华广播电台把这个好消息播出时，全市人民都非常兴奋。人民解放军驻津各部队立即出动了千多人的宣传大队和宣传卡车队，把这个好消息传播给广大市民。“毛主席万岁”的欢呼声和秧歌、锣鼓响闹声激荡着夜空。今日全市红旗招展，彩牌林立，锣鼓与欢呼声响澈云霄，游行的行列竟日不绝。
