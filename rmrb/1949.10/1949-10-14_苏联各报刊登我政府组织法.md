Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Our Government Organization Law
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: TASS, Moscow: The Pravda, Izvestia, Red Star and other newspapers all published on the 12th the Organization Law of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and news about the meeting of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 苏联各报刊登我政府组织法

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报刊登我政府组织法
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报、消息报、红星报及其他报纸，十二日均刊登中华人民共和国中央人民政府组织法和关于中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会会议的消息。
