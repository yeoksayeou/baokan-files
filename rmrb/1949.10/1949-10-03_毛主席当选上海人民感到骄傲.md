Title: Chairman Mao's Election Makes the People of Shanghai Proud
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3

　　【Shanghai Dispatch from Our Reporter on the 2nd】After the news of the election of the people's leader Mao Zedong as Chairman of the Central People's Government and the members of the Central People's Government spread, all circles in Shanghai were overjoyed. All officers and soldiers of the Third Field Army stationed in Shanghai expressed their determination to support Chairman Mao's election with practical actions. Workers from all industries and people from all walks of life in the city cheered for finding their true leader. Railway worker Chen Xingsheng excitedly said that we are proud to have the wise and great people's leader Chairman Mao, and we cheer for the Chinese people to have Chairman Mao's leadership. Responsible persons of the industrial community Liu Jingji, the Higher Education Joint Association, the Elementary School Teachers Joint Association, and the Democratic Women's Federation also stated that Chairman Mao's election has increased our infinite confidence in the victory of future New Democratic construction. The Liberation Daily, Xinwen Daily, Ta Kung Pao, Wen Wei Po and other newspapers here have published editorials, expressing warm congratulations and heartfelt support.

### 毛主席当选上海人民感到骄傲

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　毛主席当选上海人民感到骄傲
    【本报记者上海二日专电】人民领袖毛泽东当选中央人民政府主席及中央人民政府委员选出的消息传出后，上海各界欢欣若狂，第三野战军驻沪各部全体指战员表示要用实际行动来拥护毛主席当选。全市各业职工及各界人士一致为找到了自己真正的领袖而欢呼。铁路工人陈杏生兴奋地说我们有了英明的伟大的人民领袖毛主席而感到骄傲，我们为中国人民有了毛主席的领导而欢呼。产业界刘靖基、高教联、小学教联及民主妇联等负责人，亦均表示毛主席的当选使我们对于今后新民主主义的建设胜利，增加了无限的信心。此间解放日报、新闻日报、大公报、文汇报等都发表社论，表示热烈的祝贺和衷心的拥护。
