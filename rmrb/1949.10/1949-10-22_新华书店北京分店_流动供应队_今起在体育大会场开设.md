Title: Xinhua Bookstore Beijing Branch Mobile Supply Team Opens at Sports Meet Venue Today
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua Bookstore Beijing Branch Mobile Supply Team Opens at Sports Meet Venue Today
    [Our Newspaper's News] The Beijing Branch of Xinhua Bookstore will establish a "Mobile Supply Team" at the venue of the Capital People's Sports Meet starting today. The bookstore has now prepared a large number of books, magazines, and juvenile publications for the people attending the meeting to choose from. The supply team is located outside the East Gate of the Xiannongtan venue.

### 新华书店北京分店　流动供应队　今起在体育大会场开设

1949-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华书店北京分店　流动供应队　今起在体育大会场开设
    【本报讯】新华书店北京分店，今日起在首都人民体育大会会场设立“流动供应队”。该店现在准备了大批的书籍杂志和青少年读物，供到会的人们选购。该供应队设在先农坛会场的东门外。
