Title: Declaration of the China Conference for the Defense of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: The full text of the Declaration of the China Conference for the Defense of World Peace is as follows:

　　The China Peace Conference solemnly declares to the world that the Chinese people resolutely oppose wars of aggression and support world peace.

　　The China Peace Conference points out: The US-British imperialist bloc is attempting to escape its inevitable doom by launching new wars of aggression. They are frantically expanding armaments, expanding their network of military bases, organizing aggressive military alliances—the North Atlantic Treaty, and plotting the equally aggressive so-called "Pacific Alliance." They are supporting the remnants of German and Japanese fascism in order to use them as pawns to re-attack the Soviet Union, China, and other countries. All this proves that the danger of a new war of aggression exists.

　　But we believe that this danger is entirely surmountable. The insidious ferocity of imperialism precisely exposes its fragility. The world situation in 1949 is very different from that in 1939 and 1914. Today, the world democratic peace camp, headed by the Soviet Union, is unprecedentedly strong, and its strength has long surpassed that of the imperialist bloc. The brilliant achievements of Soviet construction, the continued consolidation and growth of the new democratic countries, the surging peace movements in all countries around the world, the great victory of the Chinese people and the founding of the People's Republic of China, and the national liberation struggles of the peoples of all colonies and semi-colonies have greatly strengthened and consolidated the position of world democracy and peace. Recently, the Soviet Union declared that it already possesses atomic weapons, but it still insists on the proposal to destroy all atomic weapons and to use atomic energy for production. This statement is a serious blow to the instigators of atomic war. From this, it can be seen that as long as the people of the world continue to maintain vigilance, strengthen unity, and persist in struggle, then we will certainly have sufficient power to shatter all the evil conspiracies of the warmongers.

　　We Chinese people have always stood on the side of the democratic peace camp, and the victory of the Chinese people's revolution is the victory of the world democratic peace camp. We are willing to work with our great ally the Soviet Union, with the new democratic countries, and with the people of all other countries to fight for the independence and autonomy of all nations and for lasting world peace. Therefore, we have decided to convene this Conference for the Defense of World Peace in Beijing with the participation of representatives from all walks of life across the country, and to hold large-scale rallies and demonstrations throughout the country to express the Chinese people's firm determination and great strength in opposing new wars of aggression and supporting world

### 中国保卫世界和平大会宣言

1949-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国保卫世界和平大会宣言
    【新华社北京三日电】中国保卫世界和平大会宣言全文如下：
    中国和平大会向全世界庄严地宣告中国人民坚决反对侵略战争，拥护世界和平。
    中国和平大会指出：美英帝国主义集团正在企图用发动新的侵略战争的方法，来逃脱其自身必然要覆灭的命运。它们疯狂地扩充军备，扩充军事基地网，组织侵略性的军事同盟——北大西洋公约，策动有同样侵略性的所谓“太平洋联盟”。他们扶植德日法西斯残余势力，以便利用它们作爪牙，重新进攻苏联、中国和其他国家。这一切都证明：新的侵略战争的危险是存在着。
    但我们认为，这种危险是完全可以克服的。帝国主义的阴险凶恶恰正暴露它的脆弱。一九四九年的世界情形，已与一九三九年和一九一四年大不相同了。今天以苏联为首的世界民主和平阵营已空前强大，它的力量早已超过了帝国主义集团的力量。苏联建设的光辉成就，各新民主国家的继续巩固与壮大，全世界各国汹涌澎湃的和平运动，中国人民的伟大胜利和中华人民共和国的成立，各殖民地半殖民地人民的民族解放斗争，都大大地加强和巩固了世界民主和平的阵地。最近苏联声明：它早已有了原子武器，但是它仍然坚持消毁一切原子武器的建议，并将原子能用于生产。这一声明对于原子战争的鼓动者是一个严重的打击。由此可知，全世界人民只要继续保持警惕，加强团结，坚持斗争，那么，我们一定有充分足够的力量，粉碎战争贩子们的一切罪恶阴谋。
    我们中国人民历来就站在民主和平阵营一方面，中国人民革命的胜利，就是世界民主和平阵营的胜利。我们愿意和伟大的盟友苏联一道，和各新民主国家一道，和其他一切国家的人民一道，为保卫各民族的独立自主，争取世界的持久和平而斗争。因此，我们决定在北京召开这个有全国各界代表人物参加的保卫世界和平大会，并在全国各地举行广大的集会和示威游行，来表达中国人民反对新的侵略战争，拥护世界和平的坚强决心和伟大力量。让我们的敌人在我们强大的和平进军面前发抖吧！人民的力量是不可战胜的，胜利是一定属于人民的！
    反对美英帝国主义制造新侵略战争的阴谋！
    反对美英帝国主义的侵略工具“北大西洋公约”和策动中的“太平洋联盟”！
    反对美英帝国主义扶植日本法西斯重新侵略中国！
    立即召开有中华人民共和国参加的对日和约会议！
    联合世界上以平等待我之国家、民族和人民，为保卫各民族的独立自主，加强国际友谊合作而斗争！
    全世界人民团结起来，反对新的侵略战争，争取世界持久和平！
    远东和平的强大堡垒中华人民共和国万岁！
    世界和平民主阵营的领导者苏联万岁！
    世界拥护和平大会万岁！
    人民民主与持久和平万岁！
