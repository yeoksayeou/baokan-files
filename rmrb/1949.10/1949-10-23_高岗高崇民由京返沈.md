Title: Gao Gang and Gao Chongmin Return to Shenyang from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 1

　　Gao Gang and Gao Chongmin
　　Return to Shenyang from Beijing
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 22nd] Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and Gao Chongmin, Member of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, returned to Shenyang by train from Beijing on the afternoon of the 21st. Li Fuchun, Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang, and dozens of responsible persons from various ministries and commissions of the Northeast People's Government went to the station to welcome them. Wang Jiaxiang, China's first ambassador to the Soviet Union, arrived in Shenyang on the same train from Beijing. After Ambassador Wang rested briefly after getting off the train, the special train left Shenyang at 10 p.m. for the Soviet Union.

### 高岗高崇民由京返沈

1949-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　高岗高崇民
  　由京返沈
    【新华社沈阳二十二日电】中央人民政府副主席兼东北人民政府主席高岗、中央人民政府委员兼东北人民政府副主席高崇民，于二十一日下午自京乘车返抵沈阳。前往车站欢迎的有中共中央东北局副书记李富春、东北人民政府副主席林枫、沈阳市市长朱其文和东北人民政府各部、会负责人数十人。同车由京来沈的有我国首任驻苏大使王稼祥。王大使下车稍事休息后，当晚十时专车离沈赴苏。
