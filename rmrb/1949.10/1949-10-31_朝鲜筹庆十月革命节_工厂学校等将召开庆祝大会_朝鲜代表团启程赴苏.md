Title: Korea Prepares to Celebrate the October Revolution; Factories, Schools, etc., to Hold Celebration Meetings; Korean Delegation Departs for Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th -- The Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front and the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association are actively preparing to welcome the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution. From November 4th to 8th, celebration meetings will be held in factories, mines, government offices, rural areas, and schools in the capital, Pyongyang, and the provincial capitals. Lectures will be given on topics such as "The International Significance of the October Revolution," "The Soviet Union is a Strong Supporter of the Independence and Autonomy of Small and Weak Nations," and "Opposition to the Illegal Resolution of the United Nations on the Korean Question." The monthly magazine "Korean Friendship and Korean-Soviet Culture" will publish a special commemorative issue. Cinemas will show films about the deeds of Lenin, and an October Revolution photo exhibition will be held.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th -- The Korean delegation participating in the grand celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the Great October Revolution in the Soviet Union departed from Pyongyang at 1:00 PM on the 28th, flying to Moscow. The delegation is composed of six people, including Choe Chang-ik, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of North Korea and Minister of Finance of the Cabinet, and writer Ri Tae-jun. Ri Dong-yeong, Chairman of the North Korean Red Cross Society, also traveled on the same plane at the special invitation of the Soviet Red Cross Society.

### 朝鲜筹庆十月革命节  工厂学校等将召开庆祝大会  朝鲜代表团启程赴苏

1949-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜筹庆十月革命节　
    工厂学校等将召开庆祝大会            
    朝鲜代表团启程赴苏
    【新华社平壤三十日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线和朝苏文化协会，正在积极筹备迎接十月革命三十二周年。从十一月四日到八日，将在共和国首都平壤和各省会的工厂、矿山、机关、农村、学校中，分别召开庆祝大会，并以“十月革命的国际意义”、“苏联是弱小民族独立自主的坚强支持者”、“反对联合国对朝鲜问题的非法决定”等题目，举行讲演会。“朝鲜友好和朝苏文化”月刊，将出纪念特辑，各电影院将专门放演有关列宁事迹的影片，并将举办十月革命照片展览会。
    【新华社平壤三十日电】参加苏联伟大十月革命三十二周年纪念盛典的朝鲜代表团，已于二十八日午后一时自平坏乘机飞赴莫斯科。代表团由北朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、内阁财政部长崔昌益、作家李泰俊等六人组成。北朝鲜红十字会主席李东英亦应苏联红十字会的特别邀请，同机前往。
