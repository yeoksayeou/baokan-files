Title: Parity Rate of Commodity Unit Savings Today
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 5

　　Commodity Unit Savings Parity Rate Today
    The Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces that today's (the 4th) standard commodity unit parity rate for commodity unit savings is 757.35 yuan.
    Furthermore, the parity rate on the 2nd was 757.85, and the parity rate on the 3rd was 757.35.

### 折实储蓄今日牌价

1949-10-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　折实储蓄今日牌价
    人民银行北京分行储蓄部折实储蓄今（四）日标准实物单位牌价为七五七·三五元。
    又二日牌价为七五七·八五，三日牌价为七五七·三五。
