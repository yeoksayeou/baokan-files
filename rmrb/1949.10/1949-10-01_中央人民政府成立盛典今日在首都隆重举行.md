Title: Grand Ceremony for the Establishment of the Central People's Government Held in the Capital Today

Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 1

[Report from this newspaper] The Central People's Government Establishment Ceremony of the People's Republic of China will be held today, October 1, 1949, at Tiananmen Square, with the participation of the members of the Central People's Government Committee and the delegates of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Hundreds of thousands of people, including workers, students, and citizens from various organizations and groups in Beijing, will attend the grand ceremony. The program for the ceremony is as follows:

　　　　Ceremony Program
    One. The Secretary-General of the Central People's Government announces the opening of the meeting.
    Two. The Chairman of the Central People's Government takes his place, the Vice-Chairmen take their places, and the members take their places.
    Three. Play "The March of the Volunteers."
    Four. The Chairman of the Central People's Government announces the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and raises the national flag (while firing a salute. After the salute, play "The March of the Volunteers").
    Five. The Chairman of the Central People's Government reads the Central People's Government Announcement.
    Six. Military Parade (1. The Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army inspects, 2. The Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issues the parade order, 3. Divisional parade).
    Seven. Parade.

### 中央人民政府成立盛典今日在首都隆重举行

1949-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府成立盛典今日在首都隆重举行
    【本报讯】一九四九年十月一日（今日）中央人民政府委员会暨中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体代表齐集天安门，参加中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立典礼。北京各机关团体工人学生市民数十万人将参加盛典。据悉，大会典礼程序如下：
    　　　　大会程序
    一、中央人民政府秘书长宣布开会。
    二、中央人民政府主席就位，副主席就位，委员就位。
    三、奏义勇军进行曲。
    四、中央人民政府主席宣布中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立，并升国旗（同时鸣礼炮，礼炮毕，奏义勇军进行曲）。
    五、中央人民政府主席宣读中央人民政府公告。
    六、阅兵（１、中国人民解放军总司令检阅，２、中国人民解放军总司令下达阅兵命令，３、进行分列式）。
    七、游行。
