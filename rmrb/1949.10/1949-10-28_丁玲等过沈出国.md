Title: Ding Ling and Others Pass Through Shenyang En Route Abroad
Author:
Date: 1949-10-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 27th: The Chinese delegation, including Ding Ling, consisting of fifteen people, participating in the celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the October Revolution in the Soviet Union, arrived in Shenyang from Beijing at 4:20 PM on the 27th. Representatives from Shenyang's cultural circles gathered at the station to welcome them. After a short rest, the delegation continued their journey abroad by train at 7:00 PM.

### 丁玲等过沈出国

1949-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　丁玲等过沈出国
    【新华社沈阳二十七日电】参加苏联十月革命三十二周年纪念典礼的中国代表团丁玲等一行十五人，于二十七日下午四时二十分由京抵沈。沈阳文化界代表齐至车站欢迎。代表团稍憩后，即于七时续乘车出国。
