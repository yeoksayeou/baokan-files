Title: Welcoming the October Revolution Day: Workers in Leningrad Enthusiastically Engage in Production Competition
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3

　　Welcoming the October Revolution Day
  　Leningrad Workers Enthusiastically Engage in Production Competition
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th. TASS reports: On the eve of the October Revolution Day, tens of thousands of workers in various industries in Leningrad are enthusiastically engaged in socialist competition. The vast majority of workers in the city will complete the Five-Year Plan by the end of the year, and many are now exceeding the Five-Year Plan in production. The famous toolmaker Alexei Zhandrikov has completed three times the quota of the Five-Year Plan and has won first place in the competition of 50,000 toolmakers in Leningrad for nine consecutive times. This Stakhanovite guarantees to complete his sixteenth annual quota before the thirty-second anniversary of the October Revolution (that is, he has completed sixteen years of quota in the first four years of the post-war Five-Year Plan). Thousands of workers on the Vyborg riverside, the main industrial area of Leningrad, have completed the Five-Year Plan. At the Stalin factory, more than 300 Stakhanovites have each completed their sixth or seventh annual quota. The personnel of this factory. average 140 percent of the quota. In another famous Leningrad enterprise, the "Electricity" factory, hundreds of workers have also completed their Five-Year Plan. The first place in the socialist competition is the production model Alexander Plis. Since the beginning of the Five-Year Plan, he has completed the fifteenth annual production quota. Another production model in this enterprise, Alexei Kozlov (who is also a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union), is now about to complete his thirteenth annual production quota.

### 迎接十月革命节　列城工人热烈进行生产竞赛

1949-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　迎接十月革命节
  　列城工人热烈进行生产竞赛
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社讯：在十月革命节的前夕，列宁格勒成千成万的各业工人，正热烈进行社会主义竞赛，该城绝大多数工人均将在年底以前完成五年计划，其中许多人现正超过五年计划进行生产。著名的工具制造工人阿历赛·詹德里科夫已完成三倍于五年计划的定额，并在列宁格勒城五万工具制造工人的竞赛中连续九次获得第一名。这个斯塔哈诺夫运动者保证在十月革命第三十二周年纪念日以前完成他的第十六个全年定额（按即在战后五年计划的前四年中完成了十六年的定额）。列城主要工业区维堡河畔数以千计的工人，已完成了五年计划。在斯大林工厂，三百多斯塔哈诺夫运动者每人都完成了第六个或第七个全年定额。这个工厂的人员。平均完成定额的百分之一四○。在另一个著名的列宁格勒的企业“电力”工厂中，也有数以百计的工人已经完成了他们的五年计划。在社会主义竞赛中占第一位的是生产模范亚历山大·普利斯。他自从五年计划开始以来，已经完成了第十五个全年生产定额。在此企业中的另一个生产模范亚历赛·科兹洛夫（他同时也是苏联最高苏维埃的代表），现在正要完成他的第十三个全年定额的生产。
