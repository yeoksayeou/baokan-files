Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Departs Beijing for the Soviet Union; Liu Shaoqi and Over 100 Others See Them Off at the Station; Guo Moruo Delivers Farewell Address; Simonov Delivers Reply

Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th: The Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation, including Dubrovina, Stoletov, and Gerasimov, departed Beijing for the Soviet Union by special train at 11:00 AM today. The head of the delegation, Simonov, will remain in Beijing for a short while longer. Representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of China, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government all went to the station to see them off. These included Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Vice Presidents Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Zhang Lan, and Huang Yanpei, Directors Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Ma Xulun, Shen Yanbing, Li Lisan, Cai Chang, Zhang Bojun, Teng Daiyuan, Li Dequan, Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Qian Junrui, Zhou Yang, Feng Wenbin, Xie Bangding, Shao Lizi, Zhang Zhizhong, and others; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Secretary-General Liu Ningyi, Committee members Yuan Muzhi, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Qingyang, Cao Mengjun, Xu Deheng, Li Da, Yu Xinqing, Zhou Xinmin, Zhou Jianren, Tang Yongtong, Hu Yuzhi, and others; as well as Li Kenong, representative of Premier Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director, and over 100 others.

　　After entering the station amidst applause and military music, the delegation warmly shook hands with the well-wishers one by one. Guo Moruo then delivered a farewell speech on behalf of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, followed by a reply speech from the head of the delegation, Simonov.

　　In his farewell speech, Guo Moruo said: "This time, the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers came to China and participated in the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese People's Congress for World Peace, and the founding congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, giving the Chinese people tremendous encouragement. The Chinese people hope that the Soviet delegation can come to China again soon."

　　In his reply speech, Simonov said: "We will forever remember the deep friendship of the Chinese people towards us. We regard the Chinese people's love for us as love for the entire great Soviet people. After returning to our country, we will tell all the people of the Soviet Union everything we have seen in China. We thank the people of all walks of life in China, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and even more deeply, Chairman Mao Zedong."

　　After the speeches, the delegation and the well-wishers enthusiastically shouted slogans such as "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship," "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong," and "Long live Marshal Stalin." The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association then presented a flag and flowers to the Soviet delegation. The flag was inscribed with: "Long live the eternal friendship and cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union." When the flag and flowers were presented, the military band played the Chinese and Soviet national anthems. After the presentation of flowers, the delegation once again warmly shook hands with the well-wishers to say goodbye, and then boarded the train. At 11:10 AM, the delegation's special train left the capital station amidst enthusiastic waving and cheering. (Attached picture)

### 苏文化代表团离京返苏  刘少奇等百余人到车站欢送  郭沫若致欢送词·西蒙诺夫致答词

1949-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏文化代表团离京返苏　
    刘少奇等百余人到车站欢送           
    郭沫若致欢送词·西蒙诺夫致答词
    【新华社北京二十九日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团杜伯洛维娜、斯托列托夫、格拉西莫夫等一行已于今日上午十一时乘专车离京返苏。代表团团长西蒙诺夫尚需在京稍作勾留。我国中苏友好协会、中国保卫世界和平委员会、中央人民政府外交部代表均到车站欢送，计有中苏友好协会会长刘少奇，副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒、张澜、黄炎培，理事董必武、陈云、马叙伦、沈雁冰、李立三、蔡畅、章伯钧、滕代远、李德全、张奚若、彭泽民、钱俊瑞、周扬、冯文彬、谢邦定、邵力子、张治中等，中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，秘书长刘宁一，委员袁牧之、廖承志、刘清扬、曹孟君、许德珩、李达、余心清、周新民、周建人、汤用彤、胡愈之等，及外交部部长周恩来代表李克农、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航等百余人。代表团在掌声和军乐声中进入车站后与欢送者一一热烈握手，接着由郭沫若代表中苏友好协会和中国保卫世界和平委员会致欢送词，并由西蒙诺夫团长致答词。郭沫若在欢送词中说：此次苏联文化艺术科学工作者到中国来，参加了中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立典礼和中国保卫世界和平大会、中苏友好协会成立大会，给了中国人民以极大的鼓励。中国人民希望苏联代表团能够很快地再到中国来。西蒙诺夫在致答词中说：我们将永远记着中国人民对我们的深厚友谊。我们把中国人民对于我们的热爱看做对于整个伟大苏维埃国家人民的热爱。我们回国以后将把在中国所看到的一切告诉给全苏联的人民。我们感谢中国的各界人民，感谢中华人民共和国中央人民政府，更深深地感谢毛泽东主席。致词后代表团与欢送者均热烈高呼：“中苏友好万岁”、“毛泽东主席万岁”、“斯大林大元帅万岁”等口号。中苏友好协会总会接着向苏联代表团献旗和献花，旗上写着：“中苏两国人民永远不朽的友谊合作万岁”。献旗献花时军乐队奏中国和苏联国歌。献花毕代表团再一次和欢送者热烈握手告别，旋即登车。十一时十分代表团专车在热情的招手和欢呼中开离首都车站。（附图片）
