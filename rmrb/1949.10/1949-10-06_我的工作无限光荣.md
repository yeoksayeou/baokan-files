Title: My Work is Infinitely Glorious
Author: Duō Ráng
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 6

My Work is Infinitely Glorious
Worker of Jiamusi Engineering Section, Duo Rang
　　I am a track patrolman,
　　My assistant is a flag lamp;
　　Waving the flag to meet and send off the train in the day,
　　Using colored lights for track patrol at night.
　　I have weapons: track hammer, track ruler, screw
　　driver,
　　Shovels, torches, and signals are my
　　soldiers;
　　Preventing spies, special agents and accidents,
　　Paying attention to steel rails and rail spikes;
　　If there are obstacles on the track gauge line,
　　My spirit is even more concentrated;
　　Walking on bridges and culverts,
　　I see clearly where there is construction;
　　Patrolling bends, road crossings, and mountain
　　passes,
　　Scrutinizing crawling spots with my eyes;
　　Front, back, left, right, lower side,
　　I worry and check everything clearly.

　　In spring, ice and snow melt,
　　Weeds grow in heaps;
　　Track conditions are prone to change,
　　I complete the flood prevention work first;
　　Besides clearing weeds, clean the side ditches.
　　In summer, the "scorching season,"
　　Sweat drips all over my body,
　　Preventing steel rails from expanding due to heat,
　　Not afraid of the sun, mosquito bites,
　　I still patrol back and forth.
　　In autumn, there is a lot of rain,
　　I fight against water damage;
　　Heavy rain soaks through my back,
　　Flood drowns my lower body,
　　I never stop the rescue work for a moment.
　　In winter, heavy snow falls,
　　Not afraid of the freezing cold,
　　Braving the north wind to sweep snow,
　　Eliminate frost damage from happening;
　　My task is completed.

　　I am a track patrolman,
　　I am responsible for the people's lives and property,
　　Strengthening patrols to patrol the tracks,
　　Eliminating accidents and preventing enemies,
　　Ensuring the safe operation of trains;
　　I am a track patrolman,
　　I am a track patrolman;
　　My task is very important,
　　My work is infinitely glorious!

### 我的工作无限光荣
多勷
1949-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　我的工作无限光荣
    佳木斯工务段工人　多勷
    我是一个溜道工，
    我的助手是旗灯；
    白天摇旗接送车，
    夜间溜道用色灯。
    我有武器道锤、道尺、螺
    丝把，
    ＠炖、炬火、信号是我的
    兵；
    防奸防特防事故，
    注意钢轨和道钉；
    轨距线路上有障碍，
    我的精神更集中；
    走在桥梁、涵洞上，
    有建筑的地方我看清；
    巡到弯道、马道口、跟山
    口，
    有爬行地点用目细眝盯；
    前后左右下侧面，
    我操心查分明。
　
    春季冰雪化，
    乱草堆堆生；
    线路情况容易变化，
    我把防汛工作先完成；
    除了杂草侧沟清一清。
    夏季“炎炎节”，
    身上汗直淋，
    防备钢轨热涨病，
    不怕太阳晒，蚊虫咬，
    我同样来回巡。
    秋季雨水多，
    我和水害作斗争；
    大雨淋透背脊心，
    洪水淹住下半身，
    抢救工作我一刻也不停。
    冬季大雪纷纷下，
    不怕天寒地冻冰，
    冒着北风来扫雪，
    冻害消灭不发生；
    我的任务来完成。
　
    我是一个溜道工，
    人民的生命财产由我负责
    任，
    加强巡回来溜道，
    消灭事故防敌人，
    保证列车运转得安宁；
    我是一个溜道工，
    我是一个溜道工；
    我的任务非常重大，
    我的工作无限光荣！
