Title: Polish Steel Production Completes Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 3

　　Polish Steel Production
    Completes Plan Ahead of Schedule
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: Warsaw dispatch from the Polish News Agency: Polish steel production completed its three-year plan ahead of schedule on October 7th.
