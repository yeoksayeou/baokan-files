Title: Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 2nd. The happy news of the founding of the Central People's Government and the election of Chairman Mao Zedong filled this famous city of Jiangnan with joy. The sacred national flag with red ground and yellow stars flew over the whole city. Crowds were packed around all the radios. The People's Liberation Army guarding Hangzhou was especially excited. The soldiers competed to spread the good news, brushed their hats, washed their clothes, and changed into new clothes to prepare to participate in the parade. The people of the whole city smiled and expressed their congratulations together. Representatives of various organizations, such as the Preparatory Committee of the Hangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Democratic Youth Federation, the Student Federation, the Hangzhou Branch of the Science Workers Association, and the Hangzhou Educational Workers Association, also made speeches, warmly celebrating the election of Chairman Mao Zedong and the establishment of the Central People's Government.

### 杭州

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　杭州
    【新华社杭州二日电】中央人民政府成立和毛泽东主席当选的喜讯传来，使这江南的名城欣喜若狂。红地黄星的神圣国旗，飘扬全市。所有播音机的旁边都拥满着人群。警备杭州的人民解放军，尤其兴奋万状。战士们争相传告喜讯，刷帽洗衣，换穿新装，准备参加游行。全市人民笑容满面，共申庆祝。杭州市总工会筹委会、民主青年联合会、学生联合会、科学工作协会杭州分会、杭州市教育工作者协会等各团体的代表，更纷纷发表谈话，热烈地庆祝毛泽东主席的当选和中央人民政府的成立。
