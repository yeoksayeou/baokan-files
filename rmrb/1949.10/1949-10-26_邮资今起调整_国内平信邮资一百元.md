Title: Postal Rates Adjusted Today; Domestic Regular Mail Postage is One Hundred Yuan
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 4

　　Postal Rates Adjusted Today
　　Domestic Regular Mail Postage is One Hundred Yuan
　　[This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Beijing Postal Administration recently received an order from the North China Postal Administration to adjust domestic and international postal rates, effective today (October 26th). Details are as follows:
　　(A) Domestic Postal Rates: (1) General Rates: Letters, per 20 grams, 50 yuan for local, and 100 yuan for inter-provincial; Postcards, 30 yuan for local, and 50 yuan for inter-provincial. (2) Special Rates: Registered mail, add 300 yuan to the ordinary rate; Double registered mail, add 600 yuan; Priority mail, add 100 yuan; Express registered mail, add 400 yuan; Return receipt fee, 300 yuan per item; Inquiry fee, 400 yuan per inquiry.
　　(B) International Postal Rates: (1) General Rates: Letters, per 20 grams, 500 yuan; Postcards, 250 yuan. (2) Special Rates: Registered mail, add 4,000 yuan to the ordinary rate; Double registered mail, add 6,500 yuan; Priority mail, add 1,500 yuan; Express registered mail, add 5,000 yuan; Return receipt fee, 2,500 yuan per item; Inquiry fee, 5,000 yuan per inquiry.
　　(Beijing Postal Administration Public Service Group)

### 邮资今起调整  国内平信邮资一百元

1949-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　邮资今起调整　
    国内平信邮资一百元
    【本报讯】北京邮政管理局顷奉华北邮政总局命令，自今日（二十六日）起，调整国内及国际邮资，详情如下：
    （甲）国内邮资：（一）一般资费：信函每重二十公分，本埠者五十元，国内互寄者一百元；明信片本埠者三十元，国内互寄者五十元。（二）特种资费：单挂号除普通资费外另加三百元；双挂号另加六百元；平快信另加一百元；快递挂号另加四百元；回执费每件三百元；查询费每次四百元。
    （乙）国际邮资：（一）一般资费：信函每重二十公分五百元；明信片二百五十元。（二）特种资费：单挂号除普通资费外另加四千元；双挂号另加六千五百元；平快费另加一千五百元；快递挂号另加五千元；回执每件二千五百元；查询费每次五千元。
    （北京邮政管理局公众服务组）
