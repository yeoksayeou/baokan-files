Title: Czechoslovakia Celebrates Independence Day; Most Enterprises Complete Production Plans; Torch Relay Race to Start from Czechoslovak-Soviet Border
Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague: TASS reports that Czechoslovakia celebrated the 31st anniversary of the founding of the independent Czechoslovak Republic nationwide on the 28th. The slogan of the Czechoslovak working people celebrating the 31st anniversary of their country's independence is "Without November 7, 1917, there would be no October 28, 1918!" The high tide of labor and political enthusiasm is a characteristic of commemorating this festival. Hundreds of enterprises in the Republic have already completed their 1949 production plans. Celebrations were held in various factories and workshops on the 28th. On the 30th, army units, security forces, and worker units will be reviewed in Prague and major cities across the country. A long-distance cross-country torch relay race will start from Prague, carrying the Czechoslovak people's congratulations to the Soviet people to the Czechoslovak-Soviet border, as a celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 29th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Park Heon-yeong, Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent telegrams to Czech President Gottwald, Premier Zapotocky, and Foreign Minister Clementis on October 28th, respectively, to congratulate the Czech Independence Day. The telegrams unanimously pointed out that the Czech people and the people of other people's democratic countries in Eastern Europe were liberated from the iron hooves of the Hitler bandits due to the heroic fighting of the great Soviet people, and have achieved great achievements in the cause of socialist construction. The telegrams unanimously congratulated the Czech people for their greater victories in the future.

### 捷克欢庆独立节日  多数企业完成生产计划  火炬接力赛跑将从捷京达苏边境

1949-10-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷克欢庆独立节日　
    多数企业完成生产计划            
    火炬接力赛跑将从捷京达苏边境
    【新华社北京二十九日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷全国于二十八日庆祝独立的捷克斯洛伐克共和国成立三十一周年纪念。捷克斯洛伐克的劳动人民庆祝祖国独立三十一周年的口号是“没有一九一七年十一月七日，就没有一九一八年十月二十八日！”劳动和政治热情的高涨，是纪念这个节日的特色。共和国数以百计的企业，已经完成了他们一九四九年的生产计划。各工厂和作坊二十八日举行庆祝会。三十日，陆军部队、保安部队、工人部队将在布拉格及全国各大城市受检阅。长途越野火炬接力赛跑，将从布拉格出发，这个接力赛跑将把捷克斯洛伐克人民对苏联人民的贺意，送到捷苏边境，作为对伟大的十月社会主义革命的三十二周年的祝贺。
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁总理金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金＠凤，外交部长朴宪永，于十月二十八日分别致电捷克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德、总理萨波托斯基、外交部长克利门蒂斯，祝贺捷克独立纪念日。贺电一致指出：捷克人民和东欧其他人民民主国家的人民，由于伟大苏联人民的英勇作战，从希特勒匪帮的铁蹄之下解放出来，并已在社会主义建设事业上获得了巨大的成就，贺电一致祝贺捷克人民今后的更大胜利。
