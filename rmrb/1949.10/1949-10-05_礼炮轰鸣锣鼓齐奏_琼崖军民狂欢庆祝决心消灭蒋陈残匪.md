Title: Cannons Roar, Drums Beat: Qiongya Military and Civilians Celebrate and Resolve to Eliminate Chiang Kai-shek and Chen Jitang Remnants

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Qiongya, 4th. The Party, government, military, and people's organizations in the Qiongya Liberated Area, where the Qiongya People's Government is located, celebrated the success of the People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China with immense joy. When the CPPCC opened, various agencies and troops established preparatory committees to prepare for the celebration. Everyone excitedly said, "This is the real National Day, and we must revel to our hearts' content." At daybreak on October 1st, twelve cannons boomed deafeningly, drums sounded in unison, and cheers erupted. At exactly 6:00 AM, various agencies and troops gathered in the large square of the CCP Qiongya District Committee to hold a solemn flag-raising ceremony. Amidst the melodious military music, the first national flag of the People's Republic of China was raised solemnly on the land of Qiongya. People were excited and moved, and the slogans "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live the Central People's Government!" and "Long live Chairman Mao!" resounded through the sky. At 8:00 AM, a celebration rally of several thousand people began. First, Li Yingmin, Minister of Propaganda of the CCP Qiongya District Committee, reported on the opening of the CPPCC and the list of elected personnel of the Central People's Government and the National Committee of the CPPCC. When he reported that Chairman Mao was the Chairman of the Central People's Government, it triggered thunderous applause, and the cheers of "Long live Chairman Mao!" continued for a long time. After He Jun, Chairman of the meeting, announced the opening, Chairman Feng Baiju spoke first. He pointed out that the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China would have a great impact on the liberation war in South China. It will accelerate the disintegration of the remaining Kuomintang bandits and rapidly complete the liberation of South China and even the whole country. The establishment of the Central People's Government brought infinite confidence, courage, and infinitely bright prospects to the people of Qiongya. He called on the Party, government, military, and civilians of Qiongya to thoroughly smash any attempt by the Chiang Kai-shek and Chen Jitang bandit gangs in Qiongya to struggle under the leadership of the Central People's Government, cooperate with the southward People's Liberation Army, and quickly liberate the entire Qiongya. Finally, the meeting decided to send a telegram to Chairman Mao to express their respect. The celebration ended in the majestic national anthem and slogans. At 4:00 PM, the military and civilians of the Qiongya People's Government held a two-hour-long costumed parade, and an entertainment evening party was held at night. On the second and third days, various competitions and recreational activities were held continuously to celebrate this great festival to the fullest.

### 礼炮轰鸣锣鼓齐奏　琼崖军民狂欢庆祝决心消灭蒋陈残匪

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　礼炮轰鸣锣鼓齐奏
  　琼崖军民狂欢庆祝决心消灭蒋陈残匪
    【新华社琼崖四日电】琼崖解放区人民政府所在地的党政军和人民团体，以无限欢快的心情，热烈庆祝人民政协的成功和中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立。当人民政协开幕时，各机关、部队就成立筹备会，紧张地筹备庆祝。人人兴奋地说：“这才是真正的国庆日，一定要尽情狂欢”。十月一日天色微明，十二门礼炮惊天动地的轰鸣起来，锣鼓齐奏，欢声四起。六时正，各机关部队集中在中共琼崖区党委的大广场上，举行了严肃庄重的升旗典礼。在军乐悠扬声中，琼崖土地上庄严地飘扬着第一面中华人民共和国的国旗。人们兴奋激动，“中华人民共和国万岁！”“中央人民政府万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”的口号声，响彻云霄。八时，几千人的庆祝大会开始，首由中共琼崖区党委宣传部长李英敏报告人民政协开会情形和中央人民政府及政协全国委员会当选人员名单。当他报告到毛主席为中央人民政府主席时，引起全场狂风暴雨般的掌声，“毛主席万岁！”的欢呼长久不停。大会主席何浚宣布开会后，冯白驹主席首先讲话。他指出：中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立后，对于华南解放战争影响是很大的，它将加速国民党残余匪帮土崩瓦解，迅速完成全华南以至全国的解放。中央人民政府成立，带给琼崖人民是无限的信心、勇气和无限光明的前程。他号召全琼党政军民在中央人民政府的领导下，彻底粉碎琼崖蒋、陈匪帮任何挣扎企图，配合南下大军，迅速解放全琼崖。最后，大会决定电毛主席致敬。庆祝会在雄壮的国歌和口号中结束。下午四时，琼崖人民政府所在地的军民举行了连续二小时的化装大游行，入夜并举行了娱乐晚会，第二、第三两天，连续举行各种竞赛、游艺，尽情欢度这一伟大的节日。
