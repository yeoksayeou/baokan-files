Title: Changlu Salt Sales to the South Exceed 1.9 Million Dan
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 2

　　Changlu Salt Sales to the South
    Exceed 1.9 Million Dan
    [Our Correspondent in Tianjin, 28th Dispatch] Sales of Changlu salt to the south are thriving. From February this year to date, a total of more than 1.9 million dan of salt have been sold to the Central and East China regions, accounting for 36 percent of the total salt sales of the Changlu Salt Industry Company. The sales targets are state-owned companies, and the price is 9 percent lower than the market price. The company has exchanged 110,000 burlap sacks according to its own needs.

### 长芦盐南销一百九十余万担

1949-10-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　长芦盐南销　
    一百九十余万担
    【本报记者天津二十八日专电】芦盐南销畅旺。自今年二月迄今，共销华中华东两区盐斤一百九十余万担，占长芦盐业公司销盐总数百分之三十六。销盐对象为国营公司，价格低于市价百分之九。该公司根据本身需要，换回麻袋十一万条。
