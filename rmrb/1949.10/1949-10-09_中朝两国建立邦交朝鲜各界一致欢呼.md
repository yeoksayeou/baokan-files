Title: Korean Circles Unanimously Cheer the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Korea
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. Following the announcement of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China, and the exchange of ambassadors, people from all walks of life and public opinion here have unanimously expressed warm support. Hong Ki-ju, Vice Chairman of the North Korean Democratic Party, said: "The people of Korea and China have consistently taken the same steps, whether in the struggle against imperialist aggression and reactionary rule, or in the struggle for world peace and security. In the future, the people of the two countries need to unite more closely to carry out the struggle against the aggressive policies of US imperialism. Only in this way can we consolidate and develop the victories of the people of the two countries, and make a greater contribution to the struggle of the people of Eastern countries."

The famous Korean writer Han Seol-ya expressed: He represents all Korean writers and artists in congratulating the new China. He said: "Today's China, like Korea, has become a people's country. Therefore, the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries means that new people's relations will take place between the two peoples in political, economic, and cultural fields, and the friendship between the two countries will be enhanced on this basis."

Kim Chang-jun, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front, said: "The Korean people and the peace-loving people of the world are cheering the birth of the new China and warmly support the decision of our government to establish diplomatic relations between the two governments of Korea and the People's Republic of China."

The "Democratic Korea" newspaper, the central government organ of Korea, published an editorial on the 6th entitled "The Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Korea and China Will Greatly Contribute to the Democratic Development of the Two Countries," saying: We warmly congratulate the People's Republic of China on its appearance on the Asian continent. The editorial also said: The establishment of diplomatic relations between our Republic and the newly born China will promote further close unity between the people of Korea and China. It will have a huge impact on all aspects of politics, economy, and culture between the two countries.

### 中朝两国建立邦交朝鲜各界一致欢呼

1949-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　中朝两国建立邦交朝鲜各界一致欢呼
    【新华社平壤八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与中华人民共和国建立外交关系并将交换大使的消息发表后，此间各界人士和舆论，一致表示热烈拥护。北朝鲜民主党副委员长洪箕畴说：“朝中两国人民不论在反对帝国主义侵略和反动统治的斗争中，或在为世界和平与安全的斗争中，一贯采取了同样的步骤。今后两国人民需要更密切地团结起来，进行反对美帝国主义侵略政策的斗争。只有这样，才能巩固并发展两国人民的胜利，也才能对东方各国人民的斗争有更大的贡献。”
    朝鲜著名作家韩雪野表示：他代表全朝鲜的文学家和艺术家向新中国致贺。他说：“今天的中国已经和朝鲜同样地变成了人民的国家，所以两国间外交关系的建立，意味着两国人民在政治经济文化等方面，都将发生新的人民的关系，并在这一基础上增进两国的友谊。”
    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会秘书长金昌俊说：“朝鲜人民和世界爱好和平的人民，一起欢呼新中国的诞生，热烈支持我国政府与中华人民共和国政府建立两国外交关系的决定。”
    朝鲜中央政府机关报“民主朝鲜”报六日以“朝鲜与中国建立外交关系对两国民主发展将有莫大贡献”为题，发表社论说：我们热烈地庆贺出现在亚洲大陆上的中华人民共和国。社论又说：我共和国和新生的中国建立外交关系，将促进朝中两国人民进一步的密切团结。对于两国间的政治、经济、文化等各方面，都将有巨大的影响。
