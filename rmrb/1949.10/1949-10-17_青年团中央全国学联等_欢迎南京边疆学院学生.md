Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee, All-China Student Federation, etc. Welcome Students from Nanjing Borderland College
Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 2

　　Youth League Central Committee, All-China Student Federation, etc.
　　Welcome Students from Nanjing Borderland College
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th] The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Student Federation, and the North China Higher Education Commission jointly welcomed twenty-eight Tibetan, Miao, Manchu, Hui, Lolo, and Yi students from Nanjing Borderland College on the evening of the 15th. The United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the China Youth Art Theater, Peking University, Mongolian Tibetan School, and other units also sent representatives to participate. At the meeting, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the Student Federation, first delivered a welcome speech, calling on young people of all ethnic groups to build a New Democratic China under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao. Gesang Yangying, a student from the Borderland College, gave a reply, expressing that they must study hard to promote the awakening and progress of young people of ethnic minorities. Li Xianlin, professor of the Department of Oriental Languages at Peking University, and Wei Yinggewa, representative of the Mongolian Tibetan School, also spoke successively. During the meeting, the All-China Student Federation, the Beijing Municipal Work Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation presented books to the students of the Borderland College. Finally, an evening party was held.

### 青年团中央全国学联等　欢迎南京边疆学院学生

1949-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央全国学联等
  　欢迎南京边疆学院学生
    【新华社北京十六日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国学生联合会、与华北高等教育委员会于十五日晚联合欢迎来自南京边疆学院的二十八位藏、苗、满、回、倮倮和彝族学生，中共中央统一战线部、中国青年艺术剧院、北京大学、蒙藏学校等单位亦派代表参加。会上首由学联主席谢邦定致欢迎词，号召各民族青年在中国共产党和毛主席领导下，建设新民主主义的中国。边疆学院学生格桑杨英刚致答词，表示他们一定要努力学习，推动少数民族青年的觉悟与进步。北京大学东方语文系教授李羡林、蒙藏学校代表伟英格娃也相继讲话。会中全国学联、青年团北京市工作委员会、北京市民主青年联合会筹委会等向边疆学院诸学生赠书，最后并举行晚会。
