Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Learns from Soviet Experience: Factory Leadership Regularly Meets with Workers to Hear Opinions and Improve Work

Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 2

　　Northeast News: Anshan Iron and Steel Company is adopting Soviet experience, stipulating that every Saturday from 1:00 PM to 5:30 PM will be the time for the manager and supervisory committee to meet with workers. Workers from all factories can directly present any suggestions regarding production improvement, employee benefits, or other opinions to the manager's office or the supervisory committee's office at that time without any formalities. The notification of this regulation was issued to all affiliated factories on the 14th of this month.

### 鞍钢学习苏联经验  厂方定期接见职工听取意见改进工作

1949-10-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　鞍钢学习苏联经验　
    厂方定期接见职工听取意见改进工作
    【东北讯】鞍山钢铁公司接受苏联经验，规定每星期六下午一时至五时半为经理及监委接见职工时间。各厂职工凡有关于生产改进或职工福利问题及其他意见，均可不经任何手续，届时直接至经理室或监委室提出。此一规定的通知，已于本月十四日发至所属各厂。
