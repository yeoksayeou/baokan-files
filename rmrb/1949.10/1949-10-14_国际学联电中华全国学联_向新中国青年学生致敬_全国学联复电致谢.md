Title: International Student Union Cables All-China Students' Federation to Salute the Young Students of New China; All-China Students' Federation Cables Reply of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th. The All-China Students' Federation received a cable from the International Student Union in Prague on the 8th, congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China and extending greetings to the Chinese people, youth, and students. The All-China Students' Federation immediately sent a reply of thanks. The texts of the two cables are as follows:

　　　　(1) International Student Union's Cable to the All-China Students' Federation:

On the occasion of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our heartfelt respect and warm congratulations to all Chinese students and youth.

The founding of the People's Republic of China has further consolidated the victorious struggle of the heroic Chinese people, workers, peasants, youth, and students.

You have defeated the rule of Chiang Kai-shek's feudal fascists, supported by American imperialism, ended the long-term exploitation system suffered by the Chinese people, gained true national independence, and opened up prospects for the development of talents for the broad masses of people, working youth, and students.

Moreover, the founding of the People's Republic of China has greatly strengthened the world people's movement to defend peace. Peace-loving and democratic students from around the world who participate in the International Student Union are as elated as you are. We express our respect for your determination to work with all working people to build an independent New China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　International Student Union

　　　　(2) All-China Students' Federation's Reply to President Grohman of the International Student Union:

Thank you very much for your congratulations on the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The founding of the People's Republic of China is not only a great victory for the Chinese people, but also a common victory for the people of the world. In the future, we Chinese students will continue to fight for world peace and progress in the ranks of the International Student Union, and we firmly believe that victory will surely belong to the people of the world. Please accept our brotherly greetings!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Students' Federation, October 8th

### 国际学联电中华全国学联　向新中国青年学生致敬　全国学联复电致谢

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际学联电中华全国学联
    向新中国青年学生致敬
    全国学联复电致谢
    【新华社北京十三日电】中华全国学生联合会于八日接获布拉格国际学生联合会来电，庆贺我中华人民共和国的成立，并向中国人民、青年、学生致敬。我全国学联当即复电致谢。两电电文如下：
    　　　　（一）国际学生联合会来电中华全国学生联合会：
    正当中华人民共和国成立之际，谨向所有中国学生和青年表示我们衷心的敬意和热烈的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的成立，使英勇的中国人民，工农青年及学生的胜利斗争，更加巩固。
    你们战胜了美帝国主义支持下的蒋介石封建法西斯的统治，结束了中国人民长期遭受的剥削制度，获得了真正的民族独立，并为广大的人民、劳动青年和学生们，开辟了发展才能的前途。
    不仅如此，中华人民共和国的成立，使世界人民保卫和平的运动大为增强。参加国际学联的世界爱好和平民主的学生和你们一样地欢欣鼓舞。并对你们和全体劳动人民一道共同建设独立的新中国的决心表示敬意。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　国际学生联合会
    　　　　（二）全国学联复电国际学生联合会主席格罗曼：
    十分感谢你们对中华人民共和国成立的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的成立，不但是中国人民的伟大胜利，也是世界各国人民的共同胜利。今后我们中国学生一定在国际学联的队伍中，继续为世界的和平和进步而战斗，并深信胜利一定属于世界的人民。谨致兄弟的敬礼！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国学生联合会十月八日
