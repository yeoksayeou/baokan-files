Title: Preparatory Committee for Collecting Transportation Materials in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 4

　　The Municipal Government Appoints Cao Yanxing as Chairman of the Committee

    [Beijing News] The Municipal People's Government recently ordered the appointment of Cao Yanxing, Director of the Construction Bureau, as Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for Collecting Transportation Materials, instructing him to immediately organize the committee and report back. Director Cao convened a meeting on the 11th with relevant units, including the Beijing Office of the Beijing-Tianjin Railway Bureau, the Transportation Section, and the Engineering Section, as well as the Suburban Work Committee of the Municipal Government, to discuss the organization and work of the committee. After exchanging opinions, it was decided that the Beijing-Tianjin Railway Bureau and the Municipal Government would respectively send letters to the Suburban Committee, the Civil Affairs Bureau, the Public Security Bureau, the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Chamber of Commerce, and the Industrial Association, requesting them to each appoint members. The Preparatory Committee for Collecting Transportation Materials will be officially established at 2:00 PM this afternoon (the 15th) at the Beijing Office of the Beijing-Tianjin Railway Bureau. After the establishment of the committee, it will immediately begin various tasks.　　　　　　　　　　　　(Municipal Government News Office)

### 收集交通材料京市组委员会

1949-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　收集交通材料京市组委员会
  　市府派曹言行兼主任委员
    【北京讯】本市人民政府顷令派建设局曹言行局长兼充交通材料收集委员会主任委员，饬即组织成立具报。曹局长于十一日召集京津铁路局北京办事处、车务段、工务段、市府郊区工作委员会等有关单位开会讨论关于该会的组织及工作等问题，经大家交换意见，商定由京津铁路局与市政府分别函令郊委会、民政局、公安局、市总工会筹委会、市商会、工业会等分别指派人员为委员，于今（十五）日下午二时在京津铁路局北京办事处正式成立交通材料收集委员会。该会成立以后，即可展开各项工作。　　　　　　　　　　　　（市府新闻处）
