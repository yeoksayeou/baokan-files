Title: "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy" Chinese Edition First Issue Published, Xinhua Bookstore Offers Affordable Subscriptions
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 8th:

    "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy"
    Chinese Edition First Issue Published
    Xinhua Bookstore Offers Affordable Subscriptions

    The Chinese edition of the Information Bureau of the Communist and Workers' Parties' newspaper "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" issue number 40, was published on the 2nd by the Liberation Press. This is the first issue of the Chinese edition, which includes the newspaper's editorial entitled "The Anniversary of the Information Bureau of the Communist and Workers' Parties' Resolution on the Situation of the Yugoslav Communist Party" and articles by Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Secretary Pauker and French Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Duclos commenting on the Tito group of Yugoslavia. Chairman Mao's speech at the preparatory meeting of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is also published in this issue. Other articles include Soviet prominent commentator Marinin's article on the North Atlantic Treaty and the Western European Union, and British Communist Party General Secretary Pollitt's essay on the British Labour Party. The Chinese edition of the newspaper is distributed by Xinhua Bookstore and sold by the bookstore and Sanlian Bookstore. Since its publication, it has been well received by readers in various regions. The Chinese edition of the newspaper will be published every half month. Xinhua Bookstore is offering affordable subscriptions.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主”　中文版第一期出版  新华书店廉价征求订户

1949-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　“争取持久和平，争取人民民主”
    中文版第一期出版
    新华书店廉价征求订户
    【新华社北京八日电】共产党与工人党情报局的机关报“争取持久和平、争取人民民主！”第四十期的中文版，已于二日由解放社出版。这是该报中文版的第一期，其中有该报的题为“共产党与工人党情报局关于南斯拉夫共产党情势决议的一周年”的社论和罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会书记波克尔及法国共产党中央委员会书记杜克洛评论南斯拉夫铁托集团的文章。毛主席在中国新政治协商会议筹备会上的讲词也刊载在这一期上。其他还有苏联著名评论家马里宁论北大西洋公约和西欧联盟，英共总书记波立特论英国工党的论文。该报中文版由新华书店发行并由该书店和三联书店经售，发行以来，已深受各地读者欢迎。以后该报中文版每半月出版一期。新华书店已廉价征求订户。
