Title: Overseas Chinese in Sumatra Send Respects to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: Chairman Mao Zedong received a congratulatory telegram from overseas Chinese in Sumatra. Ye Yidong, Chairman of the Medan Overseas Chinese Mass Meeting, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong stating:
    "The overseas Chinese in Medan (Sumatra), under the auspices of the Overseas Chinese General Association, held a mass meeting. The meeting passed a resolution expressing full support for the People's Republic of China and paying respects to the leaders of the Republic." The Longyan Association of East Coast of Sumatra sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong stating:
    "We are extremely delighted at the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic. Long live the People's Republic of China!"

### 苏门答腊侨胞电毛主席致敬

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏门答腊侨胞电毛主席致敬
    【新华社北京十六日电】毛泽东主席接获苏门答腊侨胞的贺电。棉兰华侨群众大会主席叶贻东致毛泽东主席电称：
    “棉兰（苏门答腊）华侨在华侨总会主持之下举行群众大会，会上通过决议，表示完全拥护中华人民共和国，并向共和国各领袖致敬。”苏门答腊东海岸龙岩同乡会致毛泽东主席电称：
    “人民共和国中央政府成立，我们极为欢欣。中华人民共和国万岁！”
