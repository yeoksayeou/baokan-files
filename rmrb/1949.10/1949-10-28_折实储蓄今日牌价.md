Title: Current Price of Commodity-Based Savings

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-28
Page: 2

　　Current Price of Commodity-Based Savings
    Today (October 28th), the standard commodity unit price for commodity-based savings at the Savings Department of the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank is 984.75 yuan.
