Title: Tens of Millions of National Flags Flutter in the Largest Metropolis in the Far East; Shanghai Held Flag-Raising Ceremony Yesterday; Six Million Citizens Paid Solemn Respects Amidst the National Anthem and Gun Salutes
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd. Shanghai, the birthplace of the Chinese Communist Party and the first metropolis in China and the Far East, solemnly held a flag-raising ceremony this morning in the rain. At exactly 8:00 AM, the sirens of factories and ships throughout the city sounded in unison, and all radio stations simultaneously broadcast the flag-raising music. All government agencies, troops, schools, organizations, factories, stores, and residential areas immediately raised tens of millions of bright red five-star national flags. Six million citizens paid solemn respects amidst the national anthem and gun salutes. More than 500 cadres attended the flag-raising ceremony of the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, which was personally presided over by Rao Shushi, Secretary of the East China Bureau and Secretary of the Municipal Committee, who also led the shouting of slogans. The beautiful national flag was raised to a height equivalent to a sixteen-story building. Rao Shushi said: "Today, we join the people of the whole country in celebrating the birth of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the Central People's Government. All members of our Communist Party and all our staff should resolutely implement all orders of the Central People's Government and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and strive for the rapid liberation of all of China and the construction of an independent, democratic, peaceful, unified, and prosperous new China." The flag-raising ceremony of the Shanghai Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government was presided over by Vice Mayor Pan Hannian, with more than 1,100 representatives from various units attending. The flag-raising ceremony of the directly subordinate team of the Political Department of the Songhu Garrison Command was also held on the top of the Command headquarters building, with a total of more than a thousand people attending. Military music was played, the national anthem was sung, and dozens of gun salutes were fired. The ceremony was extremely solemn and grand. Now, red national flags are fluttering in the sky and on the streets, washed by the sweet rain, appearing particularly bright and magnificent, further increasing the people's respect and love for the people's motherland.

### 成千万幅国旗飘扬远东第一大都市  沪昨举行升旗典礼  六百万市民在国歌和礼炮声中肃然起敬

1949-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　成千万幅国旗飘扬远东第一大都市
  　沪昨举行升旗典礼
    六百万市民在国歌和礼炮声中肃然起敬
    【新华社上海二日电】中国共产党的诞生地、中国及远东第一个大都市——上海于今晨在大雨中隆重举行升旗典礼。八时正，全市工厂和轮船汽笛齐鸣，各广播电台同时播送升旗曲，所有机关部队、学校、团体、工厂、商店及住宅区，立即升起了成千成万幅鲜红的五星国旗。六百万市民在国歌和礼炮声中肃然起敬。中共中央华东局暨中共上海市委会的升旗典礼计到干部五百余人，由华东局书记兼市委书记饶漱石亲自主持，并领导高呼口号。美丽的国旗一直升到十六层楼的高空。饶漱石讲话说：“我们今天和全国人民在一起共同来庆祝中华人民共和国的诞生和中央人民政府的成立。我们共产党的全体党员和全体工作人员应当坚决执行中央人民政府和伟大的中国人民领袖毛主席的一切命令，为迅速解放全中国及建设独立、民主、和平、统一和富强的新中国而奋斗。”上海市军管会和市人民政府的升旗典礼由副市长潘汉年主持，计到各单位代表一千一百余人。淞沪警备司令部政治部直属队的升旗典礼亦在该司令部大厦顶上举行，共到千余人，奏军乐，唱国歌，并放礼炮数十响，仪式极为庄严隆重。现在全市红色国旗正飘扬在甘霖冲浴过的天空和市街上，显得格外鲜明壮丽，更增加了人民对人民祖国的崇敬和热爱。
