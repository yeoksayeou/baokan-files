Title: Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Sends Congratulations on the Founding of New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 3

　　Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee
    Sends Congratulations on the Founding of New China
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th] The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the 4th, congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram stated:
    Dear Comrades: The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and the working people of Romania extend brotherly greetings to you. The glorious victory won by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party is an encouragement and call to the oppressed people who are resisting Anglo-American imperialist aggression. The establishment of the People's Republic of China and its Central People's Government is a new and powerful blow to the instigators of war and a great victory for the international peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, the teacher of the working people of the world.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央　电贺新中国成立

1949-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚工人党中央
    电贺新中国成立
    【新华社布拉格五日电】罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会四日致电中国共产党中央委员会，庆贺中华人民共和国的成立，电文称：
    亲爱的同志们：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和罗马尼亚劳动人民谨向你们致兄弟的敬礼。中国人民在共产党领导下所获得的光辉的胜利，对于反抗英美帝国主义侵略的被压迫人民是一个鼓舞和召唤，中华人民共和国及其中央人民政府的建立，是对战争挑拨者新的有力打击，是全世界劳动人民导师斯大林领导下的伟大苏联为首的国际和平民主阵营的伟大胜利。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会
