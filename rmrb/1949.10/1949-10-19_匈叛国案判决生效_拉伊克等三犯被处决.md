Title: Hungarian Treason Case Judgement Takes Effect: Rajk and Three Accomplices Executed
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: TASS, Budapest, 15th: After reviewing the judgement passed by the Special Committee of the People's Court of Budapest in the case of the traitor Rajk and his accomplices, the State Committee of the People's Court of the Hungarian People's Republic, on the 12th, passed a final decision rejecting all appeals. Exercising the power granted to it by law, the State Committee of the People's Court refused to submit petitions requesting leniency for Rajk, Zonyi, and Szalai to the Presidium of the People's Republic. Therefore, the judgement of the People's Court against Rajk, the enemy of the Hungarian people, and his accomplices has taken effect, and the three traitors, Rajk, Zonyi, and Szalai, were executed on the 15th.

### 匈叛国案判决生效　拉伊克等三犯被处决

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈叛国案判决生效
  　拉伊克等三犯被处决
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社布达佩斯十五日讯：匈牙利人民共和国人民法庭国家委员会审查布达佩斯人民法庭专门委员会对叛国犯拉伊克及其同谋者一案所通过的判决后，于十二日通过最后决定，驳回一切上诉。人民法庭国家委员会行使法律给予的权力，拒绝把要求对拉伊克、佐恩尼、萨莱三人从轻判罪的请愿书提交人民共和国主席团。因此，人民法庭对匈牙利人民公敌拉伊克及其同谋的判决即行生效，拉伊克、佐恩尼、萨莱等三叛国犯已于十五日被处决。
