Title: Soviet Delegation to Visit Peking University Tomorrow; Fadeyev to Deliver a Speech; Simonov Scheduled to Broadcast Tonight
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3
 
　　Soviet Delegation to Visit Peking University Tomorrow
    Fadeyev to Deliver a Speech; Simonov Scheduled to Broadcast Tonight
    [Report from this newspaper] The Deputy Head of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic and Scientific Workers Delegation, Simonov, will broadcast nationwide from Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station at 20:50 tonight.
    [Report from this newspaper] The Soviet Cultural, Artistic and Scientific Workers Delegation will visit Peking University at 10:00 am tomorrow (the 9th). Before the visit, the head of the delegation, Fadeyev, will give a lecture to the students at the university's Democratic Square, titled "Soviet Cultural Construction". Simonov and others will also give speeches.

### 苏联代表团明参观北大　法捷耶夫将发表演说西蒙诺夫定今晚播讲

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联代表团明参观北大
    法捷耶夫将发表演说西蒙诺夫定今晚播讲
    【本报讯】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团副团长西蒙诺夫，今晚二十点五十分在北京新华广播电台向全国广播。
    【本报讯】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团将于明（九）日上午十时到北大参观，参观前该团团长法捷耶夫将在该校民主广场向同学讲演，题为：“苏联文化建设”，另外并有西蒙诺夫等讲演。
