Title: Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Nanjing Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 2

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Preparatory Committee of Nanjing Branch Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th] The Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Nanjing Branch was officially established in Nanjing on the 10th of this month. The inaugural meeting elected Liu Bocheng, Su Yu, Song Renqiong, Ke Qingshi, Liang Xi, Tao Menghe, Ye Yucang, and 58 others to serve as members of the preparatory committee. The committee has established departments for secretarial affairs, organization, research and publication, service, and library materials to oversee daily work. The committee has decided to actively solicit members starting immediately. People from all walks of life who support Sino-Soviet friendship are welcome to join.

### 中苏友好协会　南京分会筹备会成立

1949-10-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会
    南京分会筹备会成立
    【新华社南京十二日电】中苏友好协会南京分会筹备会已于本月十日在宁正式成立。成立大会推选刘伯承、粟裕、宋任穷、柯庆施、梁希、陶孟和、叶雨沧等六十五人担任筹委。内设秘书、组织、研究出版、服务、图书资料等部门，主持日常会务工作。该会确定自即日起积极征求会员，凡拥护中苏友好的各界人士均可加入。
