Title: Roschin Visits Foreign Minister Zhou to Discuss Presentation of Credentials

Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th - Soviet Ambassador to China, Roschin, accompanied by Counselor Tikhvinskiy, paid his first visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 5:30 p.m. today to discuss the presentation of his credentials. Our Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Wang Jiaxiang, was present at the discussion.

### 罗申拜会周外长　就呈递国书事有所商谈

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗申拜会周外长
    就呈递国书事有所商谈
    【新华社北京十三日电】苏联驻华大使罗申于本日下午五时半，由齐赫文斯基参事陪伴首次拜会我外交部周恩来部长，就呈递国书事有所商谈。商谈时有我国驻苏大使王稼祥在座。
