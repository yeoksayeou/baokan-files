Title: Workers' Organizations from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams to New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 10th: Workers' organizations around the world continue to send congratulatory telegrams on the founding of the People's Republic of China. The Metal and Machinery Industry Department of the World Federation of Trade Unions sent a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese Metal Workers' Union, stating: The People's Republic of China is the best guarantee of a good life, freedom of the labor movement, and a steadily improving environment for workers.

The Metal Workers' Federation of the French General Confederation of Labour (CGT) telegraphed the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, representing 700,000 members, to pay tribute to the founding of the People's Republic of China and express warm congratulations. The telegram stated: "We wish you great success in the cause of consolidating peace, consolidating the welfare and freedom of the Chinese people. The French Textile Workers' Federation telegraphed the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, saying: "On the occasion of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the French Textile Workers' Federation of the French General Confederation of Labour, we extend our congratulations to the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. We assure you of the friendship and solidarity of the French textile workers with you.

　　　　　　　　　　　　Aubert, General Secretary of the French Textile Workers' Federation."

### 各国工人团体电贺新中国

1949-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国工人团体电贺新中国
    【新华社布拉格十日电】世界工人团体继续电贺中华人民共和国的成立。世界工联五金机械产业部致中国五金工人工会的贺电称：中华人民共和国是良好生活，职工运动自由和逐日改善工人们环境的最好保证者。
    法总工会五金工人联合会电中华全国总工会，代表七十万会员对中华人民共和国的成立致敬，并表示热烈的庆贺，电文称：祝你们在为巩固和平，巩固中国人民福利和自由的事业上的巨大成功，法国纺织工人联合会电贺中华全国总工会执委会称：“在中华人民共和国成立之际，谨代表法国总工会所属法国纺织工人联合会职工向中华全国总工会执委会致贺。我们保证法国纺织工人与你们的友谊和团结。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　法国纺织工人联合会总书记奥培尔”。
