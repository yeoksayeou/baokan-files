Title: Beijing Post Office Special Commemorative Postmark for Celebrating the Founding Ceremony of New China; Commemorative Stamps for the CPPCC Have Been Printed
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 4

　　Beijing Post Office Special Commemorative Postmark for Celebrating the Founding Ceremony of New China
  　Commemorative Stamps for the CPPCC Have Been Printed
    [Beijing News] In order to celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China and the success of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Beijing Postal Administration has specially prepared several kinds of commemorative stamps of "Celebrating the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference", and two kinds of commemorative postmarks. The commemorative stamps will be available for sale before the 10th of this month. The postmarks will be used from October 1st at the General Post Office, West Chang'an Street Post Office, Houmen Post Office, Luomashi Post Office, and Dongsi Pailou Post Office. The commemorative postmark is circular, with five stars in the middle of the pattern, and ears of wheat on both sides of the outer circle. The words "Celebration" and "Commemoration" are inscribed around the top, and the year is recorded at the bottom with Arabic numerals, which is very beautiful.
    [Another News] The birth of the People's Republic of China will be celebrated with a three-day national holiday. In addition to participating in various celebrations and publicity programs, postal workers will also express their enthusiasm for celebration through their work. On the morning of October 1st, all post offices in the city will still handle ticket sales, and accept ordinary, registered, and express mail (parcels and savings remittances will be suspended); the 2nd will be handled as a Sunday; on the morning of the 3rd, all services (including parcel savings and remittances) will be handled as usual, and the business will be closed in the afternoon.
    [Another News] Starting from October, the business hours of post offices in the city will be changed as follows: Usually, 9:00 am to 11:00 am, and 2:00 pm to 5:00 pm. Sundays and public holidays are from 3:00 pm to 5:00 pm.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Beijing Postal Administration)

### 庆祝新中国开国盛典京邮局特制纪念邮戳 　已印制政协纪念邮票
北京邮政管理局
1949-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝新中国开国盛典京邮局特制纪念邮戳
  　已印制政协纪念邮票
    【北京讯】北京邮政管理局为了庆祝中华人民共和国成立和人民政治协商会议成功，特制备“庆祝人民政治协商会议”纪念邮票数种，及纪念邮戳二种。纪念邮票本月十日前即可出售。邮戳自十月一日起在总局及西长安街邮局、后门邮局、骡马市邮局、东四牌楼邮局、开始使用。该项纪念邮戳系圆形，图案中间为五星，外圆两旁缀以麦禾，上面环书庆祝及纪念字样，下端用阿拉伯数字记明年号，至为美观。
    【又讯】中华人民共和国诞生，全国放假三天，邮局职工除一面参加各项庆祝及宣传节目外，并将以工作表示庆祝热忱。十月一日上午本市各邮局仍办理售票，收寄平常、挂号及快递邮件（包裹及储金汇兑停止）；二日照星期日办理；三日上午各项业务（包括包裹储金及汇兑）照常办理，下午停止营业。
    【又讯】自十月份起本市各邮亭办公时间变更如下：平常日上午九时至十一时，下午二时至五时。星期日及例假日为下午三点至五点。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（北京邮政管理局）
