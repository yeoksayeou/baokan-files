Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 3

　　Suiyuan
    [Suiyuan Electric] On October 2nd, the International Day of Struggle for Peace, all circles in Suiyuan held a warm celebration in Fengzhen City for the success of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and the support of the International Day of Struggle for Peace. More than 10,000 people attended the meeting, including provincial, county, and municipal cadres, workers, farmers, students, and merchants. At the meeting, Su, Deputy Secretary of the Suiyuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang, Chairman of the Provincial People's Government, General Dong Qiwu, a representative from Guisui, and representatives from all walks of life spoke. A lantern parade was held at night.

### 绥远

1949-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　绥远
    【绥远电】十月二日国际和平斗争日，绥远各界在丰镇市举行热烈庆祝人民政协成功、中华人民共和国中央政府成立暨拥护国际和平斗争日大。会到会有省、县、市干部，工、农、学、商等约万余人。会上有中共绥远省委苏副书记，省人民政府杨主席及归绥代表董其武将军和各界代表讲话。夜晚举行提灯大游行。
