Title: Resolutely Defend China's Gateway: Wusong Fortress Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

　　Resolutely Defend China's Gateway
    Wusong Fortress Headquarters Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th: The Wusong Fortress Headquarters of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was established on October 11th upon order. At the inaugural meeting, Han Nianlong, Deputy Political Commissar of the Shanghai Garrison Headquarters, and Xu Jincheng, Deputy Commander of the Fortress Headquarters, called on all commanders and fighters to resolutely defend China's gateway and be prepared to strike any aggressors who dare to invade the sacred territory of the People's Republic of China.

### 坚决守卫中国大门　吴淞要塞司令部成立

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　坚决守卫中国大门
    吴淞要塞司令部成立
    【新华社上海十三日电】中国人民解放军华东军区吴淞要塞司令部于十月十一日奉命成立。在成立大会上，淞沪警备司令部韩念龙副政委，及要塞司令部胥晋城副司令，号召全体指战员，坚决守卫好中国大门，准备予任何敢于侵犯中华人民共和国神圣国土的侵略者以打击。
