Title: Southern Daily Inaugurated; Xinhua News Agency South China Branch Officially Begins Dispatches; Guangzhou People's Radio Station Begins Broadcasting

Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 1

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 24th】The Southern Daily, the organ of the CCP Central Committee South China Sub-Bureau, was inaugurated on the 23rd. The Xinhua News Agency South China Branch was also officially established and began dispatching news on the same day. Guangzhou People's Radio Station began broadcasting on the evening of the 20th. The station's wavelength is 216 meters, with a frequency of 1390 kilocycles; broadcasting hours: 7:00 to 9:00, 12:00 to 13:15, 18:30 to 22:00.

### 南方日报创刊　新华社华南分社正式发稿广州人民广播电台开始播音

1949-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　南方日报创刊
  　新华社华南分社正式发稿广州人民广播电台开始播音
    【新华社广州二十四日电】中共中央华南分局机关报南方日报，二十三日创刊。新华社华南分社也在同一天正式成立并发稿。广州人民广播电台，则从二十日晚起就开始播音。该台波长为二一六公尺，周率为一三九○千周；播音时间：七点到九点，十二点到十三点十五分，十八点三十分到二十二点。
