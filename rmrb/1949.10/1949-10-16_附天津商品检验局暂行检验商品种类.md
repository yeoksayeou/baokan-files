    　2. Raw Sugar (Currently prohibited from import)
    　　A. Raw Sucrose for Industrial Use
    　　B. Syrup for Industrial Use
    (II) Glucose (Starch Sugar) (Currently prohibited from import)
    　1. Refined Glucose
    　2. Ordinary Glucose
    　　A. Solid Glucose
    　　B. Syrupy Glucose
    (III) Maltose (Currently prohibited from import)
    (IV) Lactose (Currently prohibited from import)
    (V) Honey (Currently prohibited from import)
    (VI) Other Sugar Products and Substitutes (Currently prohibited from import)
    III. Fertilizers
    (I) Nitrogen Fertilizer (II) Phosphate Fertilizer (III) Potassium Fertilizer (IV) Mixed Fertilizer
    IV. Alcohol
    (I) Pure Alcohol (II) Ordinary Alcohol (III) Denatured Alcohol
