Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of the Central People's Government Founding Celebration Today
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of the Central People's Government Founding Celebration Today
　　[Report from this newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station and People's Broadcasting Stations across the country have decided to broadcast live coverage of the People's Republic of China Central People's Government Founding Celebration, which will be held at 3:00 PM today.

### 北京新华广播电台今天全部转播中央人民政府成立庆祝大会实况

1949-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台今天全部转播中央人民政府成立庆祝大会实况
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台及全国各地人民广播电台，决定全部转播今天下午三点钟举行的中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立庆祝大会实况。
