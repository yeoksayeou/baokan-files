Title: Czechoslovakian Industrial Production Exceeds Third Quarter Plan
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, Prague, 16th: According to published materials, Czechoslovakian industry has completed 101.8% of its third quarter production plan for 1949. Among these, the mining industry completed 100.4% of its nine-month plan, the power industry 101.5%, the steel industry 101.6%, and the textile and garment industry exceeded the plan by 6.3%. Furthermore, according to a report from the Telepress Agency: Czechoslovakia's surplus in September was 161.1 million crowns (Czech currency), and the total export value from January to September reached 29 billion crowns, an increase of 11% compared to the same period last year.

### 捷工业生产超过第三季计划

1949-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷工业生产超过第三季计划
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社布拉格十六日讯：据发表的材料，捷克斯洛伐克工业已完成了一九四九年第三季生产计划的百分之一○一·八。其中采矿工业完成九个月计划的百分之一○○·四，动力工业一○一·五，钢铁工业一○一·六，纺织与成衣工业超过计划百分之六·三。另据电通社讯：捷克斯洛伐克九月份出超一亿六千一百万克朗（捷币），一月至九月出口总值达二百九十亿克朗，较去年同期增加百分之十一。
