Title: Soviet Friends' Speech Receives Warm Acclaim
Author:
Date: 1949-10-10
Page: 2

Soviet biological science doctor, Stoletov, took the stage to speak, and every brilliant remark he made was met with enthusiastic cheers and applause from the entire audience (the full text of the speech will be published separately). Qian Junrui, representative of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a congratulatory speech to the conference, calling on the people of the whole country to unite, consolidate Sino-Soviet friendship, and guarantee the democratic peace of the whole world and all mankind. Markov, Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, said in his speech that he hoped that the advanced intellectuals of New Democratic China would spare no effort to strive for the historically significant cause of peace and the cause of friendship among the people of the world. He also hoped that literary and art workers from all walks of life would help them with their organizational work and prepare for the Soviet Women's Work and Life Exhibition to be held in Beijing this December. As the conference proceeded, a delegation of children from Beijing presented flowers and gave speeches to the delegation of Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers. The Soviet representatives warmly embraced the innocent and lively children, accepting bouquets of flowers. After the flower presentation, Seleznev, Acting Consul General of the Soviet Union in Beijing, gave a speech of thanks, receiving warm applause from the audience. Subsequently, representatives of workers, Hu Xiukui, Chairman of the Tram Company Trade Union; Zheng Di, of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, representing women; and Gu Qizong, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Student Federation, representing students, delivered speeches. They unanimously congratulated the success of the conference and expressed their deep respect to the Soviet friends. The conference passed several resolutions of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on organizational issues, as well as a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin and a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong. The conference elected Peng Zhen as President, Wang Zhizhi and ten others as Vice Presidents, and Nie Rongzhen, Zhao Zhensheng and one hundred and forty-seven others as Directors (list to be issued separately). After the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Democratic Youth Federation, the Democratic Women's Federation, and the Beijing Quyi circles presented flags, Zhang Xiruo, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered the closing remarks. The conference adjourned at about 2 p.m. amidst applause and music.

### 苏联友人讲话受到热烈欢呼

1949-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏联友人讲话受到热烈欢呼
    苏联生物科学博士斯托列托夫登台讲话，他的每一段精彩的话都受到全场热烈的欢呼和鼓掌（讲话全文另发）。中苏友协总会代表钱俊瑞向大会致贺，他号召全国人民组织起来，巩固中苏友谊，保证全世界、全人类的民主和平。苏联对外文协化会远东部副部长马尔柯夫在致词中，希望新民主主义的中国先进的知识分子，要不惜任何代价，为了负有历史意义的和平事业和世界人民友好事业而奋斗。他并且希望各界文艺工作者多多帮助他们做组织工作，筹备今年十二月在北京举办的苏联妇女工作及生活的展览会。大会进行至此，由北京市儿童代表团向苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团献花并致词，苏联代表都热情地把天真活泼的小朋友拥抱起来，接受了一束束的鲜花。献花后，由苏联驻京代理总领事谢列金致谢词，获得全场热烈的掌声。此后相继致词的有职工代表电车公司工会主席胡秀魁、妇女代表京市民主妇联郑迪之、学生代表京市学联主席古奇踪等，他们一致庆贺大会的成功，并向苏联友人致深切的敬意。大会通过了北京市中苏友好协会关于组织问题的几项决定，通过向斯大林大元帅致敬电和向毛泽东主席致敬电。大会选出彭真为会长、王之相等十一人为副会长，聂荣臻、赵振声等一百四十七人为理事（名单另发）。北京市总工会、民主青年联合会、民主妇女联合会和北京市曲艺界献旗后，由北京市中苏友好协会副会长张奚若致闭幕词。下午二时许大会在掌声与乐声中散会。
