Title: Our East Zhejiang Troops Capture Xiazhidao Island
Author:
Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 1

　　Our East Zhejiang Troops
    Capture Xiazhidao Island
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 15th] The People's Liberation Army on the East Zhejiang front liberated Xiazhidao Island, east of Daheng Island, on the 12th. Several small islands surrounding the island have also been brought under the control of the People's Liberation Army.

### 浙东我军克虾峙岛

1949-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东我军
    克虾峙岛
    【新华社杭州十五日电】浙东前线人民解放军于十二日解放大横岛以东的虾峙岛，该岛周围的数小岛，亦被解放军控制。
