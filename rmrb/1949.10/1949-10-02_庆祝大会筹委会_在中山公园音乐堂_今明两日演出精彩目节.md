Title: Celebration Conference Preparatory Committee Presents Wonderful Performances at Zhongshan Park Concert Hall Today and Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 2

　　 Celebration Conference Preparatory Committee
   　Presents Wonderful Performances at Zhongshan Park Concert Hall Today and Tomorrow
    [Our Newspaper] The Literary and Art Group of the Propaganda Department of the Celebration Conference Preparatory Committee has organized four recreational programs at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall. Two shows will be performed day and night on the 2nd and 3rd, with a uniform ticket price of fifty yuan, to entertain the citizens. These programs are all automatically and voluntarily presented by the Quyi (folk art) circles, Ping Opera circles, and the North China Peking Opera Theatre, and all proceeds will be donated to the public.

### 庆祝大会筹委会 　在中山公园音乐堂  今明两日演出精彩目节

1949-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　 庆祝大会筹委会
   　在中山公园音乐堂
   　今明两日演出精彩目节
    【本报讯】庆祝大会筹委会宣传部文艺组在中山公园音乐堂举办了四场游艺节目，二三两日日夜两场轮流上演，票价一律五十元，招待市民。这些节目都是曲艺界评戏界及华北平剧院自动义务献演的，收入所得全部捐公。
