Title: Ode to New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 7

　　　　　I
　
　　People's China stands tall in the East,
　　Its radiance shines in all directions, illuminating the world.
　　Celebrating the success achieved through hardship,
　　Five-star red flags are everywhere.
　　The living are numerous, and resources abundant,
　　Workers and peasants are masters of their own destiny.
　　Making my glorious motherland,
　　Steadily move towards the Great Harmony.
　
　　　　　　II
　
　　The people's character is hardworking and heroic,
　　Consolidating national defense and innovating traditions.
　　Strong leadership by the Chinese Communist Party,
　　The vanguard of the proletariat.
　　Industrialization, with momentum like a rainbow,
　　Tillers have land, and the world is public.
　　Making my glorious motherland,
　　Steadily move towards the Great Harmony.
　
　　　　　　III
　
　　People's democratic dictatorship, democratic centralism,
　　Open and upright, a dignified leader.
　　Rivers and oceans flow with new praise,
　　Kunlun towers as the highest peak.
　　Many ethnicities, like brothers,
　　Freedom's breeze blowing in all directions.
　　Making my glorious motherland,
　　Steadily move towards the Great Harmony.

### 新华颂

1949-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

　　新华颂
　　　　　一
　
    人民中国　屹立亚东
    光芒万道　辐射寰空
    艰难缔造庆成功
    五星红旗遍地红
    生者众　物产丰
    工农长作主人翁
    使我光荣祖国
    稳步走向大同
　
　　　　　　二
　
    人民品质　勤劳英勇
    巩固国防　革新传统
    坚强领导由中共
    无产阶级急先锋
    工业化　气如虹
    耕者有田天下公
    使我光荣祖国
    稳步走向大同
　
　　　　　　三
　
    人民专政　民主集中
    光明磊落　领袖雍容
    江河洋海流新颂
    昆仑长耸最高峰
    多种族　如弟兄
    四方八面自由风
    使我光荣祖国
    稳步走向大同
