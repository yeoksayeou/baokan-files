Title: Xiangjiang Cultural Work Troupe Established
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 2
　　
　　Xiangjiang Cultural Work Troupe Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 12th] Under the leadership of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, more than 100 members of the former Anti-Enemy Drama Troupe's Sixth Team and the Zhongyuan University Cultural Work Troupe's branch troupe, who had persisted in working in the Hunan area, have joined forces and been reorganized into the Xiangjiang Cultural Work Troupe. Furthermore, in response to the call for literary and art workers to "go to the factories," the Changsha Municipal Workers' Cultural and Art Work Troupe was established at the end of last month.

### 湘江文工团成立

1949-10-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　湘江文工团成立
    【新华社长沙十二日电】在中共湖南省委领导下，在湖南一带坚持工作的前抗敌演剧队第六队和中原大学文工团分来团员共一百多人，已经会师合编为湘江文工团。又为响应文艺工作者“到工厂去”的号召，长沙市工人文艺工作团已在上月底成立。
