Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
  　Tonight's Highlights
    [By Our Correspondent] To commemorate the 13th anniversary of Lu Xun's death, Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will present a special program from 20:50 to 21:30 tonight. Xu Guangping will speak on "Remembering Mr. Lu Xun in Joy"; Feng Xuefeng will speak on "The Era Basis of Lu Xun's Thought," and a "Biographical Introduction of Mr. Lu Xun" will be broadcast. At 18:30, the first arts and literature program will feature a reading of the second chapter of Lu Xun's work "The True Story of Ah Q," etc. (The above programs will be broadcast simultaneously on the second channel.)

    Also, on the second channel at 17:15, children's time: Mr. Sun Jingxiu will continue to tell "The Story of Pavel." At 17:45, workers' time: a comprehensive report will be broadcast, titled: "Beijing Post Office Workers Respond to the Disaster Relief Movement with Streamlining and Savings." At 18:00, a music program: Beijing Mu Zhen and Huiwen Harmonica Teams will perform a harmonica ensemble. At 20:00, Voices of the Municipality: An introduction to the programs of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau regarding the municipal people's sports meet.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台为纪念鲁迅逝世十三周年，今晚二十点五十分到二十一点三十分举办特别节目，由许广平讲：“在愉快中纪念鲁迅先生”；冯雪峰讲：“鲁迅思想发展的时代根据”，并且播送“鲁迅先生的生平介绍”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，朗诵鲁迅作品“阿Ｑ正传”的第二章等。（以上节目第二台同时联播）
    又二台十七时十五分，儿童时间：由孙敬修先生继续讲“保尔的故事”。十七时四十五分，职工时间：播送一篇综合报导；题目是：“北京邮局职工精简节约响应救灾运动”。十八时，音乐节目：北京市慕贞、汇文口琴队口琴合奏。二十时，市政之声：北京市人民政府教育局关于本市人民体育大会节目的介绍。
