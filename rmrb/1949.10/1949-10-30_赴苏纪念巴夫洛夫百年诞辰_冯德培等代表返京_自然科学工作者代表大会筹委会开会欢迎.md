Title: Returning to Beijing After Commemorating Pavlov's Centenary in the Soviet Union: Representatives Led by Feng Depi; Preparatory Committee of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers Holds Meeting to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 4

　　The Preparatory Committee of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers held a symposium at 3 PM on October 28th in the auditorium of Peking Union Medical College to welcome Feng Depi, Pan Shu, and Ji Zhongpu, who had just returned from the Soviet Union after attending the celebration of the centenary of the great physiologist Pavlov. Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee, first delivered a welcoming speech. Feng Depi then gave a brief introduction to the grand occasion of the Soviet Union's two-week commemoration of Pavlov's centenary. He also spoke about the organizational situation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and the activities of various research institutes commemorating Pavlov. Pan Shu then gave a supplementary report, in which he described the Soviet people's emphasis on cultural heritage, precious museums, and the general restoration of dilapidated old churches and buildings. He said with emotion: Only in socialist countries are cultural heritages valued, cherished, and preserved.

### 赴苏纪念巴夫洛夫百年诞辰  冯德培等代表返京  自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会开会欢迎

1949-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　赴苏纪念巴夫洛夫百年诞辰　
    冯德培等代表返京            
    自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会开会欢迎
    【本报讯】中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会于本月二十八日下午三时在协和医学院大礼堂开座谈会，欢迎新由苏联回国、出席苏联纪念伟大生理学家巴夫洛夫百年诞辰的代表冯德培、潘菽、季钟朴。首由筹委会主任委员吴玉章致欢迎词，继由冯德培对此次苏联纪念巴夫洛夫百年诞辰，曾期二周的盛况作了简单介绍。并谈到苏联科学院的组织情形以及纪念巴夫洛夫的各研究院的活动情形。继由潘菽作补充报告，他叙述了苏联人民对文化遗产的重视，珍贵博物院，普遍重修破烂古老的教堂、建筑物等。他感慨地说：只有在社会主义国家，文化遗产才被重视、爱护与保存。
