Title: Prague is Decorated with Lanterns and Streamers to Celebrate Independence Day; Bulgarian Diplomatic Corps Participates in Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th: According to a report from Teletype Agency in Prague: All parts of Czechoslovakia are warmly celebrating Independence Day. From Šumava to the High Tatras, buildings in every village are decorated with lanterns and streamers, with flags and pictures flying everywhere. Wenceslas Square in the center of Prague and the capital's historical buildings are brilliantly decorated, reflecting the spirit of the working people's true independence. The official celebration of Independence Day will be held on the 31st.
　　According to TASS, Sofia: The Bulgarian people are celebrating the 31st anniversary of Czechoslovakia's independence throughout the country. The National Committee of the Fatherland Front and the Committee of the Bulgarian-Czech Friendship Association presided over the celebration. Najdev, Chairman of the People's Assembly, Bulgarian government officials and the diplomatic corps attended. Sherbachov, Secretary of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, delivered a report on the Czech Independence Day.
　　According to the Polish Press Agency, Warsaw: Polish President Bierut, Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz, and Marshal Żymierski sent congratulatory messages on the Czech Independence Day. The Workers' Party's People's Tribune also published an editorial stating: Thirty-one years after the emergence of the bourgeois republic of Czechoslovakia, the Czechoslovak state, revived by the people and ruled by the people's regime, is moving towards socialism.

### 布拉格张灯结彩欢庆独立纪念日　保外交团参加庆典

1949-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　布拉格张灯结彩欢庆独立纪念日　
    保外交团参加庆典
    【新华社北京三十日电】据电通社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克全国各地热烈庆祝独立纪念日。从苏马瓦到塔特拉斯每个村庄的建筑物，都张灯结彩，旗帜图画到处飘扬。布拉格中心温希斯拉斯广场与首都历史性建筑物，装饰得灿烂辉煌，在在都是劳动人民真正独立精神的反映。独立纪念的正式庆祝，将在卅一日举行。据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚人民在全国各地庆祝捷克斯洛伐克独立三十一周年纪念日。祖国阵线的全国委员会和保捷友好协会的委员会主持庆祝会，人民议会主席奈捷夫、保政府官员及外交团等均往参加，祖国阵线全国委员会的书记谢尔巴契夫发表关于捷克独立纪念的报告。据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰贝鲁特总统、西伦凯维兹总理、齐默尔斯基元帅，电贺捷克独立纪念。工人党人民论坛报亦发表社论称：在捷克斯洛伐克资产阶级共和国出现之三十一年后的今日，为人民所复兴的、并由人民政权所统治的捷克斯洛伐克国家正迈向社会主义。
