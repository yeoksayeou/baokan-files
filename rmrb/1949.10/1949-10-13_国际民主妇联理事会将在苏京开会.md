Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Council to Meet in Soviet Capital

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 3

　　International Democratic Women's Federation Council
    To Meet in Soviet Capital
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 12th] Electroneews Agency, Moscow, October 11th: The Council of the International Democratic Women's Federation is scheduled to convene in Moscow on October 15th. According to Gaga Lina, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, representatives from fifty-six countries will attend the meeting, which will discuss matters such as participation in the First Representative Conference of Asian Democratic Women scheduled to be held in Beijing in December.

### 国际民主妇联理事会将在苏京开会

1949-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联理事会
    将在苏京开会
    【新华社北京十二日电】电通社莫斯科十一日讯：国际民主妇女联盟理事会定十月十五日在莫斯科召开会议。据苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席嘉嘉里纳称：出席会议的将有五十六个国家的代表会议并将讨论参加定十二月在北京举行的亚洲民主妇女第一届代表会议等事宜。
