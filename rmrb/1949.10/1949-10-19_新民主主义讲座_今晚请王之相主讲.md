Title: Lecture on New Democracy Tonight: Wang Zhixiang to Speak

Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 4

Lecture on New Democracy
　　Tonight: Wang Zhixiang to Speak
　　[Our Newspaper] The 50th lecture of the "New Democracy Lecture Series" organized by the City YMCA is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Wednesday, October 19th. Mr. Wang Zhixiang will be specially invited to speak on: "The Significance of Sino-Soviet Friendship and Our Tasks." All circles are welcome to attend the lecture.

### 新民主主义讲座　今晚请王之相主讲

1949-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
  　今晚请王之相主讲
    【本报讯】本市青年会主办之“新民主主义讲座”第五十讲，定十月十九日（星期三）晚七时特请王之相先生在该会讲：“中苏友好的意义和我们应有的任务。”欢迎各界参加听讲。
