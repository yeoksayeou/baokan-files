Title: Sa Zhenbing and Others Send Telegrams Congratulating Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 5th. The Fuzhou former Kuomintang naval personnel symposium sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the telegram reads as follows:
    Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all representatives of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference: The long-awaited Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference has finally opened victoriously under the leadership of the great people's leader Chairman Mao. This epoch-making achievement of the people's revolution gives us great encouragement. We hereby send our warm congratulations and celebrate the birth of the Central People's Government. In our excitement, we must intensify our study, do our best to transform ourselves, learn from the People's Liberation Army, build a strong people's navy under the leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and vow to liberate Taiwan, liberate all of China, and completely crush the remnants of the Jiang bandits.
    Respectfully from Sa Zhenbing and 212 other naval officers in Fujian.
    Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 5th. Sa Zhenbing, specially invited representative of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and respected elder of the navy, sent a telegram congratulating Chairman Mao on his election as Chairman of the Central People's Government. The telegram reads as follows: To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government:
    I am delighted to hear that the Central People's Government has been established and that you have been elected as Chairman. The whole country is rejoicing and deeply loves you. Zhenbing is old and has limited knowledge, and I am ashamed to have been mistakenly recommended as a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference! Moreover, it is difficult for me to travel and I regret that I cannot attend the meeting in the capital! I send this telegram to pay my respects, and hope you will consider it! Respectfully, Sa Zhenbing. October 3rd

### 萨镇冰等电贺毛主席

1949-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　萨镇冰等电贺毛主席
    【新华社福州五日电】福州前国民党海军人员座谈会致毛主席、朱总司令通电，电文如下：
    毛主席朱总司令并中国人民政治协商会议全体代表：渴望已久的中国人民政协会议，终在伟大人民领袖毛主席领导之下胜利地揭幕，这个划时代的人民革命的成就，给我们万分鼓舞，谨此遥致热烈的贺忱，并庆祝中央人民政府的诞生。我们兴奋之余，更要加紧学习，尽力改造自己，向人民解放军看齐，在毛主席朱总司令领导之下，建设强大的人民海军，并誓为解放台湾，解放全中国，彻底粉碎蒋匪残余势力而努力。
    在闽海军军官佐萨镇冰等二百十二人同叩。
    【新华社福州五日电】中国人民政协特邀代表、海军耆宿萨镇冰电贺毛主席当选中央人民政府主席。电文如下：中央人民政府毛主席钧鉴：
    欣悉中央人民政府成立，而先生被选为主席，全国腾欢，同深爱戴。镇冰年衰识浅，谬膺推举为人民政协全国委员会委员，无任惭感！且难胜跋涉，未能入都出席，尤所抱歉！特电致敬，即希垂察！萨镇冰叩。十月三日
