Title: Youth League Proposes Establishing a National Sports Association
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 18th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, in order to promote national sports and develop the spirit of mass sports, has invited people from the sports circles in various regions of the country to initiate the establishment of a preparatory committee for the National Sports Association. Currently, responsible persons from the Northeast, North China, Central China, East China, Tianjin, and other places have arrived in Beijing one after another and plan to formally meet after attending the Beijing People's Sports Conference on the 22nd and 23rd of this month to discuss preparatory matters.

### 青年团倡议筹组全国体育协会

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团倡议筹组
  　全国体育协会
    【新华社北京十八日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为了提倡国民体育，发扬人民大众的体育精神，已邀请全国各地区体育界人士，发起组织全国体育协会筹备会。目前东北、华北、华中、华东、天津等地负责人，均已陆续来京，拟参加本月二十二、三日举行的北京市人民体育大会后，即正式开会，商讨筹备事宜。
