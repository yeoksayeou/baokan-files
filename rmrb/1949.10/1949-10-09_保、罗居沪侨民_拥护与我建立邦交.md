Title: Bulgarian and Romanian Residents in Shanghai Support the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th. People from Bulgaria, Romania, and other new democratic countries residing in Shanghai have expressed great joy upon hearing the news that their governments have established diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. Bulgarian resident lawyer Mr. Wilro stated that from his personal experience in the past few months, he has been deeply impressed by the construction achievements of the People's Government. He said: The success of the Chinese revolution is the greatest contribution to world peace. From now on, war schemers have suffered another severe blow. The president of the Romanian Residents Association stated that the establishment of diplomatic relations between Romania and the People's Republic of China is precisely the wish of the Romanian people.

### 保、罗居沪侨民　拥护与我建立邦交

1949-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　保、罗居沪侨民
    拥护与我建立邦交
    【新华社上海八日电】侨居上海的保加利亚、罗马尼亚等各新民主国家的人民，在听到他们的政府和中华人民共和国建立邦交的消息以后，都表示非常高兴。保加利亚侨民维尔罗律师表示，他这几个月来，从自己的亲身体验中，对人民政府的建设成绩感到十分钦佩。他说：中国革命的成功，是对世界和平最大的贡献。从此，战争阴谋者又受到了一个严重的打击。罗马尼亚侨民协会会长表示罗马尼亚和中华人民共和国建立邦交，正是罗国人民的愿望。
