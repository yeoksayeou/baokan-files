Title: Nanjing Sino-Soviet People's Grand Rally Celebrates the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 8th. People from all walks of life in Nanjing and Soviet friends staying in Nanjing jointly held a grand celebration on the 5th to celebrate the official establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the Soviet Union. On the rostrum of the meeting hall were large portraits of Lenin, Stalin, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, as well as the national flags of China and the Soviet Union. At the meeting, General Tang Liang, Deputy Director of the Nanjing Military Control Commission, delivered a speech on behalf of all circles, pointing out that the road of the Soviet people is the road of the Chinese people, and the Chinese people recognize that the Soviet Union is the most sincere friend of the Chinese people. We resolutely support Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation, and stand in the international peace and democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union to fight against warmongers. Soviet friend Shi Baifu, in his speech, warmly celebrated the establishment of Sino-Soviet diplomatic relations and celebrated the great victory of the Chinese people. He pointed out that the whole of China is about to be completely liberated. The People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government have been established, and the Chinese people's leader Mao Zedong has been elected Chairman of the Central People's Government. This is a major event of world historical significance. After the meeting, when the parade of more than 20,000 people from 44 units including the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, the Third Field Army, the Nanjing Military Control Commission, various democratic parties, various agencies, and various people's organizations passed through the venue to celebrate Sino-Soviet friendship, Chinese and Soviet people cheered in unison: "Long live Marshal Stalin!" "Long live Chairman Mao, the leader of the Chinese people!" "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!" Representatives of various agencies, people's organizations, and democratic parties offered flowers and flags to Soviet friends in front of the rostrum of the conference, and presented letters to Marshal Stalin. Applause and cheers resounded as one. For twenty-two years, the Nanjing people's love for Soviet friends, accumulated in their hearts, was expressed to the fullest at this moment. In the evening, Soviet friends invited representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing to hold a cocktail party, and the hosts and guests enthusiastically toasted "Sino-Soviet friendship and unity."

### 南京中苏人民盛大集会　庆祝中苏建立邦交

1949-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京中苏人民盛大集会
    庆祝中苏建立邦交
    【新华社南京八日电】南京市各界人民和留在南京的苏联友人，为庆祝中苏两国正式建立外交关系，于五日共同举行盛大的庆祝会。会场主席台上高悬列宁、斯大林、毛主席、朱总司令大幅画像，以及中苏两国国旗。会上，南京军管会副主任唐亮将军代表各界致词，他指出：苏联人民的道路就是中国人民的道路，中国人民认清苏联是中国人民最真挚的朋友。我们坚决拥护中苏友好合作，并站在以苏联为首的国际和平民主阵营，向战争贩子作斗争。苏联友人史白夫致词中，热情地庆祝中苏外交关系的建立，庆祝中国人民的伟大胜利。他指出：现在全中国已快彻底解放。中华人民共和国和中央人民政府已经成立，中国人民领袖毛泽东已当选为中央人民政府主席。这是具有世界历史意义的大事。会后，当人民解放军第二野战军、第三野战军、南京军管会、各民主党派、各机关、各人民团体等四十四个单位二万余人庆祝中苏人民友好的游行队伍经过会场时，中苏两方人士齐声欢呼：“斯大林大元帅万岁！”“中国人民领袖毛主席万岁！”“中苏人民友好万岁！”各机关、各人民团体、各民主党派代表于行经大会主席台前时，又纷纷向苏联友人献花、献旗，并向斯大林大元帅献信。掌声和欢呼声，响成一片。二十二年来，南京人民蕴蓄在内心的对于苏联友人的热爱，这时尽情倾吐出来了。夜晚，苏联友人邀请南京各界代表举行鸡尾酒会，宾主热烈为“中苏友好团结”而干杯。
