Title: Czech Writers' Association and Engineers' Association Telegraph Guo Moruo to Congratulate New China
Author: Yang De'erda
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st.

The Czech Writers' Association and Engineers' Association
Telegraph Guo Moruo to Congratulate New China

[Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st dispatch] The Chairman of the Czech Writers' Association, Derda, sent a telegram to Guo Moruo congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

To Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Writers' Association. On the occasion of the founding of the democratic Chinese government, the Czech Writers' Association extends its warmest fraternal congratulations to you and to all progressive Chinese writers who have fought and are fighting alongside the entire Chinese people for a free and democratic China. The establishment of this government marks the completion of the great victory of the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Mao Zedong. The founding of the new democratic Chinese government creates new conditions under which new prospects for the writers of your great heroic nation are unfolding. Your country, after a long and arduous anti-fascist and anti-imperialist struggle, has won the love and admiration of the world. Czech writers, who have always held the deepest sympathy for China, its people, and its culture, join you today in rejoicing and congratulating you on achieving the greatest success in rebuilding and constructing a new democratic China. This China will stand with all progressive people around the world and fight side by side to safeguard and strengthen world peace. Chairman of the Czech Writers' Association, Derda.

[Another dispatch] Derda also sent a letter to Guo Moruo, expressing his gratitude for inviting him to participate in the founding大会 of the Chinese Peace Committee and congratulating the establishment of the Chinese branch. The letter said:

Dear Comrade Guo Moruo: I sincerely thank you for inviting me to Beijing to participate in the Peace Congress. I am truly very sorry that I am unable to accept this invitation at this time due to being busy with work. I hope to be able to visit your country in the future. I deeply admire the fighting children of your country. Your contribution to the work of defending world peace and ultimately liberating all mankind is enormous. The establishment of the new People's Republic of China is a great victory for the peace camp. I sincerely congratulate you on the establishment of the new People's Republic of China. I extend my heartfelt congratulations to your Congress and wish it complete success.

Long live the world peace camp!

Long live the Soviet Union and the wise Stalin!

Long live the People's Republic of China and its great leader Mao Zedong!

　　　　　　　　　　　　Yang De'erda

[Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 2nd dispatch] The Czechoslovak Engineers and Technicians Association sent a telegram congratulating the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The congratulatory telegram reads as follows: To the Government of the People's Republic of China in Beijing:

Czechoslovak engineers and technicians extend warm greetings to the Chinese people, their government headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, and the entire working people of new China, especially the victorious army of engineers and technicians, and congratulate you on breaking free from the shackles of domestic and foreign capitalists, and celebrate the founding of your free and democratic People's Republic of China. We sincerely invite Chinese democratic engineers and technicians to participate in the大会 to be held in Brno, Czechoslovakia, on November 4, 1949.

　　　　Czechoslovak Engineers and Technicians Association

### 捷克作家协会及工程师协会  电郭沫若庆贺新中国
扬·德尔达
1949-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    捷克作家协会及工程师协会
    电郭沫若庆贺新中国
    【新华社布拉格一日电】捷克作家协会主席德尔达致电郭沫若庆贺中华人民共和国的成立，电文如下：
    致中国作家协会主席郭沫若。值此民主的中国政府成立之际，捷克作家协会向你和曾经并正在与全体中国人民一起为了自由民主中国而作战的一切进步中国作家的致以最热烈的兄弟祝贺。这个政府的成立标志着中国人民在以毛泽东为首的中国共产党领导下的伟大胜利的完成。新的民主的中国政府的成立，创设了新的条件，在这种条件下，你们伟大的英雄国家的作家们的新的前景展开了。你们的国家经过了长时的残酷的反法西斯主义与反帝国主义的斗争，赢得了全世界的热爱与羡慕。对于中国及其人民与文化一贯抱最深同情的捷克作家们今天和你们一起，欢欣鼓舞，祝贺你们在重建与建设新的民主的中国上获得最伟大的成功。这个中国将与全世界一切进步人民站在一起，并肩作战以保障和加强世界和平。捷作家协会主席德尔达。
    【又讯】德尔达还给郭沫若一封信，对邀请他参加中国和平分会成立大会表示感谢并祝贺中国分会的成立。该函说：
    亲爱的郭沫若同志：你邀我到北京参加和平大会，我真诚地表示感谢。我真是非常抱歉，现在因为工作繁忙不能接受这次邀请。我希望以后能拜访你们的国家。对于你们国家的战斗中的子女，我深为崇敬。你们对保卫世界和平与最后解放全人类的工作的贡献是巨大的，新的中华人民共和国的建立乃是和平阵营的一大胜利，我向你衷心庆贺新中华人民共和国的建立。我向你们的大会表示衷心的贺忱并祝圆满成功。
    世界和平阵营万岁！
    苏联及英明的斯大林万岁！
    中华人民共和国及其伟大领袖毛泽东万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　扬·德尔达
    【新华社布拉格二日电】捷克斯洛伐克工程师及技师协会电贺中华人民共和国中央人民政府，祝贺电文如下：北京中华人民共和国政府：
    捷克斯洛伐克工程师及技师向中国人民，他们的以毛泽东同志为首的政府以及新中国全体劳动人民特别是工程师和技师的胜利大军致热烈的敬礼，并庆贺你们摆脱了本国和外国的资本家的枷锁，庆祝你们自由民主的中华人民共和国的成立。我们诚挚地邀请中国民主工程师和技师参加一九四九年十一月四日在捷克斯洛伐克布尔诺城举行的大会。
　　　　捷克斯洛伐克工程师及技师协会
