Title: Cai Chang and Liu Ningyi Visit Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3

　　Cai Chang and Liu Ningyi Visit Korean Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th] Cai Chang, Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, visited Park Chung-ae and Choi Ho-min, representatives of the Korean delegation attending the China Peace Congress, today and yesterday respectively. Since arriving in Beijing on the 2nd of this month, the Korean delegation has not only participated in the China Peace Congress, the inaugural meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Tianjin International Day of Struggle for Peace rally, but has also visited famous historical sites such as the Forbidden City, the Summer Palace, and the Temple of Heaven.

### 蔡畅、刘宁一访朝鲜代表团

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　蔡畅、刘宁一访朝鲜代表团
    【新华社北京七日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅和中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，今天及昨天曾分别访问来北京参加中国保卫世界和平大会的朝鲜代表团朴正爱、崔浩敏两代表。朝鲜代表团自本月二日到达北京后，除参加了中国保卫世界和平大会、中苏友好协会成立大会及天津市的国际和平斗争日纪念大会外，并游历了故宫、颐和园、天坛等名胜古迹。
