Title: Qiqihar and Andong People of All Circles Celebrate Chairman Mao with Jubilation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 2nd. The people of Qiqihar, the capital of Heilongjiang Province, and Andong, the capital of Liaodong Province, warmly celebrated the formal establishment of the Central People's Government and the election of Chairman Mao Zedong. On the evening of the 30th, after the Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station broadcast the good news of the establishment of the Central People's Government and the election of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Qiqihar City Public Security Bureau sounded its sirens for ten minutes to announce the good news to the city. Immediately afterwards, bells, bugles, and celebratory salutes rang out everywhere. People from government offices, troops, factories, schools, and families poured into the streets one after another, parading and celebrating with jubilation. Cheers and the sound of gongs and drums continued until midnight. In Andong, after receiving the good news on the evening of the 30th, the 250,000 people of the city also rejoiced for half the night. At 9:00 a.m. on the 1st, the city's sirens sounded together, and the sounds of salutes, firecrackers, gongs and drums, and shouts of "Long Live Chairman Mao!" resounded as one. Yangko teams of workers and students appeared on the streets one after another. Every street was filled with jubilant crowds, and everyone rejoiced for the whole day. In the evening, more than 30,000 young people held a grand bonfire party in the railway station square.

### 齐齐哈尔、安东各界人民　 狂欢庆贺毛主席

1949-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　齐齐哈尔、安东各界人民
   　狂欢庆贺毛主席
    【新华社沈阳二日电】黑龙江省会齐齐哈尔市和辽东省会安东市的全体人民，热烈庆祝中央人民政府的正式成立和毛泽东主席的当选。三十日晚，北京新华广播电台播出中央人民政府成立和毛泽东主席当选的喜讯后，齐齐哈尔市公安局连鸣汽笛十分钟，向全市报喜。各处随即响起钟声、军号声和庆祝的礼炮声。机关、部队、工厂、学校、家庭里的人们，都先后涌现街头，列队游行，狂欢庆祝。欢呼声、锣鼓声，直至午夜不息。安东市在三十日晚得到喜讯后，全市二十五万人民也欢腾了半夜。一日晨九时，全市汽笛齐鸣，礼炮、鞭炮、锣鼓和“毛主席万岁！”的欢呼声响成一片。工人、学生们的秧歌队纷纷出现街头。每条街道都为狂欢的人群所挤满，大家整整欢乐了一天。入夜，三万多青年又在车站广场举行盛大的营火晚会。
