Title: Central China Military Region Eliminates Over 100,000 Bandits in Five Months
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 27th: The Central China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, in the five months from May to September, eliminated over 100,000 bandits within its territory, shattering the illusion of the Kuomintang remnant bandit gangs to "establish guerrilla bases behind enemy lines." Currently, the major bandit groups in the core areas and key transportation routes of Henan Province (most of it) and the three provinces of Hubei, Hunan, and Jiangxi have been wiped out. The remaining scattered bandits fleeing to the border areas between provinces and mountainous regions are being continuously searched and suppressed by the People's Liberation Army. According to statistics from May, June, July, August, and September, a total of 100,2357 bandits were eliminated in the entire region, including 6,633 killed or wounded, 43,895 captured, and 51,929 who surrendered or registered for a new start. Regarding the weapons seized, excluding the months of May and June, in the three months of July, August, and September alone, the total included 206 cannons of various types, 245 heavy machine guns, 1,760 light machine guns, 98,204 various rifles, 9,177 pistols, 38,851 hand grenades, 14,225 artillery shells, and 2,784,380 rounds of ammunition.

### 华中军区五个月  消灭土匪十万余

1949-10-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　华中军区五个月　
    消灭土匪十万余
    【新华社汉口二十七日电】人民解放军华中军区自五月至九月的五个月中，剿灭境内土匪十万余名，粉碎了国民党残余匪帮所谓“建立敌后游击根据地”的幻梦。目前包括河南省的大部和湖北、湖南、江西三省的腹心地区及交通要道的成股土匪均已肃清，其流窜各省边缘区及山区的部分散匪亦正被人民解放军继续搜剿中。据五、六、七、八、九五个月的统计，全区共计剿灭土匪十万零二千三百五十七名，其中毙伤六千六百三十三名，俘四万三千八百九十五名，匪投诚和登记自新者共五万一千九百二十九名。缴获武器，除五、六两月份未计外，七、八、九三个月内计各种炮二百零六门，重机枪二百四十五挺，轻机枪一千七百六十挺，各种长枪九万八千二百零四枝，短枪九千一百七十七枝，手榴弹三万八千八百五十一枚，炮弹一万四千二百二十五发，子弹二百七十八万四千三百八十发。
