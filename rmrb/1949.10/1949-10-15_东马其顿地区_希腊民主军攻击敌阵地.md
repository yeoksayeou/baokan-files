Title: Eastern Macedonia Region: Greek Democratic Army Attacks Enemy Positions
Author:
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 3
　　
　　Eastern Macedonia Region
    Greek Democratic Army Attacks Enemy Positions
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th] TASS Bucharest, 11th dispatch quoting a broadcast from Free Greece Radio: The Greek Democratic Army victoriously repelled an attack by reinforcements of the Royal Fascist Army in the Anboya region. The Democratic Army is actively operating on the island of Sesos. In eastern Macedonia, the Democratic Army is attacking the enemy's heavily fortified positions. On the 5th, the Democratic Army attacked several high grounds in the Drolia region.

### 东马其顿地区　希腊民主军攻击敌阵地

1949-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　东马其顿地区
    希腊民主军攻击敌阵地
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特十一日讯引自由希腊电台广播：希腊民主军在安保亚地区胜利地击退了保皇法西斯军增援部队的袭击。民主军正在塞索斯岛积极活动。在东马其顿，民主军正向敌军强固设防的阵地进攻。五日，民主军攻击德罗利亚地区的若干高地。
