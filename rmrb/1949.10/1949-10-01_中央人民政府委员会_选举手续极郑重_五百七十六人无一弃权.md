Title: Central People's Government Committee: Election Procedures Extremely Solemn; 576 People, None Abstained

Author:

Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th. The election of the Central People's Government Committee by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference today was conducted by secret ballot with multiple candidates, as decided by the plenary session on the 29th. The list of candidates, proposed by the Presidium after days of careful consultation, was unanimously approved by the plenary session today. According to the provisions of the electoral law, although the list of candidates has been approved, the representatives still have the full right to abandon any one or more persons on the list and hold another election. The election procedures were extremely solemn. Liu Shaoqi, Li Lisan, Sha Qianli, Liang Xi, and Sheng Pihua, the executive chairmen of today's meeting, served as the general supervisors of the election, and sixty people were designated by the Presidium as ballot supervisors. A total of 578 representatives had the right to vote at this meeting, but because two seats for overseas Chinese representatives were not filled, the actual number of voters was 576. The results of the vote showed that no one abstained and there were no invalid ballots. At the beginning of the election, the executive chairman counted the number of people and issued the ballots accordingly. Nine ballot boxes were set up next to the rostrum. Before voting, the executive chairman checked and locked them. After the voters voted in batches, the executive chairman opened the ballot boxes in public, checked the number of ballots, and handed the ballots to the ballot supervisors, who divided them into twenty groups to count the ballots simultaneously. ### 中央人民政府委员会 　选举手续极郑重 　五百七十六人无一弃权

1949-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　 中央人民政府委员会
   　选举手续极郑重
   　五百七十六人无一弃权
    【新华社北京三十日电】今日人民政协关于中央人民政府委员会的选举，是根据二十九日全体会议决定以无记名联记办法进行投票的。由主席团经连日慎重协商后提出的候选名单，今日经全体会议一致通过。按照选举法的规定，候选名单虽经通过，代表们在选举时仍然有完全的权利舍弃名单中的任何一人或若干人而另行选举。选举的手续极为郑重。本日会议的执行主席刘少奇、李立三、沙千里、梁希、盛丕华担任选举总监督，另由主席团指定六十人监票。此次会议有选举权的代表共五百七十八人，但因海外华侨代表有两名名额没有补足，所以实际投票的实为五百七十六人。开票结果表明投票者无一人弃权，亦无一张废票。选举开始时，由执行主席清点人数后照数发票。主席台旁设票箱九个，投票之前由执行主席验看加锁，在投票者分批投票入箱后，执行主席当众开启票箱，核对票数无误，即将选票交监票人分二十组同时进行开票。监票人名单如下：
