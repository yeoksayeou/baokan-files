Title: North Korean Vocational General Alliance Prepares to Participate in the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 3

　　[This News Report] According to reports, the North Korean Vocational General Alliance (General Federation of Trade Unions) is actively preparing to participate in the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Representative Conference to be held in Beijing. The Vocational General Alliance convened a meeting of trade union activists for this purpose. At the meeting, everyone unanimously expressed support for the World Federation of Trade Unions' resolution and decided to greet the convening of the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Representative Conference with the practical action of completing the two-year economic plan ahead of schedule. According to Gao Zanfu, Minister of Culture of the Vocational General Alliance, on the 19th, the workers of North Korea are preparing a rich and comprehensive exhibition. They are also studying the production of exquisite handicrafts to be presented to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu.

### 北朝鲜职业总同盟筹备参加亚澳职工会

1949-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜职业总同盟筹备参加亚澳职工会
    【本报讯】据北朝鲜职业总同盟（总工会）正积极准备参加即将在北京召开的亚澳职工代表会议。职业总同盟曾为此召集一次职工积极分子大会，会上，全体一致表示拥护世界工联的决议，并决定以提前完成二年经济计划的实际行动，来迎接亚澳职工代表会议的召开。据职业总同盟文化部长高赞甫十九日宣称：北朝鲜的工人，正在准备一个内容丰富的展览会。并正在研究制作一种精巧的工艺作物，准备送给毛主席和朱总司令。
