Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Tonight: Recordings of Speeches by International Friends at the Inaugural Meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 1

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Tonight:
    Recordings of Speeches by International Friends at the Inaugural Meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    [Our Newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast recordings of speeches delivered by Comrade Dubrovina, representative of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation; Comrade Spano, representative of the Italian people and member of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party; and Park Jung-ai, of the Korean People's Delegation at the inaugural meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at 20:50 today. At 18:30, there will be a teaching session for the National Anthem of the People's Republic of China (both programs will be broadcast simultaneously on channels one and two of our station).

### 北京新华广播电台今晚播送　中苏友好协会成立大会上  国际友人演说录音

1949-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台今晚播送
    中苏友好协会成立大会上
    国际友人演说录音
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天二十点五十分，播送苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团代表杜伯洛维娜、意大利人民代表意共中央委员斯巴诺，朝鲜人民代表团朴正爱在中苏友好协会成立大会上致词的录音。十八点三十分教唱中华人民共和国国歌（以上节目本台第二台同时联播）。
