Title: China Establishes the World Peace Congress, National Committee Officially Elected, Vice Chairman Zhu Announces Soviet Union's Formal Recognition of China, Audience Erupts in Thunderous Applause and Cheers, International Friends Spano and Others Deliver Speeches, All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Other Groups Present Gifts to International Friends
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: The China World Peace Congress today elected the National Committee and issued a declaration. When Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government announced the news that the Soviet Union had formally recognized the People's Republic of China before his speech, the entire venue immediately erupted in thunderous applause and cheers. Executive Chairmen of the Congress Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Guo Moruo, Liu Ningyi, Qian Junrui, Li Dequan, and government personnel, as well as representatives of various parties and groups including Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Wu Yuzhang, Xu Teli, and Shen Junru successively approached the Soviet delegation's seats and shook hands with Fadeyev, Simonov, and other representatives, immersing the entire venue in unprecedented jubilation for over ten minutes. International friends who delivered speeches at the congress today included Italian Senator and Central Committee Member of the Italian Communist Party Spano, Professor Gerasimov of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation, and Park Chung-ae, Chief Representative of the Korean People's Delegation and Vice Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Support of World Peace. From the Chinese side, speakers included Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman Qian Sanqiang of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Member of the Central People's Government and Overseas Chinese Chen Jiageng, People's Liberation Army Combat Hero Ma Ning, and Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Famous Peking Opera Actor Mei Lanfang. Speakers unanimously pointed out that the strength of the peace camp under the leadership of the great Soviet Union is unprecedentedly strong, and that the people of the world, united, will surely give the imperialist instigators of war the severe punishment they deserve.

The Congress passed the Declaration of the China World Peace Congress and elected 140 members of the China World Peace Committee. Guo Moruo was elected Chairman, and Liu Ningyi, Cai Chang, Liao Chengzhi, Shen Yanbing, and Ma Yinchu were elected Vice Chairmen. Liu Ningyi also serves as Secretary-General (the Declaration and the list of Committee members are in separate articles).

During the speech program, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other groups presented flowers and gifts to the Congress and the Soviet, Korean, and Italian delegations. Professor Stoletoff, Doctor of Biological Sciences and representative of the Soviet delegation, delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the Soviet delegation. In addition to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the groups presenting the gifts included the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Youth Federation , the All-China Federation of Students, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. These presentations filled the venue with the close friendship of the world's peace-loving people.

Finally, Guo Moruo delivered the closing speech, and the congress concluded amidst the strains of the Internationale.

### 中国保卫世界和平大会成立 　全国委员会正式选出  朱副主席宣布苏联正式承认我国全场爆发雷鸣一样的掌声和欢呼  国际友人斯巴诺等发表演说全总等团体向国际友人献礼

1949-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国保卫世界和平大会成立
    全国委员会正式选出
    朱副主席宣布苏联正式承认我国全场爆发雷鸣一样的掌声和欢呼
    国际友人斯巴诺等发表演说全总等团体向国际友人献礼
    【新华社北京三日电】中国保卫世界和平大会今日选出了全国委员会，并发表了宣言。当中央人民政府副主席朱德在演说之前宣布苏联已经正式承认中华人民共和国的消息时，全场立即爆发出雷鸣一样的掌声和欢呼。大会执行主席宋庆龄、林伯渠、郭沫若、刘宁一、钱俊瑞、李德全和政府人员以及各党派各团体代表董必武、李济深、何香凝、吴玉章、徐特立、沈钧儒等相继走到苏联代表席前，和法捷耶夫、西蒙诺夫等代表们一一握手，使全场浸润在空前的狂欢中达十多分钟之久。今日在大会上演说的国际友人有意大利参议员和意共中央委员斯巴诺、苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团格拉西莫夫教授和朝鲜人民代表团首席代表朝鲜拥护世界和平全国委员会副委员长朴正爱，中国方面有中央人民政府副主席朱德、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、中央人民政府副主席李济深、中华全国民主青年联合总会副主席钱三强、中央人民政府委员海外华侨陈嘉庚、人民解放军战斗英雄马宁、和人民政协全国委员会委员著名京剧演员梅兰芳，演说者一致指出在伟大的苏联领导下的和平阵营力量已空前强大，全世界人民团结起来一定能给煽动战争的帝国主义者以应得的严重惩罚。
    大会通过了中国保卫世界和平大会宣言，选举了中国保卫世界和平委员会委员一百四十人，这个委员会以郭沫若为主席，刘宁一、蔡畅、廖承志、沈雁冰、马寅初为副主席，刘宁一兼任秘书长（宣言和委员会名单见另条）。
    在进行演讲的程序中，中华全国总工会等团体向大会和苏联、朝鲜的代表团和意大利代表献鲜花和礼物。苏联代表生物科学博士斯托雷托夫教授代表苏联代表团致谢词，献礼的团体除总工会外，还有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、北京市总工会。这些献礼的节目使会场充满了世界和平人民的亲密友情。
    最后由郭沫若致闭幕词，大会乃在国际歌的乐声中闭幕。
