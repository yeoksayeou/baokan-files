Title: Vyshinsky Holds Press Conference at Lake Success, Issues Statement Answering Reporters' Questions
Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th - TASS, New York: On October 18th, Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation to the UN General Assembly, held a press conference at the United Nations Secretary-General's office at Lake Success. Approximately 300 people attended the press conference, including all UN-based journalists, diplomatic correspondents and political commentators from major American newspapers and news agencies. The press conference lasted one hour and fifteen minutes. The conference was conducted in an atmosphere of exceptionally high interest. Vyshinsky issued a statement to reporters and answered some questions posed by the reporters.

### 维辛斯基在成功湖举行记者招待会　发表声明答复记者询问

1949-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　维辛斯基在成功湖举行记者招待会
  　发表声明答复记者询问
    【新华社北京二十四日电】据塔斯社纽约讯：十月十八日，苏联出席联大代表团团长维辛斯基在成功湖联合国秘书长办公处举行记者招待会。出席记者招待会的约有三百人，包括所有的驻联合国记者，美国各大报纸及通讯社的外交记者和政治评论员。记者招待会历时一时十五分钟。招待会在异常高度关注的气氛中进行。维辛斯基向记者发表了声明，并答复了记者提出的一些问题。
