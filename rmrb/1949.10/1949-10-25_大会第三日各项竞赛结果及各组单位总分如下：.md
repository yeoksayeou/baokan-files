Title: Results of the Third Day of the Games and Total Scores of Each Group
Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 4

The results of the various competitions on the third day of the games and the total scores of each group are as follows:

    Women's Workers Group: 200-meter Medley Relay: 1. Xinhua Printing Factory, 10 points; 2. Renli, 8 points; 3. Telecommunications Bureau, 6 points; 4. Qinghe Woolen Mill, 4 points; 5. Shijingshan Power Plant, 2 points. Time: 30.1 seconds.

    Men's Workers Group: 800-meter Relay: 1. Telecommunications Bureau, 10 points; 2. Tram Company, 8 points; 3. Post Office, 6 points; 4. 70th Ordnance Factory, 4 points; 5. Qinghe Woolen Mill, 2 points. Time: 1 minute 48.2 seconds.

    Women's Senior Group: 400-meter Relay: 1. Normal University; 2. Fu Jen Girls' High School; 3. Attached High School of Normal University; 4. Baiman; 5. Fu Jen University. Time: 59.8 seconds. Total Unit Scores: 1. Fu Jen Girls' High School, 22 points; 2. Normal University, 21 points; 3. Attached High School of Normal University, 11 points; 4. Chongci, 4 points (second place); 5. Girls' No. 2 Middle School, 4 points (third place).

    Men's University Group: 400 meters: 1. Normal University; 2. Fu Jen; 3. Tsinghua; 4. Yenching; 5. Politics and Law. Time: 47.4 seconds. Total Unit Scores: 1. Normal University, 53 points; 2. Fu Jen University, 28 points; 3. Tsinghua, 9 points; 4. Jiaotong University, 4 points; 5. Yenching, 4 points.

    Men's Elementary School Group: Total Unit Scores: 1. Dongcheng Rotary, 10 points; 2. Attached Elementary School No. 1 of Huiyuan, 8 points; 3. Huiwen Elementary School No. 1, 6 points; 4. Attached Elementary School No. 1 of Normal University, 4 points; 5. Nanxinhua Street, 2 points.

    Women's Elementary School Group: Total Unit Scores: 1. Attached Elementary School No. 1 of Normal University, 10 points; 2. Nancaochang, 8 points; 3. Huiwen Elementary School, 6 points; 4. Dongcheng Rotary, 4 points; 5. Wudinghou, 2 points.

    (Total unit scores for the Men's Intermediate and Junior Groups can be found in yesterday's newspapers)

    Basketball Exhibition Match: Beijing United won against the Shanghai Visiting Delegation, 56 to 32.

    Soccer Exhibition Match: Beijing United tied with Shenyang, 0 to 0.
