Title: Soviet Women Representatives Depart for Beijing to Participate in Preparatory Work for Asian Women's Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: TASS Moscow dispatch: Representatives of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and members of the preparatory committee for the Asian Women's Representative Conference to be held in early December this year, left Moscow for Beijing on October 5th. The Soviet participants in the preparatory work for the conference include: Tamara Morozova, a university teacher; Kuliba Toktomanjetova, editor of a children's newspaper in the Kyrgyz Soviet Socialist Republic; and Nina Yeremeyeva Gorozova, assistant secretary of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee. Toktomanjetova and Gorozova are also members of the Women's Situation Investigation Committee for Colonial and Dependent Countries, affiliated with the Women's International Democratic Federation.

### 苏妇女代表启程来京参加筹备亚洲妇代会

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏妇女代表启程来京参加筹备亚洲妇代会
    【新华社北京六日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会代表、参加今年十二月初举行的亚洲妇女代表会议的筹委会的委员们，已于十月五日离莫斯科前往北京。苏联方面参加代表会议筹备工作的有：高等学校教师塔玛拉·莫罗佐娃，吉尔吉斯苏维埃社会主义共和国儿童报纸编辑库里巴·托克托曼捷托娃，以及苏联妇女反法西斯委员会助理书记尼娜·耶列梅耶娃·哥罗佐娃和托克托曼捷托娃两人为国际民主妇联所属殖民地及附属国家妇女情况调查委员会委员。
