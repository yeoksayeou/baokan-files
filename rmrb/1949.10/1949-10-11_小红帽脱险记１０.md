Title: Little Red Riding Hood's Escape 10
Author: Mei Zhi
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 6

　　Little Red Riding Hood's Escape 10
　　"I also had a dream:
　　I built a big garden,
　　Bigger than our China,
　　Wider than our Earth.
　　Inside,
　　Stones would bloom,
　　Mud would sing,
　　Ants would build houses,
　　Frogs would beat big gongs,

　　Old people's hair wouldn't turn white,
　　Teeth wouldn't fall out,
　　Children would race on trains,
　　Ride airplanes across the river,
　　Working would be more enjoyable than eating candy,
　　Farming would be more joyful than celebrating the New Year,
　　People wouldn't bully people,
　　People wouldn't exploit people,
　　Everyone would smile when they meet,
　　There would be no frightening sounds of guns and cannons,
　　Nor would there be bastards everywhere dragging off conscripts.

　　"My dream isn't finished yet,
　　The old mountain demon is coming to eat me,
　　It doesn't matter if I'm eaten,
　　But if my dream is eaten,
　　That would be too heartbreaking,
　　That would be too heartbreaking!

　　"I'm still so small,
　　Not as tall as a tree,
　　Not as strong as an ox,
　　I can only sit here and cry,
　　Letting my tears flow down my chin.
　　Ah,
　　Mom!..."

　　"Ha!
　　It's that old mountain demon,
　　That old bastard, old rogue,
　　Is he awake?
　　Is he going to eat you?
　　It doesn't matter!
　　It doesn't matter!
　　Your ambition is high,
　　Your dream is good.
　　Quickly, don't cry,
　　Red warriors
　　Never shed tears, never fear.

　　"Don't think you're too small,
　　Not as tall as a tree,
　　Don't think I'm too small,
　　Not as strong as an ox,
　　One plus one equals two,
　　Then there is strength, there is strength!
　　Ten times ten equals a hundred,
　　Then there is even more strength, even more strength!
　　That's right,
　　--Unity is strength!
　　Haven't you heard the students sing like that?

　　"I forge iron early,
　　Forge iron late,
　　Forge a sickle
　　To harvest wheat,
　　Forge an axe
　　To chop firewood,
　　Forge a plow
　　To cultivate the earth,
　　Forge a hammer
　　To open mountain heads,
　　Forge a shovel,
　　To open wide roads.

　　"One plus one,
　　Equals two,
　　Ten times ten,
　　Equals a hundred,
　　I pull the bellows,
　　You add coal,
　　I hold the tongs,
　　You strike the hammer,
　　Forge a knife,
　　To kill the old mountain demon."

　　Right!
　　Forge a knife,
　　To kill the old mountain demon!

　　"Thank you,
　　I'm not crying anymore,
　　Thank you,
　　I'm happy now.
　　But don't be like the yellow dog, the white horse, and the bee again,
　　Saying nice things,
　　And then changing your mind after getting something good."

　　"You're really a child,
　　You're really a child,
　　How can you not tell the difference?
　　I'm not a little beast raised by the old mountain demon,
　　The old mountain demon is still my great enemy!
　　He bit off my father's leg,
　　Stole the ox that plowed our fields,
　　And stole the big flowered pig we raised for the New Year,
　　And conveniently bit off one of my mother's hands!
　　For you,
　　I will kill him,
　　To get rid of this scourge!
　　For me,
　　I will kill him,
　　To avenge this hatred!

　　"Come,
　　Hurry up!
　　It's getting dark,
　　We have to hurry,
　　Hurry up and forge the knife,
　　To kill the old mountain demon!"

　　The bellows fanned up blood-red flames,
　　Rusty iron turned into blood-red chunks.
　　Whoosh whoosh,
　　Clang clang,
　　The bellows fanned desperately,
　　The hammer pounded desperately.

　　Whoosh whoosh,
　　Clang clang,
　　The bellows fanned desperately,
　　The hammer pounded desperately.

　　A sharp and bright knife,
　　In a moment,
　　Is forged!
　　A sharp and bright knife,
　　In a moment,
　　Is forged!

　　At this time,
　　The old mountain demon,
　　Limping,
　　Hunching his back,
　　Shedding tears,
　　Hobbling,
　　Came up the hillside.

　　The old mountain demon,
　　Holding incense in one hand,
　　Holding flowers in the other,
　　And shouting loudly:
　　"Little Red Riding Hood,
　　You little devil,
　　Your friends don't want you anymore,
　　This time you can't escape!"
　　He threw the flowers to the side of the hut,
　　The bees immediately flew to them,
　　Their butts wiggling as they sucked with great enthusiasm,
　　As if there were no friends,
　　And no enemies in the world.

### 小红帽脱险记１０
梅志
1949-10-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　小红帽脱险记１０
    “我还做过一个梦：
    我造了一座大花园，
    比我们中国还要大，
    比我们地球还要宽。
    那里面
    石头会开花，
    泥巴会唱歌，
    蚂蚁会造房子，
    青蛙会打大锣，
　
    老年人头发不会白，
    牙齿不会落，
    小孩子骑火车赛跑，
    坐飞机过河，
    做工比吃糖还有味，
    种地比过年还快活，
    人不欺负人，
    人不剥削人，
    大家见面笑嘻嘻，
    没有了枪炮的吓人声，
    也没有混蛋到处拉壮丁。
　
    “我的梦还没有完，
    老山妖就要来吃我，
    吃掉了我不要紧，
    吃掉了我的梦，
    那实在太伤心，
    那实在太伤心！
　
    “我还这么小，
    没有高得象一棵树，
    没有壮得象一条牛，
    只好坐在这里哭，
    让我的眼泪往下巴上流。
    啊，
    妈呀！………”
　
    “呵！
    是那老山妖，
    那老混蛋，老流氓，
    他睡醒了么？
    他要来把你吃掉吗？
    不要紧！
    不要紧！
    你的志气高，
    你的梦做得好。
    快快不要哭，
    红色的战士
    是从来不流泪，不害怕的。
　
    “不要以为你太小，
    没有一棵树那么高，
    不要以为我太小，
    没有一条牛那么壮，
    一加一等于二，
    就有力量，有力量！
    十乘十等于百，
    就更有力量，更有力量！
    对了，
    ——团结就是力量！
    未必你没有听见学生们这样唱？
　
    “我早打铁，
    晚打铁，
    打把镰力
    割麦子，
    打把斧头
    砍柴烧，
    打把犁头
    耕地球，
    打把锤子
    辟山头，
    打把铲子，
    开大路。
　
    “一加一，
    等于二，
    十乘十，
    等于百，
    我拉风箱
    你加煤，
    我掌钳子
    你打锤，
    打把刀，
    杀掉老山妖。”
　
    对！
    打把刀，
    杀掉老山妖！
　
    “谢谢你，
    我不哭了，
    谢谢你，
    我快活了。
    但你不要又象黄狗、白马、和蜜蜂，
    说到说得蛮好听，
    吃到了好的东西就又变了心。”
　
    “你真是小孩子，
    你真是小孩子，
    怎么这样分不清？
    我不是老山妖喂养的小畜牲，
    老山妖还是我的大仇人！
    他咬断了我爸爸的一条腿，
    抢走了我家耕田的牛，
    还抢走我家喂来过年的大花猪，
    顺便咬掉了我妈妈的一只手！
    为了你，
    我要杀掉他，
    才能除掉这个害！
    为了我，
    我要杀掉他，
    才能报掉这个仇！
　
    “来，
    快点来！
    天要黑了，
    我们得趁早，
    趁早打好刀，
    杀掉老山妖！”
　
    风箱扇起了血红的火焰，
    锈铁化成了血红的块块。
    呼啦呼啦，
    叮当叮当，
    风箱拼命的扇，
    铁锤拼命的敲。
　
    呼啦呼啦，
    叮当叮当，
    风箱拚命的扇，
    铁锤拚命的敲。
　
    一把又快又亮的刀，
    一会儿，
    打好了！
    一把又快又亮的刀，
    一会儿，
    打好了！
　
    这时候，
    老山妖，
    跛着脚，
    弓着背，
    流着眼泪，
    一步一拐的
    走上山坡来了。
　
    老山妖，
    一手拿着香火，
    一手拿着鲜花，
    口里还大声的叫：
    “小红帽，
    你这小鬼头，
    你的朋友都不要你了，
    这回你再也逃不掉！”
    他把鲜花抛到了茅屋边，
    蜜蜂马上飞到了那上面，
    屁股一颠一颠的吸得很起劲，
    好象世界上没有什么朋友，
    也没有什么敌人。
