Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Congratulates New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 4th: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Beijing:

Dear Mao Zedong:

The Bulgarian Communist Party sends you a telegram to extend warm congratulations to the fraternal Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people on the proclamation of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the All-China People's Government led by you—the renowned leader of the Chinese people's liberation movement.

Our Party and the entire working people of Bulgaria, nurtured by the spirit of Dimitrov—a spirit of true internationalism—view with great concern and enthusiasm the heroic victories of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Bulgarian people consider the Chinese people's struggle against the reactionary traitorous clique of Chiang Kai-shek and its allied imperialists as their own struggle.

The proclamation of the People's Republic of China is a new and great contribution to the peace and democracy camp and at the same time a heavy blow to the imperialists and the instigators of new wars of aggression. This has created conditions for the Chinese people, in a very decisive way, to begin to move towards socialism. This new victory of the democratic camp led by the Soviet Union will give new strength and confidence to the oppressed people struggling for freedom and independence.

Long live the People's Republic of China and its government!

Long live the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong!

Long live the great Stalin, the leader and teacher of all progressive people!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party

### 保共中央电贺新中国

1949-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　保共中央电贺新中国
    【新华社北京四日电】保加利亚共产党中央委员会本日致电中国共产党中央委员会，庆贺中华人民共和国的成立。电文如下：
    北京中共中央委员会
    亲爱的毛泽东：
    保加利亚共产党致电给你，对中华人民共和国的公告以及由你——中国人民解放运动的著名领袖——所领导的全中国人民政府的产生，向兄弟般的中国共产党及中国人民致热烈的祝贺。　
    为季米特洛夫的精神——一个真正国际主义的精神——所抚育的我们党和保加利亚的整个劳动人民，对于中国人民和中国人民解放军的英雄的胜利，予以很大的关切和热忱。保国人民认为中国人民反对蒋介石反动卖国集团及其同盟帝国主义者的斗争亦是他们自己的斗争。
    中华人民共和国的公告，对于和平民主阵线是一个新的伟大的贡献，而同时亦是对于帝国主义者和新掠夺战争的挑拨者们的一个沉重的打击，这就为中国人民非常具决定性地产生了条件以开始走向社会主义。由苏联领导的民主阵营的这个新的胜利将对争取自由与独立的被压迫人民给以新的力量与信心。
    中华人民共和国及其政府万岁！
    中国共产党及毛泽东主席万岁！
    一切进步人民的领袖和导师——伟大的斯大林万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　保加利亚共产党中央委员会
