Title: Welcoming – The People's Republic of China
Author: Wang Yaping
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 5

　　Welcoming – The People's Republic of China
　　Overture
　　We stand beside the roaring machines,
　　We stand in the fields full of grains and beans,
　　We stand on the front lines of victorious advance,
　　We stand on the hilltops, city towers, and river bends,
　　with red flags flying high,
　　And before the streets lit with torches,
　　Together we raise forest-like arms,
　　Millions of cheers,
　　Millions of hearts surging with victory,
　　Welcoming—the people's own
　　People's Republic of China!

　　I. The Days of Suffering Are Over
　　In the cheers of the masses,
　　On the liberated land,
　　On this day of turning the wheels of history,
　　We have ended the days of suffering.
　　Workers, having liberated their factories, liberated
　　the productive forces that had been shackled for years.
　　They joyfully produce and work,
　　Polishing the oil-stained machines.
　　They dig out scrap copper, rusty iron, steel rails, and odd machine parts...
　　From the soil,
　　Rebuilding the bridges, machines, and railways destroyed by the enemy,
　　Re-equipping the dilapidated locomotives—
　　Creating the Mao Zedong, Beijing, and Nanjing locomotives...
　　Leading the newly painted trains
　　"To the beautiful world!" (Worker's poem)
　　Farmers, from the landlord's cattle sheds,
　　From the exploitation of usury and loan sharks,
　　Liberated themselves, their tools of production,
　　And the land nourished by their blood and sweat.
　　Wheat ripens in May, autumn harvest in August,
　　The threshing grounds are piled high with stacks of grains and beans,
　　Ground into flour, milled into rice,
　　Every household eating fragrant rice porridge and steamed buns,
　　Smiling faces, hearts filled with joy.
　　Soldiers, from the blockaded and besieged areas,
　　From the years of guerrilla warfare, eating millet and gnawing on wild vegetables,
　　Liberated themselves, liberated towns and villages,
　　Liberated the starving and freezing people in dire straits.
　　Thousands of troops march into the passes,
　　Soldiers from the five major liberated areas converge in the Central Plains,
　　Like a waterfall cascading down the mountain,
　　Like a whistling wind sweeping away fallen leaves,
　　Crossing the Yellow River, crossing the Yangtze River, one victory after another,
　　Annihilating the reactionary armies,
　　Occupying the humiliated and trampled land.
　　Students, journalists, writers, doctors, all free professionals,
　　From classrooms forcing the reading of scriptures,
　　From the mire of idealism,
　　From the GMD's surveillance, inspection, tracking, killing, bayonet
　　knife threats,
　　Liberate their lives and thoughts.
　　Merchants, overseas Chinese, ethnic minorities...
　　With the liberation of the motherland's people,
　　Their status has been elevated,
　　They proudly gaze at the newborn China—
　　From years of long-term imprisonment
　　Standing up stubbornly
　　Marching towards freedom, peace, and prosperity.

　　II. Slicing Open the Floodgates of the Old Society
　　“The cypress loses its leaves,
　　The stone roller gets a soft core,
　　But the world will never change!”
　　Roll away! All you fatalists,
　　Lies of political swindlers!
　　We see clearly, see accurately,
　　The Communist Party, the people's leader Mao Zedong
　　Leads the people of the whole country
　　Using the giant axe of Marxism-Leninism
　　Slices open the floodgates of the old society.
　　Revolution, bringing light, heat, and warmth,
　　Illuminates the blood-stained land,
　　Warms the hungry villages and cold cities,
　　Everything old and rotten collapses,
　　All war criminals, feudal landlords, reactionary rulers,
　　And the American imperialist aggressors wearing tiger skins,
　　All fall from their rotten thrones,
　　Falling into a pile of mud!
　　The workers and peasants who have turned over,
　　All the people who have been deprived of their means of production,
　　All stand up from their factories and fields,
　　Standing tall with chests puffed out,
　　Pouring in from all directions,
　　Cheering and surging,
　　They say—
　　The world originally belongs to our creation,
　　We have the right to manage this world!
　　Look!—
　　The landlord's luxurious buildings and tiled houses,
　　Are now inhabited by the working tenants and poor peasants;
　　The factories of bureaucratic capitalists,
　　Are now managed by the people's state;
　　The whistles of countless factories,
　　Echo the joyful songs of the workers.
　　Cheers, shake
　　The forests of the five great mountains;
　　The sun, shines on the
　　Rushing currents of the Yangtze River, the Pearl River, and the Yellow River;
　　Spring, walks into China,
　　Everywhere blooming with beautiful flowers.

　　Those lying down rise,
　　The backward move forward,
　　People, society, history,
　　All undergo earth-shattering changes.

　　III. Welcoming the People's Republic!
　　Stepping over the edge of the old history,
　　Standing at the starting point of the new history,
　　Welcome!
　　Welcome the great People's Republic of China!

　　October 1, 1949,
　　From this day on
　　We have a solemn and beautiful national flag,—
　　The red light fills the sky and the earth,
　　Overflowing with revolutionary joy,
　　Four small yellow stars surround a large star,
　　It symbolizes the people of the whole country
　　Uniting closely under the leadership of the Communist Party.

　　Cheer!
　　Facing the national flag that represents the essence of the Chinese people!
　　The people who love freedom,
　　The people who love labor,
　　Fight tirelessly forever.
　　Salute!
　　Facing the helmsman who steers the wheel of history—Chairman Mao!
　　The practitioner of Marxism-Leninism,
　　The savior of the suffering people,
　　The mentor of the Chinese proletarian revolution!

　　We—the people of the whole country
　　With unshakable, unquenchable confidence,
　　With love as high as the mountains and as deep as the sea,
　　Welcome the young China!
　　Welcome the era of construction!

　　The Chinese working class
　　Will use light and heavy machinery,
　　Develop underground resources, create the people's welfare;
　　Farmers will overcome natural disasters and floods...
　　On the wide and fertile land
　　Operate tractors and harvesters;
　　Our long and winding coastline,
　　The blue and serene sky,
　　Will be guarded by a strong navy and air force.
　　The fruits of the revolution,
　　Will never again be swallowed by robbers!
　　The territory of the People's Republic,
　　Will never again be trampled by invaders!

　　Epilogue
　　We hold hands, our hearts are linked,
　　Our ranks are like steel,
　　We are like a moving train,
　　Advance! Move forward!
　　No turning back! No resting!

　　Our slogan—Victory!
　　Let all those entrenched in feudal fortresses,
　　Fawning and smiling at American imperialism,
　　Using bayonets to threaten the people's interests,
　　Domestic and foreign reactionary enemies
　　Tremble before us!
　　If they dare to resist,
　　Let them be destroyed under the banner of revolution!

　　Listen! Chairman Mao's voice:
　　"We have stood up!"
　　"Announcing the establishment of the People's Republic of China!
　　Our revolution has gained the sympathy and cheers of the broad masses of people all over the world,
　　Our friends are all over the world."

　　Welcome!
　　We are qualified, courageous, and passionate
　　To welcome the birth of the new China!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　1949, September 30, Beijing

### 迎接—中华人民共和国
王亚平
1949-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　迎接—中华人民共和国
　　序曲
    我们站在机器轰响的旁边，
    我们站在生满谷豆的田野，
    我们站在胜利进军的前线，
    我们站在高插着红旗的山顶、城楼、河湾
    与亮着火把的街前，
    一齐举起森林似的手臂，
    千万声欢呼，
    千万颗被胜利冲激的心情，
    迎接——人民自己底
    中华人民共和国！
  　一、苦难的日子结束了
    在群众的欢呼声里，
    在解放了的土地上，
    在扭转历史车轮的今天，
    我们结束了苦难的日子。
    工人，解放了自己的工厂，解放了
    多少年被束缚着的生产力。
    他们欢悦地生产、工作
    擦亮了油渍的机器。
    他们把废铜、烂铁、钢轨、零星的机件……
    从泥土里掘出来，
    把敌人破坏的桥梁、机器、铁路重新修起，
    把破旧的火车头重新装备，——
    创造了毛泽东号、北京号、南京号……
    带着新涂着油漆的列车
    “开向美丽的世界”！（工人诗句）
    农民，从地主的牛马棚里，
    从高利贷、印子钱的盘剥底下
    解放了自己、生产工具、
    和血汗喂养过的土地。
    五月麦熟，八月里秋收，
    场园里堆满了一垛一垛的谷豆，
    磨成面，碾成米，
    家家户户吃着香喷喷的米粥、馍馍，
    面上笑，心里填满了欢乐。
    战士，从封锁、围困的地带，
    从打游击、吃小米啃野菜的岁月，
    解放了自己，解放了城镇与乡村，
    解放了饥寒交迫水深火热的人民。
    千军万马开进关，
    五大解放区的战士会师中原，
    象山上的瀑布向下倾泻，
    象呼啦啦的大风扫落叶，
    过黄河、渡长江，一个胜利接着一个胜利，
    消灭了反动派的军队，
    占领了被污辱与被践踏的土地。
    学生、记者、作家、医师，一切自由职业者
    从强迫读经书的课堂，
    从唯心论者的泥沼，
    从国特的监视、检查、追踪、杀害、手枪刺
    刀威胁下，
    解放自己的生活和思想。
    商人、侨胞、少数民族……
    随着祖国人民的解放，
    提高了自己的地位，
    他们骄傲地望着新生的中国——
    从长期禁锢着的岁月里
    倔强地站起来
    走向自由、和平、富强的道路。
　　二、劈开了旧社会的闸门
    “柏树落了叶，
    石磙烫了瓤，
    世界也不会改变！”
    滚开吧！一切宿命论者，
    政治骗子底鬼话！
    我们看得清楚，看得准，
    共产党、人民领袖毛泽东
    带领着全国的人民
    用马克思、列宁主义的巨斧
    劈开了旧社会的闸门。
    革命，挟来了光、热、温暖，
    照亮了血迹斑斑的土地，
    温暖了饥饿的村庄与寒冷的城市，
    一切衰老、腐败的崩溃了，
    一切战犯、封建地主、反动统治者，
    和穿着虎皮的美帝侵略者，
    都从他们腐烂的宝座上跌下来，
    跌成了一堆烂泥！
    翻身的工人、农民，
    一切曾被剥夺了生产工具的人们，
    都从自己底工厂、田园里
    挺着胸脯站起
    从四面八方地涌来，
    欢呼着，奔腾着，
    他们说——
    世界原属我们创造，
    我们有权管理这个世界！
    看哪！——
    地主享乐的楼房瓦屋，
    住进了劳动的佃户、贫农；
    官僚资本家的工厂，
    让人民的国家经营；
    多少工厂里的汽笛，
    迎合着工人愉快的歌声。
    欢呼，震动了
    五大山岳的林木；
    太阳，在长江、珠江、黄河的
    奔流上照耀；
    春天，走进了中国，
    到处开着鲜美的花朵。
　  躺着的起来，
    落后的向前，
    人、社会、历史
    都起了翻天覆地的变革。
　  三、迎接人民共和国！
　  跨过旧历史的边沿，
    站在新历史的起点，
    迎接吧！
    迎接伟大的中华人民共和国！
　  一九四九年十月一日，
    从这一天起
    我们有了一个庄严而美丽的国旗，——
    满天满地的红光，
    洋溢着革命的喜悦，
    四颗黄色的小星拱围着一颗大星，
    它象征着全国的人民
    在共产党领导下紧密地团结。
　  欢呼吧！
    面向代表中国人民特质的国旗！
    爱自由的人民，
    爱好劳动的人民，
    永远地战斗不息。
    敬礼吧！
    面向掌握历史车轮的舵手——毛主席！
    马列主义的实践者，
    苦难人民的救星，
    中国无产阶级革命的导师！
　  我们——全国的人民
    用颠不倒、扑不灭的信心，
    用山样高海样深的热爱，
    迎接年青的中国！
    迎接建设的年代！
　  中国的工人阶级
    将会使用轻重机器，
    开发地下的富源，创造人民的福利；
    农民要战胜天灾、水患……
    在宽阔而肥美的土地上
    开动拖拉机与收获机；
    我们漫长弯曲的海岸，
    蓝静静的天空，
    将有强大的海军、空军守卫。
    革命的果实，
    再不许强盗们吞食！
    人民共和国的国土，
    再不许侵略者踏足！
　　尾声
　  我们手拉手、心连心，
    我们的队伍象钢铁，
    我们象开动的列车，
    向前进！向前开！
    不回头！不休歇！
　  我们的口号——胜利！
    让那一切盘踞在封建堡垒上的，
    向美帝献媚卖笑，
    用刺刀威胁人民利益的
    国内国外的反动敌人
    在我们面前发抖吧！
    如果他们胆敢反抗，
    就叫他们在革命的旗帜下毁灭！
　  听吧！毛主席的声音：
    “我们已经站立起来了！”
    “宣布中华人民共和国成立了！
    我们的革命已经获得全世界广大人民的同
    情和欢呼，
    我们的朋友遍于全世界。”
    迎接吧！
    我们有资格、有勇气、有热情
    迎接新中国的诞生！
              一九四九、九月三十日、北京
