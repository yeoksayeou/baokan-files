Title: Frontline Our Army Firmly States to Arrest White Bandit War Criminals Who Released Poison Gas
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 12th. The remnant troops of the White Bandit Chongxi are releasing poison gas on a large scale. On the 6th of this month, when a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army launched an attack on the bandit troops' 87th Division and 171st Division in Changtangpu and Niushantang areas west of Hengyang, the bandit troops actually released tear gas in an attempt to prevent the rapid advance of the People's Liberation Army. Upon hearing the news, the People's Liberation Army on all fronts were extremely angry and firmly stated that they would definitely follow the order of the General Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army to punish war criminals, arrest the war criminals who released poison gas, and severely punish them.

### 前线我军坚决表示捉拿放毒白匪战犯

1949-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　前线我军坚决表示捉拿放毒白匪战犯
    【新华社华中前线十二日电】白匪崇禧残部大肆施放毒气。本月六日，解放军某部于衡阳西长塘铺、牛山塘一带向匪军八十七师、一七一师发起攻击之际，该匪竟施放催泪性毒气，妄图阻止解放军的迅速前进。前线各路解放军闻讯，均极为震怒，坚决表示必定按照解放军总部惩办战争罪犯的命令，捉拿施放毒气的战犯予以严惩。
