Title: "Soviet-Korean Friendship Week" Enthusiastically Carried Out in Various Parts of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th: The "Korean Friendship and Soviet Culture Week" is being enthusiastically carried out in various parts of North Korea. Representatives from all walks of life in Sinuiju in North Pyongan Province and Hamhung in South Hamgyong Province presented wreaths to the graves of Soviet martyrs on the 14th and held memorial ceremonies. On the same day, Li Ki-young, Chairman of the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association, broadcast on the Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Station, expressing gratitude on behalf of all the Korean people to the Soviet army and people who liberated Korea and helped Korea carry out democratic construction. He called on Korean workers, farmers, staff, students, and scientific workers to devote themselves to studying and absorbing the outstanding Soviet science, technology, and culture. He also said: "We must not only strengthen our friendly relations with the great Soviet people, but also strengthen our friendly relations with the People's Republic of China and other people's democratic countries, as well as with freedom-loving people all over the world, so as to more actively launch struggles, smash the colonial policies of US imperialism against our motherland, overthrow the Syngman Rhee bandit gang, and strive for the peaceful reunification of the motherland."

### 朝苏友好旬　北朝鲜各地热烈进行

1949-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝苏友好旬
  　北朝鲜各地热烈进行
    【新华社平壤十九日电】“朝鲜友好和苏联文化旬间”在北朝鲜各地热烈进行中。平安北道的新义州和咸镜南道的咸兴市等地各界代表，于十四日向当地苏军烈士墓呈献花圈，并举行纪念仪式。同日，朝苏文化协会主席李箕永在平壤朝鲜中央广播电台播讲，代表全朝鲜人民向解放朝鲜并帮助朝鲜进行民主建设的苏联军队和人民，表示感谢。他号召朝鲜工人、农民、职员、学生、科学工作者全力研究和吸收卓越的苏联科学技术与文化。他又说：“我们不但要加强和伟大的苏联人民的友好关系，同时也要加强和中华人民共和国及各人民民主国家以及全世界爱好自由人民的友好关系，以便更积极地展开斗争，粉碎美帝国主义对我祖国实行的殖民地政策，打倒李承晚匪帮，争取祖国的和平统一。”
