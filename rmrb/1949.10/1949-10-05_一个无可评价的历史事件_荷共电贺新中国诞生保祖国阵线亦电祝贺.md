Title: An Inestimable Historical Event: Communist Party of the Netherlands Cables Congratulations on the Birth of New China, Fatherland Front Also Sends Congratulations

Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　An Inestimable Historical Event
　　Dutch Communist Party Cables Congratulations on the Birth of New China, Fatherland Front Also Sends Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th Dispatch] Amsterdam News: The Communist Party of the Netherlands recently sent a cable to the Communist Party of China, warmly congratulating the founding of the People’s Republic of China. The congratulatory message stated: The founding of the People's Republic of China is an inestimable historical event for the working-class struggle worldwide. The Dutch working class extends warm greetings to the Communist Party of China and watches the development of the new China with extreme concern.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th Dispatch] Sofia News: The National Committee of the Bulgarian Fatherland Front recently sent a cable to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to congratulate them. The cable read as follows: To the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:
    The National Committee of the Bulgarian Fatherland Front extends warm congratulations to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, which has adopted its Common Program and announced the establishment of the People’s Republic of China. Thanks to the selfless assistance of the great Soviet Union, the Bulgarian people are building the foundations of socialism in their country with the utmost enthusiasm. The Bulgarian people express admiration and complete solidarity with the brave Chinese people’s struggle against imperialist oppression and the terrorist regime of the Chiang Kai-shek reactionary clique. Our people firmly believe that under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of China and its leader Mao Zedong, the Chinese People's Liberation Army will achieve complete victory over the internal and external enemies of the Chinese people. The victory of the Chinese people is the victory of the democratic and progressive forces of all the world, and also a heavy blow to the imperialists and war-mongers. The victory of the great Chinese people in the camp of peace and democracy led by the Soviet Union will give new hope to the struggle of all oppressed nations to liberate themselves from imperialist enslavement and capitalist exploitation, and will give new hope to the struggle for the victory of democracy and socialism worldwide.
    Long live the great Chinese people and their glorious leader Mao Zedong!
    Long live the People’s Republic of China!
    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people!
    Long live the great Soviet Union, the bastion of peace, democracy, and socialism!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The National Committee of the Bulgarian Fatherland Front

### 一个无可评价的历史事件　荷共电贺新中国诞生保祖国阵线亦电祝贺

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　一个无可评价的历史事件
  　荷共电贺新中国诞生保祖国阵线亦电祝贺
    【新华社布拉格四日电】阿姆斯特丹讯：荷兰共产党顷致电中国共产党。热烈祝贺中华人民共和国的成立。贺电称：中华人民共和国的成立，对世界范围的工人阶级斗争来说，是一个无可评价的历史事件。荷兰工人阶级特向中国共产党致热烈的敬礼，他们以极端的关心，注视着新中国的发展。
    【新华社布拉格四日电】索非亚讯：保加利亚祖国阵线全国委员会顷致电中国人民政治协商会议祝贺，电文如下：中国人民政治协商会议鉴：
    保加利亚人民共和国祖国阵线全国委员会向已经通过其共同纲领并宣布成立中华人民共和国的中国人民政治协商会议致热烈祝贺。由于伟大苏联的无私援助，保加利亚人民正以最大的奋励在他们的国家中建设社会主义的基础。保加利亚人民对于勇敢的中国人民反帝国主义压迫与蒋介石反动集团的恐怖政权的斗争，表示钦佩与完全休戚一致。我们的人民坚信，在光荣的中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东的引导之下，中国人民解放军将对中国人民的内外敌人获得完全的胜利。中国人民的胜利就是全世界民主与进步力量的胜利，同时也是对于帝国主义者与战争贩子们的沉重打击。伟大的中国人民在以苏联为首的和平与民主阵营中的胜利，将给予一切被压迫民族自帝国主义奴役与资本主义剥削之下争取解放的斗争以新的希望，将给予争取全世界民主与社会主义胜利的斗争以新的希望。
    伟大的中国人民及其光荣的领袖毛泽东万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中保两国人民间的友谊万岁！
    和平、民主与社会主义的堡垒——伟大的苏联万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　保加利亚祖国阵线全国委员会
