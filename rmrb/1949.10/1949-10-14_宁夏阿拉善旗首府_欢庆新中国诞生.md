Title: Alxa League, Ningxia Celebrates the Birth of New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ningxia, 13th -- People from all walks of life in Dingyuanying, the capital of the Alxa League of the Mongolian Autonomous Region of Ningxia, held a grand gathering on the 6th to celebrate the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and to pay respects to Chairman Mao and the People's Liberation Army.

### 宁夏阿拉善旗首府　欢庆新中国诞生

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　宁夏阿拉善旗首府
    欢庆新中国诞生
    【新华社宁夏十三日电】宁夏蒙古族阿拉善旗首府定远营各界人民曾于六日举行盛大集会，庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立，并向毛主席及人民解放军致敬。
