Title: Our Army Enters Dihua City; Another Unit Arrives at Yanqi and Korla; Bao Erhan Cables Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Reporting the Grand Welcome

Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 21st: A unit of the People's Liberation Army arrived in Dihua on the afternoon of the 20th, and was warmly welcomed by the military and political organs and citizens of Dihua. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army arrived in Yanqi and Korla on the same day. Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Provisional People's Government of Xinjiang Province, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on the 20th, stating:

Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the Central People's Government: The Xinjiang people's preparatory committee for welcoming the People's Liberation Army into Xinjiang has been formed long ago and is working day and night to expedite all welcoming and supply matters. Around 3:00 PM on the 20th, the armored corps of the People's Liberation Army was the first to arrive in Dihua. On that day, people of all ethnic groups and walks of life in Dihua, both men and women, young and old, filled the suburbs to give a warm welcome, their shouts thundering, the scene unprecedented. Respectfully reported.

　　Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Provisional People's Government of Xinjiang Province
　　October 20th

### 我军进驻迪化市　另部到达焉耆及库尔勒  鲍尔汉电毛主席朱总司令报告欢迎盛况

1949-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军进驻迪化市
    另部到达焉耆及库尔勒
    鲍尔汉电毛主席朱总司令报告欢迎盛况
    【新华社北京二十一日电】人民解放军一部，于二十日下午到达迪化，受到迪化军政机关及市民群众的热烈欢迎。人民解放军另一部于同日到达焉耆，库尔勒。新疆临时省政府鲍尔汉主席于二十日致毛主席朱总司令电称：
    中央人民政府毛主席、朱总司令：新疆人民欢迎人民解放军入新筹委会，早就组成，昼夜赶办一切欢迎供应等事宜。二十日午后三时左右，人民解放军装甲兵团首先抵迪。是日，热烈欢迎的迪化各族各界男女老少，充满市郊，呼声雷动，盛况空前。谨闻。　新疆省临时人民政府主席鲍尔汉　十月二十日
