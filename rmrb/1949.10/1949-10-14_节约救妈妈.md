Title: Saving to Save Mom
Author: Sha Minghua
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 6

　　Saving to Save Mom
　　Sha Minghua, 15-year-old female worker at Renli Carpet Factory
　　Farmers are our moms.
　　To give us food, they plant crops,
　　To keep us warm, they plant cotton,
　　Unafraid of wind, unafraid of rain,
　　Toiling year-round, unwilling to use, unwilling to spend,
　　For what? For all of us,
　　Now the rain is heavy, the crops are flooded, Mom is suffering,
　　Our lives depend on Mom,
　　How can we not care for her,
　　As long as we have something, Mom must have something too.
　　You save rice, I save clothing, everyone must save, everyone must think of a way.
　　Save on food and clothing to save the farmers—our moms.

### 节约救妈妈
沙明华
1949-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　节约救妈妈
    仁立地毯厂、十五岁女工  沙明华
    农民是咱们的妈妈。
    为咱们吃饭，他们种了庄稼，
    为咱们不冷，他们种了棉花，
    不怕风吹，不怕雨打，
    终年辛苦，不肯用，不肯花，
    为了什么？为了咱们大家，
    如今雨水大，淹了庄稼，苦了妈妈，
    咱们生活靠妈妈，
    咱们怎能不管他，
    只要有咱们也得有妈妈。
    你省米我省衣，人人要节约，大家想办法。
    省吃省穿救农民——咱们的妈妈。
