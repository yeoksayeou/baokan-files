Title: PLA on the Fujian Front Will Swiftly Liberate Xiamen as a Gift to the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 7th—After the news of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government, and the election of Chairman Mao as Chairman of the Central People's Government reached the Fujian front, all the People's Liberation Army, who were eliminating the remaining bandits, were overjoyed. All commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army on the Xiamen front resolutely stated that they would swiftly liberate Xiamen and annihilate all the remaining bandits as a gift to the founding of the Central People's Government, and resolutely implement the order announced by Commander-in-Chief Zhu De at the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government and follow the solemn guarantees made to the assembly by their representatives, Generals Chen Yi and Su Yu. Gao Banbao, leader of the "July 1st Meritorious Company" of a certain regiment, said, "We will always be the fighting force of the people's motherland, and we will liberate all the territories that have not yet been liberated." When the Ninth Company of the "Ye Ting Troops" heard the good news of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the entire company immediately swore: "Work hard to prepare, fight bravely, take Xiamen, and perform meritorious service for the country."

### 福建前线解放军将迅速解放厦门　献给中央人民政府作贺礼

1949-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　福建前线解放军将迅速解放厦门
    献给中央人民政府作贺礼
    【新华社福建前线七日电】当中华人民共和国和中央人民政府成立，毛主席被选为中央人民政府主席的消息传到福建前线之后，正在肃清残匪的人民解放军全军上下无不欢欣鼓舞。厦门前线的人民解放军全体指战员坚决表示将迅速解放厦门，全歼残匪，作为对中央人民政府成立的贺礼，并坚决执行朱总司令在中央人民政府成立典礼上宣布的命令和遵循自己的代表陈毅、粟裕两将军向大会所作的庄严保证。某团七一功臣连连长高斑保说：“我们永远是人民祖国的战斗队，我们要解放一切尚未解放的国土。”“叶挺部队”九连听到中华人民共和国成立喜讯时，全连立即宣誓：“努力准备，英勇作战，打下夏门，为国家立功。”
