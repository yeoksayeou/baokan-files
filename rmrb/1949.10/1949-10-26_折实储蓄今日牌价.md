Title: Exchange Rate for Indexed Savings Today
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate for Indexed Savings Today
    The Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces that the exchange rate for one standard physical unit of indexed savings today (26th) is 944.29 yuan.
