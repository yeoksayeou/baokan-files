Title: Grateful for the Great Achievements in Promoting the Liberation of Eastern Europe, Governments and Peoples of Eastern European Countries Mourn the Death of Tolbukhin
Author:
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 3

　　Grateful for the Great Achievements in Promoting the Liberation of Eastern Europe
    Eastern European Countries' Governments and Peoples Deeply Mourn Tolbukhin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd] TASS news: The death of Soviet Marshal Tolbukhin has aroused unanimous mourning among the people of Eastern European countries. The Communist Parties, Workers' Parties, parliaments, governments, or armed forces ministries of Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Albania, Germany, Austria, and other countries have sent telegrams to the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, or the Ministry of Armed Forces of the Soviet Union to express their condolences. The people of these countries unanimously and deeply mourned the early death of Marshal Tolbukhin, and deeply appreciated Marshal Tolbukhin's leadership of the Soviet army in liberating the people of Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Austria, and other countries from the iron heel of the Hitlerite bandits, and promoting the great achievements of the liberation of the people of Eastern European countries. The telegrams of condolence guarantee that the glorious memory of Marshal Tolbukhin will never be forgotten, and that the infinite, deep friendship and love of the people of all countries for the Soviet Union and its leader, Stalin, will be further consolidated. The Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, in order to mourn the death of Tolbukhin, specially ordered Bulgaria to observe three days of mourning from the 18th onwards. All theaters, cinemas, and other entertainment venues will suspend entertainment, and all institutions throughout the country will hang flags at half-mast.

### 感激促成东欧解放的丰功伟绩  东欧各国政府与人民沉痛地哀悼托尔布金

1949-10-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　感激促成东欧解放的丰功伟绩
    东欧各国政府与人民沉痛地哀悼托尔布金
    【新华社北京二十二日电】综合塔斯社消息：苏联元帅托尔布金的逝世，引起东欧各国人民的一致哀悼。保、罗、匈、捷、阿、德、奥等国的共产党、工人党、议会、政府或武装部队部、均纷纷致电联共中央、苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议或苏联武装部队部吊唁。各该国人民一致沉痛哀悼托尔布金元帅的早逝，并深深感激托尔布金元帅率领苏军从希特勒匪帮铁蹄下解放了保、罗、匈、奥等国人民，并促成了东欧各国人民解放的丰功伟绩。唁电保证对托尔布金元帅光辉的记忆永志不忘，并使各国人民对苏联及其领袖斯大林的无限深厚的友谊与热爱更加巩固。保加利亚人民共和国部长会议，为了哀悼托尔布金的逝世，特下令保加利亚全国从十八日起致哀三日，一切剧场、电影院及其他娱乐场所均停止娱乐，全国各机关均悬旗致哀。
