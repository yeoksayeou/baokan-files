Title: People's Organizations in Beijing, Hebei, Pingyuan, Suiyuan and other Provinces Send Telegrams Congratulating the Chinese People's Congress for World Peace

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 2

Beijing Municipal Trade Union Preparatory Committee

[Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal Trade Union Preparatory Committee sent a telegram yesterday (2nd) to the inaugural meeting of the China Branch of the World Peace Congress, stating: "On September 28th, you called on all people's organizations and compatriots from all walks of life across the country to unite against wars of aggression and uphold world peace; and decided to formally establish the China Branch of the World Peace Congress on International Peace Day (October 2nd). Our 300,000 workers in Beijing enthusiastically responded to your call and actively participated in the International Peace Day parade.

The Chinese people have always stood on the side of the world's people's peace and democratic camp. The Chinese people, especially the Chinese working class, have made great efforts to strive for lasting world peace. The victory of the Chinese people's liberation movement and the establishment of the People's Republic of China are a great contribution to the world people's peace movement. The new China will play an important role in world affairs, especially in Far Eastern affairs. Therefore, in order to strive for world peace, we must participate actively with our past spirit. Therefore, today (October 2nd), our 300,000 workers in Beijing, in addition to enthusiastically responding to your call and holding various rallies and parades to express the firm confidence of the Beijing working class in defending world peace and opposing new wars of aggression; we also celebrate the establishment of the China Branch of the World Peace Congress. In the future, we are willing to fight with the people of the whole country under the leadership of the branch to strive for lasting world peace."

　　　　People's Organizations in Hebei Province and Baoding City

[Baoding, 2nd Dispatch] People's organizations in Hebei Province and Baoding City, including the trade union, youth, women's federations, and the Preparatory Committee of the Baoding Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, jointly sent a telegram congratulating the establishment of the China Branch of the World Peace Congress. The telegram stated: "As the people's organizations in Hebei Province are enthusiastically celebrating the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, we are even more encouraged to learn of the establishment of the China Branch of the World Peace Congress. Our peace-loving Chinese people have become a strong component of the world's peace-defending forces. We are determined to unite and organize the people of the entire province, together with the people of the whole country, to continue our efforts to completely eliminate the remnants of the reactionaries, cut off the bloody claws extended by the warmongers, and, under the unified leadership of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, resolutely stand on the side of the world peace and democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union, and strive to defend lasting world peace."

　　　　People's Organizations in Pingyuan

[Xinxiang, 1st Dispatch] The Preparatory Committee of the Pingyuan Provincial Trade Union, the Pingyuan Provincial Farmers' Association, the Pingyuan Provincial Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the Preparatory Committee of the Pingyuan Provincial Democratic Women's Federation, the Pingyuan Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Preparatory Committee of the Xinxiang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association sent a telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the China Branch of the World Peace Congress to congratulate them, and stated: "We support the unified leadership of the China Branch, and decided to hold a grand rally and parade on October 2nd to respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, oppose the US imperialist conspiracy to create a new war of aggression, and unite with all nations, countries, and peoples in the world who treat us equally, to strive for lasting world peace."

　　　　Youth Organizations in Suiyuan

[Suiyuan, 2nd Dispatch] The Suiyuan Provincial Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the Preparatory Committee of the Suiyuan Provincial Democratic Youth Federation, and the Preparatory Committee of the Suiyuan Provincial Student Federation sent a telegram to the China Branch of the World Peace Congress, stating: "The youth of Suiyuan congratulate the establishment of the China Branch of the World Peace Congress with unparalleled enthusiasm. The youth of Suiyuan, together with the broad masses, are actively responding to the peace struggle movement and the call of the World Peace Congress, holding demonstrations and parades on October 2nd, and will spare no effort day and night to oppose wars of aggression, oppose the 'North Atlantic Treaty' and the 'Pacific Alliance', and strive for lasting world peace."

### 北京市及河北、平原、绥远等省人民团体　电贺中国拥护世界和平大会

1949-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　北京市及河北、平原、绥远等省人民团体
    电贺中国拥护世界和平大会
    京市总工会筹委会
    【北京讯】北京市总工会筹委会于昨（二）日致电世界拥护和平大会中国分会成立大会，电称：“九月二十八日你们号召全国各人民团体和各界同胞，大家一致起来，反对侵略战争，拥护世界和平；并决定在国际和平日（十月二日）正式成立世界拥护和平大会中国分会。我们北京市三十万工人，热烈响应你们的号召，积极参加国际和平日的游行。
    中国人民向来是站在世界人民和平民主阵营一方面的。中国人民尤其是中国的工人阶级，曾经为争取世界人民持久和平尽过很大的努力。中国人民解放运动的胜利和中华人民共和国的成立，是对于世界人民和平运动一个伟大的贡献。新中国即将在世界事务尤其是在远东事务中起重要的作用。因此，对于争取世界和平，尤须本以往精神，积极参加。所以今天（十月二日）我们北京三十万工人，除热烈响应你们的号召，举行各种集会游行，表示北京工人阶级保卫世界和平，反对世界新的侵略战争的坚强信心外；并庆祝世界拥护和平大会中国分会的成立。今后我们愿在分会领导下，和全国人民一起为争取世界持久和平而斗争。”
    　　　　河北省及保定市人民团体
    【保定二日电】河北省及保定市职工、青年、妇女及中苏友好协会保定分会筹委会等人民团体，联合通电祝贺世界拥护和平大会中国分会成立。电称：“河北省各人民团体正在热烈庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立之际，欣闻世界拥护和平大会中国分会成立，更加鼓舞了我们的斗志。我们爱好和平的中国人民，已成为全世界保卫和平的强有力的组成部分。我们决团结与组织全省人民，与全国人民一道，再接再励，彻底消灭反动派残余，斩断战争贩子们伸出的血爪，在中国共产党和中央人民政府的统一领导下，坚决站在苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营一边，为保卫世界持久的和平而努力。”
    　　　　平原人民团体
    【新乡一日电】平原省总工会筹委会、平原省农会、新民主主义青年团平原省工作委员会、平原省民主妇联筹委会、平原省文学艺术界联合会及中苏友好协会新乡分会筹委会致电世界拥护和平大会中国分会筹委会祝贺，并称：“我们拥护中国分会的统一领导，并决定于十月二日举行盛大的集会游行，来响应世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的号召，反对美帝制造新侵略战争的阴谋，联合世界上以平等待我之民族、国家和人民，为争取世界持久和平而斗争。”
    　　　　绥远青年团体
    【绥远二日电】新民主主义青年团绥远省工作委员会、绥远省民主青年联合会筹备委员会、绥远省学生联合会筹备委员会致电世界拥护和平大会中国分会电称：“绥远青年以无比热忱，祝贺世界拥护和平大会中国分会的成立。绥远青年与广大群众一起，正积极的响应和平斗争运动，响应世界和平大会的号召，在十月二日举行示威游行，并将昼夜不懈全心全力的为反对侵略战争，反对“北大西洋公约”、“太平洋联盟”，争取世界持久和平而竭力奋斗。”
