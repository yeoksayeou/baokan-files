Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Invites All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Other Groups to Prepare for Commemoration of Lu Xun
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Invites All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Other Groups
    To Prepare for Commemoration of Lu Xun
    [Reported by this newspaper] This month, the 19th, marks the 13th anniversary of the death of the great Chinese thinker and writer, Mr. Lu Xun. In order to prepare for the first commemoration of Mr. Lu Xun's death anniversary after the birth of the People's Republic of China, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles specially invited the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, and the Beijing Municipal Party Committee and other units to discuss commemorative matters at the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles headquarters at 6:30 p.m. yesterday (the 13th). Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Ding Ling, Tian Han, Zheng Zhenduo, Zhao Shuli, Sha Kefu, Cao Yu, Xu Beihong, Feng Xuefeng, Xu Guangping, Yang Hansheng, Ai Qing, Huang Yaomian, Hu Feng, and representatives from various units, totaling more than 20 people, attended the meeting. At the meeting, it was decided that the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles would invite relevant units to jointly initiate the establishment of a preparatory committee for the 13th anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's death. Preliminary discussions were also held on the commemorative program. In addition to holding a grand commemorative meeting in the capital, the commemorative activities in schools and factories will be planned and organized separately by each unit.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 全国文联邀全总等团体　筹备纪念鲁迅
柏生
1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国文联邀全总等团体
    筹备纪念鲁迅
    【本报讯】本月十九日为中国伟大的思想家文学家——鲁迅先生逝世十三周年纪念。中国文联为了筹备纪念中华人民共和国诞生后第一个鲁迅先生的忌日，特于昨（十三）日下午六时半在全国文联会址邀请全国总工会、全国民主妇联、全国青联及北京市委等单位共同商讨纪念事宜。到会郭沫若、茅盾、周扬、丁玲、田汉、郑振铎、赵树理、沙可夫、曹禺、徐悲鸿、冯雪峰、许广平、阳翰笙、艾青、黄药眠、胡风及各单位代表共二十余人。会上决定由全国文联邀请各有关单位联合发起成立鲁迅先生逝世十三周年纪念筹备会。并初步讨论了纪念节目，除将在首都举行盛大纪念会外，至各学校各工厂之纪念活动，则由各单位自己分别计划组织。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
