Title: Mongolian Capital Holds Rally to Oppose Shameful Verdict of U.S. Imperialism; Czechoslovak People Demand Immediate Release of U.S. Communist Leaders
Author:
Date: 1949-10-28
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th: TASS Ulaanbaatar, 25th: Mass protest rallies were held by various enterprises in Ulaanbaatar (Kulun), the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, unanimously demanding the immediate release of the U.S. Communist leaders who were imprisoned by an unjust verdict.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th: TASS Prague, 25th: Members of the Czechoslovak Judicial Workers' Union yesterday sent a letter to the U.S. Embassy in Czechoslovakia, protesting the unjust verdict against the eleven U.S. Communist leaders and demanding their immediate release.

### 蒙古首都人民集会  反对美帝可耻判决  捷人民要求立即释放美共领袖

1949-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古首都人民集会　
    反对美帝可耻判决　
    捷人民要求立即释放美共领袖
    【新华社北京二十七日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托二十五日讯：蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托（库伦）各企业举行群众抗议大会，一致要求立刻释放被不公正的判决而囚禁的美共领袖。
    【新华社北京二十七日电】塔斯社布拉格二十五日讯：捷克斯洛伐克司法工作者工会会员，昨日致函美驻捷克大使馆，抗议对十一位美共领袖的不公正的判决，并要求立刻释放。
