Title: Preparing for the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference and the Asian Women's Representatives Conference; Representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the International Democratic Women's Federation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: To participate in the preparatory work for the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference and the Asian Women's Representatives Conference, the delegation of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the representatives of the International Democratic Women's Federation arrived in Beijing this afternoon. The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation includes Berezin, Director of the International Department of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union; Kuzin, Head of the Asian Division of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union; Wickremasinghe, Chairman of the Ceylon Federation of Labour; Be Quy Phuc, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labour; Anna Rosso, representative of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and Yekine, a total of eleven people. The representatives of the International Democratic Women's Federation include Marosova, member of the Asian Women's Issues Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation and teacher at a Soviet university; Toktomanbetiva, editor of a children's newspaper in the Kyrgyz Republic of Soviet Asia; Yeremeyeva, assistant secretary of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee; and Simonne Bertrand, representative of the French Women's Association and member of the Asian Women's Issues Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation. Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, went to Tianjin to welcome them and accompanied them to Beijing. Those who welcomed them at the station included Li Lisan and Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, Vice Chairmen; Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions; and Zhang Xiaomei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation. After the delegation got off the train, they were warmly welcomed by more than one hundred Chinese workers and women representatives holding flowers. Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered welcome speeches at the station. Wickremasinghe of the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, and Marosova and Simonne Bertrand of the International Democratic Women's Federation delivered reply speeches. In the evening, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation hosted a banquet for the representatives at the Beijing Hotel.

### 参加亚澳职工代表会议、亚洲妇女代表会议筹备工作  世界工联代表团、国际民主妇联代表抵京

1949-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加亚澳职工代表会议、亚洲妇女代表会议筹备工作           
    世界工联代表团、国际民主妇联代表抵京
    【新华社北京十二日电】参加亚澳职工代表会议和亚洲妇女代表会议筹备工作的世界工联代表团和国际民主妇女联合会的代表，已于今日下午到达北京。世界工联代表团包括苏联总工会国际部部长别列金、苏联总工会亚洲科科长久金、锡兰总工会主席魏克马沁格、越南总工会主席别久福、世界工联代表安娜·罗素及叶奇涅等十一人；国际民主妇联的代表包括国际民主妇联亚洲妇女问题委员会委员、苏联高等学校教师玛洛索娃、苏联亚洲吉尔吉斯共和国儿童报纸编辑托克托曼别提娃、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会助理书记耶列梅耶娃、法国妇女会代表、国际民主妇联亚洲妇女问题委员会委员西门贝特朗。中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一曾赴津欢迎并陪同来京。到车站欢迎者有中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超、李德全及北京职工总会筹备委员会主任萧明、北京市民主妇女联合会筹备委员会主任张晓梅等。代表团下车后即受到手持鲜花的一百多个中国工人和妇女代表的热烈欢迎。中华全国总工会副主席李立三及中华全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅并即于车站致欢迎词。世界工联代表团魏克马沁格、国际民主妇联代表玛洛索娃及西门贝特朗致答词。晚间中华全国总工会和中华全国民主妇女联合会于北京饭店欢宴各代表。
