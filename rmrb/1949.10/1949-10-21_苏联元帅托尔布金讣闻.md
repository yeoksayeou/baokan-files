Title: Obituary of Soviet Marshal Tolbukhin
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th (TASS): Obituary of the late Soviet Marshal Tolbukhin, the full text is as follows:

　　Marshal of the Soviet Union, the outstanding commander Fyodor Tolbukhin, passed away on October 17th after a long and serious illness.

　　Tolbukhin's death has deprived the Soviet Union and its armed forces of an outstanding leader, a creator of the Soviet armed forces, and a loyal son of our Bolshevik Party who selflessly dedicated himself to the cause of Lenin and Stalin.

　　For more than thirty years, Comrade Tolbukhin dedicated himself to the cause of strengthening the military might of our socialist country and provided an example of flawlessly completing military tasks for the motherland.

　　During the years of foreign military intervention and the civil war, Comrade Tolbukhin held responsible positions in the Red Army and fought selflessly against the enemies of the Soviet state.

　　During the period of socialist peaceful construction, Comrade Tolbukhin devoted great efforts to the sound development of the Soviet army and worked tirelessly to enrich his knowledge of military art.

　　During the years of the Great Patriotic War, Marshal Tolbukhin showed himself to be an outstanding general of the Stalinist military school. He directed armies and fronts with special skill and talent, carrying out the orders and tasks of the Supreme High Command.

　　The armies he led went through a glorious and difficult combat path, from Stalingrad to the Austrian Alps, writing brilliant chapters in our glorious military history. The military operations conducted by the armies commanded by Comrade Tolbukhin were repeatedly pointed out in Stalin's commendation orders.

　　After the end of the Great Patriotic War, Comrade Tolbukhin commanded the troops of the Transcaucasian Military District.

　　The Soviet government highly valued Comrade Tolbukhin's brilliant contributions to the country. Comrade Tolbukhin was awarded the Order of Victory, two Orders of Lenin, three Orders of the Red Banner, two Orders of Suvorov 1st Class, one Order of Kutuzov 1st Class, one Order of the Red Star, and many medals.

　　The Soviet armed forces lowered their flags in mourning before the body of Soviet Marshal Tolbukhin, a patriotic soldier of our country, and all Soviet people deeply mourn his premature death.

　　Tolbukhin's image will forever remain in the memory of our people and in the hearts of the Soviet army.

　　Bulganin, Voroshilov, Vasilevsky, Sokolovsky, Shtemenko, Konev, Govorov, Budyonny, Rokossovsky, Malinovsky, Zhukov, Timoshenko, Meretskov, Yakovlev, Yumashev, Zhigarev, Kuznetsov, Golikov, Malandin, Arinin, Voronov, Bogdanov, Antonov, Yergramyan, Yeryomenko, Petrov, Popov, Zakharov, Chuikov, Maslennikov, Kurasov, Artemyev, Nedelin, Susaykov, Zheldov, Tarasov (signed).

### 苏联元帅托尔布金讣闻

1949-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联元帅托尔布金讣闻
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：故苏联元帅托尔布金讣闻，全文如下：
    苏联元帅，杰出的将领费道尔·托尔布金，在长时间的重病之后，已于十月十七日逝世。
    托尔布金的逝世，使苏联及其武装部队丧失了一位杰出的领袖、苏联武装部队缔造者与忘我地献身于列宁斯大林事业的我们布尔什维克党的忠实儿子。
    三十余年以来，托尔布金同志献身于加强我们社会主义国家的军事力量的事业，并且提供了毫无瑕疵地为祖国完成军事任务的范例。
    在外国军事干涉与国内战争的好几年间，托尔布金同志在红军中担任负责的职位，忘我地对苏维埃国家的敌人作战。
    在社会主义和平建设时期，托尔布金同志对健全苏联军队的事业尽了很大的力量，而且孜孜不倦地工作，以丰富他的军事艺术的知识。
    在伟大爱国战争的几年间，托尔布金元帅显示了他自己是斯大林军事学派的杰出将领。他以特殊的技术与才能指挥军队和战线，执行了最高统帅部的命令和任务。
    他所领导的军队，经历了荣耀与困难的战斗道路，从斯大林格勒城到奥地利的阿尔卑斯山，在我国光荣的军事史上写下了光辉的篇幅。由托尔布金同志指挥的军队所进行的军事行动，屡次为斯大林的嘉奖令所指出。
    伟大的爱国战争结束之后，托尔布金同志即指挥外高加索军区的部队。
    苏联政府对托尔布金同志对国家的辉煌贡献，予以高度的评价。托尔布金同志曾荣获胜利勋章一枚、列宁勋章两枚、红旗勋章三枚、苏沃罗夫一等勋章两枚、库图佐夫一等勋章一枚、红星勋章一枚和许多奖章。
    苏联武装部队在我国的爱国志士，苏联元帅托尔布金灵前降旗致哀，全体苏维埃人民对于他过早的逝世，都深深哀悼。
    托尔布金的音容将永远留在我国人民的记忆里和苏维埃军队的心里。
