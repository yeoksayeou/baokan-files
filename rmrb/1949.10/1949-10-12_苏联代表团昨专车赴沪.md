Title: Soviet Delegation Left for Shanghai by Special Train Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th. The Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation left for Shanghai by special train this afternoon. Teng Daiyuan, Yang Shangkun, Luo Ruiqing, Cai Chang, Zhou Yang, Qian Junrui, Li Dequan, Lai Yali, Ding Ling, Mao Dun, Zheng Zhenduo, Cao Jinghua, Zhang Zhongshi, Ai Qing, and dozens of others were at the station to see them off. Xiao San and Li Bozhao accompanied the delegation. China's cultural troupe participating in the World Youth Congress also traveled to Shanghai on the same train.

### 苏联代表团昨专车赴沪

1949-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联代表团昨专车赴沪
    【新华社北京十一日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团已于今日下午乘专车赴沪，到车站欢送的有滕代远、杨尚昆、罗瑞卿、蔡畅、周扬、钱俊瑞、李德全、赖亚力、丁玲、茅盾、郑振铎、曹靖华、张仲实、艾青等数十人。代表团由萧三、李伯钊两人陪同前往。我国参加世界青年代表大会的文工团，亦同车赴沪。
