Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
  　Tonight's Program Highlights
  [This Newspaper News] Tonight at 18:15, Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will present an employee program featuring an introduction to members of the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference Delegation - "The Malayan Working Class in Struggle." At 19:00, a lecture on natural science common sense, titled: "Causes and Prevention of Blindness." At 18:30, the first literary and artistic program, sponsored by the Chinese Folk Art Improvement Association, features Li Xinghai singing Leting Drum "Three Sons-in-Law Pay Respects." At 22:00, the second literary and artistic program features the People's Art Troupe singing military songs.
  Also on Channel Two, at 17:15, Children's Time: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Children from Fulin Elementary School in Dongcheng District of this city sing. At 17:45, Youth Time: "Introducing the People's Elementary School of Hebei High School." At 18:00, a music program: The North China Military Region Band performs military music. At 20:00, Voice of the Municipality: (1) Notice from the Beijing Municipal People's Government regarding the collection of the second half of 1949 license plate tax starting from October 20th. (2) Proclamation No. 2391 of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Taxation Bureau (Jing Shui San Zi).

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
  【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分职工节目，亚、澳洲职工会代表会议会员团介绍之一——“马来亚工人阶级在斗争中”。十九点自然科学常识讲座，题目是：“瞎眼的原因和预防”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办，李兴海演唱乐亭大鼓“三婿拜寿”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，人民文工团演唱部队歌曲。
  又二台十七时十五分儿童时间：（一）时事讲话，（二）本市东城扶轮小学小朋友唱歌。十七时四十五分青年时间：“介绍河北高中的民众小学”。十八时音乐节目：华北军区音乐队演奏军乐。二十时市政之声：（一）北京市人民政府关于从十月二十号起开征一九四九年下半年使用牌照税的通知。（二）北京市人民政府税务局京税三字第二千三百九十一号布告。
