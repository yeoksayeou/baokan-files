Title: Qingdao City Warmly Hosts Soivet Nationals Remaining in Qingdao
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 2

　　Qingdao City
　　Warmly Hosts Soviet Nationals Remaining in Qingdao
　　[Special dispatch from our newspaper correspondent in Qingdao on the 4th] The people of all circles here welcomed the good news that the Soviet Union was the first to recognize the People's Republic of China and to establish diplomatic relations by exchanging ambassadors, with great excitement and gratitude. The Foreign Affairs Office hosted a banquet for the Soviet nationals in Qingdao. At the reception, they warmly toasted the deep friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. The People's Representatives of all circles applauded endlessly for this great happy event for the Chinese people. Hundreds of representatives shouted "Long live Marshal Stalin!" "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!" The Qingdao Student Union immediately proposed sending a telegram to Marshal Stalin to express their gratitude.

### 青岛市热烈欢宴留青苏侨

1949-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　青岛市　热烈欢宴留青苏侨
    【本报记者青岛四日专电】此间各界人民用极大的兴奋与感激心情来迎接苏联第一个承认中华人民共和国并互派大使、建立外交关系的好消息。外侨事务处欢宴在青岛的苏侨。招待会上为中苏人民的深厚友谊热烈干杯。各界人民代表会为这个中国人民的大喜事，长久不息的鼓掌。数百代表高呼“斯大林大元帅万岁！”“中苏友谊万岁！”青岛学生联合会马上提议向斯大林大元帅致电感谢。
