Title: China People's Insurance Company Tianjin Branch Officially Opens

Author:

Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 2

　　China People's Insurance Company
  　Tianjin Branch Officially Opens
    [By Our Correspondent] The Tianjin branch of the China People's Insurance Company (headquartered in Beijing) officially opened on the 20th. This company is an expansion and reorganization of the original Tianjin China Property Insurance Company and currently handles fire insurance, marine insurance, transportation insurance, war risk insurance, and parcel post insurance. On the opening day, it underwrote a total of 54 clients, with an insured amount of 1.003 billion yuan.

### 中国人民保险公司天津分公司正式开业

1949-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保险公司
  　天津分公司正式开业
    【本报讯】中国人民保险公司（设北京）天津分公司，已于二十日正式开业。该公司系由原天津中国产物保险公司扩大改组而成，现经营火险、水险、运输险、兵险及邮包险等业务。开业之日即共承保五十四户，保险额达十亿零三千余万元。
