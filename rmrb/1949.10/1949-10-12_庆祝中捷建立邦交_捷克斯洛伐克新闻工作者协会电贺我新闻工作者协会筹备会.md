Title: Czechoslovak Journalists Association Sends Congratulations to Preparatory Committee of All-China Journalists Association on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Czechoslovakia
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 11th. On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Czechoslovak Republic and the People's Republic of China, the Czechoslovak Journalists Association sent a congratulatory telegram on the 8th to the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association. The telegram stated:
    The Czechoslovak Journalists Association warmly congratulates Czechoslovakia and the People's Republic of China on the immediate commencement of diplomatic relations. Czechoslovak journalists congratulate their Chinese comrades and look forward to the closest cooperation in the future for the benefit of both countries and peace. Long live the People's Republic of China! Long live Mao Zedong, the great leader and teacher of the Chinese people!

### 庆祝中捷建立邦交  捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者协会电贺我新闻工作者协会筹备会

1949-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中捷建立邦交
    捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者协会电贺我新闻工作者协会筹备会
    【新华社布拉格十一日电】值此捷克斯洛伐克共和国与中华人民共和国建立外交关系之际，捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者协会于八日电中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会祝贺。电文称：
    捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者协会热烈祝贺捷克斯洛伐克和中华人民共和国立即开始外交关系。捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者祝贺他们的中国同志，并期望将来为了两国及和平的利益而最密切地合作。中华人民共和国万岁！中国人民的伟大领袖和导师毛泽东万岁！
