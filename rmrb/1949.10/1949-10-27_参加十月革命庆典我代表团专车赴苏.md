Title: Our Delegation to Attend October Revolution Celebrations Departs for Soviet Union by Special Train
Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th: The delegation of our country to attend the 32nd anniversary celebration of the Great October Revolution in the Soviet Union departed from Beijing by train at 6:00 PM today. The delegation includes representatives from various people's organizations such as workers, peasants, youth, women, culture, education, and science: Ding Ling (head of the delegation), Xu Zhizhen (deputy head), Wu (deputy head), Sha Kefu (secretary-general), Cao Yu (deputy secretary-general), Ding Xielin, Li Fenglian, Li Yong, Xu Guangping, Gong Pusheng, Li Peizhi, Zhao Shuli, Ma Sicong, Bai Yang, Yuan Zhihe, and other fifteen people. Zhu Zhongli, wife of our ambassador to the Soviet Union Wang Jiaxiang, and Xu Peiru, wife of Zeng Yongquan, counselor of the embassy in the Soviet Union, also departed for the Soviet Union on the same train. Those who went to the station to see them off included Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government of our country, Li Lisan, Minister of Labor, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education, Mao Dun, Minister of Culture, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Sanqiang, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Soviet Ambassador to our country, Roshchin, the embassy counselor Tikhvinsky, Simonov, head of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation, Berezin, the Soviet representative to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, and over a hundred other people. Our delegation plans to stay in the Soviet Union for a month. In addition to participating in the grand celebration of the October Revolution anniversary, they will also conduct visits. The women representatives will also prepare to attend the council meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

### 参加十月革命庆典我代表团专车赴苏

1949-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加十月革命庆典我代表团专车赴苏
    【新华社北京二十六日电】我国参加苏联伟大十月革命三十二周年纪念典礼的代表团已于今日下午六时由京乘车首途。代表团包括工人、农民、青年、妇女、文化、教育、科学各人民团体的代表丁玲（代表团团长）、许之桢（副团长）、吴（副团长）、沙可夫（秘书长）、曹禺（副秘书长）、丁燮林、李凤莲、李永、许广平、龚普生、李培之、赵树理、马思聪、白杨、袁致和等十五人。我国驻苏大使王稼祥夫人朱仲丽及驻苏大使馆参赞曾涌泉夫人徐沛如亦同车赴苏。前往车站送行的有我国中央人民政府政务院副总理兼文化教育委员会主任郭沫若、劳动部部长李立三、教育部部长马叙伦、文化部部长茅盾、中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、中苏友好协会总干事钱俊瑞、中华全国民主青年联合会常委钱三强、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全和苏联驻我国大使罗申、大使馆参赞齐赫文斯基、苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团团长西蒙诺夫、苏联出席亚澳职工代表会议代表别列金等百余人。我国代表团准备在苏联逗留一月，除参加十月革命纪念盛典外，并将分别进行参观。其中妇女代表并准备出席国际民主妇联理事会。
