Title: National Journalists Association Preparatory Committee Held Second Meeting Yesterday: Summarizing this Year's Experience is the Main Task of the National Journalists Association Inaugural Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 7th: The second meeting of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association was held in Beijing at 9:30 AM yesterday. The following preparatory committee members attended: Wang Kangzhi, Wang Yunsheng, Li Huang, Meng Qiujiang, Jin Zhonghua, Zhou Qin'yue, Shao Zonghan, Lin Zhongyi, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Pu Xixiu, Xu Ying, Xu Zhucheng, Xu Maijin, Chen Kehan, Chen Hanbo, Mei Yi, Zhang Pan, Huan Xiang, Lu Yi, Qiao Guanhua, Yun Yiqun, Yang Gang, Liao Chengzhi, Liao Mosha, Zhao Chaogou, Liu Baiyu, Liu Simu, Liu Zunqi, Sa Kongliao, and thirty others, as well as Pan Zinian and forty-two other journalists from various places in Beijing who were invited to attend. The meeting was chaired by Hu Qiaomu, Director of the Preparatory Committee. Standing Committee members Sa Kongliao, Liu Zunqi, and Huan Xiang reported on the progress of the preparatory work of the Association's General Office and local branches, as well as the drafting process of the Association's draft charter. During the discussion, Hu Qiaomu, Chen Kehan, and Sa Kongliao expressed their opinions on the publicity, organization, and management of news work. Hu Qiaomu believed that 1949 was a year of great transformation in national news work and a great convergence of national democratic journalists, and summarizing the experience of this year should become the main task of the National Journalists Association inaugural meeting to be held in January next year. The Association has successively established branches in Wuhan, Nanjing, Beijing, Xi'an, Harbin, Shanghai, Shenyang, and Hangzhou, and branches will be established successively in Tianjin, Lanzhou, Fuzhou, Jinan, Baoding, Taiyuan, Suiyuan, Xinxiang, and the Third Field Army. The Standing Committee of the Association hopes that local branches and preparatory committees should immediately carry out the registration of journalists and the election of representatives, strengthen the connection between the branches and the General Office, and collect materials, and submit the results of the above tasks to the Standing Committee before December this year, so as to convene the National Congress as scheduled in January next year.

### 全国新闻工作者协会筹备会　昨举行二次会议  总结今年经验是全国新闻工作者协会成立会的主要任务

1949-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国新闻工作者协会筹备会
    昨举行二次会议
    总结今年经验是全国新闻工作者协会成立会的主要任务
    【新华社北京七日电】中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会第二次会议，七日上午九时半在北京举行，出席筹备委员王亢之、王芸生、李荒、孟秋江、金仲华、周钦岳、邵宗汉、林仲易、胡乔木、胡愈之、浦熙修、徐盈、徐铸成、徐迈进、陈克寒、陈翰伯、梅益、张磐石、宦乡、陆诒、乔冠华、恽逸群、杨刚、廖承志、廖沫沙、赵超构、刘白羽、刘思慕、刘尊棋、萨空了等三十人及被邀列席的各地在京新闻界人士潘梓年等四十三人。会议由筹备会主任胡乔木任主席，由常委萨空了、刘尊棋、宦乡分别报告协会筹备会总会及各地分会筹备工作进行的情况，以及协会章程草案的起草经过。在讨论中，胡乔木、陈克寒、萨空了分别就新闻工作中的宣传、组织、经营等问题发表意见，胡乔木认为一九四九年是全国新闻工作大转变和全国民主新闻工作者大汇合的一年，总结这一年的经验应该成为明年一月举行的全国新闻工作者协会成立会的主要任务。按该会已先后在武汉、南京、北京、西安、哈尔滨、上海、沈阳和杭州等八地成立了分会，其他天津、兰州、福州、济南、保定、太原、绥远、新乡及第三野战军等处，都将陆续成立分会。该会常委会希望各地分会及筹委应立即进行新闻工作者的登记和代表的选举，加强分会与总会的联系，和收集资料等工作，并于今年十二月以前将上述各项工作结果汇交常委会，以便明年一月如期召开全国代表大会。
