Title: Hengyang, Qujiang Resume Postal Service with Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

　　Hengyang, Qujiang Resume Postal Service with Shanghai
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th] The newly liberated Hengyang and Qujiang have resumed postal service with Shanghai.
