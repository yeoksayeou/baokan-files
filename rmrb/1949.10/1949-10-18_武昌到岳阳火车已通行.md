Title: Wuchang to Yueyang Trains Now Running
Author:
Date: 1949-10-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 16th: The Wuchang to Yueyang section of the Guangdong-Hankou Railway resumed operation on the 14th. This section was damaged by the Chiang Kai-shek and Bai Chongxi bandit gangs. After day and night repairs by the railway corps stationed in the area and the railway workers, all the large and small bridges that had been destroyed have been completely repaired.

### 武昌到岳阳火车已通行

1949-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　武昌到岳阳火车已通行
    【新华社长沙十六日电】粤汉铁路武昌至岳阳段已于十四日起恢复通车。该段曾遭蒋白匪帮的破坏，经驻该地铁道兵团和铁路职工们不分昼夜抢修，已将被破坏的大小桥梁全部修复。
