Title: Hengyang Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 1

　　Hengyang Military Control Commission Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 15th] The Hengyang Municipal Military Control Commission was formally established on the 12th, upon the order of the People's Liberation Army Central China Military Region. Li Zhongquan is the Director of the Military Control Commission, and Liu Chang and Mao Yuanyao are the Deputy Directors. All have taken office and are working.
    The Hengyang Garrison Command and Political Department were also formally established on the 13th upon the order of the Central China Military Region. Xiao Fuquan is the Commander, Mao Yuanyao is the First Political Commissar, Li Zhencong is the Second Political Commissar, and Zhao Jingyuan is the Director of the Political Department.

### 衡阳军管会成立

1949-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　衡阳军管会成立
    【新华社汉口十五日电】衡阳市军事管制委员会，已奉人民解放军华中军区命令于十二日正式成立，李中权为军管会主任，刘昌、毛远耀为副主任，均已到职工作。
    衡阳市警备司令部、政治部亦于十三日奉华中军区命令正式成立，萧福全为司令员，毛远耀为第一政治委员，李振聪为第二政治委员，赵靖远为政治部主任。
