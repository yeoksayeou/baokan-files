Title: Beijing Municipal Bureau of Industry and Commerce Dissolved; Industry Bureau and Commerce Bureau Established; Zhao Zhensheng Appointed Director of Municipal Finance and Economics Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 4

　　[Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal People's Government's Industry Bureau and Commerce Bureau have been formally established, and the original Industry and Commerce Bureau and Enterprise Bureau have been dissolved. The Industry Bureau is composed of the original Enterprise Bureau and the industrial part of the original Industry and Commerce Bureau. The Commerce Bureau is the commercial part of the original Industry and Commerce Bureau. The North China People's Government has appointed Cheng Hongyi as Director of the Commerce Bureau and Peng Cheng as Deputy Director of the Bureau; Mou Zexian as Director of the Industry Bureau and Jia Tingsan as Deputy Director of the Bureau. As of today, the Industry Bureau will be located at 21 Songshu Hutong and the Commerce Bureau will be located at 6 Xinda Road, Gong'an Street.
　　[Further News] The Beijing Municipal People's Government received orders from the North China People's Government on September 29th, Minzhengzi No. 221 and No. 225: "Appointing Zhao Zhensheng as Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Finance and Economics Committee, Cheng Hongyi as Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Commerce Bureau and concurrently Deputy Director of the Finance and Economics Committee, Peng Cheng as Deputy Director of the Commerce Bureau, Mou Zexian as Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Industry Bureau, Jia Tingsan as Deputy Director, appointing Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, concurrently as Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau, appointing Shi Huaibi, Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Land Administration Bureau, concurrently as First Deputy Director of the Labor Bureau, appointing Ma Guangdou as Second Deputy Director of the Labor Bureau, appointing Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, concurrently as Director of the Municipal Government's Public Enemy Property Custody Bureau, appointing Tong Zheng as Deputy Director of the Public Enemy Property Custody Bureau, and appointing Ma Zhongying as Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Tax Bureau." They have all assumed their positions and are performing their duties. (Municipal Government News Office)

### 京市府工商局企业局结束　工业局商业局成立  赵振声任市财经委会主任

1949-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市府工商局企业局结束
    工业局商业局成立
    赵振声任市财经委会主任
    【本市讯】北京市人民政府工业局与商业局顷已正式成立，原工商局与企业局两单位宣告结束。工业局系由原企业局与原工商局的主管工业部分组成，商业局即系原工商局的主管商业部分。华北人民政府顷已任命程宏毅为商业局局长、彭城为该局副局长；牟泽衔为工业局局长、贾廷三为该局副局长。自即日起，工业局在松树胡同二十一号、商业局在公安街新大路六号办公。
    【又讯】北京市人民政府奉华北人民政府九月二十九日民政字第二二一号和二二五号令：“任命赵振声为北京市人民政府财政经济委员会主任、程宏毅为北京市人民政府商业局局长兼财经委员会副主任、彭城为商业局副局长、牟泽衔为北京市人民政府工业局局长、贾廷三为副局长、派北京市人民政府副市长张友渔兼任北京市人民政府劳动局局长、派北京市人民政府地政局局长史怀璧兼任劳动局第一副局长、任命马光斗为劳动局第二副局长、派北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正兼该市府公逆产清管局局长、委任佟铮为公逆产清管局副局长、应马中为北京市人民政府税务局副局长”。他们均已到职视事。　　　　　（市府新闻处）
