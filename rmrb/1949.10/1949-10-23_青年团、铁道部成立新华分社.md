Title: Communist Youth League, Ministry of Railways Establish Xinhua Branch Offices
Author:
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 4

　　The Communist Youth League, Ministry of Railways

  　Establish Xinhua Branch Offices
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 21st: The Xinhua News Agency's New Democratic Youth League branch office was established today and officially begins publishing immediately. Yang Shu, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, concurrently serves as the director of the branch office. The current main tasks of the branch office are to comprehensively report on the dynamics of the national youth movement and the activities of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the All-China Youth Federation, and the Student Federation. The provincial committees of the Communist Youth League will designate special correspondents to report to the branch office on the activities of the Communist Youth League and the youth movement in their respective regions.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 21st: The Xinhua News Agency's Ministry of Railways branch office was established a few days ago. Its main tasks are to report on the national railway construction situation, the important measures of the Ministry of Railways, and the living conditions of railway employees.

### 青年团、铁道部成立新华分社

1949-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年团、铁道部
  　成立新华分社
    【新华社北京二十一日电】新华社新民主主义青年团分社于今日成立，即日正式发稿，由青年团中央宣传部部长杨述兼任分社社长。该分社目前主要工作为综合报道全国青年运动动态和青年团中央和全国青联、学联的活动。青年团各省委将指定特约记者，负责向分社报道有关该地区青年团的活动及青年运动情况。
    【新华社北京二十一日电】新华社铁道部分社已于日前成立，主要工作为报道全国铁道建设情况、铁道部的重要措施以及铁道员工生活的情况。
