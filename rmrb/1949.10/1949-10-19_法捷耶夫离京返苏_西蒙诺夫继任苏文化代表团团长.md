Title: Fadeev Departs Beijing for the Soviet Union; Simonov Succeeds as Head of Soviet Cultural Delegation

Author:

Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, Eighteenth. Fadeev, the head of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation visiting China, departed Beijing for the Soviet Union at 1:00 PM today. Those who went to see him off included Guo Moruo, Qian Junrui, and Lai Yali of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Ningyi of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, writers Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Ding Ling, Ai Qing, Ke Zhongping, and Zhang Youyu, the Deputy Mayor of Beijing. Fadeev arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon from Shanghai. After Fadeev's return to the Soviet Union, the position of head of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation will be assumed by the deputy head of the delegation, Simonov.

### 法捷耶夫离京返苏　西蒙诺夫继任苏文化代表团团长

1949-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　法捷耶夫离京返苏
  　西蒙诺夫继任苏文化代表团团长
    【新华社北京十八日电】访问中国的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团团长法捷耶夫已于今日下午一时离京返苏。前往送行者有中苏友好协会总会郭沫若、钱俊瑞、赖亚力，中华全国总工会刘宁一，作家茅盾、周扬、丁玲、艾青、柯仲平，及北京市副市长张友渔等。法捷耶夫系于昨日下午由沪来京。法捷耶夫返苏后，苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团团长职务，即由该团副团长西蒙诺夫担任。
