Title: North China and Northeast China Railway Workers Launch Sino-Soviet Friendship Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th. The Sino-Soviet friendship movement has been launched among railway workers in North China and Northeast China. On September 24th, Wang Hefeng, Director of the Political Department of the Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Li Jiebo, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the National Railway Federation of Trade Unions, jointly initiated a signature campaign to prepare for the establishment of a branch of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in the Railway Department. More than 1,200 employees of the department have signed up, and a preparatory meeting will be held within days. The Tangshan Railway Factory is also preparing to establish a branch of the Tangshan Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in the Tangshan Railway Factory, and the initiators are signing up. The Political Department of the Tianjin Railway Bureau and the Preparatory Committee of the Bureau's Trade Union jointly held a Sino-Soviet friendship symposium on September 17th and are currently preparing to establish a branch of the Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Zhangjiakou Branch, organized by railway workers in Zhangjiakou, was established on September 13th.

Railway workers in various parts of Northeast China have established branches under the local branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Harbin and Changchun, and a preparatory committee has been established in Jinzhou. Railway workers are very enthusiastic about joining the association. When the Harbin branch was established on September 17th, it also held a photo exhibition introducing Soviet economic construction. Employees of the Northeast Railway Administration are also preparing to establish a branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shenyang Branch in the Railway Administration; the preparatory meeting was established on September 14th and is now actively developing members. In addition, the Political Department of the Northeast Railway Administration, the Northeast Railway Federation of Trade Unions, and others have jointly stipulated that the month from September 18th to the Soviet October Revolution Day will be the Sino-Soviet Friendship Movement Month for railway workers, and extensive and in-depth publicity will be conducted among workers on the significance of Sino-Soviet friendship.

### 华北东北铁路职工展开中苏友好运动

1949-10-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北东北铁路职工
    展开中苏友好运动
    【新华社北京十二日电】中苏友好运动已在华北和东北的铁路职工中展开。九月二十四日中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部政治部主任王鹤峰、全国铁路总工会筹委会主任李颉伯等联名发起筹组北京市中苏友好协会铁道部支会的签名运动，该部职工签名者已有一千二百余人，日内即将召开筹备会议。唐山铁路工厂也在进行筹组中苏友好协会唐山分会的唐山铁路工厂支会，发起人正签名发起。天津铁路局政治部及该局职工总会筹委会已在九月十七日联合召开了一次中苏友好座谈会，刻正在筹组中苏友好协会天津分会的支会。张家口铁路职工组织的中苏友好协会张家口分会的支会则已在九月十三日成立。
    东北各地铁路职工，已在各当地中苏友好协会分会之下建立支会的有哈尔滨和长春，另有锦州已建立筹委会。铁路职工入会者极踊跃。哈尔滨的支会在九月十七日成立时，并曾举行了一次介绍苏联经济建设的图片展览。东北铁路总局的职工也正筹组中苏友好协会沈阳分会的铁路总局支会；筹备会已于九月十四日成立，现正积极发展会员。此外东北铁路总局政治部、东北铁路总工会等并联合规定从九一八到苏联十月革命节的一个月为铁路职工的中苏友好运动月，在职工中对中苏友好的意义进行广泛深入的宣传。
