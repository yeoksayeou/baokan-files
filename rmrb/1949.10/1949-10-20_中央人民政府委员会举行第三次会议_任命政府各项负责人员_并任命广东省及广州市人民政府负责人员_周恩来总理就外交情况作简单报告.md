Title: The Central People's Government Committee Holds Its Third Meeting to Appoint Various Government Officials; Appoints Responsible Persons for the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal People's Governments; Premier Zhou Enlai Makes Brief Report on the Diplomatic Situation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 19th: The Central People's Government Committee convened its third meeting at 5:00 PM today, appointing the Vice Premiers, Government Council Members, Secretary-General, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council, and the responsible persons of its subordinate commissions, ministries, associations, academies, administrations, and banks; the Vice Chairmen and members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Chief of General Staff and Deputy Chiefs of General Staff; the Vice Presidents and members of the Supreme People's Court; the Deputy Procurators-General and members of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; the responsible persons of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government and the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government; and the Director and Deputy Directors of the General Office of the Central People's Government. The list of appointments was unanimously approved, as the result of full consultation among all parties prior to the meeting. The deputy positions in some departments are still under negotiation and will be announced successively in the future.

Present at today's meeting were Chairman of the Central People's Government Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Gao Gang, Central People's Government Committee Members Chen Yi, He Long, Li Lisan, Lin Boqu, He Xiangning, Liu Bocheng, Wu Yuzhang, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Saifudin, Chen Jiageng, Ulanhu, Xu Teli, Cai Chang, Liu Geping, Ma Yinchu, Chen Yun, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Zhang Yunyi, Deng Xiaoping, Gao Chongmin, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Li Xijiu, Huang Yanpei, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Bojun, Cheng Qian, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Mingshu, Tan Pingshan, Zhang Nanxian, Zhang Dongsun.

After passing the above appointments at today's meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai gave a brief report on the diplomatic situation.

第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府委员会举行第三次会议
  　任命政府各项负责人员
    并任命广东省及广州市人民政府负责人员
    周恩来总理就外交情况作简单报告
    【新华社北京十九日电】中央人民政府委员会今天下午五时召集第三次会议，任命了政务院的副总理、政务委员、秘书长、副秘书长及所属各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人员，人民革命军事委员会的副主席、委员、总参谋长和副总参谋长，最高人民法院的副院长和委员，最高人民检察署的副检察长和委员，广东省人民政府和广州市人民政府的负责人员，以及中央人民政府办公厅的主任和副主任。任命的名单得到全体一致的通过，因为这个名单是会前各方充分协商的结果。某些部门的副职尚在协商中，将于日后陆续通过发表。出席今天的会议的有中央人民政府主席毛泽东，副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜、高岗，中央人民政府委员陈毅、贺龙、李立三、林伯渠、何香凝、刘伯承、吴玉章、彭真、薄一波、聂荣臻、周恩来、董必武、赛福鼎、陈嘉庚、乌兰夫、徐特立、蔡畅、刘格平、马寅初、陈云、马叙伦、郭沫若、张云逸、邓小平、高崇民、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、陈叔通、司徒美堂、李锡九、黄炎培、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、张治中、傅作义、李烛尘、章伯钧、程潜、张奚若、陈铭枢、谭平山、张难先、张东荪。今天的会议在通过了上述的任命以后，周恩来总理就外交情况作了简单的报告。
