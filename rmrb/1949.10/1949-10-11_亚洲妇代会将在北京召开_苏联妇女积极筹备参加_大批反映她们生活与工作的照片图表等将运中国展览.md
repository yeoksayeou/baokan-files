Title: Asian Women's Congress to be Held in Beijing; Soviet Women Actively Preparing to Participate; Large Numbers of Photos, Charts, etc. Reflecting Their Life and Work to be Transported to China for Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. TASS Moscow: Under the initiative of the Women's International Democratic Federation, the Asian Women's Representative Conference will be held in early December in Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China. Petrova, Secretary of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, told TASS reporters that Soviet women were very pleased to learn that the Asian Women's Congress would be held in China and are preparing to participate in the conference. The Soviet delegation will be composed of women representatives from the Republics of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, and Azerbaijan. Soviet women are already preparing a grand exhibition for the conference that reflects their life and work. Various photos, charts, and documents will reflect the great achievements and superiority of the socialist system, which has liberated women from inequality and enslavement, and ensures that women enjoy full and equal rights with men in all sectors of national economic, social, political, and cultural life. The exhibition will include materials describing women's activities in industry, transportation, and agriculture, in state administration and economic management, in science, literature, music, drama, and film, as well as achievements in folk art, etc. The exhibition will show the Soviet state's concern for women's education and maternal and child welfare. A special section of the exhibition will be devoted to reflecting the Soviet women's struggle for peace. In the Soviet Union, preparations for the Asian Women's Representative Conference are being conducted on a mass scale. In particular, women skilled in folk arts are preparing to transport embroidery, carpets, lace, exquisite wood carvings and ivory carvings, metal products, etc., to China for exhibition.

### 亚洲妇代会将在北京召开　苏联妇女积极筹备参加  大批反映她们生活与工作的照片图表等将运中国展览

1949-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇代会将在北京召开
    苏联妇女积极筹备参加
    大批反映她们生活与工作的照片图表等将运中国展览
    【新华社北京十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：在国际民主妇联的发起下，亚洲妇女代表会议将于十二月初在中华人民共和国首都北京召开。苏联妇女反法西斯委员会书记彼特罗娃特为此对塔斯社记者发表谈话称：苏联妇女怀着十分满意的心情获悉在中国召开亚洲妇代会议，并正准备参加这一会议。苏联代表团将由哈萨克、吉尔吉斯、乌兹别克、土克曼、塔吉克、阿塞拜疆等共和国的妇女代表组成。苏联妇女已在为会议准备一个反映她们底生活和工作的盛大展览会。各种各样的照片、图表和文件将反映社会主义制度的巨大成就与优越性，这个制度把妇女从不平等和被奴役的状态中解放出来，并保证妇女在全国经济、社会、政治与文化生活各部门中，与男子一同享有完全平等权利。要展览的将是叙述妇女在工业、运输、农业方面的活动，在国家管理、经济管理方面的活动，在科学、文学、音乐、戏剧、电影的活动，以及在民间艺术方面的成就等等的材料，展览会将显示苏维埃国家对妇女的教育和母婴福利等等的关心。展览会将特设一部专门反映苏维埃妇女为和平而斗争的情形。在苏联，为亚洲妇女代表会议所进行的筹备工作，是具有群众性的，特别是精于民间艺术的妇女们正在准备把若干刺绣品、地毯、花边、精巧的木刻与象牙雕刻、金属制品等，运往中国展览。
