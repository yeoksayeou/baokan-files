Title: List of Chairman, Vice-Chairmen and Members of the Cultural and Educational Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th – The third meeting of the Central People's Government Committee approved the following candidates for the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee:

Chairman:
　　Guo Moruo (Non-Party Democrat, Chairman of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles);

Vice-Chairmen (four persons):
　　Ma Xulun (China Association for Promoting Democracy),
　　Chen Boda (Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deputy Dean of the Marx-Lenin Academy),
　　Lu Dingyi (Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China),
　　Shen Yanbing (Writer, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles).

Members (forty-two persons):
