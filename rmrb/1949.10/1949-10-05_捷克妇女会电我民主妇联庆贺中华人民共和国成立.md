Title: Czech Women's Association Sends Telegram to All-China Democratic Women's Federation to Congratulate the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author: Milena Králová
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has received a congratulatory telegram from the Czech Women's Association, congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    Dear Sisters, We, the Czech women, salute the People's Democratic China and its great leader Mao Zedong! We sincerely hope that the Chinese people will achieve great success in their hard work of building the country! The great victory of the Chinese people is also our victory, because the new People's Democratic China will surely become a pillar of peace in the Far East and the world. In our common struggle for a better future for all mankind and world peace, we Czech women assure our Chinese sisters that we will give sincere friendship and faithful cooperation. Under the invincible leadership of the Soviet Union, we will surely win this battle!
    　　　　Chairman of the Czech Women's Association, Táňa Herdinová-Spurná
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Secretary Milena Králová

### 捷克妇女会电我民主妇联庆贺中华人民共和国成立
密伦娜·克劳洛娃
1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　捷克妇女会电我民主妇联庆贺中华人民共和国成立
    【新华社北京四日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会顷收到捷克妇女会贺电一份，祝贺中华人民共和国成立。电文如下：
    亲爱的姊妹们：我们捷克妇女向人民民主中国及其伟大领袖毛泽东致敬！我们衷心地希望中国人民在他们建设国家的辛勤工作中获得巨大的成功！中国人民伟大的胜利，同时也是我们的胜利，因为人民民主的新中国，必然成为远东与世界和平的柱石。在我们为全人类美好的未来和世界和平的共同斗争中，我们捷克妇女向我们中国的姊妹们保证，我们将给予真挚的友谊与忠实的合作。在不可战胜的苏联领导之下，我们一定能够赢得这个战斗！
    　　　　捷克妇女会主席塔尼兹加·贺狄诺娃·斯伯娜
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　总书记密伦娜·克劳洛娃
