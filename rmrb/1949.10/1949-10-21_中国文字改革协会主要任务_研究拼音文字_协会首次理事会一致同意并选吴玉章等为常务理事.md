Title: Chinese Language Reform Association's Main Task: Researching Phonetic Writing; Association's First Board Meeting Unanimously Agreed and Elected Wu Yuzhang, etc., as Standing Directors
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 20th: The Chinese Language Reform Association held its first board meeting this morning. Nearly forty directors attended. Wu Yuzhang reported on the preparation and establishment of the association and explained that the association would set up several specialized committees to study phonetic writing schemes, the arrangement and simplification of Chinese characters, dialect investigation and research, the research of minority languages and the creation of writing systems, as well as the editing of publications, dictionaries, and other work. Speakers at the meeting included Situ Meitang, Shao Lizi, Chen Qiyuan, Ding Xielin, Lin Xi, Wei Jianggong, Zhang Chong, Luo Changpei, and others. Everyone unanimously agreed to take the research of phonetic writing as the main task and to take the Latinized New Writing of the Northern Dialect as the basis; currently, more attention should be paid to promoting the simplification and phoneticization of Chinese characters. Yi nationality director Zhang Chong spoke, welcoming the association to help the Yi nationality create a new Yi script. Finally, the entire body unanimously elected Wu Yuzhang, Cheng Fangwu, Shen Yanbing, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Lin Handa, Xu Teli, Lu Zhiwei, Guo Moruo, Ma Xulun, Ni Haishu, Chen Dingmin, Chen Heqin, Fan Wenlan, Zhang Zhao, Li Jinxi, Ye Shengtao, Ye Laishi, Ye Dingyi, Peng Zhen, Qian Junrui, Luo Changpei, Xiao San, Wei Jianggong, Nie Zhen, and other twenty-five people as standing directors.

### 中国文字改革协会主要任务　研究拼音文字  协会首次理事会一致同意并选吴玉章等为常务理事

1949-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国文字改革协会主要任务
  　研究拼音文字
  协会首次理事会一致同意并选吴玉章等为常务理事
  【新华社北京二十日电】中国文字改革协会今天上午举行第一次理事会。到会理事近四十人。吴玉章报告协会筹备成立经过，并说明协会将设立几个专门委员会来研究拼音文字的方案、汉字的整理和简化、方言的调查研究、少数民族语言的研究和文字的创造、以及编辑刊物、辞典等工作。会上发言者有司徒美堂、邵力子、陈其瑗、丁燮林、林曦、魏建功、张冲、罗常培等。大家一致同意把研究拼音文字作为主要任务，并且把北方话拉丁化新文字作为底案；目前更应注意促成汉字的简单化和标音化。彝族理事张冲发言，欢迎协会帮助彝族创制新的彝文。最后全体一致选出吴玉章、成仿吾、沈雁冰、胡乔木、胡愈之、林汉达、徐特立、陆志韦、郭沫若、马叙伦、倪海曙、陈定民、陈鹤琴、范文澜、张照、黎锦熙、叶圣陶、叶籁士、叶丁易、彭真、钱俊瑞、罗常培、萧三、魏建功、聂真等二十五人为常务理事。
