Title: Our Army Rapidly Advances South on the Guangdong Front: Yingde and Wengyuan Liberated; Our Army Captures Liuheng Island off the Zhejiang Coast
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangdong Front, 11th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, after liberating Qujiang, advanced rapidly south along the Yuehan Railway. It liberated Yingde County seat at 4 PM on the 9th, annihilating one enemy battalion. Our army is now valiantly pursuing and annihilating the fleeing enemy. Furthermore, Wengyuan County seat was liberated on the 6th by a unit of our local people's armed forces.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 11th: Following the liberation of Jintang Island, the People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang Front crossed the sea on the 7th and liberated Liuheng Island south of Dinghai, as well as Fodushan Island to the west of Liuheng Island. Liuheng Island is located at the mouth of Xiangshan Port, serving as a barrier for the port. It is a vast area, the third largest island in the Zhoushan Islands.
    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 11th: On the morning of the 7th, the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front shot down a Kuomintang bandit gang's Mustang fighter plane over the Songyu position on the west coast of Xiamen Island. The plane caught fire and crashed on the beach north of Songyu and south of Dayu.

### 广东前线我军急速南进　英德翁源解放　浙东沿海我军攻占六横岛

1949-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　广东前线我军急速南进
    英德翁源解放
    浙东沿海我军攻占六横岛
    【新华社广东前线十一日电】解放曲江之人民解放军某部，沿粤汉线向南急进，九日下午四时解放英德县城。歼灭守敌一个营。现我军正勇猛向南追歼逃敌。又翁源县城六日为我地方人民武装一部解放。
    【新华社杭州十一日电】浙东前线人民解放军继解放金塘岛后，于七日渡海解放定海以南的六横岛及该岛以西的佛渡山岛。六横岛位于象山港口，为该港屏障，面积广阔，为舟山群岛中第三大岛。
    【新华社福建前线十一日电】福建前线人民解放军，于七日上午在厦门岛西岸嵩屿阵地上空，击落国民党匪帮野马式战斗机一架，该机起火坠于嵩屿以北、大屿以南的海滩上。
