Title: U.S. Imperialism's "Foreign Aid" Plan Faces Growing Opposition from the People

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: TASS, New York dispatch: The Wall Street Journal, Washington dispatch, states: Many congressmen, returning from a month-long recess, say that voter dissatisfaction with the United States' foreign, economic, and military "aid" programs is growing. Two Republican members of the House Foreign Affairs Committee, Javits and Fulton, said: The people universally hope to reduce government spending for the above purposes. Whether Republican or Democratic members of Congress, all acknowledge that the people, especially workers, are increasingly demanding that the government spend more money on social security and education.

### 美帝“援外”计划人民反对者日众

1949-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　美帝“援外”计划人民反对者日众
    【新华社北京十日电】塔斯社纽约讯：华尔街日报华盛顿消息称：许多度过一月休假归来的众议员都说，选民对于美国的外交、经济与军事“援助”计划的不满，正在日益增长中。众院外委会的两个共和党议员杰维茨和富尔顿说：人民普遍希望消减政府用于上述目的的经费。不论是共和党议员或民主党议员，都承认人民特别是工人们日益增长地要求把政府更多的经费，用在社会安全福利和教育上。
