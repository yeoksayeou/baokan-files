Title: 16,000 Workers at Alcoa Strike, Forcing Nine Plants in Pennsylvania and Other States to Close
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th—According to TASS, New York: The strike of steel and coal miners in the United States has not yet been resolved, and 16,000 workers at Alcoa began a strike on the 17th, forcing the company to close nine plants, including some of the company's largest plants in Pennsylvania, Connecticut, New Jersey, Tennessee, and North Carolina. According to the New York Herald Tribune, Alcoa's aluminum production accounts for 50 percent of the country's total.

### 美国铝公司工人一万六千人罢工　宾州等九个厂被迫停工

1949-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国铝公司工人一万六千人罢工
  　宾州等九个厂被迫停工
    【新华社北京十八日电】据塔斯社纽约讯：美国钢铁与煤矿工人的罢工尚未解决，一万六千名美国铝公司的工人又于十七日开始罢工，迫使该公司关闭九所工厂，其中包括该公司在宾夕法尼亚州、康涅狄格州、新泽西州、田纳西州与北卡罗林纳州若干最大的工厂。据纽约先驱论坛报说，美国铝公司产铝的数量，占全国百分之五十。
