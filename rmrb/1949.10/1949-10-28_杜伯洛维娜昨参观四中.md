Title: Dubrovinina Visits Fourth Middle School Yesterday
Author: Ren Zhenjia
Date: 1949-10-28
Page: 4

　　Dubrovinina Visits Fourth Middle School Yesterday
　　任振甲
　　[Our Newspaper] Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation member and Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Dubrovinina, visited Municipal Fourth Middle School yesterday (27th). More than a thousand students held a welcome meeting for this international friend with the warmest emotions. At the meeting, the principal of Fourth Middle School gave a brief introduction, calling on students to study the Soviet Union hard and strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship. Then, Dubrovinina delivered a speech, pointing out that the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union are like brothers, always closely united, to completely eliminate imperialism and its running dogs. Finally, she encouraged the students, saying: Building the motherland is not an easy task, students must study hard, establish learning discipline, and never forget to work hard for their motherland at all times. Her speech won applause and cheers from the whole audience.
　　(Fourth Middle School Correspondent Group Ren Zhenjia)

### 杜伯洛维娜昨参观四中
任振甲
1949-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　杜伯洛维娜昨参观四中
    任振甲
    【本报讯】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团团员俄罗斯苏维埃联邦教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜昨（二十七）日赴市立四中参观。一千多同学以最热烈的情绪为这位国际友人举行了欢迎会。会上，四中校长作简短的介绍后，号召同学要努力学习苏联，加强中苏友谊。继由杜伯洛维娜致词，她指出中苏两大民族就象同胞兄弟一样永远亲密的团结在一起，彻底消灭帝国主义及其走狗们。最后她勉励同学说：建设祖国不是一件容易的事，同学们要好好学习，建立学习纪律，时时刻刻不要忘记为自己的祖国而努力。他的讲话博得全场的掌声与欢呼声。
    （四中通讯组任振甲）
