Title: Polish Foreign Ministry Note Verbale Severing Relations with the Guangzhou Government
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th - Polish Deputy Foreign Minister Leśkiewicz met with the Guangzhou government's Chargé d'affaires in Warsaw on October 4th and, on behalf of the Polish government, delivered a note verbale to him, stating: In view of the current situation in China, the Yan Xishan government in Guangzhou is not qualified to represent the interests of the Chinese people. The Polish government considers that diplomatic relations with the Guangzhou government have ceased to exist as of October 4 this year.

### 波兰外交部通牒与广州政府绝交

1949-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　波兰外交部通牒与广州政府绝交
    【新华社布拉格七日电】波兰外交部副部长莱西斯基于十月四日约见广州政府在华沙的代办，并代表波兰政府向他传递通牒，内称：鉴于中国目前的情况，广州的阎锡山政府没有资格代表中国人民的利益。波兰政府认为从今年十月四日起与广州政府的外交关系已不存在。
