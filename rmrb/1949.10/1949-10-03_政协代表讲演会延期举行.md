Title: CPPCC Representatives' Lecture Meeting Postponed
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3

[Beijing News] The student group of the Propaganda Department of the Preparatory Committee for the celebration of the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, has just issued a notice: The CPPCC representatives' lecture meeting, originally scheduled to be held in the three districts of Beida Sand, Beigong, and Shiren today (3rd), has been postponed due to unforeseen circumstances.

### 政协代表讲演会延期举行

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　政协代表讲演会延期举行
    【北京讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立庆祝大会筹备会宣传部学生组，顷发出通知：原订于今（三）日分北大沙滩、北工、师人三区举行的政协代表讲演会，因故延期。
