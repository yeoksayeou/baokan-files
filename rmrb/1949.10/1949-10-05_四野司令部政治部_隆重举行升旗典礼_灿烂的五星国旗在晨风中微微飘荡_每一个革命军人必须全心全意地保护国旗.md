Title: Fourth Field Army Headquarters Political Department Solemnly Holds Flag-Raising Ceremony
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Fourth Field Army Headquarters Political Department
    Solemnly Holds Flag-Raising Ceremony
    The Brilliant Five-Star Red Flag Gently Fluttered in the Morning Breeze
    Every Revolutionary Soldier Must Wholeheartedly Protect the National Flag
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 4th] The Political Department of the Fourth Field Army Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army solemnly held a flag-raising ceremony for the national flag of the People's Republic of China at its respective residences in the morning of October 2nd. After twenty-four flares were brilliantly launched into the sky, the huge, bright five-star red flag slowly rose. All the commanders and fighters participating in the flag-raising ceremony stood in solemn attention, saluting the national flag with their eyes fixed upon it. Everyone solemnly and excitedly looked up at the national flag gently fluttering in the morning breeze. After the flag-raising, everyone applauded warmly for a long time. Chief of Staff Xiao Ke and Deputy Director Tao Zhu gave brief and powerful speeches at the meeting. Chief of Staff Xiao demanded that all cadres of the headquarters, in these victorious days of the birth of the People's Republic of China and the great liberation of the Chinese people, must not relax their vigilance against the imperialist conspiracy to invade China in the slightest. Deputy Director Tao Zhu led all the personnel of the Political Department in raising their hands to take an oath, calling on every revolutionary soldier to wholeheartedly protect this sacred national flag and make it forever fly in the skies of the motherland.

### 四野司令部政治部　隆重举行升旗典礼  灿烂的五星国旗在晨风中微微飘荡  每一个革命军人必须全心全意地保护国旗

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　四野司令部政治部
    隆重举行升旗典礼
    灿烂的五星国旗在晨风中微微飘荡
    每一个革命军人必须全心全意地保护国旗
    【新华社汉口四日电】人民解放军第四野战军司令部政治部于十月二日分别于驻地早晨，隆重举行中华人民共和国国旗升旗典礼。二十四发照明弹灿然升空后，巨大的五星灿烂的国旗徐徐升起，参加升旗典礼的全体指战员肃立向国旗行举手注目礼，大家严肃而激动地仰望着国旗在晨风中微微飘荡。升旗完毕，全体热烈鼓掌，经久不息。萧克参谋长、陶铸副主任分别在会上作简短有力的讲话。萧参谋长要求司令部全体干部在中华人民共和国诞生、中国人民大翻身的胜利日子里，对帝国主义侵略中国的阴谋，不能放松一丝一毫的警惕。陶铸副主任领着政治部全体人员举手宣誓，号召每一个革命军人，必须全心全意保护这面神圣的国旗，使它永远飘扬在祖国的天空。
