Title: Soviet Union Lodges Protest with Yugoslav Government; Yugoslav Ambassador to Soviet Union Engaged in Anti-Soviet Activities and Cannot Continue to Serve as Diplomatic Representative to Soviet Union; Czechoslovakia Expels American Spy Counselor

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th: TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs delivered a note to the Yugoslav Embassy in the Soviet Union on October 25th, the content of which is as follows:

"The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, on behalf of the Soviet Government, hereby informs the Yugoslav Government as follows:

In the process of the trial of state criminals and spies Rajk and his accomplices, it has been proven that the current Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mr. Mrazovic, has been engaged in espionage and sabotage activities against the Soviet Union for a long time. Moreover, during his tenure as Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, he has conducted anti-Soviet slander and fabrications in Yugoslav newspapers.

In view of this, the Soviet Government believes that Mr. Mrazovic cannot continue to serve as the Yugoslav diplomatic representative to the Soviet Union."

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th: TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs delivered a note to the American Embassy in Czechoslovakia on the 25th, stating that John G. Erhardt, counselor of the American Embassy, abused his diplomatic position and engaged in conspiracy and sabotage activities against the security of the country where he enjoyed diplomatic privileges. The note stated that, based on verified facts, this Ministry strongly protests the conspiracy and sabotage activities of John G. Erhardt, a diplomat of the American Embassy. Based on the above, the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs demands that John G. Erhardt leave Czechoslovak territory within twenty-four hours.

### 苏照会南政府  南国驻苏大使从事反苏活动不能继续充任驻苏外交代表  捷驱逐美间谍参赞出境

1949-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏照会南政府　
    南国驻苏大使从事反苏活动不能继续充任驻苏外交代表　
    捷驱逐美间谍参赞出境
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部于十月二十五日，以照会一件递交南斯拉夫驻苏大使馆，内容如下：
    “苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部奉苏联政府之命通知南斯拉夫政府如下：
    在审判国事犯和间谍拉伊克及其同谋犯的过程中，业已证实现任南斯拉夫驻苏大使姆拉佐维奇，曾长时间从事反对苏联的间谍与破坏活动，而且在他任南斯拉夫驻苏大使期内，曾在南斯拉夫的报章上进行反对苏联的诽谤捏造。
    有鉴于此，苏联政府认为姆拉佐维奇不可能继续充任南斯拉夫驻苏联的外交代表。”
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：捷克斯洛伐克外交部于二十五日以照会一件递交美国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆，内称，美国大使馆参赞约翰·格恩滥用其外交地位，且对于他在其境内享有外交特权的国家之安全进行阴谋破坏的活动。该照会称，本外交部根据已经证实的事实，坚决抗议美国大使馆外交官约翰·格恩的阴谋破坏活动。根据以上所述，捷克斯洛伐克外交部要求约翰·格恩于二十四小时以内离开捷克斯洛伐克国境。
