Title: Two French Workers' Groups Congratulate the Birth of New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th—Pesque, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Public and Health Services Workers, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao on the 5th to offer congratulations. The telegram reads: "The French General Confederation of Public and Health Services Workers, representing its 200,000 members, expresses its respect for the birth of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the People's Government under the leadership of Mao Zedong. The workers of the French public and health services see that the liberation of the Chinese working people and their beautiful future of peace and freedom have been truly guaranteed. The workers of the French public and health services are making every effort to urge the French government to immediately recognize the Chinese People's Government."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th—(Delayed) Dougui, Secretary-General of the French National Federation of Miners, affiliated with the General Confederation of Labor, sent a letter to Chinese miners on the 6th congratulating them, saying: "The French National Federation of Miners warmly pays tribute to the birth of the People's Republic of China. The French miners have always admired and watched the struggle and victory of the Chinese people against imperialism and its running dogs, and also see that the birth of the People's Republic of China, this important event, has decisively strengthened the strength of the world peace camp headed by the Soviet Union. They are requesting the French government to immediately recognize the government led by Mao Zedong. The French miners pay special tribute to their Chinese miner comrades. They are deeply convinced that united under the leadership of the International Miners' Federation affiliated with the World Federation of Trade Unions, they will victoriously complete the work for world peace and the welfare of miners all over the world. The French Miners' Federation calls on all members to express their friendship to the People's Republic of China by demanding that the French government recognize New China."

### 法国两工人团体祝贺新中国诞生

1949-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　法国两工人团体祝贺新中国诞生
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】法国公用与卫生事业职工联合总会书记皮斯盖五日电向毛主席致贺。电文如下：“法国公用与卫生事业职工联合总会代表所属二十万会员，对中华人民共和国的诞生，和在毛泽东领导下的人民政府的成立，表示敬意。法国公用与卫生事业的工人们看到中国劳动人民的解放和他们和平自由的美好将来已经有了确实保证。法国公用与卫生事业工人们正在竭力促使法国政府立即承认中华人民政府。”
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】（迟到）法总工会所属法国矿工全国联合会总书记都贵六日致函中国矿工祝贺称：“法国矿工全国联合会对中华人民共和国的诞生，热烈致敬。法国矿工们一向钦佩地注视着中国人民反对帝国主义及其走狗的斗争中的战斗和胜利，也看到中华人民共和国诞生，这个重要的事件，使以苏联为首的世界和平阵营中的力量有决定意义地增强了。他们正向法国政府要求立即承认毛泽东所领导的政府。法国矿工特别对他们的中国矿工同志们致敬。他们深信团结起来在世界工联会所属的国际矿工联合会领导下，将胜利地完成为世界和平、为全世界矿工福利的工作。法国矿工联合会号召全体会员，以要求法国政府承认新中国，来表示他们对中华人民共和国的友谊。”
