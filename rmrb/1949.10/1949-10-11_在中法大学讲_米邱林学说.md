Title: Lecture on Michurin's Theory at the Sino-French University
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 4

　　At the Sino-French University
    Lecture on Michurin's Theory
    Storatov
    [Beijing News] Yesterday (10th) at 2 PM, an agricultural biology lecture meeting was held in the auditorium of the Sino-French University. The agricultural colleges of North China University, Tsinghua University, and Peking University, along with the biology departments of other universities, organized the lecture meeting and invited Dr. Storatov, President of the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy of the Soviet delegation, to speak on Michurin's theory. Le Tianyu, Dean of the Agricultural College of North China University, chaired the meeting. More than 2,000 university professors and agricultural experts attended. Dr. Storatov's lecture was very detailed and clear, resolving the debate in Beijing's scientific community on biology. After the lecture, the professors raised 153 questions, which Dr. Storatov answered one by one with patience and clarity, to everyone's great satisfaction. At the meeting, Comrade Le Tianyu briefly stated that the Soviet Union, with its most advanced political economy, has the conditions to become the cradle of the world's most advanced science. Now, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, we have established a New China, and we will definitely become the cradle of the world's most advanced science. We have the conditions, the reasons, and the courage to devote ourselves fully to learning agriculture from the Soviet Union in order to complete the agricultural section of the Common Program. This is the bounden duty of agricultural scientific workers.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Kang Xin)

### 在中法大学讲  米邱林学说

1949-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　在中法大学讲
    米邱林学说
    斯托拉托夫
    【京市讯】昨（十）日下午二时，在中法大学大礼堂，举行农业生物学演讲大会，由华北大学、清华大学、北京大学三校农学院及其他各校生物系，组织演讲会敦请苏联代表团第米里亚夫农学院长斯托拉托夫博士讲米邱林学说。大会由华北大学农学院院长乐天宇主席。到会各大学教授，农业专家二千余人。斯托拉托夫博士讲授非常细致明确，解决了北京科学界在生物学上的争论问题。演讲完毕，并由各教授提出一百五十三个问题，斯托拉托夫博士一一加以解答，既耐心，又明确，使大家异常满意。会上乐天宇同志略谓政治经济最进步的苏联，所以它有条件成为世界上最进步科学的摇篮，现在我们在英明的领袖毛主席领导之下，已建立一个新中国，也肯定的要成为世界上最进步科学的摇篮，我们有条件，有理由，也有勇气，应该拿出全副精神向苏联学习农业，来完成共同纲领上的农业部分，这才是农业科学工作者应尽的天职。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（康辛）
