Title: Director and Deputy Directors of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th—The third meeting of the Central People's Government Committee appointed Qi Yanming as the Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee, and Yu Xinqing, Zhou Xinmin, Qiao Guanhua, and Luo Shuzhang as Deputy Directors.

### 中央人民政府委员会办公厅主任及副主任

1949-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府委员会办公厅主任及副主任
    【新华社北京十九日电】中央人民政府委员会第三次会议任命齐燕铭为中央人民政府委员会办公厅主任，余心清、周新民、乔冠华、罗叔章为副主任。
