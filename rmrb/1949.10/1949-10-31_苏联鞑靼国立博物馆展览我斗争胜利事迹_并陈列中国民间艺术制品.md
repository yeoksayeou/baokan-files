Title: Soviet Tatar National Museum Exhibits Achievements of My Struggle and Displays Chinese Folk Art Products
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th: TASS, Kazan, 28th: The Tatar National Museum recently held an exhibition dedicated to the artworks of the great Chinese people and the heroic struggle for their liberation from imperialist oppression. To familiarize the audience with Chinese folk art, one section of the exhibition displays products of Chinese artisans, such as pottery, ivory carvings, stone carvings, metal utensils, and paintings on Xuan paper. Another section displays materials related to the great victories won by the People's Liberation Army and the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 苏联鞑靼国立博物馆展览我斗争胜利事迹　并陈列中国民间艺术制品

1949-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联鞑靼国立博物馆展览我斗争胜利事迹　
    并陈列中国民间艺术制品
    【新华社北京三十日电】塔斯社喀山二十八日讯：鞑靼国立博物馆顷举办一个展览会，专门展览伟大中国人民的艺术品，及其从帝国主义压迫之下争取解放的英勇斗争事迹。为使观众熟悉中国的民间艺术，会上一部分陈列了中国手工艺者的出品如陶器、象牙器、石器、金属器具，以及宣纸上的绘画等。另一部分则陈列有关人民解放军赢得的伟大胜利和中华人民共和国成立的资料。
