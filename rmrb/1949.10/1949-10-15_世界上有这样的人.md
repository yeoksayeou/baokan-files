Title: There Are Such People in the World
Author: Ye Weimin
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 6

　　There Are Such People in the World
    Ye Weimin
    In this world
    There are such people
    They ask for no reward
    Know no fatigue
    For the happiness of mankind
    Working, working.

    In this world
    There are such people
    Who can endure unbearable hardships
    Who can withstand all kinds of enemy temptations
    Who can withstand the test of death
    Brave and determined
    For the happiness of mankind
    Fighting, fighting.

    In this world
    There are such people
    Who possess the greatest wisdom of mankind
    Who possess the best qualities of mankind
    Who possess the highest ideals of mankind
    Who possess the deepest love of mankind
    They are the best sons and daughters of mankind
    They are the Communists.

    In this world
    There are such people
    Who sacrifice everything
    For creating a bright future for mankind
    They are the sun that makes the world shine
    Let us praise,
    These elites of mankind
    The Communists.
    　　　　　　October 6th (August 15th)
