Title: Ministry of Railways Encourages the Development of Transportation Industry; Whole-Vehicle Transportation of Transport Equipment; Railway Freight Reduced by Half
Author:
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 2

The Ministry of Railways Encourages the Development of Transportation Industry
　　Whole-Vehicle Transportation of Transport Equipment; Railway Freight Reduced by Half
　　[This Newspaper News] To develop the transportation industry of the new China, the Military Commission’s Ministry of Railways has specifically stipulated that for all transport equipment, such as: motor vehicles, car bodies, ship hulls, and transport tools belonging to mechanical, human, or animal power, transported by the whole vehicle between stations on national railways, the freight will be reduced by 50% of the current cargo freight rate. This has been implemented in the Northeast, North, and South Railway Bureaus since October 5th.

　　　　　(People's Railway Newspaper)

### 铁道部鼓励发展运输事业　整车运送运输工具  铁路减收运费一半

1949-10-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　铁道部鼓励发展运输事业
    整车运送运输工具  铁路减收运费一半
    【本报讯】军委铁道部为发展新中国的运输事业，特规定凡在全国铁路上各站相互间整车运送运输用具如：汽车车辆、车体、船体、及属于机械力或用人力、兽力之运输工具，一律按现行货物运价率减收运费百分之五十，已于十月五日起在东北、北方及南方各路局实行。　　　
　　　　　（人民铁道报社）
