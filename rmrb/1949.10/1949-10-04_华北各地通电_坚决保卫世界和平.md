Title: North China Cables Express Resolve to Defend World Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 3

　　North China Cables Express
   　Resolve to Defend World Peace
    [Our newspaper's comprehensive report] October 2nd – International Day of Struggle for Peace, the CCP Suiyuan Provincial Committee, Suiyuan Provincial People's Government, Suiyuan Military Region, Suiyuan All-Circles Celebration Meeting, Taiyuan All-Circles Celebration Meeting, Tangshan All-Circles Celebration Meeting, and others, sent telegrams to Marshal Stalin, World Peace Congress President Curie, the Standing Committee of the Paris Congress in Support of World Peace, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, expressing their firm opposition to wars of aggression and support for world peace. The telegram of respect from all circles in Taiyuan to Marshal Stalin stated: "Humanity is most proud to have you. Under your banner, we unite to struggle for lasting world peace." The telegram to Curie stated: "We firmly believe that unity is strength. If peace-loving people all over the world unite as one, they will surely be able to overwhelm the reactionaries." The telegrams from all circles in Suiyuan and Tangshan to the Chinese branch of the World Peace Congress and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association stated: "We vow to respond to every call you make, and use practical actions to restore and develop production to strike at the reactionary conspiracies of war instigators headed by U.S. imperialism." The telegram from all circles in Tangshan to the Standing Committee of the Paris Congress in Support of World Peace stated: "We firmly stand on the side of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and strike at any conspiracy of imperialists who dare to provoke wars of aggression, and defend world peace."

### 华北各地通电 　坚决保卫世界和平

1949-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　华北各地通电
   　坚决保卫世界和平
    【本报综合报导】十月二日——国际和平斗争日，中共绥远省委、绥远省人民政府、绥远军区、绥远各界庆祝大会、太原各界庆祝大会、唐山各界庆祝大会等，分别致电斯大林大元帅、世界保卫和平大会居里主席、巴黎拥护世界和平大会常委会、中国保卫和平大会、中苏友好协会，表示坚决反对侵略战争，拥护世界和平。太原各界向斯大林大元帅致敬电中称：世界人类有了您，是最值得骄傲的，在您的旗帜下，我们团结起来，为争取世界持久和平而斗争。在致居里电中称：我们坚信：团结就是力量，全世界爱好和平者团结一致，一定可以压倒反动派的。绥远、唐山各界在致世界和平大会中国分会、中苏友好协会电文中称：我们誓将响应你们的每一号召，以恢复与发展生产的实际行动，来打击以美帝为首的战争挑拨者的反动阴谋。唐山各界在致巴黎拥护世界和平大会常委会电文中称：我们坚决站在以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营，打击帝国主义者胆敢挑起侵略战争的任何阴谋行动，保卫世界和平。
