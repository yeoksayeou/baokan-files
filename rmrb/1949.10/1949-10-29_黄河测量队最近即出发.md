Title: Yellow River Survey Team to Depart Soon
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, October 28th. To prepare for the Yellow River harnessing and construction work next spring, the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission is organizing a Yellow River Survey Team. The first detachment of the team was formed on the 19th of this month, with a total of 36 members, and is preparing to depart soon to survey the topography of the Qin River.

### 黄河测量队最近即出发

1949-10-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　黄河测量队最近即出发
    【新华社开封二十八日电】为准备明春治理黄河的建设工作，黄河水利委员会正组织黄河测量队。该队第一大队已于本月十九日组成，共有队员三十六人，准备于最近出发测量沁河地形。
