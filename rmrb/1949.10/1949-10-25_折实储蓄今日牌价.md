Title: Current Price of Commodity Unit Savings

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 2

　　Current Price of Commodity Unit Savings
    Today (October 25th), the Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces the price of one standard commodity unit for commodity unit savings as 923.84 yuan.
