Title: Exchange Rate for Value-Guaranteed Savings Today
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 2

　　Exchange Rate for Value-Guaranteed Savings Today
    Today (29th), the standard commodity unit exchange rate for value-guaranteed savings at the Savings Department of the People's Bank of China's Beijing Branch is 1005.84 yuan.
