Title: Rectifying and Supporting Private Primary Schools: Beijing Education Bureau Holds Registration
Author:
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 4

　　Rectifying and Supporting Private Primary Schools
    Beijing Education Bureau Holds Registration
    [Beijing News] In order to rectify and support private primary schools, the Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau will conduct registration of private primary schools based on the "Provisional Measures for the Administration of Private Schools" stipulated by the bureau. The registration period is from now until the end of October. All private primary schools in the city must complete the registration. Schools can obtain registration forms from the Third Section of the Education Bureau and submit them as scheduled. From the date of registration, the Education Bureau will review and approve schools that can truly implement the New Democracy education policy and the people's government's education laws and decrees and have achieved results.

### 整顿扶植私立小学　京教育局举办登记

1949-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　整顿扶植私立小学
    京教育局举办登记
    【北京讯】北京市人民政府教育局为整顿与扶植私立小学，将依据该局所规定的“私立学校临时管理办法”举办私立小学登记，登记日期自即日起至十月底截止。凡属本市私立小学，均须履行登记，各校可到教育局第三科领取登记表格，如期填报。自登记之日起，经教育局审查，认为确能执行新民主主义教育方针及人民政府教育法令，并获有成绩时，即准予立案。
