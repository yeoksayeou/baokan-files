Title: Employees of the Bulgarian Public Enterprises Management Department Send a Telegram Congratulating Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 3

　　Bulgarian Public Enterprises Management Department Employees
    Send a Telegram Congratulating Chairman Mao
    【Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th】Sofia Dispatch: All employees of the Bulgarian Government's Public Enterprises Management Department recently sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to congratulate him. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party: The employees of the Bulgarian Public Enterprises Management Department extend our militant greetings to you—the leader of the Chinese Communist Party and the entire Chinese people—and hope that you will achieve new victories in finally liberating all of China from the shackles of imperialism and carrying out new democratic construction. The entry of hundreds of millions of Chinese people into the strong democratic and socialist camp led by the Soviet Union and the great Stalin will make a great contribution to defending peace and the future happiness of mankind.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live the Chinese Communist Party!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people!
    　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Employees' Congress of the Bulgarian Public Enterprises Management Department
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Bocho Nikolov

### 保公用企业管理部职工　电贺毛主席

1949-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　保公用企业管理部职工
    电贺毛主席
    【新华社布拉格五日电】索非亚讯：保加利亚政府公用企业管理部全体职工顷致电毛主席祝贺，电文如下：
    中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志：保加利亚公用企业管理部的职工们请你——中国共产党及全中国人民的领袖，向全中国劳动人民转致战斗的敬礼，并望在最后从帝国主义羁绊下解放全中国，和进行新民主主义建设中获得新的胜利，数万万中国人民进入坚强的以苏联和伟大斯大林领导下的民主和社会主义阵营，对保卫和平与人类未来的幸福将有莫大的贡献。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    　　　　　　　　保加利亚公用企业管理部职工大会主席
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　波其奥·尼可夫
