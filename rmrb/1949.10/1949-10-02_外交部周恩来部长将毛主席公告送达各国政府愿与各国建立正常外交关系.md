Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Sends Chairman Mao's Proclamation to Various Governments, Expressing Willingness to Establish Normal Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st.— Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, today sent Chairman Mao Zedong's proclamation to the governments of various countries with a letter, expressing the willingness to establish normal diplomatic relations with all countries. Minister Zhou sent the letter to the consuls of various countries in the old consulates in Beijing, requesting them to forward it to their respective governments. For countries without consulates in Beijing, but with embassies or legations in Nanjing, it was sent to the old embassies or legations of various countries in Nanjing. Minister Zhou's letter is as follows: "Dear Sir, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China issued a proclamation today. I am now sending this proclamation to you with this letter, hoping that you will forward it to your government. I believe that it is necessary for the People's Republic of China to establish normal diplomatic relations with all countries in the world. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. October 1, 1949, in Beijing."

### 外交部周恩来部长将毛主席公告送达各国政府愿与各国建立正常外交关系

1949-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　外交部周恩来部长将毛主席公告送达各国政府愿与各国建立正常外交关系
    【新华社北京一日电】中央人民政府外交部长周恩来，本日已将毛泽东主席的公告具函送达各外国政府。周部长系以公函送达各国在北京旧领事馆领事，请其转交各国政府。在北京无领事馆，而在南京有大使馆或公使馆者，则送达南京各国旧大使馆或公使馆。周部长的公函如下：“迳启者，中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席已在本日发表了公告。我现在将这个公告随函送达阁下，希为转交贵国政府。我认为中华人民共和国与世界各国建立正常的外交关系是需要的。中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来。一九四九年十月一日，于北京。”
