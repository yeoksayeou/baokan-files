Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    Tonight's Highlights
    [Our Dispatch] The Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's women's program at 18:15 will broadcast an article by Shuai Mengqi, Minister of the Organization Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation: "Revolution and Family." At 19:00, a lecture on natural science common sense, written by Zhai Yusheng of the Department of Geology, Peking University: "From Copper to China's Copper Mines." At 18:30, the first literary and art program, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Society: Guan Xuezeng performs the second section of the Qinshu "Own Flesh and Blood." At 22:00, the second literary and art program will broadcast the opera "Heroine Liu Hulan."
    Also, the second channel's children's time at 17:15: (1) Current affairs talk, (2) Songs by children from Changqiao Elementary School. At 17:45, employee time: Cui Yuying of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau Broadcasting Group will report on "The election of delegates to the Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress and the second session of the Bureau's Trade Union Executive Committee at the Second Trade Union Member Representative Congress of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau." At 18:00, new song practice: teaching the new song "Three Teams of Yellow Cattle and One Team of Horses." At 20:00, Voice of the City: Broadcasting the "Beijing Mechanism Flour Industry Labor-Capital Collective Contract" approved by the Beijing Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台　
    今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十八点十五分妇女节目，播送中华全国民主妇女联合会组织部部长帅孟奇的一篇文章：“革命与家庭”。十九点自然科学常识讲座，北京大学地质系翟裕生撰稿：“从铜谈到我国的铜矿”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：关学增演习琴书“亲骨肉”第二段。二十二点第二次文艺节目，播送歌剧“女英雄刘胡兰”。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间：（一）时事讲话，（二）由厂桥小学小朋友唱歌。十七时四十五分职工时间：由本台北京电信局广播小组崔玉英报导“北京电信局第二届工会会员代表大会选举出席本市职工代表大会的代表和该局第二届工会执行委员的情形”。十八时新歌练习：教唱新歌“三套黄牛一套马”。二十时市政之声：播送北京市人民政府劳动局批准的“北京市机制面粉业劳资集体合同。”
