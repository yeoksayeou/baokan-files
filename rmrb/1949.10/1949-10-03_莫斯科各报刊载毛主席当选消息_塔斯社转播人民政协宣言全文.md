Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish News of Chairman Mao's Election; TASS Relays Full Text of CPPCC Manifesto
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 1

　　 Moscow Newspapers Publish News of Chairman Mao's Election
   　TASS Relays Full Text of CPPCC Manifesto
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd] According to TASS Moscow dispatch of the 30th, Moscow newspapers published news from Beijing describing the enthusiastic celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China by the Chinese people. Furthermore, the Beijing receiving station has been receiving TASS broadcasts of the proceedings of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for days. On the 29th, TASS relayed the Xinhua News Agency's news of the adoption of the Common Program by the CPPCC.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd] According to TASS Moscow dispatch of the 1st: all newspapers published the Xinhua News Agency's news about the closing of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the election of Mao Zedong as Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. On the same day, TASS also relayed the full text of the People's Political Consultative Conference Declaration and the full text of the Xinhua News Agency editorial "The Solemn Declaration of the Chinese People to the World," which refuted the Kuomintang reactionary clique's representative Jiang Tingfu's shameless anti-Soviet speech at the United Nations General Assembly. Also, according to a dispatch from our agency in Prague on the 30th: the London Daily Worker, in a prominent position on the front page with a banner headline "China Denies Kuomintang Representation at the United Nations," comprehensively reported the resolutions of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on the organization of the Central People's Government and the denial of Kuomintang representation at the United Nations, as well as Vyshinsky's speech at the UN General Assembly denouncing Jiang Tingfu's "accusations" against the Soviet Union.

### 莫斯科各报刊载毛主席当选消息 　塔斯社转播人民政协宣言全文

1949-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　 莫斯科各报刊载毛主席当选消息
   　塔斯社转播人民政协宣言全文
    【新华社北京二日电】据塔斯社莫斯科三十日讯，莫斯科各报刊载北京消息，描述中国人民热烈庆祝中华人民共和国诞生的情形。又北京收抄站连日收到塔斯社广播中国人民政协会议进行情况。塔斯社二十九日转播了新华社所发人民政协通过共同纲领的消息。
    【新华社北京二日电】据塔斯社莫斯科一日讯：各报皆刊载新华社关于中国人民政治协商会议闭幕和毛泽东当选为中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席的消息。塔斯社亦于同日转播人民政治协商会议宣言的全文和新华社驳斥国民党反动集团代表蒋廷黻在联合国大会无耻的反苏演说的社论“中国人民对全世界的庄严宣告”全文。又据本社布拉格三十日消息：伦敦工人日报于第一版首要地位以通栏大字标题“中国否认国民党在联合国的代表权”综合报道中国人民政协会议关于中央人民政府的组织和否认国民党在联合国代表权的决议，以及维辛斯基在联大痛斥蒋廷黻对苏联的“控诉”的演说。
