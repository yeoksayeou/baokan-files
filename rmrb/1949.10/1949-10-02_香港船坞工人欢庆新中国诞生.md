Title: Hong Kong Dockworkers Celebrate the Birth of New China
Author:
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 2
　　
　　Hong Kong Dockworkers
   　Celebrate the Birth of New China
    【Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 1st】According to the Shanghai Ta Kung Pao: Dockworkers from the Hong Kong Naval Dockyard and the Kowloon and Laolian Dockyards jointly held a grand evening party on the 29th to celebrate the convening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the birth of the People's Republic of China. The national flag of the People's Republic of China was hung in the venue, and everyone sang the new national anthem, with very enthusiastic emotions.

### 香港船坞工人欢庆新中国诞生

1949-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　香港船坞工人
   　欢庆新中国诞生
    【新华社上海一日电】据上海大公报消息：香港海军船坞及九龙、劳联三大船坞工人，于二十九日晚联合举行盛大晚会，庆祝人民政治协商会议的召开和中华人民共和国的诞生。会场中悬挂中华人民共和国国旗，齐唱新国歌，情绪非常热烈。
