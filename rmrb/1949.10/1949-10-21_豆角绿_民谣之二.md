String Bean Green - Folk Song Two
    Tian Qi
    Pumpkin sweet, string bean green,
        Sister-in-law on the kang
        Nurturing the baby;
        Younger sister-in-law on the ground
        Cutting pumpkin;
        The two are truly like sisters,
        Chatting intimately about family matters;
        Sister-in-law says:
        "Your brother is at the front line,
        Making great contributions!"
        Younger sister-in-law says:
        "Sister-in-law, you are not weak either!
        You are still a good production model!"
        Hearing this, sister-in-law is a little shy,
        Her face like red pumpkin pulp;
        Rural women are all strong,
        Our people's country's days are getting better and better!
    Autumn, Yang Village

### 豆角绿——民谣之二
田奇
1949-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　豆角绿——民谣之二
    田奇
    南瓜甜，豆角绿，
    嫂嫂在炕上
    奶娃娃；
    小姑子在地上
    切南瓜；
    两人真象亲姊妹，
    亲亲热热话家常；
    嫂嫂说：
    “你哥哥在前线，
    立了大功劳！”
    小姑子说：
    “嫂嫂你也不弱呀！
    生产还是个好模范！”
    说得嫂嫂有些羞，
    脸蛋好象红瓜瓤；
    农村妇女个个强，
    咱人民国家的日子往上升！
    秋天·杨村·
