Title: Liu Xiufeng Appointed Deputy Mayor of Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 5

　　Liu Xiufeng
  　Appointed Deputy Mayor of Tianjin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th] The North China People's Government has appointed Liu Xiufeng as the Deputy Mayor of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government. Deputy Mayor Liu reported to his post and began work on the 29th.

### 刘秀峰任津副市长

1949-10-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　刘秀峰
  　任津副市长
    【新华社北京三十日电】华北人民政府任命刘秀峰为天津市人民政府副市长。刘副市长已于二十九日到职工作。
