Title: Government Administration Council Holds Second Meeting; Establishes Committee to Receive Requests from Puppet Central Organs; Takes Over Former North China People's Government; Central People's Government Agencies to Begin Work on the First of Next Month
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 25th: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its second administrative meeting today. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Government Councilors Tan Pingshan, Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Bo Yibo, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Bojun, Li Lisan, Ma Xulun, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shao＠, and Acting Secretary-General Qi Yanming were present. Absent were: Government Councilors Zeng Shan and Secretary-General Li Weihan (excused due to illness). The meeting resolved the following matters: (1) Regarding the issue of receiving personnel files and materials from the former Kuomintang government's central organs, it was resolved to establish a receiving committee, consisting of Chen Yun, Dong Biwu, Shao Lizi, Huang Shao＠, Zhang Naiqi, and the following five individuals: one from the Government Administration Council (Deputy Secretary-General Guo Chuntao), one from the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, one jointly recommended by the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and the Political-Legal and People's Supervision Committees, one from the Financial and Economic Committee, and one from the Cultural and Educational Committee, with Vice Premier Chen Yun taking overall responsibility. It was also resolved to task the receiving committee with formulating a draft of the receiving committee's work regulations and submit it to the next Government Administration Council meeting for discussion. (2) Regarding the takeover of various provinces and cities under the former North China People's Government and the takeover of its subordinate ministries, commissions, courts, and banks, it was resolved to request the Chairman of the Central People's Government to issue an order announcing the end of the work of the former North China People's Government. The five provinces and two cities under the jurisdiction of the former North China People's Government will be directly under the Central Government. The ministries, commissions, courts, and banks of the Central People's Government will establish working bodies based on the subordinate ministries, commissions, courts, and banks of the former North China People's Government; and it was resolved that all ministries, commissions, courts, and administrations of the Central People's Government will officially begin work on November 1st. Finally, the meeting discussed several principles regarding the general organizational rules of the various departments of the Government Administration Council.

### 政务院开二次会议  设委员会接收伪中央机关呈请接管原华北人民政府  中央人民政府各机构定下月一日办公

1949-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院开二次会议　
    设委员会接收伪中央机关呈请接管原华北人民政府            
    中央人民政府各机构定下月一日办公
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中央人民政府政务院今日举行第二次政务会议，到总理周恩来，副总理董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培，政务委员谭平山、谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、薄一波、滕代远、章伯钧、李立三、马叙伦、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、黄绍＠，代理秘书长齐燕铭。缺席者为：政务委员曾山，秘书长李维汉（因病请假）。会议决议事项如下：（一）关于接收前国民党政府中央各机关人员档案物资问题，决议组织接收委员会，由陈云、董必武、邵力子、黄绍＠、章乃器及下列五人即政务院一人（副秘书长郭春涛），人民革命军事委员会一人，最高人民法院、最高人民检察署及政治法律、人民监察两委员会合推一人，财经委员会一人，文教委员会一人组成之，由副总理陈云负总责。并决议责成该接收委员会制定接收委员会工作条例草案，交下次政务会议讨论。（二）关于接管原华北人民政府所属各省市及接管其所辖各部、会、院、行问题，决议：呈请中央人民政府主席发表命令，宣布原华北人民政府工作结束，原华北人民政府所辖五省二市归中央直属，中央人民政府各部、会、院、行以原华北人民政府所属各部、会、院、行为基础建立工作机构；并决议中央人民政府各部、会、院、署，一律于十一月一日正式开始办公。会议最后讨论了政务院各部门组织通则的几项原则问题。
