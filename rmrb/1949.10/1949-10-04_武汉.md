Title: Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 3

　　【Our Correspondent in Wuhan, 2nd Dispatch】 A rally of people from all walks of life in Wuhan celebrating the founding of the Central People's Government and supporting the International Day of Struggle for Peace was held at Zhongshan Park at 5 p.m. After lunch, 150,000 people held aloft brand-new national flags, beat gongs and drums, and flocked to the venue from all directions. When the presidium announced cheers for the new China and the world's democratic forces, red flags waved everywhere, and military music, salutes, gongs, drums, and cheers rang out simultaneously for a full quarter of an hour. At the meeting, Mayor Wu Defeng said: The establishment of the Central People's Government is a guarantee for defending world peace. Comrade Lin Biao pointed out: The establishment of the Central People's Government is a major event for China and the world. He also said: We must establish a strong national defense army to defend our country. Finally, he called on all troops on the front line to resolutely obey Commander-in-Chief Zhu's orders, continue to advance, and eliminate the remaining enemies. In the rear, we should work hard to produce and support the front. At 6:30 p.m., the meeting ended, and red flags, torches, lanterns, dragon lanterns, and bands of Yangko dancers entered the brightly lit urban area.

### 武汉

1949-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　武汉
    【本报记者武汉二日专电】武汉各界庆祝中央人民政府成立及拥护国际和平斗争日的大会于午后五时在中山公园举行。十五万人民在午饭后高举着崭新的国旗，敲锣打鼓，从各方拥往会场。当主席台宣布为新中国和世界民主力量欢呼时，到处红旗摇摆，军乐、礼炮、锣鼓、鞭炮、欢呼声齐响，达一刻钟之久。会上主席吴德峰市长说：中央人民政府的成立，是保卫世界和平的保证。林彪同志指出：中央人民政府的成立，是中国也是全世界的大事。他又说：我们要建立强大国防军来保卫我们的国家。最后他号召全线部队要坚决服从朱总司令的命令，继续前进，消灭残敌。在后方则应努力生产，支援前线。六时半，大会结束，红旗火把，灯笼龙灯，乐队秧歌，进入了灯火辉煌的市区。
