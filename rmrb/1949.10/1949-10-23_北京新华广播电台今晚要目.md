Title: Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Programs

Author:
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Radio Station
  　Tonight's Programs
    [Our Newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Radio Station will broadcast a workers' program at 18:15, introducing the labor movement in Siam, one of the member countries of the Asian and Australian Workers' Representative Conference. At 20:15, it will broadcast a recording of Liu Fei, a member of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, giving a radio speech to the remaining Kuomintang troops in Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, and Guangxi. At 19:00, a popular science lecture: "The Great Soviet Biologist Timiryazev". At 18:30, the first arts and entertainment program, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Association: Wu Yueqiu sings the Tiepian Dagu "Fiancee Persuades Husband to Join the Army". At 22:00, the second arts and entertainment program broadcasts the new Peking Opera "Mulan Joins the Army".
    Channel Two at 17:15: Children's Time: Mr. Sun Jingxiu continues to tell "The Story of Paul". 17:45 Youth Time: Broadcast a newsletter titled "We Launched a Production and Savings Competition". 18:00 Music Program: The Beijing Municipal Normal School Chorus broadcasts new songs. 20:00 Voice of the Municipality: Beijing Municipal People's Government News Office reports on "The City Tax Bureau's Inspection of Private Wine Production".

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十八点十五分职工节目，介绍亚、澳洲职工代表会议会员国之一——暹罗的职工运动。二十点十五分播送中国人民革命军事委员会委员刘斐对四川、云南、贵州、广西的国民党残余部队广播演讲的录音。十九点自然科学常识讲座：“伟大的苏联生物学家季米里亚席夫”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：吴月秋演唱铁片大鼓“未婚妻劝夫参军”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，播送新京剧“木兰从军”。
    又二台十七时十五分儿童时间：由孙敬修先生继续讲“保尔的故事”。十七时四十五分青年时间：播送一篇通讯题目是“我们展开了生产节约竞赛”。十八时音乐节目：北京市立师范学校歌咏团播送新歌曲。二十时市政之声：北京市人民政府新闻处关于“本市税务局检查私酒工作”的报导。
