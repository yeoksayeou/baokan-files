Title: Shanghai Military Control Commission Notice: Cessation of Activities of Foreign Journalists from Countries Without Diplomatic Relations with Us
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 7th: The Shanghai Military Control Commission issued a notice on the 6th, stipulating that from now on, all journalists in Shanghai from foreign newspapers, magazines, news agencies, radio stations, etc., that do not have diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China, regardless of whether they are of Chinese or foreign nationality, must cease activities in their capacity as journalists (including sending news telegrams).

### 沪市军管会通告　停止与我无外交关系之外国记者活动

1949-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪市军管会通告
    停止与我无外交关系之外国记者活动
    【新华社上海七日电】上海市军管会于六日发出通告，规定自即日起，凡与我中华人民共和国无外交关系之各外国报纸、刊物、通讯社、广播电台等在沪记者，无论其为中国籍抑外国籍，一律停止以记者身分活动（包括拍发新闻电在内）。
