Title: Soviet-Romanian Border Treaty Signed
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th. TASS Moscow dispatch: The Soviet-Romanian Boundary Commission, established in accordance with the decision of the governments of the Soviet Union and the Romanian People's Republic, has demarcated the boundary between the Soviet Union and the Romanian People's Republic in accordance with Article 1 of the Treaty of Peace with Romania (signed in Paris on September 15, 1947, and entered into force). On the 27th, the Soviet and Romanian delegations of the commission signed a treaty in Bucharest with explanatory texts, maps, and other documents of the boundary line.

### 苏联罗马尼亚签定划界条约

1949-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联罗马尼亚签定划界条约
    【新华社北京二十九日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：依照苏联和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府的决定而成立的苏罗疆界委员会业已遵照对罗和约（一九四七年九月十五日在巴黎签订并发生效力）的第一条划定了苏联和罗马尼亚人民共和国之间的疆界。该委员会的苏罗两国代表团二十七日在布加勒斯特签订了有疆界线说明文及地图和其他文件的条约。
