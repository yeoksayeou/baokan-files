Title: Leap Forward in Polish Cooperative Business: Membership Increases to Five Million

Author:

Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: The Supreme Council of Polish Cooperatives held a plenary meeting in Warsaw at the beginning of the month. Professor Lanki, Chairman of the Central Cooperative Union, reported that in 1949, the cooperative business was included in the national economic plan for the first time. As of September 1, the number of cooperative branches had exceeded 30,000, and the number of members had increased to five million. The council passed important resolutions such as developing membership and learning from Soviet experience, instructed cooperatives to strengthen the fight against illiteracy, and called for participation in the "Polish-Soviet Friendship Month" activities.

### 波合作事业跃进　社员增至五百万

1949-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　波合作事业跃进
  　社员增至五百万
    【新华社北京十九日电】波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰合作社最高理事会曾于月初在华沙召开全体会议，中央合作社联盟主席兰基教授报告工作称：一九四九年，合作事业首次列入国民经济计划中，至九月一日为止，合作社支社数目已超过三万个，社员增至五百万。理事会通过发展社员，学习苏联经验等重要决议，指令合作社加强消灭文盲的斗争，并号召参加“波苏友好月”工作。
