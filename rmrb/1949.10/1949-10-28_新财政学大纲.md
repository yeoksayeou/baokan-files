　　New Public Finance Outline
　　New China University Series · Authored by Qian Jiaju
　　The author, strictly based on the Marxist-Leninist standpoint, criticizes bourgeois financial theories while establishing a new Marxist-Leninist view of finance. This is a brand-new theoretical work on public finance. In the economic construction of New Democracy, finance is a crucial link, and to build a new finance, a correct financial theory is essential. This book pays special attention to Chinese facts and materials, analyzing and researching them from a Marxist-Leninist perspective. It can serve not only as a textbook for universities but also as a valuable reference book for financial and economic cadres. (Base price: 1.90 yuan)
