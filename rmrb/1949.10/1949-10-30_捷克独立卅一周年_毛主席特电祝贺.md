Title: 31st Anniversary of Czech Independence - Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 1

　　Czech Independence 31st Anniversary
    Chairman Mao Sends Special Telegram of Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th] Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram of congratulations on the 31st anniversary of the independence of the Czechoslovak Republic. The telegram reads: To His Excellency, President Gottwald of the Czechoslovak Republic:
    On the occasion of the 31st anniversary of your country's independence, I extend my warm congratulations and wish the friendly relations between China and Czechoslovakia grow closer day by day.
    Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    October 29, 1949

### 捷克独立卅一周年  毛主席特电祝贺

1949-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷克独立卅一周年　
    毛主席特电祝贺
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东电贺捷克斯洛伐克共和国独立三十一周年纪念。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德阁下：
    欣逢贵国独立三十一周年纪念，谨致热烈的祝贺，并祝中捷两国邦交日臻亲密。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席
    毛泽东
    一九四九年十月二十九日
