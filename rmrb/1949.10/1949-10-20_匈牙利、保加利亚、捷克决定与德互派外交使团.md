Title: Hungary, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia Decide to Exchange Diplomatic Missions with Germany
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, Budapest, 18th: The government of the Hungarian People's Republic has decided to exchange diplomatic missions with the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, Sofia, 18th: Bulgarian People's Republic Foreign Minister Pop Tomov has telegraphed German Democratic Republic Provisional Government Foreign Minister Dodinger stating: The Bulgarian government has decided to exchange diplomatic missions with the government of the German Democratic Republic.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, Prague: The government of the Czechoslovak Republic decided on the 18th to exchange diplomatic missions with the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic. Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Siroky, representing the Czechoslovak government, notified the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Dodinger, of this decision.

### 匈牙利、保加利亚、捷克决定与德互派外交使团

1949-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利、保加利亚、捷克决定与德互派外交使团
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社布达佩斯十八日讯：匈牙利人民共和国政府已决定与德意志民主共和国临时政府互派外交使团。
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社索非亚十八日讯：保加利亚人民共和国外交部长波普托莫夫已致电德意志民主共和国临时政府外长窦丁格尔称：保加利亚政府已决定与德意志民主共和国政府互派外交使团。
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克人民共和国政府十八日决定，与德意志民主共和国临时政府互派外交使团。捷副总理西罗基代表捷政府，将这一决定通知德意志民主共和国政府外交部长窦丁格尔。
