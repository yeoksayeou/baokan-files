Title: Striving to Build a New Suiyuan: Suiyuan Celebration Rally Sends Telegrams of Congratulations to Commander Nie, Political Commissar Bo, and Chairman Dong Biwu
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Striving to Build a New Suiyuan
    Suiyuan Celebration Rally Sends Telegrams of Congratulations to Commander Nie, Political Commissar Bo, and Chairman Dong Biwu
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Suiyuan People's Celebration Rally for the Establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Suiyuan Military and Civilian Uprising sent a telegram to Commander Nie of the North China Military Region and Political Commissar Bo, the text of which is as follows:
    Today, the people of all of Suiyuan are holding a grand meeting to celebrate the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the peaceful liberation of Suiyuan. The rally extends its deep congratulations to you for your consistent hard work for the people's revolution and your glorious achievements in liberating North China. After reading your reply to General Dong Qiwu and others, which provided correct and valuable instructions on transforming thinking, changing work styles, striving for progress, building Suiyuan into a true people's liberated area, and reorganizing the army into a true people's army, and striving to build a new Suiyuan, a new North China, and the complete liberation of all of China, we are very excited. We vow to resolutely fight for this goal.
    Suiyuan People's Celebration Rally for the Establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Suiyuan Military and Civilian Uprising. Xidongyin.
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Suiyuan People's Celebration Rally for the Establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Suiyuan Military and Civilian Uprising sent a telegram to Chairman Dong Biwu of the North China People's Government on October 1st, expressing congratulations. The telegram stated: "Today, we, the people of Suiyuan, are holding a grand rally to celebrate the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the peaceful liberation of Suiyuan, and thank you for your diligent leadership in the construction of the people's cause in North China. We, the people of Suiyuan, must follow your call, work hard to build a true people's liberated area, and strive to build a new China for the people. We wish you good health!" (Xinhua News Agency)

### 为建立新绥远而奋斗　绥远庆祝大会电聂司令员薄政委董必武主席致贺

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　为建立新绥远而奋斗
    绥远庆祝大会电聂司令员薄政委董必武主席致贺
    【本报讯】绥远人民庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立暨绥远军民起义大会，致电华北军区聂司令员薄政委，电文如下：
    今天全绥远的人民热烈开会庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立和绥远的和平解放，大会对你们一贯为人民革命的辛苦和解放华北的光荣业绩，致深切的慰贺。看了你们回答董其武将军们的复电，正确而宝贵的指示改造思想，改变作风，力求进步，把绥远建设成真正的人民解放区，把军队改编成真正的人民解放军，为建设新绥远、新华北及彻底解放全中国而奋斗；我们非常兴奋，我们誓为这个目标而坚决奋斗。
    绥远人民庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立暨绥远军民起义大会西东印。
    【本报讯】绥远人民庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立及绥远军民起义大会，于十月一日致电华北人民政府董必武主席，表示庆祝，电文称：“今天我们绥远人民热烈地开大会，庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立和绥远的和平解放，感谢你对华北人民建设事业的辛勤领导。我们绥远人民一定要依照你的号召，努力建设成一个真正的人民解放区并为建设人民的新中国而奋斗，祝你健康！”（华新室）
