Title: Zhou Enlai's Welcome Speech
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st: When the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers' delegation participating in the Chinese People's Conference for World Peace and the inaugural meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association arrived in Beijing, Zhou Enlai, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a welcome speech at the welcoming ceremony at the station. The full text is as follows:

Dear Mr. Fadeyev and all members of the Soviet delegation!

On behalf of the Chinese people, I warmly welcome the delegation from our great friendly country, the Soviet Union, to China, and pay our highest respects to all of you!

It is a great honor for the Chinese people that you have come to China to participate in the Chinese People's Conference for World Peace and the inaugural meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Your arrival in China coincides with the founding of the People's Republic of China. This is a symbolic gesture, indicating that the great Soviet Union is the first friendly country of the newly born People's Republic of China, and that Sino-Soviet friendship will develop along with the development of the People's Republic of China.

Long live our great friendly country, the Soviet Union!

Long live Marshal Stalin!

Long live Sino-Soviet friendship! (Attached picture)

### 周恩来欢迎词

1949-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　周恩来欢迎词
    【新华社北京一日电】参加中国保卫世界和平大会和中苏友好协会总会成立大会的苏文化艺术科学工作者代表团抵京时，中苏友好协会总会筹委会副主任周恩来在车站欢迎会上致欢迎词。全文如下：
    亲爱的法捷耶夫先生和苏联代表团全体先生们！
    我代表中国人民热烈地欢迎我们伟大友邦苏联的代表团来到中国，并向代表团的诸位先生致崇高的敬礼！
    诸位先生这次来到中国，参加中国保卫世界和平大会和中苏友好协会总会成立大会，使中国人民感觉极大的光荣。
    诸位先生来到中国的时候，正当中华人民共和国成立的时候。这是一个巧妙的象征，表示伟大的苏联乃是新生的中华人民共和国的第一个友邦，表示中苏友谊将随着中华人民共和国的发展而发展。
    我们的伟大友邦苏联万岁！
    斯大林大元帅万岁！
    中苏友好万岁！（附图片）
