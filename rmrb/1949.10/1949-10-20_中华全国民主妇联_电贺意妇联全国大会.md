Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Italian Women's Federation National Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3

　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation
  　Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Italian Women's Federation National Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram on October 19th to congratulate the Third National Congress of the Italian Women's League, which is being held in Rome. The telegram reads as follows: To Mrs. Rossi and dear sisters:
    We are delighted to learn that the Third National Congress of the Italian Women's League is being held in Rome on the 14th of this month. This congress, which represents the aspirations of millions of democratic women in Italy, is of extremely great significance for the people of the world in striving for democracy, freedom, opposing war, and defending peace. We, on behalf of the democratic women of China, extend our warm congratulations to your congress and wish it success.
　　　　　　Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation　 Cai Chang
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 19th

### 中华全国民主妇联　电贺意妇联全国大会

1949-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　中华全国民主妇联
  　电贺意妇联全国大会
    【新华社北京十九日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会于十月十九日电贺正在罗马举行的意大利妇女联盟第三届全国大会。电文如下：罗西夫人并转亲爱的姐妹们：
    欣闻意大利妇女联盟第三届全国代表大会于本月十四日在罗马举行，这个代表了意大利数百万民主妇女愿望的大会，对于世界人民争取民主、自由、反对战争、保卫和平有着极其重大的意义，我们代表中国民主妇女向贵会致热烈的祝贺，并预祝贵会成功。　　　　　　中华全国民主妇女联合会主席　蔡畅
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十月十九日
