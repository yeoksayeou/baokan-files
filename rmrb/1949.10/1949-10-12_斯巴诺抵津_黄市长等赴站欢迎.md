Title: Spano Arrives in Tianjin; Mayor Huang and Others Welcome Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 1

　　Spano Arrives in Tianjin
    Mayor Huang and Others Welcome Him at the Station
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 11th] Italian Communist Party Central Committee member and Italian Senator Spano arrived in Tianjin by train from Beijing on the afternoon of the 10th. Tianjin Mayor Huang Jing, Deputy Mayor Liu Xiufeng, and others went to the station to welcome him. This afternoon, Spano visited the No. 2 Textile Factory and was warmly welcomed by the workers.

### 斯巴诺抵津　黄市长等赴站欢迎

1949-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　斯巴诺抵津
    黄市长等赴站欢迎
    【新华社天津十一日电】意共中央委员、意参议员斯巴诺于十日下午由京乘车抵津，天津市市长黄敬，副市长刘秀峰等均赴车站欢迎。今日下午，期巴诺赴中纺二厂参观，受到职工热烈欢迎。
