Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Arrives in Shenyang, Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Welcome Reception
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 30th -- The Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation, a group of thirty-three individuals, arrived in Shenyang today on their way back to the Soviet Union. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Li Fuchun, Deputy-Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, Lin Feng, Vice-Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and over thirty other leading members of Party and government organizations. At 2 PM this afternoon, over ten thousand workers in Shenyang held a welcoming rally at the Municipal Public Stadium. Comrade Li Fuchun delivered a welcoming speech, expressing warm respect to the Soviet friends who had come to Shenyang twice and to their outstanding work achievements in China. Those who spoke at the meeting upon invitation included Dubrovin, Deputy Minister of the People's Education of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic (female), and Deomidov, Chairman of the International Book Association. Their enthusiastic and moving speeches were repeatedly drowned out by warm applause. Renowned artists of the Soviet art performance team, Klavchenko (female), Balinova (female), and Smelyov, as well as the Red Army Song and Dance Ensemble, performed wonderful recreational programs such as piano, violin, solo singing, and dance at the meeting. At 8 PM, the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association held a grand welcome reception at the Northeast Cinema. At the reception, the famous Soviet painter Finozhenov and Markov, Deputy Minister of the Far East Department of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, delivered speeches, and the art performance team performed various excellent programs.

### 苏文化代表团抵沈  东北中苏友协举办欢迎晚会

1949-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏文化代表团抵沈　
    东北中苏友协举办欢迎晚会
    【新华社沈阳三十日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团一行三十三人，在返苏途中今日抵达沈阳。前往车站欢迎者有中共中央东北局副书记李富春、东北人民政府副主席林枫及其他党政机关首长三十余人。今日下午二时沈阳万余工人在市立公共体育场举行欢迎大会，李富春同志致欢迎词，对两度来到沈阳此次胜利返国的苏联友人及其在中国的卓越的工作成绩，表示热忱的敬意。会上应邀讲话的有俄罗斯联邦共和国人民教育部副部长杜伯诺维娜（女）和国际书籍协会主席德奥米多夫，他们热情感人的演说，时时被热烈的掌声淹没。苏联艺术演出队的著名艺术家克拉夫青柯（女）、巴利诺瓦（女）、史灭略夫三人和红军歌舞团并在会上表演了钢琴、小提琴、独唱和舞蹈等精彩的游艺节目。晚八时，东北中苏友好协会又在东北电影院举行了盛大的欢迎晚会。会上苏联著名画家费诺格诺夫和苏联对外文化协会远东部副部长马尔柯夫发表演说，艺术演出队又出演了各种精采节目。
