Title: List of Premier, Vice Premiers, Government Councilors, Secretary-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

Author:

Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: The complete list of the Vice Premiers, Government Councilors, Secretary-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General of the Government Administration Council appointed by the Central People's Government Committee at its third meeting today, together with the Premier of the Government Administration Council appointed at the first meeting of the Government Committee on October 1st, is as follows:

Premier of the Government Administration Council:
	Zhou Enlai (Chinese Communist Party);
Vice Premiers (4 persons):
	Dong Biwu (Chinese Communist Party),
	Chen Yun (Chinese Communist Party),
	Guo Moruo (Non-Party Democrat),
	Huang Yanpei (China Democratic National Construction Association).
Government Councilors (15 persons):
	Tan Pingshan (Three People's Principles Comrades Association),
	Xie Juezai (Chinese Communist Party),
	Luo Ruiqing (Chinese Communist Party),
	Bo Yibo (Chinese Communist Party),
	Zeng Shan (Chinese Communist Party),
	Teng Daiyuan (Chinese Communist Party),
	Zhang Bojun (China Democratic League),
	Li Lisan (Chinese Communist Party),
	Ma Xulun (China Association for Promoting Democracy),
	Chen Shaoxian (Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang),
	Wang Kunlun (Three People's Principles Comrades Association),
	Luo Longji (China Democratic League),
	Zhang Naiqi (China Democratic National Construction Association),
	Shao Lizi (Kuomintang Democrats),
	Huang Shao＠ (Kuomintang Democrats).
Secretary-General:
	Li Weihan (Chinese Communist Party);
Deputy Secretaries-General (5 persons):
	Qi Yanming, Xu Guangping, Guo Chuntao, Sun Qimeng, Xin Zhichao.
