Title: National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference: Three Telegrams

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report from this city:

　　National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference
  　Three Telegrams

[This city's news] The National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference passed three telegrams, the original text of which is as follows:

　　　　　(1) Telegram of Congratulations to the Central People's Government on its Establishment To the Central People's Government:

On the third day of the government's declaration of its establishment, we are holding a meeting here, filled with extreme excitement and joy. The Chinese people have stood up! We have our own government! This entirely new situation is precisely what revolutionaries have striven for and what the people of the whole country have longed for over the past century. We respectfully express our warm congratulations and support to the government.

In the chapter on "Cultural and Educational Policies" of the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, there are clear provisions for the publishing industry. This is the task assigned to us by the people of the whole country. Although we have done a great deal in the past, we must do even better and more conscientiously in the future under the leadership of the government and in accordance with the provisions of the Common Program. We firmly guarantee to strive to usher in a high tide of cultural construction in the new China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1949

　　　　(2) Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Respected Chairman Mao:

The National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference has opened in the people's capital. We, the representatives from the Northeast, Northwest, East China, Central China, and North China, respectfully extend our highest respect and heartfelt gratitude to you. Over the past few years, thanks to your wise leadership and the correct instructions of our Party, we have persevered in our publishing work, overcome difficulties, and carried out the task of supplying cultural sustenance to the broad masses of workers, peasants, and the People's Liberation Army. Today, the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference has been victoriously concluded, and the People's Republic of China has been born. With the arrival of the high tide of economic and cultural construction, our tasks will become even heavier. We must follow your instructions, redouble our efforts to conscientiously do a good job in publishing, and actively strive for the early realization of the task of ensuring that the Chinese people, possessing a high level of culture, appear in the world!

We wish you good health!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1949

　　　　(3) Telegram of Respect to Commander-in-Chief Zhu Respected Commander-in-Chief Zhu:

We are now gathered in the people's capital, holding the National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference. Rejoicing in the grand celebration of the birth of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the Central People's Government, we feel extremely excited and joyous. The annihilation of the Kuomintang reactionaries' army and the overthrow of the reactionary rule are entirely due to the meritorious deeds established by our People's Liberation Army, fighting bravely and selflessly with the assistance of the people of the whole country. We respectfully extend our highest respect and deepest regards to you and all the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army. We have already begun to work on supplying the troops with cultural sustenance and promoting the study of cadres and soldiers, and have initially opened bookstores and libraries in the field armies. Through this meeting, we will determine concrete steps to greatly strengthen and develop this work. We hereby assure you that we will actively strive to extensively promote the New Democratic Culture and Mao Zedong Thought in the army! We wish you good health!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1949

### 全国新华书店出版工作会议　通电三则

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国新华书店出版工作会议
  　通电三则
    【本市讯】全国新华书店出版工作会议通过通电三则，原电如下：
    　　　　　（一）祝贺中央人民政府成立电中央人民政府：
    在政府宣告成立的第三天，我们在这里召开会议，兴奋快乐到了极点。中国人民站立起来了，我们有了自己的政府，一百多年来革命家所努力的，全国人民所期望的，就是这么样一个全新的局面。我们敬向政府表示热烈的祝贺与拥护。
    在中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领的“文化教育政策”一章里，对于出版事业有了明确的规定。这是全国人民指派给我们的任务。我们在以往虽然也做了不少，今后在政府的领导之下，在共同纲领的规定之下，一定要做得更好更认真。我们坚决保证为迎接新中国文化建设的高潮而努力奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　全国新华书店出版工作会议
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月三日
    　　　　（二）向毛主席致敬电敬爱的毛主席：
    全国新华书店出版工作会议在人民的首都揭幕了。我们来自东北、西北、华东、华中、华北各地的代表们，谨向你致崇高的敬意和衷诚的感谢。过去这些年来，由于你的英明领导，我党的正确指示，我们在出版工作岗位上坚持着，克服了困难，执行了向广大的工人、农民、和人民解放军供应文化食粮的任务。今天，中国人民政治协商会议已经胜利成功，中华人民共和国已经诞生，随着经济建设和文化建设的高潮的到来，我们的任务更将加重，我们更要遵守你的指示，加倍努力地认真作好出版工作，为争取全国人民具有高度文化出现于世界这一任务的早日实现而积极奋斗！
    敬祝你健康！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　全国新华书店出版工作会议
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月三日
    　　　　（三）向朱总司令致敬电敬爱的朱总司令：
    我们现在正集合在人民的首都，举行全国新华书店出版工作会议。恭逢中华人民共和国的诞生，中央人民政府成立的盛典，我们感到万分的兴奋和欢忭。国民党反动派的军队得以消灭，反动统治得以推翻，完全是依靠我人民解放军在全国人民援助之下，奋不顾身，英勇作战所建立的功绩。我们谨向你和人民解放军全体指战员同志们致崇高的敬意和深切的慰问。我们对于供应部队文化食粮，推动干部战士学习的工作，已在开始着手，并已初步开辟了野战军中的书店和图书馆。通过这一会议，我们将要定出具体步骤，大大加强和发展这一工作。我们谨向你保证，我们更要为在部队中广泛宣传新民主主义文化和毛泽东思想这一任务而积极奋斗！敬祝你健康！
    　　　　　　　　　　　全国新华书店出版工作会议
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月三日
