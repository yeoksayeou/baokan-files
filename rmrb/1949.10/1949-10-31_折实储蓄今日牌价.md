Title: Savings Deposits in Real Terms Today's Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 2

　　Savings Deposits in Real Terms Today's Exchange Rate
    The People's Bank of China, Beijing Branch, Savings Department announced that today's (31st) exchange rate for a standard unit of commodities for savings deposits in real terms is 1043.28 yuan.
