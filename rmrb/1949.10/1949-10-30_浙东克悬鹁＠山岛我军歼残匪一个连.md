Title: In eastern Zhejiang, our army captured a remnant bandit company on Xianbo@shan Island
Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Eastern Zhejiang Front, 29th. Following the liberation of Peach Blossom Island, the People's Liberation Army on the Eastern Zhejiang Front captured Xianbo@shan Island, northeast of Peach Blossom Island, on the 23rd, annihilating a remnant bandit company, seizing one 60mm mortar, one rocket launcher, one light machine gun, and forty rifles.

### 浙东克悬鹁＠山岛我军歼残匪一个连

1949-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东克悬鹁＠山岛我军歼残匪一个连
    【新华社浙东前线二十九日电】浙东前线人民解放军继解放桃花岛后，复于二十三日攻占桃花岛东北的悬鹁＠山岛，歼残匪一个连，缴六零炮一门，火箭筒一个，轻机一挺，步枪四十枝。
