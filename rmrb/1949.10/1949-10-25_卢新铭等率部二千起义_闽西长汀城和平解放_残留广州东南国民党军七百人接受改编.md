Title: Lu Xinming and Others Lead 2,000 in Uprising; Peacefully Liberate Changting City in Western Fujian; 700 Remnants of the Kuomintang Army in Southeastern Guangzhou Accept Reorganization
Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 24th: Lu Xinming, the former Kuomintang Fujian Province Changting Administrative Commissioner and concurrently Security Commander, and Yi Qiji, the Kuomintang Fifth Bandit Suppression Commander, led more than 2,000 troops in an uprising on the 19th of this month. Changting City in western Fujian was thus peacefully liberated.

Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 24th: Wu Xiumin, the regiment commander of the 2nd Regiment of the Kuomintang Tax Police Corps, Yang Da, the deputy regiment commander, Liu Yushu, the deputy commander, and more than 448 officers and soldiers, as well as Li Hanhui, the chief captain of the Yuehan Railway Protection Corps, Chang Hai, the deputy chief captain, and more than 300 officers and soldiers, recently announced in Wentang, Dongguan County, that they were breaking away from the Kuomintang reactionaries and accepting the reorganization of the People's Liberation Army.

### 卢新铭等率部二千起义　闽西长汀城和平解放  残留广州东南国民党军七百人接受改编

1949-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　卢新铭等率部二千起义
  　闽西长汀城和平解放
    残留广州东南国民党军七百人接受改编
    【新华社福建前线二十四日电】原国民党福建省长汀行政专员兼保安司令卢新铭及国民党第五清剿指挥官易启基率部二千余人，于本月十九日起义。闽西长汀城遂获和平解放。
    【新华社华南前线二十四日电】残留在广州东南东莞附近的国民党税警团第二团团长伍秀民、副团长杨达、团副刘玉书等以下官兵四百四十八人及粤汉铁路护路总队总队长李汉辉、副总队长常海以下官兵三百余人，日前于东莞县温塘宣布脱离国民党反动集团，接受人民解放军的改编。
