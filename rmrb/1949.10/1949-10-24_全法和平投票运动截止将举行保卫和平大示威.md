Title: Nationwide Peace Petition Movement in France to Conclude with a Grand Demonstration to Defend Peace
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 23rd: The National Council of the French Association of Warriors for Freedom and Peace will hold a meeting in the Gold Coast Province in central France. The agenda of the meeting is as follows: (1) The German question. (2) Abolition of atomic weapons. (3) Defending the republic's rights to freedom. (4) Carrying out propaganda on peace issues. (5) Organizing the Second National Congress. The results of the nationwide peace petition movement in France will also be announced at this meeting. The 23rd - the deadline for the peace petition movement - will also see a grand demonstration showing the solidarity of all classes in France to defend peace.

### 全法和平投票运动截止将举行保卫和平大示威

1949-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　全法和平投票运动截止将举行保卫和平大示威
    【新华社北京二十三日电】波兰通讯社引巴黎消息称，法国争取自由与和平战士协会全国理事会会议，将在法国中部金山坡省举行，会议的议程如下：（一）德国问题。（二）取消原子武器。（三）保卫共和国自由权利。（四）开展和平问题宣传。（五）组织第二届全国代表会议。全法国和平投票运动的结果，亦将在此次会上公布。二十三日——和平投票运动截止日，并将举行表明法国各阶级团结一致以保卫和平的盛大示威游行。
