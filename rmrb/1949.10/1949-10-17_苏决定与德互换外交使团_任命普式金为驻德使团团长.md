Title: Soviet Union Decides to Exchange Diplomatic Missions with Germany; Appoints Pushkin as Head of Mission to Germany
Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: TASS, Moscow, 15th: The Soviet government has decided to exchange diplomatic missions with the provisional government of the German Democratic Republic. The Soviet government has appointed Georgy Maksimovich Pushkin as head of the Soviet mission to Germany.

### 苏决定与德互换外交使团　任命普式金为驻德使团团长

1949-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏决定与德互换外交使团
  　任命普式金为驻德使团团长
    【新华社北京十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科十五日电：苏联政府决定与德意志民主共和国临时政府互换外交使团。苏联政府已任命乔治·玛克西莫维奇·普式金为苏联驻德使团团长。
