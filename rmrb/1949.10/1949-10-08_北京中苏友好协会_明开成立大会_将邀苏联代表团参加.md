Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Inaugural Meeting Tomorrow; Soviet Delegation Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 4

　　Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Inaugural Meeting Tomorrow
    Soviet Delegation Invited to Attend
    [Beijing News] The preparatory committee of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a standing committee meeting at 7:00 PM on the 6th at the association's address (No. 2 Yuhe Bridge). Standing committee members Peng Zhen, Zhang Xiruo, Qian Duansheng, Yu Xinqing, and over twenty others attended the meeting. Peng Zhen was elected as the temporary chairman, and Secretary-General Xiao Song reported on the preparatory work. It was finally decided that the inaugural meeting would be held at 3:00 PM on the 9th of this month at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. A delegation of Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers will be invited to attend. The meeting will hear a report on the preparatory work, adopt the association's charter, and elect the first council. An evening party will be held at the Concert Hall in the evening, and the Soviet Red Army Choir and Dance Ensemble and the China Arts Troupe will perform.

### 北京中苏友好协会　明开成立大会　将邀苏联代表团参加

1949-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京中苏友好协会
    明开成立大会
    将邀苏联代表团参加
    【北京讯】北京市中苏友好协会筹备会，于六日下午七时在该会会址（御河桥二号）召开常委会议。出席常委彭真、张奚若、钱端升、余心清等二十余人，会上推定彭真为临时主席，继由秘书长萧松报告筹备工作。最后决定本月九日下午三时假中山公园中山堂开成立大会，同时邀请苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团参加。大会将听取筹备工作报告，并通过会章，产生第一届理事会，晚间在音乐堂举行晚会，邀请苏联红军歌舞团及出国文工团演出。
