Title: Hong Kong and Kowloon People from All Walks of Life Donate Money, Goods, and Write Letters of Condolence to Comfort the People's Liberation Army

Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 29th. To celebrate the liberation of Guangzhou, people from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Kowloon have enthusiastically launched a campaign to comfort the People's Liberation Army. This movement, initiated by the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, immediately received enthusiastic responses from various trade unions, schools, and people's organizations in Hong Kong and Kowloon. At a celebration party for the liberation of Guangzhou, postal workers donated more than 400 Hong Kong dollars for the PLA. On the first day of the PLA comforting donation, students and faculty of Han Wah Middle School donated more than 5,000 Hong Kong dollars, as well as a large number of items such as gold rings, watches, pens, towels, and undershirts. Two teachers from the school voluntarily donated their entire two-month salaries. The Dockworkers' Union has formally established a PLA comforting campaign committee and is actively carrying out PLA comforting work. Various units under the union are enthusiastically mobilizing people to write letters of condolence to the People's Liberation Army. Workers in the Tsim Sha Tsui Kak Lung Tong district decided to raise money to buy 300 pairs of underwear for the PLA. The Nathan Road Student Bookstore in Mong Kok, Kowloon, held a charity sale for the PLA for one day. In the past week, the Hong Kong branch of the New China News Agency has received more than 5,700 Hong Kong dollars in donations for the PLA, as well as items such as gold rings and sweaters.

### 慰劳人民解放军　香港九龙各界人民献金献物写慰劳信

1949-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　慰劳人民解放军　
    香港九龙各界人民献金献物写慰劳信
    【新华社广州二十九日电】香港、九龙各界人民为庆祝广州解放，热烈展开慰劳人民解放军的运动。这一运动经港九工联发起后，立刻得到港九各职工会、各学校和各界人民团体的热烈响应。邮务职工会在庆祝广州解放晚会上，当场即捐献港币四百余元劳军。汉华中学员生第一日劳军献金超过港币五千元，此外还捐献了金戒、手表、钢笔、毛巾、汗衫等大批物品。该校两位教员并自动将两月薪俸全部捐出。洋务工会已正式成立了劳军运动委员会，积极展开劳军工作。该会所属各单位正热烈发动向人民解放军写慰劳信。尖沙咀却龙塘区工人决定筹募劳军内裤三百条。九龙旺角弥敦道学生书店特举行劳军义卖一天。一周以来新华社香港分社代收的劳军捐款已达港币五千七百余元，此外还有金戒、毛衣等物品。
