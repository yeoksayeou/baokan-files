Title: Beijing and Harbin Sign Materials Exchange Contract with a Total Value of Over 300 Million Yuan
Author: Peng Cheng
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 4

　　Beijing and Harbin
　　Sign Materials Exchange Contract
　　Exchange Totals Over 300 Million Yuan

　　[Reported by this newspaper] The Beijing Municipal Bureau of Commerce and Harbin Department Store, together with the Beijing Department Store, Coal and Iron Company, and Cotton Cloth Company, signed a materials exchange contract with the Harbin Department Store on the 14th of this month. Under the agreement, 3,000 cubic meters of Northeast-produced red pine will be exchanged for over 6,000 pieces of various fabrics, and over 10,000 dozens of towels and socks produced in Beijing, valued at over 300 million yuan in Renminbi. The goods are currently being packaged and will begin to be transported soon.

　　　　　　(Peng Cheng)

### 北京市与哈尔滨　订物资交换合同　交换总值达三亿余元
彭城
1949-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市与哈尔滨
  　订物资交换合同
  　交换总值达三亿余元
    【本报讯】北京市商业局与哈尔滨百货公司于本月十四日协同北京市百货、煤铁、花纱布三分公司与哈尔滨百货公司订立物资交换合同，以东北产红松三千立方米，交换北京市产各种布匹六千余匹，毛巾袜子各一万余打，约值人民币三亿余元，现正进行包装，不日开始起运。　
　　　　　　（彭城）
