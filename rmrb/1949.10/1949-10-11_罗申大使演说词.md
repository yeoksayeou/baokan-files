Title: Ambassador Roshchin's Speech
Author:
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th:

　　Ambassador Roshchin's speech at the train station upon his arrival in Beijing today is as follows:

　　I have been appointed as the first diplomatic representative—the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to the People's Republic of China. Now that I have arrived in the capital of the new China, I feel extremely honored to be entrusted with such a historic mission.

　　I have been entrusted by the millions of people of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government to celebrate the People's Republic of China established by the great Chinese people. This is a victory in the history of the Chinese people. I wholeheartedly congratulate the establishment of the Central People's Government headed by Mao Zedong and wish it success in its struggle to achieve a happy life and a prosperous new China for the people. At the same time, I extend my sincere respect to all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who have achieved a great historic victory,

### 罗申大使演说词

1949-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗申大使演说词
    【新华社北京十日电】苏联驻我国大使罗申今日抵京时在车站上的演说如下：
    我被任命为第一个外交代表——苏维埃社会主义共和国联邦驻中华人民共和国第一任特命全权大使，并于现在来到了新中国的首都，我对于所担负的这样历史使命，感到非常荣幸。
    我被表苏联千百万的人民和苏联政府，庆祝伟大的中国人民所建立的中华人民共和国，这是中国人民历史的胜利。我衷心地祝贺这以毛泽东为首的中央人民政府的成立，并祝它为实现人民的幸福生活和繁荣的新中国而奋斗。同时，我向获得了伟大历史胜利的中国人民解放军全体指战员致诚挚的敬意，并希望他们胜利地完成中华人民共和国所给与的任务。
    我以苏联政府代表的资格，当用最大的努力，来巩固苏维埃社会主义共和国联邦与中华人民共和国之间的亲切的友谊，以保卫世界和平与国际安全。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    伟大的中国人民以及中央人民政府万岁！（附图片）
    罗申大使演说
