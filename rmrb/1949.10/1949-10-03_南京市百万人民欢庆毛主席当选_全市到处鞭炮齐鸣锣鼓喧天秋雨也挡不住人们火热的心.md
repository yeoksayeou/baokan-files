Title: Nanjing's Million People Celebrate Chairman Mao's Election: Fireworks and Drums Resound Throughout the City, Autumn Rain Cannot Stop the People's Fiery Hearts
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 1st: Nanjing, once the center of the Kuomintang's reactionary rule, was filled with joy last night as its people unanimously supported the helmsman of the Chinese people – the election of Mao Zedong as Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, and Gao Gang as Vice Chairmen. Around 6 p.m., people gathered in front of various loudspeakers, quietly waiting for the good news to arrive. At 8:40 p.m., after the news broke, the Nanjing Xinhua Daily and Xinmin Daily rushed to print extra editions. All the extra editions were immediately snatched up by the citizens. Hundreds of waist drum team members, propaganda trucks, and music teams from literary and art groups also appeared on the streets at the same time. The personnel of the Nanjing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee immediately formed a team and set off, walking through the main streets of the city, holding high the bright red five-star national flag and shouting "Long live Mao Zedong!" and "Long live the People's Republic of China!" At this moment, the sound of firecrackers rang out in Nanjing, and the city was filled with the sound of gongs and drums and joy. The workers of the private Xin Yi Dyeing and Weaving Factory, who were rushing to print cloth for the national flag, decided to work all night to complete the dyeing of two hundred bolts of red cloth after hearing the good news. When the news reached the Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Nanjing, the Youth League members immediately ran out of the house, carrying the gongs and drums they had prepared, and danced the Yangko to celebrate wildly. Citizens涌现在 the streets congratulated each other, spontaneously formed teams, paraded with torches, and sang the national anthem. The cheers of "Long live Mao Zedong! Long live! Long, long live!" did not stop until midnight.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 2nd: At 3:20 p.m. yesterday, when the joyful news of Chairman Mao Zedong's official declaration of the founding of the People's Republic of China arrived, the million people of Nanjing, following the all-night celebration on the 30th, once again plunged into uncontrollable joy. The Post and Telecommunications Building, located in the central area of ​​Xinjiekou, immediately sent out a signal to congratulate the founding of the great motherland after broadcasting Chairman Mao's solemn announcement. In an instant, firecrackers exploded from the roof of the building, and firecrackers also resounded in unison from various buildings in the city. Hundreds of red flags fluttered in the sky above Nanjing at the same time. When the good news reached Xiaguan, more than twenty whistles sounded together, and the ships in the river echoed them, resounding through the clouds for twenty minutes. Although Nanjing was raining all day today, people braved the rain and rushed to the streets from factories, schools, institutions, shops, and homes to celebrate together. Propaganda teams and propaganda vehicles from dozens of units, including the Nanjing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, the Central Library, Nanjing University, and Xinmin Daily, appeared in various parts of the city at the same time, waving colorful ribbons, dancing and singing to their hearts' content. The rain became heavier and heavier, some umbrellas were broken, and clothes were soaked, but it could not stop the people's fiery hearts, the team became larger and larger, and the singing became louder and brighter. When the news reached the suburban areas, forums and mass meetings of farmers were held one after another in various villages. The actors of the Central Grand Stage in the urban area, who were performing the Peking Opera "The Dragon and the Phoenix Bring Good Fortune," immediately took off their beards after hearing Chairman Mao's announcement and enthusiastically shouted "Long live Mao Zedong! Long live! Long, long live!" with the audience. At night, the archways on the main streets of Nanjing were brightly lit, reflecting various huge colorful slogans and portraits, and people were immersed in revelry.

### 南京市百万人民欢庆毛主席当选  全市到处鞭炮齐鸣锣鼓喧天秋雨也挡不住人们火热的心

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　南京市百万人民欢庆毛主席当选
    全市到处鞭炮齐鸣锣鼓喧天秋雨也挡不住人们火热的心
    【新华社南京一日电】曾经作为国民党反动统治中心的南京市，昨晚万民欢腾，一致拥戴中国人民的舵手——中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜、高岗的当选。傍晚六时左右，人们就聚集到各个广播筒前，静候喜讯的到来。八点四十分，消息传出后，南京新华日报、新民报，赶速印制号外。所有号外立即为市民争夺一空。文艺工作团数以百计的腰鼓队员、宣传卡车、音乐队亦同时出现于街头。南京市总工会筹委会人员，随即整队出发，走遍市内各主要街道，高举鲜红的五星国旗，高呼“毛泽东万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”这时，南京市内鞭炮声齐鸣，到处洋溢着锣鼓声和欢乐声。正在赶印国旗用布的私营新毅染织厂工人们听到这一喜讯后，决定彻夜工作，完成二百匹红布的染色工作。消息传到南京市新民主主义青年团工作委员会时，青年团员们立即奔出了屋外，背上早已准备好的锣鼓，扭起秧歌，欢狂庆祝。涌现在街头的市民们，互相庆贺，自动结队，高举火炬列队游行，高唱国歌。“毛泽东万岁！万岁！万万岁！”的欢呼，直至午夜不息。
    【新华社南京二日电】昨日下午三点二十分，当毛泽东主席正式宣布中华人民共和国成立的喜讯传来时，南京市百万人民继三十日夜通宵欢腾后，再度卷入了难以抑止的欢乐中。位于市中心区新街口的邮汇大楼，在转播了毛主席的庄严宣告后，立即发出庆贺伟大祖国开国奠基的信号。刹那间，鞭炮先从大楼屋顶临空爆裂，全市各个大厦也鞭炮齐鸣。数百面红旗，同时飘扬在南京上空。喜讯传到下关，二十余台汽笛齐鸣，江心轮船随着附和，响彻云霄，达二十分钟之久。今日南京虽竟日秋雨，而人们却冒雨从工厂、学校、机关、商店、家庭奔向街头，同申庆祝。南京市总工会筹委会、中央图书馆、南京大学、新民报等数十单位的宣传队、宣传车，同时出现于市内各处，挥舞着彩色飘带，尽情地扭着，唱着。雨越下越大，有的雨伞被扭烂了，衣服湿透了，但挡不住人们火热的心，队伍愈来愈大，歌声愈唱愈嘹亮。消息传到市郊区，农民们的座谈会、群众会，接二连三地在各个村庄举行。正在上演京剧“龙凤呈祥”的市区中央大舞台的演员们，在听到毛主席的宣告后，迅即摘去髯子，热烈地和观众们同呼“毛泽东万岁！万岁！万万岁！”入夜，南京市内各主要街道的牌楼上，灯光齐放，辉映着各种巨幅的彩色标语、画像，人们沉浸于狂欢中。
