Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-10-26
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
    Adjusted Again Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has adjusted its exchange rate from yesterday (the 25th) to 1 RMB equals 52 Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价昨起再调整

1949-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价　
    昨起再调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自昨（二十五日）起牌价改为人民币一元兑东北币五十二元。
