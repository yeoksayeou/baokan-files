Title: Farewell Message from the Soviet Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-28
Page: 4

The days in democratic China I will never forget. The entire history of the Chinese people has great achievements in the face of progressive humanity. China's future is even greater!

Regarding our overall impressions, I already spoke on behalf of our delegation at the farewell party. As a writer, I only need to add one point: when I return to my country, I will do everything in my power to better write about the great cause and outstanding people of New China.

　　Simonov

The days spent in China will live forever in my memory. I saw the happy Chinese people, who have achieved historically significant victories in the arduous and lofty struggle against imperialism and domestic reactionaries and established the People's Democratic Republic. I also saw the Chinese youth at work and in study.

I hope that the Chinese people and Chinese youth will achieve complete victory and success in the construction of the new People's Republic.

Long live the Chinese people and their leader Comrade Mao Zedong!

Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!

Long live the great Comrade Stalin!

　　Voznesensky

October 27, 1949

The days I spent in New China were the happiest days of my life. Here, I once again joyfully experienced my youth.

I sincerely hope that our friendship will be consolidated, a friendship that makes all our friends happy and scares our enemies.

Dear comrades, I felt very happy staying here, thank you for your hospitality and loving welcome.

　　Malkov

October 27, 1949

Thanks to the hospitality of Chinese social organizations, we have become more aware of the treasures of China's ancient famous culture and the great achievements of the democratic New China's culture.

The art exhibition of modern Chinese artists shows great cultural art, mainly showing that many artists are integrated with the lives of the people and contribute their creations to the construction of the democratic New China.

I sincerely hope that Chinese artists will achieve great creative achievements on this difficult and noble road.

　　Fenogenov

I thank you.

With the help of comrades, I will try my best to produce films that will not disappoint the Chinese people.

　　Varlamov

I came to the people's China with the great and unprecedented call of the people's democratic revolution. I, along with the great Chinese people and the great Soviet people, heartily celebrate the historically significant victory of the Chinese nation.

　　Deomidov

October 27, 1949

We are very happy to come to China in the great and historically significant days of the great Chinese people, at the time of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government headed by Comrade Mao Zedong.

Our Chinese friends and the Chinese people have expressed their sincere reception to us. We will never forget the days we stayed in China. The care and love shown to our Soviet delegation in your great country is also the love and care for our Soviet people, for our socialist motherland, and for the one who implements the policy of peace and friendship among all nations - the friend of the working people of the world, our teacher and leader, Comrade Stalin.

We hope that the Chinese people and their leader Comrade Mao Zedong will achieve new great victories and achievements in the construction of New China.

Strive for the permanent friendship between the great China and all Soviet nationalities.

　　Stoletov

　　Agrazde

October 27, 1949

I pay tribute to the People's Daily. I hope it will always serve the interests of the people.

　　Gerasimov (with picture)
　　 Simonov Gerasimov Stoletov Agrazde Dubrovin
　　 Deomidov Varlamov Fenogenov Malkov Voznesensky

### 苏联文化代表团临别赠言

1949-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联文化代表团临别赠言
    在民主中国的日子，我将永不会忘记。中国人民整个的历史，在进步的人类面前有着伟大的功绩。中国的未来更伟大！
    关于我们的总感想，我代表我们代表团，在欢送会上刚才已说过了。作为一个作家，我只需要补充一点：当回国后我将尽我一切努力，更好地写下新中国的伟大事业和优秀的人们。
    西蒙诺夫
    留在中国的日子将永生留在我的记忆里。我看到了幸福的中国人民，他们在艰苦而崇高的反帝国主义及国内反动派的斗争中已取得了具有历史意义的胜利并建立了人民民主共和国。我也看到了劳动和学习中的中国青年。
    我希望中国人民及中国青年在新人民共和国底建设事业中取得彻底的胜利与成就。
    中国人民及其领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    中苏友谊万岁！
    伟大的斯大林同志万岁！
    伏兹尼
    四九年十月二十七日
    我在新中国所度过的日子是我生平最幸福的日子。在这里，我再一次地欣度了我的青春。
    我衷心地希望我们底友谊得以巩固，这种友谊使所有的朋友们高兴，使我们的敌人恐惧。
    亲爱的同志们，留在这里的日子我感到很幸福，感谢你们的款待和友爱的欢迎。
    马尔权夫
    四九年十月二十七日
    由于中国社会团体对我们的款待，使我们更加认识了中国古代著名文化的宝藏和民主新中国文化的巨大成就。
    中国现代艺术家作品的艺术展览，表示出伟大的文化艺术，最主要地表明了许多艺术家和人民的生活结合在一起，并把自己的创作贡献给民主新中国的建设。
    衷心地希望中国艺术家们，在这困难而高贵的道路上，达到巨大的创作成就。
    费诺格诺夫
    我向你们致谢。
    在同志们的帮助下，我将尽力制作出不辜负中国人民的影片。
    瓦尔拉莫夫
    我在人民民主革命的伟大、空前的感召之下来到了人民的中国。我和伟大的中国人民及伟大的苏联人民一起，衷心地庆祝中华民族底具有历史意义的胜利。
    德奥米多夫
    四九年十月二十七日
    我们在伟大的中国人民巨大的、具有历史意义的日子里，在中华人民共和国及以毛泽东同志为首的中央人民政府成立的时候来到中国是很幸福的。
    我们的中国朋友及中国人民向我们表示了真挚的接待。留在中国的日子我们将永远不会忘记。在你们伟大的国家内对我们苏联代表团所表示的关怀与热爱也就是对我们苏联人民、对社会主义祖国及履行着各民族间的和平及友谊政策的——全世界劳动人民底朋友，我们的导师及领袖斯大林同志的热爱与关怀。
    我们希望中国人民及其领袖毛泽东同志在新中国的建设事业中取得新的伟大的胜利及成就。
    为伟大的中国及一切苏联民族间的永久友谊而奋斗。
    斯托列托夫
    阿格拉则
    四九年十月二十七日
    我向人民日报致敬。希望它永远为人民的利益服务。
    格拉西莫夫（附图片）
