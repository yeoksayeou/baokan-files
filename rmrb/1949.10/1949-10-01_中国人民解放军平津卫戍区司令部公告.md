Title: Announcement from the Headquarters of the Pingjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 6

　　Announcement from the Headquarters of the Pingjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    This Headquarters, upon order, will be renamed the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command Headquarters, effective immediately. This is hereby announced.
    　　　　　　　　　　　September 30, 1949 (Gregorian Calendar)
