Title: Reporting Good News – A Record of Rural Suiyuan
Author: Jing Shuisheng
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 6

　　Reporting Good News
    ——A Record of Rural Suiyuan
　　(I)
    White windows, bright red flowers,
    Sister-in-law sews winter socks for her brother,
    A thousand stitches for the waist, ten thousand for the sole,
    Each stitch sewn with words from her heart.
    On the left one, embroider "Hero,"
    On the right one, embroider "Fight to the End."
    Our days of suffering are over,
    Mao Zedong has become our chairman.
　　　　　　(II)
    Carrying a letter on a thousand-mile journey,
    I bring great news to my brother,
    I won't mention how fat the oxen are,
    Nor how strong the crops are growing,
    From now on, the people are in charge,
    New China blossoms into a golden flower,
    Hoping my brother captures the bandit Chiang,
    My sister-in-law and I are busy with production.
    　　Jing Shuisheng
    　　October 13th

### 报喜——记绥远农村
泾水生
1949-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　报喜
    ——记绥远农村
　　（一）
    白窗户，红花花，
    嫂给哥哥缝冬袜，
    千针腰儿万针底，
    针针穿着心里话。
    左只绣个“英雄人”
    右只绣上“打到底”。
    咱们的日子苦到头，
    毛泽东当了咱主席。
　　　　　　（二）
    千里路上捎个信，
    俺给哥哥报大喜，
    不说黄牛吃的胖
    不提庄稼长的强，
    从此人民当了家，
    新中国开朵大金花，
    盼望哥哥捉蒋贼，
    俺和嫂嫂生产忙。
    　　泾水生
    　　十、十三、
