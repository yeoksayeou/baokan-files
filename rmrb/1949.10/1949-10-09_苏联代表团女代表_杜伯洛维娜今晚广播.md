Title: Female Representative of the Soviet Delegation, Dubrovinina, to Broadcast Tonight

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 1

　　Female Representative of the Soviet Delegation,
    Dubrovinina, to Broadcast Tonight
    [Our Newspaper] Dubrovinina, a member of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic and Scientific Workers Delegation and Deputy Minister of Education of the Soviet Russian Republic, will broadcast on Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station at 9:00 PM tonight.

### 苏联代表团女代表　杜伯洛维娜今晚广播

1949-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联代表团女代表
    杜伯洛维娜今晚广播
    【本报讯】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团团员、苏维埃俄罗斯共和国教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜今晚二十一点在北京新华广播电台广播。
