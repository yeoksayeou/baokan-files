Title: People's Political Consultative Conference Photos to be Exhibited at Qianmen Starting Today
Author: Huabei Huabaoshe Exhibition Group
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 4

　　People's Political Consultative Conference Photos
　　To be Exhibited at Qianmen Starting Today
　　[Our Newspaper] The Huabei Pictorial Society of the Political Department of the North China Military Region has scheduled a news photo exhibition at the People's Education Center in Qianmen Arrow Tower starting on the 23rd. The content includes more than 180 photos of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and photos documenting the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The exhibition will run for 15 days from October 23rd to November 6th. Visiting hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Huabei Pictorial Society Exhibition Group)

### 人民政协照片今起在前门展览
华北画报社展览组
1949-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民政协照片
  　今起在前门展览
    【本报讯】华北军区政治部华北画报社，定二十三日起在前门箭楼人民教育馆举办新闻照片展览会，其内容包括中国人民政治协商会议照片及中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立典礼纪实照片等共一百八十多幅，自十月二十三日起，至十一月六日止展览十五天，每日参观时间上午九时至下午五时。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（华北画报社展览组）
