Date: 1949-10-09

　　Xinhua Second Radio Program Highlights
　　Today, 17:15-17:45 Children's Hour: Mr. Sun Jingxiu continues to tell "The Story of Paul." 17:45-18:00 Youth Hour: Broadcasting a local news report entitled "How the Reclamation Group of the Fourth High School Grew Up," by Ren Zhenjia, a student from the Fourth High School Radio Group of this station. 20:00-20:15 "On-Time Commentary" Voice of the Municipal Government: Broadcasting an explanation from the Beijing Municipal People's Government News Office regarding several important decrees in September.
