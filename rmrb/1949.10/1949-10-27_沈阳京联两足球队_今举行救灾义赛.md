Title: Shenyang and Jinglian Football Teams to Hold Charity Match for Disaster Relief Today
Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 4

　　Shenyang and Jinglian Football Teams
    To Hold Charity Match for Disaster Relief Today
    [Reported by this newspaper] To respond to the government's call for disaster relief, the Shenyang City football team, which is participating in this Beijing People's Sports Congress, will hold a charity match with the Beijing Jinglian football team at Huiven Middle School in Dongcheng at 2:00 pm today (the 27th). Tickets will be sold on a temporary basis for 200 yuan each, and all proceeds from the charity sale will be used to help disaster-stricken people in North China.

### 沈阳京联两足球队  今举行救灾义赛

1949-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　沈阳京联两足球队　
    今举行救灾义赛
    【本报讯】参加此次北京市人民体育大会的沈阳市足球代表队为了响应政府救灾号召，特定于今（二十七）日下午两点在东城汇文中学与北京京联足球队举行义赛。临时售票，每张二百元，全部义卖所得将救济华北灾胞。
