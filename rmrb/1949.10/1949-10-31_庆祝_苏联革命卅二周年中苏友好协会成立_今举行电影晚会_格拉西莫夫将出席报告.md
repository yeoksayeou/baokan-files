Title: Celebrating the 32nd Anniversary of the Soviet Revolution and the Founding of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association: Film Evening Tonight; Gerasimov Will Attend and Report
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 4

　　Celebrating
    The 32nd Anniversary of the Soviet Revolution and the Founding of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Film Evening Tonight
    Gerasimov Will Attend and Report
    [Our Newspaper] To celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet Socialist Revolution and the founding of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Central Film Bureau and the Soviet Film Export Company will hold a film evening at the Grand China Cinema in the city at 7 pm today (October 31). The film "The Young Guard," which won the 1948 Stalin Prize, will be screened (parts one and two). Gerasimov, the director and screenwriter of the film, will also give a report.

### 庆祝  苏联革命卅二周年中苏友好协会成立  今举行电影晚会  格拉西莫夫将出席报告

1949-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝
    苏联革命卅二周年中苏友好协会成立　
    今举行电影晚会   
    格拉西莫夫将出席报告
    【本报讯】中央电影管理局与苏联影片输出公司为庆祝苏联社会主义革命三十二周年纪念及中苏友好协会成立，定于今天（三十一日）下午七时在本市大华电影院举行电影晚会，放映曾获一九四八年斯大林奖金之“青年近卫军”上下集影片，同时并请该片之编导格拉西莫夫作报告。
