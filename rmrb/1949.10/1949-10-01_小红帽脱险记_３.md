Little Red Riding Hood's Escape
    3
　　　　　(II)

　　The sun is tired of standing,
　　It wants to go home to sleep,
　　The black curtain of the sky slowly descends,
　　Everything afraid of seeing the sunlight,
　　Is preparing to move and be a hero.

　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Little Red Riding Hood, who loves to hop and skip,
　　At this time, looks like a piece of dull wood,
　　Holding her little head tightly,
　　Squatting in the corner of the room,
　　Like a great scholar,
　　Bitterly thinking about big, big problems.
　　More difficult than thinking about an arithmetic equation,
　　Even more uncomfortable to think about,
　　She thinks and thinks,
　　But can't think of any good way,
　　This is more complex
　　Than two plus two equals four,
　　More complex!
　　In the end,
　　She was so anxious that
　　She started to cry softly.

　　"Woof woof woof!
　　Woof woof woof!
　　Where did this child come from,
　　Hiding here crying, aren't you ashamed?

　　"Is that you,
　　Little Red Riding Hood?
　　Why are you crying?
　　Why are you shouting?
　　Are you hungry?
　　Then I can treat you to two small bones.
　　Don't want to work!
　　Then I can help you a few times.
　　Quickly don't cry,
　　Quickly don't shout,
　　I am so ashamed!
　　So embarrassed!
　　A crying child
　　Cannot be considered a model worker,
　　And cannot become a little master of the country."
　　"Brother Dog,
　　Don't talk nonsense,
　　If you were me,
　　I'm afraid you would have tucked your tail between your legs long ago,
　　And hid in that rotten straw nest of yours!
　　I'm not being lazy,
　　And I'm not hungry.
　　I'm crying sadly,
　　Because no one is coming to help me,
　　And I'm going to be
　　Such a small person,
　　So small,
　　And be eaten by the old mountain goblin,
　　Eaten as a snack,
　　Eaten as a snack!
　　Just thinking about it,
　　How can I not be sad?
　　Ah... Mama!"

　　"Ah!
　　It's the old mountain goblin,
　　That old bastard,
　　Is he coming to eat you tonight?
　　Quickly don't cry,
　　Quickly don't shout,
　　I have a good idea,
　　I will definitely help you,
　　You quickly hide yourself properly,
　　As long as you believe me and listen to me,
　　If the old mountain goblin comes,
　　You see, I will definitely bite him so he can't go home."

　　"Brother Dog,
　　I thank you for your kindness!
　　But I know,
　　You have always been raised by the old mountain goblin,
　　Guarding the door for him,
　　Biting people for him,
　　How come now you're turning around to help me?
　　Such a thing,
　　Is really rare!
　　Really rare!
　　Your good intentions,
　　Are really hard to believe!
　　Really hard to believe!"
　　　　　　　　　　　(To be continued)

    ３
　　　　　（二）
　
    太阳站得疲倦了，
    它要回家睡觉了，
    天顶的黑幕慢慢的扯了下来，
    一切怕见阳光的东西，
    都在准备活动起来做好汉。
　
    小红帽
    喜欢蹦蹦跳跳的小红帽，
    这时候好象一段呆木头，
    紧紧的抱着小脑袋，
    蹲在屋角里，
    象一个大大的学问家，
    在苦苦的想着大大的大问题。
    比想一个什么算术式子，
    还要想得更难受，
    他想呀想的
    可想不出什么好办法，
    这比二加二等于四，
    更复杂，
    更复杂！
    到后来，
    只急得他
    嘤嘤嘤的哭起来了。
　
    “汪汪汪！
    汪汪汪！
    哪里来的这个小孩子，
    躲在这里哭啼啼的，羞不羞？
　
    “是你呀，
    小红帽？
    你为什么哭？
    你为什么叫？
    是不是肚子饿？
    那我可以请你吃两根小骨头。
    是不是干活儿不愿意！
    那我也可以帮你几把忙。
    快快不要哭，
    快快不要叫，
    我多么羞呵！
    多么难为情呵！
    哭啼啼的小孩子
    不能算一个工作模范，
    也做不成国家的小主人。”
    “狗大哥，
    你不要乱说，
    要是你是我，
    恐怕早已急得夹起了尾巴，
    躲进了你那个烂草窠！
    我不是偷懒，
    更不是肚子饿。
    我伤心的哭，
    是谁也不来帮助我，
    要让我
    一个小人儿，
    这么小，
    这么小，
    就给老山妖，
    当作点心吃掉，
    当作点心吃掉！
    想一想，
    我怎能不伤心？
    啊……妈呀！”
　
    “呵！
    是老山妖，
    那个老混蛋，
    他今晚要来把你吃掉吗？
    快快不要哭，
    快快不要叫，

    “狗大哥，
    我谢谢你的好意！
    但是我知道，
    你一向是老山妖喂养的，

    替他咬人，

    这样的事情，
    实在不多！
    实在不多！
    你的好心，
    实在难相信！
    实在难相信！”
