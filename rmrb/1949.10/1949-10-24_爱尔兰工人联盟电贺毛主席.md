Title: Irish Workers' Union Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 1

　　Irish Workers' Union
  　Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd] London Airmail: Dennis Walsh, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Irish Workers' Union, and Secretary Sean Nolan telegraphed Chairman Mao Zedong, congratulating him on the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    On the occasion of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Irish Workers' Union, on behalf of itself and all progressive-minded people in Ireland, extends warm congratulations to you. The decisive victory you have won for the Chinese people and all peoples oppressed by imperialism is indeed a turning point in the world peace camp gaining the upper hand.
    Long live the People's Republic of China and the glorious Communist Party of China!
    Dennis Walsh, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Irish Workers' Union
    Sean Nolan, Secretary

### 爱尔兰工人联盟电贺毛主席

1949-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　爱尔兰工人联盟
  　电贺毛主席
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】伦敦航讯：爱尔兰工人联盟执行委员会主席丹尼斯·威尔什及书记锡恩·诺兰电毛泽东主席，祝贺中华人民共和国的成立，电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志：
    值此中华人民共和国成立之际，爱尔兰工人联盟代表自己及爱尔兰一切思想进步的人民向你致热烈的祝贺。你们为中国人民以及一切受帝国主义压迫的人民取得的决定性的胜利，确实是世界和平阵营在取得优势中的转折点。
    中华人民共和国及光荣的中国共产党万岁！
    爱尔兰工人联盟执行委员会主席丹尼斯·威尔什书记锡恩·诺兰
