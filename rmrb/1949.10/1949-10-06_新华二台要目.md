Title: Xinhua Second Radio Station Highlights

Author:

Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua Second Radio Station Highlights
    17:15-17:30 Children's Time, featuring stories and songs by the children of Muxin Elementary School. 17:45-18:00, Workers' Time: Comrade Wang Yongxing of the Fengtai Bridge Factory speaks on the topic: "Launch a High Tide of Studying the Documents of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Initiate a Campaign for Everyone to Submit a Manuscript." 19:30-19:45, Second Local News Broadcast. 20:00-20:15, Time Signal, Commentary, Municipal Voice: Announcement by the Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau regarding the Beijing People's Sports Meeting. 20:15-20:30: Listener Services. 20:30-21:30: Joint broadcast of the First Radio Station's Third Mandarin News Broadcast and recordings, broadcasted by the First Radio Station, of speeches by representatives of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation, Dubrovinina, Italian People's Representative, Central Committee member of the Italian Communist Party, Spano, and Korean delegate Park Jung-ae at the founding meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 新华二台要目

1949-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华二台要目
    一七·一五—一七·三○儿童时间，由穆新小学的小朋友们讲故事和唱歌。一七·四五—一八·○○、职工时间：丰台桥梁厂王永兴工友讲话，题目是：“掀起学习人民政协文件的热潮，发起每人一稿运动”。一九·三○—一九·四五、第二次本市新闻。二○·○○—二○·一五、对时　言论　市政之声：北京市人民政府教育局关于北京市人民体育大会的预告。二○·一五—二○·三○：听众服务：二○·三○—二一·三○、联播第一台第三次普通话新闻、和第一台播送的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团代表杜伯洛维娜，意大利人民代表、意共中央委员斯巴诺和朝鲜代表团朴正爱，在中苏友好协会总会成立大会上致词的录音。
