Title: Commercial Radio Telephone Service Between Beijing, Tianjin, and Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 2

　　Commercial Radio Telephone Service Between Beijing, Tianjin, and Shenyang
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st】To strengthen connections inside and outside Shanhaiguan, a commercial radio telephone service has been established between Beijing, Tianjin, and Shenyang. The two cities have already established communication with Shenyang on the 15th and 17th of this month, respectively. Beijing communicates with Shenyang three times a day, and Tianjin communicates with Shenyang four times a day. Furthermore, mail and telegram services between various locations in the Northeast and Guangzhou have been restored.

### 京、津、沈阳间　商用无线电通话

1949-10-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　京、津、沈阳间
  　商用无线电通话
    【新华社北京二十一日电】为加强关内外联系，北京、天津和沈阳间特举办商用无线电话。两地已于本月十五日和十七日，先后与沈阳通话。北京每日与沈阳通话三次，天津每日与沈阳通话四次。又：东北各地与广州间的邮件、电报业务均已恢复。
