Title: Ambassador Wang Jiaxiang Passes Through Harbin on His Way to Post Abroad
Author:
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 22nd: Wang Jiaxiang, the first Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, passed through Harbin by special train from Shenyang at approximately 10:00 AM today on his way to take up his post abroad. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Zhang Ce, Secretary of the Songjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Fanwu and Li Yanlu, Vice Chairmen of the Provincial Government, and Rao Bin, Mayor of Harbin, among others. After Ambassador Wang alighted from the train and rested briefly, he boarded the train again and set off for the Soviet Union.

### 王稼祥大使　过哈出国

1949-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　王稼祥大使过哈出国
    【新华社哈尔滨二十二日电】中华人民共和国第一任驻苏联大使王稼祥在出国赴任途中于今日上午十时许乘专车自沈过哈。赴车站欢迎者有中共松江省委书记张策，省政府副主席李范五、李延禄、哈尔滨市长饶斌等多人。王大使下车后稍事休憩，旋即登车启程赴苏。
