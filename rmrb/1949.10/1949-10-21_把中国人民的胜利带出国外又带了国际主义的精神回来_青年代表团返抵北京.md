Title: Bringing the Victory of the Chinese People Abroad and Returning with the Spirit of Internationalism: Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th -- The Chinese Youth Delegation, which participated in the Second World Youth Congress and the World Youth and Student Festival, returned to Beijing by train at 7 p.m. today. Welcoming them at the station were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Feng Wenbin and Jiang Nanxiang of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League; Xie Bangding, Zhou Shouchang, and Li Xiuzhen of the All-China Students' Federation; Wu Yaozong and Liu Liangmo of the YMCA; Xu Liqun of the Beijing Municipal Working Committee of the Youth League; Professor Shen Tilan; and representatives from various units, totaling more than 200 people. Liao Chengzhi delivered a welcoming speech at the station, saying: Two months ago, the delegates brought the victory of the Chinese people abroad, and today they bring back the spirit of internationalism. We must spread this spirit from Beijing to the whole country. Keli Geng, the acting head of the delegation, said in his reply: During this trip abroad, we saw the strength of the forces of peace and democracy, the strong unity of democratic youth, and the shining example of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League. The Chinese Youth Delegation was loved and respected by the people and youth of the world, and deeply felt the happiness and glory of being born in the era of Mao Zedong. In the future, we must, under the leadership of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, work with the people of the whole country to build a new democratic China.

### 把中国人民的胜利带出国外又带了国际主义的精神回来  青年代表团返抵北京

1949-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　把中国人民的胜利带出国外又带了国际主义的精神回来
    青年代表团返抵北京
    【新华社北京二十日电】出国参加世界青年第二次代表大会、世界青年与学生联欢大会的中国青年代表团于今日晚七时乘车返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，新民主主义青年团中央委员会冯文彬、蒋南翔，中华全国学生联合会谢邦定、周寿昌、李秀贞、基督教青年会吴耀宗、刘良模，青年团北京市工作委员会许立群，教授沈体兰，以及各单位代表二百余人。廖承志在站上致欢迎词说：两月前代表们把中国人民的胜利带出国外，今天又带回了国际主义的精神。我们要把这种精神由北京传播到全国去。代表团代团长克力更致答词时说：此次出国，看到了和平民主力量的强大、民主青年的坚强团结以及苏联列宁共产主义青年团的光辉榜样。中国青年代表团在国外受到世界人民与青年的热爱和尊重，深深感到生在毛泽东时代的幸福与光荣。我们今后一定要在中华人民共和国中央人民政府领导下，和全国人民一起，建设新民主主义的新中国。
