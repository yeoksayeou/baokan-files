Title: General Secretaries of French Textile and Construction Unions Send Respects to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 13th (Delayed dispatch) - Aubert, General Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor's Textile Workers' Union, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China on the 9th, offering congratulations: "I extend warm congratulations to the Chairman and Government of the People's Republic of China. The French General Confederation of Labor's Textile Workers' Union pays respects to this vital force of the democratic and peace camp, which seeks a better future for workers worldwide." Furthermore, Alassard, General Secretary of the French Federation of Building and Wood Workers, sent a congratulatory telegram stating: "Congratulations on the founding of the People's Republic of China, and respects to the Government of the People's Republic of China and Chairman Mao Zedong. I wish you complete success in building the new China."

### 法纺织建筑两工会总书记　电毛泽东主席致敬

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　法纺织建筑两工会总书记
    电毛泽东主席致敬
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】（迟到）法国总工会所属纺织工人联合会总书记奥培尔九日电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东致贺称：“谨向中华人民共和国主席和政府致热烈的祝贺。法总工会纺织工人联合会谨向这一为求全世界工人美好将来的民主、和平阵营的生力军致敬。”又法国建筑与木材工人联合会总书记阿拉沙德贺电称：“祝贺中华人民共和国的成立，并向中华人民共和国政府和毛泽东主席致敬。谨祝在建设新中国中完全成功。”
