Title: List of Procurator-General, Deputy Procurator-General, and Members of the Supreme People's Procuratorate

Author:
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th - The following is the complete list of Deputy Procurator-Generals and members of the Supreme People's Procuratorate approved by the third meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, together with the Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate appointed by the first meeting of the Government Committee on October 1st:
    Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate:
    	Luo Ronghuan (Chinese Communist Party, formerly Political Commissar of the Central China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Political Commissar of the Fourth Field Army);
    Deputy Procurator-Generals (two):
    	Li Liuru (Chinese Communist Party, formerly Minister of Justice and President of the People's Court of the Northeast People's Government),
    	Lan Gongwu (Non-party democratic personage, formerly Vice Chairman and Minister of Civil Affairs of the North China People's Government).
    Members (eleven):
    	Luo Ruiqing (Chinese Communist Party, formerly Political Commissar of the 19th Army Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army),
    	Yang Qiqing (Chinese Communist Party, formerly Deputy Minister of Public Security of the North China People's Government),
    	He Xiangning (Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang),
    	Li Xijiu (Supervisory Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang),
    	Zhou Xinmin (Central Committee Member of the China Democratic League),
    	Chen Shaomin (Executive Committee Member of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation),
    	Xu Jianguo (Public Security Worker),
    	Wang Jinxiang (Public Security Worker),
    	Li Shiying (Public Security Worker),
    	Bu Shengguang (Public Security Worker),
    	Feng Jiping (Public Security Worker).
