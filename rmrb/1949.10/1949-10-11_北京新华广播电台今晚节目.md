Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
Author:
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 4

    Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [Our Newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast a program for workers and staff at 18:15 tonight, introducing the national liberation struggle of the Indonesian working class, one of the member countries of the Asian and Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference. At 19:00, a lecture on general knowledge of natural science will be broadcast: "An Overview of National Forests." At 18:30, the first literary program, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Association: Beijing Drum Ballad, "Three Heroes Pushing a Boat Across the River." At 22:00, the second literary program, the Inner Mongolia Art Troupe broadcasts an introduction to Mongolian folk songs.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    北京新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分职工节目，介绍亚、澳洲职工会代表会议会员国之一——印尼工人阶级的民族解放斗争。十九点自然科学常识讲座，播送：“全国森林的概况”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：京韵大鼓。“三勇士推船渡江”。廿二点第二次文艺节目，内蒙古文艺工作团播送蒙古民歌介绍。
