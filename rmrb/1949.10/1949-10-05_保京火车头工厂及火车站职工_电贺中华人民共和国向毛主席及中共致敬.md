Title: Workers of the Baojing Locomotive Factory and Railway Station Send Telegrams to Congratulate the People's Republic of China, Respectfully Salute Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th: Sofia Dispatch: The workers of the "Dimitrov" Locomotive Factory in Bulgaria and the workers of the Sofia Railway Station sent telegrams to Chairman Mao on the 2nd to express their congratulations.

　　The telegram of congratulations unanimously passed by all the workers of the "Dimitrov" Locomotive Factory at the "International Day for the Defense of Peace" on the 2nd said: Taking advantage of the visit of the special envoys of China to pay tribute to our great leader and teacher, Comrade Dimitrov, and to hold the International Day for the Defense of Peace, we—the workers of the Dimitrov Locomotive Factory—warmly salute Comrade Mao Zedong, the heroic Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese people who are fighting for freedom and democracy. We share in the universal celebration of the new People's Republic of China. The People's Republic of China is an important member of the family of people's democracies. We are deeply convinced that under the leadership of the long-tested Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people will eliminate the remnants of the Kuomintang clique in order to consolidate peace and achieve the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people. Let us, together with other new democratic countries headed by the great Soviet Union, resolutely fight for the defense of peace and democracy throughout the world. Long live the Chinese Communist Party! Long live the Soviet Union, the bulwark of the defense of peace and democracy and the bravest fighter against war!
　　
The telegram of congratulations from the workers of the Sofia Railway Station said: "Comrades, we—the workers of the Sofia Station—extend our congratulations to the young People's Republic of China, and are willing to work for the greater, stronger friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people who stand in the anti-imperialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and the great Stalin."

### 保京火车头工厂及火车站职工  电贺中华人民共和国向毛主席及中共致敬

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　保京火车头工厂及火车站职工
    电贺中华人民共和国向毛主席及中共致敬
    【新华社布拉格四日电】索非亚讯：保加利亚“季米特洛夫”火车头工厂职工和索非亚火车站职工于二日分别致电毛主席祝贺。
    “季米特洛夫”火车头工厂全体职工于二日的“国际保卫和平日”大会上，一致通过的贺电说：趁着中国特别使节来向我们伟大的领袖和导师季米特洛夫同志致祭和举行国际保卫和平日的时机，我们——季米特洛夫火车头工厂的工人热烈地向毛泽东同志、英勇的中国共产党和为自由和民主奋斗的中国人民致敬。我们分享到对新的中华人民共和国的普遍欢庆。中华人民共和国在人民民主的大家庭中，是一个重要的成员。我们深信中国人民在久经锻炼的中国共产党领导下，将要消灭国民党集团的残余，以达到巩固和平及中国人民的繁荣与幸福。让我们和以伟大苏联为首的其他新民主国家一起，坚决地在全世界为保卫和平与民主而斗争。中国共产党万岁！保卫和平民主的堡垒与反战最勇敢的战士苏联万岁！
    索非亚火车站职工的贺电说：“同志们，我们——索非亚车站的职工们——向年青的中华人民共和国致贺，并愿为站在以苏联及伟大斯大林为首的反帝国主义阵营中的中、保人民更大坚固更的友谊而工作。”
