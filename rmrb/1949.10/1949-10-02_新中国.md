Title: New China
Author: Tian Ge
Date: 1949-10-02
Page: 6

　　New China

　　"People's Republic of China"
　　Red flags unfurl,
　　Golden stars glitter.

　　"March of the Volunteers,"
　　Brave, lively, solemn.

　　Millions of hearts;
　　Millions of voices,
　　Unite in revolution, forward!

　　New country
　　Is our mother,
　　Without her,
　　We cannot grow up well.

　　Peace-loving people of the whole
　　world
　　Form a unified team,
　　Listen! Ten thousand miles of cheers.
