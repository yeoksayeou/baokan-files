Title: Mass Rallies Held Across North Korea to Celebrate Korean-Soviet Friendship; Pyongyang Rally Sends Respects to Stalin

Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th—The people of North Korea are enthusiastically participating in the "Korean-Soviet Friendship and Soviet Culture Fortnight." Mass rallies were held in various provinces, cities, and counties on the 13th, and various activities officially commenced on the 14th. The streets and alleys of Pyongyang are decorated as if for a festival, with the national flags of both Korea and the Soviet Union visible everywhere, along with portraits of Stalin and Kim Il-sung and celebratory slogans. The Liberation Tower, standing on Moranbong Peak, is brightly illuminated by a huge five-pointed star and the words "Korean-Soviet Friendship" adorned with countless electric lights.

The capital of the Korean Republic's celebration rally was grandly held at the National Art Theater on the evening of the 13th at 7 p.m. Representatives from various political parties and people's organizations of Korea, workers, farmers, youth, women, scientific workers, cultural workers, and foreign residents in Pyongyang, totaling over a thousand people, participated. The assembly elected Marshal Stalin and Premier Kim Il-sung as honorary chairmen of the assembly. Attendees welcomed Soviet Ambassador Sidorov and other Soviet personnel with thunderous applause. Hong Myong-hui, chairman of the assembly's preparatory committee, delivered a lengthy report entitled "Strengthening Korean-Soviet Friendship is a Powerful Guarantee for Establishing a Unified and Independent Korea." After detailing the great achievements of the Soviet Union in liberating Korea and helping Korea build a democratic and independent nation, he emphasized the superiority of the Soviet socialist system. He said: The Korean people can only be happy if they unite closely with the Soviet people and learn from them. When he said, "The Korean people will never forget the name of Marshal Stalin," the entire audience stood up and expressed their heartfelt gratitude with stormy applause and cheers of "Long live Stalin." After the report, representatives from all walks of life spoke in succession. Speakers unanimously expressed the enthusiasm of the Korean people from all strata in supporting the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. Finally, Soviet Ambassador Sidorov delivered a congratulatory speech. He expressed congratulations and gratitude on behalf of the Soviet people for the friendship of the Korean people. He also said: In order to achieve Korea's unification, independence, and democratic development, the Soviet Union will provide greater assistance and believes that the friendly relations between the Korean and Soviet people will continue to grow. His congratulatory speech was met with enthusiastic applause from the audience. Finally, the assembly passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin and Premier Kim Il-sung.

### 北朝鲜各地群众集会　庆祝朝苏友好　平壤大会电斯大林致敬

1949-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜各地群众集会
  　庆祝朝苏友好
  　平壤大会电斯大林致敬
    【新华社平壤十六日电】北朝鲜人民热烈参加“朝苏友好和苏联文化旬间”。各道（省）市、郡（县）于十三日举行群众大会，十四日正式展开各种活动。平壤的大街小巷装饰得象过节一样，到处看得见朝苏两国的国旗，斯大林、金日成的画像和庆祝的标语。矗立在牡丹峰上的解放塔，高悬着用无数电灯缀饰的巨大五角星和“朝苏友好”大字，光辉夺目。
    朝鲜共和国首都的庆祝大会，于十三日晚七时在国立艺术剧场隆重举行。朝鲜各政党各人民团体代表，工人、农民、青年、妇女、科学工作者、文化工作者以及驻平壤的外国人士共千余人参加。大会选举了斯大林大元帅和金日成总理为大会的名誉主席。与会者以雷动的掌声，欢迎苏联大使西地科夫及其他苏联人士的莅会。大会筹备会主席洪命熹以“加强朝苏友好是建立统一独立朝鲜的有力保障”为题，作了长篇报告。他在详细叙述伟大的苏联解放朝鲜并帮助朝鲜建设民主独立国家的伟绩后，强调指出苏联社会主义社会制度的优越性。他说：朝鲜人民只有和苏联人民紧密团结，并向他们学习，才能幸福。当他说到“朝苏人民永远不会忘记斯大林大元帅的名字”时，全场起立，以暴风雨般的鼓掌声和“斯大林万岁”的欢呼声，表示了衷心的感谢。报告毕，各界代表相继讲话。发言者一致表示了朝鲜各阶层人民拥护以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的热忱。最后，苏联大使西地科夫致祝辞。他代表苏联人民对朝鲜人民的友谊，表示庆贺和谢意。他并说：为了朝鲜的统一、独立和民主发展，苏联将予以更大的协助，并相信朝苏两国人民的友好关系，将不断地增进。他的祝辞博得全场热烈的鼓掌。大会最后通过了向斯大林大元帅和金日成总理的致敬电。
