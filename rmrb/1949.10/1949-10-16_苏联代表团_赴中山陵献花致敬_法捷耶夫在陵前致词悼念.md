Title: Soviet Delegation Pays Respects at Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum with Floral Tribute; Fadeyev Delivers Memorial Address at the Mausoleum

Author:

Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 15th — The Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation paid respects at Sun Yat-sen's mausoleum with a floral tribute on the 14th. All the representatives of the delegation personally carried the wreath and ascended to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. The wreath was inscribed in both Chinese and Russian: "Presented to the great revolutionary Sun Yat-sen by Soviet cultural workers." Fadeyev, the head of the delegation, delivered a memorial address in front of the tomb (see separate dispatch). All the representatives personally laid flowers and stood in solemn respect; then, they entered the memorial hall to pay their respects to Sun Yat-sen's coffin. The delegation then drove to the tomb of the revolutionary martyr Liao Zhongkai to lay a wreath in tribute. The delegation also visited the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum en route. Fadeyev, Simonov, and others inquired in detail about the historical sites of the Ming Dynasty. On the same day, workers, students, and the scientific community in Nanjing held separate welcome meetings and invited the representatives to give reports. In the afternoon, the delegation took a special train to Shanghai. Tang Liang, Deputy Director of the Military Control Commission, Deputy Mayor Ke Qingshi, Chief of Staff Li Da, and representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 300 people, were at the station to see them off.

### 苏联代表团　赴中山陵献花致敬　法捷耶夫在陵前致词悼念

1949-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联代表团
  　赴中山陵献花致敬
  　法捷耶夫在陵前致词悼念
    【新华社南京十五日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团，于十四日赴孙中山陵寝献花圈致敬。代表团全体代表亲捧花圈，登上中山陵。花圈上用中苏两国文字写着：“苏联文化工作者献给伟大的革命家孙中山”。代表团团长法捷耶夫在墓前致词（见另电）。全体代表亲自献花，并肃立致敬；然后步入灵堂，瞻仰孙中山灵柩。代表团复驱车至革命先烈廖仲恺墓前献花圈致敬。代表团途经明孝陵时，并顺道游陵。法捷耶夫、西蒙诺夫等对于明朝史迹询问甚详。当日，京市工人、学生、科学界皆分别召开欢迎会，邀请各代表作报告。下午代表团即乘专车赴沪，到车站欢送的有军管会副主任唐亮、柯庆施副市长、李达参谋长及各界代表等三百余人。
