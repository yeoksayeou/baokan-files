Title: Strengthening Political Education in Primary and Secondary Schools: Beijing Municipal Education Bureau Establishes Political Education Section
Author:
Date: 1949-10-18
Page: 4

　　Strengthening Political Education in Primary and Secondary Schools
  　Beijing Municipal Education Bureau Establishes Political Education Section
    [Beijing News] In order to further strengthen political education in primary and secondary schools in Beijing, the Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau has expanded the original Political Education Unit of the Second Section (Secondary Education Section) into a Political Education Section. The section was officially established and began operations a few days ago. The current central task of the section is to organize typical reports, combining the experience of political education work in the last semester, in order to exchange and promote them.
　　　　　　　　(Municipal Government News Office)

### 加强中小学政治教育　京市府教育局设政治教育科

1949-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　加强中小学政治教育
  　京市府教育局设政治教育科
    【本市讯】为了进一步加强本市中小学的政治教育，京市人民政府教育局特将原第二科（中等教育科）的政治教育股扩充为政治教育科。该科已于日前正式成立并开始办公。该科当前的中心工作是结合上学期政治教育工作的经验，组织典型报告，以便交流推广。　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　（市府新闻处）
