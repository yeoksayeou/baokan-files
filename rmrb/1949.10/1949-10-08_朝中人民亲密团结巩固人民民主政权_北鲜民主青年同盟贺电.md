Title: Close Unity Between Korean and Chinese People Consolidates People's Democratic Power; Telegram of Greetings from the North Korean Democratic Youth League
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th.

　　The Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government. The original text of the telegram is as follows:

　　To Comrade Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League:

　　On behalf of all members and all North Korean youth, the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League warmly celebrates the great victory won by the Chinese youth and the entire Chinese people in the People's Liberation War against foreign imperialism and the reactionary rule of the Chiang Kai-shek Kuomintang, and celebrates the birth of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the Central People's Government.

　　This historic victory of the Chinese people and Chinese youth has inspired the struggle for peace and democracy around the world, and especially for the people and youth of Eastern colonial and semi-colonial countries who are fighting against foreign aggression and for national independence, it is a great encouragement. Today, the youth and people of Korea and China together should further strengthen the close unity between the two peoples and youth in order to thoroughly overthrow domestic and foreign reactionary forces and consolidate the people's democratic power.

　　We firmly believe that under the banner of the People's Republic of China, a new people's China will be built. We sincerely wish the Chinese people and youth eternal victory.

　　Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League
　　　　　　　　　　　October 5, 1949

### 朝中人民亲密团结巩固人民民主政权　北鲜民主青年同盟贺电

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝中人民亲密团结巩固人民民主政权
    北鲜民主青年同盟贺电
    【新华社北京六日电】北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会致电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，祝贺中华人民共和国与中央人民政府的成立，贺电原文如下：
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬：
    北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会以全体盟员及全北朝鲜青年的名义，热烈庆祝中国青年与全中国人民一起，在反对外来帝国主义与蒋介石国民党反动统治的人民解放战争中所取得的伟大胜利，并庆祝中华人民共和国的诞生与中央人民政府的成立。
    中国人民与中国青年的这一历史胜利，鼓舞了全世界和平民主的斗争，尤其对于正为着反对外来侵略与争取民族独立的东方殖民地半殖民地的人民与青年所进行的斗争，是一莫大的鼓舞。今天，朝中两国青年和两国人民一起，为彻底打倒国内外反动势力和巩固人民民主政权，应更加加强两国人民和青年之间的亲密团结。
    我们坚信，在中华人民共和国的旗帜下，将建设一个崭新的人民的新中国。谨以万分热忱，祝贺中国人民与青年的永远胜利。
    北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长玄正民
    　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月五日
