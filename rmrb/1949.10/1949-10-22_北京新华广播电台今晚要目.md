Title: Tonight's Highlights from Beijing Xinhua Radio Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 4

Beijing Xinhua Radio Station
  Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Radio Station's youth program at 18:15 this evening will broadcast an article by Deng Tuo, titled: "Chinese Youth" and Comrade Yun Daiying. At 19:00, a lecture on natural science knowledge, titled "Stars," written by Dai Wenshi, professor of the Mathematics Department of Yenching University. At 18:30, the first arts and entertainment program, sponsored by the Chinese Quyi Improvement Association: Lianhualuo: "Granny Ma Investigates," performed by Guan Dejun, Guan Jinfeng, and Quan Fengchi. At 22:00, the second arts and entertainment program will broadcast the new Peking Opera: "Mulan Joins the Army."
    Also, on Channel Two, at 17:15, during women's time, an article from the preparatory committee of the Municipal Women's Federation will be broadcast: titled "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference." At 17:45, during workers' time, two reports will be broadcast: "The situation of the disaster relief work carried out by the handicraft workers' working committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions preparatory committee" and "The situation of the firecracker industry workers in this city responding to the disaster relief movement." At 20:00, Voice of the City, will broadcast a report from the Beijing Municipal People's Court on "How to Educate Criminals." At 20:15, Listener Service, a notice from the Beijing Municipal Supply and Marketing Cooperative regarding the supply of winter fuel briquettes to members.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分青年节目，播送邓拓的一篇文章，题目是：“中国青年”和恽代英同志。十九点自然科学常识讲座，题目是“星”，由燕京大学数学系教授戴文实撰稿。十八点三十分，第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：莲花落：“马大娘探子”，关德俊、关金凤、全凤池演唱。二十二点，第二次文艺节目，播送新京剧：“木兰从军”。
    又二台十七时十五分妇女时间，播送本市妇联筹委会的一篇文章：题目是“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”。十七点四十五分职工时间，播送两篇报导：“本市总工会筹委会手工业工人工作委员会，救灾工作进行的情况”和“本市火柴业工友响应救灾运动的情形”。二十点市政之声，播送北京市人民法院“怎样教育犯人”的报导。二十点十五分听众服务，北京市供销合作总社关于社员冬季燃料煤球供应问题的通知。
