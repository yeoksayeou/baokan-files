Title: News of Our Army's Liberation of Guangzhou Prominently Published in Soviet and Czech Newspapers; Czech Communist "Red Power" Newspaper Publishes Special China Edition
Author:
Date: 1949-10-18
Page: 1

　　News of Our Army's Liberation of Guangzhou
  　Prominently Published in Soviet and Czech Newspapers
    Czech Communist "Red Power" Newspaper Publishes Special China Edition
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th] Soviet and Czech newspapers yesterday prominently published the news of our liberation of Guangzhou. The Czech Communist Party organ, "Red Power," also published a special China edition on the 16th under the title "The Road to Freedom." In addition to prominently publishing the news of the liberation of Guangzhou, it also printed the design of the national flag of the People's Republic of China, a large Chinese map marking the impending complete liberation, and several photos related to the new China, including a large number of American-made howitzers captured and used to arm themselves by the People's Liberation Army, spirited young students of the Northeast Military and Political University, the Shijingshan Steel Plant, and the happy lives of children in Los Angeles nurseries. The special edition also introduced in detail the history of the Chinese people's heroic struggle against imperialism and reactionaries under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao.

### 我军解放广州消息　苏捷报纸显著刊登  捷共“红色权力”报出版中国特刊

1949-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军解放广州消息
  　苏捷报纸显著刊登
    捷共“红色权力”报出版中国特刊
    【新华社布拉格十七日电】苏联和捷克的报纸，昨日都以显著的地位刊载我广州解放的消息。捷克共产党机关报“红色权力”报并于十六日在“到自由之路”的标题下出版中国特刊。除以显著地位登载广州解放消息外，还刊印了中华人民共和国的国旗样式和标志着行将全部解放的大幅中国地图及有关新中国的照片多幅，其中包括人民解放军缴获并武装了自己的大批美制榴弹炮，英气勃勃的东北军政大学青年学员，石景山炼钢厂和洛杉矶托儿所儿童们的幸福生活。该特刊又详细地介绍了中国人民在中共和毛主席领导下反对帝国主义和反动派的英勇斗争历史。
