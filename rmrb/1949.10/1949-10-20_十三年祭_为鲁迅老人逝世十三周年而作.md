Title: Thirteenth Anniversary Sacrifice - Written for the Thirteenth Anniversary of the Death of Old Man Lu Xun
Author: Xu Fang
Date: 1949-10-20
Page: 6

　　Thirteen Years Sacrifice
    ——Written for the Thirteenth Anniversary of the Death of Old Man Lu Xun
    Xu Fang
    For nearly a hundred years,
    We have used blood to open the way for the people's motherland;
    For twenty-eight years,
    We have followed Mao Zedong's large army,
    For the people's motherland,
    We have already opened up the world.

    The people's motherland,
    Is as high as the sky and as thick as the earth,
    As wide as the sea and as deep as the river;
    The five-star red flag greets the wind and clouds,
    The landscape inside and outside the Great Wall is beautiful.

    The toiling masses who have turned over,
    The Chinese people who have stood up,
    Are now for the construction of their own motherland
    Repairing bridges and roads,
    Opening mines and building factories,
    Carrying bricks and transporting tiles,
    Diligently cultivating crops.

    In the past,
    Our motherland was painful and sorrowful;
    Today,
    Our motherland
    Is green mountains, clear waters, strong people, and fat horses...
    We
    Are no longer slaves.

    And you
    Dear good old man,
    Did not see this brand new century you longed for;
    Because of this,
    Because of this victory of the battle,
    We feel all the more that we should not have lost you;

    But,
    We will never forget
    Your call:
    "Oppose the invasion of the Soviet Union!"
    We will also never disobey your battle order:
    Throw the javelin,
    Towards the "tigers and wolves that are encroaching on China!"

    You trained us in marksmanship,
    We were nurtured,
    We have become strong weapons of the people's motherland,
    Dear good old man,
    We swear:
    If the enemy dares to invade us,
    No matter at what moment,
    We will
    Heroically
    Meet the enemy
    And attack...!

### 十三年祭——为鲁迅老人逝世十三周年而作
徐放
1949-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　十三年祭
    ——为鲁迅老人逝世十三周年而作
    徐  放
    近百年来，
    我们用血为人民的祖国打开了路；
    二十八年来，
    我们跟着毛泽东的大队人马，
    为人民的祖国
    已辟出了天下。
　
    人民的祖国，
    天高地厚，
    海阔河深；
    五星红旗迎风云，
    长城里外的江山好。
　
    翻过身的劳苦大众，
    站起来的中国人民，
    正为着自己的祖国的建设
    修桥补路，
    开矿建厂，
    搬砖运瓦，
    好好的在耕种庄稼。
　
    过去，
    我们的祖国是痛苦的，忧愁的；
    今天，
    我们的祖国
    是山青，水秀，人壮，马肥……
    我们
    已经再不是奴隶了。
　
    而你
    亲爱的好老人，
    没有能看到你想望的这崭新的世纪；
    因为这，
    因为这战斗的胜利，
    我们愈感到不该丧失了你；
　
    但，
    我们将永远不会忘记
    你的号召：
    “反对进攻苏联”！
    我们也将永远不会违背你的战令：
    把标枪，
    向“侵食中国的虎狼”投射出去！
　
    你教练我们操习枪法，
    我们被抚育，
    我们已经变成了人民祖国的坚强武器，
    亲爱的好老人，
    我们发誓：
    如果敌人敢于侵犯我们，
    不管在任何时刻，
    我们便将
    英勇地
    迎战
    出击……。
