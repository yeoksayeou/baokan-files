Title: I Saw Chairman Mao
Author: Wei Lin
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 6

I Saw Chairman Mao
    Wei Lin
    Fireworks filled the sky, cheers thundered,
    On the evening of the founding ceremony,
    In endless excitement and exhilaration,
    I saw Mao Zedong—
    Our people's great leader.

    Chairman Mao
    Sat upright in the center of the rostrum;
    His robust body
    Was so eye-catching,
    Like a particularly bright star
    shining in the sky,
    When people look up,
    They are sure to see him first—
    His sparkle, his light!

    Chairman Mao cared about us,
    Just as we care about Chairman Mao.
    As the procession moved towards the rostrum,
    From afar,
    We stared without blinking
    At this great leader we longed to see day and night;

    We saw this giant constantly applauding
    With his large, powerful palms.
    From the beginning to the end of the procession,
    Chairman Mao's applause
    Forever encouraged our excited steps!
    Our procession approached the rostrum,
    Stepping onto the stone bridge over the small river in front of the stage;
    Under the bright mercury lights,
    I clearly saw
    Chairman Mao waving vigorously at us!
    Ah, dear leader,
    You are beckoning us,
    To more thoroughly eliminate the enemy;
    To serve the people more enthusiastically;
    To build a new country more diligently!
    We are certainly willing,
    Under your call,
    To advance bravely forever!

    After passing the rostrum,
    The procession had to walk back from the stone bridge;
    But everyone's gaze
    Still lingered on the center of the rostrum,
    On that great and beloved Mao Zedong.

    We watched him pick up his hat,
    Waving, waving, seeing us off;
    And with that vigorous and powerful voice
    Shouting into the microphone:
    "Long live! Long live!"

    Ah, great leader,
    I will never forget this day.
    This day,
    I saw you, saw
    Your applause and cheers;
    This day,
    I saw even more that the broad masses of the people
    Under your brilliance,
    Standing up strong and powerful,
    —They already have their own new country.
    October 2, 1949

### 我看到了毛主席
魏麟
1949-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　我看到了毛主席
    魏麟
    焰火漫天，欢声雷动，
    开国盛典那天的晚上，
    在无尽的兴奋和激动中，
    我看到了毛泽东——
    我们人民的伟大领袖。
　
    毛主席
    端坐在主席台正中；
    他魁梧的身子
    是那样地引人注意，
    犹如天空中特别明亮地闪烁着
    的星星，
    当人们仰起头来时
    首先一定就会看到他——
    他的闪烁，他的光芒！
　
    毛主席关切着我们，
    正象我们关切着毛主席。
    当队伍走向主席台，
    老远老远，
    我们就不瞬眼地注视着
    这位朝夕渴望一见的伟大领袖；
　
    我们看见这位巨人不住的鼓着
    他那硕大有力的手掌。
    自队伍起始到最后，
    毛主席的掌声
    永远鼓舞着我们兴奋的步伐！
    我们的队伍行近主席台，
    跨上了台前小河上的石桥；
    在明亮的水银灯照耀之下，
    我清晰地看到
    毛主席在使劲地向我们招手！
    啊，亲爱的领袖，
    您是在招着我们，
    要去更彻底地消灭敌人；
    更热诚地服务人员；
    更努力地建设新国家！
    我们一定愿意，
    在您的号召之下
    永远勇敢前进！
　
    经过了主席台，
    队伍要从石桥走回来；
    但是我们每个人的目光，
    还都逗留在主席台正中
    那伟大的亲爱的毛泽东的身上
　
    我们看着他拿起帽子，
    挥着，挥着，欢送我们；
    并且用那雄健有力的声音
    向扩音机中呼喊着：
    “万岁！万岁！”
　
    啊，伟大的领袖，
    我将永远忘不了这一天。
    这一天，
    我看到了您，看到了
    您底鼓掌和欢呼；
    这一天，
    我更看到了广大的人民
    在您的光辉之下，
    坚强有力地站起来，
    ——他们已经有了自己的新国家。
    　　　一九四九年十月二日
