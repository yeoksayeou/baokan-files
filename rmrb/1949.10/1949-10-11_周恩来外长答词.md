Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Reply

Author:

Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai delivered the following reply today when welcoming Ambassador Roshchin, after Ambassador Roshchin delivered his speech:

　　On behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, I now extend a warm welcome to Ambassador Roshchin's arrival. The Soviet Union is the first friendly nation to recognize the People's Republic of China, and Ambassador Roshchin is the first Soviet ambassador to the People's Republic of China. Ambassador Roshchin himself is a friend well-known to the Chinese people. I believe that through Ambassador Roshchin's efforts, the diplomatic relations between China and the Soviet Union and the profound friendship between the peoples of the two countries will be further developed and consolidated in the future.

　　Here, I wish Ambassador Roshchin good health!

　　Long live Sino-Soviet cooperation and friendship!

　　Long live Chairman Mao of the People's Republic of China!

　　Long live Marshal Stalin of the Soviet Union! (Attached picture)
　　Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai delivering his reply (Photo by Chen Zhengqing)

### 周恩来外长答词

1949-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来外长答词
    【新华社北京十日电】外交部周恩来部长在今日欢迎罗申大使时于罗申大使发表演说后致答词如下：
    我现在代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和中国人民热忱欢迎罗申大使的到来。苏联是第一个承认中华人民共和国的友邦，罗申大使是苏联派驻中华人民共和国的第一任大使，而罗申大使本人又是中国人民所熟知的朋友，我相信，中苏两国的邦交，中苏两国人民的深厚友谊，今后经过罗申大使的努力，将会更加发展和巩固起来。
    我在此，谨祝罗申大使健康！
    中苏合作和友谊万岁！
    中华人民共和国毛主席万岁！
    苏联斯大林大元帅万岁！（附图片）
    周恩来外长致答词    （陈正青摄）
