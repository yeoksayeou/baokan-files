Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing, Liu Ningyi and Others Welcome Them at the Station; Over 10,000 People Warmly Welcome Them in Tianjin
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd: The Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation returned to Beijing at 10:00 PM today after visiting Shanghai, Nanjing, and other places. The Youth Performing Arts Troupe, which attended the World Youth Congress on behalf of China, accompanied them. Representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and various circles in the capital, including Liu Ningyi, Shen Yanbing, Liao Chengzhi, Zhou Yang, Li Dequan, Ding Ling, Xu Guangping, Qian Junrui, Wang Bingnan, Yan Baohang, Qian Sanqiang, Ding Zan, Cao Yu, Shen Zhiyuan, Zhang Zhongshi, Fan Hong, Yuan Muzhi, Ouyang Yuqian, Yang Hansheng, and others, totaling over a hundred people, went to the station to warmly welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 23rd: The Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, and organizations such as the Tianjin Youth Federation and the Student Federation held a conference this afternoon to welcome the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation. Over 10,000 workers, students, and PLA soldiers attended. After Huang Songling, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a welcoming speech, Simonov, the acting head of the Soviet delegation, spoke. He first paid his respects to the working class and advanced intellectuals of Tianjin, as well as the students and professors famous for their revolutionary traditions. He praised the great victory of the Chinese people, especially the working class, in their struggle for China's independence, democracy, and freedom, and the great role they played in the international democratic and peaceful forces. Then, he introduced the close relationship between Soviet workers and intellectuals. Following Simonov, the famous Soviet film director Varlamov and the famous female pianist Klavchenko spoke. They respectively introduced the cultural and artistic work and the women's life in the Soviet Union. Klavchenko especially paid tribute to Chinese women on behalf of Soviet women. Their speeches were constantly interrupted by cheers and applause. The shouts of "Long live Stalin!" "Long live Mao Zedong!" "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!" resounded throughout the venue. Finally, the Soviet art performance team and the Chinese youth performing arts troupe performed song and dance programs respectively.
    After the meeting, the Soviet delegation took a train to Beijing at 7:53 PM. Huang Jing, Huang Huoqing, Huang Songling and others went to the station to see them off.

### 苏联文化代表团返京刘宁一等赴车站欢迎　过津时热烈欢迎者达万人

1949-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联文化代表团返京刘宁一等赴车站欢迎
  　过津时热烈欢迎者达万人
    【新华社北京二十三日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团于访问沪宁等地后今日下午十时返抵北京。我国出席世界青年代表大会的青年文工团陪同前来。中苏友好协会及首都各界代表刘宁一、沈雁冰、廖承志、周扬、李德全、丁玲、许广平、钱俊瑞、王炳南、阎宝航、钱三强、丁瓒、曹禺、沈志远、张仲实、樊弘、袁牧之、欧阳予倩、阳翰笙等百余人前往车站热烈欢迎。
    【新华社天津二十三日电】中苏友好协会天津分会筹委会，天津市总工会筹委会及津市青联、学联等机关团体，今日下午举行大会，欢迎苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团，到会工人、学生、解放军战士等共万余人。大会于中苏友好协会天津分会筹委会副主任黄松龄致欢迎词后，由苏联代表团代理团长西蒙诺夫讲话。他首先向津市工人阶级和先进知识分子以及以具有革命传统著名的学生教授们致敬，并赞扬中国人民特别是工人阶级在争取中国独立、民主、自由斗争中的伟大胜利，和在国际民主和平力量中所起的巨大作用。接着，他介绍了苏联工人及知识分子的密切结合的情形，继西蒙诺夫之后讲话的尚有苏联名电影导演瓦尔拉莫夫名女钢琴家克拉夫青柯。他们分别介绍了苏联的文化艺术工作与妇女生活情形。克拉夫青柯特别代表苏联妇女向中国妇女致敬。他们的讲话不断被欢呼与掌声所打断。“斯大林万岁！”“毛泽东万岁！”“中苏友好万岁！”的呼声响彻会场。最后由苏联艺术演出队及我国青年文工团分别演出歌舞节目。
    会后，苏联代表团于七时五十三分搭车赴京。黄敬、黄火青、黄松龄等均赴站欢送。
