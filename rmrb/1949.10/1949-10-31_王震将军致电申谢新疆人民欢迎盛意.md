Title: General Wang Zhen Sends a Telegram Expressing Gratitude for the Warm Welcome from the People of Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, October 30th:

　　Since the People's Liberation Army entered Xinjiang, it has received a warm welcome and support from people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life. To this end, General Wang Zhen, Commander and Political Commissar of the First Corps of the People's Liberation Army, recently sent a telegram expressing his gratitude. The text of the telegram is as follows:

　　To Commander Tao in Dihua, Chairman Bao Erhan, and to the gentlemen of the Joint Service Office for Welcoming the People's Liberation Army from all circles in Dihua:

　　Your telegram of October 22nd is acknowledged with respect. We are deeply inspired by the warm welcome from all circles to our troops entering Xinjiang. In the future, we will be determined to wholeheartedly promote the cause of democratic peace and abide by the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in order to repay the kindness of the people from all circles. The people of Xinjiang live in hardship, so please politely decline material慰问. We respectfully hope that prominent figures from all circles will introduce the culture, customs, and habits of all ethnic groups, the hardships of the people's lives, and production knowledge, and give guidance to all our comrades.

　　Wang Zhen, Commander and Political Commissar of the First Corps of the First Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, October 23rd

### 王震将军致电申谢新疆人民欢迎盛意

1949-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　王震将军致电申谢新疆人民欢迎盛意
    【新华社西北前线三十日电】人民解放军进入新疆以后，获得各族各界人民的热烈欢迎和拥护。为此，人民解放军第一兵团司令员兼政委王震将军日前特致电申谢。电文如下：
    迪化陶总司令、鲍尔汉主席并转迪化各界欢迎人民解放军联合服务处诸先生：
    十月二十二日电敬悉。敝部进入新疆，承蒙各界热忱欢迎，全军深为感奋。今后誓当竭诚推进民主和平事业，奉行人民政协共同纲领，以答各界人民之盛意。新疆人民生活艰难，特请婉为谢辞物资慰劳。敬希各界贤达，介绍各族文化民情风俗习惯，人民生活疾苦情形和生产知识，指教敝部全体同志为盼。
    中国人民解放军第一野战军第一兵团司令员兼政治委员王震十月二十三日
