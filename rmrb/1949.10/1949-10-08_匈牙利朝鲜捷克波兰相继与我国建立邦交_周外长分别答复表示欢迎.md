Title: Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, and Poland successively establish diplomatic relations with China; Foreign Minister Zhou responds separately with welcome

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 6th: Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, and Poland successively establish diplomatic relations with China; Foreign Minister Zhou responds separately with welcome.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 6th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received today a note dated October 4th from Károly György, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic, notifying the decision of the Hungarian People's Republic to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. The full text of the note is as follows:

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

The Hungarian people are paying great attention with immense sympathy to the heroic struggle of the Chinese people for freedom under the leadership of Mao Zedong. These are the brilliant achievements of millions of working people in China against domestic and foreign oppressors. These achievements guarantee the freedom and possibility of developing people's democracy and provide the possibility for the vast majority of the Chinese people to establish the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government. The Hungarian people and its government believe that the establishment of the People's Republic of China is a historically significant achievement for international peace, and they extend their warm congratulations to the new Central Government of the Chinese people; at the same time, they believe that the Central People's Government represents the will of the vast majority of the Chinese people and is willing to consolidate and strengthen the friendship and cooperation of our people. After studying the announcement of the Central Government of the People's Republic of China on October 1st, the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic considers the Government of the People's Republic of China to be the only legitimate government of China and wishes to take this opportunity to establish diplomatic relations between our two countries.

　　　　　　　Károly György, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic
　　　　　　　　　　　　Budapest, October 4, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 6th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, replied to Károly György, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic, with a note today, welcoming Hungary's establishment of diplomatic relations with China. The full text of the note is as follows:

To Mr. Károly György, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, Budapest:

On behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, I am honored to receive your telegram of October 4th of this year regarding your government's decision to establish diplomatic relations between the Hungarian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China.  I now inform you that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China warmly welcome the immediate establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic, and has decided to exchange diplomatic representatives.

　　　　　Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing, October 6, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 6th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received today a note dated October 4th from Pak Hon-yong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, notifying the decision of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. The full text of the note is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

The Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has received the announcement of the Government of the People's Republic of China issued on October 1, 1949, regarding the establishment of diplomatic relations with all countries.

The establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is a historical victory in the liberation struggle of the Chinese people and has become a tremendous force for the liberation struggle of the oppressed nations of the East and the peace-loving people of the world.

The Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea confirms that the Government of the People's Republic of China represents the will of all the Chinese people.

For the closer amity and prosperity and happiness of the people of Korea and China, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has decided to establish diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China and exchange ambassadors.

I am deeply honored to be entrusted by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to convey this to Your Excellency, and I express my sincere respect to Your Excellency.
　　　　　　　　　　　Pak Hon-yong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Pyongyang, Korea, October 4, 1949

### 匈牙利朝鲜捷克波兰相继与我国建立邦交　周外长分别答复表示欢迎

1949-10-08
第1版()
专栏：人民民主国家进一步加强友谊与合作

　　匈牙利朝鲜捷克波兰相继与我国建立邦交
    周外长分别答复表示欢迎
    【新华社北京六日电】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，本日收到匈牙利人民共和国外交部长卡莱·古拉本月四日的照会，通知匈牙利人民共和国决定与中华人民共和国建立外交关系。照会全文如下：
    北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来先生：
    匈牙利人民以莫大的同情注意着在毛泽东领导下所进行的中国人民争取自由的英勇斗争，这是中国千百万劳动人民反对内外压迫者的光辉成就。这些成就保证了人民民主开展的自由和可能，并给予绝大多数中国人民建立中华人民共和国和中央人民政府准备了可能。匈牙利人民及其政府认为中华人民共和国的建立是对国际和平有历史意义的成就，并向中国人民新的中央政府寄以热忱的庆贺；同时认为中央人民政府代表着绝大多数中国人民的意志，并愿巩固和加强我们人民的友谊与合作。匈牙利人民共和国政府在研究了十月一日中华人民共和国中央政府的公告以后，认为中华人民共和国政府为中国惟一合法的政府，并愿借此机会建立我们两国间的外交关系。
    　　　　　　　匈牙利人民共和国政府外交部长卡莱·古拉
    　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月四日于布达佩斯
    【新华社北京六日电】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来本日以照会一件答复匈牙利人民共和国外交部长卡莱·古拉，欢迎匈牙利与中国建立外交关系。照会全文如下：
    布达佩斯匈牙利人民共和国政府外交部长卡莱·古拉先生：
    我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府很荣幸地收到阁下本年十月四日来电关于贵国政府决定建立匈牙利人民共和国与中华人民共和国之间的外交关系的通知。我现在通知阁下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府热忱欢迎立即建立中华人民共和国与匈牙利人民共和国之间的外交关系，并决定互派外交代表。
    　　　　　中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来
    　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月六日于北京
    【新华社北京六日电】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，本日收到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外交部长朴宪永本月四日的照会，通知朝鲜民主主义人民共和国决定与中华人民共和国建立外交关系。照会全文如下：
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部长周恩来阁下：
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府收到一九四九年十月一日中华人民共和国政府发表的与各国建立外交关系的公告。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立，是中国人民解放斗争的历史的胜利，也成为东方各被压迫民族的解放斗争与世界爱好和平人民的巨大的力量。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府确认中华人民共和国政府是代表全中国人民的意志的。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府，为了朝中两国人民的更紧密的亲善和繁荣与幸福，决定了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与中华人民共和国之间建立外交关系，并交换大使。
    我受朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的委任能向阁下传达，深感光荣，并向阁下表示我的衷心的尊敬。
　　　　　　　　　　　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外交部长朴宪永
