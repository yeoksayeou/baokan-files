Title: Consolidating and Developing Sino-Soviet Friendship, Promoting Cultural Exchange Between the Two Countries: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Established; Peng Zhen and Wang Zhizhi Elected President and Vice President

Author:
Date: 1949-10-10
Page: 2

　　Consolidating and Developing Sino-Soviet Friendship, Promoting Cultural Exchange Between the Two Countries
    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Established
    Peng Zhen and Wang Zhizhi Elected President and Vice President
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held its inaugural meeting on the morning of the 9th in Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. The presence of representatives of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation and Soviet experts made the meeting filled with excitement and joy from beginning to end, and with the exceptionally close friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union. After each item on the agenda, shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao!" and "Long live Marshal Stalin!" and applause resounded throughout the hall. Attending the meeting were 410 people including Peng Zhen, Zhang Youyu, Yu Xinqing, Xu Deheng, Qian Duansheng, Liang Sicheng, Chai Zemin, Zhang Xiaomei, Xiao Song, Xue Chengye, Gu Qizong, Sun Fuling, Liu Yifeng, Liu Ren, Xue Zizheng, and Ma Yuhuai, representing various democratic parties, people's organizations, and workers, students, farmers, women, business circles, and representatives from various branches and sub-branches of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Soviet friends attending the meeting included Soviet cultural, artistic and scientific workers delegation representative Dr. Stoletov, Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Markov, the newly appointed Soviet Acting Consul General in Beijing, Seleznev, and 21 municipal experts. When the Soviet friends entered the venue one after another, all the representatives applauded to welcome them for a long time.

### 巩固发展中苏友谊沟通两国文化　北京中苏友好协会成立  彭真王之相等当选会长副会长

1949-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　巩固发展中苏友谊沟通两国文化
    北京中苏友好协会成立
    彭真王之相等当选会长副会长
    【北京讯】北京市中苏友好协会于九日上午在中山公园内中山堂举行成立大会。由于苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团代表与苏联专家的出席，更使大会自始至终充满着兴奋愉快和中苏两国人民亲切逾常的友爱。大会在进行每项程序以后，“毛泽东主席万岁！”“斯大林大元帅万岁！”的欢呼声和掌声响彻全场。出席大会的有彭真、张友渔、余心清、许德珩、钱端升、梁思成、柴泽民、张晓梅、萧松、薛成业、古奇踪、孙孚凌、刘一峰、刘仁、薛子正、马玉槐等四百一十人，包括各民主党派、各人民团体，及工人、学生、农民、妇女、工商界及北京中苏友好协会各分会、支会的代表。出席大会的苏联友人有苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团代表苏联生物科学博士斯托列托夫、苏联对外文化协会远东部副部长马尔柯夫、新任苏联北京代理总领事谢列金及市政专家等二十一人。当苏联友人鱼贯入场时，全场代表都鼓掌欢迎，历久不息。
