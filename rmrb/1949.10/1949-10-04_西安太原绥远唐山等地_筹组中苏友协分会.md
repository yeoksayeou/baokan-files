Title: Xi'an, Taiyuan, Suiyuan, Tangshan and other places preparing to form branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1949-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 29th. The preparatory committee of the Xi'an branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Xi'an City was established on the 24th. Xi Zhongxun, Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, pointed out in his speech at the establishment meeting: The most important significance of establishing the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is to learn Marxism-Leninism and the experience of the Soviet Union in large-scale national construction, and to widely promote Sino-Soviet friendship among the people. The meeting elected He Long, Ma Mingfang, Yang Mingxuan, Zhang Jiafu, Zhao Boping, Yang Zilian, Li Zijian and other 23 people as standing committee members, and elected He Long as the director of the preparatory committee, and Ma Mingfang, Zhang Jiafu, Yang Mingxuan, and Li Zijian as deputy directors.
    [This newspaper reports] Taiyuan, Suiyuan, Xinxiang and other places successively established preparatory committees for branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in late last month. The inaugural meeting of the Taiyuan branch was held on the 27th of last month, and the preparatory committee was established on the same day, electing Cheng Zihua, Deng Chumin and others as 41 preparatory committee members, with Cheng Zihua as the director. The initiators included more than 170 representatives from various agencies, troops, factories, schools, mass organizations, and democratic figures. The booklet "The Soviet Union is the most sincere friend of the Chinese people" was distributed at the inaugural meeting, including six important articles and news about Sino-Soviet friendship. The conference resolved to carry out various publicity and educational activities related to Sino-Soviet friendship in the future, and to actively develop members from urban to rural areas in a step-by-step manner, and to establish branches in various agencies, troops, schools, factories, and districts throughout the city. Finally, in the name of the preparatory committee, a telegram was sent to Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao and the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to express respect and congratulations.
    On the 23rd of last month, the Suiyuan People's Government held an inaugural meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Fengzhen, and the preparatory committee was also formed on the same day. More than 180 representatives from all walks of life attended the meeting, and their speeches were enthusiastic and unanimous in the belief that we must "lean to one side" and "follow the path of the Russians" and learn from the Soviet Union. Representatives of ethnic minorities also participated in the meeting. The conference elected 29 preparatory committee members and determined to carry out extensive publicity on Sino-Soviet friendship in the future, calling on all provinces, cities, counties, and Mongolian banners to organize branches.
    More than 400 representatives from all walks of life in Xinxiang also signed to initiate the establishment of the Xinxiang branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. An inaugural meeting was held on the 26th of last month, and 41 preparatory committee members, including Chao Zhefu, were elected to formally establish the preparatory committee. The conference also sent telegrams to Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao and the People's Political Consultative Conference to express respect and congratulations.
    [Tangshan Electric] The preparatory meeting of the Tangshan Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was established on September 30. The association was initiated by 219 people, and the initiators' meeting elected 62 people including Wu De as preparatory committee members.

### 西安太原绥远唐山等地 　筹组中苏友协分会

1949-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　西安太原绥远唐山等地
   　筹组中苏友协分会
    【新华社西安二十九日电】西安市中苏友好协会西安分会筹备委员会，已于二十四日成立。中共中央西北局书记习仲勋在成立会上的讲话中指出：成立中苏友好协会最主要的意义，首在学习马列主义和苏联大规模建设国家的经验，并在人民中广泛展开宣传中苏友好的工作。会议选出贺龙、马明方、杨明轩、张稼夫、赵伯平、杨子廉、李子健等二十三人为常务委员，并推选贺龙为筹委会主任，马明方、张稼夫、杨明轩、李子健为副主任。
    【本报讯】太原、绥远、新乡等地于上月下旬均先后成立中苏友好协会分会筹备委员会。太原分会发起人大会于上月二十七日召开，并即日成立筹委会，选出程子华，邓初民等四十一人为筹备委员，程子华任主任。发起人中包括各机关、部队、工厂、学校、群众团体、民主人士代表百七十余人。发起人大会上曾散发“苏联是中国人民最真诚的朋友”小册子，其中包括有关中苏友好的重要文章、消息六篇。大会决议今后要进行各种有关中苏友谊的宣传教育工作，并有步骤地从城市深入乡村，积极发展会员，在全市各机关、部队、学校、工厂和各区应设立支会。最后并以筹委会名义通电向斯大林大元帅、毛主席和中国人民政协表示致敬与祝贺。
    上月二十三日，绥远人民政府于丰镇召开中苏友协发起人大会，筹委会亦于同日组成。到会各界代表一百八十余人，大会上发言踊跃热烈，一致认为我们要“一边倒”，要“走俄国人的路”，向苏联学习，会中并有少数民族代表参加。大会选出筹委二十九人，并确定今后要进行中苏友谊的广泛宣传。号召全省市、县、蒙旗组织支会。
    新乡各界代表四百余人亦签名发起成立中苏友好协会新乡分会，上月二十六日召开了发起人大会，选出晁哲甫等四十一人为筹备委员，正式成立筹备委员会。大会并电斯大林大元帅、毛主席及人民政协致敬和庆贺。
    【唐山电】唐山市中苏友好协会筹备会于九月三十日成立。该会由二百一十九人发起，经发起人会议选出吴德等六十二人为筹备委员。
