Title: North China Foreign Trade Administration Stipulates Forty Items Must Be Exported with Foreign Exchange Settlement Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 2

　　North China Foreign Trade Administration
  　Stipulates Forty Items Must Be Exported with Foreign Exchange Settlement Starting Today
    [This Dispatch from Tianjin] The North China Foreign Trade Administration continues to narrow the scope of barter trade and specifically stipulates that the following goods shall not be allowed for barter trade from today (the 25th) and must be exported with foreign exchange settlement (numbers are tariff numbers).
    8 Intestines, 15-16 Young and old deer antlers, 23-27 Raw hides, tanned hides, furs, 41-44 Beans, 55-58 Vegetable fuels, 67 Walnuts, walnut kernels, 89 Anise oil, 91 Cinnamon oil, 97-104 Oil wax, 106 Almonds, 109 Hemp seeds, 111 Flax seeds, 114 Radish seeds, 115 Sesame seeds, 122-130 Tea, 131-134 Tobacco, 144 Vermicelli, 149 Charcoal, 151 Coke, coking coal, 152 Firewood, 157-162 Firewood, wood, and wood products, 169 Paper flowers, 170-190 Textile fibers, 191-195 Yarns, 198-200 Hemp, velvet, yarns, 201-206 Piece goods, 207-213 Other textiles, 215 Antimony, 216 Brass and its products, 218 Copper and its products, 220-225 Metals and their products, 226-229 Glass and glassware, 230-235 Stone, soil, sand and their products, 236-249 Chemicals and chemical products, 250-253 Printed matter, 255 Candles, 258 Antiques, 263 Ivoryware, bone ware, horn ware, 265 Umbrellas, 266 Decorated lacquerware.

### 华北对外贸易管理局　规定四十项物品今起须结汇出口

1949-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北对外贸易管理局
  　规定四十项物品今起须结汇出口
    【本报天津专讯】华北对外贸易管理局，继续缩小易货范围，特规定下列各项货物自今日（二十五）起一律不准易货，必须结汇出口（号码为税则号列）。
    八肠，一五—一六老嫩鹿茸，二三—二七生皮、熟皮，皮货，四一—四四豆类，五五—五八植物性燃料，六七核桃、核桃仁，八九八角油，九一桂皮油，九七—一○四油腊，一○六杏仁，一○九麻子，一一一胡麻子，一一四莱子，一一五芝麻，一二二—一三○茶，一三一—一三四烟草，一四四粉丝，一四九炭，一五一焦炭、焦煤，一五二柴，一五七—一六二木柴，木，及木制品，一六九纸花，一七○—一九○纺织纤维，一九一—一九五纱线，一九八—二○○麻、丝绒、纱线，二○一—二○六匹头，二○七—二一三其它纺织品，二一五锑，二一六黄铜及其制品，二一八紫铜及其制品，二二○—二二五金属及其制品，二二六—二二九玻璃及玻璃器，二三○—二三五石、泥土、砂及其制品，二三六—二四九化学品及化学产品，二五○—二五三印刷品，二五五腊烛，二五八古玩，二六三象牙器、骨器、角器，二六五伞，二六六花素漆器。
