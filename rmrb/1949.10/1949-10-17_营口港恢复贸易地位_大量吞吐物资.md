Title: Yingkou Port Restores Trade Status, Large Quantities of Goods Handled
Author:
Date: 1949-10-17
Page: 2

　　Yingkou Port
  　Restores Trade Status
  　Large Quantities of Goods Handled
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 16th] In the five months from the opening of Yingkou Port in late April to the end of September, a total of 20 foreign merchant ships entered and exited. The Northeast Export Company used barter trade to export large quantities of soybeans, coal, pig bristles, hides, and well-known Northeast specialties such as ginseng and deer antlers from Yingkou; the total value of goods exchanged was over 712.37 million Northeast currency, most of which were equipment and raw materials needed for Northeast industrial construction.

### 营口港恢复贸易地位　大量吞吐物资

1949-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　营口港
  　恢复贸易地位
  　大量吞吐物资
    【新华社沈阳十六日电】东北营口从四月底开港到九月底的五个月中，进出海外商船共二十艘。东北输出公司用以货换货的方式，从营口输出了大宗的大豆、煤、猪鬃、皮张以及人参、鹿茸等著名的东北特产；换回的货物总值达东北币七千一百二十三亿七千多万元，其中大部是东北工业建设所需的器材和原料。
