Title: First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Elects National Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th. The First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, on its final day, elected the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. According to the election method passed on the 29th, the National Committee was elected by submitting the entire list stipulated through consultation for voting. The list of candidates included one hundred and eighty people, with eighteen vacancies left to accommodate suitable representatives from newly liberated areas in the future. The result of the election was that all representatives unanimously passed the following list, arranged in order of units:
