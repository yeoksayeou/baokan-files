Title: Peace Struggle Day Held Worldwide; Hundreds of Millions of Men and Women in More Than Sixty Countries Participate in Demonstrations; Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Publicly Expresses Satisfaction
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: Moscow, 9th broadcast: Paris news, on October 7th, the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress held a meeting presided over by Curie. At the end of the meeting, a public statement was issued, stating: "The Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress expresses satisfaction with the powerful actions of 'October 2nd Peace Struggle Day' around the world. The demonstrations, in which hundreds of millions of men and women in more than sixty countries participated, not only took place on the International Day of Peace Struggle, but also in the days that followed, and were of a very broad nature."

### 世界各地举行和平斗争日　六十多个国家几万万男女参加示威  和大常委会公报表示满意

1949-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界各地举行和平斗争日
    六十多个国家几万万男女参加示威
    和大常委会公报表示满意
    【新华社北京十二日电】莫斯科九日广播：巴黎消息，十月七日，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会在居里主持下举行会议。会议结束时发表公报，内称：“世界拥护和平大会常设委员会，对‘十月二日和平斗争日’在全世界的强有力行动，表示满意。六十多个国家几万万男女所参加的示威，不仅在国际和平斗争日举行了，而且在其后几天中也举行了，并且有非常广泛的性质。”
