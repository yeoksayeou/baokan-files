Title: Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Radio Station
  　Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper's dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Radio Station will broadcast a women's program at 18:15 today, introducing the life of women in Malaya. At 19:00, a lecture on common sense of natural science, written by Yang Guangzhong of the Atomic Research Institute of the Beijing Academy of Sciences: "How to Take a Bus." At 18:30, the first literary program, sponsored by the Chinese Quyi Improvement Association, with Zhen Yuhua singing the Jingyun Dagu "Liberation Army Crosses Xiaoxitian." At 22:00, the second literary program will broadcast the third section of the new Peking Opera "Mulan Joins the Army."
    Also, the second channel will have children's time at 17:15: telling the story of a Greek child, titled "The Last Letter." At 17:45: Workers' Time: Comrade Xie Sheng of the Shop Assistants' Work Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee will give a speech titled: "Our Understanding and Efforts Regarding Disaster Relief." At 18:00, a music program: excerpts from the Huaihai Campaign Suite. At 20:00, Voice of the City: The Beijing Municipal Health Testing Institute, under the Beijing Municipal Health Bureau of the People's Government of Beijing, introduces the institute's business.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十八点十五分妇女节目，介绍马来亚妇女的生活情形。十九点自然科学常识讲座，北京研究院原子研究所杨光中撰稿：“怎样乘车”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办，阵玉华演唱京韵大鼓“解放军横渡小西天”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，播送新京剧“木兰从军”第三段。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间：讲一个希腊小朋友的故事，题目是“最后的一封信”。十七时四十五分：职工时间：市总工会筹委会店员工作委员会谢生同志演话，题目是：“我们对救灾应有的认识和努力”。十八时音乐节目：淮海战役组歌选曲。二十时市政之声：北京市人民政府卫生局所属北京市卫生试验所介绍该所的业务。
