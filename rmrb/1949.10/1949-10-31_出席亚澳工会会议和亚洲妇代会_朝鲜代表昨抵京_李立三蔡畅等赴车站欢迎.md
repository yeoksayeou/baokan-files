Title: Korean Representatives Arrive in Beijing Yesterday to Attend the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Congress; Li Lisan, Cai Chang, and Others Greeted Them at the Train Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th – Choi Ho-min, Secretary-General of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea, Kim Yong-gyu, staff member of the International Department of the General Federation of Trade Unions, and Kim Kwi-sun, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's League, and Secretary Ahn Hyo-sang, representatives to the Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Representative Conference of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing at 11:30 this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Li Lisan and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Zihiu, Minister of Culture and Education, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Qu Mengjue, Secretary-General, and staff members of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference Jiu Jin (Head of the Asian Section of the Soviet Trade Union), and Toktomanbetova (Representative of Women from the Kirghiz Republic of Soviet Central Asia) representatives of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Representative Conference, totaling more than 30 people.

### 出席亚澳工会会议和亚洲妇代会　朝鲜代表昨抵京  李立三蔡畅等赴车站欢迎

1949-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席亚澳工会会议和亚洲妇代会　
    朝鲜代表昨抵京            
    李立三蔡畅等赴车站欢迎
    【新华社北京三十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国出席亚澳工会会议的代表、北朝鲜总工会秘书长崔浩敏，总工会国际部工作人员金龙奎，和出席亚洲妇女代表会议筹备工作委员会的代表北朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会秘书长金贵善、秘书安孝相等一行，于今日上午十一时半抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席李立三、刘宁一，文教部长刘子久，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，秘书长区梦觉，亚澳工会会议筹备委员会工作人员久金（苏联总工会亚洲科科长）亚洲妇女代表会议筹委会代表托克托曼别托娃（苏联中亚细亚吉尔吉斯共和国妇女代表）等三十余人。
