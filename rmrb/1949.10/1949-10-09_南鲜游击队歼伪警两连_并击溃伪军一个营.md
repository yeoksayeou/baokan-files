Title: South Korean Guerrillas Annihilate Two Companies of Puppet Police, and Rout a Battalion of Puppet Army
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: TASS, Pyongyang, 7th: The Korean Central News Agency reports that South Korean People's Guerrillas continue to wage victorious battles. On September 29th, the guerrillas annihilated two companies of police in the Soseong (transliteration) area of North Jeolla Province. On September 26th, the guerrillas attacked and defeated a police unit in the same province. Another guerrilla battalion attacked a battalion of South Korean troops near Ba Wanshan (transliteration). After three hours of fierce fighting, they forced the battalion to retreat. The enemy lost two platoons. The guerrillas captured one enemy company commander, one platoon leader, and six squad leaders, and seized twenty heavy machine guns, twenty-five automatic rifles and rifles, and other weapons.

### 南鲜游击队歼伪警两连　并击溃伪军一个营

1949-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜游击队歼伪警两连
    并击溃伪军一个营
    【新华社北京八日电】塔斯社平壤七日讯：朝鲜中央通讯社消息，南朝鲜人民游击队继续进行胜利的战斗。九月二十九日，游击队在全罗北道消灭了苏城（译音）地区的警察两个连。游击队九月二十六日在该道进攻警察队，并将其击败。另一支游击大队在巴湾山（译音）附近袭击南朝鲜军队的一个营，经三小时激战后，迫使该营溃退。敌人损失两排人。游击队俘掳敌连长、排长各一名和班长六名，并缴获重机关枪二十挺和自动步枪、步枪二十五枝以及其他武器。
