Title: Over 300 Bandits Eliminated in You County, Xiangdong Area

Author:

Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 4th: Delayed news: A certain unit of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, assisting local armed forces in suppressing the remnants of the Kuomintang bandits under Deng Ge in the vicinity of You County, Xiangdong area, on September 13th and 14th, eliminated over 350 enemy soldiers, including the bandit army's Deputy Division Commander He Qigui, capturing over 200 bandits. A small number of fleeing remnants are being pursued. 151 rifles and pistols, over 10,000 rounds of ammunition, and 3 telephone sets were seized.

### 湘东攸县地区歼土匪三百余

1949-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　湘东攸县地区歼土匪三百余
    【新华社华中前线四日电】迟到消息：人民解放军第四野战军某部在九月十三、十四两日协助地方武装清剿湘东攸县附近地区的国民党残匪邓戈部，在作战中消灭匪军副师长何其贵以下三百五十余名，内俘匪二百余名。少数逃窜残匪正被进击中。缴获长短枪一五一支，子弹万余发，电话机三部。
