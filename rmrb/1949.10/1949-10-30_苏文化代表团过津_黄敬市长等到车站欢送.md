Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Passes Through Tianjin, Mayor Huang Jing and Others Greet and See Them Off at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 29th—The Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation returned home by train from Beijing today, passing through Tianjin at 1:57 PM. Tianjin Mayor Huang Jing, Deputy Mayor Liu Xiufeng, Huang Songling, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Tianjin Branch, and Soviet expatriate representatives in Tianjin, Mr. and Mrs. Chitov, all went to the station to greet and see them off. Members of the Soviet delegation got off the train to shake hands and bid farewell to those who greeted them, and received flowers presented by the Tianjin Municipal People's Government. The delegation stopped for about ten minutes before boarding the train to Shenyang. (Attached picture)

### 苏文化代表团过津  黄敬市长等到车站欢送

1949-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏文化代表团过津　
    黄敬市长等到车站欢送
    【新华社天津二十九日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团今由京乘车返国，下午一时五十七分过津。津市长黄敬、副市长刘秀峰、中苏友好协会天津分会筹委会副主任黄松龄及天津苏侨代表契多夫夫妇等，均赴站欢送。苏联代表团各团员下车与欢送者一一握手告别，并接受天津市人民政府的献花。代表团约停十余分钟，即登车赴沈。（附图片）
