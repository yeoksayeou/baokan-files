Title: Sumatra Siantar Overseas Chinese General Association Sends Respects to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 1

　　Sumatra Siantar Overseas Chinese General Association
  　Sends Respects to Chairman Mao Zedong
    [Beijing, 19th] The Overseas Chinese General Association of Siantar, Sumatra (southeast of Medan), cabled Chairman Mao Zedong on the 17th to express their respects, saying: To Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    The birth of the People's Republic has brought us indescribable joy. All levels and groups of overseas Chinese in Medan join us in expressing our respect to you and other members of the government, and guarantee our full support for the principles established by the People's Political Consultative Conference under your wise leadership.
    Overseas Chinese General Association of Siantar, Sumatra

### 苏门答腊仙塔华侨总会　电毛泽东主席致敬

1949-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏门答腊仙塔华侨总会
  　电毛泽东主席致敬
    【北京十九日电】苏门答腊仙塔（在棉兰东南）的华侨总会十七日电毛泽东主席致敬称：北京中华人民共和国毛泽东主席：
    人民共和国的诞生给我们带来了不可言喻的欢欣。棉兰华侨各阶层、各团体与我们一道向你及政府的其他委员表示敬意，并保证完全支持在你的英明的领导之下的人民政治协商会议所订定的原则。
    苏门答腊仙塔华侨总会
