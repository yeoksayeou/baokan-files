Title: People of the World Continue to Send Congratulatory Telegrams to New China; Chairman Mao Sends Reply Telegrams of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 1

　　People of the World Continue to Send Congratulatory Telegrams to New China
    Chairman Mao Sends Reply Telegrams of Thanks
    Telegram from Misos, Chairman of the Provisional Democratic Government of Greece, to Chairman Mao
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 26th: The congratulatory telegram from Misos, Chairman of the Provisional Democratic Government of Greece, to Chairman Mao and Chairman Mao's reply telegram are as follows:
    (1) Telegram from Misos:
    Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    The Provisional Democratic Government of Greece, on behalf of the Greek people who are fighting to uphold freedom and independence, extends warm congratulations to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, established after the glorious victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    The founding of the People's Republic of China constitutes a major defeat for the imperialist camp and strengthens the camp of people's democracy and socialism. The definite victory of the Chinese people, compared with many pains and countless sacrifices, makes us feel that although we will encounter many difficulties in the struggle for the liberation of Greece and against Anglo-American imperialism and its running dog dictatorial fascists, we are sure that the victory will be certain.
    Please accept our warm congratulations, Comrade Mao Zedong!
    Misos, Chairman of the Provisional Democratic Government of Greece
    October 9, 1949
    (2) Chairman Mao's Reply Telegram to Chairman Misos of the Provisional Democratic Government of Greece:
    Thank you deeply for your congratulations. The Chinese people express warm sympathy and high respect for the Greek people's unyielding struggle for freedom, independence, and just peace under difficult conditions. I sincerely wish the Provisional Democratic Government of Greece and the patriotic Greek people to maintain unity, overcome temporary difficulties, and strive for final victory.
    Chairman Mao Zedong
    Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    October 26, 1949
    Chairman Mao's Reply Telegram to Hoxha
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a reply telegram to the congratulatory telegram from Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania. The telegram stated:
    Chairman Enver Hoxha of the Albanian Council of Ministers:
    Thank you deeply for your congratulations to the People's Republic of China. I hereby wish the friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people to become ever closer. I believe that the brave Albanian people, under your leadership, following the correct direction, will surely be able to overcome the threats of imperialism and the Tito counter-revolutionary group, and achieve their goals together with the people of the world.
    Chairman Mao Zedong
    Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    October 26, 1949
    Chairman Mao Sends Telegram of Thanks to the Algerian Communist Party
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 26th: Chairman Mao sent a telegram of thanks to the Central Committee of the Algerian Communist Party for their congratulations. The telegram stated:
    Comrade Rabi Bouhali and all comrades of the Central Committee of the Algerian Communist Party:
    I would like to express my deep gratitude for Comrade Rabi Bouhali's congratulatory letter of October 9th.
    The Chinese people have been liberated from long-term imperialist aggression and oppression. Therefore, they have warm sympathy and firm confidence in the liberation struggles of all oppressed nations. I believe that the Algerian people, under the leadership of the Algerian Communist Party and with the assistance of the international peace and democracy camp, will eventually overthrow imperialist rule. Long live the victory of the Algerian people's liberation struggle!
    Chairman Mao Zedong
    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    October 26, 1949
    Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions
    Sayan Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 26th: The congratulatory telegram from Sayan, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, to Chairman Mao and Chairman Mao's reply telegram are as follows:
    (1) Telegram from Sayan:
    Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    At the time of the first meeting after the establishment of the new government of the People's Republic of China, the Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions is pleased to extend fraternal greetings to you on behalf of the Federation's 72 million members. The World Federation of Trade Unions takes this opportunity to pay tribute to the Chinese working people, who will give the world a historically significant example. The unforgettable victory of the Chinese people, consolidated by the establishment of the new government of the People's Republic, will significantly strengthen the relationship of the common international obligations of the People's Republic of China and the forces of world peace and progress. The World Federation of Trade Unions believes that the organizations of the Chinese trade unions, which have contributed their share in the cause of the Chinese people's victory over their oppressors, will increasingly strengthen their activities so that the newly established People's Republic of China becomes a free, democratic, strong, and peaceful country, whose people will be happy to care for all working people.
    Sayan, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions
    October 5th, Paris
    (2) Chairman Mao's Reply Telegram
    Mr. Sayan, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Paris:
    Thank you deeply for your congratulations to the People's Republic of China. The Chinese people are well aware that their victory depends on the assistance of the international workers' movement, and will strive to consolidate the immortal friendship of sharing weal and woe with the working people of all countries.
    Mao Zedong
    October 26, 1949, Beijing

### 世界人民继续电贺新中国  毛主席分别复电致谢

1949-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界人民继续电贺新中国　
    毛主席分别复电致谢
    希腊临时民主政府主席米苏致毛主席电
    【新华社北京二十六日电】希腊临时民主政府主席米苏给毛主席的贺电和毛主席的复电如下：
    （一）米苏来电
    北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    希腊临时民主政府，代表为坚持争取自由和它的独立而斗争的希腊人民，向在中国解放军的辉煌胜利之后建立的中华人民共和国中央人民政府致以热情的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的成立构成了帝国主义阵营的一个最大的失败，并加强了人民民主及社会主义的阵营。中国人民确定的胜利与许多痛苦和不可胜数的牺牲相比，使得我们感到了虽然我们在争取希腊的解放和反对英美帝国主义及其走狗独裁法西斯的斗争中也会遭遇到许多的困难，但是，我们确信胜利的取得将会是一定的。
    请您接受我们的热烈的庆贺，毛泽东同志！
    希腊临时民主政府主席米苏（ｍｉｔｓｏｓ）
    一九四九年十月九日
    （二）毛主席复电希腊临时民主政府米苏主席：
    深深地感谢你的祝贺。中国人民对于希腊人民在困难条件下不屈不挠地争取自由独立和公正和平的奋斗，表示热烈的同情和崇高的敬意。谨祝希腊临时民主政府和希腊爱国人民坚持团结，克服暂时的困难，争取最后的胜利。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府
    主席毛泽东
    一九四九年十月二十六日
    毛主席复霍查电
    【新华社北京二十六日电】毛泽东主席电复阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席霍查的贺电。电称：
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议昂凡尔·霍查主席：
    深深地感谢你对于中华人民共和国的祝贺。我在此谨祝中国人民与阿尔巴尼亚人民的友谊日臻亲密。我相信，在你领导之下的勇敢的遵循着正确方向前进的阿尔巴尼亚人民，必能和全世界人民一起，战胜帝国主义和铁托反革命集团的威胁，而达到自己的目的。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府
    主席毛泽东
    一九四九年十月二十六日
    毛主席电谢阿尔及尔共产党
    【新华社北京二十六日电】毛主席电谢阿尔及尔共产党中央委员会的祝贺。电称：
    拉比·蒲哈利同志和阿尔及尔共产党中央委员会全体同志们：
    对于拉比·蒲哈利同志十月九日的贺函，谨表深切的感谢。
    中国人民是从帝国主义长期侵略压迫下求得解放的。因此对于各被压迫民族的解放斗争，具有热烈的同情和坚定的信心。我相信，阿尔及尔人民在阿尔及尔共产党领导下和国际和平民主阵营援助下，终将推翻帝国主义的统治。阿尔及尔人民解放斗争的胜利万岁！
    中国共产党中央委员会
    主席毛泽东
    一九四九年十月二十六日
    世界职工联合会书记　
    赛扬致毛主席电
    【新华社北京二十六日电】世界职工联合会书记赛扬给毛主席的贺电和毛主席的复电如下：
    （一）赛扬来电
    北京中华人民共和国毛泽东主席：
    在中华人民共和国新政府成立后举行第一次会议的时候，世界职工联合会书记很愉快地代表本会七千二百万会员向您致兄弟的敬意。世界职工联合会，乘这时候向中国劳动人民致敬，他们将给全世界一个有历史意义的榜样。由于人民共和国新政府之建立而巩固了的中国人民的不可忘却的胜利，将显著地加强人民中国际共同义务的关系和世界和平进步的力量。世界职工联合会相信：在中国人民战胜其压迫者的事业中已经贡献了他们本份的中国职工会的组织，将愈益加强活动，使新成立的中华共和国成为一个自由民主强大和平的国家，她的人民将愉快地关心着所有的劳动的人们。
    世界职工联合会书记赛扬
    十月五日于巴黎
    （二）毛主席复电
    巴黎世界职工联合会书记赛扬先生：
    深深地感谢您对于中华人民共和国的祝贺。中国人民深知自己的胜利有赖于国际工人运动的援助，并将努力巩固与世界各国劳动人民休戚与共的不朽的友谊。
    毛泽东
    一九四九年十月二十六日于北京
