Title: Nanjing Representatives from All Circles, 3,000 Strong, Gather Enthusiastically to Welcome the Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 14th. More than 3,000 representatives from all circles in Nanjing gathered at the People's Hall at 8 p.m. on the 13th to welcome the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation headed by Fadeyev and Simonov. When Fadeyev and others entered the venue in batches, the entire audience applauded warmly to welcome them. Those being welcomed waved their hands and hats in thanks. The welcome meeting began with Vice Mayor Ke Qingshi delivering a welcome speech on behalf of the people of Nanjing, and Yin Zanxun delivering a speech on behalf of the preparatory committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Nanjing Branch and the Nanjing scientific and cultural circles. Then, Agnaz delivered a response on behalf of the Soviet delegation. After congratulating the Chinese people on the victory of the revolution and the success of the new China's construction, he pointed out that the friendship between China and the Soviet Union is a friendship of mutual equality and mutual respect. The Soviet people greatly respect the Chinese nation, which has a culture of several thousand years, and believe that the Chinese people have great creativity. Subsequently, various organs, groups, and schools presented flowers and flags to the delegation. Postal worker Wei Yanggu specially presented an ancient "Celebration Bowl" produced in Jingdezhen, Jiangxi. This gift demonstrates the beautiful culture of the Chinese nation with a long history, and also demonstrates the amazing artistic genius of the Chinese working class. When the delegation stepped onto the stage to accept, the military music and applause lasted for a long time. Fadeyev was moved by this great friendship and immediately shouted: "I pay tribute to the people of all circles in Nanjing with the most sincere and enthusiastic feelings. Long live the Chinese people and its great leader Mao Zedong!" Finally, the delegation performed wonderful programs such as piano and violin solos, dances, and solos, which won warm cheers and applause from the audience. The hosts and guests did not part until 11:30 p.m.

### 南京各界代表三千人集会　热烈欢迎苏联代表团

1949-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京各界代表三千人集会
    热烈欢迎苏联代表团
    【新华社南京十四日电】南京市各界代表三千余人，于十三日下午八时假人民大会堂集会欢迎以法捷耶夫、西蒙诺夫为首的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团。当法捷耶夫等分批进入会场时，全场热烈鼓掌欢迎。被欢迎者则不断地挥手、挥帽子致谢。欢迎会开始，首由柯庆施副市长代表南京人民，尹赞勋代表中苏友好协会南京分会筹备会及南京科学文化界致欢迎词。继由阿格那兹代表苏联代表团致答词。他在祝贺中国人民革命胜利和新中国建设成功之后，指出中苏的友谊是互相平等互相尊重的友谊。苏联人民非常尊重有着几千年文化的中华民族，相信中国人民有很大的创造力。接着由各机关、团体、学校向代表团献花、献旗。邮务工人魏阳谷，特地献送江西景德镇的出品古“喜庆碗”。这个礼物表现出中华民族有着悠久历史的优美文化，又表现了中国工人阶级惊人的艺术天才。当代表团登台接受时，军乐与掌声历久不息。法捷耶夫为这种伟大的友谊所激动，当即高呼：“我以最真诚最热烈的心情，向南京各界人民致敬，中国人民和它的伟大领袖毛泽东万岁！”最后由代表团演出钢琴、提琴独奏、舞蹈、独唱等精彩节目，博得全场热烈欢呼和鼓掌。至夜晚十一时半宾主始尽欢而散。
