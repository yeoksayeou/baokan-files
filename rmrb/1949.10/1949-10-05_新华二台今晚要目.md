　　Xinhua Second Program Tonight's Highlights
　　17:00—17:15 Overture, report on the evening program. 17:15—17:45 Children's Time: Mr. Sun Jingxiu tells "The Story of Pavel". 17:45—18:00, Workers' Time: Broadcast a comprehensive report about the capital's workers warmly celebrating the founding of the Central People's Government. 18:00—18:15 Time check, music program: The People's Art Troupe sings Soviet songs. 18:15—18:30 Joint broadcast of the First Program's Youth Program: Broadcast an editorial from the 21st issue of Chinese Youth entitled "Long Live the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference". 19:30—19:45 Second local news broadcast. 20:00—20:15 Time check, commentary, Voice of the Municipality: The Beijing Municipal People's Government Education Bureau's announcement about the Beijing People's Sports Meeting. 20:30—21:30 Joint broadcast of the First Program's third standard Mandarin news broadcast, international current events, commentary or comprehensive report, speech or communication. 21:45—22:00 Preview of tomorrow's program, finale.
