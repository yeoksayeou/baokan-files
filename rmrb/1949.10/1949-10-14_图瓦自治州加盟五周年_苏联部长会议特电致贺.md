Title: Fifth Anniversary of the Tuva Autonomous Oblast Joining the USSR - Telegram of Congratulations from the USSR Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: TASS Moscow - October 11th marks the fifth anniversary of the people of Tannu Tuva (located in Central Asia, northwest of the Mongolian People's Republic) joining the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. The USSR Council of Ministers, together with the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party, the Presidiums of the Supreme Soviets of both the USSR and the Russian Federation, and the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, all sent congratulations to the Tuva Autonomous Oblast. On the 11th, the capital's newspapers all featured on their front pages these congratulatory telegrams and the decree issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR: awarding orders and medals to a large number of working people of the Tuva Autonomous Oblast for their achievements in the process of developing the national economy, science, and culture.

Toda, Secretary of the Tuva Oblast Committee of the All-Union Communist Party, wrote in Pravda: "The working people of our oblast fully understand that only under Soviet reality could they have achieved economic and cultural successes. The achievements of these five years provide new proof of Stalin's brilliant proposition. Stalin said: 'The liberated non-European peoples, drawn into the torrent of Soviet development, are no less capable than the European peoples of developing truly progressive cultures and truly progressive civilizations.' Thanks to the care and assistance of the Soviet government, the Party of Lenin and Stalin, and the entire Soviet people, Tuva is becoming a prosperous place in Central Asia, providing a vivid illustration to the colonial peoples of the East on how to fight for happiness and prosperity."

The editorial in Izvestia pointed out, "The Soviet system provides every nation living in our country with every opportunity for free national development. Today, on the fifth anniversary of Tuva's joining the fraternal family of Soviet peoples, the Soviet people wholeheartedly congratulate the Tuva people. This extraordinary example of the rapid development of a small but capable nation once again demonstrates the great power of the Soviet state system. The Soviet state has provided the world with an example of solving national questions and organizing friendly cooperation between nations. The Soviet people proudly contrast the radiant world of friendship and brotherhood among the various nationalities of the Soviet Union with the imperialist world of national hatred and national oppression. The Soviet people's hatred for the reactionary forces of imperialism is irreconcilable; our socialist state is the hope and fortress of all those who oppose imperialism and strive for peace, democracy, and socialism, and the Soviet people are proud of this."

### 图瓦自治州加盟五周年  苏联部长会议特电致贺

1949-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　图瓦自治州加盟五周年  苏联部长会议特电致贺
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：十月十一日是唐努·图瓦（位于中亚细亚，在蒙古人民共和国西北）人民加入苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟五周年纪念日。苏联部长会议与联共中央、苏联和俄罗斯联邦的两最高苏维埃主席团及俄罗斯联邦部长会议均电图瓦自治州致贺。十一日首都各报均以首页刊登这些贺电及苏联最高苏维埃主席团所颁布的命令：因图瓦自治州一大批劳动人民在发展国民经济、科学与文化的进程中所获得的成就，而授予他们以勋章与奖章。
    联共图瓦州委员会书记托达在真理报上写道：“我们州的劳动人民完全了解，只有在苏维埃现实的条件下，他们才可能获得经济与文化的成就。五年的成果给斯大林的天才命题提供了新的明证。斯大林说过：“卷入苏维埃发展之洪流中的解放了的非欧洲人民，他们发展真正进步的文化和真正进步的文明的能力，并不亚于欧洲人民”。由于苏维埃政权、列宁、斯大林的党和全体苏维埃人民的关心和帮助，图瓦正变成中亚细亚繁荣的地方，给东方殖民地人民提供了应如何为幸福与繁荣而斗争的生动的说明。”
    消息报社论指出，“苏维埃制度为每一个生活在我国的民族提供了民族自由发展的一切机会。今天，苏维埃人民值此图瓦参加苏维埃各族人民兄弟大家庭五周年之际，衷心祝贺图瓦人民。这个小而有能力的民族迅速发展的奇异范例，再次说明苏维埃国家制度所具有的伟大力量。苏维埃国家业已向全世界提供了解决民族问题和组织各民族间友好合作的范例。苏联人民骄傲地以苏联各族人民之间的友爱和兄弟之谊的光芒万丈的世界，与民族仇恨与民族压迫的帝国主义世界相对立。苏联人民对帝国主义反动派的憎恨是无可调和的；我们的社会主义国家，乃是所有反对帝国主义、争取和平、民主与社会主义的人们的希望和堡垒，苏联人民都以此自豪。”
