Title: Commemorating Lu Xun: Beijing Library Holds Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 4

　　Commemorating Lu Xun
    Beijing Library Holds Exhibition
    [Beijing News] The 19th of this month marks the 13th anniversary of the death of the great Chinese writer Lu Xun. The National Beijing Library is holding an exhibition of Mr. Lu Xun's life and works, scheduled for a preview this morning (8th) (Saturday) at 10:00 AM, with the official exhibition opening on the 10th.

### 纪念鲁迅　北京图书馆举办展览

1949-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念鲁迅
    北京图书馆举办展览
    【北京讯】本月十九日为中国人民文豪鲁迅先生逝世十三周年纪念，国立北京图书馆特举办鲁迅先生生活作品展览会，定于今（八）日（星期六）上午十时先行预展，十日起正式展览。
