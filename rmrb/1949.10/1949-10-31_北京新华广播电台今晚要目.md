Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-31
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    Tonight's Highlights
    [Our Dispatch] The Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast a women's program at 18:15 today, discussing the life and struggle of Korean women. At 19:00, there will be a special plague prevention program introducing how to eradicate the plague. At 18:30, the first literary and artistic program, sponsored by the China Ballad and Folk Song Improvement Association: Cai Liangui will perform the Xihe Dagu "Dong Cunrui Sacrifices Himself to Blow Up the Bunker."
    Also, the second channel will have children's time at 17:15: telling two stories: 1. The Story of the Will-o'-the-Wisp, 2. Zhang Daming's Dream. At 20:00, the Voice of the Municipality will "Talk about the sources, symptoms, and prevention of plague."

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台　
    今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十八点十五分妇女节目，谈朝鲜妇女生活和斗争的情形。十九点防治鼠疫的特别节目，介绍怎样扑灭鼠疫。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：蔡连贵演唱西河大鼓“董存瑞舍身炸碉堡”。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间：讲两个故事：一、鬼火的故事，二、张大明的梦。二十时市政之声“谈谈鼠疫的病源病状和预防。”
