Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station tonight at 18:15, Workers' Program: "Visiting the Tula Province Coal Mine in the Soviet Union." 19:00 Natural Science Lecture, written by Gong Jiesheng of the Geology Department of Peking University: "Gold." 18:30 First Literary and Art Program, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Association: Paizi Opera "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix." 22:00 Second Literary and Art Program, broadcasting Soviet music.
    Also, Channel 2, 17:15 Women's Time: Dr. An of Peking University Hospital will talk about common sense of women's health, titled "Symptoms of Antepartum Hemorrhage." 17:45 Youth Time: Comrade Huang Yuzhu of the No. 2 Girls' Middle School Broadcasting Group of this station will "Introduce the Noontime Newspaper Reading System in Class C of the Senior Third Grade of No. 2 Girls' Middle School."

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
    今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分职工节目：“参观苏联图拉省煤矿”。十九点自然科学讲座，北京大学地质系巩杰生撰稿：“金”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：牌子戏“百鸟朝凤”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，播送苏联音乐。
    又第二台十七时十五分妇女时间：由北大医院安大夫讲妇女卫生常识，题目是“产前出血的病症”。十七时四十五分青年时间：由本台女二中广播小组黄玉竹同学“介绍女二中高三乙班的中午读报制”。
