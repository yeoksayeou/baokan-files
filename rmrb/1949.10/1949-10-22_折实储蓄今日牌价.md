Title: Commodity Unit Savings Exchange Rate Today

Author: 
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 2

　　Commodity Unit Savings Exchange Rate Today
    The Commodity Unit Savings Department of the People's Bank of China Beijing Branch announces that the standard commodity unit exchange rate for today (October 22nd) is 872.31 yuan.
