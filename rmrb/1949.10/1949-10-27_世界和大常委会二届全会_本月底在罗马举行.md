Title: World Peace Congress Standing Committee Second Plenary Session to be Held in Rome at the End of This Month
Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris: The Secretariat of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Paris has issued a public statement, saying that the Second Plenary Session of the Standing Committee will be held in Rome on October 28, 29 and 30 under the chairmanship of Joliot-Curie. More than 100 representatives from more than 30 countries have agreed to participate in the work of the meeting. The Second Plenary Session will summarize the peace struggles in various parts of the world since the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Paris, especially the widespread response to the International Day of Peace on October 2, and will draw due conclusions from this.

### 世界和大常委会二届全会　本月底在罗马举行

1949-10-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界和大常委会二届全会　
    本月底在罗马举行
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社巴黎讯；巴黎世界拥护和平大会常设委员会秘书处顷发表公报称：常设委员会第二届全体会议定于十月二十八日、二十九日、三十日在约里奥·居里主持之下在罗马举行。已有三十余国的一百名代表同意参加会议的工作。第二届全体大会将总结自巴黎世界拥护和平大会以来世界各地的和平斗争，特别是世界各地对十月二日国际和平日的广泛响应，并将从此作出应有的结论。
