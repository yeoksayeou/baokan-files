Title: Premier of the Greek Provisional Democratic Government Sends Telegram Congratulating Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1949-10-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: Electric News Agency, Prague, 12th: According to the Free Greece Radio Station: The Premier of the Greek Provisional Democratic Government, Basalides, sent a telegram on October 9th congratulating Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The telegram stated: This event, following the great victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, is a major blow to world imperialism and strengthens the position of the world's democratic and peace camp.

### 希临时民主政府总理　电贺毛泽东主席

1949-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　希临时民主政府总理
  　电贺毛泽东主席
    【新华社北京十四日电】电通社布拉格十二日电：据自由希腊电台报道：希腊临时民主政府总理巴萨利德斯于十月九日电贺中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东。电文称：在中国人民解放军的伟大胜利后接着发生的这一事件，是对世界帝国主义的一个重大打击，并加强了世界民主和平阵营的地位。
