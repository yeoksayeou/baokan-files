Title: Labour-Progressive Party of Canada Congratulates the Birth of New China
Author: Tim Buck
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Labour-Progressive Party of Canada
  　Telegraphic Congratulations on the Birth of New China
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th] We have received here a congratulatory telegram from Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada, celebrating the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:
    We, the communists of Canada, are inspired by the heroism of your struggle and enlightened by the glorious leadership of your struggle. On behalf of all Canadian friends of the people's China and in the true interests of the future of our democracy, we pay our respects to the honored representatives of the victorious democratic masses of China who are holding the historic People's Political Consultative Conference. We congratulate you on proclaiming the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. We consider this a historic consolidation of people's democracy, an inspiration and example to all national peoples of Asia, and a new and enormously important force for world peace.
    　　　　　　　　Tim Buck (signed), General Secretary of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada

### 加拿大劳工进步党　电贺新中国诞生
蒂姆·布克
1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　加拿大劳工进步党
  　电贺新中国诞生
    【新华社北京四日电】此间收到加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立的贺电一件。电文如下：
    我们，加拿大共产主义者，为你们斗争的英雄主义所鼓舞，并从你们斗争的光辉领导得到启示。谨以人民中国的所有加拿大友人的名义和为了我国民主未来的真正利益，向举行具有历史意义的人民政治协商会议的中国胜利的民主群众的荣誉代表们致敬礼。我们庆贺你们宣告成立中华人民共和国中央政府，我们认为这是人民民主的历史性的巩固，是对亚洲一切民族人民的鼓舞与范例，是世界和平的一个新的关系极大的生力军。
    　　　　　　　　加拿大劳工—进步党总书记蒂姆·布克（签字）
