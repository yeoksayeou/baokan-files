Title: People's Political Consultative Conference Photo Exhibition Attracts Eight Thousand Visitors on First Day
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 4
　　
　　People's Political Consultative Conference Photo Exhibition Attracts Eight Thousand Visitors on First Day

    [Our Newspaper] The current affairs photo exhibition held by the North China Pictorial Publishing House at the People's Education Center in Qianmen Arrow Tower opened yesterday (the 23rd). Visitors were extremely enthusiastic, reaching over eight thousand people on the first day. The signing area at the entrance of the exhibition hall was exceptionally crowded. The exhibition displayed over one hundred and eighty genuine photographs of the People's Political Consultative Conference and the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. One visitor wrote after the viewing: After seeing this exhibition, I feel even more the greatness of the People's Republic of China.

### 人民政协照片展览第一天八千人参观

1949-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民政协照片展览第一天八千人参观
    【本报讯】华北画报社在前门箭楼人民教育馆举办的时事照片展览，于昨（二十三）日开幕，参观者极为踊跃，第一天即达八千余人，参观室门口签名处拥挤异常。会中展览了人民政协和中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立典礼真影一百八十余幅。一位参观的人在参观后写道：看了这个展览，使我更加感到中华人民共和国的伟大。
