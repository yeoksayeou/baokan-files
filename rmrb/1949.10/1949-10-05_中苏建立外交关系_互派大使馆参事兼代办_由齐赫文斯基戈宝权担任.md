Title: China and the Soviet Union Establish Diplomatic Relations; Exchange of Embassy Counselors and Chargés d'affaires; Zikhvinsky and Ge Baoquan Appointed
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th—Following the exchange of notes between the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet government and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our government expressing their willingness to establish diplomatic relations, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet government has appointed Zikhvinsky, the former Soviet Consul General in Beiping, as Counselor and Chargé d'affaires of the Soviet Embassy in the People's Republic of China. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our government has appointed Ge Baoquan as Counselor and Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, received Zikhvinsky at 4:00 PM yesterday.

### 中苏建立外交关系 　互派大使馆参事兼代办 　由齐赫文斯基戈宝权担任

1949-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏建立外交关系
  　互派大使馆参事兼代办
  　由齐赫文斯基戈宝权担任
    【新华社北京四日电】苏联政府外交部与我国政府外交部互换照会表示愿意建立外交关系后，苏联政府外交部已任命原北平苏联总领事齐赫文斯基为苏联驻中华人民共和国大使馆参事兼代办，我国政府外交部已任命戈宝权为中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆参事兼临时代办。我外交部长周恩来已于昨日下午四时接见齐赫文斯基。
