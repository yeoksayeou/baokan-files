Title: Zhou Enlai's Report on the Common Program of the CPPCC Published in Full by the Czech "Red Power" Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 30th -- The Czech Communist Party's organ "Red Power" newspaper published in a prominent position on the 29th the full text of General Zhou Enlai's report on the Common Program of the CPPCC, broadcast by Xinhua News Agency. On the same day, the newspaper reported that Czech parliamentarian and Deputy Mayor of Prague, Klossner, discussed the heroic struggle of the Chinese people at a meeting of the Prague Municipal Central Nationalities Committee on the 27th, and suggested that the Central Nationalities Committee send a telegram to the capital of the People's Democratic China—Beijing—to congratulate it.

### 周恩来关于人民政协共同纲领的报告　 捷“红色权力”报全文刊载

1949-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　周恩来关于人民政协共同纲领的报告
  　 捷“红色权力”报全文刊载
    【新华社布拉格三十日电】捷共机关报“红色权力”报二十九日以显著地位刊载新华社广播的周恩来将军关于人民政协共同纲领的报告全文。同日该报报道，捷议员布拉格市副市长克罗斯纳于二十七日在布拉格市中央民族委员会会议上论述中国人民的英勇的斗争，并建议中央民族委员会致电人民民主中国的首都——北京祝贺。
