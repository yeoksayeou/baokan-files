Title: Our Overseas Cultural Troupe and Soccer Team Return to Harbin
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 29th. The seventy-eight members of the Chinese delegation's cultural troupe and the All-China Students' Federation basketball team, who participated in the World Youth Festival, returned to Harbin on the 27th at 6:00 PM during their journey home. More than 3,000 students from major universities and middle schools in Harbin and members of the Communist Youth League came to the station to welcome them. At the meeting, Li Bozhao, head of the cultural troupe, delivered a short speech, expressing joy and congratulations on the birth of the People's Republic of China. On the afternoon of the 28th, the cultural troupe performed the "Waist Drum Dance," which won a special prize in Budapest, and other programs at a welcome meeting for the city's masses.

### 我出国文工团球队返抵哈尔滨

1949-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　我出国文工团球队
   　返抵哈尔滨
    【新华社哈尔滨二十九日电】参加世界青年联欢大会的中国代表团所属文艺工作团和全国学联篮球队一行七十八人，二十七日午后六时在归国途中返抵哈尔滨。哈市各大中学校学生、青年团团员等三千余人，都到车站欢迎。会上文艺工作团团长李伯钊发表简短演说，对中华人民共和国诞生表示欢欣祝贺。二十八日下午，文工团在全市群众的欢迎会上表演在布达佩斯荣获特奖的“腰鼓舞”和其他节目。
