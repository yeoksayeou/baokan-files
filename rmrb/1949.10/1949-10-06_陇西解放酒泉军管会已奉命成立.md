Title: Longxi Liberated, Jiuquan Military Control Commission Established

Author:

Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 1

　　Longxi Liberated, Jiuquan Military Control Commission Established
　　[Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station broadcast] Lanzhou, 5th—Jiuquan, an important city in western Gansu, was declared liberated on the 25th of last month. The Jiuquan Military Control Commission has been ordered to be established.

### 陇西解放酒泉军管会已奉命成立

1949-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　陇西解放酒泉军管会已奉命成立
    【北京新华广播电台广播】兰州五日消息：甘肃西部的重要城市酒泉，已经在上月二十五日宣告解放，酒泉军管会已经奉令成立。
