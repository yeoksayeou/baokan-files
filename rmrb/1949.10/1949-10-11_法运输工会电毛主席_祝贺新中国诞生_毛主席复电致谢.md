Title: French Transport Workers' Union Cables Chairman Mao, Congratulating on the Birth of New China; Chairman Mao Replies with Thanks
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th - The French Transport Workers' Union cabled Chairman Mao Zedong to congratulate the birth of the People's Republic of China. Chairman Mao immediately replied to express his gratitude. The two cables are as follows:

Incoming Cable:
Chairman of the Chinese Government, Beijing:
French transport workers extend their sympathy and congratulations to the democratic government of the People's Republic and the Chinese workers, for the success you have obtained.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　French Transport Workers' Union

Reply Cable:
Comrades of the French Transport Workers' Union:
I deeply express my gratitude for your cable congratulating the birth of the People's Republic of China.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 9, 1949, Beijing

### 法运输工会电毛主席　祝贺新中国诞生　毛主席复电致谢

1949-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　法运输工会电毛主席
    祝贺新中国诞生
   毛主席复电致谢
   【新华社北京十日电】法国运输工会致电毛泽东主席祝贺中华人民共和国诞生，毛主席当即复电致谢。两电如下：来电
    北京中国政府主席：
    法国运输工人向人民共和国民主政府和中国工人致以同情和祝贺，为你们得到的成功。
   　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　法国运输工会复电
    法国运输工会的同志们：
    我对于你们来电祝贺中华人民共和国的诞生，深深表示谢意。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月九日　北京
