Title: Opening Up Sales Channels for Native Products, Organizing the Export of Mountain Products: Investigation Teams from the North China Native Produce Company Depart for Shanghai and the Northeast for Research
Author: Huabei Tu Chan Gong Si Zi Liao Shi
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 2

　　Opening Up Sales Channels for Native Products, Organizing the Export of Mountain Products
    Investigation Teams from the North China Native Produce Company Depart for Shanghai and the Northeast for Research
    [Report from this Newspaper] In order to systematically promote the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, open up sales channels for native products, strive to export large quantities of mountain goods and medicinal materials profitably, and conduct large-scale internal and external adjustments, the North China Native Produce Company, with the consent of the Trade Administration Bureau, organized two investigation teams (three people per team) which departed for Shanghai and the Northeast on the 17th of this month for research.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(North China Native Produce Company Archives)

### 打开土产销路组织山货出口  华北土产公司调查小组分赴上海东北进行考察
华北土产公司资料室
1949-10-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　打开土产销路组织山货出口
    华北土产公司调查小组分赴上海东北进行考察
    【本报讯】华北土产公司为了有计划的进行城乡物资交流，打开土产销路。大量争取山货药材有利出口，并进行内外大宗调剂，特商得贸易管理局同意，组织了两个调查小组（每组三人），于本月十七日分赴上海、东北两地进行考察。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（华北土产公司资料室）
