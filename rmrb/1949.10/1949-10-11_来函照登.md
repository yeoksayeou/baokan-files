Date: 1949-10-11
Page: 4

Letters to the Editor
To the Comrades of the People's Daily Editorial Department:
　　In today's (10th) pictorial, there is a picture of Markov giving a speech. Markov is not the Vice Chairman of the Soviet Cultural Association, but the Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Cultural Association. I hope you will make a correction. Best regards.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xiao San, October 10th
