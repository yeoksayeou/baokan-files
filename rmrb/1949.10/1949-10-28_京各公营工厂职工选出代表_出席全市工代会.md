Title: State-Owned Factory Workers in Beijing Elect Representatives to Attend Municipal Workers' Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-10-28
Page: 2

　　Beijing State-Owned Factory Workers
　　Elect Representatives
　　To Attend Municipal Workers' Congress
　　[Beijing News] Representatives from Beijing's state-owned factories who will attend the first Municipal Workers' Congress have now been completely selected, totaling one hundred and seventy people (excluding railway worker representatives). The list of representatives is currently being reviewed by the Industrial Workers' Work Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee.

### 京各公营工厂职工选出代表  出席全市工代会

1949-10-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　京各公营工厂职工　
    选出代表　
    出席全市工代会
    【北京讯】京市各公营工厂出席全市首届职工代表大会之代表，现已全部选出，共一百七十人（铁路工人代表在外），代表名单正由市总工会筹委会产业工人工作委员会审查中。
