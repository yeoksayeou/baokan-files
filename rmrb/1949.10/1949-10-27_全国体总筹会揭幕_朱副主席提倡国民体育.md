Title: National Sports Federation Preparatory Committee Inaugurated, Vice Chairman Zhu Advocates National Sports
Author:
Date: 1949-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th. The preparatory meeting of the All-China Sports Federation was inaugurated in Beijing today. The 188 representatives present included 22 representatives from 13 units, including the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Student Federation, and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Railway Federation of Trade Unions, and 166 representatives from Beijing, Northeast, North China, Central China, Shanghai, Jinan, Tianjin, Qingdao, Nanjing, and Xi'an. After the congress passed Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, Gao Tang, Li Dequan, Yi Lirong, Liu Qingyang, Liu Gongliang, Zhang Zhixiang, Jiang Ling, Ma John, Xu Yingchao, Wu Yunrui, Li Qingan, Huang Shi, Zhao Fengzhu, Li Yiqing, Chen Shengfu, Guo Xiaofen, Yu Guifang, Xiao Yan, Xi Yunfang, and Wang Lifu as the 22 members of the presidium, Liao Chengzhi, chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, delivered the opening speech on behalf of the presidium. He pointed out that in the future, sports should be promoted from schools to factories, rural areas, troops, government agencies, and every corner. Sports workers should unite under the banner of Chairman Mao and strive to develop national sports. Vice Chairman Zhu De addressed the meeting amidst warm applause. First, he extended congratulations to the congress on behalf of the Central People's Government and the People's Revolutionary Military Commission. He then pointed out that sports should serve the interests of national defense and national health. Vozni, head of the Propaganda Department of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Association, extended congratulations to the congress on behalf of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation. Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, Li Dequan, Minister of Health and Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Yi Lirong, Minister of Labor Insurance and Welfare of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered speeches successively, all hoping to popularize sports to every corner of the country, exercise the people's physique, and enable workers, women, and students across the country to well undertake the tasks of production and construction and learning. Finally, Ma John, a representative of the sports community, spoke, calling on sports workers across the country to unite and take on the great task of developing the new democratic sports movement. In the afternoon, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, gave a report to the conference entitled "New Democratic National Sports."

### 全国体总筹会揭幕  朱副主席提倡国民体育

1949-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国体总筹会揭幕　
    朱副主席提倡国民体育
    【新华社北京二十六日电】中华全国体育总会筹备会议今日在京揭幕。出席代表一百八十八人，包括中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国总工会、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国铁路总工会筹备委员会等十三个单位的代表二十二人及北京、东北、华北、华中、上海、济南、天津、青岛、南京、西安等地区代表一百六十六人。大会在通过冯文彬、廖承志、高棠、李德全、易礼容、刘清扬、刘公亮、张致祥、江凌、马约翰、徐英超、吴蕴瑞、李清安、黄狮、赵逢珠、李一青、陈盛甫、郭效汾、俞贵方、萧晏、郗运舫、王立富等二十二人为大会主席团后，由中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志代表主席团致开幕词，他指出今后体育要从学校推广到工厂、农村、部队、机关团体和每个角落去。体育工作者要在毛主席的旗帜下团结起来，为发展国民体育而奋斗。朱德副主席在热烈的掌声中向大会讲话，他首先代表中央人民政府和人民革命军事委员会向大会致贺，随即指出体育运动要为国防和国民健康的利益服务。苏联青年反法西斯协会宣传部部长伏兹尼代表苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团向大会致贺。中央人民政府教育部部长马叙伦、卫生部部长暨全国妇联副主席李德全、中华全国总工会劳动保险福利部部长易礼容先后致词，一致希望把体育运动普及到全国的每一个角落，锻炼人民体格，使全国的工人、妇女、学生能很好地担负起生产建设和学习的任务。最后体育界代表马约翰讲话，他号召全国体育工作者团结起来，担负起开展新民主主义体育运动伟大的任务。下午，青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬向大会作题为“新民主主义的国民体育”的报告。
