Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee and Other Units Initiate Preparations to Form National Sports Organizations to Assist the People's Government in Promoting and Developing Sports
Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, in order to organize a preparatory body for national sports organizations, invited more than ten units, including the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation, to a symposium on the morning of October 19th at 9:00 AM. At the meeting, everyone unanimously agreed to organize a national sports preparatory organization and established a secretariat for the National Sports Organization Preparatory Conference to be responsible for preparatory matters. At 2:00 PM on the 20th, the preparatory committee held its first preparatory meeting, attended by Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and more than one hundred representatives from the initiating units and various regions. First, Feng Wenbin reported on the purpose of initiating the preparatory committee for national sports organizations. He said: The task of the preparatory committee is to determine the direction of new people's sports and to establish an organizational structure to guide national sports activities, in order to assist the People's Government in promoting and developing sports. Liao Chengzhi then delivered a speech, calling for the popularization of sports to factories, troops, schools, and every corner of the country. Finally, the representatives exchanged views on the meeting agenda and decided to hold a forum from the 21st, visit the Beijing People's Sports Conference from the 22nd to the 24th, and officially open on the 26th. At the meeting, the organizational charter of the National Sports Organization Preparatory Committee will be adopted, and preparatory committee members will be elected.

### 青年团中央等单位发起　筹组全国体育团体  协助人民政府推广和开展体育运动

1949-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央等单位发起
  　筹组全国体育团体
    协助人民政府推广和开展体育运动
    【新华社北京二十一日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为组织全国体育组织的筹备机构，特于十月十九日上午九时邀请中华全国总工会、中国人民革命军事委员会政治部、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国学生联合会等十余单位举行座谈会。会上大家一致赞同组织全国性的体育筹备组织，并成立了全国体育组织筹备会议秘书处，负责筹备事宜。二十日下午二时，筹备会举行第一次预备会议，到有青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志和筹备发起各单位及各地区代表百余人。首由冯文彬报告发起组织全国体育组织筹备会的目的。他说：筹备会的任务是确定人民新体育的方向，成立指导全国体育运动的组织机构，以协助人民政府推广和开展体育运动。继由廖承志致词，他号召把体育普及到工厂、部队、学校和全国每个角落去。最后各代表就会议日程交换了意见，决定自二十一日起举行座谈，二十二日至二十四日参观北京市人民体育大会，二十六日正式开幕，会议上将通过全国体育组织筹备会的组织章程，并选举筹备委员等。
