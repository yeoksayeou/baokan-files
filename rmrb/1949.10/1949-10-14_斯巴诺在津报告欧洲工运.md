Title: Spano Reports on European Labor Movement in Tianjin
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 13th:

　　Spano
Reports on European Labor Movement in Tianjin

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 13th】Italian Communist Party Central Committee member and Italian Senator Spano was invited this afternoon to report to cadres of Tianjin municipal organizations on the situation of the labor movement in European capitalist countries. When he spoke of the growing strength and influence of the Italian working class and its party, the Italian Communist Party, the entire audience repeatedly responded with enthusiastic applause. When Spano spoke of the great victory of the Chinese revolution, he said: The Italian working class and the broad masses of the people thank you, thank the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people, because your work has made an important contribution to the cause of liberation of all mankind. As he concluded his report, the entire audience enthusiastically cheered: "Long live the solidarity of the Chinese and Italian people!"

### 斯巴诺在津报告欧洲工运

1949-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　斯巴诺
    在津报告欧洲工运
    【新华社天津十三日电】意共中央委员意大利参议员斯巴诺今日下午应邀向天津市各机关干部报告欧洲资本主义国家工人运动情况。当他说到意大利工人阶级及其政党意大利共产党的力量和影响日益强大时，全体听众不断地报以热烈鼓掌。斯巴诺论及中国革命的伟大胜利时，他说：意大利的工人阶级和广大人民感谢你们，感谢中国共产党和中国人民，因为你们的工作，对于全人类的解放事业有重要贡献。当他结束他的报告时，全场热烈欢呼：“中意人民团结万岁！”
