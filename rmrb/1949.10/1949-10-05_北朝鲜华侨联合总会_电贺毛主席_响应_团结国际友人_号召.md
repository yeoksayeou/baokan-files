Title: North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation Cables Congratulations to Chairman Mao, Responding to Call to "Unite International Friends"
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation
  　Cables Congratulations to Chairman Mao
  　Responding to Call to "Unite International Friends"
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th] The North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation and all Overseas Chinese in Pyongyang Special City sent a telegram to Chairman Mao on the 3rd to congratulate him. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China: We are delighted to hear about the birth of the long-awaited New Democratic People's Republic of China, and more than 40,000 Overseas Chinese in North Korea are all overjoyed. We celebrate the birth of the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government with boundless excitement, celebrating the Chinese people's march towards a new democratic road of democracy, prosperity, and independence. We respond to Chairman Mao's call to "unite international friends." Together with the Korean people, we will further unite around the Central Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, strive to complete and exceed Korea's two-year people's economic construction plan, and strive to achieve the complete liberation of the oppressed nations of the East and lasting world peace.
    　　　　　　North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation, all Overseas Chinese in Pyongyang Special City bowing together
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th] "Rodong Sinmun," the central organ newspaper of the Workers' Party, and "Democratic Korea," the organ newspaper of the Central Government of Korea, both published the news of Chairman Mao's election as Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China on the front page on the 2nd, along with a photo of Chairman Mao.

### 北朝鲜华侨联合总会　电贺毛主席　响应“团结国际友人”号召

1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜华侨联合总会
  　电贺毛主席
  　响应“团结国际友人”号召
    【新华社平壤四日电】北朝鲜华侨联合总会与平壤特别市全体华侨，于三日致电毛主席祝贺。电文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛主席：欣闻中国人民渴望已久的新民主主义的中华人民共和国诞生，北朝鲜四万多华侨莫不欢欣鼓舞。我们以无限兴奋的心情，庆祝中华人民共和国及中央人民政府的诞生，庆祝中国人民走向民主、富强、独立的新民主主义的道路。我们响应毛主席的号召，“团结国际友人”。我们和朝鲜人民一起，将进一步团结在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国中央政府的周围，为完成并超过朝鲜的两年人民经济建设计划而奋斗，为实现东方被压迫民族的完全解放和世界持久和平而奋斗。
    　　　　　　北朝鲜华侨联合总会、平壤特别市全体华侨同叩
    【新华社平壤四日电】此间劳动党中央机关报“劳动新闻”和朝鲜中央政府机关报“民主朝鲜”，于二日均以第一版头条地位刊载毛主席当选中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席的消息，并附以毛主席的照片。
