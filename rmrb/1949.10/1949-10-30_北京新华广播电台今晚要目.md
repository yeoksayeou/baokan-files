Title: Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-30
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Radio Station
    Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper reports] Beijing Xinhua Radio Station, 18:15, Workers' Program, introduces the workers' movement in Vietnam, one of the countries participating in the Asian and Australasian Trade Union Conference. 22:00, Second Art and Literature Program, a memorial program for the fourth anniversary of the passing of People's Musician Xian Xinghai. 19:00, Lecture on Natural Science Knowledge, written by Shen Keqi of the Physics Department of the Science Faculty of Peking University: "How Newton Discovered the Law of Universal Gravitation". 18:30, First Art and Literature Program, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Association: Li Xinghai sings the Leting Drum "Train Driver Zhang Fulin". 8:30 a.m., Sunday Art and Literature: "The Yellow River Cantata".
    Also, the second channel at 17:15, Children's Time, Mr. Sun Jingxiu continues to tell "The Story of Paul". 17:45, Youth Time, Li Yalin of the radio station's Male No. 2 Middle School broadcasting group speaks on "Running the Mess Hall Well to Serve the Students." 20:00, Voice of the Municipality broadcasts a report from the Beijing Municipal People's Government News Office on the inspection of private alcohol production by the city's Taxation Bureau.

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台　
    今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十八点十五分职工节目，介绍参加亚、澳州工会会议国家之一—越南的工人运动。二十二点第二次文艺节目，人民音乐家洗星海同志逝世四周年纪念节目。十九点自然科学常识讲座，北京大学理学院物理系沈克琦撰稿：“牛顿怎样发现万有引力定律”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：李兴海演唱乐亭大鼓“火车司机张福林”。上午八点三十分星期文艺：“黄河大合唱”。
    又第二台十七时十五分儿童时间，由孙敬修先生继续讲“保尔的故事”。十七时四十五分青年时间，由本台男二中广播小组李亚麟讲“办好伙食为同学服务”。二十时市政之声播送北京市人民政府新闻处关于本市税务局检查私酒工作的报导。
