Title: Czech Farmers' Central Conference Cables Congratulations on the Founding of New China and Cheers "Long Live Chairman Mao"
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 3

　　World Progressive Press Congratulates the Birth of New China

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: The Central Conference of Farmers of the Czechoslovak Republic cabled Chairman Mao on the 3rd, congratulating him, stating:

　　To Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　The highest organization of farmers in Czechoslovakia sends this telegram to congratulate the establishment of the People's Republic of China by the heroic laborers of China. This is a major event for the world, as the Chinese people, after being oppressed for a long time by international imperialists and domestic landlords, now control the power themselves. It is one of the greatest events of this century, along with the great October Revolution, and it strengthens the camp of peace, progress, democracy, and socialism. We wish the People's Republic of China prosperity and consolidation. Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the first chairman and liberator of the new People's China and a faithful follower of Lenin and Stalin.

　　　　　　　　　　Central Conference of Farmers of the Czechoslovak Republic
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman Bernatsky, Secretary-General Vlavga
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Prague, October 3, 1949.

### 捷克农民中央会议  电贺新中国成立欢呼毛主席万岁

1949-10-09
第3版()
专栏：世界进步报刊　祝贺新中国诞生

　　捷克农民中央会议
    电贺新中国成立欢呼毛主席万岁
    【新华社北京七日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国农民中央会议三日电贺毛主席称：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    捷克斯洛伐克农民的最高组织特电祝贺中国英勇劳动者人民共和国的建立。中国人民受了国际帝国主义者与国内地主们的长期压迫之后，现在已由自己来掌管政权，此为世界的大事件。它与伟大的十月革命同为本世纪中最大的事件，它加强了和平、进步、民主与社会主义的阵营。恭祝中华人民共和国的繁荣与巩固。新人民中国的第一任主席与解放者、列宁与斯大林的忠实信徒毛泽东同志万岁。
    　　　　　　　　　　捷克斯洛伐克共和国农民中央会议
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　会长贝尔纳次基　秘书长伏拉夫加
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月三日布拉格。
