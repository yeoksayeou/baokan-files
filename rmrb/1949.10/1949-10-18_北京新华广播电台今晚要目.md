Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-18
Page: 4

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station
  　Tonight's Highlights
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's workers' program at 18:15 this evening will introduce the labor movement in Burma, one of the member countries of the Asian and Australian Trade Union Conference. At 19:00, a lecture on natural science common knowledge, written by Zhang Zhisan of the Institute of Physics of the National Peking Academy: "The Moon". At 18:30, the first literary program, hosted by the China Quyi Improvement Association: Ji Lian Guan will perform Xihe Dagu "Zhao Xiangde Makes a Scene on the Zhengtai Railway". At 22:00, the second literary program will broadcast Indonesian songs.
    Also, Xinhua Channel 2 at 17:15, Women's Time: Dr. An of Peking University Hospital will talk about women's hygiene common knowledge "What kind of fetus is prone to dystocia". At 17:45, Youth Time: Broadcast "How Did Girls' High School No. 1 Elect the Student Union?" The author is Lou Jinghua, a student from the station's Girls' High School No. 1 broadcasting group. At 20:00, Voice of the Municipality: Broadcasts the Beijing Municipal People's Government's notice to levy the 1949 second half-year vehicle license tax from October 20th. At 20:15 Listener Service: Answers two questions from Li Zhaoxin and Gao Zeng regarding "Why do we learn from the Soviet Union?" and "Does the establishment of the People's Republic of China count as changing the dynasty?".

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分职工节目，介绍亚、澳洲职工会代表会议会员国之一——缅甸的职工运动。十九点自然科学常识讲座，国立北京研究院物理研究所张志三撰稿：“月亮”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：由祭连贯演唱西河大鼓“赵享德大闹正太路”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，播送印尼歌曲。
    又新华第二台十七时十五分，妇女时间：由北大医院安大夫讲妇女卫生常识“什么样的胎儿容易发生难产”。十七时四十五分，青年时间：播送“女一中是怎样选举学生会的？”作者是本台女一中广播小组娄静华同学。二十时市政之声：播送北京市人民政府决定从十月二十号起开征一九四九年下半年使用牌照税的通知。二十时十五分听众服务：回答李兆新，高增两位关于“我们为什么向苏联学习”和“中华人民共和国成立是不是也算改朝换代”两个问题。
