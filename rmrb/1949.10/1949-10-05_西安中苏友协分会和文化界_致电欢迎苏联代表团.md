Title: Xi'an Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branch and Cultural Circles Send Telegrams to Welcome Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 1

　　Xi'an Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branch and Cultural Circles
　　Send Telegrams to Welcome Soviet Delegation
　　[Broadcast by Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Xi'an, 4th. The preparatory committee of the Xi'an branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Xi'an cultural circles sent telegrams on October 1st to welcome the Soviet cultural workers delegation to the new Chinese capital, Beijing. The telegrams pointed out: The arrival of the Soviet cultural workers delegation symbolizes the further consolidation and development of the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. The socialist country, the Soviet Union, is the closest and most loyal friend of the Chinese people.

### 西安中苏友协分会和文化界 　致电欢迎苏联代表团

1949-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　西安中苏友协分会和文化界
  　致电欢迎苏联代表团
    【北京新华广播电台广播】西安四日消息：中苏友好协会西安分会筹委会和西安文化界，十月一号分别打电报欢迎苏联文化工作者代表团到达新中国的首都北京。电报中指出：苏联文化工作者代表团的到达，象征着中苏两国人民永恒友谊的进一步巩固和发展。社会主义国家苏联，是中国人民最亲密最忠诚的朋友。
