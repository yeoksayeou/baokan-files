Title: Notice from the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 2

　　Notice from the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    This command's stationed Beijing artillery unit will conduct live ammunition exercises at Lugouqiao (Marco Polo Bridge) training ground at 10:00 AM on the eighth of this month. Citizens are requested not to be alarmed upon hearing the sounds.
