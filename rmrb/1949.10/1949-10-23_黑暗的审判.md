Title: Dark Trial
Author: Ma Fanto
Date: 1949-10-23
Page: 5

　　Dark Trial
　　Ma Fanto
　　Radio loudspeakers curse and bark day and night,
　　Yellow newspapers deceive and intimidate from early morning,
　　Around the gloomy marble courthouse
　　Crowd four hundred police, cavalry, and plainclothes detectives.
　
　　Motorcycles roar and rampage at full speed,
　　Automatic rifles and grenade launchers are all loaded.
　　Is this still the America of Lincoln and Jefferson?
　　This is Hitler's Berlin, only missing the swastika armband!
　
　　The jurors are bank directors, millionaires, shipping tycoons,
　　Reactionaries who are anti-Soviet, anti-Communist, anti-Jewish, anti-Black, and anti-democratic!
　　The judge is a Wall Street lawyer with an annual income of $100,000,
　　The witnesses, a dozen or so, are either informants or secret agents!
　
　　Seated in the dock are eleven outstanding sons of the working people,
　　Their crimes are thought, writing, speech, and assembly.
　　Is this still a government of the people, by the people, for the people?
　　This is a complete replica of fascist Germany!
　
　　Today they try the Communist Party, tomorrow they suppress all the people,
　　The day after tomorrow they will burn books, burn people, concentration camps, wars of aggression,
　　Fascist Germany, Italy, Japan, and Spain walked ahead,
　　—Into the grave! Now you, America, are following closely behind!
　
　　People all over the world point at your face and spit in scorn!
　　People all over the world have issued angry protests!
　　Look, the criminals are arresting the innocent!
　　Look, the rotting corpse is judging the healthy life!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 21st
　　(Note) When the U.S. Federal Court tried the leaders of the American Communist Party earlier this year, the courthouse was surrounded by more than four hundred police and cavalry. Judge Medina's income as a private lawyer last year reached $100,000.

### 黑暗的审判
马凡陀
1949-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　黑暗的审判
    马凡陀
    无线电喇叭日夜不停地诅咒，狂吠，
    黄色报纸一清早起就欺骗，恐吓，
    阴森森的大理石的法院周围
    密布了四百个警察，骑兵和便衣侦探。
　
    机器脚踏车开足速率横冲直撞，
    自动步枪，榴弹枪，一律上膛。
    这还算是林肯、杰弗逊的美国？
    这是希特勒的柏林，只差＠字臂章！
　
    陪审官是银行董事，百万富翁，轮船老板，
    反苏反共反犹太反黑人反民主的反动派！
    法官是一年十万元收入的华尔街律师，
    证人十几个，不是告密者便是暗探！
　
    被告席上坐着十一个劳动人民的优秀儿子，
    他们的罪状是思想、著作、演说、集会。
    这还算是民有、民治、民享的国家？
    这是十足的法西斯德国的翻版！
　
    今天审判共产党，明天镇压全体人民，
    后天烧书，烧人，集中营，侵略战争，
    法西斯的德、意、日、西班牙走在前面，
    ——走进了坟墓！现在你美国在后面紧跟！
　
    全世界人民指着你的脸唾骂！
    全世界人民发出了愤怒的抗议！
    看，犯罪的人逮捕无罪的人！
    看，腐臭的尸体审判健旺的生命！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十月二十一日
    （注）今年初美国联邦法院审讯美共领袖时，法院周围满布了四百多名警察和骑兵。麦迪纳法官去年一年在当私人律师方面的收入达十万美金。
