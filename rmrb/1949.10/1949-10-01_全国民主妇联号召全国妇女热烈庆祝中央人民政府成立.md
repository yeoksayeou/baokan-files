Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Calls on Women Nationwide to Warmly Celebrate the Founding of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation issued a notice on the 30th, calling on women nationwide to warmly celebrate the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the notice is as follows: Women's Federations at all levels and sisters across the country:

　　We call on you to warmly support the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government produced by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, to support the Common Program adopted by the conference, and to strive for its complete realization. Under the leadership of this government, we women will be liberated, enjoy equal rights between men and women, and thus be able to effectively contribute our strength to participate in all construction undertakings of the new China. We task the local women's federations to actively mobilize and organize the broad masses of women to participate in local celebration meetings and various celebration activities, and to widely publicize the various resolutions of the conference, in order to express our women's love and support for the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government.

### 全国民主妇联号召全国妇女热烈庆祝中央人民政府成立

1949-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联号召全国妇女热烈庆祝中央人民政府成立
    【新华社北京三十日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会，特于三十日发出通知，号召全国妇女热烈庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立。通知全文如下：各地妇联会暨全国姊妹们：
    我们号召你们，热烈拥护中国人民政治协商会议所产生的中华人民共和国及中央人民政府，拥护大会所通过的共同纲领，并为其彻底实现而奋斗。我们妇女将在这个政府的领导下，得到解放，享受男女平等的权利，并因而能够有效地贡献我们的力量，来参加新中国的一切建设事业。我们责成各地妇联积极动员组织广大妇女群众参加各地的庆祝大会及各种庆祝活动，并广泛地宣传大会的各种决议，以表现我们妇女对于中华人民共和国及中央人民政府的热爱与拥护。
