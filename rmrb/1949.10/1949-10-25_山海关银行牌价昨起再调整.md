Title: Shanhaiguan Bank Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-10-25
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Bank Exchange Rate
　　Adjusted Again Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan announced that, starting yesterday (the 24th), the exchange rate will be adjusted to 1 RMB equaling 55 Northeast currency.

### 山海关银行牌价昨起再调整

1949-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关银行牌价
  　昨起再调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自昨（二十四日）日起牌价改为人民币一元兑东北币五十五元。
