Title: Notice from the Headquarters of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-06
Page: 1

Notice from the Headquarters of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

    This headquarters' stationed artillery unit in Beijing is scheduled to conduct live ammunition exercises at Lugouqiao (Marco Polo Bridge) firing range at 10:00 AM on the 7th of this month. Citizens are requested not to be alarmed upon hearing the sounds.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 6, 1949
