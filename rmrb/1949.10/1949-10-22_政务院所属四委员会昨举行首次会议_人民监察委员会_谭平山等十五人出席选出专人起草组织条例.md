Title: The Four Committees Under the Government Administration Council Held Their First Meeting Yesterday: People's Supervision Committee - Tan Pingshan and Fifteen Others Attended, Selected Specialists to Draft Organizational Regulations
Author:
Date: 1949-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st - The People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its inaugural meeting at 10:00 AM today. Attendees included Director Tan Pingshan, Deputy Director Pan Zhenya, and committee members Zhang Xiuyan, Zhang Muyao, Zhu Yunshan, Han Zhao'e, Zhang Nanxian, He ＠shi, Ning Wu, Guo Renzhi, An Ziwen, Xu Liqun, Liu Dachao, Shuai Mengqi, Xiao Ming, totaling fifteen people. Director Tan Pingshan first announced: According to the list of candidates for the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government approved by the Third Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government is formally established. In accordance with the spirit of New Democracy and the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the people's supervision system will be established and the people's supervision work will be carried out. The meeting discussed the principles for establishing the organizational structure of the People's Supervision Committee and the central tasks for the future. The meeting elected Tan Pingshan, Pan Zhenya, Zhang Muyao, An Ziwen, and Guo Renzhi to form the drafting committee for the organizational regulations of the People's Supervision Committee.

### 政务院所属四委员会昨举行首次会议　人民监察委员会  谭平山等十五人出席选出专人起草组织条例

1949-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院所属四委员会昨举行首次会议
  　人民监察委员会
    谭平山等十五人出席选出专人起草组织条例
    【新华社北京二十一日电】中央人民政府政务院人民监察委员会今日上午十时举行成立会，出席者有主任谭平山、副主任潘震亚，委员张秀岩、张慕尧、朱蕴山、韩兆鹗、张难先、何＠时、宁武、郭任之、安子文、许立群、刘达潮、帅孟奇、萧明等共十五人。首由谭平山主任宣布：根据中央人民政府委员会第三次会议通过的政务院人民监察委员会人选，正式成立中央人民政府政务院人民监察委员会，并根据新民主主义精神和人民政协共同纲领，开始建立人民监察制度，执行人民的监察工作。会议讨论了关于建立人民监察委员会的组织机构的原则和今后的中心工作等问题。会议推选谭平山、潘震亚、张慕尧、安子文、郭任之五人组成人民监察委员会组织条例起草委员会。
