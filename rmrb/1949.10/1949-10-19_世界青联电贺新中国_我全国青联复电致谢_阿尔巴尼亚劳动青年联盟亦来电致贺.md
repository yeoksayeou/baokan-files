Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Sends Congratulations to New China; All-China Youth Federation Replies with Thanks; Albanian Labor Youth Union Also Sends Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: The All-China Youth Federation received a telegram today from the World Federation of Democratic Youth congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The All-China Youth Federation has replied with a telegram of thanks. The two telegrams are as follows:

(1) Telegram from the World Federation of Democratic Youth to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation
	As you, under the leadership of Mao Zedong, establish the People's Republic of China and the Central People's Government, we extend our warm congratulations to you.
	The establishment of the People's Republic of China is the result of the Chinese people's historic victory over the Kuomintang fascist bandits, the running dogs of American and British imperialism.
	We wish the Chinese people and youth complete success in the cause of building a free, new-democratic China.

	Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth		October 15th

(2) Reply Telegram from the All-China Democratic Youth Federation to the World Federation of Democratic Youth
	The Chinese youth sincerely thank you for your congratulations.
	The establishment of the People's Republic of China is a victory for the Chinese people and also a victory for the people of the world.
	We vow to unite even more closely with the democratic youth of the world around the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and to struggle for lasting peace, people's democracy, national independence, and a bright future for youth.

	Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation		October 18th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: The Albanian Labor Youth Union sent a telegram to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation congratulating the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

	As the Chinese people, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Mao Zedong, establish the People's Republic of China, we, in the name of the Albanian Labor Youth Union, extend our warm congratulations to all the democratic youth of China.
	The establishment of the People's Republic of China is not only a victory for the Chinese people but also a victory for the world peace camp headed by the great Soviet Union.
	The struggle of the Chinese youth to completely eliminate the reactionaries, build a new-democratic China, and lay the foundation for socialism will surely be successful.
	Long live the friendship between the Chinese youth and the Albanian youth!

	Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth Union		October 8th

### 世界青联电贺新中国　我全国青联复电致谢  阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟亦来电致贺

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联电贺新中国
  　我全国青联复电致谢
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟亦来电致贺
    【新华社北京十八日电】中华全国青年联合总会本日收到世界民主青年联盟祝贺中华人民共和国成立的电报，我全国青年联合会已复电致谢。两电如下：
    （一）世界民主青年联盟给中华全国民主青年联合总会的贺电
    正当你们在毛泽东领导下，建立中华人民共和国和中央人民政府的时候，我们热烈地向你们祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的建立是中国人民对美英帝国主义的走狗蒋介石法西斯党徒取得历史性胜利的结果。
    恭祝中国人民和青年在建设新民主主义的自由中国的事业中获得圆满的成功。
    世界民主青年联盟秘书处　　　　　　　　　　　　十月十五日
    （二）中华全国民主青年联合总会给世界民主青年联盟的复电
    中国青年衷心地感谢你们的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的建立是中国人民的胜利，也是全世界人民的胜利。
    我们誓愿与全世界民主青年一起，更加紧密地团结在世界民主青年联盟的周围，为持久和平人民民主，民族独立与青年的美好将来而奋斗。
    中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书处　　　　　　　　十月十八日
    【新华社北京十八日电】阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟致电中华全国民主青年总会祝贺中华人民共和国成立，电文如下：
    正当中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东的英明领导下，建立中华人民共和国的时候，我们以阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟的名义，向中国全体民主青年热烈地祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的成立，不仅是中国人民的胜利，而且是以伟大的苏联为首的世界和平阵营的胜利。
    中国青年为彻底消灭反动派，建设新民主主义的中国并为社会主义奠下基础而进行的斗争，一定会获得成功。
    中国青年和阿尔巴尼亚青年的友谊万岁！
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会　　　　　　　十月八日
