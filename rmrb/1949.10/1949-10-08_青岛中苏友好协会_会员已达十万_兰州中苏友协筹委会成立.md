Title: Qingdao Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Membership Reaches 100,000; Lanzhou Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Preparatory Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 7th. The Qingdao branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was formally established on the 2nd of this month. Since the Qingdao cultural and educational circles initiated the establishment of a preparatory committee on the 18th of last month, 100,000 people have joined the association. At the first membership representative meeting held on the 2nd, 749 representatives attended. At the meeting, 39 executive committee members, including Xiang Ming, were elected, formally establishing the Qingdao branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and telegrams were sent to Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao Zedong to express respect, vowing to strengthen the profound friendship between China and the Soviet Union and to fight for the defense of lasting world peace.
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 6th. The preparatory committee of the Lanzhou branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was established at a general meeting of 54 initiators held on the 3rd. During the meeting, upon hearing that the Soviet government had announced the severance of diplomatic relations with the Kuomintang reactionary government and formally recognized the People's Republic of China, the entire venue immediately burst into warm applause. At the meeting, the provisional articles of association of the Lanzhou branch preparatory committee were adopted, and 21 people were elected as preparatory committee members, with Gan Siqi as the director of the preparatory committee and Zhang Desheng, Xu Hefu, and Wang Shitai as deputy directors. In addition, seven people were elected to form an executive council, with Zhao Dingzheng as the director-general, in charge of daily affairs.

### 青岛中苏友好协会　会员已达十万　兰州中苏友协筹委会成立

1949-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　青岛中苏友好协会
    会员已达十万
    兰州中苏友协筹委会成立
    【新华社济南七日电】中苏友好协会青岛分会已于本月二日正式成立。该会自上月十八日由青岛文化教育界发起成立筹委会以来，已有十万人入会。在二日召开的首届会员代表大会上到会代表七百四十九名。会上选出向明等三十九人为执行委员，正式成立中苏友好协会青岛分会，并分别致电斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席致敬，誓愿加强中苏两国深厚的友谊，为保卫世界持久和平而奋斗。
    【新华社兰州六日电】中苏友好协会兰州市分会筹备会在三日举行的五十四位发起人的全体会议上成立。会议进行中，闻悉苏联政府宣告与国民党反动政府断绝外交关系，正式承认中华人民共和国，全场当即热烈鼓掌欢迎。会上，通过兰州分会筹备会临时简章，并选出二十一人为筹备委员，甘泗淇任筹备会主任，张德生、徐褐夫、王世泰等三人为副主任。另选出七人组成干事会，由赵定政任总干事，主持日常事务。
