The National Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Twelve Other Organizations Initiate Preparations in the Capital to Commemorate Lu Xun
Commemoration Meeting Scheduled for Tomorrow, Inviting Guo Moruo, Xu Guangping, and Others to Speak

[Reported by this newspaper] The Capital Commemoration of the 13th Anniversary of Lu Xun's Death Preparatory Committee has been formally established. This committee was jointly initiated and prepared by twelve organizations: the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Women's Federation, the Beijing Municipal Cultural Affairs Committee, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Youth Federation, the Municipal Students' Federation, the Municipal Women's Federation, Primary and Secondary School Teachers, and the Joint Association of Faculty and Staff of Colleges and Universities. Yesterday at 2:00 PM, a preparatory meeting was held at the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles, attended by Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Zhou Yang, and representatives from various units, totaling more than twenty people. At the meeting, it was decided to hold a commemoration meeting for the 13th anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's death on the morning of the 19th. In addition to inviting Guo Moruo, Xu Guangping, and others to attend and give speeches, the commemoration meeting will also include recitations of excerpts from Lu Xun's "The True Story of Ah Q" and "Autumn Night" and "Kites" from *Wild Grass*. That evening, the Xinhua Broadcasting Station plans to broadcast an introduction to Lu Xun's life and add new programs featuring Lu Xun's works to the Quyi (Chinese folk art forms) program. Commemorations in factories and schools in the capital will be carried out separately by each unit.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

[Reported by this newspaper] To commemorate Mr. Lu Xun, faculty and students of the Beijing Normal University held a "Commemoration Evening" in the auditorium of the College of Liberal Arts at 7:00 PM on the 17th, and invited Xu Guangping, Feng Xuefeng, and others to attend and give speeches, recounting Mr. Lu Xun's life and his fighting stories.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Normal University Correspondent Group)

[Reported by this newspaper] Tomorrow (the 19th) is the anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's death. To enable students to have a deeper understanding of the significance of commemorating this great mentor, the Peking University Student Union has decided to entrust the Peking University New Literature and Art Society and the Chinese Department to jointly hold a commemoration evening at 7:00 PM that evening in the North Building Auditorium. Figures from the literary and art circles both inside and outside the university will be invited to attend and give speeches, and there will be recitation programs by the two units. The New Literature and Art Society and the Chinese Department will also publish a special commemorative issue of Mr. Lu Xun, which will provide a detailed introduction to Mr. Lu Xun's life and thought.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Peking University Correspondent Group)

### 全国文联等十二个团体发起　首都筹备纪念鲁迅  定于明日举行纪念大会邀郭沫若许广平等讲演

1949-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国文联等十二个团体发起
  　首都筹备纪念鲁迅
    定于明日举行纪念大会邀郭沫若许广平等讲演
    【本报讯】首都纪念鲁迅逝世十三周年纪念筹备会已正式成立。该会由全国文联、全国总工会、全国青联、全国学联、全国妇联、北京市文委、市工会、市青联、市学联、市妇联、中小教师、院校教职联等十二个团体共同发起筹备。昨日下午二时在全国文联召开筹备会，到全国文联副主席茅盾、周扬及各单位代表共二十余人。会上决定于十九日上午举行鲁迅先生逝世十三周年纪念大会，除请郭沫若、许广平等出席讲演外，纪念会中并将朗诵鲁迅作品“阿Ｑ正传”一段及野草中的“秋夜”、“风筝”等。当晚拟由新华广播电台广播介绍鲁迅的生平，及在曲艺节目中增播鲁迅著作的新节目。首都各工厂、学校的纪念，则将由各单位分别进行。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
    【本报讯】师大师生为了纪念鲁迅先生，由文学院师生于十七日晚七时在文学院礼堂举办“纪念晚会”，并请许广平、冯雪峰等出席讲演，讲述鲁迅先生的生前事迹及战斗故事。　　　　　　　（师大通讯组）
    【本报讯】明（十九）日是鲁迅先生的忌辰，为使同学们对纪念这位伟大导师的意义，有更深刻认识，北大学生会已决定委托北大新文艺社和中文系在当晚七时联合举办纪念晚会，地点在北楼礼堂。将请校外校内文艺界人士出席讲演，并有两单位的朗颂节目。新文艺社和中文系并出鲁迅先生纪念特刊，对鲁迅先生的生平及思想将有详细的介绍。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（北大通讯组）
