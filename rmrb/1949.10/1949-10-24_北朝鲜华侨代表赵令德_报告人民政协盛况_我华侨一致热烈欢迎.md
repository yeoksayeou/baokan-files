Title: North Korean Overseas Chinese Representative Zhao Lingde Reports on the Grand Occasion of the CPPCC; Overseas Chinese Unanimously Offer Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1949-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd – Zhao Lingde, Chairman of the North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation, who represented over 40,000 overseas Chinese in North Korea at the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), returned here from Beijing on the 15th of this month and has been warmly welcomed by overseas Chinese compatriots in recent days. He recently attended three welcome meetings hosted by overseas Chinese, where he reported in detail on the achievements of the CPPCC and the concern and expectations of the Central People's Government of the motherland for overseas Chinese. Everyone who heard the report was extremely excited. The Overseas Chinese General Association is scheduled to convene a conference of overseas Chinese representatives from various provinces in North Korea in Pyongyang on the 28th, where Zhao Lingde will report on the grand occasion of the CPPCC.

### 北朝鲜华侨代表赵令德　报告人民政协盛况　我华侨一致热烈欢迎

1949-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜华侨代表赵令德
  　报告人民政协盛况
  　我华侨一致热烈欢迎
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】代表北朝鲜四万多华侨出席中国人民政协第一届全体会议的北朝鲜华侨联合总会委员长赵令德，于本月十五日由北京返抵此间后，连日受到侨胞的热烈欢迎。他最近曾出席华侨主持的三次欢迎会，向大家详尽地报告了人民政协的成就以及祖国中央人民政府对海外侨胞的关切和期望。所有听了报告的人，无不兴奋异常。华侨总会定于二十八日在平壤召开北朝鲜各道华侨代表会议，由赵令德报告人民政协的盛况。
