Title: Ningxia Mongolian Alxa Banner Government Cables Chairman Mao to Congratulate the Birth of New China and Sincerely Supports the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ningxia, 13th. According to a delayed dispatch from Dingyuanying: The Ningxia Mongolian Alxa Banner government sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, congratulating the establishment of the Central People's Government, and expressing its sincere support. The original text is as follows: To Chairman Mao and all Vice Chairmen:
    We are immensely rejoiced to hear of the birth of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the Central People's Government in the distant border region. We hereby send a telegram to congratulate and express our sincere support. Please take note.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Alxa Banner Government, October 1st

### 宁夏蒙古族阿拉善旗政府  电毛主席贺新中国诞生竭诚拥护中央人民政府

1949-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　宁夏蒙古族阿拉善旗政府
    电毛主席贺新中国诞生竭诚拥护中央人民政府
    【新华社宁夏十三日电】据定远营迟到消息：宁夏蒙古族阿拉善旗政府致电毛主席，庆贺中央人民政府成立，并表示竭诚拥护。原文如下：毛主席暨各位副主席钧鉴：
    我们在遥远的边疆听到中华人民共和国的诞生和中央人民政府的成立，不胜庆幸，谨电庆贺，并申竭诚拥护之忱，恭请鉴察。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　阿拉善旗政府　十月一日
