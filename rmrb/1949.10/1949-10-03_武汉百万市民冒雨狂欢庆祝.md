Title: Wuhan Million Citizens Brave Rain to Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1949-10-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 2nd: Yesterday, a million citizens of Wuhan braved the drizzling rain to celebrate the grand opening ceremony. The five-star national flag fluttered everywhere, and tens of thousands of citizens poured into the streets, forming a huge current. The national anthem "Arise, ye who refuse to be slaves!" the slogan "Advance under the banner of Mao Zedong," the sound of gongs and drums, military music, and laughter resounded throughout the city. Workers said: The establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is the result of the Chinese Communist Party, the working class, and the people of the whole country leading the revolution for decades. Young students are proud that they are a happy generation. They enthusiastically shouted: "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Support the Central People's Government!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" At night, the whole city of Wuhan was brightly lit. On the majestic Jianghan Customs Building, the bright national flag was set off by countless colorful lights, and the huge golden characters "Congratulations on the Birth of a New Democratic China" shone brightly. The water tower of the Water and Electricity Company was decorated like a mountain of lights. From 6 p.m., in Hankou, thousands of workers, students, corps, and organs, and countless Yangko teams, singing teams, dragon lanterns, and land boats gathered in the square in front of the Jianghan Customs Building, forming a procession of twenty miles long for a grand parade. The parade was led by ten large red national flags, and along Jianghan Road, Shengli Street, and Lihuangpi Road, it turned to Zhongshan Avenue, mighty and magnificent, with people like surging tides. Songs and slogans resounded through the sky. The parade did not disperse until more than 10 o'clock in the evening. In addition to Hankou, there were also grand celebrations in Wuchang, Hanyang, and Luojia Mountain.

### 武汉百万市民冒雨狂欢庆祝

1949-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　武汉百万市民冒雨狂欢庆祝
    【新华社汉口二日电】武汉百万市民昨日竟冒蒙蒙细雨狂欢庆祝开国盛典。五星国旗到处招展，成千成万的市民涌向街头，汇成巨流。“起来！不愿作奴隶的人们！”的国歌声、“在毛泽东旗帜下前进”的口号声、锣鼓声、军乐声、欢笑声响遍全市。工人们说：中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立了，这是中国共产党、工人阶级和全国人民几十年来领导革命的成果。青年学生们则为他们是幸福的一代而感到骄傲。他们狂热地欢呼：“中华人民共和国万岁！”“拥护中央人民政府！”“毛主席万岁！”入夜，武汉全市灯火辉煌。巍峨的江汉关大楼上，鲜艳的国旗陪衬着无数五彩灯光辉煌夺目，“贺民主新中国诞生”的斗大金字闪烁发光。水电公司的大水塔装扮得象一座灯山一样。下午六时起，在汉口万千工人、学生、队部、机关的队伍，和无数的秧歌队、歌咏队、龙、灯旱船等在江汉关大楼前的广场上会合了，会合成二十里长行列举行了盛大的游行。游行队伍以十面大红国旗为前导，沿江汉路、胜利街、黎黄陂路折向中山大道，浩浩荡荡，人如涌潮。歌声口号，响彻云霄。游行直到晚上十点多钟才散。除汉口外，在武昌、汉阳和珞珈山也有盛大的庆祝集会。
