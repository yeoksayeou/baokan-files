Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-10-29
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
　　Adjusted Yesterday
　　[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China, Northeast Bank, has changed its exchange rate starting yesterday (the 28th) to one yuan of Renminbi equaling fifty yuan of Northeast currency.

### 山海关货币牌价昨起调整

1949-10-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价　
    昨起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行，东北银行山海关联合办事处自昨（二十八）日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券五十元。
