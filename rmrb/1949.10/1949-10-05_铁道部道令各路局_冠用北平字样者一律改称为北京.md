Title: Ministry of Railways Orders All Bureaus: All Instances of "Beiping" to be Changed to "Beijing"
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-05
Page: 2

　　Ministry of Railways Issues Order to All Bureaus
　　All Instances of "Beiping" to be Changed to "Beijing"
　　[Report from Our Newspaper] Beiping has already been renamed Beijing by the decision of the First Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. In order to unify railway names, the Ministry of Railways issued an order on September 30th to all relevant railway bureaus nationwide, stating that all instances of the name "Beiping" should be changed to "Beijing," and all instances of the character "Ping" should be changed to "Jing." This change is to be implemented from the date of receipt of the telegram. The Jing-Hu line of the Southern Bureau will be changed to the Hu-Ning line, and the Jing-Wu line will be changed to the Ning-Wu line. All units and trains under the Southern Bureau that use the character "Jing" will be changed to "Ning." The name of Nanjing Railway Station and the name "Nanjing" will remain unchanged. (People's Railway Daily)

### 铁道部道令各路局　冠用北平字样者一律改称为北京
人民铁道报社
1949-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　铁道部道令各路局
  　冠用北平字样者一律改称为北京
    【本报讯】北平业经人民政治协商会议第一届会议通过决定改为北京。铁道部为了统一铁路名称起见，特于九月三十日通令全国有关各铁路局，凡冠用北平字样者一律改为北京，或用平字者，一律改用京字。并限于电到之日起实行。南方局京沪线改为沪宁线，京芜线改为宁芜线，南方局属各单位及列车冠用京字者一律改用宁字，南京车站及南京字样仍照旧。（人民铁道报社）
