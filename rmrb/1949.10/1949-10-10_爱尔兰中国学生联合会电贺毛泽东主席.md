Title: Irish Chinese Student Union Sends Congratulations to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1949-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th:

    Irish Chinese Student Union
    Sends Congratulations to Chairman Mao
    The Central Federation of Chinese Students in Great Britain and Ireland cabled Chairman Mao: To Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Congratulations on the people's liberation and the establishment of the Central Government.
    　　　　　　Central Federation of Chinese Students in Great Britain and Ireland
