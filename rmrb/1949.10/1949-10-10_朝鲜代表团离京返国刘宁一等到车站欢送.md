Title: Korean Delegation Departs Beijing for Home; Liu Ningyi and Others See Them Off at the Station
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th—The Korean delegation, which came to our capital to participate in the China Conference for the Defense of World Peace, departed Beijing for home at 9:00 a.m. today. Liu Ningyi, Qian Junrui, and Lai Yali, members of the China Peace Committee, and Liao Mengxing, representative of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, went to the station to see them off. Before her departure, the head of the delegation, Park Jung-ai, expressed her excellent impression of the People's Republic of China and her deep gratitude for the warm reception given to her by the Chinese people.

### 朝鲜代表团离京返国刘宁一等到车站欢送

1949-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜代表团离京返国刘宁一等到车站欢送
    【新华社北京九日电】来我国首都参加中国保卫世界和平大会的朝鲜代表团，已于今日上午九时离京返国。中国保卫世界和平委员会委员刘宁一、钱俊瑞、赖亚力及中华全国民主妇女联合会代表廖梦醒等均到车站欢送。代表团团长朴正爱行前表示她对于中华人民共和国有极良好的印象，并对中国人民给予她的热情招待深为感谢。
