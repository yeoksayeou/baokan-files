Title: Northeast People's Government Commercial Delegation to Korea Hosts Banquet for Korean Party and Government Leaders; Soviet Ambassador to Korea Invited to Attend; Northeast Commercial Delegation to Korea and Xinhua News Agency Correspondent Office in Korea Also Hold Celebrations

Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3

Celebrating the Birth of New China and the Establishment of the Central People's Government

　　Northeast People's Government Commercial Delegation to Korea
　　Hosts Banquet for Korean Party and Government Leaders
　　Soviet Ambassador to Korea Invited to Attend
　　Northeast Commercial Delegation to Korea and Xinhua News Agency Correspondent Office in Korea Also Hold Celebrations
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th: The Northeast People's Government Commercial Delegation to Korea, in celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 3rd at the Pyongyang Railway Hotel for leaders of Korean party and government organs and the Soviet Embassy in Korea. Korean Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Kim Du-bong, Vice-Premier Hong Myong-hui, Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Park Heon-yeong, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the People's Army Choe Yong-geon, Minister of Internal Affairs Park Il-u, Minister of Inspection Kim Won-bong, Minister of Commerce Jang Si-u, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Jeong Jun-taek, Minister of Urban Management Li Yong, Minister without Portfolio Li Ke-lu and other key Korean government personnel, as well as Soviet Ambassador to Korea Sidorov and Minister Deng Jin, were invited to attend. During the banquet, Premier Kim Il-sung and Ambassador Sidorov first rose to propose a toast to the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, to Chairman Mao's election as Chairman of the Central People's Government, and to his health. Subsequently, attendees successively proposed toasts to Marshal Stalin, Premier Kim Il-sung, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, and to the close unity of the Soviet, Chinese, and Korean peoples. When the attendees heard the news that Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko had announced the Soviet Union's willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China, enthusiastic applause resounded for a long time.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 5th: The Northeast Commercial Delegation to Korea and the Xinhua News Agency Correspondent Office in Korea held a meeting on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the birth of the People's Republic of China and the establishment of the Central People's Government. Korean musicians Zheng Lücheng, Park Young-geun, and artist Zhang Zhenguang, along with more than twenty other Korean friends, were also invited to attend. Wen Shizhen, Chief Representative of the Commercial Delegation, called on all staff to be modest and prudent, and to sincerely cooperate with Korean friends. Korean friends Zhang Zhenguang and Park Young-geun, in their subsequent speeches, unanimously congratulated the birth of the People's Republic of China, and emphasized that the people of China and Korea should unite to oppose the current common enemy, the aggressive plans of the American imperialists.

### 东北人民政府驻朝鲜商业代表团  欢宴朝鲜党政首长　苏驻朝鲜大使应邀参加  东北驻朝鲜商业代表团、新华社驻朝鲜特派员办事处亦集会庆祝

1949-10-08

专栏：庆祝新中国诞生和中央人民政府成立

　　庆祝新中国诞生和中央人民政府成立
    东北人民政府驻朝鲜商业代表团
    欢宴朝鲜党政首长
    苏驻朝鲜大使应邀参加
    东北驻朝鲜商业代表团、新华社驻朝鲜特派员办事处亦集会庆祝
    【新华社北京五日电】东北人民政府驻朝鲜商业代表团，为庆祝中华人民共和国和中央人民政府的成立，特于三日晚在平壤铁道宾馆欢宴朝鲜党政机关和苏联驻朝鲜大使馆首长。朝鲜内阁总理金日成、最高人民会议主席金＠奉、副总理洪命熹、副总理兼外交部长朴宪永、国防部长兼人民军总司令崔庸健、内务部长朴一禹、检阅部长金元凤、商业部长张时雨、国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽、都市经营部长李庸、不管部长李克鲁等朝鲜政府首要人员和苏联驻朝鲜大使西地科夫、公使邓金等均应邀与会。席间金日成总理和西地科夫大使首先起立为中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立、为毛主席当选为中央人民政府主席和他的身体健康举杯祝贺。接着与会者纷纷提议为斯大林大元帅、金日成总理、朱德总司令等的健康以及苏联、中国、朝鲜三大民族的亲密团结而干杯。当与会者听到苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯宣布苏联愿与中华人民共和国建立外交关系的消息时，热烈的掌声更是经久不息。
    【新华社平壤五日电】东北驻朝鲜商业代表团和新华社驻朝鲜特派员办事处于一日晚开会庆祝中华人民共和国的诞生与中央人民政府的成立。朝鲜音乐家郑律成、朴荣根及美术家张振光等二十多位朝鲜友人亦应邀参加。商业代表团首席代表文士桢在会上讲话时号召全体工作人员谦虚谨慎，老老实实与朝鲜朋友团结合作。朝鲜朋友张振光、朴荣根相继讲话时一致祝贺中华人民共和国的诞生，并强调中朝两国人民团结起来，反对当前的共同敌人美帝国主义者的侵略计划。
