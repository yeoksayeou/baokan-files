Title: Ode to the Red Lantern
Author: Niu Xiaobai
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 6

　　Ode to the Red Lantern
　　Niu Xiaobai
　　Wound with golden thread, bound with silver thread,
　　The big red lantern shade is adorned with gold foil flowers.
　　Why are the red lanterns pasted so well?
　　To be hung high above Tiananmen.

　　Red lanterns high, the sky red,
　　People come to see from all directions.
　　Shining near, shining far,
　　Shining on the four hundred million people.

　　Red lantern red, emitting light,
　　Like the Big Dipper in the sky.
　　Unafraid of rain, unafraid of wind,
　　New China has hung up the emancipation lantern.

　　Which red lantern is the brightest?
　　A red lantern hangs in the center,
　　Illuminating the image of our leader.
　　Chairman Mao—you are redder than the red lantern!
　　Chairman Mao—you are brighter than the red lantern!
　　　　October 3, 1949

### 红灯颂
牛小白
1949-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　红灯颂
    牛小白
    金线缠，银线扎，
    大红的灯罩贴金花。
    红灯糊好为什嘛？
    天安门上高高挂。
　
    红灯高、红满天，
    四面八方齐来看。
    又照近、又照远，
    照耀人民四万万。
　
    红灯红、放光明，
    好比天上的北斗星。
    不怕雨、不怕风，
    新中国挂上了翻身灯。
　
    红灯最属那个亮？
    一盏红灯挂中央，
    照见了咱们的领袖像。
    毛主席—你比红灯红！
    毛主席—你比红灯亮！
    　　四九、十、三
