Title: Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation Held a Discussion Yesterday with Cultural and Educational Workers

Author:
Date: 1949-10-10
Page: 2

　　Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation
    Held a Discussion Yesterday with Cultural and Educational Workers

    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation held separate discussions with Chinese educational workers and cultural workers in Beijing yesterday afternoon. Delegation member and Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic (RSFSR), Dubrovinina, held a discussion with educational workers from Beijing. The Deputy Minister of the Far East Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Markov, and the Deputy Minister of the Records Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Tsikarev, held discussions with Xiao San, Sha Kefu, Ding Zan, Hong Shen, Feng Naichao, Ye Qianyu, and others from Chinese cultural circles. In the evening, the delegation's artistic performance team performed programs to entertain literary and artistic circles, students, and educational workers in Beijing.

### 苏文化艺术科学工作者代表团　昨与文教工作者座谈

1949-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏文化艺术科学工作者代表团
    昨与文教工作者座谈
    【本报讯】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团，昨日下午在北京分别和中国的教育工作者及文化工作者举行座谈。代表团团员、苏俄教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜和北京的教育工作者举行座谈会。苏联对外文化协会远东部副部长马尔柯夫、苏联对外文化协会记录部副部长齐卡列夫则与中国文化界萧三、沙可夫、丁瓒、洪深、冯乃超、叶浅予等座谈。晚间代表团艺术演出队，表演节目，招待北京的文艺界、学生及教育工作者。
