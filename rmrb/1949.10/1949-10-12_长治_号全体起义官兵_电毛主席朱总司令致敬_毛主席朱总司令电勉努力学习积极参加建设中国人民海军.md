Title: "Changzhi" Ship Uprising Officers and Soldiers Send a Respectful Telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu / Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send a Telegram Encouraging Diligent Study and Active Participation in Building the Chinese People's Navy
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 11th:

    "Changzhi" Ship Uprising Officers and Soldiers
    Send a Respectful Telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
    Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send a Telegram Encouraging Diligent Study and Active Participation in Building the Chinese People's Navy

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 11th: All the uprising personnel of the former Kuomintang Navy's First Fleet flagship "Changzhi," who revolted outside Wusongkou on September 19th, sent a respectful telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on September 27th. The original text of the telegram is as follows: Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu:
    We, dissatisfied with the Kuomintang reactionaries colluding with imperialism and selling out the motherland, opposing democracy and harming the people, as well as "blockading" the coast and carrying out the piratical crimes of murder and robbery, revolted outside Wusongkou on the morning of September 19th, broke away from the rule of the Kuomintang reactionary remnants, and joined the People's Navy. We firmly support the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, and under your wise leadership, we will intensify our studies, transform ourselves, participate in the construction of the People's Navy, quickly wipe out the remaining bandits, and strive to perform meritorious service for the people in the battles to liberate Taiwan and liberate all of China. Hereby, with the utmost loyalty, we extend our highest respect to you—the leaders of the Chinese people—and wish you good health!

    Respectfully, all the uprising personnel of the former Kuomintang Navy's First Fleet flagship "Changzhi."
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 27th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 11th: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu yesterday sent a telegram in reply to all the uprising personnel of the former Kuomintang Navy's First Fleet flagship "Changzhi," stating: Comrades of the "Changzhi" uprising:
    Congratulations on your heroic uprising. Due to the successive uprisings of patriotic officers and soldiers within the Kuomintang Navy, the People's Navy is developing rapidly. We hope you will study diligently, actively participate in the construction of the Chinese People's Navy, and complete the great cause of liberating the entire Chinese territory.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhu De
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 10th, 1949

### “长治”号全体起义官兵　电毛主席朱总司令致敬  毛主席朱总司令电勉努力学习积极参加建设中国人民海军

1949-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　“长治”号全体起义官兵
    电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    毛主席朱总司令电勉努力学习积极参加建设中国人民海军
    【新华社北京十一日电】九月十九日在吴淞口外起义的前国民党海防第一舰队旗舰“长治”号全体起义人员于九月二十七日致电毛主席、朱总司令致敬。该电原文如下：毛主席、朱总司令：
    我们因不满国民党反动派勾结帝国主义、出卖祖国，反对民主、残害人民，以及“封锁”沿海，实行杀人越货的海盗罪行，于九月十九日晨，在吴淞口外起义，摆脱国民党反动残余的统治，参加人民的海军。我们坚决拥护中国共产党的领导，在你们英明的领导下，加紧学习，改造自己，参加人民海军的建设工作，迅速肃清残匪，争取在解放台湾，解放全中国的战役中为人民立功。谨此，以万分的忠诚向你们——中国人民的领袖致最崇高的敬意，并祝你们健康！
    前国民党海防第一舰队旗舰“长治”号全体起义人员谨上
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月二十七日
    【新华社北京十一日电】毛主席朱总司令昨日电复前国民党海防第一舰队旗舰“长治”号全体起义人员称：长治号全体起义的同志们：
    庆祝你们的英勇的起义行动。由于国民党海军内的爱国官兵陆续起义，人民海军已在迅速发展。希望你们努力学习，积极参加建设中国人民海军和完成解放中国全境的伟大事业。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　朱　德
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月十日
