Title: It is You Who Guides Us! - Written in Commemoration of the Thirteenth Anniversary of Lu Xun's Death
Author: Zhang Ming
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 6

It is you, guiding us!
    ——Written in Commemoration of the Thirteenth Anniversary of Lu Xun's Death
    Zhang Ming
    It is you
    Like an old ox
    Suffering heavy lashes
    Diligently
    Sowing the seeds of resistance
　
    It is you
    With your milky sap
    Nurturing us
    Enabling us to grow up
　
    It is you
    With your hard bones
    Supporting the dark gate
    Releasing us to a place of light
　
    It is you
    Teaching us to use ruthless hands
    To the enemy
    Resolutely raise the javelin
　
    It is you
    Telling us
    The Soviet Union will surely win
    The proletariat will surely win
    You brought your victorious confidence and courage
    To us
　
    Lu Xun
    Our great mentor
    It is you
    In the dark days
    Guiding us
    Fighting to this day
    ——Today the people have already gained a tremendous victory.
    We will advance even more bravely

### 就是你，指引着我们！——为鲁迅逝世十三周年纪念而作
张铭
1949-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　就是你，指引着我们！
    ——为鲁迅逝世十三周年纪念而作
    张  铭
    就是你
    象一条老牛
    遭受着沉重的鞭打
    辛勤的
    播下了反抗的种子
　
    就是你
    用你那奶汁
    哺育着我们
    使我们长大成人
　
    就是你
    用你那硬骨头
    撑着黑暗的闸门
    放我们到光明的地方
　
    就是你
    教我们用无情的手
    向敌人
    坚决的举起投枪
　
    就是你
    向我们说
    苏联必胜
    无产阶级必胜
    你将你胜利的信心和勇气
    带给了我们
　
    鲁迅
    我们伟大的导师
    就是你
    在黑暗的日子里
    指引着我们
    战斗到今天
    ——今天人民已经获得巨大的胜利。
    我们将更加奋勇前进
