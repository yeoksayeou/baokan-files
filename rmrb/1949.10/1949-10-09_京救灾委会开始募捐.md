Title: Beijing Disaster Relief Committee Begins Fundraising
Author:
Date: 1949-10-09
Page: 4

　　Beijing Disaster Relief Committee
    Begins Fundraising
    [Beijing News] In order to universally launch a movement to provide relief to farmers in the disaster areas of Hebei Province and Beijing Municipality, the Beijing Municipal All-Circles Disaster Relief Committee was established on the 5th. The committee consists of 33 representatives from 18 units participating in the preparatory work, including the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Beijing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, the Beijing Municipal Organizing Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the Jiusan Society, the China Democratic National Construction Association, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Municipal Women's Federation, the Beijing Municipal Student Federation, the Beijing Municipal Joint Association of Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff, a certain unit of the garrison troops, the Muslim Work Committee, the Beijing Municipal Chamber of Commerce, the Beijing Municipal Industry Association, the Beijing Municipal Branch of the People's Bank of China, and the Beijing Municipal Trading Company. Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the city, was publicly elected as the Chairman of the Committee. The committee has three departments: organization, publicity, and storage. In addition, disaster relief sub-committees will be established by the participating units and district public offices. Each unit will conduct fundraising work within its respective departments. Departments and masses not belonging to the aforementioned units will be responsible for fundraising by the district public offices. The committee officially started its work on the 7th, and its address is located within the Municipal Government. This movement is scheduled for one and a half months and will be completed before November 20th. (Beijing Municipal Government News Office)

### 京救灾委会开始募捐

1949-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　京救灾委会
    开始募捐
    【北京讯】本市各界为普遍开展救济河北省及本市灾区农民的运动，已于五日成立北京市各界救灾委员会。以参加筹备工作的北京市人民政府、中共北京市委会、民革市分会，民盟市支部、农工民主党市整委会、九三学社、民主建国会、新民主主义青年团市筹委会、市总工会筹委会、市妇联、市学联、市中小学教职员联合会、驻军某部、回民工作委员会、市商业会、市工业会、人民银行市分行与市贸易公司等十八单位的代表三十三人为委员。公推市府张友渔副市长为主任委员。下设组织、宣传、保管三部。此外再由参加该会的各单位及区公所分别成立救灾分会。各单位分别在所属部门内进行捐募工作，不属上述各单位的部门与群众由各区区公所负责募集，该会已于七日正式开始工作，会址设于市政府内。此一运动定期一个半月，将于十一月二十日以前完成。（市府新闻处）
