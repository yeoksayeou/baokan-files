Title: US Non-Partisan Committee Launches Million-Signature Campaign Demanding Dismissal of Anti-Communist Indictments
Author:
Date: 1949-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: According to a TASS dispatch from New York: The newly formed "Non-Partisan Committee to Defend the Rights of the Twelve Communist Leaders" is launching a new campaign to dismiss the indictments in the current anti-communist trials. The committee is conducting a million-signature petition drive demanding that U.S. Attorney General McGrath dismiss the indictments and halt the trials. The committee also plans to send a delegation to Washington in the near future to demand the dismissal of the anti-communist indictments. The committee is chaired by Black leader Robeson and Indiana Judge Harris, with former Minnesota Governor Benson and United Farm Equipment and Metal Workers of America (UE) President Emspak serving as vice-chairmen.

### 美国无党派委员会　发起百万人签字运动要求撤销反共起诉书

1949-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国无党派委员会
    发起百万人签字运动要求撤销反共起诉书
    【新华社北京九日电】据塔斯社纽约讯：最近组成的“维护共产党领袖十二人权利的无党派委员会”，正开始一个新的运动，以期撤消目前反共审讯中的起诉书。委员会正在进行百万人的请愿签字运动，要求美司法部长麦格拉斯撤消起诉书和停止审讯。委员会并拟于最近派遣一代表团赴华盛顿，要求撤消反共起诉书。该委员会由黑人领袖罗伯逊和印第安纳州法官哈里斯任主席，明尼苏达州前州长宾逊及产联的农具工会主席欧克斯任副主席。
