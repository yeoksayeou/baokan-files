Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Second Executive Committee Meeting Discusses Preparations for Asian Women's Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th — The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its second executive committee meeting this morning at Beijing Chinese Language School to discuss preparations for the upcoming Asian Women's Representative Conference. Thirty-six executive committee members and six alternate executive committee members attended the meeting. After Li Dequan delivered the opening remarks, Lu Cui, deputy director of the federation's international affairs department, gave a report. She reported on the various preparatory works underway to welcome the Asian Women's Conference, such as reports, speeches, promotional materials, exhibitions, the reception of foreign representatives, visits, and the preparation of cultural and entertainment activities during the conference. In her report, Lu Cui requested that the Democratic Women's Federations in all regions, especially in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, and other large and medium-sized cities, immediately carry out extensive publicity work, convene cadre meetings and mass rallies as much as possible to convey the significance of the Asian Women's Conference, and conduct concrete internationalist education through the conference's publicity. Local women's federations should, in accordance with the united front policy, work with other women's organizations and women from all parties, classes, and professions to prepare for the Asian Women's Conference and select women representatives, and through these efforts, advance and improve women's work. After Lu Cui's report, Deng Yingchao, vice chairman of the federation, spoke. She pointed out the significance of holding the Asian Women's Conference in Beijing, the capital of China, and said: The All-China Women's Federation and local women's organizations should concentrate their efforts on publicizing and preparing for the Asian Women's Conference. Deng Yingchao's speech and Lu Cui's report were approved by the executive committee members present.

### 全国民主妇联二次执委会议　商讨筹备亚洲妇女会议

1949-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联二次执委会议
    商讨筹备亚洲妇女会议
    【新华社北京十一日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会今日上午假北京华文学校举行执行委员会第二次会议，讨论即将召开的亚洲妇女代表会议的准备工作。出席会议的计有执行委员三十六人，候补执行委员六人。李德全致开会词后，由该会国际工作部副部长陆璀报告。她报告了为着迎接亚洲妇女会议正在进行的各项准备工作，诸如报告、发言、宣传品、展览会、外国代表的招待事宜、参观及会议期间的文化娱乐活动的准备等等。陆璀在报告中，要求全国各地特别北京、天津、上海、沈阳及其他大、中城市的民主妇女联合会立即进行广泛的宣传工作，尽可能召集干部会议和群众大会传达亚洲妇女会议召开的意义，通过这个会议的宣传进行具体的国际主义教育。各地妇女联合会应根据统一战线的方针，与其他妇女团体以及各党派、各阶层、各职业的妇女界人物进行亚洲妇女会议的准备工作和推选妇女代表的工作，并通过这些工作把妇女工作推进并提高一步。陆璀报告后，该会副主席邓颖超发言。他指出亚洲妇女会议在中国首都北京举行的意义，并说：全国妇联和各地方的妇女组织，应该集中力量来进行亚洲妇女会议的宣传和准备工作。邓颖超的发言和陆璀的报告都得到与会执行委员的赞同。
