Title: Soviet Cultural, Artistic and Scientific Workers Delegation Widely Introduces Soviet Culture, Receives Individual Visits and Attends Report Meetings, Soviet Red Army Choir Performs in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: The Soviet cultural, artistic and scientific workers delegation that arrived in Beijing began today to receive individual visits from representatives of Chinese cultural and academic institutions, and was invited to report on the situation of Soviet culture, art, and science. The Soviet Red Army Choir, which came with the delegation, began performing in the capital.

　　Today's visitors included Deomidov, manager of the Soviet International Bookstore, and Stoletov, an agricultural expert. The visitors were Hu Yuzhi, Huang Luofeng, Wang Ziye, Hua Yingshen of the China Publishing Committee, and Le Tianyu, president of the North China University of Agriculture, and others. During the visit with Hu Yuzhi and others, Deomidov gave a broad introduction to the Soviet cultural publishing and distribution industry. This afternoon, the famous Soviet educator and Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic (RSFSR), Dubrovinina, gave a report to more than 1,000 education workers from various agencies and organizations in the capital on the Soviet education system and its implementation. The Soviet Red Army Choir performed various wonderful programs in the evening to entertain education workers, etc.

　　The Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation has been in Beijing for six days. On October 1st, the very day they arrived in our capital, they participated in the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. On the morning of the 2nd and the afternoon of the 3rd, they attended the China Peace Conference. On the afternoon of the 2nd, they went to Tianjin to participate in the Tianjin International Day of Struggle for Peace rally. On the 5th, they attended the founding meeting of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and in the evening, they attended the cocktail party of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

　　The Soviet Red Army Choir, which was led by the delegation, performed for the third time today. The first performance was at the joint evening party held by the preparatory committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the China Peace Conference to celebrate the birth of the People's Republic of China and the founding of the China Peace Conference. Chairman Mao and the members of the Central People's Government attended the viewing on that day. Chairman Mao expressed his thanks and greetings to them. The second time was after the cocktail party last night. In addition to attending meetings, the delegation visited Beijing's famous scenic spots and historical sites on the 4th.

### 苏文化艺术科学工作者代表团　广泛介绍苏联文化  接待个别访问应邀出席报告　苏红军歌舞团在京举行演出

1949-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏文化艺术科学工作者代表团
    广泛介绍苏联文化
    接待个别访问应邀出席报告
    苏红军歌舞团在京举行演出
    【新华社北京六日电】到达北京的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团，今天起接待中国文化学术机关代表的个别访问，并应邀报告苏联的文化、艺术、科学事业的情形。和代表团同来的苏联红军歌舞团开始在首都举行演出。今天受访问者有苏联国际书店经理德奥米多夫和农业专家斯托列托夫。访问者是中国出版委员会胡愈之、黄洛峰、王子野、华应申和华北大学农学院院长乐天宇等。德奥米多夫在接待胡愈之等人访问时对苏联的文化出版发行事业作了广泛的介绍。今天下午苏联名教育家苏俄教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜向首都各机关各团体教育工作者一千多人作了关于苏联教育制度及教育实施的报告。苏联红军歌舞团则于晚间出演各种精彩节目，招待教育工作者等。苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团到达北京已经六天。本月一日，即抵达我国首都的当天，就参加了中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立典礼。二日上午和三日下午出席中国保卫世界和平大会。二日下午并赴天津参加了天津市国际和平斗争日纪念大会。五日参加了中苏友好协会成立大会，晚间并出席中苏友好协会的鸡尾酒会。代表团所率领的苏联红军歌舞团今天已是第三次演出。第一次表演是在中苏友好协会总会筹备会和中国保卫世界和平大会为庆祝中华人民共和国诞生和中国保卫世界和平大会成立而联合主办的晚会上。其日毛主席和中央人民政府的委员们均到场观看。毛主席并对他们表示谢意和慰问。第二次是在昨晚鸡尾酒会之后。代表团六天来除参加会议外，并于四日游览了北京的名胜古迹。
