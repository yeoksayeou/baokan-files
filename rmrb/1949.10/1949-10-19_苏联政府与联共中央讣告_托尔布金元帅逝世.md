Title: Obituary from the Soviet Government and the Central Committee of the CPSU: Marshal Tolbukhin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: TASS Moscow, 17th: The Soviet Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Communist Party (Bolsheviks) issued the following statement: The Soviet Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Communist Party (Bolsheviks) announce with deep grief that the loyal son of the Bolshevik Party, one of the outstanding commanders and builders of the Soviet armed forces, and a famous hero of the Great Patriotic War - Soviet Marshal Fyodor Tolbukhin, passed away on October 17th after a long and serious illness.
    The funeral of Soviet Marshal Tolbukhin will be held in Red Square, Moscow.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: TASS Moscow, 17th: The Soviet Armed Forces Department announced: The Soviet Armed Forces Department deeply mourns the passing of Marshal Tolbukhin, Commander-in-Chief of the Transcaucasian Military District, on October 17th after a long and serious illness. The death of Marshal Tolbukhin has deprived the Soviet armed forces of an outstanding commander of the Great Patriotic War and a distinguished leader and organizer of the Soviet armed forces.

### 苏联政府与联共中央讣告　托尔布金元帅逝世

1949-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府与联共中央讣告
  　托尔布金元帅逝世
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议与苏联共产党（布）中央委员会发表声明如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议与苏联共产党（布）中央委员会以深沉的悲痛宣布：布尔什维克党的忠实的儿子，苏联武装力量的杰出的指挥者与缔造者之一，伟大爱国战争中的著名英雄——苏联元帅费道尔·托尔布金，在长时重病之后，已于十月十七日逝世。
    苏联元帅托尔布金的葬仪，将于莫斯科城红场举行。
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：苏联武装部队部公告：苏联武装部队部深深悲痛地宣布：外高加索军区总司令，苏联元帅托尔布金在长时重病之后，已于十月十七日逝世。托尔布金元帅的逝世，使苏联武装部队丧失了伟大爱国战争的卓越的指挥官和苏联武装部队的杰出领袖与组织者。
