Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Elected Chairman of the Central People's Government; Hebei Party, Government, Military, and People Send Telegrams of Support
Author:
Date: 1949-10-01
Page: 4

　　[Baoding, 30th Dispatch] This afternoon at 7:00 PM, upon hearing the news broadcast by the Beijing Xinhua Radio Station that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China had been established, and that the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party, had been elected Chairman of the Central People's Government, the Party, government, military, and people of Hebei Province were overjoyed and unanimously expressed their wholehearted support, sending telegrams of congratulations.

　　The Hebei Provincial People's Government, the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Hebei Military Region sent a congratulatory telegram stating: The Hebei Military Region, with boundless joy and excitement, congratulates the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. We vow, under the leadership of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, to strive with a hundredfold effort and firm confidence to implement the Common Program and various resolutions passed by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and to build a new China that is independent, peaceful, democratic, unified, and prosperous.

　　The Preparatory Committee of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Hebei Provincial Workers' Committee, the Preparatory Committee of the Hebei Provincial Women's Federation, and the Preparatory Committee of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles sent a congratulatory telegram stating: We warmly celebrate the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and joyfully hail the election of Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, as Chairman of the Central People's Government. From now on, the Chinese people will advance with even greater strides towards a future of independence, peace, democracy, prosperity, and unity; world peace will have an even stronger guarantee. We vow, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, to unite the people of the entire province and implement all resolutions of the Central People's Government with fighting practical action, and to work hard on various construction projects.

　　The Baoding Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Baoding Municipal People's Government, the Preparatory Committee of the various people's organizations in Baoding, the Preparatory Committee of the Baoding Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Second Session of People's Representatives from all walks of life in Baoding sent a congratulatory telegram stating: Baoding City, as the political and cultural center of Hebei Province, has seen the restoration and development of various construction projects since liberation, following Chairman Mao's wise instructions. In the future, we will more resolutely follow the leadership of the Central People's Government headed by Chairman Mao, strengthen economic and cultural construction, and vigorously support the national liberation war. We hereby send this telegram of congratulations and wish Chairman Mao good health.

### 毛泽东主席当选中央人民政府主席  河北党政军民致电拥护

1949-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东主席当选中央人民政府主席
  　河北党政军民致电拥护
    【保定三十日电】此间今日下午七时，听到北京新华广播电台播出中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立，中国人民伟大领袖、中国共产党毛泽东主席当选为中央人民政府主席的消息后，河北省党、政、军、民莫不欢欣若狂，一致表示竭诚拥护，并分别致电祝贺。
    河北省人民政府、中共河北省委会、河北省军区贺电称：河北省军区以无比的欢欣鼓舞，庆贺中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立。我们誓在中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席领导下，以百倍努力与坚强信心，为贯彻实现中国人民政治协商会议已通过的共同纲领及各项决议，建设一个独立、和平、民主、统一、富强的新中国而奋斗。
    河北省总工会筹委会、中国新民主主义青年团、河北省工委会、河北省妇联筹委会，河北省文联筹委会贺电称：我们热烈的庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立，尽情的欢呼中国人民伟大领袖毛主席当选中央人民政府主席。从此中国人民将更大踏步向独立、和平、民主、富强与统一的前程迈进；世界和平有了更坚强的保障。我们誓在毛主席与中央人民政府的领导下，团结全省人民，以战斗的实际行动贯彻实现中央人民政府的一切决议，努力进行各种建设。
    中共河北省保定市委会、保定市人民政府、保定市各人民团体筹委会、中苏友好协会保定分会筹委会、保定市第二届各界代表会，贺电称：保定市为河北省政治文化中心，解放后遵循着毛主席的英明指示，各项建设均有恢复与发展，今后我们当更坚决遵从以毛主席为首的中央人民政府的领导，加强经济、文化建设，并有力的支援全国解放战争。谨此电贺，并祝毛主席身体健康。
