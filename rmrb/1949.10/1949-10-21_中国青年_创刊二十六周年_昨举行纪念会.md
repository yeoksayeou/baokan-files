Title: "China Youth" Celebrates its 26th Anniversary; Commemorative Meeting Held Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th—The commemorative meeting for the 26th anniversary of the founding of "China Youth" was held at the China Youth Publishing House at 1 p.m. today. Attendees included Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, Deputy Secretaries Liao Chengzhi and Jiang Nanxiang, as well as staff members from the Central Committee of the Youth League. Guests included Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Shen Zijiu, Director of the Propaganda and Education Department of the All-China Women's Federation, and Xu Liqun, Secretary of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League. At the meeting, Yang Shu, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Youth League and concurrently Director of the China Youth Publishing House, delivered the opening speech. He pointed out that commemorating the 26th anniversary of "China Youth" requires inheriting the glorious tradition of the past and, in accordance with the tasks of the Chinese youth movement today, helping young people study Marxism-Leninism and guiding the work of the Youth League. Subsequently, Wei Junyi, Editor-in-Chief of "China Youth," gave a brief report on the history of "China Youth" during the Great Revolution, the War of Resistance Against Japan, and the People's Liberation War. In his speech, Comrade Feng Wenbin emphasized that "China Youth" should strengthen its combat effectiveness and become a powerful weapon for guiding youth work and youth thought, and that it should also do a good job in distribution. Comrade Liao Chengzhi also pointed out that distribution work should be regarded as a combat task. Deng Tuo, Shen Zijiu, Xu Liqun, and others also spoke in succession.

### “中国青年”创刊二十六周年　昨举行纪念会

1949-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　“中国青年”创刊二十六周年
  　昨举行纪念会
  【新华社北京二十日电】“中国青年”创刊二十六周年纪念会今日下午一时在中国青年社举行。出席者有新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，副书记廖承志、蒋南翔以及青年团中央工作人员等，来宾有人民日报总编辑邓拓，全国妇联宣教部部长沈兹九，青年团北京市筹委会书记许立群等。会上首由青年团中央宣传部部长兼中国青年社社长杨述致开会词，他指出纪念“中国青年”二十六周年，要承继过去的光荣传统，按照今天中国青年运动的任务，帮助青年学习马列主义，指导青年团的工作。继由“中国青年”总编辑韦君宜扼要报告“中国青年”经历大革命时代，抗日战争时代与人民解放战争时代的历史。冯文彬同志讲话中着重指出“中国青年”要加强战斗性，使之成为指导青年工作与青年思想的有力武器，并要办好发行工作。廖承志同志亦指出要把发行工作做为一个战斗任务来做。邓拓、沈兹九、许立群等亦相继讲话。
