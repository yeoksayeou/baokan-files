Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-10-12
Page: 4

    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's youth program at 18:15 tonight will broadcast an article by Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League: "Cherish Our Motherland, Support Our Chairman Mao." At 19:00, the popular science lecture will broadcast: "The Policies That Forestry in New China Should Have." The first art and literature program at 18:30, sponsored by the China Quyi Improvement Association, will broadcast the "Kaoshenpo" (a story about a quack doctor during imperial examinations) in Xihé Dagu (a type of storytelling accompanied by drum music). The second art and literature program at 22:00 will feature the North China University Second Art Troupe singing liberation area story songs.
    On the second channel, the children's program at 17:15 will feature Mr. Sun Jingxiu continuing to tell "The Story of Paul." The workers' program at 17:45 will feature Zhang Furui, a worker from the Telecommunications Bureau, speaking about "The Beijing Telecommunications Bureau Launches a Production Competition to Celebrate the Success of the People's Political Consultative Conference and the Establishment of the Central People's Government." From 20:00 onwards, the Beijing Municipal People's Government News Office will broadcast "Reports on Municipal Work in September."

### 北京新华广播电台今晚要目

1949-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    北京新华广播电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今晚十八点十五分青年节目，播送中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔的一篇文章：“爱护我们的祖国，拥护我们的毛主席”。十九点自然科学常识讲座，播送：“新中国林业应有的政策”。十八点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：播唱西河大鼓“考神婆”。二十二点第二次文艺节目，华北大学第二文工团演唱解放区故事歌曲。
    第二台十七点十五分儿童时间，孙敬修先生继续讲“保尔的故事”。十七点四十五分职工时间，电信局工人张福瑞讲“北京电信局展开生产竞赛，庆祝人民政协的成功和中央人民政府的成立”。二十点起，播送北京市人民政府新闻处“九月份市政工作的报导”。
