Title: Introducing Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Work: Simonov and Others Give Speeches in Shanghai; Over a Thousand Soviet Overseas Chinese in Shanghai Gather to Welcome the Soviet Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1949-10-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th.

　　Introducing Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Work
　　Simonov and Others Give Speeches in Shanghai
　　Over a Thousand Soviet Overseas Chinese in Shanghai Gather to Welcome the Soviet Cultural Delegation

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th] The Deputy Head of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation, Simonov, and five others, attended five separate lecture meetings on the afternoon of the 17th at the invitation of the literary, journalistic, scientific, theatrical, film, youth, and women's circles of Shanghai. Simonov, at the joint invitation of the Shanghai Branch of the All-China Federation of Literary Workers Associations and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association Shanghai Branch, attended the "Lecture on Literature and Journalistic Writing," delivering a two-hour speech on three questions raised by the Shanghai literary community. He first introduced the Soviet Writers' Union and its work, particularly in the areas of creation, publication, and criticism; secondly, he recounted his own writing experience; and finally, he discussed the issue of combining literature and politics. Soviet Doctor of Technical Sciences, Agraz, attended a grand welcoming meeting held by the Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the First All-China Natural Science Workers Representative Congress, with representatives from more than twenty scientific organizations, totaling over two hundred people. Agraz's topic was "An Overview of Soviet Science and Education." The famous Soviet film director, Gerasimov, gave a three-hour lecture at the invitation of the Theater and Film Association, introducing the history of Soviet cinema and the organization and production system of the current Soviet film industry, and satisfactorily answered five questions raised by Shanghai theater and film workers. After the meeting, Qin Yi presented flowers to Gerasimov on behalf of Shanghai theater and film workers. The Propaganda Minister of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Association, Vozni, was invited to attend a youth forum held by the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and cadres from various districts of the Municipal Committee, the East China League School, the Shanghai Student Federation, and other units, with hundreds of young people in attendance. He provided satisfactory introductions and explanations regarding various questions raised by Shanghai youth concerning the thoughts, lives, work, and studies of Soviet youth. The Deputy Minister of Education of the Soviet Russian Federal Republic, Dubrovin, and the Soviet People's Musician and piano virtuoso, Kravchenko, were invited to attend a lecture meeting attended by more than 1,500 representatives of Shanghai women's circles. Dubrovin gave a one-hour lecture, which was warmly welcomed by the audience. During Kravchenko's lecture, the venue was immersed in an atmosphere of high respect and friendship. After the lectures, children from the East China First Nursery and representatives from the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee presented flowers on behalf of all the women and children of Shanghai. The meeting concluded with the slogan "Long Live the Solidarity of the Chinese and Soviet Sisters."

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th] At 3:00 PM on the 16th, the Deputy Head of the Soviet Cultural, Artistic, and Scientific Workers Delegation, Simonov, and the director of the famous Soviet film "Stalingrad," Varlamov, attended a lecture meeting at the invitation of the Shanghai youth and student community. Simonov delivered a profound and moving speech amidst warm applause. He first paid tribute to the Shanghai youth and students who have a glorious history of struggle, then introduced the situation of Soviet cultural construction and the characteristics of Soviet cultural figures, and affectionately used his own experience to illustrate the long-term concern and profound friendship of the Soviet people for the Chinese revolution.

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th] Soviet overseas Chinese residing in Shanghai held a grand gathering of over a thousand people at the Soviet Overseas Chinese Club on the evening of the 17th to welcome the cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation from their motherland. Among those present were former Soviet diplomatic personnel stationed in Shanghai, Vladimir, Obrouchev, Subrev, and the head of the TASS Shanghai office, Yeshamin. On the Chinese side, Xia Yan, Ye Yiqun, Zhou Erfu, Jiang Chunfang, and others were invited to attend. A large banner at the entrance of the venue read "For Peace, For Our Happy Future," filling the atmosphere with joy and excitement. At the beginning of the meeting, the Chairman of the Soviet Overseas Chinese Association, Tsibunovsky, delivered a welcoming speech to the delegation and paid his respects. The leader of the delegation's art performance team, Karlmonov, expressed his condolences to the overseas Chinese residing in Shanghai. After the speeches, the Soviet art performance team and the Red Army Choir gave outstanding performances.

### 介绍苏联文化艺术科学工作　西蒙诺夫等在沪演讲  沪苏侨千余人集会欢迎苏文化代表团

1949-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　介绍苏联文化艺术科学工作
  　西蒙诺夫等在沪演讲
    沪苏侨千余人集会欢迎苏文化代表团
    【新华社上海十八日电】苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团副团长西蒙诺夫等六人，于十七日下午应上海文学界、新闻界、科学界、剧影界、青年界及妇女界的邀请，分别出席五个演讲会。西蒙诺夫应中华全国文学工作者协会上海分会及中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会上海分会联合邀请，出席“文学与新闻写作演讲会——就上海文学界所提出的三个问题作了二小时的演讲。他首先介绍了苏联作家协会及其工作，特别是创作、出版、批评等方面：其次，他叙述了自己的写作经验；最后谈到文学和政治的结合问题。苏联技术科学博士阿格拉则出席中华全国自然科学工作者第一次代表大会筹委会上海分会所举行的盛大欢迎会，到会者有二十多个科学团体的代表二百余人。阿格拉则的讲题为“苏联科学与教育的概况”。苏联名电影导演家格拉西莫夫应剧影协会邀请作了三小时演讲，他介绍了苏联电影的历史以及目前苏联电影事业的组织和生产制度等，并圆满地答复了上海剧影工作者提出的五个问题。会毕，秦怡代表上海剧影工作者向格拉西莫夫献花。苏联青年反法西斯协会宣传部长伏兹尼应邀出席中国新民主主义青年团上海市工委会及工委会各区干部，华东团校、上海学联等各单位青年数百人所举行的青年问题座谈会，对于上海青年所提出的有关苏联青年的思想、生活、工作、学习等各种问题，给予圆满介绍和解释。苏维埃俄罗斯联邦共和国人民教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜及苏联人民音乐家钢琴胜手克拉夫青柯则应邀出席上海妇女界代表一千五百余人的讲演会。杜伯洛维娜作了一小时的讲演，受到听众热烈欢迎。克拉夫青柯演讲时，会场沉浸在高度尊敬的友情里。讲毕，华东第一保育院儿童及上海民主妇联筹备会代表，代表上海全市妇女儿童献花。大会在“中苏两国姐妹团结万岁”的口号声中结束。
    【新华社上海十八日电】十六日下午三时苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团副团长西蒙诺夫和苏联著名影片“斯大林格勒”的导演瓦尔拉莫夫曾应上海青年学生界的邀请出席他们的演讲会。西蒙诺夫在热烈的欢迎鼓掌声中发表了深刻而动人的演说。他首先向具有光荣斗争历史的上海青年学生致敬，接着介绍了苏联文化建设情形和苏联文化人的特点，并且亲切地以他自己的经历说明苏联人民对中国革命的长期关切和深邃的友谊。
    【新华社上海十八日电】留沪苏侨十七日晚在苏侨俱乐部举行千人以上的盛大集会，欢迎来自他们祖国的文化艺术科学工作者代表团。到有留沪苏联前外交人员符拉基米洛夫、奥布罗会夫、苏布列夫及塔斯社上海负责人叶夏明等。中国方面被邀到会的有夏衍、叶以群、周而复、姜椿芳等。会场入口处写着“为和平，为我们幸福的未来”大幅标语，充满欢乐兴奋的气氛。大会开始，苏侨协会主席齐布诺夫斯基向代表团致欢迎词并致敬。代表团艺术演出队队长卡尔蒙诺夫向留沪侨胞表示慰问。讲话毕，即由苏联艺术演出队、红军歌舞团作卓越表演。
