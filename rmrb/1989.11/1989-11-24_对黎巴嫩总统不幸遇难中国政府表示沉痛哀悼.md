Title: China Expresses Deep Grief Over the Death of Lebanon's President
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 23rd.

China expresses deep grief over the death of Lebanon's President René Moawad.

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated at today's press conference that we strongly condemn any form of terrorist activity. We have always sympathized deeply with the Lebanese people who have suffered greatly from the war. We sincerely hope that all parties involved in the Lebanese conflict will prioritize national interests, actively work towards national reconciliation, and strive to restore peace and stability as soon as possible.

### 对黎巴嫩总统不幸遇难中国政府表示沉痛哀悼

1989-11-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　对黎巴嫩总统不幸遇难中国政府表示沉痛哀悼
    本报北京11月23日讯　外交部发言人在今天下午的新闻发布会上对黎巴嫩总统勒内·穆阿瓦德不幸遇难表示沉痛的哀悼。
    这位发言人说，我们反对任何形式的恐怖行动。我们对饱受战乱之苦的黎巴嫩人民一向寄予深切的同情，衷心希望黎巴嫩争端各方以民族利益为重，积极致力于实现民族和解，争取早日恢复和平与安宁。
