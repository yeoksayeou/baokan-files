Title: National People's Congress Standing Committee Inspects Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 2

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress is currently conducting inspections in various regions.

According to the Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, 44 members of the Standing Committee have, since the second half of this year, conducted inspections in 22 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, including Shanxi, Jilin, Shanghai, Zhejiang, Shandong, Henan, Guangxi, Guizhou, Shaanxi, and Xinjiang. Each inspection lasted for half a month.

The main contents of the inspections include: the implementation of the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on stopping the turmoil and suppressing the counter-revolutionary riot; the elimination of corruption, the punishment of "official speculation," and the rectification and reorganization of companies; the agricultural situation; and the implementation of some laws.

Members of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress conducted a special inspection in Hubei Province on relevant issues of foreign-related laws.

Members of the Financial and Economic Committee of the National People's Congress are currently conducting a special inspection in Anhui, Hubei, Hunan, Guizhou, Hainan, and Guangxi (six provinces and regions) on the implementation of the Forest Law, and have invited relevant personnel from the Ministry of Forestry to participate.

During the inspections, some members invited National People's Congress representatives working in the local areas to hold discussions on relevant issues.

### 全国人大常委视察各地

1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国人大常委视察各地
    新华社北京11月17日电  据全国人大常委会办公厅介绍，目前，全国人大常委会委员正在各地视察。
    据悉，今年下半年以来，已有44位全国人大常委会委员分别在山西、吉林、上海、浙江、山东、河南、广西、贵州、陕西、新疆等22个省、自治区、直辖市，进行了为期半个月的视察。
    委员们视察的主要内容包括：全国人大常委会关于制止动乱、平息反革命暴乱的决议的贯彻执行情况；清除腐败、惩治“官倒”和清理整顿公司情况；农业情况以及一些法律的贯彻实施情况。
    全国人大外事委员会委员就有关涉外法律问题在湖北省进行了专题视察。
    全国人大财政经济委员会委员现正就《森林法》实施情况在安徽、湖北、湖南、贵州、海南、广西等六省（区）进行专题视察，并吸收林业部有关同志参加。
    有些委员在视察期间，邀请在当地工作的全国人大代表就有关问题进行了座谈。
