Title: Honduras Begins General Election
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

Honduras began its quadrennial general election on the 26th. Over 2 million voters cast their ballots to elect the president, three vice presidents, members of the national congress, municipal councilors, and 20 members of the Central American Parliament.

### 洪都拉斯开始举行大选

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　洪都拉斯开始举行大选
    洪都拉斯二十六日举行四年一度的大选，二百余万选民投票选举总统、三名副总统、国会议员、市政议员和二十名中美洲议会议员。
