Title: Pakistani Crescent Weaving Factory Imports Spinning Machines from China
Author: Tang Mengzong
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency

The Pakistani Crescent Weaving Factory imported thirty-eight spinning frames and three cotton cleaning and blending machines from China. Chinese technicians were dispatched to assist with the installation. This image shows engineers from China and Pakistan collaborating to study the installation technology of the spinning frames. The photo was taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Mengzong.
