Title: Baker Proposes New Suggestions for Palestinian-Israeli Dialogue; U.S. Rejects Israeli Demand to Exclude PLO
Author:
Date: 1989-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 10th.

According to a report by Egypt's *Al-Ahram* newspaper today, U.S. Secretary of State Baker proposed new suggestions regarding Palestinian-Israeli dialogue on November 9th while en route from Australia to Washington.

Baker's new suggestions modify his previous five-point plan, with the key points being:

- Egypt and Israel agree to hold Palestinian-Israeli dialogue in Cairo;
- Egypt will not represent the Palestinians in action, but will consult with them on all matters related to the dialogue;
- Israel will engage in dialogue with the Palestinians after being satisfied with the list of members of the Palestinian delegation;
- Israel will participate in the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue based on its May 14th proposal for holding elections in the occupied territories. Palestinians can freely express opinions and raise questions regarding the procedures and election issues of the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue;
- U.S. Secretary of State Baker invited Egyptian Foreign Minister Magid and Israeli Foreign Minister Arens to Washington to discuss the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue. Baker's new suggestions essentially reject Israel's insistence on excluding the PLO from the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue. Baker said that the United States does not agree with Israel's demand and believes that limiting the content of the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue to elections within the occupied territories is impossible.

### 贝克提出巴以对话新建议  美拒绝以将巴解排除在对话外的主张

1989-11-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝克提出巴以对话新建议
    美拒绝以将巴解排除在对话外的主张
    新华社开罗11月10日电  据埃及《金字塔报》今天报道，美国国务卿贝克9日在从澳大利亚飞往华盛顿途中，提出有关巴以对话的新建议。
    贝克的新建议修改了他以前提出的5点计划，其要点包括：
    埃及和以色列同意在开罗举行巴勒斯坦同以色列对话；
    埃及行动上不代表巴勒斯坦人，但会就一切有关对话问题同他们磋商；
    以色列对巴勒斯坦代表团成员名单感到满意之后同巴勒斯坦人对话；
    以色列将在它5月14日提出的在被占领土举行选举计划的基础上参加巴以对话。巴勒斯坦人可以就巴以对话程序、选举问题自由发表意见和提出问题；
    美国务卿贝克邀请埃及外长马吉德和以色列外长阿伦斯前往华盛顿讨论巴以对话问题。贝克的新建议实际上拒绝了以色列坚持把巴解组织排除在巴以对话之外的主张。贝克说，美国不同意以色列的要求，并认为巴以对话的内容仅限于在以占领土内的选举是不可能的。
