Title: New Type of Artificial Fire-Resistant Board Introduced
Author: Pí Shùyì
Date: 1989-11-14
Page: 2

The People's Daily, Beijing, November 13th

The FM-89 artificial fire-resistant board produced by Kunming Forestry Machinery Factory is drawing attention at the International Forestry and Papermaking Technology Exhibition currently being held in Beijing. Made from crop straw and wood scraps, and treated with a fire retardant developed by Professor Meng Qingping of Fudan University's Institute of Applied Chemistry, this board possesses fire-resistant and heat-insulating properties. It can withstand prolonged exposure to flames at 1000°C and has physical and chemical properties similar to ordinary particle board. It can be widely applied in defense, industry, construction, and daily life.

### 新型人造防火板问世
皮树义
1989-11-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    新型人造防火板问世
    本报北京11月13日讯　正在北京举行的国际林业及造纸技术展览会上，昆明林业机械厂生产的FM—89人造防火板引人注目。它以农作物秸秆、木材边角料为原料，经复旦大学应用化学研究所孟清平研制的防火剂处理，具有防火、隔热功能，可耐受1000℃火焰长时间燃烧，其物理、化学性能与普通刨花板相同，可广泛应用于国防、工业、建筑、人民生活等方面。　（皮树义）
