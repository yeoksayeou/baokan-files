Title: Announcement of the Selection of Excellent Sports Essays for the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
Author: Liu Xiaoming
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 3

The selection of excellent sports essays for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation has been announced. The People's Daily, Beijing, November 28th. Reporter Liu Xiaoming reports: "A Gift to the Black and White World: The Heart of China" and nine other articles that propagate and review the achievements and glorious history of the sports sector of New China have recently been selected as the "Fragrant Grass Cup" excellent sports essays for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation. Nearly 600 sports workers, journalists, and workers, peasants, students, and officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army participated in this essay contest jointly organized by the Central People's Broadcasting Station and the Hefei Daily Chemical Factory. At today's award ceremony, Comrades Rong Gao Tang and He Zhen Liang and other responsible comrades presented awards to the winners.

### 庆祝建国40周年优秀体育征文评选揭晓
刘小明
1989-11-29
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　庆祝建国40周年优秀体育征文评选揭晓
    本报北京11月28日讯　记者刘小明报道：《奉献给黑白世界的中国心》等10篇宣传和回顾新中国体育战线成就及光辉历程的文章，近日被评选为“芳草杯”建国40周年优秀体育征文。近600名体育工作者、新闻记者和工人、农民、学生、解放军干部战士参加了由中央人民广播电台和合肥日用化工总厂联合举办的这次征文活动。在今天的颁奖仪式上，荣高棠、何振梁等负责同志向获奖者颁奖。
