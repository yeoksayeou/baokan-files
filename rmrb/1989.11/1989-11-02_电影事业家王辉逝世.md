Title: Film Industry Figure Wang Hui Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 2

Film industry figure and former director of the Chinese Film Archive Party Committee, Wang Hui, passed away from illness on October 19, at the age of 77. A memorial service was held yesterday at the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Babaoshan.

Wang Hui was born in Huaiyin, Jiangsu Province, in 1912. He joined the revolution in 1938 and the Communist Party of China in 1947. Wang Hui was one of the founders of the Chinese Film Archive. Over the years, he dedicated tremendous effort to collecting and organizing Chinese and foreign film materials, establishing the Chinese film archival industry. At the same time, he pioneered the exchange of film materials between China and other countries, making significant contributions.

### 电影事业家王辉逝世

1989-11-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    电影事业家王辉逝世
    新华社北京11月1日电　电影事业家、原中国电影资料馆党委书记王辉同志，因病于10月19日逝世，终年77岁。追悼会昨天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    王辉同志1912年出生于江苏省淮阴市，1938年参加革命，1947年加入中国共产党。王辉同志是中国电影资料馆的创建人之一。多年来，为搜集、整理中外电影资料、创立中国电影档案事业付出了艰巨的劳动。同时，他开辟了我国与世界各国电影资料的交流工作，并做出积极贡献。
