Title: Radio Golden Songs Selection Announced
Author: Wang Jingtao
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 6

The "Radio Golden Songs 40 Years Listener's Song Selection" event, organized by the Central People's Broadcasting Station, has recently been announced. The following 28 songs have been selected to receive the "40 Years Radio Golden Songs" award: "Song of Praise to the Motherland," "My Motherland," "Young Friends Gather Together," "The Moon Represents My Heart," "Sing a Folk Song for the Party to Hear," "In Praise of the Red Plum Blossom," "Toast Song," "In the Field of Hope," "The Pure Spring Water of the Frontier," and "I Love You, China."

### 广播金曲评选揭晓
王惊涛
1989-11-07
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

    广播金曲评选揭晓
    中央人民广播电台举办的广播金曲四十年听众评歌活动已于近日揭晓。选出《歌唱祖国》、《我的祖国》、《年轻的朋友来相会》、《十五的月亮》、《唱支山歌给党听》、《红梅赞》、《祝酒歌》、《在希望的田野上》、《边疆的泉水清又纯》、《我爱你，中国》等二十八首歌曲荣获“四十年广播金曲”奖。（王惊涛）
