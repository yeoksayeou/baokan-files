Title: Television Science and Technology Program Exhibition and Evaluation Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 3

The ten-day national television science and technology program exhibition and evaluation concluded yesterday. Shanxi Television's "Solar Stone" (Episode 2) and Ningxia Television's "Hail" won the special topic first prize, while Chongqing Television's "Knowledge Corridor—Introducing Three Star Fire Plan Research Projects" won the column first prize.

### 电视科技节目展评结束

1989-11-26
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　电视科技节目展评结束
    据新华社重庆十一月二十五日电　历时十天的全国电视科技节目展评会昨天结束。山西台的《太阳石》（第二集）和宁夏台的《冰雹》获专题一等奖，重庆电视台的《知识长廊——介绍三项星火计划科研项目》获专栏一等奖。
