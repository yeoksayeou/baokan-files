Title: Shanghai Develops a Large-Value Bidirectional Force Measuring Machine
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 2

The world's first 2000-ton bidirectional force measuring machine was fully installed in Shanghai's Minhang Industrial Zone on the 16th and successfully passed full-load tests and overall trial runs.

This super-heavy precision measuring instrument weighs 200 tons net, with a main unit height of 10.5 meters. It can generate a maximum pressure of 2200 tons and a maximum tensile force of 1000 tons, with a 100% domestic production rate. It will serve as China's national standard for large-force measurements, used to calibrate and standardize large-tonnage force sensors and dynamometers.

### 沪造出大力值双向测力机

1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    沪造出大力值双向测力机
    据新华社上海11月16日电　当今世界上第一台2000吨力双向测力机前天在上海闵行工业区全部安装结束，并顺利通过满负荷试验和整机试运转等测试和考核。
    这台超重型精密计量仪器净重达200吨，主机高度10.5米，可产生最大压力为2200吨力，最大拉力1000吨力，国产化率为100％。它将作为我国大力值计量的国家标准器，用来检定和标定大吨位力传感器和测力计。
