Title: East Germany Sentences Three Hooligans
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 3rd

East Germany's Dresden Court on November 3rd sentenced three hooligans who engaged in destructive activities.

A 19-year-old forester, Andreas, was sentenced to two years and two months in prison. A 23-year-old technician, Holm, was sentenced to two years and six months in prison. An 18-year-old gardener, Kai-Uwe, was sentenced to four years in prison and fined 3,000 Marks.

On the evening of October 4th, during a public demonstration, the three individuals engaged in illegal activities such as damaging public facilities and disrupting social order.

### 民主德国对三名暴徒判刑

1989-11-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民主德国对三名暴徒判刑
    据德通社报道，民主德国德累斯顿法院三日对进行破坏活动的三名暴徒判刑。
    十九岁的林业工人安德烈被判二年零二个月徒刑，二十三岁的技术工人霍尔姆被判二年零六个月徒刑，十八岁的园林工人凯—乌韦被判四年徒刑，并罚款三千马克。
    十月四日晚，在群众游行时，这三人进行了破坏公共设施、扰乱社会秩序等违法活动。　　　　　（据新华社）
