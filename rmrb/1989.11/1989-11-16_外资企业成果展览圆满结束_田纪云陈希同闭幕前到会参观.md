Title: Exhibition of Achievements by Foreign-Funded Enterprises Concludes Successfully
Author: Liu Xiao, Mei Hongru
Date: 1989-11-16
Edition: 2

The exhibition of achievements by foreign-funded enterprises in China concluded successfully today. Vice Premier Tian Jiyun and State Councilor and Mayor of Beijing Chen Xitong, along with over ten thousand spectators, visited the exhibition before its closure.

This exhibition received strong support from various provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, and foreign-funded enterprises. The 850-plus enterprises on display were selected from over 8,000 foreign-funded enterprises that have already commenced operations nationwide. Since its opening on November 9, the exhibition has attracted over 70,000 domestic and international visitors, averaging more than 10,000 visitors per day, with a highly enthusiastic response. Diplomatic envoys from various countries, commercial counselors, and high-level delegations from Japan, the United States, the Soviet Union, North Korea, Bulgaria, Poland, and Hungary have visited the exhibition.

According to incomplete statistics, during the exhibition, local exhibition teams signed export contracts worth over $20 million with foreign businesses.

### 外资企业成果展览圆满结束  田纪云陈希同闭幕前到会参观
刘霄  梅洪如
1989-11-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外资企业成果展览圆满结束
    田纪云陈希同闭幕前到会参观
    本报北京11月15日讯　记者刘霄、梅洪如报道：中国外资企业成果展览会今天闭幕。国务院副总理田纪云，国务委员、北京市市长陈希同以及上万名观众于闭幕前参观了展览。
    这次展览会得到了各个省、区、直辖市和外资企业的大力支持。参展的850多家企业，是从全国8000多家已经开业的外资企业中遴选出来的最优秀者。展览会自11月9日开幕以来，共接待中外观众7万余人，平均每天逾万，反应非常热烈。各国驻华使节、商务参赞以及日本、美国、苏联、朝鲜、保加利亚、波兰、匈牙利等国家的高层次代表团参观了展览。
    据不完全统计，大会期间，各地方展团与外商签订出口合同金额突破2000万美元。
