Title: Community Funds Telephone Installation
Author: Zhang Wei
Date: 1989-11-24
Page: 2

The first community-funded telephone exchange in Beijing, located in the Chaoyang District's Tuanjie Lake Housing Management Office, was officially opened on November 22. This 500-line telephone exchange is the first of its kind in the city. Nearly 400 households have already installed telephones. The exchange provides domestic and international long-distance calls, as well as special services such as alarms and reminders.

### 社会集资办电话（图片）
张伟
1989-11-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　社会集资办电话
    北京市朝阳区团结湖房管所和住地居民共同集资兴建的全市第一个500门电话总机站于11月22日正式开通，目前已有近400户居民装上了电话。这个总机站提供国内外长途电话和闹铃、记事等特殊服务。张　伟摄
