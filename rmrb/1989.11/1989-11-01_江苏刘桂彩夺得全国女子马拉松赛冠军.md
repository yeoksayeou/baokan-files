Title: Jiangsu's Liu Guicai Wins National Women's Marathon Championship
Author: Liu Wenzhong
Date: 1989-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, October 29th

Jiangsu athlete Liu Guicai today in the city of springs, Jinan, with a time of 2 hours, 51 minutes, and 34 seconds, won the sixth "Taishan Cup" National Women's Marathon Championship.

In addition to the full marathon, this competition also included a women's 20-kilometer run and a women's relay marathon. The top three teams in the relay marathon were Shandong, Heilongjiang, and Liaoning. Shandong's Chen Xiuying (1 hour, 13 minutes), Liu Shixiang (1 hour, 13 minutes, 12 seconds), and Wang Qinghuan (1 hour, 14 minutes, 16 seconds) took the top three spots in the women's 20-kilometer run.

### 江苏刘桂彩夺得全国女子马拉松赛冠军
袁文忠
1989-11-01
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    江苏刘桂彩夺得全国女子马拉松赛冠军
    新华社济南10月29日电　（记者袁文忠）江苏选手刘桂彩今天在泉城济南，以2小时51分34秒的最快成绩，夺得第六届“泰山杯”全国女子马拉松赛冠军。
    这次比赛除马拉松全程外，还设有女子20公里跑和女子马拉松异程接力赛。获得异程接力赛前3名的队是山东队、黑龙江队、辽宁队。山东的陈秀英（1小时13分）、刘世香（1小时13分12秒）、王庆环（1小时14分16秒）获得女子20公里前3名。
