Title: The Respected and Esteemed Leader of All Ethnic Groups in China, Comrade Deng Xiaoping
Author:
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 2

The respected and esteemed leader of all ethnic groups in China, Comrade Deng Xiaoping, in a recent photograph.

	1989年8月，邓小平同志在北戴河海滨。
	Comrade Deng Xiaoping at the seaside in Beidaihe in August 1989.
		杨绍明摄（新华社稿）
		Photographed by Yang Shaoming (Xinhua News Agency)

	一九八九年十一月，邓小平同志和江泽民、李鹏、乔石同志在一起交谈。
	In November 1989, Comrade Deng Xiaoping had a conversation with Comrades Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, and Qiao Shi.
		新华社记者　李生南摄
		Photographed by Li Shengnan (Xinhua News Agency)

	1986年9月，邓小平同志在中共十二届六中全会讨论《中共中央关于社会主义精神文明建设指导方针的决议》（草案）时发言。
	In September 1986, Comrade Deng Xiaoping spoke during the discussion of the draft resolution "Guidelines for the Construction of Socialist Spiritual Civilization" at the Sixth Plenary Session of the Twelfth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
		新华社记者　武基国摄
		Photographed by Wu Jiguo (Xinhua News Agency)

	1989年9月，邓小平同志同杨尚昆、李鹏、万里同志在一起。
	In September 1989, Comrade Deng Xiaoping was with Comrades Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, and Wan Li.
		杨绍明摄（新华社稿）
		Photographed by Yang Shaoming (Xinhua News Agency)

	1989年8月，85岁高龄的邓小平同志在盛夏季节每天下海游泳，风雨无阻。
	In August 1989, 85-year-old Comrade Deng Xiaoping swam in the sea every day during the hot summer, undeterred by wind or rain.
		杨绍明摄（新华社稿）
		Photographed by Yang Shaoming (Xinhua News Agency)

	1987年4月，邓小平、李先念、邓颖超同志会见出席六届人大五次会议的代表。
	In April 1987, Comrades Deng Xiaoping, Li Xiannian, and Deng Yingchao met with representatives attending the Fifth Session of the Sixth National People's Congress.
		新华社记者　王敬德摄
		Photographed by Wang Jingde (Xinhua News Agency)

	1987年4月，邓小平同志与出席香港特别行政区基本法起草委员会第四次会议的香港委员亲切交谈。
	In April 1987, Comrade Deng Xiaoping had a cordial conversation with Hong Kong members attending the fourth meeting of the Basic Law Drafting Committee of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region.
		新华社记者　李平摄
		Photographed by Li Ping (Xinhua News Agency)
