Title: Comrade Li Zueran Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 3

Proletarian revolutionary, former member of the Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China, and advisor to the Central Propaganda Department of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Li Zueran passed away in Beijing on November 9 due to long-term illness. He was 90 years old.

During Comrade Li Zueran's critical illness, Comrades Li Xiannian, Wu De, Li Lian, and Wang Renzhi, as well as relevant responsible comrades from the Central Advisory Commission and the Central Propaganda Department of the Communist Party of China, visited the hospital to offer their regards.

### 李卓然同志在京逝世  病危期间李先念等赴医院看望

1989-11-11
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    李卓然同志在京逝世
    病危期间李先念等赴医院看望
    新华社北京11月10日电　无产阶级革命家、原中共中央顾问委员会委员、中共中央宣传部顾问李卓然同志，因长期患病，于11月9日在北京逝世，终年90岁。
    李卓然同志病危期间，李先念、陆定一、李力安、王忍之及中共中央顾问委员会、中共中央宣传部有关负责同志前往医院看望。
