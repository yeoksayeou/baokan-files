Title: Guangdong Province Directly Managed Departments Dissolve Over 100 Companies
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 2

Guangdong Province Directly Managed Departments Dissolve Over 100 Companies

According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 27th. Guangdong Province has recently dissolved 106 companies under the direct management of provincial departments.

According to statistics, the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and the Provincial Government Office collectively manage 9 companies. This time, it has been decided to dissolve 4 companies, merge or transfer 5 companies, and no longer manage any companies. Among the companies dissolved this time, 20 are managed by party and government agencies.

### 广东省直机关撤公司百余家

1989-11-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　广东省直机关撤公司百余家
    据新华社广州11月27日电　广东省最近撤销了省直机关的106家公司。
    据统计，广东省委、省政府办公厅共办有公司9家，这次决定撤销4家，合并、移交5家，不再办公司。这次撤销的公司中，党政机关办的有20家。
