Title: Foreign Affairs News Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-11-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

Vice Premier and Governor of the People's Bank of China Li Guixian met with a delegation from the State Bank of the Soviet Union led by Deputy Governor Zakharov in the Great Hall of the People in the afternoon of the 15th.

The Soviet delegation, at the invitation of Li Guixian, arrived in Beijing on the 11th for a 10-day visit to China.

The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Ensemble of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic performed a rich and colorful program of songs and dances for 1,500 officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army at the Beijing Theatre on the evening of the 15th. Among the audience were Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Zhen, Director of the General Logistics Department Zhao Nanqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Guo Linxiang, and Chairman of the China-GDR Friendship Association Wang Guoqian.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼中国人民银行行长李贵鲜15日下午在人民大会堂会见了由副行长扎哈罗夫率领的苏联国家银行代表团。
    苏联代表团应李贵鲜的邀请于11日到达北京对中国进行为期10天的访问。
    ▲德意志民主共和国国家人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团15日晚上在北京中国剧院为1500名中国人民解放军官兵演出了一台丰富多彩的歌舞节目。观众中有中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田、总后勤部部长赵南起、总政治部副主任郭林祥以及中国民德友好协会会长王国权。
              （新华社）
