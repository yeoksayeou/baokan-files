Title: The Communist Youth League's Ideological and Political Work Conference Proposes Implementing the Spirit of the Fifth Plenary Session to Cultivate the Four Haves New People
Author: Yuan Jianda, He Wei
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 3

The Communist Youth League's Second Ideological and Political Work Conference opened today at the Red Scarf Camp in Tianjin's Fenghuangshan. Reporters Yuan Jianda and He Wei report:

The main task of this conference is to further implement and carry out the spirit of the Fourth and Fifth Plenary Sessions of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Party, to seriously summarize and reflect on the experiences and lessons of the League's ideological and political work over the past few years, and to study how to strengthen and improve the League's ideological and political work in the future.

Wang Renzhi, Minister of the Propaganda Department, spoke at the conference on behalf of Li Ruihan. He said that the ideological and political work for young people must first solve the problems of loving the Party, loving the motherland, and loving socialism. It is also necessary to help young people establish correct revolutionary views on life, values, and morality, and to study effective methods to strengthen and improve the ideological and political work for young people.

Liu Yandong, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, gave a report. She said that the correct political direction must be firmly established as the first priority in the League's ideological and political work, and that efforts should be made to comprehensively improve the quality of young people according to the "Four Haves" goal.

Liu Yandong revealed in her report that the Communist Youth League should focus on four major tasks in its ideological and political work at present: First, starting from unifying thoughts, to contribute to maintaining the stability of economic and social development. Second, starting from national conditions education, to deepen the positive education of adhering to the four cardinal principles. Third, starting from the education of hard work and self-reliance, to invigorate the spirit of young people, and to strive to complete the various tasks proposed by the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Party. Fourth, starting from the campaign to "eliminate pornography and harmful materials," to optimize the public opinion and cultural environment for the growth of young people.

### 共青团思想政治工作会议提出　贯彻五中全会精神培养四有新人
袁建达　何伟
1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　共青团思想政治工作会议提出
　　贯彻五中全会精神培养四有新人
    本报蓟县11月21日讯　记者袁建达、何伟报道：中国共产主义青年团第二次思想政治工作会议今天在这里的天津市凤凰山红领巾营地开幕。
    这次会议的主要任务是进一步贯彻落实党的十三届四中全会和五中全会的精神，认真总结和反思近几年来团的思想政治工作的经验教训，研究今后如何加强和改进团的思想政治工作。
    中宣部长王忍之受李瑞环同志的委托到会讲了话。他说，青年的思想政治工作首先要解决热爱党、热爱祖国、热爱社会主义的问题。还要帮助青年树立正确的革命的人生观、价值观、道德观，同时研究有效办法，切实加强和改进青年的思想政治工作。
    团中央书记处书记刘延东作了报告。她说，必须把坚定正确的政治方向放在团的思想政治工作的第一位，按照“四有”目标，注意全面提高青年的素质。
    刘延东在报告中透露，共青团当前要突出抓好思想政治工作四件大事：第一，从统一思想入手，为保持经济和社会的发展稳定做贡献。第二，从国情教育入手，深化坚持四项基本原则的正面教育。第三，从艰苦奋斗教育入手，振奋青年精神，努力完成党的十三届五中全会提出的各项任务。第四，从“扫黄除害”入手，优化青年成长的舆论环境和文化环境。
