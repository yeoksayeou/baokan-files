Title: The Soviet Union Sentences Lawbreakers Involved in Ethnic Unrest
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 3

The Supreme Court of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic sentenced twelve lawbreakers involved in local ethnic unrest on October 31. Two of them were sentenced to death, and ten were sentenced to imprisonment.

They were convicted for intercepting four Meskhetian Turkish villagers who were attempting to flee in a car on June 4, pouring gasoline on them, and burning them to death.

### 苏对民族动乱中的违法者判刑

1989-11-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏对民族动乱中的违法者判刑
    苏联乌兹别克共和国最高法院十月三十一日对当地发生的民族动乱中的十二名违法者判刑，其中两人被判处死刑，十人被判处有期徒刑。
    他们是由于今年六月四日拦截乘小汽车出逃的四名麦斯赫基土耳其族村民并浇上汽油将他们烧死而被判刑的。
