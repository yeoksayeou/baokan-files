Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Arrives in Beijing from Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 1

Prince Norodom Sihanouk of the Democratic Kampuchea and his wife arrived in Beijing this morning by plane from Pyongyang.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Shucai and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 西哈努克从平壤抵京

1989-11-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克从平壤抵京
    新华社北京11月3日电　民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今天上午乘飞机从平壤抵达这里。
    外交部副部长刘述卿等前往机场迎接。
