Title: Beijing Develops Convenient Fast Food Products
Author: Su Jianpeng
Date: 1989-11-19
Edition: 2

Beijing Develops Convenient Fast Food Products
People's Daily reports: The Beijing municipal grain department, based on the socialization of household labor and convenience for the public, is vigorously developing the production of convenient fast food products.

The city has successively introduced 17 sets of food production equipment for instant noodles, bread, biscuits, and noodles, forming an annual production capacity of over 170 varieties and more than 300 million kilograms of convenient, fast food products.

It is understood that the Beijing municipal grain department is currently developing rice, bean convenient porridge, and frozen dumplings.

### 北京发展方便快餐食品
隋建鹏
1989-11-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京发展方便快餐食品
    本报讯　北京市粮食部门立足于家务劳动社会化，方便群众生活，大力发展方便快餐食品生产。
    该市先后引进了方便面、面包、饼干、挂面等17套食品生产设备，形成了年产170多个品种、3亿多公斤方便、快餐食品的能力。
    据悉，北京市粮食部门正在开发大米、豆类方便粥和速冻包子等食品。　（隋建鹏）
