Title: How Tall Are Chinese Adults on Average?
Author: Xíng Zēng
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 3

	The average height of Chinese adults is a topic of interest. The State Bureau of Technical Supervision released a national standard on November 15th titled "Chinese Adult Body Dimensions." According to this standard, the average height of Chinese adult males is 1.678 meters, and the average height of Chinese adult females is 1.570 meters.

	This national standard also highlights the differences in average height among adults in various regions of China. In the Northeast and North China regions, the average height for males is 1.693 meters, and for females, it is 1.586 meters, making these regions the tallest in the country. In contrast, the average height in the Southwest region, specifically in Guizhou, Sichuan, and Yunnan, is 1.647 meters for males and 1.546 meters for females, making this the region with the shortest average height.

### 中国成年人平均有多高？男子：1.678米　女子：1.570米
行增
1989-11-18
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    中国成年人平均有多高？
    男子：1.678米　女子：1.570米
    本报讯　我国成年人平均身高是多少？国家技术监督局15日发布的一项国家标准《中国成年人人体尺寸》表明，中国成年男子平均身高为1.678米，成年女子平均身高为1.570米。
    这项国家标准还显示了我国不同地区成年人平均身高的差异。东北、华北地区男子身高为1.693米，女子为1.586米，居全国之首。西南地区的贵州、四川、云南的男子身高为1.647米，女子为1.546米，是平均身高最低的地区。（行增）
