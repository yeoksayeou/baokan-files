Title: UNGA Elects 18 Members to the Economic and Social Council
Author: Liú Kāichéng
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 3

	The 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly today elected 18 members to the Economic and Social Council. China, whose term expires at the end of this year, was re-elected.

	The election was conducted by secret ballot, with candidates needing to secure two-thirds of the votes to be elected. The other 17 elected countries are: Belize, Bolivia, Bulgaria, Canada, Denmark, Iran, Norway, Oman, Poland, Rwanda, Somalia, Sri Lanka, Sudan, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, Uruguay, and Zaire.

	The Economic and Social Council operates under the authority of the General Assembly and coordinates the economic and social work of the United Nations system. It consists of 54 member countries, each serving a three-year term.

### 联大改选经社理事会理事国  中国等18国当选
刘开宸
1989-11-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联大改选经社理事会理事国
    中国等18国当选
    本报联合国11月1日电　记者刘开宸报道：第44届联合国大会今天改选经济及社会理事会中18个理事国。今年年底任满的中国连选连任。
    选举是采取无记名投票方式，得到2／3多数票的才能当选。其它当选的17国是：伯利兹、玻利维亚、保加利亚、加拿大、丹麦、伊朗、挪威、阿曼、波兰、卢旺达、索马里、斯里兰卡、苏丹、苏联、英国、乌拉圭和扎伊尔。
    经社理事会是在大会权力之下、协调联合国组织系统的经济和社会工作的机构。由54个理事国组成，任期为3年。
