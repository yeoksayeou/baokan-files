Title: The Central Political Bureau of the SED Discusses Domestic Situation, Emphasizing Unity and Reform
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 31st.

The Central Political Bureau of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany held a meeting on October 31st to discuss the current domestic situation. The meeting emphasized that the most urgent task at hand is to unite the entire party's strength.

According to the Deutsche Presse-Agentur, the Political Bureau meeting stressed the need to strengthen the leadership role of the working-class party in socialist construction. The meeting analyzed the current political and economic situation in the German Democratic Republic, discussed comprehensive economic reforms including economic and planning system reforms, and decided to take measures to stabilize the economy.

The meeting's communiqué also emphasized the need to further promote party democracy, reform the political system, accelerate the democratization of social life, strengthen socialist legal system, and enhance the role of the People's Chamber and local people's representative bodies.

The meeting discussed the draft law on GDR citizens traveling abroad. Additionally, the meeting discussed personnel arrangements and will submit them to the upcoming 10th Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee for decision.

On the same day, *Neues Deutschland* published an editorial stating that the current priority is to remove obstacles affecting normal work, strengthen socialism through labor, and create the wealth that the people desire.

It is reported that at the invitation of the CPSU Central Committee, the General Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, Egon Krenz, arrived in Moscow on October 31st for a two-day visit to the Soviet Union.

During the visit, Krenz held talks with CPSU General Secretary Gorbachev.

This is Krenz's first foreign visit since assuming the position of General Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party.

### 民德党中央政治局讨论国内局势时指出  当前任务是把全党力量凝聚起来  克伦茨抵苏联访问

1989-11-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德党中央政治局讨论国内局势时指出
    当前任务是把全党力量凝聚起来
    克伦茨抵苏联访问
    新华社柏林10月31日电　民主德国统一社会党中央政治局31日开会，讨论当前国内局势。会议指出，当前最紧迫的任务是把全党的力量凝聚起来。
    据德通社报道，政治局会议强调，要加强工人阶级政党在社会主义建设中的领导作用。会议分析了民主德国目前的政治和经济形势，讨论了包括改革经济和计划体制在内的全面经济改革问题，并决定采取措施稳定经济。
    会议发表的公报还强调，要进一步发扬党内民主，改革政治体制，加快社会生活的民主化进程，进一步加强社会主义法制以及提高人民议院和地方人民代表机构的作用。
    会议讨论了关于民主德国公民到国外旅游的法律草案。此外，会议还讨论了关于人事安排的建议，并将提交即将举行的11届10中全会作出决定。
    同一天，《新德意志报》发表社论指出，现在当务之急是消除影响正常工作的障碍，通过劳动加强社会主义，创造人民所希冀的财富。
    本报讯　应苏共中央邀请，德国统一社会党中央总书记、民主德国国务委员会主席埃贡·克伦茨于10月31日抵达莫斯科，开始对苏联进行为期2天的访问。
    访问期间，克伦茨与苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫举行了会谈。
    这是克伦茨就任统一社会党中央总书记以后第一次出国访问。
