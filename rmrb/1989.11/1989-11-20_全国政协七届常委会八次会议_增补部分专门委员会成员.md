Title: The Eighth Meeting of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Adds Members to Some Special Committees
Author:
Date: 1989-11-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 19th.

The list of newly added deputy chairpersons and members of some special committees of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (1989-11-19 approved by the Eighth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference):

Proposal Committee:
Deputy Chairpersons: Ge Zicheng, Shen Yuan
Members: Li Yi, Sun Yiqing, Chen Shengwu, Luo Zhewen, Lei Tianjue

Literature and History Committee:
Member: Zhao Zili

Law Committee:
Members: Yan Zhongqin, Liu Jiadong, Yang Jiyuan, Zhang Like

Women and Youth Committee:
Member: Li Dawai
