Title: PLO and EEC Discuss Middle East Situation
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, November 11th

A delegation led by PLO Chairman Yasser Arafat held talks with a delegation from the European Economic Community, comprising the foreign ministers of France, Spain, and Ireland, to discuss the Middle East situation.

Both sides reviewed recent developments in the Middle East, particularly Israel's intensified crackdown on the Palestinian people and the dangers this poses to Middle East stability and international peace. They also discussed the EEC's efforts to promote the Middle East peace process and convene an international peace conference.

The EEC delegation expressed its willingness and readiness to strengthen cooperation in political, economic, social, and cultural fields and fully understood the Palestinian stance on the peace process. French Foreign Minister Roland Dumas reiterated France's position, as stated by President Mitterrand in the European Parliament, condemning the crimes of the Israeli occupation authorities and affirming the Palestinian people's right to establish an independent state.

Arafat stated that, given the historical strategic relationship between the peoples of Europe and the Middle East, it is impossible to find a solution to the Middle East crisis without the participation of the European Community.

### 巴解和欧共体会谈中东局势  法外长重申确认巴人民建立国家的民族权利  阿拉法特认为解决中东危机应有欧共体参加

1989-11-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴解和欧共体会谈中东局势
    法外长重申确认巴人民建立国家的民族权利
    阿拉法特认为解决中东危机应有欧共体参加
    新华社突尼斯11月11日电  据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，由巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特率领的巴解组织代表团今天在这里同由法国、西班牙和爱尔兰3国外长组成的欧洲经济共同体代表团就中东局势举行了会谈。
    双方讨论了中东局势最近的发展情况，特别是以色列对巴勒斯坦人民的加紧镇压以及由此对中东局势和国际和平造成的危险。双方还讨论了欧共体为促进中东和平进程和召开中东和平国际会议所作出的努力。
    欧共体代表团表示愿意和准备加强双方在政治、经济、社会和文化方面的合作关系，并对巴勒斯坦关于和平进程的立场表示完全理解。法国外长罗朗·迪马重申了法国总统密特朗在欧洲议会宣布的关于谴责以色列占领当局的罪行和确认巴勒斯坦人民有权建立一个独立国家的原则立场。
    阿拉法特说，鉴于欧洲和中东各国人民之间历史上存在着战略关系，没有欧洲共同体的参加，就不可能找到解决中东危机的任何办法。
