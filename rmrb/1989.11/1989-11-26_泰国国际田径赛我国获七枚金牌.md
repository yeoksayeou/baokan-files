Title: China Wins Seven Gold Medals at Thailand International Athletics Meet
Author: Huang Yong, Qi De Liang
Date: 1989-11-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, November 24th.

The two-day 1989 Thailand International Athletics Invitational concluded tonight in Bangkok. Chinese athletes secured 7 gold medals out of the 25 events, along with 3 silver medals and 1 bronze medal.

Eleven Chinese athletes participated in 12 events. In today's women's 100-meter hurdles and men's 110-meter hurdles, Chinese athletes Feng Yinghua and Yu Zhicheng won gold medals with times of 13.8 seconds and 14.13 seconds, respectively. Pang Yan secured a silver medal in the men's long jump with a distance of 7.74 meters. Duan Xiuquan won a bronze medal in the men's 800 meters.

Taiwanese athletes won 6 gold medals, placing them second overall.

The remaining gold medals were won by athletes from Malaysia, Japan, the Philippines, Mauritius, and South Korea.

### 泰国国际田径赛我国获七枚金牌
黄勇　戚德良
1989-11-26
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　泰国国际田径赛我国获七枚金牌
    新华社曼谷11月24日电　（记者黄勇、戚德良）为期两天的1989年泰国国际田径邀请赛今晚在这里结束，中国男女选手共夺得25个比赛项目中的7块金牌，3块银牌，1块铜牌。
    11名中国选手参加了12个项目的比赛。在今天进行的女子100米栏和男子110米栏比赛中，中国选手冯英华和余志诚分别以13秒8和14秒13的成绩为中国队夺得两枚金牌。庞岩以7米74夺得男子跳远银牌。段秀全在男子800米赛中得铜牌。
    中国台北选手共夺得6块金牌，名列第二。
    其它金牌由马来西亚、日本、菲律宾、毛里求斯和南朝鲜选手夺得。
