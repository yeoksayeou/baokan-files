Title: Versatile Functions of the Five-Star Magnetic Acupuncture Needle
Author: Lü Jìchuán
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 2

The Five-Star Magnetic Acupuncture Needle, which features traditional Chinese medicine and is the first of its kind in the country, recently passed an appraisal in Beijing. This device was developed by the Beijing Daguangming Traditional Medicine Foundation and invented by An Zhi.

This magnetic acupuncture needle, based on traditional Chinese medicine theory, combines the advantages of four therapies: acupuncture, cupping, magnetotherapy, and traditional Chinese medicine. It is a multifunctional medical device.

### 五星磁吸针功能多样化
卢继传
1989-11-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    五星磁吸针功能多样化
    本报讯　记者卢继传报道：有中医特色、国内首创的五星磁吸针（亦称回春四合经穴特效器）近日在北京通过鉴定。这是由北京大光明传统医药基金会研制、安志发明的。
    这种磁吸针是根据中医理论，集针刺、拔火罐、磁疗、中药四种疗法的优点于一体，具有多种功能的医疗器械。
