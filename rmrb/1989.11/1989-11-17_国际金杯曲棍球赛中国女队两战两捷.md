Title: Chinese Women's Field Hockey Team Wins Two Consecutive Matches in the International Gold Cup
Author:
Date: 1989-11-17
Edition: 3

The Chinese women's field hockey team won two consecutive matches in the Indira Gandhi International Gold Cup Women's Field Hockey Tournament held in India. On November 15, the Chinese team defeated the Malaysian team 7-2. The day before, the Chinese team had defeated the Soviet Union team 2-1.
