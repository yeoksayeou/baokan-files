Title: Funeral of Shen Lisheng Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 4th.

The funeral of Shen Lisheng, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, former director of the Institute of Petroleum Exploration and Development of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, and vice-chairman of the second session of the China Petroleum Society, was held today at the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Beijing.

Peng Zhen sent a wreath, and Yu Quli, Chen Xilian, Kang Shien, and Hab Compaign attended the funeral and sent wreaths.

Shen Lisheng was a native of Dali, Shaanxi. He passed away in Beijing on October 6 at the age of 71. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1933 and served as the secretary of the Shaan-Gan Provincial Committee and the mayor of Changchun, among other positions. He was a delegate to the Seventh National Congress of the Party. After the founding of the People's Republic, he dedicated his life to the rapid development of China's petroleum industry.

### 申力生遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-11-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    申力生遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京11月4日电　中国共产党的优秀党员、原石油工业部石油勘探开发科学研究院院长、中国石油学会第二届副理事长申力生遗体告别仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。彭真送了花圈，余秋里、陈锡联、康世恩、习仲勋等参加了遗体告别仪式并送了花圈。
    申力生同志是陕西大荔人，今年10月6日在京逝世，终年71岁。他1933年加入中国共产党，曾任陕甘省委秘书长、长春市市长兼党组书记等职，是党的七大代表。建国后，他长期战斗在石油工业战线，为新中国石油工业的快速发展贡献了毕生的精力。
