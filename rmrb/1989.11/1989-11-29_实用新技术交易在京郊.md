Title: Practical New Technology Trading in the Beijing Suburbs
Author: Yǒu Fū
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 28th.

More than 50 research or technology development units from Heilongjiang, Jilin, Sichuan, Fujian, and other provinces gathered in Huairou County, on the outskirts of Beijing, today to hold a fair for practical new technologies and new products. They brought over a thousand new technology projects, attracting representatives from more than 500 factories across the country, particularly from suburban township enterprises. Negotiations were lively. The fair also displayed over a hundred samples of foreign small products. The entire fair will last for three days.

### 实用新技术交易在京郊
也夫
1989-11-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　实用新技术交易在京郊
    本报北京11月28日讯来自黑龙江、吉林、四川、福建等地的50多家科研或技术开发单位，今天聚集在京郊怀柔县，举办实用新技术新产品交易会。他们带来的上千个新技术项目，吸引了全国各地500多个厂家的代表，尤其受到京郊乡镇企业的欢迎，洽谈活动十分活跃。会上还展出了上百种国外小产品样品。整个交易会将举行三天。　（也　夫）
