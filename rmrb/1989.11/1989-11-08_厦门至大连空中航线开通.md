Title: Xiamen to Dalian Air Route Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 2

Xiamen to Dalian air route opens

New China News Agency Xiamen, November 7 - The air route from Xiamen to Dalian opened today. The air distance of this route is 1900 kilometers, using McDonnell Douglas MD-82 aircraft, with a flight time of 2 hours and 40 minutes. The flight departs from Xiamen every Tuesday and returns from Dalian the same day.

### 厦门至大连空中航线开通

1989-11-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    厦门至大连空中航线开通
    新华社厦门11月7日电　厦门至大连空中航线今天开通。这条航线空中距离1900公里，采用麦道—82型客机，飞行时间2小时40分钟。班机每逢星期二从厦门起飞，当天从大连返回。
