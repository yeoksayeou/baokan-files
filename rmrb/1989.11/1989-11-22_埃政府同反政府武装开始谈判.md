Title: Ethiopian Government Begins Negotiations with Anti-Government Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 4

The Ethiopian government began a new round of preliminary talks with the anti-government armed group, the Eritrean People's Liberation Front, in Nairobi on the 20th. The aim is to end the 28-year-long Eritrean war. The formal mediators, former U.S. President Carter and Kenyan President Moi, attended the opening ceremony of the talks.

### 埃政府同反政府武装开始谈判

1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　埃政府同反政府武装开始谈判
    埃塞俄比亚政府二十日在内罗毕同反政府武装厄立特里亚人民解放阵线开始了新的一轮预备性会谈，以结束长达二十八年的厄立特里亚战争。双方的正式调解人、美国前总统卡特和肯尼亚总统莫伊参加了会谈的开幕式。
