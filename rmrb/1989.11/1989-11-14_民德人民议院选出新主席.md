Title: East German People's Assembly Elects New Chairman
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì  Jiāng Jiànguó
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 4

The ninth session of the East German People's Assembly held its eleventh meeting this morning. After two rounds of voting, Günter Mahlisch, chairman of the East German Democratic Farmers' Party, was elected as the chairman of the People's Assembly.

### 民德人民议院选出新主席
许宏治  江建国
1989-11-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德人民议院选出新主席
    本报柏林11月13日电　记者许宏治、江建国报道：第九届民主德国人民议院今天上午举行第十一次会议，经过两轮投票，德国民主农民党主席京特·马洛伊达当选为人民议院主席。
