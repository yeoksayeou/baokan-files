Title: Rajiv Gandhi Issues Election Statement
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 3

Rajiv Gandhi, the chairman of the Indian National Congress (I) and the Prime Minister, today issued an election statement at a press conference. He said that in the past five years, the Indian National Congress (I) has overcome difficulties and ensured the country's integrity, independence, unity, and security.

The statement said that the Indian National Congress (I) has made significant progress in economic growth and technological development.

Rajiv Gandhi launched the nationwide election campaign for the Indian National Congress (I) on November 3.

Voting will be held on November 22, 24, and 26 in 527 constituencies.

### 拉·甘地发表竞选声明

1989-11-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    拉·甘地发表竞选声明
    新华社新德里11月6日电　印度国大党（英）主席和政府总理拉·甘地今天在新闻发布会上发表的竞选声明说，国大党（英）在5年里克服困难，保证了国家的完整、独立、团结和安全。
    声明说，国大党（英）在经济增长、技术发展方面使国家取得了大踏步的进展。
    拉·甘地在11月3日发动了国大党（英）的全国性竞选运动。
    投票将于11月22日、24日和26日在527个选区举行。
