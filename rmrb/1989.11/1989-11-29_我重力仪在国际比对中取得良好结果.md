Title: China's Gravimeter Achieves Good Results in International Comparison
Author: Guo Jinghua, Sun Xiaojian
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 4

In the third international comparison of absolute gravimeters, China's gravimetric instrument obtained the first phase of data on November 26, with preliminary results being good.

This comparison was organized by the International Association of Geodesy and began on November 15 at the International Bureau of Weights and Measures in Paris. It will end on December 5. Participating countries brought their advanced gravimetric instruments to measure the same spatial height on Earth, converting the measurements to the ground level. By comparing the data obtained, the measurement errors can be determined, which helps to verify the accuracy of the instruments. In the comparisons held in 1981 and 1985, the gravimeters developed by China produced data that were on par with the world's advanced level.

The international comparison of gravimeters can create conditions for establishing an international high-precision absolute gravity standard network, which is of great significance for the further research in aerospace, geology, seismology, exploration, and geophysics. With the development of new technologies such as lasers, neutrons, and computers, scientists have developed high-precision, small, and portable absolute gravimeters, with measurement accuracies reaching a few microgals.

Currently, the countries that have independently developed absolute gravimeters include the United States, the Soviet Union, the International Bureau of Weights and Measures (France), Italy, China, and Japan.

A delegation of five from the China Academy of Metrology arrived in Paris on November 13 to participate in this scientific activity.

### 我重力仪在国际比对中取得良好结果
郭京花　孙小健
1989-11-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我重力仪在国际比对中取得良好结果
    新华社巴黎11月27日电　（记者郭京花、孙小健）在国际绝对重力仪第3次比对中，中国的重力测量仪器已于26日取得第一阶段数据，初步结果良好。
    这次比对是由国际大地测量协会组织的，于11月15日在巴黎国际计量局开始，将于12月5日结束。参加国携带本国先进重力测量仪器，在地球的同一个地方测量空间高度，归算到地面，通过各自取得的数据，得出比测值误差，用以检验仪器的精确度。在1981年和1985年的两次比对中，我国研制的重力仪得出的数据都在世界先进水平之列。
    国际重力仪比对可为建立国际高精度绝对重力标准网创造条件，对航天、地质、地震、勘探、地球物理等学科的进一步研究具有重要意义。随着激光、中子、计算机等新技术的发展，科学家已研制出高精度的小型可移动绝对重力仪，其测量准确度可达几微克。
    目前世界上独立研制出绝对重力仪的国家只有美国、苏联、国际计量局（法国）、意大利、中国和日本。
    中国计量科学院一行5人于11月13日来到巴黎，参加这次科学活动。
