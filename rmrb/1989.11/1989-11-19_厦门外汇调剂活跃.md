Title: Xiamen's Foreign Exchange Adjustment Market Thrives
Author: Liú Guózhù
Date: 1989-11-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xiamen, November 18th.

The Xiamen Foreign Exchange Adjustment Center has seen a 50% year-on-year increase in its foreign exchange transaction volume since its establishment three years ago. From January to October this year, the center facilitated $380 million in foreign exchange transactions.

### 厦门外汇调剂活跃
刘国柱
1989-11-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    厦门外汇调剂活跃
    新华社厦门11月18日电  （记者刘国柱）厦门外汇调剂中心成立3年来，调剂外汇成交额每年递增50％。今年1至10月，这个中心共调剂外汇3.8亿美元。
