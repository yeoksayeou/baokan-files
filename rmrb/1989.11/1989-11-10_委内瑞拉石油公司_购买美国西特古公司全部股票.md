Title: Venezuelan Oil Company Purchases All Shares of U.S. Citgo Company
Author: Zhū Mǎn Tíng
Date: 1989-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, November 7th.

The Venezuelan Oil Company announced yesterday that it has reached a preliminary agreement with the Sulfand Company, which holds the remaining shares, to purchase the other half of the shares of the U.S. Citgo Oil Company. The Venezuelan Oil Company already owned half of the shares of this company in 1986.

The Venezuelan Oil Company will purchase the shares of the U.S. oil company through installment payments, thus avoiding the use of national foreign exchange. The purchase of this U.S. oil company is another step in Venezuela's oil "internationalization" strategy.

### 委内瑞拉石油公司  购买美国西特古公司全部股票
朱满庭
1989-11-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    委内瑞拉石油公司
    购买美国西特古公司全部股票
    本报加拉加斯11月7日电　记者朱满庭报道：委内瑞拉石油公司昨天宣布已就购买美国西特古石油公司的另一半股票，与持有这些股票的索斯兰德公司达成原则协议。委内瑞拉石油公司在1986年已拥有这家公司的一半股份。
    委内瑞拉石油公司购买西特古公司股份是通过分期付款方式支付的，这样，就无需动用国家外汇。购买这家美国石油公司是委内瑞拉石油“国际化”战略的又一进展。
