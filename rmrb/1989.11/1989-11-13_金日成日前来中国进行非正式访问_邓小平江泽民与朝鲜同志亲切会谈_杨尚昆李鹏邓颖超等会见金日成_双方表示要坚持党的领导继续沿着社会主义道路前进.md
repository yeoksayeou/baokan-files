Title: Kim Il-sung Makes an Informal Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 1

Kim Il-sung recently came to China for an informal visit. Deng Xiaoping and Jiang Zemin held cordial talks with the Korean comrades. Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Deng Yingchao, and others met with Kim Il-sung. Both sides expressed their commitment to upholding party leadership and continuing to advance along the socialist path.

BEIJING, Nov. 13 (Xinhua) — The People's Daily learned today from the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee that at the invitation of the CPC Central Committee, Kim Il-sung, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Chairman of the State Affairs Commission, paid an informal visit to China from November 5 to 7.

Upon his arrival in Beijing, Deng Xiaoping went to the station to welcome him. On November 6, Deng Xiaoping held talks with Kim Il-sung and had lunch together. The two old friends met in a particularly warm atmosphere. Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Premier Li Peng, and other leaders participated in the welcoming and sending-off ceremonies.

Jiang Zemin hosted a banquet in honor of Kim Il-sung and held talks with him.

Li Peng met with Kim Il-sung.

Yang Shangkun met with and saw off Kim Il-sung.

Deng Yingchao met with Kim Il-sung at her residence.

Other leaders, including members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee such as Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, Song Ping, and Li Ruihuan, participated in the above activities.

During the talks and meetings, both sides briefed each other on their domestic situations and exchanged views on further developing friendly relations between the two parties and countries and other issues of common concern, reaching a consensus and further deepening mutual understanding and friendly cooperation between the two parties and countries. Both sides expressed their commitment to upholding party leadership and continuing to advance along the socialist path. The Chinese side firmly supports the struggle of the Workers' Party of Korea, the government, and the Korean people for national independence, peace, and reunification, and for easing tensions on the Korean Peninsula. The Korean side firmly supports the struggle of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people to uphold the Four Cardinal Principles, carry out reform and opening-up, and build socialism with Chinese characteristics. Both sides are satisfied with the good relations between China and Korea and expressed their commitment to continuing to consolidate and develop this friendly cooperative relationship.

The talks and meetings were conducted in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

Accompanying Kim Il-sung on his visit were Kim Yong-nam, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Kim Yong-sun, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Party Central Committee, among others.

### 金日成日前来中国进行非正式访问  邓小平江泽民与朝鲜同志亲切会谈  杨尚昆李鹏邓颖超等会见金日成  双方表示要坚持党的领导继续沿着社会主义道路前进

1989-11-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    金日成日前来中国进行非正式访问
    邓小平江泽民与朝鲜同志亲切会谈
    杨尚昆李鹏邓颖超等会见金日成
    双方表示要坚持党的领导继续沿着社会主义道路前进
    本报北京11月13日讯　本报记者今天从中联部获悉，应中共中央邀请，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记、国家主席金日成同志于11月5日至7日对我国进行了非正式访问。
    金日成同志抵京时，邓小平同志前往车站迎接，6日同金日成同志举行了会谈，并共进午餐。两位老朋友相会，格外亲切。江泽民总书记、李鹏总理等领导同志参加了迎送。
    江泽民同志主持了欢迎金日成同志的宴会，并同金日成同志进行了会谈。
    李鹏同志会见了金日成同志。
    杨尚昆同志会见并为金日成同志饯行。
    邓颖超同志在住处亲切会见了金日成同志。
    中共中央政治局常委乔石、姚依林、宋平、李瑞环同志和其他领导同志参加了上述活动。
    在会谈和会见中，双方各自通报了国内情况，并就进一步发展中朝两党、两国之间的友好关系和国际形势等共同关心的问题交换了意见，取得了一致的看法，进一步加深了中朝两党、两国之间的相互理解和友好合作关系。双方表示，要坚持党的领导，继续沿着社会主义道路前进。中方对朝鲜劳动党、政府和朝鲜人民为争取祖国自主和平统一、缓和朝鲜半岛局势而进行的斗争表示坚决支持；朝方对中国共产党和中国人民坚持四项基本原则、坚持改革开放，为建设具有中国特色的社会主义而进行的斗争表示坚决的支持。双方对中朝之间的良好关系感到满意，并表示要继续不断地巩固和发展这种友好合作关系。
    会谈和会见是在十分亲切、友好气氛中进行的。
    陪同金日成同志来访的有朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、政务院副总理兼外长金永南同志和朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、党中央书记许锬同志等。
