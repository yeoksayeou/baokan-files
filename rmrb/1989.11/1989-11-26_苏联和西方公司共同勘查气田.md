Title: Soviet Union and Western Companies Jointly Explore Gas Field
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

Finnish, Norwegian, and American companies signed an agreement on November 24 in Espoo, Finland, with representatives from the Soviet Union's petroleum industry to cooperate in exploring a natural gas field in the seabed of the Barents Sea.

According to reports, the Soviet Union recently discovered a large natural gas field in the western waters of the Barents Sea, with reserves estimated at approximately 300 billion cubic meters. Starting next year, these five Western companies will conduct feasibility studies on the development of this natural gas field.

### 苏联和西方公司共同勘查气田

1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联和西方公司共同勘查气田
    芬兰、挪威和美国的五家公司二十四日在芬兰的埃斯波同苏联石油工业部门的代表就合作勘查巴伦支海海底天然气田签订了意向性协定。
    据报道，苏联最近在巴伦支海西部水域发现了一个大型天然气田，储藏量大约为三万亿立方米。这五家西方公司从明年起将就开发这一天然气田进行可行性调查。
