Title: Direct Telephone Connection Between Hong Kong and North Korea Established
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 2

From today, the IDD direct international telephone service of the Hong Kong Telephone Company will be extended to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Currently, IDD direct international telephone calls from Hong Kong can reach approximately 200 countries and regions.

### 香港—朝鲜直通电话接通

1989-11-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    香港—朝鲜直通电话接通
    新华社香港11月1日电　从今天开始，香港电话公司的IDD直通国际电话服务将扩大至朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。目前从香港打出的IDD直通国际电话可通往约200个国家和地区。
