Title: OPEC's Proven Oil Reserves Increase
Author: Xinhua
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 7

According to the annual statistical report released by the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) on November 9, OPEC's proven oil reserves increased by 86.56 billion barrels in 1988, raising its share of the world's proven oil reserves from 76% in 1987 to 76.9%.

The report states that by the end of 1988, the world's proven oil reserves totaled 991.47 billion barrels, of which OPEC accounted for 762.8 billion barrels.

Saudi Arabia's proven oil reserves increased from 169.58 billion barrels in 1987 to 254.99 billion barrels in 1988, a rise of 50.4%.

The report also notes that by the end of 1988, the world's proven natural gas reserves totaled 116.64 trillion cubic meters, with OPEC holding 45.78 trillion cubic meters, accounting for 39.2% of the world's total reserves, up from 37.6% in 1987.

### 欧佩克探明的石油储量增加
辛华
1989-11-25
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

　　欧佩克探明的石油储量增加
　　辛华
    据石油输出国组织（欧佩克）秘书处11月9日发表的年度统计公报，1988年欧佩克探明的石油储量增加了865.6亿桶，使其在世界探明的石油储量中所占的份额由1987年的76％增加到76.9％。
    公报说，到1988年底止，世界探明的石油储量达9914.7亿桶。其中欧佩克占有7628亿桶。
    沙特阿拉伯探明的石油储量由1987年的1695.8亿桶增加到1988年的2549.9亿桶，增加了50.4％。
    公报说，到1988年底止，世界探明的天然气储量达116.64万亿立方米，欧佩克占有45.78万亿立方米，占世界总储量的39.2％，而1987年占37.6％。
