Title: Chinese Ambassador Meets U.S. Secretary of State
Author:
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 27th.

Chinese Ambassador to the United States, Zhu Qizhen, met with U.S. Secretary of State James Baker this morning at the U.S. State Department. The two sides had a frank exchange of views on international issues and Sino-U.S. relations. This was the first time Zhu Qizhen met with the U.S. Secretary of State since taking office.

### 我大使会见美国国务卿

1989-11-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我大使会见美国国务卿
    本报华盛顿11月27日电　中国驻美国大使朱启桢今天上午在美国国务院会见美国国务卿贝克，双方就国际问题和中美关系坦率地交换了意见。这是朱启桢大使就任以来首次会见美国国务卿。
