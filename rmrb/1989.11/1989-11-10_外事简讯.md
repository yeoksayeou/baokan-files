Title: Foreign Affairs News Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xi Zhongxun, hosted a reception and banquet on the evening of November 8 for a delegation from the Pan Africanist Congress of Azania led by Chairman Mlanbo.

	General Zhang Zhen, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, hosted a reception and banquet on November 8 for a friendly visit delegation from the Korean People's Army led by General Pak Ki-rye, Commander of the Corps.

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liao Hansheng, hosted a reception and banquet on the evening of November 8 for a delegation from the Argentinean Justice Party.

	Song Jian, State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, met with a delegation from the Indian Biotechnology Association led by Shri Vasista, Member of the Indian Planning Commission, in the afternoon of November 8 in Beijing.

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ni Zhifu, met with a delegation from the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea led by Vice Chairman Kim Yong-man on November 8.

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ni Zhifu, met with a delegation from the Pakistan Federation of Trade Unions led by Chairman Muhammad Sharif on the evening of November 8.

	(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋8日晚会见并宴请由姆兰博主席率领的阿扎尼亚泛非主义者大会代表团。
    ▲中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田上将8日会见并宴请了以军团司令官朴基瑞上将为团长的朝鲜人民军友好参观团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生8日晚会见并宴请了阿根廷正义党代表团。
    ▲国务委员、国家科委主任宋健8日下午在北京会见了以印度国家计委委员斯瑞瓦士塔为团长的印度生物技术代表团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、全国总工会主席倪志福8日会见了以副委员长金永男为首的朝鲜职业总同盟代表团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福8日晚会见了由主席穆罕默德·夏里夫率领的巴基斯坦全国工会联合会代表团。
　（据新华社）
