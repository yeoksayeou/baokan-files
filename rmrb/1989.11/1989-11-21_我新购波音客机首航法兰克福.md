Title: China's Newly Purchased Boeing Aircraft Makes Its First Flight to Frankfurt
Author: Jiang Jinguo
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 4

China's newly purchased Boeing 747-400 aircraft made its first international flight on November 7, landing at Frankfurt Airport in West Germany. The vice president and captain of China International Airlines, Li Changxin, told reporters that the company chose Frankfurt as its first international destination because it values cooperation with Western Europe and is confident in this partnership.

Representatives from Lufthansa, Frankfurt Airport authorities, and Boeing Aircraft Company were present to welcome the aircraft's arrival.

### 我新购波音客机首航法兰克福
江建国
1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我新购波音客机首航法兰克福
    本报波恩电　记者江建国报道：中国国际航空公司新购进的波音747—400型新式客机7日首次飞往国外，降落在西德法兰克福机场。该公司副总裁兼机长李长信对记者说，中国国际航空公司选择法兰克福作为它首航国外的第一站，是因为该公司重视与西欧的合作，对此充满信心。
    西德汉莎航空公司、法兰克福机场当局和波音飞机公司代表专门派代表迎接飞机到来。
