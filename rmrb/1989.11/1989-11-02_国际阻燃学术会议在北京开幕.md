Title: International Flame Retardant Academic Conference Opens in Beijing
Author: Jiang Jianke
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 2

The international flame retardant academic conference and exhibition, organized by the China Ordnance Society, opened today at the Beijing Library. This is the first large-scale international conference and exhibition in the field of flame retardancy held in China.

Flame retardant science and technology is the most effective means of preventing fires and has been widely valued by countries around the world in recent years. China's flame retardant industry has also made certain developments. Representatives from Japan, the Soviet Union, Poland, Italy, the United States, and ten other countries and regions, totaling 246, attended the conference.

### 国际阻燃学术会议在北京开幕
蒋建科
1989-11-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际阻燃学术会议在北京开幕
    本报北京十一月一日讯　由中国兵工学会举办的国际阻燃学术会议暨展览会，今天在北京图书馆开幕。这是我国在阻燃领域中首次举办的大型国际会议和展览会。
    阻燃科学技术是防止火灾的最有效手段，近几年得到世界各国的普遍重视。我国的阻燃事业也有一定的发展。来自日本、苏联、波兰、意大利、美国等十一个国家和地区的二百四十六名代表参加了会议。
                                        （蒋建科）
