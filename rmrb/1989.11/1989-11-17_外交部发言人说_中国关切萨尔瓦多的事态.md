Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs: China is Concerned About the Situation in El Salvador
Author:
Date: 1989-11-17
Edition: 1

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson said today that China is concerned about the recent developments in El Salvador. We hope that both sides will stop military actions and seek a political solution to El Salvador's domestic issues through peaceful negotiations, so that the people of El Salvador can live in peace and the peace process in Central America can continue to develop.

The spokesperson made these remarks in response to a reporter's question at the afternoon press conference today.

### 外交部发言人说  中国关切萨尔瓦多的事态

1989-11-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人说
    中国关切萨尔瓦多的事态
    本报北京11月16日讯　外交部发言人今天在这里说，中国对萨尔瓦多最近的事态发展表示关切，希望双方停止军事行动，通过和平谈判寻求萨尔瓦多国内问题的政治解决，以使萨尔瓦多人民得以在和平中生活，并使中美洲和平进程继续得到发展。
    这位发言人是在今天下午的新闻发布会上回答记者的提问时说这番话的。
