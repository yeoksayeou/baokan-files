Title: Jiangdu Establishes Pig, Poultry, and Fish Farming Production Line
Author: Chen Jing, Kang Xiawang
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 2

From: Four Corners

Jiangsu Province's first integrated pig, poultry, and fish vertical farming production line has recently been completed in Jiangdu County. This production line can annually raise 250 sows, breed 5,000 piglets, raise 80,000 poultry, and farm tens of thousands of fish. The entire production line utilizes advanced technology and intensive management models, with an investment of 800,000 yuan.

### 江都建成猪禽鱼养殖生产线
陈晶　康霞旺
1989-11-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    江都建成猪禽鱼养殖生产线
    本报讯　江苏省第一条猪、禽、鱼良性循环立体养殖工厂化生产线，最近在江都县建成。这条生产线，每年可饲养母猪250头，繁殖苗猪5000头，饲养家禽8万只，养鱼数十万尾。生产线全部采用先进工艺流程和集约化管模式，投资为80万元。　　　　　　（陈晶　康霞旺）
