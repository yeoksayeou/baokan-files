Title: The Best Endurance and Courage Competition to Be Held in Hawaii
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 4

The world's top endurance and courage competition will take place in late November on the island of Hawaii.

The competition will start with a 10-kilometer swim and a 145-kilometer bicycle race climbing 4,000 feet on the first day. On the second day, participants will continue with a 276-kilometer bicycle race climbing another 4,000 feet. On the third day, they will complete an 84-kilometer round-trip marathon.

### 最佳耐力和勇气比赛将在夏威夷进行

1989-11-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    最佳耐力和勇气比赛将在夏威夷进行
    新华社纽约11月6日电　世界最佳耐力和勇气比赛将于11月下旬在夏威夷岛进行。
    这项比赛，第一天将进行10公里游泳和145公里自行车爬高4000英尺比赛，第二天继续进行自行车276公里爬高4000英尺比赛，第三天还要进行长84公里的双程马拉松赛。
