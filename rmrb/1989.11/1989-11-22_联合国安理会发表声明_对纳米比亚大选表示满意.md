Title: The United Nations Security Council Issues a Statement Expressing Satisfaction with the Namibian Elections
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 4

The United Nations Security Council today issued a statement expressing satisfaction with the recent successful conclusion of the Namibian elections.

The Security Council's president for this month, China's Permanent Representative to the United Nations Li Luye, stated in the declaration that from November 7 to 11, under the supervision of the United Nations Transition Assistance Group, Namibia held "free and fair" elections, resulting in the formation of a constituent assembly led by the South West Africa People's Organization under the leadership of Nujoma. This has paved the way for Namibia to soon rid itself of South African colonial rule and achieve independence.

The statement calls on the constituent assembly to reflect the collective will of the people and, in accordance with United Nations Resolution 435 on Namibian independence, to draft and adopt a constitution that ensures Namibia's sovereignty without any external interference. It also calls for the determination of the powers of various levels of government and, in accordance with the United Nations' decision for Namibia to achieve independence by next April, to set a specific date for independence. The Security Council urges the United Nations Secretary-General to provide the necessary assistance and to continue efforts to ensure the full implementation of the independence plan.

### 联合国安理会发表声明　对纳米比亚大选表示满意
刘开宸
1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国安理会发表声明
　　对纳米比亚大选表示满意
    本报联合国11月20日电　记者刘开宸报道：联合国安理会今天发表一项声明，对纳米比亚大选最近顺利结束表示满意。
    安理会本月份主席、中国常驻联合国代表李鹿野在这项声明中说，11月7日至11日，纳米比亚在联合国过渡时期援助团的监督下，“自由和公正”地选出了以由努乔马领导的西南非洲人民组织为主的制宪议会，从而为纳米比亚早日摆脱南非殖民统治、获得独立铺平了道路。
    声明要求制宪议会反映人民的集体意志，按照联合国关于纳米比亚独立的435号决议，在不受任何外来干涉的情况下，拟订和通过一项使纳米比亚享有主权的宪法，决定政府各级权力机构，并按照联合国作出的纳米比亚于明年4月以前独立的决定，确定独立的具体日期。安理会要求联合国秘书长为此提供必要帮助，为确保纳独立计划的完全执行而继续努力。
