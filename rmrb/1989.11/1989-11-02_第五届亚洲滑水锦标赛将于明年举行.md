Title: The Fifth Asian Water Ski Championship to Be Held Next Year
Author: Hé Huìfēi
Date: 1989-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 1st.

The 1990 Asian-Pacific Water Ski Championship and the Fifth Asian Water Ski Championship will be held from May 24 to 31, 1990, in Zhaoqing City, Guangdong Province.

The International Water Ski Federation is divided into European, American, and Asian-Pacific regions. Every even year, the three regions hold championships. The Asian-Pacific region includes Australia, New Zealand, China, Japan, North Korea, Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, South Korea, Brunei, India, the Philippines, Chinese Taipei, and Hong Kong. Last November, China won the bid to host the 1990 Asian-Pacific Water Ski Championship, competing against Australia and New Zealand.

### 第五届亚洲滑水锦标赛将于明年举行
何惠飞
1989-11-02
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第五届亚洲滑水锦标赛将于明年举行
    新华社广州11月1日电  （记者何惠飞）1990年亚大区滑水锦标赛暨第五届亚洲滑水锦标赛定于1990年5月24日至31日在广东省肇庆市举行。
    国际滑水联合会分欧洲区、美洲区和亚澳区，凡逢双数年，3大区都要举行锦标赛。亚澳区会员有澳大利亚、新西兰、中国、日本、朝鲜、印度尼西亚、新加坡、马来西亚、南朝鲜、文莱、印度、菲律宾、中国台北和香港。去年11月，中国在和澳大利亚、新西兰的竞争中获得举办明年亚澳区滑水锦标赛的权利。
