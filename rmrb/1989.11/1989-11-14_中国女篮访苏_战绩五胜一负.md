Title: Chinese Women's Basketball Team Visits the Soviet Union with a Record of Five Wins and One Loss
Author: Zhang Zhiqiang
Date: 1989-11-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 12th.

The Chinese women's national basketball team today defeated the Soviet Central Army Sports Club women's basketball team 73:69 in Moscow, concluding their tour of the Soviet Union with a record of five wins and one loss.

The Chinese women's basketball team played three matches in Moscow. In the first match on November 10th, they narrowly defeated the stronger Central Army Sports Club team 81:80. In the second match, they easily defeated the Moscow Dynamo team 110:81.

### 中国女篮访苏  战绩五胜一负
张志强
1989-11-14
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中国女篮访苏  战绩五胜一负
    新华社莫斯科11月12日电　（张志强）中国国家女子篮球队今天在这里以73∶69再次战胜苏联中央陆军体育俱乐部女子篮球队，从而以5胜1负的成绩结束了在苏联的比赛。
    中国女篮在莫斯科打了3场比赛，在10日进行的第一场比赛中以81∶80险胜实力较强的中央陆军体育俱乐部队，在第二场比赛中又以110∶81轻松地击败了莫斯科狄纳莫队。
