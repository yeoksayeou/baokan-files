Title: European and American Countries Peddling Weapons to Japan
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Page: 4

European and American countries are seizing the opportunity of Japan's formulation of its next defense expansion plan to rush to Japan to peddle weapons.

According to the Jiji Press, citing a Japanese Defense Agency official, since this summer, the number of Western European arms dealers coming to Japan to sign contracts with Japanese trading companies has increased sharply. Their weapon catalogs include "everything except nuclear weapons."

The Japanese Defense Agency official said that the United States is even more aggressive in selling weapons.

### 欧美国家向日本兜售武器

1989-11-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    欧美国家向日本兜售武器
    欧美国家抓住日本制订下期扩充防卫力量计划的机会，竞相到日本兜售武器。
    据时事社援引日本防卫厅人士的话说，自今年夏季起，前来同日本商社签订合同的西欧国家军火商急剧增加。在他们的武器目录中，“除核武器外一应俱全”。
    日本防卫厅人士说，美国在兜售武器方面，攻势比西欧更猛。
