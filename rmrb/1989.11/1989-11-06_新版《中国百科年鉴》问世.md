Title: The New Edition of the "China Yearbook of Knowledge" is Published
Author: Chen Xiande Xiao Guanggen
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 4

The 1989 edition of the "China Yearbook of Knowledge" has been published in Shanghai. The book contains over 18 million characters, marking the tenth anniversary of the first comprehensive, annual, large-scale reference book in our country.

### 新版《中国百科年鉴》问世
陈贤德　肖关根
1989-11-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    新版《中国百科年鉴》问世
    本报讯　一九八九年版《中国百科年鉴》在上海问世，全书共一千八百余万字。这标志着我国第一部综合性、编年性大型工具书已诞生十个春秋。　　　（陈贤德　肖关根）
