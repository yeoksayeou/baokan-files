Title: Guangxi Implements a Series of Family Planning Insurance Policies
Author: Huang Hongbo
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 3

	Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region's Family Planning Commission and the Autonomous Region Insurance Company have collaborated to promote a series of family planning insurance measures across the entire region. These measures include insurance for only children, old-age insurance for parents of only children, and old-age insurance for full-time family planning workers in rural areas. The insurance premiums are to be jointly borne by the local government, the family planning department, and the insured parties, with each party contributing one-third of the total cost.

### 广西开办计划生育系列保险
黄洪波
1989-11-25
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　广西开办计划生育系列保险
    据新华社南宁11月24日电　（通讯员黄洪波）广西壮族自治区计划生育委员会和自治区保险公司合作，向全自治区推行计划生育系列保险办法，其中包括独生子女保险、独生子女父母养老保险和乡村计划生育工作专职干部养老保险。保险金原则上由地方财政、计划生育部门和投保人三方各分担一点。
