Title: Krenz Meets with Chairman of the German Communist Party, Says Two Germanys' Existence Aids European Stability
Author:
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 27th.

East German party and state leader Krenz met with the chairman of the German Communist Party of West Germany, Herbert Mies, who was visiting, and pointed out that the issue currently on the agenda is not the reunification of the two Germanys, but rather the need to strengthen the two Germanys' sense of responsibility in ensuring peace, promoting cooperation, and developing good-neighborly relations.

In discussing the relationship between East Germany and West Germany, Krenz emphasized that the existence of the two Germanys helps stabilize the European peace order. Both sides agreed to uphold the glorious anti-fascist tradition and jointly work to prevent war and the resurgence of fascism on German soil.

On the same day, the East German newspaper *Neues Deutschland* published a commentary stating that during the reform process, East Germany welcomes well-intentioned suggestions but will not allow anyone to interfere.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 27th.

According to the Deutsche Presse-Agentur, East German Council of Ministers Chairman Modrow met with the chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, Kimmel, on the 27th. Both sides agreed that stabilizing the economy, improving production efficiency, and ensuring social stability in East Germany require the joint efforts of the entire nation.

In discussing how to help East Germany out of its current situation, Modrow and Kimmel pointed out that it is now necessary for all East German workers to work together diligently, maintain the achievements already made, and fundamentally strengthen the national economy.

Both sides also discussed the necessity of implementing socialist distribution according to work, improving workers' production and living conditions, and other measures to stabilize the economy.

### 克伦茨会见德国的共产党主席时说　两个德国的存在有助于欧洲稳定

1989-11-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　克伦茨会见德国的共产党主席时说
　　两个德国的存在有助于欧洲稳定
    新华社柏林11月27日电　民主德国党和国家领导人克伦茨27日在这里会见前来访问的联邦德国德国的共产党主席赫伯特·米斯时指出，现在提到日程上的不是两个德国重新统一的问题，而是要增强两个德国对确保和平、加强合作、发展睦邻关系的责任感。
    在谈到民主德国同联邦德国的关系时，克伦茨强调指出，两个德国的存在有助于欧洲和平秩序的稳定。双方一致表示，将继承反法西斯的光荣传统，共同致力于在德国土地上防止战争、不再出现法西斯主义。
    同一天，民主德国《新德意志报》发表评论说，民主德国在改革过程中欢迎善意的建议，但决不允许任何人来指手划脚。
    新华社柏林11月27日电　据德通社报道，民主德国部长会议主席莫德罗27日在这里同自由德国工会联合会主席基梅尔举行会晤。双方一致认为，稳定经济、提高生产效率、确保民主德国的社会安宁需要全国人民的共同努力。
    在谈到如何使民主德国摆脱目前的状况时，莫德罗和基梅尔明确指出，现在需要民德全体劳动人民的共同艰苦劳动，保持已取得的成绩，从根本上来增强国民经济的实力。
    双方还谈到为稳定经济，实行社会主义的按劳分配原则、改善工人的生产、生活条件等措施的必要性。
