Title: A Batch of Color TVs Rated as Trustworthy Products
Author: Lǐ Gēngchén
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 2

From: Four Corners

    A batch of color TVs rated as trustworthy products

    A recent announcement revealed the results of the second series of the "National Survey and Evaluation of Home Appliance Product Quality." Among the evaluated products, 30 brands of color TVs, including Changhong, Mudan, Nanbao, and Taishan, were rated as trustworthy products by consumers.

    This activity was based on consumer feedback from survey forms, focusing on two key indicators: the reliability of the TVs and the clarity of the images. The final scores determined the top 30 products.

### 一批彩电被评为信任产品
李庚辰
1989-11-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    一批彩电被评为信任产品
    本报讯　“全国家电产品质量系列调查评议”活动第二系列最近揭晓，长虹、牡丹、南宝、泰山等30种牌号的彩电，被消费者评为信任产品。
    这次活动根据消费者填写的调查表，统计出电视机可靠性和图像清晰度两项指标的得分，最后产生分数较高的产品30个。　（李庚辰）
