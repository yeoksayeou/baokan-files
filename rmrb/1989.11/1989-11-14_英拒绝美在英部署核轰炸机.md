Title: Britain Refuses U.S. Request to Deploy Nuclear Bombers
Author:
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 4

A London newspaper reported on the 12th that British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher has refused the U.S. plan to deploy B-52 nuclear bombers in Britain.

The newspaper said that Thatcher had previously supported the U.S. deployment plan, and her refusal indicates a significant change in Britain's stance on the U.S. nuclear "modernization" plan.

The newspaper also stated that the British government has expressed its support for the U.S. nuclear "modernization" plan in West Germany.

### 英拒绝美在英部署核轰炸机

1989-11-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英拒绝美在英部署核轰炸机
    据伦敦一家报纸十二日报道，英国首相撒切尔夫人已拒绝美国计划在英国部署B—52载核轰炸机的要求。
    这家报纸说，撒切尔夫人原先一直支持美国的这一部署计划，她的拒绝表明英国在美国核“现代化”计划问题上的立场发生了很大变化。
    这家报纸还说，英国政府同时表示，如果美国在西德搞核“现代化”计划，英国将予以支持。
