Title: Xi'an Jiaotong University Associate Professor Zheng Nanning
Author: Zhang Yaxin
Date: 1989-11-11
Page: 5

Zheng Nanning, an associate professor at Xi'an Jiaotong University, obtained his master's degree from Keio University in Japan in 1985. After returning to China, he has made significant research achievements in the fields of pattern recognition and artificial intelligence, earning him the National Science and Technology Progress Award. The photograph shows Zheng Nanning (first from the left) and research personnel conducting thermal imaging of a candle.
