Author: Yin Keshan
Date: 1989-11-12
Edition: 2

Tianjin Completes Circular Green Belt Around the City

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 10th (Reporter Yin Keshan) Tianjin has completed a circular green belt along the outer ring road. This green belt is currently the largest in scale nationwide.

The green belt has a circumference of 73 kilometers and a width of 500 meters, covering an area of 50,000 mu. There are three main tree belts: two along the inner and outer edges and one in the middle, each consisting of poplar, white wax trees, and shrubs, with a width ranging from 10 to 20 meters. Additionally, there are secondary tree belts every 400 meters, each with two rows of trees. Between the intersecting tree belts are orchards planted with apples, grapes, red fruits, peaches, pears, apricots, and plums, as well as fish ponds shimmering with water.

### 天津建成环城绿化带
殷可善
1989-11-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    天津建成环城绿化带
    据新华社天津11月10日电 （记者殷可善）天津市沿着外环公路外侧建成了一圈目前全国规模最大的环城绿化带。
    这圈绿化带周长73公里，宽500米，占地面积达5万亩。其纵向内外两个边和居中部位，各有一条由毛白杨、白蜡和灌木组成的宽10米至20米的主林带，其横向每隔400米有一条长着两行树木的副林带。在纵横交错的林网中间，是一片片栽着苹果、葡萄、红果、桃、梨、杏、李的果园和波光粼粼的养鱼池。
