Title: The Pacific Region Automotive Engineering Annual Conference Held in Beijing
Author: liú guóshèng
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 2

The fifth Pacific Region Automotive Engineering Annual Conference was held in Beijing as scheduled today.

State Councilor Zou Jiahuai attended the opening ceremony.

In his speech, he said: China has a broad interest in automotive development technology and is willing to cooperate with all countries, especially those in the Pacific region.

This year's conference attracted more than 300 representatives from the United States, France, West Germany, the Soviet Union, Australia, Czechoslovakia, Japan, Singapore, China, and other countries and regions. A total of 166 papers were exchanged.

### 太平洋地区汽车工程年会在京召开
刘国胜
1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    太平洋地区汽车工程年会在京召开
    本报北京11月6日讯　记者刘国胜报道：第五届国际太平洋地区汽车工程年会今天在北京如期举行。
    国务委员邹家华出席了开幕式。
    他在讲话中说：中国对汽车发展技术有广泛兴趣，愿同各国特别是太平洋地区国家进行合作。
    本届年会有来自美国、法国、西德、苏联，澳大利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、日本、新加坡、中国等国家和地区的300多名代表参加，交流论文共166篇。
