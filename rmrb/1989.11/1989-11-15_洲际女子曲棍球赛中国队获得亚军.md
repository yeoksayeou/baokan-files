Title: Chinese Women's Hockey Team Secures Second Place in Intercontinental Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-11-15
Edition: 3

The defending Asian Games champion, South Korea, won the championship of the third Intercontinental Women's Hockey Tournament by defeating China 2-1 in the final today. China took second place.

In today's match, both teams competed fiercely. The score was tied 0-0 at halftime. All three goals were scored in the second half.

In the match for third place today, Spain defeated Japan 4-0 to secure third place in the tournament. Japan finished fourth.

The top four teams have qualified to participate in the World Women's Hockey Tournament to be held in Sydney, Australia, next year.

A total of 12 teams participated in this tournament.

### 洲际女子曲棍球赛中国队获得亚军

1989-11-15
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    洲际女子曲棍球赛中国队获得亚军
    据新华社新德里11月12日电　上届亚运会冠军南朝鲜队在今天的决赛中，以2∶1战胜中国队，夺得第三届洲际女子曲棍球赛冠军，中国队名列第二。
    在今天的比赛中，双方队员拚抢积极，上半场结束时以0比0战平，这场比赛的3个球均为下半场攻入的。
    在今天争夺第三名的比赛中，西班牙队以4∶0胜日本队获得本届比赛第三名。日本队名列第四。
    前四名获得者取得了参加明年在澳大利亚悉尼市举行的世界女子曲棍球赛的资格。
    共有12个队参加了本届大赛。
