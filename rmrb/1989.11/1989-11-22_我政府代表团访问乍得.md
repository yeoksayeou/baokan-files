Title: Chinese Government Delegation Visits Chad
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, N'Djamena, November 18th.

A Chinese government delegation led by Li Tieying, State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission, concluded a four-day friendly visit to Chad today. In the afternoon, the delegation departed for the Central African Republic.

Chad's Minister of State, Djidingar Dono Ngardoum, and the Executive Secretary of the National Union for Independence and Revolution, Yusufu Xidi, along with China's Ambassador to Chad, Zhou Zhendong, were present at the airport to see them off.

During the visit, Chad's President Habré and the Executive Secretary of the National Union for Independence and Revolution, Yusufu Xidi, met with Li Tieying separately. Both sides expressed their hope to further strengthen friendly relations between China and Chad, and between the two parties.

The Chinese government delegation arrived here on the 15th.

### 我政府代表团访问乍得

1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我政府代表团访问乍得
    新华社恩贾梅纳11月18日电　由国务委员兼国家教育委员会主任李铁映率领的中国政府代表团结束了对乍得为期4天的友好访问，今天下午离开这里，前往中非共和国访问。
    乍得国务部长吉丁加尔·多诺·恩加杜姆和全国独立和革命联盟执行局执行书记优素福·西迪以及中国驻乍得大使周振东等前往机场送行。
    访问期间，乍得总统哈布雷和全国独立和革命联盟执行局执行书记优素福·西迪分别会见了李铁映，双方表示希望进一步加强中乍两国和两党之间的友好关系。
    中国政府代表团是15日抵达这里的。
