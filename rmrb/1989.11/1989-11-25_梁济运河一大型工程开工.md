Title: Construction Begins on Major Project: Liang-Ji Canal
Author: Xú Dōng Xú Jié
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 2

From the columns: News from All Directions

On November 15th, 100,000 workers from Jining City, Shandong Province, began work on the largest water conservancy project in Shandong this year: the Liang-Ji Canal dredging and embankment flood control project. The Liang-Ji Canal is the section of the Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal from Liangshan to the southern entrance of the Four Lakes in Jining. It is 87.8 kilometers long and serves as the main waterway for the northern part of the Four Lakes in the Huai River Basin. This project has comprehensive benefits including flood control, drainage, irrigation, and navigation. The total investment is 108 million yuan, to be completed in three years. The first phase of the project, with an investment of 30 million yuan, is expected to be completed by the end of this year.

### 梁济运河一大型工程开工
徐东　徐洁
1989-11-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　梁济运河一大型工程开工
    本报讯　11月15日，山东济宁市10万名民工上阵，参加今年山东省最大的一项水利工程——梁济运河挖河筑堤泄洪工程。梁济运河为京杭大运河梁山至济宁南四湖入湖口的一段，全长87.8公里，是淮河流域南四湖北部的骨干河道。这次上马的工程具有防洪、排涝、灌溉、航运等综合效益，总投资1.08亿元，分3年完成。第一期工程投资3000万元，预计今年年底竣工。
        （徐东　徐洁）
