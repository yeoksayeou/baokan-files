Title: Price Inspection Teams Appear in Shijiazhuang
Author: Yu ShaoLiang Yu Xing
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 2

In the streets of Shijiazhuang, the capital of Hebei Province, a market price inspection team composed of price management personnel has recently appeared, drawing the attention and welcome of the local residents.

This market price inspection team starts work every day by patrolling their respective areas, focusing on checking the prices of daily necessities such as vegetables, pork, steamed buns, cigarettes, and cold drinks. They are always ready to accept reports from the public.

### 石家庄出现物价巡检队
于绍良　于兴
1989-11-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　石家庄出现物价巡检队
    新华社石家庄11月28日电　（记者于绍良、通讯员于兴）在河北省省会石家庄市街头，近来出现了一支由物价人员组成的市场物价巡检队，引起了市民的关注和欢迎。
    这支市场物价巡检队每天一上班，就在各自辖区来回巡视，对蔬菜、猪肉、馒头、香烟、冷饮等人民生活必需品的价格进行重点检查，随时随地接受群众举报。
