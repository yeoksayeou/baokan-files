Title: The Location of the U.S.-Soviet Summit is Confirmed to be on Warships in the Waters Near Malta
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 3

The White House spokesman, Fitzwater, said today that the leaders of the United States and the Soviet Union have decided to hold their summit meeting on warships in the waters near Malta.

Fitzwater said that an advance team from the United States will depart for Malta tomorrow to prepare for the logistics, security, and communications for the meeting between President Bush and Gorbachev.

Bush and Gorbachev will meet on December 2 and 3 on the U.S. Sixth Fleet flagship, the missile cruiser "Belknap," and the Soviet fleet's missile cruiser "Slava," respectively, to discuss bilateral economic issues, East-West relations, and global hotspots.

### 美苏首脑会晤地点确定在马耳他附近海域的军舰上

1989-11-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏首脑会晤地点确定在马耳他附近海域的军舰上
    新华社华盛顿11月1日电  白宫发言人菲茨沃特今天说，美苏两国领导人决定在马耳他附近海域的军舰上举行首脑会谈。
    菲茨沃特说，一支美国先遣队将于明日前往马耳他，为布什总统和戈尔巴乔夫的会晤作后勤、安全和通讯等方面的准备。
    布什和戈尔巴乔夫将于12月2日和3日分别在美国第六舰队旗舰“贝尔纳普”号导弹巡洋舰上和苏联舰队的“光荣”号导弹巡洋舰上会晤，讨论双边经济、东西方关系和全球的热点问题。
