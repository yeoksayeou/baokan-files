Title: A Guangzhou Police Officer Executed for Bribery
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 3

A Guangzhou police officer was executed for accepting bribes.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 21 - A police officer from the Guangzhou Public Security Bureau, Zhang Mayun, was executed today for the crime of accepting bribes.

Zhang Mayun was originally a household registration police officer at the Daxinan Street Police Station of the Yuexiu District Public Security Sub-bureau. From 1984 to June 1988, he colluded with others to illegally register 60 rural residents as Guangzhou city residents. They collectively accepted bribes totaling over 280,000 yuan and squandered the money together.

In addition, between July and August 1985, Zhang Mayun also illegally registered three rural residents as Guangzhou city residents, accepting bribes totaling 1,600 yuan.

### 广州一民警因受贿被处决

1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　广州一民警因受贿被处决
    据新华社广州11月21日电　广州市公安局户籍民警张玛云因犯受贿罪，今天被依法处决。
    张玛云原为越秀区公安分局大南街派出所户籍民警，从1984年至1988年6月，和他人勾结，先后非法为60名农村人口办理了广州市户籍。他们总共收取贿赂28万多元，共同挥霍。
    此外，1985年7、8月间，张玛云还非法为3名农村人员办理了广州市户口手续，收受贿赂1600元。
