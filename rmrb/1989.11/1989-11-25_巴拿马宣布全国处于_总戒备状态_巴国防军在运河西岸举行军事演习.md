Title: Panama Declares Nationwide State of "Total Alert"
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 4

Panama declares nationwide state of "total alert"
Panama Defense Forces hold military exercises on the west bank of the canal

	BEIJING, Nov. 23 (Xinhua) -- The National Assembly of Panama yesterday declared the country to be in a state of "total alert." The assembly stated that the U.S. government should be held responsible for any attempts to harm Noriega; it declared that if Noriega were to meet with any misfortune, Panama would not be responsible for the safety of U.S. citizens within or outside its borders. It is reported that the assembly made this announcement based on rumors in the U.S. media that Bush had authorized the CIA to allocate $3 million for secret activities aimed at "eliminating Noriega."

	PANAMA CITY, Nov. 23 (Xinhua) -- The Panamanian Defense Forces held a large-scale military exercise on the west bank of the Panama Canal today to protect this inter-oceanic waterway until the United States hands over sovereignty of the canal to Panama as scheduled.

### 巴拿马宣布全国处于“总戒备状态”　巴国防军在运河西岸举行军事演习
郭伟成
1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴拿马宣布全国处于“总戒备状态”
　　巴国防军在运河西岸举行军事演习
    本报墨西哥城11月23日电　记者郭伟成报道：昨天召开的巴拿马全国民众代表大会宣布全国处于“总戒备状态”。大会声明，要求美国政府对一切旨在谋害诺列加的企图负责；声称如果诺列加遇有不测，巴拿马将不会对其境内或境外美国公民的安全负责。据悉，代表大会作出上述宣布是基于美国报界传闻布什授权向中央情报局拨款300万美元用于“干掉诺列加”的秘密活动。
    新华社巴拿马城11月23日电　巴拿马国防军23日在巴拿马运河西岸举行了一次大规模军事演习，以保护这条洋际通道直至美国按期把运河主权归还巴马拿为止。
