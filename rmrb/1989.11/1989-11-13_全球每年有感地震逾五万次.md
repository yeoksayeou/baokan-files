Title: Over 50,000 Earthquakes Felt Globally Each Year
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 4

	According to statistics from seismologists, more than 56,000 earthquakes are felt each year around the globe, averaging over 150 earthquakes per day.

	Despite the frequent occurrence of earthquakes each year, the number of major and strong earthquakes is relatively low. According to seismological experts, earthquakes of magnitude 8 and above occur 2 to 3 times per year on average; earthquakes of magnitude 7 to 7.9 occur 18 times per year on average; earthquakes of magnitude 6 to 6.9 occur 150 times per year on average; earthquakes of magnitude 5 to 5.9 occur 800 times per year on average; earthquakes of magnitude 4 to 4.9 occur 6,200 times per year on average; and earthquakes of magnitude 3 to 3.9 occur approximately 49,000 times per year.

### 全球每年有感地震逾五万次

1989-11-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    全球每年有感地震逾五万次
    新华社波恩电　据德新社援引地震学家统计的数字，每年地球上约发生5.6万多次有感地震，平均每天达150余次。
    但是，尽管每年地震频仍，然而大地震和强地震发生的次数并不多。据地震专家统计，8级和8级以上的大地震每年平均为2至3次；7至7.9级的强地震每年平均为18次；6至6.9级地震每年平均为150次；5至5.9级地震每年平均为800次；4至4.9级地震每年平均为6200次；3至3.9级地震平均每年为4.9万次。
