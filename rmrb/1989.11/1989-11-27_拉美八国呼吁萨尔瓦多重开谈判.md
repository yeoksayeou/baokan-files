Title: Eight Latin American Countries Urge El Salvador to Resume Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1989-11-27
Edition: 4

Eight Latin American countries have called on El Salvador to resume negotiations.

A statement issued by the eight-nation group on November 24 urged the warring parties in El Salvador to resolve their conflict peacefully.

A statement read by Peruvian Prime Minister Larco in Lima said that the governments of Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Mexico, Peru, Uruguay, and Venezuela called on the government of El Salvador and the guerrillas to immediately cease hostilities and resume talks. The statement said that this is the only way to resolve the crisis in El Salvador.

### 拉美八国呼吁萨尔瓦多重开谈判

1989-11-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　拉美八国呼吁萨尔瓦多重开谈判
    拉丁美洲八国集团二十四日发表公报，呼吁萨尔瓦多交战双方和平解决冲突。
    由秘鲁总理拉尔科在利马宣读的一份公报说，阿根廷、巴西、哥伦比亚、墨西哥、秘鲁、乌拉圭和委内瑞拉的政府呼吁萨尔瓦多政府和游击队立即停止敌对行动，重开谈判。公报认为，这是解决萨尔瓦多危机的唯一途径。
