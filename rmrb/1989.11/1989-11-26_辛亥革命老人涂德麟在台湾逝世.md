Title: Revolutionary Elder of the Xinhai Revolution, Tu Delin, Passes Away in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 2

Revolutionary elder of the Xinhai Revolution, Tu Delin, passes away in Taiwan.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, November 24 – Taipei news: Revolutionary comrade Tu Delin, who participated in the Wuchang Uprising of the Xinhai Revolution, recently passed away in Taiwan due to illness. He was 95 years old.

	Tu Delin, originally from Huangpi County, Hubei Province, was born in 1894. At the age of 17, he took up arms. During the Wuchang Uprising, he participated in the defense of the Wuchang Changjie Fan Library and the official money bureau.

	Tu Delin was the last surviving participant of the Wuchang Uprising in Taiwan. He was hospitalized on November 15 due to health issues and passed away due to old age.

### 辛亥革命老人涂德麟在台湾逝世

1989-11-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　辛亥革命老人涂德麟在台湾逝世
    新华社北京11月24日电　台北消息：曾参加辛亥革命武昌起义的革命志士涂德麟老先生日前在台湾病逝。终年95岁。
    涂德麟祖籍湖北省黄陂县，1894年出生。17岁时投笔从戎，武昌起义时，他参加了固守武昌长街藩库及官钱局的战斗。
    涂德麟是当年参加武昌起义志士中在台湾的最后一位，他在11月15日因身体不适住院，终因年高而病逝。
