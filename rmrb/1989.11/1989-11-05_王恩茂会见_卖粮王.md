Title: Wang Enmao Meets the "Grain Sales King"
Author:
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, November 3rd.

Wang Enmao, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, recently met with the Uyghur grain salesman Ibrahym Danabay in Urumqi. Upon meeting, Wang Enmao warmly and humorously said, "Welcome to the 'Grain Sales King' who has made contributions to the country."

Ibrahym Danabay is a farmer from Baytuxa Township, Yining County, Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. Over the past three years, he has sold 2,700 metric tons of grain to the state. The 68-year-old Ibrahym Danabay told Wang Enmao, "I am old now, and my only wish is to make more contributions to the country and leave a good name for future generations."

### 王恩茂会见“卖粮王”

1989-11-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    王恩茂会见“卖粮王”
    据新华社乌鲁木齐11月3日电　全国政协副主席王恩茂日前在乌鲁木齐会见了维吾尔族卖粮大户伊不拉音达那。一见面，王恩茂便热情诙谐地说：“欢迎为国家作出贡献的‘卖粮大王’”。
    伊不拉音达那是新疆维吾尔自治区伊宁县巴依托海乡农民。过去的3年中，他一共卖给国家270吨粮食。今年68岁的伊不拉音达那对王恩茂说，我老了，一个心思就是能为国家多作贡献，给后代留个好名声。
