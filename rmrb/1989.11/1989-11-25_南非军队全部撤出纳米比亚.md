Title: South African Troops Fully Withdraw from Namibia
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 4

South African troops have fully withdrawn from Namibia.

    Xinhua News Agency Windhoek, November 23 - United Nations spokesman Ekhard said here today that South Africa withdrew its last 1,500 troops from Namibia before the morning of the 24th.

    Ekhard told reporters that the last batch of South African military vehicles are on their way back home.

    According to United Nations Security Council Resolution 435, South Africa should withdraw all its troops from Namibia one week after the results of the United Nations-supervised elections in Namibia are announced.

### 南非军队全部撤出纳米比亚

1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南非军队全部撤出纳米比亚
    新华社温得和克11月23日电　联合国发言人艾克哈德今天在这里说，南非于24日上午前撤出在纳米比亚的最后1500名驻军。
    艾克哈德告诉记者，南非军队的最后一批车辆已在归国途中。
    根据联合国安理会435号决议，在联合国监督下的纳米比亚大选得出结果一周之后，南非应全部撤出在纳米比亚的军队。
