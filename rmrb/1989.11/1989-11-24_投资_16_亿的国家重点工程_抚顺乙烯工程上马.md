Title: Investment of 1.6 Billion Yuan in National Key Project - Fushun Ethylene Project Commences
Author: Wang Qixing
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 2

The national key project during the "Seventh Five-Year Plan" – the Fushun 115,000-ton ethylene project – officially commenced construction on November 18 in the afternoon in Fushun. Vice President of China Petrochemical Corporation, Sheng Huaren, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee, Quan Shuren, and Governor Li Changchun attended the groundbreaking ceremony.

Fushun is China's largest oil refining base, with abundant gas and hydrocarbon resources. To increase social supply and adjust the industrial structure, the state approved an investment of nearly 1.6 billion yuan during the period of rectification and readjustment. This includes the use of foreign capital to introduce advanced technology and complete sets of equipment from the United States, Canada, France, Japan, and Italy, which are at the world's 1980s advanced level, to build an ethylene processing plant.

The project is expected to be completed and put into operation in 1991. Upon completion, it will be able to produce a large quantity of polyethylene, polypropylene, and other chemical products that are in short supply both domestically and internationally, with an annual output value of 930 million yuan and taxes and profits of 440 million yuan.

At the commencement ceremony, Mr. Matel, Executive Vice President of the American Rumos Company, spoke on behalf of the foreign investors, expressing the willingness to cooperate with China for the long term, to overcome difficulties for mutual success and benefit.

### 投资16亿的国家重点工程　抚顺乙烯工程上马
王启星
1989-11-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　投资16亿的国家重点工程
　　抚顺乙烯工程上马
    新华社沈阳电　（记者王启星）经国务院批准，国家“七五”重点工程——抚顺11.5万吨乙烯工程，11月18日下午在抚顺正式开工建设。中国石化总公司副总经理盛华仁、辽宁省委书记全树仁、省长李长春等为工程奠基剪彩。
    抚顺是我国最大的原油加工基地，拥有丰富的气烃资源。为了增加社会有效供给，调整产业结构，在治理整顿期间国家批准投资近16亿元，其中包括利用外资，从美国、加拿大、法国、日本、意大利引进具有世界80年代先进水平的技术和成套设备建设乙烯加工厂。
    这项工程预计1991年建成投产。建成后每年可生产大量国际、国内市场紧俏的聚乙烯、聚丙烯等多种牌号的石化产品，年创产值9.3亿元，利税4.4亿元。
    美国鲁姆斯公司执行副总裁马特尔先生在开工仪式上代表外商发言时表示，愿和中国长期友好合作，为双方共同的成功和相互的利益排除困难，争取胜利。
