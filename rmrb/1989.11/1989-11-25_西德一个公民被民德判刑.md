Title: A West German Citizen Sentenced by East Germany
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 4

A West German citizen was sentenced to five years in prison for espionage activities by the highest military court of the German Democratic Republic on November 23.

According to a report by the German News Agency, a 24-year-old youth named Peter Sch. has been recruited by the West German intelligence agency since 1987. He used his job in maritime transport between West Germany and West Berlin to engage in espionage activities multiple times.

### 西德一个公民被民德判刑

1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西德一个公民被民德判刑
    联邦德国一公民因从事间谍活动，二十三日被民主德国最高军事法庭判处有期徒刑五年。
    据德通社报道，二十四岁的青年彼得·施自一九八七年以来，就被联邦德国情报局收买，利用在联邦德国和西柏林之间从事航运工作多次进行间谍活动。
