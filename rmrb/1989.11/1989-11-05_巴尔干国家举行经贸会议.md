Title: Balkan Countries Hold Economic and Trade Conference
Author:
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 3

The Economic and Trade Cooperation Promotion Conference of Balkan Countries was held in Bucharest from the 2nd to the 3rd of November.

Deputy ministers of foreign trade from Albania, Bulgaria, Greece, Yugoslavia, and Romania, as well as the Turkish Ambassador to Romania, attended the conference.

According to a report from the Romanian News Agency, the attending representatives suggested holding several meetings in the near future to discuss cooperation among the chambers of commerce of the Balkan countries, the establishment of a Balkan Trade and Industry Chamber, trade barriers, the establishment of an arbitration body aimed at resolving commercial disputes, and financial and banking cooperation in the region.

The conference also discussed the establishment of a permanent institution aimed at promoting economic and trade cooperation among the Balkan countries.

### 巴尔干国家举行经贸会议

1989-11-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴尔干国家举行经贸会议
    巴尔干国家经济和贸易协作促进会议二日至三日在布加勒斯特举行。
    阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、希腊、南斯拉夫和罗马尼亚的外贸部副部长以及土耳其驻罗大使出席了会议。
    据罗通社报道，与会代表建议不久召开几次会议，讨论关于巴尔干国家各国商会的合作、建立巴尔干贸易和工业商会、贸易障碍、建立旨在解决商业争端的仲裁机构和本地区银行和财政合作等问题。
    会议还讨论了设立一个旨在推动巴尔干经贸合作的常设机构的问题。
