Title: Closing of the National Women's Federation Executive Committee
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th

The four-day-long sixth session of the second executive committee of the All-China Women's Federation concluded this afternoon in Beijing. Present at the meeting were Chen Muxia, Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, and Vice-Chairpersons Zhang Zhenying, Apei Caidanqie, Mayinuer Hasimu, and Nie Li. Huang Qixian, Vice-Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, presided over the meeting.

The meeting adopted the "Regulations on the Work of Rural Grassroots Women's Representative Assemblies," "Regulations on the Work of Urban Grassroots Women's Representative Assemblies," "Regulations on the Work of Women's Committees in Party and Government Organs and Educational, Scientific, Cultural, and Health Systems," "Regulations on the Work of Women's Friendship Associations," and the "Rules for the Work of the Executive Committee of the All-China Women's Federation."

### 全国妇联执委会闭幕

1989-11-18
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    全国妇联执委会闭幕
    据新华社北京11月17日电　为期4天的全国妇联六届二次执委会今天下午在京闭幕。全国妇联主席陈慕华，副主席张帼英、阿沛·才旦卓嘎、玛依努尔·哈斯木、聂力出席了会议。全国妇联副主席黄启璪主持了会议。
    会议通过了《农村基层妇女代表会工作条例》、《城镇基层妇女代表会工作条例》、《党政机关、教科文卫系统妇女委员会工作条例》、《妇女联谊会工作条例》和《全国妇联执委工作规则》。
