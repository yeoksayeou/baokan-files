Title: New Discoveries at the Yinxu Site in Anyang
Author: Liu Shumin Li Zixing
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 3

Archaeological experts have recently discovered four sacrificial pits from the Shang Dynasty, dating back over 3,000 years, within the 30-square-kilometer protected area of Yinxu in Anyang, Henan Province. Inside these pits are two well-preserved chariots and other artifacts. At the same time, near the palace area, a large and imposing palace foundation with a clear outline has been found and is currently undergoing further excavation. Experts have noted that these discoveries are quite rare in the 61 years of archaeological excavations at Yinxu.

### 安阳殷墟有新发现
刘书民　李自省
1989-11-24
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　安阳殷墟有新发现
    本报讯　考古专家们在河南省安阳市30平方公里的殷墟保护区内，最近发现4个距今3000多年的殷商时期殉葬坑，内有两辆保存完整的马车和其他一些文物。与此同时，在宫殿区附近还发现一个轮廓清晰、气势宏伟的大型宫殿基址，现正进一步发掘。据专家介绍，这些发现，在61年来的殷墟考古发掘中尚属稀见。（刘书民　李自省）
