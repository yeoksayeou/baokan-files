Title: Thai Prime Minister Delays Visit to Three Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-11-12
Page: 4

Thai Prime Minister Chatchai Chunhawan has postponed his planned two-week visit to the United States, Denmark, and Japan, which was scheduled to begin in December.

The decision was made by Chatchai after he completed his inspection of the typhoon-stricken areas in southern Thailand on the 10th.

### 泰国总理已推迟出访三国

1989-11-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰国总理已推迟出访三国
    泰国总理差猜·春哈旺推迟了将于十二月开始的对美国、丹麦和日本为期两周的访问。
    差猜总理是在十日视察完泰国南部台风受灾区后作出此决定的。
