Title: An American Shipborne Helicopter Explodes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 4

A spokesman for the Spanish Ministry of Defense announced on the 23rd that on the evening of the 22nd, a helicopter on the U.S. amphibious assault ship "Iwo Jima," which was patrolling in the western Mediterranean Sea in Spanish territorial waters, exploded. The cause of the accident is unknown.

### 美国一架舰载直升机爆炸

1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国一架舰载直升机爆炸
    西班牙国防部发言人二十三日宣布，二十二日晚，正在西地中海西班牙领水游弋的美国“硫黄岛”号两栖攻击舰上的一架直升机发生爆炸，事故原因不详。
