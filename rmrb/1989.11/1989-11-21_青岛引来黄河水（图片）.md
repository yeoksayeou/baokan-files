Title: Qingdao Welcomes Yellow River Water
Author: Qian Han
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 1

The Yellow River Water Diversion to Qingdao project, after 3 years and 6 months of relentless efforts by the builders, successfully completed its first trial water diversion on November 16. (See illustration)

	(Top right image) In Xinzhuang Village, Pingdu County, 75-year-old Liu Xizhang, leaning on his cane, came to the water conveyance canal and excitedly said, "I never thought in my lifetime I would get to drink Yellow River water!"

	(Bottom left image) One of the five pump stations, the Wangnü Pump Station, is undergoing trial operation in the main control room.
