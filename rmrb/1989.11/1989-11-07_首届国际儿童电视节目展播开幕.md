Title: First International Children's Television Program Exhibition Begins
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 1

The first International Children's Television Program Exhibition will provide Chinese young viewers with a feast for the eyes. Starting tomorrow, more than 80 excellent children's television programs from 44 countries and regions will be broadcast successively on China Central Television.

This exhibition, jointly organized by the National Children and Youth Work Coordination Committee, the China Children and Teenagers' Fund, the Soong Ching-ling Foundation, and China Central Television, has been in preparation since last November. It features a variety of children's television programs, including animated films, television dramas, science fiction, fairy tales, thematic documentaries, and song and dance performances. The programs are suitable for viewers ranging from kindergarten children to high school students.

### 首届国际儿童电视节目展播开幕

1989-11-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    首届国际儿童电视节目展播开幕
    新华社北京十一月六日电  首届国际儿童电视节目展播将为我国少儿观众提供一个大饱眼福的机会，四十四个国家和地区的八十多个优秀少儿电视节目，明天起陆续在中央电视台播出。由全国少儿工作协调委员会、中国儿童少年基金会、宋庆龄基金会、中央电视台联合主办的这次展播活动，自去年十一月开始筹备，集中了动画片、电视剧、科幻片、童话剧、专题纪录片、歌舞片等各种形式的少儿电视节目，适于观看的对象包括从幼儿园小朋友到中学生。
