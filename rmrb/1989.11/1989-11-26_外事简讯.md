Date: 1989-11-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 26th.

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Albania, Albanian Ambassador to China Justin Papaioannou held a reception on November 23 in Beijing. Minister of the Radio, Film, and Television Department Ai Zhensheng and other relevant Chinese officials were invited to attend.

The Polish "Chopin Trio" performed their first concert in China at the Haidian Theater in Beijing on the evening of November 23.

Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Kang Keqing met and hosted a dinner for the Macau Women's Visiting Delegation led by Lo Pak-sam, Chairman of the Macau Women's Federation, on November 24 at the Great Hall of the People.

Zhang Jinfu, a member of the Central Advisory Commission and an advisor to the State Council's Leading Group on Introducing Foreign Intelligence, met with Qu Tak-kan, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Far East Group in Hong Kong, and his entourage in Beijing on November 24.

Vice Secretary of the State Council Liu Zhongde participated in the meeting.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Saifuddin Azizi met with the Tanzanian Government Cultural Delegation led by Minister of Labor, Culture, and Social Welfare Ruggasa on November 25 at the Great Hall of the People.

State Councilor Chen Junsheng met with the delegation led by Chairman Onojuku Keichou of the Japan-China Friendship Religious Conference on November 25 at the Great Hall of the People.

Supreme People's Court President Ren Jianxin, at the invitation of Philippine Supreme Court Chief Justice Fernandez, departed for Manila on November 25 to attend the third conference of chief justices in the Asia-Pacific region.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲为庆祝中阿建交40周年，阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使尤斯廷·帕帕约尔吉23日在北京举行招待会。广播电影电视部部长艾知生等中国有关方面负责人应邀出席。
    ▲波兰“肖邦三重奏”小组23日晚上在北京海淀剧院举行了访华首场演出。
    ▲全国政协副主席康克清24日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了以澳门妇女联合会理事长罗柏心为团长的澳门妇女访问团。
    ▲中顾委常委、国务院引进国外智力领导小组顾问张劲夫24日在北京会见了全国政协委员、香港远东集团董事长邱德根一行。
    国务院副秘书长刘忠德参加了会见。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长赛福鼎·艾则孜25日在人民大会堂会见了由劳动、文化和社会福利部部长瑞加西拉率领的坦桑尼亚政府文化代表团。
    ▲国务委员陈俊生25日在人民大会堂会见了由理事长小野塚几澄率领的日中友好宗教者恳话会代表团。
    ▲最高人民法院院长任建新应菲律宾最高法院首席大法官菲尔南邀请，25日起程前往马尼拉参加亚太地区第三届首席大法官会议。
              （新华社）
