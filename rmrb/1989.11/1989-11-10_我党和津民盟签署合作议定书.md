Title: The Chinese Communist Party and ZANU-PF Sign a Cooperation Agreement
Author: Lǐ Jǐngwèi
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 4

The Chinese Communist Party and the Zimbabwe African National Union-Patriotic Front signed a cooperation agreement this morning in Harare.

Chinese Ambassador to Zimbabwe Song Guoqing and ZANU-PF's Secretary for External Affairs Mutasa signed the agreement on behalf of the two parties.

### 我党和津民盟签署合作议定书
李景卫
1989-11-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我党和津民盟签署合作议定书
    本报哈拉雷11月8日电  记者李景卫报道：中国共产党和津巴布韦非洲民族联盟（爱国阵线）两党合作议定书今天上午在哈拉雷签署。
    中国驻津巴布韦大使宋国清和津巴布韦非洲民族联盟（爱国阵线）外事书记穆塔萨分别代表两党在议定书上签字。
