Title: Yemen to Achieve Unification
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 4

Kuwaiti newspaper *Al-Yawm* reported on the 22nd that Yemeni President Ali Abdullah Saleh confirmed in an interview with the newspaper's correspondent that he will visit Aden on November 30th and sign an agreement with the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen for the unification of the two countries.

Saleh stated that according to this agreement, the unified Yemen will be named the "Republic of Yemen."

### 南北两个也门将实现统一

1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南北两个也门将实现统一
    据科威特《舆论报》二十二日报道，阿拉伯也门总统阿里·阿卜杜拉·萨利赫在同该报记者谈话中证实，他将于十一月三十日访问亚丁，并同民主也门签署一项两国统一的协定。
    萨利赫说，根据这项协议，统一后的两个也门将称之为“也门共和国”。
