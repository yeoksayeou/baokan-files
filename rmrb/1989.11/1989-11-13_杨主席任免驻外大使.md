Title: Chairman Yang Appoints and Dismisses Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 11th.

Chairman Yang Shangkun, according to the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has appointed Han Lijie (female) as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Ireland, and has relieved Zhou Yang of his duties as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Ireland.

### 杨主席任免驻外大使

1989-11-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席任免驻外大使
    新华社北京11月11日电　中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，任命韩琍琍（女）为中华人民共和国驻爱尔兰特命全权大使，免去周阳的中华人民共和国驻爱尔兰特命全权大使职务。
