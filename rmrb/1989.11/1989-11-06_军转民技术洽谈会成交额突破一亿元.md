Title: National Military Technology to Civilian Use Negotiation and Trade Fair Exceeds One Billion Yuan in Transactions
Author: Xǔ Zhìmǐn  Lǐ Guóxīn
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 2

The 1989 National Military Technology to Civilian Use Negotiation and Trade Fair, which lasted for 7 days, achieved a transaction amount of over one billion yuan. This once again demonstrates the overall advantages of military technology and the broad prospects for its transition to civilian use.

The fair, held in Ningbo, showcased a total of 2,000 military technologies.

### 军转民技术洽谈会成交额突破一亿元
许志敏  李国新
1989-11-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    军转民技术洽谈会成交额突破一亿元
    新华社宁波11月4日电　（记者许志敏、李国新）历时7天的1989年全国军工技术转民用洽谈交易会交易额达1亿多元，再次显示军工技术的整体优势和向民用转移的广阔前景。
    在宁波举办的这届交易会，共展出2000项军工技术。
