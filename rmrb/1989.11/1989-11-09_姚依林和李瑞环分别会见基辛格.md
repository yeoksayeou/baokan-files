Title: Yao Yilin and Li Ruihuan Meet with Kissinger
Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-11-09
Edition: 1

	The People's Daily, Beijing, November 8th — Reporter Sun Yi reports: Today, Politburo Standing Committee members Yao Yilin and Li Ruihuan separately met with former U.S. Secretary of State Dr. Henry Kissinger at the Great Hall of the People. They engaged in friendly and candid discussions.

### 姚依林和李瑞环分别会见基辛格
孙毅
1989-11-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    姚依林和李瑞环分别会见基辛格
    本报北京11月8日讯　记者孙毅报道：中共中央政治局常委姚依林、李瑞环今天在人民大会堂分别会见了美国前国务卿亨利·基辛格博士，同他进行了友好坦诚的交谈。
