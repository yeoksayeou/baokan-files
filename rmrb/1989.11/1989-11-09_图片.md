Title: The Charm of Maputo
Author: Shēn Mínghé
Date: 1989-11-09
Edition: 7

The capital of Mozambique, Maputo, is a beautiful city. Due to the influence of the Indian Ocean monsoon, the trees here are evergreen, and the climate is pleasant. The flowers and trees lining the streets make Maputo particularly beautiful, with parks and sculptures everywhere. The image shows a sculpture in the square of Maputo Railway Station.
