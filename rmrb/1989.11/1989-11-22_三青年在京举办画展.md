Title: Three Young Artists Hold Exhibition in Beijing
Author: Lai Renqiong
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 3

Three young artists, Li Yongqi, Jiang Rong, and Zhan Chun, held an exhibition at the National Art Museum of China today. The exhibition features over 60 works that blend traditional and modern techniques, attracting many young people from the capital.

The exhibition is jointly organized by China Central Television, "China Inspection" Magazine, and the "Business Times."

### 三青年在京举办画展
赖仁琼
1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　三青年在京举办画展
    本报北京十一月二十一日讯　青年画家李永奇、江嵘、占春今天在中国美术馆举办画展。展出的六十余幅作品，融传统、现代技法为一体，吸引了众多首都青年。
    画展由中央电视台、《中国商检》杂志社、《工商时报》主办。
　（赖仁琼）
