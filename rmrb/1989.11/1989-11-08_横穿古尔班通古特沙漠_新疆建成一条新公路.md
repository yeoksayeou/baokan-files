Title: A New Highway Across the Gurbantunggut Desert is Completed in Xinjiang
Author: Deng Fuchang He Jiasun
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 2

On November 3rd, a 100-kilometer gravel road that traverses the Gurbantunggut Desert was successfully opened to traffic. This road, constructed by over 3,000 soldiers from the Xinjiang Military Region in just 90 days, shortens the distance from Urumqi to the Altay Region by 300 kilometers.

The Gurbantunggut Desert, located in the heart of the Junggar Basin, is China's largest fixed or semi-fixed desert. In the northwestern part of the desert, there is a large salt mine with a reserve of over 260 million cubic meters of salt. The completion of this road will facilitate the development of the salt industry.

### 横穿古尔班通古特沙漠  新疆建成一条新公路
邓富昌　何家顺
1989-11-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    横穿古尔班通古特沙漠
    新疆建成一条新公路
    本报讯　11月3日，横穿古尔班通古特大沙漠的百公里沙石公路胜利通车。这条由新疆军区3000余筑路大军在90天里建成的公路，使乌鲁木齐至阿尔泰地区缩短路程300公里。
    位于准噶尔盆地中心的古尔班通古特大沙漠，是我国最大的固定或半固定沙漠。沙漠西北部有一个储盐量达2.6亿多立方米的大盐矿。这条公路的建成，将有助于盐业的开发。（邓富昌　何家顺）
