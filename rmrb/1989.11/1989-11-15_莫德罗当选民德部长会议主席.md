Title: Hans Modrow Elected Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the GDR
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì Jiāng Jiànguó
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 4

People's Daily, Berlin, November 14th

Hans Modrow was elected as the new Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the GDR at the 11th session of the People's Chamber of the GDR yesterday.

In response to reporters' questions after his election, he said that the primary task of the new Council of Ministers is economic reform, and it is necessary to seriously listen to the opinions of professional experts. Citizens of the GDR must work hard. As the head of the government, he is determined to fight against bureaucracy.

He said that today he will consult with the leaders of other parties on the composition of the government. The People's Chamber will convene its 12th session on the 17th to announce the composition of the government and issue a government statement.

Modrow was born in 1928 in Jazenitz to a working-class family and was once a fitter. In 1949, he joined the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. Since then, he has held various positions in the party, the Free German Youth, and trade unions. From 1952 to 1953, he studied at the Central School of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Later, he studied at the "Karl Marx" Higher Party School and the Berlin School of Economics, obtaining a doctorate in 1966. In 1958, he was elected as a candidate member of the Central Committee of the party, and in 1967, he was elected as a full member. Since 1973, he has served as the First Secretary of the District Party Committee of Dresden. At the 10th Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the party this November, he was elected as a member of the Politburo.

### 莫德罗当选民德部长会议主席
许宏治  江建国
1989-11-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    莫德罗当选民德部长会议主席
    本报柏林11月14日电　记者许宏治、江建国报道：汉斯·莫德罗昨天在民德人民议院第11次会议上当选为新的民德部长会议主席。
    他在当选后回答记者提问时说，新的部长会议首要任务是经济改革，必须认真听取内行的专家的意见。民德公民必须艰苦地劳动。作为政府首脑，他决心与官僚主义作斗争。
    他说，今天他将同其他政党领导人协商政府组成。17日人民议院将召开第12次会议公布政府组成，并发表政府声明。
    莫德罗1928年1月生于雅泽尼茨一个工人家庭，当过钳工。1949年加入德国统一社会党，此后担任过党、自由德国青年联盟和工会基层职务。1952—1953年在苏联中央团校学习，此后他又在“卡尔·马克思”高级党校和柏林经济学院学习，1966年获博士学位。1958年他当选为党中央候补委员，1967年当选为中央委员。1973年以来担任德累斯顿专区党委第一书记。今年11月在党中央第10次全会上当选为政治局委员。
