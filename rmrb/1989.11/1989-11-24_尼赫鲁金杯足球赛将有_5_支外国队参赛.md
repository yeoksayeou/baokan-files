Title: Five Foreign Teams to Participate in Nehru Gold Cup Football Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 3

Five foreign football teams have confirmed their participation in the Nehru Gold Cup Football Tournament to be held in December in Kolkata, India.

The five foreign teams are: the Hungarian national team, the Paraguayan Olympic team, the Soviet Union team, the Norwegian team, and the Zambian team.

### 尼赫鲁金杯足球赛将有5支外国队参赛

1989-11-24
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　尼赫鲁金杯足球赛将有5支外国队参赛
    新华社新德里电　5支外国足球队已确定将参加12月在印度加尔各答举行的尼赫鲁金杯足球赛。
    这5支外国球队是：匈牙利国家队、巴拉圭奥林匹克队、苏联队、挪威队和赞比亚队。
