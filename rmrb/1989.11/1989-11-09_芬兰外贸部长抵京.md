Title: Finnish Minister of Foreign Trade Arrives in Beijing
Author: Zhou Cixiu
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 8th.

Chinese Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin and Finnish Minister of Foreign Trade Bertil Salolainen expressed tonight their willingness to work for the further development of trade relations and economic cooperation between the two countries.

At the banquet held in honor of the arrival of Minister Salolainen and his delegation, Zheng Tuobin said that the economic relations between China and Finland have gone beyond the traditional trade relations and entered the stage of economic cooperation. He expressed the willingness of the Chinese side to strengthen long-term economic cooperation with Finland on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.

Salolainen believes that there is great potential and a bright future for the development of economic cooperation and trade relations between Finland and China.

Salolainen came to China at the invitation of Zheng Tuobin for a friendly visit and arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

### 芬兰外贸部长抵京
周慈朴
1989-11-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    芬兰外贸部长抵京
    据新华社北京11月8日电　（记者周慈朴）中国经贸部长郑拓彬和芬兰外贸部长贝尔蒂·萨洛莱宁今天晚上在这里表示要为进一步发展中芬两国贸易关系和经济合作而努力。
    郑拓彬在他为欢迎萨洛莱宁部长一行所举行的宴会上说，中芬两国经济关系已经超出了传统的贸易关系，而进入了经济合作的阶段。他表示中国方面愿意在平等互利的基础上加强同芬兰的长期的经济合作关系。
    萨洛莱宁认为，芬中两国发展经济合作和贸易关系的潜力很大，前景很好。
    萨洛莱宁是应郑拓彬的邀请前来中国进行友好访问，于今天上午乘飞机抵达北京的。
