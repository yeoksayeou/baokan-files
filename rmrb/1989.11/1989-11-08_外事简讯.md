Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Zhu Qihua met with Dr. Karl Harn, Chairman of the Board of Volkswagen AG of the Federal Republic of Germany, and his delegation at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of November 5. Before the meeting, representatives from the First Automobile Works presented a domestically produced "Hongqi" sedan to Chairman Harn.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hab Comrade Xiping held a reception and banquet for American friends Robert Williams and his wife at the Great Hall of the People on November 6.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lei Jieqiong met with former Nepalese Minister of Education K. B. Bista and his wife at the Great Hall of the People on November 6.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Sun Qimeng met with a delegation from the Italy-China Friendship Association led by Senator and Chairman of the Italy-China Friendship Association Walter Funtana at the Great Hall of the People on November 7.

The Italian guests arrived in Beijing on November 4 at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries for a two-week friendly visit.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员邹家华11月5日晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了联邦德国大众汽车公司董事长卡尔·哈恩博士一行。会见前，第一汽车制造厂的负责人将一辆国产“红旗”轿车赠送给哈恩董事长。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋6日在人民大会堂会见并宴请美国朋友罗伯特·威廉和夫人。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼6日在人民大会堂会见了尼泊尔前教育大臣凯·巴·比斯塔和他的夫人。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长孙起孟11月7日在人民大会堂会见由意大利参议员、意中商会主席瓦特·冯塔那率领的意中商会代表团。
    意大利客人应对外友协的邀请前来进行为期两周的友好访问，于11月4日抵京。
              （新华社）
