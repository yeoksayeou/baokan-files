Turkish President Takes Oath of Office

Turkey's new President Turgut Özal took the oath of office on November 9th.

After delivering his inaugural address, Özal laid a wreath at the mausoleum of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, the founder of the Republic of Turkey. Following this, a ceremony for the transfer of presidential powers was held at the presidential palace. Amid the sound of 101 cannon shots, Özal officially assumed the presidency as the eighth President of Turkey, receiving the handover of power from President Evren. After the ceremony, President Özal appointed the 54-year-old Speaker of the Parliament, Yıldırım Akbulut, to form a new cabinet.

### 土耳其总统宣誓就职

1989-11-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    土耳其总统宣誓就职
    土耳其新总统厄扎尔九日宣誓就职。
    厄扎尔在发表就职演说之后，向土耳其共和国的创始人凯末尔的陵墓献了花圈。随后，在总统府举行了总统权力交接仪式。在一百零一响礼炮声中，厄扎尔接受了埃夫伦总统移交的权力，正式就任土耳其第八任总统。仪式后，厄扎尔总统任命五十四岁的议会议长阿克布卢特组成新内阁。
