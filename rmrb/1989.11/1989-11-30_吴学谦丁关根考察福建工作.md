Title: Wu Xueqian and Ding Guanggen Inspect Work in Fujian
Author: Wu Weizhong Zhang Mingqing
Date: 1989-11-30
Edition: 2

Wu Xueqian and Ding Guanggen, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, inspected work in Fujian. Wu Xueqian is a Vice Premier of the State Council, and Ding Guanggen is a member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee. They praised the significant achievements made in Fujian's ten years of reform and opening up. They emphasized the need to earnestly study and implement the spirit of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee, to translate it into action, and to do a solid job in all aspects of work. They stressed the importance of summarizing experiences, further improving the rectification and reform, adhering to the policy of opening up to the outside world, and promoting the sustained, stable, and coordinated development of the economy.

Wu Xueqian and Ding Guanggen inspected work in Fujian from November 25 to 29. They focused on inspecting some "three-in-one" enterprises in the Hulishan Industrial Zone of the Xiamen Special Economic Zone and visited the newly developed investment areas of Xinglin and Haicang. They held a symposium with the heads of "three-in-one" enterprises and exchanged views with them. They also listened to reports from Fujian Province and coastal cities such as Xiamen and Fuzhou on their foreign economic work and gave their opinions on how to further improve the opening up to the outside world and improve the investment environment.

Chen Guangyi, Secretary of the Fujian Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Wang Zhaoguo, Governor of Fujian Province, as well as relevant officials from the State Council, participated in the inspection.

### 吴学谦丁关根考察福建工作
王炜中　张铭清
1989-11-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　吴学谦丁关根考察福建工作
    本报厦门11月29日电　新华社记者王炜中、本报记者张铭清报道：中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦，中共中央政治局候补委员、书记处书记丁关根近日在福建考察时肯定了福建十年改革开放取得的显著成绩，强调要把认真学习贯彻党的十三届五中全会精神落实到行动上，扎扎实实做好各项工作，要注意总结经验，进一步搞好治理整顿、深化改革，坚持对外开放，促进经济持续、稳定、协调地发展。
    吴学谦、丁关根是11月25日至29日在福建考察工作的。他们重点考察了厦门特区湖里工业区的部分“三资”企业，实地察看了新开辟的杏林、海沧投资区；召开了“三资”企业负责人座谈会，同他们交换了意见；还听取了福建省及厦门、福州等沿海城市关于对外经济工作的汇报，并就当前如何进一步搞好对外开放，切实改善投资环境等工作发表了意见。
    中共福建省委书记陈光毅、省长王兆国以及国务院有关部门负责同志参加了考察。
