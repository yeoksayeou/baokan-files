Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with North Korean and Japanese Guests
Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 1

	Jiang Zemin Meets with North Korean and Japanese Guests

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 12 - General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Jiang Zemin met this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People with a delegation from the Socialist Youth League of North Korea, led by Central Committee Chairman Choe Ryong-hae. They had a very cordial conversation.

	The First Secretary of the Communist Youth League of China, Song Defu, participated in the meeting.

	The delegation from the Socialist Youth League of North Korea arrived in Beijing on November 11 at the invitation of the Communist Youth League of China for a friendly visit.

	People's Daily, Beijing, November 12 - Reporter Sun Yi: General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Jiang Zemin met this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People with the 1989 Japan-China Economic Association delegation visiting China, led by Keidanren Chairman Eishiro Saito as senior advisor and Japan Economic Association Chairman Ryoi Kawai as leader. In a friendly atmosphere, both sides exchanged views candidly.

	During the meeting, General Secretary Jiang Zemin briefed the guests on China's current political and economic situation. When discussing Sino-Japanese economic cooperation, he believed that both sides should adhere to the principles of "mutual understanding, mutual trust, equality and mutual benefit, and long-term consideration." He also hoped that the Japan-China Economic Association would promote economic cooperation between the two countries.

	Japanese guests such as Eishiro Saito expressed the hope for further expansion of economic and trade exchanges between Japan and China, and stated that the Japan-China Economic Association would strive for this.

	Zhang Hongye, President of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, was present at the meeting.

### 江泽民分别会见朝鲜日本客人
孙毅
1989-11-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民分别会见朝鲜日本客人
    新华社北京11月12日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在人民大会堂会见了由中央委员长崔龙海率领的朝鲜社会主义劳动青年同盟代表团，同他们进行了十分亲切的谈话。
    中国共青团中央第一书记宋德福参加了会见。
    朝鲜社劳青代表团应共青团中央邀请前来中国进行友好访问，于11月11日抵达北京。
    本报北京11月12日讯　记者孙毅报道：中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在人民大会堂会见了以日本经济团体联合会会长斋藤英四郎为最高顾问、日本经济协会会长河合良一为团长的日本1989年度日中经济协会访华团。在友好的气氛中，宾主双方坦诚地交换了意见。
    会见时，江泽民总书记向客人们介绍了中国国内目前的政治、经济形势。在谈到中外经济合作问题时，他认为双方应该遵循“互相了解、互相信任、平等互利、长远考虑”的原则。他还希望日中经济协会能够推进两国间的经济合作。
    斋藤英四郎等日本客人希望日中之间进一步扩大经济贸易往来，并表示日中经济协会将为此而努力。
    中国国际贸易促进会会长郑鸿业会见时在座。
