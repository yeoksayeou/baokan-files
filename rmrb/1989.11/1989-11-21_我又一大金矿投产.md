Title: Another Major Gold Mine in China Starts Production
Author: Kong Deyi
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 2

	Another major gold mine in China has started production. The Shangong Gold Mine in Luoning County, Henan Province, a key project during the country's Seventh Five-Year Plan, has recently begun operations.

	The Shangong Gold Mine is a large gold deposit. The mine has proven gold reserves of 29 tons. Once the processing plant is in full operation, it will be able to process 250 tons of ore per day, producing 1.2 tons of gold and 1.6 tons of silver annually. The annual output value is expected to be 13 million yuan, with a profit of over 6 million yuan.

### 我又一大金矿投产
孔德义
1989-11-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　我又一大金矿投产
    新华社郑州11月20日电（通讯员孔德义）我国“七五”期间重点工程项目之一的河南省洛宁县上宫金矿采选厂，最近正式投产。
    上宫金矿属大型黄金矿床，这个矿区探明黄金储量为29吨。采选厂投产后，可日选矿石250吨，可年产黄金1.2万两，白银1.6万两，年产值1300万元，创利润600多万元。
