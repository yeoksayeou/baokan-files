Title: Premier Li Lays Wreath at Bangladesh National Martyrs' Memorial
Author:
Date: 1989-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, November 17th.

Premier Li Peng, who is currently visiting Bangladesh, went to Savar, about 30 kilometers west of the capital Dhaka, today to lay a wreath at the National Martyrs' Memorial.

After the wreath-laying ceremony, Premier Li signed the guest book.

Accompanied by Bangladesh's Minister of Education, Sheikh Shahid Islam, Premier Li also planted a tree symbolizing Sino-Bangladesh friendship at the base of the memorial. This is the second Sino-Bangladesh friendship tree planted by a Chinese leader in the Savar Martyrs' Cemetery. The first was planted by former Chairman Li Xiannian during his visit here in 1986.

The 45-meter-high memorial is composed of seven towering triangular concrete walls. It was built in 1982 to commemorate the nameless martyrs who sacrificed their lives in the Bangladesh War of Independence in 1971.

### 李总理向孟国家烈士纪念碑敬献花圈

1989-11-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    李总理向孟国家烈士纪念碑敬献花圈
    新华社达卡11月17日电  正在这里访问的中国总理李鹏今天来到首都达卡以西约30公里的萨瓦尔，向孟加拉国国家烈士纪念碑敬献花圈。
    献花圈仪式之后，李总理在来宾簿上签名题词。
    在孟加拉国教育部长谢赫·沙希德·伊斯拉姆的陪同下，李总理还在烈士纪念碑下种了一棵象征中孟友谊的树。
    这是中国领导人在萨瓦尔烈士陵园种植的第二棵中孟友谊树。第一棵是前国家主席李先念1986年来这里访问时种下的。
    这座高达45米的纪念碑由七片耸立的三角形水泥墙组成。它是在1982年为纪念1971年在孟加拉国独立战争中牺牲的无名烈士而修建的。
