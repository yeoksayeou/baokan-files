Title: Spanish Queen Receives Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Edition: 3

Spanish Queen Sofia expressed her hope on October 30 that she would have the opportunity to visit China again in the future. She made this remark during a reception held at the Zarzuela Palace in Madrid for Chinese Ambassador to Spain Yuan Tao and his wife. During the reception, Queen Sofia recalled the scene of King Juan Carlos and her visit to China in 1978. She said that the visit left them with wonderful memories.

### 西班牙王后接见我大使

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西班牙王后接见我大使
    西班牙王后索菲娅10月30日说，希望今后有机会再次访华。她是在马德里萨苏埃拉宫接见中国驻西班牙大使原涛和夫人时表示这个愿望的。在接见时，索菲娅王后回顾了1978年国王胡安·卡洛斯和她访问中国时的情景。她说，那次访问给他们留下了美好的记忆。
