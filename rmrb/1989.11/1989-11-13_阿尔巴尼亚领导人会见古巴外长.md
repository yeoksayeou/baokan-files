Title: Albanian Leader Meets Cuban Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Page: 4

Albanian leader receives Cuban foreign minister
Albanian Party of Labour Central Committee First Secretary and Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly Ramiz Alia received Cuban Foreign Minister Isidoro Malmierca on November 11 in Tirana. According to reports, Alia stated during the meeting that the Albanian people link their lives with socialism and will continue to defend freedom, independence, and sovereignty. He also advocated for friendship and cooperation with other countries, and for the maintenance of peace and security.

Additionally, it was reported that Albanian Council of Ministers Chairman Adil Çarçani met with visiting Cuban Foreign Minister Malmierca on November 10. Both sides held friendly talks on bilateral relations. Malmierca is the first Cuban foreign minister to visit Albania since the two countries established diplomatic relations in 1960.

### 阿尔巴尼亚领导人会见古巴外长

1989-11-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚领导人会见古巴外长
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记、人民议会主席团主席阿利雅十一日在地拉那接见了古巴外长马尔米耶卡。据报道，阿利雅在会见时说，阿尔巴尼亚人民把自己的生活同社会主义联系在一起，将一如既往地捍卫自由、独立和主权，并主张同其它国家友好与合作，维护和平与安全。
    另据报道，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席查尔查尼十日接见了来访的古巴外长马尔米耶卡。双方就两国关系进行了友好的会谈。马尔米耶卡是阿古一九六○年建交以来第一位访阿的古巴外长。
