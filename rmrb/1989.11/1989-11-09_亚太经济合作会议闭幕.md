Title: Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Meeting Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Edition: 3

The two-day inaugural Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) meeting concluded on November 7 in Canberra. The meeting was attended by ministers from 27 countries and regions, including Australia, the United States, Japan, Canada, and the six ASEAN nations.

Participants unanimously agreed that the meeting was substantive and constructive, and that this momentum should be maintained.

The meeting reached a consensus on the necessity of global trade liberalization. However, opinions differed on the establishment of a regional cooperation organization for Asia and the Pacific.

### 亚太经济合作会议闭幕

1989-11-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    亚太经济合作会议闭幕
    为期两天的首届亚洲和太平洋地区经济合作会议七日在堪培拉闭幕。参加会议的有澳大利亚、美国、日本、加拿大和东盟六国等国家和地区的二十七位部长。
    与会者一致认为这次会议是实质性的和富有建设性的，应该保持这一进程。
    会议对实行全球贸易自由化的必要性立场一致。但是，对成立亚洲和太平洋地区区域合作的机构问题意见不一。
