		Feedback Shortwave

The People's Daily, on November 10th, published on the 8th page an ink bamboo painting by Taiwanese artist Liu Senfu. The original title should be "Bamboo Reports Peace," not "Bamboo Reports Peaceful Years."

	Public Security Bureau, Reader Two
