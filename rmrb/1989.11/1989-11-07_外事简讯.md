Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

	Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with the president of the Japan Travel Bureau, Kenmatsu Manabu, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse in the afternoon of November 6.

	Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with a delegation led by the director of the India International Centre, Gonsalves, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on November 6.

	Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with the Bulgarian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Petar Mladenov, at the Diplomatic Reception Hall on November 6.

	Shanghai Vice Mayor Ni Tianzeng signed the "Shanghai-Yokohama 1990 Friendly Exchange Project Agreement" with the representative of the mayor of Yokohama and the director of the Urban Planning Bureau, Shoichi Eguchi, on November 4.

	After visiting Shenzhen and Guangzhou, the delegation led by the Czechoslovakian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Johannes, departed Beijing for their home country on November 5.
