Title: Song Renqiong Meets with the American Communist Party Delegation
Author: Zhou Cixu
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 3

Song Renqiong, Vice Chairman of the Central Advisory Commission, met with a delegation from the American Communist Party led by Political Bureau member Louis Diskin at the Great Hall of the People this evening.

During the meeting, Song Renqiong praised the American Communist Party as a party with a glorious struggle tradition. He said that the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people will never forget that the American Communist Party had actively supported and voiced their support for the Chinese people's War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation.

Song Renqiong pointed out that the Chinese Communist Party had just concluded its 13th Central Committee, 5th Plenary Session. He said that the visit by the American guests at this moment itself shows the American Communist Party's support for the Chinese Communist Party. He hopes that the traditional friendship between the two parties will continue to develop in the years to come.

Jia Liang, Director of the International Liaison Department, attended the meeting.

The American Communist Party delegation, as guests of the International Liaison Department, arrived in Beijing on November 6 and began a two-week visit to China. The delegation's main purpose for this visit to China is to learn about China's construction and reform situation.

### 宋任穷会见美共代表团
周慈朴
1989-11-11
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    宋任穷会见美共代表团
    新华社北京十一月十日电 （记者周慈朴）中顾委副主任宋任穷今天晚上在人民大会堂会见以政治局委员路易斯·迪斯金为团长的美国共产党代表团。
    会见时，宋任穷赞扬美国共产党是一个具有光荣斗争传统的政党。他说，中国共产党和中国人民不会忘记美国共产党曾经积极支持和声援他们的抗日战争和解放战争。
    宋任穷指出，中国共产党刚刚召开了第十三届五中全会。美国客人在这个时刻来访，本身就说明美国共产党对中国共产党的支持。他希望中美两党的传统友谊在今后的岁月中不断向前发展。
    中联部部长朱良参加了会见。
    美国共产党代表团作为中联部的客人于十一月六日来到北京，开始对中国进行为期两个星期的访问。代表团此次访华主要是了解中国建设和改革情况。
