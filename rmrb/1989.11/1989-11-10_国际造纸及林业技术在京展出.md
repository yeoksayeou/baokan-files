Title: International Paper and Forestry Technology Exhibition in Beijing
Author: Liú Guìlián
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

The 1989 International Pulp and Paper Industry and Forestry Technology Exhibition, hosted by the American Klaus Company, opened today at the China International Exhibition Center in Beijing. Over 100 companies from Sweden, Finland, Austria, Japan, the United States, and 17 other countries, as well as businesspeople from Hong Kong and Taiwan, participated in this exhibition. The Chinese pavilion at this exhibition showcased equipment from over 100 units involved in pulp and paper production, mechanical manufacturing, and forestry machinery.

### 国际造纸及林业技术在京展出
刘桂莲
1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    国际造纸及林业技术在京展出
    本报北京11月9日讯　记者刘桂莲报道：由美国克劳斯公司主办的1989年国际制浆造纸工业及林业技术展览，今天在北京中国国际展览中心开幕。来自瑞典、芬兰、奥地利、日本、美国等20个国家的100多家外商以及香港、台湾的客商参加了这次展览。本届展览会中国馆展出100多个单位的制浆造纸、机械制造、营林机械等设备。
