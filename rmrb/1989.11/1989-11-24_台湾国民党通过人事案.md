Title: Kuomintang Approves Personnel Changes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, November 23rd.

Taiwan's Kuomintang Central Standing Committee approved personnel changes for the positions of "Minister of National Defense" and "Minister of Justice" on November 22nd. "Chief of the General Staff" Ha Boquan's term has ended, and he has been approved to take up the position of "Minister of National Defense." The former "Minister of Justice" Xiao Tianchan has resigned, and it has been approved for Deputy Minister Lü Youwen to take over the position. It has also been approved for the current "Minister of National Defense," Zheng Weiyuan, to retire.

### 台湾国民党通过人事案

1989-11-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　台湾国民党通过人事案
    新华社香港十一月二十三日电　台北消息：台湾国民党中常会十一月二十二日通过“国防部长”及“法务部长”人事案：“参谋总长”郝柏村任期届满，核准转任“国防部长”；原“法务部长”萧天讚已辞职，核准由代部长吕有文接任，并核准现任“国防部长”郑为元退休。
