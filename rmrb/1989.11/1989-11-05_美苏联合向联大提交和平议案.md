The United States and the Soviet Union Jointly Submit a Peace Proposal to the UN General Assembly

UNITED NATIONS, Nov. 3 (Xinhua) — U.S. Assistant Secretary of State John Bolton and Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Vladimir Petrovsky held a joint press conference today, announcing that the two countries have jointly submitted a draft resolution to the current session of the UN General Assembly on maintaining peace, security, and strengthening international cooperation.

This is the first time the two superpowers have taken joint political action within the UN system, drawing attention from all sides. At the press conference, U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Bolton and Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Petrovsky said that the joint proposal marks a new stage in U.S.-Soviet relations within the UN, demonstrating a new spirit of cooperation.

When asked whether the draft resolution's opposition to the use of force means that the two superpowers will no longer support military activities against UN member states or get involved in regional conflicts, both Bolton and Petrovsky avoided giving a direct answer.

The League of Arab States' permanent observer to the UN, Makhsoud, expressed cautious welcome to the U.S.-Soviet joint proposal at a press conference this afternoon. Makhsoud believes that the new developments in U.S.-Soviet cooperation will help ease international tensions. However, he pointed out that it is unrealistic for the U.S. and the Soviet Union to ask UN member states to abandon their disputes. For example, the Middle East problem of Israel's occupation of Arab territories remains unresolved, and political struggles on this issue within the UN are inevitable.

### 美苏联合向联大提交和平议案
刘开宸
1989-11-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏联合向联大提交和平议案
    本报联合国11月3日电  记者刘开宸报道：美国助理国务卿约翰·博尔顿和苏联副外长弗拉基米尔·彼得罗夫斯基今天上午在这里联合举行记者招待会上发表的联合声明说，两国代表团向本届联大共同提出一项关于维护和平、安全和加强国际合作的决议草案。
    这是两个超级大国第一次在联合国系统内采取的共同政治行动，引起人们的注意。美国助理国务卿博尔顿和苏联副外长彼得罗夫斯基在记者招待会上说，两国共同议案表明美、苏在联合国内的关系进入一个新阶段，表现了新的合作精神。
    有记者问，决议草案提出反对使用武力，是否表明两个超级大国不再支持反对联合国成员国的军事活动，也不再卷入地区冲突？博尔顿和彼得罗夫斯基均避免了直接回答。
    阿拉伯联盟常驻联合国观察员马克苏德在今天下午举行的记者招待会上对美苏共同议案事表示有保留的欢迎，马克苏德认为美、苏合作的新发展有助于缓和国际紧张局势。他又指出，美、苏在联合声明中要联合国成员国都放弃争论是不现实的。例如以色列占领阿拉伯领土的中东问题尚未解决，在联合国内进行这方面的政治斗争是不可避免的。
