Title: Heilongjiang Has the Lowest Price Increase in the Country
Author: Xie Jinhu
Date: 1989-11-27
Edition: 2

    Heilongjiang Province has seen its price increase rate drop to the lowest in the country since implementing the "383 Project" to control prices this year. The price increase rate has fallen from 27.6% in January to 3.8% in October, and has been controlled within a single digit since August.

    Statistics show that the price increase rate for essential goods for people's daily lives has seen the most significant drop. The province has an adequate supply of consumer goods, and the market order has improved.

### 黑龙江物价涨幅全国最低
谢金虎
1989-11-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　黑龙江物价涨幅全国最低
    据新华社哈尔滨电　（记者谢金虎）黑龙江省今年实行控制物价的“383工程”以来，物价涨幅已由1月份的27.6％回落到10月份的3.8％，并且自8月份以后一直控制在一位数，为全国最低。
    据统计，今年价格涨幅回落最大的是人民生活必需品。全省消费品供应充足，市场秩序好转。
