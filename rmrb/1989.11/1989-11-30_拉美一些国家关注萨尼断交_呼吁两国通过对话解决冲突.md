Title: Some Latin American Countries Focus on Salvador-Nicaragua Break in Relations and Call for Dialogue to Resolve Conflict
Author: Pan Guojun
Date: 1989-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 28th. (Reporter Pan Guojun) The severance of diplomatic relations between El Salvador and Nicaragua has drawn widespread international attention. Many Latin American countries have called on the two nations to prioritize the peace of Central America and resolve conflicts through dialogue.

Costa Rican President Oscar Arias expressed regret on the 27th over El Salvador's decision to break off relations with Nicaragua.

Mexican Foreign Minister Fernando Solana stated on the 27th that the Mexican government is deeply concerned that the break in relations between El Salvador and Nicaragua will affect the peace process in Central America.

Honduran President José Azcona noted that the break in relations is "a bad omen" and "will harm the peace process in Central America."

A spokesperson for the Guatemalan presidency stated that the Guatemalan government hopes to convene a summit of the five Central American presidents as soon as possible to discuss new measures to achieve peace in Central America.

Peruvian President Alan García pointed out on the 27th during a press conference that the break in relations between El Salvador and Nicaragua is not conducive to achieving peace in Central America.

The People's Daily, Caracas, November 28th. Venezuelan President Jaime Lusinchi stated yesterday that the suspension of diplomatic relations between El Salvador and Nicaragua is "regrettable."

### 拉美一些国家关注萨尼断交　呼吁两国通过对话解决冲突
潘国俊
1989-11-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　拉美一些国家关注萨尼断交
　　呼吁两国通过对话解决冲突
    据新华社北京11月28日电　（记者潘国俊）萨尔瓦多和尼加拉瓜断交引起国际社会广泛关注。许多拉美国家呼吁萨尼两国以维护中美洲和平的大局为重，通过对话解决冲突。
    哥斯达黎加总统阿里亚斯27日表示，他对萨尔瓦多决定与尼加拉瓜断交感到遗憾。
    墨西哥外长费尔南多·索拉纳27日表示，墨西哥政府为萨尼断交使中美洲和平进程受影响而十分担忧。
    洪都拉斯总统阿斯科纳指出，萨尼断交“是个不好兆头”，
  “将危害中美洲和平进程”。
    危地马拉总统府发言人表示，危政府希望尽快召开中美洲5国首脑会议，以讨论采取实现中美洲和平的新措施。
    秘鲁总统加西亚27日在记者招待会上指出，萨尼断交不利于实现中美洲和平。
    本报加拉加斯11月28日电委内瑞拉总统佩雷斯昨天表示萨尔瓦多和尼加拉瓜中止外交关系是“令人遗憾”的。
