Title: Paper Card for Syphilis Diagnosis Passes Evaluation
Author: Shū Yú
Date: 1989-11-26
Page: 3

A type of paper card used for detecting and diagnosing syphilis has passed evaluation in Beijing today.

This domestically developed experimental paper card, created by the China Printing Science and Technology Research Institute in collaboration with the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences and the Peking Union Medical College, is used in conjunction with antigen reagents. After trials by Beijing Friendship Hospital and a survey of nearly 20,000 people, the card's quality has been proven to meet all requirements. Experts from the medical and printing industries believe that the card is convenient, fast, sensitive, accurate, prevents contamination, and is easy to promote. This achievement fills a domestic gap and reaches the level of similar international products.

### 诊断梅毒纸卡通过鉴定
舒瑜
1989-11-26
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　诊断梅毒纸卡通过鉴定
    本报北京十一月二十五日讯　一种对付性病、测试诊断梅毒的微量实验涂塑纸卡今日在京通过鉴定。
    国内首创的这种实验纸卡，是中国印刷科学技术研究所在中国医科院协和医科技术开发公司的协助下研制成功的，与抗原试剂配套使用。经过北京中日友好医院等单位试验和近两万人的普查，证明纸卡质量完全符合要求。医学界和印刷界的专家认为，该纸卡具备简便、快速、灵敏、准确、避免污染、易于推广的优点。此项成果填补了国内空白，达到国际同类产品的水平。
                                    （舒瑜）
