Title: Zhou Jiahua Meets with Japanese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 27th.

Vice Premier Zhou Jiahua met with a delegation from the Japan External Trade Organization (JETRO) this afternoon at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. The delegation, led by Chairman Yoshio Sakurai, consists of 60 members.

Zhou Jiahua extended a warm welcome to the delegation. He reaffirmed that China's policy of reform and opening up will not change. He told the Japanese guests that, whether looking at the current situation or the long-term prospects, the friendly economic cooperation between China and Japan should continue to develop, and there is great potential for economic cooperation between the two countries.

Yoshio Sakurai said that the members of the delegation are all concerned about how to further develop economic relations between Japan and China. He inquired about the situation of China's economic adjustment, and Zhou Jiahua provided a detailed response.

In the evening, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the China International Chamber of Commerce Chairman Zheng Hongye held a banquet to welcome the Japanese delegation.

### 邹家华会见日本访华团

1989-11-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　邹家华会见日本访华团
    新华社北京11月27日电　国务委员邹家华今天下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了以会长樱内义雄为团长的日本国际贸易促进协会访华团。
    邹家华对樱内义雄率领的60人大型访华团表示热烈欢迎。他重申，中国改革开放的政策不会改变。他对日本客人说，不论从当前来看，还是从长远来看，中日经济友好合作关系都应继续发展，两国经济合作很有前途。
    樱内义雄说，代表团成员都很关心今后如何进一步发展日中经济关系。他询问了中国经济调整的情况，邹家华作了详细的回答。
    晚上，中国国际贸易促进委员会和中国国际商会会长郑鸿业举行宴会，欢迎日本代表团。
