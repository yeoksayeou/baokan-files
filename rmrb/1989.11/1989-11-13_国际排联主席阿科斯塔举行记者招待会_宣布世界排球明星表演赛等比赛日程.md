Title: FIVB President Acosta Holds Press Conference to Announce Schedule for World Volleyball Star Exhibition and Other Competitions
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 3

The president of the International Volleyball Federation (FIVB), Acosta, held a press conference today in Osaka to announce the lineup and schedule for the World Volleyball Star Exhibition, as well as the schedule for the first Youth World Volleyball Championships.

According to the report from our correspondent Yu Qing in Osaka on November 12, Acosta announced that the World Volleyball Star Exhibition will feature the men's and women's gold medal winners from the 1988 Olympics: the men's team from the United States and the women's team from the Soviet Union. The opposing team will be composed of players selected from other top teams around the world. The matches will take place on December 16 and 17 in Singapore, with one men's and one women's match each day. The women's world star team includes Chinese players Yang Xiaojun, Jiang Ying, and Yang Xilan. The men's world star team does not include any Chinese players.

The first Youth World Volleyball Championships will be held from December 28, 1989, to January 9, 1990. The men's competition will be in the United Arab Emirates, and the women's competition will be in Brazil.

The sixth World Cup Volleyball Tournament will be held in 1991 in Japan, with 12 teams each for men and women participating for the first time. The winning team will qualify directly for the 1992 Olympics. The tournament will continue to be held every four years in Japan.

### 国际排联主席阿科斯塔举行记者招待会  宣布世界排球明星表演赛等比赛日程
于青
1989-11-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    国际排联主席阿科斯塔举行记者招待会
    宣布世界排球明星表演赛等比赛日程
    本报大阪11月12日电　记者于青报道：国际排球联合会主席阿科斯塔今天下午在大阪举行记者招待会，宣布了世界排球明星表演赛的名单和日程，以及第一届少年世界排球锦标赛的日程。
    世界排球明星表演赛的一方是去年奥运会排球男子冠军美国队和女子冠军苏联队。另一方是由其他世界排坛诸强各队中选出的队员组成。比赛于今年12月16、17日两天在新加坡举行，每天男女各赛一场。女子世界明星队的队员有中国的杨晓君、姜英、杨锡兰。男子世界明星队中没有中国队员。
    第一届少年世界排球锦标赛将于1989年12月28日到1990年1月9日期间举行。男子比赛在阿拉伯联合酋长国，女子比赛在巴西举行。
    第六届世界杯排球赛于两年后的1991年在日本举行。将首次由男女各12个队参加。获冠军的队可直接取得参加1992年奥运会的比赛资格。此后仍是每隔4年在日本举行一次。
