Title: The Fourth Round of Sino-Soviet Border Negotiations Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Edition: 2

The fourth round of Sino-Soviet border negotiations was held in Beijing from October 20 to 31. The head of the Chinese government delegation was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Tian Zengpei, and the head of the Soviet government delegation was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Rogachev.

It is understood that the two sides discussed in a serious atmosphere the issues of the border line in several areas where opinions differ, and made some progress.

Both sides agreed on the results of the joint aerial photography of the Sino-Soviet border conducted in 1988 and 1989.

Both sides pointed out that the Sino-Soviet high-level meeting in May 1989 is of great significance for the overall Sino-Soviet relations as well as for the border negotiations between the two countries. Both sides expressed their commitment to the agreements reached during the Sino-Soviet high-level meeting and will continue to discuss the resolution of the remaining issues in accordance with the principles agreed upon by both sides for solving the Sino-Soviet border problem.

### 第四轮中苏边界谈判在京举行

1989-11-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    第四轮中苏边界谈判在京举行
    新华社北京10月31日电  第四轮中苏边界谈判于10月20日至31日在北京举行。中国政府代表团团长为外交部副部长田曾佩，苏联政府代表团团长为外交部副部长罗高寿。
    据悉，双方在认真的气氛中讨论了一系列意见不一致地段的边界线走向问题，取得了一些进展。
    双方同意了在1988年和1989年对中苏边界所进行的联合航摄工作的结果。
    双方指出，1989年5月中苏高级会晤不论是对整个中苏关系，还是对两国边界谈判，都具有重大意义。双方表示将信守中苏高级会晤期间达成的有关协议，按照双方所商定的解决中苏边界问题的各项原则，继续讨论解决那些尚未解决的问题。
