Title: There Was No Life on Earth 3.8 Billion Years Ago
Author:
Date: 1989-11-30
Edition: 7

    American scientists believe that 3.8 billion years ago, there was no life on Earth. This might have been due to an asteroid impact that boiled away the Earth's oceans, creating a high-temperature steam atmosphere, making it difficult for life to survive.

    They have calculated that an asteroid with a diameter of 420 kilometers, traveling at a speed of 5909 kilometers per hour, could generate enough heat to boil away the Earth's oceans.

    American scientists believe that the Earth and its neighboring planets were formed approximately 4.5 billion years ago.

### 三十八亿年前地球上没有生命

1989-11-30
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

　　三十八亿年前地球上没有生命
    美国科学家认为，38亿年前地球上没有生命存在，可能是因小行星撞击地球，将地球上的海水煮干，形成高温蒸气大气层，从而使生命难以生存。
    他们通过计算证明，一颗直径为420公里的小行星以每小时5909公里的速度撞击地球，足以能产生将地球海水煮干的热量。
    美国科学家认为，地球及其附近的行星大约是在45亿年前形成的。
          （新）
