Title: Professor Zhong Wei Holds a Solo Concert
Author: Lǐ Zhōngchéng
Date: 1989-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 5th.

At the age of 68, Professor Zhong Wei, a soprano singer from the China Conservatory of Music, held a unique solo concert at the Beijing Concert Hall yesterday evening. It is rare for a soprano singer to hold a concert at such an advanced age in China, and it is also uncommon worldwide.

On the eve of the 72nd anniversary of the October Revolution in the Soviet Union, Professor Zhong Wei introduced "Soviet and Russian Music Works" to the people of Beijing with her plain and cheerful voice, earning enthusiastic applause from the Chinese and foreign audience present. After watching the performance, Comrade Wu Xiuquan repeatedly praised, "Excellent."

### 老教授仲伟举办独唱会
李忠诚
1989-11-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    老教授仲伟举办独唱会
    据新华社北京11月5日电　（记者李忠诚）68岁高龄的中国音乐学院女高音歌唱家仲伟教授，昨晚在北京音乐厅举行了一场别开生面的独唱音乐会。在古稀之龄举办音乐会的女高音歌唱家在我国还是罕见的，在世界上也是不多的。
    在庆祝苏联十月革命72周年前夕，仲伟教授用质朴和爽朗的歌声把“苏联及俄罗斯音乐作品”介绍给了首都人民，赢得了与会的中外听众的热烈掌声。伍修权同志看完演出后连声称赞：“很好。”
