Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 2

Luo Gan Meets with Japanese Guests

	BEIJING, Nov. 7 (Xinhua) — Luo Gan, Secretary-General of the State Council, met with a delegation from Fujitsu Limited of Japan at the Great Hall of the People this morning. He pointed out that China is committed to reform and opening up, not only to introduce advanced equipment and technology from abroad, but also to introduce foreign intelligence, invite experts to guide, and send people abroad for training.

	During the meeting, both sides exchanged views on talent cultivation and exchange between China and Japan.

	The Fujitsu Limited delegation is in China at the invitation of the China International Talent Exchange Foundation. This morning, the China International Talent Exchange Foundation and Fujitsu Limited signed an agreement on the training of Chinese computer software researchers in Japan.

### 罗干会见日本客人
孙毅
1989-11-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    罗干会见日本客人
    本报北京11月7日讯　记者孙毅报道：国务院秘书长罗干今天上午在人民大会堂会见日本富士通株式会社访华团时指出，中国坚持改革开放，除了要引进先进的设备和先进的技术外，还要引进国外的智力，请专家进来指导，派人出去培训。
    会见时，宾主双方就中日两国之间人才培养、人才交流等问题交换了意见。
    富士通株式会社客人是应中国国际人才交流协会的邀请来华访问的。今天上午，中国国际人才交流协会与富士通株式会社就中国计算机软件研究人员赴日研修事项签署了一份协议书。
