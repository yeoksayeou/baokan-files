Title: Southwest Rich in Geothermal Resources with 933 Hot Springs Exposed
Author: Chen Hua
Date: 1989-11-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 26th.

The Southwest Economic Region currently has 933 exposed hot springs, with a total daily flow of 990,000 tons, accounting for approximately one-third of China's total hot springs.

A report from the Chengdu Hydrological Center of the China Institute of Hydrogeology and Engineering Geology indicates that the distribution of hot springs in the Southwest Economic Region is widespread. Yunnan, Sichuan, Guizhou provinces, and the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region all have rich distributions, with an average density of about 9.5 hot springs per square kilometer, and the highest density reaching over 130 hot springs. Among these, Yunnan Province has the most, accounting for 52% of the total number of hot springs in the region.

Geothermal resources are a renewable resource that can be widely used in industry, agriculture, medicine, and tourism. In the current context of high energy consumption and tight supply, developing and utilizing geothermal resources is of significant importance.

### 西南地热资源丰富　出露热泉达933处
陈华
1989-11-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西南地热资源丰富
　　出露热泉达933处
    本报成都11月26日电　记者陈华报道：西南经济区目前出露的热泉已有933处，总流量每日达99万吨，约占我国热泉总数的1／3。
    中国水文地质工程地质勘察院成都水文中心的成果报告表明，西南经济区内温热泉出露广泛，云南、四川、贵州省和广西壮族自治区均有丰富的分布，平均分布密度每平方公里约为9.5处，最高达130余处。其中云南居首，占全区热泉总数的52％。
    地下热水资源是一种可再生的资源，可广泛利用于工业、农业、医疗和旅游体育事业。在当今能源大量耗用、需求紧俏的情况下，开发利用地下热水资源，具有重要的意义。
