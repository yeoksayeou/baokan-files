Title: International Architecture Academic Seminar Held in Beijing
Author: Zhang Yanhui
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 2

An international architecture academic seminar on "Cities and Architecture in Transitioning Asia" was held in Beijing today. The seminar, jointly organized by the Asia-Pacific Region of the International Union of Architects, the Chinese Society of Architects, which recently joined the Asian Architects Association, and Tsinghua University, attracted 50 foreign experts and scholars from 13 countries and regions including the United Kingdom, the United States, Sweden, and Japan, as well as 47 experts and scholars from mainland China.

### 国际建筑学术研讨会在京召开
张燕辉
1989-11-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国际建筑学术研讨会在京召开
    据新华社北京11月27日电　（记者张燕辉）转变中的亚洲城市与建筑国际学术研讨会今天在北京召开。由国际建筑师协会亚澳区、刚刚加入亚洲建筑师协会的中国建筑学会和清华大学共同主办的这次研讨会吸引了来自英国、美国、瑞典、日本等13个国家和地区的50名外国专家学者以及我国大陆的47名专家学者。
