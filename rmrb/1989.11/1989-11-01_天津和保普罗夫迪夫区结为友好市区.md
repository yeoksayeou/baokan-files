Title: Tianjin and Plovdiv District Establish Sister City Relationship
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

Tianjin and Bulgaria's Plovdiv District have established a sister city relationship. Tianjin's Acting Mayor Nie Bichu and the Chairman of the Plovdiv District People's Committee, Krestenova, formally signed the agreement to establish the sister city relationship on October 14 in Sofia.

### 天津和保普罗夫迪夫区结为友好市区

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    天津和保普罗夫迪夫区结为友好市区
    中国天津市与保加利亚普罗夫迪夫大区结为友好市区。天津市代市长聂璧初和普罗夫迪夫大区人民委员会主席克雷斯滕诺娃10月14日在索非亚正式签订了两市区结为友好市区的协议书。　（据新华社）
