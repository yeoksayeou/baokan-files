Title: Three National Fundamental Standards Begin Implementation
Author: Zhū Jiànhóng
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 2

Today, the State Administration for Quality Supervision and Inspection of China announced three important national fundamental standards. These three standards are: "General Principles and Methods for Establishing Terms," "Guidelines for Standardization Work: Regulations for Writing Terminology Standards," and "Adult Human Body Dimensions in China." These three standards fill gaps in China's national standards and have been officially implemented on July 1 and August 1, respectively.

A spokesperson for the State Administration for Quality Supervision and Inspection of China recently stated that the implementation of these three standards will help promote the development of China's information technology and improve labor efficiency.

### 三项国家基础标准开始实施
朱剑红
1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    三项国家基础标准开始实施
    本报北京11月16日讯　记者朱剑红报道：今天，国家技术监督局发布了三项重要国家基础标准的信息，这三项标准是：《确立术语的一般原则与方法》、《标准化工作导则　术语标准编写规定》、《中国成年人人体尺寸》。这三项标准均填补了我国家标准的空白，已分别于7月1日和8月1日正式实施。
    国家技术监督局发言人近日发表谈话说，这三项标准的实施，有助于推动我国信息技术的发展和劳动效率的提高。
