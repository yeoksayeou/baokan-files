Title: Pelé to Run for Brazilian Presidency in the Next Election
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

Brazil's former football superstar Pelé announced on November 7 that he hopes to run in the 1994 presidential election.

Pelé's real name is Edson Nascimento, and he is 49 years old. He has won the World Cup three times with the Brazilian team.

Pelé said on November 7 that he does not plan to run in this year's presidential election but intends to participate in the next one in five years.

He said that running for president requires more than just fame, and he will put forward his own political views.

### 贝利将参加下届巴西总统竞选

1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    贝利将参加下届巴西总统竞选
    新华社北京11月9日电　巴西前超级足球明星贝利7日宣布，他希望参加1994年下届巴西总统竞选。
    贝利的真名叫埃德松·纳西门托，今年49岁，他曾在三届世界杯足球赛上为巴西队3次夺冠立下赫赫战功。
    贝利7日说，他不准备参加本届总统竞选，而是打算5年后参加下一届的竞选。
    他说，竞选总统光靠知名度不够，他将提出自己的政治观念。
