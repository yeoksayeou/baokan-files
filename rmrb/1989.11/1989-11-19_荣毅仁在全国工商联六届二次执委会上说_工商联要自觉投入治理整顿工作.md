Title: Rong Yiren Speaks at the Sixth National Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
Author: Yuan Jianda
Date: 1989-11-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 18th.

The sixth national committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held its second session this morning in Beijing.

Rong Yiren, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, presided over the meeting and delivered a speech. He said that the Federation of Industry and Commerce is a people's organization of the industrial and commercial sectors. It should play the role of a private chamber of commerce, propagate the significance and policies of the rectification and adjustment, actively conduct investigations and research, summarize experiences, propose suggestions, reflect the opinions and demands of the industrial and commercial sectors, participate in politics and policy-making, and assist the Party and the government in understanding the situation and overcoming difficulties. It is necessary to further mobilize the broad membership to carry forward the tradition of "patriotism, professionalism, and law-abiding," and actively participate in the work of rectification and adjustment.

Sun Fuling, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, made a work report to this session on behalf of the Standing Committee, pointing out that the work of rectification and adjustment is closely related to the industrial and commercial sectors. The Federation of Industry and Commerce can play a role in assisting the Party and the government in rectification and adjustment, promoting opening up, and the reunification of the motherland.

It is understood that the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce has this year recruited more than 16,000 new members, bringing the total number of enterprise members, group members, and individual members to 500,000. Wan Shaofen, Vice Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, also spoke at the meeting.

### 荣毅仁在全国工商联六届二次执委会上说  工商联要自觉投入治理整顿工作
袁建达
1989-11-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    荣毅仁在全国工商联六届二次执委会上说
    工商联要自觉投入治理整顿工作
    本报北京11月18日讯　记者袁建达报道：全国工商联六届二次执委会今天上午在这里开幕。
    全国工商联主席荣毅仁主持今天的会议并讲了话。他说，工商联是工商界的人民团体，要发挥民间商会的作用，宣传治理整顿的意义和政策，积极开展调查研究，总结经验，提出意见，反映工商业界的意见和要求，参政议政，协助党和政府了解情况、克服困难。要进一步动员广大会员发扬“爱国、敬业、守法”的传统，自觉投入治理整顿工作。
    全国工商联副主席孙孚凌代表常务委员会向本届执委会作工作报告，指出：治理整顿工作与工商业界关系密切，工商联可以在协助党和政府治理整顿、促进对外开放和祖国统一的工作中有所作为。
    据了解，全国工商联今年又吸收新会员1.6万多个，企业会员、团体会员和个人会员总数达到50万个。中共中央统战部副部长万绍芬到会讲了话。
