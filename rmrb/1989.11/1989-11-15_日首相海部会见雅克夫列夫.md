Title: Japanese Prime Minister Kaifu Meets Yakolev
Author:
Date: 1989-11-15
Edition: 4

Japanese Prime Minister Kaifu Tsumu met with the head of the Soviet Union's Supreme Soviet delegation, Politburo member and Secretariat Secretary Yakolev, who is visiting Japan, at the Prime Minister's official residence on the evening of the 13th. Both sides agreed to work towards establishing a permanent relationship of trust between Japan and the Soviet Union.

According to a Jiji Press report, Kaifu stated during the talks that the current period is an important time for Japan and the Soviet Union to strengthen mutual understanding and trust. He expressed his intention to use Soviet leader Gorbachev's visit to Japan as an opportunity to promote the development of Japan-Soviet relations.

### 日首相海部会见雅克夫列夫

1989-11-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日首相海部会见雅克夫列夫
    日本首相海部俊树十三日晚在首相官邸会见了正在日本访问的苏联最高苏维埃会议代表团团长、苏共政治局委员、书记处书记雅克夫列夫。双方一致认为要为建立日苏间永久的信赖关系而努力。
    据时事社报道，海部在会谈时指出，当前是日苏双方加强相互理解、相互信赖的重要时期。他表示要以苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫的访日为契机，推进日苏关系的发展。
