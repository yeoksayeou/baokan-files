Title: Kim Il-sung Chairman Says Cambodia Should Become a Unified, Independent, and Territorialy Intact Nation
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 3

Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, said today at a banquet held in the Kumsusan Palace of the Sun for Prince Norodom Sihanouk, who is visiting Pyongyang, that Cambodia must become a unified, independent, and territorially intact, peaceful, neutral, and non-aligned nation.

Kim Il-sung said that the current situation in Cambodia is still very complex. The Cambodian people have great expectations for Prince Norodom Sihanouk regarding the future of Cambodia. The DPRK will continue to respect and support Prince Norodom Sihanouk's independent stance in resolving the Cambodian issue.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, in his speech, stated that the Democratic Kampuchea and the Cambodian people stand firmly on the side of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The DK will, at any time, fully support the DPRK's just stance and policy of independent and peaceful reunification.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk said that the national resistance forces striving for the complete liberation of Cambodia still face many difficulties on their forward march. However, these difficulties can be overcome. The Cambodian resistance forces are currently achieving one victory after another.

### 金日成主席表示  柬埔寨应成为统一独立领土完整国家

1989-11-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    金日成主席表示
    柬埔寨应成为统一独立领土完整国家
    新华社平壤10月31日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国主席金日成今天在锦绣山议事堂为正在平壤访问的西哈努克亲王67岁生日举行的宴会上说，柬埔寨必须成为一个统一独立、领土完整，和平、中立、不结盟的国家。
    金日成说，当前，柬埔寨问题仍然很复杂，柬埔寨人民在柬埔寨未来的命运问题上，对西哈努克亲王寄予很大的期望。朝鲜今后仍将尊重和支持西哈努克亲王在解决柬埔寨问题上的自主立场。
    西哈努克在致词中表示，民主柬埔寨和柬埔寨人民站在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国一边的立场没有变。民柬在任何时候都全力支持朝鲜自主和平统一的正当立场和方针。
    西哈努克表示争取柬埔寨彻底解放的民族抵抗力量在前进道路上虽然还有许多困难，但这些困难是能够克服的，柬埔寨抵抗力量目前正在取得一个接一个的胜利。
