Title: Nicaraguan Government Forces Counterattack Rebel Forces; Nicaragua Denies Attacking Honduran Border Outpost
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Managua, November 5th.

Nicaraguan Sixth Military Region Commander Colonel Salvador Terra on November 5th disclosed to the press that since the government announced the suspension of the unilateral ceasefire on November 1st, the Sandinista People's Army has killed or wounded 21 rebel fighters in the past four days of combat.

Salvador Terra pointed out that the Sandinista People's Army is fully prepared for combat to pursue more than 1,000 rebel fighters who have recently infiltrated the San Jose de Bocay and Rio Blanco mountain areas. According to a report from the Nicaraguan Ministry of Defense, approximately 15 government troops were killed or wounded in the past four days of combat.

On November 4th, Colonel Huilock, the head of the Sandinista People's Army's Military Intelligence Bureau, also emphasized to "Radio Sandinista" that after the government announced the suspension of the unilateral ceasefire, it has moved troops to the northern, central, and southern mountain regions of Nicaragua to "strengthen Nicaragua's defense system."

Additionally, U.S. State Department spokesman Richard Boucher has confirmed to the press that approximately 2,000 Nicaraguan rebel fighters have infiltrated and are active within Nicaragua.

Xinhua News Agency, Managua, November 5th.

The Nicaraguan Ministry of Defense on November 5th denied that the Sandinista People's Army had attacked a Honduran military border outpost.

Honduran Armed Forces spokesman Colonel Suarez on November 2nd stated that on October 30th at 21:00, the Sandinista forces had attacked a guard post near the Honduran customs office of Las Manos.

In response, Nicaraguan military spokesman Pasos emphasized that "our troops have never attacked Honduran military outposts, whether on October 30th or at any other time." Pasos accused the Honduran military of repeatedly attacking Sandinista troops stationed in border areas.

Reports indicate that since 1979, there have been over 600 armed conflicts along the approximately 900-kilometer border between Nicaragua and Honduras. About 12,000 Nicaraguan rebel fighters have long been stationed on the Honduran side of the Nicaragua-Honduras border. Recently, they have frequently entered Nicaraguan territory to carry out sabotage and harassment activities.

### 尼政府军反击反政府武装  尼否认军队曾攻击洪边防哨所

1989-11-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    尼政府军反击反政府武装
    尼否认军队曾攻击洪边防哨所
    新华社马那瓜11月5日电　尼加拉瓜第六军区领导人萨瓦尔铁拉上校5日向报界透露，自政府11月1日宣布中止单方面停火后，在最近4天的战斗行动中，桑地诺人民军共打死打伤21名反政府武装分子。
    萨瓦尔铁拉指出，桑地诺人民军已经作好了战斗准备，以追击最近渗透到圣何塞—德博卡伊和里奥布兰科山区活动的1000余名反政府武装分子。另据尼国防部的报告透露，在最近4天的战斗中，政府军方面约有15人伤亡。
    桑地诺人民军军事情报局领导人惠洛克中校4日也对“桑地诺电台”强调指出，政府在宣布中止单方面停火后，已经向尼北部、中部和南部山区调动了军队，以“加强尼加拉瓜的防卫体制”。
    此外，美国国务院发言人鲍彻已向报界证实，大约2000名尼加拉瓜反政府武装分子渗入尼境内活动。
    新华社马那瓜11月5日电　尼加拉瓜国防部5日否认桑地诺人民军曾攻击过洪都拉斯军队的边防哨所。
    洪都拉斯武装部队发言人苏亚雷斯上校2日说，10月30日21时，桑地诺军队曾进攻过洪都拉斯拉斯马诺斯海关附近的一个警卫哨所。
    为此，尼加拉瓜军队发言人帕索斯强调指出，无论是10月30日还是其它时候，“我们的军队从未攻击过洪都拉斯军队的哨所”。帕索斯指控洪都拉斯军队最近多次袭击驻扎在边境地区的桑地诺军队。
    据报道，1979年以来，在尼、洪两国约900公里的边境线上，曾发生过600多次武装冲突。1.2万名尼加拉瓜反政府武装分子长期驻扎在尼、洪边境的洪都拉斯一侧。最近，他们多次进入尼境内进行破坏和骚扰活动。
