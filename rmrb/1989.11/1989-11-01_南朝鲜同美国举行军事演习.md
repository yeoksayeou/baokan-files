Title: South Korea and the United States Conduct Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Edition: 3

South Korea's Ministry of Defense spokesman announced that from October 30 to November 9, South Korea and the United States will jointly conduct a military exercise codenamed "89 Bald Eagle."

The spokesman claimed that this military exercise is being held in response to a supposed "invasion from the North."

### 南朝鲜同美国举行军事演习

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南朝鲜同美国举行军事演习
    南朝鲜国防部发言人宣布，从十月三十日至十一月九日，南朝鲜和美国将联合举行代号为“八九秃鹰”的军事演习。
    这位发言人声称，这次军事演习是针对所谓“北方的南侵袭击”而举行的。
