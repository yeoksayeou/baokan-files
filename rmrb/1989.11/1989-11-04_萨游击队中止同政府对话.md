Title: Salvador Guerrillas Suspend Dialogue with Government
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 3rd.

Salvador Guerrillas Suspend Dialogue with Government

The Salvadoran guerrillas decided on November 2nd not to participate in the third round of talks with the government scheduled for November 20th and 21st in Caracas, the capital of Venezuela.

In a statement released in San Salvador, the Salvadoran guerrillas cited the main reason for this decision as the recent attack on the headquarters of the National Labor Union of El Salvador.

According to reports, a powerful bomb exploded at the headquarters of the National Labor Union on October 31st. The guerrillas accused the military's far-right faction of being responsible for the incident.

### 萨游击队中止同政府对话

1989-11-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    萨游击队中止同政府对话
    新华社北京11月3日电  圣萨尔瓦多消息：萨尔瓦多游击队2日决定不参加11月20日和21日在委内瑞拉首都加拉加斯同政府举行的第3轮对话。
    萨尔瓦多游击队在圣萨尔瓦多发表的一份公报中指出，作出这一决定的主要原因是萨尔瓦多全国劳工联盟总部本星期遭到袭击。
    据报道，10月31日，一枚重磅炸弹在全国劳工联盟总部爆炸，游击队指控此事是军内极右势力干的。
