Title: The Asian Badminton Championships to be Held in Shanghai in December
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Edition: 3

16 badminton teams will participate in the 1989 Asian Badminton Championships, which will be held in Shanghai from December 18 to 23. This competition will feature only men's team events.

The badminton teams from Indonesia, Japan, Malaysia, India, South Korea, Chinese Taipei, Thailand, and China will directly enter the final round of the competition.

The remaining 8 teams will be divided into two groups for preliminary round-robin matches. The first group will include teams from Sri Lanka, Pakistan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Bangladesh. The second group will include teams from Hong Kong, Singapore, Nepal, and Iran.

The top two teams from each group will advance to the final round of the competition.

The final round groupings will be as follows: Group A will include Indonesia, Japan, and the second-place team from the first preliminary group; Group B will include Malaysia, India, and the first-place team from the first preliminary group; Group C will include South Korea, Chinese Taipei, and the first-place team from the second preliminary group; Group D will include China, Thailand, and the second-place team from the second preliminary group.

### 亚洲羽毛球锦标赛十二月在上海举行

1989-11-23
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　亚洲羽毛球锦标赛十二月在上海举行
    新华社北京11月22日电　16支羽毛球队将参加于12月18日至23日在上海举行的1989年亚洲羽毛球锦标赛。这次比赛只进行男子团体赛。
    印度尼西亚、日本、马来西亚、印度、南朝鲜、中国台北、泰国和中国8支羽毛球队将直接参加决赛阶段的比赛。
    另外8支球队将分成两个组先进行小组单循环预赛，第一组有斯里兰卡、巴基斯坦、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和孟加拉国队，第二组有香港、新加坡、尼泊尔和伊朗队。
    列两个组前两名的队将有资格参加决赛阶段的比赛。
    决赛阶段的分组情况是：A组，印度尼西亚、日本、第一小组的第二名；B组，马来西亚、印度、第一小组的第一名；C组，南朝鲜、中国台北、第二小组的第一名；D组，中国、泰国、第二小组的第二名。
