Title: I Donate a Primary School to Palestinian Refugees
Author:
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 4

The inauguration ceremony for a primary school donated by China to Palestinian refugees in Jordan was held on November 15 in Amman.

The school is located in the Bakka Palestinian refugee camp, about 20 kilometers northeast of Amman, the capital of Jordan. The total construction cost of the school is 800,000 US dollars, and it was built by the China Nonferrous Metal Industry Foreign Engineering and Construction Co., Ltd. The total building area is 2,624 square meters. All teaching equipment in the school is provided by the Chinese government. Currently, 2,100 students are studying in two shifts at the school.

### 我赠送给巴勒斯坦难民一所小学

1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我赠送给巴勒斯坦难民一所小学
    新华社安曼电　中国赠送给在约旦的巴勒斯坦难民一所男子小学的落成典礼15日在安曼举行。
    这所学校位于约旦首都安曼东北20公里处的巴卡巴勒斯坦难民营内。学校总造价为80万美元，由中国有色对外工程公司承建，建筑总面积为2624平方米。校内教学设备均由中国政府提供。现有2100名学生分两部制在该校就读。
