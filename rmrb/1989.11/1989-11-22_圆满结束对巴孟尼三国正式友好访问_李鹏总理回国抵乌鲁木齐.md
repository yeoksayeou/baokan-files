Title: Premier Li Peng Returns to the Country, Arrives in Urumqi After Successfully Completing Official Friendly Visits to Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal
Author: Hé Píng, Wāng Yǒu Fù
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 1

Premier Li Peng and his wife, Ju Lin, successfully concluded their official friendly visits to Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal and returned to the country. They arrived in Urumqi, the capital of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, by special plane from Kathmandu at noon today.

Wang Enmao, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Advisory Committee, Song Hanliang, Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Party Committee, and Tiemu'er Da'wamaiti, Deputy Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Party Committee and Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, along with other party, government, and military leaders in Xinjiang, were at the airport to warmly welcome Premier Li Peng's arrival. Uygur and Han ethnic minority Young Pioneers presented Premier Li Peng and Mrs. Ju Lin with flowers.

Accompanying Premier Li Peng on his visit, Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin, and Vice Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, He Qizong, also returned to Urumqi by the same plane.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 21 - Premier Li Peng of the State Council of China sent a telegram to King Birendra of Nepal today after concluding his visit to Nepal and departing from Kathmandu. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to the King, Queen, and the government and people of Nepal for their warm reception.

The telegram stated: "I exchanged views with Your Majesty on further strengthening Sino-Nepal friendly cooperation relations and on international and regional issues of common concern to both sides, and we reached broad consensus. I am deeply satisfied with this. Strengthening Sino-Nepal friendly cooperation relations is the common wish of the governments and peoples of both countries. I believe that with the joint efforts of both sides, the deep-rooted Sino-Nepal friendship will definitely continue to achieve new developments."

### 圆满结束对巴孟尼三国正式友好访问　李鹏总理回国抵乌鲁木齐
何平　汪有富
1989-11-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    圆满结束对巴孟尼三国正式友好访问
　　李鹏总理回国抵乌鲁木齐
    新华社乌鲁木齐11月21日电　（记者何平、汪有富）国务院总理李鹏及夫人朱琳圆满结束对巴基斯坦、孟加拉和尼泊尔三国的正式友好访问回国，今天中午乘专机从加德满都抵达新疆维吾尔自治区首府乌鲁木齐。
    全国政协副主席、新疆维吾尔自治区顾问委员会主任王恩茂，新疆维吾尔自治区党委书记宋汉良，自治区党委副书记、自治区主席铁木尔·达瓦买提，以及新疆党政军其他负责同志到机场热烈欢迎李鹏总理的到来。维吾尔族和汉族少先队员向李鹏总理和夫人朱琳献了鲜花。
    随同李鹏总理出访的外交部长钱其琛、经贸部部长郑拓彬、中国人民解放军副总参谋长何其宗等同机回到乌鲁木齐。
    新华社北京11月21日电　中国国务院总理李鹏今天结束对尼泊尔的访问离开加德满都之后打电报给尼泊尔国王比兰德拉，对国王和王后、尼泊尔政府和人民的热情接待表示衷心的感谢。
    电报说：“我同陛下就进一步加强中尼友好合作关系以及双方共同关心的国际和地区问题交换了意见，取得了广泛的一致。对此，我深为满意。加强中尼友好合作关系是两国政府和人民的共同愿望。我相信在我们双方的共同努力下，具有深厚基础的中尼友谊一定会不断取得新的发展。”
