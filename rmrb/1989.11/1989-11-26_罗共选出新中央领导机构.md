Title: Romanian Communist Party Elects New Central Leadership
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

The new Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party held its first plenary session today, electing a 47-member Central Executive Committee and a 9-member Central Secretariat.

All 17 members of the previous Central Executive Committee were retained, with 4 new members added: I. Matei, the Central Committee Secretary and Head of the Propaganda Department; B. Petrescu, the First Secretary of the Bucharest City Committee and Mayor of Bucharest; I. Radu, Deputy Prime Minister; and I. Totu, Chairman of the State Planning and Technical Materials Supply Commission.

Among the 26 alternate members of the Central Executive Committee, 8 are new. Two of the previous alternate members have been promoted to full members of the Central Executive Committee.

The newly formed Central Secretariat consists of General Secretary Nicolae Ceaușescu and 8 other secretaries. The composition of the Secretariat remains unchanged.

### 罗共选出新中央领导机构

1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　罗共选出新中央领导机构
    新华社布加勒斯特11月24日电　罗共新的中央委员会今天举行首次全体会议，选出了由47人组成的罗共中央执行委员会以及由9人组成的罗共中央书记处。
    上届当选的17名中央执委全部留任，4名新的中央执委是：罗共中央书记兼中宣部长伊·马太、罗共布加勒斯特市委第一书记兼市长巴·彼得雷斯库、政府副总理扬·拉杜以及国家计划和技术物资供应委员会主席伊·托图。
    在当选的26名中央候补执委中有8名是新任。原来的中央候补执委中有2人升为中央执委。
    新产生的罗共中央书记处由总书记尼·齐奥塞斯库和8名书记组成。书记处原班人马未动。
