Title: The 8th Meeting of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) to be Held on November 16
Author:
Date: 1989-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 13th.

The 8th meeting of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) will be held in Beijing on November 16. The meeting's central theme is to study and implement the spirit of the Fifth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

The decision was made at the chairman's meeting of the CPPCC on November 11.

### 七届全国政协常委会16日召开第8次会议

1989-11-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    七届全国政协常委会16日召开第8次会议
    新华社北京11月13日电　全国政协11月11日主席会议决定：第七届政协全国委员会常务委员会第8次会议将于11月16日在北京举行。会议的中心议题是学习贯彻中共十三届五中全会精神。
