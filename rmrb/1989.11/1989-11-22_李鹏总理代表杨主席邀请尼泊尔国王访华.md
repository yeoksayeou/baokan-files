Title: Premier Li Peng Invites Nepal's King to Visit China on Behalf of Chairman Yang
Author: Li Zongxing
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 4

Premier Li Peng invites Nepal's King to visit China

	People's Daily, Kathmandu, November 20th - Correspondent Li Zongxing reports: King Birendra hosted and dined with Premier Li Peng at the Nepalese Royal Palace this evening.

	During the meeting, Premier Li, on behalf of Chairman Yang Shangkun, invited King Birendra to visit China again at his convenience. The King happily accepted the invitation and expressed his thanks.

### 李鹏总理代表杨主席邀请尼泊尔国王访华
史宗星
1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏总理代表杨主席邀请尼泊尔国王访华
    本报加德满都11月20日电　记者史宗星报道：比兰德拉国王今晚在尼泊尔王宫会见并设宴招待李鹏总理。
    李总理在会见中代表杨尚昆主席邀请比兰德拉国王在他方便的时候再次访华，国王愉快地接受了邀请并对此表示感谢。
