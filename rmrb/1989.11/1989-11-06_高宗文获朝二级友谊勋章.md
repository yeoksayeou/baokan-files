Title: Gao Zongwen Receives the Second-Class Friendship Medal from the DPRK
Author: Shang Xinwen
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 4

Shanghai Translation Publishing House deputy editor Gao Zongwen recently received the Second-Class Friendship Medal conferred by the Chairman of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung.
