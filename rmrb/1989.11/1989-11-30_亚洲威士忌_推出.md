Title: "Asian Whisky" Launched
Author: Nán Jiàn
Date: 1989-11-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 29th — (Reporter Nán Jiàn) The "Asian Whisky" jointly developed by the Yibin Wuliangye Liquor Factory and the Sichuan Provincial Institute of Traditional Chinese Medicine has recently been launched and has received favorable reviews from both domestic and international consumers. The "Asian Whisky" is crafted using high-quality grains and natural plants, brewed to a 42-degree alcohol content, which is popular internationally. It combines the typical characteristics of whisky with the unique features of traditional Chinese fine liquors.

### “亚洲威士忌”推出
南建
1989-11-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　“亚洲威士忌”推出
    据新华社成都11月29日电　（记者南建）宜宾五粮液酒厂与四川省中药研究所共同研制的“亚洲威士忌”最近问世，并受到国内外消费者的好评。“亚洲威士忌”选用优质粮谷和天然植物为原料，按照42度国际流行酒度精酿而成。它既有威士忌的典型风格，又兼有中国传统名酿特色。
