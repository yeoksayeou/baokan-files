Title: UHF Doppler Wind Radar System Developed
Author: Zhang Heping
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 2

People's Daily, Beijing, November 14th.

A major national key scientific and technological research project, the "UHF Doppler Wind Radar System," has been successfully developed. The system passed a technical appraisal in Beijing yesterday. A committee of experts in meteorology and radar technology concluded that the successful development of the Doppler wind radar signifies that China has mastered this advanced atmospheric remote sensing technology and has timely entered the ranks of the world's advanced applications in this field.

This new type of radar, jointly developed by the Institute of Atmospheric Physics under the State Meteorological Administration and the 23rd Institute of the Ministry of Aerospace, utilizes atmospheric remote sensing principles to automatically, highly accurately, and all-weather monitor the convective layer wind field. It is an advanced atmospheric detection means and modern equipment for contemporary meteorology, aviation, aerospace, military, marine, and environmental protection services. After a year of use and testing, especially during the summer disaster weather forecasting experiments in the Beijing, Tianjin, and Hebei regions, the radar has proven to be reliable and capable of continuous stable operation.

### 国家重点科技攻关项目  多普勒测风雷达系统制成
张何平
1989-11-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国家重点科技攻关项目
    多普勒测风雷达系统制成
    本报北京11月14日讯　记者张何平报道：国家“七五”重点科技攻关项目——“UHF多普勒测风雷达系统”研制成功，昨天在北京通过技术鉴定。由气象和雷达研究领域专家组成的技术鉴定委员会认为，多普勒测风雷达的研制成功，标志我国已经掌握这项先进的大气遥感技术，并及时跨入这一应用技术领域世界先进水平行列。
    由国家气象局气象科学研究院和航空航天部二院23所共同研制的这种新型雷达，利用大气遥感原理，可自动、高时空分辨和全天候地监测对流层风场，是一种为现代气象、航空、航天、军事以及海洋和环境保护业务使用的先进的大气探测手段和现代化装备。经过一年来的使用和考核，特别是今年夏季京、津、冀地区灾害性天气预报业务实验表明，这部雷达性能可靠，可连续稳定运行。
