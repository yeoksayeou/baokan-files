Title: Delivering Grain Amidst Snow
Author: Zhou Que
Date: 1989-11-19
Edition: 1

    Delivering Grain Amidst Snow
    Recently, heavy snowfall blanketed the entire province of Heilongjiang, but the enthusiasm of farmers to deliver grain to the state has not diminished. This is a scene of farmers from Dongcun Village, Dasi Station Township, Boli County, delivering their final batch of grain to the state despite the snow.

                      Xinhua News Agency photographer Zhou Que (fax photo)

专栏：

    冒雪交公粮
    近日，黑龙江省内普降大雪，但农民交粮热情未减。这是勃利县大四站镇东村农民溪凤霞冒雪将自己和邻居家的最后一批公粮交给国家。
