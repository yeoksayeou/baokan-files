Title: Mass Poisoning Incident in Luofeng and Zhaoqing: 21 Dead from Fake Alcohol
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 6th.

From November 1 to 2, severe alcohol poisoning incidents occurred in five townships of Luofeng County in Guangdong Province—Jiaxi, Jiadong, Jieshi, Jiebei—and in Zhaoqing City. A total of 21 people died, and 78 were hospitalized for emergency treatment.

Investigations revealed that the cause of the poisoning was the consumption of fake alcohol with methanol content exceeding the national standard by over 100 times.

### 陆丰肇庆发生中毒事故二十一人饮用假酒死亡

1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    陆丰肇庆发生中毒事故二十一人饮用假酒死亡
    新华社广州11月6日电　11月1日至2日广东省陆丰县甲西、甲东、碣石、碣北等5个乡镇和肇庆市相继发生严重酒精中毒事故，共死亡21人，留医院抢救78人。
    据查中毒原因都是喝了甲醇含量超过国家标准百倍以上的假酒所致。
