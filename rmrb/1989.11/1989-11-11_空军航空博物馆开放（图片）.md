Title: Air Force Aviation Museum Opens
Author: Xú Jiànzhōng
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 3

The Air Force Aviation Museum opens to the public on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Air Force.

The Air Force Aviation Museum is located at the foot of Dàtāngshān Hill in Changping County, northern Beijing. It makes full use of outdoor exhibition areas and underground storage facilities to house over 100 aircraft of various types from different historical periods, as well as hundreds of types of ground-to-air missiles, anti-aircraft guns, and radar equipment.

Top image: The fighter jet flown by Air Force Commander Wang Hai during the Korean War, which shot down or damaged 9 enemy aircraft.

Right image: Air Force high-altitude artillery, missiles, and supersonic jet fighters.

### 空军航空博物馆开放（图片）
徐建中
1989-11-11
第3版(综合)
专栏：图片新闻

    空军航空博物馆开放
    在人民空军建立40年之际，空军航空博物馆正式向中外游人开放。
    空军航空博物馆坐落在北京市北郊的昌平县大汤山脚下，它充分利用外场和洞库收藏了各个历史时期的各种型号飞机100多架，以及地空导弹、高炮、雷达等几百种武器装备。
    上图：空军司令员王海抗美援朝战争中驾驶过的击伤、击落敌机9架的战斗机。
    右图：空军部队高射炮、导弹和超音速喷气式战斗机。
