Title: Soviet Civil Aircraft Crashes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 4

A Soviet civil aircraft crashed. According to a report by TASS on the 22nd, a Soviet An-24 passenger plane crashed on landing at an airport in the western Siberian region of Tyumen on the 21st. Twenty-eight passengers and six crew members were killed, and eight others were seriously injured. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联一架客机失事

1989-11-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联一架客机失事
    据塔斯社二十二日报道，苏一架安—二十四客机二十一日在西伯利亚西部秋明州机场着陆时出事，二十八名乘客及六名机组人员遇难，八人受重伤。（据新华社）
