Title: President Nyerere Arrives in Beijing for a Visit
Author: Yang Zidi Zhou Qun
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 20th

President Julius K. Nyerere of the Southern African Development Community and Chairman of the Revolutionary Party of Tanzania arrived in Beijing this evening to continue his visit to China.

President Nyerere is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government. Before arriving in Beijing, he spent three days in Guangdong Province for a visit and tour.

Tomorrow, President Nyerere will hold talks with Acting Premier Yao Yilin. During his stay in Beijing, President Nyerere will also meet with other Chinese leaders.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan accompanied President Nyerere from Guangzhou to Beijing.

### 应邀来我国访问　尼雷尔主席抵达北京
杨子迪　生周群
1989-11-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　应邀来我国访问
　　尼雷尔主席抵达北京
    新华社北京十一月二十日电　（记者杨子迪新闻实习生周群）南方委员会主席、坦桑尼亚革命党主席朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔今晚到达北京，继续他对中国的访问。
    尼雷尔主席是应中国政府的邀请前来访问的。在到达北京之前，他在广东省进行了三天的参观访问。
    明天，尼雷尔主席将同姚依林代总理举行会谈。在北京期间，尼雷尔主席还将会见其他中国领导人。
    外交部副部长齐怀远陪同尼雷尔主席从广州飞抵北京。
