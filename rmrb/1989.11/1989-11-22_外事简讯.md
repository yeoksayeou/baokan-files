Foreign Affairs Briefing

	▲ To celebrate the 45th anniversary of the liberation and victory of the people's revolution of Albania, the China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries organized the "Albania Today Photo Exhibition," which opened on November 21 at the China Art Museum in Beijing. The week-long exhibition features more than 60 photographs, reflecting the achievements Albania has made in various fields over the past 45 years.

	▲ At the invitation of the National Progressive Front of Iraq, the Consultative Council of the Arab Republic of Yemen, and the National Progressive Front of Syria, a delegation of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, led by the Standing Committee member Han Kehua, departed on November 21 for friendly visits to Iraq, the Arab Republic of Yemen, and Syria. The delegation consists of five members.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

　　外事简讯
    ▲为庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放和人民革命胜利45周年，由中国人民对外友好协会举办的“今日阿尔巴尼亚图片展”21日在北京中国美术馆开幕。为期1周的图片展展出了60余幅图片，反映了阿尔巴尼亚45年来在各条战线上取得的成就。
    ▲应伊拉克全国民族进步阵线、阿拉伯也门共和国咨询会议和叙利亚全国进步阵线的邀请，以政协常委韩克华为团长的全国政协代表团一行5人21日离京赴伊拉克、阿拉伯也门共和国和叙利亚进行友好访问。
          （新华社）
