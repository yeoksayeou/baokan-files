Title: New Fur Product – Goose Down Fur Introduced
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 2

Four Corners

A new star in the world of fur products – goose down fur – has recently been successfully developed by Beijing Daxun High-Tech Company. This fur, tanned using new technology, has a soft hide and fluffy down, with superior insulation and moisture resistance compared to mink fur, making it an ideal material for luxurious winter clothing. The successful development of this technology will open up broad prospects for the comprehensive utilization of goose and duck resources.

### 裘皮新产品——鹅绒皮问世
金行
1989-11-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    裘皮新产品——鹅绒皮问世
    本报讯　裘皮产品中的一颗新星——鹅绒皮，最近由北京大顺高技术公司研制成功。这种采用新技术鞣制的裘皮，皮板柔软，绒毛膨松，其防寒、防潮性能均超过貂皮，是豪华冬装的理想材料。这一技术的研制成功，将为鹅鸭资源的综合利用开辟广阔前景。
                （金行）
