Title: China-Soviet Friendship Association and China-Soviet Friendship Association Hold Film Banquet to Celebrate the 72nd Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 2

China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Soviet Friendship Association held a film banquet today to celebrate the 72nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 6th - The China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Soviet Friendship Association held a film banquet today to celebrate the 72nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Wu Xiuquan, President of the China-Soviet Friendship Association, Ai Zhensheng, Minister of the Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television, and Han Xu, President of the China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, attended the film reception along with Soviet Ambassador to China Troyanovskiy, embassy officials, and other Soviet friends in Beijing.

Han Xu said in his speech at the reception that the people of the Soviet Union, with the great tradition of the October Socialist Revolution, will certainly continue to achieve new successes in the struggle to prosper their motherland.

Troyanovskiy stated that the development of friendly relations between the Soviet Union and China on the basis of equality and mutual benefit is in the fundamental interests of the peoples of both countries.

During the film reception, the Soviet Embassy provided the feature film "The Feast of Neptune" for screening.

### 对外友协中苏友协举行电影酒会庆祝十月社会主义革命72周年

1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    对外友协中苏友协举行电影酒会庆祝十月社会主义革命72周年
    新华社北京11月6日电　中国人民对外友协和中苏友协今天在这里举行电影酒会，庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命72周年。
    中苏友协会长伍修权，广播电影电视部部长艾知生，对外友协会长韩叙等，同苏联驻中国大使特罗扬诺夫斯基，大使馆官员和在北京的其他苏联朋友一起出席了电影招待会。
    韩叙在招待会上讲话时说，有着伟大十月社会主义革命传统的苏联人民一定会在繁荣祖国的斗争中不断取得新的成绩。
    特罗扬诺夫斯基说，苏中两国在平等互利的基础上发展友好关系，符合苏中两国人民的根本利益。
    在电影招待会上放映了苏联使馆提供的故事影片《海神节》。
