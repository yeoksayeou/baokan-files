Title: Jiangxi Women with Only Daughters Participate in Pension Insurance
Author: Xie Lianhui
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 1

Jiangxi is conducting a meaningful experiment through its family planning department and insurance companies—solving the problem of old-age support for women with only daughters through insurance. This form, which is welcomed by farmers, is gradually being promoted in 47 counties across the province. By October of this year, 3,988 women with only daughters have participated in this activity, with a total insurance premium of over 1.4 million yuan.

### 江西女儿户参加养老保险
谢联辉
1989-11-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江西女儿户参加养老保险
    本报讯  记者谢联辉报道：江西省计划生育部门和保险公司正在做一项有意义的尝试——通过投保形式解决女儿户老有所养问题。这种受到农民欢迎的好形式，已经在全省四十七个县逐步推开，到今年十月有三千九百八十八女儿户参加这项活动，投保金额达一百四十多万元。
