Date: 1989-11-21

Nepal's King Meets and Hosts a Banquet for Premier Li
China and Nepal Sign Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement
Leaders of Both Countries Meet Again

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 20 - (Reporter Chen Jiabao) Nepal's King Birendra met and hosted a banquet for Chinese Premier Li Peng this evening at the royal palace. After the meeting, King Birendra and Queen Aishwarya hosted a banquet for Premier Li Peng and his wife, Zhu Lin, at the palace.

A Nepalese journalist told our reporter tonight that Li Peng's visit was a success. The grand welcoming rally in the capital this afternoon and King Birendra's meeting tonight have brought Sino-Nepalese friendship to a new high.

Our correspondent reports that Nepalese Prime Minister Shrestha and his wife held a grand banquet at the Prime Minister's residence on the evening of November 19 to welcome Chinese Premier Li Peng and his wife, Zhu Lin, who arrived in Kathmandu in the morning for an official friendly visit. Shrestha and Li Peng both spoke at the banquet, expressing their commitment to further strengthening Sino-Nepalese friendly cooperation (see page 4 for details).

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 20 - (Reporter Chen Jiabao) China and Nepal signed an economic and technical cooperation agreement here today at the state guesthouse.

According to the agreement, China will provide Nepal with some construction projects free of charge. Specific projects will be determined later.

Chinese Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin and Nepalese Finance Minister B.B. Pradhan signed the agreement.

Chinese Premier Li Peng and Nepalese Prime Minister Shrestha attended the signing ceremony.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 20 - Nepalese Prime Minister Shrestha and visiting Premier Li Peng met again at the state guesthouse this noon. Both sides had friendly talks.

Li Peng invited Shrestha to visit China at his convenience. Shrestha happily accepted the invitation.

Both sides also exchanged views on further developing bilateral relations.

### 尼泊尔国王会见宴请李总理　中尼签署经济技术合作协定　两国政府首脑昨天再次会晤

1989-11-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王会见宴请李总理
　　中尼签署经济技术合作协定
　　两国政府首脑昨天再次会晤
    新华社加德满都11月20日电　（记者陈家保）尼泊尔国王比兰德拉今天晚上在此间王宫会见了中国国务院总理李鹏。会见后，比兰德拉国王和王后在王宫里设宴招待李鹏总理和夫人朱琳。
    尼泊尔一位新闻界人士今晚对本社记者说，李鹏的访问获得了成功。下午首都市民的隆重欢迎大会和今晚比兰德拉国王的会见，使尼中友好气氛达到了高潮。
    本报讯　尼泊尔首相什雷斯塔和夫人11月19日晚在首相府举行盛大宴会，欢迎上午到达加德满都进行正式友好访问的中国总理李鹏和夫人朱琳。什雷斯塔和李鹏在宴会上先后发表讲话，一致表示将进一步加强中尼友好合作关系（详见第四版）。
    新华社加德满都11月20日电　（记者陈家保）中国和尼泊尔今天在此间国宾馆签订了两国经济技术合作协定。
    根据这项协定，中国将无偿地援助尼泊尔实施一些建设项目。具体项目有待日后商定。
    中国对外经贸部长郑拓彬和尼泊尔财政大臣巴·巴·普拉丹在协定上签字。
    中国总理李鹏和尼泊尔首相什雷斯塔出席了签字仪式。
    新华社加德满都11月20日电　尼泊尔首相什雷斯塔和正在这里访问的李鹏总理今天中午在李鹏下榻的国宾馆再次会晤。双方进行了友好的谈话。
    李鹏邀请什雷斯塔在他方便的时候到中国访问。什雷斯塔愉快地接受了邀请。
    双方还就进一步发展两国关系事宜交换了意见。（附图片）
