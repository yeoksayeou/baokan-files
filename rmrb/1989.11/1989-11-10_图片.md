Title: Retired Couple from Heilongjiang Honored for Golden Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 5

Retired cadre Xiong Zhike and his wife Pang Jiling from the Heilongjiang Provincial Mudanjiang Forestry Administration got married in Yan'an in 1937. The couple participated in the Long March and this year received the National Golden Anniversary Award.

The photo shows them touring the city of Mudanjiang with their eldest son and daughter-in-law.
