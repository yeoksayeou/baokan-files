Title: University Students and Graduates Receive Patriotic Education in Yunnan
Author: Liou De'an
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 3

Recently, 50 university students and graduates assigned to work at the State Personnel Department traveled to Yunnan. They received education in patriotism and revolutionary heroism. The image shows the university students having a warm conversation with Zhou Zhongshi (right), the political commissar of the "Old Mountain Main Attack Brigade," and combat hero Yin Guangzhong (left).
