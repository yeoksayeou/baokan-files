Title: Former Deputy Commander of Fujian Provincial Military Region Guo Tingwan Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 2

The former deputy commander of the Fujian Provincial Military Region, Guo Tingwan, passed away due to illness on October 15, 1989, in Fuzhou. He was 76 years old.

Guo Tingwan was a native of Longyan City, Fujian Province. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1929, became a member of the Communist Youth League of China in 1931, and was transferred to the Communist Party of China the following year. He held various positions, including soldier, platoon leader, company commander, battalion commander, regiment commander, deputy division commander, division commander, and military sub-district commander. In 1961, he was promoted to the rank of major general.

### 福建省军区原副司令员郭廷万逝世

1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    福建省军区原副司令员郭廷万逝世
    新华社福州11月17日电　福建省军区原副司令员郭廷万同志因病于1989年10月15日在福州逝世，终年76岁。
    郭廷万同志是福建省龙岩市人，1929年参加中国工农红军，1931年加入中国共产主义青年团，次年转为中国共产党党员。历任战士、班长、排长、连长、营长、团长、副旅长、师长、军分区司令员等职。1961年晋升为少将军衔。
