Title: Gulf Cooperation Council Foreign Ministers Meet in Muscat
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 3

The foreign ministers of the six Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries held a meeting in Muscat, the capital of Oman, on November 6 to prepare for the upcoming 10th GCC Summit scheduled for next month.

According to a report from Muscat, Oman's Foreign Minister Yousuf bin Alawi said that this preparatory meeting will discuss several political and economic issues.

The GCC member countries are Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, the United Arab Emirates, and Oman.

### 海湾六国外长聚会马斯喀特

1989-11-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    海湾六国外长聚会马斯喀特
    海湾合作委员会六国外长六日在阿曼首都马斯喀特举行会议，为下月召开的第十届海湾合作委员会首脑会议作准备。
    来自马斯喀特的消息援引阿曼外长优素福·本·阿卜杜拉的话说，这次预备会议将讨论几个政治和经济问题。
    海湾合作委员会成员国有沙特阿拉伯、科威特、卡塔尔、巴林、阿拉伯联合酋长国和阿曼。
