Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Kawai Yoshiaki
Author: Cao Ruitian
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Kawai Yoshiaki

People's Daily, Beijing, November 8 — Reporter Cao Ruitian reports: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met today with a delegation from the Japan-China Economic Association led by Kawai Yoshiaki.

The delegation from the Japan-China Economic Association is visiting China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. Tian Jiyun pointed out during the meeting that China and Japan are geographical neighbors, and the peoples of both countries have been influenced by the same cultural traditions. Both countries have their respective strengths and weaknesses in the economic and other fields. Moreover, the current bilateral economic and trade relations have developed to a considerable scale. Therefore, it is inevitable that both sides strengthen their cooperation.

### 田纪云会见河合良一
曹瑞天
1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    田纪云会见河合良一
    本报北京11月8日讯　记者曹瑞天报道：国务院副总理田纪云今天会见了以河合良一为团长的日中经济协会访华团。
    日中经济协会访华团是应中国国际贸易促进会的邀请访华的。田纪云在会见时指出，中日两国在地域上是近邻，两国人民受过共同的文化熏陶，两国在经济和其他方面各有长处和不足，而且现在双方的经贸关系已经发展到相当规模，因此加强彼此间的合作是必然的。
