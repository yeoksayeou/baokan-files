Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with Nyerere, Stating China is Fearlessly Walking Its Own Path
Author: 
Date: 1989-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 22nd.

Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, met with Julius K. Nyerere, Chairman of the South Commission and Chairman of the Tanzanian Revolutionary Party, in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon.

Yang Shangkun first welcomed Nyerere's visit to China. He said, "You are a politician familiar to the Chinese people and an old friend of the Chinese people. It is a great pleasure to meet you."

Yang Shangkun briefed the guest on China's process of opening up to the outside world over the past decade. He said, China hopes to accelerate the pace of opening up, but currently faces some temporary difficulties, namely economic sanctions imposed by certain Western countries. He pointed out that such sanctions will also harm the sanctioners and will not be sustainable. He said, China has repeatedly made it clear that its policy of opening up to the outside world will not change. Yang Shangkun said, China has not closed its doors; instead, some people are trying to close China's doors from the outside, but it can be said for certain that the doors cannot be closed.

Yang Shangkun emphasized that China is steadfastly and fearlessly walking its own path. In a word, China's situation is good, and we have the confidence to handle China's affairs well.

Yang Shangkun expressed his appreciation for the work done by the South Commission under Nyerere's leadership for the Third World. He said, we Third World countries should work together to strive for the reform of international economic relations and the establishment of a new international economic order.

Nyerere briefed Yang Shangkun on the work of the South Commission and expressed satisfaction with the development of friendly relations between Tanzania and China.

Nyerere said, China is a major country in the Third World, and China's strength is the strength of the Third World. He wished China continuous progress.

### 杨尚昆会见尼雷尔时指出　中国正无所畏惧地走自己的路　有人企图从外面把中国的门关起来是关不住的
周象光
1989-11-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆会见尼雷尔时指出
　　中国正无所畏惧地走自己的路
　　有人企图从外面把中国的门关起来是关不住的
    本报北京11月22日讯　记者周象光报道：今天下午，国家主席杨尚昆在人民大会堂会见了南方委员会主席、坦桑尼亚革命党主席朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔。
    杨尚昆主席首先对尼雷尔主席这次来华访问表示欢迎。他说：“你是中国人民熟悉的政治家，也是中国人民熟悉的老朋友，有机会结识阁下，感到非常高兴。”
    杨主席向客人介绍了我国10年来对外开放的发展过程。他说，中国希望加快更加开放的速度，但目前也遇到了一些暂时的困难，这就是西方某些国家对我们进行经济制裁。他指出，这种制裁也会给制裁者带来损害，而且这种制裁也是不可能持久的。他说，我们已多次明确地表示过，我国对外开放的政策是决不会改变的。杨主席说，中国没有关门，相反的，却是有些人企图从外面把中国的门关起来，但可以肯定地说，门是关不住的。
    杨主席强调，我们正在坚定地、无所畏惧地走自己的路。一句话，中国的局势是好的，我们有信心把中国的事情办好。
    杨尚昆对尼雷尔领导下的南方委员会为第三世界所作的富有成效的工作表示赞赏。他说，我们第三世界国家应该共同努力，为改革国际经济关系和建立国际经济新秩序而奋斗。
    尼雷尔主席向杨主席通报了南方委员会的工作情况，并对坦中友好关系的发展表示满意。
    尼雷尔主席说，中国是第三世界的大国，中国的强大，也就是第三世界的强大。他祝愿中国不断进步。（附图片）
