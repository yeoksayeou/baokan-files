Title: U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee Passes Resolution on Soviet Nagorno-Karabakh Issue; Soviet Union Protests U.S. Interference in Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 19th.

The first deputy foreign minister of the Soviet Union, Besmertnykh, summoned the U.S. Ambassador Matlock on the 19th to strongly protest the U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee's interference in the Soviet Union's internal affairs regarding the Nagorno-Karabakh Autonomous Oblast.

According to TASS, Besmertnykh pointed out that the resolution passed by the U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee on the Nagorno-Karabakh issue "arrogantly and rudely attempted to dictate to the Soviet Union how to solve this purely internal matter."

The Soviet Union believes that this action by the United States is a recurrence of the old Cold War mentality and will harm the efforts of both countries to constructively develop their relations.

### 美参院外委会竟就苏联纳—卡州问题通过决议　苏联抗议美国干涉内政

1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美参院外委会竟就苏联纳—卡州问题通过决议
　　苏联抗议美国干涉内政
    新华社莫斯科11月19日电　苏联外交部第一副部长别斯梅尔特内赫19日召见美国大使马特洛克，对美国参议院外委会在苏联纳戈尔诺—卡拉巴赫自治州问题上干涉苏联内政表示坚决抗议。
    据塔斯社报道，别斯梅尔特内赫指出，美国参议院外委会通过的关于纳—卡州问题的决议，“蛮横无礼地试图指点苏联应如何解决这一纯属内政的问题”。
    苏联认为美国这一行动是“冷战”的旧病复发，会损害两国为建设性地发展关系而作的努力。
