Title: Medical Staff at the 145th Hospital of the Jinan Military Region Strive for Excellence
Author: Chu Xiangming
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 5

Medical staff in the operating room of the 145th Hospital of the Jinan Military Region are dedicated to perfecting their medical techniques and regularly conduct medical ethics education. Over the past 31 years, they have performed over 100,000 surgeries without any medical accidents.

This is a scene of medical staff carefully checking glucose injection solution.
