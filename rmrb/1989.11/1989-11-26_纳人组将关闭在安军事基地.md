Title: SWAPO to Close Military Base in Angola
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

The United Nations spokesman, Ekhard, said on November 24 in Windhoek that the military base of the South West Africa People's Organization (SWAPO) in Angola will be closed by the end of this week.

Ekhard said that the repatriation of 300 SWAPO fighters from the military base is underway. The closure of SWAPO's military base is being coordinated with South Africa's withdrawal of its last troops from Namibia.

South Africa withdrew the last 1,500 troops from Namibia on November 23.

### 纳人组将关闭在安军事基地

1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　纳人组将关闭在安军事基地
    新华社哈拉雷11月24日电　联合国发言人艾克哈德24日在温得和克说，西南非洲人民组织（人组）在安哥拉的军事基地将于本周末关闭。
    埃克哈德说，军事基地的300名人组战士的遣返工作正在进行。关闭人组的军事基地是与南非从纳米比亚撤走最后一批驻军协调进行的。
    南非23日从纳米比亚撤走了最后1500名驻军。
