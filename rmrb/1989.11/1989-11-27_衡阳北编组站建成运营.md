Title: Hengyang North Marshalling Yard Put into Operation
Author: Hu Youheng Chang Junkun
Date: 1989-11-27
Edition: 2

Hengyang North Marshalling Yard Put into Operation

XINHUA, Changsha, Nov. 25 — China's latest large-scale freight train marshalling yard, the Hengyang North Marshalling Yard, officially began operations this morning.

The Hengyang North Marshalling Yard is a key project on the Hengyang-Guangzhou line. Construction started in December 1985, with a total investment of 300 million yuan. The yard covers an area of 1.18 million square meters and has more than 40 tracks. It can handle an average of 8,000 freight trains per month and manage the marshalling of 112 trains.

### 衡阳北编组站建成运营
胡友恒　常俊昆
1989-11-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　衡阳北编组站建成运营
    本报长沙11月25日电我国又一大型货物列车编组站——衡阳北编组站今天上午正式投入运营。
    衡阳北编组站是衡广复线的一项压尾工程。它于1985年12月动工，总投资3亿元，占地118万平方米，建有40多股道，月均吞吐货物列车8000多辆，办理列车编组112对。
    （胡友恒　常俊昆）
