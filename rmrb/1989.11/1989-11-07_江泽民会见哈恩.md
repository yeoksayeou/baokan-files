Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Hahn
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 1

CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with a delegation led by Karl Hahn, Chairman of the Board of Volkswagen AG of the Federal Republic of Germany, this evening at Zhongnanhai.

Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission He Jianxiu and others attended the meeting.

Hahn and his delegation were invited by the China Association of Automobile Manufacturers to attend the Fifth Annual Meeting of the Pacific Automotive Engineering Society, which opened in Beijing today.

### 江泽民会见哈恩

1989-11-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见哈恩
    新华社北京11月6日电  中共中央总书记江泽民今天晚上在中南海会见了联邦德国大众汽车公司董事长卡尔·哈恩一行。宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    国家计委副主任郝建秀等参加了会见。
    哈恩一行应中国汽车工业联合会的邀请，前来参加第五届太平洋地区汽车工程学会年会。会议于今天在北京开幕。
