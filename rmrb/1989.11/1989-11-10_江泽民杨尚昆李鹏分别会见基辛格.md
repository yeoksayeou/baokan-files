Title: Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng Meet with Kissinger
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

	Today morning, CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with former U.S. Secretary of State Dr. Henry Kissinger in Zhongnanhai. General Secretary Jiang Zemin and Dr. Kissinger had previously met in Shanghai, and they were both pleased to meet again in Beijing to renew their friendship.

	During the 70-minute meeting, both sides had a friendly and cordial discussion on many topics of mutual interest.

	Today evening, State Chairman Yang Shangkun met with former U.S. Secretary of State Dr. Henry Kissinger at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. The two sides reviewed their past friendly exchanges and had a candid and constructive discussion on a series of important issues. Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Huaqiu were present at the meeting.

	After the meeting, Chairman Yang Shangkun hosted a dinner for Dr. Kissinger and his wife. Dr. Kissinger's old friends Huang Hua and Huang Zhen attended the dinner.

	Today morning, Premier Li Peng met with former U.S. Secretary of State Dr. Henry Kissinger at the Great Hall of the People.

	In a friendly and candid atmosphere, both sides exchanged in-depth views on economic construction and other issues of mutual interest. This was the third time Premier Li Peng and Dr. Kissinger had met, and both were pleased about it. Premier Li Peng also provided detailed information to the guest about the Chinese government's policies and measures for rectification and deepening reforms.

	After the meeting, Premier Li Peng hosted a luncheon for Dr. Kissinger and his wife Nancy Kissinger. Zhu Lin, Luo Gan, and Liu Huaqiu attended the luncheon.

### 江泽民杨尚昆李鹏分别会见基辛格

1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    江泽民杨尚昆李鹏分别会见基辛格
    本报北京11月9日讯　记者周象光报道：今天上午，中共中央总书记江泽民在中南海会见了美国前国务卿亨利·基辛格博士。江泽民总书记和基辛格博士以前曾在上海见过面，他们对这次在北京再次相会，重叙友情，感到非常高兴。
    在持续70多分钟的会见中，宾主双方就许多共同感兴趣的问题进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    本报北京11月9日讯　记者孙毅报道：国家主席杨尚昆今天晚上在首都宾馆会见了美国前国务卿亨利·基辛格博士。宾主愉快地回顾了双方过去的友好交往，并就一系列重要问题进行了坦诚和富有建设性的会谈。钱其琛外长、刘华秋副外长会见时在座。
    会见后，杨尚昆设宴招待了基辛格和夫人。基辛格的老朋友黄华、黄镇等出席宴会作陪。
    本报北京11月9日讯　记者周象光报道：国务院总理李鹏今天上午在人民大会堂会见了美国前国务卿亨利·基辛格博士。
    在友好、坦诚的气氛中，宾主双方就经济建设问题和其它共同感兴趣的问题广泛深入地交换了意见。这次是李鹏总理和基辛格博士第三次见面，他们对此都感到非常高兴。李鹏总理还向客人详细地介绍了中国政府治理整顿、深化改革的方针、政策和措施。
    会见后，李鹏总理设午宴招待基辛格博士和夫人南希·基辛格。朱琳、罗干、刘华秋等出席作陪。
