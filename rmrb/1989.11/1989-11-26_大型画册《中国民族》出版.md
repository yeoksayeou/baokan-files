Title: The Large-Scale Photographic Album "Chinese Ethnic Groups" Published
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 3

The large-scale color photographic album "Chinese Ethnic Groups," which reflects the unique characteristics of various ethnic groups in China, was published in October this year. The State Ethnic Affairs Commission held a launch ceremony for this album at the Ethnic Culture Palace today.

### 大型画册《中国民族》出版

1989-11-26
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　大型画册《中国民族》出版
    本报北京11月25日讯　以反映中国各个民族不同特点的大型彩色摄影画册《中国民族》已于今年10月出版。国家民委今天在民族文化宫为这本画册举行了首发式。
