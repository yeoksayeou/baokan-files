Title: Peugeot Has Service Points in Beijing
Author: Liu Guochang
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 2

The Beijing Service Center of Guangzhou Peugeot Automobile Company, a Sino-French joint venture equipped with modern testing and repair technologies, was established recently at the Jingying Automobile Repair Factory of the Air Force Logistics Department.

Peugeot is one of the six types of cars approved for mass production by the state, and it has a large number of users in the Beijing area. The center's responsible person said that the center will be responsible for the "one-stop" service of Peugeot cars, including warranty, testing, repair, and spare parts supply.

### 标致汽车在京有服务点
刘国昌
1989-11-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    标致汽车在京有服务点
    本报讯　记者刘国昌报道：具有现代化检测、维修等先进技术的中法合资广州标致汽车公司北京服务中心，日前在空军后勤部京鹰汽车修理厂成立。
    标致汽车是国家定型生产的六种轿车之一，在北京地区拥有大量的用户。该中心负责人说，中心将负责标致汽车的担保、检测、维修、配件供应“一条龙”服务。
