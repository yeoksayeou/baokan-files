In terms of intelligence, what kind of middle school student is considered smart, in what direction should they develop, and what kind of talent does society need? These are questions that the entire society is concerned about. Recently, CCTV and the "Middle School Student" magazine jointly organized the "Light of Wisdom – First Middle School Brain Sports Meet," conducting a comprehensive examination of middle school students' proficiency in knowledge, breadth of knowledge, hands-on ability, independent creative ability, and teamwork ability.

　　智慧之光
