Title: Opening of the Hong Kong Cultural Centre
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

Hong Kong's largest venue for cultural activities—the Hong Kong Cultural Centre—opened yesterday. Prince Charles, who is visiting Hong Kong, formally inaugurated the centre.

The Hong Kong Cultural Centre, built at a cost of 520 million Hong Kong dollars, is located by the sea at Tsim Sha Tsui in Kowloon. The main building, the Performing Arts Building, features three performance venues: a concert hall with 2,100 seats, a grand theatre with 1,750 seats, and a theatre suitable for small-scale or experimental performances.

### 香港文化中心启用

1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    香港文化中心启用
    据新华社香港11月9日电  香港最大的文化活动场所——香港文化中心昨天开幕。正在香港访问的英国王储查尔斯为这座文化中心正式启用揭幕。
    耗资5.2亿港元兴建的香港文化中心，坐落九龙尖沙咀海傍。中心的主体工程演艺大楼拥有3个演出场地，包括设有2100个座位的音乐厅，1750个座位的大剧院，以及适合小型或实验性演出活动的剧场。
