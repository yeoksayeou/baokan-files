Title: Cai Zimin Congratulates Huang Xinjie on Election as DPP Chairman
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 1st.

The Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, Cai Zimin, sent a telegram on November 1 to congratulate Huang Xinjie on his re-election as the Chairman of the DPP Central Committee. The telegram reads as follows:

Mr. Huang Xinjie:

Congratulations on your re-election as the Chairman of the DPP Central Committee.

### 蔡子民电贺黄信介当选民进党主席

1989-11-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    蔡子民电贺黄信介当选民进党主席
    新华社北京电　台湾民主自治同盟中央委员会主席团主席蔡子民11月1日致电黄信介，祝贺他当选民进党中央主席。电文如下：
    黄信介先生：获悉先生再度当选民进党中央主席，谨致祝贺。
