Title: "Jade Maiden" Chrysanthemums Rival Hangzhou's Beauty
Author: Wang Qiehong
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 2

The third Chrysanthemum Variety Exhibition held today in Hangzhou made the West Lake fragrant with flowers and vibrant with autumn colors. Chrysanthemums from 30 cities across the country, totaling 150,000, gathered in Tian Tan Park, rivaling the beauty of Hangzhou. They showcased their splendor to 30,000 visitors. This exhibition features sections for representative works from participating cities, chrysanthemum masterpieces, chrysanthemum artistry, large standing chrysanthemums, amateur chrysanthemum displays, and large-scale floral art exhibitions.

### “菊花仙子”杭州媲美
王且鸿
1989-11-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “菊花仙子”杭州媲美
    本报杭州11月1日电　今天在杭州举行的第三届菊花品种展览，使西湖花香飘溢，秋色可可。来自全国30座城市的15万“菊花仙子”汇聚天堂媲美，向3万游人大展芳容。这届菊展设有参展城市代表作、菊花精品、菊花造型、大立菊展、群众艺菊和大型插花艺术展区。
　（王且鸿）
