Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-11-05
Edition: 2

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Mu Hua received a delegation from the Czechoslovak Peace Committee led by Chairman Bedřich Švestka at the Great Hall of the People on the afternoon of November 4.

State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with the Asia-Pacific Meteorological Survey Team at the Great Hall of the People on November 4. Zhou Jingmeng, Director of the China Meteorological Administration, also attended the meeting. The survey team consists of senior officials from the meteorological departments of Fiji, Thailand, Malaysia, Maldives, Indonesia, and one official from the World Meteorological Organization.

State Councilor and Minister of Public Security Wang Fang met and hosted a banquet for Nepal's Minister of Home Affairs Tapa and his entourage at the Great Hall of the People on November 4.

Ivan Iliev, Chairman of the Bulgaria-China Friendship Association, presented a certificate of honorary academician from the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences to Zhou Guangzhao, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, at the Bulgarian Embassy in China at noon on November 4.

The exhibition of outstanding artworks from the German Democratic Republic over the past 40 years opened at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing on the afternoon of November 4. The 120 works on display were selected from the best creations of the past 40 years.

The two-week exhibition of Bulgarian folk handicrafts opened at the China Art Museum in Beijing on the afternoon of November 3.

Farak Hakimi, Deputy Minister of Culture and Information of South Yemen, and Liu Deyou, Vice Minister of Culture of China, signed the 1989-1991 Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan between China and South Yemen on behalf of their respective governments on November 1. Before the signing ceremony, He Jingzhi, Acting Minister of Culture, met with the South Yemeni cultural delegation led by Farak.

President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Han Xu and President of the China-Bulgaria Friendship Association Xie Bangzhi met and hosted a banquet for the delegation of the Bulgaria-China Friendship Association led by Chairman Ivan Iliev on November 3. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-11-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长陈慕华4日下午在人民大会堂会见由主席贝德日赫·什维斯特卡率领的捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会代表团。
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健4日在人民大会堂会见了亚太国家气象考察团。国家气象局局长邹竞蒙参加了会见。考察团由斐济、泰国、马来西亚、马尔代夫、印度尼西亚等5个国家气象局高级官员和1名世界气象组织官员组成。
    ▲国务委员、公安部长王芳4日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了尼泊尔内政国务大臣塔帕一行。
    ▲保加利亚—中国友好协会主席伊万·伊利耶夫，4日中午在保加利亚驻华大使馆向中国科学院院长周光召颁发保加利亚科学院授予其外籍院士荣誉称号证书。
    ▲德意志民主共和国近40年来优秀艺术作品展4日下午在北京开幕。参展的120件作品是从近40年来的艺术创作中选出的优秀作品。
    ▲为期两周的保加利亚民间手工艺品展览3日下午在北京中国美术馆开幕。
    ▲南也门文化新闻部副部长法鲁克·哈基米11月1日与中国文化部副部长刘德有分别代表本国政府签署了中、也两国1989—1991年文化合作执行计划。签字仪式前，文化部代部长贺敬之会见了由法鲁克率领的南也门文化代表团。
    ▲对外友协会长韩叙、中保友协会长谢邦治3日会见并宴请了由主席伊万·伊利耶夫率领的保中友协代表团。（新华社）
