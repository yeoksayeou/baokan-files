Title: PLO Rejects Baker's Peace Plan
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Edition: 3

The Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) has rejected the revised peace plan proposed by U.S. Secretary of State James Baker for talks between Palestine and Israel.

In an interview with the Cairo-based newspaper *Al-Ahram*, PLO spokesman Yasser Abed Rabbo stated, "The U.S. plan is merely a replica of Israel's plan. It is not suitable as a basis for negotiations."

Rabbo mentioned that the PLO had conveyed a seven-point statement of objections to Washington through the Egyptian government and the U.S. ambassador in Tunis.

This statement was drafted during a recent meeting of the PLO Executive Committee in Cairo. The statement emphasizes that dialogue between Palestine and Israel should be the first step towards convening an international peace conference with the participation of all relevant parties, without any preconditions.

U.S. Secretary of State Baker proposed a five-point peace plan for Palestinian-Israeli talks in early October, but it was revised a week later at Israel's request. The revised plan ensures that prominent PLO figures will not participate in the talks.

The Israeli cabinet approved the plan on November 5.

### 巴解拒绝贝克和平计划  强调巴以对话应无先决条件

1989-11-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴解拒绝贝克和平计划
    强调巴以对话应无先决条件
    新华社开罗11月7日电  巴勒斯坦解放组织拒绝了美国国务卿贝克的关于巴以会谈和平计划修正案。
    巴解组织发言人拉布在接受开罗《华夫脱报》记者采访时说，“美国的计划只是以色列计划的翻版。作为谈判的基础是不合适的。”
    拉布说，巴解组织已通过埃及政府和美驻突尼斯大使向华盛顿转交了一份7点质疑声明。
    这项声明是巴解组织执委会最近在开罗召开的会议上拟定的。声明强调，巴以对话是召开有关各方参加的、没有先决条件的国际和平会议的第一步。
    美国务卿贝克在10月初提出了一项巴以会谈的5点和平计划，但在一周前应以色列的要求进行了修改。修订后的计划保证巴解组织著名人物不参加会谈。
    以色列内阁5日同意了该计划。
