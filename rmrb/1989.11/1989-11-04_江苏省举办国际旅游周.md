Title: Jiangsu Province Hosts International Tourism Week
Author: Hua Huiyi
Date: 1989-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 1st.

Jiangsu Province's International Tourism Week kicked off today in Nanjing, aiming to showcase the new face of the province's tourism industry.

Guests from Japan, the United States, France, the Soviet Union, West Germany, Italy, Canada, Australia, Malaysia, the Philippines, and Hong Kong and Taiwan, totaling 160 tourism industry professionals, attended the event.

### 江苏省举办国际旅游周
华惠毅
1989-11-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江苏省举办国际旅游周
    据新华社南京十一月一日电　（记者华惠毅）旨在显示江苏旅游业新貌的江苏国际旅游周今天在南京揭开帷幕。
    来自日本、美国、法国、苏联、联邦德国、意大利、加拿大、澳大利亚、马来西亚、菲律宾和香港、台湾等十四个国家、地区一百六十名旅游业经营者参加了这一活动。
