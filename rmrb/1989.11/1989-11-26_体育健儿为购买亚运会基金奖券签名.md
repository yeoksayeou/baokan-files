Title: Athletes Sign for the Purchase of Asian Games Fund Lottery Tickets
Author: Lǐ Běi Dà
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 3

Athletes sign for the purchase of Asian Games fund lottery tickets

Beijing, Nov. 25 (Xinhua) -- Star athletes and film stars gathered at the front of the Xinhua Bookstore in Wangfujing to sign for the purchase of Asian Games fund lottery tickets.

When athletes Xu Yanmei, Chen Longcai, Cai Yanfu, and Chen Hongyong appeared at the lottery ticket sales point, passersby in Wangfujing immediately flocked over, eager to buy lottery tickets.

A worker bought more than 30 yuan worth of lottery tickets at once, saying, "The Asian Games are being held in our Beijing, so we should contribute our efforts."

### 体育健儿为购买亚运会基金奖券签名
李北大
1989-11-26
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　体育健儿为购买亚运会基金奖券签名
    本报北京11月25日讯　记者李北大报道：体育名星和影星坐镇王府井新华书店前，为购买亚运会基金奖券签名留念。
    当许艳梅、陈龙灿、蔡炎书、陈红勇四位健将等出现在奖券销售点时，王府井过往行人一下子涌了过来，争相购买奖券。
    一位工人一下买了30几元奖券，他说：“亚运会在咱北京开，咱不出力谁出力。”
