Title: Central Military Commission Identifies 33 Military Leaders
Author: Xú Jīngyuè
Date: 1989-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 29th.

In the epic of the Chinese People's Revolutionary War, numerous military leaders emerged who were masters of strategy and commanded respect. The Central Military Commission has identified 33 individuals to be honored with the title of "military leader," and their names are included in the recently published volume "Chinese Encyclopedia: Military Volume: Chinese People's Liberation Army Military Figures."

They are: Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Dehuai, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Yang Shangkun, Li Xiannian, Su Yu, Xu Haidong, Huang Kecheng, Chen Gong, Tan Zheng, Xiao Jingguang, Zhang Yunyi, Luo Ruiqing, Wang Shusheng, and Xu Guangda. Eight military leaders sacrificed their lives for the cause of the Chinese People's Liberation, including the founder of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ye Ting, and senior commanders of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army, Xu Jixian, Cai Shenshi, Duan Dechang, and Zeng Zhongsheng. Additionally, there are senior commanders of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army and the Eighth Route Army, Zuo Quan, and senior commanders of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army and the New Fourth Army, Peng Xuefeng and Luo Binghui. There is also Lin Biao.

### 中央军委确定33位军事家  毛泽东周恩来朱德邓小平名列其中
徐京跃
1989-11-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中央军委确定33位军事家
    毛泽东周恩来朱德邓小平名列其中
    新华社北京11月29日电　（记者徐京跃）在史诗般的中国人民革命战争中，涌现出多少运筹帷幄叱咤风云的军事家？经中央军委确定，33人被冠以“军事家”的评语，载入最近出版的《中国大百科全书·军事卷·中国人民解放军军事人物分册》。
    他们是：毛泽东、周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭德怀、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、杨尚昆、李先念、粟裕、徐海东、黄克诚、陈赓、谭政、肖劲光、张云逸、罗瑞卿、王树声、许光达。有8位军事家为中国人民解放事业献身，他们是中国人民解放军创建人叶挺，中国工农红军高级指挥员许继慎、蔡申熙、段德昌、曾中生，中国工农红军和八路军高级指挥员左权，中国工农红军和新四军高级将领彭雪枫、罗炳辉。还有林彪。
