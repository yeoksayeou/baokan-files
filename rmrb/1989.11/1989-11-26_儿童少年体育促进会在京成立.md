Title: The Children and Youth Sports Promotion Association Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 3

The Children and Youth Sports Promotion Association, initiated by a group of senior figures from the sports and education sectors, was established in Beijing today. They will assist the education, sports, and health departments in promoting the development of children and youth sports. Following the spirit of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's directive to "start from the children," they will widely carry out sports activities among hundreds of millions of children and youth, aiming to cultivate more outstanding young athletes.

### 儿童少年体育促进会在京成立

1989-11-26
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　儿童少年体育促进会在京成立
    本报北京11月25日讯　由一批体育界、教育界的老同志发起的宋庆龄基金会儿童少年体育促进会今天在京成立。他们将协助教育、体育、卫生部门，促进儿童少年体育事业的发展，遵循邓小平同志“从娃娃抓起”的指示精神，在亿万儿童少年中广泛开展体育活动的基础上，培养出更多优秀的体育新苗。
