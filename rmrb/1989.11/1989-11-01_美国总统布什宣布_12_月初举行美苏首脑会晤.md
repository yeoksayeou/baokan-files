Title: Bush Announces U.S.-Soviet Summit in Early December
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-11-01
Page: 3

Last-minute news from our correspondent:

U.S. President Bush announced today that he and Soviet leader Gorbachev will hold their first summit as U.S. president aboard U.S. and Soviet naval vessels in the Mediterranean from December 2 to 3. They will discuss the current international situation and U.S.-Soviet relations.

It is reported that Gorbachev will visit Rome on December 1, so the two leaders have arranged this meeting at this time. Earlier, the two countries had decided to hold a formal U.S.-Soviet summit in the United States in the late spring or early summer of 1990. Bush said at the press conference that the December summit will be an informal meeting aimed at better acquainted the two leaders and deepening their understanding of each other's views. He does not expect the December summit to produce any substantive decisions or agreements.

### 美国总统布什宣布  12月初举行美苏首脑会晤
张启昕
1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：本报最后消息

    美国总统布什宣布
    12月初举行美苏首脑会晤
    本报华盛顿10月31日电　记者张启昕报道：美国总统布什今天上午在白宫举行的记者招待会上宣布，苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫主席和他将于12月2日至3日在地中海美国和苏联的海军舰只上举行他就任美国总统后的第一次两国首脑会晤，讨论当前国际形势和美苏关系。
    据称，12月1日戈尔巴乔夫将去罗马访问，因而择此时机，两国领导人安排了这次会晤。由于两国早先已决定将于1990年春末夏初在美国举行一次正式的美苏首脑会晤。布什在记者招待会上说，12月的会晤是一次临时的非正式的首脑会晤，目的在于使两国领导人彼此更好熟悉，加深了解彼此的看法。他预计12月的首脑会晤不会产生实质性决定或协议。
