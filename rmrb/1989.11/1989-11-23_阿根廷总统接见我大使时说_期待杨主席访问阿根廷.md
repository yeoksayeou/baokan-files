Title: Argentine President Meets Chinese Ambassador, Expresses Hope for Chairman Yang's Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Page: 4

People's Daily, Buenos Aires, November 20th

Argentine President Carlos Menem today met with the Chinese Ambassador to Argentina, Li Guoxin. Menem stated that there are no reservations between Argentina and China, and the prospects for developing bilateral relations are broad.

Menem conveyed his greetings to Chairman Yang Shangkun and expressed his anticipation for Yang's visit to Argentina next year. Menem hopes that China will dispatch a delegation to Argentina soon to discuss specific matters related to the development of economic and trade relations and cultural exchanges. He also expressed his gratitude for the warm reception given to the delegation of the Argentine Justicialist Party during their visit to China.

### 阿根廷总统接见我大使时说　期待杨主席访问阿根廷

1989-11-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿根廷总统接见我大使时说
　　期待杨主席访问阿根廷
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯11月20日电　阿根廷总统梅内姆今天接见中国驻阿大使李国新时说，阿中两国之间没有任何保留，发展两国关系前景广阔。
    梅内姆总统请李大使向杨尚昆主席转达问候，他期待着杨主席明年访问阿根廷。梅内姆希望中国方面尽快派代表团来阿具体讨论发展两国经贸关系和文化交流问题。他对阿根廷正义党代表团在中国受到的热情接待表示感谢。
