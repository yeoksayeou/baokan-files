Title: Agricultural Crop Variety Bank Established in Xinjiang
Author: Liu Guangniu
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 2

Agricultural Crop Variety Bank Established in Xinjiang

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, November 20 (Reporter Liu Guangniu) The Xinjiang Academy of Agricultural Sciences has completed a crop variety resource bank capable of storing 30,000 seed samples, which has recently been put into use.

Xinjiang has unique natural climatic conditions ideal for preserving original crop seeds. The summer is hot and dry, while the winter is cold and humid. The establishment of this variety resource bank provides an ideal base for long-term preservation of original crop seeds, especially for inland and southern provinces.

### 农作物品种库在新疆建成
刘光牛
1989-11-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　农作物品种库在新疆建成
    新华社乌鲁木齐11月20日电　（记者刘光牛）新疆农科院建成一座可储存3万份种子的农作物品种资源库，不久前投入使用。
    新疆有得天独厚的保存农作物原种的自然气候条件，夏季温度高而湿度低，冬季则相反。这一品种资源库的建成，为我国内地尤其南方省区长期保存农作物原种提供了理想的基地。
