Title: The Television Series "Chinese Police" Begins Filming
Author:
Date: 1989-11-12
Page: 3

The television series "Chinese Police" began filming today in Beijing.

Criminal police in China are the guardians of the people's lives and property. This 60-episode series, produced by the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau, the Beijing Film and Television Artists Association, and the Beijing Municipal Civil Affairs Daily Chemical Industry Company, will use various cases as its main plotlines to enthusiastically praise this dedicated, people-loving, and self-sacrificing force.

### 电视剧《中国刑警》开拍

1989-11-12
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    电视剧《中国刑警》开拍
    本报北京11月11日讯　电视系列剧《中国刑警》，今天在这里开拍。
    我国的刑事警察，是人民生命财产的捍卫者。这部由北京市公安局、北京电影电视艺术家协会和北京市民政日用化工实业公司摄制的长达60集的电视剧，将以形形色色的案件为主要情节，热情歌颂这支忠于职守、热爱人民、不怕牺牲的队伍。
