Title: Over a Hundred Boxers Compete for the World Cup
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 21st.

Over a hundred boxers from 13 countries will compete in the World Cup amateur boxing tournament scheduled to begin on November 22nd in Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark.

Among the participants are top athletes from countries known for their high level of amateur boxing, including West Germany, Bulgaria, Cuba, and the Soviet Union. Many of these athletes have won Olympic medals and have excelled in international competitions.

### 百余拳手争夺世界杯

1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　百余拳手争夺世界杯
    据新华社北京11月21日电　据外电报道，来自13个国家的100多名拳手将参加于22日在丹麦首都哥本哈根举行的世界杯业余拳击赛。
    参赛选手当中，有来自当今世界业余拳击水平较高的西德、保加利亚、古巴和苏联的一些优秀运动员，其中包括许多奥运会奖牌获得者和国际比赛中的优胜者。
