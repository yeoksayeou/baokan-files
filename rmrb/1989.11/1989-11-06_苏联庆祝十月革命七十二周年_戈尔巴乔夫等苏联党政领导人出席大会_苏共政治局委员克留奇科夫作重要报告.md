Title: Soviet Union Celebrates the 72nd Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 3

The Soviet Union celebrated the 72nd anniversary of the October Revolution. Soviet party and government leaders, including Gorbachev, Ryzhkov, and Shevardnadze, attended the ceremony. Klyuchkov, a member of the Politburo of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Soviet State Security Committee, delivered an important speech at the meeting.

Klyuchkov emphasized in his speech that the October Revolution first revealed the prospect of socialist development for all mankind. It was the great ideals of October that inspired people to engage in the most noble heroic cause in history. Klyuchkov criticized those who took a nihilistic attitude towards history. He said that distorting history for any reason is unscientific and immoral.

When discussing the Soviet economic issues, Klyuchkov said that the crisis in the national economy has not been overcome. To fundamentally improve the economic situation, tremendous efforts are needed, and it will also take some time.

He refuted the argument spread by the West and within the country that the Soviet Union has deviated from socialism and is even heading towards the restoration of capitalism.

He stressed that the Soviet Union is not deviating from socialism but is breaking free from a narrow and dogmatic understanding of socialism.

Klyuchkov said that reform should first promote the improvement of labor productivity and the increase of production efficiency. Only through hard work can living standards be improved. People need a stable environment, and the phenomena of disrupting public order and damaging the national economy cannot be tolerated any longer.

### 苏联庆祝十月革命七十二周年  戈尔巴乔夫等苏联党政领导人出席大会  苏共政治局委员克留奇科夫作重要报告

1989-11-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联庆祝十月革命七十二周年
    戈尔巴乔夫等苏联党政领导人出席大会
    苏共政治局委员克留奇科夫作重要报告
    新华社莫斯科11月4日电　十月革命72周年庆祝大会4日下午在克里姆林宫举行。戈尔巴乔夫、雷日科夫、谢瓦尔德纳泽等苏联党政领导人出席了大会，苏共中央政治局委员、苏联国家安全委员会主席克留奇科夫在大会上作了题为《伟大的十月革命和苏联社会革新》的报告。
    克留奇科夫在报告中强调指出，十月革命首次为全人类揭示了社会主义发展的前景，正是伟大十月的理想鼓舞着人们去从事历史上最美好的英雄事业。克留奇科夫对某些人以虚无主义态度对待历史提出了批评。他说，无论以什么借口来歪曲历史都是反科学和不道德的。
    在谈到苏联经济问题时，克留奇科夫说，国民经济的危机现象尚未克服，要根本改善经济状况，必须付出巨大努力，也还需要一定时间。
    他驳斥了西方和国内散布的所谓苏联脱离了社会主义，甚至走向资本主义复辟道路的论调。
    他强调指出，苏联不是脱离了社会主义，而是摆脱了对社会主义狭隘的和教条主义的理解。
    克留奇科夫说，改革首先应促进劳动生产率的提高和生产效益的增长，只有好好工作才能改善生活。人民需要安定的环境，不能再容忍破坏公共秩序和破坏国民经济的现象继续存在。
