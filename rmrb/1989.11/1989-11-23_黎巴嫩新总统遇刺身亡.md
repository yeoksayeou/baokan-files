Title: Lebanese New President Assassinated
Author: Lǚ Zhìxīng Lín Jiǎomíng
Date: 1989-11-23
Edition: 4

Lebanese New President Assassinated

	Our reporters Lü Zhixing and Lin Jiaoming report from Damascus and Cairo: According to news from Beirut, Lebanese Prime Minister Hussein announced this afternoon that the newly elected Lebanese President Muawad was assassinated at 1:45 PM local time today.

	President Muawad and his entourage were participating in the celebration of Lebanon's Independence Day in the western part of Beirut this afternoon. After the event, as they were returning to their residence by car, their convoy was ambushed by an unidentified vehicle. A heavy bomb planted in that vehicle exploded, destroying four cars on the spot and killing Muawad instantly. Prime Minister Hussein and Speaker Hussein survived the attack.

	In this explosion, 13 people were killed on the spot and 36 were injured.

### 黎巴嫩新总统遇刺身亡
吕志星　林皎明
1989-11-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　黎巴嫩新总统遇刺身亡
    本报大马士革、开罗11月22日电　记者吕志星、林皎明报道：据贝鲁特消息，黎巴嫩总理胡斯今天下午宣布，新当选的黎巴嫩总统穆瓦德在当地时间今天下午1点45分遇刺身亡。
    穆瓦德总统一行今天下午在贝鲁特西区参加黎巴嫩独立日庆祝活动后，乘车返回寓所，途中总统车队遭到不明身份的一辆汽车伏击。装在那辆汽车上的重磅炸弹引爆后，4辆汽车当场被炸毁，穆瓦德总统当即遇难。同行的胡斯总理和侯赛尼议长幸免于难。
    这次爆炸事件中，当场死亡13人，36人受伤。
