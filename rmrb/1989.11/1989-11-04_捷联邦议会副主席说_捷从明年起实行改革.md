Title: Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak Federal Assembly Says Czechoslovakia to Implement Reforms Starting Next Year
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 3

    Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak Federal Assembly and Chairman of the Social Party, Bohuslav Kucera, stated at a press conference held today in Bonn that Czechoslovakia will begin implementing reforms starting next year.

    Kucera said that in the first half of next year, Czechoslovakia will carry out economic reforms aimed at implementing enterprise responsibility. In response to a reporter's question, he also expressed the intention to simultaneously carry out political reforms.

    This press conference was held at the conclusion of a three-day first-ever discussion between parliamentarians from NATO and Warsaw Pact countries.

### 捷联邦议会副主席说  捷从明年起实行改革

1989-11-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    捷联邦议会副主席说
    捷从明年起实行改革
    新华社波恩11月2日电  捷克斯洛伐克联邦议会副主席、社会党主席博胡斯拉夫·库切拉今天在这里举行的记者招待会上表示，捷克斯洛伐克将从明年起实行改革。
    库切拉说，明年上半年，捷克斯洛伐克将实行贯彻企业责任制的经济改革。在回答记者提问时，他还表示要同时进行政治改革。
    这一记者招待会是在为期3天的首次北约和华约国家议员讨论会结束时举行的。
