Title: The CIA Intensifies Activities in Nicaragua
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Managua, November 9th.

The CIA is currently intensifying its activities in Nicaragua to prevent the ruling Sandinista National Liberation Front from winning the February 1990 elections.

Philip Roethinger, a former U.S. Navy officer and CIA agent, revealed this on the 6th. Roethinger, who is currently the chairman of the Association of Former Intelligence Officers, made these remarks to the press in Tegucigalpa, the capital of Honduras.

### 美情报局加紧在尼活动

1989-11-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美情报局加紧在尼活动
    美国前海军军官兼中央情报局特工人员菲利普·罗廷格六日透露，中央情报局目前正在尼加拉瓜加紧活动，以防止执政的桑地诺民族解放阵线在一九九零年二月大选中获胜。
    现任美国前情报机构官员协会主席的罗廷格是在洪都拉斯首都特古西加尔巴向报界发表这一谈话的。
