Title: Over a Hundred Foreign Clients Move into the China International Trade Center
Author: Liú Xiāo
Date: 1989-11-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th.

Over a hundred foreign clients move into the China International Trade Center

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th. Correspondent Liu Xiao reports: The China International Trade Center office building, "Guomao Building," located at the Dabeiyao Interchange in Beijing, was officially put into use the day before yesterday. 60% of the office space has been rented by over 100 foreign enterprise Beijing offices. The clients moving into the building come from 20 countries and regions, including Japan, the United States, Canada, France, and Italy.

### 上百家外国客户迁入国贸大厦
刘霄
1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    上百家外国客户迁入国贸大厦
    本报北京11月17日讯　记者刘霄报道：位于北京大北窑立交桥畔的中国国际贸易中心写字楼“国贸大厦”前天正式启用，60％的办公面积已由100多家外国企业驻京机构承租。迁入大厦的客户来自日本、美国、加拿大、法国、意大利等20个国家和地区。
