Title: The Publication of "A Record of Chinese Emperors"
Author: Wei Ling
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 6

The book "A Record of Chinese Emperors" compiled by Zhou Yuanchu has been published by Hai Chao Publishing House. Based on extensive historical records, this book introduces 194 emperors from Qin Shi Huang to the last emperor of the Qing Dynasty, Puyi. It discusses the policies formulated by the imperial court under the direct control of feudal emperors and their social impacts, showcasing the changes in Chinese society during different historical periods. Each emperor's introduction in the book forms a narrative and evaluative structure, reflecting a comprehensive understanding of the merits and demerits of each emperor's life, providing readers with a relatively complete portrait of each individual. The book also touches on the relationships between the emperors and their officials, eunuchs, concubines, and daily life, making it rich in content and highly readable.

### 《中国皇帝要录》出版
魏玲
1989-11-03
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

    《中国皇帝要录》出版
    邹元初编著的《中国皇帝要录》已由海潮出版社出版。本书依据大量的史料，介绍了自秦始皇到清宣统一百九十四个皇帝，并结合他们生平事迹阐述了在封建皇帝直接控制下，朝廷所制订的各项政策及其社会效果，展现了中国社会各个不同时期的时势变化。书中对每个皇帝的介绍都能形成一个叙述与评价的结构，体现出对每一个皇帝一生功过是非的认识，给读者提供一个较为完整的人物形象。书中还对皇帝与周围的官、宦、妃、嫔的关系以及生活起居等有所介绍，内容充实，有可读性。
　（魏玲）
