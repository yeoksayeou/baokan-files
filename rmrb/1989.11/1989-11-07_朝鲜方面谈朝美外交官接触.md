Title: North Korea on Diplomatic Contacts Between North Korean and American Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th

    North Korea on Diplomatic Contacts Between North Korean and American Officials

    A spokesperson for the North Korean Foreign Ministry responded to questions from a reporter from the Korean Central News Agency on November 3rd regarding the fifth round of talks between the two countries' embassies in Beijing on November 1st.

    The spokesperson said that during this contact, North Korea drew the attention of the U.S. side to the fact that while North and South Korea were promoting various dialogues, the United States and South Korea had repeatedly conducted military exercises, escalating tensions on the Korean Peninsula. He said that both sides also discussed methods to ease tensions on the Korean Peninsula and prevent the danger of nuclear war.

### 朝鲜方面谈朝美外交官接触

1989-11-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    朝鲜方面谈朝美外交官接触
    朝鲜外交部发言人三日就朝鲜和美国两国驻北京大使馆十一月一日在北京进行第五次参赞级接触问题，回答了朝鲜中央通讯社记者的提问。
    这位发言人说，朝鲜在这次接触中提请美方注意：在朝鲜北南双方促进各种对话的时候，美国和南朝鲜多次举行军事演习，激化了朝鲜半岛的紧张局势。他说，双方还就缓和朝鲜半岛的紧张局势、防止核战争危险的方法问题进行了讨论。
