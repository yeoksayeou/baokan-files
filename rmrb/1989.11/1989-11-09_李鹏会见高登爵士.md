Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with Sir Gordon
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 8th.

Premier Li Peng met with Sir Gordon, Vice Chairman of the Hong Kong China Light and Power Company, and his delegation this evening at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

The host and guest had a friendly conversation.

Sir Gordon came to visit at the invitation of the China National Nuclear Corporation. The Hong Kong China Light and Power Company has a long-term cooperative relationship with mainland power enterprises. Li Peng expressed the hope during the meeting that this cooperative relationship would be further developed.

Minister of Energy Huang Yicheng and General Manager of the China National Nuclear Corporation Jiang Xionghong attended the meeting.

### 李鹏会见高登爵士

1989-11-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见高登爵士
    新华社北京11月8日电　国务院总理李鹏今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了香港中华电力公司副董事长高登爵士一行。
    宾主进行了友好的谈话。
    高登爵士是应中国核工业总公司的邀请前来访问的。香港中华电力公司同内地的电力企业有长期的合作关系。李鹏在会见中表示，希望这种合作关系进一步得到发展。
    能源部部长黄毅诚和中国核工业总公司总经理蒋心雄参加了会见。
