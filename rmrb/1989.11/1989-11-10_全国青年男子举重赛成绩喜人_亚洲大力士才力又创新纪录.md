Title: National Youth Men's Weightlifting Competition Produces Excellent Results
Author: Sū Dūn Lián, Hú Yǒng Kuān
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

On November 8th, during the 110kg and above category competition of the National Youth Men's Weightlifting Championships held in Kuaiji County, Zhejiang, athlete Cai Li from Liaoning broke his own national and Asian records with a 222.5kg clean and jerk.

Known as the "Asian First Strongman," the 19-year-old Cai Li rewrote the national youth, adult, and Asian records in this event with a 221.5kg clean and jerk at the first National City Games last year. In today's afternoon clean and jerk competition, he easily lifted 205kg in his second attempt. He then attempted 222.5kg, challenging his own record. After lifting the barbell over his head, his feet moved slightly, but he still stood firmly. After the referee signaled success, the packed stands erupted in thunderous applause.

### 全国青年男子举重赛成绩喜人  亚洲大力士才力又创新纪录
苏敦联  胡咏宽
1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国青年男子举重赛成绩喜人
    亚洲大力士才力又创新纪录
    本报讯　11月8日，在浙江开化县举行的全国青年男子举重锦标赛110公斤以上级比赛中，辽宁运动员才力以挺举222.5公斤的成绩刷新了由他自己保持的221.5公斤的全国和亚洲纪录。
    被称为亚洲第一大力士的才力今年才19岁，他在去年全国首届城市运动会上以221.5公斤成绩改写了这项挺举全国青年、成年和亚洲三项纪录。在今天下午的挺举比赛中，他第二次试举轻松地举起了205公斤。接着他要了222.5公斤，向自己保持的纪录冲击。杠铃被举过头顶后，他的双脚虽移动了一下，但还是稳稳地站住了。裁判员示意成功后，座无虚席的观众席上爆发出雷鸣般的掌声。
    （苏敦联、胡咏宽）
