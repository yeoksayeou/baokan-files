Title: The Soviet Union to Hold the People's Congress Next Month
Author:
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 13th.

The second session of the Soviet Congress of People's Deputies will be held on December 12. The congress will discuss the revision of the constitution and economic issues.

The Supreme Soviet of the USSR, which adjourned before the October Revolution holiday, reconvened on the 13th. The meeting believed that the second session of the Congress of People's Deputies will focus on studying measures to normalize the Soviet economy, the steps of economic reform, and the principles for drafting the 13th Five-Year Plan (1991-1995).

The congress will also discuss the draft law on constitutional supervision and elect members of the newly established Constitutional Supervision Commission of the USSR.

The first session of the Soviet Congress of People's Deputies was held in Moscow from May 25 to June 9 this year.

### 苏联下月召开人代会

1989-11-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联下月召开人代会
    新华社莫斯科11月13日电  据塔斯社报道，第二届苏联人民代表大会将于12月12日召开。大会将讨论修改宪法和经济问题。
    于十月革命节前休会的苏联最高苏维埃会议13日复会。会议认为，第二届人民代表大会将重点研究有关苏联经济正常化的措施，经济改革的步骤和第13个5年计划（1991—1995）的制订原则。
    大会还将讨论苏联宪法监督法草案，选举苏联新成立的宪法监督委员会成员。
    第一届苏联人民代表大会是今年5月25日至6月9日在莫斯科举行的。
