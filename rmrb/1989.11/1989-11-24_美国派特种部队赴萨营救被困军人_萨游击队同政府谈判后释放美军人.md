Title: U.S. Special Forces Sent to El Salvador to Rescue Trapped Soldiers; Salvadoran Guerrillas Release U.S. Personnel After Negotiations with Government
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 22nd November

The U.S. dispatched special forces to El Salvador to rescue American military personnel trapped in the Hilton Hotel in San Salvador by Salvadoran guerrillas. President Bush announced this morning en route to Memphis that he had ordered the deployment of U.S. special forces to San Salvador. Pentagon officials stated that this special forces unit was prepared to launch an assault on the hotel if necessary.

However, White House spokesman Fitzwater later stated that the U.S. special forces did not take action to enter the hotel. Through mediation, the guerrillas conducted direct negotiations with the Salvadoran government, releasing the American military personnel and withdrawing from the hotel on the morning of the 22nd.

### 美国派特种部队赴萨营救被困军人　萨游击队同政府谈判后释放美军人
张启昕
1989-11-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国派特种部队赴萨营救被困军人
　　萨游击队同政府谈判后释放美军人
    本报华盛顿11月22日电　记者张启昕报道：布什总统今天上午在飞往孟菲斯的途中向记者宣布，为了解救被萨尔瓦多游击队困留在圣萨尔瓦多市喜来登饭店的美国军事人员，他下令向圣萨尔瓦多派遣了美国特种部队。五角大楼官员称，这支派往萨尔瓦多的特种部队是准备在必要时向饭店发动进攻的。
    但据白宫发言人菲茨沃特事后称，美国特种部队未采取行动进入饭店。因为经过调解，游击队同萨尔瓦多政府进行了直接谈判，于22日上午释放了美国军事人员，并撤离了饭店。
