Title: Foundation Laying Ceremony for the Yan Yang Jianying Bridge
Author:
Date: 1989-11-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 18th.

The "Yan Yang Jianying Bridge," named by State Chairman Yang Shangkun, held its grand groundbreaking ceremony on the morning of the 18th in Yan Yang Town, Mei County.

This bridge is constructed at the Wushi Ferry in Yan Yang Town, the hometown of Marshal Ye. The bridge is 311 meters long and 9 meters wide, and is scheduled to be completed by the end of 1991.

### 雁洋剑英大桥奠基

1989-11-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    雁洋剑英大桥奠基
    新华社广州十一月十八日电  由国家主席杨尚昆亲自题名的“雁洋剑英大桥”，于十八日上午在梅县雁洋镇隆重举行动工奠基仪式。
    这座大桥架设在叶帅家乡雁洋镇文社乌石渡口，桥长三百一十一米，宽九米，一九九一年底竣工。
