Title: Premier Li Peng Visits Chinese Personnel in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1989-11-17
Edition: 4

Premier Li Peng, who is currently visiting Pakistan, went to the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan this morning to visit all the embassy staff, as well as Chinese experts, students, and representatives from other Chinese institutions in Pakistan.

Premier Li Peng explained the domestic and international situation to the Chinese personnel in Pakistan.

Premier Li said, "Pakistan is a friendly neighbor of China. Sino-Pakistani friendship is continuously deepening."

He encouraged all Chinese personnel in Pakistan to unite and continue to work hard to develop this friendship.

### 李鹏总理看望我国驻巴人员

1989-11-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    李鹏总理看望我国驻巴人员
    新华社伊斯兰堡11月16日电　正在这里访问的中国总理李鹏今天上午来到中国驻巴基斯坦使馆，看望了使馆的全体人员以及中国专家、留学生和中国其他驻巴机构的代表。
    李鹏总理向中国驻巴人员阐述了中国国内形势和国际形势。
    李总理说，巴基斯坦是中国的友好邻邦。中巴友谊不断深入发展。
    他勉励全体中国驻巴人员团结一致，继续为发展这种友谊而努力。
