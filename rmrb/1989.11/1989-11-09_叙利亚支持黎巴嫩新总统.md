Title: Syria Supports Lebanon's New President
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, November 7th.

Syrian Prime Minister Zuebi expressed satisfaction with Rene Moawad's election as Lebanon's new president and the adoption of the Lebanese National Reconciliation Charter.

According to the Syrian News Agency, Prime Minister Zuebi reviewed the current developments in Lebanon during a cabinet meeting today, emphasizing the continued importance of Syria's support for Lebanon.

Syria was the first country to support Moawad's election as Lebanon's new president. An informed source said that Syrian Vice President Khaddam also traveled to northern Lebanon today to congratulate Moawad personally.

Additionally, according to today's issue of Syria's *Al-Thawra* newspaper, Lebanon's new president Moawad, in an interview with the newspaper's reporter, emphasized the importance of consolidating and developing the good relations between Lebanon and Syria, which are based on historical and geographical foundations.

### 叙利亚支持黎巴嫩新总统

1989-11-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    叙利亚支持黎巴嫩新总统
    新华社大马士革11月7日电　叙利亚总理祖埃比对勒内·穆瓦德5日当选为黎巴嫩新总统以及通过黎巴嫩民族和解宪章感到满意。
    据叙利亚通讯社报道，祖埃比总理在今天召开的内阁会议上回顾了黎巴嫩当前局势的发展，强调叙利亚继续支持黎巴嫩的重要性。
    叙利亚是第一个支持穆瓦德当选为黎巴嫩新总统的国家。一位消息灵通人士说，叙利亚副总统哈达姆今天还专程前往黎巴嫩北部，向穆瓦德表示祝贺。
    另据叙利亚《革命报》今天报道，黎巴嫩新总统穆瓦德在接受该报记者采访时，强调将巩固和发展黎叙之间建立在历史和地理基础上的良好关系。
