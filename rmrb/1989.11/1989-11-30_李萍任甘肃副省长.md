Title: Li Ping Appointed as Vice Governor of Gansu
Author:
Date: 1989-11-30
Edition: 2

The 11th meeting of the Standing Committee of the 7th Gansu Provincial People's Congress, which concluded yesterday, decided: Li Ping will serve as Vice Governor of Gansu Province. The 57-year-old Li Ping previously held the position of Director of the Gansu Provincial Planning Commission.

### 李萍任甘肃副省长

1989-11-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　李萍任甘肃副省长
    新华社兰州11月29日电　昨天结束的甘肃省七届人民代表大会常务委员会第十一次会议决定：李萍为甘肃省副省长。57岁的李萍曾任甘肃省计划委员会主任。
