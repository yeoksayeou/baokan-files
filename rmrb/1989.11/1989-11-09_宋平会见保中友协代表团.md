Title: Song Ping Meets with Delegation from the Bulgaria-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 8th.

Song Ping, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China, met and dined with a delegation from the Bulgaria-China Friendship Association led by Ivan Iliev, Chairman of the Association and former Deputy Prime Minister and Chairman of the State Planning Committee of Bulgaria, this evening at the Great Hall of the People.

During the friendly and cordial conversation, Song Ping briefed the guests on China's current economic situation. Iliev highly praised the achievements made by China's reform and opening-up policy.

### 宋平会见保中友协代表团

1989-11-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    宋平会见保中友协代表团
    新华社北京11月8日电　中共中央政治局常委宋平今天晚上在人民大会堂会见并宴请由保中友协主席、保加利亚前部长会议副主席兼国家计委主任伊凡·伊利耶夫率领的保中友协代表团。
    在亲切友好的交谈中，宋平向客人们介绍了中国当前的经济形势。伊利耶夫高度评价了中国改革开放所取得的成就。
