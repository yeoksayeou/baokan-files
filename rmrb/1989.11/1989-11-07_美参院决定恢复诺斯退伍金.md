Title: U.S. Senate Decides to Restore North's Retirement Pay
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 3

The U.S. Senate on Tuesday night passed a bill to restore the annual $23,000 retirement pay of former White House aide Oliver North.

North, 46, is a retired Navy lieutenant. He was convicted of three felonies in the Iran-Contra affair, and the Navy had suspended his retirement pay as a result.

The bill has been sent to the House of Representatives for discussion.

### 美参院决定恢复诺斯退伍金

1989-11-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美参院决定恢复诺斯退伍金
    美国参议院二日晚通过一项议案，决定恢复前白宫助手奥利弗·诺斯每年二万三千美元的退伍金。
    诺斯，四十六岁，退伍海军上尉。他因伊朗门事件被判三项重罪，海军因此暂停发给他退伍金。
    这项议案已交付众议院讨论。
                                        （据新华社）
