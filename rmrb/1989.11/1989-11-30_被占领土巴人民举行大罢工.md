Title: Palestinians in Occupied Territories Hold Mass Strike
Author:
Date: 1989-11-30
Edition: 4

Thousands of Palestinians today held a mass strike in the Israeli-occupied Gaza Strip to commemorate the United Nations General Assembly's partition resolution for Palestine passed on November 29, 1947.

The strike resulted in the closure of shops and the suspension of traffic in the Gaza region.

In the Israeli-occupied West Bank, Palestinians plan to hold demonstrations next week after the end-of-term exams for middle school students.

### 被占领土巴人民举行大罢工

1989-11-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　被占领土巴人民举行大罢工
    新华社开罗11月28日电 据此间收到的消息，数千名巴勒斯坦人今天在以色列占领的加沙举行大罢工，纪念1947年11月联合国大会通过的巴勒斯坦分治决议。
    罢工使加沙地区的商店关门，交通停止。
    在以色列占领下的约旦河西岸，巴勒斯坦人计划下星期在该地区的中学生期末考试后举行示威活动。
