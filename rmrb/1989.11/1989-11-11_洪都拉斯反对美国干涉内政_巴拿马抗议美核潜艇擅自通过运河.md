Title: Honduras Opposes U.S. Interference in Domestic Affairs, Panama Protests U.S. Nuclear Submarine's Unauthorized Passage Through the Canal
Author:
Date: 1989-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, November 9th.

Tegucigalpa - Honduras President Azcona on the 9th lodged a protest with the U.S. government, opposing U.S. interference in Honduras' domestic affairs.

According to reports, U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Inter-American Affairs Arcos recently telephoned Honduras' representative in the U.S., expressing concern over the Honduran government's actions ahead of the upcoming national elections. President Azcona believes that Honduras' internal affairs should be resolved by Honduras itself.

On the 26th of this month, Honduras will hold national elections. The ruling Liberal Party requires voters to present not only their ID cards but also yellow identification marks provided by the National Electoral Commission when voting. Reports indicate that the Honduran armed forces have refused this measure.

Xinhua News Agency, Panama City, November 9th.

According to official sources, a U.S. nuclear submarine on November 4th passed through the Panama Canal without the permission of the relevant Panamanian authorities.

The official newspaper, "La República," published a strong-worded protest from the National Association for Renewable Resources of Panama on the 9th. The protest pointed out that the U.S. nuclear submarine, which carries radioactive materials, discharged toxic waste while passing through the canal, not only polluting the natural resources on both sides of the canal but also seriously threatening the lives of the people living there.

This Panamanian official body emphasized that the U.S. action violates the spirit of the new canal treaty signed by the U.S. and Panama in 1977 and is an infringement on Panama's national sovereignty.

### 洪都拉斯反对美国干涉内政  巴拿马抗议美核潜艇擅自通过运河

1989-11-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    洪都拉斯反对美国干涉内政
    巴拿马抗议美核潜艇擅自通过运河
    新华社墨西哥城11月9日电　特古西加尔巴消息：洪都拉斯总统阿斯科纳9日向美国政府提出抗议，反对美国干涉洪都拉斯内政。
    据报道，美国拉美事务副国务卿阿科斯最近打电话给洪驻美国的代表，对洪政府在即将举行的大选中的做法表示关注。阿斯科纳总统认为，洪都拉斯内部的事务应由洪都拉斯自己解决。
    本月26日，洪将举行全国大选，执政的自由党要求选民在投票时除持有身份证外，还需带上全国选举委员会提供的黄色标记。据报道，洪武装部队拒绝这一做法。
    新华社巴拿马城11月9日电　据此间官方9日揭露，美国一艘核潜艇11月4日未经巴拿马有关当局许可，擅自通过了巴拿马运河。
    官方的《共和国报》9日发表了巴拿马全国可再生资源协会一份措词强硬的抗议。抗议指出，这艘装载放射性物质的美国核潜艇通过运河时排放了有毒废物，不仅污染了运河两岸的自然资源，而且严重威胁着两岸人民的生命安全。
    这个巴拿马官方机构强调，美国这一行径违反了1977年美国和巴拿马共同签署的新运河条约的精神，也是对巴拿马国家主权的侵犯。
