Title: All County-Level and Above Cities in Guangdong Use Automatic Telephones
Author: Chen Bingyao
Date: 1989-11-20
Edition: 2

Today, the last county town in Guangdong, Liannan Yao Autonomous County, retired the outdated magnetic hand-crank telephones and opened 3,000 lines of automatic telephones. With this, all county-level and above cities in Guangdong Province have switched to using automatic dialing telephones.

Before the reform and opening up, the development of urban telephones in Guangdong was slow. In 1978, the total capacity of urban telephone exchanges in the province was only 87,000 lines, and there were fewer than 1,200 long-distance circuits. The reform and opening up have brought rapid development to telephone communications. From 1983 to 1988, the province completed an investment of 1.04 billion yuan in urban and rural telephone projects.

### 广东县级以上城市全部使用自动电话
陈炳尧
1989-11-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    广东县级以上城市全部使用自动电话
    据新华社广州11月19日电 （记者陈炳尧）今天广东最后一个县城连山自治县，淘汰了落后的磁石式手摇电话机，开通了3000门自动电话。至此，广东全省县级以上城市全部使用自动拨号电话机。
    改革开放前，广东城市电话发展缓慢。1978年全省城市电话交换机容量只有8.7万门、长途电路不足1200条。改革开放使电话通信获得了迅速的发展。1983年至1988年，全省完成城乡电话投资10.4亿元。
