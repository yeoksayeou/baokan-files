Title: Beijing Holds "International Science and Peace Week" Activities
Author: Yi Xiong
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 2

The first "International Science and Peace Week" activities will be held in Beijing from today until the 12th.

On December 6 last year, the 43rd United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution declaring the week containing November 11 of each year as "International Science and Peace Week." To reflect the efforts made by the Chinese government over the past 40 years in developing science and the economy, benefiting humanity, and maintaining peace, the China Association for Promoting Peace and Disarmament, the China Association for Science and Technology, the All-China Women's Federation, and the Soong Ching-ling Foundation have decided to jointly organize these activities.

It is reported that during this period, a series of activities themed "Love the Motherland, Love Peace, Love Science" will include: a symposium on scientists speaking about peace, an evening of arts and culture on "Science and Peace," opening the China Science and Technology Museum and the Soong Ching-ling Children's Science and Technology Museum, and a youth speech competition.

### 北京举行“国际科学与和平周”活动
一雄
1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    北京举行“国际科学与和平周”活动
    本报北京11月6日讯　首届“国际科学与和平周”活动今天至12日在北京举行。
    去年12月6日，第43届联大通过决议，宣布每年11月11日所属的一周为“国际科学与和平周”。为反映我国政府40年来在发展科学和经济、造福人类、维护和平诸方面做出的不懈努力，中国人民争取和平与裁军协会、中国科协、全国妇联、宋庆龄基金会等单位决定联合举办这一活动。
    据悉，在此期间，以“热爱祖国、热爱和平、热爱科学”为主题的系列活动包括：科学家话和平座谈会、“科学与和平”文艺晚会、开放中国科技馆和宋庆龄儿童科学技术馆、青少年讲演比赛等。
              （一雄）
