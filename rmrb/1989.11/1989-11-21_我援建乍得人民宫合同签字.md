Title: Contract Signing for China's Aid in Building Chad's People's Palace
Author:
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 21st.

The contract signing ceremony for the construction of Chad's People's Palace, aided by China, was held on the 10th in N'Djamena, the capital of Chad.

Chinese Ambassador to Chad Zhou Zhendong and Chad's Minister of Planning and Cooperation Saleh signed the contract on behalf of their respective governments.

The People's Palace, aided by China, is located in N'Djamena. The building covers an area of 16,846 square meters and mainly includes a grand auditorium, an international conference hall, a banquet hall, meeting rooms, and the president's quarters. The project is expected to be completed in three years.

### 我援建乍得人民宫合同签字

1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我援建乍得人民宫合同签字
    新华社北京电　恩贾梅纳消息：中国援建乍得人民宫的施工合同签字仪式10日在乍得首都恩贾梅纳举行。
    中国驻乍得大使周振东和乍得计划和合作部长萨莱赫分别代表本国政府在合同上签字。
    中国援建的人民宫，座落在恩贾梅纳，建筑面积为16846平方米，主要包括大会堂、国际会议厅、宴会厅、会议室和总统用房等。这项工程预计3年完成。
