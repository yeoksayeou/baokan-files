Author:
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

	▲ State Councilor Zhou Jia Hua met with Vasiliy Chuk, the First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet State Committee for Industrial and Mining Safety Production, and his delegation in Beijing on the 27th.
	Before the meeting, Li Boyong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Labor, and Vasiliy Chuk signed a scientific and technological cooperation agreement between the Chinese Ministry of Labor and the Soviet State Committee for Industrial and Atomic Energy Safety Operations in the field of mining industry supervision.
	▲ Zhang Jingfu, Chairman of the China International Talent Exchange Association, hosted a luncheon at the Great Hall of the People on November 28th for Japanese guests including Yoshio Sakurai, President of the Japan Association for Trade Promotion.
	▲ Zhang Jingfu, a Standing Committee member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the China International Talent Exchange Association, met with a delegation led by Siegfried Weiding, Executive Director of the German company Mannesmann, on the 28th at the Great Hall of the People. Zhang Jingfu and Weiding discussed matters related to the training of enterprise management and technical personnel by Mannesmann for the Chinese side.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员邹家华27日在北京会见了苏联国家工业与矿山安全生产监察委员会第一副主席瓦西里丘克一行。
    会见前，劳动部副部长李伯勇与瓦西里丘克签署了中国劳动部和苏联国家工业和原子动力工业安全操作监察委员会在采矿工业监察领域的科学技术合作议定书。
    ▲中国国际人才交流协会主席张劲夫11月28日中午在人民大会堂宴请了日本国际贸易促进协会会长樱内义雄等日本客人。
    ▲中顾委常委、中国国际人才交流协会主席张劲夫28日在人民大会堂会见了联邦德国曼内斯曼公司常务董事西韦丁一行。张劲夫同西韦丁就曼内斯曼公司为中国方面培训企业管理和技术人员等事宜进行了讨论。　（新华社）
