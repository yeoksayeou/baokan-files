Title: U.S. Air Force Conducts Drills in South Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 4

Korean Central News Agency reported on the 20th, quoting a military insider, that the U.S. military deployed over 450 military aircraft in South Korea on the 19th to conduct military exercises targeting North Korea.

According to the report, on the morning and afternoon of the 19th, the U.S. military conducted aerial refueling exercises and simulated nuclear weapon attacks in the South Korean region. Over 160 military aircraft participated in the simulated nuclear weapon attack exercises, including various types of fighter jets, bombers, and reconnaissance planes. The hypothetical enemy for these two exercises was North Korea.

### 美空军在南朝鲜进行演习

1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美空军在南朝鲜进行演习
    朝鲜中央通讯社二十日援引军方消息灵通人士的消息报道，美军十九日调集四百五十多架军用飞机在南朝鲜进行了针对朝鲜北方的军事演习。
    据报道，十九日上午和下午，美军在南朝鲜地区分别进行了军用飞机空中加油演习和空中核武器攻击演习。参加空中核武器攻击演习的军用飞机有一百六十多架，其中包括各种战斗机、轰炸机和侦察机。这两次演习的假想敌人是朝鲜北方。
