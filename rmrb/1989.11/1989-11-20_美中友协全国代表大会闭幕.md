Title: The 12th National Congress of the US-China People's Friendship Association Closes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-20
Page: 4

The 12th National Congress of the US-China People's Friendship Association concluded on November 13 in Washington. Mel Horowitz from New York State was elected as the new president of the association. After his election, Horowitz told a reporter from our newspaper that he would, in accordance with the association's charter and principles, work with the association's committee members to promote mutual understanding and friendship between the American and Chinese peoples.

### 美中友协全国代表大会闭幕

1989-11-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美中友协全国代表大会闭幕
    美国美中人民友好协会第十二届全国代表大会十三日在华盛顿闭幕。纽约州代表梅尔·霍罗威茨当选为新任美中友协会长。霍罗威茨在当选后对本社记者说，他将根据协会宪章原则与协会委员们一起促进美中人民之间的相互理解和友谊。
