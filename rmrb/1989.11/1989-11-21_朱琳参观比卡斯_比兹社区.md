Title: Zhu Lin Visits the Bikas-Bikas Community
Author: Zhū Shìxiāng Yìn Gāolè
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 4

	Zhu Lin, wife of Premier Li Peng, visited the Bikas-Bikas Development Community in the suburbs of Kathmandu this afternoon, accompanied by the wife of Prime Minister Shrestha.

	This development community was established in September 1987 under the instructions of Queen Aishwarya of Nepal. Its purpose is to enhance development awareness, fully utilize local human and material resources to carry out activities, and meet the basic needs of the community's people in terms of clothing, food, housing, sanitation, education, and security.

	Upon her arrival at the community, Zhu Lin was warmly welcomed by more than 500 local villagers and students.

	Zhu Lin enthusiastically visited the community's textile workshop, daycare center, family planning office, and medical care facilities. During her visit to the family planning office, she expressed her hope that the community would achieve greater success in family planning and population control.

	The head of the daycare center introduced the living conditions of the children to Zhu Lin. Zhu Lin said, "Children are the flowers of the nation. I hope they grow up healthy and contribute to the country's construction in the future." Zhu Lin presented the children with teddy bear toys.

	At the end of the visit, Zhu Lin inscribed a message for the development community: "May the seeds of development bear abundant fruits."

### 朱琳参观比卡斯—比兹社区
朱士骧　印高乐
1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　朱琳参观比卡斯—比兹社区
    新华社加德满都11月19日电　（记者朱士骧、印高乐）李鹏总理夫人朱琳今天下午在什雷斯塔首相夫人的陪同下，参观了加德满都郊外的比卡斯—比兹发展社区。
    这个发展社区是遵照尼泊尔艾什瓦尔雅王后的指示，于1987年9月创办的，目的在于增强发展意识，充分利用当地人力、物力资源开展活动，以满足社区人民的衣、食、住、卫生、教育和安全方面的基本需求。
    朱琳来到社区时，受到500多名当地村民和学生的热烈欢迎。
    朱琳兴致勃勃地参观了该社区的纺织、托儿所、计划生育、医疗保健等单位。在参观计划生育办公室时，她希望社区在计划生育和控制人口方面作出更大的成绩。
    日托中心负责人向朱琳介绍了孩子们的生活情况。朱琳说，孩子是祖国的花朵，希望他们健康地成长，今后能为国家建设作出贡献。朱琳向孩子们赠送了狗熊玩具。
    参观结束时，朱琳为这个发展社区题词：“祝发展的种子结出丰硕的果实。”
