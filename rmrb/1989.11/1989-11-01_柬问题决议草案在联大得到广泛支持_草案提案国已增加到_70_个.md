Title: Resolution Draft on Cambodia Gains Broad Support in UNGA
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, October 31st.

Local newspapers reported today that the number of countries co-sponsoring the ASEAN resolution draft on the Cambodian issue for this session of the United Nations General Assembly has increased to 70.

The spokesperson made this disclosure yesterday to the press.

He said that last year, 64 countries co-sponsored the ASEAN resolution draft on the Cambodian issue. This year, Mauritius, Denmark, France, India, Spain, and the Netherlands have also agreed to co-sponsor the draft.

The ASEAN resolution draft on the Cambodian issue gained the support of 122 countries during last year's General Assembly session.

### 柬问题决议草案在联大得到广泛支持  草案提案国已增加到70个

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    柬问题决议草案在联大得到广泛支持
    草案提案国已增加到70个
    新华社曼谷10月31日电  此间报纸今天援引泰国外交部副发言人的话说，本届联合国大会同意作为东盟关于柬埔寨问题的决议草案的提案国已经增加到70个。
    这位发言人是昨天对报界透露的。
    他说，去年参加东盟关于柬埔寨问题决议草案的提案的有64个国家。今年，毛里求斯、丹麦、法国、印度、西班牙和荷兰六国也同意参加这一提案。
    东盟等国关于柬埔寨问题的决议草案在去年的联大会议上获得122个国家的支持。
