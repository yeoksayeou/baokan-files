Title: Air Force Photography Exhibition Marking 40 Years
Author: Qí Hánxiào  Sūn Guóqiáng
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 4

The photography exhibition commemorating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Air Force opened this morning at the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum. The exhibition features more than 200 photographs that vividly depict the journey of the People's Air Force from its inception to becoming a powerful, multi-branch modern military force.

### 空军40年摄影作品展出
齐含笑  孙国强
1989-11-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    空军40年摄影作品展出
    新华社北京11月7日电　（记者齐含笑、通讯员孙国强）纪念人民空军建立40年蓝天摄影作品展览，今天上午在中国人民革命军事博物馆开幕。展出的200多幅作品，真实地再现了人民空军从无到有，从弱到强，发展成为一支多兵种组成的现代化军种的战斗历程。
