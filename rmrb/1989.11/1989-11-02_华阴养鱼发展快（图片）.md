Title: Rapid Development of Fish Farming in Huayin
Author: Wang Tianyu
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 1

Huayin County in Shaanxi Province began developing community-based fish farming in 1984. It has since established a total fish farming area of 4,400 mu, becoming one of the national breeding grounds for yellow river carp and one of the four major fisheries bases in Shaanxi Province.

	Wang Tianyu (Photographer)

    华阴养鱼发展快
