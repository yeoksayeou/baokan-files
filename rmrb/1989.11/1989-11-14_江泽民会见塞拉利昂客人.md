Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Sierra Leonean Guests
Author: Zhou Shuchun
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin Meets with Sierra Leonean Guests

New China News Agency, Beijing, November 13th - (Reporter: Zhou Shuchun) Today in the afternoon, Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Central Military Commission, warmly received a delegation from the Sierra Leone People's National Party led by Edward Tamba Kamara, a member of the Guidance Committee, National Administrative Secretary, and Minister of Party Affairs and State Affairs.

Jiang Zemin expressed a warm welcome to the delegation's visit to China and genuine joy at the achievements made by Sierra Leone under the leadership of the Sierra Leone People's National Party. He also introduced the current domestic situation in China and the spirit of the recently concluded Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the guests.

Kamara conveyed the "brotherly greetings" from Sierra Leonean President and General Secretary of the Sierra Leone People's National Party Joseph Saidu Momoh to the Chinese leaders and congratulated Jiang Zemin on his election as Chairman of the Central Military Commission. Jiang Zemin asked Kamara to convey his sincere greetings to General Secretary Momoh.

Kamara said that there has always been a friendly relationship between the Sierra Leone People's National Party and the Communist Party of China, and that bilateral relations have developed very well. He highly praised the great achievements made by the Communist Party of China in leading the Chinese people to build socialism with Chinese characteristics.

During the meeting, Kamara, on behalf of the Sierra Leone People's National Party, the Sierra Leonean government, and the people, once again reiterated that there is only one China, the People's Republic of China.

Jiang Zemin expressed his appreciation for this. He reaffirmed China's consistent principle of firmly opposing any attempt to create "two Chinas" or "one China, one Taiwan."

The delegation arrived in China on November 6th at the invitation of the Communist Party of China.

Jia Liang, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Communist Party of China, and Sierra Leonean Ambassador to China Sekou Dumbuya, among others, attended the meeting today.

### 江泽民会见塞拉利昂客人
周树春
1989-11-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见塞拉利昂客人
    新华社北京11月13日电  （记者周树春）中共中央总书记、中央军委主席江泽民今天下午在中南海亲切会见了由指导委员会成员、全国行政书记、党务国务部长爱德华·坦巴·卡马拉率领的塞拉利昂全国人民大会党代表团。
    江泽民对代表团来华访问表示热烈欢迎，对塞拉利昂在全国人民大会党的领导下在各方面取得的成就表示由衷的高兴。他还向客人介绍了当前中国国内形势和最近召开的中国共产党十三届五中全会的精神。
    卡马拉转达了塞拉利昂总统、全国人民大会党总书记约瑟夫·赛义杜·莫莫对中国领导人“兄弟般的问候”，并对江泽民当选中央军委主席表示祝贺。江泽民请卡马拉转达他对莫莫总书记诚挚的问候。
    卡马拉说，塞拉利昂全国人民大会党和中国共产党之间一直存在着友好关系，双边关系发展得非常好。他高度赞扬了中国共产党领导中国人民建设具有中国特色的社会主义所取得的巨大成绩。
    会见时，卡马拉代表全国人民大会党、塞拉利昂政府和人民再次重申：世界上只有一个中国，就是中华人民共和国。
    江泽民对此表示赞赏。他重申了中国坚决反对任何制造“两个中国”或“一中一台”企图的一贯原则立场。
    代表团应中国共产党邀请于11月6日来华访问。
    中联部部长朱良和塞拉利昂驻华大使谢库·敦布亚等参加了今天的会见。
