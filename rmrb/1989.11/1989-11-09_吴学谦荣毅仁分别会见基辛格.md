Title: Wu Xueqian and Rong Yiren Meet with Kissinger
Author: Sūn Yì
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 8th.

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian of the State Council met with former U.S. Secretary of State Dr. Henry Kissinger this morning at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. Afterward, the host and guest had a pleasant breakfast together.

Kissinger arrived in Beijing last night at the invitation of China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs to make a friendly visit to China.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 8th.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Rong Yiren met with old friend Dr. Henry Kissinger this noon, engaging in friendly conversation and hosting a home-style banquet in his honor.

### 吴学谦荣毅仁分别会见基辛格
孙毅
1989-11-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    吴学谦荣毅仁分别会见基辛格
    本报北京11月8日讯　记者孙毅报道：国务院副总理吴学谦今天早晨在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了美国前国务卿亨利·基辛格博士。随后，宾主双方愉快地共进早餐。
    基辛格是应我国外交部的邀请，于昨天晚上抵达北京对中国进行友好访问的。
    本报北京11月8日讯　全国人大常委会副委员长荣毅仁今天中午会见了老朋友亨利·基辛格博士，同他进行了友好的交谈，并设家宴款待。
