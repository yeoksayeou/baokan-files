Title: Key Discussion Points for This Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Edition: 5

	Key discussion points for this issue:

		★ Attention should be paid to the transportation industry in impoverished regions, with more policy support provided;
		★ Multiple agencies managing highways have led to overlapping responsibilities and unclear duties;
		★ Highway enterprises should adopt corporate management practices.
