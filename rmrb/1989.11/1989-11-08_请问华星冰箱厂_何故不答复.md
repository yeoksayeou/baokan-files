Title: Why Doesn't Hua Xing Refrigerator Factory Respond?
Author: 
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 6

I purchased a Hua Xing brand refrigerator (manufactured by Hangzhou Hua Xing Refrigerator Factory) through someone in Shanyin County, Henan, last October. After using it for only 4 hours, the compressor malfunctioned. I sent a registered letter to the factory to contact them, but there was no response for over two months. Later, the county committee's communication group also wrote to the factory. It was not until March 15 of this year that we finally received a response from the factory. They first asked for the production date and serial number of the refrigerator to be sent to them, and then they asked for the compressor to be disassembled and sent to them. We complied, but since sending the compressor on April 30, there has been no response. On July 14 and August 28, two more registered express letters were sent to inquire, but there was still no reply.

Mountain West Women's Federation, Jiang County, Shanxi Province, Shanshuo Qiaoyun

### 请问华星冰箱厂  何故不答复
闪巧云
1989-11-08
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：监督哨

    请问华星冰箱厂
    何故不答复
    我于去年10月托人在河南沁阳县购买了一台华星牌电冰箱（是杭州华星冰箱厂出品），购回后仅使用了4个小时，压缩机就出了故障。我给厂家去挂号信联系，两个多月没见回音。后来，我县县委通讯组又给厂家去信。直到今年3月15日，才盼来了厂家回音。他们先是让把电冰箱的出厂日期和编号寄去，接着又叫把压缩机拆下来寄去，我们照着办了，但自从4月30日把压缩机寄去后，一直未见回信。7月14日和8月28日又去了两封挂号快件信催问，仍无人答理。
                  山西绛县妇联　闪巧云
