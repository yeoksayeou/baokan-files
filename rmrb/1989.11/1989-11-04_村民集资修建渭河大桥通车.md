Title: Villagers Raise Funds to Build Weihe River Bridge, Which Opens to Traffic
Author: Zhang Ruoyu
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 2

The Weihe River Bridge in Luojia Village, built primarily with funds raised by villagers, opened to traffic today. This bridge was constructed with 4 million yuan raised by 2,400 households in Luojia Village, Jiangzha Town, Xunyi County, Shaanxi Province, and an additional 2.8 million yuan in assistance from the provincial, municipal, and county governments.

### 村民集资修建渭河大桥通车
张若愚
1989-11-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    村民集资修建渭河大桥通车
    新华社西安11月1日电　（记者张若愚）主要由村民集资修建的罗家村渭河大桥今日正式通车。这座大桥是由陕西省扶风县绛帐镇罗家村的2400户农民集资400万元修建的，省市县资助280万元。
