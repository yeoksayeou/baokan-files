Title: China's Ambassadors Present Credentials to Three Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, November 16th.
China's new ambassador to Austria, Hu Benyao, presented his credentials to President Waldheim at the presidential palace on November 16th. After the credential presentation ceremony, Waldheim had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Hu.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 15th.
China's new ambassador to Albania, Gu Maoxuan, presented his credentials to Chairman Alija of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania on November 15th. After the credential presentation, Alija and Gu Maoxuan discussed the development of relations between Albania and China and exchanged views on the international situation.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 21st.
Praia News: China's new ambassador to Cape Verde, Xie Zhenjiao, presented his credentials to President Pereira of the Republic of Cape Verde on November 21st. President Pereira had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Xie.

### 我驻奥阿佛三国大使递交国书

1989-11-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我驻奥阿佛三国大使递交国书
    新华社维也纳电　中国新任驻奥地利特命全权大使胡本耀11月16日在总统府向瓦尔德海姆总统递交了国书。在递交国书仪式结束后，瓦尔德海姆同胡大使进行了友好谈话。
    新华社地拉那电　中国新任驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使顾懋萱11月15日向阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席阿利雅递交了国书。递交国书后，阿利雅和顾懋萱就发展阿中两国关系问题进行了交谈，并就国际形势交换了看法。
    新华社北京电　普腊亚消息：中国新任驻佛得角大使谢振骝11月21日向佛得角共和国总统佩雷拉递交了国书。佩雷拉总统同谢振骝大使进行了友好的谈话。
