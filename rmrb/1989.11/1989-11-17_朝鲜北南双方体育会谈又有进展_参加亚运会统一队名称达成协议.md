Title: North and South Korea Make Progress in Sports Talks, Agree on Unified Team Name for Asian Games
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-11-17
Page: 3

Our reporter Xu Baokang reports from Panmunjom, November 16th: The sports delegations from North and South Korea held their fourth round of talks today in Panmunjom. The two sides discussed the formation of a unified team to participate in the 11th Asian Games and reached an agreement on the name of the unified team.

Both sides agreed that the name of the North-South Korean unified team in English will be “KOREA.”

During today's talks, the two sides also discussed methods for forming teams in various events and the selection of athletes, but did not reach a consensus. Both sides agreed to focus on these issues during the fifth round of talks scheduled for November 24th in Panmunjom.

### 朝鲜北南双方体育会谈又有进展  参加亚运会统一队名称达成协议
徐宝康
1989-11-17
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    朝鲜北南双方体育会谈又有进展  参加亚运会统一队名称达成协议
    本报板门店11月16日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜北方和南方的体育代表团今天在板门店举行了第四次会谈，双方就共同组成统一队参加第11届亚运会问题再次举行磋商，并在统一队的名称上达成协议。
    双方同意，朝鲜北南统一队的名称英文为“KOREA”。
    在今天的会谈中，双方还就各项目组队的方法和运动员的选拔问题进行了商讨，但没有取得一致意见。双方最后商定11月24日在板门店举行的第五次会谈中，将集中讨论上述问题。
