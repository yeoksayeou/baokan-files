Title: Ministry of Railways Decides to Add 20 Pairs of Semi-Temporary Trains for Next Spring Festival Travel Season
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Railways announced at the national railway Spring Festival transportation work meeting held in Lanzhou today that 20 pairs of semi-temporary direct passenger trains will be added during the 1990 Spring Festival travel season, and 3 pairs of scheduled trains will extend their running sections.

The 1990 Spring Festival travel season will start on January 12 and end on February 20. During these 40 days, the nationwide additional direct passenger trains are as follows:

Harbin-Jinan 160/157, 158/159;
Harbin-Fangzi 188/185, 186/187;
Changchun-Tianjin 244/243;
Qiqihar (Baicheng)-Tianjin 154/153 (238/237);
Wuchang-Guangzhou 253/254;
Beijing (Shanghai)-Guangzhou 175/176 (255/256);
Shenyang-Zhengzhou 104/101, 102/103;
Beijing-Chongqing 115/116;
Beijing-Chengdu (Baoji) 141/142 (281/282);
Taiyuan-Wuchang 292/293, 294/291;
Wuchang-Nanning 181/182;
Zhanjiang-Changsha 292/291;
Chengdu-Guangzhou 257/5/4/1, 252/3/6/8 (half pair);
Pukou, Xuzhou-Urumqi 196/197, 198/195 (half pair);
Lanzhou-Urumqi 245/246;
Zhengzhou-Urumqi 193/194;
Chongqing North-Wuchang 208/205, 206/207;
Hohhot-Baicheng 178/175, 176/177;
Xi'an (Zhengzhou)-Shanghai 108/105, 106/107 (114/111, 112/113);
Taiyuan (Shijiazhuang)-Shanghai West 152/149, 150/151;
Beijing-Hefei 125/126.

The scheduled trains with extended running sections are as follows:

Nanchang-Pingxiang 387/388 extended to Wuchang, train numbers changed to 187/186, 185/188;
Kunming-Hengyang 166/165 extended to Guangzhou, train numbers changed to 166/163, 164/165;
Lanzhou-Wuchang 204/201, 202/203 extended to Zhuzhou, train numbers changed to 128/125, 126/127.

The Ministry of Railways requires all railway staff to work together, overcome difficulties, and ensure that the first Spring Festival travel season after the railway fare adjustment is better than previous years, with a focus on safety.

### 铁道部决定  明年春运增开20对半临时客车

1989-11-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    铁道部决定
    明年春运增开20对半临时客车
    新华社兰州11月8日电  今天在兰州结束的全国铁路春节运输工作会议宣布，1990年春运期间全国将增开临时直通客车20对半，还有3对图定客车延长运行区间。
    1990年春运将从1月12日开始到2月20日止。在这40天内，全国增开的直通客车是：哈尔滨—济南160／157、158／159次，哈尔滨—坊子188／185、186／187次，长春—天津244／243次，齐齐哈尔（白城）—天津154／153（238／237）次，武昌—广州253／254次，北京（上海）—广州175／176（255／256）次，沈阳—郑州104／101、102／103次，北京—重庆115／116次，北京—成都（宝鸡）141／142（281／282）次，太原—武昌292／293、294／291次，武昌—南宁181／182次，湛江—长沙292／291次，成都—广州257／5／4／1、252／3／6／8次（半对），浦口、徐州—乌鲁木齐196／197、198／195次（半对），兰州—乌鲁木齐245／246次。郑州—乌鲁木齐193／194次，重庆北—武昌208／205、206／207次，呼和浩特—白城178／175、176／177次，西安（郑州）—上海108／105、106／107（114／111、112／113）次，太原（石家庄）—上海西152／149、150／151次，北京—合肥125／126次。延长运行区间的图定客车是：南昌—萍乡387／388次延长到武昌，车次改为187／186、185／188次；昆明—衡阳166／165次延长到广州，车次改为166／163、164／165次；兰州—武昌204／201、202／203次延长到株洲，车次改为128／125、126／127次。
    铁道部要求全路广大干部职工，齐心协力，克服困难，把铁路调价后的第一次春运工作做得比往年更好，切实保证安全。
