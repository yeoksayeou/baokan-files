Title: 400,000 Mu of Cotton Achieves Near 100 Kilograms per Mu Yield: Shandong's Large-Scale High-Yield Development Succeeds

Author: Jiā Jiànzhōu Yú Zhènjiāng

Date: 1989-11-07

Edition: 2

    The Shandong Provincial Agricultural Commission recently organized experts and professors to conduct yield inspections on a 400,000 mu cotton high-yield development project, aiming for 100 kilograms of lint cotton per mu. The average yield of these 400,000 mu of cotton fields was 96.9 kilograms of lint cotton per mu, with a total production of 801,500 piculs. Among these, 283,797 mu yielded over 100 kilograms of lint cotton per mu, accounting for 68.6% of the total development area. In Huimin, Leling, and Yucheng counties (cities), some cotton fields achieved yields of over 125 kilograms per mu. Some high-yield plots reached 135 to 150 kilograms per mu, which is 42.4% higher than the average yield of non-development cotton fields in the seven counties and cities responsible for the development. The cumulative increase in production was 239,000 piculs. The 400,000 mu cotton high-yield development project, led by the Shandong Cotton Research Center, is one of the key agricultural development projects identified by the provincial government for the near term. The goal is to achieve an average yield of 100 kilograms of lint cotton per mu over three years from 1989 to 1991. The success of this project's first phase demonstrates a broad prospect for large-scale high-yield cotton development.

### 40万亩棉花亩产近百公斤  山东大面积高产开发告捷
贾建舟　于振江
1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    40万亩棉花亩产近百公斤  山东大面积高产开发告捷
    本报讯  山东省农委最近组织有关专家、教授，逐县对40万亩棉花亩产皮棉100公斤开发项目进行了测产验收。这40万亩棉田平均亩产皮棉96.9公斤，总产80.15万担。其中，亩产皮棉100公斤以上的面积达28.3797万亩，占开发总面积的68.6％。惠民、乐陵、禹城3县（市）部分棉田亩产达125公斤以上。一些高产地块达到135至150公斤，比7个承担开发县、市的非开发棉田平均每亩增42.4％，累计增产23.9万担。由山东棉花研究中心主持的40万亩棉田高产开发，是省政府今年确定的近期农业重点开发项目之一，要求在1989年至1991年3年中，平均亩产皮棉100公斤。这一项目首战告捷，为棉花大面积高产开发展示了广阔的前景。　（贾建舟　于振江）
