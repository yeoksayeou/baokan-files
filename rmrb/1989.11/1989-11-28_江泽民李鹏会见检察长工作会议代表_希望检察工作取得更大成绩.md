Title: Jiang Zemin and Li Peng Meet with Representatives of the National Prosecutors' Conference
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 27th.

Jiang Zemin and Li Peng met with representatives of the National Prosecutors' Conference today in the Great Hall of the People. They expressed the hope that the work of the procuratorate would achieve greater success.

New China News Agency, Beijing, November 27th. General Secretary of the Communist Party of China Central Committee Jiang Zemin and Premier of the State Council Li Peng met with representatives attending the National Prosecutors' Conference this afternoon in the Great Hall of the People.

Jiang Zemin, in his speech, on behalf of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, extended cordial greetings and expressed high respect to comrades working in the procuratorate and the political and legal front, as well as to their families. He said that the work of the procuratorate and the political and legal system is very important in our people's democratic socialist state. During this political turmoil, the national procuratorial team has stood firm and clear-cut, and the Central Committee is very satisfied.

Jiang Zemin said that the recent announcement by the Supreme People's Procuratorate and the Supreme People's Court has been warmly welcomed by the broad masses and has had a great deterrent effect on criminals. Within the time limit specified in the announcement, nearly 40,000 criminals have surrendered. He said that if criminals still voluntarily confess after the time limit specified in the announcement, we also welcome them.

Jiang Zemin emphasized that the most critical and necessary work at present is to stabilize the domestic situation, which includes two aspects: political stability and rapid economic development. He said that in maintaining a stable and united political situation and stabilizing the situation, the work of the procuratorate is very important. He hoped that the procuratorate would achieve new and greater successes.

Li Peng, in his speech, said that the procuratorate is a legal supervisory body and has done a lot of work in maintaining social order, safeguarding national stability, and strengthening people's democratic dictatorship. He said that the procuratorate should protect the legal rights of citizens and crack down on various criminal activities of criminals.

Li Peng stressed that the struggle between foreign hostile forces and us over peaceful evolution and counter-peaceful evolution will be long-term, and we must use legal weapons to fight against them. He hoped that the procuratorate would strengthen the construction of clean government and build a good team.

Members of the Secretariat of the Communist Party of China Central Committee Yang Baibing, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Hanbin, State Councilor Wang Fang, and Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Liu Fuzhi, among others, attended the meeting.

### 江泽民李鹏会见检察长工作会议代表　希望检察工作取得更大成绩

1989-11-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　江泽民李鹏会见检察长工作会议代表
　　希望检察工作取得更大成绩
    新华社北京11月27日电　中共中央总书记江泽民、国务院总理李鹏今天下午在人民大会堂会见了出席全国检察长工作会议的代表。
    江泽民在讲话中代表党中央、国务院向在检察机关和政法战线上工作的同志们以及他们的家属表示亲切的慰问和崇高的敬意。他说，检察和政法工作在我们人民民主专政的社会主义国家是十分重要的。在这次政治风波中，全国检察队伍立场坚定、旗帜鲜明，中央是非常满意的。
    江泽民说，高检、高法前不久发布的《通告》，受到广大群众的热烈欢迎，对犯罪分子是很大的威慑。在《通告》规定的期限内，近4万名犯罪分子投案自首。他说，《通告》规定的期限过后，如果犯罪分子还主动坦白交代，我们也是欢迎的。
    江泽民强调，现在最关键和最必要的工作是稳定国内的形势，这包括两个方面，一是政治上稳定，二是把经济尽快搞上去。他说，在维护安定团结的政治局面、稳定局势中，检察机关的工作是很重要的，希望检察工作取得新的更大成绩。
    李鹏在讲话中说，检察机关是法律监督机构，在维护社会秩序、保卫国家安定和加强人民民主专政方面，做了很多工作。他说，检察机关一要保护公民的合法权益，二要打击犯罪分子的各种犯罪活动。
    李鹏强调说，国外的敌对势力同我们之间和平演变与反和平演变的斗争，将是长期的，我们要运用法律的武器同他们进行斗争。他希望检察机关加强廉政建设，把队伍建设好。
    中共中央书记处书记杨白冰、全国人大常委会副委员长王汉斌、国务委员王芳、最高人民检察院检察长刘复之等，参加了会见。
