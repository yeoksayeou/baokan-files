Title: Irrigation Projects Boost Grain Production
Author: Zhao Huaji
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 1

	Irrigation Projects Boost Grain Production

	Actively undertaking irrigation projects and relying on well irrigation to overcome a severe drought, the village of Wuhu in Yutai County, Shandong Province, achieved the highest historical yield for its 26,000 mu (17,333 acres) of rice this year. This is a grand scene of farmers enthusiastically delivering grain.
