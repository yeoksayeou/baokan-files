Title: The Beijing Spare Parts Service Center of Krones Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Page: 2

China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Krones Beijing Spare Parts Service Center officially opened in Beijing today. This is a technical cooperation project between the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the China Light Industry Machinery Corporation, and the German company Krones.

Krones is a world-renowned manufacturer of beer and beverage filling equipment. Over 100 countries use their products. Nearly 100 breweries, wineries, and beverage factories in China have introduced their equipment and technology.

### 克朗斯北京备件服务中心开业

1989-11-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    克朗斯北京备件服务中心开业
    本报北京十一月一日讯　中国技术进出口总公司联邦德国克朗斯北京备件服务中心今天在北京开业。这是中国技术进出口总公司、中国轻工业机械总公司和联邦德国克朗斯公司的技术合作项目。
    联邦德国克朗斯公司是世界著名的生产啤酒、饮料灌装设备的厂家。一百多个国家使用他们的产品，我国近百家啤酒、葡萄酒和饮料厂引进了他们的设备和技术。
