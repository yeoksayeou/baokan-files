Title: Renowned Petrochemical Expert Gu Jinxin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 2

Renowned petrochemical expert, Vice Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial People's Congress, and Honorary Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League, Gu Jinxin, passed away in Shenyang on October 23, 1989, at the age of 82 due to illness.

Gu Jinxin was a native of Nanhuixian, Shanghai. He graduated from Nanjing Central University in 1928 and studied in Germany in 1931, earning a Ph.D. in Engineering in 1936. After the Marco Polo Bridge Incident, he returned to China. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, he served as the Deputy Director and Chief Engineer of Fushun Petroleum Plant No. 3 and No. 1, Director and Chief Engineer of Fushun Petroleum Design Institute, and Deputy Director and Chief Engineer of the Liaoning Provincial Petroleum and Chemical Industry Bureau. He dedicated himself to the petroleum and chemical industry for many years, leading and participating in numerous key projects in China's petroleum and petrochemical industry, including research, design, construction, and renovation. He made significant contributions to the development of China's petroleum and chemical industry.

The farewell ceremony for Gu Jinxin was held on October 31 at the General Hospital of Shenyang Military Region. Wreaths were sent by the Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others, including Yu Qiu-li.

### 著名石化专家顾敬心逝世

1989-11-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    著名石化专家顾敬心逝世
    新华社沈阳电　著名石油化工专家、辽宁省人大副主任、民盟辽宁省委员会名誉主任委员顾敬心同志，因病于1989年10月23日在沈阳逝世，终年82岁。
    顾敬心同志是上海市南汇县人，1928年毕业于南京中央大学，1931年赴德国留学，1936年获工学博士学位，“七七”事变后回国。新中国成立后，他历任抚顺石油三厂、一厂副厂长兼总工程师，抚顺石油设计院院长兼总工程师，辽宁省石油化工厅副厅长兼总工程师等职。他长期奋战在石油化工战线，多次主持和参加我国石油及石油化工重点项目的研究、设计、建设、改造等重要工作，为发展我国石油化工工业做出了重要贡献。
    顾敬心遗体告别仪式，10月31日在沈阳军区总医院举行。全国人大常委会办公厅、全国政协、中共中央统战部、中国科学院等单位，余秋里等同志送了花圈。
