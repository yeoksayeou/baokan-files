Title: Shanghai Updates World Record in Superconductivity Research
Author: Zhang Xuequan
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

Chinese scientists have made significant advancements in superconductivity research: they have used the metal-organic chemical vapor deposition method and a single-layer film technology to successfully develop a yttrium-based superconducting thin film with a zero-resistance temperature of 90.5K, setting a new world record.

The metal-organic chemical vapor deposition method involves using metal-organic compounds to deposit a single layer of film through chemical vapor deposition. Previously, the highest zero-resistance temperature achieved using this method by scientists at Tohoku University in Japan was 89K.

In the international competition for superconductivity research, scientists have recently mostly used direct current magnetron sputtering technology to develop thin films. However, this method can only be applied to relatively regular objects. The single-layer film technology using metal-organic chemical vapor deposition has the advantages of simple equipment and suitability for electronic device production processes. It is particularly useful for creating superconducting thin films on complex-shaped objects such as wires and strips, helping to overcome the processing difficulties caused by the brittleness of ceramic superconducting materials.

The superconducting thin film developed by China using single-layer film technology is the result of collaborative research between the Shanghai Institute of Metallurgy, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the Shanghai Institute of Organic Chemistry.

### 上海刷新世界超导研究纪录
张学全
1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    上海刷新世界超导研究纪录
    据新华社上海11月9日电　（记者张学全）我国科学家在超导研究方面再获重大进展：他们运用金属有机源化学气相沉积法，用一次成膜技术，最近研制成功零电阻温度为90.5K的钇系超导薄膜，刷新了世界纪录。
    金属有机源化学气相沉积法，是用金属有机化合物通过化学气相淀积一次成膜的新技术。在此之前，日本东北大学运用这一技术制成的超导薄膜，零电阻温度最高为89K。
    在国际超导研究的竞争中，科学家们近年大多采用直流磁控溅射技术研制薄膜。但这一方法要在较为规则的物体上才能应用。而使用一次成膜的金属有机源化学气相沉积技术，具有研制设备简单，适合电子器件生产流程的特点。尤其是可在复杂形状的物体如丝材、带材上制成超导薄膜，有助于克服因陶瓷超导材料的脆性引起的加工困难。
    我国用一次成膜技术研制的超导薄膜，是中国科学院上海冶金研究所和上海有机化学研究所协作攻关的结果。
