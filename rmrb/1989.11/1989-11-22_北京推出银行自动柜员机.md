Title: Beijing Introduces Bank Automatic Teller Machines
Author: Wu Enfang
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 2

Beijing Introduces Bank Automatic Teller Machines

    The first batch of bank automatic teller machines in the capital began operating today at the China Bank Beijing Branch and other institutions. This is the first time that automatic teller machines have been used north of the Yangtze River in China. It is another product in the Long City Card series following the foreign exchange Renminbi Long City Card and the Renminbi Long City Visa Card introduced by the Beijing Branch. This machine currently provides users with four types of services: deposits, withdrawals, checking the current day's deposit balance, and changing personal passwords.

### 北京推出银行自动柜员机
吴恩芳
1989-11-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京推出银行自动柜员机
    本报北京11月21日讯　首都第一批银行自动柜员机今天起在中国银行北京分行营业厅等机构投入使用。这是我国长江以北首次使用的自动柜员机，是北京分行继外汇人民币长城卡、人民币长城万事达卡后推出的又一项长城卡系列产品。这种机器现阶段可向用户提供存款、取款、查阅本人当日存款余额、修改本人密码四种功能服务。
                                （吴恩芳）
