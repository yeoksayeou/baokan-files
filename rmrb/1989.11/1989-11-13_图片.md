Title: A West German Company Develops a Mouth-Dialing Telephone for the Disabled
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 7

A company in West Germany has developed a telephone device that allows users to dial by blowing air instead of using their hands. This innovation is particularly beneficial for disabled individuals, enabling them to make phone calls using a blowing method.
