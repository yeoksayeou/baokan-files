Title: Commemoration of Karl von Ossietzky's 100th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1989-11-16
Edition: 7

This year marks the 100th anniversary of the birth of Karl von Ossietzky, a German anti-fascist political writer. In 1935, he was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize. The image depicts young students from the German Democratic Republic paying their respects at the gravesite of the martyr.
