Title: Several Jewelry Stores and a Bank in Hong Kong Robbed
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, November 8th.

Several jewelry and watch stores in the bustling commercial and tourist district of Tsim Sha Tsui in Kowloon, Hong Kong, were robbed on November 6th by armed thugs wielding knives and pistols. The total value of the stolen jewelry, watches, and foreign currency is approximately HK$2,146,000.

At the same time, a bank in Sau Mau Ping, Kowloon, was also robbed by a single thug, resulting in a loss of over HK$50,000.

### 港数家珠宝店银行被劫

1989-11-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    港数家珠宝店银行被劫
    新华社香港11月8日电　香港繁华的商业旅游区九龙尖沙咀三家钟表珠宝及首饰公司6日分别遭手持尖刀手枪的匪徒的抢劫，被劫的珠宝钟表及外币总值约214.6万港元。
    九龙秀茂坪一家银行同时也遭一匪徒的抢劫，损失5万多港元。
