Title: Thailand International Athletics Invitational Opens; Chinese Athletes Win Six Gold Medals on the First Day
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 3

The 1989 Thailand International Athletics Invitational opened this afternoon in Bangkok. Chinese athletes won 6 gold medals in the 13 events held on the first day, and broke one national record.

After Chen Dongmei secured the first gold medal for China in the women's 400-meter hurdles with a time of 57.25 seconds, Zhao Cunlin added another gold with his victory in the men's 200-meter dash, finishing in 20.80 seconds. This result broke the national record of 20.97 seconds for the event.

The other four gold medals won by the Chinese team today were:
- Men's 400-meter hurdles: Wu Hongyou (52.62 seconds)
- Men's triple jump: Du Benzhong (16.55 meters)
- Men's 1500-meter run: Duan Xiuquan (3 minutes 52.79 seconds)
- Men's 4×100-meter relay: (40.47 seconds)

The gold medal for the women's 200-meter dash was won by Wang Huizhen of the Chinese Taipei team, with a time of 23.68 seconds.

In addition to Josephine Mary winning the gold medal in the women's 400-meter dash, the Malaysian team also took first place in the women's 4×100-meter relay.

Japanese, South Korean, Philippine, and Mauritian athletes won the gold medals in the men's shot put, men's high jump, men's 400-meter dash, and women's 1500-meter dash, respectively.

### 泰国国际田径邀请赛开幕　我国选手首天获六枚金牌

1989-11-25
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　泰国国际田径邀请赛开幕
　　我国选手首天获六枚金牌
    新华社曼谷11月23日电　1989年泰国国际田径邀请赛今天下午在这里开幕。中国选手在首天进行的13个项目的决赛中，获得6枚金牌，并打破一项全国纪录。
    继陈冬梅以57秒25的成绩为中国队在女子400米栏中取得第一枚金牌后，赵存林又锦上添花，夺得男子200米跑的金牌，成绩是20秒80。他的这个成绩打破了该项目20秒97的全国纪录。
    中国队今天获得的另外4枚金牌是：男子400米栏，吴宏有（成绩52秒62）；男子三级跳远，都本忠（成绩16米55）；男子1500米跑，段秀全（成绩3分52秒79）；男子4×100米接力（成绩40秒47）。
    女子200米跑的金牌被中国台北队的王慧珍摘取，成绩是23秒68。
    马来西亚队今天除约瑟芬·玛莉获女子400米跑金牌外，还获得了女子4×100米跑接力赛的第一名。
    日本、南朝鲜、菲律宾、毛里求斯选手分别获得男子铅球、男子跳高、男子400米跑和女子1500米跑的金牌。
