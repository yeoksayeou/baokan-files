Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 2

Vice Premier and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with the delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea led by Kim Yong-ho, Vice Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission, at the Great Hall of the People on the morning of November 3. After the meeting, both sides signed the minutes of the 29th session of the Sino-DPRK Governmental Science and Technology Cooperation Committee. Both sides will cooperate on 58 projects in the fields of energy, light industry, machinery, and agriculture.

Li Yimang, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and President of the China International Exchange Association, met with and hosted a dinner for a delegation of prominent Soviet scholars led by V.N. Kashin, Deputy Secretary of the Economic Department of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of November 1. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-11-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健3日上午在人民大会堂会见以国家科学技术委员会副委员长金应浩为团长的朝鲜政府科技代表团。会见后，双方签署了中朝政府科技合作委员会第29届会议议定书，双方将在能源、轻工、机电、农业等方面进行58个项目的合作。
    ▲中顾委常委、中国国际交流协会会长李一氓1日晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请由苏联科学院院士、经济学部副秘书瓦·尼·卡申率领的苏联知名学者代表团。（新华社）
