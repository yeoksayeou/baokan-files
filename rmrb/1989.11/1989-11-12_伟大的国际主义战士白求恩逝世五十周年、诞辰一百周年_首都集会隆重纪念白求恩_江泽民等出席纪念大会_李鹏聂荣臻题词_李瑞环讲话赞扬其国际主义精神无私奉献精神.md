Title: Great Internationalist Warrior White Memorial Ceremony Marking the 50th Anniversary of His Death and 100th Anniversary of His Birth
Author: Liáo Lián
Date: 1989-11-12
Edition: 1

		Great Internationalist Warrior White Memorial Ceremony Marking the 50th Anniversary of His Death and 100th Anniversary of His Birth

		Beijing Holds Grand Memorial for White

		Jiang Zemin Attends Memorial Meeting; Li Peng and Nie Rongzhen Write Inscriptions

		Li Ruihuan Speaks, Praising His Internationalist Spirit and Selfless Dedication

		Beijing, November 11 (Xinhua) — Reporter Liao Lian reports: This morning, 3,000 representatives from various circles in the capital gathered at the Great Hall of the People to solemnly commemorate the 50th anniversary of the death and the 100th anniversary of the birth of the great revolutionary humanitarian, internationalist, and communist warrior, Dr. Norman Bethune.

		General Secretary of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Central Military Commission Jiang Zemin attended the meeting. Member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Li Ruihuan delivered an important speech.

		President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Han Xu presided over the memorial meeting. He read the inscriptions from Member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Li Peng and Marshal Nie Rongzhen for the 50th anniversary of Dr. Bethune's death: "Carry forward the spirit of Dr. Bethune, serve the people wholeheartedly"; "Learn from Comrade Bethune's selfless dedication."

		Li Ruihuan first, on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese people, expressed deep remembrance for Comrade Bethune.

		Li Ruihuan pointed out that due to the advocacy of the Communist Party of China and the influence of Mao Zedong's famous article "In Memory of Norman Bethune," the spirit of Dr. Bethune has become a model, a standard, and a tradition among the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people. The spirit of Dr. Bethune has become an integral part of the soul of the Chinese nation.

		Li Ruihuan said, the spirit of Dr. Bethune is the spirit of internationalism, the spirit of communism, the spirit of selflessness, and the spirit of serving the people wholeheartedly, the spirit of hard work and selfless dedication.

		Norman Bethune was born on March 3, 1890, in a pastor's family in Canada. In 1938, Bethune led the Canadian-American Medical Team to the Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region in China, treating wounded soldiers of the Eighth Route Army and the local people. Unfortunately, he passed away on November 12, 1939.

		Attending the memorial meeting were: Li Tieying, Wu Xueqian, Xiao Ke, Geng Biao, Hab Comrade, Zhao Puchu, Bethune's former comrades-in-arms Yang Chengwu and Lü Zhengcao, and responsible comrades from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Health, the Beijing Military Region, and the Beijing Municipal Government. The Canada-China Friendship Association, the Canadian Bethune Foundation, and the Canadian Bethune Memorial Museum also sent representatives to attend the meeting. Attending the meeting were also: Canadian Ambassador to China Di E and Canadian experts, students, and other Canadian guests in Beijing.

		At the meeting, Bethune's former comrade-in-arms Jiang Yizhen and "a guest from Dr. Bethune's hometown" — Chairman of the Canada-China Friendship Association Molly Phillips — spoke successively.

		Before the memorial meeting, leaders such as Jiang Zemin and Li Ruihuan met with the Canadian delegation attending the memorial activities and other Canadian guests.

		The memorial meeting was organized by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 伟大的国际主义战士白求恩逝世五十周年、诞辰一百周年  首都集会隆重纪念白求恩  江泽民等出席纪念大会　李鹏聂荣臻题词  李瑞环讲话赞扬其国际主义精神无私奉献精神
廖玒
1989-11-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    伟大的国际主义战士白求恩逝世五十周年、诞辰一百周年
    首都集会隆重纪念白求恩
    江泽民等出席纪念大会　李鹏聂荣臻题词
    李瑞环讲话赞扬其国际主义精神无私奉献精神
    本报北京11月11日讯  记者廖玒报道：今天上午，首都各界代表3000余人在人民大会堂隆重集会，纪念伟大的革命人道主义者、国际主义者、共产主义战士白求恩逝世50周年、诞辰100周年。
    中共中央总书记、中共中央军委主席江泽民出席大会，中共中央政治局常委李瑞环作了重要讲话。
    中国人民对外友好协会会长韩叙主持纪念大会。他宣读了中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理李鹏和聂荣臻元帅分别为纪念白求恩逝世50周年的题词：“继承和发扬白求恩精神，全心全意为人民服务”；“学习白求恩同志无私奉献的精神”。
    李瑞环首先代表中共中央、中国共产党人和中国人民，对白求恩同志表示深切怀念。
    李瑞环指出，由于中国共产党的倡导和毛泽东同志《纪念白求恩》这篇著名文章的影响，白求恩精神已在中国共产党和中国人民中间形成为一种风范，一种楷模，一种准则，一种传统。白求恩精神已经成为中华民族之魂的一个组成部分。
    李瑞环说，白求恩精神，就是国际主义精神，就是共产主义精神，就是毫无自私自利之心的精神，也就是我们常说的全心全意为人民服务的精神，艰苦奋斗、无私奉献的精神。（全文另发）
    诺尔曼·白求恩1890年3月3日生于加拿大一个牧师的家庭。1938年，白求恩率加美医疗队来到中国的晋察冀边区，为八路军战士和边区人民治病。不幸于1939年11月12日去世。
    出席纪念大会的还有：李铁映、吴学谦、肖克、耿飚、习仲勋、赵朴初，白求恩生前战友杨成武、吕正操，及外交部、卫生部、北京军区和北京市的负责同志。加中友协联合会、加拿大白求恩基金会和加拿大白求恩纪念馆，也派代表专程参加了大会。出席大会的还有：加拿大驻华大使狄鄂以及在京的加拿大专家、留学生和其他加拿大外宾。
    会上，白求恩生前战友江一真和“来自白求恩大夫故乡的客人”——加中友协联合会主席莫莉·菲利普斯先后讲了话。
    大会前，江泽民、李瑞环等领导同志会见了参加纪念活动的加拿大代表团、加拿大驻华大使等加拿大外宾。
    纪念大会是由中国人民对外友好协会主办的。
