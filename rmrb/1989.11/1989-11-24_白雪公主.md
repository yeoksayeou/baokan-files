Title: Snow White
Author: Huang Dongcheng
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 8

Snow White
A vast expanse of white, a vast expanse of white… The beautiful Snow White gracefully appears, trailing a pristine white veil, a white headscarf, and a slender figure. Her pure white skin and gentle waves of tenderness dissolve into water, gently washing away all impurities, cleansing the world of all pollution, leaving it spotless and free of any dust. The greens become greener, the reds become redder, making the vibrant colors more vibrant and the transparent more transparent. All colors are restored to their original luster. This is the beauty of Snow White's heart, bestowing upon the earth a pure white tenderness. A vast expanse of white, a vast expanse of white…
