Title: East German Free German Youth League Elects New First Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

The Free German Youth League of the German Democratic Republic has elected a new first secretary, F. Tikowski.

According to a report by the German News Agency, Tikowski was elected by a majority of votes in a secret ballot held on the 24th. The former first secretary, E. Honecker, resigned on the 17th of this month.

### 民德青盟选出新的第一书记

1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　民德青盟选出新的第一书记
    弗·蒂科夫斯基当选为民主德国自由德国青年联盟中央第一书记。
    据德通社报道，在二十四日举行的秘密选举中，蒂科夫斯基是以超过半数的选票而当选的。原第一书记埃·奥里希于本月十七日提出辞职。
