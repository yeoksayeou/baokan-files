Title: United Nations General Assembly Passes Resolution Calling for Protection and Peaceful Use of Antarctica
Author: Yang Yuehua
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

The United Nations General Assembly today passed a resolution calling for the protection and peaceful use of Antarctica.

The resolution states that Antarctica is of special significance to international peace and security, global climate, economy, and scientific research. Therefore, it should not become a site or target of international disputes but should be used solely for peaceful purposes.

The resolution also states that any institution established to protect Antarctica must first consult with all countries in the world.

This resolution, drafted by 24 non-aligned countries, was passed with 87 votes in favor and no votes against. Including China, 7 countries abstained.

### 联合国大会通过决议呼吁　保护并和平利用南极大陆
杨跃华
1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国大会通过决议呼吁
　　保护并和平利用南极大陆
    新华社联合国11月22日电　（记者杨跃华）联合国大会今天通过一项决议，呼吁保护和和平利用南极大陆。
    决议说，南极大陆对国际和平与安全、全球气候、经济和科研等都具有特殊的意义，因此，它不应成为国际纷争的场所或目标，而只准用于和平目的。
    决议还说，建立任何保护南极的机构都必须事先与世界各国协商。
    这份由24个不结盟国家起草的决议，是以87票赞成、零票反对通过的。包括中国在内的7个国家弃权。
