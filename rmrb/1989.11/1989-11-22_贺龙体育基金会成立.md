Title: The Establishment of the He Long Sports Foundation
Author: Dú Ruǒyuán
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, November 21st.

The He Long Sports Foundation was established in Changsha. Vice President Wang Zhen inscribed the name of the foundation.

The He Long Sports Foundation is established to commemorate Marshal He Long's significant contributions to China's sports industry, accelerate the construction of sports facilities, promote mass sports, and improve athletic skills.

The foundation will handle domestic and international voluntary donations, organize sports fundraising activities, and can issue sports lottery tickets.

It is understood that this is the first sports foundation in the country to be approved by relevant departments. Song Renqing, Liao Hansheng, Wang Shoudao, and others have written inscriptions for the foundation. The State Sports Commission and the National Olympic Sports Center, among others, have expressed congratulations on the establishment of the foundation.

### 贺龙体育基金会成立
杜若原
1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　贺龙体育基金会成立
    本报长沙十一月二十一日电　记者杜若原报道：贺龙体育基金会在长沙成立。国家副主席王震为基金会题写会名。
    贺龙体育基金会是为纪念贺龙元帅对我国体育事业作出的重大贡献、加快体育事业场地设施建设、普及群众体育和提高运动技术水平而成立的。
    基金会将办理国内外自愿捐赠资金，开展体育募捐活动，也可发行体育奖券。
    据悉，这是国内首家经过有关部门批准的体育基金会。宋任穷、廖汉生、王首道等为基金会题了词。国家体委、国家奥林匹克体育中心等对基金会的成立表示祝贺。
