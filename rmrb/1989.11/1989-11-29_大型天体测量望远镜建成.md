Title: Large Astronomical Telescope Completed
Author: Yang Yude
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 2

A large astronomical telescope has been completed. According to Xinhua News Agency in Shanghai on November 28, the first large astronomical optical observation device designed by China—the 1.56-meter astrometric telescope—has recently passed the appraisal at the Shanghai Astronomical Observatory.

Experts believe that this is a comprehensive astronomical observation device that integrates modern precision optics, precision machinery, and automatic control technologies. Its aperture, design features, measured performance, and manufacturing processes are all superior to the largest similar telescopes currently in use internationally. This telescope will soon be open to both domestic and international users.

### 大型天体测量望远镜建成
杨玉德
1989-11-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　大型天体测量望远镜建成
    据新华社上海11月28日电　（通讯员杨玉德）我国自行设计的第一架大型天文光学观测设备——1.56米天体测量望远镜，日前在上海天文台通过鉴定。
    专家认为，这是一架综合了现代精密光学、精密机械和自动控制等多项先进技术的天文观测设备，通光口径、设计特点、实测性能及制造工艺，都优于目前国际上最大的同类望远镜。这台望远镜不久将向国内外开放。
