Title: Six Countries Officially Express Intent to Participate in Beijing Asian Games
Author: Lǐ Hèpǔ
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 4

Six countries have officially expressed their intention to participate in the Beijing Asian Games. Japan will send a strong team to compete.

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 31 (Reporter Li Hepu) The reporter learned from the Chinese Olympic Committee that by October 31, the Olympic Committees of Japan, Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, and Bangladesh have formally informed the Beijing Asian Games Organizing Committee of their intention to send delegations to participate in the 11th Asian Games to be held in Beijing next year.

	In a letter to the Executive Chairman of the Beijing Asian Games Organizing Committee, Wu Shaozu, the Chairman of the Japanese Olympic Committee, Dī Yìmíng, wrote: "I am pleased to inform you that upon receiving the invitation, the Japanese Olympic Committee has decided to send a strong team to participate in the Beijing Asian Games."

	It is understood that the Olympic Committees of Kuwait and Iran have designated liaison officers to contact the Chinese Olympic Committee.

	The registration for this Asian Games is divided into three deadlines: March 30 next year, all members of the Asian Olympic Council submit their intentions to participate in the events; June 22, submit the events and number of participants; August 22 is the final registration deadline.

### 六个国家正式表示参加北京亚运会  日本将派强队参赛
李贺普
1989-11-02
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    六个国家正式表示参加北京亚运会
    日本将派强队参赛
    新华社北京10月31日电　（记者李贺普）记者从中国奥委会获悉，截至10月31日，已有日本、新加坡、印度尼西亚、马来西亚、泰国和孟加拉国六个国家的奥委会正式向北京亚运会组委会表示，将派代表团参加明年在北京举行的第十一届亚运会。
    日本奥委会主席堤义明在写给北京亚运会组委会执行主席伍绍祖的信中说：“我高兴地通知你，在接到邀请书之际，日本奥委会决定，将派遣一支强有力的队伍参加北京亚运会。”
    据了解，科威特和伊朗的奥委会已指定联络员，同中国奥委会进行联系。
    本届亚运会分3个报名日期：明年3月30日前各亚奥理事会成员提出参加项目的意向，6月22日前提出参加比赛项目和人数，8月22日为最后报名日期。
