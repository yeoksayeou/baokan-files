Title: Veterans Watch a Folk Opera from the Old Revolutionary Base Areas
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 3

Veterans who had fought and worked in the Taihang Mountains, including Li Desheng, Chen Xilian, Liao Hansheng, and Kang Keqing, enthusiastically attended the Navy Theater tonight to watch a performance by the Shanxi Xingyuan Yangge Drama Troupe from the old revolutionary base areas. The troupe performed the modern yangge opera "Old Eighth Route Army Soldier."

"Old Eighth Route Army Soldier" depicts the historical period when Marshal Zhu De commanded the anti-Japanese base areas and the people in their struggle against the enemy, showcasing the deep bond between the military and the people.

### 老战士观看老区的秧歌剧

1989-11-24
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　老战士观看老区的秧歌剧
    据新华社北京11月23日电　李德生、陈锡联、廖汉生、康克清等曾经在太行山区战斗工作过的领导同志今晚兴致勃勃地来到海军礼堂，观看来自太行老区的山西襄垣秧歌剧团演出的现代秧歌剧《老八路》。
    《老八路》展示了朱德总司令指挥抗日根据地军民抗敌斗争的历史岁月，表现了军民的鱼水深情。
