Title: Li Premier Meets Diplomatic Envoys at Reception Hosted by Nepalese Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 19th.

This afternoon, Nepalese Foreign Minister Uprety hosted a reception for Premier Li Peng's visit to Nepal. Li Peng warmly shook hands with and exchanged greetings with the diplomatic envoys attending the reception.

Those present included ambassadors from the United Kingdom, Italy, Japan, Thailand, North Korea, the German Democratic Republic, the Soviet Union, Tanzania, Egypt, Pakistan, and Poland, as well as chargé d'affaires from the United States, France, Bangladesh, and Australia.

Li Peng also met with former Nepalese ambassadors to China, praising their contributions to the development of Nepal-China friendship.

### 在尼泊尔外交大臣举行的招待会上　李总理会见驻尼外交使节
朱士骧　印高乐
1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　在尼泊尔外交大臣举行的招待会上
　　李总理会见驻尼外交使节
    新华社加德满都11月19日电　（记者朱士骧、印高乐）今天下午，尼泊尔外交大臣乌帕德亚雅为李鹏总理访问尼泊尔举行招待会。李鹏同出席招待会的驻尼泊尔外交使节们亲切握手，互致问候。
    参加招待会的还有英国、意大利、日本、泰国、朝鲜、民主德国、苏联，坦桑尼亚、埃及、巴基斯坦和波兰等国驻尼大使以及美国、法国、孟加拉国和澳大利亚的临时代办。
    李鹏还会见了尼泊尔历任驻华大使，赞扬了他们为发展尼中友谊作出了贡献。
