Title: India's Second Phase of General Elections
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

India's second phase of general elections was held on the 24th in 266 constituencies under strict security measures. A total of 2,976 candidates are competing for 266 seats out of the 524 seats in the lower house.

Despite the strict security measures, violent incidents continued to occur in various constituencies, and the voter turnout was not high. In the elections in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, nine people were killed and seventy-five were injured in violent incidents.

### 印度大选进行第二阶段选举

1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　印度大选进行第二阶段选举
    印度大选第二阶段选举在严密的保安措施下二十四日在二百六十六个选区举行投票。共有二千九百七十六名候选人竞选下院五百二十四个席位中的二百六十六个席位。
    尽管采取了严密的保安措施，各选区仍不断发生暴力事件，出来投票的选民比率不高。在北方邦选举的暴力事件中，有九人被杀，七十五人受伤。
