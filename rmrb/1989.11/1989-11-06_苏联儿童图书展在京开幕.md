Title: Soviet Children's Book Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author: Wáng Nà Méi
Date: 1989-11-06
Page: 4

A one-week "Soviet Children's Book Exhibition" was held at the China Children and Youth Activity Center (Guan Yuan). The opening ceremony took place on the morning of November 4 at the China Children and Youth Activity Center. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Re Jieqiong, Soviet Ambassador to China, Troianovskii, and his wife, as well as relevant officials from China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Culture, and other departments, and some Chinese and Soviet children attended the opening ceremony.

### 苏联儿童图书展在京开幕
王娜梅
1989-11-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    苏联儿童图书展在京开幕
    本报北京十一月四日讯　记者王娜梅报道：为期一周的“苏联儿童图书展览”在中国儿童少年活动中心（官园）举行。十一月四日上午在中国儿童少年活动中心举行了开幕式。人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼、苏联驻华大使特罗扬诺夫斯基和夫人以及我国外交部、文化部等有关方面负责人和部分中苏儿童出席了开幕式。
