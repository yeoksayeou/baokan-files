Title: Member of the CPC Central Committee Politburo, Knotek, Says Czechoslovakia is Determined to Follow the Socialist Path
Author:
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 4

    Member of the CPC Central Committee Politburo and Central Committee Secretary Knotek said, "In the current complex historical situation, the CPC is determined to follow the path of building socialism. The reform program adopted at the 7th Plenary Session of the Central Committee expresses this determination."

    Knotek made these remarks at a meeting held in Prague on November 6 to celebrate the 72nd anniversary of the October Revolution.

    Knotek also pointed out that reform is not a modern trend. Implementing reform should not lead to the disruption of social and economic stability, which is the basic prerequisite for the success of the reform.

    When discussing the current situation where many people are skeptical and hesitant about Czechoslovakia's reforms, he emphasized the need to strengthen the offensive nature of ideological work, to persuade and win over people to support and implement the reforms with practical actions.

### 捷共中央主席团成员克诺特克说  捷决心走社会主义道路  社会和经济稳定是改革成功前提

1989-11-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    捷共中央主席团成员克诺特克说
    捷决心走社会主义道路
    社会和经济稳定是改革成功前提
    新华社布拉格11月7日电  捷共中央主席团成员、中央书记克诺特克说，“在当前复杂的历史形势下，捷共决心走建设社会主义的道路，7中全会所通过的改革纲领，表达了这一决心”。
    克诺特克是在布拉格6日举行的庆祝十月革命72周年大会上发表这番讲话的。
    克诺特克还指出，改革不是摩登浪潮。实施改革应不导致出现破坏社会和经济稳定，这是改革成功的基本前提。
    在谈到当前有许多人对捷克斯洛伐克改革持怀疑和观望态度时，他强调指出，必须加强思想工作的进攻性，说服和争取人们以实际行动支持和实施改革。
