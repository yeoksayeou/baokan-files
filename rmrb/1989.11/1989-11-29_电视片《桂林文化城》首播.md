Title: The Premiere of the Television Series "Guilin Cultural City"
Author: Dé Rèn
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 3

The large-scale television documentary art film "Guilin Cultural City" has recently been officially broadcast. This film truthfully reflects the historical period from 1938, when Wuhan fell, to 1944, during which Guilin carried out rescue movements and anti-war cultural undertakings.

### 电视片《桂林文化城》首播
德任
1989-11-29
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　电视片《桂林文化城》首播
    本报讯　大型电视文献艺术片《桂林文化城》最近正式播出。该片真实反映了1938年武汉沦陷后到1944年这段历史时期桂林开展的救亡运动和抗战文化事业。　（德　任）
