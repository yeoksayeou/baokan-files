Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 31st.

    **Foreign Affairs Brief**

    ▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Alphu Alphu Jinmei, received a delegation led by Robert Attlee, Chairman of the China Group of the British Parliament, at the Great Hall of the People on October 31.

    The delegation of the China Group of the British Parliament, which arrived in China on October 23, has visited Beijing, Lhasa, Chengdu, and Kunming.

    ▲ The signing ceremony of the Cultural Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Chad was held in Beijing on the morning of October 28.

    He Jingzhi, Acting Minister of Culture, and Gibrillah Hussein Greinke, Minister of Culture, Youth, and Sports of Chad, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

    The Chad government's cultural delegation arrived in Beijing on October 26 and began an 11-day visit to China.

    ▲ The minutes of the fifth session of the China-Bangladesh Government Economic, Trade, and Technology Cooperation Joint Committee were signed in Beijing on the afternoon of October 31. Liu Xuejin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation, and Enam Ahmed Chowdhury, Secretary of the Bureau of Foreign Resources of the Ministry of Planning of Bangladesh, signed the minutes on behalf of their respective governments.

    The fifth session of the China-Bangladesh Government Economic, Trade, and Technology Cooperation Joint Committee was held in Beijing from October 27 to 31.

    ▲ Shanghai Mayor Zhu Rongji met with former U.S. President Nixon in Beijing on the evening of October 31. Both sides had a friendly discussion on continuing the cooperation between Shanghai and the U.S. business community.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长阿沛·阿旺晋美10月31日在人民大会堂会见了由英国议会中国小组主席罗伯特·艾德礼率领的代表团。
    英国议会中国小组代表团自10月23日抵达中国后，曾访问了北京、拉萨、成都和昆明。
    ▲中华人民共和国政府和乍得共和国政府文化协定签字仪式10月28日上午在北京举行。
    文化部代部长贺敬之和乍得文化、青年、体育部长吉布里纳·侯赛因·格林基分别代表两国政府在协定上签了字。
    乍得政府文化代表团是10月26日抵达北京开始对中国进行为期11天的访问的。
    ▲中国、孟加拉国政府经济、贸易、科技合作联合委员会第五次会议纪要10月31日下午在北京签署。经贸部副部长、中国政府经济贸易代表团团长吕学俭和孟加拉国计划部外资总局秘书、孟加拉国政府经济贸易代表团团长依纳姆·阿罕默德·乔杜里分别代表本国政府在会议纪要上签字。
    中孟政府经济、贸易、科技合作联合委员会第五次会议于10月27日至31日在北京举行。
    ▲上海市市长朱熔基10月31日晚上在北京会见了美国前总统尼克松先生。双方就继续发展上海市同美国经济界的合作关系等问题进行了友好的交谈。　（新华社）
