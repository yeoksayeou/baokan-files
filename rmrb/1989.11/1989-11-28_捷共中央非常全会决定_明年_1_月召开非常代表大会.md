Title: The Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee's Extraordinary Plenary Session Decides to Hold an Extraordinary Party Congress Next January
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

The Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee's extraordinary plenary session, which began on the 26th, concluded in the early hours of the 27th. The plenary session decided to hold an extraordinary party congress on January 26, 1990.

The newly elected General Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Urbánek, reported on the domestic situation in Czechoslovakia. He said that the most important task at present is to formulate a clear short-term policy for the entire party.

The plenary session made decisions on personnel issues. It elected 7 new members to the Central Committee Presidium, 2 new members to the Secretariat, and promoted 8 alternate members to full members of the Central Committee.

### 捷共中央非常全会决定　明年1月召开非常代表大会

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　捷共中央非常全会决定
　　明年1月召开非常代表大会
    新华社布拉格11月27日电　26日开始的捷共中央非常全会于27日凌晨结束，全会决定明年1月26日召开捷克斯洛伐克共产党非常代表大会。
    新当选的捷共中央总书记乌尔班内克在会上就捷国内局势问题做了报告。他说，目前最重要的任务是制定全党近期的明确方针。
    全会就人事问题作出了决定。全会增选了7名中央主席团委员、2名中央书记和书记处成员，并将8名候补中委递升为中央委员。
