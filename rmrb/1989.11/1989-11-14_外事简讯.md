Author:
Date: 1989-11-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 14th.

	▲ The Japan-China Economic Association delegation for the 1989 fiscal year held a farewell banquet at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of November 11. The highest advisor, Eishiro Saito, and the delegation leader, Ryogai Kawai, expressed in their speeches that the friendly relations between China and Japan are tied together by strong bonds and will not be severed by any particular reasons.

	State Councilor Zhu Jiahu attended the banquet.

	▲ A delegation of Chinese Communist Party workers, led by Yang Dezhong, a member of the Central Committee and the first deputy director of the General Office of the Central Committee, departed Beijing on the morning of November 13 to visit Bulgaria for a friendly visit.

	▲ On the morning of November 13, the People's Liberation Army Air Force organized a visit to the Aviation Museum for military attachés from various countries' embassies in China and air force attachés.

	Air Force Deputy Commander Lieutenant General Lin Hu presented each military attaché with a copy of "The Contemporary Chinese Air Force."

	The military attachés were also invited to watch a video introducing the 40-year history of the People's Liberation Army Air Force.

	▲ On the evening of November 13, Lieutenant General Zhou Keyu, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, hosted a banquet at the Xishan Hotel in Beijing to welcome the "Erich Weinert" Song and Dance Troupe and the "Vorwärts" Sports Association delegation from the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic. The guests from the Democratic Republic of Germany were invited by the General Political Department. The song and dance troupe will perform in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲日本1989年度日中经济协会访华团11日晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆举行了答谢宴会。访华团最高顾问斋藤英四郎、团长河合良一在致辞中表示，中日之间的友好关系是由强有力的纽带连接起来的，不会由于某些原因而被切断。
    国务委员邹家华出席了宴会。（本报讯）
    ▲由中共中央委员、中央办公厅第一副主任杨德中率领的中国共产党党的工作者友好访问团13日上午离开北京前往保加利亚进行友好访问。
    ▲中国人民解放军空军11月13日上午组织各国驻华大使馆武官和空军武官参观了航空博物馆。
    空军副司令员林虎中将向各国武官赠送了《当代中国空军》一书。
    各国武官还应邀观看了介绍人民解放军空军40年历史的录像片。
    ▲中国人民解放军总政治部副主任周克玉中将13日晚在北京香山饭店主持宴会，欢迎德意志民主共和国国家人民军“艾里希·魏纳特”歌舞团和“前进体协”代表团。民主德国客人是应总政的邀请来访的。歌舞团将在北京、上海、杭州访问演出。　（新华社）
