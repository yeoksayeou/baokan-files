Title: Chinese Team Wins 3 Gold Medals at the Saddam Weightlifting Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 6th

Chinese athlete Lai Runming won three gold medals today in the 60-kilogram category at the second Saddam International Weightlifting Championship held here.

Lai Runming, who is an Olympic silver medalist, achieved the following results today: 120 kilograms in the snatch, 140 kilograms in the clean and jerk, and a total of 260 kilograms.

Today, competitions were also held in the 67.5-kilogram, 75-kilogram, 82.5-kilogram, 90-kilogram, and 100-kilogram categories. Iraqi athletes won a total of 8 gold medals, Soviet athletes won 4, and Bulgarian athletes won 3.

### 萨达姆举重赛中国队再夺3枚金牌

1989-11-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    萨达姆举重赛中国队再夺3枚金牌
    新华社巴格达11月6日电　中国选手赖润明今天在这里举行的第二届萨达姆国际举重锦标赛60公斤级的比赛中，夺得3枚金牌。
    赖润明是奥运会亚军，他今天的3项成绩是，抓举120公斤、挺举140公斤、总成绩260公斤。
    今天还进行了67.5公斤、75公斤、82.5公斤、90公斤和100公斤5个级别的比赛，伊拉克选手共夺得其中8枚金牌，苏联选手夺得其中4枚，保加利亚运动员摘走3枚。
