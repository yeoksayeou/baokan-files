Title: Hebei's Foreign Economic Cooperation Talks See Active Deals
Author: Liú Yīngrú
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 2

Hebei's foreign economic cooperation talks saw active deals. The New China News Agency reported from Shijiazhuang on November 10 that the 1989 Hebei Provincial Foreign Economic Cooperation Talks recently concluded in Shijiazhuang. A total of 450 foreign businesspeople from Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, Japan, Singapore, the United States, France, Belgium, Australia, and 12 other countries and regions participated in the talks. The talks were lively, with a total signed contract value of 80.4368 million US dollars.

### 河北外经合作洽谈成交活跃
刘英茹
1989-11-11
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

    河北外经合作洽谈成交活跃
    新华社石家庄11月10日电 （记者刘英茹）河北省1989年对外经济合作洽谈会日前在石家庄结束。来自香港、澳门、台湾、日本、新加坡、美国、法国、比利时、澳大利亚等17个国家和地区的450名外商参加了这次洽谈会。洽谈会成交活跃，对外签约成交总金额为8043.68万美元。
