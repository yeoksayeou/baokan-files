Title: Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Establishment of the State of Palestine
Author:
Date: 1989-11-16
Edition: 4

In the eve of the first anniversary of the establishment of the State of Palestine, Palestinian President Yasser Arafat delivered a speech in Tunis today, calling on the Palestinian people to strengthen their unity.

In his speech, Arafat reviewed the diplomatic achievements made by the State of Palestine in its first year. He said that so far, the State of Palestine has been recognized by 93 countries around the world. He emphasized that the Palestinian people must thwart all attempts to undermine Palestinian unity and strengthen national solidarity.

Regarding the issue of the Middle East peace conference, Arafat said that the Palestinian people respond to all peace initiatives and that he believes that the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue should become an important preparatory stage for the international peace conference in the Middle East.

According to another report, the National Unified Command of the Palestinian Uprising in the Occupied Territories also called on the Palestinian people to strengthen their struggle to end Israeli occupation of Arab territories on the same day.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, November 14th, Arab League Secretary-General Chedli Klibi said today that the Arab League supports the Palestinian people's struggle to recover the occupied territories and exercise their right to self-determination.

Klibi made these remarks in a statement commemorating the first anniversary of the establishment of the State of Palestine.

### 庆祝巴勒斯坦国成立一周年  阿拉法特呼吁人民加强团结  卡利比说阿盟支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争

1989-11-16
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    庆祝巴勒斯坦国成立一周年
    阿拉法特呼吁人民加强团结
   卡利比说阿盟支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争
    新华社突尼斯11月14日电　在庆祝巴勒斯坦国成立一周年前夕，巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特今天在这里发表讲话，呼吁巴勒斯坦人民加强团结。
    阿拉法特在讲话中回顾了巴勒斯坦国成立一年来在外交上取得的可喜成就。他说，迄今为止，巴勒斯坦国已经得到世界上93个国家的承认。他强调说，目前巴勒斯坦人民要挫败一切企图破坏巴勒斯坦团结的阴谋，加强民族团结。
    在谈到中东和会问题时，阿拉法特说，巴勒斯坦人民响应一切和平倡议，他认为巴以对话应该成为中东和平国际会议的重要准备阶段。
    另据报道，同一天，被占领土巴勒斯坦起义全国统一指挥部也呼吁巴勒斯坦人民加强起义斗争，以结束以色列对阿拉伯领土的占领。
    据新华社突尼斯11月14日电　阿拉伯联盟秘书长卡利比今天在这里说，阿盟支持巴勒斯坦人民收复被占领土和行使民族自决权利的斗争。
    卡利比是在纪念巴勒斯坦国成立一周年之际的一项声明中讲这番话的。
