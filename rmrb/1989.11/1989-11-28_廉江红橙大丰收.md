Title: Bumper Harvest of Red Oranges in Lianjiang
Author: Lǐ Gāng
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 2

Lianjiang, the largest red orange production base in China, has achieved a total output of 17,000 tons this year, reaching an all-time high.

Lianjiang red oranges are renowned for their balanced sweet and sour taste, thin skin, red flesh, and unique flavor. In 1986, they were rated as a national quality fruit. They are popular in Hong Kong, Macau, and many other countries, earning the title "Queen of Fruits."

Currently, it is the season of "orange yellow and tangerine green." The city of Zhanjiang is holding its first Red Orange Festival, attracting a steady stream of domestic and international businesspeople and tourists.

### 廉江红橙大丰收
李刚
1989-11-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　廉江红橙大丰收
    本报讯　我国最大的红橙生产基地县——广东廉江县今年总产达1.7万吨，创历史最高水平。
    廉江红橙以甜酸适中、皮薄肉红、风味独特而在1986年被评为全国优质水果，出口畅销港澳地区和许多国家，有“果中皇后”美称。
    当前正值“橙黄桔绿时”，湛江市在此间举办首届红橙节，闻讯而至的国内外商人、游客络绎不绝。（李刚）
