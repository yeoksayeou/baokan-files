Title: Arafat Writes to Mubarak to Seek Clarification from the U.S. on Baker's Proposals
Author:
Date: 1989-11-12
Edition: 4

The Palestine Liberation Organization has decided to request the United States to clarify the peace plan proposed by U.S. Secretary of State Baker.

According to a report by the Egyptian Middle East News Agency today, a member of the PLO Executive Committee, Sulani, arrived in Cairo today to deliver a letter from PLO Chairman Arafat to Egyptian President Mubarak. In the letter, Arafat raised five points:

1. The PLO has the right to form a Palestinian delegation composed of representatives from both inside and outside the occupied territories. The PLO, as the sole representative of the Palestinian people, will discuss all relevant issues, including the Palestinian issue and the Middle East peace process, with Israel in Cairo or any other location.

2. The PLO hopes to understand whether the United States accepts the Palestinian peace plan as the basis for any Palestinian-Israeli dialogue.

3. The PLO hopes to understand whether the United States supports open dialogue without any preconditions.

4. The PLO wants to know whether the United States supports preliminary talks between Palestine and Israel and considers such talks, along with Israel's proposed elections in the occupied territories, as a step towards convening an international conference on the Middle East issue.

5. The PLO hopes the United States will guarantee Israel's commitment to four principles of U.S. Middle East policy: recognizing UN Security Council Resolutions 242 and 338 as the basis for resolving the Middle East issue, ensuring the security of all countries in the region, recognizing the inalienable rights of the Palestinian people, and accepting the principle of land for peace.

The PLO's five points were raised after Baker proposed five new points on the 9th to supplement his previous five-point peace plan.

### 阿拉法特致函穆巴拉克  要美国澄清贝克建议的一些问题

1989-11-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿拉法特致函穆巴拉克
    要美国澄清贝克建议的一些问题
    新华社开罗11月10日电  据埃及中东通讯社今天报道，巴解组织已决定要求美国澄清国务卿贝克提出的巴以对话计划。
    报道说，巴解组织执委会成员苏拉尼今天到达开罗，转交巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特写给埃及总统穆巴拉克的一封信件。阿拉法特在信中提出五点要求：
    一、巴解组织有权成立由被占领土内外代表组成的巴勒斯坦代表团。巴解组织作为巴勒斯坦人民的唯一代表同以色列在开罗或任何地方讨论所有有关问题，其中包括巴勒斯坦问题和中东和平进程问题。
    二、巴解组织希望了解美国是否接受巴勒斯坦和平计划作为任何巴以对话的基础。
    三、巴解组织希望了解美国是否赞成不带任何先决条件的公开对话。
    四、巴解组织希望知道美国是否赞成巴以举行预备性会谈，并把这种预备性会谈和以色列提出的在被占领土举行选举看成是召开中东问题国际会议的一个步骤。
    五、巴解组织希望美国保证以色列对美国中东政策4项原则的承诺，即承认联合国安理会242号和338号决议为解决中东问题的基础，保证本地区所有国家安全，承认巴勒斯坦人民不可剥夺的权利，接受以土地换取和平的原则。
    巴解组织的5项要求是在贝克9日提出5点新建议用以补充他从前的5点和平计划之后提出的。
