Title: The University of Hong Kong Sets Up a Children's Cancer Fund
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 2

The "University of Hong Kong Children's Cancer Fund" was officially established on November 3. The initial goal of this fund is to raise 5 million Hong Kong dollars.

According to statistics, currently, an average of 5 to 6 children in Hong Kong die from cancer each month. The number of new childhood cancer cases each year ranges from 100 to 180. Childhood cancer in Hong Kong is predominantly leukemia.

### 香港中大设立儿童癌病基金

1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    香港中大设立儿童癌病基金
    新华社香港电　香港“中文大学儿童癌病基金”11月3日正式成立。这个基金的初步目标是筹措经费500万港元。
    据统计，目前香港平均每月有5至6名儿童因患癌症而死亡。每年新患癌症的儿童则在100至180人之间。香港儿童癌症以血癌居首。
