Title: Vibration 851 Donates 1 Million Yuan to Asian Games
Author: Lǐ Lì
Date: 1989-11-12
Edition: 3

Vibration 851 Biological Engineering Research and Development Corporation donated 1 million yuan to the Beijing Asian Games. Tonight, Professor Yang Zhenhua of the corporation and Wang Zhiliang, director of the fundraising department of the Asian Games Organizing Committee, signed the donation agreement.

Professor Yang Zhenhua is a national model teacher. At today's donation ceremony, he expressed his willingness to contribute his inventions and creations to the Asian Games and hoped that this would inspire more scientific and technological workers to care for and support the Asian Games.

Yang Chengwu, Lü Zhengcao, Li Menghua, Zhang Baifa, and more than 400 people from various circles attended the signing ceremony.

### 振华851向亚运会捐款一百万元
李莉
1989-11-12
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    振华851向亚运会捐款一百万元
    新华社北京11月11日电　（记者李莉）振华851生物工程研究开发总公司向北京亚运会捐款100万元人民币。今晚，该公司教授杨振华和亚运会组委会集资部部长王志良分别在捐赠协议书上签字。
    杨振华教授是全国模范教师，在今天的捐赠仪式上，他表示愿意用自己的发明创造为亚运会做出贡献，并希望以此带动更多的科技工作者关心和支持亚运会。
    杨成武、吕正操、李梦华、张百发，以及各界人士400多人参加了签字仪式。
