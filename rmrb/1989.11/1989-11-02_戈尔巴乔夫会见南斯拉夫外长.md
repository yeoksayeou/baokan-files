Title: Gorbachev Meets with Yugoslav Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 3

Gorbachev Meets with Yugoslav Foreign Minister

The General Secretary of the CPSU, Gorbachev, met with the Yugoslav Foreign Minister Lončar, who was visiting Moscow, on October 31. Gorbachev said that the current period is the best era for Soviet-Yugoslav cooperation. The Soviet-Yugoslav Declaration signed in Belgrade in March 1988, along with other documents, has provided a broad prospect for such cooperation.

According to TASS, during the meeting, both sides expressed interest in strengthening ties in various fields of national social life, particularly in the economic sphere. Both sides believe that developing economic cooperation is fully in the interests of the two friendly countries.

### 戈尔巴乔夫会见南斯拉夫外长

1989-11-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    戈尔巴乔夫会见南斯拉夫外长
    苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫十月三十一日会见正在莫斯科访问的南斯拉夫外长隆查尔时说，目前是苏南合作的最好年代，一九八八年三月在贝尔格莱德签署的苏南宣言及其他文件为这种合作提供了广阔的前景。
    据塔斯社报道，会见时，双方表示了在国家社会生活各个领域，特别是经济领域加强联系的兴趣。双方认为发展经济合作完全符合两个友好国家的利益。
