Title: Comoros President Killed in Coup
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

Comoros President Ahmed Abdallah Abderamane was shot to death late on the 26th in a coup attempt in the capital, Moroni.

Reports say the coup failed. According to the constitution, Chief Justice Haribou Chebani will temporarily assume the role of head of state.

Abdallah is 70 years old and has been in power since 1978. Comoros is an island nation in the Indian Ocean, located between the African continent and Madagascar, consisting of four islands with a population of less than 500,000.

### 科摩罗总统在兵变中遇害

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　科摩罗总统在兵变中遇害
    科摩罗总统艾哈迈德·阿卜杜拉·阿卜杜拉曼二十六日深夜在首都莫罗尼的一次兵变中被枪杀。
    报道说，兵变没有成功。根据宪法，最高法院院长哈瑞布·切班尼将临时担任国家元首。
    阿卜杜拉今年七十岁，一九七八年开始执政。科摩罗是印度洋的一个岛国，位于非洲大陆和马达加斯加岛之间，由四个岛屿组成，人口不足五十万。
