Title: Romanian "Scînteia" Publishes Article Exposing Imperialist Attack on Socialism
Author:
Date: 1989-11-17
Edition: 4

Romania's "Scînteia" today published an article stating that currently, imperialist reactionary forces are launching an ideological assault on socialism, maliciously attempting to deny the proven superiority of the socialist system.

The article states that the class struggle between the two diametrically opposed social and political systems of socialism and capitalism is an objective reality of the contemporary world. It has not weakened or disappeared; instead, it is intensifying.

The article lists the methods used by Western countries: denying the achievements of socialism, exaggerating the difficulties of some socialist countries; extolling the economic, political, and social organizational methods of capitalism; denying the leadership role of the Communist Party, and openly funding opposition groups.

The article states that imperialist reactionary forces, in order to achieve their goals, have employed various tactics: applying pressure, making threats, and offering enticing promises. The actions of capitalist countries fully demonstrate that they operate entirely according to their own ideological principles and class interests.

### 罗马尼亚《火花报》发表文章  揭露帝国主义对社会主义的进攻

1989-11-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗马尼亚《火花报》发表文章
    揭露帝国主义对社会主义的进攻
    新华社布加勒斯特11月14日电　罗马尼亚《火花报》今天发表文章说，当前，帝国主义反动集团正在向社会主义发动意识形态的进攻，处心积虑地企图否定已被历史证实了的社会主义制度的优越性。
    文章说，社会主义和资本主义两种截然对立的社会政治体制间的阶级斗争是当代世界的客观现实，它远未减弱或消失，甚至正在加剧。
    文章列举了西方国家使用的手法：否定社会主义的成就，肆意夸大一些社会主义国家的困难；大肆吹捧资本主义经济、政治和社会生活的组织管理办法；否定共产党的领导作用，公开资助反对派。
    文章说，帝国主义反动势力为了达到目的采取了种种手法：施加压力，进行威吓、胁迫，并许下一些动听的诺言，资本主义国家的所作所为充分说明它们完全按自己的意识形态准则和阶级利益行事。
