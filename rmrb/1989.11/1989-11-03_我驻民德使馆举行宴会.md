Title: The Chinese Embassy in East Germany Hosts a Banquet
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Page: 3

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the German Democratic Republic, at the request of Ambassador Zhang Daoke, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, Liu Changye, hosted a banquet at the embassy on the evening of November 1. East German Foreign Minister Oskar Fischer, State Secretary and First Deputy Foreign Minister Klaus Krolikowski, and other guests attended the banquet.

### 我驻民德使馆举行宴会

1989-11-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我驻民德使馆举行宴会
    为庆祝中国和民主德国建交40周年，受张大可大使的委托，中国驻民主德国大使馆临时代办刘昌业1日晚在大使馆举行宴会。民主德国外交部长菲舍尔，国务秘书、第一副外长克罗利科夫斯基等应邀出席了宴会。　（本报专电）
