Title: Qian Xuesen and Others' "Establishing Human Body Science" Published
Author:
Date: 1989-11-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th.

The first volume of "Establishing Human Body Science" authored by renowned Chinese scientist Qian Xuesen and others has been published by Sichuan Education Press. This book showcases the achievements of human body science research in China.

### 钱学森等著《创建人体科学》问世

1989-11-20
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    钱学森等著《创建人体科学》问世
    据新华社北京11月17日电　我国著名科学家钱学森等著的《创建人体科学》（第一辑），已由四川教育出版社出版。这本书集中展示了我国人体科学研究工作所取得的成果。
