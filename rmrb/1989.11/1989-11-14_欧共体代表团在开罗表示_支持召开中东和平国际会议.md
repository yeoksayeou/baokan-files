Title: The EEC Delegation in Cairo Expresses Support for Holding an International Conference on Middle East Peace
Author:
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 4

The European Economic Community delegation in Cairo has expressed support for holding an international conference on Middle East peace, including the participation of the Palestine Liberation Organization and other relevant parties.

French Foreign Minister Roland Dumas stated today that the European Economic Community supports the convening of an international conference on Middle East peace, which would include the Palestine Liberation Organization and other relevant parties.

Dumas said that the EEC delegation held a "fruitful" meeting with Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak this morning, discussing the latest developments in the Middle East peace process and the international situation.

The delegation, composed of representatives from France, Spain, and Ireland, arrived in Cairo late on November 11 for a two-day visit.

### 欧共体代表团在开罗表示  支持召开中东和平国际会议

1989-11-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    欧共体代表团在开罗表示
     支持召开中东和平国际会议
    据新华社开罗11月12日电　法国外交部长迪马今天在这里表示，欧洲经济共同体支持召开包括巴勒斯坦解放组织在内的有关各方参加的中东和平国际会议。
    迪马说，欧共体代表团今天上午与埃及总统穆巴拉克举行了“富有成果”的会谈，讨论了中东和平进程和国际形势的最新发展。
    由法国、西班牙和爱尔兰3国组成的代表团是11日晚抵达开罗作为期两天访问的。
