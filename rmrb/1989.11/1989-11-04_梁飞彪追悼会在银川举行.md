Title: Funeral of Liang Feibiao Held in Yinchuan
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan

The funeral of the renowned patriot Liang Feibiao, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region People's Congress, member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association (CDNCA), Chairman of the Ningxia CDNCA, and a member of the 7th National People's Congress, was held in Yinchuan on October 31. Liang Feibiao passed away in Yinchuan on October 21 due to cancer, at the age of 76.

Wreaths were sent by Yang Jingren, Qu Wu, Zhu Lirang, and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as the Party, government, and military leadership of Ningxia.

During his early years in Beijing, Liang Feibiao participated in the democratic and patriotic movements, promoting anti-Japanese and national salvation efforts. He served as the chairman of the "Ningxia Students' Association in Beijing" and was involved in progressive student organizations such as the "Anti-Imperialist United Front" and the famous "December 9th" patriotic movement. After the "July 7th Incident," he went to Shaanxi as the leader of the "Northwest Refugee Group" and established the "Ningxia Students' Association in Shaanxi." Later, he returned to Ningxia to continue promoting anti-Japanese efforts. After the founding of the People's Republic, Liang Feibiao dedicated himself to educational work for an extended period.

### 梁飞彪追悼会在银川举行

1989-11-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    梁飞彪追悼会在银川举行
    新华社银川电　知名的爱国人士、宁夏回族自治区人大常委会副主任、民革中央委员、民革宁夏区委主任委员、七届全国人大代表梁飞彪同志追悼会10月31日在银川举行。梁飞彪因患癌症，于10月21日在银川逝世，终年76岁。
    杨静仁、屈武、朱学范和全国人大常委会、宁夏党政军领导机关等送了花圈。
    梁飞彪早年在北京求学时就投入民主爱国运动，宣传抗日救国，并担任“宁夏留平学生会”主席，参加过进步学生组织“反帝大同盟”和著名的“一二·九”爱国运动。“七七事变”后，又以“西北流亡团”负责人的身份赴陕西，成立了“宁夏旅陕同学会”。后回宁夏继续宣传抗日。建国后，梁飞彪长期从事教育工作。
