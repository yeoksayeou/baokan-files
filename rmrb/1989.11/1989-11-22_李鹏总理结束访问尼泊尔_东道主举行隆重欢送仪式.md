Title: Premier Li Peng Concludes Visit to Nepal; Host Nation Holds Grand Farewell Ceremony
Author: Lǐ Shìxiāng, Yìn Gāolè
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 21st

Chinese Premier Li Peng concluded his three-day official friendly visit to Nepal today, departing by special plane.

At the airport, the host nation held a grand farewell ceremony for Li Peng. Around 9 a.m. local time, Li Peng and Nepalese Prime Minister Shreshta ascended the reviewing stand. The honor guard presented arms. The band played the national anthems of both countries. Five young girls dressed in traditional Nepalese attire presented floral garlands to Li Peng and his wife, Zhu Lin.

At the VIP gate, Li Peng and Zhu Lin bid farewell with handshakes to Shreshta, Chief Justice D.B. Singh, Chairman of the National Assembly Subedi, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers A.P. Singh, along with their wives. The airport fired 19 gun salutes to see off Li Peng.

Also present at the airport to see off Li Peng were King Birendra's Chief Military Secretary Pandey, Chief of Staff Rana, various ministers, and other high-ranking officials.

China's Ambassador to Nepal, Li Dezhao, the entire diplomatic staff of the embassy, Chinese technical personnel working in Nepal, and representatives of the Nepal-China Friendship Association were also at the airport to see off Li Peng. Young women from the embassy presented fresh flowers to Li Peng and his wife.

Nepal was the final stop on Li Peng's South Asian tour. Prior to this, he visited Pakistan and Bangladesh. Before departing from his hotel, the Suryodaya, Li Peng held a press conference where he stated that during his visits to the three countries, he was warmly received everywhere. This reflected the friendly sentiments of the peoples of the three countries towards the Chinese people.

Li Peng also mentioned that through discussions with the leaders of the three countries, mutual understanding was enhanced, and cooperation in economic, trade, technology, culture, and other fields between China and the three countries was strengthened.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin, and Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army He Qizong, who accompanied Li Peng on his visit, also departed by the same plane.

### 李鹏总理结束访问尼泊尔　东道主举行隆重欢送仪式
朱士骧　印高乐
1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    李鹏总理结束访问尼泊尔
　　东道主举行隆重欢送仪式
    新华社加德满都11月21日电　（记者朱士骧、印高乐）中国总理李鹏，在结束对尼泊尔的3天正式友好访问后，今天上午乘专机离开这里回国。
    在机场，东道主为李鹏举行了隆重的欢送仪式。9时许（当地时间），李鹏和尼泊尔首相什雷斯塔登上检阅台。仪仗队持枪行礼。乐队奏中尼两国国歌。5名身着尼泊尔民族服装的小姑娘向李鹏和夫人朱琳献了花环。
    在贵宾门前，李鹏和朱琳同什雷斯塔、大法官德·巴·辛格、全国评议会主席苏贝迪及国务会议常务委员会主席阿·普·辛格及他们的夫人握手告别。机场鸣放19响礼炮，欢送李鹏。
    今天到机场欢送李鹏的还有比兰德拉国王首席军事秘书潘迪、参谋长拉纳、各部大臣及其他高级官员。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使李德标、使馆全体外交官、中国在尼工作的技术人员及尼中友好组织的代表也到机场欢送李鹏。使馆女青年向李鹏夫妇献了鲜花。
    尼泊尔是李鹏这次南亚之行的最后一站。在此之前，他访问了巴基斯坦和孟加拉国。李鹏在启程前在他下榻的苏尔蒂旅馆举行的记者招待会上说，在这次3国访问中，他所到之处都受到热烈欢迎。这反映了3国人民对中国人民的友好感情。
    李鹏还说，通过与这3国政府首脑会谈，增进了彼此之间的了解，加强了中国对这3国之间的经济、贸易、技术、文化等领域的合作关系。
    陪同李鹏访问的外交部长钱其琛、对外经贸部长郑拓彬和中国人民解放军副总参谋长何其宗等也同机离开这里回国。
