Title: Soviet Quantum-2 Experimental Module Encounters Malfunction
Author:
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 4

The Soviet "Quantum-2" experimental module, launched into space on November 26, encountered a malfunction where one of its solar panels failed to fully open. To conserve energy, the ground control center has shut down several systems on the new module.

After the new module was launched into space, its orbit was found to be similar to the planned trajectory. However, one of the two solar panels on the module did not fully open. Despite multiple commands from the ground control center, the malfunction has not been resolved.

The report states that, in principle, the "Quantum-2" can still dock with the orbital station using just one solar panel. After docking, astronauts can address the malfunction while working in the open space. Currently, Soviet aerospace experts are continuing to analyze this unexpected incident and discuss countermeasures.

### 苏量子—2实验舱发生故障

1989-11-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏量子—2实验舱发生故障
    据塔斯社报道，二十六日被发射上天的苏联“量子—2”号实验舱上的一块太阳能电池板没能完全打开。为节省能源，地面指挥中心已关闭这个新实验舱的一系列装置。
    新实验舱被发射到宇宙空间后，其运行轨道与预计的轨道相近似，但它上面的两块太阳能电池板中的一块未能完全打开。尽管地面指挥中心多次发出指令，这一故障一直没有排除。
    报道说，在原则上只靠一个太阳能电池板，“量子—2”号也能与轨道站对接。对接后，宇航员可在到开放的宇宙空间中作业时排除故障。目前苏联宇航专家在继续分析这一意外事故，并商量对策。　（据新华社）
