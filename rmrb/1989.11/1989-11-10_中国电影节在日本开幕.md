Title: Chinese Film Festival Opens in Japan
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 4

The Chinese Film Festival opened in Japan recently, following the Japanese Film Week held in Xi'an, China, on October 12.

Tonight, the Japan Motion Picture Producers Association and the Chinese Film Festival Organizing Committee held a reception at the Tokyo Kaikan to celebrate the opening of the 13th Chinese Film Festival in Japan. A delegation of eight Chinese film representatives, led by Gao Honghu, director of the Guangxi Film Studio, attended the reception.

### 中国电影节在日本开幕
于青
1989-11-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中国电影节在日本开幕
    本报东京11月9日电　记者于青报道：继10月12日日本电影周在中国西安市举行之后，中国电影节近日在日本开幕。
    今晚，日本电影制作者联盟、中国电影节组织委员会在东京会馆举行酒会，庆祝1989年第13届中国电影节在日本开幕。以广西电影制片厂厂长高鸿鹄为首的中国电影代表团一行8人出席了酒会。
