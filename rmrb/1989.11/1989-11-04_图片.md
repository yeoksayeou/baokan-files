Title: Air China's Cabin Crew Training Center Conducts Standardized and Procedural Service Training
Author: Liú Bīn
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 5

    Air China's Cabin Crew Training Center has conducted standardized and procedural service training for its cabin crew members, with 94% of them achieving excellent results. The image shows an instructor evaluating the meal service procedures and standardized actions of first-class cabin crew members. Photographed by Liu Bin.
