Title: Presidents of Argentina, Brazil, and Uruguay Hold Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 4

Argentine President Menem, Brazilian President Sarney, and Uruguayan President Sanguinetti on the 11th expressed that the integration process of Argentina, Brazil, and Uruguay should be continued and accelerated.

President Sarney and President Sanguinetti made a six-hour short visit to Argentina on the 11th. After the meeting of the three presidents, they issued a joint statement saying that cooperation in political, economic, cultural, and social aspects should be strengthened, and the Latin American integration process should be actively promoted.

### 阿根廷巴西乌拉圭三国总统会晤

1989-11-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿根廷巴西乌拉圭三国总统会晤
    阿根廷总统梅内姆、巴西总统萨尔内和乌拉圭总统桑吉内蒂十一日表示，应继续并加速阿、巴、乌三国一体化进程。
    萨尔内总统和桑吉内蒂总统十一日对阿根廷进行六个小时的短暂访问。三位总统会晤后发表了联合公报说，应加强三方在政治、经济、文化和社会方面所进行的合作，并应积极推动拉美一体化进程。
