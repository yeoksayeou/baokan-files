Title: The Connecting Tunnel from Lianyungang to Sucheng Opens

Author: Wang Xunyuan

Date: 1989-11-30

Edition: 2

The longest highway tunnel in China, the Lianyungang-Sucheng Tunnel, was officially completed yesterday.

This tunnel connects Lianyungang Port to Sucheng, with a total length of 3,641 meters, a width of 8 meters, and a height of 8.3 meters. It features both a vehicle lane and a pedestrian lane. The opening of this tunnel integrates the port with Sucheng, resolving the issue of the narrow land area at Lianyungang Port.

### 连云港至宿城公路隧道贯通
王绪年
1989-11-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　连云港至宿城公路隧道贯通
    新华社南京11月28日电（通讯员王绪年）我国最长的公路隧道——连宿隧道昨天正式贯通。
    这条隧道从连云港口通向宿城，全长3641米，宽8米、高8.3米，设车行和人行双道。这条隧道的开通，把港口与宿城连为一体，解决了连云港口陆地狭窄的弊端。
