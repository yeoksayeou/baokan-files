Title: Tianjin Economic Development Zone Achieves Steady Economic Growth
Author: Liang Xiaqin
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 2

Reporter Liang Xiaqin reports from Tianjin:

This year, Tianjin Economic Development Zone has taken effective measures to overcome various unfavorable factors and has continued to maintain steady economic growth. From January to September, the zone's main economic indicators, export earnings, and the number of approved foreign-invested projects have all seen significant increases compared to the same period last year.

According to statistics, the total industrial output value of Tianjin Economic Development Zone for the first nine months reached 331 million yuan, a 33% increase over the same period last year. The zone realized a profit of 54 million yuan, a 25% increase over the same period last year. Among these, the output value of foreign-invested enterprises was 295 million yuan, a 38% increase over the same period last year. The zone's export earnings grew by 45% compared to the same period last year. Additionally, 25 new foreign-invested projects were approved, with a total investment of 66.76 million US dollars, a 95% increase over the same period last year. The proportion of foreign investment has also seen a significant increase.

### 天津开发区经济稳定增长
梁小琴
1989-11-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    天津开发区经济稳定增长
    本报天津电　记者梁小琴报道：今年以来，天津经济开发区采取有效措施，克服种种不利因素，继续保持了经济的稳步增长。1至9月份，开发区的主要经济指标、出口创汇额、吸收三资项目等均比去年同期有较大幅度增长。
    据统计，天津开发区前9个月工业总产值达3.31亿元，比去年同期增长33％；实现利润5400万元，比去年同期增长25％。其中，三资企业产值2.95亿元，比去年同期增长38％。开发区出口创汇比去年同期增长45％。新批准三资企业25个，项目总投资6676万美元，比去年同期增长95％，外商投资比例也有较大幅度提高。
