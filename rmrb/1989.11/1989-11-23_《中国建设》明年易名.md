Title: China Construction Magazine to Change Name Next Year
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Edition: 3

	China Construction Magazine to Change Name Next Year

	According to a report from our newspaper, starting from next January, the foreign language editions of China Construction Magazine will be renamed Today's China, and the Chinese edition will be renamed Modern China.

	China Construction Magazine is currently distributed in more than 140 countries and regions.

### 《中国建设》明年易名

1989-11-23
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　《中国建设》明年易名
    本报讯　明年元月起，《中国建设》杂志各外文版，将改名为《今日中国》，中文版改名为《现代中国》。
    《中国建设》已在一百四十多个国家和地区发行。
