Title: Chinese and Foreign Experts Gather in Beijing to Discuss China-Africa Economic Issues
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 1

Chinese and foreign experts gathered in Beijing today to discuss China-Africa economic issues. The "China-Africa Economic Reform and Adjustment Seminar," organized by the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences and supported by the United Nations Development Programme and the Ford Foundation of the United States, opened this morning in Beijing.

Nearly 30 representatives from African countries including Ivory Coast, Ethiopia, Kenya, Zambia, Ghana, Nigeria, Senegal, Tanzania, and Zimbabwe, as well as Chinese representatives, attended the economic seminar. Senior officials and representatives from the United Nations Development Programme, the United Nations Economic Commission for Africa, and the Ford Foundation of the United States also participated in the conference.

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian of the State Council attended the opening ceremony.

Ma Hong, Director-General of the State Council's Development Research Center, delivered a speech titled "China's Current Economic Development Strategy" at the opening session.

The first half of the conference will focus on discussing the experiences and lessons of economic development in China and Africa. The latter half will be divided into specialized discussions on topics such as "Efficiency of State-Owned Enterprises," "Price Issues," and "Human Resource Development." The seminar will conclude on November 24.

### 中外人士聚会北京　研讨中国—非洲经济

1989-11-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中外人士聚会北京
　　研讨中国—非洲经济
    本报北京11月21日讯　由中国社会科学院举办、联合国开发计划署和美国福特基金会支持的“中国—非洲经济改革与调整研讨会”今天上午在北京开幕。
    来自非洲科特迪瓦、埃塞俄比亚、肯尼亚、赞比亚、加纳、尼日利亚、塞内加尔、坦桑尼亚和津巴布韦的将近30名代表以及中国代表参加了这次经济研讨会。联合国开发计划署、联合国非洲经济委员会、美国福特基金会的高级官员和代表也参加了这个大会。
    中国国务院副总理吴学谦出席了今天的开幕式。
    国务院经济技术社会发展研究中心总干事马洪在开幕会上作了《中国现阶段的经济发展战略》的报告。
    会议的前半部分将集中讨论中国和非洲经济发展的经验与教训；后半部分将按“国营企业的效益”、“物价问题”、“人力资源开发”等专题分组讨论。研讨会将于11月24日闭幕。
