Title: China to Continue Providing Economic Aid to African Countries
Author: Liu Kaicheng
Date: 1989-11-28
Page: 4

People's Daily, United Nations, November 17th, Reported by Liu Kaicheng: China's Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Ambassador Li Luye, spoke at the UN General Assembly's plenary session on African economic issues on November 17th. He announced that as China's strength grows, it will gradually increase its economic aid to Africa.

Li Luye stated, "China has always been concerned about Africa's development and has provided assistance to the best of its ability. China's aid to developing countries has been significant."

Li Luye expressed, "We will continue to provide long-term interest-free loans to African countries within our capacity. If the relevant countries face difficulties when the loans are due, they can apply for an extension. China is a low-income developing country, and our economic relations with African countries are mutual assistance between poor friends. Under any circumstances, China will not demand repayment."

Li Luye pointed out that China will continue to strengthen economic and technological cooperation with African countries based on the principles of equality, mutual benefit, and common development.

The Assembly held a special discussion on November 17th on the "Alternative Framework for Structural Adjustment for Economic Recovery and Reform in Africa" adopted by the Economic Commission for Africa's Ministerial Conference in April this year.

At the end of the debate on November 17th, the Assembly passed a resolution with 137 votes in favor, 1 vote (the United States) against, urging the international community, including multilateral financial and development institutions, to use this "alternative framework" as a basis for "constructive dialogue and effective consultations."

### 我代表在联大发言时表示　中国将继续向非洲国家提供经援　联大通过一项关于非洲经济问题的决议
刘开宸
1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我代表在联大发言时表示
　　中国将继续向非洲国家提供经援
　　联大通过一项关于非洲经济问题的决议
    本报联合国电　记者刘开宸报道：中国常驻联合国代表李鹿野大使17日在联大全体会议审议非洲经济问题时发言，宣布中国随着国力的加强，会逐步增加对非洲的经援。
    李鹿野说，中国一直关注非洲的发展，提供了力所能及的援助，在中国对发展中国家的经援中占有重要地位。
    李鹿野表示，“我们将继续量力而行，向非洲国家提供长期无息贷款，贷款到期后，如果有关国家确有困难，可以延期偿还。我国是低收入的发展中国家，我们和非洲国家的经济关系是穷朋友之间的相互帮助。在任何情况下，中国都不会逼债。”
    李鹿野指出，中国还将根据平等互利、共同发展的原则，继续加强与非洲国家的经济与技术合作。
    大会17日专门讨论非洲经济委员会部长会议在今年4月通过的《非洲社会经济复苏和改革的结构调整方案的替代框架》。
    大会17日在辩论结束时，以137票赞成，1票（美国）反对，通过了一项决议，吁请国际社会、包括多边金融和发展机构，以此“替代框架”为基础，开展“建设性对话和富有成效的协商”。
