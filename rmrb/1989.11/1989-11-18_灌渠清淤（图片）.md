Title: Dredging Irrigation Canals
Author: Yang Peiqin
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 1

Farmers in Shandong Province's Huimin Region have raised funds to build water conservancy projects, organizing 120,000 laborers to launch a campaign to dredge the Yellow River irrigation canals. The image shows a dredging machine team from Liangcai Township, Binzhou City, at work dredging the irrigation canals.
