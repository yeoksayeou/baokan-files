Title: U.S. Guam Peripatetic Ambassador Visits China
Author: Liú Xiǎo
Date: 1989-11-07
Edition: 2

At the invitation of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Shen Jue Ren, Ms. Yú Lì Yún, heir to the Eli Lilly Company and Peripatetic Ambassador of Guam, U.S., recently paid a friendly visit to China.

During her visit, Ms. Yú attended the "Seminar on Economic and Trade Cooperation between China and the Asia-Pacific Region" and made constructive suggestions. In talks with Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuo Bin and Vice Minister Shen Jue Ren, both sides exchanged views on Sino-U.S. economic and trade cooperation.

Ms. Yú also met with Xinhua News Agency President Mu Qing, Sichuan Provincial Governor Zhang Hao Ruo, and officials from the State Forestry Administration, State Sports Commission, and State Pharmaceutical Administration, discussing matters of friendly cooperation.

### 美国关岛巡回大使访华
刘霄
1989-11-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    美国关岛巡回大使访华
    本报北京11月6日讯　记者刘霄报道：应经贸部副部长沈觉人的邀请，美国礼来公司继承人、美国关岛巡回大使余丽云女士近日对我国进行了友好访问。
    访问期间，余女士参加了“中国与亚太地区经济贸易合作研讨会”，并提出了积极的建议。在与经贸部部长郑拓彬、副部长沈觉人的会谈中，双方就中美经贸合作问题交换了意见。
    余女士还会见了新华社社长穆青、四川省省长张皓若，以及林业部、国家体委、国家医药管理总局负责人，探讨了有关友好合作事宜。
