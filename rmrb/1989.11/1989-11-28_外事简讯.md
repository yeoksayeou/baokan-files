Date: 1989-11-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

	▲ The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a reception on November 27 in Beijing to celebrate the 45th anniversary of Albania's liberation and people's revolutionary victory and the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Albania. Han Xu, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and Justin Papayorgi, Albanian Ambassador to China, delivered speeches at the reception. Attendees included Zhu Xun, Minister of the Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources, and Albanian diplomats and experts in Beijing.

	▲ Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, met with a delegation from the Palestinian trade unions on November 27 in Beijing.

	The delegation, consisting of two members led by Muhammad Badran, Deputy Secretary-General of the General Federation of Palestinian Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing on November 27 at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a friendly visit.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲中国人民对外友好协会11月27日在北京举行招待会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放和人民革命胜利45周年和中阿建交40周年。对外友协会长韩叙和阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使尤斯廷·帕帕约尔吉在招待会上发表了讲话。出席今晚招待会的有地质矿产部部长朱训和在京的阿尔巴尼亚外交官、专家等。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福27日在北京会见了巴勒斯坦工会代表团。
    由巴勒斯坦工人总联合会总书记助理穆罕默德·巴德兰一行2人组成的巴勒斯坦工会代表团，是应中华全国总工会的邀请于27日抵达北京进行友好访问的。　　（新华社）
