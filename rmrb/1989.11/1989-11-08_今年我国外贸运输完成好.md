Title: China's Foreign Trade Transportation Completed Well This Year
Author: Cai Guoyan
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 1

According to Xinhua News Agency Xiamen, November 7th, (Reporter Cai Guoyan) The reporter learned from the national port foreign trade transportation work meeting held yesterday in Xiamen City that in the first three quarters of this year, China's foreign trade transportation volume reached 139 million tons, completing 81% of the annual plan. Compared with the same period last year, sea transportation volume increased by nearly one and a half times, air transportation volume remained the same, and road transportation volume also increased.

### 今年我国外贸运输完成好
蔡国烟
1989-11-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    今年我国外贸运输完成好
    据新华社厦门十一月七日电  （记者蔡国烟）记者从昨天在厦门市召开的全国港口外贸运输工作会议上获悉，今年头三个季度，我国外贸运输总量达一点三九亿吨，完成了年计划的百分之八十一。与去年同期相比，海运量增长近一倍半，空运量持平，公路运输量也有所增加。
