Title: Sincere Friendship and a Successful Visit – Premier Li Peng's Tour of Three South Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 4

Premier Li Peng, at the invitation, visited Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal from November 14 to 21 for official friendly visits. This visit achieved the expected goals of enhancing friendship, deepening understanding, and expanding cooperation, and was a complete success. It was a visit of great significance and far-reaching influence. On November 16, Premier Li Peng planted a friendship tree at the top of Margalla Hills in Islamabad, Pakistan.

	New China News Agency Photographer Liu Shaoshan

On November 20, Premier Li Peng visited the famous Hanuman Dhoka Palace in Kathmandu, Nepal. The image shows Premier Li Peng waving to the welcoming crowd in the square.

	New China News Agency Photographer Liu Shaoshan

On November 18, the Mayor of Dhaka, Rahman, presented the key to the city of Dhaka to Premier Li Peng and his wife at a grand citizens' reception he held for them.

	New China News Agency Photographer Guo Zhanying

### 真诚的友情  成功的访问——李鹏总理南亚三国之行（图片）

1989-11-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　真诚的友情  成功的访问
　　——李鹏总理南亚三国之行
    李鹏总理应邀自11月14日至21日先后对巴基斯坦、孟加拉国和尼泊尔三国进行正式友好访问。这次访问达到了增进友谊、加深了解和扩大合作的预期目的，获得圆满成功。这是一次具有重大意义和深远影响的访问。十一月十六日，李鹏总理在巴基斯坦伊斯兰堡山顶公园栽植友谊树。
                                       新华社记者　刘少山摄
　　十一月二十日，李鹏总理在尼泊尔加德满都参观著名的哈努曼多卡老王宫。图为李鹏总理向广场上欢迎的人群挥手致意。
                                       新华社记者　刘少山摄
　　十一月十八日，孟加拉国达卡市长拉赫曼在他为李鹏总理和夫人举行的盛大市民招待会上向李鹏总理赠送达卡市的金钥匙。
                                   新华社记者　郭占英摄
