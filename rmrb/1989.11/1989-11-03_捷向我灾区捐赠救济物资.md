Title: Czechoslovakia Donates Relief Supplies to Chinese Disaster Areas
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 2

Czechoslovakia has donated relief supplies to the flood and earthquake-stricken areas in China. The handover ceremony for these supplies took place today at the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Hall. The 21 tons of relief supplies, donated by the National Front of Czechoslovakia through the CPPCC to the Chinese disaster-stricken people, include 3 tons of clothing and other textiles, and 18 tons of milk powder. The relief supplies have already arrived in full. The CPPCC has entrusted the Chinese Red Cross Society to distribute these supplies to the flood and earthquake-stricken areas in Zhejiang, Sichuan, and Shanxi provinces.

Vice Chairman of the CPPCC Qian Zhengying and visiting Czechoslovakian Foreign Minister Jaroslav Jarnik attended the handover ceremony.

### 捷向我灾区捐赠救济物资

1989-11-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    捷向我灾区捐赠救济物资
    新华社北京11月1日电　捷克斯洛伐克向中国水灾、地震灾区捐赠救济物资的移交仪式今天在全国政协礼堂举行。这批由捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线通过全国政协转赠中国灾区人民的救济物资共21吨，其中被服等纺织品3吨，奶粉18吨。救灾物资现已全部运到。全国政协委托中国红十字会把这批物资分送到浙江、四川、山西等水灾、地震灾区。
    全国政协副主席钱正英和正在中国访问的捷克斯洛伐克外长雅罗米尔·约翰内斯等出席了移交仪式。
