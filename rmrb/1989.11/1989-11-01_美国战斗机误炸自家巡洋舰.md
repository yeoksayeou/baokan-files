Title: U.S. Fighter Jet Accidentally Bombs Its Own Cruiser
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Edition: 3

A U.S. military aircraft on October 30th dropped a 500-pound bomb over the Indian Ocean, mistakenly hitting the U.S. Navy missile cruiser "Reeves" which was conducting exercises in the Indian Ocean. The incident resulted in five sailors being injured and a hole over a meter deep on the main deck. The fire caused by the bomb was quickly extinguished.

### 美国战斗机误炸自家巡洋舰

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国战斗机误炸自家巡洋舰
    一架美国军用飞机十月三十日在印度洋上空，误将一枚重五百磅的炸弹投到正在印度洋上演习的美国海军导弹巡洋舰“里夫斯”号上，造成舰上五名水兵受伤，并在主甲板上炸了一个一米多深的洞。炸弹引起的一场小火灾很快被扑灭。
                                （本报专电）
