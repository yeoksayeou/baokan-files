Title: China Food Week Opens in Luanda
Author:
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Luanda

The China Food Week, jointly organized by the Angolan Hotel Corporation and the China State Construction Engineering Corporation, officially opened on the evening of the 15th at the Luanda Hotel in Luanda, the capital of Angola.

Angola's Deputy Minister of Commerce, Silvestre, attended the opening ceremony accompanied by China's Ambassador to Angola, Hu Lipeng.

Chefs from the Beijing Hotel prepared a variety of Chinese dishes, including braised shrimp in oil, salted duck, and crispy fried chicken, along with various snacks for the guests.

During the opening ceremony, dancers from the Central National Song and Dance Troupe, who were visiting Angola, performed traditional Chinese dances for the guests.

### 中国食品周在罗安达开幕

1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中国食品周在罗安达开幕
    新华社罗安达电　由安哥拉酒店总公司和中国建筑工程总公司联合举办的中国食品周，15日晚在安哥拉首都罗安达旅游旅馆正式开幕。
    安哥拉贸易部副部长席尔维斯特在中国驻安哥拉大使胡立鹏陪同下出席了开幕式。
    来自北京民族饭店的厨师们为客人们准备了中国风味的油焖大虾、盐水鸭、香酥鸡等菜和各种小吃。
    在开幕式上，正在这里访问的中央民族歌舞团的舞蹈家们为来宾表演了民族舞蹈。
