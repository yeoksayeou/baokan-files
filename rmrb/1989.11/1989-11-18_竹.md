Title: Bamboo
Author: Wang Hong
Date: 1989-11-18
Page: 8

    Bamboo
    Wang Hong

    The tall and robust Yulong bamboo,
    The graceful and swaying Fengwei bamboo,
    The vibrant and colorful Biyu bamboo,
    The plump and round Fodubu bamboo,
    Clustering and spreading across mountains and valleys.

    The sturdy ones are used to build houses,
    The solid ones can be made into chairs and tables,
    Bamboo mats, bamboo boxes, bamboo tubes, bamboo bowls,
    Bamboo spoons...
    And there are bamboo flutes and bamboo pipes,
    Pouring out the heartfelt melodies of the Dai people.

    Each has its own aspirations,
    Each has its own destiny,
    Even if split into thin bamboo strips,
    They will be woven into baskets to store the harvest.

    Salute! To the family of bamboo in Xishuangbanna.

### 竹
王鸿
1989-11-18
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    竹
   王鸿
   挺拔茁壮的亚龙竹，
   袅娜婆娑的凤尾竹，
   色彩绚丽的碧玉竹，
   大腹便便的佛肚竹，
   丛丛簇簇，遍山漫谷。
   粗壮的，用来盖楼造屋，
   坚实的，可以制椅打桌，
   竹席、竹箱、竹筒、竹碗、
   竹勺……
   还有一支支竹童、竹囡，
   尽情倾吐傣家的心曲。
   各有各的抱负，
   各有各的归宿，
   即使被劈成一缕篾丝，
   也要编入箩筐贮存收获。
   敬礼！西双版纳竹的家族。
