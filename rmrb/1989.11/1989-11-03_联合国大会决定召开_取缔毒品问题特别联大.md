Title: United Nations General Assembly Decides to Hold Special Session on Drug Abuse
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 3

The 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly today unanimously adopted a resolution to convene a special session on drug abuse.

The draft resolution was jointly proposed by 121 countries, including China, reflecting the international community's widespread concern over the seriousness of the drug problem.

The resolution calls for the special session to be held at a high political level, to discuss "international cooperation in combating the illegal production, supply, demand, trafficking, and distribution of drugs, in order to expand the scope of this cooperation and improve its efficiency."

The Assembly requested the United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar to make the necessary administrative arrangements for convening this special session. The specific date for the special session will be determined later.

### 联合国大会决定召开  取缔毒品问题特别联大
刘开宸
1989-11-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国大会决定召开
    取缔毒品问题特别联大
    本报联合国11月1日电  记者刘开宸报道：第44届联合国大会今天一致通过一项决议，决定举行关于取缔毒品问题的特别联大。
    此项决议草案是由包括中国在内的121国共同提出的，表明国际社会对毒品问题的严重性普遍关切。
    决议要求该特别联大是高政治级别的，将审议“国际合作取缔毒品的非法生产、供应、需求、贩运和分销问题，以期扩大此项合作范围并提高效率”。
    大会请联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔为召开此次特别联大作出必要的行政安排。至于召开特别联大的具体日期待以后确定。
