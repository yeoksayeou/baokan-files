Title: U.S. Congress Approves Aid Bill for Poland and Hungary
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Page: 4

The U.S. Congress on the 19th approved a bill to provide $938 million in aid to Poland and Hungary, aiming to promote the development of free-market economies in these two Eastern European countries.

According to the bill, the U.S. will provide $852 million to Poland and $86 million to Hungary.

President Bush is expected to sign the bill. This aid package is double the amount initially proposed by Bush.

### 美国会批准援助波匈议案

1989-11-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国会批准援助波匈议案
    美国国会十九日批准了一项向波兰和匈牙利提供九点三八亿美元援助的议案，以促使这两个东欧国家发展自由市场经济。
    根据该项议案，美国将向波兰提供八点五二亿美元，向匈牙利提供零点八六亿美元。
    布什总统预料将签署这项议案。这笔援助比布什提出的援助数额增加了一倍。
