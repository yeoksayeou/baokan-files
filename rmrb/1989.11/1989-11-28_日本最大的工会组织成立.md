Title: Japan's Largest Trade Union Organization Established
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

Japan's largest trade union organization—the Japanese Trade Union Confederation (referred to as the new federation) was officially established. The chairman of the All-Japan Telecommunications Union, Yamagishi Akira, was elected as the first chairman.

The founding ceremony of the Japanese Trade Union Confederation was held in the afternoon of the 21st in Tokyo. The new federation is composed of the National Public Service Union and the Japanese Private Sector Trade Union Confederation (referred to as the federation). The new federation includes 78 industry trade union organizations with approximately 8 million members.

### 日本最大的工会组织成立

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本最大的工会组织成立
    日本最大的工会组织——日本工会总联合会（简称新联合）宣告成立，全日本电通工会委员长山岸章当选为首任委员长。
    日本工会总联合会成立大会于二十一日下午在东京举行。新联合是由政府雇员工会和全日本民间工会联合会（简称联合）联合组成的。新联合包括七十八个行业工会组织，有会员约八百万。
                                      （据新华社）
