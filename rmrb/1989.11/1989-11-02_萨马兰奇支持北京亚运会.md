Title: Samaranch Supports Beijing Asian Games
Author:
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 4

The International Olympic Committee fully supports the 11th Asian Games to be held in Beijing next year and believes that the games will definitely be a success. This was the attitude expressed by IOC President Juan Antonio Samaranch at a press conference held here today.

He also told Wan Siquan, Secretary-General of the 11th Asian Games Organizing Committee, and Wei Jianmin, Secretary-General of the Chinese Olympic Committee, who were invited to attend the opening ceremony of the Islamic Friendship and Peace Games, that the IOC is ready to provide various kinds of assistance to the 11th Asian Games at any time.

### 萨马兰奇支持北京亚运会

1989-11-02
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    萨马兰奇支持北京亚运会
    新华社科威特10月31日电　“国际奥委会全力支持明年在北京举行的第十一届亚洲运动会，并相信这次运动会一定会取得成功。”这是国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇今天在此间举行的记者招待会上表明这一态度的。
    他还对应邀前来参加伊斯兰友好和平运动会开幕式的第十一届亚运会组委会秘书长万嗣铨和中国奥委会秘书长魏纪中说，国际奥委会准备随时为第十一届亚运会提供各种帮助。
