Title: The Birth of "Children's World"
Author: Wei Guang
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 3

A magazine titled "Children's World," primarily aimed at primary school students and also catering to older preschool children, was published at the end of October. The renowned writer Bingxin penned the title for it. This magazine is published by the China Peace Publishing House.

### 《孩子天地》问世
玮光
1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　《孩子天地》问世
    本报讯　一份以初小学生为主要对象，兼顾较大的学龄前儿童的杂志《孩子天地》，在十月底问世。冰心老人为它题写了刊头。这份杂志由中国和平出版社主办。　（玮光）
