Title: Malaysia to Host the Next Asian Athletics Championships
Author: Huang Ping
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 13th

The ninth Asian Athletics Championships will be held in November or December 1991 in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia. This decision was made today at the conclusion of the Asian Amateur Athletics Association's representative conference held here.

Both Malaysia and South Korea expressed their interest in hosting the event. After a vote by the representatives, Malaysia secured the hosting rights with fifteen votes in favor and twelve against.

The conference also decided that the Asian Amateur Athletics Association's council will elect one vice-chairman and two council members at the next conference.

### 马来西亚将主办下届亚洲田径赛
黄平
1989-11-15
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    马来西亚将主办下届亚洲田径赛
    新华社新德里十一月十三日电 （记者黄平）第九届亚洲田径锦标赛将于一九九一年十一月或十二月间在马来西亚首都吉隆坡举行，这是今天在这里结束的亚洲业余田径联合会代表大会做出的决定。
    马来西亚和南朝鲜都表示有意主办这一赛事，经过代表投票，马来西亚以十五票赞成、十二票反对获得主办权。
    这次会议还决定亚洲业余田联理事会将在下届大会上增选一位副主席和两位理事。
