Title: Soviet Film Week Opens in Beijing
Author: Yuán Xī
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 4

To celebrate the 72nd anniversary of the October Revolution, the Soviet Film Week opened today in the Beijing Nationalities Culture Palace. Vice Minister of Radio, Film, and Television Chen Haosu, Soviet Ambassador to China Troyanovskiy, and members of the Soviet Film Delegation attended the opening ceremony.

The Soviet Film Week will be held in Beijing, Xi'an, Taiyuan, and Guiyang from today onwards, screening films such as "The Forgotten Melody of the Flute," "The Female Political Commissar," "The Film Tragedy," and "Good Things Never Come in Pairs."

### 苏联电影周在京开幕
袁晞
1989-11-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    苏联电影周在京开幕
    本报北京11月7日讯　为庆祝十月革命72周年，苏联电影周开幕式今天下午在北京民族文化宫举行。广播电影电视部副部长陈昊苏，苏联驻华大使特罗扬诺夫斯基和苏联电影代表团成员出席了开幕式。
    这次苏联电影周从今天起依次在北京、西安、太原、贵阳4城市举办，放映《被遗忘的长笛曲》、《女政委》、《电影悲欢曲》和《好事不成双》等影片。　（袁晞）
