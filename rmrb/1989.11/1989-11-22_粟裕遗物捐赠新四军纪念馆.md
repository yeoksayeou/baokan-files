Title: General Su Yu's Relics Donated to the New Fourth Army Memorial Hall
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 3

General Su Yu's relics have been donated to the New Fourth Army Re-establishment Headquarters Memorial Hall in Yancheng City, Jiangsu Province.

These items include a magnifying glass, leather bag, Parker pen, manuscripts, and a map book that Su Yu used during the war years. Some of the items were seized from Japanese invaders during battles in the Suzhou region.

### 粟裕遗物捐赠新四军纪念馆

1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　粟裕遗物捐赠新四军纪念馆
    新华社南京十一月二十一日电　粟裕将军一批遗物捐赠给江苏盐城市新四军重建军部纪念馆。
    这些物品包括粟裕同志在战争年代使用过的放大镜、皮包、派克笔、文稿、地图册等。有的物品是粟裕将军在苏中地区战斗中从日本侵略军手里缴获的。
