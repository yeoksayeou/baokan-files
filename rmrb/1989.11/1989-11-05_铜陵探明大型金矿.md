Title: A Large Gold Mine Discovered in Tongling
Author: Gao Shixi
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 2

    A large gold deposit of the "Huangma-style" has been discovered in Tongling City, Anhui Province. This discovery marks the end of the historical belief that Tongling has copper but no gold.

    The gold deposit, found by the East China Metallurgical Geological Team 812 of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry on the periphery and outskirts of the Tongguanshan Copper Mine, stretches over 1,150 meters in length, with a thickness of 4 to 6 meters. The average gold content is about 6 grams per ton, with some areas containing over 100 grams per ton. Additionally, the silver content reaches 30 grams per ton.

### 铜陵探明大型金矿
高世喜
1989-11-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    铜陵探明大型金矿
    本报讯　安徽省最近在铜陵市境内找到大型“黄马式”金矿床。这一发现，结束了铜陵只有铜没有金的历史。
    由冶金工业部华东冶金地质812队在铜官山铜矿的边部及外围找到的这一金矿床，矿体沿走向长1150多米，厚度为4至6米，平均金含量达每吨6克左右，高的达100多克。同时，银含量达30克／吨。　　
　　（高世喜）
