Title: Autumn in a Small Town
Author: Yú Shūhéng
Date: 1989-11-16
Edition: 8

A cool breeze scatters
A few fallen leaves,
But it can't blow away
The skirts full of flowers on the streets.
A light rain falls,
The autumn colors freshen,
Washing away the dawn, brightening the dusk;
Maple leaves and chrysanthemums
Adorn the small town with gold and silver.

The small town's spirit is ever-renewing,
Inside the homes, they love the local dialect,
On the many balconies,
A small cage of "childlike hearts" hangs,
Cicadas—cicadas—
Sing the town and the countryside closer.

In this autumn,
There is also a brave seaside,
The seawater cools down,
But the physique of a nation
Is heating up,
At this time, the surfers,
Are the true masters of the sea.

Autumn comes to the small town, no one notices,
Only feeling that day and night
Time is unevenly divided,
People use fast-forward
To smooth out the rhythm of life,
Wheels, machines, numbers,
All rush towards the peak of the target.

In the era of reform,
There are poetic eyes and artistic souls,
An essay on "Autumn Colors"
Becomes "Spring in the Small Town"....

### 小城秋
于书恒
1989-11-16
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    小城秋
    于书恒
    一阵凉风，吹散了
    几片落叶，
    可没能刮走
    满街的花裙。
    洒一场小雨，
    秋色更嫩，
    洗净了黎明，擦亮了黄
     昏；
    枫叶、菊花
    给小城披金戴银。
    小城的兴致不断更新，
    户里爱听乡音，
    偌多的阳台上，
    吊一小笼“童心”，
    蝈蝈——蝈蝈——
    把城乡唱近。
    这里的秋季，
    还有个勇敢的海滨，
    海水凉了，
    一个民族的体质
    却在升温，
    这时的击浪者，
    才是大海的主人。
    秋临小城，谁也没留心，
    只感觉昼与夜
    时间分得不匀，
    人们用快镜头
    把生活节奏理顺，
    车轮、机床、数字呵，
    都往指标的尖上奔。
    改革的年代，
    才有诗眼画魂，
    一篇《秋色赋》，
    变成《小城春》……
