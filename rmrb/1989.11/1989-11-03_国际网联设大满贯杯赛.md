Title: International Tennis Federation Establishes Grand Slam Cup
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 4

The International Tennis Federation has decided to establish the Grand Slam Cup, with the champion receiving a prize of 2 million US dollars. The first competition will be held next December in the Federal Republic of Germany, following the conclusion of the four major tennis tournaments.

The newly established Grand Slam Cup is recognized as an official competition by the International Tennis Federation. Participants will include the top 8 ranked players in the world and two additional players, totaling 10 players.

### 国际网联设大满贯杯赛
于青
1989-11-03
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    国际网联设大满贯杯赛
    本报东京10月31日电　记者于青报道：国际网球联盟决定新设大满贯杯赛，冠军获得者的奖金为200万美元。首届比赛定于明年四大网球赛结束后的12月间在联邦德国举行。
    新设的大满贯杯赛作为国际网联承认的正式比赛，参加选手只包括比赛世界排位前8名的选手和另外两名，共10名选手。
