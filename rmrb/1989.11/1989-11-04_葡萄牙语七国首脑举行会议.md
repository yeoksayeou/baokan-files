Title: Heads of State from Portuguese-Speaking Countries Hold Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 3

The heads of state and government leaders from the seven Portuguese-speaking countries held a meeting in São Luís, the capital of Maranhão state in northeastern Brazil, from November 1 to 2. This was the first summit of Portuguese-speaking countries. During the two-day meeting, the leaders reached a consensus to establish the Community of Portuguese Language Countries, aimed at strengthening cultural cooperation and promoting talent exchange. The presidents or prime ministers of Brazil, Portugal, Mozambique, Guinea-Bissau, São Tomé and Príncipe, Cape Verde, and Angola attended the meeting.

The meeting was initiated by the Brazilian government.

### 葡萄牙语七国首脑举行会议

1989-11-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    葡萄牙语七国首脑举行会议
    讲葡萄牙语的七个国家元首和政府首脑十一月一日至二日在巴西东北部马拉尼昂州州府圣路易斯城举行了会议，这是葡语国家第一次举行的首脑会议。两天会议期间，各国领导人就业成立国际葡萄牙语协会达成了一致协议，其宗旨在于加强各国文化合作、促进人才交流。巴西、葡萄牙、莫桑比克、几内亚比绍、圣多美和普林西比、佛得角和安哥拉的总统或总理出席了会议。
    这次会议是由巴西政府倡议召开的。　（本报专电）
