Title: Chinese Military Delegation Visits East Germany
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Edition: 3

    East German Defense Minister Kessler met with Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xu Xin, in Berlin on October 27, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

    On the evening of October 23, General Fritz Streletz, Chief of the General Staff of the National People's Army of East Germany, hosted a banquet for General Xu Xin and his entourage. On October 24, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Xu Xin held talks with General Streletz, Chief of the General Staff.

### 我军事代表团访民德

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我军事代表团访民德
    民主德国国防部长克斯勒10月27日在柏林会见了中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐信，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    10月23日晚，民主德国国家人民军总参谋长弗里茨·施特雷勒茨上将设宴招待徐信上将一行。24日，徐信副总参谋长同施特雷勒茨总参谋长举行了会谈。
