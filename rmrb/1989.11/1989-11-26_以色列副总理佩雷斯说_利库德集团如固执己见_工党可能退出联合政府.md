Title: Israeli Deputy Prime Minister Peres Says
Author:
Date: 1989-11-26
Edition: 4

Israeli Deputy Prime Minister Peres said today that if Prime Minister Shamir's Likud Party continues to insist on its position on the Middle East peace process, the Labor Party he leads may withdraw from the coalition government.

According to reports, since last year when the Likud Party and the Labor Party formed a coalition government, the two parties have had differences over the Palestinian issue.

The Labor Party believes that part of the occupied territories should be withdrawn from.

The Likud Party, however, opposes giving up any inch of land occupied during the 1967 Middle East War.

### 以色列副总理佩雷斯说　利库德集团如固执己见　工党可能退出联合政府

1989-11-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　以色列副总理佩雷斯说
　　利库德集团如固执己见
　　工党可能退出联合政府
    新华社开罗11月24日电　据以色列电台报道，以色列副总理佩雷斯今天说，如果沙米尔总理领导的利库德集团在中东和平进程问题上继续固执己见，那么他所领导的工党可能退出联合政府。
    据报道，自去年利库德集团和工党组成联合政府以来，两党在巴勒斯坦问题上一直存在分歧。
    工党认为，应该退出一部分被占领土。
    利库德集团则反对放弃1967年中东战争中占领的每一寸土地。
