Title: Hong Kong Plans to Spend Billions to Address Sewage Issues
Author:
Date: 1989-11-05
Edition: 2

Hong Kong government plans to spend 14.26 billion Hong Kong dollars over the next ten years to address the city's sewage problems.

The plan includes the construction of several deep underground tunnels and other facilities to collect and treat sewage from urban areas. The treated sewage will then be discharged into the sea through a 30-kilometer-long underwater pipeline, far from the coast of southern Hong Kong. Additionally, the government will strengthen the existing sewage collection systems in certain areas. The Hong Kong government hopes to cooperate with mainland China to carry out this project.

### 计划动用巨资  香港处置污水

1989-11-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    计划动用巨资  香港处置污水
    新华社香港十一月四日电  香港政府计划在未来十年动用一百四十二点六亿港元处置香港的污水问题。
    这项计划包括修建一些深地下隧道及其它设施，把市区的污水收集后加以处理，然后利用一条三十公里长的海底排水管，排放在香港南面远离海岸的水域，同时加强个别污水收集区的现有污水渠系统。港府希望与中国内地合作开展这个项目。
