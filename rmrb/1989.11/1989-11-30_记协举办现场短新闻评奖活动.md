Title: The Journalists Association Holds an On-Site Short News Award Event
Author: Wáng Xiǎowěi Sūn Zhèngyī
Date: 1989-11-30
Edition: 3

The Journalists Association recently issued a notice to hold an "On-Site Short News" award event for some news organizations in the capital. The aim is to promote and encourage journalists to write more and better firsthand news reports from the scene, thereby enhancing the effectiveness of propaganda and reporting.

This award event is commissioned by the Central Propaganda Department of the Communist Party of China and organized by the Journalists Association, with the assistance of the Beijing Zhang Guang 101 Hair Regeneration Essence Factory.

The participating organizations are 22 news units in the capital: Xinhua News Agency, People's Daily, Economic Daily, Guangming Daily, Liberation Army Daily, China News Service, China Daily, Workers' Daily, Farmers' Daily, China Youth Daily, China Women's Daily, Legal Daily, Science and Technology Daily, China Education Daily, News and Publishing Daily, Central People's Radio Station, Central Television Station, China International Radio Station, Beijing Daily, Beijing Evening News, Beijing People's Radio Station, and Beijing Television Station.

From October 1, 1989, to the end of January 1990, outstanding on-site short news works published or broadcasted by the above news units can participate in the selection. For radio and television stations, on-site oral reporting is encouraged. The word limit for newspapers is 1000 characters; the time limit for radio broadcasts is 5 minutes; and the time limit for television broadcasts is 3 minutes.

### 记协举办现场短新闻评奖活动
王小伟　孙正一
1989-11-30
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　记协举办现场短新闻评奖活动
    本报讯　中国记协近日发出举办首都部分新闻单位“现场短新闻”评奖活动通知，以提倡和鼓励广大新闻工作者采写更多更好发生在现场的第一手新闻作品，增强宣传报道的效果。
    这次评奖活动，是受中共中央宣传部的委托，由中国记协主办，北京章光101毛发再生精总厂协助。
    参评单位为首都22家新闻单位，它们是：新华社、人民日报、经济日报、光明日报、解放军报、中国新闻社、中国日报、工人日报、农民日报、中国青年报、中国妇女报、法制日报、科技日报、中国教育报、新闻出版报、中央人民广播电台、中央电视台、中国国际广播电台、北京日报、北京晚报、北京人民广播电台、北京电视台。
    1989年10月1日至1990年1月底，上述新闻单位刊播的优秀现场短新闻作品均可参加评选。对电台、电视台的作品提倡现场口头报道。报纸的文字限制在1000字以内；广播的时间限制在5分钟以内；电视的时间限制在3分钟以内。
                        （王小伟　孙正一）
