Title: Representatives of Both Sides in Nicaragua Resume Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 4

The negotiations between the Nicaraguan government and the anti-government armed forces, which were suspended on the 15th, resumed on the 16th at the headquarters of the Organization of American States.

On the 15th, the representatives of the anti-government armed forces proposed changing the venue of the talks to Guatemala and requested a postponement of the meeting. They then withdrew from the negotiations.

According to the chief negotiator for the Nicaraguan government, Tinoco, the representatives of the anti-government armed forces changed their minds on the 16th and agreed to continue the talks.

### 尼加拉瓜两方的代表恢复谈判

1989-11-18
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    尼加拉瓜两方的代表恢复谈判
    十五日中断的尼加拉瓜政府同反政府武装关于结束尼内战的谈判十六日在美洲国家组织总部重新举行。
    尼反政府武装代表十五日提出将会谈的地点改在危地马拉并要求会议延期举行。随后他们便退出了合谈。
    据尼政府首席谈判代表蒂诺科说，反政府武装代表十六日又改变了主意，同意继续举行谈判。
