Title: Nicaraguan Government and Anti-Government Forces Agree to Talks
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-11-09
Page: 3

People's Daily, United Nations, November 7th. Reporter Liu Kai Chen reports: According to the spokesperson for the United Nations, Giuliani, today it was announced that the Nicaraguan government and anti-government forces have accepted the invitation from the International Support and Verification Commission to hold direct talks.

The two sides agreed that the talks will be held on the 9th and 10th of this month at the United Nations headquarters. The discussions will focus on the issue of resuming the ceasefire. The Nicaraguan government has also requested that the issue of demobilizing the anti-government forces be discussed.

### 尼政府与反政府武装同意会谈
刘开宸
1989-11-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    尼政府与反政府武装同意会谈
    本报联合国11月7日电　记者刘开宸报道：据联合国发言人朱利亚尼今天宣布，尼加拉瓜政府和反政府武装已接受国际支持与检查委员会关于尼双方举行直接会谈的邀请。
    尼双方同意此会谈于本月9、10两日在联合国总部举行。双方将讨论恢复停火问题。尼政府还要求讨论遣散尼反政府武装人员问题。
