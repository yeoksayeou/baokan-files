Title: Li Tieying to Visit Four African Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced at today's press conference that at the invitation of the governments of Chad, the Central African Republic, Congo, and Zaire, State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieying will lead a Chinese government delegation to visit these four countries starting from November 15 for a friendly visit.

### 李铁映将出访非洲四国

1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    李铁映将出访非洲四国
    新华社北京11月9日电　外交部发言人在今天的新闻发布会上宣布，应乍得、中非、刚果和扎伊尔四国政府的邀请，国务委员兼国家教育委员会主任李铁映将率领中国政府代表团于11月15日起对上述四国进行友好访问。
