Title: The Brand of Love
Author: ZHU Genhua
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 7

	The Brand of Love
		·ZHU Genhua·

	In the vast expanse of the universe, there is a star that shines brightly in the night sky. This star is not just a celestial body; it is a symbol of love, a beacon of hope. It is the love that binds us together, the love that transcends time and space.

	Love is a powerful force that can overcome any obstacle. It is the force that drives us to strive for a better world, a world where peace and harmony prevail. Love is the foundation of our society, the cornerstone of our civilization.

	In the face of adversity, love is the light that guides us through the darkness. It is the strength that sustains us, the courage that empowers us. Love is the bond that unites us, the force that binds us together.

	As we journey through life, let us remember the brand of love. Let us cherish the love that we have for one another, and let us strive to create a world where love and compassion reign supreme. Let us be the stars that shine brightly in the night sky, guiding others through the darkness and leading them to a brighter future.
