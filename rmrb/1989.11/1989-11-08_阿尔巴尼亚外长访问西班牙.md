Title: Albanian Foreign Minister Visits Spain
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 3

At the invitation of Spanish Foreign Minister Francisco Fernández Ordóñez, Albanian Foreign Minister Reiz Malile paid an official friendly visit to Spain from November 1 to 4.

He was the highest-ranking Albanian official to visit Spain since the two countries established diplomatic relations in September 1986.

During the visit, Malile was received by King Juan Carlos I of Spain and exchanged views with the Spanish Foreign Minister on the possibilities and ways to expand relations and cooperation between the two countries. The two sides signed a cultural cooperation agreement between the governments of the two countries.

### 阿尔巴尼亚外长访问西班牙

1989-11-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚外长访问西班牙
    应西班牙外交大臣奥多涅斯的邀请，阿尔巴尼亚外长马利列于十一月一日至四日对西班牙进行了正式友好访问。
    他是一九八六年九月阿西两国建交以来到西班牙访问的最高级阿尔巴尼亚官员。
    访问期间，马利列受到西班牙国王卡洛斯一世的接见，并同西外长就扩大两国关系和合作的可能与途径交换了看法。双方签署了两国政府文化合作协定。
