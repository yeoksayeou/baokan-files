Title: Cuban Foreign Minister Visits Albania
Author:
Date: 1989-11-12
Page: 4

Albania and Cuba signed the Albania-Cuba Economic, Industrial and Technological Cooperation Agreement, the Albania-Cuba Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and the Albania-Cuba 1990 Barter and Payment Agreement in Tirana on the 9th.

The Cuban Foreign Minister Isidro Malmierca and the Albanian Foreign Minister Marille signed the agreements on behalf of their respective governments on the 8th, upon Malmierca's arrival in Albania for a visit.

Malmierca is the first Cuban foreign minister to visit Albania since the two countries established diplomatic relations in 1960.

### 古巴外长访问阿尔巴尼亚

1989-11-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    古巴外长访问阿尔巴尼亚
    阿尔巴尼亚与古巴九日在地拉那签署了阿古经济、工业和技术合作协定、阿古文化合作协定和阿古一九九○年换货和付款议定书。
    八日抵阿访问的古巴外长伊希多罗·马尔米耶卡和阿外长马利列分别代表本国政府在协议上签了字。
    马尔米耶卡是阿古一九六○年建交以来第一位访阿的古巴外长。
