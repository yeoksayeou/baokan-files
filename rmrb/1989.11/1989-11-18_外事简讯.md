Foreign Affairs Brief

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hab Comrade Xiping met with a delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Gangwon Province, led by Vice Chairman Li Qingri of the Administrative Economic Guidance Commission, on November 17th at the Great Hall of the People. The delegation from the DPRK arrived in China on November 4th at the invitation of Jiangsu Province, which has established friendly relations with Gangwon Province. They visited Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Guilin, and will return home tomorrow.

	State Councilor and Minister of National Defense Qinxijwei hosted a dinner and met with a delegation from the Cameroonian Armed Forces, led by General Samang, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of November 17th. The delegation will visit Shijiazhuang on November 18th and then proceed to Xiamen, Guangzhou, and Shanghai for further visits. The delegation is scheduled to return home on November 24th.

	Minister of Commerce Hu Ping hosted a dinner and met with a delegation from the Soviet Union's Ministry of Commerce, led by Minister of Commerce K. Z. Dzelekh, at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of November 17th. The Soviet commercial delegation arrived in Beijing in the afternoon of November 17th and will stay in China for a five-day visit.

	The China National Machinery Import and Export Corporation and the Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources of Bangladesh signed a memorandum of understanding on the cooperation in building a 210,000-kilowatt power plant in Chittagong at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the afternoon of November 17th.

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Rong Yiren met with a delegation from the American University Alumni Enterprise Association in the Great Hall of the People on the morning of November 16th. This delegation was invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit China.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋17日在人民大会堂会见了由行政经济指导委员会副委员长李清日率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国江原道友好代表团。朝鲜客人是应同江原道建立了友好关系的江苏省的邀请于11月4日来中国访问的。他们访问了北京、天津、南京、上海和桂林，并将于明天回国。
    ▲国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟17日晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了由总参谋长赛芒格少将率领的喀麦隆武装部队代表团。代表团18日将访问石家庄，然后前往厦门、广州和上海进行参观访问。代表团定于24日回国。
    ▲商业部部长胡平17日晚在人民大会堂会见并宴请了由商业部长康·齐·杰列赫率领的苏联商业代表团。苏联商业代表团于17日下午到达北京，将对中国进行为期5天的访问。
    ▲中国机械设备进出口总公司同孟加拉国能源矿产部17日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆签署了关于在孟加拉国合作建设吉大港21万千瓦机组电厂的协议备忘录。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长荣毅仁16日上午在人民大会堂会见了美国大学校友企业家协会代表团。这次该协会是应中华全国青年联合会的邀请组团来华访问的。
          （据新华社）
