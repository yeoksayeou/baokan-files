Title: Closing of the Wang Shaotang Art Symposium
Author: Zhang Shiying
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 3

To commemorate the 100th birthday of our late outstanding Yangzhou storytelling artist Wang Shaotang, nearly 100 renowned figures from the literary and artistic circles across the country gathered in Nanjing and Yangzhou from November 12 to 14 to hold the Wang Shaotang Art Symposium.

Wang Shaotang was born into a family of storytellers. He learned from various masters, boldly innovated, and gradually formed a unique artistic style.

Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province Yang Yongyi, renowned figures from the literary and artistic circles such as Luo Yang, Wu Zongxi, Yuan Kuocheng, Liu Lanfang, Wang Xiongfei, and relatives of Wang Shaotang attended the meeting.

### 王少堂艺术研讨会闭会
张世英
1989-11-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　王少堂艺术研讨会闭会
    本报讯　为纪念我国已故杰出的扬州评话表演艺术家王少堂百岁诞辰，来自全国各地的文艺界知名人士近百人，十一月十二日至十四日，分别在南京、扬州举行王少堂艺术研讨会。
    王少堂出身于说书世家。他博采众长，锐意改革创新，逐步形成独特的艺术流派。
    江苏省副省长杨泳沂、文艺界知名人士骆玉笙、罗扬、吴宗锡、袁阔成、刘兰芳、汪雄飞及王少堂亲属参加了会议。（张世英）
