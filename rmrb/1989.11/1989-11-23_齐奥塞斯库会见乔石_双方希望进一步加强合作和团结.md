Author: Zhou Rongzi
Date: 1989-11-23
Edition: 4

Ceausescu Meets with Qiao Shi

Both Sides Hope to Further Strengthen Cooperation and Unity

	BEIJING, Nov. 21 (Xinhua) -- Nicolae Ceausescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party and President of the Republic, met with Qiao Shi, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, who came to attend the 14th Congress of the RCP, at the Republic Palace on Nov. 21. They had a friendly and cordial talk.

	Qiao Shi conveyed the warm greetings from Jiang Zemin, Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, and Li Xiannian. Ceausescu expressed his thanks and asked Qiao Shi to convey his greetings and best wishes to the Chinese leaders.

	Ceausescu said in the meeting that he had made a comprehensive exposition on the situation in Romania and the work to be done in the future in his report to the 14th Congress of the RCP.

	Qiao Shi extended warm congratulations on the successful convening of the 14th Congress of the RCP. He wished the Romanian people continued progress along the socialist road and greater achievements. Both sides expressed the desire to further strengthen the friendly cooperation and unity between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples.

	Chinese Ambassador to Romania Wang Jinqing was present at the meeting.

### 齐奥塞斯库会见乔石　双方希望进一步加强合作和团结
周荣子
1989-11-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　齐奥塞斯库会见乔石
　　双方希望进一步加强合作和团结
    新华社布加勒斯特11月21日电　（记者周荣子）罗共总书记、共和国总统齐奥塞斯库21日在共和国宫会见了前来出席罗共14大的中共中央政治局常委、书记处书记乔石，同他进行了亲切、友好的谈话。
    乔石向齐奥塞斯库转达了江泽民、邓小平、杨尚昆、李鹏、李先念的热烈问候。齐奥塞斯库对此表示感谢并请乔石转达他对中国领导同志的问候和最良好的祝愿。
    齐奥塞斯库在会见时说，关于罗马尼亚的情况和今后如何开展工作他在罗共14大的报告中都作了广泛的论述。
    乔石对罗共14大的胜利召开表示热烈祝贺。他祝愿罗人民沿着社会主义道路继续前进并取得更大的成就。双方在谈话中表达了进一步加强中罗两党、两国、两国人民友好合作和团结的愿望。
    中国驻罗大使王荩卿会见时在座。
