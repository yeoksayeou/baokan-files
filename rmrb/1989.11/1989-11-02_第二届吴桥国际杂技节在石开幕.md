Title: The Second Wuqiao International Circus Festival Opens in Shijiazhuang
Author: Chen Guoqing
Date: 1989-11-02
Edition: 1

The second China Wuqiao International Circus Festival opened today in Shijiazhuang. The second Hebei Provincial Drama Festival and the 1989 Hebei Foreign Economic Cooperation and Trade Fair are also being held simultaneously.

The "two festivals and one fair" adopt a combination of arts and trade, aiming to use art as a medium to promote Hebei's economy and cultural undertakings. This circus festival features performances from Bulgaria, Thailand, Sweden, Mongolia, Denmark, Poland, and the Soviet Union, with 8 acts from these 7 countries. Nine domestic acts are also participating in the competition. The festival's jury includes renowned figures from the international circus world from Norway, the Soviet Union, Japan, Bulgaria, and Mongolia.

### 第二届吴桥国际杂技节在石开幕
陈国琦
1989-11-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    第二届吴桥国际杂技节在石开幕
    本报石家庄11月1日电　记者陈国琦报道：第二届中国吴桥国际杂技艺术节今天在石家庄隆重开幕。第二届河北省戏剧节、河北省1989年对外经济合作洽谈会同时举行。
    “两节一会”采取文贸结合的形式，目的在于以艺为媒，推进河北经济和文化事业。本届杂技节有保加利亚、泰国、瑞典、蒙古、丹麦、波兰、苏联等7个国家的杂技演出团体带来的8个节目。国内9个节目参加比赛。杂技节评委会邀请了挪威、苏联、日本、保加利亚、蒙古等国的国际杂技界知名人士作为评委。
