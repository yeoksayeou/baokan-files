Title: Earthquake in Chongqing Kills 4, Injures Over 100
Author: Lǐ Pèi
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, November 21st.

As of now, the 5.2 and 5.4 magnitude earthquakes that occurred in Chongqing yesterday morning have resulted in 4 deaths, 26 severe injuries, and 135 minor injuries. The epicenter, located in Jiangbei County's Tongjing Town and Long'an Township, has seen over 1,000 homes collapsed. According to officials from the Chongqing Seismological Bureau, aftershocks are still occurring, but none have reached a magnitude of 2.

### 重庆地震死4人伤百余人
李珮
1989-11-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　重庆地震死4人伤百余人
    新华社重庆11月21日电　（记者李珮）到目前为止，昨日上午在重庆接连发生的5.2级、5.4级地震中，有4人死亡、26人重伤，135人轻伤。震中江北县统景镇和龙安等乡有1000余户房屋倒塌。据重庆地震办公室同志介绍，目前仍有余震发生，但均未达到2级。
