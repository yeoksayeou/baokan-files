Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with a delegation led by Iraqi Minister of Transportation and Communications Muhammad Hayim Zayid on the morning of November 10 in the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Pavilion.

Vice Premier Li Tieying received a delegation from the Korean Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, led by Committee member Li Menghu, at the Great Hall of the People on the morning of November 10.

State Councilor and Defense Minister General Qin Jiwei received a delegation from the Korean People's Army Friendship Visit Group, led by Corps Commander General Park Ki-rye, in Beijing on the morning of November 9.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Ye Fei met with a delegation led by Deputy Mayor of Paris Jacques Ferron at the Great Hall of the People on the afternoon of November 10.

President of the Supreme People's Court Ren Jianxin received a delegation from the Thai Ministry of Justice, led by Minister of Justice Paramon Mankalarat, in Beijing on the evening of November 9.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-11
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务院副总理田纪云十一月十日上午在中南海紫光阁会见了伊拉克运输和交通部长穆罕默德·海姆宰一行。
    ▲国务委员李铁映十日上午在人民大会堂会见了以委员李梦虎为团长的朝鲜对外文委会代表团。
    ▲国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟上将九日上午在北京会见以军团司令官朴基瑞上将为团长的朝鲜人民军友好参观团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长叶飞十日下午在人民大会堂会见法国巴黎市副市长雅克·费隆一行。
    ▲最高人民法院院长任建新九日晚上在北京会见了以司法部长参腊·芒卡拉腊为团长的泰国司法部代表团。
                                    （据新华社）
