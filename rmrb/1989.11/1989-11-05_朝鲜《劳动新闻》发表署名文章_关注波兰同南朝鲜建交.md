Title: North Korea's *Rodong Sinmun* Publishes Signed Article
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 3

People's Daily, Pyongyang, November 3rd - Correspondent Xú Bǎokāng reports: Today, North Korea's *Rodong Sinmun* published a signed article on Poland's formal establishment of diplomatic relations with South Korea. The article emphasized that Poland's diplomatic relations with South Korea do not align with the Korean people's efforts for peaceful reunification of their homeland and the development of friendly relations between North Korea and Poland.

The article stated that Poland's establishment of diplomatic relations with South Korea has caused a rift in the traditional good relations between North Korea and Poland.

The article concluded by saying that North Korea will closely monitor the developments in Poland and Poland's attitude towards North Korea.

### 朝鲜《劳动新闻》发表署名文章  关注波兰同南朝鲜建交
徐宝康
1989-11-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    朝鲜《劳动新闻》发表署名文章
    关注波兰同南朝鲜建交
    本报平壤11月3日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜《劳动新闻》今天就波兰正式同南朝鲜建立外交关系发表署名文章强调，波兰同南朝鲜建交不符合朝鲜人民争取祖国和平统一事业和朝鲜与波兰之间友好关系的发展。
    文章说，波兰同南朝鲜建交使朝鲜和波兰之间传统的良好关系出现裂痕。
    文章最后说，朝鲜将密切注视波兰事态的发展和波兰对朝鲜的态度。
