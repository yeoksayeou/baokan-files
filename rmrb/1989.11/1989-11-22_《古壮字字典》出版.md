Title: Publication of the Dictionary of Ancient Zhuang Characters
Author: Jiang Yaqiang
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 3

    The dictionary of ancient Zhuang characters, which is listed as a key project in the national minority ancient book compilation "7·5" plan, has recently been published and distributed by the Guangxi Nationalities Publishing House. This dictionary contains approximately 500,000 characters and includes 107,000 ancient Zhuang characters.

### 《古壮字字典》出版
蒋耀强
1989-11-22
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　《古壮字字典》出版
    木报讯　被列为全国少数民族古籍整理“七·五”规划重点项目的《古壮字字典》，近日已由广西民族出版社出版发行。这部字典约五十余万字，收入古壮字一点零七万个。　（蒋耀强）
