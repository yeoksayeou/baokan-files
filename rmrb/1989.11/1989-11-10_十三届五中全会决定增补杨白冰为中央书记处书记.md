Title: The Fifth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee Decides to Add Yang Baibing as Secretary of the Central Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 2

The Fifth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Decides to Add Yang Baibing as Secretary of the Central Secretariat

New China News Agency, Beijing, November 9 — Decision of the Fifth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Adding Comrade Yang Baibing as Secretary of the Central Secretariat (Adopted on November 9, 1989)

Upon the nomination of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, the Fifth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China decided: to add Comrade Yang Baibing as Secretary of the Central Secretariat.

### 十三届五中全会决定增补杨白冰为中央书记处书记

1989-11-10
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

    十三届五中全会决定增补杨白冰为中央书记处书记
    新华社北京11月9日电  中国共产党十三届五中全会关于增补杨白冰同志为中央书记处书记的决定
    （1989年11月9日通过）
    根据中央政治局常务委员会的提名，中国共产党第十三届中央委员会第五次全体会议决定：增补杨白冰同志为中央书记处书记。
