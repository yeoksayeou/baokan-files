Title: AFC Decides to Suspend Five Athletes
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 22nd.

The Asian Football Confederation (AFC) chairman Hamza announced in Kuala Lumpur today that the AFC will impose a two-year or three-year suspension on athletes who assault referees.

Kuwait national team players A. Yaseer and A. Al-Mansour will be suspended for three years each. Another Kuwait player, A. Mahlood, and a club player, F. Nafae, will be suspended for two years each. The latter's club, Al-Arabi Football Club, will be fined $5,000.

Bangladesh footballer E. Hossain was suspended for three years for assaulting a referee during the South Asian Games football match two weeks ago.

### 亚足联作出决定5名运动员被罚停赛

1989-11-23
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　亚足联作出决定5名运动员被罚停赛
    新华社北京11月22日电　据外电报道：亚洲足联主席哈姆扎今天在吉隆坡宣布，亚洲足联将对殴打裁判的运动员处以停赛两年或三年的处罚。
    科威特国家队队员阿·亚西尔和阿·阿尔—曼苏尔被分别停赛三年。另一名科威特国脚阿·马赫卢德和一名俱乐部队员法·纳法埃被停赛两年，后者所在的阿尔—阿拉伯足球俱乐部被罚款5000美元。
    孟加拉国足球运动员埃·霍绍伊恩因两周前在南亚运动会足球赛上殴打裁判被停赛三年。
