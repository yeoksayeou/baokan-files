Title: Beijing Cancels Over 300 Companies
Author: Shixinchu
Date: 1989-11-30
Page: 2

    Beijing's efforts to clean up and reorganize companies continue to progress. Recently, the city announced the cancellation of 313 more companies.

    According to reports, 155 companies were canceled by municipal departments and commissions, with 74 of these being from the municipal commerce and economic commissions. To ensure this work is carried out effectively, the city has assigned specialized personnel to handle the liquidation of assets, rights, and debts of the canceled companies, and to properly arrange for the personnel of these companies.

    It is understood that, including the first batch that was canceled, Beijing has now canceled a total of 661 companies.

### 北京又撤三百余家公司
史辛初
1989-11-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京又撤三百余家公司
    本报讯　北京市清理整顿公司工作继续进展，日前再次公布313家被撤销公司名单。
    据介绍，市属部委办局带头撤消公司155家，其中市商委、经委系统就有74家。为了做好这项工作，市设有专门人员负责清算被撤公司的财产、债权、债务，并对被撤消公司人员进行妥善安排。
    据悉，连同第一批被撤的在内，北京市已撤消公司661家。
            （史辛初）
