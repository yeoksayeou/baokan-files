Title: The Socialist Unity Party of Germany to Hold a Party Congress
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-11-11
Edition: 4

The Socialist Unity Party of Germany will hold its 10th Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee on the 9th, passing a resolution to convene the 4th National Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany in Berlin from December 15 to 17 this year.

The congress will assess the situation within the party and the country, discuss the tasks of continuing preparations for the 12th Party Congress, and decide on changes in the Central Committee personnel. General Secretary Krenz will report on the situation within the party and the country, as well as the party's tasks in socialist renewal.

### 德国统一社会党将召开党代会
许宏治
1989-11-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    德国统一社会党将召开党代会
    本报柏林11月9日电　记者许宏治报道：德国统一社会党11届10中全会9日通过一项决议，定于今年12月15日至17日在柏林召开德国统一社会党第4次全国代表会议。
    代表会议将对党内和国内形势作出估计，讨论继续准备12大的任务，并决定中央委员会的人员变动。克伦茨总书记将在会上就党内、国内形势及党在社会主义革新中的任务问题作报告。
