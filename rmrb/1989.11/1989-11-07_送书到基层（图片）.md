Title: Books Delivered to the Grassroots
Author: Qiao Jun
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 4

On October 29th, the Xinhua Bookstore in Xiangyang County, Hubei, carefully selected tens of thousands of books and periodicals with healthy content, loaded them onto vehicles, and delivered them to the Xiangyang Bearing Factory, which has nearly ten thousand workers, for sale. The move was warmly welcomed by the workers.
