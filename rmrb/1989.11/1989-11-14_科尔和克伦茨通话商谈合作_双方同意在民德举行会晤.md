Title: Kohl and Krenz Discuss Cooperation Over the Phone
Author: Jiang Jian Guo, Xu Hong Zhi
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 4

Kohl and Krenz Discuss Cooperation Over the Phone
Both Sides Agree to Meet in East Germany

BEIJING, Nov. 13 (Xinhua) -- West German Chancellor Kohl and East German leader Krenz spoke over the phone on the evening of Nov. 11 to exchange views on steps for bilateral cooperation. Both sides agreed to meet in East Germany.

Chancellor Kohl welcomed East Germany's recent decision to open its borders, believing that this decision will help further the development of bilateral relations. Krenz said that this decision was made independently by East Germany and demonstrates East Germany's resolve to implement reform policies. In bilateral cooperation, practicality, trust, and goodwill are indispensable prerequisites. He emphasized that opening the borders does not question the borders themselves, and the stability of bilateral relations is a decisive factor for the stability of the European continent.

Kohl and Krenz agreed that both Germanys should maintain rationality and avoid emotional outbursts, and handle the issues that arise with a shared sense of responsibility.

Both sides agreed to meet privately in East Germany. West German Chancellor's Office Minister Seitter will visit Berlin on Nov. 20.

### 科尔和克伦茨通话商谈合作  双方同意在民德举行会晤
江建国  许宏治
1989-11-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    科尔和克伦茨通话商谈合作
    双方同意在民德举行会晤
    本报柏林11月13日电　记者江建国、许宏治报道：西德总理科尔和民德领导人克伦茨11日晚通过电话，就双边合作步骤交换了意见。双方同意在民主德国进行会晤。
    科尔总理欢迎民德最近开放边界的决定，认为这一决定有助于双边关系的继续发展。克伦茨说，这一决定是民德独立自主作出的，它表明民德革新政策的决心。在双方合作中，务实、可信和良好愿望是必不可少的前提。他强调，开放边界并不是对边界本身提出疑问，双边关系的稳定是欧洲大陆保持稳定的决定性前提。
    科尔和克伦茨一致认为，目前两个德国都应保持理智，避免感情激动，以共同负责的态度处理出现的问题。
    双方同意在民主德国进行私人会晤。西德总理府部长赛特尔斯将于本月20日到柏林访问。
