Title: The National Youth Men's Volleyball League Concludes
Author: Zī Hóngqí
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 3

The 1989 National Youth Men's Volleyball League concluded today after 14 days of intense competition in Zhengzhou. The Beijing team and the Shanghai team won the championship and the runner-up position, respectively.

### 全国青年男排联赛结束
訾红旗
1989-11-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国青年男排联赛结束
    新华社郑州11月9日电　（记者訾红旗）1989年全国青年排球联赛男子组的比赛，经过14天的激战，今天在这里结束。北京队和上海队分别获得冠、亚军。
