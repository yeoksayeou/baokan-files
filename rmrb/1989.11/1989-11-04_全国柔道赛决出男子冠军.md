Title: National Judo Championship Decides Men's Champions
Author:
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 4

The national men's and women's judo championship, on November 2, decided the men's champions in eight weight classes in Yueyang. The champions for the 78kg, 86kg, 95kg, and over 95kg weight classes were won by Zhang Jifeng from Liaoning, Buhe from Inner Mongolia, Jiang Fabin from Jilin, and Xu Guoqing from Liaoning, respectively. Guo Deyong from Shandong, Du Hongli from Shaanxi, Renqinzhun from Inner Mongolia, and Shi Lei from Hebei won the 60kg, 65kg, 71kg, and open weight classes, respectively.

### 全国柔道赛决出男子冠军

1989-11-04
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国柔道赛决出男子冠军
    新华社长沙11月3日电　全国男女柔道冠军赛，2日晚在岳阳决出男子8个级别的冠军。78公斤级、86公斤级、95公斤级、95公斤以上级的冠军，分别由辽宁的张继峰、内蒙古的布和、吉林的姜法斌和辽宁的徐国清夺得；山东队的郭德勇、陕西队的杜洪里、内蒙古队的仁钦忠乃和河北队的石磊分别夺走60公斤级、65公斤级、71公斤级和无差别级的冠军。
