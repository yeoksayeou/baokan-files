Title: Czechoslovak Defense Minister Hosts Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Czechoslovak Defense Minister General Václavík hosted a reception for a Chinese military friendship delegation led by Deputy Chief of the General Staff Xu Xin on November 1 in Prague. The hosts and guests engaged in friendly discussions on domestic situations and international issues in their respective countries, and they also looked forward to the prospects of friendly exchanges between the two militaries.

### 捷国防部长宴请我代表团

1989-11-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    捷国防部长宴请我代表团
    捷克斯洛伐克国防部长瓦茨拉维克大将1日在布拉格会见并宴请由徐信副总参谋长率领的中国人民解放军友好代表团。宾主在友好的气氛中就本国国内情况和国际局势问题进行了交谈，并展望了两军友好交往的前景。
            （据新华社）
