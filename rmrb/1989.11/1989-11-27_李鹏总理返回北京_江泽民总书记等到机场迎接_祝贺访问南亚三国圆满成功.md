Title: Premier Li Peng Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-27
Edition: 1

Premier Li Peng returned to Beijing today after successfully concluding his official friendly visits to Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal, and after inspecting work in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. He arrived on a special plane from Urumqi this evening.

CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Xi Zhongxun, and State Council Secretary-General Luo Gan were at the airport to welcome Premier Li Peng and congratulate him on the successful completion of his visits to the three South Asian countries.

Accompanying Premier Li Peng on his return were his wife Zhu Lin, Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army He Qizong, and others. Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin had already returned to Beijing earlier. The ambassadors of Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal to China were also at the airport to welcome him.

### 李鹏总理返回北京　江泽民总书记等到机场迎接　祝贺访问南亚三国圆满成功

1989-11-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏总理返回北京
　　江泽民总书记等到机场迎接
　　祝贺访问南亚三国圆满成功
    新华社北京11月26日电　国务院总理李鹏圆满结束对巴基斯坦、孟加拉国和尼泊尔三国的正式友好访问，并在新疆维吾尔自治区视察工作后，今天晚上乘专机从乌鲁木齐回到北京。
    中共中央总书记江泽民、国务院副总理田纪云、全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋、国务院秘书长罗干等到机场迎接李鹏总理，祝贺他这次访问南亚三国取得圆满成功。
    陪同访问的李鹏总理的夫人朱琳、外交部长钱其琛、中国人民解放军副总参谋长何其宗等同机回到北京。陪同出访的对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬已经先期回到北京。
    巴基斯坦、孟加拉国和尼泊尔驻华使节也到机场迎接。（附图片）
