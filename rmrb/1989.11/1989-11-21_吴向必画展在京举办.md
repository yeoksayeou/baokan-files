Title: Exhibition of Wu Xiangbi's Paintings Held in Beijing
Author: Jin Yan
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 3

	Exhibition of Wu Xiangbi's Paintings Held in Beijing
	Our reporter in Beijing, November 20th: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Aphai Alqingjinmei and Liao Hansheng, along with people from various circles in the capital, viewed the exhibition of Wu Xiangbi's paintings held at the Nationalities Cultural Palace this morning.

	Former Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and former Secretary of the Guizhou Provincial Committee, Wu Xiangbi, despite suffering a cerebral hemorrhage in 1981 that left him paralyzed, has persevered with unyielding determination to learn painting. Over the past few years, he has created nearly 200 paintings and calligraphy works on various themes. Wu Xiangbi's name has been included in the "World Famous People's Directory."

### 吴向必画展在京举办
金岩
1989-11-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　吴向必画展在京举办
    本报北京十一月二十日讯全国人大常委会副委员长阿沛·阿旺晋美、廖汉生等同志今天上午同首都各界群众一起观看了在民族文化宫举办的吴向必画展。
    原全国人大常委会民族委员会副主任、前贵州省委书记吴向必同志在一九八一年身患脑血栓而全身瘫痪的情况下，仍以顽强的毅力学画。几年来，创作出各种题材的书画作品近二百幅。吴向必的名字已列入《世界名人录》中。（金岩）
