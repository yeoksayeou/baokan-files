Title: Gorbachev Warns Western Countries Not to Use Economic Means to Achieve Political Goals During Meeting with Canadian Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Edition: 4

Gorbachev warned Western countries not to use economic means to achieve political goals during his meeting with the Canadian Prime Minister.

	The New China News Agency reported from Moscow on November 21 that Soviet leader Gorbachev said on the 21st that the Soviet Union is prepared to engage in various forms of economic exchanges with the West. He also warned the West not to use this as a means to achieve political goals or to influence the political decisions of sovereign countries.

	Gorbachev made the above remarks during his meeting with Canadian Prime Minister Mulroney regarding Western economic aid. Mulroney is currently on an official visit to the Soviet Union.

### 戈尔巴乔夫同加拿大总理会晤时警告　西方不要用经济手段达到政治目的

1989-11-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫同加拿大总理会晤时警告
　　西方不要用经济手段达到政治目的
    新华社莫斯科11月21日电苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫21日表示，苏联准备同西方进行多种多样的经济交流。他同时又警告西方不要借此来达到政治目的，对主权国家的政治决定施加影响。
    戈尔巴乔夫是在与加拿大总理马尔罗尼会晤中谈到西方的经济援助时发表以上见解的。马尔罗尼现在在苏联进行正式访问。
