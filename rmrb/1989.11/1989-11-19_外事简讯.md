Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-11-19
Edition: 2

	National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Vice Chairman Cheng Siyuan held a reception on the afternoon of the 18th in the Great Hall of the People for a delegation of Nepalese youth led by Bilendra Chhetri, Vice Chairman of the Nepalese Youth Organization.

	The delegation arrived in Shenzhen on the 11th and began their visit to China.

	The Romanian Embassy organized a Romanian Book and Picture Exhibition, which opened on the afternoon of the 18th at the China Institute of Industrial Relations in Beijing.

	This one-week exhibition features more than 500 books and over 50 pictures published in Romania in recent years.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

    外事简讯
    ▲全国政协副主席程思远18日下午在人民大会堂会见了由全国青年组织副主席比兰德拉·切特利率领的尼泊尔青年代表团。
    代表团是11日抵达深圳开始对中国进行访问的。
    ▲由罗马尼亚大使馆举办的罗马尼亚图书图片展览18日下午在北京中国工运学院开幕。
    这次为期一周的展览共展出近年来罗马尼亚各类书刊500余册，图片50余幅。　（新华社）
