Title: The "East Water West Transfer" Project in Beijing Begins Construction
Author: Su Huizhi
Date: 1989-11-16
Edition: 2

The "East Water West Transfer" project, aimed at solving the water supply issues for agriculture, industry, and residents in western Beijing, officially began construction today at Yuquanshan in western Beijing. The project will transfer water from Miyun Reservoir through Tuancheng Lake near the Summer Palace to Chengzi Water Plant in Mentougou District, with water expected to flow by July next year.

### 北京“东水西调”工程动工
苏会志
1989-11-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京“东水西调”工程动工
    据新华社北京11月15日电　（记者苏会志）解决北京西部工农业生产、居民生活用水的“东水西调”工程今天在京西玉泉山正式开工。“东水西调”工程是将密云水库的水通过颐和园附近的团城湖，再引至门头沟区的城子水厂，计划明年7月通水。
