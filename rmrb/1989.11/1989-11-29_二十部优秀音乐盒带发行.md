Title: Release of Twenty Excellent Music Cassettes
Author: Yi Kai
Date: 1989-11-29
Edition: 3

China's largest audio-visual publishing unit—the China Record Corporation—has recently released twenty newly compiled music cassettes and records. Among them are "Gold Record Award," "Pipa Complete Collection," "Guzheng Complete Collection," and the "Chinese National Instrument 'Sound and Picture' Series."

### 二十部优秀音乐盒带发行
易凯
1989-11-29

专栏：

　　二十部优秀音乐盒带发行
    本报讯　我国最大的音像出版单位——中国唱片总公司，新近推出二十部新编录的民族音乐盒带和唱片，其中有《金唱片奖》、《琵琶大全》、《古筝大全》、《中国民族器乐“音画”系列》。
