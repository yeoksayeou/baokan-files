Foreign Affairs Brief

▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Ni Zhifu met on November 22 in Beijing with Oscar Figuera, General Secretary of the Venezuelan Workers' United Central Union, and Eduardo Surita, Secretary for International Relations of the Ecuadorian Workers' Union. Oscar Figuera and Eduardo Surita are in China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a 10-day friendly visit.

▲ Li Ximing, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, met and hosted a banquet on November 22 in the Great Hall of the People for the Tanzanian Revolutionary Party delegation headed by Salmin Amour, member of the Central Committee and Secretary for Economic Planning of the National Executive Committee.

▲ Li Guixian, State Councilor and Governor of the People's Bank of China, met on November 22 in Beijing with Richard S. Regan, Senior Executive Vice President of MasterCard International.

▲ Zhang Jinfu, President of the Supreme People's Court, met and hosted a banquet on the evening of November 22 in Beijing for some Chinese and foreign representatives attending the international conference on the protection and promotion of children's rights.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

　　外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福22日在北京会见了委内瑞拉工人统一中央工会总书记奥斯卡尔·菲盖拉和厄瓜多尔工人联合会国际关系书记爱德华多·苏里塔一行。奥斯卡尔·菲盖拉和爱德华多·苏里塔一行是应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国进行为期10天的友好访问的。
    ▲中共中央政治局委员李锡铭22日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了以坦桑尼亚革命党中央委员、全国执委会经济计划书记萨尔铭·阿莫尔为团长的坦桑尼亚革命党代表团。
    ▲国务委员、中国人民银行行长李贵鲜22日在北京会见了美国万事达卡国际组织高级执行副总裁罗世治一行。
    ▲最高人民法院院长任建新22日晚上在北京会见并宴请了参加保护和促进儿童权利国际讨论会的部分中外代表。
