Title: Lenovo Group Company Established in Beijing
Author: Fei Weiwei
Date: 1989-11-16
Edition: 2

Lenovo Group Company Established in Beijing

	BEIJING, Nov. 14 (Xinhua) -- A company dedicated to the research, development, and promotion of computer products, the Beijing Lenovo Computer Group Company, was established in Beijing today.

	Lenovo Group Company was founded by the Chinese Academy of Sciences Computer Technology Research Institute, which successfully developed the "Lenovo Chinese Character System," a product that won the National Science and Technology Progress Award.

### 联想集团公司近日在京成立
费伟伟
1989-11-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    联想集团公司近日在京成立
    本报北京11月14日讯  记者费伟伟报道：以研究、开发、推广应用计算机产品为宗旨的北京联想计算机集团公司今天在京成立。
    联想集团公司是由曾成功地开发出国家科技进步一等奖获奖产品——联想汉字系统的中国科学院计算所公司组建成立的。
