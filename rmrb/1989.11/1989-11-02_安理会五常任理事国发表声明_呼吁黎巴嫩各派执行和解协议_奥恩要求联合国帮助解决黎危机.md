Title: UN Security Council Permanent Members Issue Statement Urging Lebanese Factions to Implement Reconciliation Agreement
Author: Liú Kāi Chén
Date: 1989-11-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, October 31st.

The representatives of the five permanent members of the UN Security Council, after several days of consultations on the issue of resolving the Lebanese crisis, issued a statement today. They welcomed the agreement reached by Lebanese factions in Taif, Saudi Arabia, under the auspices of the Arab Three-Party Committee, on the peace process and national reconciliation in Lebanon.

The statement called on all Lebanese people to work together for the full implementation of the agreement after its approval by the Lebanese Parliament.

China, the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France, as the five permanent members of the Security Council, expressed their support for Lebanon's restoration of full sovereignty over its entire territory.

The five permanent members also expressed their continued support for Lebanon's completion of unity, sovereignty, territorial integrity, and independence, and for its peaceful coexistence with neighboring countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, October 31st.

Lebanese Christian militia leader General Michel Aoun wrote a letter to UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar on October 31st, accusing Israel and Syria of continuing to occupy Lebanese territory.

"The Voice of Lebanon" radio station broadcasted on October 31st that Aoun, in his letter, cited all UN resolutions on the issue of foreign occupation and all previous documents from the Lebanese government and presidents demanding the withdrawal of Syrian troops from Lebanon. He requested that the UN Security Council distribute his letter as an official document to all member states. Additionally, Aoun's government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs met with the ambassadors of the UN Security Council member states in Lebanon on October 31st to discuss the possibility of issuing a statement guaranteeing the withdrawal of Syrian troops.

### 安理会五常任理事国发表声明  呼吁黎巴嫩各派执行和解协议  奥恩要求联合国帮助解决黎危机
刘开宸
1989-11-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    安理会五常任理事国发表声明
    呼吁黎巴嫩各派执行和解协议
    奥恩要求联合国帮助解决黎危机
    本报联合国10月31日电　记者刘开宸报道：联合国安理会5常任理事国代表就解决黎巴嫩危机问题进行几天磋商后，今天在这里发表声明，欢迎黎巴嫩各派议员最近在阿拉伯3方委员会主持下在沙特的塔伊夫城就黎巴嫩的和平进程和民族和解达成的协议。
    声明呼吁全体黎巴嫩人在黎巴嫩议会批准该协议后，为其全面执行而共同努力。
    中国、苏联、美国、英国和法国等5常任理事国表示支持黎巴嫩在其整个领土上恢复全部主权。
    5常任理事国还表示继续支持黎巴嫩完成统一、主权、领土完整和独立，并与邻国和睦共处。
    新华社贝鲁特10月31日电　黎巴嫩基督教军政府领导人奥恩31日写信给联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔，指责以色列和叙利亚继续占领黎巴嫩领土。
    “黎巴嫩之声”电台31日广播说，奥恩在信中引用联合国作出的关于外国占领问题的全部决议和以往黎巴嫩政府和总统要求叙利亚军队撤出黎巴嫩的全部文件，要求联合国安理会将他的信件以正式文件发给各成员国。此外，奥恩军政府外交部31日同联合国安理会成员国驻黎巴嫩大使进行了会晤，讨论发表一项保证叙利亚撤军声明的可能性。
