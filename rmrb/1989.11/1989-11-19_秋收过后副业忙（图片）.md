Title: Busy with Sideline Work After the Autumn Harvest
Author: Wáng Jǐngrén
Date: 1989-11-19
Page: 5

The sideline industry of willow weaving in Gu'an County, Hebei Province, has a history of over 400 years. Today, willow products are exported to more than 20 countries and regions, including Japan, the United States, Britain, and the Netherlands, earning farmers an additional income of over 15 million yuan. Currently, there are more than 3,000 people engaged in willow weaving, 350 professional households, and 60 cooperative organizations. This is a scene of farmers in Bai Village engaged in willow weaving production in their courtyards.

		People's Daily reporter Wáng Jǐngrén took the photo.

### 秋收过后副业忙（图片）
王景仁
1989-11-19
第5版(国内专页（经济）)
专栏：

    秋收过后副业忙
    河北省固安县的柳编副业生产迄今已有400多年历史了。如今，柳编出口到日本、美国、英国、荷兰等20多个国家和地区，为农民增加收入1500多万元。现在，从事柳编的编工3000多人，专业户350个，联合体60个。这是柏村的农民在庭院从事柳编生产。                                                            
                            本报记者　王景仁摄
