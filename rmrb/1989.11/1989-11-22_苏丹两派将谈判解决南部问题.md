Title: Sudanese Factions to Negotiate Resolution of Southern Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 4

Sudanese government issued a statement on the 19th, welcoming the southern anti-government armed forces' agreement to hold talks with the government in Nairobi on December 1st without any preconditions.

The statement said that resolving the southern issue through negotiations and achieving national unity and stability is the strategic goal of the Sudanese government.

The statement praised Kenyan President Moi and former U.S. President Carter for their efforts in facilitating talks between the Sudanese government and the Sudan People's Liberation Army.

### 苏丹两派将谈判解决南部问题

1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏丹两派将谈判解决南部问题
    苏丹政府十九日发表声明，欢迎南部反政府武装同意于十二月一日在内罗毕同政府进行无任何先决条件的会谈。
    声明说，通过谈判解决南部问题，实现国家的统一与安定，是苏丹政府的战略目标。
    声明赞扬肯尼亚总统莫伊和美国前总统卡特为苏丹政府与苏丹人民解放军举行和谈所作的努力。
