Title: Saudi Diplomat Assassinated in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Page: 3

According to a report from the Beirut Television Station, a diplomat from the Saudi Arabian Embassy in Lebanon was shot and killed by three unidentified individuals in the commercial district of Beirut in the morning of November 3.

An organization calling itself "Islamic Holy War" claimed responsibility for the assassination, accusing the diplomat of being a spy.

The Saudi government strongly condemned the assassination and demanded that the Lebanese authorities immediately arrest the perpetrators.

Lebanese authorities have begun an investigation into the incident.

### 沙特一名外交官在黎遭暗杀

1989-11-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    沙特一名外交官在黎遭暗杀
    据贝鲁特电视台报道，沙特阿拉伯驻黎巴嫩使馆的一名外交官，一日上午在贝鲁特商业区被三名身份不明的人开枪打死。
    一个自称“伊斯兰圣战”的组织宣称，对这起暗杀事件负责，该组织指控这名外交官是谍报人员。
    沙特政府强烈谴责这起暗杀事件，并要求黎巴嫩当局立即逮捕凶手。
    黎巴嫩有关方面已着手调查这一事件。
