Title: The Soviet Union Launches a Rocket into the Pacific for the Third Time
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th.

The Soviet Union is set to launch a carrier rocket into the Pacific region between November 1st and 6th, as authorized by TASS on October 27th.

This will be the third time in recent months that the Soviet Union has launched a carrier rocket into the Pacific region.

### 苏第三次向太平洋发射火箭

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏第三次向太平洋发射火箭
    塔斯社十月二十七日受权宣布，十一月一日至六日，苏联将向太平洋地区发射运载火箭。
    这是苏联近期内第三次向太平洋地区发射运载火箭。
                                    （据新华社）
