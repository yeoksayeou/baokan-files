Title: The Soviet Union Launches Another Experimental Module into Space
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

The Soviet Union's "Proton" rocket successfully launched the "Quantum-2" experimental module of the "Peace" orbital station into space at 16:01 Moscow time on the 26th. It is expected that the module will dock with the orbital station on December 2.

This is the first new experimental module to be added to the "Peace" orbital station since it ended its unmanned phase on September 6 last year. The module includes an airlock that allows astronauts to enter open space, equipped with various tools and devices for working in open space, including a space rover that can move autonomously in open space.

### 苏联又一实验舱进入太空

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联又一实验舱进入太空
    苏联“质子”号运载火箭，于二十六日莫斯科时间十六时零一分把“和平”号星际轨道站的又一个实验舱“量子—2”号送进太空，预计十二月二日，该实验舱与轨道站对接。
    这是“和平”号轨道站自去年九月六日结束无人驾驶状态以来，接合的第一个新实验舱。该舱有供宇航员进入开放宇宙空间的闸舱，闸舱内有在开放宇宙空间工作的各种设备和用具，包括能在开放宇宙空间自动行走的宇宙车。
