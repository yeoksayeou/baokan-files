Title: Namibia Announces Terms for the Establishment of the Constituent Assembly
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Windhoek, November 6th.

After more than three months of negotiations, the United Nations Transitional Assistance Group in Namibia and South Africa have reached an agreement on the formal document for the "Terms for the Establishment of the Namibian Constituent Assembly."

According to the document released today, the Namibian Constitution will be passed by a two-thirds majority of the 72 representatives in the Constituent Assembly.

The document stipulates that the candidate who receives the most votes in the party with the most seats in the Constituent Assembly, or a representative designated by him, will become the acting chairman of the Constituent Assembly.

### 纳公布制宪议会成立条款

1989-11-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    纳公布制宪议会成立条款
    新华社温得和克11月6日电　在进行3个多月的谈判后，联合国驻纳米比亚援助团和南非已就“纳米比亚制宪议会成立条款”的正式文件达成协议。
    根据今天公布的这份文件，纳米比亚宪法将由制宪议会72位代表的2／3多数票通过。
    文件规定，在制宪议会中获得席位最多政党中赢得选票最多的候选人，或由他指定的另一名代表，将成为制宪议会的代理主席。
