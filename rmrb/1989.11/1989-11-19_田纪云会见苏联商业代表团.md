Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Soviet Commercial Delegation
Author: Cáo Fèngyuán
Date: 1989-11-19
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Soviet Commercial Delegation

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 18 – (Reporter Cao Fengyuan) Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met this afternoon with a delegation from the Soviet Union's Ministry of Trade led by Minister of Trade K. Z. Derekh in Zhongnanhai.

	Tian Jiyun welcomed the delegation's visit. He said that in recent years, Sino-Soviet trade has developed rapidly, and the two countries have a very broad scope for economic cooperation. He said that the economies of China and the Soviet Union each have their own strengths and weaknesses, and there are many areas where they can learn from each other. He suggested that the commercial departments of the two countries adopt various forms to expand trade.

	Tian Jiyun expressed his belief that as long as the relevant departments of the two countries actively promote it, bilateral economic and trade relations will definitely reach a new level.

	Derekh believed that the prospects for developing economic and trade cooperation between the Soviet Union and China are good. He said he was very interested in Chinese goods entering the Soviet market. Minister of Commerce Hu Ping was present at the meeting.

### 田纪云会见苏联商业代表团
曹凤元
1989-11-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    田纪云会见苏联商业代表团
    新华社北京11月18日电　（记者曹凤元）国务院副总理田纪云今天下午在中南海会见了由商业部长康·齐·杰列赫率领的苏联商业部代表团。
    田纪云对代表团的来访表示欢迎。他说，近几年中苏贸易发展很快，两国经济合作领域是非常宽广的。他说，中苏两国的经济各有自己的优势和弱点，有很多方面可以互相取长补短。他建议两国商业部门采取多种形式来扩大贸易。
    田纪云表示相信，只要两国有关部门积极加以推动，双边经贸关系一定可以达到新的水平。
    杰列赫认为苏中双方发展经贸合作的前景良好。他说，他对中国商品进入苏联市场很感兴趣。商业部长胡平会见时在座。
