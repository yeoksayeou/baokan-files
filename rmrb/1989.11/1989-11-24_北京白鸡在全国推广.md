Author: Tang Qian
Date: 1989-11-24

Four Corners

Beijing White Chickens Being Promoted Nationwide

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 24th.

The "Beijing White Chicken," bred by the Beijing Poultry Breeding Company, is now being widely promoted across 28 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities nationwide. On average, over 40 million parent-generation eggs are promoted annually. The Beijing White Chicken has a high survival rate, generally between 91% and 98%, and each chicken lays over 280 eggs per year.

### 北京白鸡在全国推广
唐前
1989-11-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京白鸡在全国推广
    本报讯　由北京种禽公司培育的“北京白鸡”，目前已在全国28个省、区、市大面积推广，平均每年推广父母代种蛋400万枚以上。北京白鸡成活率高，一般在91％到98％，每只鸡产蛋率在年280个以上。（唐前）
