Title: Completion of the Yellow Bridge Battle Revolutionary Martyrs Memorial Tower
Author: Dīng Hèlín
Date: 1989-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 4th.

The New Fourth Army Yellow Bridge Battle Revolutionary Martyrs Memorial Tower was completed, and the unveiling ceremony was held this morning in Huangqiao Town, Taixing County, Jiangsu Province. More than a thousand people, including old soldiers who participated in the Yellow Bridge Battle and representatives from relevant departments and various sectors, attended the ceremony.

The memorial tower is 23.8 meters high. The main structure features two flying red flags, symbolizing the unity of the New Fourth Army and the Eighth Route Army, as well as military-civilian unity. The base of the tower is octagonal, and the tower is inscribed with words by Comrade Su Yu: "New Fourth Army Yellow Bridge Battle Revolutionary Martyrs Memorial Tower."

### 黄桥战役烈士纪念塔落成
丁鹤林
1989-11-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    黄桥战役烈士纪念塔落成
    新华社南京十一月四日电　（通讯员丁鹤林）新四军黄桥战役革命烈士纪念塔落成，揭幕仪式今天上午在江苏省泰兴县黄桥镇举行。当年参加黄桥战役的老战士、有关部门负责人和各界代表共一千余人参加揭幕仪式。
    纪念塔高二十三点八米，主体是两面飘扬的红旗，象征新四军、八路军两军团结和军民团结。塔基为八角形，塔上刻着粟裕同志的题字：“新四军黄桥战役革命烈士纪念塔”。
