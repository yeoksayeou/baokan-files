Title: The Third International Children's Painting Competition Held
Author: Lǐ Huáilín  Zhōu Qún
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 4

The third international children's painting competition, featuring 80 young artists from China and abroad, was held this afternoon at the Indian Embassy in Beijing.

The competition was jointly organized by the China International Culture Exchange Center and the Indian Embassy.

### 三届中外儿童绘画赛举行
李怀林  周群
1989-11-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    三届中外儿童绘画赛举行
    新华社北京十一月四日电　（记者李怀林、新闻实习生周群）有八十名中外儿童参加的第三届中外儿童绘画比赛今天下午在印度驻华大使馆举行。
    这次绘画比赛是由中国国际文化交流中心和印度大使馆联合举办的。
