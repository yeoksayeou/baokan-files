Title: 61 Counties in Shandong Province Achieve Basic Rat-Free Status
Author: Liuguanquan
Date: 1989-11-19
Edition: 2

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Jinan on November 18, a reporter from the Shandong Provincial Health Department learned that by the end of the first half of this year, 61 counties (cities, districts) in Shandong Province had reached the standard of being basically rat-free.

According to a survey, due to the significant reduction in rat density, the province reduced grain losses by nearly 15 billion kilograms in 1988.

### 山东61个县基本无鼠害
刘关权
1989-11-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    山东61个县基本无鼠害
    据新华社济南11月18日电 （记者刘关权）记者从山东省卫生厅获悉，截至今年上半年，山东省已有61个县（市、区）达到了基本无鼠害的标准。
    据调查推算，由于鼠密度大幅度下降，全省1988年减少粮食损失近15亿公斤。
