Title: LTTE Applies to Become a Political Party
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

According to well-informed sources in Sri Lanka, the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE)—the main military organization of the Sri Lankan Tamil people—will soon apply to become a political party.

The organization's leaders have accepted the request to change the organization's name, believing that the original name is no longer suitable for participating in national political activities as a political party.

### 斯猛虎组织申请成为政党

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　斯猛虎组织申请成为政党
    据斯里兰卡消息灵通人士二十二日说，斯里兰卡泰米尔猛虎组织——斯里兰卡泰米尔人的主要军事组织不久将申请成为一个政党。
    该组织领导人已接受更改该组织名称的要求，认为原名称在作为一个政党参加国家政治活动时已不适宜。
