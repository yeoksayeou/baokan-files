Title: All-China Women's Federation Calls on Women to Study the Fifth Plenary Session Spirit and Contribute to Reform
Author: Hú Xiǎomèng, Sūn Yǒng
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 14th. (Reporters Hu Xiaomeng and Sun Yong) The sixth session of the second executive committee of the All-China Women's Federation opened today in Beijing. The meeting called on the broad masses of women to deeply study the documents of the Fifth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and to make new contributions to the rectification and deepening of reforms.

Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Chen Muxia said at the meeting, "The Fourth and Fifth Plenary Sessions of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China have determined the guiding principles for China's political and economic work in the future and have made comprehensive arrangements. All levels of the Women's Federation should take the implementation of the spirit and arrangements of the two plenary sessions as the central task for the next stage of work. All levels of the Women's Federation should unify their thinking according to the spirit of the Fifth Plenary Session, strengthen their confidence, unite and mobilize the broad masses of women, carry forward the spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle, and play an active role in the rectification and deepening of reforms. This will also help to better promote the work of the Women's Federation in various aspects."

Vice-Chairman and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation Zhang Zhenying reported on the work and arranged tasks for the winter of this year and next year: study the documents of the Fourth and Fifth Plenary Sessions of the Central Committee, unify the thinking of the Women's Federation's work and the work of the Women's Federation system with the spirit of the plenary sessions; continue to strengthen propaganda and ideological work, and effectively carry out ideological and political work as a regular and important task; continue to mobilize the masses of women to contribute to the rectification and deepening of reforms; continue to do a good job in protecting women's legal rights in the reform; continue to strengthen grassroots work; continue to do a good job in children's work; continue to develop the united front of women; and continue to strengthen international liaison work.

More than 300 executive committee members of the All-China Women's Federation and relevant department heads attended today's meeting.

### 全国妇联号召广大妇女  学习五中全会精神为改革作贡献
胡晓梦  孙勇
1989-11-15
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    全国妇联号召广大妇女
    学习五中全会精神为改革作贡献
    新华社北京11月14日电　（记者胡晓梦、孙勇）全国妇联六届二次执委会今天在北京开幕。会议号召广大妇女群众深入学习党的十三届五中全会文件，为治理整顿、深化改革作出新的贡献。
    全国妇联主席陈慕华在会上说，中共十三届四中全会和五中全会确定了我国今后政治和经济工作的指导方针，并做出了全面部署。各级妇联都要将贯彻两次全会的精神和部署作为下一阶段工作的中心内容。各级妇联都要按照五中全会精神统一认识，坚定信心，团结和动员广大妇女，发扬自力更生，艰苦奋斗的精神，在治理整顿深化改革中发挥积极作用，并以此更好地带动妇联各方面的工作。
    全国妇联副主席、书记处第一书记张帼英在会上做了工作报告，布置了今冬和明年的工作：学习党的四中、五中全会文件，将妇联工作的思想和妇联系统的工作统一到全会精神上来；继续加强宣传、思想工作，切实把思想政治工作作为经常性的重要任务抓出成效来；继续发动妇女群众为治理整顿、深化改革作贡献；继续抓好改革中的维护妇女合法权益工作；继续加强基层工作；继续做好儿童工作；继续发展妇女统一战线；继续加强国际联络工作。
    全国妇联执委和有关方面负责人300多位参加了今天的会议。
