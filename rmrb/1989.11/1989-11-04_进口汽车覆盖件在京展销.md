Title: Imported Car Cover Parts Exhibition in Beijing
Author: Yang Lili
Date: 1989-11-04
Edition: 2

Imported Car Cover Parts Exhibition in Beijing

	The exhibition and sale of imported car cover parts, jointly organized by the China Automobile Trade Corporation and the Beijing Municipal Petroleum Products Sales Corporation, recently opened in Beijing. This event is expected to partially alleviate the difficulties in purchasing imported car parts.

### 进口汽车覆盖件在京展销
杨莉莉
1989-11-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    进口汽车覆盖件在京展销
    本报讯　由中国汽车贸易总公司、北京市石油产品销售总公司举办的进口汽车覆盖件展销会，日前在北京开幕。这将部分缓解进口汽车零部件购买难的矛盾。　（杨莉莉）
