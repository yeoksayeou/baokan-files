Title: The United States Launches a Space Shuttle
Author:
Date: 1989-11-24
Edition: 4

The United States "Discovery" space shuttle was launched into space at 7:21 PM local time on the 22nd from the Kennedy Space Center at Cape Canaveral.

The primary mission of the "Discovery" space shuttle this time is to send a $300 million, 2.5-ton secret military satellite into orbit.

There are five astronauts on board the space shuttle. The commander, Frederick Gregory, is 48 years old and is the first African American to command a space shuttle. There is also a 37-year-old female astronaut, Kathryn Thornton, who is the first woman to undertake a military space mission.

### 美国发射航天飞机

1989-11-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国发射航天飞机
    美国“发现”号航天飞机于二十二日当地时间下午七点二十一分，在卡纳维拉尔角的肯尼迪航天中心发射上天。
      “发现”号航天飞机此次的主要使命是将一颗价值三亿美元、重二点五吨的秘密军事卫星送入空间轨道。
    航天飞机上有五名宇航员，机长弗雷德里克·格雷戈里四十八岁，是第一个美国黑人任航天飞机机长，还有一名是三十七岁的女宇航员凯思林·桑顿，她是第一个执行军事航天使命的女性。
