Title: Liaoning Establishes Science and Technology Leading Enterprises
Author: Dong Jianzhen, Zhao Nailin
Date: 1989-11-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, November 18th — (Reporters Dong Jianzhen, Correspondent Zhao Nailin) State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian today at the Liaoning Province meeting on the experience of creating science and technology leading enterprises pointed out that in the face of some difficulties in China's economic development, relying on technological progress to improve enterprise quality and overcome difficulties has become even more urgent.

Song Jian said, Liaoning started the activity of creating science and technology leading enterprises relatively early, beginning in 1987. After several years of exploration and continuous summarization, some achievements and experiences have been made, which are worth promoting nationwide.

Song Jian pointed out that the activity of creating science and technology leading enterprises is in line with the spirit of the recently concluded Fifth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee. With the vigorous development of the new technological revolution worldwide, the role of science and technology in economic development and social progress is becoming increasingly prominent and important. Whether it is to overcome the current economic difficulties in China or to ensure long-term stable economic development and achieve the grand goal of socialist modernization, it is necessary to place the promotion of technological progress in a very important strategic position.

Song Jian emphasized that enterprises must resolutely shift to the track of relying on technological progress. Carrying out the activity of creating science and technology leading enterprises is an important measure and an effective way to promote technological progress. It meets the needs of enterprise development and also creates a good environment for science and technology work to fully enter the enterprise.

### 辽宁办科技先导型企业　宋健肯定这一经验提出向全国推广
董践真　赵乃林
1989-11-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　辽宁办科技先导型企业
　　宋健肯定这一经验提出向全国推广
    据新华社沈阳11月18日电　（记者董践真、通讯员赵乃林）国务委员、国家科委主任宋健今天在辽宁省创建科技先导型企业经验交流会议上指出，在我国经济发展遇到了一些困难的形势下，依靠科技进步提高企业素质、渡过难关，显得更加迫切。
    宋健说，辽宁开展创建科技先导型企业活动的时间比较早，从1987年开始，经过几年的探索、不断总结，取得了一些成绩和经验，值得在全国推广。
    宋健指出，创建科技先导型企业活动符合刚刚闭幕的十三届五中全会精神。随着世界新技术革命的蓬勃发展，科学技术对经济发展和社会进步的作用越来越明显，越来越重要。无论是克服当前我国的经济困难，还是保证经济的长期稳定发展，实现社会主义现代化的宏伟目标，都必须把促进科技进步放到十分重要的战略位置上来。
    宋健强调，企业要坚决转向依靠科技进步的轨道。开展创建科技先导型企业活动是推动科技进步重要的措施和有效的途径，它适应企业发展的需要，也为科技工作全面进入到企业创造了良好环境条件。
