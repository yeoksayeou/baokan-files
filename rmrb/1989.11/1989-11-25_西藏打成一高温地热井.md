Title: A High-Temperature Geothermal Well Drilled in Tibet
Author: Hufu
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa

The Tibet Bureau of Geology and Mineral Resources' Geothermal Geology Brigade recently drilled a geothermal well in Yangyin Township, Lhasa City, with a water temperature exceeding 200 degrees Celsius. According to tests, the single-unit power generation potential of this well can reach 10,000 kilowatts, equivalent to 5 to 6 times the single-unit power generation capacity of a typical geothermal well, setting a new record for geothermal exploration on the Chinese mainland.

According to measurements, the water temperature of this well is 204 degrees Celsius, with a steam-water flow rate of over 400 tons per hour, and a working pressure at the wellhead of 12.6 atmospheres.

### 西藏打成一高温地热井
沪夫
1989-11-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　西藏打成一高温地热井
    新华社拉萨电　（通讯员沪夫）西藏地矿局地热地质大队最近在拉萨市羊应乡地热田打出一口水温超过200摄氏度的地热井。据测试，这口井的单机发电潜力可达1万千瓦，相当于一般地热井单机发电量的5至6倍，创造了我国大陆地热勘察的新纪录。
    据测定，这口井的水温为204摄氏度，汽水流量为每小时400余吨，井口工作压力12.6个大气压。
