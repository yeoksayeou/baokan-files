Title: Former Salvadoran Supreme Court Chief Justice Assassinated
Author:
Date: 1989-11-30
Page: 4

The former Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of El Salvador, Francisco José Guerrero, was assassinated on the morning of the 28th in the capital, San Salvador.

According to reports, Guerrero was shot multiple times by a group of armed assailants while driving to work, sustaining 18 bullet wounds. He died in the hospital despite emergency treatment. Following the incident, the government military accused the guerrillas of killing Guerrero, but the guerrilla side immediately issued a statement denying the accusation.

Guerrero was a prominent political leader in El Salvador, advocating for the government and the guerrillas to resolve conflicts through negotiations.

### 萨前最高法院院长被暗杀

1989-11-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　萨前最高法院院长被暗杀
    萨尔瓦多前最高法院院长弗朗西斯科·何塞·格雷罗二十八日上午在首都圣萨尔瓦多遭暗杀。
    据报道，格雷罗驾车上班途中突然遭到一伙武装歹徒猛烈扫射，身饮十八颗子弹，在医院经抢救无效而亡。事件发生后，政府军指责游击队杀害了格雷罗，但游击队方面随即声明否认。
    格雷罗是萨有影响的政治领导人，他主张政府和游击队通过谈判解决冲突。
