Title: Hainan Constructs Expressway
Author: Wu Qiu Guang
Date: 1989-11-27
Edition: 1

    With the approval of the State Council, Hainan Province is currently constructing an east-line expressway that runs across the entire island from north to south. The total investment for the expressway is 930 million yuan, with a total length of 272 kilometers. The entire route is fully enclosed and features full-grade intersections, with a design speed of 100 kilometers per hour. The image shows the road section in Ding'an County being widened.

### 海南建高速公路（图片）
吴秋光
1989-11-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　海南建高速公路
    经国务院批准，海南省正兴建一条横贯全岛南北的东线高速公路。公路总投资9.3亿元人民币，全长272公里，全线实行全封闭、全立交结构，行车时速100公里。图为定安县路段正在拓宽路面。
                                        吴秋光摄
