Title: International Exhibition of Food Processing and Packaging Machinery Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 24th.

The first International Exhibition of Food Processing and Packaging Machinery opened today at the China International Exhibition Center. Companies from Denmark, France, Hungary, Italy, Japan, Sweden, Switzerland, the United States, the United Kingdom, West Germany, Belgium, and the Hong Kong region participated in the exhibition.

It is understood that this is a regular international professional exhibition, which will be held every two years (in odd-numbered years) in Beijing in the future.

The exhibition is organized by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics.

### 食品加工　包装机械  国际博览会在京举行

1989-11-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　食品加工　包装机械国际博览会在京举行
    据新华社北京11月24日电　首届国际食品加工和包装机械博览会今天在中国国际展览中心开幕。来自丹麦、法国、匈牙利、意大利、日本、瑞典、瑞士、美国、英国、西德、比利时和香港地区的厂家参加了博览会。
    据了解，这是一个定期举办的国际性的专业博览会，今后将每两年（逢单年）在京举办一次。
    博览会是由中国贸促会和机械电子部主办的。
