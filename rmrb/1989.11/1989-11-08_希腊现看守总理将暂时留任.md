Title: Greek Interim Prime Minister to Temporarily Remain in Office
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Athens, November 6th.

Greek interim Prime Minister Ioannis Grivas will continue to serve until a new government is formed in Greece.

Following the Greek elections on November 5th, Grivas submitted his resignation to President Christos Sartzetakis on the morning of the 6th. This move was intended to allow the president to consult with party leaders to explore the possibility of forming a new government.

### 希腊现看守总理将暂时留任

1989-11-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    希腊现看守总理将暂时留任
    据雅典通讯社六日报道，希腊政府看守总理约安尼斯·格里瓦斯将继续留任，直至成立希腊新政府。
    本月五日希腊大选之后，格里瓦斯曾于六日上午向总统赫里斯托斯·萨采塔基斯提出辞呈，以便由总统与党派领导人磋商，探讨组成新政府的可能性。
                                    （据新华社）
