Title: The Soviet Union to Build Petrochemical Complex in Tyumen
Author:
Date: 1989-11-12
Edition: 4

The Soviet Union plans to construct five petrochemical complexes in the Tyumen region. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Nikolai Ryzhkov, believes that building petrochemical complexes in Western Siberia can change the past situation where the Soviet Union sold oil and natural gas while simultaneously purchasing chemical raw materials from the West. This will be beneficial in alleviating the current tense economic and social issues.

### 苏将在秋明建石化综合体

1989-11-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏将在秋明建石化综合体
    据《真理报》十日报道，苏联计划在秋明地区建造五个石化综合体。苏联部长会议主席雷日科夫认为，在西西伯利亚兴建石化综合体可改变过去苏联出售石油和天然气而同时又向西方购买化工原料的状况，有利于缓和当前紧张的经济和社会问题。
