Xinhua News Agency

The National People's Congress Standing Committee Appointment and Removal List

1989-10-31, the 10th meeting of the 7th National People's Congress Standing Committee passed the following:

1. Appoint Dao Anju (Dai ethnic), Yang Wengui (Li ethnic), Nurmuhammat Rehesi (Kazakh ethnic), and Peng Yingming (Tujia ethnic) as members of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee.

2. Appoint Wang Songda as a member of the National People's Congress Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee.

3. Appoint Sun Poshen, Zhang Wenxue, Li Shimin (female), Yang Jinqi, Yin Dezheng, Li Tianshun, and Jia Jianbin as judges of the Supreme People's Court.

4. Remove You Lin from the position of Vice Secretary-General of the National People's Congress Standing Committee.

5. Remove Wang Qi, Xu Peiran, Li Tao, Chen Zhiming, and Zhang Kun from their positions as judges of the Supreme People's Court.

6. Remove Wang Qingchuan from the position of Prosecutor of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

### 全国人大常委会任免名单

1989-11-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国人大常委会任免名单
    1989年10月31日第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十次会议通过
    一、任命刀安巨（傣族）、杨文贵（黎族）、努尔莫合买提·热衣斯（哈萨克族）、彭英明（土家族）为全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员。
    二、任命王宋大为全国人民代表大会华侨委员会委员。
    三、任命孙泊生、张文学、李世敏（女）、杨金琪、尹德政、李天顺、贾建斌为最高人民法院审判员。
    四、免去有林的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长职务。
    五、免去王奇、徐沛然、李铎、陈志明、张锟的最高人民法院审判员职务。
    六、免去汪庆传的最高人民检察院检察员职务。（新华社）
