Title: Jiang Zemin Congratulates Mladenov on Election as General Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1989-11-15
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, sent a telegram on November 14 to Comrade Petar Mladenov, congratulating him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party. The text of the telegram is as follows:

On the occasion of your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and in my personal capacity, extend my warm congratulations to you. I sincerely wish you success in fulfilling the great mission entrusted to you by the Party in your important leadership position.

May the traditional friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of the two countries continue to be strengthened and developed.

### 江泽民电贺姆拉德诺夫当选为保共中央总书记

1989-11-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民电贺姆拉德诺夫当选为保共中央总书记
    新华社北京十一月十四日电　中国共产党中央委员会总书记江泽民十四日打电报给佩特尔·姆拉德诺夫同志，热烈祝贺他当选为保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨代表中国共产党中央委员会，并以我个人的名义向您致以热烈的祝贺。衷心祝愿您在重大责任的领导岗位上顺利地完成党赋予的崇高使命。
    愿中保两党、两国和两国人民之间的传统友好合作关系不断得到巩固和发展。
