Title: Bush Signs Bill to Raise Debt Ceiling: U.S. National Debt to Exceed $3 Trillion
Author:
Date: 1989-11-10
Page: 4

People's Daily, Washington, November 8th

To prevent the federal government from becoming paralyzed due to a lack of funds and to avoid causing turmoil in the financial markets, the U.S. Senate and House of Representatives rushed through a bill yesterday with an overwhelming majority, raising the U.S. debt ceiling from the current $2.8 trillion to $3.123 trillion by October 31, 1990.

Today, Bush signed the bill into law.

After Bush signed the bill, the U.S. Treasury Department immediately sold $160 billion in short-term treasury bills. Most of this money will be used to repay debts maturing tomorrow.

### 布什签署提高国债上限法案  美国国债将突破三万亿美元

1989-11-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布什签署提高国债上限法案 
    美国国债将突破三万亿美元
    本报华盛顿11月8日电  为了防止联邦政府因无钱支出而陷入瘫痪状况和避免在金融市场上引起混乱，美国国会参众两院昨晚以压倒多数票匆匆通过一项法案，将美国国债限额由现在的2.8万亿美元提高到1990年10月31日为止的3.123万亿美元。
    今天，布什签署了此法案。
    布什签署法案后，美国财政部立即抛售了160亿美元的短期国库券。这笔钱的大部分将用来偿还明天到期的债务。
