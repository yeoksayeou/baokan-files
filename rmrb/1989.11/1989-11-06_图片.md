Title: Lufthansa's Technical Service Center in Hamburg Gains Trust Through Excellent Service
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 7

The Lufthansa Technical Service Center located in Hamburg has earned the trust of its customers through its excellent service. A total of 180 airlines from around the world send their aircraft here for maintenance, including 67 long-term clients. The image shows technicians and workers performing maintenance on an engine.
