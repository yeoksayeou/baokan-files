Title: Twelve Gold Medals Awarded at National Boxing Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, November 6th.

The National Boxing Championship concluded after four days of intense competition, with champions in all 12 weight classes being determined tonight here.

The champions in each weight class are: Xu Ganlong from Zhejiang (48 kg), Tu Jun from the People's Armed Police (51 kg), Wang Junhua from the People's Armed Police (54 kg), Li Fang from Liaoning (57 kg), Zuo Quan from Liaoning (60 kg), Zhu Xiaojun from Hubei (63.5 kg), Li Bo from Heilongjiang (67 kg), Pan Limin from Anhui (71 kg), Liu Xinjun from Anhui (75 kg), Bai Chongguang from Heilongjiang (81 kg), Zhao Shupeng from Shandong (91 kg), and Dong Yiyan from Heilongjiang (over 91 kg).

After the competition, some boxing experts commented that while China's boxing sport is still not fully mature, the technical level is continually improving. Some athletes can skillfully use various boxing techniques in combination, enhancing their tactical awareness. However, most of the champions this time are members of the national team, with few newcomers. Additionally, some technical issues exposed during the matches reflect that training methods and techniques are still outdated and urgently need to be changed and improved.

### 全国拳击冠军赛产生十二枚金牌

1989-11-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国拳击冠军赛产生十二枚金牌
    新华社合肥11月6日电　全国拳击冠军赛经过4天的交锋，12个级别的擂主今晚在这里全部产生。
    获得各级别冠军的是：浙江的徐感龙（48公斤级）、武警部队的涂军（51公斤级）、武警部队的王俊华（54公斤级）、辽宁的李方（57公斤级）、辽宁的左权（60公斤级）、湖北的朱晓军（63.5公斤级）、黑龙江的李波（67公斤级）、安徽的阚利民（71公斤级）、安徽的刘新军（75公斤级）、黑龙江的白崇光（81公斤级）、山东的赵书鹏（91公斤级）、黑龙江的董逸燕（91公斤以上级）。
    赛后一些拳击行家们发表评论认为，我国的拳击运动虽然还不很成熟，但技术水平却在不断提高。一些选手能够熟练运用各种拳法交替进攻，增强了战术意识。但这次获得冠军的绝大多数是国家队队员，新人不多。另外从场上暴露出的一些技术问题来看，反映出训练方法和手段仍很落后，亟待改变和提高。
