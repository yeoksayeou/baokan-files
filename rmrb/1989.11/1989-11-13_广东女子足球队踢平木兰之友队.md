Title: Guangdong Women's Football Team Draws with Mu Lan Friends Team
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, November 11th.

The Guangdong women's football team and the Chinese Taipei Mu Lan Friends team faced off today at the opening ceremony of the Hong Kong Kwai Chung Sports Festival, resulting in a 0-0 draw.

The Guangdong team includes two national team players and recently finished ninth in the national women's football tournament held in Xi'an.

### 广东女子足球队踢平木兰之友队

1989-11-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    广东女子足球队踢平木兰之友队
    新华社香港11月11日电　广东女子足球队和中国台北木兰之友队今天在这里举行的香港葵青体育节的开幕式上交锋，结果双方踢成0∶0平。
    广东队拥有两名国脚，最近在西安举行的全国女子足球赛上列第九名。
