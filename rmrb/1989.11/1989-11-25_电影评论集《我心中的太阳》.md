Title: A Collection of Film Reviews: The Sun in My Heart
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 8

The Chinese Youth Press recently published the film review collection "The Sun in My Heart" by Chen Haosu. Chen Haosu has served as a leader in the film industry for many years and has deep insights into the themes, forms, theories, criticism, and management of film art. This collection includes over 60 articles, featuring meticulous theoretical writings, lively short essays, passionate speeches, and letters, covering various aspects of film art. Many of the insights are unique.

### 电影评论集《我心中的太阳》

1989-11-25
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　电影评论集《我心中的太阳》
    中国青年出版社最近出版了陈昊苏的电影评论集《我心中的太阳》。陈昊苏同志担任电影界领导数年，对电影艺术的题材、形式、理论、评论和管理工作感受颇多。这部评论集收有文章60多篇，其中有谨严的理论文字，有活泼的议论短文，有热情的讲话和信函，涉及电影艺术各个方面，有不少独到的见解。（郁）
