Jin Jiping Acts as Acting Governor of Qinghai

XINING, Nov. 1 (Xinhua) -- The 11th session of the Standing Committee of the Qinghai Provincial People's Congress, which concluded today, decided that Jin Jiping will act as the governor of Qinghai Province. Jin Jiping, 54, formerly served as the Secretary of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Jiangsu Provincial Salt City Committee. He was transferred to Qinghai in April to serve as the deputy secretary of the CPC Qinghai Provincial Committee and was appointed as the deputy governor of Qinghai Province by the Standing Committee of the Qinghai Provincial People's Congress in June. The meeting also accepted the resignation request of former governor Song Ruixiang.

### 金基鹏代理青海省长

1989-11-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    金基鹏代理青海省长
    本报西宁11月1日电　今天闭会的青海省人大常委会第11次会议决定：金基鹏代理青海省省长。54岁的金基鹏，曾任中共江苏省盐城市委书记，今年4月调任青海省委副书记，6月被省人大常委会任命为副省长。会上，还接受了原省长宋瑞祥辞职的请求。
