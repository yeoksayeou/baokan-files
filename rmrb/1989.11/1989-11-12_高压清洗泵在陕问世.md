Title: High-Pressure Cleaning Pumps Debut in Shaanxi
Author: Liu Xiao
Date: 1989-11-12
Edition: 2

High-pressure cleaning pumps, which incorporate American technology and are manufactured in China, have recently been produced at the Baoji Pump Factory in Shaanxi Province.

The high-pressure cleaning pump can replace manual labor and is widely used in the petroleum, chemical, transportation, environmental protection, hydropower, and municipal engineering industries for pipe cleaning and surface descaling. Its performance indicators all reach the advanced level of the 1980s internationally.

### 高压清洗泵在陕问世
刘晓
1989-11-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    高压清洗泵在陕问世
    本报讯　引进美国技术、我国自己制造生产的高压清洗泵，日前在陕西宝鸡水泵厂问世。
    高压清洗泵能代替人工，广泛用于石油、化工、交通、环保，以及水电、市政工程等行业的管道清洗、表面除垢。其各项性能指标均达到国际80年代先进水平。　（刘晓）
