Title: Yanwu Recorders to Be Produced in the Soviet Union
Author: Zhang Zizhong
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 2

The "Yanwu" brand recorder will be produced in the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union. This was announced in the cooperation agreement signed on November 11 between the economic and trade delegation of the Kurdan District of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic and the Yuncheng Radio Factory of Jiangsu Province. According to the agreement, the district will introduce technology from the Yuncheng Radio Factory and establish a production plant, achieving an annual production capacity of 100,000 units.

### 燕舞收录机将在苏生产
张自忠
1989-11-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    燕舞收录机将在苏生产
    据新华社南京十一月十三日电  （记者张自忠）“燕舞”牌收录机将在苏联哈萨克加盟共和国生产。这是苏联哈萨克加盟共和国库尔丹区经贸代表团与江苏省盐城无线电总厂十一日签定的合作协议书中宣布的。根据协议，该区将从盐城无线电总厂引进技术，并建立生产厂，形成年产十万台的生产能力。
