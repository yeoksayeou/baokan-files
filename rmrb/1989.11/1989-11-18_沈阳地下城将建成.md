Title: Construction of Shenyang Underground City to be Completed
Author: Yu Wenjiang
Date: 1989-11-18
Page: 2

    The main body of the construction of the largest nationwide civil and military combined underground civil defense project in China—the Shenyang North New Station Underground Street—was completed on October 26th.

    The underground project, with a total area of 40,000 square meters, will be a modern, multifunctional, grand-scale underground city that can be used both in peacetime and wartime. It will include a central hall, cultural and entertainment center, a friendly commercial street, an underground parking lot, and other supporting service networks.

### 沈阳地下城将建成
于文江
1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    沈阳地下城将建成
    本报讯　目前全国最大的平战结合地下人防工程——沈阳北新客站站前地下街主体工程于10月26日竣工。
    总面积为4万平方米的地下工程，将是一座现代化、多功能、规模宏伟、平时和战时都可使用的地下城。内有中央大厅、文化娱乐中心、友好商业大街、地下停车场等配套服务网。（于文江）
