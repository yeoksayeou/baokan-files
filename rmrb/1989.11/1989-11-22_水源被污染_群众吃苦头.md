Title: Water Pollution Causes Hardship for the Masses
Author: Xú Shuǐmíng
Date: 1989-11-22
Page: 6

Chuán Huá Shěng Míng Shān Xiàn Huán Jìng Bǎo Hù Bàn Gōng Shì, Míng Shān Xiàn Lín Fēi Chǎng Měi Nián Yǒu Yī Bǎi Sān Shí Wàn Dùn Wèi Chǔ Lǐ De Hái Yǒu Gǒng, Shēn, Fú Huà Wù Děng Dū Zhǒng Yǒu Hài Wù Zhì De Fèi Shuǐ Pái Rù Yù Xué Hé Yòu Gàn Qū, Yán Zhòng Wū Rǎn Lè Míng Shān, Pú Jiāng Hé Qióng Lái Děng Xiàn De Shuǐ Yuán, Jǐn Pú Jiāng Xiàn Jiù Yǒu Nóng Tián Shí Qiān Wǔ Bǎi Mǔ Fāng Chéng Wū Rǎn, Yǐng Xiǎng Jìn Shì Wàn Rén Hé Wǔ Wàn Shòu Lù De Yǐn Shuǐ. Jiǔ Yuè Èr Shí Yī Rì, Gèi Chǎng Zhòng Liú Suān Shuǐ Zhòng Yán Dà Diē Lòu, Shùn Tù Shuǐ Chǎn Zī Yuán Jiāo Dào Huǐ Mì Xìng Pò Huài, Jù Gèi Chǎng Èr Shí Duō Gōng Lǐ De Pú Jiāng Xiàn Gān Xuī Xiāng Èr Qiān Bǎi Duō Mǐ Fāng De Gōng Dào Jīn Shǔ Wǎng Hé Wēi Liú Shuǐ Yǎng Yú, Zài Shì Guò Hòu Èr Shí Sì Xiǎo Shí Nèi Biàn Dú Sǐ Chéng Yú Wǔ Qiān Duō Jīn, Zhí Jiē Jīng Jì Sǔn Shī Wǔ Wàn Yú Yuán. Xiāng Guān Bù Mén Yī Xiǎng GUǎn WÈN ZHÈ JIÀO SHÌ.

    Míng Shān Xiàn Wěi Yán Zhòng Wū Rǎn Lè Yù Xué Hé Yòu Gàn Qū, Yán Zhòng Wū Rǎn Lè Míng Shān, Pú Jiāng Hé Qióng Lái Děng Xiàn De Shuǐ Yuán, Jǐn Pú Jiāng Xiàn Jiù Yǒu Nóng Tián Shí Qiān Wǔ Bǎi Mǔ Fāng Chéng Wū Rǎn, Yǐng Xiǎng Jìn Shì Wàn Rén Hé Wǔ Wàn Shòu Lù De Yǐn Shuǐ. Jiǔ Yuè Èr Shí Yī Rì, Gèi Chǎng Zhòng Liú Suān Shuǐ Zhòng Yán Dà Diē Lòu, Shùn Tù Shuǐ Chǎn Zī Yuán Jiāo Dào Huǐ Mì Xìng Pò Huài, Jù Gèi Chǎng Èr Shí Duō Gōng Lǐ De Pú Jiāng Xiàn Gān Xuī Xiāng Èr Qiān Bǎi Duō Mǐ Fāng De Gōng Dào Jīn Shǔ Wǎng Hé Wēi Liú Shuǐ Yǎng Yú, Zài Shì Guò Hòu Èr Shí Sì Xiǎo Shí Nèi Biàn Dú Sǐ Chéng Yú Wǔ Qiān Duō Jīn, Zhí Jiē Jīng Jì Sǔn Shī Wǔ Wàn Yú Yuán. Xiāng Guān Bù Mén Yī Xiǎng GUǎn WÈN ZHÈ JIÀO SHÌ.

    According to an investigation by the Environmental Protection Office of Mingshan County, Sichuan, the Mingshan County Phosphorus Fertilizer Factory annually discharges 130,000 tons of untreated wastewater containing mercury, arsenic, and fluorides into the Yuxi River's right branch, severely polluting the water sources of Mingshan, Pujian, and Qionglai Counties. In Pujian County alone, 175,000 mu of farmland has been contaminated, affecting the drinking water for nearly 100,000 people and 50,000 livestock. On September 21, a severe leak of concentrated sulfuric acid from the factory caused catastrophic damage to aquatic resources along the route. In Ganxi Township, Pujian County, more than 20 kilometers away from the factory, 2,800 square meters of metal net cages and micro-flow fish farming in the irrigation channels were poisoned, resulting in the death of over 8,000 kilograms of fish within 24 hours of the accident, causing direct economic losses of over 50,000 yuan. We hope that relevant departments will address this issue.

    Sichuan Pujian County Committee, Xú Shuǐmíng

### 水源被污染  群众吃苦头
徐水铭
1989-11-22
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：监督哨

    水源被污染  群众吃苦头
    据四川名山县环境保护办公室调查，名山县磷肥厂每年有一百三十万吨未经处理的含有汞、砷、氟化物等多种有害物质的废水排入玉溪河右干渠，严重污染了名山、蒲江和邛崃等县的水源，仅蒲江县就有农田十七万五千亩受污染，影响近十万人和五万牲畜的饮水。九月二十一日，该厂浓硫酸严重泄漏，沿途水产资源遭到毁灭性破坏，距该厂二十多公里的蒲江县甘溪乡二千八百多平方米的渠道金属网箱和微流水养鱼，在事故后二十四小时内便毒死成鱼八千多公斤，直接经济损失五万余元。希望有关部门过问这件事。
                四川蒲江县委　徐水铭
