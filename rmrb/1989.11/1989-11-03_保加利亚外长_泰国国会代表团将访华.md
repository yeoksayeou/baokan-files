Title: Bulgarian Foreign Minister and Thai Parliamentary Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 2

	The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesman announced today at a press conference that at the invitation of Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Foreign Minister Petar Mladenov of the People's Republic of Bulgaria will make an official friendly visit to our country from November 5 to 9.

	At the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, a parliamentary delegation from Thailand, led by General Phisal Hemabut, the First Deputy President of the Senate, will arrive in Beijing on November 9 to make a friendly visit to our country.

### 保加利亚外长  泰国国会代表团将访华

1989-11-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    保加利亚外长  泰国国会代表团将访华
    本报北京11月2日讯　外交部发言人今天在新闻发布会上宣布：应外交部长钱其琛的邀请，保加利亚人民共和国外交部长佩特尔·姆拉德诺夫将于11月5日至9日对我国进行正式友好访问。
    应全国人大常委会的邀请，由上议院第一副议长披实·赫马布上将率领的泰国国会代表团，将于11月9日抵京，对我国进行友好访问。
