Title: Pakistan to Hand Over Two Soviet Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

Pakistan will soon hand over two Soviet prisoners of war to the Soviet Embassy in Pakistan as a gesture of goodwill between the two countries, according to a Pakistani official on the 19th.

The Pakistani government has informed the Soviet Embassy in Pakistan to notify the families of the prisoners of war to come to Pakistan to take them back home.

The two prisoners of war were captured during combat with Afghan guerrillas in Afghanistan.

### 巴将向苏联送交两名战俘

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴将向苏联送交两名战俘
    据巴基斯坦官方人士十九日说，巴基斯坦不久将向苏联驻巴基斯坦大使馆送交两名苏联战俘，以作为双方友好的表示。
    巴基斯坦政府已让苏驻巴大使馆通知战俘的亲属前来巴基斯坦接战俘回国。
    这两名战俘是在阿富汗与阿游击队作战时被俘的。
