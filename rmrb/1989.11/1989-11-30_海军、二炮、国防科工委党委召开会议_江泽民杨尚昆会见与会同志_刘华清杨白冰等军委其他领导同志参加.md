Title: The Party Committees of the Navy, Second Artillery, and the Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense Hold a Meeting
Author: Huang Caihong
Date: 1989-11-30
Edition: 1

The Party Committees of the Navy, Second Artillery, and the Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense held a meeting. Jiang Zemin and Yang Shangkun met with the attendees.

The Central Military Commission's other leaders, including Liu Huaqing and Yang Baibing, also participated.

New China News Agency, Beijing, November 29th - (Reporter Huang Caihong) This afternoon, Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Central Military Commission, and Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China and First Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission, met with all the attendees of the meeting of the Party Committees of the Navy, Second Artillery, and the Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense at the Great Hall of the People. They also took a group photo together.

Attending the meeting were Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission Liu Huaqing, Secretary of the Central Military Commission Yang Baibing, and members of the Central Military Commission Qin Jiwei, Zhao Nanqi, as well as Xu Xin, Guo Linxiang, and Liu Anyuan.

When Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Liu Huaqing, Yang Baibing, and other leaders of the Central Military Commission entered the meeting hall, all the attendees applauded warmly.

Jiang Zemin and Yang Shangkun met with the attendees of the meeting of the Party Committees of the Navy, Second Artillery, and the Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense.

### 海军、二炮、国防科工委党委召开会议　江泽民杨尚昆会见与会同志　刘华清杨白冰等军委其他领导同志参加
黄彩虹
1989-11-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　海军、二炮、国防科工委党委召开会议
　　江泽民杨尚昆会见与会同志
　　刘华清杨白冰等军委其他领导同志参加
    新华社北京11月29日电　（记者黄彩虹）中共中央总书记、中央军委主席江泽民，国家主席、中央军委第一副主席杨尚昆等今天下午在人民大会堂会见了出席海军、二炮、国防科工委党委会议的全体同志，并一起照了像。
    参加会见的还有中央军委副主席刘华清、中央军委秘书长杨白冰和军委委员秦基伟、赵南起，以及徐信、郭林祥、刘安元等。
    当江泽民、杨尚昆、刘华清、杨白冰等军委领导同志走进会见大厅时，全体同志热烈鼓掌。（附图片）
    江泽民、杨尚昆会见出席海军、二炮、国防科工委党委会议人员。
                新华社记者　吴森辉摄
