Title: Mongolian Leaders Receive Buhe Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-11-22
Edition: 4

Mongolian leaders received the Buhe delegation

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 20th: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee General Secretary and Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural, Batmonkh, today received the delegation led by Buhe, Chairman of the Inner Mongolian Autonomous Region Government of China, who came to visit upon invitation.

	Batmonkh said, the party and government relations between Mongolia and China are developing well. Mongolia not only develops friendly relations with the Soviet Union but also with China. Mongolia hopes to contribute to the cause of peace and security in the Asia-Pacific region.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 17th (reporter Sun Chengping): Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Sodnom, and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia and Chairman of the Mongolian side of the Mongolia-China Economic and Technological Cooperation Committee, Suren, received the delegation led by Buhe, Chairman of the Inner Mongolian Autonomous Region Government of China, on the 17th and 13th respectively.

### 蒙古领导人接见布赫一行

1989-11-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　蒙古领导人接见布赫一行
    据新华社乌兰巴托11月20日电　蒙古人民革命党中央总书记、大人民呼拉尔主席团主席巴特蒙赫今天在这里接见了应邀前来访问的中国内蒙古自治区政府主席布赫一行。
    巴特蒙赫说，蒙中两国党政关系发展得很好；蒙古既与苏联发展友好关系，也与中国发展友好关系，蒙古希望为亚太地区的和平与安全事业作出努力。
    据新华社乌兰巴托11月17日电（记者孙承平）蒙古部长会议主席索德诺姆和蒙古部长会议副主席、蒙中经贸科技合作委员会蒙方主席苏伦，分别于17日和13日接见中国内蒙古自治区政府主席布赫一行。
