Title: Egyptian Foreign Ministry Official Says Egypt and Syria to Resume Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-11-09
Edition: 3

	Egyptian foreign ministry officials say that Egypt and Syria will soon resume diplomatic relations. The normalization of relations between the two countries is just a matter of time.

	This official said that the normalization of relations between the two countries is expected to be achieved by the end of this year. He mentioned that there has been a real easing of tensions between the two countries recently, especially in the mutual visits of economic and political delegations.

	He said that a Syrian political delegation is currently visiting Cairo. This is the first visit since the two countries severed ties in 1978 due to Egypt's signing of the Camp David Accords with Israel.

	According to Arab diplomatic officials, Libyan leader Muammar Gaddafi is currently arranging for a meeting between the presidents of Egypt and Syria in Libya to resolve their differences.

### 埃及外交部人士说  埃叙将恢复外交关系

1989-11-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    埃及外交部人士说
    埃叙将恢复外交关系
    新华社突尼斯11月7日电  据突尼斯非洲通讯社报道，埃及外交部权威人士今天向记者发表谈话说，埃及和叙利亚不久将恢复外交关系，两国关系正常化只是一个时间问题。
    这名权威人士说，两国关系正常化可望在今年年底以前实现。他提到了两国关系最近有了真正的缓和，特别是表现在两国经济和政治代表团的互访。
    他说，一个叙利亚政治代表团目前正在开罗访问。这是两国自1978年因埃及同以色列签署戴维营协议而断交以来的首次访问。
    据阿拉伯外交官员说，利比亚领导人卡扎菲目前正在安排埃、叙两国总统在利比亚举行一次会晤，以便消除彼此间的分歧。
