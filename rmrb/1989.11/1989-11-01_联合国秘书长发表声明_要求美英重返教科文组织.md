Title: UN Secretary-General Calls for US, UK to Rejoin UNESCO
Author:
Date: 1989-11-01
Edition: 3

UN Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar issued a statement today in New York, calling on the United States and the United Kingdom to rejoin UNESCO. The statement said, "Science, education, culture, and exchange should not be confined by national borders. International organizations dealing with global issues should truly be inclusive."

The United States and the United Kingdom had previously accused UNESCO of having political bias, being anti-Western, mismanaged, and having excessive expenditures. They withdrew from the organization in 1984 and 1985, respectively.

### 联合国秘书长发表声明  要求美英重返教科文组织

1989-11-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国秘书长发表声明
    要求美英重返教科文组织
    新华社联合国10月30日电　联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天在此发表声明，要求美国、英国重新加入联合国教科文组织。声明说，“科学、教育、文化和交流不应受国界限制。涉及全球性问题的国际组织应该真正是由全体参加的组织。”
    美国和英国曾指责联合国教科文组织带有政治色彩、反对西方国家、管理不善和开支太大，并分别于1984年和1985年退出了该组织。
