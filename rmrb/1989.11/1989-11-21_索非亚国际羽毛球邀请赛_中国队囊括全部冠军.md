Title: China Team Sweeps All Titles at Sofia International Badminton Invitational
Author: Wang Jinbo
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 3

The Chinese badminton team concluded the Sofia International Badminton Invitational today, securing all the championships.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Sofia on November 19, the Chinese team won all the titles in the tournament. Lin Yanfen of China defeated Chernyavskaya of the Soviet Union 2-0 to claim the women's singles title. In the men's singles, Liang Zhigang of China defeated Yu Lizhi to win the championship. The men's doubles final saw China's Yu Lizhi and Zheng Shutai defeat Zhuyev and Uvarov of the Soviet Union 2-0 to take the title. In the women's doubles final, Lin Yanfen and Zhang Wanling of China defeated Diana of Bulgaria and Chernyavskaya of the Soviet Union 2-0 to secure the championship. Wu Chibing and Lin Yanfen of China won the mixed doubles title.

### 索非亚国际羽毛球邀请赛　中国队囊括全部冠军
王金柏
1989-11-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　索非亚国际羽毛球邀请赛
　　中国队囊括全部冠军
    据新华社索非亚11月19日电　（记者王金柏）中国羽毛球队今天在这里结束的索非亚国际羽毛球邀请赛夺得了全部冠军。
    中国的林燕芬以2∶0战胜了苏联的切尔妮亚夫斯卡，荣获女子单打冠军；男子单打，中国运动员梁志强战胜余立志夺得冠军。男子双打决赛，中国的余立志／郑寿泰以2∶0胜苏联的朱耶夫／乌瓦罗夫夺得冠军。在女子双打决赛中，中国的林燕芬和张婉玲以2∶0战胜了保加利亚的迪阿娜和苏联的切尔妮亚夫斯卡，荣登冠军宝座。中国的吴赤兵／林燕芬获得混合双打的冠军。
