Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 3

Tokyo Sagawa Express Donates Vehicles to the Asian Games

BEIJING, Nov. 24 (Xinhua) -- On the afternoon of the 24th, Tokyo Sagawa Express (Co., Ltd.) held a vehicle donation ceremony for the 11th Asian Games at the Beijing Yuantan Sports Stadium.

The representative of Tokyo Sagawa Express (Co., Ltd.), Chairman and President Hiroshi Watanabe, presented a donation letter for 100 trucks to Zhang Baifa, the Executive Vice Chairman of the 11th Asian Games Organizing Committee and the Acting Mayor of Beijing. Zhang Baifa, on behalf of the 11th Asian Games Organizing Committee, awarded an honorary certificate and a souvenir to Watanabe and expressed gratitude for his support of the Asian Games.

### 日本东京佐川急便（株）向亚运会赠车

1989-11-25
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　日本东京佐川急便（株）向亚运会赠车
    本报讯　24日下午，日本东京佐川急便（株）向第11届亚运会赠车仪式在北京月坛体育馆举行。
    日本东京佐川急便（株）代表取缔役社长渡边广康先生向第11届亚运会组委会常务副主席、北京市常务副市长张百发递交了100辆货车的赠送函。张百发代表第11届亚运会组委会向渡边社长颁发荣誉证书和纪念品并对渡边广康先生给予亚运会的支持表示感谢。
