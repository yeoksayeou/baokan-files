Title: The Liuzhou City Export Commodities Exhibition Held in Bangkok
Author:
Date: 1989-11-21
Edition: 4

The Liuzhou City Export Commodities Exhibition was held in Bangkok on November 11.

The exhibition was held at the Guanghua Hall of the Thai Chinese Chamber of Commerce. The chairman of the Thai Chinese Chamber of Commerce, Zheng Mingru, the chairman of the Liuzhou City International Trade Promotion Commission, Yuan Dingguo, and the Chinese Ambassador to Thailand, Li Shichun, jointly cut the ribbon for the exhibition. The opening ceremony was lively, with hundreds of representatives from Thailand's industrial and commercial sectors in attendance. Yuan Dingguo and Zheng Mingru both spoke at the opening ceremony.

### 柳州市出口商品展销会在曼谷举行

1989-11-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　柳州市出口商品展销会在曼谷举行
    新华社曼谷电　中国广西柳州市出口商品展销会11日在曼谷开幕。
    展销会在泰国中华总商会光华厅举行。中华总商会主席郑明如、广西省柳州市国际贸易促进委员会会长袁定国和中国驻泰国大使李世淳为展销会联合剪彩。开幕式气氛热烈，泰国工商各界代表数百人光临。袁定国和郑明如先后在开幕式上讲话。
