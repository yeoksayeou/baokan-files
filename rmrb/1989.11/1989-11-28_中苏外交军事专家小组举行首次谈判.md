Title: Sino-Soviet Diplomatic and Military Experts Group Holds First Round of Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1989-11-28
Edition: 4

Sino-Soviet diplomatic and military experts group held its first round of negotiations in Moscow from November 13 to 27. The discussions focused on the guiding principles for mutual reduction of military forces in the Sino-Soviet border regions and strengthening trust in the military domain.

The Chinese delegation was led by Liu Guangzhi, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Chinese Foreign Ministry. The Soviet delegation was headed by Gilyev, Director of the Department of Asian Socialist Countries of the Soviet Foreign Ministry.

Both sides agreed that reducing military forces in the Sino-Soviet border regions and enhancing mutual trust are of great significance for the development of neighborly relations between the two countries.

The discussions were conducted in a serious and constructive manner.

Deputy Foreign Minister Karpov and Luo Gaosou of the Soviet Union met with the Chinese delegation separately. Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Yu Hongliang participated in the meetings. Soviet defense officials also held talks with the Chinese delegation.

Both sides agreed that the negotiations will continue in Beijing.

### 中苏外交军事专家小组举行首次谈判

1989-11-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中苏外交军事专家小组举行首次谈判
    新华社莫斯科11月27日电　中苏外交军事专家小组11月13日至27日在莫斯科举行了首次谈判，就在中苏边境地区相互裁减军事力量和在军事领域加强信任的指导原则问题进行了讨论。小组中方组长为中国外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘广志，苏方组长为苏联外交部亚洲社会主义国家局局长吉列耶夫。
    双方一致认为，裁减两国在中苏边境地区的军事力量，加强信任，对发展两国睦邻关系具有重要意义。
    讨论是本着认真和建设性的精神进行的。
    苏联外交部副部长卡尔波夫和罗高寿分别会见了中方代表，中国驻苏联大使于洪亮参加了会见。苏联国防部官员同中方代表进行了座谈。
    双方商定谈判将在北京继续进行。
