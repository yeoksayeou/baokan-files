Title: Citizens of Tehran Hold Anti-American Protest
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 3

		Thousands of Iranian citizens, mostly students, held an anti-American protest march in Tehran today to commemorate the 10th anniversary of the seizure of the U.S. Embassy in Iran.

		The protest was jointly organized by the Islamic Association of Iranian Students, the Iranian Ministry of Information, and several religious organizations.

		The protesters gathered outside the former U.S. Embassy, which Iranians call the "spy den," holding signs with slogans such as "Death to America" and chanting anti-American slogans.

		Ali Akbar Mohtashamipour, a hardliner and former Iranian interior minister, addressed the protesters, calling for continued anti-American struggle. The protesters also burned 160 American flags.

		A 14-point resolution read at the rally stated that Iranians should follow the leadership of the current Supreme Leader of Iran, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, and adhere to a policy of no compromise with the United States.

		The resolution also warned certain "liberal elements" who are preparing for the U.S. to return to Iran, and criticized those who, under the guise of rebuilding Iran's economy, want to place the Islamic Republic of Iran under the control of Western countries.

### 伊朗德黑兰市民举行反美示威

1989-11-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊朗德黑兰市民举行反美示威
    新华社德黑兰11月4日电　伊朗数万名市民，其中主要是学生，今天在德黑兰举行反美示威游行，纪念占领美国驻伊朗大使馆10周年。
    这次示威活动是由伊朗大学生伊斯兰协会联盟、伊朗新闻部和一些宗教组织联合组织的。
    示威群众聚集在被伊朗人称为“间谍窝”的美国大使馆外面，手举“让美国死亡”等标语牌，高呼反美口号。
    伊朗强硬派人物、前内政部长阿里·阿克巴尔·穆赫塔希米在那里对示威群众发表了讲话，他号召人们继续反美斗争。示威群众还焚烧了160面美国国旗。
    在集会上宣读的一项14点决议书说，伊朗人要遵从现伊朗最高领导人哈梅内伊的领导，坚持不向美国作任何妥协的路线。
    决议还严厉警告一些为美国重返伊朗作准备的“自由化分子”，并攻击一些人以伊朗经济重建为由想要把伊朗伊斯兰共和国置于西方国家的控制下。
