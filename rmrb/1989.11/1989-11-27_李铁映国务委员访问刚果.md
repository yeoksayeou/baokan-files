Title: State Councilor Li Tieying Visits Congo
Author:
Date: 1989-11-27
Edition: 4

State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieying held a working meeting with Abibi, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Congolese Labor Party, on November 22 in Brazzaville.

At the welcome banquet hosted by Abibi for the Chinese delegation, Li Tieying said that the two countries are carrying out various forms of cooperation in the fields of politics, economy, culture, and education, which have been fruitful. He wished the Congolese people greater achievements under the leadership of Congolese President Sassou-Nguesso.

### 李铁映国务委员访问刚果

1989-11-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　李铁映国务委员访问刚果
    新华社布拉柴维尔电　中国国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映11月22日在这里同刚果劳动党中央政治局委员阿比比举行了工作会谈。
    在阿比比举行的欢迎中国代表团的晚宴上，李铁映说，两国在政治、经济、文化和教育等方面进行着多种形式的和富有成效的合作。他祝愿刚果人民在刚果总统萨苏—恩格索的领导下，取得更大的成就。
