Title: China Wins Most Gold Medals at Asian Rowing Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 5th.

The third Asian Rowing Championships concluded today at Sukhna Lake in northwest India. The Chinese team secured 7 gold medals out of the 14 events, placing first in the gold medal count and breaking one Asian record.

The Democratic People's Republic of Korea ranked second, and Japan came in third. The host nation, India, had a significant haul of silver medals, totaling 7, but only managed to win one gold medal, placing them fourth.

### 亚洲赛艇锦标赛我队获金牌第一

1989-11-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲赛艇锦标赛我队获金牌第一
    新华社新德里11月5日电  第三届亚洲赛艇锦标赛今天在印度西北部的苏克哈纳湖结束，中国队在全部14个项目的争夺中夺得7枚金牌，获金牌数第一，并打破了一项亚洲纪录。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队排名第二；日本队排名第三。东道主印度队银牌大丰收，共获7枚，但金牌只有一枚，排名第四。
