Title: Zhuhai Helicopter Airport Completed
Author: Zhang Yusheng
Date: 1989-11-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Zhuhai, November 22nd

The most advanced helicopter airport in China to date has recently been completed and put into use in Zhuhai.

The Zhuhai Helicopter Airport covers an area of 210 mu (approximately 35 acres) and can accommodate 30 helicopters. The airport features a 450-meter-long and 30-meter-wide runway, equipped with modern electronic tracking, instrument landing, and other communication and navigation systems.

### 珠海直升机场竣工
张愈升
1989-11-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　珠海直升机场竣工
    新华社珠海11月22日电　（记者张愈升）我国目前设备最先进的直升机场，近日在珠海竣工并投入使用。
    珠海直升机场占地210亩，可容纳30架直升飞机。机场还有长450米，宽30米的跑道，并装备了现代化的电子跟踪、盲降等通信导航系统。
