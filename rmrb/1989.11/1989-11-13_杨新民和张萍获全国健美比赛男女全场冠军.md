Title: Yang Xinmin and Zhang Ping Win National Bodybuilding Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-11-13
Edition: 3

Yang Xinmin from Shandong and Zhang Ping from Shanghai tonight won the men's and women's overall championships respectively at the National Bodybuilding Championships held here.

### 杨新民和张萍获全国健美比赛男女全场冠军

1989-11-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    杨新民和张萍获全国健美比赛男女全场冠军
    新华社贵阳11月11日电　山东选手杨新民和上海选手张萍今晚在这里分别获全国健美冠军赛男、女全场冠军。
