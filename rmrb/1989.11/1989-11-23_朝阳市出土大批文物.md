Title: A Large Number of Cultural Relics Unearthed in Chaoyang City
Author:
Date: 1989-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, November 22nd.

During recent maintenance work on the North Pagoda in Chaoyang City, Liaoning Province, hundreds of precious Buddhist relics were discovered from the underground palace and the upper palace within the pagoda. Experts have pointed out that these relics, which include stone sutra pillars, relic gold pagodas, gilded silver pagodas, sutra pagodas, glass bottles, and silver bodhi trees, are of various kinds. Some of these relics are the first of their kind to be found in the country, making this a significant discovery in Chinese religious archaeology since the discovery at Famen Temple in Shaanxi Province.

### 朝阳市出土大批文物

1989-11-23
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　朝阳市出土大批文物
    据新华社沈阳十一月二十二日电　辽宁省朝阳市的北塔在近年的维修过程中，先后从塔内的地宫和天宫中发现数百件珍贵佛教文物。有关专家指出，这批包括石经幢、舍利金塔、鎏金银塔、经塔、玻璃瓶、银菩提树等多种多样的佛教文物，有一些在国内属首次发现，是继陕西法门寺之后我国宗教考古的又一重大成果。
