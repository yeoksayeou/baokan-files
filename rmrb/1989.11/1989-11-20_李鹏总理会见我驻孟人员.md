Title: Premier Li Peng Meets Chinese Personnel in Bangladesh
Author:
Date: 1989-11-20
Edition: 4

Premier Li Peng and his wife, Zhu Lin, visited the Chinese Embassy in Dhaka this evening and met with embassy staff and other Chinese personnel in Bangladesh. Premier Li encouraged them to continue their efforts to expand Sino-Bangladesh friendship and cooperation.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army He Qizong, and Chinese Ambassador to Bangladesh Chen Songlu, along with all members of the delegation, attended the reception.

Li Peng spoke to the embassy staff and other Chinese personnel in Bangladesh, praising Sino-Bangladesh friendship and introducing the significance of the recently concluded Fifth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. He urged the Chinese personnel working in Bangladesh to strive for further strengthening of Sino-Bangladesh friendship and cooperation.

After the meeting, Li Peng posed for a group photo with the embassy staff, experts, and students.

### 李鹏总理会见我驻孟人员

1989-11-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    李鹏总理会见我驻孟人员
    新华社达卡11月18日电　李鹏总理及夫人朱琳今天晚上到中国大使馆，会见了使馆工作人员及中国在孟其他人员。李总理勉励他们为扩大中孟友谊和合作继续努力。
    外交部长钱其琛、对外经贸部长郑拓彬、中国人民解放军副总参谋长何其宗、中国驻孟大使陈嵩禄等全体代表团成员参加了接见。
    李鹏向使馆工作人员及在孟其他中国人员发表讲话，盛赞中孟友谊并介绍了最近闭幕的中共十三届五中全会的意义。他要求在孟加拉国工作的中国人员为进一步加强中孟友谊与合作而努力工作。
    会后，李鹏与使馆工作人员、专家和留学生合影留念。
