Title: White's Birthplace Commemorates White
Author: Yuán Róngshēng
Date: 1989-11-14
Edition: 4

The Norman Bethune Memorial Museum in Canada held a reception this afternoon in Gravenhurst, Ontario, the birthplace of the internationalist fighter Norman Bethune, to commemorate the 50th anniversary of his death.

Canadian federal parliamentarian Stan Darling and Chinese Ambassador to Canada Zhang Wenpao, Consul-General in Toronto Tang Fucuan, and others were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador Zhang Wenpao gave a brief speech at the reception, praising Bethune's contributions to the Chinese revolution, and expressing the belief that the friendship between the peoples of China and Canada will be further strengthened.

### 白求恩诞生地纪念白求恩  我驻加大使赞扬白求恩对中国革命的贡献
袁荣生
1989-11-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    白求恩诞生地纪念白求恩
    我驻加大使赞扬白求恩对中国革命的贡献
    新华社加拿大格雷文赫斯特镇11月12日电 （记者袁荣生）加拿大诺尔曼·白求恩纪念馆今天下午在白求恩诞生地安大略省的格雷文赫斯特镇举行招待会，纪念国际主义战士白求恩逝世50周年。
    加拿大联邦议员斯坦·达林和中国驻加拿大大使张文朴、驻多伦多总领事唐福泉等应邀出席了招待会。
    张文朴大使在招待会上发表了简短讲话，赞扬白求恩对中国革命做出的贡献，并表示相信中国和加拿大两国人民的友谊将会进一步加强。
