Title: Ticket Revenue Donated to the Asian Games
Author: Xú Jīngxīng
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 3

To welcome the 11th Asian Games, China Population Daily, Health Daily, Beijing Daily, Beijing Evening News, and other units, in collaboration with the Beijing Performance Company, are organizing a large-scale artistic performance titled "The Asian Games Are Coming to Us" from November 25 to December 4. The ticket revenue from the 8-day, 11-performance artistic event will be donated to the 11th Asian Games Organizing Committee. The image shows the performance at the Yuantan Sports Hall.

### 门票收入捐亚运会（图片）
徐京星
1989-11-29
第3版(综合)
专栏：

　　门票收入捐亚运会
    为迎接第十一届亚运会，中国人口报、健康报、北京日报、北京晚报等单位，协助北京市演出公司于11月25日至12月4日举办“亚运会向我们走来”大型文艺演出。8天11场文艺演出的门票收入将捐献给第11届亚运会筹委会。图为在月坛体育馆演出情况。　徐京星摄
