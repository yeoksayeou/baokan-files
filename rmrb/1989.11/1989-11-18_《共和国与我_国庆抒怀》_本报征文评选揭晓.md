Title: The Republic and Me — Reflections on National Day
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 1

The results of the essay contest "The Republic and Me — Reflections on National Day" have been announced. Twenty-one works have been selected.

Among them, two works received first prize: "A Voice for the Motherland" (Qi Yue, Beijing Broadcasting Institute) and "My Heart Remains in Old Border Ridge" (Huang Zili, West Valley County, Henan Province); four works received second prize: "My 'Garden of Eden'" (Hu Mingdao, Beijing Military Region General Hospital), "Let's Talk About Our 'Open-Air'" (Gao Zixuan, Fuxin Mining Bureau Haisi Open-Pit Coal Mine), "I Sewed the First National Flag" (Zhao Wenrui, Beijing), and "Standing on the 'Second Border Line'" (Xue Zhiqiang, Capital Garrison of the People's Armed Police); ten works received third prize; and five works received encouragement awards.

The essay contest organized by the editorial department of this newspaper received enthusiastic support and participation from a wide range of authors and readers. In less than two months, the editorial department received approximately five thousand submissions. We express our heartfelt thanks for this support.

### 《共和国与我——国庆抒怀》  本报征文评选揭晓

1989-11-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    《共和国与我——国庆抒怀》
    本报征文评选揭晓
    本报讯  《共和国与我——国庆抒怀》征文评选近日揭晓，二十一篇作品榜上有名。
    其中获一等奖两篇：《献给祖国的声音》（北京广播学院齐越）、《此心永在老界岭》（河南省西峡县黄自立）；二等奖四篇：《我心中的“伊甸园”》（北京军区总医院胡明道）、《说说咱
  “大露天”》（阜新矿务局海州露天煤矿高子轩）、《我缝第一面国旗》（北京赵文瑞）、《站在“第二国境线”上》（首都武警部队薛兆强）；三等奖十篇；鼓励奖五篇。
    本报总编室举办的这次征文活动，受到了广大作者、读者的热烈欢迎和支持。在前后不到两个月的时间里，编辑部共收到稿件约五千篇，在此，我们表示衷心的感谢。
