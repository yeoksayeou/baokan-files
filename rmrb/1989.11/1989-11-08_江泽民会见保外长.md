Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Bulgarian Foreign Minister
Author: Zhou Xiangguang
Date: 1989-11-08
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin Meets with Bulgarian Foreign Minister

BEIJING, Nov. 7 (Xinhua) -- CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with Bulgarian Foreign Minister Petar Mladenov and his delegation in the Zhongnanhai compound this morning.

Jiang Zemin expressed his welcome to Mladenov's visit to China and briefed him on China's reform and construction.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen was present at the meeting.

### 江泽民会见保外长
周象光
1989-11-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见保外长
    本报北京11月7日讯  记者周象光报道：今天上午，中共中央总书记江泽民在中南海会见了保加利亚外交部长佩特尔·姆拉德诺夫及其一行。
    江泽民总书记对姆拉德诺夫外长来华访问表示欢迎，并向他介绍了我国改革和建设的情况。
    会见时，外交部长钱其琛在座。
