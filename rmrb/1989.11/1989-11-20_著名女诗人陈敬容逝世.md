Title: Renowned Female Poet Chen Jingrong Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-11-20
Edition: 2

Renowned female poet, essayist, and translator Chen Jingrong passed away in Beijing on November 8 due to illness. She was 72 years old.

Chen Jingrong was born in 1917 in Leshan, Sichuan Province. In her early years, she was influenced by the New Culture Movement. Starting in 1935, she began publishing poetry and prose in newspapers such as the *Morning Post* and the *Dagongbao*. During the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression, she participated in the "Cultural Resistance" movement and, along with friends like Cao Senzhi, co-edited the monthly journal *Chinese New Poetry* and the poetry anthology *Forest Poetry Collection*. After liberation, she worked as an editor for publications such as *World Literature*, *People's Literature*, and *Poetry Magazine*. As one of the "Nine Leaves" poets, her poetry collections *Symphony Collection* and *Yingying Collection*, her essay collection *Star Rain Collection*, and her poetry collection from the new period *Time of Aging* have had a wide influence both domestically and internationally. Her translations of *The Hunchback of Notre Dame*, *The Report from the Gallows*, and *Andersen's Fairy Tales* have been reprinted numerous times and remain popular to this day.

### 著名女诗人陈敬容逝世

1989-11-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    著名女诗人陈敬容逝世
    新华社北京11月18日电　我国著名女诗人、散文家和翻译家陈敬容，因病于11月8日在京逝世，终年72岁。
    陈敬容1917年生于四川省乐山市，早年受到新文化的影响，1935年开始在《晨报》、《大公报》发表诗文。抗日战争时期，她参加了“文抗”，与友人曹辛之等共同创编《中国新诗》月刊及《森林诗丛》。解放后在《世界文学》、《人民文学》、《诗刊》等刊物从事编辑工作。作为“九叶”诗人之一，她的诗集《交响集》、《盈盈集》，散文集《星雨集》，以及新时期创作的诗集《老去的时间》，在国内外有着广泛的影响。她翻译的《巴黎圣母院》、《绞刑架下的报告》、《安徒生童话》一版再版，至今不衰。
