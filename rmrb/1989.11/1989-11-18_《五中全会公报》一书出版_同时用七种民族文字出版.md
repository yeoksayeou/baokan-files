Title: The Publication of the Bulletin of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Edition: 1

The book "Bulletin of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China" has been published by the People's Publishing House and will soon be available in bookstores across Beijing and the rest of the country.

In addition to the bulletin, the book also includes "Decision of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Approving the Resignation of Comrade Deng Xiaoping from the Position of Chairman of the Central Military Commission," "Letter from Comrade Deng Xiaoping Requesting to Resign from the Position of Chairman of the Central Military Commission," "Decision of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Adjusting the Composition of the Central Military Commission," "Decision of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Appointing Comrade Yang Baibing as a Member of the Secretariat," "Bulletin of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China," and "Bulletin of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Central Discipline Inspection Commission of the Communist Party of China." The book also features two reports on Comrade Deng Xiaoping's meeting with participants of the expanded meeting of the Military Commission and the expanded meeting of the Military Commission itself, as well as the November 11 People's Daily editorial titled "More Resolutely Implementing the Policies of Rectification and Deepening Reform." The book also includes brief biographies of Comrades Liu Huaqing and Yang Baibing.

This book is also published by the Nationalities Publishing House in Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, Korean, Zhuang, and Yi languages.

### 《五中全会公报》一书出版  同时用七种民族文字出版

1989-11-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    《五中全会公报》一书出版
    同时用七种民族文字出版
    新华社北京11月17日电　《中国共产党第十三届中央委员会第五次全体会议公报》一书已由人民出版社出版，近日将在北京和全国各地新华书店发行。
    书中除公报外，还汇辑了《中国共产党十三届五中全会关于同意邓小平同志辞去中共中央军事委员会主席职务的决定》、《邓小平同志请求辞去中央军委主席职务的信》、《中国共产党十三届五中全会关于调整中共中央军事委员会组成人员的决定》、《中国共产党十三届五中全会关于增补杨白冰同志为中央书记处书记的决定》、《中共中央顾问委员会第五次全体会议公报》、《中国共产党中央纪律检查委员会第五次全体会议公报》，还收录了邓小平同志会见参加军委扩大会议的同志和有关军委扩大会议的两篇报道，以及11月11日人民日报题为《更加坚定地贯彻治理整顿和深化改革的方针》的社论。书中并附有刘华清、杨白冰二同志的简历。
    这本书同时由民族出版社用蒙古、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、朝鲜、壮、彝七种文字出版。
