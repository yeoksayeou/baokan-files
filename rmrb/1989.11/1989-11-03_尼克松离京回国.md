Title: Nixon Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author: Ding Gang
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 2

	Nixon Leaves Beijing to Return Home

	Beijing, November 2 (Xinhua) - Reporter Ding Gang reports: Former U.S. President Richard Nixon concluded his visit to China today and departed from Beijing to return home. Vice Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu saw him off at the airport.

	Before boarding the plane, Nixon said that this visit to China was successful and he felt very satisfied.

	Foreign Ministry spokesman Li Zhaoxing, at today's press conference, answered questions from foreign journalists regarding Nixon's visit to China. He said that Mr. Nixon came to China at a time when Sino-American relations faced serious difficulties. This visit is conducive to increasing mutual understanding and is undoubtedly beneficial for restoring and developing normal relations between China and the United States.

### 尼克松离京回国
丁刚
1989-11-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    尼克松离京回国
    本报北京11月2日讯　记者丁刚报道：美国前总统理查德·尼克松结束了对我国的访问，今天上午离京回国。外交部副部长刘华秋到机场送行。
    临上飞机时，尼克松说，这次访华是成功的，他感到非常满意。
    外交部发言人李肇星在今天下午的新闻发布会上，回答了外国记者有关尼克松访华的提问。他说，尼克松先生是在中美关系遇到严重困难时来华访问的。这次访问有利于增进相互了解，对恢复和发展中美之间的正常关系无疑是有益的。
