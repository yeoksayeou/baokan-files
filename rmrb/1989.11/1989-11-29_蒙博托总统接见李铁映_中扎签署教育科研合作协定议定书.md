Title: President Mobutu Receives Li Tieying; China and Zaire Sign Educational and Scientific Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kinshasa, November 26th.

President Mobutu of Zaire today received Li Tieying, a member of the State Council and Minister of the State Education Commission, who is currently visiting Zaire, at his presidential yacht in the suburbs of Kinshasa. The two had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Mobutu said in the conversation that Zaire and China are two friendly countries, and the relationship between the two countries is the best in Zaire's foreign relations.

Li Tieying handed over a letter from Chinese President Yang Shangkun to Mobutu. At the same time, Li Tieying also briefed him on the current situation in China.

Xinhua News Agency, Kinshasa, November 27th.

The supplementary agreement on cooperation in higher education and scientific research between China and Zaire was signed here today.

Wang Mingda, Vice Minister of the State Education Commission of China, and Lombea Bosongo, Minister of State for Higher Education and Scientific Research of Zaire, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments. Li Tieying, a member of the State Council and Minister of the State Education Commission of China, who is currently visiting Zaire, and An Guozheng, Chinese Ambassador to Zaire, attended the signing ceremony.

### 蒙博托总统接见李铁映　中扎签署教育科研合作协定议定书

1989-11-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　蒙博托总统接见李铁映
　　中扎签署教育科研合作协定议定书
    据新华社金沙萨11月26日电　扎伊尔总统蒙博托今天在金沙萨郊区的总统游艇上接见了正在这里访问的中国国务委员、国家教委主任李铁映，并同他进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    蒙博托在交谈中说，扎伊尔和中国是两个友好国家，两国间的关系是扎伊尔对外关系中最良好的。
    李铁映向蒙博托转交了中国国家主席杨尚昆的一封信。同时，李铁映还向他介绍了中国目前的局势。
    新华社金沙萨11月27日电　中国和扎伊尔关于高等教育和科学研究合作协定议定书今天在这里签字。
    中国国家教委副主任王明达和扎伊尔高等教育和科学研究国务委员隆贝亚·博松戈分别代表本国政府在议定书上签字。正在这里访问的中国国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映和中国驻扎伊尔大使安国政出席了签字仪式。
