Title: The Soviet Union Will Significantly Reduce Defense Spending
Author:
Date: 1989-11-07
Page: 3

Soviet Deputy Defense Minister Shabanov said in an interview with a TASS reporter on the 3rd that the Soviet Union's defense budget for 1990 will be reduced by 8.3% compared to 1989.

He said that this is the first time the Soviet Union has made such a significant reduction in military spending.

According to official Soviet materials, the Soviet Union's defense spending in 1989 was 77.3 billion rubles (approximately $11.89 billion), and it will be reduced to 70.9 billion rubles (approximately $10.9 billion) in 1990.

### 苏联将大幅度削减国防开支

1989-11-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联将大幅度削减国防开支
    苏联国防部副部长沙巴诺夫三日对塔斯社记者发表谈话说，苏联一九九○年的国防预算将比一九八九年减少百分之八点三。
    他说，苏联对军费开支进行如此大幅度的削减还是第一次。
    据苏联官方公布的材料，一九八九年苏联的国防开支为七百七十三亿卢布（约合一千一百八十九亿美元），一九九○年将减为七百零九亿卢布（约合一千零九十亿美元）。
