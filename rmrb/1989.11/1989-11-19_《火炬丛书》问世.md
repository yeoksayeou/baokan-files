Title: The Torch Series Published
Author: Yuan Xi
Date: 1989-11-19
Edition: 3

The Torch Series was published. The Sichuan Literature and Art Publishing House and the Communist Youth League of Chongqing City presented the newly published "Torch Series" to the youth of the capital today. Comrade Nie Rongzhen inscribed the following words for the publication of this book: "Learn from the heroes, love the Communist Party of China, love socialist motherland, and love the People's Liberation Army of China!"

The book-giving ceremony was held in the Yunnan Hall of the Great Hall of the People. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairwoman of the All-China Women's Federation Chen Mu-hua, as well as Liu Zhongde, Xu Weicheng, and Li Yuanchao, attended the ceremony. They praised the editing and publishing of the "Torch Series" as a good deed for the youth.

### 《火炬丛书》问世
袁晞
1989-11-19
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    《火炬丛书》问世
    本报11月18日讯　四川文艺出版社和共青团重庆市委今天把新出版的《火炬丛书》赠送给首都青少年。聂荣臻同志为此书出版题词：“学习英烈，热爱中国共产党，热爱社会主义祖国，热爱中国人民解放军！”
    赠书仪式在人民大会堂云南厅举行。人大常委会副委员长、全国妇联主席陈慕华以及刘忠德、徐惟诚、李源潮等出席了赠书仪式。他们称赞《火炬丛书》的编辑出版为青少年作了一件好事。　（袁晞）
