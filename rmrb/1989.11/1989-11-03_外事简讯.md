Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-11-03
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Li Tieying met with the president of the New Zealand Red Cross, Jane Cockburn, and her delegation at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of November 2.

The Czechoslovakian Foreign Minister, Jaromir Johanes, who is currently visiting Beijing, said on November 2 that his meetings with Chinese leaders and his talks with Foreign Minister Qian Qichen were "fruitful" and would help promote the friendly cooperative relationship between Czechoslovakia and China.

He expressed satisfaction with the rapid development of friendly cooperative relations between the two countries in recent years at a press conference held in the afternoon of November 2.

When talking about the reforms taking place in Czechoslovakia, Johanes said that the reforms are aimed at consolidating socialism and fully utilizing the potential of socialism.

The "1990 Trade Agreement between China and Mongolia" was signed in Beijing on November 2. Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, Li Lanqing, and the First Vice Minister of the Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Economic Supply, Gantulga, signed the agreement.

On November 1, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Sima Yi Aimaidi, met with the Iraqi Islamic delegation at the Great Hall of the People.

On the evening of November 1, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, Zhou Peiyuan, met and hosted a banquet for the Czechoslovakian Peace Committee delegation led by Chairman Bedrich Svestka at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

### 外事简讯

1989-11-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员李铁映2日晚在人民大会堂会见了新西兰红十字会会长简·考克布恩一行。
    ▲正在北京访问的捷克斯洛伐克外长雅罗米尔·约翰内斯2日说，他同中国领导人的会见和同钱其琛外长的会谈是“富有成效”的，这将有助于进一步推动捷、中友好合作关系的发展。
    他在2日下午举行的记者招待会上表示，对最近几年来，捷、中两国友好合作关系的迅速发展感到满意。
    谈到捷国内进行的改革时，约翰内斯说，改革是为了巩固社会主义，使社会主义的潜力得到充分发挥。
    ▲“中蒙1990年度贸易议定书”2日在北京签署。经贸部副部长李岚清和蒙古对外经济供应部第一副部长高陶布在议定书上签字。
    ▲全国政协副主席司马义·艾买提1日在人民大会堂会见了伊拉克伊斯兰代表团。
    ▲全国政协副主席、中国人民争取和平与裁军协会会长周培源1日晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见和宴请了以主席贝德日赫·什维斯特卡为团长的捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会代表团一行。
              （新华社）
