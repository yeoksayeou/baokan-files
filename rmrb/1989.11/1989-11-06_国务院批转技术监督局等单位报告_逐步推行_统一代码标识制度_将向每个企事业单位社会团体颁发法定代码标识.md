Title: The State Council Approves and Transmits the Report from the Bureau of Technical Supervision and Other Units
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 2

The State Council recently issued a notice approving and transmitting the report from the State Bureau of Technical Supervision and ten other units regarding the establishment of a unified code identification system for enterprises, institutions, and social organizations.

The State Council's notice states: Establishing a unified code identification system for enterprises, institutions, and social organizations is a reform to enhance the overall effectiveness of the national supervision and management system and strengthen management.

The report approved by the State Council points out: The "unified code identification system" involves issuing a unique, permanent legal code identification to each enterprise, institution, and social organization nationwide. This system provides government departments with effective technical means to strengthen administrative management and supervise the social and economic activities of various enterprises, institutions, and social organizations. It also ensures the technical feasibility of computerized automated management and information exchange, laying the technical foundation for improving the national supervision and management system and enhancing its overall effectiveness.

Currently, documents related to industrial and commercial enterprise registration, organizational code management, social organization registration, bank account management, taxation, social security, and other aspects, as well as planning work, statistical work, material allocation, financial allocation, and intellectual property registration, all urgently need the use of a unified code identification. This is an urgent need for improving the economic environment and rectifying economic order.

The compilation, overall control, management, maintenance, and database construction of the national enterprise, institution, and social organization code identification will be handled by the China Standardization and Information Classification Coding Research Institute under the State Bureau of Technical Supervision. The issuance of code identifications will be handled by the industrial and commercial administrative, personnel, and civil affairs departments at the county level and above. The utilization of code identifications will be enforced by the banking, taxation, planning, statistics, finance, and materials departments.

To carry out this work in an orderly and phased manner, the State Bureau of Technical Supervision plans to approve and issue the national standard "Code Compilation Rules for National Enterprises, Institutions, and Social Organizations" and implement it starting this December. The plan is to first implement this in the industrial and commercial administrative, personnel, and civil affairs departments, in conjunction with enterprise legal person registration changes, institutional code cleaning and rectification, and social organization approval and registration. The provinces of Hunan, Tianjin, Chongqing, and the city of Shenzhen will be selected for pilot implementation to gain experience and gradually promote the system. The goal is to fully implement the "unified code identification system" nationwide within three years or a slightly longer period.

### 国务院批转技术监督局等单位报告  逐步推行“统一代码标识制度”  将向每个企事业单位社会团体颁发法定代码标识

1989-11-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国务院批转技术监督局等单位报告
    逐步推行“统一代码标识制度”
    将向每个企事业单位社会团体颁发法定代码标识
    新华社北京11月5日电  国务院日前发出通知批转国家技术监督局等十个单位关于建立企业、事业单位和社会团体统一代码标识制度的报告。
    国务院通知称：建立企业、事业单位和社会团体统一代码标识制度，是国家发挥监督管理体系整体效能、强化管理的一项改革。
    国务院批转的报告指出：“统一代码标识制度”就是给每一个企业、事业单位和社会团体颁发一个在全国范围内唯一的、始终不变的法定代码标识。它为政府各部门加强行政管理，监督各企业、事业单位和社会团体的社会经济行为提供有效的技术手段，为实现计算机自动化管理和信息交换提供技术保证，为完善我国监督管理体系，发挥整体效能建立技术基础。
    目前，我国工商企业登记、机构编制管理、社会团体登记、银行帐户管理、税收、社会安全等方面的文件，以及计划工作、统计工作、物资调拨、财政拨款、知识产权登记等，都迫切需要使用统一的代码标识。这是当前治理经济环境、整顿经济秩序的急需。
    全国企业、事业单位和社会团体代码标识的编制、整体控制、管理、维护及建库工作，由国家技术监督局直属中国标准化与信息分类编码研究所负责；代码标识的颁发，由县级以上（含县级）各级工商行政管理、人事和民政等部门负责；代码标识的利用，由银行、税务、计划、统计、财政、物资等部门负责强制推行。
    为有计划、有步骤地开展这项工作，国家技术监督局拟批准发布《全国企业、事业单位和社会团体代码编制规则》国家标准，并于今年12月开始实施。拟首先在工商行政管理、人事和民政部门推行，结合企业法人登记换照，事业机构编制清理整顿和社团核准登记开展这项工作；选择湖南省、天津市、重庆市、深圳市四地进行试点，取得经验，逐步推广。力争用三年或更长一点时间在全国范围内全面实行“统一代码标识制度”。
