Title: South Korean Military Provokes in Demilitarized Zone
Author:
Date: 1989-11-27
Page: 4

According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency, on the 25th, the South Korean military fired shots towards the North in the Demilitarized Zone, engaging in serious provocative activities.

The report stated that on the morning of the 25th, several South Korean soldiers secretly entered the southern side of the Demilitarized Zone in Kumhwa County's Kŭmdong-ri, firing dozens of rounds from a large-caliber machine gun towards the North. This action put the lives of North Korean personnel on duty in serious danger.

The report said that this was a planned provocation by the South Korean side to escalate tensions on the Korean Peninsula.

### 南朝鲜军队在非军事区进行挑衅

1989-11-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南朝鲜军队在非军事区进行挑衅
    据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，南朝鲜军队二十五日在非军事区向北方开枪，进行严重的挑衅活动。
    据报道，二十五日上午，数名南朝鲜军人秘密进入军事分界线非军事区南方一侧的金化郡芹洞里，用大口径机枪向北方发射了数十发子弹，使北方执勤人员的生命受到严重威胁。
    报道说，这是南朝鲜方面有计划地激化朝鲜半岛紧张局势的挑衅活动。
