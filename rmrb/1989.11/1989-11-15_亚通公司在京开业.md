Title: Asia Communications Company Opens in Beijing
Author: Yè Zǐ
Date: 1989-11-15
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 14th.

    Asia Communications Company Opens in Beijing

    A joint venture between the Chinese Academy of Sciences Satellite Communications Technology Development Company and Singapore's Science and New Technology Limited, the Beijing Asia Satellite Communications Technology Company, officially opened in Beijing today.

    The company will primarily engage in the research, development, production, and sales of satellite television reception antennas and satellite communication antennas.

### 亚通公司在京开业
叶紫
1989-11-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    亚通公司在京开业
    本报北京十一月十四日讯　记者叶紫报道：由中科院卫星通信技术开发公司和新加坡科新技术有限公司合资经营的北京亚洲卫星通信技术有限公司今天在京开业。
    这家公司主要从事卫星电视接收天线和卫星通信天线的研制、生产和销售。
