Title: Moscow City Committee First Secretary Replaced
Author:
Date: 1989-11-25
Edition: 4

At the full meeting of the Moscow City Committee held on the 21st, Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Politburo member and Secretary Zaykov was relieved of his duties as the First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee and transferred to the position of First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Defense Council.

Soviet Communist Party General Secretary Gorbachev attended the meeting and delivered a speech.

The former Second Secretary of the Moscow City Committee, Prokofyev, was elected as the new First Secretary.

### 莫斯科市委第一书记易人

1989-11-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　莫斯科市委第一书记易人
    在二十一日举行的莫斯科市委全体会议上，苏共中央政治局委员、中央书记扎伊科夫被解除了莫斯科市委第一书记的职务，调任苏联国防委员会第一副主席。
    苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫出席了会议，并发表了讲话。
    原莫斯科市委第二书记普罗科菲耶夫当选市委第一书记。
