Title: State Council Appoints and Dismisses State Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-11-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 17th.

The State Council recently appointed and dismissed a number of state officials.

Xu Shaohai was appointed as the Consul General to São Paulo, and Luo Lipeng as the Consul General to Marseille.

Liu Shuqing was relieved of his position as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Huang Zhiliang was relieved of his position as Consul General to São Paulo, Zhang Enyang was relieved of his position as Consul General to Marseille, and Yu Zhizhong was relieved of his position as Deputy Representative to the United Nations Office at Geneva and other international organizations in Switzerland.

### 国务院任免国家工作人员

1989-11-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国务院任免国家工作人员
    新华社北京11月17日电　国务院最近任免一批国家工作人员。
    任命徐绍海为驻圣保罗总领事，罗丽鹏为驻马赛总领事。
    免去刘述卿的外交部副部长职务，黄志良的驻圣保罗总领事职务，张恩扬的驻马赛总领事职务，俞志忠的常驻联合国日内瓦办事处和瑞士其他国际组织副代表职务。
