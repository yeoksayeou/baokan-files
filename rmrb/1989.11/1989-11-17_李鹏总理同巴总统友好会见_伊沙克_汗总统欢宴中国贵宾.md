Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with Pakistani President in a Friendly Meeting
Author: Shǐ Zōngxīng, Rèn Yùjùn
Date: 1989-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Islamabad, November 15th.

Premier Li Peng and his wife, Mrs. Zhu Lin, held a friendly meeting with Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan and his wife at the presidential palace this evening. Premier Li Peng expressed his great pleasure in meeting his old friend, President Ishaq Khan.

President Ishaq Khan warmly welcomed Premier Li Peng's visit, stating: "Your visit will be another milestone in Pak-China friendship. I am very pleased to hear that you and Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto have reached broad consensus in your talks."

Premier Li Peng said, "My two meetings with Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto were very successful. The deep friendship of the Pakistani people has deeply moved us. I believe that no matter what happens, the friendship between China and Pakistan will last forever." Premier Li Peng also briefed President Ishaq Khan on the domestic situation and internal and external policies of China. President Ishaq Khan said, "Deng Xiaoping is a great statesman. His retirement from political life shows that he has fulfilled the principles he proposed. I believe his wise views and suggestions will continue to benefit the Chinese people."

Premier Li conveyed the greetings of Chairman Yang Shangkun to President Ishaq Khan and, on behalf of Chairman Yang Shangkun, invited President Ishaq Khan to visit China next year at his convenience. President Ishaq Khan happily accepted the invitation.

After the meeting, President Ishaq Khan held a grand banquet to welcome Premier Li Peng and his wife. The hosts and guests both gave toasts.

Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto and her husband, Asif Ali Zardari, attended the banquet to accompany the guests.

Accompanying the visit, Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin, and Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army He Qizong were invited to attend the banquet.

### 李鹏总理同巴总统友好会见  伊沙克·汗总统欢宴中国贵宾
史宗星  任毓骏
1989-11-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏总理同巴总统友好会见
    伊沙克·汗总统欢宴中国贵宾
    本报伊斯兰堡11月15日电　记者史宗星、任毓骏报道：李鹏总理和夫人朱琳今晚在这里的总统官邸分别同巴基斯坦总统伊沙克·汗和总统夫人举行了友好会见。李鹏总理对有机会见到中国的老朋友伊沙克·汗总统表示非常高兴。
    伊沙克·汗总统对李鹏总理来访表示热烈欢迎，他说：你的这次访问将是巴中友谊的又一座里程碑。听说你和贝·布托总理在会谈中取得了广泛的一致，我感到非常高兴。
    李鹏总理说，我和贝·布托总理的两次会谈很成功。巴基斯坦人民的深情厚谊使我们深受感动。我相信，不管发生什么事情，中巴友谊都将与世长存。李鹏总理还向伊沙克·汗总统通报了中国国内的情况和内外政策。伊沙克·汗总统说，邓小平是一位伟大的政治家。他退出政治生涯，说明他实现了自己提出来的原则。我相信他明智的见解和建议将继续造福于中国人民。
    李总理转达了杨尚昆主席对伊沙克·汗总统的问候，并代表杨尚昆主席邀请伊沙克·汗总统在明年方便的时候访华，伊沙克·汗总统愉快地接受了邀请。
    会见后，伊沙克·汗总统举行盛大宴会欢迎李鹏总理和夫人一行。宾主分别致了祝酒辞。
    贝·布托总理和丈夫阿里·扎尔达里出席宴会作陪。
    随同访问的中国外长钱其琛、对外经贸部长郑拓彬、中国人民解放军副总参谋长何其宗应邀出席了宴会。
