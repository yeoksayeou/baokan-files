Title: Li Peng Congratulates Norodom Sihanouk on His Birthday
Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 4th

Today evening, Premier Li Peng hosted a banquet at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to celebrate the 67th birthday of Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea.

Before the banquet, the hosts and guests engaged in friendly conversations. Prince Sihanouk reiterated his stance that the Cambodian issue requires a comprehensive political solution. He emphasized two key points: first, under the supervision of the United Nations, Vietnam must fully withdraw its troops; second, a quadripartite coalition government must be established in Cambodia. He stressed that these two points are the foundation for resolving the Cambodian issue and cannot be changed. He said that the Cambodian issue must be resolved comprehensively, not partially.

Prince Sihanouk also briefed Premier Li Peng on the recent joint efforts and coordinated actions of the three resistance forces in Cambodia.

Premier Li Peng expressed support for Prince Sihanouk's stance on a political solution to the Cambodian issue and congratulated the Cambodian resistance forces on their recent victories on the battlefield.

This morning, Assistant Foreign Minister Xu Dunxin, on behalf of Chairman Yang Shangkun and Comrade Deng Yingchao, presented a birthday cake and a floral basket to Prince Sihanouk, extending birthday wishes for his 67th birthday.

### 李鹏祝贺西哈努克寿辰  杨尚昆邓颖超派人向亲王赠送花篮
孙毅
1989-11-05
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏祝贺西哈努克寿辰
    杨尚昆邓颖超派人向亲王赠送花篮
    本报北京11月4日讯　记者孙毅报道：国务院总理李鹏今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆举行宴会，祝贺民柬主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的67岁寿辰。
    宴会前，宾主双方进行了友好地交谈。西哈努克亲王说，他仍然坚持自己提出的主张，即：柬埔寨问题需要全面地政治解决，一是要在联合国主持的国际监督之下，越南实现全面撤军；二是要在柬埔寨成立一个四方联合政府。他强调指出，这两条是解决柬埔寨问题的基础，这个基础是不能改变的。他说，柬埔寨问题必须是全面地解决，而不是部分地解决。
    西哈努克亲王还向李鹏总理介绍了最近柬埔寨三方抵抗力量在战场上团结合作、协调行动的斗争情况。
    李鹏总理表示支持西哈努克亲王关于政治解决柬埔寨问题的主张，并对柬埔寨抵抗力量最近在柬埔寨战场上取得的胜利表示祝贺。
    今天上午，外交部部长助理徐敦信受国家主席杨尚昆和邓颖超同志的委托，向西哈努克亲王赠送了生日蛋糕和花篮，对他的67岁寿辰表示祝贺。
