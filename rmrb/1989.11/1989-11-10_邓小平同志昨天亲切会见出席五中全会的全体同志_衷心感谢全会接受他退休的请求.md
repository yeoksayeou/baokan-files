Title: Deng Xiaoping Meets with All Attendees of the Fifth Plenum
Author: Xú Xīn Huá
Date: 1989-11-10
Edition: 1

Deng Xiaoping met with all attendees and non-voting participants of the Fifth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon and posed for a group photo with them.

When Deng Xiaoping arrived at the meeting hall accompanied by Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, Song Ping, Li Ruihuan, and Wang Zhen, the entire hall erupted in warm applause. Deng Xiaoping, beaming with a smile, waved to the crowd and said: "I appreciate your understanding and support. The plenary session has accepted my request to retire. I sincerely thank the plenary session and all of you."

### 邓小平同志昨天亲切会见出席五中全会的全体同志  衷心感谢全会接受他退休的请求
徐心华
1989-11-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    邓小平同志昨天亲切会见出席五中全会的全体同志
    衷心感谢全会接受他退休的请求
    新华社北京11月9日电  （记者徐心华）邓小平同志今天下午在人民大会堂亲切会见了出席和列席党的十三届五中全会的全体同志，同他们一起合影留念。
    当邓小平同志由江泽民、杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、乔石、姚依林、宋平、李瑞环、王震等同志陪同来到会见大厅时，全场响起了热烈的掌声。邓小平同志笑容满面地向大家挥手致意。他高兴地说：感谢同志们对我的理解和支持，全会接受了我退休的请求。衷心感谢全会，衷心感谢同志们。
