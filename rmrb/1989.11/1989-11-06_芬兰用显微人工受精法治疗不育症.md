Title: Finland Uses Micromanipulation Artificial Insemination to Treat Infertility
Author:
Date: 1989-11-06
Edition: 3

	Finland's Population Federation Infertility Treatment Center has successfully applied the micromanipulation artificial insemination method to treat infertility.

	This new method involves using a microscope to create a small hole in the outer membrane of an egg using acid or mechanical means, allowing sperm with poor motility to enter the egg and fertilize it. This method can treat infertility caused by male factors.

### 芬兰用显微人工受精法治疗不育症

1989-11-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    芬兰用显微人工受精法治疗不育症
    新华社赫尔辛基电　据芬通社10月31日报道，芬兰人口联盟不育症治疗中心应用显微人工受精法，治疗不育症获得成功。
    这种新方法是：在显微镜下用酸或机械手段使卵子的卵膜外壁形成微孔，以便让活动能力差的精子进入卵子，促使卵子受精。这种方法可治疗由于男性引起的不育症。
