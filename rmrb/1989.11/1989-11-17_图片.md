Title: A Couple Pays Respects at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier
Author: Zhang Yunwen
Date: 1989-11-17
Edition: 7

A newlywed couple from the Soviet Union visited the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Moscow's Red Square after their wedding ceremony to pay tribute to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives in World War II. This has become a tradition among Soviet youth.
