Title: The United States Agrees to Return a Batch of Iranian Assets
Author:
Date: 1989-11-08
Page: 3

The United States has agreed to return $5.67 billion in Iranian assets. U.S. officials stated that this decision is not directly related to the issue of American hostages being held in Lebanon, but it is the first concrete action taken by the Bush administration to ease U.S.-Iranian relations.

### 美国同意归还伊朗一批资产

1989-11-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国同意归还伊朗一批资产
    美国官方六日称，美国已同意把五点六七亿美元的伊朗资产归还伊朗。美国官员说，这项决定与美国被扣留在黎巴嫩的人质问题无直接关系，但这是布什政府缓和美伊关系的第一个具体行动。
