Title: Ministry of Machinery and Electronics Introduces a Batch of Energy-Saving Lamps
Author: Pi Shuyi
Date: 1989-11-15
Edition: 2

An 11-watt electronic high-efficiency energy-saving lamp has the same lighting effect as a 60-watt ordinary incandescent lamp. If 70 million ordinary incandescent lamps are replaced with these energy-saving lamps, the energy saved would be equivalent to the power generation capacity of a 3 million kilowatt power plant. Today, the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics in Beijing introduced a batch of energy-saving light source products, demonstrating the broad prospects for saving electricity in lighting.

This batch of energy-saving light source products includes: thin-tube fluorescent lamps, rare earth fluorescent lamps, electronic high-efficiency energy-saving lamps, high-pressure sodium lamps, and metal halide lamps, all of which have significant energy-saving effects. Electronic high-efficiency energy-saving lamps save 70-80% energy compared to ordinary incandescent lamps, and high-pressure sodium lamps have a luminous efficiency 5-6 times that of ordinary incandescent lamps. The electronic control compact-type energy-saving fluorescent lamps developed by Shaoxing Electronic Tube Factory and Fudan University's Light Source Research Institute have reached the level of the 1980s abroad and have begun to be exported in batches.

### 机电部推出一批节能灯
皮树义
1989-11-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    机电部推出一批节能灯
    本报北京11月14日讯　记者皮树义报道：一只11瓦的电子高效节能灯，照明效果相当于一只60瓦的普通电灯，如果有7000万只普通电灯（白炽灯）换成这种节能灯，节电效果就相当于一个装机容量300万千瓦电站的发电能力。机电部今天在京推出的一批节能光源产品展示了节约照明用电的广阔前景。
    这批节能光源产品包括：细管径荧光灯、稀土荧光灯、电子高效节能灯、高压钠灯、金属卤素灯等，节电效果都很显著。电子高效节能灯比普通电灯节电70—80％，高压钠灯发光效率是普通电灯的5—6倍。绍兴电子管厂与复旦大学电光源研究所研制的电控式紧凑型节能荧光灯，达到国外80年代水平，已开始批量出口。
