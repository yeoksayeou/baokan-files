Title: The All-China Women's Federation Sends a Congratulatory Message on the 7th Anniversary of the Founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1952-12-01
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. December 1st marks the 7th anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a congratulatory telegram. The telegram reads: Dear Mrs. Gordon and Mrs. Gukuli:
    We warmly congratulate you on the 7th anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Under the banner of the Women's International Democratic Federation, women and mothers who love peace all over the world are united to work together to defend world peace, oppose wars of aggression, and protect the rights of women and children, and have achieved great results. I wish you new achievements and victories in leading this noble cause.
    All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 国际妇联成立七周年  我全国妇联致电祝贺

1952-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国际妇联成立七周年
    我全国妇联致电祝贺
    【新华社三十日讯】十二月一日为国际民主妇女联合会成立七周年纪念日。中华全国民主妇女联合会特致电祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的戈登夫人暨古久里夫人：
    我们热烈祝贺国际民主妇联成立七周年纪念日。在国际民主妇联的旗帜下，团结着全世界爱好和平的妇女与母亲，共同为保卫世界和平、反对侵略战争、保护妇女与儿童的权利而努力工作，已经得到了很大的成绩。谨祝你们在领导这一崇高的事业中获得新的成就与胜利。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会
