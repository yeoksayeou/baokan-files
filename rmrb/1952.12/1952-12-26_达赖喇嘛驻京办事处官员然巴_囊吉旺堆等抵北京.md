Title: Dalai Lama's Beijing Office Official Rampa Namgyal Wangdu and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Six officials from the Dalai Lama's Beijing Office in Tibet, led by Director Rampa Namgyal Wangdu, and their entourage of 35 people, arrived in Beijing on December 24th. Greeting them at the station were Liu Chun, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Commission members Sa Kongliao, Weng Dujian, and Ma Yuhuai; Yang Jingren, Director of the General Office; Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director; Luo Wang, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office; Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director; students from the Central Institute for Nationalities and the Nationalities Class of the Central Youth League School, and representatives of lamas from Beijing temples, totaling more than 140 people.

As Director Rampa Namgyal Wangdu stepped off the train, he presented a Hada to Liu Chun, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, amidst warm applause from the welcoming crowd.

The officials of the Dalai Lama's Beijing Office in Tibet departed from Lhasa on September 15th, passing through Qamdo, Kangding, Ya'an, Chengdu, Chongqing, Hankou, and other places, and were warmly welcomed by the people along the way.

### 达赖喇嘛驻京办事处官员然巴·囊吉旺堆等抵北京

1952-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛驻京办事处官员然巴·囊吉旺堆等抵北京
    【新华社二十五日讯】西藏达赖喇嘛驻京办事处官员六人及随行人员共三十五人，在处长然巴·囊吉旺堆率领下，于十二月二十四日抵京。到站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘春，委员萨空了、翁独健、马玉槐，办公厅主任杨静仁、副主任甘春雷，班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺、副处长连慧珠，中央民族学院、中央团校民族班学员及北京市寺院喇嘛代表等共一百四十余人。
    然巴·囊吉旺堆处长下车后在欢迎行列所发出的热烈掌声中，向中央人民政府民族事务委员会刘春副主任委员献哈达。
    西藏达赖驻京办事处官员等于九月十五日自拉萨出发，途经昌都、康定、雅安、成都、重庆、汉口等地，沿途受到各地人民的热烈欢迎。
