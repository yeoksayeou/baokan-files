Title: Fedorov, Hanon, and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Fedorov, head of the Soviet film art workers delegation, members Cherkasov and six others, and Tamara Hanon and her party from the Soviet art troupe, returned to Beijing on the 15th and 16th, respectively. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Deputy Secretaries-General Li Zhanwu and Yan Baohang, Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Central People's Government Wang Lanxi, as well as dozens of people from film, literary, art circles, and representatives from various sectors, went to the station to welcome them. Shiford, the chief representative of the Soviet Film Export Company in China, and Zhukfov, a representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also went to the station to welcome them.
    Since leaving Beijing on November 15th, Fedorov and others have visited Shenyang, Changchun, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and other cities. After visiting Guangzhou, the Soviet film art workers delegation split into two groups: one group, consisting of Fedorov, Cherkasov, Ladynina, Smirnova, and three others, visited Changsha and Chairman Mao's hometown - Shaoshan Township, Xiangtan County, Hunan Province, as well as Wuhan; the other group, consisting of Cherkov, Petrov, Pavlov and three others, visited Kunming, Chongqing, and then went to Wuhan to meet with Fedorov and others. Fedorov's visits were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of people and literary and film workers in various regions.
    Tamara Hanon and her party left Beijing on November 15th and visited Xi'an, Kaifeng, Xuzhou, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Zhengzhou and other major cities. During her visits to various cities, Tamara Hanon performed Soviet ethnic songs and dances for the broad audience in our country about twenty times. Each of her performances was filled with great enthusiasm, often performing more than a dozen programs continuously. The people in various regions of our country unanimously praised her performances, which were rich in ethnic characteristics and possessed superb skills.

### 费道罗夫哈侬等返京

1952-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    费道罗夫哈侬等返京
    据新华社讯：苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫、团员契尔卡索夫等七人和苏联艺术工作团团员塔马拉·哈侬等一行，分别于十五日和十六日返抵北京。先后到车站欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞、副总干事李霑吾、阎宝航，中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，中央人民政府文化部电影局副局长王阑西，和电影、文学、艺术界人士及各界代表数十人。苏联影片输出公司驻中国总代表史福德和苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福也曾分别到车站欢迎。
    费道罗夫等从十一月十五日离开北京后，曾先后访问了沈阳、长春、天津、南京、上海、杭州、广州等城市。在访问广州后，苏联电影艺术工作者代表团又分为两路：一路为费道罗夫、契尔卡索夫、拉迪尼娜、斯米尔诺娃等四人，曾访问了长沙和毛主席故乡——湖南省湘潭县韶山乡以及武汉；一路为契尔柯夫、彼得罗夫、巴甫洛夫等三人，曾访问了昆明、重庆，又到武汉和费道罗夫等会合。费道罗夫等的访问，受到了各地广大人民群众和文艺及电影工作者的热烈欢迎。
    塔马拉·哈侬等一行从十一月十五日离开北京，先后访问了西安、开封、徐州、上海、杭州、南昌、广州、武汉、郑州等各大城市。塔马拉·哈侬在访问各城市时，先后为我国的广大观众表演苏联各民族歌舞达二十次左右。她每次表演都充满了高度的热情，常常是一次连续表演十几个节目。我国各地的人民群众对她的富于民族色彩和具有纯熟的技巧的表演，一致赞赏。
