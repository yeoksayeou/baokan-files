Title: Soviet Delegation Head to the UN General Assembly, Vyshinsky, Departs US for Home

Author:

Date: 1952-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 10th: Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation to the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly and Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, left New York for Moscow on December 9th. Those who came to see him off included Polish Minister of Foreign Affairs Skrzeszewski, head of the Czechoslovak delegation and Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Sekaninova, head of the Ukrainian Republic delegation Baranovs'kyi, head of the Byelorussian Republic delegation Kiselev, Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations Zorin, as well as many press representatives and members of the public.

    At the request of reporters from American newspapers, news agencies, television and radio stations, Vyshinsky delivered a brief speech. Vyshinsky said: "Although you asked me to say a few words about the upcoming holiday, I must talk about the daily situation, first of all about the work of the General Assembly." Vyshinsky pointed out: "The work of the United Nations General Assembly as a tool for peace is unsatisfactory. The resolution on the Korean question passed by the General Assembly proves this conclusion. Because the purpose of this plan is not to achieve peace, but to continue the war. The United Nations was established as a tool for maintaining peace. It can and must become such a tool. However, at present, it is far from a tool for peace. As for the Soviet Union, the Soviet delegation has and will continue to make every effort to make the United Nations a true tool for peace." Finally, Vyshinsky congratulated all American people who advocate peace on the upcoming holidays.

### 出席联大苏联代表团团长  维辛斯基离美返国

1952-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    出席联大苏联代表团团长
    维辛斯基离美返国
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社纽约十日讯：出席联合国第七届大会的苏联代表团团长、苏联外交部部长维辛斯基已于十二月九日离纽约返莫斯科。前往送行的人有波兰外交部部长斯克列杰夫斯基、捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长塞卡尼诺娃、乌克兰共和国代表团团长巴拉诺夫斯基、白俄罗斯共和国代表团团长基塞列夫、苏联驻联合国常任代表佐林以及许多报界代表和群众。
    维辛斯基应美国报纸的记者以及通讯社、电视广播台和广播电台的代表们的要求，发表了简短的谈话。维辛斯基说：“虽然你们要求我说几句关于行将来临的休假的话，可是我必须谈谈每天的情况，首先是关于大会的工作。”维辛斯基指出：“联合国大会作为一个和平工具来说，它的工作是不能令人满意的。大会通过的解决朝鲜问题的方案，就证明了这个结论。因为这个方案的目的不是获致和平，相反的却是要继续战争。联合国是作为维持和平的工具而建立的。它能够而且必须成为这样的工具。然而目前它却远不是一个和平的工具。至于苏联，苏联代表团已经并且将继续尽一切的努力，使联合国成为真正的和平工具。”维辛斯基最后祝贺一切拥护和平的美国人民欢度行将来临的假期。
