Title: The Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations Holds a Commemoration Meeting to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of Bulgaria's Dimitrov Constitution Day
Author:
Date: 1952-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a commemoration meeting in Beijing on the afternoon of the 5th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the Dimitrov Constitution Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations, Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wei Baoshan, Deputy Commissioner of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and more than 900 cadres from various agencies in the capital and representatives from all walks of life attended the meeting. Petkov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, and other members of the embassy also attended the meeting.

　　Hong Shen first delivered the opening speech. Then Chen Hansheng gave a speech on "The Constitution of Bulgaria." He discussed the creation and content of the Dimitrov Constitution and described the achievements of the Bulgarian people in building socialism. Afterwards, Ambassador Petkov delivered a speech, briefly introducing the Dimitrov Constitution. He pointed out that the Dimitrov Constitution is the fundamental law and guiding principle of Bulgaria in its socialist construction. It consolidates and guarantees the fraternal ties with the great Soviet Union, China, and the people's democratic countries.

　　Finally, the Bulgarian feature film "Dawn of the Motherland" was shown at the meeting.

### 我对外文化联络事务局举行纪念会  庆祝保加利亚季米特洛夫宪法日五周年

1952-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我对外文化联络事务局举行纪念会
    庆祝保加利亚季米特洛夫宪法日五周年
    【新华社讯】为庆祝保加利亚人民共和国季米特洛夫宪法日五周年纪念，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特于五日下午在北京举行纪念大会。参加大会的有对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、外交部苏联东欧司副专员魏宝善及首都各机关干部和各界人民代表九百余人。出席大会的还有保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使彼得科夫和大使馆其他人员。
    大会首由洪深致开会词。接着由陈翰笙作了“关于保加利亚宪法”的讲演。他论述了季米特洛夫宪法的创制经过和内容，并且叙述了保加利亚人民在建设社会主义中所获得的成就。随后，彼得科夫大使致词，扼要地介绍了季米特洛夫宪法。他指出：季米特洛夫宪法是保加利亚在社会主义建设中的根本大法和指导原则。它巩固并保证了与伟大的苏联、中国及人民民主国家的兄弟联系。
    大会最后并放映了保加利亚电影故事片“祖国的黎明”。
