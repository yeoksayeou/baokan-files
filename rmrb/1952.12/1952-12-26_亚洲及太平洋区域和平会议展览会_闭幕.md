Title: "Asia and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Exhibition" Closing

Author: 
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th:

    The "Asia and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Exhibition," organized by the China Peace Committee, has received unanimous praise since it opened on November 24th at the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park. Visitors included representatives from over ten countries attending the Asia and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Soviet experts, prominent Chinese peace advocates such as Soong Ching-ling, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and 64,074 members of the public. In order to transport the complete set of exhibition images for display in other locations, the exhibition concluded on the 24th of this month.

### “亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议展览会”闭幕

1952-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    “亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议展览会”闭幕
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会举办的“亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议展览会”，自十一月二十四日于中山公园中山堂展览以来，获得一致的好评。参观的有出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的十余国代表、苏联专家、中国著名和平人士宋庆龄、郭沫若、彭真等和群众六万四千零七十四人。为了将展览会全套图片运送外地展览，展览会已于本月二十四日结束展览。
