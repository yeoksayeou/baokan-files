Title: Soviet Peace Committee Elects Presidium

Author:

Date: 1952-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th: TASS Moscow, December 5th - The Soviet Peace Committee, newly elected by the Fourth All-Soviet Peace Conference, held a plenary meeting on the 5th. The meeting elected the Presidium of the Soviet Peace Committee. The outstanding peace fighter and famous Soviet poet Nikolai Tikhonov was elected Chairman; the Vice-Chairmen are: Academician Boris Grekov, writer Ilya Ehrenburg, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League Zoya Dumanova, and Soviet People's Artist Sergei Gerasimov. Journalist Mikhail Kotov was elected Executive Secretary of the committee.

### 苏拥护和平委员会选出主席团

1952-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏拥护和平委员会选出主席团
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：第四届全苏拥护和平大会新近选出的苏联拥护和平委员会，在五日召开了全体会议。会议选出了苏联拥护和平委员会主席团。卓越的和平战士、苏联名诗人尼古拉·吉洪诺夫当选为主席；副主席是：科学院院士鲍利斯·格列科夫，作家伊利亚·爱伦堡，列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记卓娅·杜马诺娃和苏联人民艺术家塞尔吉·格拉西莫夫。记者米哈伊尔·科托夫当选为委员会执行书记。
