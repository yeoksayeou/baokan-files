    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese Changchun Railway will soon be transferred from Soviet to Chinese management. Soviet experts serving on the Chinese Changchun Railway are unreservedly teaching all advanced technologies to Chinese technicians. Here, Soviet expert A.G. Remennikov is explaining the equipment of the wireless electric locomotive device to Chinese engineering personnel.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国长春铁路即将由苏联移交我国管理。在中国长春铁路服务的苏联专家毫无保留地将全部先进技术教给我国技术人员。在这里，苏联专家阿贺列面珂正向我国工程人员讲解无线电机车装置的设备情况。
