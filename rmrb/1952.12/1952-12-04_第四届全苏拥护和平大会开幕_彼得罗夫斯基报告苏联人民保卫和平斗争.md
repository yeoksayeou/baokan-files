Title: Opening of the Fourth All-Soviet Peace Congress: Petrovsky Reports on the Soviet People's Struggle to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1952-12-04
Page: 4

　　Fourth All-Soviet Peace Congress Opens
    Petrovsky Reports on the Soviet People's Struggle to Defend Peace
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd] According to TASS, Moscow: The Fourth All-Soviet Peace Congress opened on the evening of the 2nd in the Column Hall of the House of Unions in Moscow. Several hundred representatives of all nationalities from all over the Soviet Union attended the congress. They included famous Stakhanovite workers, advanced collective farmers, and celebrities from the cultural, scientific, and artistic circles. They are all people who contribute all their strength to the Soviet Union's peaceful communist construction, to consolidating world peace, and to ensuring the happiness of mankind. Religious figures were also among the representatives.
    The hall was magnificently decorated. Portraits of Lenin and Stalin hung on the rostrum. The words "Long Live International Peace!" glittered in gold on the deep red velvet curtain.
    The opening of the congress was presided over by Sergei Gerasimov, People's Artist of the Soviet Union. Then, amidst warm applause, the attendees elected the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by the great standard-bearer of peace, Stalin, as the honorary presidium of the congress.
    The congress unanimously adopted the following agenda: (1) Report: "The Soviet People's Struggle to Defend and Consolidate Peace"; (2) Election of the Soviet Peace Committee; (3) Election of delegates to the World Peace Congress; (4) Adoption of a letter of mandate to be given to the Soviet delegation attending the World Peace Congress.
    Ivan Petrovsky, President of Moscow State University and Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, then delivered a report on the first item of the agenda. The audience applauded warmly at the end of the report.

### 第四届全苏拥护和平大会开幕  彼得罗夫斯基报告苏联人民保卫和平斗争

1952-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　第四届全苏拥护和平大会开幕
    彼得罗夫斯基报告苏联人民保卫和平斗争
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：第四届全苏拥护和平大会于二日晚在莫斯科工会大厦圆柱大厅揭幕。出席大会的有来自苏联各地的各族人民的代表数百人。其中包括著名的斯塔哈诺夫式工人、先进集体农民和文化、科学、艺术界名流。他们都是为苏联的和平的共产主义建设、为巩固世界和平和保证人类幸福而贡献全部力量的人们。代表中还有宗教界人士。
    大厅布置得富丽堂皇。讲台上挂着列宁和斯大林的画像。深红色的天鹅绒的帷幕上闪耀着“国际和平万岁！”的金字。
    大会由苏联人民艺术家塞尔吉·格拉西莫夫主持开幕。接着到会者在热烈的掌声中选举了以伟大的和平旗手斯大林为首的苏联共产党中央委员会主席团为大会的名誉主席团。
    大会一致通过议程如下：（一）报告：“苏联人民为保卫和巩固和平而斗争”；（二）选举苏联拥护和平委员会；（三）选举出席世界人民和平大会的代表；（四）通过交给出席世界人民和平大会的苏联代表团的委托书。
    国立莫斯科大学校长、苏联科学院院士伊凡·彼得罗夫斯基接着在会上就议程的第一项作了报告。报告毕全场热烈鼓掌。
