Title: Romanian National Assembly Election Concludes Victoriously; People's Democratic Front Candidates Achieve Complete Victory
Author:
Date: 1952-12-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: Bucharest Dispatch: The Romanian People's Republic held elections for the National Assembly on November 30th. The candidates of the People's Democratic Front, led by the Romanian Workers' Party, achieved a complete victory. According to the election results announced by the Romanian Central Election Commission, out of 10,574,475 eligible voters, 10,353,469 people, or 98% of the electorate, participated in the election. Of these, 10,233,369 people, or 98.84% of those who voted, cast their ballots for the candidates of the People's Democratic Front. A total of 423 deputies were elected, including: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, Secretary Gheorghe Apostol, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Metallurgical and Chemical Industry Chivu Stoica, Minister of Armed Forces Emil Bodnăraş, as well as renowned Stakhanovite workers, advanced farmers, scientists, and cultural and artistic workers.

    On the day of the election, Romania was filled with surging political and labor enthusiasm everywhere, from cities to villages. In the capital Bucharest and the streets of various towns, and on buildings, festive decorations were displayed everywhere, with portraits of the leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government hanging, and slogans celebrating the election posted. From six o'clock in the morning, working people went to the polling stations in droves to vote. At the same time as the election, news of the Romanian working people's production achievements celebrating the election poured in. The "Maghiarász-Rákosi" factory in Bucharest completed its 1952 production plan on the eve of the election, with labor productivity increasing by an average of 16% per person. The famous Stakhanovite miner Gesa Kopaiting and his work team have already completed the production quota for the 1954 plan.

### 罗马尼亚国民议会选举胜利结束  人民民主阵线的候选人获得完全的胜利

1952-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国民议会选举胜利结束
    人民民主阵线的候选人获得完全的胜利
    【新华社四日讯】布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚人民共和国在十一月三十日举行了国民议会的选举。由罗马尼亚工人党领导的人民民主阵线的候选人，获得了完全的胜利。根据罗马尼亚中央选举委员会公布的选举结果，在一千零五十七万四千四百七十五名合格选民中，有一千零三十五万三千四百六十九人，即百分之九十八的选民参加了选举。其中有一千零二十三万三千三百六十九人，即百分之九十八点八四的投票选民，投了人民民主阵线候选人的票。当选的议员共四百二十三人，其中包括：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记乔治乌—德治、书记格奥尔基·阿波斯托尔，部长会议副主席兼冶金与化学工业部部长基伏·斯托依卡、武装部队部部长埃密尔·波德纳拉斯，以及著名的斯塔哈诺夫式工人、先进农民、科学家和文化艺术工作者等。
    在选举的这一天，罗马尼亚全国各地，从城市到乡村，到处洋溢着高涨的政治和劳动热情。在首都布加勒斯特和各城镇的街道上、建筑物上，都披上了节日的盛装，悬挂着罗马尼亚工人党和政府领袖们的画像，张贴着庆祝选举的标语。从早晨六点钟起，劳动人民就成群结队地到投票处去投票。和选举同时，罗马尼亚劳动人民庆祝选举的生产成就的消息纷纷传来。布加勒斯特的“玛吉阿斯·拉科西”工厂在选举前夕完成了一九五二年的生产计划，劳动生产率平均每人提高了百分之十六。著名的斯塔哈诺夫式矿工格萨·柯派丁和他的工作队已完成了一九五四年计划的生产定额。
