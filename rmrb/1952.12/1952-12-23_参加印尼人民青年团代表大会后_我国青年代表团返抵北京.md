Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending Indonesian People's Youth League Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-12-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. At the invitation of the Central Executive Committee of the Indonesian People's Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth sent a four-member Chinese youth delegation to participate in the first Congress of the Indonesian People's Youth League, held in Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, from November 11th to 16th.
    The Chinese youth delegation, composed of Wu Xueqian (head of the delegation), Zhu Guohua, Zhu Chengxue, and Luo Ju (female), was warmly welcomed by Indonesian youth, students, and the masses. After staying in Indonesia for three weeks, the delegation returned to Beijing on December 18th.
    At the first Congress of the Indonesian People's Youth League, the Chinese youth delegation conveyed the deep concern of the Chinese youth to the Indonesian youth, and recounted the achievements of the new China in construction, as well as the life and work of Chinese youth. During their stay in Indonesia, the delegation met with representatives of eleven youth organizations from various Indonesian parties and visited some schools, cultural institutions, and places of interest in Jakarta.
    Overseas Chinese youth and students in Indonesia warmly welcomed the Chinese youth delegation. The delegation held a forum with representatives of twenty-eight overseas Chinese youth groups, as well as representatives of the Javanese Chinese School Student Union and the Jakarta Student Union. At the forum, they conveyed the cordial concern of the motherland's youth for the overseas Chinese and reported on the great achievements of the motherland over the past three years.

### 参加印尼人民青年团代表大会后  我国青年代表团返抵北京

1952-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    参加印尼人民青年团代表大会后
    我国青年代表团返抵北京
    【新华社二十二日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会应印度尼西亚人民青年团中央执行委员会的邀请，曾派遣由四人组成的中国青年代表团，参加十一月十一日到十六日在印度尼西亚首都雅加达举行的印度尼西亚人民青年团第一届代表大会。
    由吴学谦（团长）、朱国华、朱成学和罗琚（女）四人组成的中国青年代表团，受到了印尼青年、学生与人民群众的热烈欢迎。代表团在印尼停留了三个星期后，已于十二月十八日返抵北京。
    中国青年代表团在印度尼西亚人民青年团第一届代表大会上，转达了中国青年对印尼青年的深切关怀，并讲述了新中国在建设方面的成就和中国青年的生活与工作情况。代表团在留印尼期间，曾和印尼各党派的十一个青年组织的代表会见，并参观了雅加达的一些学校、文化机构和名胜。
    印尼的华侨青年和学生热烈欢迎中国青年代表团。代表团曾和二十八个华侨青年团体的代表以及爪哇华校中学生联合会和雅加达学生联合会的代表举行了座谈会，在会上转达了祖国青年对侨胞的亲切关怀，并报告了祖国三年来的伟大成就。
