Title: Opening Ceremony of the Academy of Sciences of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-12-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 3rd: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 3rd that the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand opening ceremony on December 1st. At the opening ceremony, Comrade J.V. Stalin, General Kim Il Sung, and Chairman Mao Zedong were elected as Honorary Presidium members. Hong Myung-hee, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, delivered a report at the meeting. He pointed out that the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was established to promote the scientific development of the Republic, and it has great significance for the development of Korean people's culture.
    At the meeting, congratulatory messages from the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Academies of Sciences of the People's Republic of China and other fraternal countries, as well as some scientists, were read out.

### 朝鲜科学院举行开幕式

1952-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜科学院举行开幕式
    【新华社讯】塔斯社平壤三日电：朝鲜中央通讯社三日报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院于十二月一日举行隆重的开幕式。开幕会上，选举了约·维·斯大林主席、金日成将军和毛泽东主席为名誉主席团。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院院长洪命熹在会上作了报告。他指出朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院是为了促进共和国的科学发展而成立的，它对朝鲜人民文化的发展有着重大的意义。
    会上宣读了苏联科学院主席团、中华人民共和国及其他兄弟国家的科学院以及一些科学家的贺电。
