Title: West German Opinion Poll Results: Fourteen Million People Demand a Just Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1952-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin dispatch from TASS: According to figures released by the organization for promoting mutual understanding among the German people and the conclusion of a just peace treaty, as of November 27th, the results of the opinion poll indicate that in West Germany, 13,991,982 people oppose the Bonn "General Treaty" and demand the signing of a peace treaty with a unified Germany.

### 西德民意测验结果  一千四百万人要求缔结公正和约

1952-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    西德民意测验结果
    一千四百万人要求缔结公正和约
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：根据争取德国人民相互谅解和缔结公正和约组织所公布的数字，截至十一月二十七日为止，民意测验的结果表示，在西德有一千三百九十九万一千九百八十二人反对波恩“一般性条约”，而要求签订一个与统一的德国缔结的和约。
