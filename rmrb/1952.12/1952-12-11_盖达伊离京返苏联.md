Title: Gaidai Departs Beijing to Return to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-12-11
Page: 1

    Gaidai Departs Beijing to Return to the Soviet Union
    (Xinhua News Agency, 10th) Gaidai, a member of the Soviet art troupe and a famous female singer, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to the Soviet Union.
    Those who came to the airport to see her off included Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and more than 20 people from literary and artistic circles, including Zhou Weizhi, Cao Yu, Ding Li, and Guo Lanying. Zhukoff, a representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also came to the airport to see her off.

### 盖达伊离京返苏联

1952-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    盖达伊离京返苏联
    【新华社十日讯】苏联艺术工作团团员、著名女歌唱家盖达伊在十日晨乘飞机离北京回苏联。
    到机场欢送的，有中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾，文学艺术界人士周巍峙、曹禺、丁里、郭兰英等二十多人。苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福也到机场欢送。
