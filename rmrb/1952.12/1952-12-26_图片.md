Title: Our Country and Ceylon Sign Five-Year Trade Agreement on Rubber and Rice
Author: Liu Dongao
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 1

    On the 18th in Beijing, our country and Ceylon signed a five-year trade agreement on rubber and rice, and simultaneously signed two contracts for rice and rubber for 1953. The picture shows the signing ceremony. On the right is Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of our country, and on the left is Mr. Fonceska, Head of the Ceylon Trade Delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao photo.
