Our Great Motherland

Large-scale basic construction projects are underway in Anshan, and many processing factories have been established on the construction site. This is a corner of the concrete mixing plant. An excavator is using an electric shovel to load stones into iron cars, which are transported to the machine room to be crushed and mixed with cement.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山的大规模基本建设工程正在紧张进行，在工地上设立了许多加工工厂。这是混凝土搅拌厂的一角。挖土机正用电铲把石子装进铁车，运到机器房打碎后，和水泥搅拌在一起。
