Title: Southwest Nationalities Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The Southwest Nationalities Delegation, consisting of one hundred and sixty-nine members, led by delegation leader Songmou (Tibetan), deputy leaders Zhuomu Wengqing (Tibetan), Awang (Tibetan), Wei Zuochen (Shui nationality), and Li Peizhen (female, Yi nationality), arrived in Beijing on the 12th. More than one hundred people, including Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, went to the station to welcome them. The Southwest Nationalities Delegation includes thirty-six ethnic groups such as Tibetan, Miao, and Yi.

### 西南各民族代表参观团抵京

1952-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西南各民族代表参观团抵京
    据新华社讯：西南各民族代表参观团一行一百六十九人，在团长松谋（藏族）、副团长着木翁青（藏族）、阿旺（藏族）、韦佐臣（水家族）、李培贞（女，彝族）率领下，于十二日到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷等百余人。西南各民族代表参观团包括藏、苗、彝等三十六个民族成分。
