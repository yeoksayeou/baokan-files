Title: Churchill to Visit Eisenhower in the US Soon
Author:
Date: 1952-12-30
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. British Prime Minister Churchill announced on the 26th that he would travel to the United States by ship on December 31st, where he will meet with both the incoming US President Dwight D. Eisenhower and the current US President Harry S. Truman. According to news from American and British news agencies, Churchill will discuss various issues in the international situation with them, particularly the Korean War.

### 邱吉尔即将赴美访艾森豪威尔

1952-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    邱吉尔即将赴美访艾森豪威尔
    【新华社二十九日讯】英国首相邱吉尔二十六日宣布将在十二月三十一日乘船赴美，在美时将分别会见即将上任的美国新总统艾森豪威尔和美国现任总统杜鲁门。据美、英通讯社的消息透露，邱吉尔将和他们讨论国际形势中的各项问题，特别是朝鲜战争问题。
