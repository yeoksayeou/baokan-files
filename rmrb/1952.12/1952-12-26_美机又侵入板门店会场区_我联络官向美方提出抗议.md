Title: U.S. Aircraft Again Intrudes into Panmunjom Conference Area; Our Liaison Officer Lodges Protest with U.S. Side
Author:
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, December 25th: On December 24th, at approximately 4:30 PM, U.S. military aircraft violated the agreement on the Panmunjom conference area and intruded into the airspace above the Panmunjom conference area. Our delegation’s chief liaison officer lodged a protest with the other side on the 25th, demanding that the other side earnestly abide by the agreement and take truly effective measures to prevent similar incidents from recurring.

### 美机又侵入板门店会场区  我联络官向美方提出抗议

1952-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又侵入板门店会场区
    我联络官向美方提出抗议
    【新华社开城二十五日电】十二月二十四日下午四时三十分左右，美方军用飞机违反朝鲜停战谈判会场区协议，侵入板门店会场区上空。我方代表团首席联络官在二十五日向对方提出抗议，要求对方切实遵守协议，采取确实有效措施，使类似事件不再发生。
