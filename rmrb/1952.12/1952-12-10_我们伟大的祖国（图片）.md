Author:
Date: 1952-12-10

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the task of large-scale economic construction across the country and to prepare for next year's capital construction, various departments are vigorously training cadres. This is the internal combustion team of the military engineering technology training class sponsored by the New Line Construction Bureau of the Ministry of Railways. The students are listening to a technician explain the principles of gasoline engine structure.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了迎接全国大规模经济建设的任务，做好明年基本建设准备工作，目前各部门正在大力进行训练干部的工作。这是铁道部新线施工局主办的军工技术训练班的内燃队。学员们正在听技术工人讲解汽油机构造的原理。
