Title: Resolving Grievances (Image)
Author: Kang Dong
Date: 1952-12-10
Page: 3

    Resolving Grievances
    Artwork by Kang Dong
    After the peaceful liberation of Tibet, Tibetan compatriots, under the banner of Chairman Mao's national unity, have in a short period of time resolved the grievances of generations of mutual killings caused by imperialist and Kuomintang provocateurs. They are holding reconciliation meetings, exchanging Hada scarves, and are determined to build a new socialist Tibet for the motherland.

### 解冤家（图片）
康东
1952-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

    解冤家
    康东作
    西藏和平解放后，藏族同胞在毛主席民族团结的旗帜下很短时间内，已解除了因帝国主义和国民党挑拨者所造成的世代相残杀的冤仇。他们举行着和解会议交换哈达，决心建设好祖国的新西藏。
