Title: Finnish Envoy to China Holds Reception Celebrating Finnish Independence Day; Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Attends and Offers Congratulations in Speech

Author:
Date: 1952-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th December: The Finnish Envoy to China, Mr. K. G. Knoellinger, held a reception in Beijing at 6 p.m. on the 6th to celebrate the 35th anniversary of Finnish Independence Day.

Among those invited were: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administrative Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Minister of Higher Education Ma Xulun; Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan; Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin; Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Xiao Xiangrong; and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han, totaling nearly two hundred people.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China were also present.

At the beginning of the reception, Envoy Knoellinger and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches, offering mutual congratulations. The reception proceeded from beginning to end in a friendly atmosphere.

### 芬兰驻华公使举行庆祝芬兰独立日招待会  周恩来总理兼外长应邀到会并致词祝贺

1952-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华公使举行庆祝芬兰独立日招待会
    周恩来总理兼外长应邀到会并致词祝贺
    【新华社六日讯】芬兰驻我国公使克诺林于六日下午六时在北京举行招待会，庆祝芬兰独立三十五周年纪念日。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，高等教育部部长马叙伦，教育部部长张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，文化部副部长丁西林，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等近二百人。
    到会的并有各国驻我国外交使节及使馆人员。
    招待会开始时，克诺林公使与周恩来总理曾先后致词，互相祝贺。招待会自始至终在友好空气中进行。
