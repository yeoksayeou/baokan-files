Title: CPPCC National Committee and China Peace Committee Hold Report Meeting: Liu Jingfan and Chen Yi Invited to Report on the Work of the Delegation to Korea

Author:
Date: 1952-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China Peace Committee held a report meeting at Huairentang on the 16th. Liu Jingfan, head of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, was invited to give a "Work Report of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea," and Chen Yi, deputy head of the delegation, reported on "Our Return from Korea."

The report meeting was presided over by Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. More than 2,500 people attended, including cadres from various organs of the Central People's Government and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations.

### 政协全国委员会与抗美援朝总会举行报告会  刘景范陈沂应邀报告赴朝慰问团的工作

1952-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会与抗美援朝总会举行报告会
    刘景范陈沂应邀报告赴朝慰问团的工作
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会于十六日在怀仁堂举行报告会。邀请中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团总团长刘景范作“中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团工作报告”、副总团长陈沂报告“我们从朝鲜回来”。
    报告会由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深主持。到会的有中央人民政府各机关干部及各民主党派、人民团体等代表二千五百余人。
