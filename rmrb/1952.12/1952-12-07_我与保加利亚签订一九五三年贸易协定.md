Title: I Sign Trade Agreement with Bulgaria for 1953
Author:
Date: 1952-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th. After friendly and amicable negotiations, the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria signed the 1953 Sino-Bulgarian Trade Agreement in Beijing on December 3, 1952. The barter volume stipulated in this trade agreement is more than 70 percent higher than that of 1952. According to this agreement, the People's Republic of China will supply the People's Republic of Bulgaria with non-ferrous metals, cotton and other important materials needed by the Bulgarian people's economy; the People's Republic of Bulgaria will supply the People's Republic of China with machinery, electrical equipment, chemicals and other important goods needed by the Chinese economy.

The signatories to this agreement were: for the Chinese side, Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; for the Bulgarian side, Beberev, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Those who attended the signing ceremony were: for the Chinese side, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and other responsible officials; for the Bulgarian side, Petkov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, and all officials of the Embassy.

### 我与保加利亚签订一九五三年贸易协定

1952-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我与保加利亚签订一九五三年贸易协定
    【新华社六日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部与保加利亚人民共和国商务代表团之间，经过友好与融洽的谈判，已于一九五二年十二月三日在北京签订了一九五三年中保贸易协定。这个贸易协定所规定的易货额比一九五二年增加了百分之七十以上。根据这个协定，中华人民共和国将供给保加利亚人民共和国有色金属、棉花和保加利亚人民经济所需要的其他重要物资；保加利亚人民共和国将供给中华人民共和国机器、电器设备、化学品和中国经济所需要的其他重要货物。
    签订此协定者：中国方面为中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒；保加利亚方面为保加利亚人民共和国对外贸易部副部长别比列夫。
    参加签字典礼者：中国方面有外交部副部长伍修权、对外贸易部副部长雷任民及其他负责官员；保加利亚方面有保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使彼得科夫及大使馆全体官员。
