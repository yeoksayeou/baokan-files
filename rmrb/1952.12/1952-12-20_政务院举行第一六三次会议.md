Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 163rd Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th—The 163rd session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 19th. The meeting heard the report by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources of the Central People's Government, summarizing the 1952 flood control work and the work on farmland water conservancy during the 1952 drought prevention and anti-drought campaign, and approved the instruction on mobilizing the masses to continue carrying out drought prevention and anti-drought campaigns and vigorously promoting soil and water conservation work. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一六三次会议

1952-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六三次会议
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十三次政务会议在十九日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的一九五二年防汛工作总结报告和一九五二年防旱抗旱运动中的农田水利工作报告，通过了关于发动群众继续开展防旱抗旱运动并大力推行水土保持工作的指示。会议并通过了提请中央人民政府委员会批准的各项任免案。
