Title: Chairman Mao's Letter in Reply to All Workers of Anshan Iron and Steel Works Steelmaking Plant: Congratulating on New Achievements in Steelmaking, Encouraging Continued Efforts to Produce High-Quality Steel
Author:
Date: 1952-12-17
Page: 1

    [People's Daily News] The steelmaking plant of the state-owned Anshan Iron and Steel Company, with the enthusiastic help of Soviet experts, created a new national record for open-hearth steelmaking of one furnace of steel in six hours and nine minutes on November 22nd. On December 2nd, all the workers of the steelmaking plant wrote to Chairman Mao Zedong to report the good news. Chairman Mao Zedong replied on December 14th to commend them. The full text of Chairman Mao's reply is as follows:

    Comrades of all workers of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company Steelmaking Plant:

    I am very happy to read your letter of December 2nd. Congratulations on your latest achievements in open-hearth steelmaking production. With high labor enthusiasm and creativity, and with the help of Soviet experts, you have created a new record exceeding the level of capitalist countries in steelmaking time and furnace bottom area utilization coefficient. This is not only your glory, but also a major event on my country's path to industrialization. I hope you will continue to work hard and strive to complete the new task of producing high-quality steel in 1953.

    Mao Zedong
    December 14, 1952

### 毛主席函复鞍钢炼钢厂全体职工  祝贺炼钢新成就、勉励继续炼好优质钢

1952-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席函复鞍钢炼钢厂全体职工
    祝贺炼钢新成就、勉励继续炼好优质钢
    【本报讯】国营鞍山钢铁公司炼钢厂在苏联专家热诚帮助下，于十一月二十二日创造了六小时零九分钟炼一炉钢的平炉炼钢全国新纪录。十二月二日炼钢厂全体职工写信给毛泽东主席报告这一喜讯。毛泽东主席于十二月十四日复信嘉勉。毛主席复信全文如下：鞍山钢铁公司炼钢厂全体职工同志：
    我很高兴地读了你们十二月二日的来信。祝贺你们在平炉炼钢生产上的最新成就。你们以高度的劳动热情和创造精神，在苏联专家的帮助之下，创造了超过资本主义各国水平的炼钢时间和炉底面积利用系数的新纪录。这不仅是你们的光荣，而且是我国工业化道路上的一件大事。希望你们继续努力，为完成一九五三年度炼好优质钢的新任务而奋斗。毛泽东
    一九五二年十二月十四日
