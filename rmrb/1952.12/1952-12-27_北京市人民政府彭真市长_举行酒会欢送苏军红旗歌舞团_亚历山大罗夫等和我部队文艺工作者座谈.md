Title: Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Reception to Bid Farewell to the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble; Aleksandrov and Others Hold Discussions with Our Military's Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1952-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen held a grand reception on the evening of the 26th to bid farewell to the entire delegation of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by Colonel B.A. Aleksandrov, who are about to leave Beijing.

Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wu Han, hosted the reception on behalf of Mayor Peng Zhen.

Among those attending the reception were Wu Yuzhang and Li Jishen, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, as well as directors and staff in Beijing. Also attending the reception were Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Zhang Xiruo and Cao Jinghua, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, heads of various units of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and figures from the cultural and artistic circles such as Tian Han, Zheng Zhenduo, Zhou Weizhi, Cao Yu, Li Bozhao, Cheng Yanqiu, Zhao Feng, Song Zhi's, Li Wei, Chen Qitong, and Lan Ma.

Invited to attend the reception were Counselor Gudev, Sikachev, and Li Haiqiu of the Soviet Embassy in China, Chief Deputy Military Attaché Fominko, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhukov, TASS reporter Bemyanov, Pravda reporter Delyusin, and Soviet Deputy Trade Representative in China, Navich.

Vice Mayor Wu Han delivered a speech, extending sincere gratitude and high respect to the entire Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble on behalf of the capital's government and people. He said: "From the outstanding performances of our Soviet friends, the people of our country have more deeply realized that the Soviet Red Army is not only an invincible force in defending the cause of peace in the Far East and the world, but also a team with high political and cultural qualities. Our dear Soviet friends are about to part with us, but the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people will continue to be consolidated and developed. This friendship, as Chairman Mao Zedong pointed out, is 'permanent, indestructible, and no one can separate it.'"

Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, thanked the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. He said: "The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble is a literary and artistic team armed with Lenin's and Stalin's literary and artistic thought. Its artistic performances, full of vitality and firm optimism, have given the Chinese people great encouragement."

B.A. Aleksandrov, head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, said in his speech: The stay in China has left indelible impressions. The friendship between the great Soviet and Chinese people is forever unbreakable.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. During the banquet, the guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, and the health of Comrade Stalin.

After the reception, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association hosted a soiree, with the Literary and Art Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performing programs such as the opera "Dong Cunrui" to entertain the entire delegation of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble.

Also, B.A. Aleksandrov, head of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, Deputy Head Lomonosov, Choir Captain Vinogradov, and others held a symposium with our country's military cultural and artistic workers on the evening of the 26th. The symposium was presided over by Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government. More than 70 cultural and artistic workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Song Zhi's, attended the symposium.

During the symposium, B.A. Aleksandrov described the development process and working methods of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble in his speech. Lomonosov introduced the political work of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble at the meeting. B.A. Aleksandrov and Lomonosov also answered specific questions about the work and study of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble.

### 北京市人民政府彭真市长  举行酒会欢送苏军红旗歌舞团  亚历山大罗夫等和我部队文艺工作者座谈

1952-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人民政府彭真市长
    举行酒会欢送苏军红旗歌舞团
    亚历山大罗夫等和我部队文艺工作者座谈
    【新华社二十六日讯】北京市人民政府彭真市长二十六日晚举行盛大酒会，欢送行将离京的以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫上校为首的苏军红旗歌舞团全体团员。
    北京市人民政府副市长吴晗代表彭真市长主持酒会。
    出席酒会的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、李济深，总干事钱俊瑞，以及在京理事、干事。出席酒会的还有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂，出席酒会的还有北京市中苏友好协会副会长张奚若、曹靖华，北京市人民政府各单位负责人，以及文化艺术界人士田汉、郑振铎、周巍峙、曹禺、李伯钊、程砚秋、赵沨、宋之的、李伟、陈其通、蓝马等。
    应邀出席酒会的，有苏联驻华大使馆参赞顾德夫、司高磋、李海秋，首席副武官富敏科，苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福，塔斯社记者边缅诺夫，真理报记者杰柳辛，以及苏联驻华商务副代表那维琪等。
    吴晗副市长致词，他代表首都的政府和人民向苏军红旗歌舞团全体致以深切的感谢和崇高的敬礼。他说：“从苏联朋友们卓越的演出中，使我国人民更深刻地认识到苏联红军不仅是保卫远东及全世界和平事业中的一支不可战胜的力量，同时也是一支具有高度政治、文化素养的队伍。亲爱的苏联朋友们即将和我们分别了，但中苏两国人民伟大友谊将继续巩固与发展，这种友谊正如毛泽东主席所指出的，是‘永久的，不可破坏的，没有人能够分离开的。’”
    中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章代表中苏友好协会全体会员向苏军红旗歌舞团致谢。他说：“苏军红旗歌舞团是一支以列宁、斯大林文艺思想武装起来的文艺队伍。它的充满了生气勃勃和坚定乐观的艺术表演，给了中国人民以极大的鼓舞”。
    苏军红旗歌舞团团长鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫致词，他说：在中国逗留的期间，留下了永远不可磨灭的印象。伟大的苏中两国人民的友谊是永远的牢不可破的。
    酒会在友好的气氛中进行。席间，宾主频频举杯，为中苏两国人民的友谊、为毛泽东主席的健康、为斯大林同志的健康而干杯。
    酒会毕，中苏友好协会总会主办招待晚会，由中国人民解放军文艺工作团演出歌剧“董存瑞”等节目，招待苏军红旗歌舞团全体团员。
    又讯：苏军红旗歌舞团团长鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫，副团长罗蒙诺索夫，合唱队长维诺格拉陀夫等二十六日晚和我国部队文化艺术工作者举行座谈会。座谈会由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂主持。参加座谈会的有中国人民解放军文化艺术工作者宋之的等七十多人。
    座谈会中，鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫在发言中叙述了苏军红旗歌舞团的发展过程和工作方法。罗蒙诺索夫在会上介绍了苏军红旗歌舞团的政治工作。鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫和罗蒙诺索夫还解答了关于苏军红旗歌舞团的工作、学习等方面的具体问题。
