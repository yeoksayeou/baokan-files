Title: Visiting Groups, such as the Sixth Sub-Group of the Comfort Mission to Korea, Go to Various Places to Deliver Reports
Author:
Date: 1952-12-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The Sixth Sub-Group of the Second Chinese People's Comfort Mission to Korea went to various parts of North China to deliver reports. The Second, Fifth, and Seventh Sub-Groups have arrived in Xi'an, Hankou, and Shenyang, respectively.

The Sixth Sub-Group, on the 9th and 10th, departed from Beijing in three routes to various parts of the North China region to deliver reports to people from all walks of life. The first route went to Hebei Province, the second to Shanxi Province, and the third to the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Suiyuan Province. The reporting period is estimated to be one month. The representatives will report on the heroic struggles of the Chinese and Korean People's Armies to workers, farmers, cadres, and business people in the main cities and industrial and mining areas of each province. During the stay of all members of the Sixth Sub-Group in Beijing, the North China Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea General Association held a gathering on the 6th to welcome their triumphant return. More than 1,000 cadres from the North China-level directly affiliated organizations and people's organizations attended the welcome meeting.

The Second Sub-Group, consisting of 75 people, arrived in Xi'an at 12:30 pm on the 8th. Yang Mingxuan, Chairman of the Northwest Resist-America Aid-Korea General Association, Vice Chairmen Zhang Jiafu, Ke Zhongping, and Li Furen, Wang Feng, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Niu Shushen, Director of the Political Department of the Shaanxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons of the Party and government organs and people's organizations of Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City, and more than 1,000 representatives of people from all ethnic groups and walks of life in Xi'an City gathered at the station to welcome them. Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Zhizhong, and Ma Mingfang, Vice Chairmen of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, hosted a banquet for all members of the Second Sub-Group of the Comfort Mission that evening.

The Fifth Sub-Group (representatives from all circles in the Central South region) returned to Hankou on the 9th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Chen Mingshu, Vice Chairman of the Central South Resist-America Aid-Korea General Association, Chen Jingshu and Li Qixin, Standing Committee Members of the Wuhan Resist-America Aid-Korea Branch, and more than 400 representatives of people from all walks of life in Wuhan City.

The Seventh Sub-Group (representatives from all circles in the Northeast region) returned to Shenyang on the 7th. On the 8th, the Northeast Resist-America Aid-Korea General Association and the Shenyang Resist-America Aid-Korea Branch jointly hosted a banquet for the representatives of the Seventh Sub-Group. The Seventh Sub-Group plans to report to the people of Northeast China in cities such as Shenyang, Anshan, Benxi, Fushun, and Lushun-Dalian and other places in Northeast China through reports, radio rallies, and performances by literary and art troupes about the situation of the comfort mission on the Korean front and the heroic deeds of the Chinese and Korean People's Armies.

### 赴朝慰问团第六分团等赴各地作传达报告

1952-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    赴朝慰问团第六分团等赴各地作传达报告
    据新华社讯：中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团第六分团赴华北各地作传达报告。第二分团、第五分团、第七分团已分别抵达西安、汉口、沈阳。
    第六分团、于九、十两日由北京分三路赴华北区各地向各界人民作传达报告。第一路到河北省，第二路到山西省，第三路到内蒙古自治区和绥远省。传达时间预计一月，代表们将在各省的主要城市和工矿区分别向工人、农民、干部、工商界人士报告朝中人民军队英勇斗争的事迹。第六分团全体人员在北京期间，华北抗美援朝总分会曾于六日举行集会，欢迎他们胜利归来。华北级各直属机关、各人民团体干部共一千多人参加了欢迎会。
    第二分团一行七十五人，已在八日上午十二时半到达西安。西北抗美援朝总分会主席杨明轩，副主席张稼夫、柯仲平、李敷仁，中共中央西北局统一战线工作部部长汪锋，中国人民解放军陕西军区政治部主任牛书申，陕西省及西安市党、政机关和人民团体负责人及西安市各族各界人民代表共一千多人，在车站集会欢迎。西北军政委员会副主席习仲勋、张治中、马明方于当晚欢宴慰问团第二分团全体团员。
    第五分团（中南区各界代表）于九日回到汉口。前往车站欢迎的，有中南抗美援朝总分会副主席陈铭枢，武汉市抗美援朝分会常务委员陈经畬、李启新及武汉市各界人民代表共四百多人。
    第七分团（东北区各界代表）于七日回到沈阳。八日东北抗美援朝总分会和沈阳市抗美援朝分会联合欢宴第七分团代表。第七分团准备在沈阳、鞍山、本溪、抚顺、旅大等城市和东北各地，用报告、广播大会、文艺工作团演唱等方式向东北人民报告在朝鲜前线进行慰问的情形和朝中人民部队的英勇事迹。
