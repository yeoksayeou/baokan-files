Title: Western European Progressive Newspapers Prominently Publish Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Reply to Pearson

Author:
Date: 1952-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 20th - The British "Daily Worker," the French "L'Humanité," "Ce Soir," and the Italian "Unità" all prominently published summaries of Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's reply to Pearson regarding the United Nations General Assembly's illegal resolution on the Korean question on December 16th.
