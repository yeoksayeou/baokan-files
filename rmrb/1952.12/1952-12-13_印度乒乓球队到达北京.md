Title: Indian Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th. The Indian table tennis team, a group of eight led by team leader Ranga Ramanujan, arrived in Beijing on the night of the 12th for a visit to China. Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General, and Kosla, First Secretary of the Indian Embassy, were at the station to welcome them.

### 印度乒乓球队到达北京

1952-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度乒乓球队到达北京
    【新华社十二日讯】前来中国访问的印度乒乓球队一行八人在领队朗加·拉马努金率领下，于十二日夜到达北京。到车站迎接的有中华全国体育总会秘书长荣高棠、副秘书长黄中，还有印度大使馆一等秘书柯斯拉等人。
