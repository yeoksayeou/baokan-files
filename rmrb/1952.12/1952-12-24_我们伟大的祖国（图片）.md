Author:
Date: 1952-12-24

The Xiamen Port typhoon shelter is a semi-natural, semi-artificial harbor. The Xiamen Municipal People's Government's Municipal Construction Committee carried out a project to clear silt from the typhoon shelter in the second half of this year, so that all local and foreign fishing boats can enter the harbor to take shelter from typhoons. The fishermen affectionately call it the "Life-Saving Harbor."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    厦门港避风坞是一个半天然半人工的港坞。厦门市人民政府市政建设委员会于今年下半年进行了清理避风坞泥沙的工程，使得全港和外来的渔船在台风侵袭时可以全部入港避风。渔民们都亲热地称它为“保命坞”。
