Title: Churaki and Others Arrive in Shenyang from Beijing, Hannon and Others Leave Beijing for Performances in Various Parts of Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1952-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: Churaki, the leader of the Soviet Art Troupe, along with troupe members Halima Nasyrova, Obraztsov, Peybutov, Yemelyanova, Kavelzneva, Sokolova, Pomazkov, Chitoshvili, Karachova, Puchkin, Obraztsova, Zaitseva, and administrative staff member Salnikov, a total of fourteen people, arrived in Shenyang from Beijing on a special plane on the 20th. When Churaki and others left Beijing, Yan Baohang and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Zhang Xiruo and Cao Jinghua, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and representatives of the capital's art circles went to the airport to see them off. On the evening of Churaki's arrival in Shenyang, a grand welcome party was held by people from all walks of life in Northeast China.

Another report: Soviet Art Troupe members Tamara Hannon, Rakhimov, Maksimova, Adzhimov, Bakayev, Dadadzhanov, Makarova, and administrative staff member Bubenin, a group of eight people, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 21st to perform in various parts of Northeast China. More than 200 people, including Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, representatives from cultural and artistic circles, and members of the Young Pioneers, went to the station to see them off.

Before leaving Beijing, Tamara Hannon and others performed dance programs at Huairentang, Beijing, on the evening of the 20th. The performance was sponsored by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. More than 1,600 working cadres from central, North China, and Beijing municipal level organs watched the performance. Tamara Hannon performed seventeen dance programs from ten Soviet nationalities and five nationalities including Chinese, Mongolian, Korean, Czech, and Indian. Amidst enthusiastic applause and cheers from the audience, she also performed the Chinese Northeast folk song "Stalin, Mao Zedong" as an encore. The players Dadadzhanov, Bakayev, and Adzhimov, who accompanied Tamara Hannon's dance, played Uzbek folk instruments such as short flute, dalar, and hand drum, which were also warmly welcomed by the audience. At the end of the performance, Zhou Weizhi, a Chinese composer, presented Tamara Hannon with a gift of three Chinese paintings on behalf of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Tamara Hannon gave a thank-you speech in Chinese, expressing her gratitude.

### 楚拉基等由京抵沈阳  哈侬等离京赴东北各地表演

1952-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    楚拉基等由京抵沈阳
    哈侬等离京赴东北各地表演
    据新华社讯：苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基，团员哈利马·纳赛罗娃、奥布拉兹卓夫、裴布托夫、叶美里扬诺娃、卡威尔兹涅娃、索科洛娃、波马兹科夫、契托希维利、卡拉巧娃、普奇岑、奥布拉兹卓娃、查伊采娃和行政工作人员萨尔尼柯夫等十四人，在二十日由北京乘专机抵达沈阳。楚拉基等离北京时，中苏友好协会总会副总干事阎宝航、李沾吾，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，北京市中苏友好协会副会长张奚若、曹靖华，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗和首都艺术界代表等曾到机场欢送。楚拉基等抵沈阳的当晚，东北各界举行了盛大的欢迎会。
    又讯：苏联艺术工作团团员塔马拉·哈侬、拉希莫夫、马克西莫娃、阿吉莫夫、巴卡耶夫、达达让诺夫、马卡洛娃和行政工作人员布别宁等一行八人在二十一日下午乘火车离京赴东北各地表演。到车站送行的有中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾和文化艺术界等的代表以及少年儿童队队员共二百多人。
    塔马拉·哈侬等离京前，二十日晚在北京怀仁堂表演舞蹈节目。表演会由中苏友好协会总会主办。观看这次表演的有中央一级、华北一级和北京市一级各机关工作干部一千六百多人。塔马拉·哈侬在会上表演了苏联十个民族以及中国、蒙古、朝鲜、捷克、印度等五个民族的十七个舞蹈节目。在全场热烈鼓掌欢呼声中，她又加演了中国东北民歌“斯大林，毛泽东”。为塔马拉·哈侬的舞蹈作伴奏的演奏者达达让诺夫、巴卡耶夫、阿吉莫夫在会上演奏了乌兹别克民间乐器短笛、达拉琴和手鼓。同样受到观众的热烈欢迎。在表演结束时，我国作曲家周巍峙代表中华全国文学艺术界联合会向塔马拉·哈侬赠送礼品——中堂三幅。塔马拉·哈侬用中国话致答词，表示谢意。
