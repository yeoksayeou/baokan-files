    Our Great Motherland
    The new "Beijing Geological College" has been established. It will train a large number of geological cadres to meet the needs of our country's large-scale economic construction. Students majoring in mineral geology and exploration at the college are studying spherical projections of crystals here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新型的“北京地质学院”已经成立。它将大量培养地质工作干部，以适应我国大规模经济建设的需要。该院矿产地质及勘探专修科的学生们正在这里研究结晶体的球面投影。
