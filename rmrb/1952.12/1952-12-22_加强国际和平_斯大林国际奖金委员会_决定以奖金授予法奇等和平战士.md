Title: "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize Committee Decides to Award Prizes to Peace Fighters like Faji

Author:
Date: 1952-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st: Moscow newspapers on the 21st published a communique from the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize Committee regarding the awarding of the 1952 prizes. The communique stated that the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize Committee held meetings on the 17th and 20th, presided over by the Committee Chairman, Academician Skobeltsyn of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The committee reviewed suggestions for awarding the 1952 Stalin International Prizes and decided to award the prizes to the following representatives of democratic forces from various countries who have made outstanding contributions to the struggle to defend and strengthen peace:

    (1) Yves Farge, France;
    (2) Saifuddin Kitchlew , Chairman of the All-India Peace Council;
    (3) Elisa Branco, distinguished member of the Brazilian Women's Federation;
    (4) Paul Robeson, United States;
    (5) Johannes Becher, writer, German Democratic Republic;
    (6) Dyson Carter, Canada;
    (7) Ilya Ehrenburg, writer, Soviet Union.

The communique was signed by the Committee Chairman, Academician Skobeltsyn of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Vice-Chairmen: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Louis Aragon, French writer; Committee Members: Martin Andersen Nexø, Danish writer; John Desmond Bernal, Professor at the University of London, England; Pablo Neruda, Chilean poet; Stanisław Dąbrowski, Professor at the University of Łódź, Poland; Mihail Sadoveanu, Academician of the Romanian Academy; and Alexander Fadeyev, Soviet writer.

### “加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会  决定以奖金授予法奇等和平战士

1952-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    “加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会
    决定以奖金授予法奇等和平战士
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：二十一日莫斯科各报发表了“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会关于颁发一九五二年奖金的公报。公报说：“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会于十七日和二十日在委员会主席、苏联科学院院士斯科贝尔琴主持下举行会议。委员会审查了颁发一九五二年斯大林国际奖金的建议，并决定以奖金授予下列在保卫和加强和平的斗争中有卓越贡献的各国民主力量的代表：
    （一）伊夫·法奇，法国；
    （二）赛福丁·克其鲁，全印和平理事会主席；
    （三）艾利萨·布兰柯，卓越的巴西妇女联合会会员；
    （四）保罗·罗伯逊，美国；
    （五）约翰尼斯·贝希尔，作家，德意志民主共和国；
    （六）文幼章，加拿大；
    （七）伊利亚·爱伦堡，作家，苏联。
    在公报上签名的有委员会主席、苏联科学院院士斯科贝尔琴，副主席：中国科学院院长郭沫若、法国作家阿拉贡，委员：丹麦作家尼克索、英国伦敦大学教授贝尔纳、智利诗人聂鲁达、波兰罗兹大学教授第姆鲍夫斯基、罗马尼亚科学院院士沙杜维亚努、苏联作家法捷耶夫。
