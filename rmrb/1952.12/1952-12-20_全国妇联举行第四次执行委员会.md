Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Fourth Executive Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-12-20
Page: 3

    The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its fourth Executive Committee meeting on December 10th. Twenty-eight Executive Committee members and nine alternate Executive Committee members attended the meeting. All department-level cadres and above from the All-China Women's Federation were present at the meeting. The main agenda of the meeting included: approving the "Report on Current Issues in Women's Work," discussing and deciding on changes to the organizational structure and tasks of the democratic women's federations at the Greater Administrative Area level, and convening the Second National Women's Congress.

    The meeting was presided over by Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation. The meeting first heard a report given by Zhang Yun, Secretary-General of the All-China Women's Federation, at the National Women's Work Conference regarding current issues in women's work. After discussion, it was principally approved and decided to be submitted to the Standing Committee for detailed review and publication.

    Next, Deng Yingchao gave explanations on the issues of changing the organizational structure and tasks of the democratic women's federations at the Greater Administrative Area level and convening the Second National Women's Congress. The meeting unanimously passed the decision on changing the organizational structure and tasks of the democratic women's federations at the Greater Administrative Area level. Regarding the convening of the Second National Women's Congress, the meeting unanimously agreed that it was necessary and decided to hold it in April or May of 1953. The date of the congress, the number of delegates, and the election methods will be decided and handled by the Standing Committee.
                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国妇联举行第四次执行委员会

1952-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    全国妇联举行第四次执行委员会
    中华全国民主妇女联合会在十二月十日举行第四次执行委员会，出席这次会议的有执行委员二十八人，候补执行委员九人，全国妇联部长级以上干部均列席会议。会议主要议程是：批准“关于当前妇女工作问题的报告”，讨论并决定改变大行政区一级民主妇女联合会机构和任务以及召开第二次全国妇女代表大会等问题。
    会议由全国妇联副主席邓颖超主持。会议首先听取了全国妇联秘书长章蕴在全国妇女工作会议上所作的关于当前妇女工作问题的报告，经讨论后作原则通过，并决定交常务委员会详细审查后发布。
    接着由邓颖超对改变大行政区一级民主妇女联合会机构和任务以及召开第二次全国妇女代表大会的问题分别作了说明。会议一致通过了关于改变大行政区一级民主妇女联合会机构与任务的决定。关于召开第二次全国妇女代表大会，会议一致认为是必要的，并决定于一九五三年四、五月间举行，大会日期、代表人数、选举办法等将由常务委员会议定办理。
                    （新华社）
