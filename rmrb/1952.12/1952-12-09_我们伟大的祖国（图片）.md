Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-12-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the 17th anniversary of the "December 9th" Movement. The youth of the whole country are inheriting and carrying forward the patriotic spirit of the "December 9th" Movement, studying hard, and preparing to participate in the large-scale construction work of the motherland. This is a picture of second-year students in the mining department of the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, which specializes in training senior technical personnel for the iron and steel industry. They are studying the principles of grinding machines.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是“一二、九”运动十七周年纪念。全国青年正继承和发扬“一二、九”运动的爱国主义精神，努力学习，准备参加祖国的大规模建设工作。这是专门培养钢铁工业高级技术人材的北京钢铁学院采矿系二年级的学生们。他们正在研究磨矿机的原理。
