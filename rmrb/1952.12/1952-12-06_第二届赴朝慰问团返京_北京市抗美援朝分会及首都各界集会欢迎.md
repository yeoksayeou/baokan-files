Title: Second Delegation to North Korea Returns to Beijing, Beijing Branch of the China Resist America, Aid Korea Association and People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold Rally to Welcome Them

Author:

Date: 1952-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: The Second Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea arrived in Beijing from Tianjin on the morning of December 5th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the China Resist America, Aid Korea Association, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Xu Baoju, Minister of the Organization Department of the Chinese People's Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, Deputy Ministers Tan Tiwu and Mo Yichang, Zhang Zhixiang, Standing Committee Member of the North China Resist America, Aid Korea General Branch, Wen Ying, Deputy Minister of the Organization Department, Liu Zhijian, Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Li Zhaobing and Liu Baiyu, Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, Jin Ziming, Deputy Minister of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and over 600 people from all walks of life in the capital.

Also present at the station to welcome them were Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and staff members of the embassy.

The Beijing Branch of the China Resist America, Aid Korea Association and people from all walks of life in the capital also held a grand rally at the station to warmly welcome the delegation. Chairman Zhang Xiruo first delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. On behalf of the people of the capital and all the people of Beijing, he expressed a warm welcome to the delegation's triumphant return and paid high tribute to the delegation for their tireless efforts in extensively and thoroughly comforting the Chinese and Korean People's forces and the Korean people. He said: Comrades, you will soon be heading to various parts of the country to convey the great victories on the front lines to the people of the whole country. I hope that you will convey the heroic struggles of the Chinese and Korean People's forces and the Korean people that you have seen and heard to the people of the motherland, and encourage the people of the motherland to actively participate in the large-scale construction that is about to begin, so that New China will move from one victory to a greater victory in all aspects.

Liu Jingfan, head of the delegation, expressed his heartfelt thanks and respect to the people from all walks of life in the capital on behalf of all the comrades of the delegation. After briefly reporting on the work of the delegation in Korea and the heroic struggles of the Chinese and Korean People's forces and the Korean people on the Korean front, he said: "We hope that the people of the whole country will learn from the patriotism and internationalism of the Chinese and Korean People's forces, strengthen the campaign to increase production and practice economy, deepen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, continue to support the Chinese and Korean People's forces and the Korean people, and fight to the end for justice and peace."

In addition, in addition to the general delegation and the first sub-delegation making reports to people from all walks of life in the capital, other sub-delegations will soon be heading to various places to conduct extensive reports to people from all walks of life in their respective regions. ### 第二届赴朝慰问团返京  北京市抗美援朝分会及首都各界集会欢迎

1952-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    第二届赴朝慰问团返京
    北京市抗美援朝分会及首都各界集会欢迎
    【新华社五日讯】中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团在本月五日上午由天津抵达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的，有北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国人民抗美援朝总会组织部部长许宝驹，副部长谭惕吾、莫艺昌，华北抗美援朝总分会常务委员张致祥，组织部副部长文英，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部宣传部部长刘志坚，文化部副部长李兆炳、刘白羽，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明及首都各界人民共六百多人。
    前往车站欢迎的，还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使权五稷和大使馆的工作人员等。
    北京市抗美援朝分会及首都各界人民并在车站上举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎慰问团。大会首由张奚若主席致欢迎词。他代表首都各界暨北京市全体人民，对慰问团的胜利归来表示热烈的欢迎，并对慰问团不辞辛劳、普遍而深入地慰问中、朝人民部队和朝鲜人民表示崇高的敬意。他说：同志们，你们很快就要分赴各地向全国人民传达前线的伟大胜利，希望你们把这次见到和听到的中、朝人民部队及朝鲜人民的英勇斗争事迹，传达给祖国人民，鼓舞祖国人民积极参加即将到来的大规模的建设，使新中国在各方面由一个胜利走向更大的胜利。
    刘景范总团长代表慰问团全体同志向首都各界人民致以衷心的感谢和敬意。他在简要地报告慰问团在朝鲜的工作情况及朝鲜前线中、朝人民部队和朝鲜人民的英勇斗争事迹后说：“我们希望全国人民学习中、朝人民部队的爱国主义与国际主义精神，加强增产节约运动，深入抗美援朝工作，继续支援中、朝人民部队和朝鲜人民，为正义为和平而斗争到底。”
    又讯，慰问团除总团及第一分团将向首都各界人民作传达报告外，其他各分团即将分赴各地，向各该地区的各界人民进行广泛的传达报告。（附图片）
    右：北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若（右第一人）在欢迎第二届赴朝慰问团大会上致欢迎词。　新华社记者　陈之平摄
