Title: Shandong Province's Party Committees at All Levels Have Organized Forces to Inspect and Handle Work Related to Mass Letters

Author: 
Date: 1952-12-25
Page: 6

Column: Response to People's Daily Readers' Criticisms and Suggestions

    People's Daily Readers' Letters Group: The December 6th "Readers' Letters Page" published a letter entitled "Cadres in Certain Counties and Districts of Shandong Province Severely Suppress Mass Criticism." Regarding the unresolved cases mentioned therein, we have sent letters urging relevant departments to resolve them quickly. Regarding the inspection and handling of people's letters, party committees at all levels have organized forces and are currently carrying out this work. We hereby inform you.
    General Office Secretariat, CCP Central Committee Shandong Branch

### 山东省各级党委已组织力量  检查处理群众来信的工作

1952-12-25
第6版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    山东省各级党委已组织力量
    检查处理群众来信的工作
    人民日报读者来信组：十二月六日“读者来信专页”刊登了“山东省某些县区干部严重压制群众批评”来信。其中所提未解决的案件，我们已去信督促有关部门迅速解决。关于检查处理人民来信的工作，各级党委均已组织了力量，正在进行。特告。
    中共中央山东分局办公厅秘书室
