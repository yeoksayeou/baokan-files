Title: Indian Parliamentarians Visit China; Sino-Indian Friendship Association Hosts Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1952-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: Three Indian parliamentarians, including Govind Das, arrived in Beijing on the evening of December 2nd for a visit to China. Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and Chen Hansheng, Vice President, hosted a banquet to welcome them on the evening of the 3rd. The banquet was also attended by Wu Zuoren, Hong Shen, Hu Yuzhi, Zhang Mingyang, and Liu Zunqi, all council members of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Zheng Zhenduo, Ye Dingyi, and Qian Weichang, former members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to India; Zhou Gengsheng and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and many figures from cultural and academic circles. The Indian Ambassador to China, Raghunathrai Jai Gopal Nath, was invited to attend.

　　The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 印度议员三人来我国访问  中印友协设宴欢迎

1952-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    印度议员三人来我国访问
    中印友协设宴欢迎
    【新华社四日讯】来中国访问的印度国会议员高温德·达斯等一行三人，在本月二日晚抵京。中印友好协会会长丁西林和副会长陈翰笙在三日晚设宴欢迎。参加宴会的还有中印友好协会理事吴作人、洪深、胡愈之、张明养、刘尊棋，前我国赴印文化代表团团员郑振铎、叶丁易、钱伟长，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、钱端升及文化学术界人士多人。印度驻华大使赖嘉文应邀作陪。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
