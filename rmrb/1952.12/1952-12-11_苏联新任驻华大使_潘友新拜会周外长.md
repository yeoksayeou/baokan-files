Title: The New Soviet Ambassador to China, P.A. Yudin, Pays a Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1952-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: The new Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, P.A. Yudin, and the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, G.T. Kudryavtsev, paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 10th to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Director of the General Office Wang Bingnan, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department Xu Yixin, and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru.

### 苏联新任驻华大使  潘友新拜会周外长

1952-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联新任驻华大使
    潘友新拜会周外长
    【新华社十日讯】苏联新任驻华特命全权大使潘友新同苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫在十日下午到中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有：外交部副部长伍修权、办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司司长徐以新和交际处处长王倬如。
