    Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Northwest No. 2 Cotton Textile Mill is currently undergoing large-scale capital construction. This is a picture of workers participating in the capital construction, they are tying reinforcing steel bars on the roof of the opening and cleaning workshop of the spinning mill.
