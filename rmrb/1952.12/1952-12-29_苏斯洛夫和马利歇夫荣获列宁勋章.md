Title: Suslov and Malyshev Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1952-12-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 27th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, N.M. Shvernik, has, in accordance with the order of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, awarded the Order of Lenin to M.A. Suslov, to celebrate his 50th birthday and to reward his merits to the Party and the Soviet state; and also awarded the Order of Lenin to V.A. Malyshev, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, to celebrate his 50th birthday and to reward his merits to the Soviet state in developing Soviet industry.

### 苏斯洛夫和马利歇夫荣获列宁勋章

1952-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏斯洛夫和马利歇夫荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席尼·米·什维尔尼克已依照苏联最高苏维埃主席团的命令，以列宁勋章授给米·安·苏斯洛夫，以祝贺他的五十寿辰，奖励他对党和苏维埃国家的功勋；同时以列宁勋章授给苏联部长会议副主席维·阿·马利歇夫，以祝贺他的五十寿辰，奖励他在发展苏维埃工业方面对苏维埃国家的功勋。
