    Our Great Motherland
    Basic construction departments across the country are intensifying preparations for capital construction. A large quantity of steel plates needed for capital construction are being supplied from the Northeast to various parts of the country. The picture shows steel plates being shipped from Dalian Port to various regions.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地基本建设部门，正在加紧进行基本建设的准备工作。为基本建设所需要的大量钢板，正由东北供应全国各地。这是由大连海港码头运往各地的钢板正在装运的情形。
