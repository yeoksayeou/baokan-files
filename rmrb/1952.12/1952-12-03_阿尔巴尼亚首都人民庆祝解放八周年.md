Title: Albanian Capital Celebrates Eighth Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1952-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana: The people of Tirana, the capital of Albania, held a celebration meeting at the People's Theater on November 28, on the eve of the eighth anniversary of Albania's liberation. Huge portraits of Stalin and Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, were hung in the meeting place, as were the national flag of the People's Republic of Albania and the flag of the Soviet Union. A huge banner was also hung in the meeting place, which read: "Long live the Soviet Union, the best friend and savior of the Albanian people, and the glorious defender of world peace, and the great Stalin!"

    Enver Hoxha, the leader of the Albanian people, Omer Nishani, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and other leaders of the Party and the government attended the celebration meeting. When they took their seats on the podium, the hall resounded with stormy applause. Foreign guests present included: the Soviet government delegation headed by General K. N. Galitsky, General Huang Zhen, representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and representatives of government delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, the Mongolian People's Republic, and other people's democratic countries.

    The celebration meeting was opened by Spiro Koleka, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, after the band played the national anthems of the People's Republic of Albania and the Soviet Union. The attendees listened with great attention to the report by Mehmet Shehu, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania. The entire venue burst into stormy applause after the report.

    General K. N. Galitsky, head of the Soviet government delegation, then spoke. Everyone stood up and cheered "Stalin-Enver." Representatives of friendly countries also conveyed warm congratulations to the meeting on behalf of their respective governments.

    Finally, in a warm atmosphere, the meeting passed a telegram of salute to Stalin.

    In addition: Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, held a reception on November 29 to celebrate the eighth anniversary of Albania's liberation. The reception was attended by: leaders of the Albanian Parliament, government, and Labor Party, representatives of various countries participating in the celebration meeting, and diplomatic personnel from various friendly countries stationed in Albania.

### 阿尔巴尼亚首都人民庆祝解放八周年

1952-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚首都人民庆祝解放八周年
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那人民于阿尔巴尼亚解放八周年纪念日前夕——十一月二十八日，在人民剧院举行了庆祝大会。大会会场悬挂着斯大林和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席恩威尔·霍查的巨幅画像，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的国旗和苏联的国旗。会场中还悬挂着巨幅标语，上面写着：“阿尔巴尼亚人民的最好的朋友和救星、世界和平的光荣的保卫者苏联和伟大的斯大林万岁！”
    阿尔巴尼亚人民的领袖恩威尔·霍查、国民议会主席团主席奥麦尔·尼山尼、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局的委员们，以及党和政府的其他领袖们都参加了庆祝大会。当他们在主席台上就座时，大厅中响起了暴风雨般的掌声。到会的外宾有：以克·纳·加利茨基上将为首的苏联政府代表团、中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表黄镇将军、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国以及其他人民民主国家的政府代表团的代表们。
    庆祝大会在乐队演奏阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国歌和苏联国歌后，由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席斯皮罗·柯勒卡主持开幕。到会者以极大的注意倾听了阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席麦哈迈德·谢胡的报告。报告毕全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    苏联政府代表团团长克·纳·加利茨基上将接着发言。全体起立，并欢呼“斯大林—恩威尔”。各友好国家的代表们也分别代表他们的政府向大会热烈祝贺。
    大会最后在热烈的气氛中通过了向斯大林致敬的电报。
    又讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席恩威尔·霍查在十一月二十九日举行招待会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放八周年。出席招待会的有：阿尔巴尼亚议会、政府和劳动党的领袖们，参加庆祝大会的各国代表们和各友好国家驻阿外交人员。
