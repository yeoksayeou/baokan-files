Title: Death of Shirakawa Seiichi, Member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party; The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Sends a Telegram to the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party Expressing Condolences

Author:

Date: 1952-12-06
Page: 1

    Death of Shirakawa Seiichi, Member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party
    The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Sends a Telegram to the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party Expressing Condolences
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th:  The Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party issued an obituary, sadly announcing that Shirakawa Seiichi, a member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, passed away on October 29th at 3:00 PM due to illness and ineffective treatment.  Shirakawa Seiichi was 50 years old.

    General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Tokuda Kyuichi, issued a message of condolence on October 30th, mourning Shirakawa Seiichi. The message called on all party members to inherit Shirakawa Seiichi's revolutionary spirit, remember Shirakawa Seiichi's last words, strive to strengthen the cultivation of party members, and complete the great cause of national liberation that Shirakawa Seiichi was still thinking about one second before his death.

    The Japanese Communist Party held a funeral for Shirakawa Seiichi on November 10th. The Japanese Communist Party also decided to hold a formal funeral for Shirakawa Seiichi at the Aoyama Cemetery in Tokyo on March 15, 1953, and Shirakawa Seiichi's remains will be buried in the Tomb of Unknown Soldiers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, mourning the death of Comrade Shirakawa Seiichi, Member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party. The telegram reads as follows: To the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party:

    We are shocked to hear of the death of Comrade Shirakawa Seiichi, Member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and hereby express our condolences.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, December 5th

### 日共中央委员白川晴一逝世  中共中央致电日共中央表示哀悼

1952-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日共中央委员白川晴一逝世
    中共中央致电日共中央表示哀悼
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：日本共产党中央指导部发表讣闻，沉痛地宣布：日本共产党中央委员白川晴一因病医治无效，在十月二十九日下午三时逝世。白川晴一享年五十。
    日本共产党总书记德田球一在十月三十日发表唁辞，哀悼白川晴一。唁辞号召全体党员继承白川晴一的革命精神，记取白川晴一的遗训，努力加强党员的修养，完成白川晴一在临死前的一秒钟仍然念念不忘的民族解放的伟大事业。
    日本共产党已在十一月十日为白川晴一举行殡仪。日本共产党并决定在一九五三年三月十五日在东京青山公墓为白川晴一举行正式葬仪，白川晴一的遗体将埋葬在无名战士之墓。
    【新华社五日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电日本共产党中央委员会，哀悼日本共产党中央委员白川晴一同志逝世。电文如下：日本共产党中央委员会：
    惊闻日本共产党中央委员白川晴一同志逝世，谨致哀悼之忱。
    中国共产党中央委员会十二月五日
