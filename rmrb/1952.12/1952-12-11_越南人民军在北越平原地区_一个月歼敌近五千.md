Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates Nearly 5,000 Enemies in the North Vietnamese Plains in One Month
Author:
Date: 1952-12-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: The General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army announced that from October 25th to November 30th, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated more than 4,900 French invading troops in the North Vietnamese plains alone (prisoners and those who surrendered accounted for one-third), seized 3 cannons, 16 mortars, 26 heavy machine guns, 124 light machine guns, 411 submachine guns, 1,542 rifles, and a large number of other military supplies.
　　The Vietnamese People's Army expanded and consolidated its guerrilla bases on both sides of the Red River, isolated many French military strongholds, and forced the French troops besieged there to rely on air transportation for supplies.

### 越南人民军在北越平原地区  一个月歼敌近五千

1952-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在北越平原地区
    一个月歼敌近五千
    【新华社十日讯】越南通讯社北越讯：越南人民军总司令部公布：从十月二十五日起到十一月三十日止，越南人民军仅在北越平原便歼灭了法国侵略军四千九百人以上（俘掳的和投降过来的占三分之一），缴获了大炮三门、迫击炮十六门、重机枪二十六挺、轻机枪一百二十四挺、冲锋枪四百十一支、步枪一千五百四十二支以及大量其他军事供应品。
    越南人民军在红河两岸扩大和巩固了游击根据地，并孤立了法军据点多处，使困守在那里的法军须依赖空运接济。
