Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-12-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is the joyous scene of a bumper harvest at the Heping Collective Farm in Keshan County, Heilongjiang Province. In previous years, each laborer could only earn an average of more than 7,000 jin to 9,000 jin of grain; this year, those who worked well earned 15,000 jin per person.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是欢乐的黑龙江省克山县和平集体农庄的丰收景象。他们往年每个劳动力平均只能收入七千多斤到九千多斤粮食；今年劳动得好的每人收入一万五千斤。
