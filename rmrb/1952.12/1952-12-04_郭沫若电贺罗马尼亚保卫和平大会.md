Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Romanian Peace Conference
Author: 
Date: 1952-12-04
Page: 4

　　Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Romanian Peace Conference
    【Xinhua News Agency, 2nd】 The Romanian Peace Conference will open on December 5th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram on the 2nd to the Standing Committee of the Romanian People's Republic for the Defense of Peace to express his congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the Standing Committee of the Romanian People's Republic for the Defense of Peace:
    On the occasion of the opening of your country's Peace Conference, we, on behalf of the peace-loving people of all of China, extend our warm congratulations to the conference.
    We wish your conference success and wish the friendly unity between the Chinese and Romanian people in the cause of defending world peace to be increasingly consolidated and developed.
                       Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee

### 郭沫若电贺罗马尼亚保卫和平大会

1952-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若电贺罗马尼亚保卫和平大会
    【新华社二日讯】罗马尼亚保卫和平大会将在十二月五日开幕，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在二日致电罗马尼亚人民共和国保卫和平常设委员会致贺。该电电文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国保卫和平常设委员会：
    欣逢贵国保卫和平大会召开之期，我们谨代表爱好和平的全中国人民向大会致热烈的祝贺。
    谨祝你们的大会成功，并祝中、罗两国人民在保卫世界和平事业中的友爱团结日益巩固和发展。
                       中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若
