Title: Shanghai Laboring People and PLA Troops Stationed in Shanghai Hold a Meeting to Celebrate the 24th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1952-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 2nd: On December 1st, the Shanghai populace celebrated the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, which has been awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor, the Order of the Red Star, and the Order of the Red Banner of Battle, and is currently visiting Shanghai. That evening, the laboring people of East China and Shanghai and the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Shanghai held a grand celebration meeting.

Attending the celebration meeting were: Tan Zhenlin, Deputy Political Commissar of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Guo Huaruo, Commander of the Public Security Forces of the East China Military Region and Commander of the Shanghai-Songhu Garrison Area; Yuan Yielie, Commander of the Navy of the East China Military Region; Chen Huatang, Commander of the Shanghai Air Defense Forces of the East China Military Region; commanders, fighters, political workers, logistics workers, and literary and art workers of various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai; Pan Hannian, Vice Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government and Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; and responsible persons and people from all walks of life from the East China and Shanghai Branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and various people's organizations in East China and Shanghai, totaling more than 20,000 people. Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Shchetili, and Soviet representative of the All-Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries (VOKS) in Shanghai, V.N. Likhachev, also attended the celebration meeting.

The celebration meeting began with the orchestra playing the "Moscow-Beijing" tune. Then, Deputy Political Commissar Tan Zhenlin delivered a speech. On behalf of the East China Military Region troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the laboring people of East China and Shanghai, he extended his heartfelt congratulations and high respect to all comrades of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by B.A. Alexandrov. At this moment, the audience burst into warm applause and cheers. Subsequently, model worker Zhong Mingyue and Wang Lihe, winner of the Second-Class People's Hero Medal of East China, presented a banner to the head of the delegation, B.A. Alexandrov, on behalf of the laboring people of East China and Shanghai. The large red banner was inscribed with: "Warm congratulations to you, celebrating the glorious 24th anniversary of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by Comrade B.A. Alexandrov, in Shanghai! Your magnificent songs, cheerful dances, and beautiful music demonstrate the great achievements of socialist art, enable us to more intimately understand the great Soviet Union, and will surely further deepen the great friendship between the peoples of our two countries!" Qin Hualong, Director of the Political Department of the Shanghai-Songhu Garrison Area of the East China Military Region, also presented a banner to the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army East China Military Region troops stationed in Shanghai, inscribed with "Learn from the great Soviet Army." More than 200 female soldiers from the land, air, and sea forces of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army presented flowers to all members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble. Finally, B.A. Alexandrov delivered a reply amidst warm applause from the masses. He said: The warmth and heartfelt friendship and care you have shown to us is the love and friendship of the Chinese people for the Soviet people!

After the meeting, all members of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble celebrated their birthday with a grand evening banquet. Responsible persons from the East China and Shanghai Branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and senior officers from various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai also attended the banquet to congratulate the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble.

### 上海劳动人民及解放军驻上海部队举行大会  庆祝苏军红旗歌舞团诞生二十四周年

1952-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    上海劳动人民及解放军驻上海部队举行大会
    庆祝苏军红旗歌舞团诞生二十四周年
    【新华社上海二日电】十二月一日是正在上海访问的荣获荣誉革命红旗奖、红星勋章和战斗红旗勋章的苏军红旗歌舞团诞生二十四周年的纪念日。华东及上海市的劳动人民和中国人民解放军驻上海的部队在这天晚上举行了隆重的庆祝大会。
    参加庆祝大会的有：中国人民解放军华东军区副政治委员谭震林，华东军区公安部队司令员兼淞沪警备区司令员郭化若，华东军区海军司令员袁也烈，华东军区上海防空部队司令员陈华堂和中国人民解放军驻上海各部队的指挥员、战斗员、政治工作人员、后勤工作人员、文艺工作人员，上海市人民政府副市长、上海市中苏友好协会副会长潘汉年，上海市人民政府外事处长黄华以及华东和上海市中苏友好协会、华东和上海市各人民团体的负责人和各界人士共二万多人。苏联驻上海总领事施捷利、苏联对外文化协会驻上海代表魏立夏金等也参加了庆祝大会。
    庆祝大会开始，在乐队高奏“莫斯科—北京”乐曲后，谭震林副政委致词。他代表中国人民解放军华东军区部队，华东和上海市的劳动人民向以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团全体同志们表示衷心的祝贺和崇高的敬意。这时，全场爆发了热烈的鼓掌与欢呼。接着，劳动模范锺明月和华东二级人民英雄奖章获得者王立和代表华东暨上海市劳动人民向鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫团长献旗；大幅的红色锦旗上题写着：“热烈的庆贺你们，庆贺以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫同志为首的苏军红旗歌舞团在上海欢度你们光荣的二十四周年！你们雄壮的歌声、愉快的舞蹈、优美的音乐，表现了社会主义艺术的伟大成就，使我们更亲切地认识伟大的苏联，而必将进一步地加深我们两国人民之间的伟大友谊！”华东军区淞沪警备区政治部主任秦化龙也代表中国人民解放军华东军区驻上海部队向苏军红旗歌舞团献了“向伟大的苏联军队学习”的锦旗。中国人民解放军华东军区陆、空、海部队中的二百多位女战士，向苏军红旗歌舞团全体献花。最后鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫在群众热烈的欢呼声中致答词。他说：你们对我们所表示的这种热诚和由衷的友谊和爱护，是中国人民对苏联人民的热爱与友谊！
    会后，苏军红旗歌舞团全体同志以隆重的夜宴欢庆生日。华东和上海市中苏友好协会负责人和中国人民解放军驻上海各部队的高级军官等多人也出席了宴会，向苏军红旗歌舞团致贺。
