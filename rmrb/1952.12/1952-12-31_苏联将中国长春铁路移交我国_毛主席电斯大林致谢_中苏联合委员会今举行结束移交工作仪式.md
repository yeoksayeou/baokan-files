Title: Soviet Union Transfers Chinese Changchun Railway to China; Chairman Mao Cables Stalin to Express Gratitude; Sino-Soviet Joint Committee to Hold Ceremony Today to Conclude Transfer Work
Author: 
Date: 1952-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st: In accordance with the Sino-Soviet Agreement on the Chinese Changchun Railway, Port Arthur, and Dalian of February 14, 1950, and the Sino-Soviet Communiqué on the Transfer of the Chinese Changchun Railway of September 15, 1952, the government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics will, at the end of 1952, transfer to the government of the People's Republic of China, without compensation, all rights to the joint administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway, as well as all property belonging to the railway. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China cabled Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on December 31st, as the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee completed the transfer work, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to Comrade Stalin, the Soviet people, and the Soviet government on behalf of the Chinese people and government. The full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's message of thanks is as follows:

To Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:

During the period of Sino-Soviet joint administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway, the Soviet side made enormous contributions to the cause of railway construction for the Chinese people, and the Chinese people will never forget this fraternal and friendly assistance. As the Soviet government, in accordance with the 1950 agreement and the 1952 communiqué, is now transferring to the government of the People's Republic of China, without compensation, all rights to the joint administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway, as well as all property belonging to the railway, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to the great Soviet people, the Soviet government, and you, on behalf of the Chinese people and government. I also wish the great friendship between the Soviet Union and China to develop and consolidate day by day.

Mao Zedong
December 31, 1952

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, December 30th: The Sino-Soviet Joint Committee responsible for handling the transfer of the Chinese Changchun Railway has decided to hold the signing ceremony of the final record of the committee meeting at 1:00 PM on December 31st in Harbin. The Sino-Soviet co-management of the Chinese Changchun Railway will end at 6:00 PM Beijing time on the same day.

To commemorate the profound friendship of the Sino-Soviet co-management of the Chinese Changchun Railway, the memorial tower and memorial hall built in Harbin will be unveiled on January 1, 1953.

### 苏联将中国长春铁路移交我国  毛主席电斯大林致谢  中苏联合委员会今举行结束移交工作仪式

1952-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联将中国长春铁路移交我国
    毛主席电斯大林致谢
    中苏联合委员会今举行结束移交工作仪式
    【新华社三十一日讯】根据一九五○年二月十四日中苏关于中国长春铁路、旅顺口及大连的协定和一九五二年九月十五日中苏关于移交中国长春铁路的公告，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府在一九五二年年底将共同管理中国长春铁路的一切权利以及属于该路的全部财产无偿地移交中华人民共和国政府。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席特于十二月三十一日中苏联合委员会完成移交工作之际，致电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林同志，代表中国人民和政府向斯大林同志、苏联人民和苏联政府表示衷心的感谢。毛泽东主席致谢电全文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林同志：
    在中苏两国共同管理中国长春铁路期间，苏联方面对于中国人民铁路建设事业作了巨大的贡献，中国人民对此兄弟般的友谊援助将永志不忘。兹值苏联政府根据一九五○年的协定和一九五二年的公告将共同管理中国长春铁路的一切权利以及属于该路的全部财产无偿地移交给中华人民共和国政府之际，我谨代表中国人民和政府向伟大的苏联人民、苏联政府及您表示衷心的感谢。并祝苏联和中国伟大友谊的日益发展和巩固。
    毛泽东
    一九五二年十二月三十一日
    【新华社哈尔滨三十日电】负责办理中国长春铁路移交事宜的中苏联合委员会已决定十二月三十一日午后一时在哈尔滨举行该委员会会议终结纪录的签署仪式，中国长春铁路的中苏共管将在同日北京时间十八时结束。
    为了纪念中苏共管中国长春铁路的深厚友谊，在哈尔滨修建的纪念塔和纪念馆，将在一九五三年一月一日揭幕。
