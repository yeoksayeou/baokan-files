Title: Soviet Artists Continue Visits to Changsha, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou, etc. Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation Returns to Moscow on the 6th

Author:
Date: 1952-12-08
Page: 1

    Soviet Artists Continue Visits to Changsha, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou, etc.
    Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation Returns to Moscow on the 6th

    Xinhua News Agency: The leader of the Soviet art delegation, Tsuraki, and members Halima Nasyrova, Obraztsov, and fourteen others, arrived in Changsha by plane from Beijing on the afternoon of the 6th. Welcoming them at the airport were Jin Ming, Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as more than 50 representatives of literary and art workers, workers, youth, and women from Hunan Province and Changsha City. In the evening, the Hunan Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association hosted a banquet.

    After arriving in Hangzhou on the 1st, Tamara Hanon and other members of the Soviet art delegation, at the invitation of the Zhejiang Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held two grand performances, which were warmly welcomed by the audience. The members also toured the West Lake and scenic spots such as Lingyin Temple, Yuquan, and Huanglong Cave in Hangzhou, and visited the Chinese Railway Workers' Sanatorium located in Liu Zhuang, West Lake. Tamara Hanon and others left Hangzhou on the evening of the 3rd and arrived in Nanchang City on the afternoon of the 4th. Shao Shiping, Vice Chairman of the Jiangxi Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and more than 5,000 representatives from all walks of life welcomed them at the station. That evening, the Jiangxi Provincial and Nanchang City Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations held a welcome banquet. Tamara Hanon and others held two performance meetings in Nanchang, performing a total of nineteen wonderful programs, each of which was warmly welcomed by the audience. The Jiangxi Provincial and Nanchang City People's Broadcasting Stations broadcast the live performances twice, with more than 143,000 people tuning in. Tamara Hanon and others left Nanchang by car on the morning of the 7th.

    The Soviet Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble has been conducting visits, seminars, and other activities in Shanghai for several days. On the morning of the 4th, some members of the song and dance ensemble went to the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory and No. 19 Cotton Textile Factory to have a get-together with the workers of Shanghai. On the same evening, the members participated in a radio rally organized by the East China and Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations and the People's Broadcasting Station. On the 5th and 6th, Bao A. Alexandrov, head of the song and dance ensemble, Vinogradov, leader of the choir, and Vilski, leader of the dance troupe, were invited to participate in music and dance seminars respectively. The East China and Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations held four performance receptions from the 2nd to the 4th to entertain the Soviet Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble.

    After arriving in Hangzhou on the afternoon of the 2nd, the Soviet Film Artists Delegation met with a large number of Hangzhou viewers who love Soviet films in the Victory, Xinhua, and Pacific cinemas that evening, and were warmly welcomed. The representatives also toured the West Lake in Hangzhou and visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Yue Fei Temple, Lingyin Temple, and Huanglong Cave. The delegation left Hangzhou on the 3rd and arrived in Guangzhou on the 6th. Shi Fude, the chief representative of the Soviet Film Export Company in China, accompanied them. More than 5,000 workers, students, and film artists from Guangzhou held a welcome meeting on the station square. Zhu Guang, vice president of the Guangzhou Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the people of Guangzhou. Fedorov also delivered a speech at the meeting to express his gratitude.

    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th] The Soviet cultural workers' delegation, led by团长吉洪诺夫(Tuánzhǎng Jíhōngnuòfū), who were invited to China to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," returned to Moscow on the 6th.

### 苏联艺术家续访长沙杭州南昌广州等地  苏联文化工作者代表团六日返抵莫斯科

1952-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家续访长沙杭州南昌广州等地
    苏联文化工作者代表团六日返抵莫斯科
    据新华社讯：苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基和团员哈利马·纳赛罗娃、奥布拉兹卓夫等十四人，六日下午由北京乘飞机抵达长沙。到机场欢迎的有湖南省中苏友好协会会长金明以及湖南省、长沙市文艺工作者、工人、青年、妇女代表五十余人。晚间，湖南省中苏友好协会设宴招待。
    苏联艺术工作团团员塔马拉·哈侬等一行八人在一日到达杭州后，应浙江省中苏友好协会的邀请，举行了两次盛大的演出，受到观众的热烈欢迎。团员们在杭州还游览了西湖和灵隐、玉泉、黄龙洞等名胜，并曾往设在西湖刘庄的中国铁路工会工人休养所参观。塔马拉·哈侬等于三日晚间离开杭州，四日下午抵达南昌市。江西省中苏友好协会副会长邵式平以及各界代表共五千多人在车站欢迎。当晚，江西省和南昌市中苏友好协会举行了欢迎宴会。塔马拉·哈侬等在南昌举行了两次表演会，共演出了十九个精彩节目，每个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。江西省、南昌市人民广播电台并广播了两次表演会的实况，参加收听的有十四万三千多人。塔马拉·哈侬等于七日上午乘车离开南昌。
    苏军红旗歌舞团连日在上海进行访问、座谈等活动。四日上午，歌舞团部分团员分别到国营上海第一棉纺织厂和第十九棉纺织厂和上海工人们联欢。同日晚，团员们参加了华东和上海市中苏友好协会及人民广播电台等单位主办的广播大会。五、六两日，歌舞团团长鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫，合唱队长维诺格拉陀夫和舞蹈队长维尔斯基，分别应邀参加音乐座谈会和舞蹈座谈会。华东和上海市中苏友好协会在二日到四日先后举行四次演出招待会，招待苏军红旗歌舞团。
    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团二日下午到达杭州后，当晚分别在胜利、新华、太平洋等三家电影院里和杭州市广大爱好苏联电影的观众见面，受到热烈的欢迎。代表们在杭州还游览了西湖，参观了岳飞庙、灵隐寺、黄龙洞等名胜古迹。代表团于三日离杭州，六日到达广州。同行的有苏联影片输出公司驻中国总代表史福德。广州市工人、学生和电影艺术工作者等五千多人在车站广场上举行了欢迎会。广州市中苏友好协会副会长朱光代表广州各界人民致欢迎词。费道罗夫也在会上致词表示谢意。
    【新华社莫斯科七日电】应邀到中国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联文化工作者代表团在团长吉洪诺夫率领下，在六日返抵莫斯科。
