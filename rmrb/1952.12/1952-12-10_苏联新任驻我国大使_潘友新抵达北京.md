Title: New Soviet Ambassador to China, P.F. Yudin, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Former Soviet Ambassador to China, N.V. Roshchin, has been recalled to his country, and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed P.F. Yudin as the Ambassador to China.

Ambassador Yudin arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow on the afternoon of the 9th. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council Qi Yanming, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department Xu Yixin, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Yang Chengwu, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Qian Junrui, among others.

Staff members of the Soviet Embassy in China, headed by Chargé d'affaires ad interim Goudev, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 苏联新任驻我国大使  潘友新抵达北京

1952-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联新任驻我国大使
    潘友新抵达北京
    【新华社九日讯】苏联前任驻我国大使罗申已奉调返国，苏联最高苏维埃主席团已任命潘友新为驻我国大使。
    潘友新大使在九日下午乘飞机由莫斯科到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，政务院副秘书长齐燕铭，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司司长徐以新、交际处处长王倬如，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗及中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞等人。
    以顾德夫临时代办为首的苏联驻我国大使馆人员，以及各国驻我国使节也到机场欢迎。
