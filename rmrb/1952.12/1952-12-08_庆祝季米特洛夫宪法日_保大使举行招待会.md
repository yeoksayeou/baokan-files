Title: Ambassador of Bulgaria Holds Reception to Celebrate Dimitrov Constitution Day
Author:
Date: 1952-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the Dimitrov Constitution Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov, held a film screening at the Capital Cinema in Beijing on the evening of the 7th, screening the Bulgarian feature film "Dawn of the Motherland" to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital.

Those invited to attend included Zhang Zhizhiang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government, Zhang Xiruo and Peng Zemin, Deputy Directors of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in the capital.

Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China were also invited to attend.

### 庆祝季米特洛夫宪法日  保大使举行招待会

1952-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝季米特洛夫宪法日
    保大使举行招待会
    【新华社讯】为庆祝保加利亚人民共和国季米特洛夫宪法日五周年纪念，保加利亚驻我国大使彼得科夫特于七日晚在北京首都电影院举行电影放映会，放映保加利亚故事片“祖国的黎明”，招待首都各界人士。
    应邀参加的有中央人民政府最高人民法院副院长张志让，政务院政治法律委员会副主任张奚若、彭泽民，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，以及首都各界人士一千多人。
    各国驻华使节也应邀参加。
