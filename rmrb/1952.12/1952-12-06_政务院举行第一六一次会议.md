Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 161st Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-12-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th—The 161st meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 5th. The meeting heard a report by Zhang Linchi, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government and head of the Chinese Farmers Delegation, on his visit to Soviet agricultural construction. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment and removal cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第一六一次会议

1952-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六一次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十一次政务会议在五日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府农业部副部长、中国农民代表团团长张林池所作的参观苏联农业建设的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
