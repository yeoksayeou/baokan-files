Title: Our Delegation Participating in the World Peace Congress of the Peoples Embarks on Return Trip

Author:
Date: 1952-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, December 23rd: The Chinese delegation participating in the World Peace Congress of the Peoples left Vienna on the 23rd to embark on its return trip. During the Congress, the Chinese delegation was highly regarded and respected. At the Congress, the head of the Chinese delegation, Soong Ching Ling, delivered a speech entitled "The People Can Turn the Tide," and the deputy head, Guo Moruo, made a report on "Stopping the Existing Wars," both of which received warm welcomes and widespread support. The speech by delegate Professor Chen Wengui on the U.S. military's bacteriological warfare in Korea and China aroused great attention. Members of the Chinese delegation also participated in the work of the various subcommittees of the Congress, jointly consulting with representatives from various countries on the various issues that the people of the world urgently need to resolve. The delegation successfully completed the mission entrusted to it by the Chinese people.

### 参加世界人民和平大会的我国代表团启程返国

1952-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    参加世界人民和平大会的我国代表团启程返国
    【新华社维也纳二十三日电】参加世界人民和平大会的中国代表团在二十三日离开维也纳启程返国。在大会期间，中国代表团极受重视与尊敬。在大会上，中国代表团团长宋庆龄曾作了“人民能够扭转局势”的讲话，副团长郭沫若作了关于“停止现有战争”的报告，都受到了热烈的欢迎及得到了广泛的支持。团员陈文贵教授作的关于美军在朝鲜及中国进行细菌战的发言引起了很大的重视。中国代表团团员们还分别参加了大会各小组委员会的工作，与各国代表们共同协商全世界人民所迫切需要解决的各项问题。代表团顺利完成了中国人民的托付。
