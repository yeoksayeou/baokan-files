Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party National Conference Closes; Unanimously Adopts Resolution Fully Endorsing Gottwald's Report
Author:
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 25th: The National Conference of the Czechoslovak Communist Party victoriously concluded in Prague on the 18th, lasting three days.

    The meetings on the evening of the 16th and on the 17th discussed the report by Klement Gottwald. Those who spoke during the discussion included: Deputy Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic Zdeněk Fierlinger, Deputy Prime Minister Jan Ďuriš, Minister of National Defense A. Čepička, Minister of Food Procurement J. Krosnář, Minister of Information and Culture V. Kopecký, Minister of the Interior V. Nosek, Chairman of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions G. Kliment, and General Ya. Seman, Director of the General Political Department of the Czechoslovak Army, as well as members and secretaries of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, secretaries of many state committees and members, secretaries of district committees, and advanced figures from industry, agriculture, and the arts. At the meeting on the 16th, a reply telegram to the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was also read. Those present passed this telegram amidst stormy and prolonged applause and cheers, and the entire venue shouted "Long live Stalin!", "Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!", "Stalin—Gottwald—Peace!".

    At the meeting on the 18th, the Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, delivered a closing speech, summarizing the achievements of the Party's National Conference. He emphasized that this conference strengthened the Party's unity and combat effectiveness, and made fundamental instructions - Comrade Gottwald's report and the new Party Constitution - in order to more resolutely unite the Party to fight for the victory of socialism in Czechoslovakia. The National Conference unanimously adopted resolutions fully endorsing Gottwald's report and the new Party Constitution, and passed resolutions on the resignation and removal of several members and alternate members of the Central Committee, as well as resolutions on the promotion of alternate Central Committee members to Central Committee members and the election of new alternate Central Committee members.

    After the National Conference, the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party immediately held a meeting.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共产党全国代表会议闭幕  一致通过决议完全赞同哥特瓦尔德的报告

1952-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共产党全国代表会议闭幕
    一致通过决议完全赞同哥特瓦尔德的报告
    【新华社二十五日讯】布拉格迟到消息：捷克斯洛伐克共产党全国代表会议十八日在布拉格胜利闭幕，会议共开了三天。
    十六日晚间和十七日的会议讨论了克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的报告。在讨论时发言的有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府副总理兹德尼克·费林格、副总理雅·杜兰斯基、国防部部长阿·契皮茨卡、粮食采购部部长约·克罗斯纳日、宣传和文化部部长瓦·柯别茨基、内务部部长瓦·诺西克、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会主席古·克利门特和捷克斯洛伐克军队总政治部主任雅·塞曼将军以及捷克斯洛伐克共产党的中央委员、许多州委员会书记和委员、区委员会书记，以及工、农、艺术界的先进人物等多人。十六日的会议上还宣读了对苏联共产党中央委员会贺电的复电。到会的人在暴风雨般经久不息的掌声和欢呼声中通过了这一电文，全场高呼“斯大林万岁！”，“苏联共产党万岁！”，“斯大林—哥特瓦尔德—和平！”。
    十八日会议上，捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理安·萨波托斯基致闭幕词，他总结了党的全国代表会议的成就。他着重指出：这次会议加强了党的团结和战斗力，并作出了基本的指示——哥特瓦尔德同志的报告和新党章——以便更坚决地团结党为社会主义在捷克斯洛伐克的胜利而斗争。全国代表会议一致通过了完全赞同哥特瓦尔德的报告和新党章的各项决议，并通过了关于若干中央委员和候补委员辞职和免职的决议，以及由候补中央委员递补中央委员和选举新候补中央委员的决议。
    全国代表会议结束后，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会立即举行了会议。
