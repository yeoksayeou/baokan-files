Title: The Dalai Lama Solemnly Accepts Gifts from Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Others
Author:
Date: 1952-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 22nd: The Dalai Lama of the Tibetan local government recently accepted gifts from Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, and Chairman Li Weihan of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government in Lhasa with immense gratitude. On December 14th, the Dalai Lama received Gui Xiangba·Gongga Najia, Sanduocang·Luosang Gedeng, and Li Zhigan, three representatives from the Tibetan region attending the preparatory meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in Beijing, at Norbulingka in Lhasa. The representatives presented the Dalai Lama with more than 110 valuable gifts, including a film projector, films, Jiangxi porcelain, Hangzhou silk, and personal letters from Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Chairman Li. When the gift-giving procession arrived at Norbulingka, the Dalai Lama's band and guards went to greet them. Amidst the music, the Dalai Lama personally accepted the letters from Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou and performed the "head-raising" (holding the letters above the head) ceremony, the most respectful Tibetan etiquette, to express his respect. The representatives reported to the Dalai Lama on the implementation of the policies of national equality and freedom of religious belief they had witnessed during their visits to various parts of the motherland, as well as the rapid development of economic, cultural, and other construction undertakings. The Dalai Lama expressed his deep gratitude to Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Chairman Li for their concern.

### 达赖喇嘛隆重接受毛主席周总理等的礼品

1952-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛隆重接受毛主席周总理等的礼品
    【新华社拉萨二十二日电】西藏地方政府达赖喇嘛最近以万分感激的心情在拉萨接受了中央人民政府毛泽东主席、政务院周恩来总理、中央人民政府民族事务委员会李维汉主任委员赠与的礼品。达赖喇嘛于十二月十四日在拉萨罗布林卡接见了出席北京中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的西藏地区代表桂香巴·贡尕那嘉、三多仓·罗桑格登、黎之淦等三人。代表们送上毛主席、周总理和李维汉主任委员送给达赖喇嘛的电影放映机、影片、江西瓷器、杭州绸缎以及亲笔信件等各种名贵礼品共一百一十余件。当送礼行列到达罗布林卡时，达赖喇嘛的乐队和卫队前往迎接。在乐声中，达赖喇嘛亲手接受毛主席和周总理的信件，并按西藏最尊敬的礼节举行“头顶”（将信件举至头上）表示敬意。代表们向达赖喇嘛报告了他们在祖国各地参观时看到民族平等、宗教信仰自由政策贯彻执行的情况，和经济、文化等建设事业飞跃发展的情况。达赖喇嘛万分感激毛主席、周总理、李主任委员对他的关怀。
