Title: Northwest Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-08
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 3rd) The Northwest Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, consisting of 101 members, left Beijing on the 3rd to visit the Northeast and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Among those who saw them off at the station were Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Luo Wang, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director, and cadres from the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government.

During their stay in Beijing, the Northwest Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation visited factories, schools, scenic spots, historical sites, Buddhist temples, and mosques in the capital.

### 西北牧区各民族参观团离京

1952-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

     西北牧区各民族参观团离京
    【新华社三日讯】西北牧区各民族参观团一百零一人于三日离北京赴东北和内蒙古自治区参观。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷，班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺、副处长连慧珠及中央人民政府民族事务委员会干部等多人。
    西北牧区各民族参观团在北京期间，曾参观了首都的工厂、学校、名胜、古迹和佛教寺院、清真寺。
