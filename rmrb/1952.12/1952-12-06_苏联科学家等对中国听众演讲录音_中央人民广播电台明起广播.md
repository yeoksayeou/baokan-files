Title: Recordings of Speeches by Soviet Scientists and Others to Chinese Listeners to be Broadcast by the Central People's Broadcasting Station Starting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1952-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: At the request of the Central Broadcasting Bureau of our country, the Soviet Broadcasting Committee, during the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month," specially invited ten Soviet scientists, writers, film directors and actors, Soviet heroes, advanced producers, and well-known social figures familiar to the Chinese people to give speeches to Chinese radio listeners, and recorded and sent them. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the recordings of these speeches in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Special Program" on December 7th, 8th, and 9th. The broadcasting time each day is from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM. The broadcasting dates and content are as follows:

    December 7th: (1) Speech by Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; (2) Speech by Professor Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; (3) Speech by Simonov, Soviet writer and Stalin Prize winner; (4) Speech by Soviet writer Kozhevnikov.

    December 8th: (1) Speech by Kosmodemyanskaya, mother of the two Soviet heroes Zoya and Shura; (2) Speech by Soviet hero Maresyev; (3) Speech by Dubkovetsky, Deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Hero of Socialist Labor, and Chairman of the "October Victory" Collective Farm in the Kiev Region of Ukraine; (4) Speech by Rozhneva, female worker of the Soviet Kubavin Fine Wool Factory, Stakhanovite worker, and Stalin Prize winner.

    December 9th: (1) Speech by film director and Soviet People's Actor Gerasimov; (2) Speech by Natasha Zashchipina, a seventh-grade student who starred in "First Grader" and "They Have a Motherland" (the recording of this speech will also be broadcast on the Central People's Broadcasting Station's children's program at 5:30 PM on the same day).

    The Central Broadcasting Bureau of our country has also received a large number of musical and drama program recordings from the Soviet Broadcasting Committee, which will be broadcast successively on the Central People's Broadcasting Station.

### 苏联科学家等对中国听众演讲录音  中央人民广播电台明起广播

1952-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家等对中国听众演讲录音
    中央人民广播电台明起广播
    据新华社讯：苏联广播委员会应我国中央广播事业局的要求，在“中苏友好月”期间，特邀为中国人民所熟悉的苏联科学家、作家、电影导演和演员、苏联英雄、先进生产者和社会知名人士等十人对中国广播听众演讲，并代为录音寄来。中央人民广播电台定于十二月七日、八日、九日在“中苏友好特别节目”中广播这一批讲话的录音。每天广播时间为下午七点到八点。广播日期和内容如下：
    十二月七日：（一）苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫演讲；（二）苏联对外文化协会主席吉尼索夫教授演讲；（三）苏联作家、斯大林奖金获得者西蒙诺夫演讲；（四）苏联作家科热夫尼科夫演讲。
    十二月八日：（一）两位苏联英雄卓娅和舒拉的母亲柯斯莫捷米扬斯卡娅演讲；（二）苏联英雄马列西耶夫演讲；（三）苏联最高苏维埃代表、社会主义劳动英雄、乌克兰基也辅州“十月胜利”集体农庄主席杜布柯维茨基演讲；（四）苏联库巴维因细绒工厂女工、斯达哈诺夫工作者、斯大林奖金获得者罗日聂娃演讲。
    十二月九日：（一）电影导演、苏联人民演员格拉西莫夫演讲；（二）七年级学生、主演“一年级小学生”和“他们有祖国”的娜塔莎·札希品娜娅演讲（这一演讲的录音并将在中央人民广播电台同日下午五点半的少年儿童节目中播送）。
    我国中央广播事业局同时收到苏联广播委员会寄赠的大批音乐和戏剧节目录音，这些录音也将在中央人民广播电台陆续广播。
