Title: Soviet Friends Are With Us (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1952-12-06
Page: 5

The photos in this issue's pictorial section, besides those from the Xinhua News Agency, are provided by the East China and Shanghai "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" Preparatory Committee, Northeast Daily, Yangtze River Daily, Chongqing Xinhua Daily, and Zhejiang Daily.

　　When the Soviet cultural workers delegation, headed by Tikhonov, arrived in Shanghai on November 26th, they were warmly welcomed by the people of Shanghai at the train station square. (Photo by Chen Yanru)
　　Churaki, leader of the Soviet art troupe, and members, including the ballet dancer Ulanova, look out over "Three Pools Mirroring the Moon" on the Su Causeway of West Lake in Hangzhou. (Photo by Zhao Dingyin)
　　Tikhonov, Yefimov, and others visit the state-owned Shanghai No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory. (Photo by Wang Chaozhen)
　　When the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by B.A. Alexandrov, arrived in Shanghai, they were warmly welcomed by the people of East China and Shanghai. (Photo by Wang Chaozhen)
　　On November 16th, Tikhonov, leader of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, Churaki, leader of the Soviet art troupe, and others paid tribute at the International Cemetery in Wuhan to the fifteen Soviet Air Force martyrs who sacrificed their lives in 1938 fighting against the Japanese invaders to help China resist Japan. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Jia Huamin)
　　Tikhonov, leader of the Soviet cultural workers delegation, and members Grusenko and Yefimov visited Chairman Mao's hometown – Shaoshan Township, Xiangtan County, Hunan on November 19th. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)
　　Tikhonov and others tour East Lake in Wuhan. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)
　　When the Soviet film artists delegation, headed by Fedorov, arrived in Shanghai on November 25th, they walked out of the Shanghai train station amidst a warm welcome from the masses. (Photo by Wang Gang)
　　Tikhonov, Churaki, Ulanova, and others visit the Chrysanthemum Exhibition in Wuhan. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)
　　Tikhonov and others visit the Chuanshan Academy in Chairman Mao's hometown – Shaoshan Township, Xiangtan County. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)
　　The Soviet cultural workers delegation and the Soviet art troupe, a total of twelve people, arrived in Chongqing on November 25th, and over ten thousand people held a welcoming rally in Liberation Monument Square. (Photo by Li Rongqing)
　　The Soviet cultural workers delegation and the Soviet art troupe members, a total of fourteen people, led by the famous composer Zakharov, arrived in Xi'an on November 18th. When the delegates entered the city by car, they were warmly welcomed by the masses. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Yuan Hao)
　　At the symposium in Shenyang where people from all walks of life welcomed the Soviet film artists delegation, Tian Guiying, a Chinese female train driver, vividly recounted the encouragement and education she received from the excellent Soviet film art. (Photo by Liang Feng)
　　At the invitation of the China Peace Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions, Tikhonov gave a report in Wuhan on "Sino-Soviet Friendship and World Peace." When Tikhonov and others arrived at the Wuhan University venue, they were warmly welcomed by the masses. (Xinhua News Agency photo by Fang Hong)

### 苏联朋友和我们在一起（图片）

1952-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联朋友和我们在一起
    本期画刊照片除新华社稿外，是由华东暨上海市“中苏友好月”筹备委员会、东北日报、长江日报、重庆新华日报和浙江日报供给的。
    以吉洪诺夫为首的苏联文化工作者代表团于十一月二十六日抵达上海时，在车站广场受到上海市人民热烈的欢迎。（陈雁如摄）
    苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基和团员、巴蕾舞名演员乌兰诺娃等在杭州西湖苏堤上远眺“三潭印月”。（赵丁寅摄）
    吉洪诺夫、叶菲莫夫等参观国营上海第二棉纺织厂。（王朝桢摄）
    以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团抵达上海时，曾受到华东暨上海市人民的热烈欢迎。（王朝桢摄）
    苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫和苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基等，于十一月十六日到武汉市万国公墓凭吊在一九三八年为帮助中国抗战而与日本侵略者作战牺牲的十五位苏联空军烈士。（新华社稿·贾化民摄）
    苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫、团员格鲁森科、叶菲莫夫于十一月十九日访问了毛主席故乡——湖南湘潭县韶山乡。
    （新华社稿·方弘摄）
    吉洪诺夫等在武汉游览东湖。
    （新华社稿·方弘摄）
    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团团长费道罗夫等于十一月二十五日抵达上海时，在群众热烈的欢迎中走出上海车站。（汪刚摄）
    吉洪诺夫、楚拉基、乌兰诺娃等在武汉参观菊花展览会。（新华社稿·方弘摄）
    吉洪诺夫等在毛主席故乡——湖南湘潭县韶山乡参观船山学社。（新华社稿·方弘摄）
    苏联文化工作者代表团和苏联艺术工作团一行十二人于十一月二十五日抵达重庆，一万余群众在解放碑广场举行了欢迎大会。（李荣卿摄）
    苏联文化工作者代表团和苏联艺术工作团团员一行十四人，在著名作曲家查哈罗夫率领下于十一月十八日抵达西安。当代表们乘车进入市区时，受到群众热烈欢迎。（新华社稿·袁浩摄）
    在沈阳市各界人民欢迎苏联电影艺术工作者代表团的座谈会上，中国女火车司机田桂英生动地讲述了优秀的苏联电影艺术给予她的鼓舞和教育。　　（梁枫摄）
    吉洪诺夫在武汉市应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会中南总分会的邀请，作“中苏友好与世界和平”的报告。当吉洪诺夫等到达武汉大学会场时，受到群众热烈欢迎。
    （新华社稿·方弘摄）
