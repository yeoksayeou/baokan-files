Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Sends Representatives to Mongolia to Attend the Congress of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association

Author:

Date: 1952-12-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association is scheduled to hold a congress in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, on December 20th. At the invitation of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has specially dispatched Lai Yali, Deputy Secretary-General of the Headquarters, and Gong Zhuo, Deputy Director of the Organization Department, as representatives, and they left Beijing by plane on the 17th to go to Ulaanbaatar to participate.

    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters; Pan Defeng, Deputy Director of the Secretariat; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

    Jalkhasaikhan, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.

### 中苏友协总会派代表赴蒙古  参加蒙苏文化协会代表大会

1952-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会派代表赴蒙古
    参加蒙苏文化协会代表大会
    【新华社十八日讯】蒙苏文化协会订于十二月二十日在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托举行代表大会。中苏友好协会总会应蒙苏文化协会的邀请特派总会副总干事赖亚力和组织部副主任弓濯之为代表，在十七日乘飞机离北京前往乌兰巴托参加。
    前往机场送行的有：中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾、秘书处副主任潘德枫，中央人民政府外交部亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如等。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗及使馆人员也到机场送行。
