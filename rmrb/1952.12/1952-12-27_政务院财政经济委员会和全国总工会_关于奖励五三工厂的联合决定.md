Title: Joint Decision of the Government Administration Council Financial and Economic Committee and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on Rewarding the 53rd Factory
Author:
Date: 1952-12-27
Page: 1

    Government Administration Council Financial and Economic Committee and All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    Joint Decision on Rewarding the 53rd Factory
    The Party, government, labor union, and Communist Youth League organizations of the Northeast 53rd Factory, based on the clear ideology of relying on the working class to improve production, led all employees, highly stimulated the enthusiasm and creativity of the workers, overcame various difficulties, transformed the old enterprise, established a scientific management system, and overfulfilled the national plan year after year, creating excellent achievements and advanced experiences. In order to encourage all employees of the 53rd Factory to continue their efforts and achieve new victories, and also to promote the advanced experiences of the 53rd Factory in factories and mining enterprises throughout the country, it is hereby decided to grant the 53rd Factory the honorary title of Model Factory, and to issue a prize banner and a bonus of 500 million yuan. Responsible comrades from this committee and the federation will be sent to the 53rd Factory to present the banner and award the prize.
    Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government Administration Council
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    December 24, 1952

### 政务院财政经济委员会和全国总工会  关于奖励五三工厂的联合决定

1952-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院财政经济委员会和全国总工会
    关于奖励五三工厂的联合决定
    东北五三工厂的党、政、工、团组织，在明确的依靠工人阶级搞好生产的思想基础上，领导全体职工，高度发挥了职工群众的积极性和创造性，克服了各种困难，改造了旧企业，建立了科学的经营管理制度，逐年超额完成国家计划，创造了优越的成绩和先进经验。为了鼓励五三工厂全体职工同志们再接再厉，取得新的胜利，也为了在全国各厂矿企业推广五三工厂的先进经验，特决定授予五三工厂以模范工厂的光荣称号，并发给奖旗一面、奖金伍亿元，并由本委、会派负责同志到五三工厂授旗发奖。
    中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会
    中华全国总工会
    一九五二年十二月二十四日
