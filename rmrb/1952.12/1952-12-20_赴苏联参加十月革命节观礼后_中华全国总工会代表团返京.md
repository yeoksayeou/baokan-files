Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending Soviet October Revolution Celebration
Author:
Date: 1952-12-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 19th—The thirteen-member delegation from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, led by Ma Peixun, returned to Beijing on December 17th after attending the celebrations for the 35th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union. After the celebrations, the delegation visited many factories, cultural palaces, and Young Pioneer Palaces in Moscow, Stalingrad, Leningrad, Gorky, and Sochi, the famous resort area on the Black Sea. They also visited the great communist construction project of the Lenin Canal between the Volga and Don Rivers. On their way back to China, they gave reports in Shenyang, Harbin, and other places in the Northeast, sharing their impressions of their visit to the Soviet Union with the workers.

### 赴苏联参加十月革命节观礼后  中华全国总工会代表团返京

1952-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    赴苏联参加十月革命节观礼后
    中华全国总工会代表团返京
    【新华社十九日讯】中华全国总工会派赴苏联参加十月社会主义革命三十五周年观礼的代表团一行十三人，在马佩勋率领下于十二月十七日返抵北京。该代表团观礼后在苏联莫斯科、斯大林格勒、列宁格勒、高尔基及黑海岸著名的疗养区索契等地参观了许多工厂、文化宫、少先宫，并参观了伏尔加河与顿河之间的列宁运河这一伟大的共产主义建设工程。他们在回国路过东北时，曾向沈阳、哈尔滨等地工人作了访苏观感的报告。
