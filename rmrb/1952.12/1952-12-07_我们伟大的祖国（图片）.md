Our Great Motherland
	The Northeast State-operated "September 3rd" Mechanical Farm possesses 150,000 mu of land. Thanks to timely sowing this year, the per unit area yield of soybeans increased by 20% compared to last year. This is the farm's motor vehicle team. They are transporting the harvested soybeans to the soybean stacking yard.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北国营九三机械农场，拥有十五万市亩的土地。由于今年及时播种，大豆的单位面积产量比去年提高百分之二十。这是农场的汽车队。它们正把收获后的大豆运到堆豆场去。
