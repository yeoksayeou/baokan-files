Title: Second National Health Conference Opens in Beijing; Summarizing the Experience of the Patriotic Health Campaign This Year; Conference Elects Presidium and National Health Model Award Committee
Author: 
Date: 1952-12-09
Page: 1

    Second National Health Conference Opens in Beijing
    Summarizing the Experience of the Patriotic Health Campaign This Year
    Conference Elects Presidium and National Health Model Award Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 8th] The Second National Health Conference solemnly opened in Beijing on the 8th.
    The main purpose of this conference is to summarize the experience of this year's Patriotic Health Campaign. In order to hold this conference successfully, various regions have conducted inspections of the Patriotic Health Campaign, selected national health models, and prepared the National Patriotic Health Campaign Exhibition.
    Participating in this conference were 152 representatives of national health model units and model workers, 47 specially invited representatives of well-known medical and health experts from across the country, all 15 members of the Chinese health workers delegation to the Soviet Union, 19 Soviet medical and health experts, representatives from units directly under the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government and the Health Department of the General Logistics Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, as well as representatives from relevant agencies of the Central People's Government and national people's organizations, totaling 775 attendees and observers.
    On either side of the rostrum hung Chairman Mao's inscription for the conference: "Get mobilized, pay attention to hygiene, reduce diseases, improve health, and smash the enemy's germ warfare." Commander-in-Chief Zhu's inscription for the conference read: "Unite health workers nationwide, rely on the broad masses, implement the principle of prevention first, and strive to guarantee national defense, economic, and cultural construction." These inscriptions indicated a clear direction for this conference and for the future Patriotic Health Campaign and people's health care endeavors. The atmosphere at the venue was very solemn and grand. When the representatives of national health model units and model workers, wearing red flowers, entered the venue, the audience responded with enthusiastic applause.
    After Vice Minister of Health of the Central People's Government, Fu Lianzhang, delivered the opening speech, the conference elected the presidium. Minister of Health of the Central People's Government Li Dequan, Vice Ministers He Cheng, Su Jingguan, Fu Lianzhang, and Xu Yunbei, Vice Ministers of the Health Department of the General Logistics Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Sun Yizhi and Dai Zhenghua, Director of the Central Institute of Health Shen Qizhen, as well as Zhang Gongzhi, Deputy Mayor of Qingdao Municipality who was selected as a national health model worker this time, Ren Sizhong, Political Commissar of a certain division of the People's Liberation Army, Li Hao, County Head of Chang'an County, Shaanxi Province, health models Li Zhongbo, Lu Dama, Pan Yuexian, representatives of model units Liu Xuanting, Wen Junkui, Chen Jingxin, and Sun Rongguo, and well-known medical and health experts from across the country, totaling 52 people, were elected to the presidium.
    The conference also unanimously elected the list of members for the National Health Model Award Committee, the Exhibition Review Committee, and others.
    Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, Ma Xulun, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that this year's Patriotic Health Campaign not only thwarted the US imperialists' conspiracy to wage germ warfare, but also reduced the incidence of disease and improved people's health throughout the country. Especially in the just war of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea, our health workers have made their greatest efforts selflessly at the front and in the rear, strengthened the fighting forces, and established great merits in defending the motherland, which the people will never forget. He called on everyone to consolidate the achievements of this year's Patriotic Health Campaign, continue to carry out this movement, and make this work more in-depth and popular. He urged everyone to continue to mobilize the masses to participate in this movement, so that people are consciously and regularly aware of personal and public hygiene, consolidate the hygienic habits they have learned in this movement, reduce diseases, improve health levels, eliminate disease-carrying animals, and smash the US imperialists' conspiracy to continue waging germ warfare.
    The conference adjourned after a speech by Vice Mayor of Beijing Municipality, Wu Han.

### 第二届全国卫生会议在京开幕  总结今年爱国卫生运动的经验  会议选出主席团及全国卫生模范评奖委员会

1952-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    第二届全国卫生会议在京开幕
    总结今年爱国卫生运动的经验
    会议选出主席团及全国卫生模范评奖委员会
    【新华社八日讯】第二届全国卫生会议已于八日在北京隆重开幕。
    这次会议主要是总结今年爱国卫生运动经验。为了开好这次会议，各地曾进行了爱国卫生运动的检查，评选了全国卫生模范，并准备全国爱国卫生运动展览会。
    参加这次大会的有全国卫生模范单位代表及模范工作者一百五十二名，特邀全国知名的医药卫生专家代表四十七名，中国卫生工作者赴苏参观团全体团员十五名，苏联医药卫生专家十九名，中央人民政府卫生部、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总后方勤务部卫生部直属单位和各级卫生部门的代表，以及中央人民政府有关机关和全国性人民团体等出席、列席人员共七百七十五名。
    会场主席台两旁挂着毛主席给大会的题字：“动员起来，讲究卫生，减少疾病，提高健康水平，粉碎敌人的细菌战争。”朱总司令给大会的题字：“团结全国卫生工作者，依靠广大群众，贯彻预防为主的方针，为保证国防、经济、文化建设而努力。”这些题字给这次大会以及今后爱国卫生运动、人民卫生保健事业指示了明确的方向。会场气氛显得非常庄严隆重。当全国卫生模范单位代表及模范工作者佩着红花进入会场时，全场报以热烈的鼓掌。
    在中央人民政府卫生部副部长傅连暲致开幕词以后，会议选举大会主席团。中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全、副部长贺诚、苏井观、傅连暲、徐运北，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总后方勤务部卫生部副部长孙仪之、戴正华，中央卫生研究院院长沈其震，以及这次被评选为全国卫生模范工作者的青岛市人民政府副市长张公制，解放军某师政治委员任思忠，陕西省长安县县长李浩，卫生模范李中波、鲁大妈、潘月仙，模范单位代表刘轩亭、文俊奎、陈镜新、孙荣国和全国知名的医药卫生专家等共五十二人当选为主席团。
    大会并一致选出全国卫生模范评奖委员会、展览会审查委员会等名单。
    中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会马叙伦副主任在会上讲话，他指出今年的爱国卫生运动不但粉碎了美帝国主义进行细菌战的阴谋，而且在全国范围内减少了疾病的发生，增进了人民的健康。尤其是在抗美援朝的正义战争中，我们卫生工作者在前方和后方忘我地尽了最大的努力，增强了战斗力量，对保卫祖国树立了伟大的劳绩，人民对他们是永远不会忘记的。他要求大家巩固今年爱国卫生运动的成绩，继续开展这一运动，使这一工作更加深入和普及。他要求大家今后继续动员群众来参加这一运动，使人民对个人和群众的卫生做到自觉的经常化，巩固他们在这次运动中学得的卫生习惯，以减少疾病，提高健康水平，扑灭病媒动物，粉碎美帝国主义继续进行细菌战的阴谋。
    大会最后在北京市人民政府副市长吴晗讲话后休会。
