Title: UN General Assembly Decides to Adjourn Until February Next Year; Soviet Union's Proposal Condemning U.S. Massacre of POWs Unreasonably Vetoed by U.S.-British Aggression Group
Author:
Date: 1952-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th dispatch from New York: According to TASS, the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly decided at its morning meeting on the 22nd to adjourn and reconvene on February 24, 1953. The last proposal discussed at the meeting was the Soviet delegation's proposal regarding the collective massacre of Korean and Chinese prisoners of war by the U.S. military authorities on Pongam Island.

    Gromyko, Deputy Head of the Soviet Delegation, emphasized the criminal acts of the U.S. military authorities on Pongam Island, as well as the massacres of prisoners of war in the Koje Island, Jeju Island, and Busan POW camps, which, in nature, constituted a planned massacre of Korean and Chinese prisoners of war by the U.S. Command in Korea.

    U.S. Representative Gross attempted to justify the collective massacre of Korean and Chinese prisoners of war by using the pretext of preventing riots.

    The U.S.-British bloc activated its voting machine and vetoed the Soviet proposal requesting the General Assembly to condemn the crimes of the U.S. military authorities in Korea. The Soviet Union, Ukraine, Byelorussia, Poland, and Czechoslovakia voted in favor of the proposal. India, Indonesia, Iran, Egypt, and other countries abstained.

### 联大决定休会至明年二月  苏联谴责美国屠杀战俘提案竟遭美英侵略集团无理否决

1952-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    联大决定休会至明年二月
    苏联谴责美国屠杀战俘提案竟遭美英侵略集团无理否决
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯：联合国第七届大会在二十二日的上午会议上决定休会到一九五三年二月二十四日再开。会上最后讨论的一个提案是苏联代表团提出的关于美国军事当局在蜂岩岛上集体屠杀朝中战俘的问题。
    苏联代表团副团长葛罗米柯着重指出美军当局在蜂岩岛上的罪恶行为，以及对巨济岛、济州岛和釜山战俘营中战俘的屠杀，从性质上看是朝鲜的美军司令部有计划地屠杀朝中战俘的行为。
    美国代表葛罗斯妄图以防止骚乱为藉口来为集体屠杀朝中战俘辩解。
    美英集团开动了投票机器，否决了苏联所提要求大会谴责美国军事当局在朝鲜的罪行的提案。苏联、乌克兰、白俄罗斯、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克投票赞成这个提案。印度、印度尼西亚、伊朗、埃及等国弃权。
