    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of transportation development between Juyongguan and Nankou on the Beijing-Baotou Railway, the construction preparation for the new Fengtai-Shacheng section railway is actively underway. The line is approximately over 100 kilometers long, passing through more than 70 tunnels and 30 bridges, making it the most difficult railway construction project in the country at present. This is a scene of surveying personnel dispatched by the Ministry of Railways conducting surveys.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了满足京包线居庸关南口间运输发展的需要，丰（台）沙（城）段新线铁路的施工准备正在积极进行。该线全长约一百余公里，经过七十多个山洞和三十多座桥梁，是目前全国铁路修筑工程中最艰难的一段。这是铁道部派出的勘测人员正在勘测的情形。
