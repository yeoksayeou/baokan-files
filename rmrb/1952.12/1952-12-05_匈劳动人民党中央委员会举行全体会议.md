Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author: 
Date: 1952-12-05
Page: 4

    [Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 3rd] According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party held a plenary session on November 29th. Ernő Gerő, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Working People's Party of Hungary, made a report at the meeting on the development of Hungary's national economy in 1952 and the tasks for developing the national economy in 1953. After discussion, the meeting adopted Gerő's report.

### 匈劳动人民党中央委员会举行全体会议

1952-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党中央委员会举行全体会议
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会在十一月二十九日举行了全体会议。劳动人民党中央委员会政治局委员兼书记处书记额尔诺·格罗在会上作了关于匈牙利一九五二年国民经济发展情况和一九五三年发展国民经济的任务的报告。会议经过讨论后，通过了格罗的报告。
