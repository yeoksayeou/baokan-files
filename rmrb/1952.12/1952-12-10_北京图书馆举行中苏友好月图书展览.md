Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Month Book Exhibition Held at Beijing Library
Author:
Date: 1952-12-10
Page: 3
Culture Briefs

    Beijing Library held a "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month Book Exhibition" from November 12th to December 5th. The exhibition displayed over 600 carefully selected books, magazines, newspapers, pictures, cartoons, and posters. The first part of the exhibition was "Sino-Soviet Friendship," displaying Sino-Soviet friendship newspapers and periodicals published throughout China, collections of speeches by Soviet experts in China, and travel notes from Chinese delegations visiting the Soviet Union.

    The second part was "Classic Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin," displaying the Russian-language versions of "Das Kapital," "Collected Works of Lenin," "Collected Works of Stalin," and the Chinese translation of Stalin's "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR," placed alongside the Russian translation of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," which was particularly eye-catching. The third part of the exhibition was "Chinese Works Translated into Russian," displaying Russian-language versions of "Lao Tzu," "Selected Poems of Bai Juyi," and "Selected Works of Lu Xun," as well as Liu Shaoqi's "Internationalism and Nationalism," Chen Boda's "Chiang Kai-shek, Public Enemy of the People," and Li Siguang's "Geology," among others. The Russian translations of the three works by Chinese writers that won the Stalin Prize, "The Sun Shines on the Sangkan River," "The Hurricane," and "The White-Haired Girl," were also on display. The fourth part was "Introduction to Soviet Science and Advanced Experience," exhibiting many Chinese translations of Soviet books on philosophy, law, education, psychology, agricultural biology, and industrial production experience. The final part was "Soviet Literary Works and Chinese Translations," which displayed the original texts and Chinese translations of Soviet literary works together, including novels, poems, and essay collections by writers such as Tolstoy, Pushkin, Gorky, Mayakovsky, Ostrovsky, Ehrenburg, Fadeyev, Tikhonov, and Simonov.

### 北京图书馆举行中苏友好月图书展览

1952-12-10
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    北京图书馆举行中苏友好月图书展览
    北京图书馆在十一月十二日至十二月五日举办“中苏友好月图书展览”。展览会陈列着精选的书籍、杂志、报纸、图片、漫画、招贴画等六百多种。展览的第一部分是“中苏友好”，陈列着我国各地出版的中苏友好报刊，苏联专家在中国的演讲文集以及我国代表团到苏联去参观的游记等。
    第二部分是“马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林经典著作”，陈列着俄文版的“资本论”、“列宁全集”、“斯大林全集”及斯大林“苏联社会主义经济问题”的中译本，和“毛泽东选集”的俄译本并列在一起，特别引人注目。展览的第三部分是“中国作品俄译本”，陈列着俄文版的“老子”、“白居易诗选”和“鲁迅选集”等，还有刘少奇的“国际主义与民族主义”、陈伯达的“人民公敌蒋介石”以及李四光的“地质学”等。我国作家荣获斯大林奖金的三部作品“太阳照在桑干河上”、“暴风骤雨”和“白毛女”的俄文译本也陈列在那里。第四部分是“苏联科学与先进经验的介绍”，展览着很多种苏联的哲学、法律、教育、心理学、农业生物学、工业生产经验等书籍的中译本。最后一部分是“苏联文艺作品与中译本”，这里将苏联文学作品的原文本和中译本陈列在一起，有托尔斯泰、普希金、高尔基、马雅可夫斯基、奥斯特洛夫斯基、爱伦堡、法捷耶夫、吉洪诺夫、西蒙诺夫等作家的小说、诗歌、论文集。
