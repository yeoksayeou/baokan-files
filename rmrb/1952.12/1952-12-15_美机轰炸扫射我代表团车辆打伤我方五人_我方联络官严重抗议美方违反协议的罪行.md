Title: US Aircraft Bomb and Strafe Delegation Vehicles, Wounding Five of Our Personnel; Our Liaison Officer Strongly Protests the US Side's Crimes of Violating the Agreement
Author:
Date: 1952-12-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 14th—At 11:30 AM on December 13th, US military aircraft violated the agreement by bombing and strafing supply vehicles of our delegation to the Korean armistice talks, marked with red flags and traveling from Kaesong to Pyongyang, near Angok-ri, north of Sariwon, wounding five members of our delegation, one of whom was seriously injured. Our chief liaison officer lodged a strong protest with the other side, demanding that they strictly restrain their military personnel to prevent similar incidents from happening again.

### 美机轰炸扫射我代表团车辆打伤我方五人  我方联络官严重抗议美方违反协议的罪行

1952-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    美机轰炸扫射我代表团车辆打伤我方五人
    我方联络官严重抗议美方违反协议的罪行
    【新华社开城十四日电】十二月十三日上午十一时三十分，美方军用飞机违反协议，在沙里院以北安谷里附近轰炸扫射设有红旗标志目开城开往平壤的朝鲜停战谈判我方代表团供应车辆，打伤我方代表团人员五人，其中一人重伤。我方首席联络官向对方提出严重抗议，要求对方严格约束其军事人员，使类似事件不再发生。
