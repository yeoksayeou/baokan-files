Title: The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam and the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front Issue Letter to the People and Army of the Whole Country on the Sixth Anniversary of the Anti-French Resistance War
Author:
Date: 1952-12-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, December 19th: The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam and the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front jointly issued a letter to the people, army, and cadres of the whole country of Vietnam to commemorate the sixth anniversary of the nationwide resistance war. The letter reviewed the many achievements made by the Vietnamese people and army in all aspects during the past six years of resistance war, and pointed out the bright future of the Vietnamese people's resistance war. The letter also pointed out the main tasks that the Vietnamese people must carry out in the future in order to further strengthen their struggle for independence and defend peace, especially to resolutely implement the agricultural policy stipulated by President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese Government, thereby strengthening the strength of the entire people's resistance war, and enabling the Vietnamese people's resistance war to turn into a victorious all-out counter-offensive as soon as possible.

    The letter concluded by saying: Doing these things well means "effectively implementing the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, and effectively supporting the World People's Peace Congress."

### 越南国民大会常务委员会和越联战线全国委员会  纪念越南抗战六周年发表告全国军民书

1952-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会常务委员会和越联战线全国委员会
    纪念越南抗战六周年发表告全国军民书
    【新华社二十日讯】越南通讯社北越十九日讯：越南国民大会常务委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会为纪念越南全国抗战六周年，联名发表了告越南全国人民、军队和干部书。文告回顾了越南人民和军队过去六年抗战中在各方面获得的许多成就，并指出越南人民抗战的光明前途。文告还指出了越南人民今后为了进一步加强他们争取独立的斗争和保卫和平而必须执行的主要任务，特别是要坚决执行胡志明主席和越南人民政府所规定的农业政策，从而加强全体人民的抗战力量，使越南人民的抗战早日转为胜利的全面反攻。
    文告最后说：我们做好了这些工作，就是“有效地执行亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的决议，就是有效地支持世界人民和平大会”。
