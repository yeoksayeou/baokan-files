Title: Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd:

    The Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble, led by B. A. Alexandrov, returned to Beijing by special train from Wuhan on the afternoon of the 23rd, after visiting Tianjin, Jinan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and Wuhan.
    Those who welcomed them at the station included Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Yan Baohang and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General; Li Zhaobing, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xu Deheng, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and over sixty people from the capital's literary and art circles, including Cao Yu, Li Wei, Lan Ma, and Tian Hua. S.G. Skatsev, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Fomin, Chief Deputy Military Attaché, and Zhukov, Representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, were also at the station to welcome them.
    Since leaving Beijing on November 15th, the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble has held broadcast rallies in seven cities including Tianjin and Jinan, with over ten million listeners; they performed eighteen times, with an audience of 400,000. They held grand gatherings, garden parties, and forums with people from all walks of life in various locations; they also held get-togethers with the People's Liberation Army garrisons stationed in various places. The extensive and in-depth visit of the Soviet Army Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble was warmly welcomed by the people everywhere, enhancing the deep friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

### 苏军红旗歌舞团返京

1952-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏军红旗歌舞团返京
    【新华社二十三日讯】以鲍·亚·亚历山大罗夫为首的苏军红旗歌舞团，在访问了天津、济南、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉等城市后，已于二十三日下午乘专车由武汉返抵北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，副总干事阎宝航、李霑吾，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳，北京市中苏友好协会副会长许德珩，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗和首都文学艺术界人士曹禺、李炜、蓝马、田华等六十余人。苏联驻华大使馆参赞司高磋、首席副武官富敏科、苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福也到车站欢迎。
    苏军红旗歌舞团在十一月十五日离京后，先后在天津、济南等七城市举行了广播大会，听众有一千万人以上；演出十八次，观众有四十万人。他们在各地和各界人民举行了盛大的联欢会、游园会和座谈会等；他们还和各地人民解放军驻军进行了联欢。苏军红旗歌舞团的广泛和深入的访问，受到各地人民热烈的欢迎，增进了中苏两国人民间的深厚友谊。
