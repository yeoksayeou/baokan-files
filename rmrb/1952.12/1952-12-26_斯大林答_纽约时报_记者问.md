Title: Stalin Answers Questions from "New York Times" Reporter
Author:
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th: TASS Moscow, December 25th: J.V. Stalin answered the questions posed on December 21, 1952, by James Reston, a foreign affairs reporter for the "New York Times," as follows:

    Question: As the New Year approaches and a new American government is about to take office, do you still believe that the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the United States can coexist peacefully in the future?

    Answer: I still believe that a war between the United States of America and the Soviet Union should not be considered inevitable, and that our two countries will be able to coexist peacefully in the future.

    Question: In your opinion, what is the source of the current international tension?

    Answer: In all the aggressive actions that reveal the anti-Soviet "Cold War" policy.

    Question: Would you welcome diplomatic talks with representatives of the new Eisenhower government to consider the possibility of a meeting between you and General Eisenhower on the issue of easing international tension?

    Answer: I welcome such a proposal.

    Question: Will you cooperate in any new diplomatic measures aimed at ending the Korean War?

    Answer: I agree to cooperate because the Soviet Union is concerned about ending the Korean War.

### 斯大林答“纽约时报”记者问

1952-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林答“纽约时报”记者问
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十五日电：约·维·斯大林对一九五二年十二月二十一日接到的、“纽约时报”外交记者詹姆斯·赖斯顿所提出的问题，答覆如下：
    问：在新年到来、美国新政府即将就职的时候，您仍然相信苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和美国今后能够和平共处吗？
    答：我仍然相信：不能认为美利坚合众国和苏联之间的战争是不可避免的，我们两国在今后也能够和平共处。
    问：照您看来，目前的国际紧张形势的来源何在？
    答：在一切只要反苏“冷战”政策的侵略行动显示出来的地方和事情上。
    问：您欢迎和新的艾森豪威尔政府的代表举行外交会谈，以便考虑您和艾森豪威尔将军就缓和国际紧张形势的问题进行会晤的可能性吗？
    答：我欢迎这样的建议。
    问：您将在任何以结束朝鲜战争为目的的新的外交措施上进行合作吗？
    答：我同意合作，因为苏联是关心结束朝鲜战争的。
