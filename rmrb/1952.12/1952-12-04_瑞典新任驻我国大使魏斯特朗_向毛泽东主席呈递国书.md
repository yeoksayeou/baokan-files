Title: New Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand, Presents Credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1952-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd

　　New Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand
    Presents Credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd] The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Sweden to China, Mr. Wistrand, presented his credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government at 3:00 PM this afternoon. Present at the ceremony were Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs Huan Xiang, and Chief of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru. Ambassador Wistrand delivered a eulogy to Chairman Mao Zedong when presenting his credentials, and Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a reply.

    Ambassador Wistrand's Eulogy:
    Mr. Chairman:
    I am honored to have been appointed by His Majesty the King as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China. I am now honored to present to you my Letter of Credence and the Letter of Recall of my predecessor.
    First, I request that you allow me to express my fervent wish. I am willing to do my utmost to fulfill the lofty mission entrusted to me.
    I would also like to take this opportunity to assure you that, on my part, I will make my best efforts to maintain and develop the friendly relations between Sweden and China and to consolidate the long-standing friendship between our two countries.
    In the course of completing this mission, it would be invaluable to me to have your trust and the support of the Chinese government.
    Mr. Chairman, while hoping to obtain this support, I wish to express, with the most sincere enthusiasm, my best wishes for your health and for the happiness and prosperity of China and the great Chinese people.

    Chairman Mao Zedong's Reply:
    Mr. Ambassador:
    I am very pleased to accept the Letter of Credence from the King of Sweden presented by Your Excellency, and I thank you for your congratulations.
    I welcome your appointment as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Sweden to the People's Republic of China.
    I am also very pleased to accept your assurance of striving to strengthen the friendly relations between China and Sweden. I am convinced that your wishes and efforts in this regard will receive the support of the Government of the People's Republic of China.
    I wish good health to the King of Sweden and prosperity to the Swedish nation and happiness to its people.

### 瑞典新任驻我国大使魏斯特朗  向毛泽东主席呈递国书

1952-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞典新任驻我国大使魏斯特朗
    向毛泽东主席呈递国书
    【新华社三日讯】瑞典王国新任驻我国特命全权大使魏斯特朗于三日下午三时向我中央人民政府毛泽东主席呈递国书。在场者有中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、西欧非洲司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如。魏斯特朗大使在呈递国书时曾向毛泽东主席致颂词，毛泽东主席致答词。
    魏斯特朗大使的颂词主席先生：
    我奉我王陛下任命为驻中华人民共和国的特命全权大使。现在我荣幸地向阁下呈递到任国书和前任的召回国书。
    我首先请求阁下允许我向您表达我的热烈愿望。我愿尽我的棉薄，完成我所奉的崇高使命。
    我也愿顺这个机会，向阁下提出保证，在我的方面，我务必尽最大努力，以维持和发展瑞典和中国间的友好关系并巩固你我两国间一向存在的睦谊。
    我在完成这一任务期间，如能取得阁下的信任和中国政府的支持，这对于我是很宝贵的。
    主席先生，我在希望取得这一支持的同时，愿以最诚挚的热忱，谨祝阁下的健康，中国与伟大中国人民的幸福与繁荣。
    毛泽东主席的答词大使先生：
    我很高兴地接受贵大使呈递的瑞典国王的国书，并感谢您的祝贺。
    我对于您出任瑞典王国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使，表示欢迎。
    我也很高兴地接受您对于努力加强中瑞两国友好关系所提的保证。我深信您在这方面的愿望和努力必能获得中华人民共和国政府的支持。
    谨祝瑞典国王健康及瑞典国家繁荣和人民幸福。
