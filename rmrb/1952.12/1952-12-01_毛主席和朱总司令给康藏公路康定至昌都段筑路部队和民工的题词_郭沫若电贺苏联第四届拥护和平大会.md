Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's Inscriptions for the Road-Building Troops and Civilian Laborers of the Kangding-Changdu Section of the Kangzang Highway; Guo Moruo's Telegram Congratulating the Soviet Union's Fourth Congress in Defense of Peace
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1952-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: The Fourth All-Soviet Congress in Defense of Peace will be held in Moscow on December 2nd. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram of congratulations. The full text is as follows:

To the Soviet Peace Committee
The Fourth All-Soviet Congress in Defense of Peace:

Dear Comrades, we are very excited about your fourth convening of the All-Soviet Congress in Defense of Peace. On behalf of all the Chinese people, I extend warm congratulations and high respect to you and all the beloved Soviet people.

Under the direct guidance of the greatest peace standard-bearer, Stalin, the tremendous efforts made by the Soviet people to defend the cause of peace have greatly inspired the courage of the people of the world to fight for peace. The large-scale communist peaceful construction and the great achievements in various constructions carried out by the Soviet people have incomparably strengthened the strength of the peace camp. The Soviet Union is an indestructible fortress of peace. The Soviet Union is a shining lighthouse of peace. The actions and voices of the Soviet Union's peace comrades are watched and listened to by peace-loving people all over the world.

We are deeply convinced that the convening of the Fourth All-Soviet Congress in Defense of Peace will contribute to the upcoming World People's Peace Congress and will further inspire peace-loving people all over the world to strive for the great struggle to completely crush the war conspiracy and win lasting peace.

I wish the conference a complete success. Long live peace.

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
November 30, 1952

### 毛主席和朱总司令给康藏公路康定至昌都段筑路部队和民工的题词  郭沫若电贺苏联第四届拥护和平大会
郭沫若
1952-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和朱总司令给康藏公路康定至昌都段筑路部队和民工的题词
    郭沫若电贺苏联第四届拥护和平大会
    【新华社三十日讯】第四届全苏拥护和平大会将于十二月二日在莫斯科召开，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若特致电祝贺。全文如下：
    苏联拥护和平委员会转
    第四届全苏拥护和平大会：
    亲爱的同志们，你们第四次召开全苏拥护和平大会，使我们非常兴奋。我谨代表全中国人民，向你们和全体亲爱的苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺与崇高的敬意。
    苏联人民在最伟大的和平旗手——斯大林的直接指导之下，为保卫和平事业所作的巨大努力，极大地鼓舞了全世界人民为和平而斗争的勇气。苏联人民所进行着的大规模的共产主义的和平建设和各项建设中的伟大成就，更无可比拟地加强了和平阵营的力量。苏联是坚不可摧的和平堡垒。苏联是光芒四射的和平灯塔。苏联的和平战友们的行动和呼声，是全世界爱好和平的人民所注视而倾听着的。
    我们深信：第四届全苏拥护和平大会的召开，定将有助于即将举行的世界人民和平大会，并将更进一步地鼓舞全世界爱好和平的人民为彻底粉碎战争阴谋，争取持久和平的伟大斗争而努力。
    祝大会圆满成功。和平万岁。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席
  　 郭沫若
    一九五二年十一月三十日
