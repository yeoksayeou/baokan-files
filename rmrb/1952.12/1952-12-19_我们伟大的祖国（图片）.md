Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-12-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region People's Government in Guangxi Province has been established, and the more than 6.2 million people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region are united more closely. This is a dance performance by the Guangxi Military Region Cultural Troupe at the opening of the first People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in the Autonomous Region in Nanning on December 6th.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广西省桂西僮族自治区人民政府成立了，自治区内各族六百二十多万人民，更加亲密地团结起来了。这是自治区首届各族各界人民代表会议于十二月六日在南宁开幕时广西军区文工团的舞蹈表演。
