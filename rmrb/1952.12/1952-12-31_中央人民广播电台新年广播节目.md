Central People's Broadcasting Station New Year Broadcast Program

Major Programs for December 31, 1952
　　17:30 Children's Program: Poet Ai Qing recites a New Year's poem to children: "To the Children"; 18:00-18:30 Military Program: Minister of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, Xie Juezai, delivers a New Year's speech to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, revolutionary disabled soldiers, demobilized soldiers, wounded and sick soldiers, families of revolutionary martyrs, and families of revolutionary soldiers; 20:30 Writer Yang Shuo speaks on: "People Who Build the Motherland with Their Lives"; 20:45 New Year's broadcast program by famous Beijing opera performers: Part 1: Quyi (1) Crosstalk: New Almanac (performed by Sun Yukui and Hui Wanhua); (2) Henan Zhui Zi: Mutual Affection (sung by Cui Wenru and Li Yancheng); Part 2: Ping Opera (1) Female Teacher (sung by Li Yilan); (2) Little Son-in-Law (sung by Li Zaiwen); Part 3: Peking Opera (1) Lotus Lamp (sung by Xie Ruiqing); (2) Wenzhao Pass (sung by Sun Yue); (3) Hong Niang (sung by Wu Suqiu, Zhang Manjun, and Li Debin); (4) Wild Boar Forest (sung by Li Shaochun); (5) Stealing the Imperial Horse - Sitting on the Stockade (sung by Hao Shuchen); (6) The Miraculous Reunion (sung by Ye ShengLan and Du Jinfang); (7) Sangyuan寄子 [Sangyuan Sends His Son] (sung by Tan Xiaopei); (8) Farewell My Concubine (sung by Mei Lanfang). January 1, 1953, 0:00 Special program to welcome the New Year: "1953 Has Arrived!"

Major Programs for January 1, 1953
　　13:30-14:45 Minister of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, Xie Juezai, delivers a New Year's speech to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, revolutionary disabled soldiers, demobilized soldiers, wounded and sick soldiers, families of revolutionary martyrs, and families of revolutionary soldiers; Literary Program: Yu Opera: Interrogation of Hong (sung by Chang Xiangyu), etc.; 20:30 National Industrial Labor Model Liu Dezhen New Year's Speech; 20:40 Female writer Han Zi, who returned from Korea, speaks about: The situation of the Battle of Shangganling; 21:00 New Year's broadcast program by famous Beijing opera performers: Part 1: Quyi (1) Single String: Urban and Rural Joy (sung by Ma Zenghui); (2) Beijing Rhyme Drum: Yang Guoqiang Defends Flying Tiger Mountain (sung by Bai Fengming); Part 2: Ping Opera (1) Beating the Dog and Persuading Her Husband (sung by Xi Cailian); (2) New Things Done in a New Way (sung by Xing Shaoying); Part 3: Peking Opera (1) Returning to Jingzhou (sung by Liu Xiurong); (2) Imperial Orchard (sung by Wu Yuzhang); (3) Yutangchun (sung by Zhang Junqiu, etc.); (4) Jianghan Fishermen's Song (sung by Li Hezeng); (5) Mulan Joins the Army (sung by Yun Yanming); (6) Entering the Palace Twice (sung by Wang Quankui and Li Zongyi); (7) Assassinating Bajie (sung by Ma Decheng);
　　(8) Liansheng Inn (sung by Xiao Changhua and Jiang Miaoxiang); (9) Generals and Ministers Unite (sung by Tan Fuying and Qiu Shengrong).

Major Programs for January 2
　　12:00 Opera Program: Yunnan Opera: Thunder God Cave (sung by Zhao Yintao and Bi Jinyu); Jin Opera: Beating the Golden Branch (sung by Niu Guiying); 19:30 Recording broadcast program by famous Soviet artists who came to China to participate in the "China-Soviet Friendship Month" to Chinese listeners; 20:30 National Industrial Labor Model Ma Liuhai, National Agricultural Labor Model Qu Yaoli New Year's Speech; 20:45 Literary Program: Opera: Xiao Erhei Gets Married (performed and recorded by Guo LanYing and Yu Fu).

Major Programs for January 3
　　12:00 Opera Program: (1) Sichuan Opera: The Story of Willow Shade (sung by Chen Shufang); (2) Qin Opera: Su Wu Herding Sheep (sung by Su Yumin and Zhang Xinhua); 18:30 Literary Program: (1) Clapper Talk: Soldiers' Home (sung by Guo Yiguang and Teng Wu); (2) Lian Zhu Quick Talk: Centipede Ridge (sung by Cao Baolu); 20:30 (1) Anshan Iron and Steel Company Special Labor Model Zhang Mingshan New Year's Speech; (2) Painter Gu Yuan, who returned from Korea, speaks: The Glorious Position of Defending Peace; 20:45 Literary Program: Yue Opera: The Legend of the White Snake (sung by Yuan Xuefen, Fu Quanxiang, and Fan Ruijuan).

### 中央人民广播电台新年广播节目

1952-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台新年广播节目
    一九五二年十二月三十一日主要节目
    十七点三十分少年儿童节目：朗诵诗人艾青向小朋友们祝贺新年的一首诗：“给小朋友们”；十八点至十八点三十分部队节目：中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长新年向中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员，革命残废军人，转业军人，伤病员，革命烈士家属和革命军人家属讲话；二十点三十分作家杨朔讲：“用生命建设祖国的人们”；二十点四十五分北京著名戏曲演员新年广播节目：第一部分：曲艺（一）相声：新历书（孙玉奎、回婉华演播）；（二）河南坠子：两情愿（崔温如、李炎诚演唱）；第二部分：评剧（一）女教师（李忆兰演唱）；（二）小女婿（李再雯演唱）；第三部分：京剧（一）宝莲灯（谢锐青演唱）；（二）文昭关（孙岳演唱）；（三）红娘（吴素秋、张曼君、李德彬演唱）；（四）野猪林（李少春演唱）；（五）盗御马——坐寨（郝寿臣演唱）；（六）奇双会（叶盛兰、杜近芳演唱）；（七）桑园寄子（谭小培演唱）；（八）霸王别姬（梅兰芳）。一九五三年一月一日零点零分迎接新年特别节目：“一九五三年来到了！”
    一九五三年一月一日主要节目
    十三点三十分至十四点四十五分中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长新年向中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员，革命残废军人，转业军人，伤病员，革命烈士家属和革命军人家属讲话；文艺节目：豫剧：拷红（常香玉演唱）等；二十点三十分全国工业劳动模范刘德珍新年讲话；二十点四十分从朝鲜回来的女作家菡子讲：上甘岭战斗的情形；二十一点北京著名戏曲演员新年广播节目：第一部分：曲艺（一）单弦：城乡乐（马增蕙演唱）；（二）京韵大鼓：杨国强坚守飞虎山（白凤鸣演唱）；第二部分：评剧（一）打狗劝夫（喜彩莲演唱）；（二）新事新办（邢韶英演唱）；第三部分：京剧（一）回荆州（刘秀荣演唱）；（二）御果园（吴钰章演唱）；（三）玉堂春（张君秋等演唱）；（四）江汉渔歌（李和曾演唱）；（五）木兰从军（云燕铭演唱）；（六）二进宫（王泉奎、李宗义演唱）；（七）刺巴杰（马德成演唱）；
    （八）连升店（萧长华、姜妙香演唱）；（九）将相和（谭富英、裘盛戎演唱）。
    一月二日主要节目
    十二点戏曲节目：滇剧：雷神洞（赵吟涛、碧金玉演唱）；晋剧：打金枝（牛桂英演唱）；十九点三十分来我国参加“中苏友好月”的苏联著名艺术家对我国听众录音广播节目；二十点三十分全国工业劳动模范马六孩、全国农业劳动模范曲耀离新年讲话；二十点四十五分文艺节目：歌剧：小二黑结婚（郭兰英、于夫演出录音）。
    一月三日主要节目
    十二点戏曲节目：（一）川剧：柳荫记（陈书舫演唱）；（二）秦腔：苏武牧羊（苏育民、张新华演唱）；十八点三十分文艺节目：（一）快板：战士之家（郭艺光、謄武演唱）；（二）联珠快书：蜈蚣岭（曹宝禄演唱）；二十点三十分（1）鞍山钢铁公司特等劳动模范张明山新年讲话；（2）从朝鲜回来的画家古元讲话：保卫和平的光荣阵地；二十点四十五分文艺节目：越剧：白蛇传（袁雪芬、傅全香、范瑞娟演唱）。
