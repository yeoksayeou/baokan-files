Title: Romanian Newly Appointed Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Teodor Rudenco, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 23rd. Those who went to the airport to welcome him were: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Xu Yixin, Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command.
    Diplomatic envoys and personnel from various countries' embassies in China, as well as Mr. Şerban Chirilă, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian People's Republic's Embassy in China, all embassy staff, and the head and deputy head of the Romanian trade delegation currently in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 罗新任驻华大使抵京

1952-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    罗新任驻华大使抵京
    【新华社二十三日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使郭佐文于二十三日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、苏联东欧司司长徐以新、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美等。
    各国驻我国使节、外交人员和罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办席拉德、全体馆员以及现在北京的罗马尼亚商务代表团团长、副团长也到机场欢迎。
