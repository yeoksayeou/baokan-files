Title: China and Ceylon Sign Five-Year Rubber-Rice Trade Agreement; Simultaneously Sign 1953 Rice and Rubber Contracts
Author:
Date: 1952-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th:

    The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon, for the purpose of buying and selling rubber and rice, signed a five-year rubber and rice trade agreement and a communique on the first-year contracts:

    Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, representing the Chinese government, and Mr. Fang Xika, Head of the Ceylon Trade Delegation, representing the Ceylon government, signed a five-year trade agreement on rubber and rice on December 18, 1952, in accordance with the general trade agreement signed in Beijing on October 4, 1952. At the same time, two contracts for rice and rubber for 1953 were also signed. The signing of this five-year trade agreement further enhances the friendship between the governments and peoples of China and Ceylon, and establishes a lasting foundation for trade between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th

    The five-year trade agreement on rubber and rice, and the two contracts for rice and rubber for 1953, between the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon were signed in Beijing on December 18th. Representing the Chinese side was Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; representing the Ceylon side was Mr. Fang Xika, Head of the Ceylon Trade Delegation. Those attending the signing ceremony included Zeng Shan, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, He Ying, Deputy Director, Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

    After the signing ceremony, Acting Minister Lei Renmin hosted a banquet in honor of Head of Delegation Fang Xika and others.

### 中锡签订橡胶大米五年贸易协定  同时签订一九五三年大米和橡胶两个合同

1952-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中锡签订橡胶大米五年贸易协定
    同时签订一九五三年大米和橡胶两个合同
    【新华社十九日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府与锡兰政府为了买卖橡胶和大米而签订五年橡胶和大米贸易协定以及第一年合同的公报：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部代理部长雷任民代表中国政府，锡兰贸易代表团团长方席卡代表锡兰政府，根据一九五二年十月四日在北京签字的一般贸易协定，于一九五二年十二月十八日签订了关于橡胶和大米的五年贸易协定，同时并签订了一九五三年的大米和橡胶两个合同。这个五年贸易协定的签订，进一步增进了中、锡两国政府和人民之间的友谊，并为两国间的贸易来往建立了持久的基础。
    【新华社十九日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府与锡兰政府关于橡胶和大米的五年贸易协定以及一九五三年的大米和橡胶两个合同，于十二月十八日在北京签订。代表中国方面签字的是对外贸易部代理部长雷任民；代表锡兰方面签字的是锡兰贸易代表团团长方席卡。参加签字仪式的有中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任曾山、外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康、副司长何英、中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等。
    签字后，雷任民代理部长设宴招待方席卡团长等。
