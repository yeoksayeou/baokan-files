Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-12-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    People's lives are better this year than last year. The largest flour mill in Beijing—the Eastern Suburbs Flour Mill of the Beijing Flour Company—has increased its flour production by 85 percent this year compared to last year, due to the improvement in people's living standards. The flour produced by the mill is being loaded here, ready to be sent to cooperative stores in various districts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年人民的生活比去年好。北京市规模最大的面粉厂——北京市面粉公司东郊面粉厂，由于人民生活水平的提高，今年生产的面粉比去年增加了百分之八十五。该厂出品的面粉正在这里装运，准备送到各区合作社去。
