Title: Soong Ching-ling and Others Receive a Warm Welcome in Budapest En Route Home; Hungarian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Rákosi and Others Greet Them Cordially at the Station
Author:
Date: 1952-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 24th: Soong Ching-ling, head of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Congress, and her party, on their way home from Vienna, arrived in Budapest at 7:00 PM on the 22nd. Also on the train were Soviet representatives Korneichuk, Ehrenburg, and Simonov.

Those who welcomed them at the station included: Hungarian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Rákosi, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nagy, Minister of Foreign Affairs Molnár, Minister of Education Darvas, President of the Academy of Sciences Rusznyák, as well as representatives from the Hungarian National Peace Council, the Hungarian Democratic Women's League, and cultural circles. Also present at the station was Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Kiselev. Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the station.

After alighting from the train and resting briefly, Soong Ching-ling took a car to tour the central districts of Budapest and climbed Buda Hill, visiting the Soviet Army Memorial Tower. After Soong Ching-ling returned to the station, Rákosi personally received her again and had a long conversation with her. All those who welcomed her watched this moving scene, which fully demonstrated the deep friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian people, with excitement. As Soong Ching-ling and her party boarded the train to depart, Rákosi bid them farewell earnestly at the front of the train, presented them with famous Hungarian products as souvenirs, and repeatedly asked Soong Ching-ling to convey his regards to Chairman Mao.

### 宋庆龄等返国途经布达佩斯受到热烈欢迎  匈部长会议主席拉科西等到车站亲切招待

1952-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄等返国途经布达佩斯受到热烈欢迎
    匈部长会议主席拉科西等到车站亲切招待
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】出席世界人民和平大会中国代表团团长宋庆龄等一行在由维也纳返国途中，于二十二日晚七时抵布达佩斯，同车的有苏联代表考湼楚克、爱伦堡和西蒙诺夫等人。到车站欢迎的有：匈牙利部长会议主席拉科西，部长会议副主席纳吉，外交部部长莫尔那尔，教育部部长达尔瓦斯，科学院院长鲁斯尼亚克，以及匈牙利全国和平理事会、匈牙利民主妇女联盟和文化界代表。前往车站欢迎的尚有苏联驻匈牙利大使基塞列夫。我国驻匈牙利大使黄镇和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。宋庆龄下车稍事休息后，即乘车游览布达佩斯中心市区、并登布达山，参观了苏军纪念塔，宋庆龄返回车站后，拉科西又亲往招待，并畅谈甚久。全体欢迎者都兴奋地注视着这一充分表现中匈人民深厚友谊的动人场面。宋庆龄等登车启程时，拉科西在车前殷殷道别，赠送匈牙利名产作纪念，并一再请宋庆龄代为向毛主席致意。
