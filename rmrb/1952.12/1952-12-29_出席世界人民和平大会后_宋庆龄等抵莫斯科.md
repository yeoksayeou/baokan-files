Title: Song Qingling and Others Arrive in Moscow After Attending the World People's Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-12-29
Page: 1

    After Attending the World People's Peace Congress
    Song Qingling and Others Arrive in Moscow
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 28th] The Chinese delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress, led by head of delegation Song Qingling and deputy head of delegation Guo Moruo, arrived in Moscow in batches on the 25th and 26th. The Chinese delegation, at the invitation of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, will stay in Moscow for a few days for sightseeing.
    Also arriving in Moscow at the same time as the Chinese delegation were representatives from Burma, India, Japan, North Korea, Malaya, Mongolia, the Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, and Australia and the Americas who participated in the World People's Peace Congress.
    [Xinhua News Agency] TASS Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress returned to Moscow from Vienna on the 26th. The delegation included Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, and others. Representatives from all walks of life in Moscow, including scientific and cultural workers, as well as Stakhanovite workers, greeted the delegation at the station.
    The delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Indian delegation, who will be returning home via the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow on the same train as the Soviet delegation.

### 出席世界人民和平大会后  宋庆龄等抵莫斯科

1952-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界人民和平大会后
    宋庆龄等抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十八日电】出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表团，在团长宋庆龄和副团长郭沫若率领下，已在二十五日和二十六日分批到达莫斯科。中国代表团应苏联对外文化协会的邀请，将在莫斯科停留几天，作参观游览。
    和中国代表团同时到达莫斯科的，还有参加世界人民和平大会的缅甸、印度、日本、朝鲜、马来亚、蒙古、菲律宾、泰国、越南和澳洲、美洲各国代表。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席世界人民和平大会的苏联代表团于二十六日由维也纳返抵莫斯科。代表团包括苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼·吉洪诺夫等人。到车站迎接代表团的有莫斯科各界代表：科学和文化工作者以及斯塔哈诺夫式工作者。
    和苏联代表团同车抵达莫斯科的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团和印度代表团，它们将取道苏联返国。
