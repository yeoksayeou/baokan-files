Title: Churaki and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The leader of the Soviet Art Troupe, Churaki, and his party returned to Beijing by special plane from Taiyuan at noon on the 17th. Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and dozens of people from cultural and artistic circles in the capital went to the airport to welcome them. Zhukoff, a representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, was also present to welcome them.

After leaving Beijing for the second time on the 6th of this month, Churaki and his party successively visited Changsha, Guangzhou, Jinan, Taiyuan and other cities. Soviet artists performed more than ten times in various places and were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life.

### 楚拉基等返抵北京

1952-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    楚拉基等返抵北京
    据新华社讯：苏联艺术工作团领导人楚拉基等一行在十七日中午乘专机由太原返抵北京。前往机场欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会副总干事李霑吾和首都文化艺术界人士数十人。苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福也到场欢迎。
    楚拉基等一行在本月六日第二次离开北京后，相继访问了长沙、广州、济南、太原等城市。苏联艺术家们在各地共演出十次以上，受到各界人民的热烈欢迎。
