Title: Commemorating the Sixth Anniversary of the Nationwide Resistance War in Vietnam - Slogans Released by the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Vietnam National United Front

Author:
Date: 1952-12-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, December 17th: The Vietnam News Agency reports that the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Vietnam National United Front, to commemorate the sixth anniversary of the Nationwide Resistance War in Vietnam (December 19th), have specifically released the following ten slogans:
    I.  People of Vietnam, unite and fight!
    II. Long live the heroic main forces and local forces, militia, guerrillas of the Vietnam People's Army, and the heroic frontline workers!
    III.Celebrate the glorious victories of Nghia Lo and Son La!
    IV. Actively launch kill-the-enemy and increase-production campaigns! Enthusiastically support the front lines and fulfill agricultural tax obligations!
    V.  Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos, unite and fight!
    VI. Celebrate the convocation of the World People's Peace Congress!
    VII. Our long-term resistance war will surely be victorious! The independence and unification of our country will certainly be realized!
    VIII. Eliminate the French colonialists, American interventionists, and traitors!
    IX. Long live the Vietnam Workers' Party!
    X.  Long live President Ho Chi Minh!

### 纪念越南全国抗战六周年  越南劳动党和越南国民联合战线发布口号

1952-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念越南全国抗战六周年
    越南劳动党和越南国民联合战线发布口号
    【新华社十八日讯】越南通讯社北越十七日讯：越南劳动党和越南国民联合战线为纪念越南全国抗战六周年（十二月十九日），特发布十项口号如下：
    一、越南全国人民，团结起来战斗！
    二、英勇战斗的越南人民军主力部队和地方部队、民兵、游击队和英勇服务的战地工作者万岁！
    三、庆祝义路和山萝的光荣胜利！
    四、积极展开杀敌竞赛和增产竞赛！踊跃支援前线，完纳农业税！
    五、越南、高棉和寮国联合起来进行战斗！
    六、庆祝世界人民和平大会的召开！
    七、我们的长期抗战必胜！我们国家的独立和统一必定实现！
    八、消灭法国殖民者、美国干涉者和卖国贼！
    九、越南劳动党万岁！
    十、胡志明主席万岁！
