Title: Notice from the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council
Author: Chen Yun
Date: 1952-12-31
Page: 1

    Notice from the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council
    In order to ensure the normal progress of railway loading work during and around the New Year's Day holiday in 1953, the following provisions are hereby made:
    (1) Railways and all consigning departments should mobilize employees before and after New Year's Day to ensure that loading is carried out as usual according to the original daily average loading number of the consignment plan.
    (2) The wages of employees who work on New Year's Day for loading and unloading shall be paid in accordance with the method stipulated in this Commission's Notice No. (52) Finance and Economics Plan and Transportation No. 655 of April 28, 1952.

    Director Chen Yun
    December 30, 1952

### 政务院财政经济委员会通知
陈云
1952-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院财政经济委员会通知
    为保证在一九五三年元旦日假期前后铁路装车工作的正常进行，特规定：
    （一）铁路与各托运部门在元旦日前后应发动职工，保证按原提托运计划之每日平均装车数照常进行装车。
    （二）对元旦日出勤装卸之职工工资，照本委一九五二年四月二十八日（52）财经计交字第六五五号通知所规定的办法支付装卸工资。
    主任　陈云
    一九五二年十二月三十日
