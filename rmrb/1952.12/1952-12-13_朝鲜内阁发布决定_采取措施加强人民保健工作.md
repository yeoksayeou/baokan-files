Title: Korean Cabinet Issues Decision: Measures to Strengthen People's Health Work
Author:
Date: 1952-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decision on strengthening people's health work.

The Cabinet decided: while implementing the free treatment system, various health institutions in the same production unit should be reorganized into industrial comprehensive health centers; strengthen the leadership of rural doctors' district treatment work; and improve the quality of patient care and meals. The Cabinet also decided that health and epidemic prevention work must be strengthened, sanitary inspections must be thoroughly implemented, and the sanitary equipment of important factories must be inspected again, with the state providing assistance in improving sanitary equipment.

### 朝鲜内阁发布决定  采取措施加强人民保健工作

1952-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁发布决定
    采取措施加强人民保健工作
    据新华社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁发布了关于加强人民保健工作的决定。
    内阁决定：与实施免费治疗制度同时，要把同一生产机关里的各种保健机构改组为产业综合保健所；加强对农村医生分区治疗工作的领导；并提高对患者的看护工作与伙食的质量。内阁并决定：必须加强卫生防疫工作，彻底实行卫生检查，同时要再一次检查重要工厂的卫生设备，由国家在改善卫生设备方面给予协助。
