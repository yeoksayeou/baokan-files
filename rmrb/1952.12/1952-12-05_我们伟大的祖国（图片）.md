Our Great Motherland
    In order to supply the needs of basic construction in various regions, the Shenyang No. 1 Ceramics Factory is rushing to produce a large number of ceramic sanitary equipment every day. The workers here are inspecting the finished products, preparing them for packaging and shipment to various regions.
