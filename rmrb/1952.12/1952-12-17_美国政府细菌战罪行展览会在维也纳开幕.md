Title: US Government Germ Warfare Crimes Exhibition Opens in Vienna
Author:
Date: 1952-12-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 16th. The exhibition on the germ warfare crimes of the US government, jointly sponsored by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Austrian Peace Council, opened here today. Among the visitors were Professor Bressoya (Brazil), Professor Zhukov-Veresnikov (USSR), members of the International Scientific Commission for Investigating the Facts of Germ Warfare in Korea and China, Professor Brandweiner, head of the investigation team of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, as well as representatives and journalists from many countries.

On display were documents, pictures, containers used for germ warfare, and recorded confessions of captured American pilots. In addition, there were reports and photos from the International Scientific Commission for Investigating the Facts of Germ Warfare in Korea and China. The documents and other items on display convincingly proved that the US government had engaged in germ warfare in Korea and Northeast China. All exhibits had explanations in five languages: German, English, French, Russian, and Spanish.

The exhibition was held in the Ehrbar Hall, a concert hall in Vienna. Today, advertisements for the opening of the exhibition were posted on the streets, with black backgrounds and golden Chinese and German characters. The exhibition will be held until December 19th for representatives and citizens of Vienna to visit.

### 美国政府细菌战罪行展览会在维也纳开幕

1952-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    美国政府细菌战罪行展览会在维也纳开幕
    【新华社维也纳十六日电】由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和奥地利和平理事会主办的美国政府细菌战罪行展览会，今日在此间开幕。参观者中有调查在朝鲜和中国的细菌战事实国际科学委员会委员贝索亚教授（巴西）、茹科夫—维勒斯尼科夫（苏联）和国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团长布兰德魏纳教授，以及许多国家的代表和新闻记者。
    展览的有文件、图片、进行细菌战所用的容器和被俘的美国飞行员的供词录音。此外还有调查在朝鲜和中国的细菌战事实国际科学委员会的报告和照片。展览着的文件及其他物品，令人信服地证明了美国政府曾在朝鲜和中国东北进行过细菌战。各项展览品都有德文、英文、法文、俄文和西班牙文等五种文字的说明。
    展览会是在欧厄尔柏厅举行的，这是维也纳的一个音乐厅。今天街头巷尾都贴着展览会开幕的广告，这些广告的底子是黑色的，上面印着中文和德文的金黄色大字。展览会将举行到十二月十九日，以供代表们和维也纳市民参观。
