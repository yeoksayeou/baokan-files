Title: Ambassador Kwon O-jik, on behalf of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, awarded the Medal of Merit to model workers of the Chinese Medical and Preventive Service Team to Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 20th: Ambassador Kwon O-jik of Korea, on behalf of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, awarded the Medal of Merit to thirty model workers from the First and Seventh Detachments of the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Preventive Service Team who had served the Korean people and army in Korea. Chai Yuanqing and five other model workers from the First and Seventh Detachments of the International Medical and Preventive Service Team, who are currently in Beijing, attended the award ceremony. Peng Zemin, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China, and He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also attended the award ceremony. After Kim Dong-hyun, Secretary of the Korean Embassy, read out the decree of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea on awarding the Medal of Merit, Ambassador Kwon awarded the Medal of Merit to the model workers and delivered a speech. He extended warm congratulations to the model workers who received the awards. He said: The Korean people must further consolidate the friendship between the Korean and Chinese people and fight to the end to defeat the American aggressors. Then, Chai Yuanqing, the representative of the model workers who received the awards, delivered a reply speech. On behalf of the thirty award recipients, he expressed his gratitude to the Korean people and stated that they would continue to stand on the frontlines of the Red Cross cause and serve the people. Finally, Vice President Peng Zemin spoke, saying: We must continue to work for the cause of peace with an internationalist spirit.
    The award ceremony concluded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 权五稷大使代表朝鲜最高人民会议常委会  授予我赴朝医防服务队模范工作者功劳章

1952-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    权五稷大使代表朝鲜最高人民会议常委会
    授予我赴朝医防服务队模范工作者功劳章
    【新华社二十日讯】朝鲜驻华大使权五稷在十九日代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，以功劳章授予曾去朝鲜为朝鲜军民服务的中国红十字会国际医防服务队第一和第七两大队的模范工作者三十人。现在北京的国际医防服务队第一和第七两大队的模范工作者柴元庆等六人出席受奖。参加授奖仪式的有中国红十字会总会副会长彭泽民及外交部亚洲司副司长何英等。朝鲜大使馆秘书金东现宣读朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会关于授予功劳章的政令后，权大使将功劳章授予模范工作者并致词，他向受奖的模范工作者致热烈的祝贺。他说：朝鲜人民一定要更加巩固朝中人民的友谊，为战胜美国侵略军而奋斗到底。继由受奖的模范工作者代表柴元庆致答词，他代表三十名受奖者向朝鲜人民致谢，并表示将继续站在红十字事业的岗位上，为人民服务。最后彭泽民副会长讲话，他说：要以国际主义精神，继续为和平事业而努力。
    授奖仪式在热烈友好的气氛中结束。
