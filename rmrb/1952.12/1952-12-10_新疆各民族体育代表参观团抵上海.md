Title: Xinjiang Ethnic Minorities Sports Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-12-10
Page: 3
Cultural Briefs:

    Xinjiang Ethnic Minorities Sports Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
    The Xinjiang Ethnic Minorities Sports Delegation, a group of sixty-seven people led by Zhao Lingyun, the head of the delegation, and Rouzi Usman, the deputy head of the delegation, arrived in Shanghai on the evening of the 6th. Shen Tilan, the vice chairman of the East China Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, along with over a hundred representatives from the East China Administrative Committee, the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and various people's organizations, welcomed them at the station.

### 新疆各民族体育代表参观团抵上海

1952-12-10
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    新疆各民族体育代表参观团抵上海
    新疆各民族体育代表参观团一行六十七人，在团长赵凌云、副团长肉孜·乌斯满率领下，于六日晚抵达上海。中华全国体育总会华东总分会副主席沈体兰和华东行政委员会、上海市人民政府及各人民团体代表共百余人到车站欢迎。
