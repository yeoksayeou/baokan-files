    Our Great Motherland
    The Kangding to Qamdo section of the Kangzang Highway is now open to traffic. The soldiers who participated in the construction of this highway made tremendous achievements. This is a scene of soldiers singing and dancing on the Kangzang Plateau after the completion.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    康藏公路康定至昌都段已经通车。参加这个公路修筑工程的部队战士们做出了极大的成绩。这是战士们完工后在康藏高原上尽情歌舞的情形。
