Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Grand Banquet to Bid Farewell to Tikhonov and Others; Vice Mayor Wu Han Presides Over the Banquet and Delivers a Speech on Behalf of Mayor Peng Zhen
Author:
Date: 1952-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: The Beijing Municipal People's Government, led by Mayor Peng Zhen, hosted a grand banquet on the evening of December 3rd to bid farewell to Tikhonov, the head of the departing Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, and delegation members Beloshapko, Grusenko, Zakharov, Yefimov, and members of the Soviet Art Troupe Mikhailov, Gaidai, Nechayev, Ulanova, Kondratov, Kogan, and Metnik, totaling twelve people.

Vice Mayor Wu Han of the Beijing Municipal People's Government presided over the banquet on behalf of Mayor Peng Zhen.

Among those attending the banquet were Wu Yuzhang and Li Jishen, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, secretary-general; Xiao Hua, deputy director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Xiao Ming, Sun Xiaocun, and Cao Jinghua, vice presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Duansheng, vice chairman of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; the secretary-general and deputy secretaries-general of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, heads of various bureaus and offices, and heads of people's organizations; as well as figures from the capital's cultural circles such as Zhou Yang, Li Siguang, Fan Wenlan, Jian Bozan, Ai Qing, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Li Zhaobing, He Qifang, Chen Huangmei, Zhao Zhaobi, and others.

Also invited to the banquet were Gudev, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Counselors Skochov and Lihaiqiu, Zhukov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Navich, deputy representative of the Soviet Trade Representative in China.

Vice Mayor Wu Han delivered a speech. He first expressed, on behalf of the government and people of the capital, deep gratitude and high respect to the dearest friends from afar for the series of activities they had carried out in China over the past month to consolidate and develop Sino-Soviet friendship. He said: "During the 'Sino-Soviet Friendship Month' activities, every word and every action of the comrades of the Soviet delegations has left an unforgettable impression on the Chinese people and has become an important driving force for us in our various tasks and construction endeavors. Undoubtedly, this is of great significance for consolidating and developing Sino-Soviet friendship." He said: "The 'Sino-Soviet Friendship Month', as a large-scale mass movement, is about to come to a victorious conclusion. However, the consolidation and development of the profound friendship between China and the Soviet Union will never end. Please convey to the Soviet people the Chinese people's respect for the Soviet people and the Chinese people's love for Comrade Stalin."

Tikhonov, head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, delivered a reply. (Full text to be published separately)

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of unity and friendship. Guests and hosts repeatedly raised their glasses in toasts to the strong and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, and to the health of Stalin, the great leader and teacher of progressive mankind worldwide and the closest friend of the Chinese people. During the banquet, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, proposed that everyone raise their glasses to celebrate the 56th birthday of Tikhonov, head of the Soviet Cultural Workers Delegation, a famous Soviet poet and great peace fighter.

After the banquet, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association presented gifts to the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, including Chinese musical instruments, sheet music, works of art, ivory carvings, large porcelain vases, silk tapestry panels, and replicas of Tang Dynasty pottery figurines.

### 彭真市长盛宴欢送吉洪诺夫等  吴晗副市长代表彭真市长主持宴会并致词

1952-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭真市长盛宴欢送吉洪诺夫等
    吴晗副市长代表彭真市长主持宴会并致词
    【新华社三日讯】北京市人民政府彭真市长三日晚举行盛大宴会，欢送行将离京的苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫，团员别洛沙普柯、格鲁森科、查哈罗夫、叶菲莫夫，苏联艺术工作团团员米哈伊洛夫、盖达伊、聂恰耶夫、乌兰诺娃、康德拉托夫、柯岗、梅特尼克等十二人。
    北京市人民政府吴晗副市长代表彭真市长主持宴会。
    出席宴会的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、李济深，总干事钱俊瑞，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，北京市中苏友好协会副会长张奚若、许德珩、萧明、孙晓村、曹靖华，北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席钱端升，北京市人民政府秘书长、副秘书长、各局处长和人民团体负责人，以及首都文化界人士周扬、李四光、范文澜、翦伯赞、艾青、田汉、欧阳予倩、李兆炳、何其芳、陈荒煤、赵?h等。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫，参赞司高磋、李海秋，苏联对外文化协会代表茹克福，苏联驻华商务副代表那维琪等。
    吴晗副市长致词，他首先代表首都的政府和人民，对来自远方的最亲爱的朋友们一个月来在我国为巩固与发展中苏友谊而进行的一系列的活动，表示深深的感谢和崇高的敬意。他说：“在‘中苏友好月’的活动中，苏联各个代表团的同志们的每一句话，每一个行动，都在我国人民中间留下了难忘的印象，并已成为鼓舞我们在各项工作上和各方面建设事业中的重要动力。无疑地这对于巩固与发展中苏友好，是有着重大的意义的。”他说：“‘中苏友好月’作为一个群众性的大规模的运动，即将胜利地结束了。但中苏两大国的深厚友谊的巩固与发展则是永远没有止境的。请你们把中国人民对于苏联人民的敬意，把中国人民对于斯大林同志的热爱，传达给苏联人民吧。”
    苏联文化工作者代表团团长吉洪诺夫致答词。（全文另发）
    宴会在团结友好的气氛中进行，宾主频频举杯，为中苏两国人民强大的牢不可破的友谊干杯，为中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席健康干杯，为全世界进步人类的伟大领袖和导师、中国人民最亲密的朋友斯大林健康干杯。席间，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞提议，全场举杯，为庆贺苏联文化工作者代表团团长苏联名诗人、伟大的和平战士吉洪诺夫五十六岁寿辰干杯。
    中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会，在宴会后向苏联对外文化协会赠送礼品，包括中国乐器、乐谱、美术作品、象牙雕刻、大磁瓶、缂丝屏条以及复制唐代陶俑等。
