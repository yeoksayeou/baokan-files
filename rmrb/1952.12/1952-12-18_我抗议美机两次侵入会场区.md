Title: I Protest the Two Intrusions of American Aircraft into the Conference Area

Author:

Date: 1952-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, December 17th: At approximately 9:15 AM and 9:40 AM on December 16th, American military aircraft violated the agreement on the Kaesong conference area by twice intruding into the airspace above the Panmunjom conference area. Our chief liaison officer lodged a protest with the other side on the 17th, demanding that the other side strictly restrain its military personnel so that similar incidents do not occur again.

### 我抗议美机两次侵入会场区

1952-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我抗议美机两次侵入会场区
    【新华社开城十七日电】十二月十六日九时十五分左右与九时四十分左右，美方军用飞机违反朝鲜停战谈判会场区协议，两次侵入板门店会场区上空。我方首席联络官在十七日向对方提出抗议，要求对方严格约束其军事人员，使类似事件不再发生。
