Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 162nd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-12-13
Page: 1

　　(Xinhua News Agency, December 12th) The 162nd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 12th. At the meeting, Liu Jingfan, the head of the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, reported on the work of the delegation in representing the Chinese people in慰问(offering solace to) the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the Korean People's Army, and the Korean people in Korea. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第一六二次会议

1952-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六二次会议
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十二次政务会议在十二日下午举行。会上，中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团刘景范总团长报告了该团代表中国人民慰问在朝鲜英勇奋斗的中国人民志愿军、朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民的工作。会议并通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
