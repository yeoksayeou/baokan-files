Title: "Exhibition of Poland Building a Socialist Society" Opens

Author: 
Date: 1952-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: An exhibition entitled "Poland Building a Socialist Society," co-sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government and the Embassy of the People's Republic of Poland in China, opened on December 27th at the Wenhua Hall in the Forbidden City, Beijing. Among those attending the opening ceremony were: Ma Xulun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and more than 20,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing. Also present at the opening ceremony were Mr. Kirilok, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China.

The opening ceremony began with a speech by Deputy Director Ma Xulun, who said: This exhibition vividly depicts Poland's present and future. It not only introduces the wealthy and happy life that the Polish people have already obtained under the leadership of Chairman Beirut, but also reflects the various aspects of their construction as they move towards socialism. This exhibition will be a very good new contribution to cultural exchanges between China and Poland.

Ambassador Kirilok then spoke, saying: The fraternal friendship and cooperation between the People's Republic of Poland and the great People's Republic of China are developing, strengthening, and consolidating day by day. This exhibition is another proof of the deepening friendship between our two countries.

After the speeches, Deputy Director Ma Xulun cut the ribbon for the exhibition, and those attending the opening ceremony then visited the exhibition.

The exhibition uses large-scale photographs and various figures to introduce Poland's victorious march towards socialism, and explains the achievements of Poland's national economic Three-Year Plan and the Six-Year Plan since its implementation. The exhibition also displays Polish national costumes, exquisite handicrafts, and many books.

### “正在建设社会主义社会的波兰”展览会揭幕

1952-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    “正在建设社会主义社会的波兰”展览会揭幕
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局协助波兰人民共和国驻我国大使馆举办的“正在建设社会主义社会的波兰”展览会，二十七日在北京故宫文华殿举行揭幕式。参加揭幕式的有：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副主任马叙伦、中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林、外交部副部长伍修权和中央人民政府各部门首长以及北京各界人士二万多人。出席揭幕式的还有波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克以及各国驻我国外交使节和外交官员。
    揭幕式首由马叙伦副主任致词，他说：这个展览会生动地描绘着波兰的今天和明天，它不仅介绍波兰人民在贝鲁特主席领导下业已获得的富裕幸福的生活，而且反映了他们向社会主义迈进的各方面建设情况。这个展览会对于中波两国文化交流，将是一个很好的新的贡献。
    基里洛克大使接着讲话，他说：波兰人民共和国与伟大的中华人民共和国之间的兄弟般的友谊和合作，正在一天天地发展着、加强着、巩固着。这个展览会是我们两国间加深着的友谊的又一次证明。
    讲话完毕后，展览会由马叙伦副主任剪彩，参加揭幕式的人随即参观了展览会。
    展览会用大型图片和各种数字介绍波兰胜利地向社会主义迈进的情形，并说明波兰国民经济三年计划以及六年计划执行以来的成就。展览会上还陈列了波兰民族服装，精致的工艺品和许多书籍。新华社更正二十日发“马特洛索夫式的英雄黄继光”稿（载本报二十一日第一版）二段“黄继光生在四川省通江县……”句中“通江县”应为“中江县”。二十六日发“政务院关于发动群众继续开展防旱、抗旱运动并大力推行水土保持工作的指示”稿（载本报二十七日第一版）（二）的第二段“进行养冰蓄冰”句中“蓄冰”应为“蓄水”。
