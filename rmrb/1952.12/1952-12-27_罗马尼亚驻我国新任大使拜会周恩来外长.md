Title: New Romanian Ambassador to China Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1952-12-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: Guo Zuowen, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China, together with Xilaide, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Romanian People's Republic in China, paid a visit to Minister Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government at 8:00 PM on the 26th to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 罗马尼亚驻我国新任大使拜会周恩来外长

1952-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚驻我国新任大使拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社二十六日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使郭佐文同罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办席拉德，在二十六日下午八时赴中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有：外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新和交际处处长王倬如。
