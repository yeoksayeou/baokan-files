Title: Soviet Photo Exhibition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1952-12-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: The Soviet Photo Exhibition held in Beijing by the All-China Soviet Friendship Association to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution and "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" concluded on November 20th, having begun on November 9th. During the exhibition, there were over 63,000 visitors. In addition to soldiers, workers, students, and other citizens, many farmers from the Beijing suburbs traveled dozens of miles specifically to visit.

### 苏联图片展览结束

1952-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联图片展览结束
    【新华社七日讯】中苏友好协会总会为庆祝十月社会主义革命三十五周年和“中苏友好月”而在北京举办的苏联图片展览，自十一月九日开始到二十日结束。展览期中，观众共有六万三千多人。除了战士、工人、学生和其他市民外，北京郊区许多农民还从几十里地外专程赶来参观。
