Title:慰问团 in Tianjin Holds Memorial Meeting for Martyr Gao Qingsheng
Author:
Date: 1952-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 1st—The Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea held a memorial meeting for Martyr Gao Qingsheng in Tianjin City on the morning of the 1st. In addition to representatives from the general delegation and various sub-delegations of the慰问团, representatives from all walks of life in Tianjin and family members of the martyr attended the memorial meeting.

　　The meeting began in solemn music. Liu Jingfan, head of the慰问团, presided over the ceremony. Chen Yi, Hu Juewen, Li Minghao, and Zhou Qinyue, deputy heads of the团, assisted in the ceremony and presented wreaths. The Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Tianjin Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, the August First Film Studio, and the martyr's family also presented wreaths.

　　Martyr Gao Qingsheng was from Linyu County, Hebei Province, 25 years old, a member of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and a photography assistant at the August First Film Studio. During this trip to Korea for慰问，he gloriously sacrificed his life on October 17th while rescuing a camera during an enemy air raid.

　　Deputy Head Chen Yi expressed deep condolences for the sacrifice of Martyr Gao Qingsheng in his eulogy. He called on all comrades to emulate Martyr Gao Qingsheng's spirit of cherishing state property and work hard in their respective posts to comfort the martyr and the martyr's family.

　　Yu Zhenying, representative of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, Fang Ji, representative of all circles in Tianjin, Liu Yan, representative of the Tianjin Municipal Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Gao Guobin, younger brother of Martyr Gao Qingsheng, spoke successively. They all expressed their condolences for the martyr's sacrifice and resolved to continue strengthening the work of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea, supporting the Chinese and Korean people's forces, thoroughly defeating US imperialism, and avenging Martyr Gao Qingsheng.

### 赴朝慰问团在天津开追悼高庆生烈士大会

1952-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    赴朝慰问团在天津开追悼高庆生烈士大会
    【新华社天津一日电】中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团追悼高庆生烈士大会，于一日上午在天津市举行。参加追悼会的除慰问团总团及各分团代表外，还有天津市各界代表及烈士家属等。
    大会在哀乐声中开始。慰问团刘景范总团长主祭，陈沂、胡厥文、李明灏、周钦岳副总团长陪祭并献花圈；相继献花圈的还有中国人民抗美援朝总会、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部、天津市抗美援朝分会、解放军电影制片厂及烈士家属等。
    高庆生烈士河北省临榆县人，二十五岁，中国新民主主义青年团团员，解放军电影制片厂摄影助理。此次赴朝慰问，在十月十七日因抢救摄影机，不幸遭敌机空袭光荣牺牲。
    陈沂副总团长在致悼词时，对高庆生烈士的牺牲，表示沉痛的悼念。他号召全体同志，要以高庆生烈士爱护国家财产的精神，在各种岗位上努力工作，以安慰烈士和烈士家属。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会代表于振瀛、天津市各界代表方纪、中国新民主主义青年团天津市工作委员会代表刘焱、高庆生烈士的弟弟高国彬都相继讲话，他们一致对烈士的牺牲表示哀悼，并决心继续加强抗美援朝工作，支持中朝人民部队，彻底打垮美帝国主义，为高庆生烈士复仇。
