Title: One ROK Air Force Pilot Defects
Author:
Date: 1952-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, 2nd Lieutenant Guk Young-am, a pilot in the Aviation Section of the Operations Education Department of the ROK Army Headquarters' Gwangju Dispatch Team, defected on December 3rd. He flew a plane from South Korea to North Korea, defecting to the people. The plane flown by Guk Young-am was a U.S.-made L-19 reconnaissance aircraft, with the aircraft number 14794. After landing at a base in northern Korea, Guk Young-am was warmly welcomed by the local military and civilians.

### 李伪军空军驾驶员一人起义

1952-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    李伪军空军驾驶员一人起义
    【新华社平壤八日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：李承晚伪军陆军本部作战教育部航空科光州派遣队少尉驾驶员鞠永岩在十二月三日起义，从南朝鲜驾驶飞机一架飞到北朝鲜，投向人民。鞠永岩驾驶的飞机是美制L十九型侦察机，飞机番号是一四七九四号。鞠永岩在朝鲜北部某基地降落后，受到当地军民的热烈欢迎。
