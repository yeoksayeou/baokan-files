Title: Linze Local Armed Forces Protect Wheat Planting, Defeat Eight Hundred Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Invading
Author:
Date: 1947-10-27
Page: 1

    Linze Local Armed Forces Protect Wheat Planting
    Defeat Eight Hundred Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Invading
    [This newspaper's dispatch from Ji-Lu-Yu, 25th] The Linze local armed forces protected wheat planting and defeated eight hundred enemies. On the morning of the 12th, more than eight hundred enemies from Linze, Juye, and Dingtao launched an attack on our Huangzhenji, Caihua, Liulou, Kongji, and other villages in multiple routes, destroying the masses' efforts to rush to plant wheat. Upon hearing the news, the Linze County and District armed forces immediately went to intercept the enemy. After several hours of fierce fighting, they killed more than thirty enemy soldiers, including the company commander, and captured three enemy soldiers, including the lieutenant platoon deputy. They also recovered more than forty carts of grain that had been looted. We suffered no casualties.

### 临泽地武保卫种麦  打垮八百蒋匪进扰

1947-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    临泽地武保卫种麦
    打垮八百蒋匪进扰
    【本报冀鲁豫二十五日电】临泽地方武装保卫种麦，打垮八百敌人。十二日晨，临泽、巨野、定陶敌人八百余，分数路向我黄镇集、采花、刘楼、孔集等村进犯，破坏该地群众突击种麦。我临泽县区武装闻讯，立即前往截敌，经数小时激战，毙敌中队长以下三十余人，并俘敌中尉区队副以下三名，夺回被抢去之粮食大车四十余辆，我无一伤亡。
