Title: Local Armed Forces in Central Shandong Annihilate 2,000 Bandits in Half a Month
Author:
Date: 1947-10-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 21st -- To coordinate with the counter-offensive in Jiaodong, local armed forces in the Central Shandong area launched extensive attacks in Changle, Anqiu, Zibo, Laiwu, Xintai, Mengyin and other areas. In the first half of this month, they annihilated a total of 2,000 Chiang Kai-shek and puppet troops. According to incomplete statistics, in half a month, they captured more than three machine guns, more than 260 rifles and pistols, and more than 6,000 rounds of ammunition.

### 鲁中地方武装半月歼匪两千

1947-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　鲁中地方武装半月歼匪两千
    【新华社山东二十一日电】鲁中各分区地方武装策应胶东反攻，于昌乐、安邱、淄博、莱芜、新泰、蒙阴等地区广泛出击，本月上半月共歼蒋伪两千名。据不完全统计，半月中共缴获机枪三挺，长短枪二百六十余支，子弹六千余发。
