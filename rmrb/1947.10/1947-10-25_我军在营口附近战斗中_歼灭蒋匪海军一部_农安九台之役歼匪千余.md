Title: Our Army Annihilated a Portion of the Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Navy in Battles Near Yingkou; Over a Thousand Bandits Annihilated in the Battles of Nongan and Jiutai
Author:
Date: 1947-10-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 23rd: A unit of the Liaonan Democratic Allied Army engaged with Chiang Kai-shek's bandit naval forces in a battle near Yingkou on the 8th. In this battle, over 600 enemies were killed or wounded, including 14 naval personnel, and an enemy small warship and a anti-aircraft gun were destroyed.
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 23rd: In the battles to capture Nongan and Jiutai on the 18th, the Democratic Allied Army annihilated over 1,200 local enemy troops. The battle results are as follows: In the Nongan battle, over 150 enemies, including the county magistrate Hu Langge, were killed or wounded, and over 700 enemy officers and soldiers were captured; in the Jiutai battle, over 70 bandits were annihilated, and over 240 bandits were captured. Another unit of over 100 enemies who fled to Xinlitun, east of Changchun, were also completely annihilated. The main spoils of war from both battles include 12 artillery pieces of various types, 29 light and heavy machine guns, 490 rifles, 220,000 rounds of ammunition, and over 5,000 artillery shells.

### 我军在营口附近战斗中　歼灭蒋匪海军一部　农安九台之役歼匪千余

1947-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军在营口附近战斗中
  　歼灭蒋匪海军一部
  　农安九台之役歼匪千余
    【新华社东北前线二十三日电】辽南民主联军某部八日于营口附近战斗中，曾与蒋匪海军部队接触，是役毙伤敌六百余名，中有海军十四名，并击毁敌小型军舰一艘，高射炮一尊。
    【新华社东北前线二十三日电】民主联军十八日攻克农安、九台之役，歼敌地方军一千二百余人。战果如下：农安战斗毙伤敌县长胡郎阁以下一百五十余，俘敌官兵七百余；九台战斗歼匪七十余，俘匪二百四十余人，另部敌百余逃至长春以东新立屯时亦全部被歼。两役主要缴获有各种炮十二门、轻重机枪二十九挺、步枪四百九十支、子弹二十二万发、炮弹五千余发。
