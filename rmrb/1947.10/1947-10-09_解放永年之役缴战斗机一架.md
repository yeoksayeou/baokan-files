Title: One fighter plane seized in the Yongnian Campaign

Author:

Date: 1947-10-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 7th. In addition to the previously reported battle results in the liberation of Yongnian, it has now been ascertained that one Chiang Kai-shek bandit fighter plane equipped with six machine guns was captured.

### 解放永年之役缴战斗机一架

1947-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放永年之役缴战斗机一架
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫七日电】解放永年之役除前报战果外，现又查明缴获备有机枪六挺之蒋匪战斗机一架。
