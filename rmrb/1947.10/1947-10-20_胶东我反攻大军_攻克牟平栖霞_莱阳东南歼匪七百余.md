Title: Jiaodong Counter-offensive Forces Capture Muping and Qixia, Eliminating Over 700 Bandits Southeast of Laiyang
Author:
Date: 1947-10-20
Page: 1

　　Jiaodong Our Counter-offensive Army
  　Captures Muping and Qixia
  　Eliminating Over 700 Bandits Southeast of Laiyang
    【Xinhua News Agency, Jiaodong Front, 18th】Late news: The Jiaodong People's Liberation Army recovered the two county towns of Muping and Qixia on the 10th. The Chiang Kai-shek army's 54th Division and the 322nd Regiment of the reorganized 25th Division, which had occupied the area, fled in panic. Since the People's Liberation Army in the Jiaodong area switched to the counter-offensive, it has recovered five cities, including Ye County, Changyi, Weihaiwei, Qixia, and Muping, in a week.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Jiaodong Front, 18th】On the morning of the 13th, a unit of the Jiaodong People's Liberation Army fiercely attacked two regiments of the Chiang Kai-shek army's 25th Division that had ventured out of Laiyang City near Qugezhuang, more than 40 li southeast of Laiyang. After nine hours of fierce fighting, over 700 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded.

### 胶东我反攻大军　攻克牟平栖霞　莱阳东南歼匪七百余

1947-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　胶东我反攻大军
  　攻克牟平栖霞
  　莱阳东南歼匪七百余
    【新华社胶东前线十八日电】迟到消息：胶东解放军于十日收复牟平、栖霞两县城。侵据该地之蒋军五十四师一部，及整二十五师三二二团均仓惶逃窜。自解放军在胶东地区转入反攻后，一周内共收复掖县、昌邑、威海卫、栖霞、牟平等五座城市。
    【新华社胶东前线十八日电】胶东解放军某部十三日晨于莱阳东南四十余里之曲格庄一带，痛击由莱阳城出扰之蒋军二五师两个团，激战九小时，毙伤敌七百余名。
