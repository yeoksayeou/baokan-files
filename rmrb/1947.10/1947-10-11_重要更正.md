　　Important Correction

　　In yesterday's (10th) "Declaration of the Chinese People's Liberation Army," the word "等" (etc.) was omitted in the sentence "These properties were all seized by Chiang Kai-shek, etc., using their dictatorship..." Also, the word "样" (yàng - appearance) in "本军对于蒋方人员，并不一样排斥" ("Our army does not exclude Chiang's personnel in the same way") is a mistake for the word "概" (gài - generally). We hereby correct these errors.

### 重要更正

1947-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    昨日（十日）“中国人民解放军宣言”内，“这些财产都是蒋介石等利用其独裁……”一句中，遗漏“等”字。又，“本军对于蒋方人员，并不一样排斥”，“样”子系“概”字之误。特此更正。
