Title: Western Henan Establishes Postal Sub-bureau
Author:
Date: 1947-10-27
Page: 2

    (This newspaper's Taiyue dispatch on the 25th) Western Henan News: The Taiyue Postal Western Henan Sub-bureau, following the army south, after more than a month of effort, has rapidly established a main postal route of 650 *li*. County bureaus and postal stations have been established in Xinan, Mianchi, Shanxian, Luoning, Yiyang and other places. Now mail can be delivered smoothly from Lingbao in the west to Mengjin in the east and Yiyang in the south. In particular, new books and newspapers from Taiyue can now reach various counties on time.

### 豫西成立邮政分局

1947-10-27
第2版()
专栏：

    豫西成立邮政分局
    【本报太岳二十五日电】豫西讯：随大军南下之太岳邮政豫西分局，经月余努力，已迅速建立了长达六百五十余里之主干邮线，并在新安、渑池、陕县、洛宁、宜阳等地，已建立了县局及邮站，现西至灵宝，东至孟津，南达宜阳，可以畅通邮递，尤其太岳新出书报，已能按时到达各县。
