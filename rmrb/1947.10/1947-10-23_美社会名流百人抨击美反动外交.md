Title: 100 American Social Celebrities Criticize Reactionary American Diplomacy
Author:
Date: 1947-10-23
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st: New York dispatch: The National Committee of the American Friends Service Committee recently released a statement to the press signed by nearly 100 social celebrities, urging the immediate revision of American foreign policy. The statement claimed that any person with a conscience can see that the United States' current efforts to manipulate the United Nations against the Soviet Union are in violation of the UN Charter. The statement criticized the United States' attitude towards the issues of Indonesia, Spain, Greece, and Korea, and stated that the United States cannot refuse the Soviet Union's right to reparations from Germany. The statement also criticized the United States for not taking measures to disarm and reduce arms production.

### 美社会名流百人抨击美反动外交

1947-10-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　美社会名流百人抨击美反动外交
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】纽约讯：美国友谊协会全国委员会，顷向报界发表由社会名流近百人签字之声明一件伦敦促立即修正美国外交政策。声明称：任何一个有良心的人都能看到，美国现在操纵联合国以反对苏联的努力，是违背联合国宪章的。声明中抨击美国对印尼、西班牙、希腊及朝鲜问题之态度；并称：美国不能拒绝苏联自德国获得赔偿。声明又抨击美国没有采取解除军备与减少武器生产之措施。
