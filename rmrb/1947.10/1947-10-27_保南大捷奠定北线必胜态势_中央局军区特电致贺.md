Title: Victory at Baonan Establishes the Inevitable Victory of the Northern Front; Central Bureau and Military Region Send Special Telegram of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1947-10-27
Page: 1

    Victory at Baonan Establishes the Inevitable Victory of the Northern Front
    Central Bureau and Military Region Send Special Telegram of Congratulations
    [This Newspaper] News: The Jin-Cha-Ji Military Region's great victory at Baonan, the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Central Bureau and Military Region sent a congratulatory telegram. The original telegram is as follows:
    To Commander Nie and all commanders and fighters of the Jin-Cha-Ji Field Army: We are delighted to hear of the great victory at Baonan, annihilating the enemy's Third Army Headquarters and the entire Seventh Division, the Sixteenth Army's Sixty-sixth Regiment and four other regiments, capturing and killing over ten thousand men, creating a new record for our army's annihilation of the enemy since the counter-offensive in Jin-Cha-Ji, echoing the great offensive of our army in the Northeast, and establishing the inevitable victory of the northern front. We hereby send a special telegram of congratulations.
          Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Central Bureau Military Region

### 保南大捷奠定北线必胜态势  中央局军区特电致贺

1947-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    保南大捷奠定北线必胜态势
    中央局军区特电致贺
    【本报讯】晋察冀保南歼敌大捷，晋冀鲁豫中央局及军区驰电祝贺，原电如下：
    聂司令员并转晋察冀野战军全体指战员同志：欣闻保南大捷歼匪第三军军部及第七师全部、十六军六十六团等四个团，斩俘万余人，开创晋察冀我军反攻以来之歼敌新纪录，与东北我军之大攻势遥相辉映，奠定北线战局必胜之态势。特电驰贺。
          晋冀鲁豫中央局军区
