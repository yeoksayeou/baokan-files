Title: Our Army Launches Offensive in Northern Section of Pinghan Railway; Over 10,000 Bandits Annihilated Southwest of Baoding; Four Cities Including Rongcheng and Xiongxian Consecutively Captured North of Daqing River
Author:
Date: 1947-10-26
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jinchaji) 24th: In response to the Northeast Democratic Allied Army's autumn offensive, a unit of the Jinchaji People's Liberation Army launched a powerful offensive in the Pinghan Railway's Ping-Bao section and the Ping-Jin-Bao triangle area from the 8th to the 22nd, successively capturing four cities: Rongcheng, Xiongxian, Baxian, and Xinzhen, as well as over twenty important stations and strongholds including Songlin Dian, Caohe, Niutuo, and Liuquan. Thus, the vast area north of the Daqing River has been recovered by us. The Jinchaji Field Army Headquarters issued the first war communique, stating: A unit of our army launched a fierce attack on the Pinghan Line between Zhuoxian and Baoding, a distance of over two hundred li, starting on Double Tenth Day. After several fierce battles, Rongcheng was recovered, and important stations such as Songlin Dian and Caohe were recaptured. The Ping-Bao transportation line was cut by us in several sections. From Zhuoxian to Baoding, except for a few strongholds such as Dingxing, Xushui, and Gucheng, the entire railway line is under our control. In a panic, Sun Lianzhong urgently dispatched the 95th Division of the original 62nd Army, the 109th Division of the 16th Army, the 94th Division, the 121st Division of the 94th Army, and the 3rd Tank Regiment from the east and north to reinforce. From the 15th to the 21st, the enemy launched fierce attacks on our positions south of Gucheng with several tanks every day, but they were all repelled by us, and the Chiang bandits suffered heavy casualties. On the 16th, the enemy attacked our positions under the cover of nine tanks, but were met with our powerful resistance. Five of their tanks were destroyed, and three were captured. The enemy was unable to withstand and collapsed. In recent days, we have annihilated more than 2,000 enemy troops. At the same time, a unit of the People's Liberation Army in the Ping-Jin-Bao triangle area also launched a fierce attack starting on the 8th. From the 8th to the 10th, the enemy strongholds of Niutuo, Liuquan, and nearby Mazhuang and Qugou (all south of Gu'an) were recovered. On the 11th and 12th, Xinzhen and the Mao'erwan stronghold, more than ten li southwest of the city, were recovered. From the 13th to the 15th, Baxian and Hongcheng and Mawutou, more than twenty li west of Xinzhen, were recovered. On the 22nd, Xiongxian was recovered. Currently, most of the vast area of ​​Xinzhen, Baxian, Xiongxian, Yongqing, and Gu'an counties in the Pingjinbao triangle area has been recovered by us.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jinchaji) 24th Urgent Telegram: The People's Liberation Army has won an unprecedented victory near Qingfengdian, 90 li southwest of Baoding. The entire 7th Division of the Chiang bandit's 3rd Army and the 66th Regiment of the 22nd Division of the 16th Army were annihilated, and more than 10,000 people, including the 3rd Army Commander Luo Lirong and Deputy Army Commander Yang Guangyu, were not spared. The Second War Communiqué of the Jinchaji Field Army Headquarters stated: When our army on the northern front launched a fierce offensive on the Ping (Beiping)-Bao (Baoding) section, the Chiang bandits resorted to robbing Peter to pay Paul, urgently dispatching the entire 7th Division of the 3rd Army stationed in Shijiazhuang and the 66th Regiment of the 22nd Division of the 16th Army, a total of four regiments, airlifted from Shimen, led by the 3rd Army Commander Luo Lirong himself, to risk going north via Zhengding and Xinle to reinforce. When the enemy advanced to the vicinity of Qingfengdian, our surprise troops suddenly attacked and encircled the enemy in one fell swoop. After two days and nights of fighting, our army launched a general offensive at 4 am on the 22nd, ending the battle in seven hours. The entire army was wiped out and countless materials were captured. The results of the battle are being cleared.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jinchaji) 24th: In the Qingfengdian annihilation battle, our air defense forces also shot down a Chiang "B25" heavy bomber. All five pilots were captured alive by us, and fourteen light machine guns and four radio stations installed on the fuselage were captured.

### 平汉北段我军发动攻势　保定西南歼匪万余  大清河北连克容城雄县等四城

1947-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　平汉北段我军发动攻势
  　保定西南歼匪万余
    大清河北连克容城雄县等四城
    【新华社晋察冀二十四日电】晋察冀人民解放军一部与东北民主联军秋季攻势相呼应，八日至二十二日间，于平汉铁路平、保段及平津保三角地区发动强大攻势，先后攻克容城、雄县、霸县、新镇四城，及松林店、漕河、牛驼、柳泉等重要车站据点二十余处。至此大清河北广大地区，已为我收复。晋察冀野战军司令部发布第一号作战公报称：我军一部于双十节开始向涿县至保定间二百余里的平汉线上展开猛烈攻击几经激战后，收复容城，再度攻克松林店、漕河等重要车站，平、保交通为我横切数段。自涿县至保定间，除定兴、徐水、固城等少数据点外，铁路线全为我控制。孙连仲于仓惶中急调原属六二军之九五师、十六军之一零九师、九四师及九四军之一二一师及战车第三团等部，由东、北两面增援。自十五日起至二十一日，敌每日以坦克数辆向我固城以南阵地猛扑，均为我击退，蒋匪死伤惨重。十六日敌在九辆坦克掩护下，向我阵地攻击，遭我有力阻击，击毁其坦克五辆，并缴获三辆，敌不支溃退。连日来我已歼敌二千余人。与此同时，平、津、保三角地区人民解放军一部，自八日起亦猛烈出击，八日至十日，收复牛驼、柳泉及其附近马庄、渠沟（均在固安南）等匪军据点。十一至十二两日，收复新城镇及该城西南十余里之毛尔湾等据点。十三至十五日收复霸县城及新镇城西二十余里之洪城、马务头等处。二十二日收复雄县。现平津保三角地区新镇、霸县、雄县、永清、固安等县广大地区，大部由我收复。
    【新华社晋察冀二十四日急电】人民解放军于保定西南九十里之清风店附近获空前大捷，蒋匪第三军第七师及十六军二十二师六十六团全部就歼，第三军军长罗历戎、副军长杨光钰以下万余人无一漏网。晋察冀野战军司令部第二号作战公报称：当北线我军向平（北平）保（保定）段发动猛烈攻势时，蒋匪乃挖肉补疮，急调驻石家庄之第三军第七师全部及空运石门之第十六军二十二师六十六团共四个团兵力，由第三军军长罗历戎亲自率领，经正定、新乐冒险北上增援，当敌进至清风店附近时，我奇兵突出，一举将敌加以包围，击战两昼夜，至二十二日上午四时我军即发动总攻，历七小时结束战斗，该军全部覆没，缴获无算，战果正清查中。
    【新华社晋察冀二十四日电】清风店歼灭战中，我防空部队并击落蒋“Ｂ２５”式重型轰炸机一架，驾驶员五人全部被我生俘，缴获机身装设之轻机枪十四挺，电台四部。
