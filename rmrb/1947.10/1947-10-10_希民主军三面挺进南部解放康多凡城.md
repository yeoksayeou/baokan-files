Title: Greek Democratic Army Advances on Kandovan City from Three Sides in Southern Liberation
Author:
Date: 1947-10-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th. Athens Dispatch: The Greek Democratic Army continues its victorious offensive. In the south, a unit of the Greek Democratic Army liberated Kandovan City. In the northeast, the Peikon unit of the Democratic Army has been launching successful attacks for days. On one side, it is attacking the Kosana area; on the other hand, it is fiercely attacking the fortified positions of the fascist government army in the Greater Platanos Mountains, forcing the enemy to abandon their positions and flee in panic. The unit also broke through the enemy's defense line in the Vapsolis Mountains on the 2nd, and advanced into its positions, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.

### 希民主军三面挺进南部解放康多凡城

1947-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　希民主军三面挺进南部解放康多凡城
    【新华社陕北八日电】雅典讯：希民主军仍继续胜利攻势。在南部，希民主军某部解放康多凡城。在东北部，民主军培康部连日胜利出击，一面进攻柯萨纳地区；另一方面则猛攻在大普拉塔诺斯山区的法西斯政府军设防阵地，迫使敌军放弃阵地仓惶逃窜。该部并于二日突破瓦普索利斯山区敌军防线，进驻其阵地，敌伤亡惨重。
