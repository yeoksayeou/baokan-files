Title: Annihilation of 2,300 Bandits in the Yantai-Qingdao Highway Campaign
Author:
Date: 1947-10-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 26th: In the battles of Sunshou and Dongtaipingzhuang on the Yantai-Qingdao Highway southwest of Laiyang, Jiaodong, which were won by the People's Liberation Army on the 16th of this month, the confirmed battle results are as follows: Over 760 officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang bandit New 74th Division, 57th Brigade, 170th Regiment, including Regiment Commander Xiao Fengming (killed in action), were killed or wounded; over 1,600 were captured alive; and the following were seized: a total of 12 machine guns, mortars, and 60mm guns; 71 light and heavy machine guns; over 600 rifles and carbines; 55 submachine guns; over 740 rounds of artillery shells; over 106,000 rounds of ammunition; 4 cars; and 28 mules and horses.

### 烟青公路之役歼匪二千三百

1947-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    烟青公路之役歼匪二千三百
    【新华社华东前线二十六日电】本月十六日解放军攻克胶东莱阳西南烟（台）青（岛）公路上之孙受、东太平庄之役，查明战果如下：毙伤蒋匪新编之七四师五七旅一七○团团长萧凤鸣（被击毙）以下官兵七百六十余名，生俘其一千六百余名，缴获机关炮，迫击炮，六○炮共十二门，轻重机枪七十一挺，长短枪六百余支，冲锋枪五十五支，炮弹七百四十余发，子弹十万六千余发，汽车四辆，骡马二十八匹。
