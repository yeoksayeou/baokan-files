Title: Slogans of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1947-10-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th: The General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issued sixty-seven slogans on the Double Tenth Festival, instructing the entire army to implement them. The original text is as follows:

    Order of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    Comrades, Military and Political Leaders at all levels of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: We are now issuing sixty-seven slogans of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After receiving them, you should immediately distribute them to all commanders and fighters, explain them item by item, remember them firmly, and implement them conscientiously. At the same time, wherever you go, you should write, post, print, and publicize these slogans to the people of the whole country, to those deceived and coerced in the Chiang Party and Chiang Army, so that they fully understand, unite as one, overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, and establish a new China.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. October 10, 1947
    Slogans of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    (1) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek and establish a new China.
    (2) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek who betrayed the Political Consultative Conference resolutions.
    (3) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek who sabotaged the truce agreement.
    (4) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, the mastermind of the civil war.
    (5) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, the dictator.
    (6) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, the counter-revolutionary.
    (7) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, who slaughtered the people.
    (8) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, who deceived the people.
    (9) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, who trampled on human rights.
    (10) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, who imposed exorbitant taxes and levies.
    (11) Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, the running dog of American imperialism.
    (12) Only by overthrowing Chiang Kai-shek can there be peace.
    (13) Only by overthrowing Chiang Kai-shek can there be food to eat.
    (14) Only by overthrowing Chiang Kai-shek can there be democracy.
    (15) Only by overthrowing Chiang Kai-shek can there be independence.
    (16) Resolutely, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely eliminate Chiang Kai-shek's invading army.
    (17) Try war criminals and compensate the people for their losses.
    (18) Dissolve the secret service agencies and bring the murderers to justice.
    (19) Overthrow the Four Big Families and confiscate bureaucratic capital.
    (20) Overthrow corrupt officials and bureaucrats.
    (21) Overthrow local tyrants and evil gentry.
    (22) Workers, peasants, soldiers, students, and merchants unite to form an anti-Chiang united front.
    (23) Various people's organizations and democratic parties unite to form a democratic coalition government.
    (24) Intellectuals unite with the people and serve the people.
    (25) The people have freedom of speech, publication, assembly, and association.
    (26) Abolish all exorbitant taxes and levies.
    (27) Protect national industry and commerce.
    (28) Improve the lives of workers and staff.
    (29) Open granaries to relieve the poor.
    (30) Peasants get land, tillers own their land.
    (31) Equally distribute land and abolish old debts.
    (32) Nationalist soldiers get land and debt cancellation.
    (33) Everyone has land to cultivate (or everyone has fields to till).
    (34) Everyone has food to eat.
    (35) Everyone has clothes to wear.
    (36) Everyone has a house to live in.
    (37) Everyone has work to do.
    (38) Everyone has books to read.
    (39) Equality between men and women.
    (40) Equality among nationalities.
    (41) Religious freedom.
    (42) Protect overseas Chinese.
    (43) Protect foreign nationals.
    (44) American troops get out of China.
    (45) Denounce traitorous diplomacy and abolish the Sino-American Commercial Treaty.
    (46) Do not allow the revival of Japanese aggression forces.
    (47) The Chinese and American people unite to oppose American imperialism's aggression against China.
    (48) The Eastern nations unite to resist imperialist aggression.
    (49) Unite with all nations in the world that treat us as equals and fight together.
    (50) Mao Zedong is the savior of China.
    (51) The Communist Party represents the people of the whole country, and Chiang Kai-shek represents the corrupt and evil gentry.
    (52) The People's Liberation Army is the army of the people.
    (53) The poor want to turn over a new leaf, join the People's Liberation Army.
    (54) Implement the Three Main Rules of Discipline and the Eight Points for Attention.
    (55) Do not take a single needle or thread from the people.
    (56) Take good care of wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, and give preferential treatment to the families of the People's Liberation Army.
    (57) The liberated areas are the general rear of the people of the whole country.
    (58) The liberated areas thoroughly implement land reform, practice production and thrift, and support the front lines.
    (59) The people in the Chiang-controlled areas arm themselves, resist conscription and grain requisition, and divide land and abolish debts.
    (60) Nationalist Party members, break away from Chiang Kai-shek, the traitor to Sun Yat-sen.
    (61) Chiang's army officers and soldiers, do not fight for the corrupt and evil gentry.
    (62) Welcome Chiang's army officers and soldiers to join the People's Liberation Army.
    (63) Bad people who repent and reform are allowed to atone for their crimes.
    (64) Chiang Kai-shek is bound to perish, and the Communist Party is bound to prosper.
    (65) Fight to Nanjing and capture Chiang Kai-shek alive.
    (66) Long live the Chinese People's Liberation.
    (67) Long live the liberation of the Chinese nation.

### 中国人民解放军口号

1947-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军口号
    【新华社陕北九日电】中国人民解放军总部于双十节颁布口号六十七条，训令全军实行，原文如下：
    中国人民解放军训令
    中国人民解放军各级军政首长同志们：现在，我们发布中国人民解放军口号六十七条。你们接到后，应即发给全军指挥员、战斗员，逐条讲解、牢记，认真实行。同时，在你们所到之处，你们应当将这些口号向全国人民，向蒋党蒋军被欺骗被胁从的人们，普遍地书写、张贴、印发、宣讲，务使他们完全了解，万众一心，打倒蒋介石，建立新中国。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国人民解放军总部。一九四七年十月十日
    中国人民解放军口号

    （二）打倒背叛政协决议的蒋介石。

    （四）打倒内战祸首蒋介石。
    （五）打倒独裁者蒋介石。
    （六）打倒反革命的蒋介石。
