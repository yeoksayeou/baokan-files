Title: The Two Paths of Post-War World Economic Development (Volume 7)
Author:
Date: 1947-10-14
Page: 1

Which will win out in the struggle between the world's reactionary forces, headed by US imperialism, and the world's democratic forces? This is a question that everyone is studying and paying attention to. This book examines the future development of world democratic forces and reactionary forces from the foundation of all power – the economy. Once we clearly understand this point, we will realize that the inevitable decline of US imperialism is something that no one can prevent.

### 战后世界经济发展的两条道路（第七辑）

1947-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　战后世界经济发展的两条道路（第七辑）
    以美帝国主义为首的世界反动势力，和世界民主力量之间的斗争，到底谁胜谁败？这是大家都在研究、注意的事情。这本书是从一切力量的基础——经济，来看世界民主力量与反动势力的发展前途。我们把这一点看清楚了，对美帝国主义的必然没落，便知道那是谁也挽救不住的。
