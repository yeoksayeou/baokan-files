Title: Chen and Su's Army Gallops Through Eastern Henan and Northern Anhui, Capturing Eleven Cities Including Fuyang; Liu and Deng's Army in Eastern Hubei Annihilates Two Thousand Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-10-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Front, 15th: The southward Chen and Su army continues to expand its offensive, galloping through eastern Henan and northern Anhui, clearing out the remaining Chiang bandits. In the five days from the 8th to the 12th of this month, on the vast plain eight hundred li wide, pressing on Zhengzhou in the west and approaching Xuzhou in the east, they recovered eleven captured county towns including Weichuan, Fuyang, Lingbi, and Sixian, as well as the famous town of Zhuxian. A total of over 1,200 Chiang Kai-shek county magistrates, deputy heads of security corps, and battalion commanders were captured, and over 200 enemies were killed or wounded. It is estimated that Tongxu city was recovered on the 9th, capturing over 80 Chiang bandit security regiment battalion commanders and below, and seizing six light and heavy machine guns and over 30 rifles. On the same day, Shenqiu city was recovered. At dawn on the 10th, Weishi city was recovered. The Chiang armies in both cities fled upon hearing the news. On the 10th, Weichuan county town southeast of Zhengzhou and Zhuxian town, 50 li south of Kaifeng, were also recovered, capturing over 100 enemies and seizing two light machine guns and 43 rifles. On the 11th, Lingbi city southeast of Xuzhou in northeastern Anhui was recovered, capturing over 40 enemies. On the 12th, the Chiang army in Sixian scattered upon hearing the news, and the Liberation Army immediately recovered the city. On the same day, Fuyang city, an important military location and transportation hub in northern Anhui, was captured, completely annihilating the Chiang Kai-shek Taihe refugee county government and county corps, capturing over 314 people including Taihe County Magistrate Qiu Tianmin and Deputy Commander Hu Haitian, killing over 100 enemies, and seizing one mortar, seven light machine guns, 281 long and short guns, and one car. On the same day, Xincai city was recovered, completely annihilating the Chiang Kai-shek county government and four security regiment companies, capturing over 550 people including County Magistrate Dong Caimin, and seizing one mortar, five light machine guns, and over 400 rifles and carbines. On the same day, Kuigou and Yanling cities were recovered, and the Chiang armies fled upon hearing the news. In addition, the Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Liberation Army coordinated operations and recovered Suixian city on the 8th, annihilating the entire Chiang Kai-shek county government and security regiment, capturing over 180 enemies, killing or wounding over 100, and seizing two mortars, 13 light and heavy machine guns, 360 rifles, and over 10,000 rounds of ammunition.
    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 15th: Liu and Deng's counter-offensive army annihilated four battalions of Chiang bandit troops in the area east of Huangpi on the 8th and 9th, totaling 1,800 men. The seventh communique of the Liu-Deng Field Headquarters stated: Our army annihilated part of the direct subordinate team of the New 17th Brigade of the 56th Division of Chiang bandit army, the third battalion of its first regiment and the second regiment (missing the third battalion), and the entire enemy force of the third battalion of the 99th regiment of the 52nd division in the Liuzigang-Lijiaji area east of Huangpi on the 7th and 8th of this month, totaling over 580 enemy soldiers killed, over 1,200 officers and soldiers captured, 12 mortars seized, 15 60mm mortars, 13 grenade launchers, 61 various light and heavy machine guns, 580 rifles and carbines, tens of thousands of rounds of ammunition, over 500 bombs, 4 radio stations, 4 cars, 16 telephones, and many other spoils of war.

### 陈粟大军驰骋豫东皖北　连克阜阳等十一城　鄂东刘邓大军歼匪两千

1947-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈粟大军驰骋豫东皖北
  　连克阜阳等十一城
  　鄂东刘邓大军歼匪两千
    【新华社豫皖苏前线十五日电】南下陈粟大军继续扩展攻势，驰骋豫东皖北，清扫残余蒋匪，于本月八日至十二日五天内，在西逼郑州、东薄徐州的八百里广大平原上，收复新旧被占了洧川、阜阳、灵璧、泗县等十一座县城及朱仙名镇，共俘蒋记县长、保安总队副、大队长以下一千二百余名，毙伤敌二百余名。计九日收复通许城，俘蒋匪保安团大队长以下八十余名，缴轻重机枪六挺，步枪三十余支。同日收复沈邱城，十日拂晓收复尉氏城，两城蒋军均闻风逃逸。十日并收复郑州东南之洧川县城及开封南五十里之朱仙镇，俘敌百余名，缴轻机枪两挺、步枪四十三支。十一日收复皖东北徐州东南之灵璧城，俘敌四十余。十二日泗县蒋军闻风逃散，解放军当即收复该城。同日攻克皖北军事要地与交通中心阜阳城，全歼蒋记太和流亡县府及县总队，俘太和县长仇天民、总队副胡海天以下三百一十四名，毙敌百余名，缴迫击炮一门，轻机枪七挺，长短枪二百八十一支，汽车一辆。同日收复新蔡城，全歼蒋记县府及保安团四个中队，俘县长董采民以下五百五十名，缴迫击炮一门，轻机枪五挺，步马枪四百余支。同日收复块沟、鄢陵两城，蒋军均望风逃逸。又豫皖苏解放军配合作战，于八日收复睢县城，歼蒋记县府及保安团全部，俘敌一百八十余，毙伤百余，缴迫击炮两门，轻重机枪十三挺，步枪三百六十支，子弹万余发。
    【新华社中原前线十五日电】刘邓反攻大军，于八九两日在黄陂以东地区歼灭蒋匪军四个营，计一千八百人。刘邓野战司令部第七号公报称：我军某部于本月七八两日在黄陂以东之柳子港李家集地区，歼灭蒋匪五十六师新十七旅旅直属队一部，及其第一团之第三营及第二团（缺第三营），以及五十二师三十三旅九九团第三营等敌全部，共计毙敌五百八十余名，俘敌官兵一千二百余名，缴获迫击炮十二门，六○小炮十五门，掷弹筒十三个，各式轻重机枪六十一挺，长短枪五百八十枝，子弹数万发，炸弹五百余枚，电台四部，汽车四辆，电话机十六部，其他胜利品甚多。
