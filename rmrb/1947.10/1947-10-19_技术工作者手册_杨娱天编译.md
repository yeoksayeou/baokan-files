　　Technical Worker's Handbook
  　Compiled by Yang Yutian
    (Taihang Industrial Series, No. 4)
    This book includes quadrature, geometric drawing, various number tables, general industrial knowledge, machinery, electricity, materials, properties, material weights, and Chinese and foreign weights and measures. It encompasses the basic knowledge, formulas, and calculation tables needed for current industrial and mining construction in the Liberated Areas. With this book in hand, formulas that are difficult to remember and frequently used numbers can be easily looked up. It is truly an essential book for cadres in industrial and mining construction, technical workers, and industrial students.
    This book is printed on woodfree paper, quarto size, cloth cover, hardcover, with gold lettering. It is beautiful, durable, and easy to carry. The pre-order price is three thousand yuan, and the pre-order period ends on November 15th.
