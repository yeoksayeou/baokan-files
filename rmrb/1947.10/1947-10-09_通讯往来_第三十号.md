Author:
Date: 1947-10-09
Page: 1

　　Communications and Exchanges
  　No. 30 To Comrades Gao Meng, Kui Lin, Fu Guang of Lincheng, and Feng Ze of Shahe:
    Your in-depth practical investigation revealed that the difficulties faced by poor and hired peasants in planting wheat this autumn are: insufficient land, lack of fertilizer, livestock, farm tools, and seeds. You have also put forward preliminary solutions. This is a major issue. We hope you will continue to report on the difficulties in resolving this issue, the methods to overcome them, the achievements and experiences, in order to meet the wheat planting needs of the poor and hired peasants.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Communications and Liaison Section of this Newspaper

### 通讯往来　第三十号

1947-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
  　第三十号沙河高孟、奎林、临城福广、风泽等同志：
    你们深入实际调查研究，发现了贫、雇农今秋种麦的困难是：土地不够，缺乏肥料、牲畜、农具和种子，并提出初步解决办法、这是一个大问题。希速继续报导解决这一问题的困难、克服办法，成绩与经验，达到满足贫雇农种麦要求的目的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
