Title: Our Army Recovers Juan Cheng, Local Armed Forces Eliminate 400 Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-10-21
Page: 1

　　Our Army Recovers Juan Cheng
  　Local Armed Forces Eliminate 400 Bandits
    [Our Newspaper, Ji Lu Yu, 19th Dispatch] When Juan Cheng was recovered, a certain regiment of our local armed forces actively cooperated with the main force in combat, eliminating more than 400 enemies and seizing a great deal of spoils, earning commendation from the military region headquarters. On the 2nd of this month, when the East China troops besieged Juan Cheng, a certain regiment of our local armed forces, in order to cooperate with the main force in combat, arrived at Dongkou (under the jurisdiction of Juan Cheng) at dawn after a night of rapid march to block the enemy, and captured more than 300 men from a battalion of the 355th Regiment of the 119th Brigade of the Kuomintang's reorganized 68th Division and more than 100 members of the Home Returning Corps, thus Dongkou was recovered by us. In this battle, a total of one 60mm mortar, four heavy machine guns, twenty light machine guns, one hundred and twenty-three rifles and carbines, four pistols, more than 22,700 rounds of ammunition, one telephone, and many other military supplies were seized. Another local corps on the same day, at Maliang Teng Village between the Shan (County) Dang (Shan) highway, defeated the engineering battalion of the 96th Brigade of the Kuomintang's 5th Division and a part of the 286th Regiment of the brigade, which were being transferred south along the highway, capturing more than 20 enemies, killing and wounding 72, seizing seven light machine guns, ten rifles, two Thompson submachine guns, more than 20,000 rounds of ammunition, and destroying three vehicles.
    [Our Newspaper, Ji Lu Yu, 19th Dispatch] The Ji Lu Yu Military Region Headquarters recently announced that our local armed forces' achievements in September were as follows: 21 battles, 369 enemies killed or wounded, 184 enemies captured, three 60mm mortars seized, one mortar, ten light machine guns, two heavy machine guns, one hundred and seventy-two rifles, nine pistols, and more than 40,000 rounds of ammunition. The casualty ratio was sixteen to one.

### 我军收复鄄城之役　地武歼匪四百

1947-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军收复鄄城之役
  　地武歼匪四百
    【本报冀鲁豫十九日电】鄄城收复时，我地方武装某团积极配合主力作战，歼敌四百余，缴获甚丰，获得军区司令部嘉奖。本月二日，华东部队围攻鄄城时，我地方武装某团为配合主力作战，经一夜的急行军，于拂晓赶到董口（鄄城属）阻击，并将盘据之蒋匪军整六八师一一九旅三五五团一个营三百余人及还乡团一百余人生俘，董口乃为我收复。此役计缴获六○炮一门，重机枪四挺、轻机枪二十挺，步马枪一百二十三支，短枪四支，子弹二万二千七百余发，电话机一部，及其他军用品甚多。又另一地方兵团于同日在单（县）砀（山）公路间之马良誊庄，将沿公路南调之蒋匪军第五师九六旅工兵营及该旅二八六团一部击溃，俘敌二十余，毙伤七十二名，缴轻机枪七挺，步枪十支，汤姆枪两支，子弹两万余发，毁汽车三辆。
    【本报冀鲁豫十九日电】冀鲁豫军区司令部顷公布我地方武装九月份战绩计：作战二十一次，毙伤敌三百六十九名，俘敌一百八十四名，缴获六○炮三门，迫击炮一门，轻机枪十挺，重机枪两挺，步枪一百七十二支，短枪九支，子弹四万余发。伤亡比例为十六比一。
