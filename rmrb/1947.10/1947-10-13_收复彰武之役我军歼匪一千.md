Title: Our Army Annihilated 1,000 Bandits in the Battle to Recover Zhangwu
Author:
Date: 1947-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 11th: A certain unit of the Democratic Allied Army captured Zhangwu on the 7th and annihilated one regiment of Chiang bandit Temporary 57th Division (reorganized from the puppet Northeast Security Force 12th District unit). The results of the battle are as follows: Over 200 bandit officers and soldiers were killed or wounded, 2 bandit battalion commanders, 1 county magistrate, and 775 officers and soldiers below the rank of company commander were captured. A total of 5 mortars and 60 mortars, 51 light and heavy machine guns, 570 rifles and pistols, and a portion of other ammunition and supplies were seized.

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 11th: The leaders of the Liaodong Military Region have issued an order to commend a certain meritorious unit that recovered Haicheng and Dashiqiao. This unit, with its spirit of active mobility, heroism, and decisiveness, launched a surprise attack and recovered Haicheng and Dashiqiao in one fell swoop, and annihilated the Chiang bandit 52nd Army 25th Division 74th Regiment, which was fleeing north from Yingkou, between Niuzhuang and Gengjiazi. They have made outstanding military achievements. Order all to be credited once.

Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe Liao, 11th: A certain unit of the Jicha Rehe Liao People's Liberation Army announced the supplementary report on the results of cutting off the Chiang bandit gang's civil war artery and sabotaging the Beining Railway as follows: Four stations, including Shahousuo, Baitangsi, Dongxinzhuang, and Wanghaidian, between Xingcheng and Suizhong were captured; 45 fortresses were captured; 178 *li* of rails were overturned, a total of 16,368 rails (all calculated as single track); 528 rails were blown up; 145,126 sleepers were overturned; 22 bridges were destroyed; more than 70 enemies were killed, wounded, or captured; 1 machine gun, 44 rifles and pistols, and 5,000 rounds of various bullets were seized.

### 收复彰武之役我军歼匪一千

1947-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　收复彰武之役我军歼匪一千
    【新华社东北前线十一日电】民主联军某部七日攻克彰武歼灭蒋匪暂五十七师（伪东北保安队十二区部队改编）一个团，战果如下：计毙伤匪官兵二百余名，俘匪营长二名，县长一名，连长八名以下官兵七百七十五名，缴获迫击炮六○炮共五门，轻重机枪五十一挺，长短枪五百七十支，及其他弹药物资一部。
    【新华社东北前线十一日电】辽东军区首长顷传令嘉奖收复海城、大石桥某有功部队，该部以积极机动英勇果敢之精神，出奇兵一举收复海城及大石桥，并歼灭由营口北逃之蒋匪五十二军二十五师七十四团于牛庄及耿家子之间，战功卓著。令全体记功一次。
    【新华社冀察热辽十一日电】冀察热辽人民解放军某部公布截断蒋匪帮内战动脉破击北宁路的战果续报如下：攻克兴城至绥中间沙后所、白堂寺、东辛庄、望海店等车站四个，堡垒四十五座，翻铁轨一百七十八里，共一万六千三百六十八根（均按单轨计），炸铁轨五百二十八根，翻枕木十四万五千一百二十六根，炸毁桥梁二十二座，毙伤俘敌七十余名，缴机枪一挺，长短枪四十四支，各种子弹五千发。
