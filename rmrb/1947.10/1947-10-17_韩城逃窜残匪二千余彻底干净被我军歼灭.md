Title: Over Two Thousand Remnants of Fleeing Bandits from Hancheng Completely Annihilated by Our Army
Author:
Date: 1947-10-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 15th: The Northwest People's Liberation Army on the 12th and 13th completely and cleanly annihilated the 158th Regiment Direct Unit and its two battalions of the 53rd Brigade of the 90th Division of the Chiang Kai-shek-Hu bandit army, which had abandoned Hancheng and fled, along with two companies of the field artillery battalion of the 78th Brigade of the First Division, and two battalions of the self-defense forces of Hancheng and @yang counties, totaling over 2,000 enemy soldiers captured alive, and over 300 enemy soldiers killed or wounded. Detailed battle results are still being tallied. It is reported that when the Liberation Army captured Hancheng County on the 11th, it captured over 300 enemy rifles and seized six intact field guns.

### 韩城逃窜残匪二千余彻底干净被我军歼灭

1947-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　韩城逃窜残匪二千余彻底干净被我军歼灭
    【新华社西北十五日电】西北人民解放军于十二及十三两日，彻底干净全部歼灭由韩城弃城逃窜之蒋胡匪军九十师五十三旅一五八团团直及其两个营，与第一师七十八旅野炮营之两个连以及韩城＠阳两县自卫团之两个营，计生俘敌二千余名，毙伤敌三百余名，详细战果正清查中。按解放军于十一日攻克韩城县城时，曾俘敌人枪三百余，缴获完好之野炮六门。
