Title: The Flourishing Development of the Fujian People's Army: Implementing Grain Distribution to Aid the Poor, Enthusiastically Welcomed by the Masses
Author:
Date: 1947-10-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th: According to a report in the Shanghai Ta Kung Pao on the 14th of last month: The Fujian People's Army has been flourishing recently, establishing "Fujian People's Livelihood Teams" everywhere, implementing grain distribution to aid the poor, and seizing weapons from the Jiang bandit's township offices to strengthen themselves. People from all over are actively participating. The "People's Livelihood Teams" in various regions have unified leadership, with Ningde and Fu'an in northeastern Fujian being the most active. Zhao Suiming's 12th Detachment of the Livelihood Team in Ningde roams freely, leaving the Jiang bandit government helpless. Furthermore: A Fuzhou dispatch in the Tianjin Yi Shi Bao on the 27th of last month revealed that people's guerrilla units in Jinjiang, Nan'an, and Anxi in southern Fujian, Ninghua and Liancheng in western Fujian, and various places in northern Fujian are all extremely active.

### 福建民军蓬勃发展　实行开仓济贫群众热烈欢迎

1947-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　福建民军蓬勃发展
  　实行开仓济贫群众热烈欢迎
    【新华社陕北二十日电】据上月十四日沪大公报透露：福建民军近来蓬勃发展，到处建立“福建人民谋生队”，实行开仓济贫，并夺取蒋匪乡公所枪械以壮大自己，各地人民纷纷参加。各地“人民谋生队”有统一领导，其中以闽东北之宁德、福安为最活跃，宁德境内谋生队第十二支队赵随明部纵横驰骋，蒋匪政府束手无策。又讯：上月廿七日津益世报载福州通讯中透露：闽南晋江、南安、安溪，闽西宁化、连城及闽北各地人民游击队均极活跃。
