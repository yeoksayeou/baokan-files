Title: Central Plains Recovers Huang'an, Luotian, and Annihilates Over a Thousand Chiang Kai-shek Bandits in Western Henan
Author:
Date: 1947-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 7th: A certain unit of our army recaptured Huang'an County seat on the 3rd in one fell swoop, annihilating two battalions of the Chiang-supported Hubei Provincial Security Second General Brigade. Preliminary statistics show that over a hundred enemies were killed (including one deputy battalion commander), and over four hundred were captured, including Zhou Xiaoxuan, Hubei Provincial Government Military Inspector and Deputy Commander of the First and Second Columns, and the county magistrate. Our army suffered only four minor injuries. We seized five mortars and small cannons, over thirty light machine guns, one anti-aircraft machine gun, three hundred and ninety long and short guns, three hundred rounds of various shells, eighty thousand rounds of ammunition, two radio stations, one car, and many other military supplies. Huang'an was first liberated by us on September 4th, and then our army voluntarily withdrew. This is the second time it has returned to our hands.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 7th: A certain unit of our army operating in the Macheng area recovered important towns such as Zhongguan and Songbu on the 3rd, annihilating over a hundred Chiang-supported local troops entrenched in the area. Our army is now taking advantage of the victory to expand its gains.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 7th: Delayed news: The county seats of Luotian and Lihuang have also been recovered by us. Luotian was occupied by the enemy on September 26th. After a slight attack by a certain unit of the Liberation Army on the morning of the 29th, the defending enemy abandoned the city and fled, and the city was liberated by us for the third time. Lihuang was occupied by the Chiang bandit 58th Division on September 25th. After continuous attacks and encirclement by a certain unit of the Liberation Army day and night, the defending enemy fled north on the 27th, and the city was also liberated by us for the second time.

Xinhua News Agency, Western Henan Front, 7th: Delayed news: On September 20th, a unit of our army on the eastern front engaged in a fierce battle with the enemy's 49th Brigade of the Third Division at Luhunling Mountain, 30 miles northeast of Song County, killing, wounding, and capturing over seven hundred enemy soldiers. Furthermore, on the 13th, a unit of our army on the western front annihilated one battalion of the enemy's 167th Brigade of the First Division between Sixiang and Zhongdizhen, killing, wounding, and capturing over four hundred enemy soldiers, including the battalion commander.

Xinhua News Agency, Western Henan Front, 7th: General Chen Geng's headquarters has just issued Communiqué No. 11, announcing the results of the annihilation of the 64th Brigade at Tiemen, Xin'an, as follows: Over three hundred enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, two thousand enemy soldiers were captured alive, including Deputy Brigade Commander Wang Wencai, two mountain guns, nineteen mortars and 60mm small cannons, seventy-seven light and heavy machine guns, twenty hand-held machine guns, six hundred and forty-seven rifles, thirty thousand rounds of ammunition, six cars, five radio stations, eight telephones, over a hundred head of livestock, and many other military supplies were seized.

### 中原收复黄安罗田豫西再歼蒋匪千余

1947-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原收复黄安罗田豫西再歼蒋匪千余
    【新华社中原前线七日电】我军某部三日一举收复黄安县城，歼灭蒋记湖北省保安第二总队两个大队。初步统计，毙敌百余名（内大队副一名），俘湖北省政府军事视察员兼第一、二纵队副指挥官周晓轩及县长等以下四百余名。我军仅轻伤四名。缴获迫击炮、小炮五门，轻机枪三十余挺，高射机枪一挺，长短枪三百九十支，各种炮弹三百发，子弹八万发，电台两部，汽车一辆，及其它军用品甚多。按黄安于九月四日第一次为我解放，后我军主动撤出，此次重归我手。
    【新华社中原前线七日电】活动于麻城地区之我军某部，于三日收复中馆、宋埠等重镇，歼盘踞该地之蒋记地方部队百余名，我军刻正乘胜扩张战果中。
    【新华社中原前线七日电】迟到消息：罗田、立煌两县城又已为我收复。罗田系于九月二十六日为敌窜占，解放军某部于二十九日上午稍加攻击，守敌即弃城而逃，该城遂为我第三次光复。立煌于九月二十五日为蒋匪五十八师窜踞，经解放军某部日夜出击围困，守敌遂于二十七日向北逃窜，该城亦为我再度光复。
    【新华社豫西前线七日电】迟到消息：东线我军一部，九月二十日于嵩县东北三十里陆浑岭山头与敌第三师四十九旅激战，毙伤俘敌七百余名。又十三日，西线我军一部在寺乡中底镇间歼灭敌第一师一六七旅一个营，毙伤俘该敌营长以下四百余名。
    【新华社豫西前线七日电】陈赓将军总部顷发表第十一号公报，公布新安属铁门歼灭六十四旅战果如下：毙伤敌三百余名，生俘敌副旅长王文才以下二千人，缴获山炮二门，迫击炮及六○小炮十九门，轻重机枪七十七挺，手提机枪二十挺，步枪六百四十七支，子弹三万发，汽车六辆，电台五部，电话机八部，牲口百余头，及其它军用品甚多。
