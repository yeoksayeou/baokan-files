　　The Backbone of the World's Democratic Forces
  　An Introduction to Communist Parties in Various Countries

The communique of the Nine-Nation Communist Party Conference in Europe pointed out that the strength of the world's people is great, and the aggressors are bound to fail. What is the basis for this? We can understand by looking at the statistics of the number of Communists in various countries, the mainstay of the people's strength, and know that "underestimating one's own strength and overestimating the strength of the imperialist camp" is groundless.

The following statistics are based on existing materials and the "Communism on the March" chart made by the British Communist Party in January of this year. Naturally, the statistics here are not complete, because there are still some countries in the world where the Communist Party is being destroyed and suppressed by the remnant forces of Fascism and cannot publicly display its activities and strength, such as Spain, Portugal, and other countries. But as far as is already known, there are already 20 million Communists in the world. What about their influence and strength?

First, one out of every hundred people in the world is a Communist.

Second, according to the estimation of the Communist International before the war, each Communist Party member influences and unites at least fifteen to twenty workers and laborers (such as trade unions all over the world). In addition, the world's democrats influenced by the Communist Party are all over the place, and their influence is very obvious.

Third, according to the Communist International's announcement before the war, Belgium had only 7,000 party members, but it won 160,000 votes in the municipal council elections. Now it is ten years later. During the war, the people of all countries quickly awakened, and the Communist Parties of all countries have won the enthusiastic support of the people, and their influence has certainly increased even more.

Fourth, the Chinese materials in the table below are incomplete, and the strength of the Communist Parties in African countries is not included. However, Communist Parties such as the Union of South Africa, Algeria, Eritrea, Madagascar, and Tunisia also have a considerable number of members and a great influence.

### 世界民主力量的中坚　各国共产党介绍

1947-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界民主力量的中坚
  　各国共产党介绍
    欧洲九国共产党会议公报中指出：世界人民力量强大，侵略者必失败；这有什么根据呢？我们看看人民力量的中坚——各国共产党人数的统计就可以明白了，就知道“过低估计自己的力量与过高估计帝国主义阵营的力量”是毫无根据的了。
    以下统计是根据现有材料及今年一月英共所制“共产主义的前进”图表制成的。自然，这里统计的并不完全，因为世界上还有一些国家的共产党受着法西斯残余势力的摧残压制而不能公开自己的活动和力量，如西班牙、葡萄牙等国家。但就这已知道的，世界共产党员已有二千万了，他们的影响和力量怎样呢？
    第一、世界每百人中就有一个共产党员。
    第二、根据共产国际在战前估计，每一个共产党员至少影响与团结着十五个及二十个工人及劳动者（如布满全世界的职工会），此外，共产党员所影响着的世界民主人士布满各处，其影响之大，是很显然的。
    第三、在战前据共产国际发表，在比利时才七千党员，可是在市议会选举当中就得了十六万张选票。现在已经是十年以后了，在战争中各国人民迅速觉醒，各国共产党都得到了人民热烈的拥护，影响当然更加增涨了。
    第四、下表中国材料不全，非洲各国共产党力量未列入，但如南非联邦、阿尔及利亚、厄立特里亚、马达加斯加、突尼西亚等共产党亦拥有相当人数，影响甚大。
