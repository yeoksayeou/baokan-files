Title: Northeast Captures Nine Cities in One Week, Eliminating 20,000 Chiang Kai-shek Troops; Raids on the Chinese Changchun Railway and Beining Railway Continue

Author:
Date: 1947-10-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 10th: The Democratic Allied Forces Headquarters issued a public communique on the 12th, announcing the results of operations for the week from the 1st to the 7th (the results of the capture of Zhangwu on the 7th are not included): (1) 19,066 Chiang Kai-shek troops were killed, wounded, or captured. Among them, 4,447 enemy regular army soldiers, including Major General Jin Zanchong (previously misreported as Jin Zansong), commander of the 2nd Cavalry Division, were killed or wounded, and 426 local troops were killed or wounded. 10,828 regular army soldiers, including division commander Liu Runchuan and deputy division commander Zhang Shaoxian of the 116th Division, were captured, and 3,365 local troops were captured. (2) The nine cities of Yitong, Xifeng, Faku, Lishu, Gongzhuling, Changtu, Kaiyuan, Haicheng, and Zhangwu were recovered. (3) 232 artillery pieces, 527 light and heavy machine guns, 423 submachine guns, 6,614 rifles and carbines, over 800,000 rounds of various bullets and shells, and 42 vehicles (10 of which were destroyed) were seized. Two Chiang Kai-shek aircraft were shot down, and 958 war horses and other ammunition and military supplies were captured.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 10th: On the 8th, there were continued small-scale battles on the front lines. Chiang Kai-shek's New First Army attempted to probe from Siping and Changchun. Two regiments of the enemy's New 38th Division, which had fled from Banlashanmen southeast of Siping, moved under the cover of artillery fire. They were routed by our forces and fled back after more than 50 were captured. A portion of the enemy's New 30th Division, which had fled south from Changchun by car to the vicinity of Zhengjiatun, fired a burst of gunfire and then fled in panic.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 10th: Belated news: On the evening of the 2nd, a certain unit of the Democratic Allied Forces on the southern front captured important strongholds such as Gaoguansai and Xingtaizi north of Benxi. Raids continue to unfold, and hundreds of pillboxes and barbed wire fences on the Chinese Changchun Railway and Beining Railway have been leveled.

### 东北一周克城九座　歼蒋匪达两万　中长、北宁破袭战继续展开

1947-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北一周克城九座
  　歼蒋匪达两万
  　中长、北宁破袭战继续展开
    【新华社东北十日电】民主联军总部十二号公报，公布一日至七日一周作战战果（七日攻克彰武战果未计在内）如下：计（一）毙伤俘蒋军一万九千零六十六名。其中毙伤敌正规军骑二师少将师长金赞中（前电误为金赞松）以下四千四百四十七名，地方军四百二十六名。俘正规军一一六师正副师长刘润川、张绍贤以下一万零八百二十八名，地方军三千三百六十五名。（二）共收复伊通、西丰、法库、梨树、公主岭、昌图、开原、海城、彰武等九城。（三）缴各种炮二百三十二门，轻重机枪五百二十七挺，冲锋枪四百二十三支，长短枪六千六百一十四支，各种子弹、炮弹八十余万发，汽车四十二辆（内十辆击毁），击毁蒋机二架，战马九百五十八匹及其它弹药军用品甚多。
    【新华社东北前线十日电】八日前线续有小战斗，蒋匪新一军企图由四平及长春伸出试探，其由四平东南半拉山门出窜之匪新三十八师两个团在大炮掩护下蠢动，当被我击溃，并俘其五十余名后窜回。其由长春乘汽车南窜至郑家屯附近之匪新三十师一部，乱放一阵枪炮后即仓惶窜回。
    【新华社东北前线十日电】迟到消息：民主联军南线某部于二日晚攻克本溪以北之高关塞、行台子等重要据点。破袭战仍继续展开，中长路及北宁路上数以百计之碉堡、铁丝网均被平毁。
