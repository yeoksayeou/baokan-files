Title: Current International and Domestic Situation
Author:
Date: 1947-10-29
Page: 4

　　Current International and Domestic Situation
    Since the establishment of the Information Bureau of the Nine Communist Parties of Europe and the large-scale counter-offensive of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, major changes have taken place in the international and domestic situation. Everyone is eager to study the ins and outs of these changes, and this book provides you with materials to study these issues. The contents include: recent speeches by Vyshinsky, Dimitrov, Tito, etc., the declaration of the Information Bureau, and the editorials of the Xinhua News Agency, the declaration and slogans of the People's Liberation Army, etc., with five appendixes for easy reference.

### 目前国际形势与国内形势

1947-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　目前国际形势与国内形势
    自欧洲九国共产党情报局成立与中国人民解放军大举反攻以来，国际国内形势已经起了重大的变化。这些变化的来龙去脉，大家都是渴望着把它研究清楚的，这本书正供给了你研究这些问题的材料。内容有：维辛斯基、季米特洛夫、铁托诸氏最近的演说，情报局的宣言，及新华社的社论，人民解放军的宣言、口号等，并有附录五篇，参考启尤便。
