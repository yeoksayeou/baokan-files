Title: Czechoslovak Communist Leader Addresses Youth Festival, Calls on World's Youth to Unite and Fight Resolutely for Peace and Democracy
Author: 
Date: 1947-10-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th: Prague dispatches belatedly: Czech Prime Minister and leader of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Klement Gottwald, delivered a speech at the closing ceremony of the World Youth Festival on August 17th. The full text is as follows:
    Dear friends,
    Only at the closing am I given the opportunity to welcome the World Youth Festival, and now, at its victorious climax, I take this opportunity to salute all foreign and Czech representatives!
    Nothing can compare to this Youth Festival. Here, over 17,000 guests from 71 countries are gathered with tens of thousands of our Czech youth. The youth of the world, after shouldering the burdens of war and the terror of fascism, have expressed in extraordinary form at this Prague Youth Festival their determination to never again allow the world to be plunged into a new bloody war. The call for peace has spread from Prague to the entire world and to all honest people, and it will resonate in all honest hearts.
    This festival clearly demonstrates another fact: that only the victory of democracy can guarantee peace for us in the present and in future generations. Today, the meaning of democracy—true democracy—is beyond doubt. First, democracy means our relentless elimination of all remnants of fascism and its nascent seeds. Democracy means we must realize the aspirations of the common people for a peaceful and happy life. Democracy means the freedom, independence, development, equality, friendship, and cooperation of all nations. Democracy means uniting in the struggle to achieve these goals!
    “Peace and Democracy!” I think this is the message of this Youth Festival to the world. The Czech people wholeheartedly support this message, and today we say to our guests, who were warmly welcomed in Prague, "Farewell!" We believe that for them, as for our youth and all of us, the Youth Festival is also an inspiration. It is an inspiration in the struggle to achieve the two great goals of mankind!
    Long live the World Federation of Democratic Youth!
    Long live the common struggle of the youth of the world and all honest people for peace and democracy!

### 捷共领袖在青年节大会演讲  号召全世界青年团结  为和平民主坚决斗争

1947-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    捷共领袖在青年节大会演讲
    号召全世界青年团结
    为和平民主坚决斗争
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】布拉格迟到消息：捷克总理捷共领袖哥特瓦尔德八月十七日在世界青年节大会闭幕典礼上发表演讲，全文如下：
    亲爱的朋友们：
    闭幕的时候我才得到机会欢迎世界青年节，现在我在它胜利的高潮中借机会向所有外国及捷克的代表们致敬！
    没有什么东西可以和这次青年节相比，在这里有七十一国一万七千以上的客人和我们上数万的捷克青年相聚会。全世界的青年于肩负了战争的重担和法西斯恐怖之后，在这次布拉格的青年节中，用非凡的形式表示了他们那再不许世界被投入新的血腥战争的决心。要求和平的呼声已经从布拉格传播到全世界及一切正直的人们了，并将在所有正直的心灵中得到反响。
    这一节日很清楚的表明了另一个事实：那就是只有民主的胜利，才能保证我们目前以及未来年代的和平。今天民主——真正的民主的意义已经无可怀疑了，首先民主就是我们毫不留情的消灭一切法西斯的残余及其新生的种子，民主就是我们必须实现那些憧憬和平幸福生活的普遍人民的愿望，民主就是一切民族的自由、独立、发展、平等、友谊与合作。民主就是在实现上述目标的斗争中团结起来！
    “和平与民主！”我想就是这次青年节给世界的通告。捷克人民全心全意的拥护这个通告，并在今天向我们曾为布拉格城热烈欢迎的客人们讲：“告别了！”我们相信对于他们，青年节同样是一种鼓舞，象对我们的青年及我们一切人一样。这是在实现人类两大目标的斗争中的一个鼓舞呵！
    世界民主青年联盟万岁！
    全世界青年及一切正直的人们争取和平民主的共同斗争胜利万岁！
