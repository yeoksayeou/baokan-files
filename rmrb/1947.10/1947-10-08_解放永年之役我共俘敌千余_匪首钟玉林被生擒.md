Title: Victory in Yongnian Campaign: Our Army Captures Over 1,000 Enemy Soldiers; Bandit Chief Zhong Yulin Captured Alive

Author:

Date: 1947-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinji Luyu, 6th: The progress of our army's capture of Yongnian city in southern Hebei is as follows: On the 4th of this month, after our attacking forces completed preparations, they launched a general offensive on the 22nd, braving the rain. After a night of fierce fighting, they entered the city on the dawn of the 5th. In addition to those killed and wounded, over three hundred enemy soldiers were captured alive, and Yongnian was liberated. Bandit chiefs Zhong Yulin, Wang Zemin, and Xu Tieying (nicknamed Iron Demon) and others fled in panic, only to be intercepted by our ambushing forces just outside the city. Most of them were annihilated, and over seven hundred people including Zhong Yulin and others were captured alive. In this battle, a total of over one thousand enemy soldiers were captured, and sixteen light and heavy machine guns, four anti-aircraft machine guns, and over five hundred rifles were seized. The remaining enemies fled westward from Yongnian city, and our pursuing forces are currently encircling and annihilating them.

### 解放永年之役我共俘敌千余　匪首钟玉林被生擒

1947-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放永年之役我共俘敌千余
  　匪首钟玉林被生擒
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫六日电】我军攻克冀南永年城之经过如下：本月四日，我攻城部队完成攻城准备后，乃于二十二日起，冒雨发起总攻，经彻夜激战，于五日拂晓攻入城内。除毙伤外，生俘守敌三百余名，永年遂告解放。匪首钟玉林、王泽民、许铁英（外号铁魔头）等仓惶突围逃窜，甫出城外，即为我军预伏部队截获，将其大部歼灭，生俘钟玉林等以下七百余人。是役共计俘敌一千余人，缴轻重机枪十六挺，高射机枪四挺，步枪五百余支。残敌向永年城西逃窜，我追击部队正包围聚歼中。
