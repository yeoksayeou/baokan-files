Title: Boyan Theater Troupe Abuses Fruits of Struggle
Author: Ding Guo, Guo Zhen, Fan Sen
Date: 1947-10-06
Page: 2

Boyan Theater Troupe Abuses Fruits of Struggle

To the Editor:

　　The Boyan Theater Troupe in the Third District of Wu'an, since its establishment in July of last year, has hired old opera artists to teach plays. The consumption and supply of some actors and teachers in the troupe have been paid for by the fruits of the masses' struggle. From July of last year to now, according to incomplete statistics, they have consumed 1715.25 kilograms of wheat, 1014.64 kilograms of millet, 273.3 kilograms of millet, and 182,400 yuan in current currency. In addition to using a full box of the Farmers' Association, they have also taken 55 pieces of clothing from the fruits of the struggle, totaling 3000 kilograms of millet. This kind of waste is really inappropriate.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Ding Guo, Guo Zhen, Fan Sen)

### 伯延剧团滥用果实
定国　国珍　繁森
1947-10-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　伯延剧团滥用果实编辑同志：
    武安三区伯延剧团，自去年七月份成立起，聘请旧剧艺师传剧，剧团有部分演员和教师的消耗供给，系由群众斗争果实开支，自去年七月到现在，据不完全统计，吃麦子一千七百一十五斤四两，谷子二千零二十九斤十三两，小米五百四十六斤十一两，现洋十八万二千四百元，除用农会全箱一幅外，又从斗争果实内抽去衣服五十五件，总共计算折合小米六千斤。这种浪费，实不应该。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（定国、国珍、繁森）
