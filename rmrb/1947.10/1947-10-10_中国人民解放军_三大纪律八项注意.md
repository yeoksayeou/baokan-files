Title: The Three Main Rules of Discipline and the Eight Points for Attention of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1947-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th: The General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, for the Three Main Rules of Discipline and the Eight Points for Attention implemented in various places for many years, the content has slightly differed. It is now re-promulgated on the Double Tenth Festival to achieve uniformity. The original text is as follows:

Order from the General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

To all levels of military and political leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

(1) Our army's Three Main Rules of Discipline and Eight Points for Attention have been implemented for many years, and their content varies slightly in different regions and armies. Now, a unified regulation is made and re-promulgated. It is hoped that this will be used as the standard for in-depth education and strict implementation. As for other matters that should be paid attention to, the highest leaders in various regions and armies can stipulate a number of items according to the specific situation and implement them by order.

(2) The Three Main Rules of Discipline are as follows: (1) Obey orders in all actions; (2) Do not take anything from the masses, not even a needle or thread; (3) Turn in everything captured to public ownership.

(3) The Eight Points for Attention are as follows: (1) Speak politely; (2) Be fair in trade; (3) Return what you borrow; (4) Pay for anything you damage; (5) Do not hit or swear at people; (6) Do not damage crops; (7) Do not flirt with women; (8) Do not mistreat prisoners.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 10, 1947

### 中国人民解放军　三大纪律八项注意

1947-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军
  　三大纪律八项注意
    【新华社陕北九日电】中国人民解放军总部为各地实行多年之三大纪律八项注意，内容略有出入，特于双十节重新颁布，以求统一。原文如下：
    中国人民解放军总部训令
    中国人民解放军各级军政首长同志们：
    （一）本军三大纪律，八项注意，实行多年，其内容各地各军略有出入。现在统一规定，重行颁布。望即以此为准，深入教育，严格执行。至于其他应当注意事项，各地各军最高首长可根据具体情况，规定若干项目，以命令实行之。
    （二）三大纪律如下：（１）一切行动听指挥；（２）不拿群众一针一线；（３）一切缴获都归公。
    （三）八项注意如下：（１）说话和气；（２）买卖公平；（３）借东西要还；（４）损坏东西要赔；（５）不打不骂人；（６）不损坏庄稼；（７）不调戏妇女；（８）不虐待俘虏。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国人民解放军总部
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四七年十月十日
