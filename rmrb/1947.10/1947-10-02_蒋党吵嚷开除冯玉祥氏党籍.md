Title: Chiang Clique Clamors to Expel Feng Yuxiang from the Party

Author:
Date: 1947-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th—The Tianjin Min Guo Daily revealed on the 13th that Feng Yuxiang recently made speeches in the United States criticizing Chiang Kai-shek's bandit internal war measures. Chiang Kai-shek's henchmen were ashamed and furious. When the Chiang Kai-shek party's Central Supervisory Committee met on the 12th, the issue was raised for discussion. Some advocated expelling Feng from the party, while others advocated a serious warning. After arguing, no result was achieved. Finally, it was decided to hand it over to the Central Supervisory Committee for handling.

### 蒋党吵嚷开除冯玉祥氏党籍

1947-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋党吵嚷开除冯玉祥氏党籍
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】十三日天津民国日报透露：冯玉祥氏近在美国发表言论抨击蒋匪内战措施，蒋匪喽罗羞恼成怒，十二日蒋党中央监委会开会时提出讨论，有主张开除冯之党籍者，有主张严重警告者，吵吵嚷嚷未获结果，最后乃决定交中央监委会处理。
