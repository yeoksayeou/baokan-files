Title: Bulgarian and Yugoslavian Miners Initiate Revolutionary Competition
Author:
Date: 1947-10-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th. Belgrade Dispatch: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Miners' Trade Union recently sent a letter of revolutionary competition challenge to the Central Committee of the Yugoslavian Miners' Trade Union. The "Combat News" here has published it, and the miners of the two countries will compete in fulfilling the annual production plan. The competition includes a wide range of items, including mining work itself, the completion of miners' housing construction plans, improving ideological and cultural work, and developing trade union organizations. This challenge may mark the beginning of a New Democratic competition among the working classes of Eastern European countries.

### 保南两国矿工挑起革命竞赛

1947-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　保南两国矿工挑起革命竞赛
    【新华社陕北十七日电】贝尔格莱德讯：保加利亚矿工工会中央委员会最近以革命竞赛的挑战书一封送达南斯拉夫矿工工会中央委员会，顷由此间“战斗报”予以发表，两国矿工即将在履行一年生产计划的工作上进行竞赛。竞赛项目极为广泛，除采矿工作本身外，并包括完成矿工住房建筑计划，提高思想与文化工作及发展工会组织等。此项挑战可能标志东欧各国工人阶级展开新民主主义竞赛之开始。
