Title: Generals Liu and Deng Send Telegram Congratulating on the Qingfengdian Victory
Author:
Date: 1947-10-29
Page: 1

　　Generals Liu and Deng
  　Send Telegram Congratulating on the Qingfengdian Victory
    【Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 27th】The entire army was excited by the news of the great victory at Qingfengdian in Jin-Cha-Ji. Generals Liu and Deng sent a telegram of congratulations, the original telegram is as follows: Commander Nie, to the comrades of the front line:
    Your great victory on the 22nd in annihilating the main force of Chiang Kai-shek's 3rd Army between Wangdu and Dingxian, the good news came, and the entire field army was jubilant. We are now coordinating with the victory on the northern front by annihilating the Chiang Kai-shek army in the south and mobilizing the masses to distribute grain and land. We hereby send a telegram to express our congratulations.
    　　　　　　　　　Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, October 26.

### 刘邓两将军电贺青风店大捷

1947-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘邓两将军
  　电贺青风店大捷
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十七日电】晋察冀清风店大捷传来，全军振奋。刘邓两将军特电致贺，原电如下：聂司令员转前线将士同志们：
    你们二十二日在望都、定县间歼灭蒋匪第三军主力之大胜利，捷报传来，我野战军全军欢腾，现我们正以歼灭南面蒋匪军和发动群众分粮分田的胜利，来配合北线胜利，特电致贺。
    　　　　　　　　　刘伯承、邓小平十月二十六。
