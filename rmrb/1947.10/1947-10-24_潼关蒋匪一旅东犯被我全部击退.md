Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Brigade's Eastern Advance at Tongguan Completely Repulsed by Our Forces
Author:
Date: 1947-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines of Henan and Shaanxi, 22nd: The People's Liberation Army on the Western Front repulsed an invasion by a Chiang Kai-shek bandit brigade at Tongguan. At 13:00 on the 17th, the entire 27th Brigade of the enemy's 30th Division fiercely attacked Baoli Village, forty miles east of Tongguan. After more than an hour of intense fighting, they were completely repulsed by our forces and fled towards Tongguan. The enemy suffered over forty casualties, while we sustained only two minor injuries.

### 潼关蒋匪一旅东犯被我全部击退

1947-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　潼关蒋匪一旅东犯被我全部击退
    【新华社豫陕前线二十二日电】西线人民解放军击退潼关蒋匪一个旅之进犯。十七日十三时，敌三十师二十七旅全部猛犯潼关东四十里之堡里村，激战一小时许，被我全部击退，向潼关逃窜，敌伤亡四十余名，我仅轻伤二名。
