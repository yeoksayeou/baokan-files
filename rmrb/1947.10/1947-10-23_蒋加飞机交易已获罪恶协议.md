Title: Chiang's Aircraft Deal Achieves Sinister Agreement
Author:
Date: 1947-10-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st -- The Canadian government's sale of one hundred and fifty Mosquito bombers to the Chiang Kai-shek bandit government has reached a final agreement. According to an Agence France-Presse report from Ottawa on the 20th, under this agreement, the price of these aircraft totals approximately $12 million, half of which will be paid in US dollars. This sinful act by the Canadian government, which is hostile to the Chinese people, has aroused strong opposition from the Chinese and Canadian people.

### 蒋加飞机交易已获罪恶协议

1947-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋加飞机交易已获罪恶协议
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】加拿大政府以一百五十架蚊式轰炸机售与蒋匪政府事，已取得最后协议。据法新社渥太华二十日消息，根据该协议，该项飞机售价共约一千二百万美元，其中半数以美元付给。加拿大政府这一与中国人民为敌的罪恶行为，曾激起中国人民与加拿大人民的强烈反对。
