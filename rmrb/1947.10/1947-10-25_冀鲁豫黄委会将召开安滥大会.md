Title: Ji-Lu-Yu Yellow River Committee to Convene a Flood Prevention Success Conference
Author:
Date: 1947-10-25
Page: 2

　　Ji-Lu-Yu Yellow River Committee to Convene a Flood Prevention Success Conference
    [This newspaper, Ji-Lu-Yu, 23rd dispatch] The Yellow River Committee, in order to summarize this year's Yellow River management and dike repair work experience, and to celebrate the victorious completion of the great task of "ensuring Yellow River management, firmly defending the golden dikes, and preventing breaches," is preparing to convene a Flood Prevention Success Conference on November 10th (安滥, meaning that the Yellow River has not breached and flooded). At the meeting, heroes and role models in river management will be commended, and heroes and role models will be rewarded with hero medals, model flags, and warrior carts and iron carts. Martyrs' monuments will also be established by the repair and defense departments for those who sacrificed themselves for Yellow River management. It is estimated that 60 heroes and role models from various places will attend the conference. The conference preparatory committee has been formed a few days ago.

### 冀鲁豫黄委会将召开安滥大会

1947-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫黄委会将召开安滥大会
    【本报冀鲁豫廿三日电】黄委会为总结今年治黄修堤工作经验，庆祝“确保治黄固守金堤不准决口”伟大任务的胜利完成，准备于十一月十日召开安滥（即黄水未决口泛滥之意）大会。会上将表扬治河中之英雄模范，以制定之英雄奖章、模范旗及战士车铁车等奖励英雄模范。并以修防处为单位，建立为治黄而牺牲的烈士碑。预计大会可到各地英雄模范六十人。大会筹备会已于日前组成。
