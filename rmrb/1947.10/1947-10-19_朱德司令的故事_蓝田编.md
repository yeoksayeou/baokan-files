　　The Story of Commander Zhu De
  　Compiled by Lan Tian
    Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army, General Zhu De, has fought for the revolution for decades. Many stories about him are circulating among the millions of soldiers in the People's Liberation Army and the broad masses of the people. Unfortunately, they are not fully recorded. Now, after the editor's collection and arrangement, readers can learn about the revolutionary qualities of Commander Zhu from these stories. By reading these stories together, they can also understand Commander Zhu's decades of struggle for the people.

### 朱德司令的故事　蓝田编

1947-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱德司令的故事
  　蓝田编
    人民解放军总司令——朱德将军，为革命奋斗了几十年，有许许多多故事，流传在几百万人民解放军，和广大人民口中，但可惜记载不全，现经编者收集整理，使读者能于这些故事里来学习朱总司令的革命品质，把这些故事合拢来读，又能了解朱总司令为人民奋斗数十年的历史。
    华北新华书店发行
