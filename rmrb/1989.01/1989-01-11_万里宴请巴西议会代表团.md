Title: Wan Li Hosts Dinner for Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 1

Wan Li Hosts Dinner for Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 10 - Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wan Li hosted a dinner tonight to welcome the Brazilian parliamentary delegation led by Senate President Humberto Lucena. Wan Li said that Sino-Brazilian relations have entered a stage of stable development.

He stated that since the establishment of diplomatic relations in 1974, friendly cooperation between the two countries has rapidly developed in various fields and shows a broad prospect.

He reaffirmed that China is willing to maintain and develop friendly cooperation with Brazil in various fields on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and strive for world peace and the common prosperity of all countries.

Lucena believes that Brazil and China have many similarities and common goals in politics, economics, and other aspects, and the two sides should further promote cooperation in various fields.

This morning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Ni Zhifu held talks with Lucena.

### 万里宴请巴西议会代表团

1989-01-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　万里宴请巴西议会代表团
    据新华社北京１月１０日电  全国人大常委会委员长万里今晚在欢迎由国会主席兼参议长温伯托·卢塞纳率领的巴西议会代表团的宴会上说，中巴关系已经进入了一个稳定发展阶段。
    他说，中巴自１９７４年建交以来，两国间的友好合作关系在各个领域都得到了迅速的发展，并展现出广阔前景。
    他重申，中国愿意在和平共处五项原则的基础上保持和发展同巴西在各个领域中的友好合作关系，为维护世界和平、促进各国的共同繁荣而努力。
    卢塞纳认为，巴中两国在政治、经济等方面有着许多相似的地方和共同目标，双方应进一步推动各个领域里的合作。
    今天上午，全国人大常委会副委员长倪志福同卢塞纳举行了会谈。
