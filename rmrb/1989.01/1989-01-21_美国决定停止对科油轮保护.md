Title: The United States Decides to Stop Protecting Kuwaiti Tankers
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 6

The United States has decided to stop protecting six Kuwaiti tankers flying the American flag due to the easing of tensions in the Gulf region.

A U.S. official said on the 19th that this decision was made after extensive consultations with the Kuwaiti government.

Since the summer of 1987, the United States has agreed to allow eleven Kuwaiti tankers to fly the American flag to receive protection from the U.S. Navy and avoid Iranian attacks. The official said that the remaining five Kuwaiti vessels will continue to fly the American flag and receive protection from the U.S. Navy until February 1990.

### 美国决定停止对科油轮保护

1989-01-21
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　美国决定停止对科油轮保护
    由于海湾局势趋向缓和，美国决定停止对悬挂美国国旗的六艘科威特油轮的保护。
    一位美国官员十九日说，这一决定是在美国同科威特政府进行了广泛的协商之后作出的。
    美国自一九八七年夏开始同意让十一艘科威特油轮悬挂美国国旗，以接受美国海军的保护，免受伊朗袭击。这位美国官员说，其余五艘科威特船只在一九九○年二月前将继续悬挂美国国旗，接受美国海军的保护。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
