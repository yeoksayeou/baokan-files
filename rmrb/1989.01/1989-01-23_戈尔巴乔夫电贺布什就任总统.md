Title: Gorbachev Congratulates Bush on His Inauguration as President
Author:
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 6

Soviet leader Gorbachev hopes that during Bush's term as U.S. President, Sino-U.S. relations will achieve stable and comprehensive development. Gorbachev expressed that the Soviet Union will strive in this direction.

According to TASS, Gorbachev said in a telegram congratulating Bush on his inauguration as U.S. President today that Bush is taking office at a time when Sino-U.S. relations are improving. Continued joint efforts by the U.S. and the Soviet Union will help accelerate the resolution of the most urgent issues of the day, primarily the elimination of the danger of war.

### 戈尔巴乔夫电贺布什就任总统

1989-01-23
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫电贺布什就任总统
    新华社莫斯科１月２０日电  苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫希望，在布什任美国总统期间苏美关系能得到稳定、全面的发展。
    戈尔巴乔夫表示苏联将朝这一方向努力。
    据塔斯社报道，戈尔巴乔夫今天在祝贺布什就任美国总统的电报中指出，布什是在美苏关系改善的形势下上任的。苏美继续共同努力有助于加快当代最紧迫问题的解决，首先是消除战争危险问题的解决。
