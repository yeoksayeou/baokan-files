Title: A U.S. Organization Invites Arafat to Attend a Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 6

The White House spokesman, Fitzwater, said today that the United States will "carefully consider" whether to issue a visa to PLO leader Arafat, so that he can come to Washington to attend a meeting of a local mass organization.

According to a statement issued by the American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee yesterday, Arafat has formally accepted the invitation to attend the committee's annual meeting in Washington on April 13 and deliver a speech.

Fitzwater said, "We have not yet received a visa application related to this matter. But we will carefully consider this issue."

Last November, U.S. Secretary of State Shultz refused Arafat's application for a visa to attend the UN General Assembly. In early December, after Arafat publicly acknowledged Israel's right to exist and expressed opposition to terrorism, the United States agreed to dialogue with the PLO. On December 6, U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia, Robert Pelletreau, met with PLO representatives in Tunis for the first time.

### 美一组织邀阿拉法特参加会议  政府表示将考虑阿签证问题

1989-01-19
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　美一组织邀阿拉法特参加会议
    政府表示将考虑阿签证问题
    新华社华盛顿１月１７日电  美国白宫发言人菲茨沃特今天说，美国将“非常仔细地”考虑是否向巴解组织领导人阿拉法特发放签证，以便使他能够来华盛顿参加当地一个群众组织的会议。
    据美国阿拉伯人反种族歧视委员会昨天发表的一份声明说，阿拉法特已正式接受邀请，前来参加该委员会于４月１３日在华盛顿举行的年会，并发表讲话。
    菲茨沃特说，“我们尚未收到与此事有关的签证要求。但我们将非常仔细地考虑这一问题。”
    去年１１月，美国国务卿舒尔茨拒绝了阿拉法特申请赴美参加联大会议的签证。１２月初在阿拉法特公开承认以色列的生存权并表示反对恐怖主义之后，美国同意与巴解组织对话。１２月６日，美国驻突尼斯大使罗伯特·佩莱特鲁同巴解组织代表在突尼斯举行了首次会晤。
