Title: Nanchang Successfully Breeds Black Swans
Author: Xiang Ru Shun Ren
Date: 1989-01-16
Edition: 3

Nanchang Successfully Breeds Black Swans
Two Young Swans Ready to Take Flight

	The black swans raised in the waterfowl lake of Nanchang City Zoo laid five eggs in early February last year. After 41 days of incubation, two young swans hatched. With careful nursing and feeding, the young swans have grown well over the past year and are now ready to take flight.

	The black swans, native to the western and southern parts of Australia, are one of only five species of swans in the world and are rare migratory birds. The pair of black swans introduced to Nanchang City Zoo in 1982 from abroad have successfully begun to reproduce due to scientific breeding methods.

专栏：

　　南昌繁殖黑天鹅成功
    两羽幼鹅已展翅欲飞
    本报讯  南昌市动物园水禽湖中饲养的黑天鹅，在去年２月初产卵５枚，经过４１天孵化，先后孵出２羽幼鹅。通过精心护理、喂养，近一年来，幼鹅生长发育良好，已展翅欲飞。
    产于澳大利亚西、南部的黑天鹅，是世界上仅有的５种天鹅之一，属于珍稀游禽。南昌市动物园１９８２年从国外引进的一对黑天鹅，由于科学饲养，终于开始繁殖后代。
