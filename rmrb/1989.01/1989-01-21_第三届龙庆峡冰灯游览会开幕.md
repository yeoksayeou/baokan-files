Title: Opening of the Third Longqingxia Ice Lantern Festival
Author: Shi Zhongyuan
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 2

The third Longqingxia Ice Lantern Festival, themed around the legendary tale of "The White Snake," opened on January 19 in Longqingxia, Yanqing County, Beijing.

The third Longqingxia Ice Lantern Festival has been designated as a key winter tourism event in Beijing. The exhibition area is divided into two main sections: the colored lantern area and the ice lantern area, covering a total of 45,000 square meters, which is double the size of the previous year's exhibition.

### 第三届龙庆峡冰灯游览会开幕
石中元
1989-01-21
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    第三届龙庆峡冰灯游览会开幕
  　以《白蛇传》传奇故事为主题的北京龙庆峡第三届冰灯游览会，一月十九日在延庆县龙庆峡开幕。
    龙庆峡第三届冰灯游览会已被列为北京市冬季旅游活动的重要项目。展区分彩灯区和冰灯区两大部分，总占地面积四点五万平方米，是上届冰展的二倍。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（石中元）
