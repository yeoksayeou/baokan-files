Title: U.S. President-Elect Nominates Another Batch of Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-01-09
Edition: 6

According to a report by the Washington Post on the 7th, the U.S. President-elect George Bush nominated Lawrence Eagleburger as the U.S. Deputy Secretary of State and Margaret Tutwiler as the U.S. State Department spokeswoman and Assistant Secretary of State for Public Affairs in a written statement released on the 6th.

Bush also nominated U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Vernon Walters and Henry Catto as the U.S. Ambassadors to West Germany and the United Kingdom, respectively.

### 美当选总统又提名一批官员

1989-01-09
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　美当选总统又提名一批官员
    据《华盛顿邮报》七日报道，美国总统当选人乔治·布什在六日发表的一份书面声明中提名劳伦斯·伊格尔伯格为美国副国务卿，提名玛格丽特·塔特怀勒为美国国务院女发言人和负责公共事务的助理国务卿。
    布什在声明中还提名美国驻联合国大使弗农·沃尔特斯和亨利·卡托分别为美国驻西德大使和驻英国大使。
