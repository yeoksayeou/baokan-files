Title: A Decorative Chandelier in Bangkok
Author: Tao Junfeng
Date: 1989-01-05

In the heart of Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, a department store features a beautifully decorated chandelier. The long tassels emit a golden glow, not only enhancing the store's ambiance but also attracting more customers.
