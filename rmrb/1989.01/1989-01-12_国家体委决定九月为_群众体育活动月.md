Title: The State Sports Commission Designates September as Mass Sports Month
Author:
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 3

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the State Sports Commission has designated September of this year as Mass Sports Month. The Commission is calling on people from all walks of life to actively participate in sports activities.

### 国家体委决定九月为  群众体育活动月

1989-01-12
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　国家体委决定九月为
    群众体育活动月
    为庆祝中华人民共和国诞生四十周年，国家体委将今年九月定为群众体育活动月，发动各阶层人民群众，积极参加体育活动。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
