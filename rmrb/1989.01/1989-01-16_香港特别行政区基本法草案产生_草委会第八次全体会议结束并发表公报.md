Title: The Draft of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region is Produced
Author: Changzheng
Date: 1989-01-16
Edition: 1

The draft of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China was produced at the eighth plenary meeting of the Basic Law Drafting Committee, which concluded today in Guangzhou.

The New China News Agency reported from Guangzhou on January 15: (Reporter Changzheng) The eighth plenary meeting of the Basic Law Drafting Committee of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China concluded this afternoon in Guangzhou. The meeting adopted and produced the "Draft of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China."

The chairman of the Basic Law Drafting Committee, Ji Pengfei, said that the meeting was conducted in accordance with democratic procedures. The drafting committee took a very serious, responsible, and solemn attitude towards the draft of the Basic Law submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for publication.

Ji Pengfei said that the work of the drafting committee will not be completed before the National People's Congress adopts the "Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China." He said that if this draft is accepted by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and decided to be published, a second round of soliciting opinions will be conducted in Hong Kong and mainland China. At that time, it is hoped that all members of the drafting committee and the advisory committee will make further efforts to widely listen to the opinions and suggestions of people from all walks of life.

At today's meeting, the committee members also unanimously adopted the "Notice of the Eighth Plenary Meeting of the Basic Law Drafting Committee of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China" (see the full text on page four).

Forty-seven members of the Hong Kong Basic Law Drafting Committee attended today's meeting, with eight absent. The vice-chairman of the Basic Law Drafting Committee, Hu Sheng, presided over the meeting.

### 香港特别行政区基本法草案产生  草委会第八次全体会议结束并发表公报
长征
1989-01-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　香港特别行政区基本法草案产生
    草委会第八次全体会议结束并发表公报
    新华社广州１月１５日电  （记者长征）中华人民共和国香港特别行政区基本法起草委员会第八次全体会议今天下午在广州结束。会议通过、产生了《中华人民共和国香港特别行政区基本法（草案）》。
    基本法起草委员会主任委员姬鹏飞说，会议是按照民主程序进行的。起草委员会对提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会公布的基本法草案采取了十分认真、负责和严肃的态度。
    姬鹏飞说，在全国人民代表大会通过《中华人民共和国香港特别行政区基本法》之前，起草委员会的工作并没有结束。他说，这个草案如经全国人大常委会接受并决定公布，将在香港和内地开展第二次征求意见的工作。届时希望全体起草委员和咨询委员再作努力，广泛地听取各界人士的意见和建议。
    在今天的会议上，委员们还一致通过了《中华人民共和国香港特别行政区基本法起草委员会第八次全体会议公报》（全文见第四版）。
    出席今天会议的香港基本法起草委员４７人，有８人请假。基本法起草委员会副主任委员胡绳主持了会议。
